<h2>Sales Associate - Entry Level</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b><i>Sales Associate - Entry Level: </i></b></p>
<p>Accounting Seed is a rapidly growing accounting software company selling a leading edge application on the Salesforce.com platform. We are the fusion of two disciplines: Information Technology and Accounting.</p>
<p>We are seeking several professional, dynamic Account Executives to join our inside sales team. The ideal candidate enjoys working in a fast-paced, engaging, and collaborative environment and possesses a passion for success. This position is ideal for a junior Sales Executive or someone with 1 or 2 years of experience.</p>
<p><b>What You’ll Do: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Identify and qualify opportunities from marketing generated leads and industry-related activities.</li>
<li>Pursue conversions with qualified leads that educate and position the value of Accounting Seed software.</li>
<li>Present the complete value proposition via customer virtual meetings and telephone calls.</li>
<li>Bring to light stakeholders and their roles within prospective client organizations.</li>
<li>Consult and engage our team of product experts to clarify and properly answer all prospect questions.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Essential Skills/Qualifications: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Ideal candidates should possess some B2B sales experience (software sales is a plus)</li>
<li>Associate’s Degree. A Bachelor’s degree is preferred</li>
<li>Ability to quickly learn new web-based technologies</li>
<li>Confidence, as well as strong verbal &amp; written communication skills when interacting with clients</li>
<li>Flexibility, responsiveness, and professional agility to satisfy multiple, parallel opportunities</li>
<li>Ability to discover and understand customer needs and meet those needs with a successful product sale</li>
<li>The ability to work well in a dynamic, fast-paced, fun, and ever growing team environment.</li>
<li>Task-focused, highly functional in a self-directed environment.</li>
<li>A motivated, driven, self-starter…We love those with grit and accountability!</li>
</ul>
<p><i>Accounting Seed is an equal opportunity employer. We celebrate diversity and are committed to creating an inclusive environment for all employees. All employment is decided on the basis of qualifications, merit and business need.</i></p>
<p>Job Type: Full-time</p>

<ul><li>Relevant: 1 year (Preferred)</li></ul>

</ul>
