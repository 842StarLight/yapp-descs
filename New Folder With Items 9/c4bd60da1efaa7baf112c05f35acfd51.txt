<h2>Senior Account Manager - Commercial Insurance</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>The J. Archer Insurance Group/Archer Commercial Insurance Group, is a full service independent insurance agency located in the West University area of Houston, TX. We are an energetic and modern firm <b>seeking an experienced Commercial Lines CSR candidate with complete knowledge of Commercial Insurance coverages, practices, and procedures.</b> This position is in house primarily marketing and servicing accounts, processing endorsements, issuing certificates, completing account reviews on renewals and throughout the year. The account manager may also be required to write new business. This position will work directly with management and also assist producers with writing new accounts.</p>
<p>We are looking for a candidate with strong carrier/market relationships and experience working in an independent agency environment. If you are a self-starter that truly values the role of being a customer advocate and advisor, we'd love to have you!</p>
<p><b>Responsibilities: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Full account management</li>
<li>Negotiate and communicate with underwriters to provide best coverage &amp; pricing available</li>
<li>Prepare submissions, quote new business and handle renewals</li>
<li>Assist producers in meeting new business production goals and objectives as established</li>
<li>Create proposals and assist in making sales presentations</li>
<li>Treat each customer contact as an opportunity to round their account</li>
<li>Ongoing service as needed</li>
</ul>

<li>Proficiency to multi-task, document, follow-thru and follow-up</li>

<li>Strong Technical skills including Office365 and other software</li>

</ul>
