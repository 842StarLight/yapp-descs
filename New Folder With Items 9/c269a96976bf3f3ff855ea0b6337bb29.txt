<h2>Project Engineer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Project Engineer Job Purpose:</b> Comprehensive management of individual customer projects of existing product line.</p>
<p><b>Report To:</b> Engineering Manager</p>
<p><b>Job Duties:</b></p>
<p><b>Planning and Project implementation:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Review Customer bid packages inclusive of specification data sheets, drawings and equipment lists and confirm overall product scope committed by the sales team.</li>
<li>Determine project schedule and develop a plan to meet delivery requirements</li>
<li>Work with customer and sub-vendors throughout project to maintain delivery commitments.</li>
<li>Prepare project documentation and deliverables i.e. customer approval drawings, test procedures, material certifications and product data</li>
<li>Procurement of instrumentation and other non-raw material components</li>
<li>Confirms product performance by designing and performing tests</li>
<li>Work within and adhere to ISO Quality Management System as well all mandated safety regulations</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Safety:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Follow established safety procedures as outlined in employee manual and updated guidelines communicated by safety committee</li>
<li>Ensure Product design I in accordance with applicable national and international codes and standards</li>
<li>Maintain general housekeeping policies of a safe and clean work area</li>
<li>Adhere to test procedures standards</li>
<li>Follow all safety procedures outlined at customer sites during Commissioning and field service work</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Quality Assurance:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Maintain quality standards of department as it relates to ISO 9001 Quality Manual</li>
<li>Adhere to engineering standards with relation to document control and engineering design</li>
<li>Ensure product is assembled in accordance against the work file issued by engineering</li>
<li>Adhere to ASME Sec. VIII quality procedures</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Communication:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Customer interface with regards to progress reports, specification review and project revisions.</li>
<li>Continuous follow up with Sub-Vendors to ensure on-time delivery of long lead purchased components</li>
<li>Routine interface with various departments within the company including, Engineering, Production and Sales throughout project life.</li>
<li>Post Project follow up with customer as well as possible face to face dealings during startup and or post commission field service visits.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Essential Requirements:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Proficient in latest version of AutoCAD and Inventor</li>
<li>Mechanical Engineering degree or minimum of 5 years of industry experience</li>
<li>Work with existing software to develop and produce drawings and Bills of Materials</li>
<li>Develop project schedules, Gant charts and other documentation using various computer software based programs</li>
<li>Willingness to work with hands</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Non-Essential Requirements:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Electrical design</li>
<li>Knowledge of electrical codes and regulations (UL 508A, NEC)</li>
<li>Knowledge of hazardous areas, ATEX and global standards</li>
<li>Commission equipment at customer site and field service visits</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Skills and Qualifications:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Self-Motivator, able to work independently with minimal supervision</li>
<li>Team Player, ability to work well with others</li>
<li>Versatile, able to handle demands of dealing with vendors and customers in multiple projects on a daily basis.</li>
<li>Leadership, strong management skills, able to oversee production of individual projects and provide direction where needed</li>
<li>Problem Solver, ability to work through various challenges</li>
<li>Innovator, Finding innovative solutions for common and unusual problems</li>
<li>Mechanical, Hands on, ability to understand the mechanics behind the design</li>
<li>Organized, Systematic control of all facets of project management</li>
</ul>
