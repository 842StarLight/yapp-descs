<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Overview
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div><b>Technical Support Specialist - HVAC/VRV/VRF</b></div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
<b>
The Opportunity</b><br>
</div>
<p></p>
<div>
Daikin TMI located in Lenexa, KS is seeking a professional, skilled individual for our Technical Support Specialist position. This individual will assist contractor technicians with troubleshooting of Daikin systems including; Residential, Ductless, VRV, and Light Commercial Rooftops as a technical expert for Daikin Thermal Mechanics, Inc.</div>
<div>
This position will provide first contact via phone fielding technical support requests from contractors, distributors, engineers, and end users for Daikin’s US domestic product lines. Provide pre and post sales Technical support including equipment troubleshooting and applications assistance via telephone, email and fax. If you meet the qualifications listed below, then we invite you to apply for our open position by visiting our website http://careers.daikincomfort.com, and submitting your resume.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div><i>May include:</i></div>
<div></div>
<br>
<ul>
<li>
Provide prompt and courteous response to calls coming into Daikin TMI's Technical Support Center hotline</li>
<li>
Identify and facilitate training programs for contractor technicians either classroom or on-site</li>
<li>
Maximize residential and commercial customer satisfaction through training, directing, and assisting the distribution service network to ensure installed product quality for the life of the equipment.</li>
<li>
Provide pre and post sales technical support via telephone, email</li>
<li>
Assist Reps, Distributors, and Contractors in troubleshooting and in the application of Daikin. equipment, connection ratio, capacity de-rates, and equipment selection</li>
<li>
Log all calls and incidents into the SM+ module to track information by customer, model number, and project</li>
<li>
Upon request, provide information such as submittal drawings, wiring diagrams, capacity tables, clearances, piping recommendations based on piping rules, or other information as published in Daikin’s equipment literature</li>
<li>
Assist Manager in the distribution of requests for field visits and training to the appropriate Daikin Service representative</li>
<li>
Record all requests for field visits and training in SM+</li>
<li>
Follow up on customer inquiries to ensure job completion and job satisfaction</li>
<li>
Test equipment and controls to verify warranty status</li>
<li>
Provide selection software support</li>
<li>
Help explain control related questions</li>
<li>
Perform other related job duties as assigned</li>
</ul>
