<h2>Customer Service Rep/Admin</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">CUSTOMER SERVICE REPRESENTATIVE/ADMIN
<br><br>
At Wisconsin Aviation in Madison, our Customer Service Representatives (CSR) have a front row view to everything aviation. We are seeking motivated individuals who can provide superior service to our customers. Upon joining the CSR Team, the doors in aviation are wide open!
<br><br>
We are looking for individuals interested in working full-time shifts. Our operations are 24/7 and you should expect to work some weekends and holidays.
<br><br>
Duties include the responsibility to welcome, direct, and provide information services to customers and visitors in person at our Madison location. Individuals must be able to answer telephone and radio systems regularly while providing product and service information. CSRs are responsible for providing front desk administrative support and day-to-day customer service including sales transactions; concierge services; and keeping in-house management informed and contributing to the Wisconsin Aviation Team.
<br><br>
Come join our team and start your aviation journey today! Wisconsin Aviation is one of the state's premier, full-service provider of general aviation services including charter, flight training, aircraft rental, aircraft sales, maintenance, avionics, interiors, and line services.
<br><br>
<b>Requirements:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Be 18 years of age or older and have a high school diploma or GED equivalent</li>
<li>Above-average customer service and communication skills</li>
<li>Ability to take and give instruction</li>
<li>Working knowledge of Microsoft Word, Outlook and Excel data entry</li>
<li>The ability to learn new software</li>
<li>Aviation knowledge is a plus, but not required</li>
<li>Candidates must pass a background check and a Federal fingerprint check</li>
<li>Possess a valid driver's license</li>
<li>Physical Requirements:</li>
<li>The ability to stand for long periods of time and move briskly in a constant changing environment</li>
<li>The ability to see and respond to security situations</li>
</ul>
<b>Location:</b> Madison, WI (MSN)
<br><br>
Our focus is on delivering the best possible customer service
