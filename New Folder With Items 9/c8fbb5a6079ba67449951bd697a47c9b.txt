Tasting Room Manager Job Description
<br>
<br>
The primary roles of the TRM are:
<br>
<br>
To manage the day-to-day operations of the Tasting Room and Pub both front of house and back of house.
<br>
<br>
To continually strive to improve the experience of everyone who visits the Meadery.
<br>
<br>
To develop and implement new and creative ways to bring new customers in the door and generate marketing and earned media opportunities relating to the tasting room.
<br>
<br>
Specific Duties
<br>
<br>
<ul>
<li>Tasting Room Management</li>
<ul>
<li>Manages/trains all tasting room/pub employees according to SHM’s Tasting Room Procedures and Checklists</li>
<li>Is responsible for ordering and inventory management.</li>
<li>Continuously stocks shelves, replenishes tasting, food, beer, and cocktail stocks and generally keeps the space organized functionally and aesthetically.</li>
<li>Adheres to the cleaning procedures strictly. Is responsible for and checks the cleanliness and organization of the entire property.</li>
<li>Deals with customers, giving tastings, conducting tours, taking orders, preparing food, drinks, etc. and manages employees to do the same according to the TRM procedures.</li>
<li>Must accumulate, keep and communicate information about mead, Sap House Meadery and its products.</li>
<li>Communicates feedback and sales information to other management team members. Provides input and insight into but not limited to new products, marketing plans, and events.</li>
</ul>
<li>Events, Marketing, and Innovation</li>
<ul>
<li>The position, along with other management team members, is responsible for developing and executing new and continuing events.</li>
<li>Promoting the events via social media and within the meadery as well as email lists will be required.</li>
<li>Be on the lookout for new products, methods, ideas and recipes for the Tasting Room that could lead to increased sales or exposure and fit with the brand.</li>
<li>Position may additionally be required to staff events in the market.</li>
</ul>
<li>Sales Support</li>
<ul>
<li>Assist the NHWS team by taking orders via the phone/voicemail.</li>
<li>Keeping awareness of new potential accounts opening, feedback and always understanding that the position represents Sap House Meadery when out in public.</li>
<br>
</ul>
</ul>
<br>
<br>
The duties listed above do not constitute all potential work that may be performed. As a small and growing company in a changing market, responsibilities and tasks may change, arise or fall away. The position must be capable and willing to thrive in a dynamic environment.
