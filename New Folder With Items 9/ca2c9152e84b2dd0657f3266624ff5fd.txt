<b>Position Description </b><i>(This position can be remote with travel)</i>
</div>
<div>This is a full-time, permanent role with CBI. As a Cybersecurity Strategist within the Strategic Security Programs practice, you will act as a trusted advisor to CBI’s client base. The Strategist will provide expertise in post-sales service delivery efforts. This role will be supporting the development of the Strategic Security Programs operating model and a service-oriented client engagement model and supporting the operationalization of various strategic security capability areas such as enterprise security risk management, compliance management, policy management, third party risk management, and metrics/reporting, as clients look to mature these areas.<br>
</div>
<div><b>Key Responsibilities</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Provide expertise in cybersecurity strategy consulting and peer level advisory servicesWork with various operational teams across various clients as to drive towards a cohesive view of security risk and drive remediation items to closure</li>
<li>Develop policy, plans, and strategy in compliance with laws, regulations, policies, and standards in support of organizational cyber activities</li>
<li>Deliver on technical security assessments, business continuity planning, incident response planning and IT internal controls design and implementation</li>
<li>Collaborate with clients to discover security gaps within current infrastructure, process, compliance standards, or business continuity planning</li>
<li>Develop Statement of Work (SOW) and business plan personalized to client needs and environment, designed to eliminate gap between current and desired level of risk</li>
<li>Individually and/or with team resources, deliver on client engagement according to scope, within budget, and on time</li>
<li>Create and deliver comprehensive, clear documentation and deliverables within project scope</li>
<li>Build and maintain meaningful relationships with clients, driving CBI as the trusted advisor within the cybersecurity space</li>
<li>Provide guidance and leadership to other risk management team members</li>
<li>Partner with the sales organization as a trusted, technical resource to engage potential clients and close on new business opportunities</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Knowledge &amp; Competencies</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Strategic thinker with deep technical and broad business knowledge</li>
<li>Expert level knowledge of cybersecurity and privacy principles</li>
<li>Expert level knowledge of risk management processes (e.g., methods for assessing and mitigating risk)</li>
<li>Expert level knowledge of laws, regulations, policies, and ethics as they relate to cybersecurity and privacy</li>
<li>Expert level knowledge in one or more of these areas: compliance/frameworks, cloud security, Identity management and OT/ICS</li>
<li>Proven mentorship abilities with a desire to lead</li>
</ul>
<b>Education &amp; Qualifications</b>
<ul>
<li>7+ years of experience in a cybersecurity advisory capacity within consulting or enterprise</li>
<li>Bachelor’s degree in Information Systems Management/Cybersecurity</li>
<li>MBA in relevant area is preferred</li>
<li>CISSP, CISM or CISA certifications are preferred</li>
<li>Experience delivering and driving security requirements for operational technology and Internet of Things</li>
<li>Deep rooted experience in security governance, risk assessments and regulatory controls</li>
<li>Strong capabilities in assessing clients against a wide variety of security and compliance frameworks including state based privacy and security regulations/frameworks, SOX, GDPR, NIST-CSF, ISO/27001/2</li>
