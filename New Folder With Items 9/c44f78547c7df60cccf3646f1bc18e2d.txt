<h2>Technical Consultant</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Are you passionate about being the ‘GO TO’ technical guru for customers? Do you thrive in an environment in which you provide technical assistance and troubleshooting solutions? If so, Voalte welcomes you to explore this exciting opportunity.</p>
<p>Voalte offers cutting edge mobile communication strategies to the country’s top healthcare providers. Our products support continuous efforts to improve patient care and increase caregiver productivity using the latest mobile devices.</p>
<p><b>RESPONSIBILITIES:</b></p>
<p>The Technical Consultant’s responsibilities include, but are not limited to:</p>
<div>
<ul><li>Providing exceptional customer support</li></ul>
<ul><li>Escalation point for Level 1 and Level 2 Support team</li></ul>
<ul><li>Fill a role of CEM (Critical Event Manager) and develop a support plan to resolve multiple issues at customer sites, when necessary</li></ul>
<ul><li>Earn the status of “Trusted Advisor” by managing client expectations and building relationships with all client stakeholders</li></ul>
<ul><li>Creating and maintaining documentation within an effective knowledge base, including accurate account documentation</li></ul>
<ul><li>Engaging in cross-functional team interaction with System Engineers, Voalte Care Specialists, Engineering, Product Management, Marketing and Sales</li></ul>
<ul><li>Developing a strong understanding of the entire Voalte solution including technical architecture</li></ul>
<ul><li>Assisting the Engineering team with alpha testing and bug reporting</li></ul>
<ul><li>Utilizing key performance indicators (KPI) in driving customer satisfaction and team growth</li></ul>
<ul><li>Identify and coordinate system changes activities/requests using industry standard change management protocols (ex. upgrades, patches)</li></ul>
<ul><li>Manage and trend client utilization, identify gaps and deviations from the norm</li></ul>
<ul><li>Monitoring the system health of the Voalte client in a remote support capacities</li></ul>
<ul><li>Travel up to 10% of the time, to support clients across the United States</li></ul>
<ul><li>Participating in on-call/emergency support rotation 24x7x365</li></ul>
</div>
<p><b>REQUIREMENTS:</b></p>
<div>
<ul><li>3+ years of previous experience (in healthcare industry preferred)</li></ul>
<ul><li>5+ years of technical support in a customer service environment</li></ul>
<ul><li>Ability to understand applications or device logs to identify issues or possible root cause (iPhones, Android, ejabberd, or tomcat logs)</li></ul>
<ul><li>Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science or comparable experience</li></ul>
<ul><li>Experience with troubleshooting hardware, software, and network related issues</li></ul>
<ul><li>Strong understanding of mobile devices on the Android and iOS platforms</li></ul>
<ul><li>Strong critical and analytical thinking skills</li></ul>
<ul><li>Excellent oral and written communication skills, including public speaking</li></ul>
<ul><li>Ability to interact effectively with all levels of customers and staff</li></ul>
<ul><li>Extremely detail oriented, independent, motivated and organized</li></ul>
<ul><li>Dedication to improving the customer experience</li></ul>
<ul><li>Adherence to established group processes and methodologies</li></ul>
<ul><li>Ability to multi-task while maintaining a positive attitude</li></ul>
<ul><li>Ability to embrace and adapt to change in a fast-paced environment</li></ul>
<ul><li>Familiarity with Confluence, JIRA, ZenDesk, Netsuite and Salesforce applications a plus</li></ul>
<ul><li>Certifications a plus</li></ul>
<ul><li>A high degree of <b><i>Passion, Respect and Imagination</i></b><b>:</b>
</li></ul>
</div>
