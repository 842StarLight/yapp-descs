<h2>Senior Sales Engineer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>This is a unique opportunity for someone who is a qualified technical resource and an experienced professional to move into an essential role in our growing company.
</p>
<p>The Senior Sales Engineer position is ideal for someone who is an IT generalist. If you can quickly grasp and apply new technology concepts, you will excel in this role as it offers opportunities to discover and apply a variety of skills.
</p>
<p>In this role, you will collaborate with sales executives as well as work autonomously. This position will report to the Director of Sales Engineering.</p>
<p>
Responsibilities:</p>
<ul>
<li>Deliver introductory product demonstrations</li>
<li>Develop and demonstrate custom prototypes and proofs of concept</li>
<li>Communicate product benefits and features to technical and non-technical audiences</li>
<li>Assist prospects through their technical evaluation of the InRule Decision Platform</li>
<li> Serve as the first line of support for prospects during the course of their product evaluation</li>
<li>Respond to Requests for Information/Proposal (RFIs/RFPs)</li>
<li>Maintain knowledge of existing solution and future product direction</li>
<li>Identify opportunities for product enhancements, features and functionality</li>
<li>Stay abreast of technology trends in enterprise IT environments</li>
<li>Contribute to InRule marketing efforts, including blog posts, conference presentations, webinars or videos
</li>
</ul>
