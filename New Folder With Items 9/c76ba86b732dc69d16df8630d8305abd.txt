<div><b>Function</b></div>
<div></div>
<div>We are currently hiring an experienced Channel Account Manager, IT Integrators located in the Western region for our dynamic Enterprise division. This individual will be responsible for promoting IT Integrator account sales, identify and develop new IT integrator accounts in both AV and IT markets. Establishing and maintaining a good working relationship with Barco distributors and implementing channel programs will also be required.</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div><b>Key Responsibilities</b></div>
<div></div>
<ul>
<li>Define and executing partner sales plans. Set-up Quarterly Business Plan (QBP) and Quarterly Marketing Plan (QMP).
</li>
<li>Manage and be the main point of contact (SPOC) for existing or new channel partners within the defined Region.
</li>
<li>Work with Marketing and Channel Marketing to drive programs and events to extend the relationships to current and new Channel Partners
</li>
<li>Drive partners to maximize sales and total partnership potential through sales best practices, training and support.
</li>
<li>Create systems and procedures to streamline partner management
</li>
<li>Manage the RFP / RFQ, special prices processes.
</li>
<li>Joint visits, together with the local sales team, to Value Added Resellers or Corporate Resellers or any identified potential managed reseller key account.
</li>
<li>Providing weekly reporting of pipeline and forecast.
</li>
<li>Responsible for managing key channel accounts within the Enterprise Channel as well as identifying and closing opportunities with key end user accounts</li>
<li>
With a primary focus on the ClickShare product line this role requires the ability to identify and actively manage entire product portfolio in the settings of meeting rooms, education, employee communication and general collaboration.
</li>
<li>Experience in selling, developing and establishing channels within the enterprise space with large AV and IT system integrators</li>
<li>
Effective communication skills and emotional intelligence
</li>
<li>Demonstrated strategic skills in building and growing the channel</li>
</ul>
