<h2>General Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p><b>To connect with a recruiter on current openings, text "ADS" to 44844.</b></p>
<p><b>
POSITION SUMMARY</b><b>:</b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
Manage the operations and financial success of a business unit which may include one or more hauling companies, transfer stations, landfills and/or MRFs. Responsible for the efficient and safe work activities of all division employees; responsible for providing assistance to managers, supervisors and employees and resolving the problems and concerns as required</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
PRIMARY DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES:</b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
This list of duties and responsibilities is not all inclusive and may be expanded to include other duties and responsibilities as management may deem necessary from time to time.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Interface with regional staff in formulating short and long-term goals consistent with the company's financial and operational objectives</li>
<li>Represent and uphold company ethics - environmental operating, regulatory, safety and accounting policies and standards - by encouraging and exemplifying professionalism</li>
<li>Effectively communicate company objectives, goals, business plans, concerns and philosophies to division employees. Ensure positive morale of those employees</li>
<li>Provide guidance and direction to division managers/supervisors in budgeting process. Influence staff in designing budgets that contribute acceptable margin improvements and significant annual growth</li>
<li>Ensure appropriate staffing levels and expertise, and oversee hiring, training and performance management of business unit employees</li>
<li>Interact with staff on current operations. Assist staff with their needs, resolve concerns and difficulties, and conduct routine site inspections</li>
<li>Monitor current status vs. budget (P&amp;L's CER cash flow, A/R-DSO etc.) and develop realistic attainable action items for areas needing improvement</li>
<li>Responsible for the execution, follow up and sustainability of planned operating improvements</li>
<li>Improve and maintain communications and timeliness, and ensure accuracy of reporting and collection of data</li>
<li>Oversee and maintain an effective accident prevention program, ensure all reasonable actions are taken to prevent accidents or injuries, and ensure a safe and productive work environment for all employees</li>
<li>Interact professionally and courteously with customers, vendors, members of the community and municipal and other government employees</li>
<li>Perform other job-related duties as assigned</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p>
</p>
<p><b>KNOWLEDGE, SKILLS, AND ABILITIES:</b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Ability to effectively manage multiple projects</li>
<li>Ability to plan and execute planned improvements and action items</li>
<li>Strong written and oral communication skills</li>
<li>Effective interpersonal communication across various levels of the organization and with external customers and vendors</li>
<li>Ability to read, analyze and interpret financial reports, business documents, professional journals, technical procedures, or governmental regulations</li>
<li>Knowledge of DOT, OSHA and other related regulations</li>
<li>Strong leadership, coaching and motivational skills</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p>
</p>
<p><b>MINIMUM REQUIREMENTS:</b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Education: </b>High School Diploma, GED and/or equivalent work experience. Associates or Bachelor's degree in business, civil engineering, construction management, operations management or related field is preferred.</p>
<p><b>
Years of Experience:</b> Minimum of 5 years management and job-related experience including experience in sales, operations and/or maintenance functions. Significant experience working around construction sites or heavy equipment requiring awareness of health and safety requirements is required. Prior experience in waste or transportation industry desired</p>
<p><b>
Certifications: </b>Valid Class A or B CDL is preferred</p>
<br>
<p></p>

MACHINES, TOOLS, EQUIPMENT AND SOFTWARE USED:</b></p>

<li>MS Office including Word, Excel and Outlook</li>
<li>Company system applications (ex. TRUX, Haul-It, Weigh-It, RTA)</li>
<li>Standard office software - Word, Excel, Explorer</li>
<li>Other software as applicable (mapping software and GPS-systems)</li>
<br>
</ul>
