<h2>CYCLEBAR ASSISTANT MANAGER - MATTHEWS, NC</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>CycleBar is seeking an Assistant Manager for our state-of-the-art location in Matthews, NC<b>. </b>Our Assistant Managers are passionate about fitness and delivering a superior experience. They prioritize driving sales and revenue within the studio.</p>
<p>Assistant Managers are responsible for supporting the General Manager with studio sales goals, membership renewals and ancillary service goals by pre-planning with strategic new customer outreach, in-club leads (first time riders) and securing member referrals. Under the management of a Franchise Owner, national sales director, and the support of a CycleBar Experience team, Assistant Managers must exemplify leadership qualities in all areas of the business. Assisant Managers must have the ability to build lasting relationships with prospective and current members in order build a strong ambassadorship and retain members. The Assistant Manager is rooted in hospitality and upholds our Mission of fueling energetic communal experience.</p>
<p><b>Requirement:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Confident in generating personal sales and training Sales Reps in sales.</li>
<li>Ability to support the General Manager to drive 2 revenue streams: memberships and retail</li>
<li>Must be fluent in English and have excellent communication and strong interpersonal skills in person, on the telephone and via email</li>
<li>Ability to excel in a fast changing, diverse environment.</li>
<li>Ability to recognize areas of improvement and make changes using good judgment.</li>
<li>An affinity and passion for fitness.</li>
<li>Solid writing and grammar skills.</li>
<li>Highly organized, proficient in data management, ability to prioritize and meet deadlines.</li>
<li>Professional, punctual, reliable and neat.</li>
<li>Strong attention to detail and accuracy.</li>
<li>Trustworthy and ability to handle confidential information.</li>
<li>Ability to work harmoniously with co-workers, clients and the general public.</li>
<li>Proficiency with computers and Studio software.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Duties:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Support the General Manager with sales and studio operations</li>
<li>Membership sales</li>
<li>Manage staff schedule</li>
<li>Supervise CBX Teams</li>
<li>Maintain cleanliness and organization of the Studio</li>
<li>Schedule and participate in networking/community events and studio promotions</li>
<li>Strategically manage marketing campaigns to generate leads for the studio</li>
<li>Any other duties as assigned Responsible for the acquisition of new members and the retention of existing members</li>
<li>Conversion of non-membership based users to membership base</li>
<li>Conduct pre-planning activities each month including strategic outreach, for securing first time rider/lead flow</li>
<li>Build rapport and lasting relationships with prospective and current members. Embrace hospitality and drive the Cyclebar Culture.</li>
<li>Assistant Manager will help the General Manager actively coach, train and educate our CBX team on Hospitality and Sales Conversion tactics. Manager will work with owner on team accountability</li>
<li>Working with General Manager and Studio Owner on setting Monthly, weekly, and daily targets for meeting goals</li>
<li>Driving Referral business through ambassadorship</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Compensation &amp; Benefits:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>This position offers competitive pay + Commission; based on experience &amp; performance.</li>
<li>Commission paid on sales</li>
<li>Complimentary CycleBar Classes at Studio</li>
</ul>
