<h2>Service Account Manager III</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Job Summary: </b></p>
<p>The Service Account Manager III is responsible for developing long-term customer relationships that drive customer retention, growth and satisfaction. Reporting directly to the Team Manager or Team Leader, the SAM III is accountable for providing the highest standard of service excellence for CPA Global’s top-tier Patent Renewal Customers. The SAM II acts as a principal interface between the customer and all business departments within CPA Global, taking ownership of issues and working with colleagues across the organization to secure existing business and increase revenue.</p>
<p>The SAM III is supported by a Customer Service Specialist II, who will report directly to the Team Manager or Team Leader.</p>
<p><b>Primary Responsibilities: </b></p>
<p><b>Customer Satisfaction and Retention: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Identify issues and provide resolutions in a timely manner to the satisfactory of the customer, such as completing audits; identifying trends; determining system improvements and implementing changes.</li>
<li>Ensure timely and accurate responses to customer queries, escalations, concerns or complaints</li>
<li>Drive improvements and initiatives in partnership with relevant business stakeholders to resolve delivery issues, failures or adverse customer’s feedback.</li>
<li>Build and develop awareness of the commercial and strategic value of the customer within business</li>
<li>Conduct internal annual reviews to deliver on engagement and retain business growth.</li>
<li>Provide effective customer account management and ensure customer retention for an assigned portfolio of clients.</li>
<li>Assure delivery of services in line with or exceeding customer’s expectations or operational / service success criteria's throughout the relationship lifecycle</li>
<li>Co-ordinate and actively participate in customer visits, presentations, workshops, user groups, forums, etc.</li>
<li>Communicate effectively with customer to ensure they receive a professional and high quality renewals service.</li>
<li>Responsible for understanding and complying with the Business Operations risk management principles to mitigate unnecessary risk.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Problem identification/solving: </b></p>
<p>Service Account Managers are responsible for identifying and implementing process improvements to raise service quality and reduce risk, specifically:</p>
<ul>
<li>Responsible for making recommendations that relate to improved communication, processes, workflow and automation with the aim of increasing profitability while retaining the client.</li>
<li>Responsible for regularly reviewing customer data to identify potential problems and risks to ensure efficient and accurate data transfer.</li>
<li>Analyse customer renewal behaviour, financial information, query data and internal processing data to identify client issues, trends and possible efficiency gains.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Project work: </b></p>
<p>The Service Account Manager is responsible for supporting any project assigned by Management. The responsibilities include the support and management of new business requests (BTO Projects) for existing customers.</p>
<ul>
<li>Coordinate with Senior Project Manager to ensure speed to revenue and to meet customer expectations.</li>
<li>Responsible for ensuring that the department’s procedures are fully documented, communicated and monitored.</li>
<li>Responsible for ensuring that the global processes and procedures are being followed and kept up to date with any changes as required.</li>
<li>Effectively manage multiple projects and tasks, including the appropriate prioritisation and time management while ensuring that servicing standards and expectations are met.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Business Interactions: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Escalate issues to management when necessary.</li>
<li>Develop the knowledge and skills of other members of the team through coaching, mentoring and implementation of procedures to promote CPA’s services.</li>
<li>Build internal relationships with other teams and cross train other teams to promote a consistent and professional approach.</li>
<li>Ensure compliance with procedures to mitigate risk and take immediate actions when appropriate..</li>
<li>Build relationships with the all global teams to facilitate a consistent and unified approach to customers.</li>
<li>Participate in quarterly meetings with Business Managers, Customer Success Managers, Software teams, etc. to discuss account management and facilitate open discussion to improve service deliverables.</li>
<li>Participate in the mentoring, coaching and training of support staff.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Primary Qualifications: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’sDegree and/or ten+ years of business to business customer service, account management or equivalent combination of education and experience.</li>
<li>Minimum of 10 years Intellectual Property experience required.</li>
<li>Ability to travel up to 25% to client sites and other locations in the surrounding area as needed</li>
<li>Strong project management skills in terms of the ability to multi-task, prioritize, set and meet deadlines.</li>
<li>Strong organizational skills</li>
<li>Ability to complete critical tasks with Global teams in a matrixed environment</li>
<li>Advanced proficiency in MS Word and Excel</li>
<li>Ability to work autonomously</li>
<li>Ability to work well under pressure and delivers on time across multiple accounts and conflicting priorities</li>
<li>Advanced strong written and verbal communication skills</li>
<li>Exceptional business writing skills and the ability to write reports, business correspondence, business cases, and procedure manuals.</li>
<li>Advanced skills in the preparation and delivery of Power Point presentations to clients, colleagues and/or senior management when needed.</li>
<li>Exceptional analytical and problem solving skills.</li>
<li>Senior professional with negotiations, leadership and executive presence</li>
<li>Advance business acumens</li>
<li>Ability to mentor, coach and train junior team members</li>
<li>Delegate and monitor/supervise support staff to maintain high level of customer service excellence</li>
<li>Available for customer and/or business travel as required.</li>
</ul>
