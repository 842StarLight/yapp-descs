<h2>Estimator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>ESTIMATOR</b></p>
<p><b>Classification: </b>Salary- Exempt</p>
<p><b>Reports to: </b>Production Services Manager</p>
<p><b>SUMMARY OF POSITION: </b></p>
<p>This role will gather manufacturing job specifications information provided by corresponding departments and create cost estimate, and price quote, for jobs produced in-house, as well as jobs produced at outside sources. The Estimator will coordinate with material vendors, and outside finishing vendors to provide cost and timing information to corresponding departments. The Estimator converts estimated jobs in the MIS database ensuring high accuracy to enable the Project Management to oversee the next production steps.</p>
<p>DCG ONE uses a Management Information System supported by “efi” called Pace Print Management System. Technical aptitude and ability to quickly learn this software will be crucial for success.</p>
<p><b>KEY DUTIES &amp; RESPONSIBILITIES: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>The estimator must have a high level of knowledge as it pertains to the multiple production, printing and paper finishing processes/capabilities.</li>
<li>Ability to configure efficient press form layouts when necessary</li>
<li>Communicates with outside vendors using proper material specifications</li>
<li>Works with sales, project management and production to maintain successful production methods</li>
<li>Create ultimate client satisfaction mentality and approach</li>
<li>Have a high level of organization and understanding multiple print and paper finishing production processes</li>
<li>Possess a proactive approach to identify and solve problems before they arise</li>
</ul>
<p><i>The above statements are intended to describe the general nature and level of work being performed by employees in this position.This is not intended to be an exhaustive list of all responsibilities.</i></p>
