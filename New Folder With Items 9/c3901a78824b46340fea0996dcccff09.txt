<p><b>
JOB SUMMARY</b></p>
<p></p>
<p>
This position performs financial and business related research and analysis to support Pharmacy Pricing for Commercial self-funded business. Functions include but are not limited to: development of pharmacy pricing proposals for client RFPs and renewals, client reporting and modeling, preparation of forecasts and/or analysis of financial reports, records and trends; develops and maintains spreadsheets and databases to facilitate analysis and reporting; and is responsible for conducting and documenting financial analysis projects.</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>
ESSENTIAL RESPONSIBILITIES</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Communicate effectively. Display effective communication skills.</li>
<li>
Coordinate with other departments, locations, and divisions.</li>
<li>
Communicate with other departments and/or outside agencies to resolve problems.</li>
<li>
Communicate with Management at Highmark, subsidiaries, affiliates, other third parties, etc. as to project status and completion deadlines.</li>
<li>
Advise management of expected outcomes, and recommending ways to improve the outcomes.</li>
<li>
Conduct presentation to all levels of management in order to provide a summary of analysis regarding financial results and trends.</li>
<li>
Communicate with Management as to project status and completion deadlines.</li>
<li>
Perform financial and business related analysis.</li>
<li>
Analyze, evaluate and interpret appropriate financial and statistical data.</li>
<li>
Develop and/or document business policies, conducts special financial and business related studies and cooperate with other departments in the preparation of analyses.</li>
<li>
Conduct financial and business related analyses and research including the preparation of forecasts and/or analysis of trends.</li>
<li>
Analyze financial reports and records.</li>
<li>
Provide reasoning for variances and make recommendations relative to the accounting of reserves, assets, and expenditures.</li>
<li>

Independently pursue fundamental problem solving and documents the recommendations to management.</li>
<li>
Facilitate analysis and reporting</li>

<li>
Develop and maintain spreadsheets and databases.</li>
<li>
Utilize PC and/or mainframe based systems and software, compile and prepare reports, graphs and charts of data developed.</li>
<li>
Serve as a project lead for special projects within the department.</li>
<li>
Lead and document financial analysis projects including the preparation of ad hoc reports; to include but not limited to; gathering of data, analysis, documentation of findings, and calculation of results and generation of reports for distribution.</li>
<li>
Coordinate activities to ensure the project is advancing properly and to ensure the project is completed within the timeframes established. Included in this process will be communication with Management at Highmark, subsidiaries, affiliates, other third parties, etc. as to project status and completion deadlines.</li>
<li>
Make recommendations based on findings when necessary.</li>
