<p><b>Job Summary:</b></p>
<p>
NerdsToGo is a cool, fast-growing brand that provides computer solutions to residential consumers and small businesses. We service every type of technology from computers, phones, tablets and printers to networks, internet connections, security and much more! Our Nerds happily provide onsite service at the customer’s location or customers can drop by our service center.</p>
<br>
<p>
The <b>Business Development Specialist</b> is responsible for marketing our services to small businesses, building brand awareness and closing sales. Building revenue is the primary goal! The successful candidate would be responsible for marketing our services to a list of small businesses, building brand awareness and closing sales. Excellent communication and time-management abilities are needed. This role is part of a franchise startup and offers a $25K-35K annual base salary, plus commission and residuals. First year expected earnings are between $50-60K. Over 1500 SMB warm leads, multiple business network memberships, insurance(health, dental, and vision), 401k(with company match), and professional financial planning &amp; advising.<br>
<br>
<b>Duties Include</b>:</p>
<ul>
<li>Demonstrated ability to create a lead list from scratch, target leads with sales and marketing activities and convert them to customers</li>
<li>Enthusiastic about building relationships with small businesses, educating them about NerdsToGo services and converting them to small business service agreements</li>
<li>Conducts lead database management including data entry and moving leads through the NerdsToGo Sales Process</li>
<li>Conducts sales and guerilla marketing activities throughout the local market</li>
<li>Plans daily route and completes Sales Tracking Sheet</li>
<li>Consistently conducts ongoing follow up activities with leads and existing small business customers</li>
<li>Attends business networking events and other community meetings to generate brand awareness and introduce NerdsToGo services</li>
<li>Great presentation skills, high energy level, exceptional prospecting skills, history of meeting sales goals, sales planning, solution selling.</li>
<li>Proven ability to close sales and convert leads to customers<br>
<br>
</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Requirements:</b><br>
Minimum three years sales or marketing experience. A positive, outgoing attitude, good PC skills and a clear speaking voice a must. Outside sales experience a MUST!<br>
</p>
