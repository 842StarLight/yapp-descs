<h2>Cybersecurity Program Manager (Senior / Executive Level)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>We are seeking an energetic and motivated Cybersecurity Program Manager (PM) to serve as a key point of contact for work performed under a large federal cybersecurity focused contract within the Department of Transportation (DOT). You will be responsible for providing project management functions including technical leadership, quality assurance, project scheduling and tracking, and decision oriented recommendations across all contract service areas. You will act as the key liaison between DOT and the delivery team, and should be experienced with CDM, NIST, FISMA, IT Audit Techniques and compliance as well as trends in privacy.</p>
<p>
The PM Performs day-to-day management of a group of highly skilled cyber experts providing complete end-to-end Information Assurance, Risk Management, and Cyber support services to secure, enhance, and support systems/applications in the governments boundaries to include large cloud environments, specifically Amazon Web Services (AWS), but potentially Microsoft Azure as well.</p>
<p>
We need a strong cybersecurity leader that will bring high-energy to oversee a growing portfolio of federal projects that range from security policy and cybersecurity strategy to advanced engineering. You'll lead operations, consult and advise executives, help drive new sales, coach and develop junior to mid-level staff, and drive initiatives to improve our client's enterprise security posture. You will also support the cybersecurity service line growth strategy, have exceptional people skills and be able to develop in-depth customer knowledge and relationships that ultimately result in new growth.</p>
<p>
This is a unique opportunity for a polished cybersecurity professional to launch their career upwards.</p>
