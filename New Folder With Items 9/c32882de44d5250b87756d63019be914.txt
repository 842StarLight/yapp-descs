<h2>Marketing Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>SUMMARY </b><br>The Marketing Specialist will partner closely with the marketing, sales and product teams to develop and manage marketing pieces which align with the overall sales objectives for the company. Qualified candidates will understand the strategies and tactics of today's marketing ecosystem and create campaigns that drive business results.</p>
<p><b>ESSENTIAL DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Understand the business needs of various departments and create messages and campaigns that meet their needs</li>
<li>Use available market data to create marketing collateral to reach specific consumer and agent groups</li>
<li>Manage the compliance process to secure the approval of advertising pieces in a timely and orderly fashion</li>
<li>Communicate changes in products, processes, etc. to ensure the sales staff is aware of changes to products and procedures</li>
<li>Create product training materials for sales use</li>
<li>Coordinate and follow-through on eblast customization requests</li>
<li>Work with IHC departments to ensure all relevant communication is updated and delivered in a timely manner and to the intended audience</li>
<li>Coordinate and track projects of the marketing team</li>
<li>Perform similar job-related duties as assigned</li>
</ul>
<p><b>SUCCESS FACTORS</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Highly professional and able to interact with C-level customers</li>
<li>Strong team player with ability to develop effective working relationships across the organization</li>
<li>Takes initiative, focusing on ways to improve processes</li>
<li>Creative, proactive, and resourceful.</li>
<li>Able to work with minimal direction and solve problems.</li>
<li>Build a collaborative/cross-functional team atmosphere</li>
<li>Organized; detail-oriented; able to consistently meet deadlines</li>
<li>Able to establish and meet both short-term and long-term goals</li>
<li>Flexible; able to make progress on multiple tasks simultaneously</li>
<li>Willingness to accept new challenges</li>
<li>Experience in the health insurance industry or a similarly regulated field is ideal</li>
</ul>
<p><b>REQUIREMENTS </b><br>To perform this job successfully, an individual must be able to perform each essential duty satisfactorily. The requirements listed below are representative of the knowledge, skill and/or ability required. Reasonable accommodations may be made to enable individuals with disabilities to perform the essential functions.</p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s Degree in Marketing, Communications or related field</li>
<li>1-3 years’ experience in marketing</li>
<li>Strengths in analytical thinking, process-minded</li>
<li>Excellent written and verbal communication skills</li>
<li>Experience with the Adobe Creative Suite tools, InDesign, Photoshop and Illustrator preferred</li>
<li>Strategic planning skills</li>
<li>Proficiency in MS Office applications (Word, Excel, Outlook, PowerPoint)</li>
</ul>
