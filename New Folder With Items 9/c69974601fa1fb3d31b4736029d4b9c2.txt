<div>Job Summary
</div>
<div><p>The Manager - Administration (Regional) ensures the efficient running of the regional administration team. The role monitors adherence to Lidl US procedures and KPIs in an effort to successfully achieve a professional, confidential, and efficiently run team. This role oversees the regional Administration Assistants, the Inventory Specialist, and the IT Specialists.
</p></div>
</div>
<div>
<div>Essential Functions
</div>
<div><ul>
<li>Implement and ensure adherence to all Lidl US regional administration procedures around system monitoring, reporting, store mail, price tickets, cash management, direct store deliveries, and inventory</li>
<li>Supervise, manage, and direct a team of one or more employees</li>
<li>Compile, check, and analyze weekly, monthly, and yearly reporting on all administration topics</li>
<li>Coordinate and control the daily, monthly, and annual accounting (booking) processes</li>
<li>Coordinate all inventory topics in collaboration with regional sales</li>
<li>Deputize the Director - Administration in department controlling, data protection, and CCTV duties</li>
<li>Analyze raw data, create reports, and present them to the Regional Distribution Center (RDC) Directors, District Managers, and Store Managers</li>
<li>Perform other duties as assigned<br>
</li>
</ul></div>
</div>
