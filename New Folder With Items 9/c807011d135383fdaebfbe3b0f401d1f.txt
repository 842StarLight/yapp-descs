<h2>Dairy Quality Assurance Director</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Monitor all production and quality functions of Red Diamond Ready to Drink Tea and other Red Diamond products at Ready to Drink Partner facilities as well as other co-manufacturing sites. Oversee highest standard of food safety in the production of Red Diamond products in partner facilities and co-manufacturing sites while remaining focused on minimizing expenses and maintaining the production schedule. Develop strategies and gain buy-in to achieve Red Diamond goals while maintaining strong relationships with partner facilities and co-manufacturing sites. Adapt to changing conditions and influence others on change, new processes and products. Work with retail sales on opportunities in the retail trade involving quality issues. Perform plant audits for other co-packed Red Diamond products when necessary. Routine paperwork and reports including monthly usage reports for RTD sites and trip reports for site visits. Position involves up to 70% travel. Flexible on location as long as home is in close proximity to major airport.</p>
<p><b>
 </b><b>Knowledge, Skills, and Abilities Required:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Working knowledge of dairy plant/beverage processing/Quality Control Programs to understand product flow, processing, packaging, shipping, and quality functions.</li>
<li>Working knowledge of FDA Food Safety Regulations and HACCP</li>
<li>Understanding of SQF/GFSI Certification and Program</li>
<li>Good oral and written communication skills with all levels in a manufacturing plant.</li>
<li>Good math skills.</li>
<li>Proficient skill with personal computer and job-related software including Microsoft Office products.</li>
<li>Detail oriented and ability to manage multiple tasks simultaneously in a deadline-oriented environment.</li>
<li>Works well in group problem solving. Communicates and influences on change effectively. Builds commitment and overcomes resistance.</li>
<li>Ability to work well under pressure.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Education and Experience Required:</b> Bachelor of Science degree with prior dairy/beverage experience in Quality Assurance and Manufacturing. Good understanding of beverage processing/filling/packaging equipment helpful. Prior co-manufacturing supervision helpful. This position requires frequent travel on a weekly basis.</p>
<p><b>
Licenses or Certifications Required</b><b>: </b>HACCP, PCQI, SQF certifications helpful/preferred.</p>
