<p><b>Job Overview:</b></p>
<p>
Exostar is looking for a Product Manager to drive our initiatives to develop and launch products that deliver information and actionable intelligence to our shareholders and customers within the Aerospace and Defense sector. The Product Manager will be responsible for evolving and broadly commercializing Exostar’s Supplier Portal, for building our master repository of A&amp;D supplier information, and for new information services to be exposed through the Supplier Portal.</p>
<p>
The successful candidate should have a strong product management experience and a background in supply chain management and analytics. This product manager role will report to the Group Product Manager - Cyber Risk, Analytics and Insights, Senior Director. The product market scope includes supply chain risk factors and, specifically, cybersecurity risk management and compliance. The industry sector is primarily Aerospace and Defense (A&amp;D).</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Your day, if you join us: Essential Job Functions</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Identifying product opportunities and product features by engaging with current and prospective customers and conducting market research dealing with addressable market size and ability to execute</li>
<li>Determining the functional and non-functional requirements and/or specifications of product features including product usability from the product user roles’ perspectives</li>
<li>Proposing and vetting synergistic technologies and business partners for fully functional customer solutions</li>
<li>Establishing the product roadmap and production timetables in conjunction with the other delivery stakeholders</li>
<li>Developing and executing go-to-market plans and preparing written content for the use in sales presentations, blogs, white papers, data sheets, competitive analysis, etc.</li>
<li>Working closely with sales and marketing on product positioning and competitive assessments</li>
<li>Being the subject matter expert for customers, industry analysts and industry conferences</li>
<li>Developing product business plans and pricing models</li>
</ul>

<p>Exostar’s cloud-based, innovative solutions promote secure business collaboration within and between organizations in highly-regulated industries, including aerospace and defense and life sciences. Exostar’s identity and access management platform serves as the foundation for its credentialing, risk management, enterprise collaboration, and supply chain collaboration solutions.</p>
<p>
The Exostar “collect once, connect once, certify once” methodology and web-based single sign-on access allow organizations and individuals to engage with one another quickly, seamlessly, and productively. The solutions’ security and certifications mitigate risk, protect sensitive data and intellectual property, and meet industry and regulatory compliance directives.</p>
<p>
Over 150,000 aerospace, defense, and life sciences organizations in more than 150 countries worldwide belong to the Exostar community.</p>
<p>
Founded in 2000 and headquartered in Herndon, VA, Exostar’s notable customers include Amgen, AstraZeneca, BAE Systems, Bell Helicopter, Boeing, Computer Sciences Corporation, Fujitsu, Huntington Ingalls Industries, Lockheed Martin, Merck, Northrop Grumman, Pfizer, Raytheon, Rolls-Royce, United Technologies Corporation (UTC), and the UK Ministry of Defence.</p>
