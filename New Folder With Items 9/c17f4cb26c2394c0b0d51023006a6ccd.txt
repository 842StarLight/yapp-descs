<h2>Quality Assurance (QA) Engineer (Temporary)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>The <b>Quality Assurance Engineer</b> works as part of the project team to release products that have been quality approved, are usable, and meet or exceed customer expectations. Is responsible for promoting quality standards, methods and practices; this includes providing input to quality planning, test case and test script creation and management, providing input for evaluations of scalable quality processes and procedures relating to the development of Web-based solutions. Able to create &amp; maintain automated testing scripts/suites leveraging either Java-based Selenium WebDriver, or JavaScripting-based Cypress.io.</p>
