<h2>Marketing Implementation Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Role</p>
<p>The Marketing Support Specialist 2 - Onboarding and Training Coordinator is a highly industrious and self-motivated individual tasked with assisting all Loan Originators—and other relevant field staff—with understanding and utilizing Academy’s marketing systems. This includes internal systems, social media, collateral, and more. This position will also help develop training curriculum and provide training to new and existing employees on Academy marketing tools.<br>Essential Functions &amp; Responsibilities</p>
<ul>
<li>Provide online and onsite training on company marketing systems on recurring and as- needed basis for new and existing employees.</li>
<li>Provide day-to-day support and training on all Academy marketing systems to Loan Officers and branch staff.</li>
<li>Providing timely updates to system structures, training materials and other internal communication material in response to org changes and marketing initiatives/ enhancements.Performance Measurements</li>
</ul>
<p>1. Demonstrates ability to effectively and efficiently communicate and educate employees.</p>
<p>2. Demonstrates ability to manage and track a potentially large number of customers at any given time.</p>
<p>3. Develops and maintains expert-level knowledge of internal marketing systems and software, and social media platforms.</p>
<p>4. Carries out assigned responsibilities in a timely manner, with minimal errors as established by Marketing Department guidelines.<br>Knowledge, Skills and Abilities</p>
<p>Experience</p>
<p>3+ years’ experience in customer service. Mortgage industry experience a plus.</p>
<p>Education</p>
<p>High school diploma or equivalent. Bachelor’s degree is desirable.</p>
<p>Interpersonal Skills</p>
<p>Work will involve a great deal of contact with members of sales and support staff for purposes of giving or obtaining information and education/training, which will require some discussion. The ability to be both patient and courteous at all times is essential.</p>
<p>Other Skills</p>
<p>Highly industrious and self-motivated. Adept with Microsoft Office products. Proficient on a Windows PC environment.</p>
<p>Solid understanding of web and browser technologies.</p>
<p>Able to quickly learn and adapt to new technologies.</p>
<p>Physical Requirements</p>
<p>Requires effective communication with employees using common telecommunications and computer equipment. Must be able to read, examine, and organize documents. May require the ability to sit, stand, and walk around for extended periods of time. May also require the ability to lift up to 25 pounds.</p>
<p>Work Environment</p>
<p>Position will often be in a busy office environment. The ability to multitask and focus in this environment is essential. Regular and predictable attendance is an essential requirement of this job.</p>

All offers are contingent on successful completion of a criminal background check and credit checks, as required by state regulation. All successful candidates must provide original documents that will establish their identity and eligibility to work in the United States as required by the Immigration Reform and Control Act.</p>
