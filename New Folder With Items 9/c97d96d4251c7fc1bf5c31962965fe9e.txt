<b>Summary:</b> Responsible for clerical functions and administrative support of food service programs.<br>
<br>
<b>Essential Duties and Responsibilities:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Answer telephones and direct inquires in a professional and client centric manner.
</li>
<li>Maintain confidential personnel files.
</li>
<li>Assist management staff in preparing confidential employment and labor relations documents, including but not limited to proposed disciplinary notices.
</li>
<li>Assist with staffing, including finding staff when employees call out on short notice.
</li>
<li>Work effectively and maintain good working relationships with co-workers, school personnel, administrators, studentâ€™s parents and Supervisor.
</li>
<li>Complete and maintain accurate and up to date records of students eligible for free and reduced priced lunches if applicable.
</li>
<li>Enter weekly cash sales and meal counts using computer.
</li>
<li>Perform daily bank deposit reconciliation.
</li>
<li>Process vendor invoices for payment - using computer, making sure addition is correct and checking item prices against bid specifications, making sure there is no discrepancy in pricing.
</li>
<li>Perform monthly vendor statement reconciliation.
</li>
<li>Prepare monthly state claim form for reimbursement.
</li>
<li>Assist in preparation of end of month financial reports.
</li>
<li>Attend in-service and/or safety meetings as required.
</li>
<li>Maintain clean and safe work environment; ability to perform job safely.
</li>
<li>Performs other duties as assigned.
