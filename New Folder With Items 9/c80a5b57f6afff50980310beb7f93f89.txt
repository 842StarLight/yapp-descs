<p>
As a Sales Development Representative, you are energetic, motivated, and articulate.</p>

<p><b>
The Role</b></p>
<p>
As our <b>Sales Development Rep,</b> you will work closely with the <b>Account Executives</b> and our <b>Marketing</b> team to carry out the game plan for <b>small and medium businesses</b> and <b>enterprises/corporations accounts</b>. Your job will be to make the initial contact, educate the customer on our capabilities, and get them excited about our solution with the <b>final goal of arranging qualified meetings.</b></p>

Your day-to-day here</b></p>

<li>You will make several calls per day, sending emails, working with channel partners, generating interest, and qualifying prospects</li>
