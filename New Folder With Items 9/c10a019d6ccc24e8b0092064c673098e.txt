<h2>Account Executive</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Local Candidate Only (Applicants from the Bay Area will be most seriously considered, remote applicants must be able to relocate to Bay Area).</b></p>
<p><b>About Odoo</b></p>
<p>Odoo is a suite of business apps that are perfectly integrated and easy to use.</p>
<p>We give millions of companies easy access to the software they need to run and expand their business. This comes in the form of a variety of different apps, from CRM, Manufacturing, Inventory, POS, eCommerce, website development, etc. There are over 40 applications that companies use to grow their businesses!</p>
<p>Odoo is seeking to significantly expand its vibrant and innovative sales teams. Apply to work within an organization that values independence, flexibility, and personal growth.</p>
<p><b>Role Overview</b></p>
<p>The Direct Sales Team is helping customers across Latin America to streamline their business processes with Odoo. This is a sales job for people who are excited to work at the intersection of software and business. You’ll learn different management practices throughout a variety of industries and how Odoo’s diverse apps (there’s over 50!) can be used to alleviate company needs.</p>
<ul>
<li>Understand clients' business processes and suggest ways to improve efficiency and performance</li>
<li>Analyze all aspects of clients' business operations</li>
<li>Establish a tailored implementation package and negotiate its content with the customer</li>
<li>Network with a large diverse pool of customers in many regions covering different industries.</li>
<li>Contact potential customers who have shown interest in Odoo through inbound (calls, emails, product demos, and webinars)</li>
<li>Be part of the entire sales cycle from the opportunity qualification to analyzing business needs, providing product demos, presenting the best-suited implementation program, and final negotiation.</li>
<li>Manage accounts for 9 months after closing deals</li>
<li>Contact potential customers who have shown interest in Odoo through inbound and outbound leads (calls, emails, product demos, and webinars)</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Must-Have</b></p>
<ul>

<li>Previous experience in a SaaS company</li>
<li>ERP experience</li>
</ul>
