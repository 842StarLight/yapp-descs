<h2>Workers' Compensation Account Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>National retail insurance agency in Pleasanton, CA, is looking for a <b>Commercial Lines Assistant that’s ready to MOVE UP to a workers’ compensation Account Manager in our E-COMP Department. Please visit our company’s sites </b><b>www.goecomp.com</b><b> and </b><b>www.graniteins.com</b></p>
<p>
Granite Insurance Brokers with offices in Pleasanton CA and Del Mar CA specializes in providing custom commercial lines and employee benefit programs and services to America's Businesses.</p>
<p><b>
Summary of Position:</b></p>
<p>
You’ll be trained to manage an E-COMP book of business within the department. This position is responsible for delivering fast, friendly and accurate service to our workers’ compensation clients, including the following key elements:</p>
<ul>
<li>Receive and initiate client communication with in-force policy holders on a daily basis, including phone calls and emails</li>
<li>Process change requests and communicate with workers’ compensation policy holders as needed</li>
<li>Help clients who have audit and billing questions</li>
<li>Proactively manage renewals and re-marketing of accounts</li>
<li>Work closely with our Workers’ Compensation Claims Director</li>
<li>Assist customers with their workers’ compensation audits</li>
<li>Work with our processing team to make sure that renewals, cancellations and changes were processed accurately</li>
<li>Work with existing clients to ensure coverage adequacy, and answer questions regarding exposure, premiums, and coverage questions</li>
<li>Document and update agency management system using Applied EPIC and Agency’s propriety pay-go software</li>
<li>Become an expert with all things workers’ compensation</li>
<li>Provide back up for co-workers within the department, as needed</li>
</ul> <p><b>Qualifications and Skills:</b></p>
<ul>
<li> Exceptional customer service skills with attention to detail</li>
<li>Strong computer skills, PC proficiency, including MS Office Suite</li>
<li>Likes to work in a technology-levered agency</li>
<li>Strong phone contact handling skills and active listening</li>
<li>P &amp; C license required</li>
<li>Excellent multi-tasking, organizational, delegation, decision-making and time management skills</li>
<li>Outstanding verbal/written communication skills</li>
<li>Exceptional personal character, including a strong work ethic, positive attitude, and willingness to assist others at all times</li>
<li>Professional and dependable, and works well within a team environment</li>
<li>Bilingual (English/Spanish) a major plus</li>
</ul>
