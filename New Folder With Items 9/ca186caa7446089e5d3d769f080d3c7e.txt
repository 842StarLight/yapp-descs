<h2>INSIDE SALES REPRESENTATIVE</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>A growing precision machining and manufacturing company is looking for a high-energy, Inside Sales Representative who delivers sales results, and provides top level service to its customers. You have a record of performance, and you have a trail of deals to prove it. You’ll have a strong knowledge of metals and value-add processes, to be able to estimate and provide quotes, quickly to customers.</p>
<p><b>
The following duties are considered essential to the job. Qualified individuals must have the ability – with or without reasonable accommodation – to perform the following duties but not limited to:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Provides Customer Service support by processing quotes for orders and jobs to customers in a timely manner.</li>
<li>Sources vendors for outside processing requirements on parts and purchases required material.</li>
<li>Provides Customer Service support by accepting orders and jobs, reviewing information for correctness, correctly entering orders, RMAs and other data into the ERP system, verifying confirmation from Production Control and/or Engineering for delivery to ensure customer needs are met in a timely manner.</li>
<li>Provides customer support by returning customer calls, emails and checking status/completion for specific customers, and communicating with other departments to check for issues and confirm customer calls to ensure customer needs are met.</li>
<li>Provides clerical support by entering orders and processing all paperwork to the system, creating job travelers as required.</li>
<li>Schedules and performs outbound calls to current, dormant and potential customers to build trust and rapport; discovers new selling opportunities, checks status, handles any problems or issues and develops referrals to new potential customers.</li>
<li>Performs other related duties as assigned by management.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>To accomplish these tasks, you must possess the following qualifications:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Relentless, action-oriented focus on moving into, up, down, and through accounts</li>
<li>3+ years of proven sales experience (with revenue generation) in related industries</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>
<i>Connected </i><b><i>– Existing customer relationships</i></b><i> </i>in any of the previously mentioned industry segments</li>
<li>Practical – Demonstration of selling techniques and presentation skills, as well as successful completion of interview and background check</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Success traits for our culture and goals:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Hands-on – Do-what-it-takes experience in a machining and manufacturing environment; experience as a machinist, application engineer, tooling sales person, or similar role would enhance your ability to win relationships</li>
<li>Collaborative – Organized and dedicated to coordinating sales approaches with our team</li>
<li>Competent – Ability to read blueprints and CAD drawings, plus understanding their geometric dimensioning and tolerancing, knowledge of materials and machining processes, and knowledge of assembly and fabrication would be very useful.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>WHAT WE BRING TO YOU</b></p>
<ul>
<li>A can-do, engineering and manufacturing culture supporting your entrepreneurial sales efforts</li>
<li>Active marketing and advertising</li>
<li>A family culture where stakeholders matter – employees, customers, suppliers, and owners</li>
<li>Competitive base salary and generous commission program</li>
<li>Benefit package – health insurance, a 401(k) plan, and education reimbursement.</li>
</ul>
