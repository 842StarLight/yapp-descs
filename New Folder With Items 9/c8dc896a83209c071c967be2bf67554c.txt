<h2>Merchandise Analyst - Williams Sonoma</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p><b>Williams-Sonoma, Inc.'s eCommerce team is looking for a Merchandise Analyst! </b>The right person will have at least 1 year experience within eCommerce or merchandising.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
You're excited about this opportunity because you will...</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Review plans and eCommerce merchandising goals; execute website merchandise strategies to achieve sales and gross margin goals</li>
<li>Ensure brand marketing initiatives are in line with merchandising strategy</li>
<li>Assist in the development, maintenance, and execution of seasonal plans for the channel</li>
<li>Assist in the development, maintenance, and execution of all new products launches on site</li>
<li>Partner with Merchants, Web Operations, and Creative teams to ensure that product is set up accurately and maintained appropriately</li>
<li>Participate in weekly site testing in order to ensure correct presentation and product data integrity</li>
<li>Assist Site Merchandisers with on-site search optimization</li>
<li>Partner with Inventory Management to determine product availability needs and sale and clearance strategies</li>
<li>Assist in eCommerce photography request process</li>
<li>Analyze eCommerce sales to identify opportunities for the channel. Communicate opportunities and findings to cross channel partners</li>
<li>Analyze site metrics to connect web behavioral profiles to customer, sales, and merchandising data</li>
<li>Partner with analytics team to develop more comprehensive tracking and analysis</li>
<li>Perform and maintain competitive website analysis, keep abreast of market trends and eCommerce consumer trends, customer service issues, and eCommerce technology and dynamics</li>
<br>
</ul>

<p><b>About Williams-Sonoma, Inc.
</b></p>
<p>Founded in 1956, Williams-Sonoma, Inc. is the premier specialty retailer of high-quality products for the home. Our family of brands includes Williams Sonoma, Pottery Barn, Pottery Barn Kids, PBteen, West Elm, Williams-Sonoma Home, Rejuvenation, and Mark and Graham. These brands are among the best known and most respected in the industry. We offer beautifully-designed, stylish and functional products for every area of the home, including the kitchen, living room, bedroom, home office, closet, laundry room, and even outdoor spaces. We've seen some big changes since our first brick-and-mortar store opened more than half of a century ago. What hasn't changed is our passion for high-quality products, functional design, outstanding customer service, and enhancing the lives of our customers and the communities where we operate. In 2017, we acquired Outward, Inc., a 3-D imaging and augmented reality platform for the home furnishings and d√©cor industry. Today, we're a multi-brand, multi-channel, global enterprise supported by state-of-the-art technology and some of the most talented teams in retailing - and we're always looking for new energy and ideas.</p>
