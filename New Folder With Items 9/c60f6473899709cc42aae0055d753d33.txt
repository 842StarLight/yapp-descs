<h2>Credit/Collections Analyst</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>MEI - Total Elevator Solutions is currently seeking motivated and qualified candidates to join our team as a Credit/Collections Analyst in our Accounting Department.</p>
<p>If you are interested in a new and exciting opportunity with a growing company, we invite you to visit our website at www.meiusa.com and apply.</p>
<p>This position is responsible for managing the delinquent Accounts Receivable balances of the Company, providing administration support for the Credit/Collections Department and assisting in the protection of the Companies financial risks and opportunities.</p>
<p>Position Responsibilities:</p>
<ul><li>Responsible for initiating calls to customers to collect past due payments and settle account questions. Business to Business and Business to Consumer collection required.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Recommend policies and procedure improvements aimed at reducing DSO and limiting bad debt write offs.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Responsible for directing difficult collection situations to management in a timely and professional manner.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Research and examine credit risks, assess credit histories of new and existing customers, and approve or disapprove extension of credit within approved credit authority guidelines.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Work with the Accounting, Sales, and Administrative personnel/departments to reconcile and resolve any discrepancies found in customer balances owed, and to mediate any disputes customers may have regarding their account.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Facilitate creation and distribution of internal and external collection letters, reports and other information on a regular basis.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Cross-training to provide back up for certain accounting processes including cash application.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Process credit card payments.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Follow established company and departmental precautions and procedures in the performance of all job duties to ensure a safe work environment for self and others.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Demonstrate respect and regard for customers and coworkers to ensure a professional, responsive and courteous environment at MEI.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Participate as a team member by performing additional assignments to meet organizational goals as noted and observed by supervisor and coworkers.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Participate in self-development activities to enhance job performance either through formal means (classes, workshops, seminars, in-services, etc.) or less structured means (reading, networking, etc.)</li></ul>
<ul><li>Follow company guidelines regarding punctuality and attendance in order to ensure a smooth working environment for department.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Actively participate in the MEI I-power Program by attending weekly meetings and submit ideas to innovate and optimize MEI products and service.</li></ul>
<p>Supervisory Responsibilities: None.</p>
<p>Qualifications:</p>
<ul><li>Education/Experience – Credit Management Certification from a college or technical school, related Associates Degree preferred, 3+ years related experience and/or training; or equivalent combination of education and experience. Microsoft Office proficiency a must (Excel, Outlook, Word, PowerPoint).</li></ul>
<ul><li>Language Skills – ability to read, analyze and interpret common scientific and technical journals, financial reports and legal documents. Ability to respond to common inquiries or complaints from customers, regulatory agencies or members of the business community. Ability to write speeches and articles for publication that conform to prescribed style and format. Ability to effectively present information to top management, public groups and/or boards of directors.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Mathematical Skills – Ability to calculate figures and amounts such as discounts, interest, commissions, proportions, percentages, area, circumference and volume. Ability to apply basic concepts of algebra and geometry.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Reasoning Ability – Ability to define problems, collect data, establish facts, and draw valid conclusions. Ability to interpret an extensive variety of technical instructions in mathematical or diagram form and deal with several abstract and concrete variables. Ability to listen and respond professionally to upset customers.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Other Skills/Certificates/Licenses and Abilities – CCE – Certified Credit Executive from National Association of Credit Management, ability to work with integrated accounting software, knowledge of third-party vendor systems a plus (Textura, Ariba, Taulia, WAWF, Tungsten, GCPay, etc.).</li></ul>
<ul><li>Physical demands – Position requires approximately 70% sitting, 15% walking and 15% standing. The ability to use hands to finger, handle or feel and reach with hands and arms on a consistent basis is required. The ability to express or exchange ideas by means of spoken words and have clear vision at 20 inches or less and the ability to identify and distinguish colors is required. The ability to lift up to 25 pounds on an occasional basis is required. Jobholder is not exposed to any environmental conditions. The position is exposed to moderate noise (e.g. business office machines such as computers, printers and light traffic).</li></ul>
<p>We offer a competitive salary and benefits package including health, dental and vision insurance, disability insurance, life insurance, 401K, vacation days and the use of an onsite fitness facility. If you would like an excellent job opportunity with a solid and flexible organization; please submit your resume today.</p>
