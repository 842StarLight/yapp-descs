 Description
<p>The <b>Content Manager</b> is responsible for the production, enhancement, and maintenance of web sites that support the MMH Pharma Sciences business-to-business publishing brands. Ensures that sites are focused around the needs of the audience and advertisers.</p>
 Position Requirements
<p><b>Responsibilities:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Responsible for production of the Pharma Sciences brand sites using Drupal, Alfresco, and other content management systems and tools.</li>
<li>Is the authority on Drupal, Alfresco, and other systems. Provides training, troubleshoots, and answers questions.
</li>
<li>Acts as lead support contact for editors, other staff, and vendors.</li>
<li>Documents and improves production processes.</li>
<li>Works on tasks such as updating content, building landing pages, creating forms, exporting data, configuring blocks, improving usability, organization, and navigation.</li>
<li>Coordinates closely with Product Manager as well as Editorial, Sales and other stakeholders.</li>
<li>Participates with larger Product Team and supports releases.</li>
<li>Creates test plans and participates in cross-browser testing of websites and digital products.</li>
<li>Maintains some existing digital Products.</li>
<li>Compiles and reviews web analytics reporting; recommends improvements.</li>
<li>Stays current with tech industry trends and learns new tools and software as needed.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Qualifications:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>1-3 years’ experience in production and maintenance of websites with a content management system such as Drupal or WordPress. Drupal experience a plus.</li>
<li>Basic HTML. CSS and image editing software a plus.
</li>
<li>A basic understanding of Web usability, taxonomy, SEO.</li>
<li>Ideally some knowledge of Web analytics (Adobe, Google Analytics, similar)</li>
<li>Ideally some publishing knowledge</li>
<li>Experience with bug tracking and project management tools a plus.</li>
<li>Detailed-oriented and able to communicate effectively with technical and non-technical staff, be a quick learner and work independently.</li>
<li>Ability to handle multiple projects simultaneously and work in a dynamic environment where priorities can change often.</li>
</ul>
