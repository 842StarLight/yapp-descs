<div><b>Position Overview</b></div>
<div>
<p>The Account Manager is responsible for the sale of Company products and services through developing and maintaining customer contacts within an assigned territory or area of responsibility.</p>
<p>
Works under general direction of the GMO/ Sales Manager; exercises discretion and judgment on work priority on a regular basis; and a significant degree of creativity is expected.</p>
</div>
</div>
</div>
<br><div>
<p><b>Principal Duties / Responsibilities</b></p>
<div><div><ul>
<li>Services and maintains positive relationships with current customer base.</li>
<li>Identify customers' needs and solve problems</li>
<li>Understand and perform cost savings analysis for customers</li>
<li>Generates new sales by promoting product line to new and existing customers.</li>
<li>Troubleshoots problems for customer.</li>
<li>Monitors sales market in assigned region.</li>
<li>Prepares various reports as required.</li>
<li>Performs other duties as required.</li>
</ul></div></div>
