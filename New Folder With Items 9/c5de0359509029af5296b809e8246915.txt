<h2>Project Finance Assistant I</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>What We're Looking For
</b></h2>Performs a variety of routine and some semi-routine project finance activities in accordance with standard procedures in project accounting and project controls to support contracting, work planning, and delivery of projects. Coordinates with other departments and/or outside agencies to resolve problems. This is an entry level position that receives direct guidance on activities.
<p></p>
<p>Primary Focus Will Include:
</p>
<ul>
<li>Manages subconsultant billing by reviewing, budgeting, processing, coding, and tracking invoices as well as payments.
</li>
<li>Financial reporting related to items such as project performance, sales, backlog, MWBE utilization, and other contractual reporting as needed.
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p>Preferred Qualifications and Skills:
</p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelors Degree
</li>
<li>Excellent communication skills (written and verbal)
</li>
<li>Basic Microsoft Excel and Word skills
</li>
<li>Attention to detail and organizational time management skills, ability to prioritize effectively
</li>
<li>Experience with Microsoft Dynamics AX preferred but not required
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>What You'll Need
</b></h2>
<p>Essential Responsibilities:
</p>
<ul>
<li>Transfers quotations into project budgets and assists with routine maintenance of project budgets in project accounting system, including but not limited to project/task status updates, project contract information, project assignee adjustments, and fee adjustments.
</li>
<li>Provides various financial reporting to various project owners on a daily, weekly, monthly and quarterly basis as established with project teams.
</li>
<li>Performs labor corrections and accounts payable adjustments as identified by the local offices or the Enterprise Operations Cash Management group.
</li>
<li>Generates, stores, distributes, files, maintains, and updates ad hoc project accounting reports, documents, etc.
</li>
<li>Performs baseline and periodic updates to gross margin calculations with information provided by the project team. Provide system generated or system based financial information related to project and client profitability, payment histories, sales amounts, and other project accounting data as needed.
</li>
<li>Codes project costs, such items may include subconsultant costs, expense report receipts, vendor invoices etc., with appropriate project accounting codes conforming to standard procedures and contractual requirements to ensure proper entry into HNTB's financial system.
</li>
<li>Prepares or updates billing notes based on feedback from the project team. As appropriate, provides an additional math check and back up information confirmation related to the billing (accounts receivable) portion of the monthly invoice preparation process.
</li>
<li>Assists with monthly (or as needed) DBE and subconsultant reporting, other contractual reporting, insurance certificates, project filings, etc.
</li>
<li>Performs other duties as assigned.
</li>
</ul>
