<p><b>SAP Data Architect (MDG, BODS) - Senior Consultant</b></p>
<p></p>
<br>
<div>
Are you looking for a career trajectory that few other organizations can offer in SAP? What about access to a high-quality portfolio of businesses and clients with opportunities to work across capabilities, industries, and geographies – in roles and on projects aligned with your SAP Technology skills? If this matches your interests and aspirations, Deloitte may be the right fit for you.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
<b>
Work you’ll do</b><br>
</div>
<ul>
<li>
Experience in working with the client stakeholders/vendor to design and configure new API enhancements.</li>
<li>
Hands-on experience of SAP Cloud Platform - API management for end to end lifecycle from build, mask, publish and monetize of an API.</li>
<li>
Strong knowledge on usage of standard policies, policy flows and policy templates from API Business Hub.</li>
<li>
Experience to build API specification for developer portal with OpenAPI specification model.</li>
<li>
Demonstrable ability to connecting and mashup of multiple endpoints from heterogeneous landscape.</li>
<li>
Extend solutions with additional SAP Cloud Platform capabilities for mobile, offline and integration.</li>
<li>
Simplify managed API’s and collaborations with Customers.</li>
<li>
Experience using API Designer and OpenAPIs to create an omni-channel mobile experience across devices.</li>
<li>
Solve technical issues and identify modifications needed in existing APIs to meet changing user requirements.</li>
<li>
Provide technical expertise and recommendations in assessing new APIs and initiatives to support and enhance the existing systems.</li>
<li>
Assist in managing relationships for 3rd Party application Vendors.</li>
</ul>

<div><b>
Team</b></div>
<p></p>
<div>
<br>
Our SAP team delivers large-scale software applications and integrated systems, and assists clients with architecture design, assessment and optimization, and definition. We develop service-oriented architecture (SOA) and other integration solutions to enable information sharing and management between business partners and disparate processes and systems, and we deliver technology enablement to support CIO services transformation. We work with clients who want to improve their ability to react on digital world and enable scalability, remove performance barriers, enable innovation, and modernize core systems and reimagine their business with SAP digital solutions. Applications include SAP S/4HANA and Cloud (Hybris, Ariba, IBP, SuccessFactors, and SAP S/4HANA Public Cloud) across the following capabilities:</div>

<li><div>
<br>
 Finance</div></li>

 Supply Chain</div></li>
<li><div>
 Customer and Sales Operations</div></li>

 Technology (Mobility and User Experience, Cloud and Virtualization, Data Management and Analytics)<br>
</div></li>
</ul>

<div><b>
Qualifications</b></div>
<ul>
<li><div>
Functional/technical proficiency with at least 2+ full life cycle SAP implementations and 4+ years of industry and/or consulting experience</div></li>
<li><div>
At least 4 years of SAP experience with SAP Data Architecture and SAP MDG</div></li>
<li><div>
Master level functional depth of customer, vendor, product/material and finance master data in S/4</div></li>
<li><div>
S/4 data architecture and data modeling experience</div></li>
<li><div>
S/4 enterprise data hub architecture or implementation experience</div></li>
<li><div>
SAP Data Services architect and data conversion and implementation experience</div></li>
<li><div>
SAP Information Steward architect and data quality leadership and implementation experience</div></li>
<li><div>
Data cleansing implementation experience</div></li>
<li><div>
Experience defining systems strategy, developing systems requirements, designing and prototyping, testing, training, defining support procedures and implementing practical business solutions under multiple deadlines</div></li>
<li><div>
Adept at implementing technology-enabled business solutions for clients as part of a high-talent team and as a Team Lead or Project Manager on at least 1+ full cycle implementation</div></li>

Able to collaborate with clients and have a strong desire to excel</div></li>
