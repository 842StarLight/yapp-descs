<h2>Senior Channel Operations Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Mitel is a global market leader in business communications, powering more than two billion business connections with our cloud, enterprise and next-gen collaboration applications.<br>
<br>
With more than 70 million users in nearly 100 countries, Mitel is the only company that wakes up every day exclusively focused on helping customers take their communications from where they are today to where they expect them to be.<br>
<br>
Mitel is looking for an experienced Senior Channel Operations Manager to join our team!</p>
<p>
The Channel Operations Manager will play an integral role in driving the growth, expansion, and support of our channel team. The role will also interact with the internal Channel teams and channel partners as necessary to facilitate the operational processes. The position will support tracking performance of partners, Partner managers and their goals and objectives. The ideal candidate will be well versed in channel and operations methodologies.<br>
<br>
Responsibilities include:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Lead Business Intelligence resource responsible for disseminating channel sales data to the Mitel field, CAMs, channel leaders, and Sales Operations teams for their use in business planning and quota tracking efforts. Report, analyze and maintain dashboards in SFDC, Excel and other tools for channel sales programs and overall reseller performance and provide insights to help grow the business.</li>
<li>
Assist with process around channel transactions</li>
<li>
Work directly with partner programs team, channel sales team and managers and channel partners to help execute and track innovative joint programs that engage partnersâ€™ customers, build awareness, generate qualified leads, and drive new business.</li>
<li>
Work with channel sales managers to understand and provide support with overall territory and reseller planning to ensure optimized activity to grow the business.</li>
<li>
Provide assistance on Back End Credit auditing, POS submission tracking, corporate-level projects</li>
</ul>
<p>
Requirements:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Bachelors degree, or equivalent years of experience</li>
<li>
Have excellent Excel skills, SAP Business Intelligence skills preferred, Salesforce skills preferred</li>
<li>
5 to 8 years of prior channel/sales operations, reseller channel and/or distribution experience</li>
<li>
Possess excellent verbal and written communication skills</li>
<li>
Attention to detail and drive to deliver consistently high quality outputs</li>
</ul>
