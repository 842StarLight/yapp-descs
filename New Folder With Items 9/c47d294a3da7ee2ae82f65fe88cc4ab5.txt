<h2>Commercial Relationship Manager Assistant (N3)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p>Under general supervision of the Commercial Relationship Managers, provide diversified support to the initiation, growth and management of commercial loan accounts. Prepare, update and distribute reports for management as requested. Organize and coordinate meetings, events and conferences for business banking personnel. Provide general administrative support such as sorting and handling of daily correspondence, scheduling meetings, answering phones and by providing notary services.</p>
<div>
<div><ul>
<li>
Provide diversified support for Commercial Relationship Managers to develop and manage a portfolio of commercial accounts.</li>
<li>
Support the preparation of loan approval packages by effectively communicating with customers to acquire additional documents and information necessary to fully underwrite and decision loan requests.</li>
<li>
Review and evaluate loan packages for completeness. Follow up with customers for the timely receipt of financial statements to ensure renewals are processed as required.</li>
<li>
Complete data entry into financial analysis software of financial information obtained as part of a new loan request, renewal or review.</li>
<li>
Provide assistance to relationship managers to facilitate the timely processing of new loan requests as well as monitoring and growing existing loan portfolios.</li>
<li>
Respond to telephone callers and visitors to resolve routine and complex inquiries.</li>
<li>
Obtain and review routine reports and coordinate with the Commercial Relationship Managers on current and upcoming customer financial reporting requirements; delinquencies, overdrafts, loan maturities and periodic reviews.</li>
</ul></div>
<br>
<div><ul>
<li>
Coordinate and collaborate with others to identify, establish, facilitate and maintain effective support processes that will ensure consistency and streamline workflow.</li>
<li>
Identify, obtain and distribute reports related to the commercial loan portfolio and / or business loan requests for the Commercial Relationship Managers or management as required.</li>
<li>
Resolve administrative and loan processing concerns by analyzing issues; identify and coordinate appropriate solution</li>
<li>
Anticipate and identify business needs; initiate and participate in the development of plans to meet these needs.</li>
</ul></div>
<br>
<div><ul>
<li>
Provide general administrative support such as sorting and handling of daily correspondence and by providing notary services.</li>
<li>
Provide notary services to customers relating to the execution of loan documents as required.</li>
<li>
 Prioritize and process mail for the Commercial Relationship Managers and forward mail items to appropriate departments or staff for action.</li>
</ul></div>
<br>
<div><ul><li>
Provide outstanding customer service to internal and external customers. Foster and ensure regular communications with commercial lending staff, retail services, credit administration and support personnel. Collaborate with others to foster team building, internal process development and improvement, report preparation, problem-solving, learning, and continuing education.</li></ul></div>
<br>
<div><ul><li>
Ensure compliance with Bank Secrecy Act (BSA) regulations and other federal and state regulations pertinent to this position. Complete annual BSA training in a timely manner in accordance with BSA regulations and internal policies and procedures.</li></ul></div>
<br>
<div><ul><li>
Maintain a sound knowledge of Association policies, procedures, documentation and compliance needs in order to effectively exercise assigned responsibilities.</li></ul></div>
<br>
<div><ul>
<li>
Maintain regular and predictable attendance.</li>
<li>
Perform related duties and responsibilities as required.</li>
</ul></div>
<br>
</div>
<p><b>
Education:</b><b>
</b></p>
<p>High school diploma or certified equivalency. An associate's degree in a business related curriculum or the equivalent preferred.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Experience:</b><b> </b>Typically requires three (3) years of demonstrated successful experience providing advanced and diversified support to commercial lending staff and management personnel.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Knowledge, Skills &amp; Abilities:</b><b>
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Thorough understanding of the structure of financial statements and the relationship between income statement and balance sheets is expected.</li>
<li>
Develop and maintain a working knowledge of First Federal policies, procedures, and systems.</li>
<li>
Working knowledge of commercial lending processes and procedures including commercial loan processing and documentation requirements.</li>
<li>
Broad knowledge of administrative and office management practices and procedures.</li>
<li>
Highly effective verbal and written communication, organizational, and planning skills are essential.</li>
<li>
Ability to multi-task and handle inquiries from of a wide variety of sources and direct the performance of multiple sources simultaneously.</li>
<li>
Must have the ability to deal with confidential and highly sensitive situations with discretion, tact, and diplomacy.</li>
<li>
Must be able to effectively elicit cooperation and support from others, and coordinate the work and efforts of others.</li>
<li>
Demonstrated skill and proficiency in the use of personal computers and the Microsoft Suite of software programs (MS Word, Excel, Access, Visio, PowerPoint) is required.</li>
<li>
A valid Washington State license is required.</li>
</ul>

<p><b>
Physical Requirements:</b></p>
<p>
The essential functions of this position require the ability to lift and carry up to 40 pounds. Boxes and supplies may be lifted on and off counters, tables, desks, and shelves. The work requires the ability to operate office machines and equipment, such as personal computers, printers, copying machines, fax machines, calculators, and telephones. The work requires the ability to communicate clearly with customers, coworkers, and others in person and on telephones. Work activities involve combinations of sitting/standing for extended periods, lifting, and carrying.</p>
