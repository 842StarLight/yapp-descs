<h2>Marketing Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Overview</b></p>
<p>Tyto Athene is a full service integrator focused on helping clients to accelerate their ability to make decisions by providing ubiquitous and secure access to enterprise information throughout their operating environment. Tyto Athene uses a myriad of technologies, innovative thinking, and proven processes to deliver successful outcomes for its clients worldwide.</p>
<p></p>
<p>Tyto is looking for a marketing specialist to join our team. This position will be responsible for implementing our digital marketing strategy and assisting the team with event planning, partner relationships and graphic design.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Responsible for the execution of Tyto’s Digital Marketing strategy, including Social Media, website, SEO, email marketing, and digital advertising</li>
<li>Create and publish engaging content on social media: Facebook, Linkedin, and Twitter</li>
<li>Build and maintain Digital Marketing content calendar</li>
<li>Engage with customers, partners, employees, to increase followers on multiple social media platforms</li>
<li>Create and publish relevant content to company blog</li>
<li>Analyze and improve website performance using Google Analytics</li>
<li>Support Marketing Manager in identifying best practice organic SEO strategies</li>
<li>Guide paid search strategy including monitoring PPC/AdWords campaigns</li>
<li>Create quarterly reports on Digital Marketing performance for review by Marketing Manager and Director</li>
<li>Provide support to all graphic and design projects as directed by Marketing Manager and Director</li>
<li>Create custom imagery for website, Social Media, and Digital Marketing adhering to Tyto brand standards</li>
<li>Assist in establishing the company's brand by maintaining consistent branded templates and marketing collateral including: PowerPoints, pdfs, print materials, targeted materials for customers / events</li>
<li>Help oversee internal communications campaigns including Human Resource initiatives</li>
<li>Collaborate with marketing team and other departments to establish sales promotions to current customers and new accounts</li>
<li>Assist sales leaders with reporting</li>
<li>Fulfill requests from Account Executives, Senior Sales Managers and Executives for assistance with presentations and custom documents.</li>
<li>Draft and edit press releases</li>
<li>Plan and execute events &amp; trade shows in support of the sales team and executive staff</li>
<li>Support the sales team in event follow-up</li>
<li>Co-Chair annual charity event – including fundraising</li>
<li>Attend networking events to promote Tyto Athene to customers and partners.</li>
<li>Work with Marketing Director to coordinate internal training</li>
<li>Establish professional working relationships with OEM partners</li>
<li>Work with strategic partners to ensure compliance with partner agreements</li>
<li>Track and coordinate OEM partner training, including scheduling on-site training and required OEM partner training to maintain partner status</li>
<li>Work with OEM partners to maximize co-op dollars to fund joint initiatives</li>
<li>Engage with OEM partners to resolve issues and explore opportunities</li>
<li>Collaborate with OEM partner marketing organizations on joint partner activities</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Qualifications:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree required</li>
<li>Graphic design experience a plus</li>
<li>Experience with SEO tools (Moz, Google Search Console) and Google Analytics</li>
<li>Experience with Google Ads, Google Ads-Editor</li>
<li>Experience with Email Marketing tools (Campaign Monitor, Mail Chimp, Constant Contact)</li>
<li>Comfortable working in CMS (Wordpress) to manage website content</li>
<li>Experience in managing Social Media Platforms and Social monitoring tools (Hootsuite, Sprout Social)</li>
<li>Experience in Adobe Suite (Photoshop, Illustrator, Acrobat)</li>
<li>Strong writing, editing, and ability to synthesize data in a written narrative</li>
<li>Able to provide design portfolio upon request</li>
<li>Strong interpersonal skills to effectively work with, communicate and collaborate with diverse teams</li>
<li>Strong computers skills with the ability to quickly learn new software applications</li>
<li>Must be able to perform with minimal supervision</li>
<li>Excellent written and oral communication skills</li>
<li>Marketing tactics including content marketing, social media, advertising, public relations, research, event planning and trade shows</li>
<li>Excellent organizational skills</li>
<li>Ability to adapt to changing priorities and manage multiple tasks</li>
<li>Flexibility in work hours, as some activities occur before/after normal working hours</li>
<li>Travel – up to 25%</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p>Equal Opportunity Employer: Disability/Veteran</p>
