<h2>Receiving & Returns Clerk</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>GENERAL SUMMARY</b></p>
<p>
We currently have a need for a Receiving &amp; Returns Clerk on 1st shift. This position will report to the 1st Shift Supervisor and be responsible for assisting with the day to day running of the department by carrying out the duties as identified below. A person in this job will interface with customers, customer service, and follow-up to see that orders in your area of responsibility are shipped in a timely manner.</p>
<p>
Team members are also responsible for learning and following all Standard Operating Procedures including safety rules. Productivity is measured and work must be performed within set quality goals. Team members must meet all reasonable standards for quality, productivity and safety.</p>
<p><b><i>
All Warehouse Associates will demonstrate behaviors consistent with Legacy Supply Chain Services values-based culture.</i></b></p>
<p><b>
ESSENTIAL JOB FUNCTIONS</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Perform task Safely</li>
<li>Complete Monthly OSHA training</li>
<li>Ensure area Housekeeping requirements are met</li>
<li>Assist Business Unit Manager and Lead in setting daily plan</li>
<li>Back Up Business Unit Manager and Receiving Lead and Customer service Questions from GE and Customers</li>
<li>Work Reports including but not limited to ROR / Suspense Screens, In Transit reports, problem area</li>
<li>Oracle Receipts</li>
<li>Supply ROs to the Receiving team</li>
<li>Communicate to customers and Customer Service when processing returns and lost shipments</li>
<li>Report any needs of the Business Unit to the Business Unit Manager and/or Lead</li>
<li>Cross Train to other Positions in the Business Unit to include all functions in the receiving Business Unit</li>
<li>Help cover for any absence in the Business Unit</li>
<li>Assist Business Unit Manager and Lead in achieving KPIs</li>
<li>Assist Business Unit Manager and Lead in meeting Daily Service Requirements</li>
<li>Other assignments and special projects as assigned by the Business Unit Manager or Lead</li>
<li>Perform all work in the Business Unit</li>
<li>Training of Employees in the Business Unit</li>
<li>Assist inventory with location and receipt of Product</li>
<li>Help to Maintain WMS Trailer Log report</li>
<li>Update the GE plant scorecard, workflow as required</li>
<li>Send out daily / weekly variance, quality files to the plants.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>WMS Receiving
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Enter new flag in WMS and SAP (New catalog)</li>
<li>Break down, sorting and verifying</li>
<li>Receive product in WMS CRT</li>
<li>Restack product, take quality pictures, and relabeling incorrect material</li>
<li>Unload trailers</li>
<li>Update the trailer information</li>
<li>Locate miss placed material in warehouse</li>
<li>Search for hot parts for customer service GE</li>
<li>Cleaning Assignment</li>
<li>Help team with any receiving issues</li>
<li>Physically stage each cross-dock shipment in the Shipping (outbound) area. The appropriate staging lane for cross -dock shipments in lane SH21081</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Processing returns:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Research sales orders (SAP)</li>
<li>Display sales orders (SAP)</li>
<li>Display Outbound (SAP)</li>
<li>Verified and inspect all forms, reports and records relating to product returns</li>
<li>Maintain and manage a database of all product returns</li>
<li>Send email for every return to Valerie Carter to obtain an RGA numbers</li>
<li>Deliver all returns with an RGA number to RGA department</li>
<li>Track the shipments with all carries to why was the shipment not deliver.</li>
<li>Call customers to conform the correct address for all unable deliveries from a carrier. (Returns with incorrect address)</li>
<li>Reship all incorrect address returns every day</li>
<li>Blind load undamaged product into WMS</li>
<li>Research for lost shipments</li>
<li>Investigation returns.</li>
<li>Post Shipment. Locate all lost shipments.</li>
</ul> <p><b>SAP Processing:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Create inbound delivery for outside vendors</li>
<li>Look up inbound.</li>
<li>Complete GR for PO reversal 102 movement</li>
<li>Read and respond to e-mails</li>
</ul>

<li>Strong communication skills; timely, accurate and professional</li>
<li>Well organized; flexible</li>
</ul>
