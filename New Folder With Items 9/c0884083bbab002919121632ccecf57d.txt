<h2>Automotive General Service Technician</h2>

If you believe inEmpowering Ownership, Giving Legendary Guest Service and are committed toOperational Excellence, we want you to join our team! <p><b>AutomotiveGeneral Service Technician Responsibilities: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Demonstrates good customer service skills.</li>
<li>Drive vehicles with automatic and manual transmissions.</li>
<li>Perform oil changes, lubricate chassis, check, fill, and replace various fluids/filters, Services customer's vehicles in inspecting, troubleshooting, servicing and replacing items such as, batteries, belts, hoses, lights, fuses, preventative maintenance, etc.</li>
<li>Parking lot, bay area navigation and vehicle safety.</li>
<li>Write work orders.</li>
<li>Tires -- Definitions, sizing, sidewall markings, inspection, repair, installation, balancing, inventory, tire ancillary services, TPM, Inflate tires.</li>
<li>VIP, vehicle inspections, Workflow process, parking lot, bay area navigation, work orders.</li>
<li>Follow all shop safety policies and procedures.</li>
<li>Lifting and lowering best practices.</li>
<li>Look up information for various applications.</li>
<li>Operate point of sale (NAVEX) hardware and software.</li>
<li>Prepare and inspect work orders, VIP Checklist and Vehicle Inspection Form (Health Check) for completeness.</li>
<li>Ensure compliance with OSHA and Federal and State Environmental Regulations.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>AutomotiveGeneral Service Technician Qualifications</b></p>
<ul>
<li>High school diploma or equivalency certificate is preferred.</li>
<li>Ability to speak read and write English is preferred.</li>
<li>Ability to read and interpret documents such as safety rules, operating and maintenance instructions, and procedure manuals.</li>
<li>Ability to speak effectively with customers and employees.</li>
<li>Good computer skills. Ability to use the company hardware and software.</li>
<li>While performing the duties of this job the employee is regularly required to stand; walk; use hands and fingers, handle, or feel; reach with hands and arms; work in awkward positions; transfer oneself from one work level to another; and move materials from one height to another.</li>
</ul>
