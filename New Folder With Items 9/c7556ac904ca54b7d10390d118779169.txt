<h2>Educational Software Regional Account Manager - Entry Level</h2>

<p>WIN Learning is the leading provider of career readiness solutions that help districts prepare pathways for students’ futures, whether they are college, trade school, military or workplace bound. <p>We are seeking an energetic, entrepreneurial Regional Account Manager to focus on product sales and customer satisfaction in a geographic territory. <ul>
<li>Attaining sales objectives and reaching profit objectives</li>
<li>Working closely with customers and potential customers in all facets of the sales and implementation process for our products</li>
<li>Helping to develop and implement regional and local marketing promotions and plans</li>
<li>Collecting, interpreting and providing market, customer contact and competitive information</li>
</ul>
<p>Ideal candidates will have:</p>
<ul>
<li>BA/BS degree, Business or Education degrees will be a real plus</li>
<li>Sales and customer service abilities or aptitudes</li>
<li>Demonstrated drive for success and high achievement</li>
<li>Strong communications abilities - both verbal and writing</li>
<li>Ability to establish rapport and work effectively with others</li>
<li>Willingness to travel extensively within the assigned geographic territory</li>
</ul>
<p>Our competitive compensation package includes base salary, commission plan and benefits.</p>
