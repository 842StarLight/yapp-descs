<p><b>PROGUARD WARRANTY, INC. (Texas)</b><br>
<br>
Automotive consumer coverage provider offering a comprehensive menu of fully insured new and pre-owned protection products is seeking a sales representative to support our rapidly growing enterprise. ProGuard Warranty is a family-owned business with over 70 years of automotive experience and core values promoting a culture built on transparency, flexibility and accessibility. With a focus on innovation and industry-leading customer service, ProGuard aspires to be the partner of choice for F&amp;I products as we expand our reach across the country. We are currently looking for a motivated sales representative to market our products and services to prospective independent and franchise automobile dealers. The ideal candidate will be a motivated self-starter who will drive new business, manage existing accounts, and build a territory. We offer training, cutting-edge sales tools and innovative technology that will contribute to your success. This opportunity offers solid earning potential, including a starting base salary, semi-monthly paid commission and monthly expense allowance.<br>
<br>
<b>Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Establish sales strategies to increase territory revenue through new account development</li>
<li>Develop and nurture relationships with new and existing dealer accounts in designated territory</li>
<li>Utilize sales process to uncover customer needs and perform presentations illustrating the benefits of vehicle protection products to dealers and customers</li>
<li>Employ effective negotiation skills to close sales and secure new business</li>
<li>Perform daily territory management and revenue growth through visits and calls to customer sites</li>
<li>Meet or exceed established monthly sales targets, new account acquisition, and retention goals</li>
<li>Complete account status reporting requirements accurately and in a timely manner</li>
<li>Follow up on commitments with dealers to ensure 100% satisfaction</li>
</ul>
