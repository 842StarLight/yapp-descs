<p><b>About us:</b></p>
<p>At Retrax, a Truck Hero company, we take pride in our reputation for providing innovative and high quality retractable truck bed covers. We love what we do and thrive on the disciplines of design, development, and manufacturing. We strive for excellence in all we do.</p>
<p>When you join our team, we offer competitive pay and benefits, an opportunity to build your career, and the chance to be part of our expanding product line-up. We welcome new employees to be part of the exciting future of Retrax!</p>
<p>As a Customer Service Representative, you will meet and exceed customer expectations in all aspects of the job. Follow established work instructions, achieve established goals, and work as a team player. Assist customers with sales and technical support.</p>
<p><b>Position Requirements:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Meet and exceed customer expectations</li>
<li>Communicate features and benefits of products to customers through various communication avenues</li>
<li>Maintain positive customer relations through professionalism and accuracy when addressing matters</li>
<li>Identify customer technical needs in an efficient manner; convey solutions in an easily interpreted manner</li>
<li>Work as a team player to address customer's sales and/or technical needs</li>
<li>Achieve established goals or deadlines set for the department</li>
<li>Ensure accuracy of customer records; update as needed</li>
<li>Complete all required paperwork and/or data entry in a timely manner</li>
<li>Maintain a safe, clean work environment; address any unsafe conditions or behaviors with a supervisor</li>
<li>Capable of lifting up to 25lbs, adhering to safe lifting techniques</li>
</ul>
