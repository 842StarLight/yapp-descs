<h2>Inside Sales Representative</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>The Inside Sales Representative will be responsible for company’s tele-sales efforts to the United States mid-market (enterprise companies with $1 bn to $3 bn annual revenue) in non-retail industries. The Inside Sales Representative is accountable for developing leads and closing opportunities with new clients for the company’s recovery audit, contract compliance, supplier collaboration, and analytics solutions.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p>The ISR will develop and grow this business through outbound lead development efforts, using a creative, motivated approach to reach PRGX’s target audience. The ideal candidate has a track record exceeding their quotas and is process-oriented. This individual is focused on hitting revenue goals.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Outreach to ideal customer profile target prospects using a mix of telephone calls, email, and other communication mediums</li>
<li>Manage the full prospect to close lifecycle (including prospecting, qualifying, and nurturing outbound leads; then converting those leads to sales qualified opportunities, negotiating contracts, and winning new business)</li>
<li>Create a favorable first impression to prospects with a friendly attitude, compelling messaging, and consistent follow up</li>
<li>Educate and develop leads to turn contacts into qualified opportunities; then win those opportunities</li>
<li>Maintain accurate and detailed lead, account, contact, opportunity, and sales activity in CRM system (Salesforce.com)</li>
<li>Gain a solid understanding of the source to pay and recovery audit landscape<br>
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Requirements</b></p>
<ul>
<li>A self-motivated, goal oriented, and customer driven sales mentality</li>
<li>A coachable and curious mindset</li>
<li>Excellent phone and presentation skills</li>
<li>Successful experience in high activity sales development, business development or inside sales for a business to business SaaS company is preferred</li>
<li>Ability to work in a fast-paced changing environment with limited supervision</li>
<li>Demonstrated ability to exceed sales goals</li>
<li>Bachelor’s degree from a four-year college or university; five to seven years related experience and/or training; or equivalent combination of education and experience</li>
<li>Familiarity with CRM software (Salesforce.com preferred)</li>
<li>US Citizen or Permanent Resident</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p>
</p>
<p><b>Additional Skills</b></p>
<p>
Knowledge of Salesforce CRM and sales prospecting tools</p>
