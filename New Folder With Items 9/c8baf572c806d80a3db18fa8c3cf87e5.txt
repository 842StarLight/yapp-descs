<h2>Associate Director, Product Management - Energy Studio</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>As Product Manager, you are responsible for understanding client workflow requirements, market needs and the competitive landscape to define the product vision, roadmap and development requirements for Energy Studio, IHSM's next generation global energy content visualization and analysis application. This position plays a critical role in supporting the revenue growth of Upstream content products by providing a solution that addresses key client workflows across market segments, customer personas and content streams. To be effective, the product manager works closely with existing and potential clients and collaborates with sales, product leads, product managers, product owners, software development, product marketing and customer care to ensure Energy Studio is a uniquely positioned, innovative and workflow-driven solution with a clearly articulated value proposition. This highly visible position requires strong customer facing skills, solid understanding of the breadth and depth of IHSM Upstream content, and a proven track translating customer workflows into a product roadmap and working with development to deliver on that roadmap.</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>
General Job Duties:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Manages product life cycle from inception through retirement</li>
<li>
Manages customer advisory group / user group meetings</li>
<li>
Assesses market/client needs and competitive positioning to define a differentiated, innovative, forward-looking product vision</li>
<li>
Develops investment business cases for management approval</li>
<li>
Works closely with clients and the broader product management organization to define customer workflows, translate workflows into product requirements and prioritize development roadmap to support current and emerging market needs</li>
<li>
Monitors customer usage to identify support requirements and help prioritize development/enhancement opportunities</li>
<li>
Defines product evolution opportunities through buy, build or partner strategies</li>
<li>
Assesses and recommends product packaging, commercial and pricing strategies to maximize value realization across customer segments</li>
<li>
Works with Product Marketing to define a value proposition that drives customer adoption of new products or enhancements to existing products</li>
<li>
Develops product overview materials for use in (internal) and sales opportunities (external)</li>
<li>
Supports and mentors customer-facing staff conducting demos and training</li>
<li>
Monitors new business and renewal pipeline to provide proactive support for sales opportunities</li>
<li>
Establishes, tracks and reports on product performance metrics</li>
<li>
Manages customer advisory group / user group meetings</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Specific Job Duties</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Act as Subject Matter Expert on products within Upstream Content business line, and how these products support customer workflows</li>
<li>
Interacts with members of the commercial, product development, sales and customer care organizations, and works directly with customers to support customer requests, gather information and resolve any issues that may arise</li>
<li>
Coordinates with other Energy product teams and IHSM BLs to exploit synergies and opportunities to share technologies and/or integration of products</li>
