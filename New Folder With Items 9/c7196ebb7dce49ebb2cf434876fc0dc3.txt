<p><b>
Position:</b> Director of Sales &amp; Marketing</p>
<p><b>
Reports To: </b>General Manager</p>
<p><b>
Summary of Position:
</b></p>
<p>Under the general guidance of the General Manager, lead and enhance the hotel’s Sales &amp; Marketing efforts in all categories to exceed revenues while being heavily involved in daily Revenue Management. Generate a high profile and quality of service in the community through involvement in local organizations while also implementing the Zenique culture for all associates and guests.</p>
<p><b>
Requirements:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Minimum high school diploma. Higher-level education preferred.</li>
<li>
3 years of sales experience in the hospitality industry with a minimum of one (1) year at the supervisory level.</li>
<li>
Familiarity with CRM platforms</li>
<li>
Willing to travel and must possess a valid driving license</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
General Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Conduct and continuously update local market research.</li>
<li>
Sell incremental room nights for the assigned property(s).</li>
<li>
Develop and execute the hotel’s marketing plan.</li>
<li>
Achieve and exceed the revenue targets for the assigned property(s).</li>
<li>
Assess the market potential and develop new business opportunities while at the same time increasing revenues from existing customers.</li>
<li>
Conduct and enhance public and community relations.</li>
<li>
Act as the Manager on Duty (MOD) when scheduled as such by the General Manager.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Primary Functions:</b></p>
<p>
Revenue:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Sales &amp; Marketing: Work with the Regional Manager of Revenue and Marketing to prepare and implement the hotel’s annual Sales and Marketing plan, make sales calls to Key accounts, potential clients, group business and capitalize on all revenue opportunities. Effectively market the hotel's amenities, services, events, and brand through digital, print, and social media outlets.</li>
<li>
Financial: Aggressively pursue revenue goals and effectively utilize yield management and revenue maximization tools.</li>
</ul>
<p>
Community Involvement:</p>
<ul><li>
Ensure good standing in the community by developing and maintaining relationships with, Chamber of Commerce, Convention and Visitors Bureau, other hotels, local schools, local government and other community and civic organizations.</li></ul>
<p>
Quality:</p>
<ul><li>
Guest Service: Work closely with the Front Office and Housekeeping staff to ensure that the hotel meets/exceeds Zenique and brand standards for guest satisfaction.</li></ul>
<p>
Leadership:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Leading: Motivate, coach and train team members, set goals and holding team members accountable, and provide feedback, rewards, and recognition.</li>
<li>
Work Ethic: Demonstrate a high level of integrity, take ownership of personal and team actions, communicate effectively, promote collaboration, nurture a positive, professional work environment, and adhere to Zenique’s Standard Operating Procedures.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Specific Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Send a daily end of the day activity and accomplishment email to the General Manager (GM), Regional Revenue and Marketing Manager (RRMM), Sales Coordinator, and a copy to the Zenique’s Regional Director of Operations (RDO).</li>
<li>
Partner with RRMM to achieve Revenue and RevPar targets for all business segments.</li>
<li>
Establish monthly sales forecasts. Keep the GM and Corporate apprised of the status of these forecasts to ensure that the property has a steady stream of business through the year.</li>
<li>
Develop and execute advertising programs within the RRMM &amp; GM guidelines and the approved budgets.</li>
<li>
Devote a minimum of one (1) hour per week researching on-brand portal sites for updates, tools, and resources that can enhance our hotel</li>
<li>
Maintain a relationship with all brand Global Sales Managers/Key Account Managers</li>
<li>
Leverage cross-sell opportunities amongst all Zenique’s properties.</li>
<li>
Maintain high visibility in the surrounding community.</li>
<li>
Maintain efficient sales office procedures for productive use of staff time and ensure the maintenance of accurate and updated account files and follow-up procedures.</li>
<li>
Ensure all sales leads are followed up.</li>
<li>
Assist in the development and recommendation of rate strategies.</li>
<li>
Ensure compliance with the sales and marketing Bonus plan.</li>
<li>
Maintain history trend analysis for the property.</li>
<li>
Ensure the prompt and systematic servicing of all business accounts.</li>
<li>
Identify and analyze competition, both locally and regionally.</li>
<li>
Attend Chamber of Commerce monthly meetings, and other pertinent community activities.</li>
<li>
Develop strategic relationships with third-party providers.</li>
<li>
Ensure that the web and third party electronic distribution channels are continuously reviewed, researched and updated for maximum hotel exposure and maximum room night generation.</li>
<li>
Maintain and input all leads, accounts, and groups into the CRM, if applicable.</li>
<li>
Attend relevant tradeshows and events to promote the assigned properties.</li>
<li>
Post to Social Media outlets frequently with professional style photos and caption</li>
<li>
Respond to comments, tags, and other posts from followers, guests, and local organizations.</li>
<li>
Promote specials, packages, events, and hotel amenities via Social Media outlets.</li>
<li>
Maintain relevant content and descriptions across all Social Media outlets</li>
<li>
Provide sales call reports to the GM, RRMM, and RDO as required by the Standard Operating Procedures of Zenique Hotels. Or provide CRM reports showing this information.</li>
<li>
Attend all department and hotel meetings as necessary.</li>
<li>
Develop strong relationships with on property Front Office team to ensure working in unity and striving to achieve goals.</li>
<li>
Active member in Zenique Diversity Committee.</li>
