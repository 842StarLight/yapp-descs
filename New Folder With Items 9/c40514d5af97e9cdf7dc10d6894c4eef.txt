<h2>Manager, Sales Development</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<b>Job Requirements</b><br>
<p><b>We are looking for an energetic manager to help lead our Sales Development Representatives (SDRs) to their sales goals. Our SDRs are the first point of contact prospective customers have with our Sales team. The SDRs are vital contributors to Phenom People’s overall growth. We are evolving as a company, and we're now searching for another SDR manager to lead a best-in-class SDR team.</b></p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
You’ll report into our Director of Sales Development and will be responsible for overseeing the success of your assigned SDR team. You’ll be the engine behind the sales culture we create, and you’ll have a team of team leads to support your day-to-day management responsibilities. We’re looking for not only a coach to deliver constructive, real-time feedback to help our reps hit and exceed their quotas, but also a data-driven project manager who can ask the right questions to improve processes and manage change initiatives by taking an execution-intensive approach.</b></p>
<p><b>
Who will love this job:</b></p>
<p><b>
A leader; you inspire the buy-in of your team, as well as of cross-functional stakeholders.</b></p>
<p><b>
A coach; you care deeply about the success of your reps and provide hands-on mentorship in a way that shows no task is too small for you.</b></p>
<p><b>
A quantitative thinker; you recognize the importance of using numbers make decisions, as well as using data to recognize patterns and test for optimized results.</b></p>
<p><b>
A communicator; you’re great at celebrating wins, but you’re also an expert at addressing issues in a way that leads to increased engagement and productivity.</b></p>
<p><b>
An executor; you have a bias for action and are known for proactively solving problems.</b></p>
<br>
<b>
Work Experience</b><br>
<p><b>To be successful in this role, you will need the following</b></p>
<p><b>
Proven success managing and coaching a 5+ person sales team</b></p>
<p><b>
Experience managing team leads in addition to individual contributors</b></p>
<p><b>
Prior experience selling a SaaS product</b></p>
<p><b>
Experience in a fast-growth environment</b></p>
<p><b>
Proficiency with Salesforce, Linkedin Sales Navigator and/or Excel</b></p>
<p><b>
An understanding of / experience with sales acceleration tools</b></p>
<p><b>
A track record hitting and exceeding quota as a sales rep, a plus</b></p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Education</b></p>
<p><b>
Bachelor's Degree Preferred</b></p>
