<p><b>POSITION PURPOSE:</b><br>
</p>
<p>
The Director of Cyber Security Operations is responsible for directing and coordinating the protection, safeguarding and security of both our internal corporate infrastructure, as well as our customer environments. S/he will take steps to enact and execute proactive and reactive measures to prevent a cyber security related incident. Should a cyber security incident occur, s/he will partner with the necessary parties to lead the investigation and remediation efforts.</p>
<p>In order to be eligible for the Employee Bonus Plan, all employees are required to be performing their job duties satisfactorily during the applicable bonus period. This includes consistent responsiveness during any assigned On Call periods. Employees should also review the Employee Bonus Plan eligibility requirements to determine if they are eligible. If you have any questions on your eligibility for the Employee Bonus Plan please contact Human Resources.</p>
<p><b>Role Outcomes:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Provide strong leadership and own the effort to migrate a minimum of 70% of existing security customers from AlertLogic to our new, internally-delivered managed security offer.
</li>
<li>Maintains the security of both our internal infrastructure &amp; customer environments through timely identification, tracking and remediation of risks.</li>
<li>Leads in responding to and remediating any and all suspected cyber security incidents</li>
<li>Achieve a minimum of 45 relational NPS for our MSSP customers.
</li>
<li>Implement a structured development process that enables security team members to advance skills in support of our MSSP rNPS goals as well as their ability to advance their careers as security professionals at Ntirety.
</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Manage day to day operations of the Security Operations team, which includes our SOC, Security Administrators and Security Architects</li>
<li>Manage Security queue and all tickets in queue in accordance with ticket SLA’s</li>
<li>Identify new risks in internal and client infrastructure, track through remediation</li>
<li>Act as primary incident response lead during suspected or confirmed security incidents</li>
<li>Act as a point of escalation for customers on security related issues</li>
<li>Act as pre-sales SME for security questions</li>
<li>Manage and refine our MSSP product offering</li>
<li>Have a strong understanding of compliance standards such as PCI-DSS, SOC2, &amp; HIPAA, and how they relate to security</li>
<li>Partner with CISO and Compliance team to achieve annual compliance requirements</li>
<li>Perform internal DR tabletop exercise scenarios quarterly</li>
<li>Take part in operationally focused strategic initiatives and projects</li>
<li>Work cross functionally with product, compliance, operations or other teams on any relevant initiatives that should involve security
</li>
<li>Daily management of the security team staff
</li>
<li>Ensure adequate staffing for the SOC &amp; Security Team</li>
<li>Be able to perform all aspects of Security Systems Administrator role &amp; SOC Analyst role</li>
<li>Perform employee reviews and scheduling for all direct reports.</li>
<li>Provide mentoring and coaching to all direct reports</li>
<li>Develop the team’s security, troubleshooting and administration capabilities</li>
<li>Develop, Improve and Refine Processes for the Security Operations Team</li>
<li>Control and Manage Documentation for All Products and Services that fall under security</li>
<li>Keep abreast of latest threats, trends and risks that could affect our/our customer’s infrastructure and put that knowledge to action</li>
<li>Prepare and present monthly performance, service and quality metrics for security services
</li>
<li>Takes an active role in improving security product offerings</li>
<li>Point of contact for security vendors</li>
<li>Manage projects within your team, and cross-functionally as needed.</li>
<li>Work closely with the operations leaders and sales staff to handle and resolve security related customer service issues</li>
<li>Mentor, coach &amp; train staff</li>
<li>Maintain shift schedules, time cards, attendance for the Security team.</li>
<li>Ability to accurately document, refine &amp; enforce process and procedure</li>
<li>Handle escalated customer issues as necessary</li>
<li>Work with other teams as needed</li>
<li>Assist in other tasks as assigned</li>
</ul>
