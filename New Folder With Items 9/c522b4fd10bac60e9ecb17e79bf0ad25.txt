<b>BASIC FUNCTION</b>
<br>
<br>
Responsible for assisting with achievement of sales, customer service, P&amp;L management, operations and employee development goals in their respective branch and contributing to the achievement of market goals. Responsible for assisting with development and retention of a high performing sales/service team through leadership and coaching in all aspects of sales, service and operational excellence. Responsible for overseeing all daily branch functions and overall customer experience in absence of the Branch Manager. The Assistant Manager I position supervises in a Small-Tiered Branch as defined by net income.
<br>
<br>
<b>RESPONSIBILITIES</b>
<br>
<br>
Leadership &amp; People Management
<ul>
<li>Observe and coach the entire branch team in a timely and effective manner</li>
<li>Provide input and recommendations in support of the employee performance management and counseling processes.</li>
<li>Promote teamwork and a positive working environment in order to achieve team goals.</li>
<li>Assist in the establishing of development goals/plans for self and staff members.</li>
<li>Meet/exceed individual development goals and objectives.</li>
<li>Assist in the interview selection process under the guidance of the Branch Manager.</li>
<li>Assist in the facilitation of and reinforcement of team-training sessions consistent with Webster's Sales &amp; Service process.</li>
<li>Make recommendations to reward and recognize performance of team members utilizing established programs and sources.</li>
</ul>Sales &amp; Service
<ul>
<li>Consistently meet/exceed all sales goals, retention goals and customer service standards</li>
<li>Model behavior consistent with the Webster Sales and Service Process.</li>
<li>Assist branch manager in developing, implementing, and managing a detailed business plan and participate in establishing team sales and service goals.</li>
<li>Build strong internal/external relationships through ownership of problem solving and follow through.</li>
<li>Participate in and encourage team involvement in team and telemarketing events and community involvement efforts.</li>
<li>Effectively utilize decision/exception tools to support valuable customer retention.</li>
<li>Ensure excellence in customer service and maintain service standards.</li>
<li>Maintain a working knowledge of all Webster asset, liability, and partner products and services as well as alternative delivery channels.</li>
<li>Commit to add positive growth to Webster as a whole by assisting with the coaching of staff to cross sell products and services.</li>
</ul>Operations &amp; Compliance
<ul>
<li>Ensure compliance with Federal and State laws as well as Webster Bank policies and procedures. Meet/exceed branch evaluation requirements.</li>
<li>Assist with the completion of required periodic audits and inventories on a timely basis.</li>
<li>Assign duties and schedule workflow on a daily basis. Schedule all branch employee shifts to meet customer service needs.</li>
<li>Assist in the compliance of all aspects of the Leadership Scorecard</li>
</ul>Financial
<ul>
<li>Assist with the achievement of year over year profitability through daily expense management consistent with the branch P&amp;L statement.</li>
<li>Ensure the prevention of fraudulent activities through adherence to bank policies and procedures.</li>
<li>Ensure fee waivers and rebates are granted within bank guidelines.</li>
</ul>Other duties as required.
