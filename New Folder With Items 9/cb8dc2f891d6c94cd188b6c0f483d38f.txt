<p><b>Job Description</b></p>
<p>This position is a blend of Kitchen and Bath Design and office-based project management. The Project Coordinators job is to assist in taking sold projects from the sales person (the framework: contract, plan, and price sheet) and develop a build-able, detailed packet and schedule, coordinate and order the materials for a successful job. This will include drawing &amp; editing floor plans in CAD software. An ideal candidate must be extremely organized, self-motivated, and have the skills necessary to manage multiple tasks at one time. Patience and strong communication skills are a must as you will be working closely with homeowners and our field project managers to help execute the homeowner’s vision.</p>
