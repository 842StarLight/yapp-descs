<p><b><br>
Position Overview<br>
</b></p>
<p></p>
<p><b>The Manufacturing Operations Management Presales Solution Consultant position is a key technical role that develops and executes strategies in our sales efforts to expand MOM solutions in the Industrial Machinery, Heavy Equipment, Automotive, and Transportation industries.</b></p>
<p></p>
<p><b><br>
This position is a lead role engaging in a wide variety of sales opportunities that will a have a substantial impact on the success of customers through direct involvement in presales activities. The person filling this position will report directly to the Director of Solution Consulting.</b></p>
<p></p>
<p><b><br>
Responsibilities
</b></p>
<p><b>Primary Responsibilities include working with sales orchestrators as they identify and engage in Siemens Manufacturing Operations Management Portfolio opportunities in the field. This individual will play a key role in helping to develop new business opportunities and will take a lead role in communicating and demonstrating the value of our products to prospect executives and prospect evaluation teams. Must have a broad understanding of manufacturing environments, be able to demonstrate core capabilities, and communicate the value of industry specific digital threads</b></p>
<p></p>
<p><b><br>
Additional Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li><b>
Assistance in qualification of new opportunities.</b></li>
<li><b>
Assistance in development of demonstration and presentation storyboards and supporting collateral.</b></li>
<li><b>
Participation in customer/prospect facing events supporting presentations and demonstrations.</b></li>
<li><b>
Coordination and leadership of extended Simulation Presales resources.
</b></li>
<li><b>Assistance in demand generation activities such as webinars, seminars and roadshows.</b></li>
</ul>
