<h2>Enterprise AR/VR Account Management</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div><div>Facebook's mission is to give people the power to build community and bring the world closer together. Through our family of apps and services, we're building a different kind of company that connects billions of people around the world, gives them ways to share what matters most to them, and helps bring people closer together. Whether we're creating new products or helping a small business expand its reach, people at Facebook are builders at heart. Our global teams are constantly iterating, solving problems, and working together to empower people around the world to build community and connect in meaningful ways. Together, we can help people build stronger communities — we're just getting started.
</div></div>
<div>The role of the AR/VR Enterprise Account Manager is a unique opportunity to be a key contributor to the sales strategy and execution in Facebook AR/VR. The position will primarily involve developing and managing relationships with key sales channels specifically focusing on end customer relationships along with AR/VR developers, VARs and LARs while creating and implementing partner specific business plans and supporting AR/VR initiatives that deliver AR/VR adoption, sales growth and deployment into the enterprise.
<br>
The candidate will possess superior analytical skills, communication skills and can effectively influence and communicate cross-functionally with all levels of management, internally and externally. The candidate will thrive in a team-oriented environment, working cross-functionally internally with the Product Management, Engineering, Business Development, Marketing, Operations and PR teams to create win-win scenarios with our customers to ensure that they deploy Facebook AR/VR solutions and amplify the visibility of those solutions to the industry and prospective customers.
</div>
