<p><b>Portfolio Officer</b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Summary
</b></p>
<p>The Portfolio Officer is responsible for assisting Portfolio Managers, Relationship Managers and Center Managers in the solicitation of new relationships along with the retention of existing relationships including loans and deposit accounts as well as other services and products offered by the Bank. The Portfolio Officer is involved in the analysis of customer/prospect business financial needs and opportunities, prospect/customer negotiations and internal presentation of credit and product requests. The Portfolio Officer is responsible for assisting the sales process with the sales team.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Essential Duties &amp; Responsibilities</b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Provides sales and operational support to the sales teams in the Centers.
</li>
<li>Participates in all Center functions necessary to contribute to the achievement of Center earnings and growth objective that can be measured and actively participates in various community and civic activities in the Center Service area.</li>
<li>Contributes significantly to meeting or exceeding the Center budgetary goals and ensures that deposit, loan and net income goals are met.</li>
<li>Works with or through the Manager to ensure that all bank operating and lending policies and procedures are properly and promptly implemented and followed.
</li>
<li>Is compliant with all CBB policies and procedures to ensure a passing or better, Audit.
</li>
<li>Works with or through the Manager to ensure the Banks CRA policies and objectives are met for its assigned service area.</li>
<li>Works independently, with or through Management of the Center’s sales and service efforts to ensure retention and growth of existing relationships.
</li>
<li>Manage personal sales efforts that meet or exceed Center goals with a specific production goal based on experience and Center location.
</li>
<li>May be involved in customer calls at customer's place of business.</li>
<li>Prepare, process, organize and assemble loan documentation.</li>
<li>Obtain credit information from various sources.</li>
<li>Ensure proper documentation requirements are met for accounts maintained by the Center.</li>
<li>Timely and accurate completion of all required monthly reports.</li>
<li>Provide, present and promote The Citizens Experience to all external and internal customers.
</li>
<li>Complies with and stays abreast of all policies and procedures, federal and state laws applicable to the job.</li>
<li>Other duties as assigned.</li>
<br>
</ul>
