<p>Customer Service / Technical Support</p>
<p><b>
Who we are looking for:</b></p>
<p>
As a Customer Support Representative, you will work with our software customers and will be responsible for providing world class customer support for any of the Vantagepoint ai proprietary financial trading applications and services. The customer support representative must be able to adequately communicate information and instructions to customers that have a wide range of technical experience. This will include providing technical support to resolve a wide variety of technical issues. The position involves working within a dynamic team of individuals who are responsible for providing preventative and reactive support to our customers.</p>

Job Description:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Provide world class customer service to all new and existing Vantagepoint end-users by inbound call distribution, from the office via remote connection or over the Internet.</li>
