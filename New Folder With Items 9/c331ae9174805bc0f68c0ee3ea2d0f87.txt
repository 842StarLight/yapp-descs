BioMerieux has a position open for a Territory Director. The Territory Director will develop and drive clinical market adoption of NephroCheck leading to increasing revenue growth within assigned territory.
<br><br>
<b>Main Accountabilities:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Effectively position and sell the NephroCheck Test to CV Surgeons, Hospitalists, Intensivists, Nephrologist, Pharmacy Directors, Chief Medical Officers, Laboratory administration, and Quality.</li>
<li>Use SFDC to develop a 30, 60, 90 day rolling forecast and to develop a yearly territory business plan.</li>
<li>Develop and manage key thought leaders within the territory to help educate customers on the value of Nephrocheck.</li>
<li>Develop a yearly regional territory plan and account forecast that will be rolled up to the RBM and then to the VP of Sales.</li>
<li>Strategically work with the Marketing team to execute Marketing Plans to include – New Product Launch, Market Development, Distributor Training, Speakers Bureau and Publication Strategy.</li>
<li>Identify market opportunities to advance product penetration to include symposium, trade shows, and customer education events.</li>
</ul>
<b>Qualifications:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Bachelors required/ Master Degree preferred in business related major with a minimum of (5) years sales experience within the medical marketplace.</li>
<li>Experience selling and marketing medical diagnostic products utilized in the acute care settings like the surgery suite, intensive care unit and emergency department.</li>
<li>Proven ability to successfully contribute to the revenue growth.</li>
<li>Proven success growing territory sales and sales achievement.</li>
<li>Ability to travel as required.</li>
<li>Ability to understand and interpret a variety of scientific and clinical literature.</li>
<li>Excellent verbal and written communication skills.</li>
<li>Extensive experience and knowledge selling and marketing products that require intense clinical positioning and market development.</li>
<li>Ability to plan and makes decisions by considering impact from all relevant perspectives; able to verbalize decisions in a thorough and effective manner.</li>
<li>Holds self and others accountable for adherence to high work and ethical standards.</li>
</ul>
