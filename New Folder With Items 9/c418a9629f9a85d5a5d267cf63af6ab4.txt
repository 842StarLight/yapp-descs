<h2>Regional Vice President, Enterprise Sales</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div><div>Yelp is seeking a Regional Vice President of Enterprise Sales who can help us continue to expand our business across large Enterprise accounts with HQs on the West Coast. The role will be responsible for managing a small team of Regional Directors and Sales Managers, who lead teams of sales reps that generate revenue by closing sales directly with clients and their agency partners. This is a hands-on, leadership position.</div></div>
<div><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities:</b></h3>
<div><ul>
<li>
Build, oversee and drive a team of regional leaders and field sales representatives to maximize revenues and exceed sales targets</li>
<li>
Forecast monthly, quarterly and annual account revenue streams accurately</li>
<li>
Identify, develop, and implement effective sales plans and strategies</li>
<li>
Provide hands-on coaching, support and motivation to help individuals meet and exceed their defined sales targets</li>
<li>
Train and coach sales representatives to structure deals that position them to exceed revenue expectations</li>
<li>
Serve as an escalation point for issues beyond team authority; resolve team conflicts as necessary</li>
<li>
Participate in key sales cycles by strategizing internally with team as well as participating in prospect / client meetings</li>
<li>
Maintain open communication and collaborate with cross-functional partners, including Customer Success, Product, Legal, Marketing, Sales Planning, Revenue Operations, etc. to maximize revenue</li>
<li>
Participate in sales team meetings, sales leadership meetings and business reviews</li>
<li>
Live the company values</li>
<li>
Perform other duties as needed/assigned</li>
</ul></div>
</div></div>
<div><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Minimum Requirements:</b></h3>
<div><ul>
<li>
BA/BS degree or similar college level education</li>
<li>
10+ years of demonstrated success leading and managing an enterprise sales team and exceeding team targets</li>
<li>
Must be an expert in paid media, specifically cost per click advertising</li>
<li>
Strong sales, recruiting, leadership, organization, planning and prioritization skills</li>
<li>
Results oriented with multiple years of meeting or exceeding revenue targets</li>
<li>
Excellent interpersonal skills to facilitate positive working relationships across the organization and in the marketplace</li>
<li>
Exceptional Project Management skills</li>
<li>
Ability to develop and critically analyze a sales pipeline and forecast using Salesforce (or equivalent)</li>
<li>
Excellent verbal and written communication skills</li>
<li>
Highly motivated and flexible</li>
<li>
Desire to travel for in-person meetings</li>
</ul></div>
</div></div>
