<h2>Leasing Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>As a Leasing Manager you will be responsible for the promotion and marketing of the student housing community to achieve the leasing and resident retention goals. In addition, the Leasing Manager role is responsible for the training, supervision and evaluation of the Leasing Agent and Community Assistant positions assigned to the property.</p>
<p><b>
Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Performs all responsibilities in a timely and efficient manner</li>
<li>
Recommends and implements corrective action on service, presentation, organization, and productivity</li>
<li>
Projects a favorable image of the community to achieve property objectives and public recognition</li>
<li>
Adheres to standards and complies with: corporate procedures, government laws on Fair Housing, and all inspections of the facility</li>
<li>
Generates traffic of prospective customers by utilizing advertisements for periodicals, direct mailers, follow up letters, telemarketing campaigns, in-house programs,</li>
<li>
Assists with the design of advertisements for periodicals, direct mailers, follow up letters, telemarketing campaigns, in-house programs,</li>
<li>
Conducts all functions necessary to close the sale, including making appointments, generating new leads, conducting tours, maintaining show rooms, and any follow-up necessary</li>
<li>
Regularly completes market surveys, including university and local marketing conditions</li>
<li>
Conducts all functions necessary to maximize revenue during the summer, including leasing all space, coordinating with other departments,</li>
<li>
Performs after hours' duties as needed (i.e., attend student functions, PR events, )</li>
<li>
Assists with the development and implementation of ideas to retain residents</li>
<li>
Places customer needs, requests, and follow-up as a number one priority</li>
<li>
Assists the General Manager with the supervision of the leasing staff, including training, development, evaluation, and rewards</li>
<li>
Maintains daily contact with staff and attends meetings as appropriate</li>
<li>
Gathers and/or compiles information for requested reports</li>
<li>
Completes all necessary paperwork for move-in, check-out, and other peak periods</li>
<li>
Processes all paperwork associated with generating a lease in a timely fashion</li>
<li>
Assists in corporate projects as requested.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Qualifications:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
High school diploma or equivalent; college degree preferred</li>
<li>
Certified Apartment Leasing Agent (CALA) preferred</li>
<li>
Previous experience in multifamily housing strongly preferred</li>
<li>
Experience with OneSite, Yieldstar, Entrata or similar software systems</li>
<li>
2+ years of leasing experience required</li>
<li>
Demonstrate strong oral and written communication skills</li>
<li>
Demonstrate ability to diffuse and respond to customer concerns to avoid escalation of the problem</li>
</ul>
