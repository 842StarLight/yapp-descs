<h2>FISHING/Sporting Goods Associate-Part-Time (GG)</h2>

<div></div>

<li>Assist customers with sporting goods special orders.</li>
<li>Demonstrate the use of sporting goods products to customers.</li>
</ul>
<div><b>Knowledge, Skills and Ability Requirements:</b></div>

<li>Outgoing energetic individual needed with a dedication to exceptional customer service.</li>
<li>Capability for moderate physical effort with frequent lifting, reaching, pulling bending, standing and walking.</li>
<li>Technically skilled in basic computer knowledge and software (such as, Word, Excel, Email, internet research)</li>
<li>Must have good written and oral communication skills</li>
<li>High School Diploma or General Education Degree (GED) or equivalent combination of education and experience.</li>
</ul>
