<h2>Graphic Designer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p><b>THE DEETS:</b></p>
<p>
Ledge Lounger in-pool and outdoor furniture and accessories can be seen worldwide at high-end pools, resorts, and homes. Experiencing rapid growth, we’ve recently been honored for the second consecutive year on Inc Magazine’s 5000 Fastest Growing Companies in America, as well as Houston Business Journal’s Fast 100.</p>
<p><b>
The graphic designer</b> will join our fast-paced team and balance a multitude of projects from email campaigns, print collateral, digital and social media, product packaging, and more. If you’re drawn in by collaboration, multi-tasking, and variety, read on.</p>
<p><b>
WARNING</b>:
</p>
<p>We move quickly. We set high standards, then challenge ourselves to exceed expectations. We’re candid in the pursuit of greatness. We’re looking for a designer who is ready to grow creatively and challenge themselves day in and day out. If you’re looking for a job where you can meet, rather than exceed the status quo, this might not be the place for you.</p>
<p><b>
THE DAY-TO-DAY:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Design a diverse portfolio of projects including but not limited to:
<ul>
<li>all marketing collateral such as product catalog, flyers, price sheets, mailers, packaging inserts, business cards, and booklets</li>
<li>all digital assets such as email blasts, web banners, and assets for social media</li>
<li>all print advertising including national and international publications</li>
<li>all items related to trade show marketing such as promo items, booth design, and large-scale displays</li>
<li>all technical items related to product marketing including specification sheets, cleaning &amp; care guides, instruction manuals, and product packaging</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>Design, build, and execute ongoing drip-email campaigns for a diverse set of Ledge Lounger audiences</li>
<li>Collaborate on the look and styling of the Ledge Lounger website</li>
<li>Collaborate with our internal sales and product development teams to execute projects</li>
<li>Work with our vendors and clients to provide tailored marketing support</li>
<li>Retouch and Photoshop images as needed</li>
<li>Own the printing process from start to finish including quoting, printing, reorders, and quantity management</li>
</ul>
<p><b>THE ESSENTIALS:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Ability to innovate, think outside the box, and bring creative ideas to the table</li>
<li>Ability to multi-task, balance several projects, and manage deadlines in a fast-paced environment</li>
<li>A clean, stylish design aesthetic</li>
<li>Self-starter who will seek out opportunities for growth and challenges</li>
<li>Exceptional communication and collaboration skills</li>
<li>Must be open to coaching, advice, and collaboration from team members and manager</li>
<li>Self-motivated, uses resources to seek out answers and improve their own knowledge</li>
<li>Ability to target diverse market segments while maintaining brand consistency</li>
<li>Knowledge of design concepts and their effect on marketing, click through and call to action</li>
<li>Understanding of branding, layout, color theory and typography in both print and digital media</li>
<li>Must be proficient in InDesign, Photoshop, and Illustrator</li>
<li>Aftereffects and animation skills are a plus</li>
<li>College degree required</li>
<li>2+ years’ experience graphic design</li>
<li>Will be required to lift up to 10 lbs with ease</li>
<li>Some standing, moving, climbing, bending, kneeling, and handling is required</li>
</ul>
