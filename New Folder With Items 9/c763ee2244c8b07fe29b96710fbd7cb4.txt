<b>Description</b><br>
ThriftBooks is looking for a part-time intern to join their team who has experience in accounting. Experience gained in the classroom and through personal application will be acceptable. This internship will provide an opportunity for the selected candidate to work on a small team, allowing you to gain real work experience and build your resume. Responsibilities include, assisting with closing monthly financial statements, analyzing operating results, preparing account analyses, bank reconciliations &amp; other general accounting duties.</p>

<p><b>
About Us</b><br>
Based in Seattle, WA, ThriftBooks is the largest online seller of used books in the world, having sold more than 100 million books since its inception. Founded in 2003 and backed by KCB Management, ThriftBooks employs more than 750 people and operates 9 fulfillment centers in the US that purchase, grade, and distribute used and collectible books. ThriftBooks relies on proprietary software to identify and list books, as well as a sophisticated pricing model that dynamically prices books across a variety of online platforms, including ThriftBooks.com, Amazon.com, eBay, and others.</p>
