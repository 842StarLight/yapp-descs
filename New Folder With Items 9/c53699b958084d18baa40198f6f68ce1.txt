<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Job Description
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div>Zapproved is seeking a Demand Generation Manager to join our growing legal software company headquartered in Portland, OR. In this role, you will be a key player in the growth of our product offerings, responsible for executing demand generation strategies that increase qualified leads in the sales pipeline. You will identify and segment audiences and develop integrated campaigns that engage, inform and activate leads. Our ideal candidate will be an acquisition maven that can roll up their sleeves, partner with stakeholders and team members in a fast-paced, high-growth organization.</div>

<div><b>
What You’ll Be Doing:</b></div>
<div>
In this role, you’ll…</div>
<ul>
<li>
Work with cross-functional sales &amp; marketing team to take a data-driven approach to design, launch, and optimize demand gen programs (including paid digital, content syndication, SEO/SEM, email and offline campaigns i.e. direct mail) to generate leads and create pipeline</li>
<li>
Manage the implementation and setup of all paid media efforts - including landing pages, forms, and emails.</li>
<li>
A/B test and continually optimize key conversion points in the marketing funnel</li>
<li>
Partner with Sales to increase the velocity of the sales funnel; optimize each stage of the buyer journey to increase conversion rates and accelerate deals.</li>
<li>
Leverage analytics to assess channel and campaign performance and understand how our marketing programs generate leads, opportunities, and revenue. (attribution)</li>
</ul>
<ul><li>
Collaborate with Content Marketing to create campaign promotion content, such as copywriting for landing pages, email promotions, banner ads, etc.</li></ul>
<div></div>
<br>
<div><b>
What You’ll Need:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
3-5+ years in demand generation in a B2B environment; A proven track record of managing lead generation and nurture programs.</li>
<li>
Experience across a variety of acquisition channels including digital, email, events and beyond.</li>
<li>
Clear understanding of ROI analysis and proven ability to analyze results and recommend changes.</li>
<li>
Proficiency in Marketo’s marketing automation software; Salesforce experience.</li>
<li>
Analytical and able to extract meaning from data and use this to optimize a team’s approach and a company’s forecast.</li>
<li>
BA/BS or equivalent working experience.</li>
</ul>
