<h2>Research Analyst</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>You get excited when reading about the newest trends in technology. You enjoy figuring out how those technologies can be used to solve problems in different industries and improve their digital operations. You can draw out complex ideas from various sources of research and distill them into clear, streamlined messaging that will be used by sales and marketing campaigns. You like improving efficiency, helping execute projects, and being the glue for all the details scattered about. And you like being given space to run in a global open source company with a start-up culture. Sound like you? Apply now and let's chat.<br>
</p>
<br>
<p><b>Major Objectives</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Research specific industries (e.g., insurance, manufacturing, healthcare, etc.) and gain a deep understanding of the following:</li>
<ul>
<li>
The industry’s current challenges and areas they are looking to invest to deal with these challenges</li>
<li>
Top trends in the industry and technologies that support those trends</li>
<li>
Hot industry topics that you’d encounter if you went to an industry conference</li>
</ul>
<li>
Be highly knowledgeable about our product line and find ways the products can contribute to each industry you research</li>
<li>
Create research reports that highlight key findings and make recommendations for what/who we should pursue in each industry based on our products’ strengths</li>
<li>
Work with key stakeholders to refine reports into messaging that can be used by Sales and Marketing teams as well as requirements that will be used to build out solutions</li>
<li>
Participate in analyst briefings specific to each industry</li>
</ul>
<br>
<p><b>
Preferred Qualifications</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Bachelor’s degree in Business, Communications, Marketing, Technology or related field</li>
<li>
3-5 years of experience in a strategy or research/business analyst related role</li>
<li>
Experience with project management and development frameworks</li>
<li>
Experience in an Agile work environment
</li>
<li>Experience with projects in process improvement, requirements management, and facilitating workshops</li>
<li>
Proficiency in Microsoft Office and experience in process diagramming or other business analysis tools</li>
<li>
Strong written and verbal communication skills</li>
<li>
Strong consulting and critical thinking skills</li>
<li>
Ability to manage multiple tasks and prioritize between key deliverables</li>
<li>
Understands the value and uses of portal in an enterprise environment</li>
<li>
Experience with Liferay, Websphere Portal, Web Logic or other platforms preferred</li>
</ul>
