F&amp;I Manager
<br><br>
Pioneer-Family Automotive is seeking a qualified Automotive Finance and Insurance (F&amp;I) Manager. The F&amp;I Manager will help our customers and sales department arrange the financing of purchases and also presents them with additional products that enhance their vehicle and ownership experience. The F&amp;I Manager must be very accomplished working in tandem with the General Sales Manager to maximize the volume sales and gross profit opportunity at our new sales operation.
<br><br>
The F&amp;I Manager will ensure all deals are fully compliant with local, state and federal guidelines. In addition, the manager will prepare paperwork, contracts, and deliver deals. Following deal delivery, the manager will mail all deal packages with complete and proper documentation to lenders or follow any other process that may be in place in their respective department.
<br><br>
**Please DO NOT apply without F&amp;I or Sales Manager experience**
