<h2>Project Manager, Research & Development</h2>

<div><div><div>
<div>
<div>The Project Manager, R&amp;D develops and manages the project plans for Akornâ€™s portfolio of drug development projects, starting at the pre-development phase all the way through to drug launch. Project will include those being developed internally at Akorn as well as those being developed with Contract Manufacturing Organizations (CMOs).<br>
<br>
Working with a team in formulation, analytical, regulatory, manufacturing, marketing, sales, and supply chain, the PM R&amp;D ensures that all project deliverables, timelines and budgets are met throughout the project. The PM, R&amp;D creates and maintains all project related documentation, project schedules, milestone due dates, and resource requirements. This role has responsibility for reporting out information to the Project Team, Department Managers, Directors and Vice Presidents, including identification of risks and potential mitigation strategies.<br>
<br>
This position reports directly to the Director, Project Management R&amp;D.<br>
<br>
<b>Essential Duties and Responsibilities</b><ul>
<li>
Develops and implements project plans, which includes clear project scope, budget, timelines, scheduling project meetings, setting agendas, preparing and presenting project progress reports to Senior Management</li>
<li>
Facilitates and leads project team meetings including setting agendas, taking and disseminating meeting notes and action registers; responsible for building an effective project team, typically 6-8 project team members</li>
<li>
Prepares project and other analytical reports; provides project summaries and recommendations as needed to project team and other stakeholders throughout all phases of the project</li>
<li>
Assists with the management of vendors and other 3rd party resources</li>
<li>
Tracks and controls project plans and budget</li>
<li>
Ensures proper historical documentation for all projects is kept</li>
<li>
Follow the Quality Assurance and Regulatory requirements for the assigned project.</li>
<li>
Identifies critical project issues and risks, and timely communication to senior leadership</li>
<li>
Resolves project issues by working with team members, project customers, and others as appropriate.</li>
<li>
Maintains direct interaction with R&amp;D, quality, regulatory, operations, and manufacturing organizations at Akorn.</li>
</ul>
