You will be trained on the specific products we offer and their benefit to our customer.</p>
<ul>
<li>Present vehicle attributes, model options, features, purchase and finance options and store policies and services to customers.</li>
<li>Ensure timely follow up and maintain strong relationships with previous and prospective customers.</li>
<li>Track customers using store specific management system/s.</li>
<li>Keep abreast of new products, features and accessories. Attend product and training courses.</li>
</ul>
