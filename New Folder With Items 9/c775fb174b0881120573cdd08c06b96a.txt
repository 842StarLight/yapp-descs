<h2>Regional Director, Enterprise Sales</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>
<div>Yelp’s mission is to connect people with great local businesses. Yelp’s Enterprise team partners with multi-location businesses to provide industry leading advertising solutions. Our native search advertising products deliver strong ROI by helping clients connect with potential customers at the bottom of the purchase funnel. Yelp has become an essential part of a client’s Local advertising mix and the Enterprise team is growing fast.</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
We are looking for an experienced Regional Director, Enterprise Sales in Chicago to lead a team of Enterprise Sales Directors and Senior Enterprise Sales Directors in all areas from prospecting, pitching and closing new business to driving incremental revenue from existing enterprise accounts. The ideal candidate has a proven record of developing and coaching a team, while navigating through an evolving organization. The Regional Director, Enterprise Sales role is key to Yelp’s success and offers significant career growth opportunities within Yelp’s Enterprise Sales team.</div>
</div>
<div><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>What you will do:</b></h3>
<div><ul>
<li>
Manage and coach a team of 6-8 sales directors to develop successful sales strategies including implementing multi-tiered approaches, establishing a wide network of contacts, defining measurable success metrics, developing successful campaigns, navigating conversations around attribution, identifying and staying ahead of risks, and accurately forecasting opportunities</li>
<li>
Meet and exceed quarterly sales team quotas by holding your team accountable for activities that lead to revenue generation</li>
<li>
Provide regular updates to management on team activities, opportunities, and risks</li>
<li>
Implement strategic planning, forecasting and ongoing development of sales team</li>
<li>
Identify improvement in all areas of productivity, process and efficiency</li>
<li>
Develop and maintain executive level relationships to help drive sales activities</li>
<li>
Assist in closing complex multi-million dollar programs with your team</li>
</ul></div>
</div></div>
<div><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>What we are looking for:</b></h3>
<div><ul>
<li>
3+ years managing a team of 5 or more</li>
<li>
10+ years relevant Enterprise experience across Sales, Digital, and Advertising roles</li>
<li>
Proven track record of exceeding multi-million dollar Sales Targets</li>
<li>
Experienced communicator with proven track record of Executive outreach</li>
<li>
Entrepreneurial with history of building partnerships from scratch</li>
<li>
Ability to handle critical conversations internally with reps as well as client escalations</li>
<li>
Able to navigate uncertainty successfully for all parties and lead to desired outcomes</li>
<li>
Belief in Yelp’s mission and naturally puts the company and team first</li>
<li>
Able to Travel 30% of the time</li>
<li>
Easy-going, cool headed, with a track record of collaboration and working well internally</li>
<li>
Ready and willing to roll up your sleeves and build something big at Yelp</li>
</ul></div>
</div></div>
<div><div>

<div><ul>
<li>
Guaranteed base salary plus uncapped commission</li>
<li>

15 days PTO and 11 paid holidays (per year)</li>

Generous paid parental leave policy</li>

</ul></div>
