<h2>SAMPLE EXPRESS MANAGER</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p>Join the largest manufacturer of tile and natural stone in the United States and watch your career stand out with Dal-Tile, a subsidiary of Mohawk Industries.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p>Our close-knit team of dedicated professionals has made us the success we are today. In exchange for their hard work, we support our people with a family-friendly work environment, a commitment to promoting from within, unique benefits that go beyond just medical and dental, and a belief that every employee deserves a productive life outside of work. If this sounds outstanding to you, take the first step forward and explore a career with Dal-Tile.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p>Increases product sales by providing exceptional samples service and management. Develops and retains effective working relationships sales teams, planning, Operations Director, and customers to achieve and exceed samples fulfillment requirements. Manages Sample Express personnel and operations to ensure all activities are performed in accordance with company policies and procedures.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Function as the samples fulfillment expert and a liaison between sales, sales operations/Director of Sales Operations, internal departments, external resources, and all applicable system to provide exceptional sample services meeting and exceeding all internal and external customer requirements and expectations. Critical, timely, and accurate execution in all aspects of ownership of the samples business
</li>
<li>Clearly defines goals and objectives for SamX personnel to measure performance and communicate these objectives to the staff on a regular basis.
</li>
<li>Trains, leads and develops associates in areas of customer service, order management, purchasing, warehouse duties, office administration, and system processes optimizing team productivity and expenses in all areas
</li>
<li>Work with Planner to maintain an accurate profile including tracking and maintaining inventory levels
</li>
<li>Order new supplies in a timely manner
</li>
<li>Constantly work to improve processes and makes suggestions for productivity improvements
</li>
<li>Track most important warehouse KPIs, take actions when KPI’s are below expectations
</li>
<li>Communicate and collaborate with other team members
</li>
<li>Participates in market meetings to understand product trends, ongoing, and future sample needs determining actions and resources needed to meet demand while also communicating realistic expectation on the ability and lead time to fulfil those needs.
</li>
<li>Communicate and collaborate with sales
</li>
<li>Analyzes and controls expenditures of the Sample Express to conform to budgetary requirements
</li>
<li>Cross trains on all areas of SamX operations; customer service, warehouse, and office administration duties. Assists where necessary. Recruits, hires and manages SSC staff associates. Includes daily support and direction, as well as, on-going performance management and development.
</li>
<li>Communicates the safety program goals and expectations and ensures associates are properly trained in the Safety program.
</li>
</ul>
<p>Any other tasks and duties as required<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p>HS diploma and 4+ years relevant operational service location experience required OR
</p>
<p>Bachelor’s degree in Business or related field and 1-3 years relevant operational service location experience.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p>At least 4 years related experience in the operation of a Sales/Service/slab/Sample center in the tile industry or in the durable consumer products industry with documented exceptional results. Familiar with a variety of the sales and sample best practices, and procedures<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p>Core competencies include a strong leader with the ability manage associates. A clear and proactive communicator of goals, knowledge, and ideas to associates at all levels in the organization. Customer service and operationally excellence focused individual Detail oriented. Ability to analyze data and apply results effectively, training/facilitator skills, and strong project management skills and abilities. Self-starter with a wide degree of creativity and latitude is expected. Competent in Microsoft Office software applications (Excel).<br>
</p>
