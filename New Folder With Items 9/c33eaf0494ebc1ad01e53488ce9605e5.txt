<h2>Loan Servicing Rep 3</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Job Title</b>: Loan Servicing Representative 3 <b>Department</b>: Collections</p>
<p><b>
Supervisory Role</b>: No <b>FLSA</b>: Non-Exempt</p>
<p><b>
Supervisor’s Title</b>: Loan Servicing Assistant Manager</p>
<p><b>
Position Summary</b></p>
<p>
Under the supervision of the Loan Servicing Assistant Manager, performs a variety of Loan Servicing functions in connection with Commercial, Real Estate and consumer loans including processing the final closing and distribution of paid loans, preparation of payoff demand statements, and maintenance, tracking and review of all pending loan documents and exceptions. Ensures accurate responses to all support areas related to Credit Union insurance products, loan account information research including the appropriate rework of any records, creation and completion of modifications and workout loans, provides various tasks to support the Collection/Lending Team in order to achieve goals of the department and minimize losses to the Credit Union.</p>
<p><b>
Essential Functions and Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Personally, provides exceptional Member service; uses Service Standards in every work-related interaction.</li>
<li>Completes research and processing for more complex member accounts issues such as account disputes, loan reworks related to loans and credit cards that require extensive knowledge of Credit Union policies/procedures and compliance guidelines; corrects account transactions and provides member correspondence and contact.</li>
<li>Processes, reviews all insurance claims and reporting related to payment protection, Guaranteed Auto Protection (GAP), Mechanical Breakdown Protection (MBP) and Collateral Protection Insurance (CPI).</li>
<li>Processes all paid loans and ensures distribution of paid Commercial, Real Estate and Consumer loans according to established procedures.</li>
<li>Reconcile all department General Ledger’s (GL), balance and maintain recons.</li>
<li>Prepare payoff demand statements and other requests to provide payoff information in written form.</li>
<li>Maintain, manage and track all pending/outstanding loan documents and exceptions.</li>
<li>Perform all final checklist sign-off of all credit and collateral files after confirming that all documents are in file and that the file is complete.</li>
<li>Index and image loan documents.</li>
<li>Process department invoices, debit accounts, create/update expense tracking records.</li>
<li>Create/update settled accounts; credit reporting fields and 1099c tracking.</li>
<li>Assists with telephone support.</li>
<li>Process Electronic Cash Management (ECM)and Automated Clearing House (ACH) exceptions.</li>
<li>Assist with repo Anticipated Write Down (AWD) process and balancing.</li>
<li>Responsible for daily balancing of the department and reconciling all negotiable items utilized by the department.</li>
<li>Creates modification/workout loan documents based on approval stipulations, sends and monitors timely receipt and processing and accuracy of input.</li>
<li>Prepares forms and documents for Team.</li>
<li>Documents contact with members in the Akcelerant system.</li>
<li>Makes telephone calls on notices of stored vehicles received, makes notations in the Akcelerant system of that status and forwards information to Repo desk.</li>
<li>Sorts and distributes all faxes and incoming Collection Department mail.</li>
<li>Processes payments, advances, adjustments. (Teller transactions)</li>
<li>Performs file maintenance.</li>
<li>Prepares and completes Sales Tax tracking.</li>
<li>Maintains a working knowledge of Bank Secrecy Act (BSA)/Anti-Money Laundering (AML) laws/regulations and Office of Foreign Asset Controls (OFAC) applicable to area of responsibility.</li>
<li>Maintains a working knowledge of all Fair Debt Collection Practices Act (FDCPA) &amp; Graham Leach guidelines and other consumer financial protection laws and regulations.</li>
<li>Provides assistance in the packaging of incoming Department of Motor Vehicles (DMV) paper work from the branches, provides updates when research is required.</li>
<li>Backup support to internal DMV area.</li>
<li>Logs, calls and documents incoming impound notices and forwards results to Specialist.</li>
<li>Regular and predictable attendance and punctuality.</li>
<li>Other duties as necessary to achieve the goals of the department and the credit union.</li>
</ul>
