<h2>Business Development Manager - Pacific Northwest Territory</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>JOB SUMMARY: </b></p>
<p>The BDM - Business Development Manager will focus on generating new business and opportunities for MRP in new markets and existing accounts in the pacific northwest territory. The focus will be on our core competency, promoting marketing and tele-prospecting services to technology companies and experience in doing so is highly desired and beneficial.</p>
<p><b><i>Essential Functions: </i></b></p>
<ul>
<li>Achieving/exceeding an annual sales quota primarily through the successful promotion of MRP’s marketing and tele-prospecting services to technology clients.</li>
<li>Telling and selling the MRP story in a compelling and value-focused manner</li>
<li>Proactively prospecting/cold calling to generate interest and open target markets for MRP</li>
<li>Leverage business from new &amp; established relationships</li>
<li>Interact with the Executive Management team to ensure unity of direction in management focus.</li>
<li>Record accurate and detailed updates of his/her efforts in MRP’s CRM, (Siebel).</li>
<li>Effectively manage time to meet the required performance metrics of the position.</li>
<li>Demonstrate a professional and courteous demeanor that includes phone interaction as well as face to face communication.</li>
</ul>
<p><b><i>SKILLS AND ABILITIES: </i></b></p>
<ul>
<li>Demonstrated ability to effectively interact with internal and external groups, to achieve results.</li>
<li>Excellent verbal and written communication skills. Excellent presentation skills.</li>
<li>Demonstrated ability to work in an MS Office and Siebel CRM</li>
<li>Proven talent in sales with the ability to overcome obstacles in the sales process.</li>
<li>Demonstrated ability to effectively and efficiently use time and resources to reach goals.</li>
<li>Ability to understand and utilize business marketing and technology concepts, client-server</li>
<li>technology concepts, enterprise solutions, e-commerce, intranet, and complex technical solutions.</li>
</ul>
<p><b><i>EDUCATION AND EXPERIENCE</i></b>:</p>
<ul>
<li><i>1-3 years of SaaS and/or marketing technology sales experience</i></li>
<li><i>Experience in cold-calling and exceptional closing skills</i></li>
<li><i>Entrepreneurial drive and work ethic</i></li>
<li><i>Excellent presentation and communication skills, both written and verbal</i></li>
<li><i>Demonstrated effectiveness with personal ethics and integrity</i></li>
<li><i>College degree strongly preferred</i></li>
</ul>
<p>Job Type: Full-time</p>
