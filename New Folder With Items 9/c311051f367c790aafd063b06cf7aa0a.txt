<b>Job Description</b><br>
</div>
<div>Using our 3D CAD tool, Drafting Co-ops will primarily be responsible for producing new detail drawings. In this position, co-ops will assist with backlog management, issue identification and resolution, drawing revisions via change orders, and daily team responsibilities.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
</div>
<div>
<b>Job Responsibilities</b><br>
</div>
<ul>
<li><div>
Drafting detail drawings using CAD software while conforming to Fisher and ANSI/ASME drafting standards.</div></li>
<li><div>
Ensuring form, fit, and function of Fisher produced assembly drawings.</div></li>
<li><div>
Revising drawings to resolve issues and meet customer needs.</div></li>
<li><div>
Communication across multiple departments, project teams, and world area groups.</div></li>
<li><div>
Develop and maintain tools to aid in knowledge transfer between employees.</div></li>
</ul>
