<h2>Corporate Account Executive</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div></div>
<div><div><div><div><div>
<div>The level of role depends on candidate background, education, and experience:</div>
<div>Corporate Account Executive I (A5)</div>
<div>Corporate Account Executive II (A6)</div>
<div>Sr Account Executive (A7)</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div>Job Summary:</div>
<div>CEVA Logistics is currently hiring a Corporate Account Executive in Vancouver, BC. The Corporate Account Executive will be responsible for generating profitable revenue growth through marketing all products and services within a defined geographic territory.</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div>In this role, the Corporate Account Executive will:</div>
<ul>
<li>Retain and grow revenue with existing customers</li>
<li>Identify and develop new business opportunities.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<div></div>
<div>Summary of Key Responsibilities:</div>
<ul>
<li>Develop and maintain functional knowledge of the products, services and operations offered by CEVA and actively participate in product training sessions.</li>
<li>Interact regularly with Station Manager and Ops Managers, and develop close and cooperative working relationship with operational staff to ensure the customer’s needs are met</li>
<li>Schedule and conduct a pre-determined number of face-to-face sales calls as directed by the Regional Sales Manager or Station Manager</li>
<li>Input sales call information into CEVA-I, or other designated sales call software provided by the company and/or provide sales reports as directed by Regional Sales Manager or Station Manager</li>
<li>Maintain a pre-determined number of target accounts and demonstrate active attention to those accounts and progress toward closing</li>
<li>Meet sales threshold/quota as defined by company</li>
<li>Work cooperatively with other sales and operational staff to support a team-selling environment</li>
<li>Perform responsibilities in accordance with the terms of the Sales Performance Policy, Commission Model, and Acknowledgement Form</li>
<br>
</ul>
<div></div>
<div><b>Education, Experience, and Certifications:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>High School Degree or GED required with a Bachelor’s Degree preferred; or equivalent combination of work experience and education</li>
<li>Minimum five (5) years’ related in sales</li>
<li>Minimum three (3) years’ industry experience</li>
<br>
</ul>
<div></div>
<div>Additional Skills and Experience that Contribute to Success:</div>
<ul>
<li>Proven track record in generating profitable revenue growth and retaining existing customers</li>
<li>Ability to communicate and interact effectively with multi-functional and diverse backgrounds</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Ability to speak effectively before groups of customers or employees and recommend solutions to common problems</li>
<li>Must be able to read, write and speak English fluently</li>
<br>
</ul>
<div></div>
<div>Certifications and Licenses</div>
<ul>
<li>Valid state issued driver’s license</li>
<li>Acceptable driving record</li>
<li>Proof of current automobile insurance</li>
</ul>
