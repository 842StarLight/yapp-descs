<p>National Accounts Manager (H&amp;K Smart Fence)</p>
<p>H&amp;K Smart Fence is seeking a full-time National Accounts Manager to further develop and implement H&amp;K’s sales plan targeting private label offerings in electric fence and other related products, as well as our soon-to-launch Starkline® brand of electric fence products, to regional and national retail and online distributor outlets. This is a mainly office-based position in our downtown Des Moines headquarters, with travel regionally and nationally for trade shows and customer visits. Compensation consists of salary plus commissions and bonuses, a competitive insurance package and PTO. This position offers a unique opportunity to contribute to the growth and evolution of a successful global company at its early stages in the U.S., with compensation, performance measures and advancement opportunities designed to reward a long-term commitment to our company.</p>
<p><b>Main responsibilities: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Identify and attract strategic customers that offer the greatest growth opportunity and cultivate existing relationships to drive greater sales growth;</li>
<li>Collect and analyze market trends and data;</li>
<li>Develop and conduct effective sales presentations;</li>
<li>Respond productively to existing customer requests in a timely, professional manner;</li>
<li>Work closely with China-based staff to coordinate product sourcing and pricing;</li>
<li>Actively communicate and collaborate with H&amp;K Smart Fence team members to drive further development of the company’s brand and to most efficiently implement sales plan;</li>
<li>Utilize ERP software for customer relations and order management;</li>
<li>Attend trade shows and conduct effective and timely follow-up with leads.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Required Qualifications: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Excellent communication, presentation and leadership skills;</li>
<li>Team player with the ability to plan and implement sales strategy with limited supervision;</li>
<li>Ability to thrive in a competitive, goal-driven environment;</li>
<li>Ability to prioritize responsibilities and to operate with changing priorities;</li>
<li>Facility with MS Office, ability to read technical drawings.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Preferred qualities: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Past sales experience developing and maintaining relationships with major retail or online distributor customers in agricultural, fencing and/or garden hardware product lines;</li>
<li>Experience working with foreign manufacturers and their representatives;</li>
<li>Expertise with electric and traditional fencing, and/or agricultural/ranching products;</li>
<li>Positivity in representing an organization or company to a diverse customer base;</li>
<li>Excitement in engaging new (and old) technologies;</li>
<li>Understanding, patience and persistence in approaching opportunities and challenges;</li>
<li>Flexibility in facing adversity.</li>
</ul>
<p>The successful candidate for the position will be invigorated by thinking big (and having fun!) to further his/her own AND H&amp;K’s growth. H&amp;K is an EEO/AA employer. Interested applicants should send cover letter, resume, and ANY questions regarding H&amp;K and this position to Sol Varisco, Assistant General Manager, <i>sol(at)hkmanufacturing.com,</i></p>
<p>(515) 707-0020. We look forward to meeting you!</p>
<p><i>H&amp;K Smart Fence</i> and <i>American Ground Screw</i> are family-owned companies located in downtown Des Moines, IA. H&amp;K Smart Fence manufactures and sources welded metal and plastic products for the agricultural and consumer goods markets serving wholesale, large-scale retail and e-commerce customers throughout North America. Sharing ownership with major Chinese manufacturing and global trading companies, including one of the single largest and longest-operating manufacturer of electric fence products, H&amp;K Smart Fence recognizes considerable technical, production, distribution and price advantages across a variety of fence and garden hardware product lines. H&amp;K Smart Fence booked over $2mil in sales in its first full year of operations (2018) and is currently projecting 50% annual growth through 2021.</p>
