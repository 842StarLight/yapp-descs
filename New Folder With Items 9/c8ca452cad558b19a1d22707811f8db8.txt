<h2>Gemologist/Appraiser</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div><div>
<p>Our Gemologists are responsible for performing tasks that require the application of the Gemological Institute of America's standards and practices. The ideal candidate will possess a degree certificate from the Gemological Institute of America. The gemologist must be proficient in diamond and gemstone grading and analysis, as well as preparing jewelry appraisals and supporting our sales team.</p>
<br>
<p>Qualifications:</p>
<ul>
<li>Minimum of High school diploma required</li>
<li>Graduate Gemologist Diploma from the Gemological Institute of America</li>
<li>3 years proven experience as a gemologist is desired but we are willing to consider others with extremely positive references</li>
<li>Ability to use Windows-based personal computers, Point of Sale and CRM software applications, and software office suite</li>
<li>Excellent writing and oral communication skills</li>
</ul>
