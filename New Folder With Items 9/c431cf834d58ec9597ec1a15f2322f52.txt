<p><b>Opportunity </b></p>
<p>We have an exceptional opportunity for an independent and self-motivated professional to be a part of a cutting-edge investment advisory company. RE Wealth Advisors is a fast-growing and highly profitable investment &amp; wealth advisory company that is disrupting corporate finance through a combination of leveraging strategies, integrity, education, technology, and client-partner alignment.</p>
<p>We are seeking to add a best-in-class executive assistant to help us grow exponentially. Candidates should be entrepreneurial, possess an elevated level of aptitude, have excellent research and writing skills, be creative, organized, extremely detailed in nature, trustworthy, and disciplined. Our Executive Assistants must undertake complex assignments, meet tight deadlines and deliver superior performance. The ideal candidate possesses practical knowledge in corporate finance and financial markets, applies strong analytical skills to inform senior management of key trends and operates with an intense sense of urgency in a fast-paced setting.</p>
<p>Furthermore, you must also possess effective skills in delivering maximum client satisfaction while demonstrating capacity to provide comprehensive support to executive-level staff; schedule meetings, coordinate travel, process invoices, operate data spreadsheets, and manage all essential tasks with ability to prioritize, complete multiple tasks and follow through to achieve project goals.</p>
