<h2>MARKETING COORDINATOR (NYC)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>About Metro Media</b></p>
<p>Metro is the inventor of the free daily newspaper concept and the world’s largest global newspaper. In the US, Metro is the fourth largest newspaper by circulation and reaches over 1 million readers every day in New York, Philadelphia and Boston.Over the years, Metro has evolved into Metro Media, a multi-brand,multi-platform media house, designing advertising and content solutions across print, outdoor and digital and reaching over 4 million people every month.</p>
<p><b>Job Description</b></p>
<p>Metro Media is currently seeking a talented Marketing Coordinator. Based out of New York and reporting to the Marketing Director, this position is responsible for the production and dissemination of marketing content for both B2B and B2C audiences in order to promote and expand Metro and its clients’ brands across all platforms using multiple channels.</p>
<p>The responsibilities include, but are not limited to:</p>
<p>· B2B – Maintain and develop Metro Media’s website and audience; create content such as posts and case studies, disseminate content via email, social media, print and digital advertising campaigns to generate sales leads and develop advertising sales.</p>
<p>· Sales Support – Collaborate with sales teams and designers to create sales collateral such as sell sheets or media kits, and develop sales pitches, presentations and proposals.</p>
<p>· Digital – Assist sales reps with developing digital campaign pitches; manage DSP and set up, optimize, maintain and report on programmatic ad campaigns; regularly update sales team with any new education materials.</p>
<p>· B2C – Manage and develop Metro’s readers’ engagement program: work with sales teams to set up in-house and client-sponsored sweepstakes within editorial CMS, handle email and print promotion, work with vendor to process winner notification.</p>
<p>· Analytics – Monitor websites analytics and advertising campaigns’ performance.</p>
<p>· General marketing duties – As assigned such as tracking client giveaways, helping to manage media partnerships,street promotions, or other special projects.</p>
<p><b>Requirements: </b></p>
<p>· 2-3 years’ marketing experience, producing marketing content and deploying multi-channel campaigns incl. email and social media.</p>
<p>· BA/BS degree or equivalent working experience.</p>
<p>· Excellent writing and interpersonal skills, attention to detail and project management skills.</p>
<p>· Eager-to-learn, self-starter, creative thinker, and excellent communicator, yet pragmatic to get things done with limited resources;</p>
<p>· Proficient with email marketing platforms such as MailChimp or dotmailer, CMS such as WordPress,Microsoft Office, Hootsuite; programmatic experience a bonus, but not required.</p>
