<h2>Supply Chain Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div><div><div>
<p>This position will focus on the development, implementation and ongoing execution of the Sales &amp; Operations Planning (S&amp;OP) process for TH Foods. This will include demand planning and supply planning.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Essential Duties and Requirements</b> include the following. Other duties may be assigned.</p>
<ul>
<li>Lead Cross Functional Team (CFT) to implement formalized S&amp;OP Process</li>
<li>Lead adoption of new processes throughout the organization</li>
<li>Formalize Sales Planning/Forecast process</li>
<li>Enhance current material planning processes for greater efficiency and sustainability for future growth</li>
<li>Develops and improves processes, tools, and methodologies, and performs training to increase staff capability</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Leads processes to assure leverage of the organization’s purchasing power in relationships with group purchasing organizations, distributors, suppliers, and inventory management</li>
<li>Participates during the design and development stage to ensure Supply Chain capability and capacity supports the needs of the capital projects.</li>
<li>Responsible for recognizing and reporting food safety and employee safety issues.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Supervisory Responsibilities</b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>This position does have supervisory responsibilities of the material planners, and demand planner.</p>

<p>JOB REQUIREMENTS</p>
<p><b>Education and/or Experience</b></p>
<p>Bachelor’s degree required. At least 5-7 years of relevant experience required. Extensive experience with Microsoft Office, including Word, Excel, and PowerPoint is required. Experience with integrated ERP software. Proven experience with Sales &amp; Operations Planning (S&amp;OP) implementation</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Knowledge Skills and Abilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Knowledge of accounting practices, costs related to production, general commodity market, production processes, negotiating material prices, organizing and analyzing data.</li>
<li>Ability to accurately analyze data and review work carefully</li>
<li>Ability to multi-task numerous responsibilities at the same time</li>
<li>Ability to work with others well, ability to communicate orally and in written form across all levels of the organization</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Must be a team player, be organized and have the ability to handle multiple projects</li>
<li>Ability to work independently with minimal supervision or function in a team environment sharing responsibility, roles, and accountability</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Strong attention to detail and accuracy.</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Understanding of complex information and requirements</li>
<li>Good prioritization skills and be flexible enough to adapt plans</li>
<br>
</ul>
