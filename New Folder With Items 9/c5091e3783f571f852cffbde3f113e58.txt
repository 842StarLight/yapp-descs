<h2>Senior Marketing Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">POSITION OVERVIEW
<br><br>
Alarm.com, an innovative and rapidly growing smart home technology company, is seeking a Senior Marketing Manager to help build and manage our multi-channel partner content strategy. The Senior Manager will work cross-functionally with product management, sales, partner services, and marketing communications to develop the voice of the brand for our partners, as well as create multi-channel content including sales presentations, partner trainings, white papers, trade advertising, email communications, and more.
<br><br>
The Senior Manager will also be responsible for collecting and assimilating competitive intelligence, which will help drive messaging and positioning across all marketing content, while also providing best practices and selling points for all internal, partner-facing employees.
<br><br>
The ideal candidate is a highly strategic self-starter, with outstanding communication and project management skills.
<br><br>
<b>How you will contribute:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Lead cross-functional sales, product and marketing communications teams to create a consistent, singular voice for all partner content</li>
<li>Ensure consistent messaging across partner communications, developing an internal review process for all content contributors</li>
<li>Evaluate existing partner-facing content and identify new opportunities as well as areas for improvement in both approach and message across multiple channels</li>
<li>Develop and maintain new partner content based on evaluations, including (but not limited to), sales presentations, white papers, trainings, and advertising</li>
<li>Build a market intelligence program, analyzing key products, services and marketing efforts, drawing comparisons to Alarm.com products and identifying Alarm.com differentiators; maintain on a regular basis with internal distribution up to the executive level</li>
<li>Liaise with product management on new product launches to create positioning, leveraging market intelligence to identify key features and benefits to promote</li>
<li>Provide internal education for cross-functional teams on market intelligence and landscape, partner messaging and communication best practices</li>
<li>Assist consumer marketing team in the creation of short form content that conveys messaging and learnings from partners</li>
<li>Engage, influence and gain buy-in cross-functionally across product management, sales, engineering and marketing to drive major projects forward</li>
</ul>
<b>Requirements:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>8 + years of experience in marketing, communications, product marketing and/or product management</li>
<li>Proven experience developing and optimizing marketing communications and programs</li>
<li>Exceptional attention to detail and problem-solving capacity are crucial</li>
<li>Effective at driving cross team collaboration and building consensus</li>
<li>Outstanding verbal and written communication skills, with experience crafting and presenting strategic initiatives to multiple stakeholders</li>
<li>Ability to develop and maintain strong working relationships with internal and external parties</li>
<li>Ability to thrive in a fast-paced, high-energy environment</li>
</ul>
