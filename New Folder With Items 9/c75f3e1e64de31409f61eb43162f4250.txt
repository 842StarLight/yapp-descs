<h2>AP/Accounting Clerk</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div><div><div><div><div>
<p>Our firm has an excellent opportunity for an accounts payable/accounting clerk to work in an administrative role. Professional staff members in this role will gain valuable experience in serving a wide variety of clients.</p>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Responsibilities:</b></h3>
<ul>
<li>Assist with accounts payable and accounts receivable transactions including data entry, cash receipts, and cash disbursements</li>
<li>Prepare state sales and use tax returns</li>
<li>Prepare state registration forms</li>
<li>Prepare applications for extension of filing individual and business income tax returns</li>
</ul>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Skills/Qualifications:</b></h3>
<ul>
<li>Proven ability to work and focus in a fast-paced, results based, and quality service environment</li>
<li>Contributes to team effort and maintain individual work</li>
<li>Attention to detail</li>
<li>Ability to follow instructions precisely and resolve any questions promptly</li>
<li>Possess a working knowledge of the following software programs: Microsoft Word and Excel</li>
</ul>
