<div><b>Position Overview:</b></div>
<div>Good Earth Tools is seeking a Mechanical Design Engineer to add to our innovative, customer-focused team of engineers! Candidates must be creative with a strong mechanical aptitude and be self-motivated to take ideas from concept to completion in a fast-paced environment. This position requires experience with 3D design software such as Solid Works. The responsibilities for this position include, but are not limited to, the following:</div>
<div></div>

<ul>
<li>
<div>Support Customers, Sales and Marketing efforts to provide solutions to heavy industrial issues.</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Complete concept, design, and analysis of industrial products consisting of machined components, steel fabrications, forgings and castings utilizing SolidWorks.</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Qualify and perform engineering change requests as required.</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Partner with Mechanical and Electrical colleagues on system design and component integration.</div>
</li>
<li>

</li>
<li>
<div>Coordinate out-sourced activities with regard to new developments in the areas of forgings and cast products.</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Provide hands-on installation and troubleshooting of new and existing designs.</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Effectively source, support manufacturing, and offer technical expertise.</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Develop bills of material, production routings, inspection documentation, and work instructions.</div>
