<h2>Account Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>VIRTUS ROCKY MOUNTAIN </b></p>
<p><b>ACCOUNT MANAGER DUTIES &amp; RESPONSIBILITIES</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bring a Relentlessly Positive Attitude to work daily.</li>
<li>Assist Account Executive on assigned accounts, managing key processes that serve clients and undertaking the client-facing and internal activities related to these processes.</li>
<li>Coordinate the renewal of current client business from origination to completion with direction from Account Executive</li>
<li>Informs Client of any and all changes that may affect insurance premiums or coverage.</li>
<li>Generates materials for client presentations and meetings</li>
<li>Gather and compile exposure information from the client, including compiling loss-run schedules</li>
<li>Respond in a timely manner to the requests and needs of the client, Producer and/or team.</li>
<li>Effectively manage the daily needs of current clients – additions, deletions, lender negotiations, contract reviews and certificates</li>
<li>Manage the invoice process and follow-up on receivables for assigned clients, ensuring timely collections.</li>
<li>Ensure that account data supports the integrity of company accounting and tracking systems.</li>
<li>Assist Account Executive in nurturing strong client relationships and assist in driving additional sales</li>
<li>Primary internal interface with Virtus on client administration matters.</li>
<li>Act with responsiveness, urgency and professionalism in all client matters.</li>
<li>Accept accountability for the quality of their work and the work of the account team.</li>
<li>Proficiency in writing Professional, Thorough and Detailed communications to all Clients and Carriers is required.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Preferred Experience: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Minimum 5 years of previous related Property and Casualty insurance experience</li>
<li>Active Property and Casualty Insurance license <b>required</b>
</li>
<li>Proficiency in Microsoft office products such as Word, PowerPoint, Excel and Outlook are required.</li>
<li>Demonstrates strong organizational and project management skills. Exudes confidence and sound technical knowledge.</li>
<li>Applies a solid knowledge of major insurance coverages, (including general/product liability, commercial automobile, workers’ compensation, property, time element, umbrella, financial coverages, etc.), client-specific coverages.</li>
<li>Understands the structure and content of insurance contracts and is aware of most commercial insurance coverages.</li>
<li>Insurance Designations or desire to complete designations a plus</li>
<li>Experience with an Agency Management Software system, AMS360 experience preferred</li>
<li>Experience with Drop Box is desired</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Preferred Qualities: </b></p>
<ul><li>Positive, Hard-Working, Self-Motivated, Team Player, Adaptable to Change.</li></ul>
<p>Job Type: Full-time</p>
<p>Experience:</p>
<ul><li>Property &amp; Casualty: 5 years (Preferred)</li></ul>
<p>Work Location:</p>
<ul><li>One location</li></ul>
<p>Job Duties:</p>
<ul>
<li>Manage multiple accounts, ensuring that a high level of customer retention and satisfaction is maintained</li>
<li>Engage with multiple customers on a daily basis by reaching out to new clients, and by developing campaigns for maintaining customer loyalty</li>
<li>Implement strategies for retaining customers requesting to terminate or downgrade their account</li>
<li>Other duties as requested</li>
</ul>
<p>This Job Is Ideal for Someone Who Is:</p>
<ul>
<li>Detail-oriented -- would rather focus on the details of work than the bigger picture</li>
<li>Dependable -- more reliable than spontaneous</li>
<li>Adaptable/flexible -- enjoys doing work that requires frequent shifts in direction</li>
</ul>
<p>This Company Describes Its Culture as:</p>
<ul><li>People-oriented -- supportive and fairness-focused</li></ul>
<p>Schedule:</p>
<ul><li>Monday to Friday</li></ul>
