<h2>Business Development Service Representative</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>No experience necessary. Will train the right candidate. GREAT opportunity to work for an awesome company. Hourly wage PLUS Weekly Bonus! If you have been in the car business, Baystate is unlike any other dealership. Best dealership to work for around. Small town dealership feel with potential to make great $$$.</p>
<p>Responsibilities:</p>
<ul>
<li>Set First Service Appointments for customers who bought a vehicle</li>
<li>Call customers who missed their service appointment to reschedule</li>
<li>Call customers who declined service work at last visit</li>
<li>Send email blasts with service specials and coupons to our customer database</li>
<li>Call customers after service visit to see how it went, how the car is running and if there is anything that would prevent them from giving a good survey</li>
<li>Take service calls and schedule service appointments</li>
<li>Call customer when special order parts come in to schedule service appointment</li>
<li>Post service specials, promotions and coupons on Baystate Facebook page</li>
<li>Schedule recall appointments</li>
<li>Use company software to determine when a customer is out of warranty</li>
</ul>
