<h2>Account Manager, Enterprise Solutions</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Are you looking to join a dynamic startup environment with hyper growth? If so were looking to talk with you. As an Enterprise Solutions Account Manager you will be the mastermind behind the entire success of your all your partners and will oversee partnerships with a total annual worth of 7 figures.</p>
<br>
Requirements:<br>
<ul>
<li>This team is lean, young and hungry. Therefore, being energetic and having a drive to be part of an innovative fast-paced startup environment is key to your success.</li>
<li>
There are many moving parts to juggle with internal and external stakeholders. You will need excellent organization, prioritization and communication skills (verbal, written communication, presentation and facilitation) to excel.</li>
<li>
The ability to multitask and prioritize your workload, as well as handle details accurately while keeping the big picture in mind will help you succeed.</li>
<li>
This team is constantly looking for new ways to grow partnerships with their clients. A strong analytical mindset with the ability to build strategies through collecting, organizing, dissecting, evaluating, and analyzing data using Microsoft Excel is a huge benefit.</li>
<li>
Self-motivated and a team player with a can do attitude that is willing to go the extra mile will be able to lead their professional growth to new heights.</li>
<li>
Detail oriented with an ability to see the bigger picture – will allow you to think at an advanced level.</li>
<li>
We are collaborative and work cross functionally not only locally but globally. You will need to hone in on your verbal, written, presentation and facilitation skills in order to succeed with various stakeholders across the globe.</li>
<li>
One of the following; Previous experience in the online or mobile advertising industry, history of growing accounts and achieving revenue targets, or one or more years of experience in a customer facing role</li>
<li>
Willingness to travel (client meetings, conferences)</li>
<li>
Bachelor’s degree</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
Role:<br>
<ul>
<li>You will work with major app developers, game developers, mobile agencies, and media agencies</li>
<li>
Be the main point of contact to lead and maximize business opportunities and revenue targets.</li>
<li>
Establish and maintain strong relationships with our <b>key</b> clients/advertising partners.</li>
<li>
You will own the performance and success of <b>high profile</b> clients.
</li>
<li>You will be responsible for setting up, monitoring, optimizing and analyzing the performance of all your campaigns.</li>
<li>
Derive insights into the delivery and performance as well as apply them in the optimization process to ensure the highest level of service.</li>
<li>
Analyze performance and strategize on improvements that will increase the success of the clients business goals and expectations</li>
<li>
Understand the product inside and out to project manage all troubleshooting efforts and integrations with the Technical Team to ensure issues and integrations are communicated, resolved and completed in a timely manner</li>
<li>
Think critically about how to best use data for driving business/product decisions</li>
<li>
Support Sales Managers in closing new business including developing campaign strategies to meet client objectives</li>
<li>
Develop product and market expertise in order to best advise clients regarding ad-buys, supply sources, attribution, bidding, ad-units and targeting</li>
</ul>
