<h2>Demand Planner 1 or 2</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Johnstone Supply, Inc., the nation s largest HVACR distribution Cooperative, has an opportunity for a <b>Demand Planner 1 or 2 </b>at our corporate office in Portland, Oregon.
</p>
<p>Our Planners provide advanced level skills and expertise aligned with best practice and innovative techniques to optimize inventory across the cooperative network. This includes the organization, development, execution, maintenance, communication, continuous improvement, and reporting on detailed demand, inventory, purchase, stock transfers, and disposition activities for all products within the cooperative network.
</p>
<p>This position is responsible for managing large volumes of purchase orders and small to medium volumes of demand management and moderately difficult inventory optimization activities.</p>
<p>
A descriptive approach from a <b>Planner 1</b> and a predictive approach from a <b>Planners 2</b> to supply chain problem solving, optimization, and execution is expected respectively.</p>
<p><b>
Key Responsibilities:</b></p>
<p><b>
Demand Alignment</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Executes SIOP/S&amp;OP activities for planning portfolio</li>
<li>Create and maintain system forecasts utilizing forecasting software (Logility) and other tools</li>
<li>Collaborate with Senior Planners, analyze, execute, and communicate strategic inventory plans to balance supply and demand.</li>
<li>Collaborate with Senior Planners, and maintain stocking strategies which align with service level goals and product life cycles.</li>
<li>Ensure replenishment and stocking plans align with all types of anticipation, hedge, fluctuation, buffer transportation, lot size or other inventory buy strategies.
</li>
<li>Collaborate and influence the organization toward program improvements and optimization</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Purchase Order Management</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Create, maintain, and manage all aspects of purchase orders from creation to close while maintaining a high level of timeliness, transactional, and data accuracy.</li>
<li>Review and execute purchasing activities to align with strategic, opportunity, or other operations plans.</li>
<li>Provide forecasts, expediting lists, and communicate other tactical requirements to suppliers</li>
<li>Ensure purchase orders are placed to align with short, medium, and long term planning and inventory targets.</li>
<li>Purchase order placement must balance the capacities and efficiencies for the distribution centers, transportation companies, and suppliers.</li>
<li>Monitor inventory levels and execute corrective actions to balance the flow of levels of inventory</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Reporting and Analysis</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Participate in SKU maintenance activities across the network including activities such as ABC ranking settings, product life cycle settings within the systems including obsolescence.</li>
<li>Perform routine audits of systems and reports to ensure the highest level of performance and accuracy and identify problems, root causes, and implement solutions.</li>
<li>Utilize and create reporting, benchmarking, and analysis techniques to ensure accuracy of data, performance, and efficiencies of processes.</li>
<li>Utilize and create reporting and analysis to identify, communicate, and drive continuous improvement activities across the organization.</li>
<li>Monitor and manage KPI related tasks and serve as a back-up for other team members</li>
</ul>
