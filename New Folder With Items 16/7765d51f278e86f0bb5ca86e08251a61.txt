<div>With new innovations and exciting expansions plans in place, there has never been a better time to join the Ferrero Group and be extraordinary with us!</div>

<div></div>
<div>The Senior Trade Analyst will be responsible for partnering with key business partners within the Sales team to improve customer planning and trade promotion processes required to drive future growth in North America. The person will report to the Trade Promotions and Planning Manager and sit in our Parsippany office.</div>
<p></p>
<div>
<b>Main Responsibilities:</b>
</div>
<div></div>
<ul>
<li>Develop a keen understanding of the Sales Strategy and Trade Management objectives to drive improved customer planning and trade promotion effectiveness across the Sales team</li>
<li>Execute details analysis of customer plan activities, trade budget risks and trade promotion performance</li>
<li>Provide insights for course corrections and recommendations to drive top and bottom line growth</li>
<li>Support Channel leadership to measure and explain business results</li>
<li>Collaborate with the finance team to provide insight on current liabilities and future risks</li>
<li>Develop and educate on best practices in Customer and Trade Promotions procedures</li>
<li>Work cross functionally with necessary departments to maximize performance of Sales Revenue Management processes and tools to ensure business needs are being met</li>
<li>Be the SME on key business and tool processes to support daily operations</li>
<li>Lead various aspects of project work associated with the Sales and Trade processes and toolsets</li>
<li>Support the development of materials and lead Sales training on the processes and tools</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<div>
<b>Who we are looking for:</b>
</div>
<div></div>
<ul>
<li>At least 5 years of Sales experience, with at least 2 of those in Sales Operations, Trade Management or Finance</li>
<li>Superior analytical and problem solving skills</li>
<li>Experience leading business process and integration process</li>
<li>Demonstrated ability to sort through complex issues, translate and communicate them in a clear, concise manner</li>
<li>Strong attention to detail, time management, and prioritization skills</li>
<li>Bachelor’s Degree in either Business in Finance, with an advanced Business degree preferred</li>
<li>SAAS and SAP CRM/ERP experience is preferred</li>
<br>
</ul>
