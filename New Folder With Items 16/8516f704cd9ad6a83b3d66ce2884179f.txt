<h2>Project Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div><div>
<p>Manage all site industrial projects to ensure that technical and commercial aspects of projects are successfully completed and meets internal and external customer requirements and design standards.</p>
<br>
<br>
<b>Key Accountabilities</b>
<ul>
<li>Directs and oversees project team to ensure design and engineering is properly carried out with respect to customer requirements and (internal and external) design standards.</li>
<li>Analyzes and monitors available engineering resources and, if necessary, involves external engineering sub-contractors.</li>
<li>Permanently monitors the project progress and checks the project results based on customer requirements and company guidelines.</li>
<li>Ensures that the corresponding agreements on technical details and deadlines are made with the customers and register additional deliveries and services (claims management).</li>
<li>Ensures SGL support for installation of the delivered equipment on site.</li>
<li>Provides professional advice and support to the procurement group for process equipment during the order negotiations, especially regarding mechanical design related issues.</li>
<li>Guides, encourages and motivates the assigned team members and makes proposals regarding personnel individual measures.</li>
</ul>
</div></div>
