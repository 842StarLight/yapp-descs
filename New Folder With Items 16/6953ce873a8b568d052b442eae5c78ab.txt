Job Description<br>
<br>
<b>Role overview</b>
<br>
<br>
The Regional Sales Manager for Middle Market is responsible for meeting or exceeding the annual bookings quota for the assigned territory.
<br>
<br>
<b>A day in the life…</b>
<ul>
<li>Consistently identifies opportunities, develops sales strategy and closes new business in the assigned middle market segment</li>
<li>
Identifies, develops and retains new business relationships</li>
<li>
Effectively presents Accolade’s offerings to qualified prospects</li>
<li>
Effectively leverages Accolade executives, the pre-sales team and services staff to support customer presentations</li>
<li>
Develops and executes strategies to generate cost effective sales leads</li>
<li>
Develops a pipeline of new sales opportunities in alignment with objectives that are established annually</li>
<li>
Develops and maintains an effective sales and market penetration plan for assigned territory</li>
<li>
Leverages the sales milestone process to ensure accuracy with monthly, quarterly and annual forecasts</li>
<li>
Documents all sales contacts, activities and sales process steps in Accolade’s sales tools and systems</li>
<li>
Solicits product and market feedback from customers, prospects and partners. Communicates this feedback to sales leadership, marketing and the product development team (feature requests, market trends, competition intelligence, new opportunities)</li>
<li>
Complies with all corporate policies and completes all administrative tasks on time</li>
</ul>Qualifications<br>
<br>
<b>What we are looking for…</b>
<ul>
<li>BA in a business or technical related area of study</li>
<li>
Minimum 3-5 years of strategic selling experience</li>
<li>
Experience selling complex, multi-stakeholder solutions. SaaS, health &amp; welfare benefits, healthcare capital equipment, healthcare IT, health and welfare benefits outsourcing, human resources IT benefits software</li>
<li>
Embraces and thrives in a culture of transparency, accountability and trust</li>
<li>
Self-starter with ability to work independently, prioritize work and coordinate multiple tasks within tight time constraints</li>
<li>
Ability to navigate the strategic levels of customer organizations, identify key decision-makers, build relationships with senior executives and schedule meetings with key stakeholders</li>
<li>
Ability to establish strong team relationships in a matrix organization</li>
<li>
Ability to perform in a dynamic and fast-paced environment</li>
<li>
Possess account knowledge, executive-level customer contacts and industry relationships inside the employer market space</li>
<li>
Command of social media for the benefit of lead generation, gaining access to executives and networking with key stakeholders</li>
<li>
Strong aptitude with business software tools (Word, Excel, PowerPoint, Outlook etc)</li>
<li>
Strong business acumen</li>
<li>
Demonstrated ability to successfully negotiate complex contract</li>
<li>
Demonstrated ability to accurately forecast monthly, quarterly, and annual revenue</li>
