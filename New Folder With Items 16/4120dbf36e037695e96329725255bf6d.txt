<h2>Director Product Management - ERP/Financials</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p><b>Summary</b></p>
<p>
The Director of Product Management will identify new and expanded market opportunities in the Public Sector space, show ownership of requirements definition and design of products / solutions that meet demonstrated market needs, project management of creation and delivery of products / solutions, coordinating with development, quality assurance, business leaders and other stakeholders; and drive a product roadmap that sets CentralSquare up for success in the market.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
The individual filling this role must have a passion for technology and designing innovative new products and solutions, and a love for hands-on Agile-style project management.</p>
<p><b>
Essential Duties and Responsibilities</b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Understanding what great product looks like, create a product roadmap to support our business strategy, leverage strong process and project management abilities to envision and manage product lifecycle from idea inception, support go-to-market plans, and lead with a customer first mindset.</li>
<li>Become an expert within CentralSquare on the Public Sector market segment, current and future. Provide SaaS thought leadership and direction as new SaaS applications are created and for the re-engineering of existing capabilities.</li>
<li>Develop the requirements for highly differentiated Public Sector solutions, products, and / or services, including strategy definition, requirements analysis, and delineation of the market opportunity.</li>
<li>Provide timely and effective reports of product performance – specifically for development and product release plans.</li>
<li>Collaborate with team to get feedback from the field on new feature requirements and enhancements - in efforts to drive product utilization.</li>
<li>Conduct market research, monitor competitive activity, and identify customer targets and their needs; apply learnings to current products and in ideation sessions for the development of new products or solutions.</li>
<li>Participate in sales, business development, and existing customer calls as or meetings as appropriate to hear and understand first-hand the needs and requirements of prospects and customers.</li>
<li>Ensure product development processes adhere to Agile (or similar) principles and align with company resource planning and allocation efforts.</li>
<li>Articulate new product features and benefits to internal groups, including marketing and sales.</li>
<li>Stewardship of Products from Concept to Launch</li>
<li>Manage the P&amp;L of the product line and demonstrated success in expanding into adjacencies.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p>
</p>
<p><b>Supervisory Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Lead a team of product management and engineering professionals in the development of both strategic and tactical deliverables, and manage the team, a budget, and associated resources toward those ends.</li>
<li>Assist in the assignment and management of a large cross-functional team to deliver product development results, leading through influence and by example.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Qualifications</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Passion for and ability to understand customer needs and deliver great solutions. A track-record for building software that is both efficient and effective with exceptional user experience.</li>
<li>Excellent strategic thinking and prioritization skills. Ability to create a winning roadmap to achieve business goals while making difficult trade-off decisions</li>
<li>Demonstrable organizational and project management skills are required.</li>
<li>Must be able to know how to do research, mine data and apply strategic thinking to executive-level pitches and presentations.</li>
<li>Strong analytical, verbal and written communications and presentation skills required.</li>
<li>Facility with basic business software, including the Microsoft Office Suite and Google Apps, is required. Facility with project management applications such as Jira, BaseCamp or MS Project is also required.</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Experience with defining, designing, implementing, and managing various product management and development methodologies.</li>
<li>Thorough understanding of PM skills, processes, best practices and ability to teach others.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p>
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Education and/or Experience</b></p>
<ul>
<li>8+ years of software Product Management experience is desired.</li>
<li>8+ years of experience in managing, coaching, and leading senior level and mid-level staff including multi-functional teams.</li>
<li>Master’s in Business or Computer Science preferred (or equivalent experience)</li>
<li>Experience in community development, citizen engagement and work/asset management</li>
<li>Experience with Pragmatic Marketing Framework</li>
<li>Hands on design experience with enterprise B2B applications for both technical and business users</li>
<li>Experience defining technical product features; from inception to execution</li>
<li>Demonstrated experience developing Software products.</li>
<li>SaaS experience is preferred.</li>
<li>Demonstrated visionary leader. Ability to develop and cultivate a dynamic, progressive, and technologically advanced organization.</li>
</ul>
