<p><b>Title: Sales Territory Manager</b></p>
<p><b>Location: Area / Regional</b></p>
<p>bidadoo auctions is the largest and most trusted construction and industrial auction / remarketing company on the world’s largest online marketplace. Our customers include leading Fortune 500 and Regional companies such as United Rentals, Terex/Genie, Sunbelt Rentals, Hertz Equipment, John Deere Remarketing, Skanska Construction, H&amp;E Equipment, GE Capital, and many others. As featured in Entrepreneur Magazine’s Hot Business category, bidadoo is rapidly expanding to meet the tremendous growth in online global equipment sales and remarketing.In this role you will work with new and existing accounts, building long term customer relationships.</p>
<p>You will work with a broad range of primarily used construction and industrial equipment, selling our auction, remarketing, eCommerce and other services. You will work with companies in various equipment-related industries, including construction, equipment manufacturing, equipment finance, equipment sales and rental, mining, transportation, utilities, government, etc.</p>
<p>You will aggressively source new business opportunities as well as maintain existing relationships through a combination of techniques, including networking, cold calling, advertising, direct mailings with follow-up, and other proactive prospecting methods.</p>
<p><b>Responsibilities include: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Have a comprehensive knowledge of new and used equipment sales in the areas of construction, transportation, material handling, manufacturing, etc.</li>
<li>Consult with customers on the valuation and sales estimates of machinery and equipment.</li>
<li>Drive revenue through fees associated with our professional auction, remarketing and consignment services</li>
<li>Develop a strategy and plan to effectively develop and manage your assigned territory</li>
<li>Conduct and present research on various equipment segments, industries and product lines.</li>
<li>Developing and delivering superior proposals and presentations showing business value to customers and prospects</li>
<li>Establish relationships with senior management within target accounts</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Background and Qualifications include: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>At least 3-5 years of proven sales experience, including outside/field sales and large accounts</li>
<li>Proven revenue attainment record through proactive lead generation and consultative selling techniques</li>
<li>Have excellent oral and written communication skills</li>
<li>Have excellent business presentation skills</li>
<li>Proficiency in basic computer skills and applications</li>
<li>Familiarity and experience with internet technologies and sales</li>
<li>Background in the areas which would be helpful include: new and used equipment sales, equipment rental, business valuation and appraisal consulting, equipment auctions, etc.</li>
<li>Experience with buying and/or selling over the internet would be beneficial</li>
</ul>
<p>Job Type: Full-time</p>
