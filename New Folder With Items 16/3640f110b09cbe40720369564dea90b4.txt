<h2>Administrative Assistant</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Supports the Enterprise by supporting the field operational team and provides visibility and guidance on Payroll and Training initiatives by performing the following duties:</p>
<p>Contract Administrative Assistant Roles and Priorities Are:</p>
<p>1. Regular and recurring interaction in our field locations to make routine contact with Operations field employees, supervisors and managers and have daily contact with the HR/Payroll Field Representative.</p>
<p>a. Ensure timely receipt, visibility and resolutions of their Payroll needs/requirements</p>
<p>b. Ensure timely completion of Compliance Actions and Documentation as requested by the Operational / HR Field leaders</p>
<p>c. Operational Support</p>
<p>2. Execute the Contract Administrative Assistant actions required by the HR/Payroll Field Representative</p>
<p>Essential duties and responsibilities include the following; however other duties may be assigned.</p>
<p>Compliance:</p>
<p>1. Prepares Contract Deliverables: Calculates and reports Weekly Productive Man Hours; prepares and formats Weekly Completed Training Report; prepares and formats Quarterly and Annual Self-Assessments.</p>
<p>2. Prepares Contract Meeting Agenda and maintains Contract Action Tracker.</p>
<p>3. Oversees Employee Bioassay: Assigns temporary dosimeters; schedules In Vivo Exposure Chest Count Appointments for enrolled employees according to their frequency; tracks and manages internal and external bioassay equipment and/or supplies; updates Radiation Exposure Monitoring in the Dosimetry Enrollment System based on employee location assignments and access authorizations and maintains related documentation.</p>
<p>4. Assists employees in completing on-line E-qip Package for Initial Clearance Investigations and Clearance Reinvestigations.</p>
<p>5. Composes and formats Integrated Work Documents for each High-Hazard Area/Facility in conjunction with the area supervisor annually.</p>
<p>6. Prepares Quarterly Overtime Report and provides it to the HR/Payroll Field Representative.</p>
<p>7. Primary Contact for Custodial Dept./LANS Security Random Drug Testing</p>
<p>8. Provides back up support in training as required: schedules and cancels training sessions; retrieves and prepare reports; verifies training completion.</p>
<p>9. Performs other duties as requested.</p>
<p>Operations Support:</p>
<p>1. Acts as main point of contact for all incoming telephone calls: Fields customer concerns and relay their concerns and contact information to the appropriate area supervisor; directs calls to appropriate person; takes and relays phone messages; deflects sales and creditor calls; coordinates telephone roll-over as needed.</p>
<p>2. Creates documents and manages document workflow: Assists operational staff in development of program tracking and creates associated documents and/or posters; creates and distributes weekly Training Calendar; copies and distributes printed materials; maintains employee home contact information; oversees and maintains Weekly Safety Talks and completed rosters, which can include research and composition of a relevant topic; scans and forwards documents; sorts and distributes incoming mail and faxes; updates Active Employee Listing, Employee Crew Listing, On Call List, Overtime List, Seniority List, or other employee listings upon request; creates other documents as needed: Flow Charts, Roster, Memos, Posters, Flyers, Forms, Tabs, Databases, Charts, Calendars</p>
<p>3. Act as first-line computer and technical support.</p>
<p>4. Creates and tracks equipment vouchers for safety glasses and safety shoes; maintains equipment acknowledgement forms.</p>
<p>5. Attend meetings and take minutes as required.</p>
<p>6. Maintains Office Supply Inventory and places Office Supply Orders.</p>
<p>7. Performs other duties as requested</p>
<p>Payroll:</p>
<p>1. Oversees and coordinates Safety Incentive Program and Performance Incentive Program and creates and/or maintains any associated records and documentation</p>
<p>2. Creates and maintains Annual PTO Calendars.</p>
<p>3. Review daily Mission Control logs for timekeeping issues.</p>
<p>4. Reviews and edits employee timecards, creates and maintains associated records and documentation.</p>
<p>5. Codes employee rate types and adjusts employee timesheets according to reported shift, safety premium, and acting foreman time worked.</p>
<p>6. Collects and remits salaried overhead employee timesheets and leave slips.</p>
<p>7. Update shifts, and alerts in online timekeeping database (KRONOS).</p>
<p>8. Inputs, adjusts, and verifies balances for employee PTO requests and maintains associated records and documentation.</p>
<p>9. Prepares Weekly Payroll Exceptions Report including: pay rate exceptions, shift employees and rates, temp employees and hours worked, worker's compensation payments, under 40 hours report, travel reimbursements, environmental pay, overtime worked, and check adjustments created.</p>
<p>10. Verifies Input Validation Report and coordinates with Payroll to correct errors.</p>
<p>11. Formats and distributes PTO Accruals spreadsheet</p>
<p>12. Creates and maintains payroll reports</p>
<p>13. Sort and distribute paycheck by crew.</p>
<p>14. Track and report employee FMLA usage.</p>
<p>15. Fields and researches employee pay and leave accrual concerns then explains research findings to employees and prepares Check Adjustments if required.</p>
<p>16. Prepare timekeeping reports as requested</p>
<p>17. Assists the Payroll Department in the identification and correction of issues related to the functionality of time recording devices</p>
<p>18. Print and distribute weekly time sheets to all crews</p>
<p>19. Collect signed weekly time sheets and scan to appropriate company</p>
<p>20. Performs other duties as requested.</p>
<p>Job Type: Full-time</p>
<p>Salary: $50,000.00 to $58,000.00 /year</p>
<p>Experience:</p>
<ul>
<li>Data Entry: 1 year (Preferred)</li>
<li>administrative assistant: 1 year (Preferred)</li>
</ul>
<p>Education:</p>
<ul><li>High school or equivalent (Preferred)</li></ul>
