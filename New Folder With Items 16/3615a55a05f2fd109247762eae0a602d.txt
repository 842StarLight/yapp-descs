<h2>In-house Sign Graphic Artist/Designer (5YRS EXP. REQUIRED)NO CONTRACT WORK</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Billings Sign Service Sign Graphic Artist/ Representative</b></p>
<p>The Production and Graphic Design/Representative will work from start to finish of the sign orders, including taking orders from customers, sending quotes and then giving specifics to those who would manufacture the sign. This is a position that requires a designer ON SITE to meet with customers in and out of office. Design/Representative will proofread, check, and print layouts, as well as participate in product development and marketing to identify graphic design needs. This is a career opportunity and we are seeking an intelligent, polished, and exemplary service orientated employee.</p>
<p><b>Customer Service: </b>Employees must provide great customer service by interacting with walk-in, telephone and email customers; assisting team leaders with quotes and sales orders and responding quickly and considerately to customer requests.</p>
<p><b>Qualifications and Abilities: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Knowledge of sign assembly, production, and installation.</li>
<li>Ability to create high-quality designs.</li>
<li>Must be professional with a well-groomed appearance.</li>
<li>Proficient in Adobe Illustrator or comparable software.</li>
<li>Well-spoken and effective written and oral communication skills.</li>
<li>Personable and outgoing; ability to assist with marketing of the company.</li>
<li>Attention to detail and deadline-oriented.</li>
<li>Excellent follow up skills</li>
</ul>
<p>Experience:</p>
<ul>
<li>Graphic Design: 5 years experience (Required)</li>
<li>Sign manufacturing knowledge (Required)</li>
</ul>
