<h2>Customer Success Account Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Cerkl is seeking a Customer Success Account Manager to manage and delight our enterprise customers while helping the Customer Success team develop sustained scalability.</p>
<p>As a part of our Customer Success Team, the Enterprise Account Manager would be responsible for a portfolio enterprise customers. S/he will guide each customer through the launch process and provide ongoing support to ensure account growth. The Customer Success Account Manager will be responsible and rewarded for renewing their portfolio of customers.</p>
<p><b>Specific responsibilities include but are not limited to: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Host quarterly insights &amp; strategy sessions with portfolio of clients</li>
<li>Manage &amp; enhance enterprise onboarding for portfolio of clients</li>
<li>Lead minor product changes working with product, design &amp; tech teams</li>
<li>Communicate platform updates/changes to portfolio of clients</li>
<li>Keep portfolio of clients up to speed on product enhancements &amp; improvements through training sessions, webinars &amp; in person meetings</li>
<li>Analyze portfolio performance - Tracking at risk &amp; high performing customers</li>
<li>Cultivate and tend to long-lasting customer relationships</li>
<li>Collect, submit &amp; advocate for product enhancements coming from portfolio of clients</li>
<li>Build empathy and understand the ‘why’ behind a customer's specific needs/wants/questions etc.</li>
<li>Provide sales, marketing, product, event and technical teams useful feedback exemplifying Cerkl’s Customer Centric model</li>
<li>Identify the must-have experience that keeps customers coming back</li>
<li>Solidify account renewals based on client portfolio</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Requirements: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Empathy</li>
<li>Passion for helping people</li>
<li>Ability to problem solve in a fast-paced environment</li>
<li>Self-starting and regulating</li>
<li>Strong written and verbal communication skills</li>
<li>Technical aptitude and general understanding of software</li>
<li>Skilled in time management</li>
<li>Interest in seeing others succeed</li>
<li>Results driven with strong analytical skills</li>
<li>Detail-oriented</li>
<li>Hard working</li>
<li>Collaborative</li>
<li>Creative &amp; progressive thinking</li>
<li>Interest in learning</li>
<li>Bored with the status quo</li>
</ul>

<p>Annual salary + commission based on performance.</p>
