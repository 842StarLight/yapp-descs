<h2>Operations Assistant</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>No Relocation Assistance Offered<br>
# 75124 - Bowling Green, Kentucky, United States</p>
<p>
At Hill’s we have a purpose. Every day around the world, we transform the lives of millions of pet families through pioneering innovation, amazing nutrition, and the best and brightest people. If you’re interested in <b>work that matters, fueled by passion for pets</b>, we invite you to apply to our Operations Assistant position at our Bowling Green, KY dry pet food manufacturing facility.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
This position assists the operations technicians in meeting or exceeding daily production goals by providing support in the following production areas and functions: material and equipment handling, changeovers, quality checks, cleaning and sanitation, and finished goods inspection. This entry level position serves as part of a talent pool from which Full Time Operations Technicians will be developed and selected.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Principal Duties and Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Attends team meetings and insures the effective transfer of key information from incoming and outgoing shifts</li>
<li>
Cleans and sanitizes equipment throughout the packaging and process area</li>
<li>
Handles stacks of all bag sizes (1-50 lbs bags) as needed</li>
<li>
Assist technicians with the preparation and execution of line changeovers, including primary and ancillary equipment</li>
<li>
Conducts product sampling and quality checks in an effective and timely fashion</li>
<li>
Assists with finished goods preparation and inspection; including top cap placement and inspection checklists</li>
<li>
Produces end of shift reports that include: number of pallets produced and amount of pounds produced through finished goods</li>
<li>
Responsible for managing Dump back/Regrind/Reblend product movement</li>
<li>
Monitors and support line operation during lunches and breaks or as needed</li>
<li>
Responsible for maintaining a clean and sanitary work area (requires sweeping, mopping, sanitizing, shoveling, and pushing)</li>
<li>
Identifies and communicates production issues to appropriate personnel as needed.</li>
<li>
Accountable for meeting or exceeding safety standards according to the published Hill’s Safety Policy at the Bowling Green plant</li>
<li>
Production line waste removal and disposal</li>
<li>
Participates on other cross-functional operation teams as assigned</li>
<li>
Other job functions that may be assigned*</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<ul><li>All of the essential job functions include, but are not limited to, the duties and requirements listed in the job description.</li></ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Basic Qualifications</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
High school diploma or the equivalent</li>
<li>
Ability to lift 50 pounds repeatedly for extended periods of time</li>
<li>
Ability to work with cleaning chemicals in non-temperature controlled environments</li>
<li>
Ability to climb stairs and ladders, bend to 90 degrees repeatedly, squat and lay prone to the floor for extended periods.</li>
<li>
Must be able to work any shift, weekends and mandatory overtime as needed.</li>
<li>
Proficient with computers, software, and data processing.</li>
<li>
Minimum 2 years experience in manufacturing environment.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Preferred Qualifications:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Demonstrated competency of Attention to Detail, Work Standards, Initiative and Managing Work</li>
<li>
Technical college/vocational school certificate or Associates degree in related field highly preferred</li>
<li>
Food safety experience a plus</li>
<li>
Previous manufacturing work team experience preferred.</li>
<li>
Demonstrated competency in problem solving/decision making, effective communication, results oriented, and job fit motivation.</li>
</ul>
