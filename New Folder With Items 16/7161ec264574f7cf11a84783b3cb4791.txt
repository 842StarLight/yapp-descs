<div>
National and Alamo has an exciting opportunity for a Rental Sales Agent. The Rental Sales Agent provides a superior, friendly, and efficient transaction (at time of rental and return) using the company approved sales and service techniques. Performs all responsibilities with a focus on performance and the wants and needs of our customers and in accordance with our Quality Standards.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
This position is part time, no more than 25 hours per week, and based at the Burlington Airport, Burlington, VT. Various shifts within the seven day operation 7am-midnight, will discuss schedule during interview.</div>
<br>
<b>
Responsibilities:</b><br>
<ul>
<li><div>Use company approved sales and service techniques when determining customer needs</div></li>
<li><div>
Offer optional products to meet customer wants and needs.</div></li>
<li><div>
Prepare all rental and return documents accurately and completely.</div></li>
<li><div>
Qualify each customer using our company rental requirement guidelines.</div></li>
<li><div>
Provide customers assistance with directions, maps, local area information, appropriate service information, etc.</div></li>
<li><div>
Review rental parameters with all customers to ensure a complete understanding of our rates and service charges.</div></li>
<li><div>
Ensure that the return date and time on the rental agreement is accurate.</div></li>
<li><div>
Review all charges at the time of vehicle return.</div></li>
<li><div>
Prepare the Rental Agreement Folder with all required information.</div></li>
<li><div>
Answer the phones to assist customers in a friendly, helpful and prompt manner.</div></li>
<li><div>
Assist customers by effectively resolving all customer service issues.</div></li>
<li><div>
Assist and coordinate customers within the queue to minimize any wait time and promote the most efficient service method as required.</div></li>
</ul>
