<h2>Senior Partner Account Executive</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>
<div>Yelp is seeking a tenacious, sales professional with a proven track record of success in a fast-paced, high growth company to join Yelp’s newest division, Agency &amp; Partner Sales.</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
 This entrepreneurial leader will build relationships with senior executives across the local advertising industry, launch and grow existing relationships, and execute cross-functional partnerships with direct visibility into Yelp's senior management.</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
The primary responsibility of the Senior Partner Account Executive is to acquire new business for Yelp by developing relationships with Agencies &amp; 3rd parties focused on the SMB market. These Agencies partner with Yelp to resell and buy Yelp’s local products.</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
The ideal candidate for this position will have the ability to quickly understand the business and demonstrate the value of Yelp’s product offering and partnership program. This position requires a highly motivated and energetic individual who possesses the ability to develop strategic relationships and generate new business. The ideal candidate takes a consultative approach to sales - understanding agency models and uncovering the needs and decision criteria of your prospects. Above all, you will be responsible for hitting your revenue targets.</div>
</div>
<div><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities:</b></h3>
<div><ul>
<li>
Establish a collaborative business relationship between Yelp and SMB (primarily local) agencies and their advertisers.</li>
<li>
Consistently deliver new partner-driven revenue every quarter, exceeding sales targets.
</li>
<li>Lead the partnership and deal-origination process, managing each step from strategy/planning, pipeline development, outreach, implementation, and ongoing growth.
</li>
<li>Create consultative sales presentations to demonstrate how Yelp’s advertising products can be leveraged to connect with consumers.</li>
<li>
Represent Yelp as a spokesperson and evangelist at industry events.</li>
<li>
Contribute to evolving vision of Yelp's advertising partner program.</li>
<li>
Develop and manage partner relationships to ensure over-achievement of assigned goals.</li>
<li>
Identify new agency partnerships which will meet Yelp partner compliance as needed to meet growth expectations.</li>
<li>
Map out agency structure and identify key decision makers, budget owners and the agencies’ decision making process.</li>
<li>
Engage with agencies on an ongoing basis to foster relationships, validate advertisers needs and keep Yelp’s partnership top of mind.</li>
<li>
Work collaboratively with cross-functional teams to drive revenue growth with new customers.</li>
<li>
Come to work each day with a positive attitude and ready to win.</li>
</ul></div>
</div></div>
<div><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Requirements:</b></h3>
<div><ul>
<li>
Minimum 5-8 years of experience in agency &amp; partner sales, BD or digital sales role</li>
<li>
Experience is preferred with advertising products, APIs, and channel programs across the industry (e.g. Google, Bing, Facebook)</li>
<li>
Demonstrated success in hitting sales goals in a previous position</li>
<li>
Strong network of tech industry contacts, ranging from startups to established brands.</li>
<li>
Influential communication skills in all forms: written, presentations, negotiations, and listening.</li>
<li>
Passion for Yelp’s product, as well as other consumer internet/mobile products.</li>
<li>
Flexible, startup mindset, and ability to operate within a very dynamic, fast-paced work environment</li>
<li>
Is self-motivated, a team player and has a strong drive to exceed targets</li>
<li>
Travel as needed</li>
</ul></div>
</div></div>
<div><div>

Guaranteed base salary plus uncapped commission</li>
<li>

<li>
15 days PTO and 11 paid holidays (per year)</li>

Generous paid parental leave policy</li>

Pre-tax commuter benefits</li>

</ul></div>
