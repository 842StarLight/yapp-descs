<p>Would you like to be part of a new team focused on managing and growing business in strategic and Fortune 1000 customers? Do you have account management experience working with large and complex customers, as well as the sales acumen and technical background to drive adoption of cloud services?<br>
Amazon Web Services provides companies of all sizes with an infrastructure platform in the cloud and we are growing our Sales team to help more companies make the move to AWS. If you are passionate about the cloud and have the skills, the Account Manager - Enterprise Accounts role provides the opportunity sell the industry’s most relevant solutions with the industry’s leading company.<br>
The Account Manager - Enterprise Accounts is responsible for aggressively growing the AWS customer base within Enterprise or Named customer segments. The ideal candidate will possess both a sales and technical background that enables them to successfully maintain relationships, advise customers on AWS cloud solutions and grow AWS usage across a defined set of customers. A successful candidate will maintain active account management engagements with customers and prospects as well as consistently meet and exceed sales quotas. A single AM might own either; one or more territories in conjunction with one or more field reps, or the growth responsibilities in largest customer’s divisions or subsidiaries within the Enterprise or Named segments.</p>
