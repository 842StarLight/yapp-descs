<h2>Producer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Summary</b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>An experienced creative producer to develop and deliver all aspects of experiential engagements, large and small, including digital media, AV, talent and other custom creative concepts. The producer works with the production team —including strategists/writers, exhibit designers, graphic designers, interactive designers, developers, special effects producers and sales teams—from creative concept to final delivery. If you have a pharma background, even better.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Essential Duties and Responsibilities</b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<ul><li> <i>Broad Production Experience</i>: Do you have a strong knowledge and experience in digital media, video production, A/V, staging and theatrical production? Are you able to figure out how to make things happen to execute ideas that haven’t been tried before?</li></ul>
<br>
<p></p>
<ul><li> <i>Strong Teamwork</i>: Can you lead a project and manage a diverse team from the creative development process — through to the final delivery onsite?</li></ul>
<br>
<p></p>
<ul><li> <i>Excellent Budgeting Skills</i>: Can you create a budget—and then work within its constraints of time and dollars?</li></ul>
<br>
<p></p>
<ul><li> <i>Client Savvy:</i> Do you keep the client’s goals in mind through the creative and development process?</li></ul>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Qualifications and Skills:</b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>7+ years of experience producing content and live activations in tradeshows and/or events</li>
<li>Creating and managing budgets for multiple types of deliverables (digital, AV, lighting, and crazy creative solutions that are yet to be thought up. Knowledge of accounting/financial processes. Excellent Excel skills.</li>
<li>Strong experience managing teams of various types of players with diplomacy, concern for their varied points of view, and the ability to understand how they all fit together: creatives, engineers, programmers, AV technicians, designers, clients, account teams</li>
<li>Excellent management of external partners</li>
<li>Expertise in onsite installation and delivery of activations in a tradeshow and/or event environment.</li>
<li>Organization and detail-oriented. Able to maintain all project documentation, from proposal to timelines to final reconciliation.</li>
<li>Strong Knowledge of Microsoft Office products: Word, Excel, Outlook, Powerpoint. Other creative tools are a plus.</li>
<li>Poise under pressure, and a good sense of humor are appreciated.</li>
<li>Able to travel in the US frequently, as required, and occasionally internationally. Travel is usually over weekends.</li>
<li>Bachelor's Degree preferred</li>
<li>Pharmaceutical experience is a plus, but not required.</li>
<br>
</ul>

<p><b>Other Skills and Abilities</b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>Must be passionate about working with people and be customer-centric. Must show good attention to detail. Team Work, Customer Service Focused, Positive Attitude Provide strong project and budget management, ensuring adherence to timelines and delivery against key milestones. Effective communication skills are required: including the ability to make effective presentations; prepare written documentation including proposals, quotations and letters; maintain accurate and complete client and project files. Exhibit the ability to multi-task, sell, and operate under severe time constraints and immovable deadlines. Good organization, detail and follow-up skills are required. Experience in the event marketing or a creative solutions based agency. Thrives in an entrepreneurial and independent environment. Embodies flexibility, open-mindedness and receptivity. Actively works out solutions. Identifies something valuable in being part of a team and ability to effectively collaborate. Must be able to develop excellent rapport with existing and prospective clients. Must have a good “business sense”. Proficient using Microsoft office programs.</p>
