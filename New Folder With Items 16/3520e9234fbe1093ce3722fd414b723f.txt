<h2>SBA Relationship Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Summary:</b> Originate loans and offer products and services to business and professional clientele. Establishes, develops and retains customer relationships through referrals and business development. Responsible for development and maintenance of a loan portfolio and providing high quality, value-added client service.</p>
<p><b>Essential Duties &amp; Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Perform assigned duties in accordance with the Bank's Mission, Vision and Core Values; and provide the Bank's customers with timely, responsive and exceptional service in accordance with the Bank's customer service standards</li>
<li>Take time to understand applicant's business/management</li>
<li> Provide applicant bank products and services that enhance business success</li>
<li>Accept credit applications; interview loan applicants</li>
<li>Review applications and credit histories</li>
<li>Review and analyze credit reports and financial information</li>
<li>Evaluate existing and projected company cash flow</li>
<li>Determine value of collateral; through review of appraisal, valuations, business financials, etc.</li>
<li>Respond to inquiries regarding business and consumer lending and other Bank products</li>
<li>Recommend approval or rejection of credit applications within bank and SBA Criteria</li>
<li>Maintain existing customer relationships; develop and expand new customer relationships</li>
<li>Prepare detailed, complete, loan presentations (Credit Memos) for new credit applications, WAMS and renewal requests</li>
<li>Monitor loans through periodic reviews and documentation in conjunction with SBA Portfolio Manager and/or as requested</li>
<li>Perform credit checks and site inspections as requested</li>
<li>Review maturing loans, loan extensions and construction draws</li>
<li>Collect past due accounts and/or support SBA Operations Officer in the collection of accounts</li>
<li>Oversee loan documentation and processing including review and negotiation</li>
<li>Properly maintain credit files including file memos updating the status of the client relationship</li>
<li>Keep up to date on government guaranteed lending requirements</li>
<li>Maintain knowledge of and compliance with banking regulations</li>
<li>Be knowledgeable of and promote all bank products and services through the referral to bank partners</li>
<li>Provide timely and responsive customer service</li>
<li>Understanding of all internal Bank policies</li>
<li>Perform other duties as assigned</li>
<li>Ability to utilize technology to communicate effectively</li>
<li>Understanding of the SBA Standard Operating Procedures (SOP's), including origination, servicing and liquidation</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Required Qualifications</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor's degree in Finance, business or other relevant area; or equivalent work experience.</li>
<li>5 years' experience in government guaranteed lending</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Preferred Qualifications</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Proven business development skills</li>
<li>Thorough knowledge of credit services and other banking services</li>
<li>Thorough knowledge of lending policies and procedures</li>
<li>Thorough knowledge of business cash flow</li>
<li>Proficient computer skills with collaboration and productivity tools including Windows-based operating systems and office suites (Microsoft Word, Outlook, Excel, PowerPoint); spreadsheet applications to organize data used for advanced data analysis; internet; email; and bank software.</li>
<li>Effective oral and written communication skills</li>
</ul>
