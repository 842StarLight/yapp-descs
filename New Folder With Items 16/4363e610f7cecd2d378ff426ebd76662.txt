PRIMARY PURPOSE OF POSITION<br>
<br>
The Office of the CIO (OCIO) is charged with leading the IT transformation and building an organization that ensures the standardization of processes and tools, that enable the effective and efficient delivery of IT solutions to our business partners. The Service Portfolio Management and Projects Office (SPMPO) within the OCIO plays an instrumental role with driving IT wide project, portfolio, resource, financial and organizational change management standards, processes, tools and governance that consistently deliver the desired business outcomes.<br>
<br>
Within SPMPO, the IT Organizational Change and Communication Management (OCCM) Center of Excellence team drives standardization of change and communication management processes and tools, resulting in the effective adoption, utilization, and realized value of IT services.<br>
<br>
The Change Management Practitioner is responsible for developing and executing change activities, communications, and training tasks for large-scale, transformational IT programs as well as smaller, focused IT projects.<br>
<br>
This individual should be a critical thinker with a proven history of task execution and management who is also able to:<br>
<br>
Work with other project team members and business partners to effectively complete OCCM deliverables such as stakeholder analysis, change impact analysis, establishing and mobilizing change networks, drafting and organizing communications such as road shows, lunch and learns, newsletter articles, talking points, and measuring and monitoring end user adoption and readiness, etc.<br>
<br>
Provide support to the IT Organizational Change and Communications Management (OCCM) Center of Excellence (CoE) and execute change management programs and activities applying the Exelon IT OCCM Methodology (Prosci®), processes and tools.<br>
