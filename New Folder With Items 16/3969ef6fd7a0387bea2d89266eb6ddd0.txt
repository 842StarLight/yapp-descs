<h2>ServiceNow Business Analyst (ITBM)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>Integrated Solutions Management (ISM) is seeking a ServiceNow ITBM Business Analyst to join our team for an opportunity located in Baltimore MD. (Note: This is a W2 position. U.S. Citizenship or appropriate work authorization required.)</p>
<p>
ISM is an IT performance company for global 2000 companies &amp; government entities providing organizational change and transformation through our ITSM and Project Portfolio delivery framework. We have built a reputation of trust, excellence, and experience, by creating world class IT strategies for highly complex and secure institutions.</p>
<p>
The ITBM Business Process Consultant will have diverse responsibilities focused on technology, business process analysis, market analysis, sales lead generation, RFP responses, and will support critical business and operational initiatives. Analyst will lead, coordinate, communicate, integrate, and be accountable for capturing clear and concise requirements that support the needs of the business and for facilitating successful project outcomes. Lead analysis efforts and provide consultation on best practices. Collaborate with Customers, Process SMEs, and Technical Consultants to deliver value to Customers and ISM.</p>
<p>
Location: Baltimore, MD</p>
<p>
Responsibilities and Duties:
</p>
<ul>
<li>Utilize ServiceNow ITBM to implement and operationalize Project Portfolio Management and reporting functions currently captured in our ServiceNow backlog.</li>
<li>Create draft governance model for standardized process for the selection and prioritization of work.</li>
<li>Define ServiceNow requirements for project intake, tracking and reporting of project and portfolio levels.</li>
<li>Create cost and license management interim model</li>
<li>Create draft standard taxonomy to facilitate consistent reporting and project transparency</li>
<li>Finalize proposed Enhance Request Intake Process for SNOW.</li>
<li>Define data access rules and approach across applications and constituencies</li>
<li>Provide ongoing ServiceNow as a service support to other entities utilizing the ServiceNow platform.</li>
<li>Develop reports and dashboards used to manage work</li>
<li>Assist all other divisions as necessary to capture and upload data to support ServiceNow project monitoring and reporting.
</li>
<li>Upon data upload, develop ServiceNow workflows as needed for future operations and maintenance to operationalize the data and keep current.</li>
</ul>
<p>Required Skills:</p>
<ul>
<li>Minimum of 3+ years demonstrated success as a Business Analyst and have experience with Requirements elicitation
</li>
<li>A subject matter expert in ServiceNow, have expert knowledge of the ITBM module, and have experience with the latest ServiceNow platform release (Madrid)</li>
<li>B.A. in Computer Science (or equivalent work experience)</li>
<li>Advanced software skills including MS Office, Excel, Visio and PowerPoint.</li>
<li>Be performance driven with strong analytical and organizational skills</li>
<li>Leading and facilitating meetings</li>
<li>Running workshops and giving presentations</li>
<li>Verbal and written communication skills</li>
<li>Problem Solving and Conflict Resolution</li>
<li>Work independently and drive projects</li>
<li>Experience working with the Agile framework a plus</li>
<li>Advanced analysis and requirements gathering skills</li>
</ul>
