<h2>Head of Global Business Communications</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div><div><div>
<div>
<div>This role is responsible for the successful development and implementation of traditional and digital marketing strategies and concepts at Munich Re global entities including PIRI and Life. The role is focused on driving business results by uniquely positioning the Munich Re and/or subsidiary brand(s) with clients and other key stakeholders, and driving awareness, reputation and business interest, thereby making a meaningful contribution to profitable growth. He/she drives positive change with business unit leaders and shapes marketing and sales strategies along the needs of the business. This person is responsible together with his/her leadership team to ensure that Marketing Business Partners are seen as valued members of the business units. He/she oversees the global marketing team results and supports the ongoing growth and development of team members. The person in this position collaborates with the global Com management team and cross-functional teams in Global Communications and across the global enterprise to support a unified and strategic view and appropriate marketing and sales activities company wide.<br>
<br>
As Head of Global Business Communications, you’ll:<br>
<ul>
<li>
Responsibility for building and maintaining highly collaborative partnerships with business and Global Com leaders, peers and direct reports to ensure effective and efficient delivery of global business first approach</li>
<li>
Responsibility for the global marketing team results, including planning, budgets and personnel management as well as development</li>
<li>
Responsibility for planning and execution of all marketing strategies and activities based on business and client needs</li>
<li>
Responsible that traditional and digital marketing strategies are successfully developed and implemented with high impact and with an unified strategic view as appropriate</li>
<li>
Responsible for the team’s focus on the use of data and digital in every aspect of their work to drive marketing effectiveness and efficiency.</li>
<li>
Responsible for close connectivity of all global marketing team members to ensure insights, best practices, needs, and opportunities are known to optimize Global programs, assets, toolkits, and processes together with his/her marketing leadership team</li>
<li>
Connect with business area leaders to understand their business needs and to ensure appropriate measures and marketing programs, promotions and activities and to help guide future marketing decisions, spending and allocation.</li>
<li>
Lead, coach, and mentor the marketing leadership team – including goal setting, performance reviews, on-going feedback, and individual and group educational opportunities.</li>
<li>
Responsible for a good understanding of company stakeholders and company goals and objectives to align marketing strategy</li>
</ul>
</div>
</div>
<div>
<p><b>Qualifications</b></p>
<div>Successful candidates will possess the following skills/capabilities:<br>
<ul>
<li>
Master’s degree in Marketing or Sales required or MBA</li>
<li>
10+ years of relevant experience in heading marketing teams and developing and executing marketing strategies, campaigns and product launch strategies with the end goal of acquiring new customers, increasing share of wallet, and raising brand awareness to achieve profitable growth</li>
<li>
Outstanding strategic and analytic thinking</li>
<li>
Intercultural leadership experience preferred in the financial industry sector</li>
<li>
Business driven, solution-oriented, resourceful with strong ability to problem-solve</li>
<li>
Creative, embraces change, idea leadership, desire to pioneer (comfortable with ambiguity and “finding a way”)</li>
<li>
Highly collaborative</li>
<li>
Strong negotiation and influence management skills, along with oral and written communication</li>
<li>
Experience managing a diverse and dynamic team, including freelancers and external agencies</li>
<li>
Knowledge of marketing trends and new developments</li>
<li>
Native language English preferred at least fluently</li>
<li>
Well-connected amongst a global marketing network</li>
</ul>
