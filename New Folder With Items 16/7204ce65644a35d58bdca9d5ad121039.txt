<h2>Warehouse Manager/Parts Procurement</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Job Description</b></p>
<p><b>Position: </b> Warehouse Manager</p>
<p>The position requires the Warehouse Manager to report to the Operations Manager.</p>
<p>Managers Responsibilities:</p>
<p>Will be responsible for the day to day operations of the warehouse, Electrical parts logistics, purchasing, parts co-ordination, inventory, inventory control, shipping and receiving logistics. Will be responsible for policy and procedure development, implementation and review. Suggestive procedures is expected for constant improvement of all aspects of the position as well as ways to make tasks more efficient for others.</p>
<p>Will require customer interaction in person as well as via phone.</p>
<p>Computer work will be required using Microsoft Office, Microsoft Project and Sage 50 accounting system.</p>
<p>The ability to lift up to 100lbs is required.</p>
<p>Forklift license is required.</p>
<p>Will be responsible for at least 2 or more other employees/team members.</p>
<p>2500 sq Ft warehouse</p>
<p>Responsible for maintaining inventory counts and restock</p>
<p>Responsible to work on their own initiative with set goals and tasks to complete.</p>
<p>Responsible for the on time delivery and follow up of ordered parts. Will mostly be to commercial, industrial, shipyard facilities and Yachts as well as marinas and private estates.</p>
<p>Must have the ability to organize their department with efficient use of time and prioritize tasks.</p>
<p>Electrical systems and equipment knowledge is required.</p>
<p>Ability to clearly communicate with Technical Managers and Technicians.</p>
<p>Clearly communicate with assistant setting daily tasks</p>
<p><b>Tasks and Responsibilities</b></p>
<p><b>Counter Sales </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Assist customers with product questions, technical assistance and material research.</li>
<li>Answer incoming phone calls.</li>
<li>Generate invoices for products sold over the counter.</li>
<li>Create “Sales Quotations” for customers</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Shipping &amp; Logistics </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Wrap, box, pack all outgoing shipments</li>
<li>Generate commercial invoicing for all international shipments</li>
<li>Generate packing slips for domestic shipments</li>
<li>Contact various logistics shipping companies to schedule pickups, shipping questions, drop offs.</li>
<li>Pick up all packaging materials, keep stock available (tape, boxes, popcorn, etc.)</li>
<li>Receive incoming shipments and notify supervisor/ employee upon arrival</li>
<li>Ensure SOF’s are filled out and correct.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Warehouse Inventory </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Order parts for stock and/or materials from jobs</li>
<li>Walk isles, organize locations for all parts</li>
<li>Inventory counts</li>
<li>Clear warehouse of trash, crates and pallets</li>
<li>Book out parts when technician picks up for job</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Service Help</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Assign purchase order numbers and book out stock</li>
<li>Research, locate and order parts from vendors</li>
<li>Find parts needed that are in-stock</li>
<li>Pick up parts from local vendors</li>
<li>Drop off parts to local vessels</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Administrative Help</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Assist with questions about customer invoicing, shipping and vendor billing</li>
<li>Help create Sage 50 inventory item numbers</li>
<li>Contact customers with overdue invoices</li>
<li>Make sure all packing slips, invoices, and any other pertinent information gets to administration.</li>
<li>Process credit card transactions over the phone.</li>
</ul>
<p>6 Enter purchase order on quotation and book out slips</p>
