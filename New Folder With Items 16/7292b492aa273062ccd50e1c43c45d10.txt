<p><b>
Function Overview</b></p>
<p>
Whirlpool is seeking qualified candidate for the role of a <b>Senior Process Engineer, </b>to support our plastics extrusion and thermoforming operations for our Amana, Iowa operations plant. In this role you will be responsible for ensuring the uninterrupted operation of assigned extrusion and thermoforming equipment/tooling while achieving the highest level of quality while driving waste elimination through the use of advanced World Class Manufacturing (WCM) tools and methodologies.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Job Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Develop and maintain equipment documentation including, but not limited to: Tool and operation sheets, machine drawings, tool drawings, PFMEA's (process failure mode effects analysis), control plans, OPL's (one point lessons), software back-up programs, etc.</li>
<li>
Lead continuous improvement projects partnering with equipment operators, skilled trades and quality teams to improve quality, reduce mean time to repair (MTTR), improve mean time between failures (MTBF) and reduce cost to run and maintain the equipment.</li>
<li>
Participate in activities supporting the introduction of new products such as Make vs. Buy decisions, Equipment OEM selection, equipment design/startup, First Part Approval, trial runs, etc.</li>
<li>
Provide hands on troubleshooting support for the equipment operators, maintenance and tooling technicians to minimize production interruptions resulting from downtime.</li>
<li>
Track and monitor equipment/process performance metrics to identify improvement opportunities</li>
<li>
Experienced with the maintenance, repair specification and qualification of plastics equipment and tooling.</li>
<li>
Proven success leading equipment/process improvement projects from inception to completion</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
