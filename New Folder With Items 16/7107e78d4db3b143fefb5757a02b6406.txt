<h2>Sr. Account Executive</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Our customers and prospects are solving difficult problems that create massive amounts of unstructured data that has become unmanageable. Igneous software simplifies and automates data management at scale, allowing for seamless data discovery, protection, and movement. You’ll get to work with cutting-edge customers and use cases, from pharmaceutical research to streaming media, high-tech manufacturing to machine learning, and even researchers curing cancer.</p>
<p>
Join our team as a Sr. Account Executive, and put your energy, work ethic, and entrepreneurial mindset to great use developing your territory with your channel and alliance partners. You’ll get to work directly with the largest and highest-profile customers in your territory while helping them solve some of their hardest data management problems. Close enterprise SaaS contracts quickly and focus on customer success to drive expansion business rapidly.</p>
<p>
As a Sr. Account Executive you will:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Execute a territory sales strategy highly focused on key accounts in our focus verticals</li>
<li>
Collaborate with Igneous Channel team and partners, Alliances team, inside sales, and marketing to accelerate territory development.</li>
<li>
Attain sales revenue and growth by landing new business quickly and expanding that footprint through successful adoption</li>
<li>
Provide enough activity to consistently hit sales goals</li>
<li>
Understand and identify client strategic objectives/needs</li>
<li>
Track Sales activity and forecast through the use of sales tools</li>
</ul>
<p>
Our Ideal Sr. Account Executive will have:</p>
<ul>
<li>
6+ years of consistent success selling solutions to enterprise customers in a direct sales role</li>
<li>
Experience managing and closing complex sales cycles with many stakeholders in IT, lines of business, and the C-suite</li>
<li>
Experience in a high-volume SaaS environment focused on driving customers to a free trial of the software</li>
<li>
A track record of delivering new customers</li>
<li>
A proven ability to shift priorities and communicate effectively with customers/partners</li>
<li>
Experience growing and fostering relationships long-term</li>
<li>
BA/BS Degree</li>
</ul>
<p>
Perks and Benefits:</p>
<ul>
<li>
100% premium benefit coverage for employees</li>
<li>
Flexible work and time off schedules</li>
</ul>
