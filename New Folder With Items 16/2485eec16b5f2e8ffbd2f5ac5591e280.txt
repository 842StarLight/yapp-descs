<p><b>Job Description</b></p>
<p>
We have an opportunity for a senior sales professional whose main responsibilities will be managing the Luxury relationships at the Corporate Director, VP-level, and property level relationships with our hotel chain and management company partners. The role entails identifying business development opportunities with these partners. You will carry a new booked business and renewal quota. This will be required demonstrating solution-selling and total account management skills, while effectively working with the other Cvent Account Owners representing on these accounts. The goal, to grow overall partnerships and increase our Luxury products market share.</p>
