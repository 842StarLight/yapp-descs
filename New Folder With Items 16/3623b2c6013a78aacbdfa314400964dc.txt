<h2>Associate Residential Sales Territory Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div></div>
<div>
<div><div>Koch Air, LLC has been focused on the distribution of quality Carrier residential and commercial products since 1936, and is among the oldest and largest Carrier distributors. Today, Koch Air, has nine branch locations, 35 delivery vehicles, and more than 300,000 square feet of equipment and parts warehousing throughout the Midwest.
</div></div>
<div>
<b>Purpose:</b> Assist in the growth in profitable sales of all Koch Air products (equipment, parts, supplies, and services) within an assigned territory assigned to Lead Territory Manager.
<div>
<b>RESPONSIBILITIES:</b>
</div>
<ul>
<li><div>Perform all duties in accordance with Koch Air and Koch Enterprises Code of Conduct, Values, Ethics, Environmental, Safety, and Human Resources policies
</div></li>
<li><div>Assist in the sales functions for accounts assigned to Lead Territory Manager to achieve sales, margins, share of wallet, and share of market targets by including growing existing accounts and recruiting new accounts.
</div></li>
<li><div>Perform all duties in accordance with Koch Air and Koch Enterprises Code of Conduct, Values, Ethics, Environmental, Safety, and Human Resource Policies.
</div></li>
<li><div>Be an active member of the sales team in its entirety, which includes implementing Koch Air Strategic Plan, participation in sales team meetings, participation in the overall sales process, assisting with commercial sales, implementing sales strategies and participation in trade shows as assigned.
</div></li>
<li><div>Fully understand and promote the Koch Air Value Proposition and core products (equipment, parts, supplies, and services), dealer programs, advertising, promotions, warranty procedures, business processes, E-Commerce, and other programs and policies. Actively look for and recommend improvements.
</div></li>
<li><div>Learn Customer Development Plans to focus efforts and manage time for superior territory performance.
</div></li>
<li><div>Assist in training meetings for customers on Koch Air products, and business processes and customer lead generation, business management and customer service. Make recommendations for Koch Air training programs.
</div></li>
<li><div>Survey customers, quotes, and orders to detect business trends and opportunities for new products, services, and accounts. Make specific recommendations on inventory management to help purchasing optimize turns and availability.
</div></li>
<li><div>Assist in developing and maintaining a good relationship between customers and the Customer Finance department.
</div></li>
<li><div>Assist Inside Sales in writing and pricing customer orders for equipment and related materials if required.
</div></li>
<li><div>Maintain Koch Air files as required by Carrier DOM and Koch Air.
</div></li>
<li><div>Assist in sales forecasts, budgets, expense reports, competitive pricing studies and requests for competitive pricing and other reports as required.
</div></li>
<li>
<div>Perform other duties as assigned to achieve the Koch Air Strategic Plan.<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<div>
<b>MINIMUM QUALIFICATIONS:</b>
</div>
</li>
</ul>
<ul><li>
<div>BA degree in business or equivalent sales experience, preferably in HVAC.<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<div>
<b>KEY SKILLS AND COMPETENCIES:</b>
</div>
</li></ul>
<ul>
<li><div>Must be responsive to customers and exhibit a service and sales attitude.
</div></li>
<li><div>Ability to understand customer requirements and manage the customer relationship.
</div></li>
<li><div>Excellent organizational and communication skills both written and verbal required.
</div></li>
<li><div>Ability to use PC, Microsoft Office suite proficiently.
</div></li>
</ul>
