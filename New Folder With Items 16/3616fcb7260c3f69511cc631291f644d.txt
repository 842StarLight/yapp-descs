<p><b>
Job Description:
</b></p>
<p>Harris Communications Systems is seeking a highly motivated, team-oriented technical systems engineer to join their Advanced Customer Engineering Services team.</p>
<p>
The ideal candidate will have an understanding of tactical communications, and a working knowledge of IP networking, network architectures and system design, practical application of principles and techniques of IP networking, integration of tactical wireless radios and wired IP networks and integration of 3rd party applications and solutions. The candidate will be responsible for the development and implementation of systems, operational concepts and network architectures for integrated tactical communication systems. Business requirements include being an internal networking consultant to Engineering, Product Management, Sales teams and our customers in the field.</p>
<p>
In this role, the successful candidate will be required to work in a fast-paced, dynamic environment executing tasks over a variety of programs and proposal related activities to meet business needs. Tasking will include/require:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Act as the network engineer on teams which are responsible for system/network designs, proposal development, system development, integration and delivery for custom solutions</li>
<li>
Works with technical leads on business and customer objectives and requirements</li>
<li>
Ability, technical expertise and skillset to design and implement a tactical network from high level requirements and integrate third party solutions to meet customer requirements</li>
<li>
Perform product analysis, test design, development, execution and development of test tools to aid in test execution</li>
<li>
Ability to troubleshoot networking issues at all layers, including proficient knowledge/use of the latest test/debug tools used to analyze network connectivity and traffic flow</li>
<li>
Excellent debugging and problem-solving skills with network systems, an understanding and proven track record of resolving hardware/software integration issues</li>
</ul>
<p>
If you are looking to take the next step in expanding your horizons as an engineer, then the systems engineering position is for you.</p>
<p><b>
Qualifications:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
BS in Electrical or Computer Engineering, Network Engineering, Computer Science or related field or equivalent work experience with minimum of 2 years of prior technical experience</li>
<li>
Working knowledge of networking concepts with a minimum of 2 years of relevant experience particularly with network integrated solutions</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Preferred Additional Skills:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
A successful candidate will possess excellent technical, interpersonal and customer skills, plus demonstrate the capacity to effectively foster team building within and beyond his/her work group</li>
<li>
Strong communication skills coupled with the ability work to within a large engineering team</li>
<li>
Experience with tactical and RF communication products is highly desirable</li>
<li>
Cisco CCNA Routing and Switching Certification is a plus</li>
<li>
Familiarity with wireless networking and tactical wideband networking is highly desirable</li>
<li>
Experience with Face-to-Face customer interactions is a plus</li>
<li>
Enjoys working with people, developing relationships, and sharing knowledge</li>
<li>
Must be able to work independently as well as in a dynamic team environment</li>
<li>
Layer 3 networking design and implementation experience with integrated solutions</li>
<li>
Experience in working with and integrating third party vendors to integrate their solutions</li>
<li>
US Government Secret Security Clearance or Ability to Obtain Clearance</li>
</ul>
