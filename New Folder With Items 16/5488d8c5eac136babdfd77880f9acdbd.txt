<p>ANM is looking for a Consulting Engagement Manager to join our amazing Managed Services team in Albuquerque or Denver!</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>Who We Are:</p>
<p>ANM is a fast growing IT consulting firm focused on building long-term, customer focused relationships. We offer engineering excellence and quality customer service with a local focus. Our specialties include designing, developing and deploying solutions for enterprise networking, data center &amp; cloud, collaboration, security, audio visual and Salesforce.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p>Our Benefits:</p>
<ul>

<p></p>
<p>Who We Are Looking For:</p>
<p>At ANM a Consulting Engagement Manager in Managed Services is responsible for working proactively with the ANM clients to provide technical guidance and consultation services. This position requires a blend of technical competence, consultative approach, and client focused services to enhance the solutions and success of our clients.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>What You Will Do As a Consulting Engagement Manager?</p>
<ul>
<li>Will act as a technical lead and escalation point for ANM clients, and Managed Services Engineers.</li>
<li>Will work with clients on assigned issues and work closely with the ANM technical teams to resolve problems.</li>
<li>Strong Customer Service skills are required.</li>
<li>Clearly and consistently communicates with customer’s end users and technical teams.</li>
<li>Generates &amp; implements clear and consistent documentation of customer environments.</li>
<li>Performs analysis and diagnosis of system problems.</li>
<li>Run Technical review sessions with ANM MS clients to provide proactive support and feature enhancement recommendations.</li>
<li>Review client environments to provide best-practice guidance recommendations.</li>
<li>Work with Professional Services and Pre-Sales engineering Teams to develop Quarter Business Review for Clients Collaboration Infrastructure.</li>
<li>Monitors and maintains customer collaboration and networking systems to provide a reliable, consistent and enjoyable user experience.</li>
<li>Provides second/third level technical support for numerous technologies to ANM customers.</li>
<li>Interacts directly with customers via phone, ticketing and in person.</li>
<li>Applies analytical skills and technical knowledge to solve technology problems of moderate to high complexity.</li>
<li>Effectively utilizes lab setups to recreate and solve problems</li>
<li>Uses knowledge of technology, products and services to troubleshoot and maintain networking solutions of all sizes</li>
<li>Invest time in continued education to learn new technologies.</li>
<li>Work with Tools development team to continuously improve and enhance the offering ANM provides its clients.</li>
<li>Assist in developing process and procedure documentation to help ensure a consistent experience is provide to all ANM clients.</li>
<li>Provide mentorship to the Junior members of the team.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p>REQUIRED EDUCATION, TECHNICAL SKILLS AND/OR EXPERIENCE:</p>
<ul>
<li>Five (5) years of Cisco Collaboration support experience. OR CCIE Collaboration.</li>
<li>Two (2) years of Cisco Collaboration deployment experience.</li>
<li>Strong Leadership skills</li>
<li>Good verbal communication skills, strong work ethic, and a positive attitude.</li>
<li>Strong desire to learn and grow in a dynamic, fast paced and collaborative work environment.</li>
<li>Basic operation system, virtualization and networking knowledge.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p>PREFERRED EDUCATION, TECHNICAL SKILLS AND/OR EXPERIENCE:</p>
<ul>
<li>CCIE is highly desired, or a combination of certifications, education and work experience.</li>
<li>Experience with Cisco Collaboration Solutions, 3rd party integrations into Cisco Collaboration systems, Cisco Route/Switch, end user applications (CAD/CSD/Finesse/CUIC, Jabber, Webex Teams, BroadSoft, etc.)</li>
<li>Experience with Multi-cluster environment support</li>
<li>Experience with Video platforms (CMS, TMS, TMSXE, Expressway, Hybrid Services, Webex, and VCS)</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p>TRAVEL:</p>
<p>Up to 25% required for this position. Must have reliable transportation, valid driver’s license and motor vehicle insurance.</p>
