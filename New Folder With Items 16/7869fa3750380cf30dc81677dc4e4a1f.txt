<h2>Professional Services Advisor</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Overview
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div>FUJIFILM North America Corporation is a marketing subsidiary of FUJIFILM Corporation in Tokyo, Japan that provides equipment, consumables and technical support to the graphic arts industry; digital photo processing equipment, technical support and fulfillment services for commercial and consumer customers; consumer digital cameras, and motion picture films. The company is comprised of five operating divisions: Photo Imaging Products; Graphic Systems Products; Electronic Imaging Products; Motion Picture Film Products; and Optical Devices.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
The Professional Services Advisor, HSIJ (high-speed inkjet), will report to the Group Leader, Professional Services and be responsible for application and production training of HSIJ (high-speed Inkjet) products. Reporting to the Professional Services Advisor, HSIJ are no direct reports. The Professional Services Advisor, HSIJ will reinforce Fujifilm’s leadership values by transforming Professional Services Advisor responsibilities into a customer focused, proactive operation that will help drive Fujifilm’s business.</div>
</div></div></div>
