<h2>Director of Marketing- Shore Club- Miami Beach</h2>

<p><b>
Job Purpose:</b></p>
<p>
The Director Marketing will be responsible for assisting in the creation of strategies and the direct oversight of the execution of plans and tactics used to focus on increasing direct revenue for all venues and broader brand recognition.</p>
<p><b>
Duties &amp; Functions:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Providing functional leadership as well as strategic support for subordinates, executive leadership, internal customers and vendors, including agencies.</li>
<li>Leading a team of professionals with functional expertise including but not limited to Public Relations, Multi-Media and Graphic Design.</li>
<li>Frequent communication with the operations team including F&amp;B, retail, HR, IT, other marketing areas and vendors.</li>
<li>Driving revenue for the property in conjunction with other marketing areas.</li>
<li>Budget management.</li>
<li>On property communication (static, digital and video communications.)</li>
<li>External advertising (Print, Broadcast, Online, etc.)</li>
<li>Tracking and resolving problems/issues escalated by end users.</li>
<li>Devising or modifying procedures to solve process problems that will result in more efficient productivity and higher caliber product.</li>
<li>Gathering business objectives from operations teams and proposing marketing solutions to help achieve them.</li>
<li>Ensure that all guests feel welcome and are given responsive, friendly and courteous service at all times</li>
</ul>
<p><b>ADDITIONAL RESPONSIBILITIES
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Communicate effectively, both verbally and in writing, to provide clear direction to the venue teams.</li>
<li>Communicate with employees using a positive and clear speaking voice, listen to and understand requests, respond with appropriate actions and provide accurate information.</li>
<li>Remain calm and alert, especially during emergency situations and/or heavy restaurant activity, serving as a role model for team and other employees. Interact with other department personnel and venue staff as needed.</li>
<li>Make decisions and take actions based on previous experience and good judgment, sometimes revising procedures to accommodate unusual situations.</li>
<br>
</ul>

</b></p>
<p><b>To be aware of and ensure constant compliance with all necessary operational policies including:</b></p>

<p></p>
<ul>

<li>Food Hygiene</li>
<li>Maintenance</li>

<li>Liquor Licensing</li>
</ul>
<p><b>SUPPORTIVE FUNCTIONS</b></p>
<p>
In addition to performance of the essential functions, this position may be required to perform a combination of the following supportive functions, with the percentage of time performing each function to be solely determined by the supervisor based upon the particular requirements of the company.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Attend mandatory meetings including divisional meetings, staff meetings, etc.</li>
<li>Participate in community events and ensure corporate social responsibility goals of sbe are met.</li>
<li>Utilize traditional software programs such as Microsoft Office (Word, Excel, Outlook, and PowerPoint), Micros, ADP, Open Table and any departmental specific systems used.</li>
<li>Keep work area clean and organized.</li>
<li>Ensure confidential documents are kept in a secured area.</li>
<li>When disposing confidential documents that contain any personally identifiable information, they must be shredded or pulverized.</li>
<li>Complete other duties as assigned by the General Manager.</li>
<li>Demonstrate positive leadership characteristics, which inspire Team Members to meet and exceed standards.</li>
<li>Ensure compliance with sbe’s policies and procedures.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>OTHER DUTIES</b></p>
<p>
Assimilate into sbe’s culture through understanding, supporting and participating in all sbe elements. Demonstrate working knowledge of the service standards.</p>
<p>
Regular attendance in conformance with the standards, which may be established by sbe from time to time, is essential to the successful performance of this position.</p>
<p>
Due to the cyclical nature of the hospitality industry, employees may be required to work varying schedules to reflect the business needs of the property.</p>
<p><b>
SPECIFIC JOB KNOWLEDGE, SKILL AND ABILITY</b></p>
<p>
The individual must possess the following knowledge, skills and abilities and be able to explain and demonstrate that he or she can perform the essential functions of the job, with or without reasonable accommodation.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>BA or BS degree with emphasis in Marketing is required.</li>
<li>At least five years of progressive years in progressive roles within a marketing function, preferably in an upscale or lifestyle brand hotel</li>
<li>Proven team leader with a high level of energy and motivation with a proven track record of living the company's values</li>
<li>An intermediate to proficient understanding of Computer systems such as: Opera, Microsoft Word, Excel &amp; Outlook is preferred</li>
<li>Enter and locate work related information using computers and/or point of sale systems</li>
<li>Ability to spend extended lengths of time viewing a computer screen</li>
<li>Possess a gracious, friendly, and fun demeanor</li>
<li>Ability to multitask, work in a fast-paced environment and have a high level attention to detail</li>
<li>Maintain positive and productive working relationships with other employees and departments</li>
<li>Ability to work independently and to partner with others to promote an environment of teamwork</li>
<li>Must be able to stand or walk a minimum eight-hour shift.</li>
<li>Must be able observant and quick to respond to various situations while also multitasking and handling stressful situations.</li>
<li>Must be able to twist, tow (push or pull), reach, bend climb and carry objects as necessary.</li>
<li>Must have excellent communication skills and be able to read, write, speak and understand English.</li>
<li>Must be able to work inside and outside at all times of the year as needed, based upon business volumes.</li>
<li>Knowledge of federal, state and local laws governing equal employment opportunity and civil rights, occupational safety and health, wage and hour issues, and labor relations</li>
</ul>
