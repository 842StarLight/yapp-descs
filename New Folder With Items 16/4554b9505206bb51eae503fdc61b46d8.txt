<h2>Parkhurst - Unit Marketing Internship</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>This is a unique opportunity for a student currently enrolled on campus. Are you looking for a great job with fantastic pay, flexible hours, and a convenient, on-campus location? Parkhurst Dining is seeking a Marketing Intern for this academic year. Assist our Parkhurst team on campus with the development and implementation of strategic marketing initiatives aimed at increasing sales, creating exciting food experiences and showcasing our Parkhurst philosophy to the campus community.</p>
<p><b>
ESSENTIAL JOB FUNCTIONS AND RESPONSIBILITIES:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Help manage the local social media presence including building, branding, planning, maintaining and measuring social media outlets such as Facebook, Twitter, Instagram and Snapchat</li>
<li>
Identify and execute strategies for connecting the campus audience with the dining team through fun and engaging events, activities and marketing campaigns</li>
<li>
Assist team in designing and activating attractive and on-brand signage and visual displays that are compelling and impactful</li>
<li>
Act as a student liaison with various departments and organizations on campus</li>
<li>
Build a marketing plan for increasing sales and driving traffic to specific on-campus dining locations that includes communication plans, promotion tactics and measurement of success</li>
<li>
Collaborate with the Parkhurst team in developing new ideas and tools for marketing and communication</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
BENEFITS:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Hands-on Experience - this position will provide you with opportunities to develop a portfolio</li>
<li>
Mentorship - collaborate with an experienced marketing mentor to develop ideas</li>
<li>
Flexible Schedule - we will build your work schedule around your classes</li>
<li>
Free Food - one free meal during each full work shift</li>
<li>
Networking - connect with members of the corporate marketing team and other interns</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
QUALIFICATIONS:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Current student pursuing a degree in Marketing, Advertising, Communications, or related field</li>
<li>
Highly organized and a motivated self-starter with an attention to detail</li>
<li>
Experience utilizing social media sites including Facebook, Twitter, Instagram and Snapchat</li>
<li>
Fluent in Microsoft Office Suite and technologically savvy</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
START DATE AND HOURS:</b></p>
<p>
Requires a full academic year commitment beginning in the fall semester. 10-20 hours per week, as agreed upon with supervisor, carried out over a minimum of two days per week. Schedule is flexible to accommodate class schedules and other on-campus commitments</p>
