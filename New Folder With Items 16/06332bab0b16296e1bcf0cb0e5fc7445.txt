<h2>Computer Tech 1</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div><div>
<div>
<div><b>Description:</b></div>
<br>
<div>
<p>
</p>
<p>The Computer Tech 1 position is responsible for support of all information systems and related infrastructure. This includes computers, server, networks, CCTV, wireless, cabling and point of sale. The ideal candidate will have extensive exposure to a desktop environment with different hardware and software vendors. This individual should have a strong interest in I.T. and be driven to provide excellent service.</p>
