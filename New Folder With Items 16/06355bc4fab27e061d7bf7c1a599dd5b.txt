<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Please review the following job description:
</b></h3>
<p></p>Identify, solicit, and develop new client opportunities in the restaurant and hospitality segment within an assigned market. Utilize a consultative sales approach by helping business prospects assess their point-of-sale operating environment and payment processing needs, and deliver integrated, value-add solutions that address those needs. Self-source leads and actively work with the Community Bank Merchant Sales team to identify lead opportunities. Facilitate on-site client product demonstrations and consultations. Identify cross-sell opportunities for other Bank products and services. Educate staff and clients on industry news, trends, and new products and services. Ensure referrals and new business opportunities are addressed in a high quality, responsive manner, striving to deliver the Perfect Client Experience (PCE) with every client interaction
<p></p>
<p><b>Essential Duties and Responsibilities:
</b><br>
Following is a summary of the essential functions for this job. Other duties may be performed, both major and minor, which are not mentioned below. Specific activities may change from time to time.
<br>
1. Lead new business development efforts related to the sale of integrated point-of-sale hardware, software and cloud-based solutions primarily to larger high volume clients.
<br>
2. Consult with clients on assessing their business requirements and deliver a proposal that addresses those requirements.
<br>
3. Evaluate a client’s payment processing needs and refer any merchant processing opportunities to the appropriate Merchant Sales Consultant.
<br>
4. Facilitate product/service demonstrations.
<br>
5. Cross-sell new or additional value-added products and services to clients.
<br>
6. Support the Bank’s Integrated Relationship Management strategy by identifying and referring other banking services to the appropriate area.
<br>
7. Develop and maintain a thorough knowledge of all applicable integrated point-of-sale solutions and payment processing products, services, terminology, and industry trends by attending all mandatory training.
<br>
8. Successfully complete all applicable certifications and training related to this position.
</p>
