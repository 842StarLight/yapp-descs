<h2>Marketing Director</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div><div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>The Company</b></h2>
<p>Founded and operated by former and current restaurant owners, MarginEdge’s mission is to create a world where restaurant operators can focus on the business they love. By eliminating unproductive paperwork and streamlining the flow of operational data, MarginEdge is reimagining the back office and freeing restaurants to spend more time on their culinary offerings and guest experiences. MarginEdge serves 1000 restaurants in 34 states, having now tripled sales for 3 consecutive years (no lie). We are on track to do it again this year and are looking for your help to keep it up! We are a venture capital backed company scaling up in an enormous market that’s just ripe for change.</p>
<p>
This is an opportunity of a lifetime to join a startup that is not PLANNING to grow, but is in the midst of explosive growth! Hop on board!</p>
</div></div>
<div><div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>The Role</b></h2>
<p>Working from our Merrifield, VA office (two blocks from Mosaic, 10-min walk from Metro), you’ll be part of a growing marketing team focused on massively accelerating direct lead generation from a variety of B2B and SaaS marketing strategies while increasing MarginEdge local and national brand awareness. You will have proven B2B marketing experience, with a hands-on, execution-focused track record. You’ve worked across a variety of acquisition channels including digital, email, events and media, implementing cohesive campaigns to deliver meaningful revenue generation and outstanding buyer experiences.</p>
<p>
With your entrepreneurial mindset and hands-on abilities, you’ll be responsible for executing against critical strategies of B2B marketing, including demand generation, events, content marketing, marketing analytics, SEO, SEM and product marketing. You will quickly become an expert in our market and its makeup and use that information to help maximize marketing ROI. You’ll report to the Chief Marketing Officer while also interacting with other groups, including sales, partnerships and the development team.</p>
</div></div>
<div><div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Key Responsibilities</b></h2>
<ul>
<li>Build and manage brand; you are specifically excellent at positioning, messaging and content creation.</li>
<li>Lead and execute projects and campaigns, using your strong project management skills</li>
<li>Conceptualize and execute lead-acquisition email strategies, as well as ongoing nurturing campaigns</li>
<li>Assist in the execution of local and national event marketing activities</li>
<li>Manage execution of paid digital demand-gen campaigns, such as search/PPC/social</li>
<li>Bring new and creative ideas to the table around lead acquisition and nurturing</li>
<li>Measure, analyze, and ensure decisions are made based on data vs. gut-feel</li>
<li>Work with outside consultants and vendors as needed</li>
<li>Work with and help develop other awesome marketing team members over time</li>
<li>Be a key point of contact from the company to the marketing organization on day-to-day tasks and responsibilities</li>
</ul>
</div></div>
<div><div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Qualifications</b></h2>
<ul>
<li>6+ years experience in B2B marketing and demand generation strategies with direct responsibility for executing campaigns and achieving target ROI</li>
<li>4+ years of demand generation experience and leveraging digital marketing channels for growth</li>
<li>Hands-on experience in a variety of acquisition channels including email marketing, SEM, social and events</li>
<li>Hands-on experience with email and/or marketing automation platforms</li>
<li>Degree in business or marketing</li>
<li>Fantastic people and management skills</li>
<li>Solid strategic thinking, time management and organization skills</li>
<li>Experience overseeing the design and production of print materials and publications</li>
<li>Good eye for design and creative assets</li>
<li>Low ego and high intellectual curiosity</li>
</ul>
</div></div>
