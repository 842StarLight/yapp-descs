<h2>Senior Truss Designer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>We are looking for a Senior Truss Designer for our Dallas Truss Operation.</p>
<p>The Sr. Truss Designer performs layout and design, assists with the sales of floor/roof trusses, EWP products and mentors and trains Jr. Designers.</p>
<p>The Sr. Designer must be able to read blueprints to create accurate bids, material and price lists for clients. The ideal candidate will have excellent math skills, practical problem-solving skills and must possess at least 5 years of experience designing trusses and creating truss placement plans using Alpine Software as well as AutoCAD.</p>
<p>Knowledge of Microsoft Office products and a degree in Drafting or Design is preferred.</p>
<p><b>Educational Requirements: </b>High school diploma or GED;</p>
