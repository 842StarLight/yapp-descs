<p><b>
Job Summary</b></p>
<p>
This position also provides technical and analytical support for various Service offerings including, but not limited to, monitoring and dispatch, remote access, software distribution and patch management, and electronic journals and screen changes. You will work with and guide Field Service Technicians, Network Engineers, other Systems Engineers, Service Owners, Project Management and Client Services Specialists to verify completion or identify and troubleshoot problems within applications and identify software programming defects. In addition, you will work with Implementations and Client Services as first level to resolve customer problems and assist with new customer installs, reimages and upgrades.</p>
