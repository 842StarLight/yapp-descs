<p>Description</p>

<div><div>
<p>Goodwill Northern New England is an influential social enterprise that creates jobs, delivers needed services for people with diverse challenges, and uses environmentally friendly products and processes — all while earning revenue to pay for and expand innovative workforce services in Maine, New Hampshire and northern Vermont. We operate retail stores, employment services, neuro rehabilitation centers, residences for people needing 24/7 support and cleaning/property services.</p>
<p>
Goodwill Northern New England — good works here!</p>
<p>
 In addition to employment that is fulfilling, meaningful and supportive, Goodwill employees also receive:</p>
<ul>
<li>30% Employee discount at Goodwill stores in ME, NH &amp; VT</li>
<li>Valuable job training with growth potential</li>
<li>Access to Internal Job Board</li>
<li>Discount on personal cell phone plans</li>
<li>Local community discounts</li>
<li>403(b) retirement plan</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Schedule: Nights and weekends required</b></p>
<p><b>
Starting at $11.25 per hour!</b></p>
<p><b>
Job Summary</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
<b> </b>Participate in effective day-to-day operation of all sales floor activities including merchandising, running a cash register, and maintaining a clean and neat sales floor, sorting and pricing merchandise, and receiving and tracking donations.</li>
<li>Represents the Agency in all interactions with other members of the Goodwill community and with the general public, supporting the agency Vision of creating sustainable communities that thrive through the fullest participation of their diverse residents.</li>
<li>Furthers the organizational commitment to triple bottom line results: integrating social, economic and environmental goals into thinking, planning and actions.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Essential Functions
</b></p>
<p><b>Communication</b>:</p>
<ul>
<li>Provides high-quality customer service by helping customers to locate merchandise, answering questions, etc. Receives donations, issues donation receipts and assists donors when applicable. Works with supervisors to ensure individual production goals are met on a consistent basis.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Knowledge:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Complies with applicable federal, state, and local laws and regulations as well as Agency policies</li>
</ul> <p><b>Administration:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Sorting merchandise into categories</li>
<li>Pricing merchandise using best practices and stocking to sales floor plan</li>
<li>Operate power equipment to organize and store donated product</li>
<li>Loading material in to gaylords, trucks, and trailers for export</li>
<li>Processes material at donation site by sorting donations made throughout the day</li>
<li>Accepts Donations from customers, loading goods in to hampers for sort</li>
<li>Running a cash register, ensuring accurate tenders and a balanced cash drawer.</li>
<li>Pre-scanning books for processing</li>
<li>General housekeeping</li>
<li>Merchandising inventory according to sales floor plan</li>
<li>Pulling merchandise from the sales floor as needed.</li>
<li>Attending to fitting rooms</li>
</ul> <p><b>Requirements:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Criminal background check that meets Agency standards</li>
<li>The ability to: sit, stand, bend, reach, and lift
</li>
<li>Ability to push/pull up to fifty (50) pounds.</li>
<br>
</ul>
