<h2>Vehicle Marketing Specialist (Part-time)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>*Here we grow again!*** PART TIME ***<br>
Territory Centered in Palm Springs. You need to live in the Palm Springs area or very close by. Do you thrive when you're working on your own without someone watching over your shoulder throughout the day while also being part of a large team? AutoUpLink USA connects automotive retailers with their customers by designing easy to use, captivating web and native software applications. Tired or working in an office for a flat salary or an hourly wage? Do you thrive when you’re working on your own without someone watching over your shoulder throughout the day? Do you enjoy being outdoors? Then come join one of the biggest providers of on-the-lot services in the automotive industry!</p>
<p>The Vehicle Marketing Specialist is a unique position that blends photography and customer service at automotive dealerships that have contracted with AutoUpLink USA for marketing content creation services. You will visit assigned, client sites multiple times per week to photograph vehicles to create content that our customers use to advertise vehicles to shoppers.</p>
<p>With continued growth in your area, we have found the need to locate someone who can deliver on-the-lot services part-time to contracted clients. At inception this role is a part-time position that will require 10 - 20 hours each week, but the position may be able to expand to a full-time role in the future.</p>
<p>The successful candidate will have the ability to grow and take on more responsibility based on your performance. The ideal candidate will have 2 - 3 years of photography knowledge and a working knowledge of automotive retail vehicle prep and listings online; however, all candidates with relevant skills and "mojo" will be considered!</p>
<h4 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>About Us</b></h4>
<p>AutoUpLink USA provides automotive retailers with applications and tech-enabled services, with a focus on inventory management and merchandizing, that allow retailers to interact with their customers throughout the shopper lifecycle from initial interest to vehicle ownership. Simply put, we bring people together around vehicles.</p>
<p>Our team includes brilliant people who share the goal to ethically design technology solutions that address customer needs in new and innovative ways. It’s exciting stuff, and you can be part of it. Work with amazing people doing amazing things. You’ll learn heaps and be challenged daily. Bring passion and dedication to your job and there’s no telling what you could accomplish.</p>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>EARNING POTENTIAL</b></h3>
<p>You will be paid as a 1099 Contractor. The compensation plan for this critical role includes:</p>
<ul>
<li>Commission Based - (a % of your billing generated from vehicle photography), with weekly minimum guarantee. Earnings average $15-25 + per hour.</li>
<li>Mileage reimbursement to travel to and from job sites</li>
</ul>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>RESPONSIBILITIES</b></h3>
<ul>
<li>Visit client dealership locations on scheduled week days to photograph vehicles</li>
<li>Photograph vehicles at client sites using a company-provided camera</li>
<li>Capture video of vehicles (participating accounts)</li>
<li>Identify vehicles to be serviced using the company's mobile app on a company-provided mobile device</li>
<li>Print window labels (i.e. window stickers and Buyer's Guides) and apply them to vehicles</li>
<li>Interact with dealership personnel during visits to ensure customer satisfaction</li>
<li>Communicate with your supervisor in person, by phone, text and email regularly about work performance and client satisfaction</li>
<li>Synchronize (i.e. upload) the captured photos or video clips to company's cloud environment using company software</li>
</ul>
