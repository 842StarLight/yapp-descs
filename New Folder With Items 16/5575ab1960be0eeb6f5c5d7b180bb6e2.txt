<h2>Head of Corporate Marketing & Communications</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p><b>POSITION SUMMARY:</b></p>
<p>
The Head of Corporate Marketing &amp; Communications will be responsible for driving, and implementing an integrated marketing strategy and plan for all marketing, communications, branding and public relations in support of Groq’s corporate strategy and business plan. The individual will build, promote and protect Groq’s brand and reputation with internal and external stakeholders. Primary areas of responsibility include social media postings, web/digital content development, employee communications, event planning and support.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
ESSENTIAL DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES:</p>
<ul>
<li>
<p>Drive outbound messaging, and PR strategies to achieve short-term and long-term company objectives with key target audiences including the AI community, editorial/investor/analyst community and target customers</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Drive execution of Groq’s communications strategy in order to establish and advance Groq’s brand reputation</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Identify corporate media opportunities and strategies</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Embrace strategies that engage employees from many different geographic and cultural backgrounds</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Implement and own brand strategy and standards</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Work closely with Groq’s senior leaders to identify opportunities to effectively communicate the Groq story, and to craft and deliver content</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Partner closely with the sales and product management teams to help them hone and align their customer-facing messages</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p>QUALIFICATIONS:</p>
<ul>
<li>
<p>5-10+ years of marketing and communications experience in B2B semiconductor, software or systems companies</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>BA or BS in Business, Marketing, Communications, English or related major preferred</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>MA/MS in a related field is a plus</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Proven track record in Corporate Marketing &amp; Communications with B2B companies (silicon, software or system-level)</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Able to manage and execute an effective budget with a focus on measurable results</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Proven expertise in establishing and tracking marketing success metrics with the goal of driving results</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Able to partner across the organization</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Outstanding verbal and written communicator and presenter</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Understanding of industry, competitive and related product trends to inform Groq’s go-to-market, business, and people strategies</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Motivated self-starter willing to work under pressure in a deadline-oriented environment</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>A vital, trusted person; possesses ability to participate in challenging, fast paced execution and to contribute to big-picture thinking and strategic decision-making</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p><b>PERSONAL ATTRIBUTES:</b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>
<p>Ability to build strong relationships</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Good written and oral communication skills; strong technical documentation skills</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Good interpersonal skills; a history of mentoring junior engineers and interns a huge plus</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Highly self-motivated and directed; self-confidence and self-starter</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Keen attention to detail</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Proven analytical and problem-solving abilities</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Ability to effectively prioritize and execute tasks in a high-pressure environment</p>
</li>
<li><p>Experience working in a team-oriented, collaborative environment</p></li>
</ul>
