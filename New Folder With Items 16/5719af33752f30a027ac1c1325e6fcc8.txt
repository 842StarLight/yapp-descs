<h2>General Manager / VP of Operations</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>General Manager, Vice President: Turbine Division</p>
<p>Reports to CEO</p>
<p>Manage P&amp;L of $30M business that makes up currently 50% of annual sales volume of 3 portfolio companies. Major customers include Siemens, GE Industrial, GE Aviation, Pratt &amp; Whitney, Rolls Royce, Solar and Chromalloy. Growth is expected in Aerospace Parts production with GE, PW and RR. Responsible for 160 employees across two specific manufacturing locations in Bloomingdale and Glendale Heights, IL.</p>
<p>Essential duties and responsibilities include but are not limited to the following:</p>
<ul>
<li>Manage P&amp;L of build to print machining operations for complex Turbine Blade and Vane Shop that supports 12 major customers across Aero and Industrial Gas Turbine Platforms</li>
<li>Candidate must have high-energy approach and strong sense of urgency.</li>
<li>Assess current business processes and change/modify where necessary to eliminate waste and increase efficiency with the business.</li>
<li>Improve and leverage key metrics and key performance indicators to drive operational performance, introduce new metrics as needed to drive specific areas of the business.</li>
<li>Take an ongoing leadership role relative to the implementation, utilization and adoption of the ERP upgrade project with Epicor software.</li>
<li>Lead and own critical project management for initiatives related to growing throughput of the business as sales grows with Aero customers.</li>
<li>Develop, manage and direct a high-performance team to delivers results.</li>
<li>Direct day to day operations of growing $30M annual business with regards to Operations, Human Resources, Quality. Customer Service, Procurement, Scheduling and Engineering.</li>
<li>Collaborate with CEO and Board of Directors to develop and define strategic business direction.</li>
<li>Produce timely and accurate reporting of operational results and regularly and proactively communicate those results to the organization and Board.</li>
<li>Lead annual budgeting of the P&amp;L under his/her direct control, as well as periodic reforecasts.</li>
<li>Analyze operations to evaluate performance of the Company and its staff to determine areas of potential cost reduction, process improvement and/or policy changes. Develop plans, measure execution and improvement items as needed.</li>
<li>Utilize cost accounting data to drive increases in margins and trending to increase efficiency of machines and labor.</li>
<li>Play an integral role with building a culture of trust, respect, collaboration and professionalism with employees and clients through delivering on time and meeting requirements of the business.</li>
<li>Oversee NPI process at the Turbine Division to ensure on-time development and flawless transition to operations for production runs.</li>
<li>Transition facility from a 20% Aerospace parts business, to a 40-50% business in 2 years.</li>
<li>Drive CI process with a direction towards best practices with regards to Lean culture and an improved 5S visual factory.</li>
<li>Work closely with direct reports to mentor and develop staff. Provide day-to-day leadership, direction and motivation while advancing a culture of performance and accountability.</li>
<li>Other items as required or directed by CEO and Board</li>
</ul>
<p>Experience Required</p>
<ul>
<li>Managed a $25 to 50M P&amp;L in the recent past with direct experience with a machining build to print operation for complex product.</li>
<li>Continuous Improvement experience with business and operations processes that has resulted in increased efficiency and profitability.</li>
<li>Understanding of Quality processes as they relate to Aerospace product, AS9100 and NADCAP as needed for NDT, EDM, Milling and Grinding.</li>
<li>Strong Operational background with previous solid roles held in manufacturing operations that has dealt with customer facing challenges with regards to Quality and Delivery performance.</li>
<li>OEM experience at manufacturing or aftermarket parts repair facility dealing with customer requirements on Aero and or Industrial Gas Turbine Experience.</li>
<li>Direct Applicable Experience implementing effective changes in a manufacturing environment that required culture changes to initiate sustainable change.</li>
<li>Experience managing an aerospace supply chain with outside vendors while maintaining costs and associated quality performance metrics.</li>
<li>Direct Planning and Scheduling experience as it relates to capacity planning and delivery performance in a complex machining environment.</li>
</ul>
