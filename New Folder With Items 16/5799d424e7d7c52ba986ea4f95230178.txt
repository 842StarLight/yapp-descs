<h1 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Job Description</b></h1>
<p><b>
Summary</b></p>
<p>
The Medication Management Solutions (MMS) business is a $2.2B global provider of Medication Management technology serving Acute Care Hospitals, Non-Acute Care, and Retail Pharmacies. We are focused on making the medication use process simpler, safer and smarter.<br>
<br>
Our medication management solutions have effectively changed the way medications are obtained and distributed throughout a hospital. Our technologies can help to increase financial results, reduce medication errors, streamline clinical workflow, facilitate regulatory compliance and enhance security and the quality of patient care. Marketing and Product Management is responsible for defining the market strategy, including product offering, pricing, positioning, distribution and promotion to drive the overall success of the business.</p>
<p>
BD Pyxis is looking for a talented Product Manager to join our team working in San Diego, CA. This Product Manager will own their assigned products over the full life cycle.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Job Description</b></p>
<p><b>
KEY RESPONSIBILITIES:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Develop and demonstrate understanding and experience in product/market management, specifically an expertise in acute care and non-acute care pharmacy operations, including automation and workflows.</li>
<li>
Increase or develop knowledge of healthcare information technology capabilities in the context of medication management.</li>
<li>
Strong understanding of the voice of the customer, representing their needs</li>
<li>
Become an expert on the specified products, understanding the impacts on the customer workflows</li>
<li>
Communicate with customers how the product works and integrates with other products</li>
<li>
Execute lifecycle management strategies to ensure install base can evolve</li>
<li>
Understand and influence dependencies on adjacent business roadmaps</li>
<li>
Advocate for internal groups ensuring product is easily sold, implemented, adopted and supported</li>
<li>
Work closely with downstream marketing to provide content and drive positioning of new products and services</li>
<li>
Triage incoming customer requests within product enhancement process</li>
<li>
Assess financial implications, including profitability and viability; define and analyze metrics that inform the success of products.
</li>
<li>Communicate effectively and work extremely well with complex multi-disciplinary teams; work collaboratively across all functions of the organization to ensure successful product development, market launch, and development.</li>
<li>
Provide leadership to technical and non-technical members of the team.</li>
<li>
Must be able to manage expectations and possess excellent organizational skills with the flexibility to readily adapt to change.</li>
<li>
Be willing to travel up to 25%.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>

QUALIFICATIONS AND EXPERIENCE:</b></p>
<ul>

3-5 years experience in product management, preferably in healthcare automation and/or healthcare operations.</li>

Experience developing and launching software, hardware, integrated systems, and mobile solutions desired.</li>
<li>
Partner management experience desired.</li>
<li>
Bachelor's Degree required, with a strong preference for degrees inMarketing, or Business; MBA preferred.</li>
<li>
Proficient with Microsoft Office products including: Word, Excel, and PowerPoint.</li>
<li>
Must have excellent communication presentation and writing skills (e.g., responsive, persuasive, and polished) and be comfortable communicating across various levels of stakeholders (Sales to Executive Leaderss).</li>
<li>
Personal characteristics: dynamic, team player, detail-oriented, critical thinker, self-motivated, enthusiastic, problem solver, resourceful - "will figure it out", customer-focused, interested in professional development/career progression, personable.</li>
</ul>
