<h2>Equipment Operator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>CPM, a CRH company, is a division within Oldcastle APG. APG is North America’s leading manufacturer and supplier of concrete masonry, dry mix, and hardscape products. With over $2B in sales, APG operates across 36 states and 5 Canadian provinces through a network of over 195 operating locations and more than 6,500 employees.</p>
<p>Job Duties</p>
<ul>
<li>Operate heavy equipment, specifically a front end loader and/or forklift, and production machinery as directed and specified while adhering to all safety rules and regulations</li>
<li>Load bins for production while ensuring product is correct mix and quantity to prevent delay in the production process</li>
<li>Keep work area clean</li>
<li>Read job specifications to determine machine adjustments and material requirements</li>
<li>Assist machine operators to set up machines and any other necessary tasks to adhere to deadlines and completion of product</li>
<li>Other duties and responsibilities related to the nature of the job may be assigned on a temporary or permanent basis as needed</li>
</ul>
<p>Requirements</p>
<ul>
<li>High school diploma or GED equivalent; or at least three years of related manufacturing experience in trades specific to job requirements; or equivalent combination of education and experience</li>
<li>Ability to read and interpret documents such as safety rules, operating and maintenance instructions, shipping and receiving tickets, and procedure manuals</li>
<li>Must be able to pass internal certification program</li>
<li>Must have the ability to lift and/or move up to 100 lbs</li>
<li>Bilingual in English and Spanish preferred</li>
</ul>
