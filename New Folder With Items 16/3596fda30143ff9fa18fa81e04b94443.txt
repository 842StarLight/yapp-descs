<p><b>OVERVIEW OF THE COMPANY
</b></p>Fox Sports
<p></p>FOX Sports is the umbrella entity representing FOX Corporation’s wide array of multi-platform US-based sports assets. Built with brands capable of reaching more than 100 million viewers in a single weekend, FOX Sports includes the sports television arm of the FOX Network; FS1, FS2; FOX Soccer Plus; FOX Deportes, FOX College Sports and a majority stake in the Big Ten Network. FOX Sports includes ownership and interests in linear television networks, digital and mobile programming, broadband platforms, multiple web sites, joint-venture businesses and several licensing relationships.
<p></p>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>JOB DESCRIPTION
</b></h2>
<p>From facilitating and spearheading the pre-production, production, and post-production needs of every on-air promo produced, to managing and training the group’s Production Assistants, we are seeking a Project Manager to join our FOX Sports On-Air Promotions team in Los Angeles. As an integral member to our Marketing Department, you’ll oversee promos, campaigns, presentations, sales tapes, and collateral media produced by the on-air department. You’ll serve as liaison between the Creative and Operations departments within the Marketing Department, as well as a liaison between the department and outside vendors, and as a liaison between the department and Network Engineering &amp; Ops team. In addition, you’ll procure all needed elements, resources, and information needed for creation of each spot/campaign. Simply, you’ll play a significant role to our team!
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>A SNAPSHOT OF YOUR RESPONSIBILITIES
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Direct all daily management needs for each Creative project - includes assigning proper staff, facilities, and technical resources to each Creative Group project
</li>
<li>Communicate with Planning and Ops on all deliveries
</li>
<li>Communicate all strategy and content needs to Creative Directors
</li>
<li>Create daily production calendars and workflow timelines for each project
</li>
<li>Point person for out-of-house projects, shoots, and vendors (graphic design, and Visual Effects companies we work with)
</li>
<li>Seek and cultivate key relationships with desired vendors in the industry, including securing/hiring of DP’s, production companies, visual efx companies, agencies, music composers and negotiation of deal and execution of contract
</li>
<li>Communicate and participate in negotiating deals with outside vendors hired to do creative work on behalf of our group
</li>
<li>Manage and train departments (5) PA’s
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>WHAT YOU WILL NEED
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>College degree in Broadcast, Film, Advertising, or Marketing
</li>
<li>Minimum 5 years’ experience in television or film production
</li>
<li>Must understand internal procedures and policies for delivery of On-Air Promo creative product
</li>
<li>Must be detailed oriented and organized individual
</li>
<li>Must have technical knowledge of software and capacity of high-end visual effects and editing equipment
</li>
<li>Must have contacts in commercial television industry
</li>
<li>Must be efficient and persuasive to ensure best product is delivered on time and on budget
</li>
</ul>
