<h2>Lab Packaging Supervisor</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">CORE JOB DUTIES
<br><br>
Responsible for day to day operations and performance of the packaging department
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>* Maintains inventory levels and production standards</li>
<li>Creation of Packaging Work Orders in a timely manner to keep up with order fulfillment</li>
<li>Collaborates with sales department to ensure orders are properly fulfilled</li>
<li>Oversees inventory, updates inventory levels as needed to reflect sales</li>
<li>Recognizes areas of opportunity that improve Safety, Product Quality, Food Safety, Cost, Productivity, and Employee Moral</li>
<li>Responsible for managing employee records (write ups, time off requests)</li>
<li>Creation of compliance labels from Biotrack</li>
<li>Maintains fast pace and high attention to detail in all areas of the team</li>
<li>Collaboration with quality teams to ensure labels are going on correctly and packaging remains compliant with regulations for the industry</li>
</ul>
REQUIRED EXPERIENCE, EDUCATION AND SKILLS
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Strong attention to detail and ability to adhere to strict policies and procedure</li>
<li>Ability to measure, package and label in a precise manner</li>
<li>Effective time-management skills and ability to multi-task</li>
<li>Ability to work in a fast-paced, changing and challenging environment</li>
<li>Proficiency in windows-based software and point of sale applications a plus</li>
</ul>
ADDITIONAL REQUIREMENTS
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Must be 21 years of age or older to apply</li>
<li>Must comply with all legal or company regulations for working in the industry</li>
</ul>
