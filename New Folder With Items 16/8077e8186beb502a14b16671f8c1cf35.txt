<h2>Leasing Consultant, Part-Time</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>The Excelsior Group, a growing Property Management/Investment company, is looking for a part-time <b>Leasing Consultant</b> to join our team at Rafter Apartments. Rafter is a brand new, 283-unit luxury apartment community located in North East Minneapolis.</p>
<p><b>
Job Objective(s</b>)</p>
<p>
Support the Property Manager in daily property operations, leasing of apartments and customer service.</p>
<p><b>
Schedule:</b> Part-time, flexible, includes two to three weekends per month</p>
<p><b>
Job Responsibilities and Duties</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Leases Apartments. Answers phone and on-line inquiries, meets and greets rental prospects, identifies and meets prospect needs, shows available apartments, follows-up with prospects. Contributes to achieving team leasing goals.</li>
<li>Processes Rental Applications. Assists prospects in completing the application process, assists Manager with reviewing applicant qualification and obtaining necessary information for resident screening.</li>
<li>Provides prompt and friendly customer service to existing residents. Processes work requests, maintains effective communications, answers questions, manages resident concerns, problem solving, etc.
</li>
<li>Collects deposits, rent payments, ensures that lease files are complete and that the correct entries are posted in the accounting system.</li>
<li>Welcomes new residents, provides property orientation and coordinates move -in process.</li>
<li>Ensures property readiness. Inspects the property and units regularly to ensure product standards are met, schedules maintenance as needed. Assists in light property upkeep including maintaining coffee bar, ensuring neat and orderly appearance of the office, amenity areas, models and common areas of the property, general property pick up, light dusting or “sparkle” cleaning as needed, etc.</li>
<li>Completes traffic sheets, market surveys, and maintains guest card files. Prepares correspondence such as thank you notes and other general correspondence.</li>
<li>Assists Manager is new lease and lease renewal processes</li>
<li>Utilizes and manages resident and prospect database through YARDI (property management software) computer program.</li>
<li>Assists with property marketing activities including off site promotional events</li>
<li>Attends company training and round-table events as scheduled.</li>
</ul> <p><b>Job Qualification Standards</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Customer service or sales/leasing experience preferred.</li>
<li>Strong written and personal communication skills.</li>
<li>Positive, out-going and friendly outlook.</li>
<li>General understanding of e mail, internet, MS Office applications (Word and Excel)</li>
<li>Well organized and able to juggle multiple work projects.</li>
<li>Works well independently and on a team.</li>
<li>Ability to work some weekends.</li>
<li>Neat, well-groomed, professional appearance.</li>
<li>High School diploma or equivalent required</li>
<li>Possess a valid driver’s license, acceptable driving record, and access to a vehicle.</li>
</ul>
