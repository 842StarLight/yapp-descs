<h2>Field Service Engineer II</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">Scope/Role/Description
<br><br>
The Field Service Engineer II will install, repair, and maintain Agena proprietary equipment at customer sites. Provide support services to new and existing customers. Provide technical direction, training, and support to research and commercial customers.
<br><br>
Responsibilities
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Provide technical service to customers through the installation, calibration, repair, training, and maintenance of Agena systems via phone, email, internet and on-site support.</li>
<li>Serve as customer’s single-point-of-contact on technical and service related issues.</li>
<li>Develop a working and collaborative relationship with customers’ applications staff.</li>
<li>Interface with company Sales Representatives, Customer Service, Applications &amp; Technology, Product Support and other departments to build and support professional and comprehensive customer relationship management.</li>
<li>Follow departmental protocol including, but not limited to, service call scheduling, parts ordering, Return Materials Authorization, service call logging and expense reporting.</li>
<li>Complete field change orders, customer complaint handling, and instrument configuration documentation for ISO compliance.</li>
<li>Repair, install and maintain mass spectrometers, dispensing equipment, liquid handlers, computers/databases, and application software.</li>
<li>Attend scientific meetings and seminars.</li>
<li>Promote and facilitate the sale of system service agreements.</li>
<li>Manage spare parts trunk-stock inventory, and company owned test equipment.</li>
</ul>
