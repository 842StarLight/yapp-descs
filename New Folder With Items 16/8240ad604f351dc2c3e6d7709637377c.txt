<h2>Community Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Looking for an experience multi-family community manager to join our growing team! We have the opportunity to add the most sought after luxury community in Greenville S.C. to our portfolio and we are looking for the best of the best!!!</p>
<p>JOB SUMMARY</p>
<p>Responsible for insuring the efficient operation of the property under the direction of the Regional Manager (RM).</p>
<p>DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES Conducts all business in accordance with company policies and procedures, Fair Housing, Americans with Disabilities Act, Fair Credit Reporting Act, and all other laws pertaining to apartments.</p>
<p>➢ Operating the property within the financial guidelines, i.e., the budget, established by the Regional Manager and Owner</p>
<p>➢ Daily physical inspections of the property and direct supervision of the service team.</p>
<p>➢ Direct supervision of the office staff and bookkeeping functions.</p>
<p>➢ Responsible for interviewing and hiring all property employees under the direction of the Regional Manager.</p>
<p>➢ Responsible for insuring a professional appearance and attitude at all times for yourself and all property employees.</p>
<p>➢ Responsible for the thorough knowledge, implementation and enforcement of all policies and procedures of Southwood Realty; insuring through constant supervision and review that all personnel in all departments are operating within those regulations.</p>
<p>➢ Responsible for the leasing of the property; insuring through supervision of the office and leasing personnel that all sales techniques and methods required by Southwood Realty are being used effectively and in a professional manner.</p>
<p>➢ Responsible for insuring that all personnel respond to resident requests of complaints in a timely, efficient and courteous manner.</p>
<p>➢ Responsible for generating various reports, i.e., Company-required reports.</p>
<p>➢ Responsible for implementing, designing and maintaining a resident retention program, i.e., newsletter, and social activities.</p>
<p>➢ Responsible for insuring that all personnel in all departments operate within OSHA (Occupation Safety &amp; Health Act) standards and company safety policies at all times.</p>
<p>➢ Responsible for reporting any unusual or extraordinary circumstances regarding the residents of the property.</p>
<p>➢ Responsible for seeking educational opportunities and self-improvement for personal growth and development.</p>
<p>➢ Responsible for securing at least 10% of the overall closed leases at the property.</p>
<p>➢ And anything else that the Regional Manager feels falls into the Property Manager’s job description.</p>
<p>QUALIFICATIONS</p>
<p>To perform this job successfully, an individual must be able to perform each essential duty satisfactorily. The requirements listed are representative of the knowledge, skill, and/or ability required. Computer Skills: Word Processing (MS Word) and basic knowledge of the On-site Rental system (MRI), Spreadsheet (MS-Excel), Internet and Printing Software (Print Shop). Office Equipment: Computer, Multi-Function, and Telephone. Work Hours: Employee will be required to work a flexible work week which will include working weekends.</p>
<p>SUPERVISORY RESPONSIBILITIES</p>

<p> Responsibilities include interviewing, hiring, and training employees; planning, assigning, and directing work; appraising performance; rewarding and disciplining employees; addressing complaints and resolving problems.</p>
