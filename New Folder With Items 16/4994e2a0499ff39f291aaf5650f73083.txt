<p><b>POSITION:</b> Inventory Analyst/IT</p>
<p><b>
RATE/SALARY:</b> $23-29/hr $49K - $60K</p>
<p><i>
Beatty Lumber &amp; Millwork Co.</i>, is a leading LBM (Lumber &amp; Building Material) distributor. We are currently recruiting for an Inventory Analyst for our Upper Darby, PA (Delaware County) facility. Our 97 year history is marked by consistent, steady growth which has significantly widened our scope of experience and capabilities as an LBM distributor.<br>
<br>
The Inventory Analyst/IT works closely with the Sales Staff, Purchasing Manager, EVP and President. In this position being able to evaluate, plan and monitor specific inventory levels to meet demand forecast and corporate financial objectives. These decisions are optimized based on understanding of the appropriate inventory levels necessary to support the company’s sales order fill rate objective balanced against seasonal peaks, delivery lead-times and financial goals.<br>
<br>
<b>ESSENTIAL DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITES</b> include but are not limited to the following. The Company reserves the right to add to, delete, change or modify the essential duties and responsibilities at any time. All work is to be completed with minimal supervision and in accordance with Company standards. Other duties may be assigned as needed.</p>
<ul>
<li>Analyze demands daily/weekly/monthly to identify procurement requirements.</li>
<li>Analyze, manage and maintain inventory levels aimed at specific desired days of inventory on hand optimize against seasonal peaks and sales order fill rate objectives.</li>
<li>Review product/category lifecycles for proper sourcing and exiting strategies.</li>
<li>Monitor order status, stock outages, inventory turns, forecast, and inventory stocking levels for sales staff and others.</li>
<li>Work with purchasing manager to maintain best practices in both purchasing &amp; needs</li>
<li>Determine, evaluate, and redeploy obsolete and excessive inventory; collaborate with Sales and Operational leaders to orchestrate promotional sales of excess or slow-moving inventory.</li>
<li>Contribute to the transition of discontinued products to new replacement products.</li>
<li>Monitor and measure back order frequency; report variance from sales order fill rate.</li>
<li>Analyze inventory to rebalance stock levels.</li>
<li>Coordinate and monitor cycle inventory counts and reconciliations.</li>
<li>Recommend standard policies &amp; procedures to ensure adequate control of purchases and inventories.</li>
<li>Maintain accurate inventory data including new inventory item set-up, product codes, product names, authorized substitutes, and stocking levels for supply planning and inventory management.</li>
<li>Always represent Beatty Lumber &amp; Millwork CO. with integrity and professionalism.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>COMPETENCY:</b><br>
<br>
To perform the job successfully, an individual should demonstrate the following competencies:</p>
<ul>
<li>Inventory management, allocation or replenishment, financial planning or financial analysis.</li>
<li>Inventory Management Principals – In-depth knowledge of Stocking, Inventory Turn Analysis, Inventory Ranking, and Financial Analysis with respect to inventory.</li>
<li>Computer/IT – database organization, retrieval and analysis techniques, including ERP and Excel software programs. MS Office 365</li>
<li>Interpersonal – focus on conflict resolution rather than blame; maintain confidentiality; keep emotions under control; remain open to other’s ideas and new processes.</li>
<li>Oral Communication – Speak clearly and persuasively in positive or negative situations; listen and get clarification; respond well to questions; participate in meetings.</li>
<li>Quality – demonstrate accuracy and thoroughness; look for ways to improve and promote quality; apply feedback to improve performance, monitor own work to ensure quality.</li>
<li>Quantity – meet productivity standards, complete work in a timely manner; strive to increase productivity.</li>
</ul>
