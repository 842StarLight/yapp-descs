<h2>Sr. Manager, Global Strategic Planning</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<div>
<div>Johnson Controls is hiring a Sr. Manager, Global Strategic Planning in Lansdale PA.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div><b>
What You Will Do</b></div>
<div>
Johnson Controls Fire Suppression Products is the leading supplier of a diverse portfolio of solutions that serve a multitude of needs in the Fire industry. With a revenue of approximately $1.3 Billion, we develop and deploy our products to over 130 countries worldwide. The Senior Manager of Global Strategic Planning facilitates a wide variety of activities that aid in our continued growth and leadership in the industry.<br>
<br>
<b>How You Will Do It</b>
</div>
<ul>
<li>Business M&amp;A lead for Fire Suppression Business – Develop pipeline, evaluate targets, lead preliminary due diligence efforts, collaborate with Corporate Development Office on deal terms, lead post-transaction integration efforts</li>
<li>Core Strategic Planning for Fire Suppression Business – Working cross-functionally with finance, product management, sales, operations, and engineering to align on fiscal year strategic plan, business transformation efforts, and additional growth opportunities</li>
<li>Specialty Projects for Natural Business Units and/or Product Lines – Serve as the champion for specialty projects across various natural business units and/or product lines within the Fire Suppression Business. Collaborate with Senior Leadership Team to outline goals and objectives.</li>
<li>Work cross-functionally across the organization and throughout the corporate functions to achieve the stated objectives</li>
<li>Executive Presentations – Develop performance presentations for the Fire Suppression Business to be presented to Executive JCI Leadership</li>
<li>
</ul>
<div>
Additional Functional Areas<br>
Competitive Intelligence – Identify competitive developments across regions, product lines, segments, etc.<br>
Market Intelligence – Monitor developments in key end customer verticals. Evaluate potential underlying impact to FSP<br>
Ad hoc projects – Resiliency and willingness to shepherd ad hoc projects as they arise<br>
</div>
