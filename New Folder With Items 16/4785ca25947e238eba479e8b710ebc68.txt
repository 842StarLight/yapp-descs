<h2>Business Development Director</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Business Development Director</p>
<p><b>ESSENTIAL FUNCTIONS AND RESPONSIBILITIES</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Develop new business by presenting MeridianLink solutions to financial institutions</li>
<li>Articulate the vision of how MeridianLink solutions accelerate trust and deliver value in the financial institution market</li>
<li>Build and maintain a pipeline large enough to meet or exceed annual sales quota</li>
<li>Present value to banking executives</li>
<li>Self-generate leads and support lead follow up generated through partnerships and marketing</li>
<li>Utilize excellent listening skills to identify issues and present solutions</li>
<li>Ensure that established territorial goals are met</li>
<li>Develop overall strategic approach to assigned territory or market</li>
<li>Build credible relationships with prospects</li>
<li>Analyze software configurations, develop proposals and demonstrate software</li>
<li>Understand a financial institution’s loan/deposit workflow and the specific challenges facing CEOs and their management teams</li>
<li>Perform the duties and support other territories in the absence of the assigned Business Development Manager when required</li>
<li>Attend tradeshows when assigned</li>
<li>Always maintain the highest levels of professionalism representing MeridianLink to the marketplace</li>
<li>Work alongside Partner Success Managers to help ensure client satisfaction is maximized</li>
<li>Help clients by promoting maximum utilization of the solution</li>
<li>Update and maintain contact management system (CRM)</li>
<li>Work closely with the EVP of Sales on strategy and revenue growth</li>
<li>Exceed annual sales quota</li>
</ul>
<p><b>QUALIFICATIONS</b></p>
<ul>
<li>7+ years of professional software sales experience</li>
<li>Sales performance documentation over the last 3 years</li>
<li>Financial institution lending experience is ideal, but not required</li>
<li>Credit union/financial institution background helpful</li>
<li>Experience selling loan origination software preferred</li>
<li>Contacts in bank, credit union, finance company market ideal, but not required</li>
<li>The ability to skillfully demonstrate software and articulate the features, advantages and benefits that are in line with the customer’s buying criteria</li>
<li>Experience working from a virtual office</li>
<li>Exceptional written, verbal and interpersonal communication skills</li>
<li>Proficient with Microsoft Office products</li>
<li>Bachelor’s degree in Business, Finance or related field</li>
<li>Ability to travel nationwide up to 70%</li>
</ul>
