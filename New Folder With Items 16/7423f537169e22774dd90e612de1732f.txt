<h2>Director of Project Management</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Director of Project Management</p>
<p>At Luxer One, we’re committed to making life simpler by automating package acceptance and completely solving the package problem using the best technologies available. Whether it’s last mile delivery at multifamily properties and offices, or click &amp; collect orders in retail, we find the best solutions for our customers and ensure their success.</p>
<p>We’re seeking a leader to run our Project Management group. The Project Management group consists of three teams including Project Management, Logistics and Installations. The ideal candidate for Director of Project Management excels in process improvement, project management, relationship management, scaling capabilities and demonstrates leadership and approachability.</p>
<p>Minimum qualifications include a B.S. Degree or equivalent experience, demonstrated experience in leading projects and programs, and has a PMP, CAPM or equivalent credentials. The candidate is comfortable working in a fast-paced environment and is proficient in the coaching and development of a team. The Director of Project Management is passionate about people, process improvement and achieving department and company goals.</p>
<p>Responsibilities:</p>
<ul>
<li>Leading and empowering teams to achieve KPIs and execute deliverables</li>
<li>Assigning project workloads</li>
<li>Manages ongoing relationships and communicates with key internal and external partners including property owners/managers, general contractors, sales teams, installation and logistics vendors and others</li>
<li>Mitigating and resolving escalated matters with clients</li>
<li>Identifying new or improved processes to reduce waste and improve workflow</li>
<li>Leads three teams including:</li>
<li>Project Management - this team manages the end-to-end process from closed sale to post-delivery of Luxer One systems to the customer.</li>
<li>Logistics - This team manages the transportation of the deliver of Luxer One systems through third-party logistics providers (3PL)</li>
<li>Installations - This team oversees the installation processes and solutions used by internal, external and Value Added Resellers</li>
<li>Recruit, staff and supervise a team of 20 employees</li>
<li>Evaluates outside service providers, contractors and vendors; provides direction in the development of tailored services; negotiates contract terms and conditions</li>
<li>Leads innovative thinking and fosters continuous improvement across all Project Management, Logistics and Installation activities</li>
<li>Responsible for internal project communications, resource and budget management, as well as internal client satisfaction for all assigned projects</li>
<li>Manages routine installations, testing meetings and monitors employee workloads to maximize performance</li>
<li>Develops ROI models and evaluate savings vs. projections for logistics planning and improvements</li>
<li>Develops strategy and overall operation of transportation and distribution; Leads and participates in planning processes which includes the inventory control, capacity planning, throughput and distribution</li>
</ul>
<p>Requirements:</p>
<ul>
<li>Ability to use company CRM and planning software</li>
<li>Ability to use a MAC computer, Google suite, Microsoft Office and other company systems</li>
<li>Ability to multitask</li>
<li>Skilled in influencing outcomes by providing sound, persuasive rationale for ideas and opinions.</li>
<li>Quick Learner</li>
<li>Highly organized with demonstrated experience in managing competing priorities in a high-volume environment</li>
<li>Collaborative problem-solver that values inclusive decision making within the team. Excellent communication and leadership skills; Strong people skills and ability to motivate teams through vision and communication.</li>
<li>Experience at the strategic level, helping with long-term road maps as well as process development</li>
<li>Ability to work full-time; some evening and weekend hours may be required</li>
</ul>
<p>Benefits:</p>
<ul>
<li>Medical, Dental, Vision, and 401k</li>
<li>Fast-paced, fun, and energetic company with a friendly culture</li>
</ul>
