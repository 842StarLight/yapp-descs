<h2>Case Manager Registered Nurse</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p><b>Come join BrightStar Care, a team that believes in serving with a passion and doing the right thing. BrightStar Care of Madison is looking for a Registered Nurse to provide clinical oversite for our team.
</b></p>
<p>BrightStar Care is the industry leader in supportive home care and medical staffing. We make a difference in the way we provide care of our clients and our staff, fulfilling our mission of peace of mind. Our perfect candidate will be a self-driven, high-energy individual with an entrepreneurial spirit.
</p>
<p>If you think you know Home Health Care, we are different. We are not certified with Medicare or Medicaid, which allows you to focus on the client and our staff, and not be bogged down with paperwork and complicated reporting. BrightStar Care of Madison is part of our multi-unit operation, providing high quality care throughout southern Wisconsin for 12 years. Quality of care is of utmost importance to us, as evidenced by the fact that we are Joint Commission accredited.
</p>
<p>The Registered Nurse Case Manager will work with our Enterprise Director of Nursing and Madison Branch Manager to oversee the care provided to our clients. A typical day may include performing new client assessments, overseeing the care of existing clients (personal care and skilled), and care coordination with skilled service organizations and hospices. Compassion, patience and an outgoing personality are qualities that will build trust and a level of comfort with clients and their families. The Case Manager will work primarily (approximately 75%) in the field and 25% of the time in the office.
<br>
<br>
<b>Case Manager Essential Duties and Responsibilities:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Provide care as defined within the scope of the state’s nurse practice act
</li>
<li>Make the initial evaluation visit and regularly reevaluate the client’s nursing and/or personal assistance needs
</li>
<li>Assist with the intake of new clients and inform potential clients of our services and differentiators
</li>
<li>Provide those services requiring substantial and specialized nursing skill
</li>
<li>Initiate appropriate preventive and rehabilitative nursing procedures and counsel client and family on nursing related needs
</li>
<li>Prepare clinical and progress notes and summaries of care
</li>
<li>Assist in the development of organizational goals and agency performance improvement activities
</li>
<li>Administer agency policies and procedures
</li>
<li>Assist with assessing the competency of staff to promote quality, continuity and safety of client care
</li>
<li>Provide information to be used for statistics, reports and records for purposes of evaluation and reporting of organizational activities
</li>
<li>Assure proper maintenance of clinical records in compliance with local, state and federal laws
</li>
<li>Promote home care referrals in the health care community
</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Position Requirements
</b><br>
<br>
<b>Education and Experience:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Registered nurse with current unencumbered license to practice professional nursing in Wisconsin
</li>
<li>Minimum of three (3) years of documented experience required. Two (2) or more years of clinical experience with one (1) year experience in a home health agency or case management preferred
</li>
<li>Flexibility and good time management skills A MUST
</li>
<li>Possess exceptional nursing assessment skills and comply with accepted professional standards of practice
</li>
<li>Experience with, or desire to, participate in marketing and sales a plus
</li>
<li>Excellent interpersonal skills, Treat clients, staff and the public with courtesy, respect and presents a positive public image
</li>
<li>Analytical/Critical thinker and problem solver
</li>
<li>Ability to travel including weekends, nights and holidays if necessary
</li>
<li>Understanding of regulatory compliance local, state and federal laws, Joint Commission, OSHA and HIPAA
</li>
</ul>
