<div><b>Accountability Statement
</b></div>
<p>Develop, implement and support process integration solutions for Operations and other Corporate Functions.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<div><b>Position Responsibilities
</b></div>
<div>Position Summary</div>
<div>The SAP PI Application Lead is responsible for designing, developing or adopting SAP PI technology to meet Lubrizol’s requirements. Most often the requirements will surround B2B integrations with customers, suppliers, and third party solution providers.</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div>Key Responsibilities</div>
<ul>
<li>Developing technical solutions for our business customers, including requirements gathering, feasibility and gap analysis, architecture design, implementation, and support.</li>
<li>Provides expert advice and counsel to business users and IT project teams regarding PI and integration.</li>
<li>Provides support such as troubleshooting application shortcomings and/or performance issues.</li>
<li>Adhere to and recommend development standards, naming conventions and industry best practices.</li>
<li>Stay knowledgeable of the SAP offerings - including SAP Process Orchestration, S/4HANA, SOAP, REST, etc.<br>
Mentor and lead contract developer resources.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<div></div>
<div>
<b>Basic Qualifications</b><br>
Minimum Qualifications</div>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science, Computer Engineering, or related discipline</li>
<li>8+ years in IT</li>
<li>4+ years working with SAP NetWeaver and Process Integration</li>
<li>Must be extremely proficient with development, deployment, and support of PI solutions</li>
<li>Experience with PI adapters and/or protocols including: HTTP, AS2, FTP, REST, Proxy, RFC, IDOC, SOAP, File, Mail, Web Services, etc
</li>
<li>Proficient with digital certificates and the use of certificate based authentication</li>
<li>Knowledge of Enterprise Services Repository (ESR), Integration Directory (ID), System Landscape (SL), Configuration and Monitoring (CM), Alert and Notification Management</li>
<li>Self-starter, ability to work independently</li>
<br>
</ul>
<div></div>
<div>Desired Qualifications</div>
<ul>
<li>Experience with B2B and/or EDI solutions</li>
<li>Experience with XML, Schemas, ANSI X12 EDI Standard<br>
Knowledge of SAP Process Orchestration (PO) and S/4HANA<br>
</li>
</ul>
