<h2>Director of Product Management, Monetization</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>Seeking an outstanding Director of Product Management who desires an exciting career challenge building a world-class digital media platform. You will work with world-renowned magazine brands including Men’s Health, Bicycling, Runner’s World, ELLE, and Cosmopolitan. Ideal candidates possess big-picture vision yet the drive and attention to detail to make that vision a reality. You must enjoy spending time with customers and evangelizing their needs. You balance strategic thinking with outstanding execution – holding yourself and your team accountable to delivering successful outcomes. You enjoy solving complex business problems at the core of the digital transformation of a world-leading media company.<br>
<br>
As Director of Product Management, Monetization, you will lead a team to define and deliver revenue and traffic optimization strategies across the Hearst stable of media properties. You will develop innovative on-site advertising solutions, evolve programmatic performance systems, and create advertising operations and campaign management tools for the hundreds of sites on Hearst’s MediaOS publishing platform.<br>
<br>
You will work closely with the Advertising Operations, Advertising Sales, and Data Studio groups - balancing quality consumer experiences with long-term value generation and operational efficiency. You will leverage the scale and resources of a successful, well-known business combined with the nimbleness and forward-looking feel of a startup.<br>
<br>
This position can be based in New York or San Francisco.<br>
<br>
<b>Primary responsibilities:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Develop strategies and execute plans to drive revenue and scale the business – directing a holistic, integrated product strategy across the Ad Experience and Ad Tools portfolios.</li>
<li>Own overall metrics: effective RPM (Revenue per thousand pageviews) and operational efficiency of the Revenue Operations group.</li>
<li>Build, lead, grow and motivate a team of high-output Product Managers.</li>
<li>Analyze, determine and prioritize target customer, end-user, and internal stakeholder needs.</li>
<li>Define and communicate a product roadmap aligned with company objectives, and supported by business cases, market research, customer data and competitive analysis.</li>
<li>Collaborate with cross-functional stakeholders to build momentum and alignment, and shepherd product concepts through execution, validation and adoption.</li>
<li>Ensure appropriate customer research, usability testing and rapid prototyping. Improve revenue while maintaining a valuable, engaging and enjoyable end-user experience.</li>
<li>Document, validate and prioritize product specifications within an Agile/SCRUM development environment.</li>
<li>Coordinate go-to-market launch and post-launch activities. Gather customer feedback to validate that the product meets stated metrics. Prioritize and deliver necessary enhancements.</li>
</ul>
<br>
<b>
Required Experience and Qualifications:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>8+ years in Internet/Web product management, with at least 5 years expertise in programmatic advertising, ad tools and/or digital media platforms.</li>
<li>3+ years building and managing teams of at least five or more independent-contributor Product Managers, across all levels of seniority.</li>
<li>A Bachelors degree or equivalent is required. An MBA or advanced degree preferred.</li>
<li>Extensive knowledge of the digital advertising ecosystem and related emerging technologies and trends.</li>
<li>Proven track record of full responsibility through the product lifecycle in a fast-paced, Agile environment. Has shipped highly successful products.</li>
<li>Extensive cross-functional and senior stakeholder exposure and management.</li>
</ul>
<br>
<b>
Demonstrable Skills:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Leadership. Takes responsibility from inception to outcomes. Determines needs, aligns priorities, delivers to market, measures outcomes, and seeks to build upon success.</li>
<li>Customer focused. Deeply understands the market and unmet needs. Loves to create and deliver outstanding products that delight customers and passionate for constant innovation.</li>
<li>Highly collaborative. Builds trust and strong relationships at all levels, influences without formal authority, pushes back when appropriate, earns respect, and respects the contributions of others.</li>
<li>Excellent communicator. Articulates the product strategy and roadmap, structures the problem at hand, experienced and comfortable gaining alignment and in presenting to senior executives.</li>
<li>Strategic thinker. Understands business objectives and converts them into clear product plans, follows through with execution.</li>
<li>Analytic and results orientated. Uses hard data to assess opportunities, metrics driven, ruthless in prioritization, overcomes obstacles to drive business outcomes.</li>
<li>Driven and motivates others. Takes initiative, sets context, listens and finds compromises, drives for clarity, relentless, hard-working, self-accountable.</li>
<li>Understands technology and keeps current with the latest trends. Works with implementation teams to adapt to technical constraints without compromising end-user solutions.</li>
<li>Comfortable with ambiguity and challenge. Thrives in a fast-paced environment, concurrently manages multiple initiatives, remains optimistic.</li>
</ul>
