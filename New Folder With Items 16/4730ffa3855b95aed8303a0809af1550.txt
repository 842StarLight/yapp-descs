<b>What you will do</b><br>
<br>
The Systems Support Technician provides advanced technical, product, and services solutions to technicians, sub-contractors and sales representatives. You will assist associates in selling, installing, and servicing commercial security systems and will have knowledge of down loading software. The Systems Support Technician should have a basic knowledge of computers, networking, routers and Windows (XP, Vista and 7).Must be able to provide support on Web Browsers (Windows Internet Explorer and Fire Fox). You must have previous experience in Burglar alarms, Fire alarms, Video equipment or Card access equipment.<br>
<br>

<br>
