<div><b>Product Marketing Manager</b></div>
<div></div>
<div>Velosio, one of the largest and most recognized Microsoft Dynamics partners, is looking to add a Product Marketing Manager to our team. Headquartered in Columbus, Ohio, Velosio’s 300 employees serve over 4,000 clients. Reporting to the Chief Marketing Officer, the Product Marketing Manager will be responsible for owning the overall product marketing strategy for Velosio’s product and service offerings.</div>
<div></div>
<div><b>Responsibilities</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Develop Velosio product and service IP positioning that differentiates Velosio by understanding customer needs, product roadmap/service deliverables, competitive differentiation and market dynamics</li>
<li>Plan and execute product/feature and packaged service offering launches across cross-functional teams (spanning Product Management, Marketing, Support, Service, Sales, Channels) and related sales enablement</li>
<li>Collaborate with Sales to understand &amp; support ongoing enablement, and maximize impact of messaging for the target audience</li>
<li>Build “first and best” content in the forms of presentations, feature sheets, white papers, etc. that will be used by sales (direct and channel) for positioning Velosio to prospects and customers</li>
<li>Develop messaging, positioning, sales and customer facing collateral in coordination with your peers in the Marketing and Product teams</li>
<li>Collaborate with peers contributing to the GTM strategy for your products target audience and buyer persona in coordination with Sales and Enablement teams</li>
</ul>
<div><b>Qualifications</b></div>
<ul>
<li>5+ years product marketing, product management experience in the cloud, software or technical B2B space</li>
<li>Knowledge of industry marketing execution are highly desired</li>
<li>Eager storyteller who can articulate both verbally and in writing a compelling vision</li>
<li>Strong technical acumen with ability to quickly synthesize technical content into ‘positioning’ for non-technical audiences and craft messaging that connects with both internal stakeholders, partners and customers</li>
<li>Can manage multiple initiatives at the same time and energetically pursue them to show continued iterative progress</li>
<li>Leader who is comfortable in virtual environment and who can operate independently to build out new programs and initiatives for an emerging segment</li>
<li>Team player who can work across an organization to build a plan, get alignment and execute it</li>
<li>Customer-first mentality</li>
<li>Bachelor’s degree required in (preferably) Computer Science, Engineering or Marketing/Business Administration. MBA preferred</li>
<li>Willingness to travel up to 20%</li>
<li>This position can work remotely</li>
</ul>
