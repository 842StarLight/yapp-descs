<h2>Project Manager (IT)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>Working under minimal supervision of the Manager, Technical Services Operations, referring only exceptional problems and issues for management review or approval, the Project Manager manages small to large complex projects for customers, as well as multi-disciplinary projects. The Project Manager assesses and develops business requirements, develops functional specifications, integrates with back office systems, and identifies business tasks, UAT, and implementation. Project Manager can be assigned to large scale roll out projects with in the configuration center to insure projects are being completed by specified service level agreements. Manages the SharePoint sites for project onboarding design, production, depot and R&amp;D and develops standard operating procedures (SOPs) in accordance with PMI standards for the different teams. Provides Project Management leadership to the assigned teams.<br>
