<h2>VP, Digital Strategy & Success</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>Job Description<br>
<br>
<div>The Digital Strategy &amp; Success Department at APEX is responsible for constructing strategic media plan recommendations for Publicis Groupe clients while delivering campaign performance, vendor billing and reconciliation, and investment management.<br>
<br>
These practices are executed by three key functions including:<br>
<ul>
<li>Our team of Digital Strategists, who are tasked with partner selection, margin management, media planning, campaign forecasting, and audience analysis.</li>
<li>Our Digital Success team, who ensures standout campaign delivery – campaign punctuality, pacing, and performance – while managing partner billing with our Media Operations team.</li>
<li>Our Media Operations team, who oversees reporting needs of our Digital Success group while also supporting the reconciliation process.</li>
</ul>
</div>
<p><br>
This role will oversee the above functions in our New York office and will report to the SVP of Digital Strategy &amp; Success in New York.<br>
</p>
<div>
<b>
Responsibilities</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Act as escalation point and support for Apex digital management team &amp; agency leads to address business, service, and operational challenges.</li>
<li>Develop and manage bi-weekly reporting on business &amp; operational efficiency across the Digital Strategy &amp; Success group.</li>
<li>Work alongside offline teams at Apex to discuss best practices and synergies that can impact Apex’s greater business and operation.</li>
<li>Cultivate relationships with key account leads and conduct quarterly check-ins to discuss service expectations and growth opportunities.</li>
<li>Communicate regularly with Product and Investment teams to deliver recommendations, citing market feedback and capability audits.</li>
<li>Provide hands-on support to Digital Strategy &amp; Success management team in addressing vendor, billing, and personnel-related issues.</li>
<li>Regularly meet with region leads to discuss account-based needs, and work with Brand Development (Sales) leads to create account action plans.</li>
<li>Manage cross-departmental collaboration projects with Ad Operations, Finance, Human Resources, Product, Legal, and Value Management.</li>
</ul>
</div>
</div>
<br>
<div>Qualifications<br>
<br>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>8+ years of digital advertising industry experience (preferably agency or adtech/programmatic), with over five years of management.</li>
<li>
A bachelor’s degree (preferably in a relevant field of study)</li>
<li>
Systems &amp; software proficiency (Salesforce, Excel, Powerpoint, OracleBlueKai, LiveRamp, market-leading DSPs, etc.)</li>
<li>
Top-notch verbal and written communications skills, as well as presentation skills</li>
</ul>
