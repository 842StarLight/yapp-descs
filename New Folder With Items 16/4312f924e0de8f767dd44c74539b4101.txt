<h2>Business Development Associate</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Business Development Associate </b></p>
<p><b>THE CHALLENGE</b></p>
<p>Recently recognized as one of Kansas City’s <b>Best Places to Work</b> by the Kansas City Business Journal, VeriShip is expanding rapidly!</p>
<p>We need self-motivated, confident, and collaborative sales professionals to join our Business Development team.</p>
<p>Your primary responsibility will be to identify qualified prospects, uncover their challenges, review our solutions with them and enroll future clients in a free trial. For more strategic opportunities, the BDA will facilitate demonstrations of VeriShip’s software with a Sr. Account Executive.</p>
<p><b>RESPONSIBILITIES</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Gain subject matter expertise of VeriShip’s solutions within the multibillion-dollar parcel shipping market</li>
<li>Make daily outbound calls following up on targeted marketing efforts</li>
<li>Initiate and nurture meaningful business discussions with prospective customers to discover key challenges they face</li>
<li>Meet and exceed goals for lead qualification and new opportunity creation</li>
</ul>
<p><b>DESIRED SKILLS AND EXPERIENCE</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Possess limitless ambition, goal orientation, and a desire to fail fast and learn quickly</li>
<li>Intensely motivated by a strong compensation plan</li>
<li>Take 100% acceptance of responsibility for results</li>
<li>Lead generation or prospecting experience preferred but not necessary</li>
<li>Previous inside sales experience with software is a plus, but not required</li>
</ul>
<p><b>WHAT WE PROVIDE</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Formal, professional Sales Training through an industry-leading program</li>
<li>One of the best cultures and working environments in Kansas City (our brand-new offices are adjacent to the restaurants, coffee shops, and shopping of Ward Parkway Mall)</li>
<li>Competitive base salary plus monthly commissions and bonus program</li>
<li>Unmatched comprehensive employer-paid benefit program</li>
<li>Real career and personal growth opportunities in a fun and competitive environment where your voice matters</li>
</ul>
<p>Job Type: Full-time</p>
