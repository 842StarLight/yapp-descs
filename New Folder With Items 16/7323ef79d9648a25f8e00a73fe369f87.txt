<h2>License Concierge - Microsoft</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">Working under general supervision of the Dir, Microsoft Center of Excellence as an integral member of the Microsoft Center of Excellence (COE), the Licensing Concierge, Microsoft (LCM) supports the requirements of Business Solutions customers (those with less than 2,000 seats). The LCM maintains and nurtures trusted customer relationships as the lifeline to the customer for Cloud, MPSA, and Enterprise Agreements and monitors the associated benefits throughout the agreement lifecycle. The LCM assists the Licensing Solutions Specialists to support their goals of Microsoft sales penetration.
