<h2>Field Operations Manager (Automotive) for Minnesota Region</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>COMPANY OVERVIEW: </b></p>
<p>LifeSafer, Inc. is the leading manufacturer, distributor, and service provider of Ignition Interlock Devices (IIDs). Ignition Interlocks are installed in a vehicle and measure the alcohol in the driver’s breath, only allowing them to start the vehicle if their breath alcohol content is below a set point. In short, we help save lives by limiting one’s ability to drink and drive.</p>
<p><b>POSITION OVERVIEW: </b></p>
<p>Working with your Territory Manager, Regional Director, Training &amp; Standards and Business Development, a Field Operations Manager will oversee and support all Company and Contractor Service Center locations with an end goal of superior customer service and a superior client experience from all service locations. You will also be skilled to identify, acquire and set up new Service Center locations in a given market on a detailed and managed timeline. A Field Operations Manager must be intimately familiar with all state and local regulations regarding ignition interlock services.</p>
<p><b>ROLE AND RESPONIBILITIES: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Oversee that all Contractor and Company Service Centers are in compliance with all IID Regulations for their market.</li>
<li>Oversee that all Contractor and Company Service Centers are following all procedures as outlined in their Terms and Conditions, as well as all LMG Operational Procedures.</li>
<li>Assure that all Service Centers are providing a superior customer/client experience and satisfaction.</li>
<li>Provide support to Service Center Locations to resolve any Client Complaints in a priority fashion.</li>
<li>Manage Market footprint of Service Center locations, with the support of the Territory Manager and Business Development, to ensure the assigned Market has proper coverage with superior Company and Contractor Service Center locations.</li>
<li>Ability to identify, acquire, setup and train all new Service Center locations.</li>
<li>Must maintain a regular and documented travel schedule to properly oversee all field operations.</li>
<li>Ability to effective and efficiently communicate between departments for support.</li>
<li>Must be intimately familiar with the Service Center Operations Manuals for their assigned Market and be able to communicate any changes required to match all current or upcoming regulations regarding ignition interlock services.</li>
<li>Must perform a Service Center Visit Report during each visit to a Service Center.</li>
<li>Must perform a Service Center Operations Report at each Service Center once per calendar year.</li>
<li>Must be intimately familiar with all devices and software servicing platforms.</li>
<li>Up to 75% travel.</li>
</ul>
