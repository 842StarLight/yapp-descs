<h2>Pay Per Click Marketing Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>PRIMARY FUNCTION:</b></p>
<p>Manages Internet advertising campaigns and oversees existing ones by performing tasks to minimize the overall pay per click cost of a web-based campaign.</p>
<p><b>ESSENTIAL DUTIES/ RESPONSIBILITIES:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Handle day-to-day management of PPC campaigns on GoogleAds, Bing, and Yahoo for local and national consumer-facing and B2B programs</li>
<li>Ongoing campaign optimization, testing and performance improvement</li>
<li>Provide performance analysis, key data, reporting and recommendations to Senior Digital Marketing Manager and Vice President of Marketing</li>
<li>Monitor Google Analytics in addition to ad management platforms to gain insight into PPC traffic performance</li>
<li>Test new campaign strategies including ad copy, display campaigns, bid management and ongoing keyword research to consistently improve performance and gain key insights over time</li>
<li>Test lead generation strategies, including UI/UX, landing page variations, conversion optimization and behavioral profiling</li>
<li>Work with patient intake teams and other cross-departmental partners to augment keyword strategies with real-world data, experiences and needs</li>
<li>Maintain deadlines, tactics and other business items in an organized, detailed manor</li>
<li>Work with IT and marketing teams to implement and refine downstream data collection and analysis</li>
<li>Manage and monitor call tracking software for end to end lead tracking</li>
<li>QA all campaigns, ad sets and ad variations for errors and effective deployment</li>
<li>Maintain vigilance around current best practice, new ad products, trends and changes in regulatory landscape</li>
<li>Identify new and existing target audiences based on demographic, geographic, behavioral and interest-based data</li>
<li>Work with Senior Digital Marketing Manager to define internal process for campaign implementation</li>
<li>Set short-term and long-term PPC roadmap for all service lines and locations, with a focus on proactive strategy rather than reactive changes</li>
</ul>
<p><b>OTHER DUTIES:</b></p>
<p>Other duties as assigned.</p>
<p><b>SUPERVISORY RESPONSIBILITIES:</b></p>
<p>None</p>
<p><b>QUALIFICATIONS/EXPERIENCE REQUIRED:</b></p>
<p>To perform this job successfully, an individual must be able to perform each essential duty satisfactorily. The requirements listed below are representative of the knowledge, skill, and/or ability required. Reasonable accommodations may be made to enable individuals with disabilities to perform the essential functions.</p>
<p><b>EDUCATION and/or EXPERIENCE:</b></p>
<p>Bachelor's degree in Business, Marketing, Communications or a related degree</p>
<p>2+ years of experience in hands-on digital paid media management</p>
<p>Experience making data-driven decisions using analytics, A/B testing, and campaign reports and dashboards</p>
<p>Experience with third-party media management tools such as AdRoll, Marin, etc.</p>
<p>Experience with programmatic media buying platforms such as Trade Desk, Choozle, etc.</p>
<p>Experience in healthcare or multi-location marketing landscapes</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>KNOWLEDGE, SKILLS AND ABILITIES:</b></p>
<p>Demonstrated experience managing large monthly media spends</p>
<p>Expert knowledge of GoogleAds and Google Analytics</p>
<p>Understanding of PPC best practices, trends and regulatory considerations</p>
<p>Ability to write and optimize ad copy based on keyword research, brand requirements and brand voice</p>
<p>Working knowledge of SEO and content marketing strategies, and how paid campaigns interact with organic strategies</p>
<p>Ability to proactively manage projects and budgets, and collaborate with team members an external vendors to meet goals and deadlines</p>
<p>Salesforce Marketing Cloud knowledge</p>
<p>Familiarity with paid social media channels</p>
<p><b>CERTIFICATES, LICENSES, REGISTRATIONS:</b></p>
<p>GoogleAds and Google Analytics certifications preferred</p>
