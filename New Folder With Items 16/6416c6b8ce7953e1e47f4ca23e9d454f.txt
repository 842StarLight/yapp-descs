<div><b>INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY (IT) TECHNICIAN (CUSTOMER SUPPORT)</b></div>
<br>
<p>
</p>
<p></p>
<div><b>DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES</b></div>
<br>
<div></div>
<p>
</p>
<p></p>
<div>Provides customer service and support methods for Information technology (IT) systems installed within the organizations assigned. Provides training methods and knowledge base applications for multiple Local Area Networks (LAN) customers. Monitors, maintains, analyzes LAN infrastructure, servers, point of sale, time management systems, various databases, and data back-up systems. Performs LAN security support functions. Uses established IT techniques to adequately and promptly resolve LAN and computer related problems. Works independent and within supervisor standards to conform to established procedures. Refers problems not covered by the instructions or guides to the supervisor for help or a decision. Supervisor will review work to verify accuracy/conformance to required procedures and review findings/conclusions. Supervisor reviews atypical work in detail. Must utilize established procedures/techniques and uses judgment in selecting appropriate practices with minor deviation within existing guidance. Refers to the supervisor where guidance is not clearly established. Incumbent identifies important factors/conditions in order to identify interrelationships among various IT functions. Assists in planning to ensure unit needs are met. Assists managers/work groups to plan/enhance the operation and productivity of the various disciplines. Expected to establish criteria; formulate projects; assess IT Program effectiveness and analyze a variety of unusual conditions, problems or issues affecting a wide range of unit/base activities. Additional duties may include alternate Information Assurance Officer; Telephone Control Officer; Functional System Administrator; Client Support Administrator; IT Equipment custodian. Performs other related duties as assigned. Position of Trust.</div>
<br>
<p>
</p>
<p></p>
<div><b>QUALIFICATIONS</b></div>
<br>
<p>
</p>
<p></p>
<p>Experience should demonstrate a range of computer techniques, requirements, methods, sources and procedures. Must have experience working w/and maintaining computer hardware/software/peripherals. Must demonstrate a familiarity w/ Microsoft operating systems and Office products; an understanding of server based computing environment; PCs, server architecture and cabling topologies. Ability to install/maintain/analyze network infrastructure/interface. Must demonstrate ability to communicate and instruct users in a technical manner. Must achieve Security+ per DoD 8570-M requirements within 180 days of hire. Must work with minimal supervision. Must be able to climb/crawl in confined spaces. Must satisfactorily complete pre-employment physical. Must satisfactorily complete and maintain a National Agency Check with Inquiries (NACI). Valid driver's license required. This is a testing designated position (TDP) under the Air Force Civilian Drug Testing Program.</p>
