<h2>Bilingual Customer Support Analyst</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>RockAuto Bilingual Customer Support Analysts do not work from scripts or process workflows; they determine the needs of customers and use technology to accurately respond to a high volume of customer concerns. You will use your Spanish, German and/or French skills to respond to customer inquiries, create and implement solutions to customer concerns and help translate, maintain and improve our user-friendly website (available in Spanish, French and German versions).<br><b>Position Requirements </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree or currently enrolled in a bachelor’s degree program</li>
<li>Fluency in Spanish, French and/or German (as well as English)</li>
<li>Excellent customer service skills: ability to read closely, think quickly and problem-solve effectively</li>
<li>Familiarity with car model names, part descriptions and other automotive terms</li>
<li>Advanced computer skills – you will be using our automated translation tools and order-processing software, and contributing design ideas for our e-commerce website</li>
<li>Eagerness to have an immediate impact on business</li>
</ul>
<p>Must be able to work from our Madison, WI office and have the ability to work for any US employer without visa sponsorship.</p>
<p>We are open seven days a week. Part Time Bilingual Customer Support Analysts must work a minimum of 16 hours per week up to 40 hours per week (early afternoon to late evening availability). Some weekend availability is required. Full Time Bilingual Customer Support Analysts must work 40 hours per week (early morning to early evening availability).</p>
