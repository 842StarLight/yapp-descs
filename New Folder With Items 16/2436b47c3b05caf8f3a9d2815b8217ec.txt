<p><b>
About the Role</b></p>
<p>
CrowdStrike is seeking a software engineer who wants to work at scale, be challenged, and take on a highly impactful role in the API engineering team. You will work on and develop new customer facing API products as well as internal API Management systems which will be used by teams including Cloud Engineering and Intel. You will work closely to refine and improve internal API standardizations, frameworks and strive to enable other engineers in externalizing their products through the API infrastructure. Your experience of RESTful microservices and Open API standards will be applied to a rich and vast internal microservice architecture. Solid experience with large scale web services, open source tools and libraries to an understanding of under the hood (HTTP/S, TLS, etc) are strongly desired. If you want a job that makes a difference in the world and operates at high scale, you’ve come to the right place.</p>
