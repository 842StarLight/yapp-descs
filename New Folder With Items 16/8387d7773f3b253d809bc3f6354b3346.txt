<h2>Accounting Assistant</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<b>Description:</b><br>
Nova Association Management Partners is an Association Management Company providing full-service as well as customizable management services for Condominiums, Townhomes, HOA and Community Centers throughout the greater Seattle and Portland areas.
<br><br>
At Nova, we are committed to helping association boards protect homeowner investments. As leaders and innovators in our industry, we leverage knowledgeable, seasoned professionals and best-in-class technology and support systems to offer boards the operational expertise to steward their communities with confidence.
<br><br>
Our company, formerly known as Pinnacle Association Division, was established in 2006 and now manages more than 18,000 units across the Pacific Northwest. Our executive team has worked together for 10-plus years and our senior association managers have been managing associations with us for over five years.
<br><br>
We use a team approach to serve communities and maximize performance by integrating all areas of property operations with the daily tasks of association management. We align expectations and regularly measure our performance against expectations.
<br><br>
<b>SUMMARY:</b><br>
Assist Accountants with full cycle accounting for a portfolio of Homeowners/Condominium Associations.
<br><br>
Accounting
<br><br>
<b>Assist the Accounting team in the following areas:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Bank account maintenance and reconciliation</li>
<li>General ledger accounts analysis and reconciliation</li>
<li>Preparation and review of financial statements</li>
<li>Accounts Payable processing</li>
<li>Accounts Receivable processing</li>
<li>Responsible for time sensitive payments including, but not limited to mortgages, insurance, bonds, owner distributions and property taxes</li>
<li>Responsible for owner entries, recording ownership transfers, assisting with audits and auditor inquiries</li>
<li>Performs other duties as assigned</li>
</ul>
Cognitive
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Effective critical thinking and problem-solving skills, with ability to recognize problems early and produce prompt resolutions.</li>
<li>Ability to prioritize and focus on critical tasks as well as shift focus or multitask when appropriate.</li>
<li>Capable of working within a fast-paced, deadline driven environment while generating timely results.</li>
</ul>
Communication/Interpersonal
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Ability to discover, understand and meet the needs of clients in a timely manner.</li>
<li>Integrity, objectivity, and accountability in performing assigned duties.</li>
<li>Clear and concise written and verbal communication skills.</li>
<li>Diplomacy to develop and preserve positive relations with clients and team members.</li>
</ul>
