<h2>Sales Development Representative</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<b>The Basics:</b><br>
-----------
<br><br>
The Sales Development team is all about generating lead volume for our Account Executives while helping marketers and developers understand the many ways in which Branch can improve their company's success.
<br><br>
As a Sales Development Representative at Branch, you'll prospect and identify key decision-makers at target companies and lay the foundation for a strong relationship. You're an energetic and organized self-starter. You understand the sales process, can build relationships with the right decision-makers, and can succinctly define and communicate the specific benefits of Branch appropriate for each user. You will be a key driver of growth for the company, and on the front lines for evangelizing our product to prospective and existing users.
<br><br>
<b>What You'll Do:</b><br>
---------------------------
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Execute an outbound business development plan to deliver qualified opportunities to partners inside the business</li>
<li>Work closely with Account Executives to build a pipeline of Branch Customers</li>
<li>Develop and qualify opportunities with large app developers and mobile-centric companies</li>
<li>Make every potential Branch user happy with every interaction, regardless of deal size</li>
<li>Establish relationships with app marketers and developers around the world</li>
<li>Explain how Branch brings value to the customer's needs</li>
</ul>
<b>We're Looking for Someone With:</b><br>
-------------------------------------
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Strong technical aptitude and a desire to work in mobile, emerging technologies and startups</li>
<li>1+ years of relevant experience in B2B sales - client facing, consulting or marketing</li>
<li>SaaS experience preferred</li>
<li>Driven to succeed and ability to work in fast-paced environment</li>
<li>Track record of over-achieving against defined metrics (top 10-20%)</li>
<li>Disciplined and demonstrates excellent time management skills</li>
<li>Experience in prospecting and evangelizing SaaS, Marketing/Ad Technology</li>
<li>Ability to maintain a high level of productivity, manage multiple competing priorities, and work effectively under the pressure of time constraints in a fast-paced, complex, collaborative, and team-oriented environment</li>
<li>Understand and apply effective business development strategies and techniques</li>
</ul>
<b>Nice to Haves:</b><br>
--------------
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Previous Sales Methodology training</li>
<li>Ability to work in a fast paced, team environment</li>
<li>Bachelor's degree from an accredited University is strongly preferred</li>
<li>Willingness to work in a collaborative work environment</li>
<li>Passion for building new business</li>
<li>Technical expertise and ability to acquire knowledge of new technology</li>
</ul>
<b>About Us:</b><br>
---------
<br><br>
At Branch, we're transforming how brands and users interact across digital platforms. Our mobile marketing and deep linking solutions are trusted to deliver seamless experiences that increase ROI, decrease wasted spend, and eliminate siloed attribution. By integrating Branch technology into core marketing channels including apps, web, email, social media, search, and paid ads, leading brands are driving higher-value conversions than ever before.
<br><br>

We power mobile links, attribution, and measurement for more than 3 billion monthly users and over 50,000 apps, including Airbnb, Ticketmaster, Reddit, Buzzfeed, Twitch, OfferUp, Poshmark, and many more, and are working on exciting new ways to improve discovery in the mobile ecosystem.
