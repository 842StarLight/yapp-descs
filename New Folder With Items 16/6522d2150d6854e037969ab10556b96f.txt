<h2>Senior Product Marketing Manager - DIS</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><ul>
<div>
<p>Roche Diagnostics has created a new strategic area charged with building a modern portfolio of decision support software products that will change the way patients are cared for. Our challenge is to bring together complex diagnostic and treatment data from medical devices and IT systems within a hospital and intelligently process and present this data to clinicians, researchers, and caregivers to improve research outcomes and patient care.
</p>
<p></p>
<p>Roche Diagnostics and DIS (Diagnostics Information Solutions) launched NAVIFY Tumor Board solution, a clinical workflow and decision support software that optimizes decision-making for cancer patient case reviews in tumor boards, or multi-disciplinary team meetings. The solution fundamentally changes the way these meetings are organized and conducted. Patient data security and privacy are of the highest priority for Roche. The NAVIFY Tumor Board solution is built on a secure cloud platform that is HIPAA compliant.</p>
<p></p>
<p>
We are looking for candidates with strong expertise in building and executing Marketing strategies and the ability to operate in a fast paced environment to join our team and contribute to this exciting initiative.</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>
The Opportunity:
</b></p>
<p></p>
<p>We have a great opportunity for an experienced, passionate storyteller and product marketer with experience within the Healthcare or oncology software space organizations to join the DIS team in an important role. This is a critical position that will have a direct hand in driving the growth of a new portfolio of software decision support products. This is an exceptional opportunity to help define the product strategy and messaging and its competitive positioning, and go to market strategy for an emerging and transformative category, and be a driving force at the forefront of the next evolution of personalized healthcare. As a Senior Product Marketing Manager for DIS, you will think strategically, collaborate with the DIS customer success team, the Roche regional affiliates, product management, and support teams to deliver effective messaging, content, and tools to our key audiences, including prospects, customers, partners, and customer experts.</p>
