<br>Role
<br>****
<br><br>
This role is responsible for the creation, development, delivery, and evolution of Workato's sales productivity program. You are a versatile leader who is comfortable and effective in different settings including training in large forum settings, 1-on-1 coaching, building enablement content and collateral, measuring success, and establishing processes to ensure Workato AEs ramp quickly and continuously improve. This role will work closely not only with all of the sales leaders and AEs at Workato, but also with our marketing, product, and business development teams to ensure AEs are fully enabled.
<br><br>
****************
<br>Responsibilities
<br>****************
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Build the sales on-boarding program to effectively speed the ramp time of new Workato Account Executives and ensure they make their quota</li>
<li>Lead regular enablement sessions for Workato sales org as well as specific sales teams</li>
<li>Identify knowledge gaps and collaborate across departments to build and deliver content to address</li>
<li>Measure and report back on AE improvements</li>
<li>Organize all of Workato's sales enablement collateral to make it easy for Account Executive to find what they need, when they need it</li>
<li>Establish certification system to ensure knowledge retention</li>
<li>Assist in-flight deals by providing AEs with the best and latest enablement collateral</li>
<li>Evolve Workato's sales process to improve win rates and reduce cycle times</li>
<li>Identify technologies to purchase to improve sales productivity</li>
<li>Build a global sales enablement team that supports current and future business needs</li>
</ul>
************

<br><br>

<li>Proven track record of scoping, building and executing integrated sales communications, and enablement programs and content</li>
