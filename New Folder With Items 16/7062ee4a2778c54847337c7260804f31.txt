<div><b>Consumer Products and Retail – Cloud Marketing Technology for Commerce – Manager</b></div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
Are you an innovative thinker and marketing technologist with an expertise in helping Consumer Product &amp; Retail organizations on the future of their marketing and advertising? Do you use leading industry practices in the Sales and Service process areas for B2B and B2C business scenarios, enabled by cloud solutions like SAP Hybris, Adobe and Anaplan?</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
The Consumer industry is focused on serving the changing consumer, who has rapidly evolving needs, behaviors and preferences for products, services, and experiences. Our Consumer Products and Retail sector is undergoing a transformation fueled by an empowered consumer, technology advancements, market fragmentation and blurring lines across the industry. Organizations are racing to compete for the consumer’s attention and win share of wallet, which is creating unique challenges to innovate, modernize technology and transform business models with the winners ultimately seeing this as a consumer-led renaissance.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div><b>
The Team</b></div>
<div>
</div>
<p>Our Advertising, Marketing &amp; Commerce (AM&amp;C) team focuses on delivering marketing and growth objectives aligned with our clients’ brand values for measurable business growth. We do this by creating content, communications, and experiences that engage and inspire their customers to act. We implement and operate the technology platforms that enable personalized content, commerce and marketing user-centric experiences. In doing so, we transform our clients’ marketing and engagement operations into modern, data-driven, creatively focused organizations. Our team brings deep experience in creative and digital marketing capabilities, many from our Digital Studios.</p>
<div></div>
<br>
<div><b>
Work You’ll Do</b></div>
<div>
<div>
As a Manager, you will lead and deliver small or components of large, complex client engagements that identify, design, and implement creative business and technology solutions for large companies.</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
Your responsibilities will include providing service excellence by identifying key client business issues, determining client needs by supplementing the standard assessment techniques and tools with innovative approaches, evaluating and validating analysis and developing recommendations for the client in the context of the overall engagement.</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
You will be expected to implement and oversee the quality of deliverables and effectively manage the team and day-to-day relationships to ensure exceptional performance. Managers participate in the development and presentation of proposals for business development activities.</div>

Qualifications</b></div>
<div><b>
Required:</b></div>
<div>

5+ years of experience working in Consumer Products space (wholesale or distribution companies or with a firm who sells and distributes goods directly to consumers) and/or Retail industry (general merchandisers, specialty apparel or footwear, large food or drug stores, big-box stores, etc.) <b>This industry experience is a must.</b>
</div></div></li>

5+ years relevant consulting experience.</div></li>
<li><div>
Strong project management and delivery experience, including budget oversight and staffing of project teams.</div></li>
