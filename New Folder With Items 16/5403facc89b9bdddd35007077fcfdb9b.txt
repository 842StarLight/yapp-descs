<h2>Teller</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p><b>Job Title:</b> Teller<br>
<b>Reports to:</b> Branch Manager<br>
<b>FLSA Status:</b> Non-exempt<br>
<b>Salary Grade:</b> NE07<br>
<b>Supervisory Responsibility:</b> No</p>
<p><b>SUMMARY:</b></p>
<p>Provides above standard customer service. Accurately and efficiently completes customer transactions. Responsible for following and adhering to all bank operational and security policies and procedures including compliance and privacy policies and procedures to maintain customer confidentiality.</p>
<p><b>ESSENTIAL DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES </b>include the following. Other duties may be assigned by supervisor and/or Trustco Bank?s Board of Directors.</p>
<ul>
<li>Accepts deposits, verifying cash and endorsements and issue receipts.</li>
<li>Cashes checks.</li>
<li>Cross-sells the Bank?s products and services.</li>
<li>Process credit card payments.</li>
<li>Issues money orders and bank checks.</li>
<li>Accepts loan payments.</li>
<li>Process night deposit bags.</li>
<li>Admits customers to safe deposit boxes.</li>
<li>Answers customer inquiries and provides balances.</li>
<li>Balances transactions at end of the day and verifies cash totals.</li>
<li>Balances Automated Teller Machines.</li>
<li>Maintains reports and logs for record retention.</li>
<li>Continues to building proficiency of banking principles and sales techniques by attending internal training, external training, and completion of continuing education courses.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
