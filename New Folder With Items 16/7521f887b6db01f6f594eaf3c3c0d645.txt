<p><b>Job Description - Full Charge Bookkeeper</b></p>
<p>Responsibilities include the followings:</p>
<p>Payroll and payroll tax experience required.Accounting software (QuickBooks Enterprise knowledge, accounts payable,accounts receivable, income tax experience a plus. Must be able to communicatewith clients effectively.</p>
<p>Create financial transactions and generaterelevant reports by being detail-oriented, thorough, and organized. Collaborateand communicate with vendors, and customers to ensure accuracy of financialtransactions.</p>
<p>Details to the responsibilities: Full-cyclebookkeeping, general ledger accounting, processing accounting payable andreceivables, payroll, and bank reconciliations</p>
<p>· Perform general bookkeeping duties (Keep records of purchase,sales, payment receipts, bill payment, invoicing and, day to day financialtransactions)</p>
<p>· Obtain necessary tax forms from new vendors.</p>
<p>· Process bi-weekly payroll</p>
<p>· Preparation of Sales Tax Returns</p>
<p>· Perform reconciliations of credit card and bank accounts on amonthly basis to ensure accuracy.</p>
<p>· Manage account receivable and distribute customer invoices andensure that receivables are collected in a timely manner.</p>
<p>· Processes and generate 1099’s.</p>
<p>· Maintain internal control systems.</p>
<p>· Monitor and maintain office supplies</p>
<p>· Comply with relevant reporting requirements.</p>
<p>· Collaborate/Coordinate with QC/QA teams and Inventory ControlSupervisor</p>
<p>· Maintain complete filing system to support financial records.</p>
<p>· Maintain an organized and streamlined accounting system.</p>
<p>· Administrative work may be assigned/ i.e: Client reception andanswering phones</p>

<p>· Strong work ethic and professional appearance and manner</p>

<p>· Fluent in accounting and comfortable with QuickBooks Enterprise</p>
<p>· Responsible and professional in appearance and demeanor</p>
<p>· A self-starter, motivated to maintain a productive office</p>
