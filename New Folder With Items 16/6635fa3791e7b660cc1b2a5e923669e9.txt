<div><b>The Role</b></div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div>This position will develop and execute commercial mortgage loan origination and production strategies and business plans for his/her assigned area(s) of responsibility to optimize net investment income for Lincoln Financial Group (LFG). S/he will consistently achieve or exceed individual and team annual commercial mortgage loan origination targets.<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<div><b>Responsibilities</b></div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div><b>Commercial Mortgage Loan and Real Estate Investments</b></div>
<br>
<div></div>
<ul>
<li>Contributes to the overall strategic vision for the Commercial Mortgage Loan and Real Estate functional area(s) by developing and executing commercial mortgage loan origination and production strategies/business plans for his/her assigned area(s) of responsibility and territory.
</li>
<li>Ensures his/her assigned area(s) of responsibility’s strategic plans and programs are aligned with the divisions and Lincoln Financial Group’s (LFG) overall business plans and specific strategic and tactical initiatives.</li>
<li>Ensures that his/her assigned area(s) of responsibility are meeting all objectives pertaining to quality, compliance, regulations, productivity, budget and project management.
</li>
<li>Consistently achieves or exceeds individual and team annual commercial mortgage loan origination targets including, but not limited to:
<ul>
<li>identifying business opportunities, enhancing and managing existing client base (e.g. mortgage bankers, real estate developers and borrowers), and developing new client relationships;
</li>
<li>analyzing and evaluating commercial mortgage loan and real estate investment opportunities;</li>
<li>ensuring transactions are screened to fit underwriting standards and pricing;
</li>
<li>making recommendations; prudently investing corporate funds to achieve the best possible risk/reward within established investment guidelines.</li>
<li>negotiating commercial loan origination transactions with applicable parties;</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>Supports and enhances commercial mortgage loan organization by managing and leading team members to consistently achieve commercial mortgage loan origination targets and profitability, including but not limited to:
<ul>
<li>mentoring team members and providing feedback intended to enhance individual team member performance;</li>
<li>sharing technical expertise and market experience;</li>
<li>providing support and guidance to team members through complex business/credit issues;</li>
<li>collaborating with manager to ensure team member performance expectations are achieved.</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>Manages the commercial mortgage loan portfolio for his/her area(s) of responsibility to maximize investment income while simultaneously minimizing risk, including:
<ul>
<li>Collaborating with portfolio and asset management team to proactively assess existing holdings.</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>Contributes to the achievement of department commercial mortgage loan origination targets and objectives by providing support to other assigned territories or accounts, as needed.</li>
<li>Builds, maintains, and leverages key relationships with external business partners, including correspondent mortgage bankers, real estate developers, borrowers, and external legal teams.
</li>
<li>Partners with closing and legal team to ensure an efficient, customer focused closing experience and reviews, and if necessary resolves, business related closing matters.
</li>
<li>Monitors economic and market trends, peer trends and activities for his/her area(s) of responsibility to assess competitive opportunities in the marketplace.
</li>
<li>Analyzes benchmarks, metrics and performance monitoring systems to track new commercial mortgage loan origination strategy and business plan strategy/plan progress, effectiveness, return on investment and bottom-line impact. Recommends mitigation strategies.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Strategy
</b></p>
<ul><li>Develops and implements for his/her area(s) of responsibility effective commercial mortgage loan marketing and messaging consistent with broader strategies/business plans which optimize Lincoln’s return on investment and gain market share.
</li></ul>
<p><b>Collaboration</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Provides commercial mortgage loan and real estate investment expertise and partners with product line and other key internal stakeholders to determine investment platforms offered to clients/customers.</li>
<li>Provides commercial mortgage loan and real estate investment expertise to stakeholders to facilitate an in-depth understanding of investment products, performance and risks.
</li>
<li>Maintains knowledge on current and emerging developments/trends for assigned area(s) of responsibility, assess the impact, and collaborates with executive/senior management to incorporate new trends and developments in current and future strategies.</li>
<li>Manages the collaboration and effective leveraging of key internal stakeholders (e.g. Investment Management organization, Corporate Finance, Compliance, Internal Audit and Law) to accurately and timely complete commercial mortgage transactions/processes and optimize net investment income for the enterprise</li>
<br>
</ul>

<p><b>Requirements</b></p>
<p><b>Education</b></p>
<ul>
<li>4 Year/Bachelors Degree or equivalent work experience (4 years of experience in lieu of Bachelors)</li>
<li>MBA preferred</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Experience</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Ten plus years of experience in commercial real estate finance, national platform lending, or asset management.
</li>
<li>Three plus years of management, supervisory and/or demonstrated leadership experience including influencing senior management and critical stakeholder responsibility.</li>
<li>Advanced knowledge of commercial real estate principles and fundamentals.</li>
<li>Advanced knowledge of national commercial real estate and mortgage markets and macro-economic trends that affect those markets.</li>
<li>Advanced ability to understand and evaluate real estate financial statements.</li>
<li>Advanced knowledge and understanding of commercial real estate valuation.</li>
<li>Expert knowledge of real estate legal concepts and understanding of loan documents.</li>
<li>Advanced ability to understand and apply financial concepts such as but not limited to Future Value, Present Value, Compound Interest, Discounting, Capitalization Rates, and Internal Rate of Return (IRR).</li>
<li>Proficiency with Microsoft Office Suite (Word, Excel, PowerPoint, Outlook).</li>
<li>Strong interpersonal skills and the ability to lead/manage in a matrix influenced organization.</li>
<li>A demonstrated track record of consistently meeting and/or exceeding performance expectations</li>
<li>Possesses a bias for execution and avoids workplace distractions.</li>
<li>Drives performance targets to completion.</li>
</ul>
