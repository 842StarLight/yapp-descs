<h2>leasing consultant</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>CFH Group has an immediate opening for a part-time leasing consultant to work in the North Lauderdale, FL area. This candidate must have at least 2 years of experience, have general computer skills, (Yardi knowledgeable is a plus) possess excellent communication and people skills, and maintain a professional appearance and demeanor on-site. The leasing consultant provides a high level of customer service and assists the property manager in efforts to help maximize occupancy. Some of the job responsibilities include but are not limited to:
</p>
<ul>
<li>Understand the properties rental rates, special promotions and availability and be well informed of existing market conditions and competing rental properties nearby</li>
<li>Lease vacant units by qualifying prospective residents in accordance with company standards and Fair Housing guidelines</li>
<li>Oversee ongoing resident and lease related issues including coordinating with maintenance staff on work orders, move-in and move-out inspections</li>
<li>Handle rental income and issue late fee/eviction notices as needed</li>
<li>Inspect all pre-leased apartments prior to scheduled move-ins and show vacant apartments to prospective residents
</li>
<li>Record all traffic (ex. internet leads, walk-ins, or phone calls) and enter into Yardi (our accounting software) on a daily basis
</li>
<li>Outreach marketing to local businesses near the area</li>
</ul>
<p>
Wage is based on experience. It is a full time hourly rate with benefits including health and dental insurance, 401 K, vacation, personal and sick time.</p>
<p>
Local candidates only. No phone calls please. No Staffing Agencies.</p>
<p>
Please email resumes to: hr@cfhgroup.com</p>
</div></div>
