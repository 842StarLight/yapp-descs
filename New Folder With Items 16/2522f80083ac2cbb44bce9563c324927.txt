<h2>Leasing Agent</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Athens Real Estate Group is a full service real estate firm handling residential and commercial sales, as well as property and community association management services. We are a team made up of University of Georgia Alumni and we love our Georgia Bulldogs and the Classic City we live in!</p>
<p>We are seeking a Leasing Agent to join our team. The Leasing Agent will play a key role in maintaining optimum levels of occupancy for the 500 plus homes we manage in the Athens area. Excellent organizational and communication skills are important qualifications for this position, as well as good customer relations and the ability to multi-task. The ideal candidate is a high energy, go-getter who takes responsibility and has a positive attitude.</p>
<p><b>Responsibilities Include: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Responsible for touring and leasing all properties under management</li>

<li>Coordinate and process lease renewals in order to increase resident retention</li>
<li>Process all lease amendments according to company policy including but not limited to: tenant swaps and early termination requests.</li>
<li>Update all advertising daily and set up marketing and outreach programs</li>
<li>Organize all applications, lease agreements, guarantors, and data entry processes</li>
<li>Participate in all turn related activities</li>
</ul>
