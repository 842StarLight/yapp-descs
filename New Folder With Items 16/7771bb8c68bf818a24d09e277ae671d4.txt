<h2>Product Marketing Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>Consumer Information Services (CIS) offers a comprehensive portfolio of big data and advanced analytics products and services that help lenders in their marketing, underwriting and account management activities. CIS is continuously unleashing innovative new product offerings and enhancing existing products to drive client business outcomes.<br>
<br>
The Product Marketing team helps Experian capture maximum value by more effectively connecting customer needs to product capabilities and driving deep understanding through simple, streamlined sales and marketing enablement tools and content.<br>
<br>
As Product Marketing Manager, you are part of the Product Marketing team and are assigned to a set of products. As a partner to the Product Managers for your products, you become the subject matter expert on the market, the buyer journey, the sales experience, and the challenges our clients and their customers face. You define our go-to-market strategy for new products and key enhancements, position offerings effectively, and deliver compelling and differentiated messages in multiple formats across multiple channels so they resonate with the market. You will help stakeholders across the organization understand our products and their benefits.<br>
<br>
The ideal candidate is someone with a product marketing background and experience working for a B2B enterprise software vendor or financial institution. The individual must have strong communication and organization skills, the ability to work cross-functionally and leverage teams across the business to implement the strategy.<br>
<br>
<b>Responsibilities include:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Client/ market research – research and understand the market, buyers, needs, and competitors.</li>
<li>Go-to-market plans and strategies – build go-to-market plans for new products and enhancements</li>
<li>Value proposition and positioning – deliver product messaging that differentiates our products and resonates with targeted market segments and across channels.</li>
<li>Sales enablement and product marketing content – design, develop and manage internal/external product content (e.g., pitch decks, case studies, videos, product sheets, etc.) and other resources such as sales presentations, product sheets, videos, training tools, website content, white papers, objection handling, FAQs and case studies</li>
<li>Data insights and analytic skills—leverage data to make informed decisions as well as track, analyze and optimize marketing performance.</li>
</ul>

</div>

<b>Knowledge, Experience &amp; Qualifications</b><br>
<ul>
<li>BS/BA and related work experience required, MBA a plus (or equivalent experience)</li>
<li>3+ years’ experience in product marketing and 5+ in a related product or marketing role</li>
<li>Demonstrable experience in end to end product and value proposition development, content creation, and go to market execution</li>
<li>General experience with digital marketing strategies, channels and best practices.</li>
<li>Experience with applying analytics to support data-driven product marketing decisions.</li>
<li>Naturally curious and a ‘continuous learner’ with excellent critical thinking, analytical, and problem-solving skills.</li>
<li>Ability to work at the strategic level (e.g. generating innovative ideas and new use cases for growth) and at the tactical level (e.g. product readiness and sales enablement).</li>
<li>Effective in collaboration and cross-functional coordination</li>
<li>Strong verbal and in-person communication skills; excellent written communication skills - clear, concise and streamlined</li>
<li>Highly proficiency computer skills and adept at evaluating, learning and adapting to new systems and tools.</li>
</ul>
