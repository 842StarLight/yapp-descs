<h2>Sales Representative</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>The Sales Representative identifies sales opportunities for the BioPharm International brand, print advertising, websites and/or e-media platforms, custom projects, etc.; generates revenue and closes sales to all levels of customers. Builds and maintains customer relationships and records all interactions in Salesforce.com. Distributes market information for current/potential advertisers, contributes to target research efforts and prepares reports as requested.</p>
<p>
</p>
<p>Responsibilities:</p>
<ul>
<li>Contact customers &amp; prospects, prepare presentations, and close sales opportunities</li>
<li>Identify and understand customer marketing goals and objectives</li>
<li>Develop lists of new prospects and existing accounts for contact and/or follow-up purposes</li>
<li>Research customer/client needs and create integrated advertising/marketing solutions that meet their objectives</li>
<li>Maintain strong industry credibility through visible attendance at trade shows and other product presentations, staying up-to-date on new developments and technologies and communicating this information to both key existing accounts and potential new accounts</li>
<li>Generate direct sales from cold calling, follow-up calling and face-to-face meetings</li>
<li>Utilize Salesforce.com for all client contact and scheduling and opportunities for pipeline management</li>
</ul>
