<h2>Accounts Payable Coordinator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>The Accounts Payable Coordinator is primarily responsible for managing the accounts payable, fixed asset and sales and use tax processes. This position will also be responsible for assisting in the payroll processes. In addition, to the already mentioned responsibilities this role will collect certain financial information that is required to be submitted to corporate on a monthly basis in the time frame required.</p>
Essential Responsibilities:
<ul>
<li>Manage accounts payable process (i.e., review open commitments, vendor contact, voucher, and payment)</li>
<li>Submits appropriate paperwork for monthly sales and use tax returns to various states.</li>
<li>Perform various month end reconciliations and work to resolve any discrepancies.</li>
<li>Maintain CAR and fixed asset reporting.</li>
<li>Review AP accounts for accuracy.</li>
<li>Record journal entries into database and ensure supporting documentation is attached.</li>
<li>Perform and manage monthly closing and assist with year-end closing.</li>
<li>Assist with the organization of the quarterly balance sheet reviews.</li>
<li>Administration of vendor and customer records in database.</li>
<li>Back up the management and reporting of all cash activities on a daily and monthly basis.</li>
</ul>Job Requirements / Skills, Knowledge and Abilities:

<li>Highly proficient in MS Office software including Word, Excel, PowerPoint.</li>
<li>Strong interpersonal and communication skills, by written, oral, and electronic means.</li>
<li>Ability to handle discreet and confidential information.</li>
<li>Meticulous attention to detail</li>
<li>Ability to organize wide array of material and data</li>
</ul>
