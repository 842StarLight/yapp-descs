<h2>Design Engineer (Media & Entertainment / Broadcast) - Atlanta, GA</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">We are seeking an Atlanta based Design Engineer to design Audio Visual Broadcast Systems to join our Media and Entertainment group. This person may reside anywhere in the Southeastern US.
<br><br>
What part will you play?
<br>The Design Engineer will be the single point of responsibility for definition, design and documentation of assigned projects. You will take customer requirements and design a system that meets those requirements. It could range from a simple automated playout to an entire newsroom studio and production control room. Design Engineers should be highly motivated, eager to learn new technologies and able to react to rapidly changing conditions while maintaining professionalism, possess strong written and verbal communication skills to build strong and trusting customer relationships.
<br><br>
What will you be doing?
<br><ul>
<li>Manages own time to perform within defined schedule</li>
<li>Interfaces with assigned sales staff in developing solutions</li>
<li>Interfaces with clients in developing solutions</li>
<li>Interfaces with all Tech-Ops and Engineering staff in developing site-specific solutions</li>
<li>Maintains awareness of emerging solutions and technologies</li>
<li>Maintains a working knowledge of emerging trends in the industry</li>
<li>Provides solutions within the customer’s budget without sacrificing quality and reliability</li>
<li>Develops detailed and accurate Bill of Materials (BOM) as part of system design</li>
<li>Develops conceptual / functional broadcast system documentation</li>
<li>Ensure Job Summary or Scope of work is complete and accurate</li>
<li>Develop detailed and accurate engineering drawings for rack layout, system wiring and custom plates or cabling assemblies</li>
<li>Prepares submittals for consultant-specified and bid jobs</li>
<li>Assists technicians with field questions and clarifications</li>
<li>Provides testing, commissioning and training services as required for customers and end users</li>
<li>Performs other tasks as requested</li>
</ul>
What do we require from you?
<br><ul>
<li>College degree or requisite experience in Audio Visual / Broadcast system integration design</li>
<li>Proven experience designing and developing broadcast solutions required</li>
<li>Must be familiar with broadcast vendor solutions</li>
<li>A thorough, demonstrable understanding of systems integration techniques, as well as creative problem-solving capability</li>
<li>Skilled in the use of common MS Office applications – Excel, Word, PowerPoint, Outlook and Visio</li>
<li>Working knowledge of AutoCAD software required</li>
<li>IT Network Experience</li>
</ul>
