<h2>Specifications Engineer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Job Description:
</b></h2>
<p></p>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"></h3>
<p><b>Pentair is currently seeking an upwardly-mobile Specifications Engineer to join our Flow Technologies business and ensure that our pump products are designed to meet our clients’ needs in an efficient and effective manner. This person will be expected to analyze &amp; interpret a wide variety of product orders in order to prepare basic submittals, Bills of Material [BOM’s], and general work orders. This person will work on complex, non-standard quoted orders which require extensive analysis. This role is based out of our facility in Kansas City, KS.
</b></p>
<p></p>
<p><b>We will:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li><b>Create career growth and rotational opportunities within various areas of the organization;
</b></li>
<li><b>Pay competitively;
</b></li>
<li><b>Provide on the job training and mentoring in Pentair’s proven best practices;
</b></li>
<li><b>Offer health benefits &amp; coaching;
</b></li>
<li><b>Support your long term retirement goals through a competitive 401K matching and stock purchase plan;
</b></li>
<li><b>Offer employee discounts;
</b></li>
<li><b>Protect you with life insurance and other options;
</b></li>
<li><b>Tell you more about all the great benefits – just ask!
</b></li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>You will:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li><b>Prepare requests for technical data to be supplied by Product Engineering (such as performance curves, special part drawings, bearing life, etc.).
</b></li>
<li><b>Receive vendor submittal and reviews for compliance.
</b></li>
<li><b>Collaborate cross-functionally with Application Engineers, Contract Administrators, Product Engineers, Order Entry Representatives, and Buyers to facilitate the processing of orders.
</b></li>
<li><b>Routinely analyze job specifications for compatibility with the order and factory quote: this involves comparing the order with specifications; listing discrepancies for clarification; determining what is needed for submittal; and requesting relevant information (when it is not readily available).
</b></li>
<li><b>Prepare submittal package in accordance with specification requests and order using submittal templates; this involves reviewing setting plans, assembly drawings, technical data, and material list for compliance with the order as well as preparing a list of clarifications &amp; exceptions to be included.
</b></li>
<li><b>Process work orders, parts lists, and routings to comply with the order; if necessary, request special parts from Engineering and prepares pre-releases, as required.
</b></li>
<li><b>Contribute to preparation of complex as-built Operation and Maintenance [O&amp;M] manuals.
</b></li>
<li><b>Maintain familiarity with standard and non-standard construction of each product line as listed and specified in product catalogs &amp; price pages.
</b></li>
<li><b>Check orders prepared by other associates, as required.
</b></li>
<li><b>Assist in maintenance of master submittal templates.
</b></li>
<li><b>Provide assistance to resolve shop/manufacturing issues.
</b></li>
<li><b>Perform additional duties, as assigned.
</b></li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>You should:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li><b>Have earned at least a Bachelor's Degree in an Engineering-oriented field of study (e.g. BSME).
</b></li>
<li><b>Have at least 1 year of professional experience in Engineering, Customer Service, Sales, and/or Marketing.
</b></li>
<li>
<b>Demonstrate excellent communication skills in English (written &amp; verbal </b><b>).
</b>
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>We like, but don’t require:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li><b>Completion of coursework in Mechanical Engineering.
</b></li>
<li><b>Experience working with rotating equipment, especially industrial pumps and/or hydraulic systems; exposure to Fire, Centrifugal, Reciprocating, or Submersible Wastewater pumps is a plus.
</b></li>
<li><b>Understanding of manufacturing operations in an Engineered-to-Order [ETO] product environment, e.g. Design for Manufacturing [DFM], application, testing, etc.
</b></li>
<li><b>Above-average mechanical and mathematical aptitude.
</b></li>
<li><b>Proven ability to interpret blue prints &amp; drawings; experience with reading technical plans &amp; specifications is a plus.
</b></li>
<li><b>Strong documentation and analytical skills.
</b></li>
<li><b>Proven experience demonstrating interpersonal skills, team player mentality, and a self-motivated work ethic.
</b></li>
<li><b>Excellent organizational skills; experience with project management, multi-tasking, and/or meeting multiple deadlines is a plus.
</b></li>
<li><b>Exposure to Process Improvement methodologies (e.g. Lean Manufacturing, Kaizen, etc.) a plus.
</b></li>
<li><b>Advanced ability to use basic software tools (e.g. MS Office); exposure to AutoCAD, SolidWorks, and/or SAP is a plus.
</b></li>
</ul>
