<h2>FRONT END DEPARTMENT</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">In our Front End Department, your potential to thrive is at your fingertips. As the face of the Company and whether ringing up orders, problem solving or bagging groceries you have an opportunity to interact and leave a lasting impression with our valued customers. The possibilities are endless if you have a passion for customer service.
<br><br>
<b>Checker/Food Clerk:</b><br>
As a Checker, your primary responsibility will include operating a cash register to accurately ring up our customers' grocery orders. You will be responsible for tendering multiple forms of payment while ensuring our customers experience a friendly, efficient checkout process. You will interact with a high-volume of customers in a fast-paced environment ensuring that all of their grocery shopping needs were met. More details are available in store. We are an equal opportunity employer.
<br><br>
<b>Courtesy Clerk :</b><br>
As a Courtesy Clerk, some of your duties will include bagging groceries, providing carryout service, checking prices, and gathering shopping carts from the parking lot. You will ensure that our customers have a clean store to shop in by cleaning restrooms and cleaning up spills. Your job will be to provide our customers with exceptional customer service. More details are available in store. We are an equal opportunity employer.
<br><br>
<b>Customer Service Clerks :</b><br>
As a Customer Service Clerk, some of your duties will include the sale of money orders, money-wire transfers, lottery tickets, and other services to customers. You will assist customers with product refunds and questions and answer the store's main telephone line. Your job will be to provide customers with exceptional customer service and resolve problems effectively. More details are available in store. We are an equal opportunity employer.
<br><br>
<b>Bookkeeper/Personnel Coordinator Bookkeeper :</b><br>
As a Bookkeeper, some of your duties will include balancing cash registers tills accounting for cash, checks, and other forms of payment. You will utilize computer bookkeeping software to count and verify funds, payments, and other items such as lottery tickets. You will assist at the Customer Service Desk as needed. You will assist customers with questions and refunds and any necessary problem resolution. You will assist store management with processing employee payroll and file maintenance. Your job will be to provide the customer with exceptional customer service. More details are available in store. We are an equal opportunity employer.</div>
