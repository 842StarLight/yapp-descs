<b>Qualifications:</b><br>
<ul>

<li>Freedom from illegal use of drugs.</li>

<b>Essential Functions and Responsibilities:</b><br>
<b>Care and Services:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Achieve facility census goals and annual budget projections.</li>
<li>Act as a liaison between the facility and the outside community.</li>
<li>Develops strategies to maximize admissions of patients requiring a sub acute level of care including daily contact with potential referral sources including hospitals, physicians, insurers, case management companies and health care agencies.</li>
<li>Conduct admission screening of potential patients; determine level of care, services required, equipment needs and insurance coverage, as indicated.</li>
<li>Authorize acceptance of patients and coordinate admissions with appropriate staff.</li>
<li>Assist in marketing services to the managed care industry. Participate in contract closings and price negotiations with insurance companies, as assigned.</li>
<li>Work with local community agencies to generate a positive image and encourage word-of-mouth referral activities.</li>
<li>Participate in corporate trade shows and marketing activities, as assigned.</li>
<li>Maintain knowledge of current regulations governing Medicare/Medicaid and private insurance company reimbursement.</li>
<li>Access continuing education opportunities appropriate to discipline and responsibilities.</li>
<li>Ensures timely delivery of census report to corporate office and essential services.</li>
<li>Serve as a resource person for patient care planning activities.</li>
<li>Supports program development activities.</li>
<li>Attends and participates in departmental/facility meetings, as required.</li>
</ul>
