<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilties</b></h2>
<b>
Job Description</b><br>
<p>At Wells Fargo, we want to satisfy our customers’ financial needs and help them succeed financially. We’re looking for talented people who will put our customers at the center of everything we do. Join our diverse and inclusive team where you’ll feel valued and inspired to contribute your unique skills and experience.</p>
<p>
Help us build a better Wells Fargo. It all begins with outstanding talent. It all begins with you.</p>
<p><b>
Wells Fargo Technology</b> sets IT strategy; enhances the design, development, and operations of our systems; optimizes the Wells Fargo infrastructure footprint; provides information security; and enables continuous banking access through in-store, online, ATM, and other channels to Wells Fargo’s more than 70 million global customers.</p>
<p>
The position is for a software developer in the Equity Derivatives Technology group to work on the buildout of the strategic Equity Derivatives platform for Trade Capture, Processing, and Lifecycle Management to support the strategic business initiatives.</p>
<p>
Requires strong technical knowledge of Java and a solid foundation of server design methodologies. Must be able to write highly functional and performant code in a structured manner that can be applied to domains beyond equity derivatives. Successful applicant will have experience developing &amp; supporting full software development lifecycle and ideally using cutting edge technologies (e.g. – microservices, containers, cloud, distributed solutions, and monitoring).</p>
<p>
Candidates applying for this position should be prepared to work in this software environment with the following expectations:</p>
<ul>
<li>Enhance, build and deliver application frameworks and specific functionality around trading and risk</li>
<li>Perform in the high level technical and analytics role as an individual contributor developing software for the various trading components</li>
<li>Work directly with business analysts, traders, sales, operations group to understand requirements</li>
<li>Read and understand business specifications and create functional specifications from them</li>
<li>Create thorough designs - taking care to assure that designs integrate well into existing architecture</li>
<li>Write high quality code in the Java language using object oriented principals and design patterns as appropriate</li>
<li>Create/modify SQL Server database objects (tables, views, stored procedures, etc.)</li>
<li>Perform high quality thorough unit testing and documentation of development activities</li>
<li>Work on complex problems where analysis of situations and/or data requires a solid grasp of business/risk domains</li>
<li>Assure quality, maintainability, and extensibility for supported systems and risk applications</li>
<li>Provide Level 2 production support as required</li>
</ul>
<p>
Required</p>
<ul>
<li>5+ years in Core Java development with expert understanding of memory management, concurrency, JNI, native I/O</li>
<li>5+ years of working with computing Algorithms and Data Structures</li>
<li>3+ years of designing and implementing distributed systems paradigms</li>
<li>1+ years of working with in-memory cache architectures (Oracle Coherence, Gemfire etc.)</li>
<li>4+ years of Object Oriented experience</li>
<li>3+ years of SQL experience</li>
<li>3+ years of securities industry experience with understanding of Financial instrument valuation, life cycle and risk management especially pertaining to Equity Derivatives</li>
<li>Familiar with trading concepts as they relate to flow trading, synthetics, exotics &amp; convertibles and technology used to solve problems for these business lines</li>
<li>Able to build components across all functional domains : trade capture, lifecycle mgmt, risk, pricing</li>
<li>Outstanding communication skills as required for a front office role</li>
<li>A BS/BA degree or higher</li>
</ul>
<p>
Desired</p>
<ul>
<li>Advanced experience in capital markets business and processes</li>
<li>5+ years of experience developing multi-tiered, low latency, highly reliable, applications</li>
<li>Basic knowledge and understanding of mathematical modeling</li>
<li>Experience with Equity Swap Trade Entry and Lifecycle management</li>
<li>Knowledge and understanding of risk management or quantitative modeling</li>
<li>Firm understanding of Design Patterns</li>
<li>Basic knowledge and understanding of SEC, FNRA, and international regulations for building technological solutions</li>
<li>Knowledge and understanding of derivative products risk and valuation methodologies</li>
<li>Knowledge and understanding of technology platform development including large scale technology in a capital markets environment</li>
<li>Knowledge and understanding of technology object oriented design patterns and their application</li>
<li>Knowledge and understanding of application lifecycle, from design and development to testing, implementation and production support</li>
<li>Knowledge and understanding of technology architecture distributed and client server technologies</li>
<li>Autosys experience</li>
</ul>
