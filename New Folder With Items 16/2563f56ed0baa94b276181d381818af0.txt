<h2>Associate Corporate Counsel</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p><b>Hi, We’re AppFolio.</b></p>
<p>
We’re innovators, changemakers, and collaborators. We’re more than just a software company — we’re a cloud-based powerhouse that creates products to make our customers’ lives easier. We’re revolutionizing the way people do business, and we want your ideas, your enthusiasm, and your passion to help us keep on innovating.</p>
<p><b>
What You'll Do</b></p>
<p>
Assist Corporate Counsel and key business constituencies in achieving viable and sustained success by providing timely, thoughtful legal and regulatory advice and related practical solutions.</p>
<p><b>
What we’re looking for</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Leverage real estate industry knowledge and experience to develop systems and reporting processes to identify and address legal and regulatory developments that impact our AppFolio Property Manager customers.</li>
<li>
Ensure that the business and legal and compliance team are well informed and understand the real world impact of such legal and regulatory developments on our customers.</li>
<li>
Keep up-to-date with MyCase product development and business initiatives, identify key legal and regulatory issues within the vertical and provide recommended solutions in partnership with Corporate Counsel.</li>
<li>
Take responsibility for MyCase terms of service and other customer-facing agreements as the business expands and evolves.</li>
<li>
Identify legal training opportunities for various departments and employees supporting the MyCase vertical and work with Corporate Counsel to develop such trainings.</li>
<li>
Ensure that the MyCase sales and customer service teams receive timely and reliable advice in response to questions from prospects and clients.</li>
<li>
Partner with AppFolio Investment Management product and engineering teams in growing and supporting the technology by identifying and establishing relationships with third parties and other AppFolio constituencies (APM, MyCase).</li>
<li>
Identify key legal and regulatory issues that apply to or otherwise affect AppFolio Investment Management and provide recommended solutions in partnership with Corporate Counsel.</li>
<li>
Identify legal training opportunities for various departments and employees supporting AppFolio Investment Management and work with Corporate Counsel to develop such trainings.</li>
<li>
Ensure that AppFolio Investment Management sales and customer services teams receive timely and reliable advice in response to questions from prospects and clients.</li>
<li>
Take responsibility for AppFolio Investment Management terms of service and other customer-facing agreements as the business expands and evolves.</li>
<li>
Support the procurement team by reviewing and drafting large third party agreements.</li>
<li>
Provide coaching and trainings to the procurement team to enable them to review and handle small to medium sized procurement issues on their own.</li>
<li>
Assist Corporate Paralegal and Procurement Team in identifying a document management and retention system that will be implemented over the course of the fiscal year.</li>
<li>
Review existing vendor agreements and, if necessary, amend to address California Consumer Privacy Act concerns.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Must-Haves</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Minimum of six years of sophisticated legal experience (a combination of law firm and in-house experience, especially in the SaaS industry, preferred).</li>
<li>
Excellent organizational skills and time management skills.</li>
<li>
Excellent verbal and written communication skills.</li>
<li>
Proactive and autonomous, able to set priorities, handle multiple matters contemporaneously and meet deadlines.</li>
<li>
Confident and able to interact with people at all levels of the organization.</li>
<li>
Dependable and trustworthy, detail oriented.</li>
<li>
A calm demeanor, even under pressure.</li>
<li>
Must be flexible, creative and able to work in a fast-paced and demanding environment.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Our Story</b></p>
<p>
AppFolio (NASDAQ: APPF) was founded in 2006 with the mission to revolutionize vertical industry businesses by providing great software and service. Our easy-to-use, cloud-based software helps our customers more effectively market, manage, and grow their businesses. Today our software solutions exist in two verticals including the real estate (AppFolio Property Manager, AppFolio Property Manager PLUS, and AppFolio Investment Manager) and legal (MyCase) industries.</p>
