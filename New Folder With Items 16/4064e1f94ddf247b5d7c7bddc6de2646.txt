<p>OVERVIEW</p>
<p>
PDI helps convenience store retailers and petroleum wholesale marketers worldwide thrive in a digital economy with enterprise management software. Over 1,200 customers operating more than 100,000 locations trust PDI to optimize their operations, whether they are a single site, multi-site, dealer or a franchise operator. PDI's enterprise software, wholesale and logistics management software solutions and retail back office systems have been designed around the evolving needs of customers for more than 35 years. We reimagine enterprise management to help our customers transform their business and deliver exceptional experiences. Backed by the investment of TA Associates and Genstar, PDI is embarking upon an aggressive expansion strategy through internal investment in development as well as intensive M&amp;A activity. The result will be a broader, more powerful, international company that serves customers across the entire supply chain, from wholesalers, to retailers, and every stop in between.</p>
<p>
JOB SUMMARY</p>
<p>
PDI is seeking an experienced Marketing Specialist who will play a pivotal role in defining and executing the marketing and engagement plan for the Fuel Rewards Loyalty Program. We are looking for someone with experience project managing CRM marketing programs. This person can think both strategically in driving overall communication enhancements as well as can execute working team members on implementing and tracking performance of overall communication program. We are looking for someone passionate about customer engagement who wants to be part of taking our Fuel Rewards CRM program to the next level.</p>
<p>
RESPONSIBILITIES AND DUTIES</p>
<p>
Communication Program Management:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Oversees and implements Marketing Plan for the Fuel Rewards Loyalty Program including email, text, push, and personalized messaging</li>
<li>
Serves as Project Manager for Fuel Rewards CRM Campaigns - Manages timelines and deliverables of assets required for program execution</li>
<li>
Experience running a communication program from end to end (strategy, creative development, implementation, set up, QA, testing, performance tracking and optimization)</li>
<li>
Develops Marketing Plans for Fuel Rewards Partners, including outlining the goals and marketing plan (tactics, channels, messaging, cadence, user experience and on-going measurement)</li>
<li>
Develops and implements effective lifecycle, marketing, triggered and automated campaigns</li>
</ul>
<p>
Testing and Optimization:</p>
<ul><li>
Defines tests and optimizations needed in email, push, text messaging including creative, offer, subject line, landing page/user experience, segmentation and/or dynamic content used for personalization</li></ul>
<p>
Reporting &amp; Analytics</p>
<ul>
<li>
Review communication performance reporting including email metrics as well as deeper engagement (transactions, downloads, earning and redemption behavior)</li>
<li>
Ability to easily draw insights from the data to make recommendations on tests, optimizations and overall communication plan improvements</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
