<h2>Merchandising Assistant</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>General Overview:
</b></p>
<p>The Merchandising Assistant is an entry level position. The primary responsibility is to provide administrative, data entry and sample support to the buying team. Direction, training and guidance will be provided by the buyer of the department assigned.</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Principal Duties and Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Create and maintain all SKU set up information in Genesis and AS400 system.</li>
<li>
Assist with maintaining the line list for the department and E-Commerce line list.</li>
<li>
Sample management - tracking, unpacking and organizing new samples and purging discontinued samples.</li>
<li>
Complete and maintain all merchandising photos, presentation boards and product information sheets.</li>
<li>
Advertising, Marketing, Visual and ECOM support: generate content sheets, packaging, hangtags, assist with product turnover, signage for the stores and promotional entry into the data base. Assist buyer and planner with proofing of the advertising tabloids, emails and all marketing exposure and signs.</li>
<li>
Input and maintain all sku information, pricing and actuals in the ad comp sheet.</li>
<li>
Generate weekly reports for Monday sales reporting.</li>
<li>
Special projects as assigned may include assisting other departments or buyers.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Skill Set Required for the Position
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Demonstrated flexibility, ability to work independently on multiple projects in a fast-paced, resulted driven environment.</li>
<li>
Maintain and meet daily deadlines on multiple projects and tasks.</li>
<li>
Strong organizational, prioritizing, analytical, problem solving and time management skills.</li>
<li>
Detail-oriented with a high degree of accuracy and an ability to detect inconsistencies in data.</li>
<li>
 Basic understanding in Microsoft Office Suite tools including Excel, Word and PowerPoint.</li>
<li>
Excellent communication and interpersonal skills; ability to communicate with all levels of the organization and work in a team environment.</li>
<li>
Basic math skills.</li>
<li>
BA/BS degree preferred, but not required.</li>
<li>
Qualified applicants with criminal histories will be considered for employment consistent with the requirements of all applicable law.</li>
<li>
Light lifting.</li>
</ul>
