<div>
What You Will Be Doing</div>
<div><div>
<div>
<p>Wyndham Capital Mortgage (WCM) is looking for a Post-Closing Manager to lead a seasoned team!<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Manage daily production of personnel and ensure timeliness of deliveries and loan purchases by investor</li>
<li>Identify, research, and solve issues within the production environment. Serve as escalation contact for the department</li>
<li>Work closely with other departments to ensure production goals are met</li>
<li>Train, develop, and mentor new and existing employees in Post-Closing department</li>
<li>Constantly monitor processes to identify gaps and implement better efficiencies</li>
<li>Keep personnel updated on changes in policy and procedures</li>
<li>Compile reports for Senior Management staff for review, and implement any necessary changes</li>
<li>Provide stellar service for all customers – internal and external</li>
</ul>
</div>
</div></div>
<div>What We Need</div>
<div><div>
<div>
<ul>
<li>Strong background in Post-Closing/Closing with a minimum of 5 years of mortgage experience.</li>
<li>Must have management experience in the mortgage industry.</li>
<li>Must have a working knowledge of warehouse lines and investor sales.
</li>
<li>Conventional, FHA, and VA experience.</li>
<li>Great attention to detail.</li>
<li>Excellent written and oral communication skills mandatory.</li>
<li>Knowledge of Encompass software a plus.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>You are someone who…</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Strives to provide +1 Service to ensure success, to both internal teams and external clients</li>
<li>Has the upmost integrity, period.
</li>
<li>Is naturally curious - you enjoy seeking out and applying new things in your job</li>
<li>Knows how to make smart, informed decisions</li>
<li>Has passion for your job and positive energy that is infectious!
</li>
<li>Cares about your work and performance, and you want to provide impact across the organization</li>
<li>At times, can be innovative and not afraid to bring big thinking and ideas to the table
</li>
<li>Is able to deliver influential communication to others
</li>
<li>You take your work seriously, but not yourself. You like to have fun at work!</li>
</ul>
