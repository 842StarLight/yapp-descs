<h2>Public Notice and Advertising Coordinator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>The Rochester Business Journal and The Daily Record (Rochester) are seeking a Public Notice and Advertising Coordinator to join our dynamic team.
</p>
<p>In this role the candidate will serve as the point person to assist customers in placing public notice advertising in both the business and legal publications, paying close attention to deadlines, proofreading notices, confirming publication, processing cancellations and coordinating invoicing.
</p>
<p>The position also includes support services for the advertising and events team, including duties associated with promotions, sales reports, special projects, advertising contracts and event planning.
</p>
<p>On the accounting side, the candidate will be responsible for payment processing for advertising and public notice, approving billing reports, collecting on past due invoicing, process aging reports and work closely with the business manager and corporate contacts for all assignments related to accounting.
</p>
<p>Other duties for this position involve office management/production: answering direct mainline phone calls, monitoring office supplies, processing incoming mail, managing the press run for the Rochester Business Journal, and additional general tasks as assigned.</p>
<p>
We offer competitive compensation and a comprehensive benefits program.</p>
<p>
BridgeTower Media and all subsidiaries are Equal Opportunity Employers and value diversity in our workplace.</p>
</div>
<br>This position requires a minimum of 3 years related industry/office management and data entry experience. The ideal candidate will have MS Office profciency, be detail oriented, organized, excellent verbal and written communication skills and work independently. Attention to detail and excellent customer service is needed. Bachelor's degree preferred.</div>
