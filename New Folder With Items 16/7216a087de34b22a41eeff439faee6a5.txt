<h2>Delivery Driver</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>Atlas Sales is looking for a hardworking and focused team member who enjoys interacting with customers and ensuring accurate and friendly deliveries in our community. Working hours are at the driver’s discretion, Tuesday-Friday, approximately 6am-4pm.</div>
<div>
We are a fun team that works in one of the most enjoyable industries in the world: beverage distribution. If you think you have what it takes to join the Atlas crew</div>
<div>

Atlas Sales, Inc.<br>

<div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Qualifications:</b></h2>
<ul>
<li>Enjoys working with people.</li>
<li>High school diploma or equivalent.</li>
<li><b>Valid commercial driver license (CDL)</b></li>
<li>Ability to learn quickly and implement new procedures.</li>
<li>Capable of handling public relations situations competently and comfortably.</li>
<li>Qualified with regard to the Michigan Motor Carrier Safety Act.</li>
<li>Good driving record.</li>
<li>Follow all applicable provisions of current Union Agreement.</li>
</ul>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Personal Qualifications:</b></h2>
<ul>
<li>Pleasant and friendly</li>
<li>Energetic</li>
<li>Cooperative</li>
<li>Good health</li>
<li>Ability to work without direct supervision</li>
</ul>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Duties:</b></h2>
<p><i>A varied work environment, with duties that may include:</i></p>
<ul>
<li>Ensure that truck is loaded with needed stock and verify accuracy of load required before leaving building.</li>
<li>Deliver full containers to retail outlets and pick up empty containers.</li>
<li>Use provided mobile device to complete transactions and submit notes.</li>
<li>Assist in placement of temporary sign material and displays as requested.
<ul>
<li>Help build and maintain displays as requested.</li>
<li>Report any problems with current advertising or displays.</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>Collect payment; figure transactions.</li>
<li>Account for money, merchandise and returned empties when checking in.</li>
<li>Greet customers by name; maintain respect and good will of customer, employees and consumers.</li>
<li>Have complete knowledge of product, packages, prices, and policies.</li>
<li>Maintain neat, clean appearance and cheerful, respectful manner.</li>
<li>Be aware of and constantly practice good public relations — when driving and when servicing accounts.</li>
<li>Keep truck cab and assigned work spaces clean and organized at all times.</li>
<li>Work closely with salespeople in the pursuit of the mutual responsibilities of:
<ul>
<li>Selling brands and packages.</li>
<li>Securing good selling locations in accounts and coolers.</li>
<li>Acquiring new and existing draught knobs.</li>
<li>Stock product on shelves, on floor, and in coolers.</li>
<li>Rotate stock in stockroom, on floor, and on shelves.</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>Alert management to problems that could lead to overage beer.
<ul>
<li>Read code dates.</li>
<li>Know policy on code dates and overage beer.</li>
<li>Remove outdated product.</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>Other duties as may be assigned from time to time by the supervisor.</li>
</ul>
