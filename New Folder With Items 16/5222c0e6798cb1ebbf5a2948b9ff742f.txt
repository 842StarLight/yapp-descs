<h2>Senior Manager of Quality and Operational Excellence</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Company Overview:</b></p>
<p>
Brady Corporation is an international manufacturer and marketer of complete solutions that identify and protect people, products and places. Brady's products help customers increase safety, security, productivity and performance and include high-performance labels, signs, safety devices, printing systems and software. Founded in 1914, the Company has a diverse customer base in electronics, telecommunications, manufacturing, electrical, construction, medical, aerospace and a variety of other industries. Brady is headquartered in Milwaukee, Wisconsin and as of July 31, 2018, employed approximately 6,200 people in its worldwide businesses. Brady's fiscal 2018 sales were approximately $1.17 billion. Brady stock trades on the New York Stock Exchange under the symbol BRC. More information is available at www.bradycorp.com.</p>
<p><b>
Division Description:</b></p>
<p>
PDC is a global leader in people identification solutions, manufacturing high-quality, innovative identification products for a wide assortment of industries. PDC is headquartered in Valencia, CA and has offices globally. PDC has just over 1,500 people and manufacturers its products in six manufacturing sites spanning the U.S., Europe, Mexico and China.</p>
<p><b>
Position Summary:</b></p>
<p>
The Senior Manager of Quality and Operational Excellence will establish, oversee, and manage the Global Quality and Continuous Improvement programs at PDCs multiple manufacturing sites. S/he will provide leadership to establish and maintain quality levels that meet and exceed customer expectations. The Senior Manager of Quality and Operational Excellence will implement and maintain a statistical based quality system, develop and maintain quality systems in compliance with ISO and TS standards, and maintain supplier quality relationships. The Senior Manager of Quality and Operational Excellence will drive implementation of PDC's Continuous Improvement initiatives and use them to drive overall improvements to the business.
</p>
<p><b>Essential Duties and Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Determine the overall quality and continuous improvement strategy to align with PDC's overall strategic plan.</li>
<li>Build strong quality systems for determining the appropriate infrastructure and resources required to achieve business objectives.</li>
<li>Ensures internal processes and metrics are aligned to create highest product quality and competitive advantage.</li>
<li>Provide leadership throughout the phase gate process</li>
<li>Provide leadership and guidance to ensure proper interface of the quality/CI function to manufacturing, engineering, and marketing.</li>
<li>Foster strong relationships with PDC's customers and vendors and effectively relates the quality function to the business as a whole.</li>
<li>Act as management representative for ISO requirements. Coordinate and maintain a quality system in compliance with ISO.</li>
<li>Implement and maintain the necessary programs to support the Corporate Quality Policy, the Quality System, and the CI initiatives.</li>
<li>Coordinate investigations of internal and external customer quality issues to insure timely closed loop corrective actions and reports as required.</li>
<li>Organize the quality engineering department, approving plans and expenditures necessary to achieve objectives.</li>
<li>Support supplier quality programs; conduct supplier audits as needed to meet objectives.</li>
<li>Drive efforts to reduce CONC (cost of non-conformance) and other key metrics such as warranty and final yield PPM.</li>
<li>Utilize appropriate lean tools and champion general continuous improvement initiatives to improve overall business performance as measured by productivity, on-time performance and quality trends.</li>
<li>Coordinate the plant's Six Sigma initiatives.</li>
<li>Partners with the Directors of Manufacturing and Supply Chain to create and direct programs to ensure the quality of the product.</li>
<li>Lead creation and implementation of tactical plans to achieve PDC's Strategic Vision</li>
<li>Consistently exceed customer expectations for product delivery and quality.</li>
<li>Provide training and leadership and represent a positive and enthusiastic role model to ensure high engagement across the team.</li>
<li>Participate in identifying transaction opportunities and related due diligence, negotiation and post-acquisition integration.</li>
<li>Coach team leaders on leadership development and assists in creating development and training plans.</li>
<li>Accountable for developing and monitoring processes and practices necessary for legal and ethical compliance in assigned area.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p>
</p>
<p><b>Knowledge, Skills &amp; Abilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor's Degree, preferably in business, engineering, or a related technical field. A master's degree in a related field is preferred.</li>
<li>Minimum five years of quality management experience, which includes experience in Quality Systems.</li>
<li>Experience with quality assurance (QA) administration, QA engineering, QA audit.</li>
<li>Inspection and test experience within manufacturing, which includes work with production, accounting, information systems, materials, planning, human resources, engineering and purchasing.</li>
<li>Original equipment manufacturer (OEM) experience is a plus.</li>
<li>Strong leadership and organizational skills - ability to adjust priorities to rapidly changing work environment.</li>
<li>Excellent understanding of the quality process and manufacturing operations.</li>
<li>Demonstrated successful performance in business planning, marketing and sales in a high growth environment.</li>
<li>Demonstrated successful leadership in new product planning and development.</li>
<li>Strong collaborative work ethic and ability to build and maintain effective working relationships with colleagues, customers, and vendors.</li>
<li>Outstanding analytical and problem-solving skills and a high tolerance for ambiguity.</li>
<li>Excellent communication, interpersonal and customer service skills.</li>
