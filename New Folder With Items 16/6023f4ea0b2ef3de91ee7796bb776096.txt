<h2>Shipping Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div><b>Position Description</b></div>
<div></div>
<div>
<b>Company Summary:</b> Corrugated Supplies Company, LLC is the innovative technological leader in the corrugated industry. Our manufacturing process involves the newest technologies and state-of-the-art equipment. Our employees, machinery, and custom software are best in class. Our dynamic product line and service offerings create profitable business solutions for our customers throughout the country.</div>
<div></div>
<div>
<b>Position Summary:</b> The Shipping Manager will be involved in all activities related to managing the Shipping and Receiving department. The Shipping Manager will oversee 3 shifts and will also work directly with Customer Service, Scheduling, and Production to guarantee on-time delivery to customers.</div>
<div></div>
<div><b>Duties &amp; Responsibilities:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Manage Shipping and Receiving personnel in executing inbound and outbound freight deliveries and shipments on 3 shifts, including general supervision, scheduling, and conducting performance evaluations</li>
<li>Schedule and manage all transportation carriers and freight forwarders, including delivery appointments and document preparation</li>
<li>Coordinate truck driver runs in the most cost &amp; time efficient manner possible</li>
<li>Enhance the work flow to enable shipment increases per month</li>
<li>Inventory management - complete cycle counting and full inventory on finished goods</li>
<li>Ensure positive interactions with Customer Service, Sales, Production, and Accounting.</li>
<li>Ensure constant communication with customers for orders status, delivery notification, and invoicing</li>
<li>Conduct weekly Production/Safety meetings with departmental personnel</li>
<li>Enforce corrective action procedures when applicable and conduct meetings with the violating employees</li>
<li>Keep upper management informed of problems or situations that may affect the performance of the department</li>
<li>Recommend process improvements, where applicable</li>
<li>Other responsibilities assigned as business demands</li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<div><b>Skills Needed:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Excellent verbal and written communication skills</li>
<li>Problem solving and critical thinking</li>
<li>Organization and multi-tasking</li>
<li>Energetic self-starter who thrives in a team-oriented environment</li>
<li>Strong computer skills (Microsoft Office and MAS 500)</li>
<li>Inventory management</li>
</ul>
