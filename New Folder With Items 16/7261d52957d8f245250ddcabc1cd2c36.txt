<h2>Scrap Processor/Buyer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Metro Group, Inc. is a premier metal recycling company servicing the Wasatch Front. Family owned and operated since 1976, Metro Group is committed to providing quality service with integrity. As a growing company, we are looking for a self-motivated individual passionate about customer service, operating equipment, optimizing experience and enhancing the effectiveness of our team. We want to execute winning strategies to ensure employees are providing WOW customer service in a safe and effective manner.</p>
<ul><li>JOB DESCRIPTION</li></ul>
<ul>
<li>Do you enjoy creating a "WOW" customer service experience? We are looking for someone who is friendly and outgoing. You will often be Metro Group's initial contact with customers, drivers and vendors. If you are willing to go the extra mile to help others with their questions or needs, you will be very popular here at Metro. Ideally you should be courteous, friendly and professional.</li>
<li>If you like a variety of duties, this is the perfect role for you. As our Non-Ferrous Buyer/Production employee, you will be interacting with customers, vendors, and other employees. A typical day might include weighing customer's material on the scale, prepping material to be shipped, organizing and sorting different grades, and kinds of metals, communicating to the office staff on customer needs as applicable.</li>
<li>Are you naturally a happy and positive person? You will be helping customers inspect material, answer questions and communicate processes to the customer. So a pleasant attitude it critical for this position.</li>
<li>Do you like things to be organized and accurate? Our Metal Buyers must be able to accurately create scale tickets, grade materials, sort metals and operate equipment. Attention to detail and accuracy is critical for your success.</li>
