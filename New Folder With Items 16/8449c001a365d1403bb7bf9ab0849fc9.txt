Holiday Retirement helps older people live better, and we accomplish this by following these principles:<br>
</p>

<li>
Older people are vibrant and deserve the best experience.</li>
<li>
We promote independence and create choice to do your thing.</li>

<li>

<p>If you have a passion for making a difference in the lives of older people, we’d love to talk to you.</p>
<br>
We have a rewarding opportunity for Regional Director. In this role, you will lead a portfolio of communities; positively impacting the lives of our residents, developing an effective team, and driving occupancy.<br>
<br>
<b>Regional Director Responsibilities:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Direct the overall daily operations of multiple senior living residence facilities in an assigned geographic area to ensure the highest quality operation in accordance with facility standards.</li>
<li>
Recruit, hire, develop, and evaluate team members; building a collaborative culture.
</li>
<li>Develop long-range objectives and strategies.
</li>
<li>Prepare and manage budgets, cash flow, occupancy, and financial performance.
</li>
<li>Ensure resident and associate satisfaction.
</li>
<li>Ensure compliance with all federal agencies.
</li>
<li>Demonstrate proficiency in communication and organizational skills.
</li>
<li>Demonstrate a desire to be a team player.
</li>
</ul>
<br>
<b>Qualifications:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s Degree (B.A/B.S.).
</li>
<li>Minimum of 8 years of experience in Operations and Financial Administration or like position.
</li>
<li>Minimum of 5 years of experience leading teams.
</li>
<li>Knowledge of applicable laws, rules, and regulations governing Independent Living/Assisted Living communities.
</li>
<li>Proven track record implementing sales routines, advertising, marketing, and public relations.
</li>
<li>Ability to travel extensively throughout the Organization/District/Region by automobile and airplane as needed.
</li>
