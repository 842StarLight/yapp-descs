<h2>Order Fulfillment Associate - Cut & Match</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>Circle Graphics and The World Art Group are the world’s foremost producers of grand and large-format digital graphics. We are an industry leader in creating innovative products for outdoor advertising, business signage, wall decor, art reproduction, and the professional photography and art communities.<br>
<br>
Our combined 407,000 square feet of manufacturing facilities across the United States house over 88 grand and large format digital printers, custom automated finishing equipment, and coating capabilities to produce printed recyclable materials up to 16 ft wide. Our prepress department processes more than 20,000 files per day with the aid of proprietary software automation tools. We operate around-the-clock, 7 days a week to deliver extraordinary customer service, and print over 431 million square feet of material annually. We specialize in industry leading innovations accompanied by world class service.<br>
<br>
<b>Job Description Overview:</b><br>
The role of the Production Associate – Cut and Match / Sort Orders / Package is to move rolls of canvas and paper to the cutting table, using a handheld trimmer or scissors to cut canvas images, matching images up with job sheet paperwork.<br>
<br>
<b>Essential Duties and Responsibilities:</b><br>
<br>
<b>Cut/Match/ Sort Prints 90% of position responsibility</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Trimming/cutting canvas and paper</li>
<li>Organizing and matching prints to orders</li>
<li>Measure prints as necessary.</li>
<li>Recognize quality issues with prints and communicate imperfections and errors with Production Team.</li>
<li>Maintain a clean work space daily, including recycling and trash removal.</li>
<li>Be able to prioritize the order in which rolls are cut, finding essential and rush orders quickly when necessary.</li>
<li>Assist with the labeling and organization of sales kits</li>
</ul>
<br>
<b>
Assist Shipping Department 10% of position responsibility</b><br>
<br>
<ul>
<li>Assist the Shipping Department with making of and packing of tubes</li>
<li>Perform additional shipping duties when necessary, including basic knowledge of using the UPS Worldship software program</li>
</ul>
