<div>
Xerox is seeking a Client Manager on our Government, Healthcare and Educations team. The responsibilities and purpose of this position are as follows:</div>
<div></div>
<ul>
<li><div>Sell the full line of Xerox technology, services, and solutions to both current Xerox customers and non-users within a specified geographic territory and vertical.</div></li>
<li><div>
Drive both short-term sales while developing, and implementing long-term strategies to ensure Xerox retains the account and grows new business.</div></li>
<li><div>
Negotiate prices, contract terms and deadlines with prospective and existing clients to increase quarterly revenue growth.</div></li>
<li><div>
Analyze client office environments and develop assessment-driven solutions</div></li>
<li><div>
Collaborate with Xerox internal stakeholders, including Finance, Operations, Distribution Chain Managers, and Analysts, to guarantee asset optimization and cost reduction strategies.</div></li>
<li><div>
Develop prospect pipeline to expand current and additional offerings resulting in sales revenue and incremental annualized rate of return (ARR) through signings.</div></li>
<li><div>
Increase profitable revenue growth by introducing new products, services/solutions and increasing demand for existing products/services/solutions.</div></li>
<li><div>
Leverage industry and strategic alliance partnerships to develop client relationships and uncover new opportunities for cloud, cyber, and managed print and mobility solutions.</div></li>
</ul>
