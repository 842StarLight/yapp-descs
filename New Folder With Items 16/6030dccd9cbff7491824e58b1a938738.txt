 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">

<i>Omni-Channel Supervisor </i>*</li></ul>
<p><b>General Description</b></p>
<p>As the Omni-Channel Supervisor you are accountable for driving Omni-Channel initiatives, supervising and engaging Omni-channel team training and developing associates.</p>
<p>Primary Responsibilities:</p>
<ul>
<li>Customer Service &amp; Sales</li>
<li>Greets and assists customers in finding products and partners with other team members when additional help is needed</li>
<li>Models for and holds team accountable for outstanding customer service</li>
<li>Supports the Manager on Duty program</li>
<li>Directs customer service activities throughout the store</li>
<li>Enterprise Fulfillment</li>
<li>Responsible for the completion of all Ship from Store &amp; Buy Online Pick Up in Store orders within prescribed time periods</li>
<li>Achieves productivity targets by reviewing available reports and metrics to identifying opportunity items and reduce shrink</li>
<li>Partners with Operations Supervisor to resolve operational concerns that impact Enterprise Fulfillment</li>
<li>Conducts audits of Enterprise Fulfillment orders to ensure accuracy of orders, drive customer experience and minimize shrink</li>
<li>jcp.com</li>
<li>Responsible for the jcp.com Return Shipment Processes and ensures that all items are returned</li>
<li>Responsible for stockroom standards for all Omni-channel processes to ensure efficient execution and a safe work environment</li>
<li>Reviews available jcp.com reports and metrics to identify opportunity items and reduce shrink</li>
<li>Conducts quarterly audit of jcp.com packages and takes action as needed</li>
<li>Team Development</li>
<li>Responsible for the completion of Omni-channel associate training and policy compliance</li>
<li>Consistently provides ongoing feedback and coaching to associate</li>
<li>Takes action on performance and policy issues within the team</li>
</ul>
<p>Core Competencies &amp; Accomplishments:</p>
<p>To achieve success at JCPenney, a Omni-Channel Supervisor will possess the following:</p>
<ul>
<li>Work experience- Minimum of 2 years retail leadership experience</li>
<li>Education- Bachelor degree or equivalent work experience</li>
<li>Drives Performance - Holds self and others accountable to a higher standard to achieve results. Provides feedback, visibility and recognition to the team</li>
<li>Service-Oriented- Empathetically services the needs of customers and associates; seeks to understand diverse points of view to help find the best solution</li>
<li>Builds Relationships – Develops positive relationships: establishes strong networks, shows respect for different styles, and communicates effectively to a wide audiences</li>
<li>Drives Change - Adapts quickly to changing situations with energy and a positive attitude</li>
</ul>
