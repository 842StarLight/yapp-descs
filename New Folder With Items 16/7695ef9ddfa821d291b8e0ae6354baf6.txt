<h2>Channel Program Coordinator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Overview<br>
</b></h2>
<div></div>
<div><div><div>
<div>When a team member joins the Corporate Marketing team at Kyocera they join a network of professionals with one common goal; improve the overall customer experience with Kyocera. The marketing team is seeking a Channel Program Coordinator to organize, promote and track effective initiatives. The ideal candidate should be analytical and focused, but with insight into customer behavior and motivational forces. This job involves both short- and long-term programs that inspire, educate and attract dealer involvement and customer retention.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
Kyocera is passionate about improving our customers’ lives with technology. The Channel Program Coordinator will assist Kyocera to create and improve on programs to better serve their customers.</div>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<ul>
<li>Implement, and maintain program initiatives that adhere to organizational objectives for direct sales and dealer engagement</li>
<li>
Maintain and track programs from our direct sales and dealer organizations for satisfaction, quality, and performance</li>
<li>
Identify key requirements needed for collaboration with team members across departments to ensure workflows are in alignment with overall business plans</li>
<li>
Work closely with project sponsor, cross-functional teams, and assigned project managers to plan and develop scope, deliverables, required resources, work plan and timing for new initiatives</li>
<li>
Analyze, evaluate, and overcome program risks, and produce reports for management and stakeholders</li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
Additional Responsibilities</div>
<ul>
<li>
Comply with and maintain accurate and timely reports as established by department management</li>
<li>
Actively participate in Kyocera training programs as well as third party training programs, as directed.</li>
<li>
Continually provide the necessary guidance and governance to ensure the Kyocera brand identity is properly presented in all instances</li>
<li>
Ensures all objectives and targets are met, including long-term and time-based objectives for the Corporation.</li>
<li>
Perform other related duties as assigned by the Director of Channel Management &amp; Event Marketing, or VP of Corporate Marketing</li>
</ul>
</div></div></div>

</b></h2>

About Kyocera Document Solutions America, Inc.</div>
<div>
Kyocera Document Solutions America, Inc is a group company of Kyocera Document Solutions Inc., a global leading provider of total document solutions based in Osaka, Japan. The company’s portfolio includes reliable and eco-friendly MFPs and printers, as well as business applications and consultative services which enable customers to optimize and manage their document workflow, reaching new heights of efficiency. With professional expertise and a culture of empathetic partnership, the objective of the company is to help organizations put knowledge to work to drive change. Kyocera is looking for enthusiastic and innovative people to help our customers run their businesses more efficiently and more profitably. We offer a generous benefits package including medical, dental and vision plans, a 401k match, flexible spending, disability and life insurance, plus paid time off and holidays.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
Kyocera Document Solutions Inc. is a core company of Kyocera Corporation, a leading supplier of semiconductor packages, industrial and automotive components, electronic devices, solar power generating systems and mobile phones. During the year ended March 31, 2019, Kyocera Corporation’s consolidated sales revenue totalled 1.62 trillion yen (approx. US$14.6 billion). Kyocera appears on the “Derwent Top 100 Global Innovators 2018-19” list by Clarivate Analytics and is ranked #655 on Forbes magazine’s 2019 “Global 2000” list of the world’s largest publicly traded companies.<br>
