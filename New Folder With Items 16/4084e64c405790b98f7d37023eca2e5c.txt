<h2>Tax Research Analyst Associate Principle</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>In this role, you will serve as an indirect taxes subject matter expert for the compliance needs of the Vertex Indirect Tax Returns product. Lead highly complex projects requiring research and interpretation of the reporting requirements for indirect tax as well as determine the data needs of front-end calculation systems for a full end to end solution. Structure complex tax content within Vertex products in a logical manner that is consistent with system architecture and platforms. Perform quality assurance tasks following documented processes. Test complex tax content and functionality within Vertex products and internal databases. Respond to customer tax content and reporting issues while learning systems feature functionality to develop solutions.</p>
<p></p>

Perform quality assurance tasks following documented processes.</p></li>

Lead complex projects and participate as a team member on projects.</p></li>
<li><p>
Contribute to the development of requirements for new functionality and product enhancements.</p></li>

Understand and resolve complex customer issues to develop solutions.</p></li>
<li><p>
Represent Vertex as a subject matter expert at product demonstrations, presentations, seminars, panels and webcasts, if needed.</p></li>
<li><p>
Work without supervision with wide latitude for independent judgment.</p></li>

Lead or participate in other operational and strategic projects or duties as assigned.</p></li>

Quickly and effectively research and define complex tax and reporting requirements and provide recommendations to tax and nontax personnel.Occasional travel may be required.</p></li>

Other functions may be assigned, and management retains the right to add or change the duties at any time.</p></li>
