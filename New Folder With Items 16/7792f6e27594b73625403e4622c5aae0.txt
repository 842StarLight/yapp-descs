<h2>Marketing Operations Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>The <b>Marketing Operations Specialist </b>will be responsible for driving increased success of demand generation programs for ACTIVE Network and its B2B and B2C business. The Marketing Operations Specialist will report to the Director of Marketing Operations. This role will be responsible for managing the marketing automation and campaign execution needs of our global business-to-business (B2B) marketing organization. Specifically, they will be responsible for set-up and administration of Eloqua 10 automated marketing and demand generation software and services, including oversight of account access and integration with Salesforce Customer Relationship Management (CRM) software. They will manage all B2B email marketing campaigns and execute split-tests for each market to continually improve the company’s email programs and landing pages. They will be responsible for ensuring submitted content and copy is well-proofed, engaging and on-strategy. Lastly, they will manage email reporting and tracking, recommend improvements, enhancements and testing strategies for future programs and participate in pre-project planning and campaign tracking.</p>
<p>
<b>Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Global support of demand generation programs by executing outbound email marketing and nurture programs in our enterprise marketing automation platform (Oracle Eloqua)</li>
<li>Take an active role in driving and managing the data capture and maintenance strategy (i.e. web form and data hygiene), understand how to prioritize list pull activities including list scrubbing, importing and processing</li>
<li>Drive marketing campaigns to develop, implement and manage an email deliverability strategy and production</li>
<li>Engage with marketing leaders &amp; sales operations to ensure audience segments, personas and lists are accurate</li>
<li>Design and analyze performance reports and dashboards to measure and optimize marketing ROI</li>
<li>Design and code HTML emails to be used in global marketing campaigns</li>
<li>Take ownership of managing email projects, ensuring all campaigns are launched on time</li>
<li>Assemble marketing campaigns and implement campaign tracking parameters</li>
<li>Create segments, shared filters and utilize other marketing automation tools</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p>
</p>
<p><b>Skills/Requirements:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>2+ years’ experience in marketing automation, with at least 1 year of enterprise level marketing automation platform (MAP) experience</li>
<li>Certified on any major MAP and applications, with extensive knowledge and experience creating and maintaining programs for multi-touch high-volume campaigns</li>
<li>Experience in CRM tools and reporting such as Salesforce.com &amp; Excel</li>
<li>Knowledge of data capture and management strategies preferred (i.e. working with forms and managing data hygiene)</li>
<li>High attention to detail and the ability to effectively quality control test campaigns prior to launch.</li>
<li>Strong sense of urgency and experience in deadline driven roles.</li>
<li>Ability to work in a fast paced environment and across organizations to achieve goals.</li>
<li>Proficient in HTML/CSS/JavaScript</li>
</ul>
