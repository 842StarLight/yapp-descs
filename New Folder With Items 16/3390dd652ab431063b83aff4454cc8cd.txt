<h2>Assistant Finance Director</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<b>About the Opportunity</b><p></p>
<p>
An established hospitality company located in New York City is actively seeking a diligent and experienced accounting professional for a promising opportunity as their new Assistant Finance Director. In this role, the Assistant Finance Director will be responsible for the delivery of clean internal and external audit reports. Apply today!</p>
<p></p>
<b>
Company Description</b><p></p>
<p>
Hospitality Company</p>
<p></p>
<b>
Job Description</b><p></p>
<p>
The Assistant Finance Director will:</p>
<ul>
<li>Prepare and post journal entries, ensuring efficient completion of all month-end functions for review with Controller</li>
<li>Monitor, supervise, and prepare month-end balance sheet account reconciliation and analysis</li>
<li>Participate in the creation of the Annual Operating and Capital Budgets, Strategic Plan, and Monthly Operating Forecasts</li>
<li>Coordinate and ensure that all daily, semi-monthly and monthly management reports are issued on a timely and accurate basis</li>
<li>Assist in the effective utilization and integration of hotel and accounting technology systems</li>
<li>Participate in Senior Duty Manager schedule, rotating with other senior managers of the hotel throughout the year</li>
<li>Actively contribute to hotel overall leadership and direction</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<b>Required Skills</b><p></p>
<ul>
<li>Professional designation or acceptable university degree with an appropriate specialization in Accounting</li>
<li>3+ years of hotel accounting experience and previous accounting experience at a senior level</li>
<li>Proven leadership and coaching skills with a track record of developing a highly motivated and cross-trained group of progressive accounting professionals</li>
<li>Familiarity with hotel operating systems and software, including GFS/SUN, Hyperion, Microsoft Office, Genesis Call Accounting, POS, Opera and Sales &amp; Catering</li>
<li>Demonstrated administrative skills</li>
<li>Excellent written and verbal communication skills</li>
</ul>
