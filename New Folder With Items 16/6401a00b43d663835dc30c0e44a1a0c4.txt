<h2>Senior Director of Enterprise Information Services Sales</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>The Senior Director of Enterprise Information Services Sales is responsible for all aspects of acquiring new clients for Cox Automotive’s Information Services products. Initial responsibility will focus on selling a set of well established Information Services products that are highly credible in the market and have a long track record of providing significant value to various segments in the Automotive industry.<br>
Cox Automotive has identified segments of under-serviced clients and this role will aim to proactively build relationships and ultimately sell Cox Automotive’s services to these companies. The role will leverage Cox Automotive’s leading brand as well as the Marketing assets from a long established leader in the Automotive industry<br>
The position will have responsibility across the U.S. and will work in conjunction with account management leaders that currently service a strong group of existing clients after sales are negotiated.<br>
</p>
<ul>
<li><p>
Lead net new pursuits for Information Services products/solutions, specifically focused on Cox Automotive’s valuations products.</p></li>
<li><p>
Quickly develop a funnel of opportunities and over time a book of business for Cox Automotive’s Information Services solutions.</p></li>
<li><p>
Create specific sales plans for target accounts and then develop new relationships or leverage existing relationships from prior work experiences to educate prospective clients on the value of Cox Automotive’s Information Services solutions and close new deals.</p></li>
<li><p>
Represent Cox Automotive Information Services at national trade shows such as the National Automobile Dealer’s Association annual event or American Financial Services Association annual conference.</p></li>
<li><p>
As new deals are closed, work with existing teams to transition clients to them for on-going account management, allowing time to continually focus on additional net new sales.</p></li>
<li><p>
Leverage technology and/or Information Services sales expertise to represent the value of Cox Automotive’s strategic assets. Use familiarity with data delivery platforms, client data ingestion capabilities and data pricing methodologies and to creatively develop approaches that best meet client’s use cases and to structure deals that are advantageous for Cox Automotive.</p></li>
<li><p>
Develop knowledge of key competitive Information Services solutions available in the marketplace and demonstrate an ability to successfully position Cox Automotive’s solutions relative to these competitors.</p></li>
<li><p>
Collaborate as appropriate with other members of Enterprise Sales who are also focused on selling other products and services to the largest national target accounts of Cox Automotive.</p></li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<h4 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Qualifications:</b></h4>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li><p>
BA or BS Degree required.</p></li>
<li><p>
12+ years of increasing and relevant experience in business to business sales</p></li>
<li><p>
Academic background in business, finance, computer science, mathematics, engineering, or related field; advanced degree preferred</p></li>
<li><p>
Demonstrated successful sales experience including significant net new proactive development of strategic clients and new revenue streams.</p></li>
<li><p>
Demonstrated comfort selling technical solutions such as other Information Services products or technology solutions sold in a subscription service model</p></li>
<li><p>
Strong interpersonal and negotiation skills</p></li>
<li><p>
Excellent written and verbal communication abilities</p></li>
<li><p>
This is a field-based role with minimum of 50% travel.</p></li>
</ul>
