<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Job Description<br>
</b></h2>
<div></div>
<div><div><div>
<div>
<b>Job Title:</b> Account Manager</div>
<div>
<b>
Location:</b> New York</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div><b>
Job Function:</b></div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
This position will be client facing across multiple network accounts. The Account Manager will act as the day-to-day liaison between Comscore and the client, working to resolve any data issues that come up, train client groups on products, and maintain relationships with each user of the products. The Account Manager will report directly into sales and will assist with sales initiatives on each account.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div><b>
What You'll Do:<br>
</b></div>
<div></div>
<ul>
<li>Clear understanding of client business, all networks included in portfolio</li>
<li>
Understanding key programming and initiatives, programming model</li>
<li>
Complete understanding of account status, client subscriptions, current point of contact by product Maintain and update the client books for each account as needed</li>
<li>
Track all client network performance weekly in a trend to capture any anomalies in the data. The Usage Summary report in the AME TVE subscription can be used to easily pull this data Prepared to present demos to new and existing clients for the following products: TV Essentials - plus Advanced Demographics, Rubik, OnDemand Essentials, OTT Intelligence report</li>
<li>
Understand methodology and metrics for the above products, including general understanding of refreshes and impact statements provided to clients</li>
<li>
Alert clients in a timely manner (day of) to TVE Site News updates, especially any site freezes, updates regarding advanced demographics they subscribe to, or any updates that may impact data in products they subscribe to Enterprise accounts provide weekly Executive Summary</li>
<li>
Assist in preparation of client meetings including assisting with decks and stories utilizing available data sources</li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<br>
<div><b>
What You'll Need:</b></div>
<div></div>
<br>
<ul>
<li>
2 years account management, networks, market sales, ad sales, ratings or market research experience Bachelor's degree (BA or BS) from four-year college or university</li>
<li>
Competency working with complex research/measurement data</li>
<li>
Strong data, analytics, and decision-making abilities</li>
<li>
Demonstrated success in managing executive relationships and delivering to client needs</li>
<li>
Excellent interpersonal and communication skills; ability to communicate well with people at all levels of an organization both internally and externally, both written and verbally</li>
<li>
Ability to formulate action plans and execute initiatives.</li>
<li>
Outstanding project management, presentation, and customer service skills</li>
<li>
Strong competency in Word, Excel, and PowerPoint Self-motivated, organized, and resourceful</li>
</ul>
