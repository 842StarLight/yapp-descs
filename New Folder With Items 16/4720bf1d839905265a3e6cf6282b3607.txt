Position Summary
<br><br>
The Solutions Engineering role is to assist in the sales pursuit by providing high level consultation of customer requirements, customer needs and most importantly how all of those relay into the Nextiva solution. This individual will work hand in hand with the sales team to ensure that all team members are aware of the design requirements as well as participate as a technical liaison to the sales teams. Additionally, this individual will be responsible for creating and introducing solutions to the customer that will be key to the sales process.
<br><br>
<b>Responsibilities:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Execute thorough discovery of customer requirements and participation in direct sales interactions via calls, webinars and onsite visits; play an instrumental role in the customer facing component of the sale</li>
<li>Develop comprehensive and creative use cases for prospective Nextiva customers</li>
<li>Present the Nextiva solution in conjunction with customer predefined requirements</li>
<li>Review quotes, RFPs, plans and other customer documents to develop and prepare an effective response or proposal</li>
<li>Maintain a working knowledge of the industry and competitors in order to act as a valuable resource to the customer and sales team</li>
<li>Participate in work groups regarding custom requests and professional services for unique customer requirements</li>
<li>Develop relationships with vendors including keeping track of new information and sharing with your team</li>
<li>Develop comprehensive documentation in the statement of work that will be utilized in the post-sale process.</li>
<li>Provide technical assistance to other departments, including Product Management, Marketing, and Learning Development teams</li>
<li>Ensure the solution will work for the customer through demonstration and also through proof of concept or trial deployments</li>
</ul>
<b>Communications and Presentations:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Must be compelling, passionate and enthusiastic public speaker with communication skills of the highest caliber</li>
<li>Deliver comprehensive and customized product and service demonstrations focused on functional capabilities in business terms easily understood by the customer</li>
</ul>
