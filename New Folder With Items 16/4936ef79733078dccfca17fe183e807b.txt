<h2>Brand Ambassador</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>We are looking for outgoing individuals to join our team as event brand ambassadors. You will be representing Bath Fitter around the Greater Austin area and its surrounding counties while attending various events/shows/festivals/etc in the area. You will be creating brand awareness and sales opportunities for our team. You are to engage and convert prospects into potential customers by obtaining their contact information. You are to engage those who are interested in our products and services through our proven techniques and training.</p>
<p>The ideal candidate will:</p>
<p>- Have a great attitude, a strong work ethic, be punctual, and energetic</p>
<p>- Ability to communicate, connect, and interact with prospects</p>
<p>- Show enthusiasm and professional knowledge of product</p>
<p>- Be able to work a flexible schedule and weekends</p>
<p>- Be willing and able to travel to surrounding counties</p>
<p>- Have knowledge and/or appreciate word of mouth advertising</p>
<p>*This is a great part time position for students, active retirees, anyone looking for a second job income, and anyone looking to gain experience in sales and marketing.</p>
<p>* This is a fun and social position at local events. No cold calling, no phone work, no selling, all interactive face to face work.</p>
<p>*Hourly wage, mileage, plus bonus opportunities.</p>
<p>Must be able to lift, carry, push or pull 50 lbs</p>
<p>Must have a valid driver license and reliable transportation</p>
<p>Must successfully pass a drug screen</p>
<p>Must be able to attend weekly meetings at our main office.</p>
<p>Job Type: Part-time</p>
<p>Salary: $12.00 to $13.00 /hour</p>
