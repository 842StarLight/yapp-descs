<b>Position Responsibilities</b><ul>
<li>
Plan and execute testing activities for data warehouse project.</li>
<li>
Create and execute tests of ETL processes.</li>
<li>
Validate data quality.</li>
<li>
Test downstream consumption of reports, calculations, etc.</li>
<li>
Develop, execute and maintain test plans, test scenarios, test suites and test cases.</li>
<li>
Support testing efforts as a part of Agile / Scrum teams.</li>
<li>
Provide estimates associated with testing activities and tasks.</li>
<li>
Facilitate the defect management and triage process for assigned projects.</li>
<li>
Collaborate with the business and development teams to troubleshoot and resolve defects and other related issues.</li>
<li>
Partner with solution analysts and project managers to ensure requirements are documented, understood, testable and accurately tested.</li>
<li>
Track and report on test status using established QA metrics and KPIs for assigned projects.</li>
<li>
Provide input on go / no-go decisions for production releases.</li>
<li>
Collaborate with QA manager and project teams to develop, implement and improve innovative QA capabilities, approaches and services.
</li>
</ul>
<br>

4+ years QA experience in an IT environment.
</li>

4+ years of experience in testing analytical processes.

</li>
