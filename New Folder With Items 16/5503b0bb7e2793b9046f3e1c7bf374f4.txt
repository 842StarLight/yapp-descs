<b>
Primary Function of Position:</b><br>
<br>
Develop and maintain the System&amp; Vision Business Unit Master Production Schedule and Forecast File. Responsible for working across multiple functional areas such as product marketing, finance, sales operations, planning, purchasing and manufacturing engineering to develop the best manufacturing strategy to meet the company’s quarterly revenue and customer service goals. Performs forecast load and MRP maintenance in the ERP system per Intuitive’s SOX policy. Supports S&amp;OP process. Collaborates with New Product Introduction teams to incorporate NPI in the planning process. Works on/Leads continuous improvement projects targeting efficiency improvements and transparency of data.<br>
<br>
<b>Roles and Responsibilities:</b><br>
<br>
This position has responsibility and authority to:<div>
<br>
</div>
<div><ul>
<li>Collaborate with Demand Planning, Manufacturing, Planning, Engineering, and Service Operations team members to develop supply plan</li>
<li>Attend monthly and/or quarterly Demand and Supply Review meetings and effectively communicate with Marketing, Sales, Finance and Operations on the overall operations material and manufacturing plans, highlighting any constraints and proposing solutions.</li>
<li>Manage MPS in o9 S&amp;OP software and ensure alignment with Operations Mfg.
</li>
<li>Analyze monthly inventory reports with Finance. Coordinate inventory concerns with organization and support monthly inventory review with executive management</li>
<li>Help resolve and troubleshoot SAP (MPS/MRP) issues related to Buyer/Planner concerns for all plants (regions). Interact and support IT issues/projects in SAP/Hyperion, etc.</li>
<li>Develop, maintain and effectively communicate ATP reporting to the organization
</li>
<li>Coordinate ECO change activity with functional groups as needed</li>
<li>Write and maintain departmental procedures
</li>
<li>Accurately maintain product hierarchy coding in SAP for all part numbers</li>
<li>Assist buyers and/or planners in verification of data in SAP/o9</li>
<li>Manage and review SOX compliance for MRP load in multiple plants</li>
<li>Improve processes and simplify/automate wherever possible.</li>
<li>Assist in training others as needed.</li>
</ul></div>
