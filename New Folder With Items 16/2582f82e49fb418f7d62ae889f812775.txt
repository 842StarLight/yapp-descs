<h2>Director Portfolio Marketing</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>Those who join Emergent BioSolutions feel a sense of ownership about their future. You will excel in an environment characterized by respect, innovation and growth opportunities. Here, you will join passionate professionals who advance their scientific, technical and professional skills to develop products designed-to protect life.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>JOB SUMMARY<br>
</b></p>
<p></p>
<p>The Director, Portfolio Marketing is a core member of the Commercial Operations team and serves as the dedicated Business Partner to 3 Business Units (BUs) and the International Sales Team. They contribute substantially to shaping the strategy and direction of Medical Counter Measures ROW expansion through the development, implementation and continued optimization of a global MCM portfolio strategy. This role includes working internally with marketing directors, regulatory affairs, government affairs, manufacturing/supply chain, finance and commercial stakeholders.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>RESPONSIBILITIES<br>
</b></p>
<p></p>
<p>Dedicated business partner / single point of contact with Product Directors across 3 Business Units (BUs) and the Head of International Sales. Partner with stakeholders in the legal, regulatory affairs, government affairs, contracting, compliance, supply chain/manufacturing, sales operations and marketing communications to optimize governance decision-making, and build and maintain an expertise in (in-line) portfolio management. Lead the effort for a 5-year ROW MCM market development strategy for the EBS CBRNE MCM Portfolio.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>This would include:<br>
</b></p>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Determining market prioritization for the CBRNE MCM portfolio. Product prioritization within each international market and ROI analysis indicating which markets may have market access potential, but where the costs (legal, regulatory, contracting, manufacturing, etc) may not warrant a positive return, to help inform enterprise trade-offs across 3 Business Units (BUs)
</li>
<li>Responsible for presenting this assessment and recommendations to the EVP of Business Operations, BU Heads, and key cross functional partner function heads, for approval
</li>
<li>Maintaining annual plan updates and coordinating with Product Leads within BUs to ensure these priorities were incorporated into their annual planning process
</li>
<li>Responsible for facilitating the development of country specific MCM portfolio promotional plan, and ensuring consistency with product strategy
</li>
<li>Participating in the annual operational planning process by shaping the tactical plan and budget required to achieve the Product Leadâ€™s stated objectives on balance with International Sales goals, in accordance with the approved MCM Portfolio Strategy
</li>
<li>Partner with Head International Sales, Head of Government Affairs and Head of Contracting Strategy to ensure the voice of the customer and local market knowledge is considered in all strategy assessments.
</li>
<li>Partner with Business Insights team and external partners to define success metrics and analyze performance of marketing programs on an ongoing basis; Ensure plans are optimized as needed to improve impact and develop recommendations for future plans based on metrics and impact.
</li>
<li>Monitor portfolio progress and related milestones, identify gaps and make strategic recommendations.
</li>
<li>Provide strategic leadership and expertise with respect to portfolio analytics and decision-making including structuring of problems and interpretation of insights that facilitates trade-off discussions and buy-up decisions for new projects.
<br>
</li>
</ul>
<p><i>The above statements are intended to describe the general nature of work performed by those in this job. It is not an exhaustive list of all duties, and other duties may be assigned.<br>
</i></p>
