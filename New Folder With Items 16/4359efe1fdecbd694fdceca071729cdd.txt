<p><b>
Position Title:</b> Supervisor Fiber Engineering- 6E</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Position Summary</b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
The Supervisor of Fiber Engineering is responsible for a team of Fiber Engineers performing a wide range of activities related to the installation, maintenance and documentation of metropolitan and long haul fiber optic networks. The Supervisor of Fiber Engineering will ensure timely completion of team assignments in support of monthly revenue targets and will maintain an on-call schedule to provide 24x7 Fiber Engineering support for network troubles and outages. This position will represent Crown Castle in a professional manner if front of customers (internal and external), utilities, municipalities, and contractors; and, be able to work cross functionally within a matrix organizational structure to achieve implementation goals in conjunction with Sales, RF Engineering, Operations, Regulatory, Finance, Product Development, and Supply Chain Management.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Essential Job Functions</b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Supervise the production of detailed fiber level designs associated with budgeted customer and network projects. Duties include communicating with contractor or internal workforces with oversight for OSP/ISP cable work (line, splice and civil) related to fiber design engineer’s issued scopes of work.</li>
<li>Supervise Regional Fiber Design staff in the performance of their daily responsibilities. Assign workload, manage team deadlines and commitments, resolve technical and business challenges and effectively handle escalations.</li>
<li>Ensure fiber optic network and customer services are properly documented in all Crown Castle Fiber designated records and inventory systems and tools.</li>
<li>Overall responsibilities for material/asset management associated with various fiber optic cable and associated hardware used in Crown Castle Fiber’s fiber optic network.</li>
<li>Develop and train Fiber Engineers to use a wide range of industry standard and company specific equipment and systems</li>
<li>Schedule Fiber Design Engineering resources and participation in Crown Castle Fiber’s emergency response &amp; callout system.</li>
<li>Work closely with all departmental teams ie: NOC, Sales, Network Engineering &amp; Planning, and Crown Castle Fiber customers.</li>
<li>Supervise employees; including hiring, coaching, goal determination and setting, performance reviews, termination, rewards determination, training/development and scheduling.</li>
<br>
</ul>
