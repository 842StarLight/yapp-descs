<p><b>Responsibilities:</b></p>

Work with the Marketing Director to implement marketing strategies outlined in the marketing plan and provide feedback to ensure metrics are being met.</li>
<li>
Assist Marketing Director in development of annual Marketing Plan for Tehaleh with the goal of meeting or exceeding traffic and sales goals</li>

Manage overall community presentation, including day to day operation of our Welcome Center, community events put on by Newland, resident events put on by community Lifestyle Manager, on-site marketing promotions as well as resident-led/hosted events</li>
<li>
Develop and lead a team of Customer Representatives who bring the Tehaleh community story to life in the Welcome Center.</li>
<li>
Manage guest experience within the Welcome Center (including a third-party operated café)</li>
<ul><li>
Mentor the Community Representatives to maintain the highest level of customer service</li></ul>
<div><ul>
<li>
Utilizing the company wide CRM system</li>
<li>
Sharing competitive market set and differentiators</li>
<li>
Sharing Tehaleh development updates</li>
<li>
Learning how to overcome objections</li>
<li>
Understanding the community brand and how it is communicated in collateral, social media and any other marketing tools created for the community</li>
</ul></div>
<ul>
<li>
Stewarding the community brand at all touch points</li>
<li>
Hiring, coaching and retaining effective staffing</li>
<li>
Collateral management</li>
<li>
Manage the day-to-day operations of the Welcome Center building including staffing, building maintenance, supplies, technology needs, budget, etc.</li>
<li>
Communicate obstacles and potential issues as well as recommended solutions to Marketing Director</li>
<li>
Back up for Marketing Coordinator entering Sales and Traffic into Tochi</li>
</ul>
<li>
Thoroughly understand the consumer through analysis of shopper, buyer and market data on a routine basis to help drive marketing and business decisions</li>
<ul>
<li>
Shopper and Buyer segmentation</li>
<li>
Product segmentation</li>
<li>
Traffic and sales data, including competitive analysis</li>
<li>
On-site and online guest data, via MarketSync</li>
<li>
Market share data</li>
<li>
Absorption numbers – ours and competitors</li>
<li>
Regular and rigorous competitive assessments</li>
<li>
Being the source of current information for Community Representatives, builders, real estate brokers and residents</li>
</ul>
<li>
Have a passion for Customer Nurturing using CRM software</li>
<ul>
<li>
Work with Marketing Director and Copywriter to generate Nurturing Campaign email content</li>
<li>
Become intimately familiar with company platforms including Market Sync, Umbraco and Tochi</li>
<li>
Responsible for loading of Nurturing Campaign content into Umbraco</li>
<li>
Generate queries and utilize reports as needed</li>
<li>
Liaise with Home Office point person to identify issues and/or enhancements within Customer Nurturing System</li>
</ul>
<li>
Website and Social Media Platform Management</li>
<ul>
<li>
Regular website audits, content review and updates to ensure accurate and relevant content for the consumer</li>
<li>
Obtain and maintain builder assets and upload into Umbraco</li>
<li>
Review and implement weekly blog articles and load them into Umbraco</li>
<li>
Maintain ongoing calendar of events</li>
<li>
Work with agency partners to implement social media strategy for Facebook and Instagram</li>
</ul>
<li>
Lead Day-to-Day On-Site Builder Marketing Relationships</li>
<ul>
<li>
Visit each model regularly and speak with sales representatives</li>
<li>
Plan and execute quarterly builder meetings providing updates on Tehaleh development, sales and traffic, advertising campaigns, promotions, etc.</li>
<li>
Regularly communicate updates on this same information via email</li>
<li>
Conduct marketing orientations for new builders</li>
</ul>
<li>
Manage community on-site and off-site signage programs</li>

Ensure community signage is looking its best at all times</li>
<li>
Work with signage vendor to update all wayfinding signage as necessary as builders move their model homes</li>
<li>
Work with builders to maintain branding integrity of their on-site signage</li>
<li>
Work with vendors to manage the weekend off-site directional signage program as well as the permanent off-site signage program</li>

<li>
Oversight of Marketing Coordinator</li>
<ul>
<li>
Assist in development and implementation of real estate broker program including broker presentations, orientations, tours, and the Broker-specific Nurturing Campaign</li>

Assist in development and execution of Ambassador Program which utilizes key resident influencers</li>
<li>
Day-to-day marketing activities</li>
