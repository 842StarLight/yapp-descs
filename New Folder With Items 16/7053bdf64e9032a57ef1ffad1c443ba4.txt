<h2>Plant Maintenance Mechanic-Midland</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Overview of the Production Mechanic position</b></p>
<p>This position is responsible for preventative maintenance, troubleshooting, and repair on all plant machinery and equipment to sustain production at high efficiency levels.</p>
<p><b>Responsibilities</b></p>
<p>· Maintain equipment including mechanical, electrical, hydraulic, pneumatic and fluid handling systems.</p>
<p>· Accurately record project activities and prepares appropriate operating standards and/or maintenance procedures for all equipment and processes.</p>
<p>· Maintain cleanliness of maintenance equipment area.</p>
<p>· Maintain appropriate inventory of maintenance supplies.</p>
<p>· Establish and maintain communication with appropriate equipment vendors and suppliers.</p>
<p><b>Requirements</b></p>
<p>· High School Diploma or GED PREFERRED.</p>
<ul><li>Minimum three (3) years of production equipment maintenance experience, preferably in food and beverage environment.</li></ul>
<p>· Hydraulic and pneumatic systems knowledge.</p>
<p>· Ability to understand blueprints, diagrams and ladder logic programs.</p>
<p>· Experience with software interface tools for Allen Bradley or GE PLCs and windows based software preferred.</p>
<p>· Proficient in using hand, power, shop and diagnostic tools.</p>
<p>· Skilled in electrical power/controls, mechanical and fluid handling systems.</p>
<p>Trade or Technical school certification preferred.</p>
<ul><li>Forklift experience (required in some locations).</li></ul>
