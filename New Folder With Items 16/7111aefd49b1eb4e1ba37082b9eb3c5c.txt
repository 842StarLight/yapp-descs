<h2>Installation Coordinator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Position Summary:</b></p>
<p>
We are seeking a team-oriented Installation Coordinator for one of our branch office locations. This position ensures the branch is supported by a professional, well trained installation team, as well as an adequate number of compliant sub-contractors to deliver excellent customer service.</p>
<p>
The Installation Manager will be in constant communication with Installers and customer throughout the flooring installation process. Candidate must have leadership qualities, strong organization skills and communicate well with various personality types.</p>
<p><b>
Installation Manager Position Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Oversees recruitment, development and supervision of third-party flooring contractors</li>
<li>Leads and trains installers to provide excellent customer service</li>
<li>Assists customers and Installers to identify solutions to issues a mind to maintaining both customer satisfaction and Installer contractual relationship</li>
<li>Manages and hold accountable sub-contractors to company installation standards</li>
<li>Routes/schedules installations daily</li>
<li>Supports the management team by handling customer concerns promptly using good judgement</li>
<li>Visits new installer’s initial installation jobs and proactively visit installer’s job sites to inspect work quality</li>
<li>Maintains order status daily and update company software with pertinent data</li>
<li>Analyzes business results then develop and execute action plans for improvement</li>
<li>Performs other functions as necessary to ensure customer satisfaction and/or Installer retention</li>
<li>Builds rapport with customers and with Installation teams to improve company brand and retain relationships</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Skills Needed to Succeed:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Strong management and leadership skills</li>
<li>Positive and professional with the ability to maintain a calm demeanor during hectic periods</li>
<li>Customer-oriented approach and desire to assist others</li>
<li>Superior organizational skills</li>
<li>Ability to work as a team</li>
<li>Excellent written and verbal communication</li>
<li>Strong time management skills and ability to multi-task and prioritize work</li>
<li>Great attention to detail and problem-solving skills</li>
<li>Must work well with limited direction using independent judgement</li>
<li>Demonstrate a sense of urgency and decisiveness with diplomacy</li>
<li>Knowledge of local competitors within the industry helpful</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Requirements:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>1+ years of experience in management; Contractor management</li>
<li>High School Diploma or equivalent</li>
<li>1+ years of experience with diverse flooring products and installation processes (hardwood, carpet, tile, laminate, etc.) preferred</li>
<li>Ability to work a five-day, 40+ hour work week, including availability on some Saturdays. Additional days or hours may be required based on volume and seasonality, in addition to the flexibility to take calls afternoon/evening and/or weekend hours.</li>
</ul>
