<h2>Transaction Coordinator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>Coldwell Banker Burnet is looking for a Transaction Coordinator to support the data entry and administrative functions of our branch offices.<br>
<br>
The Transaction Coordinator is responsible for data entry and transaction processing. This role creates and processes sales transactions, along with maintaining large amounts of information across various real estate specific databases. This position will also offer agent support as needed by answering agent questions and requests and communications regarding missing/incomplete paperwork. Employees in this position will may also be asked to assist in additional projects based on business need.<br>
<br>
<b>Essential Job Duties:</b><br>
<br>
Processing Listings: Responsible for overseeing the process of all new home listings. This may include creating and managing the transaction in our digital document repository, answering agent questions and requests, sending communications regarding missing/incomplete paperwork, entering Listings into local MLS, uploading photos to market the listing, sending communications regarding status updates to needed parties, ensuring data entry accuracy, etc.<br>
<br>
<b>Other Duties:
</b><ul>
<li>Provide back up support as needed when support staff members are out of office (This does require regional travel)</li>
<li>
Other administrative responsibilities within the office as needed</li>
</ul>

Strong verbal and written communication skills.</li>
