<p><b>
Position Overview</b><br>
Looking for a <i>job</i> that provides meaningful <i>work</i> and competitive compensation? Consider a position with Insurance Cleveland Agency, an independent agency.</p>
<p><b>
Responsibilities</b><br>
Performs administrative and <i>office</i> support activities. Duties may include fielding telephone calls, receiving and directing visitors, word processing, creating spreadsheets and presentations, and filing. Extensive software skills, Internet research abilities and strong communication skills are required. Other activities could include:</p>
<ul>
<li>Develop Leads</li>
<li>Schedule appointments</li>
<li>Identify <i>client</i> needs.</li>
<li>Market appropriate products and services</li>
<li>Establish <i>client</i> relationships and follow up with clients, as needed.</li>
<li>Provide prompt, accurate, and friendly customer service. Service can included responding to inquiries regarding</li>
<li>insurance availability, eligibility, coverage, policy changes, transfers, claim submissions, and billing clarification.</li>
<li>And more..</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Schedule:</b><br>
Our <i>office</i> is open Monday – Wednesday 9 am – 2 pm and Thursday – Friday 9 am – 5 pm. *Potential to <i>work</i> from <i>home</i> after proven <i>job</i> proficiency. Must live within 30 miles of the Parma, Ohio <i>office</i>.</p>
<p><b>
As an employee, you will receive…</b><br>
Hourly pay<br>
Flexible hours<br>
Valuable experience</p>
<p><b>
Qualifications/Requirements</b><br>
One or more years of college education or relevant <i>work</i> experience. <i>Work</i> or educational experience must include:</p>
<ul>
<li>Customer service, sales and/or influencing others</li>
<li>Excellent interpersonal skills</li>
<li>Fast paced and changing environment</li>
<li>Multitasking and <i>time</i> management</li>
<li>Effective communication, verbally and in writing</li>
<li>Computer skills</li>
<li>Must be dependable and have pride in getting <i>work</i> done accurately and timely</li>
<li>Property &amp; Casualty license (must have currently or willing to obtain within 60 days of hire)</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Pay Rate:</b> $12.50 to $16.50 per hour depending on experience, plus:</p>
<p><b>
Production Bonus on personally produced (New and Renewal) business from friends, family &amp; referrals.</b></p>
