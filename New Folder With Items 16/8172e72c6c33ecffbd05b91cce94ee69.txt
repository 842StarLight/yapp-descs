<h2>Designer (Residential Construction)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Lyman Lumber is the go-to building material supplier in the Chippewa Valley. high-end showroom for the best in cabinetry, flooring, and window and door design. Our <b>Design Associates</b> meet with customers and contractors to design a home that best reflects the customers personal style. We are looking for a driven Designer to join our team!</p>
<p><b>Things you should know about working at Lyman Lumber:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>We are all about teamwork! All positions are hands on and we band together when necessary.</li>
<li>We support each other.</li>
<li>We work hard.</li>
<li>Our primary customer is professional builder and remodeler</li>
</ul>
<p><b>The Designer is responsible for (and not limited to):</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Greet clients to the design center, assess clients needs and assist the customer with product selection as it pertains to the project design.</li>
<li>Designs residential construction plans.</li>
<li>Discusses changes and corrections with contractors, homeowners, truss designers, and sales staff prior to and during construction.</li>
<li>Resolves architectural and design discrepancies.</li>
<li>Maintain an active file of new and existing customers.</li>
<li>Maintain a clean, neat, professional looking showroom and customer area.</li>
<li>Participate in semi-annual inventory</li>
<li>Complies with Company’s attendance policy by maintaining regular and predictable attendance.</li>
<li>Performs other duties as necessary.</li>
<li>
<p><b>May Also:</b></p>
</li>
<li>Read and understand blueprints and detailed technical drawings.</li>
<li>Use computer or other technical equipment to provide a technically accurate structural calculations.</li>
<li>Adhere to local building codes and material constraints when designing building plans.</li>
<li>Assist customers as required, providing accurate, timely and thorough customer service.</li>
<li>Measure and scale projects on-site.</li>
<li>Attend training as required to maintain current knowledge of drafting and relevant aspects.</li>
</ul>
<p><br>
<b>We offer…</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Health care benefits, starting the first of month after 30 days of employment</li>
<li>401(k) with company match</li>
<li>Paid holidays</li>
<li>Paid time off</li>
<li>Employee discount</li>
</ul>
<p><b>We want you to join the team if you can check these boxes:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Minimum education required— High school or GED, may require an AAS in civil, architectural or design related field, prefer Bachelor’s degree. Applicable work experience may be substituted for education requirement.</li>
<li>Minimum experience required—1-year drafting experience in a construction or building materials-related industry.</li>
<li>Special skills required—Proficient technical writing skills. Excellent salesmanship and communication skills. Proprietary software, SoftPlan, computer literate, ability to read technical drawings.</li>
<li>Special knowledge required—Reading and interpreting blueprints in an architectural related industry.</li>
<li>Other—PC Literate with Microsoft Office products, SoftPlan, and other design programs that facilitate learning in-house program with ease.</li>
<li>Physical demands include sitting for extended periods of time, standing and walking, bending or stooping, lifting up to 25 pounds frequently and up to 50 pounds occasionally.</li>
</ul>
<p>If this sounds like the perfect fit for you we hope to hear from you soon.</p>
