<p><b>As the Marketing Coordinator, you may look forward to:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Working closely with the marketing team to create successful campaigns and communicate properly to the team</li>
<li>Executing a marketing plan to attain revenue goals</li>
<li>Promoting the overall brand identity of the company</li>
<li>Collecting customer data and market research data and applying to current projects</li>
<li>Providing status reports with relevant data concerning current marketing projects</li>
<li>Executing campaigns across multiple social media platforms</li>
<li>Writing content for marketing material</li>
<li>Other duties as assigned</li>
<li>Obeys all applicable Federal laws, rules, and regulations relating to Anti-Money Laundering (AML) including the Bank Secrecy Act (BSA)</li>
</ul>
<p><b>You are a person that (required skills):</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Has an Associate’s Degree in design, Production, Media, or related field</li>
<li>Has an interest and passion for creative media production and marketing</li>
<li>Is able to demonstrate a high level of professionalism and effectively communicate verbally and in writing</li>
<li>Has experience in Adobe InDesign and Photoshop</li>
<li>Is able to learn new production software</li>
<li>Is able to use media to effectively communicate with social posts on all social media interfaces</li>
<li>Has excellent listening and comprehension skills</li>
</ul>
<p><b>You’ll be better prepared if you have (desired skills):</b></p>
<ul>
<li>A Bachelor’s Degree in design, Production, Media, or related field</li>
<li>Two years of experience in design work or media production</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Physically you can anticipate to:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Express or exchange ideas by means of the spoken word via email and verbally</li>
<li>Exert up to 10 pounds of force occasionally, use your arms and legs, and sit most of the time</li>
<li>Have close visual acuity to perform activities such as analyzing data, viewing a computer terminal, reading, and preparing documentation</li>
<li>Not substantially exposed to adverse environmental conditions</li>
<li>The physical demands described here are representative of those that must be met by an employee to successfully perform the essential responsibilities of this job. Reasonable accommodations may be made to enable individuals with disabilities to perform the essential responsibilities.</li>
<br>
</ul>
