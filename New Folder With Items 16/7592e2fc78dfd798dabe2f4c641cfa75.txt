<div><b>DETAILED JOB DUTIES<br>
</b></div>
<br>
<div></div>
<ul>
<li>Monitors foreclosure actions to ensure compliance with investor, insurer and regulatory guidelines.
</li>
<li>Prepares bidding instructions, ensuring compliance with investor, insurer, state and regulatory guidelines.
</li>
<li>Completes quality control of bidding instructions process.</li>
<li>
Orders and monitors appraisal for completion of bidding instructions.
</li>
<li>Processes all foreclosure sale results within the investor, insurer, state and regulatory guidelines.
</li>
<li>Monitors the receipt of funds from 3rd party sales.</li>
<li>
Maintains caseload and ensures all systems are updated accurately and timely.</li>
<li>
Works all task, processes, events, issues, re-projections and holds within their required deadlines.</li>
<li>
Responds to any phone calls/voice messages, intercoms and emails within 24 hours.</li>
<li>
Communicates with attorneys, trustees, investors and Loan Resolution daily.</li>
<li>
Manages vendor and attorney network to ensure files are worked in accordance with all investor, insurer, state and regulatory guidelines.</li>
<li>
Escalates matters to team lead to ensure resolution.
</li>
<li>Monitors and manages exception reports.</li>
<li>
Stays informed of industry changes as related to servicing activities.</li>
<li>
Possesses a general understanding of the different legal processes to foreclose in our various states.</li>
<li>
Possesses a general understanding of mortgage loan servicing and the default, foreclosure, bankruptcy and loss mitigation processes.</li>
<li>
Work closely with Company management to insure client satisfaction.</li>
<li>
Communicates all client policy and procedure changes to Company management.</li>
<li>
Research and resolve any escalations/inquiries brought by management (internal and client)</li>
<li>
Provide feedback to management (internal and client) on any process suggestions, client concerns heard on floor (internal), or any system issues.</li>
<li>
This is not a work from home opportunity.</li>
</ul>
