<h2>Senior Competitive Intelligence Manager - Enterprise Analytics</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<div>The Senior Competitive Intelligence Manager – Enterprise Analysis will report to the Director of Competitive Intelligence and will be responsible for ongoing evaluation and analysis of business trends to develop competitive and financial outcome scenarios. He/she will partner with the Cox Automotive Enterprise Strategy, BU Financial Planning and Analysis and Competitive Intelligence/Research teams to stand up and deliver this work which will inform leadership on implications to long range plans and potential investment opportunities. In addition to keeping a pulse on key competitive activity, the Senior Competitive Intelligence Manager – Enterprise Analysis and will be responsible for delivering competitive landscape insights, strategic analyses and actionable insights that help direct Cox Automotive’s short and long-term strategic roadmap. He/she will also lead and provide ongoing expertise and execution in the areas of financial modeling, forecasting and market analysis in support of the CASG department and executive leadership. The Senior Manager will responsible for developing, executing and maintaining competitive intelligence data visualization, intelligence repository and content distribution tools and platforms.<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<div>Job Responsibilities:</div>
<ul>
<li>Analyzing companies, industries, historical financial statements and prospective financial information</li>
<li>
Responsible for driving quarterly earnings call process and liaising with Cox Auto FP&amp;A teams for cross-BU financial comparison</li>
<li>
Perform competitive analyses of competitor businesses by leveraging insights from quarterly earnings calls and other market information</li>
<li>
Development of insights from analyses and reporting activities to provide timely and actionable recommendations
</li>
<li>Drive credible and data-backed influence on the decision-making process within a collaborative and innovative environment</li>
<li>
Define processes and operational best practices related to financial and strategic analyses, adapting organizational requirements as needs change</li>
<li>
Demonstrate thought leadership and subject matter expertise of financial analysis, modeling and forecasting, serving as a trusted advisor to Cox Automotive Enterprise Strategy, Sales, Product and Marketing by building alignment on strategic opportunities and interpretation of competitive threats, and making recommendations that help influence and drive the direction of Cox Automotive</li>
<li>
Develop and maintain competitive landscape to support likely M&amp;A activities, due diligence, market sizing/share and LRP risks/opportunities</li>
<li>
Drive development of competitive inputs for long-range strategic, annual operating, and business development planning</li>
<li>
Support Strategy &amp; Enterprise Groups with deep understanding of competitive and marketplace trends; conveying key insights, current and potential capabilities, strategic implications and recommend courses of action</li>
<li>
Execute competitive intelligence data strategy and lead the development of analytics capabilities (Dashboards, Intelligence portal(s), etc.). Strategically evaluates data to extract key business implications.</li>
<li>
Effectively conveys strategic implications and recommended course of action</li>
<li>
Support Cox Automotive with deep understanding of competitive and marketplace trends, including:</li>
<ul>
<li>
Product Management / Sales: Understand competitors’ business models, resources and capabilities; Marketing: Understand competitors’ go-to-market strategies, brand positioning, product packaging and pricing strategy</li>
<li>
Enterprise Strategy: Understand competitive landscape to effectively project likely M&amp;A activities, market share and long-term profit pool scenarios as inputs into corporate-wide strategic planning efforts</li>
</ul>
<li>
Establish a strong network, including key industry groups, clients and experts (internal and external)</li>
<li>
Support ad hoc competitive research requests and assume other responsibilities as they arise</li>
</ul>
