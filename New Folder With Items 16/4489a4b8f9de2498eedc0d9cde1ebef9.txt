<h2>Bookkeeper</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Paul Davis Restoration is a full-service contractor that provides services to repair and clean up damage to residential and commercial properties from fire, water, mold, storm or other disasters. With a culture of integrity and quality, Paul Davis Restoration is at the top of the property restoration industry, and interested in adding another talented proven professional to the team.</b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
BASIC FUNCTIONS</b>: Professionally represent the company mission statement, vision and values while maintaining the financial and accounting records of the company. Utilize established accounting and clerical procedures.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
RESPONSIBILITIES/TASKS</b>:</p>
<ul>
<li>Utilize accounting and project management software (RMS, QuickBooks, TSheets)</li>
<li>Comply with local, state and federal government reporting requirements</li>
<li>Maintain employee and personnel files</li>
<li>Process accounts payable</li>
<li>Process accounts receivable and collections</li>
<li>Process payroll and remit payroll taxes</li>
<li>Perform job cost analysis</li>
<li>Office supply inventory</li>
<li>Remit sales and use taxes (Accountant prepared)</li>
<li>Reconcile bank balances and make bank deposits</li>
<li>Perform month end close</li>
<li>Process quarterly and year-end government reporting</li>
<li>Monitor office and overhead expenses</li>
<li>Miscellaneous job duties as directed</li>
<br>
</ul>
