<h2>Account Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Launch your career forward with a unique opportunity to play a vital role at a dynamic aerospace components manufacturer. At the HORST Engineering Family of Companies, the sky isn’t the limit. It’s just the beginning. For more than seventy years we have manufactured components that keep aircraft flying. With relentless precision and quality, we continuously improve and learn new ways to propel aerospace and the other high technology industries upward. And we make it all happen as a family. As a third generation family business, our commitment to our community has been the cornerstone of our success. We aren’t just made in the USA, we are made by the USA. And together, we lift you up.</p>
<p>The aerospace sector is experiencing fantastic growth and investment. We manufacture precision parts for some of the best OEM customers in the industry. Join a modern company that makes the critical hardware that helps keep commercial and military planes, helicopters, and rockets in the air. We help people fly safely.</p>
<p><b>Why join our team?</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Work for an established 3rd generation family business with a strong Core Purpose and Core Values.</li>
<li>Work for a Senior Leadership Team that has established a multi-year growth plan</li>
<li>Engage with dynamic professional leadership that vigorously supports innovation.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Job Summary: </b></p>
<p>Develop long-term relationships with a portfolio of customers, connecting with key business stakeholders. Liaise between customers and cross-functional internal teams to ensure the timely and successful delivery of our solutions according to customer needs. Manage and develop customer accounts to initiate and maintain favorable relationship. Prepare and manage RFQ and business case proposals and present content to senior leadership. Work closely with customers to identify needs and consult on best practices for solutions. Train and help develop direct and indirect support staff.</p>
<p><b>Responsibilities: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Be the primary point of contact and build long-term relationships with customers</li>
<li>Help customers through email, phone, online presentations, screen-share and in person meetings</li>
<li>Develop a trusted advisor relationship with key accounts, customer stakeholders and executive sponsors</li>
<li>Ensure the timely and successful delivery of our solutions according to customer needs and objectives</li>
<li>Communicate clearly the progress of monthly/quarterly initiatives to internal and external stakeholders</li>
<li>Forecast and track key account metrics</li>
<li>Update job knowledge by participating in educational opportunities; reading professional publications; maintaining personal networks; participating in professional organizations</li>
<li>Enhance department and organization’s reputation by accepting ownership for accomplishing new and different requests; exploring opportunities to add value to job accomplishments</li>
<li>Responsible for keeping current clients satisfied and delivering exceptional client service on a day-to-day basis</li>
<li>Monitor and analyze customer’s product delivery, contract execution and identify future opportunity</li>
<li>Responsible for working with the Sales team to onboard and integrate new clients and developing existing client relationships</li>
<li>Liaise between the customer and internal team</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Requirements: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Proven account management or other relevant experience</li>
<li>Demonstrated ability to communicate, present and influence credibly and effectively at all levels of the organization</li>
<li>Experience in delivering client-focused solutions based on customer needs</li>
<li>Proven ability to manage multiple projects at a time while paying strict attention to detail and deadlines</li>
<li>Excellent listening, negotiation and presentation skills</li>
<li>Excellent verbal and written communications skills</li>
<li>Bachelor’s Degree in appropriate field of study or equivalent work experience</li>
<li>Deep digital understanding</li>
<li>Self-motivated and able to thrive in a results-driven environment</li>
<li>Natural relationship builder with sincerity, reliability and maturity</li>
<li>Ability to prioritize among competing tasks</li>
<li>Critical thinking and problem-solving skills</li>
<li>Understanding of measurement and analytics, internet technology, Microsoft Office Suite, Google Apps, software applications and Epicore experience preferred</li>
<li>Excellent time and project management skills. You’re always looking to improve inefficient processes</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Preferred Qualifications: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>5+ years of Aviation &amp; Aerospace experience specifically</li>
<li>5+ years working on cross-functional teams</li>
<li>5+ years working in a manufacturing environment</li>
<li>3+ years of Supplier portal experience and familiarization</li>
<li>3+ years working in ERP systems, preferably Epicore</li>
<li>Demonstrated knowledge of FAR &amp; DFAR requirements and government transactions</li>
</ul>
