<h2>Marketing Coordinator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>WXY is seeking a Marketing Coordinator with strong visual and communication skill to assist the firm with proposal production, development of marketing collateral, and related marketing activities. Working under the direction of the Marketing Manager, and in conjunction with the firm Principals and Directors. The ideal candidate must have strong organizational skills and be able to work as a team player under tight <i>deadline</i> situations. The candidate will know current graphic layout software and have proficient writing skills. The position requires the ability to communicate effectively and collaborate in an interdisciplinary environment. The candidate will be <i>part</i> of the Marketing Team in a fast-paced, <i>deadline</i>-driven atmosphere and must be able to <i>work</i> on multiple projects simultaneously.</p>
