<h2>Product Engineer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p><b><i>Core Competencies ? Knowledge, Education, Skills and Abilities</i></b></p>
<ul>
<li>Associates or bachelor?s degree in mechanical engineering</li>
<li>Excellent project management skills</li>
<li>Ability to work in a cross-functional development team</li>
<li>3-5 years of experience with 2D and 3D CAD package (AutoCAD and SolidWorks preferred)</li>
<li>Experience with product data management software (EPDM preferred)</li>
<li>Working knowledge of manufacturing processes (cold-forming, stamping, plastics, etc.)</li>
<li>Experience with DFMEA and CAE/Simulation a plus<b>
</b>
</li>
<li>Professional written and verbal communication and organizational skills<b>
</b>
</li>
<li>Experience in continuous improvement strategies a plus</li>
</ul>
</div>

<div>
<p><b><i>Essential Responsibilities</i></b><b>
</b></p>
<p><b>Project Management</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Manage the design, testing, introduction and field support of new products while maintaining a project schedule</li>
<li>Project Liaison between customer and internal/external resources (VOC).</li>
<li>Acquire vendor quotes and develop costing</li>
<li>Assist in the design and manufacturing of new product prototypes for research and development</li>
<li>Design, construct and coordinate in-house and 3rd party testing</li>
<li>Assist in the design and sourcing of appropriate inspection gaging for new products</li>
<li>Evaluate existing products for product improvements and cost reductions</li>
<li>Troubleshoot customer issues and manage engineering change requests</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
CAD</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
<b>Develop and Maintain Product Develop CAD Documents:</b> Utilize CAD software to document product development designs, gaging, sales &amp; marketing, and supporting items. Responsible for maintaining controlled documents at internal and external locations.</li>
<li>
<b>SolidWorks EPDM:</b> Develop, organize and maintain work flows within product development to support document storage objectives.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
ISO</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
<b>Document Control: </b>Maintain Engineering Drawing Log and distribute controlled documents (i.e. – prints, engineering specifications, work instructions) to internal and external locations.</li>
<li>
<b>SOP’s: </b>Assist in developing work instructions for all applicable areas.</li>
</ul>
