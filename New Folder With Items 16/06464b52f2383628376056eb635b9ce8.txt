Position Summary
<br><br>
This position is an Individual Contributor and reports to the Senior Director of Core Applications Product Management.
<br><br>
Responsibilities
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Create useful, intuitive features that help grow the use of DocuSign's Sending and Signing applications</li>
<li>Collaborate across internal partners (Sales, Account Management, Support, Engineering, and Marketing) as well as external customers to understand challenges/requirements for innovative solutions that help customers get to agreement faster</li>
<li>Review competitors and vendors in adjacent spaces for feature requirements and comparisons to ensure a competitive and relevant product</li>
<li>Prioritize and manage the feature backlog for effective release and sprint planning</li>
<li>Evaluate trade-off decisions across the customer experience, development and operational support in partnership with teams</li>
<li>Drive the triage process for incoming bugs and maintain a healthy list of bugs to be resolved in maintenance queue in partnership with engineering and QA</li>
<li>Monitor and escalate high priority support issues including Go/No Go on release and feature/product customer readiness</li>
<li>Continuously monitor, measure and report on key business and customer metrics. Identify areas for continuous testing and optimization</li>
<li>Work closely with Customer Support, Customer Experience, and Marketing to launch new capabilities. Responsible for feature product marketing for smaller less strategic features</li>
</ul>
Basic Qualifications
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>5 years of experience in product management for an enterprise cloud/SaaS product/service or a combination of product management and engineering or management consulting working on solving large-scale technology issues.</li>
<li>Bachelor's degree OR 3+ years and a Master's degree and/or Bachelor's or Master's degree in Computer Science, Electrical Engineering, Information Systems, Business Systems, or a related Engineering discipline</li>
</ul>
Preferred Qualifications
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>MBA strongly preferred</li>
<li>Experience taking a product from concept to launch</li>
<li>Testing and Analysis: Prior experience applying qualitative and quantitative testing to feature development</li>
<li>Process: Direct experience in delivering web capabilities using iterative or Agile methodologies (e.g. SCRUM), including modelling experience using task/storyboards, user stories or use cases, etc.; working knowledge of testing/QA fundamentals</li>
<li>Superior analytical and problem-solving skills enabling the candidate to align strategy, customer need, research, data and industry trends</li>
<li>Strong written and verbal communications skills</li>
<li>Experience working in cross-functional teams and ability to influence others</li>
<li>User Experience: Prior experience applying usability principles and practices, including early and rapid prototyping, usability testing, cross-browser/platform, and design constraints on the web and designing for accessibility and SEO</li>
<li>Tools: Experience with, or ability to quickly learn, tools such as Mixpanel, Optimizely, Google Analytics, Salesforce.com, Azure, JIRA (or other SCRUM tools)</li>
</ul>

About DocuSign
<br><br>
DocuSign® is changing how business gets done by empowering hundreds of thousands of companies and tens of millions of users in most countries around the world to sign, send and manage documents anytime, anywhere, on any device with confidence. DocuSign replaces printing, faxing, scanning and overnighting documents with the easiest, fastest, most trusted way to make every approval and decision digital. Organizations of all sizes and industries are accelerating contracts, approvals and workflows with DocuSign's Digital Transaction Management (DTM) platform and eSignature solution. DocuSign keeps life and business moving forward.
