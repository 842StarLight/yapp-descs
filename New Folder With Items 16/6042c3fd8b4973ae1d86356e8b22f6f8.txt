<h2>Content Curator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>THE DEETS: </b><br>Ledge Lounger in-pool and outdoor furniture and accessories can be seen worldwide at high-end pools, resorts, and homes. Experiencing rapid growth, we’ve recently been honored for the second consecutive year both on Inc Magazine’s 5000<br>Fastest Growing Companies in America, as well as Houston Business Journal’s Fast<br>100.</p>
<p>The Marketing Content Curator will join our fast-paced team and balance a multitude of platforms from social media, email, collaborations, campaigns, print collateral and more. This role focuses on strategy, goals, and brand development, and reports directly to the Brand Strategist.</p>
<p><b>WARNING: </b><br>We move quickly. We set high standards, then challenge ourselves to exceed expectations. We’re candid in the pursuit of greatness. We’re looking for an individual that is charismatic and intuitive with the ability to persuade through an influential communication style. This person is ready to grow creatively and challenge themselves day in and day out. If you’re looking for a job where you can meet, rather than exceed the status quo, this might not be the place for you.</p>
<p><b>THE DAY-TO-DAY: </b><br><b>- </b>Ability to create, produce, edit, and publish photo and video content<br>- Maintain brand consistency across all photo and video content<br>- Conceive and produce engaging and compelling content for all channels daily<br>- Write articles and blog posts on a variety of Ledge Lounger related topics<br>- Optimize all content in terms of SEO, social platforms, and engagement<br>- Regularly seek out, organize, and capture opportunities for content<br>- Utilize analytic tools to review content performance consistently<br>- Ideate strategic social partnerships and brand development for growth opportunities<br>- Leverage data, insights, trends and best practices to represent brand authenticity, voice, tone<br>- Work closely with Graphic Designer to maintain the highest standards in visuals and creative<br>- Partner with Sales Team to up-funnel extensions for strategic sponsorships, collaborations, and campaigns<br>- Collaborate during photoshoot process from start to finish</p>
<p><b>THE ESSENTIALS: </b><br><b>- </b>Camera and tech savvy. Capable of handling all aspects of a campaign from shooting, to editing, to sharing the content on social</p>
<p><b>- </b>Photo and video experience ideal<br><b>- </b>Solid understanding of what makes a story relevant and click-worthy, while still being on-brand<br><b>- </b>Open to working some nights and weekends, as social media is always on<br><b>- </b>Advanced knowledge of social, digital, and emerging media<br><b>- </b>Passion, knowledge, and a good sense regarding lifestyle topics<br><b>- </b>Ability to track trends to identify new, innovative ways to evolve strategy, grow audience, and drive meaningful conversations<br><b>- </b>Understanding of consumer behavior, calls to action, and general marketing concepts<br><b>- </b>Knowledge of design concepts and their effect on marketing, click through and call to action<br><b>- </b>Understanding of branding, layout, color theory and typography in both print and digital media<br><b>- </b>Strong communication skills and copywriting ability with a solid understanding of the Ledge Lounger voice<br><b>- </b>Passionate about driving content, community, and commerce<br><b>- </b>Organized and resourceful with strong attention to detail<br><b>- </b>Ability to innovate, think outside the box, and bring creative ideas to the table<br><b>- </b>Ability to multi-task, balance several projects, and manage deadlines in a fast-paced environment<br><b>- </b>A clean, stylish design aesthetic<br><b>- </b>Self-starter who will seek out opportunities for growth and challenges<br><b>- </b>Must be open to coaching, advice, and collaboration from team members<br><b>- </b>Must be proficient in Adobe Creative Suite including Photoshop and After Effects<br><b>- </b>College degree in marketing, advertising, communications, or related field of study<br><b>- </b>Will be required to lift up to 10lbs with ease<br><b>- </b>Some standing, moving, climbing, bending, kneeling, and handling is required</p>
