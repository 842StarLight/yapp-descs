<p><b>
Summary/Objective:</b><br>
This position is primarily responsible for application support on Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP) and local ancillary systems with interfaces included. The main goal is to support business operations and make sure they are running smoothly and effectively. This position is responsible for managing ERP and related tickets and change requests with prioritization, tracking, and escalation. The position delivers these objectives by collaborating with internal, external, local and global resources.</p>
<p><b>
Essential Functions and projects:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Collaborates with internal, external, local resources on ERP and related support.</li>
<li>Actively participates in ERP implementation or Change Request (CRs).</li>
<li>Collaborates with business to fully understand business processes, identify business issues, and recommend opportunities for improvement.</li>
<li>Understands business processes and ERP core functions to troubleshoot.</li>
<li>Responsible for documentation and knowledge transfers.</li>
<li>Responsible for change management and communication.</li>
<li>Works flexible hours and weekends to meet business/customer needs.</li>
</ul>
