<h2>Site Director</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>BCS FM is a Data Center management firm managing a data center located in Richmond, VA. We are looking for a local professional to join our team, serving one of our valued Virginia clients.</p>
<p><b>SUMMARY</b></p>
<p>The Site Director (SD) is charged with providing the leadership, tactical execution and business acumen necessary to support the diverse site needs. The Site Director coordinates, synthesizes and integrates all aspects of corporate real estate management, financial analysis, and maintenance. The SD coordinates and synthesizes maintenance contracting for service to critical equipment, as well as master-planning for the facility’s data center floor and new construction. SD has responsibility for the local Management Team of the facility which oversees maintenance, physical security, data center operations and business continuity/disaster recovery procedures for the property.</p>
<p>The SD has profit and loss (P&amp;L) responsibility and accountability for their assigned site(s). The SD owns and implements the strategy and associated programs necessary to improve the P&amp;L. The SD maintains a strong command of every program and item contributing to profits or expenses. The SD is responsible for communicating to the site team the status and outlook for the P&amp;L and ensuring alignment of activities necessary to achieve P&amp;L goals. The SD sources new ideas from site team organization and leverages external parties, suppliers, advisers and consultants to improve profitability.</p>
<p><i>To perform this job successfully, an individual must be able to perform each of the essential duties and responsibilities listed below with or without reasonable accommodation. The requirements listed below are representative, but not all-inclusive, of the knowledge, skill, and/or ability required. </i></p>
<p><b>ESSENTIAL DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Manages the site team, develops capabilities, and sets policies necessary to satisfy customers, engage employees, and achieve financial objectives.</li>
<li>Implements operating standards required for cost control, waste reduction, quality, safety, and to be an environmentally-friendly community partner.</li>
<li>Manages expenditures, staffing, and capital projects to ensure compliance with financial goals. Oversees site operations and capital expense budgets to ensure compliance with organizational expenditure requirements.</li>
<li>Provides leadership in conjunctions with local managers (DCO, FAC, Security) and remote line of business owners to ensure operational standards are achieved.</li>
<li>Manages operations, space planning, power and cooling services, equipment installations, physical security, maintenance, janitorial services, landscaping, business continuity/disaster recovery for site(s). Confers with professional staff Center of Excellence (COE) to find resolution to operational problems, authorizes operational procedures and approves all operating expenditures.</li>
<li>Understands and implements federal, state and local operating regulations or laws. Establishes, implements, and maintains facilities policies, procedures and organizational structures necessary to maintain compliance with all laws and regulations. Obtains and keeps current all necessary licenses, permits and certifications as required.</li>
<li>Drives innovation into facilities programs/systems with an environmentally conscious attitude.</li>
<li>Negotiates contracts with suppliers. Develops and coordinates maintenance agreements, maintenance procedures, testing procedures, inspections, reporting, and utilizes best accounting practices.</li>
<li>Supervises the preparation of and implementation of effective operating budget.</li>
<li>Leads in the community. Participates in community activities and organizations as appropriate through the maintenance of professional association affiliations and participation within association programs.</li>
<li>Maintains positive relationships and communication with staff and executive level management.</li>
<li>Ensures proper training and growth of staff.</li>
<li>Participates on acquisition teams to assess and integrate new facilities when required.</li>
<li>Interfaces and works with sales team on new customer planning, changes and implementation.</li>
<li>Works with other COE Managers to set standards and to constantly look for ways to improve.</li>
<li>Consistently informs V.P. and upper Management of customer-affecting situations and provides monthly reports and metrics as required.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>SUPERVISORY RESPONSIBILITIES</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Formally supervises individual employees and/or subordinate supervisors/managers on site(s).</li>
<li>Assigns staff appropriately for proper coverage of the Data Center.</li>
<li>Responsible for promotion of growth opportunities and development needs of the current DCO staff; and making recommendations for hiring and firing.</li>
<li>Responsible for providing annual reviews and continuous feedback as it relates to job performance.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Competencies</b></p>
<ul>
<li>The successful candidate must have strategic agility and good decision-making skills. They should also possess the ability to build and lead teams and to work well in a collaborative, team-oriented environment. The role requires keen planning skills along with the ability to lead through influence and to be a proactive partner with a strong customer-service orientation. This leader should be able to solve problems at a tactical and functional level, have excellent oral and written communication skills, a solid work ethic and unquestioned integrity.</li>
<li>Must have excellent communication skills (verbal/written), organization, and strong customer service. Good grammar and the ability to take accurate notes are required.</li>
<li>Must be capable of handling multiple simultaneous tasks under pressure while maintaining strong customer service and professionalism. Ability to maintain composure and calm demeanor under pressure.</li>
<li>Flexibility, including the willingness and ability to readily respond to changing circumstances and expectations. Openness to new ideas and procedures.</li>
<li>Proven leadership, management, interpersonal, negotiation, organizational, analytic, and communication skills. The ability to build and lead teams and to work well in a collaborative, team-oriented environment. Ability to manage projects effectively.</li>
<li>The ability to lead through influence and be a proactive partner with a strong customer service orientation who builds relationships by building trust.</li>
<li>Solid work ethic and integrity, able to work effectively and be self-directed.</li>
</ul>
