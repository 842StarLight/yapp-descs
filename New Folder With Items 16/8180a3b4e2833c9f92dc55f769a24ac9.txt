<div><div><div>
<ul>
<li>Exceed quarterly sales targets by selling full stack Microsoft Cloud solutions into key accounts and within an assigned geography or market</li>

Territory identification and research in an effort to formalize a go-to-market strategy for your designated territory, creating a qualified target account list</li>

Manage the end-to-end sales cycle through the engagement of appropriate resources including but not limited to; Business Development Managers, Inside Sales Executives, Solution Specialists, and Cloud Support</li>

Continuous improvement in self-research, learning and readiness on the new and existing SoftwareONE product offerings</li>
<li>
