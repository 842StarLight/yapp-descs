<p><b>Our Company</b></p>
<p>Lavu is at the forefront of transforming the way that restaurants are managed through its mobile Point of Sale (mPOS). Lavu provides mobile and cloud-based POS solutions for fine-dining and quick serve restaurants, franchises, bars, coffee shops, pizzerias and food trucks within 90 countries. Our mission is to partner with restaurant owners with the knowledge, solutions, and expertise that accelerate their growth and success, while tailoring innovative solutions that advance the restaurant industry.</p>
<p><b>Position Description</b></p>
<p>As a Senior Full-Stack Developer, you will be a significant contributor to our development team, helping innovate functionality within Lavu?s iPad-based restaurant point-of-sale platform. You will also help us evolve our back-end code into a language-agnostic REST/JSON-based micro-services model, develop our next generation of internal &amp; external APIs, and expand our in-app functionality with new payment processing integrations and features to help our customers manage and run their restaurants with ease.</p>
