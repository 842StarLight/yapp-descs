<h2>Account Manager for Richmond and Surrounding Area</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>Account Manager for Richmond and Surrounding Area<br>
<br>
Duties and Responsibilities
<br>
<ul>
<li>Interview and hire qualified applicants to fill open post positions following company guidelines and regularly evaluate employee performance.
</li>
<li>Maintain consistent and regular communication with client contacts and maintain a strong partnership to ensure clients expected security needs are met.
</li>
<li>Report to client sites to investigate security incidents or concerns.
</li>
<li>Create, modify, and manage schedules, ensuring adequate coverage.
</li>
<li>Ensure all positions are staffed per contract requirements, and effectively manage overtime through proper scheduling and staffing. Maintain a qualified pool of rovers for backfill of vacant positions as needed.
</li>
<li>Conduct payroll verification.
</li>
<li>Maintain proactive contact with Security Officers to ensure all employees are being effectively utilized, properly trained, and ready for placement as needed.
</li>
<li>Maintain high employee morale and low employee turnover through effective and proactive communications and timely problem resolution. Ensure all hiring, counseling, disciplinary and termination procedures are executed in a consistent manner.
</li>
<li>Ensure a smooth-running operation by enforcing post-specific policies and procedures through proper communications and training.
</li>
<li>Participates in operations meetings and attends other meetings and seminars.
</li>
<li>Approve or disapprove application for leave request on your weekly schedule and ensure that all paid time off request is immediately submitted to payroll upon approval.
</li>
<li>Manages and partners with the Assistant Account Manager.<br>
</li>
</ul>

Requirements
<br>
<ul>
<li>Minimum high school diploma or equivalent required. Bachelor’s Degree in Business or a related field preferred. Prior security industry and/or military experience preferred.
</li>
<li>Minimum 3 – 5 years of professional-level experience required, managing employees in a fast-paced, service organization. Prior experience in the security industry, law enforcement, military and/or customer service highly desired. Experience in scheduling, operations and employee management in a service-related industry a significant plus.
</li>
<li>Must be able to secure a valid Department of Criminal Justice Service (DCJS) Guard License.
</li>
<li>Requires a current Virginia state driver’s license, and the ability to safely operate a vehicle. May be required to drive to client sites to address scheduling or employee issues on an as-needed basis; and must provide own reliable transportation.
</li>
<li>Ability to write effective and concise reports and document investigations and counseling sessions. Must be able to effectively communicate coaching, counseling and disciplinary actions, as well as termination meetings in a professional manner in accordance with company standards.
</li>
<li>Demonstrate working knowledge of personal computers and Microsoft Office software. Ability to learn and efficiently operate various timekeeping and payroll programs such as WinTeam, eHub and CompleteReport
</li>
<li>Energetic and focused personality with a demonstrated ability to take initiative, successfully handle and prioritize multiple competing assignments, and effectively manage deadlines. Must demonstrate a high level of integrity, and apply rules, policies and employment actions in a consistent, fair and equitable manner in all situations.
</li>
<li>Professional, articulate and able to use independent judgement and discretion.
</li>
<li>Excellent verbal and written communication skills required.
</li>
<li>Must be able to maintain appropriate confidentiality.
</li>
</ul>
