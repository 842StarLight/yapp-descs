<h2>Cyber Risk Product Marketing Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p><b>Job Description
</b></p>
<p><b>The Opportunity
</b></p>
<p><i>Are you a software product marketer looking to take the next step in you career? Do you take pride in working with others to identify new sales opportunities? Are you looking for a new challenge? If so, this Product Marketing Manager position may be a fit for you. Intrigued? Read more….
</i></p>
<p></p>
<p><i>In this role you will help craft product marketing strategies, conduct competitive research, and create compelling content that supports FICO’s cybersecurity products. The ideal candidates will have a proven ability to learn new skills, markets and technologies quickly, and must have a willingness to roll up their sleeves to get things done. In this role you will work collaboratively with the product management, sales, engineering, and other marketing teams.
</i></p>
<p></p>
<p><b>What You’ll Contribute
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Write and manage the development of collateral using internal and external resources.
</li>
<li>Work with sales and other internal stakeholders to develop and refine go-to-market strategies.
</li>
<li>Conduct competitive analysis and research customer insights that inform product positioning and external communications.
</li>
<li>Help cultivate a social media presence.
</li>
<li>Support FICO events and industry tradeshows.
</li>
<li>Develop ideas for new sales tools focused on clearing buying obstacles
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>What We’re Seeking
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Undergraduate degree in marketing, business, communications or related field required.
</li>
<li>Working knowledge of cybersecurity principles.
</li>
<li>Experience marketing analytical software.
</li>
<li>Extensive software marketing experience.
</li>
<li>Extraordinary communication skills – both written and oral. Recent writing samples will be a required part of the interview process.
</li>
<li>A proven ability to convey complex ideas simply and clearly in written, presentation and spoken formats.
</li>
<li>Strong attention to detail.
</li>
<li>A track record of crafting product collateral and thought leadership content.
</li>
<li>Knowledge of consultative sales methodologies a plus.
</li>
<li>Willingness to adapt in a dynamic environment.
</li>
<li>Works well with others.
</li>
<li>Ability to travel up to 10% throughout US with possibility of international travel.
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Our Offer to You
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>A culture and work environment strongly reflecting our core values: Act Like an Owner, Delight Our Customers and Earn the Respect of Others.
</li>
<li>The opportunity to make a difference by leveraging your unique strengths.
</li>
<li>Highly competitive compensation and rewards.
</li>
<li>Flexible work options, opportunities to give back to your community, social events with colleagues and a comprehensive benefits program inclusive of progressive parental leave.</li>
</ul>
