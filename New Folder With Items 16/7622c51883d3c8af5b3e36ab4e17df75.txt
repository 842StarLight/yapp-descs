<h2>Account Executive</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div></div>
<div>
<p><b>Position Objective</b></p>
<p>
Accountable for developing and delivering strategic benefit solutions that meets the needs of Lockton clients under the general guidance of the Team Lead and/or Unit Manager and in accordance with Lockton procedures.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Essential Duties</b></p>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li><p>
Conduct effective quarterly Gate meetings with clients</p></li>
<li><p>
Provide thought leadership regarding benefit strategies</p></li>
<li><p>
Maintain strong client relationships in both professional and personal settings</p></li>
<li><p>
Establish and maintain carrier-partner relationships</p></li>
<li><p>
Maintain a thorough knowledge of products available in the market</p></li>
<li><p>
Monitor and analyze the financial position of each line of coverage throughout the year</p></li>
<li><p>
Prepare cost projections throughout the year and at renewal</p></li>
<li><p>
Utilize InfoLock data in managing overall benefit and HRS strategies</p></li>
<li><p>
Integrate HRS concepts into benefits strategies</p></li>
<li><p>
Determine when and why coverages will be marketed</p></li>
<li><p>
Initiate and manage entire renewal process in a timely manner</p></li>
<li><p>
Manage the bid solicitation process for all lines of coverage</p></li>
<li><p>
Analyze the financial and service aspects of vendor proposals</p></li>
<li><p>
Effectively integrate Financial Analyst into Client Service Team as directed</p></li>
<li><p>
Provide recommendations based on marketing results</p></li>
<li><p>
Identify opportunities for business expansion/growth.</p></li>
<li><p>
Negotiate terms and conditions with carriers</p></li>
<li><p>
Provide guidance to account team during coverage implementation</p></li>
<li><p>
Identify opportunities and gaps in coverage</p></li>
<li><p>
Proactively deliver regulatory and compliance support to clients</p></li>
<li><p>
Provide insight and recommendation on employee benefit communication campaigns</p></li>
<li><p>
Ensure client deliverables are peer reviewed prior to delivery</p></li>
<li><p>
Conduct benefit audit for prospects</p></li>
<li><p>
Participate in formal presentations to prospective and current clients, as well as insurance vendors</p></li>
<li><p>
Efficiently manage the utilization of company resources and talent</p></li>
<li><p>
Develop mutually beneficial relationships with:</p></li>
<li><p>
Insurance Carriers</p></li>
<li><p>
External Clients</p></li>
<li><p>
Lockton Associates and Producers</p></li>
<li><p>
Initiate involvement of Unit Manager and other Lockton resources as needed</p></li>
<li><p>
Assist in development of other associates by identifying additional skills, training needs, etc.</p></li>
<li><p>
Possess strong presentation skills, including preparation, execution, and delivery of materials</p></li>
<li><p>
Continually develop understanding of evolving funding options, underwriting principles, and pricing</p></li>
<li><p>
Analyze complex data, interpret, and communicate findings</p></li>
<li><p>
Listen to clients and colleagues, provide meaningful feedback, and collaborate to identify problems and solutions</p></li>
<li><p>
Share subject matter expertise in development of other associates</p></li>
<li><p>
Mentor and coach others</p></li>
<li><p>
Provide strategic planning and consulting advice to clients, prospects, and team members</p></li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<div></div>
<div>
<div><b>Qualifications</b></div>
<div></div>
<ul>
<li><div>
Bachelor's degree or equivalent education and/or experience</div></li>
<li><div>
Advanced working knowledge of the employee benefits industry</div></li>
<li><div>
Current resident-state license, or ability to obtain same immediately</div></li>
<li><div>
Substantial experience in marketing and servicing the insurance employer sponsored benefit plans</div></li>
<li><div>
Demonstrate ability to instill confidence and comfort with clients so client looks to AE and to Lockton as more than just and insurance broker, but a partner in business</div></li>
<li><div>
Organizational and time management skills to prioritize heavy workloads to meet time sensitive deadlines</div></li>
<li><div>
Strong knowledge of Microsoft products (Word, Excel, PowerPoint, Outlook) or similar software applications</div></li>
<li><div>
Ability to compile, analyze, and interpret financial information and data to facilitate decision making</div></li>
<li><div>
Develop and maintain a working knowledge of Presentation Master</div></li>
<li><div>
Demonstrated professional, tactful negotiation and persuasion skills to achieve objectives</div></li>
<li><div>
Ability to make decisions in compliance with established Lockton standards of quality performance and service</div></li>
<li><div>
Personal presence to interface with top management of other businesses, representing Lockton in then highest professional manner</div></li>
<li><div>
Demonstrated understanding of when to proceed alone and when to involve the Producer or others</div></li>
<li><div>
Ability and willingness to proactively coach and mentor other Associates</div></li>
<li><div>
Understanding and executing superior service and relationship in accordance with Lockton quality procedures and performance standards</div></li>
<li><div>
Ability to provide clear direction and act as leader of account team</div></li>
<li><div>
Ability to travel by automobile and aircraft, and stay for more than one day and night</div></li>
</ul>
