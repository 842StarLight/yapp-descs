<h2>Salesforce Solution Architect/Dev Lead</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div><div>As Penumbra's Salesforce Solutions Architect, you will bring your Salesforce knowledge and experience with the eCommerce development process in an agile environment to the task of building solutions to enable the execution of our business strategy. By working to improve software capability and functionality, supporting the relationship with the global sales and marketing organizations, utilizing proactive account management strategies, and developing strong business relationships,</div></div>
<div><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>What You'll Achieve</b></h3>
<div><ul>
<li>
Take the lead on new Salesforce projects, from initial interaction with clients during the requirements discovery phase through implementation and deployment of the completed project.</li>
<li>
Lead the technical direction during the design and development processes by driving decisions and coordinating efforts.</li>
<li>
Calculate and communicate accurate development estimates based on high-level business and technical requirements.</li>
<li>
Provide insight and effectively communicate with internal and external stakeholders to demonstrate how the Salesforce Sales, Marketing, Service and Commerce Cloud B2B solution will be leveraged to meet client business demands on new projects.</li>
<li>
Drive the technical culture forward as a thought leader on the team, mentoring teams of engineers working on eCommerce solutions.</li>
</ul></div>
</div></div>
<div><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>What You Bring</b></h3>
<div><ul>
<li>
A keen interest in emerging technologies and how they can impact design decisions and strategic directions</li>
<li>
Experience engineering leading-edge systems for clients in a variety of industries and customer models</li>
<li>
Strong knowledge of integrations with back-end systems in the Salesforce landscape, including ERP, marketing systems, and Tableau</li>
<li>
Understanding of data structures, Apex classes, Visualforce pages, SQL SOQL, JavaScript, JQuery, RESTful, Custom UI (Bootstrap), and Salesforce Lightening.</li>
<li>
Hands-on experience building custom fields and objects, and with production launch readiness and cloud-based deployment models, permission sets, and field level security.
</li>
<li>Bachelor's degree in Computer Science or a related field, and at least 7 years of experience in Salesforce development.</li>
<li>
Salesforce Developer, Admin, Scrum Agile, and PMP certifications strongly desired.</li>
</ul></div>
</div></div>
<div><div>

<div><ul>
<li>
A collaborative teamwork environment where learning is constant and performance is rewarded.</li>
<li>
The opportunity to be at the forefront of technology that is revolutionizing the treatment of some of the world's most devastating diseases.</li>
<li>
A generous benefits package that includes medical, dental, vision, and life insurance; a 401(k) match; and an Employee Stock Purchase Plan.</li>
</ul></div>
</div></div>
<div>
<div>Penumbra, Inc., headquartered in Alameda, California, is a global healthcare company focused on innovative therapies. Penumbra designs, develops, manufactures and markets innovative products and has a broad portfolio that addresses challenging medical conditions and significant clinical needs across two major markets, neuro and vascular.
