<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Openings in Fargo &amp; Grand Forks</b></h2>
<p>We’re looking for great sales professionals to join our team. Our salespeople teach clients how to market their businesses using non-traditional media such as Indoor Advertising, Outdoor Digital, Social Media and Online Campaigns. You will learn how to develop and sell advertising and marketing campaigns to new and existing clients. Responsibilities include soliciting new business through prospecting and cold calling, managing and growing existing assigned accounts, identifying clients’ advertising needs and developing and presenting customized solutions to meet those needs. These individuals are also held accountable for achieving sales budgets and collecting on the accounts.</p>
<p><b>
Learning Objectives:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Learn how to ask questions to create strategy</li>
<li>Learn how organize your time to maximize productivity</li>
<li>Learn how to position products in the marketplace</li>
<li>Learn how to communicate with business decision makers about the marketing of their products to increase sales.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Job description:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Responsibilities include but are not limited to</li>
<li>Monitoring and planning strategic advertising/marketing plans specific to a business’ needs</li>
<li>Servicing existing accounts and prospecting new accounts in the Grand Forks or Fargo area</li>
<li>Working in a team environment to brainstorm campaigns and creative messaging for particular clients.</li>
<li>Monthly reporting of sales progress and sales goals</li>
<li>Perform analysis of marketing and sales data</li>
<li>Prepare presentations for prospective clients</li>
<li>Some day travel may be required</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Qualifications:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Sophomore, Junior or Senior in College</li>
<li>Good academic standing with minimum 3.0 Cumulative GPA</li>
<li>Must be working towards a degree in marketing, sales, advertising, communications or another related field</li>
<li>Past experience in Sales/Customer Service</li>
<li>Knowledge of Microsoft Office Suite</li>
<li>Knowledge of marketing and advertising tactics and best practices</li>
<li>Knowledge of different advertising mediums (ex. digital, social, print etc.)</li>
<li>Excellent verbal and written communication skills, outgoing personality, self-confidence and determination</li>
</ul>
<p>This is a paid internship.</p>
