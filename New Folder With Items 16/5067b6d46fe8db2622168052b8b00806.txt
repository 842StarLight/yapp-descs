<h2>Marketing Manager Beauty Products</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Basic Position Objective Statement:</b></p>
<p>
The incumbent responsibilities will be to manage the Graham Beauty product portfolio including creation and development of new products and as well as the development and implementation of an effective marketing strategy for Graham Beauty. This position will work closely with the National Sales Manager – Graham Beauty.</p>
<p><b>
Safety Philosophy:</b></p>
<p>
Little Rapids Corporation will manufacture products in a manner that respects the safety and health of our associates, the environment, and the communities in which we operate. We believe that each Associate plays critical role in creating a safe work environment by exhibiting personal safe behaviors. To be successful as a company, we will have exemplary safety performance.</p>
<p><b>
Principal Responsibilities and Accountabilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Develop a deep understanding of end-user customer base, their needs and behaviors through external research and market research and articulate those needs via active management of the product portfolio.</li>
<li>Lead the execution of the Graham Beauty Marketing including but not limited to all channel marketing and end user marketing execution points. This would include advertising, promotional programs, e-commerce channel activity, strategic marketing programs and distributor marketing programs.</li>
<li>Establish and manage Distributor and End User Pricing and Product Margins.</li>
<li>Lead the new product development process, with support from product development, manufacturing, sourcing and the business unit, from concept through finished product including product specifications, timeframes, cost, quality and packaging.</li>
<li>Develop and execute product Marketing plans for new products, product relaunches, category management and identifying and recommending new channel opportunities.</li>
<li>Establish and manage Distributor and End User Pricing and Product Margins.</li>
<li>Capitalize on new growth opportunities through analyses of competitors in terms of breadth and depth of product range and innovation, obtain and maintain current competitive information on pricing, policies, and products.</li>
<li>Recommend and implement cost reduction projects.</li>
<li>Manage social media &amp; company website, including Facebook, Instagram, etc.</li>
<li>Coordinate trade show setup and attend trade shows as required</li>
<li>Work in coordination with National Sales Manager – Graham Beauty to represent Graham Beauty at industry conferences and key customer meetings</li>
<li>Provide input for the preparation of yearly business plans and budgets for Graham Beauty.</li>
<li>Identify and maintain a file of targeted end-user groups.</li>
<li>Effectively work cross functionally with all LRC associates to motivate and lead a strong customer-centric culture in the business.</li>
<li>Effectively manage and utilize market research information, integrating the information to provide thorough analyses of evaluation and selection of new product opportunities.</li>
<li>Other assignments as made by management.</li>
</ul>
<p>This is not an exhaustive list of duties or functions and may not necessarily comprise all the “essential functions” for purposes of the ADA.</p>
<p><b>
Knowledge, Education and Training Qualifications and Certifications:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree in marketing or a closely related field strongly required.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Key Functional Competencies:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Minimum 3 years of progressively responsible marketing experience generating superior results.</li>
<li>Demonstrable experience in leading cross-functional teams with a proven track record of success in companies in rapidly changing environments and highly competitive industries.</li>
<li>Established ability to manage multiple projects simultaneously utilizing excellent interpersonal skills with an appreciation of cross-cultural differences, excellent communication, analytical and presentation skills and the ability to work across the entire LRC organization.</li>
<li>Beauty industry experience not required, but preferred.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Key Performance Competencies:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Results-oriented and strong decision maker with ability to prioritize complex organizational objectives and meet aggressive deadlines.</li>
<li>Excellent organizational, sales, communication, analytical, leadership and interpersonal skills</li>
<li>Regular attendance at work is required. Ability to work additional hours during the week or on weekends may be required.</li>
<li>Must have the ability to travel (air/land). Must have valid driver’s license.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Physical Requirements:</b></p>
<p>
The ability to work in a normal office environment to include: sitting or standing for long periods of time, operating various standard office equipment, carrying paper documents/files, reaching and bending, speaking on the telephone, walking through our manufacturing plant, and wearing required personal protection gear (i.e. hearing, eye and foot protection).</p>
