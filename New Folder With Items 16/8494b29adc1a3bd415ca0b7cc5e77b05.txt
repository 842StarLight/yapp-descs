<p><b>Leasing Specialist </b></p>
<p>Housing Helpers of Colorado is currently seeking to add a Leasing Specialist to join our team based out of our Colorado headquarters in Boulder CO. This is a unique opportunity to grow your career in real estate with the industry leader in leasing, property management, and real estate.</p>
<p>Housing Helpers Leasing Specialists gain an incredible amount of hands-on experience in a fun and fast paced environment. Last year, our rental specialists matched over 3,000 people to rental homes in Colorado. This job requires an outgoing personality and is aimed for those who love helping and meeting new people. You will be leasing a wide variety of single family homes, condos, and apartments in the Boulder County, Broomfield, North Denver Metro, and across Colorado.</p>
<p><b>About Housing Helpers</b></p>
<p>Housing Helpers of Colorado is a relocation services company providing integrated housing and real estate solutions which include: Corporate Housing, Real Estate Sales, Relocation Management, Property Management, Rental Locating, and Area Tours. Housing Helpers team members are local professionals that are committed to the Colorado communities where we live and work.</p>
<p>Housing Helpers of Colorado is proud to be a Top 100 Woman Owned Colorado company and a Top 250 Privately Owned Colorado Company.</p>
<p><b>Qualifications</b></p>
<ul>
<li>An outgoing and hardworking personality. As a Housing Helpers rental specialist you will be working with the public and should be comfortable connecting with all types of people.</li>
<li>Prior sales, apartment leasing, property management, hospitality, or customer service experience is beneficial.</li>
<li>Must have a presentable, clean, and insured vehicle.</li>
<li>The ability to work on Saturdays. Housing Helpers is open six days a week to serve our customers.</li>
<li>The ability to work on a commission sales structure based on your sales after an initial training period.</li>
<li>An understanding of the Boulder County, Broomfield, Westminster, and north Denver geography and neighborhoods is required.</li>
<li>Professional attire and appearance.</li>
<li>Clean background check and drivers license.</li>
<li>Excellent written and verbal communication skills.</li>
<li>Computer and smartphone/tablet proficiency.</li>
<li>Appfolio Property Management Software experience is a plus.</li>
</ul>
