<p>We are People Against Dirty, a growing house of brands that includes method, ecover, babyganics, and coming soon...mrs. meyers clean day. We've always done things differently – we’re fearless thinkers, mad scientists and adventurous designers who believe that making soap leads to brave ideas, bold inventions and beautiful bubbles. <p><b>
THE ROLE</b></p>
<p>
Based in San Francisco and reporting to the Brand Director - method + Ecover, this individual will develop and support execution of brand strategies, communication, as well as annual integrated plans to build brand awareness and drive revenue and growth.</p>
<p><b>
AREAS OF RESPONSIBILITY
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Support North American development of both method and Ecover brand strategies and plans that drive brand equity and growth, acting as brand champion and guardian across all brand communications (look/tone/feel, personality, values)</li>
<li>
Develop and execute inspiring and effective Brand Experience campaigns and activations, collaborating with key leaders in and around the Brand Experience teams (Insights, Media, PR, Social, Partnerships, Creative)</li>
<li>
Lead the development of brand toolkits as well as best in class creative, enabling brand consistency and flawless execution</li>
<li>
Act as brand voice and collaborate as thought partner with product and sales/category teams to ensure a unified execution of the brand across product, sales, and marketing</li>
<li>
Collaborate with product experience and business teams to define a successful innovation pipeline</li>
<li>
Live and breathe the company’s values</li>
<li>
Work reception once in a while*</li>
</ul>
<ul><li><i>We are not joking. Everyone in the office takes a spin as receptionist. Costumes welcome.
</i></li></ul>
<p><b>QUALIFICATIONS:</b></p>
<p><b>
EXPERIENCE</b><b>:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>4-6 years experience in marketing/brand management/account management/project management, preferably in-house within the CPG industry</li>
<li>
Experience handling and/or understanding the full mix of marketing disciplines including: consumer research/insights, brand/marketing strategy development, advertising development, media planning and execution, digital/social media marketing, PR and events.</li>
<li>
Experience with non-traditional marketing methods of reaching consumers</li>
<li>
Success as an innovative marketing professional who has developed unique solutions to problems</li>
<li>
Experience taking a non-traditional, grassroots approach to building values-based brands</li>
</ul>

REQUIREMENTS</b><b>:
