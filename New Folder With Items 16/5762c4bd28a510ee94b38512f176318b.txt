<h2>Product Manager (Owner Products - Clutch)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>Clutch, a Cox Automotive company, has built the leading software platform for automotive subscriptions. Now Clutch is investing in extending the platform to manage and optimize mobility fleets of all kinds. The product will use data and algorithms to determine optimal fleet size and mix, to recommend buy and sell decisions, and to schedule fleet tasks optimally.</p>
<p></p>
<div><div><div>
<p>
We are looking for a Product Manager to join the team that is building the mobility fleet platform. Your focus will be on the systems that customers use to manage their fleets, on integrations with a network of partners who will execute fleet tasks and on connectivity with the vehicles. A major goal will be to define discrete customer scenarios based on existing demand, then implement those scenarios on the platform.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<div><div><div><div><b>
What You'll Do</b></div></div></div></div>
<div></div>
<div><div><div><div>
<ul><li>
Own your products from end-to-end</li></ul>
<ul><li>
Own the roadmap for 10 to 15 engineers</li></ul>
<ul><li>
Structure the market opportunities that we are finding and how those map to product functionality that you own.</li></ul>
<ul><li>
Collaborate with integration partners to facilitate initial integrations then enhance those over time.</li></ul>
<ul><li>
Translate product strategy into UX and detailed user stories.</li></ul>
<ul><li>
Be a team player, seek feedback and influence the right decisions.</li></ul>
<ul><li>
Prioritize ruthlessly, ensuring tight alignment between development and sales priorities.</li></ul>
<ul><li>
Work closely with the teams implementing the products and with our customers, ensuring that the product works as intended and identifying new opportunities.</li></ul>

<p><b>
Must Haves</b></p>
