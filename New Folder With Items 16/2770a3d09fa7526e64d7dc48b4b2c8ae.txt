<h2>Food & Beverage Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">Summary
<br>At Allegiant Nonstop, we are becoming the premier Family Entertainment Center (FEC) in the nation. We know that the only way we are going to get there is by attracting and hiring the right people in the game. If you love having fun and creating opportunities for other to have fun, the Food &amp; Beverage Manager role is for you! You will have the opportunity to blaze a new path while working with customers and inspiring Team Members to be their best. This role will oversee all restaurant and bar operations to ensure quality products and services are delivered to our customers.
<br><br>
Minimum Requirements
<br>Combination of Education and Experience will be considered. Must be authorized to work in the US as defined by the Immigration Act of 1986. Must pass a Criminal Background Check. Visa sponsorship is not available for this position.
<br><b>Education:</b> High School Diploma/GED
<br><b>Certification:</b> Yes
<br><b>Certification Other:</b> ServSafe-Manager, TiPS
<br><b>Years of Experience:</b> Minimum five (5) years of experience in a full-service restaurant or related environment. Clear understanding for the operation of a food and beverage department.
<br><b>Credit Check:</b> No
<br><br>
Preferred Requirements
<br><ul>
<li>Understanding of how to train and motivate Team Members in associated areas of the Food and Beverage department, including but not limited to; food preparation, drink creation and service, food service, inventory turnover, waste management, etc.</li>
<li>Excellent leadership, interpersonal, communication, management and organizational skills with a proven track record of the ability to motivate, change, and lead others to success.</li>
<li>Ability to work under pressure and meet deadlines.</li>
<li>Adaptability, flexibility, general enthusiasm for the business.</li>
<li>Professional appearance and standards at all times.</li>
<li>Respect for people (customers, vendors and Team Members).</li>
<li>Positive, motivating attitude ensuring a productive environment.</li>
<li>Proven track record of ethical leadership and integrity.</li>
<li>Ability to problem solve and use good judgement.</li>
<li>Technical Skills such as Microsoft Office: Word, Excel, Outlook, as well as inventory management, point of sale (POS), scheduling and timekeeping systems, and financial reporting software.</li>
</ul>
Job Duties
<br><ul>
<li>Coordinate, with the Director of Operations and the General Manager, the oversight of all food and beverage areas, including marketing, while ensuring the standards for quality of service and operations are maintained.</li>
<li>Safety first; create a well maintained, safe, secure and sanitary environment for all customers and Team Members.</li>
<li>Manage inventory efficiently, accurately and in a cost-effective manner.</li>
<li>Work with the Director of Operations to create and manage an appealing offering of menu items that are cost effective for the business.</li>
<li>Work hand-in-hand with entire management team to drive strong financial results.</li>
<li>Your office is on the floor, leading the team, setting the tone, correcting behaviors as needed, and providing recognition to continuously improve operations.</li>
<li>Consistent interaction with customers ensuring they are receiving exceptional service; follow-up on issues, complaints, or suggestions they may have.</li>
<li>Prepare schedules, provide well-balanced shift coverage that drives sales and provides excellent customer service. Create contingency plan for scheduling, should the need arise.</li>
<li>Responsible for alcohol service and menu. Understand limit and service regulations with the objective of keeping customers safe at all times. Reinforce and monitor that alcohol is only served and consumed in designated areas.</li>
<li>Provide recommendations to Director of Operations and the General Manager on fiscal and customer service improvements.</li>
<li>Assist in the recruitment, training and retention of an energetic team for the department.</li>
<li>Assist the GM and Assistant Manager in quality control across departments, training Team Members on the proper way to accomplish tasks.</li>
<li>Subject Matter Expert (SME) for the food and beverage department; know machinery, tools, State specific health department and alcohol regulations.</li>
<li>Assists other Team Members as needs dictate.</li>
<li>Other duties as assigned, may act as Manager on Duty (if needs of the business dictate).</li>
</ul>
Physical Requirements
<br>The Physical Demands and Work Environment described here are a representative of those that must be met by a Team Mate to successfully perform the essential functions of the role. Reasonable accommodations may be made to enable individuals with disabilities to perform the essential functions of the role.
<br><br>
General Labor Restaurant/Gaming - While performing the duties of this job, the Team Member is regularly required to walk, stand, sit, talk, hear, see, reach, stoop, kneel, and use hands and fingers to perform repetitive motions such as operate a point-of-sale register, games and attractions, restaurant equipment, time clock, and phone. May be required to lift, push, pull, move, transport, or carry up to 50 lbs. May be required to work various shifts/days in a 24-hour situation. Regular attendance is a requirement of the role. Exposure to noise (i.e. restaurant, games, and attractions), temperature (i.e. kitchen and refrigeration) and light fluctuations (i.e. games and attractions). Ability to work in an open or confined area as well as the ability to stand at an attraction for an extended period of time.
