<h2>HR Compensation/HRIS Analyst</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>About the Organization
</p>
<div><div><div>
<div>Sun Chemical, a member of the DIC group, is a leading producer of printing inks, coatings and supplies, pigments, polymers, liquid compounds, solid compounds, and application materials. Together with DIC, Sun Chemical has annual sales of more than $7.5 billion and over 20,000 employees supporting customers around the world.</div>
</div></div></div>
<p>Description
</p>
<div><div><div>
<div>The Compensation &amp; HRIS Analyst will work out of the corporate office in Parsippany, New Jersey reporting into the Director, Compensation. The individual will be working as part of a small team on various projects and multiple country rewards and programs and will be directly involved in the administration and development of compensation and the Human Resources Information System (HRIS). The ideal candidate is a hands-on individual interested in Compensation &amp; Total Rewards as the primary role while working and supporting HRIS.<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<div><b>ESSENTIAL JOB FUNCTIONS:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
Work on a team that will refine Total Rewards and enhance the HR information system on an annual basis.
</li>
<li>Support the administration of the annual HR processes (Benefits, Compensation, Performance Management &amp; Organizational Management) within an SAP environment, providing internal comparators and external market assessments, and making recommendations and adjustments as necessary.</li>
<li>
Working with supervision and HR functional staff, assist in the design and development of Total Rewards &amp; HRIS training and support materials.
</li>
<li>Participate in cross-functional HR team related projects, developing spreadsheets, consolidating data, analyzing data, drawing appropriate conclusions and making recommendations.
</li>
<li>Prepare timely HR reports summarizing findings and recommendations.</li>
<li>
Work collaboratively with all HR functions to determine analysis and reporting needs and then work with the HR/IT team to standardize reports.</li>
<li>
Identify, recommend, and manage process improvement opportunities throughout HR.</li>
<li>
Conducts system audits to ensure data integrity and overall system performance.
</li>
<li>Assist in the development of scorecards regarding risk/reward performance, metrics on turnover, time to fill, analysis of incentive plans, participate in various salary surveys and leverage SAP wherever possible.</li>
<li>
Coordinate between HR Business Partners and clients to ensure salary administration and overall Total Reward programs are understood and assist clients/HRBPs.</li>
</ul>
</div></div></div>
<p>Position Requirements
</p>
<div><div><div>
<div><b>REQUIRED EDUCATION AND EXPERIENCE:
</b></div>
<ul>
<li>BS in HR, Business, Finance or related field required.</li>
<li>
Experience - Preferably 1-3 years of HR Total Rewards experience in a hands-on environment.</li>
<li>
Knowledge and understanding of Compensation programs, processes in a Total Rewards environment needed.</li>
<li>
Proficiency with HRIS required, working with SAP environment a plus.</li>
<li>
Strong analytical and numerical aptitude.</li>
<li>
Internship experience in HR, Financial, Information Technology Analyst or related field.
</li>
<li>Sense of urgency, attention to detail and accuracy, well organized and able to prioritize.</li>
<li>
Strong PC and systems skills, intermediate knowledge in MS Office and an HRIS system (e.g. SAP, PeopleSoft, JD Edwards, Cyborg, etc.) strongly preferred.</li>
<li>
Strong technical skills in Microsoft Excel
</li>
<li>Excellent written and oral presentation skills.</li>
</ul>
