<h4 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>About this job</b></h4>
<p>This position is uniquely suited for self-managing, self-starting professionals and comes with a diversity of business challanges that make this job never dull.</p>
<p>
Applicants should preferably have at least 2 years of experience in the managed IT services industry or 4 years serving in an corporate IT department and be able to demonstrate very high skill supporting desktop operating systems,tablets and smartphones. They should also have intermediate to advanced knowledge of networking, networking security and server operating systems.</p>

Carceron offers a benefits package including Health, Dental, Vision &amp; Life Insurance.</b></p>
