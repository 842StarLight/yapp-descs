<h2>Advertising Sales Planning Analyst</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>
<div>As a member of the advertising sales planning and operations team, the Sales Planner will support sales activities and client/account management through:</div>
<ul>
<li>
Creating and modifying sales proposals for Linear, Addressable, and Digital
</li>
<li>Generating planning costs for assigned advertising agencies</li>
<li>
Liaison between sales, traffic and external advertising agencies</li>
<li>
Managing Stewardship of deals ensuring timely delivery of pre/post buy reports</li>
<li>
Working with Inventory team to effectively utilize inventory across networks</li>
<li>
Working with sales coordinators on client scheduling, billing and related areas</li>
<li>
Partnering with the traffic team to ensure copy instructions and materials are received</li>
<li>
Adhering to deadlines, policies and procedures</li>
</ul>
<br>
</div>

Qualifications</b></h2>
