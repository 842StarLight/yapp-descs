<h2>Fulfillment Representative - Wholesale Order Management</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<div>
<p><b>Job Summary:</b></p>
<p>
The Fulfillment Representative - Wholesale Order Management will collaborate with dealers, customers, and internal business partners to improve customer service, delivery performance, and increase partner satisfaction through driving consistency, quality and accuracy in the handling of retail orders, and requests. The Representative is responsible for end-to-end handling of orders from receipt through delivery to customer as well as resolving issues related to those orders.</p>
<p><b>
Essential Duties, Responsibilities, and Key Results Area:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Enter, process, and track orders from receipt through delivery to the account. All new orders should be entered same-day or within 24-hours of receipt (following day) and completed by local plant/planner cut-off times</li>
<li>Responsible for order tracking and completion, partnering with order tracking personnel/team member to ensure timely delivery and order closure</li>
<li>Troubleshoot and solve problems related to order fulfillment, accounting and other customer or Sales requests.</li>
<li>Resolution and coordination of product availability, production scheduling, and transportation resources.</li>
<li>Answer phones and emails in a timely manner. Expectation is to respond to both within four business hours. Representative will be logged into phone systems throughout their workday.</li>
<li>Analyze reports for internal and customer review, take action to prevent or minimize impact of order fulfillment failures.</li>
<li>Will be expected to be a primary or secondary back-up on other key accounts</li>
<li>Work in a team environment across multiple functions (customer service, Manufacturing/Assembly, Logistics, Accounting, Sales and the customer account) to provide a high quality of service.</li>
<li>Provide regular updates to Supervisor/Manager, Sales Representative, and customer account as required.</li>
<li>Be a voice/advocate for the customer as well as a responsible representative of Tempur-Sealy.</li>
<li>Responsible for maintaining updated documents located on H-Drive. Main document will feature format consistent with other accounts. Documentation of all applicable processes will need to be defined as well. Folders on H-Drive will be organized to only show necessary information on initial</li>
</ul>
<p><i>Other primary duties and responsibilities include the following; however, Management reserves the right to assign or reassign other appropriate duties to this job at any time. This document excludes the marginal functions of the position that are incidental to the performance of fundamental job duties.</i></p>
