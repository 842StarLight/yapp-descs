<p><b>
Your Mission:</b></p>
<p>
Medidata is looking for an experienced, hands-on engineering manager to lead a team of engineers to design, implement, and enhance new features and functionality in Medidata’s Medical Coding Service - Coder. Medical coding is a critical piece of the clinical trial process and part of Medidata’s strategy to grow to over one billion in revenue.</p>
<p></p>
<p>
Coder is a mature, single-instance, multi-tenant web service supporting hundreds of clinical trials. Our application is built with C#, leverages Microsoft’s SQL server database and is hosted in AWS. Our front end uses Asp.NET, HTML/CSS, Bootstrap and AngularJS. As an engineering manager on Coder, you'll be working with the engineering team as well as product management to develop new features in response to market demand and improve the performance and scalability of the application to meet the rapidly growing user base.</p>
<ul>
<li><p>
Provide clear, consistent leadership, management and mentoring for direct reports and the broader technology organization. Inspire, empower and motivate the team, leading by example.</p></li>
<li><p>
Help team members increase the breadth and depth of their technical and soft skills</p></li>
<li><p>
Perform personnel management tasks for all developers, including performance reviews, promotions, compliance with company policies, mentoring, and helping plan career growth</p></li>
<li><p>
Design and enhance features for a large-scale service environment using OO principles with a deep focus on performance, scalability, quality and extensibility</p></li>
<li><p>
Embrace test-driven development with complete code coverage and continuous integration</p></li>
<li><p>
Provide diagnostic support and fix bugs for service features</p></li>
<li><p>
Follow Medidata’s Standard Operating Procedures to ensure all software meets regulatory and company requirements</p></li>
<li><p>
Work with Agile team members, particularly Test and Product Management, to write clear, concise and comprehensive feature files</p></li>
<li><p>
Participate in Agile working practices such as daily scrum meetings, sprint planning meetings, reviews and retrospectives</p></li>
<li><p>
Consistently execute against a product roadmap to deliver multiple features across the Rave RTSM portfolio of service components enabling our clients to more effectively run their clinical trials</p></li>
<li><p>
Build relationships with product management, marketing, sales, and users to enable development teams to more effectively refine system requirements and size delivery efforts</p></li>
<li><p>
Share your knowledge and experience with a highly motivated and collaborative team regarding code reviews, software designs and all other engineering and business aspects</p></li>
</ul>
