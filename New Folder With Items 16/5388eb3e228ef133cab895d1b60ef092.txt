<p><b>The Company</b></p>
<p>
Worldpay is a global payments leader powering international commerce with deep fintech expertise and a shared passion for our customers. Whether in-store, online, or on a mobile device, we process over 40 billion transactions annually and offer more than 300 payment methods supporting 126 currencies across 146 countries. It's the perfect place for exceptional people to take their careers to the next level.</p>

<p></p>
<p><b>
The Opportunity</b></p>
<p>
Worldpay's growth is one attributed not only to our first-in-class products and services, but also our ability to find new avenues of opportunity. This sense of discovery is thanks to the innovative and visionary work of our Marketing team; a team that leaves no stone unturned in seeking out and exploring new channels for Worldpay. When we are proactive and beating the trends in our marketing strategy, we stay ahead of the competition and assure continued growth and success at Worldpay.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
The Senior Strategic Pricing Analyst applies deep research to uncover new insight into competitive pricing to better position Worldpay's market strategy. He/she will partner with cross-functional teams to better understand our product portfolios and acquire data from current and potential clients. When we are analytical in our evaluation of competitive market as well as our internal structures, Worldpay can continue to improve how we do business.</p>

<p><b>
The Day-to-Day</b></p>
<p>
Responsibilities:</p>
<ul>
<li><ul>
<li>Develops, evaluates and implements pricing strategies and models based upon current business and competitive market dynamics.</li>
<li>Research, analyze and conduct competitive pricing analysis.</li>
<li>Partners with Sales/Marketing, Business Development and Finance to correctly position product portfolio and establish pricing programs aligned with product/sales strategy</li>
<li>Analyzes and interprets data to generate key insights that drive pricing decisions.</li>
<li>Identifies, evaluates and presents a variety of pricing models such as subscription-based pricing, bundling, Software as a Service</li>
<li>Demonstrates business acumen and market/customer based thinking</li>
<li>Quantifies and reports on the impact of pricing actions</li>
<li>Influence business outcomes through presence, communication professional networking both internally and externally.</li>
</ul></li>
</ul>
<p>Qualifications:</p>
<ul>
<li>Typically requires a minimum of 5 years of related experience with a Bachelor's degree; or 3 years and a Master's degree; or a PhD without experience; or equivalent work experience.</li>
</ul>
