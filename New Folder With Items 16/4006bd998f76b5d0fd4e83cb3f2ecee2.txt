<p><b>ESSENTIAL JOB FUNCTIONS</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Serve as a positive representation of Heritage Cooperative’s Core Values</li>
<li>Provide Superior Customer Service</li>
<li>Highest Standards of Integrity and Trust</li>
<li>Continuous Improvement</li>
<li>Being a Good Partner</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Abide by all safety regulations to ensure safety of oneself, coworkers and customers</li>
<li>Support multiple offerings of farm data collection tools</li>
<li>Learn and comprehend full suite of software &amp; service offerings</li>
<li>Analyze data to provide the sales team and growers with valuable insights found within the technology programs</li>
<li>Execute the collection and input of as applied details into data platforms</li>
<li>Proactively follow up with customers for new product interest and sign ups</li>
<li>Assist in collection of nutrient sampling test</li>
<li>Install, startup, and diagnose In Cab Data collection systems</li>
<li>Review customer request and find a best solution to provide a positive result</li>
<li>Provide help to Agronomy Operations team when necessary</li>
<li>Other duties as assigned</li>
