<p><b>
Who is Arrow Electronics?</b></p>
<p>
Arrow Electronics is a global provider of products, services and solutions to industrial and commercial users of electronic components and enterprise computing solutions. Arrow serves as a supply channel partner for more than 150,000 original equipment manufacturers, value-added resellers, contract manufacturers, and commercial customers through a global network. The company maintains over 300 sales facilities and 45 distribution and value-added centers, serving over 80 countries.</p>
<p></p>
<p>
A Fortune 109 company with 20,000 employees worldwide, Arrow brings technology solutions to a breadth of markets, including telecommunications, information systems, transportation, medical, industrial and consumer electronics.</p>
<p></p>
<p>
Arrow provides specialized services and expertise across the entire product lifecycle. Arrow does this by connecting customers to the right technology at the right place, right time and right price</p>
<p></p>
<p>
Arrow provides extraordinary value to customers and suppliers - the best technology companies in the world - and connects them through the company's industry-leading services.</p>

<p><b>
Functional Area</b></p>
<p>
We have an exciting opportunity for IT/Computer Information Systems Interns to work full time over the summer. The IT/ Computer Information Systems Intern will develop applications that are used by Arrow’s core businesses, as well as external customers, to deliver data and performance reports. The intern will provide data analysis focusing on the business needs and drivers. They will focus on the definition, construction, and deployment of reports, dashboards, metrics and analytic components within a dynamic and high profile business intelligence environment.</p>
