<p>Under minimal supervision, develops and executes strategy for one or more marketing initiatives. Resolves problems and makes recommendations related to complex and interrelated matters and projects. Provides specialist-level analysis in several marketing disciplines and/or expert-level in one or more. Recommends project budgets and forecasts anticipated budget spending. Coaches others in less senior positions.<br>
<br>
<b>Job Summary:</b><br>
<br>
The successful candidate will transform how consumer recipients proactively manage the packages being sent to them. The person will define and implement FDM’s indirect channel strategy and road-map. She/He will lead the cross-functional efforts needed to recruit omni-channel partners. Strategic Thinking: Assessing customer and partner needs to define a product / channel vision and actionable strategy.<br>
<br>
Product Management: Using Quality Driven Management (QDM)/Six Sigma principals to assess product / channel performance. Build a solid fact-base critical to influencing the strategy, road-map and justify investments. Applying Agile/SAFe to develop the requirements needed to direct product enhancements. Building omni-channel relationships that increase FDM enrollments and business value for all stakeholders.<br>
<br>
Selling Ideas: Evangelizing the FDM Omni-Channel strategy across all levels to gain the officer support needed to accelerate strategy execution and business results.<br>
<br>
Collaboration: Demonstrated ability to build and maintain strong relationships with cross functional stakeholders including executive management.<br>
<br>
Adaptability/Flexibility: Leading teams/self through changing priorities and responding to issues as they come up while executing the strategy.<br>
<br>
Project Management: able to prioritize and manage multiple deliverables simultaneously in order to achieve business goals.<br>
