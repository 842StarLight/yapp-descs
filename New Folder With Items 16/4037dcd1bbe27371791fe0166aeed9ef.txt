<div><b>
About the role:</b></div>
<div>
As a Regional Business Manager, you will become the voice of the customer for Cobalt Robotics, building trust with our customers and ensuring that our products and services continue to provide value. Your role is focused on building long-term relationships with customers and ensuring our customers are not just satisfied, but are vocal advocates for Cobalt Robotics.
</div>
</div>
<div><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>What you’ll do:</b></h3>
<div><ul>
<li>
Coordinate sales and promotion within the local territory</li>
<li>
Build strategic customer relationships to foster relationships with head of security and/or operations based on service, product knowledge, integrity and trust, and favorably position for future product launches</li>
<li>
Develop strategies, tactics, marketing initiatives, and sales programs</li>
<li>
Plan and organize meetings in the territory</li>
<li>
Maintain knowledge of product/service, market/industry trends, competitors, and customers and communicate to management</li>
<li>
Perform special projects and assignments as directed by the leadership team</li>
<li>
Ensure clear, concise and accurate communication of product information</li>
</ul></div>
</div></div>
