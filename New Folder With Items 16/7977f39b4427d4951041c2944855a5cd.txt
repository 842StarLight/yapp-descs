<h2>Store Manager Trainee</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>Job Description
</p>
<div><div><div>
<div>The primary purpose of this position is to learn all aspects of operating a retail store. The incumbent is also required to perform all tasks in a safe manner consistent with corporate policies and state and federal laws.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
The associate is responsible for the functions below, in addition to other duties as assigned:<br>
1. Attend all Management Development Program (MDP) training classes.<br>
2. Learn about all of the possible situations that can occur in a retail store to prepare for the Assistant Store Manager or Store Manager position.<br>
3. Assist the Store Manager with the operation of the retail store and complete his/her duties in his/her absence.<br>
4. Enforce all policies and procedures to ensure excellent customer service is provided and to maximize the P&amp;L performance of the store.<br>
5. Ensure the appearance of the store is pleasing to the customer and meets the requirements of the corporate office.<br>
6. Ensure the store opens and closes at the appropriate time.<br>
7. Ensure the proper procedures are followed for cash transactions and bank deposits.<br>
8. Interact with vendors to order ad, seasonal, and basic merchandise and ice cream (where applicable) for the store.<br>
9. Ensure all merchandise is set up according to plan-o-grams received from the corporate office.<br>
10. Execute weekly sales ads and price changes.<br>
11. Process recalled, damaged, outdated, and transferred merchandise.<br>
12. Receive merchandise deliveries from vendors and Rite Aid distribution centers.<br>
13. Verify vendor invoice information is accurate and enter it into the accounts payable system.<br>
14. Prepare the retail store for physical inventory by ensuring merchandise on the sales floor and in the stock room is in order and easy to access.<br>
15. Analyze operating reports and make recommendations for improvement.<br>
16. Utilize StaffworksÂ® software to complete the associate work schedule.<br>
17. Remain knowledgeable of the One-Hour Photo department and film processing, if applicable.<br>
18. Assist the Pharmacy department when there is a high volume of customers.<br>
19. Request store maintenance when required.<br>
20. Assist with the general maintenance of the store, both inside and outside.<br>
21. Enforce all fire and safety rules and regulations set by the company and governmental agencies.<br>
22. Provide leadership and development for associates by communicating career opportunities, providing regular performance feedback, and demonstrating RAPTAR (Recognition, Appreciation, Praise, Treat Associates Respectfully) behaviors.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<ul><li>All duties described in this document are to be performed in keeping with the core values and service attributes consistent with the Rite Aid brand and strategy.</li></ul>
<div></div>
<br>
</div></div></div>
<p>Experience / Requirements
</p>
<div><div><div>
<div>The following qualities are required:<br>
<ul>
<li>In accordance with state law, candidates must satisfy minimum age requirements to sell and/or supervise the sale of alcohol and/or tobacco products.</li>
<li>Ability to pass drug test.</li>
<li>Committed to providing customer service that makes both internal and external customers feel welcome, important, and appreciated.</li>
<li>Ability to preserve confidentiality of information.</li>
<li>Ability and willingness to move with purpose and a strong sense of urgency.</li>
<li>Ability to work weekends and extended days on a frequent basis.</li>
<li>Ability to work day or evening hours.</li>
<li>Accuracy and attention to detail.</li>
</ul>
</div>
<ul>
<li>Ability to organize and prioritize a variety of tasks/projects.</li>
<li>Familiarity with industry/technical terms and processes.</li>
<li>Ability to work within strict time frames and resolute deadlines.</li>
</ul>
</div></div></div>
<p>Education
</p>
<div><div><div><div>High school diploma or general education degree (GED), plus one (1) to two (2) years' of retail experience; or equivalent combination of education and experience.</div></div></div></div>
