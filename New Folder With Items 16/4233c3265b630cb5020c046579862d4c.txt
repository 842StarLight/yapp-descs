<h2>Senior Accountant</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Senior Accountant - (Full-Time)<br>Holtmeier Construction, Inc. - Mankato, MN 56001</p>
<p>Description:<br>Holtmeier Construction, Inc. is currently looking for a Senior Accountant. This position is responsible for recording financial transactions, maintaining records, reporting to upper management and ensuring general ledgers are accurate, complete and reconciled. This position is also responsible for assisting with payroll preparation and related payroll reporting. This position will be responsible for assisting the ownership and management to provide accurate financial statements with supplementary schedules for use by outside parties such as banking and bonding relationships.</p>
<p>Holtmeier Construction is one of the largest heavy construction companies in southern Minnesota. Holtmeier Construction is growing and looking to expand so we need talented and driven individuals to be part of the team. We have a great company culture consisting of working hard individuals having fun and supporting the community.</p>
<p>Individuals applying for this position should possess the skills to work in a fast-paced environment of a seasonal nature with a high level of attention to detail. A candidate should also possess the following qualifications:</p>
<p>Duties and Responsibilities</p>
<p> Daily entry of financial transactions in accounting software programs.<br> Prepare weekly payroll and related payroll reports and remittances.<br> Ensure correct entry, approval, and matching of accounts payable.<br> Prepare invoicing for construction, quarry, hauling and aggregate sales.<br> Prepare and reconcile various adjusting journal entries.<br> Maintain, document and reconcile accounting information.<br> Provide assistance and support to external and internal clients.<br> Assist with month-end and year-end close procedures.<br> Assist with drafting monthly and annual financial statements for both internal and external users.<br> Assist ownership and management with budgeting and planning.<br> Perform special projects as they are needed and assigned.</p>
<p>Requirements:</p>
<p> Bachelor's degree in Accounting.<br> 2-5 years of accounting related experience (public accounting or private industry).<br> Prior experience with construction industry helpful.<br> Knowledge and ability to apply Generally Accepted Accounting Principles with a basic understanding of tax accounting methods.<br> Proficiency in Excel and Word.<br> Demonstrated knowledge of Quickbooks.<br> Excellent communication skills; both verbal and written.<br> Ability to effectively interact with all levels of management and staff, suppliers and customers.<br> Ability to adapt to changing priorities and activities.<br> Ability to pay close attention to detail with minimal supervision.<br> Strong teamwork and collaboration skills.<br> Function in accordance with established standards, procedures and applicable laws.</p>
<p>Consider joining Holtmeier Construction, Inc. as we look to grow and expand taking advantage of various opportunities.<br>The right candidate will enjoy working with a hard-working and dedicated team.</p>
