<h2>Business Development Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p>As a <b><i>Business Development Manager– Mass </i></b>, you will be a member of the Mass Market sales team charged with supporting the Mass retail channel business (Walmart, Sam's Club) with a heavy focus on managing Belkin/Linksys/Wemo supply chain &amp; replenishment and online sales for the Mass channel. BDM will work closely with Director of Sales, National Account Managers, and Supply Chain team. Successful candidate will have experience working directly with Walmart and Sam's Club accounts. Responsibilities include, but not limited to, the following:
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Specific responsibilities include:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Belkin and Linksys In Store Business
<ul>
<li>Work with Supply Chain team and National Account Managers on forecasts and order management business.
</li>
<li>Manage daily relationships with Walmart/Sam's Club Replenishment Managers and Planners.
</li>
<li>Manage Item Setup process with National Account Managers.
</li>
<li>Work closely with internal teams at the Playa Vista corporate office to provide project management support for all Mass channel projects.
</li>
<li>Assist National Account Managers with management of the Salesforce system and all trade fund related programs, customer related expenses.
</li>
<li>Provided weekly sell through reporting for Walmart and Sam's Club businesses.
</li>
<li>Manage internal Walmart OTIF reporting.
</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>Belkin and Linksys eCommerce Business
<ul>
<li>Assist with account management of eCommerce partners including Walmart.com, Sam’s Club.com
</li>
<li>Work with Belkin distribution team to manage items sold to Mass channel eComm through distribution partners.
</li>
<li>Review top-selling Belkin SKUs and NPIs, then ensure placement on eCommerce.
</li>
<li>Develop and maintain promotional calendars for 500+ SKUs for Mass channel eComm.
</li>
<li>Manage daily relationships with Walmart and Sam's Club eCommerce merchants and inventory managers
</li>
<li>Provide ongoing analysis and ad hoc reports for business planning
</li>
<li>Manage the day-to-day operations of Walmart's website operations, including product page content, inventory availability, pricing and promotional activities in order to optimize conversion and revenue.
</li>
<li>Partner with Interactive Marketing team to drive search engine optimization, search engine marketing, online advertising, digital media, email marketing, website(s) and interactive campaigns to drive consumer and merchant acquisition, retention and loyalty.
