<h2>Software Solutions Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Software Solutions Manager</b></p>
<p><b>Scope of Position</b></p>
<p>MarkeTeam is an award-winning marketing and sales promotion agency, specializing in the hospitality, leisure and food &amp; beverage industries. We are currently searching for a Software Solutions Manager to lead our eCraft technology products division. Candidate will need to be well versed in current technological trends including a familiarity with best practices in software research and implementation, user experience, project management, process improvement, product testing and customer success.</p>
<p>Responsibilities:</p>
<ul>
<li><b>Project Oversight</b></li>
<li>Complete technical projects within the department, utilizing outside resources as needed</li>
<li>Evaluate and implement new systems and infrastructure</li>
<li>Strong timeline management</li>
<li><b>Identify, Define &amp; Rollout Software Solutions</b></li>
<li>Clearly understand and outline client requirements</li>
<li>Work alongside client and internal departments to execute job requirements and objectives</li>
<li>Envision and recommend ideal solutions, through existing or new white-label avenues</li>
<li>Research, identify, vet and test the software to ensure it's the right solution for the client’s need</li>
<li>Direct the delivery of solution to client, utilizing best practices of software release management, customer implementation</li>
<li>Serve as software liaison for updates, upgrades, new features, etc.</li>
<li><b>User Experience</b></li>
<li>Ensure products have simple intuitive user experiences, in collaboration with design department and external resources</li>
<li><b>Process Management, QC &amp; Testing</b></li>
<li>Lead efforts to improve processes and ensure technology standards and best practices are met</li>
<li>Work to refine process documents and forms to streamline and make processes more efficient and successful.</li>
<li>Standardize testing protocols, environment and improvements</li>
<li><b>Customer Success</b></li>
<li>Develop strategies to increase customer adoption and retention</li>
<li>Customer Support – make sure resources are in place to monitor and quickly respond to all user support emails/requests</li>
<li><b>High Level Vision &amp; Leadership</b></li>
<li>Guide department with thought leadership, innovation, creativity, strategic thinking and problem-solving aptitude</li>
<li>Maintain department staff by training and counseling staff</li>
<li>Collaborate with marketing to create effective sales &amp; marketing materials</li>
<li>Assess market competition by comparing company’s product to competitors</li>
<li>Identify new market opportunities and monitor new technologies that yield competitive advantage</li>
<li>Advise on product pricing by utilizing market research data</li>
</ul>
<p>Basic Qualifications</p>
<p>Required:</p>
<ul>
<li>BS/MS in computer science or equivalent</li>
<li>5+ years of industry experience with cloud software services and an understanding of design for scalability, performance and reliability</li>
<li>2+ years of experience managing a software development team, and a proven track record of shipping software on time</li>
<li>Experience managing third-party vendors</li>
</ul>
<p>Preferred Qualifications:</p>
<ul>
<li>Experience managing offshore development resources</li>
<li>Strong Computer Science fundamentals in data structures, algorithm design, and problem solving</li>
<li>Excellence in technical communication with peers and non-technical cohorts.</li>
<li>Programming experience in one or more relevant languages</li>
</ul>
<p>In addition to the above, the perfect candidate is someone who works well within a team while being independent, thrives in a fast-paced environment, has excellent communications, is pro-active, tenacious, quality driven, creative, detail-oriented, and takes a “whatever it takes” approach to achieving client’s goals. He/she has the ability to quickly build relationships, be influential, and is sociable and fun!</p>
<p>Job Type: Full-time</p>
<p>Experience:</p>
<ul><li>Relevant: 5 years (Preferred)</li></ul>
