<h2>Event Marketing Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<div>
<div><p>We are looking for a senior events marketing professional to act as the senior strategic and operational events leader for our presence at tradeshows around the globe.</p></div>
</div>
<div>
<p>RESPONSIBILITIES</p>
<div><ul>
<li>Provide leadership by setting tradeshow direction in strategic alignment with key stakeholders</li>
<li>
Create clarity for all workflows and functional teams</li>
<li>
Operate transparently and build partnerships with all areas of the company</li>
<li>
Manage processes, teams, and schedules to deliver events on time and on objective</li>
<li>
Bring together exciting content and experiences for our attendees</li>
<li>
Develop short- and long-term vision for conferences</li>
<li>
Develop strong partnerships with sales, marketing, product teams, corporate branding, senior management, and others to build consensus and align goals and activities</li>
<li>
Research and assess best practices and comparative reviews of competitor, industry, and leading events and programs</li>
<li>
Leverage your creativity to bring the left and right brain aspects of your planning to life</li>
</ul></div>
</div>
<div>
<p>REQUIREMENTS</p>
<div><ul>
<li>10+ years of experience designing, developing, and delivering major event programs and tradeshows for customers and prospects globally, preferably in the software or related industries</li>
<li>
Exceptional leadership, teambuilding, and collaborative skills developing and working with high performing teams who understand business and audience needs</li>
<li>
Ability to communicate well at all levels via presentation, written, and spoken channels</li>
<li>
Successfully led several complete events or diverse work streams from initial objective setting through brainstorming, development, and deployment</li>
<li>
Desire to thrive in rapid growth, fast-paced environment; ability to adapt well to change and act with urgency</li>
<li>
2+ years of direct team management experience</li>
<li>
3-4+ years of leadership experience</li>
<li>
Bachelor's in marketing, business, or related field (master's preferred)</li>
</ul></div>
