<p><b>MULTIMEDIA MARKETING SPECIALIST</b></p>
<p>Digital Resource is the 262nd fastest-growing private company in America of 2018, as ranked by <b>Inc. Magazine’s annual Inc. 500 listing. Inc.</b></p>
<p>We are a full-service digital marketing agency. Our team develops effective content strategies for forward-thinking companies. We have a proven track record in increasing search engine rankings, building social followings and most importantly, converting leads into real revenue.</p>
<p><b>We are currently looking for a Multimedia Specialist</b> <b>to join our talented content team. This position reports to the Multimedia Manager. The requirements and responsibilities of this position are listed below.</b></p>
<p>The Multimedia Specialist assists in the execution of photo and video projects needed to complement multi-platform digital marketing campaigns.</p>
<p>The Multimedia Specialist will work on multiple projects at a time, ranging from one-time client headshots to ongoing video series, with the end goal of meeting or exceeding client expectations in a timely manner.</p>
<p>Under the guidance of the Multimedia Manager, this position is responsible for executing video concepts brought to the Multimedia Team by trained Account Managers. Success in this role is defined not only by producing content efficiently but taking creative measures to effectively and visually speak to and resonate with a client’s ideal audience.</p>
<p>Qualifications &amp; Skills</p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor's degree</li>
<li>A "Can Do!" mentality</li>
<li>Excellent organizational and time management skills</li>
<li>Positive outlook and good interpersonal skills</li>
<li>Desire to develop a strong working knowledge of digital content creation and marketing</li>
<li>Experience with DSLR photography and videography (Nikon is currently used in-house)</li>
<li>Willingness to adapt to Nikon interface, if not already familiar</li>
<li>Experience using content creation software (Adobe Suite – primarily Lightroom, Premiere Pro, Photoshop)</li>
<li>Experience using Adobe Audition and After Effects is a plus</li>
<li>Strong research and writing skills</li>
<li>Ability to work on multiple projects independently and as a team</li>
</ul>
<p>Job Duties</p>
<p>· Develop creative content for digital campaigns</p>
<p>· Execute photo and video concepts for client and Digital Resource marketing material (includes print &amp; web)</p>
<p>· Edit photo and video content for client and Digital Resource marketing material (includes print &amp; web)</p>
<p>· Optimize photo and video content per Search Engine and Social Media Marketing parameters</p>
