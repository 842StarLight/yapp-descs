<p><b>What does a Front End Supervisor do?</b><br>
<br>
The Front End Supervisor enables Best Buy to deliver our customer promise of personalized service to ensure customers are provided the right solution to meet their needs. You will be a key player in driving effective front of store processes that provide a world class experience for our customers including: store pick-up, checkout, customer services (return / exchange / trade-in, recycle processing), asset protection, in-store phone experience and overall information technology hardware health at an individual store level.<br>
<br>
You will play a vital role in delivering efficient and effective processing, shrink plan integrity, safety and profitability within the store.<br>
<br>
As the Front End Supervisor you will:</p>
<ul>
<li><p>
Adhere to Standard Operating Procedure for functions such as daily cash management, payroll processing, scheduling, store expense tracking and IT help desk ticket maintenance.</p></li>
<li><p>
Provide employee coaching, training, and validation of department and store shrink plans.</p></li>
<li><p>
Ensure all Standard Operating Procedures for transactions and interactions at the front of the store are followed including: checkout, pickup, returns / exchanges / trade-ins, and recycling.</p></li>
<li><p>
Prepare and conduct team meetings, trainings and other special events to communicate clear goals to team.</p></li>
<li><p>
Provide input to store leadership on employee performance and development needs.</p></li>
</ul>
<p><b>
What are the Professional Requirements of a Front End Supervisor?</b></p>
<p>
Basic Qualifications</p>
<ul>
<li><p>
1 year of experience in leadership</p></li>
<li><p>
1 year of experience in sales, customer service, operations or related fields</p></li>
</ul>
<p>
Preferred Qualifications</p>
<ul>
<li><p>
Associate degree or higher in business or related field</p></li>
<li><p>
1 year consumer electronics experience</p></li>
<li><p>
Operations experience</p></li>
<li><p>
Supervisory experience</p></li>
<li><p>
Previous experience with coaching and recognition</p></li>
</ul>
