<h2>Implementation Project Coordinator - Entry Level</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">Job Introduction
<br>The Corporate Payments Implementation Project Coordinator works directly with the customer and associates across all departments to manage smaller customer project implementations as well as internal implementation processes. By facilitating the implementation of products/services in a customer-focused manner, this position promotes new and/or additional revenue opportunities for our company. Implementation Project Coordinator positions demand attention to detail, a thorough command of organization and communication skills as well as the ability to manage multiple projects simultaneously. To perform this job successfully, an individual must be able to perform each essential duty satisfactorily. The requirements listed below are representative of the knowledge, skill, and/or ability required. This position is responsible for onboarding our clients with the products that they purchased. This could include purchasing/Travel and Entertainment cards as well as ePayables virtual cards for AP payments. Implementation Project Coordinators support the customer while ramping and then transition to full time support after the client’s program is stable.
<br><br>
Main Responsibilities
<br><br>
Oversee all aspects of the projects, sets and adheres to deadlines, assigns responsibilities, and monitors and summarizes the status of the project
<br>Responsible for achieving highest possible revenue attainment from each customer
<br>Assesses training needs and conducts training with the client as needed via webinars
<br>Handles smaller sized accounts
<br>Work with sales, account setup specialist, technical support, the customer and other associates for a successful customer implementation
<br>Review contracts, customer profile, business cases, vendor lists and conducts introductory and implementation calls with the client to review and validate the products sold
<br>Collect all necessary data from the client to accurately complete the setup and implementation of the account
<br>Also responsible for reviewing new accounts that are credit approved and validating information before assigning
<br>Follow up with sales and credit departments for any missing pieces of information
<br>Monitors implementation Queue and adheres to standard Implementation SLA’s
<br>Supports and Trains account setup specialists with mentoring and answering questions
<br><br>
The Ideal Candidate
<br><br>
Bachelor’s degree or equivalent combination of education and experience that is required for the job
<br>3+ years of experience in a professional work environment
<br>2+ year of experience with implementations, client support, or customer-interfacing
<br>Accounting knowledge is strongly preferred
<br>Expense Management knowledge (Concur, Expense Watch, iExpense, SDG2, etc) preferred
<br>Demonstrated strong interpersonal skills, solid analytical skills and attention to details; and excellent follow-up skills
<br>Demonstrated ability to work calmly in a fast-paced team environment
<br>Excellent communication skills, both verbal and written, in order to properly communicate our product offerings and functionality
<br>Highly responsive to calls and emails; utilize available tools to track appointments and manage priorities without compromising other responsibilities
<br>Superior customer service skills, with the ability to react quickly and decisively to resolve customer issues; ability to analyze customer processes and provide creative solutions for implementation
<br>Demonstrated ability to conduct virtual presentations and effectively facilitate meetings
<br>Ability to aid in the effective and timely revenue ramp of each client is critically important
<br>Keep informed of new enhancements to the system which will affect existing or future implementations
<br>Familiarity with project management software, tools and techniques
<br>Seek up sell opportunities and provide leads to sales representatives
<br>Make suggestions for process improvements to address project quality, cost reduction, cycle time and/or productivity
