<p></p>
<br>
<p>
The Construction Operations Manager for the Construction Unit of this Developer Builder will provide management oversight, direction and control over the region/business unit’s construction planning, project execution, risk management, resources management, administration, development and investor relationships.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Construction Operations Manager Essential Duties &amp; Responsibilities:</b></h3>
<p></p>
<br>
<ul>
<li> Lead overall construction performance through Senior/Project Managers. Promote, enforce and ensure operational procedures and policies are established up front and maintained to ensure all project objectives are met, including scheduling and QA/QC.</li>
<li> Supervise, guide and direct the Safety and Risk Management for projects under assigned region in compliance with corporate directives.
</li>
<li> Lead, train and develop Senior/Project Managers and other direct reports, including completion of performance evaluations.</li>
<li> Ensure strict adherence to ethics and compliance requirements throughout their business unit.</li>
<li> Provide leadership in risk evaluation, contract negotiations, fee and pricing decisions.</li>
<li> Manage the “people resources” to meet the goals established for work in place, budget, earnings, and new sales.</li>
<li> In conjunction with CEO, COO and Human Resources, establish staff recruiting programs, overall staff development (training, job rotations, evaluations, appraisals, etc.), salary administration, assignments and transfers, long range and short -range staff planning.</li>
<li> Provide operational support to business development and preconstruction services as required either directly or by assignment of appropriate staff for preconstruction assignments.</li>
<li> Establish and maintain effective relationships with development associates, architects, engineers and subcontractors.</li>
<li> Direct and maintain labor relations consistent with area practice.</li>
<li> In conjunction with Human Resources, establish, develop and deliver a comprehensive training program.</li>
<li> Support and drive utilization of various company initiatives and technologies.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Construction Operations Manager Qualifications:</b></h3>
<p>
Self-starter who can work well independently with minimal supervision and broad direction.</p>
<p>
Bachelor’s degree plus a minimum of 15 years’ of progressive divisional leadership experience. Strong knowledge or experience in standard business practices such as Contract/ Project Management, Quality Control, Risk Management, Estimating and Accounting. Excellent organizational skills and ability to perform detail- oriented work. Strong knowledge of Microsoft Office, Procore and accounting software. Must have P &amp; L experience including writing business unit and budget plans</p>
