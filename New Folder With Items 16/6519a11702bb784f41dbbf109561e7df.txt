<h2>Account Executive, Digital Banking</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>About NCR
</b></p>
<p>NCR Corporation (NYSE: NCR) is a leading software- and services-led enterprise provider in the financial, retail, hospitality, telecom and technology industries. NCR is headquartered in Atlanta, Ga., with 34,000 employees and does business in 180 countries. NCR is a trademark of NCR Corporation in the United States and other countries.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Position </b>: Account Executive, Digital Banking
</p>
<p><b>Location </b>: Northwest of USA
</p>
<p><b>Grade </b>: 13
</p>
<p></p>
<p>This role will cultivate CFI decision maker contacts from referrals, personal research, and corporate marketing campaigns. You will engage those contacts through orchestrated touch patterns including phone, email, on-site visits and social media.
</p>
<p>The goal is to discover and advance leads through the sales stages, utilizing NCR’s Digital Banking sales methodology. You must be a hunter, accustomed to developing and closing new business. This is a role for a highly experienced individual in enterprise technology sales.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Key Responsibilities:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Research accounts, build a business plan to understand targets, create communication plans, and generate prospect interest.
</li>
<li>Perform outbound sales activities such as introductory calls, prospect visits, tradeshows and lead validation discussions.
</li>
<li>Proactively look for opportunities to engage Marketing to influence campaign planning and optimize the inbound lead management process.
</li>
<li>Build relationships internally and externally and effectively communicate with prospects to positively influence deal progression
</li>
<li>Educate prospects on Digital Insight’s value proposition and product capabilities.
</li>
<li>Track all sales activities using Salesforce.com.
</li>
<li>Meet or exceed quota metrics for growing the Digital Banking pipeline (3X) and new annual contract value.
</li>
<li>Partner with NCR leadership and finance teams on winning pricing models and strategies.
</li>
<li>Partner with NCR Contracts and legal on negotiating terms and conditions of service agreements with prospects in sales.
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Basic Qualifications:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor Degree or equivalent experience
</li>
<li>5 years+ of Software Sales experience, preferably in the Financial Services industry.
</li>
<li>Executive level verbal and written communication.
</li>
<li>A contagiously positive, self-starter attitude.
</li>
<li>Strong desire to achieve goals and share what works in a highly collaborative environment.
</li>
<li>Outgoing personality and the ability to engage prospects easily over the phone, in email, and over social media, as well as onsite.
</li>
<li>Provide presentations to decision makers and executive level participants.
</li>
<li>Passion to win
</li>
</ul>
