<h2>Entry Level Sales Professional</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>About the Job</b></p>
<p>Our firm is searching for a driven sales professional who understands the skills and</p>
<p>commitment needed to market professional services to people in need.</p>
<p>We are a rapidly growing company that is seeking a qualified person to work Monday -- Friday 40 hours a week,</p>
<p>make and receive around 150 inbound and outbound calls daily , daily call log report.</p>
<p>We are not a call center. This is a Full time hourly pay position with potential to grow.</p>
<p>Tax Group Center (TGC) is a full service tax debt negotiation and resolution company serving</p>
<p>individual taxpayers, corporations, associations, and small business.</p>
<p>Our goal is to negotiate the lowest possible IRS payment amount, under the most favorable schedule,</p>
<p>allowed by law, and we do not sell to consumers who are not a legitimate candidate for tax relief.</p>
<p>Through our training program, our Sales Professionals learn how to diagnose a tax problem and set up a consumer for a successful resolution by our licensed professionals and Client Services team.</p>
<p>Historically, Sales Professionals with financial experience (including tax, credit and debt consolidation) have an easier transition to success.</p>
<p>With the horsepower of national advertising the right candidate has the annual earning potential over $100,000</p>
<p><b>Responsibilities: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Commitment to a high volume of outgoing and incoming calls</li>
<li>Establish timely contact with potential clients</li>
<li>Represent our firm in the most integral, professional and ethical manner possible at all times</li>
<li>Identify sales opportunities by qualifying leads through a detailed evaluation of the client’s position and needs; research and recommend solutions we can offer</li>
<li>Offer consumers a comprehensive analysis of their position, options, and potential outcomes</li>
<li>Willingness to engage in continuing education</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Requirements: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Openness to coaching and ability to learn quickly</li>
<li>Committed to serving the clients’ best interest.</li>
<li>High-energy team player</li>
<li>Excellent organization, prioritization and follow up skills</li>
<li>Strong communication skills – verbal and written</li>
<li>Strong negotiation skills</li>
<li>Strong interpersonal/teamwork and customer service attributes</li>
</ul>
<p>Please note: The qualified candidate must be in compliance with all personal tax filings and clear of any tax liabilities –</p>
<p>this means that all returns have been filed and all outstanding tax has been paid or is current with a properly structured payment plan</p>
<p>Job Types: Full-time, Commission</p>
<p>Salary: $12.00 /hour</p>
<p>Experience:</p>
<ul><li>Sales: 2 years (Required)</li></ul>
