<p>Description</p>
</div>
<div><div>
<p>The IT Support Specialist provides technical assistance for Point of Sale (POS) systems, peripherals, hardware, + software. This position triages multiple incoming priorities, understands business’ needs, + confirms + documents the response to inquiries/requests/issues. In addition, the IT Support Specialist ensures business needs are addressed in a timely manner + delivers the highest level of satisfaction.</p>
<p><b>
THE GIG</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Provides technical support to corporate, field, + Shop teams for technical inquiries, issues, incidents, + requests in a timely manner to maintain a positive + productive work environment</li>
<li>Escalates issues + partners accordingly with internal/external teams to troubleshoot + effectively resolve issues</li>
<li>Identifies systemic/recurring issues + builds + executes processes, procedures, and/or training to effectively address them</li>
<li>Documents resolution steps + actions as well as other processes + procedures to effectively maintain a knowledge base</li>
<li>Supports ongoing systems maintenance, upgrades + enhancements, including legacy software + systems</li>
<li>Collaborates with project leads + vendors to implement new technologies + effectively integrate them into our environment (e.g., credit software, POS upgrades, etc.)</li>
<li>Troubleshoots + supports online ordering, + partners cross functionally to improve platforms + guest experience</li>
<li>Occasionally partners with internal IT + vendors to update menu pricing, promotions, etc.</li>
<li>Other duties as assigned</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p>
</p>
<p><b>THE MUST HAVES</b></p>
<ul>
<li>High School or equivalent required; advanced degree a plus</li>
<li>2+ years of experience supporting organizations preferably in the restaurant, retail and/or hospitality industry with software + hardware troubleshooting and issue resolution</li>
<li>Working knowledge + experience with hardware + peripheral troubleshooting/support, service delivery systems, remote user networking, enterprise POS management, + operating system support</li>
<li>Knowledge of networking (e.g., TCP, IP, etc.)</li>
<li>Ability to work with users with varied levels of computer/technical knowledge</li>
<li>Knowledge of Payment Card Industry Data Security (PCI) a plus</li>
<li>Knowledge of the build + maintenance of POS menus a plus</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Available to work some evenings + weekends as needed</li>
<li>Travel required (25% of the time, includes local Shop visits)</li>
</ul>
