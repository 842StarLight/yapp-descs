Description</b><div>
</div>
<div>
<b>
Forestar Group Inc.</b> is a residential lot development company and a majority-owned subsidiary of D.R. Horton, Inc., the largest homebuilder by volume in the United States since 2002.<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<div>
<b>Forestar Group</b> is currently looking for an <b>Operations Asset Specialist II</b>. The right candidate will Provide centralized support to Project and Investment Managers. Coordinate closely with related analysts for all aspects of complex acquisitions and development projects.</div>
<br>
<div>
<br>
<b>Essential Duties and Responsibilities</b> include the following. Other duties may be assigned:</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<ul>
<li>Analyze and convey real estate development and property operation for the projects assigned</li>
<li>Research, collect and review data on properties including title policies, deeds, tax records, environmental reports and HOA by-laws and covenants</li>
<li>Participate in contract administration including setting up and maintaining standardized files and purchase orders in JDE</li>
<li>Request and verify draws and funding requests</li>
<li>Generate data entry for project information and assist in maintaining lot detail information</li>
<li>Secure and maintain filing of required documents for acquisition, development and management of assigned project</li>
<li>Prepare check requests for various permits, fees and other acquisition or development related services</li>
<li>Process documentation for lot and sale closings</li>
<li>Participate in property tax management</li>
<li>Ability to work overtime</li>
<li>Regular and reliable attendance</li>
<li>Assist vendors</li>
<li>Coordinate with related analyst to ensure that contract and direct pay invoices are coded accurately and as budgeted</li>
<li>Verify budget updates</li>
<li>Provide assistance regarding HOA documents and HOA issues</li>
<li>Assist in property operation functions as assigned by the Asset Manager</li>
</ul>
