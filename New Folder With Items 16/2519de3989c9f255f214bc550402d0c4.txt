<div>Our vision for Food Safety is to support Amazon’s mission of offering the widest selection of products available, while protecting customers from potential hazardous products. We are looking for a dynamic, organized self-starter to join our Worldwide Food Safety and Compliance team as a Regional Food Safety Manager. This is an exciting opportunity to work in a highly visible space and be part of a vibrant and fast-growing company!<br>
<br>
We are looking for a Regional Food Safety Manager to join our team.<br>
<br>
High-level summary of deliverables:<br>
<ul>
<li>Implement operational site programs and processes for receipt, storage, transportation, and disposal of food to ensure customer safety;</li>
<li>Complete food safety audits at sites;</li>
<li>Work with regulatory agencies for site inspections;</li>
<li>Leverage technology to manage compliance processes;</li>
<li>Monitor business metrics to track compliance and process effectiveness;</li>
<li>Continuously identify and communicate improvement opportunities;</li>
<li>Work with stakeholders to ensure food safety requirements are met.</li>
</ul>
