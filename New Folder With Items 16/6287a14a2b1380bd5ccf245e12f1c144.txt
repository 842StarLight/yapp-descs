<h2>Assistant Manager, Retail Concessions</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>Our teammates are at the core of what we believe in: People, Collaboration, Learning, and Excellence (PeopleCLE). We look to hire individuals who are committed to our purpose of uniting and inspiring our city through the power of team. Our mission is to win the World Series while creating a compelling fan experience. If you believe you possess these important traits, please consider a career with the Cleveland Indians.</p>
<p><b>
PRIMARY PURPOSE:</b> The Assistant Retail Concessions Manager will focus on managing the daily operations of a $3M business (game day and non-game day) of the Progressive Field Concessions to maximize sales revenue and profit with a focus on customer service to ensure a quality fan experience, which promotes a positive image of the Cleveland Indians organization.</p>
<p><b>
DUTIES &amp; RESPONSIBILITIES:</b></p>
<p>
Business Management</p>
<ul>
<li>Manage sales, expense and payroll plans and goals at every level within discipline</li>
<li>Assure effective implementation of yearly financial plans and goals</li>
<li>Complete weekly, quarterly, and yearly business analysis • Additional projects/responsibilities as assigned by the Retail Concessions Manager</li>
<li>Understand and use Tableau/Chain Drive for reporting and coaching purposes</li>
</ul>
<p>People Management</p>
<ul>
<li>Interview, recruit, hire, train and develop Concessions game day and 50/50 staff; manage a team of 120+ associates</li>
<li>Develop relationships with local College and University professor’s to support Retail College Partnership program</li>
<li>Assure compliance with all Human Resources standard operating procedures</li>
<li>Oversee volunteer groups as needed for jewel events; including concerts</li>
<li>Develop diversified internal and external bench strength</li>
</ul>
<p>Inventory Management</p>
<ul>
<li>Assist Inventory Manager in overseeing inventory functions to include Concessions rolling inventories and end of season inventory. PF Team Shop midyear, end of season and yearend, Game Used rolling inventory and yearend, and the Distribution Center year-end inventory</li>
<li>Ensure proper staffing of inventory team to meet business needs</li>
<li>Assure focus of staff is always on accuracy of inventory process</li>
<li>Hold staff accountable for achieving performance levels.</li>
<li>Plan, lead and participate in pre-inventory meetings</li>
</ul>
<p>Merchandising</p>
<ul>
<li>Ensure stand appearance and stock rooms are organized and maintained to Company standards</li>
<li>Assure recovery (re-stocking, sizing, “pinch &amp; pull”, general cleanliness)</li>
<li>Assure markdowns are completed timely and accurately (including stockroom)</li>
<li>Based on research, communicate merchandising needs/opportunities to buying staff and Retail Concessions Manager</li>
</ul>
<p>Operations/Loss Prevention</p>
<ul>
<li>Proper staffing/scheduling to support the needs of the business</li>
<li>Manage payroll budget and projections based on sales and business trends</li>
<li>Manage controllable operational expenses (supplies, maintenance, etc.)</li>
<li>Understand and perform all management functions on the POS system and back room PC</li>
<li>Inventory Control (receiving, product distribution, transfers, cycle counts, damages, yearend physical inventory)</li>
<li>Proper training of staff on internal/external loss prevention methods</li>
<li>Oversee closing and banking procedures for all Ballpark and 50/50 locations</li>
<li>Prepare stands for the season and take down at the end of the season</li>
<li>Coordinate Tribe Fest/Special Event staffing and sales</li>
<li>Maintain standard operating procedures and hold all accountable for compliance to standards</li>
<li>Ensure efficient use of time and resources</li>
<li>Additional projects/responsibilities as assigned by the Retail Concessions Manager</li>
</ul>
<p>Selling/Fan Satisfaction</p>
<ul>
<li>Drive Concessions merchandise sales results</li>
<li>Role Model START selling model and suggestive selling for Concessions merchandise staff</li>
<li>Maintain the “Image of Champions” to maximize sales opportunities and ensure fan satisfaction</li>
<li>Execute corporate and departmental marketing &amp; mission plans</li>
<li>Assure focus of staff is always sales &amp; customer service based</li>
<li>Create contests to drive sales and profit results</li>
<li>Daily tracking and planning of sales</li>
<li>Utilize and analyze game day sales reports</li>
<li>Assist in planning weekly management meeting</li>
<li>Create and maintain a "Surprise and Delight" program for all areas of the Ballpark</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>EDUCATION &amp; EXPERIENCE REQUIREMENTS:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s Degree in business administration, marketing, hospitality or related major preferred</li>
<li>Previous experience managing multi-unit business or multi departmental operation</li>
<li>Previous responsibility of managing business with revenues in excess of $3M annually</li>
<li>Proven success of handling multiple responsibilities in a fast paced environment</li>
<li>Previous retail experience within a high volume operation with diverse product mix, preferred but not required</li>
<li>Computer knowledge including but not limited to Microsoft Word, Excel, and Office, as well as POS software</li>
</ul>
<p><b>ROLE REQUIREMENTS:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Strong management, planning and organizational skills</li>
<li>Ability to consistently meet deadlines</li>
<li>Ability to manage a large cadre of part-time staff</li>
<li>Required to work irregular and long shifts as the business dictates</li>
<li>Required to work a minimum of 95% of all home baseball games based on job responsibilities and workload</li>
<li>Required to work 100% of all jewel events (Opening Day, Post Season, etc.), holiday home baseball games and concerts</li>
<li>Game-day hour requirement: minimum of 2.5 hours prior to gates open and 1-hour post game as dictated by job responsibilities and workload</li>
<li>Non-game-day hours should be compliant with front office hours, with days off given during the week at the completion of a home stand, working not less than 5 days and 40 hours per week, based on job responsibilities and workload</li>
</ul>
<p><b>ORGANIZATIONAL REQUIREMENTS:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Reads, speaks, comprehends and communicates English effectively in all communications</li>
<li>Represents the Cleveland Indians in a positive fashion to all business partners and the general public</li>
<li>Ability to develop and maintain successful working relationship with members of the Front Office</li>
<li>Ability to act according to the organizational values and service excellence at all times</li>
<li>Ability to work with multicultural populations and have a commitment to fairness and equality</li>
<li>Ability to walk, sit or stand for an entire shift</li>
<li>Ability to work extended days and hours, including holidays and weekends</li>
<li>Ability to move throughout all areas and levels of the Ballpark</li>
<li>Ability to work in a diverse and changing environment</li>
<li>Occasional physical activity such as lifting and carrying boxes up to 55 lbs.</li>
</ul>
