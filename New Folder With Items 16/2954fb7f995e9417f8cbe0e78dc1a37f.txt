<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>What You'll Do Here:<br>
</b></h2>
<div></div>
<div><div><div>
<div>
<b>Position Summary:</b> As a member of the National Geographic Learning (NGL), Cengage, Sales Team, the Customer Success Program Manager is empowered with ensuring customer facing tools are accelerating the sales process. The Customer Success Program Manager is a new position that actively engages with the NGL sales team and NGL resources across the organization- such as marketing, product, and technology, to ensure we are advancing our market leading print/digital solutions. Applicants are required to personify a learner-mindset—be curious to understand the Customer’s needs and how NGL solutions can be represented in the most engaging and convincing way</div>
<div>
Key Responsibilities
</div>
<ul>
<li>Collaborate in order to curate and assemble content for presentation enablement tools in a fixed deadline</li>
<li>
Create dynamic visuals/high impact experiences to infuse in presentation enablement tools</li>
<li>
Facilitate a cross-functional review/sign-off process of presentation enablement tools</li>
<li>
Coordinate with the Customer Success Consultant Associate Mgr and CustomerSuccess Consultant(s) on customization needs and ensure customized presentation enablement tools go through review and sign-off process</li>
<li>
Actively participate in go-to-market launches and technology roadmap update meetings so evolving advantages are continually updated and emphasized in presentation enablement tools used in front of customers.
</li>
<li>Stay current and creative with presentation styles, trends, and approaches</li>
<li>
Manage a current shared repository of presentation enablement tools
</li>
<li>Post-sales training
</li>
<li>Pre-sales training – significant<br>
</li>
</ul>
