<h2>Manager - Premium Seat Sales</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Job Summary:</p>
<p><b>
Who are we?</b></p>
<p>
Live Nation Entertainment is the world’s leading live entertainment company comprised of global market leaders: Ticketmaster, Live Nation Concerts, Live Nation Media &amp; Sponsorship and Artist Nation Management. Ticketmaster is the global event ticketing leader with over 465 million tickets sold and more than 12,500 clients in 22 countries. Live Nation Concerts has become the single largest provider of live entertainment in the world producing over 25,500 shows annually for over 3,300 artists in 37 countries. Artist Nation Management is one of the world’s leading artist management companies, representing more than 350 artists. These businesses power Live Nation Media &amp; Sponsorship to deliver music marketing programs across 900 brands enabling advertisers to tap into the 63 million fans Live Nation Entertainment delivers annually through its live event and digital platforms. For additional information, visit www.livenationentertainment.com.</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Who are you?</b></p>
<p>
Passionate and motivated. Driven, with an entrepreneurial spirit. Resourceful, innovative, forward thinking and committed. At Live Nation Entertainment, our people embrace these qualities, so if this sounds like you then please read on!</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>
The Role:</b></p>
<p>
We are looking for a Manager of Sales who will be responsible for generating VIP revenue; this includes box suite, season tickets, some hospitality events and other revenue-generating programs as added to portfolio of menu items to sell.</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Responsibilities:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Sell Premium Seating to new customers and responsible for renewing contracts for existing customers. Budget requirements are set for the Director on new business sales; renewal rate is the responsibility of the Director.</li>
<li>
Develops and executes Business Plan for the VIP Premium Seat Program including: pricing, advertising/marketing, expense outline, etc.</li>
<li>
Develop and maintain client relationships in keeping with standards of the VIP Experience.</li>
<li>
Overall management and quality control of VIP Club operation in conjunction with General Management.</li>
<li>
Act as venue liaison with local Convention and Visitor’s Bureau, Chambers of Commerce, Meeting Planners Associations, and like type organizations.</li>
<li>
Develop and manage operating expense budget and adhere to budget parameters.</li>
<li>
Responsible for client entertainment at events.</li>
<li>
Assist in negotiation with internal and external vendors pertaining to the premium Seat Program under the direction of the General Manager.</li>
<li>
Management of ticket distribution to VIPs.</li>
<li>
Responsible for collection of accounts receivable for VIP Program.</li>
<li>
Secure promotional and advertising materials for department including: brochures, website information page, e-cards, etc.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Qualifications:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s Degree in General Business categories, Communications, or like field.</li>
<li>
Three to Five years direct business to business sales experience, preferably in season seats, sponsorship, media or sports marketing sales.</li>
<li>
Experience with Microsoft Office products: Word, Excel, PowerPoint</li>
<li>
Proven ability to work as a team member</li>
<li>
Proficient written and verbal communication skills required</li>
<li>
Organized and detail oriented</li>
<li>
Self motivated</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Physical Requirements/Work Environment
</b></p>
<ul><li>Sits for extended periods of time at a computer station or work desk; stands and walks regularly throughout the day; uses hands and fingers to operate computers and office equipment for up to 8 hours each day; lifts up to 10 pounds regularly; exposed to typical office environment conditions and noise levels.</li></ul>
