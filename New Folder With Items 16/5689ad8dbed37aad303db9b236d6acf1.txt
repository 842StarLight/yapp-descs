<h2>Reprise - Director, Paid Search</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div><div><div><div><div><div>
<p><b>Who we are:</b></p>
<p>Reprise is among the media industry’s fastest growing digital agencies servicing Fortune 500 brands –thanks to our culture of innovation, excellence, personal growth, and fun. We’re transforming digital experiences for our clients every day and reinventing digital marketing so that it finally works like it should.</p>
<p><b>Who you are:</b></p>
<p>You will serve as the primary day-to-day client contact on a specific piece of business. This role involves deep client vertical knowledge which leads to managing the Account Plan, developing and executing innovative search media plans and cross-discipline, and is responsible for ensuring that the client receives comprehensive media and communications plans that address their strategic objectives. Being well versed in search engine marketing is essential for this role. These responsibilities will also include the development of strong cross-functional teams and client relationships, serving as the team lead for the execution of world-class search programs.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>What your day-to-day requires:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Key participant in strategic media planning and execution</li>
<li>Serves as a resource for managers, sr. analysts / analysts for advice and counsel</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Recommends targeted, innovative media vehicles consistent with media and content strategies</li>
<li>Becomes an expert in the client’s business and builds trusted partnerships with all levels of clients</li>
<li>Ensures best practices are applied across account responsibilities</li>
<li>Demonstrates ability to present and sell strategies/plans through to the client</li>
<li>Proactively seeks out and studies information to remain current on media trends</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Recognizes and acts on opportunities to grow business</li>
<li>Primary client contact and interface for the search/social business</li>
<li>Primary contact for partner agencies for the search/social business</li>
<li>Relationship liaison between the client, the partner agency and the search/social practice</li>
<li>Develops client / partner agency presentations and leads all external communication on strategy</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Develops search/social strategy in conjunction with peers who work on the campaigns</li>
<li>Prioritizes, manages and ensures the timeliness and quality of client deliverables</li>
<li>Develops a deep understanding of the client business, relates this to the growth of the search/social practice and effectively communicates this to the teams</li>
<li>Ability to provide thought leadership in an evolving Social and Search landscape</li>
<li>Identifies up-sell and cross-sell opportunities through strategic and tactical campaign management, with detailed attention to profitability and client success</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Interpretation of client reports, with specific insight to testing validation and refinement of campaign strategies</li>
<li>Support external Business Development and Marketing initiatives such as sales pitches, case studies, etc.</li>
<br>
</ul>

<p><b>What You Bring to the Table</b></p>
<p>Education:</p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor's degree or related work experience</li>
<li>Concentration in Marketing or a related field is preferable</li>
</ul>
<p>Work Experience:</p>
<ul>
<li>6-8 years of experience in media/search agency, advertising agency, or similar media environment. Experience in a previous SEM marketing role is required. SEO and/or social experience is a plus.</li>
</ul>
<p>Skills:</p>
<ul>
<li>Ability to judge and quantify results of advertising efforts placed on behalf of our clients (i.e., understand/describe impact of results, compare actual results to needs of client) and the knowledge of how to apply this information in future campaigns.</li>
<li>Senior level client negotiation and management skills with the ability to write clearly and to make lively, imaginative but substantive presentations</li>
<li>Working knowledge of online performance tracking and measurement tools as well as leading edge media target profiling technology</li>
<li>Well versed on the latest news within the industry that fuel opinions on latest trends for clients to consider</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Proven ability to represent company philosophies and expertise to external parties; including clients and partner agencies</li>
<li>Continued enthusiasm towards the medium and the desire to constantly educate one’s self on the latest information</li>
<li>Experience in growing, managing and inspiring internal search/social teams; strong leadership and administrative skills</li>
<li>Strong interpersonal skills including clear communication skills and positive attitude</li>
<li>Solid organizational skills, attention to detail, and accuracy when working with numbers</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Strong understanding of cross channel planning, demonstrating strong coordination of digital media efforts.</li>
<li>Extensive experience writing and presenting relevant information (inclusive of case studies and quarterly business reviews)</li>
<li>Understanding of marketing and communications process, and ability to execute streamlined processes across clients, media partners, and other agencies.</li>
<br>
</ul>
