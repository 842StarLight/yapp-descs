<h2>Operations Lead, Exclusive Brands</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div><div><div><div><div><div>
<div>Position owns the operational components of the Exclusive Brands S&amp;OP for North American Retail.<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<div>
The key traits required for this position are leadership, attention to detail, problem solving, coaching, analytical mindset, ability to influence across multiple levels within the enterprise and effective communication all while embracing cultural differences spanning multiple global regions. This position primarily interfaces with the ExB Global Product Managers, Demand Planning, Forecasting and Merchant teams, Global Sourcing Operations, Global Trade Services (Customs Compliance &amp; International Transportation), Domestic supply chain (Logistics &amp; Domestic Transportation), Finance and Accounting Services.</div>
<div></div>
<div>
<b>
Job Responsibilities:</b><br>
</div>
<ul>
<li>
Global Operations and Supply Chain Management<ul>
<li>
Analyze sales demand trends and advise adjustment plan as needed.</li>
<li>
Own sales order management within SAP.</li>
<li>
Manage receipt and production planning across multiple platforms (SAP, Retek, CPFR) .</li>
<li>
Validate forecasts and understand sources of demand.</li>
<li>
Lead Operations / Category Specific Planning and Strategy Meetings.</li>
<li>
Proactively engage Demand Planning in developing strategies to address any overstock, end-of-life and product transition opportunities.</li>
<li>
Responsible for resolving operational issues with global teams.</li>
<li>
Manage expediting budget dollars.</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>
Communication<ul>
<li>
Communicate effectively across multiple functional areas including: Product Development Teams, Business Groups, Global Sourcing and International Supply Chain.</li>
<li>
Responsible for communicating key product procurement exceptions (Forecast changes, P.O. Expediting needs, etc).</li>
<li>
Lead and represent Exclusive Brands in product sales and inventory meetings.</li>
<li>
Identify and execute training / coaching opportunities for cross-functional teams on how to most effectively manage ExB products.</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>
Capability development<ul>
<li>
Determine most effective method of fulfillment: domestic fulfillment, ocean, air to DC, or DTS taking into account strategic goals and cost analysis.</li>
<li>
Be a driver in the definition and evolution of Global SOP’s.</li>
<li>
Lead/champion companywide supply chain optimization projects or initiatives.</li>
</ul>
</li>
</ul>
<div><b>
Job Qualifications:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
Bachelor’s Degree</li>
<li>
Proficient in MS Suite.</li>
<li>
4 or more years of Global Supply Chain, Inventory, Demand Planning or Merchant experience.</li>
<li>
1 or more years of Retek ERP experience, any of the Retail, Inventory or PO modules preferred.</li>
</ul>
