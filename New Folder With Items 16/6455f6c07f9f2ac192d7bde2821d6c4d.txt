<h2>Video Production Assistant</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Title</b>: Video Production Assistant</p>
<p><b>Reports to</b><b>: </b> Audiovisual Technical Director &amp; Vice President of Online Learning</p>
<p><b>Status</b>: Full-Time</p>
<p><b>Department</b>: Sandler Online</p>
<p><b>Description</b><b>: </b></p>
<p>This position is responsible for assisting with production and post-production capacities for online reinforcement programs (audio/video) and delivering live Virtual Instructor Led (VILT) training programs to clients through internal Learning Management System (LMS) platforms.</p>
<p><b>Position Location: </b><br>Sandler Systems, Inc.<br>300 Red Brook Blvd – Suite 10<br>Owings Mills, MD 21117</p>
<p><b>Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Assist with the development of multimedia (audio/video) content for corporate Learning Management System (Sandler Online). This is to include corporate training, external promotional content, product marketing, internal company announcements, and other enterprise-wide projects.</li>
<li>Support logistical and operational requirements as determined by specific event needs. Maintain daily quality control of the lower-level studio, virtual classroom, and production equipment.</li>
<li>Assist with the recording of video and sound projects. Perform post-production tasks to include; editing audios/videos, creating visual effects and motion graphics, file formatting, and archiving.</li>
<li>Create short-form training reinforcement videos for Sandler Online (LMS) and promotional videos for marketing purposes (YouTube, Vimeo, LinkedIn).</li>
<li>Utilize video production skills to assist with the technical delivery and support of Virtual Instructor Led Training (VILT) programs to clients worldwide.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Skills/Knowledge</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Must have advanced knowledge of the Adobe Creative Cloud suite. Specifically, Adobe Premiere Pro, Adobe After Effects, and Adobe Media Encoder.</li>
<li>Must have knowledge of field production tools for shooting video and capturing audio.</li>
<li>Must be a team player with the ability to work with multiple coworkers and projects concurrently.</li>
<li>Must be able to interact professionally and effectively with all levels of the organization, and clients.</li>
<li>Should have strong creative direction, graphic design skills, and professional design sense.</li>
<li>Should have familiarity with remote collaborative services such as GoToMeeting, GoToWebinar, GoToTraining, WebEx, Skype, Zoom, etc.</li>
<li>Should have strong awareness of digital landscape including web and mobile video on demand technologies, such as Vimeo, YouTube, Wistia, and other video-based applications.</li>
<li>Should be independent and adaptable to a fast-paced work environment focused on results.</li>
<li>Should have excellent written and verbal communication skills.</li>
<li>
<b>Additional applicable skills are a plus, such as: </b> motion graphics, illustration, animation, and image editing.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Education/Job Requirements</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Should have bachelor’s degree in either Multimedia/Film/Video, Mass Communication, or similar media driven majors. Candidates currently pursuing a degree in this field or with comparable job experience will also be considered.</li>
<li>Should be comfortable working in a Microsoft Windows environment.</li>
<li>Typical hours are Monday-Friday from 9AM-5PM.</li>
<li>Some overtime may be required; other duties as assigned.</li>
</ul>
