<div>REPORTS TO: Director of Sales and Marketing</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
<b>
Purpose for the Position:</b> To increase hotel revenues by meeting with 3rd party planners, trade associations, corporate accounts, individuals or other markets in order to promote the hotel and secure additional sales.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div><b>
Essential Responsibilities:</b></div>
<div></div>
<ul>
<li>
Provide professional and courteous customer service at all times.</li>
<li>
Make personal sales calls.</li>
<li>
Implement approved sales plan and action plans as directed.</li>
<li>
Develop and search for potential markets for hotel.</li>
<li>
Develop, create, and implement innovative Sales and Marketing techniques.</li>
<li>
Execute and distribute all in-house advertising and promotional campaigns.</li>
<li>
Maintain competitive analysis statistical information.</li>
<li>
Maintain current filing system on company accounts and prepare required reports.</li>
<li>
Contact in house meeting planners to promote good will and foster additional and repeat business.</li>
<li>
Maintain good rapport with local civic groups and associations.</li>
<li>
Prepare reports as requested: Sales Call Reports, Internal Sales Report.</li>
<li>
In the absence of a Director of Sales, attend and lead Sales and BEO meetings with the team.</li>
<li>
Attend weekly staff meetings.</li>
<li>
Maintain STS and prepare BEO packets and layouts for the team.</li>
<li>
Complete projects as determined by the Director of Sales and Marketing.</li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<div>
To do this kind of work, you must be able to:</div>
<div></div>
<ul>
<li>
Understand sales principles relating to the product you are selling.</li>
<li>
Organize your own activities to make the best use of your time and efforts.</li>
<li>
Express yourself well when talking to potential buyers or sellers, to discuss features of the products or services involved and convince the other person of both your knowledge and integrity.</li>
<li>
Use arithmetic in computing the cost of sale to customer.</li>
<li>
Maintain enthusiasm and interest throughout all conferences with buyers and sellers.</li>
<li>
Keep accurate records of contacts, sales, and purchases.</li>
<li>
Be available on nights, weekends and holidays.</li>
</ul>
