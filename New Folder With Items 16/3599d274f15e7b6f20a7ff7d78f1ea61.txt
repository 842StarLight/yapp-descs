<h2>Technical Sales Specialist - New England</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<b>Role:</b><br>
Hologic is seeking a Technical Sales Specialist (TSS).
<br><br>
<b>Duties &amp; Responsibilities:</b><br>
Participate in the sales process in both selling of connected devices and partnering with sales in selling of our product. Partnering with sales during the selling process by presenting the technical connectivity aspects of our product and they will integrate into their environment.
<br><br>
Assist Mammography Specialists and Account Managers with the technical aspects of our products, workflow and system functionality in an integrated healthcare environment.
<br><br>
Responsible for serving the pre-sales connectivity and customized workflow needs of the assigned sales regions.
<br><br>
Provide technical product knowledge to the Hologic Sales organization through education, sales tools, conference calls, site visits and other means of written and verbal communication.
<br><br>
Produce and deliver customer presentations related to digital mammography workflow, which highlight Hologic products and demonstrate how they can be successfully integrated into their PACS and medical IS environments. The presentations can include pictorial representations of suggested workflows and equipment layout.
<br><br>
Understand competitive threats, as related to technical product aspects and take action to minimize their impact through all means possible (i.e. sales and customer education, counter-attacks, product enhancement requests).
<br><br>
Provide support to the Engineering organization by minimizing the need for engineering’s involvement in customer pre-sales support.
<br><br>
Manage and complete special projects related to Hologic's continuous improvement in serving internal and external needs related to Connectivity and Sales. Connectivity in this context refers to the interoperation of Hologic products with other medical devices and information systems in integrated healthcare environments.
<br><br>
Participate in HSI training (Hologic Sales Institute) for new Hologic Account Managers, to explain the basics of the pre-sales process, connectivity, and how it applies to each of Hologic’s products.
<br><br>
Will be involved in cyber security conversation with both our internal and external customers.
<br><br>
Assist Connectivity Manager and other functional managers with special projects aimed at continuous improvement in product design, business operations and customer support as they relate to the integrated operation among our products as well as with our PACS and medical IS products.
<br><br>
Other duties and responsibilities as determined by business.
<br><br>
This position requires heavy travel at times and could travel up to 60% of time.
<br><br>
Must be able to repetitively lift and carry product weighing approximately 30 lbs.
<br><br>
<b>Qualifications:</b><br>
To perform this job successfully, an individual must be able to perform each essential duty satisfactorily. The requirements listed below are representative of the basic knowledge, skills, and/or abilities required:
<br><br>
Demonstrated proficiency in written and oral communications.
<br><br>
Demonstrated success in customer interaction.
<br><br>
Self-motivated and able to thrive in a team environment.
<br><br>
Available to travel to customer sites, Hologic locations and key industry conferences. Travel percentage depends on many factors including Hologic sales performance, customer and dealer needs, and overall management of the territory.
<br><br>
Ability to create and lead conference calls, customer meetings and other technical product discussion scenarios on an “on-demand” basis.
<br><br>
Ability to consistently update the sales force on new product introductions and enhancements.
<br><br>
Strong proficiency in PACS, DICOM, HIS/RIS, Networking, Storage and knowledge of industry trends.
<br><br>
Ability to translate detailed technical concepts into those that can be understood by Technologists, Radiologists and non-technical hospital personnel.
<br><br>
Ability to perceive customer requests and related technical concepts in communicating these requests to engineering.
<br><br>
<b>Education:</b><br>
Minimum of B.S. degree in technical/professional discipline such as Business Administration, Information Technology, Medical Informatics, Computer Science, and/or equivalent work experience considered.
<br><br>
<b>Experience:</b><br>
Minimum 5 years of experience in medical imaging or medical informatics industry preferred.
