<h2>Freight Supervisor</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>The Freight Supervisor functions as a member of the Store Leadership Team and is responsible for<br>
providing leadership and direction to Stock Associates while ensuring efficient and effective store<br>
operations in the freight area. Position may also provide supervision on the sales floor as assigned by the<br>
Store Manager.<p></p>
<p><b>
Responsibilities:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Supervise and oversee the store shipping &amp; receiving function.</li>
<li>
Lead and functionally supervise a team of Stock Associates.</li>
<li>
Provide direction and guidance to Stock Associates to ensure efficient and effective operation of the stock room and product flow.</li>
<li>
Maintain company standards and guidelines around product flow process and shipping &amp; receiving procedures.</li>
<li>
Ensure accurate receipt &amp; delivery of product into and out of the store. Provide efficient order management from placement of order to delivery of goods.</li>
<li>
Prepare monthly department updates to Store Manager.</li>
<li>
Ensure necessary shipping supplies are always available by planning and ordering in advance.</li>
<li>
Partner with distribution centers, customer service, and accounting when necessary. Secondary Functions:</li>
<li>
Perform opening and closing procedures such as balancing registers, preparing cash bags, and activating security system.</li>
<li>
Maintain department presentation and sales performance through upholding company and/or store direction and guidelines.</li>
<li>
Oversee and authorize cash register transactions made by Sales Associates and provide assistance/training with cash register transactions as needed.</li>
<li>
Provide excellent customer service by assisting customers with questions, concerns and product information</li>
<li>
Create and maintain an environment of strong sales and exceptional customer service through teamwork, coaching Associates, and leading by example.</li>
<li>
Perform other duties as assigned.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Requirements</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
High School Diploma or equivalent experience.</li>
<li>
Prefer 3 years previous retail operations experience with at least one year of retail supervisory, management or leadership experience.</li>
<li>
Combination of previous experience, education and relevant experience considered.</li>
<li>
Previous shipping, receiving and/or supply chain experience preferred.</li>
<li>
Skill in operating personal computers, POS systems and various software packages including Microsoft Word and Excel are needed.</li>
<li>
Ability to communicate effectively with all levels of company personnel through oral and written means is required.</li>
<li>
Effective retail math experience/aptitude/ability required</li>
</ul>
