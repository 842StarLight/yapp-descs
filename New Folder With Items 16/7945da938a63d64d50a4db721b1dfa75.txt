<h2>General Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>General Manager - Glover Park Brewery </b></p>
<p>The GM will be responsible for the management and operation of the GPB Taproom and its<br>Private Event Venue, overseeing all aspects of the business and insuring a consistent and high quality customer experience. The GM will be tasked with leading staff to achieve company objectives in sales, service, quality and appearance while maintaining a culture and atmosphere consistent with our brand. The GM will create an environment that makes people feel welcome, cultivates an appreciation for craft beer, and celebrates and supports our local community. The GM will also be responsible for basic company financial processes including accounts payable and receivable and monthly sales reporting.</p>
<p><b>Primary Duties </b></p>
<ul><li>Hire, train, and manage Taproom and Private Event staff, insuring that all duties and responsibilities are being carried out and that performance standards are being met. Insure that GPB is always staffed with quality, friendly employees that are committed to high standards of customer service.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Maintain and develop standards, policies and training programs for the Taproom and Private</li></ul>
<p>Event Venue and insure adherence</p>
<ul><li>Oversee and drive marketing, advertising, promotion and bookings for the GPB Private Event</li></ul>
<p>Venue, insuring that sales goals are met.</p>
<ul><li>Develop creative ideas for promotions for Taproom and brewery-sponsored events and work with GPB management to plan, market and execute.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Insure that the Private Events calendar is accurate and up-to-date and that GPB is adequately staffed and consistently prepared to execute and deliver a first rate guest experience.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Work with Private Event staff to maintain contract templates, insure that billing and payment records are accurately maintained, and that surveys are issued and overall customer satisfaction levels are measured and reported.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Work with Taproom assistant managers to manage Taproom employee schedule and insure consistently adequate staffing levels for all shifts.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Work with Taproom assistant managers and Private Event Staff to maintain and manage proper levels of inventory for supplies, glassware and merchandise.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Develop and maintain positive working relationships with all vendors including approved caterers, food trucks, musicians and others.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Establish and manage a rotating schedule of food trucks to serve the Taproom</li></ul>
<ul><li>Book quality music acts as needed for normal Taproom schedule and special brewery- sponsored events.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Conduct tours of the brewery for our Taproom or Private Event guests from time-to-time as needed.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Assist in maintaining GPB web site and social media content, including publishing and updating food truck and brewery events calendar.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Insure that the building and its surroundings are consistently cleaned and maintained to insure a sanitary, safe and comfortable environment.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Insure that facilities and systems are always in good working order; work with outside vendors as required for routine maintenance and repairs including pest control, HVAC, electrical, etc.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Perform administrative duties including bill payment, daily and weekly cash management, bi- monthly time and attendance reporting for payroll, and monthly sales reporting.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Understand and insure compliance with all federal, state, county and local regulations related to health, safety and labor requirements for the Taproom, the Private Event Venue, and our employees and customers.</li></ul>
<p><b>Requirements </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Minimum of 4 years management experience in the hospitality industry (preferably bar, restaurant or event venue)</li>
<li>Ample knowledge of craft beer</li>
<li>Experience operating a cash register and POS system</li>
<li>Proficient with Excel</li>
<li>Ability to lift 55 pounds and to transport heavy objects using a dolly or pallet jack</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Qualifications </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Friendly, customer service oriented personality and a healthy sense of humor</li>
<li>Calm under pressure with crisis management skills</li>
<li>Strong commitment to helping build a new business and brand</li>
<li>Excellent written and verbal communication skills</li>
<li>Proficiency with Square and Gather is a plus</li>
</ul>
<p>GPB offers competitive compensation based on experience and qualifications. If interested please forward a resume and cover letter describing why you would be a great fit our GM role.</p>
<p>Job Type: Full-time</p>
<p>Experience:</p>
<ul><li>Hospitality Management: 4 years (Required)</li></ul>

<ul><li>Marietta, GA (Required)</li></ul>
