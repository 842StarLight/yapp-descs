<h2>Marketing Operations Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>
<div>Kenna Security is revolutionizing cyber risk with a SaaS-based platform that uses data science to combine vulnerability data with exploit intelligence to measure risk, predict attacks and prioritize remediation. We are leading the way in helping Enterprises to reduce their risk while increasing their efficiency and preventing attacks.</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
As a member of the Demand Generation team, the Marketing Operations Manager will be focused on driving results for the entire marketing organization by bridging the gap between marketing strategy, processes, metrics and technology. Specifically, the ideal candidate will drive data and functional integration between our website and prospect signup pipeline, as well as marketing lead nurturing and sales qualification processes. This unique opportunity will allow you to own measurement and attribution of our B2B marketing and drive sales marketing alignment through process and technology.</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
You will work with cross functional teams such as Demand Generation, Field Marketing, Sales Development Reps, and Sales Operations to design, build and measure meaningful touch points across multiple channels that resonate with our target audience. This position is based out of our San Francisco headquarters and will report to the Director of Demand Generation.</div>
</div>
<div><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>KEY RESPONSIBILITIES</b></h3>
<div><ul>
<li>
Build, scale, and maintain the integrity of our MarTech stack, including SFDC, Marketo, Engagio, and InsightsSquared.</li>
<li>
Own our Marketo instance including database health, data acquisition and flow across systems, automation logic, forms/form handlers, segmentation, campaign management, and list management.</li>
<li>
Collaborate with Sales Operations, Demand Generation and Field Marketing to continuously improve our scoring models in Marketo and Engagio.</li>
<li>
Effectively measure Marketing campaign attribution and pipeline using both Salesforce and multi touch attribution platform.</li>
<li>
Define and deliver core business metrics, dashboards, reporting and analytics on demand gen activities and funnel performance across all business lines using InsightsSquared.</li>
<li>
Evaluate new technologies and systems to improve and optimize performance.</li>
<li>
Document all processes to facilitate collaboration and learning across the entire organization.</li>
</ul></div>
</div></div>
<div><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>EXPERIENCE</b></h3>
<div><ul>
<li>
BA/BS degree with a strong academic record.</li>
<li>
5+ years of experience in marketing operations at a B2B SaaS company.</li>
<li>
Extensive experience managing a SFDC-based MarTech stack, including integrations.</li>
<li>
Deep hands-on experience with marketing automation software (Marketo and Engagio strongly preferred).</li>
<li>
Successful track record of creating, executing, and measuring campaigns, including database management, segmentation, setup, testing, launching and scoring of campaigns and programs, and reporting on campaign analytics.</li>
<li>
Experience with reporting tools such as InsightsSquared.</li>
<li>
Strong familiarity with B2B demand gen programs within a SaaS environment, lead lifecycles and direct marketing principles and strategy.</li>
<li>
Experience in making data-driven decisions and driving business transformation initiatives.</li>
<li>
Exceptional analytical and quantitative skills with great attention to detail.</li>
<li>
Growth mindset with inherent curiosity and personal drive; ability to navigate ambiguity and operate independently in a fast-paced, startup environment.</li>
</ul></div>
