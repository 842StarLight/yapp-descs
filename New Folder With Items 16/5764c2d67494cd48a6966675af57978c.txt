<h2>Collection Supervisor</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>The Collection Supervisor is responsible for the collections and recoveries operations of McCarthy, Burgess &amp; Wolff, Inc. (MBW), including all aspects of operational management, performance, and quality. The Collection Supervisor oversees several industry-specific business unit collection departments, the staff within those units, and the duties and accomplishments of those units. The Collection Supervisor is responsible for Collectors and their production. Additionally, the Collection Supervisor ensures compliance with SSAE 16, ISO 27002, and Sarbanes Oxley within the business units she/he oversees.</p>
<p><b>Essential Job Functions</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Manages, trains, mentors, guides, and directs collection agents ensuring professional development aimed at building bench strength of management and supervisory candidates to draw on as company grows.</li>
<li>Delivers performance feedback, including performance appraisals, development action plans, and disciplinary actions.</li>
<li>Facilitates team meetings and training sessions.</li>
<li>Analyzes reports and statistical data to improve operational performance.</li>
<li>Optimizes daily workload volumes and prioritizes work efforts for Collection staff.</li>
<li>Manages inventory levels, staffing needs, segmentation, and reporting.</li>
<li>Provides motivation, coaching, and development to meet and exceed departmental goals.</li>
<li>Drives team performance and delivers on critical performance measurements including team productivity, efficiency, staffing, and quality.</li>
<li>Fosters a team environment to promote the highest level of services to all MBW clients with a goal of achieving the number one competitive position in the marketplace.</li>
<li>Continually recruits, interviews, and hires qualified employees.</li>
<li>Oversees front-line hiring, training, and coaching.</li>
<li>Optimizes daily workload volumes and prioritizes work efforts for entire collection staff.</li>
<li>Manages inventory levels, staffing needs, segmentation, and reporting.</li>
<li>Prepares collections forecast for each business unit and monitors actual vs. forecasted performance.</li>
<li>Develops manual and dialer collection strategies.</li>
<li>Enforces strict adherence to all processes and protocols for effective debt collection in accordance with Federal and State laws and in accordance with the requirements of MBW and its clients.</li>
<li>Builds collaborative business partner relationships with stakeholder groups (e.g., HR, IT, Executive Management) to optimize results.</li>
<li>Leads continuous improvement initiatives in the collection process and results while working in cooperation with other departments.</li>
<li>Reads, comprehends and adheres to the Fair Debt Collections Practices Act (FDCPA).</li>
<li>Reads, comprehends, and adheres to any and all applicable International Organization for Standardization (ISO) policies.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Job Requirements</b></p>
<p><b>Knowledge: </b>Must have comprehensive knowledge and experience in collections, skip-tracing, compliance, client service, staff development and training, and administrative functions; familiarity with MBW policies, procedures, client standards and requirements, FDCPA, HIPAA, CRS Titanium, and Microsoft Outlook. Comprehensive grasp of all collection laws, both federal and state.<br><b>Experience: </b>At least 5 years experience in working as a team Supervisor in 3rd party Collections. Must have a proven operations track record in the collection industry of driving results through teams, including teams managed by direct reports; must have a history of complex project and process management and continuous improvement practices.<br><b>Skills/Aptitudes</b>: Exceptionally organized and detail oriented with the demonstrated ability to work in a fast-paced environment, multifaceted and works well under pressure. Proven negotiation, analytical, and critical thinking skills. Must have good project management, sales and strategic planning abilities; in-depth knowledge about MBW's offerings; and be comfortable training end users in one-on-one and group settings. Strong problem-solving and follow-through skills and be able to work independently, lead teams and interact with various levels of management. Good computer skills and proficiency with word processing and spreadsheet software programs are required.<br><b>Interpersonal Skills: </b>Ability to drive cross-functional projects, prioritize conflicting demands, take initiative, work proactively and be resourceful; outstanding interpersonal skills with the ability to work both independently and collaboratively with a diverse set of personalities and a strong client focus; and self-starter with a desire to grow/learn. Proven ability to lead, teach, and persuade others while interacting effectively in difficult situations; must be able to effectively interact with and influence Executive management, working simultaneously to achieve company objectives.</p>
