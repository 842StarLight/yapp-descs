<h2>Fire Alarm Lead Technician</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<b>Location:</b> Nashville, TN
<br>
<p></p>
<div><p>Oversight and development of Fire Alarm Technicians who install, inspect and repair fire alarm systems in new and existing buildings in all types of construction, using conduit, hangers, wires, alarm devices, and panels. The Lead Technician must be able to perform test and inspection, and basic repair and maintenance on major makes and models of fire alarm systems with minimal supervision. This position will require the ability to support our sales staff as well do estimates for customers.</p></div>

Requirements:</b></h3>
<div><ul>
<li>College Degree a Plus, High School diploma or GED required</li>
<li>Fire Alarm Service Experience</li>
<li>Low Voltage Electronics Experience</li>
<li>Special Hazards Experience a plus</li>
<li>Gamewell/FCI experience/certification a plus</li>
<li>Fike Corporation experience/certification a plus</li>
<li>NICET Certification II or above a plus. The ability to become NICET Certified required. Fire Alarm Design Experience a plus.</li>
<li>Physically capable of performing the essential functions of being a fire alarm technician including: handling material potentially in excess of 80lb; working with tools off scaffolds, ladders, and aerial lifts at heights potentially in excess of 100 ft; working in confined spaces; and working in adverse weather conditions.</li>
<li>Strong values and a dedication to working with honesty and integrity at all times</li>
<li>Commitment to excellence and driven to team success</li>
<li>Strong written and verbal communication skills. Ability to use and learn computer software and devices.</li>
<li>Well-developed active listening, critical thinking, and deductive reasoning skills</li>

<li>Provide guidance and direction to crews in meeting the budgets and schedule dates of each project.</li>
<li>Ensure job quality and customer satisfaction is maintained in every stage of the project.</li>
