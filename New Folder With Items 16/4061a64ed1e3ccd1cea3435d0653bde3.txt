<h2>Sales Representative- Fabrication</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Leading steel processing company is seeking a Fabrication Sales Representative for its Huger, SC plant. This position requires a wide range of functions related to sales, administrative, and customer service tasks.</p>
<p></p>
<p>Position Requirements:</p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor's Degree Preferred or Associates Degree or Equivalent Work Experience;</li>
<li>Minimum Two (2) Years Steel or Metal Fabrication Sales Experience Required;</li>
<li>Valid driver's license;</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p>The right person possesses;</p>
<ul>
<li>Excellent interpersonal and communication skills with ability to negotiate and influence other, develop and deliver presentations, and ability to create, compose and edit written materials;</li>
<li>Working knowledge of advertising and sales promotion techniques with emphasis on post-sale customer service;</li>
<li>Ability to maintain professional appearance and provide positive, trustworthy company image to public;</li>
<li>Proficient reasoning ability to define problems, collect data, establish facts, and draw valid conclusions to facilitate sales objectives;</li>
<li>Working knowledge of Microsoft Office applications with ability to learn and use specific ERP software and other manufacturing software systems;</li>
<li>Ability to multitask and stay focused in a fast pace environment;</li>
<li>Ability to work a flexible schedule with significant (up to 80%) local. overnight travel;</li>
<li>Total visual field, multiple limb coordination, and ability to sit/ stand as needed;</li>
<li>Distinct aptitudes to perform job duties with care, commitment, passion, and pride;</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p>Salary is commensurate with education/ experience.</p>
<p></p>
<p>Excellent benefits include medical with deductibles as low as $1,000 and wellness credit up to $750; voluntary 401k plan deduction with varying company match per quarter up to 12%; voluntary dental and vision plans; free telemedicine services; basic life and short term disability coverage.</p>
