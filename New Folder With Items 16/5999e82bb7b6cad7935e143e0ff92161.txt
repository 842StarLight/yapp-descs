<h2>Process Solutions Expert</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p>Sulzer Chemtech, a division of Sulzer Ltd, has leading positions in the fields of process technology, separation towers and polymer and crystallization. With locations for sales, engineering, production as well as installation and maintenance service, Sulzer Chemtech maintains a worldwide presence for its clients in the oil and gas, hydrocarbon processing and other industrial markets.</p>
<div>
Looking for a new opportunity? Sulzer Chemtech is seeking a <b>Process Solutions Expert</b> to join our team in Tulsa, OK.</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div>
<b>SUMMARY: </b>Responsible for coaching, guiding and training ES-APT engineers on conceptual process designs, support management in the implementation of improved structures for handling inquiries, project screening and executing complex projects</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div><b>ESSENTIAL FUNCTIONS:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Coaching, guiding and training ES-APT on conceptual process design with an emphasis on process solutions and hybrid processes.</li>
<li>Consulting and support for ES-APT regarding separation process feasibility, new applications or technologies, available know-how and experience</li>
<li>Process design lead for selected applications</li>
<li>Reviews/Checks/Approves output of colleague’s process engineering work (inc. Senior Engineers)</li>
<li>Strategic support to Sales in finding "new" customer's, markets and applications for process solutions</li>
<br>
</ul>
<div></div>
<div><b>SECONDARY FUNCTIONS</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Performs duties of a Senior Process Engineer when needed</li>
<li>Support to Operations and Sales groups</li>
<br>
</ul>
<div></div>
<div><b>SUPERVISORY RESPONSIBILITIES:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Supervises, checks and approves the work of other Process Engineers (incl. Senior Engineers)</li>
<br>
</ul>
<div></div>
<div><b>QUALIFICATIONS:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor of Science Degree in Chemical Engineering (Masters preferred)</li>
<li>15+ years related process engineering experience working with thermal separation equipment and systems
</li>
<li>Demonstrate industry leading knowledge of processes and systems for the Fine and Specialty Chemical markets</li>
<li>Ability to interpret customer requirements and to design innovative separation processes and equipment</li>
<li>Analytical capability as required in collecting, reviewing, assessing and interpreting customer’s data and process requirements and developing responsive design specifications and parameters.</li>
<li>Communication and interpersonal skills, as required in working effectively with internal and customer personnel to interpret their requirements, and resolve problems and issues that arise.</li>
<li>Oral communication skills, as required in interacting effectively one-on-one with all levels of personnel and delivering professional presentations to groups.</li>
<li>Proficiency in the use of personal computers, including word-processing, database, spreadsheet, electronic simulation software applications such as PROII, Chemcad or Aspen, drawing software such as Autocad</li>
<br>
</ul>
<div></div>
<div>
<b>TRAVEL: </b>This position will require business travel, including occasional international travel</div>
