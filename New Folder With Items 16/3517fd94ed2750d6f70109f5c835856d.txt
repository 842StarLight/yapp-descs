<h1 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>RVP (Regional Vice President) - Sales</b></h1>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Location: Salt Lake City, Utah</b></h3>
The RVP Sales leads a defined sales territory effort to meet the Enterprise sales goals. These goals include revenue, hiring and managed growth from the sales team. This position also develops new sales strategies and teaches the sales team to implement. The RVP Sales is responsible for the corporate sales team within NICE inContact and will manage the Enterprise sales team to specific performance targets, exceeding quotas and delivering appropriate quantities of new deals.<br>
<br>
<br>
<b>As an RVP Sales, a Typical Day Might Include the Following:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Support Senior Vice President of Sales by achieving or exceeding sales targets.</li>
<li>
Recruit, Select and Train inside/outside sales talent.</li>
<li>
Sales Forecasting.</li>
<li>
Negotiate profitable contracts, terms and conditions.</li>
<li>
Develop unified messaging with lead generation team.</li>
<li>
Increase pipeline through demand generation and targeted campaigns.</li>
<li>
Manage/overcome prospect objections.</li>
<li>
Learn and maintain in-depth knowledge of NICE inContact product lines.</li>
<li>
Create new revenue opportunities with focus on driving business to close in new and existing accounts.</li>
<li>
Train sales team on managing complex sales cycles.</li>
<li>
Build collaborative corporate and field teams with strategies for sales execution.</li>
<li>
Assist with sale and closure of national house accounts.</li>
<li>
Develop, Coach, Mentor Sales Leadership team.</li>
<li>
Travel 50%+ via air, car, rail, etc.</li>
<li>
Maintains a home office setting if residing outside of the SLC, UT region in compliance with NICE inContact guidelines.</li>
<li>
Maintains valid driver’s license and vehicle insurance and registration.</li>
</ul>
