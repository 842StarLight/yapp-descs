<h1 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Business Development</b></h1>
<p>The Business Development position is responsible for promoting and selling service agreements for Growth of CCG Automation locally, regionally, and nationally. Determine customer needs using a consultative Total Cost of Ownership approach and develop sales strategies to meet those objectives. Develop a positive ongoing relationship with customers. Develop and maintain an active proposal backlog that will support the sales plan.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Essential Duties and Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Manage &amp; grow opportunities by proactively calling new prospects, sales leads or customers</li>
<li>Establish long-term business relationships with new customers</li>
<li>Develop comprehensive account strategies to win new business</li>
<li>Present, propose, negotiate &amp; close new accounts using a consultative sales approach</li>
<li>Close sufficient sales to meet sales plan objectives</li>
<li>Make necessary sales presentations to customers and management as requested</li>
<li>Business development activities including, but not limited to:
<ul>
<li>Attending industry-related association events</li>
<li>Participation in trade shows</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>Excellent interpersonal skills &amp; ability to work successfully with a variety of people</li>
<li>Apply interpersonal skills to influence &amp; persuade stakeholders to achieve desired outcomes</li>
<li>Communicate regularly with Senior Vice President to provide information on specific issues, sales, information, and concerns</li>
<li>Keep detailed sales forecasts of identified &amp; proposed opportunities utilizing the company’s CRM software</li>
<li>Prepare sales reports as required</li>
<li>Remain proficient &amp; knowledgeable on company’s service offerings</li>
<li>Partner with internal departments to communication customer needs</li>
<li>Highly organized, self-motivated individual with ability to prioritize own work to meet agreed upon deadlines</li>
<li>Must be energetic &amp; have a positive attitude</li>
<li>Ability to work in a fast-paced, challenging environment</li>
<li>Strong work ethic &amp; ability to perform with minimal supervision and work well as part of a team</li>
<li>Position requires travel as related to clients, training or management meetings</li>
<li>Performs other duties as required<br>
</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Education and Experience</b><br>
Must have excellent communication skills; have the ability to persuade and close sales. Ten (10) + years of sales experience in a related business with a proven successful record of accomplishment that is verifiable. Bachelor’s Degree in a technical or business discipline preferred, or equivalent combination of education and experience. HVAC knowledge is strongly preferred.<br>
</p>
<p><b>Required Certificates, Licenses &amp; Registrations</b><br>
Valid driver’s license and clean driving record and background check.<br>
</p>
<p><b>Required Position Knowledge, Skills and Abilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Sales and Marketing — Knowledge of principles and methods for showing, promoting, and selling products or services. This includes marketing strategy and tactics, product demonstration, sales techniques, and sales control systems.</li>
<li>Customer and Personal Service — Knowledge of principles and processes for providing customer and personal services. This includes customer needs assessment, meeting quality standards for services, and evaluation of customer satisfaction.</li>
<li>Critical Thinking — Using logic and reasoning to identify the strengths and weaknesses of alternative solutions, conclusions or approaches to problems.</li>
<li>Complex Problem Solving — Identifying complex problems and reviewing related information to develop and evaluate options and implement solutions.<br>
 </li>
</ul>

<ul>
<li>Microsoft Word (Advanced)</li>
<li>Microsoft Excel (Beginner – Intermediate)</li>
<li>Microsoft PowerPoint (Beginner – Intermediate)</li>
<li>Microsoft Outlook</li>
<li>CRM software<br>
</li>
</ul>
