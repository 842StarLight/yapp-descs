<h2>Inside Client Success Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>JOB SUMMARY:
</b></p>
<p></p>
<p>An Inside Client Success Manager is responsible for developing and training TRUECar Independent Dealers to increase their participation and success in our program. Inside Client Success Managers report to a Market Director.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>JOB RESPONSIBILITIES:
</b></p>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Build and grow long-term, trusting relationships with a range of contacts at assigned Independent dealerships (buyer/signer, implementer, stakeholder, etc.), including the highest level in each organization (e.g., Owner/General Manager)
</li>
<li>Consult with customers to maintain up-to-date understanding of their business needs, goals, and objectives
</li>
<li>Ability to articulate, communicate and train the full suite of TRUECar Services over the phone and via ClearSlide presentations
</li>
<li>When required, in-person dealership meetings will take place to strengthen our dealer relations
</li>
<li>Interpret and deliver new rates on a monthly, quarterly or Bi-Annual basis to their assigned market and dealer customers
</li>
<li>Interpret and deliver monthly TRUECar reporting and analytics to explain dealer's performance, optimization issues, and best practices to the customer in the context of their business goals and market trends
</li>
<li>Train customers to use core TRUECar services and Affinity Partner Programs; utilize other business unit specialists for advanced service training when needed. (Dealer Support, Dealer Sales, Training Managers, etc.)
</li>
<li>Compile and share relevant reports or documentation related to the customers' business and performance with TRUECar as it relates to their business for the benefit of customers and sales and business development
</li>
<li>Serve as a first point-of-contact for dealer issues; diagnose and resolve customer performance concerns
</li>
<li>Effectively manage the progress and results of customers assigned and work with sales and business development leadership to contribute and manage actions according to account strategy
</li>
<li>Reduce Churn across assigned book of business of Independent Dealers
</li>
<li>Determine the right frequency to meet with all assigned customers based on developed dealer plan(s), each customer's individual needs, as well as prioritization across all customers.
</li>
<li>Escalate risks or roadblocks for a customer; work with Dealer Support and Sales Leadership to resolve
</li>
<li>Provide accurate and complete documentation in Salesforce
</li>
<li>Consistently meet goals and objectives; foster high standards within the team
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>REQUIRED SKILLS:
</b></p>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>High School Diploma or GED required
</li>
<li>3+ years of sales, service or support or related experience required
</li>
<li>Experience with Microsoft Suite, including PowerPoint
</li>
<li>Experience with Customer Relationship Management (CRM) platforms, such as, Salesforce
</li>
<li>Experience delivering presentations to key decision makers
</li>
<li>Knowledge of successful training techniques and keep a small audience engaged
</li>
<li>Up to 20% travel required with the ability to travel by airplane or car including overnight trips
</li>
</ul>
