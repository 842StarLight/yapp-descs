<h2>Territory Account Specialist/ Sales Merchandising</h2>

<b>Division Information</b><br>
<p>Hardware and Home Improvement (HHI) is a major manufacturer and supplier of residential locksets, residential builders' hardware, faucets, and commercial locksets and doors, with a portfolio of renowned brands, including Kwikset®, Weiser®, Baldwin®, National Hardware®, Stanley®, FANAL®, Pfister™, EZSET®, and Tell. HHI is a leader in its key markets with #1 positions in U.S. residential locksets (Kwikset), Canada residential locksets (Weiser), U.S. luxury locksets (Baldwin), and U.S. builders' hardware (Stanley-National Hardware), #2 in Mexico residential hardware (Fanal), #3 in U.S. retail plumbing (Pfister), and #1 in U.S. retail commercial locksets. Headquartered in Orange County, California, HHI has a global sales force and operates manufacturing and distribution facilities in the U.S., Canada, Mexico and Asia. HHI is a division of Spectrum Brands Holdings, Inc.</p>
<br>
<b>
Job Summary</b><br>
<p>If you’re a strong salesperson with the drive and desire to build a lucrative book of business by promoting a well-known hardware brand, this is your opportunity to take ownership of a prime territory. This dynamic sales position is responsible for driving top line sales and key customer penetration for a specific territory of National Hardware accounts. Responsibilities will include a variety of sales, merchandising, and marketing activities designed to increase sales and build market share.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<b>
Primary Duties &amp; Responsibilities</b><br>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>
<b>(70%) Drive Sales
</b><ul>
<li>Actively manage and grow established accounts within a large defined territory, as well as develop and close new business.</li>

<li>Identify and prioritize new/prospective customers, and actively pursue opportunities for product placement; competitive conversion of new customers.</li>
<li>Develop and conduct sales presentations for key customers regarding New Products, Line Expansions, and opportunities for Additional Product Placement.</li>
<li>Execute competitive category conversion within existing accounts, core National Hardware and ancillary category sales.<br>
<p></p>
</li>
</ul>
</li>
