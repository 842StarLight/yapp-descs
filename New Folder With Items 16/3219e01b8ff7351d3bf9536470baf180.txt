<h2>Customer Support Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>RxVantage is a small company making a big impact. Located in El Segundo, CA, we create software to transform the way physicians receive education about the latest medical breakthroughs and technology, to improve patient care. To date, we’ve powered over 5 million educational exchanges between physicians and life science companies. We have an important mission, proven product-market fit, a great team and technology that’s second to none. We are on the hunt for exceptional talent that is eager to do the best work of their life and help build a great company.</p>
<p>We're looking for a bright, affable and motivated Customer Support Specialist to join our Customer Success team. In this role, you will be assisting new and existing users on the platform. You should have excellent follow-up skills, be willing to wear many hats in a fun, fast-paced environment and be able to communicate information clearly to customers and internal teams. In short, you will ensure that our customers have an excellent overall experience on the RxVantage technology platform.
</p>
<p><b>If this sounds like a good fit for you, we should chat.</b></p>
<br>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
What you’ll be doing:</b></h3>
<ul>
<li>Assist new and existing customers with product specific technical support and navigating the company's web application, via email, telephone and chat.</li>
<li>Direct complex technical issues to appropriate company personnel</li>
<li>Provide input to develop and refine Customer Support systems and processes</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Requirements</b></p>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader">
<b>
What you should have:</b><br>
</h3>
<ul>
<li>Excellent ability to multi-task and prioritize to maintain required high level of customer service</li>
<li>Ability to problem-solve, coupled with a desire to take on responsibility</li>
<li>Must be high energy and possess excellent written and verbal communication skills</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Must be detailed in documenting information and possess strong follow-through techniques</li>
<li>4 year degree required</li>
<li>Nice-to-haves:<ul>
<li>Prior sales or customer support experience</li>
<li>Experience working in Salesforce</li>
</ul>
</li>
</ul>
