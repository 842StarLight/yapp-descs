<h2>Marketing Specialist - BIOVIA</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<div><b>Imagine new horizons</b></div>
<div><ul>
<li>
BIOVIA, a brand of Dassault Systèmes, is a leading scientific product lifecycle management software and services company. BIOVIA’s customer base includes the world’s largest pharmaceutical, biotechnology, chemical and petroleum companies.</li>
<li>
As a member of the BIOVIA Brand Marketing team, you will represent the BIOVIA brand and support the Team’s objective to connect with more users and get to know them better via user events as well as via online communities.</li>
</ul></div>
<div><b>
What will your role be?</b></div>
<div><ul>
<li>
Build the customer champion program for BIOVIA brand with customers, prospects, user groups and capture and grow customer groups for the BIOVIA organization.</li>
<li>
Execute the Brand Advocacy Marketing strategy to strengthen brand awareness and reputation through the attraction, activation, engagement and the retention of the actual and potential users</li>
</ul></div>
<div><b>
The challenges ahead</b></div>
<div><ul>
<li>
Identify and engage with client and brand advocates; capture influencers’ stories and develop the relationships to strengthen their loyalty to BIOVIA and build sustainable user references.</li>
<li>
Leverage technical social expertise and experience to create more brand awareness amongst user groups on social media and internal platforms</li>
<li>
Grow BIOVIA communities, engagement, followers and activity through the creation of interactive topics/threads, trend awareness in BioScience space, etc.</li>
<li>
Define, execute and manage strategies and programs that result in the growth interaction for online communities (blogs, discussion platforms, LinkedIn, Facebook, Twitter, etc)</li>
<li>
Provide reporting on the pulse and engagement of the community, community growth, and other relevant topics; advise executive and product teams on major trends within user groups</li>
<li>
Ensure the delivery, relevancy, consistency of Brand content through communication, events, Social Media in line with the Brand Promise</li>
<li>
Ensure relevancy, consistency and convergence of Brand messages with the Brand Promise and Strategic Plan and shared through the multi-touch marketing programs</li>
<li>
Monitor Public &amp; Private/ Physical &amp; Digital user communities’ participation and define user groups and editorial publishing calendar, develop and curate content (user testimonies, brand content), monitor discussions, postings, and trends within the communities</li>
<li>
Define, implement and follow-up Customer Loyalty programs in order to identify Brand user champions and influencers to strengthen their loyalty to the Brand and to build sustainable user references</li>
</ul></div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div><b>Your key success factors?</b></div>
<div><ul>
<li><div>
Education: BA/BS required, preferably in a marketing discipline</div></li>
<li><div>
2+ years of experience in a Marketing based role in a professional setting</div></li>
<li><div>
2+ years of experience managing social and digital marketing communities and corporate social media presence</div></li>
<li><div>
Possesses a strong strategic planning and analysis skillset in sales, marketing, and business (competitive) strategy</div></li>
<li><div>
Familiarity with using social media for business (Facebook, LinkedIn, YouTube, Twitter, etc.)</div></li>
<li><div>
Articulate and well-written/spoken with a proactive, resourceful and optimistic approach</div></li>
<li><div>
Experience in coordinating and monitoring social media strategies that have contributed to the achievement of corporate marketing goals</div></li>
<li><div>
Experience being responsible for updating and maintaining social media and community best practices and emerging trends documentation is preferred</div></li>
<li><div>
Experience providing performance reports and recommendations through collecting, analyzing, and summarizing data and trends is preferred</div></li>
<li><div>
Influence employee product experts to participate and provide content for blogs and social and conferences</div></li>
<li><div>
Assist in coordination of customer conferences for logistics and communication</div></li>
<li><div>
Ensure accurate scientific content is published by coordinating with employees</div></li>
</ul></div>
