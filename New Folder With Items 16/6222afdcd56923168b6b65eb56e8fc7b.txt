<h2>IT Integration Delivery Manager II</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>Overview
</p>
<div><div><div>
<div>Leveraging deep integrations into retailers’ point-of-sale systems, InComm is revolutionizing the commerce experience through innovative payments technology. InComm leverages end to end solutions to bring branded activatable products to retailers both in store and digitally. Our process includes everything from card printing, inventory management and forecast analytics, to the development and integration of activation technology in point of sale machines world-wide. From there we leverage our backend platform as a service technology to drive product activation to the tune of several thousand card activations every second. Whether those consumers are activating prepaid products, paying bills, enjoying real-time discounts through a membership card, purchasing digital goods in-store or adding funds to an online account, InComm is there to provide unique gift-gifting opportunities, cater to on-the-go shoppers, deliver added value through loyalty programs and serve cash-based consumers.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
InComm is headquartered in Atlanta, GA with a presence in over 30 countries, an organization of approximately 3,000 employees, several hundred international technical patents, and a network that includes over 600,000 points of retail distribution. With innovation as our number 1 company value, our speed to market for new products and services is accelerating rapidly and quickly positioning InComm as a global leader in fintech innovation. Learn more at www.incomm.com or connect with us on Twitter, Facebook, LinkedIn, or Our Blog.</div>

<p>Responsibilities
</p>
<div><div><div>
<ul>
<li>Drive the collaboration of cross functional technology and business teams to produce a technology onboarding strategy for integrations, acquisitions and operational initiatives.</li>
<li>
Provide leadership for urgent tactical or strategic initiatives to ensure business growth and service delivery.</li>
<li>
Partner with the enterprise architecture, infrastructure, application and security teams to ensure an understanding of the technology roadmap and guidelines which will be utilized to recommend integration for strategic direction.</li>
<li>
Engage and collaborate with release teams to provide oversight or input on the approach for technical integrations, implementations and migrations. Act as an escalation point of contact to assist with removing challenges and blockers and assist with developing mitigation plans when necessary.</li>
<li>
Lead teams to identify IT Operational process gaps and then implement procedures and process improvements to address those gaps.</li>
<li>
Lead teams to provide end to end delivery management of a program or project; facilitate groups and mobilize resources to quickly agree to strategy that provides clear direction.</li>
<li>
Create and deliver clear, concise and engaging status updates to both peer and executive level audiences across both IT and the Business.</li>
<li>
Collaborate with the Integrations Delivery Management team to develop the framework for integration opportunites through creation and delivery of overall integration strategy, process, and execution plans.</li>
<li>
Take initiative and have the ability to make independent decisions to remove blockers or expedite project timelines.</li>
<li>
At times leverage project management skillsets to organize teams, assignments, common formats, meeting schedules, and standardized structures to coordinate or deliver integration initiatives.</li>
<li>
Adapt and re-calibrate plans as an initiative or strategic delivery evolves, keeping business objectives, timeline, and integration at the forefront of prioritization.</li>
<li>
Become a subject matter expert and primary point of contact for organizational functions inclusive of system integrations, operations, as well as products and services.</li>
<li>
Approach mergers and integrations with an innovative and entrepreneurial blueprint to foster both immediate and long-term methodologies for the business.</li>
</ul>
