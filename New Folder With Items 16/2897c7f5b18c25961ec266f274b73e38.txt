<h2>Sr Director, Product Management – Digital Commerce</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Senior Director Product Management – Digital Commerce
 </b></p>
<p>We're looking for a self-motivated individual who will take pride in owning product management responsibility to build our marketing leading Digital Commerce products as part of Apttus Quote-to-Cash product portfolio. This individual will have strong customer-centric product management leadership experience, the ability to work on multiple releases, and will take the initiative to improve our products.</p>
<p>
The Sr. Director Product Management position will play a major role in defining, prioritizing, and executing product features within Apttus’ core product line. The Product Manager must be able to research existing functionality and solution new features in accordance with the vision and direction of the product. To succeed in this role, you must have strong attention to detail, great communication skills, and the ability to manage deadlines, learn quickly, defend your decisions, and deliver clear and concise specifications. This position will require constant and effective interaction with developers, UX designers, QA, and customer-facing teams. Product management at Apttus represents a great opportunity to be a part of some of the hottest trends in technology and the quote to cash category today.</p>
<p><br>
<b>PRIMARY RESPONSIBILITIES</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Lead Apttus B2B Digital commerce product line</li>
<li>
Bring the voice of the customer to the product development team</li>
<li>
Work closely with engineering to innovate on and build out Apttus <b>Digital Commerce</b>. You'll be driving strategy, prioritizing deliverables, managing design and supporting the engineering process.</li>
<li>
Manage end-to-end product lifecycle including product strategy, product specs, product development, testing, product demos, supporting sales among others</li>
<li>
Document features/functionality for internal/external consumption, e.g., release notes</li>
<li>
Perform gap analysis and business analysis</li>
<li>
Use web and data analytics, and competitive analysis to prioritize and design products</li>
<li>
Participate, drive, and deliver against the software development lifecycle (SDLC)</li>
<li>
Provide input and guidance for UX/UI design and prototyping</li>
<li>
Research/resolve issues and questions quickly and accurately as a subject matter expert</li>
</ul>
