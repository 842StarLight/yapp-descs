<h2>Account Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>APPLY TO BECOME A NAME BEHIND THE FLAME!</b></p>
<p>
Ferrellgas is seeking an Account Manager to drive new profitable business, create business relationships that build customer loyalty and maintain customer base of the Service Center.
</p>
<p><b>Benefits:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Medical, dental, and vision plans</li>
<li>401(k) retirement plan with company match</li>
<li>Employee Stock Ownership Plan (ESOP)</li>
<li>Paid holidays, sick leave, and vacation</li>
<li>Wellness program</li>
<li>Tuition Reimbursement Program</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Essential Functions:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Identifies sales leads and qualifies prospects.</li>
<li>Makes value based sales presentation to prospective customers.</li>
<li>Maintains key customer relationships.</li>
<li>Builds and maintains customer files; power point presentations and follow up letters.</li>
<li>Monitors the new customer installation process.</li>
<li>Ensures customer satisfaction and safety.</li>
<li>Works with service center to drive growth in all business segments including retail, Autogas, and Blue Rhino.</li>
<li>Builds and maintains a pipeline of new prospects, closed business and installed business to meet objectives.</li>
<li>Works with the DO to develop marketing plans and strategies for growth.</li>
<li>Communicates new customer expectations and follow through on commitments.</li>
<li>Responsible for meeting sales GMD annual goal.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Experience:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor's degree in related field.</li>
<li>1-2 years' sales experience.</li>
<li>Good customer service skills.</li>
<li>Excellent interpersonal, communication, and organizational skills.</li>
<li>Ability to pass background check and have clean driving record.</li>
<li>Proficient Microsoft Office skills including Excel, Word, and PowerPoint.</li>
<li>SFA - Sales Force Automation Software experience preferred.</li>
<li>Proven interpersonal, communication and organization skills.</li>
<li>Self-starter and team focused.</li>
<li>PeopleSoft experience preferred.</li>
<li>Proven sales background of achieving sales goals preferred.</li>
</ul>
