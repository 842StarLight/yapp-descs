THE PASSION:</b></p>

We purchase unique and seasonal products that support local farms and growers. But we’re much more than just produce. As a Sysco owned company, we provide the benefits and resources of a fortune 100 enterprise with a real family feel.</p>

<br>
<p>
Opportunity is knocking! At FreshPoint we understand that building a great company is just like creating a great meal - you must have all the right ingredients. For us, those ingredients are the cultures, backgrounds, ideas, and experiences behind our diverse workforce that embodies FreshPoint. FreshPoint is a leader in selling, marketing and distributing produce, dairy and other food products to restaurants, healthcare and educational facilities, lodging establishments and other customers who prepare meals away from the home.

<p><b>POSITION SUMMARY:</b></p>
<p>
This is an outside sales position responsible for promoting the company's products and services and for building relationships with new and existing accounts. The main focus is to help FreshPoint customers succeed while achieving sales and profit goals established by the company.</p>
<ul>
<li><p>
Pay Type: Salaried to Commission</p></li>
<li><p>
Employment Type: Full Time</p></li>
<li><p>
Travel Percentage: 50%</p></li>
<li><p>
Work Location: <b>Connecticut</b></p></li>
<li><p>
The Schedule: May often require working non-traditional hours (evening, weekends and holidays) in order to successfully meet customers’ needs</p></li>
</ul>
<p><br>
An individual must be able to satisfactorily perform each essential duty listed below. The requirements listed are representative of the knowledge, skill, and/or ability required. Reasonable accommodations may be made to enable individuals with disabilities to perform the essential functions.<br>
<br>
Essential Duties:</p>
<ul>
<li><p>
Develop new business, penetrate existing accounts, and minimize lost business to achieve profitable sales growth and special objectives within assigned territory.</p></li>
<li><p>
Seek and qualify prospects in accordance with Company account stratification goals.</p></li>
<li><p>
Research customer business needs and develop a mix of products and service to meet needs.</p></li>
<li><p>
Evaluate market trends and recommend products to customers, based on business needs and goals.</p></li>
<li><p>
Be informed of market conditions, product innovations, and competitors' products, prices, and sales; share information with customers as part of value-added services provided.</p></li>
<li><p>
Answer customers' questions about products, prices, availability, and product uses.</p></li>
<li><p>
Provide product information and practical training to customer personnel.</p></li>
<li><p>
Drive personal vehicle to customer accounts, conventions, company meetings, etc.</p></li>
<li><p>
Communicate and collect accounts receivable as necessary, working with the credit department and client; collect all balances due based on approved credit terms.</p></li>
<li><p>
Participate in company functions, promotions, customer visits, and customer events.</p></li>
<li><p>
Attend and participate in sales meetings.</p></li>
<li><p>
Review and analyze daily and weekly sales reports for sales penetration opportunities.</p></li>
<li><p>
Other duties may be assigned.</p></li>
<li><p>
Participate in ongoing training sessions.</p></li>
<li><p>
Assist with the training of new employees as requested.</p></li>
</ul>
