<h2>Sr. Marketing Associate</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>Highlights is seeking a driven, analytical, digital-centric and team-oriented self-starter to join our growing Amazon marketing team. This person will drive promotional activities for Amazon and planning to coordinate the way Highlights for Children is represented and merchandised on Amazon. The ideal candidate is an entrepreneurial-minded individual with the ability to think outside of the box and is comfortable with an ever-changing marketing landscape. This person is hungry to learn and understand best practices of driving marketing activities on Amazon, is detail oriented, is strategically minded with the ability to understand the strengths and limitations of running promotions on Amazon and how those strategies will impact the channel as a whole.</p>
<p></p>
<p>The ideal candidate will bring experience working with cross-functional business partners to foster alignment, champion change and innovation, and develop marketing plans that generate results. This role will understand the consumer and retailer needs and opportunities specific to Amazon, and then execute and track success of marketing and promotional programs. This person should have a strong marketing background and experience with the launch and promotion of products. The ideal candidate has excellent communication skills and is comfortable in pushing to get things done. They are data driven, and proficient in excel. They need to be analytical and have the ability to tie marketing activities to an ROI in both revenue and impressions. Experience with Amazon is a big plus.</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Partner with advertising agency in developing the strategy for Amazon sponsored brand, sponsored product and DSP campaigns that promote our product and subscription businesses.</li>
<ul>
<li>Interface with advertising agency to plan, facilitate and review Amazon channel initiatives and promotional planning</li>
<li>Help put in place KPIs by product line to measure marketing activities effectively</li>
<li>Monitor and report on all Amazon advertising campaigns
</li>
<li>Compiling and analyzing reports for different advertising strategies, ensuring that spend and performance are meeting expectations</li>
<li>Communicate key advertising metrics including conversion, spend, ACoS, ROI, etc. to product teams</li>
</ul>
<li>Implement merchandising strategies to achieve sell-through growth and revenue goals</li>
<ul>
<li>Develop and update product listings to optimize for the Amazon marketplace</li>
<li>Partner with editorial and creative teams to develop enhanced brand content for product pages</li>
<li>Execute Brand Store best practices to improve customer experience and improve advertising conversion</li>
</ul>
<li>Create strategies that drive awareness and sales to our Amazon listings
</li>
<ul>
<li>Coordinate special promotions such as coupons, Lightening Deals, etc.</li>
<li>Recommend offers for seasonal promotions such as Black Friday, Prime Day, etc.</li>
<li>Work with product leaders on pricing strategies</li>
</ul>
<li>Manage the portfolio of products assigned to ensure optimal growth and contribution.</li>
<ul>
<li>Develop models for inventory forecasting</li>
<li>Coordinate a comprehensive strategy to solicit customer reviews</li>
<li>Oversee the day-to-day operations of Highlights’ Seller Central account, which includes inventory planning and scheduling, customer messages, product listings, pricing, daily orders, reporting and overall account health</li>
 </ul>
</ul>
<p><b>Qualifications:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree in business, marketing, communications</li>
<li>2+ years of marketing experience in direct marketing; e-commerce and/or paid search</li>
<li>Ability to effectively and efficiently manage multiple, concurrent projects, collaborating with cross-functional teams while keeping projects moving and on track to meet deadlines</li>
<li>Proficient with web analytics and able to conduct ad hoc ROI analysis</li>
<li>Ability to translate trends into brand focused business opportunities that drive to sales and profit goals</li>
<li>Strong verbal and written communication skills with the ability to communicate effectively by adapting approach, language and style to a wide variety of audiences and stakeholders; ability to grasp and communicate the big picture while remaining conversant with details</li>
<li>Strong relationship builder both within the organization and with external partners</li>
</ul>
</div>
