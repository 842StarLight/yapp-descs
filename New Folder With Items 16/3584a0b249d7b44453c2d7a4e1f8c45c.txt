<h1 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Solution Developer – Salesforce</b></h1>
<div></div>
<br>
<p><b>
Job Summary:</b></p>
<p>
The Salesforce Developer position will be responsible for the overall design, implementation and support of solutions in the Salesforce.com platform. This requires the ability to develop and support applications using Sales Cloud, Service Cloud, Community Cloud, Pardot, and the Force.com platform using Apex, SOSL, SOQL, SOAP and other programming technologies. The Salesforce Developer develops, configures, codes, tests, and debugs complex software, enhancements to existing proprietary software or purchased software packages. Developer may also be tasked with administrative duties such as configuration of Visual Flows, Process Builders, modifying Workflow rules, Page Layouts, and Instance Management.</p>

<p><b>
Skills and Certifications:</b></p>
<ul>
