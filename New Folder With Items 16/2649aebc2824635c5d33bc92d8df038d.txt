<h2>Machine Shop Engineering Estimator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Peridot Corporation is seeking a highly motivated and skilled Engineering Estimator for our rapidly growing business. Join a fast paced team that works on the most advanced minimally invasive surgical tools using modern high end CNC machines. Clean, well lit, air conditioned facility in Pleasanton CA.</p>
<p><b>ESSENTIAL DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Understands the customer requirements, design intent and timelines.</li>
<li>Interact with sales, engineering, purchasing, production and customer service to obtain and provide information needed to resolve issues and establish an accurate cost estimate for product.</li>
<li>Identify processes needed to complete product and determine compatibility to our capabilities.</li>
<li>Determines the correct material, machines, labor and outside processing required.</li>
<li>Retrieves historical data and analyzes data for improved efficiencies.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>QUALIFICATIONS</b></p>
<ul><li>Must have a strong background in metal fabrication and machining processes (Stamping, Forming, Welding, Drilling, Tapping and CNC Milling/Turning.</li></ul>
<ul>
<li>Ability to read and understand GDT.</li>
<li>Must be highly proficient with computers, including CAD and ERP software.</li>
<li>Knowledgeable of methods/time studies and ability to calculate run rates.</li>
<li>Knowledgeable of finishing processes (grinding, plating, heat treating, etc.).</li>
<li>Excellent verbal and written communication skills.</li>
</ul>
