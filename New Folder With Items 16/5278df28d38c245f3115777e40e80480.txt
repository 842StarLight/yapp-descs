<h2>Manager, Sales & Service Training</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>PURPOSE</b><br>
<br>
The primary purpose of this role is to understand the enterprise learning strategy and lead, develop, and maintain a global selling &amp; Service model and associated learning content and programs at Lowe's. This role manages the development and integration of human resource learning programs for associates to achieve strategic business goals and operational objectives. Assures operations and deliverables meet or exceed organizational objectives and integrates as appropriate with other HR functions. The role also manages the learning and development demand planning cycle for the appropriate associates in conjunction with business requirements. This role ensures solutions meet or exceed individual objectives, adhere to HR and performance goals, and align with Lowe's business strategies. To accomplish this, the individual must have knowledge of Lowe's leadership principles/core behaviors, business policies, strategies and operational environment. This role is critical to Lowe's because it ensures Lowe's associates are equipped with the confidence and competence to serve Lowe's customers.<br>
<br>
<b>RESPONSIBILITIES</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Supports the Director of Field Associate Readiness to coordinate service delivery strategies and processes across the organization. Support cross-functional projects and ensure timely completion of assigned responsibilities within established constraints.</li>
<li>
Manages the analysis, design, development, and implementation of learning solutions to address sales and service initiatives.</li>
<li>
Ensures design solutions are adapted to both the culture of the organization and the diversity of the workforce.</li>
<li>
Is a trusted advisor who proactively engages leaders, embraces ‘leader as teachers’ and builds strong, collaborative working relationships with HR Business Partners (HRBPs), business leaders, team members, vendors and other key stakeholders.</li>
<li>
Drives the development and maintenance of a comprehensive suite of solutions to address the performance gaps in a business, an audience, a function, or an initiative.</li>
<li>
Manages programs/projects to ensure the overall portfolio for team is effectively executed.</li>
<li>
Manages risks and determines mitigation strategies to meet timelines.</li>
<li>
Consults and advises with sponsors, HRBPs, and work owners at the functional and/or business unit on the best way to develop the workforce.</li>
<li>
Oversees multiple product and vendor evaluation and selection initiatives, including advising the team on how to maintain good professional relationships with all vendors.</li>
<li>
Designs and delivers requests for proposal; suggests alternative licensing arrangements through interpreting benefits and drawbacks.</li>
<li>
As required may serve as a facilitator using approved facilitator resources.</li>
<li>
Stays apprised of best in class solutions to create engaging programs for multiple workforce skill sets and generational needs.</li>
<li>
Establish measurement strategy to ensure learning solutions directly support and can drive positive impacts on business metrics.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
REQUIRED EDUCATION / EXPERIENCE</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Bachelor’s Degree in Learning &amp; Development, Instructional Design, Business, Organizational Development, HR, or related field</li>
<li>
7+ years experience in training delivery and/or instructional design experience required; preferably for a large multi-state and/or global company as a manager.</li>
<li>
7+ years progressive experience one or more specialty functions of Human Resources, with a significant portion focused on talent management (e.g., employee and/or leadership development, instructional design, training, performance management) and a mix of operational (stores, supply chain and contact center), strategic, and systems thinking experience</li>
<li>
7 years experience successfully managing multiple priorities &amp; projects through successful delivery &amp; implementation under time, budget, and political pressure</li>
<li>
5 years retail experience</li>
</ul>
<p><br>
<b>
PREFERRED EDUCATION / EXPERIENCE</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Master’s Degree in Education; Instructional Design; Organizational Development; Human Resources; Industrial-Organization Psychology; MBA or related field</li>
<li>
10 years experience in training delivery and/or instructional design experience required; preferably for a large multi-state and/or global company as a manager.</li>
<li>
5 years experience leading and mentoring a team and helping to foster/create a “great place to work.”</li>
</ul>
