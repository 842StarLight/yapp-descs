<h2>Product Marketing Manager - Sales and Partner Enablement</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>The Product Marketing Manager is responsible for the execution of go-to-market programs for SheerID. In this highly visible role, you will develop programs to support our direct and indirect sales and partner channels. You will develop selling materials for vertical markets, evangelize the SheerID solution, and serve as a product expert in a fast-growing, competitive market. Experience working with or as part of a sales and marketing team is critical.</p>
<p>Candidates will have a passion for cross-functional collaboration, simplifying the complex, and storytelling.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>Role Specific Job Duties</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Sales and Partner Enablement: complement traditional product marketing enablement with solution-specific content that simplify product features into tangible solution benefits.</li>
<li>
Develop enablement assets and training materials including sales playbooks, competitive battlecards, demo training videos, and more.</li>
<li>
Create and maintain propositions for target markets, industries, and our products, solutions, and services based on target customers and buyer personas.</li>
<li>
Provides strategic and tactical support for marketing, sales, and services including participation in major account sales calls, and product requirement workshops.</li>
<li>
Ensures stakeholders are informed on competitive offerings – help gather and disseminate competitive information and positions across the organization.</li>
<li>
Manage messaging and content development for product materials, webcasts, speaking events, and other internal and external communications.</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Collaborate: develop relationships with customers and technology partners to determine key use cases and document customer success stories.</li>
<li>
Support new product launches to drive positioning, messaging, pricing and adoption.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>Required Skills/Experience</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor's degree an/or equivalent years of experience required.</li>
<li>
3-5 years' marketing or sales experience in an enterprise SaaS company with a focus in digital marketing.</li>
<li>
Experience creating, planning and executing persuasive sales materials.</li>
<li>
Demonstrated ability to build and communicate a compelling value proposition.</li>
<li>
Exceptional written, presentation and verbal communication skills with the ability to communicate at various levels within and outside of the organization.</li>
<li>
Ability to influence cross-functional teams without formal authority.</li>
<li>
Has an engaging attitude – personable, approachable, collaborative and eager to help</li>
<li>
Is proactive, self-motivated and takes ownership for problem resolution</li>
<li>
Is self-aware, values feedback and personal development</li>
<li>
Highly organized with a keen sense of attention to detail.</li>
<li>
A proven track record of gaining trust and respect by consistently demonstrating sound critical-thinking skills and maintaining composure in all situations.</li>
</ul>
