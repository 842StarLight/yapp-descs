<h2>Sales Representative</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p><b>Do you have experience selling EHRs to Critical Access Hospitals and ambulatory clinics in the rural marketplace? If this sounds like your expertise and you're looking to join a rapidly growing Healthcare IT company, Azalea Health has an exciting opportunity for an aggressive salesperson.</b></p>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Azalea Health is a Health IT company providing web-based Electronic Health Records (EHR) and Revenue Cycle Management (RCM) services to clients nationwide. We are an entrepreneurial organization previously recognized as the one of the 5000 Fastest Growing Private Companies in America by Inc. Magazine and as the Emerging Health IT Company of the Year by the Metro Atlanta Chamber.</b></p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Duties &amp; Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li><b>
Sell Azalea products and services to a targeted regional territory and achieve sales quota</b></li>
<li>
<b>
Prospect via cold call, email, social media, and networking against an assigned territory with the objective of qualifying the prospect with an opportunity</b><ul><li><b>
Prospecting may be for your sale or for hand off to the Regional Director</b></li></ul>
</li>
<li><b>
Work in partnership with the Regional Team to pursue and achieve goal</b></li>
<li><b>
Coordinate and provide support to Regional Director on Large Accounts</b></li>
<li><b>
Leverage Sales Engineering to create unique Azalea Solutions to win business</b></li>
<li><b>
Identify business needs and present appropriate solutions to multiple decision makers and influencers</b></li>
<li><b>
Execute sales process and negotiations to lead accounts to close</b></li>
<li><b>
Generate, promote and maintain a positive presence for Azalea within assigned territory</b></li>
<li><b>
Expand Azalea presence within assigned territory by attending trade shows and conferences.</b></li>
<li><b>
Generate and/or tailor quotes and proposals</b></li>
<li><b>
Fully understand Azalea products and services at a level to successfully sell them</b></li>
<li><b>
Fully understand Azalea contracts to effectively negotiate with customers</b></li>
<li><b>
Fully understand the Azalea price list and pricing strategy and process to generate quotes to effectively create proposals that win in the market</b></li>
<li><b>
Assist Marketing, Sales Engineering and Enablement in developing effective sales tools, process and materials for your region</b></li>
<li><b>
Develop presentations and proposals that differentiate the value proposition including responses to RFPs</b></li>
<li><b>
Maintain accurate records in salesforce.com or other CRM</b></li>
<li><b>
Represent market needs to the Company and influence feature function road map</b></li>
<li><b>
Drive creative solutions to meet quota</b></li>
<li><b>
Will travel 25%</b></li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Qualifications</b></p>
<ul>
<li><b>
Bachelor's degree in Business, Marketing or a related field, required</b></li>
<li><b>
Healthcare SaaS sales experience highly preferred</b></li>
<li><b>
5(+) years of recent experience in a similar role</b></li>
<li><b>
Experience documenting sales activities and forecast in Salesforce or similar CRM</b></li>
<li><b>
Strong communication skills with the ability to present the products and services Azalea provides in person or over web conference</b></li>
<li><b>
Solution sales training and knowledge of turning business problems into a measurable ROI</b></li>
<li><b>
Must be decisive, conscientious, and interact well in a team environment</b></li>
<li><b>
Additionally, the role requires an accelerated ramp up of essential product and industry knowledge, including but not limited to practice management, clinical workflow, revenue cycle management and the current medical regulatory environment</b></li>
</ul>

</ul>
