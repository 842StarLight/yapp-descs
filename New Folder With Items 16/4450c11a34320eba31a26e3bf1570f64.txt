<p>
We're looking for a <b>Payroll and AP Specialist</b> who will work out of our headquarters in Sacramento, California. The Payroll and Accounts Payable (AP) Specialist is responsible for full cycle payroll processing and associated duties as well as assisting in all AP matters. This includes day-to-day AP activities, overseeing fixed and pre-paid assets, and monthly GL account reconciliation.</p>
<p><b>
Responsibilities</b><b>:</b></p>
<p>
Payroll Responsibilities:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Process multi-state bi-weekly payroll for 200 employees, half of which are non-exempt</li>
<li>
Audits timecards and follow up with employees and/or managers on discrepancies</li>
<li>
Processes sales commissions and performance bonuses</li>
<li>
Answers employee and management payroll inquiries in a timely and professional manner</li>
<li>
Maintain HRIS/Payroll/Timekeeping system in UltiPro</li>
<li>
Provides information and reconciliations to internal and external auditors</li>
</ul>
<p>
Accounts Payable Responsibilities:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Assist in full-cycle AP activities, from matching invoices and obtaining approvals to making vendor payments</li>
<li>
Matches invoices to checks, obtain all signatures for checks and distribute checks accordingly</li>
<li>
Reconciles vendor statements, researches, and corrects discrepancies</li>
<li>
Maintains files and documentation thoroughly and accurately, in accordance with company policy and accepted accounting practices</li>
</ul>

</li>

<i>
