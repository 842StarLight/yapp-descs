<h2>Technical Writer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>We have an immediate opening for a Technical Writer in our Bellingham, WA facility.</p>
<p>The ideal candidate in this position will create user manuals, data sheets, instruction sheets, warning labels, and press releases for a variety of machines and products. To ensure accurate documentation, this position regularly works with various departments in the company including Engineering, Quality Control, Technical Support, Service, Graphics, and Parts.</p>
<p>A typical work day can be quite varied, including writing and page layout work at the computer; removing a new machine from its box, assembling it, setting it up, and adjusting it; working closely with our in-house photographers and editing photos; drawing simple illustrations to enhance the usability of written instructions; or thoroughly reviewing exploded-view diagrams and parts lists to ensure that they are accurate. This position requires a very high degree of attention-to-detail. The position involves an extensive proofing process, so the candidate must be comfortable with giving and getting regular constructive criticism of their work.</p>
<p><b>Job Responsibilities: </b></p>
<p>Creation/editing of:</p>
<ul>
<li>Owner’s Manuals</li>
<li>Instruction Sheets</li>
<li>Part Breakdowns</li>
<li>Wiring Diagrams</li>
<li>Specification Sheets</li>
<li>Safety Labeling &amp; Product Warnings</li>
<li>Press Releases</li>
<li>Engineering/Tech Support Bulletins</li>
<li>Catalog &amp; Web Sales Copy</li>
<li>Training Modules &amp; Style Guides</li>
<li>Company SOPs (Standard Operating Procedures)</li>
<li>Video Scripts</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Approximate time breakdown for position: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>40% Technical Writing, Desktop Publishing, Photo Editing, Illustrations</li>
<li>25% Part Breakdowns, Wiring Diagrams</li>
<li>15% Reading, Proofing, Editing</li>
<li>10% Hands-on Work with Machinery and Tools</li>
<li>10% Meetings, Research, Other</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Job Requirements: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>High degree of mechanical aptitude</li>
<li>Very high attention to detail</li>
<li>Strong experience with machinery, mechanics, or power tools</li>
<li>A college degree (or related work experience) in technical documentation, writing, or communications</li>
<li>Knowledge of the Adobe Creative Suite or similar desktop-publishing software is preferred, but not required</li>
<li>A strong work ethic</li>
<li>High degree of ingenuity, confidence, and resilience</li>
<li>Strong organization/project management skills</li>
<li>Good time management skills</li>
</ul>
<p>Our Tech Docs team operates under regular deadlines in a production-oriented atmosphere. Our Tech Writers regularly coordinate with inspectors and engineers around the globe, as well as a variety of internal Company staff in multiple departments. All work produced is subjected to multiple rounds of extensive proofing, so the right candidate must be comfortable with constructive criticism and a deeply involved editorial process.</p>
