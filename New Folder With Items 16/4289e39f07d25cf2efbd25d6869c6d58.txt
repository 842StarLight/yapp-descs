<h2>Product Management Leader- Manual Verification</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Market the organization´s products and/or services including market research, direct marketing, product management, market development, communications and pricing. Creative discovery and development process for new product ideas or strategic product extensions. Translating research discoveries into usable and marketable products. Researching, developing, planning, designing, maintaining and implementing policies and programs that enhance the organization´s relations with the community, the public, governmental and regulatory authorities, shareholders and/or employees.</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Who is Equifax?
</b></p>
<p>Equifax is a global information solutions company that uses trusted unique data, creative analytics, technology and industry expertise to power organizations and individuals around the world by transforming knowledge into insights that help make more informed business and personal decisions.</p>
<p>
Regardless of location or role, the individual and collective work of our people makes a difference in our business. We are looking for individuals who can help us disrupt the marketplace. You will do this by delivering leading-edge technology to build and deliver unparalleled customized insights that enrich both the performance of businesses and the lives of consumers. We will give you the opportunity to drive innovation and automation across the enterprise. This will include tool and process integrations across all business units within Equifax globally.</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Key Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Maximizes growth opportunities via a strong and comprehensive product portfolio strategy and marketing plan based on market and segment needs</li>
<li>
Understand multi-level customer needs and validate solutions that support strategic portfolio of products/platforms</li>
<li>
Develop and communicate the product vision and road map, enforcing internal processes to prioritize the road map</li>
<li>
Provide sales and operational support and enablement, by serving as a “product expert” and responding to product and technical questions, including participation in select sales and customer calls</li>
<li>
Define key measurements of product line performance and success criteria. Supports tracking efforts and provides market data for the analysis of each segment of the product line.</li>
<li>
Product release planning: creating release plan, working with other groups to drive the release (Marketing, Legal, Operations, etc.) and ensuring that all aspects of the release planning process are complete upon product availability.</li>
<li>
Lead efforts in developing product and product line strategies, directions and recommendations as input to overall company planning process</li>
<li>
Support internal operational leaders by providing current information on product developments and competitive market positioning</li>
<li>
Evangelize the product capabilities internally and to the external partners</li>
<li>
Stay current with industry and market trends; Attends user group meetings, trade shows, and seminars as required to support product management and marketing programs.</li>
<li>
Stay current on regulatory changes that impact our products/platforms and determine our go forward strategies to address these changes.</li>
</ul>
