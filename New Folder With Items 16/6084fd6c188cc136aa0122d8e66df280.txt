<p><b>Essential Duties and Responsibilities: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Subject Matter Expertise; PdM Technologies including Vibration, IR Thermography, Oil Analysis, Ultrasonic, and Motor Circuit Analysis. Specific knowledge of the CSI 2130 Machinery Analyzer, CSI 6500 Online System, and AMS Suite: Machinery Health Management software and applications.</li>
<li>In-depth knowledge on all of the CSI Software, Products &amp; Services and the ability to communicate &amp; apply that knowledge to reliability service contracts and projects, and support the Novaspect sales force through Solutions Selling methodology.</li>
<li>Have diverse experience in organizational management, maintenance, and reliability, as well as practical experience in plant reliability and/or maintenance management.</li>
<li>Provide analysis and concise reports on recommendations for repairs and urgency for problems identified.</li>
<li>Ability to plan, schedule and manage travel, appointments, and activities, willing to work off-hours as required by customers.</li>
<li>Working knowledge of routine and advanced data acquisition techniques, knowledge in spectral, time waveform, and Peakvue analysis.</li>
<li>Ability to set up vibration routes for routine monitoring on diverse plant equipment, and provide database maintenance on a regular and consistent basis.</li>
<li>Knowledge and experience in balancing rotating equipment and laser alignment on complex machine trains.</li>
<li>Project Support - Actively supports project pursuit opportunities in collaboration with the Novaspect Sales force.</li>
<li>Support and/or Lead execution of reliability projects as required</li>
<li>Technical Seminars &amp; Presentations * Develop the ability to conduct live demonstrations &amp; formal presentations that clearly communicate the capabilities and value of the AMS Suite portfolio</li>
<li>Services/Solution Growth Initiatives * Develop and execute a comprehensive services growth strategy and plan for assigned accounts.</li>
</ul>
