<h2>EXECUTIVE DIRECTOR</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>Capital Analytics’ <b>Executive Director</b> is an outside sales position responsible for putting together our annual economic reports and large-scale conferences through sponsorship and advertising sales. For each annual project, our Executive Directors meet with over 200 top CEOs, thought leaders and decision makers to increase revenue, secure annual advertising contracts and expand activity across all our Invest: platforms.</p>
<p><b>
Key Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Drive revenue and grow the base of advertising and sponsorship accounts.</li>
<li>Manage a list of 70 or more core clients, with regular contact to communicate the company’s message and value and to become a trusted consultant.</li>
<li>Build and maintain good relationships with local CEOs, decision-makers and government officials, obtaining 15 meetings a week</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Is this position for me?</b></p>
<p>
This position is perfect for you if you enjoy or excel at…</p>
<ul>
<li>Meeting new people and building relationships</li>
<li>Operating autonomously and independently</li>
<li>Running the show!</li>
<li>Meeting with C-level executives</li>
<li>Communication – especially interpersonal</li>
<li>Connecting…people, ideas, more</li>
<li>Networking, attending events and conferences</li>
<li>Building a strong strategy to exceed sales targets</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Perks of the position</b></p>
<p>
This position is unique and not just your typical sales position! Some perks include:</p>
<ul>
<li>Operating like your own CEO – you will oversee the project on the ground</li>
<li>No cap on commissions!</li>
<li>Access to key decision makers and industry leaders in your home city and building a portfolio like none other</li>
<li>Ability to be your city’s cheerleader – our mission is to promote and grow the economies of the markets we cover</li>
<li>Extensive training both at our HQ in Miami as well as on the ground</li>
<li>A valid driver’s license issued in one of the 50 United States is required (all meetings are conducted outside the office)</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Compensation</b></p>
<p>
Current team members earn over $75,000 per annum</p>
<p>
Salary range is $37,000-$50,000 based on experience</p>
<p>
Commissions are uncapped and targets will get employees to six figures</p>
