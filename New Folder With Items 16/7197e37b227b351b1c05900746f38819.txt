<h2>Fall 2019 Faherty Internship Program</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">About the Program
<br>The Faherty Summer Internship Program is designed for undergraduate students who are interested in learning more about the apparel industry and want to gain hands on experience at a rapidly growing business that has a vibrant culture.
<br><br>
Outside of the experience you’ll gain from exposure to the business, you will engage in high-impact programming while building meaningful relationships with other interns and team Faherty through a variety of summer activities. This includes 1:1 mentorship, custom learning and development programming, and opportunities to impact business decisions.
<br><br>
<b>Timeline:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Open Application: Friday, July 26th – Friday, August 23rd, 2019 at 11:59pm EST</li>
<li>Interview Timelines: Continuous throughout the application process</li>
<li>Length of Internship: 12 weeks - Monday, September 16th through Friday, December 6th</li>
</ul>
<b>Minimum Qualifications:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Currently enrolled in an undergraduate or graduate program</li>
<li>Must receive school credit for internship</li>
<li>Must be able to commit to the entire 11-week program</li>
<li>Must be authorized to work in the United States</li>
<li>Strong interpersonal and organizational skills, with the ability to navigate a fast paced environment</li>
<li>Have an understanding that a position as an intern will be unpaid</li>
</ul>
<b>How to Apply:</b><br>
Once you apply, we will provide regular status updates to all applicants on a rolling basis. Based on your background, experience and interests, you will be invited to interview if matched with one of the departments who will host an intern. You will meet with a member of our People Team and then with the Department Intern Lead. You can view a list of our teams who will be hosting interns this fall. The team at Faherty Brand doesn’t like to say good-bye to talented and high performing individuals. Therefore, should a full-time opportunity become available in the future, we encourage high performing interns to throw their hat in the ring for consideration.

<b>Customer Experience:</b> YOU ARE HERE TO MAKE A DIFFERENCE. When a customer inquiries on our website or phones in, we are the very first person with whom he/she will come in contact with. Whether it is a customer’s first time shopping with Faherty, or they are a loyal shopper, our roles in CX is critical to the success of delivering exceptional guest experiences and ensuring clients leave with results that are nothing less than amazing.
<br><br>
This an incredible opportunity to impact the experience by supporting and leading alongside the CX management the implementation of our new CRM platform and Customer Loyalty project. Both critical projects, you will be part of a team responsible for opening up new lines of communication with our customers, setting our tone of voice and delighting our customers daily whilst ensuring their custom is repeated, recognized and rewarded. Having an intimate knowledge of our Faherty products and of product locations will make you successful in your role alongside an awareness of omni-channel shopper experiences.
<br><br>
You will be responsible for staying consistent with the Faherty message, communicating in a positive manner with the customer and the wider Faherty team, and educating our CX team on the value of our new CRM software. You will also be responsible for dealing with any and all customer questions about the products and services that we offer. You are expected to continuously update your knowledge of our products, services, and policies.
<br><br>
Your primary job duty includes maintaining a positive attitude and focusing on customer satisfaction. You will work closely with the CX team to understand the complex needs when creating an omni-channel CX department. Encompassing the following:
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Be proactive in answering some 200+ customer inquiries through our multiple channels including: phone calls, emails, and live-chats.</li>
<li>Participate in product knowledge sessions and weekly touch-bases with your manager to learn customer service and sales techniques on closing sales, up-selling to achieve specific sales goals as assigned by Faherty management.</li>
<li>Master our internal point of sale systems in order to efficiently and effectively process customers orders. You will learn to communicate directly with our store teams to manage the shipment of merchandise to customers.</li>
<li>Partner with the CX Management and Senior Associates to implement our new CRM software and communications channels (Chat/Email/Phone/Social Media). Project lead, train and create tools to help with a smooth transition across the entire Faherty estate, inclusive of Customer Tone of Voice.</li>
<li>Partner with finance, e-commerce, marketing and other areas of the business to create a loyalty program that transcends retail and e-commerce resulting in repeat custom, increased customer lifetime value and satisfaction.</li>
<li>Collect and act upon how customers view Faherty products, support, and company. Help improve our customer service, design, product, marketing, and technology by providing detailed notes about all customer interactions.</li>
</ul>
<b>Must have qualifications include:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Currently pursuing a B.A. in either Marketing, Business Administration, Hospitality, Communications, or a related major</li>
<li>Works well both independently and in a team environment</li>
<li>1 year experience with POS and/or CRM systems</li>
<li>Familiarity with and knowledgable on e-commerce strategies</li>
<li>Ability to navigate between multiple e-commerce platforms</li>
<li>Has a strong interest in consumer behavior and loyalty program strategies</li>

<b>Merchandising &amp; Planning:</b> Fashion is fun by definition! The Merchandising team will provide the intern with hands on experience that they would receive as an Assistant Merchandiser. These skills include style creation, sample management, and trend projects. The intern will support both the men's and women's merchandising teams to get an in-depth look into the many aspects of merchandising. Integration into the Faherty’s Production and Design team will also be warranted. A focus on the fundamentals, including retail math, how to build a line, and cross-functional relationships will provide the intern with ample experience to continue along their career path. The intern’s engagement in daily work allow for the comprehension of and extension into larger projects including quarterly hindsights and market preparatory meetings with the Faherty Executive Team. When the intern has completed his or her time at Faherty Brand, he or she will have aided in the preparation for the Fall 20 Market, SKU and product analysis, competitive benchmarking, and white space opportunity presentation.
<br><br>
<b>Must have qualifications include:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Currently pursuing a B.A. in Fashion Merchandising, Fashion Design, Fashion Business Management or related fields</li>
<li>Proficient to strong retail math experience</li>
<li>Eager to immerse themselves in style, trends and inventory forecasting</li>
<li>Ability and confidence to work independently</li>
<li>Maintains a strong understanding of Faherty Brand identity and company consumer base</li>
</ul>
