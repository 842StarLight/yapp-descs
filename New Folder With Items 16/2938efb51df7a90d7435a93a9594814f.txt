<h2>Accounting Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Our Accounting team at Workiva offers a dynamic and collaborative working environment, where you will continuously learn and be supported. As an Accounting Specialist, you will focus on a range of general staff accounting tasks as well as reading of contracts to invoice. You will perform tasks directly related to your area of expertise as well as assisting with assigned special projects.</p>
<p><b>
What You’ll Do</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
You will enter customer invoice entries.</li>
<li>
You will communicate with Workiva’s sales team on all new contracts and customers.</li>
<li>
You will submit invoices through an automated system.</li>
<li>
You will invoice customer contracts.</li>
<li>
You will be researching and responding to questions; escalating more complex questions as needed.</li>
<li>
You will assist with collections.</li>
<li>
You will interpret contracts with the available knowledge.</li>
<li>
You will implement credit memos within scope of authority.</li>
<li>
You will complete assigned month end reports for the Accounting Manager.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
What You’ll Need</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Associates degree in Accounting preferred or 3+ years of progressive accounting experience
</li>
<li>Experience in collections and/or Accounts Receivable background preferred</li>
<li>
Analytical ability to interpret contracts and policies</li>
<li>
Proficient accounting software knowledge, along with generic computer skills required</li>
<li>
Spreadsheet skills preferred</li>
<li>
Strong communication/collaborative skills to work with many personalities inside and outside of the company</li>
</ul>
