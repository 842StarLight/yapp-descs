<h2 class="jobSectionHeader">
<b>Job Description</b><b>
</b>
</h2>
<div><div><div>
<div>We are seeking a Marketing Coordinator to join our team in Sacramento, CA. This individual will be responsible for leading the development and coordination of proposals, presentations, and marketing collateral. The ideal candidate will think strategically, maintain flexibility, take initiative, and has the ability to shift priorities frequently while maintaining attention to detail and achieving high quality standards.<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<div><b>Essential Duties &amp; Responsibilities</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
Prepare responses to Request for Proposals (RFP), Request for Qualifications (RFQ) and similar procurements in the requested formats (e.g. SF 330). Coordinate with in-house staff, proposed team members, subconsultants, and posting entities as appropriate to ensure complete and compliant responses that are delivered on-time.</li>
<li>
Identify and reinforce winning messages and strategies in all marketing deliverables.</li>
<li>
Lead, coordinate, and manage production of proposals, presentations, and marketing collateral.</li>
<li>
Proofread and edit technical and non-technical pursuit documents for accuracy and consistency.</li>
<li>
Develop interview materials such as PowerPoint presentations, boards, and leave-behinds.</li>
<li>
Create project descriptions and resumes by interviewing project personnel and reviewing existing documentation; perform other writing and editing assignments as needed.</li>
<li>
Assist fellow Marketing Coordinators by performing quality control checks, providing style and grammar edits, and verifying data provided in proposals.</li>
<li>
Provide updates and corrections to the marketing database (Cosential) for information used in proposals and other marketing materials.</li>
<li>
Assist with competitor analysis, selection panel profiling, and conducting web research.</li>
<li>
Maintain the Vanir brand throughout all deliverables.</li>
</ul>
