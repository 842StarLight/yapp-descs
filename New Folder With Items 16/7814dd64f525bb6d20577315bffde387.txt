<h2>Account Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Overview</b></p>
<p>The Account Manager is expected to support an existing team to retain and grow eLogic’s existing client base. He or she builds relationships with clients and internal teams to ensure contract renewal with Absorb, product utilization, and resolve client issues. This role helps manage the selling/buying process for new business in existing accounts via upsell and cross sell, including lead generation, qualification, needs discovery, solution development/presentation, value articulation, negotiations and close.</p>
<p><b>The Company: </b></p>
<p>eLogic is now a part of Absorb Software.</p>
<p>eLogic Learning, founded in 2001, was built on the principle and belief that the most valuable asset of top performing companies is the people behind and in front of the brand. Utilizing our proprietary web-based learning management technology, content capabilities, professional experience and a partnership attitude, we provide companies the tools they need to continually enhance and enrich those invaluable assets.</p>
<p>Our vision is to show our commitment to excellence in training and development as the industry leader in Learning Management System functionality and other training and development products and services.</p>

<ul>
<li>Company paid benefits including medical, dental, vision, Section 125 cafeteria plan and life insurance</li>
<li>401k plan (employer matching of up to 5% of annual salary)</li>
<li>Paid vacation including additional company holiday days</li>
<li>Competitive salary</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Roles/Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Ensure client contract renewal with a focus on long-term retention</li>
<li>Provide demonstrations for add-on products to existing clients</li>
<li>Generate new leads and opportunities within assigned existing client accounts via upsell and cross-sell</li>
<li>Identify and develop strategic client relationships based on organizational structure and KPIs</li>
<li>Generate quotes and other client facing documentation to assist the inside sales process</li>
<li>Participate in team meetings and projects to improve sales processes &amp; sales output</li>
<li>Actively develop the skills required to close business independently</li>
<li>Support expansion of sales opportunities in existing client base</li>
<li>Assist in the achievement of retention and growth targets set by the sales organization</li>
<li>Maintain a pipeline of qualified, active non-net-new opportunities and manage them to close</li>
<li>Orchestrate internal and external resources on an organization-wide account management plan throughout the client lifecycle</li>
<li>Keep up to date with latest product releases and training seminars to ensure thorough product knowledge</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Qualifications and Required sills</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Team player</li>
<li>Embraces change</li>
<li>Outgoing and personable nature with a sales-driven attitude</li>
<li>Bachelor’s degree or equivalent. Preference given to Business, Marketing or Psychology.</li>
<li>Proven ability to sell with a minimum 2 years of experience. This can be in the service industry, field sales, door to door, retail or at an office. Technology or SaaS sales is an asset.</li>
<li>The ability to deal with and thrive on objections and rejection daily</li>
<li>Proficient in conducting detailed data analyses and using metrics to drive business results</li>
<li>Working knowledge of Salesforce, Adobe Acrobat, Excel, etc.</li>
<li>Experience identifying and qualifying sales opportunities within an existing customer base</li>
<li>Very strong verbal and written communication skills (writing sample required)</li>
<li>Ability to thrive in a dynamic, fast-paced environment that changes constantly</li>
<li>Experience working with a diverse and global client base</li>
<li>Interested in long-term career opportunities in SaaS sales</li>
</ul>
<p>Applicants must be able to show proof of legal eligibility to work in the United States.</p>
