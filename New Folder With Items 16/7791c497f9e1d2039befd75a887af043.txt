Description<div>
</div>
<div><b>
Purpose:
</b></div>
<div></div>
<div>The most important value at UNFI is “Do the Right Thing – Put Safety and Integrity at the Forefront of Everything We Do.” This role is part of the Risk &amp; Safety leadership team and is a guardian to ensure that the organization delivers on this critical value – day in and day out. The role is fast-paced and complex and has enterprise-wide oversight for all facets of security resulting in safe workplaces for our associates, customers, and visitors as well as active asset management. Development, maintenance, and deployment of effective business continuity and life-safety emergency plans are an integral part of maintaining a safe workplace.<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<div>This role is a both a subject matter and cultural expert on security and will lead, develop, and nurture an efficient and talented team that infuses a deeper culture and practice of security awareness into our organization. If you are a seasoned security leader with proven success in integrations and change management across multiple locations, this roll-up-your-sleeves position is right for you.<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<div><b>Job Responsibilities:
</b></div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>As the leader of the Security function, you will:</div>
<ul>
<li>Develop best in class security strategy across the organization including strategic review and improvement of current programs.
</li>
<li>Be a cultural ambassador of security and ensure that all associates, visitors, and vendors understand the Company’s commitment to providing safe workplaces for our associates, customers, visitors, and inventory.</li>
<li>
Be a key advisor to leadership (both corporate and field) teams so they understand weaknesses, deficiencies, and challenges within our security programs – including physical security measures, technology, and people. Work collaboratively with leadership to prioritize and address these issues.
</li>
<li>Develop, deploy, manage, and maintain training programs that prepare team and other associates to proactively recognize and report suspicion indicators of all types of violence in the workplace including idiopathic mass violence. Ensure there is a high-functioning threat assessment team that can react quickly and in real-time to potential and actual threats.</li>
<li>
Stay abreast of emerging innovations in security including best practices, benchmarking, recommended technologies to ensure that our policies/practices/measures match current societal trends.
</li>
<li>Create department budget and be good steward of resources.</li>
</ul>
<div><b>DISTRIBUTION CENTER</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Provide analysis and guidance to Distribution Center locations regarding overall security risk profile of locations as it relates to physical security, location/facility considerations, and type of operation to ensure leadership can make effective decisions and prioritization when it comes to security investments.</li>
<li>
Provide on-the-ground support as needed to support major investigations related to potential or actual violence as well as theft or other criminal activity.</li>
<li>
Ensure that all Distribution Centers have consistent and understood policies as it relates to all facets of security including access control, video coverage, guard coverage, loss prevention, and other applicable policies.
</li>
<li>Provide guidance on enhanced security needs in the event of labor disruption.</li>
<li>
Supervise and provide strategic direction to Director, Distribution Loss Prevention &amp; Security.</li>
</ul>
<div><b>CORPORATE</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Ensure that all corporate sites have proper security measures, including physical, technological, and people resources in place including working cross-functionally with facilities team in smaller non-headquarter locations by way of providing training, quick-reference guides, and access to the security team.
</li>
<li>Coordinate and direct exceptional security services for all major Company and Board events including Annual Meetings, Sales Expos, Investor Events, Board Meetings, and other similar-type events.</li>
<li>
Update and develop policies related to security including criminal background checks, violence-free workplace, badge requirements, etc. as needed.
</li>
<li>Work with Investor Relations and other external-facing functions to identify and mitigate risks to all associates including Named Executive Officers.</li>
<li>
Supervise and provide strategic direction to Manager, Corporate Security and Safety.</li>
</ul>
<div><b>BUSINESS CONTINUITY
</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Supervise and manage outside vendor to develop and update Business Continuity Plans including Business Impact Analysis related to potential disruptions.
</li>
<li>Ensure that all facilities have life-safety evacuation plans that are understood and practiced.</li>
<li>
Identify and update technology (like “In Case of Crisis”) to ensure real-time notifications of business interruptions.</li>
<li>
Ensure there is continuous monitoring and notification of events using technology (like “NC4”) that could impact our associates and/or business including severe weather and civil unrest.
</li>
<li>Supervise the development, deployment, and practicing of life-safety evacuations at facilities.</li>
</ul>
<div><b>Job Requirements:</b></div>
<div>(Relevant Experience, Education, Certification, Knowledge, Skills and Abilities)</div>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s Degree preferred or equivalent experience</li>
<li>
20+ years of leadership experience in corporate security preferably with government or a large public company (with a recognizable profile) with previous responsibility for security, loss prevention, and business continuity.
</li>
<li>Ability to work collaboratively and professionally with partners across the enterprise with particular focus on HR and Legal.</li>
<li>
Demonstrated ability to design and implement an effective strategic framework for security operations.</li>
<li>
Ability to respond vs. react in intense situations and ability to use appropriate de-escalation skills as needed.</li>
<li>
Excellent written and verbal communication skills.</li>
<li>
Ability to balance multiple priorities and ensure significant issues are communicated clearly to relevant stakeholders in timely manner</li>
<li>
Exceptional presentation and training skills
</li>
<li>Exceptional ability to deal with conflict in all levels and areas of the organization</li>
</ul>
