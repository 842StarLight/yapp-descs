<h2>Customer Experience Agent</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Come help us protect what matters most!</b></p>
<p><b><i>
Mountain Alarm is hiring for a Customer Experience Agent in our Ogden, Utah location.</i></b></p>
<p><b>
Position Summary:</b></p>
<p>
Establishes and maintains working relationships with external and internal customers with excellent communication via telephone, mail (postal or electronic), or in person. Provides administrative support to external and internal customers. Responsible for achieving the goals set by the office manager. This position is expected to handle phone traffic and a variety of administrative duties from 8:00 am to 5:00 pm.
</p>
<p><b>Duties &amp; Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Professionally administers all incoming/outbound calls, redirects accordingly while providing excellent customer service.</li>
<li>Handle customer billing questions, service questions, comments, and complaints</li>
<li>Performs basic troubleshooting for inbound callers regarding their alarm systems.</li>
<li>Uses administrative skills to perform tasks in company software; Sedona, Stages, Office365, Outlook, OneNote, and various other Microsoft Office products.</li>
<li>Accepts phone and EFT payments and processes through accounting software.</li>
<li>Enters credit requests to be approved by the office manager.</li>
<li>Works directly with corporate RMR specialists to process cancellations.</li>
<li>Schedules service calls and alarm inspections. Adjusts technicians schedules and move appointments.</li>
<li>Performs basic data entry in monitoring and accounting software.</li>
<li>Provides feedback to management regarding customer service breakdowns or customer concerns.</li>
<li>Performs other duties as assigned.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Education &amp; Required Skills</b>:</p>
<ul>
<li>High School Diploma/GED</li>
<li>Excellent customer service skills</li>
<li>Excellent phone etiquette skills</li>
<li>Working knowledge of electronics</li>
<li>Must read and speak English</li>
<li>Flexible style, perseverance, action-oriented</li>
<li>Punctual with excellent attendance</li>
<li>Ability to work a full-time schedule</li>
<li>Must possess strong time management skills</li>
<li>Ambitious results oriented individual</li>
<li>Aptitude for problem-solving, ability to determine solutions for customers</li>
<li>Clear communication skills (email, phone, in-person) primarily among internal departments, field technicians, sales consultants, and customers.</li>
<li>Able to handle daily deadline pressure with positive "can-do" attitude.</li>
<li>Ability to maintain and project a positive outlook to customers at all times.</li>
<li>Maintains a good working environment with all employees, vendors, and customers.</li>
