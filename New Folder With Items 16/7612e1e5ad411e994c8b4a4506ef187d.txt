<h2>Admissions Representative</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p><b>POSITION DESCRIPTION</b></p>
<p>The Admissions Representative (AR) is responsible for handling all types of inquiries received by the college which include telephone, direct mail, and Internet. This admissions department process will require a proven ability to control a call in a professional yet conversational tone building relationships with prospective students. Additionally, this position requires the generation of personally developed leads through current students, outside resources and referrals. The AR must have the ability to meet goals, multi-task, prioritize duties and exhibit excellent communication skills both written and verbal. An important component of the AR’s job is identifying and enrolling qualified students who are likely to graduate. We look for extraordinary people who want to excel in an organization that has a life changing impact on its customers. This is a full time position that reports to the Director of Admissions, the Campus President or the Operations Manager.</p>
<p> <b>DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES</b></p>
<p> The Admissions Representative will also be responsible, but not limited to, the duties and tasks listed below:</p>
<ul>
<li>Adhere strictly to accrediting agency and U.S. Department of Education guidelines for proprietary school admissions</li>
<li>Follow all policies and procedures outlined in the Admissions manual and established by the campus and/or company</li>
<li>Follow Admissions Code of Conduct</li>
<li>Participate in all orientations if at a campus</li>
<li>Conduct high call volume in fast-paced environment</li>
<li>Listen and create rapport to identify opportunities for prospective students’ education and career development</li>
<li>Use established phone scripts for both inbound and outbound calls</li>
<li>Schedule admission interview call appointments</li>
<li>Conduct admission interviews over the phone, in person, or by video</li>
<li>Enroll qualified applicants</li>
<li>Conduct scheduled follow-up procedures with both enrolled and non-enrolled prospects</li>
<li>Enter all tasks and notes into CampusVue daily</li>
<li>Track incomplete enrollment files to ensure deadlines are met</li>
<li>Check and respond to all emails</li>
<li>Post all appointments on Outlook Calendar</li>
<li>Attend all Admissions and staff meetings</li>
<li>Complete required quota of inbound and outbound calls daily</li>
<li>Schedule required quota of appointments daily</li>
<li>Generate required quota of personally generated leads</li>
<li>Maintain clean and professional work area</li>
<li>Dress professionally as outlined in Employee manual</li>
<li>Other duties as assigned
</li>
</ul>
