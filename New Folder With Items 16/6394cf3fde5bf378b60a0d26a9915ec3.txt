<h2>Clinical Consultant Telecommute Central or Eastern Time Zones preferred</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<div>The Clinical Consultant will lead and manage ongoing contract relationships, pharmacy benefit analysis and consultation delivery to clients and mentor the team and drive processes to deliver results. Collaborate in development of strategy as outward - facing, dedicated resources for assigned accounts, typically with direct client contact for large / complex accounts. Build client relationships and serve as primary point of contact for overall and day - to - day service delivery for clinical pharmacy needs. Represent client(s) internally and coordinate with other functions to implement client systems, complete projects, and address ongoing pharmacy service needs. The Clinical Consultant will serve as the primary liaison between OptumRx clinical operations, professional practice, and clinical products and the consultant’s assigned client base. The role provides clinical support for OptumRx account management, operations, information technology, and other internal departments. This individual is relied upon to provide proactive clinical recommendations, information regarding pharmacy trend, programs and industry changes to clients and to foster consultative relationships.</div>

<div><b>Primary Responsibilities:</b></div>
<p></p>
<br>
<ul>
<li>
Collaborates with clients to establish achievable but aggressive clinical program goals, including generic dispensing rate improvements, implementation of utilization management programs, improvement in medication adherence rates, improvements in therapy gaps for key chronic disease states, formulary compliance targets, and HEDIS &amp; NCQA measures</li>
<li>
Provide superior clinical consultation and clinical account management with a focus on client satisfaction, client retention, and trend management</li>
<li>
Comprehends and effectively explains formulary and clinical program changes to clients</li>
<li>
Stays aware of and provides clinical market intelligence to clients</li>
<li>
Provides education for clients, pharmacists, members and physicians per contractual requirements</li>
<li>
Communicates drug information to clients and responds to plan-specific clinical inquiries</li>
<li>
Formulate and deliver client recommendations based on available data</li>
<li>
Identifies opportunities for clinical program up - sell</li>
<li>
Makes clinically sound recommendations to clients based on trend</li>
<li>
Routinely collaborating with account management to provide clinical aspects of client quarterly reviews</li>
<li>
Incorporates treatment and practice guidelines into client presentations, including up-selling opportunities</li>
<li>
Effectively manages client objections to new programs and required changes</li>
<li>
Manages / Facilitates custom P&amp;T committees for select clients</li>
<li>
Analyzes and interprets benefit designs</li>
<li>
Clearly communicates analytical and reporting needs to supporting departments</li>

Supports sales and marketing including participating in sales activities, as needed based on criteria</li>

Helps clients be competitive within their market</li>

Identifies opportunities to increase efficiency<br>
