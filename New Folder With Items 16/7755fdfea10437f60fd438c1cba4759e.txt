<h2>Field Service Technician - Houston</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<b>Description:</b><br>
<b>Position Overview:</b><br>
Under the direction of the Field Service Manager, the Field Service Technician is someone who schedules and performs field service work in accordance, as well as manages a limited parts inventory, with company policies and procedures. Duties include performing service work along with timely and accurate documentation of work performed.
<br><br>
<b>Responsibilities:</b><b>Field Service (primary):</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Completes dispatches in 48 hours or rejects dispatch so it can be reassigned</li>
<li>Complete work thoroughly to achieve 95% or higher FTFR</li>
<li>Follow field testing and rework procedures accurately</li>
<li>Document all inspections, maintenance, repair work and failures in the field through dispatch in Online Remedy</li>
<li>Provide complete and accurate information about the inspections and steps taken to make the repair per department work practices</li>
<li>When assigned, inspect newly installed equipment</li>
<li>Must attend the required trainings, learn required CRM and other systems, and pass certification on all relevant equipment.</li>
</ul>
<b>Customer Service (primary):</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Schedule and perform field service work assigned by Customer Tech Support supervisory staff</li>
<li>Log all service claims in Online Remedy within 24 hours of work completed</li>
<li>Answer Field Technician service calls, diagnose and set service remedy into motion</li>
<li>Maintain an accurate inventory of service parts and perform audits</li>
<li>Discuss, Present and Close Preventive Maintenance Service Contracts and Out of Warranty Service Quotes available to Customers</li>
<li>Discuss with Customer continued Service when product is no longer under warranty</li>
<li>Discuss New Products with Customers</li>
<li>Keep Sales informed of upcoming purchases Customers are planning</li>
</ul>
<b>Management Support:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Provide accurate service reports to management when requested</li>
<li>Frequent communication with Customer Tech Support, Quality, and Sustaining Engineering teams to assure high levels of quality and design of JHTNA products.</li>
<li>Report to Manager if Customer is having any issues that should be addressed at a higher level.</li>
</ul>
<b>Requirements:</b><br>
<b>Qualifications:</b><b>Education:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>High school diploma or equivalent required</li>
<li>College degree in related field preferred</li>
<li>Additional training in commercial fitness industry or combination of education and experience preferred</li>
</ul>
<b>Experience:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Minimum of 2-3 years’ experience in a technical support or service role required</li>
<li>Experience in technical and mechanical problem solving preferred</li>
<li>Working knowledge of commercial fitness industry preferred</li>
<li>Strong working knowledge of Microsoft Office Suite and similar communication related software</li>
<li>Experience with Microsoft CRM</li>
<li>Experience in field service preferred</li>
</ul>
<b>Other Requirements:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Valid driver’s license</li>
<li>Valid passport</li>
<li>Ability to analyze technical and mechanical problems</li>
</ul>
