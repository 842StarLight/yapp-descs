<h2>Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">The Front of House Manager will support restaurant operational objectives, delivering on promises to our team members, guests, vendors and our community to drive sales and profits. The Front of House Manager assists with executing on cost initiatives, developing employees, and maintaining high levels of guest satisfaction.
<br><br>
PHYSICAL REQUIREMENTS
<br><ul>
<li>Frequent walking, kneeling, bending and reaching.</li>
<li>Able to stand and be active for extended periods of time which may be at least 8 hours per shift.</li>
<li>Must be able to lift, move and carry up to 20 pounds frequently.</li>
</ul>
<b>POSITION REQUIREMENTS:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>High School Diploma or Equivalent</li>
<li>One year of previous supervisory experience in a hospitality environment preferred.</li>
<li>One year of prior hospitality experience preferably in restaurants.</li>
<li>Proficiency communicating (speaking, reading and writing) in English.</li>
<li>Familiarity with restaurant management software, NetChef, OpenTable and MICROS a plus.</li>
</ul>
</div>
