<h2>Technology Business Consultant Senior</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Are you curious, motivated, and forward-thinking? At FIS you’ll have the opportunity to work on some of the most challenging and relevant issues in financial services and technology. Our talented people empower us, and we believe in being part of a team that is open, collaborative, entrepreneurial, passionate, and above all fun.
</p>
<p><b>About the team:
</b><br>
Our support team supports <b>TouchPoint Teller </b>technology for US clients.
</p>
<p><b>TouchPoint Teller </b>complements traditional teller responsibilities by automating all monetary transactions, inquiries, compliance requirements, and administrative functions while providing a reliable store-and-forward environment to ensure transactions are not lost. Enhanced navigation features, image capture, and automated decisioning mitigate the effect of these changes on the teller. The solution also extends traditional teller responsibilities by providing expanded service and sales functions. It proactively displays cross-sell opportunities for tellers to act upon or refer to other areas of the institution.
</p>
<p><b>What you will be doing:
</b><br>
As a Technology Business Consultant Senior, you will be responsible for helping the customer and the support team break down complex business requirements into stories to deliver product functionality that solves problems impacting some of the largest banks in the U.S. You will work with the team to conduct gap analysis against existing functionality/frameworks, collaborate with software architects on functional design, and develop detailed functional specifications. You will empathize with both the clients and the software developers to reach solutions that achieve the best balance of business objectives and software design. You will help ensure that the product is well tested and documented.
</p>
