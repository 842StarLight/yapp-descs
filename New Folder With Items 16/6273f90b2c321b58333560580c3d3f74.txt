<h2>Senior Counsel</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p>The Senior Counsel is responsible for providing broad-based legal support for a publicly-held electricity and natural gas retailer. Responsibilities will include counsel and support related to regulatory compliance and litigation and customer complaint issues.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>SUMMARY OF ESSENTIAL JOB FUNCTIONS</b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>Analyze legal and business issues across all departments and develop recommendations that meet the legal and business needs of the company.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>Assist Assistant General Counsel with litigation management; attend to demand letters, settlement negotiations, discovery matters, related investigations, and responses; draft litigation hold letters as needed.</p>
<p>Attend to internal complaints leading to potential litigation; assist with managing litigation and perform necessary internal investigations. Will work with outside counsel</p>
<p>Draft, negotiate and analyze vendor and licensing agreements related to sales and marketing activity and back office functionality. Provide legal counsel on advertising, marketing and promotional issues, including door-to-door sales and telemarketing.</p>
<p>Perform discovery on cases as needed; actively participate in all settlement conferences and mediation</p>
<p>Work with Compliance Director and Quality Assurance/Quality Control team to review/revise audit plans for D2D and OTM quality control visits</p>
<p>Provide TCPA counsel on operations matters</p>
<p>Review new programs for outside sales and coordinate review and concerns with Regulatory (Can Spam Act/FTC/TCPA/State PUC guidance)</p>
<p>Work closely with Regulatory Director and Regulatory team to ensure compliance with applicable rules and regulations</p>
<p>Must be able to work on a variety of different legal matters in a fast paced environment</p>
<p>Additional responsibilities as assigned</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>MINIMUM REQUIREMENTS</b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>J.D. degree from an ABA accredited law school</p>
<p>Active license to practice law in the State of Texas, must be in good standing</p>
<p>3 years at reputable law firm in corporate transactional practice</p>
<p>In practice for a minimum of 8 years</p>
<p>Previous in-house experience in contract drafting and dispute management preferred</p>
<p>Relevant experience working for a retail electricity provider preferred</p>
<p>Superior verbal, written and interpersonal communication skills</p>
<p>Must be a quick thinker, highly analytical, articulate, and motivated</p>
<p>A team player, capable of working effectively on both a collaborative basis and independently, with the ability and understanding of how to operate in a dynamic, multi-cultural organization with a diverse portfolio of clients</p>
<p>Capable of generating a high degree of respect and trust rapidly from team members, as well as the various business units and management</p>
<p>Self-starter, results and success oriented, with the ability to be responsive and follow through on programs and plans
</p>
<p>Proficient use of Microsoft Office programs, including Word, Excel and Outlook</p>
<p>Must be able to work a minimum of 40 hours per week</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>ABILITIES REQUIRED</b></p>
<p>Ability to meet highest attendance requirements</p>
<p>Ability to handle multiple tasks concurrently</p>
<p>Ability to use personal computer, calculator, etc.</p>
<p>Could involve some lifting up to 20 pounds</p>
<p>Ability to sit for extended periods of time without much movement</p>
<p>Ability to work in time-sensitive environment with non-flexible deadlines</p>
<p>All candidates must have the ability to speak, read and write English</p>
