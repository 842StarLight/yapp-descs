<div><b>
About the Sales Operations Analyst:</b></div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
As a member of the Field Operations team, you will be responsible for providing data, models, and forecasting for the sales management team. In addition, supporting the direct sales organization with order management, pricing, contract vehicles, and assisting in getting deals across the finish line.
</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>The ideal candidate can is comfortable working with large sets of data, generating forward-looking insights, is structured with a slight flair for creativity, has a do-what-it takes mentality, is naturally curious and committed to creating the foundation for a strong business. Must enjoy winning, fun, and delicious food.</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div><b>
Position Responsibilities:</b></div>
<div>
</div>
<ul>
<li>Partner with sales leadership and operations to analyze and programatize areas for improvement across the sales organization</li>
<li>Assist with Order Management Process (Order Forms, Contract Vehicles)</li>
<li>Assist in maintaining OpenGov Sales Efficiency and Sales Insights</li>
<li>Assist with Territory Modeling, Pricing Analysis, and Ad-Hoc Analysis to Support Sales Management Team</li>
<li>Identify opportunities for operations process optimization and redesign to support a growing sales organization.</li>
<li>Master data integrity, ARR Recognition, and learn critical business systems (Salesforce, Outreach, and more)</li>
</ul>
<div>
</div>

Position Qualifications</b></div>
<div>
</div>

<li>Successful field support in B2B, SAAS, finance and/or accounting</li>
<li>Self-motivated, creative, results driven, solution oriented, driven to succeed</li>
<li>Ability to remain focused and flexible during rapid change</li>
<li>Crisp written communication and verbal communication skills</li>
<li>Quantitative Skills: Proficiency in Microsoft Excel and experience using multiple data sources, enriching data to complete analyses</li>
<li>BA/BS degree in Business, Finance, Computer Science, Accounting or other related field</li>
</ul>
