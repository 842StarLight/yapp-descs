Key Responsibilities
<br><br>
We are seeking a highly motivated Sales Director with a proven history of closing complex, high-value deals in a competitive and evolving marketing space to identify and ensure prospects become successful customers of MediaMath.
<br><br>
As a Sales Director at MediaMath, you will be responsible for identifying and closing key prospects, and working with solution engineering and marketing on new business strategy. You will foster a culture of sales excellence across all aspects of the sales cycle by targeting Economic Buyers at large, direct brands and adhering to a Customer-Focused Sales Methodology.
<br><br>
<b>You will:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Plan, direct, and coordinate sales activities across MediaMath and self-sourced prospects that align to our ideal customer profile</li>
<li>Develop robust sales opportunities from the above activity that result in new business wins to exceed target</li>
<li>Develop initial sales plan &amp; solution design based on excellent business and functional diagnosis during the sales cycle that results in successful client on-boarding, and handover to the account teams</li>
<li>Identification of upper third decision makers, their buying mode and priorities</li>
<li>Work cross-functionally, communicating with and activating key business units within MediaMath to ensure a successful sales process</li>
<li>Utilize sales software (e.g., Salesforce) to track pipeline, revenue forecasts, as a repository for client needs, and to for resource and product requests</li>
</ul>
<b>You are:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Able to communicate effectively with senior executives at Fortune 500 brands</li>
<li>Personable, charismatic, and positive</li>
<li>Ability to present and express yourself in a clear, confident manner</li>
</ul>
<b>You have:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>5+ years of Enterprise B2B strategic sales experience targeting Economic Buyers at large, direct brands</li>
<li>A history of hitting and exceeding revenue goals by adhering to a Customer-Focused Sales Methodology</li>
<li>Excellent communication skills; specifically, strong listening, negotiation, and presentation</li>
<li>Proven ability to establish meaningful relationships with decision makers with a track record of retaining key clients</li>
<li>Demonstrated ability to partner with Solutions Engineers on key accounts to drive deals and influence</li>
<li>Solution oriented and creative thinking skills to evaluate problems and to drive win-win outcomes</li>
