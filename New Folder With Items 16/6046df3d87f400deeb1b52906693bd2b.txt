<h2>Operations Reporting Analyst</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">Company Overview
<br><br>
Mosaic makes it simple to go solar by leveraging technology and financial innovation, with a goal of providing access to clean energy for everyone. We are looking to collaborate with passionate, thoughtful people who want to make a real social impact while working to solve climate change. Come join our growing team located in Phoenix and help build the movement towards 100% clean energy for all.
<br><br>
The Opportunity
<br><br>
You will own the analysis that enables the leadership in Mosaic's contact center to develop and enhance strategies to most efficiently serve consumers and our partners through call center and email interactions. You will be responsible for generating crisp and consumable analytics that help identify opportunities for process improvement. Your job is to be a fully embedded thought partner with the operations team.
<br><br>
The Operations Reporting Analyst will be a critical member of Mosaic's Enterprise Analytics team. This role will provide critical real-time insights to the operational front lines at Mosaic, including executive level reporting, business line reporting, team reporting, agent level reporting on KPI's, etc. In this role you will be working directly with the business line managers across Credit Operations (loan verifications, welcome call, and voice application by phone teams), Customer Support, Inside Sales, Quality Assurance, and our FCC Complaint &amp; Customer Advocacy groups. Your products will be highly visible throughout the organization and will drive visibility to our goals, the metrics that drive us towards achieving our goals, and improving accountability and better driving results across all levels.
<br><br>
This is a fast-paced and dynamic environment that holds great opportunity for you to impact business results. Your stakeholders are engaged and highly data driven. You will get to couple strong business understanding with analytical design thinking in a creative and collaborative environment.
<br><br>
Your day-to-day
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Create Business Intelligence tools that enable operations management to view KPIs in real time on a self-service basis.</li>
<li>Monitor operational KPI's, and conduct analysis to isolate causes for variances between realized and expected performance.</li>
<li>Develop and champion data-driven operational strategies for Credit Operations, Customer Support, Inside Sales, Quality Assurance, and FCC Complaint &amp; Customer Advocacy groups.</li>
<li>Work within the Enterprise Analytics team to identify, improve, and coordinate data sources and reconcile reporting insights provided across the company.</li>
<li>Develop and maintain an internal controls framework for owned reporting, ensuring data integrity of reporting results.</li>
<li>Partner with Credit Operations, Risk, and Legal to identify and implement opportunities to improve operational strategies and execution.</li>
<li>Create Powerpoint presentations on end of month operational results and performance trends across business lines for executive level reporting</li>
<li>Remain apprised of applicable regulations and compliance requirements, and support interactions and ad hoc reporting and analysis with Compliance, Legal, and external bank partners.</li>
</ul>
What you bring to the team
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>3+ years of consumer credit operations experience in a bank or fintech setting.</li>
<li>Experience reporting on and developing KPIs for call center and servicing operations.</li>
<li>Solid data handling and analysis skills – SQL, Python/R/SAS, etc.</li>
<li>Experience creating BI tools for self service to data and insights – especially Tableau.</li>
<li>Bachelor's degree.</li>
<li>Advanced MS Excel skills and strong working knowledge of other MS Office applications.</li>
<li>Ability to understand business drivers and engage in high quality design thinking to move from a problem statement to a tailored solution independently and quickly</li>
<li>You are a team player with exceptional verbal and written communication skills.</li>
<li>Articulate; you are able to negotiate with difficult stakeholders when needed</li>
<li>Able to multi-task and thrive in a high volume, fast-paced environment.</li>
</ul>
