<h2>Product Owner (Agile)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b><i>This position can work remotely much of the time (since Prognos primarily has offices in NYC and Boston-although, we are members of the WeWork network of offices)</i></b></p>
<p>
The ideal candidate will have a history of success defining and prioritizing stories/requirements for new and existing product lines, to deliver products that meet our customers' needs. This is a not a management position, though it is a technical product owner role that works hand-in-hand with our product leaders. The candidate will combine strategic thinking, exceptional communication and negotiation skills to manage complex initiatives with the team while managing product development work efforts. Working closely with both customers and internal stakeholders, the technical product owner will manage the detailed scope and delivery of initiatives on our product roadmap within Prognos' product suite.</p>
<ul>
<li>
Collaborate with key stakeholders to define goals and create an execution plan for development projects, with a focus on outcomes rather than solutions. For example, identify and evaluate custom requirements across multiple projects in order to construct feature requirements that will enable scale.</li>
<li>
Translate product initiative (single product or cross-product) requirements to functional team members throughout the product development lifecycle - such as defining user stories, grooming sprint backlogs and validating stories are done.</li>
<li>
Engage in conversations with key stakeholders across the team, including product leaders, data scientists, clinical solution architects and engineers to make sure that development goals and priorities are clear and aligned with business objectives.</li>
<li>
Engage with customers (internal and external) as needed to establish and validate requirements throughout development process</li>
<li>
Lead agile/scrum activities such as sprint planning, daily standups, demos and retrospectives. Write user stories from scratch and refine / elaborate those created by other team members.</li>
<li>
On occasion, help mitigate impediments impacting successful team completion of a release or sprint.</li>
<li>
Prepare and maintain the project planning / sprint planning tools, such as milestone plans, resource alignment maps, burndown charts, and the necessary backlog of stories so that teams have adequate visibility and information on work that is planned and how the work is progressing.</li>
<li>
Drive product release planning and set expectations for delivery of new capabilities, features, and functionalities. For example, may provide training on the product to other groups and may support training efforts with customers, as needed.</li>
<li>
Provide vision and direction to the development teams and stakeholders throughout the product life cycle, ensuring alignment with business objectives.</li>
<li>
Work closely with the implementation and customer success teams to develop delivery processes that encourage adoption of new products and features.</li>
<li>
Support sales and marketing with demonstrations of new products and features, and contribute technical content to help drive marketing materials, website or sales collateral</li>
</ul>

About Prognos</b></p>
<p>
Prognos is a healthcare AI company focused on predicting disease to drive decisions earlier in healthcare in collaboration with payers, Life Sciences and diagnostics companies. The Prognos Registry is the largest source of clinical diagnostics information in 40 disease areas, with over 20B medical records for 200M patients. Prognos has 1000 extensive proprietary and learning clinical algorithms to enable earlier patient identification for enhanced treatment decision-making, risk management and quality improvement. The company is supported by a $42M investment from Safeguard Scientifics, Inc. (NYSE: SFE), Merck Global Health Innovation Fund (GHIF), Cigna (CI), GIS Strategic Ventures, Hikma Ventures, Hermed Capital, and Maywic Select Investments.
