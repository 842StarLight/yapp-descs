<h2>Inside Sales Representative</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>McClatchy </b>has an exciting opportunity for a Inside Sales Representative on our Inside Sales Team. Both inbound and outbound category opportunities are available. The successful candidate will drive growth for our clients by offering a variety of solutions to best fit the customer’s needs. The successful candidate will also be comfortable offering appropriate solutions for each of our different types of business categories.</p>
<p><b>
Primary Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Understand client’s business needs typically in a short close sale cycle to efficiently offer solutions, ensuring they receive their desired ROI</li>
<li>
Verify all aspects of the ad with the customer prior to placement for accuracy</li>
<li>
Manage relationship with clients to ensure they receive best-in-class service. Identify opportunities for cross-sell and up-sell</li>
<li>
Follow credit policies and obtain pre-payment when necessary</li>
<li>
Service the clients if any post-sale issues occur</li>
<li>
After the contact, access other opportunities and follow procedures for handing off customer if a larger opportunity is identified</li>
<li>
Prospect potential opportunities and maintain relationships with existing classified accounts clients</li>
<li>
Identify and pursue prospects within opportunity categories</li>
<li>
Service clients across multiple markets and speak to their various value propositions</li>
<li>
Manages time to focus on outbound sales calls when not handling inbound</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Key Competencies</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Manages a quick sales cycle well</li>
<li>
Is able to make an immediate connection with a client over the phone</li>
<li>
Understands each client and their unique business needs in order to provide ROI-impactful solutions.</li>
<li>
Exhibits technical competency, e.g. updating our CRM</li>
<li>
Thrives in high-volume, fast-paced environment</li>
<li>
Able to converse with multiple markets, manage various market value propositions, switch gears quickly</li>
<li>
Embraces change</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Job Requirements</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Publishing, advertising or media experience preferred.</li>
<li>
At least 1-2 years sales experience. Telemarketing sales experience preferred.</li>
<li>
High School - Required, College - Preferred</li>
<li>
Ability to work Monday - Friday; occasional Saturdays may be needed. Early evening hours required.</li>
<li>
Proficient in Microsoft Word, Excel, and Powerpoint and Google Office Tools</li>
<li>
Excellent written and verbal communication skills</li>
<li>
Excellent customer service skills</li>
</ul>
<br>
The fulltime position includes a competitive salary, commission program, and a comprehensive benefits package, including 401(k) match.<br>
