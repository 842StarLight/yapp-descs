<p><b>
POSITION SUMMARY</b>:</p>
<p>
The Lead UX Designer works within the UX Design Team at CDK Global to strategize, conceptualize and produce the creation of Web-based tools and consumer interfaces. The Lead UX Designer will work with the project team to create visual, interactive, multi-device solutions that meet the needs of the client, meet business objectives, and provide an engaging and compelling user experience. Must have advanced experience and demonstrated ability conceptualizing, wireframing, prototyping, and delivering interface designs.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
KNOWLEDGE AREAS</b>:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Advanced experience and demonstrated ability designing web and mobile experiences for consumers.</li>
<li>
Advanced ability creating end-user objectives and developing creative concepts to support them.</li>
<li>
Strong design and concept presentation, client relationship, and written and verbal communication skills.</li>
<li>
Practiced in the following usability practices: Surveys, user interviews, card sorting, prototyping, personas, information architecture, heuristic evaluations, user session videos, behavioral targeting, landing page optimization, and usability testing.</li>
<li>
Experience developing and working within established style guidelines.</li>
<li>
Experience writing estimates and setting timelines.</li>
<li>
Training and experience working with and/or producing content for web.</li>
<li>
Practiced in facilitating multi-stakeholder meetings to determine design objectives.</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>
High attention to detail and ability to work and manage drafts and deliverables in an extremely organized manner.</li>
<li>
Open and positive attitude toward clients and co-workers.</li>
<li>
Basic knowledge of major browsers and cross-browser/platform/device compatibility issues.</li>
<li>
Expert ability to communicate changing requirements or priorities across a project team and achieve timely and universal acceptance.</li>
<li>
Advanced proficiency in tools like Adobe Suite, Omnigraffle, Sketch or Axure.</li>
<li>
Advanced level use of MS Office Suite and Windows and Macintosh platforms.</li>
<li>
WYSIWIG editing of HTML, knowledge of capabilities for cross-browser, multi-device, CSS, HTML5, JavaScript and JSP is a plus.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
PRINCIPAL DUTIES &amp; RESPONSIBILITIES:</b></p>
<p><b><i>
Essential Functions:</i></b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Interpret use cases and design effective user interaction through the creation of workflow diagrams and wireframes.</li>
<li>
Designing effective UI for Web-based designs and widgets. Outputs: wireframes with creative brief, initial look and feel mockups to communicate project vision internally and externally, specification docs. For large projects: Personas, Usability testing and reports, PowerPoint presentation highlighting key features.</li>
<li>
Estimation of time to complete design and production phase of projects.</li>
<li>
Establishing and maintaining high quality relationships with Software Engineers, Project Managers and Sales Associates to ensure accurate implementation of specified design &amp; functionality.</li>
<li>
Create style guides, prototypes and index sites for viewing mockups.</li>
<li>
Collect quantitative data from Business Intelligence and both prepare and share findings with recommended changes to solutions.</li>
</ul>

<br>

Staying abreast of the latest developments in Internet software development technologies for desktops, tablets and mobile.</li>
<li>
Consistent tracking and entry of hours worked, for a minimum of 80% personal and team utilization.</li>
