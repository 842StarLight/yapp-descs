<div><h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Job Purpose</b></h3></div>
<div></div>
<div><div>
<p>The Account Executive position plays a critical role within the Sales organization and is responsible for marketing and sale of Evolves products and services within a defined territory, working with existing customer relationships as well as by developing new customers. The role involves assisting customers finding appropriate solutions to their energy management and room controls needs as well as to develop software solutions (SaaS), and service agreements. The Account Executive will be reporting to the SVP of Sales.</p>
</div></div>
<div><h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Position Description</b></h3></div>
<div></div>
<div><div>
<p>Including but not limited to:</p>
<ul>
<li>Nurture existing customer relationships to develop sales opportunities.</li>
<li>Prospect for new customer to develop relationships and sales.</li>
<li>Account Executives shall act as the liaison between Evolve Controls and customers.</li>
<li>Develop sales proposals to meet the customer needs and margin expectations of Evolve Controls.</li>
<li>Continue Sales relationship by proposing Service Agreements including service offerings, remote services, cloud-based services, energy efficiency projects and additional services.</li>
<li>Work with operations to conduct pre-bid reviews, to ensure accurate, high quality projects.</li>
<li>Maintain an accurate CRM database, including proper forecasting, project probability.</li>
<li>Ensure project documentation such as signed agreements, purchase orders, and Terms &amp; Conditions are received and processed on time.</li>
<li>Participate in conventions, meetings, seminars, and training sessions associated with our product/industry.</li>
<li>Account Executives shall maintain current industry knowledge, understand the market and our competitors, and Evolve Controls solution(s).</li>
</ul>
</div></div>
