<h2>Customer Support Advisor</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>TalentGuard is a market leader in the talent management space. Our award-winning talent management suite measurably improves a company’s ability to develop and retain people.</p>
<p>
TalentGuard is searching for a talented Customer Support Advisor to join the team and report directly to the Director of Customer Success.</p>
<p>
In this role, you will provide guidance and support for our cloud-based applications, to customers both large and small. You will provide technical support and guidance on software configuration, utilization, and troubleshooting to ensure successful implementation and usage. This person will analyze internal customers' environment and explain complex issues to maintain customer satisfaction and maximize technology utilization. You will observe and report on activities used to support customers; providing ideas, input, and innovation throughout the customer engagement process.</p>
<p><b>
Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Utilize tools and techniques for maintaining an environment where all understand and are committed to providing excellent service to internal and external customers.</li>
<li>Provide insight into clients' key objectives and ability to work and think alongside clients to achieve clients' current and future aspirations and successes.</li>
<li>Apply principles and techniques of communicating with a customer; the ability to utilize practices, tools, and techniques for customer interaction.</li>
<li>Apply problem management processes and experience bringing a reported problem to a successful resolution.</li>
<li>Uses principles and methodology of customer request management and ability to apply the processes, activities and tools associated with managing customer requests.</li>
<li>Use self-management practices and ability to manage multiple concurrent objectives, projects, groups, or activities, making effective judgments as to prioritizing and time allocation.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Minimum Requirements:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>3+ years of successful sales experience in customer support</li>
<li>Achieve top ratings from customers on post-service surveys</li>
<li>Manage multiple chat requests concurrently</li>
<li>Analytical, detailed-oriented and passionate about servicing clients</li>
<li>Demonstrated ability to self-motivate and work under pressure</li>
<li>Background supporting Saas software specifically talent management or human resources applications</li>
<li>Strong verbal, written and presentations skills</li>
<li>Bachelor’s degree, preferably in Business</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Tools Used in Customer Support:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>ZenDesk</li>
<li>Zopim</li>
<li>Zoho CRM</li>
<li>Team Foundation Services</li>
<li>Trello</li>
<li>PandaDoc</li>
<li>WebEx</li>
</ul>
