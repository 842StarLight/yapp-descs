<h2>Bookkeeper for Non-Profit Retreat and Spa</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Working at Menla means being part of a small vibrant community, working for a meaningful cause, and being immersed in a healthy and stunning natural environment. Our retreat center is a hidden oasis in the Catskills featuring spiritual and wellness retreats and a world-class healing Spa.</p>
<p>We are seeking a highly organized, detail-oriented, self-motivated and friendly Bookkeeper.</p>
<p>Duties:</p>
<p>Works in conjunction with the Director, Department Managers, and off-site primary Bookkeeper to ensure basic on-site bookkeeping duties are fulfilled. After receiving approvals from Dept Managers to pay statements and invoices, promptly paying credit card bills and all vendors, including spa therapists, ensuring we do not overpay/double-pay invoices, incur late payment fees, nor pay sales tax (we are tax exempt) and also that we have W9s on file for all vendors before bills are paid.</p>
<p>Distribute monthly credit card expense activity reports to credit cardholders to obtain receipts and explanations for each line item. Upload all credit card activity into Quickbooks each month and code with the appropriate general ledger account according to the activity reports received back from cardholders.</p>
<p>Code all invoices received according to general ledger chart of accounts and enter all bills into Quickbooks Online. Process checks for each bill and mail payments to vendors.</p>
<p>Uploading deposits electronically to bank and entering sales receipts into Quickbooks Online, matching sales receipts to bank deposits.</p>
<p>Maintaining correspondence with the parent organization and the third-party accounting firm as necessary.</p>
<p>Processing payroll: enter payroll hours, wages and deductions for each employee into online accounting software.<br>Provide new employee paperwork as needed and set up new employees in payroll system.</p>
<p>Regularly filing/maintaining orderly bookkeeping and personnel files</p>
<p>Helping the Director fill out and submit miscellaneous forms, including annual Permit to Operate.</p>
<p>Qualifications:<br>In Depth knowledge and experience working with basic double-entry accounting, GAAP, FASB as it relates to the structure of financial statements, key financial metrics</p>
<p>Organizational skills, attention to detail, effective time management, and good communication skills</p>
<p>Basic computer skills and proficiency in web-based technology such as Microsoft Office, Quickbooks Online, and Mindbody (we can provide basic training in Mindbody).</p>
<p>Previous experience with Intuit Quickbooks accounting software is preferred, ideally Quickbooks Online.</p>
<p>At least 2 year of experience in either an accounting or bookkeeping position and at least two years of higher education with a concentration in accounting and business, ideally a four year Business or Accounting Degree</p>
<p>Must be available during normal business hours at least 2 days per week. Job should ideally take 2-3 full days per week, less in winter/early spring and more spring-fall.</p>
<p>Serious inquiries only please. Email resume and cover letter to Michael Burbank to apply.</p>
