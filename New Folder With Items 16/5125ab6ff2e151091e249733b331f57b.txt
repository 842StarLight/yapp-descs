<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Rebel Who?</b></h3>
<h5 class="jobSectionHeader">
<b>
Rebel Athletic, based in Dallas TX, is the fastest-growing cheerleading uniform and accessory company in the world. Over the past 5 years Rebel has turned heads quicker than any other manufacturer – and now owns 50% of the US All-Star Cheerleading apparel market. Rebel’s cutting-edge designs, technological innovations and unstinting focus on service, quality, choice, and value have set a new standard in an industry that was starving for something new. Rebel is the brand that everyone else follows. So, if you want to be a follower, go and work for them. If not, read on.</b><br>

<b>To level the playing field so that every athlete can feel like a superhero</b><br>

Rebels are born, not made. There’s something in every Rebel that rebels against the status quo, against mediocrity, against unfairness, and against business and thinking as usual. Rebels love challenges, they may or may not work 9-5, but they are always thinking of new ways to innovate, new ways to drive the business forward, and new ways to surprise and delight their fellow workers, customers and the industry at large. <b>What’s This Role All About?</b><br>
<b>The Bare Bones:</b>
</h5>
<h5 class="jobSectionHeader">
<b>
Summary of Position: The Senior Account Manager will be assigned a geographical sales territory. You will be responsible for introducing our products to new customers as well as generating a new orders from our existing customers.</b><br>
<br>
<b>You must be excellent at sales and have a proven record of success excelling in previous outside sales positions. You have a dynamic personality, don’t like to follow conventional thinking, have big plans for your life, are highly motivated, deeply organized and want to go to bed every night and know that you are a big part of the next “It” brand in our industry. Your passion for sales is second only to your passion for life and family. You love being around people and appreciate a job structure that allows you to travel and be out on the road often rather than always in an office. You take great joy in seeing an idea grow from a piece of art into a real uniform on an athlete and knowing that they LOVE it.</b><br>
