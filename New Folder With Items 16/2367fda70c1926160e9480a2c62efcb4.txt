<h2>Graphic Designer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Cove is a startup DIY security company. We don't have sales teams, just simple installation and customer support that constantly amaze customers. Our design team is focused on iterating with usability and split testing. We are looking for a passionate, driven, designer who is willing to jump into multiple projects while incorporating feedback from stakeholders and the team.</p>
<p>Responsibilities</p>
<p>Research and design concepts for web that are clean, usable, and marketable.</p>
<p>Work with stakeholders and other departments to provide thoughtful design solutions.</p>
<p>Rapidly iterate on design concepts-from initial briefing through final delivery.</p>
<p>Work within a design system while looking for ways to improve.</p>
<p>Work closely with developers, remotely and in person.</p>
<p>Work collaboratively with a team.</p>
<p>Qualifications</p>
<p>1—3 years of working experience</p>
<p>A Bachelor’s degree in design, advertising, art direction or a related field</p>
<p>Strong design skills with a portfolio showcasing thought, communication, and beautiful design.</p>
<p>Ability to empathize with users and customers.</p>
<p>An eye for detail, with a love of typography, color, and consistency.</p>
<p>Proven track record of working with a multidisciplinary team to deliver elegant design solutions.</p>
<p>Working knowledge of Sketch, Adobe Illustrator, and Invision.</p>
<p>Understanding of HTML, CSS, browsers, and devices is a plus.</p>
<p>Illustration skills are a plus.</p>
<p>Motion experience is a plus.</p>
<p>Candidates must provide a portfolio to be concidered.</p>
