<h2>Membership Coordinator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Position Objective: </b></p>
<p>Oversees all aspects of the Skowhegan Regional Chamber of Commerce Membership, and development strategies and initiatives, and all marketing and communication efforts. This includes maintaining and enhancing current successful programming and creating new revenue streams and initiatives. This position increases awareness and understanding of the Skowhegan Regional Chamber of Commerce mission and work in the communities to promote and attracting business for the advancement of the region through leadership, communication and education.</p>
<p>This position will start as part-time and work to full time depending on the right candidate.</p>
<p><b>Responsibilities: </b></p>
<p>We are seeking an individual that has a proven sales track record and excellent verbal and written communication skills. We need someone who is not afraid of the cold call. The desired candidate must be outgoing and work well with people and be able to meet goals. The selected candidate must be able to close a sale with minimal supervision; and must be a self-starter. In addition, this position is responsible for helping to retain the existing members. The selected candidate must be able to provide excellent customer service and demonstrate a desire to excel.</p>
<p>The job requires travel in personal vehicle as well as some evenings and weekend days.</p>
<p>This position manages recruiting and retention of our chamber members, coordination of member events and benefits such as Business After Hours, promotions and educational programming, website and guidebook advertising.</p>
<p>Job Type: Part-time</p>
