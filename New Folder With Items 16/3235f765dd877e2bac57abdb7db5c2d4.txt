<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>About Us</b></h3>
<p>
We are a boutique consulting firm that helps businesses optimize and streamline their processes by implementing cloud ERP (Enterprise Resource Planning) and CRM (Customer Relationship Management) systems. Our highly skilled executive team works hand in hand with your organization to achieve exceptional business improvements, so that each member of your staff can focus on what they do best rather than on repetitive manual processes, spreadsheet management and other non-value-added activities. Our team of consultants is dedicated to helping businesses achieve their goals with smart, effective and scalable cloud solutions. Acting as true partners, we contribute our diverse experiences and in-depth expertise to the success of your organization.</p>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Role</b></h3>
<p>
Big Bang ERP is seeking a Business Development Executive to work as an external partner with our fast-growing team. <b>This is a 100% commission-based sales role</b>. An average performer can expect to make about $50,000 and an exceptional performer can be making $150,000. We are open to employed individuals ready to leverage their professional network exclusively for Big Bang ERP.</p>
<p>
Reporting to the Director of Business Development, the Business Development Partner will actively participate in generating and managing new business to expand the company’s North American footprint.</p>
