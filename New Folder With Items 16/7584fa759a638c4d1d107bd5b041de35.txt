<h2>Project Manager Coordinator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>COMPANY OVERVIEW</b></p>
<p>
RSi is a leading provider of retail data science based analytics and a big data management platform which helps top retailers and CPG companies manage their supply chain and marketing initiatives to drive sales results. Using machine learning, streaming analytics and predictive models, we are the go-to cloud based; peta-scale SaaS data management platform in the retail industry. Join our team as a Project Manager Coordinator and join a global innovative technology leader with a strong presence in Providence, RI, USA.</p>
<p>
The Project Manager - SATO will be responsible for successfully scheduling, implementing and tracking various projects as they relate to activating, terminating, and/or upgrading various services to meet contractual and other provided timelines for revenue recognition and client activations. This position will play an integral role in various production processes including, but not limited to, service activations, terminations, migrations and silo re-configurations (hot fixes, metric builds, CRs).
</p>
<p><b>Primary Responsibilities</b>
</p>
<ul>
<li>Oversee full production schedules including new silo builds, silo upgrades, and/or migrations. Manage any conflicts accordingly to meet required deadlines.
</li>
<li>Work to insure services are turned on efficiently with high quality. Insuring services are up-to-date, complete and accurate upon service activation.
</li>
<li>Working closely with ProdOps, Product Management and Engineering to understand the implications of any code changes on SATO processes and/or various project timelines.
</li>
<li>Provide accurate, thorough and timely updates to key project stakeholders on project status, risks, dependencies, and timelines.
</li>
<li>Assist Program Manager with Provisioning Database daily responsibilities and integration/enhancement projects.</li>
<li>
Manage incoming PO/SATO JIRA enhancement requests insuring proper prioritization and timely completion of tasks including, but not limited to, accurate business requirement definition, clear understanding of scope and impact on various teams/projects, integration opportunities between projects, and successful deployment.
</li>
<li>Continually evaluate the SATO processes to identify and remove areas of inefficiency to bring supplier services live as quickly as possible.
</li>
<li>Perform additional duties as assigned.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Skills and Experience Required</b>
</p>
<ul>
<li>BA/BS degree or equivalent business experience, some background in technology organization is a plus.</li>
<li>
Minimum 3 years of related experience in a project management role. A strong emphasis on technology projects is a plus.
</li>
<li>Ability to understand the relationships between various aspects of a process and foresee the potential impact on project timelines and deliverables.
</li>
<li>The ability to establish and maintain effective relationships with managers and employees at all levels in efforts to meet, and exceed, project deliverables.
</li>
<li>Strong computer skills with MS Office products.</li>
<li>
Exceptional organizational, communication, presentation and leadership skills.</li>
<li>
Ability to multi-task and prioritize changing objectives and work with minimal supervision and direction.</li>
<li>
Ability to take initiative and take ownership of projects assigned.</li>
<li>
Excellent verbal and written communication skills.</li>
<li>
High degree of honesty and integrity.</li>
<li>
Team-player, positive attitude and flexible.</li>
<li>
Familiarity with Jira, Salesforce, and/or Gainsight a plus.</li>
<li>
Familiarity with contracts, financial accounting a plus.</li>
</ul>
