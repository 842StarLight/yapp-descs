<p><b>Insurance Customer Service Rep.</b></p>
<p>You’re not looking for a job. You’re looking for a career. You want to do meaningful work and learn. You want to contribute to a growing enterprise that knows—and acts like—people are its most important asset.</p>
<p>You want to work for a company where everyone—from the owners to insurance professionals and everyone in between—lives the Core Values of Smart, Accountable and Trustworthy. You want to sleep at night knowing you work for a company that openly declares “we do it right or we make it right.” You want to experience the pride that comes with going above and beyond to help others.</p>
<p>If this describes you, welcome home to Brightway Insurance.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>ABOUT THIS CAREER OPPORTUNITY:</p>
<p><b>As a Customer Service Representative:</b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>You will participate in our comprehensive 9-week paid training program and enjoy ongoing coaching and development, ensuring your success in the role and a great start to your insurance career. Your focus will be on building rapport and positive relationships with existing customers, agents and our insurance company partners to understand and meet their needs and ensure a memorable and positive customer experience.<br>
</li>
<li>Develop knowledge and skills of an Insurance Professional, including active listening and critical thinking skills to quickly analyze and clearly understand specific requests and customer needs, then leverage knowledge and resources to provide the appropriate solutions</li>
</ul>
<p><b>QUALIFICATIONS:</b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Background in customer service, insurance, retail, sales or a related customer-facing field<br>
</li>
<li>Excellent communication skills including the ability to understand complex information and relay it accurately, simply and confidently to customers<br>
</li>
<li>Ability to thrive in a fast-paced environment, navigate multiple programs and effectively use online resources to successfully complete customers’ requests<br>
</li>
<li>Passion for high achievement with the ability to self-manage and demonstrate personal accountability, as well as effective collaboration in a team environment<br>
</li>
<li>Strong customer service skills with a focus on delivering high customer value</li>
<li>Demonstrate strong personal ethics and teamwork</li>
</ul>
