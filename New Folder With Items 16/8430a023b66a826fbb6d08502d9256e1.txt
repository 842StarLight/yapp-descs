<p><b>
Position Summary<br>
</b></p>
<p></p>
<p>CDK Global is seeking a highly-skilled and experienced Manager Success Consultant to be a critical part of our Learning &amp; Performance team and join our mission of building stronger dealerships with cutting-edge technology. In this role, you will design, develop and deliver leadership programs for new and experienced people managers in the CDK Client Delivery. The ideal candidate is a self-starting and flexible individual seeking exciting opportunities within a dynamic company.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Position Responsibilities &amp; Essential functions</b></p>
<p></p>
<br>
<ul>
<li>
Design, develop and deliver leadership programs for new and experienced people managers in the CDK Global Client Delivery team.</li>
<li>
Curate and develop leadership training and program content (facilitator led, self-paced/online).</li>
<li>
Evaluate existing internal and external leadership development programs and resources for inclusion in manager curricula.</li>
<li>
Facilitate virtual and in-person workshops and conduct one on one coaching sessions/calls for people managers at all levels of experience.</li>
<li>
Work with subject matter experts (SMEs) to develop and implement leadership programs.</li>
<li>
Facilitate virtual and in-person leadership events, as well as one on one coaching, for people managers at all levels of experience.</li>
<li>
Assist people managers in developing actionable development plans.</li>
<li>
Establish and effectively manage project outlines and deadlines for development projects.</li>
<li>
Partner with leadership, stakeholders and HR to identify metrics and develop KPIs.</li>
<li>
Facilitate and administer a wide variety of assessments; generate reports and analyze trends based on assessment results.</li>
<li>
Communicate and gain buy in from leadership and key stakeholders.</li>
<li>
Leverage industry best practices from sources outside of CDK Global, as appropriate.</li>
<li>
Ensure leadership communications to managers and regular, targeted communication to drive awareness of the program and new materials added on an ongoing basis.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Qualifications &amp; Job Requirements</b></p>
<p></p>
<br>
<ul>
<li>
Bachelor’s degree in related field or equivalent combination of education and relevant work experience</li>
<li>
8+ years experience in Training, Talent Development or HR within large, global high tech, or related industries preferred.</li>
<li>
Minimum of 2 years of experience as a leadership development instructor or equivalent demonstrated first line supervisory experience.</li>
<li>
Self-driven to execute with minimal direction while also effectively collaborating in a team environment.</li>
<li>
Strong business acumen, consultation skills and solid understanding of the link between learning solutions and business results.</li>
<li>
Detail-oriented project manager with the ability to align and hold multiple stakeholders accountable to project scope, timelines, and activities.</li>
<li>
Demonstrated program management and blended instructional design experience including assessing needs, partnering with subject matter experts, building learning programs, facilitating live and virtual courses, and measuring outcomes.</li>
<li>
Strong facilitation and communication skills in a fast paced and evolving environment.</li>
<li>
Knowledge of adult learning principles.</li>
<li>
Experience in facilitation, coaching and delivery of 360 feedback and other leadership assessments preferred.</li>
<li>
Ability to network and build a range of trusted stakeholder relationships.</li>
<li>
Commitment to continuous learning and development.</li>
</ul>
