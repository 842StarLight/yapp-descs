<h2>Account Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Job brief</b></p>
<p>Headquartered in the Tampa Bay / St. Petersburg area, we are a rapidly growing provider of highly customized signage solutions for retail sign companies, with unmatched expertise in dimensional letters, ADA compliant signs, sandblasted signs, monuments, CNC routing, post and panel and a wide array of other custom sign products. We proudly offer industry-best service, support and advice to our customers, who rely on us as a turnkey partner for signage for projects across the country. Our company has experienced significant growth and is looking for new Account Managers to join our sales team to lead and participate in our continued success and growth.</p>
<p>Account Managers play a critical role in our organization. The role is to follow new projects and manage customer projects from beginning to end (collaborating with various operations and manufacturing colleagues). Success as an Account Managers is often predicated on developing long-term relationships with customers, connecting with key decision-makers inside regional and national sign companies. Our Account Mangers work on the front line with our customers while also interacting with various internal teams, driving our need to identify and hire dynamic and talented individuals who take pride in cultivating relationships and delivering a high level of service.</p>
<p><b>Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Operate as part of a dynamic fast paced team, collaborating with all parts of the organization</li>
<li>Deliver a superior level of customer service- we do what we say we are going to do, when we say we are going to do it.</li>
<li>As trusted advisor for all things relating to signage and other architectural products, serve as the lead point of contact for any and all matters specific to your customers</li>
<li>Maintain a meticulous detail-orientation, capturing relevant information into our CRM software</li>
<li>Demonstrate the ability to prospect, qualify and close on opportunities, with the initiative to follow up on lost projects for constructive feedback</li>
</ul>
