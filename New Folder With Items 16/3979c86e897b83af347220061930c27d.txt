<p><b>
Summary - Main Purpose of the Position</b></p>
<p></p>
<p>
Associate Director of Distribution has full responsibility (Profit and Loss, personnel, and facility) for the designated distribution center within North America. This role manages the establishment and administration of a defined set of procedures, requirements, goals and key initiatives while focused on metric and financial performance. Collaborates directly with senior leadership on the development of strategies to enhance the value and cost-effectiveness of all outcomes within the functional areas of the operation and participates in overall decision making as a member of the senior management team of the enterprise.</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Primary Responsibilities and Duties</b></p>
<p></p>
<p>
Plans and oversees strategic, operational, and administrative programs, projects and/or services of broad significance to the organization within the designated functional areas of focus. Establishes and implements short and long range organizational goals, objectives, strategic plans, policies and operating procedures. Will monitor and evaluate programmatic and operational effectiveness effecting changes required for improvement. Provides strategic advice and recommendations to leadership in the development, implementation and evaluation of new or modified operating policies, practices and procedures within the distribution environment. Analyzes, designs, documents and implements internal reporting systems and procedures for the distribution center within the areas of distribution, administration and facilities. Designs, coordinates and implements training programs for associates within the distribution center regarding the nature and application of operating policies and procedures. Manages and/or provides day to day leadership to various operational and administrative personnel engaged in specific project activities. Assesses the level of talent in key positions and implement development and mentoring activities to promote organizational growth. Drive robust development plans that drive succession plans. Ensure compliance with regulatory requirements (GMP, CBP, OSHA, DOT, ISO, etc.) in all areas of responsibility.</p>
<p></p>
<p>
These duties and responsibilities are intended to be representative of the work performed by the incumbent(s) in this position and are not all-inclusive. The omission of a specific duty or responsibility will not preclude it from the position.</p>
