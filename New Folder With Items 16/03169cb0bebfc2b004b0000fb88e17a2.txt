<h2>Aerospace - Accounts Receivable Specialist -1st shift (Monday- Friday 7am-3:30pm)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p><b>JOB SUMMARY
</b></p>
<p>As part of our recent growth, we are seeking an<b> Accounts Receivable Specialist </b>to join our team. The ideal candidate is expected to record payments to customers' accounts and maintain account receivable records by performing the essential duties and responsibilities.
</p>
<p><b>ESSENTIAL DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITES</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Prepare, verify and process invoices and coding payment documents.
</li>
<li>Post customer payments by recording cash, checks, and credit card transactions.
</li>
<li>Review and analyze Accounts Receivable aging reports and make appropriate collections by examining customer payments and payment history.</li>
<li>
Verifies validity of account discrepancies by obtaining and investigating information from sales, trade promotions, customer service departments, and from customers.</li>
<li>
Communicates with customers in a timely manner.
</li>
<li>Calls and/or mails correspondence to customers as necessary in order to update accounts.</li>
<li>
Monitor customer account details for non-payments, delayed payments and other irregularities, and notify customers of overdue accounts to solicit payment.</li>
<li>
Establishes credit information and approval processes for new accounts. Basic analysis of credit risks and worthiness.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
PREFERRED QUALIFICATIONS

Ability to provide excellent customer service.</li>

Ability to add, subtract, multiply, and divide in all units of measure, using whole numbers, common fractions, and decimals.
</li>
