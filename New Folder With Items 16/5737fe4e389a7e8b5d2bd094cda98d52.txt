<p><b>
GENERAL FUNCTION</b></p>
The Graphic Designer position works closely with the Senior Designer to translate brand objectives into compelling deliverables across a broad range of platforms including printed materials, web design, advertising, in store signage, social media, and other marketing materials. The Graphic Designer conceptualizes and produces layouts, mock-ups, and renderings with direction from the Senior Designer for selection and approval. The Graphic Designer executes all prepress production services for print vendors.<p><b>
MAJOR DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Executes artwork for in-store visuals including window designs, banners, branding visuals, and display units for all points of sale globally.</li>
<li>
Designs print literature including magazine ads, point of sale materials, packaging, catalogs, look books, and training materials; prepare and revise files for production process.</li>
<li>
Creates designs for brand websites.</li>
<li>
Helps to establish seasonal visual style guidelines.</li>
<li>
Conceptualizes translation of seasonal visual campaigns into creative but practical in store designs.</li>
<li>
Presents design in a clear and concise manner.</li>
<li>
Ensures graphics and art are aligned with broader brand identity aesthetic.</li>
<li>
Designs logos needed for special projects, events and initiatives.</li>
<li>
Manages the design project files/archives.</li>
<li>
Reacts to ongoing needs of the company across all departments.</li>
</ul>
