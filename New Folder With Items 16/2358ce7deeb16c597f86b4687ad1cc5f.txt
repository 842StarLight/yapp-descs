Job Objective:</b></p>
<p>
In order to maximize the growth of our organization, we are looking for an experienced Recruiter to join the team. This position will recruit, research, screen and refer candidates for job openings throughout the US by performing the following duties.</p>
<p><b>
Recruiter Duties and Goals:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Confers with management to identify personnel needs.</li>
<li>Assist in the development of recruiting programs, to include brochures about the organization and itâ€™s opportunities to attract applicants and to fill specific job openings.</li>
<li>Develops and maintains network of contacts to help identify and source qualified candidates.</li>
<li>Initiates contact with possibly qualified candidates for specific job openings</li>
<li>Reviews applications and phone screens applicants to obtain work history, education, training, job skills and salary requirements and to provide information about the organization and position.</li>
<li>Screens and refers candidates for additional interviews with others in organization.</li>
<li>Writes and places job advertising in various media.</li>
<li>Develops and maintains contacts with schools, alumni groups and other public organizations to find and attract applicants.</li>
