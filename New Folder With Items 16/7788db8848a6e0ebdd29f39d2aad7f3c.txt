<h2>Customer Success Enablement Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">We're looking for a detail-oriented self starter to join our Customer Success (CS) Enablement team to support and grow the pillars of our internal enablement program: education, communication and career growth.
<br><br>
The CS Enablement team empowers our global CS team to execute their role functions seamlessly and develop their careers by providing onboarding, training, resources and support. Our internal programs ensure that CS can maximize time and energy on ensuring our customers manage change to adopt Asana as both a technology product and a holistic approach to teamwork.
<br><br>
As the second member of the CS Enablement team, you'll play a critical role in ensuring the success and scalability of the program. You will support with the development and delivery of new hire onboarding and continued learning content, as well as manage the coordination and maintenance of new and existing programs. Based in our San Francisco office, you will work with our teams in Dublin, London, Munich, New York, Sydney and Tokyo.
<br><br>
What you'll achieve
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Manage and scale global CS new hire onboarding in partnership with Enablement Leads across Asana</li>
<li>Coordinate the schedule and logistics for new and existing global CS training programs</li>
<li>Support the CS Enablement Lead in creating, delivering and iterating on new and existing global CS training programs</li>
<li>Help scale the internal enablement program for Asana's change management framework: Asana Way of Change</li>
<li>Manage and create self-serve learning and enablement content within our Learning Management System and internal handbook</li>
<li>Gather and analyze data of the efficacy of the training programs and use a data-driven approach to iterate</li>
<li>Help ensure the CS team and broader Asana Business teams stay up to date on CS opportunities and initiative</li>
<li>Research and share external global opportunities for ongoing learning and professional development</li>
<li>Collaborate with Customer Success and Professional Services to ensure enablement initiatives are aligned with team needs</li>
<li>Partner with Global Enablement counterparts to ensure our roadmaps are strategically aligned, including User Operations and Sales</li>
<li>Work closely with Product Marketing to build CS-focused content &amp; facilitate ongoing learning</li>
</ul>
About you
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>2+ years of related experience</li>
<li>Program and project management experience for a complex program</li>
<li>Demonstrated experience in training or educating adult learners, including measurement of mastery and checks for understanding</li>
<li>Demonstrated experience in developing learning solutions and strategies to fill identified needs</li>
<li>Strong written and verbal communication skills, including experience with learning facilitation</li>
<li>Ability to gather insights, iterate and deliver in a data-driven manner</li>
<li>Ability to create high-quality content autonomously</li>
<li>Ability to work across a wide range of stakeholders, internal and external, to drive work forward</li>
<li>Ability to execute under swift deadlines (must love moving fast and being flexible)</li>
<li>Ability to embrace new challenges with confidence, curiosity and an open mind</li>
<li>An entrepreneurial problem-solving attitude and growth mindset</li>
<li>Love for learning and educating others</li>
<li>Eager, positive and highly reliable</li>
<li>Comfort with ambiguity</li>
</ul>
Nice to Haves
