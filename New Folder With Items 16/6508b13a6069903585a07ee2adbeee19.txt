<h2>Product Marketing Manager - IoT Products/Services</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>SUMMARY:</b> KVH is uniquely positioned to apply its global connectivity solutions to maritime IoT needs through KVH Watch, an IoT connectivity as a service product. The Product Marketing Manager, IoT Products &amp; Services, will play a critical role in establishing KVH’s leadership position within this market space and driving significant growth with an entirely new audience of potential customers – equipment manufacturers. This role requires a marketing professional with IoT service and technology experience. This individual will have ownership of such activities as the IoT marketing plan development and implementation; voice of customer research and application; competitive research and assessment; and identification, management, and execution of industry-specific tradeshow, events, and speaking opportunities. Reports to the Sr. Director, Marketing</p>
<p><b>ESSENTIAL DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES </b>include, but are not limited to the following.
</p>
<ul>
<li>Identify, plan, and manage KVH Watch/IoT product and service awareness and lead generation marketing campaigns, including strategic goals, positioning and messaging, concept development, prospect and lead communications, public relations, and advertising; establish and report on campaign goals and performance metrics</li>
<li>Serve as the Marketing Department subject matter expert for IoT technology, services, and trends while working closely with the KVH Watch product owner, business development manager, and development team on new business pursuits and product/service evolution</li>
<li>Conduct maritime IoT market and competitive research; maintain an up-to-date assessment of competitive positioning, products, trends and price points for marketing and sales reference; help identify business development partnerships in the IoT ecosystem</li>
<li>Identify and recommend opportunities for differentiation in KVH’s competitive positioning and product/service messaging as well as in KVH’s rate plan design and pricing strategy</li>
<li>Develop creative briefs and key messaging for IoT product and service launch initiatives based on voice of customer insights, competitive differentiators, and industry requirements; serve as copywriter and work with designers to create and publish compelling, effective marketing collateral, web//digital content, and presentation materials</li>
<li>Identify IoT-specific and maritime market-specific trade shows, events, and speaking opportunities; plan and execute KVH’s participation in those activities in cooperation with regional sales leads and the marketing team</li>
<li>Work with the Marketing UI lead, Senior Director for Customer Experience, and the KVH mini-VSAT Manager team on the ongoing development of the KVH Watch user interface and overall user experience</li>
<li>Develop new customer onboarding materials and ongoing customer engagement materials to drive customer success and retention in collaboration with relevant sales and marketing team members</li>
<li>Establish tracking and report on new subscribers and retention, analyzing by segment to inform and refine lead generation and customer engagement efforts</li>
<li>Develop materials for internal and external sales and service channel training; conduct training for relevant audiences</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Work with relevant regional sales leaders, the Global Proposal Manager, and the Senior Director, Marketing, to help craft winning, compliant proposals for IoT services</li>
</ul>
<p><b>QUALIFICATIONS</b>:</p>
<ul>
<li>Required: Internet of Things service and technology experience along with high-tech marketing background and experience; experience in the maritime industry and/or high-technology products and services preferred</li>
<li>Must be able to understand high-tech products and services with the ability to assimilate and articulate that information</li>
<li>Proven copywriting ability</li>
<li>Bachelor’s degree in Marketing or Engineering</li>
<li>Experience in commercial maritime industry a plus</li>
<li>7+ years practical experience in the marketing field, specifically experience launching new services, developing and executing marketing plans, and working across 360° marketing programs, including events, advertising, digital advertising, content marketing, and lead generation</li>
<li>Proven ability to understand an audience and creatively explain ideas in a way that will be appealing</li>
<li>Experience creating effective presentations for internal training and sales presentations</li>
<li>Outstanding time management skills and proven project management skills with the ability to organize, lead cross-functional teams, manage multiple assignments, and write under tight deadlines
</li>
<li>Proficient in Microsoft Office software (Word, Excel, Access, PowerPoint) and Adobe Acrobat</li>
</ul>
