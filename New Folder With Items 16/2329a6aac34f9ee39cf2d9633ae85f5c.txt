<h2>Client Executive</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<b>Position Summary</b><br>
STANLEY Healthcare provides enterprise solutions to transform the operations, asset management and patient safety of healthcare organizations. We are currently seeking an experienced, exceptional and process-driven Client Executive to join our team of experienced professionals.<br>
<br>
The Client Executive will grow STANLEY Healthcare’s footprint in acute healthcare facilities within a defined territory. The incumbent must have a proven track record of working with the hospital C-suite. The position requires both entrepreneurial and team skills to leverage resources to meet goals. This position will work to expand offerings at current customers; however, prospecting skills are required to expand the SHS brand and solution set with net new customers as well. Travel requirements about 60%<br>
<b>Essential Job Functions</b><ul>
<li>
Grow footprint of all Stanley Healthcare Solution Products within new territory</li>
<li>
Establish territory priorities based on Company direction and overall goals</li>
<li>
Managing and supporting relationships that follow up on operational and senior levels</li>
<li>
Establish a trusted advisor role with C suite and Departmental leaders</li>
<li>
Become experts on SHS products and the value proposition they offer to healthcare</li>
<li>
Provide timely updates to activity and forecast through SalesForce.com</li>
<li>
Meet or exceed quarterly and annual quota</li>
<li>
Manage to and meet defined activity, conversion, pipeline and revenue objectives</li>
</ul>
