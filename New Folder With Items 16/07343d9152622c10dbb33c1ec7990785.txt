<p><b>Org Marketing Statement</b>
</p>With annual sales of $14.3 billion in fiscal year 2018, Parker Hannifin is the world's leading diversified manufacturer of motion and control technologies and systems, providing precision-engineered solutions for a wide variety of mobile, industrial and aerospace markets. The company has operations in 50 countries around the world. Parker has increased its annual dividends paid to shareholders for 62 consecutive fiscal years, among the top five longest-running dividend-increase records in the S&amp;P 500 index.<p><b>
Essential Functions</b>
</p>We are currently seeking an Accounting Specialist for our Hose Products Division. This position will be based out our Division Headquarters in Wickliffe, Ohio. The individual in this role will perform a variety of monthly accounts payable transactions and payment distributions to meet monthly deadlines and closings; while assisting Accounting personnel with duties and projects.<p><b>
Responsibilities</b>
</p>• Vouchers through the Accounts Payable and MSSV Systems processing invoices and check requests. • Performs month end balance sheet reconciliations. • Provides customer service to plant personnel, outside Parker divisions and suppliers; investigating issues and answering questions. Directs questions to appropriate resources. • Assist Accounting Team with duties and special projects. • Maintains and enhances Lean Office culture at the Division.<p><b>
Qualifications</b>
</p>Education: High school graduate (minimum). Associates Degree (Preferred) Experience: 1 to 2 years experience in accounts payable function. Computer Skills: • Proficient in Parker Hannifin mainframe computer skills. • Microsoft Office Software – Excel, Word, and PowerPoint. Personal Skills: • Excellent written and oral communication skills. • Must be able to multi-task and prioritize workload. • Must be well organized. • Must be able to work and participate in a team environment.</div>
