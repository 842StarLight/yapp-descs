<p><b>Requirements</b></p>
<p>
POSITION PURPOSE:</p>
<p>
The Director of Product Marketing will report to Chief Marketing Officer. The Director is responsible for providing product line support for marketing program strategy, sales support, and channel support. This position requires tight interaction with sales, customer service, and product management, to ensure that all marketing collateral accurately reflects features &amp; benefits from each user persona &amp; perspective.</p>
<ul>
<li>Making Ntirety solutions and service value propositions real for internal and external (prospects, customers, partners) audiences.</li>
<li>Align with Product Management to define user personas and requirements, and GTM messaging</li>
<li>You will be a master collaborator</li>
<li>Work with the marketing team to help define and drive GTM campaigns to produce sales pipeline</li>
<li>Work with Demand Generation, Content Marketing, Design, and Web Site Content teams to create marketing collateral such as white papers, data sheets, FAQ, feature/benefit sheets, case studies, web site copy, etc., as well as with Field Marketing to support trade shows, events, city tours and promotions.</li>
<li>Work cross-functionally with Sales Operations and Sales Enablement to develop standardized sales scripts, presentations, proposals, demos, etc.</li>
<li>Support channel partners with sales training, collateral, and best practices</li>
<li>Create and provide product positioning materials &amp; training for inside sales teams.</li>
<li>Synthesize a deep understanding and point-of-view on competitor offerings in the space and teach the broader organization how to compete and win</li>
<li>Envision, guide and support the GTM strategy for product launches</li>
<li>Build core foundational pieces including positioning, messaging and other key content</li>
<li>Create, communicate, and drive consensus of marketing strategy with senior leadership, shared services team and partner organizations</li>
</ul>
<p></p>

<p>
DESIRED MINIMUM QUALIFICATIONS:</p>
<ul>
<li>BA/BS Degree in Business, Marketing or related field</li>
<li>10+ year’s Product Marketing experience in Managed Hosting, Cloud Hosting, SaaS or related industry</li>
<li>Experience with compliance (HIPAA, HITECH, PCI-DSS, FedRAMP, etc) a plus</li>
<li>Exceptional knowledge of marketing, sales, and development processes</li>
<li>Ability to travel at least 15% or as required</li>
<li>Strong understanding of the Enterprise IT segment – personas, technology adoption, infrastructure drivers, decision makers, pain points, etc.</li>
<li>Exceptional written and verbal communication skills.</li>
<li>Able to effectively operate in a matrix organization - coordinating people and resources from multiple areas of the company.</li>
<li>Based in Austin, Tx or Denver, CO</li>
</ul>
