<h2>Marketing Operations Program Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader">
<b>New Relic</b><br>
<b>Marketing Operations Program Manager</b><br>
<br>
<b>Your Opportunity</b>
</h2>
<p>
Are you a passionate problem solver? Are you adaptable and thrive in dynamic environments? Then this might be the role and team for you.</p>
<p>
As a Marketing Operations Program Manager, you will work to create global scalable processes that ensure oversight of our marketing programs and account development activities. You will also conduct data analyses that provide insightful conclusions and inform strategic decisions by stakeholders across the company.</p>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
What You'll Do</b></h2>
<ul>
<li>
Executive stakeholder management and collaborative cross-functional alignment with Sales, Alliances &amp; Channels, and GoToMarket Operations leaders</li>
<li>
Connecting broader strategy to day-to-day tasks and vice versa</li>
<li>
Lead, monitor, measure, and analyze the effectiveness of marketing programs, campaigns, activities &amp; initiatives as they relate to company objectives.</li>
<li>
Build and maintain reports on the impact of marketing and SDR activities on the business and provide insights and recommendations for optimization.</li>
<li>
Contact and persona management as it relates to our marketable database.</li>
<li>
Collaborate with other marketing program managers to establish a working model to forecast marketing performance, track the health of the marketing funnel against goals, and spot efficiencies.</li>
<li>
Bridge the gap between the marketing and sales development teams through strong leadership, teamwork, and communication</li>
</ul>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Your Qualification</b></h2>
<ul>
<li>
5+ years in marketing, marketing operations; analytics or similar role with 3+ years of marketing operations experience in software (preferably SaaS companies)</li>
<li>
Confirmed ability to look across multiple systems and multiple data sources to define and optimize success metrics.</li>
<li>
Advanced proficiency and hands-on experience in marketing automation systems (Marketo), CRM (Salesforce) and integrating those systems with other technologies.</li>
<li>
Passionate about marketing operations and interested in demonstrating your extensive experience to help create a best in class marketing technology stack</li>
<li>
Additional nice-to-haves: Experience spent leading analytics strategies and/or developer marketing</li>
<li>
Ability to lead multiple projects at the same time in a fast-paced environment.</li>
<li>
Technically capable, excellent communicator, and a desire to improve processes. Able to explain complex items to various stakeholders and understand projects that have different analytical goals and needs.</li>
<li>
Bachelor’s degree or equivalent experience.</li>
</ul>
<p>
Please note that visa sponsorship is not available for this position.</p>
