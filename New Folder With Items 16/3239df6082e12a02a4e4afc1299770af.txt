<b>POSITION TITLE:</b> Customer Service Representative<br>
<br>
<b>LOCATION: </b>Hoffman Estates, IL<br>
<br>
<b><i>Who We Are:</i></b><br>
<i>Planmeca is one of the largest privately-held dental equipment companies in the industry. Our technology-driven product line includes digital, dental imaging (x-ray) equipment, dental CAD/CAM systems, dental units, and cabinetry. We provide imaging and software solutions for all dental specialties and partner with dental schools, government, universities, and dental service organizations. In the U.S., our products are available through authorized dealers and distribution partnerships.</i><br>
<br>
<b><i>What We Offer:</i></b><br>
<i>We offer a professional work environment, training, growth and advancement. Competitive compensation and a benefits package including a medical, dental, and vision plan, voluntary benefits including life, short-term and long-term disability, 401(k) savings plan with company match, holiday pay.</i><br>
<br>
<b>Come Join our Planmeca USA Team!</b><br>
You are the ideal candidate who has attention to detail, customer service skills, strong phone presence, excellent interpersonal and communication skills. You will act as a single point of contact for most customer interaction and is responsible for providing support for dealers and Planmeca representative and consumers.<br>
<br>
<b>Summary of Duties:
</b><ul>
<li>Omni-channel communication (fax, email, voice, instant messaging, and chatter) about general inquiries with dealers, Planmeca representatives and consumers. Common topics include:
<ul>
<li>pricing, product availability, product information, order status, tracking information, warranties, invoices and credits.</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>Order Management
<ul>
<li>Process orders from dealers, Planmeca sales, and technical representatives and government facilities over all forms of communication</li>
<li>Maintain digital registration for licensing</li>
<li>Salesforce maintenance</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>Account and Document Maintenance
<ul>
<li>Salesforce Maintenance: Enter leads, convert leads to opportunities, update account information, input license codes and product codes, link assets and review promotions</li>
<li>Match paperwork with corresponding purchase orders and submit to Invoicing</li>
<li>Backorder fulfillment</li>
<li>Generate reports upon request</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>Government Duties
<ul>
<li>Biomed training scheduling</li>
<li>Hotel reservations for Biomed groups</li>
<li>Generating Biomed certificates</li>
<li>Generate shipping labels
</li>
<li>Government returns, and quotes for parts and new sales orders</li>
</ul>
</li>
</ul>
<ul><li>All other duties as assigned
</li></ul>
<br>
<b>MINIMUM QUALIFICATIONS:</b><br>
Training and Experience
<ul>
<li>Two years of experience in the customer service area, using a personal computer, with a moderate to heavy amount of inbound/outbound phone activity; or an equivalent combination of training and experience.</li>
<li>Knowledge of company products, services and customers. Working knowledge of computers. Strong phone presence.</li>
<li>Ability to communicate effectively, both verbally and in writing. Ability to answer incoming phone calls for the majority of each day.</li>
</ul>
