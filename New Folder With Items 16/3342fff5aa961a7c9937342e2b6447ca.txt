The Job:</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
The finance org at Tango Card is looking for an Accounts Payable Specialist to join our growing team! The accounts payable specialist is a crucial part of our mission to make rewards easy to send and awesome to receive. To be successful in this role, you will need to have a high degree of accuracy, strong organizational skills, and the ability to work efficiently in a fast-paced company. If this sounds like you, we cant wait to see your application!</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
<b>
Reports to:</b> Financial Controller</div>
</div>
<div><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>As Tango Card’s Accounts Payable (AP) Specialist, you will:</b></h3>
<div><ul>
<li>
Act as the main point of contact for operating accounts payable and Non-Profit payables</li>
<li>
Manage Accounts Payable process, including the entry and coding of invoices across multiple subsidiaries into the ERP system (NetSuite)</li>
<li>
Initiate and process payments for approval via ACH, Wire, and check</li>
<li>
Create, review, and maintain vendor records in NetSuite</li>
<li>
Perform corporate card expense entry and reporting</li>
<li>
Maintain fixed asset register</li>
<li>
Participate in the month end close process, including balance sheet reconciliations and recording journal entries
</li>
<li>Collaborate with FP&amp;A department on special projects</li>
</ul></div>
</div></div>
<div><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Our ideal Accounts Payable (AP) Specialist will have most of the following skills and experiences:</b></h3>
<div><ul>
<li>
2+ years of accounts payable experience or bookkeeping, high volume a plus</li>
<li>
Possess a basic understanding of accounting or bookkeeping</li>
<li>
Strong proficiency in Excel, Gmail, and learning new software systems</li>
<li>
Experience with NetSuite and business online banking a huge plus</li>
<li>
Can challenge existing practices and make suggestions for process improvements</li>
<li>
Strong attention to detail and drive for accuracy
</li>
<li>Ability to balance multiple priorities, meet deadlines, and perform well under pressure</li>
</ul></div>
