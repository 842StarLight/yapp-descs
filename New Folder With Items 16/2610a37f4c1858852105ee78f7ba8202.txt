<h2>Dir, Customer Delivery</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div><div>
<p>IQVIA™ is the leading human data science company focused on helping healthcare clients find unparalleled insights and better solutions for patients. Formed through the merger of IMS Health and Quintiles, IQVIA offers a broad range of solutions that harness the power of healthcare data, domain expertise, transformative technology, and advanced analytics to drive healthcare forward.</p>
<p><i>Essential Functions</i></p>
<p>
Lifecycle Safety Growth:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Contributes to achieving Lifecycle Safety sales, revenue and profitability targets, with a specific emphasis on driving repeat business through enhanced customer focus.</li>
<li>
Provides Lifecycle Safety strategic leadership and customer focus throughout the opportunity chain: from opportunity development, to targeted BD/Sales/Proposals activities, to project design and execution, to contract renewal.</li>
<li>
Provides overall review and analysis of financial results at project, program, account, CDU levels and directs implementation of corrective actions, as necessary.</li>
</ul>
<p>Lifecycle Safety Customer Relations:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Develops and implements customer-specific and service line strategies within own CDU, and collaborates across CDUs, as well as with Lifecycle Safety Service Operations (SO) and Lifecycle Safety Infrastructure on process design and execution.</li>
<li>
Represents Lifecycle Safety, promoting increased visibility by providing LSM interface with internal and external customers at account and CDU level.</li>
<li>
Provides direction for all Lifecycle Safety customer-facing activities on assigned accounts, ensuring customer needs, deliverables and standards are met across service lines, across projects/programs and throughout the product lifecycle.</li>
<li>
Provides leadership structure for customer service. Promotes customer focus, customer alignment and integration across Lifecycle Safety service lines.</li>
<li>
Forms customer-aligned, dedicated teams with cross-functional and subject matter expert s, to provide consistent leadership on accounts, where feasible.</li>
<li>
Implements management strategies to promote delivery of creative and viable solutions for integrated Lifecycle Safety services, moving customer relationships into partnership mode and account management.</li>
</ul>
<p>Lifecycle Safety Customer Deliverables:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Provides oversight and overall direction of project/program management activities.</li>
<li>
Drives Lifecycle Safety project/program performance within assigned Account(s)/CDU with respect to timelines, scope and budget management, standards, process optimization and quality.</li>
<li>
Responsible for demonstrating leadership and ensuring accountability between customer-facing activities and operational service delivery, working cross-functionally and across the opportunity lifecycle.</li>
</ul>
<p>Lifecycle Safety and Corporate Initiatives:</p>
<ul><li>
Participates actively in local and global initiatives, as needed.</li></ul>
<p>Line Management:</p>
<ul><li>
Provides line management for assigned CTM and CDL staff. Performs HR-related staff professional development and administrative activities, including: Goal-setting, performance review and career planning. Salary administration, hiring, termination and transfer. Timesheet, training record, travel request and expense report review.</li></ul>
<p>Performs other duties as assigned.</p>
