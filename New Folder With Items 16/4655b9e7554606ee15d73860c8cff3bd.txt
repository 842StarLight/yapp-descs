<p>Are you interested in leading a team that is building innovative ad experiences for Amazon shoppers?<br>
<br>
Amazon is building a world class advertising business and defining and delivering a collection of self-service performance advertising products that drive discovery and sales of merchandise. Our products are strategically important to our Retail and Marketplace businesses driving long term growth. We deliver billions of ad impressions and millions of clicks daily and are breaking fresh ground to create world-class products. We are highly motivated, collaborative and fun-loving with an entrepreneurial spirit and bias for action. With a broad mandate to experiment and innovate, we are growing at an unprecedented rate with a seemingly endless range of new opportunities.<br>
<br>
We are looking for a strong engineering leader for the Sponsored Products Search Supply team. Sponsored Products helps merchants, retail vendors and brand owners grow incremental sales of their products on Amazon through native product advertising. The Search Supply team works on complex engineering, optimization, econometric, and user-experience problems in order to deliver relevant product ads on Amazon search pages world-wide.<br>
<br>
As the manager of the Search Supply engineering team you will have ownership of critical systems and services at the interface of Amazon's advertising and retail businesses. You will hire and lead a team of full-stack and back-end engineers, design and build high-throughput distributed systems for ad serving and predictive learning models, develop engineering and operational best practices, and collaborate with product, engineering and science leaders to deliver value for Amazon's customers. You will have the opportunity to shape the experience of millions of Amazon customers everyday, and your success will be measured ultimately by your ability to improve their experience with Sponsored Products.</p>
