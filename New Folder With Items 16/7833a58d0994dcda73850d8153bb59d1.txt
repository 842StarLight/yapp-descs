<p><b>DIGITAL MARKETING SPECIALIST</b>, <i>School of Engineering-MIT Professional Education (PE)</i>, to collaborate with colleagues, program director/s, other staff, faculty, clients, and vendors in the delivery of world class professional education programs to companies and individuals. Will work with internal MIT departments and external marketing vendors such as PR, ad agencies, and internal PE programs to increase productive program collaborations. The main responsibilities will include developing targeted digital content and implementing a digital communications strategy, working with media agency to traffic digital ads and messages for conversions, analyzing trends and reports, and managing content for digital platforms.<br>
<br>
MIT Professional Education (PE) offers a suite of education programs and products to serve the continuing educational needs of science and engineering professionals globally.
