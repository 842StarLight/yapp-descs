<div>
<b>What is a Marketing Graphic Designer at Swire Coca-Cola?</b><br>
</div>
<p>
</p>
<div><b>Purpose</b></div>
<p><br>
</p>
<div>
Design a multitude of effective communication and marketing materials<br>
within brand standards for our in-house sales and marketing teams. Reports to Creative Production Manager</div>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
</p>
<div><b>
Essential Job Functions &amp; Skills</b></div>
<p><br>
</p>
<ul>
<li><div>
Designs point of sale (POS) marketing/advertising material for specific activation.<br>
</div></li>
<li><div>
Must follow Coca-Cola branding standards.<br>
</div></li>
<li><div>
Proficient in Adobe Programs (Illustrator, Photoshop, InDesign, Acrobat, etc.).<br>
</div></li>
<li><div>
Working Knowledge of Microsoft Suite Programs. Knowledge of SharePoint is a plus.<br>
</div></li>
<li><div>
A self-motivated and directed attitude and able to handle multiple projects and priorities.<br>
</div></li>
<li><div>
Able to work under tight deadlines and pressure in a fast-paced environment.<br>
</div></li>
<li><div>
Must understand the Printing Process – Digital and offset. Ability to prep artwork for printing.<br>
</div></li>
<li><div>
Collaborate with multiple internal team members to develop concepts and complete assignments on-time. A team player.<br>
</div></li>
<li><div>
Need to be organized and prioritize projects accordingly.<br>
</div></li>
<li><div>
Take ownership of projects from start to finish.<br>
</div></li>
<li><div>
Managing vendors to ensure cost &amp; quality requirements are met (including ensuring<br>
invoices are accurate and processed for payment timely).<br>
</div></li>
<li><div>
Excellent communication skills in design and the ability to partner with product and marketing teams.<br>
</div></li>
<li><div>
Develop and create digital assets for digital ads, menu boards, emails, and websites across multiple platforms, including mobile devices.<br>
</div></li>
<li><div>
Able to use templates and customize for clients.<br>
</div></li>
<li><div>
Perform other duties as assigned.</div></li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<div>
<br>

Skills/Requirements<br>
</div>
<div>
<b>
Qualifications</b><br>
</div>

Associates degree – required / Bachelor's degree preferred
</li>

</li>
