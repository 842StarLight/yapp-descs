<h2>Temporary- Accounting and HR specialist</h2>

<p><b>Position Summary: THREE MONTH ASSIGNMENT – Accounts Payable (AP) and Human Resources (HR) Specialist</b></p>
<p><b>Location: Nashua, NH</b></p>
<p>AICC, a group in Amphenol, is the market leader for high speed, high bandwidth electrical connectors for the Telecom/Datacom market (Mobile Networks, Storage, Servers, Routers, Switches, etc.). Our products help to enable the electronics revolution and remain a key enabler for all the major Tier 1 OEM’s globally. Our global headquarters are located in Nashua, NH and we have design, sales and manufacturing locations globally. We are currently seeking an Accounts Payable and Human Resources Specialist – Payroll and Benefits for a THREE MONTH ASSIGNMENT to join our team. The position will be located in Nashua, NH.</p>
<p>The Accounts Payable and Human Resources Specialist is responsible for employee travel expense processing and auditing, payroll, benefits and Human Resources-related functions on a professional level. They will work closely with managers, supervisors and employees to support U.S. locations of a large division headquartered in Nashua, NH. In addition to payroll and benefits, the role includes some reception, employee records management and other functional responsibilities as needed.</p>
<p><b>RESPONSIBILITIES:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Accounts Payable: Weekly activities related to employee travel expense auditing and processing</li>
<li>Payroll
<ul>
<li>Assist in processing weekly and semi-monthly payroll for 300-350 employees in the United States</li>
<li>Provide payroll reports to Finance</li>
<li>Help transition to new payroll systems – training, implementation, data entry, plus more</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>Benefits
<ul>
<li>Support benefits administration (including leaves) process</li>
<li>Prepare Open Enrollment and Benefit-related communication, including new hire benefit orientation</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>Maintain compliance with federal and state regulations concerning employment: Engage in compliance initiatives like SOX, COSO, FLSA, FMLA, ADA, OSHA other Payroll and Benefit audits, including W-2, 401(K) as needed</li>
<li>Manage employee, payroll and medical/benefit records, and file room</li>
<li>Provide customer service by answering expense, benefits and payroll questions</li>
<li>Data entry and other duties and projects as needed</li>
<li>Office management – managing visitors, answering phones, various administrative duties</li>
</ul>
<p><b>QUALIFICATIONS:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Ability to handle sensitive information and maintain a high degree of confidentiality</li>
<li>Experience in applying standard concepts, practices and procedures of Human Resources – Human Resources or Payroll Certification a plus</li>
<li>Strong IT skills including proficiency in Excel (pivot tables and VLOOKUP), PowerPoint and Outlook</li>
<li>Ultimate Software and Benefit provider software a plus</li>
<li>Detail-oriented with accuracy in data management</li>
<li>Customer-focused, organized, flexible, adaptable, analytical, creative</li>
<li>Able to work with various levels of the organization</li>
<li>Self-directed individual with ability to work individually as well as within a team environment</li>
</ul>
