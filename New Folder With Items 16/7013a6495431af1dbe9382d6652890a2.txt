<h2>Manufacturing Planner 4 -39399</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<ul>
<li>Plans and leads department/organization processes for developing, implementing and maintaining various manufacturing plans, Bill of Materials, work instructions and illustrations to define and document as-built configuration.
</li>
<li>Develops and plans manufacturing concepts and strategies to support business objectives.</li>
<li>Prepares build analyses and cost estimate reports. Leads integration of complex suppliers, processes, materials, data and technology to meet manufacturing and delivery requirements.
</li>
<li>Participates in source selection to ensure manufacturing requirements are met.
</li>
<li>Develops complex tooling requirements based on manufacturing and engineering requirements.
</li>
<li>Manages tooling from initial design to end of use disposition.</li>
<li>Leads investigation and resolution of complex or systemic manufacturing related issues.
</li>
<li>Leads teams performing producibility assessments of design concepts.
</li>
<li>Supports enterprise level benchmarking to incorporate best practices.</li>
<li>Leads development of complex work statements and sequencing of events to support delivery commitments.
</li>
<li>Develops scheduling templates for use by others.
</li>
<li>Drafts, updates and reviews processes and procedures to support business and regulatory agency requirements.
</li>
<li>Develops and documents new job practices, techniques and standards.
</li>
<li>Works under minimal direction.</li>
</ul>
<p>AA degree in related discipline and typically 6 or more years of related experience or an equivalent combination of education and experience.</p>
