Job Details</b></p>
<p>
The Customer Success Rep is responsible for owning and executing a portfolio of accounts in an assigned territory. The rep will lead the deployment of implementation and migration projects for our clients while contributing several key deliverables to the project including an implementation timeline, schedule and overall management of successful job completion. The rep will work with key stakeholders to resolve issues that impede customer and project success. The rep has sufficient latitude to exercise a wide degree of creativity in performing this role and will work to streamline the setup process for new clients coming onboard with ComplySci</p>
