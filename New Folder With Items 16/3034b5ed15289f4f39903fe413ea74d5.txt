<h2>CME Shipping and Receiving Worker</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<div><div>
<div>
<div>Buffalo Rock is an award-winning Pepsi-Cola Bottler founded in Birmingham, AL, more than 100 years ago. The CME Tech Shop Worker is a key member of the Corporate Marketing Equipment Team responsible for performing many general functions in order to assist and support the efficient operations of the warehouse.</div>
</div>

<br>
</div></div>

</div>
<br>
</div></div>

<div><div>
<div>
<div><b>Essential Functions Statement(s)</b></div>
</div>
<br>
<div><div><div><div>
<div>
<li> <div>When orders arrive, required to unload shipment via forklift and or pallet jack.</div>
</div>
<br>
<div>
<li> <div>When shipping, required to hand stack some special equipment, lifting up to 50 pounds maximum.</div>
</div>
<br>
<div>
<li> <div>Required to band and wrap shipment utilizing bander and wrapper.</div>
</div>
<br>
<div>
<li> <div>Required to complete other activities assigned by Supervisor.</div>
</div>
<br>
<div>
<li> <div>Resonsible for wearing all assigned safety equipment and observing safety rules.</div>
</div>
<br>
<div>
<li> <div>Must follow safety guidelines required by Management.</div>
</div>
<br>
<div>
<li> <div>Assist in general housekeeping functions within the warehouse and grounds.</div>
</div>
<br>
<div>
<li> <div>Must be proficient with Microsoft Office programs.</div>
</div>
<br>
<div>
<li> <div>Required to process equipment orders received from the Divisions by getting approval and prepare to be sent out.</div>
</div>
<br>
<div>
<li> <div>Must receive in used equipment from Divisions to be sorted for remanufacturing or sale.</div>
</div>
<br>
<div>
<li> <div>Once orders have been pulled, orders must be processed through computer software to be sent to the Divisions.</div>
</div>
<br>
<div>
<li> <div>Required to complete and process shipping orders/load tickets, truck load forms and WMS reports for equipment being sent.</div>
</div>
<br>
<div>
<li> <div>Daily communication with divisions and technicians on orders, back orders and out of stock items.</div>
</div>
<br>
<div>
<li> <div>Required to order equipment when necessary; keep up with and update drop ship equipment orders to the divisions by drop ship date from MFG's and divisions drop ship forms from the Divisions.</div>
</div>
<br>
<div>
<li> <div>Required to maintain inventory functions: adding new equipment, remanufactured equipment and internal refurbished equipment, correctly issuing equipment out to divisions, maintain new and remanufactured equipment inventory counts accurately on a daily basis.</div>
</div>
<br>
<div>
<li> <div>Required to maintain and file all records / invoices pertaining to CME functions: receiving, shipping, equipment inventories and Purchase Orders from all divisions and manufactures.</div>
</div>
<br>
<div>
<li> <div>Required to carry out other duties as assigned by management such as but not limited to; sweeping, mopping, banding, emptying trash, recycling cardboard/metal, cleaning offices, working outside of your immediate department etc.</div>
</div>
<br>
<div>
<li> <div>Responsible for continuous self-improvement through the knowledge and use of all Company and Division provided training i.e. interactive web-based training, facilitated group training, video-on-demand, on-the-job training, individual or group coaching, or any other company provided method.</div>
</div>
