<h2>Account Executive</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p><b>Position Summary
</b></p>
<p></p>
<p>Univision Local Media Houston is looking for Stellar Account Executive to join our team. This is a great career opportunity for experienced, dynamic individuals with an entrepreneurial spirit to join our local sales team. These are outside sales positions selling to new clients and servicing established accounts. Candidates must understand the importance of successful cold calling, goal-setting, and time management; and be well versed on different buyer types and their styles.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Job Responsibilities
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Develop strong executive level client and agency relationships and sell Univision’s various platforms
</li>
<li>Develop client-specific pitches, attend client meetings, make presentations and close sales
</li>
<li>Handle an active list and maintain and grow existing and new accounts
</li>
<li>Prospect, negotiate, and generate new leads, as well as qualify leads generated by support functions
</li>
<li>Strong communication and collaboration skills internally and externally
</li>
<li>Keep management apprised of activities going on within accounts
</li>
<li>Attend promotional events and other client networking events
</li>
<li>Work in partnership with support team on pre-sale/post-sale processes
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Required Skills and Experience
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Strategic, motivated, enthusiastic self-starter who can work effectively in a dynamic sales environment
</li>
<li>Ability to develop customized marketing solutions for clients, possess exceptional presentation skills
</li>
<li>Proven track record of success in media sales at agencies and on client side
</li>
<li>Client skills including prospecting, conveying value proposition and overcoming objections
</li>
<li>Must possess a good driving record and a valid driver’s license
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Desired Skills and Experience
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Ability to collaborate/communicate effectively with relevant departments at Univision
</li>
<li>Proficient in MS Office, Matrix, and Wide Orbit Traffic
</li>
<li>Bachelor's degree in business and/or marketing or previous advertising/media sales experience
</li>
<li>Tangible experience in related media sales (broadcast &amp; digital
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Eligibility Requirements
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Must be willing to work from office in Houston, Texas
</li>
<li>Employment/education will be verified
</li>
<li>Must have unrestricted authorization to work in the United States
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Physical Requirements
</b></p>
<ul><li>Ability to stand, walk, bend, type, and sit for up to (8) hours
</li></ul>
