Your day to day...
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Partner with Sales leaders to define, implement and lead a successful sales training program</li>
<li>Develop content and strategy for the on-boarding and continuous training sessions</li>
<li>Onboard, train, and develop sales team members to help meet Instacart's growing sales team size and goals</li>
<li>Help with defining and measuring results of the program and success metrics of the sales team (i.e. time to productivity)</li>
<li>Identify and partner with cross-functional teams to execute various sales enablement tools</li>
</ul>
<b>We rely on our values to guide our decisions:</b> Solve for the Customer, Every Minute Counts, This is Your Baby, Go Far, Together and Of Course, But Maybe. What does this mean for your role?
<br><br>
You have...
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>College degree</li>
<li>3-4 years work experience</li>
<li>Sales team training experience within a SaaS or technology company</li>
<li>Experience presenting to large groups of sales people</li>
<li>Experienced in Google Slides, Sheets, Docs, facilitating webinars / video</li>
</ul>
