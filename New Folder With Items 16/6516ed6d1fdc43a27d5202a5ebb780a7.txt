<div>
The Business Systems &amp; Solutions Analyst Supply Chain &amp; Operations position is responsible for developing and sustaining partnerships with the business, providing counsel on all Information Technology related issues and initiatives, service level management of applications and systems, design and implement architecture to ensure expansion and future capabilities, maintains local voice and data infrastructures, leads the execution of project deployment, user training and adoption for systems, and develops and implements process improvements. This partnership role with the business is key to enabling flexible and agile business capabilities in an end to end solution for the functional area they are responsible for. Key areas such as commercial and sales, supply chain, operations, human resources, finance, etc. This role will be responsible for optimizing and adapting the end to end solutions in partnership with the business to provide the capabilities needed to grow revenue, increase operational efficiencies, and become a flexible data driven enterprise.<br>
</div>
