<h2>Marketing & Communications Coordinator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>The Michael Kaim Team is looking for an innovative and experienced marketing coordinator to help a successful real estate team grow their brand. This position is responsible for assisting in the planning and implementation of sales and marketing campaigns, both short and long term, utilizing technology and innovation to gain a competitive advantage for future growth. In addition, working to achieve consistent and effective branding across all sales functions, will be a priority.</p>
<p>Job Duties:</p>
<p>Evaluating and developing our marketing strategy and marketing plan from start to finish</p>
<p>Communicating the marketing plan</p>
<p>Tracking and measuring results of paid ads campaigns, including social media ads</p>
<p>Developing and managing advertising campaigns</p>
<p>Building brand awareness and positioning</p>
<p>Supporting sales and lead generation efforts</p>
<p>Handling social media, public relation efforts, and content marketing</p>
<p>Advanced copywriting</p>
<p>Test and implement new technologies to streamline and condense daily workload</p>
<p>Requirements:</p>
<p>A team player who enjoys working with others, but, can also work alone to complete projects</p>
<p>Outstanding communication skills, both verbal and written</p>
<p>Marketing experience preferred</p>
<p>Real estate knowledge or background helpful</p>
<p>Strong organizational and time management skills</p>
<p>Ability to use data to make informed decisions</p>
<p>Graphic design experience a plus</p>
