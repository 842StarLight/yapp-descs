<p><b>
Position Summary</b></p>
<p>
ALL Crane Rental of Georgia, Inc. is seeking a certified Crane Operator for our rental taxi-fleet. Prefer local candidates or those willing to relocate to the area. This is a full-time, non-exempt position.</p>
<p><b>
Essential Functions</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Must be able to safely operate complex heavy lift equipment</li>
<li>
Must be able to read lift plans, load charts and operate a personal computer</li>
<li>
Maintain conformity to safety requirements and other regulations</li>
<li>
Prepare proper documentation of actions taken</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
 </b><b>Skills and Experience Requirements</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Must have an active certification from an accredited crane operator testing organization which meets OSHA criteria</li>
<li>
Must have a Class-A or Class-B CDL with an acceptable driving record</li>
<li>
Experience with rigging is preferred</li>
<li>
Experience in crane set-up and tear-down a definite plus</li>
<li>
Strong initiative required; ability to work independently with minimal direct supervision</li>
<li>
Ability to work safely around moving machinery</li>
<li>
Able to work in conditions with marked changes in temperature &amp; humidity</li>
</ul>
