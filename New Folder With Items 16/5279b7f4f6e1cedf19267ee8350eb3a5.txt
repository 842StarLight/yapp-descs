<p><b>Do you want to play a role in global commerce?
</b></p>
<p>As a Property Manager, you would be part of a team that strives to provide best in class service to customers in our 33 million square feet of industrial buildings in the Los Angeles Market. Our customer base is diverse and we’re looking for someone who enjoys working with both the biggest names in retail logistics and the smaller shop operations.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>What’s needed in order to do that:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>The ability to adapt to change and willingness to take on new company initiatives with the most positive attitude.
</li>
<li>Dedication to delivering exceptional customer service through proactive and responsive service.
</li>
<li>Proficient lease analysis and lease administration skills.
</li>
<li>Highly developed negotiation and management skills to ensure safe/well maintained properties through competitively bid service agreements/capital projects and work oversight.
</li>
<li>Strong financial reporting background to insure budget and financial goals are met for the portfolio.
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Tell us if you’re ready.
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Minimum 3 years industrial/commercial real estate experience required.
</li>
<li>Works well in a team environment.
</li>
<li>You’re in possession of a CA Real Estate sales license or have the goal to obtain a license within first 6 months in role.
</li>
<li>You have a valid CA driver’s license with ability to travel to multiple properties within given portfolio.
</li>
<li>You’ve experienced property transitions, portfolio acquisitions or company mergers.
</li>
<li>You’re able to describe your experience participating in budgeting cycles and CAM reconciliation processes.
</li>
<li>You’re able to tell us about new initiatives, campaigns or system upgrades that you’ve experienced in other positions and what you liked or disliked about them, how you navigated the change, etc.
</li>
<li>You’re able to multi-task; prioritize and work with minimal supervision, have strong attention to detail and excellent time management skills to ensure all established deadlines are met.
</li>
<li>You are skilled in the use of the internet, spreadsheets, relational databases, and word processing software. Ability to process computer data and to format and generate reports. Ability to create, compose, and edit written materials.
</li>
</ul>
