<h2>Accounts Receivable Collection & Support Specialist, AJC</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>This position, located in Atlanta, Georgia, as a member of the Atlanta Journal Constitution Accounts Receivable team, the Collections Specialist will be responsible for a broad range of collection activities relating to all CMG Accounts Receivable. The employee will proactively manage assigned customer accounts to maximize timely payments from our customers. This position requires the ability to prioritize, problem solve, and multitask. The employee will work closely with both internal and external customers to identify and resolve collection issues and share information regarding payer trends. The position will also be responsible for managing customer credit status in a manner that maximizes advertising revenue while minimizing bad debt risk.<div><ul>
<li>
Collection of outstanding accounts receivables, primarily through email and phone communication</li>
<li>
Minimize Days Sales Outstanding (DSO) and Bad Debt Write-offs, while helping to maintain positive client relationships through a customer-friendly collections approach</li>
<li>
Track and maintain customer accounts through the collection process, ensuring prompt payment of balances due to The Atlanta Journal Constitution.</li>
<li>
Review aging reports and reconcile accounts as needed</li>
<li>
Research and resolve customer payment deductions and discrepancies in a timely manner</li>
<li>
Establish and maintain effective and cooperative working relationships with Account Executives</li>
<li>
Communicate with internal staff regarding customer payments and any issues that might prevent a timely resolution</li>
<li>
Prepare credit adjustments as need to resolve billing discrepancies</li>
<li>
Recommend accounts for third-party collections</li>
<li>
Meet defined department and individual goals</li>
<li>
Prepare monthly and periodic reports as needed</li>
<li>
Follow up frequently regarding payment commitments</li>
<li>
Maintain collection notes in accounts receivable system</li>
<li>
Participate in process improvement projects and support other management directives as prescribed.</li>
</ul></div>
