<h2>Senior Accountant, Tax</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Stephano Slack LLC is a mid-sized public accounting firm with offices in Wayne, PA and Wilmington, DE. The firm is a full-service CPA firm providing audit and attest services, tax and consulting services, business valuation and small business advisory services for clients of all entity types and sizes stretching across a broad range of industries.</p>
<p>
Established in 2004, Stephano Slack has experienced explosive growth in the last few years. This growth is expected to continue into the foreseeable future and will provide plenty of opportunity for advancement within the firm.</p>
<p>
Stephano Slack is not the average accounting firm, so we’re not looking for the average individual to join our team! The firm is looking for a candidate that understands the difference between work and passion, between a job and a career.</p>
<p>
We currently have openings for a full-time CPA and/or future CPA as a Senior Accountant for our Tax Department in the Wayne office of our growing firm:</p>
<p><b>
Staff Accountant Job Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Preparation of:
<ul>
<li>Corporate, partnership, individual, trust, and not-for-profit tax returns</li>
<li>Tax workpapers for accrual-to-cash conversions, book-to-tax differences and tax provisions</li>
<li>Tax projections for individual and business clients</li>
<li>Correspondences/responses to tax notices and tax audit assistance</li>
<li>Sales and use tax filings, research, and compliance</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>Completion of ad hoc projects and memorandums as requested</li>
<li>Professionally communicate with clients and build positive, sustainable client relationships</li>
<li>Assign work to Staff, ensuring work is completed in a timely and thorough manner</li>
<li>Review tax returns prepared by Staff, providing guidance and development opportunities</li>
<li>Prepare tax projections for individual and business clients</li>
<li>Participate in business development initiatives and activities</li>
<li>Perform tax research</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Desired Skills &amp; Experience</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor's or Master’s degree in accounting and in-progress CPA certification, completed certification preferred</li>
<li>3 – 5 years of experience in public or private accounting experience</li>
<li>Passionate about a career in public accounting and committed to ongoing professional growth</li>
<li>High attention to detail and follow through with excellent organizational skills</li>
<li>Ability to switch from proactive, independent work to collaborative, integrated team member as the task or project demands</li>
<li>Proficient in time management with an emphasis on the importance of meeting deadlines while managing multiple projects and clients</li>
<li>Strong communication and analytical skills</li>
<li>Flexibility to adapt to firm- and client- specific practices</li>
<li>Knowledge of account principles prescribed by the Internal Revenue Code</li>
<li>Familiarity with Microsoft applications (Outlook, Excel, Word) and experience with industry software such as Prosystems fx Engagement, Prosystems fx Tax Preparation, and CCH Intelliconnect preferred</li>
</ul>
