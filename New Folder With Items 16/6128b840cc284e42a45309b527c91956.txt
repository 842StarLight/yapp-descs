<p><b>Who we are?</b></p>
With 90,000 people on five continents, SUEZ is a world leader in smart and sustainable resource management. We provide water and waste management solutions that enable cities and industries optimize their resource management and strengthen their environmental and economic performances.<br>
SUEZ Water Technologies &amp; Solutions looks for innovation everywhere. For more than 100 years, SUEZ has been at the forefront of innovation, from the SUEZ Canal to addressing water scarcity. Finding solutions to the world's biggest problems has never been more important than right now. Join us today and become an essential part of the Resource Revolution!

<p><b>What will you be doing?</b></p>
<div>The Account Manager will demonstrate leadership in communicating business goals, programs, and processes for an area or business segment. This individual will utilize experience or expertise to solve problems, develop and execute objectives for self and others, and have the ability to effect short-term and some long- term business goals. In addition, you will:</div>
<p></p>
<br>
<ul>
<li> Be primarily focused on selling water processing solutions and products to expand and maintain customer accounts</li>
<li> Actively listen to customers’ needs and provide effective technical service to resolve issues and inquiries</li>
<li> Drive growth by continuously creating and selling customer value propositions for assigned account base that focuses on our competitive advantage while leveraging our broad product portfolio</li>
<li> Participate in collecting and testing water samples on site and analyzes results to provide recommendations to improve quality of treatment</li>
<li> Serve as a knowledge resources to customers using WTS technology to solve identified water treatment needs</li>
<li> Partner cross-functionally within organization to optimize our solutions offering</li>
<li> Develop a plan to effectively meet sales targets by meeting or exceeding key metrics for revenue, account retention, profitability</li>
<li> Learn and maintain awareness of all safety and environmental practice</li>
<li> Use and maintain to company standards an assigned fleet vehicle to perform the duties and responsibilities of the job</li>
</ul>
