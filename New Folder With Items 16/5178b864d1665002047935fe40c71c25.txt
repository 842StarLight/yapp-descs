<p><b>
Function Overview</b></p>
<p>
Whirlpool is seeking qualified candidate for the role of <b>Senior Analyst Electrical Engineering-System Intergration</b>, to join our team in Benton Harbor, MI. Whirlpool’s goal is to have all appliances perform flawlessly. This function will enable the service organization by providing innovative diagnostic tools and solutions for control systems. This function will collaborate with hardware, software, controls/wiring and architecture/system integration team to the delivery of the service tool/module, diagnostic scripts. Work with the Quality and service team to deploy on the field.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Job Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Define an execution schedule in sync with project delivery milestones</li>
<li>
Plan technical reviews with relevant technical teams for service module, key, Maintenance of Business (MoB), application as well as integration testing</li>
<li>
Provide technical inputs in the RFP/RFQ ( request for proposal/request for quotation ) process and make sure the right partners are selected</li>
<li>
Work with IT, system integration and relevant functions to define release plans for the individual module as well as database release on MoB (Maintenance of Business) application.develop and maintain Whirlpool Service and Diagnostic Tool and relevant requirements and continued support</li>
<li>
Define diagnostic script functions which include firmware updates/ board flashings</li>
<li>
Drive New Product Development ( NPD) and Maintenance of Business (MOB) projects ensuring that the existing tool base is technically supported</li>
<li>
Work with external partners to develop mobile app/wireframe and contents</li>
<li>
Participate in technical design reviews for projects and providing an assessment of project timeline through partnerships with the key development team members (e.g. Hardware, Software, Service, Quality, Testing, etc.) and by consulting with key architecture development team members when necessary</li>
<li>
Execute the development and testing of the service diagnostic tool hardware and software</li>
<li>
Execute qualification tests (internal or external) so units can be leveraged by the WHR Global Service Organization</li>
<li>
Track milestones and execute/deploy the full product solution in the field</li>
<li>
Execute Legal audit and review mobile app contents and wireframes</li>
<li>
Define harness kits for the selected program with wiring team</li>
<li>
Collaborate with cross-functional teams and drive a high level of transparency and trust by keeping a cadence of global reviews</li>
<li>
Make sure all regional service needs are presented for consideration in annual planning with funding and allocation specified</li>
<li>
Assist service organization in full solution deployment and adoption</li>
</ul>
