<h2>Account Executive</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>About Arreva: </b></p>
<p>Leading uniquely at the intersection point of technology and social good, Arreva provides software, services, and expertise, that empowers and connects people to advance the social good movement. We serve the entire social good community, which includes nonprofits, foundations, corporations, education institutions, and the individual change agents who support them. We work with thousands of organizations, helping them realize their goals, fund their missions, manage their operations, and develop long-lasting supporter relationships. Our customers are passionate about making the world a better place, and we’re inspired by the opportunity to help them.</p>
<p>We are currently recruiting high-energy, driven Account Executives with relevant experience in Software or Solution sales, value-driven sales approach. The Account Executive is responsible for selling Arreva’s Online Fundraising and Donor Relationship Management Cloud Solutions for all focused sectors in the nonprofit marketplace, you will execute a defined strategy with metric-driven processes to achieve sales goals. The ideal candidate excels in outbound prospecting and sales. They have a successful record of past sales over-achievement, and be willing to focus on the nonprofit marketplace. The nonprofit market is a highly competitive, fast-moving, and underserved market with significant growth potential.</p>
<p><b>Your Impact</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Learn Arreva's products, platform, and value proposition</li>
<li>Work in partnership with our team of Sales Development Representatives to advance sales leads and sales opportunities</li>
<li>Drive a high volume of sales by managing the complete sales process</li>
<li>Up-selling and leveraging business from new and established customer relationships</li>
<li>Strengthen client relationships through regular engagement</li>
<li>Managing industry events and user groups to generate market interest</li>
<li>Build a sales plan to meet and exceed sales targets</li>
</ul>
