<h2>Global E-Commerce Director</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">We are looking for a Global E-Commerce Director to join our Global Marketing team that supports the Industrial Group here at Fluke.
<br><br>
<b>Responsibilities:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Own the global e-commerce P&amp;L and related revenue, margin and NPS KPI’s for IG and other Fluke BU’s in each region as they launch and scale e-commerce.</li>
<li>Act as the overall e-commerce project leader. Serve as a liaison and primary point of contact within the e-commerce organization for cross functional partners acting as a project manager to evaluate, prioritize, develop, manage, implement and test e-commerce initiatives.</li>
<li>Partner with key cross-functional teams to ensure all relevant organizational goals and implications are factored into e-commerce projects.</li>
<li>Responsible for collaborating with cross-functional partners to manage all aspects of the e-commerce business including, business planning, content strategy and development, promotional campaigns and other online marketing, website design, customer service, web analytics and web technologies.</li>
<li>Participate in the development and execution of strategic and tactical business plans with other members of the cross functional team supporting the e-commerce strategy, including Operations, BU’s, Regional Sales, Service &amp; Support, Legal, Compliance, Tax, etc.</li>
<li>Manage all aspects of web analytics related to e-commerce; communicate relevant information to team members, executive leadership and cross-functional partners.</li>
<li>Build and direct a cohesive team of internal and external personnel to effectively develop e-commerce solutions, including CX before, during and after purchase.</li>
<li>Work with the Digital team and other stakeholders to optimize the user experience of the website including site navigation, content development, checkout funnel and promotional campaigns. Oversee conversion performance optimization.</li>
<li>Develop and oversee the effective planning, QA and execution of content across the websites and online marketing campaigns to ensure efficiency, accuracy and timeliness of all web content publishing.</li>
<li>Generate and implement new sales and marketing initiatives to increase e-commerce sales and profitability in alignment with KPI’s</li>
<li>Provide expertise on current e-commerce industry best practices. Consult on cross-functional projects to ensure e-commerce principles are incorporated.</li>
<li>Develop the e-commerce channel strategy. Identify new areas of e-commerce opportunity beyond current organizational thinking</li>
<li>Outstanding analytical skills, strong experience interpreting test results &amp; drawing conclusions.</li>
<li>Demonstrated strong business judgment and decision-making skills; ability to identify, prioritize, and articulate highest impact initiatives</li>
</ul>
KPI
<br><ul>
<li>E-commerce revenue, margin and NPS</li>
<li>Selected cross-functional KPI’s that enable e-commerce success</li>
</ul>
<b>Qualifications:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor Degree Required, MBA preferred</li>
<li>10-15 years experience in commercial leadership roles or global business-to-business marketing, ideally in a matrix organization</li>
<li>Strong e-commerce and/or digital marketing experience</li>
<li>Excellent written and verbal communication skills</li>
<li>Strong analytic capability</li>
<li>Flexibility and strength in dealing with tight deadlines and rapidly changing environment</li>
<li>Ability to develop digital talent</li>
</ul>
