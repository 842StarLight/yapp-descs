<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Description</b></h3>
<p></p>
<p><b>Your Career</b></p>
<p>
Palo Alto Networks is looking for an experienced enterprise support delivery manager. You will be responsible to enable customer success with our award-winning network security products. In this high growth business, scaling, optimization, dynamic spirit and sustained performance are the essentials. The Support Delivery team is a high-performance group of engineers supporting business critical customer environments. You will be responsible for the front line support delivery team and is expected to implement and optimize performance that will keep Palo Alto Networks at the forefront in the industry.</p>
<p><b>
Your Impact</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Lead daily operations of a team of Technical Support Engineers in line with the organizational goals and objectives</li>
<li>
Provide customer support as a strategic differentiator for Palo Alto Networks, helping drive customer loyalty, reference ability and repeat business</li>
<li>
Ability to achieve the customer satisfaction goals, which is the single most important measurement of the support organization, and the best way to measure our ability to achieve our mission to “Make our customers successful!”</li>
<li>
Ensure that engineers have the resources and processes necessary to ensure success and performance through release cycles</li>
<li>
Mature and experienced guidance and direction to the team to deliver on goals and objectives and maximize engineer opportunity to exceed targets and earn rewards and recognition</li>
<li>
Provide appropriate technical and soft skills training and mentoring</li>
<li>
Ensure training is effectively addressing our goal to create a learning environment and maximizing customer satisfaction levels</li>
<li>
Create consistent onboarding training programs are used and delivered effectively</li>
<li>
Propagate Goals &amp; Objectives: Set team goals in-line with overall organizational goals. Set specific goals for direct reports</li>
<li>
Perform annual reviews of all direct reports; set quarterly goals and provide regular feedback on performance with respect to agreed upon goals</li>
<li>
Customer Escalation Management, triage, prioritize issues, negotiate customer priorities and manage expectations</li>
<li>
Build strong relationships with sales, customers, and partners</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Your Experience</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
BA/BS in computer science or equivalent (MBA a plus)</li>
<li>
10+ years of experience in enterprise support and service delivery preferred</li>
<li>
Prior management experience</li>
<li>
Technical experience in the Network Security Industry</li>
<li>
Experience as a network administrator (or equivalent) in an NT or Unix environment</li>
<li>
Knowledge of Network Security (Routers, Switches, firewalls, VPN routers - Checkpoint, Juniper (Netscreen), Fortinet, Cisco)</li>
<li>
Background in internetworking, LAN, and WAN technologies</li>
<li>
Background in debugging broad, complex, and unique networks with mixed media and protocols</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
The Team</b></p>
<p>
Our technical support team is critical to our success and mission. As part of this team, you enable customer success by providing support to clients after they have purchased our products. Our dedication to our customers doesn’t stop once they sign – it evolves. As threats and technology change, we stay in step to accomplish our mission.<br>
<br>
You’ll be involved in implementing new products, transitioning from old products to new, and will fix integrations and critical issues as they are raised – in fact, you’ll seek them out to ensure our clients are safely supported. We fix and identify technical problems, with a pointed focus of providing the best customer support in the industry.</p>

Our Commitment</b></p>
