<h2>Marketing Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Overview
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div>The <b>Marketing Manager</b> will work closely with the Marketing Director in creating and <b>executing complex marketing strategies</b> for the <b>Max Lucado and Lysa TerKeurst publishing brands, specifically their front and backlist non-fiction trade books</b>. The candidate should be well-versed and able to develop and implement book marketing best practices online, in-store, and at events. Additionally, the candidate should possess strong writing skills as they will be required to create marketing copy both independently and collaboratively. The candidate will bring innovation and thought leadership to the Special Imprints team’s overall work, resulting in robust marketing initiatives that ultimately elevate the brands and increase company revenue. This role requires a high level of attention to detail, organization, and strong communication skills. Editorial background a plus.</div>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<ul>
<li>Individually and collaboratively develops and implements marketing and publicity campaigns for assigned titles and brands.
</li>
<li>Individually and collaboratively develops messaging, themes and tangible product selling hooks for new and backlist products as well as for seasonal and thematic campaigns.</li>
<li>
Presents or co-presents marketing plans at key meetings with authors, agents, sales teams, and company executives.</li>
<li>
Works with team to write/compile book cover, flap, and advertising copy.</li>
<li>
Creates and manages social media campaigns or works with third-party or author to ensure execution.</li>
<li>
Responsible for the execution of print, online, radio, and television advertising according to marketing plan.</li>
<li>
Provides creative direction for in-house designers and secures and manages freelance designers for creation of ads, websites, and in-store merchandise.</li>
<li>
Manages relationship with outside publicists secured for Lucado and Special Imprint titles.</li>
<li>
Manages and/or coordinates editorial schedule as assigned
</li>
<li>Creates compelling pre-sale and post-sale promotional strategies, including eBook and audio channels.
</li>
<li>Leverages best practices in digital (social media, search, email, etc.) and traditional (advertising, publicity, events) marketing methods to maximize unit sales and revenue.</li>
<li>
Expands efforts in reaching internal mailing lists through sales data analysis and research of current market trends.</li>
<li>
Develops catalog pages for all titles and manage catalog process.</li>
</ul>
</div></div></div>
