<h2>Senior Analyst - Risk Management</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p><b>What you’ll be doing...</b>
</p>
<p>The National Compliance Team is responsible for overseeing Verizon Consumer policies, processes, people and systems and partnering with business leaders to identify and mitigate financial, operational, legal, regulatory and reputational risks facing the business unit.
</p>
<p>The Senior Analyst of Risk Management is responsible for Non Sales Compliance (including Service, Network &amp; Technology, ISO and Marketing Operations) assesses and mitigates risks and ensures proper internal controls exist and are functioning as intended across various business functions.
</p>
<ul>
<li>Drive and execute an integrated plan to identify control gaps, define and implement process and technical solutions.
</li>
<li>Work independently to perform in-depth analyses of business risks utilizing reporting and analytic tools provided by the Business Intelligence team. Identify and interpret root-cause issues to recommend and develop action plans for risk mitigation.</li>
<li>
Lead compliance reviews exercising discretion and independent judgment in the Global Technology space.
</li>
<li>Identify risks inherent in the department and process and make decisions on the scope of work to be performed based on those risks. Perform data analysis and coordinate fieldwork and responses.
</li>
<li>Develop unique work plans, review of formal policy documents, and manage the implementation and completion of process improvements.
</li>
<li>Identify activities requiring investigation; Lead confidential, independent investigations of suspected fraud involving employees, agents and customers.
</li>
<li>Work with HR, Corporate Security as necessary when conducting these investigations, ensure proper handoff for interviews.</li>
<li>
Partner with department management to identify and resolve policy and process issues, perform analysis and draw conclusions directly related to policy or business operations.</li>
<li>
Provide active involvement as a subject matter expert in national and cross-functional initiatives/teams.
</li>
<li>Exercise authority to formulate, affect, interpret and implement policy to ensure a strong control environment.
</li>
<li>Develop action plan based on results.</li>
<li>
Utilize professional expertise and judgment to evaluate, develop, and approve proposed enhancements to all Verizon Wireless systems ensuring proper functionality and controls.</li>
<li>
Partner with cross-functional teams nationally to design system enhancements and troubleshoot technical issues.</li>
</ul>
