<h2>Sr. Data and Qlik Analyst</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Job Title</b></h3>
Sr. Data and Qlik Analyst<p></p>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Job Description</b></h3>
<p>
We welcome you to join Philips as a Data &amp; Reporting Analyst reporting to the Sr. Manager, Sales Operations – Image Guided Therapy Devices. You will be a part of IGT-D’s growing operations team. Within this role you will interface with sales leaders, marketing, IT, and other business stakeholders across Philips North America.</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Your Responsibilities</b></p>
<p>
In this role, you will tackle complex challenges and solve them through process improvement and analytics. You will deliver innovative insights by merging multiple data sets, performing advanced analyses, and creating compelling data visualization within BI platforms. To ensure 100% accuracy, application of rigorous quality control and process will need to be applied to your day to day activities. . The right candidate will enjoy working with multiple teams to connect business strategy/analytics, and will leverage their in-depth knowledge of business processes and analysis to become a trusted advisor to the business.</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>
We are looking for</b>:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Project Management, including experience with Scrum Agile development</li>
<li>
Business Strategy expertise with a proven ability to distill complex analyses into clear business implications</li>
<li>
Commercial Data Management with the ability to manipulate and integrate databases with SQL, SAS, or other language. The right candidate will also have familiarity with commercial data lakes and backend integration into analytical and reporting platforms such as SFDC, Anaplan, etc.</li>
<li>
Process Optimization and Quality with experience automating processes and quality control VBA, R/Python, SAS, etc.</li>
<li>
Analytical experience with a background in building robust analytical models, including forecasts, customer segmentation, etc. Critical thinking skills, including the ability to assess numbers, trends, and data in order to reach new conclusions based on findings</li>
<li>
Reporting and Communication experience up to senior leadership with a background in front and back-end development of Tableau, QLIK, or other BI tools</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>
To succeed in this role, you will need:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Analytical Bachelor’s degree such as engineering, finance, computer science, etc. and 6+ years of experience in analytics function such as sales operations, finance, IT, etc., or Advanced Analytical Degree (e.g. finance, statistics, engineering, etc.) and 4+ years of experience in analytical role</li>
<li>
6+ years in analytical role such as sales operations, IT, marketing, etc.</li>
<li>
2+ years of experience front-end development in BI platform such as QLIK</li>
<li>
2+ years of experience back-end development in BI platform such as QLIK</li>
<li>
2+ years of experience with IT project management</li>
<li>
Strong quantitative skills, including statistical analysis, process design, &amp; data management</li>
<li>
Organization / Time management - Ability to handle multiple tasks simultaneously</li>
<li>
Experience managing, maintaining, and enhancing commercial data systems</li>
<li>
Experience with sales alignment, business intelligence, data visualization, and CRM tools, such as Salesforce.com, Cognos BI, Tableau, Varicent ICM</li>
<li>
Proficiency with Excel macros, and the potential to use advanced decision support analytical tools (e.g., decision analysis software, Monte Carlo simulation, optimization)</li>
<li>
Expertise in QLIK, Tableau, or other BI Platform</li>
<li>
Proficiency in SQL</li>
<li>
Proficiency in Python, R, or other statistics platform</li>
<li>
Ability to navigate a complex matrix environment and manage competing priorities</li>
<li>
Ability to successfully work on cross functional projects/teams</li>
<li>
Prior experience in the Medical Devices industry</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>
In return, we offer you</b></p>
<p>
A path towards your most rewarding career. Succeeding in this market-based role in a complex environment will open many doors for your long-term career, inside and outside of Philips. We also believe that we are at our best as a company when you are at yours as a person. Thus, we offer competitive health benefits, a flexible work schedule and access to local well-being focused activities.</p>
