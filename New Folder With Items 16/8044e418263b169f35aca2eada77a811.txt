<h2>Business Manager, Biomedical Engineering/LORRE</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div><div><div>
<div>
<b>Job Summary</b>
</div>
<div>
<div>Direct the management of finances and business processes for Biomedical Engineering and Laboratory of Renewable Resources Engineering (LORRE) with mid-range level expenditures/revenues annually. Responsible for the management and administration of the business functions and act as the resource/liaison among the School College and central offices. Provide proper interpretation of University, federal and sponsoring agency policies and procedures. Work closely with central offices and other business entities in meeting the strategic goals of the University. Maintain a unit that provides excellent business management support to Department Heads, Directors, faculty, staff and students. Coach and review the work of lower level professionals.</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div><b>Required:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor's degree in Business Administration, Management, Finance or related field of study</li>
<li>Two years of business administration, financial management, or related experience</li>
<li>Ability to analyze, interpret and implement University policies and procedures</li>
<li>Demonstrated oral and written communication skills</li>
<li>Excellent planning, problem-solving, organizational, project management and customer service skills</li>
<li>Ability to influence and build work relationships among a diverse workforce at all levels</li>
<li>Ability to foster a participative supervisory style that utilizes teamwork to successfully guide the work of others</li>
<li>Must possess a learning orientation to changing technology impacting business processes, e.g. SAP, etc.</li>
<li>Able to manage multiple priorities and work with frequent interruptions</li>
<li>Personal computer and related software skills</li>
<br>
</ul>
<div></div>
<div><b>Preferred:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Six credit hours of accounting coursework</li>
<br>
</ul>
