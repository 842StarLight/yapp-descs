<p>
Responsibilities:</p>
<ul>
<li>
PASSIONATE ABOUT TECHNOLOGY: You understand that technology and automation is the future of sales. You understand the need for a sales and marketing platform that manages workflow in the offline world.</li>

<li>
PROBLEM SOLVER: You enjoy learning how to solve problems in the most efficient way possible. Complexity doesn't scare you. The challenge of distilling something complex into something simple invigorates you.</li>
<li>
BIASED TOWARD ACTION: You are a self-motivated, action-oriented person. You don't sit on your hands and wait for someone else to fix a problem. If there is something you can do to make a situation better, you do it.</li>
<li>
