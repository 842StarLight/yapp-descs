<h2>Retail Brand Supervisor</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>PART I<br>
Job Title: Brand Supervisor<br>
Reports To: Retail Manager<br>
FLSA Status: Non Exempt<br>
Department: Retail<br>
Division: Turnpike</p>
<p>
PART II<br>
Broad Functions:</p>
<p>
The Retail Brand Supervisor will assist the Brand or Concept Manager to achieve targeted sales for respective brands, to ensure adherence of visual standards as per brands requirements, ensure proper assortment planning and inventory budgeting by buyers. Ensures that Guest Service takes priority.</p>
<p>
PART III<br>
Duties and Responsibilities:</p>
<p><br>
1. To coach and work with all associates to ensure that excellent GUEST service is provided in the store(s).<br>
2. To train and work with all associates to ensure that all store policies and procedures are understood and followed.<br>
3. To work with all associates on implementing store promotions and sales contests.<br>
4. To work with all associates to ensure that all “Specialty Brand” programs are followed and standards upheld.<br>
5. To assist the Manager with maintaining store programs and standards.<br>
6. Responsible for store replenishment of goods; including buying to realistic ROS (rate of sale), following sales and turnover goals, creating purchase orders and paying invoices.<br>
7. Assist with monthly inventory.<br>
8. Perform other related duties as assigned.</p>
