<h2>Account Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Synapse Marketing Solutions</b> , a leading provider of marketing and business growth solutions for best-in-class companies throughout the United States, is seeking an <b>Account Manager</b> to add to our growing team.</p>
<p><b>OVERVIEW</b></p>
<p>As an Account Manager, you’ll be a key member of our team as you work closely with our clients, strategists, interactive and creative teams to develop, execute, and manage cross-channel digital and brand marketing campaigns aimed at retention, acquisition, brand awareness, and loyalty. This position requires the ability to multi-task, develop creative solutions to business challenges and take a proactive approach in order to keep projects on track.</p>
<p>The right person for this position will have agency or corporate in-house agency experience, the ability to work with multiple deadlines, manage workflows, multiple projects of varying complexities, and work well under pressure. Content development, editing and proofreading skills are also necessary. Getting the little details right is important in this position.</p>
<p>Simply put, we are looking for top talent - the best of the best. This is a challenging, rewarding, high energy position with an excellent opportunity for career growth.</p>
<p><b>RESPONSIBILITIES OF THIS POSITION</b></p>
<p>The Account Manager will:</p>
<ul>
<li>Create and manage project plans and schedules with tight deadlines using Gantt charts, Workamajig management software and other project tools.</li>
<li>Manage multiple, simultaneous project schedules working under direction of cross-functional team members and collaborating with various project teams.</li>
<li>Create, track and manage estimates and budgets.</li>
<li>Oversee and manage print production and coordinate projects using outside print vendors.</li>
<li>Ability to up sell products and services to existing customers.</li>
<li>Have the ability to think creatively and develop innovative solutions to marketing challenges.</li>
<li>Have exceptional communication, organizational, email and writing skills.</li>
<li>Have the ability to multi-task in a deadline-driven, fast-paced environment.</li>
<li>Be able to work independently and collaboratively in a group setting.</li>
<li>Have a demonstrated aptitude and understanding of direct and digital marketing tactics, from strategy to creative to implementation.</li>
<li>Solid understanding of social media platforms.</li>
<li>Understand website development strategies and workflow.</li>
<li>Create, develop, and execute new and innovative client marketing strategies and tactics while continuously re-evaluating our current approaches in order to identify opportunities to apply best practices.</li>
<li>Have the ability to organize thoughts and ideas and see “the big picture” in order to successfully direct client marketing initiatives.</li>
<li>Ensure all program elements managed are consistent with principles of strategic design and reflective of clients’ brand standards.</li>
<li>Proactively incorporate analytics and measurement within campaigns; providing detailed reporting of analytical information. A solid understanding of Google Analytics and Microsoft Excel is required.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>REQUIREMENTS</b></p>
<p>The right candidate for the Senior Account Manager position will have:</p>
<ul>
<li>Minimum 2 to 5 years of marketing and project management experience, preferable in an agency setting.</li>
<li>Bachelor’s Degree in communications, marketing, advertising, journalism or a related field.</li>
<li>Effective written and oral communication skills are essential for this position.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>SUMMARY</b></p>
<p>If you like people and desire variety in your work, this is the position for you. You’ll be a critical part of a team that’s dedicated to ensuring that Synapse continues to meet our client commitments.</p>
<p>Synapse is a fast-paced, exciting company to work for. While we don’t get bogged down in a lot of corporate red tape, we are looking for people that work well with others, follow instructions, are trustworthy and accountable, and basically behave in a respectful manner. If you’re rude, don’t play well with others, are disorganized, lack common sense, or thrive on drama, Synapse isn’t the place for you.</p>
<p>Synapse offers an outstanding working environment, creative and entrepreneurial atmosphere, a wide range of benefits (including healthcare, paid vacations and holiday time, and company-matched retirement benefits) and competitive salary and generous bonus program.</p>
