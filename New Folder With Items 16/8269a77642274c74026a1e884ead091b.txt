<h2>Human Resources Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Overview
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div>Brown Distributing Company is a leading beverage distributor throughout the states of Virginia and Florida. Since 1919, Brown Distributing has grown into a beverage distribution business with combined sales of over 11 million cases annually and serving in excess of 8,000 retailers with over 500 employees. Brown Distributing offers their employees competitive compensation, complete benefit package, including medical, dental, vision, company paid life insurance, company paid short term disability, long term disability and 401K employer match.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
We are seeking an experienced <b>Human Resources Manager</b> to join our team in <b>Richmond, VA.</b> This position will be responsible to provide human resources services, including employee relations, recruitment support, full cycle recruitment and employment processing, compensation, health and company benefits, training and continued development, records management, safety and health, and AA/EEO compliance. This position works under the direction of the VP of HR to support management and employees, by helping to address their needs and concerns.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
The ideal candidate will be able to interface at all levels within our organization, as well as multitask, be detailed oriented and have the ability to set priorities and meet deadlines and provide timely follow up.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
<b>
Compensation:</b> Will commensurate with experience. Phone allowance is included.</div>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div><b>The individual must be experienced in handling employee relation matters and have the ability to conduct employee and manager training, provide coaching and guidance to managers and employees on a broad variety of employment issues. Must be well organized, detail oriented; possess excellent written &amp; verbal communication skills.</b></div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div><b>
TRAINING &amp; DEVELOPMENT</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
Manage Corporate Trainer to ensure training schedules are completed for all newly hired employees.</li>
<li>
Working with Directors to meet expectations related to training of new and existing employees.</li>
<li>
Ensure LMS, Thinkzoom is up to date with annual and new hire courses</li>
<li>
Execute yearly all company trainings for Anti-Harassment and Anti-Discrimination, Reasonable Suspicion, DFWP and other Management training activities.</li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<br>
<div><b>
EMPLOYEE RELATIONS</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
Serve as facilitator in the resolution of inter-employee and/or employee management conflicts</li>
<li>
Exceptional communication skills with the ability to communicate difficult and delicate matters to varying levels of employees and management. Manage employee relations while ensuring legal compliance. Develop recommendations and provide guidance to Managers for resolution.</li>
<li>
Handle all unemployment claim issues and information request.</li>
<li>
Serve as intermediary between front line employees and department management to the Executive team for process and policy changes
</li>
<li>Documenting BDC efforts to maintain a harassment and discrimination free workplace</li>
<li>
Assists as a point of contact for EEOC complaint and arbitration resolution</li>
</ul>
<div><b>
SAFETY / WORKERS COMPENSATION</b></div>
<div></div>
<br>
<ul>
<li>
Ensure the proper management and handling of W/C/auto accident claims is completed including the initial reporting, communication, scheduling of employees, informing management of restriction changes and employee status
</li>
<li>Working with nurse case manager, claims manager and other insurance representatives to maintain compliance and facilitate care to injured employees.</li>
</ul>
<div><b>
COMPENSATION AND BENEFITS</b></div>
<div></div>
<br>
<ul>
<li>
Coordinating with department Directors and Supervisors regarding compensation related issues. Including but not limited to: review of pay for performance, commission statements, conflicts related to hours / days worked.</li>
<li>
Responsible for reviewing and processing status change requests including transfers, terminations, pay changes, position job title changes, etc.</li>
<li>
Assisting employees with changes to Insurances, 401(K) setup and other BDC benefits as needed.</li>
</ul>
<div><b>
CUSTOMER SERVICE</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
Provide and ensure exceptional customer service to employees, customers and vendors</li>
<li>
Provide and ensure excellent follow up to issues, request and questions</li>
</ul>
