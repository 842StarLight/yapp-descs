<p>ARIAT is seeking a Director of eCommerce Engineering with experience leading teams responsible for the delivery of eCommerce/Omni-Channel platform development and integration.<br>

<p><b>EXPECTATIONS AND TASKS<br>
</b></p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Overview:<br>
</b><br>
</p>
<p>The Director of eCommerce Engineering will partner with business stakeholders to develop a technology and team strategy roadmap in service of eCommerce and Omni-Channel business goals. This role is responsible for leading a team of BSAs, architects, developers, and QA resources to provide site enhancements and production support to the company’s eCommerce and Omni-Channel businesses.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p>Managing these channels requires a diligent, organized, and self-disciplined technical Director that can effectively lead a team to provide on-time delivery of planned site functionality. In this role it is imperative to have a strong ability to build and manage a team tasked with effectively managing incoming incidents and requests from various external teams. It is necessary for this position to work closely with the Enterprise Application and Data Services teams, other application development teams, 3 rd party implementation partners, and a diverse cross section of functional business partners. The Director will be expected to provide analytical dashboards reporting on the progress of initiatives, including ongoing production support levels and strategic initiatives. This role collaborates with diverse business partners to plan feature deliverables as well as analyze and solve complex business problems on an ad-hoc basis.<br>
</p>
