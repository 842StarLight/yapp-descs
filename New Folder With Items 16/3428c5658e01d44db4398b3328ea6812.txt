<p><b>Position Description:</b></p>
<p>
The Solution Architect will have responsibility for the successful implementation, integration, and customization of Salesforce applications for Eagle Creek’s customers from project initiation to full customer production roll-out. In this capacity you will be an expert in Salesforce functionality, technology, and implementation in order to provide your accounts and team with a combination of pre-sales activities, solution consulting, leadership, and mentoring.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Skills:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>A proven functional/technical professional with multiple full lifecycle SFDC implementations and at least 4+ years of experience working with SFDC</li>
<li>Direct experience with SFDC Sales Cloud and Service Cloud (application design, architecture and development using Salesforce.com), certified a MAJOR PLUS</li>
<li>Strong SFDC knowledge and Integration components for SFDC ( for example, SFDC APIs, Apex controllers, Apex Web Services, Apex callout, outbound messaging, SFDC data loaders and ETL Tools - e.g. Datastage)</li>
<li>Strong technical foundation including: 5+ yrs experience with advanced structured programming - APEX, Force.com, .Net, Java, SOA</li>
<li>Strong understanding of RDBMS concepts and structures, knowledge of SQL, structured system analysis and design patterns</li>
<li>Proven experience in overseeing the technical direction, development, and implementation of SFDC software solutions</li>
<li>Understands complex ETL and data migration practices and has performed multiple complex data migrations to SFDC</li>
<li>Work with leads to transform and develop new requirements into designs, development approaches and implementation</li>
<li>Ability to comprehend client requirements and provide the Business Solutions</li>
<li>Effectively work with management, on-site/off-site development staff to accomplish SFDC project deliverables</li>
<li>Effectively balance and prioritize multiple projects concurrently</li>
<li>Leads design and execution of business solutions</li>
<li>Demonstrates a broad understanding of the SFDC ecosystem and technical architectures</li>
<li>Demonstrates expert understanding of the SFDC development processes, serving as a resource to others</li>
<li>Demonstrates an in-depth knowledge of leading edge technologies and plans for the future of a technology and/or applications area</li>
<li>Brings technical and/or business systems knowledge from external sources and incorporates those ideas into IT</li>
<li>Ability to function in a team</li>
<li>Applied knowledge of SFDC and CRM processes including Sales, Services and Marketing functions.</li>
<li>Strong communication and interpersonal skills</li>
<li>Proven experience in mentoring and coaching team members</li>
<li>Strong knowledge of AGILE/SCRUM Processes</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p>
</p>
<p><b>Duties:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Function as Salesforce.com (SFDC) Solution Architect, responsible for overall solution design including all SFDC components, integration and reporting</li>
<li>Present architecture options and recommendations - including configuration vs. customization</li>
<li>Provide advice on best practices and approaches to technology teams and internal business clients</li>
<li>Resolve enterprise issues related to IT architecture</li>
<li>Work with infrastructure teams to develop solutions</li>
<li>Lead development teams to deliver full lifecycle SFDC implementations from inception to roll-out, including code reviews</li>
<li>Perform gap analysis between SFDC capabilities and client requirements</li>
<li>Write comprehensive design specifications (functional specifications and solution/technical design document)</li>
<li>Assist in developing data movement strategies and implementing data cleansing/data quality tools</li>
<li>Work with IT and Product teams to identify and define the business needs and the solution to fulfill the requirements</li>
<li>Gather functional requirements, conduct analysis, and establish metrics for success</li>
<li>Assist in analyzing SFDC plugins and add-ons</li>
<li>Interface with SFDC technical SME’s</li>
<li>Determine and enforce exit criteria through the different development phases</li>
<li>Design migration strategies with technical teams</li>
<li>Additional duties as required</li>

</ul>
