<h2>Sales Engineer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Job Overview:
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div>Are you a master presenter with experience demoing recruiting software solutions? Do you relish the idea of being the first pair of boots on the ground in the sales process? If so, then you may be the perfect fit for our opening for a Sales Engineer III, a critical position on our Sales Team that demonstrates our recruiting software solutions for customers and prospects! You will develop and maintain knowledge of the ins and outs of our software, and be able to translate them into simple, easy-to-understand terms that speak to everybody with a say in the sales process. If this is the type of job that you’ve been doing and doing well, and you possess the proven communication skills that command the attention of rooms filled with executives, then please apply!</div>
</div></div></div>
