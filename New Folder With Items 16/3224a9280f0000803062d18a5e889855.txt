<h2>Transaction Coordinator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>The Transaction Coordinator plays an important role in the organization by performing, under direct supervision, a number of activities related to assisting originators build complete loan files for submission. This role performs Lead to Contract duties.</p>
<p>Essential Functions:</p>
<p>Lead to Contract duties Assist Mortgage Broker in appointment setting with prospective and new applicants</p>
<ul><li>Provide new lead call backs within 20 minutes and maintain a 35% conversion rate from lead to appointment.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Provide administrative support managing various client databases (lead tracker, MyCircle, Zillow, etc.). Send new clients online pre-qualification link on behalf of originator.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Pull and prep web applications and other administrative based duties, including updating credit reports and AUS findings, for licensed teammates.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Set and confirm appointments for the Mortgage Broker and manage calendar.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Prepare and send thank you cards to referral sources and new clients.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Act as a point of contact for the customer and referral source, keeping them informed throughout the process, subject to the limitations as set forth in Imagination Age's SAFE Act and Consumer Privacy/Safeguarding in Information policies.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Run credit on loans assigned to Mortgage Broker</li></ul>
<ul><li>Request status updates from pre-approved clients and referral sources (weekly TBD calls).</li></ul>
<ul><li>Contact expired, pre-approved clients to gather necessary documentation for licensed teammates to update pre-approval documentation and/or letters.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Provide marketing support by coordinating, creating, obtaining compliance approval, tracking, and distributing advertising materials as needed.</li></ul>
<p>Minimum Qualifications:</p>
<ul><li>High school diploma or equivalent.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Minimum 2 years' experience in financial or mortgage banking sales work environment.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Knowledge of mortgage process, guidelines, regulations and automated underwriting systems.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Proficiency with data entry, PDF software programs, Microsoft Word, Excel and Outlook required.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Ability to manage multiple priorities; strong detail orientation and highly organized</li></ul>
<p>Job Type: Full-time</p>
<p>Salary: $14.00 to $18.00 /hour</p>
<p>Experience:</p>
<ul><li>financial or mortgage banking sales: 2 years (Required)</li></ul>
<p>Additional Compensation:</p>
<ul><li>Bonuses</li></ul>
