<h2>Digital Advertising Coordinator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div><b>Position Summary/Objective:</b></div>
<div>
The Digital Advertising Coordinator is a key role in activating for our partners in the Social/Digital Space. They will be responsible for managing advertising programs within PBR websites, mobile apps, social media, &amp; RidePass. They will work with the Sponsorship and Sales team to develop, service, and execute digital campaign activation, delivery &amp; fulfillment. They will also be responsible for maintenance and updates to PBR.com.</div>
<div>
<br>
<b>
Essential Duties &amp; Responsibilities:</b>
</div>
<ul>
<li>Set up &amp; Schedule all Digital Media Advertising Campaigns using our ad server(s) &amp;/or digital content platforms</li>
<li>Supply Social Media team with assets &amp; schedules for Advertising Campaigns on Social Media Platforms</li>
<li>Supply RidePass team, with assets &amp; schedules for Advertising Campaigns on RidePass platform/broadcasts</li>
<li>Supply Email Marketing Team with assets and schedule for Email/Newsletter Advertising Campaigns</li>
<li>Track campaign delivery of all digital, email &amp; social media plans, making all necessary adjustments to ensure delivery</li>
<li>Responsible for all campaign maintenance including optimization suggestions &amp; screen shots of all placements</li>
<li>Responsible for tracking reports and Social/Digital recap slides for mid-year and end-of-year recaps</li>
<li>Manage available ad inventory across Social Media, Website, Email, RidePass, and any other digital assets</li>
<li>Assist in creation &amp; distribution of digital media plans to internal teams, clients and agencies</li>
<li>Pulling and distributing weekly Digital &amp; Advertising reports to internal teams, clients and agencies</li>
<li>Assistance with building pitches &amp; campaign recap presentations</li>
<li>Assist in pulling data from Social/Digital/RidePass to be included in pitch decks</li>
<li>Assist in gathering digital creative from clients/agencies for all sold and internal campaigns</li>
<li>Work to be a communications bridge between the Sales, Partnership, and Social/Digital teams</li>
<li>Work with graphic design on development of internal digital ad creative, when needed</li>
<li>Work with Partnership Marketing team to add sponsored assets (outside of digital campaigns: i.e. event activities/sponsor logos) to the PBR website.</li>
<li>Building PBR Events for the PBR Website</li>
<li>Manage and track assets as they are pitched and sold</li>
<li>Other duties as assigned</li>
</ul>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div><b>Knowledge, Skills &amp; Abilities:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Familiarity with ad-serving platforms, digital best practices, measurement tools, as well as current digital trends.</li>
<li>Ability to be successful in a fast-paced, time-sensitive sports environment.</li>
<li>Strong ability to interpret data and make strategic decisions based on data analysis.</li>
<li>Strong communication skills</li>
<li>Demonstrate strategic thinking and problem-solving skills.</li>
<li>Proficient in Microsoft Office software – Excel, Word, PowerPoint, Outlook</li>
<li>Familiarity with Adobe software- Photoshop, Premiere Pro</li>
</ul>
