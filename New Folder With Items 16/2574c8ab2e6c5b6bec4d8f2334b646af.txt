<div><b>Job Summary</b></div>
<div>
The Senior Manager of Project Management is responsible for the leadership, development, and performance of Project Coordinators, Project Managers, Senior Project Managers, and Program Managers. The Sr. Manager, Project Management should have substantial team leadership experience with a focus on project management staff development, as well as a proven track record of successful project delivery and continuous improvement. In addition to the direct supervision, the Sr. Manager of Project Management is also responsible for ensuring the team’s understanding and use of the EPMO’s policies and procedures. The position reports to the Director of Project Management. The Senior Manager will play a key role in understanding the portfolio of work for their given areas and will need to ensure the assignment and completion of that work.</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div><b>
Essential Job Functions</b></div>
<div><div>
<div>
Provides leadership, organization and coordination to support the EPMO’s key activities, encompassing program and project planning, governance and project management.</div>
<div>
Works with EPMO leadership to advance the EPMO’s policies, procedures and project management methodology.</div>
<div>
Ensures project management staff understand and adhere to the best practices and standard approaches for program and project management, as appropriate. Ensures project managers follow appropriate project management discipline including tracking, reporting, and communicating as necessary for the projects as assigned.</div>
<div>
Works with project management staff to remove roadblocks, assist in resolving issues, and ensure high quality delivery of projects.</div>
<div>
Coordinates formal and informal training of project managers and develops and maintains project manager career paths and performance measurements.</div>
<div>
Establishes strategies for the implementation of continuous improvement efforts designed to maintain and/or surpass goals for stakeholder satisfaction.</div>
<div>
Mentors and advises project management staff on client relations, stakeholder engagement, risk identification and management, project quality, issue resolution, conflict management and negotiation techniques.</div>
<div>
Assigns appropriate resources based on project value, complexity, risk, visibility and resource experience.</div>
<div>
Oversees the development of effective project tools to aid in effective, efficient, and high quality delivery.</div>
<div>
Analyzes key performance measures and works to continuously improve team performance and value by looking for new and innovative ways to meet customer needs.</div>
<div>
Proactively thinks ahead, identifies and monitors trends, and seeks permanent resolution to problems that prevent staff from delivering their best.</div>
