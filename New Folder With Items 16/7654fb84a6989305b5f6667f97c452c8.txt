<h2>Mechanical Engineer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<div><div><div>
<p>Design and review of mechanical systems, including motion devices, weldments, material transport systems, hydraulics, pneumatics, and safety devices by performing the following duties:</p>
<h5 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Primary Responsibilities</b></h5>
<ul>
<li>Designs, develops, and documents machine structures/systems.</li>
<li>Designs, develops, and documents precision motion assemblies.</li>
<li>Designs hydraulic systems to include creating models, detail drawings, schematics and documentation.</li>
<li>Designs pneumatics systems to include creating models, detail drawings, schematics and documentation.</li>
<li>Specify materials, tolerances and machining of fabricated machine parts.</li>
<li>Specify purchased components.</li>
<li>Create Bills of Materials (BOMs), used in the production of components and assemblies.</li>
<li>Coordinate system development with Electrical and Software Engineers, and Technical Writers, as part of a product development team.</li>
<li>Interface with Production team in manufacturing, assembly, and debug of equipment.</li>
<li>Communicate project status to the customer and Director of Engineering.</li>
</ul>
<h5 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Hiring Qualifications</b></h5>
<ul>
<li>BS degree in Mechanical Engineering.</li>
<li>Mechanical engineering design-related experience: one (1) year desired, but will consider strong candidates with less experience.</li>
<li>Strong CAD drafting skills, 3D modeling (SolidWorks preferred) essential. AutoCAD Rel.14 experience essential.</li>
<li>Proficient in drawing layouts, and industry standards for dimensioning and tolerancing.</li>
<li>Pneumatics, hydraulics and/or electrical experience a plus.</li>
<li>Proven background in machine design.</li>
<li>Personal computer proficiency.</li>
<li>Ability to work as part of a design team.Interested in working on custom or "one off" systems.</li>
<li>Demonstrated technical or process experience in at least two of the following areas:
<ul>
<li>materials handling components (e.g., robotics, actuators, dial tables, feeder bowls, pick-and-place mechanisms).</li>
<li>automated material processing systems such as welding or cutting systems that process individual metallic parts.</li>
<li>automated web and/or sheet converting systems for materials such as paper, plastic, fabric, and composite films.</li>
<li>process knowledge relating to processing metallic, composite, or non-metallic materials.</li>
<li>industrial controls and/or software experience that use personal computers (PC) or programmable logic controllers (PLC).</li>
<li>tooling and fixture design for locating individual parts</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>Knowledge of Solid Works, AutoCAD, PC-based software for word processing, spread sheets, design, and manufacturing preferred.</li>
</ul>
<h5 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Physical Demands:</b></h5>
<ul>
<li>Extensive direct and phone communications with customers, component suppliers, company personnel, and contract personnel.</li>
<li>Travel to customer facilities for sales, installation, and service related activities.</li>
<li>Extended hours of sitting and viewing a computer screen.</li>
<li>Extended hours of standing or working in a shop environment.</li>
</ul>
