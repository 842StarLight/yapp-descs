<h2>Lean Process Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Position Overview</b></p>
<p>MSCI is seeking a Lean Process Engineering Leader with a background in Enterprise Lean Transformation with a focus on shared service workflows in HR, Finance, Sales Operations and IT to join the Lean/Agile Center of Excellence team in New York, NY. MSCI’s Lean/Agile Center of Excellence is mandated to drive the adoption of Lean practices to significantly improve end-user experience with shared services at MSCI. We are looking for a leader who can help establish this practice, support a sustainable mindset shift in employees, and deliver high-impact, generative outcomes.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>The candidate will work across the organization with embedded ‘change agents’ and coaches to collaboratively design and execute on systematic improvements in quality, cost, and end-user experience. The successful candidate should have experience with Enterprise Lean Transformations across multiple functions within a financial or technology based firm.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p>We’re looking for a lifelong learner, with strong leadership and coaching skills, who is energized by idea of applying systems thinking and Lean/Agile practices to generate high impact results.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Scope of Responsibility</b></p>
<ul><li> Coach and teach continuous improvement methodologies and embed lean data based thinking into others. Respectfully challenge the status quo and encourage fellow employees to get better every day.<br>
</li></ul>
<p></p>
<ul><li> Develop and implement the MSCI lean management system across institutional services. Ensure that all elements such as recommended processes, problem solving practices and governance are in place to drive sustainable results.<br>
</li></ul>
<p></p>
<ul><li> Help MSCI functions to understand and map their value chain. Use lean tools to select the right blend of events, initiatives and “just do its” to drive business improvement.</li></ul>
<br>
<p></p>
<ul><li> Lead and facilitate lean activities such as kaizen events, lean process reviews, process improvements and A3 techniques.<br>
</li></ul>
<p></p>
<ul><li> Create a 6-12 month lean transformation roadmap and work with MSCI teams to deliver agreed outcomes.<br>
</li></ul>
<p></p>
<ul><li> Collaborate with third party lean consultants where applicable.<br>
</li></ul>
<p></p>
<ul><li> Work across technology and business functions to connect software development agile practices and Lean practices so that the organization can continuously improve.<br>
</li></ul>
<p></p>
<ul><li> Develop metrics to quantify quality, financial improvements, and innovation.</li></ul>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Specific Knowledge/Skills</b></p>
<ul><li> Bachelor’s Degree required, preferably in an engineering discipline.
</li></ul>
<ul><li> Lean and or Six Sigma Black Belt certification.
</li></ul>
<ul><li> Background in ITSM/ITIL desired.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Familiarity with Agile Product Delivery: Scrum, Lean Start-Up, Scaled Agile Framework</li></ul>
<ul><li> 10 years of demonstrated lean application experience and training at the “Gemba”.
</li></ul>
<ul><li> Exceptional relationship-building skills: Leads by building relationships and trust. Demonstrates respect for people by creating the mindset that performing waste free work exudes the ultimate respect for fellow employees.
</li></ul>
<ul><li> Lean knowledge: Possesses hands on knowledge in value stream mapping, standard work, pull systems, inventory right sizing and policy deployment.
</li></ul>
<ul><li> Communication: Clearly exchanges thoughts, ideas and messages through written, verbal and non-verbal methods that promote an understanding with the target audience. Shares information and ideas with others in a timely manner. Listens carefully and attentively.
</li></ul>
<ul><li> Problem Solving: Is able to solve complex problems using various tools and gets to root cause. Drives preventative thinking into the application of countermeasures.
</li></ul>
<ul><li> Attention to Detail: Is attentive to detail and accuracy, performs quality work, continuously looks for improvements, finds root cause of problems, owns/acts on problems, seeks opportunities to increase effectiveness and efficiency.
</li></ul>
<ul><li> Innovation: Generates new ideas, challenges the status quo, pursues ongoing improvements, supports change, and solves problems creatively.</li></ul>
