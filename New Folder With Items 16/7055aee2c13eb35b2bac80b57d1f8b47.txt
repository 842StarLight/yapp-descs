<h2>Aerospace NDT Inspector</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Responsibilities - NDT Inspector: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Interpreting and completing the process controls required for penetrant inspection</li>
<li>Processing parts through the complete penetrant inspection cycle</li>
<li>Interpretation of process procedures, specifications and evaluation techniques specific to liquid penetrant inspection</li>
<li>Documentation of liquid penetrant inspection results</li>
<li>Develop inspection techniques as derived from processing procedures, codes, specifications, etc.</li>
<li>Ensure that all required maintenance actions are performed in accordance with FAA approved data and applicable manufacturer's maintenance manual</li>
<li>Document all actions correctly and completely for the appropriate maintenance forms</li>
<li>Maintain test areas in a clean orderly condition, at all times and ensure tolls and equipment are properly calibrated as required</li>
</ul>
<p>Career Level<br>Experienced Non-Manager</p>
<p>Requirements</p>
<p><b>Education - NDT Inspector: </b></p>
<ul><li>High School Diploma or GED equivalent</li></ul>
<p><b>Experience - NDT Inspector: </b></p>
<ul><li>At least two years of relevant education and/or experience</li></ul>
<p><b>Skills - NDT Inspector: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Familiarity with the scope and limitations of the liquid penetrant methods (PT)</li>
<li>Familiarity with the scope and limitations of the radiographic inspection methods (RT)</li>
<li>English: ability to read and interpret documents such as emails, reports and correspondence</li>
<li>Excellent ability to communicate both orally and in writing</li>
<li>Must work well and communicate with others</li>
<li>Must be flexible in handling multiple tasks</li>
<li>Proficient working knowledge of computer hardware and software, including Microsoft Office Suite (Outlook, Word, Excel)</li>
<li>Ability to use logic and reasoning to identify the strengths and weaknesses of alternative solutions to problems</li>
<li>Strong organizational skills, strict attention to details and ability to multitask and prioritize with minimal supervisory direction</li>
</ul>
