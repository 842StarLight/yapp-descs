<br>Our Opening and Your Responsibilities
<br>We have one of the largest global service and sales organizations among precision instrumentation and weighing companies, and we need to add to this team immediately in Chicago, IL. We are currently hiring a Field Service Technician. In this role, you will perform preventive maintenance, troubleshooting, repairing, IT/Software services and installations for our Mettler Toledo Transport and Logistics weighing equipment at customer locations throughout the US and Canada. Your responsibilities would include: * Participate to develop and maintain a feeling of teamwork within the service area * Maintain great customer relationships and completing all service requirements in a timely manner while exhibiting quality service * Traveling overnight 70% of the time * Working evenings and weekends occasionally * Lifting and carrying 50+ pounds occasionally * Standing and squatting for long periods of time
<br>What You Need to Succeed
<br><ul>
<li>Worked in field service or have related experience in an engineering or manufacturing environment * Calibration, preventive maintenance, and repair skills * The ability to identify the root causes of service issues the first time using your keen knowledge of the products and your comfort with hand tools and calibration equipment * Excellent customer service skills, detail orientation, and great verbal and written communication skills * The discipline to update service records on time and ensure data integrity * The desire and ability to promote and sell services and products, generating sales leads * An Associate's degree within a scientific or technical area of study or equivalent professional experience or a combination of both * A valid driver's license and a safe driving history</li>
</ul>
