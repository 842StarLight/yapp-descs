<h2>Product Implementation Analyst 1</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div><b>Role Purpose:
</b></div>
<div></div>
<div>The Product Implementation Analyst I is primarily responsible for supporting systems and processes for product development, implementation, and some renewal activities and acts as a support for business leaders and managers in performing implementation activities related to all lines of business for Dean Health Plan. Works collaboratively with Product Line team and other business and technical areas to ensure that products /variations are added into systems and tested through approved processes and methods. This role completes configuration, per requirements for systems supported by Product. In addition, they act as a testing resource on various efforts for product area for key systems or processes related to loading product or rates on core systems, subsystems or partner systems. May give input on estimates and follows appropriate documentation standards for implementation processes and procedures. Product Implementation Analyst I may execute defined ad-hoc reporting requests or vendor/partner implementation and testing activities, as needed. In addition, this role may support financial or business metrics and use reporting tools.</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div><b>Responsibilities:
</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Completes configuration tasks for rate or product input, including completion of templates, test execution, test design and documentation, as directed.</li>
<li>
Supports renewal implementation with build and test for renewals of existing product and rate activities the supports completion of product line documents as needed.</li>
<li>
Resource for inputting QHP and/or other product data for filings required to support or load product/rate information and reviewing QHP or other information for plan preview or other filing-related testing activities.</li>
<li>
May input /test/review results of outsourced capabilities, internal systems, and execute specific configuration tasks or testing tasks as assigned.</li>
<li>
Reviews information from sales, benefits, underwriting and/or actuarial for input to systems related to rating and product, and supports those functions for configuration, and participates</li>
<li>
Supports Product Implementation Manager and Product Management team in day to day activities related to product development and implementation.</li>
<li>
Follows all required documentation protocols, ensures that documentation is updated, and contributes to maintaining the SharePoint site for the Product area.</li>
<li>
Documents information in meetings to provide clear minutes and ensures that distribution occurs after meetings.</li>
<li>
Engages in customer experience projects and activities, and helps create awareness of issues and works with business areas, and the organization as a whole, to improve customer satisfaction.</li>
<li>
May acts as a SME for product on a variety of implementation teams for efforts related to product systems.</li>
<li>
Supports Product to Operations and Product Strategy Team implementation activities, research, discovery and documentation to support business-wide decision making, as assigned.</li>
<li>
Assists Product staff with special projects, as needed.</li>
</ul>
<br>
