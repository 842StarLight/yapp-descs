<p><b>Engineering Manager - Strategic Staffing</b></p>
<p>
World Wide Technology, Inc. is seeking to fill an Engineering Manager position within our Professional Services team.</p>
<p>
The Engineering Manager will work with internal partners and customers to provide qualified staff for our Strategic Staffing engagements. Additionally, the manager will be responsible for developing a strategy and plan to handle future growth in the various technical verticals through capacity planning, training, and process improvement. The successful manager will also provide leadership, mentorship, and guidance to the engineers as they execute their assigned engagements.</p>
<p><b>
RESPONSIBILITIES:</b></p>
<p>
Manage consultants who have been placed onsite with customers</p>
<ul>
<li>Onboarding, regular meetings, and communication, status reports, etc.
</li>
<li>Function as the escalation path for ANY issues for both resources and customers</li>
<li>Encourage the use of WWT tools and processes, and encourage resource involvement with other teams within WWT.
</li>
<li>Responsible for staffing projects and managing utilization for customer engagements</li>
<li>Capacity planning and forecasting for future engagements</li>
<li>Coach and mentor engineering staff on PS best practices</li>
<li>Consistently reinforce WWT and our Core Values.
</li>
</ul>
<p>EM establishes solid relationships with the Customer &amp; the Account Management teams</p>
<ul>
<li>Regular check-ins manage issues/concerns and provide feedback to customers and team members. Provide weekly status reports to sales account teams and customers.</li>
<li>Work with the customer to understand their Pain Points and Channel back the information to the Sales Team.</li>
<li>Build relationships with the entire sales cycle - CSE/CSE Managers, AM's, Hiring Managers, etc.
</li>
<li>Grow the staffing business through continued displays of adding value to the customer. Ascertain additional staffing needs or business opportunities and gain an understanding of the customer environment, upcoming projects and client initiatives that WWT might be able to assist with. Relay this to Managing Consultants and the Account Teams.
</li>
</ul>

<p><b>Qualifications</b></p>
<div><div>
<p><b>QUALIFICATIONS:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>5-10 years of Technical management experience, preferably in a Professional Services environment.</li>
<li>Strong knowledge of and proven experience with outsourcing/managed service engagements. Experience in the staffing or recruiting industry a plus</li>
<li>Proven experience interacting with large, enterprise clients</li>
<li>Prior experience managing engineering teams or technical staff in a project execution capacity.</li>
<li>Ability to work independently and solve customer-facing problems.</li>
<li>Strong communication skills (written, verbal, and presentation).</li>
<li>Bachelor's Degree or equivalent experience</li>
<li>Need to go onsite at the Customer's Premises twice a week in Sacramento</li>
</ul>
