<b>POSITION SUMMARY:</b> The Leasing Specialist assists with day-to-day leasing and administrative operations for the community. Leasing Specialists assist Community and Leasing Managers with reaching all KPI goals.
<br><br>
<b>KEY DUTIES &amp; RESPONSIBILITIES:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Assist residents, &amp; prospects with daily needs in a professional manner (rent payments, ledger questions, etc.)</li>
<li>Consistently and genuinely demonstrate exemplary principles of customer service and teamwork</li>
<li>Work with your team to reach daily and weekly leasing goals</li>
<li>Maintain a working knowledge of all aspects of the community to quickly respond to inquiries</li>
<li>Work collaboratively with other staff members in a highly entrepreneurial environment.</li>
</ul>
EXPERIENCE &amp; QUALIFICATIONS
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>1-2 years of customer service and sales experience</li>
<li>Strong communication skills</li>
<li>Experience with Entrata Software or familiarity with PM software is a plus</li>
<li>Ability to remain calm and collected in high-stress situations</li>
<li>Enjoy and take pride in providing excellent service</li>
<li>Warm, friendly demeanor</li>
<li>Basic computer skills: typing and writing ability for correspondence, memos, etc.</li>
<li>High School Diploma or equivalent</li>
</ul>
<b>COMPENSATION:</b> Hourly, full-time, with the ability to earn performance bonuses</div>
