If you are interested and qualified for this role, we invite you to apply.

<p></p>Drive retention, adoption and engagement of customers through non-traditional banking channels using new and unique technology, programs and associations.
<br>
<br>
This role will be responsible for providing customer transaction services using a new video enabled interactive teller technology. A video Teller’s primary responsibility is to process customer transactions received via the two-way, video enabled teller transaction system. These transactions need to be handled accurately and efficiently in a fast-paced environment, in accordance with established policies and procedure to exceed customer expectations.
<p></p>
<p>Essential Functions/Responsibilities:
</p>
<ul>
<li>Sells and cross-sells all bank products and services while providing quality service and information to customers and prospective customers to build and maintain effective account relationships
</li>
<li>Must have strong communication skills in a face to face customer environment and be comfortable being on camera, as customers will see the Video Teller employee via video transmission from the ATM with Teller Assist to the Video Teller Agent computer desktop.
</li>
<li>Operates an interactive teller terminal and manages a fund of cash, while maintaining a minimum balancing standard of 90% year-to-date; maintains supply of cash for terminal; balances terminals at end of shift; and keeps informed of current security and holdup procedures.
</li>
<li>Greets customers and prospective customers in a courteous manner, addressing them by name whenever possible, ascertains needs, answers questions, provides services or refers customers to appropriate staff members for specialized services.
</li>
<li>Provides direct customer service by handling all transactions pertaining to cash, cash items, checking and savings deposits, certificates of deposit, cashier’s checks and various types of payments in accordance with operating procedures and policies.
</li>
<li>Use computerized systems for customer authentication, information gathering/validation and processing business as usual, as well as exception transactions.
</li>
<li>Ensure day-to-day operational excellence of ATM with Teller Assist transactions, including compliance with regulations and bank policies &amp; procedures. Ensure compliance with federal regulations including, but not limited to, USA PATRIOT Act, Anti-Money Laundering and Bank Secrecy Act.
</li>
<li>Agent must act as Bank’s face to the customer and consistently exhibit a high level of associate and customer engagement to delight our customers by demonstrating appropriate customer service standards.
</li>
<li>Build customer loyalty through courtesy and friendliness, including addressing each customer by name.
</li>
<li>Contribute to a positive team environment in the contact center by modeling appropriate behaviors and demonstrating Bank’s core values in all actions.
</li>

<p></p>
<p>Minimum Knowledge, Skills, and Abilities Needed to Perform Essential Functions of the Job:
</p>
<ul>
<li>Associate’s degree preferred plus two years of bank, general office or sales skills experience and above average computer skills, verbal and written communications skills. Requires ability to identify and explain products and services which offer variable options.
</li>
<li>Requires high sales ability to conduct direct sales of products and services with offer variable options, but are normally not complex; also, requires above average verbal and written communication skills and effective listening skills to identify customer needs and suggest products to meet those needs. Candidates must be energetic, well spoken, comfortable with technology and have the ability to provide consistent levels of service regardless of customer
</li>
<li>Ability to compile and assimilate data of moderate complexity from multiples sources, which may be reviewed and analyzed by senior managers and external entities. Ability to add, subtract, multiply, and divide in all units of measure, using whole numbers common fractions, and decimals. Ability to gather and organize data, operate an interactive teller virtual cash drawer, manage a fund of cash, and maintain a 90% YTD minimum balancing standard.
</li>
<li>Ability to use discretionary judgment within a sphere of non-controversial activity, clearly guided by law, regulation, operations standards or company policy.
</li>
<li>Ability to apply visual attention, dexterity and coordination in the regular operation of interactive teller terminal, calculator, CRT, typewriter, telephone and fax equipment, photocopier, check imprinter, personal computer and word processing and spreadsheet software; requires strong retail knowledge and proven sales abilities
</li>
</ul>
