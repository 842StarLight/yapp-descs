<h2>Sales + Leasing Consultant</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>JOIN OUR TEAM!</b></p>
<p></p>
<p>We have an immediate need for an experience Leasing Consultant to join the team at one of our professionally managed, Senior apartment communities located in the Annapolis, MD area - <b><i>The Gardens of Annapolis</i></b>. This luxury active adult community is located just moments from Annapolis Mall and Town Center and is exclusive for seniors 62 and older.<br>
</p>
<p><br>
The Leasing Consultant is responsible for leasing available apartments. Additionally, the Leasing Consultant is responsible for assisting the Property Manager in maintaining the physical asset while maximizing the financial returns for that asset in accordance with the Owner's objectives.<br>
</p>
<p><br>
Reports directly to the Property Manager. Maintains relationships with all residents, ensuring consistent application of property policies and resident satisfaction. Maintains relationship with other departments within the Company. Maintains relationships with clients, the community, suppliers, vendors, and professionals servicing the Company or property.<br>
</p>
<div>
<br>
<b>RESPONSIBILITIES</b><br>
<br>
The responsibilities that are listed below are not all inclusive; however, they are indicative of the type of responsibilities normally performed by the Leasing Consultant:<br>
<br>
<b>MARKETING AND LEASING:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Assists in preparation of advertising and marketing materials</li>
<li>Assists in preparation of regular market surveys</li>
<li>Prepares weekly and monthly reports</li>
<li>Makes recommendations to improve marketing and leasing programs</li>
<li>Tours visitors of the community</li>
<li>Shows apartments to interested guests</li>
<li>Encourages interested guests to apply for residency</li>
<li>Leases apartments to qualified applicants</li>
<li>Assists applicant with application process</li>
<li>Closes sale of apartments</li>
<li>Keeps units leased to meet or exceed budget<br>
</li>
</ul>
</div>
<div>
<br>
<b>ADMINISTRATION:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Assists in the input of all data into computer system</li>
<li>Assists in preparation of all necessary packages for lease up move-ins</li>
<li>Creates letters/newsletters and/or flyers</li>
<li>Ability to handle any emergencies that may arise on site</li>
<li>Handles incoming phone calls</li>
<li>Communicates all problems and makes recommendations to the Property Manager<br>
</li>
</ul>
</div>
<div>
<br>
<b>QUALIFICATIONS</b><br>
<br>
<ul>
<li>Bachelors degree or related experience in Sales/Marketing</li>
<li>Strong organizational, management, and teamwork skills</li>
<li>Solid administrative skills - knowledge of Microsoft Office products, i.e. Excel, Outlook, Word, PowerPoint, Publisher</li>
<li>Ability to handle finances and work within a budget; attention to details</li>
<li>Professional image compatible with United Group - positive attitude, energetic, assertive, and ability to serve as a role model for subordinates</li>
<li>Demonstrates integrity on personal and professional level</li>
<li>Willingness to work respectfully with people of diverse backgrounds required</li>
<li>Ability to solve problems involving residents, personnel, emergency situations, etc.</li>
<li>Ability and willingness to substitute for any position at the site<br>
</li>
</ul>
</div>
<div>
<br>
<b>SCOPE + COMPENSATION</b><br>
<br>
<ul>
<li>Minimum forty hours per week; additional hours as required. Regular schedule to be consistent with established hours of operation</li>
<li>Salary commensurate with level of experience and job requirements</li>
<li>Paid sick, personal, and vacation as outlined in the Employee Handbook</li>
<li>A portion of the single health insurance premium paid towards employee health insurance</li>
<li>Additional benefits offered to full-time employees of the United Group as outlined in the Benefits Enrollment Guide</li>
<li>Reasonable business expenses to be paid/reimbursed within the scope of performing routine necessary business</li>
</ul>
</div>
