<h2>Director of Digital & Demand Generation</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Director of Digital &amp; Demand Generation</b>
</p>
<p>The Director of Digital &amp; Demand Generation will lead the development and execution of our global digital marketing and demand generation strategy to grow sales pipeline. This person will lead improvements in user experience, user engagement, conversions, and analytics across digital channels (web, SEO, paid search, display advertising, mobile, social, content syndication, email marketing, and account-based advertising). This person will work across functions, including corporate marketing, field marketing, product marketing, and sales, to drive an integrated strategy to increase market awareness, create demand, and expand our digital footprint.</p>
<p><b>
Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Evaluation/selection of marketing technology stack, database growth, global demand generation strategic plan, and integrated campaigns to drive MQLs</li>
<li>Partner closely with sales teams to set targets, measure, analyze and report on demand creation activities on sales pipeline</li>
<li>Develop the strategy and manage the execution of the global digital strategy; work with cross-functional teams to ensure alignment with marketing goals across key geos</li>
<li>Lead site development, user experience, and site performance, consistent with user audiences/personas, user data and behavior, and overall site performance</li>
<li>Lead SEO strategy and execution, site architecture optimization and on-page and off-page optimization; manage SEO firm and provide analytics with recommendations for improvement</li>
<li>Support global integrated campaigns with landing pages, SEO, SEM, display advertising, content syndication, and reporting</li>
<li>Conduct ongoing competitive research to help inform digital strategies</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Qualifications</b></p>
<ul>
<li>8+ years of demand generation and digital marketing experience in global organizations (SaaS experience a plus)</li>
<li>Success record of developing and executing effective, high ROI demand generation campaigns</li>
<li>Ability to think strategically and drive execution in collaboration with multiple, geographically dispersed groups</li>
<li>Solid understanding of marketing automation tools, content management systems, web development platforms, and web analytics tools to ensure an optimal tech stack</li>
<li>Expertise in digital marketing best practices, including digital advertising and media, measurement and analytics, SEO, SEM, display advertising, and content syndication</li>
<li>Experience managing global websites – deep understanding of information architecture, user experience, web design, digital content strategy, localization, search, conversion optimization, testing, and analytics</li>
<li>Excellent project management skills with exceptional attention to detail and follow up</li>
<li>Exceptional relationship management skills – extensive experience working with international teams</li>
<li>BA/BS degree in marketing or communications required</li>
</ul>
