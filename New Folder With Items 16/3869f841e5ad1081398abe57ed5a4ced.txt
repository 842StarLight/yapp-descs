<h2>Data Analyst</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Primary duty is business analysis and reporting, and creation of decision making tools for retail store inventory support. Additional duties entail systems management, project management, process improvement, and coordination among external and internal partners.</p>
<ul>
<li>Perform data analytics of varying breadth/depth to assist with strategic partnership decisions between Supply Chain, Marketing and Sales</li>
<li>Lead project work that improves field service levels, drives out variability and cost throughout the supply chain, and enforces field compliance with policies</li>
<li>Ideate, design, and deliver new reports or changes to existing reports</li>
<li>Utilize technical skills to build operational and analytical models, processes, and reports that are flexible, scalable, and provide actionable takeaways.</li>
<li>Key skills preferred:</li>
<ul>
<li>Highly advanced to expert-level MS Excel skill</li>
<li>Familiarity with VBA or another programming language</li>
<li>Familiarity with SQL and/or MS Access (including the ability to write SQL queries by hand)</li>
<li>Familiarity with Sharepoint</li>
<li>Exposure to Tableau or similar visualization tools</li>
<br>
</ul>
</ul>
<br>
<br>
Required Skills<br>
<ul>
<li>Technical expertise regarding data models, database design development, data mining and segmentation techniques</li>
<li>Strong knowledge of and experience with reporting packages (databases, programming, etc.)</li>
<li>Strong analytical skills with the ability to collect, organize, analyze, and disseminate significant amounts of information with attention to detail and accuracy</li>
<li>Adept at queries, report writing and presenting findings</li>
<br>
</ul>
<br>
Required Experience<br>
<ul>
<li>3+ years of experience as a data analyst or business analyst</li>
<li>Knowledge of statistics and experience using statistical packages for analyzing large data sets (Excel, SPSS, SAS etc.)</li>
<li>BS in Mathematics, Economics, Computer Science, Information Management or Statistics or equivalent experience</li>
</ul>
