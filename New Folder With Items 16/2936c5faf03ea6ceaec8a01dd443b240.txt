<div>Job description</div>
<div>TravelCreed is a travel technology and services company, and rapidly growing as a leader in the travel industry. With clients across the globe, we are considered a leader in this specialized area of travel software applications. We are seeking an experienced lead generator in a technical field, ideally travel or other tourism education and/or experience. This is a highly visible position having direct contact with future and current clients. While the company is headquartered in San Jose, California, our branch offices and prospective clients are in various countries. This position is for candidates possessing the proper experience, professionalism, and dedication. As an entry-level lead generator, you will conduct market research, identify a target audience, make outbound calls to generate appointments and market TravelCreed’s products and services. You will be the first step in the prospecting process and our sales team relies on your persuasive abilities and creative methods of finding potential customers. Your primary tool will be the telephone, though you also might find new business through email and social media campaigns. You will be sourcing and reaching out to travel specialists and other decision makers daily. We need a confident, driven, self-starter who is professional and positive and not afraid of challenging work. This position can evolve into a full-time sales position with benefits for the most successful lead generators. If interested in this position, please submit your resume showing your previous experience and education.
</div>
