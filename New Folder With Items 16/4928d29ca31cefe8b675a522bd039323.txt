<h2>Quality Control Technician-Wharton, NJ</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p>Job ID: 154883</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
Safety, Integrity, Quality are the foundation or core values on which Tilcon New York Inc., a CRH company operates. Located in New York and New Jersey, Tilcon New York Inc. is an integrated materials company with multiple quarry locations, water terminals, heavy highway construction division, asphalt and recycle plants. Tilcon manufactures sells and ships, made in the USA, products of crushed stone, asphalt, sand, gravel and recycled materials throughout New York, New Jersey, and some areas of Pennsylvania. Growth opportunity, professional development and a culture of support and resources are provided to each employee for their personal growth and success within Tilcon New York Inc., and CRH.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<div><b>
Position Overview</b></div>
<div>
Responsible for performing quality control testing and monitoring of crushed aggregates, recycle materials and hot mix asphalt mixtures produced at their assigned location.<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<div><b>Roles and Responsibilities</b></div>
<div>
(Essential Duties and Functions)</div>
<ul>
<li>
Formally and visibly demonstrate support and understanding of CRH and Tilcon’s core values of Safety, Integrity, Quality</li>
<li>
Work safely, complying with all OSHA and MSHA regulations and Tilcon environmental, health and safety policies and procedures.</li>
<li>
Perform quality control testing as required by State and local agencies, private, and in accordance with Tilcon procedures ensuring the highest quality material is being produced.</li>
<li>
Provide test data to plant manager and other pertinent plant personnel in a timely manner.</li>
<li>
Provide process and mix adjustment recommendations as required.
</li>
<li>Maintain current State agency QC/QA certifications, upholding all aspects of the quality control program staying in compliance with agency specifications.</li>
<li>
Maintain accurate and up-to-date data base and record logs.
</li>
<li>Support and assist internal customers (sales, construction, estimating, operations and senior management) and external customers by reviewing and providing detail information/insight on material specifications and test results as requested.</li>
<li>
Regular and timely attendance is required.</li>
<li>
Additional duties as assigned or required.</li>
</ul>
