<h2>Customer Success Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p><b>What You Will Do</b></p>
<p>
The Customer Success Manager is responsible for managing the customer account after initial purchase of perpetual or SaaS products. Throughout the account management process, the Customer Success Manager uses a variety of technologies and sales methodology to onboard new customers, create opportunities for upsell/cross-sell, and maintain established retention rates for both maintenance and SaaS renewal.<br>
</p>
<p><b>
Your Day to Day Essential Duties</b></p>
<p></p>
<br>
<ul>
<li>
Serve as the main point of contact and trusted advisory to assigned customers.</li>
<li>
Develop and maintain relationships with assigned customers including understand the customer’s needs, short/long term goals, and their business</li>
<li>
Collect analytics to personalize the customer experience</li>
<li>
Maintain account strategies to drive adoption and expansion of our product and services</li>
<li>
Ask for feedback to enhancing the customer experience</li>
<li>
Acquire and maintain a working knowledge of our products</li>
<li>
Receive and respond to calls and emails from customers in relation to requests, questions, and escalations. Ensuring they are handled in a timely manner and resolutions are acceptable and satisfactory to the customer.</li>
<li>
Develop and maintain a well-coordinated internal relationship with key decision makers within the organization</li>
<li>
Implement customer engagement strategy with assigned customers including outbound communication schedule, feedback, and renewal strategies with all assigned accounts.</li>
<li>
Field cancellation requests and proactively collect on past due accounts</li>
<li>
Meet and grow product upsell and cross-sell and maintain established renewal rate</li>
<li>
Other duties as assigned</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
What You Bring to the Team</b><br>
</p>
<ul>
<li>
Bachelor’s Degree + 1 year of B2B sales exp OR Associates Degree and 2 years of account management or sales experience OR HS Diploma + 3 years of account management or sales experience OR 1 year of successful experience in a ConstructConnect role.</li>
<li>
Perpetually exhibits the ConstructConnect values.</li>
</ul>
<p><br>
<b>
Physical Demands and Work Environment:</b></p>
<p></p>
<br>
<ul>
<li>
Must be able to remain in a stationary position 50% of the working day</li>
<li>
Needs to occasionally move about the office to communicate with staff and attend meetings</li>
<li>
Constantly operates a computer</li>
<li>
Frequently communicates verbally with peers and management</li>
<li>
Must be able to analyze detail close-up</li>
</ul>
