<h2>Area Visual Merchandising Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>The iconic look of our stores starts with you. In partnership with the Regional Visual Merchandising Manager and Area Manager, you will work with the visual merchandising team to set the stage for an exceptional visual experience in our stores. Through planning, coordination, creativity and execution, you utilize your eye for design and energetic attitude to lead the region to implement the company design and visual vision in order to maximize company sales growth and profitability. Mentorship is also key. You lead, educate and empower the store and visual teams in the area to plan, communicate, and execute a strong visual message while encouraging professional development.</div>
<div></div>
<div>
This is an Area position supporting our stores throughout the Mid-Atlantic Area. The candidate could sit in our Philadelphia, Atlanta or North Carolina markets.</div>
<br>
<div><b>
Primary Responsibilities:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
Execute the regional design and visual strategy, in coordination with the Area Manager, to drive sales through store displays and all other aspects of merchandising.</li>
<li>
Lead Store Managers and Assistant Store Managers to execute and maintain the design and visual strategy.</li>
<li>
Lead the execution of product presentation and displays to ensure merchandising is consistent with the company vision.</li>
<li>
Collaborate with Store Managers to train, coach, and manage Assistant Store Managers.</li>
<li>
Accountable to visual budgets and fixture replenishment, repairs, and maintenance in assigned stores.</li>
<li>
Support Store Managers and Area Managers in all aspects of training, development, and performance management of store and leadership teams.</li>
<li>
Execute tests of new concepts, merchandising initiatives, fixtures and visual elements and provide feedback to corporate partners.</li>
</ul>
<div><b>
Knowledge, Skills, and Abilities:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
Clear understanding of the company visual merchandising concepts and standards and how to use them effectively and creatively to drive sales.</li>
<li>
Ability to coordinate Area implementation of seasonal merchandising programs.</li>
<li>
Demonstrated understanding and awareness of all current product and product information.</li>
<li>
Awareness of all intranet communications, current advertising, promotions and other marketing initiatives, and the appropriate way to communicate all pertinent information to store associates.</li>
<li>
Knowledge of stock availability and how it impacts optimum use of product to increase sales in stores.</li>
<li>
Ability to managemedesign expenses, budgets, fixture replenishment, repairs, and maintenance.</li>
<li>
Awareness of loss prevention, security, safety, and human resources policies and procedures.</li>
<li>
Ability to organize the visual merchandising aspect of Area store expansions, renovations and physical upkeep of stores in coordination with the store leadership.</li>
</ul>
</div></div>
