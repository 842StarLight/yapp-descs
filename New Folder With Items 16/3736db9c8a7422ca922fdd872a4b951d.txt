<h2>Business Intelligence Portfolio Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Phenomenal opportunity with an industry leader!</b></p>
<p></p>
<p>
We started in 1952 when Les Schwab bought one tire store in Central Oregon. Since then, we have remained true to Les Schwab's vision of World-class Customer Service and unsurpassed benefits and opportunity to our employees. Today, we have over 400 locations including Retail Tire Stores, Distribution Center, Production, Transport, Equipment, and Headquarters. As the largest tire dealer in the western states, you will have a meaningful role as our Business Intelligence Portfolio Manager leading two teams comprised of data scientists, data analysts and data engineers to turn data into actionable insights. We look for candidates who are not satisfied with the status quo, are intellectually curious and confident in their abilities. If you are looking to join a dynamic, exciting and growing leader, consider Les Schwab!</p>
<p></p>
<p>
You will report to the Vice President of Information &amp; Digital Services and will be based out of our headquarters in Bend, OR.</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>
About the opportunity</b></p>
<p>
Your teams will support revenue generation, company growth and profitability by working closely with line-of-business leaders and their teams in Marketing, Sales/Operations, Real Estate, Supply Chain, Digital, Finance &amp; HR. The BI Insights team will collaborate with line-of-business counterparts to develop valuable insights, meaningful KPI’s, predictive/prescriptive models and decision-making tools. The BI Platform team will architect, engineer and support foundational components such as data management processes, data lake, data warehouse, master data management, data integration and reporting tool sets.</p>
<p>
As co-chair of a cross-department steering committee, this role facilitates collaboration across all levels of the company from executive to the operational/transactional levels. You will work with others to define/understand data, insight and reporting needs and propose services and solutions to meet requirements. You will oversee the design, development and delivery of these services including one time data analyses, continuous insight “products”, prototyped tools and production systems that span reporting, data cleansing/management and Master Data Management.</p>
<p></p>
<p>
The primary objectives of this role are to:<br>
Contribute to company success by supporting sales growth and solid profitability<br>
Ensure service level targets are defined/met and investment levels are meeting the desired objectives.<br>
Build and maintain a strategic data assets platform that continuously improves the quality of and access to data by BI scientists/analysts and LOB business analysts.<br>
Build and manage a high-performing team by inspiring, mentoring and leading individual employees and contractors.</p>
<p></p>

Primary responsibilities/functions you are responsible for:</b><br>
Strategic Management</p>

<li>
Develop associated plans and continuous improvement goals - directing team activities to accomplish goals.</li>

<p>
