<h2>Business / Community Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Overview
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div><b>Who We Are</b></div>
<div>
We are growing! We are hiring! We are <b>South Oxford Management LLC</b>, a full-service property management company with a portfolio of 48 communities across Texas, Florida, Georgia and Connecticut with 12,164 apartment units (and counting). We are dedicated to offering an exceptional experience to our residents and team members. Our owners bring years of expertise in the industry to <b>South Oxford Management</b>, assuring quality in every aspect of our business. From asset management to leasing, we are passionate about making positive experiences for people. From our residents to our team members, we are committed to putting people first in everything we do. If this sounds like a team you would like to join, apply today.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div><b>
How You Will Contribute</b></div>
<div>
The <b>Community Manager</b> is the power house that drives the community. They manage and coordinate people, activities and available resources to provide a seamless community to residents, future residents, visitors and team members. The Community Manager understands the busy nature of the community and is willing to assist at a moment’s notice.</div>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div><b>Things You Will Do</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
Hire, train and supervise all team members though a positive mentoring approach</li>
<li>
Take ownership and lead by example</li>
<li>
Empower team members to take on new challenges and bring new ideas to the forefront</li>
<li>
Provide comprehensive feedback to all team members, document and facilitate disciplinary procedures as necessary</li>
<li>
Conduct on-going training with team members</li>
<li>
Prepare and execute community budget</li>
<li>
Prepare weekly and monthly reports</li>
<li>
Ensure that rent is collected and posted in a timely manner and bank deposits are made immediately and reported to the home office</li>
<li>
Maintain open communication with vendors and contractors concerning work scheduling, billing, vendor relations and certificates of insurance</li>
<li>
Submit invoices for payment promptly</li>
<li>
Suggest rental rate recommendations to Regional Manager</li>
<li>
Report liability and community incidents to home office</li>
<li>
Oversee and approve lease files</li>
<li>
Assume responsibility for office and model apartments opening on schedule and in excellent condition</li>
<li>
Provide outstanding customer service to residents and future residents</li>
<li>
Take pride in the apartment community; visually inspect and address areas of concern, keep community clean and report service needs to maintenance team members</li>
<li>
Maintain an organized environment even during busy times</li>
<li>
Answer incoming phone calls and handle accordingly</li>
<li>
Build rapport with residents and future residents</li>
<li>
Walk and inspect community regularly, paying close attention to vacant apartments</li>
<li>
Think safety first and ensure that unsafe conditions are corrected promptly</li>
<li>
Willing to work flexible schedule including weekends</li>
<li>
Work closely with Maintenance Supervisor to monitor and schedule make-readies and other maintenance activities</li>
<li>
Update online advertising</li>
<li>
Know your market by being abreast of competition and surrounding area</li>
<li>
Communicate effectively with residents, future residents, visitors, vendors and team members while presenting a positive, professional image</li>
<li>
Perform additional duties as assigned by district or regional manager</li>
</ul>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Qualifications
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div><b>Things You Need</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
High School diploma or equivalent; bachelor’s degree preferred</li>
<li>
Three or more years of experience in property management with at least one year as a Community Manager</li>
<li>
Large site experience is required</li>
<li>
Background in supervision and successful track record for accomplishments</li>
<li>
Excellent verbal and written communication skills</li>
<li>
Proficiency in Microsoft Office (Word, Excel)</li>
<li>
Able to multitask and meet deadlines in a timely manner</li>
<li>
Willing to work flexible schedule including weekends</li>
<li>
Knowledge of Yardi or other industry software preferred</li>
<li>
A sharp, professional appearance</li>
<li>
Must be able to walk the property which includes climbing stairs</li>
<li>
Able to bend, stoop, squat, kneel, climb stairs, push, pull, reach, carry supplies and stand for extended periods of time</li>
</ul>
