<h2>Senior Sales Engineer & Team Lead</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Company Overview</b></p>
<p>
iboss is a cloud security company that provides organizations and their employees secure access to the Internet on any device, from any location, in the cloud. This eliminates the need for traditional security appliances which are ineffective at protecting a cloud-first and mobile world. Leveraging a purpose-built cloud architecture, backed by 110 patents and over 100 points of presence globally, iboss protects more than 4000 organizations worldwide. The Company is headquartered in Boston, MA, and has operations in Orlando, San Diego, London, and the Philippines.</p>
<p>
At iboss, we believe that exceptional employees are the key to our success. Our teams are small, diverse, nimble and highly empowered to drive excellence. Come be a part of the team that will transform the way cybersecurity is delivered!<br>
<br>
<b>Job Description</b><br>
<b>
</b><br>
We are looking for an exceptional Senior Sales Engineer &amp; Team Lead to grow and lead our sales engineering organization. You will lead our existing sales engineers and work closely with product management, engineering, and senior leadership to help close deals and execute strategic solutions for customers and partners. We are seeking a seasoned pre-sales leader with a proven track record of growing a sales engineering organization.</p>
<p><b>
Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Work with sales engineering team to define and scale the core sales engineering processes such as discovery, technical qualification, and proof-of-concept</li>
<li>
Work with senior leadership and clients to design, create, and present business solutions to increase client revenue growth</li>
<li>
Provide technical pre-sales subject matter expertise and mentoring sales engineers for customer meetings, webinars and product demos</li>
<li>
Perform remote and on-site product/solution proof-of-concepts, initial configurations, and product training</li>
<li>
Drive the development of account plans for larger clients</li>
<li>
Provide advice and support response to requests for proposals (RFPs), and present recommendations and associated benefits to clients</li>
<li>
Familiar with Sales Force and accurately tracking team reports and status updates</li>
<li>
Collaborate with a variety of internal functional areas to ensure solutions are implemented and supported in-line with client expectations</li>
<li>
Attend and present at trade shows and present on-site training</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Qualifications</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Excellent customer-facing presentation and communication skills</li>
<li>
Bachelor’s Degree in Engineering or a related field or equivalent experience</li>
<li>
7+ years of experience in sales engineering or systems engineering with at least 3 years managing and directing a sales engineering team</li>
<li>
Excellent knowledge and prior experience selling network security technologies including caches, firewalls, SSL/IPsec, VPN’s, DLP, anti-virus, spam, spyware solution, gateway and SaaS environments</li>
<li>
Thorough understanding of the sales life-cycle process</li>
<li>
Ability to simplify complex issues and articulate technical &amp; business solutions to a wide range of customer roles from senior executives (CxOs) to hands-on engineers</li>
<li>
Strong interpersonal skills with excellent project management skills</li>
<li>
Ability to influence technical decision makers and executive</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Requirements</b></p>

BS/MS degree in Engineering and/or Computer Science</li>
<li>

Solid understanding of networking, server, and web technologies</li>
<li>
Experience with IT management products and services</li>
<li>
Database query language (SQL) and report development is preferred</li>
<li>
Domain expertise with IT operations, application management and/or security/compliance is desirable</li>
</ul>
