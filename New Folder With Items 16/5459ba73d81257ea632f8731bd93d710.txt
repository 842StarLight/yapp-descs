<h2>Marketing Communications Operations Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Current Need
</b></p>
<p>We are seeking a Marketing Communications Operations Specialist to join our team in Richmond, VA.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Position Description
</b></p>
<p>McKesson Medical-Surgical is seeking a highly motivated Marketing Communications Operations Specialist to support business and marketing initiatives. This position is responsible for managing the flow of our Marketing Communications department print and mailing production requests and assisting in the optimization of day-to-day Marketing Communications activities. This position will require an individual that is detailed-oriented and able to work on multiple priorities at one time.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Duties and Responsibilities:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Supports the entire print and mailing production process related to Marketing Communications.
</li>
<li>Interfaces with a variety of third-party vendors to produce marketing collateral and direct mail projects intended to support Medical-Surgical campaigns or initiatives.
</li>
<li>Communicates with Marketing Communications managers daily as well as third-party vendors, manages schedules and timelines.
</li>
<li>Supports the Marketing Communications Operations Manager during execution of special projects
</li>
<li>Helps research processes that ensure best practices in campaign and capacity management
</li>
<li>Helps with software and hardware subscriptions and vendor negotiations
</li>
<li>Helps evaluate new technologies to improve and optimize marketing team performance
</li>
<li>Project managing various efforts around Marketing Communications and Marketing
</li>
<li>Create and manage Standard Operating Procedures (SOPs) for the department
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Minimum Requirements:
</b><br>
Entry level experience in marketing/project management
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Critical Skills:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>1+ years experience with Project Management
</li>
<li>1+ years experience using Adobe Acrobat
</li>
<li>Advanced proficiency with Microsoft Office (Word, Excel, PowerPoint)
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Additional Skills:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Excellent time management
</li>
<li>Ability to prioritize tasks in order to juggle and track multiple projects in different stages in the production process.
</li>
<li>Team player
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Education
</b><br>
4-year degree in Marketing or related field
<br>
<br>
<b>Physical Requirements
</b><br>
General Office Demands
<br>
<br>
<b>Must be authorized to work in the US. Sponsorship is not available for this position </b>.
</p>
