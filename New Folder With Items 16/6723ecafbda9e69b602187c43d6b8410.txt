<h2>Commercial Account Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>POSITION SUMMARY</b></p>
<p>The Commercial Account Manager (CAM) contacts potential customers through both inbound leads and aggressive self-generation to develop flooring sales opportunities in commercial settings such as (but not limited to) residential and commercial property management, building owners, hotels, restaurants, or office buildings. The CAM works with customers to determine the correct flooring products and applications for specific projects. The CAM continually prospects for viable new opportunities while developing a key awareness of what is happening within the market’s geographic area. This position reports to the market Commercial Market Manager and has an annual sales target of $2M</p>
<p><b>Duties and Responsibilities: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Consistently demonstrates leadership traits consistent with the Empire Way</li>
<li>Takes proactive action to drive increased sales performance in order to achieve commercial flooring sales objectives.</li>
<li>Contacts and cultivates relationships with local representatives of National Accounts in various industries to drive flooring sales.</li>
<li>Possesses commercial flooring product knowledge in order to be able to sell the best product to the customer and answer their questions.</li>
<li>Conducts research through avenues such as the web, periodicals, and networking to determine prospective clients</li>
<li>Attends networking events to cultivate and develop relationships.</li>
<li>Works with potential customers to layout, design, &amp; coordinate flooring selections</li>
<li>Maximizes existing Empire relationships locally.</li>
<li>Ensures 100% of sales activity is recorded in the Customer Relationship Management (CRM) system</li>
<li>Utilizes the PQ software per Empire Today corporate requirements</li>
<li>Up to 80 % travel to various locations, including markets or customers.</li>
<li>Provides regular &amp; relevant reporting to local, regional and national sales management on field activities</li>
<li>Assists in the development of the commercial focused 1099 sales associates in the designated market area.</li>
<li>Other duties as requested or assigned.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Knowledge and Skills Required: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>HS Diploma/GED, Bachelor’s degree in Sales/Marketing or general business is preferred</li>
<li>3-5 years experience in a related sales background with focus on commercial flooring sales preferred</li>
<li>Knowledge of various flooring products such as carpeting, hardwood, laminate, etc.</li>
<li>Excellent customer service and sales skills</li>
<li>Strong ability to communicate effectively verbally and in writing with various levels of management</li>
<li>Computer skills including MS Office (Outlook, Word, Excel, PowerPoint)</li>
<li>Flexibility in a rapidly changing work environment</li>
<li>Ability to work independently and be a self-starter as well as a work in a team environment</li>
<li>Ability to multi-task and delegate</li>
<li>Ability to adapt quickly and create interim solutions until permanent tool/process are in place</li>
<li>Strong commitment to quality service</li>
<li>Highly motivated with a sense of urgency</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Physical Abilities Required</b></p>
<ul>
<li>The ability to physically and mentally perform the essential functions of the position, with or without reasonable accommodation</li>
<li>This position is an outside sales position with 20% of the time spent in an office/cubicle setting. The noise level ranges from moderate to high with travel to customer locations.</li>
</ul>
