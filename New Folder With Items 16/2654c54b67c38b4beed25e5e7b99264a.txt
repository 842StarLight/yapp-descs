<h2>2020 Associate Product Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<div>
<div>Textron Specialized Vehicles Inc. is a leading global manufacturer of golf cars, utility and personal transportation vehicles, snowmobiles, side-by-sides, all-terrain vehicles, professional turf-care equipment, and ground support equipment. Textron Specialized Vehicles markets products under several different brands. Its vehicles are found in environments ranging from golf courses to factories, airports to planned communities, and theme parks to hunting preserves.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
Responsibilities:</div>
<ul><li> Establish and execute on product strategy, road map, and feature definition of products and product lines.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Directs marketing research efforts to determine profitability and feasibility of new product ideas for all TSV aftermarket brands.
</li></ul>
<ul><li> Identify and define innovative new product opportunities that serve the needs of the TSV dealer base and end consumers.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Help to define gaps in product portfolio, strategize and recommend solutions.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Rationalize product offerings through analysis of sales and inventory data.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Understand market drivers of demand and sensitivities by developing relationships and maintaining open communication with key industry contacts, distributors, dealers and customers.
</li></ul>
<ul><li> Create dynamic business cases, sales tools, product presentations and marketing collateral to drive product knowledge, channel acceptance and sales productivity.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Create presentations on market research, product training and new product recommendations.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Assist with developing marketing materials such as catalogs, product fliers, and websites.</li></ul>
