<h2>Inside Sales Representative - Adobe Federal</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>The challenge
</b></p>
<p>The Inside Sales Executive (ISE) position is for a high-energy, aggressive individual to penetrate Enterprise Public Sector Government agencies and respond to marketing leads about Adobe solutions. The objective of the ISE is to create viable high-quality sales opportunities. You will be responsible for all aspects of the Adobe Experience Cloud.
</p>
<p></p>
<p>This role is based in the McLean, VA office.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>What you’ll do
</b></p>
<p>We are looking for a polished candidate who is able to manage multiple relationships, multiple customers, and dozens of projects at a time, paying attention to the details and representing Adobe in a professional manner to the customer. You will be assigned to 5-7 field AEs.
 </p>
<p></p>
<p><i>Existing Accounts
</i></p>
<ul>
<li>Know all aspects of the account, players, subsidiaries divisions, etc.
</li>
<li>Know their business. What are the drivers?
</li>
<li>Where are they going this year? Over the next 5 years, etc.?
</li>
<li>Which divisions and subsidiaries are using which products?
</li>
<li>Understand AE roadmap for getting additional Adobe products in their respective agencies.
</li>
<li>Be proactive, not waiting for the AE to take the lead in strategy
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><i>Greenfield accounts
</i></p>
<ul>
<li>Identify and stack rank with the AE all green field accounts and agree on prospecting approach
</li>
<li>Include as quasi-greenfield any subsidiaries or divisions of the existing named accounts and agencies
</li>
<li>Actively seek opportunities for pipeline growth
</li>
<li>Government: know the contract and know the usage so you can drive the re-contract/true-up
</li>
<li>Lead generation – Need to understand and how to prospect
</li>
<li>Customer Satisfaction
</li>
<li>Expiring consulting and training
</li>
<li>Working smaller or untouched divisions/departments/subsidiaries
</li>
<li>Measurables: Sourced Opportunities, Net New Opportunities created.
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>What you need to succeed
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Understanding of United States federal and/or state &amp; local governments preferred.
</li>
<li>Strong working knowledge of MS Office Suite - Word, Excel, PowerPoint. Prior experience with Salesforce.com.
</li>
<li>Strong written and verbal communication skills.
</li>
<li>BS/BA or equivalent experience.
</li>
<li>US Citizenship required.
</li>
</ul>

</p>
