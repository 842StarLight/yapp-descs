<h2>Enterprise Customer Success Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>
<div>Works collaboratively with SAI enterprise customers to ensure a positive return on investment from SAI’s Acadia® software platform and to expand the use of Acadia within the account.</div>
<div>
</div>
<div>
SAI is a full-spectrum technology consulting and software company serving commercial, higher education and healthcare clients across North America. From our office in Hunt Valley, Maryland we provide enterprise class software products and digital consulting services. We wake up every day focused on helping our clients solve complex enterprise problems with world class technology – all with the goal of helping clients thrive.</div>
<div>
</div>
<div>
Organizations everywhere strive to identify best practices and integrate them into their daily operations, but until now, doing so successfully has remained an elusive goal.<b> </b>SAI’s Acadia Performance Platform breaks new ground as a software solution that provides just-in-time training and procedural information to employees, anytime, anywhere, on any web-enabled device. It is a simple but powerful management tool that allows for continual performance monitoring and improvement.</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
You will build relationships with our Acadia customers to ensure the best possible experience from the initial engagement of prospects through customer onboarding and expansion.</div>
<div>

You will be working with an experienced team of software and consulting industry professionals as we continue to grow a significant user base among Fortune 500 Banking, Manufacturing and Healthcare customers.</div>

<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Essential Roles and Responsibilities</b></h3>
<div><ul>
<li>
Support the sales process through interactions with prospective clients, helping them analyze problems and develop solutions</li>
<li>
Deliver online or in-person product demonstrations to multiple stakeholders during the sales process</li>
<li>
Work with members of the business development and executive team to develop the scope and approach for new customer projects</li>
<li>
Plan for and deliver successful customer onboarding experiences</li>
<li>
Optimize the customer experience by providing customer coaching and training online and in person</li>
<li>
Analyze customer usage and behavior, updating customer and internal stakeholders on customer progress and results through quarterly business reviews with Customer Champions and senior Customer Executives</li>
<li>
Develop detailed expansion plans to solve client business challenges and document ROI of Acadia</li>
<li>
Develop and execute client specific playbooks to create and expand usage and revenue over time across targeted accounts</li>
<li>
Serve as a customer advocate in our software product management activities to help capture and refine new product requirements</li>
<li>
Remain engaged with customers to solicit feedback and deliver information about our products and optimize retention, upsell and cross sell activities</li>
</ul></div>
</div></div>
