<h2>IT Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Overview<br>
</b></h2>
<div></div>
<div><div><div>
<div>Copaken Brooks, LLC is a full-service commercial real estate firm headquartered in Kansas City and serving the Midwest. The company’s full suite of services includes investment acquisition and sales, tenant representation and HQ relocations, condominium management, property management, asset management, development, leasing (office, retail, medical, industrial and underground), and construction management. We have over 90 years of proven experience in playing a major role in where millions of people go to work, shop, and live.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
We are currently seeking an<b> IT Manage</b>r to lead and oversee all IT systems and associated enterprise for Copaken Brooks properties and operations<b>.</b>
</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
This supervisory position will be responsible to maintain operations of all production hardware, software and networking systems, identify and integrate new enabling technologies to support the business strategy and drive competitive advantage, and establish technology vendor relationships.
</div>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<ul>
<li><b>Advises CB management on IT solution needs and requirements to support business growth and evolution.</b></li>
<li>
Maintain all essential IT operations and components across several local properties for the company, including email, phones, internet, LAN/WAN, wireless networks, database, laptops, desktops, software, hardware, backup/continuity/disaster recovery, security tools, operating systems, data, and servers</li>
<li>
Test, troubleshoot and resolve all IT-related issues in timely manner</li>
<li>
Mitigate technology risks by maintaining IT business continuity and disaster recovery plans</li>
<li>
Maintain technologies and procedures to mitigate a security/data breach of company information and systems</li>
<li>
Monitor, evaluate, recommend, install/upgrade and maintain IT network, hardware and software components, as necessary</li>
<li>
Respond after-hours (as needed) to resume system/network uptime if enterprise technical issues occur</li>
<li>
Negotiate IT agreements and manage associated vendor relationships</li>
<li>
Continuously analyze IT processes, technologies, services and vendors to identify improvement opportunities</li>
<li>
Develop/execute company IT strategy</li>
<li>
Develop/manage annual IT capital and operating budget</li>
<li>
Maintain/revise IT policy for the organization</li>
<li>
Contribute technology expertise toward major projects that improve/extend company operations</li>
<li>
Maintain/administer confidential information and access to all data stored on workstations and servers</li>
<li>
Any additional duties as assigned</li>
</ul>

<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Qualifications
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree in Information Technology, Computer Science, or equivalent education</li>
<li>
5+ years of proven experience in hands-on cross-functional IT operations roles</li>
<li>
Strong hands-on proficiency with IT infrastructure components, including Microsoft Windows, Microsoft Office/Office 365, Microsoft Exchange, Active Directory, SQL Server and related RDBMS database/data storage administration, firewalls and network security, internet/intranets, distributed network infrastructure, Citrix, routing/switching, wireless devices, and phone systems</li>

Ability to identify, troubleshoot and resolve IT-related problems quickly and effectively</li>

<li>
Strong written and oral communications skills</li>
<li>
Detail-oriented with strong organization skills</li>
<li>
