<h2>Sales Executive- Pacific Northwest</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">Transmit Security is an cross-channel identity and orchestration platform designed to simplify, accelerate, and reduce the cost of identity-related projects, such as authentication (password replacement), authorization, fraud prevention, and new account opening (identity verification).
<br><br>
Transmit is hiring a Sales Account Executive superstar to grow our team. We are seeking an experienced, quota carrying sales performer who is looking to focus their skills to drive the effective use of security technology for the safety and security of our customers. The Account Executive is responsible for energetically championing Transmit's Security product. Additionally, this role is responsible for acquisition of new customers within the assigned territory. If you have a passion for security and an interest in how it can facilitate positive change, combined with security technology sales experience, we want to talk with you.
<br><br>
<b>Responsibilities:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Development of a strategic sales plan to effectively and efficiently cover the accounts within the assigned territory.</li>
<li>Manage lead qualification and conversion from interested prospects.</li>
<li>Aggressively prospect and generate new relationships within named accounts.</li>
<li>Consulting with and advising large, strategic Customers resulting in increased adoption, success and deploy Transmits product.</li>
<li>Some travel, as required, to prospects, customers or marketing events within territory (40-50% travel).</li>
</ul>
<b>Required Skills/Experience:</b><br>
<ul>

<li>Track record of success in Security Software or related business application sales</li>
<li>Detailed knowledge of and passion for SaaS applications</li>
<li>Strong technical background.</li>
<li>Strong interpersonal and presentation skills.</li>
<li>Exceptional verbal and written communication skills.</li>
<li>Experience with web based conferencing tools such as Go-To-Meeting, WebEx, etc.</li>
<li>Ability to work in a fast paced, team environment.</li>
<li>A passion for security work.</li>
<li>Bachelors Degree highly preferred.</li>
</ul>
