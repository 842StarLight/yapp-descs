<p><b>The Opportunity:</b></p>
<p>The United States and Canada are in the middle of a communications boom. The drive for more bandwidth is fueling amazing growth in the deployment of fiber and wireless networks. ikeGPS is enhancing the way communication companies like AT&amp;T, Verizon and Crown Castle deploy fiber and wireless networks. This growth is putting tremendous pressure on the Joint Use Departments of electric utilities across the country as they attempt to keep up with growing demand for attaching to their utility poles. At ikeGPS our mission is to help communication companies attach faster and make it easier and safer for utilities to review and approve new attachment permitting applications. We accomplish this by establishing an IKE Analyze Record as the common denominator between both parties. An IKE Analyze Record means the communication companies can complete the applications in less time while providing better information that utilities trust and can review from the safety and convenience of their office. ikeGPS is seeking an experienced sales professional that also has experience working with the Joint Use department of one or more utilities. Were also interested in talking to you if youre currently a Joint Use professional whos interested in growing your earning potential by trying sales and spreading the word about a better way. If one of these descriptions sounds like you for our Sales Account Executive role, wed love to talk to you about joining the ike team.</p>

<p><b>Sales Account Executive (Utilities Joint Use Sales Manager)</b><b> Role:</b></p>
<p>The Sales Account Executive (Joint Use Sales Manager) will be a key member of the Americas sales team for the Communication &amp; Utilities Business Unit focused on promoting and selling our IKE Fast Lane solution with an emphasis on securing opportunities with utilities that outsource the review of inbound new attachment permitting applications. This member of the team will be responsible for qualifying leads generated from our unique and continual approach to Demand Generation and develop resulting opportunities to close with the help of our team of Solution Engineers. The position will be supported by marketing, subject matter experts and an Enterprise Sales Team that is key to generating leads from our customers. The Sales Account Executive (Joint Use Sales Manager) will report to the Director of Sales for Electric Utilities.</p>
<br>
