THE ROLE
<br>Each Dentist on the HENRY team will be part of the overall patient and team experience. Dentists will oversee the clinic, directing Hygienists and Dental Assistants, as well as treating patients. Each Dentist will be expected to provide the highest level of care to patients, including but not limited to:
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Creating a high-end, welcoming service environment for patients</li>
<li>Supporting a friendly and positive work environment for colleagues</li>
<li>Providing and oversee patient treatments including cleaning, xray, exam, filling, crown, night guard, whitening, Invisalign, and other services that might be offered from time to time</li>
<li>Recommending patient treatments in the best interest of patient health and oral wellness</li>
<li>Inputting patient records into patient software management</li>
<li>Maintaining a clean, sterile, and on-brand working facility</li>
<li>Building relationships and expanding patient volume for the clinic through referrals and sales support efforts</li>
<li>Flexibility to work within our high-end mobile practice, and travel locally within designated territory</li>
</ul>
WHAT WE'RE LOOKING FOR
<br><br>

<li>Knowledge of ability to learn all systems (Denticon, Nomad x-ray system, iTero, scanner, etc.)</li>
