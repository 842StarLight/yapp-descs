<div><b>The Role</b></div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div>
</div>
<div>
This position will perform and deliver on routine and non-routine assignments for his/her assigned area(s) of responsibility independently in accordance with established procedures/guidelines. S/he will be responsible for processing quotes and pre-qualification of new business. This position aligns to support specific regions and provides support to sales and administrative functions across the business, as needed.</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div>
</div>
<div><b>Responsibilities</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Calculates and formats census data for input into the Quote Engine System.</li>
<li>Communicates and obtains from brokers any needed information such as rate verification, experience, census and other missing materials.</li>
<li>Communicates and provides a diverse range of information to brokers and clients on LFG service model, policies and procedures.</li>
<li>Communicates with multiple business areas demonstrating flexibility and responsiveness to deliver on customer needs.</li>
<li>Develops a comprehensive understanding of product information and guidelines to support brokers with general questions.</li>
<li>Follows up and confers with internal stakeholders to support marketing representatives.</li>
<li>Prequalifies potential new business by reviewing and entering information on appropriate system and reviewing underwriting guidelines while maintaining accuracy and quality.</li>
<li>Processes increasingly complex quotes and provides brokers with proposals after applying appropriate quoting parameters, pricing and discounting in accordance with established procedures and guidelines.</li>
<li>Provides additional support as needed with the sales office and across the enterprise.</li>
<li>Provides customer service at every touchpoint when greeting and interacting with internal and external customers in person, email and via the telephone in a professional courteous and helpful manner.</li>
<li>Reviews request for quotes and other pertinent materials submitted by brokers for completeness and accuracy.</li>
<br>
</ul>
