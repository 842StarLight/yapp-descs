<h2>VP, Shipper Strategy</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Wake up each day knowing that you are a part of something special. Something real. Something bold. What you do can make a difference. Coyote is an industry-leading transportation and logistics service provider that believes in doing the right thing every time. Every hour. Every minute. Every second. Are you looking for a place where you will have a role in shaping an industry? Are you Tenacious, True, Tribal, and Smart? Then it’s time for you to apply to join the pack.
</b></h2>
<p></p>
<p>The VP of Shipper Strategy is responsible for ensuring Coyote’s portfolio of service offerings &amp; the delivery of those offerings efficiently address the current and future needs for each respective Shipper segment – notably the SMB and Enterprise segments – and in doing so creates value by addressing common challenges that ripple across industries &amp; geographies.
</p>
<p></p>
<p>He/she will work exclusively through a Shipper oriented frame, informed by key inputs from Shipper Insights, research/competitive analysis, and external channels, to drive value to Shippers and differentiation for Coyote in the Global 3PL landscape.
</p>
<p></p>
<p>Tasked with fostering an environment of innovation and objective review of the solutions portfolio within the broader 3PL &amp; Shipper landscapes, he/she will work directly with Senior Leadership on a range of projects and inform critical decisions supporting growth initiatives, new products/services, cost reduction/efficiency efforts, and demand growth within the Coyote Marketplace.
</p>
<p></p>
<p>The VP will report to the <i>SVP, Strategy &amp; Innovation </i>and be instrumental in the attainment of Coyote’s strategic business objectives.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>TASKS &amp; RESPONSIBILITIES (include, but not limited to):
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Develop comprehensive intake method for diverse shipper products/services, technology &amp; other needs discovery
</li>
<li>Collaborate with F&amp;A to establish rational assumptions and forecast to define commercial success, timing and results tracking mechanism across all service lines and geographies
</li>
<li>Drive shipper adoption/growth in new and existing service lines, in the legacy analog and newer digital channels, across the broader shipper landscape
</li>
<li>Maintain a high level of connectivity &amp; work collaboratively with Sales, Operations and Marketing to ensure Coyote Shipper strategy remains relevant and aligned for success across all service lines and geographies
</li>
<li>Develop and integrate service line specific opportunity landscape, go-to market strategy, engagement model and investment roadmap into a comprehensive strategic plan to address shippers’ needs
</li>
<li>Drive diversification efforts across new and existing Shippers – broader solutions engagement across wider customer base
</li>
<li>Proactively build internal awareness of evolving shipper expectations and dynamically integrate into Coyote strategic plan
</li>
<li>Provide input in overall Strategy &amp; Innovation thought leadership
</li>
<li>Execute projects and/or discrete workstreams and present findings to Senior Leadership
</li>
<li>Collaborate with a counterpart(s) on the carrier side to align expectations, prioritization, and complementary strategies where it makes sense
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>ESSENTIAL SKILLS, CHARACTERISTICS, &amp; EXPERIENCE:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree required
</li>
<li>5+ years in Sales, Operations or Consulting role within the Transportation Industry
</li>
<li>An individual who brings forward outside-in thinking that challenges status quo and provides objective views of opportunities/risks
</li>
<li>Ability to synthesize information into actionable insights and develop an expert level of knowledge of all service lines across all geographies
</li>
<li>Strong project management, communications and ability to work cross-functionally and manage competing interest while developing a reasonable level of expertise across all service lines and geographies
</li>
<li>Problem solving ability and issue resolution skills
</li>
<li>Experience in working within fast-paced &amp; dynamic business environment
</li>
<li>Ability to manage and lead a growing team as the job may dictate over time
</li>
<li>Entrepreneurial and competitive spirit
</li>
<li>Strong organization and attention to detail
</li>
</ul>
