<p><b>
THE ROLE</b><b>: Jr. Account Enterprise Manager</b></p>
<p>
At <b>Vivint Smart Home</b> we are dedicated to redefining the home experience with intelligent products and services. We have combined our innovative home security products with Nest Thermostat, Space Monkey and Amazon Echo to continue leading the way in home automation as well as provide convenience and peace of mind. Imagine voice and mobile control for temperature, lights, locks, garage doors and more. Think seeing and talking to people in your home and at your front door when you are away.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p>The leaders in our <b>Inside Sales Channel Department</b> are excited to offer unique, lucrative careers with opportunities for growth and development. This position will be based out of Atlanta, Georgia but travel to our corporate office in Provo, Utah occasionally where we enjoy a large cafeteria, on-site gym, full-size basketball court, medical clinic and so much more. The department has an energetic team-focused culture where growth and development are always top of mind.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
As<b> Jr. Enterprise Account Managers</b> (EAM) we spend our days building and maintaining relationships with our channel partners; interacting with corporate partners to grow sales volume and drive inbound calls. We strive to share our excitement, product knowledge, and the true value of Vivint Smart Home products and services; through presentations, webinars, and events; ultimately helping individuals and our partners to fulfill their homes security and automation needs.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
The EAM role includes a compensation plan, with a <b>competitive base salary + commission</b>. This position is fulltime and eligible for <b>benefits.</b></p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
You would be an excellent candidate to join our team if</b> you are a competitive team player and know how to effectively train and motivate others. Those of us who are excelling in this role are detail oriented, sales-minded, autonomous, and self-motivated. We take advantage of the opportunities to visit our channel partners face to face across the country and spend about 70% of our time traveling.</p>
