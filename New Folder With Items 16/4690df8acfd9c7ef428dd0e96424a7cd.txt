<h2>Staff Accountant</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div><div><div><div>
<p>Summary:</p>
<p>The primary responsibility of this position is to be a key member of the financial accounting</p>
<p>organization, whose main purpose is ensure the primary financial statements – income statement,</p>
<p>balance sheet and cash flow – are accurately reflecting the results of the company and are</p>
<p>published in a timely manner.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>Essential Functions:</p>
<ul>
<li>Process daily cash receipts – timely and accurately apply incoming cash to customer accounts.</li>
<li>Maintain the Company’s fixed asset systems. Record monthly depreciation, asset additions and disposals.</li>
<li>Prepare and submit monthly sales tax returns for approximately 10 states.</li>
<li>Report daily on the Company’s cash position – reconcile checks within the Company’s ERP system and report daily available cash balance to the Controller.</li>
<li>Prepare general ledger account reconciliations.</li>
<li>Prepare month end journal entries.</li>
<li>General accounting skills in corporate-level accounting and reporting activities.</li>
<li>Support the annual financial audit and quarterly bank field examination process with external audit teams.</li>
<li>Perform special projects (i.e. ERP implementation) and studies on accounting and control matters as requested.</li>
<li>Every position within the finance team have a clear, uncompromising responsibility to ensure internal controls and business policies are followed.</li>
<li>Other duties as assigned</li>
</ul>
<p>Competencies:</p>
<ul>
<li>Effective oral and written communication skills.</li>
<li>Excellent interpersonal/customer service skills.</li>
<li>Ability to process computer data and to format and generate reports.</li>
<li>Proficient in office computer software use (Word, Excel, Outlook, etc.)</li>
<li>Excellent time management skills.</li>
<li>Handle multiple projects with deadlines in a fast-paced environment.</li>

<li>Project a professional demeanor as well as using tact when dealing with others.</li>
<li>Excellent organizational skills required</li>
<li>Work Successfully in a team environment</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p>Education &amp; Experience:</p>
<p>Accounting degree and 2-3 years of experience or equivalent combination of education and</p>
<p>experience.</p>
<br>
<p></p>

<p>hands and fingers to handle or feel; reach with hands and arms; and talk or hear. The employee</p>
<p>must regularly lift and/or move up to 5 pounds. Specific vision abilities required by this job include</p>
<p>close vision, color vision, and ability to adjust focus. Ability to perform the essential functions of the</p>
<p>job with or without reasonable accommodation.</p>

<p></p>

<p>General office environment. Work is generally sedentary in nature, but may require standing and</p>
