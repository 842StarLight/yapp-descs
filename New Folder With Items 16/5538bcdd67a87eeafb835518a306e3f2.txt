<h2>Inside Sales Representative</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Do you love to take initiative and thrive as part of a team?  Are you a great listener who is enthusiastic and resourceful, ready to get to the root of customer need in order to find a solution? If you are a seasoned salesperson with a hunger to succeed, join the Double A Solutions family as an Inside Sales Specialist. </p>
<p><b>THE COMPANY</b>:   <br>Double A Solutions has been offering innovative software solutions nationwide since 2003. Our mission is to develop cutting-edge SaaS software allowing companies to automate and eliminate the manual activities they may be doing today. <p>Learn more at https://www.doubleasolutions.net/ </p>
<p><b>THE POSITION</b>:  </p>
<p>We are looking for a full-time Inside Sales Representative with a drive to not just succeed, but create new opportunity and growth. An effective Inside Sales Representative has excellent problem-solving skills, knows how to use their resources, works well on a team, takes initiative, and is detail-oriented. In this role, you would be responsible for working with new and existing customers by:  </p>
<ul><li>Make outbound business to business calls to leads, referrals, inbound requests, and/or past customers</li></ul>
<ul><li>Make outbound business to business appointment-setting calls</li></ul>
<ul><li>Demonstrate our products to potential customers</li></ul>
<ul><li>Maintain accurate documentation of the calls and contacts that are made</li></ul>
<ul><li>Cross-sell our products to maximize the customer's process efficiency</li></ul>
