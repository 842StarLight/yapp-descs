<div><b>Alpha Data: Director, Business Development West</b></div>
<div></div>
<div>Alpha Data (formerly known as Buzz Angle Music) is hiring an experienced Business Development leader to onboard clients to our music analytics platform. Our ideal candidate will have strong experience in platform sales and a thorough knowledge of the music industry. This person must be able to creatively pitch our platform and navigate demo sessions with prospective clients. This person should have existing relationships across the music industry including record labels, management companies, PR firms, digital agencies and more.</div>
<div></div>
<div><b>Job Responsibilities:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Active engagement in all stages of the sales cycle, from prospecting and education to developing customer rapport, and from closing business to servicing and managing accounts</li>
<li>Partner with product and engineering to customize client experiences</li>
<li>Analyze customer use to optimize our platform</li>
<li>Conduct market research on competitors in adjacent markets</li>
<li>Persuasively communicate the benefits of Alpha Data’s platform over the competition through a clear presentation style and knowledge of the music industry</li>
</ul>
<div><b>Job Requirements:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>5+ years of experience selling digital/print advertising and integrated programs; recent online sales experience required</li>
<li>Strong knowledge of the LA market and willingness to travel to other western and central territory markets</li>
<li>Proficient at Power Point/ Keynote, Excel, and Salesforce</li>
<br>
</ul>
<div></div>

The company has established itself as an industry leader by providing granular sales tracking in its professional-tier product. Alpha Data is the data supplier for the <i>Rolling Stone</i> Charts</div>
