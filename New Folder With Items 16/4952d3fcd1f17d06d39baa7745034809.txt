<p>Description</p>

<div><div><div><div>
<div><div><div><b>Major Responsibilities
</b></div></div></div>
<br>
<div><div>
<ul>
<li>Processes and posts all prior days credit card transactions to individual customer accounts.</li>
<li>Processes and logs all credit card orders on a daily basis; releases approved orders.</li>
<li>Communicates order status to customers and Sales associates.</li>
<li>Makes appropriate changes to customer maintenance in ISCI.</li>
<li>Maintains and ensures accuracy of all customer credit card information.</li>
<li>Identifies and processes credits (returns) and additional credit card changes for NL invoices/advertising.</li>
<li>Understands and demonstrates Essendants' Core Values.</li>
<li>Performs other duties as assigned.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p>
</p>
</div></div>
<br>
<div><div><div><b>Skills/Knowledge Required
</b></div></div></div>
<br>
<div>
<div>
<ul>
<li>Ability to review and reconcile transactions/accounts.</li>
<li>Excellent verbal and written communication skills.</li>
<li>Solid analytical skills with the ability to research and gather pertinent data.</li>
<li>Intermediate level organizational skills with the ability to multi-task and manage work activities.</li>
<li>Strong attention to detail.</li>
<li>MS Office proficiency (Word, Excel, Access); FOCUS skills preferred.</li>
<li>Ability to readily understand and use credit card software.</li>
<br>
</ul>
