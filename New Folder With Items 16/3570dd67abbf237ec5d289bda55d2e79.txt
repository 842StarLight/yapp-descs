<h2>SameDay Bot Product Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>FedEx Office is building a same day autonomous delivery service powered by the FedEx SameDay Bot. The Product Manager is responsible for researching, planning, and leading the successful incubation and commercialization of this innovative, new market service and product. This role will be accountable for the financial success, platform definition, agile product ownership and customer satisfaction of the FedEx SameDay Bot service.</p>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
General Duties and Responsibilities</b></h2>
<p></p>
<div>
(This is a representative list of the general duties the position may be asked to perform, and is not intended to be all-inclusive)</div>
<ul>
<li> Under minimal supervision, support the creation and refinement of the FedEx SameDay Bot product strategy.</li>
<li> Identify, validate and refine key business model assumptions and business justifications through product incubation.</li>
<li> Act as business owner for agile sprint activities related to a set of product releases.</li>
<li> Collaborate with other SameDay Bot business owners on defining the roadmap of features, target markets and supported use cases.</li>
<li> Perform market sizing, competitive assessments and other market-based research.</li>
<li> Maximize profit and sales potential of the SameDay Bot service through effective pricing and go to market strategies.</li>
<li> Leverage customer-centered design methods to empathize and deeply understand customer needs and pain points.</li>
<li> Support the preparation and management of business plans and performance tracking processes to monitor and evaluate SameDay Bot-related service success.</li>
<li> Partner in negotiations for favorable terms with 3rd parties and vendors.</li>
<li> Present status of product strategies and ideas to all levels of management.</li>
<li> Collaborate effectively with cross functional, global stakeholders across the FedEx enterprise.</li>
<li> Manage and resolve concerns and/or feedback related to the product.</li>
<li> Resolve problems and makes recommendations related to highly complex and interrelated matters and projects.</li>
<li> All other duties as needed or required.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Minimum Qualifications and Requirements</b></h2>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li> Bachelor’s degree or equivalent experience</li>
<li> 5+ years related experience in a Product Management or related role</li>
<li> Experience in Product Management, preferably in logistics, transportation, autonomous delivery or related industry</li>
<li> Experience as Product Manager or Product Owner within the agile product development process</li>
<li> Experience with Customer-Centered Design methods</li>
<li> For new hires, must meet all FedEx Office employment qualifications in force at time of hiring</li>
<li> For current FedEx Office team members, must meet hiring criteria for the position and transfer requirements as outlined in the Team Member Handbook</li>
</ul>
