<h2>Store Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Overview
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div>The Store Manager (Head Coach) oversees and is responsible for the store's sales effort in each department in the store and supervises the store's overall operation to ensure efficiency and profitability. It is the Head Coach's responsibility to convey, monitor, and enforce the company's policies, goals, and standards to the store's sales team and keep communication lines open to the District Sales Manager and Store Support Center. The Head Coach represents the company in appearance, attitude and professionalism portrayed to employees, customers, and the community.</div>
</div></div></div>
