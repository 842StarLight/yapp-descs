<h2>Lead, Strategic Pricing</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<b>The Opportunity:</b><br>
Shutterstock is a leading global technology company that offers high-quality assets, tools and services through our creative platform. This platform inspires creators throughout the world by providing diverse content to businesses, marketing agencies and media organizations around the world. And the people behind the scenes are a super talented, fiercely passionate group of individuals with the unified goal of working together to build a stronger company for our customers, contributors and colleagues alike.
<br><br>
The Strategic Pricing Lead position will offer a unique opportunity to evolve and continuously optimize Shutterstock's global pricing framework, one that aligns with the company's strategy. You will initially be responsible for managing pricing for our Enterprise sales distribution channel across all content-types, including images, videos, and music. The objective is to maximize short and long term revenue and profitability of Shutterstock through pricing improvements. The ideal candidate is well-experienced in leading pricing programs and applying best practices in a B2B context. You will work closely with Sales, Sales Operations, Product and Research teams to understand the value drivers for each of our customer segments, maintain a high awareness of the competitive pressure, and drive process efficiencies. You also enjoy taking on new initiatives and leading them to a successful outcome, working cross-functionally with both technical and non-technical teams.
<br><br>
This position is a part of our Marketing &amp; Pricing organization, reports to the Director of Pricing, and is based out of Shutterstock's New York headquarters in the Empire State Building.
<br><br>
What You'll Do
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Carry out competitive research analyses, design customer surveys and lead global market research to help refine our product offering and pricing structure.</li>
<li>Lead quantitative analyses on pricing, customer segmentation and global customer buying patterns and organize them into digestible and actionable business insights that drive pricing decisions.</li>
<li>Report on the effectiveness of pricing initiatives throughout the company by measuring the end-to-end impact of all changes.</li>
<li>Partner with business, sales, and product teams on packaging, product, and deal strategies.</li>
<li>Develop and structure the process around pricing requests across all stakeholders and manage the approval process.</li>
<li>Work closely with Shutterstock's product, marketing, and operations teams to define the future of our pricing products and infrastructure.</li>
</ul>
What you have
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>BS/BA degree in a quantitative field such as Computer Science, Engineering, Mathematics, Statistics, Actuarial Science, Economics.</li>
<li>Minimum 7 years of experience in a quantitative or analytical role, with 4 years of pricing responsibility including (but not limited to) finance, consulting, eCommerce, technology, marketing.</li>
<li>Experience with building customer segmentation and retention models</li>
<li>Experience with implementing global pricing strategies.</li>
<li>Strong proficiency using SQL, Tableau, and/or Python.</li>
<li>Strong interpersonal skills.</li>
<li>Understands the Shutterstock brand, marketplace, and competition.</li>
<li>Thrive in a fast-paced environment.</li>
</ul>
Who You Are
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Innovative and Courageous: You have an entrepreneurial spirit, willing to take initiatives to tackle problems that might be outside of your day-to-day responsibilities.</li>
<li>Analytical and Curious: you are eager to continuously learn new skills and grow.</li>
<li>Collaborative and accountable: You are a team player with strong interpersonal skills, not afraid to go the extra miles to exceed expectations.</li>
<li>Honest and Passionate: You always look to do the right thing, and don't always choose the easy path.</li>
</ul>
