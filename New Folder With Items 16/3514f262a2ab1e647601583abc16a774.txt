<div>
Hillrom is a $2.8B leading worldwide manufacturer and provider of medical technologies and related services for the health care industry, including patient support systems, safe mobility and handling solutions, non-invasive therapeutic products for a variety of acute and chronic medical conditions, medical equipment rentals, surgical products and information technology solutions. Hillrom's comprehensive product and service offerings are used by health care providers across the health care continuum and around the world in hospitals, extended care facilities and home care settings to enhance the safety and quality of patient care.<div>

Description</b><div>

<div>
The Role</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div>Hillrom’s Care Communications is a product portfolio comprised of Hillrom® Nurse Call, Hillrom® Bed Connectivity Applications, Hillrom® Care Sign, Enterprise Reporting, Staff Locating and SmartConnect™ Integrations all focused on a vision of unifying the software platform to drive workflow efficiency, improving patient outcomes and strengthening patient engagement.<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<div>The Global Marketing Lead, Care Communications is a member of the Patient Support Systems (PSS) business unit Global Marketing leadership team with responsibility for all upstream marketing aspects for the rapidly growing and expanding Care Communications portfolio (nurse call, mobile communications, real-time locating systems, patient engagement, etc.). This is a highly visible, role and will manage a team of marketing professionals responsible for leading the development and execution of the global product portfolio strategy which encompasses thought leadership, new product development, product life cycle management, strategic pricing, branding, market access and development, geographic expansion and overall growth strategies. This is completed by obtaining input, insights and alignment with customers, market research, sales, commercial marketing, R&amp;D, business development, operations, strategic marketing, sourcing and external integration partners. S/he will manage multiple, highly-visible initiatives and cross-functional trade-offs is critical to maximize global investments and overall success.<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<div>Reporting Relationships</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div>The Global Marketing Lead, Care Communications reports directly to the Vice President, Global Marketing, Smart Beds, Monitoring, Mobility &amp; Communications within the Patient Support Systems (PSS) business unit. S/he will have 5 direct reports and a complete team of approximately 12.</div>
