<div>The Global Head of Client Services is responsible for providing both strategic and tactical leadership for a portfolio of Twitch’s advertising business. They serve as the senior partner and confidant for clients as well as internal cross-disciplinary teams to ensure quality of relationship and work product.<br>
The Global Head of Client Services will manage a team across the world including Client Service Managers who are responsible for day-to-day account operations and relationship management. They consistently demonstrate excellent team management skills and operational/business management capabilities in order to drive successful engagements.<br>
This role will be a valued contributor to the development of existing and future service offerings by representing a qualified and strong voice of the customer inside the organization. Success will be measured by customer service survey results, revenue growth and retention from existing customers, and reducing the value of makegoods.<br>
<br>
Responsibilities<br>
<ul>
<li>Team Leadership: Develop sound business practices built around strategy; goal setting; hiring/mentoring; advising and training. Foremost responsibility is achievement of team revenue targets. Lead advocate in coordinating internal resources for the preparation and delivery of client-centric product solutions.</li>
<li>Client Relations: Trusted advisor with ability to manage client expectations throughout the duration of client engagement. Strategize and develop best in-class practices to effectively create, communicate and deliver customized sales opportunities that address the client’s compelling events and help drive incremental sales growth. Retain existing clients by ensuring high satisfaction and quality.</li>
<li>New Client Onboarding: Effectively facilitate day-to-day management of accounts both internally and externally. Build relationships with key marketing and media decision makers within client organizations, while understanding landscape and gaining account penetration.</li>
<li>Revenue Acceleration: Drive team oriented revenue growth through highly-focused sales and development strategies. Establish best practices and drive team to substantial account revenue growth on YoY basis. Accountable to Client Development team and senior leadership for all client retention and performance-based upselling initiatives.</li>
