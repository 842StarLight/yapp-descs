<br>
<b>Position Summary</b><br>
<b>Our Local Campus Admissions Representative works to change the world, one life at a time, by helping students achieve their dreams through technical training. One should enjoy making connections and thrive in a fast-paced, goal driven, and compliance focused environment. This is the role for you if you are a competitive, charismatic, sales professional who has the ability to coach, collaborate and motivate others. This is a work hard/play hard environment where you will have monthly enrollment goals, and you'll be motivated by competing within your own team. You have been successful in sales because you are an active listener, you are able to work with and through different personalities, you don't let rejection knock you down, you're open to coaching and feedback, and you thrive in an environment where helping students achieve their goal of graduating from UTI is rewarded.</b><br>
<br>
<b>Principle Accountabilities &amp; Deliverables</b><br>
</p>
<ul>
<li>
<b>
Interfaces with students located within the local state area where campus resides; determines goals and motivations by using a consultative sales approach that includes open ended questions and active listening.</b><br>
</li>
<li>
<b>Effectively delivers the Educational Value Proposition for all UTI, NTI and MMI programs to prospective students.</b><br>
</li>
<li>
<b>Delivers program information via telephone and written materials, communicating school policies, processes, and procedures throughout the entire student life cycle.</b><br>
</li>
<li>
<b>Addresses issues or concerns regarding cost, financing, dress code, curriculum, tutoring, testing procedures, enrollment paperwork information, cancellation policy, or other related items to ensure student understands the entire UTI/NTI/MMI education decision.</b><br>
</li>
<li>
<b>Conducts follow-up calls throughout the student life cycle to ensure student success.</b><br>
</li>
<li>
<b>Participates in Campus events and tours prospective students as needed.</b><br>
</li>
<li>
<b>Maintains CRM database to company standards.</b><br>
</li>
<li>
<b>Proactively establishes relationships with other departments at the Campus.</b><br>
</li>
<li>
<b>Accountable to perform the functions of the job while remaining compliant with organizational policies as well as state, federal and other regulatory bodies</b><br>
</li>
<li>
<b>Stays informed of current trends and changes in the industry through participation in admission training meetings and required representative programs.</b><br>
</li>
<li><b>Other duties as assigned.</b></li>
</ul>
