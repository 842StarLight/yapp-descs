<p><b>Requirements:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Create and input purchase orders into system; manage purchase order log</li>
<li>
Purchase order creation, track orders and resolve delivery issues through communication with import office, distribution centers, vendors and stores</li>
<li>
Execute Price changes</li>
<li>
Assist in presenting advertising layouts, designs and concepts to marketing department</li>
<li>
Review / proof advertising layouts for design, content and pricing and communicate with marketing department</li>
<li>
Provide information for point-of-sale signage and maintain integrity of signage for ease of store execution</li>
<li>
Provide ad hoc analysis and summarize findings to support assortment planning, merchandise financial planning and store planning processes</li>
<li>
Provide vendor and style selling reports to business partners as required/needed</li>
<li>
Shop the competition</li>
<li>
Promote teamwork within the Merchant Team through daily communication, interaction and sharing of best practices with peers</li>
</ul>
