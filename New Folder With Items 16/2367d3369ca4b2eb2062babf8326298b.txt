<p><b>The challenge:
</b></p>
<p>The Cloud Technology organization builds platform and client services that are foundational building blocks for many other Adobe products and services. Areas of focus include: identity, security, cloud storage, e-commerce, workflow management, synchronization, customer facing web apps, scalability, infrastructure management and search, just to name a few. Our mission is to build highly scalable, highly available and highly resilient services that fulfill the business objectives of Adobe. Cloud Operations is responsible for ensuring Adobe’s cloud platform, products and ecosystems are reliable, scalable and secure. From core infrastructure to enabling "As-a-Service" capabilities, our dedicated team of architects, software engineers, program managers and cloud administrators ensure that Adobe’s customers are delighted by their experience with our products.
</p>

<p></p>
<p>The IT PMO &amp; Acquisitions Integration team was established to manage technology integrations which include Enterprise Architecture, Corporate infrastructure, Business applications and engineering environments. As a key member of this team, you will be responsible for driving key initiatives and strategies to scale, operate and integrate new capabilities. You will partner with architects, engineers and business teams in product management, marketing, sales and finance operations to deliver target and solution architectures aligned with IT strategies and business goals.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>What you'll do:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Responsible for executing Adobe’s initiatives with a focus on Enterprise Architecture
</li>
<li>Develop and strengthen relationships across all areas of Adobe, partnering with the EA Leadership sponsoring business units and functional executives to ensure initiatives are planned and executed effectively
</li>
<li>Drive day-to-day execution, including leading Initiative team meetings to ensure plans remain relevant and are being executed effectively
</li>
<li>Provide project management activities for the functional area including, but not limited to: project plan creation and updating, weekly status reporting, Governance reviews, etc.
</li>
<li>Oversee project financial planning and management; establishing budgets, tracking actuals to plan
</li>
<li>Drive continuous improvement by incorporating feedback and lessons learned into updated processes and tools (playbook)
</li>
<li>Assist in building presentations &amp; program management related documentation
</li>
<li>Enable “best in class” delivery process for Adobe
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>What you'll need to succeed:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>10+ years of Project Management
</li>
<li>Foundational understanding of technology architecture, solution engineering and strategy development
</li>
<li>Strong customer/stakeholder engagement focus
</li>
<li>Proven Agile project management experience
</li>
<li>Ability to quickly establish credibility and rapport with key stakeholders and work well in a highly collaborative environment
</li>
<li>Strong presentation skills, ability to facilitate meetings
</li>
<li>Technology platform: SAP (Financials, ECC and CI) and SFDC, MS Dynamics, other SaaS and ERP systems helpful
</li>
</ul>
