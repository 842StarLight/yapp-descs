<h2>Integration Engineer - Apple</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>As a rapidly growing global provider of IT solutions, we’ve expanded from Seattle to Sydney and beyond – with reach in over 80 countries around the world. Today’s top brands count on Zones and our world-class partners – Microsoft, Apple, Cisco, Dell, Lenovo, and more – to transform their enterprises. To support this transformation, Zones offers products and services across four Solution Environments: Workplace Modernization, Network Optimization, Data Center Transformation, and Security Fortification.<br>
<br>
<br>
<p></p>The Apple Integration Engineer will be responsible for developing and documenting key processes focused on Apple macOS/iOS product deployment. Communicate with key personnel in implementation and deployment of Apple product deployments. Provide direction, feedback, and Quality Control to assigned technicians during macOS/iOS deployments, and maintain up to date knowledge on key Apple product enhancements and changes.<br>
<p><b>Key Responsibilities</b></p>
<p>
The requirements listed below are representative of the knowledge, skill, and/or ability required. Reasonable accommodations may be made to enable individuals with disabilities to perform the essential functions.</p>
<p>
The <b><i>Integration Engineer</i></b>is primarily responsible for:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Develop and document essential Apple macOS/iOS deployment processes.</li>
<ul>
<li>
Review current macOS and iOS tools available to Zones.</li>
<li>
Review current processes (both documented and undocumented)</li>
<li>
Review industry best practices (including Apple provided documentation)</li>
<li>
Update and enhance Zones Deployment processes based on best practices.</li>
</ul>
<li>
Engage in meetings with key personal in preparation for Apple product deployments.</li>
<ul>
<li>
Actively participate in the Zones team in support of Apple deployments.</li>
<li>
Review submitted and approved Apple Statements of Work and identify skill sets necessary and voice any concerns to Integration Management.</li>
<li>
Lead Pre-Engagement calls with customers to ensure project scope is defined with customer leading to a successful deployment.</li>
<li>
Provide daily status of engagements to Project Manager to ensure timely reporting back to Apple.</li>
</ul>
<li>
Develop project specific documentation to support deployments.</li>
<ul>
<li>
Review supplied Statements of Work to determine the scope of the project.</li>
<li>
Utilizing the following sources, develop process documentation specific to project:</li>
<ul>
<li>
Original Statement of Work</li>
<li>
Apple supplied Best Practices</li>
<li>
Zones standard deployment processes</li>
<li>
Test deployments and customer feedback.</li>
</ul>
</ul>
<li>
Lead on site service delivery teams as needed</li>
<ul>
<li>
Act as main point of contact for all customer-facing deployments</li>
<li>
Train field technicians as needed</li>
<li>
Provide daily progress reports to service delivery manager and project manager</li>
</ul>
<li>
Provide training, guidance, feedback, and Quality Control during Apple Engagement</li>
<ul>
<li>
Train technicians on basic deployment processes</li>
<li>
Provide engagement-specific documentation as necessary.</li>
<li>
Review technician process during deployments to ensure processes are being followed</li>
<li>
Provide primary QC process for deployments.</li>
</ul>
<li>
Maintain high level Apple Certification and related product knowledge.</li>
</ul>
