<div>Security Technician Internship position will understand Security, Access Control, CCTV, Intrusion Alarm systems, Intercom systems or other building or electronic control systems.<br>
</div>
<p></p>
<p>Installation and service of security systems as required. This internship will assist with the servicing and installing complex systems on time and to the customer’s satisfaction. This intern will be responsible for troubleshooting and repairing customer systems, performing scheduled maintenance, performing warranty work.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<div><b>What do you get to do in this position?
</b></div>
<div></div>
<div>· Install and commission new systems
</div>
<div>· Collaborate with Engineers and Project Manager
</div>
<div>· Create, load, and test system databases
</div>
<div>· Check out and start up systems
</div>
<div>· Suggest system improvements, modifications, etc.
</div>
<div>· Work with sales personnel to develop multi-year customer system upgrade plans, including short- and long-term customer budgets and account plans.
</div>
<div>· Reporting to PM frequently, completes assignments, to insure timely and cost effective installation and completion of assigned projects or tasks.
</div>
<div>· Manages and provides documentation for the project file including correspondence, requests for information and timelines. High quality communication adhering to company standards.
</div>
<div>· Adheres to safety standards. High degree of regard to employee and subcontractor safety.
</div>
<div>· Maintains viable relationships with customers, consultants, prime contractors, subcontractors and trade personnel. Attends job progress meetings as directed. Provides detailed instructions to and interfaces with installers and subcontractors. Ensures that subcontractors readily understand expectations of the project.
</div>
</div>
<p></p>
<div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Qualifications
</b></h2>
<p><b>This job might be for you if:
</b></p>
<div>· Currently enrolled in degree program for a technical field, such as Electrical Engineering or IT
</div>
<div>· Basic understanding of Security, Access Control, CCTV, Intrusion Alarm systems, Intercom systems or other building or electronic control systems.
</div>
<div>· Knowledge of: Security technology equipment (Pelco, Continuum, AccessXpert, Genetec, Avigilon, Dvtel, Amag, Verint), electrical (low voltage), Information Technology, TCP/IP networks, servers, domains, firewalls, routing, windows and mobile Operating systems.
</div>
<div>· Demonstrated ability to develop, maintain and improve customer relationships and provide highest level of customer service.
</div>
<div>· Excellent verbal and written communication skills including, but not limited to, the ability to relate technical terminology to Customers, listen effectively, persuade and influence decision-makers, and solicit input from others
</div>
<div>· Proficiency in software applications including but not limited to Microsoft Word, Excel, and Visio.<br>
</div>
