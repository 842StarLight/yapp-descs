<h2>Activation Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>
<div><b>We are on the hunt for an experienced activation manager and media buyer who thrives on bringing creative media ideas to life. We're looking for an excellent negotiator with proven experience across traditional, digital and experiential media. If you're a big idea thinker who revels in collaboration, let's talk.</b></div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
This position is responsible for the creation, implementation and stewardship of media buys, ensuring they are executed accurately and efficiently. These channels include but are not limited to television (including direct response), online media (programmatic and publisher direct), paid social, radio, print, out-of- home, and non-traditional tactics. In addition, this individual is responsible for ensuring strong delivery upon campaign objectives for all clients.</div>
</div>
<div><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities</b></h3>
<div><ul>
<li>
Responsible for campaign management and optimization within various platforms, such as a DSP, Facebook/Instagram, Twitter, Snapchat, Strata, etc.</li>
<li>
Builds relationships and maintains ongoing communications with media</li>
<li>
representatives.</li>
<li>
Negotiate the best possible rates, placements and added value by tactic, documenting accordingly to share out with the larger team.</li>
<li>
Integral buying lead in key client communication.</li>
<li>
Present at all key client presentations and meetings.</li>
<li>
Designs optimal buys in terms of cost efficiency, goal delivery and vendor agreements.</li>
<li>
Keeps a pulse on all media buys and is ultimately responsible for the post-buy results and ongoing reporting.</li>
<li>
Updates Supervisor on status of current accounts on an ongoing basis and elevates and issues in a timely manner.</li>
<li>
Responsible for overseeing all campaign tracking set-up and implementation before launch of a campaign.</li>
<li>
Oversees creation of all reporting dashboards and is integral in presentations to clients on an ongoing basis.</li>
<li>
Lead in collaboration with finance team members to resolve media billing issues.Ensures strict adherence to client budgets while meeting client objectives.</li>
<li>
Oversees uploading of broadcast spots to transmission platform.</li>
<li>
Stays current on all media trends and opportunities in the industry.</li>
<li>
Other duties/responsibilities may be assigned from time to time.</li>
