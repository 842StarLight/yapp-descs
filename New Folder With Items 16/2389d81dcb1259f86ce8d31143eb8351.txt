<h2>Distribution Unit Manager - Evening Shift</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>
<div>Company Information
</div>
<div><p>PACCAR is a Fortune 200 company established in 1905. PACCAR is a global industry leader in the design and manufacture of premium quality commercial vehicles and engines. PACCAR commercial vehicles are sold worldwide under the Kenworth, Peterbilt, and DAF nameplates. PACCAR distributes aftermarket truck parts to its dealers through a worldwide network of Parts Distribution Centers. Finance and leasing subsidiaries facilitate the sale of PACCAR products in many countries worldwide. PACCAR manufactures and markets industrial winches under the Braden, Carco and Gearmatic nameplates. PACCAR maintains exceptionally high standards of quality for all of its products; they are well engineered, highly customized for specific applications and sell in the premium segments of their markets where they have a reputation for superior performance and pride of ownership.</p></div>
</div>
<div>
<div>
Division Information
</div>
<div><div>Dynacraft, a PACCAR Company, is headquartered in McKinney, TX. a suburb of Dallas. Dynacraft is a diversified global manufacturer of PACCAR medium- and heavy-duty truck parts sold under Kenworth, Peterbilt, and DAF nameplates. For fifty years Dynacraft has provided the company’s truck divisions and PACCAR Parts division with high quality components, subassemblies, and specialized services. Dynacraft has production facilities in Algona, WA, Louisville, KY, and McKinney, TX.</div></div>
</div>
<div>
<div>
Requisition Summary
</div>
<div><p>The Distribution Unit Manager reports to the Assistant Plant Manager and is responsible for day-to-day supervision of receiving, warehousing, shipping and inventory control for a growing plant supporting Paccar truck manufacturing. The Unit Manager will ensure that the plant will meet production goals and standards for all operations. This includes Safety, Quality, Efficiency, Process and Procedural standards. Participate in an opportunity in building the foundation for the Division’s future growth.</p></div>
</div>
<div>
<div>
Job Functions / Responsibilities
</div>
<div><ul>
<li>Manages the operations of one or more production areas to ensure that quality, production and deadline goals are met efficiently and cost effectively.</li>
<li>
Plans and establishes work schedules, staffing requirements and work sequences to optimize productivity.</li>
<li>
Trouble-shoots operational issues and works closely with team leads to resolve problems causing interruption of the operation.</li>
<li>
Supervise all aspects of employment for shop floor associates and communicate and apply policies and procedures as needed to support employees.</li>
<li>
Support safety initiatives and participate in facility safety programs including job injury process and prevention.</li>
<li>
Recommend improvements in production methods, equipment, operating efficiency and working conditions.</li>
<li>
Collaborate with support teams including quality, safety, procurement, customer service, engineering, maintenance and other teams to ensure quality, safe and timely production.</li>
<li>
Coach, mentor, develop and evaluate performance of team leaders and shop employees. Play an active role in promoting positive employee relations and engagement.</li>
<li>
Other duties and projects as assigned.</li>
</ul></div>
