<h4 class="jobSectionHeader">
<b>
POSITION SUMMARY</b><b>
</b>
</h4>
<p>The Business Systems Analyst IV provides advanced level of expertise in identifying, driving, developing and/or deploying technical systems and/or reporting solutions within the Services group. Associate is responsible for independently conducting the analysis of business issues and requests that require the creation, updating and/or enhancements to Services business systems and/or reporting solutions. Applies strong knowledge of established Services business processes to recommend appropriate solutions that help solve the identified business need(s). Utilizes advanced knowledge of SQL, Tableau, SalesForce and/or other applicable tools, technology, and business systems to perform gap analysis, document business requirements, recommend solutions, identify level of effort and deliver on complex business-critical projects. Works directly with business and technology stakeholders, Services program management and supporting resources to identify and recommend appropriate technical solutions. Performs configuration and testing of agreed upon solutions as assigned. Provide expert level of technical and business expertise to other team members including solution analysis, quality review and testing of developed solutions. Actively participates in business strategy discussions relating to new functionality, system solutions or other broadly scoped initiatives as assigned.</p>
<p>
.</p>
<p><b>
PRINCIPAL DUTIES &amp; RESPONSIBILITIES</b></p>
<p></p>
<br>
<ul>
<li>
<b>
Business Analysis</b>: Plans, conducts, and documents analysis efforts associated with assigned business processes and issues or initiatives that require creation of new and/or updating of existing business systems, tools and/or critical business processes. This includes managing business process review (BPR) sessions with stakeholders, documenting business requirements, providing relevant solution options and creation of functional specifications and use-cases to support any associated business system configuration or development efforts.</li>
<li>
<b>
Systems/Application Support and Administration: </b>Provides expert level of systems support, maintenance and administration for assigned systems. This includes administration and configuration associated with assigned database, CRM, telephony or related applications and tools to ensure alignment with the requirements of the business. Provide subject matter expert to team members and stakeholders on assigned systems and other business-critical applications.</li>
<li>
<b>
Reporting Development and Data Analysis</b>. Develops, executes, and maintains assigned datasets, reports and data mining efforts specific to all core business systems supported by the Services Operations team. This includes performing expert level data and impact analysis on assigned metrics, business processes and systems to ensure accuracy and relevance to Services initiatives and business priorities.<p></p>
