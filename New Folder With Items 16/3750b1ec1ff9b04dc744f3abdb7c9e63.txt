<h4 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Position Overview</b></h4>
<p>Responsible for providing service to members over the phone or by email by promoting credit union products and services, answering questions, and resolving problems.</p>
<h4 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Essential Functions</b></h4>
<ul>
<li>
<p>Answer incoming calls or emails to assist members with specific inquiries and requests.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Resolve issues or problems in a timely and accurate manner.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Actively build member interest in SELCO and SELCO Companies’ products and services.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Provide personalized service to internal, external, and potential members via telephone, mail, fax, email, web chat, online banking email system and in person.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Provide accurate information concerning Credit Union products, services, accounts, loans and eligibility requirements.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Adhere to policies and procedures related to a variety of functions, including, but not limited to: properly identifying members, opening new accounts, performing account file maintenance, coordinating with other Credit Union departments to resolve member issues.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Adequately document accounts to reflect communication with the member, explanation of file maintenance, information that is useful to other team members or departments and any follow-up.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Assist members with online banking access and use.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Deepen member relationships by determining member needs and promoting and cross-selling SELCO companies’ products and services.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Actively engage with people in a respectful and professional manner.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Communicate with internal, external, and potential members in a friendly, professional manner in person, over the phone, and via e-mail.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Adhere to federal and state regulations, credit union policies, and other compliance obligations.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Consistently achieve stated goals.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Participate in required meetings and training.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Demonstrate leadership skills and maintain corporate values and mission statement.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Represent the Credit Union with a high level of integrity and professionalism.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Support Management and member decisions and goals in a positive, professional manner.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Actively participate in security, robbery, disaster recovery and fire training.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Actively contribute to Credit Union and department team goals and functions, including but not limited to, initiating resolution of observed or known issues concerning other departments and championing other departmental changes that will benefit the organization.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Perform other duties as assigned.</p>
</li>
</ul>
<h4 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Interaction and Environment</b></h4>
<p>Reports To: Service Center Assistant Manager and Service Center Manager</p>
<p>
Work areas are inside in a climate-controlled environment in close proximity to other employees with moderate background noise.</p>
<h4 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Material and Equipment Used</b></h4>
<p>Computer, Fax Machine, Copier, Scanner, Telephone &amp; Headset, Calculator, General Office Supplies</p>
<h4 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Physical Activities Required To Perform Essential Functions</b></h4>
<p>Sitting/Mobility: Approximately 95% of time is spent working at a desk. Balance of time (approximately 5%) is spent moving around work areas.</p>
<p>
Communication: Ability to effectively communicate with co-workers, members and outside agencies in writing, in person and over the phone.</p>
<p>
Vision: Ability to effectively use a computer screen and interpret printed materials, memos and other appropriate paperwork.</p>
<p>
Lifting/Carrying: Ability to transport files and office supplies.</p>
<p>
Stooping/Kneeling: Ability to access files/stock supplies in low cabinets and shelves.</p>
<p>
Reaching/Handling: Ability to input information into computer systems, retrieve and work with appropriate paperwork, equipment and supplies.</p>
