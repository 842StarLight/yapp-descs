<b>Key Responsibilities</b><br>
<br>
As a Workday Implementation Project Manager, you will be the person who creates, maintains and directs the tasks on the project plan for the implementation of Workday Human Capital and/or Financial Management software. You may have more than one project to manage at a time, depending upon the project size and complexity.</div>
</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div>
<div>You report to project executive leadership and take an active role in issue resolution, planning and guiding the project.</div>
</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div>
<div>The project manager also assists as needed in the sales process including scoping projects and estimating efforts required to complete the implementation.</div>
</div>
<br>
<div>
<div>
<div>
</div>
</div>
<div><ul>
<li>
<div>Establish and maintain strong client relationships</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Create and deliver project level documentation (i.e., status reports, RAID logs, project plans etc)</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Ensure project is delivered on-time and on-budget</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Maintain responsibility for performance, cost, scope, schedule, quality, and appropriate business measurements</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Assist as needed in the sales process including scoping projects and estimating efforts to complete the implementation</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Ensure projects are properly planned and staffed.</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Track issues and risks, communicate status and escalate concerns applications.</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Work with the client project manager to identify/resolve all issues that could impact project scope and/or time frames.</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Ensure the project is delivered within the budget outlined in the SOW.</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Present client with change orders in the event they're asking Workday to perform tasks that are outside the scope of the SOW.</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Present at Executive Steering Committee meetings as needed .</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Work with Delivery Assurance to ensure compliance with agreed to checkpoints.</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Participate in the sales cycle when requested to gather requirements, formulate delivery approach and develop a proposal/SOW.</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Understand and apply Workday and Accenture DayNine methodologies.</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Support innovation through the creation of new industry leading methods and assets.</div>
</li>
</ul></div>
