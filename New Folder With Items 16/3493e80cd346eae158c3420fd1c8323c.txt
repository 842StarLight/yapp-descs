<h2>IT Director, Sales & Marketing</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>IT Director, Sales &amp; Marketing</b><b>-</b><b>020447</b></p>
<p></p>
<div>
<div>
The Director of IT Sales and Marketing will oversee the IT strategic direction, project execution and service support delivery for global enterprise Sales &amp;Marketing applications for Edwards Lifesciences. Working in close partnership with the business stakeholders, this role will be accountable to create the overall Enterprise IT Sales and Marketing applications strategy with thorough understanding of end-to-end business processes and identifying gaps while aligning/creating multi-year enterprise application technology roadmaps for both projects and enhancements. This role will be accountable for the creation of the service delivery catalog for IT Sales and Marketing service offerings and meet strict service levels in a global environment. The individual in this role must be a strong people leader with global team experience, solid communication skills and can influence others to action. We are looking for a high energy, self-motivated, results-oriented change leader, who is comfortable working in a global, highly-matrix team-oriented IT environment and has a proven success record to lead transformational initiatives. This role reports into the Sr. Director of Global IT Applications.</div>

Dedicated to quality business end user service and pro-active and responsive to business partner needs.</div></li>
<li><div>
Manage subordinate team members and direct IT activities with overall responsibility of planning, staffing, budgeting, managing expense priorities, and recommending and implementing changes to methods through several department managers.</div></li>
