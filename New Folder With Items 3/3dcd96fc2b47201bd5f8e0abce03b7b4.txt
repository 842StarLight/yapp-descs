<div><b>Description of the Job</b></div>
<div>
<p>Work effectively with our fixIT team at the on-campus RamTech computer store to in-process and repair VCU faculty, staff and student Apple and Dell computers. Provides a high level of customer service to parents and students regarding computer sales and repair issues. Process customer ticketing requests and Safeware warranty requests as applicable.</p>
<p>
Ability to be on time and follow instructions. Be willing to consult with technicians and management to ensure accuracy and compliance with fixIT standards.</p>
</div>
</div>
<br>
<div>
<div><b>Required Qualifications</b></div>
<div>
<p>Working knowledge of common and current PC operating systems and software application programs, such as macOS, Windows 7, 8, 8.1, Windows 10, MS Office and Adobe. Working knowledge of Apple and Dell computers.</p>
<p>
Strong communication and customer service skills in assisting faculty and staff who need technical assistance including repairs, diagnostics and installations of PCs, peripherals and printers.</p>
<p>
Ability to lift 50 pounds, operate a van and use a hand truck.</p>
<p>
Valid driver’s license and clean driving record.</p>
<p>
Awareness of safety and security at all times.</p>
<p>
Ability to work in a diverse work environment,</p>
</div>
</div>
<br>
<div>
<div><b>Preferred Qualifications</b></div>
<p>Apple ACMT or Dell hardware certifications. Pursuit of Business, Information Systems, or Computer Science related degree path.</p>
