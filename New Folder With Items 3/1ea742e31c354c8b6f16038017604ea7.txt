<h2>Content Development Layout Technician</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<div><div><div>
<p>Impactful. Disciplined. Measurable. That's why savvy marketers turn to IWCO Direct - to inspire performance across all marketing channels. From all-inclusive creative, digital and paper-based direct mail services to leading technology platforms, advanced postal optimization strategies and industry know-how, IWCO Direct won't just help you along the way; we'll Power your Marketing</p>
<p></p>
<p>IWCO Direct also plays an active role in our communities and works closely with industry partners, the U.S. Postal Service, and our Congressional representatives to build awareness of the mailing industry and its importance to the national economy.</p>
<p>
If you're thinking of a new career at IWCO Direct, our pace is fast, but safety, quality and an enriching work environment are top priorities. We offer a competitive compensation and benefits package and on-site wellness programs-not to mention the opportunity to work with some of the most talented people around.</p>
<p></p>
<p>IWCO Direct is seeking a Content Layout Technician to join our high-growth, creative organization at its office in Hamburg, PA. This person will work Monday - Friday, from 8:30am - 5:30pm.</p>
<p>
The Content Layout Technician develops and maintains digital content layout programs for customer mailings for both IWCO Direct clients and in-house use. The role involves setting up letter composition via variable data programming utilizing GMC/Quadient Inspire Designer. The Technician handles numerous different content layout projects at any given time and communicates with internal departments as needed for clarification of job instructions and/or customer specifications.</p>
<p><b>
Key responsibilities include</b>:</p>
<p><b>
GMC Inspire (60%)</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Program, test and troubleshoot programs utilizing the appropriate technology.</li>
<li>QA checks on software upgrades, and new feature implementation.</li>
<li>Support Team Lead/Supervisor on implementation of business solutions and client requests.</li>
<li>Proactively identify opportunities to improve process.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Requirements and Analysis (25%)</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Assist in resolving internal/external inquiries related to input/output requirements, intended usage, strategic planning, and alignment of these elements to internal checks, controls, and quality assurance.</li>
<li>Participate in team efforts to standardize process.</li>
<li>Provide general hands-on demonstrations of common process.</li>
<li>Become a Subject Matter Expert (SME) of our systems.</li>
<li>Assist with data file analysis.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Project and Data Management (15%)</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Participate in special projects and project management duties as needed.</li>
<li>Frequently work on client onboarding projects.</li>
<li>Collaboration with clients in both the sales and solution stages.</li>
<li>Contribute to the upkeep and consistency of internal documentation related to issues, resolutions, usage topics, and training.</li>
</ul>
<p>This person must be open to possible off-hour on-call support as needed.</p>
<p>
Pre-employment drug screen and background check is required.<br>
