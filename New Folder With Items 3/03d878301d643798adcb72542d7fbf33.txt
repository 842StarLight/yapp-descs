<p><b>Job Title: Ad Operations Associate</b></p>
<p><b>
Department Ad Operations, Data Solutions</b></p>
<p><b>
Location New York / Ann Arbor</b></p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Objectives of the Role:
</b></p>
<p>The Ad Operations Associate, Data Solutions, is responsible for the day-to-day operations for select accounts. The associate will coordinate communication among internal client teams, creative agencies, and third party vendors to ensure the timely launch and proper execution of advertising campaigns.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Business Responsibilities:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Traffic all creative from the campaign management system into 3rd party ad server.</li>
<li>
QA all creative files and ads. Coordinate with creative and/or internal media teams to ensure creative files meet specs.</li>
<li>
Distribute site placement tags and creative assets to publishers.</li>
<li>
Assist in investigating reporting discrepancies and serve as a point of contact for publishers for technical issues.</li>
<li>
Coordinate with internal teams and client in implementation of conversion tags.</li>
<li>
Other responsibilities as assigned.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
People Responsibilities:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Work successfully within a team as well as independently.</li>
<li>
Establish and maintain effective relationships with internal and external stakeholders.</li>
<li>
Ability to work collaboratively.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Skills and Behaviors:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Experience with third-party ad-serving systems preferred: Doubleclick, Mediaplex, DG MediaMind, Atlas.</li>
<li>
Experience with rich media solutions preferred: Doubleclick RM, Mediaplex RM, DG MediaMind, Pointroll, Flashtalking, etc.</li>
<li>
Knowledge of Flash, HTML, and JavaScript preferred.</li>
<li>
Proven ability to work successfully both within a team and independently.</li>
<li>
Effectively prioritize work and manage time and projects.</li>
<li>
Detail-oriented.</li>
<li>
Possess advanced analytical skillset.</li>
<li>
Ability to produce high-quality work within a fast-paced, dynamic environment.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Qualifications and Other Relevant Information:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s Degree preferred.</li>
<li>
1+ years relevant job experience preferred.</li>
</ul>
