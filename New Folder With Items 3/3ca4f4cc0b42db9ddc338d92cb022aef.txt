Description:</b></p>
<p>
In 2004, James Whelan founded The James R. Whelan Agency from his office at home, with a vision to build a media empire. In the years since, the company has grown and flourished both in number of employees and sales volume. Our staff has well over a century of combined sales and marketing experience and we are seeking an employee who can help our marketing IQ and sales grow. Our services include ad sales for publishers, strategic planning, multi-media planning and buying, creative design, public relations and search engine optimization.</p>
<p><b>
Duties and Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Negotiate rates for all media outlets, such as TV, radio, banners, emails etc</li>
<li>Research new websites and advertising opportunities for clients</li>
<li>Request media kits and media information for the Z drive</li>
<li>Maintain Vendor relationships</li>
<li>Create media proposals and sales agreements for sales team</li>
<li>Maintain media library on internal network drive</li>
<li>Update Media Kits/One Sheets</li>
<li>Maintain datacards in marketing databases (SRDS, Nextmark/MIN)</li>
<li>Assist in media director as needed</li>
<li>Marketing Emails- as needed/ writing copy</li>
<li>Create spreadsheets as needed to track daily tasks or special projects</li>
<li>Research/analyze new applications and software which might be useful to the agency</li>
<li>Thorough knowledge of the principles of marketing and advertising as well as an understanding of media concepts and terms, creative and budget requirements</li>
<li>Manage workflow escalate priority / rush assignments as needed</li>
<li>Team player with great attitude!</li>
</ul>

<li>Strong Negotiating skills and research skills</li>

<li>Previous experience with banner ads, email blasts, text ads, ad tags, marketing databases</li>
<li>Organized with a strong work ethic and ability to work well under strict deadlines</li>
