<h2>Field Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Job Title: </b>Field Manager</p>
<p><b>
Reports to: </b>Office Manager</p>
<p><b>
Location: </b>Office</p>
<p><b>
FLSA Status:</b> Seasonal, Non-Exempt</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Summary/Objective:</b></p>
<p>
The Field Manager oversees and evaluates employees who travel to customer sites and to ensure they are accomplishing their daily responsibilities.</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Responsibilities:
</b></p>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Generate leads/sales opportunities for self and team</li>
<li>
Train new agents on policies, procedures, software, marketing and all relevant job responsibilities in order to meet the Company’s objectives</li>
<li>
Provide customer service to agents by answering questions</li>
<li>
Take team on marketing tours to acquire new clientele</li>
<li>
Manage and oversee employees to ensure they are accomplishing the Company’s goals</li>
<li>
Contributes to team efforts as needed in order to meet deadlines</li>
<li>
Other duties as assigned</li>
<li>
Complete all associated paperwork in neat and timely manner</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Qualifications:</b></p>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>High school diploma or GED; Associates degree in business or related field preferred</li>
<li>
3-5 years customer service or sales experience</li>
<li>
Exceptional customer service skills</li>
<li>
Strong organization skills</li>
<li>
Extreme professionalism at all times</li>
<li>
Ability to communicate consistently and clearly</li>
</ul>
</div></div>
