<p><b>
Sr. Manager, Finance - Sparks, MD</b><br>
<br>
</p>
<p>This position interacts with respective leadership team to analyze monthly results, develop pricing and promotion strategies, monitor profitability by channel and product, oversee the preparation of the annual operating budget and prepare and load the quarterly forecasts in the Hyperion Financial Management software. This position will need to understand the company’s channels to market, cost structure / profit drivers, develop pricing methodologies, and partner with cross-functional leaders to ensure delivery of financial commitments.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Main Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Analyze monthly results in SAP and Hyperion HFM platforms. Report monthly results and variance analysis to senior leadership including division President and CFO.</li>
<li>
Provide insight and influence to impact performance and maintain competitive advantage alongside financial and contract support leadership and direction in the Business Unit.</li>
<li>
Own the commercial portion of the quarterly forecast and annual budget cycle including coordination of Sales forecast, marketing budgets, and margin analysis as well as updating Hyperion Financial Planning system. Will also lead communication efforts with Corporate FP&amp;A through each cycle.</li>
<li>
Partner with sales leaders to develop pricing strategies and competitive bids and present recommendations to senior leadership. Will have pricing authority over specific pricing levels and promotions.</li>
<li>
Drive revenue generating and expense efficiency initiatives and create actionable insights through root cause / scenario analysis and financial performance decomposition extracting and leveraging key learnings to drive performance.</li>
<li>
Lead sustainable financial growth by taking ownership of the financial operations, controls and management information for the Business Unit.</li>
<li>
Collaborate with cross-functional departments to forecast and assess commodity fluctuations, manufacturing costs, customer profitability, market/category data, as well as supply chain constraints.</li>
<li>
Provide financial insights, recommendations, and best practices to help the company make effective resource allocation decisions</li>
<li>
Researches issues as identified, reports findings, suggests alternative courses of action, and constantly look for process improvements</li>
<li>
Analyze commercial profit drivers including volume, mix and pricing changes at the customer and brand levels. Will summarize trends with respect the external market, performance vs prior year, forecast and plan, and work with cross-functionally to accurately reflect those trends into the budget/forecast.</li>
<li>
Directly support the global Cutting SBU (Strategic Business Unit) including development of the product roadmap, perform external market analysis, provide cost benchmarking &amp; competitiveness, and track success of product launch &amp; commercialization.</li>
<li>
Provide strong leadership, mentoring and coaching to a local team of two analysts.</li>
<li>
Build, lead and motivate a diverse and effective team. Empower team members to take responsibility and accountability<br>
</li>
</ul>
<br>
<p><b>Qualifications</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
BS Degree in Accounting/Finance Required. MBA, CPA or Master of Accounting preferred.
</li>
<li>8+ years’ progressive experience in an accounting or controlling function with demonstrated experience in forecasting and budgeting.</li>
<li>
Knowledge of GAAP, financial consolidation processes and internal controls.</li>
<li>
Experience working with enterprise resource planning software package (SAP, and Hyperion Financial Management strongly preferred) as well as Microsoft Excel.<br>
</li>
</ul>
