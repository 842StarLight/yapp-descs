<div>
<p>Join the largest manufacturer of tile and natural stone in the United States and watch your career stand out with Dal-Tile, a subsidiary of Mohawk Industries.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p>Our close-knit team of dedicated professionals has made us the success we are today. In exchange for their hard work, we support our people with a family-friendly work environment, a commitment to promoting from within, unique benefits that go beyond just medical and dental, and a belief that every employee deserves a productive life outside of work. If this sounds outstanding to you, take the first step forward and explore a career with Dal-Tile.<br>
 </p>
<p></p>
<p>The Manager Tile and Stone Gallery manages the Tile and Stone Gallery to maximize sales development and obtain customer sales referrals for Sales Service Centers.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Interviews and hires associates, plans and prepares work schedules, assigns associates to specific duties, supervises associates taking of inventories, keeping operating records, and performs work of subordinates, as needed.
</li>
<li>Establishes and maintains the Gallery Training Program and/or facilitates the continuing education of the staff through classes, seminars and other similar methods.
</li>
<li>Ensures compliance of associates with established security, operating, and record keeping procedures and practices.
</li>
<li>Responsible for the safe and proper opening and closing of the store.
</li>
<li>Responsible for securing expensive assets such as digital cameras, portable phones, projector and similar items and set up a log to keep record of their use.
</li>
<li>Establishes a log for the Deposits required for Stone Samples and keep it in a safe place.
</li>
<li>Receives customers (homeowners, designers, architects, contractors) referred form associates and determines their needs. Provides assistance as appropriate.
</li>
<li>Responsible for the operation of the gallery and the maintenance of all contracts for facility services.
</li>
<li>Prepares and coordinates sales promotion activities such as preparing sales displays and advertising copy.
</li>
<li>Promotes gallery through special events such as design classes, exhibitions and tours for designer associations.
</li>
<li>Responsible for selecting and defining materials and how to display with the assistance of the Corporate Marketing Department.
</li>
<li>Overviews and designs panels to display tile and stone surfaces.
</li>
<li>Maintains and updates design aid tools that help secure customer sales.
</li>
<li>Maintains and updates building permits source file for metropolitan area.
</li>
<li>Performs all aspects of design consulting especially for customers with larger residential and commercial buildings.
</li>
</ul>
<ul><li>Follows up on customer selection sheets until sales are finalized or customer’s interest are resolved; routes sales to appropriate Sales Service Center.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Answers customer's complaints.
<br>
<br>
Education, Experience and Knowledge:
</li></ul>
<ul>
<li>Requires Bachelors of Art degree in interior design (FICAR approved) or Bachelors of Science in Business Administration and knowledge of tile, porcelain and stone surfaces (finishes).
</li>
<li>Must possess a good level of knowledge of Auto Cad and DOMUS, as well as, a good level knowledge of Microsoft Suite (Excel, PowerPoint and Word).
</li>
<li>Requires 4 to 6 years of experience in management and or interior design within the tile and stone industry.
<br>
<br>
Competencies:
</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Excellent interpersonal and management skills
</li>
<li>Effective communication and listening skills and a very good level of design abilities
</li>
<li>Familiarity with design of accentuating materials and finishes other than tile to balance customer selections is essential.<br>
</li>
</ul>
