<h2>Manager, Customer Care</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<b>Job Description:</b><br>
<b>Unit/Department Description:</b><br>
The Husqvarna Customer Care Team is responsible for providing support to the dealer and consumer customer channels. This includes both Business to Business and Business to Consumer level service.
<br><br>
<b>Position/Job Description:</b><br>
Directs and supervises activities within the Customer Care Contact Center – dealer, retailer and eCommerce customer support, technical support, warranty claims and technical training. Develops and implements process improvement initiatives that positively affect the customers experience, while optimizing productivity and controlling costs. Oversees the management of personnel schedules to meet forecasted workflow demand and service levels. Evaluates and trains supervisors on their effectiveness in managing customer service operations. Manages budgets and costs to ensure corporate guidelines are adhered to.
<br><br>
<b>Essential Duties:</b><br>
Ensure Customer Care Team is aligned with Husqvarna Group goals, mission and values.
<br><br>
Lead team of Customer Care professionals – motivate, develop, manage performance and provide guidance.
<br><br>
Engage with Husqvarna Brand leadership to share key metrics and gain alignment on strategic initiatives.
<br><br>
Responsible for sharing the voice of the customer throughout the organization and ensuring that the Husqvarna Group remains customer focused.
<br><br>
Lead activities that define and report on the delivery of the Customer Experience
<br><br>
Ensure that Customer Care Contact Center meets or exceeds all customer service metrics.
<br>Use data gathered from multiple touch points (e.g. contact center metrics, transaction monitoring, end-user satisfaction surveys, etc.) to make recommendations, decisions on process changes/ improvements to the Customer Experience
<br>Responsible for verifying the Customer Care team is performing key business processes that are critical to delivering the products and services that meet the expectations of internal and external Customers.
<br><br>
Evaluate and delegate work assignments of staff as necessary and monitor scope of work.
<br><br>
Complete performance evaluations for staff and recommend increases or advancement.
<br><br>
Support customer service, sales team, and general management team with new programs throughout program development, implementation, and maintenance.
<br><br>
Consult with operations to determine training and quality needs, procedural updates, trainee performance, assess and evaluate calibration sessions and monitoring sessions, etc.
<br><br>
Organize weekly department meetings to review project updates, KPI’s, service levels, and staffing requirement.
<br><br>
Conduct internal calibration sessions with managers to ensure overall team development and performance levels are being met
<br><br>
Identifies trends in the business via the quality process and provides analysis to finance, logistics, technical services, human resources, sales and marketing.
<br><br>
Maintain positive, consistent and effective communication with staff, peers and senior management.
<br><br>
Study and analyze Customer Care performance, data and trends
<br><br>
Research and prepare executive summaries to facilitate strategic decision making.
<br><br>
Build and implement process to gain better controls
<br><br>
Cross Division coordination to ensure alignment and execution of the Customer Care strategy
<br><br>
<b>Job Requirements:</b><br>
Required
<br><br>
Bachelor’s degree or a combination of 7-10 years of progressive experience in a Customer Service/Contact Center environment, specifically in managing teams, and developing &amp; executing strategies
<br><br>
Desired
<br><br>
Bachelor’s degree
<br><br>
<b>Skills and Capabilities (Knowledge, Skills, Abilities) :</b><br>
Required
<br><br>
Excellent leadership skills, including an approachable, hands-on style and the ability to coach and inspire team members to perform at high levels.
<br><br>
Solid knowledge and application of Customer Care best practices.
<br><br>
Sound business judgment and a demonstrated ability to think strategically and execute objectives.
<br><br>
Ability to assess individual team skills and capabilities, in addition to utilizing team building skills to provide effective communication, training and development.
<br><br>
Solid knowledge and application of customer service and key metrics that drive improvement.
<br><br>
Strong interpersonal and cross functional business skills, including a demonstrated ability to effectively collaborate with key team leaders in Husqvarna Service Corp, Sales, Operations, Finance, IT, Engineering, Procurement, Parts, Planning, HR, etc.
<br><br>
Excellent presentation, verbal, and written communication skills.
<br><br>
Strong initiative and the ability to be self-directed in completing tasks.
<br><br>
Strong problem-solving and analytical skills, including a demonstrated ability to synthesize, interpret, and communicate information and data.
<br><br>
Highly organized with a demonstrated ability to simultaneously manage and follow through on multiple projects and priorities to meet critical deadlines in a fast-paced environment.
<br><br>
Demonstrated ability to be flexible and adaptive to internal processes and change, delays or unexpected events.
<br><br>
Advanced proficiency with MS Office software (Word, Excel, Powerpoint) and email applications. Skilled in understanding of CRM packages.
<br><br>
Results-oriented and the ability to work effectively under pressure and with a sense of urgency to meet deadlines, including working after normal business hours and weekends to accomplish objectives.
