<h2>Cyber Security Program Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div><div>
<div><div><div><div>
<p><b>TRC Companies, Inc</b>., a pioneer in groundbreaking scientific and engineering developments since the 1960's, is a national engineering and environmental consulting firm that provides integrated services to the Energy, Environmental, Oil &amp; Gas, and Infrastructure markets. TRC serves a broad range of clients in industry and government, implementing complex projects from initial concept to delivery and operation.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
TRC is seeking a full-time Cyber Security Program Manager to work in any of our following office locations: Austin TX, San Antonio TX, Boston MA, or Lowell MA. Candidate will work closely with client staff, maintaining priorities while working in challenging situations, must have good inter-personal and professional skills, be capable of organizing and prioritizing multiple concurrent projects with complex tasks, and have experience managing a team of professionals in implementing IT and cybersecurity systems in a sensitive environment.</p>
<br>
<p>
<b>
Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
<p>Support TRC clients in development and deployment of cybersecurity processes, architecture, and tools across multiple sites to maintain secure operations of critical infrastructure and control systems.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Assess client requirements and determine appropriate and required tasks and services needed for the solution to be delivered.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Understand and support both TRC internal and client-mandated monitoring and reporting on policies that protects the clients' IT systems and services.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Utilize established security standards to ensure work product is compliant with client policies and procedures, regulatory requirements, and industry best practices.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Provide improvements to the operation, reliability, and regulatory compliance of the client's networking, data management, and cyber security environment.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Lead efforts to ensure that solid standards and practices are developed, documented, maintained, and applied to assure a secure operating network and cyber infrastructure.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Keep abreast of technological changes, Cyber Security requirements, and industry regulations as they apply to NIST CSF, NERC CIP, and other regulatory and standards-based cybersecurity frameworks and standards.</p>
</li>
</ul>
</div></div></div></div>
<div>
<div>
<p><b>Requirements</b></p>
<div><div>
<p><b>Requirements</b>:</p>
<ul>
<li>
<p>10+ years' experience leading cybersecurity program development for company or clients.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Bachelor's Degree in engineering, computer science, or related field.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Candidate must be conversant with NIST CSF concepts, NERC CIP policy implementation, cybersecurity systems architecture, developing and deploying hardware and software, and managing staff responsible for patching, upgrades, penetration testing and vulnerability assessment, and device configuration.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Excellent written and verbal communication skills.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Experience in delivering technical presentations to industry groups.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Experience in both technical "hands-on" work, as well as business roles (sales, marketing, business development, etc.).</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Must be able to interface with all levels of management and provide effective reporting as balanced with business needs.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Must posses strong planning and communications skills</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Travel up to 10-20%</p>
<br>
</li>
</ul>
<p>
</p>
<p><b>Preferred, Not Required:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
<p>Experience with industrial control systems and critical infrastructure highly desired.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Experience with utility operations networks and operations environments</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Certified Information Systems Security Professional (CISSP) or CISSO (Certified Information Systems Security Officer)</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Prefer A+/Network+/Security+, ITIL, or similar certification. GIAC Information Security Professional (GISP) - SANS (Preferred)</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Cisco Certified Network Administrator / Professional (CCNA / CCNP) (Preferred)</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>AWS/Azure experience</p>
<br>
</li>
</ul>
