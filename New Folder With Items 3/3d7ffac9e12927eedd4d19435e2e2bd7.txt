<h2>Graphic & Brand Identity Designer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>As our graphic designer, you will contribute to our company strategy by handling the development of our brand identity, leading the design and creation of graphical assets, and maintaining consistent visual communication across multiple social media platforms.</p>
<p>
Your role will be integral to the company: you will help define and develop the image we broadcast as a brand to our customers, internal teams, and business partners.</p>
<p>
You have strong creative, artistic, organizational and highly collaborative skill sets. You will work closely with our executives, marketing, sales, and technical teams. With your help and talent, we will collectively define a brand and vision that accurately represents who we are.</p>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Requirements &amp; Responsibilities</b></h3>
<ul>
<li>
Able to create graphic assets for ads, emails, website, social media and packaging</li>
<li>
Proficient in developing style guides covering use across digital, print, and video mediums</li>
<li>
Strong command of visual and communication design principles</li>
<li>
Excellent skills with Adobe Creative Cloud software suite</li>
<li>
Experience in web and print asset design and creation</li>
<li>
Experience in product photo retouching</li>
</ul>
