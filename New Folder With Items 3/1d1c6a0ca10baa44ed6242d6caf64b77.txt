<h2>Technical Support Agent</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Our New Hartford Call Center is looking for a career-driven individual who likes a fast-paced support roll. This is an entry level position with a lot of room for growth and upward mobility. We promote from within, so this is a chance to get in on the ground floor and work your way up. If you like working with computers, customer service and a fast-paced work environment, please apply!</p>
<p>
PAR Technology Corporation is the parent company for three wholly owned business units providing systems and services for both government and hospitality sectors. PAR's largest business unit, Restaurant &amp; Retail Systems (PARTech, Inc.), is the world's leader in technology products and service integration solutions for the quick service restaurant industry. With revenues of approximately $150M, this unit serves many of the largest restaurant companies in the United States and around the world.</p>
<p><b>
JOB RESPONSIBILITIES</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Provide first level problem solving for Point of Sale hardware and software, including, but not limited to, operational and technical problems</li>
<li>
Utilize service management systems gathering and maintaining service incident data</li>
<li>
Contribute to the knowledge management system and adhere to and continually review published escalation and work flow procedures based on ISO 9000</li>
<li>
Ability to work a variety of shifts, including weekends and holidays, perform to defined standards, carry out special work assignments, and meet all conditions of employment</li>
<li>
Work in a team environment participating as a team member, focusing on continuous improvement and total customer satisfaction</li>
<li>
Perform customer service administrative task as assigned and understand customer database and processing customer part requests</li>
<li>
Decisions are limited and routine for which clearly defined procedures are documented</li>
<li>
Must display genuine concern and ownership for customers' situations</li>
<li>
Exercise sound judgement and analytical skills in determining a resolution to problems</li>
<li>
Provide and obtain general information which is technical in nature</li>
<li>
Work with moderate supervision</li>
<li>
Additional contacts are PAR and third-party service management and field engineers</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
KNOWLEDGE AND SKILLS</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
A minimum of one-year Customer Service experience. Helpdesk experience preferred.</li>
<li>
Problem solving, communication skills, typing, and basic Windows computer knowledge is required.</li>
<li>
Networking experience is desired.
</li>
<li>AAS degree in electronics technology, computer science, or its equivalent is a plus.</li>
<li>
A+ certification and Microsoft certifications are also a plus</li>
</ul>
