<h2>Chief Financial Officer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<b>Description:</b><br>
<b>Job Location:</b> Wings Over the Rockies Air &amp; Space Museum, 7711 East Academy Blvd., Denver CO 80230 (primary location), Wings Over the Rockies Exploration of Flight, 13005 Wings Way, Englewood CO, 80112 (secondary location)
<br><br>
<b>Department:</b> Administration/Human Resources/IT
<br><br>
<b>FLSA Status:</b> Exempt Full-Time, benefit eligible
<br><br>
<b>Salary Range:</b> $85,000 - $95,000
<br><br>
<b>Job Summary:</b> Wings Over the Rockies is seeking a CFO to oversee growing day to day budget and accounting operations. The ideal candidate will have a solid understanding of GAAP, high energy, and a desire for professional development. With a growing staff, great volunteers, $3 million budget, and $24 million capital campaign, Wings is a growing museum in the heart of the historic Lowry neighborhood with a secondary location at Centennial Airport. This position reports to the President/CEO, oversees a full-time assistant controller, has direct reports including Curator, Facility Manager, Corporate Events Director, and Guest Services Director.
<br><br>
<b>Responsibilities for the CFO and Assistant Controller include:</b>
<br><ul>
<li>Financial reporting to management and the Board of Directors</li>
<li>Preparation of the annual budget and continued variance monitoring</li>
<li>Serve on the Board Finance Committee</li>
<li>Preparing cash-flow analysis and capital project budgets in conjunction with the project managers when necessary</li>
<li>Track net assets and program grant funding</li>
<li>Serve as organization liaison to SCFD</li>
<li>Submit grant financial information and reimbursement requests as required by grant</li>
<li>Provide ad-hoc reporting as requested by CEO and Board of Directors</li>
<li>Work with the auditors preparing the annual audit and 990 filing</li>
<li>Supervise and coach the Assistant Controller</li>
<li>Oversee the AR and AP processing and procedures</li>
<li>Administer and maintain all credit applications and vendor files</li>
<li>Reconcile bank and investment accounts</li>
<li>Reconcile and approve journal entries</li>
<li>Reconcile General Ledger accounts</li>
<li>Track restricted funds and release to individual budgets when used</li>
<li>Prepare month-end close and present to the Finance, Account and Budget Committee</li>
<li>Oversee timely preparation and filing of sales tax reports, payroll tax reports, annual 1099 filings and annual W-2 reporting</li>
<li>Oversee payroll processing and payroll reports</li>
<li>Serve as liaison to insurance brokers for all benefit and organizational insurance needs</li>
<li>Perform Human Resource functions including:</li>
<li>Instituting management policies, including background and fingerprint checks</li>
<li>Ensuring the organization is complying with current laws and regulations</li>
<li>Update and/or write employee manual as needed</li>
<li>Plan for FMLA preparedness</li>
<li>Work with Employment Council as needed</li>
<li>Serve as liaison to IT service providers for needs including:</li>
<li>Hardware and software needs for staff</li>
<li>Accounting software and interface with other databases</li>
<li>Any IT issues that may arise with computers, phones, wireless, etc.</li>
<li>Other duties as assigned by CEO</li>
</ul>
<b>Full-time Benefits:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>100% Company paid Medical, Dental, and Vision insurance for the employee</li>
<li>Optional participation in Flex Spending Account</li>
<li>Optional participation in group retirement plan with company discretionary match</li>
<li>Optional participation in Short Term Disability, Accident and Critical Coverage</li>
<li>Generous Vacation, Sick, and Personal leave</li>
</ul>
<b>Working Conditions:</b> Wings Over the Rockies Air &amp; Space Museum operates at two locations: in an historic USAF plus hangar in the Lowry community and at a new site on Centennial Airport known as Exploration of Flight. Wings Air &amp; Space Museum (primary location) operates in a 70 plus-year-old- aircraft hangar. Environmental conditions may vary. .
<br><br>
<b>Requirements:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree in Accounting; professional certification a plus</li>
<li>Minimum 8 years’ related experience</li>
<li>Minimum 3 years in a supervisory role overseeing financial staff</li>
<li>Familiarity with HR and IT responsibilities</li>
<li>Significant experience working with external auditors, internal controls, and compliance related issues</li>
<li>Minimum 3 years experience preparing budgets</li>
<li>Previous experience processing payroll</li>
<li>High professional standards and attention to detail</li>
<li>Desire to develop professionally</li>
<li>Ability to work independently as well as in conjunction with the team</li>
<li>High interpersonal skills for working with staff, board, volunteers, and patrons</li>
<li>Good communication skills, both oral and written, able to communicate in technical terms and user-friendly terms</li>
<li>High energy and quick learner</li>
<li>Experience in nonprofits</li>
<li>Experience with Blackbaud products, and QuickBooks highly desirable</li>
<li>Proficient with Microsoft office, expert with Excel</li>
</ul>
