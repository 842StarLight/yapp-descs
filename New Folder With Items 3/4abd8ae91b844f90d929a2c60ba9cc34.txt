<div>Description</div> <div>The position is responsible for processing first mortgage and home equity loan applications from inception through loan approval and closing-following current TruWest Credit Union Portfolio and FNMA guidelines. Must be able to prioritize workload and work independently managing a pipeline of 30-40 loan applications in order to fund 25 units per month.<br>
<br>
Essential Duties and Responsibilities:<br>
<br>
Reasonable accommodations may be made to enable individuals with disabilities to perform the essential functions.<br>
<br>
1.Required processing approximately 30-40 loans per month while maintaining accuracy and timelines and selected partner relationships.<br>
2.Performance in accordance with credit union established policies and procedures.<br>
3.Timely communications with Assistant Manager or Vice President of Mortgages.<br>
4.Accuracy in evaluating title reports and recognizing proper action to secure desired position lien.<br>
5.Accuracy in analyzing property values based on comparable sales or appraisal provided by credit union approved vendors and/or software programs.<br>
6.Provides accurate tracking, follow up and processing for mortgage loans.<br>
7.Ultimately responsible for timely and accurate document control logs ensuring various tracking needs for mortgage loans are met at all times.<br>
8.Follow credit union policies for compliance with all laws and regulations, specifically, but not limited to, the Bank Secrecy Act, Security Policies and Confidentiality Policies.<br>
<br>
Secondary Duties and Responsibilities<br>
1.Provide additional support to fellow employees and manager as needed.<br>
2.Professional interactions with employees, members, departments, and business associates.<br>
3.Maintain knowledge of marketplace programs and rates for mortgage loans and related products.<br>
4.Accuracy in obtaining all required documents and signatures at closings.<br>
5.Identify cross selling products that meet member needs.<br>
6.Provides accurate tracking, follow up and processing for HMDA.<br>
7.Additional duties as assigned.<br>
