<br>
<div>Supervise Disa Operators with enthusiasm and integrity. Produce quality castings for our customers. Direct, maintain, and enforce the safety program</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
</div>
<ul>
<li>Promote safety, quality and productivity, and maintain employee morale.</li>
<li>Make sure correct patterns and cores are ready for production.</li>
<li>Order raw materials for the molding operation.</li>
<li>Carry a beeper when needed and monitor furnaces on weekends per schedule.
</li>
<li>Ensure that direct reports are trained, equipped and motivated.</li>
<li>Maintain a clean and orderly department.</li>
<li>Hold monthly communication (Tool-Box) meetings with employees.</li>
<li>Maintain an acceptable raw material inventory.</li>
<li>Ensure that Tier-3 Work Instructions are adequate and up-to-date.</li>
<li>Complete a safety inspection monthly.</li>
<li>Check out defects and communicate problems to appropriate department.</li>
<li>Fill in for other department supervisors as needed.
</li>
<li>When filling in, follow guidelines for the position being filled in for.<br>
</li>
</ul>
