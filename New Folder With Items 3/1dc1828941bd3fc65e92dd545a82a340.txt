<h2>Print Sales Representative</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Position Summary:</p>
<p>
Real Green Systems is looking for a great candidate for our Print Sales Representative Position. You will be working out of our offices in Walled Lake, MI. We are looking for someone interested in a long-term career who will sell and upsell our clients in the green industry on our Print and Digital Marketing Products. If you are self-motivated, have a strong desire for growth and a desire to be part of a sales team with great longevity that directly contributes to the success of the organization, we want to meet you!</p>
<p>
Responsibilities:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Serve as the lead point of contact for customer account matters</li>
<li>
Interact with customers over the phone and in person; this job is 70-80% working on the phone with customers to sell and upsell them on our products and 20-30% travel to customers</li>
<li>
Identify Print and Marketing Leads and design solutions for them using our Marketing tools</li>
<li>
Build and maintain strong, long-lasting client relationships</li>
<li>
Negotiate contracts and close agreements to maximize profits</li>
<li>
Develop trusted adviser relationships with key accounts, customer stakeholders and executive sponsors</li>
<li>
Ensure the timely and successful delivery of our solutions according to customer needs and objectives</li>
<li>
Clearly communicate the progress of monthly/quarterly initiatives to internal and external stakeholders</li>
<li>
Working with design, sales team, creative, advertising, logistics, managers, marketing, and team members from other departments dedicated to the same client account to ensure the highest quality of materials are being produced and all client needs are met</li>
<li>
Develop new business with existing clients and/or identify areas of improvement to meet sales quotas</li>
<li>
Forecast and track key account metrics (e.g. quarterly sales results and annual forecasts)</li>
<li>
Prepare reports on account status</li>
<li>
Collaborate with sales team to identify and grow opportunities within territory</li>
<li>
Assist with challenging client requests or issue escalations as needed</li>
<li>
Proven experience on a job with a great deal of phone calls and travel; this job is 70-80% working on the phone with customers and 20-30% travel to customers (larger firms)</li>
</ul>
<p>
Requirements:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Knowledge or experience in the print industry? E.g. proofing, knowledge of the process of creating a design and printing it, etc.</li>
<li>
Demonstrable ability to communicate, present and influence key stakeholders at all levels of an organization, including executive and C-level</li>
<li>
Solid experience with CRM software (e.g. Salesforce) is preferred</li>
<li>
Experience with MS Office is required</li>
<li>
Experience delivering client-focused solutions to customer needs</li>
<li>
Proven ability to juggle multiple account projects at a time, while maintaining sharp attention to detail</li>
<li>
Excellent listening, negotiation and presentation abilities</li>
<li>
Strong verbal and written communication skills</li>
</ul>
<p>
About Us:</p>
<p>
We are Real Green Systems, a leading Michigan enterprise software company. We provide software solutions to lawn care, pest control, and other types of organizations in the green industry. We believe that success is achieved through the pursuit of excellence in customer and employee relations. We keep “a finger on the pulse” of our company by staying tuned in with the status of our customers and the well-being of our Real Green family of employees. We strive to exceed expectations by providing exceptional products and services that are innovative, affordable and meets the needs of our service industry partners. You can learn more about us at https://www.realgreen.com/about-our-company</p>
<p>
Our Culture:</p>
<p>
Real Green Systems is a respectful, family-friendly company. We are proud of our culture and of the dedication of our employees. Many of our employees have been here for years and the organization is very loyal to them, providing ample opportunity to learn on the job along with a great place to work.</p>
