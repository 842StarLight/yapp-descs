<h2>Rebuild Application Engineer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>The Rebuild Application Engineer supports the outside sales team with rebuild proposals and technical consultation regarding large custom industrial equipment projects. Rebuild Application Engineering activities include equipment selection and layout, developing cost estimates, preparing equipment proposals, and presenting project expectations to stakeholders at project kick-off. The Rebuild Application Engineer is an internal and external client facing role and must have strong presentation and communication skills, as this role routinely presents information to key influencers and decision makers.
</p>
<p>This role involves a mix of technical innovation and commercial strategy, a working knowledge of standard industry practices and awareness of competitive products.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>PRIMARY DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES:</b></p>
<p></p>
<p>
Develop rebuild proposals and layout drawings</p>
<p>
Develop project cost estimates</p>
<p>
Support outside sales team with technical consultation and strategy</p>
<p>
Travel with outside team to sales calls, attend trade shows, sales meetings, etc.</p>
