<h2>senior project manager, Licensed Stores – Programs & Strategy – Seattle, WA</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<div><b>Now Brewing – senior project manager! #tobeapartner</b></div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div>From the beginning, Starbucks set out to be a different kind of company. One that not only celebrated coffee and the rich tradition, but that also brought a feeling of connection. We are known for developing extraordinary leaders who share this passion and are guided by their service to others.</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div>In the Licensed Store business unit of Starbucks, you will support innovation for our dynamic and diverse business partners. Our team executes enterprise projects as well as creating industry-leading solutions in equipment, store design and technology. You’ll impact our bottom line and customer experience as you balance the needs of multiple projects, getting them completed on time and within budget while ensuring risks are mitigated.</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div><i>As a licensed store senior project manager, you will…</i></div>
<ul>
<li><div>
<b>Define, Develop and Sustain</b> – You will utilize your project management expertise to help define a set of tools for the greater PMO to utilize for intake, project and resource tracking. You will be responsible for providing an all-up view at various check-ins along the way.
</div></li>
<li><div>
<b>Inspire and influence others to meet business goals</b> – You will partner with store development, design, R&amp;D, equipment teams, sourcing, and sales teams to drive innovation plans forward to reach their full potential.</div></li>
<li><div>
<b>
Manage delivery of projects</b> from requirements gathering, testing, development, identifying and managing risks, monitoring and escalating issues, and ensuring documentation and communications materials.</div></li>
<li><div>
<b>
Build, Lead and Mentor </b>– As the senior project manager, you will provide tools and mentorship to other project managers on the team as we continue to establish and grow a PMO to support Licensed Stores.
</div></li>
<li><div>
<b>Represent business unit on cross-functional project teams.</b> Provides functional expertise to assess potential financial and operational impact.</div></li>
<li><div>
<b>
Enjoy working on an energetic, fun team</b> and have a clear ability to drive the business forward as part of a highly collaborative team, while acting in accordance with Starbucks guiding principles.</div></li>
</ul>
</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
<div><i>We’d love to hear from people with:</i></div>
<ul>
<li>Strong project management background with knowledge of multiple methodologies, from intake through implementation and evaluation.</li>
<li>
Desire to assist in defining and developing tools to support a growing PMO.
</li>
<li>Ability to define objectives, scope and resources for projects extending beyond a department or business unit.</li>
<li>
Ability to establish cross-functional, collaborative relationships with business partners.</li>
<li>
Understanding of business unit, department or functional area's goals and practices.
</li>
</ul>
