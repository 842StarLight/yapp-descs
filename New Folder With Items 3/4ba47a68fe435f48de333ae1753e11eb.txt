<p><b>
Summary:</b> The Project Assistant will administer all Leopardo administrative responsibilities of specific projects. The role will support all levels of the project team in an effort to secure successful project completion. The immediate day to day contact/supervisor is the Project Manager(s) with additional supervision and interaction with the Office Manager.</p>
<p>
</p>
<p><b>Essential Duties and Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Create, update and generate the following per project utilizing Viewpoint software: project set up, all contractual documents including subcontract agreements, limited work authorizations, work authorizations, design/build contracts, consultant agreements, purchase orders, change orders, requests for information (RFI), project submittals, and drawing logs. Produce weekly distributions related to project status, project call sheets, submittals and RFI logs.</li>
<li>Follow up for timely receipt of executed copies of all contractual agreements, change orders, purchase orders, and sub-tier compliancy through insurance and signed Exhibit C Agreements. Licensing and proof of municipal contracts registration and bonding may be necessary. Distribute, track, and file all executed subcontractor and owner change orders.</li>
<li>Work within the guidelines established for subcontract agreements and insurance per Master Agreements.</li>
<li>Work directly with Leopardo s insurance agent for accuracy of insurance coverage per project, obtaining certificates of insurance from subcontractors, tracking compliancy and renewal.</li>
<li>Partner with project team to ensure all subcontractors are compliant and have appropriate documentation needed throughout the project.</li>
<li>Interaction and support of project team as required (e.g., Project Executive, Project Manager, Project Engineer, Superintendent, Project Accountant), may include completion of monthly expense reports, file creation, form completion, and job site materials.</li>
<li>Maintain all current project documents (e.g., project manual, plan sets, submittals, RFIs, ASKs, shop drawings) on PlanGrid.</li>
<li>Responsible to update and maintain Company software systems (e.g., View Point, Sales Force) with project detail information for marketing and project history.</li>
<li>Prioritize incoming and outgoing correspondence/mail/package on a daily basis. May include but not limited to processing UPS, FedEx, Messenger services and ordering blueprints.</li>
<li>File and maintain current job files (electronically and in paper format depending on retention requirements).</li>
<li>Work in the AIA program to create, edit and finalize various forms of Owner Contracts, Certificates of Substantial Completion, etc. Coordinate final executions and return/record documents.</li>
<li>Research potential job prospects and coordinate documentation needed to prepare an RFQ/RFP to secure new projects.</li>
<li>Generate check requests for project requirements, renewals, and events/fundraisers. Assist with coordinating and preparing for marketing opportunities/trade show exhibits. Coordinate travel arrangements as required.</li>
<li>Coordinate documentation and communication of project specific samples/submittals/shop drawings to the architects, engineers, and superintendent; process as required. Track timely return of submittals and update logs for review by project team.
</li>
<li>Schedule and coordinate calendars and on site meetings including Outlook scheduling, reserving conference room (beverage set-up if needed), audio/visual requirements, conference lines and Web-ex meetings. Coordination of catering may also be required.</li>
<li>Organize, generate reports and participate in pre and post job meetings. Coordinate job start and job closeout with Superintendent and Project Manager. Maintain client rapport.</li>
<li>Provide the Administrative Assistant pertinent and accurate project information to facilitate the document closeout process.</li>
<li>Create subcontractor bid lists and the Invitation to Bid (ITB) in Leopardo bid software as required. Track receipt of ITB responses, coordinate distribution of bid package/blueprints to subcontractors, and send addenda and/or clarifications as required; utilize box.net as required. Assemble bid book, call subcontractors for bids, and track bids as they are received. Type final proposal to Owner.Attend bi-monthly Market meetings, Project Assistant meetings, TQM and other miscellaneous TQM committee meetings as required.</li>
<li>Properly record and box project files for dead file storage at the close of each project. Move and archive all project PlanGrid files to the project files on the Company shared directory.</li>
</ul>
