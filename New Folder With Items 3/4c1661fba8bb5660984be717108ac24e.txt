<h2>Marketing Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<b>OVERVIEW:</b>
<br>
<br>
This position ties all marketing activity back to the INFINITY Science Center’s mission and vision, keeps the big picture in focus and serves as the key point of contact for all major internal and external relationships.
<br>
<br>
<b>KEY ROLES:</b>
<br>
<br>
Management
<br>
<br>
<ul><li></ul>Develop annual comprehensive marketing and advertising plan.
<br>
<br>
<ul><li></ul>Develop, implement, and evaluate comprehensive marketing campaigns to generate ticket revenue for the INFINITY Science Center.
<br>
<br>
<ul><li></ul>Develop and manage all direct marketing efforts, including direct mail, e-marketing, customer segmentation, target market strategies and audience development.
<br>
<br>
<ul><li></ul>Manage and direct the production marketing deliverables, including advertising (print, broadcast, outdoor, online), direct mail, email, promotional materials and other collateral.
<br>
<br>
<ul><li></ul>Manage marketing staff, which includes, but is not limited to the Marketing Assistant and Graphic Designer
<br>
<br>
<ul><li></ul>Creates editorial content in conjunction with the Guest Relations and Education Managers for special programs and events via newsletters, social media, the INFINITY website and any other platforms deemed reasonable and necessary
<br>
<br>
Public/Media Relations and Communications
<br>
<br>
<ul><li></ul>Promote and communicate INFINITY’s core mission and values by creating and utilizing brand development, messaging, web and social media strategies
<br>
<br>
<ul><li></ul>Manage the development and implementation of strategic and tactical public relations plans to promote special events and education/outreach activities
<br>
<br>
<ul><li></ul>Create press releases and serve as the primary media contact for INFINITY and as a spokesperson, as deemed appropriate by the Executive Director
<br>
<br>
<ul><li></ul>Cultivate relationships with key media contacts, maintain a media list, and manage press opportunities
<br>
<br>
<ul><li></ul>Respond to media queries and requests for information
<br>
<br>
<ul><li></ul>Coordinate speaking engagements to promote INFINITY at the discretion of the Executive Director
<br>
<br>
Finance/Analytics
<br>
<br>
<ul><li></ul>Provide financial oversight over the marketing expense budget
<br>
<br>
<ul><li></ul>Create expense projections for the annual budgeting process
<br>
<br>
<ul><li></ul>Participate in the planning and management of the INFINITY Annual Fundraising Gala
<br>
<br>
<ul><li></ul>Increase community awareness of the INFINITY brand; develop new audiences
<br>
<br>
<ul><li></ul>Develop community partnerships to increase INFINITY visibility
<br>
<br>
Other Duties
<br>
<br>
<ul><li></ul>Develop strategic long-term program goals and budgets with the Director of Finance and the Executive Director
<br>
<br>
<ul><li></ul>Collaborate Guest Relations and Education departments to identify funding and marketing sponsorship opportunities and responsibilities
<br>
<br>
<b>SKILL AND KNOWLEDGE REQUIREMENTS</b>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor's degree in Business, Marketing, Mass Communications or a related discipline (or equivalent); other degrees considered if the candidate has strongly demonstrated marketing or sales results.</li>
<li>
A minimum of five to seven years of marketing/sales experience with demonstrated supervisory skills</li>
<li>
Extensive knowledge of Microsoft Office software (Word, Excel, Outlook, PowerPoint) essential</li>
<li>
Excellent promotional skills, excellent communications skills, and extensive writing and editing experience and skills with a variety of print and online communications media</li>
<li>
Ability to produce high quality, persuasive written materials suitable for general public understanding, requiring minimal revision</li>
<li>
Proven ability to effectively manage projects including meeting tight deadlines</li>
<li>
Strong knowledge an demonstrated use of social media to engage and deepen relationships with the media and public</li>
</ul>
<b>PHYSICAL REQUIREMENTS/WORK ENVIRONMENT.</b>
<br>
<br>
Ability to:
<ul>
<li>Sit or stand for long periods of time is required</li>
<li>
Employee is subject to both inside and outside environmental conditions.</li>
<li>
Communicate orally in small or large group settings is required</li>
<li>
Navigate from place to place throughout the community and travel to meetings and events as necessary; Other travel may be required</li>
</ul>
