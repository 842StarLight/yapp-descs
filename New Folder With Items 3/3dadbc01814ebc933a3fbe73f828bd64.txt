<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Please review the following job description:
</b></h3>
<p></p>Review and document the end-to-end client journey across multiple processes and channels to better understand the client experience, identify friction in the process and make recommendations for client experience improvements. Conduct quantitative and qualitative analysis to review the client experience across systems, channels and processes from a client’s perspective. Create a unified client experience through strong collaboration and partnership between channel and process owners.
<p></p>
<ul><li><i>On February 7, 2019, it was announced that SunTrust Banks, Inc. and BB&amp;T Corporation intend to merge, with the combined entity to be named Truist Financial Corporation (“Truist”). The merger is subject to regulatory approval, and BB&amp;T and SunTrust remain separate and independent companies until the merger closes. This position, which would be for a role with Truist or an affiliate, is contingent upon the merger receiving regulatory approval and closing.
</i></li></ul>
<p></p>
<p>Job Duties:
</p>
<ul>
<li>Identify and define key client journeys for client segments, channel or products
</li>
<li>Serve as a point of contact and relationship for Journey Owners and LOB Leadership while collaborating with business counterparts and act as end-to-end co-owner of the product, responsible for exercising business mandate, ongoing partnership with LOB
</li>
<li>Develop and drive the target product vision aligned with business strategy
</li>
<li>Communicate process, vision and status to key stakeholders and squad members
</li>
<li>Help to build, improve, and prioritize the backlog of product features (in collaboration of business)
</li>
<li>Drive sprint and release plans with Scrum Master
</li>
<li>Drive journey design delivery for their respective BU counterparts, using both quantitative and qualitative research tools and data, guide the creation of client experiences, interactions, and flow while guiding the design of front end digital solutions
</li>
<li>Partner with team members across the Client Centricity Team to create digital client experiencers for Truist, with a focus to the seamless Omni Channel delivery
</li>
<li>Partner with Business Units and various Enterprise functions to understand current end to end process and define future state end to end processes
</li>
<li>Own library of test approaches and process documentation &amp; manuals
</li>
<li>Proactively collaborate and socialize recommendations with stakeholders to integrate ideas into execution processes, such as the Client First Council, Funding Councils and other work streams
</li>
</ul>
