<h2>Sephora Talent / Experience Supervisor</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div><div>
<p>The <b>Sephora Talent / Experience Supervisor </b>is responsible for leading, educating and instructing Sephora associates on relevant product information, technology and client service initiatives enabling the team to consistently deliver a personalized, fun and engaging shopping experience to clients that will drive sales and profit. The Sephora Talent / Experience Supervisor will lead the execution of services an events and ensure all team members perform Sephora Inside JCP services within approved standards.</p>
<p><b>
Primary Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Ensure all Sephora associates are trained to deliver a memorable and lasting client experience</li>
<li>
Support Sephora Manager in the onboarding of new Sephora associates including orientation, new hire checklist completion and new hire certification</li>
<li>
Ensures all Sephora associates leverage technology innovations within services while assisting clients; accountable for the adoption of all in-store digital technology in support of services</li>
<li>
Facilitate education for company programs including, but not limited to Services, Beauty Insider and Credit</li>
<li>
Manage the development of artistry skills; demonstrate an understanding of artistry skills through conducting services as needed; provide opportunities for team to elevate artistry skills</li>
<li>
Review sales and client service goals with Sephora Manager; assist Sephora Manager in goal setting to drive sales and profit</li>
<li>
Models a strong brand identity based on the Sephora Inside JCP values and culture</li>
</ul>
<p><br>
<b>
Core Competencies &amp; Accomplishments:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Strong communication and relationship building skills</li>
<li>
Prior supervisory leadership recommended</li>
</ul>
