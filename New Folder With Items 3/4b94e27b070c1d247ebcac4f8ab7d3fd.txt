The world's leading private security organization, G4S, has an immediate job opportunity for a Product Implementation &amp; Support Administrator. The Product Implementation &amp; Support Administrator is responsible for implementing technology-based solutions that address specific operational and business needs of G4S customers and prospects. These solutions include Secure Trax and G4S Insight, which is a G4S proprietary operations mobile application and corresponding reporting portal, and RISK360, an AMAG proprietary incident and case management solution. This position is responsible for coordinating all aspects of a given project within their area including presenting and meeting with customers/prospects, gathering and documenting requirements, implementing agreed upon solution, training and roll-out. In addition to the initial implementation, the position is also responsible for monitoring to ensure solution continues to provide the value expected, identify additional value and provide user support as needed.
<br><br>
G4S is a security provider for the United States government, fortune 500 companies, nuclear power plants, oil and gas companies, airport, ports, banks, hospitals, factories, warehouses, commercial facilities, residential communities and much more.

Main Responsibilities
<br>Specific Duties and Essential Functions
<br><br>
Provide implementation support for all Secure Trax, RISK360, G4S Insight installations within the region (Local and Enterprise)
<br>Meeting with customers to demonstrate and discuss product capabilities
<br>Assist customers in determining optimal implementation
<br>Assist field offices with setup processes such as device order and software installs, geofences, inspections, tours, notifications, reporting
<br>Provide training to field office staff on G4S products
<br>Provide training to customers on G4S Insight
<br>Provide additional help desk support for calls and emails submitted to Corporate Secure Trax Support
<br>Monitor support activity to determine root/cause and implement refresher training when necessary
<br>Telecommunications support and analysis (i.e. zero usage, excessive data/voice usage, phone utilization)
<br>Continuous follow up with customer implementations to ensure product is exceeding customer expectations
<br>Provide Region with Monthly metrics by branch on Secure Trax usage and performance
<br>Review Incident Reporting data for customer within Market and provide Market leaders/customers with data analysis
<br>Support sales staff with demonstrations and product knowledge as needed
<br>Assist with testing new features as needed
