About the Position:</div>
<div>
The Contrast Security Go-To-Market team is looking for an experienced Program Manager to drive marketing launches, product go-to-market programs, and help improve cross-functional processes. We are looking for an individual contributor with the agility to facilitate multiple strategic cross-functional projects/programs that will accelerate our growth.</div>

The ideal candidate will be highly skilled with project management, operational planning, facilitation, reporting and communication. They’ll be working with our Product Management, Product Marketing, Corporate Marketing, Strategic Alliances, Engineering, Sales, and Customer Success teams to drive new products to market, strategic growth projects, launches and campaigns.</div>
