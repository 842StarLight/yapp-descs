<h2>Branch Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>
<b><i>AAA East Central </i></b>is looking for an individual to join our team as a full-time <b><i>Branch Manager III</i></b> in our <b><i>Royersford Branch Office</i></b> located at 70 Buckwalter Road in Royersford, Pennsylvania. Business hours at this location are Monday – Friday from 10:00 a.m. – 6:00 p.m., Thursday from 10:00 a.m. - 8:00 p.m. and Saturday from 10:00 a.m. to 2:00 p.m. Starting salary for this position begins at <b>$54,500 annually</b> and is based upon experience.</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div><b><i>Responsibilities include, but are not limited to:</i></b></div>
<ul>
<li>Oversees daily activities of assigned branch to provide the highest quality of customer service to customers and members; investigates complaints, identifies potential problems and takes corrective action.</li>
<li>
Accountable for effective, efficient budgeting and planning for assigned branches; controls costs; meets quarterly performance expectations in the areas of sales, employee productivity and member satisfaction.</li>
<li>
Provides guidance, training, leadership and coaching</li>
<li>
Interprets policies and procedures and communicates changes</li>
<li>
Maintains a qualified staff and works with HR recruitment</li>
<li>
Determines standards of performance as a basis to review progress</li>
<li>
Fosters a cooperative and harmonious working climate conducive to maximizing employee morale and productivity</li>
<li>
Prepares and submits clear, concise, timely and accurate reports</li>
<li>
Maintains service level audit of 95% satisfaction</li>
<li>
Must be committed to visiting branch sites within the division and attend administrative meetings as required.</li>
</ul>
<br>
<b>
Qualifications</b><br>
<br>
<div><b><i>Qualified individuals, will have:</i></b></div>
<ul>
<li>Demonstrated management skills to include 3-5 years’ experience in full range of managerial responsibilities; preferably in AAA field or related business.</li>
<li>
Bachelor's degree in business or related field or equivalent experience</li>
<li>
Ability to supervise, coach, motivate and develop staff.</li>
<li>
Strong analytical skills; solid oral and written communication skills.</li>
<li>
Computer literacy with proficiency in spreadsheet and word processing software</li>
<li>
Must be willing to work in excess of 40 hours per week and on some Saturdays</li>
<li>
The selected candidate will be required to successfully complete pre-employment assessments, background and drug screenings.</li>
</ul>
