<p><b>Job Description<br>
</b></p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Application Specialist is responsible for communication between sales, service, leadership, customers and other team members; building and maintaining strong customer/channel/employee relationships. The Application Specialist is expected to maintain an exceptional working relationship with the service organization and build out best practices to support customer needs.
</b><br>
<br>
<b>Detailed Description:
</b></p>
<ul><li><b>Work with the sales team to manage pre- and post- sale support of customer applications.
</b></li></ul>
<ul><li><b>Provide Reach-Back method analysis and development services to customers.
</b></li></ul>
<ul><li><b>Partner with the sales and service teams - implement the Go-to-Market business development strategy for GC/MS products.
</b></li></ul>
<ul><li><b>Develop application notes for sale support.
</b></li></ul>
<ul><li><b>Provide feedback to marketing, product management and R&amp;D regarding application, hardware, and software features needed to stay ahead of changing market.
</b></li></ul>
<ul><li><b>Make sound, well informed, and objective decisions; perceives the impact and implications of these decisions; commit to action, that supports accomplishment of organizational goals.
</b></li></ul>
<p><br>
<b>Requirements:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>PhD in Chemistry
</li>
<li>5+ years of experience in customer support
</li>
<li>A demonstrated detailed understanding of GC/MS technology and applications
</li>
<li>A demonstrated understanding of cutting-edge technologies and applications
</li>
<li>Aptitude to understand and communicate scientific concepts, applications, and methods.
</li>
<li>Ability to communicate clear, concise and professionally with customers, management, and internal staff both verbally and in written format.
</li>
<li>The ability to build and maintain positive and productive working relationships across a broad range of people and groups.
</li>
<li>The ability to balance customer demands with sound business management that effectively match resources to the job and execute to meet business needs.
</li>
<li>The ability to prioritize tasks and projects with little direction and supervision.
</li>
<li>Ability to multitask and assess, accept/make changes quickly to meet timelines.
</li>
<li>Extensive knowledge of MS Office programs.
</li>
<li>Possession of or ability to attain a US Passport for international travel
</li>
<li>Possession of or ability to attain a DOD Security Clearance
</li>
<li>Prior experience with Mobile Mass Spectrometers (FLIR or competitor) would be a plus
</li>
<li>US Military experience a strong preference
</li>
<li>Ability to speak a second language a plus
</li>
<li>Must be available to travel approximately 20%
</li>
<li>Ability to lift 40 pounds (the approximate weight of a Griffin G510)<br>
</li>
</ul>
