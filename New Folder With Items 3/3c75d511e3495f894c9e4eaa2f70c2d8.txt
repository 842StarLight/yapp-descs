<h2>Senior Programmer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">HPOne, named CT Magazine's 2018 Great Places to Work, is looking for an Account Manager to join our team.
<br><br>
The Senior Programmer will prepare detailed specifications from which computer programs will be written. The senior programmer will communicate with users to identify current operating procedures and clarify program objectives.
<br><br>
<b>Responsibilities:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Design, develop and optimize web applications in a Microsoft based development environment</li>
<li>Troubleshoot, debug and maintain web and batch applications</li>
<li>Translate business users' requirements into actionable specifications</li>
<li>Meet programming standards by following production, productivity, quality, and customer-service standards; identify work process improvements</li>
<li>Strive to increase operating efficiency or adapt to new and improved systems</li>
<li>Architect, develop and implement software programs to meet business requirements</li>
<li>Develop application code and modules for business and technical requirements</li>
<li>Tune up design for maintainability, scalability and efficiency</li>
<li>Design and develop systems, sub-systems and programs</li>
<li>Coordinate and support technical staff and operations</li>
<li>Resolve and troubleshoot problems and complex issues</li>
<li>Perform unit tests and fix bugs</li>
<li>Integrate best qualitative practices in design and development aspects of programs</li>
</ul>
<b>Requirements:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Five + years related experience</li>
<li>Bachelor's degree in Computer Science or related field</li>
<li>Experience with leading teams for assigned projects</li>
<li>Familiarity or proficiency in the following areas: Net, Ajax, Analysis, Analyst, ASP, ASP.NET, C#, CSS, Database, Developer, Development, HTML, Javascript, jQuery, Lifecycle, Linux, OOD, Programmer, Project, SQL, SQL Server, UML, Web Developer, Windows, XML</li>
<li>Work nights/weekends/Holiday as needed</li>
<li>Understand complex issues and solve problems</li>
<li>Flexible, responsive and work effectively under pressure</li>
<li>Self-motivated and creative</li>
<li>Work well in a team oriented environment, helping others to succeed</li>
<li>Work well with and communicate with people</li>
<li>Willing to assist and support others</li>
</ul>
