<h2>Lead Massage Therapist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<ul><li>Perform various types of massage, body treatments, facials and waxing. Must be license on all services</li></ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<ul><li>
Greet and welcome guests.</li></ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<ul><li>
Follow all procedures and guidelines while administering massage and facial therapy. I.e., adhere to draping policy, refer guests with medical problems to their physicians, adhere to time length and treatment protocols when administering treatments, and conduct a pre-screening to determine any client contraindications.</li></ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<ul><li>
Produce retail sales.</li></ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<ul><li>
Hearing and visual ability to observe and detect signs of emergency situations.</li></ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<ul><li>
Must be able to lift, push, and pull a minimum of 25 lbs.</li></ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<ul>
<li>
Must be able to stand/walk up to 4-8 hours per day.</li>
<li>
Must have at least one year of lead experience</li>
<li>
Must be able to train team members on body and facial protocols</li>
<li>
Assist with inventory and ordering</li>
<li>
Complete SOPS</li>
