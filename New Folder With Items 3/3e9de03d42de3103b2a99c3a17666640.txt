<h2>Protein Buyer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Our company has been providing local restaurants with the finest ingredients available since 1915. Located in Philadelphia, we are a family owned food wholesale distribution company. In the past several years we have experience tremendous growth and we are currently looking to add to our team.</p>
<p>This position assumes full buying responsibility for protein commodity product lines. Oversees the purchasing, pricing, service level, shrink and turns of items to meet customer needs and the profitability objectives.</p>
<p><b>REQUIREMENTS/ESSENTIAL JOB FUNCTIONS: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Candidate must have a minimum of 5 years of protein commodity purchasing, pricing, and inventory control in a distribution environment.</li>
<li>Sales, and knowledge of portion control meats/proteins</li>
<li>Requires an in-depth knowledge of branded products with an emphasis on protein commodity product lines.</li>
<li>Participate in receiving and monitoring warehouse stock</li>
<li>Communicate any customer special requests to management</li>
<li>Must have familiarity with company operations including available products, pricing and promotion programs.</li>
<li>Must be proficient with computers and associated business software as well MS-Word and Excel.</li>
<li>Must have analytical and problem solving skills.</li>
<li>Negotiation skills are required.</li>
<li>Must have the ability to communicate effectively, both orally and in writing, with owners, customers, vendors and sales staff.</li>
<li>Must be detail oriented with the ability to prepare, review, and evaluate purchase contracts and associated documents.</li>
<li>Additional duties as assigned</li>
</ul>
