<div>Essential Duties and Responsibilities: Include the following: Other duties may be assigned.</div>
<div></div>
<div>Customer Service:</div>
<ul>
<li>Greet and offer assistance to customers in a prompt and friendly manner</li>
<li>Know product locations to find products for customers through the co-op</li>
<li>Study and gain knowledge about products in the department, including product preparation and related foods</li>
<li>Locate a knowledgeable person to provide information to customers when necessary.</li>
</ul>
<div>Quality:</div>
<ul>
<li>Price mark individual products before moving them to sales floor, if needed</li>
<li>Report inventory problems to supervisor</li>
<li>Stock products assuring new products are stocked behind available products on display</li>
<li>Dust and wipe clean shelves, cases and products.</li>
</ul>
<div>Department Maintenance:</div>
<ul>
<li>Open boxes for pricing and stocking safely, not cutting products or stocker</li>
<li>Stack cases safely when creating a case stack so products are easily seen</li>
<li>Clean any spills or messes in the store</li>
<li>Remove damaged, out of date, or spoiled product</li>
<li>Maintain clear pathways in aisles when stocking</li>
<li>Wash hands frequently</li>
<li>Wear protective clothing, as necessary, including gloves, boots, shoes, heavy aprons, coats, etc.</li>
<li>Watch for and report shoplifting and employee theft.</li>
</ul>
<div>Other Responsibilities:</div>
<ul>
<li>Perform other tasks as assigned by Grocery Dept. Manager</li>
<li>Attend department and storewide meetings.</li>
</ul>
<div>Responsibilities to Safety:</div>
<ul>
<li>Protect the safety of self, co-worker's, and BriarPatch Co-op patrons at all times.</li>
<li>Report any potentially harmful equipment or situations to the immediate supervisor without delay.</li>
<li>Report safety-related accidents and incidents at once to supervisor.</li>
<li>Follow all company and safety policies and procedures.</li>
<li>Operate equipment in a safe manner that will not lead to injury of self or others.</li>
</ul>
<div>Supervisory Responsibilities: None</div>
