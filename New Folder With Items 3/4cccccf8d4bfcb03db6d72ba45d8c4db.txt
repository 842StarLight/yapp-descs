<h2>Research Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">Samba TV is uniquely positioned at the forefront of the TV revolution. The way people discover, watch, and engage with television has fundamentally changed, and we're connecting the dots to help better understand audience trends and viewership habits for marketers. Help us build the first real-time cross-screen TV analytics platform that brings the power of online analytics to TV data.
<br><br>
We are looking for an individual with a passion for data and a love of television. The Associate Research Manager position will be an exciting opportunity to work in a cross-functional and client facing role at an exciting and fast growing startup. As an ARM, you will work directly with senior members of the Research, Client Services, Data Science, and Business Development teams to create and deliver research studies to a wide variety of clients and industries. In addition to duties involved with reporting, you will have an opportunity to work with product and engineering teams to help design new products and create more efficient processes. Most importantly, you enjoy working with clients to best serve their research needs.
<br><br>
<b>Responsibilities:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Consult with clients on research design and implementation</li>
<li>Lead multiple research studies at once and manage deliverables and timelines</li>
<li>Work with Associate Research Managers to implement and execute studies</li>
<li>Communicate with clients, agencies, and publishers to ensure all study materials are collected and implemented appropriately</li>
<li>Review and analyze large datasets</li>
<li>Work with Data Analysts to ensure data output meets objectives, timelines, and quality standards</li>
<li>Write and present reports directly to clients</li>
<li>Collaborate with product and engineering teams to evolve research products and processes</li>
<li>Strategize with the Sales organization to scope research prior to sale</li>
<li>Contribute to the company's Marketing/PR research reporting</li>
</ul>
<b>You:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Are an expert in quantitative research methodologies</li>
<li>Are experienced in leading and executing research studies</li>
<li>Enjoy analyzing data and solving problems</li>
<li>Are detail-oriented, highly organized, and proactive</li>
<li>Have strong verbal and written skills</li>
<li>Can communicate effectively with clients via email, over the phone, and in-person</li>
<li>Are comfortable navigating ambiguous situations</li>
<li>Thrive in a dynamic and fast-paced environment</li>
<li>Are highly driven and excited to work with cutting-edge technology</li>
<li>Love TV!</li>
</ul>
<b>Qualifications:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor's or advanced degree in business, marketing, psychology, statistics or related area of study</li>
<li>1+ years previous work in client service research role</li>
<li>Experience in digital or TV advertising research a plus</li>
<li>Proficient in Excel and PowerPoint</li>
<li>Bonus Qualifications:</li>
<li>Experience with SAS, SPSS, SQL or similar data tools</li>
<li>Knowledge of Nielsen, Rentrak, and TNS</li>
</ul>
</div>
