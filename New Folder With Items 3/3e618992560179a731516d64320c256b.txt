<h2>IT Project Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p>The primary purpose of this position is to be responsible for the successful completion of IT projects for our customer base. The project manager's involvement begins with the statement of work and last through the completion of the project. The project manager is responsible for all aspects of the project including customer communications, overall project delivery and the assignment of resources to ensure the project meets the customer's expectations.</p>
<p>
The project manager is responsible for coordinating and managing multiple projects simultaneously from initiation to project closure. The underlying goal for each project is to meet or exceed our customer's expectations. In addition, for projects that do not require a dedicated project manager, the project manager will leverage and communicate to the lead consultant to do handle any issue that may arise during the project.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Typical duties and responsibilities of the project manager include but not limited to:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Responsible for the management and success of all projects</li>
<li>
Responsible for defining and scheduling the consulting resources needed for the project</li>
<li>
Assist and manage the statement of work (SOW) process, RFP responses and proposals and ensure that we meet the SOW quality and delivery expectations of our client.</li>
<li>
Clearly communicate to clients any risks involved in project and recommend ways to avoid these risks</li>
<li>
Responsible for creating project plans, project setup in our internal system</li>
<li>
Provide pre-sales assistance for new project / service opportunities but helping to define the project scope (i.e.; customer goals, solution requirements, business impact, constraints, assumptions, resource requirements)</li>
<li>
Ensure project deliverables are completed on time and meet customer expectations</li>
<li>
Continuously provide status updates to stakeholders throughout the project life cycle</li>
<li>
Assist in the creation of project plans, status reports, and change request / approval forms</li>
<li>
Monitor resource availability and ensure resources are being utilized effectively to meet internal operational goals</li>
<li>
Work closely with Account Managers to keep them abreast of new project opportunities and to report status of projects</li>
<li>
Ensure timing of product delivery with the project schedule</li>
<li>
Monitor out of scope work and ensure customer / stakeholders are made aware of predicted scope changes</li>
<li>
Monitor work progress and report variance (budget vs. worked performed to date)</li>
<li>
Facilitate meetings throughout project lifecycle, including final project closure meeting with customer to formally close out the project</li>
<li>
Periodic reporting to our management on project, SOW and deliverable status<br>
