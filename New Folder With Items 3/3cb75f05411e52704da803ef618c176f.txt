<b>Purpose:</b><br>
<br>
The e-Sales Office Manager is the direct Supervisor of the e-Sales Agents ("the Agents") and is responsible for the following but not limited to: managing the Agents, ensure that department metrics and goals are met in relation to customer contacts, appointments set, appointments show and sold.<br>
<br>
 <b>Duties and Responsibilities:</b><br>
<br>
Ensure that department metrics and goals are met in relation to appointments set, appointments show and sold for Internet and Inbound phone leads<br>
<ul>
<li>Maintain and increase ROI levels set by the company</li>
<li>Assist in the process of generating the sales of vehicles, service, aftermarket products and finance products</li>
<li>Assist in the training of sales and the Agents as it relates to the e-Sales office functions</li>
<li>Be proficient in dealing with customers via telephone and email</li>
<li>Measure and maintain performance levels set by the company</li>
<li>Compile all necessary reports on a timely basis</li>
<li>Manage employee schedules and time clock reporting for payroll</li>
<li>Measure, monitor and report all shows and sales (delivered) deals by the Agents for commissions and bonus</li>
<li>Hiring, evaluating, managing, counseling, terminating and disciplining of all BDC personnel</li>
<li>Effectively utilize CRM and Lead Management tools</li>
<li>Install processes and train employees</li>
<li>Be knowledgeable about sales process, business philosophy, and products</li>
<li>Knowledge of office practices and administrative procedures</li>
<li>Train staff all processes described in the Sonic e-Sales Office Playbook</li>
<li>Will follow behavioral expectations consistent with the Company's visions/values</li>
</ul>
<b>Qualifications:</b><br>
<ul>

<li>Ability to work in call center environment</li>
<li>Ability to organize and handle multiple tasks with flexibility</li>
<li>Ability to thrive in a team environment and consistently display professional demeanor.</li>
</ul>
