<h2>Senior Director Engineering- Industrial</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>Sensata Technologies is looking for a Senior Design Engineering Director for its 600+M Industrial Business Unit. Primary responsibilities include overall leadership, management of business processes and design, resource planning (people, capital and expenses), competitor benchmark, and M&amp;A capabilities assessment and integration. This person will be responsible for all new product development and sustaining engineering for the Industrial business.
<br>
<br>
The ideal candidate has a strong background in the Industrial space with responsibilities in a large global engineering team reaching from mechanical and electrical designs to software layer in order to deliver full sub-system capabilities. Operationally, he/she will have strong business acumen in competitive technologies analysis, quality and return on investment. He/She needs to have experience in change management to deliver improved performance and use critical thinking to prioritize business decisions.
<br>
In addition, he/she will need to have a forward-looking perspective and should be constantly seeking to drive innovation and improvements for the future.
<br>
<br>
Success in this position requires good market trends understanding, good team-building and influencing skills; both in working with internal teams, external teams and customers. Often times this position may require keen negotiations with internal and external teams to develop a win-win situation for the business and customers. This position reports to the Vice President of the Industrial business unit.
