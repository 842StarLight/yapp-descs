<p><b>
Role Overview:</b></p>
<p>
The competitive intelligence lead will be part of the Portfolio Marketing team at Secureworks. He/she will be responsible for competitive analysis of Secureworks competitors in the cybersecurity industry. In this role, he/she will develop a deep understanding of Secureworks offerings and become the trusted advisor for all things related to competitive information. At the core of this role is technical competitive analysis and the ability to turn this into high-impact competitive intelligence content. The competitive intelligence developed will help to fuel successful go-to-market and sales acceleration. In order to be successful in this role, he / she will need to be able to effectively collaborate across multiple groups within Secureworks and be passionate about cybersecurity. This is a high-visibility and high-impact role within Secureworks.<br>
</p>
<p><br>
<b>Key Responsibilities
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Manage the competitive analysis effort at Secureworks</li>
<li>
Provide competitive analysis to support internal product management and marketing organization in terms of development of positioning, messaging and product strategy</li>
<li>
Develop and deliver sales enablement material to help improve overall sales effectiveness and performance</li>
<li>
Support product launches and influence messaging by providing competitive insights</li>
</ul>
