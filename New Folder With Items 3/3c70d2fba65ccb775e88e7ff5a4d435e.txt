<h2>Integrated Business Planning Process Lead</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>
<p><b>Careers that Change Lives</b></p>
<p>Medtronic’s Supply Chain Transformation initiative is an enterprise initiative focused on process, organization and technology. The Cardiac and Vascular Group (CVG) is looking for an Integrated Business Planning (IBP) Process Leader who will be responsible for enabling a Global approach and mindset across all Business Groups and Regions by engaging leaders from across functions. This role will partner with the Enterprise Plan Process Owner, Business Unit Management and Region Process Leads to build a common approach for CVG while leveraging the industry best practice IBP framework. The Process Lead has ownership to deploy across all CVG businesses and monitors process KPIs to ensure maturity.</p>
<br>
<div></div>
<p>Medtronic is intensely focused on creating a workplace environment which reflects our standing as the world’s top medical device company. The ideal candidate will have a passion for the patients we serve and an unrelenting desire to improve our business.</p>
<br>
<div></div>
<p><b>A Day in the Life</b></p>
<p>The IBP Process Lead defines and embeds the process strategy and approach as the new way of working, this includes a focus on processes and mindset/behavior shifts, to support business decision-making.</p>
<br>
<div></div>
<p>Responsibilities may include the following and other duties may be assigned.<br>
</p>
<div></div>
<ul>
<li>Responsible for ongoing process leadership in alignment with Enterprise Process Owner (EPO) leadership</li>
<li>
Responsible for modifying the process to fit the needs of Medtronic and culture, leveraging internal and external experts.</li>
<li>
Collaborates with business leaders and subprocess representatives to ensure that the process and practices are aligned with the principles of IBP and that processes are designed to meet the requirements of the business.</li>
<li>
Documents processes, procedures, and training materials in alignment with common Global Supply Chain (GSC) standards</li>
<li>
Drives continuous improvement and monitors the achievement of identified process performance metrics</li>
<li>
Facilitates connection with Regional Process Leads to maintain alignment on process improvements and understand Region and Group challenges/needs</li>
<li>
Develops training curriculum and materials for Groups and Regions on processes, tools and metrics</li>
<li>
Assess and maintain KPIs for process area reflecting overall goals of the Plan processes</li>
<li>
Partner with Medtronic Plan network (Demand, Supply, Inventory and Product Lifecycle Management) to identify technology selections in support of the Plan processes.</li>
</ul>
</div>
