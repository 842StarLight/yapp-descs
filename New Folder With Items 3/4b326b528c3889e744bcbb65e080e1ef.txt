<h2>Order Processing Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>POSITION SUMMARY</b></p>
<p>
Diono, a leading juvenile products manufacturer based in Sumner, Washington is looking for an Order Processing Specialist to join our team. This position is responsible for performing work related to corporate supply chain performance targets. Specifically, the job entails focus on inventory reduction, increased inventory turns, improved PO placement-to-delivery results and dedicated support to sales order administration for international and EDI customers. This role reports to the Purchasing Manager.</p>
<p><b>
ESSENTIAL DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Support On-Time deliveries, Fill Rates, PO strategies, Inventory Control, Safety Stock and Days of Supply goals at “Best in Class” levels.</li>
<li>Develop a deep understanding of sales forecasting and effects on supply chain operations</li>
<li>Leads, coordinates, and executes sales order entry efforts and Purchase Order submission in support of international FOB customers.</li>
<li>Administrates release of Purchase Orders to suppliers in support of Purchasing department functions.</li>
<li>Will serve as back-up resource for EDI order acknowledgement for EDI-enabled customers in the absence of the primary resource.</li>
<li>Support maintenance of ERP databases for accuracy with inbound Purchase Orders and drive data improvements in support of optimal inventory decisions
</li>
<li>Evaluate inventory, sales and vendor performance analysis
</li>
<li>Develop quarterly reporting scorecard of Suppliers for on-time deliveries and other logistics metrics.</li>
<li>Query complex data warehouse and multiple data sources for strategic reporting and analyses.</li>
<li>Analyze business processes to quantify costs or values and identify opportunities for improvement.</li>
<li>Collaborate with Sales, Finance, Operations, and Product Development with strategies product
</li>
<li>Collaborate with Sales, Product Management, and Finance as a leader of quantitative analysis, and with PM and Operations on metrics related to Material Management, End of Life, Discontinued Stock and varied strategies for Purchase Orders.</li>
<li>Other duties may be assigned as needed</li>
</ul>
<p><b>QUALIFICATIONS</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree (B.A. or B.S.) from four-year college or university in Supply Chain and/or Logistics Management and two to four years related experience and/or training preferably in a similar role; or equivalent combination of education and experience.
</li>
<li>Strong analytical, communication and collaboration skills.</li>
<li>Technical/Functional Competencies: SAP, SCM/ERM, Excel, Word and Access applications.</li>
<li>Proficiency in Excel is an absolute requirement (pivot tables, vlookups, macros).</li>
<li>Experienced and familiar with demands of Big-Box Retailers (i.e. Amazon, WalMart, Target, etc.).</li>
<li>Have the solid vision and strategy skill set to develop, streamline and improve processes.</li>
<li>Written and verbal communication skills.</li>
<li>Ability to communicate effectively with key process partner.</li>
<li>Self-directed, passionate and energetic.</li>
<li>Demonstrates operational curiosity to challenge status quo.</li>
<li>Strong business acumen demonstrating ability to work independently but seeks guidance when needed</li>
<li>Familiarity with Consumer Products, Textiles and Juvenile Products a plus.</li>
<li>Advanced ability to operate computers and related software programs.</li>
</ul>
