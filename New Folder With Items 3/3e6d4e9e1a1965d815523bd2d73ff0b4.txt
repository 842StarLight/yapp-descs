<h2>Senior Buyer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p>Clever Devices is seeking a Senior Buyer to join our growing Supply Chain and Procurement operations in our Long Island (Woodbury, NY) offices. In this role, the Sr. Buyer will manage the purchasing activities for parts and services for assigned commodities. Processes MRP output from the ERP system. This encompasses quoting, processing purchase orders, executing reschedule messages, resolving receiving and invoice issues. The Sr. Buyer will work with Supplier Quality to ensure parts meet quality and reliability requirements, and help identify new Suppliers and participates in cost reduction programs</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<br>
<br>
<p><b>Primary Responsibilities:<br>
</b></p>
<p></p>
<br>
<ul>
<li>Analyze output from the ERP system<br>
</li>
<li>Release Purchase orders to approved Suppliers generated from MRP<br>
</li>
<li>Verify Supplier acknowledgments to Purchase order detail<br>
</li>
<li>Process MRP exception messages.<br>
</li>
<li>Manage material due dates to meet Production and Sales order requirements<br>
</li>
<li>Maintain Supplier details in ERP system<br>
</li>
<li>Process manual purchase requisitions from all departments<br>
</li>
<li>Drive down material cost while maintaining appropriate quality levels and delivery requirements<br>
</li>
<li>Reconcile Credit card statements with Accounts Payable<br>
</li>
<li>Manage long lead-time parts and EOL parts<br>
</li>
<li>Assist Customer Service with any queries regarding status of critical items<br>
</li>
<li>Manage sub-contractor relationships<br>
</li>
</ul>

</li>

</li>

</li>

</li>

</li>

</li>

</li>
<li>Strong computer skills including Excel, Word and, and ability to learn new software programs such as Microsoft Dynamics AX<br>

</ul>
