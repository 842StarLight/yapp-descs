<h2>Account Executive</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<ul><li> Are you the kind of salesperson that loves to get in front of perspective customers?*</li></ul>
<ul><li> Do you thrive on getting client meetings because you’re gifted at building relationships that turn into sales?*</li></ul>
<p>As an Account Executive for Xerox Business Systems (XBS), you would take on a critical outside sales role. You'll work harder and smarter as you think through ways to help businesses improve their workflow using Xerox hardware, high-end software, and managed services. Professionals who do extremely well in this role (six-figure income opportunity), are good at:</p>
<ul><li>HIGH levels of sales activity, including cold calling, appointment setting, and "tele-prospecting" to a wide variety of industries while also taking care of current customers.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Being your customers' #1 advocate and ensuring their needs are met.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Giving demos, presentations, and collaborating with our incredible sales specialists on your accounts.</li></ul>
<p><i>Sound good so far? Let's explore it further....</i></p>
<p><b>Here's the kind of person you’ll need to be in this role: </b></p>
<ul><li>Tenacious, highly self-motivated, and loves to win!</li></ul>
<ul><li>Excellent at building new relationships and aren't intimidated to network with C-level executives.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Doesn’t have an IT degree, but enjoys learning about new technology such as hardware, software, and technical services.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Has a reputation for achieving your goals and overcoming obstacles along the way.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Very professional, ethical, and well-organized.</li></ul>
<p><b>And here's why you'll love working with us: </b></p>
<p>XBS is part of Xerox, a Fortune 1000 Company that operates in over 120 countries. MT Business Technologies, Inc. is a division of XBS, so when you join us, you're part of an industry titan that has a tremendous impact on Xerox's bottom-line. In addition, you'll also enjoy:</p>
<ul><li>Incredible income potential, including base salary, uncapped commission and bonus opportunities.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Competitive prizes and give-aways for top performers, including a Trip of Excellence to incredible destinations!</li></ul>
<ul><li>A fun, team-oriented company culture filled with people you'll really enjoy having as co-workers.</li></ul>
<ul><li>World-class training that truly equips you for your assignment, and so much more!</li></ul>
