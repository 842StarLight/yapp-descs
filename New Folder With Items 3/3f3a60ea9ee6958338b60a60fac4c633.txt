WHAT ARE WE LOOKING FOR?
<br><br>

<b>YOU WILL GET THE CHANCE TO:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Develop and deliver written solution proposals and presentations to clients including responses to RFPs/RFIs/RFQs</li>
<li>Analyze requirements and develop customers' business cases</li>
<li>Create and deliver demos and presentations to map Globant's benefits to specific business and technical needs</li>
<li>Assist Sales teams in getting answers to questions and resolving technical issues</li>
<li>Serve as liaison between sales and operations</li>
<li>Stay abreast of internal changes, updates of Globant solutions and capabilities to facilitate sales efforts</li>
<li>Maintain technical, market, and industry knowledge</li>
</ul>
<b>WHAT WILL MAKE YOU SUCCEED:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Engineering, Information Systems, Business, related field or equivalent years of experience</li>
<li>2+ years of sales experience, especially selling technology and related professional services to regional and global enterprises</li>
<li>5+ years in software development or pre-sales with proven ability to understand and analyze prospects needs and identify, develop and present appropriate solutions</li>
<li>Experience in services model on a global basis that includes: up-front implementation, service level agreements, operations, on-going customer management and change management</li>
<li>Excellent customer service, oral and written communications skills</li>
<li>Strong problem solving, presentation and organizational skills</li>
<li>Independent, self-starter with excellent matrix management skills</li>
<li>Ability to master a broad range of expertise ranging from understanding the business process to gathering and analyzing business and technical requirements</li>
</ul>
