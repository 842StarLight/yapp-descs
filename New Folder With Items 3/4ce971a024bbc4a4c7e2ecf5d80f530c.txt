<h2>Data/Business Analyst</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Infiniti Consulting Group is searching for an Data/Business Analyst in the Folsom, California area.</p>
<p>
Primary Focus</p>
<ul>
<li>Amazon Web Services (AWS) Account Management</li>
<li>Cloud Partnership Management (AWS, Azure, Google)</li>
<li>CRM and Sales Pipeline Management</li>
<li>Employee Resource Planning and Mapping</li>
</ul>
<p>Key tasks will include performing detailed requirements analysis of these various systems, documenting processes and procedures, frequently maintaining/updating sources of information and communicating current status to identified members of management.</p>
<p>
Responsibilities include:</p>
<ul>
<li>Performing requirements analysis.</li>
<li>Evaluating business processes, anticipating requirements, researching areas for improvement, selecting, developing and implementing solutions, monitoring performance.</li>
<li>Leading ongoing reviews of focus areas and developing optimization strategies in each focus area.</li>
<li>Conducting meetings and presentations to share findings and gather input.</li>
<li>Gathering critical information from meetings with various stakeholders and producing useful reports.</li>
<li>Effectively communicating insights and findings to cross-functional team members and management.</li>
<li>Reviewing allocation of resources and work to maintain cost efficiency. Communicating variances in resource planning and scheduling. Monitoring changes in plan.</li>
<li>Updating, implementing and maintaining procedures.</li>
<li>Prioritizing work based on business needs and requirements.</li>
</ul>
<p>Minimum Requirements:</p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree in business, finance, computer studies or related field.</li>
<li>Two years’ experience in business, financial analysis, or a related field; alternatively, Masters degree in business or computer-related field.</li>
<li>Exceptional analytical and conceptual thinking skills.</li>
<li>The ability to influence stakeholders and work closely with them to determine acceptable solutions.</li>
<li>Advanced technical skills.</li>
<li>Excellent documentation skills.</li>
<li>Competency in Microsoft applications including Excel, Word, PowerPoint, Visio, and Outlook.</li>
<li>Experience creating detailed reports and giving presentations.</li>
<li>Excellent planning, organizational, and time management skills.</li>
</ul>
<p>Desired</p>
<ul>
<li>Experience using customer relationship management tools strongly desired.</li>
<li>Associate level cloud certification or designation strongly desired.</li>
<li>Experience analyzing Amazon Web Services accounts strongly desired.</li>
<li>Experience with cloud platform management tools strongly desired.</li>
</ul>
