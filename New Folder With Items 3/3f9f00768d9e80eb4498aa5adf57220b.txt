<h2>Project Designer/Planner</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>
<div>Towmaster is GROWING and looking to add a Project Designer to our team!!</div>
<div>As a Project Designer at Towmaster, you will develop designs and detailed drawings of Towmaster products using company-established drawing standards. Develop and maintains bills of materials and production routers Processes sales orders into design and build packages to be built by manufacturing.</div>
<div></div>
<div>
<b>Additional Essential Functions</b>:</div>
<ul>
<li>Use engineering principles that meet Towmaster design standards and Federal, State, County and Municipal requirements.</li>
<li>Prepare designs and detail drawings from sales orders.</li>
<li>Use completed design package to enter BOM and production routers.</li>
<li>Reviews piece part drawings and establish best method of manufacturing for the creation of manufacturing router.</li>
<li>Investigate, assess and implement change requests.</li>
<li>Enter service work orders into ERP/MRP.</li>
<li>Assist production to resolve various design issues that may arise.</li>
<li>After completion of order, ensures all materials and purchased components have been issued to the work order.</li>
<li>Inherent skills required:
<ul>
<li>Must be self-motivated and organized.</li>
<li>Must be able to work independently or within a team environment.</li>
<li>Must be able to multi-task.</li>
</ul>
</li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<div>
<b>Physical Demands</b>:</div>
<div>Working conditions typically associated with an office setting. Some walking, stair climbing, lifting, pushing and pulling approximately 20-25 lbs. Motor skills are needed for writing, answering calls, and computer operations.</div>
<div></div>
<div>
<b>Qualifications</b>:</div>
An AAS Degree in Computer Aided Drafting and Design or equivalent work experience with a minimum of three years of mechanical design experience.. 3 years of experience using MRP/ERP systems in a manufacturing environment is required. Strong computer skills required. Problem solving abilities. Proficiency with MS Office Suite, and familiarity with Solid Works preferred (or similar CAD software), 2-D drafting products (AutoCAD, Draftsight, etc<b>
