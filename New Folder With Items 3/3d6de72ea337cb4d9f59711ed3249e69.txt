<h2>Regional Practice Manager - Primary Care Offices</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>A member of Trinity Health, Holy Cross Hospital is a full-service, non-profit, Catholic, teaching hospital operating in the spirit of the Sisters of Mercy. This is a FT position on day shift with benefits. This position is responsible for managing several primary care office.</p>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>
Manages day to day activities of all assigned practice locations.</li>
<li>
Assures productive, full schedules for each provider. Assists Director of Operations in finding replacement staff.</li>
<li>
Assists with employee screening and interviews. Works with the human resources department on successful on boarding of new employees.</li>
<li>
Performs time keeping reporting and monitoring.</li>
<li>
Handles all staff issues, complaints and concerns in a timely and confidential manner. Understands and applies disciplinary/discharge policy as appropriate.</li>
<li>
Evaluates team members and perform associate reviews.</li>
<li>
Ensures office adherence to OSHA, TJC and CMS standards for safety, infection control.</li>
<li>
Manages all office audits, practice reviews and reporting.</li>
<li>
Assures follow up of all billing and documentation inquiries.</li>
<li>
Communicates frequently with Director of operations on status of all practice functions.</li>
<li>
Responsible for productivity, financial, compliance reports, and sending daily, weekly and monthly practice reports.</li>
<li>
Motivates, communicates, and provides a positive environment in which all employees find job satisfaction.</li>
<li>
Performs other duties as assigned</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Minimum Qualifications (education, experience)</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
College degree in business administration, commerce, management, medical technology, nursing or industrial engineering or more than five years of medical office management experience required.</li>
<li>
Industry relevant experience.</li>
<li>
Knowledge and experience in organizational effectiveness and operations management.</li>
<li>
Knowledge of business and project management principles and practices.</li>
<li>
Knowledge of financial and accounting principles and practices.</li>
<li>
Knowledge of human resource principles and practices.</li>
<li>
Information technology skills including but not limited to practice management, EHR and hospital billing and reporting systems.</li>
</ul>
