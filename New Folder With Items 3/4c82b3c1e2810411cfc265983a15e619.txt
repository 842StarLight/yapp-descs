<h2>Senior Technical Content Developer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>
<div>Bazaarvoice connects brands and retailers to consumers, so that every shopping experience feels personal. From search and discovery to purchase and advocacy, Bazaarvoice’s solutions reach in-market shoppers, personalize their experiences, and give them the confidence to buy. Each month in the Bazaarvoice Network, more than a billion consumers view and share authentic content including reviews, questions and answers, and social photos across 6,000 brand and retail websites. Across the network, Bazaarvoice captures billions of shopper signals monthly - data that powers high-efficiency digital advertising and personalization with unmatched relevance.</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
As a Senior Technical Content Developer, you will join a highly talented global research and development team focused on delivering high-quality content that enlightens our clients and inspires them to do more with our solutions. You won’t do it alone, in this role you will enjoy the support of other content developers and a network of passionate subject-matter experts. We’re a close-knit team, working on a rapidly evolving suite of solutions, and we’re seeking someone who is ready to bring creativity and enthusiasm to help take our client experience to the next level. We are looking for someone who understands how to make technical content clear, concise, and relevant. Our clients must be able to quickly and easily integrate our solutions with their websites.</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
Our team ensures that written guidance in Bazaarvoice solutions is clear, consistent, and improves client understanding. We further develop the knowledge resources that clients and support personnel rely on to implement and use BV solutions. During the development and roll-out process, you'll play a key role in a partnership with engineers, product managers, and services teams.</div>
