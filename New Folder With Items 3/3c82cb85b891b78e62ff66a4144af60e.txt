<h2>Production Assistant / Operations Coordinator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Mimosa Digital / Perfectly Invited, a printing and stationery shop, is currently seeking a self-motivated, independent, reliable individual. The main portion of the job is to handle all of the print orders. Filling this position to the full capacity would mean:</p>
<ul>
<li>working on multiple projects simultaneously,</li>
<li>working within a deadline-driven industry,</li>
<li>having incredible attention to detail, and</li>
<li>possessing the skill-set to work out some technical problems with computers as well as the printers.</li>
</ul>
<p>In addition to having the skills listed above, we’re looking for someone who maintains a friendly and positive attitude while working. A “can-do” attitude is necessary as you will be responsible for handling the print production of jobs generated by the sales associates and graphic designers, and providing well-executed work to suit the customers’ project needs. When customers request for a job to be done a certain way that isn’t quite possible, we need you to be able to offer appropriate alternative solutions to help the customer accomplish what they are looking for.</p>
<p>You’re going to be multi-tasking and you’re going to be managing some projects. We want someone who looks at that with eagerness! It’s an exciting and fast-paced environment! You have to be comfortable figuring out solutions using different software and several different pieces of equipment. We also want someone who will talk to a customer and help them execute their project!</p>
<ul><li>Do you have a little retail experience that would come in handy?</li></ul>
<p>Yes? Excellent! No? As long as you’re eager to learn, it’s something we can work on!</p>
<ul>
<li>Minimum requirement: working knowledge of the Adobe suite and MS Office.</li>
<li>and a high school diploma or equivalent.</li>
<li>Graphical training is a plus. Any art background is also a plus!</li>
</ul>
<p>Your job duties will include the following, but custom projects and new machines might require you to change it up every once in a while. We hope this role interests you!</p>
<ul>
<li>Operate machines that print, finish and bind as well as video equipment</li>
<li>Set-up the equipment, run jobs, proof final output and perform quality control</li>
<li>Understand and make fine adjustments to equipment when necessary</li>
<li>Perform regular and preventative maintenance on the machines</li>
<li>As needed, interact directly with customers to provide solutions for their projects</li>
<li>Help grow the business by suggesting other uses of existing equipment and solutions not currently advertised or offered to customers</li>
<li>Paper is heavy. You will have to possess the ability to lift heavy cases of paper (up to 35 lbs.)</li>
</ul>
<p>Visit our websites (Mimosa Digital &amp; Perfectly Invited) to learn more about what we do. If you love it as much as we do, we’d love to hear from you!</p>
<p>We will consider full-time and part-time candidates. Full-time candidates will receive health benefits.</p>
