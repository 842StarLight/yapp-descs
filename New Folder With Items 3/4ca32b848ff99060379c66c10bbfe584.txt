<h2>Account Executive, Eagle Recorder/Appraisal & Tax</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p>The Eagle™ product suite provides appraiser, assessor, treasurer, and official records solutions to county offices. It manages functions such as calculating real estate values, assessing properties, and managing the taxation distribution/collection, along with land recording functions such as imaging, indexing, redaction, receipting, and search and retrieval.</p>
<p>The primary territories for this position will be focused in the <b>midwest</b>. We look forward to having you join our team!</p>
<p>Work with our technology-driven solutions for property assessment and tax software and mass appraisal services. From data collection to computer-assisted mass appraisal to tax billing and collections, you can help develop appraisal and tax solutions for the public sector.</p>
