<h2>Client Success Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p>The Client Success Manager is responsible for management and nurturing of the overall client relationship and coordination of all deliverables for Experity’s Clockwise key clients. This position works in collaboration with internal teams including Sales, Implementation, Support, Development and Product, among others and oversees the unified delivery of our solutions within committed parameters.</p>
<p><b>
Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Manage the relationship with the client leadership team for Experity’s Clockwise clients.</li>
<li>
Meet with client management on a regular basis and build relationships at multiple levels. Communication and documentation cadence to include, at minimum, monthly business review meetings that focus on optimization reporting and software utilization.</li>
<li>
Responsible for ensuring service level commitments are met for each client. Coordinate and hold accountable internal service teams as necessary to ensure successful delivery of our products and services.</li>
<li>
Support the implementation and training process with understanding of system configuration and client workflows to ensure a successful client experience.</li>
<li>
Support client adherence to prescriptive best practice setup and configuration of our products, monitor performance metrics and aid in identification of opportunities for improvement.</li>
<li>
Build an understanding of client workflows and common market problems pertaining to the ClockwiseMD solution.</li>
<li>
Increase customer satisfaction by understanding business needs and supporting expanded utilization of Experity solutions. Elevate client relationships from vendor/partner to trusted business advisor.</li>
<li>
Probes for expansion opportunities and involves Sales and Implementation for demos and contracting services</li>
<li>
Actively initiates and engages in client recovery efforts upon receipt of client notice of intent to terminate services</li>
<li>
Engages with members of internal teams to ensure client projects are completed and questions are answered in a timely and efficient manner. Stay up-to-speed in all new product releases to share with clients.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
QUALIFICATIONS &amp; SKILLS</b></p>
<p><b>
Required:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Bachelor’s degree or equivalent experience</li>
<li>
1 year of experience in Project Management, Client Relationship Management or related client success role</li>
<li>
Excellent communication skills (written and oral) as well as the ability to explain technical information in non-technical language to all levels of an organization</li>
<li>
Strong organization skills and multi-tasking required</li>
<li>
Problem-solving and conflict resolution aptitude</li>
<li>
Some travel required, up to 20%</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Preferred:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Worked previously with Software as a Service</li>
<li>
Knowledge of Healthcare environment workflow</li>
</ul>

 <b>Every team member exhibits our core values:</b></p>

Team First</li>
<li>
