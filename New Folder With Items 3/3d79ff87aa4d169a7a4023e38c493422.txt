<h2>Director, Data Engineering</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p><b>Director, Data Engineering | Informa Intelligence - Atlanta, GA</b></p>
<p>
The <b>Director, Data Engineering </b>is the overall data leader for the Asset Intelligence group of Informa " including the EquipmentWatch, Price Digests, FleetSeek and CostTrax brands. This role oversees data structuring, data product creation, data quality, BI dashboard development, and technical sales support.</p>
<p>
As data businesses, <b>our products are data.</b> Our analyst teams track market activity at scale, turning that raw data into well-structured and actional datasets. Using proprietary algorithms, our teams create benchmark data and build predictive models for asset values, costs and rental rates. Additionally, our teams have built industry-best specification and VIN-decoding APIs, leveraging data from open resources as well as from strategic partners in-market. Lastly, our analysts are actively involved in strategic account selling, delivering customized BI dashboards, competitive intelligence reports, API integrations, and more.</p>
<p><b>
As the Director, Data Engineering, you will lead one of the most advanced data and intelligence teams in the equipment and commercial trucking industries, aiming to move our business to the next level on a global stage.</b></p>
<p><b>
The Asset Intelligence group of DaaS/SaaS businesses is a group of successful, strategically-connected businesses</b> with modern, fresh products which have built a performance-centric, marketing-first culture; they rely on scaled, innovative data teams and industry-leading market positions.</p>
<p><b>
Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
<b>Create scalable solutions</b> to manage the collection, processing, calculation, and quality control of data between analyst and production environments to keep equipment databases in line with industry trends</li>
<li>Develop expertise and innovation for <b>quality control processes</b>
</li>
<li>
<b>Partner with product management</b> to create new data product solutions, including data attributes, databases, and APIs</li>
<li>
<b>Create a culture of constant training</b>, ensure analysts are skilled in modern languages, such as R, Python, and SQL.</li>
<li>
<b>Manage external data acquisition partners</b>, driving significant growth in data partner count</li>
<li>
<b>1020% travel</b>, supporting strategic accounts sales efforts and industry conference attendance</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Requirements</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
<b>Minimum 10+ years previous experience leading large, diverse quantitative teams, </b>ideally in the subscription product world</li>
<li>
<b>Strong interpersonal and communication skills</b> with the ability to partner and work effectively with senior business leaders and partners across a larger, matrixed organization</li>
<li>
<b>A data guru, </b>able to quickly understand data creation processes, including acquisition, normalization, algorithm creation, quality, and release in market.</li>
<li>
<b>But not just a quant. </b>The ideal candidate has proven customer appreciation and knows customer proximity not only leads to more customer happiness and sales, but better data products</li>
<li>
<b>Strong leadership skills</b> with the ability to drive culture and high performance for 20+ analysts and 30 core stakeholders.</li>
<li><b>Relevant industry experience a plus.</b></li>
<li>
<b>Bachelor's degree required.</b> Masters in analytics, statistics, or applied statistics preferred.</li>
</ul>
