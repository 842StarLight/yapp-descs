<h2>Administrative Assistant</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>In 1990 Crawford Electric Supply opened its first store in Dallas, Texas and began a tradition of excellence. Within five years of opening, the store was ranked #1 in the twin city market. Crawford has grown from one location and six associates to 26 locations across Texas and Louisiana and more than 650 associates. Over the last 25 years, Crawford Electric has lived up to, and exceeded its motto of "Whatever it Takes!" Crawford is a subsidiary of Sonepar USA.</p>
<p><b>
PURPOSE</b></p>
<p>
Responsible for providing clerical and administrative support to a branch or an area of branches</p>
<p><b>
RESPONSIBILITIES</b></p>
<br>
<div>
<ul>
<li>Responsible for dispatching Incoming Calls</li>
<li>Collect and apply all charges from 3rd party freight carriers to sales orders Pull request proof of delivery information</li>
<li>Process all overhead billing</li>
</ul>
</div>
<ul><li>Maintain Office Supplies for Branch</li></ul>
<br>
<p>
