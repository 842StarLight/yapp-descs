<h2>Accounts Receivable Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Position Summary
</b></p>
<p>This specific role will report directly to the Accounts Receivable Manager and support the finance organization. In this capacity, the individual will perform accounts receivable functions including billing, cash posting and collections.
</p>
<p><b>Job Duties
</b></p>
<p><b>SCOPE AND RESPONSIBILITIES:</b></p>
<p>The Accounts Receivable Specialist is responsible for accurate and timely billing, cash posting and credit memo processing, communications with customers both written and verbal, and interaction with internal customers.
</p>
<p>The Accounts Receivable Specialist is a key part of the Director and Corporate Controller’s team and is expected to perform the following responsibilities:</p>
<p><i>Accounts Receivable</i></p>
<ul>
<li>Complete accounts receivable functions in a thorough, accurate and timely manner
<ul>
<li>Review Packing Slip report for errors</li>
<li>Process customer invoices and credit memos</li>
<li>Review Core Check-in (CCI) documents, resolve overages/shortages, and process related credit memos</li>
<li>Review lockbox and bank report by customer, request support from customer as needed, process cash postings</li>
<li>Run Outstanding Statement and follow up with customer on any old outstanding invoices</li>
<li>Handle all customer inquiries in a timely manner</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>Ensure all customers are invoiced per contract terms and collection issues are handled in a timely manner</li>
<li>Collaborate with Sales and Pricing departments and other internal customers to resolve issues</li>
<li>Comply with BBB financial policies and procedures within accounts receivable</li>
<li>Communicate identified financial issues proactively to Accounts Receivable Manager
</li>
<li>Support all audit requests relating to accounts receivable as requested</li>
</ul>
<p><i>Other Duties</i></p>
<ul>
<li>Support implementation/updates of accounting systems as they relate accounts receivable</li>
</ul>
<p><b>WORK ENVIRONMENT:</b></p>
<p>Work is performed mostly in a controlled office atmosphere.</p>
<p><b>PHYSICAL DEMANDS:</b></p>
<p>Sitting 70%, standing/walking 30%. Ability to lift and carry up to 20 pounds is required. The physical demands described here are representative of those that must be met by an employee to successfully perform the essential function of this job. While performing the duties of this position, the employee is regularly required to walk, stand, use hands and fingers to handle or feel; talk and hear.</p>
<p>Note: This job description is not intended to be all-inclusive. Employee may perform other related duties as needed to meet the ongoing needs of the organization.</p>
<p><b>ON-THE-JOB TRAINING REQUIRED:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>BBB 101</li>
</ul>
<p><b>MINIMUM REQUIREMENTS</b></p>
<p>Minimum Qualifications</p>
<ul><li>Bachelor’s degree or equivalent combination of education and experience in Accounting, Business or related field
</li></ul>
<p>Minimum Experience</p>
<ul>
<li>A minimum of 2 years of accounts receivable experience</li>
<li>Experience with Oracle or other ERP software
</li>
<li>Knowledge of the end-to-end accounts receivable process
</li>
<li>Excellent verbal and written skills necessary</li>
</ul>
<p>Personal Attributes</p>
<ul>
<li>Demonstrated independent and objective thinking to properly evaluate, communicate, educate, influence and gain cooperation in reaching consensus, decisions and action</li>
<li>Effective communication and customer service skills for dealing with internal and external customers</li>
<li>Curiosity and drive to understand underlying issues and to simplify process, resolve problems and pro-actively present issues to manager</li>
<li>Results-oriented with a strong sense of urgency and skill for driving teams to results</li>
<li>Very detail-oriented professional with a desire to dig into the details to understand data and customer trends</li>
<li>Solid organizational skills and ability to balance multiple priorities in a dynamic environment</li>
<li>Must be able to maintain an effective working relationships with, and among co-workers and supervisors</li>
</ul>
