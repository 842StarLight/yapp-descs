<b>The Role</b><br>
This position will perform and deliver on routine and non-routine assignments for his/her assigned area(s) of responsibility independently in accordance with established procedures/guidelines. S/he will serve as the direct point of contact for facilities service requests and will administer day-to-day facilities services activities.</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div><b>Responsibilities</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Supports and promotes change management and/or departmental/enterprise initiatives within assigned area(s) of responsibility.</li>
<li>Meets or exceeds departmental quality and service standards.</li>
<li>Works overtime as needed.</li>
<li>Delivers routine work independently, in accordance with established procedures and guidelines, in a timely manner and meets deadlines appropriately.</li>
<li>Makes routine and non-routine decisions, applying limited but increasingly more discretion within role except for matters of significance which affect the business as a whole or a significant part of it, in accordance with established procedures and guidelines.</li>
<li>Applies expanded knowledge obtained from the role in increasingly more complex situations and continues to acquire more knowledge to apply in role.</li>
<li>Provides a diverse range of information and performs a diverse range of tasks/transactions related to common programs and services.</li>
<li>Organizes and maintains assigned departmental correspondence and records, following up on pending matters as needed.</li>
<li>Maintains mailroom documentation including but not limited to mail code distribution lists and procedures.</li>
<li>Back-Up for mailroom in the event of an emergency.</li>
<li>Issues and documents building access identification cards for employees, vendors, and consultants in accordance with Company guidelines and policies.</li>
<li>Maintains accurate databases of outstanding access IDs by auditing and reconciling database to active employee rosters and tracking of staff additions, terminations, and moves.</li>
<li>Provides responses to a diverse range of service requests and escalates more complex service/maintenance issues or concerns to management or appropriate internal stakeholders.</li>
<li>Reviews and provides specific information to questions/concerns from internal/external stakeholders (e.g. employees, vendors, real estate companies) by applying expanded knowledge.</li>
<li>Communicates effectively and provides customer service to internal/external stakeholders, recognizes what needs to be done to meet customer needs and demonstrates flexibility and responsiveness to meet customer needs on routine work independently.</li>
<li>Orders supplies and coordinates routine maintenance and repairs to office equipment.</li>
<li>Processes invoices for prompt payment as needed.</li>
<li>Plans and coordinates special events as needed.</li>
<li>Recognizes increasingly complex issues/concerns for assigned area(s) of responsibility and suggests process improvements.</li>
<br>
</ul>
