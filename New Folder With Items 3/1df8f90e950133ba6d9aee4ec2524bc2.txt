<h2>Community Engagement Director</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Choosing a career with ECMC Group means joining a nonprofit corporation with a mission to help students succeed. With a vibrant mission and values, great benefits, and strong community involvement, ECMC Group companies are an ideal team to join.
</p>
<p></p>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Job Summary
</b></h2>
<p></p>
<p>The Community Engagement Specialist is the local external representative for a campus for generating awareness and developing prospective student interest in CTE programs. They promote Altierus and CTE within the campus’s geographic territory across employer partner, workforce boards, philanthropic foundations and High School channels. They provide basic information, routine guidance and assistance to potential students on matters related to CTE programs and the enrollment process.
</p>
<p></p>
<p>The specialist will be responsible for activities related to advocacy for CTE programs and developing relationships across employer, workforce and High School channels that will ultimately drive student enrollment. They will partner with the SVP of Growth and Development to monitor the local implementation of the strategic relationship development process. They provide guidance to campus leadership in setting goals for lead generation and objectives for community and CTE advocacy and assist in establishing processes and infrastructure to support the community relations functions of the campus, across the institution.
</p>
<p></p>
<p>The specialist will demonstrate exemplary leadership and teamwork skills, positive attitude, accountability to Company values, and excellent customer service. They demonstrates positive attitude and motivation, concern for students, teamwork, excellent time management skills and the work ethic to follow-through on commitment to students and the organization. This level will be assigned complex tasks and projects beyond the core responsibilities.
</p>
<p></p>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>ESSENTIAL DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES:
</b></h2>
<ul>
<li>Manage conflicting priorities and handle multiple tasks/projects concurrently
</li>
<li>Organize, plan, control and prioritize work/projects effectively.
</li>
<li>Communicate progress regularly to appropriate stakeholders. Efficiently manages resources and understands work/project scope, key players, urgency, inherent risks and business benefits.
</li>
<li>Develop short and long term strategic plans, utilizing excellent project management skills.
</li>
<li>Displays and promotes high standards of ethical conduct and behaviors consistent with organizational and government standards.
</li>
<li>Develop goal-oriented, comprehensive programs of community outreach and engagement with prospective students.
</li>
<li>Implement best practices of and research for community outreach and engagement programs, partnership development, and community advocacy.
</li>
<li>Anticipate, understand and manage customer expectations and consistent delivery of services.
</li>
<li>Establish and build a referral network within assigned market specific communities and to advocate for CTE.
</li>
<li>Conduct presentations in assigned market to generate interest with key decision makers e.g.: school guidance counselors and CTE program chairs and instructors, workforce boards, employer partners, etc.
</li>
<li>Work closely with campus leaders to ensure that the school is a catalyst for community engagement.
</li>
<li>Collaborate with SVP Growth and Development, AVP of Enrollment, Campus Directors and Directors of Enrollment to accomplish key community recruitment objectives.
</li>
<li>Support campus leadership in the areas of strategic planning and territory management, including but not limited to target market identification, prospecting, effective meeting planning, communicating the ZEG/Altierus value proposition, maintaining strategic partnerships, and overall pipeline management.
</li>
<li>Give guidance in utilizing Customer Relationship Management (CRM) technology to effectively track and manage the various stages in the development/pursuit of strategic community partner relationships.
</li>
<li>Performs other duties as assigned
</li>
</ul>
