<h2>Accounts Payable Vendor Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p><b>Since 1928, the ASPLUNDH TREE EXPERT CO. has been dedicated to safe, efficient and innovative clearance services to the line clearance industry. A family-owned and operated corporation headquartered near Philadelphia, Pennsylvania, Asplundh has grown to employ over 34,000 diverse, energetic professionals throughout the U.S., Canada, New Zealand and Australia.</b></p>
<br>
<p><b>
</b></p>
<p><b>At Asplundh, our mission is to be the recognized world leader in providing professional, safe, cost-effective and environmentally sustainable vegetation management and other utility-related services. We challenge our people to consistently exceed the expectations of the customers for whom we work and the people they serve.</b></p>
<br>
<p><b>
</b></p>
<p><b>Our mission drives us. Our people inspire us. Asplundh values your skills, education and experiences. Join us on our mission to becoming the world leader in utility-related services!</b></p>
<p>

<p><b>JOB SUMMARY </b>(This section should be a brief general statement, typically one to two sentences that summarize the overall purpose of the position.)</p>
<p>
</p>
<p><i>Under the direction of the Accounts Payable Supervisor, the Vendor Specialist is responsible for maintaining the vendor master in PeopleSoft and overseeing the Accounts Payable electronic mailbox &amp; records. This is one of two Vendor Specialist positions which work in conjunction with each other.</i></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES </b>(List the major job duties/responsibilities in order of importance, beginning with the most important.)</p>
<p>
Due Diligence Research &amp; verification of new vendors</p>
<p>
New Vendor additions &amp; existing vendor changes</p>
<p>
Monitoring of vendor docs with expiration dates, i.e. Certificate of Insurance, Diverse spend.</p>
<p>
Accounts Payable electronic mailbox distribution &amp; record retention</p>
<p>
Vendor Statement Review</p>
<p>
Prepare and scan hardcopy records</p>
<p>
Assist with document requests for Audits</p>
<p>
Effectively follow department procedures</p>
<p>
Special projects and tasks as assigned</p>
<br>
<p><b>
</b></p>
<p><b>EDUCATIONAL &amp; EXPERIENCE REQUIREMENTS</b> (Minimum formal education, if any, required to perform this job (e.g., High School, Bachelor s Degree. Minimum job content knowledge required to perform this job, e.g., previous supervisory experience, basic accounting skills, and computer skills.)</p>
<p>
</p>
<p>High School Diploma or Equivalent</p>
<p>
Proficient in desktop applications including Microsoft Office Applications (Word, Excel and Outlook)</p>
<p>
PeopleSoft experience a plus</p>
<p>
Must be detail oriented and accurate</p>
<p>
Must be able to operate basic office equipment, i.e. copier, scanner, calculator, etc.</p>
<p>
Professional and positive demeanor</p>
<p>
Ability to thrive in a team environment</p>
<p>
Must be able to work independently</p>
<p>
Ability to work at a sustained pace to meet filing of incoming records</p>
<p>
Familiarity with imaging hardware and software a plus</p>
<p>
</p>
<p><b>FINANCIAL RESPONSIBILITY (</b>Specify the measure and size of this position s financial responsibility, e.g., $100,000 payroll budget, $8,000,000 sales volume, $20,000,000 inventory.)</p>
<p>
There are approximately 500 vendor additions and/or changes per month. The overall vendor master averages 13,000 vendors.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>COMMUNICATION SKILLS </b>(List the most frequent INTERNAL and EXTERNAL contacts. Also indicate the type of communication that regularly occurs (e.g., verbal vs. written, brief conversations or exchange of information, counseling, negotiating, conducting meetings or leading discussions, written proposals, reports or contracts, etc.)</p>
<p>
Ability to effectively communicate with all levels of internal personnel
</p>
<p>Ability to take initiative, use discretion and independent judgment</p>
<p>
</p>
