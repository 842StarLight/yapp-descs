<p><b>Company Overview</b></p>
<p>
Spotlight is constantly growing and changing. Throughout the years, we helped hundreds of companies with their advertising needs. That's when we learned that they needed even more help with their marketing needs. Now, after nine years of producing award winning and engaging content and websites, we are so excited to be able to help businesses with all of their marketing needs and launched a full-service Marketing Agency.</p>
<p><b>
Job Summary</b></p>
<p>
Spotlight is looking for an experienced Inside Sales Manager to manage, oversee and support our inside sales representatives team. This job is an exciting opportunity for ambitious sales people who look for an opportunity to advance their career! This role is ideal for candidates who enjoy sales, teamwork and inspiring others to give their best in finding opportunities and achieving great outcomes.</p>
<p>
As an inside sales manager, you understand the degree of engagement a typical representative has with their prospects. They will be in contact with their lead several times, through a variety of channels. Understanding that sales are almost always never closed on the first contact.</p>
<p><b>
Responsibilities and Duties</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Manage and oversee the work of inside sales representatives team</li>
<li>
Coach, train, evaluate and monitor sales representatives performance</li>
<li>
Set and track sales targets and KPIs</li>
<li>
Coordinate sales projects to meet deadlines</li>
<li>
Execute sales analysis, prepare sales reports and suggest improvements</li>
<li>
Prepare monthly, quarterly and annual sales forecasts</li>
<li>
Use customer feedback to generate ideas about new features or products</li>
<li>
Research and discover methods to increase customer engagement</li>
<li>
Ensure sales, finance and legal policies and procedures are met</li>
<li>
Build an open-communication culture inside your team</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Qualifications and Skills</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
3-5 years of previous working experience as an Inside Sales Manager</li>
<li>
Hands on experience with CRM software</li>
<li>
In-depth understanding of the sales process</li>
<li>
Excellent communication, interpersonal and team management skills</li>
<li>
Strong leadership skills with the ability to coach and motivate a team</li>
<li>
Strong analytical and organizational skills</li>
<li>
Driven and ambitious individual with a strong desire to succeed</li>
</ul>
