<h2>Enterprise Account Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<h4 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Job Description</b></h4>
<div>
<p>The Enterprise Account Manager is responsible for identifying, developing and closing new business and expanding market share across the World. This person will play a pivotal role in driving our company’s expansion globally. This is a career changing opportunity due to the visibility of the role within Imanami. If you’re up for the challenge, we’re interested in connecting.</p>
<p><b>
What You’ll Do</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Exceed booking and revenue quota targets</li>
<li>Target and gain access to decision makers in key prospect accounts</li>
<li>Develop and execute account strategy for major accounts and opportunities as per territory assignment</li>
<li>Establish access and relationships with key decision makers, typically at the CIO and CSO level</li>
<li>Establish &amp; manage relationships with key channel partners such as Resellers, Incident Response firms &amp; MSSPs</li>
<li>Work cooperatively with Imanami Marketing to create visibility with target accounts and drive engagement of target prospects at both the individual contributor and executive level</li>
<li>Work cooperatively with Imanami partners to leverage their established account presence and relationships</li>
<li>Drive demand generation programs with Imanami partners</li>
<li>Qualify and understand prospect security priorities and provide compelling presentations of Imanami's solutions</li>
<li>Manage demonstration and evaluation activities with the help of the SE team</li>
<li>Capture, maintain, and disseminate accurate and relevant prospect information</li>
</ul>
<p><b>What You’ll Bring</b></p>
<ul>
<li>4+ years of sales experience with significant experience selling software security and/or infrastructure products to corporate enterprises</li>
<li>Demonstrated history of exceeding booking and revenue targets</li>
<li>Significant and proven experience developing relationships with senior executives</li>
<li>Highly effective presentation and closing skills</li>
<li>Excellent oral and written communication skills</li>
<li>Strong time management, organizational and decision-making skills</li>
<li>Ability to work independently and as part of a team</li>
<li>Dynamic, high-energy self-starter</li>
<li>Possess the drive to succeed and to participate in the growth of an exciting, fast-paced company</li>
<li>BA/BS or equivalent combination of education and experience</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Why you should join us</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Voted #49 of Top 100 Fastest Growing Companies by San Francisco Business Times</li>
<li>Mature product line and a large customer base of Fortune 100 references.</li>
<li>Opportunity to get in on the ground floor of an emerging leader.</li>
<li>Higher quality of life, work and live in the East Bay of Northern California.</li>
<li>Competitive on-target earnings</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Company Description</b></p>
<p>
Imanami, a Microsoft Gold Certified Partner, is the leader in Active Directory Group Management Solutions. Our software suite provides a powerful set of solutions to provision users and get them into the correct distribution and security groups immediately. This is the most basic and important tenet to Identity Management, getting users in the system and productive immediately.</p>
