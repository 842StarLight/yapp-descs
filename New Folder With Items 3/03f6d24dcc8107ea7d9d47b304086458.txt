What sets us apart is giving our customers the ability to personalize their homes from homesites and floor plans to cabinets and countertops, at a price that fits their needs. <div><b>***This is a regional position for the KB Home Florida and North Carolina Divisions. Position can be based out of one of these division offices (Raleigh, NC or Jacksonville, FL or Orlando, FL or Tampa, FL) ******</b></div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
<b>
JOB SUMMARY:</b> Liaison between Division Marketing and Corporate Marketing to assist in shaping marketing strategy and executing all marketing materials.<br>
</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div><b>ESSENTIAL DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES:</b></div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div><b>
Strategic Marketing &amp; Partnerships with Divisions:</b></div>
<div><ul>
<li>
Guide and support divisions related to our new brand positioning. Find new and innovative ways to build KB's brand and increase brand awareness.</li>
<li>
Conduct weekly calls with division marketing leaders to review current and future marketing plans and share best practices.</li>
<li>
Participate in division new community opening kickoff meeting and work closely with division marketing to develop new community marketing plans.</li>
<li>
Oversee and provide strategic marketing assistance in executing all advertising and marketing collateral for divisions.</li>
<li>
Evaluate advertising effectiveness with in-depth digital reporting, lead analysis, sales and traffic results, email performance; provide recommendations on how to improve results.</li>
<li>
Support division management in struggling divisions by providing hands-on analysis of issues. Offer assistance and direction in hiring division marketing leaders as needed.</li>
<li>
Provide recommendations on the utilization of new systems and tools (e.g. email marketing workflow, Datorama dashboards, social initiatives).</li>
<li>
Implement model feature sheets with divisions to accurately convey to consumers the features showcased in our model homes.
</li>
</ul></div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div><b>Division Visits:</b></div>
<div><ul>
<li>
Visits divisions on a regular basis to tour communities and collaborate on local marketing strategy and implementation of programs.</li>
<li>
Tour competitive communities and report back on key learnings.</li>
<li>
Develop strong working relationships with division leadership and marketing personnel.
</li>
</ul></div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div><b>Media Agency:</b></div>
<div><ul>
<li>
Work with media agency and divisions and act as facilitator to foster positive relationships.</li>
<li>
Problem solve escalated marketing issues between media agency and divisions.</li>
<li>
Ensure alignment between media agency and division advertising plans.</li>
<li>
Monitor the production flow to ensure ads are processed on-time.
</li>
</ul></div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div><b>Performance Management and Development:</b></div>
<div><ul>
<li>
Provide management and oversight of coordinator-level role.</li>
<li>
Mentor and guide new directors of regional marketing and collaborate regularly.</li>
<li>
Monitor production process on a regular basis and provide feedback to team.</li>
<li>
Prepare and maintain documentation to train division marketing personnel and conduct and oversee divisional, corporate and media agency training sessions.<br>
</li>
</ul></div>
