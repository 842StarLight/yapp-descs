<p><b>Assistant Controller</b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>Tris Pharma, Inc. (www.trispharma.com) is an innovative specialty pharmaceutical company with an exciting pipeline of NDA and products. In April of 2016 Tris launched its first Commercial Branded product and has been building an infrastructure to support the expanded business. To support its’ expectations of continued growth Tris is expanding its’ Accounting team by adding an Assistant Controller located at its Monmouth Junction, NJ facility.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>The Assistant Controller - reporting directly to, and collaborating closely with, the Controller - is responsible for managing assigned accounting functions including, but not limited to: day-to-day transactional accounting, revenue accounting, financial audits, technical accounting, quarterly financial reporting requirements, etc. The incumbent plays a proactive role in establishing, directing and maintaining accurate and ethical accounting principles, practices, and procedures in order to establish and maintain sound financial controls. She/he also assists in setting strategic and operational direction for the accounting department, supervising staff in Controller’s absence and aligning department goals to help support business development and achieve company objectives.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<ul><li>Ensures all revenue accounting procedures and activities are followed in accordance, and in compliance, with Generally Accepted Accounting Principles (GAAP); Oversees and supports daily accounting activities including, but not limited to: general ledger, procurement, accounts payable/receivable, sales reserve analysis, cash management, fixed assets and financial reporting and filings, etc.; Directs accounting activities to timely and accurately record transactions in the general ledger</li></ul>
<br>
<p></p>
<ul><li>Manages general ledger and preparation of the consolidated financial statements; Contributes to, and supports, assigned aspects of the company’s financial reporting requirements to ensure accuracy and timeliness of financial statements, reports and disclosures including but not limited to: quarterly and year-end tax filings and financial statements, risk management, treasury, variance analytics, etc.; Identifies, addresses and rectifies technical accounting issues, requiring high accounting acumen, arising from business transactions</li></ul>
<ul><li>Assists with technical accounting and research; Documents company’s accounting treatment of business transactions in an accurate and timely manner</li></ul>
<ul><li>Provides guidance on new accounting pronouncements, as needed; Assists in the generation and advancement of assigned process improvements, internal controls and optimization of business processes that impact the company and support future growth</li></ul>
<ul><li>Acts as the liaison between the company and the third-party auditors and tax consultants; Collaborate closely with external auditors to guide the audit process</li></ul>
<ul><li>Ensures adequate and timely communication with the Controller and Senior Leadership to identify and properly record and address issues affecting accounting reporting, timelines, deliverables, etc.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Collaborates closely with cross functional teams to ensure financial policies/processes and process improvements/optimization are understood and supported across company to ensure scalability of our operations to support future growth</li></ul>
<br>
<p></p>
<ul><li>Supervises, coaches and mentors Accounting staff</li></ul>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>QUALIFICATIONS</b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>Bachelors degree in Finance or Accounting AND minimum 8 years related experience in roles of increasing technical and strategic responsibility REQUIRED</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<ul><li>Excellent, hands -on knowledge of accounting and financial processes (budgeting, financial closure, reporting/filing etc.) REQUIRED</li></ul>
<ul><li>Excellent, hands-on knowledge of GAAP rules and regulations REQUIRED</li></ul>
<ul><li>Experience working directly with external auditors and/or tax consultants REQUIRED</li></ul>
<ul><li>Working knowledge and understanding of revenue recognition accounting REQUIRED</li></ul>
<ul><li>Experience with Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP) systems REQUIRED, JDEdwards exp PREFERRED</li></ul>
<ul><li>Certified Public Accountant (CPA) HIGHLY PREFERRED</li></ul>
<ul><li>Advanced business degree (MBA, MSA) PREFERRED</li></ul>
<ul><li>Finance or Accounting experience in the pharmaceutical or biotechnology industry HIGHLY PREFERRED</li></ul>
<ul><li>Public Accounting experience PREFERRED</li></ul>
<ul><li>People management experience HIGHLY PREFERRED</li></ul>
<br>
