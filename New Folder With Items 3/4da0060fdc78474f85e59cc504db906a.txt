<p><b>
Job Summary</b>:</p>
<p>
We are currently<b> seeking a Rental Inventory Manager</b> to join our team in the <b>Cincinnati offices of the Rental Division</b>. The inventory manager is responsible for purchasing, receiving, warehousing, and inventory of all consumable sales items as well as the contract administration and distribution of consumables/sale items to project sites and regional rental facilities. This individual will oversee and manage the warehouse and personnel. This individual will focus on a structured purchasing program that leverages Messer's purchasing power and combines that with a distribution network to deliver the same level of service and availability in every region we serve, while meeting inventory turns goals. This Individual must possess basic purchasing knowledge with a high level of organization, structure, and time management skills, as well as the ability to deliver creative solutions to multiple locations. <b>A customer service mindset, vendor relationship focused, and strong communication skills are all essential</b>.</p>
<p><b>
Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Manage a staff of inventory specialists to effectively and accurately purchase, receive and warehouse consumable products necessary to build Messer projects.</li>
<li>
Utilize the software reorder reports to maintain targeted inventory levels that meet inventory turn targets while establishing a distribution network that allows regional locations the opportunity to purchase at a local level when necessary</li>
<li>
Issues purchase orders in accordance with company policy and the accounting system</li>
<li>
Reconciles vendor accounts at the end of the month</li>
<li>
Keep the inventory selection up to date with the latest and most advanced products in the construction industry</li>
<li>
Proactive steps to dispose of dead stock inventory on a regular basis</li>
<li>
Create and maintain an inventory “specials” flyer and distribute to all Messer field operations and craft teams on special pricing offers negotiated with suppliers</li>
<li>
Identifying and provide training to employees to assure they remain current on warehousing trends and technology while being compliant with OSHA and Messer regulations.</li>
<li>
Driving productivity measures of the employees to make sure they remain productive and cost effective, as well as compliant with departments Gemba metrics</li>
<li>
Establish and maintain computer generated paperwork on every single transaction from ordering, to picking, transferring, receiving, returning, and count verification.</li>
<li>
Conduct and reconcile regularly scheduled inventories</li>
<li>
Maintain Vendor Relationships to assure competitive pricing and product availability</li>
<li>
Grow and diversify products being offered to Messer project sites</li>
<li>
Maintaining "The Brand" by ensuring warehouse is clean, organized, and efficient supporting Messer 5S program</li>
<li>
Support and promote Lean performances and efficiencies of operations at all times</li>
<li>
Support, maintain, and adhere to all safety policies and procedures of the company in everything you do</li>
<li>
Drive the development and usage of technology such as bar code/ tag scanning of inventory and mobile app’s in an effort to deliver accurate paperwork on every order</li>
<li>
Solicit operations and divisional input into new products and services, new technologies and advancement in methods so we can lead the industry as "Best in Class"</li>
<li>
And all other duties and responsibilities determined by the management of the rental division</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Qualifications:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Self-starter, ability to work with minimal guidance</li>
<li>
Knowledge of the latest construction equipment and technologies</li>
<li>
Excellent leadership, organizational, communication, and problem solving skills</li>
<li>
Computer skills including advanced level using Microsoft Office products, "point of sale" mastery experience that includes the ability to track, maintain adequate inventory levels, report writing and interpretation skills, and conduct accurate yearly inventory reconciliations</li>
<li>
Strong problem solving skills that utilizes rigorous logic and methods to solve difficult problems with effective solutions</li>
<li>
Willingness to learn and grow professionally with an eye on advancement opportunities</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
The general goal for this position is to </b><b>support the Messer Rental Division's strategy to provide equipment, supplies, and services that reduce the total cost of projects and are the choice of all project participants because that expertise is valued by Messer customers.</b> To strategically purchase and inventory cost competitive products that are consumed by our projects with a level of transparency that is considered <b>"Best in Class"</b>. To plan and communicate with all Messer rental division customers' in the products supplied by the Rental Division</p>

Abilities:</b></p>
