<h2>Account Executive</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">JOB DESCRIPTION
<br><br>
Summary/Objective
<br><br>
Execute sales strategy, initiate contact with existing and potential customers, identify needs and sell appropriate services to meet those needs, close contracts at defined monthly goals, and ensure customer satisfaction.
<br><br>
Essential Functions
<br><br>
Reasonable accommodations may be made to enable individuals with disabilities to perform the essential functions.
<br><br>
1. Manage assigned accounts.
<br><br>
2. Developing new business relationships with targeted leads through cold calling, and company leads.
<br><br>
3. Obtaining signed service contracts from new customers in effort to meet and/or exceed all quotas.
<br><br>
4. Managing and maintaining CRM database for all prospect information to 100% accuracy.
<br><br>
5. Providing management with accurate forecast, funnel and all additional required activity/sales metrics.
<br><br>
6. Up-selling existing customer base with new products and/or technologies.
<br><br>
7. Attending sales meetings, conference calls, trainings and coaching sessions with Sales team and Manager of Customer Operations.
<br><br>
8. Call prospective customers and establish buying cycles, customer needs, and create a customer/provider relationship.
<br><br>
9. Prepare sales information for customers.
<br><br>
10. Establish buying influences, budgets, and purchasing criteria for assigned accounts.
<br><br>
11. Secure and place orders taking into consideration expected service delivery dates.
<br><br>
12. Identifies revenue opportunities within customers' communities through communications, programs, and other activities as needed.
<br><br>
13. Prospects, qualifies, and generates sales within the company’s established trading partners.
<br><br>
14. Ensures planning, account qualification, and needs analysis at all prospect levels.
<br><br>
15. Identify appropriate contacts, qualify, and drive leads through the sales pipeline.
<br><br>
16. Responds to requests from customers for information and gives sales presentations.
<br><br>
17. Engages in technical discussions with potential clients through demonstrations and presentations.
<br><br>
18. Remains knowledgeable and up-to-date on changes and developments in the telecommunications industry.
<br><br>
Competencies
<br><br>
1. Business Acumen
<br><br>
2. Communication Proficiency
<br><br>
3. Customer/Client Focus
<br><br>
4. Results Driven
<br><br>
5. Technical Capacity
<br><br>
Supervisory Responsibility
<br><br>
This position has no supervisory responsibilities.
<br><br>
Work Environment
<br><br>
This job operates in a professional office environment. This role routinely uses standard office equipment such as computers, telephones, photocopiers, filing cabinets, fax machines, etc.
<br><br>
Physical Demands
<br><br>
The physical demands described here are representative of those that must be met by an employee to successfully perform the essential functions of this job.
<br><br>
While performing the duties of this job the employee is frequently required to stand, walk, sit, use hands to handle or feel objects, tools, or controls, reach with hands and arms, climb stairs, balance, stoop, kneel, and crouch or crawl. The employee is frequently required to operate a motor vehicle to travel to and from customer premises. The employee must occasionally lift or move up to 50 pounds.
<br><br>
Position Type and Expected Hours of Work
<br><br>
This is a full-time position. Days and hours of work are Monday through Friday, 8:00 a.m. to 5:00 p.m.
<br><br>
Travel
<br><br>
This position requires up to 75% travel.
<br><br>
Required Education and Experience
<br><br>
Two years of related experience and/or training, or equivalent combination of education and experience. Experience in B2B sales role with software solutions.
<br><br>
Preferred Education and Experience
<br><br>
Associate degree or equivalent from college or technical school.
<br><br>
Additional Eligibility Qualifications
<br><br>
None required for this position.
