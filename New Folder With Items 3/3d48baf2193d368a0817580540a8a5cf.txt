<h2>Technical Project Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<div><div>
<p>C2 Systems is currently seeking a Technical Project Manager in support of our software development programs.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Job summary<br>
</b></p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>In this role, you will be an integral part of the Software team! You will be responsible for creating and maintaining project plans and reporting project status back to Senior Management. You will work with the Development, QA, and Support teams to help coordinate their deliverables and provide unified schedules across the different functional groups. As a Technical Project Manager, you will need to have familiarity with the software development process to facilitate collaboration across teams. You will be expected to be self-motivated and accountable for your own commitments and deliverables. This position is based in our Auburn, NH office and is a great opportunity to make a difference in a rapidly growing company!</p>
<p><b>Primary Job Responsibilities
</b></p>
<p><b>Position Overview</b></p>
<ul>
<li>C Squared PMs are responsible for their projects from their inception through to final delivery and beyond
</li>
<li>We expect our PMs to deliver projects on time, ensuring deadlines are met while helping to develop, grow and support their team along the way
</li>
<li>You must love a challenge and be a good problem solver
</li>
<li>Act as a Liaison between teams, including upper management, Development, QA, Architecture &amp; Design, Support, End Customers and the Sales Team.<br>
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Core Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Initiate and plan the approach of each project with the cross functional teams to formulate a detailed scope definition
</li>
<li>Drive detailed estimates, work breakdown, and planning to create accurate project plans and detailed project tracking</li>
<li>Identify, quantify, and manage risk as you monitor and control the project scope and schedule throughout the project lifecycle</li>
<li>Monitor project milestones and oversee and lead day-to-day project operations
</li>
<li>Create detailed project documentation and facilitate client working sessions and project status meetings</li>
<li>Be a team player and advocate for your team to build relationships within the organization</li>
<li>Foster a long lasting, “trusted advisor” relationship with internal and external stakeholders
</li>
<li>Be flexible and adapt to the needs of your stakeholders in a fast-paced, ever changing environment</li>
<li>Report on project performance status and baseline comparisons as requested</li>
</ul>
</div></div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div><div>
<p><b>Requirements</b></p>
<p>Knowledge and experience with Project Management methodologies
</p>
<p>Familiarity with the software development life cycle (Waterfall or Agile)</p>
<p>Strong attention to detail</p>
<p>Exceptional verbal and written communication skills</p>
<p>Understand the Expectation of “Owning a Project End to End”</p>
<p>Background in Microsoft Office Products (Office, Word, Excel, Powerpoint, MS Project)
</p>
<p>Ability to manage multiple complex projects simultaneously.</p>
<p>Ability to shift priorities at the drop of hat
</p>
<p>Comfort in working with external stakeholders in multiple companies simultaneously.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Preferred</b></p>
<p>Project Management certification (PMP, CSM, etc.)</p>
<p>Technical Background</p>
<p>Understanding of any of the following: basic computer networking, database management, and/or software development, DAS or WLAN</p>
