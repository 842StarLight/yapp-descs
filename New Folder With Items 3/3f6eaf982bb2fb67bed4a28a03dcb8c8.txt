<h2>Club Director</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><i>Anytime Fitness</i> is considered to be a premier place to work within the industry. We are seeking a highly motivated individual with a passion for helping others improve their lives through health and fitness. This is a position to help grow our Kaukauna location by working in a creative, fun and upbeat atmosphere where every day is different.</p>
<p><b><i>
Club Director</i></b></p>
<p>
The club director is responsible for establishing relationships with new and existing members within the facility, helping them “Make Healthy Happen”. Building the club's business by meeting with new prospective members and engaging with the community are key to your success in this position. This is the job for you if you personally enjoy attaining goals while helping others do the same!</p>
<p>
What we offer:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Multi-level compensation plan for membership sales &amp; retention</li>
<li>
Fun, supportive &amp; rewarding work atmosphere</li>
<li>
Benefits for full time employees including medical, dental and vision</li>
<li>
Future career opportunities within multiple Anytime Fitness locations</li>
</ul>
<p>
Responsibilities:</p>
<ul>
<li>
New member acquisition through marketing, community outreach, and one-on-one sales</li>
<li>
Oversight of the entire club membership base including operational and financial responsibilities, back-end reporting and management of member needs</li>
<li>
Management of a team and driving results through motivation and club culture to ensure overall club goals are met</li>
<li>
All other duties as assigned</li>
</ul>
<p>
Qualifications:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Several years of experience within either the service or retail sector with a proven track record of leadership</li>
<li>
Previous experience with fitness club management software</li>
<li>
Excellent communication (verbal and written) skills are a must, along with a top-tier customer service approach to conducting business</li>
<li>
Past sales skills a plus and a desire to build a business to success in an entrepreneurial environment</li>
<li>
Current CPR and AED certifications preferred</li>
</ul>
</div></div>
