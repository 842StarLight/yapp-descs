<h2>Payroll Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Our Client is searching for an experienced PEO Payroll Specialist with experience on processiong payroll on Prism Payroll Software.</p>
<p>The Payroll Specialist is responsible for keying and processing accurate payroll, responding to client inquiries in a timely manner and managing and maintaining assigned client base while providing a high degree of customer service. This position will also be responsible for handling confidential information, while researching and responding to all internal communications in a timely manner.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<ul><li>Responsible for payroll for all assigned clients to include:</li></ul>
<ul><li>Enters new employee data into Prism Payroll Software</li></ul>
<ul><li>Obtain status change documents for employees and clients</li></ul>
<ul><li>Maintain changes in Prism</li></ul>
<ul><li>Provide timesheets to clients: Data entry/import of employee hours</li></ul>
<ul><li>Compute and process timesheets based on wage and salary guidelines</li></ul>
<ul><li>Computes and disburses wages and salaries, deductions, taxes and other withholdings</li></ul>
<ul><li>Coordinates all court ordered deductions, child support garnishment, levies etc.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Posts payroll data and prepares routine reports</li></ul>
<ul><li>Coordinates statement messages and payroll stuffers</li></ul>
<ul><li>Calculates and processes special checks, reviews, edits and makes corrections and adjustments as needed</li></ul>
<ul><li>Files all employee and client maintenance</li></ul>
<ul><li>Research and resolve all client inquiries in a timely manner</li></ul>
<ul><li>Maintain and strengthen relationships with all key contacts including internal personnel</li></ul>
