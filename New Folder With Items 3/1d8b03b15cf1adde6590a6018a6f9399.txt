<h2>Tasting Room Associate</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>The primary focus of our Tasting Room Associate is to create a memorable guest experience by sharing knowledge of wine, the wine-making process, and the history of the Firehouse company. The ideal candidate will be a team player, possess a strong work ethic, and have a true love for customer service.</p>
<p>Wage: DOE hourly plus tips and wine incentives.</p>
<p><b>ESSENTIAL DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES</b></p>
<p>The following are essential functions of this job, however, management may assign additional duties and responsibilities at any time. Duties include, but are not limited to:</p>
<ul>
<li>Greet guests in a friendly and hospitable manner.</li>
<li>Meet and exceed Tasting Room sales goals by utilizing outstanding customer service skills.</li>
<li>Promote brand awareness by educating guests on Firehouse Wine Cellars’ wines, beer, winemaking techniques and history.</li>
<li>Recruit members to the FWC exclusive wine club.</li>
<li>Accurately handle routine purchasing transactions on the FWC POS software system.</li>
<li>Manage wine and merchandise inventory.</li>
<li>Maintain a positive and enjoyable work environment by being a team player.</li>
<li>Serving and attending to guests during public and private events both on and off premises.</li>
<li>Complete opening and closing responsibilities which include cleaning and other tasks outlined in the Opening/Closing checklist.</li>
<li>Attend scheduled education, training and other seminars.</li>
<li>Find additional tasting room tasks to complete when business is slow.</li>
<li>Additional tasks will be assigned by management.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>QUALIFICATIONS</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Interest in wine and beer</li>
<li>Ability to sell product and offerings with a personal touch.</li>
<li>Open availability – can work nights, weekends and around most holidays.</li>
<li>Communicate with guests in a confident, knowledgeable, warm and friendly manner.</li>
<li>Excellent verbal and interpersonal communication skills.</li>
<li>Has the ability to work independently, as well as a member of a team.</li>
<li>Ability to thrive in a fast-paced environment with changing priorities.</li>
<li>Must 21+ years old</li>
<li>Ability to lift 40 lbs. frequently.</li>
<li>Ability to stand for entire 8 hour shift</li>
<li>Self-motivated.</li>
<li>Must have reliable transportation</li>
</ul>
