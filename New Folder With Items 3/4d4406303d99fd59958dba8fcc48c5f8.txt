<h2>Advertising Account Executive</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>About The Company: </b><i>The Philadelphia Tribune Media Group</i> is a prestigious, multi-media company and the nation’s oldest, continuously published African American newspaper. The company also publishes Tribune and Sojourner Magazines, and as well as producing events of interest that impact the African American community.</p>
<p><b>Job Requirement</b>: We have a unique opportunity for highly motivated sales professionals, who can drive sales through networking, creating referral partnerships, cultivating relationships and creative marketing. This individual will represent <i>The Philadelphia</i> <i>Tribune </i>and its newspaper, magazine and digital products in the Greater Philadelphia region.</p>
<p>In your role, you will report directly to the Manager of Advertising and Marketing and work with a dynamic team of sales professionals. You will be representing a newspaper of unprecedented authority within the region’s African American community. (Salary plus Commission)</p>
<p>Requirements:</p>
<ul>

<li>Must have high energy and demonstrate a passion for selling</li>
<li>Must have the ability to build effective consultative relationships with advertisers and agencies</li>
<li>Must have the ability to meet targeted revenue goals</li>
<li>Must have a valid driver’s license and possess an insured vehicle</li>
</ul>
