<h2>Field Reimbursement Manager - Oncology (San Diego, CA)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>At <b>AstraZeneca</b> we turn ideas into life changing medicines. Working here means being entrepreneurial, thinking big and working together to make the impossible a reality. We’re focused on the potential of science to address the unmet needs of patients around the world. We commit to those areas where we think we can really change the course of medicine and bring big new ideas to life. As a <b>Field Reimbursement Manager - Oncology</b> <b>(San Diego, CA territory</b>) , you’ll play a pivotal role in channeling our scientific capabilities to make a positive impact on changing patients’ lives.</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Main Duties and Responsibilities</b></p>
<p></p>
<p>
The general purpose of the Field Reimbursement Manager (FRM) role is to minimize reimbursement barriers for patients and providers and, therefore, support access to AstraZeneca products in the Oncology therapeutic area. This purpose will be achieved by delivering high quality interactions with key physicians, accounts and institutional customers through specialized services. The FRM’s must have a strategic mindset and complex problem-solving skills, to be successful in their role.</p>
<p></p>
<p>
FRMs will work directly with and across several different internal departments and cross-functional partners including Market Access, Access Services, Key Accounts, Trade &amp; Channel as well as Sales &amp; Marketing supporting accounts in resolving challenging reimbursement cases and issues for AZ’s oncology portfolio. FRM’ s are expected to develop and execute ethical and customer-oriented reimbursement strategies for multiple products across the AZ portfolio. They are expected to demonstrate market expertise in communicating payer criteria/terms and practices, as these relate to assigned AZ products.</p>
<p></p>
<p>
As all other employees, FRMs are fully accountable for compliance with all laws, regulations, and policies that govern the conduct of Astra Zeneca activities.</p>
