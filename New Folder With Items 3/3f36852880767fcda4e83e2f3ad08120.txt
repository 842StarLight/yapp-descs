<div>
The Director of Video Production is responsible for coordinating Pax8’s myriad film and audio projects, including educational, web, documentary, and broadcast content, and delivering professionally edited results of the highest quality. Additionally, the Director of Video Production owns AV strategy, AV/Video budgets and equipment, and AV and show production for any and all of Pax8’s large-scale, live events. As the driving force behind film, audio, and major event production projects at Pax8, and the senior member of the Video &amp; Media Production team, the Director of Video Productions responsible for sourcing and managing talent, as well as coordinating schedules and capacity to ensure the Video &amp; Media Production team can execute and deliver on the needs of the organization and its video and education strategies.</div>
</div>
<div><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Essential Responsibilities:</b></h3>
<div><ul>
<li>
Establish list of equipment, tools, and software necessary to build out and maintain two Pax8 studios, including camera, audio, and lighting equipment, timers, teleprompters, rigging, and editing software and computer programs, screens, backup, and storage. Maintain equipment and systems for use.</li>
<li>
Lead Video &amp; Media Production team, including hiring and development of FTE video editors and subcontracted AV/Production teams, freelance editors, motion graphics artists, colorists, and more, as needed.
</li>
<li>Establish and maintain video and media production budgets, including special event budgets, rights and licenses, and purchasing of props and equipment. Deliver actualized budgets and complete wrap documents upon completion of projects.
</li>
<li>Work in collaboration with other stakeholders, including eStaff, Education, Marketing, Sales, Events, and other teams to establish a video/media calendar, key metrics, and expected deliverables.</li>
<li>
Oversee all aspects of production and ensure deadlines are met, including coordination and supervision of pre- and post-production leads through editorial, sound mix, color correction, QC and creation of deliverables, and distribution.</li>
<li>
Oversee production of complete suite of video and audio content designed to position Pax8 as the leader in technology and sales education in the channel.</li>
<li>
Show production for audiences of 30 to 2000+, including development and adherence to AV budgets; procurement of proposals and recommendations from outside AV vendors; creative direction, strategy and planning; development of Run of Show spreadsheets; and explicit pre-event and on-site direction.</li>
</ul></div>
</div></div>
