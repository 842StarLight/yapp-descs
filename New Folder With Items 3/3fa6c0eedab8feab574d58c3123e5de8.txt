<h2>Vice President & General Manager - Health Language</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>The VP &amp; General Manager, Health Language (HL) is responsible for leading the organization and driving growth. The General Manager supports the strategy of Wolters Kluwer and leads the strategic approach for the Health Language Operating Unit (OU) ensuring the vision and strategy are in alignment with the Health Division and ensuring business objectives are met. This role is actively involved in sales, product development, client services, marketing and commercial partnerships, with a strong influence over all other aspects affecting the Health Division and the Wolters Kluwer (WK) organization as a whole. Key responsibilities include driving revenue growth through the transformation of Terminology Management products and services, establishing and sustaining processes, adapting the business model to meet evolving market needs including identifying new partnerships and sales channels, driving profitability and market position, developing and inspiring strong performance from HL employees and serving as a key business and thought leader within the health care industry. Activities of the General Manager include: creating the vision, value proposition and the overall strategy for the Health Language operating unit, the execution of company-wide strategies and tactics; leading the management of the entire HL team; driving growth and transformation through comprehensive product development and marketing strategy; building a high performing sales and business development organization, creating and maintaining a reliable system for measurement of business performance for all operations; directing budget development and administration; representing HL in cross-organizational activities; maintaining performance standards; and representing Wolters Kluwer within the industry.
</p>
<p></p>
<p>ESSENTIAL DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES
</p>
<ul>
<li>Aligns vision, creates value proposition, provides strategic input and implements overall strategy for the Wolters Kluwer HL operating Unit by engaging in in-depth and on-going analysis of HL products, services, processes, and customer preferences; staying abreast of industry standards, methodologies, processes, and best practices; evolving and continuing the rolling 3 year business development plan; determining product strategies based on proprietary information and understanding of the Point of Care space; translating business needs into solutions that the organization can deliver; and communicating all goals, objectives and strategies to staff and associates.
</li>
<li>Drives the development, management, and marketing of new and existing service and product lines for HL by directing research into potential services and products; identifying the needs of payers and providers; creating awareness of HL products and services within the market; and facilitating the development of product suites; evolving or adding to current resources to meet the needs of product development initiatives; and driving innovation and creativity through the OU to develop and implement new products and services.
</li>
<li>Drives implementation of planned strategies for Health Language by evaluating the current business development plan; determining execution feasibility (e.g., scope, resources, timelines); working with product management and development staff to create full project timelines with Gantt charts, fully articulated milestones, and resource requirements; and working with HL Leaders units to ensure execution to the plan.
</li>
<li>Drives strong financial performance and achieves sales and EBITA growth targets by establishing a high performing sales culture, retaining top sales talent, ensuring sales training and certification, establishing effective sales incentive plans, ensuring consistent pipeline reviews, execution of effective sales processes and accurately forecasting and closing new and renewal business to achieve business targets. Ensure alignment of personnel and resources with sales channels including partnership development. Set and achieve sales quotas including ensuring consistent performance across the team. Manage effective lead generation and subscription renewal processes.
</li>
<li>Oversees and owns Health Language P&amp;L by directing forecasting of product line revenue, gross margin and operating costs; consolidating business functions plans and resource requests (e.g. business travel, training, client support, laptops, mobile phones); estimating financial resources required to support the activities; driving OU leaders to assemble and execute to a practical budget; ensuring budgets are captured and iterated into standard templates; reviewing and verifying the spending against the budgets throughout the year; justifying and securing unplanned resources as necessary to maintain business operations; and collaborating with Health leadership to ensure P&amp;L budget is represented in the overall operating budget.
</li>
<li>Oversees and guides functional areas in the execution of company-wide strategies and tactics by participating in strategic planning meetings within each functional area leader; share strategy, motivate staff, inspect operations, and evaluate outcomes; working with senior-level staff to translate the WK and HL vision, mission, and strategy into market-specific versions based on knowledge of the local customers, competition and regulatory climates; driving the creation and evaluation of sub-strategies/tactics that exemplify the corporate strategies, leverage resources effectively, and produce market share, customer satisfaction, and revenue for the business; and overseeing the execution of consistent policies, strategies, and tactics within each functional area.
</li>
<li>Leads the management of the HL team by evolving the organizational structure to meet business needs; monitoring key performance indicators; analyzing the data for patterns or indications of performance slippage; documenting and discussing performance issues with underperformers early and often; providing additional training and work opportunities to resolve issues and develop talent; working with HR Business Partner to establishing Performance Improvements Plans (PIPs) and proactive hiring plans; and working with Talent Acquisition team to fill open positions (e.g., providing information on candidate requirements; be available for interviews; making decisions quickly).
</li>
<li>Creates and maintains a reliable system for measurement of business performance for all operations by identifying the performance metric criteria needed to fully evaluate current and future states of productivity, efficiency, profitability, and customer satisfaction; overseeing the implementation of metric collection into processes; and evaluating the metric data to improve business plans and processes.
</li>
<li>Represents HL in cross-organizational and industry wide activities by participating in cross-functional/joint-venture Health division initiatives (e.g., market research, strategic marketing activities, editorial, sales, new product development); assisting with the RFP/RFI and sales process with key customers; collaborating with peers to develop and advance company-wide initiatives and strategies; serving as a key business leader, thought leader, and role model in WK as a whole; and participating as a functional member of WK committees.
</li>
<li>Represents Wolters Kluwer within the industry by developing and maintaining comprehensive knowledge of WK products, processes, industry trends and general business and financial acumen through various sources and initiatives; communicating in a professional, compelling, and articulate manner in speech, writing, and formal presentation; behaving in ways that demonstrate corporate core values and culture; developing professional and positive relationships with customers and colleagues; serving as a key industry thought leader; presenting as a subject matter expert at health care conferences and meetings; and maintaining a reputation of competence, integrity, and professionalism.
</li>
</ul>
