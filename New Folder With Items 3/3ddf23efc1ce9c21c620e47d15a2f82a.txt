<h2>General Service Technician</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>We are Western Automotive Ventures, Inc., the largest franchisee owner of Big O Tires, a full-service automotive center with 448 stores nationally. We are positioned to be the leader in the automobile service industry. If you believe in Empowering Ownership, Giving Legendary Guest Service and are committed to Operational Excellence, we want you to join our team! At Big O Tires, we believe with the best people and best operations, we will be the guest's choice for their vehicle maintenance.</p>
<br>
<b>
POSITION SUMMARY:</b> Perform service and installation activities associated with store products and services in an accurate and timely manner.<br>
<br>
<b>ESSENTIAL DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES</b> include the following. Other duties may be assigned.<ul>
<li>
Demonstrates good customer service skills.</li>
<li>
Drive vehicles with automatic and manual transmissions.</li>
<li>
Perform oil changes, lubricate chassis, check, fill, and replace various fluids/filters, Services customer's vehicles in inspecting, troubleshooting, servicing and replacing items such as, batteries, belts, hoses, lights, fuses, preventative maintenance, etc.Parking lot, bay area navigation and vehicle safety.Write work orders.</li>
<li>
Tires - Definitions, sizing, sidewall markings, inspection, repair, installation, balancing, inventory, tire ancillary services, TPM, Inflate tires.</li>
<li>
VIP, vehicle inspections, Workflow process, parking lot, bay area navigation, work orders.</li>
<li>
Follow all shop safety policies and procedures.</li>
<li>
Lifting and lowering best practices.</li>
<li>
Look up information for various applications.</li>
<li>
Operate point of sale (NAVEX) hardware and software.</li>
<li>
Prepare and inspect work orders, VIP Checklist and Vehicle Inspection Form (Health Check) for completeness.</li>
<li>
Ensure compliance with OSHA and Federal and State Environmental Regulations.</li>
</ul>

</li>
