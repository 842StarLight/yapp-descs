<p><b>
POSITION OVERVIEW:</b></p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
The main focus of this position is to be the primary point of contact for Product, Professional Services, and Operations Project Managers as it relates to the release of new products, services, and enhancements.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
Solutions Managers are highly experienced associates with deep operational understanding of key functional areas within Digital Marketing Services. They are technically proficient and knowledgeable regarding specific CDK Digital Marketing products and Services, with strong technical leadership skills. A successful Solutions Manager understands and can correlate between the Services we deliver, products we support and the impacts to customer/consumer experience.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
The Solutions Manager supports highly complex projects with strong operational knowledge and understanding of key business workflows across all functional areas of the Customer Experience Team. They can clearly articulate these workflows and how they are impacted by the introduction of new products and tools, functionality updates/changes and/or business process changes. The Solutions Manager is responsible for relationships with primary functional areas and the delivering of ongoing knowledge transfers and influence of the Product Roadmap.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
PRINCIPAL DUTIES &amp; RESPONSIBILITIES:</b></p>
<p></p>
<br>
<ul>
<li>
Drive enhancements and product innovation to support new offerings and services</li>
<li>
Act as the primary Services technical point of contact for assigned Functional Areas and Business Owners</li>
<li>
Stays abreast of competitive offerings and industry best practices</li>
<li>
Manages and drives Services prioritized product and tool bugs / product enhancements with Product Development (PD), Professional Services (PS), Information Technology (IT), and/or other technical teams</li>
<li>
Takes a proactive approach in identifying opportunities based on user feedback, customer feedback and observed activity and supports effort to apply feedback to future product/tool improvements</li>
<li>
Updates and maintains key project documentation as it pertains to their specific functional areas of expertise</li>
<li>
Performs other related duties as assigned.</li>
</ul>
