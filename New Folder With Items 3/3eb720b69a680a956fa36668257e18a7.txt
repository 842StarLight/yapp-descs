<h2>R&D Engineer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>Job Description<br>
<br>
<p>Avery Dennison is hiring a Research &amp; Development (R&amp;D) Engineer to lead and drive medical product developments from concept to commercialization. Projects include new product development and introduction, and maintenance of existing product lines. This role is part of our growing Vancive Medical Technologies business, which is one of our high value segments in Avery Dennison.</p>
<p><b>
Primary Responsibilities and Essential Job Functions:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Translate product requirements into product specifications by selecting and testing appropriate materials, product designs, and manufacturing processes</li>
<li>
Lead and communicate the status of projects: prepare project plans, decide timelines and resources, and present and effectively communicate information and progress to cross-functional teams and management</li>
<li>
Act as the R&amp;D project leader to interface externally with customers and suppliers to define projects, communicate progress, identify solutions to technical issues, and demonstrate applied knowledge.</li>
<li>
Develop products following the New Product Design and Development Process and Stage Gate principles. Own design control requirements including creation of detailed specifications and design verification and validation plans, protocols and reports.</li>
<li>
Develop test methods for new products.</li>
<li>
Assist with product risk management.</li>
<li>
Use internal and external authorities to accomplish goals (e.g., Vendors, Research Institutes, etc.)</li>
<li>
Independently identify and develop creative approaches to solutions.</li>
<li>
Provide hands-on technical support towards sales and marketing and manufacturing</li>
<li>
Understand and ensure compliance with cGMP, ISO 13485, and FDA 21 CFR Part 820 regulations.</li>
<li>
Assist in the investigation of customer complaints and non-conformances as required.</li>
</ul>
