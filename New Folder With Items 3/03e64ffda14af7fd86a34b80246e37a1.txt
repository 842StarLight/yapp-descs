<p><b>
Essential Functions</b>.</p>
<ul>
<li>Develop banker’s knowledge, sales and production by conducting sales coaching routines with all direct reports including performing on the spot coaching, regular “1 on1” meetings, management meetings and appropriate observations through Landmark Bank Sales routines.</li>
<li>Ensure all bankers provide exceptional customer service while promptly and efficiently processing customer transactions utilizing STARS standards.</li>
<li>Identify training and development needs of direct reports and meet regularly to coach on professional development.</li>
<li>Reward and recognize banker’s excellent performance through established company wide-recognition programs and creative branch contests, games and recognition events.</li>
<li>Meets with customers to discuss personal and business banking financial needs, accurately opens new accounts, including: Checking, Savings, Time Deposit, IRAs, Safe deposit boxes, and sign up customers for e-services including internet banking, bill pay and e-statement.</li>
<li>Maintain full knowledge of bank products and services. Identifies cross-sell opportunities including and not limited to the following departments: Wealth Management, Investments, Private Banking, Real Estate, Business Services, and Commercial Banking. Makes outbound sales calls by phone or in person to increase product per customer ratio. Develops business relationships with potential customers through sales calls, personal contacts/networking and outside activities.</li>
<li>Interviews consumer loan applicants as needed to develop information concerning their needs and determine whether a loan may be an acceptable risk within the Bank’s policy. Works within the loan guidelines, policies and procedures to provide information to central loan processors for loan documentation preparation</li>
<li>Serve as working manager by supporting new accounts and teller transactions. Support the day to day activity of the branch as assigned, including: verifying cash, balancing drawers, vault and coin machine, processing night drop bags, tracking and servicing ATM machines, assisting customers with safe deposit access.</li>
<li>Ensure all bankers respond quickly to customer inquiries. Research customer questions and resolve related account issues to ensure customer satisfaction, including phone and in-person. Turn customer issues into opportunities to deepen relationships.</li>
<li>Review all necessary reports and communicate results with upper management.</li>
<li>Ensure the branch is functional, clean and safe. Work with internal and contract resources to handle facilities management.</li>
<li>Responsible for all personnel management, including hiring, coaching, performance evaluations, and ensure adequate staffing.</li>
<li>Oversee monthly and quarterly audits.</li>
<li>Open and close the location, following established procedures.</li>
</ul>
<p>Maintain a thorough knowledge and understanding to complete the required forms or tasks to be in compliance with the following systems and regulations.</p>
<ul>
<li>Bank Secrecy Act</li>
<li>CIP</li>
<li>CTRs and SARs</li>
<li>Completion of hold forms</li>
<li>Bank’s data processing system</li>
<li>Branch Imaging Equipment</li>
<li>Branch scanner</li>
<li>E signature Equipment</li>
<li>Bank’s contact relationship management system</li>
<li>Bank’s policy for security and confidentiality</li>
<li>Loan origination software</li>
</ul>

<p><b>
Education, Experience, Competencies and Skills</b><b>.</b></p>
<p><b>
Required:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>High school diploma or equivalent.</li>
<li>Excellent face-to-face communication and customer relations skills.</li>
<li>Display a professional, positive, team-oriented attitude.</li>
<li>One year of previous banking experience.</li>
<li>Ability to perform basic mathematical calculations.</li>
<li>Ability to read and learn written procedures and policies and the ability to apply those policies and procedures to situations and arrive at acceptable decisions.</li>
<li>Ability to perform transactions quickly and efficiently under pressure.</li>
<li>Ability to maintain composure and professionalism while working with the public.</li>
<li>Ability to operate standard office equipment, such as an adding machine and computer.</li>
<li>Ability to establish and maintain effective working relationships with staff and vendors.</li>
<li>Ability to communicate effectively, orally and in writing.</li>
<li>Ability to work independent of daily supervision.</li>
</ul>

<li>Previous supervisory experience.</li>
<li>Retail sales experience.</li>
</ul>
