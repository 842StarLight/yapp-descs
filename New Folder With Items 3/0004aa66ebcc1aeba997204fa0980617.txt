<h2>Enterprise Account Executive - Startups</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>Job Description</div>
<p></p>
<p>
Zendesk is looking for an Enterprise Account Executive to work with and be a strategic advisor to one of our customer bases - Startups!</p>
<p>
The Startups team’s goal is to Increase Zendesk’s exposure with early-stage companies and entrench ourselves as a valuable resource within the startup community at large. Helping these up and coming organizations lay a solid foundation to build their business on is essential to their success and Zendesk’s overall success. The ideal candidate is highly experienced at direct selling in a consultative way, and has experience leading a virtual team. You will be responsible for winning highly competitive opportunities, working with the startup customer success teams to secure high-reaching renewals, and hunting for and winning direct net new business.</p>
<p></p>
<div><b>
Responsibilities:</b></div>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>
Exceed activity, pipeline, and revenue goals on a quarterly basis</li>
<li>
Think creatively about prospecting into net new accounts and constantly contribute to organizations in the startup world</li>
<li>
Become the subject matter expert on Zendesk’s landscape and what sets us apart from anyone else in the space</li>
<li>
Work with your SDRs to identify, work and close a targeted set of net new customers</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<div><b>
Requirements:
</b></div>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>A fearless, hunter sales person who is comfortable working autonomously</li>
<li>
Experience selling to C level executives</li>
<li>
3+ years of software selling experience; SaaS experience preferred (SDR/BDR experience is NOT considered as selling experience; only years running end to end sales cycles is)</li>
<li>
Track record of success selling into SMB or Mid-market companies</li>
<li>
Track record of over-achieving quota (top 10% in your company)</li>
<li>
Strong interpersonal and presentation skills</li>
<li>
Outstanding verbal and written communication skills.</li>
<li>
Bachelor’s degree</li>
<li>
Willingness to travel (15% travel)</li>
</ul>
