<h2>LMS Administrator/Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>Sitecore is the global leader in experience management software that combines content management, commerce, and customer insights. The Sitecore Experience CloudTM empowers marketers to deliver personalized content in real time and at scale across every channel—before, during, and after a sale. More than 5,200 brands––including American Express, Carnival Cruise Lines, Kimberly-Clark, and L’Oréal––have trusted Sitecore to deliver the personalized interactions that delight audiences, build loyalty, and drive revenue.</p>
<p>
Sitecore is experiencing spectacular growth all over the world. To build on Sitecore’s success and growth, we’re looking for a skilled LMS Administrator/Manager to work as part of our Product Department.</p>
<p><b>
Position Summary:</b></p>
<p>
The LMS Manager will set strategy and standards for all operational and administrative activities associated with Sitecore’s Learning Management System.</p>
<p>
This position is responsible for the support, strategy and design, development, implementation, delivery, and maintenance of content, assets, and other resources used for instructional functions. This position is fast paced, extremely exciting, and sometimes stressful! Our ideal candidate is a quick learner who is self-motivated and can help create and support a learner centric environment.</p>
<p><b>
Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Supports the resolution of user issues, provides creative options for LMS needs, and suggests opportunities for use as an integrative business solution.</li>
<li>
Leads the facilitate of data gathering, reporting, and daily dashboard</li>
<li>
Oversee curricula, catalogs, and assignment profiles master data and online content processes</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>
Assess, improve, and manage internal processes and systems that support training and development operations. Ensure processes are defined and documented and learners are adequately trained to ensure consistency and reliability.</li>
<li>
Communicate directly with customers and partners to assess and document their LMS-related requests</li>
<li>
Research and implement, as applicable, industry standard best practices to improve efficiencies.</li>
<li>
Resolve identified LMS issues independently or by escalating to partners / vendors as needed</li>
<li>
Responsible for the successful facilitation of a project from estimation through launch and maintenance</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>
Manage multiple projects from start to finish</li>
<li>
Upload, manage, and organize training content created in-house to the LMS</li>
<li>
Assist with development and oversight of content repositories</li>
<li>
Assist in managing localized versions of content to support global delivery in multiple languages, if applicable</li>
<li>
Assist in ensuring compliance with document control policies and requirements</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>
Assist content producers in troubleshooting and reprocessing e-learning courses or assessments</li>
<li>
Oversee curricula, catalogs, and assignment profiles master data and online content processes</li>
<li>
Prepare status reports on system usage, compliance, and task completion</li>
<li>
Routinely audit the learner experience to identify improvement opportunities</li>
<li>
Additional duties as assigned</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Job Qualifications:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
2-4 years of experience working with a Learning Management System (LMS)</li>
<li>
1-2 years of experience as an LMS content publisher or administrator</li>
<li>
Bachelor's degree from an accredited institution</li>
<li>
Exemplary written and verbal communication skills</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>
Detail-oriented with strong analytical skills</li>
<li>
Process driven, with the ability to recommend and implement changes for process improvement</li>
<li>
Ability to self-manage multiple development projects in meeting deadlines</li>
<li>
MS Office (Word, PowerPoint, Excel) expertise</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Preferred Skills</b>:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Technical certifications or coursework related to LMS administration</li>
<li>
1-2 years of experience in software company</li>
<li>
1-2 years of experience supporting projects for a global audience</li>
<li>
Experience working with strict document control and Quality Management Systems</li>
<li>
Experience working with an LRS in an xAPI ecosystem</li>
</ul>
<ul><li>
Experience working with a web-based content publishing system</li></ul>
