<h2>National Account Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p><b>Come Join a Winning Team:</b>
</p>
<p>We are a leader in the exploding space of GPS-based vehicle and asset tracking solutions. We serve businesses and consumers through our web-based and mobile SaaS solutions. Today we serve more than 40,000 customers and track more than 170,000 vehicles and assets nationwide. We market our services through highly recognized brands such as Linxup.com and MOTOsafety.com.. We’ve been experiencing explosive growth, and we’re positioned for even more in the years ahead.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>About the Position:</b>
</p>
<p>Agilis Systems is hiring a National Account Manager that is responsible for:
</p>
<p>1.) Rapid expansion of existing, assigned strategic accounts,<br>
2.) Developing new business through referrals of the existing business, and<br>
3.) Self-prospecting other strategic outside sales opportunities targeted and assigned by the business.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p>Culturally we are aggressive self-starters and naturally entrepreneurial in nature. You will need to be able to quickly learn and articulate our brand’s product and business strategies to existing customers and new prospects alike. You will be a trusted advisor on matters relating to our product offerings. You will know how to leverage executive relationships and be able to use those relationships to displace competition. Our top producers are hunters; they think outside of the box to develop and deliver solutions for customers while being relentless in their pursuit to drive results. They will be held accountable to workflows and cadences as assigned by the group’s director as well as be able to show successful funnel development in addition to quota results.
</p>
<p>We are looking for someone to join our team who enjoys spending most of their time engaging with customers at multiple levels, managing partner relationships, and closing deals. This is a home office-based position in the Greater St. Louis area.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Essential Functions – Responsibilities:</b>
</p>
<ul>
<li>Rapidly expand and develop key customer relationships; creating new and renewal business
</li>
<li>Lead sales efforts for an assigned book of business covering a strategic sales industry vertical for the company
</li>
<li>Develop new business through referrals from the existing book of business
</li>
<li>Self-prospect other strategic outside sales opportunities targeted and assigned by the business
</li>
<li>Work seamlessly across the organization with a cross-functional team in order to smoothly engage and coordinate all functional areas as required
</li>
<li>Responsible for complete and accurate on-going maintenance of accounts, forecasts, proposals, and account activity in Salesforce.com – attention to detail and over-communication to internal management a must
</li>
<li>Grow existing named accounts by developing broader relationships in new divisions not currently using our services and prospecting into newly identified accounts
</li>
<li>Develop a comprehensive customer relations strategy and monitor its effectiveness through regular contact with customers and Quarterly Business Reviews
</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Qualifications:</b>
</p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor's Degree in Business/Technical discipline or equivalent experience in a related field
</li>
<li>5-7+ years of experience in a client facing, strategic sales role
</li>
<li>Strong understanding of large client accounts and the ability to partner and influence decision making
</li>
<li>Prior B2B SaaS experience is preferred, but not required
</li>
<li>Confirmed solution selling experience, including the ability to diagnose the impact of trends, client data and translate that into ROI advantages for rapid sales growth
</li>
<li>Experience working with senior-level account employees, including an ability to immediately establish credibility with executives and build/maintain those relationships
</li>
<li>Ability to drive urgency in the sales cycle and the execution of solution strategies; ability to run a robust pipeline
</li>
<li>Proven experience in driving significant revenue growth with highly complex strategic clients
</li>
<li>Experience with an enhanced solution selling approach to create sales opportunities by expanding the network of contacts, utilizing existing relationships and through extensive networking within and outside of Agilis
</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Work Environment:</b>
</p>
<ul>
<li>The hours of this position are Monday through Friday from 8:00 AM until 5:00 PM
</li>
<li>Travel may be required up to 30% of the time
</li>
</ul>

</p>
<p>We value hard work and on-the-job-excellence, so we offer competitive compensation and benefits packages:
</p>
<ul>

</li>
<li>Attainable quota with full commission potential
</li>
<li>Medical, Dental, Vision, Life and 401(k) benefits are offered
</li>
<li>17 Flex Days (PTO)</li>
</ul>
