<h2>Shipping Department Freezer Controller - Calabasas Bakery</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<b><i>Position Overview:</i></b>
<div>You may know us for our delicious cheesecakes…and for being recognized by Fortune Magazine as one of the “100 Best Companies to Work For®” 6 years in a row! What you may <i>not </i>know is at The Cheesecake Factory Bakery, our staff members are our highest quality ingredient.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>Reporting to the Shipping Supervisor, you will work with a team of approximately 6 staff members at The Cheesecake Factory Bakery in Calabasas, CA. As the Shipping Freezer Controller, you will be responsible for the material handling equipment operation for the freezer storage area, including receiving finished goods from production and entering them into the system, for a bakery with more than 600 staff members that create delicious cheesecakes and memorable experiences.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div><b><i>You’ll thrive in this position if you are:</i></b></div>
<div><ul>
<li>
<b>Driven by results:</b> you are conscientious and persistent about delivering timely, high quality deliverables and performing all job functions safely while following instructions.</li>
<li>
<b>Service oriented:</b> you’re a people person who is systematic and trustworthy, interacting constructively with peers or teams of various levels while demonstrating strong, positive communication skills.</li>
<li>
<b>Adaptable:</b> you’re at ease in a fast-paced environment and able to effortlessly change direction when priorities shift, learning to perform new tasks as required for business needs and work extended hours if necessary.</li>
<li>
<b>A team player:</b> while you’re a capable self-manager, you thrive when you’re part of a team and don’t mind getting your hands dirty on your own if needed.</li>
</ul></div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div><b><i>Here’s more of what you’ll get to do:</i></b></div>
<div><ul>
<li>Physically receive finished goods inventory from production.</li>
<li>Use handheld scanner to enter finished goods inventory into the Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP) system.</li>
<li>Wrap finished goods inventory and place in racks for storage in the finished goods freezer.</li>
<li>Assist in loading transfer orders of finished goods inventory to outside storage.</li>
<li>Assist in fulfilling UPS, sample, and employee sales orders.</li>
<li>Work in collaboration throughout the day with the Production and Quality Assurance staff.</li>
</ul></div>
<p><b>Qualifications</b></p>
<br>
<b><i>
What we require:</i></b>
<div><ul>
<li>3+ years of experience in material handling.</li>
<li>3+ years of experience with stand-up and pallet-jack forklifts.</li>
<li>Stand-up and pallet-jack forklift certifications.</li>
<li>Basic Math skills.</li>
<li>Experience using a handheld scanner.</li>
<li>High safety awareness.</li>
<li>Flexibility to work different shifts when needed.</li>
<li>High School Diploma or G.E.D.</li>
</ul></div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div><b><i>What we prefer:</i></b></div>
<div><ul>
<li>Experience with UPS World Ship program.</li>
<li>Experience working in a freezer environment.</li>
<br>
</ul></div>
