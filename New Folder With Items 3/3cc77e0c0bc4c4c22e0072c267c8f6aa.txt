<h2>Day Sr. Customer Assistance Representative Part Time</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b><i>Are you looking for a consistent part-time job with a good company and an industry leader? Do you want a fun, team-oriented work environment?</i></b></p>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
We have an exciting opportunity in customer service and sales as a part time <b>Sr. Customer Assistance Representative</b> with our team at <b>Enterprise Rent-A-Car at Denver International Airport</b>!</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
In this position you will gain knowledge through local training and hands-on experience to provide administrative and rental support at our large DIA airport location. Our fun and friendly team will teach you about all areas of our business to ensure that we are delivering excellent customer service with every single customer.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
This is a <b>part-time position</b> (25 hours/week) in a team-oriented work environment.</div>
<div>
Compensation is <b>$16.50/hour</b> with customer service bonus opportunities monthly.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div><b>
Available Schedule:</b></div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
Any 4 days during the week. Shifts are either <b>8:00am-2:00pm</b> or <b>9:00am-3:00pm</b>
</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<b>
Responsibilities:</b><br>
<ul>
<li><div>Provide a high level of customer service by assisting customers and assessing their rental needs in person and over the phone</div></li>
<li><div>
Meet and greet customers in a friendly and timely manner</div></li>
<li><div>
Provide directions and general assistance</div></li>
<li><div>
Assist to assess condition of rental upon return</div></li>
<li><div>
Process returns, check-ins and exit kiosk transactions</div></li>
<li><div>
Understand and communicate rental terms and conditions, vehicle features and other services</div></li>
<li><div>
May sell optional protection products, upgrades, fuel options and other additional equipment</div></li>
<li><div>
Responsible for notifying Management of any known vehicle problems and any required vehicle maintenance</div></li>
<li><div>
Perform various administrative and basic accounting functions such as: research and billing support tasks, accounts receivables, transfer and key logs, run miscellaneous reports, supply maintenance, process customer billing</div></li>
<li>
Take reservations, rate quotes, general questions and answers, provide information and resolution for customers</li>
<li>
Manage outgoing calls for callback management, A/Rs and miscellaneous calls as assigned</li>
<li><div>
Perform miscellaneous and backup duties job-related duties as assigned</div></li>
</ul>
