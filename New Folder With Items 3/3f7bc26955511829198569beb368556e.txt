<h2>Director, Digital Marketing</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Job Title: </b>Director, Digital Marketing<br>
<br>
<b>Department: </b>Global Marketing<br>
<br>
<b>FLSA Code: </b>Exempt<br>
<br>
<b>Job Reports To:</b> SVP, Global Head of Marketing</p>
<p><b>
Summary:</b></p>
<p>
Cohen &amp; Steers, a global asset manager and leader in listed real assets seeks an experienced digital marketing manager to oversee and manage our websites and digital marketing campaigns. This is a key role who will also help run the day to day while helping the Chief Marketing Officer define the future state of Digital Marketing for our firm. We are looking for someone with 7-10 years of digital marketing, website management and leadership experience.</p>
<div>
<b>
Major Responsibilities/Activities:</b><br>
<br>
<ul>
<li>Manage the public and intranet sites, developing a website traffic plan and create goals and benchmarks to meet</li>
<li>Develop and execute a web strategy (working with channel marketing peers) including any technology enhancements required like Subscriptions, Lead Capture</li>
<li>Manage our web development agency</li>
<li>Generate monthly reports on our marketing campaign performance and website performance</li>
<li>Optimize website and social media channels for SEO as well as usability</li>
<li>Define technology requirements working with our Technology and Analytics Teams</li>
<li>Post content using CMS (Expression Engine and Word Press)</li>
<li>Develop custom tracking codes for marketing campaigns leveraging Pardot (Marketing Automation system)</li>
<li>Manage SEO tracking and tagging</li>
<li>Partner with PR agencies and Channel Marketing to increase our results</li>
<li>Develop, evaluate and oversee the implementation of A/B testing protocols and procedures</li>
<li>Develop a digital strategy including directing the website redesign</li>
<li>Develop the technical requirements for the website redesign including lead management, campaign tracking and subscriptions enhancements</li>
<li>Ensure the web is up to date, compliant and accurate</li>
<li>Manage the website budget and enhancements</li>
<li>Partners with Channel and IT to integrate Pardot, Seismic and Salesforce functionality to support lead management and campaign tracking</li>
</ul>
<br>
<b>
Minimum Requirements:</b><br>
<br>
<ul>
<li>7-10 years of digital marketing experience at an asset manager, financial services firm or agency serving a fin serv company</li>
<li>Skills and proficiency of key marketing automation and CMS software including Expression Engine, Pardot, Sales Force and Word Press, Google Analytics, audience manager, etc.â€¦</li>
<li>Knowledge of asset management or experience in financial services a plus</li>
<li>Bachelor of Arts or Science required, Digital Marketing Certification also a plus</li>
<li>Strong collaboration skills and team player</li>
<li>Effective communicator who can translate technical language in a straightforward way to partners who may not be technical of conversant in digital marketing terms</li>
<li>Strong people leader and mentor who is motivated by developing talent</li>
</ul>
