<h2>Apparel Product Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Organization: </b></p>
<p>Berne Apparel,established in 1915, is a manufacturer and distributor of work and outdoorapparel. The company offers a full lineof insulated and unlined work clothing, such as bib overalls, jackets,coveralls, bottoms, shirts, sweatshirts, and accessories for men, women, andyouth. Distribution of products isprimarily concentrated within the United States to farm and home retailers,sporting goods retailers, industrial laundry resellers, and promotional productdistributors.</p>
<p><b>Job Description: </b></p>
<p>We are looking for adedicated employee to join our Product Development team. The Apparel Product Manager is a mid-levelposition responsible for managing the product development life cycle of allproducts from beginning to end. Thisperson will lead the Product Development team and work closely with the DesignTeam to develop new products. Thisposition is located at the corporate office, in Ossian, Indiana.</p>
<p><b>Job Duties: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Manage the product developmentcycle of all products to ensure deadlines are met as per the productdevelopment calendar.</li>
<li>Collaborate with Design Team to determinethe new products that align with our business strategy.</li>
<li>Develop products andrecommend materials and construction techniques through existing and newsuppliers that meet design expectations and gross margin targets.</li>
<li>Create and manageproduct tech packs, maintain all records, and obtain necessary productcertifications for compliance.</li>
<li>Ensure execution ofproper fit and fit consistency throughout product assortment.</li>
<li>Confirm productquality is made to our standards, as well as industry standards.</li>
<li>Interact withProduction, Marketing, Sales, Logistics, Supply Chain, Customer Service, andQuality Assurance to distribute technical product details and assure theaccuracy, legality, and integrity of all product performance claims.</li>
<li>Maintain and updatelicensing agreements.</li>
<li>Manage and developskill sets of two to four direct reports to achieve business and career goalsthrough training, workshops, webinars, etc.</li>
<li>Execute annualperformance reviews.</li>
<li>Conduct research analysison competitors and market trends.</li>
<li>Some domestic andinternational travel required.</li>
<li>Perform these andother duties as assigned.</li>
</ul>
