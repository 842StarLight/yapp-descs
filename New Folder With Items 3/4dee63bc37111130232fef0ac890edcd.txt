<h2>Sales Development Representative</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<div>
<div>Do you consider yourself a go-getter and an effective communicator? Do you find it easy to connect with people and enjoy building relationships? Linux Academy is a leading online technology platform that supports thousands of students in their mission to learn and grow. We’re seeking an innovative <b>Sales Development Representative </b>with an entrepreneurial spirit and fearless attitude to jump in and join our team.
</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>Our <b>Sales Development Rep </b>will be a self-starter, professionally aggressive and well organized. You will play a key role on our sales team by identifying potential prospects and creating qualified opportunities for our sales team. You will work with inbound leads that are generated by our marketing team, as well as perform outbound, prospecting activity via email and phone, using a variety of sales tools to assist your efforts.
</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
<b>What’s in it for you?</b> This entry-level role is a chance to get your foot in the door of a B2B SaaS, expand your sales and marketing knowledge base and make an impact. Training for this role provides the foundation for future career growth as a Sales Account Executive or on any of our customer facing teams. In our work hard play hard environment you’ll develop the skills you need to excel in any complex sales role, enjoy a competitive salary and be an integral part of a high performing sales team.<br>
</div>
</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<br>
<div><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>What you'll do:</b></h3>
<div><ul>
<li>
Be an <b>awesome </b>brand ambassador- create a great first impression to prospects and customers</li>
<li>
Generate new business opportunities by prospecting relentlessly via email, cold calls and social selling and qualify inbound contacts to our website
</li>
<li>Be accountable for achieving or exceeding your monthly goals of opportunity creation and contribute to overall team quotas. Self- manage yourself to these goals but raise your hand when you need help.</li>
<li>
Research targeted accounts to identify key contacts and critical account information prior to prospecting calls</li>
<li>
Work closely with our Sales Operations Manager to uncover new revenue opportunities
</li>
<li>Partner with our Sales Account Executives to secure meetings with qualified leads</li>
<li>
Manage a prospecting account list; make prospecting part of the regular routine to ensure that new prospects/opportunities are being added to the pipeline on a consistent basis.
</li>
<li>Manage and track your pipeline using our CRM tools and tech stack and provide accurate forecasts to sales and leadership
</li>
<li>Demonstrate focus, resiliency and hustle in the pursuit of excellence</li>
<li>
Become a Linux Academy superstar, and be an expert at articulating our value proposition to anyone who asks (or who doesn’t ask).
</li>
</ul></div>
</div></div>
<div><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>What makes you a great fit for this role:
</b></h3>
<div><ul>
<li>You're not just looking for another job. You’re looking for a real opportunity to learn and grow, make an impact, and advance your career within a growing company
</li>
<li>You’re self-motivated and competitive but also a team player who enjoys being part of a high energy, collaborative group that works together and plays together</li>
<li>
Your competitive nature means you enjoy beating goals and quotas and achieving the resulting rewards</li>
<li>
You have impeccable communication skills; you love talking to people on the phone, listening and learning about their needs and getting them excited about a solution</li>
<li>
You're comfortable working in a rapidly expanding and changing environment</li>
<li>
You have a high level of poise, judgement and attention to detail</li>
<li>
You manage your time well, and have a reputation for getting things done
</li>
<li>You have a positive and energetic personality with the innate ability to smile over the phone and make the other end smile as well.</li>
<li>
You’re able to handle objections and think fast on your feet</li>
<li>
You have 1-3 years of business experience or commensurate internship experience with a strong interest in sales and technology. <b><i>Recent college grads are encouraged to apply
</i></b>
</li>
<li>You can work in the main office located in Keller, TX, you’re ok with occasional weekend or evening work for a special project, and you can work in the US without Sponsorship</li>
</ul></div>
