<p><b>
Position Summary:</b></p>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<p>
Provide mechanical engineering sustaining support for medical device products such as hospital beds and equipment. This role will involve mechanical engineering design projects as well as providing technical support to operations. Design responsibilities include mechanical design and development, problem solving and support of design into manufacturing. Strong problem-solving skills and the ability to work well in a cross functional team are required. The lead mechanical engineer will be considered a subject matter expert and must also be able to provide guidance to other engineers for technical issues, design reviews, and complex problem resolution.</p>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<p><b>
Position Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Lead the design and develop of mechanical solutions for hospital beds and equipment</li>
<li>
Develop engineering requirements, risk documentation, cost estimates, schedules and work with various team members to complete engineering design changes.</li>
<li>
Work in a team environment with daily contact with other engineers, technicians, designers, manufacturing, supply management, and marketing personnel. Will have frequent contact with customers, regulatory affairs, sales, senior management, and suppliers.</li>
<li>
Support manufacturing as problems arise on current products</li>
<li>
Evaluate product complaints and field returns to determine root cause</li>
<li><div>
Strong Interpersonal skills with the ability to work collaboratively with others in a team environment.</div></li>
<li><div>
Recognized subject matter expert in mechanical design related to the development of hospital beds and equipment</div></li>
</ul>
