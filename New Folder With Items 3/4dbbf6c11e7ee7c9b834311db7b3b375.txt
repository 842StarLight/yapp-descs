<h2>Restaurant Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>We are looking for a candidate with the following qualities:</p>
<p>- Provide organizational directions to deliver the short and long-term sales and profit results through people, development and improving restaurant operations.</p>
<p>- Manages food, labor, paper costs and other controllable expenses while developing and implementing appropriate plans to resolve unfavorable trends and enhance profits.</p>
<p>- Develops subordinates to maximize their contributions and future promotions while executing proper training and development through established systems and practices.</p>
<p>- Utilizes ongoing systems for the recruitment, development, recognition and retention of store management and crew.</p>
<p>- May develop marketing strategy, implement advertising and promotional campaigns to increase business.</p>
<p>- Provides quality, timely performance feedback and performance appraisals for staff.</p>
<p>- Excellent communication and organization skills and ability to interact professionally and diplomatically with a variety of business partners.</p>
<p>- Fluency in Microsoft Office Suite: Word, Excel, PowerPoint.</p>
<p>- Aloha POS, Avero, Homebase experience is a plus.</p>
<p>- Wine knowledge is a plus.</p>
