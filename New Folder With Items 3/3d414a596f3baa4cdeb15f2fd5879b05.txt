<b>Program Components</b><br>
<b>
</b><br>
</div>
<div>We offer an exciting 12-week summer engineering internship with an emphasis in <b>Product Research and Development</b>. You will experience unrivaled departmental opportunities supporting a wide variety of highly specialized teams such as: New Product Development, Mechanical Design, Computational Modeling and Simulation, Materials Development, Robotics, Research and Testing, Manufacturing and Design Quality.<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<div>Not only will you have the ability to develop your engineering skills through on the job assignments. You will:</div>
<ul>
<li><div>Improve your industry knowledge through open forum conversations with senior company leaders and shadowing of senior engineers.</div></li>
<li><div>
Create or improve your personal brand through skills building workshops and internship networking events.</div></li>
<li><div>
Have the power to connect and support our local towns through community activities.</div></li>
</ul>
<div>
As an R&amp;D Leadership Development Intern, you will:</div>
<ul>
<li><div>
Work in a fast-paced cross functional, technologically advanced corporate environment in an internship program focused on developing individual engineers capable of pursuing careers across medical device businesses and high-volume manufacturers.</div></li>
<li><div>
Have the opportunity to learn about anatomy, disease states, and deformities and the surgical procedures and products used to address these conditions.
</div></li>
<li><div>Be involved in the design, development, manufacturing and commercialization of new products or product modifications.</div></li>
<li><div>
Assist with the development of advanced materials, process improvements, risk management, and/or mechanical testing or advance a medical device through the development process.</div></li>
<li><div>
Utilize solid modeling to design products, create drawings and tolerance analyses of mating components.</div></li>
<li><div>
Become familiar with design control documentation required by regulatory governing bodies.</div></li>
<li><div>
Present to leadership your learnings and development experience.</div></li>
</ul>
