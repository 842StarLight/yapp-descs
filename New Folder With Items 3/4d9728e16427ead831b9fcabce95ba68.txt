<h2>Shipping & Receiving Clerk</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Ameristar Perimeter Security is the largest ornamental fence manufacturer in the world and we are looking for a Shipping &amp; Receiving Clerk to join our team. From its beginnings over a quarter-century ago, Ameristar concentrated on enhancing the aesthetic appearance of modern fence products, promoting ease of installation, while increasing strength &amp; durability. Pursuing this focus, Ameristar designed and built a new, environmentally friendly, high-production facility. This resulted in the creation of the highest quality residential, commercial, industrial and high-end security fences in today's market place.</p>
<p><b>What are the duties of a Shipping &amp; Receiving Clerk?</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
<div>Prepares and scans all shipping documents for all LTL and truckload shipments.</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Provides a high level of customer service to all areas of the company by promptly addressing all shipping and receiving questions.</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Prepares shipping and receiving weekly and monthly reporting. Tracks and maintains KPI data for shipping and receiving departments.</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Coordinates daily LTL and TL volume with Shipping and Sales groups to ensure all orders are at the proper shipping status.</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Provides a high level of customer service by quickly resolving any shipping or receiving issues.</div>
<br>
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>

<br>
</li>
</ul>
