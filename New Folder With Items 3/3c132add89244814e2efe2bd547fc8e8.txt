<h2>Front Desk Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<h1 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Job Description:</b></h1>
<p>The Crunch Front Desk Manager will ensure the members receive the highest quality of service and facilities. He or she will exhibit an ability to achieve the financial targets for the club as outlined in the annual budget by motivating, leading, supervising and coordinating the activities of employees engaged in servicing our member needs. He/She will demonstrate an aptitude and command of all company wide policies and initiatives to ensure the integrity of the Crunch brand.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<h1 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Reports to:
</b></h1>
<p>General Manager and Area Director</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<h1 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Requirements:
</b></h1>
<ul>
<li>4 year college degree preferred</li>
<li>2 years management experience required</li>
<li>Fitness management experience preferred</li>
<li>Current Cardiopulmonary Resuscitation (CPR) Preferred</li>
<li>
<p>Qualifications:</p>
<ul>
<li>Must be dotFIT certified within 30 days of hire</li>
<li>Must be certified through Crunch-U within 2 weeks of hire</li>
<br>
</ul>
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<h1 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Special Skills:
</b></h1>
<ul>
<li>Excellent written and verbal communication</li>
<li>Ability to adapt in a fast paced environment</li>
<li>Creative management techniques</li>
<li>Strong organizational skills</li>
<li>Strong leadership skills</li>
<li>Strong administrative skills</li>
<li>Strong customer service skills</li>
<li>Strong computer skills</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p>
</p>
<h1 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities:
</b></h1>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Administration/Organization
</b></h3>
<ul>
<li>Demonstrate a working knowledge of all standard operating procedures and policies that pertain to the club.</li>
<li>Communicate and implement club policies and procedures to employees.</li>
<li>Encourage staff to work as a team and be productive.</li>
<li>Illustrate an ability to make decisions.</li>
<li>Recruit and hire the highest possible caliber of staff.</li>
</ul>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Sales/Revenue Management
</b></h3>
<ul>
<li>Demonstrate the ability to lead, motivate, and manage team.</li>
<li>Achieve desired sales goals.</li>
<li>Achieve desired revenue goals thru the leadership and motivation of employees.</li>
<li>Implement and support company programs and promotion to help generate new sales leads for optimum new membership growth.</li>
<li>Ensure that Team maintains proper tracking forms and the daily leads.</li>
<li>Ensure that all promotions are effectively communicated to the team and all other appropriate staff.</li>
<li>Ensure ongoing prospecting and generation of new prospective members.</li>
<li>Review sales-related written communication such as proposal, letters and promotional pieces for effectiveness, spelling, accuracy and distribution</li>
<li>Ensure that he staff has a high level of knowledge about the clubs programs, facilities and equipment.</li>
<li>Emphasize importance of staff involvement in the community and neighborhood businesses.</li>
</ul>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Operations
</b></h3>
<ul>
<li>Coordinate and work within club support functions of Fitness, Sales and Marketing, Accounting, Information Technology.</li>
<li>Support personnel related problems or difficulties by following club procedure and documentation.</li>
<li>Resolve member complaints in an expeditious and tactful manner following club procedure and documentation.</li>
<li>Promote a professional and welcoming atmosphere that enhances the quality of service and care offered to the members.</li>
<li>Ensure the club meets standards for cleanliness, maintenance, safety, and security.</li>
<li>Conduct weekly detailed inspection of the club with the Maintenance Manager utilizing cleaning checklist and forward information to appreciate staff.</li>
<li>Ensure visible maintenance items are repaired promptly, proper signage is posted and if possible out of service equipment removed from the floor.</li>
<li>Reinforce to staff the cleanliness is everyone’s responsibility, not just the maintenance staff.</li>
<li>Ensure proper inventory of maintenance parts.</li>
<li>Track completion of opening/closing checklists, stage management, repair &amp; maintenance log, locker log and cleaning checklist.</li>
<li>Assist in the processing/submission and approval of payroll.</li>
</ul>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Financial
</b></h3>
<ul>
<li>Exhibit an understanding of budgets and income statements.</li>
<li>Establish controls of expenses and purchasing of club supplies.</li>
<li>Display an ability to keep expenses at or below budget.</li>
<li>Demonstrate an ability to articulate variances in revenue/sales/expenses versus budget.</li>
</ul>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Leadership/Motivation
</b></h3>
<ul>
<li>Serve as a role model for employees.</li>
<li>Communicate effectively by holding weekly and individual meeting with all key club personnel.</li>
<li>Provide an inspirational environment that welcomes honest feed back from employees and takes action to ensure a quality, working environment.</li>
<li>Oversee, support, direct and develop department heads.</li>
</ul>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Profit Centers
</b></h3>
<ul>
<li>Illustrate an ability to drive profit center revenue such as personal training, retail, etc…</li>
<li>Monitor flagged check-in’s to increase revenue and collections.</li>
<li>Demonstrate an ability to increase revenue per member.</li>
</ul>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Meetings</b></h3>
<ul>
<li>Monthly or Weekly Department Meetings</li>
<li>Employee Training Meetings</li>
<li>Daily “One Minute Meetings” with club staff</li>
<li>Daily Personal Training Manager Meeting</li>
<li>Weekly Club Management Meeting</li>
<li>Annual Performance Evaluations</li>
</ul>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Accountabilities
</b></h3>
<ul>
<li>Implement and support company programs and promotion to help generate new sales leads for optimum new membership growth.</li>
<li>Oversees expense goals by managing payroll and general and administrative expenses.</li>
<li>Ensure that the clubs meet Crunch standards for cleanliness, maintenance, safety, security and physical plant operations.</li>
<li>Keep current in knowledge of key competitors.</li>
<li>Conduct frequent walk thrus.</li>
</ul>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Measurement Standards
</b></h3>
<ul>
<li>Successful management of all financial budgetary goals.</li>
<li>Ensure standards of clubs cleanliness and customer service excellence.</li>
<li>Demonstrate professionalism by leading by example.</li>
<li>Membership retention.</li>
<li>Timely completion of assigned tasks and projects.</li>
<li>Follow all policies and procedures.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Above description may be subject to change or alteration at any time.</b></p>
