<br>Job Description
<br>Warehouse Supervisor - Position Overview
<br>This position is accountable for managing warehousing and loader associate activities as well as provide production line support.
<br><br>
<b>Responsibilities:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>This position will ensure high-quality standards with regards to loading and inventory management at the lowest achievable cost while protecting and maintaining company assets.</li>
<li>Supervision of checker/loader associates, inventory management, QA, shipping/receiving, equipment services, and branch maintenance.</li>
<li>Ensure the efficient operation of all phases of loading, warehousing, quality, and maintenance.</li>
<li>Work closely with Route Operations to ensure all product and equipment needs are met on time.</li>
<li>Responsible for the efficient operation of designated production equipment as assigned.</li>
<li>Set daily workload schedules that will efficiently manage the department while maintaining all needs for the sales and marketing departments.</li>
<li>Maintain and inventory all parts, supplies, and equipment used in all aspects of the position's responsibility.</li>
<li>Demonstrate a complete understanding of the loading/fleet area to maximize efficiencies and output. Quickly and thoroughly react to all new programs that could help the company reduce costs, generate growth and increase revenues.</li>
<li>Select, hire, and train associates to operate and maintain all equipment ensuring safety, quality, and efficiency.</li>
<li>Complete daily/period end cycle counts and maintain an accurate inventory.</li>
</ul>
<b>Requirements:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>High school diploma or GED, a college degree in a technical field preferred.</li>
<li>Prior warehouse supervision experience, preferably in the food and beverage industry.</li>
<li>Excellent problem-solving skills, ability to systematically analyze issues and development of corrective actions that permanently eliminate recurring problems are a must.</li>
<li>Strong organizational skills, including time management and multi-tasking.</li>
<li>The computer is proficient with knowledge of MS Word, Excel, Access and inventory software system. Oracle experience is a plus. The ability to learn and understand programs is a must.</li>
<li>Forklift experience required</li>
</ul>
