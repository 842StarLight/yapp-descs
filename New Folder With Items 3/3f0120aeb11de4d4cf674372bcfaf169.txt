<h2>Manager, Sales Analytics</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p>The New York Times is seeking an intellectually curious individual who is eager to dive into the various advertising analytics and optimization challenges in our advertising business.
</p>
<p></p>
<p>The Manager, Sales Analytics will serve the Advertising team by leading a team of analysts responsible for growing the business through actionable intelligence, rigorous data management, and easily digestible data visualizations. The Manager, Sales Analytics and analysts will partner with internal stakeholders to provide essential recommendations to crucial business decisions.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Responsibilities:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li><p>Lead and develop a team of analysts supporting the advertising growth within the direct sales channel in collaboration with Sales Operations, Finance, Ad Technology, and the Advertising Sales team.
</p></li>
<li><p>Owner of designing, developing, and maintaining reporting platforms (dashboards and/or automated email reports) and reports that meet the needs of our internal decision makers. This will be accomplished in partnership with The New York Times Data &amp; Insights Group and Ad Technology teams.
</p></li>
<li><p>Maintain strong understanding of ad platforms, data sources, and sales workflow, relying on these various inputs to develop data-driven recommendations for driving direct sales and operational efficiencies.
</p></li>
<li><p>Responsible for direct sold and pipeline data reconciliation and quality assurance. Including creating necessary data requirements, policies, and processes to ensure automation, accuracy, and efficiency.
</p></li>
<li><p>Provide key business insights to senior sales and advertising operations leadership based on sales performance trends. Visualize key metrics on an ongoing basis to communicate out to product, sales, and exec teams.
</p></li>
<li><p>Develop quantitative analysis, ad hoc reports, models, and A/B tests to support advertising decisions.
</p></li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Qualifications:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li><p>Education: BA/BS in Economics, Statistics, Mathematics, or related field
</p></li>
<li><p>4+ years of digital advertising experience required, yield or analytical experience preferred.
</p></li>
<li><p>Strong analytical skills and ability to clearly synthesize large dataset into key insights.
</p></li>
<li><p>Highly proficient in the complexities of programmatic buying, selling, and technology.
</p></li>
<li><p>Experience with SQL, SAP Business Objects, Tableau, or Domo strongly desired.
</p></li>
<li><p>Strong communication skills: ability to translate quantitative details into clear, succinct summaries and recommendations for peers, sales, and executive audiences. Possess an ability to work effectively and interface with clients, sales team, and product/platform teams.
</p></li>
<li><p>Background in ad sales operations or inventory/yield management preferred.
</p></li>
<li><p>Excellent time management and attention to detail.
</p></li>
<li><p>A proven ability to do both tactical work and set an overall strategy when working on a project.
</p></li>
</ul>
