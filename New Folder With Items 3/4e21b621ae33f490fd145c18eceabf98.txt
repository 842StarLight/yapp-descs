What we need (in addition to all that stuff above)</div>
<div><div>
<div>
<p>The Loss Mitigation Supervisor monitors and processes accounts that are in the foreclosure process as well as establishing contact, communicating and negotiating with borrowers, investors and real estate agents in an attempt to resolve at-risk mortgages.
</p>
<p><b>Major Tasks and Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Handles multiple mortgage products that are 60+ days delinquent, as governed by investor, departmental and legal guidelines</li>
<li>Keeps servicing files up-to-date so borrowers can receive the best assistance</li>
<li>Minimizes loss due to foreclosure through the use of loss mitigation options, such as pre-foreclosure sales, loan modifications and repayment plans</li>
<li>Arranges for contractual services relating to property inspections, appraisers, real estate agents, etc., who may be involved in the inspection or evaluation of the property</li>
<li>Reviews title searches to ensure that no title issues threaten the standing of our investor's lien on the property</li>
<li>Makes calls to borrowers, borrowers' authorized representatives, and brokers</li>
<li>Analyzes financial statements, tax returns, credit reports, and the value of the collateral in order to determine and recommend the most feasible and appropriate workout to the customer</li>
<li>Accurately underwrites the borrower's income, through the review of paystubs, tax documents, benefit statements, business profit and loss statements, etc.</li>
<li>Addresses any borrower question or concern. Handles escalated files appropriately by providing an articulate and complete response</li>
<li>Certifies that all required actions have taken place prior to completing a foreclosure sale</li>
<li>Ensures compliance with company policies and procedures</li>
<li>Completes pre-foreclosure reviews to ensure loans are referred for foreclosure based on company requirements.
</li>
<li>Completes, monitors progress and insures deadlines are met for invoice approval, foreclosure referrals, foreclosure breach letters and collection letters.
</li>
<li>Completes, monitors progress and insures deadlines are met for property preservation, order values, and inspections.
</li>
<li>Calculates and prepares reinstatement, payoff figures, and other debt information to attorneys.
</li>
<li>Answers calls from attorney firms and vendors.
</li>
<li>Reviews tasks daily to ensure they are completed within company timelines.
</li>
<li>Other duties as assigned</li>
<br>
</ul>
