<div>

<p><b>GENERAL JOB DESCRIPTION</b></p>
<p>The VP of Marketing and Sales is responsible for the strategy, tactics and programs to create interest, demand and recognition for the Hoist &amp; Crane Service Group and its products and services using Sales Teams, Service and Product Marketing, Social Media, Advertising, Strategic Relationships, Direct Contract Negotiations and Events. The position is responsible for creating, budgeting, implementing and measuring the efficiency and revenue impact of the overall marketing strategy across the branches. The VP of Marketing and Sales further oversees the product marketing group, which is responsible for providing the sales team with product information and training to advance product sales and build product, brand and service awareness in the crane industry. Overall, the position is responsible for the strategic trajectory of sales growth, profit, department staff and managing all marketing functions to achieve company goals and position HCSG to remain competitive in the crane industry.</p>
<p><b>DUTIES &amp; RESPONSIBILITIES</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Manage and train new and existing Zone Managers and National Account Managers; Must ensure consistent and fair management style within the department</li>
<li>Develop incentive plans including commission structure and contests that drive performance against plan</li>
<li>Work with HR and Training to continue to develop recruiting, on-boarding, training and mentoring of staff.</li>
<li>Conduct monthly calls with sales leadership teams to review sales activities, performance versus plan and new ideas to leverage in-market.</li>
<li>Prepare monthly executive level reporting- YTD sales results vs. budget, growth trends, sales metrics by market and in total</li>
<li>Prepare quarterly board meeting presentations- active summary of performance, results from key initiatives, new programs/initiatives recommended, and overview of what worked, what didn’t, why and next steps.</li>
<li>Work closely with the CEO, CAO, VP of Operations and executive team to develop annual budgets and forecasts.</li>
<li>Develop key metrics, dashboards and reporting in order to drive sales performance and effectively communicate performance versus plan to executive team</li>
<li>Work with suppliers to create a media schedule, negotiate new rates and execute deliverables.</li>
<li>Directs sales and marketing efforts to achieve objectives and financial goals established in the HCSG’s Strategic Business Plan.</li>
<li>Ensure consistent corporate and product branding through social media to attract customers in the industry.</li>
<li>Other duties as assigned</li>
</ul>
</div></div></div>
