<h2>Specialist, Platform Partnerships & Strategy</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">Collective Health is a software and services company creating the healthcare experience we all deserve. Its team of engineers, designers and actuaries are starting by redefining the $1 trillion-dollar market of employer-sponsored health insurance with data driven products. Using Collective Health's complete health insurance solution, companies can design, administer and transform the consumer experience of health insurance by harnessing the power of design and technology
<br><br>
The Role
<br><br>
Collective Health is seeking a strategic and execution-oriented Specialist / Senior Specialist, Platform Partnerships &amp; Strategy (PP&amp;S) to help build, improve, and further define our growing ecosystem of partnerships with medical, pharmacy, dental, and vision networks. These represent several of the most strategic and most mission-critical relationships Collective Health maintains, and are critical to our success. The primary areas of focus will be:
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Own the day-to-day relationships with our network partners</li>
<li>Cultivate deep ties with partners and internal stakeholders to create sustainable and scalable workflows and processes</li>
<li>Leverage data to yield insights into the health of partnerships and implement new initiatives to optimize and grow the relationships, and in the process help shape Collective Health's partnership strategy</li>
</ul>
You will cultivate and nurture network partnerships, and be responsible for the overall success and growth of our partnerships. You will lead recurring business reviews, resolve strategic and operational issues, represent the voice of the partner within Collective Health, and serve as an ambassador for Collective Health to several of our most critical partnerships. Through your experiences at Collective Health, you will become an expert in partnership management, process improvement, and operations while being at the forefront of transforming the healthcare industry.
<br><br>
Responsibilities
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Manage relationships with strategic Collective Health partners across day-to-day operations, and long-term partnership growth</li>
<li>Work cross-functionally (with Operations, Client Success, Sales, etc.) to quickly resolve issues and proactively identify opportunities to improve workflows and processes</li>
<li>Develop strong commercial relationships with partners and help drive areas for mutual business growth</li>
<li>Collect, analyze, and report meaningful data on the overall health of partnerships to internal and external stakeholders to assess effectiveness</li>
<li>Conduct regular business reviews with partners to assess progress made against joint business objectives</li>
<li>Act as a subject matter expert on Collective Health's touchpoints and processes with partners</li>
<li>Further develop internal partner knowledge base to help PP&amp;S and other internal teams to operate effectively</li>
<li>Actively collaborate with internal and external stakeholders to drive partnership development initiatives forward</li>
</ul>
Requirements
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>5+ years healthcare experience in managing external business relationships</li>
<li>Combined operational pre-sales and post-sales experience preferred</li>
<li>Bachelor's degree required</li>
<li>Experience being externally facing and owning strategic relationships</li>
<li>Experience with healthcare, health insurance, and healthcare systems is strongly preferred</li>
<li>Ability to put structure and process around issues that are complex or ambiguous</li>
<li>Excellent verbal and written communication skills</li>
<li>Comfortable navigating ambiguity</li>
<li>Ability to thrive in cross-functional teams</li>
</ul>
