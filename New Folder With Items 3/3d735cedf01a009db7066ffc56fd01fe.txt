<h2>Assistant Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div></div>
<p></p>
<div><div><div><div>
<div>Apartment Management Consultants (AMC) is a leader in the property management industry. Our passion for helping people, creating authentic relationships, and common values has established a working environment that undeniably stands out. Every day our associates have the opportunity to collaborate together in a thriving culture with a determined attitude that inspires and rewards original ideas. AMC provides significant career advancement opportunities, competitive compensation, benefits, and incentive programs.</div>
<div></div>
<div>We are currently seeking a full-time Assistant Manager!</div>
<div></div>
<div>This position will assist with two properties in Nob Hill totaling 266 apartments. Working Weekends are required with two consecutive days off during the week.</div>
<div></div>
<div>The Assistant Community Manager supports and assists the Community Manager in overseeing and managing the financial and operational facets of the community.</div>
<div></div>
<div>Responsibilities include:</div>
<div></div>
<ul><li>Oversee file management and run assigned reports</li></ul>
<ul><li>Utilize proper customer service and sales techniques and ensure efficient and courteous response to all resident requests</li></ul>
<ul><li>Collect all monies due and oversee audit evictions, potential skips, and move outs by walking units to evaluate occupancy</li></ul>
<ul><li>Communicate with outside legal counsel during the eviction process</li></ul>
<ul><li>Finalize move in/out and renewals files and enter data into property management software</li></ul>
<ul><li>Oversee resident renter’s insurance procedure</li></ul>
<ul><li>Collect deposits and process future residents’ applications</li></ul>
<ul><li>Ensure the model/target apartments are ready for show and maintain a clean work space</li></ul>
<ul><li>Maintain a thorough knowledge of lease terms, unit specifications, availability, current market conditions, and all company and community policies and ensure understanding of required application information, screening process, policies and procedures</li></ul>
<ul><li>Maintain contact with all apartment locator services and local businesses to provide informational material</li></ul>
<ul><li>“Shop” surrounding or competing properties and conduct outreach marketing</li></ul>
<ul><li>Develop and maintain on-going resident retention programs</li></ul>
<ul><li>Report unusual or extraordinary circumstances regarding the property or residents</li></ul>
<ul><li>Maintain a professional appearance and conduct at all times</li></ul>
<div></div>
<div>Requirements:</div>
<div></div>
<ul><li>Customer service experience</li></ul>
<ul><li>6 months experience in the property management industry</li></ul>
<ul><li>Strong communication skills both written and verbal</li></ul>
<ul><li>The ability to remain professional and courteous in a fast-paced working environment</li></ul>
<ul><li>Organization skills with strong attention to detail</li></ul>
