<h2>Digital/Events Marketing Coordinator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Hear Again America</b> <b>│Digital</b> <b>Marketing Specialist</b></p>
<p><b>Summary: </b></p>
<p>Hear Again America is seeking an experienced professional responsible for the daily coordination and execution of pay-per-click (PPC) advertising, as well as supporting digital campaign planning/optimization and other various marketing activities. As well, must be flexible, quick to adapt and comfortable with working on a range of tasks that range from event planning/coordination, print and social media management.</p>
<p>This position requires knowledge and experience in setting up, executing, and monitoring PPC campaigns in Google AdWords, Bing and Yahoo for effectiveness, efficiency, and ROI. Critical thinking is a necessity and regular reporting on performance/contribution to team stretch goals is required.</p>
<p><b>Responsibilities and Duties</b></p>
<p><b>Responsibilities: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Create, manage, analyze, optimize and report on PPC campaigns in Google AdWords and other search platforms to maximize ROI and maintain consistent throughput of qualified leads, funnel and sales.</li>
<li>Monitor and optimize placements, keywords, bids, budget caps, impression share, quality score, and other key metrics.</li>
<li>Create, monitor, and optimize PPC text and image ads to increase quality score, click-through rate, conversion rate, and sales.</li>
<li>Plan PPC campaign landing pages and optimize through experimentation</li>
<li>Assist with web purchase path optimization</li>
<li>Assist in larger-scale region-specific campaign localizations and launches</li>
<li>Determine and execute testing methodologies and reporting requirements to support paid advertising channels</li>
<li>Daily, weekly, and monthly internal reporting, as well as quantitative analysis using Excel, Google Analytics, and Google Webmaster Tools</li>
<li>Communicate, to team management, project development, timelines, and results</li>
<li>Flexibility to handle other duties as required.</li>
<li>Travel to company events (Lunch and Learns &amp; Community Seminars) and assist with the planning, logistics, appointments and overall success.</li>
<li>Oversee the company’s organic marketing for all locations.</li>
<li>Create and post monthly marketing calendar on all social media platform (Facebook, Google+, Twitter and LinkedIn)</li>
<li>Maintain and manage all marketing supplies</li>
<li>Coordinating of monthly marketing events</li>
<li>Manages each element of email and marketing automation campaigns including creative development, setup, targeting and testing to deployment and data monitoring</li>
</ul>
