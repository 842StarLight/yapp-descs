JOB SUMMARY
<br><br>
The Manufacturing Manager will oversee and be responsible for all types of medicinal extracts from Cresco Labs product. The Manufacturing Manager completes will maintain the organization, cleanliness and efficiency of production area. The Manufacturing Manager maintains quality control measures to ensure high quality product and carries out day-to-day tasks including: prepping, extracting, and packaging. The Manufacturing Manager must monitor and maintain production facility in accordance with the commonwealth and standards set by Cresco Labs.
