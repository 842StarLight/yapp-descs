<p>
Oldcastle BuildingEnvelope®, a CRH company, is the leading supplier of products specified to close the building envelope. They include: custom-engineered curtain wall and window wall, architectural windows, storefront systems, doors, skylights and architectural glass.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Summary</b></p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
Oldcastle BuildingEnvelope® is seeking full-time Door and Frame Fabricator/Assembler to work at our Fremont, CA location. This position requires an ability to follow written and verbal instruction and have organizational skills. The ideal candidate will have some mechanical aptitude.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Essential Duties and Responsibilities</b></p>
<p></p>
<br>
<ul>
<li>
Fabricating and assembling architectural aluminum products with various tools and machinery</li>
<li><p>
Layout and fabricate door/frame components to accept standard and custom hardware</p></li>
<li>
Read and comprehend sales orders, fabrication drawings, hardware templates, die drawings, and product catalogs</li>
<li>
All other duties as assigned</li>
<li>
Regular and predictable attendance, ability to safely perform tasks, and adherence to all safety policies and procedures are essential functions of your job</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Requirements</b></p>
<p></p>
<br>
<ul>
<li>
Must speak, read, and understand English to understand and communicate paperwork and labels</li>
<li>
Ability to read and interpret basic mathematical measurements and effectively read and use a tape measure</li>
<li>
Must be able to work overtime as needed</li>
<li>
All production workers are expected to be available to work in all areas of production</li>
<li>
Ability to use machinery and hand tools to fabricate products</li>
<li>
Welding knowledge and skill is a plus</li>
<li>
High School Diploma or GED, preferred</li>
<li>
Previous fabrication experience preferred</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Physical Requirements</b></p>
<p></p>
<br>
<ul>
<li>
Ability to squat, crouch, bend, twist, lift, pull, reach, and walk frequently every day</li>
<li>
Must have the ability to lift up to 50 lbs from floor level and up to 75 pounds from waist level</li>
<li>
Ability to work in varied temperatures consistent with same climate conditions of the region (Heat, humidity, etc.)</li>
</ul>
