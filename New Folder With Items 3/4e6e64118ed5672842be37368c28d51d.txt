<h2>Technical Revenue Manager - Business Partnerships</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">Anaplan is looking to hire a motivated and results driven Technical Revenue Manager to join the Accounting team. This person is enthusiastic about engaging cross functionally on initiatives directly leading to the growth of Anaplan. You will bring both technical ASC 606 revenue recognition expertise and operational SaaS order to cash process flow experience to the team. The person in this role will project manage various new and existing initiatives to help the company scale and mature in compliance with SOX while bringing efficiencies to the Order To Cash and Revenue Recognition process flows. This is a unique opportunity to succeed in a company focused on Connected Planning across the organization.
<br><br>
Responsibilities Include
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Lead special projects related to revenue and ensure compliance with established regulations, company practices and SOX</li>
<li>Manage cross functional partnerships with FP&amp;A, Legal, Deal Desk, Pricing, and Operations teams to drive operational efficiencies</li>
<li>Project manage technical and operational impact of new revenue initiatives on the Order to Cash and Revenue processes, including reporting requirements and accounting treatment</li>
<li>Collaborate with cross-functional departments to drive operational efficiencies</li>
<li>Assist the revenue accounting team with ad hoc research and reporting needs</li>
<li>Be a contributing member to the team, obtaining information and competently analyzing and prioritizing information to make appropriate recommendations and suggest solutions</li>
<li>Potential for managing other technical and cross functional processes</li>
</ul>
Required Experience and Skills
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>BS/BA degree in Accounting</li>
<li>Three (3) - five (5) years SaaS industry</li>
<li>Big 4 audit manager experience</li>
<li>Certified Public Accountant required</li>
<li>Excellent written and verbal communication skills</li>
<li>Detail oriented and able to effectively organize and manage multiple responsibilities</li>
<li>Able to work both independently and collaboratively with a team and suggest improvements to</li>
<li>various processes</li>
<li>Positive and upbeat personality to work in a dynamic environment with all levels of management and functions</li>
<li>Strong understanding of accounting principles and operational best practices for SaaS</li>
<li>Salesforce user experience</li>
</ul>
Strong Plus
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Experience with Workday as an ERP and Revenue system</li>
<li>User or model builder experience with Anaplan</li>
</ul>
