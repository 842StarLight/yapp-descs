<b>About the role:</b><br>
A Customer Success Specialist at Dialpad helps maintain high levels of satisfaction and engagement among our customer base. A Customer Success Specialist is ultimately responsible for making sure the Small Business customers are realizing the value of their account, ultimately driving adoption and outcomes leading to renewals, expansion, and advocacy. It's a great opportunity for a SaaS Customer Success or Account Manager with 1 to 2 years of experience to help build out and grow with a dynamic start-up. The Customer Success Specialist is a member of the Sales organization and reports directly to the Director of Customer Success.
<br><br>
<b>About You:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Lead all post-sales activities for Dialpad's customers through strong relationship-building, product knowledge, planning, and execution.</li>
<li>Act as the voice of the customer within Dialpad. As the owner of the customer relationship, the Customer Success Specialist is expected to work cross-functionally with Sales, Marketing, Product, Engineering and Data Science to ensure consistent and strong customer message is embedded in everything we do.</li>
<li>Develop a positive, trusted relationship with your customers by providing value throughout the customer partnership.</li>
<li>Retain long-term partnerships with customers by independently renewing successful customers.</li>
<li>Establish and oversee the customer's adoption, training, and development of best practices to continually drive incremental value and return on the customer's investment.</li>
<li>Maintain a deep understanding of our solutions and speak with customers about the most meaningful features/functionality for their specific business needs.</li>
<li>Manage customer escalations by documenting open issues and working cross-functionally to resolution.</li>
<li>Track accounts to identify churn risks and work proactively to mitigate those risks.</li>
</ul>
<b>To be successful in the role, you will bring:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>1 - 2 years of experience in Customer Success, Sales, Support/Service or customer facing roles</li>
<li>Real passion for providing top-notch account management</li>
<li>Strong written communication skills</li>
<li>Excellent time management and organizational skills with the ability to track numerous details</li>
<li>Desire to work in a dynamic startup where your input is desired to help craft our offerings and how we interact with clients</li>
</ul>
