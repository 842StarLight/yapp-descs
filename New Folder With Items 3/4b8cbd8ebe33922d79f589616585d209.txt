<h2>AWS Accounting and Operations Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>SHI has an exciting opportunity available as an Accounting and Operations Specialist to run and improve the accounting and operational processes used by the cloud team. The Accounting and Operations Specialist will work through the billing, transactional and accounting processes to close out monthly cloud business, act as the hands-on financial subject matter expert and a resource for the team, be the point person between SHI and AWS on billing and transaction processing matters, accounting issues with AWS partners, between the cloud team and SHI’s Accounting teams, and responsible for innovating supporting processes in support of our massive revenue growth.</p>
<p><b>Responsibilities/Job Function</b></p>
<p><i>Include but not limited to: </i></p>
<ul>
<li>Own and collaborate with Accounting, AX, BI, CRM, MySHI, internal and other teams as needed to process and automate workflows with applicable data reporting.</li>
<li>Process and manage the monthly AX and ordering processes supporting cloud transaction finances for AWS, Google and other cloud business.</li>
<li>Work with internal, external, reporting and processing tools to accurately and timely process billing.</li>
<li>Investigate payment disputes and follow-up with SHI, client or partner accounting teams.</li>
<li>Compile and publish financial reports internally, externally and to clients.</li>
<li>Identify areas for improvement and take initiative to properly automate and expand capabilities to meet growing demand.</li>
<li>Work with internal team account managers and external SHI reps across all segments to service invoice, discounts, credit limit, taxation, payment term and other aspects of monthly billing processing.</li>
<li>Report on workflow compliance to drive efficiency and high levels of customer satisfaction.</li>
</ul>
