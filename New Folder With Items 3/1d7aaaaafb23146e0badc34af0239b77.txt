<h2>VP, Client Delivery</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Mobiquity is a digital engagement provider for more than 200 of the world’s leading brands. We are a collective of consultants, designers, developers, cloud advisors, and data scientists - spread across 3 continents and 9 offices - who create compelling digital experiences to help clients show they care to the people who matter most - their consumers, patients, and employees.</p>
In order to continuously and constantly grow our team, scale our business properly, and provide the best solutions and support to our clients, we are in need of a Vice President of Client Delivery to our team.<br>
<p>Responsibilities:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Lead the Client Delivery practice for a defined region or portfolio of clients</li>
<li>
Oversee delivery of all programs within the portfolio ensuring client satisfaction while meeting Mobiquity’s business goals</li>
<li>
Participate in developing client growth plans with VP Sales for key strategic accounts within the portfolio. Utilize SMEs to help plan and execute the growth plan.</li>
<li>
Ensure programs are adequately resourced and managed to deliver successfully and gain client satisfaction</li>
<li>
Support business development discussions with clients related to new streams of work within the Mobiquity offerings portfolio</li>
<li>
Continue to improve and innovate Mobiquity’s delivery offerings</li>
<li>
Manage successful client delivery including project management, workshop facilitation, deliverable document planning and production</li>
<li>
Provide mentoring to the delivery the group on process and market/industry knowledge</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
Required Experience:</p>
<ul>
<li>
At least 10 years of experience as a delivery leader in technology within large enterprises</li>
<li>
Effective at interacting with C-suite executives on key strategic issues</li>
<li>
Knowledge of delivery tools and techniques for software development</li>
<li>
Demonstrated business development and sales capabilities</li>
<li>
Strong project management skills</li>
<li>
General knowledge of enterprise IT architectures and business processes</li>
<li>
Good team leader, contributor</li>
<li>
Good communications skills for internal and client correspondence</li>
<li>
Independent, self-starter</li>
</ul>
