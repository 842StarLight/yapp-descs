<h2>ACTIVECORE OPS SPECIALIST</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Comcast Business offers technology solutions ranging from Ethernet, internet, and WiFi connectivity to voice, television, and managed Enterprise solutions to power businesses of all sizes to perform better. From small businesses to mid-market and large Enterprise organizations, Comcast Business serves business customers across the country. Powered by an advanced, Gig-speed network and backed by 24/7 technical support, Comcast Business is one of the largest contributors to the growth of Comcast Cable. The organization is the nation's largest cable provider to small and mid-size businesses and has emerged as a force in the Enterprise market, recognized by leading industry associations as one of the fastest growing provider of Ethernet services.</p>

<div><b>Position Summary:<br>
</b></div>
<div></div>
<div>The Specialist-3 position is responsible for ActiveCore service order entry accuracy in various systems along with other administrative and coordination duties such as building customer accounts and dispatch work orders in the local biller application and driving resolution on ActiveCore pending cancel orders. Order entry accuracy includes verification based on clean order policy. Clean order policy ensures all information is received for order entry, provisioning, network operations center and billing to build, invoice and support ActiveCore services. The ActiveCore customer base includes E-Rate, Rural Healthcare, Large Enterprise &amp; California Teleconnect Fund orders which must follow federal guidelines.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
The ActiveCore Ops Specialist position requires the ability to understand contractual obligations, service level agreements, network designs &amp; plant test dates (PTD). This position will work closely with Sales, Engineering, Project Management, Provisioning, Network Operations, and Billing.</div>

</li>

</li>
