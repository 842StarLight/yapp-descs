<h2>Accounts Payable Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div></div>
<div><div>
<p>National Veterinary Associates (NVA) is the largest private owner of freestanding veterinary hospitals in the United States. NVA owns over 780 veterinary hospitals and boarding facilities in the United States, Canada, Australia and New Zealand. NVA has grown rapidly and profitably every year since its inception in 1996.<br>
</p>
<br>
<p><b>Job Summary:</b></p>
<p>
We are currently seeking a full-time, experienced Accounts Payable Manager to join our Support Center team. Our invoices are received and scanned into our payables processing system and paid on a defined schedule from our corporate office in Agoura Hills. This role is integral to NVAs operations, since timely and accurate recording and payment of invoices is a key part of our financial operations and reporting processes. We need a focused, organized, and team-oriented individual to perform varied tasks accurately and efficiently.</p>
<br>
<p><b>
Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Develops, executes and manages Accounts payable procedures and policies in line with company guidelines.</li>
<li>
Establishes and implements technology solutions pertaining to accounts payable &amp; department projects.</li>
<li>
Monitors approval workflows and manages invoice processing in accounts payables systems</li>
<li>
Mentors the accounts payable staff and prioritizes the department’s daily workflow.</li>
<li>
Oversee the invoice coding/keying and payment processing, ensuring accuracy and timeliness.</li>
<li>
Leads the team on resolving various accounts payable issues and reports any discrepancies or deviation from the department’s procedures for quick resolution.</li>
<li>
Provides trainings to the newly hired staff on processing records for all A/P transactions, including vendor invoices, payroll advances, expense reports, expedited requests, etc.</li>
<li>
Manages weekly check runs by payment terms, ensures on proper approvals, maintenance of checks register &amp; AP system interfaces for electronic payments.</li>
<li>
Manages month-end tasks, recording of accruals, runs controls on account balances &amp; checks on the accurate recording of invoices.</li>
<li>
Manages credit cards and payment reconciliations processes.</li>
<li>
Manages vendor relationships and adherence to document requirements including 1099s regulations &amp; reporting.</li>
<li>
Oversees and supports day-to-day operations related to the processing and disbursements of accurate and approved Non-PO and PO invoices.</li>
<li>
Develops and updates the accounts payable desk procedures, internal controls, accounts payables policies as needed</li>
<li>
Analyzes A/P metrics and balances team workload on regular basis.</li>
<li>
Recruits, selects, hires, trains and provides performance appraisals, employee coaching and counseling and regularly evaluates all personnel resources.</li>
<li>
Ensures the timely submission of all performance appraisals, employment-related paperwork and other employee status changes within the division including informing HR of employee leaves of absence, work-related injuries and employee incidents.</li>
<li>
Attends and participates in staff meetings to provide further input towards department development and staff guidance.</li>
<li>
Assists in regular external audits and ensures compliance.</li>
<li>
Communicates effectively &amp; professionally, while promoting favorable interaction with managers, co-workers and other departments.</li>
<li>
Assumes additional responsibilities as assigned and assists with projects as needed.
</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Required Qualifications:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Bachelor’s degree in business administration, accounting, finance or related field</li>
<li>
Five-Seven (5-7) years’ experience in accounts payable role, of which minimum two-three (2-3) have been spent in supervising/managing an accounting department</li>
<li>
Knowledge of accounts payable principles, policies and procedures</li>
<li>
Possess time management skills with ability to prioritize job tasks effectively</li>
<li>
Must have strong work ethics</li>
<li>
Ability to adapt to changing organizational and operational needs</li>
<li>
Possess strong organizational and leadership skills</li>
<li>
Must be able to follow standard procedures &amp; instructions</li>
<li>
Must be computer-savvy &amp; able to work on a multi-platform environment</li>
<li>
Detail oriented, professional attitude, reliable (this is must)</li>
<li>
Ability to communicate effectively verbally and in writing</li>
<li>
Ability to work independently and with a team in a fast-paced and high volume environment with emphasis on accuracy and timeliness</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Preferred:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Communication – Effectively and consistently provides directives to team. Encourages interactive discussions and maintains an open-door policy. Ensures that all staff within the program are properly educated and informed about matters relating to the department</li>
<li>
Technical - Proficient in MS Office: Excel, Word, PowerPoint, accounting ERP software experience is a plus</li>
<li>
Operational – Strong accounting principles knowledge, thorough understanding of applicable accounts payable/general ledger systems and procedures, financial chart of accounts and procedures</li>
<li>
Problem Solving – Ability to analyze problems and implement acceptable solutions</li>
<li>
General – Effective research and resolution skills, data analysis and multi-tasking skills</li>
<li>
Relationships and Attitude – Models professionalism by maintaining effective working relationships, following all policies and procedures and approaching challenges with a proactive and positive attitude. Also develops strong, trusted relationships with colleagues and field sites</li>
<li>
Attendance –Models good attendance by adhering to their regular work schedule and at times working additional or varied hours to accommodate workflow</li>
<li>
Confidentiality - Maintains the confidentiality of all business documents and correspondence</li>
</ul>
