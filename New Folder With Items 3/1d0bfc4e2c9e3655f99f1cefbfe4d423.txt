<h2>Senior Product Manager, San Mateo</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<b>Position:</b> We are looking for a Senior Product Manager to work on our flagship product integrator.io, our Integration Platform-as-a-Service (iPaas). This is a great opportunity for a product manager with some SaaS experience and a desire to drive the future of a platform that has enormous growth potential in a vibrant, competitive market. This is a full-time role that can be done remotely anywhere in the USA, but preferably based out of our San Mateo, CA headquarters location.
<br><br>
Who are we looking for?
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>2-5 years product management experience with a successful, growing SaaS product.</li>
<li>Domain experience with one or more large ERP, CRM, eCommerce or similar SaaS/Cloud apps and related business processes.</li>
<li>A passionate advocate for the needs of customers and users.</li>
<li>Strategic thinker that speaks with authority and can back everything they say with proper research, data and facts.</li>
<li>Dynamic, confident personality with ability to successfully define and drive product requirements with minimal guidance amidst multiple priorities and goals.</li>
<li>Someone that is pragmatic, decisive, hands-on and embraces a modern, agile release process; we do monthly feature releases on the platform.</li>
<li>Genuine interest in learning about integration as a valuable, independent domain.</li>
<li>Strong communicator with exceptional written and verbal communication skills, as well as active listening skills.</li>
<li>Someone that is able to effectively negotiates work scope with business partners to align on objectives around time to market, effort and scope</li>
<li>A self starter with strong project management skills who is motivated, ambitious and has a strong work ethic.</li>
<li>Someone who can work cross-functionally to drive product delivery</li>
<li>Work very comfortably with stakeholders at the department or division level</li>
<li>Ability to look ahead to anticipate blockers, conflicts and obstacles, and work with management to address and appropriately solve them</li>
</ul>
What would you do, if hired?
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Defining, designing and overseeing a large number of features while entrenching yourself in all the day to day activities around the platform.</li>
<li>Becoming an expert user as quickly as possible.</li>
<li>Establishing yourself as a leader within a cross-functional internal team in order to inspire the team to create a product that has the most impact on the market.</li>
<li>Mastering the integration domain and its best practices.</li>
<li>Creating Sales enablement presentations.</li>
<li>Conducting competitive analysis.</li>
<li>Interpreting existing analytics while designing a new class of analytics to help us make better decisions.</li>
<li>Gathering and analyzing customer feedback.</li>
<li>Contributing to product strategy discussions, and helping lead execution efforts once decisions are made.</li>
<li>Helping with platform documentation and rollout; communicate feature releases to customers and internal teams.</li>
<li>Evangelizing the platform and the company.</li>
</ul>
<b>Bonus Points:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Previous experience in managing an API as a product.</li>
<li>Previous experience as a product owner of a SaaS marketplace.</li>
<li>A deep understanding of at least one large cloud application/ecosystem.</li>
</ul>
Why You'll Love It Here
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>We are solving a really hard problem that affects almost every business on the planet: integrating cloud apps</li>
<li>You get the best of both worlds: a start-up opportunity at a company that has already established itself in the market with thousands of happy customers.</li>
<li>You'll have the opportunity to make a significant contribution to the company as well as the iPaaS industry as a whole.</li>
<li>We're at an exciting crossroads as we re-imagine the Integration and iPaaS space. This will take teamwork, creativity, strategic thinking and a willingness to advance the market.</li>
<li>We offer a strong benefits package, 401k, pre-tax commuter expense reimbursement, fully stocked kitchen, snacks, and many other perks.</li>
</ul>
