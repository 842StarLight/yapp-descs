<p>
Pricing Consultant, Product Management</p>
<p></p>
<p><b><br>
Role Overview</b><br>
Our Pricing team within Product Management is responsible for developing, implementing and evaluating these strategies. We conduct research and competitive pricing analysis and monitor the ongoing business environment to ensure Secureworks maintains a competitive market position. Leads, develops, evaluates and implements pricing strategies and models based upon current business and competitive market dynamics. Maintains and communicates competitive price position. Leads the proposal for price position changes across platforms and markets (per price band) with other functional leaders to ensure competitive and financially viable price position. Monitors and reports regional and/or global competitive and/or cost trends. Focuses list pricing activities and influences discounting and promotional practices with the intent of maximizing overall operating income. Ensures proper cost uploads to sales tools are delivered and leads cost impact clarity to RUM (Revenue, Units, Margin) P&amp;L. Supports the RUM guidance process, which incorporates revenue and margin implications of forward pricing plans for assigned product categories as appropriate. Creates the list pricing at launch of new models or products and generates the associated financial projections based on proposed price and anticipated costs. Develops and maintains competitive pricing databases designed to monitor changes in the marketplace and their impact on the corporation. Works cross-functionally to correctly position product portfolio and establish pricing programs aligned with product strategy, sales strategy and revenue recognition requirements.</p>

<b>
Key Responsibilities
</b>

<li>Set list pricing and allowable discount range for all products in the Secureworks portfolio</li>

Perform Win/Loss and other price optimization analyses</li>

Create price proposals to adjust pricing with the goal of optimizing revenue and margin results</li>

Perform cost analysis to capture and articulate gross margins based on proposed and current pricing</li>
