<h2>Production Maintenance Mechanic</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Overview of the Production Maintenance Mechanic position</b></p>
<p>This position is responsible for preventative maintenance, troubleshooting, and repair on all plant machinery and equipment to sustain production at high efficiency levels.</p>
<p><b>Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Maintain equipment including mechanical, electrical, hydraulic, pneumatic and fluid handling systems.</li>
<li>Accurately record project activities and prepares appropriate operating standards and/or maintenance procedures for all equipment and processes.</li>
<li>Maintain cleanliness of maintenance equipment area.</li>
<li>Maintain appropriate inventory of maintenance supplies.</li>
<li>Establish and maintain communication with appropriate equipment vendors and suppliers.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Requirements</b></p>
<ul><li>High School Diploma or GED required. Minimum three (3) years of production equipment maintenance experience, preferably in food and beverage environment.</li></ul>
<ul>
<li>Hydraulic and pneumatic systems knowledge.</li>
<li>Ability to understand blueprints, diagrams and ladder logic programs.</li>
<li>Experience with software interface tools for Allen Bradley or GE PLCs and windows based software,</li>
<li>Proficient in using hand, power, shop and diagnostic tools.</li>
<li>Skilled in electrical power/controls, mechanical and fluid handling systems.</li>
<li>Trade or Technical school certification preferred.</li>
<li>Forklift experience (required in some locations).</li>
</ul>
