<h2>Spa Receptionist - VillaSpa at VillaSport</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>POSITION SUMMARY:</b></p>
<p>
The purpose of this position is to facilitate guest/member flow for the spa. This position may be assigned to either reception responsibilities or valet responsibilities. Valets greet guests, provide spa tours, guide guests to prepare for treatments, and ensure the cleanliness of the common areas of the spa. Receptionists greet guests, check in guests, schedule spa services, answer inquiries and sell and promote services and retail products. Both roles actively interact with guests/members providing unsurpassed service levels.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
QUALIFICATIONS:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Excellent communication skills, to encompass phone and face-to-face interaction. Active hospitality.</li>
<li>Proficient use and knowledge of the spa computer software system, the spa menu and club services.</li>
<li>Strong background in a hospitality industry preferred.</li>
<li>Ability to work with minimal supervision.</li>
<li>Excellent written and verbal communication skills</li>
<li>Must fluently speak, read, write and understand the primary language used in the work place.</li>
<li>Experience in sales, retail, or administration is preferred; salon/spa experience is a plus.</li>
<li>High School graduate; junior college, college, or college courses preferred but not required.</li>
<br>
</ul>
