<h2>Commercial Learning & Development Leader, GE Aviation</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Role Summary:</b></h2>
Design, build and manage the global strategy and framework of Commercial Learning &amp; Development solutions. This role provides strategic learning and development consultation to key stakeholders in Sales, Marketing, and Product Management and designs, delivers and measures a portfolio of programs, offerings and experiences that enhance and advance the skills and capabilities of GE Aviation Commercial employees at all levels in the organization.<br>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Essential Responsibilities:</b></h2>
<br>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div><ul>
<li>Lead strategy, design, &amp; execution of commercial learning solutions globally, including identifying, analyzing, and prioritizing training needs<br>
</li>
<li>Partner with the Aviation Commercial and HR Leaders, to build and implement learning in support of key GE Aviation commercial initiatives<br>
</li>
<li>Own end-to-end curriculum for functional training within the Commercial organization<br>
</li>
<li>Design, deliver and facilitate training/earning solutions utilizing a number of channels and modalities<br>
</li>
<li>Identify, source and manage best-in-class vendors to meet training needs<br>
</li>
<li>Partner and consult with GE L&amp;D teams to leverage previously developed core, consistent, and common content where applicable to ensure overall alignment, as well as continuity across multiple programs and teams<br>
</li>
<li>Synthesize existing needs analyses and supplement with additional qualitative and quantitative data as needed<br>
</li>
<li>Conduct commercial capability model assessments and support findings with scalable learning strategies for employees<br>
</li>
<li>Identify and drive cutting edge contemporary commercial content, design, and delivery approaches into the commercial learning strategy<br>
</li>
<li>Formulate delivery strategies for curricula, including train-the-trainer, eLearning, and internal and/or external instructor development<br>
</li>
<li>Build and drive strong impact of the commercial learning community on the learning strategy<br>
</li>
<li>Measure training effectiveness in terms of impact on business goals, behavior change, and return on investment; monitor and adjust accordingly</li>
</ul></div>
