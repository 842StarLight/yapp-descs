<h2>Part time Graphic Designer / Marketing Coordinator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>425 Fitness is a local health club including Bothell, Redmond and Issaquah locations. We are seeking a talented Graphic Design / Marketing Coordinator with a passion for designing marketing messages! This position is a hybrid of graphic design as well as marketing analysis to put together professional materials for club announcements, advertising and events. We want someone who can bring 425 Fitness to life in our communities and clubs.</p>
<p>
The duties of the position include:</p>
<ul>
<li>Working with area management to develop and roll out monthly marketing initiatives as part of our yearly plan</li>
<li>Create excitement around the 425 Fitness brand by utilizing existing channels and developing new ideas to get to the next level</li>
<li>Manage social media presence</li>
<li>Work with our current advertising partners to ensure they have all the materials they need</li>
<li>Learn and become comfortable with 425 Fitness web programs including the site itself</li>
<li>Promote local events</li>
</ul>
<p>You will be working directly with the Director of Sales and Marketing to keep the clubs on the cutting edge of fitness marketing. Main work location will be at our corporate office in Bothell with potential monthly trips to other locations depending on the current marketing schedule.</p>
<p>
We are looking for someone who wants to utilize their skills to help our communities become healthier and more involved in health and fitness!</p>
<p>
Job Type: Part-time</p>
<p>
Salary: $20 an hour depending on experience, minimum 20 hours a week (9am-1pm)</p>
<p>
Required experience:</p>
<ul>
<li>Graphic and Web Design, Social Media Management.</li>
</ul>
