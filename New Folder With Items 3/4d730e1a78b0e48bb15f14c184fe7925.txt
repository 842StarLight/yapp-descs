<div><b>
Ready Mix Customer Service Representative</b></div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div><b>
Position Overview</b></div>
<p>
The dispatcher processes and coordinates the phone sales and distribution of concrete products for multiple plants. Responsible for staffing personnel based on customer orders. Requires day-to-day communication with other employees and customers.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Key Responsibilities (Essential Duties and Functions)</b></h3>
<ul>
<li>
Dispatch must be aware of special jobsite conditions that require specific trucks, spacing, slump requirements, etc.</li>
<li>
Dispatcher needs to be familiar with the delivery area and must be able to assist lost drivers in finding jobsites, determine travel times to jobs.</li>
<li>
Dispatcher must be customer service minded and, be able to keep a level head under stressful conditions.</li>
<li>
Responsible for a safe work environment for themselves and co-workers.</li>
<li>
Properly follow all company policies and OSHA/MSHA regulations for safe working procedures and environment. Wear personal protective equipment (PPE) in designated operations and production areas as stated by OSHA and/or MSHA.</li>
<li>
Follow directions of Foreperson as to daily tasks and expectations.</li>
<li>
Display a professional and courteous attitude to co-workers, supervisors, and the general public at all times.</li>
<li>
Reliable attendance is essential to performance in this role.</li>
</ul>

<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Qualifications<br>
</b></h3>
<p></p>
<p>To perform this job successfully, an individual must be able to perform each essential duty satisfactorily.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
Education/Experience</p>
<ul>
<li>
Minimum high school/general education degree (GED) required.</li>
<li>
3 years previous related experience in dispatch for concrete or transportation industry required.</li>
<li>
Bilingual (Spanish/English) preferred.<p></p>
<br>
<p>
Work Requirements</p>
</li>
<li>
Communicate effectively with customers as well as truck drivers to dispatch orders in a timely and efficient manner.</li>
<li>
Must provide excellent customer service by phone; scheduling and organizing large numbers of people and equipment efficiently and quickly.</li>
<li>
Handle multiple tasks concurrently and perform under pressure.</li>
<li>
Perform opening and closing procedures.</li>
<li>
Must be able to act quickly and creatively to solve problems; work independently and make decisions.</li>
<li>
Utilize GPS tracking software and enter orders into dispatch scheduling software; ability to quickly learn different software programs.</li>
<li>
Perform preventative maintenance to plant equipment.</li>
<li>
Strictly adhere to safety requirements, policies, and procedures as outlined in the employee policy manual.</li>
<li>
Report to work at the designated start time.</li>
<li>
Work in a team environment and assist co-workers or supervisors/managers with other duties as required.</li>
<li>
Available to work overtime, nights and weekends when necessary. Reliable attendance is essential in performance of the role.</li>
<li>
Travel and work away from home when necessary.<p>
Knowledge/Skill Requirements</p>
</li>
<li>
Knowledge of concrete products, concrete mix designs and suitability for their intended use; weights and measures laws and regulations; trucks and transportation requirements and regulations; local area and distances for pricing and delivery.</li>
<li>
Must possess excellent verbal skills.</li>
<li>
Read and understand maps.</li>
<li>
Proficient computer knowledge.<p></p>
