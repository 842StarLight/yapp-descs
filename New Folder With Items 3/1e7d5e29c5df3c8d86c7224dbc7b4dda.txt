<h2>Vice President, Marketing</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">Job Description
<br>Medline Industries, Inc. has an immediate opening for a Vice President, Marketing, located in Northfield, IL. This person will strategically and tactically lead all marketing efforts as well as support and coordinate significant ‘sales operations’ efforts; rep and customer support. We are seeking a dynamic, hard-working professional with a solid education or background in Corporate Marketing with healthcare industry experience and a desire to work for a fast-paced, large corporate organization. In addition to exceptional leadership and analytical skills, you must have a “roll-up your sleeves” attitude.
<br><br>
<b>The challenging responsibilities of the position include:</b><br>
Develop and implement a clear marketing strategy for current products and markets
<br>Develop marketing plans that are consistent with both corporation and division goals that meet the growth targets of the business
<br>Lead new business development activity, including current products into new and existing sites
<br>Maintain contacts with key customers (National Accounts / Group Purchasing Organizations) and other clients
<br>Work collaboratively with cross-functional leaders and departments to support the complex operational components of the business
<br>Work with the sales organization to support sales and margin commitments to the group
<br>Review significant financial expenditures, adjusting for contingencies and evaluating results at the end of the year
<br>Develop and manage all sales communications and training programs
<br>Ensure group promotion programs optimize the return on investment while meeting the needs of the markets and customers served
<br>Review and approve materials relating to advertising, market research, forecast and pricing
<br>Assist the Division President in meeting sales and margin performance objectives
<br>Develop and manage a structured process for all RFP responses In return, Medline offers a business casual, entrepreneurial work environment with strong growth potential; a competitive compensation package; along with a complete benefits package including medical/dental/vision/life insurance; 401(k) with company match and much more!
