<h2>Internal Buyer Inspector</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Position Summary
</b></p>
<p></p>This position is responsible for assisting Internal Buyers Inspection for quality control processes
<p></p>
<p><b>Role and Responsibilities
</b></p>
<p></p>
<p>Reasonable accommodations may be made to enable individuals with disabilities to perform the essential functions of this position)
</p>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Perform IBI (Internal Buyer Inspection) on daily basis.
</li>
<li>Inspect and/or test finished good products using standard SEHA processes and methods
</li>
<li>Communicate with related teams regarding the findings found during the inspection
</li>
<li>Have temporary/permanent corrective actions been sustained based on countermeasures for defect findings with OQC, Production, PE-PQC or related teams
</li>
<li>Register inspection results in the system
</li>
<li>Make a daily summary report of the findings
</li>
<li>Other responsibilities as assigned by management; job duties may change at any time with or without notice
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Skills and Qualifications
</b></p>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Skillful with measuring gauges and lab equipment
</li>
<li>Good verbal and written communication skills
</li>
<li>Must demonstrate strong problem-solving ability
</li>
<li>Capability to learn new specific software and system
</li>
<li>Team working environment with respect to each other
</li>
<li>Able to lift/move washers (appx 205 lbs.) using a given equipment
</li>
</ul>
