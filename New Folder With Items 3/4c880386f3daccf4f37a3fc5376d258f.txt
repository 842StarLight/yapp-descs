<h2>Demand Planner - Yankee Candle</h2>

<div>
<b>Position Title:</b> Demand Planner</div>
<div>
<b>Location:</b> South Deerfield, MA</div>
<div>
<b>Reports to:</b> Planning Manager #LI-BB1</div>
<div>
<b>Shift:</b> 1st Shift, Monday through Friday 8:30 AM - 5:00 PM</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div></div>
<div><b>Job Summary:</b></div>
<div></div>
<div>The Demand Planner will be responsible to plan and forecast future manufactured and purchased goods based on historical sales, current trends, and customer plans for our Yankee Candle Wholesale accounts or Retail Stores. Implements a collaborative forecasting approach to maximize sales while minimizing inventory exposure through appropriate sales to stock ratios. The successful candidate will have strong strategic and analytical skills with the ability to adapt and learn broad enterprise objectives.</div>
<div></div>
<div><b>Key Responsibilities:</b></div>
<div></div>
<div><ul>
<li><div>Uses current and historical sales information, market trends, and POS to develop SKU level demand plan maintained in weekly/monthly buckets, utilizing demand planning software.</div></li>
<li><div>Aligns demand plan with monthly and quarterly sales forecasts. Presents, solicits, and assesses feedback on the various sales plans. Leads discussions and challenges discrepancies to ensure sales forecasts and demand plans accurately reflect expectations.</div></li>
<li><div>Performs detailed analysis of new and seasonal products to provide initial and future forecasts based on trends and sales results. Works with internal &amp; external customers to understand trends, promotions, and product needs/requirements.</div></li>
<li><div>Presents recommended forecast revisions to Sr. Management, Sales, Marketing, Brand Management teams or external customers.
</div></li>
<li><div>Functions as primary expert for assigned products and customers. May represent division on cross-functional review teams as relates to capacity and long-range planning needs.</div></li>
<li><div>Responsible for ad-hoc analysis and project work related to demand planning and execution.</div></li>
<li><div>Participate in departmental strategic planning.</div></li>
<li><div>Perform post-mortem analyses for promotional events and strategies.</div></li>
</ul></div>
<br>
<div><b>
Minimum Qualifications:</b></div>
<div></div>
<ul>
<li><div>Bachelor's Degree in related discipline or equivalent experience; 1-3 years of forecasting and planning experience.</div></li>
<li><div>Experience with forecasting and demand planning tools.</div></li>
<li><div>Proficient with MS Office applications (Excel, Word, Access).</div></li>
<li><div>Detail oriented, organized, and efficient; strong communication skills with ability to influence.</div></li>
<li><div>Ability to work in an ever-changing environment with efficiency and accuracy.</div></li>
</ul>
