<h2>Associate Global Market Development Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<b>Job Description</b><br>
<p>In Product Marketing, we develop product messaging and positioning, go-to-market strategies, digital programs, campaigns, events and sales enablement. We measure our success on our ability to achieve high growth, acquire, retain and delight customers and gain thought leadership. Our Product Marketers are driving the go-to-market for their products with unique, differentiated messaging delivered across a range of marketing tactics. Each Product Marketer has the opportunity to grow their career by deepening domain expertise, working alongside R&amp;D, product management, and sales teams, presenting to executives and meeting with customers.</p>
<p>
In this role, you will be working closely with Product Management, R&amp;D, regional, and commercial sales teams on the business and Go-to-market for our Nucleic Acid Purification portfolio.<br>
<br>
<b>What will you do?</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Assist in the development and execution of strategic marketing plans for a portion of the Nucleic Acid Purification Sample Prep portfolio, including analysis of customer segments, competitor analysis, business trends, bold marketing tactics, and market opportunities</li>
<li>
Act as a brand ambassador, understanding, upholding, and communicating our multi-brand strategy in all aspects of our marketing programs</li>
<li>
Assist in and manage the creation and implementation of marketing content to support multiple channels, including social media, web, video, print, and more.</li>
<li>
Collaborate with others including Product Management, R&amp;D, Global Market Development, and Regional Commercial teams to develop salesforce training and customer education materials</li>
<li>
Work with global Channels to ensure consistency with brand profiles and company standards</li>
<li>
Work closely with Regional Marketing Development Managers to support the execution of marketing programs within their regions</li>
<li>
Support go-to-market strategy and execution of existing and new Automation product portfolio globally</li>
<li>
Develop marketing plans for how to develop and drive adoption for new ad solutions, supporting the development of the value proposition and messaging frameworks, and drive articulation of product portfolio positioning, aligned to Business Unit’s goals</li>
<li>
Create compelling sales and marketing collateral to drive adoption and understanding of new and existing product features</li>
<li>
Drive product adoption globally, and develop recommendations for new growth initiatives</li>
<li>
Work closely with Product and R&amp;D Managers to identify needs and sharpen product offerings based on feedback from customers, sales, and competitive offerings</li>
<li>
Build and continually assess competitive offerings to ensure product differentiation and competitiveness</li>
<li>
Identify internal and 3rd party research needs to support product positioning and go-to-market plans</li>
<li>
Guide the prioritization, development, and implementation of case studies, applications, workflows, and research to communicate the benefits of Nucleic Acid and Protein purification solutions in the market</li>
<li>
Create and curate content needed to help drive awareness, demand, and conversion of Sample Prep products.</li>
<li>
Work closely with Digital Marketing Manager to support programs on the web, digital, and social marketing channels.</li>
<li>
Assist with strategy and execution of digital marketing programs and processes across multiple areas of business</li>
<li>
Delivery of ongoing reporting and analysis of Sample Prep marketing programs and select business focus areas on a monthly basis to identify key trends and recommended actions.</li>
<li>
Analyze customer journey and identify opportunities to accelerate leads, consideration, and conversion in all channels</li>
<li>
Analyze key marketing programs for Return on Investment and program optimization</li>
<li>
Collaborate with centralized division resources for best in class digital learning and reapply into Sample Prep.</li>
<li>
Elevate the Sample Prep business and influencers (internal and external) and complementary workflows in global social media channels.</li>
<li>
May perform other related duties as required and/or assigned.</li>
<li>
Travel is required (Up to 10%).</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
How will you get there?</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Bachelor’s, Master’s, or Ph.D. degree in biology, business, marketing, or related degree</li>
<li>
2-3 years of experience in product marketing, product management, product development or other closely related position</li>
<li>
Global marketing experience managing multiple portfolios and products across various customer segments</li>
<li>
Strong bias for data-driven decision-making and experience in distilling complex, analytically dense products into client-facing solutions</li>
<li>
Demonstrated experience and ability in driving product positioning development</li>
<li>
Ability to create compelling and unique sales tools and marketing collateral and materials</li>
<li>
Knowledge of DNA, RNA, and Plasmid Isolation and how they align with various workflows (Gene Expression, RTqPCR, Protein Expression, etc.)</li>
<li>
Fluency in Mandarin a plus</li>
</ul>

Prior Lab or Bench experience in Nucleic Acid Isolation or Purification</li>
<li>
Experience with marketing and voice of customer VOC</li>
<li>
Digital advertising and advertising technology experience</li>
<li>
Experience working with a fast-paced and geographically diverse commercial sales organization</li>

Ability to work cross-functionally in a matrixed organization</li>

Entrepreneurial spirit/ability to try different things with minimal direction</li>

Analytical thinking abilities are shown to drive metrics and benchmarks to ensure continuous improvement in process and results</li>

Communication skills and clear customer focus</li>
<li>
Quick decision-making abilities demonstrating bias for action under tight timing and pressure</li>
</ul>
