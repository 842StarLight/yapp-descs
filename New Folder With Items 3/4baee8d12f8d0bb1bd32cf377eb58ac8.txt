<h1 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Theatre Administration Summer Internship</b></h1>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Summer 2019</b></h2>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>The deadline for this application is Friday, March 29th, 2019.</b></h3>
<br>
<p>Join a team of hardworking staff members who take pride in the artistic quality of their work. Virginia Repertory Theatre is seeking a summer theatre administration intern to join our staff. We are Central Virginia's largest nonprofit professional theatre company, with an annual operating budget of $5.6 million, four distinct venues, an educational touring arm, and an annual audience of 530,000. Virginia Rep creates professional productions of the great comedies, dramas and musicals - past, present and future. We also are dedicated to the development and production of new plays, and we seek to ensure these new works have lives beyond their productions at Virginia Rep.
</p>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities:</b></h3>
<ul>
<li>Assist Concessions/Gift Shop Manager with weekly inventory and supply ordering</li>
<li>Assist Manager of Administrative Affairs with weekly reporting and daily accounting</li>
<li>Assist Manager of Administrative Affairs with HR responsibilities</li>
<li>Train with Tessitura software</li>
<li>House manage for select Virginia Rep productions at both the Children's Theatre and the November Theatre (supervise volunteer ushers, maintain appropriate conditions in the theatre, and ensure that patrons have an enjoyable theatre experience).</li>
<li>Assist with financial reporting</li>
<li>Additional projects as assigned</li>
</ul>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader">
<b>Desired Qualifications</b><b>:</b>
</h3>
<ul>
<li>Prior customer service and/or sales experience required</li>
<li>Basic knowledge of accounting and finance</li>
<li>Demonstrated customer service experience</li>
<li>Discretion with confidential information</li>
<li>Fluency in all Microsoft Office products, especially Excel</li>
<li>Work comfortably and effectively in a diverse, collaborative environment</li>
<li>Strong interest in theatre and/or working for a nonprofit organization preferred</li>
</ul>
