<h2>Director, Enterprise Sales, ODX</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">At ODX, we're focused on helping banks reinvent the small business lending process. We provide a unique combination of market-leading software, analytic insights, and professional services to deliver a game changing digital experience. As a wholly-owned subsidiary of OnDeck (NYSE: ONDK), one of the largest online small business lenders, ODX draws on the heritage of $10 billion loaned over the last decade. We're improving the world's economic landscape by changing the way small businesses access capital. We care intensely about each other, our company and the customers we serve, and are committed to making every day count.
<br><br>
An ODX-powered bank experience allows customers to apply digitally, receive immediate decisions, and obtain funding as fast as same day. At the core of the ODX solution is a modular, scalable, and secure SaaS platform that enables banks to create a fully end-to-end digital experience. By utilizing the ODX platform, banks can focus on their desired business outcomes such as improved customer experiences, increased portfolio growth, and reduced processing costs.
<br><br>
ODX is looking for a Director of Enterprise Sales that can help drive ODX's enterprise sales strategy with banks, including identifying target clients and the appropriate contacts within those banks. The Director of Enterprise Sales will focus on engaging prospective bank clients through various mediums, including calls, in-person meetings, and conference attendance, while also determining the right contacts within potential partners to engage with (both business and technology leadership). A successful candidate will be able to prospect and source partnership opportunities and lead the effort to advance those prospects through the steps of the sales process to a signed term sheet.
<br><br>
<b>A successful candidate will be able to:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Lead communications with banks and generally manage the bank relationship until definitive documentation is executed (at which point relationship to be transitioned to the account management team), which shall include:</li>
<li>maintaining the opportunity pipeline</li>
<li>managing and completing requests for proposals, demonstrations and diligence processes, with the support of the pre-sales engineering team, and</li>
<li>maintaining a quota of net new sales</li>
<li>Create a repeatable, manageable process for new opportunities</li>
<li>Provide feedback to the product and technology teams based on sales process learnings</li>
<li>Represent ODX at industry conferences and speaking engagements</li>
</ul>
<b>Necessary qualifications for success:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor's Degree required and masters or business-degree preferred</li>
<li>10+ years of sales experience in technology, banking or both</li>
<li>Proven track record of achieving targets and driving the top line</li>
<li>Expertise in lending technology, fintech or relevant enterprise software</li>
<li>Background in small business financial services is not required but must demonstrate interest</li>
<li>Outstanding people-influencing skills and empathy for how to best partner with decision-makers at banks, which we think entails being a good listener and communicator both in-person and through formal presentations</li>
<li>Positive can-do attitude. Strong passion for SMB and enterprise technology and doing what it takes to help drive company initiatives forward</li>
<li>Professional Courage. Willing to take risks, aiming high to drive ODX forward. Has the bravery to talk straight and ask tough questions. Is open to and accepting of change, adapting to our evolving business. Proactively raises challenges and identifies solutions</li>
<li>Willingness to travel. Role requires travel for onsite meetings and conference attendance.</li>
</ul>
<b>About ODX:</b><br>
ODX's groundbreaking partnership efforts with banks began in 2015 and have subsequently proved that a bank and a financial technology (FinTech) company can work collaboratively to mutual benefit on a digital lending platform. ODX is now offering expanded platform solutions for small business lending to a broad range of banks and financial institutions globally.
<br><br>
<b>About OnDeck:</b><br>
As the largest online small business lender in the U.S. and a leading online lender in Canada serving more than 700 different industries, we have been trusted by approximately 100,000 small businesses by providing them with financing to help them build growing and thriving enterprises. Since 2007, we've issued over $10 billion in capital.

Join us as we enable small businesses to achieve their goals. At OnDeck, we're reinventing small business financing. We care intensely about each other, our company and the customers we serve, and are committed to making every day count. We are small enough to be nimble and strong enough to make a big impact.
