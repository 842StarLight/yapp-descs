<p><b>eCommerce Coordinator</b></p>

<p></p>
<p><b>J</b><b>OIN ONE OF THE NATION’S FASTEST GROWING COMPANIES.</b></p>
<p>Aeroflow Healthcare is opening a fulltime position for a well-rounded, self-starting eCommerce Coordinator. Work is available to start now!
</p>
<p>Aeroflow Healthcare is taking the home health products and equipment industry by storm. We’ve created a better way of doing business that prioritizes our customers, our community, and our coworkers. Now we’re looking for an enthusiastic eCommerce Coordinator.</p>
<p>We believe in career building. We promote from within and reward individuals who have invested their time and talent in Aeroflow. If you’re looking for a stable, ethical company in which to advance, you won’t find an organization better equipped to help you meet your professional goals than Aeroflow Healthcare.</p>
<p><b>JOB DESCRIPTION:
</b></p>
<p>This position will be assisting in all aspects of the Aeroflow Healthcare eCommerce strategy and online execution. The eCommerce Coordinator should have a proven track record of innovative eCommerce vision and accelerated sales growth strategies.</p>
<p>Under the guidance of the eCommerce Manager and in partnership with other Aeroflow Healthcare division leaders, this role will help shape, evolve, and grow our eCommerce online presence, laddering to Aeroflow Healthcare’s overall objectives and goals.</p>
<p><b>RESPONSIBILITIES &amp; DUTIES INCLUDE:
</b></p>
<p><b><i>eCommerce Strategy
</i></b></p>
<ul>
<li>Support and maintain the e-Commerce strategy and platforms for all Aeroflow Healthcare divisions as necessary.</li>
<li>Curate a comprehensive, sales-driving e-Commerce product assortment to meet customer needs and enhance the online buying experience.</li>
<li>Suggest and drive product cross-selling opportunities.</li>
<li>Identify product expansion categories based on trends and customer data.</li>
<li>Drive eCommerce email strategy and vision, working with the Email Marketing Coordinator to schedule and execute.</li>
<li>Create banners and graphics as necessary for marketing campaigns.</li>
<li>Work with the Purchasing Manager to monitor online inventory and make recommendations related to process, product assortment, and moving through aging inventory items.</li>
<li>Monitor and analyze eCommerce KPIs (sales, traffic, conversation rate, UPT, ATV, etc…) for monthly business scorecards.</li>
<li>Coordinate maintenance &amp; enhancement projects with the eCommerce Manager and Development Team.</li>
</ul>
<p><b><i>Product Management</i></b></p>
<ul>
<li>Design product pages that are informative and compelling.</li>
<li>Maintain product listings for all websites by adding new products and updating existing products.</li>
<li>Maintain long and short descriptions, photos, prices, categories, product attributes, etc.</li>
<li>Work directly with vendors and division leaders to obtain most recent price sheets and promotions.</li>
<li>Organize any marketing campaigns as necessary for promotional items.</li>
</ul>
<p><b><i>Customer Service</i></b></p>
<ul>
<li>Responsible for answering customer phone calls as necessary.</li>
<li>Responsible for managing orders over the phone as necessary.</li>
<li>Work with internal fulfillment systems and teams to verify order status, etc.</li>
<li>Manage and respond to online Social Media and Yotpo comments and reviews as necessary.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>QUALIFICATIONS &amp; SKILLS:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>2-3 years of eCommerce experience is preferred.</li>
<li>Background in Magento 1 &amp; 2 is preferred.</li>
<li>Proficient in Microsoft Office, especially Excel and Access</li>
<li>Proficient in Adobe CC – Photoshop, Dreamweaver, etc.</li>
<li>Must possess knowledge of Basic HTML, CSS.</li>
<li>Understanding of Google Analytics and Google Tag Manager.
</li>
<li>Knowledge of internet best practices and how they affect sales and SEO.</li>
<li>Excellent writing, editing, presentation and communication skills.</li>
<li>Comfortable working with team management software. Examples: Trello, Visual Studio, etc.</li>
</ul>
<p><b><i>Preferred Field of Study should be related to ANY of the following…</i></b></p>

<li>Marketing</li>
<li>Information Technology</li>
<li>Computer Science</li>
</ul>
