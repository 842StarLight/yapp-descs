<h2>Social Media Marketing Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div><div>
<div><div>
<p>As our <b>Social Media Marketing Specialist,</b> you will be responsible for running social media campaigns and social media advertising across multiple brands in the fitness software industry, similar to running campaigns at an agency, but with the benefits of working on an in-house team. You stay up-to-speed on social media trends and have an eye for innovating new ones! You are highly-organized, quick to adapt, a self-starter, and an excellent verbal and written communicator with a passion for doing what it takes to become an expert on fitness business management.</p>
<p><b>
In this role, you will...</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Prepares and develops social media content to support and enhance the organization's brand and operations.</li>
<li>
Monitor, post and analyze social media channels to build a community of followers and enhance interaction.</li>
<li>
Report on the effectiveness of social media campaigns and tweak your approach as necessary to achieve key metrics</li>
<li>
Be responsible for all social media advertising across multiple social media platforms to drive leads and promote our gated content.</li>
<li>
Create strategies, analyze performance, optimize to achieve goals and manage a large budget on multiple social media platforms.</li>
</ul>
</div></div>
<div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Qualifications</b></h2>
<div><ul>
<li>
1-3 years of related experience</li>
<li>
Experience testing social media strategies and audiences</li>
<li>
Data-driven &amp; detail oriented</li>
<li>
Ability to work across teams and multiple strategies</li>
<li>
Able to tie success back to ROI</li>
</ul></div>
