<h2>Sales Operations Support Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<div>
<p>Altice USA (NYSE: ATUS) is one of the largest broadband communications and video services providers in the United States, delivering broadband, pay television, telephony services, proprietary content and advertising services to approximately 4.9 million Residential and Business customers across 21 states through its Optimum and Suddenlink brands.</p>
<p>
From high-speed broadband and ultra-HD video, to digital advertising solutions, local news and voice offerings, we are making connections possible around the country - to businesses, to consumers, to communities, to schools, to hospitals, and everyone in between.
</p>
</div>
<p></p>
<div>
<p>This position will troubleshoot Operations Support requests from all Telemarketing Sales entities to ensure Sales representative productivity is maximized. This position administers all software applications used by the Consumer Sales organization. The Sales Operations Support Specialist will report, analyze and track all call traffic and applications issues to the Call Center Management group including but not limited to Inbound Residential Sales, Inbound Commercial Sales, Outbound Sales, Retention, Sales Support, Direct (Residential and Commercial) Sales Order Entry and Senior Management. Must be flexible regarding traveling based on business needs.</p>
<p>
Onboarding and Attrition of Internal and BPO Sales personnel. Manage access and compliancy for CDE and Non-CDE business required network Contact Center devices. Agent Call Router Administrator - Create new user id’s for all new hires within the Consumer and B2B Sales groups, drop calls stuck in queue, reset users when application malfunctions to ensure immediate usability for front line Sales representatives. Execute Call Center close procedures on holidays and in emergencies for Virtual Call Center. Record ad-hoc close announcements on the customer facing call flow. Administer the Telephony Data Warehouse reporting application - Add new users, create, update and modify reporting hierarchy as needed. Create and distribute business critical reports to internal customers, on an as needed basis. Monitor and ensure peak performance of all Telephony Circuits, Staffing and Call Center order processing applications. Administer Predictive Dialer – Create new users, System Maintenance, Loading and managing campaign lists monitoring error logs, and system security, routine and disaster recovery procedures. Administer Telephony Call Recording - Create new users, system maintenance, monitor error logs, system security, recover lost calls, troubleshoot customer log-in issues and call retrieval issues. Administer eWorkforce and Time Off Management tool – Create, modify and delete system users for Call Center staff. Update and maintain call flow applications. Create and modify RTA canvases, monitor RTA and report staffing variances to Call Center Troubleshoot system application issues. Perform UAT testing on all new PC Desktop tools introduced to Call Centers, including PC and Network Hardware, IDAAMDOCs, PC Operating Systems, CVT, Unified Call flow, etc. Perform quarterly audit on all Telemarketing Sales users of EWFM, ACD, Predictive Dialer, BOSS, CableData, ZOOM, TRiO, Remedy, Modem Control, KOMS, Novell, Restel OTS, CVT, Clear Tran, Dialer, Call Recording, to ensure license and SOX compliancy. Canvas Management – Monitor canvases for unusual activity in staff, trunks and call flow. Troubleshoot canvas issues. Resolve issues where possible escalate all others to CCIS. Participates in special projects and performs other duties as assigned. Mentoring new and existing team members and customers. Develop step by step troubleshooting and operational process documentation. Requisition of Call Center type equipment and other supplies necessary to support the business operation.</p>
