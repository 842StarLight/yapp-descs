<h2>Express Technician</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>As an Express Technician, you're one of the most important team members at <b>Norm Reeves Honda in Cerritos</b>. You're responsible for showing our guests a super experience and getting them on the road quickly. You'll provide light vehicle maintenance, keep a safe, neat workspace, and handle your responsibilities with honesty and integrity.</p>
<p>
Your work will ensure guests leave 100% satisfied with their Norm <b>Reeves Honda in Cerritos</b> experience. This role is a great entry point into your career with us!</p>
<p>
What You'll Do:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Deliver your guests' cars every time, on time, as promised</li>
<li>
Perform oil changes, check batteries, and top off fluids</li>
<li>
Inspect brake linings and adjust if necessary</li>
<li>
Rotate, repair, mount, and balance tires according to vehicle standards</li>
<li>
Conduct multi-point inspections (121-point inspection) and alignment checks</li>
<li>
Perform minor services including software updates, key programming, and windshield wiper and filter replacements</li>
<li>
Take detailed notes during and post-inspection</li>
<li>
Keep work station clean at all times</li>
<li>
Work with your team to prepare and service vehicles efficiently</li>
</ul>
<p>
What You'll Need:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Full time availability. You'll also need to be at least 18 years old</li>
<li>
A valid driver's license and acceptable driving record</li>
<li>
Experience at an Automotive School or Program</li>
<li>
Automotive 609 Certificate a plus</li>
<li>
Prior dealership experience a plus</li>
<li>
Positive team minded attitude</li>
</ul>
