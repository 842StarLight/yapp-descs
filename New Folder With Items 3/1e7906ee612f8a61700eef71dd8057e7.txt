<p><b>JOB SUMMARY
</b></p>
<p>Responsible for developing comprehensive client strategies that solve their key challenges by applying appropriate Harland Clarke solutions. Consultants will work closely with Harland Clarke Account Executives to understand clients' situations and needs. Consultant will lead the determination of comprehensive strategies for Harland Clarke to contribute concrete client benefits and results in long term revenue growth for Harland Clarke. In order to be successful, Consultants will bring industry and functional knowledge to bear as a basis for recommended solutions from within and beyond Harland Clarke. Consultants will be key contributors to client satisfaction based on objective partnership in solving client needs. Consultants will be seen internally as an expert who can create comprehensive and holistic business and marketing client strategies that lead to Harland Clarke revenue success. Further, Consultants will contribute to industry literacy across the company, so channel, product, sales, and technology functions are in tune with the evolving Financial Institution industry needs<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>KEY DUTIES/RESPONSIBILITIES
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Work with Account Executives in a client needs assessment process that will serve to align internal teams on the client's priority issues and current situation - 20%
</li>
<li>Craft recommendations drawing on the full suite of Harland Clarke solutions across both Payments &amp; Marketing Services. Consultants prepare recommendations for account executives to deliver to clients and are available for a limited amount of direct client consultation. Detailed support for individual components of a strategy is provided by sales engineers. 30%
</li>
<li>Review clients' business results internally and recommend iterations and opportunities for additional Harland Clarke services in collaboration with Account Executives &amp; client delivery specialists. Receive support from channel, product, and data analytics to craft the strategy but also to forecast and evaluate effectiveness. Use the output of internal reviews to assist in development of periodic client business reviews in conjunction with Account Executives 30%
</li>
<li>Consultants will be a prime source of financial services marketplace needs for the benefit of product and technology development planning and refinement of solutions. 10%
</li>
<li>Periodically lead review of results of all programs and incorporate those recommendations and updates into client programs through either Account Executives or client delivery specialists (depending on how we define roles). These program level insights will be the obligation of the data analytics leadership. 10%
<br>
</li>
</ul>
<p><b>EDUCATION AND COMPETENCIES
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor's Degree in business administration, marketing, advertising, economics, accounting or other relevant field of study (Required)
</li>
<li>Master's Degree or equivalent coursework in business strategy, marketing, advertising, economics, accounting or analytics or equivalent professional experience (Required)
</li>
<li>Ideal candidate will have an advanced knowledge of direct response marketing and/or experience with customer segmentation management. A solid understanding of profitability for financial institutions, including individual financial institution line of business P&amp;L knowledge, is preferred.
</li>
<li>Strong understanding of business and marketing strategy to align solutions with well-rounded internal processes to maximize positive outcomes.
</li>
<li>Candidate should be familiar with the financial industry’s regulatory environment in order to effectively consult with clients for business and marketing strategies.
<br>
</li>
</ul>
