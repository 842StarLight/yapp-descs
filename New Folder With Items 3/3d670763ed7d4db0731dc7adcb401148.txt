<div>Summary of Position
</div>
<div>The <b>Service Sales Account Manager </b>role is critical to the success of ECi
</div>
<div>by forging strong, deep, and lasting business partnerships with our customers. A successful Service Sales Account Manager has the skills and motivation to identify the key stakeholders, influencers, and decision makers within a customer organization, and create mutually beneficial relationships with them, while appropriately selling them the right services to further aid in their success. An ECi Service Sales Account Manager wants to interact with multiple levels and roles within an organization, including, and especially, the Business Owner. A Service Sales Account Manager will succeed only by engaging in business discussions with customers to understand their future business plans and the opportunities and challenges they face – and then discussing how ECi products and services will help them succeed.<br>
