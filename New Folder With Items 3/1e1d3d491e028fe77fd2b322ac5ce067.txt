<p><b>What you will be doing: </b></p>
<ul>

<li>Collaborate and strategize with other members of the Business Development team to strengthen your approach in targeting and discovering qualified leads.</li>
<li>Work side-by-side with Senior Sales Representatives as prospects progress from lead to newly signed customers. This high-level interaction will provide you with extremely valuable training and unique insight into the entire the sales cycle.</li>
<li>Practice Deacom’s Kaizen philosophy by striving to exceed expectations and continuously improve through a metrics-driven approach.</li>
<li>Apply your communication skills to adapt to the customer’s needs and help them solve their business challenges through a consultative sales approach.</li>
<li>Reach your sales quota by targeting and qualifying leads through perseverance and hard work.</li>
</ul>

<li>Experience speaking with senior level executives.</li>
<li>Rock solid written and verbal communication skills.</li>
<li>A knack for investigative research and critical thinking.</li>
<li>Strong analytical, problem-solving, and decision-making skills.</li>
<li>A degree in whatever. We’re Serious! Business, Graphic Design, or even Brewing is accepted. <i>(Actually, Brewing would be awesome!)</i>
