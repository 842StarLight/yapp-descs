<h2>Marketing Director</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div></div>
<div>
<div>The Food Hall Co. creates culinary and entertainment destinations which offer a platform for chefs, restauranteurs, mixologists, brewers and entertainers to bring their craft to an engaged local community, all under one roof. The Food Hall Company’s first property, Legacy Hall, opened its doors late 2017 in Plano, Texas at 7800 Windrose Ave.</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div>
</div>
<div>Legacy Hall is an innovative, European-style food hall complemented with the best elements of a beer garden, craft brewery and live entertainment venue. The three-story, 55,000+-square-foot space features more than 20 curated food stalls and 5 full-service bars offering cuisine and flavors from around the world. Unlawful Assembly Brewing Co., a craft brewery and taproom, occupies the third floor and focuses on brewing with unexpected, fresh ingredients. The Box Garden is the Hall’s expansive outdoor entertainment venue, where guests can enjoy live music, movies, sports- watching parties and much more.</div>

<div><b>JOB SUMMARY:</b></div>
<br>
<div><b>
</b></div>
<div>We are currently seeking a Marketing Director to join the Legacy Food Hall team. This person is a key leader reporting to the SVP Marketing for The Food Hall Company. The Marketing Director will develop the marketing strategy for Legacy Hall, Unlawful Assembly Brewing Company and the Box Garden, and in the process, create the marketing model for other food halls the company is developing. She/He will be responsible for building trusted brands, driving sustained weekly traffic and achieving revenue objectives for food, LBW and event traffic/ticket sales. She/He will manage a small team and agencies to execute marketing initiatives including branding, marketing communications, digital marketing, public relations, content &amp; social media marketing, market research, sponsorship marketing, live event promotions and the development and execution of a robust marketing calendar of events and music acts.</div>
<br>
<div>
</div>
<div></div>
<div><b>ESSENTIAL FUNCTIONS &amp; RESPONSIBILITIES:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Develop and own the marketing strategy for Legacy Food Hall to achieve brand awareness, industry leadership and revenue growth goals</li>
<li>Develop the annual marketing plan, events calendar and budget - all focused on increasing consumer discovery, engagement, trial, retention and frequency</li>
<li>Create initiatives to drive bookings of groups/private parties including sales tools and campaigns that drive inbound leads to the events sales team</li>
<li>Develop, mentor and manage a small team of marketing managers, agencies and contractors</li>
<li>Work with executive management and external agencies to refine the brand identity and creative assets.</li>
<li>Implement brand style guidelines and creative execution that ensures all marketing is on-brand, consistent in style, quality and tone, and optimized across all content channels.</li>
<li>Build brand awareness and affinity for Legacy Hall while assisting the SVP Marketing in developing the brand and digital identity for new markets</li>
<li>Execute unique consumer acquisition campaigns leveraging high impact mix of paid advertising – digital media, print, social, and radio</li>
<li>Own and execute a robust calendar of traffic-driving events/music/experiences inclusive of holidays, festivals, music acts, beer releases, food/beer collaborations, special contests, local community events, watch parties, etc.</li>
<li>Work closely with sponsors to create integrated marketing campaigns that improve the overall guest experience</li>
<li>Develop ongoing social media marketing and email marketing programs to drive high rates of consumer awareness, engagement and retention – pulling them back to the Hall over and over again for food, entertainment, events, music acts, beer releases, etc.</li>
<li>Drive sustained earned media coverage in key publications by developing distinct, breakthrough communications and actively managing PR agencies</li>
<li>Identify, plan and execute local marketing opportunities and grass roots tactics</li>
<li>Build, maintain and enhance brand websites and extended digital presence across online directories, social media and review sites</li>
<li>Build the brand’s online reputation by monitoring guest reviews and social sentiment, responding to comments and pulling in new positive reviews</li>
<li>Select and lead external agencies in the production of impactful creative/design, advertising, videos, signage and PR messaging</li>
<li>Design and produce impactful in-store marketing materials in collaboration with the GM, food stall owners, brewery and bar staff</li>
<li>Analyze consumer feedback/data to derive insights that translate into actionable improvements</li>
<li>Measure, test and optimize key programs that drive continuous improvement for campaigns and creative</li>
<li>Partner with FP&amp;A and Operations to develop a marketing dashboard of performance metrics and analyze results of programs in order to adjust tactics/budgets to achieve higher ROI on marketing spend</li>
<li>Lead marketing initiatives, in partnership with brewery team, for Unlawful Assembly Brewing Company with focus on driving brand awareness, distribution, sales and revenue growth<br>
