The Proposals and Contracts Associate is responsible for the development of Proposals and Contracts in consultation with the Business Development Team. Working with the client representative and functional group leads, the Proposals and Contracts Associate elucidates the scope of work and drives project budget development and finalization. Specifically, the Proposals and Contracts Associate works with applicable Veristat personnel to complete Sponsor Requests for Information (RFIs) and other Sponsor-required documentation that may precede a formal Request for Proposal (RFP); receives and disseminates Requests for Proposals (RFPs) to applicable Veristat personnel; and ultimately is responsible for the production of Proposals, including the document text and associated fees (i.e., bid grids) and payment schedules.</p>
<p>
For awarded projects, the Proposals and Contracts Associate is responsible for monitoring contract compliance on an ongoing basis in collaboration with a breadth of internal and external team members, subcontractors, and project management.</p>
<p>
The Proposals and Contracts Associate works closely with Business Development and Client Relations personnel to develop new and improve existing proposal development tools, including rates, algorithms, and templates, as well as tracking tools to ensure the timely, efficient, and accurate responses to RFIs and RFPs. The Proposals and Contracts Associate also performs appropriate client/indication/therapeutic area research to support these goals.</p>
