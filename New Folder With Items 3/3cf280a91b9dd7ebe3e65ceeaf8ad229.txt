<h2>Inside Sales Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>The Challenge:
</b></h3>
<p>One of Adobe's primary goals is to continue the growth of the company as a creative and significant provider of enterprise-class software solutions. Adobe Sign is looking for an Inside Sales Account Executive to impact our enterprise customer segment. We are seeking a motivated sales professional to join the team.
</p>
<p>This position will be based in San Jose or San Francisco.
</p>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"></h2>
<p><b>What You'll Do:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li><b>Ownership over the sales process and driving key engagements within the Adobe Sign Deals.
</b></li>
<li><b>To take ownership within driving a revenue number.
</b></li>
<li><b>Have the ability to partner with Enterprise Field Account Executive.
</b></li>
<li><b>You will take charge within filling the pipeline with new Adobe Sign deals
</b></li>
<li><b>You'll be taking ownership around deal development &amp; qualification
</b></li>
<li><b>Driving existing leads through cold-calling
</b></li>
<li><b>Engaging with mid and C-level decision-makers to showcase the value the Adobe Sign solution.
</b></li>
</ul>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>What you'll need to be successful:
</b></h3>
<ul>
<li>Minimum of 2 years inside or outside sales experience, preferably in enterprise software
</li>
<li>Preferred 1+ year in a sales closing role with successful history of relationship building, deal negotiations and territory planning
</li>
<li>Shown success in penetrating new markets and closing new business
</li>
<li>Consistent over-achievement of quota
</li>
<li>Prior experience with CRM solutions such as Salesforce.com
</li>
<li>Technically adept, ambitious, and motivated by results
</li>
<li>Ability to accurately forecast based upon realistic opportunity assessments
</li>
<li>Ensure 100% customer happiness with all respective customers
</li>
<li>Maintain professional internal and external relationships that meet our core values and align with our culture
</li>
<li>Strong written and verbal interpersonal skills
</li>
<li>BS/BA Degree required
</li>
</ul>
