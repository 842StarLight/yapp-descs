<h2>Customer Support Engineer (Upper Providence, PA)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>Duties and Responsibilities:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Install, maintain, verify performance and repair PerkinElmer and other multivendor equipment at customer sites ensuring best-in-class service within established time frame and assigned geographical area</li>
<li>
Drive toward zero service recalls for any reason</li>
<li>
Provide scheduled preventative maintenance and all warranty services which apply to the products covered under the agreement with the customer</li>
<li>
Complete assigned preventive maintenance calls at customer sites within prescribed time frame and established performance standards</li>
<li>
Demonstrate knowledge of and adherence to the Installation, warranty, and service agreement provisions to provide adequate billing information to the customer</li>
<li>
Drive the sale of new service agreements, service agreement upgrades and instrument add-ons to existing contracts</li>
<li>
Encourage customers to purchase training, support, software, laboratory services products and field installed computer hardware upgrades</li>
<li>
Assist and support local sales representative secure new instrument orders</li>
<li>
Contribute to account profitability through billable labor/travel orders, parts orders, support orders, billable install orders, and consumables</li>
<li>
Provide appropriate referral to other employees for answers to specific questions or to obtain product information</li>
<li>
Maintain up to date electronic and repair documentation for dispatch, inventory and service support functions of the job</li>
<li>
 Perform timely uploads and downloads of required data to ensure the integrity of the service system</li>
<li>
 Accountable for assigned company assets to include: Computers, tools, test equipment, telecommunication equipment, personal service parts inventory, etc.</li>
<li>
Manage inventory levels to ensure that adequate supply and appropriate records are maintained</li>
<li>
Take an active role in developing technical skills and soft skills to main</li>
<li>
Seek higher level certification through participation in company sponsored training &amp; development initiatives</li>
</ul>

Bachelor’s degree or equivalent in Biology, Chemistry, Electronics or related discipline</li>

<li>
Strong interpersonal skills; strong team player with customer satisfaction focus</li>
<li>
Knowledge of laboratory safety practices as defined by the Company and/or the customer’s site safety code</li>
