<p>
Bob Evans Farms operates in multiple convenience foods categories within grocery. It holds #1 market positions in three major categories. This winning brand has been experiencing strong growth, a trend it’s expecting to continue mostly through its leadership in the refrigerated dinner and breakfast categories. In addition to its flagship Bob Evans Farms brand, its portfolio proudly includes Owens, Simply Potatoes, Davidson’s and Abbottsford Farms.</p>
<p>
Bob Evans Farms products are distributed nationally in retailers throughout North America. This includes national grocery chains like Kroger, Safeway, Whole Foods Market, and SUPERVALU; mass merchants Target and Walmart; as well as club stores and via e-commerce.</p>
<p><br>
POSITION OVERVIEW<br>
The Brand Manager, will be a key leader in the management his/her assigned brands. Reporting to the Director, Brand Marketing this position is responsible for strategic growth plans that deliver on revenue and profitability goals. The person in this position will be responsible for the development of products, portfolio management and marketing program development that acquire new markets and consumers, ensure strong relationships with our key customers and achieve business revenue and growth goals. Through influence and effective partnering, he/she will align the organization to recommended strategies and annual plans while rallying the sales force for flawless execution.</p>
<p>
Incremental sales, strong program ROIs, increased household penetration, increased market share and increased buy rate are some of the critical measures that this role will be evaluated on. This position will require the ability to thrive in an entrepreneurial environment, multi-task, and keep various projects on track. Strong leadership and communication skills, both written and verbal, will be required to keep other functional areas apprised of and engaged in team initiatives.</p>
<div>
<br>
KEY ACCOUNTABILITIES<br>
<ul>
<li>Lead and manage plans that deliver net sales and profit growth for assigned categories.</li>
<li>Effectively integrate business plan with those of key cross-functional peer leaders to align activities across the organization.</li>
<li>Maintain an optimized portfolio evaluating product lines on a SKU basis and identifying opportunities to recommend actions that enhance volume and profitability. Demonstrate deep understanding of P&amp;L drivers, pricing and trade spend.</li>
<li>Lead the development of marketing plans that support key business and brand initiatives, new product launches and seasonal activations.</li>
<li>Ensure alignment of marketing strategy and execution across all 4 Ps of marketing (product, price, place, and promotion).</li>
<li>Maintain extensive knowledge of the business to make decisions based on the unique needs of our consumer and/or the category.</li>
<li>Must have a deep level of expertise and have achieved proven results across various disciplines including: consumer promotions, advertising, in-store volume drivers, and customer/shopper marketing.</li>
<li>Partner extensively with cross-functional partners to ensure alignment.</li>
<li>Be a strong partner and leader to our agency teams providing a clear vision and concise direction and feedback that drives strong strategic plans and flawless implementation. Their success is critical to your success.</li>
<li>Prepare periodic business reviews, market assessments and ROI/performance analysis to help develop selling stories, recommend actions to improve business performance and to track and measure results.</li>
<li>Partner with marketing teams at key retailers to improve the company’s overall retail relationships, exercise our role as category leader and ultimately to build a stronger business.</li>
<li>Must be great with numbers, able to evaluate program performance and use learnings to forecast future results.</li>
</ul>
QUALIFICATIONS<br>
The successful candidate will have a bachelor’s degree (MBA preferred) in marketing or a related field with a minimum of 5-7 years of experience in marketing. The candidate should have no less than 3 years of experience in a brand management or higher-level role. In order to be successful, substantial experience within the CPG industry is required. A thorough understanding of marketing and business functions within a CPG company is necessary and integrated business planning experience is a plus. It is also favorable that the successful candidate has proven success in multiple product categories and varied brand portfolios. In addition to the marketing knowledge, skills, and abilities listed above, the leadership and interpersonal qualities we seek in this role are as follows:<br>
<ul>
<li>Servant leader with ego in check. Does not mistake style for substance and knows when to lead and when to follow.</li>
<li>Effective team player focused on the good of the organization over self-promotion.</li>
<li>Active listener and skilled integrator.</li>
<li>Must always behave as a professional. Able to represent Bob Evans culture and values with every person they interact with regardless of level/title.</li>
<li>Achievement-driven with a focus on results and business performance, even when it means pushing back and challenging the status quo, albeit in a respectful manner.</li>
<li>Self-assured without being arrogant.</li>
<li>Someone who thrives in a high-energy, fast-paced, collaborative environment and who treats team members and agency partners with respect.</li>
<li>Creative strategic thinker and problem solver, accustomed to working in teams.</li>
<li>Must possess strong business judgment, leadership and integrity.</li>
<li>He/she should be a tenacious decision maker, able to bring a healthy, aggressive, yet responsible approach to business.</li>
<li>He/she should foster innovative thinking, drive critical decisions, hold business partners accountable, and be able to consistently deliver results.</li>
<li>Display an intense focus on achieving both short and long-term goals. He/she should be able to drive and execute an agenda to the benefit of the organization.</li>
<li>A self-starter who holds themselves and their team accountable for performance while still able to keep it fun!</li>
</ul>
