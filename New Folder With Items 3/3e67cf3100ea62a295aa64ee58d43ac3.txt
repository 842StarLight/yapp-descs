<b>
Overview</b>:<div>
</div>
The Customer Service Representative is a vital representative of the company and must represent Meritor in a positive manner to both internal and external customers alike. This role has primary responsibility for addressing customer sales and service requests, entering customer purchase orders and quotations for service parts. This role will also issue return authorizations and assist customers with technical concerns when customers are unsure of what is needed to purchase for service reasons or when they need assistance with product service manuals or for any other business reason. Maintain customer service orders and production unit orders with appropriate ship dates or re-schedule ship dates when required, utilizing the customer’s preferred method of acknowledging ship date changes<br>
Job Responsibilities:<br>
<br>
<ul>
<li>Act as the primary contact for external customers both foreign and domestic, primarily being responsible for entering, confirming and managing orders.
</li>
<li>Responsible for accurate order entry, obtaining appropriate order documentation, maintaining and analyzing open orders.
</li>
<li>Monitor delivery process and taking necessary action to avoid any late deliveries.
</li>
<li>Provide customers with accurate information regarding order status, product availability, pricing, technical assistance, and any other issues or concerns.
</li>
<li>Ensure that “drop-in” orders are managed in a manner that balances MSP’s and the customer’s needs.
</li>
<li>Responsible Spot Order Management consisting of reconciling accums &amp; RMS contracts and checking error reports.
</li>
<li>Effectively and efficiently address customer complaints and concerns. Report customer dissatisfaction to supervisor for follow up and improvement.
</li>
<li>Create new accounts within computer system for new customers and obtain proper tax documentation where applicable.
</li>
<li>Coordinate between the customer and the plant when the issuance of a Return Material Authorization (RMA) is needed.
</li>
<li>Manage customer accounts as assigned by the supervisor and complete assigned tasks accurately and within the designated time frames.
</li>
<li>Maintain strict confidentiality with customers and other outside entities regarding proprietary and privileged MSP, customer and supplier information.
</li>
<li>Communicates immediately to MSP outside sales and supervisors regarding potential service issues, customer disputes or significant opportunities.
</li>
<li>Maintain correspondence, sales orders, quotations and other files accurately.
</li>
<li>Responsible for processing customer RFQs / quotations for units and parts within company guidelines.
</li>
<li>Ability to work with customers in a cooperative manner to resolve issues.
</li>
<li>Ability to work under pressure and balance numerous customer priorities.
</li>
<li>Ability to work in a “team” environment.
</li>
<li>Must be able to understand and apply quality and continuous improvement principles</li>
</ul>
