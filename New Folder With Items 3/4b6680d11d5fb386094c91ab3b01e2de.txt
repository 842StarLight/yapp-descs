<h2>Technical Account Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">At Rubicon Project, the Senior Technical Account Manager (TAM), as a part of our Seller Accounts team, plays a crucial role in maximizing our integrations with our publisher customers. Our TAMs are experts in the Rubicon Project platform and the programmatic landscape and provide guidance in how our customers integrate with us. They are responsible for assisting with integration management and providing resources and insight to help customers achieve their revenue goals. If you have experience in the digital advertising field with strong analytical aptitude and excellent customer service and technical skills, we have a great opportunity for you.
<br><br>
<b>Duties and Responsibilities:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Provide technical support to Seller Accounts team and their customers during integrations</li>
<li>Analyze and optimize existing integrations</li>
<li>Define and improve integration and optimization processes, documentation, and best practices</li>
<li>Provide product feedback to product teams</li>
<li>As part of their duties, they regularly interact w/ customers within an assigned area to ensure products are functioning well and advise them of opportunities for additional services and product solutions.</li>
<li>Maintain close communication with larger customers, as well as facilitate communication with Solutions Engineering as necessary.</li>
<li>Identify potential areas of concern for customers before they arise</li>
<li>Make recommendations based on their recognition of a customer's current and future product solution needs during their continued business relationship.</li>
<li>Travel required to existing/potential customers based on support needs of the customer/account management team's direction.</li>
</ul>
<b>Qualifications:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor's degree required. Mathematics, statistics, economics, or finance background a plus.</li>
<li>4 years of relevant ad tech experience</li>
<li>Outstanding computer skills including spreadsheets (i.e. Excel) and presentation software (i.e., PowerPoint).</li>
<li>Experience with HTML, Javascript, Prebid and SQL.</li>
</ul>
<b>Skills/Personal Characteristics:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Self-starter, with demonstrated track-record of executing and delivering excellent results with speed.</li>
<li>Professional demeanor and excellent verbal and written communication skills.</li>
<li>Demonstrated analytical and problem solving abilities.</li>
<li>Innovative thinker that can adapt to a changing environment quickly and easily.</li>
<li>Ability to function independently, while also actively communicating with teammates.</li>
<li>Able to effectively juggle multiple tasks &amp; prioritize.</li>
<li>Strong organizational skills and attention to detail.</li>
</ul>
</div>
