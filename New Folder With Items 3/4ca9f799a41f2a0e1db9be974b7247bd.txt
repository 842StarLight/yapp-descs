<h2>Marketing and Public Relations Coordinator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>A Group of Privately owned Physical Therapy practice seeking an experienced, motivated, and dynamic team player for Marketing Director. Knowledge of social media management, relationship building, medical terminology, and some graphic design is required. Must have an outgoing personality mixed with professionalism and organization.</p>
<p>The Marketing Director will be an influential member of the team. The position is responsible for promoting our company and services to maintain an appropriate census. Job responsibility will include tasks such as visiting area referral sources, scheduling and planning company events, maintaining and developing social media platforms, ensuring the website is maintained appropriately, and taking ownership of marketing reports and record-keeping. We are seeking an individual who is personable and naturally friendly, bright and inquisitive and an excellent communicator. This position requires someone who is creative and can plan company events and market them.</p>
