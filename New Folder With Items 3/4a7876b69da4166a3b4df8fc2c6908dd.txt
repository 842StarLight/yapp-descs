<h2>Junior Account Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Do you possess an entrepreneurial spirit? Do you have a creative mind and a passion for delighting customers and consumers? If the answer is yes, then this is your opportunity to join a dynamic, experienced and motivated team of commercial executives as our newest <b>Junior Account Manager!</b> This newly created role will offer you the opportunity to make immediate contributions to our aggressive sales growth initiatives and develop a unique skillset by serving as a trusted advisor to our customers.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
As a Junior Account Manager you will spend your time...</b></p>
<p>
In this role you will drive sustainable profitable sales &amp; market share growth within our Consumer Fragrances division by identifying customer needs &amp; opportunities, establishing/building solid customer relationship, leading &amp; coordinating IFF activities and promoting/selling relevant IFF fragrance solutions for the client. Reporting to the Regional Account Manager, you will be accountable for achieving budgeted sales revenue and gross profit for one of the largest accounts in the scent division.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
About You...</b></p>
<p>
You have a passion for fragrances with a strong interest in product development, as well as a solid consumer and market understanding. If you have an excellent knowledge base of fragrances and olfactive skills, this opportunity may be perfect for you! You're a team planner with strong organizational and planning skills, financial concepts and processes are part of your expertise as well.</p>
<p><b>
About Us...</b></p>
<p>
IFF (International Flavors &amp; Fragrances) is a global leader in natural taste, scent and nutrition used in a wide variety of consumer products and packaged goods. Our international team uses artistry, science, and expertise to create unique and unexpected scents, tastes, experiences and nutrition for the products our world craves—from global icons, to boutique innovations. And, along the way, we’re redefining what a flavors and fragrances company can stand for by questioning everything, championing creators, and doing more good at every opportunity.</p>
<p>
Our 13,000 team members globally take advantage of leading consumer insights, research and development, creative expertise, and customer intimacy to develop differentiated products. We are fueled by our four corporate values of passion, creativity, expertise and empowerment. It is the unique blend of these values that make us IFF.</p>
