<p><b>Summary</b><b>
</b></p>
<p></p>
<p>WellDyneRx seeks a Proposal Specialist to help respond to the growing volume of formal and informal Requests for Quote / Information / Proposals. This person will manage all proposal production pieces, and help coordinate BAFO/ Finalist/ Follow-up pieces needed for proposals. Exceptional project management skills are required to help keep the growing number of response projects on-track and on-time. Successful candidates will have strong collaborative and interpersonal skills to help navigate cross-functional team members and subject matter experts. Successful candidates must have excellent oral and written communication skills and be well- organized with attention to detail. This role requires the ability to multi-task with varied timelines, and must be able to work independently. This role reports directly to the Director of Proposals.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Essential Duties &amp; Responsibilities</b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>This role directly supports the proposal team with the following duties:</p>
<ul><li> Manages timelines and ensures timely responses are received</li></ul>
<ul><li> Coordinates responses for BAFO/ Finalist/ Follow-up from the pricing team</li></ul>
<ul><li> Reviews materials received to ensure all items have been completed</li></ul>
<ul><li> Support writers by;</li></ul>
<p>o Handling last minute requests and urgent issues</p>
<p>o Input existing responses and assist with pre-population of proposal templates</p>
<ul><li> Hosts Quality Check meetings before proposal submission</li></ul>
<ul><li> Sends out Proposals on behalf of sales</li></ul>
<ul><li> Receives instruction from supervisor/senior writers to format necessary documents</li></ul>
<ul><li> Prints hard copy submissions in coordination with writers and prepare for shipping</li></ul>
<ul><li> Upon notification of new BAFO or Follow-up, work with pricing and sales to collect and zip necessary files for Sales to send</li></ul>
<ul><li> Assists writers in all areas of production and quality of appearance/ formatting</li></ul>
<ul><li> Assists writers as needed and know a little bit about the entire process and be available to help with ongoing asks</li></ul>
<ul><li> Assists with additional Marketing needs</li></ul>
