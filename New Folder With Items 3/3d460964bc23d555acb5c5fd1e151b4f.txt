<h2>Director of Strategic Planning, Operations, and Analytics - Media Partnerships</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div><div>Facebook's mission is to give people the power to build community and bring the world closer together. Through our family of apps and services, we're building a different kind of company that connects billions of people around the world, gives them ways to share what matters most to them, and helps bring people closer together. Whether we're creating new products or helping a small business expand its reach, people at Facebook are builders at heart. Our global teams are constantly iterating, solving problems, and working together to empower people around the world to build community and connect in meaningful ways. Together, we can help people build stronger communities — we're just getting started.
</div></div>
<div>The Media Partnerships team works with media companies and individuals to help them grow their businesses on Facebook, which is critical to helping people around the world connect with the high-quality professional media content they care about.
<br>
Facebook is looking for an experienced executive to drive sound business decisions and operational improvements for the Media Partnerships organization. This person will work in close partnership with the leaders of the Media Partnerships team and other cross-functional teams, such as Product Marketing, Finance, and HR, with the primary objective of enabling partnerships leadership to make effective, analytically driven, and strategic operating decisions on how to better work with media partners. The role requires leading thoughtful resourcing decisions, driving accountability and efficiency in operations, and building a deeper understanding of how to drive more value to partners. Examples of specific responsibilities include market segmentation for active management, building books of business, creating streamlined operating models between cross-functional teams, driving a rhythm of the business system of focus and accountability, understanding the true drivers of business performance, and driving efficiencies in go to market activities by streamlining processes and automating work through tool development.
<br>
The ideal candidate is passionate about Facebook and the media industry. They are also a deeply strategic thinker who can drive results, has sophisticated analytical and communication skills, and is able to move fast while keeping focused on multiple high-impact projects.
<br>
