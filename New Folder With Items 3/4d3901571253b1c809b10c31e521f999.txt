<h2>Coordinator Contracts</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<div>
<div><b>What will I be doing?</b></div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>As a <b>Contracts Coordinator, </b>you would be responsible for generating Real Estate Contract documents for various properties based on the basic supporting information provided by the Sales department. The ideal candidate would accurate, efficient, and detail oriented.</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
Job Responsibilities:</div>
<div><ul>
<li>
Prepares and generates contracts and related closing documents accurately and in a timely manner</li>
<li>
Verify data entry through our company software and program systems reviewing related closing documents</li>
<li>
Process payments for various transactions (ie. Japan Notary Fee, ADP, Maintenance Fee, Interest Due, Wire Transfers), while ensuring payment is made to the appropriate project</li>
<li>
Establishes and maintains the purchaser’s file</li>
<li>
Process and handles rescission/cancellation, as needed</li>
<li>
Obtains credit card authorizations and retrieves credit reports for all sales types, if applicable
</li>
<li>Generates end of day closing reports and financial closing sheets, as needed</li>
<li>
Actively works on developing their contracts knowledge for all contract-type transactions</li>
<li>
Ensures that all required documentation is scanned in a timely manner and meets necessary requirements</li>
<li>
Scans, splits and reviews completed files in preparation for offsite distribution</li>
<li>
Completes prescribed activities daily as noted on opening and closing checklists</li>
<li>
Be self-motivated and intuitive to the needs of the contract department and provide assistance to the team members, as needed</li>
<li>
Work closely and maintain a professional relationship with Quality Assurance department to obtain necessary documentation to complete contract files in a timely manner</li>
<li>
Reviews files after clients signs to assure accuracy with underwriting guidelines and title requirements</li>
<li>
Verifies all pertinent information on submitted worksheet is accurate for contracts processing</li>
<li>
Monitor and coordinate the timely closing of all business, prior to 60 days. Systematically monitor the suspense aging report from Cognos to ensure timely follow up and processing</li>
<li>
Responsible for completing the required site end of day process</li>
<li>
Carry out all other reasonable requests by management of which the associate is capable of performing.</li>
<li>
Runs supporting reports for sales contracts, if applicable</li>
</ul></div>
