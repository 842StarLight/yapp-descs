<h2>Telecom Tower Technician I (Sacramento)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div><div>
<div>Are you a highly detailed person who’s not concerned with heights and willing to perform work at altitudes of 300+ feet? Do you love working alongside highly skilled and well-trained tower climbers? Are you passionate about working for an organization that is safety conscious and offers industry leading benefits, paid training, guaranteed hours and career advancement opportunities? Ericsson is that place, and we have an exciting opportunity for you in a professional, dynamic, growing environment.</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
Our Exciting Opportunity:</div>
<div>
— Work on a team of tower climbers performing upgrades and repairs on cellular equipment mounted to various elevated structures such as guyed towers, lattice towers, water towers, monopoles and rooftops.</div>
<div>
— Use state of the art tools, equipment, and resources by joining the company that manufacturers the software and hardware you work on.</div>
<div>
— Cross-train and seek career path opportunities as a top hand, ground tech, integrator, and more.</div>
<div>
— Receive all your own new safety equipment and new gear to be safe and effective in your role.</div>
<div>
— Take part in a mentoring program designed to grow your skills and knowledge.</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
You Have:</div>
<div>
— Tower climbing experience while performing troubleshooting, cellular radio and antenna swaps, legacy cable upgrades from coaxial to fiber, site upgrades, reading customer blueprints, and capacity expansions.</div>
<div>
— The ability to work in a challenging and stressful environment with flexibility and composure.</div>
<div>
— A desire to learn and retain physical and mechanical installation specifications.</div>
<div>
— Excellent communication, organizational, and administrative skills while working as a great teammate.</div>
<div>
— A knowledge of industry test equipment, networking principals, ethernet standards, power systems, and cable testing.</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
You might also have:</div>
<div>
— Experience identifying potential add-on sales to increase company revenue.</div>
<div>
— Training to serve as a rescue climber if and when needed.</div>
<div>
— Proficiency in observing and reporting compound / equipment defects or hazards.</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
