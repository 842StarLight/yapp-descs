<p><b>
Your Career</b></p>
<p>
We are looking for an accomplished developer who has a track record of implementing best practices in marketing technology and the web and is constantly pushing the limits of systems. You will use your creativity to bring innovative ideas to our current tools with the goal of optimizing our marketing and sales processes. This role will require you to design, develop and integrate leading-edge enhancements of our tools while ensuring scalability and adoption.</p>
<p>
This senior technical role is for candidates who enjoy looking at existing processes and formulating new, innovative, system altering solutions. To start, your time will be focused on analyzing and innovating systems such as Drift conversational chat and the sales prospecting platform Outreach to ensure we are conceptualizing new designs and driving a leading-edge roadmap. The customer and end user’s point of view should be top of mind as you work with business to formulate new approaches. You will partner with stakeholders to understand key manual pain points, define business requirements; design, implement and operationalize solutions.</p>
<p>
Our fast moving culture demands that you can bridge between talking to business users and building a practice, as well as collaborating effectively across organizations like IT and Sales Ops. You are free to create, innovate and transform the business. This is an awesome opportunity for somebody who wants to take on new exciting challenges and a leadership role.</p>
