<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Position Summary:</b></h2>
<p>
Serve as the Kroger Company’s eyes, ears and strategic liaison to the produce growing community and key suppliers. Demonstrate the company’s core values of respect, honesty, integrity, diversity, inclusion and safety.</p>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Essential Job Functions:</b></h2>
<ul>
<li>
Interact with suppliers to review current shipment(s) of product to ensure the highest possible quality; modify PO’s as needed</li>
<li>
Identify and capture opportunity buys of produce to enhance enterprise selling; coordinate with Category Management/Commodity Buyers/Commodity Replenishment Buying teams for proper placement</li>
<li>
Facilitate the procurement of local produce for divisions within specific regions; collaborate with the category manager and commodity buyer to identify the impacts of crops, seasonal opportunities and product that will assist in store execution of merchandising plans and enhance sales</li>
<li>
Investigate innovative solutions for supply chain efficiencies while helping to enhance the overall freshness of the produce</li>
<li>
Research potential and prospective suppliers for review of new business opportunities</li>
<li>
Visit competition, in and out of market</li>
<li>
Visit divisions to review current quality and discover new opportunities</li>
<li>
Collaborate with the category manager and commodity buyer to establish long term business commitments e., supplier volume, promotional and advertising positioning and product pricing</li>
<li>
Represent Kroger by attending industry events, conventions, field days, expositions, university functions, and state or locally sponsored learning opportunities</li>
<li>
Report on the field inspections of product; provide detailed quality analysis, including photos, samples, etc., for review and to create shared learning opportunities</li>
<li>
Partner with the category manger in continuously improving and refining our produce specifications; serve as a liaison to suppliers to communicate long and short-term modifications to those specifications</li>
<li>
Coordinate and document all quality training materials; organize and schedule ongoing training with distribution center quality assurance teams – both on-site and in coordinated sessions</li>
<li>
Represent Kroger in a highly visible and positive manner to suppliers, industry associations and regulatory agencies</li>
<li>
Support commodity buying teams with any required procurement duties (e.g. iTrade)</li>
<li>
Travel 50% to buying regions</li>
<li>
Must be able to perform the essential functions of this position with or without reasonable accommodations</li>
</ul>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Minimum Position Qualifications/Education:</b></h2>
<ul>
<li>
3 years of produce experience gained from roles in produce retail, produce wholesale</li>
<li>
Demonstrated experience working with cross-functional teams</li>
<li>
Demonstrated ability to communicate effectively with customers and suppliers</li>
<li>
Self-starter and the ability to work with minimal supervision and convey a sense of urgency in own work</li>
<li>
Strong negotiation skills</li>
</ul>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Desired Previous Job Experience/Education:</b></h2>

Bachelor’s degree in Agricultural Business</li></ul>
