<b>Job Description Summary</b><br>
<b>YOU WILL INHERIT A BOOK OF BUSINESS AND HAVE ACCESS TO UNLIMITED REFERRALS TO GROW YOUR BOOK!</b>
</div>
<div>
<br>
Our Financial Advisors (FA's) work independently and with the branch team members to assist clients with their financial planning &amp; retirement planning strategies. The FA's assess the client's past, present and future financial/retirement needs, objectives and requirements. Meets the clients needs by proactively building relationships with them, providing ongoing advice and drawing on the range of available Retirement &amp; Investment products. The FA will utilize the exceptional marketing and financial planning software to conduct in-depth Retirement checkup analysis for clients. The FA will initiate and proactively cultivate relationships with bank partners including Branch Managers and others.<br>
<br>
<b>Essential Job Functions</b><br>
</div>
<ul>
<li>
Provides retirement strategies for client’s needs by collecting and analyzing client financial data to determine which products best meet the client’s retirement needs, and providing ongoing advice to the client as to what investment products are in the client’s best interest.<br>
</li>
<li>Must be able to develop / maintain cumulative book of business.<br>
</li>
<li>Maintains an in-depth knowledge of the Bank’s products and offerings, and promotes these products and offerings amongst clients as appropriate.<br>
</li>
<li>Provides training to and promotes products amongst bank branch personnel in conjunction with Regional Manager and Wealth Relationship Managers.<br>
</li>
<li>Establishes and maintains relationships with 3-5 bank branches to generate viable referrals for Retirement &amp; Investment products.<br>
</li>
<li>Provides outstanding customer service by following up on any client service request.<br>
</li>
<li>Fulfills all regulatory and firm compliance obligations as detailed in policies, procedures and regulations.<br>
</li>
<li>Creates financial plans for customers and deepens relationships with existing book of business clients.</li>
</ul>
<b>
Other Job Duties</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Collaborates with all bank and other business partners.<br>
</li>
<li>Responsible to grow fee based book of business to meet and exceed annual goal levels.<br>
</li>
<li>Performs other duties as assigned.</li>
</ul>
