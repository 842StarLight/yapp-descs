<p><b>
SUMMARY:</b></p>
<div>
<p>
Plan, conduct and manage engineering research, design and development projects involving either hardware or software solutions. Must be able to perform the essential duties and responsibilities with or without reasonable accommodation. The duties and responsibilities may change from time to time without notice and include but are not limited to the duties described below.</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Primary Job Duties:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
High level of oversight for personnel screening systems development projects. Responsible for project schedule and budget and drives cross functional technical team to achieve established delivery schedule.</li>
<li>
Lead through complete lifecycle phases including requirements, concept development, design reviews, V&amp;V and release to production using established formal development process.</li>
<li>
Responsible to manage and publish technical design specifications including analysis and decomposition of customer, product lines, sales and key suppliers requirements into subsystem specifications. Define test strategy and V&amp;V plan with traceability back to requirements to ensure compliance.</li>
</ul>
</div>
<p></p>
</div>

<div>
<p><b>Qualifications/Requirements:</b></p>
<p></p>
<p>The ideal candidate will have project management experience and have hands on prior design engineering experience with the design, development, and production of electronics equipment for use in medical devices, government security and/or military appliances. Having a current DOD security clearance is a plus, but not required.</p>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>BS/BA in Computer Engineering, Computer Science, Electrical Engineering or equivalent experience
</li>
<li>5+ years� project management experience
</li>
<li>Familiarity with Earned Value Management
</li>
<li>Strong planning skills including defining requirements, time estimating, communicating progress to stakeholders at each phase of project, documentation, and testing</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Required Qualifications:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Excellent oral and written communications skills combined with the ability to effectively communicate programmatic and technical issues
</li>
<li>Broad Engineering background sufficient to analyze design data related to electrical, software, physical science disciplines. Position requires working with subject matter experts to make design tradeoffs with respect to cost and performance.
</li>
<li>Experience with managing major suppliers and sub-contractors.
</li>
<li>Proven experience to estimate project budget and schedule required across engineering disciplines.
</li>
<li>Demonstrated past success as an individual contributor with current position in a leading role as a project engineer, a technical engineer, or a high level system engineer.
</li>
<li>Excellent verbal, written and interpersonal communication skills are required for effective interface with all internal and external contacts.
</li>
<li>Previous experience with the development airport checkpoint or check baggage solutions preferred.</li>
</ul>
