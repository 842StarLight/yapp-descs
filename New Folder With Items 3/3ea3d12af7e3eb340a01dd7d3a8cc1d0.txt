<p><b>Position Summary: </b></p>
<p>Munchkin is seeking a top tier candidate for a TemporaryCopywriter role. The Temporary Copywriter will be a lead contributor tothe continued development of the voice of the brand across all brand marketingmaterials ensuring consistency and telling great stories for the consumers toengage with our products. They will collaborate closely with designers, brandmanagers, the VP of Marketing, and other cross-functional teams. Projects consistof packaging copy, brand documents, go-to-market messaging, integratedcampaigns, e-commerce product copy and other copy needs across the varioustouch points of the business.</p>
<p>The ideal candidate will have in-depth knowledge of writingfor a consumer-based audience that is focused on parents and mothers. They willneed to bring a high level of organization, structure, strategy, story-telling,writing techniques and personality to the voice of the global Munchkin brand.</p>
<p><b>You’ll Do: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Manage other freelance copywriters and workflows between global teams.</li>
<li>Contribute to localization process of materials to ensure consistency acrossregions.</li>
<li>Write engaging and persuasive headlines, product descriptions and collateralfor all consumer communications.</li>
<li>Communicate complex instructional data (when needed) in a clear and compellingmanner to our target audiences.</li>
<li>Develop compelling copy that differentiates the consumer brand and stays trueto the brand voice and attributes - Modern, Clever and Innovative.</li>
<li>Ensure copy is right for our customers and helps to aggressively drive keyinitiatives.</li>
<li>Create concepts that are extendable into campaigns and synchronize extensionsacross other collateral.</li>
<li>Interprets and adapts all information provided for each initiative, includingcreative briefs and other supporting documents.</li>
<li>In-depth understanding of industry marketing strategy and business principle.</li>
<li>Other duties may be assigned.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Bring It!</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree required, preferably with English/Journalism focus.</li>
<li>Minimum 3-5 years of copywriting experience in both print and web, with emphasis in CPG branding.</li>
<li>Digital portfolio demonstrating strategic and creative strength.</li>
<li>English proficiency and excellent grammatical, written and editorial skills.</li>
<li>Demonstrated in-depth knowledge of CPG advertising techniques and marketing principles.</li>
<li>Ability to understand technical content and quickly learn new material.</li>
<li>Flexibility and resilience, including ability to adapt to changing/uncertain conditions.</li>
<li>Solid understanding of consumers’ interaction with various media and strength in adjusting language based on medium.</li>
</ul>
