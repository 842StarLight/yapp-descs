<h2>Membership Development Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>EDUCAUSE (http://www.educause.edu), a highly creative, entrepreneurial, national nonprofit association with the mission to advance higher education through the use of information technology, is currently seeking a <b>Membership Development Specialist </b>for our Membership team. This is a full-time, exempt position in our Louisville, CO office.</div>
<div></div>
<div><b>Position Summary:</b></div>
<div>In this role, you will be responsible for generating revenue for the association through proactively recruiting new members (institutional, corporate and emerging edtech), negotiating corporate engagements for their investment in a broad product and research portfolio, and supporting new strategic initiatives of the association.</div>
<div></div>
<div>Working in partnership with the Senior Manager of Member Development and the Director of Membership, the Membership Development Specialist will identify new opportunities for growth and initiate programs (recruiting, retention and corporate engagement) to build revenue, in addition to managing a broad suite of more mature opportunities.</div>
<div></div>
<div>You'll need a highly professional approach, a deep curiosity to understand all elements of the association’s broad value proposition, and a dedication to excellent performance in everyday work.</div>
<div></div>
<div><b>Duties and Responsibilities:</b></div>
<div></div>
<div>Recruit and retain new members to increase membership:</div>
<ul>
<li>Create and follow through on membership drives.</li>
<li>Monetize the association’s member-focused resources, including, membership, face-to-face and online conferences and events, exhibits, sponsorships, and research opportunities.</li>
<li>Work with current EDUCAUSE members, both institutional and corporate, to help them understand the EDUCAUSE value proposition and how organizations currently engage with EDUCAUSE.</li>
<li>Onboard new members: ensure logistics and benefits are delivered to client base by coordinating activities with the Membership Operations Team.</li>
<li>Assists the marketing process by offering ideas, suggestions and recommended products to promote, based on client input and available inventory of unsold items.</li>
<li>Follow up on inbound inquiries to the association. Respond in a timely manner to all sales inquiries, client follow up, maintaining client information in association database.</li>
<li>Generate proposals and develop working relationships with existing and new corporate customers.</li>
<li>Consistently provide quality customer service to members, non-members and others in the higher education community.</li>
</ul>
<div>Act as the liaison to members:</div>
<ul>
<li>Provide orientation for new companies, including proposal writing, and ensuring benefits of all sales are delivered.</li>
<li>Nurture and maintain relationships, providing feedback and input.</li>
<li>Acclimate new members to the best use of benefits and opportunities to engage with higher education professionals.</li>
</ul>
<div>Provide service and support:</div>
<ul>
<li>Represent EDUCAUSE in a professional manner at all times; engages with co-workers, volunteers, and members in a civil, caring, respectful way.</li>
<li>Support the professional development of others in their work to advance the mission and vision of EDUCAUSE.</li>
<li>Perform other duties as assigned in support of the association.</li>
<li>Support members in a manner that facilitates their growth, engagement, and enhances their EDUCAUSE experience.</li>
<li>As a frontline user of EDUCAUSE systems and data services, comply with data quality standards; adhere to data governance policies, procedures, and processes for ensuring data integrity. May act as the application or data collection lead for the department.</li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<div><b>Qualifications:
</b></div>
<div>Education and Experience</div>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree in communication or related field preferred in communications <b>OR</b> equivalent work experience</li>
<li>
<b>And </b>a minimum of three years’ customer service, sales or similar experience required</li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<div>Knowledge, Skills, Abilities</div>
<div></div>
<div>To be successful in this role, you will have:</div>
<ul>
<li>Strong verbal and written communication skills</li>
<li>Self-motivated with the ability to prioritize tasks</li>
<li>Detail orientation with good organizational, time and project management skills</li>
<li>Ability to work both independently and as a team member</li>
<li>Intermediate computer skills; proficiency in Microsoft Word, Excel, Outlook required</li>
<li>Requires effective and diplomatic interpersonal skills</li>
<li>Working knowledge of netFORUM or other CRM database preferred</li>
</ul>
<div>Job Conditions</div>
<ul>
<li>This position may experience high work demands under tight timelines</li>
<li>Travel may be required to support EDUCAUSE conferences/events, team operations, and external events relevant to the position.</li>
</ul>
