<p><b>Purchasing Manager</b></p>
<p>Dayka &amp; Hackett LLC is a leading grower/shipper/importer of fresh fruit to North American supermarkets and club stores. The company specializes in table grapes, tree fruit, mangoes, kiwi, apples, pears, and citrus. Products are sourced globally. Dayka &amp; Hackett LLC maintains offices in Camden, NJ, Wilmington, DE, Nogales, AZ, and its headquarters in Reedley, CA.</p>
<p>Dayka &amp; Hackett and its members include farmers, packers, shippers and marketers; we are truly a vertically integrated farming, shipping and sales organization. We source product globally from such fertile growing areas as California, Mexico, Chile, Brazil, Peru, Spain, and New Zealand. Dayka &amp; Hackett ships more than 14 million cartons of fresh fruit annually.</p>
<p>As a Purchasing Manager you will be responsible for sourcing packaging materials, interacting with sales and managing vendors.</p>
<p><b>Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Develop, lead and execute purchasing strategies</li>
<li>Maintain an accurate inventory that includes units and dollar values within ERP system</li>
<li>Demonstrate a thorough understanding of retailer specifications including UPC/PLU/PTI</li>
<li>requirements</li>
<li>Seek and partner with reliable vendors and supplies</li>
<li>Assess, manage and mitigate risk</li>
<li>Monitor and forecast upcoming levels of demand</li>
<li>Determine quantity and timing of deliveries</li>
<li>Perform cost and scenario analysis, and benchmarking</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Qualifications</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Proven working experience as purchasing manager</li>
<li>Familiarity with sourcing and vendor management</li>
<li>Knack for negotiation and networking</li>
<li>Working experience of vendor management software</li>
<li>Ability to gather and analyze data and to work with figures</li>
<li>Solid judgement along with decision making skills</li>
<li>Ability to work with personnel at remote facilities</li>
<li>Strong interpersonal skills required.</li>
<li>Proficient in Microsoft Excel</li>
<li>Thrives in a fast-paced environment</li>
</ul>
