<h2>Order Fulfillment Associate II</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>LP Building Solutions is a Fortune 1000 leading global manufacturer of engineered wood products located in the heart of downtown Nashville. At LP, creating new solutions for building is who we are. Since we pioneered our first engineered wood product, we’ve been continually creating better products to fit the changing needs of the building industry.
</p>
<p>LP’s performance-driven, positive culture creates an environment where talented and hardworking people thrive in a safe, ethical, fun, challenging and rewarding place to work. For more than 45 years, we’ve developed careers and provided advancement opportunities in the building products industry.
</p>
<div>
<div>
<div><b>Job Purpose
</b></div>
<div><p>To oversee and coordinate customer service for a mill and a customer distribution center (CDC).
</p></div>
</div>
<div>
<div><b>Key Responsibilities
</b></div>
<div><ul>
<li>Manage LP inventory at a CDC; including purchasing &amp; inventory rotation.
</li>
<li>Develop and maintain positive customer relationships; respond to and resolve customer concerns and complaints.
</li>
<li>Coordinate shipping and delivery of materials, including negotiating and quoting shipping rates.
</li>
<li>Oversee inventory, including forecasting, analyzing trends, receiving/shipping, coordinating movement between mills and distribution centers, reporting, and paperwork.
</li>
<li>Prepare, enter, update and change sales orders.
</li>
<li>Work with customers on orders, shipping, invoicing, and technical service. May include calling customers to solicit orders.
</li>
<li>Prepare and track invoices, purchase orders, rebates and other accounting/customer service forms.
</li>
<li>Prepare reports for business transactions.
</li>
<li>Perform all duties in accordance with environmental and safety rules and regulations.
</li>
<li>Perform other duties as necessary.
</li>
</ul></div>
</div>
<div>
<div><b>Qualifications
</b></div>
<div>
<p><b>Knowledge, Skills and Abilities:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Ability to accurately forecast inventory requirements according to historical demand and business forecasts.
</li>
<li>Knowledge of basic accounting and bookkeeping methods.
</li>
<li>Knowledge of effective phone sales techniques.
</li>
<li>Thorough knowledge of LP products.
</li>
<li>Thorough knowledge of inventory management.
</li>
<li>Knowledge of shipping and transportation methods and rates.
</li>
<li>Excellent phone and customer service skills.
</li>
<li>Strong written and oral communication skills.
</li>
<li>Strong analytical and mathematical skills.
</li>
<li>Ability to operate a computer and supporting software.
</li>
<li>Ability to work in a team environment.
</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Education:
</b></p>
<ul><li>Associates’ or Bachelors’ degree in Business Administration or related field preferred.
</li></ul>
<p><b>Experience:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>2 - 3 years’ demonstrated sales or customer service experience.
</li>
<li>Experience with forest products or manufacturing industry desirable.
</li>
<li>Or any equivalent combination of education and experience that demonstrates the ability to perform the key responsibilities of this position.
</li>
</ul>
