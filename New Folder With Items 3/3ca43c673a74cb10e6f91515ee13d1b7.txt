<h2>Channel Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<div><p>Job Description</p></div>
<br>
<div><div>
<p>
Role</p>
<p>
Manages and develops multiple strategic partners and alliance programs, in and out side NTT Family Companies in accordance with Secure-24’s key strategic focus areas.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
Responsibilities</p>
<p>
Works closely will all departments and levels of the organization from customers, sales, legal, accounting, marketing and operations to successfully manage and develop strategic partner programs</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
Educates for the purposes of sales enablement with key partners to drive sales pipeline between organizations.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
Facilitates and administers lead tracking and sales engagements using CRM and lead tracking tools.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
Develops relationships with key partner contacts at every level of both organizations: Executives, Sales, Marketing, Finance and Service Delivery.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
Support improving documentation around partnered solutions and joint product development efforts.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
Maintains a joint marketing plan with partners and reports partner status weekly/monthly/quarterly.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
Provides effective insights to generate additional revenue between partner organizations</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
Plan and carry out partner activities to agreed budgets, sales volumes, values, product mix and timescales.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
Respond to and follow up partner sales inquiries by email, telephone, and personal visits.</p>
<p>
Maintain and develop existing and new customers through planned individual account support, and liaison with internal staff.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
Monitor and report on activities and provide relevant management information.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
Attend training and develop relevant knowledge and skills.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
Develop ideas and marketing programs for direct Sales team activities to existing accounts, as well as new accounts.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Leadership and Team Focus</b></p>
<ul><li>Provides recommendations on changes and design enhancements to improve overall business model with key partners.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Takes responsibility for meeting commitments; desire to succeed and has a history of meeting solutions by deadline.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Builds respectful relationships with team members; consistently acknowledges and appreciates each member's contributions.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Effectively utilizes each team member to his/her fullest potential; motivates team to work together in the most efficient manner.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Provides quality customer service above else; ensures an understanding between customer and Secure-24 and helps provide workable solutions.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Keeps track of lessons learned, deal wins and losses and shares those lessons with team members.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Mitigates team conflict and communication problems; provides information that insures interest and understanding.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Promotes innovation, creativity, and effectiveness, always striving for continuous improvement.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Grows professionally and personally; eager to acquire new knowledge while promoting Secure-24’s values and culture.</li></ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Qualifications</b></p>
<p><b>
EDUCATION REQUIRED</b><b>:</b></p>
<p>
Bachelor’s Degree or equivalent in Business Administration, Computer Science, Computer Information Systems, Marketing or Finance. Will also accept equivalent of Bachelor Degree through any suitable combination of education, training, or experience. Masters of Business Administrator preferred.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
EXPERIENCE REQUIRED:</b></p>
<p>
Minimum of 3 years’ experience in channel development, partner management and/or IT operations/project management role(s)</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
SPECIAL REQUIREMENTS:</b></p>
<p>
Experience must include:</p>
<ul><li>Demonstrated moderate technical knowledge in enterprise managed services (SAP, Oracle, etc)</li></ul>
<ul><li>Success in various channel sales roles in the past</li></ul>
<ul><li>Strongly recommended from peers at all levels of organization</li></ul>
