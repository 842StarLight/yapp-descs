<h2>Consultant - HR/Payroll</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Construction Industry Solutions (COINS) has been providing business software and services to the construction, engineering, home building and service sectors for more than 30 years. Our global team has accumulated over 4,000 man-years of unrivaled, incremental industry knowledge that we put at the service of our clients.</p>
<p><b>
Job Role</b></p>
<p>
We are hiring for a Consultant who can guide construction industry clients in the set-up and use of COINS modules with new implementations. The Consultant role has primary responsibility for performing COINS product and business consulting as an individual contributor or as part of a team during software implementation projects or other assignments. Typical activities will include guiding customers in the set-up and use of COINS software, requirements gathering and solution design as well as customer staff training. The Consultant will use their subject matter expertise to support co-workers across the COINS organization, also helping to train less experienced consultants.</p>
<p><b>
Additional Job Duties:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Aid our sales organization in championing COINS-customized solutions to meet client business &amp; technology needs.<br>
</li>
<li>Provide hands-on technical consulting across a variety of COINS platforms.</li>
<li>Achieve and continually develop subject matter expertise in an assigned area of focus, attaining deep, specialized knowledge of COINS customer businesses as well as with the COINS products and solutions that support them.</li>
<li>Guide project team and customer representatives through product implementations and other assignments, providing solutions that successfully meet identified business requirements.</li>
<li>When required, travel to customer locations to perform on-site consulting activities as laid out in the agreed visit scope of work. Present content effectively, efficiently and professionally, diligently completing any post-assignment follow-up work.</li>
<li>Provide clear and concise written customer communication of all types including but not limited to product presentations, Site Visit Consulting Reports, Business Cases, 'How To' Documents along with general e-mail and instant messaging.</li>
<li>Ensure assigned tasks and activities are completed within agreed timescales and adhere to working practices. Escalate project issues and risks in a timely manner.</li>
<li>Aid in the onboarding and other training activities for new members of the team.</li>
<li>Participate in the continual process improvement of standards and good working practices across the Consultancy group.</li>
<li>Routinely participate in the COINS SDLC by assisting the QA Team with evaluating software changes against agreed test plans.</li>
<li>Collaborate and communicate effectively with peers and co-workers across all teams, departments and regions within the Coins Global Company.</li>
<li>Perform any other duties required by the Consultant Team Leader.</li>
</ul>
