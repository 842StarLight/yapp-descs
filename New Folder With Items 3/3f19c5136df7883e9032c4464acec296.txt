<h2>Document Control Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p><b>Company Description</b></p>
<p>
Hamilton Company is a global enterprise headquartered in Reno, Nevada with manufacturing facilities in Reno, Boston and Bonaduz, Switzerland. Branch sales offices are located in France, Germany, Canada, Latin America, Japan, Singapore and the United Kingdom.</p>
<p>
We are the worldwide leader in the design and manufacture of manual, semi-automated and robotic products for precision fluid measuring. For over 56 years, Hamilton has been satisfying customer needs by combining quality materials with skilled workmanship, ensuring the highest level of performance of every precision fluid measuring device we manufacture.</p>
<p>
The Document Control Specialist is responsible for filing, maintaining, and storing of Engineering Documentation.</p>
<p><b>
Essential Job Functions:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Responsible for printing and copying Engineering Documents per request and distributing of controlled copies of documents.</li>
<li>
Filing of engineering documentation, companywide documentation, and documents of external origin such as industry standards, contracts, patents, trademarks, etc. This includes file maintenance of active and historic files, and proper storing and labeling requirements.</li>
<li>
Responsible for logging in and out controlled documents that are not part of the electronic document management system.</li>
<li>
Responsible for part master, BOM master data input in the company MRP data base per the Engineer Change Order process.</li>
<li>
Responsible for the uncompromised exactness and correctness of MRP data, word-processing of standards and procedures, as provided for processing by the originating groups or people.</li>
<li>
Assist in training Document Control Clerks.</li>
<li>
Active involvement in creating and maintaining Document Control procedures and routines to maintain consistency, quality, training, and to simplify processes.</li>
<li>
Transferring manufacturing routings to the live MRP database.</li>
<li>
Logging Engineering experiments into experiment database.</li>
<li>
Responsible for duplication of software for internal use and sale to customers as requested.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Experience and Education:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
High School Diploma or equivalence.</li>
<li>
5 plus years of experience working in an equivalent office environment manufacturing document handling, document controlling, etc..</li>
<li>
Must be familiar with computers and basic office programs such as word processing and spreadsheets.</li>
<li>
Must be familiar with MRP system.</li>
<li>
Previous experience with alpha-numeric filing of documents</li>
<li>
Familiar with computer file duplication.</li>
<li>
Must be familiar with electronic document management software.</li>
<li>
Responsible for issuing lab notebooks to Engineers.</li>
<li>
Responsible for routing documents due for review to the appropriate reviewers.</li>
<li>
Must possess excellent verbal and written communication skills as required to interface with other department and company personnel in a service function.</li>
<li>
Demonstrated ability to comprehend office routines and procedures, including ability to independently handle routine tasks with minimum supervision.</li>
<li>
The position requires exerting up to 10 pounds of force occasionally and/or negligible amount of force to lift, carry, push, pull or otherwise move objects. Sedentary work involves sitting most of the time.</li>
<li>
All other duties as assigned.</li>
</ul>
