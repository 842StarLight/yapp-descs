<h2>Customer Engagement Specialist - Hybrid</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Join our rapidly growing company and team at Invisi-Tag. Be an important member of our fast pace multi-functional environment. Our team members are hybrid multi-talented, energic with the ability to work in a super-changed entrepreneurial environment.</p>
<p>We are seeking a Customer Engagement Specialist to join our team!</p>
<p>Bring your ideas and talents to an organization that will recognize and reward your efforts. You will be responsible for helping customers by providing product and service information and resolving technical issues. You would be responsible for engaging with customers via follow up calls, emails, marketing email blasts, social media etc.</p>
<p>As a fast-growing company, you will need to have the ability to change and prioritize your day to day tasks depending on the needs of the clients. You must be flexible and have the ability to work in a fluid environment. There will always be new things to learn and new processes to implement as a hybrid team member.</p>
<p>You will be a member of our core administrative team going forward.</p>
<p><b>Responsibilities: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Handle customer inquiries and complaints</li>
<li>Provide information about the products and services</li>
<li>Troubleshoot and resolve product issues and concerns</li>
<li>Document and update customer records based on interactions in CRM software</li>
<li>Develop and maintain a knowledge base of the evolving products and services</li>
<li>Follow up with existing customers via phone, email, social media and marketing emails</li>
<li>Process customer orders and enter into accounting system</li>
<li>Pick, pack and ship out orders to customers</li>
<li>Setup and train new customer on product utilization</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Qualifications: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Detailed Oriented and self-motivated to work in a fast paced environment</li>
<li>Previous experience in customer service, sales, or other related fields</li>
<li>Ability to build rapport with clients</li>
<li>Ability to prioritize and multitask</li>
<li>Positive and professional demeanor</li>
<li>Excellent written and verbal communication skills</li>
<li>Ability to change your day to day tasks based on the needs of the clients</li>
<li>College graduate preferred</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Required: </b></p>
<ul><li>Technological understanding of Windows OS, Mac OS, Microsoft Office, Android/iOS Mobile devices</li></ul>
