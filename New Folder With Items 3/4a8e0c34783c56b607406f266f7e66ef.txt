<h2>Customer Service Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Job Description:
</b></h2>
<p></p>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"></h3>
<p><b>Pentair currently has an opening for a Customer Service Manager to join our Flow Technologies business at our Delavan, WI facility. This person will manage day-to-day customer assistance functions for our Residential Products: order entry; customer resolutions; shipment accuracy resolution; and pricing discrepancy resolution. This Customer Service Manager will develop / coach associates to drive a cultural change that embraces Continuous Improvement as a means to improve Quality, Delivery, and Cost.
</b></p>
<p></p>
<p><b>We will:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li><b>Create career growth and rotational opportunities within various areas of the organization
</b></li>
<li><b>Pay competitively
</b></li>
<li><b>Provide on the job training and mentoring in Pentair’s proven best practices
</b></li>
<li><b>Offer health benefits &amp; coaching
</b></li>
<li><b>Support your long term retirement goals through a competitive 401K matching and stock purchase plan
</b></li>
<li><b>Offer employee discounts
</b></li>
<li><b>Protect you with life insurance and other options
</b></li>
<li><b>Tell you more about all the great benefits – just ask!
</b></li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>You will:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li><b>Maintain situational awareness of the activity in the Customer Service department, and prioritize / direct the activities of the staff to most efficiently meet departmental goals.
</b></li>
<li><b>Manage Past Due issues to ensure timely shipment of expedited shipments, as well as feedback to customers and Pentair’s Sales teams.
</b></li>
<li><b>Ensure that all customer correspondences are responded to within 48 hours of receipt in the department.
</b></li>
<li><b>Drive continuous improvement practice through the department; this includes Kaizen, Value Stream Mapping, 5S, and Lean workshops.
</b></li>
<li><b>Track statistical information, as needed, for the department; this involves compiling data into reports that are submitted to management in a timely manner.
</b></li>
<li><b>Communicate with Operations regarding product status (i.e. late orders, extended lead times, etc.).
</b></li>
<li><b>Participate in the strategic deployment of Pentair products, insuring departmental alignment with the business.
</b></li>
<li><b>Manage the departmental Customer Service budget.
</b></li>
<li><b>Meet with employees on a regular basis to review overall performance as well as setting expectations for improvement and personal growth, when applicable.
</b></li>
<li><b>Coach employees in correcting performance issues.
</b></li>
<li><b>Conduct communication meetings with employees on a regular basis to ensure that changes in policies, procedures, and standards are disseminated in a clear &amp; concise manner in order to be understood by each employee.
</b></li>
<li><b>Utilize communication meetings to have employees vocalize any questions and concerns about operational issues.
</b></li>
<li><b>Participate in Pre-SIOP and SIOP monthly meetings.
</b></li>
<li><b>Conduct Vendor Agreement reviews with sales and shipping; this involves implementing actions in SAP.
</b></li>
<li><b>Perform additional duties, as needed.
</b></li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>You should:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li><b>Have earned a Bachelor’s degree.
</b></li>
<li><b>Have at least 2 years of professional leadership experience over a Customer Service team.
</b></li>
<li><b>Have at least 5 years of professional experience working in Customer Service, Sales Support, Project Management, Engineering, Sales, and/or Marketing.
</b></li>
<li>
<b>Demonstrate excellent communication skills in English (written &amp; verbal </b><b>).
</b>
</li>
</ul>

</b></li>
<li><b>Proven skills with developing, compiling, and/or monitoring performance metrics; experience setting and overseeing Key Performance Indicator [KPI] metrics is a plus.
</b></li>
<li><b>Proven ability to think strategically and to translate strategies into actionable plans.
</b></li>
<li><b>Proven project management and organizational skills.
</b></li>

</b></li>
<li><b>Proven expertise with change management – demonstrated excellence with navigating internal &amp; external challenges to yield measurable operational improvements is a plus.
