<div><b>Responsibilities</b></div>
<div></div>
<ul>
<li>Establish, and build relationships with potential customers at multiple levels in the organization.</li>
<li>Develop and leverage relationships with all Microsoft, SHI (and other partner) representatives in your territory.</li>
<li>Manage incoming sales qualified leads from marketing team as well as field Account Executives.</li>
<li>Proactively contact prospects and be able to evangelize KiZAN solutions and services.</li>
<li>Record and track sales activities in KiZAN CRM on a daily basis.</li>
<li>Drive sales to new accounts via telephone, email and marketing campaigns to generate opportunities.</li>
<li>Complete weekly and monthly forecasting and pipeline reporting.</li>
<li>Work to get KiZAN on approved vendor lists for customers</li>
<li>Provide backup customer support for Account Executives when they are offsite or out of the office.</li>
<li>Transition prospects to an Account Executive by providing detailed and accurate information.</li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<div><b>Minimum Requirements</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Experience using a CRM (Microsoft CRM preferred)</li>
<li>College diploma/university degree or equivalent experience</li>
<li>High level of commitment to exceptional customer service and relationship build</li>
<li>Ability to travel, overnight up to 25%.</li>
<li>Ability to travel independently; if by car, a valid driver’s license or reliable method of transportation is required.</li>
<li>Authorization to work in the US without sponsorship.</li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<div><b>Skills and Abilities</b></div>
<div></div>
<ul>
<li>Strong written and verbal communication skills</li>
<li>Strong problem solving, organizational and interpersonal skills</li>
<li>Ability to work both individually and in a team environment</li>
<li>Self-motivated with the ability to work in a fast paced and constantly changing environment</li>
<li>Ability to work independently/self-reliant and show initiative with minimum supervision</li>
<li>Ability to quickly learn new technology and apply appropriately to business situations</li>
<li>Strong team player who enjoys working in a fast paced team atmosphere</li>
<li>Ability to balance team and individual responsibilities</li>
<li>Ability to work under pressure; meet strict deadlines</li>
<li>Ability to listen effectively and seek clarification</li>
<li>Excellent written and oral communication skills in English. Must be able to effectively and comfortably communicate with all types of individuals from the customer technical expert to the customer executive management</li>
</ul>
