<p><b>Click to Chat/Inside Sales Representative</b></p>
<p>Yale Appliance &amp; Lighting is a leading retailer in Massachusetts and has been named one of the best places to work by the Boston Business Journal and the Boston Globe, as well as being named Best of Boston over 12 times. We are currently seeking a motivated and organized Click to Chat/Inside Sales Representative. He/she will assist in the growth of click to chat, ecommerce, and overstock product sales.</p>
<p>Ideal candidate will have a minimum of 1-3 year’s customer service and inside sales experience, appliance industry preferred, but not necessary.</p>
<p><b>E*</b>xpected first year compensation including base and commission: $70,000-$100,000*</p>
<p>We offer an exciting work prospect in a growing &amp; forward-thinking company, with opportunity for advancement. Full benefits package offered including medical, dental, and disability, as well as profit sharing and a 401K matching program. This position can report to any of our 4 locations (Stoughton, Dorchester, Framingham or Hanover.)</p>
<p><b>Responsibilities: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Respond to inbound chat inquires and effectively convert chat inquiries into sales</li>
<li>Assist with Ecommerce sales, including customer follow up, order entry, and confirmation</li>
<li>Follow up on Hubspot leads via outbound calling</li>
<li>Responsible for managing and selling overstock inventory via online marketplaces. This includes creating online ads, product photos etc.</li>
</ul>

<li>Self-motivated and with ability to multi-task in a team atmosphere</li>
<li>Strong communication skills (verbal &amp; written)</li>

<li>Excellent organizational and follow-up skills</li>
<li>Experience with site merchandising/marketing</li>
<li>Familiar with CRM software</li>
<li>Able to perform 40 WPM at a minimum</li>
<li>Ability to work in a fast-paced and changing environment</li>
<li>Ideal candidate will have previous click to chat and/or email customer service experience</li>
<li>Bachelor’s Degree or related experience required</li>
