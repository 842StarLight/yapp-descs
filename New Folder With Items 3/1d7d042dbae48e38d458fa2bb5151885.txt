<h2>Digital Marketing Social Media Specialist</h2>

<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Overview
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div>Responsible to create and maintain a real time online presence for the organization to improve web traffic, sales and customer engagement. Must have strong knowledge of multiple social media platforms, digital marketing and engagement analytics including relationship development.
