<p><b>Job Description:
</b></p>
<p>The Professional, Strategic Initiatives (Program Management) will p artner with the business owners to lead strategic initiatives and drive achievement of business outcomes aligned to increasing top line revenue growth and optimization of organizational effectiveness for the largest provider of Real Estate and Tax Payment services in the industry. This role is aligned to the Strategic Programs function, focused on business process re-engineering/ transformation, go to market campaigns, expansion of services/ product portfolios, expansion into adjacent/ new markets and verticals, product lifecycle management, etc. working in close collaboration with a variety of stakeholders across Real Estate Tax and Payment Solutions BU and other supporting Corporate Functions (such as the Enterprise Data Group, Technology, Legal, HR, Marketing &amp; Communications, etc.). The ideal candidate is a self-starter that is adept at problem solving, logical structuring, financial analysis, industry/ market research, etc. and will be able to analyze complex data and deliver recommendations and insights to senior leadership at CoreLogic.
</p>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Coordinate with management and project teams to analyze, evaluate and formulate recommendations/ next steps on proposed projects/ initiatives; Establish a business case/ financials associated with the initiative; Document the project charter and drive consensus amongst the management and project teams
</li>
<li>Support market research and benchmarking efforts to quantify and define the value proposition (including ROI) associated with expansion initiatives (new products, new market/ verticals penetration, go-to- market strategy, M&amp;A, etc.).
</li>
<li><p>Drives a structured approach and uses data driven analysis to quantify problems and identifies solutions across a variety of functional areas. Proficient in understanding and driving influence within matrix organizations with a focus on execution of outlined objectives.
</p></li>
<li>Develop project plans specifying goals, strategy, staffing, scheduling, identification of risks, contingency plans, budgets and allocation of available resources. Defines the role and responsibilities of the project team members.
</li>
<li>Reviews project materials with management/ stakeholders to drive consensus on goals, timelines, budgets, identifying and allotting resources to the projects. Detects and resolves risks and issues through the duration of the project.
</li>
<li><p>Facilitates implementation, status and milestone meetings with cross functional groups. Generates and distributes status reports catering to a wide variety of audiences (operational, management, executive and board levels). Captures key decisions, meeting notes/ minutes, and tracks action items.
</p></li>
<li><p>Uses metrics and data to determine risk at the beginning of each project and assesses risk throughout to detect situations that may cause risk to the project and its completion
</p></li>
</ul>
