<h2>Advertising Sales Representative</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>We are seeking an outside sales representative to market newspaper print and digital advertising within the dairy industry. The territory lies mostly in eastern Wisconsin. This position is home-based and we have flexibility on where the representative can live. Candidate will travel most days on a regular route to contact accounts within your territory. Knowledge of the agricultural industry, especially dairy, is preferred.</p>
<p>Candidate must be disciplined and motivated to work daily without direct supervision, possess strong listening skills, and knowledge of basic computer skills. Due to the fast-paced environment candidate must possess a creative mind, have the ability to react quickly, and handle multi-tasking on a daily basis.</p>
<p>If you are looking to begin a new career, advance upward from a current selling position, or want an opportunity to make your mark in a sales career we would love to talk to you.</p>
<p>This is a long-term position and most employees have been with us for many years.</p>
<p>We provide a company car, cell phone, profit-sharing, health insurance and paid vacation.</p>
<p>If you have long term career goals, want to work in the dairy industry, and have a strong sense of self-confidence we want you to contact us and see what we are all about.</p>
