<h2>Business Intelligence & Analytics Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Key Objective(s):</b></p>
<p>
The main purpose of the Business Intelligence &amp; Analytics Manager is:
</p>
<ul><li>To work closely with our business partners as well as IT team members to design and build out our Business Intelligence, Data Warehouse and Data Integration tool sets at American Dream. Assist for data warehouse and data integration project initiatives and daily operations. Design and maintain company-wide analytics and business intelligence reporting solutions. Keen technical skills, business acumen and creativity; enabling the build of tools to automate reporting and empower with rapid insights into the business.</li></ul>
<p><b>
Primary Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Support and maintain the requirements for the technical and data infrastructure to support the relevant data and analytics processes.</li>
<li>
Work with IT to define SLA for performance, data freshness, data availability and infrastructure requirements for BI solutions to ensure appropriate design and quality of the BI solution.</li>
<li>
Act as a translator between business and technical teams, by consulting on the data available or feasibility of acquiring data, keeping cost versus benefit in mind</li>
<li>
Develop proof of concept solutions to test and ascertain requirements for BI needs.</li>
<li>
Serve as lead data architect on multiple concurrent projects and collaborate with IT Developers and Project Managers to convert functional requirements into technical specifications.
</li>
<li>Develop business relationships with cross-functional teams and assist with BI project scoping and requirements gathering, understanding complex business questions and needs. Make valuable contributions by providing adhoc statistical analysis</li>
<li>
Develop executive dashboard and implement new data and analytics processes &amp; strategy, end-user self-services reporting.</li>
<li>
Constantly monitor data science trends, emerging tools and technologies to continually improving frameworks, processes and best practices for predictive models</li>
<li>
Use data visualization, reports and data-driven presentations to present digestible conclusions and recommendations to top decision makers, so that business strategies can be designed around them</li>
<li>
Frame and investigate theories and assumptions with high-impact potential and utilize descriptive, predictive and prescriptive analytics techniques to test your theories</li>
<li>
Bring analytical expertise into cross-functional projects and work collaboratively with team leaders and subject matter experts across multiple departments, to enable data-driven decision making</li>
<li>
Other responsibilities as required.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Desired Qualifications:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Post-secondary education with a bachelor’s degree in a Marketing-related program, Commerce or Applied Communication.
</li>
<li>Two (2) – Four (4) years professional experience working in design and development of web- based initiatives and e-marketing.</li>
<li>
A strong working knowledge of Social Media websites such as Facebook, Twitter, Instagram, Snapchat, Periscope and LinkedIn as well as experience developing progressive e-Marketing strategies.
</li>
<li>Working knowledge of Content Management System (CMS).</li>
<li>
Highly analytical mind with a strong business understanding. A self-starter capable to taking initiative and working with minimal direction</li>
<li>
Technical Skills: Business intelligence analysts use complex computer programs to mine data sources and look for trends. Good understanding of SQL, BI software (Microsoft BI, Microstrategy, Tableau) as well as statistical packages (R, SAS, Python). Familiarity with data infrastructure and instrumentation
</li>
<li>Analytical Skills: A major part of the job is to determine what data trends mean. Being able to analyze the data is crucial. Experience in the field of digital analytics, preferably with a subscription-based business model. Experience with web analytics principals and tools preferred
</li>
<li>Communication Skills: Excellent communication skills (verbal and written), including the ability to effectively communicate with both technical and non-technical teams Problem-Solving: Business intelligence analysts need to look at the data trends and recommend solutions for creating more revenue and reducing loss.</li>
<li>
Time Management: Business intelligence analysts work on large projects and many data streams and many deadlines. Being able to juggle it all is crucial to the job.</li>
<li>
Bachelor’s degree in Business, Economics, Computer Science, or a related field 5 years of relevant professional experience in an ecommerce, business intelligence or consulting environment.</li>
</ul>
