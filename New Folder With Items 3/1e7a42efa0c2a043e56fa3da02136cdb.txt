<h2>Cloud Native Staff System Engineer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Business Summary:</b></p>
<p>
VMware is the leader in virtualization and cloud infrastructure solutions that enable our more than 350,000 customers to thrive in the Cloud Era/ A pioneer in the use of virtualization and automation technologies, VMware simplifies IT complexity across the entire data center to the virtual workplace, empowering customers with solutions in the software-defined data center to hybrid cloud computing and the mobile workspace.</p>
<p></p>
<p>
The VMware Cloud Native Applications Business Unit is seeking pre-sales Staff Solution Engineers to help lead the company in this ever-evolving space.</p>
<p></p>
<p>
According to the CNCF, the hardest cloud native challenges are around integrating infrastructure technologies, and that’s exactly what VMware is solving with the software you will be representing - PKS.</p>
<p>
This is a rare opportunity to join one of the most dynamic pre-sales organizations anywhere today. VMware's growth over the past years is unparalleled in enterprise software.</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Job Role and Responsibilities:</b></p>
<p>
We are looking for individuals that have a strong track record of working with new technology throughout their careers and are ready for their next challenge.</p>
<p></p>
<p>
As a member of this team you will need a unique technical skill set. In addition to understanding infrastructure operations (compute, network, storage, and security) you should understand how cloud native technologies map onto VMware and public cloud infrastructures. You will leverage your Platform Operations skills in this role educating the VMware install base on technologies such as containers (Docker) and Kubernetes.</p>
<p></p>
<p>
Come join the growing team of dedicated Systems Engineers in VMware’s Cloud Native Applications business and help our customers elevate their skill sets to support this massive evolution in IT.</p>
<p></p>

Required Skills:</b></p>
