<h2>Assistant Community Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Do you aim high? Are you genuinely helpful? Are you looking for a place where you can make a difference, receive ongoing support and training, and build a rewarding, long-lasting career? Then you may be a good fit for FirstService Residential, North America's foremost property management firm. We're all about our associates, and as we continue to grow, we're looking for even more quality people who share our dedication to doing what's right, improving residents' quality of life, building great relationships and truly making a difference for their clients, their colleagues and themselves. Is that you? If so, we think you should get to know us.</p>
<p><b>
Jo</b><b>b Responsibilities</b></p>
<p>
Provide support for General Manager in the daily operation of Association business, including but not limited to processing and responding to resident emails &amp; phone calls, member requests, assisting board and committee members in the performance of their duties, attending board and committee meetings, take minutes of meetings, mastering FSRConnect database features, keeping corporate files, and planning, coordinating and facilitating community events and activities (if applicable).</p>

Essential Duties &amp; Responsibilities</b></p>

<div><ul>
<li>Return phone calls and emails with 24 hours addressing any concerns or questions regarding compliance letter(s) received.</li>
<li>Regularly re-inspect properties and close-out the noncompliance file if property issue has been corrected.</li>
<li>Provide daily/weekly reports of areas/properties toured as requested.</li>
<li>Inspect property prior to sale/closing to ensure property is in compliance with Community standards prior to sale to new owners.</li>
<li>Stay current with violations system processes and procedures using FSRConnect</li>
<li>Respond in a professional and timely manner to all email, voicemail and faxed messages/requests frequently during the day</li>
<li>Assist in preparation of management reports for delivery to board of directors per departmental procedure</li>
<li>Work with General Manager to report service requests (as applicable) to the appropriate service provider as approved by the Association, notifying community manager as they occur</li>
<li>Initiate procedure for architectural submittals from homeowners and update/maintain architectural logs in FSRConnect and pertinent information as per community policy</li>
<li>Follow established procedures for appropriate preparations for annual meetings</li>
<li>Type correspondence for General Manager</li>
<li>Type and input all correspondences and notices in FSRConnect</li>
<li>Maintain updated board member lists on server database and in FSRConnect</li>
<li>Maintain emergency contact information in FSRConnect and server database</li>
<li>Ensure that copies of all correspondence, management reports, meeting minutes, newsletters and other pertinent documentation are distributed according to community procedure</li>
<li>Ensure prompt scanning and uploading of Association meeting minutes to the server and FSRConnect; ensure the distribution of recorded minutes to parties as specified by community procedure</li>
<li>File and scan correspondence, minutes, management reports, annual meeting paperwork, etc.; ensure timely filing of written correspondence to maintain desk and work area in an organized, professional manner</li>
<li>Distribute equipment as required by community policy and procedure, and document any special instructions for these items in FSRConnect</li>
<li>Operate automated mailing equipment, fax machines, copiers and other standard office equipment</li>
<li>Add appropriate information to FSRConnect for annual meetings, returned mail, home ownerphone calls, community alerts, etc.</li>
<li>Provide telephone coverage for on-site office</li>
<li>Attend all meetings as required by FirstService for training and education of company directives</li>
<li>Report, as necessary, all complaints to General Manager</li>
<li>Assist all other departments with office support as necessary</li>
<li>Demonstrate a team environment and build company spirit with FirstService Residential and community staff</li>
<li>Perform all special projects given by General Manager</li>
<li>Communicate with General Manager all important matters</li>
<li>Facilitate and maintain all on-site storage and inventory</li>
<li>Coordinate the development and distribution of the Association newsletter and other lifestyle public relations and marketing publications as assigned</li>
<li>Perform all other duties as assigned</li>
