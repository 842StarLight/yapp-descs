<h2>DIgital Optimization Specalist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Bonneville International has been a leader in broadcasting for over 50 years, with a mission to build up, connect, inform and celebrate communities and families across our markets. We currently own and operate 22 radio stations in Seattle, Phoenix, Denver, Sacramento, San Francisco and Salt Lake City, along with the NBC Affiliate TV station, KSL TV 5, in Salt Lake. We're proud of our history, and we want talented people to join us as we continue to grow!</p>
<p><b>
POSITION OBJECTIVE:
</b></p>
<p>The Digital Optimization Specialist is responsible for providing the support required to process digital campaigns from pre-sale to, campaign launch and campaign reporting. The Digital Optimization Specialist will play an active role in designing and implementing solutions to support growth of digital revenue. The Digital Optimization Specialist will interact with Promotions, Advertising Operations, and/or Sales teams to ensure seamless digital campaign execution, providing a high-level of client service support. This person will be a key contributor to the digital department by supporting digital sales efforts centrally and/or locally with the digital ad operations and execution team.</p>
<p><b>
POSITION RESPONSIBILITIES:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Assist with entering aspects of digital campaigns into multiple digital advertising sales systems</li>
<li>
Assist with creating holistic advertising campaigns with the ability to manipulate and calculate pricing structures utilizing metrics such as CPA cost per acquisition, CPM cost per thousand, CPC cost per click</li>
<li>
Work with agency contacts and/or sales teams to research, write and create sales presentations</li>
<li>
Ensure that all elements of digital advertising campaigns are completed in an accurate and timely manner</li>
<li>
Enter and maintain customer data in an order management system OMS or customer relationship management system CRM</li>
<li>
Advise/consult with internal departments regarding ad inventory, forecasting, and sell through rates</li>
<li>
Ensure complete and accurate revenue recognition in a variety of billing systems</li>
<li>
Prepare digital reports and information to support customer meetings</li>
<li>
Compare digital reporting with third parties to confirm campaign reporting is tracking and campaigns are running as contracted</li>
<li>
Generate monthly client campaign reports and interpret the results, providing key metrics and campaign recommendations to assist the sales team with client retention</li>
<li>
Work closely with Operations and/or Account Executives to capture customer needs analysis CNA for execution of digital campaigns</li>
<li>
Leverage digital tools to support digital campaign pricing and planning</li>
<li>
Support and adhere to digital revenue operational processes, policies, procedures and standards of performance</li>
<li>
Liaise with sales, digital production, marketing and programming regarding planning and prioritization to assist in execution of the campaigns</li>
<li>
Work collaboratively with Operations and/or the sales team to recommend campaign modifications for optimal results</li>
<li>
Cultivate positive professional relationships with clients, vendors and internal teams</li>
<li>
Collaborate with billing team to resolve monthly invoicing queries</li>
<li>
Maintain master local inventory avails and digital campaign calendars</li>
<li>
Attend sales training and planning meetings<p><b>
PREFFERED QUALIFICATIONS:</b></p>
</li>
</ul>
<p>
Familiarity with Digital Media and Broadcast advertising</p>
<p>
General knowledge of third party ad serving solutions as well as rich media technologies, including impression and click through tracking pixels</p>
<p>
Experience with web analytics, e.g., Omniture, Quantcast, and Google Analytics</p>
<p>
Experience with Wide Orbit traffic, DoubleClick, and FatTail order management systems</p>
