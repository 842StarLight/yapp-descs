<h2>Credit Supervisor</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p>Hoffmaster Group Inc. is seeking a Credit Supervisor for their Corporate office in Oshkosh, WI. The Credit Supervisor is responsible for monitoring daily activities and mentoring credit analysts. This position has an escalated level of approval and write-off limits. Research and evaluate credit risk, perform financial statement analysis, assess credit history performance and extend credit limits to customers. Responsible for the collection activities for invoices and deduction resolution on short pays. Position reports to Credit Manager and will interact with other Credit Analysts, Customers, Sales Personnel, Sales Admin, Pricing, Customer Service and Shipping. Position serves as back-up for Manager.</p>
<p><b>
Essential Duties and Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Provides direction and is a mentor to the Credit Analysts. Responsible for overseeing training, critical accounts and new account set up approvals over $100,000.</li>
<li>
Identifies and resolves shorts pays on invoices through interaction with sales administration, pricing, distribution and sales.</li>
<li>
Interprets financial statements to determine customer’s financial strength and recommends appropriate credit limit and actions in accordance with established guidelines and company policy. Prepares account analysis for review by superiors.</li>
<li>
Reviews daily pending orders to determine whether or not product can be shipped, based upon account status. Responds to requests from field sales and customers regarding account hold status.</li>
<li>
Improves cash flow through monitoring and timely collection of past due invoices and monitoring of daily billing to ensure invoices are created timely and effectively</li>
<li>
Input to performance evaluations of analysts at goals and review time with Manager</li>
<li>
Recommends and arranges for forced collections with outside sources.</li>
<li>
Prepares standard daily weekly or monthly accounts receivable reports as assigned</li>
<li>
Ensures lean initiatives and synergies are maximized in the department</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Job Requirements:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Supervisory formal training and experience</li>
<li>Minimum five years prior credit and collection related experience</li>
<li>National Association of Credit Management CBA or CBF designation preferred</li>
<li>Must be dependable with attention to detail and accurate with numbers</li>
<li>Must possess good organizational and verbal/written communication skills</li>
<li>Experience in Microsoft Office – Excel, Word, Outlook365, network environment, and ONBASE preferred</li>
<li>Experience with automated software, interfaced platforms and workflow that are available to assist the Credit department with but not limited to cash application, credit application and approval processes, deductions management and claims resolution desired</li>
<li>Infor LX and i-Series experience a plus</li>
</ul>
