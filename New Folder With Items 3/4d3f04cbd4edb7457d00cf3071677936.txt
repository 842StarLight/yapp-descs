<h2>Experienced Account executive</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>The UPS Store is seeking a highly motivated and dynamic <b>Sales Account Executive </b>to join our winning team. If you're passionate about sales, an influential negotiator, and results driven, we want to hear from you!</p>
<p>The Sales Account Executive strategically converts new business, penetrates territories, and fulfills quotas, with a primary objective to grow and retain profitable revenue. He/She analyzes sales reports and transportation trends to identify new customers, growth from existing customers, and shipment reductions. The Sales Account Executive tracks sales opportunities and develops a pipeline of potential customers through strategic relationships. He/She promotes cross-functional sales by sharing leads, informing peers on freight services and bundling opportunities, and collaborating on sales proposals.</p>
<p><b>Sales Account Executive Responsibilities and Duties</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Compiles weekly sales recaps on achievements, losses, and competitive information</li>
<li>Analyzes account recaps and monitors revenue trends to develop service recommendations</li>
<li>Utilizes shipping technology and systems for account activity review and customer database sign-up</li>
<li>Manages accounts by advising customers on billing processes, resolving inquiries, and entertaining customers</li>
<li>Trains customers on use and advantages of web-based shipping and tracking functions</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Sales Account Executive Requirements</b></p>
<ul>
<li>At least 1+ year of Sales Experience</li>
<li>Bachelor's Degree</li>
<li>Understanding of UPS Store leverage over competitor products, services, and technology</li>
<li>Critical thinker with analytical skills to project future customer needs</li>
<li>Strategic relationship builder with focus on customer pipeline and key decision makers</li>
<li>Persuasive negotiator with tactical techniques to overcome objections</li>
<li>Problem solver with ability to identify issues and provide solutions</li>
<li>Solid grasp on industry trends and financial impact</li>
</ul>
