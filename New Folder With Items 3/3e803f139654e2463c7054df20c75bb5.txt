We're looking for a driven and self-starting Business Development Representative, who can talk high level about data science, predictive analytics, and our A.I. platform. You'll be prospecting and qualifying leads for our sales team, making a huge difference at an early-stage startup that has been recognized as one of the hottest companies in LA.</div>

A natural wit and excellent phone presence are a necessity. We're looking for someone to join a team responsible for outbound email and cold call campaigns. You'll also be responding to inbound web leads. Compensation is competitive, salaried, and incentive-based. The right individual will be rewarded for team selling and scheduling key meetings.</div>
