ABOUT THE ROLE:</div>
<div>
WalkMe is looking for a creative Events Marketing Manager responsible for the planning, development and execution of our US-based events program. You will have experience driving event strategy that builds brand momentum, market leadership, lead generation and client engagement. This is a unique opportunity to help take our events program to the next level.</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
The Events Marketing Manager role is a highly visible role and will work collaboratively with our internal global teams, industry organizations, and external vendors to establish and provide unique event experiences that guide measurable return on investment. If you are an expert B2B event marketer who is data-driven and navigate easily between projects, this is the job for you!</div>
</div>
<div><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>YOU WILL</b></h3>
<div><ul>
<li>
Manage programs including, third-party trade shows, special events, ancillary events, experiential activations, partner events, roadshows, and our user conferences</li>
<li>
Collaborate with internal and external teams to build programs, including scoping and goal setting, overall project management and execution, analysis.</li>
<li>
 On the ground, hands-on role in our events, work with participants, represent our brand, promote our story, and attract the desired audience</li>
<li>
Contribute to an event portfolio that is excellent by researching and identifying trade shows and conferences that are in consideration of business goals</li>
<li>
 Produce sponsored events from concept through completion: determine strategy and goals, oversee budget, negotiate with and manage vendors, plan and track deliverables, analyze metrics and return on investment</li>
<li>
 Assist with WalkMe owned events such as our annual user conference, roadshow series, and regional field events</li>
<li>
 Collaborate with other teams including demand generation, product marketing, and design to guide thoughtful event experiences pre, during, and post event</li>
<li>
 Partner with sales ops and the sales team to ensure smooth hand off and follow up of leads</li>
<li>
 Continuously brainstorm unique activations and creative ideas to make our brand stick in the minds of our buyers</li>
<li>
 Improve the planning process to ensure our programs are performed flawlessly, stay ahead of schedule and remain within budget</li>
<li>
 Update the marketing leadership team on status with trends, metrics, challenges/solutions, forecasts, and strategy to guide brand awareness and pipeline growth</li>
</ul></div>
</div></div>
<div><div>

<div><ul>

 Bachelor's degree or equivalent combination of education and experience</li>
<li>
2+ years event management and planning experience, including building complex events (conferences, executive programs, trade shows), preferably in a SaaS or B2B, high-tech</li>
<li>
Experience with budget management of $1M+
</li>
<li>High-level understanding of lead lifecycle
</li>
<li>A passion for creatively transforming brand principles into real-life experiences</li>
<li>
Measure marketing effectiveness and gaining customer insights</li>
<li>
Ability to work self sufficiently, and excel in an ever-evolving environment</li>
<li>
Excellent written and people skills, communicate with all members of the team from individual contributors to executive staff</li>
<li>
Experience with Google and Microsoft tools, Salesforce; experience working with Pardot/Marketo, Splash, other email marketing tools a plus
</li>
<li>Ability to travel up to 30%, mostly domestic</li>
<li>
Team player with integrity</li>
