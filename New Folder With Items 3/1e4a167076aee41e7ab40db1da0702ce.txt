<h2>Account Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>Are you a relationship-focused professional who thrives on high level corporate account management? Are you familiar with the dynamic and intensely exciting real estate industry? Do you love adding value to brokerages to make them and their agents more successful? If you are looking for a fast-paced environment focused on real estate marketing and you love program and relationship management, we’ve got a place for you at Corefact!</p>
<p>
The Business Development Team is looking for a Senior Account Manager to oversee enterprise relationships with our most valuable brokerage brands and clients at a national level. We will entrust this individual with growing influencer relationships, driving corporate initiatives and programs, and implementing new high value account launches.</p>
<p><b>
Key Responsibilities include:</b>·</p>
<ul>
<li>Main point of contact for Brokerage Corporate staff for assigned accounts</li>
<li>Proactively and regularly consult with key client contacts with intention to grow business and drive corporate promotion and programs</li>
<li>Create and implement business plan and growth strategy for each Enterprise account</li>
<li>Promote Corefact usage through live presentations, webinars, regional trainings, new agent classes, event attendance, etc.</li>
<li>Collaborate with other Corefact teams for support and implementation</li>
<li>Achieve and maintain an expert level of understanding of the Corefact system</li>
<li>Reports to the VP of Business Development</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Minimum Qualifications:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>3-7 years Sales or Account Management experience, preferably in Real Estate or a related field
</li>
<li>Marketing, print, or direct mail experience a plus</li>
<li>Exceptional Interpersonal skills</li>
<li>Extremely detail oriented and organized</li>
<li>Impeccable follow up</li>
<li>Technical competence</li>
<li>Motivated, goal oriented, and persistent</li>
<li>High level of initiative and creativity</li>
<li>Excellent written and oral communication skills</li>
<li>Ability to communicate effectively with high level corporate clients</li>
<li>Strong presentation skills both via webinar and in front of groups</li>
<li>Handles stressful situations and deadline pressures well</li>
<li>Ability to prioritize and manage multiple simultaneous projects and tasks</li>
<li>Plans and carries out responsibilities with minimal direction</li>
<li>Proactively seeks and develop solutions and systems.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Required Travel:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Occasional travel to represent Corefact at regional or national events</li>
</ul> <p><b>About Corefact:
</b></p>
<p>Corefact, based in Hayward, CA, is a privately held company providing sales organizations and sales people with a complete marketing solution that leverages state-of-the-art conversion tools and timely data driven content – both online and offline – that engage customers and convert prospects since 2005. The Corefact patent-pending marketing solutions help generate leads, build relationships and promote products and services using a one-to-one approach, matching multiple unique data points to create a personalized experience for the consumer. Combined with market-timed content and unique tracking codes, the company’s systems are able to generate unprecedented response and conversion rates utilizing the best of direct mail and web technology.</p>
