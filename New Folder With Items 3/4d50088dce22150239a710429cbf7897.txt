<p><b>About You:</b></p>
<p><b><i>
Do you have energy, enthusiasm, and a passion for excellence? We are seeking a driven individual that is motivated by client satisfaction and continued success to join our Finance Team at our Headquarter offices.</i></b></p>
<p>
This role is responsible for assisting the Finance department in creating accurate and timely financial records for the organization. This position reports directly to the Financial Controller. This role is expected to maintain financial reports, records and general ledgers, prepare and analyze budgets and perform general bookkeeping. Additionally, responsible for billing activities, such as accounts payable and accounts receivable.</p>
<p>
Essential Duties:</p>
<ul>
<li>Reconciles processed work by verifying entries and comparing system reports to balances.</li>
<li>Charges expenses to accounts and cost centers by analyzing invoice/expense reports; recording entries.</li>
<li>Pays vendors by monitoring discount opportunities; verifying federal id numbers; scheduling and preparing checks; resolving purchase order, contract, invoice, or payment discrepancies and documentation; insuring credit is received for outstanding memos; issuing stop-payments or purchase order amendments.</li>
<li>Maintains accounting ledgers by verifying and posting account transactions.</li>
<li>Verifies vendor accounts by reconciling monthly statements and related transactions.</li>
<li>Posts customer payments by recording cash, checks, and credit card transactions.</li>
<li>Verifies validity of account discrepancies by obtaining and investigating information from sales, trade promotions, customer service departments, and from customers;</li>
</ul>
<p>Required Education and Experience:</p>
<ul>
<li>At least 2 or more years in accounting or bookkeeping role. Must have prior experience with A/P, A/R, bank reconciliations, and financial reports.</li>
<li>Bachelor degree desired but may substitute experience for degree</li>
<li>Working knowledge of Accrual Accounting</li>
<li>Attention to Detail, thoroughness, organization, analytical</li>
<li>Data entry skills, general math skills, accounting rules, financial software (QuickBooks Online)</li>
<li>Must be dependable and reliable</li>

</ul>
