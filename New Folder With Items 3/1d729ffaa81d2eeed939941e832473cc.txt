<h2>Process Control Engineer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>POSITION OVERVIEW
</b></p>
<p></p>
<p>Ascend Performance Materials is the premium provider of high quality chemicals, fibers and plastics. With world scale integrated manufacturing facilities we are able to develop new products from our core technologies, and provide flexibility to respond to the expanding needs of our customers. Ascend has global sales and distribution with five manufacturing facilities in the United States.
</p>
<p>The Chocolate Bayou process control Department is seeking a highly-motivated candidate for the position of process control engineer. The position offers a challenging environment with opportunities for growth in technical experience and personal development. The Ascend Chocolate Bayou site is a tightly integrated chemical manufacturing facility with fixed batch and continuous control systems.
<br>
<br>
Good communication, strong analytical and technical skills, an interest for problem solving and trouble-shooting, and knowledge of handling database software, and other computer systems are essential. Experience with managing and programming PLCs and DCS such as Fisher ProVOX and/or DeltaV systems is also a critical need for the position.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>TARGETED OUTCOMES
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Measurable improvements in unit performance aligned to business financial objectives including raw material usage, energy usage, reliability, capacity, and quality.
</li>
<li>Technical support, guidance and expertise for all phases of automation projects, from front-end engineering through detailed design, configuration, implementation, startup, commissioning, and maintenance.
</li>
<li>Well-defined Process Automation productivity improvement plans (current year +1) and pipelines (5 years) for the Chocolate Bayou site.
</li>
</ul>
<ul><li>Develop and maintain dynamic operational entitlement models in order to understand best performance for any given set of conditions (production demand, catalyst age, etc.)
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Application of cutting edge controls technology to improve manufacturing processes to provide competitive advantage.
</li></ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>KEY RESPONSIBILITIES
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Design, develop, implement and maintain process automation productivity improvement for chemical processes.
</li>
<li>Propose enhancements to existing control strategies.
</li>
<li>Use a broad range of automation tools such as advanced regulatory control, model predictive control, abnormal situation management, adaptive tuning, optimal control, robust control, adaptive control, or soft sensors to optimize existing processes.
</li>
<li>Work closely with manufacturing units to define appropriate controls applications.
</li>
<li>Develop knowledge of existing processes to identify process control improvement opportunities.
</li>
<li>Work with multi-disciplined teams to design, develop and implement solutions for manufacturing processes.
</li>
<li>Lead automation projects
</li>
<li>Develop key performance indicators, track key process parameters and overall process performance.
</li>
<li>Leverage Six Sigma techniques to improve process performance and efficiency.
</li>
<li>Train and facilitate the transfer of new process control technologies into manufacturing units
</li>
<li>Document process control findings in technical reports.
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>REQUIRED EXPERIENCE
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Education - BS in Chemical Engineering or equivalent degree
</li>
<li>Minimum of 3 years experience as a process controls engineer with a Bachelors or Masters in Chemical engineering. Other engineering degrees will be given consideration based on relevant experience
</li>
<li>Knowledge and prior participation in process Hazard Analysis
</li>
<li>Ability to apply Layer of Protection Analysis (LOPA) to high hazard scenarios
</li>
<li>Knowledge of the requirements of ISA84.2004
</li>
<li>Strong programming and programming design skills as applied to batch process control, data communication, data collection and display systems
</li>
<li>Prior experience with DCS programming and management
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>DESIRED CERTIFICATION
</b></p>
<ul><li>Prior experience DeltaV and Provox/Envox a plus.
</li></ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>REQUIRED SKILLS
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Understanding of the reliability, documentation and Management of Change requirements imposed by process control systems.
</li>
<li>Demonstrated ability to apply Continuous Improvement Tools
</li>
<li>Strong initiative to identify and address areas for improvement
</li>
<li>Strong interpersonal and organizational skills
</li>
<li>Excellent verbal and written communication skills
</li>
<li>Demonstrated ability to work in a team environment and effectively manage multiple complex projects
</li>
</ul>
