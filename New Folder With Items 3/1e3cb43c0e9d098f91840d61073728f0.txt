<h2>Translator (Full-Time)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<b>OVERVIEW</b><p>
At Sentry, we're looking for an individual to translate verbally or rewrite documents in specified language following established rules pertaining to factors such as word meanings, sentence structure, grammar, punctuation and mechanics. Verbal translations are provided over the phone, and at times in person.</p>
<p>
If you’re looking for a position to build upon your customer service skills while utilizing your strong phone and translating experience in a professional, fast-paced, and supportive environment, then take the next step towards joining Sentry!</p>
<p><b>
To meet the needs of our customers, we're looking for availability during the following times:</b></p>
<ul>
<li><b>Monday - Friday: 8:00am - 10:00pm</b></li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Schedules are based on your availability and business needs and are created two weeks in advance.</b></p>
<b>
WHAT YOU'LL DO</b><p>
As a Translator, you’ll work within our First Notice of Loss call center translating verbally phone conversations and rewriting documents in both English and Spanish. In addition, you’ll:</p>
<ul>
<li>Participate on phone calls between Sentry and our Claimants</li>
<li>Interpret various dialect meanings while translating verbally or in a written form</li>
<li>Provide translation services to all Sentry offices</li>
<li>Translate a high volume of calls daily</li>
<li>Provide a high quality of customer service for internal and external customers</li>
</ul>
<b>
WHAT IT TAKES</b><ul>
<li>High School Diploma/GED</li>
<li>Call center experience or a customer service background preferred</li>
<li>Excellent ability to speak, read, and write fluently in English and Spanish</li>
<li>Familiar with various dialect meanings in both English and Spanish</li>
<li>Ability to multi-task, remain organized, and communicate effectively both oral and written</li>
<li>Effective listening skills and the ability to summarize provided information</li>
<li>Good working knowledge of PC based computers and Microsoft Office</li>
</ul>
