<h2>Brand Marketing Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<b>Division Information</b><br>
<p>Hardware and Home Improvement (HHI) is a major manufacturer and supplier of residential locksets, residential builders' hardware, faucets, and commercial locksets and doors, with a portfolio of renowned brands, including Kwikset®, Weiser®, Baldwin®, National Hardware®, Stanley®, FANAL®, Pfister™, EZSET®, and Tell. HHI is a leader in its key markets with #1 positions in U.S. residential locksets (Kwikset), Canada residential locksets (Weiser), U.S. luxury locksets (Baldwin), and U.S. builders' hardware (Stanley-National Hardware), #2 in Mexico residential hardware (Fanal), #3 in U.S. retail plumbing (Pfister), and #1 in U.S. retail commercial locksets. Headquartered in Orange County, California, HHI has a global sales force and operates manufacturing and distribution facilities in the U.S., Canada, Mexico and Asia. HHI is a division of Spectrum Brands Holdings, Inc.</p>
<br>
<b>
Job Summary</b><br>
<p>The Brand Manager is responsible for independently creating and managing the brand presence for the brands. This position has direct interaction with channel marketing, product management, category management, retail and field sales, graphic design, finance, web and ecommerce teams, as well as outside vendors.</p>
<br>
<b>
Primary Duties &amp; Responsibilities</b><br>
<p><b>Primary Duties and Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
<b>(50%) Brand Management
</b><ul>
<li>Ensure brand continuity and accuracy, along with proper use of brand logos, sub-brands, naming conventions, fonts, colors, imagery, etc. based on the brand guidelines.</li>
<li>Effectively manage vendor base to execute ongoing projects.</li>
<li>Provide project management for creative execution of all product launches, brand initiatives and retail marketing programs</li>
<li>Manage production of video assets, obtaining quotes from vendors, drafting scripts, coordinating location, purchasing props, etc.</li>
<li>Project Management – manage several different Brand initiatives / projects simultaneously, working cross functionally to deliver on commitments.</li>
<li>Event management – support communication functions for events, trade shows and meetings as required</li>
<br>
</ul>
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>
<b>(30%) Brand Awareness and Vitality
</b><ul>
<li>Plan and execute annual advertising plan, identifying new opportunities for reaching consumer and retailers</li>
<li>Work with internal departments to identify, analyze and implement programs based on consumer insight and trend data. Is able to effectively link consumer insights into tangible, strategic plans.</li>
<li>Develop strong relationships with social media partners and public relations agencies to maximize end-user and trade exposure. Evaluate ROI performance and effectiveness of programs.</li>
<li>Oversee the development and execution of content for advertising, promotion, collateral, and website programs; working with outside agencies and internal Web, in-house design and eCommerce departments.</li>
<br>
</ul>
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<ul><li>
<b>(20%) Business Processes
</b><ul>
<li>Lead the development of the annual Brand Marketing Budget; optimizing the spend while meeting key P&amp;L metrics. Consistent P&amp;L management including: individual project budgets, aligning quotes, job numbers, invoices, and deliverables.</li>
<li>Collaborate with the in-house design group/photography departments to prioritize and optimize creative projects.</li>
<li>Process Development – develop processes necessary to accomplish assigned projects from inception to execution including development of communications plan and execution of all elements.</li>
</ul>
</li></ul>
