<h2>Systems Engineer - Sr Staff for Gas and Environmental Sensing</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Founded in 2003, InvenSense Inc., a TDK Group Company, is the world’s leading provider of MEMS sensor platforms. InvenSense’s vision of Sensing Everything™ targets the consumer electronics and industrial markets with integrated Motion and Sound solutions. Our solutions combine MEMS (micro electrical mechanical systems) sensors, such as accelerometers, gyroscopes, compasses, and microphones with proprietary algorithms and firmware that intelligently process, synthesize, and calibrate the output of sensors, maximizing performance and accuracy. InvenSense’s motion tracking, audio and location platforms, and services can be found in many of the world’s largest and most iconic brands including smartphones, tablets, wearables, drones, gaming devices, internet of things, automotive products, and remote controls for smart TVs.</p>
<p>
InvenSense is headquartered in San Jose, CA and has offices in Boston, China, Taiwan, Korea, Japan, France, Canada, Slovakia, and Italy. We’re looking for top-notch Engineers to join our global team. If you’re interested in being a part of our journey and helping us grow to become the leading provider of SoC platform solutions, we definitely want to hear from you.</p>
<p>
Job Responsibilities:</p>
<p>
We are looking for a <b>Systems Engineer – Sr Staff for Gas and Environmental sensing group </b>for our San Jose, CA office.</p>
<p>
The person in this position will work on a range of activities involved in the development, evaluation, system integration and customer presentation of TDK’s next generation and emerging sensor technology, specifically focused on gas and environmental sensors. This is an opportunity for the right candidate to become part of a well-funded entrepreneurial activity within a large company and grow along with the group and its business, as well as contribute early on to productizing an excellent and unique technology that TDK has built over the last few years.</p>
<p>
Specific responsibilities:</p>
<p>
The successful candidate is expected to play a leadership role in bringing an exciting new sensor technology to market, by drawing upon his/her sound understanding of gas and environmental sensing solutions for consumer, automotive and commercial (industrial) applications. Specific responsibilities include:</p>
<ul>
<li>
<p>Interact with customers and be the technical go-to resource for pre-sales issues</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Specify and actively assist the development of a system-level integrated solution for customers, including board level design and any firmware/software development and integration</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Interface with cross-disciplinary teams (including ASIC, MEMS, software, manufacturing, customer engineering, etc)</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Assist with developing best-in-class product requirements specification, including for sensors and support electronics (ASIC)</p>
</li>
<li>Provide leadership in hiring and growing an engineering group</li>
</ul>
<p>Qualifications:</p>
<ul>
<li>
<p>BS EE or equivalent; MS EE or equivalent is desirable</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>10+ years hands-on experience primarily in systems engineering for sensors – preferably gas and environmental sensors – along with deep specialization in one of the related areas: ASIC design, sensor-based system development and integration, and complex board design, or software/firmware design for sensor-based systems.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Strong knowledge of sensors, esp. MEMS based gas and environmental sensors, is very desirable</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Experience dealing with a wide range of customers globally, especially on technical and product issues</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Excellent communication skills and a dynamic, growth-oriented personality</p>
</li>
</ul>
