<h2>Customer Account Manager - CDH</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p><b>The Customer Account Manager</b> is responsible for developing high-trust relationships with Benefitfocus customers, measured through case response, case resolution, case quality scores, customer satisfaction and referenceability. The Customer Account Manager provides continuity and ensures a seamless experience spanning all stages of the customer lifecycle.</p>
<p>
This role requires a strong understanding of carrier benefits administration, technology (particularly SaaS), carrier integration processes, and issue resolution best practices. The Customer Account Manager is an extension of his/her customerâ€™s technical and business teams, helping them to drive their desired outcomes while maximizing the value of their Benefitfocus solution and ensuring a quality operation is in place that delivers to customer expectations.</p>
<p><b>
Responsibilities</b>:</p>
<ul><li>
Build and maintain operational and other key relationships with a portfolio of CDH providers.<br>
<ul>

<li>Manage the internal delivery to customer expectations through internal managing initiatives, fixing reliability and quality issues, and tracking service metrics.</li>
<li>In a matrixed environment acts as a leader of the Benefitfocus professional teams responsible for servicing CDH products and processes.</li>
<li>Manage customer relationships to maximize desired outcomes for the customers during operational issue response and resolution interactions.</li>
<li>Work directly with customers and vendors to troubleshoot, research, and resolve questions and issues within our products; Collaborate with other associates and resources required to resolve issues</li>

<li>Act as the subject matter expert and lead the internal Benefitfocus team on behalf of the customer, motivating the team, coordinating across departments, anticipating and addressing needs, driving an education and process focus, and ensuring the highest possible quality of work generated by the team</li>
