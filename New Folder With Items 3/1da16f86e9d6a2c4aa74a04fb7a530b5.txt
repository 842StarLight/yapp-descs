<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Job Description
</b></h2>
<p>Hall Acura in Newport News is now hiring for part-time and full-time lot attendants. If you are ready to start a career in the automotive industry, this is a great way to learn the business. Apply today to learn more.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>SUMMARY:
</b></p>
<p>The Lot Attendant moves and cleans vehicles, keeps the vehicles in sound working condition, and helps the General Manager as requested.<br>
<br>
</p>
<div>
<b>ESSENTIAL DUTIES:
</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Maintains new and used vehicle inventory appearance and performance
</li>
<li>Places buyer guides and stock tags in vehicles
</li>
<li>Keeps new and used vehicle lots neat and orderly, moving cars as directed by the General Manager and in accordance with dealership display standards
</li>
<li>Sweeps, shovels, or vacuums loose debris or salvageable scrap into containers and removes containers from work areas
</li>
<li>Drives vehicles to and from service lane, service stalls, and parking lot as needed
</li>
<li>Makes key tags for vehicles
</li>
<li>Performs other duties as assigned<br>
</li>
</ul>
</div>
<div>
<b>QUALIFICATIONS:
</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Must have clean driving record with no more than 2 moving violations
</li>
<li>Must be able to drive both an automatic and manual car shift
</li>
<li>Must be at least 18 years old
</li>
<li>Ability to read and comprehend simple instructions, short correspondence, and memos
</li>
<li>Basic MS Office knowledge; computer software, internet proficiency and general mathematic skills
</li>
</ul>
</div>
