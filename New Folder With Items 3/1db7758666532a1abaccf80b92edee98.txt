<p>Summary</p>
<b>
</b><p></p>
<p>This<br>
position is responsible for installing, troubleshooting and repairing TRUMPF<br>
CNC machines.</p>
<b>
</b><p><b>Principle Duties &amp; Responsibilities</b></p>
<b>
</b><p>The statements<br>
below are intended to describe the general nature and level of work in this<br>
position. They are not intended to be an exhaustive list of all<br>
responsibilities. The position may require that employees perform other duties<br>
as assigned.</p>
<b>
</b><ul>
<li>Provide exceptional customer service
</li>
<li>Clearly and professionally interact with customers
</li>
<li>Work closely with customers and machines to ensure equipment operates within expected tolerances</li>
<b>
</b><li>Pursue machine diagnosis of our internal Product Support Group (PSG) to repair TRUMPF equipment</li>
<b>
</b><li>Work carefully with the PSG for technical assistance and provide them with diagnostic feedback</li>
<b>
</b><li>At times, use visual clues, computer programs, diagnostic tools to further diagnose machine difficulties</li>
<b>
</b><li>Use electrical and electronic knowledge to help troubleshoot and solve issues</li>
<b>
</b><li>Read and utilize company repair manuals and work orders for assistance with repairs and diagnostics</li>
<b>
</b><li>Clean, replace or install parts to maintain smooth operation of machinery and equipment</li>
<b>
</b><li>Assemble, install, align equipment as needed</li>
<b>
</b><li>Accurately report technical conditions and information of machines to PSG and management</li>
<b>
</b><li>Interface with cross functional work teams such as applications, sales, engineering, production, etc.
</li>
<li>When needed, provide onsite training to customers for operation and/or repair of machines</li>
<b>
</b><li>Assist other Field Service Engineers as needed</li>
<b>
</b><li>Receive factory product training and technical service knowledge as needed</li>
<b>
</b><li>Submit organized and helpful service reports for each repair mission</li>
<b>
</b><li>Help to develop or adjust existing technical documentation</li>
<b>
</b><li>Work as required with minimal supervision in compliance with all safety requirements</li>
<b>
</b><li>Travel up to 100%. Travel includes regional and national travel</li>
<b>
</b><li>Must be able to lift 70 pounds without aid</li>
<b>
</b>
</ul>
<p><b>Experience/Education</b></p>
<b>
</b><p>Associates program or technical school degree in Engineering principles or a suitable combination of education and work experience. Experience in computer software, file management, electronics, digital circuits, wiring, mechanics and hydraulics is helpful. Well-mannered, able to work with all different types of customers. Must have and maintain a clean and valid drivers license and record.</p>
