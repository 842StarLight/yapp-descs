<p><b>Inside Sales - Sales Executive</b></p>
<p>
The Unearth Strategic Account Advisor will be responsible for applying an understanding of Unearth’s products, sales methodology, processes, prospecting techniques, and customer base while selling to our various line of business accounts. In this role, you will focus on companies that can benefit Unearth’s GIS-based progress documentation for the built world industries. The primary function of this position is new account acquisition and expansion. The aim of this role is to grow revenue with an emphasis on new accounts created and expansion within those accounts. This includes following up on inbound leads, prospecting, qualifying, consultative selling, negotiation, and closing.</p>
<p>
This position is based in our office in downtown Seattle a block from the Space Needle.</p>
<p>
 <b>Responsibilities:</b></p>
<p>
Timely follow up and qualification of new prospects from either inbound leads or customer requests generated by marketing</p>
<p>
Develop prospecting plans for territory development to build rapport and create opportunities</p>
<p>
Research accounts, identify key players, generate interest and obtain business requirements</p>
<p>
Maintain accurate and up-to-date forecasts</p>
<p>
Provide sales management with reports on sales activities and projects as requested</p>
<p>
Pursue and increase knowledge of key competitors to ensure that our value proposition is effectively communicated to customers</p>
<p>
Manage and maintain accurate leads, opportunities, and account information within HubSpot</p>
<p>
Achieve or exceed monthly and quarterly targets</p>
<p>
Obtain repeat business, referrals, and references by applying an understanding of the unique requirements of your customers</p>
<p>
Networking, relationship building, cold calling, lead follow-up through emails, product demonstrations, and execution of service agreements</p>
