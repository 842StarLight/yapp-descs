<h2>Experienced Payment Processing Division Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p>Beyond is seeking an experienced sales professional who is tired of the status quo and wants to help change the face of the merchant services industry. You must be the type of individual that enjoys working with other high performers and craves the challenge of closing deals. The Division Director position includes a lucrative compensation model that offers overrides on every sale in their division, lifetime residuals, portfolio equity, and portfolio buybacks.</p>
<p>
In addition, every Beyond employee hired will have the opportunity to own part of the company. The Division Director will lead a team of Business Advocates and Team Leads that are responsible for providing point-of-sale, payment processing, lending, payroll, and integrated HR services to small and mid-size business owners.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
What You Will Do</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Drive revenue growth by onboarding and training a team of highly motivated Business Advocates and Team Leads.</li>
<li>
Be a subject matter expert for your sales team and clients in all areas of merchant services</li>
<li>
Grow your team through outbound recruiting and interviewing</li>
<li>
Learn and understand all statements, hardware, software, and reporting offered by Beyond</li>
<li>
Deliver solution driven presentations to business partners</li>
<li>
Organize your day and week around competing demands</li>
<li>
Implement a team strategy to exceed sales and headcount growth goals</li>
<li>
Work closely with your team for prospecting, training, and development</li>
<li>
Accurately track and record activities in Salesforce</li>
<li>
Be a strong leader and mentor within our organization</li>
<li>
Serve as local sales manager of designated areas within division</li>
</ul>

Proven sales professional, 5+ years of experience</li>

<li>

Advanced presentation, interpersonal, time management, and organizational skills</li>

Proficiency in MS Office applications, e.g., Word, Excel and PowerPoint</li>
