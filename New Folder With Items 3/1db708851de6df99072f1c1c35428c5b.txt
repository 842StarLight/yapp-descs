<h2>Commercial Portfolio Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>Provide support to lenders and business bankers, in their daily duties in underwriting and monitoring existing and proposed credits including line renewals, maturing loans, term loan reviews and criticized asset reports. Monitors status of existing loans takes appropriate action on loans that are maturing or past due, and assists in loan closings. Perform special projects for the department manager.</p>
<ul>
<li>Responsible for the preparation of the Loan Summary/Top Sheet and coordination with the Loan Administrator, Lenders, Business Bankers, Department Manager and Credit Department for the new loan requests and renewal of lines of credit to ensure the analysis is accurate and lines are renewed in a timely manner.</li>
<li>Responsible for managing maturing loans to ensure extension requests are prepared in a timely manner and/or that the credit analysis is timely and accurate.</li>
<li>Responsible for managing annual term loan reviews and coordinating with the Credit Department to ensure the term loan review is timely and accurate.</li>
<li>Participate when requested in the loan approval process.</li>
<li>Coordinate with the Loan Administrator to ensure files are up to date to with appropriate financial information and memos to ensure satisfactory loan reviews.</li>
<li>Responsible for preparing responses to internal audits and internal or external loan reviews.</li>
<li>Assist Loan Administrator to ensure Criticized Asset Reports are completed in a timely and accurate manner.</li>
<li>Ensure loans are properly risk rated and complete necessary forms to make the appropriate changes to risk ratings.</li>
<li>Coordinate covenant compliance issues to ensure waivers are approved or covenant default letters are sent to Borrowers.</li>
<li>Complete review of third-party reports including appraisal, environmental, property condition and other reports as necessary.</li>
<li>Assist the Loan Administrator in past due loan management.</li>
<li>Assist the Loan Administrator in management and collection of tickler items.</li>
<li>Accompany relationship manger, department manager and Bank staff on sales calls when appropriate.</li>
</ul>
</div>
<p></p>
<br>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor and applicable degree in accounting, finance or economics preferred.
</li>
<li>Ten plus years of credit analysis and/or loan review experience, preferably in the banking industry or an advanced business degree with three or more years of business-related experience.</li>
<li>Must be proficient in basic software applications such as Microsoft Word and Excel.
</li>
<li>Strong oral and written communication skills.
</li>
<li>Strong customer service skills.
</li>
<li>Ability to think pro-actively and work independently of supervision.</li>
</ul>
</div>
