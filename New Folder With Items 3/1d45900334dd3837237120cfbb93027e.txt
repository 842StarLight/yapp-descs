<h2>Content Marketing Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<b>Description:</b><br>
Summary Purpose
<br><br>
Responsible for coordination, creation and execution of marketing content and communications. Execute day-to-day and forward-looking content marketing initiatives which may include: research, writing, editing, website content optimization, marketing communications, CRM content integrations, copy for digital advertising, lead generation support and more. Effectively work with business partners, cross-functional team members and suppliers while balancing scope and quality. Demonstrate the company’s core values to continuously deliver on the TechSolve brand promise. The Content Marketing Specialist will work under the direction of the Marketing Manager.
<br><br>
Essential Functions
<br><b>1. Content Creation:</b> Write and edit various types of marketing content including but not limited to: blogs, website copy, case studies, email, collateral, webinars, presentations, social media, etc. Coordinate with various subject matter experts to research, create and publish content which effectively embodies TechSolve’s tone, positioning &amp; brand values.
<br><b>2. Editorial Calendar:</b> Collaborate with marketing team and subject matter experts to build and manage an effective content and editorial calendar that supports TechSolve’s goals including but not limited to social media, email marketing, blogs, promotional campaigns, etc.
<br><b>3. Audience Development:</b> Grow subscriber base by providing regular, meaningful content that’s aligned with audience needs and interests and fulfills TechSolve’s brand promise.
<br><b>4. Content Strategy:</b> Serve as a Content Marketing expert on the TechSolve team. Keep up with the latest trends in content marketing, analytics, the ROI of various tactics, etc.
<br><b>5. SEO:</b> Manage the health of TechSolve’s content-driven SEO. Insure content is SEO optimized, maintain a priority keyword list and communicate SEO goals and strategies for content to marketing team.
<br><b>6. Website:</b> Insure website content drives a high quality website user experience, SEO, lead conversion, and meets other KPI-driven optimization goals. Maintain accurate and current website content.
<br><b>7. Digital Advertising:</b> Coordinate and optimize content and keywords for paid digital media, (e.g. PPC, Banner Ads, etc.).
<br><b>8. Lead Nurturing:</b> Assist sales and marketing team with lead generation and nurturing efforts including promotional campaigns, inbound marketing, development of website landing pages and assets, etc. Insure marketing and sales teams have current and accurate collateral and other materials.
<br><b>9. Reporting, Analytics &amp; Optimization:</b> Monitor, optimize and report on TechSolve’s content marketing efforts using methods like A/B testing, keywords, etc. Establish closed-loop analytics with sales to understand how inbound marketing activity turns into customers, and continually refine our process to convert customers.
<br><b>10. Technology Stack &amp; Operations:</b> Effectively leverage and assist with maintenance and integrity of TechSolve’s marketing technology stack (e.g. HubSpot (CRM), WordPress, Mailchimp (Email Marketing &amp; Automation), WebEx, MOZ, Yoast, Hotjar, Google Analytics, Microsoft Office 365, Social Media, Paid Media, etc.) to support business growth.
<br><b>11. Vendor Management:</b> Assist with vendor communications and collaboration for campaigns, content, analytics and asset creation.
<br><br>
Secondary Functions
<br><br>
1. Perform other job related duties as assigned.
<br><br>
Supervisory Responsibility
<br>Supervises 1-3 Co-ops/interns.
<br><br>

Requirements/Qualifications
<br><br>

<li>Bachelor’s degree in Marketing, Communications, Public Relations, Writing or a related field.</li>
</ul>
<b>Required Skills/Competencies:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Capable of writing high quality creative and technical content for a variety of business applications.</li>
<li>Demonstrated understanding of content strategy for a variety of platforms (i.e. website, video, blog, social media, technical presentations, etc.) and business objectives (i.e. website traffic generation, SEO, lead nurturing and conversion, etc.)</li>
<li>Extremely organized and capable of managing multiple projects simultaneously.</li>
<li>Communicates effectively with leadership, external stakeholders, subject matter experts, etc.</li>
<li>Comfortable working both independently and in collaborative team settings.</li>
<li>Adaptable to and comfortable with ever-changing business demands. Takes initiative to work through ambiguity and effectively problem solve.</li>
<li>Proficient with Microsoft Office applications.</li>
