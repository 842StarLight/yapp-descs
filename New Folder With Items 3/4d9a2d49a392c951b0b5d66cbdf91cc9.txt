<p>With over 300 consultants on staff, Eagle Creek Software Services operates with the focused mission to provide the highest quality and most cost effective CRM, Information Management and Application Development services to Fortune 2000 companies. We utilize our U.S. based Project Centers to offer a customer-centric, remote delivery model designed to adapt to our customers and their technology solutions. Our project teams are comprised of highly experienced on-site consultants and Project Center consultants working collaboratively to execute our clients’ vision for their IT initiatives.</p>
<p>
Through this model we are able to achieve consistency, sustainability, and scalability in the provisioning of technical and consulting capabilities to our clients, helping them increase quality and efficiencies by managing cost and risk in software services investments, while providing a unique, professional experience for our employees.</p>
<p>
Eagle Creek is investing in the rapid expansion of our Salesforce practice and as such, we are looking to add the best Salesforce talent to our growing practice. For more information on everything Eagle Creek, please visit www.eaglecrk.com.</p>
<p><b>
Job Overview:</b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
Eagle creek is seeking a Salesforce.com Business Analyst to support sales and delivery teams on the Sales Cloud, Service Cloud, Marketing Cloud and force.com platforms. The ideal candidate will have a background in identifying application requirements, functional design, configuration and testing.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Duties</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Gather and refine requirements for the creation of functional and technical design.</li>
<li>Conduct and lead requirements and solution design workshops.</li>
<li>Drive discovery conversations with our clients’ IT staff and business stakeholders.</li>
<li>Author design and configuration specifications based on client business and technical requirements.</li>
<li>Define process, create, and maintain workflow and related documentation</li>
<li>Evaluate client processes and technologies for the purpose of innovation and improvement.</li>
<li>Develop and maintain requirements documentation including RTM.</li>
<li>Provide configuration expertise for validation and testing.</li>
<li>Work with client user groups to identify and set priorities for future innovation.</li>
<li>Advise and guide clients towards best practices in Salesforce.com configuration, workflow, customization and data validation.</li>
<li>Participate in pre-sales efforts and strategic project planning as required.</li>
<li>Mentor and train, formally and informally, lesser experienced resources.</li>
<li>Develop project-specific training material.</li>
<li>Create and execute Functional Test Scripts</li>
<li>Conduct training sessions.</li>
<br>
</ul>
