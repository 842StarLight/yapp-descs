<p>
The <b>Payments, Virtual Solutions, and Innovation (PVSI)</b> team includes Operations, Consumer Credit Card, Retail Services, Consumer Deposits, the Innovation Group, Treasury Management, Merchant Services, Marketing and Wells Fargo Virtual Channels and the professional services teams that partner with these businesses. PVSI's focus is on delivering the next generation of payments and deposit capabilities and advancing our digital and online offerings to design new customer experiences and products. Our guiding principles are to start with the customer, move faster, and partner effectively.</p>
<p>
The Roles within Digital, Innovation, and Shared Services Control These newly created positions within the PVSI Business Control organization support the Digital, Innovation, and Shared Service businesses. These senior business risk consultants will be responsible for enabling business growth by managing risk. The candidate will be part of a team responsible for the identification, assessment, monitoring, and management of risks related to the development and delivery of Digital, Innovation and Shared Services products, services, and processes within the following business functions:</p>
<ul>
<li>Money Movement and Omni-Channel Development - supporting Wells Fargo Virtual Channels Digital Money Movement, Omni-Channel, and Application Programming Interfaces.</li>
<li>Digital Self-Service - supporting product development processes for digital self-service, consumer platform simplification and other key business initiatives.</li>
<li>Digital Sales &amp; Delivery - supporting processes related to WFVC Digital Acquisition, Activation, and Migration as well as processes related to digital servicing.</li>
<li>Group Program Management Office - supporting processes related to PVSI project and program management.</li>
<li>Ecosystems &amp; Innovation - supporting the Innovation Group within PVSI as well as the enterprise Digital and Innovation ecosystem planning.</li>
</ul>

Required Qualifications</b><br>
