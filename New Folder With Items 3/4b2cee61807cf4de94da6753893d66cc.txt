<h2>Business Development Representative, Sales Support</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Overview
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>The Business Development Sales Support Representative is responsible for making outbound calls into prospective clients within a targeted geographic territory. This function will use tele-prospecting with a multi-channel communication approach to targeted prospects and contacts. Individual will need to have an understanding of the Account Base Marketing concept.
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities
</b></h2>
<div><div><ul>
<li>Contact potential clients through cold calls and emails.</li>
<li>Set qualified appointments with decision makers.</li>
<li>Work closely with the outside sales team.</li>
<li>Have a genuine dialogue to gatekeepers and intermediaries to help build trust and credibility in order to engage with Decision Makers.</li>
<li>Thoroughly manage list of leads to ensure proper nurturing has been met.</li>
<li>Special projects based on territory focus, to include but not limited to event recruitment.</li>
<li>Possible Participation in any prospect branch events, open houses, and lunch learns.</li>
<li>Participate in branch WebEx staff meetings when needed.
</li>
</ul></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Qualifications
</b></h2>
<div><div><ul>
<li>Minimum Associates Degree or equivalent work experience.</li>
<li>Experience with software/printing/equipment/electronics sales preferred, but not required.</li>
<li>1-2 years’ experience in the marketing field, business field or other large firm with multiple concurrent projects.</li>
<li>Experience with software/ printing /equipment/ electronics sales preferred, but not required.</li>
<li>Desire to initiate a high volume of outbound calls.</li>
<li>Ability to handle questions and objections.</li>
<li>Ability to have a meaningful dialogue with C Level contacts.</li>
<li>Must be comfortable contacting both small and large organizations and know how to navigate through the organization.</li>
<li>Ability to process data and information quickly.</li>
<li>Ability to qualify the gatekeepers and answer questions.</li>
<li>Professional, outgoing, persistent personality traits.</li>
<li>Excellent interpersonal and verbal communication skills.</li>
<li>Must demonstrate in depth knowledge and understanding of the targeted audience.</li>
<li>Must quickly learn and adapt to the geographic location associated with leads provided.</li>
<li>Strong engagement with the decision makers.</li>
<li>Ability to work closely with outside sales reps.</li>
<li>Must be able to think quickly and demonstrate knowledge of the products.
</li>
</ul></div></div>
