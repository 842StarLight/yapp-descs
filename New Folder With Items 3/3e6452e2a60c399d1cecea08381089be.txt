<p>We are seeking an experienced <b>Systems Tester</b> for our <b>Microbial Solutions business </b>located in <b>Charleston, SC.</b></p>
<p>The Systems Tester performs system testing of products developed within and for Microbial Solutions- these products include instrumentation and software. They will work closely with all members of Product Development, Instrument Service and Technical Service teams through all phases of the product life cycle, including reliability testing and product release. Duties will have them performing testing methodologies and approaches to evaluate software development and induce failures that would be seen in customer environments, and assist in the development of solutions to prevent and reduce these failures.</p>
<p>The ideal candidate will have:</p>
<ul>
<li>
<b><i>Education:</i></b><i> </i>Bachelor’s degree (B.S.) or equivalent in Computer Science, Engineering, or Biomedical.</li>
<li>
<b><i>Experience:</i></b><b> </b>0-2 years in related field, with understanding in reliability, product service, product development, preferably in an instrument product development environment.</li>
<li>An equivalent combination of education and experience may be accepted as a satisfactory substitute for the specific education and experience listed above.</li>
</ul>
