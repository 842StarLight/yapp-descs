<h2>Customer Experience Scheduling Analyst</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>Job Overview</p>
<p>
The CX Scheduling Analyst is responsible for the daily efforts on providing a great experience for the CX customers and employees by effectively delivering real-time staffing management with the purpose of optimizing resources to achieve business objectives. Key functional responsibilities include the ability to demonstrate a strong understanding of how planning/forecasting staffing requirements translate to the creating of optimal schedules and real time management to support multi-department CX operations. This person should be able to create accurate forecasting and then scheduling to that forecast is crucial.</p>
<p>
Essential Duties and Responsibilities</p>
<ul>
<li>Create daily, weekly and monthly base schedules and forecasts utilizing scheduling software to analyze call volume trends.</li>
<li>Creating and maintaining all Contact Center schedules (shifts, in-services, training, lunch, etc.), and other Contact Center operations.</li>
<li>Monitors CX Dept. activities to ensure Contact Center efficiencies.</li>
<li>Perform continual analysis of current performance (noting recent historical trends) and conduct real-time re-forecasting to make necessary adjustments in staffing plans and coordinate with Operations teams to achieve service level goals / business objectives for a multi-site operation.</li>
<li>Maintain constant communication with Operations teams to coordinate needed staffing adjustments based on current and forecasted results.</li>
<li>Produces call volume forecasts on a daily, weekly, monthly and annual basis for multi-site operation and provides recommendations based on analysis.</li>
<li>Generates and evaluates staffing schedules, ensures data updates into scheduling system.</li>
<li>Provide daily/intra-day performance reports to leadership.</li>
<li>Assist with the design and programming of the call flow for the Contact Center as well as the fulfillment of telecommunication needs for all company employees.</li>
<li>Monitor telecommunications system and manage all Contact Center related functions for multiple sites.</li>
<li>Will be trained to assist with</li>
<li>Meet or exceed Customer Loyalty standards for sales, quality, productivity and attendance.</li>
<li>Promotes a positive, professional image in all aspects of interpersonal actions.</li>
<li>Assist with new catalog, product and promotional advertising proofing and review.</li>
<li>Build positive relationships with cross-functional departments.</li>
<li>Other related duties as assigned.</li>
</ul>
<p>Physical Requirements and Working Environment</p>
<ul>
<li>Ability to rarely lift and carry &lt;25 lbs. and rarely push/pull up to 50 lbs.</li>
<li>Constant sitting or standing dependent upon desk preference and rare walking</li>
<li>Rare bending/reaching, twisting</li>
<li>Frequent fine manipulation with fingers to operate computer
</li>
<li>Heated and air-conditioned indoor work</li>
</ul>
<p>Required Skills and Qualifications</p>
<ul>
<li>Proficiency in Microsoft Office, especially Word and Excel.</li>
<li>Strong analytical skills in call volume, employee workloads, and staffing optimization.</li>
<li>The ability to identify and solve process challenges through training, analysis of performance metrics, and use of advanced optimization techniques.</li>
<li>Able to remain calm during stressful situations.</li>
<li>High level of confidentiality and trust with employee information and management information.</li>
<li>Strong written and verbal communication skills.</li>
<li>Able to work independently and within a team with little supervision.</li>
<li>Experience with SOP platform preferred.</li>
</ul>
