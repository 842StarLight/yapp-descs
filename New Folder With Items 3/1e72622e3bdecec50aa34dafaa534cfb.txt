<h2>Demand and Inventory Planning Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>Job Description<br>
<br>
<p>The Demand &amp; Inventory Planning Manager supports the Supply Chain group by developing material forecasts and plans to provide consolidated material requirements for all ITS sites. Responsible for controlling the company’s investment in materials by minimizing inventory levels while maximizing material availability to ensure proper levels of inventory resources are maintained for optimum operating efficiency, cost effectiveness, and customer support. Additionally, the Demand &amp; Inventory Planning Manager is to manage and improve the demand planning and inventory control function within the Supply Chain group through the implementation of best practices and the full utilization of ERP (Enterprise Resource Planning) and other reporting systems.</p>
<p><b>
Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Directs Supply Chain Analyst team and manages projects in an effort to implement, and maintain world class supply chain analytic tools for purchasing, planning, sales and operations planning (S&amp;OP), ERP/MRP systems and others.</li>
<li>
Must be a model of customer service working with all levels of supply chain and other functions to develop and execute to inventory strategies in support of ITS’ goals and initiatives.</li>
<li>
Responsible for creating the strategy for the development of the Supply Chain Analyst function.</li>
<li>
Responsible for the management of ITS’ global inventory in support of maximizing turns potential, reduction of aged inventory, and global site optimization in support of the sales and gross profit goals.</li>
<li>
Directs and coaches team of Supply Chain Analysts to identify opportunities to maximize inventories, reduce costs, and improve overall supply chain performance.</li>
<li>
Monitors the effectiveness of the strategies and how well supply chain is executing to it via ITS’ management operating system.</li>
<li>
Develop data driven global supply chain strategies and lead the successful execution of these strategies across the supply chain.</li>
<li>
Supports ITS Airframes by providing timely and accurate information that allows the team to properly execute daily and future activities.</li>
<li>
Establishes measurements and analytics for the supply chain analyst and inventory performance via ITS’ management operating system.</li>
<li>
Interfaces with procurement to resolve issues regarding reporting, analytics, and other tools that support regular activities.</li>
<li>
Communicates regularly with executives and other managers flowing up relevant information regarding progress to strategy and initiatives</li>
<li>
Utilizes previous experience and working knowledge of MIS systems and data management to progress ITS’ sophistication and efficiency in inventory management.</li>
</ul>
</div>
