<h2>Product Training & Support Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div><b>PRODUCT TRAINING &amp; SUPPORT SPECIALIST</b></div>
<div></div>
<div><b>Essential Functions</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
<div>Provide technical support in person, over the phone, and electronically to internal and external customers prior to and after the sale regarding use of REDCOM products.</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Regularly prepare and deliver classroom training onsite and offsite to meet customer and internal employee needs.</div>
<ul>
<li>
<div>Continuously assess the training needs of the end-user, which includes current customers, prospective customers, and internal employees.</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Evaluate all methods of training delivery, such as in-person, virtual, self-paced online, video, etc. to optimize effectiveness and cost.</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Develop training objectives and training plans.</div>
</li>
<li>Develop supportive training aids, such as manuals, user guides, and instructional materials as necessary.</li>
<li>
<div>Set-up and breakdown of the training classroom to ensure facility is ready for training start dates and times.</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Develop alternative training methods if expected improvements are not seen</div>
</li>
<li>Meet with customers to determine training needs, necessary products and tools, coordinate training schedules, and agree upon deliverables.</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>
<div>Conduct research to answer routine and non-routine technical questions, as well as resolve technical problems remotely and onsite.</div>
</li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<div><b>Knowledge, Skills &amp; Abilities</b></div>
<div>
<ul>
<li>
<div>Working knowledge of traditional telecommunications, telecommunications test equipment and software, and servers and network connections.</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>The following IT Networking experience in a telecom environment is necessary to be successful:</div>
<ul>
<li>
<div>Session border controllers</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Proficient at Wireshark analysis</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Layer2/Layer3 switches &amp; routers</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Wireless and server technologies</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Various Hypervisors (e.g., Hyper-V, KVM, VMware ESXi, VMware Workstation, VirtualBox)</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Adult learning strategies</div>
</li>
</ul>
</li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<div><b>Required Skills</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
<div>Strong customer service and organizational skills</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Ability to develop and make presentations by clearly articulating highly technical concepts to a variety of audiences.</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Ability to adapt instructional style based on learnersâ€™ needs</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Ability to analyze group dynamics and interpret non-verbal messages to determine if/when learners are struggling to understand content. Ability to adapt delivery to engage learners that are struggling.</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Strong oral and written communication skills, strong presentation skills, and exceptional interpersonal skills.</div>
</li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<div><b>Special Working Conditions</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Must be available to travel independently, both domestically and internationally, as required</li>
<li>Must be available to answer customer calls on a rotational basis, 24/7, for 7 days at a time</li>
<li>Must be available to assist the on-call person with answering customer calls 24/7, 365 days a year, except during approved time off</li>
</ul>
