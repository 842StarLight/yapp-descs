<p><b>Overview: </b></p>
<p>The Referral Management Specialist will be responsible for qualifying all orders received via fax, email and via software. The specialists may have daily, direct correspondence with Customer Service representatives, sales reps, referrals and customers, as well as prioritizing workload by order of importance.</p>
<p><b>Responsibilities: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Develop and maintain working knowledge of current home medical equipment products and services offered by the company.</li>
<li>Receive all inbound orders via fax, email and software.</li>
<li>Use system to create patient accounts or add information to an existing patient account.</li>
<li>Verify insurance eligibility with any of the provided tools/websites.</li>
<li>Same and Similar referenced for Medicare orders.</li>
<li>Develop and maintain knowledge of insurance guidelines and qualifications for all products.</li>
<li>Review all documentation to verify qualifying information per insurance guidelines.</li>
<li>Create detailed notes, upload all documents and create shell orders for customer service representatives.</li>
<li>Participate in educational programs as required.</li>
<li>Perform other related duties as assigned.</li>
</ul>
