<h2>Salesforce Delivery Manager</h2>

<p><b>Who We Seek:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
<b>Passion Seekers. </b>You genuinely care about the work that you do and its impact on society.</li>
<li>
<b>Self-Starters. </b>You’re a go-getter who isn’t afraid to step up and disrupt the status quo.</li>
<li>
<b>Entrepreneurs.</b> You bring fresh ideas to the table, work hard, develop business and consistently seek new challenges.</li>
<li>
<b>Collaborators.</b> You’re a great contributor to a high performing team that accomplishes great feats for our clients.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>What You Will Do:</b></p>
<p>As a Delivery Manager, you will work directly with our clients to understand their business needs and translate them into effective software solutions. Our managers drive projects through the complete SDLC while building long-term relationships with our clients.</p>
<ul>
<li>Supervising and mentoring teams of consultants/analysts throughout the implementation life cycle.</li>
<li>Developing implementation plans for projects, including scope, timelines, resource requirements, work plans and cost estimates.</li>
<li>Identifying and resolving issues.</li>
<li>Monitoring and achieving project financial objectives.</li>
<li>Identifying and pursuing additional business opportunities.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>What You Need:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>5+ years’ experience managing implementing business systems software products in a project management capacity required.</li>
<li>Experience with Non Profit and/or Higher Education Institutions ideal.</li>
<li>COTS implementation experience.</li>
<li>Experience with CRM required, Salesforce.com strongly preferred.</li>
<li>Consulting project management skills including managing client relationships and communications.</li>
<li>Experience managing project teams including staff assignments, project scope, budget and schedule.</li>
<li>Ability to manage staff through entire SDLC to include, requirements analysis, conceptual design, detailed design, software development and configuration, quality assurance and training.</li>
<li>Understanding of data modeling, data mapping and/or data conversion.</li>
<li>Solid organizational and communication skills.</li>
<li>Experience supporting business development activities by attending client sales meetings and assisting the business development team with project proposals.</li>
<li>Enthusiasm and ability to drive results.</li>
<li>Degree in Information Technology or Business degree with IT experience required.</li>
</ul>
