<b>Responsibilities:</b><br>
-----------------
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>You will, prospect, identify, develop, negotiate and, ultimately, close new enterprise accounts by uncovering opportunities from new clients and current customers</li>
<li>Develop and lead pipeline to meet or exceed target</li>
<li>Lead the sales process from qualifying to closing</li>
<li>Maintain laser focus on target and named account acquisition</li>
<li>Thoroughly understand enterprise customer needs and effectively communicate Invoca value proposition</li>
<li>Build and execute sales strategies, presentations and software demonstrations for enterprise level customers</li>
<li>Ensure that salesforce.com is at all times updated with the most recent accurate forecasts, latest activities and next steps</li>
<li>Understand the business landscape and be able to articulate differentiators</li>
<li>Provide feedback to the marketing, SDR and product teams</li>
<li>Present effectively to external customers across all levels from marketing staff to C-suite executives</li>
<li>Thrive, working in a fast pace, hectic and intense sales environment</li>
<li>Effectively organize internal and external teams to work together to drive deals through complex sales cycles</li>
</ul>

<br>Qualifications
<br>--------------
<br><br>
We are seeking a proactive, responsible SaaS sales professional to develop and close opportunities by building relationships, face-to-face, phone, or your personal creative approach. You're ready to hop on a plane at a moment's notice to go close that big deal! Specific qualifications include:
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>7+ years of on-quota, B2B SaaS experience selling marketing tech and ad tech to large, complex organizations</li>
<li>Confident, experienced user of modern SaaS sales tech stack including Salesforce, LinkedIn, Slack, Salesloft, Bluejeans, various remote teamwork tools, etc.</li>
<li>Skilled at negotiating business terms with senior management and C-suite executives including MSSA, Order Forms, SOWs and other deal documentation.</li>
<li>Deep understanding of marketing and customer acquisition processes</li>
<li>Experience selling into Travel, Insurance, Telecom, Financial Services, Auto and Home Services verticals a plus</li>
<li>Stellar written and verbal communication skills, particularly the ability to write clear, persuasive proposals, emails and presentations.</li>
<li>Bachelor's degree, marketing or business preferred</li>
<li>Experience in a dynamic, high accountability start-up environment</li>
<li>You have high integrity and can credibility and the business ethics</li>
<li>Extensive Travel is required across the US and Canada (Road Warrior)</li>
</ul>
