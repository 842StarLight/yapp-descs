<div><b>Job Summary:<br>
</b></div>
<div></div>
<div>Sets up cost control system, monitors and controls costs and schedules on contracts requiring validated cost schedule control system. Performs analyses and prepares reports in order to ensure that contracts are within negotiated and agreed-upon parameters and government cost control guidelines. Prepares budgets and schedules for contract work and performs and/or assists in financial analyses such as funding profiles, sales outlook, and variance analysis. Prepares program plans to ensure program requirements and statement of work are captured and scheduled. Performs schedule risk assessments to identify and mitigate program cost and scheduling risks. Ensures adequate funding availability by maintaining accurate records of expenditures, directing preparation of expenditure projections, and submitting timely requests for additional funding to the government. Incorporates contractual changes into control systems by staying aware of outstanding work against each contract in order to maintain realistic contract cost and schedule baselines.<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<br>
<br>
<div>
<p><b>Duties and Responsibilities:
</b></p>
<p>This position is responsible for the collection, analysis and reporting of financial data on all assigned programs and their performance obligations. The position has a secondary role of analysis and reporting on pricing quotes, stockroom inventory, WIP and Sarbanes Oxley administration. This position will prepare estimate to completions (EAC) on assigned programs. You will partner with Program Managers to develop accurate weekly and monthly forecast/analysis of project financial results with focus on driving management understanding and commitment as well as prepare trend and variance reporting/analysis to Narda West management which identifies program issues that require management attention. Highlight areas at risk for not achieving expected financial or operational results. You will assist in monthly, quarterly, semi-quarterly, and yearly financial schedules and reviews and work with the Program Manager to provide project earned value management reporting and analysis that provides insight on the program operational, schedule, and cost performance. You will prepare journal entries/cost entries for WIP/COGS or General Ledger Activity, support customer and government audits as they pertain to proposal cost inputs and develop program time-phased budgets with focus on driving program and functional management commitment. You will work with the Program Manager to achieve cash milestone billings and identify, suggest, and assist in development and implementation of improved cost tools and systems, as needed. You will provide updates of policies and procedures related to cost processes, and manage Sarbanes Oxley testing to improve effectiveness. This position supports miscellaneous projects for Vice President of Finance, as required. You will prepare trend analysis to test reasonableness of budgets and provide leadership and promote collaboration over various internal functional areas to achieve financial goals. You will be the subject matter expert for ASC 606 implementation and will be required to access all new contracts to determine the type of revenue recognition method to use as well as properly ascertain the performance obligations for each contract.
</p>
<p>Assists with related special projects as required and other duties as assigned by manager.
