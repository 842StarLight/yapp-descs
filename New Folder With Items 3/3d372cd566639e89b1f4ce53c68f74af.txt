<h2>Account Executive</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>Are you driven by achieving goals and advancing your career?
<br>
<br>
Are you interested in working for an innovative company with a laser focus on finding and developing new sales professionals?
<br>
<br>
"Live here. Work here". That's what we are all about: helping people find rewarding careers, where they live. It's about connecting people with a better job to provide them with a better life; it's about helping an employer find that "right" person to make their team complete: it's about helping the people around you, right here in your community.
<br>
<br>
Our approach to connecting people with employers has helped us develop an extremely successful niche in the online community. Today, Infosoft Group operates over 600 employment and diversity web sites (i.e. MilwaukeeJobs.com, JobsinMinneapolis.com) and provide services to thousands of employers throughout the United States. Our offices are located in Milwaukee and Minneapolis.
<br>
<br>
As an Account Executive, you will sell recruiting solutions to HR professionals. We are looking for energetic, self-motivated, passionate sales professionals to join our elite team!<br>
</p>
<br>
<b>Responsibilities
</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Identify and qualify prospective customers and key decision makers
</li>
<li>Secure business meetings through cold-calling, email, and inbound leads
</li>
<li>Effectively manage sales pipelines and drive urgency in sales processes
</li>
<li>Generate new business by establishing key value differentiation while conducting online business presentations
</li>
<li>Execute effective sales strategies to meet and exceed sales quotas
</li>
</ul>
<br>
<b>Qualifications
</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Bachelors degree or higher or strongly preferred. In lieu of a degree, additional work experience of 5+ years may be substituted
</li>
<li>Requires 1 to 3 years previous sales experience, preferably in business-to-business sales
</li>
<li>SaaS sales experience a plus
</li>
<li>Excellent communication, written and verbal skills
</li>
<li>Self-starter and team player, with the ability to thrive in a fast-paced environment
</li>
<li>Excellent problem solving and time management skills
</li>
<li>Experience working with a CRM system or enterprise technologies a plus
</li>
<li>Technology savvy and quick to adopt to new sales tools and processes
</li>
</ul>
