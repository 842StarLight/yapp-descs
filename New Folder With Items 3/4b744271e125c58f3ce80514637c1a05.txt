<p>Senior Director, Sales Development (SDR Team)</p>
<p>The Senior Director, Sales Development (SDR Team) will be responsible for leading a Sales Development Rep team that develops and expands our key markets. The ideal candidate has 3-5 years’ experience leading an SDR team focused on B2B enterprise software sales. This is an opportunity to join a high growth company that is rapidly expanding into new markets. We are looking for a high-performance self-starter who wants to take the team to the next level. This is your opportunity to build out a world-class team, framework and tech stack.
</p>
<p>The SDR team is responsible for sales growth as measured by the creation of net new sales opportunities and pipeline. Ownership of new business prospecting, territory planning, campaign ideation, pre-call planning, and discovery meetings. The director is responsible for hiring and training a high output team of top performers. Experience in B2B software sales to education, public sector and healthcare targets is desired but not required. This role should understand how to penetrate new markets and launch new product effectively.</p>
<p>
You can expect a dynamic environment that cultivates and challenges, while providing valuable career-building experience. The role is ideal for a highly motivated, can-do attitude individual who can network and establish relationships, both internally and externally, influence decision makers at all levels and wishes to immerse themselves into the commercial dynamics of a successful company. You will enjoy coaching, spearheading campaigns, and are adept at running meaningful analytics to drive success. You will work in a professional environment, with ample opportunity to be exposed to new tools, skills, and different departments across our entire business.<br>
<br>
This role reports to the CMO and works closely with sales leadership daily.</p>
<p>
Responsibilities</p>
<ul>
<li>Develop, deploy, and lead a best of class team that can unlock the full market potential of the TeamDynamix suite of solutions.
</li>
<li>Actively grow and manage your team’s sales pipeline.</li>
<li>Educate, train, guide and support your team members to develop them into successful account managers; help establish clear career pathing the sales organization.</li>
<li>Understand and implement insight selling / solution selling structure – working closely with the core marketing team to amplify existing campaigns.</li>
<li>Creatively drive to identify new areas to lever new/existing partnerships into additional product and/or service offerings that can generate incremental revenue and margin for the company.</li>
<li>Coach team to identify, prospect, qualify, negotiate and close deals in an aggressive timeframe.</li>
<li>Assist with developing business models to analyze and prioritize deals.</li>
<li>Occasional travel required.</li>
</ul>
<p>About Us</p>
<p>TeamDynamix offers Service and Project Management together on a single cloud-based platform. The solution transforms IT from order taker to strategic innovator. Organizations in the public sector, education, and healthcare leverage the solution to improve IT maturity, optimize resources, and deliver enhanced end-user service. The platform delivers IT Service Management (ITSM), Project Portfolio Management (PPM), and Enterprise Service Management (ESM) together in one solution. More at TeamDynamix.com, @TDXBuzz, LinkedIn.</p>
