QUALIFICATIONS:<br>
<br>
Bachelors degree in Business Administration/Management. Seven to ten years of progressively increasing experience in the management and operations of a retail or multi-branch organization, with three years' progressively more responsible supervisory or management experience required. Advanced understanding of salesmanship required. Demonstrated leadership and coaching skills required, with the ability to build a team environment. Strong written and verbal communication skills required. Must exercise good judgment in problem solving and decision-making. Ability to build effective working relationships with all levels of management and members. Complete familiarity with the Microsoft Office Platform of products and all business line software required.<br>
