The Warehouse is responsible for performing warehousing activities, inventory control, and recordkeeping.
<br><br>
Responsibilities
<br><br>
1. Check in all daily incoming shipments for all vendors.
<br><br>
2. Put away all incoming stock in the appropriate bin location and insure that all parts put into stock are properly labeled.
<br><br>
3. Check in all incoming inter-branch shipments from all branches.
<br><br>
4. Update incoming stock and incoming inter-branch shipment packing slips in computer and generate appropriate picking lists and reports.
<br><br>
5. Pull all customer orders as indicated on the picking list and place in either will call, package for shipment, or dispatch for delivery.
<br><br>
6. Maintain all "Will Call" parts.
<br><br>
7. Maintain all junk core inventory.
<br><br>
8. Dispatch Parts Delivery Drivers.
<br><br>
9. Process and maintain all returns.
<br><br>
10. File freight claims for any shortages or damaged parts received.
<br><br>
Basic Qualifications
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>High school diploma or general education diploma (GED).</li>
<li>Three months' related experience and/or training; or equivalent combination of education and experience.</li>
<li>If required to drive, for any reason, on behalf of the company, must possess a current and valid driver license with a driving record that meets the insurability guidelines set forth by the company's insurance carrier.</li>
<li>Computer literate with the willingness to learn the software programs utilized by the Company.</li>
</ul>
