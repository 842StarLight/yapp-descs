<h2>Marketing, Sales, and Communications Coordinator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<b>MARKETING, SALES, &amp; COMMUNICATIONS COORDINATOR– </b>Job # NAAM005
<p><b>
</b></p>
<p><b>Reports to: </b>Executive Director</p>
<p><b>
Schedule:</b> Full Time</p>
<p><b>
Location:</b> Seattle, WA (Remote Possible)</p>
<p>
</p>
<p><b>About the Role</b></p>
<p>
</p>
<p>The Northwest African American Museum seeks a dynamic, creative, and experienced brand ambassador to manage Marketing, Public Relations, Communications, and Online Media Relations. He/She will develop stunning marketing, proposal, and presentation materials and visuals to help lead the rhythm of NAAM’s cultural and artistic offerings.</p>
<p>
</p>
<p><b>What You Will Do</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Create and execute overall marketing and public relations collateral, plans, and strategies.</li>
<li>Secure local, regional, national, and international media coverage of the museum’s exhibitions, collections, public programs, fundraising activities, and other initiatives.</li>
<li>Lead copywriting for press releases, marketing materials, graphics, and other forms of media outreach.</li>
<li>Work with tourism and hospitality partners to promote the museum as a cultural destination of choice.</li>
<li>Develop cross-promotional partnerships and collaborations with key arts and business organizations.</li>
<li>Work with outside vendors including advertising representatives, PR consultants, etc.</li>
<li>Manage media buys and negotiations for media sponsorships for events and programs.</li>
<li>Manage the production and approval of collateral, including rack cards, signage, flyers, advertisements, and copy for radio and television spots.</li>
<li>Manage brand enforcement ensuring consistency of design and messaging for all marketing campaigns and communications.</li>
<li>Manage all aspects of museum retail operations.</li>
<li>Lead marketing meetings with other staff members ensuring all materials and campaigns are organized, intentional, effective, and sustainable.</li>
<li>Maintain positive relations with media and build ongoing public interest and support for the museum.</li>
<li>Facilitate audience and visitor growth in support of the museum’s mission and goals by managing all outgoing and incoming communication (e-blasts, museum newsletters, etc.).</li>
<li>Thoughtfully position the museum as an active participant in all relevant social media platforms by facilitating the museum’s social media platforms</li>
<li>Responsible for managing the museum’s website content and technical operations.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>What You Will Bring</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Deep knowledge of, passion about, and appreciation for African American history and culture.</li>
<li>Commitment to accuracy and attention to detail.</li>
<li>Strong ability to be creative and think critically.</li>
<li>Self-motivation with strong organizational skills.</li>
<li>Ability to multitask and meet deadlines.</li>
<li>Available to work evenings and weekends.</li>
<li>A knack for and successful track of promoting brands of choice.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>What Makes You Qualified</b></p>
<br>
<p><b>
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>3-5 years’ experience in a related field.</li>
<li>Bachelor’s degree in Communications, Marketing or a related field, Masters’ preferred.</li>
<li>Demonstrated knowledge in planning and conducting marketing and communications campaigns.</li>
<li>Demonstrated knowledge of brand management.</li>
<li>Optimal written, oral, interpersonal, and communication and editing skills.</li>
<li>Ability to perform effectively with a wide range of constituencies across a diverse communities.</li>
<li>Demonstrated mastery of social media platforms</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p>To apply for this opportunity, submit your resume and a cover letter detailing what about your KSAs makes you ideal for this role to: rwilson@naamnw.org.</p>
