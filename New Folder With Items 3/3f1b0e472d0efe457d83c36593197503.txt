<h2>Order Processor</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<b>Customer Service:</b><b>:</b>
<div>
<ul><li>Excellent phone and communication skills</li></ul>
<ul><li>Primary team fielding inbound calls</li></ul>
<ul><li>Ability to utilize multiple order entry and tracking systems simultaneously</li></ul>
<ul><li>Ability to handle multiple priorities; shift between job functions i.e.: data entry, handling customer inquiries, etc.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Answer phones, monitor emails, respond to customers inquiries, Follow up with Sales Reps./Customers over inquiries, order change requests, etc.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Complete all post warehouse administrative functions. Tracking info, Procure, etc., Complete/Close. This must be done no later than 6:00 am the day after an order is completed</li></ul>
</div>
<b>Work Order:</b><b>:</b>
<div>
<ul><li>Take orders from individuals or large clients and input those orders into software so that order fillers or other warehouse associates can complete the process and get materials moving to the customer.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Print WO to appropriate department</li></ul>
<ul><li>Ensure all due dates, shipping details, and special instructions have been reviewed and clearly noted</li></ul>
<ul><li>Enter/ transfer orders into system as necessary</li></ul>
</div>Note: Management can always add or delete the responsibilities based on the future needs of the company.<br>
