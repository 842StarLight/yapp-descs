our humble roots 20 years ago, Eagle Eye has now expanded to over 45,000 square feet becoming a<br>
premium retail partner for the best brands in the outdoor industry. As a leader in the retail community in<br>
South Alabama, we use outdoor retail as a platform to encourage outdoor pursuits, environmental<br>
stewardship and fulfillment of our community mission statement. It is always our goal to offer our customers<br>
the very best quality with unmatched radical customer service<br>

<b>Merchandiser Job Description Summary</b><br>
Under the Supervision of the Head Merchandiser, a Merchandising Associate is responsible for providing an<br>
excellent in-store experience for customers by ensuring their responsible departments are well stocked,<br>
displayed, signed, and visually appealing. The goal of this position is to use visual merchandising to drive<br>
branding, support inventory management, meet sales objectives, and maximize profitability.<br>
<br>
<br>
<b>RESPONSIBILITIES</b><br>
<b>
 </b></p>
<p>Supports a culture within merchandising team that ensures radical customer service and high<br>
performance is the focus of all activities. Greatness at Eagle Eye is defined by one’s ability to serve<br>
others – both internally and externally.
 </p>
<p>Provides quality &amp; accurate work, achieves goals of the merchandising department, and ensures the<br>
customer is at the center of every decision made.
 </p>
<p>Arranges and maintains attractive visual presentations. Follows a floor plan and/or directive of Head<br>
Merchandiser to merchandise an area in preparation for seasonal changes and special events.
 </p>
<p>Creates &amp; maintains store fixture and mannequin presentations.
 </p>
<p>Maintains &amp; presents visual displays in a compelling and exciting manner in accordance with<br>
directives (written or verbal) from Head Merchandiser.
 </p>
<p>Implements store signage programs
 </p>
<p>Continuously checks selling floor for proper merchandise presentation and performs general<br>
housekeeping, and straightening as needed. Rotates product periodically on fixtures &amp; displays to<br>
maximize style exposure.
 </p>
<p>Maintains organization and cleanliness of fixture storage/workroom.
 </p>
<p>Keeps track of prop inventory and reports needs/discrepancies to Head Merchandiser.
 </p>
<p>Executes promotional &amp; floor change calendar seamlessly and efficiently
 </p>
<p>Assists with special projects &amp; events as assigned. Assists with marketing photo &amp; video shoots as<br>
needed.
 </p>
<p>Complies with inventory control procedures &amp; company standards.
 </p>
<p>Knowledgeable of and follows all store safety procedures.<br>
</p>
