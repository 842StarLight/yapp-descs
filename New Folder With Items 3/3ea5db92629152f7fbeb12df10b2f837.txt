<h2>Manager, Marketing and Communications</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Overview<br>
</b></h2>
<div></div>
<div><div><div>
<div>Under the direction of the Executive Director/VP of Customer Relations and Communications, the Manager, Marketing and Communications is responsible for the planning and execution of all agency sales and marketing, social media and internal and external communications to keep employees and stakeholders informed about LBT’s programs and services. The Marketing and Communications Manager serves as the agency’s Public Information Officer and directly supervises one staff person: A Marketing and Communications Coordinator.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
The Marketing and Communications Manager is a member of a critical cross departmental team including community relations and customer care.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div><b>
CLOSING DATE: 10/22/19</b></div>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities<br>
</b></h2>
<div></div>
<div><div><div>
<div>
<b>Essential Duties and Responsibilities </b><b>include the following:</b>
</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<ul>
<li>
Prepares annual agency marketing plan, develops specific goals and objectives and strategic plans</li>
<li>
Develops and implements internal and external communications plans and campaigns with the goal of building community and employee engagement</li>
<li>
Manages LBT’s social media presence, responds to comments and develops engagement strategies</li>
<li>
Builds industry partnerships to increase ridership and pass sales</li>
<li>
Manages all agency media relations, including issuing press releases, responding to inquiries and arranging interviews, press events and other programs to increase LBT’s media exposure</li>
<li>
Promotes and maintains LBT’s brand identity on all digital, print and other collateral</li>
<li>
Works with the Board Secretary, executive leaders and others to develop compelling reports, presentations and other communication collateral</li>
<li>
In conjunction with the Community Relations Manager, manages LBT’s website and content. Collaborates with community relations staff on social media</li>
<li>
Works with creative agencies and consultants and directs advertising, creative and promotional work</li>
<li>
Coordinates production of all fare media including monthly passes, tickets and coupons</li>
<li>
Works closely with other departments, stakeholders and other transit agencies on joint projects and tasks</li>
<li>
Keeps well informed on the agency’s service area, its demographics, diversity, development, challenges and community events</li>
<li>
Ensures that each level of supervision and all employees are made aware of the elements of the safety program, and that those elements are implemented. Assures that proper training is being provided, and that employees are working in a safe manner.</li>
<li>
Spearheads programs to create conduits to external partners for furthering LBT’s growth, revenue and corporate recognition</li>
<li>
Works collaboratively other LBT departments to create programs and collateral material to enhance the agency’s image and brand both internally and externally</li>
<li>
Complies with all safety regulations as stated in LBT’s safety program, and reports safety related accidents, incidents and unsafe work conditions; attends safety training programs as required.</li>
<li>
Other duties as assigned</li>
</ul>
