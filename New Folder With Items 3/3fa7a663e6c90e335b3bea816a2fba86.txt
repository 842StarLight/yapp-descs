<h2>AWS Alliance Manager - Enterprise West</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">Mission
<br><br>
This role will manage and build alliances with Amazon Web Services Enterprise West teams to achieve greater scale across the western North America enterprise space.
<br><br>
The ideal candidate will have deep experience working with AWS or other public cloud partners, deep analytical and business strategy acumen, experience with platform businesses as well as sales enablement and incentives; and an understanding of how software ecosystems are built, where the leverage lies.
<br><br>
Outcomes
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Faster Revenue Growth: Exceed sourced and influenced revenue goals with AWS and Databricks sales field</li>
<li>Accelerated Joint GTM: Align AWS sales teams with Databricks GTM investments by exceeding customer engagement metrics for virtual and field customer events</li>
<li>Partner Field sales impact: Establish close working relationships with AWS ENT teams to raise awareness of Databricks and exceed goals around sales enablement and education</li>
<li>Greater Desire to Partner: Scale the AWS business through the engagement, cosell and training of consulting partners on AWS</li>
<li>Creatively co-sell with AWS: Manage and grow key pipeline across internal and external field teams while diplomatically navigating product co-opetition between Databricks &amp; AWS teams</li>
</ul>

<br><br>

<li>Proven, tangible results working with public cloud partners, AWS highly preferred</li>
<li>Strong product instincts and ability to identify opportunities for Databricks with partners as well as ability to work collaboratively with Databricks sales teams</li>
<li>Ability to flourish with minimal guidance, be proactive, and handle uncertainty and ambiguity</li>
