<h2>Enterprise Account Executive</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">Copado is a fast-growing organization that believes in its people to enable our business to scale and grow. Operating out of WeWork locations around the world, we provide our people with flexible working environments and benefits for our international organization. Copado is looking for talented, hardworking individuals with great energy, leadership, and initiative to drive awareness for one of the fastest growing application in the Salesforce ecosystem.
<br><br>
Enterprise Account Executive must demonstrate the ability to develop a strategy for targeting an assigned channel or geography. He or she must build an effective go to market strategy to drive awareness, build advocacy, engage in account planning and identify and manage new Sales opportunities by working proactively and collaboratively with our supporting teams.
<br><br>
As a Copado Enterprise Account Executive, a typical day might include:
<br><ul>
<li>Develop and drive their channel strategy for the assigned market.</li>
<li>Build and convey compelling value propositions to channel partners in order to generate pursuit opportunities for Copado.</li>
<li>Track, analyse and communicate key metrics and business trends as they relate to your channels, in region and globally.</li>
<li>Supporting all aspects of partner relationships from program architecture through implementation, including content strategy, joint customer targeting, events, etc.</li>
</ul>
<b>This job might be for you if:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>You have been selling technology and IT Solutions to the enterprise for that last 7+ years.</li>
<li>You have been working in the Salesforce ecosystem for the last 3+ years in a sales capacity.</li>
<li>You have a demonstrated ability to think strategically about business, product, and technical challenges.</li>
<li>You pride yourself on your ability to engage with all kinds of people and answer questions by trying on different perspectives, researching and leveraging relationships.</li>
<li>You’re the queen or king of multitasking and working with shifting priorities and uncertainty in a startup environment.</li>
<li>You aren’t afraid to pick up the phone and reach out to a company we’ve never worked with before, and at the same time, you are comfortable resolving complex problems.</li>
</ul>
<b>Bonus points for:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Previous experience with DevOps solutions.</li>
<li>Previous experience with Salesforce platform solutions.</li>
</ul>
<b>You might be a fit for Copado if:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>You are energetic and enjoy working in a fast-paced global environment.</li>
<li>You love working in an agile environment and continuously strive for improvement.</li>
<li>You take ownership of your work and committed to making our customers successful.</li>
</ul>
</div>
