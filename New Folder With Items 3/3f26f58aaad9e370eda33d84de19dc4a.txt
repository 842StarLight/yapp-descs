<h2>Commercial Attorney</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Elevate's Customer, a <b>Global Safety Products Manufacturer, </b>is looking for a Commercial Attorney to work part-time for 20 hours per week for a period of 3-4 months. This role will be mostly remote, and requires work on site in their Cranberry Township, PA headquarters one day per week.</p>
<p><b>
You might be the right person if you...</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Have experience working with clients in the manufacturing industry.</li>
<li>
Have prior in‐house legal experience (temporary or permanent).</li>
<li>
Are familiar with contract review technology tools and contract management systems.</li>
<li>
Have the ability to work one (1) day each week at company headquarters in Cranberry Twp., PA.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Specifically, you will:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Draft and/or edit commercial agreements and contract templates for various business units and functional teams. Main business clients include Sourcing, Sales, Finance, Marketing, Software‐as‐a‐Service business, and Product Development teams.</li>
<li>
Negotiate commercial agreements, including supply agreements, confidentiality agreements, terms and conditions of sale, channel partner agreements, SaaS Subscription Agreements, and more.</li>
<li>
Ensure executed agreements are appropriately filed in a contract management system.</li>
<li>
Advise business leaders on day‐to‐day commercial contract and legal questions that may arise.</li>
<li>
Provide weekly report to Associate General Counsel, Corporate Secretary, and Chief Compliance Officer.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Experience:</b></p>
<ul><li>
3+ years' experience practicing general commercial law.</li></ul>
<p><b>
Qualifications:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
J.D. required</li>
<li>
Licensed to practice law and in good standing in at least one U.S. Jurisdiction.</li>
</ul>
