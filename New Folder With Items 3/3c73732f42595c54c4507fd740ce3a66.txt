<h2>Principal Solution Architect</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>Do you want to help a company grow from $150M to $500M or more through Digital Transformation? Smart Care is seeking a dynamic and proactive <b>Lead Solution Architect</b> to be the overall Technical Lead for a major digital transformation effort encompassing Field Service Management (FSM), Schedule/Dispatch Optimization, Customer Relationship Management (CRM), and Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP). Future initiatives will include Artificial Intelligence (AI), Merged Reality, and IoT among numerous other possibilities.</p>
<p></p>
<p>
We are looking for a Lead Solution Architect to join our growing Technology team and build out the next generation of technologies for our rapidly growing organization. <b>The individual in this position will be the overall technology lead (Solution Architect) for all technology initiatives in the organization.</b> The ideal candidate is a hands-on platform builder with significant experience in architecting integrated platforms. They must have strong technical expertise in a variety of technologies and the proven ability to fashion robust scalable solutions. This person should embody a passion for continuous improvement and test-driven development. This is a hands-on position, both developing and leading. The Solution Architect will work in a collaborative team environment with business partners, product owners and contract development teams. They will lead and participate in the full development life cycle in the creation and enhancements of system functionality.</p>
<p></p>
<p>
To be successful in this role, you must have experience working with a variety of technologies including Java, C#, XML, Oracle and/or SQL Server database platforms, web interface technologies in a service-oriented architecture platform. You also need to demonstrate a proven track record of building strong technical customer relationships, working with cross-functional teams and connecting customer requirements with geographically-distributed delivery teams to ensure a high level of customer satisfaction. The Solution Architect is a key member of the delivery team, supporting the upstream and downstream technical interfaces during the entire system delivery process.</p>
<p></p>
