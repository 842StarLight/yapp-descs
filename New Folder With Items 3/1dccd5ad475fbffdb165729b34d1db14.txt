<p><b>PROPERTY MANAGER – </b></p>
<p>Koplar Properties is currently seeking a full time, experienced Property Manager and Leasing Specialist to join our Residential Team. This position is located in the Central West End (St. Louis) area. Ideal candidate must possess good communication and computer skills. We provide a highly competitive benefits package including medical premiums plus offer dental, vision, HSA, and 401K with company match. Please send salary requirements along with resume.</p>
<p><b>Our pledge to our employees...</b> We are dedicated to developing our employees personally as well as professionally. Initial and ongoing training for all employees, in all positions, keeps our team members on the cutting edge and ready for promotion.</p>
<p><b>SUMMARY OF RESPONSIBILITIES: </b></p>
<p>The Property Manager is responsible for the successful day-to-day administrative, sales and marketing operations of the community while ensuring that company standards are achieved and excellent customer service is delivered. We're looking for an individual with a strong work ethic, a can-do attitude, and a strong focus on customer service. Final candidates must be able to successfully complete a background investigation. Current and or previous employer will be contacted for verification of work ethic. Please provide references.</p>
<p><b>ESSENTIAL JOB FUNCTIONS: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Showcasing the property to prospects and converting them into qualified residents;</li>
<li>Excellent communication skills - answering phones with a smile</li>
<li>Knowledge of state law as it relates to fair housing</li>
<li>Exhibit strong leadership skills; time management skills and ability to prioritize wisely</li>
<li>Responsible for all paperwork required to get application approval; lease execution, and file maintenance,</li>
<li>Day-to-day operations of the property,</li>
<li>Marketing initiatives for rent growth, resident retention, and outreach.</li>
<li>Regularly inspects the property ensure company standards are being met; identifies areas of improvement and offers suggestions to improve the efficiency, productivity and profitability of the property.</li>
<li>Prepares, monitors and analyzes traffic logs, budget guidelines, renewal information, marketing data, etc. to be able to give up to date and proper information as requested.</li>
<li>Develops resident retention by communicating and responding to residents in a timely manner and taking necessary action to address service issues; monitors resident satisfaction in person or through follow-up calls and emails to ensure a positive living experience.</li>
<li>Works with Property Maintenance Manages in coordinating requests for repairs and maintenance and ensures appropriate response to resident requests for service orders; perform pre-move-in and post-move-out walk-throughs with Property Maintenance Manager with proper documentation.</li>
<li>Prepares related weekly/monthly property operational reports to include, but not limited to leasing, renewal, and financial.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>SPECIAL REQUIREMENTS: </b> <b>Physical Demands</b>: The employee will be regularly required to sit; stand; walk; reach using feet, legs, back, arms and hands; talk and hear. The employee may occasionally be required to climb. The employee will be required to work at a personal computer and talk on the phone for extended periods of time. Must possess the ability to detect auditory and/or visual alarms and be available to work extended/flexible hours and weekends, as needed.</p>
<p><b>Attendance/Travel</b>: This position is a full-time position and may require the availability to work some nights, weekends, and occasional holidays. Regular attendance and active participation/planning at company functions and events.</p>

<li>Ability to thrive in a fast-paced, competitive environment</li>

</ul>
