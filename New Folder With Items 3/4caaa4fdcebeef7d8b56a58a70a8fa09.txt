<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Job Requisition:</b></h2>
ServiceNow Solutions Architect<p></p>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Job Description:</b></h2>
<p>
Leidos is seeking a ServiceNow Solutions Architect for the Civil IT shared services team. The shared services team is an internal Leidos Service Center that supports programs across Leidos with IT infrastructure and support requirements. These services include cloud services, data center services, and managed help desk services, among others.</p>
<p></p>
<p>
The ServiceNow Solutions Architect will drive innovation and productivity for a growing ServiceNow practice that focuses on implementation and continuous improvement of ServiceNow implementations for federal agency clients. The successful candidate will be adept at the following:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Reviewing existing implementations and implementing improvements to support already contracted work</li>
<li>
Recommending additional enhancements to provide added value to existing implementations</li>
<li>
Provide pre-sales technical guidance and support to proposal generation for new projects</li>
<li>
Ensure security architecture is consistent with federal standards and industry best practice</li>
<li>
End-to-end architecture and implementation of ServiceNow out of box and customized solutions to support clients</li>
<li>
Synthesis with ITIL compliance and industry best practice process design</li>
<li>
Communication and coordination within a matrix organization to ensure on-time delivery of project requirements</li>
<li>
Managing multiple projects simultaneously and coordinating project activities in a way that minimizes project risks and issues in a client services organization.</li>
<li>
Management of reporting, KPIs, dashboards, project plans, and project documentation from a solutions architecture perspective</li>
<li>
Facilitate requirements discussions with internal and external stakeholders and third-party contributors in support of lead project managers</li>
<li>
Supporting development of training initiatives for end users</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p>
Required Qualifications:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Bachelor’s Degree in related field and 12 years prior relevant experience with solutions architecture of ITSM solutions</li>
<li>
Experience with ServiceNow architecture across multiple product lines (ITSM, ITOM, ITBM, Asset Management, CSM, etc.)</li>
<li>
Experience with migration from legacy toolsets into ServiceNow</li>
<li>
Experience with green field development of ServiceNow solutions</li>
<li>
Experience with integration of legacy tools with ServiceNow</li>
<li>
Experience building custom automation workflows, RPA, and development of customized modules</li>
<li>
Familiarity with organizational change management including end user training</li>
<li>
Experience creating and managing project documentation per project goals and objectives</li>
<li>
Demonstrates a high degree of customer service and team work</li>
<li>
Ability to manage multiple projects simultaneously</li>
<li>
Knowledge of Agile/Scrum concepts with experience operating in Scrum teams</li>
<li>
Knowledge of ITIL concepts</li>
<li>
Experience working in a highly matrixed organization</li>
<li>
Must have or be able to obtain a Public Trust Clearance.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p>
Preferred Qualifications:</p>
<ul>
<li>
ServiceNow Implementation Specialist Certification</li>
<li>
ITIL v3/V4 Foundations Certification, advanced certifications preferred</li>
<li>
Scrum Certification (PSM or CSM)</li>
<li>
Experience working with the federal government</li>
</ul>
