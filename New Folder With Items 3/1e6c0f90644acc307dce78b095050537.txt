<h2>Technical Account Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">Scope
<br>The Technical Account Specialist is responsible for maintaining and coordinating the support needs from a technical account perspective, providing project implementation, field support, and customer services to ensure customer success.
<br><br>
Responsibilities include, but not limited to…
<br><br>
Development &amp; implementation of preventive and predictive maintenance programs for assigned key accounts.
<br><br>
Providing expert level consultation services and assistance to resolve complex problems relating to products, technologies and industry workflow processes. Including implementing root cause analysis to identify and correcting system issues.
<br><br>
Provide technical field support for live broadcasts/events. Including travel to/from customer facilities and event-based locations. Candidate must be able to accurately document configurations and workflows implemented, as it relates to EVS and 3rd party products.
<br><br>
<b>This position is a:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Hands on a tactical role that requires a broad technical knowledge of EVS &amp; Broadcast workflows.</li>
<li>Act as the customers’ advocate for internal and external escalation paths, determining, communicating, &amp; setting effective goals</li>
<li>Responsible for defining professional service needs on a project basis by authoring statements of work and directly managing the delivery of professional services at client companies</li>
</ul>
The position requires the ability to work in a dynamic, face-paced environment. Prior experience in similar environments is highly desirable and sought after. The position works cross-functionally with all departments to maximize customer satisfaction.
<br><br>
This role is a direct customer facing position that will directly convey the EVS message and EVS Product families.
<br>Tasks and responsibilities
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Providing advanced level technical consultation while resolving technical inquiries relating to the EVS product families</li>
<li>Frequent on-site customer visits in the North/South America Region</li>
<li>Conduct advanced operational &amp; technical knowledge, including guidance &amp; training, (both internal/external).</li>
<li>Training &amp; workflow guidance as it relates to Sales/Support</li>
<li>Act as a key point of contact for assigned accounts by providing knowledge of entire end to end workflows</li>
<li>Provide client support and technical issue resolution on-site, via phone, and email.</li>
<li>Provide accurate documentation of workflow analysis &amp; requirements for key product initiatives.</li>
<li>Quick decision-making skills and the ability to act independently while in a broadcast environment</li>
<li>Categorizing and submitting customer issues through the customer support ticket system</li>
<li>Act as Product contact with regards to products assigned</li>
<li>Provide leadership and in-depth product knowledge</li>
<li>Maintain up to date knowledge and coordinate with HQ Product managers on releases and hardware changes</li>
<li>Monitor product defects, trends, and issue occurrences in the region</li>
<li>Properly document encountered problems and their solutions submitted through our internal ticketing system.</li>
<li>Including technical documentation notes &amp; catalog into a knowledge base for Tier 1-2 usage</li>
<li>Train and provide information to the Support/Sales team on issues, known bugs, and new product workflows.</li>
<li>Coordinate and reports to Senior Management in regards to key accounts &amp; field support needs/status’</li>
<li>Including regular updated of outstanding issues.</li>
<li>Interacting with Product Specialist &amp; Senior Management to continually evaluate and improve product support solutions &amp; strategies.</li>
<li>Capturing and clearly documenting customer requests and product needs for ongoing development.</li>
<li>Commissioning &amp; configuring high level, complex proprietary systems.</li>
<li>Coordinate with Sales/Pre-Sales to attain expectations and workflows desired by customers</li>
<li>Work closely with Sales and Technical Support staff to assist in implementing appropriate policies.</li>
<li>Perform cross-functional role with Sales, Pre-Sales, Project &amp; Support, as required.</li>
<li>Remain engaged with obtaining an advanced understanding of application operations, as related to EVS products &amp; services (operationally as well as technically).</li>
<li>Available to assist with coverage of after-hours Techline/SLA hotline, when not engaged with other commitments.</li>
</ul>

About EVS
<br>Founded in 1994, EVS revolutionized live sports broadcasting thanks to its innovative Live Slow Motion system relying on state-of-the-art technology. Headquartered in Liège, Belgium with about 20 offices, 500 employees and 5 development centers worldwide, EVS is a key player in the broadcast and media technology market. Its products and solutions are sold in more than 100 countries worldwide.
<br><br>
Thanks to its customer focus, EVS and its employees have developed a unique expertise in live production technology, capable of fast innovations that respond to customer needs, while offering unmatched customer support. Its live production tools, with ultra-high levels of reactivity, combined with its product range around the live video servers XT and XS, offer a unique level of reliability and performance. These solutions allow producers and directors to transform live video flows into rich and emotional content for viewers across the world.
