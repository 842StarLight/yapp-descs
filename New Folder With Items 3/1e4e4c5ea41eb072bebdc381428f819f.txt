<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>What You'll Do
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div>This role will work under the managerial direction of the Director of Product and will be responsible for constructing the Helmet &amp; Goggle offering that meets the expectations of athletes and target consumers and coordinating the flexibility of products between categories while meeting the objectives of the category.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<ul>
<li>Create helmet and goggle range including line plan assortment, development and sourcing</li>
<li>Drive the product development process, from product briefing to delivery to market</li>
<li>Liaise with sourcing team’s suppliers to achieve cost, quality and time targets</li>
<li>Ensures model-lines, features, colors and all aspects of product design meet the needs of the athletes, consumers, sales (internal) and marketing (internal)</li>
<li>Identify trends, market needs, consumers and retailers</li>
<li>Use sales forecasting or strategic planning to ensure the sale and profitability of products, analysing business developments and monitoring market trends</li>
<li>Maintain product line management tool inputs</li>
<li>Present product range at sales meetings and to key retailers</li>
</ul>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>What We're Looking For
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<ul>
<li>Minimum of 3-5 years in a similar role</li>
<li>3-5 year degree in Design or related</li>
<li>Software : Command of Microsoft Office, 2D and/or 3D CAD software (Solidworks, AutoCAD or similar)</li>
<li>Marketing knowledge, winter sport market knowledge, consumer goods manufacturing</li>
<li>Experience in product management in sporting hardgoods, consumer goods or helmets &amp; goggles</li>
<li>Knowledge of injection molding techniques is an advantage</li>
<li>Detail oriented &amp; highly focused on completing deliverables on-time</li>
<li>Ability to manage multiple priorities, anticipate workload demands, communicate project status appropriately, and maintain a high level of operational transparency and accountability</li>
<li>Strong hands-on problem solving, organization and communication skills: self-motivated team player with ability to work independently with minimal supervision, and can work on multiple projects efficiently</li>
<li>Output orientation: a creative, proactive and organized individual with clear communication to other groups within the company</li>
<li>Ability to work as a team member and to collaborate: Promote information sharing and team spirit, contributing to collective efficiency</li>
<li>Professional commitment: Demonstrate passion and personal investment in order to develop best-in-class product designs.</li>
</ul>
