<h2>PreSales Engineer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Overview
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<p>The Solution Engineer will report directly to the Manager, Solution Engineering and will be located in the Vistex Hoffman Estates, IL office; and can expect to have frequent interaction with Sales, Marketing, Client Services, Product Management as well as external prospect/client facing interactions. The main purpose of this position is to design and deliver customized demo scenarios for potential and existing customers of the Vistex suite of products. Candidate must be able to travel across the U.S. and internationally. Expected travel up to 25% but may exceed this during busy times of year.</p>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities<br>
</b></h2>
<p></p>
<div><div><div>
<ul>
<li>Develop and maintain in-depth technical, product functionality and operation knowledge of Vistex software products</li>
<li>
Collaborate closely with Sales team members, prospects and client contacts ranging from systems administrators, end-users to C-level leadership to gather all necessary business and technical requirements to build successful, customized product demonstrations (proof-of-concept, pilot presentations, etc.)</li>
<li>
Perform product demos and technical presentations for potential clients, partners, and sales team via audio/video web-based conferencing, phone, and on prospect/client site</li>
<li>
Provide technical sales support via a variety of media and formats, to include: tradeshows, events, webinars, Q&amp;A sessions, etc.</li>
<li>
In partnership with sales team members, reply to RFQ’s, RFI’s and RFP’s with appropriate level of business and technical depth depending on the prospect/client organization, contact and audience</li>
<li>
When a well-defined RFP is not part of the initial process, perform detailed requirements analysis to fully understand existing prospect/client infrastructure; current and future needs; motivations; and timelines</li>
<li>
Provide ongoing product-specific technical guidance to Sales team members and other internal/external audiences as required and appropriate</li>
<li>
Develop, as needed, supplemental product/solution documentation complementary to prospect/client business and technical demonstrations</li>
<li>
In partnership with Sales, proactively track and manage prospect/client product evaluation process</li>
<li>
Other duties as required or directed</li>
</ul>
</div></div></div>
