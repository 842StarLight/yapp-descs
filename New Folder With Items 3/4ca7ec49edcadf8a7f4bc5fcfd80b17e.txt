<h2>Market Segment Analyst</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div><div><div><div><div><div>
<p>The Market Segment Analyst is responsible for prospecting, qualifying and generating new sales leads to create a steady stream of opportunities for our existing sales team.</p>
<p><b>
PRIMARY DUTIES:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Follow up on leads and conduct research to identify potential prospects</li>
<li>
Compile daily lists of leads and delegate individual leads to sales team associates</li>
<li>
Maintain an organized and accessible file system for administrative and sales professionals</li>
<li>
Handle all administrative duties for the organization and scheduling of client meetings and conferences</li>
<li>
Follow up with clients on the telephone for administrative purposes</li>
<li>
Perform data entry duties with regard to sales figures, metrics and other key data</li>
<li>
Work with the Sales Team to develop and grow the sales pipeline to consistently meet desired targets</li>
<li>
Manage data for new and prospective clients</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
REQUIRED QUALIFICATIONS AND SKILLS</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Clerical experience in a high-volume office highly desired</li>
<li>
Strong researching skills</li>
<li>
Strong communication and interpersonal skills</li>
<li>
Strong relationship management skills; ability to build relationships and work well with others</li>
<li>
Highly motivated and persistent personality with a strong commitment to achieve company sales goals</li>
<li>
Ability to multitask while learning new information about the client base and the industries Land Gorilla serves</li>
<li>
Working knowledge of G Suite, MS Office, and comfortable learning sales software</li>
<li>
A desire to succeed in a sales role and develop your skill set</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
This job description is not exhaustive and the job holder may be required to undertake additional duties from time to time to ensure the smooth running of the department</b></p>
