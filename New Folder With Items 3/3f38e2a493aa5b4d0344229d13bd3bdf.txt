<p><b>Job title: </b>Business Process Management (BPM) – Program Manager – Staff level
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>About the role – you will:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Lead large, cross-functional strategic business transformation and process improvement projects of business &amp; technology processes
</li>
<li>Lead the project team to document complex value stream processes, analyze processes to identify improvement opportunities and solutions using methods such as Lean or Six Sigma
</li>
<li>Identify current and target process metrics and systems
</li>
<li>Manage process improvement project plans and actions within agreed upon timelines
</li>
<li>Effectively communicate and influence, cross-functionally to achieve business objectives and target performance
</li>
<li>Develop and deliver project status presentations and videos, including process models, supporting metrics, business requirements, improvement recommendations, etc.
</li>
<li>Recognize barriers to improvement efforts and effectively work to remove them
</li>
<li>Advise the business in maintaining process mapping, identifying key metrics, and benchmarking
</li>
<li>Provide training and education to enable employees to continuously identify, reduce, and eliminate non-value added activity from processes and to have the tools and knowledge to do this effectively
</li>
<li>Develop, train, and mentor other Business Excellence team members
</li>
<li>Utilize a BPM (Business Process Management) System (Signavio) to create process flows and other attributes (RACI, SIPOC, etc) using BPM Notation. Apply analysis and simulation modeling
</li>
<li>Manage relationships with key stakeholders and SME’s (Subject Matter Experts) to ensure they are positive and effective
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>About our group:
</b></p>
<p></p>
<p>Business Excellence enables our enterprise to move beyond standards and compliance onto the path of enabled continuous improvement with all process owners benefiting from a singular structure, common vernacular, and a means to continuously identify and eliminate non-value added activity from our work and have the tools and knowledge to do this effectively. Business Excellence centers on understanding Seagate’s current state performance and creating a desired future state for all processes and metrics with a target to lead in all benchmarking KPI’s. The outcome is to achieve strong employee engagement and advocacy for improvement leading to a strong total customer experience.
</p>
