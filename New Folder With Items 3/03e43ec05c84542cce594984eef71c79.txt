<h2>Template Technician - Granite/Marble</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<b>Description:</b><br>
Template designated areas within commercial and residential job sites.
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Act as the liaison between the sales staff and the customer when required.</li>
<li>Work with Scheduling Team and Fabrication facility to meet predetermined deadlines.</li>
<li>Communicate with sales staff as needed.</li>
<li>Process measure information in a timely manner.</li>
<li>Have general knowledge about all facets of granite fabrication and installation procedures.</li>
<li>Expedite jobs as needed.</li>
</ul>
<b>Qualifications:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Telephone communication skills.</li>
<li>Ability to analyze technical data.</li>
<li>Possess a valid drivers license with an acceptable driving record that meets the minimum requirements of the companys insurance provider.</li>
<li>Ability to read and analyze blue prints.</li>
<li>Ability to learn the company computer software program.</li>
<li>Mathematical competency.</li>
</ul>
<b>Requirements:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Must have a valid drivers license</li>
<li>Must have a clean driving record</li>
<li>Must pass a criminal history, drug test and physical assessment prior to employment</li>
</ul>
