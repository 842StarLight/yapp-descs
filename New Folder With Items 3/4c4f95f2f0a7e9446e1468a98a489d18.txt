<h2>Senior Solutions Consultant</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Your Opportunity</b></h2>
<p>
If you have that special mix of DNA that makes an extraordinary Solution Consultant – the blend of great business savvy and technical prowess – then we are a match made in heaven!</p>
<p>
With the swift adoption of our application performance management (APM) tooling among enterprise customers, New Relic is showing that large organizations are ready for SaaS APM solutions. Working with the sales organization, you will use your technical expertise and business insight to ensure our high-value customers are able to grow their business by using the full scope of New Relic’s product suite. You will champion New Relic’s strategic direction and vision, as well as promote and advocate new product direction on behalf of your customers.</p>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
What You’ll Do</b></h2>
<ul>
<li>
Support the Enterprise Sales team with opportunity qualification, demonstrations, Proof of Concept presentations (POC), RFP responses &amp; business justification in a presales capacity</li>
<li>
Lead the adoption, optimization, and integration of our products within customers’ business infrastructure</li>
<li>
Evangelize new product features &amp; provide customer feedback to New Relic product management and engineering groups</li>
<li>
Assist the Customer Success team the post-sales technical process: installation, deployment, training, technical check-ups, and escalation management</li>
<li>
Ensure timely resolution of customer issues by coordinating support responses</li>
<li>
Maintain a deep understanding of the customer’s business as well as their technical environment</li>
</ul>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Your Qualifications</b></h2>
<p>
Must-have:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Strong technical background and sales/customer orientation</li>
<li>
Hands on experience in one or more of the following languages: Java, .NET, Ruby, C, Python</li>
<li>
Excellent organizational and project management skills</li>
<li>
Strong presentation &amp; negotiation skills</li>
<li>
Excellent communication, critical thinking &amp; analytical skills</li>
<li>
Understanding of cloud technologies, from both an Iaas and a Paas perspective</li>
<li>
Bachelor's degree in Engineering/Computer Science or equivalent</li>
</ul>
<p>
Nice-to-have:</p>
<ul>
<li>
5+ years of pre-sales experience with the high tech industry</li>
<li>
Specific experience is Application Performance Management (APM)</li>
<li>
A clear understanding of network &amp; system Management solutions</li>
<li>
Background in mobile application development concepts (IOS &amp; Android)</li>
<li>
Experience with real user monitoring and customer digital experience</li>
<li>
Deep understanding of AWS, Azure, GCP offerings, including hands onexperience</li>
</ul>
<p>
Please note that visa sponsorship is not available for this position.</p>
