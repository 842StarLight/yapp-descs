<h2>Front Counter Person- Tolleson, AZ</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">Velocity Vehicle Group is hiring a Front Counter Person in Tolleson, Arizona
<br><br>
The Parts Department Front Counter Person performs a variety of customer service, in store marketing, promoting, advertising and stocking duties related to the sale and delivery of parts and accessories that contribute to the efficient, safe and profitable operation of the dealership parts department. The Front Counter Person is responsible for counter parts sales and providing excellent customer service.
<br><br>
<b>Job Duties:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Sells products for the repair of trucks according to customers’ needs</li>
<li>Sells services for the repair of trucks to meet customer needs</li>
<li>Maintains customer goodwill by greeting and acknowledging all customers</li>
<li>Answers customer inquiries</li>
<li>Fulfills customer requests</li>
<li>Sells additional parts and services by recognizing opportunities to up-sell</li>
<li>Follows prescribed cash, credit and check processing procedures of the dealership</li>
<li>Pulls parts for customers</li>
<li>Receives returns from customers</li>
<li>Assists in keeping parts department clean and orderly</li>
<li>Records all sales and/or customer transactions</li>
<li>Assists Parts Manager with merchandise displays</li>
<li>Maintains the retail sale floor and displays to meet or exceed the dealership’s standards and customer satisfaction</li>
<li>Performs routine housekeeping tasks that maintain and enhance the cleanliness of products and the retail sales area</li>
<li>Uses dealership equipment and resources safely and proficiently Customer Service Duties</li>
<li>Greets customers immediately in a courteous manner</li>
<li>Assists in resolving customer complaints and concerns and ensures the problem is corrected to the customer and dealership’s satisfaction</li>
<li>Treats customers fairly and honestly and demonstrates company’s commitment to superior customer service and ethical business practices After Hour Duties</li>
<li>Performs cashiering functions after hours</li>
<li>Answers telephone calls after hours</li>
</ul>
