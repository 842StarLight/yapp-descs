<b>Job Description:</b><br>
<p>American Bank of Commerce is excited to provide you with an opportunity to come work with us. We are currently seeking qualified applicants to work as an Intern in our Marketing Department in the West Texas Region.
</p>
<p>As an Intern with ABC Bank you are expected to project the banks Mission, Vision, and Values Statement by providing a better banking experience built on quality service, competitive products, devoted employees whose goals are based on exceeding the expectations of our customers, and stakeholders, while maintaining compliance with regulatory guidance.
</p>
<p>This person serves as an ambassador to the Bank and is responsible for assisting in the development and implementation of a strategic marketing plan to be utilized by all ABC markets. They will also work with the Bank's advertising agency to coordinate marketing efforts, design and distribute marketing materials and assist in product promotions. They will work with Compliance to ensure all marketing meets regulatory standards. Additional duties include ordering promotional items and assisting with event planning.
</p>
<p>Flexible hours Monday through Friday!
</p>
<p>Think this is something you are interested in? We invite you to apply and learn more about our great company!<br>
