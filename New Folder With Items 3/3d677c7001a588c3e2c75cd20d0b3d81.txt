<h2>Enrollment Counselor</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>An eCornell Enrollment Counselor is responsible for contacting (through outbound and inbound calls) and converting qualified prospects into active learners through a consultative inside sales approach. This is a sales position utilizing relationship-building skills, needs analysis, and in-depth knowledge of eCornell online programs to gain commitment from perspective learners. The role consists of phone- and computer- based interaction to evaluate educational qualifications and needs, and recommend solutions to individuals who are interested in educational opportunities for adult professionals.</p>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Responsibilities</b></h3>
<ul>
<li>
Utilize your extensive eCornell product knowledge to advise prospective learners.</li>
<li>
Investigate and discover prospect needs, guide prospects through eCornell’s programs and offerings, present features and payment options, recommend appropriate programs and customized solutions.</li>
<li>
Utilize automated tools and complex software applications to manage customer data.</li>
<li>
Meet and exceed pre-established sales and enrollment goals based on monthly and quarterly objectives.</li>
<li>
Meet and exceed customer expectations.</li>
<li>
As a key member of the enrollment team, support team discussions, 1:1 call coaching sessions and communications.</li>
<li>
Provide information, creative selling opportunities and other business suggestions to management.</li>
<li>
Continuously interact with customers both by phone and by computer.</li>
</ul>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Qualifications</b></h3>
<p>
Minimum of two years of inside sales experience in a high-call environment, selling a complex product while working toward established sales goals/quota with the following criteria:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Experience with relationship-oriented (non-transactional) selling with a comprehensive sales cycle, which often entails multiple points of contact.</li>
<li>
Demonstrated success at meeting and exceeding sales goals.</li>
<li>
Experience selling high-value products and services to a diverse customer base.</li>
<li>
Expertise leveraging sales and customer-tracking software applications and tools.</li>
<li>
Excellent communication, investigation and listening skills to solve problems and provide solutions.</li>
<li>
Self motivated and organized to remain focused on objectives.</li>
<li>
Willing to complete eCornell courses in order to be comfortable representing a complex product that requires in depth product knowledge.</li>
<li>
Displayed success in a high phone contact performance-driven sales environment, utilizes metrics and measurements to track results.</li>
<li>
Advanced understanding of consultative selling and relationship building, uncovering buying decisions and understanding customer needs.</li>
<li>
Time Management skills to multi-task a variety of activities including outbound calling and applicant follow up.</li>
</ul>
<p>
Associate’s or Bachelor’s degree desired. Course work in field relevant to eCornell courses preferred.</p>
<p>
No visa sponsorship is provided for this position. eCornell is an affirmation action/equal opportunity employer.</p>
