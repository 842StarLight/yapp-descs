<h2>RMA Order Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Job Opening: RMA Order Specialist (Full-Time)</b></p>
<p><b>
Location: Irvine, CA</b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
Advantech (www.advantech.com ), the leading Embedded Platform Hardware provider, has been an innovator in the development and manufacturing of high quality, high-performance embedded solutions in the industrial computing and automation markets since 1983. We are looking for an energetic team player to join Advantech Embedded &amp; Networking Computing Group in Irvine.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Response customer’s inquiry on return, repair, and swap program for post sales related activities.</li>
<li>Identify return request, issue RMA tickets for in warranty, out of warranty and provide quote on component or system level.</li>
<li>Manage and close all open tickets for IW/OW</li>
<li>Monitor/Response to RMA support emails from external customers and internal departments, FAE, QA, QC and service center.</li>
<li>Full responsibility of the management on reversed logistics assigned customer accounts.</li>
<li>Understand the T&amp;C of return policy, credit, cross ship, restocking fees.</li>
<li>Review weekly RMA report, follow-up on opens claims and improve tickets TAT.</li>
<li>Communicate with FAE, production staff and PM team to ensure trending warranty issues are recognized.</li>
<li>Work closely with service center to ensure warranty and parts are properly inventories.</li>
<li>Cross-functional communication and collaboration with Financial, Repair center, Logistics on day-to-day open tickets oversight.</li>
<li>Ability to learn and support order processes for department back up.</li>
<li>Establish excellent customer service with clients.</li>
<li>Other duties as required</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p>
</p>
<p><b>Requirements</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Minimum 2 years of demonstrated order administration experience in a fast pace environment, especially in a reserve logistics supporting role
</li>
<li>Experience in SAP and E-RMA portal preferred</li>
<li>Global RMA experience is a plus</li>
<li>Must have solid knowledge in Excel, Outlook and other Microsoft Office applications</li>
<li>Strong follow-up and also follow-through skills</li>
<li>Highly organized, attentive to details, and strong analytical skills</li>
<li>Excellent interpersonal, written and verbal communication skills is a must</li>
<li>Exceptional response time mindset and commitment</li>
<li>Minimum 2 years college graduate</li>
<br>
</ul>
