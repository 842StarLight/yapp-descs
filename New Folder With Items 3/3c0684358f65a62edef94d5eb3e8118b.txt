<h2>Revenue Manager/Assistant Controller</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>The Revenue Manager / Assistant Controller will be responsible for managing 1-2 accountants, the month end close, and leading accounting, tax, and treasury functions for three entities (US, UK and Canada). This newly created position reports to the VP Finance &amp; Operations.</p>
<p>
 <b>Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Manage and oversee the daily operations of the accounting department</li>
<li>Process monthly, quarterly and end year process, general ledger, revenue variance analysis, balance sheet reconciliations, and fixed asset activity</li>
<li>Oversee the entire revenue recognition process to ensure complete and accurate revenue recognition</li>
<li>Develop and maintain effective internal controls</li>
<li>Responsible for timely and accurate close of the revenue cycle</li>
<li>Ensure accurate and timely monthly account reconciliations and financial reporting deliverables for all balance sheet accounts</li>
<li>Prepare audit schedules and assist external auditors during quarterly review and annual audit periods</li>
<li>Manage and comply with local, state, and federal government reporting requirements and tax filings, including calculation of provisions, maintenance of transfer pricing policies and filing for all appropriate tax credits and rebates</li>
<li>Manage all financial transactions on a timely and fully accurate basis, including accounts payable, accounts receivable, payroll and expenses</li>
<li>Manage treasury, including cash, investments, banking and debt relationships</li>
<li>Serve as the financial lead for our financial management software (NetSuite)</li>
<li>Maintain and manage the global chart of accounts, staying flexible with changing business requirements</li>
<li>Oversee payroll process and ensure accuracy and timely manner</li>
<li>Participate in ad-hoc special projects</li>
</ul> <p><b>Qualifications:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor's degree in Accounting or Finance</li>
<li>5-8 years of relevant experience ideally with experience working in the SaaS space</li>
<li>Public Accounting experience preferred but not required</li>
<li>CPA preferred but not required</li>
<li>Strong knowledge of GAAP</li>
<li>NetSuite experience preferred</li>
<li>Expert in software revenue recognition</li>
