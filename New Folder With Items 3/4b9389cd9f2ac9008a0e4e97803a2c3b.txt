<h2>Material Handler</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">Packages orders for domestic and international shipments, as well as operates machinery such as a forklift.
<br><br>
<b>Primary Responsibilities:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Responsible for packaging orders for domestic shipments to include both small parcel and LTL.</li>
<li>Route packed materials to the appropriate carrier based on the specific routing instructions of the customer.</li>
<li>Instruct and/or assist in preparing merchandise for shipment.</li>
<li>Arranges stock to best advantage for efficiency and accessibility. Adheres to all safety rules and may use pallet jack and other material moving tools.</li>
<li>Must be able to lift up to 35 pounds.</li>
<li>Must be available for up to 10 hours of overtime in any given week.</li>
<li>Coordinate pick-ups with LTL carriers based on routing guides.</li>
<li>Access and utilize customer 3PL systems in order to ensure all customer routing requirements are met.</li>
<li>Requires a minimum of 1-2 year of related experience</li>
</ul>
<b>Working Conditions and Basic Requirements:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Manufacturing, Warehouse and Office environments. Physical requirements vary based upon location &amp; product line assignments</li>
</ul>
