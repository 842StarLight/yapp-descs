<p><b>The role:</b></p>
<p>
We are looking for an experienced, detail-oriented customer service representatives to field calls and handle tickets in our fast-paced technical support call center. This position is responsible for documenting and resolving advanced trouble tickets, helping clients learn features of the Miva Merchant software, and assisting with DNS, FTP, SSL, email, and other website-related technologies. If you love helping customers have an unbeatable experience and have prior experience with Linux, and HTML/CSS, than we want to talk to you!</p>
<p><b>
You will:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Provide phone support for inquiries relating to our hosting platform and e-commerce software</li>
<li>
Answer phone calls and create and document detailed tickets in our ticketing system</li>
<li>
Demonstrate professionalism and outstanding customer service on all phone calls and email replies</li>
<li>
Identify and reproduce client issues, providing a full description of the problem and resolution</li>
<li>
Diagnose and resolve issues that result in poor website performance, such as DDOS attacks, runaway server processes, etc</li>
<li>
Educate clients on best practices regarding credit card data storage and adherence to PCI/PA-DSS guidelines.</li>
</ul>
