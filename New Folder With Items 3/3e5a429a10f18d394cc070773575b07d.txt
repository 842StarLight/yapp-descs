<h2>Account Representative I</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>The RealPage Sales Team is looking for motivated, outgoing sales professionals to join our growing organization. If you thrive in a fast-paced and competitive environment, have an interest in working for an industry leader, and have experience selling value-focused software solutions in a B2B environment - we would love to hear from you!<br>
<br>
SUMMARY<br>
<br>
The Account Representative I is responsible for adding new customers as well as maintaining existing client account relationships. The Account Representative will have assigned revenue goals and activity responsibilities. The position will be responsible for visiting management companies and owners in a geographic territory, attending managers meetings with RealPage clients, attending industry functions on behalf of RealPage, Inc. and meeting with client executive management.<br>
<br>
PRIMARY RESPONSIBILITIES<br>
<ul>
<li>New sales activities include the management of prospecting, qualifying, closing and follow up on implementations associated with new product and service sales.</li>
<li>Generating new sales and add-on sales revenue within a given territory and/or named account listing.</li>
<li>The work environment will be split equally between telephone work with clients in the office and outside fieldwork at client locations.</li>
</ul>
<br>
REQUIRED KNOWLEDGE/SKILLS/ABILITIES<br>
<ul>
<li>Four-year college degree in business or equivalent work experience</li>
<li>Ability to identify, qualify and prioritize prospects</li>
<li>Ability to present products and solutions to large groups</li>
<li>Ability to assess a situation and quickly formulate the correct solution</li>
<li>Ability to effectively and efficiently manage a geographic territory on an on-going basis</li>
<li>Sales experience with a software solution highly preferred</li>
<li>Computer software skills with Microsoft Excel, Word, Internet Explorer and PowerPoint</li>
<li>Excellent oral and written communication skills</li>
<li>Any certifications with Real Estate associations such as IREM or NAA is desired</li>
<li>Contact management software experience with Salesforce.com is a plus</li>
<li>Industry meetings with industry associations and Trade Shows will also be required. These meetings are mandatory and may occur on evenings and weekends
</li>
<li>This person will be a self-motivated, hard working, detail-oriented individual with punctual follow-up with clients on daily activities</li>
<li>This individual needs to be a self-starter that is motivated by resolving client issues and selling solutions in order to collect commission income</li>
<li>Occasional travel of up to 25% may be required, including overnight and some weekend travel</li>
</ul>
