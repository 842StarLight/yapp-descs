<h2>Accounts Receivable Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p><b>Overview</b></p>
<p>The <b>Accounts Receivable Specialist</b> is an administrative accounting position with responsibilities across multiple companies. Individuals will manage multiple priorities and will be responsible for the overall collection of accounts receivable and to ensure timely payment of accounts receivable to prevent financial losses. The Accounts Receivable Specialist will report directly to the Controller and work closely with management and the sales team. This position is based out of our corporate office in Stilwell, KS.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Role and Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul><li> Collects past due accounts receivable by telephoning and emailing customers</li></ul>
<ul><li> Follows up on problem accounts; obtains information from customers, producer, or sales manager assigned to account</li></ul>
<ul><li> Reports and discusses potential trouble accounts to Controller, offers solutions for collection and provides recommendations for handling and/or escalation</li></ul>
<ul><li> Oversees follow-through efforts on collections resulting from audits.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Monitors and analyzes receivables reports</li></ul>
<ul><li> May process accounts receivable payments using NetSuite accounting software</li></ul>
<ul><li> Ensures accuracy of data entered</li></ul>
<ul><li> Makes positive contribution to customer satisfaction and constantly strives to improve service to the customer</li></ul>
<ul><li> Communicates in a positive manner to contribute to a cohesive, pleasant work environment.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Protects the confidentiality of all information learned by performing the duties of the position</li></ul>
<ul><li> Could be responsible for month-end closing of assigned agencies</li></ul>
<ul><li> Must be open to cross training on other aspects of the revenue and collection cycle</li></ul>
<ul><li> Maintain professional, timely, communication with customers, FDS management and staff</li></ul>
<ul><li> Any other duties that are assigned in support of the operation</li></ul>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Job Requirements</b></p>
<ul><li> Bachelor’s degree in Accounting, Finance or equivalent work experience</li></ul>
<ul><li> Ability to interpret and apply general understanding of accounting concepts and principles.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Six month’s experience in accounting support or academic accounting coursework</li></ul>
<ul><li> Professional presentation and verbal and written communication/instruction skills</li></ul>
<ul><li> Demonstrated ability to interact and communicate effectively with all levels of the organization clients and staff alike</li></ul>
<ul><li> Ability to meet deadlines with great attention to detail</li></ul>
<ul><li> Ability to work under pressure and perform multifaceted projects in conjunction with daily activities</li></ul>
<ul><li> Extensive experience with Microsoft Office Suite, including Outlook, Word, Excel, PowerPoint and Calendar</li></ul>
<ul><li> NetSuite experience is preferred but not necessary</li></ul>
<ul><li> Ability to learn other software systems used throughout our company</li></ul>
<ul><li> Strong analytical skills, with the ability to critically think</li></ul>
<ul><li> Demonstrated ability to instill confidence and comfort with customers to facilitate account collection using high degree of tact and diplomacy</li></ul>
<ul><li> Ability to work outside of normal business hours as needed</li></ul>
<ul><li> Team player with a positive personality with a high level of confidentiality</li></ul>
<ul><li> Work independently and within a team on special, nonrecurring and/or ongoing projects</li></ul>
<ul><li> Strong organizational, communications and interpersonal skills</li></ul>
<ul><li> Professional appearance and demeanor</li></ul>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Additional Requirements</b></p>
<ul><li> Must be 21 years of age</li></ul>
<ul><li> Must have valid driver license</li></ul>
<ul><li> Must pass drug screen, driver’s license check and criminal background check</li></ul>
