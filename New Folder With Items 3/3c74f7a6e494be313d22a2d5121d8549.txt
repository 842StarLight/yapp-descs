<p><b>Start your Career as a Lifestyle Consultant</b><br>

<p>At Massage Heights our mission is to provide quality, convenient and affordable massage therapy to everyone. We are looking for professional Lifestyle Consultants for our three San Antonio area locations.</p>

<p></p>
<p>

<ul><li>Supervise a professional, high-volume, therapeutic, massage Retreat</li></ul>
<ul><li>Ensure complete customer satisfaction</li></ul>
<ul><li>Must lead by example to meet personal and team goals</li></ul>
<ul><li>Help maintain a positive and professional atmosphere</li></ul>
<ul><li>Schedule appointments and guide guests to appropriate therapists to meet their needs</li></ul>
<ul><li>Recommend products and services to guests based on services rendered and recommendations of therapists</li></ul>

<p></p>

<ul><li>Basic knowledge of computers and software and ability to learn new systems</li></ul>
<ul><li>People oriented with strong customer-service and sales skills</li></ul>
<ul><li>Highly motivated team-player with excellent interpersonal and communication skills</li></ul>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
**MUST be available to work varied hours (weekends, evenings, holidays, etc.)</p>
