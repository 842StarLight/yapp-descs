<h2>Lending Operations Officer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Position Title:</b></p>
<p><b>
Department:</b> Office of Chief Operation Officer</p>
<p><b>
Grade:</b> 19</p>
<p><b>
Position Summary:</b> Assist the Chief Operating Officer with planning, organizing and controlling the day-to-day activities of the Bank’s commercial lending operations and assist in the formulation of strategy to equip and transform the operational engines of the Bank to be more competitive over the long run. Major Bank functions reporting to the Lending Operations Officer include the Commercial Loan Documentation Department(s), Loan Operations, Loan Servicing and the Imaging Center, and other areas or departments as may be designated in the future. Provide leadership and management support to Department Managers and their staff and ensure that all monitoring, reporting and recordkeeping requirements are met.</p>
<p><b>
Major Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Together with the COO, provide the leadership, management and vision necessary to ensure that the Bank lending operations has the proper operational controls, administrative and reporting procedures in place to effectively grow the organization while remaining compliant with Federal, State, and local governmental agencies’ regulatory requirements.</li>
<li>Assist the COO to develop and implement business strategies to accommodate current and future organizational needs and attain established operational and financial goals.</li>
<li>Collaborate with the management team to develop and implement plans for the operational infrastructure of systems, processes, and personnel designed to accommodate the rapid growth objectives of the Bank.</li>
<li>Drive performance in assigned areas to support achievement of the Bank’s sales, profitability and business goals and objectives. Produce the services required by customers while managing resources as efficiently as possible.</li>
<li>Lead bank projects and initiatives that involve new software, services, or process improvements, including workflow automation solutions.</li>
<li>Develop cost analysis as required for evaluating outside systems and service providers, including expense controls and budgeting processes.</li>
<li>Administer budget for assigned areas conducting monthly reviews and identifying and explaining variances.</li>
<li>Negotiate contracts and terms that aid in cost containment with Bank’s vendors.</li>
<li>Spearhead the development, communication and implementation of effective growth strategies and processes in areas of responsibility.</li>
<li>Motivate and lead high performance managers; attract, recruit, and retain key members of the management team and support personnel.</li>
<li>Manage, coordinate, mentor and support personnel in specifically assigned departments or functional areas.</li>
<li>Foster a success-oriented, accountable environment within the Bank. Represent the Bank with customers, auditors, examiners, and business partners.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Other Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Create and implement effective Policy, Programs and Procedures for all assigned areas or functions.</li>
<li>Provide timely, accurate and complete reports on the operating condition of all assigned areas, to include remediation of findings from audits and examinations.</li>
<li>Effectively oversee assigned managers to verify they are monitoring the daily workflow and volumes to achieve department processing standards.</li>
<li>Perform operational and risk reviews and provide performance reporting back to Senior Management.</li>
<li>Provide direction, guidance and coaching to subordinate staff, including issues involving sensitivity and risk.</li>
<li>Ensure complete and accurate logs, files and records are maintained according to established procedures.</li>
<li>Ensure procedures are maintained as they relate to the business continuity plan/disaster recovery within areas of responsibility.</li>
<li>Maintain confidentiality regarding all customer and employee information.</li>
<li>May act as a leading “customer care” officer through direct contact with customers, ensuring optimal customer experience at each customer touch-point.</li>
<li>Maintain effective and cooperative relationships with internal customers and vendors.</li>
<li>Develop and maintain effective and positive relationships with management and stakeholders.</li>
<li>Manage special projects and perform other duties as assigned.</li>
<li>Identify the inherent risks in products, services, activities, processes and systems and provide recommendations.</li>
<li>Perform other work-related duties as assigned.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Education, Experience, And Other Skills Required:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Undergraduate degree in business administration, accounting or equivalent preferred.</li>
<li>Minimum 15 years professional executive level experience in banking with extensive experience in Lending Operations including Commercial, Retail and Business banking.</li>
<li>Excellent communication and management skills.</li>
<li>A strong leader who is able to inspire and coach other leaders. Capable to provide guidance, coaching and training to staff and to other Bank employees.</li>
<li>Ability to balance the need for operational efficiency and accuracy with nimbleness and innovation. Ability to take initiative and prioritize tasks; must be hands-on, highly organized and creative.</li>
<li>Possesses strong financial and analytical skills.</li>
<li>Understands financial markets and has the ability to formulate operational strategy in a competitive environment.</li>
<li>Holds high ethical standards and integrity.</li>
<li>Exceptional knowledge of regulatory compliance, information technology and data security.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Company Profile</b></p>
<p>
Established in 1975, Tri Counties Bank is a wholly-owned subsidiary of TriCo Bancshares (NASDAQ:TCBK) headquartered in Chico, California, providing a unique brand of customer <b><i>Service with Solutions </i></b>available in traditional stand-alone and in-store bank branches in communities throughout Northern and Central California.</p>
<p>
The Bank provides an extensive and competitive breadth of consumer, small business and commercial banking financial services, along with convenient around-the-clock ATM, online and mobile banking access.</p>
<p>
Tri Counties Bank has remained strong and profitable through a top-down commitment to its core values, sound business principles and responsible lending practices.</p>
<p>
Our success is also based on our involvement in the communities we serve. The personal touch comes naturally to Tri Counties Bankers. You may recognize us at business, school and non-profit events, baseball games and local eateries, skiing in the mountains, boating on the lakes, and fishing the local streams or attending an event in The City or on the peninsula.</p>
