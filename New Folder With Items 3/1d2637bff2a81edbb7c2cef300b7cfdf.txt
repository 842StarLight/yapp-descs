<div>
The <b>Staff Accountant</b> is responsible for day to day transaction recording for accounts payable, cash disbursements, and payroll; monthly closing of the financial records; and, maintenance of the Human Resource systems.</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<br>
<div><b>ESSENTIAL DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES:</b></div>
<div>To perform this job successfully, an individual must be able to perform each essential duty satisfactorily. The requirements listed below are representative of the knowledge, skill, and/or ability required. Reasonable accommodations may be made to enable individuals with disabilities to perform the essential functions, including but not necessarily limited to the following:
</div>
<ul>
<li>Accounts Payable - Process vendor invoices for payment, insuring all control procedures are met to determine propriety of payment.</li>
<li>
Inventory Maintenance – Assists Controller in monitoring Inventory Cycle counting, Standard cost variances</li>
<li>
Treasury – Perform monthly bank reconciliations.</li>
<li>
Sales and Use Tax – Maintain the tax system and prepares the periodic tax returns.</li>
<li>
Payroll – Process the weekly payroll; maintain the payroll and HR systems; provide requested reporting to management. Develops and maintains productivity dashboards</li>
<li>
Fixed Assets and Depreciation – Maintains Fixed Asset records and prepares monthly depreciation</li>
<li>
Monthly Close – Prepare the journals and reports necessary to close the general ledger accounts; assist the Controller in preparation of the financial statements.</li>
<li>
Any other related tasks as assigned</li>
</ul>
