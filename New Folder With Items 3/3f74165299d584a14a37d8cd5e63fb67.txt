<h2>Business Development Center Representative</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Business Development Center Representative</b></p>
<p>This is a base plus commission role, working 30 hours a week. You must be able to make 75 to 100 calls daily, with 2-3 hours of talk time, make appointments for the sales team and make follow-up calls with customers who have already purchased vehicles.</p>
<p><b>Main Responsibilities Include: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Conduct calls and e-mail outreach to prospects assigned through our lead delivery system</li>
<li>Navigate our lead delivery system to review and manage assigned leads, document activity, and update or submit information, as necessary</li>
<li>Achieve established activity targets for the number of outbound calls/hours spent conducting calls to meet or exceed sales appointment quotas</li>
<li>Adhere to internal policies and procedures in conducting sales calls and coordinating the handoff of qualified leads to sales team members</li>
<li>Protect both the company’s and customer’s information in accordance with company policies and procedures</li>
<li>Deliver excellent customer service via the phone or email.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Required Skills: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Ability to learn new computer software quickly</li>
<li>Must be comfortable talking and answering questions quickly to customers</li>
<li>Able to make 75 to 100 phone calls daily, with 2-3 hours of talk time</li>
<li>Enjoys being a team player but able to effectively work independently</li>
</ul>
