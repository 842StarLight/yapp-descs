<h2>Spa Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>BGMG MED SPA is looking for an SPA MANAGER to help Lead and Manage our SPA Department and team of Estheticians. Candidate needs to have a minimum of 1 year management experience in the spa industry. Must also have a professional and friendly personality with <b>SUPER SALES Experience and skills!</b></p>
<p><i><b>Job</b> Description &amp; Duties* - Primary responsibilities are two fold in this role</i></p>
<p>As Spa Manager it is to provide leadership, direction, training, and supervision of the staff in the daily operations of the spa. This includes but is not limited to, staffing &amp; scheduling, customer service and guest satisfaction, retail ordering and inventory, problem solving, maintaining the spa environment and promoting the business.</p>
<p>Provide beyond exceptional customer service to all guests. This includes performing consultations and closing packages and selling products, sharing details on our space and services via phone and email, efficiently booking and managing the appointment book, checking guests in and out, maintaining common areas throughout the day and creating a calm, relaxing guest experience from start to end.</p>
<p><b><i>Job Requirements</i></b></p>
<ul>
<li>Prior management experience in a SPA / MED SPA setting is required</li>
<li>CA Licensed Esthetician (preferred)</li>
<li>Candidates must be computer literate and capable of learning our Spa Software Reservations System</li>
<li><b>Must have the ability to sell packages, products and close deals</b></li>
<li>Must have the utmost customer service skill</li>
<li>Ideal candidates must be organized, able to effortlessly multitask, possess excellent time management skills, possess a natural attention to details and present a professional, polished appearance and demeanor</li>
<li>Hours for this position are a minimum of 40 BUT willing to stay after hours if needed</li>
<li>Candidates must be available to work Saturdays and have a flexible schedule</li>
<li>Candidate must have their own reliable transportation</li>
<li><b>Candidate must be bilingual / Spanish speaking</b></li>
</ul>
