We are seeking an accomplished content marketer to own content for our global set of websites (advertising.amazon.xx). This role will support the Amazon Advertising website team by providing content recommendations and ensuring content quality is at a high bar for customers. This role includes owning new content development, content testing plan, and final mile content reviews for all site content. Content deliverables include copy on home and product pages, case studies, resources, and landing pages. The day to day of this role includes working with UX and website program management to deliver new content and review submitted content for global set of websites (12 locales), ideating new content programs, ensuring content defects don’t ship to the site, and project managing review and approvals of content for launching emerging locales.<br>
<br>
To be successful you will need to establish clear processes, set prioritization, and be able to communicate effectively. The right candidate will be a self-starter and have strong interpersonal, project management, and analytical skills. This role will need to have a high bar for writing, grammar, and high attention to detail to deliver high-quality, customer obsessed content.<br>
<br>
Your responsibilities will include:<br>
<ul>
<li>Recommend and execute content ideas enabling the website team</li>
<li>Review inbound site requests and make recommendations for site taxonomy or content design and execution</li>
<li>Work cross-functionally with marketing and training, channel, acquisition, insights, legal, and PR teams to deliver accurate and consistent customer experiences with site content</li>
<li>Use site metrics and A/B testing to learn what content works best to drive site outcomes</li>
<li>Ability to identify global stakeholder needs, implement prioritization</li>
</ul>
