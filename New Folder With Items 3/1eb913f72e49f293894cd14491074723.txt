<h2>Purchasing Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Job Description:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Source and purchase hardware, equipment, services and supplies as necessary</li>
<li>Manage relationships with supplier partners and distribution vendors of information technology related products to meet the needs of QPI and its customers.</li>
<li>Perform all activities associated with placing purchase orders to vendors for assigned lines to include:
</li>
<ul>
<li>Review and verify cost against vendor quote for accuracy</li>
<li>Place purchase orders with supplier, confirming pricing and delivery</li>
</ul>
</ul>
<p><b>Qualifications:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Must have at least 4 years in Federal Government contracting and Procurement</li>
<li>Must have Deltek Costpoint - Purchasing and Sales Order Module experience</li>
<li>Must have a working knowledge of the Federal Acquisition Regulations (FAR) and the FAR Defense Supplement (DFARS)</li>
<li>Must be able to learn and execute new skills</li>
<li>Must be dedicated to meeting the expectations and requirements of internal and external customers</li>
<li>Must have the ability to build relationships both internally and externally is a key priority</li>
</ul>
