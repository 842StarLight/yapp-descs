<h2>Business Development Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Mattleman, Weinroth &amp; Miller, P.C. seeks a dynamic and successful marketing and business development professional to join the Mattleman, Weinroth &amp; Miller, P.C. team as the Business Development Specialist to assist with new business development and client relations. This will include the firm’s legal intakes, referrals, new business development and client relationship management goals (including social media and digital marketing). The successful candidate will report directly to the attorney in charge of business development, while working collaboratively with the legal practice groups and legal intake teams.This individual will be an integral part of our business development team and will be independently managing the firm’s digital marketing, working directly with the business development team on strategy and positioning, as well as key stakeholders across legal departments. This position will maintain proposal milestones schedule such as issue and review dates, kickoff meetings, planning sessions, drafting and review, and delivery dates ensuring (i) full understanding of business requirements and strategy; (ii) a smooth production process; (iii) an intelligent, professional, accurate and compelling final product; and (iv) timely delivery of the finished product. In addition, this position with will be responsible for the coordination of an experience database rollout. The individual will demonstrate independent judgment, strong attention to detail, project management skills and have a solid understanding of digital marketing and lead generation process in a law firm setting. It is preferred (but not required) the individual has knowledge of Google Business, Google AdWords, Facebook Business Pages, website design, and business networking. The ability to communicate effectively with a high level of professionalism and exemplary client service and teamwork skills is a must. The individual will also be an important part of our client relations team and will be the first point of contact for our clients. This means the individual is tasked with making a wonderful first impression, discussing of services with clients and determining whether the caller meets our case criteria. Exceptional customer service skills are a must. It is of the upmost importance for client relations is that the individual exhibits patience, respect and understanding while dealing with all of our potential clients. Additionally, the New Business Coordinator will be responsible for managing a high volume intake system, responding to all intakes in a timely manner; properly documenting all events and communications; conducting lead screenings; preparing, sending and explaining retainer agreements to clients; working with the other legal intake teams to accomplish special projects;proactively find ways to improve / strengthen the intake system; and working closely with management to implement improvements and increase the productivity of the legal intake team.We anticipate that this position will grow and encompass additional tasks and responsibilities. Managerial experience in a legal and/or call center setting is strongly preferred. Experience with lead generation and/or case management software programs such as Mailchimp/Constant Contact, Litify, Lexicata, Clio, and/or other CRM. Experience with digital marketing and/or web design (i.e. WordPress, Facebook, and/or Google Business/Adwords. Fluency in English &amp; Spanish preferred, but not required.Understanding of legal terminology and medical terminology in the personal injury practice preferred, but not required.</p>
