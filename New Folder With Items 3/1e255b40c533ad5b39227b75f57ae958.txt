<h2>Senior Manager, Sales Strategy & Analytics</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>Job Description</div>
<p></p>
<p>
As an expert within Sales Strategy &amp; Analytics, you will focus on utilizing a mix of market intelligence, market analysis, strategy and deep business insights. We will be part of a high-performance team chartered with driving Zendesk's Sales Strategy &amp; Analytics into the mid-market and enterprise segments. This includes setting financial goals, sales force composition, partner channel and deep analytics. This role requires a strong analytical mindset with the capability to provide sound strategic advice and practical operational insight to senior executives. As the Senior Manager, you will gain experience in key functional areas such as strategy, sales, marketing, customer experience, advocacy, finance and analytics.</p>
<p></p>
<p>
You will have the opportunity to learn from and partner with the Head of Sales Strategy &amp; Analytics, Hanish Rathod, who will be your direct manager. It will be vital for you to collaborate across the company to drive key initiatives.</p>
<p>
You will have a committed curiosity, and possess a real passion for crafting innovative strategies and deep analytical framework to assemble market share and monetize Zendesk products. Moreover, we will excel at developing recommendations that transcend the numbers with outstanding communication skills, high initiative, and strong leadership drive. We as a team should be able to generate hypotheses, independently analyze complex data and make strategic recommendations despite uncertainty and ambiguity.</p>
<p></p>
<div><b>
Responsibilities:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
We will partner closely with sales executives to develop and help drive Sales Strategy</li>
<li>
You will lead analysis of business performance to identify future roadblocks and present strategic recommendations to GTM leadership for decision-making and investment</li>
<li>
Lead cross-functional strategic initiatives and link strategy to operations by partnering with key business partners including BizOps and PMO</li>
<li>
Identify untapped market potential, then develop and implement plans to capture it</li>
<li>
Support strategic initiatives developed by GTM leadership or strategy team</li>
<li>
We will work intimately with sales ops to analyze and locate opportunities for sales productivity improvement</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<div><b>
Desired Qualifications and Experience:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
7+ years of prior work experience in a strategy consulting or analytics type role</li>
<li>
Exceptional analytical skills: demonstrated ability to structure complex problems, perform deep analytics, derive business insights and develop solutions</li>
<li>
Excellent communication skills, including the ability to clearly communicate complex information to functional leaders and executives</li>
<li>
Understanding of software industry / SaaS sales and marketing models is a plus</li>
<li>
Experience in sales, marketing, or customer success is preferred</li>
<li>
Highly collaborative, team player who can work cross-functionally</li>
<li>
Bachelor degree preferably in an analytical discipline such as mathematics, engineering, computer science, economics, etc.</li>
<li>
MBA Preferred</li>
</ul>
