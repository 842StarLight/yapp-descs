<h2>Public Relations and Marketing Coordinator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>Under the direction of the PR/Brand Manager, projects a favorable image of On With Life to the public and other key stakeholders by proactively communicating programs and services. Develop and execute PR campaigns with the goal of sharing the On With Life mission and enriching the On With Life brand. Conceptualizes and creates marketing materials, manages projects, and ensures organization messages are consistent.<br>
<br>
ESSENTIAL FUNCTIONS<br>
</p>
<ul>
<li>Create promotional materials and monitor throughout the production and printing process, including working with external freelancers as needed.</li>
 <li>Support the development and execution of a social media outreach plan.</li>
 <li>Coordinate and develop online communications, including the On With Life e-newsletter.</li>
 <li>Assist with special events planning and coordination including the annual fall conference, community events, conference exhibits, webinars and educational events.</li>
 <li>Create and deliver press releases, media relations content, and human interest stories to generate positive news stories.</li>
 <li>Maintain and update the On With Life external website with an emphasis on fresh content.</li>
 <li>Write content for various On With Life publications including our bi-annually magazine, Headway.</li>
 <li>Coordinate community outreach projects to enhance On With Life’s presence in the marketplace.</li>
 <li>Develop internal employee communications and monitor internal communications programs in other organizations for “best practice” thinking.</li>
 <li>Provide PR support to the Foundation, including assisting with annual fundraising events and developing collateral material for events and various campaigns.</li>
 <li>Ensures all materials adhere to our brand standards and exemplify On With Life's brand principles</li>
</ul>
<p><b>An excellent candidate for this position meets the following criteria:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Is willing to be a daily <b><i>champion</i></b><b> </b>for individuals impacted by brain injury and <b><i>treat them like family</i></b>
</li>
<li>Is team-oriented, a lifelong learner, and an excellent communicator in all formats: in-person, phone, email, etc.</li>
<li>Is a positive and compassionate person even on the tough days</li>
<li>Is a servant leader who puts others before her/himself, treating all with dignity &amp; respect</li>
<li>Has some personal or professional experience with individuals with disabilities or special needs</li>
<li>Strong verbal, organizational, visual and written communication skills</li>
<li>Ability to define and work to deadlines</li>
<li>Adobe Design Software or the ability to learn</li>
</ul>
<p>Qualified applicants must have:</p>
<p>
A bachelor’s degree with a major in public relations, journalism, communications, marketing, English or other related field. Must be able to read, write and speak fluent English. A minimum of one to three years’ previous professional work experience preferred; preferably in the area of public relations or marketing communications.</p>
