<h2>Senior Accounts Receivable Analyst</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div><b>Job Opportunity: Senior Accounts Receivable Analyst– Senneca Holdings - Pittsburgh, PA</b></div>
<div></div>
<div><b>Problem solving, exceeding customer expectations, follow through and commitment to excellence...these are the skills we are seeking. We are looking for dynamic, high achievers who are ready to learn and grow with our organization!</b></div>
<div></div>
<div>At Senneca, we embrace the entrepreneurial spirit of the innovators that created our brands from traffic doors, to cold storage doors to fiberglass doors and beyond. We are committed to exceeding our customers’ expectations and to provide doors that offer safety and protection to our customer’s most important assets…people, products, capital investments and processes.</div>
<div></div>
<div>We are looking for a <b>Senior Accounts Receivable Analyst </b>who will be responsible for the timely collections of outstanding accounts receivable, resolving customer billing issues, and reducing overall accounts receivable delinquency for Senneca’s Industrial Division. The successful candidate will be a self-starter with strong communication skills, interacting daily with sales, customer service, and external customers to drive down overall DSO while leaving an overall positive impression.</div>
<div></div>
<div><b>What we need from you:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Preferred 5-10+ years of successful experience with a demonstrated effectiveness in reducing past due accounts receivable</li>
<li>Strong communication skills (verbal and written) and understanding of cultural sensitivities</li>
<li>Basic knowledge of MS Office applications required; ability to learn in-house software application</li>
<li>Excellent problem-solving skills</li>
<li>Able to work with a variety of personalities and maintain composure under stressful conditions</li>
<li>Able to effectively locate, communicate and document information</li>
<li>Associate or Bachelor’s Degree preferred, but equivalent experience acceptable</li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<div><b>Job Duties and Responsibilities:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Initiates a minimum of 50 phone calls to delinquent customers daily to collect payments in a timely manner reducing overall % of past due AR.</li>
<li>Demonstrates initiative to provide superior customer service to both internal and external customers at all times.</li>
<li>Efficiently review and prioritize customer aging reports for daily collection activities.</li>
<li>Ability to research complex problems and bring prompt resolution to drive faster collections.</li>
<li>Maintain detailed reports and records of receivables and collections activity.</li>
<li>Resolve billing and client discrepancies in a timely manner.</li>
<li>Send clients copies of invoices or statements upon request.</li>
<li>Other special projects and duties as appropriate.</li>
</ul>
