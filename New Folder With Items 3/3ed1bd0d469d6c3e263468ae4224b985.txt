<p>
This position is within the Payment Services’ Global Pricing Organization and is responsible for supporting various departments within Payment Services. This Global Pricing role offers a challenging opportunity for someone to apply their skills in support of the fast-paced and growing portions of our business. The primary focus is supporting DGN’s Global Acceptance and International Markets team in their efforts to successfully secure new acquiring and issuing opportunities, as well as other unique partnerships with DFS. Works with internal departments to ensure initiatives and strategies are implemented and communicated. With the direction of the Srnior Manager, this person will conduct pricing analysis to ensure account-level and program-level profitability and will assist in the implementation of pricing policies around the world.</p>
<p>
Provide research and analysis that will aid in the evaluation of pricing strategies and solutions that drive revenue yield and the advancement of pricing infrastructure within Payment Services. The focus is to manage and grow revenue yield for Payment Services through research and conducting pricing analysis that ensure account-level and program-level profitability as well as monitoring current strategy or pricing proposal performance. This position is also responsible for working with internal departments to ensure that initiatives and strategies are implemented and communicated properly. This requires an introductory knowledge of competitive pricing practices, the drivers of the revenue, rates and fees for a wide variety of external client types (i.e., merchants, acquirers, networks, issuers, etc.) as well as an ability to identify details and cross-functional implications. This position also aids in the evaluation of a pipeline of pricing initiatives and requests under evaluation for review and approval, ensuring requests are evaluated in a consistent and rigorous manner and that all audit, risk, and governance requirements are met. This position works closely with personnel across all functions to contribute to various strategy efforts, pricing systems enhancements, and other initiatives designed to grow acceptance, sales volume, and revenue. It is unlikely that this position manages direct reports.</p>
<p>
Responsibilities</p>
<ul>
<li>Perform analysis of economics for various partnership programs, including impacts to network, acquiring and issuing partners</li>
<li>Develp strong, collaborative working relationships with Global Pricing, Global Participant Operations, International Markets and the Global Acceptance teams.</li>
<li>Researches competitive pricing trends and utilizes knowledge for analytics, assisting in policy recommendations, and risk assessment.</li>
<li>Assists in the delivering of the procedures and business rules required to support stated policy, as well as the documentation and communication of policy both internally and externally.</li>
<li>Performs thorough, detailed, and precise financial and mathematical analysis or visual representations of policy, volume, and revenue performance by partner and enterprise-wide to support business initiatives</li>
<li>Collaborates with management and other key business area decision makers to identify and solve a variety of issues, clarify management objectives, and ensure successful implementation of chosen solutions.</li>
</ul>
