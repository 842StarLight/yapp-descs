<h2>Shift Leader</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>COME BUILD A FUTURE WITH US ONE TOPPING AT A TIME!<br>
<br>
Starting pay varies from $11.00 - $15.00/hr., based on experience.</p>
<br>
<p>
Manager who is adaptable, self-motivated, and have a passion for customer service. Efficient and profitable operation of the store through achieving and maintaining high standards of customer relations, product quality, cleanliness, and safety.</p>
<p><b>
Essential Duties &amp; Responsibilities:</b></p>
<p><i>
Customer:</i></p>
<ul>
<li>
Drives customer-focused culture by serving as a role model in resolving serious customer issues and training team members to meet or exceed customer service standards.</li>
<li>
Verifies food quality is at the highest standard that is received by vendors and served to the customers.</li>
<li>
Check safe working and customer experience environment by facilitating safe work behaviors of the team.</li>
<li>
Ensures that food safety standards are met in the store through direct observation as well as follow up on food safety with team members.</li>
</ul>
<p><i>
People:</i></p>
<ul>
<li>
Administer prompt, fair, and consistent corrective action for any and all violations of company policies, rules and procedures.</li>
<li>
Setting priorities, managing details and carrying out effective coaching and providing recognition; working lunch, dinner, late night, weekend, and holidays.</li>
<li>
Verifying maintenance of equipment, facility, and grounds are being maintained to company standards.</li>
<li>
Adheres to cash handling and reconciliation procedures in accordance with company policies and procedures.</li>
<li>
Maintains prescribed opening and closing hours for stores along with following all security measures.</li>
<li>
Hires, trains, and develops team members within the store.</li>
</ul>
<p><i>
Sales/Profit:</i></p>
<ul>
<li>
Responsible for working with marketing team to ensure initiatives are in place and being implemented to drive sales growth.</li>
<li>
Analyzes sales, labor, inventory, and controllable on continual basis.</li>
<li>
Take specific corrective action to meet or achieve margin and sales growth targets.</li>
<li>
Ensures store is within compliance of Federal/State/Local requirements.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Education and Experience Requirements:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Must be at least 21+ years of age.</li>
<li>
Obtain and maintain a valid driver’s license.</li>
<li>
Able to maintain a liquor license and any other required permits.</li>
<li>
Minimum of 2 years’ experience in restaurant operations, or equivalent combination of education and experience.</li>
<li>
Systems &amp; Software – proficient level knowledge of Microsoft Office software applications.</li>
</ul>
