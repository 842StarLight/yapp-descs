<h2>Manager of Association Sales</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<div><div>
<p>Higher Logic is seeking a high-performing Sales Manager to lead a division of its Association market sales team. As Manager of Association Sales, you will be responsible for developing and growing one of Higher Logic’s fastest growing market segment. This includes identifying the optimal strategy in targeting the market’s prospective customer, maximizing the existing sales team’s potential through coaching and process implementation and scaling the of the market over time.</p>
<p><b>
Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Work with cross functionally to refine the company’s sales strategy within the Association Market and develop a road-map to achieve sales targets for the Association Sales Team.</li>
<li>
Manage team of 8 – 10 Account Executives.</li>
<li>
Mentor, coach and develop early career sales reps, including providing deal strategy and customer facing assistance and advising reps on how they can improve their performance.</li>
<li>
Recruit and retain members of a best-in class team that is incredibly engaged and results-driven, and will over-achieve sales goals.</li>
<li>
Onboard, train and develop new sales reps.</li>
<li>
Develop, lead, communicate and manage all phases of a highly scalable sales process, from prospecting and presenting to negotiating, closing and transition post sale operations. Focus on continuous improvement of the sales process.</li>
<li>
Ensure sales reps understand and are executing against the sales process and enforce compliance.</li>
<li>
Develop a culture of accountability, ensuring performance and quality of sales reps, including having tough conversations and making tough decisions when necessary.</li>
<li>
Manage Association Sales team’s weekly meeting cadence (scrums, pipeline reviews, forecast reviews, etc.).</li>
<li>
Manage sales metrics, deal pipeline and KPI’s.</li>
<li>
Provide accurate bookings forecasts and pipeline visibility for the Association Sales Team.</li>
<li>
Work with Marketing to enhance initiatives and materials for the Market and identify / recommend tools that will improve the effectiveness of Commercial Sales team and the broader Higher Logic Sales team.</li>
</ul>
</div></div>
<div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Qualifications</b></h2>
<div><ul>
<li>
7+ years of software sales experience with preferred experience in SaaS based solutions.</li>
<li>
3+ years of Sales Management or Team Lead experience, with a focus on on-boarding, training, and ramping early career Account Executives.</li>
<li>
Driven, sales focused, accountable individual that is passionate about achieving sales goals every quarter and expects the same from others.</li>
<li>
A top-performer as well as a strategist who can do both at the same time.</li>
<li>
A pragmatic approach to the market, hands-on leadership and problem-solving abilities in a high growth environment.</li>
<li>
Excellent leadership acumen with exceptional interpersonal and mentoring skills. Proven ability to select and motivate competent, successful sales talent.</li>
<li>
Proven success and demonstrable knowledge of the sales process for organizations of all sizes, focusing on driving rep compliance and passion for that process.</li>
<li>
Coaches value selling and appreciates the requirement of “team selling” in a sales environment.</li>
<li>
Strong quantitative skills with demonstrated ability to interpret data into actionable items / makes data driven decisions.</li>
<li>
Ability to work effectively and efficiently toward goals in a complex, diverse environment with multiple and changing demands.</li>
<li>
Past history of working and thriving in a high-growth environment is a plus.</li>
<li>
Familiarity with Challenge sales methodology is a plus.</li>
<li>
Bachelor's degree or equivalent.</li>
</ul></div>
