<h2>Senior SEO Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>The Senior SEO Specialist will be responsible for the management and analysis of multiple dealership group, Key, or Major SEO campaigns. Through proactive communication with the Digital Advisors, the Senior SEO Specialist will understand the needs of each client and manage accounts accordingly. The Senior SEO Specialist will also assist in the training and development of new hires and SEO Specialists. The Senior SEO Specialist will have a strong understanding of L2T’s suite of products and industry knowledge to ensure maximum customer value and satisfaction as well as be able to assist the SEO management team with projects that will aid in the betterment of L2T product and processes.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
PRINCIPAL DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES</b>:</p>
<ul>
<li>Implement and maintain SEO strategies such as, SEO copywriting, keyword research, and link building efforts.</li>
<li>Create high quality SEO Content monthly for SEO client websites</li>
<li>Provide recommendations and strategies for continued SEO campaign improvement for clients and potential clients</li>
<li>Regularly manage high quality reporting and communicate performance and insights to clients as well as SEO Leadership</li>
<li>Consult with Digital Advisors and Field Sales while implementing SEO strategies and monitor results in order to communicate necessary details</li>
<li>Complete understanding and application of Google Analytics and Webmaster Tools</li>
<li>Stay current on the latest industry trends, best practices, and search algorithm updates</li>
<li>Contribute to innovation of the SEO product through researching industry trends and competitive analysis that position us as a SEO leader</li>
<li>Assist in the training of new hires and SEO Specialists on L2T processes and SEO best practices</li>
<li>Respond to client requests accurately and in a timely fashion</li>
<li>Complete understanding of MAP and it’s functions</li>
<li>Work with technology team, providing recommendations, user-experience testing and support for internal platform development while adapting to Agile software development methodology</li>
<li>Act as key point of contact with Digital Advisors and Field Sales Team</li>
<li>Be a subject matter expert on all L2T products which are relevant to the SEO team. Act as a point of contact for other teams to elevate product knowledge to others within the organization</li>
<li>Complete account related tasks as assigned by your manager within the given time frame</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p>
</p>
<p><b>SKILL REQUIREMENTS:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Exceed client expectations on a daily basis by monitoring and optimizing assigned client campaigns</li>
<li>Ability to manage time and complete multiple tasks while continually meeting deadlines as well as delegate tasks appropriately and efficiently</li>
<li>Act as a team leader for SEO Specialists</li>
<li>Customer focused and takes accountability for their individual clients as well as other SEO projects assigned to them</li>
<li>Continuously thinks about personal and team improvement through providing ways the SEO team can expand on their SEO knowledge and we can better SEO process/SEO product</li>
<li>Must have strong computer skills, including familiarity with Microsoft Office products as well as advanced problem solving and analytical skills</li>
<li>Strategic thinking with the ability to come up with new, creative solutions</li>
<li>Exceptional communication skills to effectively message verbally and in writing, using a variety of communication methods to build productive relationships</li>
<li>Prioritizes and completes tasks with a concern for all the details involved; monitors and checks work for accuracy.</li>
<li>Easily adapts to change in responsibilities, work processes, timeframes, performance expectations, organizational culture or work environment.</li>
<li>Must be self-directed and effective working independently, yet equally comfortable contributing in a team environment</li>
<br>
</ul>
