<h2>Sr Implementation Analyst</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<b>Position:</b> Sr Implementation Analyst
<br><br>
<b>Location:</b> Hagerstown MD
<br><br>
Job Responsibilities
<br><br>
Act as a liaison between Fiserv and Clients on client implementations, projects and/or conversions for small/medium and at times large complex implementations.
<br>Develops, coordinates, and executes project plans, task schedules and resource activities to ensure on-time and effective project delivery.
<br>Identifies opportunities to streamline procedures, enhance revenue and improve product efficiency.
<br>Interface with other Fiserv departments to generate and offer appropriate solutions.
<br>Interprets and communicates Fiserv's services and Fiserv's products to clients, sales, and vendors.
<br>At times will be responsible for gathering client requirements for software development that will support the client implementations, projects and/or conversions.
<br>Provides client consultative input on Fiserv product and system features.
<br>Ability to work quickly and efficiently for timely turn around. Understanding of hierarchy needs required.
<br>Knowledge of Marketplace and Salesforce are beneficial.
<br>Scope of Job
<br><br>
Works on moderate to high level complex projects and tasks.
