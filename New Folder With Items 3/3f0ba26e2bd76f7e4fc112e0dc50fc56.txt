<p>
As the Sales Manager, you will have an opportunity to work from identification, through qualification and to closure. You should have a proven track record and special <b>expertise selling IT Implementation Services</b>. We prefer candidates that are able to conduct a consultative sales approach. In this role, you have the ability to articulate and craft an implementation proposal with support from our presales experts. Demonstrating expert sales, organizational, business and professional skills, the Sales Manager has primary responsibility for generating new opportunities and for bringing them to closure.<br>
