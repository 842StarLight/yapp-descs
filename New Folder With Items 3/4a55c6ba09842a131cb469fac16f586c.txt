<h2>Restaurant Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">We are a growing, independent franchisee company of Charleys Phillysteaks, that values our employees. We are a very energetic company that has a main focus on the customer experience. Give a Great Experience Every time is Charleys mission statement and it applies to our employees as not just our customers. We are looking for an individual to provide strong leadership by managing a philly cheese steak restaurant in a mall food court environment in an efficient, profitable, safe, clean manner while providing a wow experience to each customer. Someone who wants opportunities for professional growth and development. Acts with integrity, honesty, and knowledge that promote the culture and values of restaurant. Key areas of focus are customer service, sales, marketing, product quality, service, and implementing company's policy.
<br>The manager is responsible to uphold what is in the best interest of the brand, as well as be a communicator for initiatives impacting the restaurant because they are the face of the company.
<br><br>
Responsibilities* Swiftly resolve customer complaints.
<br><ul>
<li>The manager will supervise, train, provide direction, advice, and assist staff in all areas of the business according to proper steps of training. Able to adhere and abide by company guidelines, including hiring, terminating, payroll and other HR administrative duties in-store. Promote a culture of training and development to ensure staff are ready for additional responsibilities should the opportunity arise.</li>
<li>Directly perform hands-on operational work as needed and ensure resolution of any performance gaps.</li>
<li>Ability to perform, and supervise opening, and closing procedures as outlined in the restaurants Operations Manual as amended from time to time.</li>
<li>Oversee and manage Company owned restaurant while driving operational improvement.</li>
<li>Responsible for the P&amp;Ls related to controllable costs, cash flow, cash handling, bottom line profit, operations, inventory, budget and sales.</li>
</ul>
Qualifications* Must have 2 years of restaurant management experience. High school diploma or equivalent required.
<br><ul>
<li>Must have strong time management/problem solving skills.</li>
<li>Ability to become trained across a wide spectrum of competencies (operations, marketing, store financials).</li>
<li>Must have strong written, verbal, and interpersonal communication skills to positively influence others.</li>
<li>The position may require occasional lifting of products to waist level weighing up to 50 pounds</li>
<li>Be reliable with a positive enthusiastic attitude and be able to handle pressure in a fast paced environment.</li>
<li>Must be computer literate and familiar with restaurant management software (Microsoft Office)</li>
<li>Available to travel to other stores for training, new store openings, and promotion opportunities</li>
</ul>
</div>
