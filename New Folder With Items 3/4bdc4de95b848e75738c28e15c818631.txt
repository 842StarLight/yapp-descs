<h2>Sales Data Analyst</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>THE ROLE<br>The role of a Customer Care Data Analyst is to monitor and maintain database integrity, report sales data, study trends, and perform analysis to deliver information that aids the company in developing sales. This role will also help identify and propose solutions that will help mitigate and/or eliminate customer pain points that drive contacts into our business, while enhancing the customer experience. This role will assist with the creation and management of all incentive plans for all teams within Customer Care.</p>
<p>THE POSITION<br>Customer Care Data Analyst is responsible for the following results:</p>
<ul>
<li>Providing actionable data analysis regarding sales trends, promotional impacts, sales strategies, and customer retention, acquisition, insight, segmentation and profiling</li>
<li>Developing, preparing, and distributing standard and ad hoc reports, weekly, monthly, quarterly and yearly, to assist in the monitoring, measurement, and management of sales</li>
<li>Preparing and publishing monthly sales forecasts/key performance indicators</li>
<li>Using modeling and analysis techniques as a means to conduct deeper analysis of complex data and illustrate findings</li>
<li>Expressing ideas for process improvements to increase flexibility, reduce administrative costs, increase interactivity with customers and decreasing touch points.</li>
<li>Demonstrating advanced proficiency with a variety of software applications (e.g. Siebel, CyberQuery, CMS, Excel, Sharepoint) to effectively perform functions of this role</li>
<li>Help manage various incentive plans for Customer Care and Specialty Market teams</li>
<li>Contributing to a team effort by accomplishing related results or projects</li>
</ul>
