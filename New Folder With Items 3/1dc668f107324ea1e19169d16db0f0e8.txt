<h2>Senior Outsource Accounting Specialist – Lansing, MI Office</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<div><b>Senior Outsource Accounting Specialist – Lansing, MI
</b></div>
<div></div>
<div>The Outsource Accounting Group at Layton &amp; Richardson / Clark Schaefer Hackett is hiring a Senior Outsource Accounting Specialist in our Lansing, MI office. This will be a full time, benefits eligible position-including 5 weeks PTO! The ideal candidate will have proven full charge accounting / bookkeeping and QuickBooks expertise, with the ability to manage client relationships.</div>
<div></div>
<div>The Outsource Accounting Group provides all of the benefits of an in-house accounting staff, at a fraction of the cost with considerably augmented expertise. Our clients have more time to focus on their core business operations, while we focus on the processing and reporting of financial information. We customize the scope and frequency of our services to meet each individual client's needs. Our approach emphasizes being an active partner, staying involved in the process, and using the right technology solution to meet our client’s needs.</div>
<div></div>
<div><b>Job Description:</b></div>
<div>Working closely with Senior Management, the professional in this role will provide a broad spectrum of accounting, financial reporting, and payroll services to our clients. This role will also include addressing client issues/questions as they arise and provide problem resolution as needed.</div>
<div></div>
<div><b>Core Responsibilities:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Perform accounting record keeping functions (e.g., maintain G/L, process A/P, A/R, maintain fixed assets, etc.)</li>
<li>Prepare monthly, quarterly and annual financial statements</li>
<li>Reconcile monthly bank statement and post adjustments</li>
<li>Perform account reconciliations to ensure accuracy/completeness of financial data</li>
<li>Assist clients with software support related to QuickBooks and Accounting CS</li>
<li>Perform and review payroll services for clients</li>
<li>Manage client relationships</li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<div><b>Education &amp; Experience:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>A minimum of five years of relevant accounting/bookkeeping experience required</li>
<li>Working knowledge of QuickBooks is required and Accounting CS experience preferred</li>
<li>Bachelor or Associate degree in accounting or finance (or equivalent experience) required</li>
<li>General tax knowledge (sales, payroll, 1040, 1120) preferred</li>
<li>Commitment to integrity, confidentiality and developing and maintaining remarkable relationships with our clients is required</li>
</ul>
</div>
