<h2>Field Service Apprentice</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Gatekeeper Systems Inc., is a rapidly growing global software manufacturing company based in Southern California. A leader in retail loss prevention, Gatekeeper has patented designs to ensure companies prevent theft utilizing intuitive and active technology, while minimizing risks to other customers and security personnel. In 2018, Gatekeeper celebrated 20 years of success, innovation, and industry impact.</p>

<p></p>
<p><b>Essential Job Functions</b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<ul><li> Work under the day-to-day guidance of a Field Services Technician I, II or III</li></ul>
<ul><li> Travel with the Field Services Technician to various areas of the country to complete installations, service and maintenance of the Company’s systems</li></ul>
<ul><li> Assist in the installation and service of the Company's Cart Containment and other systems</li></ul>
<ul><li> Assist in the performance of Preventive Maintenance of the Company’s Cart Containment and other Systems</li></ul>
<ul><li> Assist in the completion of any service and repair work orders</li></ul>
<ul><li> Perform multiple tasks in a fast-paced environment, pay attention to detail, while remaining highly organized and efficient</li></ul>
<ul><li> Take and follow through on directions and instructions while remaining customer focused</li></ul>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>QUALIFICATION REQUIREMENTS</b></p>
<p>To perform this job successfully, an individual must be able to perform each essential duty satisfactorily. The requirements listed below are representative of the knowledge, skill and/or ability required. Reasonable accommodations may be made to enable individuals with disabilities to perform the essential functions.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Perform multiple tasks in a fast-paced environment; adaptability</li>
<li>Pay attention to detail</li>
<li>Be highly organized and efficient</li>
<li>Ability to take directions and follow through on instructions</li>
<li>Be customer focused and a good problem solver</li>
<li>Have good oral and written communication skills</li>
<li>Have experience working with Security Camera Systems, Point of Sales Systems, Electrical Devices, and Wireless Devices</li>
<li>Have experience with dustless concrete cutting and asphalt</li>
<li>Have experience installing devices in a retail environment</li>
<li>Have experience running wires through walls and ceilings</li>
<li>Have experience troubleshooting and tuning electrical devices</li>
<li>Have experience bending and running conduit</li>
<li>Have experience using power tools and scissor lifts</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>EDUCATION AND/OR EXPERIENCE</b></p>
<p>Minimum 1+ years of experience directly related to the installation of devices in a retail environment such as point of sale systems, alarm systems, or security camera systems; experience in electrical and low voltage devices, concrete cutting, bending and running conduit, and running wires through walls and ceilings; and experience with power tools and scissor lifts. Experience as general contractor or related fields are highly desirable. Strong communication and collaboration skills are a must to ensure cooperation and positive results.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>LANGUAGE SKILLS</b></p>
<p>Ability to read, analyze and interpret documents such as safety rules, operating and maintenance instructions, and procedure manuals. Ability to write routine reports and correspondence. Ability to speak effectively to all team members and levels within the organization.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>MATHEMATICAL SKILLS</b></p>
<p>Ability to add, subtract, multiply and divide in all units of measure, using whole numbers, common fractions and decimals. Ability to compute rate, ratio and percent and to draw and interpret bar graphs.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>REASONING ABILITY</b></p>
<p>Ability to apply common sense understanding to carry out instructions furnished in written, oral or diagram form. Ability to deal with problems involving several concrete variables in standardized situations.</p>
