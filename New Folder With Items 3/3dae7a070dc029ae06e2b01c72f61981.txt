<p><b>Marketing &amp; Communications Manager</b><br>
<br>

<b>City/State </b>Little Rock, AR
<br>
<br>
<b>Description</b> <b>GENERAL DESCRIPTION OF POSITION<br>
</b></p>
<p><br>
Our growing company is seeking to hire a Marketing &amp; Communications Manager who will be an integral<br>
part of the Marketing Department in organizing and leading various internal and external client projects.<br>
This job involves monitoring project plans, schedules, work hours, budgets and expenditures, scheduling<br>
and participating in stakeholder meetings, and ensuring that project deadlines are met in a timely<br>
manner.<br>
<br>
This person will tap into their intellectual drive, creativity, and relationship-building attributes to create
</p>
<p>robust marketing and brand strategies in support of growth-oriented short and long-term business
</p>
<p>goals. He/she will execute strategy, branding, messaging, publications, and social media.
</p>
<p>The Marketing &amp; Communications Manager must be highly knowledgeable with and have experience in<br>
all online communication channels: website content, email marketing, social media, and the next digital<br>
medium on the horizon. This individual will demonstrate creativity and intellectual agility to be the voice<br>
of our company in a social media setting and for our customer.<br>
<br>
To be successful this person will need to be able to work on tight deadlines, be competent in using<br>
Microsoft Office applications, and have exceptional verbal, written, and presentation skills. A bachelor’s<br>
degree in a related field of study and at least three (3) years of relevant experience is required for<br>
consideration.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Requirements ESSENTIAL DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES</b><br>
<b>
</b></p>
<p>In conjunction with the Director of Marketing &amp; Communications:
</p>
<ul><li>Work effectively as part of a multi-disciplinary marketing and communications team to</li></ul>

practices to all efforts.
<ul><li>Work with the Director of Marketing to develop marketing strategies that build customer loyalty,</li></ul>
brand recognition and customer satisfaction.
<ul><li>Identify marketing and social media trends affecting client industries and develop/execute growth</li></ul>
strategies to increase constituency engagement, membership recruitment and retention, event<br>
attendance and product sales.
<ul><li>Work with internal stakeholders to determine and coordinate effective marketing strategies,</li></ul>
content development and appropriate distribution channels needed to achieve attendance and<br>
sales goals for client events and products.
<ul><li>Monitors, measures, evaluates, and reports marketing and communication activities and results on</li></ul>
an ongoing basis. Establishes benchmarks, develops supporting reports, and communicate results.
<ul><li>Maintain awareness of industry trends, standards, advancements and influence improvements.<br>
