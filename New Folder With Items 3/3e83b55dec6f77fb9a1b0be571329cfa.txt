<h2>Buyer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>CarOffer is rapidly growing their Dealer Relations team and is seeking a<b> Buyer</b> to help onboard new accounts and source vehicles! The <b>Buyer</b> will provide strategic consultation to our dealer partners regarding their vehicle acquisition strategy as well as assist in sourcing vehicles directly. Our team’s primary focus is to serve our dealer partners as needed and provide consultation around their acquisition, management, and disposal of vehicle inventory, as well as their ongoing use of the CarOffer products and platform.<br>
<br>
<b>Responsibilities:</b>
</div>
<ul>
<li>Train our dealer partners via webinar on how to source vehicles using the CarOffer platform.</li>
<li>Communicate complex, data-driven analysis to key decision makers in order to encourage our partners take full advantage of the platform’s buying capabilities.</li>
<li>Identify opportunities within a dealer’s current vehicle acquisition strategy and, using their previous sales history and market analysis, encourage them to expand their buying parameters.</li>
<li>Help our dealer’s acquire inventory by sourcing vehicles online from used vehicle auctions and other sources.<br>
</li>
</ul>
<div><b>Skills/Requirements:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Experience as an automotive buyer is required with a solid knowledge of inventory acquisition operations.</li>
<li>Must have the ability to prepare for and bid in up to two auctions per day online. Must be familiar with commonly used auction software platforms.</li>
<li>Must have the ability to work in a fast-paced, demanding work environment with a high level of self-motivation.</li>
<li>Must be comfortable and effective leading webinars with up to 10 people in attendance, most of whom are executives and/or dealership management.</li>
<li>Preferred candidates will be strong, confident presenters that are able to handle impromptu conversations and/or objections with ease.</li>
<li>Proficiency in Microsoft Word, PowerPoint, and Excel is a must.</li>
<li>Require 3+ years of Automotive experience.</li>
<li>Prefer 5+ years of Vehicle Buying experience, ideally for a franchised dealer, national group, or as an independent wholesaler.</li>
</ul>
</div></div>
