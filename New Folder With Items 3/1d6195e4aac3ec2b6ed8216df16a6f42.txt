<h2>Customer Care Specialist with Graphic Design</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>This position holds the responsibility of increasing revenue through front counter sales and pro-active outside sales activity, website order management, account management, database marketing and providing an outstanding client experience. Following a prescribed consultative sales process, the Customer Care Representative will manage several forms of client communication to uncover needs, provide solutions and lead the sales process through completion. Role also includes graphic design and wide-format printing (hands-on) in support of the design and production processes on an as needed basis.</p>
<p><b>PRIMARY RESPONSIBILITIES</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Evaluate clients’ needs and develop solutions based on those needs</li>
<li>Make initial marketing follow-up calls to and arrange and conduct visits of existing and prospective customers</li>
<li>Manage client walk-in traffic, incoming calls, and emails and take the appropriate actions</li>
<li>Quality assurance and client service of outgoing jobs</li>
<li>Ability to be a conduit between our operations team and clients in order to ensure strong client service, timeliness and quality.</li>
<li>Oversee in-house front counter sales</li>
<li>Systematic estimate management and follow up</li>
<li>Update client records and purchasing history</li>
<li>Market to existing clients</li>
<li>Maintain clean and organized showroom.</li>
<li>Provide troubleshooting and technical assistance to customers.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>KNOWLEDGE AND SKILL REQUIREMENTS</b></p>
<ul>
<li>At least 2 years business-to-business sales and customer service experience desired</li>
<li>Proficiency in Microsoft Word, Microsoft Excel and Microsoft Powerpoint</li>
<li>Proficiency in Adobe Creative Suite product applications</li>
<li>Professional, upbeat, and outgoing with excellent people skills</li>
<li>Detail-oriented, dependable, and self-motivated</li>
<li>Positive outlook, goal-oriented, and reliable</li>
<li>Excellent strategic thinking skills</li>
<li>Clean driving record with dependable vehicle and valid driver’s license</li>
<li>Expertise in Internet search</li>
<li>Strong communication skills, both written and verbal</li>
<li>Keen multi-tasking abilities</li>
<li>Self-motivated and team player</li>
<li>Ability to continue and build professional relationships with diverse customer/prospect base</li>
<li>Knowledge of sign industry substrates, media, products a plus but not necessary</li>
<li>Knowledge of print industry a plus</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Experience </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Associates Degree, minimum</li>
<li>Previous experience in the sign / graphic industry.</li>
<li>Previous experience in the print industry.</li>
<li>Knowledge of sign / graphic industry substrates, media, and products.</li>
<li>Design software applications:</li>
<li>Adobe InDesign – current version</li>
<li>Adobe Illustrator – current version</li>
<li>Adobe Photoshop – current version</li>
<li>Adobe Acrobat Professional – current version</li>
<li>Microsoft Office – current version (Word, Excel, Power Point)</li>
<li>FlexiSign – current version</li>
<li>Wide-format printer, vinyl plotter, laminator and/or Onyx RIP operation.</li>
<li>Color management concepts and procedures.</li>
</ul>
