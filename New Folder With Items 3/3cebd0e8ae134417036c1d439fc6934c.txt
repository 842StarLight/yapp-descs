The VP, Sales Side Support will partner with our Account Executives to remove technical and business related obstacles in front of a sale, advocating for customer interests to relevant internal teams, and creating a compelling solution sale. As a Solutions expert, you are the technical liaison between customers, service engineering and support teams. You will collaborate with our internal product managers, growth marketers, and also know how to distill technical concepts to our partners.
<br><br>
<b>Your primary responsibilities:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Understand and qualify customer goals and effectively articulate EVERFI's ability to achieve these goals.</li>
<li>Collaborate with strategic sales and contribute to effective technical demonstrations throughout the sales process.</li>
<li>Conduct deep-dive "hands-on" education/training sessions to transfer knowledge to customers considering or already using the EVERFI platform</li>
<li>Gain and maintain a deep understanding of the customers' architectural solutions/environment.</li>
<li>Assist in Sales stages such as opportunity qualification, demonstrations, Proof of Concept presentations (POC), RFP responses &amp; business justification in a technical pre-sales capacity</li>
<li>Ensure the success of customer Proof of Concepts through effective management of success criteria, planning and technical execution</li>
<li>Be an evangelist. Establish credibility &amp; build deep relationships with senior technical individuals within customer organizations to enable them to be EVERFI advocates</li>
<li>Distill and advocate customer interests, needs, and product feedback to internal EVERFI Product and Engineering teams. Maintaining awareness of internal roadmap and new product features to evangelize with customers and distill customer feedback to product management and engineering groups</li>
<li>Capture and share best-practice knowledge amongst EVERFI networks, author or contribute to customer-facing publications such as whitepapers, tutorials, etc.; present sessions at EVERFI and industry conferences</li>
<li>Continuously learn and update skills and keep aware of industry trends in the integration and data management space</li>
</ul>
<b>Desired skills and experience:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>8+ years of experience in a solutions engineering / technical consulting role ideally in the SaaS space</li>
<li>Well-rounded, business savvy professional who is comfortable being customer facing the majority of the time</li>
<li>Industry knowledge or the ability to quickly develop a deep knowledge base in the Corporate Training/Compliance space</li>
<li>Superior organizational skills working with multiple customers and have the ability to switch context quickly throughout the day with various competing demands</li>
<li>Bachelor's degree in Engineering, Computer Science, MIS or a comparable field</li>
<li>Ability to travel 25% to 50%</li>
</ul>
