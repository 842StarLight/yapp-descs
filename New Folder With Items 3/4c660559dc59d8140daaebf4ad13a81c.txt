<h2>Mid-Market Account Executive-Channel Sales</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">Kerauno is seeking a Mid-Market Account Executive that has a successful track record selling software as a service (SaaS), or unified communications as a service (UCaaS). We are looking for a passionate and results driven individual that will be responsible for driving sales and substantial revenue growth within a geographic territory. This is primarily a hunting role however you will be responsible for following up on leads from marketing and channel partner community while exceeding booking targets.
<br><br>
Job Responsibilities
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Define and execute a sales strategy for the assigned territory</li>
<li>Discover and manage sales opportunities through self-generated business development activities</li>
<li>Participate in joint sales calls and assist in closing opportunities</li>
<li>Generate, develop and manage an ongoing pipeline of 4x quota</li>
<li>Track and measure daily activity and sales goals metrics in Salesforce</li>
<li>Provide accurate and detailed weekly, monthly and quarterly forecast funnel of identified and proposed opportunities to meet or exceed quota via Salesforce</li>
<li>Develop a strong understanding of key differentiators, internal/external systems, sales methodologies and processes</li>
<li>Collaborate with internal teams to provide feedback</li>
<li>Negotiate favorable pricing and business terms by positioning the Kerauno value and ROI</li>
<li>Able to travel throughout the region</li>
</ul>
<b>Qualifications:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>5+ years proven success in direct sales preferably in SaaS, UCaaS or related industries.</li>
<li>Demonstrated success in prospecting, engaging and acquiring SMB and Mid-market level customers.</li>
<li>Autonomous, well organized, and results oriented. Creative and proactive thinker.</li>
<li>Thrives in a fast-paced, high-growth and rapidly changing environment</li>
<li>Enthusiastic, team-oriented and customer focused</li>
<li>Exceptional negotiating skills including procurement, licensing and professional services contracts</li>
<li>Advanced proficiency with solution selling, demonstrating in-depth sales and consulting skills</li>
<li>Exceptional oral and written communication skills including the ability to deliver professional documents and presentations to all levels of an organization</li>
<li>Proficient in MS Office and Salesforce CRM and data intelligence tools i.e., DiscoverOrg</li>
</ul>
