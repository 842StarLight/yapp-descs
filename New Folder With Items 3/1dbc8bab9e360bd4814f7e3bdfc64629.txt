<h2>MARKETING - ACCOUNT MANAGEMENT</h2>

<p>We are growing our Marketing Client Services teams and looking for career-minded individuals with the desire to succeed in a high performing, team oriented environment. Our clients are major companies in healthcare, financial, consumer products, publishing, energy, retail and travel industries. There are no sales requirements with this position. ALSO A GREAT OPPORTUNITY FOR A NEWER GRAD!</p>
<p><b>What you will be doing: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Primary or secondary point of contact for assigned client's program(s)</li>
<li>Accountable for maintaining positive, productive relationships with your client</li>
<li>Responsible for day-to-day service delivery for your client program(s)</li>
<li>Implementing, coordinating, and managing the set-up of new client programs with our corporate and contact center staff</li>
<li>Providing contact center staff with program specifications and goals</li>
<li>Generating, analyzing, and delivering regular client program reports</li>
<li>Monitoring and analyzing program performance</li>
<li>Monitoring live agent phone calls</li>
<li>Representing clients internally and addressing ongoing service needs</li>
<li>Proactively identifying and pursuing resolution of issues affecting client programs</li>
<li>Fulfilling established performance levels and continuously making recommendations for improvements</li>
<li>Occasionally traveling to clients headquarters and/or call centers</li>
<li>Presenting or assisting in the presentation of program results and summaries to internal staff/clients</li>
</ul>
