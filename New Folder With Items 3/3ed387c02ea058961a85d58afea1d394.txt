<p><b>Who you are?</b></p>
<p>The Software Sales Consultant possesses advanced knowledge of CemSites solutions. They participate in sales cycles as a member of the sales team in support of organizational sales strategy. A Software Sales Consultant interacts with clients and prospective clients through discovery conversations, product solution demonstrations, executive presentations, and follow-up discussions. He/she is responsible for actively driving the product evaluation stage of the sales process, working in conjunction with the sales team and management as the key solution adviser. The Software Sales Consultant must be able to articulate technology and product positioning through consultative efforts, guiding prospective clients through the delivery of all solution demonstrations and meetings.</p>
<p><b>In the role of Software Sales Consultant, you will wear many hats; your attention will be key in the following: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Design and deliver product presentations based on necessities and competitive sales condition.</li>
<li>Define the best company product design to address the requirements of prospect business.</li>
<li>Responsible for representing the product to clients and prospects at field events such as conferences, seminars, etc.</li>
<li>Expected to maintain and demonstrate a depth of knowledge of product capabilities, value/benefit points, and client stories to be leveraged in-field consulting and demonstration delivery</li>
<li>Able to conceptualize and communicate how CemSites software solutions affect internal efficiency and consumer experience within the organization</li>
<li>Consultative review of prospective customer operations and make recommendations for improving business process opportunities with CemSites solutions.</li>
<li>Perform client and prospect needs assessments and facilitate consulting sessions.</li>
<li>Responsible for development, configuration, and maintenance of product demonstration environments</li>
<li>Able to respond and assist the functional and technical elements of RFIs/RFPs</li>
<li>Able to convey customer requirements to Implementation team, Account Management, and Product Management teams</li>
<li>Able to travel throughout sales territory.</li>
<li>Requesting and coordinating resources required to support the sales department.</li>
<li>Provide responses for product and technical requests.</li>
<li>Other sales operations and support activities as required</li>
</ul>
