<h2>Shift Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">What do Shift Managers do while working for Black Bear Diner?
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>In conjunction with other Managers they oversee shift operations of a Black Bear Diner Restaurant by working with team members to ensure outstanding customer service to maximize sales and to control food, labor and other operating costs.</li>
<li>Maintains shift operations by following established policies and standard operating procedures; implementing production, productivity, quality, and patron-service standards</li>
<li>Controls food costs by reviewing portion control and quantities of preparation; minimizing waste; ensuring high quality of preparation.</li>
<li>Conducts and accounts for precise daily inventory of restaurant food products.</li>
<li>Assists Manager to attract customers by developing and implementing marketing, advertising, public and community relations programs.</li>
<li>Works with Manager to achieve restaurant operational objectives by contributing information and recommendations to action plans; assists with implementing production, productivity, quality, and customer-service standards and resolving problems.</li>
<li>Is learning to assists Managers in accomplishing restaurant human resource objectives by recruiting, selecting, orienting, training, assigning, scheduling, coaching, counseling, and disciplining hourly staff; communicating job expectations; assisting with planning, monitoring, appraising, and reviewing job contributions; planning and reviewing compensation actions; enforcing policies and procedures.</li>
<li>Maintains safe, secure, and healthy environment by establishing, following, and enforcing sanitation standards and procedures; complying with legal regulations; securing revenues; developing and implementing disaster plans; maintaining security and sprinkler systems; maintaining parking lot and walkways. Reports issues following procedures.</li>
<li>Maintains ambiance by controlling lighting, background music, linen service, utensil quality and placement; monitoring food presentation and service.</li>
<li>Continually improves job knowledge by participating in manager meetings, educational opportunities; reading professional publications; maintaining personal networks; participating in professional organizations.</li>
<li>Enhances department and organization reputation by accepting ownership for new and different requests; exploring opportunities to add value to job accomplishments.</li>
</ul>
What you need to be successful with us?
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Experience with or desire to learn how to control food, labor and other operational costs to show increase profits</li>
<li>Experience with or desire to learn how manage people</li>
<li>Experience with or desire to learn how to nurture, coach, mentor and hold team members accountable</li>
<li>Capable to develop team member schedule and flex based on sales volume demands</li>
<li>Skillful to provide outstanding customer service and maintain customer focus to increase sales</li>
<li>Able to resolve conflict with customers and team members</li>
<li>Possess solid communication (verbal and written) and interpersonal skills</li>
<li>Sound decision making and problem solving skills</li>
<li>High level of initiative and ability to multitask</li>
<li>Time management and deadline oriented</li>
<li>Self-Motivated with a High Energy Level</li>
<li>Average minimum 2 years’ experience in food service, preferred</li>
<li>Experience with fast casual restaurant with delivery, preferred</li>
<li>High school diploma/GED, required</li>
<li>Associate’s degree or higher in Hospitality or Food Service, preferred</li>
<li>Must be legally eligible to work in the United States and able to provide appropriate documentation to complete the form I-9 within 3 days of their first day of employment</li>
<li>Must be 18 years of age, acceptable criminal background check.</li>
</ul>
What’s in it for you?
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Competitive starting hourly rate</li>
<li>50% discount toward meals while working a shift</li>
<li>Performance reviews and potential pay increases after 6 and 12 months and annually thereafter.</li>
<li>Advancement opportunities – we prefer to promote from within</li>
</ul>
</div>
