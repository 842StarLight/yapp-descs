<p><b>Job Description
</b></p>
<p></p>
<p>A Sales Engineer (SE) provides technical expertise to support Sales Representatives in successfully selling Curvature’s products and services. Their knowledge of business applications and data center operations practices help an SE earn the role of a customer’s trusted advisor.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>PRIMARY RESPONSIBILITIES
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Works directly with customers to understand their challenges
</li>
<li>Translates product and service features into relevant customer benefits
</li>
<li>Develops and delivers presentations and reports specific to a customer
</li>
<li>Works well in a fluid environment and adjusts to varying demands quickly
</li>
<li>Works independently from a Sales Representative in order to further the sales process
</li>
<li>Mentors other Sales Engineers and provides technical community leadership across the company
</li>
<li>Provides technical support during the sales discovery and prospecting process, including answering questions and requests related to products, and services
</li>
<li>Develops a proactive relationship with Sales Representatives in qualifying opportunities, and developing account plans and pursuit strategies
</li>
<li>Develop solutions based on customer’s needs in order to write customer proposals
</li>
<li>Assembles a pursuit team from internal and/or external resources
</li>
<li>Assists in presenting a proposal by answering technical questions, overcoming objections and developing a revised proposal as required to meet the customer’s needs
</li>
<li>Provides necessary information to Delivery and Operations once business is won
</li>
<li>Provides feedback to Delivery on quality of services, as well as feedback to Product Management on prices and new customer requirements
</li>
<li>Participates in periodic Sales or Service reviews, providing input and helping the team to anticipate customer’s questions and/or concerns
</li>
<li>Evangelizes product and services through internal and external training sessions
</li>
<li>Addresses concerns from Sales team, and escalates to others as needed
</li>
<li>Partners with Sales management to identify gaps in Sales Representative knowledge of products and services
</li>
<li>Participates in pilots, R&amp;D efforts, and custom solutions for unique client needs
</li>
<li>Collaborates with Marketing, Product, and Sales teams to create industry-specific collateral
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>MINIMUM QUALIFICATIONS
</b></p>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Minimum of 10 years’ experience selling or operating enterprise infrastructure in data centers
</li>
<li>Minimum 5 years’ experience as a Subject Matter Expert in one or more of the following technologies: storage, network, hardware or services
</li>
<li>Works well in a fluid environment and adjusts to varying demands quickly
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>PREFERRED QUALIFICATIONS
</b></p>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree in a related field
</li>
<li>Data center operations experience
</li>
<li>Experience configuring and supporting HP, Dell, IBM and Cisco server infrastructure
</li>
<li>Current industry certifications
</li>
<li>International experience
</li>
<li>Familiarity with SalesForce, Workday and SAP as a user
</li>
<li>Able to understand the customer’s business and industry through research and analysis
</li>
<li>Has deep technical knowledge in several relevant disciplines: Server (Mainframe, Midrange, Open Systems, Wintel), Storage (NAS, FC, Backup/Recovery/Archive), Networking and Security
</li>
<li>Demonstrated knowledge of virtualization technologies
</li>
<li>Working knowledge of IT Data Center Operations
</li>
<li>Successful track record of designing innovative solutions collaborating with a customer’s staff
</li>
<li>Able to translate features into customer realizable benefits
</li>
<li>Has superior communications skills
<ul>
<li>interviewing &amp; active listening
</li>
<li>business and technical writing
</li>
<li>presentation design and delivery
</li>
<li>communicates clearly and concisely with confidence and conviction
</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>Is trustworthy
</li>
<li>Proficiency with Office applications; advanced competency with PowerPoint and Excel
</li>
<li>Competency in pre-call planning, call control, leading client meetings and effective follow up
</li>
<li>Understanding of Curvature’s offerings, brand identity, and value proposition
</li>
</ul>
