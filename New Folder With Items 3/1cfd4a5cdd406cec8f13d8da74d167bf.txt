JOB SUMMARY</div>
<div>
The Shipping Clerk will be responsible for receiving and verifying incoming product, including parts and other supplies. This role carries a need for accuracy and integrity as an up-to-date inventory system is integral to plant operations.</div>
<div>
The successful candidate who performs well in this role will have growth opportunities available within the business. Learn more about the Stanley Security business at www.stanleysecuritysolutions.com and learn more about Stanley Black &amp; Decker at www.stanleyblackanddecker.com.</div>
<div></div>
<div>
<br>
ESSENTIAL DUTIES &amp; RESPONSIBILITIES</div>
<ul>
<li>Ensuring that all deliveries are checked in, quantities verified and placed into proper locations or delivered to appropriate person.</li>
<li>Processing all outgoing shipments for sales orders and internal employees. Candidate should be familiar with shipping applications such as Fed Ex Ship Manager and UPS Web software.</li>
<li>Some light assembly will be required; this will include assembling materials to support the system level production team, cable kits to support OEM customer orders and other basic assembly items.</li>
<li>Packaging camera kits and other accessories</li>
<li>Drive forklifts safely and responsibly.</li>
<li>Other duties as assigned</li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<div>
<br>
Required Qualifications<br>
</div>
<ul>
<li>High school graduate or equivalent.</li>
<li>1+ year shipping experience</li>
</ul>
<br>
Preferred Qualifications<br>
<ul>
<li>Knowledge of proper use of hand tools.</li>
<li>Knowledge of proper use of pallet jacks, forklifts, and hoists. (Training/certification program available where needed.)</li>
<li>Ability to understand and follow written and oral procedures.</li>
<li>Ability to fill out all documents and forward to the appropriate recipient.</li>
<li>Ability to perform multi-step transactions in a software application (PC).</li>
<li>Continuous standing and lifting/lowering.</li>
<li>Intermittent walking, use of hand/wrist, and bending/twisting.</li>
<li>Occasionally pushing/pulling, and crouching.</li>
<li>Never sitting or working at heights.</li>
<li>Must carry 1 to 40 pounds occasionally.</li>
<li>40 pounds or more lifting device available.</li>
</ul>
