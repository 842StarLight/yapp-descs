We’re looking for an <b>Quality Assurance Engineer</b> who will work out of our Corporate Headquarters in Sacramento, California. Our business supports a host of web applications which are constantly evolving with the needs of the business. We are looking for a QA tester to join our QA team and help ensure that coding meets requirements and that it all stays working right for our customers. The successful candidate will also have the opportunity to undertake a variety of interesting and challenging projects within a forward-thinking environment. This is a high growth tech company so get ready to go fast!<br>
<br>
<b>Company Description</b><br>
POS Portal was founded in Sacramento in 2000 and is now part of the global Scansource group of companies. Spearheading the Payments business, POS Portal offers the unique opportunity of working for a local tech company with the resources of a billion dollar+ global organization! We enable payments technology and focus on innovations and programs designed to streamline the process of getting secure and maintained Point-of-Sale devices anywhere and everywhere merchants need them. Hardware, software, mobile solutions…we solution for everything in the Payments ecosphere.</p>
<p><b>
Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Create test plans and test cases using test case management system</li>
<li>
Perform both manual and automation tests</li>
<li>
Validate code changes against existing test suites</li>
<li>
Create detailed error reports</li>
<li>
Participate in product and feature design with developers</li>
<li>
Support product deployments to production environment</li>
<li>
Applies and maintains quality standards for the development and manufacture of products</li>
<li>
Promote and execute quality standards, inspection processes, and test methodology, quality plans, documents and reports</li>
<li>
Coordinate code releases, ensuring that products are traceable to documented requirements specifications</li>
<li>
Performs other duties as required to meet business needed</li>
</ul>
