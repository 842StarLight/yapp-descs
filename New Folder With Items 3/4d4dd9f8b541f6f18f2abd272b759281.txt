<h2>Sr. Account Manager - Plastics / Elastomers Custom Molding</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b><i>Quadion LLC/ Minnesota Rubber and Plastics</i></b><i> is a global designer and developer of precision molded plastic, silicone and rubber components, assemblies and finished products for the Medical, Transportation and Fluid Delivery markets. Since 1945, we have built a world-wide reputation in the molding and assembly of products made from elastomers and thermoplastics. Our reputation is founded on our unique science-based approach that has made us the preferred manufacturing partner for industry leaders in North America, Europe and Asia.
</i></p>
<p><i>Our world headquarters is located in Plymouth, Minnesota with manufacturing operations in Litchfield, Minnesota, Mason City, Iowa, River Falls, Wisconsin, Reynosa, Mexico and Suzhou, China.</i></p>
<p>
We are seeking an experienced Senior Account Manager. This is a home office position and the desired candidate will reside in one of the following areas: Atlanta, Cleveland, Philadelphia, Pittsburgh, Chicago, Buffalo or Boston.
</p>
<p>Position Summary</p>
<p>
Responsible for revenue and profit growth of strategic, global accounts spanning across multiple geographies and product segments. Provide leadership within Minnesota Rubber &amp; Plastics to execute account plans in alignment with Water, Transportation and Medical Business strategies. Responsible for overall customer satisfaction and alignment of value proposition to drive organic and inorganic growth.</p>
<p>
Accountabilities</p>
<p>
New Sales/Revenue Generation: Proactively drive new business at existing and new accounts to obtain maximum sales potential, and to achieve or exceed goals for volume and growth at optimum selling price. Includes:</p>
<ul>
<li>Creating a strategic roadmap for protecting existing business, and identifying growth area(s) within the territory</li>
<li>Generating new profitable accounts by cold calling, as well as following up on advertising, trade show and other leads</li>
<li>Attending trade shows and other sales/marketing efforts to represent the organization</li>
<li>Increasing business at existing accounts by anticipating customers potential needs, and active solicitation</li>
</ul>
<p>Account Management: Service existing customer base by developing, maintaining and reinforcing commercial / technical relationships in one or more market segments. Includes:</p>
<ul>
<li>Coordinates with appropriate departments (Engineering, Operations, R&amp;D and Customer Service) to exploit opportunities</li>
<li>Provides prompt guidance to resolve issues / complaints from key accounts.</li>
<li>Acts as interface to provide detailed customer specs and analysis to ensure design and production of custom products are accurate on-time</li>
</ul>
<p>Completes other related items necessary to sales and account management role. Includes:</p>
<ul>
<li>Timely reporting of sub-market opportunities, prospect activity, forecasts / forecast changes and review (and understanding) of commercial reports/analytics</li>
<li>Maintaining product awareness, competitor knowledge and continuing to increase product knowledge</li>
</ul>
<p>Relationship to Others</p>
<p>
Has frequent contact with Customers, Sales Management, Engineering, Advanced Materials, Customer Service, Quality Assurance, and Manufacturing.</p>
<p>
Qualifications</p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor's degree in Mechanical Engineering, Chemical Engineering, Business, or related field is desirable, or equivalent experience</li>
<li>Five or more years of highly technical components sales to OEMs</li>
<li>Excellent interpersonal and written communication skills</li>
<li>Demonstrated ability to effectively organize, prioritize and accomplish multiple tasks, make decisions and solve problems independently</li>
<li>PowerPoint, Outlook, Excel and Word computer skills are required</li>
<li>Must have strong business acumen and ability to lead across functions</li>
