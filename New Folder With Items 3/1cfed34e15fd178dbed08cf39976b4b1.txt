<p><b>KITCHEN DESIGNER</b></p>
<p><b>Job Description</b></p>
<p>Spanning eight decades of innovation and craftsmanship, St. Charles New York is the essence of design leadership and excellence delivering exquisite kitchens that have earned top ranking with master architects, famed designers, and discerning homeowners.</p>
<p>Come join a growing business and a dynamic team. St. Charles New York is seeking an experienced Kitchen Designer specializing in residential remodeling and new construction sales to join its team. We are looking for a creative designer who has a discerning eye and experienced with custom cabinetry, accessories, hardware, lighting and all the "finishing" details in high-end residential projects.</p>
<p>RESPONSIBILITIES AND DUTIES:</p>
<ul>
<li>Conceptualize and execute designs of kitchen solutions with CAD Design Software.</li>
<li>Manage overall progress of projects: conduct site visits, take field measurements, coordination with factories, prepare installation drawings, and field supervision.</li>
<li>Prepare for client presentations.</li>
<li>Budget management on projects</li>
</ul>
<p>QUALIFICATIONS AND SKILLS</p>
<p>The successful candidate will have the following qualifications:</p>
<ul>
<li>Knowledge of CAD.</li>
<li>Prepare accurate construction drawings, elevations, and final documents.</li>
<li>Ability to take design and gather quotes for trades; i.e., countertops, flooring, lighting</li>
<li>Familiar with home renovation.</li>
<li>Excellent communication skills for showroom, clients and vendors.</li>
<li>Ability to work independently and in a team environment</li>
<li>Prefer a minimum of 5-7 years of sales and design experience with a kitchen &amp; bath showroom, residential design build remodeling company or a residential interior design &amp; remodel firm.</li>
</ul>
