<h2>Loan Officer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>The Loan Officer is responsible for loan preparation, evaluation, processing and successfully closing of 1-4 loans monthly. The Loan Officer will analyze and interpret financial statements, tax returns and loan applications with consideration of underwriting guidelines and loan programs being offered by Doorway Home Loans to learn and understand customer’s goals.</p>
