<h2>Senior Tax Accountant</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Position: </b> Senior Tax Accountant</p>
<p><b>Description: </b></p>
<p>A large association in DC seeking a dedicated and experienced Senior Tax Accountant. The individual must possess solid tax provision understanding and ability to work in a dynamic environment with critical deadlines throughout the year; demonstrate and apply a comprehensive knowledge of tax laws and regulations, GAAP, internal controls, and reporting requirements. The individual is expected to identify problems and provide solutions to a variety of complex issues, streamline and improve current processes, and serve as a liaison between the tax team and the other financial and operating staff.</p>
<p><b>Responsibilities: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Prepare accurate and timely state and federal tax and information returns, filings for sales and use tax, foreign countries value-added tax and goods and services tax, applications for business registrations, permits, and licenses.</li>
<li>Develop and maintain complete, accurate and organized work papers and comply with document retention practices.</li>
<li>Prepare tax-related journal entries and perform reconciliations of tax general ledger accounts. Understand how tax and other software applications function and post to the general ledger. Research issues back to the source, effectively summarize findings and recommend appropriate actions.</li>
<li>Perform research on tax issues; understand, and incorporate laws and regulations into analyses, and make recommendations on alternative treatment of issues.</li>
<li>Perform, oversee, or review production processes; maintain critical, fundamental tax information in the Society’s tax software, including customer and product exemptions for sales tax, value-added tax, and goods and services tax purposes; participate in system upgrades and implementations, and coordinate and perform testing before and after system implementation.</li>
<li>Plan and execute complex tasks and projects within time and/or other constraints.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Qualifications: </b></p>
<p>Detail-oriented, organized, excellent customer service and ability to communicate</p>
<ul><li>Bachelor’s Degree in Accounting or related field (M.S. in Accounting/Tax and/or CPA preferred)</li></ul>
<ul><li>6 – 8 years of experience preferably in Public Accounting or a Non Profit</li></ul>
<ul>
<li>Advanced computer skills, PeopleSoft and NVISION knowledge preferred</li>
<li>Ability to set priorities, evaluate results and collaborate with others</li>
</ul>
<p>Job Type: Full-time</p>
<p>Experience:</p>
<ul><li>Accounting and Tax: 5 years (Preferred)</li></ul>

<ul><li>Bachelor's (Required)</li></ul>

</ul>
