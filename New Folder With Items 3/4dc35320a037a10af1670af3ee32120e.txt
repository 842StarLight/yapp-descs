<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Job Details
</b></h2>
<p>The Account Executive is the “quarterback “ of the customer relationship, and we are looking for superstars to grow our team. We are seeking an experienced, quota carrying sales performer who is looking to focus their skills to drive the effective use of technology for social benefit. If you have a passion for technology and an interest in how it can facilitate positive social change, combined with large organization/enterprise technology sales experience, we want to talk with you.
<br>
<br>
<b>Responsibilities:
</b><br>
- Develop of sales plans to effectively and efficiently cover the accounts within an assigned geography.
<br>
- Manage lead qualification and conversion from interested non-profit accounts.
<br>
- Aggressively prospect and generate new contractual relationships with non-profit accounts.
<br>
- Manage the success, adoption and growth of existing customers.
<br>
- Consult with and advising large, strategic nonprofit customers resulting in increased adoption, success and expansion.
<br>
- Inside sales position with limited travel requirements (&lt;5%).
<br>
<br>
<b>Required </b><b>Skills/Experience:
</b><br>
- Track record of success (3+ year) in CRM or related business application sales.
<br>
- Detailed knowledge of SaaS applications (hands on experience with Salesforce, Force.com and AppExchange offerings preferred).
<br>
- Passion for helping advance the mission of non-profit organizations.
<br>
- Strong technical background.
<br>
- Strong interpersonal and presentation
<br>
- Exceptional verbal and written communication skills.
<br>
- Extensive experience with web based conferencing tools such as Go-To-Meeting, WebEx, etc.
<br>
- Ability to work in a fast paced, team environment.
<br>
- Bachelors Degree strongly preferred.
<br>
<br>
Please submit your resume in Word or PDF format only.
<br>
<br>
If hired, a Form I-9, Employment Eligibility Verification, must be completed at the start of employment.<br>
