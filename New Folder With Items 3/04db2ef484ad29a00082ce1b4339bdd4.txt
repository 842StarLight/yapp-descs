<h2>Technical Solutions Consultant - XML</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div></div>
<div>
<div>The Technical Solutions Consultant provides primarily pre-sales, implementation and ongoing support for the development and implementation of products/applications/solutions for our Healthcare vertical with a focus on identity management products; provides technical expertise to sales staff and the customer through sales presentations and product demonstrations; assists in assessing product applications; implements and may provide follow-up support. XML experience is crucial for this product line. Technical Solutions Consultant will partner with other LexisNexis Risk Solutions teams to ensure a holistic approach to software, products and services in support of the client's business needs.</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
<ul>
<li>
Serves as a technical resource and partner to the sales team during the sales process. Serves as a trusted advisor to the client in implementation and process decisions.</li>
<li>
Scopes, prepares and conducts product demos and technical presentations works directly with customers to understand their business needs makes recommendations on optimizing product and processes.</li>
<li>
Understands and documents the technical requirements and implementation specifications for clients. Leads follow-up on requests, resolve technical support issues and communicates solutions directly with client technical teams. Develops recommendations and formally documents implementation recommendations via service delivery plan and presents to client and account team.</li>
<li>
Use project management methods to document, prioritize, track and drive implementations. Manage the delivery of recommend/agree-upon services to achieve high customer satisfaction and trust.</li>
<li>
Create, manage and update existing knowledge base of assigned products. Mentor and coach less senior team members. Cross-train other teams/team members on processes and client-specific implementations.</li>
<li>
Has a detailed understanding of databases, programming, search technologies and web technologies.</li>
<li>
Has a consultative style incorporating technical skills while delivering business value, yet focused on revenue and be willing to take the initiative to ensure that LexisNexis Risk Solutions wins the decision.</li>
</ul>
Qualifications:</div>
<div><ul>
<li>
Bachelor's degree or equivalent years of experience; Preferred - Computer Science, Management Information Systems, Engineering or related discipline.</li>
<li>
Minimum 5-7 years technical experience with demonstrated 3 years of field sales or customer experience. Preferred - Health Care, analytics or data science experience.</li>
<li>
Job duties are varied and complex requiring independent judgment. May have project lead role.</li>
<li>
Subject matter expertise in industry/product space. Preferred - experience in identity management, digital identity or clinical analytics.</li>
<li>
Must possess knowledge of system parameters to control, configure and install in multiple system environments. Preferred experience includes knowledge of SQL and MySQL database, working knowledge of HTML/XML.</li>
<li>
Must possess knowledge of key industry leaders and management. Knowledge of LexisNexis competition.</li>
<li>
Presentation and product demonstration experience.</li>
<li>
Some understanding of consulting arenas.</li>
<li>
Excellent communication skills with prospects and customers.</li>
<li>
25% Travel may be required.</li>
</ul></div>
