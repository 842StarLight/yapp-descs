<h2>Account Executive</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>WHO WE ARE:</b></p>
<p>
Resy is a reservations platform, built and powered by restaurant lovers.</p>
<p>
Launched in New York City in 2014, the company is co-founded by Ben Leventhal, co-founder of Eater.com; Michael Montero, co-founder and former CTO of CrowdTwist and current Techstars mentor; and Gary Vaynerchuk, noted social media expert, entrepreneur and investor. Other company leaders have deep experience in restaurants and hospitality, too.</p>
<p>
Our mission is to power the world's best restaurants, using technology to imagine the future of hospitality. With Resy, you're an insider — and the amazing world of restaurants is just two taps away.</p>
<p>
Rated as one of Built In NYC's "100 Best Places to Work," Resy is actively hiring in multiple cities. Come join our growing team!</p>
<p><b>
ABOUT THE ROLE:</b></p>
<p>
We are looking for a tenacious Account Executive to join our fast-growing outbound sales team focused on providing hospitality technology solutions to restaurants of all shapes and sizes! The ideal candidate is passionate about restaurants and hospitality, self-motivated, solutions-driven, and a dynamic thinker. As an Account Executive, you will be responsible for the entire sales cycle from prospecting to closing. Account Executives work in territory-specific pods alongside Sales Development and Outside Sales Representatives to grow Resy's presence within those territories. Our sales team operates on quarterly revenue goals with weekly and monthly milestones.</p>
<p><i>
Note: While this is a full-time position, your first three months will be considered a trial period. Your first week at Resy will consist of week-long, intensive trainings. During the first month, you will receive clear goals for your first 30, 60, and 90 days. You will have weekly check-ins with the Director of Inside Sales, to keep track of your progress and success. At 90 days, you will be reviewed.</i></p>
<p><i>
Applicants must be based in the New York City area. In your role as an Account Executive, you will be assigned a region outside of the greater New York area. This is an in-office position with no travel, as the selling will be done via phone and e-mail.</i></p>
<p><b>
WHAT YOU'LL DO:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Identify and prospect restaurants to drive new client acquisition via email and cold call</li>
<li>
Sell Resy's technology directly to restaurants across the country through online web demos</li>
<li>
Strategically manage your pipeline to ensure consistent deal flow</li>
<li>
Work closely with the Implementation and Account Management team to support restaurant onboarding</li>
<li>
Achieve and exceed quarterly sales quotas and weekly top of funnel metrics</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
WHO YOU ARE:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
3-5 years of sales experience including at least 1-2 years of closing experience and 1-2 years of Sales Development experience (prospecting and self-generated meetings/demos through both cold calls and emails)</li>
<li>
You are excited to work in a fast-paced startup environment and thrive under pressure</li>
<li>
Software/SaaS experience strongly preferred</li>
<li>
You take pride in consistently achieving your goals and believe failure isn't an option</li>
<li>
You are coachable, self-motivated and a fast learner</li>
<li>
You have strong communication skills, both written and over the phone</li>
<li>
You have excellent time management skills with the ability to recognize and focus on high priority tasks</li>
<li>
You're a great listener, assertive, persistent and persuasive</li>
<li>
You are obsessed with restaurants</li>
<li>
Salesforce.com fluency</li>
</ul>
