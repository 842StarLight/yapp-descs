Job Location : 1L12 5200 New Windsor, NY</p>
<p>
Account Manager’s are not only the primary contact between Coca-Cola, large and small store managers and store owners but are also responsible for building and maintaining customer relationships. Account Manager’s increase business by selling and ordering products within a defined sales territory of existing customers.</p>
<p>
Position Responsibilities:</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<ul>
<li>
Execute and close all sales calls</li>
<li>
Sell in incremental displays, equipment placements and promotional programs ensuring dealer compliance</li>
<li>
Stay in close contact with sales calls,
</li>
<li>Maintain appropriate inventory levels, company assets and point of sale material</li>
<li>
Ensure accounts meet company merchandising standards
</li>
<li>Determine stores product needs by placing and transmitting the appropriate order in conjunction with existing geographic sales routes</li>
<li>
Communicate account activities to appropriate parties</li>
<li>
Transport, replace and maintain Point of Sale advertising as appropriate for account</li>
<li>
Periodic lifting of 50+ pounds, bending, reaching, kneeling and light merchandising</li>
<li>
Build, change and remove product displays as needed</li>
<li>
Maintain product signage, cleaning product space and securing damaged or defective product</li>
<li>
Act as an Ambassador by answering questions, locating product, or providing assistance to consumers and store personnel<br>
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p>Position Minimum Requirements:</p>
<ul>
<li>
High School or GED (General Education Diploma) required</li>
<li>
Bachelor’s Degree preferred</li>
<li>
1+ years of general work experience</li>
<li>
1+ years previous sales experience preferred</li>
<li>
Food/beverage industry experience a plus</li>
<li>
Ability to handle multiple customer accounts</li>
<li>
Strong attention to detail and follow-up skills</li>
<li>
Excellent planning and organization skills</li>
<li>
Proficient computer application skills</li>
<li>
Ability to create and conduct sales presentations preferred</li>
<li>
Valid driver's license and clean driving record within MVR policy guidelines</li>
</ul>
