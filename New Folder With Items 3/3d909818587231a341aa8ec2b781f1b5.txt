 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">

<div>
<p>Job ID: 160632</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
Northfield, a CRH company, is a division within Oldcastle APG. APG is North America’s leading manufacturer and supplier of concrete masonry, dry mix, and hardscape products. With over $2B in sales, APG operates across 36 states and 5 Canadian provinces through a network of over 195 operating locations and more than 6,500 employees.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Role and Responsibilities</b></p>
<p></p>
<br>
<ul>
<li>
Complete assignments in a repetitive and/or sequential order to maximize productivity and maintain consistency</li>
<li>
Use personal protective equipment at all times and follows all safety regulations</li>
<li>
Report any unsafe conditions or defective equipment to the supervisor immediately</li>
<li>
Clean up around machinery and maintain general work areas</li>
<li>
Operate machinery, equipment, and hand/power tools as trained and instructed following safety guidelines (concrete saws, forklifts, dust collectors, shot blaster, etc.)</li>
<li>
Assists lead coordinator in preparing for production of samples and filling sample requests</li>
<li>
Take regular inventory counts of samples and sample supplies</li>
<li>
Package samples for shipping and assist with general shipping and receiving in and out of the facility</li>
<li>
Other duties and responsibilities related to the nature of the job may be assigned on a temporary or permanent basis as needed</li>
</ul>

<p><b>
Requirements/Education/Experience</b></p>
<p></p>
<br>
<ul>
<li>
Ability to read and interpret documents such as safety procedures, operating and maintenance instruction and manuals</li>
<li>
Follow applicable housekeeping and safety procedures</li>
<li>
Ability to lift objects weighing up to 75 pounds each at heights from floor level to 4 feet high</li>
<li>
Must wear personal protective equipment – ear plugs, hard hat, metatarsal steel toe shoes and protective glasses</li>
<li>
High school diploma or GED equivalent; or related experience in trades specific to job requirements; or equivalent combination of education and experience</li>
<li>
Have basic computer skills in Outlook and Excel</li>
<li>
Ability to add, subtract, multiply and divide in all units of measure, using whole numbers, common fractions and decimals</li>
<li>
While performing the duties of this job the employee will be required to frequently stand on their feet for extended periods of time</li>
<li>
Ability to demonstrate lock out tag out procedures</li>
</ul>
