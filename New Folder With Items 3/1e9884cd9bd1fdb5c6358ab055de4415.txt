<h2>Card Sales Leader - Banking and Lending</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>We are looking for smart, dynamic people who can deliver at Equifax. The Equifax Card Sales leader is passionate about Customers, employees, transformation, innovation and growth!</p>
<p></p>
<p>
Reporting directly to the Banking and Lending Strategic Segment SVP, this Senior Sales Leader will have responsibility for driving the direction of the assigned sales teams to ensure profitable growth within the Card Customer segment. He/She is an expert who understands the Card industry and the challenges facing our largest Banking &amp; Lending Clients. The Card Sales Leader provides thought leadership in driving solutions that solve those problems and position all parties for success. The Card Sales leader is Customer-centric and adept at understanding and influencing decision making across complex financial institutions. He or she will be actively involved in the development of new solutions, new go-to-market initiatives, and assessing and analyzing strategic business opportunities.</p>
<p></p>
<p>
Responsibilities:</p>
<ul>
<li><p>
Leverage industry knowledge, creativity, innovation, analytics, data, and technology to drive new solutions for our largest Card Customers.</p></li>
<li><p>
Direct, lead, develop and evaluate assigned sales professionals ensuring the highest level of client satisfaction and achievement of assigned quotas in accordance with company goals, strategy, standards and policies. Leverage and direct the Financial Services Sales teams to develop/execute both short-and long-term sales strategies that maximize revenue, margin, market share and relationships.</p></li>
<li><p>
Develop and clearly communicate goals for managing assigned account(s) along with logical strategies for meeting and exceeding those goals. Clearly communicate goals for the accounts by Line of Business and develop effective strategies to meet client and Equifax goals.</p></li>
<li><p>
Enable team of sales professionals to accurately identify problems, recognize symptoms, and formulate effective solutions and alternatives.</p></li>
<li><p>
Provide market and Voice of Customer feedback to the COE teams regarding competitive offerings and client needs to shape Equifax product and solution development.</p></li>
<li><p>
Establish and maintain strong customer relationships, specifically at the senior leadership level. Consistently explore new growth solutions and retention of existing business.</p></li>
<li><p>
Represent the organization on industry boards, functions and conferences to ensure an active and visible profile in the industry.</p></li>
<li><p>
Recruit, develop and manage team performance. Provide coaching and mentoring to team members to ensure a strong succession strategy is in place. Maintain an effective departmental structure that optimizes resources and skill sets to meet both the customers and the organization's objectives.</p></li>
</ul>
