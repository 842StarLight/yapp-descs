<p><b>Key Responsibilites</b></p>
<div>
<div><div>
<br>
<b>
ROLES AND RESPONSIBILITIES:</b>
<ul>
<li>Manages all aspects of the companies’ accounting department including: Accounts Payable, Accounts Receivable, Payroll, 401K Administration, and Operational Financial Heath</li>
<li>
Month &amp; Year-end preparation: Responsible and accountable for the accuracy of the income statement and balance sheet</li>
<li>
Manages the annual budget, forecast, cash flow, and financial metrics of all operation and support divisions within the companies</li>
<li>
Provides financial analysis as needed to various operational leaders and company Presidents</li>
<li>
Prepares for year end and various other financial audits</li>
<li>
Oversees and reports on various taxes, including but not limited to, gross receipts, sales tax, etc.</li>
<li>
Manages all operational month end financial procedures; job cost, work in progress, and receivables</li>
<li>
Manages the entire accounting division’s scorecard, ensuring goals and actions are achieved</li>
<li>
Participates and presents in strategic planning meetings and ADB’s internal I404 system- complete buy-in to the Keeley Way</li>
<li>
Creates agendas and leads weekly staff meetings and monthly division STP meetings</li>
<li>
Ability to educate management teams on financial health of project managing; understanding the cost and profit of an entire project</li>
<li>
Ability to support a fast paced, demanding work schedule; schedule could include; early mornings, evenings, weekends, etc.</li>
<li>
High level of leadership ability including, but not limited to, making challenging business decisions, gaining a people following, participating in and driving the company culture, holding others accountable, and managing the outcomes of results</li>
</ul>
<br>

<ul>
<li>Bachelors degree in Accounting Preferred</li>
<li>
CPA Preferred</li>
<li>
Masters degree in Accounting a Plus</li>
<li>
10+ Years experience in Construction Accounting</li>
<li>
Advanced knowledge of the construction industry</li>
</ul>
