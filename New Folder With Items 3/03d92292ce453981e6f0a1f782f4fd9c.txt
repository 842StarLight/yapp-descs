<p><b>Mold and Water Technician - Based in Fort Myers Florida</b></p>
<p>Mr and Mrs Restore is looking for an energetic, experienced WATER AND MOLD Technician that is ready to work in the disaster restoration industry. When someone experiences water, fire, or mold damage, you will be called to inspect their property and provide an estimate for cleanup and remediation service. The work environment is fast-paced, hands on, and requires travel in a company provided vehicle within our service area.</p>
<p>Position includes opportunity for growth, and supplemental raises set at performance milestones. Prepared to relocate the qualified candidate.</p>
<p><b>Who Are We?</b></p>
<p>Mr and Mrs Restore provides 24 hour emergency services with water damage, fire damage, mold remediation, and soft goods restoration to homeowners in the South-West Florida area. We empathize with the difficult situations our customers are in, and we're driven to help them and make sure their properties are properly restored. Our goal is to make it as if the damage “never even happened.”</p>
