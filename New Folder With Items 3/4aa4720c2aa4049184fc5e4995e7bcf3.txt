<h2>Systems Engineer, SMB (AMSL2892)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p><b>Overview</b><br>
</p>
<br>
<b>Objective:</b><br>
We are looking for a Systems Engineer to work closely with channel and regional sales representatives in a defined territory. This Systems Engineer's main mission will be to support the sales organization in all technical matters regarding pre and post-sales as well as channel partner enablement. This position will be solely focused on SMB customers. (Customers that are doing 0-100 million in annual revenue.)<br>
<br>
<b>Responsibilities:</b><ul>
<li>
Serve as one of the lead technical contacts for both SMB customers and partners within the assigned region.</li>
<li>
Serve as the main technical resource on sales calls and answer/ educate the customer and partner on issues ranging from features, specifications and functionality to integration.</li>
<li>
Assist the regional account manager and channel partner representatives in qualifying sales leads from a technical standpoint.</li>
<li>
Provide pre and post sales assistance to identified SMB customers within the assigned region</li>
<li>
Provide training and enablement assistance to the channel partners that are within the assigned region</li>
</ul>
