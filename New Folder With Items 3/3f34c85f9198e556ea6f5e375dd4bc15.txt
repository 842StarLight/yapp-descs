<h2>Technical Writer - RFP/RFI Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>
<div>WHO WE ARE:</div>
<div>
Tealium is the leader in real-time customer data orchestration solutions. We help brands seamlessly integrate their siloed data and applications, and drive more profitable interactions across online and offline customer touchpoints. Tealium’s Universal Data Hub (UDH) enables digital, data and analytics, and IT organizations to manage, govern, enrich, activate, and own their overarching customer data strategy, reducing chaos and delivering better customer experiences.
</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>Founded in 2008, Tealium’s award-winning solutions are used by hundreds of global enterprises, including Ancestry.com, T-Mobile, HP, Uber, Facebook, Expedia, Kimberly Clark, LDS Church, Hyatt, Sportsbet, American Airlines, The Gap, Univision, Orange, Sony, and many others.</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
We are looking for an experienced writer to join our Solutions Consulting Operations team as a dedicated resource supporting our RFI/RFP response process. This person must have a general understanding of the Martech space and a firm grasp of Software-as-a-Service. Ideal background would be someone who has left behind a software development or information security career and is interested in focusing full time on writing.
</div>
</div>
<div><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>YOUR DAY TO DAY
</b></h3>
<div><ul>
<li>Write a high volume of RFPs, RFIs, and Due Diligence Questionnaires
</li>
<li>Liaise with various departments throughout the organization to collect information needed to respond to requests, including but not limited to legal, engineering, product management/marketing and sales.</li>
<li>
Maintain consistent product messaging and updated product language.</li>
<li>
Work closely with Sales, Sales Operations, Solutions Consultants, Product Managers, Engineering and others to update existing RFP language and develop new language.</li>
<li>
Handle follow-up work associated with RFPs moving to the next phase: coordinating answers, additional information, etc.</li>
<li>
Oversee production of final proposals (formatting, technical editing and reproduction).</li>
<li>
Coordinate multiple projects simultaneously to meet strict deadlines.</li>
</ul></div>
</div></div>
<div><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>WHY YOU ARE THE PERFECT FIT</b></h3>
<div><ul>
<li>
Strong technical acumen gained through development, engineering, or infosec background.
</li>
<li>Detail-oriented with excellent project management and organizational skills; able to prioritize work and handle multiple tasks to ensure that complex projects are completed by deadline, with interim review steps incorporated.</li>
<li>
Strong verbal and written communication and interpersonal skills as well as the ability to work independently and as part of a team in a large global organization.</li>
<li>
Demonstrated desire and ability to learn technical info</li>
<li>
Demonstrated initiative and good judgment as well as the ability to work well under pressure and with time constraints.</li>
<li>
Interest in and ability to interact directly and effectively with Sales, Solutions Consultants, and Product Managers</li>
<li>
Proficient in Google Suite, and possess the ability to quickly master internal systems and applications.</li>
<li>
College education required.
</li>
<li>1-2 years of RFP experience preferred.
</li>
</ul></div>
