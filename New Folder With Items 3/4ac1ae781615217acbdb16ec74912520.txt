<h2>Senior Store Manager - Bench</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>As a Retail Store Manager – Bench, this position will be trained in all aspects of running a retail business for FedEx Office. Training takes place in one or more FedEx Office retail store locations with a focus on all aspects of running the business . After successful completion of training, the candidate must be open to potential relocation and have the ability to be placed within the specified geographic region.<br>
<br>
The Retail Store Manager – Bench will be assigned a retail store, and will be responsible for managing the overall operations of that retail store including supervision of team members, the administration of store sales performance, profitability, procedural compliance and customer experience objectives. Although the Retail Store Manager – Bench’s management of the assigned retail store during the bench assignment will be overseen by experienced FedEx Office management Team Members, the Retail Store Manager – Bench will have responsibility for daily management of the retail store.</p>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
General Duties and Responsibilities</b></h2>
<p>
(This is a representative list of the general duties the position may be asked to perform, and is not intended to be all-inclusive)</p>
<ul>
<li>
Achieve company objectives for sales and profit performance and customer experience objectives within the assigned retail store</li>
<li>
Direct supervision of team members, including responsibility for:</li>
<li>
Hiring of all team members and monitoring new hire orientation procedures</li>
<li>
Training and evaluating the efficiency and productivity of team members by establishing performance standards and objectives; conducting regular Operations Reviews and Performance Appraisals and making recommendations for wage increases and promotions, when applicable; ensuring execution on all people related initiatives (i.e. bench planning, climate survey follow up)</li>
<li>
Initiating disciplinary procedures, as necessary, for team members, up to and including termination of employment</li>
<li>
Ensuring compliance with Standard Operating Procedures (SOP) as immediate supervisor of team members</li>
<li>
Ensuring a positive customer experience</li>
<li>
Ensure the maintenance of fiscal reporting procedures within the retail store, including accounts receivable, inventory reports, daily sales recaps, and daily bank deposits in full compliance with established company policies</li>
<li>
Monitor and direct marketing activities within the retail store to achieve pre-established sales objectives including monthly marketing calendars, media advertising, specialized sales, in-store signage, etc.</li>
<li>
Recommend equipment and machinery required for efficient production operations and for monitoring inventory level of supplies and materials</li>
<li>
Ensure federal/state law safety requirements are established within the retail store. In addition, may be required to perform quarterly safety inspections of the retail store</li>
<li>
Ensure team members within the retail store are consistently complying with FedEx Office Policies and Procedures</li>
<li>
Ensure retail store cleanliness</li>
<li>
Ensure execution of internal processes</li>
<li>
All other duties as needed or required</li>
</ul>

Minimum Qualifications and Requirements</b></h2>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>
3+ years of related retail experience, including 1+ years of prior progressively responsible supervisory experience required</li>
<li>
Advanced level of reading, writing and mathematical ability</li>

Proven ability to lead, direct and supervise</li>
<li>
Proficient in Microsoft Office tools including Word, PowerPoint, Outlook, and Excel</li>

Bachelor’s Degree or equivalent experience</li>
