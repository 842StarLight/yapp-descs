<h2>Account Director</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>This role will be responsible for prospecting and developing new client accounts with a focus on vendors working with CPG companies in the Northeast. It will also include maintaining and building existing sales relationships with booking clients. In addition to management support and training, will work with the Account Management team to strategize, develop, and execute digital media campaigns for Consumer Package Goods, Retail, and Shopper Marketing accounts.<br>
<br>
<b>Responsibilities</b>
</div>
<ul>
<li>Meet/exceed quarterly and annual revenue growth expectations across all the CPG, Retail, and Shopper Marketing community</li>
<li>Aggressively prospect and close untapped lines of business and/or revenue opportunities within existing client base</li>
<li>Establish and grow relationships with Shopper Marketing agencies, Shopper Marketing, Sales and Insights/Analytics teams at CPG companies including key decision-makers, principals and influencers.</li>
<li>Leverage your knowledge and experience within the Shopper Marketing/CPG vertical to establish yourself as a trusted resource and industry expert for all existing and prospective clients</li>
<li>Confidently present Valassis Digital digital media technology platform and all related benefits at a senior level to Shopper Marketing clients</li>
<li>Identify clients' digital advertising, marketing, and measurement needs; and work closely with internal teams to propose insightful solutions that meet those needs</li>
<li>Travel as needed to company meetings and conferences as well as some travel for team events, training, etc. (&lt;20%)</li>
</ul>
<div><b>Preferred Skills</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Passion for and deep understanding of CPG and Shopper Marketing community-as a whole!</li>
<li>Ability to consistently meet and exceed sales revenue goals</li>
<li>Exceptional communication (written and oral), presentation, listening, and negotiation skills</li>
<li>Ability to cultivate and grow strong relationships with clients</li>
<li>Ability to think strategically, understand marketer needs, and creatively apply suite of solutions to solve for those needs</li>
<li>Entrepreneurial, self-starter with "can’t lose" attitude</li>
<li>Ability to prioritize and multi-task while staying focused and organized</li>
<li>Knowledge of and experience in Digital Media and Ad Tech</li>
<li>Collaborative, team player who works well internally with other salespeople and non-sales colleagues</li>
</ul>
<div><b>Qualifications</b></div>
<ul>
<li>BS, BA or equivalent from an accredited 4-year university or college is preferred</li>
<li>2+ years demonstrated success in Sales, working with the CPG/Shopper Marketing or digital vendor community</li>
<li>Experience selling Shopper Marketing and/or other CPG-oriented solutions. Prior digital media sales a plus, but not required</li>
</ul>
