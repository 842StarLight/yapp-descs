<h2>Customer Contract Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Reynolds and Reynolds is an industry leader in developing software and business forms for the automotive industry. These products enable car dealerships to operate more efficiently and effectively in a competitive marketplace.</p>
<p><b>Responsibilities: </b></p>
<p>As a Customer Contract Specialist, you will be responsible for processing customer termination requests according to their current contract terms. You will also be responsible for providing contract payoffs for both internal and external customers. Additional responsibilities include preparing cancellation exhibits and providing contract copies.</p>
<p>On the job training in a team environment; team lead will guide employee through a 3 month training program.</p>
<p><b>Requirements: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor's degree required</li>
<li>Proficient typing skills</li>
<li>Experience in Microsoft Excel and Word</li>
<li>Must be self-motivated</li>
<li>Excellent communication skills and attention to detail</li>
</ul>
