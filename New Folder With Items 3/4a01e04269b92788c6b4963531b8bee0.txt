<div><b>
POSITION SUMMARY</b></div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
In order to grow our existing consulting practice, we are currently seeking an Intacct Financial Systems Consultant in our Nashville, TN offices. Qualified consulting candidates must have a strong financial and accounting background with a desire and aptitude for understanding and applying technology to solve business problems. Previous experience in consulting is preferred but not required. In addition, the qualified consulting candidate will have experience in the financial areas of revenue recognition and the effects of new accounting rules around ASC 606.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
Depending upon the candidate's experience and current skills sets, LBMC will customize a comprehensive Intacct technology training program. LBMC will also provide on-the-job business consulting training, and an exciting career path in the world of Financial System Consulting.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
The qualified candidate should have a detailed understanding of accounting standards and methodologies and related business processes. Occasional overnight travel will be required dependent upon the project.</div>
<div>
Once trained, responsibilities would include business requirement discovery and analysis, software implementation, software configuration, data migration, data integration, training, and customer support. In addition, once experience is obtained, consultant may support the sales process utilizing business and technology experience, and demonstration of Intacct Financial solutions.</div>
