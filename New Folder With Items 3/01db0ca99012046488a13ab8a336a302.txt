</h1>
<p>Field Marketing Representative – Part Time<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p>Job Title:
<br>
Field Marketing Representative<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p>Summary:
</p>
<p>This position is at the forefront of MTI Global Services’ Sales and Marketing efforts to increase client sales at
<br>
the retail store level. The candidate
<br>
should have diverse skills that include a focus on detail, relationship
<br>
building and the ability to communicate effectively with key personnel (retail
<br>
store sales associates, general managers, sales managers) and consumers. This position combines weekly or bi-weekly
<br>
training store visits combined with periodic project based selling day and
<br>
special events.
</p>
<p>Responsibilities:
</p>
<ul>
<li>Primary
<br>
duty is to create consumer awareness, product brand preference, and
<br>
product mind share for hardware and software companies at the retail store
<br>
level
</li>
<li>Influence
<br>
others to share enthusiasm for both the company and the product line
</li>
<li>Ability
<br>
to merchandise client products and/or manage the client objectives in an
<br>
assigned territory
</li>
<li>Position
<br>
requires gathering client product competitive intelligence at the store
<br>
level
</li>
<li>Effectively
<br>
collect and communicate product information/data through appropriate
<br>
channels
</li>
<li>Efficiently
<br>
and effectively train retail personnel on products and selling techniques
</li>
<li>Ability
<br>
to visually access on-site needs i.e., product and promotional/marketing
<br>
material stocking
</li>
<li>Meet
<br>
timely required reporting deadlines
</li>
<li>Periodic
<br>
travel to attend other company sponsored training events or client
<br>
sponsored special events, or to cover other market areas as needed
</li>
<li>Occasionally
<br>
assist in selling days – selling products directly to customers in retail
<br>
stores.<br>
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p>Skills and Requirements:
</p>
<ul>
<li>Good
<br>
knowledge and experience with consumer electronics and various other
<br>
consumer-based electronics products
</li>
<li>Technically
<br>
savvy and computer proficient
</li>
<li>Computer
<br>
with daily email access
</li>
<li>Strong
<br>
oral and written communication skills
</li>
<li>Must
<br>
have the willingness and ability to build relationships at the retail
<br>
store level on behalf of both MTI Global Services and our clients
</li>
<li>Professional
<br>
image and approach to business
</li>
<li>Friendly
<br>
demeanor, easy to approach, energetic
</li>
<li>Group
<br>
training, demonstrating or presentation experience
</li>
<li>Retail
<br>
sales or retail management is a plus
</li>
<li>Quick
<br>
learner who is self-motivated
</li>
<li>Ability
<br>
to self-train on client product(s)
</li>
<li>Ability
<br>
to lift 20 pounds, as needed (for arranging store displays)
</li>
<li>Reliable
<br>
transportation with valid state driver’s license and proof of auto
<br>
insurance<br>
</li>
</ul>
