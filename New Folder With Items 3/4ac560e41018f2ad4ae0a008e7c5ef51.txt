<p>Established in 1997, DQE Communications (DQE) is a subsidiary of Pittsburgh-based Duquesne Light Holdings and headquartered in Pittsburgh’s Southside Works development. DQE Communications is one of the leading providers of high-speed, fiber-optic data networking for business throughout thirteen -counties in southwestern Pennsylvania and West Virginia. Already in over 1600 commercials buildings with approximately 3,250 route miles, DQE is continually working to expand its network.
</p>
<p>Through Metro Ethernet, Wavelength Services, Dark Fiber, Internet Services and Colocation services, DQE offers its customers secure, reliable and flexible network services.
</p>
<p>Through Metro Ethernet, Dark Fiber, Internet Services and Colocation services, DQE offers their customers secure, reliable, flexible network services unmatched by any other provider in the region.<br>
</p>

<p>Job Family: Sales

<p>Discipline: Major Account Manager IV – Sales Manager

<p></p>
<p>Overall Purpose:
</p>
<p>The Sales Manager will have responsibility for Managing lower level Sales Executives (Sr. Sales Associate and Account Executive) day to day activity, sales funnel and sales process activity.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p>Job Responsibilities:
</p>
<p><b>Responsible to meet a monthly revenue quota goal associated with the Sr. Sales Associate and Sales Associates sales quotas </b><b>.
</b><br>
<b>Accountable for meeting and/or exceeding assigned sales objectives and monthly revenue quotas, maintaining existing and building new revenue within associated team members account base by selling DQE products and services to Enterprise customers.
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
<b>Must provide superior customer service on a day to day basis </b><b>.
</b>
</li>
<li><b>Must be self-motivated and self-disciplined and provide prompt follow-up to all customer inquiries.
</b></li>
<li><b>Will build and maintain accurate and up to date account management and sales professional’s management plan.
</b></li>
<li><b>Acquire and integrate industry knowledge related to general trends, emerging technologies, &amp; competitors.
</b></li>
<li><b>Partner with customers through a strategic and consultative sales approach to understand their business needs, issues, strategies and priorities to deliver a value-adding business solution.
</b></li>
<li><b>Oversight management of sales funnel to analyze and manage pipeline activity and monitor sales activity against assigned associate’s quotas.
</b></li>
<li><b>Utilize Excel to create spreadsheets and manage data related to customer accounts and sales funnel management.
</b></li>
<li><b>Manage CRM sales contact and funnel Software.
</b></li>
<li><b>Must have excellent verbal and written communication skills and be able to present in both small and large group settings.
</b></li>
<li><b>Must be able to keep organized, accurate records on daily activities and results.
</b></li>
<li><b>Strong organizational skills imperative.
</b></li>
<li>
<b>Must have a polished professional presentation to Customer </b><b>and Prospects.<br>
</b>
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p>Supervisory:
</p>
<p>Coordinate the work flow of a given team. Support the review of work and training of other employees. Reports directly to Director of Sales.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p>Education/Experience Requirements:
</p>
<p>Bachelor’s Degree or above preferred. 7+ Years Major Account Management Experience in Telecom or related field.</p>
