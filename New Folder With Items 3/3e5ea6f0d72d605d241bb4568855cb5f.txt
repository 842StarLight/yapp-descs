<h2>Events Coordinator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div><div>The Events Coordinator is responsible for supporting the Marketing team in the execution of Innovative’s global tradeshow and events programs.<br>
<br>
DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES<br>
Essential Functions:<br>
<ul>
<li>Works with colleagues, convention staff, library associations, and labor to effectively plan and execute industry events for the company, including but not limited to internal communication, booth and equipment logistics, staff scheduling, and materials handling.</li>
<li>Manages event budget and handles all financial aspects required to execute assigned events.</li>
<li>Collaborates with marketing team to ensure show attendance, booth designs, and sponsorships align with corporate brand identity and strategic goals of the business.</li>
<li>Ensures staff are fully prepared by distributing event communication prior assigned events.</li>
<li>Serves as liaison with vendors such as hotel, AV, signage/printer, booth staff, and others on event-related matters.</li>
<li>Assists with managing on-site production and clean up for events as necessary.</li>
<li>Create, update and distribute event emails through marketing automation tool.</li>
</ul>
Additional Responsibilities:<br>
<ul>
<li>Assist sales as needed with weekly requests for packing, shipping materials to customer meetings.</li>
<li>Maintains marketing materials and collateral shipments, as needed for events.</li>
<li>Coordinates staff hotel room blocks in conjunction with events.</li>
<li>Track and submit large event and marketing-related expenses.</li>
<li>Maintains corporate promotional item inventory, track incoming requests.</li>
<li>Assists with other tasks for the marketing team as requested.</li>
</ul>
<br>
EXPERIENCE AND QUALIFICATIONS<br>
Required<br>
Education, Licenses, and Certifications:<br>
<ul><li>Bachelor’s degree in marketing, communications, event planning, or other related field; or some college with at least 4 years of related experience.</li></ul>
Experience:<br>
<ul>
<li>Minimum 2 years of experience in event marketing and trade show exhibit management.</li>
<li>Demonstrated experience in event planning.</li>
</ul>
Critical Knowledge, Skills, and Abilities Requirements:<br>
<ul>
<li>Basic office software knowledge (Word, Excel, PowerPoint, etc.).</li>
<li>Ability to work effectively in team environments, is dependable, motivated, and responsive.</li>
<li>Ability to work with all levels of management.</li>
<li>Ability to manage multiple projects and priorities simultaneously</li>
<li>Strong organizational skills, detail-oriented, responsible, and self-directed.</li>
<li>Willingness to pitch in across tasks to help make events and corporate marketing initiatives a success.</li>
<li>Demonstrate sound judgment and the poise to interact with people from across the company, demonstrate integrity, and ability to handle stressful situations.</li>
</ul>
ADDITIONAL REQUIREMENTS OR INFORMATION<br>
<br>
<ul>
<li>Experience using Marketo a plus.</li>
<li>Strong writing skills.</li>
<li>Although duties are typically performed during normal business hours, occasional off-hours work may be required to meet customer and/or business needs.</li>
<li>Position is required to travel domestically and internationally at least 50% of time for onsite demonstrations, conferences, etc.</li>
</ul>
