<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities<br>
</b></h2>
<div></div>
<div><div><div>
<ul>
<li>Ability to work with Business Owners under the guidance of Senior Team members, and gradually take this function over.</li>
<li>
Provides functional and technical support on CRM applications.</li>
<li>
Manages and prioritizes support needs and training, in cooperation with the CRM user community (Sales Operations users and Sales Team). Besides reactive support focuses on finding permanent solutions (proactive approach) by means of functional analysis and optimizations within the framework of the existing technologies.</li>
<li>
Assists in the future implementation of CRM software, under the guidance of a senior Applications team member. Owns the support of this software once the system has been implemented.</li>
<li>
Documents in a structured way all the different support issues in order to optimize future support calls. This library of documentation will also help in doing root cause analysis, in order to solve the root causes of the issues, rather than just solving the symptoms.</li>
<li>
Assists when needed with the development, configuration and testing of CRM applications, under the guidance of a senior team member.</li>
<li>
Be a Second-tier support provider for sales support, troubleshooting, research and resolution of issues</li>
<li>
Assists in (regression) testing of new system modules or system improvements in cooperation with the QA Teams.</li>
<li>
Own the daily operations administration of Salesforce.com including but not limited to: Initial user setup, report creation, account/project assignment, layout changes, workflows while interfacing with business leaders</li>
<li>
Interfaces with all levels of the organization (from senior management to operators) in order to understand the specific support problem, identify its causes and guarantee a timely and quality solution, in order to achieve the highest level of customer satisfaction.</li>
<li>
Fully understands the complete CRM processes from a functional viewpoint, so problems can be easily identified in the correct context and efficient problem resolution can be guaranteed. Excellent database skills will contribute to an efficient troubleshooting process.</li>
<li>
Documents all current and future CRM related processes from an IS perspective.</li>
<li>
Some travel is required for training End Users. (10%)</li>
<li>
Provides end user support on the CRM systems on a 24 / 7 basis.</li>
<li>
Performs other duties as assigned.</li>
</ul>

<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>About Mohawk
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div>Mohawk Industries is a leading global flooring manufacturer that creates products to enhance residential and commercial spaces around the world. Mohawk’s vertically integrated manufacturing and distribution processes provide competitive advantages in the production of carpet, rugs, ceramic tile, laminate, wood, stone and vinyl flooring. Our industry-leading innovation has yielded products and technologies that differentiate our brands in the marketplace and satisfy all remodeling and new construction requirements. Our brands are among the most recognized in the industry and include American Olean, Daltile, Durkan, IVC<b>,</b> Karastan, Marazzi, Mohawk, Mohawk Home, Pergo, and Quick-Step. During the past decade, Mohawk has transformed its business from an American carpet manufacturer into the world’s largest flooring company with operations in Australia, Brazil, Canada, Europe, India, Malaysia, Mexico, New Zealand, Russia and the United States.</div>
