<h2>Staff Accountant</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>Job Summary:<br>
The Staff Accountant for the Starwood Infrastructure Finance (“SIF”) segment will assist the Assistant Controller in accounting and reporting on the company’s assets by maintaining the appropriate controls, reconciliations and reports over investment accounts, calculations of interest and principal collections, payments to vendors and all material balances and transactions.<br>
<br>
Job Duties and Responsibilities:<br>
<ul>
<li>Reconciles cash activity related to collections and the clearing of the interest and principal receipts by loan.</li>
<li>Works with Treasury and Loan Servicing in resolving problems with cash transactions.</li>
<li>Prepares loan acquisition, sale, and transfer entries.</li>
<li>Prepares monthly bank reconciliations.</li>
<li>Runs reports and assists with variance explanations as needed for Flash.</li>
<li>Performs and assumes other duties and responsibilities as may be required by the Assistant Controller of SIF.</li>
</ul>
</div>
<br><div>Knowledge and Skills:<br>
<ul>
<li>Detailed, analytical, and organized.</li>
<li>Skilled in interpersonal communications.</li>
<li>High degree of skill in using the PC and Excel, plus ERP (PeopleSoft) software.</li>
<li>Positive problem-solving mentality that seeks solutions and improvements to processes to enhance the reporting and accuracy of the department and company.</li>
<li>Skilled in the analysis and explanation of variances and the ability to dig in to the details to understand financial transactions.</li>
<li>Focus on accuracy and timeliness of the monthly close and reporting processes.</li>
</ul>
<br>
Education and Experience:<br>
<ul>
<li>Bachelors in Accounting with Masters preferred</li>
<li>Big 4 public accounting experience</li>
<li>Experience that demonstrates the ability to apply accounting theory and analysis to solve and document financial transactions</li>
</ul>
</div>
