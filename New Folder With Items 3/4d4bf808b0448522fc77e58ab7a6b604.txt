<div>
The Upwork Sales team brings our contingent staffing solutions to SMB customers. As a Customer Success Manager. You will own the day to day operations of product and services offerings including customer education, expansion, and retention for our high value marketplace customers. You will partner cross-functionally to swiftly resolve customer issues to delight our clients every step of the way. As a team member, you’ll be responsible for a book of current customers and identify strategies to keep them engaged. This role reports to our Manager of Customer Success and based in Chicago, IL.</div>

<div><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>You Responsibilities:</b></h3>
<div><ul>
<li>
You’ll work closely with assigned accounts to establish lasting relationships with decision makers and users to ensure repeat usage on the platform</li>
<li>
You'll execute platform training for users to become comfortable with the platform and how to engage with Freelance and agency talent</li>
<li>
You'll develop adoption plans, establish communication with key partnerships of each assigned accounts.</li>
<li>
Establish communication with decision makers, manage reporting to measure success against customer expectations, and drive the renewal process</li>
<li>
Implement strategies to maximize customer lifetime value and minimize churn risk while increasing overall customer satisfaction and identifying up-sell and cross-sell opportunities</li>
<li>
Develop a deep understanding of the product to recommend the software’s features and functionality that meet customers' specific business needs</li>
<li>
Consistently bring creative ideas and solutions to the team on how to improve the customer experience</li>
</ul></div>
</div></div>
<div><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>What is needed:</b></h3>
<div><ul>
<li>
2+ years of experience in customer success, sales, or account management in staffing or Human Capital Management. SaaS success/sales/recruiting experience is desirable</li>
<li>
Passionate about providing an exemplary customer experience and building lasting relationships</li>
<li>
Demonstrated understanding and application of effective service and selling strategies</li>
<li>
Exceptional organizational and communication skills – both verbal and written</li>
</ul></div>
</div></div>
<div><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>What it takes to catch our eye:</b></h3>

2+ years experience in day to day operations in a post sale environment in either a hyper-growth environment or within the Human Capital Management space</li>
<li>
Experience working with multiple clients and creating operational playbooks to insure service quality and responsiveness for reported issues</li>
<li>
Previously demonstrated success in expansion, upsells and retention in a highly competitive environmentIntimate knowledge in training customers through multiple mediums</li>
