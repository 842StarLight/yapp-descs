<h2>Account Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Job Title:</b> Manager, Account Services (Rev. 02/2014)</p>
<p><b>Organization Unit:</b> Account Management</p>
<p><b>Reports To:</b> Director, Account Services</p>
<p><b>FLSA Status:</b> Exempt</p>
<p><b>Job Status:</b> Full-Time</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Summary:</b> Responsible for meeting the needs of clients in all aspects of being partially self-funded from the implementation of a new group to the renewal of existing business by managing the day to day supervision, training and development of account managers.</p>
<p><b>Essential Functions:</b></p>
<ul><li> Renew existing cases and deal with various stop loss vendors and brokers</li></ul>
<br>
<p></p>
<ul><li> Train and develop Account Managers</li></ul>
<br>
<p></p>
<ul><li> Coordinate the scheduling of account managers to make sure there is coverage in the office.</li></ul>
<br>
<p></p>
<ul><li> Deal with any day to day issues the account managers need help with</li></ul>
<br>
<p></p>
<ul><li> Provide support to the Director of Account Services on special projects or issues</li></ul>
<br>
<p></p>
<ul><li> Process renewals including quotes, spreads, presentations, and set-up</li></ul>
<br>
<p></p>
<ul><li> Coordinate with claims/customer service on claim payments, corrections and letter writing</li></ul>
<br>
<p></p>
<ul><li> Assist with fact sheet meetings on new accounts</li></ul>
<br>
<p></p>
<ul><li> Coordinate with actuary on projects and renewals</li></ul>
<br>
<p></p>
<ul><li> Schedule and conduct client meetings/monthly/quarterly</li></ul>
<br>
<p></p>
<ul><li> Review plan documents for benefit changes that could produce savings to groups upon renewal</li></ul>
<br>
<p></p>
<ul><li> Consult with broker, client and carrier regarding amendments and plan documents</li></ul>
<br>
<p></p>
<ul><li> Contact various PPO vendors regarding addition of doctors, hospitals and other services</li></ul>
<br>
<p></p>
<ul><li> Consult with PBMâ€™s on prescription benefits, changes or additions</li></ul>
<br>
<p></p>
<ul><li> Troubleshoot complaints regarding claims, etc.</li></ul>
<br>
<p></p>
<ul><li> Participate in special projects (mail outs, benefit fairs, etc.)</li></ul>
<br>
<p></p>
<ul><li> Inform and educate clients about coverage, exclusions and exposures</li></ul>
<br>
<p></p>
<ul><li> Perform other duties as assigned</li></ul>
<ul><li> Perform all tasks in accordance with HIPAA guidelines</li></ul>
<br>
<p></p>
<ul><li> Complete HIPPA training annually</li></ul>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Qualifications:</b></p>
<p>To perform this job successfully, an individual must be able to maintain a high level of accuracy and attention to detail to perform each essential duty satisfactorily. The requirements listed below are representative of the knowledge, skill, and/or ability required. Reasonable accommodations may be made to enable individuals with disabilities to perform the essential functions.</p>
<p><b>Education and/or Experience:</b></p>
<p>High School Diploma or GED required, Bachelor degree preferred, and a minimum of three years of third party administrator account management experience to include three years of GPA account management experience required or equivalent combination of education and experience.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Licenses and/or Certificates:</b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>Texas General Lines Agent license required. Must possess a valid driver license, proof of automobile insurance and a clean DMV record.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Language Ability:</b></p>
<p>Ability to read and interpret documents such as HIPAA compliance, safety rules, operating and maintenance instructions, and policy and procedure manuals. Ability to write routine reports and correspondence. Ability to speak effectively to other employees of GPA, Brokers, clients, vendors, or customers. Advanced presentation skills before others required.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Math Ability:</b></p>
<p>Ability to calculate figures and amounts such as discounts, interest, commissions, proportions, and percentages. Ability to apply concepts of basic mathematics and fundamental accounting principles.</p>
<p><b>Reasoning Ability:</b></p>
<p>Ability to apply common sense understanding to carry out instructions furnished in written, oral, or diagram form. Ability to deal with problems and exercise sound judgment involving mulitlel variables in standardized situations.</p>
<p><b>Computer Skills:</b></p>
<p>To perform this job successfully, an individual must have intermediate knowledge and skills using Microsoft Office including Word, Excel, and PowerPoint software; Internet software; and Database software.</p>
<br>
