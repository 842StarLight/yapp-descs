<h2>AppD Salesforce Business Analyst (4773)</h2>

<p><b>About Us</b></p>
<p>
AppDynamics is an application performance monitoring solution that uses machine learning and artificial intelligence (AI) to provide real-time visibility and insight into IT environments. With our unique AIOps solution, you can take the right action at exactly the right time with automated anomaly detection, rapid root-cause analysis, and a unified view of your entire application ecosystem, including private and public clouds. Using AppDynamics, you’ll finally align IT, DevOps, and the business around the information that helps you protect your bottom line and deliver flawless customer experiences at scale.</p>

<p><b>
About You</b></p>
<p>
You have a proven track record of successfully documenting and communicating complex business requirements for both technical and non-technical audiences. You are able to transform these requirements into effective and innovative solutions on the <b>salesforce.com</b> platform You have:<br>
<br>
</p>
<ul>
<li> 5-7 years of experience delivering enterprise software solutions using <b>salesforce.com</b> in an enterprise environment</li>
<li> Experience with sales and sales operations business processes: territory planning, sales productivity, partner operations, quote-to-cash, license provisioning</li>
<li> Experience and passion for designing or configuring Lightning solutions</li>
<li> Experience communicating with senior VPs to drive alignment amongst business stakeholders from divergent functions.</li>
<li> Experience writing detailed specifications of user requirements, business processes and procedures required to develop and implement software solutions</li>
<li> Desire to work independently in a fast-paced high-growth team environment.</li>
<li> Deep knowledge of the <b>salesforce.com</b> platform, including complex configuration, data and governance.</li>
<li> Project management experience</li>
<li> Meticulous attention to detail and exceptional communication skills.</li>
<li> Experience with CPQ (Configure Price Quote)</li>
<li> Experience with Netsuite and ETL tools are a plus.</li>
<li> Knowledge of scrum or agile processes are a plus.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
About The Role</b></p>
<p>
AppDynamics’ Business Information Systems Team is looking for a Salesforce Senior Business Analyst to support the Sales Operations team for the quote-to-cash and license provisioning business processes. The person in this role will work closely with the sales organization to scope, design and implement high quality technical solutions in <b>salesforce.com</b> and other integrated systems. Responsibilities include:<br>
<br>
</p>
<ul>
<li> Create and maintain all project documentation: (Project plans, Business Requirement documents, Solution Design Documents.)</li>
<li> Autonomously run projects end-to-end</li>
<li> Ensure that requests from the Sales organization are effectively qualified, prioritized and executed</li>
<li> Assist the Sales organization in their transition to the Lightning UI</li>
<li> Help business stakeholders prioritize and evaluation business needs</li>
<li> Collaborate as needed with various departments throughout the organization, including sales, marketing, finance</li>
<li> Collaborate with developers as needed to design and build solutions</li>
<li> Configure and test <b>salesforce.com</b> solutions</li>
<li> Plan and execute end user training and testing of new features and applications</li>
</ul>
