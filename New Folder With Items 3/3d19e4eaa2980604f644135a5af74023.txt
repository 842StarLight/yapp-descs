We are currently seeking a Director, Sales - Automated Content Recognition (ACR) Data for the advertising technology ecosystem.
<br>The Director of Sales will drive revenue for Nilesen’s ACR vertical by leveraging Smart TV Data to enable addressable advertising for Demand Side Platforms, Supply Side Platforms, Data Management Platforms and other Advertising Technology companies to enable signicantly better advertising performance.
<br>This position reports to the VP ACR Sales and will regularly collaborate with the Gracenote C-Level executives, Product Managers, Business Development, Finance and HR Teams on Media revenue strategy and partnership opportunities.
<br><br>
<b>IN THIS ROLE YOU WILL:</b><br>
Use strategic sales methodologies including prospecting and developing relationships, analyzing needs and opportunities, negotiating contract terms, documenting activity and thoroughly following up with customers to exceed revenue targets set for the territory.
<br>Assist in the development of the ACR vertical revenue plan, specifically advising on: realistic forecasts for product and market segments in the N. American territory (based on historical data, market trends, competitive activity, and promotional strategy and sales effort).
<br>Implement strategies to ensure expansion of current customers.
<br>Utilize Salesforce to enable monitoring sales performance and productivity, as well as optimize activity
<br>Communicate with operations to deliver products and services to customers in assigned territory.
<br>Identify new business opportunities and improve current products by maintaining a strong working knowledge of industry trends and competitive threats.
<br>Assist with developing and implementing competitive sales pricing and marketing strategies that allow Gracenote/Nielsen to compete and win major industry clients.
<br>Work with internal stakeholders to drive product development and continuous improvement; coordinate targeted marketing campaigns and be the voice of Gracenote/Nielsen with key reference customers.
<br><br>
<b>FOR THIS ROLE WE ARE LOOKING FOR INDIVIDUALS THAT HAVE:</b><br>
Proven track record of new direct sales revenue execution within the digital media services space, with business transactions in the six and low seven figures per deal.
<br>Track record of 5+ years of executive level strategic sales combined with exceptional tactical execution.
<br>Exceptional executive poise, communication, leadership, presentation, time management, multi-tasking and organization skills.
<br>Solid track record of good business judgment and instincts.
<br>Past proficiency or ability to quickly become proficient with salesforce.com
<br>Bachelor’s degree in Business, Marketing or related field preferred.
<br>The ability to travel as required.
