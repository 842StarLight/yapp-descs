<h2>Sr. Director, SEO</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>Job Description<br>
<br>
<p><b>Overview:</b></p>
<p>
The Group Director (GD) is responsible for leading the execution teams and PC department vision. The GD is also responsible for overseeing the cross channel service delivery and integration for clients, including paid search, display, social, mobile, and video. The GD will manage and direct Performance Content teams, including Content Directors, Content Managers, and Associate Content Managers.</p>
<p>
The GD is accountable for the implementation of SEO/content/CO execution and ensuring seamless delivery of best practice and processes. Proven account management and team leadership experience is essential, along with experience in online marketing and digital media.</p>
<p><b>
Role Objectives:</b></p>
<p><b>
Account Development</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Develops, manages, and monitors client service delivery executions of the assigned accounts</li>
<li>Oversees process development and execution management</li>
<li>Leads SEO client services and owns departmental vision</li>
<li>Assures quality control of all media planning and implementation</li>
<li>SEO Activation staffing, recruiting, employee retention, and motivation</li>
<li>Owns, defines, and enforces client service standards</li>
<li>Creates service consistency across all client accounts</li>
<li>Ensures Junior staff continue to grow and progress to fill more senior roles in conjunction with account growth</li>
<li>Owns departmental financial forecasting/revenue retention and margin evaluation</li>
<li>New business support, cross-agency support, and cross-sell support</li>
<li>Client escalation point of contact</li>
<li>Training and mentoring of CD staff; Skills management for SEO activation staff; spot training and mentoring of ACS/CS staff</li>
<li>Coordinates efforts across different digital media channels to drive optimized recommendations.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Essential Skills</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Demonstrated ability to manage group resources and effectively delegates appropriate responsibilities</li>
<li>Consistently able to maintain forward progress with all client engagements, regardless of client situation</li>
<li>Pushes client teams to alter approach or pilot new ways of managing SEO, strategy through tactics</li>
<li>Excellent ability to renew client engagements and grow client investments in like or new initiatives and services</li>
<li>Forecasts overall departmental resource needs based on client pool and budgets</li>
<li>Excellent ability to handle client/personnel issues</li>
<li>Experience creating and launching new service offerings</li>
<li>Demonstrated ability to successfully participate in new business situations and new client on-boarding</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Team Leadership &amp; Development</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Embraces and encourages a culture based on team work, collaboration, and intellectual curiosity</li>
<li>Motivates, leads, and manages internal teams, working collaboratively to drive programs that meet our clients’ performance and business objectives</li>
<li>Drives adoption of stated account management best practices through example setting, positive reinforcement and behavioral correction</li>
<li>Assists in interviewing, hiring, training, mentoring, developing, and evaluating team members</li>
<li>Conducts performance reviews and career path plans for direct reports</li>
<li>Provide training in cross media planning to their directs reports and bellow teams</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Process Improvement</b></p>
<ul><li>Facilitates the development and documentation of best practices for improving operational efficiencies within the team and the group</li></ul>
<p><b>
Cross-Selling</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Understands and explains the features and benefits of all Performics products and services</li>
<li>Support Client Solutions with cross sells of products and services to grow Performics’ share of marketing spend</li>
<li>Exceeds semi-annual and annual revenue targets</li>
<li>Supports business development sales in selling solutions as appropriate</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Minimum Qualifications:</b></p>
<p></p>
<br>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree, concentration in business, marketing, or advertising preferred</li>
<li>7-10 years of account management experience in online advertising, direct marketing, or SEO; client and agency experience preferred</li>
<li>4 years of demonstrated success in managing and leading people</li>
<li>2-4 years of digital marketing preferred with desirable focus on search engines, display and/or social media</li>
<li>Experience in managing an organization</li>
<li>Demonstrated understanding of online marketing principles</li>
<li>High competency level in MS Office with specific strengths in Excel and PowerPoint</li>
<li>Able to work independently and balance multiple tasks</li>
</ul>
