<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Description</b></h2>
<div>
DESCRIPTION<br>
Backed by one of the world’s largest collections of digital, film and TV properties; the new advertising arm of AT&amp;T, Xandr, is providing the premium advertising solutions for advertisers and publishers to reach specific audiences at scale in premium digital, linear and OTT environments. This role will support the ad sales teams across Xandr for sales compensation.<br>
<br>
RESPONSIBILITIES<br>
<ul>
<li>Responsible for sales compensation modeling and communicating payouts to the sales team.</li>
<li>Responsible for analysis of sales compensation payout curves – evaluate and make recommendations relating to business opportunities.</li>
<li>Assist with evaluating and developing sales compensation plans to drive revenue in strategic areas.</li>
<li>Gathers, interprets and evaluates financial information.</li>
<li>Works on problems of diverse scope where analysis of data requires evaluation of identifiable factors. Demonstrates good judgment in selecting methods and techniques for obtaining solutions.</li>
</ul>
