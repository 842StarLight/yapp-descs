<h2>Fabrication Coordinator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>JOB SUMMARY: </b> The Fabrication Coordinator has primary responsibility to prep, program, and release work orders to the Fab departments for Laser, Plasma and Saw cutting.</p>
<p><b>RESPONSIBILITIES AND ESSENTIAL JOB FUNCTIONS: </b></p>
<p>Utilize CAD / nesting software and manufacturing experience to:</p>
<ul><li>Program Nests on Lasers and Plasma Tables throughout the SpecSys organization.</li></ul>
<ul>
<li>Ability to calculate square ft. and lineal ft. usage of material and create material requisition for the purchasing department.</li>
<li>Optimization of sheet steel, with minimal drop where possible and applicable.</li>
<li>Understanding of partial sheet inventory and other raw material inventory, by location, to further utilize that material that initially could not be fully optimized based on job schedules put forth.</li>
<li>Release Nests per build schedules, by product or project, to the Fabrication Departments at the respective SpecSys plants.</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Review drawing requirements and identify special requirements or possible manufacturing concerns.</li>
<li>Determine if the item aligns with SpecSys manufacturing capabilities and product scopes.</li>
<li>Identify missing information. Request missing information from Project Management, Sales Team, SpecSys6 Engineering, etc. in a clear and professional manner.</li>
<li>Verify customer provided drawings are accurate and drawn to scale; also verify file format is compatible with nesting /viewing software used by SpecSys.</li>
<li>Verify required tolerances are clear, and noted on documentation to assure capability and quality of end product.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>JOB REQUIREMENTS AND STANDARDS: </b></p>
<p>· Pro-Nest, Global Shop, and Microsoft Excel Software knowledge and use is essential, or equivalent.</p>
<p>· Basic knowledge of computers and controls is a must.</p>
<p>· Detailed understanding of part drawings, dimensions and tolerances, and material specifications.</p>
<p>· Understanding of each facilities Plasma, Laser, and Saw capabilities.</p>
<p>· Read and understand written information.<br>· Identify problems, review information and use math &amp; software skills to solve problems.</p>
<p>Job Type: Full-time</p>
<p>Experience:</p>
<ul><li>Fabrication: 1 year (Preferred)</li></ul>
<p>Education:</p>
<ul><li>High school or equivalent (Preferred)</li></ul>
<p>Benefits:</p>

<li>Health insurance</li>
<li>Dental insurance</li>
<li>Vision insurance</li>
<li>Paid time off</li>
</ul>
<p>Schedule:</p>
<ul><li>Monday to Friday</li></ul>
