<p><b>EES Non-Stock Buyer</b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Position Profile:</b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>At Anixter, we give you an opportunity for a career in a great work environment. We are a friendly, employee-focused organization that prides itself on customer service, flawless execution and a hard day’s work. If you are looking for a great work environment and an opportunity to grow your career, please read on!</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>Anixter is seeking a <b>Non-Stock Buyer</b> to join our Electrical&amp; Electronic Solutions Inventory Management Group in our <b><i>Glenview, IL </i></b>office. We are searching for an individual with a positive attitude and exceptional time management skills to join our team. An ideal candidate will be able to provide great internal support for our sales team as well as build on external relationships with our suppliers all while placing numerous purchase orders on a daily basis.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p>You will have the opportunity to be responsible for contributing to the supply chain process by:</p>
<ul>
<li><p>Coordinating the procurement of non-standard material</p></li>
<li><p>
Managing the movement of goods between distribution centers</p></li>
<li><p>
Supporting the needs of the sales representatives within a designated region.<br>
</p></li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Additional </b><b>responsibilities and duties include, but are not limited to:</b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li><p>Partner with our sales team to purchase material that Anixter does not currently have on hand within our distribution network and ship direct to the end user or to one of our warehouses across the U.S.
</p></li>
<li><p>Coordinate inventory deliveries with sales representatives and warehouses to ensure we meet or exceed customer expectations by solving any unexpected issues</p></li>
<li><p>
Remain responsive and committed to high levels of customer service when communicating concerns or status changes both internally and externally in regards to purchase orders</p></li>
<li><p>
Work with sales, accounts payable, operations, product management, finance and project management to solve issues and improve our quality policy</p></li>
<li><p>
Be accountable for any unresolved discrepancies in terms of price changes, quantity updates, and freight issues that may delay the placement of purchase orders</p></li>
</ul>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Requirements:<br>
</b></p>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li><p>Ability to quickly learn key systems, efficiently multi-task, resolve problems in a timely manner, and adapt to new technologies.
</p></li>
<li><p>Possess excellent communication, organizational and interpersonal skills with a service-oriented nature.</p></li>
<li><p>
Ability to multi-task and thrive in a fast-paced environment.</p></li>
<li><p>
Bachelors degree is preferred.</p></li>
<li><p>
Previous experience in data/order entry &amp; customer service is preferred.</p></li>
</ul>
