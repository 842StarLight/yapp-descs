<h2>Franchise Services Coordinator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>SUMMARY: Using financial tools, animal welfare requirements, operating systems and POS metrics provided, the Franchise Services Consultant provides guidance and oversight to the franchisee and/or GM on executing the Petland Business Model proficiently in execution of the franchise agreement.<br>
</p>
<div>DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES:<br>
<ul>
<li>Ensures all aspects of animal welfare and care within the daily operations of a Petland store, escalating issues as appropriate.</li>
<li>Evaluates and provides guidance and training for Petland’s primary sales tools (the Petland Mission Statement, 5-Step Sale process, 7-Step Puppy Sale process and the Four Basic Needs process)</li>
<li>Evaluates the effectiveness of the franchisee merchandising and dry good vendor relationships (i.e. Mix, placement, reporting, evaluations, etc.). Provides guidance as necessary.</li>
<li>Acts as a liaison between the franchisee/GM and Petland’s support functions, identifying where needs exist and where those support functions can add value (Finance, Marketing/Advertising, Distribution, HR, etc.)</li>
<li>Provides recommendations for and introductions to Petland approved third party vendors (Petkey, ISEA, Shop Window, Animal Suppliers).</li>
<li>Educates the Franchisee/GM on best practices in the licensed and inspected breeder world.</li>
<li>Provides all initial staff training for newly established stores and ongoing supplemental training as needed.</li>
<li>Reviews and evaluates the performance of Franchisee 5 Key Management positions (GM, Sales Managers, Inventory Control Managers, Kennel Managers and Pet/Fish Managers) as outlined in the established job descriptions and provides guidance and training where necessary.</li>
<li>Performs other related duties as assigned by management.</li>
</ul>
<br>
SUPERVISORY RESPONSIBILITIES:<br>
<ul><li>This job has no supervisory responsibilities.</li></ul>
<br>
QUALIFICATIONS:<br>
<ul>
<li>High school diploma or general education degree (GED) required.</li>
<li>Bachelor's Degree (BA) from four-year college or university preferred.</li>
<li>Must have at least 3-4 years experience as a successful Sales Manager, Assistant General Manager or General Manager in a Petland Company Store or Franchise location.</li>
<li>Computer skills required: (POS; Microsoft Office)</li>
<li>Other skills required: Ability to read and interpret a P&amp;L statement and Balance Sheet.</li>
</ul>
</div>
