<div>
<b>Who We're Looking For:</b><br>
Geoforce is seeking an experienced Sales Development Representative to join our US sales organization where you will have the opportunity to work with the leader in the dynamic GPS-based field asset tracking space. In this role, you will generate qualified opportunities through outbound calling and other various forms of outreach that will drive pipeline and ultimately generate revenue for Geoforce. You will prospect non-Geoforce customers to drive new logos. This role provides substantial opportunity for career growth in a fast-paced high-volume sales environment. If you are looking to take your career to the next level, this position may be for you.</div>
<ul>
<li>A high intellect in order to quickly learn Geoforce’s products, industries and competitive differentiation.</li>
<li>Professional phone presence and excellent written/verbal communication skills</li>
<li>Competitive drive and strong desire for success</li>
<li>Results oriented personality with a sales focus.</li>
<li>Strong desire to move up in other sales functions at Geoforce</li>
<li>Eagerness to join a growing technology company and a high aptitude for learning how tracking technology can make field operations less chaotic (see Geoforce’s tag line)</li>
<li>Ability to multi-task, prioritize, work with others, and be a team player.</li>
<li>Relentless attention to detail and ability to follow a proven system</li>
<li>Consistent top performer</li>
</ul>
<div><b>What Will You Do:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Conduct high level conversations with prospects</li>
<li>Contact prospects daily via phone, email, and other forms of outreach to generate opportunities and set up meetings for Regional and Enterprise Sales Managers.</li>
<li>Accurately record activities (calls, emails, etc.) within Salesforce.com</li>
<li>Assist in the execution of Marketing and call campaigns</li>
<li>Work with cross functional groups such as Marketing, Sales, Customer Success, and Sales Operations</li>
</ul>
<div><b>Requirements:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>1-2 years’ experience in a professional environment (experience on phone or public speaking preferred)</li>
<li>Ability to meet and exceed achievable targets</li>
<li>Bachelor’s degree</li>
<li>Experience with Salesforce.com</li>
<li>Referenceable experience</li>
<li>Existing competency in Microsoft Office (Excel, PowerPoint) and Google Suite of applications (Drive, Docs, Sheets)</li>
</ul>
<div><b>Additional Skills Desired:</b></div>

</ul>
