<h2>Insurance Service Rep</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>AAA Texas is currently seeking an Insurance Service Representative to work in our Allen Branch.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>The Insurance Service Representative (ISR) provides high quality service to members and insureds regarding changes in insurance policies, renewal information, and billing statements. They quote insurance rates, qualify applicants, and verify/enter new business applications and related documents. Representatives also resolve membership and insurance problems using discretion and independent judgment. Representative are also responsible for handling accounting problems and correspondence. In addition, Representatives type letters, forms, cards, rush escrows, and evidence of insurance.</p>
