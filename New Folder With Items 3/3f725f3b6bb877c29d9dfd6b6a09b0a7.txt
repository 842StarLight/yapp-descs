<div><b>What do you get to do in this position?<br>
</b></div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
<b>Mission: </b>Ensures that <b>customers, branches, and partners </b>realize the full benefit and maximizes the use of the contracted remote service.
</div>
<p>Ensures <b>service excellence </b>and consistent customer experience for Building Advisor offer <b>execution </b>.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>Responsibilties:
</b></p>
<p>Maintain ongoing relationship with key customers with responsibility for retention, expansion, and extension of agreements (liaise with account manager, sales, service):
</p>
<p>Create and manage individual customer adoption scorecard
</p>
<p>Develop customer confidence in value of offer, ensure client is delighted, serve as primary lead for delivery team
</p>
<p>Extensive interaction with client counterpart (usually energy manager or team) over multitude of both contracted issues as well as items that arise (lots of “can you help us with this?” emanates)
</p>
<p>Ensure value is demonstrated/delivered throughout life of contract
</p>
<p></p>
<p>Schedule follow-ups to review success, expectations, etc.
</p>
<p>Ensures service excellence for offer and functions by providing feedback on:
</p>
<p>Processes and tools
</p>
<p>Customer experience measurements/insights (MTR, RTSM, etc)
</p>
<div>Supports the sales teams (e.g. understands customer’s needs, provides value of the offer)
</div>
</div>
