<h2>Consultant Sr</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Requisition Number: </b><b>74358</b></p>
<p>
As a Sr. Consultant you will enter a high-energy and tech savvy world. We are looking for someone who has exceptional interpersonal skills, is client focused, and has a proven track record of delivering best in class networking solutions. Successful consultants are able to adapt quickly to changing technical and professional environments.
</p>
<p>Insight is looking for talented individuals who are passionate about emerging technologies and excited about the opportunity to work for industry-leading organizations!</p>
<div></div>
<br>
<div><b>
What you’ll do at Insight:</b></div>
<div></div>
<br>
<ul>
<li>
Actively participate in the assessment, planning, designing, and implementing of Network and Security solutions including high availability, routing/switching, VPN, security policies and address translation.
</li>
<li>Design, deploy, support and troubleshoot large-scale network implementations based on networking designs.</li>
<li>
Maintain, upgrade, and provide solutions to complex hardware/software problems.
</li>
<li>Develop and maintain relationships with clients and internal teams.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
What you’ll need to join Insight:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
3+ years implementing and troubleshooting Cisco Networking and Data Center technologies.</li>
<li>
Bachelor’s degree in a technical or business discipline or demonstrated performance in a technical work environment.</li>
<li>
Experience working in a consulting environment is preferred</li>
<li>
Growth in technical skills through certifications (CCNA/CCNP/CCDP/JNCIP) or other similar achievements is desired.</li>
<li>
Ability to effectively communicate, present and articulate strategies across various audiences; sales, marketing, partners, clients, consultants, executive leaders (internal and external).<br>
</li>
</ul>
