<h2>Vice President, Operations</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<h1 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>SUMMARY</b></h1>
The Vice President Operations provides the leadership, management and vision necessary to ensure that the company has the proper operational controls, administrative and reporting procedures, and people systems in place to effectively grow the organization and to ensure financial strength and operating efficiency. He/She is accountable for the management of the overall quality and efficiency of operations, and ensuring the company delivers outstanding service that is client focused and efficient at all times.<br>
<br>
<b>ESSENTIAL DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES</b><b> </b>include the following. Other duties may be assigned.<br>
<br>
Provides strategic and tactical leadership to department heads of the Operations team, including but not limited to Account Management, Client Services, Provisioning, Order Management, Field Support, IT Infrastructure and Software Development, in a fashion that supports the Company’s culture, mission and values.<br>
<br>
Collaborates with the management team to develop and implement plans for the operational infrastructure of systems, processes, and personnel designed to accommodate the growth objectives of the Company.<br>
<br>
Leverages the advantages of bringing together operational expertise and drives through more efficient ways of working. Coordinates the efforts of the different operational areas under management to ensure minimal duplication of efforts, maximum efficiency and works to maximize value for investments in resources and suppliers. Ensures this effective and efficient operating model is continually improved.<br>
<br>
Ensures strategic objectives shaped at the Senior Leadership Team level are translated into tactical business plans with mechanisms for key measurements in place to monitor progress. Provides timely, accurate and complete reports on the operating condition of the company.<br>
<br>
Monitors productivity of all Operations teams to ensure the achievement of service level commitments (SLAs), deliverables and customer satisfaction.<br>
<br>
Reviews attrition reporting to create programs and/or make suggestions on improving product offers, etc., in order to reduce company attrition.<br>
<br>
Creates accountability within the company by developing appropriate metrics and coordinating compensation with these metrics.<br>
<br>
Demonstrates the ability to lead the organization through changes.<br>
<br>
Aligns with CEO’s vision and establishes both short-term results and long-term strategy. Implements predictable, repeatable, and operations processes using data analytics, and manages consistent pipeline and forecasting methodology. Demonstrates the ability to execute those strategies.<br>
<br>
Fills management gaps by building and training individuals and teams in Account Management, Client Services, Service Fulfillment and Software Development.<br>
<br>
Drives a “lean startup” style environment of constant experimentation and learning.<br>
<br>
Leverages Voice of Customer research (quantity and quality) to improve Net Promoter Score and Customer Experience results.<br>
<br>
Owns budget responsibility for all Operations groups, including but not limited to Account Management, Client Services, Provisioning, Order Management, Field Support, IT Infrastructure and Software Development. Manages the expenses to budget and reports any deviations to the budget. Manages the capital expense process for the Operations department.<br>
<br>
Works in a consultative fashion with other department heads, such as sales, marketing, finance and product as an adviser of technologies that may improve their efficiency and effectiveness.<br>
<br>
Acts as lead "client-care officer" through direct contact with clients and partners.<br>
<br>
Ability to travel to support company objectives and execution of initiatives.<br>
