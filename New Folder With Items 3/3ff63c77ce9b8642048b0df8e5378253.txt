<b>Requirements:</b><br>
Responsibilities Include
<br>1. Performs appraisals, identifies pre-determined damages, prepares and submits appraisals indicating costs for the parts, materials and labor necessary to restore vehicles to their pre-damaged condition
<br>2. Provides claims professionals with expert advice on vehicle repair, parts costs, and garage expertise
<br>3. Determines if damages are related to a specific incident
<br>4. Conducts interviews with insureds and witnesses
<br>5. Evaluates claims and makes recommendations on total losses
<br>6. Negotiates settlements with insurance companies
<br>7. Makes recommendations regarding litigation
<br>8. Completes Direct Repair Program re-inspections, supplements as warranted to verify damage or confirm repairs are completed
<br>9. Documents vehicular, specialty equipment, and property damages to determine reparability
<br>10. Processes claim payments using automated and manual systems
<br>11. Applies knowledge of local laws, parts distribution, sublet repair vendors, and trade relationships to assist in maintaining cost control while providing a positive PDA image
<br>12. Evaluates, negotiates and settles total losses and completes necessary documentation. May be responsible for moving salvage and securing titles and other transfer of ownership documents
<br>13. Manages client relationships while developing growth and new client base. May be involved in sales, negotiating, and contracting of new clients
<br>14. Markets PDA to potential service vendors by calling on body shops, auto dealerships, Third Party Administrators, Insurance Companies, and investigators, establishing relationships and promoting the PDA brand
<br>15. Monitors market share performance and makes strategic adjustments to ensure retention is maintained and growth is experienced.
<br>16. Review of company metrics while providing analytical resolutions to performance of the area aligned to company goals
<br>17. Establishes strong relationships to maintain client satisfaction levels.
<br>18. Serves as client liaison and provides one-stop service for the location
<br>19. May assist in the training of staff and evaluation of staff, independent and Direct Repair Program shops
<br>20. Travel between 75% and 85% is required daily
<br>21. Other duties as assigned
