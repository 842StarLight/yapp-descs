<h2>Account Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Summary/Objective</p>
<p>The focus of sales personnel is to attract, engage and cultivate new business while growing and servicing existing business. The Account Manager will be key to Astrolite®/Tricor Alloys success in the marketplace.</p>
<p>Required Education and Experience</p>
<ul>
<li>High School Graduate</li>
<li>Associates or Bachelor’s degree preferred</li>
<li>2+ years inside sales/account management experience in fast paced environment</li>
</ul>
<p>Technical Skills</p>
<ul>
<li>Ability to take instruction and work independently</li>
<li>Ability to work well with others</li>
<li>Accurate data entry/order entry, order tracking</li>
<li>Ability to proof read and self-audit work</li>
<li>Track customer performance and create reports</li>
<li>Time management and ability to prioritize duties in fast paced environment</li>
<li>Experienced with MS Office Suite – Must have intermediate MS Excel skills (spreadsheet creation, formatting, basic formula creation, cell manipulation)</li>
</ul>
<p>Essential Functions</p>
<ul>
<li>Experience in inbound/outbound sales, customer service and account management</li>
<li>Excellent customer service; coordinating services, while diplomatically handling challenging situations</li>
<li>Detail Oriented, able to multi-task and prioritize workload in a fast paced environment, facilitating customer communication to ensure complete customer satisfaction</li>
<li>Ability to partner effectively with customers to identify additional areas of business; think outside the box</li>
<li>Manage a book of business, interpret customer requirements, research items, quote, acknowledge and process orders ensuring accuracy and quality standards are met</li>
<li>Work well independently as part of a team; other departments, to ensure proper scheduling and processing of orders</li>
</ul>
<p>Competencies</p>
<ul>
<li>Experience in domestic and international sales and pricing negotiations</li>
<li>Knowledge of scheduling, logistics and expediting</li>
<li>Aerospace Industry experience (raw materials, metals, with emphasis on AMS and AWS, Pratt &amp; Whitney, Rolls Royce, GE and SNECMA specifications, etc.)</li>
<li>Commercial/Aerospace welding wire sales experience</li>
<li>Experience in ISO environment, ideally ISO9100/AS9100D</li>
<li>Experience in Production/Distribution software similar to SAP, ERP, AS400, MCMS, MAPICS, GUI (Graphic User Interface Software)</li>
<li>Bilingual (English-Spanish)</li>
<li>Research and marketing domestic and international customer base</li>
</ul>
<p>Work Environment</p>
<p>This job operates in an office environment that will require the individual to go into the manufacturing floor at times requiring the use of safety glasses. This role routinely uses standard office equipment such as computers, phones, photocopiers, filing cabinets, printers, and fax machines.</p>
<p>Physical Demands</p>
<p>While performing the duties of this job, the employee is regularly required to talk or hear. The employee frequently is required to stand; walk; use hands to finger, handle or feel; and reach with hands and arms. Able to lift up to 25 LBS.</p>
<p>Other Duties</p>
<p>Please note this job description is not designed to cover or contain a comprehensive listing of activities, duties or responsibilities that are required of the employee for this job. Duties, responsibilities and activities may change at any time with or without notice.</p>
<p>Job Type: Full-time</p>
