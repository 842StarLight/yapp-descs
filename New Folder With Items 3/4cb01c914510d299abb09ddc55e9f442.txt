<h2>Manufacturing Planner/Customer Service</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>Job Title:</p>
<p>Production Planner / Customer Service</p>
<p>Overview:</p>
<p>The Production Planner / Customer Service is responsible for managing the customer order cycle from receipt of purchase order, establishing production schedules, ensuring that products are shipped to meet customer delivery requirements and effectively communicate with customers to guarantee a high level of satisfaction with their experience with SCP Limited</p>
<p>Company:</p>
<p>SCP Limited</p>
<p>Duties:</p>
<p>Receives customer purchase orders and requests via various electronic means, customer portals, emails, hard copy PO, etc and enters customer demand / forecasts into SCP Limited ERP system.</p>
<p>Plans and manages production schedules to meet customer delivery requirements and to optimize the company?s productive capacity.</p>
<p>Analyzes sales order releases with respect to capacity planning and material requirements, consults with Production Management and notifies customers when schedule conflicts exist.</p>
<p>Manages inventory levels, schedules and availability of selected item, either manufactured or purchased, to meet production schedules. Completes shipping information and delivery date input during sale order reviews.</p>
<p>Answer termination or cancellation requests from sales.</p>
<p>Reviews work in process material sweeps from production floor.</p>
<p>Receives and reviews sales orders against current requirements and work in process using the material requirement planning report.</p>
<p>Interfaces with manufacturing personnel, the purchasing department and supervisors on material status and production planning issues.</p>
<p>Build external relationship with SCP Limited customers and Sales staff.</p>
<p>Work closely with customers and sales to identify and communicate customer schedule and delivery requirements and serve as an interface between the customer and operations.</p>
<p>Prioritize customer requirements in the department and expedite critical customer needs.</p>
<p>Coordinate activities to resolve customer complaints and achieve customer satisfaction and loyalty.</p>
<p>Support Accounts Receivable in resolving customer debits to include approving credits up to the specified limit.</p>
<p>Visit customer facilities and host or participate in visits by customers.</p>
<p>Understand and operate within ISO standards and procedures.</p>
<p>Effectively carry out the initiatives and policies set by management and maintain compliance with procedures</p>
<p>Competencies:</p>
<p>Experience with MRP/ERP production planning, capacity management software</p>
<p>Problem Solving/Analysis.</p>
<p>Customer Service, Communication and Team Leading Proficiency.</p>
<p>Decision Making.</p>
<p>Collaboration Skills.</p>
<p>Proficient in Microsoft Office applications including Excel, Microsoft PowerPoint and Access</p>
<p>Good communication skills, both oral and written</p>
<p>Strong organizational skills with an ability to manage multiple tasks</p>
<p>Ability to deliver high level of customer service and proven track record of following through with assignments.</p>
<p>Requirements:</p>
<p>Associate?s or bachelor?s degree or equivalent work experience</p>
<p>5 years of experience in a production planning/materials controls function using MPR/ERP system (Microsoft Dynamics GP a plus)</p>
<p>Experience communicating directly with customers a plus</p>
<p>BS degree in Production / Operations Management or Supply Chain a plus</p>
