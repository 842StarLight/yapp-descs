<h2>Cyber Security Operations Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<b>OVERVIEW</b><p>
Sentry has a great leadership opportunity to join a growing team as the Cyber Security Operations Manager. This role will require the direct management of the following key areas, such as but not limited to; Vulnerability Management Program, advance incident response, cyber forensic investigation, endpoint security, EDR Tool, and foundational red and blue team development.
</p>
<b>WHAT YOU'LL DO</b><ul>
<li>Identify protection goals, objectives and metrics consistent with Information Security strategic plan and works with leadership to prioritize security initiatives and spending based on appropriate risk management and alignment with the overall strategy.</li>
<li>Develop, implement, and manage the monitoring and incident handling program including the technologies, processes, training and documentation needed to ensure the organization can effectively detect and respond to security incidents.</li>
<li>Develop, implement, and manage the vulnerability management program including the technologies, processes, training and documentation needed in order to ensure that vulnerabilities in systems, applications, databases, and third parties are effectively addressed and managed.</li>
<li>Develop, implement, and manage the digital forensics and e-discovery program to support security, human resources, and legal teams during investigations and sensitive matters by preparing and delivering digital evidence.</li>
<li>Operate as the primary incident handler coordinating event response in the event of a security incident.</li>
<li>Manage the red team in conducting convert penetration testing activities and mimicking attackers across the organization looking for signs of control weaknesses and attack vectors to provide valuable threat information for the Information Security program.</li>
<li>Mature and manage threat intelligence and threat hunting programs in order to provide invaluable threat intelligence information to the monitoring platform, allowing the security team to focus on events that are likely to lead to a compromise.</li>
<li>Manage administration of human resources-related activities including timeline performance and salary reviews and ensure all subordinates adhere to human resources policies and practices.</li>
</ul>
<b>
WHAT IT TAKES</b><ul>
<li>Bachelors Degree or equivalent work experience.</li>
<li>5-8+ years of related work experience.</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Information Security Management experience is preferred
</li>
<li>Understands and can implement information security risk management countermeasures</li>
<li>Project management skills</li>
<li>Systems integration experience</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Active CISSP or related security certification required</li>
<li>Deep technical expertise specific to security technologies and solutions.</li>
<li>Possess excellent management skills, communication skills, human relation skills, organizational skills and analytical skills as well as proven information security leadership experience in a medium-to-large organization.</li>
<li>Demonstrates strong intellect and is able to appropriately articulate and persuasively lead teams. Serves as an effective member of the management team and who is able to communicate security-related concepts to a broad range of technical and non-technical staff.</li>
<li>Demonstrated experience planning, developing and implementing large projects.</li>
</ul>
