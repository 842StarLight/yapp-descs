Mars Petcare is looking for A&amp;CP Finance Analyst, Franklin, TN</p>
<div></div>
<br>
<div><b>
Purpose of Role</b></div>
<div>
This role is responsible for ensuring the proper accounting, forecasting, reporting, governance and control processes for all Advertising &amp; Consumer Promotions (A&amp;CP) activities. This includes period brand allocations of expenses and sound analyses of deferred, accrued and expense balances as well as updating internal control processes. The role will serve as primary co-pilot to the Shopper Marketing team and Marketing Operations / Brand teams with regard to A&amp;CP budget, activity and ROI.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div><b>
Principal Accountabilities</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
Performs period close journal entries and brand allocation for expense recognition, accruals/deferrals and account analysis/reconciliation at the end of each period for Advertising, Consumer Promotions and/including Shopper Marketing
</li>
<li>Provide analysis and insights of period results, periodic/annual A&amp;CP forecast and support annual operating plans while working closely with Marketing, Shopper Marketing, and FP&amp;A.</li>
<li>
Lead periodic reviews with Brand Managers and Shopper Marketers highlighting insights and providing relevant budget tracking reporting with potential risk or opportunities to budgets while ensuring completeness and accuracy of assumptions utilize for accruals</li>
<li>
Aid in development of and execute robust reporting to track budget performance of A&amp;CP for business partners -Brand Managers/Operations &amp; Shopper Marketers</li>
<li>
Deliver world class customer service by acting as the first point of contact for internal stakeholders with questions regarding shopper promotional programs, Media/Production or coupon information and funding guidelines
</li>
<li>Drive efficiencies by finding solutions to improve reporting and data systems used to support our A&amp;CP reporting and accounting.
</li>
<li>Drive continuous improvement across A&amp;CP (including Shopper Marketing) opportunities with an ROI mindset to help deliver financial objectives.</li>
<li>
Maintains adherence to Mars financial policies and procedures through the integrity of SAP data, Compass (trade management system), A&amp;CP chart of accounts and internal orders.</li>
<li>
Prepares and supports audit requirements as required.</li>
<li>
Complete ad hoc projects and requests as required.</li>
</ul>
