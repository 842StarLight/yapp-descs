<h2>Marketing Coordinator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Position Title: Marketing Coordinator</p>
<p>Marshfield Area YMCA, Inc.</p>
<p>Supervisor: Marketing &amp; Development Director</p>
<p>Status: Part-time Hourly</p>
<p>Includes YMCA membership to the Marshfield Clinic Health System<br>YMCA</p>
<p>Department: Marketing &amp; Development</p>
<p>Reporting to the Director of Marketing &amp; Development, the Marketing Coordinator creates and manages production of print and digital materials and is responsible for email marketing, website management and the social media calendar. This position assists with photography, video production, media relations, events, writing, editing and proofreading.</p>
<p>The Coordinator provides quality control for the department, ensuring all projects are brand compliant and meet the needs of the organization. The position works directly with program directors to guarantee marketing materials and plans are executed on time and on budget.</p>
<p>KEY RESPONSIBILITIES:<br>- Enthusiastically represents The Marshfield Area YMCA, Inc. by displaying a positive attitude, maintaining a high level of energy and commitment to quality throughout all aspects of the job through the four character values of: caring, honesty, respect and responsibility.<br>- Responsible for creating/designing marketing assets such as program brochures, flyers, posters, invitations, print and digital ads, and more that drive membership, retention and support.<br>- Oversees, creates and manages email campaigns and communications including member and donor newsletters, and measures results.<br>- Develops social media content, including video, photography and stories. Manages social media calendar and reports on effectiveness of social media efforts. Provides training and support to program directors.<br>- Monitors and updates the website and tracks results in Google Analytics.<br>- Serves as Brand Champion, and ensures brand compliance for all marketing assets.</p>
<p>- Provides quality control to the Marketing &amp; Development department for all projects completed.<br>- Supports special events and philanthropic activities to ensure they advance key communications, marketing and fundraising goals.<br>- Other duties as assigned.</p>
<p>KEY YMCA COMPETENCIES<br>- Communication - Listens for understanding and meaning. Speaks and writes effectively.<br>- Innovation - Embraces new approaches and discovers ideas to create a better member experience, drive revenue and build awareness.<br>- Project Management - Establishes goals, clarifies tasks, plans work and actively participates in meetings.<br>- Quality Results - Strives to meet or exceed goals and deliver a high-value experience for members.<br>- Self-Development - Pursues self-development that enhances job performance.<br>- Interest in storytelling via testimonials and video.</p>
<p>QUALIFICATIONS<br>- Academic background in Marketing, Communications, Graphic Design, or other related academic focus<br>- Minimum two years of experience in marketing including graphic design<br>- Proficient in Adobe Creative Suite (InDesign, Illustrator and Photoshop)<br>- Computer proficiency in Microsoft Office applications<br>- Knowledge of iContact or other email software<br>- Wordpress or other Web content management system knowledge<br>- Knowledge of best practices in communications, marketing and social media<br>- Knowledge of the Y Brand or other experience managing a brand preferred<br>- Excellent graphic design, writing, and communications skills<br>- High skill level in organizing, planning and implementation of administrative procedures and adept at maintaining systems for easy access to information and data<br>- Ability to work well in a collaborative environment and individually on projects<br>- Attention to information and accuracy in performance<br>- A portfolio that demonstrates evidence of design abilities, in print and digital</p>
