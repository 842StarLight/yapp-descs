<div>COMPANY OVERVIEW:
</div>
<div><i>Boardriders, Inc., is the world’s leading action sports and lifestyle company that designs, produces and distributes branded apparel, footwear and accessories for Boardriders around the world. The Company’s apparel and footwear brands, represent a casual lifestyle for young‐minded people who are inspired by a passion for outdoor action sports. The Company’s Quiksilver, Billabong, Roxy, DC Shoes, RVCA, Element, VonZipper, Xcel, Kustom and Palmers brands have authentic roots and heritage in surf, snow and skate. With approximately 10,000 team members globally, the Company’s products are sold in more than 110 countries in a wide range of distribution, including surf shops, skate shops, snow shops, its proprietary Boardriders stores and other Company‐owned retail stores, select department stores and through various e‐ commerce channels.</i></div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div>SUMMARY:<b>
</b>
</div>
<div>Based in Irvine and reporting to the brand President, the Digital Marketing Coordinator will be responsible for executing VonZipper and Dot Dash’s digital and social media strategy per the marketing plan. The role will work closely with the Sales and Direct to Consumer teams to insure alignment with their respective sales strategies. A meaningful two-way conversation with the company’s online audience to drive demand, activate promotions, lead consumers to various points of sale, both on and offline, and expanding VZ’s social following are key performance indicators for this position.</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div>RESPONSIBILITIES:</div>
<ul>
<li>Collaborate with the Sales, Marketing, Art and ecommerce teams to construct manage and execute VZ and Dot Dash’s social go-to-market calendars.</li>
<li>Construct consumer community profiles and engage in two-way conversations with our social audiences through social platforms, blogs and emails.</li>
<li>Create and drive social engagement, with measurable consumer journeys with customer service and DTC alignment.</li>
<li>Maintain and create relationships with brand ambassadors, generating and sharing relevant social content</li>
<li>Conduct qualitative and quantitive market research through social media listening.</li>
<li>Develop social media release calendar schedule for the brands</li>
<li>Integrate social messaging around current campaigns and monthly &amp; seasonal events</li>
<li>Maintain shared file database of imagery and brand materials</li>
<li>Integrate relevant geo-targeted messages that hits the right markets at the right times</li>
<li>Work with international partners to share content</li>
<li>Monitor global social pages, to ensure the brand values and messaging are consistent with brand values &amp; direction</li>
<li>Create unique messaging opportunities including behind the scenes storylines at events, photoshoots and any other relevant opportunities</li>
<li>Provide monthly reports to the VZ team as it relates to VZ’s &amp; Dot Dash’s social &amp; digital domains</li>
<li>Identify, target and comminicate relevant brand influencers</li>
<li>Actively prospect opportunities through targeted consumer-based communication strategies and new platforms, including HookIt.</li>
<li>Contribute to the production of content, imagery and motion media</li>
<li>Engage with consumers to and assist customer service with direct messaging via social channels</li>
<br>
</ul>
