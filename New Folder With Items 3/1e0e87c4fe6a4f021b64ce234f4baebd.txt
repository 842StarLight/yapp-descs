<h2>Salesforce Business Systems Analyst</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p><b>JOB OVERVIEW:</b><br>
<br>
The Covetrus <b>Salesforce.com Business Systems Analyst</b> is responsible for continuous improvement in the effective use and adoption of the company’s SFDC and related systems. They work with stakeholders to perform full-lifecycle implementation of technology enabled business processes that make serving our customers more effective and productive. This role performs needs assessment, cost/benefit analysis, project planning, change management, system configuration, testing, training, implementation, and follow up. As functional experts in our CRM and ERP software, they seek to first to leverage our systems existing functionality and commercially available solutions before considering custom programming.<br>
</p>
<p><b>KEY RESPONSIBILITIES:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Partner with stakeholders in sustaining a continuous improvement environment that keeps SFDC and related systems agile and aligned with current and emerging business objectives</li>
<li>Receive, solicit and initiate business systems needs from users and form solutions that have a measurable impact</li>
<li>Engage directly with stakeholders to prepare requests for approval by performing initial needs assessments, resource and impact analysis as appropriate for size and scope of request</li>
<li>Assist stakeholders in determining and adopting best practices that are natively supported by company standard or commercially available business systems</li>
<li>Develop dashboards and reports for enterprise-wise analytics, sales process effectiveness, revenue forecast and trends that provide decision support with actionable data.</li>
<li>Design and manage the development of robust dashboards and reports</li>
<li>Create detailed function design documents and project plans to drive successful implementation and adoption of new or changed business systems</li>
<li>Work in project manager and change manager as needed for small to medium sized projects.</li>
<li>Translate requirements to perform business system configuration and setup in our major business systems to meet project objectives</li>
<li>Prepare documentation, work instructions, and train the end-user community relating to operating procedures for system implementations and changes. Log related configurations with appropriate archiving methodologies for maintenance in future.</li>
<li>Provide on-call support to the end-user community for systems in production</li>
<li>Use appropriate tools as provided by Company to track tasks and production support. Enable efficient management of information system projects and resources.</li>
<li>Provide business systems capability and process consulting for upper management, and consultants regarding programming standards.</li>
<li>Maintain and enhance existing applications and respond to user’s questions and problems related to these systems.</li>
<li>Collaborate with business systems and IT Infrastructure &amp; Operations teams to keep systems up to date and secure.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>KNOWLEDGE:</b><br>
Seasoned, experienced senior level professional; in-depth knowledge and full understanding of multiple disciplines and good knowledge of other related disciplines. Assignments are broad in nature and require excellent judgment and analytical ability, evaluation, originality and ingenuity to solve. Complete understanding and wide application of principles, theories and concepts of a particular field. Resolve a wide range of complex issues and assignments in creative and effective ways. Serve as a resource to others to resolve complex problems and issues. May play a support role in high-level projects that have an impact on company’s future direction. Will often take on project lead role on medium-sized or smaller projects.</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>COMPLEXITY:</b><br>
Work on complex issues where analysis of situations or data requires an in-depth evaluation of variable factors. Interpret internal and/or external business issues and recommend solutions/best practices to solve a range of complex problems and assignments; take a broad perspective to identify solutions. Exercise judgment in selecting methods and techniques for obtaining solutions. Guide others in resolving complex issues based on existing solutions and procedures. Network with key senior internal and external personnel inside and outside own area of expertise.</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>COLLABORATION:</b><br>
Work with stakeholders on methods and procedures for changes and new needs, capable of working independently with guidance in only needed in complex situations. Will act as a resource to formally train and mentor TSMs. Assignments are broad and complex in nature. May manage the work of outside consultants.</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>WORK EXPERIENCE:
</b></p>
<p>Typically 5 or more years of increasing responsibility and complexity in terms of any applicable professional experience with 3 or more years with Salesforce.com system design and configuration, preferably in both Salesforce.com sales and Saleseforce.com Service Cloud.</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>PREFERRED EDUCATION:</b><br>
Typically a Bachelor's Degree or global equivalent in related discipline. Master's degree or global equivalent preferred. May hold two or more industry certifications.</p>
