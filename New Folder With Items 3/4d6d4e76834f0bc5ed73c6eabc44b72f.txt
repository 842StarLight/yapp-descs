<p><b>Marketing Specialist</b></p>
<p>lnXpress Global Ltd,a top-ranked international franchise company with an affiliate in South Jordan, is seeking an experienced Marketing Specialist. In this role, the Marketing Specialist is a detail-oriented individual with talents to support and follow process-oriented marketing programs that benefit the network of independently-owned franchise locations.</p>

<ul>
<li>Coordinate national marketing campaigns utilizing automated marketing techniques</li>
<li>Prepare and coordinate intra-company communications</li>
<li>Support website construction, updates and other functions</li>
<li>Build and maintain the weekly internal network newsletter that informs Franchisee Partners and their staff of important announcements, programs and other information about the business</li>
<li>Build and manage new franchise marketing start up packages</li>
<li>Collaborate with the Director of Marketing to write and edit copy for marketing collateral and marketing campaigns</li>
<li>Support new and existing Franchisees with marketing other related topics</li>
<li>Answer all email communications and redirect customer leads received in general company email to a specific location</li>
<li>Coordinate communication efforts with the Director of Marketing and other department heads</li>
<li>Participate in the creation, coordination and delivery of franchise-wide self-promotion efforts that can be used throughout the network</li>
<li>Department coordination including purchase orders, vendor relations, and other departmental administrative tasks</li>
<li>Provide Franchisee Partners marketing and communication support</li>
<li>Provide support to the Director of Marketing</li>
