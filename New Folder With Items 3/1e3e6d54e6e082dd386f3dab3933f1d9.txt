<h2>Routing Specialist / CSR</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>RAS Logistics is a leader in the logistics industry and provider of last mile delivery. We are centrally located in northwest Elkhart County, less than 15 minutes from Granger/Edwardsburg and less than 30 minutes from South Bend/Mishawaka. Due to continued growth, we are looking for a <b>Routing Specialist/Customer Service Representative (CSR)</b> to join our team.<br>
<br>
This position will be responsible for planning and coordinating the routing schedules for all RAS locations. The other related duties, will be, as a CSR when not performing routing duties.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>The key responsibilities for <b>routing</b> will be as follows:</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<ul><li> Plan the delivery of the route to be taken, by each driver in the delivery of products to customers.</li></ul>
<ul><li> This position will play an integral role in the implementation and roll-out of our new dispatch software.</li></ul>
<ul><li> You will be responsible, to determine the needed stops and the most precise use of available trucks to most efficiently meet delivery demands.</li></ul>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>The key responsibilities for <b>CSR</b> will be as follows:<br>
<br>
</p>
<ul><li> When not performing the duties as a Routing Specialist, you will be the primary contact for all inbound calls from customers, co-workers and vendors. This is estimated to be 90% of the time spent on the phone assisting customers with questions and problem solving (but again only when you are not performing the routing duties).</li></ul>
<ul><li> The ability to work in a fast-paced and ever changing work environment.</li></ul>
<ul><li> This candidate will also need to possess sound knowledge of telephone etiquette with great verbal and communication skills.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Ability to remain professional and courteous with customers at all times.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Above average proficiency with computers, knowledge and ability to use Microsoft Office tools.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Ability to multitask, think-talk-type during customer calls, type 25 WPM.</li></ul>
<p>This is a day shift position and you must have the ability to work a flexible schedule during our peak sale seasons. And the flexibility to work increased hours as needed.<br>
<br>
Previous customer service experience in an office setting preferred. And a High School Diploma or equivalent, is required.</p>
<p>If you are self-motivated and willing to take on additional tasks as assigned, this job would be a great fit for you! And we offer an excellent benefits package to include medical, prescription, dental and vision along with a 401k plan which includes a company match.</p>
