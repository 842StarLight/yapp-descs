<h2>Quality Engineer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p>The Quality Engineer position will have primary responsibility for Customer focus and assuring Fujimi’s overall Quality System aligns with Customer expectations and requirements. Is responsible for tracking quality issues, developing solutions, and adoption and implementation of appropriate quality systems and procedures. This position will drive quality improvement by monitoring data collected from the process operations and apply problem solving techniques, quality verification and continuous improvement programs (CIP) for Fujimi Corporation. Must have strong verbal and written communication skills with the ability to successfully interface with technical and non-technical personnel to lead teams to resolve quality issues or improvement.</p>
<p>
<b>ESSENTIAL FUNCTIONS</b></p>
<p>
<b>Reasonable Accommodations Statement</b></p>
<p>
To accomplish this job successfully, an individual must be able to perform, with or without reasonable accommodation, each essential function satisfactorily. Reasonable accommodations may be made to help enable qualified individuals with disabilities to perform the essential functions.</p>
<p>
<b>Essential Functions Statement(s)</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Assist Quality Assurance Manager in establishing, implementing and maintaining the quality system, including corrective actions and change management follow up/resolution</li>
<li>Create and maintain company quality documentation, such as quality manuals, quality procedures, etc.</li>
<li>Oversight for Fujimi’s Process Control System (PCS) including</li>
<li>Facilitating weekly reviews</li>
<li>Tracking actions required (ARs) and process indicators</li>
<li>Driving root cause determination for abnormal shifts, trends and outliers</li>
<li>Develop reports as required</li>
<li>Provide technical Customer support with the Sales Account Mgr regarding</li>
<li>Internal alignment with Customer quality expectations</li>
<li>Development and tracking of quality initiatives (CIP) projects to align with Customer expectations</li>
<li>Complaint investigation, resolution and reporting</li>
<li>Facilitating product transfers from R&amp;D along with other Fujimi sites to ensure transfer of new products to manufacturing are in accordance with documented requirements and aligned with Customer expectations</li>
<li>Provide statistical oversight as required and develop training to build quality awareness as required</li>
<li>May be responsible for reviewing analytical results of other QC staff. May assemble and prepare completed data packages for submission to Management and/or Customers as required.</li>
<li>Responsible for CIP activities to enhance the quality system, such as 5S, Kaizen lean methods, etc.</li>
</ul>
