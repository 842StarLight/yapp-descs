<h2>General Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Summary:</b></p>
<p>
You are the captain of the ship, steering us in the direction of success! Using our core values as your compass, the General Manager is responsible for managing people, operations, and profit for their location using the policies and management guidelines set by Garden Catering. You are constantly giving structured feedback to your team to improve performance and reach personal and company defined goals, and youre listening to guest feedback for ways in which we can enhance the guest experience.</p>
<p><b>
Whats in it for you:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Competitive salary based on experience</li>
<li>
Quarterly bonus package</li>
<li>
Retention bonus after 2 years</li>
<li>
Annual performance and salary reviews</li>
<li>
Earn up to 3 weeks paid time off</li>
<li>
Generous healthcare package</li>
<li>
Flexible scheduling</li>
<li>
Paid Holidays off</li>
<li>
Free shift meals</li>
<li>
Discounts for personal catering orders + events</li>
<li>
Continued leadership development through company sponsored education and training</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Focus on People First! Maintain a culture of internal development through cross training and promoting from within. Recruit, interview, and hire only the best team members who align with our core values. Execute the companys people process to ensure proper onboarding, training, and advancement and give feedback using our coaching model with routine performance reviews.</li>
<li>
Manage Profit &amp; Loss results for your location by following cash controls, ordering and maintaining inventory while keeping cost of goods under a 1.5% variance and schedule labor within your stores given budget. Review financial results weekly with operations and business managers and create action plan to improve controllable prime costs.</li>
<li>
Commit to happier guests! Ensure fast, accurate, and positive guest experiences with exceptional customer service, food production ticket times under 7 minutes, and managing guest feedback through our Tattle software on a daily basis. Address concerns and issues within 24 hours of the complaint.</li>
<li>
Ensure safe food handling, cleanliness, and security policies are met according to Company and local health standards and maintain health inspection score of 90% or above.</li>
<li>
Must understand and be able to perform all team member position responsibilities in order to role model the standards of each position and hold team member accountable to their duties. Knowing their jobs allows you to train service standards, assist when needed during peak periods, and to save labor during slow periods of the day. This includes being an expert on our catering services so you can assist customers placing large orders for special events.</li>
<li>
Understand and adhere to labor laws, basic human resources and payroll functions and guidelines.</li>
<li>
Act as a representative for Garden Catering in your local community. Search for potential partnerships, events, and non-profits where we can be of-service while raising brand awareness and good spirit.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Qualifications for Ideal Candidate:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
High School or GED preferred and/or culinary education</li>
<li>
At least 3 years management experience in food service</li>
<li>
Knowledge of financial statements; including P&amp;L, labor reports, cost of goods, sales &amp; product mix, etc</li>
<li>
Serv Safe certified preferred</li>
<li>
Great verbal &amp; written communication skills and strong interpersonal and conflict resolution skills</li>
<li>
Advanced computer skills or ability to learn new skills quickly; excel, word, google docs.</li>
<li>
Business math and accounting skills, and strong analytical &amp; decision making skills</li>
<li>
Flexible, open minded, and able to pivot with changing goals and initiatives</li>
<li>
Able to work in a fast paced environment and maintain a high degree of pace and intensity for an extended period of time</li>
<li>
Able to be exposed to chemicals and food allergens (including but not limited to; meats, fish, nuts, dairy, and oils) without issue</li>
</ul>
