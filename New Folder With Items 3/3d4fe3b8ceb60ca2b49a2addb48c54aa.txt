<h2>Product Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p>At E2Open, the Product Manager is responsible for the product planning and execution throughout the product life cycle, including: gathering and prioritizing product and customer requirements, and working closely with engineering, sales, marketing and support to ensure revenue and customer satisfaction goals are met. The Product Manager's job also includes ensuring that the product supports the company's overall strategy and goals.</p>
<p>
Product Manager is responsible for creating functional specification and solution design documents for external customers, E2open deployment teams, as well as external systems integrators. In addition, he/she will need to work with Product Marketing on outbound messaging, pricing, competitive analysis, and support Sales teams in the early stages of joint-solution proposals and demonstrations. The role requires an in-depth understanding of the E2open product suite to ensure effective representation of E2open’s products internally as well as external (customer, analysts, etc).</p>
<p>
This is a technical product management position and good understanding of the supply chain Management space is a must. The applicant should have worked on building and/or deploying ERP or SCM software applications for supply and/or demand-side processes. Understanding of various integration options and standards such as EDI, RosettaNet, and Web Services is desirable. Prior experience with product management and development process such as Agile preferred.</p>
<p><b>
Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Work with external and internal customers to define a jointly agreeable strategy and roadmap</li>
<li>
Own Business Process areas, requirements and successful deployment of these integrated modules</li>
<li>
Responsible for analyzing and documenting business requirements, use cases and deliver detail technical specifications for supporting various multi-enterprise Business Processes</li>
<li>
Own one or more products and work with the E2open development team to develop roadmap, plan and execute</li>
<li>
Perform product demos to alliance partners, customers, and prospects and conduct training</li>
<li>
Build collaterals for sales and marketing as well as work with analysts to evangelize the combined products</li>
<li>
Establish and maintain relationships with key customers, Field Consultant teams, and Marketing</li>
<li>
Act as a leader within the company</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Experience &amp; skills:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
4-6 years of relevant work experience. 1-2 years experience as a Product Manager is preferred or Strong Program Managers or Solution Architects with good understanding of the enterprise software products can be considered.</li>
<li>
Demonstrated success in management across multiple software products and companies</li>
<li>
Experience preferred in one or more of the following areas: Supply Chain Planning, ERPs, Collaborative Applications, Supply Chain Analytics, SaaS business models, eBusiness Integration</li>
<li>
Experience with developing systems architectures for involving various applications for addressing supply chain use cases across multiple tiers of the supply chain</li>
<li>
Knowledge of enterprise applications such as SAP, Oracle ERP, Transportation Management Systems, Supply and Demand Planning systems especially from an integration point of view a plus.</li>
<li>
Experience with Standards bodies such as RosettaNet, OAGIS, ASC X12, EDIFACT, etc. a plus</li>
<li>
Excellent written, verbal communication and teamwork skills</li>
<li>
Experience with Agile/SCRUM ALM processes and tools</li>
<li>
Proven ability to influence cross-functional teams without formal authority</li>
<li>
Bachelors degree; Masters and higher preferred</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Personal Qualities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Exceptional interpersonal communication and relationship management skills at all levels of an organization including executive level</li>
<li>
Very high level of initiative, self confidence, energy and personal integrity</li>
<li>
Sharp intellect, curiosity and attention to detail</li>
<li>
Ability to manage multiple engagements and discussion streams simultaneously and capable of influencing cross–functional teams without being bestowed with formal authority</li>
<li>
Demonstrated career success with outstanding organizations</li>
<li>
Readiness to travel frequently</li>
</ul>
