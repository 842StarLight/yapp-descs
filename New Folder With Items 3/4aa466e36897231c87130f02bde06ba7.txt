<h2>Solution Architect (Field Application Engineer)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Code Corp is looking for a highly skilled Solution Architect Field Application Engineer for their growing OEM image-based data collection and machine vision business. Reporting into sales and managed by the OEM business manager, your role will be missioned primarily to "eliminate all technical barriers to closing deals". In support of this mission, you will interface with global customers and be the liaison to Code's engineering groups owning each project from a technical perspective in both pre- and post-sales integrations. Product knowledge and project management skills are essential. You will be the expert on all OEM products, advising customers on integration and application-specific best practices. Internally you will communicate customer requirements and use-case specifics while owning the project management through completion, testing, and delivery. Although technical in nature this role also requires soft skills, business acumen and an understanding of the sales process.</p>
<p><b>
Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Eliminate technical barriers to closing business.</li>
<li>
Be the product technical expert for OEM and related products.</li>
<li>
Technical and project manager-owner of OEM pre- and post-sale projects.</li>
<li>
Liaison between customer and engineering.</li>
<li>
Travel to customers to present technical information on products and attend trade shows as necessary.</li>
<li>
Solve highly technical problems remotely and onsite.</li>
<li>
Front-line technical support for OEM products.</li>
</ul>
