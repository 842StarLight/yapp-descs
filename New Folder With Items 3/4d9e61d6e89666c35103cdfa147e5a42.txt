<h2>Customer Care Coordinator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Position Summary</b>: Provide uncompromised service support and administration to internal and external Service Center customers. The role includes detailed daily follow-up with customers and account management.</p>
<p><b>
Essential Functions:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Comply with state and federal regulations, company policies, procedures, and guidelines</li>
<li>
Act as point of contact for general customer service</li>
<li>
Confer with customers by telephone, email, and mail</li>
<li>
Administer high volume of inbound customer service calls</li>
<li>
Scheduling appointment for Technicians and Sales</li>
<li>
Convert accounts receivable and process customer payments</li>
<li>
Respond to answering service messages</li>
<li>
Specialize in customer resolution</li>
<li>
Maintain and update customer account information</li>
<li>
Enter new services into database</li>
<li>
Invoice services rendered</li>
<li>
Support other office administrative staff in the completion of their duties when needed</li>
<li>
Attend and complete training and meetings as required</li>
<li>
Perform other duties as required by management</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Knowledge, Skills &amp; Abilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Customer Service - Knowledge of principles and processes for providing customer service. This includes active listening, assessing customer needs, and meeting quality standards for services.</li>
<li>
Compliance &amp; Safety - Knowledge of relevant regulation, procedures, and documentation to ensure effective, compliant, and safe treatment and services.</li>
<li>
English Language - Ability to write, speak, and communicate in English and ability to read and understand technical product labels.</li>
<li>
Active Listening - Giving full attention to what other people are saying, taking time to understand the points being made, asking questions as appropriate, and not interrupting at inappropriate times.</li>
<li>
Active Learning - Understanding the implications of new information for both current and future problem-solving and decision-making.</li>
<li>
Time Management - Managing one's own time and the time of others.</li>
<li>
Computers - Ability to transact and interact with multiple software systems</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Minimum Qualifications:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Working knowledge of Microsoft Office Suite</li>
<li>
Compliance with Drug-free Workplace Policy</li>
<li>
Able to pass a pre-employment background check upon offer of employment</li>
<li>
High school diploma or equivalent</li>
<li>
Present a professional appearance</li>
<li>
Able to work a 40-hour minimum work week</li>
<li>
Willing to work minimal evenings and weekends as needed</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Physical Demands</b>:</p>
<p>
The work environment and physical demands described below is representative of those that an employee encounters while performing essential functions of this job.<br>
</p>
<ul>
<li>
Regularly required to sit for long periods of time</li>
<li>
Constant interaction with computers and prolonged periods of viewing monitors</li>
<li>
Frequent telephone use on a multiline system</li>
<li>
Occasionally required to stand, walk, stoop, kneel, crouch, or crawl</li>
<li>
Occasionally lift and or/ move 10 to 25 pounds</li>
<li>
May be required to climb stairs depending on location</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Environmental Factors</b>:</p>
<p>
Work mostly indoors, in shared office environment</p>
