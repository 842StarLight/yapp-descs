<p>GDS has an exciting opportunity for a <b>Marketing Associate</b> at our corporate headquarters in Marietta, GA. Specific duties include (but not limited) the following:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Marketing Materials: Develop and update marketing materials.</li>
<ul>
<li>
Update lists of projects completed for clients.</li>
<li>
On-going maintenance of GDS qualifications and experience documents.</li>
<li>
Development of special marketing materials and brochures.</li>
<li>
Create, store, and maintain pertinent marketing files.</li>
</ul>
<li>
Social Media: Prepare news updates, monitor, and respond to inquiries on all GDS social media sites.</li>
<li>
Website: Add content and news updates to the GDS website.</li>
<li>
Gather / analyze website analytics via Google Analytics / Social Media Analytics on effectiveness of digital marketing programs/efforts and make suggestions /recommendations.</li>
<li>
Prepare advertisements to be published in statewide directories of municipal and electric cooperative utilities.</li>
<li>
Branding: Assist in creating a brand strategy and oversee implementation company-wide marketing plan.</li>
<li>
Assemble monthly, internal e-newsletter.</li>
<li>
Marketing Plan: Assist with creating and implementing company-wide marketing plan.</li>
<li>
Marketing Strategy: Assist with maintaining and coordinating a consistent marketing strategy across all services and departments.</li>
<li>
Trade Shows &amp; Conferences: coordinate with GDS departments on trade show/conference marketing support, transport and set-up marketing booths as necessary.</li>
<li>
Coordinate opportunities for GDS to publish technical articles and papers for industry-relevant trade journals.<br>
</li>
