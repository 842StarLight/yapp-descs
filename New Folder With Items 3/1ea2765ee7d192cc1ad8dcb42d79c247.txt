<p>As a member of the Line of Business (LoB) Leadership Team the People Business Partner defines and delivers the people plan to support the LoB achieving their strategic objectives and outcomes.
</p>
<p>Accountabilities and Essential Job Functions
</p>
<ul>
<li>Functional People Partner within the organization, while collaborating with other People team members across the globe; Member of the functional/LoB leadership team
</li>
<li>Business partner with function/LoB leadership team; leadership coach and advisor.
</li>
<li>Develop and drive the functional/LoB people plan in alignment with broader strategic plan and roadmap.
</li>
<li>Develop and drive a culture and engagement plan to ensure the team is living the Finastra Way and connected to a purpose-led Finastra.
</li>
<li>Dedicatedly analyze people data across a variety of sources to identify trends, patterns, and areas of opportunity. Work with partners in Total Rewards, L&amp;D, Diversity, , Talent Attraction, to drive impactful people solutions for the business
</li>
<li>Help the business identify, prioritize and build organizational capability for a future ready organization
</li>
<li>Create workforce plan for respective function/LoB optimizing different sourcing models and in alignment with financial targets and location strategies for the function/LoB in partnership with leaders, Talent Attraction and Finance.
</li>
<li>Lead Executive level hiring processes and hold the business accountable for building a diverse and inclusive leadership teams
</li>
<li>Tight alignment with finance on headcount forecasting;
</li>
<li>Regular review of organizational health and people metrics for collaboration with leaders, decision support and actioning
<p>Skills, Knowledge &amp; Experience
</p>
</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Trusted business partner who holds a seat at the functional/LoB leadership table
</li>
<li>Forward thinker with focus on delivery and execution
</li>
<li>Detailed planner
</li>
<li>Strong communication and influencing skills
</li>
<li>Ability to analyze data and draw insights to drive effective leadership discussions
</li>
<li>Team player
</li>
<li>Growth mindset
</li>
<li>Global HR experience; ability to travel
</li>
<li>Thrive when working in a fast paced, creative, agile environment
</li>
<li>Comfortable in an outgoing, social environment
</li>
</ul>
