<p><b>The Role
</b></p>
<p></p>
<p>Pricing Operations is responsible for creating, maintaining, and improving global pricing processes throughout the company. The Senior Pricing Manager will partner and collaborate with FP&amp;A, sales, revenue accounting, product marketing, legal, customer support, and provisioning to ensure a clean quote-to-cash process. This person would own SKU creation, maintenance, and rollout, and help define our global pricing strategy to maximize revenue and facilitate competitive market pricing and product offerings. This role will also support SKU and strategy alignment, to build additional efficiencies across quoting, approval and booking processes.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Your day-to-day
</b></p>
<p></p>
<p>SKU and Price Book Management
</p>
<ul>
<li>Establish and maintain SKU nomenclature, special terms, and product hierarchy
</li>
<li>Quarterly Pricebook rollout
</li>
<li>Manage channel pricing
</li>
<li>NPI &amp; M&amp;A pricing
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p>Pricing Strategy and Governance
</p>
<ul>
<li>Go-to-market and promotion planning
</li>
<li>Participate in pricing committee
</li>
<li>Support Approval Matrix
</li>
<li>Create pricing, SKUs, and strategy for specific market segments, verticals, and regions
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p>Reporting/Pricing Data Management
</p>
<ul>
<li>Develop KPIs and reports for product management in Power BI
</li>
<li>Build pricing modeling and analytics across product line as needed
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p>Documentation and Communication
</p>
<ul>
<li>Process documentation
</li>
<li>Communicate pricing and product changes across the company
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>What you bring to the team
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>BS/BA required in finance, economics, or technical concentration; MBA preferred
</li>
<li>5+ years in B2B pricing strategy, FP&amp;A, or management consulting
</li>
<li>Experience in SaaS and/or cloud software industries
</li>
<li>Demonstrated ability to use data analysis to impact business decisions, pricing strategy, and operations
</li>
<li>Strong interpersonal, communication and influencing skills, with the ability to build credible relationships with multiple functions and manage in a matrixed environment
</li>
<li>Ability to manage multiple complex, cross-functional projects, and to gain a quick understanding of product lines and BUs
</li>
<li>Salesforce power user; proficiency in Power BI, SQL, and R is a strong plus

<li>Detail-oriented, accountable, strong program management skills, and ability to quickly identify and resolve inefficiencies
 </li>
