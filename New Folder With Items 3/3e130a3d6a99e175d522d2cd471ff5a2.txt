<p><b>Virtual Account Manager
</b></p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Who We Are
</b></p>
<p>Solera is a global leader in data and software services that strives to transform every touchpoint of the vehicle lifecycle into a connected digital experience. In addition, we provide products and services to protect life’s other most important assets: our homes and digital identities. Today, Solera processes over 300 million digital transactions annually for approximately 235,000 partners and customers in more than 90 countries. Our 6,500 team members foster an uncommon, innovative culture and are dedicated to successfully bringing the future to bear today through cognitive answers, insights, algorithms and automation. For more information, please visit solera.com.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>The Role
</b><br>
AutoPoint is looking for a Virtual Account Manager who performs telephone and video-based consulting with automobile dealership management concerning their service &amp; marketing operations. The Virtual Account Manager identifies areas of opportunity for the dealership to increase revenue as well as makes suggestions for the improvement of dealership staff’s performance and productivity using AutoPoint products &amp; services. The Virtual Account Manager also consults to marketing strategies to promote &amp; grow dealership business via email, mail, &amp; call campaigns.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>What You’ll Do
</b></p>
<p>Productivity: Own assigned region of dealerships by providing monthly consultative events with every account, which is achieved with personal daily efficiency, consultative quality, and exceptionally organized scheduling Relationships: Have a genuine business relationship with service management &amp; key personnel within every assigned accounts Knowledge: Strong knowledge of all AutoPoint products &amp; services, their application within the dealership environment, and their representation within all key reporting; this achieved through initial 30-day training program, daily experience, &amp; ongoing learning events Growth: Drive new business growth within the assigned region by leveraging consultative credibility at each dealership &amp; recommending products that guarantee return on investment
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>What You’ll Bring
</b></p>
<ul>
<li><i>High school diploma
</i></li>
<li><i>2+ years professional experience with a minimum of one of the following: sales, or customer service, or automotive, or technological
</i></li>
<li><i>Self-motivated with little need for management involvement
</i></li>
<li><i>Strong verbal &amp; written communication skills
</i></li>
<li><i>Strong phone communication skills; good command of the English language
</i></li>
<li><i>Strong interpersonal communication skills; able to interface comfortably and effectively with all levels of dealership management
</i></li>
<li><i>Strong ability to interpret analytical data and share that information clearly with dealership personnel; ability to translate data into process improvement opportunities
</i></li>
<li><i>Sales Experience is preferred
</i></li>
<li><i>Must be able to sit for long periods of time in front of a computer
</i></li>
<li><i>Must be able to communicate on phones for 50% + of the day
</i></li>
</ul>
