<h2>Advertising Sales Coordinator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><p>SUMMARY:<br>
<br>
Assist the Brand &amp; Content Marketing team with advertising prospecting, research, invoicing, and collateral creation/dissemination. Secure revenue for the Your AAA Network by monetizing the local events calendar; selling marketplace ads; selling email banner space; digital packages; print magazine ads. Conceptualize new, on-brand campaigns and revenue ideas.
<br>
<br>
QUALIFICATIONS:<br>
<br>
Prospecting for print/digital Your AAA Network advertiser leads. Leverage MediaRadar and other research/data points to assist in increasing revenue and brand awareness. Liaises with AAA business lines as needed.<br>
<br>
Customizes and files insertion orders, recording advertising specifications and pricing. Manages the invoicing of clients, following up on unpaid invoices as needed. Handles other related administrative sales duties as assigned.<br>
<br>
Contact potential advertisers and close non-traditional revenue, including but not limited to, monetizing the events calendar, selling smaller local/marketplace-type ads, selling social media, tackling print co-ops ads, etc.<br>
<br>
Creates sales support collateral; upkeep of Media Kit; prepares and disseminates advertising-related reports to internal and external stakeholders.<br>
<br>
EDUCATION &amp; EXPERIENCE<br>
<br>
Bachelor degree in Marketing, Sales, Business or or 2 years of experience in lieu of degree<br>
<br>
<br>
REQUIRED SKILLS<br>
Networking<br>
Negotiation<br>
Task oriented<br>

Excellent communication and problem-solving skills<br>
Organization<br>
Business Acumen<br>
Creativity<br>

Ability to work independently and as part of the team<br>

We provide valuable roadside service to our 5.1 million members, as well as many specialized services such as Financial Services &amp; Loans, Insurance, Travel, Discounts, Auto Glass, and Driver Training. Through a 100-year legacy of service stewardship, our employees make valuable contributions to our workplace and the community that surrounds us.
