<p><b>About Avalon</b></p>
<p>
If you're looking to join a company with a dynamic vision for the future, set your sights on a rewarding career with Avalon. Recognized as the #1 brand of bottleless water cooler dispensers, our products continually set new standards in the hydration industry. <b>In-house opportunity only.</b></p>

<p><b>
Job description ** Only for Applicants who have worked for $50M+ consumer products brands**</b><br>
Marketing is for the person that loves a good dive deep and show a genuine interest into the world of Consumer Goods. These are the people that are consistently asking themselves what can we do to we make things even easier for our consumers. Marketing at Avalon is for the person who is never satisfied with creating anything less than the best product on the market.</p>
<p>
In this newly created position, the <b>Chief Marketing Officer</b> is responsible for the overall marketing execution for Avalon across all channels. The CMO will define and drive all branding and commercial activities for Direct to Consumer and Retail business for Avalon globally. You will <b>lead Brand Marketing, Digital, eCommerce, D2C, Media, PR and Creative teams</b> and will partner with Product Marketing/Development and Sales to ensure consistent and flawless execution. The CMO will be accountable for building and driving an A+ team and helping achieve annual revenue and profit targets.</p>
