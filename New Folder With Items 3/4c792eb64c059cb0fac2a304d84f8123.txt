<h2>Seasonal Customer Service Representative</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>The Seasonal Customer Service Representative is responsible for assisting customers with placing orders, answering questions and resolving issues with exceptional customer service via phone and/or email. There is a possibility for this position to evolve into a seasonal lead role.</p>
<p><i>Warning</i>: agents are exposed to the aroma of cookies and brownies daily.</p>
<p><b>Agent Profile: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Customer passionate!</li>
<li>Motivated and enthusiastic</li>
<li>Willing to go the extra mile</li>
<li>Ethical and organized</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Job Duties &amp; Responsibilities: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Receive and process customer orders by phone, fax and email.</li>
<li>Assists management with new hire training</li>
<li>Ensure calls are answered in a timely manner.</li>
<li>Be aware at all times of any customer calls in queue.</li>
<li>Perform outbound call programs.</li>
<li>Sell Mrs. Field’s products to customers; suggest items and up-sell on each call.</li>
<li>Track and research package issues for customers</li>
<li>Negotiate effectively with customers; find effective solutions to any problem with orders or Mrs. Field’s service and products.</li>
<li>Maintain a superior customer service image for Mrs. Field’s.</li>
<li>Process catalog requests and removals.</li>
<li>Participate in monthly and quarterly sales incentive programs; achieve sales goals as set by management.</li>
<li>Work overtime: during the peak sales season there will be required overtime work; some weeks exceeding 50hrs /week.</li>
<li>Participates in monthly and weekly team meetings.</li>
<li>Other duties as assigned.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Minimum Qualifications: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>High School diploma, GED certificate or equivalent</li>
<li>The ability to type 35 words per minute (a grammar and typing speed test will be required of each candidate considered for hire).</li>
<li>Proficiency in Microsoft Windows, Word and Internet browsing software.</li>
<li>Availability for a flexible schedule with some weekend shifts required.</li>
<li>3 to 6 months of previous call center experience (sales/suggestive selling experience is a plus).</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Job Environment and Conditions: </b></p>
<ul><li>General office environment.</li></ul>
<p><b>Physical Demands: </b></p>
<p>While performing the duties of this job, the employee is regularly required to talk and hear. Must be able to remain in a stationary position for at least 75% of the time or more. The employee must occasionally lift and/or move up to 10 pounds. Specific vision abilities required by this job include close vision, and ability to adjust focus. Reasonable accommodations may be made to enable individuals with disabilities to perform the essential functions.</p>
<p><b>Other: </b></p>
<p>All applicants must be eligible to work in the U.S.</p>
<p>The above statements describe the general nature and level of work only. They are not an exhaustive list of all required responsibilities, duties, and skills. Other duties may be added, or this description amended at any time.</p>

<ul>

</ul>
