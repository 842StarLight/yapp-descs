<h2>Stylist & Administrative Assistant</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Job Description:</p>
<p>
The Stylist &amp; Administrative Assistant’s position is a diverse position that involves daily and sometimes routine functions as needed. The Administrative Assistant will serve to support all of Boca Cosmetics Group key objectives. She/he will be responsible for generating online sales and implementing Professional and Consumer Education programs, email marketing, Customer/Client tracking and support. Support and assist customers/clients understanding the proper use of products, problem-solving, handling disputes and promoting additional products via telephone live chat, website, email, and social media networks.</p>
<p>
Duties and Responsibilities may include but is not limited to the following:</p>
<p>
a) Generate online sales by maintaining consistent contact with prospects and existing clients and customers through all available communications channels.</p>
<p>
b) Maximize company interactions through the use of “Boca Academy”.</p>
<p>
c) Recommend and implement approved online promotional offers with the objective to increase sales</p>
<p>
d) Implement Professional and Consumer Education programs</p>
<p>
e) Participate in product testing, in-house demonstrations programs.</p>
<p>
f) Assist management in staying current on latest salon trends and services</p>
<p>
g) Responsible for maintaining training center facilities</p>
<p>
h) Receiving and responding to inbound phone calls and emails from new and or existing customers, explaining the use of the products.</p>
<p>
i) Outbound calls to ensure customer or client satisfaction with product and service.</p>
<p>
j) Monitor website “live chat” and company emails for questions.</p>
<p>
k) Respond to inquiries from Professionals and Consumers promptly.</p>
<p>
l) Process all online orders received promptly.</p>
<p>
m) Maintain CRM system so that it is current at all times.</p>
<p>
n) Facilitate distribution of email newsletters as needed.</p>
<p>
o) Notify management of any unresolved issues immediately.</p>
<p>
p) Proactively propose updates to improve client relations</p>
<p>
q) Report and maintain a log of key customer comments including product performance, satisfaction, and suggestions.</p>
<p>
r) Responsible for ensuring that the physical inventory is in line with the inventory management software.</p>
<p>
Required Skills:</p>
<ul>
<li>Extremely well organized</li>
<li>Proactive and solution oriented</li>
<li>Strong interpersonal skills,</li>
<li>Excellent writing and communication skills</li>
<li>Typing 35-55 wpm,</li>
<li>Proficient in the use of Microsoft software, Word, Excel and PowerPoint</li>
<li>Ability to research (online)</li>
<li>Ability to problem Solve</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Education:</b> Licensed cosmetologist, two year Degree in Communications, PR or advertising. Social Media skills a plus.</p>

Email resume: careers@bocacosmeticsgroup.com</p>
