<h2>Digital Marketing Coordinator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>ConvergePoint (www.convergepoint.com) is seeking to hire a <b>Digital Marketing Coordinator</b><b> </b>to join our fast-growing team. The Digital Marketing Executive<b> </b>will be responsible for developing, implementing and managing marketing campaigns that promotes our company along with our products and services. This position will play a major role in enhancing brand awareness within the digital space as well as driving website traffic and acquiring leads/customers.<br>
</p>
<p>
ConvergePoint is a growing software startup and the leading provider of regulatory compliance software solutions on Microsoft SharePoint and Microsoft Office 365. We provide our clients with five full-featured software products for complete life-cycle management. Our products include Policies &amp; Procedures, Contracts, Conflict of Interest Disclosures, Incident Tracking and Employee Training. We currently serve mid-size and enterprise customers across the United States, Canada, UK, Australia, and South Africa.<br>
