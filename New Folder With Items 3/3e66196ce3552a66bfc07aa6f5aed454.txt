<h2>Customer Care Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Master Service Companies is looking for a highly motivated and positive individual to serve on our team of Customer Care Specialists.</p>
<p><b>Essential Job Functions:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Represents the company through telephone customer contact. Answers calls, sets sales appointments, sets annual maintenance or customer warranty service appointments, and follows up with customers on a variety of issues/occasions. (60%)</li>
<li>Keeps customer records and files current and updated, as well as sends customers information about the company and/or services performed. (25%)</li>
<li>Performs administrative functions to support all departments as needed. (15%)</li>
<li>Regular and punctual attendance. Ability to work overtime if needed.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Required Skills:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Knowledge of customer service principles and practices.</li>
<li>Skill in operating personal computer and programs such as spreadsheets, database, and word processing software.</li>
<li>Skill in verbal and written communication.</li>
<li>Ability to handle a variety of tasks.</li>
<li>Ability to perform accurate data entry.</li>
<li>Possess talent and personal traits:<br>
<ul>
<li>Customer Focus</li>
<li>Self Management</li>
<li>Planning &amp; Organization</li>
<li>Empathetic Outlook</li>
<li>Resiliency</li>
<li>Teamwork</li>
<li>Diplomacy &amp; Tact</li>
<li>Integrity</li>
</ul>
</li>
</ul>
<p>We are a friendly, laid-back, family-oriented company and offer great pay and benefits to our employees! Dress is casual! If you want to be part of an incredible team, apply today!</p>
