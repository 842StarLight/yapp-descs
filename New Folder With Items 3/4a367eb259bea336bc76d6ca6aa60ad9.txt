<p>Accessibility Software Trainer</p>
<p>CommonLook (commonlook.com), a pioneer and world leader in developing software, training, and providing services for document accessibility, is seeking an accessibility trainer to teach users on the various CommonLook software products.</p>
<p>The ideal candidate for this role will be personable, professional, highly motivated, autonomous, organized, methodical, reliable, punctual, detail-oriented, creative, confident in teaching others, and excited about new opportunities in a growing technology field. If you feel like this is you, please submit your cover letter and resume.</p>
<p><b>Essential Duties: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Present in-person and virtual training classes using a variety of different media and tools</li>
<li>Travel to our customers' locations to train on-site (approx. 35% travel)</li>
<li>Assist in developing new training initiatives and improving current offerings</li>
<li>Work closely with the Sales team to create and present tailored demos for clients</li>
<li>Based in California/ willing to relocate</li>
</ul>

</ul>
