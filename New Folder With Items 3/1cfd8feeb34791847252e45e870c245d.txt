Our company:</b><br>

<b>
Highlights:</b><br>
</div>
<div></div>
<div>As an Enterprise Account Executive Generalist, you focus on client-centric sales techniques and outlining how our solutions simplify efforts and improve efficiency. You are passionate about proactively pursuing new local enterprise accounts while managing an existing portfolio. Through your expertise and understanding of the client’s current and future needs, you outline uniquely beneficial combinations of our networking products.<br>
</div>
<div>You have developed a keen ability for identifying roadblocks and overcoming obstacles to increase business while enhancing the client experience. In this hybrid account executive and account management role, your goal structure is comprised of 80 percent new sales and 20 percent renewal sales from the territory’s existing client base. You excel at traveling extensively to capture new business accounts and support your current ones within a specified footprint. You report directly to Sales Management for goals, guidance and assistance.</div>

What you will do:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
Be an impactful member of the sales team through the achievement of monthly sales and revenue goals.</li>
<li>
Consult with prospective clients to develop tailored product solutions for their complex business needs.</li>
<li>
Develop long-term client relationships to support renewals and upsell opportunities.
</li>
<li>Drive the sales process by identifying target markets, industries and contacts for the product portfolio.</li>
<li>
Generate sales leads and build a network through persuasive sales calls, company visits, industry events and peers.</li>
<li>
Entice clients by designing and delivering informative and lucarative product proposals and presentations.</li>
<li>
Maintain an accurate client database to generate sales activity reports.
</li>
<li>Qualify leads through the submission of a return on investment (ROI) analysis and determine serviceability for prospects by requesting site surveys.</li>
<li>
Maintain quality service and encourage retention through coordinated efforts with the Account Executive, Account Management, Sales Engineering, Sales Support and Marketing teams.</li>
<li>
Develop key sales skills by attending sales meetings and participating in training sessions.</li>
</ul>
