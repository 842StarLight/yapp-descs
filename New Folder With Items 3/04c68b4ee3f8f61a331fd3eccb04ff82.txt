<h2>Developer Relations Director – Omniverse</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>NVIDIA Omniverse is a powerful new collaboration platform for 3D production pipelines based on Pixar Universal Scene Description (USD) and NVIDIA RTX™. Developers of 3D applications can build portals to connect their products to Omniverse so customers can seamlessly share assets with other industry standard applications. Omniverse viewer can also be integrated into the application interface, adding an ultra-high-quality real-time ray traced application viewport.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>What you’ll be doing:
</b></p>
<p>This is a public-facing role and you will evangelize and champion the use of Omniverse with the leading VFX studios, while working closely with NVIDIA product management and engineering to ensure the right things are being done to support studio adoption of this revolutionary platform. You will be required to prioritize and track your work and share notes and observations with the entire Omniverse team. You will work closely with the <b>Omniverse </b><b>Developer Relations: ISV Liaison </b>who will be driving the integration to Omniverse with the leading creative applications used in VFX and Film production. Some travel required to support product marketing activity at Tradeshows, Industry Events (e.g. Academy, VES), Conferences as well as Technical Sales Training.
</p>
<p></p>
<p>You will be working cross functionally with our organizations, as well as ensuring a positive experience for external customers and partners. You should be comfortable working in a dynamic environment experiencing rapid growth. Your ability to work independently is important, and you will rely upon your excellent communication skills during consulting engagements, and to communicate opportunities and threats internally.
</p>
