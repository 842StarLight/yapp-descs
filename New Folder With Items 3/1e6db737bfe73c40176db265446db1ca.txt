<p>

<p><b>
Business Intelligence Analyst</b>: This role will be responsible for meeting with business users to understand and document their key reporting requirements and then translating those requirements into reports and/or interactive dashboards for key decision makers. All report and dashboard creation will be done utilizing the Oracle Business Intelligence Suite.</p>
