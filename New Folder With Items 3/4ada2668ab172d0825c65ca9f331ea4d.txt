<h2>Restaurant Assistant Manager - Northern Quest Resort and Casino</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Northern Quest Resort and Casino</b>, the Inland Northwest’s Premier Resort and Casino, is seeking a <b>Restaurant Assistant Manager</b> to lead our Service and Culinary team for <b>Masselow’s Steakhouse</b>, our award winning restaurant and lounge. We are growing and are looking for individuals who are capable of leading the way.</p>
<p><b>Summary of Functions</b><br>The primary function of the Venue Assistant Manager is to support the Venue Manager with creating an excellent food and beverage experience for the customer within the venue and areas of the casino. Through active participation on the floor, the Assistant Manager will ensure their venues are pristine, orderly and the daily operations and customer interactions run seamlessly. This role is responsible for increasing food and beverage sales and profitability through training, coaching and budgetary management; successful implementation of organizational standards and procedures as outlined in franchise and/or organizational manuals; adheres to the Core Values and Mission Statement as established by the Executive Team and Tribal Counsel.</p>
<p><b>Qualifications</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Associate of Arts (A.A) or Associates Degree in related field, or equivalent hospitality industry experience</li>
<li>2 years of experience in hospitality or food and beverage supervisory or lead role.</li>
<li>Must have a good understanding/experience with Forbes and AAA 4 Star / 4 diamond service standards</li>
<li>Proven experience with motivating and leading teams of employees.</li>
<li>Experience in craft cocktails, specialty beer, and wine service required.</li>
<li>Inventory systems and budget management experience preferred.</li>
<li>Experience with POS systems required</li>
<li>Proficient in the use of Microsoft programs and other computer software.</li>
<li>Knowledge of procurement procedures and inventory systems.</li>
<li>Excellent organizational, communication and interpersonal skills.</li>
<li>Work nights, weekends and holidays as required.</li>
<li>Available and willing to work nights, weekends and holidays as required.</li>
<li>Washington State health card – By date of hire.</li>
<li>Ability to pass a background check.</li>
</ul>
