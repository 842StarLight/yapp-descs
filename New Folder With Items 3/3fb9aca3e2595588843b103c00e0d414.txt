<div><b>Position Summary</b></div>
<div>
<p>The UNC Kenan-Flagler Business School is seeking an Associate Director of Individual Giving to be a part of a successful and dynamic alumni engagement and individual giving team. Reporting to the Executive Director of Alumni Engagement and Individual Giving, the Associate Director will focus on leadership annual giving and the Kenan-Flagler Alumni Council. The person in this role will split time between frontline fundraising and volunteer management – advancing the mission of the School through individual donor meetings.</p>
<p>
The Associate Director will also work closely with the Dean’s Circle (giving society for leadership-level donors) in creating a strategy for growth of the Circle. This person will coordinate across the Advancement Team to ensure that members of the Dean’s Circle have a unique donor experience and specific events and stewardship.</p>
<p>
In partnership with the Assistant Director of Alumni Engagement, the Associate Director will also develop a growth plan for the School’s Alumni Council, working with Council leadership and committees to coordinate annual meetings, and orchestrate the advancement of key Council initiatives.</p>
</div>
