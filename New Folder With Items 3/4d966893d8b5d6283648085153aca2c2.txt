<h2>Demand Generation Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>Job Description<br>
<br>
<p>We’re out to build a global Hiring Success movement, and the events team is the heart of it all. We’re looking for a rockstar marketer who will be responsible for driving marketing campaigns including email, registration strategy, as well as working cross-functionally with sales to drive event success.<br>
<br>
You’ll wear multiple hats, think creatively and work with our leadership team to ensure a great event. We’re looking for someone who will pay attention to the small details, that can build and measure success.<br>
<br>
This person will be accountable for driving significant registration for our San Francisco and EMEA events. Last year we drive 1000 registrations and it is one of the coolest conferences on the market. Check it out at https://www.smartrecruiters.com/hiring-success/americas/</p>
<p>
This role will be responsible for driving marketing campaigns, registrations, as well as building one-of-a-kind activities and experiences. You’ll wear multiple hats and both drive strategy, while also executing logistics and effectively measure program success. We’re looking for someone who will pay attention to the small details (while also thinking creatively), and can help drive results with cross-functional team members.</p>
<p>
This full-time role will be based in our San Francisco office and report to our Director of Events.</p>
<p>
Job Duties and Responsibilities:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Lead promotional campaigns and registration programs for annual industry conference to drive desired results and outcomes</li>
<li>
Develop strategy and execute marketing campaigns across multiple channels, employee incentive programs, sales promotions, outbound calling campaigns and more</li>
<li>
Build targeted campaigns for customers, prospects and partners including custom messaging, segmentation and metrics for all campaigns</li>
<li>
Test, track and measure ROI of all offers for all audiences</li>
<li>
Deliver delightful and engaging experiences for customer, partner and prospect attendees with various event tasks and asks</li>
<li>
Partner with stakeholders across the company, including executive staff, product, sales, operations and customer success teams</li>
</ul>
</div>
<br>
<div>Qualifications<br>
<br>
<ul>
<li>2-4 marketing experience in demand generation and campaigns, conference experience is preferred but not required</li>
<li>
Creative, flexible self-starter</li>
<li>
Strong attention to detail, proven experience managing multiple projects at once</li>
<li>
Strong written and verbal skills, ability to present to both internal and external audiences</li>
<li>
Experience in B2B enterprise software space</li>
<li>
Preferred: Experience building programs and events for HR audience</li>
</ul>
