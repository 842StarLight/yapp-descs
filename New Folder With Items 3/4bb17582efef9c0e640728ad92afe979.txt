<h2>Systems Engineer - Service Provider</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Your Career:</b></p>
<p>
We are currently seeking a seasoned Service Provider Sales Engineer to help drive sales engagements from a technical perspective into strategic accounts in the US across verticals. In this position, you will play a key role in navigating complex accounts in order to generate and deliver winning proposals, contract bids, statements of work, and RFI/RFP responses.</p>
<p>
The Systems Engineer, with Managed Security Services specialty, is directly responsible for the technical sales motion for his/her targeted service provider customers. The focus is to ensure that that our service provider customers can build Managed Security for Services Provider (MSSP); and offer the strongest security posture/differentiation possible - selling and correctly implementing the complete Palo Alto Networks solution. This is an exciting time in MSSP with strong growth and large prospect customers.</p>
<p><b>
Your Impact:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Understand and effectively present our security platform and solutions</li>
<li>
Create and deliver compelling proof of concept demonstrations of Palo Alto products and solutions</li>
<li>
Create strategies and work successfully in a channel-driven sales model</li>
<li>
Create successful go to market strategies, provide education and training to sales reps and attend technical forums to upsell Palo Alto products and services</li>
<li>
Work closely with sales leadership on creating go to market strategies and business development and identifying new opportunities to drive growth on the Service Provider account</li>
<li>
Educate customers and partners on the modern network security landscape</li>
<li>
Identify and qualify new opportunities and support technical pre-sales activities to opportunity closure</li>
<li>
Expand the Palo Alto Networks install base within the assigned Service Provider account</li>
<li>
Assist customers through the support escalation process</li>
<li>
Continuous self-improvement and learning to maintain current technical knowledge, participate in technical communities of interest</li>
<li>
Identify problems in customer environments and drive successful outcomes</li>
<li>
Effectively engage SP partner and end customers at the technical level and management levels</li>
<li>
Effective in customer-facing engagement, and in writing, presentation, and creative problem-solving skills</li>
</ul>

Your Experience:</b></p>
<ul>

Sales engineering experience</li>
