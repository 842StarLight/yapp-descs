<h2>Executive Host</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p><b>Job Summary:
</b></p>
<p>This is a sales and customer support position. The casino host will be an individual who is self-motivated, high energy and attentive to guests needs. This position requires the ability to multi-task between direct customer contact on the casino floor, telemarketing and letter writing.</p>
<p><b>
Essential Duties and Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Fluent at making inbound and outbound telemarketing calls.</li>
<li>
Proficient letter and email writing skills.</li>
<li>
Understands table games operations.</li>
<li>
Responsible for achieving specific revenue goals through developing high end play.</li>
<li>
Serves as a liaison for casino guests who meet applicable criteria.</li>
<li>
Focuses on driving department productivity through acquisition of targeted player signups and repeat visitation.</li>
<li>
Develops and sustains working relationships with all guests, with emphasis on high end players.</li>
<li>
Recruits new players through a variety of direct sales activities such as direct mail, telemarketing, referrals, guest visitation and events.</li>
<li>
Manage table games tournaments.</li>
<li>
Exercises discretion and independent judgment with respect to manage guest comp budgets.</li>
<li>
Issues comps in a manner that supports business goals.</li>
<li>
Promptly and professionally responds to gust requests, complaints, and problems.</li>
<li>
Makes and confirms guest reservations for future events.</li>
<li>
Acts as master of ceremonies or host at player events and promotions.</li>
<li>
Becomes fluent in the loyalty program software. Makes and reprints loyalty cards.</li>
<li>
Maintains a clean and welcoming environment for guests to the host office.</li>
<li>
Required to maintain proper attendance including reporting to work on time in accordance with applicable policies.</li>
<li>
Represents the Canterbury Park in a positive and professional manner in the community.</li>
<li>
Performs other special projects and duties as required or assigned.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Required Qualifications:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>High School Diploma/GED</li>
<li>
Extensive game knowledge and experience</li>
<li>
Must be adaptable, versatile and tactful in dealings with the public</li>
<li>
Reliable, pleasant personality, able to deal with difficult customers and situations.</li>
<li>
The ability to learn quickly and work well under pressure is essential</li>
<li>
Must have excellent oral and written communication skills</li>
<li>
Leveraging sales techniques to maximize performance</li>
<li>
Internally motivated to graciously serve, delight and build player loyalty</li>
<li>
Must have basic computer knowledge</li>
<li>
Able to speak multiple languages preferred.</li>
<li>
Canterbury Park conducts pre-employment screening for all positions which includes a criminal background check and verification of work history.</li>
<li>
Must be able to attain a Minnesota Racing Commission (MRC) license.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Working Conditions and Physical Requirements:</b></p>
<p>
The physical demands described here are representative of those that must be met by an employee to successfully perform the essential functions of this job. Reasonable accommodations may be made to enable individuals with disabilities to perform the essential functions.</p>
<ul>
<li>
While performing the essential functions of this job, the employee is occasionally required to stand for 100% of shift.</li>
<li>
Maintain a flexible working schedule, which may include nights and weekends.</li>
<li>
Must be able to interact with guests, co-workers and vendors in a professional, courteous manner.</li>
<li>
Must be able to work in an office environment utilizing computer and phone.</li>
<li>
Must be able to lift up to 10 pounds.</li>
<li>
Requires frequent wrist or hand use.</li>
</ul>
