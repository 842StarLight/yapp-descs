<h2>Inside Channel Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>Be the key in unlocking business opportunities in the convergent world of Open Platform IP Video Management Systems! Join Milestone, the global market leader!<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>The ICM will focus on software and hardware sales through the development of Milestone resellers within an assigned region. The ICM will work to develop and execute a scalable, cost effective and focused channel partner development process that will help increase the number of active selling partners, see YoY revenue growth and accelerate the sales of newly signed partners.</p>
<br>
<p>
</p>
<p>As a Milestone Employee, YOU are the key to our success. Come join our dynamic international team and be a part of our rapidly growing, successful business.</p>
<p>
Responsibilities:</p>
<ul>
<li>Develop and manage a region of channel partners as mutually agreed with management and field sales personnel. Main focus will be the generation, support and continued growth of:</li>
<li>Milestone video management software sales
</li>
<li>Milestone network video appliance sales
</li>
<li>Existing reseller partner development</li>
<li>Provide on-boarding assistance to newly signed channel partners</li>
</ul>
<p>Tasks:</p>
<ul>
<li>Daily phone calls to assigned key partners to assist in growing their revenue stream with Milestone</li>
<li>Daily phone calls to new and inactive partners, getting them onboard and supporting their growth</li>
<li>Inform partners of Milestone resources they can use – MyMilestone Portal, Server Design Tools, Pricing Tools, eLearning/Certification options, partner events (local/national)</li>
<li>Document call activities and log/manage sales opportunities in Salesforce</li>
<li>Lead informative webinars and perform live software web demos
</li>
<li>Communicate with the field personnel when needed</li>
<li>Sales forecasting</li>
<li>Occasional travel into the assigned region required</li>
<li>Other tasks as assigned by manager</li>
</ul>
<p>Professional Qualifications:
</p>
<ul>
<li>Inside Sales experience
</li>
<li>Channel partner management experience</li>
<li>Bachelor’s Degree or equivalent work experience</li>
<li>CRM/Salesforce experience</li>
</ul>
<p>Personal Qualifications:</p>
<ul>
<li>Ability to exercise discretion and independent judgement with important matters</li>
<li>Ability to uphold organizational values</li>
<li>Excellent communication skills</li>
<li>Excellent organizational skills</li>
<li>Excellent problem-solving skills</li>
<li>Willingness to provide excellent customer service</li>
<li>Detail oriented</li>
<li>Flexible</li>
<li>Interested in traveling
</li>
</ul>
