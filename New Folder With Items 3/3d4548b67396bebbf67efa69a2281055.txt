<h2>Tech Support for Alarm Security Systems and Home Automation Products</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>We are seeking a full-time, tech-Savvy Specialist who will be responsible for supporting customers experiencing problems with their integrated security, cameras, home automation, and/or network connections. The Specialist will work from our Hagerstown, Maryland office, fielding incoming calls via telephone and e-mail and documenting, diagnosing, troubleshooting, and solving issues.</p>
<p>In addition to being technology-minded and having the ability to resolve issues, this Specialist must have an amazing customer-centric attitude, provide outstanding service and follow-through, be dependable and accountable, own their job, and be committed to resolving issues.</p>
<p><b><i>About us: </i></b></p>
<p>Dynamark Monitoring, Inc. is a rapidly growing provider of security and fire monitoring and equipment, video security and managed access control. Our Central Monitoring Center is UL and FM Listed and Five Diamond Certified by CSAA placing us in the top 2% of monitoring centers nationwide.</p>
<p><b><i>Duties and Responsibilities of Specialist</i></b></p>
<p>Primary:</p>
<ul>
<li>Provide technical assistance and support for incoming queries and issues related to residential alarm systems. Communicate directly with customers via phone or email.</li>
<li>Use detective-like troubleshooting skills to ask the right questions and gather the data to narrow down the root cause of issues. The goal is to resolve the issue via phone whenever possible while providing excellent service.</li>
<li>Walk customers through problem-solving process.</li>
<li>Document detailed notes about the purpose of the customer contact and corrective actions taken.</li>
<li>Schedule/coordinate equipment repair and follow up on field technician service tickets until issues are completely resolved.</li>
<li>Assist with retaining customer accounts through proactive/reactive calls.</li>
<li>Expand and maintain technical knowledge as required.</li>
</ul>
<p>As needed:</p>
<ul>
<li>Review invoices and submit for payment.</li>
<li>Make outbound calls to collect past due balance on customer accounts.</li>
<li>Various other projects and duties as assigned.</li>
</ul>
<p><b><i>Requirements</i></b></p>
<ul><li>Highly desired 1+ years experience with the following systems: intrusion detection(Interlogix, Qolsys, 2 GIG, Honeywell, DSC), Alarm.com, Honeywell total connect, and some uplink.</li></ul>
<ul>
<li>High-level technical aptitude, problem-solving skills, and attention to detail required. Knowledge of networks, structured cabling, and telephone cabling a plus</li>
<li>Associate's degree in Electronics, Computer Science or related area or equivalent experience, including field service a plus</li>
<li>Ability to guide partners/techs in diagnosing and repair/replace recommendations for alarm systems</li>
<li>Positive attitude, empathy, and desire to help people</li>
<li>Clear communication with customers, team members, and field technicians</li>
<li>Ability to deal with urgent matters in a calm professional manner, maintaining service excellence</li>
<li>Ability to multi-task and prioritize workflow</li>
<li>Proficient computer skills for documenting, reporting, communicating, tracking and calculating. Microsoft Office and spreadsheet skills highly desired.</li>
<li>Ability to work a flexible schedule (We currently serve customers 8 am to 8 pm M-F)</li>
<li>Sales experience a plus</li>
</ul>
<ul><li>Bilingual Spanish a plus</li></ul>
<p><b>Hourly non-exempt position. Pay depends on experience and technical expertise.</b></p>
