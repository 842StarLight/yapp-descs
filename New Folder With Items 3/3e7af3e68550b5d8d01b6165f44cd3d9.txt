<h2>Senior Account Executive, Mid-Market</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">We're looking for a dynamic, high-performing Senior Account Executive to join the Honest team. Honest Buildings is now a Procore ( https://www.procore.com/press/procore-to-acquire-honest-buildings-to-enhance-project-management-for-owners ) company. Together, our platforms create unified financials and cost tracking for owners, general contractors, and subcontractors.
<br><br>
As a Senior Account Executive, you'll grow revenue by adding new logos to our existing customer base. You'll manage, proactively hunt, and close a list of named target accounts. The ideal candidate has experience selling technology and a history of bringing on new business and hitting sales quotas.
<br><br>
This position is based at our NY-Downtown, Honest Buildings office. We're looking for someone to join our team immediately.
<br><br>
<b>What you'll do:</b><br>
---------------------
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Manage complex sales cycles to close net new logos and nurture developing accounts through a disciplined "land and expand" strategy</li>
<li>Deliver monthly, quarterly, and annual revenue targets on a named list of strategic target accounts</li>
<li>Develop and execute a sales plan; maintain and communicate an accurate sales forecast</li>
<li>Thoughtfully work with Business Development Representatives and Marketing to develop strategic account plans</li>
<li>Clearly communicate our value proposition to prospects, including C-level executives, via phone, email, and in-person</li>
<li>Partner cross-functionally with Product, Customer Success, and Deployments to deliver on customer expectations</li>
<li>Manage activities, pipeline, and forecast within CRM system (Salesforce.com)</li>
</ul>
<b>What we're looking for:</b><br>
-----------------------------
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>5+ years in a SaaS Account Executive role</li>
<li>At least 2 years of Enterprise SaaS Sales experience</li>
<li>Experience with managing a list of ~100 target accounts or less</li>
<li>Demonstrated history of closing multiple six figure deals</li>
<li>Proven ability to comfortably navigate C-Level discussions across both business and technology stakeholders</li>
<li>Consistent track record of over-achievement against plan in past positions (top 10-20% of the company)</li>
<li>Exceptional organizational and time-management skills with a demonstrated history of being a self-starter</li>
<li>Knowledge of CRM software (eg. Salesforce) is a plus</li>
<li>CRE Tech experience is a plus</li>
</ul>
