<div>CDM Smith is a multi-discipline engineering and architectural firm. Our firm is responsible for large-scale water and wastewater plant design, transit facilities, industrial facilities, office space design, facility planning and condition assessment work. As an Architect at CDM Smith you will have the opportunity to be a part of a collaborative multidisciplinary team of technical experts working on challenging projects in Environment, Industry, Commercial, Transportation, and Water.<br>
<br>
The Architect will have the following responsibilities:<br>
<br>
<ul>
<li>Plan, program, and formulate building design and prepare design presentation and construction contract documents.</li>
<li>Develop building design criteria directly with clients. Lead preparation of presentation materials and make presentations.</li>
<li>Lead development and coordination of building design and construction contract documents with architectural and multi-discipline engineering staff.</li>
<li>Lead and guide junior architectural staff on multi-discipline projects.</li>
<li>Lead determination of construction materials, technologies and methodologies for detailing and specifications.</li>
<li>Lead compliance with applicable planning, zoning, and building codes and regulations, including coordination with agencies having jurisdiction.</li>
<li>Lead development of project budgets and schedules, both internal and external. Monitor projects through close out.</li>
<li>Support sales, meet with clients and serve as a Project Technical Lead for Architectural driven projects.</li>
</ul>
