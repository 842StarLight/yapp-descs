<h2>Enterprise Marketing Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>ScreenCloud helps businesses communicate better with their teams and customers using simple digital signage software. Think of us as the operating system for all the screens at a company.</p>
<p>
We have over 8000 customers around the world, including household names like Amazon, BBC, Sonos, Dropbox, and Uber.</p>
<p>
We are looking for an Enterprise Marketing Manager to join our Marketing team. The role is based in our offices in our LA office. This person will be responsible for increasing enterprise pipeline in the US with inbound and outbound campaigns and manage enterprise-focused events/roadshows.</p>
<p>
This is a great time to come on board. We're well-funded, have a growing customer base, and are established as an excellent educator. Our vision is to be the go-to brand for learning about digital signage. This is a chance to assist with our ambitious brand plans, it's going to be quite an adventure.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Key Areas of Responsibility:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Building awareness of ScreenCloud among enterprise audience, with a particular focus on US market</li>
<li>Running inbound and outbound account-based marketing campaigns to generate enterprise leads</li>
<li>Working closely with Sales Development Reps (SDRs) to create a top of the funnel pipeline of leads for them to qualify</li>
<li>Working closely with the Sales Team to improve sales messaging, collateral and engagement with enterprise leads</li>
<li>Building awareness and urgency of ScreenCloud solutions using content from our Content Team</li>
<li>Owning our presence at key industry events and partner roadshows aimed at enterprise</li>
<li>Working with industry media to showcase ScreenCloud’s thought leadership in workplace technology and internal communications</li>
<li>Managing the enterprise marketing budget</li>
<li>The majority of the Marketing Team is in London – this role would act as an ambassador and connection to the US team</li>
<li>Giving us insight into the market and help us maintain our culture and tone of voice in the US</li>
</ul>
