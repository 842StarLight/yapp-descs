<h2>Sales Representative</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Sales Representative</p>
<p><b>Department: </b>Sales - Roofing, Windows, Siding and Decks</p>
<p><b>Title: </b>Sales Representative (Team Player)</p>
<p><b>Reports to: </b>Sales Manager</p>
<p><b>Job Summary</b></p>
<ul>
<li>You must be comfortable and confident up on roofs, ladders and be comfortable with heights.</li>
<li>The Sales Rep is responsible to learn and apply the Certified Contractor Network Selling Systems. The Sales Rep’s primary mission is to demonstrate to the prospective client, using the CCN Process, that the services offered by Krueger Brothers Construction are a better value than those offered by our competition.</li>
<li>Respect and carry out any and all rules, regulations, or policies, which may be established from time to time by Krueger Brothers Construction.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>The Company's Role and Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>The Company is committed to give you every opportunity to realize your full potential. Your growth, development, and satisfaction are of paramount importance to us. We are dedicated to your personal development through ongoing sales, personal development and product training.</li>
<li>We are a Team always looking for a better way to satisfy our customers and employees. If you have a suggestion, let us know. You will be acknowledged and recognized.</li>
<li>We will provide you with the necessary tools. These include the latest samples, brochures, literature, business cards, contracts; computer generated proposals, training and personal development seminars, etc. In short, everything you need to ensure success.</li>
<li>We will supply you with leads on an ongoing basis. These leads are obtained through referrals, job sign advertising, display online advertising in various publications, etc.</li>
<li>Regular one-on-one meetings are held to review the past week's sales activities and to help you strategies and prioritize the following week's activities.</li>
<li>We will receive and dispatch to you on a timely basis all of your messages and phone calls.</li>
<li>Our management, installation crews, and office support staff are committed to provide you, your prospects and customers with excellent service satisfaction. We expect you will do the same.</li>
<li>We will provide you with a detailed breakdown of your commissions earned on a weekly basis. In addition, you will receive a regular monthly report on all relevant sales and lead related statistical information.</li>
<li>Commission: Up to a set amount per contract will be paid within three weeks from the client signing the contract. giving a deposit and the dollar amount and scope are acceptable to the KBC standards. Sales Commission are paid in advance and not withheld (up to a certain $$ amount) until the project is complete.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Sales Person’s Responsibilities include</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
<b>Goals</b>- Setting and obtaining goals for personal and professional growth and success.</li>
<li>
<b>Training</b>- Attend Company and CCN meetings &amp; training as directed. Have a personal Continuing Education program, such as, audio training, sales training literature, etc.</li>
<li>
<b>Practice</b>- Participate in role playing of sales calls, product presentations, and closing with other sales personnel to improve each other’s skills.</li>
<li>
<b>Learn</b>- Learn and apply new technical information and product knowledge and sales techniques as changes occur so you are better able to overcome competition.</li>
<li>
<b>Measure Call</b>- Professionally handle Measure calls. Establish trust and confidence with the consumer. Set a Sales Call appointment when the complete buying party will be present.</li>
<li>
<b>Leads -</b>When necessary make sure you are successful by implementing self-developed lead methods to assure an adequate number of Sales Appointments.</li>
<li>
<b>Samples</b>- Maintaining assigned samples and sales tools in a professional condition.</li>
<li>
<b>Vehicle</b>– The company will provide you with vehicle, to carry samples and attend sales calls. You are responsible for keeping it neat and clean.</li>
<li>
<b>System</b>- Make sales calls following the Company Selling System.</li>
<li>
<b>Close</b>- Close sales calls using “THE PROCESS".</li>
<li>
<b>Communications</b>- Prepare paperwork necessary to facilitate the sold project from Sales Department through the Production Department to ultimate Customer Satisfaction.</li>
<li>
<b>Debriefing</b>- Attending and participate in Debriefing of sales calls.</li>
<li>
<b>Availability</b>- Be available to make sales calls at times required which includes early mornings, evenings and Saturdays.</li>
<li>
<b>Reports</b>- Provide the required reports by management.</li>
</ul>
<p>*</p>
<p><b><i>Work Performed &amp; benefits.</i></b></p>
<ul>
<li><b>Must be willing and able to confidently use a ladder, handle a ladder, climb up on ladders, and roofs, decks, window inspection for a minimum of 2+ stories.</b></li>
<li>
<b>Working Hours</b>- may occur during the day, early mornings or evenings, and Saturdays. This is not an 8-5p Mon-Friday job.</li>
<li>
<b>Change Orders</b>- <b>Field</b> <b>Relations</b>- <b>Reporting - Training</b>
</li>
<li>
<b>Base Salary –</b>Will be $33-40K gross / year, paid weekly. DOE in the exterior construction industry. With commission 80-100K+ per year</li>
<li>
<b>Benefits -</b>After 90 days the Rep may participate in a company insurance which is estimated at $350 a month.</li>
<li>
<b>401k</b>is available after the employee is full invested</li>
<li>
<b>Truck –</b>A Company marked truck and fuel will be provide (all insurance, tag and plates, maintenance, and tires included) for business use. It is expected that this truck will be kept clean and available when not used for sales / customer appointments etc.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Education Knowledge and Skills</b></p>
<p>The Sales Person should be in good health with a positive mental attitude, able to preform light manual labor, and use / climb ladders when necessary for their job. In addition to sales skills, the Sales Person is expected to develop a comprehensive knowledge about the products being sold. Self-motivation and a burning desire to be successful (long hours and hard work) and the ability to not take rejection personally are essential for success. The Sales Person should have a Continuing Education plan which includes attending sales training meetings, read sales books, study sales and products training videos, audio tapes and trade journals. Extreme attention to detail is required, follow through and follow up. A go getter!</p>

</ul>
