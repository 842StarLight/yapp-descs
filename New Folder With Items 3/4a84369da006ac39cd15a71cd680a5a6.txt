<h2>Advertising & Marketing Intern</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p><b>DOMINO</b> is looking to hire an Intern to join our Brand Partnerships team at our NYC headquarters. The Brand Partnerships team is primarily responsible for leading the pre-sale activation process by brainstorming marketing strategies and creating highly customized programs for clients.This is a marketing role that covers both the proposal and execution process for branded programs. As an Intern you will assist with pulling together client proposals, ideating and creating proactive sales materials, running competitive analysis and much more! You will work with our Senior Brand Partnerships Manager on programs that bring the Domino POV to our advertisers’ products and services.<br>
</p>
<p><b>Who’s a good fit?</b></p>
<p>A passionate, curious, eager person who can come in and begin at full speed. You’re both creative and strategic and excel at creative storytelling. You can problem-solve on your feet with ideas that help champion the Domino brand, while helping to identify advertiser and partner needs. You are goal oriented and want to help Domino become a publishing leader and go-to media partner for advertisers and media agencies alike.<br>
</p>
<p>As an Intern, your responsibilities will include:<br>
</p>
<ul>
<li>Assist with agenda and recap for weekly meetings with the Brand Partnerships team, updating the proposal management doc daily</li>
<li>Help with any administrative duties related to marketing initiatives and/or events</li>
<li>Collaborate with internal teams to build strategic marketing proposals that leverage Domino’s platforms to reach advertisers’ target audiences and meet campaign KPI’s</li>
<li>Brainstorm with sales, editorial, and creative teams to help structure campaigns that incorporate the Domino brand filter along with the client’s objectives</li>
<li>Always take note of what’s next and what’s new in omni channel media sales and think about how these can be applied to our business; become a thought leader on how to make the old seem new, and propose fun test products for advertisers</li>
<li>Create sales tools for team to close business with</li>
<li>Collect, synthesize and interpret post-campaign performance metrics to provide advertisers with key insights and learnings to ultimately inform strategies for future Domino campaigns in the form of end-of-campaign recap decks<br>
</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Qualifications:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Current rising senior pursuing undergraduate degree or recent graduate</li>
<li>Coursework related to business/marketing/design</li>
<li>Eagerness to learn</li>
<li>Ability to absorb information quickly and execute tasks efficiently</li>
<li>High-energy, outgoing personality</li>
<li>Excellent verbal and written communication skills</li>
<li>Analytically minded</li>
<li>Deep knowledge of all major social channels Instagram, Facebook, Twitter, Pinterest</li>
<li>Proficiency in all Google platforms, Microsoft Office and Apple Keynote<br>
</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Benefits:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Paid internship</li>
<li>Timing: November and beyond</li>
<li>Work from Domino HQ in NYC</li>
<li>Mentorships from leaders within our company</li>
</ul>
