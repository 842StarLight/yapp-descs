Develop core business applications and provide technical support. The job encompasses responding to developing enhancements requested by the business for existing clients business applications.</li>

<li>Assist with technical/business evaluation and decision making processes for solutions.</li>
<li>Develop and support approved solutions in accordance with Information Systems Standards.</li>

<li>Document approved solution in accordance with Information Systems Standard Operating Procedures.</li>
<li>Coordinate approval of solution documentation with appropriate stakeholders.</li>
<li>Perform and document tests on developed solution.</li>
<li>Identify and learn appropriate software applications used and supported by the organization.</li>
<li>Provide after hours on-call support as assigned to address emergency and urgent issues.</li>
