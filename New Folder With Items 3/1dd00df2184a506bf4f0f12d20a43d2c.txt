<p><b>GENERAL DESCRIPTION OF POSITION</b></p>
<p>
 The Assistant Branch Manager is responsible for the efficient daily operation of a full service retail branch. In the absence of Branch Manager, they would assume full responsibility for full service branch. Their responsibilities include the promotion of bank products and services that support overall company goals.</p>
<p>
 <b>ESSENTIAL DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES</b></p>
<p>
 1. Partner with management to coordinate and attain branch, region, and company objectives.</p>
<p>
2. Assist with hiring, coaching, and leading a strong support team of retail staff.</p>
<p>
3. Comply with all company policies and procedures.</p>
<p>
4. Must have the ability and knowledge to backup all retail positions within the branch.</p>
<p>
5. Ensure all retail or branch operational functions are performed accurately and in a timely manner.</p>
<p>
 6. May originate, process and close various consumer and small business loan products.</p>
<p>
 7. Responsible for maintaining and developing business for the branch.</p>
<p>
 8. Participate in civic and community organizations as needed.</p>
<p>
 9. Complete regular monthly branch audits.</p>
<p>
10. Ensures all security procedures are strictly followed.</p>
<p>
11. Mitigate monetary losses by utilizing good judgement and sound decision making skills in managing the daily operations of the branch(es).</p>
<p>
12. Conduct regular staff meetings to keep staff informed.</p>
<p>
13. Complete employee's annual performance reviews in a timely manner, and disciplinary action as needed.</p>
<p>
14. Maintain a safe and professional business environment. Ensure all equipment is in good working order.</p>
<p>
15. Participate in regular SWOT analysis for competitive advantage.</p>
<p>
16. Lift coin bags up to 50 lbs.</p>
<p>
 17. The ability to work in a constant state of alertness and in a safe manner.</p>
<p>
 18. Completes required BSA/AML training and other compliance training as assigned. This duty is performed as needed.</p>
<p>
 19. Perform any other related duties as required or assigned.</p>
<p>
 <b>QUALIFICATIONS</b></p>
<p>
 To perform this job successfully, an individual must be able to perform each essential duty mentioned satisfactorily. The requirements listed below are representative of the knowledge, skill, and/or ability required.</p>
<p>
 <b>EDUCATION AND EXPERIENCE</b></p>
<p>
 Knowledge of a specialized field (however acquired), such as basic accounting, computer, etc. Equivalent of four years in high school, plus night, trade extension, or correspondence school specialized training, equal to two years of college, plus 2 years related experience and/or training, and 1 to 6 months related management experience, or equivalent combination of education and experience.</p>
<p>
 <b>COMMUNICATION SKILLS</b></p>
<p>
 Ability to effectively communicate information and respond to questions in person-to-person and small group situations with customers, clients, general public and other employees of the organization.</p>
<p>
 <b>MATHEMATICAL SKILLS</b></p>
<p>
 Ability to calculate figures and amounts such as discounts, interest, commissions, proportions, percentages, area, circumference, and volume. Ability to apply concepts such as fractions, ratios, and proportions to practical situations.</p>
<p><b>
CRITICAL THINKING SKILLS</b></p>
<p>
 Ability to solve practical problems and deal with a variety of known variables in situations where only limited standardization exists. Ability to interpret a variety of instructions furnished in written, oral, or diagram formats.</p>
