<h2>Property Manager II</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">Market. Manage. Maintain. These are just a few of the many hats you’ll be wearing in this highly visible role of Property Manager at the Princess Anne Townhouses! In this role, you will be responsible for maintaining the community, supporting its residents, and developing team members all while meeting financial objectives of the owner and management company.
<br><br>
Responsibilities
<br><ul>
<li>Collaborate with senior management to establish and review all matters related to rent</li>
<li>Process timely and accurate move-ins, move-outs, and renewals</li>
<li>Process re-certifications for Section 42 and Low-Income Housing Tax Credit (LIHTC) programs</li>
<li>Ensure that wait-lists, resident files, and all operations are in complicance with all HUD and LIHTC requirements as well as Fair Housing Laws</li>
<li>Approve rental applications following property and agency standards</li>
<li>Ensure all property resident files are organized, complete, and accurate</li>
<li>Ensure the property and grounds are well maintained</li>
<li>Resolve resident issues and conflicts in a timely manner and in accordance with site guidelines</li>
<li>Prepare the property’s annual budget for approval by senior management</li>
<li>Manage timely collection and deposits of all rents and fees</li>
<li>Hire, develop, and support new team members in accordance with Winn’s Guiding Principles</li>
</ul>
Requirements
<br><ul>
<li>High School Diploma or GED</li>
<li>1-3 years of property management experience</li>
<li>Excellent customer service skills</li>
<li>Experience with Section 42 housing</li>
<li>Proficient in Microsoft Office Suite and experience with property management software (Yardi, OneSite, etc.)</li>
</ul>
Preferred Qualifications
<br><ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree</li>
<li>1+ years of supervisory experience</li>
<li>OneSite Property Management software experience</li>
<li>Certifications in NAHP, CPL, SHCM, CAM</li>
</ul>
