<h2>Registered Administrative Assistant</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p><b>Position Goals:</b></p>
<p>
Provides administrative, operational, sales and service support for assigned Investment Consultants within the First Merchants Investment Services Team.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Direct Management Received:</b></p>
<p>
Under the general management of the Operations &amp; Administration Manager, Investment Services.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Direct Management Exercised:</b></p>
<p>
None</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Essential Duties and Responsibilities:</b></p>
<p><b>
Business Management</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Review and act upon items for attention from broker / dealer (Cetera)
</li>
<li>Monitor incoming and outgoing transfers
</li>
<li>Process death claims
</li>
<li>Assist with 1099s and other tax time issues
</li>
<li>Communicate and process client Required Minimum Distributions (RMD)
</li>
<li>Review of insufficient funds for periodic distributions
</li>
<li>ACH maintenance
</li>
<li>Manage and maintain client withdraw/ deposit requests
</li>
<li>Beneficiary updates/ personal information updates (with Cetera &amp; Product sponsors)
</li>
<li>Cost basis information
</li>
<li>Filing client documents<br>
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Client Service</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Place unsolicited trades &amp; securities received
</li>
<li>Complete necessary documentation to handle the processing of trades
</li>
<li>Meet with clients to obtain client and/or advisor signatures
</li>
<li>Establish online access to financial planning software or other pertinent websites
</li>
<li>Schedule client review appointments
</li>
<li>Handle phone calls &amp; emails; respond appropriately to client needs
</li>
<li>Communicate with product partners on behalf of the client and/or Investment Consultant<br>
</li>
</ul>
<p>
</p>
<p><b>Business Development</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Assist in preparation of documents for account opening, transfer of assets, etc.
</li>
<li>Annuity Net Order Entry (when applicable/ necessary)
</li>
<li>Review items for attention
</li>
<li>Establish ACH and or Periodic / Systematic deposit
</li>
<li>Scanning and submitting new account documentation (when applicable / necessary)
</li>
<li>Filing new account documents
</li>
<li>Assist in review of My Business Status
</li>
<li>Confirm all direct accounts are linked to Cetera accounts<br>
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Administrative Support</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Handle phone calls &amp; emails; respond appropriately to internal/ external partners
</li>
<li>Assisting Consumer Bank Employees with FMIS client issues, referrals &amp; setting appointments
</li>
<li>Maintain &amp; Order Office Supplies
</li>
<li>Meet with advisor to review instructions when preparing to meet with new clients.<br>
</li>
</ul>
<p>
</p>
<p><b>Operational Support</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Confirmation of daily ACH / settlements
</li>
<li>Processing and filing of monthly transmittals to Cetera Principal Review Desk
</li>
<li>Submitting marketing / advertising to compliance (business cards, stationary, signage, etc.)
</li>
<li>Process regional incoming / outgoing mail
</li>
<li>Maintain/ monitor check blotter
</li>
<li>Assistance in maintaining compliance of all required FINRA files and documentation
</li>
<li>Document all contact and actions taken with client in CRM<br>
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>

<p><b>Position Requirements</b>:</p>
<p>
Education - High school diploma or equivalent (GED).</p>
<p>
Experience - Over one (1) year of administrative, clerical or related experience.</p>
<p>
 Experience utilizing a Contact Management System (CRM)</p>
<p>
Licensing- FINRA Series 6 License- Investment Company and Variable Contracts Products Representative within 6 months of employment</p>
<p>
 FINRA Series 63 License- Uniform Securities Agent State Law within 6 months of employment</p>
<p>
Other - Proficient with Windows based software, including MS Word, Excel, and PowerPoint</p>
<br>
<p></p>

Preferred Requirements</b>:</p>
<p>
Licensing- FINRA Series 7 License- General Securities Representative</p>
<p>
 FINRA Series 65 License- Uniform Investment Adviser Law</p>
<p>
 FINRA Series 66 License- Uniform Combined State Law</p>
