<p><b>Account Manager or Senior Account Manager</b></p>
<p>
Viracor Eurofins is dedicated to being the leading specialty diagnostics laboratory partner in immunology, infectious disease, and allergy testing. We offer a range of unique clinical laboratory and biopharmaceutical services and provide exceptional test turnaround times. More than 4,000 healthcare providers, commercial laboratories, and biopharmaceutical companies rely on us for our unique and convenient services.</p>
<p>
Viracor Eurofins is dedicated to being the leading specialty diagnostics laboratory partner in immunology, infectious disease, and allergy testing. We offer a range of unique clinical laboratory and biopharmaceutical services and provide exceptional test turnaround times. More than 4,000 healthcare providers, commercial laboratories, and biopharmaceutical companies rely on us for our unique and convenient services.</p>
<p>
Viracor Eurofins is searching for an <b>Account Manager or Senior Account Manager. </b><b>The ideal candidate would live within a major metropolitan city inside the sales territory.</b></p>
<p><b>
Sales Territory includes: Orange County, San Diego, Inland Empire, Hawaii</b></p>
<p>
This is a <b>Full Time</b> position. Schedule is <b>Monday – Friday, 8:00AM – 5:00PM,</b> with overtime as needed.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Basic Function and Scope of Responsibility:</b></p>
<p>
The Account Manager is primarily responsible for achieving the financial objectives of an assigned territory by effectively implementing Viracor Eurofins’ sales and marketing strategies to grow test volume and revenue. Specifically, the Account Manager will be responsible for expanding business in existing Accounts and securing business from new Accounts.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Employee Responsibilities:</b></p>
<p>
Level I Minimum</p>
<div>
Attainment:</div>
<div>
Achieve 100% of sales quotas for assigned territory</div>
<ul>
<li>Territory Management:
<ul>
<li>Analyze information and data to develop a territory plan to achieve financial objectives - maximizing call schedules, targeting, and appropriate company and test positioning</li>
<li>Travel, including overnights as needed, to effectively cover geographical territory responsibility</li>
<li>Organize, prioritize and perform sales activities which must adhere to business needs and objectives set annually by Sales Management and will include territory routing, call activity and pre-call planning goals.</li>
<br>
</ul>
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<div>Selling:</div>
<ul>
<li>Work with manager to develop and implement a territory and strategic sales plan involving multiple stakeholders in the growth of the Viracor Eurofins product portfolio.</li>
<li>Responsible for maintaining and growing the base revenue stream and insuring client satisfaction through consistent and ongoing client contact</li>
<li>Calls on prospective clients to create demand, communicate medical, clinical and patient outcome benefits, deliver product information, prepare quotes and proposals within company guidelines</li>
<li>Promptly and efficiently move a client through the sales process from cold call to close and follow-through</li>
<li>Effectively prepare and deliver formal sales presentations to clients;</li>
<li>Follow through after the close to ensure appropriate sales processes are in place to maximize test volume from an account and to ensure timely payment from account</li>
<li>Conduct client business reviews, as dictated by annual goals, throughout the year to retain and grow client business</li>
<li>Develop and maintain an effective/professional working relationship with external customers and internal colleagues to provide the best service possible
</li>
<li>Represent department and organization professionally and favorably and in accordance with established Company standards and associate attributes at all times</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p>
</p>
<div>Administration:</div>
<ul>
<li>Perform routine weekly &amp; monthly administrative duties by established due dates</li>
<li>Enter call notes into Salesforce.com asap but no later than Monday of the following week the call was made</li>
<li>Update sales opportunities indicating close date, staging, products/revenue for weekly department meeting</li>
<li>Submit expense reports within 30 days of being incurred</li>
<li>Complete Media Lab and other assigned departmental training</li>
<li>Performance Expectations:</li>
<li>Other duties as assigned by management</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p>
</p>
<p>Level II – Fully meets the responsibilities of Level I plus the following:</p>
<ul>
<li>Independently develop and implement territory strategy
</li>
<li>Mentor/coach other Account Managers</li>
<li>Assist with training sessions for sales team members</li>
<li>Assist marketing dept. with research opportunities, data collection, customer reviews and promotion development</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p>
</p>
<p><b>The Ideal Candidate would possess:</b></p>
<p>
Level I - Minimum</p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s Degree required, degree in the Sciences,Business Administration or Marketing preferred</li>
<li>0 – 3 years of direct sales experience, laboratory services or medical sales experience is preferred</li>
<li>Ability to travel up to 80%</li>
<li>Customer service focused and professional attitude</li>
<li>Ability to solve problems, prioritize and multi-task
</li>
<li>Goal oriented, with excellent time management and organizational skills</li>
<li>Excellent interpersonal skills, with ability to interact effectively and work efficiently with people at all levels in an organization</li>
<li>Excellent verbal &amp; written communication skills</li>
<li>Keenly attentive to detail</li>
<li>Ability to keep sensitive information confidential</li>
<li>High level of proficiency with PC based software programs, specifically Microsoft Excel, SF.com, iPad and associated apps</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p>
</p>
<p>Level II – Fully meets the qualifications of Level I plus the following:</p>
<ul>
<li>2+ direct sales experience required</li>
<li>2 years territory goal achievement in last 3 years</li>
<li>Work with minimum supervision on highly complex projects</li>
</ul>
<ul><li>Exceptions to these qualifications must be pre-approved by hiring manager and HR.</li></ul>
