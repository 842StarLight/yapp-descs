<h2>Entry Level Management Trainee</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>(Chicago)</div>
<div>
<p>
The ENTRY LEVEL Sales Representatives at UCG Inc are a valued team members in the marketing and advertising department. The position is considered entry level to start during training which includes a comprehensive overview of basic marketing strategy, direct advertising, promotional techniques, and consumer market research. Management trainees are taken though a 6month accelerated program to prepare them for UCG’s Campaign Manager role
</p>
<p>If you possess a passion for marketing, customer service, sales or retail and would enjoy learning how to manage the varying dimensions of a business, this position could be an excellent fit for the right candidate.
</p>
<h5 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Duties and Responsibilities:
</b></h5>
<ul><li>Assisting in the daily growth and development of assigned campaigns</li></ul>
<br>
<ul><li>Assisting with efforts of customer acquisition and retention</li></ul>
<br>
<ul><li>Expertly managing the needs of external customers</li></ul>
<br>
<ul><li>Developing strong leadership and interpersonal skills</li></ul>
<br>
<ul><li>Driving sales through community-based promotional campaigns</li></ul>
<br>
<ul><li>Build brand recognition through local outreach and experiential marketing</li></ul>
<br>
<ul><li>Strategize, execute and manage with the Brand Ambassador teams</li></ul>
<br>
<ul><li>Interact and communicate with customers</li></ul>
<br>
<ul><li>Problem solve and make professional judgment on whether customers qualify for services that the firm’s clients offer (candidate will be trained in this area)<br>
</li></ul>
<h5 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Qualifications:
</b></h5>
<ul><li>Must be able to work full time hours and some weekends for special events</li></ul>
<br>
<ul><li>Ability to excel in unsupervised solo assignments as well as team projects</li></ul>
<br>
<ul><li>Desire to travel at least 1 or 2 weeks a year for further training</li></ul>
<br>
<ul><li>Great communication skills</li></ul>
<br>
<ul><li>Must be able to work in an energetic, fast paced environment</li></ul>
<br>
<ul><li>2 or 4 year college degree in related field</li></ul>
<br>
<ul><li>Self-starter, creative thinker, problem solver</li></ul>
