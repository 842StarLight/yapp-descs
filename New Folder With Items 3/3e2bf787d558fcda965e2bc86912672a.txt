<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Business Unit Overview</b></h2>
Warner Bros. Home Entertainment Group (WBHEG) brings together all of the Company’s businesses involved in delivering home entertainment content to consumers. This includes global distribution of packaged and digital media, and formats including DVD, Blu-ray, video-on-demand, cable, satellite, online, mobile, electronic sell-through and more.</div>
<div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Opportunity Overview</b></h2>
<b>
What part will you play?</b><br>
<br>
Warner Bros. Home Entertainment Inc. seeks a Manager, Film Marketing for the Film department.<br>
<br>
In this role, the Manager will:
<br>
<ul>
<li>Develop physical and digital distribution business plans for New Release (films) and Franchise Back Catalog films (DC), including defining marketing strategy for each title, product positioning, volume justifications, street date analyses, product configurations, and managing fixed advertising costs for US and Canada.
</li>
<li>Communicate and manage tactical details with cross-functional teams (Creative Services, Promotions, Operations, Sales Administration, Media, Interactive Marketing, Research, Publicity, Sales, Legal and Management) Tasks would include writing media strategic brief, developing TV spot rotation, writing consumer creative brief, maintaining all title level updates in distributed reports, etc… for US and Canada.</li>
<li>
Coordinate retail account execution with Trade Marketing: Develop selling guides and retailer creative assets, review and approve trade materials, provide meta data direction, coordinate delivery of retail assets for Us and Canada.</li>
<li>
Manage communication with Int’l territories. Share product strategies with other markets and gather key strategic and tactical information to help manage a consistent consumer message on a global basis. Drive global marketing initiatives and share best practices.</li>
<li>
Review and approve all creative materials for accuracy and brand integrity including promotional assets, PR clips, packaging, ad mat, one sheets, TV inset art, online banner ads, social media posts etc.</li>
<li>
Support Director and VP on ad hoc analysis and budget needs.</li>
</ul>
<b>
What do we require from you?</b><ul>
<li>
BA/BS degree in Business Administration, Literature, Film, or related field or equivalent work experience.</li>
<li>
MBA preferred.
</li>
<li>2-4 years experience in marketing, entertainment marketing, consumer packaged goods, advertising agency or account management.</li>
<li>
High proficiency with MS Office Software (Word, Excel, Powerpoint).</li>
<li>
In depth knowledge of theatrical and home entertainment product.</li>
<li>
Analytical skills (both quantitative and qualitative).</li>
<li>
Must be able to review &amp; comprehend detailed documents or instruments, particularly P&amp;L statements and financial scenarios.
</li>
</ul>
