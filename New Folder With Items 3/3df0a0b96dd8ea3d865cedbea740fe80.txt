<h2>Account Manager, Client Success</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Job Summary</b></p>
<p>
As an Account Manager, you will develop real relationships with clients earning a position as a trusted advisor. You will guide clients in making decisions that result in overcoming the challenges they face in their operations, budgets and reporting. In this role, you will deliver regular account updates and reports, including time-sensitive action items for clients to execute.</p>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
The role has an approximately 70/30 split between strategic account management and escalated client support. 70% of your time will focus on driving strategic development and execution, while 30% of your time will target general account management activities and internal advocacy for the client in high-value or critical performance situations.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div><b>
In this role, you will have the opportunity to:</b></div>
<div></div>
<br>
<ul>
<li>
Become the trusted advisor for your clients, building relationships that allow your clients to choose to be enthusiastic advocates for Sequoia.</li>
<li>
Be on the front lines of retention, upsell, and engagement.</li>
<li>
Provide ongoing strategic advice to clients to help them achieve their operational, economic and environmental goals.</li>
<li>
Present quarterly Savings &amp; Account Status reports.</li>
<li>
Use your knowledge, creativity, communication skills, and organizational skills, in collaboration with other Sequoians, to deliver solutions that are tangible and yield positive results towards your clients' goals.</li>
<li>
Drive adoption of our predictive sensor technology and client utilization of our digital first tools.</li>
<li>
Increase the retention rate of your clients.</li>
<li>
Assist clients and fellow Sequoians with account needs as necessary.</li>
<li>
De-risk and defuse client escalations</li>
</ul>
<div><b>
We are looking for people who have:</b></div>
<div></div>
<br>
<ul>
<li>
An extraordinary understanding of proactive client service and datadriven development.</li>
<li>
5-7 years of equivalent experience in a relevant industry/field.</li>
<li>
An ability to develop and lead an account strategy over the client's lifecycle.</li>
<li>
A desire to be an advocate for the best solution for the client.</li>
<li>
The confidence to perform presentations and demonstrations to express the value of our technology and reporting capabilities.</li>
<li>
Analytical thinking, and a willingness to challenge established practices and orthodoxy.</li>
<li>
Concise written and verbal communications.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Certifications, Licenses, Credentials:</b></p>
<ul><li>
None Required</li></ul>
<p><b>
The Company</b></p>
<p>
Founded in September 2011, Sequoia Waste Solutions unscrambles the confusing world of waste and recycling for our clients. We leverage proprietary IoT hardware, software, and control one of the largest databases of industry pricing to empower our customers, resulting in lower costs, more efficient operations, and a reduction in environmental impact.</p>
