<h2>IT Analyst II (TSG)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<div><b>Position Summary</b></div>
<div>
Responsibilities include software testing estimation, analysis, planning, execution, management, and<br>
</div>
<div>Reporting on software testing projects. These responsibilities include assisting in the development,</div>
<div>
Implementation and support of the Software Quality Assurance standards, methods, techniques and tool usage related to quality systems development and testing.</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div><b>
Key Duties / Responsibilities</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Provide application and workflow support to our internal users.</li>
<li>Support and administration for the DP application and services.</li>
<li>Support of sales and implementations teams with developing and implementing services and solutions.</li>
<li>Applies good modeling principles and practices.</li>
<li>Assists in maintaining low complexity applications/systems.</li>
<li>Infrequent systems analysis work.</li>
<li>Responsible for fulfilling end-user requests.</li>
<li>Good facilitation skills.</li>
<li>Perform work with minimal supervision. Exercise considerable latitude in determining testing objectives of assignments</li>
</ul>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div><b>
Experience / Skills / Abilities Required</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Strong understanding of application design and functionality concepts needed for rapid diagnosis of a problem.</li>
<li>Strong data and data management techniques, queries, and database concepts needed to investigate and resolve data-driven errors or issues.</li>
<li>5+ years software testing experience.</li>
<li>Experience administrating/utilizing software testing tools is required.</li>
<li>Excellent written and verbal communications skills are required.</li>
<li>Knowledge of defect tracking tools is required.</li>
<li>Adapt at anticipating user behavior and solving problems efficiently and creatively.</li>
</ul>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div><b>
Education &amp; Knowledge Required</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Associates degree or equivalent work experience</li>
<li>Reporting/report-writing and DSS experience.</li>
</ul>
