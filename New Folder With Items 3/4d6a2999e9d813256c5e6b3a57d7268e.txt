<h2>Front Desk Sales and Service Coordinator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Promotes and oversees the quality and success of the sales and service operation. This will encompass all front desk operations including sales functions, customer service, retail and vending, and workplace employee activities. Will act as the first point of contact for potential and current customers, making you responsible for presenting a positive image for the company. In addition, this individual will be responsible for providing weekly audits to the General Manager and Assistant General Manager.
</p>
<p><b>Position Outcomes &amp; Activities:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Develop sales for new memberships</li>
<li>
Encourage innovation and use ideas from employees as a means to grow the business.</li>
<li>
Schedule and manage birthday/pool parties to ensure standard operating procedures are being maintained.</li>
<li>
Maintain and achieve key business performance goals sales, margins, and net operating profit</li>
<li>
Assist in directing and controlling the daily operations: Answer telephones, responds to inquires, and service existing membership accounts.</li>
<li>
Processes student registrations using effective sales/customer service techniques.</li>
<li>
Provide leadership, discipline, training and constructive feedback to all qualified front desk and sales staff.</li>
<li>
Gain a high level of knowledge and experience in the CRM and Booking software to achieve a high level of competence in bookings, class scheduling, reporting and administrative functions.</li>
<li>
Provide weekly reporting to management covering all key performance indicators Customer issues, discrepancy in numbers, registration/withdrawal log, leads, missing leads, contacted leads, and enrolled leads.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Position Qualifications: </b>Experience and Education requirements.
</p>
<ul>
<li>High School Diploma, 2+ years of University or Bachelor's Degree preferred</li>
<li>
2+ years experience of strong management and/ or sales background</li>
<li>
Demonstrated skills in customer service and relationship building</li>
<li>
Strong organization skills, computer skills, and the ability to multi-task and effectively manage multiple situations at once</li>
<li>
Able to sustain a high level of energy and enthusiasm</li>
<li>
Demonstrated a consistent trend of improved performance in previous leadership positions</li>
</ul>
