Position: </b>DevOps Engineer</p>
<p><b>
Position Description:</b></p>
<p>
We are looking for an experienced DevOps professional to join our growing organization. In this position, you will manage infrastructure projects and processes. A keen attention to detail, problem-solving abilities, and solid knowledge base are essential.</p>
<p><b>
Duties:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Proactively identify performance weaknesses and propose solutions</li>
<li>Manage and evolve our CI/CD pipelines</li>
<li>Building/managing an internal test environment that mirrors our production systems</li>
<li>Interaction with the engineering team to support and grow backend application infrastructure.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Requirements:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Individuals who value teamwork, are customer-centric, and understand and get excited about doing what it takes to succeed at a fast-paced software company.
</li>
<li>Bachelor’s Degree in Information Systems, Computer Science, or equivalent experience</li>
<li>2+ years of experience as system administrator or DevOps engineer</li>
<li>Excellent Linux/UNIX systems administration skills</li>
<li>Experience with configuring and maintaining automated deployment tools</li>
<li>Experience with continuous integration tools</li>
<li>Experience with monitoring tools</li>
<li>Strong understanding of IP networking fundamentals</li>
<li>Superb attention to detail</li>
<li>Ability to troubleshoot emergency infrastructure incidents to achieve optimal application performance and stability</li>
</ul> <p>We do not offer sponsorship at this time.</p>
