<p><b>Job Title
</b></p>Director, Sales Technology
<p></p>
<p><b>Job Description
</b></p>
<p><b>I. Job Summary </b>| <i>Major goals and objectives.
</i></p>
<p>The Director, Sales Technology will lead a team of business analysts and technical professionals who oversee the day-to-day activities of Meredith’s sales technology platforms, primarily the Salesforce.com environment. This involves collaborating with consulting partners, assessing needs of the user base and working with application owners of ancillary business systems to establish integration strategies. This role will work with Advertising Sales and Advertising Operations personnel to identify new opportunities to enhance functionality or achieve greater process efficiencies.
</p>
<p></p>
<p>It is imperative that this person possess strong communication skills, recognizing their audience and adjusting the message accordingly. The Director will serve as the communication hub for requests coming from Meredith’s Advertising business which will require a level of comfort and ability to communicate effectively with front-line personnel and corporate executives to capture business requirements. It is equally important that these business requirements be relayed to the technical teams for implementation and delivery.
</p>
