<h2>Field Service Coordinator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<div>
<div>
<b>Summary:</b><br>
Candidate will be a key member in KVH’s Global Service Team working to coordinate new equipment installations and repairs on board ships in North America, Central America, and South America. The ideal candidate may have experience working for a Fleet Manager or a Maritime hardware supplier coordinating projects/services on vessels. Candidate must be able to work a flexible schedule, including regular weekends</div>
<br>
</div>
<div><div>
</div></div>
<div>
<div><b>Essential Duties and Responsibilities:</b></div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
<ul><li> Coordination of equipment installations:</li></ul>
<br>
</div>
<div> <div>o Work with Customer and KVH’s professional services team to identify:</div>
<br>
</div>
<div> <div>§ Equipment list to be installed</div>
<br>
</div>
<div> <div>§ Installation scope of work</div>
<br>
</div>
<div> <div>§ KVH service partner who will perform on board work</div>
<br>
</div>
<div> <div>§ Measures to assure installation quality</div>
<br>
</div>
<div> <div>o Communicate installation plan to ship owner or management company</div>
<br>
</div>
<div> <div>o Assure equipment is sent to installation prior to the commencement of work</div>
<br>
</div>
<div> <div>o Act as escalation point for any problems encountered during an installation</div>
<br>
</div>
<div> <div>o Secure post install documentation and file as appropriate</div>
<br>
</div>
<div> <div>o Route information required to invoice customer and pay service partner</div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
<ul><li> On Board Equipment Repair:</li></ul>
<br>
</div>
<div> <div>o Vessels requiring service will be identified by KVH’s technical support team</div>
<br>
</div>
<div> <div>o Assure replacement parts are sent to the port where service will be provided</div>
<br>
</div>
<div> <div>o Contract with local service partner for required installation work</div>
<br>
</div>
<div> <div>o Assure service records are updated to reflect repair event</div>
<br>
</div>
<div> <div>o Determine whether service event is covered by warranty and invoice customer as appropriate</div>
<br>
</div>
<div> <div>o Route information required to pay KVH service partner for work performed</div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
<ul><li> Service partner training administration:</li></ul>
<br>
</div>
<div> <div>o Help coordinate service partner training events when scheduled</div>
<br>
</div>
<div> <div>o Process participant training certificates</div>
<br>
</div>
<div> <div>o Update training records in a database</div>
<br>
