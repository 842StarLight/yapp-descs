<h2>Field Sales Representative</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>The Field Sales Representative (FSR) is responsible for business development, service to customer and customer relationships. The FSR increases business by selling additional brands and packages within his or her sales territory’s existing customer base. The Field Sales Representative at Coke Florida works with specific strategic customers, specifically 7-11 stores.</p>
<p><b>
Role Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul><li> Making sales and assist in obtaining orders from customers</li></ul>
<ul><li> Customarily and regularly performs these primary duties in the field visiting customer accounts away from a company facility or office</li></ul>
<ul><li> Build and maintain profitable customer relationships</li></ul>
<ul><li> Execute and close all sales calls</li></ul>
<ul><li> Sell in incremental displays and equipment placements; sell in promotional programs and ensure dealer compliance</li></ul>
<ul><li> Review business results with key decision makers at customer outlets</li></ul>
<ul><li> Ensure Company assets meet performance requirements and develop a plan of action for those assets generating zero or low volume</li></ul>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Additional Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul><li> Ensure outlets comply with Coke Florida standards. Communicate account activities to appropriate parties to ensure customer accounts are appropriately serviced</li></ul>
<ul><li> Service the account on every visit – to include merchandising and rotation inspection</li></ul>
<ul><li> Lead, schedule and at times execute resets in order to expand portfolio</li></ul>
<ul><li> Resolve customer inquiries, including researching and closing customer service tickets</li></ul>
<ul><li> Transport, replace, and maintain Point of Sale advertising as appropriate for account</li></ul>
<ul><li> Maintain regular and punctual attendance</li></ul>
<ul><li> Work a variable schedule, weekends and holidays required</li></ul>
<ul><li> Comply with all company policies, procedures, and applicable laws</li></ul>
<ul><li> Identify and report any unsafe working conditions</li></ul>
<ul><li> Comply with dress code or appearance/grooming standards</li></ul>
<ul><li> Work shifts and work schedules as assigned</li></ul>
<br>
<p></p>

<p><b>Role Requirements and Skills</b></p>
<ul><li>High School or GED (General Education Diploma)
</li></ul>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor Degree preferred</li>
<li>Must be 18 years of age or older</li>
<li>1+ years of general work experience</li>
<li>1+ years previous sales experience preferred</li>
<li>Food/beverage industry experience preferred</li>
<li>Ability to create and conduct sales presentations required</li>
<li>Strong attention to detail and follow-up skills</li>
<li>Excellent planning and organization skills</li>
<li>Proficient in computer application skills</li>
<li>Ability to use and understand mobile applications</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Ability to work variable schedule, weekends and holidays required</li>
<li>Must have and maintain a valid driver's license</li>
<li>Must have and maintain current vehicle liability insurance, as specified by company</li>
<li>Must have a driving record with no major moving violations in the last three (3) years*</li>
<li>Must provide and maintain a personal vehicle for use during employee working hours</li>
<li>Frequent lifting of 25+ pounds. Occasional lifting of 50+ pounds, bending, reaching, kneeling, and light merchandising</li>
<li>Ability to read information in small, medium, and large print</li>
<li>Ability to stand a minimum of 6 hours during the workday</li>
<li>Ability to walk a minimum of 4 miles during the workday</li>
</ul>
<br>
<ul><li>Major moving violations include, but are not limited to, leaving the scene of an accident, driving under the influence, and reckless driving. Minor moving violations include, but are not limited to, speeding, driving too close, failure to stop, and improper lane changes. Driving records, including minor moving violations, will be evaluated according to Coke Florida guidelines to determine eligibility for the Field Sales Representative position.</li></ul>
