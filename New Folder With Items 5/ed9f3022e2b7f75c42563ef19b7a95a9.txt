<h2>CRA Relationship Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div><i>Community Development Act of 1977, 12 U.S.C. § 2901 et seq.) is a United States federal law designed to encourage commercial banks and savings associations to help meet the needs of borrowers in all segments of their communities, including low-and moderate-income neighborhoods.”</i></div>

<div><b>POSITION SUMMARY:</b></div>
<div></div>
<div>Responsible for originating conventional and government residential mortgage loans to low-to moderate income individuals (LMI) or consumers in low-to-moderate income census tracts (LMIT) who qualify based on underwriting criteria and guidelines. Originate loan applications for LMI/LMIT borrowers. Develop market in assigned geographical CRA Assessment Areas through fostering new relationships with community partners; maintain existing relationships; identifying centers of influence; managing referral sources. Facilitate educational workshops on affordable housing, economic development and financial literacy. Identify sponsorship opportunities. The Relationship Manager is expected to call on government sponsored entities and not-for-profit agencies that support LMI/LMIT and support the banking centers. This position is expected to manage referrals from banking centers, lines of business and manage the relationship of the customer from application, origination to closing or alternate credit-decision of the application. This person is expected to maintain exceptional customer service and remain the point of contact with the customer during the duration of the loan. Customer satisfaction and quality standards are essential to this position.</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div></div>
<div><b>ESSENTIAL DUTIES/RESPONSIBILITIES:</b></div>
<div></div>
<ul>
<li>Managing the Bank’s CRA Mortgage Action Plan for residential mortgage lending; meet goals for assigned assessment areas, provides updates and feedback to ensure the goals are met with recommendations on how to improve where the goals fall short.</li>
<li>Advising Senior Management and Bank personnel of emerging CRA issues to ensure compliance directives are maintained</li>
<li>Maintaining, facilitating and managing the Bank’s CRA Homebuyers Education Program</li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<ul>
<li>Leading the preparation and serving as a point of contact for regulatory evaluation, exams and reviews related to CRA</li>
<li>Overseeing corrective action taken by management to address CRA issues, including assisting with implementation of corrective actions and conducting follow-up reviews to determine the adequacy and effectiveness of the action taken.</li>
<li>Developing and maintain effective working relationships and communications internally as well as with community groups and CRA related organizations throughout the corporate footprint</li>
<li>Developing and implementation of and internal CRA Training curriculum for bankers and senior management and Board of Directors.</li>
<li>Establishing, participating and maintaining relationships with internal and external stakeholders such as non-profit organizations, local advocacy organizations and businesses.</li>
<li>Performing other job duties or special projects as assigned/needed.</li>
<li>Provide leadership and operational oversight on assigned key duties (i.e. Settlement Agreement, Federal Home Loan Bank, Financial Literacy, etc.)</li>
<br>
</ul>
