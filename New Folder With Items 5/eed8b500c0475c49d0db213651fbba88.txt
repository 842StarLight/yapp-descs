<h2>Assoc Dir/Director, Marketing - Technology & Services</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div><div>
<p>Join us on our exciting journey! IQVIA™ is The Human Data Science Company™, focused on using data and science to help healthcare clients find better solutions for their patients. Formed through the merger of IMS Health and Quintiles, IQVIA offers a broad range of solutions that harness advances in healthcare information, technology, analytics and human ingenuity to drive healthcare forward.</p>
<p><b>
Role Summary:
</b></p>
<p>This position is responsible for the development and implementation of marketing plans that support the U.S. Technology Solutions business at IQVIA. This position will market technology solutions focused on CRM, incentive compensation and performance management within the Life Science industry. Specifically, this role will collaborate closely with subject matter experts to perform a situation analysis for key initiatives within the business. This includes understanding and documenting current market and competitive dynamics, defining the appropriate customer segmentation and key audiences, and understanding client business issues and needs. The candidate will use the analysis and other insights to develop and implement marketing plans that include objectives, strategies, value propositions, a persona messaging framework and a campaign calendar.</p>
<p><b>
Responsibilities:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Lead the marketing plan process including a situation analysis, competitive analysis, customer segmentation, audience analysis, key message development with differentiated positioning and value propositions</li>
<li>
Build and maintain a consistent persona messaging platform that clearly articulates the offerings’ market position, the key differentiators, value proposition, and how individual personas benefit across the Commercial Services solutions portfolio.</li>
<li>
Create and implement integrated marketing campaigns that translate the marketing objectives and strategies into successful tactics aimed at increasing awareness, providing lead generation and driving customer engagement as well as supporting the overall corporate repositioning strategy.</li>
<li>
Coordinate with other colleagues within the U.S. Marketing group to incorporate best practices and leverage capabilities to drive efficiencies in campaign development, execution and measurement.</li>
<li>
Responsible for scanning the market and leveraging new and innovative technologies that help to build impactful, integrated marketing campaigns using web, digital, email, search, social media, PR and traditional print advertising to improve client responsiveness and engagement. Develop a mechanism to regularly validate and maintain an accurate contact database.</li>
<li>
Create and execute successful content marketing and Thought Leadership strategies that support key initiatives within the business unit.</li>
<li>
Collaborate with subject matter experts to develop effective sales support materials including capabilities presentations, sell sheets, case studies, white papers and webinar content. Work with marketing support staff and outside agency partners to ensure the highest level of professionalism and adherence to brand guidelines in the creation of all collateral materials and conference graphics.</li>
<li>
Implement rigorous metrics around all campaign results to ensure strategies and tactics are well aligned and executed to maximize results and report to senior leaders monthly and/or quarterly. Course correct where necessary and make recommendations for improvements during the next planning cycle. Provide ideas and new methods that constantly strive to improve the marketing ROI process.</li>
<li>
Assist in developing and maintaining marketing expense budgets, taking control to ensure budgets stay on track, providing corrective action when and where necessary.</li>
<li>
Collaborate with other marketers to further the company repositioning efforts, build a positive company image and help create and maintain a motivated high-performance marketing team.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Qualifications and Experience</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Bachelor’s degree in marketing or communications and a minimum of 10 years experience in developing successful marketing plans driving positive business results</li>
<li>
Successful track record in developing innovative marketing campaigns and tactics to grow early stage businesses.</li>
<li>
Prior experience in healthcare marketing with an understanding of pharma commercial functions (commercial/sales operations, marketing brand management, managed markets) highly desirable.</li>
<li>
Strong digital marketing experience with the ability to track and report ROI</li>
<li>
Well developed written and verbal communication skills with the ability to understand and write about complex ideas and solutions.</li>
<li>
Ability to build solid cross-functional relationships and work with all levels within an organization.</li>
<li>
Self-starter with a “take charge” attitude and the ability to work with minimal direction</li>
<li>
Comfortable working in a fast-paced changing environment with a certain level of ambiguity.</li>
<li>
Strong organizational skills and the ability to organize and manage multiple priorities.</li>
<li>
Innovative thinker with a desire for continuous learning.</li>
</ul>
