<h2>Entry Level Staff Accountant</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Henry Industries Inc. (HII), an established provider of nationwide logistic services, distribution center, &amp; 3rd party warehousing, is seeking an <b>entry level Staff Accountant</b> to be located at its corporate office in Wichita, KS. This position offers an opportunity to work with a great team dedicated to employee growth and maximization of personal potential. HII also offers a comprehensive benefits package. If you are a qualified staff accountant, with a strong understanding of accounting principles, organizational skills and critical thinking and are looking to experience career growth, HII may be the place for you! Starting salary is between $36,000.00 and $40,000.00 depending on experience.</p>
<p>Our goal is to hire an individual who will contribute to keeping the accounting and financial processes of the company up to standard and ensure accurate financial reporting and control. This position will report to the Controller.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Duties and Responsibilities</b>:</p>
<ul>
<li>
<p>Understand proper balance sheet reconciliation techniques and assist with reconciliations both monthly and annually</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Process credit card payments on a daily basis, as needed</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Journal entry preparation and posting for daily, month end and annual entries and/or accruals</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Support Accounts Payable activities and review for accuracy</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Research P&amp;L variances within assigned markets</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Create and reproduce financial reports to support the markets and managers decision making</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Protect organizations value by maintaining information confidentiality</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Property tax return preparation and research</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Build documents for training and support of accounting processes, including new and/or revised Standard Operating Procedures as needed</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Research and correction of accounting errors as needed</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Additional responsibilities and duties as assigned by VP of Finance and/or Controller</p>
<br>
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Requirements:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
<p>Accounting/Finance degree required</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Some experience in the accounting field or internship is a plus, but not required</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Previous Accounts Payable experience a plus</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Previous data analysis, financial compilation and forecasting a plus</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Previous payroll, property tax and/or sales tax a plus</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Working knowledge of MS Office (especially Excel) and accounting software</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Well-organized with ability to prioritize</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Excellent communication and interpersonal skills</p>
</li>
<li>Attention to detail and problem-solving ability</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Benefits:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
<p>Competitive salary - $36,000 to $40,000 DOE</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Voluntary benefits Medical, Dental, Vision, and Life</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>401K with employer matching</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Career advancement opportunities</p>
<br>
</li>
</ul>
