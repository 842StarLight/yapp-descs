<p><b>What You Will Be Doing</b></p>
<p>
In this role you will be responsible for deriving requirements, documenting the design and data structures, and developing software in Java and C++. The ideal candidate must be familiar with the software development life-cycle and familiarity with Agile principles. Additionally, understanding and how to follow CMMI practices is a plus.</p>
<p>
</p>
<p><b>What You Need for this Position</b></p>
<p>
Minimum Education and/or Experience required:</p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s of Science Degree in Computer Science, Computer Engineering, or similar technical degree is required with a minimum of 6 years’ experience or a Master’s Degree and 4 years of experience, or at least 10 years of software development experience may be substituted in lieu of bachelor’s degree on a case-by-case basis.</li>
<li>Experience developing software with Java and C++ in object oriented</li>
<li>Working experience with Microsoft and LINUX operating environments</li>
</ul>
<p>Preferred, but not required:</p>
<ul>
<li>Current DoD Secret Security Clearance</li>
<li>Experience developing HTML5 Web Applications</li>
<li>Experience developing software under Agile principles</li>
<li>Experience with User Interfaces (UI) and Human/Machine Interface (HMI) concepts</li>
<li>Experience with Eclipse and Visual Studio is desired</li>
<li>Other preferred Experience with the following tools and application concepts is a plus: Maven, Junit, Easy Mock, JIRA, GET, Fisheye, Bitbucket, World Wind, SIMDIS, ESRI, GJIS, BOSS, NSITE, Wireshark, LUA, Bash, Perl, and VMWare.
</li>
</ul>
