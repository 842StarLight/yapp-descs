<h2>Business Analyst - CPQ</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">Purpose of Position
<br><br>
The CPQ Configuration Analyst is responsible for the design, implementation and support of digital sales tools with a very strong focus on Configure, Price and Quote (CPQ) throughout the enterprise. The ideal candidate will demonstrate strong technical aptitude, an understanding of business processes, a focus on user experience improvement, and a drive to improve sales effectiveness and efficiency through implementation and maintenance of Gilbarco’s CPQ system along with other associated sales tools. The CPQ Analyst will work to achieve streamlined processes, superior quality of service, best cost position , and outstanding reliability and availability.
<br><br>
Key Responsibilities
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Implement and maintain logic in configuration system per engineering design documents producing accurate custom bills of material</li>
<li>Enable correct price experience for customer by implementing business requirements</li>
<li>Design and develop clean, top-quality user interfaces for different products and services.</li>
<li>Resolve issues pertaining to work, product and projects within agreed upon service levels. This includes troubleshooting, resolution tracking and communication of resolution to the user community using the case process or other means as needed.</li>
<li>Deployment and maintenance of digital sales tools with a focus on configure, price, and quoting (CPQ) systems</li>
<li>Translate general design requirements into system specifications.</li>
<li>Adhere to software migration, documentation storage and retention policies as it relates to IT audit requirements.</li>
<li>Assist in developing, implementing, and maintaining policies, procedures, and supporting documentation.</li>
<li>Ensure work is completed to meet schedules and deadlines for implementation and upgrade projects.</li>
<li>Learn new applications/platforms to support the job function as needed. Actively works on related projects as assigned to broaden experiences and help the business accomplish its goals.</li>
<li>Support, maintain and develop interfaces between CPQ system and related business critical systems.</li>
<li>Work effectively with remote team members, outsourced resources, and users to complete tasks throughout development life cycle.</li>
</ul>
Background and Skill
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree from a four-year college or university preferred</li>
<li>At least 5 years Business Process or IT experience or other related experience</li>
<li>Software analyst and technical programming skills. Understands how software applications work and interface with each other.</li>
<li>Experience in Experlogix desirable but not essential.</li>
<li>Experience in Microsoft Access development desirable but not essential.</li>
<li>Analytical skills with the ability to translate business requirements into technical specifications.</li>
<li>Ability to learn quickly in a challenging environment and work as part of a team or independently to achieve objectives under pressure and tight deadlines</li>
<li>Exposure to CRM and ERP systems as used in a manufacturing company</li>
<li>Attention to detail, self-disciplined, excellent problem-solving and organization</li>
<li>Good oral and written communication skills are required.</li>
<li>Demonstrated competency in other common applications, such as Microsoft Word, Excel, PowerPoint, Project, Access, Visio and Outlook.</li>
<li>Understanding and experience with SQL and relational databases desirable but not essential</li>
</ul>
Personal Trait Profile
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Customer Focus: Success in facilitation with business leaders, process owners, technical resources, and project leaders.</li>
<li>Creativity / Innovation: Comes up with new and unique ideas. Makes connections between previously unrelated things.</li>
</ul>
