<h2 class="jobSectionHeader">
<b>New Relic</b><br>
<b>Manager, Solutions Consulting</b><br>
<b>New York, New York</b><br>
<br>
<b>Your Opportunity
</b>
</h2>
<p>Our Enterprise Solution Consulting organization champions our strategic direction and vision, as well as promoting and advocating new product direction on behalf of our customers. They are both a trusted advisor and solution consultant.</p>
<p>
We are now looking to grow our team by adding a trailblazer to direct and guide this incredible team. You will define &amp; improve the processes used by the team to continue our success through a period of exponential growth, mentoring your team to reach even greater heights.</p>
<p>
You have a proven record of building and developing technical teams and have worked extensively in the pre-sales technical space. You'll also have the right mix of business savvy, technical prowess and a hands-on leader.</p>
<p>
Opportunity to work from a remote office may be available depending on applicant location.</p>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
What You’ll Do</b></h2>
<ul>
<li>
Develop and mentor consultants to ensure that customers are blown away by the quality of our account teams.</li>
<li>
Actively engage and communicate with customers and New Relic Product Development teams regarding product feedback and competitive landscape.</li>
<li>
Improve the scalability &amp; efficiency of our technical integration and account management process.</li>
<li>
Solve tricky integration and product questions/issues and coordinate resolutions, including technical troubleshooting of customer issues.</li>
<li>
Partner with Sales and customer success to identify revenue prospects and opportunities to increase our high value service impact.</li>
</ul>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Your Qualifications</b></h2>
<ul>
<li>
10+ years of technical pre-sales experience with at least 5+ years experience of people management</li>
<li>
Strong background of working in a customer-facing environment and driving initiatives to improve customer satisfaction</li>
<li>
Excellent prioritization and organizational, and project management skills</li>
<li>
Experience in Application Performance Management (APM) concepts</li>
<li>
Development experience in one or more of the following languages – Java, .Net, Ruby, C/C++, Python, Node.js, or PHP</li>
<li>
Experience with Public Cloud Providers, such as AWS, Azure, Google</li>
<li>
Clear understanding of network &amp; system management solutions</li>
<li>
Firm understanding of OO concepts</li>
<li>
Excellent communication, critical thinking &amp; analytical skills</li>
<li>
Bachelors degree in Engineering/Computer Science or equivalent</li>
</ul>
<p>
Please note, visa sponsorship is not available for this position.</p>
