<p>The Collector is responsible for the collection and clearing of past due balances on customer accounts. Each Collector is assigned a group of customers to monitor; the team collectively brings in $20 million each week in cash receipts. Incumbent must find creative means to effect resolution of problem accounts. Using one’s own personal initiative, collection and negotiating skills, the collectors secure payment or otherwise settle open balances, and strive to achieve delinquency goals set by the department manager.</p>
<p><b>Accountabilities
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Identifies collection targets by creating own “hit lists,” prioritizing which accounts to contact based upon account balance, aging and Manager input to a weekly basis, this process includes the utilization of various accounts receivable aging reports available on-demand for each Collector.
</li>
<li>Duns late paying accounts by compiling and sending dunning letters and statements to the customer via emails or postal service</li>
<li>Contacts customers to secure payment on open items; escalates collection contact within the customer’s organization until payment or resolution is achieved</li>
<li>Negotiates settlement of disputed account balances</li>
<li>Reviews customer cash flow situation and formulates repayment plans</li>
<li>Contacts loading and unloading warehouses, truckers and other agents to obtain customer paperwork to prompt payment of open invoices and assist with obtaining proper paper work for payment.</li>
<li>Coordinates credit &amp; collection activity with Matson’s sales, customer service, pricing and operations department</li>
<li>Recommends credit deadlines, after conferring with Sales and upper management, to customers who do not resolve past due balances.</li>
<li>Process credit reports by running the D&amp;B reports, checking reference, analyzing results and recommending lines of credit or declining.</li>
<li>Process bankruptcies, filing with the court, completing claim forms and coordinating with legal if necessary.</li>
<li>Review one time aging list and coordinate with Customer Service to ensure accurate customer aging.
</li>
<li>Work with the third-party collection agency to file claims, follow up to monitor collection progress and provide Manager with updates and recommendations in regards to settlement offers.</li>
<li>Provide weekly statements using data retrieved from Oracle AR and other Matson sytems to customers as needed.</li>
<li>Approve refund of customer’s credit.</li>
<li>Work with Data Admin to ensure the Customer Profile is created on new accounts requesting credit.</li>
<li>Work closely with Sales and develop strategies to minimize or eliminate past due balances of customers.
</li>
<li>Provide weekly updates and recaps to management as needed</li>
</ul>
