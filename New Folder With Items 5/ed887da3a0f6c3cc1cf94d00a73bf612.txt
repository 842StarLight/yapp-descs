<p><b>Meritech is currently seeking a Staff Accountant to support our Finance Department </b></p>
<p><b>Empowering businesses through technology</b></p>
<p>Founded in 1978, Meritech is the business community’s elite managed services and office technology partner of choice, delivering an exceptional customer experience backed by superior organizational talent. As an employer of choice, we employ the best and the brightest people, providing them with opportunity and direction, while holding them accountable to our core values of integrity, honesty, and accountability.</p>
<p>At Meritech, you'll work for an established and growing technology company where there is room for advancement, great benefits, passionate employees, and a small family feel.</p>
<p><b>OVERVIEW: </b></p>
<p>We are looking to hire a Staff Accountant with an analytical mind and fantastic organizational skills. Staff Accountants are expected to be detail oriented, have strong problem-solving skills, work comfortably under pressure and deliver on tight deadlines.</p>
<p>To ensure success, Staff Accountants should be critical thinkers, proficient in math and accurate with a solid understanding of account and financial reporting principles and practices. Top candidates will have outstanding report writing skills with the ability to work on their own initiative and as part of a team.</p>
<p><b>ESSENTIAL FUNCTIONS &amp; RESPONSIBILITIES</b></p>
<p>· Analyze financial information and prepare balance sheets.</p>
<p>· Coordinate with management and staff to prepare budgets.</p>
<p>· Ensure compliance with organizational guidelines and procedures, and federal and state regulations.</p>
<p>· Monitor Vendor Statements and other transactions.</p>
<p>· Resolve account payable and receivable issues or queries.</p>
<p>· Accurately perform daily reconciliations of cash, check and credit card transactions, and tally and file invoices.</p>
<p>· Comply with Generally Accepted Accounting Principles (GAAP) for financial statements.</p>
<p>· Assist in financial and tax audits, and <i>general ledger preparation<b>.</b></i></p>
<p>· Prepare income tax returns and corporate reporting requirements.</p>
<p>· Develop monthly financial statements that include cash flow, profit and loss statements, and balance sheets.</p>
<p>· <i>Complete end of month close procedures.</i></p>
