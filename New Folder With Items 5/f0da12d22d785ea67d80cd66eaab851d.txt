<h2>Account Executive</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>The PRO EM Family of Companies<b> </b>is in search of an experienced full-time, Account Executive in Chicago,IL. Candidates must be professionals who are friendly, outgoing, ambitious, and self-motivated. This is a fantastic opportunity for an enthusiastic, high-energy, team player who is detail oriented, loves to sell, and is driven to be the best while exceeding our clients' expectations every step of the way! The Account Executive is a sales position responsible for Developing, Qualifying and Closing new business.</p>
<p><b>
Job Responsibilities</b><b>:</b></p>
<ul><li>Identify business opportunities by identifying prospects and evaluating their position in the industry; research and analyze sales opportunities.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Establish new accounts by conducting cold calls, approaching prospective customers through face-to-face interaction; respond to qualified referrals, and follow-up to build/expand the sales territory.</li></ul>
<ul><li>High level of customer service standards.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Establish rapport with potential customers through effective communication and product knowledge to generate opportunities for revenue growth.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Contact clients to ensure ongoing satisfaction, increase sales through cross-selling, up-selling and add-on sales.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Highlight product features and benefits, quote prices, discuss payment terms, and prepare sales order forms.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Meet personal and company goals while working efficiently with other members of the organization.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Keeps management informed by submitting activity reports, weekly plan of action, and monthly territory analysis.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Remain current on product knowledge and consumer preferences.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Monitors completion by gathering information on pricing, product, and new products.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Resolve issues with customers that result in positive feedback and improves business relationships.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Maintain quality of service by following organizational standards.</li></ul>
<p><b>
Job Requirements:</b></p>
<ul><li>Minimum of three years of Sales (or relevant) experience, preferably in the Event Rental Industry, DMC Industry, Catering Sales industry, Event Management/Production Industry, Hotel/Resort Group Industry, Venue Space Sales or Management Industry, or Trade Show Sales Industry. PRO EM will consider outside sales experience from other industries.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Experienced Customer Service professional.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Business Development experience.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Ability to research and qualify new opportunities</li></ul>
<ul><li>Astute Closing skills.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Strategic territory planning and management.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Highest standards of ethics and integrity.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Negotiation skills.</li></ul>
<ul><li>High level of self-confidence.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Product knowledge.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Detail oriented.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Presentation skills.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Highly energetic with the motivation to sell.</li></ul>
<ul><li>High level of persuasiveness.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Can work independently in an Outside Sales setting.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Demonstrated history of outstanding professional sales achievements that aligned with corporate goals.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Excellent verbal and written communication skills.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Proficiency in using personal computer and inventory control software.</li></ul>
