<h2>Hotel General Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Responsible for the overall management of the property to include but not limited to: profitability, guest service, associate satisfaction, product quality, risk management, and overall cleanliness and maintenance of the hotel property.</p>
<p><b>
Experience:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Minimum of 3 years’ experience as a General Manager in similar type &amp; size hotel</li>
<li>
Proven track record of increasing profit (GOP) while exceeding Brand &amp; Company goals for guest and associate satisfaction</li>
<li>
Local market experience preferred</li>
<li>
IHG experience preferred</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Hotel Profitability:</b></p>
<p><i>
Ensures the attainment of established budgeted goals for all departments of Hotel</i></p>
<ul>
<li>
Monitors compliance with labor standards and staffing guidelines by all departments</li>
<li>
Monitors compliance with annually established room rate plan</li>
<li>
Monitors operating expense tracking system for all departments</li>
<li>
Administers approved incentive programs</li>
<li>
Conducts required meetings (ex. Daily Huddle, weekly staff) to ensure interdepartmental communication and coordination of mutual goals</li>
<li>
Audits departmental procedures and performance. Modifies procedures as needed.</li>
<li>
Monitors rooms inventory and merchandising procedures.</li>
<li>
Monitors and ensures compliance with amenity programs, franchise and company standards, as well as promotional materials</li>
<li>
Assures compliance with established Manager on Duty (M.O.D.) Program</li>
<li>
Participates in scheduled meetings as required by corporate. This includes revenue management, safety, budget, sales &amp; regional operations meetings</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Asset Management:</b></p>
<p><i>
Exceeds Brand quality standards ensuring a favorable franchise inspection grade for the property</i></p>
<ul>
<li>
Submits annual Capital and Repair &amp; Maintenance (R&amp;M) budgets for approval by Corporate Office</li>
<li>
Ensures completion of all approved Capital and R&amp;M items, coordinating with Regional Area Director of Operations</li>
<li>
Conducts daily inspections of guest rooms to ensure housekeeping is meeting/exceeding cleanliness standards</li>
<li>
Conducts bi-weekly property inspections and approves action plans to include time-table to resolve problems</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Guest Satisfaction/Public Relations:</b></p>
<p><i>
Promotes guest satisfaction to obtain repeat business of leisure and business markets</i></p>
<ul>
<li>
Evaluates all guest complaints and ensures corrective action is taken by department heads</li>
<li>
Monitors guest responses, responding as needed; executes action plan to correct issues</li>
<li>
Hosts social events/receptions as required by the Brand</li>
<li>
Plays active role in community through affiliation with various community organizations to promote and maintain positive image for hotel</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Human Resources:</b></p>
<p><i>
Ensures associates are provided with the necessary structure, motivation and training to satisfy their needs and achieve organizational goals</i></p>
<ul>
<li>
Interviews, selects, trains, and counsels’ associates</li>
<li>
Ensures that all new hires are given the proper onboarding experience</li>
<li>
Ensures that staff is properly trained by their managers</li>
<li>
Ensures adequate staffing levels are maintained, adhering to budgetary and staffing guidelines</li>
<li>
Ensures compliance of all department heads with goal-oriented job descriptions and reviews goals with everyone</li>
<li>
Maintains associate files, ensuring all documents are current (including job descriptions and version of handbook)</li>
<li>
Ensures that performance evaluations are being issued as required by corporate</li>
<li>
Encourages promotion from within, associate engagement and development through goal setting, associate training and participating in all Commonwealth mandated associate-focused initiatives</li>
<li>
Ensures hotel is following all Commonwealth policies, Federal and State laws with regards to all personnel practices, safety initiatives, &amp; labor-related postings</li>
<li>
Payroll processing and reconciliation</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
The ideal candidate for this position will have the following characteristics:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Proven records of commitment and professionalism in meeting the challenges and pressures of a 24-hour, 7-day week operation.</li>
<li>
Proficiency with MS Word, Excel, PowerPoint and ten-key calculator</li>
<li>
Excellent organizational and time management skills, ability to take initiative and handle multiple projects simultaneously.</li>
<li>
Ability to multi-task</li>
<li>
Ability to communicate effectively, both written and oral</li>
<li>
Bilingual (Spanish &amp; English) preferred, depending on market</li>
<li>
Ability to work the shifts required for the position</li>
<li>
Ability to learn and adhere to Brand &amp; Commonwealth Hotels' standards</li>
<li>
Ability to take information from various sources and determine a responsible course of action</li>
<li>
Ability to understand interdepartmental relationships</li>
<li>
Ability to operate office equipment and industry specific software</li>
<li>
Ability to remain calm during stressful situations</li>
<li>
Ability to motivate and lead a team</li>
<li>
Ability to read, interpret and analyze financial reports, P&amp;L Statements, Sales &amp; Marketing reports</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Skills/Qualifications</b></p>
<p><b>
Education:</b></p>
<ul><li>
Bachelor's degree in Hospitality or related field preferred; however, skills/knowledge gained through on-the-job training and previous experience may substitute for degree</li></ul>
<p><b>
Certifications / Licenses</b>:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Must possess and maintain a motor vehicle operator's license in good standing</li>
<li>
Brand General Manager certification preferred</li>
</ul>
