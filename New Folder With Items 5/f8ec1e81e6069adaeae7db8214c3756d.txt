<h2>Director of Business Development</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>The Director of Business Development role requires a combination of consultative selling and solution architecting. This person must demonstrate all the behaviors associated with a high-performance sales culture by creating high quality sales opportunities with decision makers and delivering consistent results against a quota. In this role, you will be responsible for establishing sales targets that meet and exceed the organization's objectives. You will report directly to the CEO and assist with building a high-caliber sales organization while securing relationships with mid-market to enterprise level organizations. You will join us as a player, and upon being successful in your role, shift towards a player-coach, and eventually build a leading sales organization as a coach.</p>
<p>This role is full-time in our Costa Mesa, CA office.
</p>
<p><b>Requirements</b></p>
<p><b>
The Ideal Candidate will have experience with:</b><br>
</p>
<ul>
<li>Experience architecting and successfully selling solutions to ecommerce and retail advertisers spending over $100k monthly on Facebook, Google, Display, Native, and other similar media channels</li>
<li>At least 5 years of relevant experience (advertising technology and/or agency experience, and Facebook + Google Platform familiarity are a must)</li>
<li>Ability to quickly build relationships with C-level executives</li>
<li>Proven success in closing B2B enterprise deals over $1M in a timely manner</li>
<li>Ability to listen to a client and build a solution that fits their business needs
</li>
<li>Consultative sales style</li>
<li>Experience building and defining a sales department to develop repeatable processes</li>
<li>Provide detailed and accurate sales forecasting</li>
<li>You are excited about developing, building and driving a sales department</li>
<li>Ability to generate leads and opportunities, with a “no matter what it takes” attitude</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>Some Key Responsibilities:</b><br>
</p>
<ul>
<li>Generate new business and assist in developing new techniques to creatively reach decision makers at target accounts</li>
<li>Participate in sales calls to ask questions and determine qualified opportunities</li>
<li>Demonstrate the value of adQuadrant’s value proposition</li>
<li>Continually seek and take advantage of opportunities to network with clients at industry events</li>
<li>Provides customer feedback to our top level leadership</li>
<li>Leads in process development and improvement. You are a catalyst for change.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>Desired Qualifications:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>5+ years sales or related market/business experience
</li>
<li>Candidates should possess the following: advertising technology and/or agency experience, and Facebook + Google (at a minimum) Platform familiarity</li>
<li>The ability to write succinct, crisp emails and a great phone manner
</li>
<li>Knowledge of the following industry sectors: omni-channel marketing, content marketing, e-commerce, digital and mobile marketing/advertising</li>
<li>Proven experience working within a small company environment</li>
<li>Sharp eye for detail and organizational skills</li>
<li>Comfort with ambiguity in a fast-paced, always-on, start-up environment</li>
<li>Critical thinking and problem solving capabilities</li>
</ul>
