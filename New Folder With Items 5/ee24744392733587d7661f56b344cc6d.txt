<p><b>Position Title: </b> Design Engineer</p>
<p><b>Location: </b> Greer, SC</p>
<p><b>Reports to: </b>Director – Design and Engineering</p>
<p>This position reports to the Director of Design and Engineering and has two key areas of responsibility: 1) Work with Sales and Engineering to drive the design and engineering of injection molded components that meet both the internal and external customer expectations. 2)Research, evaluate and design mechanical products utilizing knowledge of manufacturing engineering principles. Work environment includes complex injection molding tooling and automation for Medical Device and Consumer Products. Design Engineer will adhere to Design Specifications and procedures.</p>
<p><b>Specific Responsibilities</b></p>
<p>1. Perform in all aspects of the business including design engineering, 3D modeling, digital surfacing, drawing creation, reverse engineering, component selection. Drives achievement of agreed-to deliverables to internal and external customers by working closely with cross functional manufacturing and project teams to execute projects for molding and assembly per customer and Company requirements and specifications.</p>
<p>2. Supports New Business Development, Account Managers and Program Managers in technical subject matter execution and creation of designed for manufacturing injection molded components that meet functional and cost objectives. Requires a functional understanding of – and adherence to - program management guidelines, processes and systems</p>
<p>3. Interact with clients and is expected to perform in a professional manner and present a professional image at all times</p>
<p>4. Supports other technical activities or projects within the facility, as requested by the General Manager</p>
<p>5. Establishes and maintains effective work relationships within the Company and outside parties.</p>
<p><b>Key Qualifications: </b></p>
<ul><li>Bachelor’s Degree in engineering or related field.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Five years’ experience in design of plastics injection molded parts, with skills to develop unique and novel design solutions.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Seven years’ experience in injection molding, automation, and project management and processes.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Minimum of 5 years in a comparable position</li></ul>
<ul><li>Extensive knowledge of injection molded part design</li></ul>
<ul><li>Solid understanding of processing of plastic materials</li></ul>
<ul><li>Proficient with 3D CAD and Moldflow</li></ul>
<ul><li>Proficiency in Program management tools required, such as Gannt charts, timelines, change control and budgets,</li></ul>
<ul><li>Great organizational and planning capabilities with exceptional problem-prevention/solving skills.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Must be detail oriented, able to meet deadlines on complex projects and work effectively in a fast-paced environment.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Exceptional teamwork and communication skills, including the ability to get things done and meet commitments by working with and through others; requires being able to establish effective work relationships at all levels, internally and with customers/suppliers</li></ul>
<ul><li>Black belt or lean experience preferred.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Proficiency in use of medium to large scale Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP) and Product Data Management (PDM), MS Office.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Maintains the professional competence, knowledge and skill necessary for the satisfactory performance of all assigned responsibilities</li></ul>
