<h2>Senior Technology Architect</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>Job Description<br>
<br>
<p>A Senior Technology Architect at CapTech are called upon to perform various responsibilities within CapTech’s Systems Integration practice area, usually in the context of a technology assessment, strategy formation, or a development project performed for a particular client. They often lead a team of developers and collaborate with experienced project managers, analysts, designers, and clients to architect and deliver vital internal and customer facing systems in partnership with a variety of Fortune 500 clients. Typically, Senior Technology Architects have a broad understanding of many tools, technologies, and products, with some specialization in a few areas. Over time these specialties may change due to industry transformation or a changing client profile, and as such, our Senior Technology Architects lead by example and still get hands-on experience with the latest technologies while working with passionate CapTechers solving complex problems across numerous industries.</p>
<p>
Specific responsibilities for the Senior Technology Architect position include:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Position yourself as a trusted advisor to CapTech clients.</li>
<li>
Oversee the technical decisions that must be made, work with client staff charged with architecture standards, and ensure quality of final results.</li>
<li>
Perform productively at many levels, from the abstract world of enterprise architecture to the concrete day-to-day world of coding.</li>
<li>
Design, implement, integrate and support Java platform solutions.</li>
<li>
Create documents that specify strategy, assessment, or design in a way that delivers obvious value to the client and is understandable by any stakeholder.</li>
<li>
Articulate architectural differences between solution methods and the challenges and approaches to integrating solutions built on different platforms including a working knowledge of different architectural frameworks that may be used by our clients.</li>
<li>
Define the technical and solution architecture for a phased software development delivery and provide hands-on technical direction working with other developers.</li>
<li>
Apply detailed knowledge of organizational procedures to make independent decisions and serve as a credible resource for the client’s senior management team.</li>
<li>
Collaborate with CapTech business development team members during sales efforts to demonstrate CapTech’s technical capabilities, create a vision for a solution that CapTech can build, and create estimates and work plans.</li>
<li>
Develop less-senior resources (both CapTech and client) by teaching, empowering, reviewing results, both in technical areas and in consulting “soft” skills.</li>
<li>
Motivates teams to deliver high-quality work, add-value to client and collaborate across business units</li>
<li>
Shares project outcomes with other CapTechers to improve delivery for similar projects</li>
<li>
Aligns work to Account strategy and/or roadmap</li>
<li>
Proactively establishes new relationships with senior-level decision-makers, influencers, and connectors</li>
<li>
Participates and supports client culture</li>
<li>
Empowers team leads and supports their decisions; encourages dialogue within and across project teams</li>
<li>
Recognizes members for efforts and accomplishments; shares success with entire team</li>
<li>
Coaches team leads to effectively address and resolve conflicts and develops future leaders</li>
<li>
Creates stretch opportunities and inspires confidence in others’ abilities</li>
<li>
Identifies and closes technical skill gaps within or across teams</li>
<li>
Aligns self-development with organizational technology strategy</li>
<li>
Helps drive innovation/ Technology thought leadership by identifying emerging trends and solutions that fuel growth for clients and CapTech</li>
</ul>
</div>
<br>
<div>Qualifications<br>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>
Bachelor's Degree in Computer Science, Information Technology, or equivalent degree and/or experience</li>
<li>
Ability to think strategically and relate architectural decisions/recommendations to business needs and client culture.</li>
<li>
Extensive knowledge of design patterns and when to apply them.</li>
<li>
Extensive knowledge of the Java language, its core libraries, and many 3rd party or open-source frameworks often used in the industries of CapTech’s clients.</li>
<li>
Strong ability to apply rigor and discipline to the design/development process regardless of the tools/technologies used.</li>
<li>
Ability to discuss/debate architectural issues without rancor or personal investment.</li>
<li>
Ability to seamlessly modify communication based on the technical level of the target audience.</li>
<li>
Knowledge of how to assess the performance of software systems, how to diagnose performance problems, and tools used to monitor and tune performance.</li>
<li>
Extensive knowledge of the concepts and tools required for effective software development including: Integrated Development Environments, Continuous Integration, Operating Systems (specifically UNIX/Linux), Test-driven Development, SDLC Methodologies including Waterfall, Scrum, XP, Kanban, and SAFe.</li>
<li>
Exposure to Cloud and DevOps Technologies</li>
<li>
Ability to build, coach and mentor development teams</li>
</ul>
