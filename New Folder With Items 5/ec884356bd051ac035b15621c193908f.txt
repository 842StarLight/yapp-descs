<b>Job Description</b><br>
<p>As a successful candidates your responsibilities may include:</p>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>
Set priorities for the team</li>
<li>
Get results across boundaries</li>
<li>
Ensure an inclusive work environment</li>
<li>
Develop employees</li>
<li>
Manage performance.
</li>
<li>Manages a team of marketing communication specialists.
</li>
<li>Design, planning, development and implementation of marketing communication programs to support the Corporate or product marketing strategies and branding programs.
</li>
<li>Accountable for achieving high return/impact on the marketing spend.
</li>
<li>Development of promotional material, collaterals, POP merchandising, ad campaigns, trade shows, development of advertising and promotional tools.
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p>A successful candidate will demonstrate:</p>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>
Ability to think strategically and ensure relevancy by "connecting-the-dots" and effectively mapping Intel's business objectives to conversations in the marketplace.</li>
<li>
Ability to develop strong relationships with industry luminaries, technology and business reporters, bloggers and new influencers who cover and closely follow the enterprise and cloud markets.</li>
<li>
Be well-versed on the current traditional and social media environment as it relates to Intel's business as well as the broader industry.</li>
<li>
Customer and team oriented. Ability to build strong relationships and successfully partner with peers across Intel and partner companies.</li>
<li>
Solid written and verbal communication skills and can craft and deliver key messages to a variety of audiences. Is adept at translating technical topics into easy-to-understand and relevant language.</li>
<li>
Able to identify and leverage opportunities - both long term and quick twitch - to advance the company's communications objectives in creative, memorable ways that utilizes a variety of media vehicles social media, traditional media, broadcast, etc.</li>
<li>
Results driven. Thrives on hard work and consistent challenge. Proven ability to be hands-on and strategic able to execute plans as well as help develop strategy and work across multiple product lines.</li>
</ul>

<b>
Inside this Business Group</b><br>
<p>Intel's Sales and Marketing (SMG) organization works with global customers and partners to solve critical business problems with Intel based technology solutions. SMG works across business units to amplify the customers voice and deliver solutions that accelerate their business. Our teams work across the entire sales cycle, pushing ingredient products to our "billings" customers while also pulling end solutions through to "consumption". We work across numerous industries, including retail, enterprise and government, cloud services and healthcare as examples. The operations team focuses on forecasting, driving alignment with factory production and delivering efficiency tools and our marketing capability drives demand and localized marketing in locations around the globe. Our sales force navigates a complex partner and customer ecosystem while shaping product roadmaps, driving value for our customers, and collaborating to harness emerging technology trends to deliver comprehensive solutions.</p>
