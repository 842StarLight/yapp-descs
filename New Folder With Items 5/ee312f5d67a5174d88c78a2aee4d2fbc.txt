<h2>Associate Director, Business Insights & Analytics</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>General Scope and Summary</b></p>
<p>
SAGE Therapeutics is searching for a creative, resourceful, integrative thinker for an important role that is responsible for leveraging his or her market knowledge and modeling expertise to generate in-depth, customer-focused analytical insights, articulate actionable recommendations, and drive critical business decisions. Working closely with the Commercial Organization, Global Commercial Strategy team, Marketing, and Finance, this person will be responsible for leading the development, analysis, and effective communication of forecast and brand level insights for current and future products at SAGE. This individual will help identify key drivers and performance metrics to achieve optimal returns and resource allocation. This position will also have regular interaction with Commercial and Senior Leaders and require an ability to present complicated analysis to individuals with a wide variety of backgrounds.</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Roles and Responsibilities</b></p>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Business Analysis &amp; Forecasting</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Develop, analyze, and effectively communicate all forecasts (including innovative forecast models) and assumptions for all US in-line market products and pre-launch assets</li>
<li>
Partner with Global Commercial Strategy and lead the development of the US long range forecast plan</li>
<li>
Partner with stakeholders (including the Commercial Organization Leadership Team) to align on assumptions and impact of current and future market events</li>
<li>
Partner with head of US Marketing Analytics in utilizing output from primary and secondary market research to identify and assess high business impact events. Recommendation should be influenced from outputs from impact of current and future market events, Promotional Response, Sales Force Effectiveness, etc.</li>
<li>
Lead the commercial insights team in engaging with Finance for their financial planning process and Supply Chain for their S&amp;OP process. Work with Investor Relations Group in messaging business performance</li>
<li>
Build and assess analogs where relevant to inform forecast assumptions and to help generate business insights</li>
<li>
Lead the evaluation and integration of available data sources such as patient and market audit data, competitive intelligence, sales analytics, and managed care data</li>
<li>
Initially lead indirect reports (e.g. Temps/consultants) and over time lead direct reports.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Intelligence and Insights</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Perform “Deep-Dives” into market related events, competitor entrants, brand opportunities and performance. Use the output of these analyses to influence brand specific performance, tactics, and strategies</li>
<li>
Build and maintain a database of all insights and performance data for the brand(s), including market and competitive trends, health care environment influences and investment performance against various channels</li>
<li>
Partner with cross functional teams and act as the insights lead for providing strategic insights, forming a trusted relationship, and staying abreast of commercial issues (this will include leading weekly/monthly meetings)</li>
<li>
Ensure key information from other functions (like HEOR, Medical, etc.) are integrated into analyses and insights</li>
<li>
Collaborate with the Sales Analytics team to track and analyze Key Performance Indicators (KPIs) and other brand and company specific goals</li>
<li>
Provide ad hoc and recurring data analysis and reports to the commercial organization and senior management</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Experience, Education and Specialized Knowledge and Skills</b></p>
<p>
Must thrive working in a fast-paced, innovative environment while remaining flexible, proactive, resourceful and efficient. Excellent interpersonal skills, ability to develop important relationships with key stakeholders, good conflict management and negotiation skills, ability to analyze complex issues to develop relevant and realistic plans, programs and recommendations. Demonstrated ability to translate strategy into action; excellent analytical skills and an ability to communicate complex issues in a simple way and to orchestrate plans to resolve issues and mitigate risks.</p>
<ul>
<li>
BA or BS required, MBA preferred</li>
<li>
8+ years of appropriate business experience in marketing sciences or business analytics function in a pharmaceutical/biotech company or consulting firm</li>
<li>
5+ Years experience in forecasting with MBA, or 7+ years experience with BA or BS</li>
<li>
Experience working with all levels of management and consulting with key business stakeholders. Proven track record of driving broad organizational change and an ability to influence for greater outcomes</li>
<li>
Experience managing people required</li>
<li>
Ability to communicate clearly with a wide range of constituents both internally and externally at SAGE</li>
<li>
Demonstrated ability to lead multi-disciplinary teams and initiatives</li>
<li>
Applied experience in building and maintaining robust forecasting models and thorough understanding of various time-series and patient-based forecasting techniques</li>
<li>
Proficiency in Monte Carlo decision models and application based statistical techniques</li>
<li>
Experience with forecast and data analytic software like Flexicast, Forecast Pro, Crystal Ball, Tableau a plus</li>
<li>
Fluency in Excel, Access and in PowerPoint is required</li>
<li>
Deep expertise in generating commercial insights from large transactional data sets and possess a strong understanding of industry secondary data and syndicated sources and their strength and weaknesses</li>
<li>
Experience in the process of managing outsourced projects (RFP, contract project management, etc.)</li>
<li>
Strong team player that has a customer service approach and is solution oriented.</li>
<li>
Attention to detail and the ability to work individually, within a multi-disciplinary team, as well as with external partners and vendors</li>
<li>
Possess strong written and verbal communication skills, problem solving/resolution, and customer focus.</li>
<li>
Embrace our core values: Put People First, Do Big, Be Accountable, Grow through Learning and Change, and Work Fun</li>
<li>
Excitement about the vision and mission of Sage and thrives in a fast-paced, innovative environment while remaining flexible, proactive, resourceful and efficient</li>
</ul>
