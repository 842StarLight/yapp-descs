<h2>Account Executive - Southeast Region</h2>

What You'll Do
<br>--------------
<br><br>
You​ ​will​ ​use​ ​your​ ​extensive​ ​experience​ ​and​ ​consultative​ ​software selling​ ​skills​ ​to​ ​develop​ ​new​ ​sales​ ​opportunities ​by​ ​prospecting,​ ​forecasting​ ​pipeline,​ ​territory​ ​planning​, ​and​ ​software presentations.​ ​You​ ​will​ ​be​ ​managing​ ​the​ ​sales​ ​process​ ​including​ ​contract​ ​preparation​ ​and​ ​work​ ​effectively as​ ​an​ ​individual,​ ​while​ ​also​ ​providing​ ​team-on-team​ ​leadership​ ​and​ ​orchestrating​ ​the​ ​resources​ ​needed​ ​to effectively​ ​manage​ ​and​ ​win​ ​sales. This person should possess outstanding sales experience, honed customer service skill and a passion for technology, new systems, and innovative business solutions in a variety of settings and inceptions. We value executives who are passionate about growing our existing accounts while proactively pursuing new accounts and identifying roadblocks and obstacles to increasing business. The ideal fit will be innovative enough to suggest tailored solutions to common account problems. Dedication to growing the business and facilitating smoother connections between our clients and our internal executives is a must. IT Service Management knowledge a definite plus.
<br><br>
