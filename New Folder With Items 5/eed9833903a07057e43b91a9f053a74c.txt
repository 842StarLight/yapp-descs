<h2>Associate Marketing Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p><b>THE CHAMPION'S CHOICE</b><br>
Florida State University Credit Union, the Champion's Choice, is seeking an ambitious team player who will be a champion for our members and play a leading role in maximizing their financial well-being. Are you looking for a fulfilling career with a forward thinking, collaborative team where you are not only leading the charge but being coached to reach your full potential? Ready to join our winning team? Apply online today!</p>
<p><b>General Summary:</b></p>
<p>Under supervision of the Marketing Director, this position will be responsible for supporting the marketing and communication activities of Florida State University Credit Union, including advertising, public relations, promotional campaigns, events, market research, and other marketing initiatives.</p>
</div>
<br>
<div>
<p><b>Essential Functions:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Assist in the production of targeted marketing promotions designed to meet strategic Credit Union goals and initiatives.</li>
<li>Assist in the management and content development for diverse communications, including member newsletter, website, email, and social media.</li>
<li>Assist with the planning, execution and evaluation of credit union special events; be present at events as needed.</li>
<li>Assist in the production and distribution of advertising, marketing brochures, sales tools, and other promotional materials.</li>
<li>Assists branch offices with displays, promotions and marketing materials, clearly communicating the purpose and details of promotions to ensure brand consistency and the timely update of materials.</li>
<li>Actively seek out sales and cross-sell opportunities and promote credit union products and services based on member needs.</li>
<li>Assists in maintaining and updating websites to ensure that content is accurate and up-to-date.</li>
<li>Manage donation and sponsorship requests.</li>
<li>Assists with the coordination of activities for annual meeting, special meetings, and seminars relating to credit union business.</li>
<li>Promote the service and sales culture by maintaining basic knowledge of products and services and referring members to the appropriate person/department.</li>
<li>Conduct research and complete special projects as assigned by the Marketing Directo</li>
</ul>
<p>
Note: The list of essential functions is not exhaustive. It may be supplemented, as necessary, from time to time.</p>
<p><b>
Job Specifications:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Work requires extensive knowledge of Marketing, Communications and Business concepts. These characteristics are normally acquired through completion of a bachelor’s degree in Marketing, Communications or Business and some relevant work experience. Two to three years of relevant work experience can substitute for a bachelors’ degree.</li>
<li>Skills in Microsoft Word, Excel, Outlook and Adobe Creative Suite preferred</li>
<li>Willingness to work nights and weekends as needed.</li>
<li>Outgoing personality with ability and willingness to offer new ideas and work with all levels of the organization, including members.</li>
<li>Desire to pursue and attend opportunities for professional development.</li>
<li>Familiarity with social media channels and an understanding of how these channels are used by businesses.</li>
<li>Strong communication skills.</li>
<li>Ability to work independently and meet deadlines.</li>
<li>This job operates in a clerical office setting. This role routinely uses standard office equipment such as computers, phones, photocopiers, scanners, printers, filing cabinets and fax machines.</li>
<li>This position requires manual dexterity, the ability to lift files and open filing cabinets. Should be able to lift full boxes of marketing materials and other materials weighing 40lbs or less. This position requires walking, sitting, bending, stooping or standing as necessary.</li>
</ul>
