<h2>Dell Technologies National Security Group Business Development Professional</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Dell Technologies National Security Group Business Development Professional (Commercial Sales)</b></p>
<p></p>
<p>
Dell EMC delivers the most advanced enterprise infrastructure for mission-critical environments, and is the only true provider of the end-to-end IT capabilities needed to drive agency missions through digital transformation – from the edge to the core to the cloud. Under the banner of Dell Technologies: Dell, Dell EMC, Pivotal, RSA, SecureWorks, Virtustream, and VMware align to support our customers to meet their missions and transform IT, build their digital future, and protect their most important asset: information. With an intense mission focus, Dell Federal is the customers’ reliable solution ensuring the product received is the product expected, and the delivered system, service, or capability operates as intended anywhere in the world…all the time.</p>
<p></p>
<p>
The National Security Group (NSG) team is looking for an exceptional individual to develop business opportunities within the intelligence community (IC) and DoD, and to support their missions with leading-edge IT solutions and services. This position, also known as the “Business Development CIO”, has the following core responsibilities:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Developing external relationships, partnerships, and business opportunities amongst the customer base to include the intelligence community (IC), the warfighter, and the associated senior executive leadership; earning their trust by demonstrating the highest caliber of professional and mission competence.</li>
<li>
Developing internal relationships and building trust amongst the account executives, program/project managers, executive leadership team members, infrastructure delivery teams, and security offices.</li>
<li>
Possessing a strong, in-depth understanding and relationship with the IC/DoD and other governmental/nongovernmental agencies; appropriately leveraging these resources and connections to support the customer and Dell Technologies in advancing the mission and growing the business.</li>
<li>
Assisting government agencies in understanding the inherent advantages of Dell Technologies and partnering with decision-makers to support their efforts to align IT resources with agency missions, goals, programmatic priorities, and statutory requirements in accordance with Federal IT Acquisition Reform Act (FITARA) and other agency requirements/initiatives.</li>
<li>
Enabling and assisting agency leadership, CIOs, CTOs, and acquisition officials with respect to the development, integration, delivery, and operations of any type of IT, IT service, or information product to enable agency functions, missions, or operations.</li>
<li>
Assisting the sales team in anticipating customer needs beyond existing scope of enterprise/client products and services.</li>
<li>
Preforming critical functions of assisting customer, sales team, PMO, services, and support with the selling, deployment, and sustainment of system or services into its mission environment. This includes applied leadership and ensuring that optimal management and production activities are occurring within Dell Technologies, with Federal Support Integrators, and with other external agencies who have program equities and responsibilities. Travelling globally, as required, for program support.</li>
<li>
Developing strategies for aligning organizational processes to optimally meet customers’ requirements and business goals.</li>
<li>
Develop strong alignment with the Federal Office of the Chief Technology Officer for Dell EMC Federal for futures planning and strategies.</li>
<li>
Possessing a credible, technical understanding of the pan Dell Technologies portfolio to include storage, compute, networking, applications, cybersecurity, converged and hyper-converged systems, and edge-core-cloud synergies. Understanding the advantages and disadvantages associated with various architectures, especially as these relate to mission, expense, and maintenance.</li>
<li>
Accomplishing other duties as directed by the NSG Directors.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p>
As a Dell Technologies NSG BD-CIO, you will be professionally managed by the NSG PMO Director with additional training and developmental resources being provided by Dell Federal’s Chief Technology Officer. It’s a hugely varied, and dynamic field of work where your leadership and competence are the elixir of success—from the tactical engagement to the strategic win. To be sure, you will work closely with the IC/DoD and the Dell Technologies Federal team to provide game-changing solutions for the customer and the mission.</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Essential Requirements
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Must have an active TS/SCI with FS poly.</li>
<li>
Significant experience and connections within the IC/DoD mission space to include strategic intelligence agencies and tactical intelligence mission support to real-world operations in support of services, Combatant Commands or Joint Task Forces globally.</li>
<li>
Business development experience.</li>
<li>
Executive management/leadership training.</li>
<li><p>
Must have the ability to obtain and maintain a U.S. Government personnel security clearance; active clearance preferred.</p></li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Desired Requirements</b></p>
<ul><li>
Typically requires 20+ years of related experience with a Master’s degree.</li></ul>
