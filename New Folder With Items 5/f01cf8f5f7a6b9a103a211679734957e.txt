<p><b>Account Executive Job Description</b></p>
<p>We are searching for high-energyAccount Executives who can recognize opportunities and turn leads intolong-lasting partnerships. Account Executives will communicate directly withclients and prospects, understand their individual needs, and recommend digitalmanufacturing services that maximize value. You should be an adaptable,knowledgeable multi-tasker with strong computer and communication skills andwilling to research and open new accounts on weekly bases.</p>
<p>Successful Account Executives will beskilled communicators and presenters who can find the best fit between client needsand our custom digital manufacturing solutions. The ideal candidate willbe organized, passionate about client relations, and focused on enhancing thebuyer experience.</p>
<p><b>Account Executive Responsibilities: </b></p>
<p>· Providingsupport for clients by learning about their needs and matching the best digitalmanufacturing solution for them.</p>
<p>· Researching the market, identifying potential accounts todevelop, making cold calls and reaching out to key decision makers.</p>
<p>· Following up with prospects regularly in order to ensure needsare being met and focus to expand the accounts.</p>
<p>· Presenting and demonstrating our value proposition and digitalmanufacturing services to prospective buyers.</p>
<p>· Developing key account sales strategies and setting quotas.</p>
<p>· Building long-lasting, mutually beneficial relationships with externalcontacts and internal departments to create a better customer experience andexpand the accounts.</p>
<p>· Executing targeted sales channel projects developed togetherwith the management.</p>
<p><b>Account Executive Requirements: </b></p>
<p>· Proven previous experience in key account development is a bigplus.</p>
<p>· Business, engineering background or significant exposure tomolding and plastic manufacturing would be an advantage.</p>
<p>· Ability to research, identify new leads and cold call isessential.</p>
<p>· The drive and energy to develop multiple accounts while lookingfor new opportunities.</p>
<p>· Excellent verbal and written communication skills.</p>
<p>· Ability to understand client needs and handle the negotiationprocess.</p>
<p>· Computer skills, especially MS Office, Outlook and CRM type software.</p>
