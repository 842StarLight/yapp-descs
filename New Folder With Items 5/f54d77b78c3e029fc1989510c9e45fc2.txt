<h2>Product Marketing Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div><div>
<div>
<div>When you join Varian, you’re joining an organization with nearly $3 billion in annual revenue, and more than 6,500 team members across more than 30 countries. You’ll be backed by a culture that is centered on fostering your creative potential, and experience the full support of an organization and leadership committed to inspiring you to do your best work. Develop technology that turns promising cancer research into leading cancer treatments. Varian continues to transform the next generation of cancer treatment through our proton therapy technology. Our state-of-the art ProBeam® system delivers precise, targeted treatment directly to tumors – potentially sparing the surrounding healthy tissue. When you join Varian’s Particle Therapy team, you’ll join one of the fastest growing businesses in this market. Your work will support the future of cancer care, as we drive adoption at treatment centers around the world.</div>
</div>
<div>
<p><b>Job Description</b></p>
<div>Mission: As a ProBeam product manager, you will be a champion for our customers by representing their perspective and injecting customer experience improvements into the product development lifecycle. You will represent the critical service and installation stakeholders in the proton solution portfolio to lead product initiatives that support installation and service improvements. Additionally, you will be responsible for both managing individual features within a release and overseeing ProBeam version releases.<br>
Roles and Responsibilities:<ul>
<li>
ProBeam Product Management<ul>
<li>
Define and lead the ProBeam service and installation roadmaps, including management, prioritization, and execution of cost-reduction activities.</li>
<li>
Write and present concept proposals to enhance and distinguish ProBeam Service offering by working with engineering, manufacturing and sales to develop new products or enhance existing product(s) or product line(s).</li>
<li>
Write and present concept proposals to improve installation and commissioning processes and costs by working with engineering, manufacturing, and sales.</li>
<li>
Build service program and general ProBeam marketing content for downstream marketing use.</li>
<li>
Support sales trainings on ProBeam workflows, new features, and future roadmap.</li>
<li>
Oversee Professional Services Agreements and research agreements to get pre-release feedback on products in the early development phases.</li>
<li>
Support customer go-live for first treatments and for version upgrades.</li>
<li>
Manage the ProBeam product lifecycle, including launch of new features/programs to obsolescence planning.</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>
Service Marketing Management<ul>
<li>
Lead the ProBeam Service marketing offering.</li>
<li>
Collaborating cross-functionally with sales, field service, product marketing, product management, and engineering to execute high-impact fully-integrated programs that drive service revenue growth, market leadership, and awareness.</li>
<li>
Advocate for and drive creation of new service offerings, enhance existing product(s) for customers as well as process and optimization.</li>
<li>
Develop Service business and marketing plans for product positioning and entitlements (options) to enhance and distinguish ProBeam Service offering by working with engineering, manufacturing and sales to develop new products or enhance existing product(s) or product line(s).</li>
<li>
Understand the customer needs through customer engagement, market research and market data while assessing the competitive landscape.</li>
<li>
Develop commercialization and go-to-market solutions and launching strategies. Coordinate commercialization deliverables, launch activities and monitor effectiveness of existing offering.</li>
<li>
Identify and develop messaging and positioning that uniquely differentiates Varian’s service solutions to address our customers’ challenges and needs by creating content for executive briefings, analyst briefings, industry events, webcasts, and trade shows to communicate the value proposition our service offers. Defining key buyers and influencers and creating the appropriate compelling messaging.</li>
<li>
Gains deep knowledge of best practices for service commercialization and pricing models in health care and high-tech industries. Oversee market research, monitors competitive activity and identifies customer needs.</li>
<li>
Set and monitor cost, pricing and overall margin of the service program.</li>
</ul>
</li>
</ul>
</div>
</div>
