<h2>Assistant Manager in Training</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Job Summary</p>
<p>Our mission is to help as many people as possible achieve the benefits of a healthy lifestyle. To fulfill this mission, we will create a nationwide network of sports clubs, offering our members the widest range of amenities and the friendliest service at an affordable price. To carry out this mission we are searching for stand out candidates to grow within our organization who align with our vision. I am here to guide you to the path of success with the tools, skill set, talent and energy within the industries leading organization. If you have the passion for fitness, enjoy helping others and are looking to improve your lifestyle, then you can be the perfect candidate in our district.</p>
<p>Job Description:</p>
<p>The Manager in Training (MIT) position is a leadership role who is responsible for the club's overall production and weekend success. This candidate will assist a Senior General Manager to ensure that the club meets or exceeds financial goals as well as training and directing all club employees. This role is a temporary training position to prepare our future General Mangers. The Senior General Manager will evaluate this candidates growth on a weekly and monthly basis with the District Vice President, specifically on the leadership qualities one must possess in the position, performance, as well as the ability to adapt to our culture and business plan. Based on this candidates influence and performance, one can expect to step into a General Manager role as soon as three months but no longer than 12 months. This is an appointed role for internal and external candidates with high potential. Once in the role, we will expect this candidate's continued support with company initiatives to help generate new sales leads for continued membership growth as well as executing directives without hesitation or alteration.</p>
