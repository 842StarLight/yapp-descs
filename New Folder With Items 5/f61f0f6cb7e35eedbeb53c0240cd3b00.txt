<h2>Watson Discovery Go-To-Market Offering Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<b>Introduction</b><br>
Ready to re-imagine the product management role? At IBM, as an Offering Manager, you are responsible for the full lifecycle of an offering: from validating need to determining the business model and defining the solution requirements. Going beyond the traditional focus of timetables and budgets, you use your keen insights into what customers want in their own business plan - and work across disciplines to build the best solutions to a problem. If being the CEO of your offering excites you, consider an offering management career at IBM.<br>
<br>
<b>Your Role and Responsibilities</b><br>
IBM Watson uses AI to tackle some of humanity's most challenging problems - like revolutionizing how doctors research cancer or transforming how businesses engage with their customers. If you have a cross functional background and a passion for building great products that change the world then the <b>Watson Discovery Go-To-Market (GTM) Offering Manager</b> role may be the perfect fit.<br>
<br>
<b>IBM Watson Discovery</b> lets you rapidly build cognitive applications that extract value from structured and unstructured data. Bring your own data or use pre-enriched content. Save time with automated data ingestion and a simplified query language. Build your cognitive exploration app once with Discovery, and easily integrate it into existing solutions. Check it out: ibm.biz/WatsonDiscovery<br>
<br>
As the Go-to-Market <b>Offering Manager</b> for the Watson Discovery portfolio, you will:<ul>
<li>
Lead the go-to-market strategy for a Watson Discovery portfolio offering. Work collaboratively with Technical Offering Managers, Marketing, business partners, professional services teams and others to ensure a successful go-to-market approach for the launch and adoption of new Watson products.</li>
<li>
Determine pricing, marketing communication strategy, identify routes to market, and be responsible for enabling the sales force on a Watson Discovery offering.
</li>
<li>Lead customer and stakeholder feedback programs and funnel this information into the product to inform the user experience and product requirements. Present Watson capabilities to clients and work directly with sponsor clients to shape the product.</li>
<li>
Aid in the research and design of enhanced and new offerings. This will include researching the current state of the portfolio, competitive product offerings, market trends, and the users of high-end unstructured data analytics software to make strategic recommendations for future product direction.</li>
<li>
Act as an entrepreneur, strategist, cross-functional team leader, and client advocate.</li>
<li>
Organize the strategic planning including launch and post-launch GTM activities for a Watson Discovery offering.</li>
<li>
Lead efforts and coordinate with marketing around the creation of offering demonstrations, sales assets, and seller enablement.</li>
<li>
Shape a strategic vision for the next generation of Watson products by working closely with designers, developers and sponsored users.</li>
<li>
Apply a design-based thinking methodology to generate useful market insights.
</li>
<li>Develop experience in the field of Artificial Intelligence, text analytics and unstructured data analytics and related disciplines through research, competitive analysis and other resources.</li>
<li>
Document key lessons learned recommendations for future work.</li>
<li>
Partner with Marketing, Sales and other customer-facing client teams to determine necessary collateral and sales tools to promote a Watson Discovery offering</li>
<li>
Develop and shape cohesive messaging for a Watson Discovery offering and ensure optimal positioning of each offering within the portfolio</li>
</ul>
<br>
Successful candidates will possess a unique blend of business and technical savvy, a big-picture vision, a user-centric viewpoint, and the drive to maximize market opportunities by designing and developing new offerings.<br>
