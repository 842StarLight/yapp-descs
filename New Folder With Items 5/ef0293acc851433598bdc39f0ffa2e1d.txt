<h2>Client Services Ops Analyst</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">AppsFlyer is looking for a Client Services Ops Analyst to help drive efficient growth of our Client Services team through analytics, process management &amp; optimization. We are looking for an expert in Salesforce and Excel to provide the Client Services team with emerging opportunities, pattern recognition, key insights, and initiatives to drive new strategy forward.
<br><br>
Our Client Services Ops team works with a wide range of stakeholders from Client Services, Senior Management to Billing, Legal and Sales and is a new function to our Client Services department. This is a great opportunity to build from the ground up.
<br><br>
<b>Responsibilities:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Take part in defining the ongoing strategy and execution of the Client Services Ops team</li>
<li>Build and maintain data and create reporting dashboards on various platforms such as Looker, Salesforce, Zendesk and more</li>
<li>Own and manage Client Services processes to make sure all stakeholders are aligned and meeting team goals</li>
<li>Train the Client Services US team on processes &amp; KPI's</li>
<li>Draw insightful conclusions from data to assess and implement new opportunities to increase recurring revenue and operating leverage</li>
<li>Define and implement a framework to prioritize short-term and long-term Client Services initiatives</li>
<li>Recognize and recommend software system improvements and re-evaluate and maintain software systems as needed</li>
<li>Work hand in hand with HQ team in Israel to facilitate and train the Client Services teams in both New York and San Francisco offices on new projects, processes, and tools and provide feedback to HQ</li>
<li>Work closely with the Finance team on ad hoc requests; billing holds, credit requests, etc</li>
</ul>
<b>Requirements:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>3 - 4 years of experience as a Business / BI / Data Analyst or similar role. Saas experience is a plus</li>
<li>Strong analytical skills with the ability to collect, organize, and analyze significant amounts of data</li>
<li>Expert with Excel, Looker or similar BI tool</li>
<li>Expert with Salesforce - strong knowledge of reporting and dashboard creation</li>
<li>Experience managing operational projects and/or processes from identification to implementation to execution</li>
<li>Excellent verbal and written communication skills - clear and articulate communicator</li>
<li>Experience working with Finance, Legal, and Sales teams</li>
<li>Team player with proven ability to lead through influence</li>
<li>Bachelor degree in Business Management / Statistics / Mathematics / Economics / Information System Engineering</li>
</ul>
<b>Preferred Requirements:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Experience working in a client services function (Customer Success and/or Technical Support)</li>
<li>Being referred by an AppsFlyer team member is a plus!</li>
</ul>
