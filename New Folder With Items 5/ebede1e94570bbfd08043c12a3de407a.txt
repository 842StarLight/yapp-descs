<h2>Manager, Financial Planning & Analysis</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Manager, Financial Planning &amp; Analysis</b></p>
<p>
This is a <b>Full Time</b> position. Schedule is <b>Monday - Friday, 8:00</b><b> – 5:00 PM,</b> with overtime as needed.<br>
<b>Basic Function and Scope of Responsibility:</b></p>
<p>
Manage the organization’s financial analysis, financial planning and contract negotiation efforts in support of its strategic planning, commercial efforts, operational efficiency and other organizational development goals. Provide leadership for the Company’s annual budgeting process, monthly/quarterly operating plan variance analysis, special projects, capital expenditure planning and analysis, profitability analysis, strategic pricing comparisons, industry/peer group comparisons and other analytical support to business leaders to optimize organizational performance. Oversee the analysis of complex business information and provide recommendations based on results. Learn and incorporate the organization’s regulatory requirements, business strengths and capabilities into work product ranging from financial analysis to competitive assessment to contract arrangements with vendors and clients. Provide competitive insights, data analysis and process improvement recommendations to achieve the organization’s growth and efficiency goals. A wide degree of creativity and latitude is expected.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Employee Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Oversee and manage the continued development of budgeting, long-range financial planning, regular forecasting, project-based financial analysis, sales performance analysis, commission design/management and M&amp;A review/support (list not all-inclusive).</li>
<li>Prepare, manage and provide expertise to the preparation of complex analytical and financial reports and presentations to business leaders across functions; ability to manage the work of others providing similar work product.</li>
<li>Responsible for acquiring key knowledge of the company’s operational interworking and business strategies and proactively anticipate and respond to environmental shifts.</li>
<li>Serve as lead contract negotiator between the Company and other commercial entities (e.g., clinical and biopharma clients, landlords, patent holders, etc.).Ability to independently review, modify, draft language and defend the Company’s interests in contract negotiations.Use of, but not dependent upon, external legal resources when liability mandates or practice-area expertise requires such usage.</li>
<li>Application of raw intellectual fire power.Conduct and direct financial-based research and analysis projects. Develop, interpret, and implement financial concepts for business planning and decision making.</li>
<li>Ability to work collaboratively across all departments and effectively communicate complex financial matters to non-financial business users.</li>
<li>Utilize extensive experience and judgment to plan and accomplish goals.</li>
<li>Responsible for developing financial models for forecasting revenue and expense for potential M&amp;A targets.Capable of identifying and analyzing transaction components and conducting financial due diligence on potential targets.Ability to lead the financial analysis component of an M&amp;A project team and target review.</li>
<li>Support senior management team and department heads with in-depth analysis.Ability to analyze complex financial information in time-sensitive situations and provide accurate and rapid financial recommendations for decision making purposes.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p>
</p>
<p><b>The Ideal Candidate would possess:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>A Bachelor's degree in Finance or related field required, MBA or Master's Degree is preferred</li>
<li>7+ years of professional Finance experience preferred</li>
<li>Dynamic, creative, and hands-on financial professional with demonstrated leadership in operations analysis and planning</li>
<li>Familiarity with laboratory billing and third party payers</li>
<li>Possesses impressive business acumen and strong leadership, organization, and communication skills</li>
<li>Takes accountability for the business’s performance and actively seeks to improve it</li>
<li>Up to date knowledge of current financial and accounting computer applications</li>
<li>Strong working knowledge of GAAP accounting and U.S. taxation principles preferred</li>
<li>Superior problem solving abilities</li>
<li>Ability to influence leadership and others without direct oversight</li>
<li>Ability to foster team building with mutual respect and Associate development</li>
<li>Ability to prioritize business needs, delegate work assignments, authorize others to work independently, set expectations and monitor delegated activities</li>
<li>Ability to partner directly with department leaders to assist them in meeting their business needs</li>
<li>Willingness to work outside normal business hours as needed</li>
<li>Ability to make independent decisions regarding matters of significance</li>
<li>Goal oriented, with excellent time management and organizational skills</li>
<li>Excellent interpersonal skills, with ability to interact effectively and work efficiently with people at all levels in an organization</li>
<li>Excellent verbal and written communication skills</li>
<li>Keenly attentive to detail</li>
<li>Ability to keep sensitive information confidential</li>
<li>High level of proficiency with PC based software programs</li>
<li>Positive attitude, role model for others within the organization</li>
<li>Ability to travel up to 15%</li>
<br>
</ul>
