<h2>Marketing Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>AXIS GeoSpatial LLC </b>is looking for a Marketing Specialist to help coordinate and implement marketing communication projects with responsibilities that include public relations, special events management, advertising, and creating brand awareness.<br>
</p>
<p>If you are offered the position, you will help to secure new business projects and assist with maintaining client relationships for a full service geospatial solutions provider. <b>AXIS</b> provides land surveying, mapping and geospatial consulting for integration into civil engineering, land surveying and GIS applications. As an industry leader in innovative remote-sensing and measurement technologies, <b>AXIS</b> has a proven track record of stability and longevity in the geospatial market.</p>
<p><b><i>
Your duties will include:</i></b></p>
<ul>
<li>Assisting in developing and producing marketing materials such as qualification packages, presentations, social media, and website content.</li>
<li>Assisting in creating materials such as project write-ups, case studies, and resumes for proposal documents and client presentations.<br>
</li>
<li>Assisting proposal manager, AXIS Team, and stakeholders in proposal development and preparation, writing, editing, and publication of various types of documents.</li>
<li>Updating social media and website information.<br>
</li>
<li>Event management, planning and coordinating of special events, and relationship management with specialized consultants.
</li>
<li>Assist with trade-show preparation, planning, staffing, set-up, and post-lead management.</li>
</ul>
<p><b><i>To succeed in this, you should have the following skill set:</i></b></p>
<ul>
<li>Proficiency with word processing, spreadsheets, desktop publishing software, and the MS Office suite.</li>
<li>Experience working with Adobe Creative Cloud; in particular, Photoshop, InDesign, and Premiere Pro.</li>
<li>Excellent written and oral communication skills</li>
<li>Ability to work well independently and in teams; pro-actively anticipating work requirements and problem solving.</li>
<li>Well-organized, ability to set priorities and meet deadlines, and able to handle multiple tasks simultaneously.</li>
</ul>
<p><b><i>A</i></b><b><i>dditional skills that will lead to your success, include:</i></b>
</p>
<p>Being meticulous, collaborative, a quick learner, a multi-tasker, and passionate about communication. Ability to support the Marketing Team and entire AXIS Team with enthusiasm and creativity.<br>
</p>
<ul>
<li>1-3 years' experience of relevant B2B industry experience is required. A/E industry experience will be helpful.<br>
</li>
<li>A Bachelor's Degree in Marketing, Journalism, and/or Communication.</li>
<li>Ability to travel to PR events, trade-shows, and exhibits.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>AXIS</b> offers an excellent compensation and benefits package that includes: health, dental, vision, disability, and life insurance, HRA, 401k with company match, education assistance, paid leave, and more.</p>
<p>
The selected candidate will report to the company's headquarters in Easton, Maryland. This arts and cultural center is rated among the Top Ten Best Small Towns in America and Top 100 Small Arts Communities in America; offering beautiful scenery, historic charm with an urban feel, countless amenities and fine dining - all at a cost of living well below the rest of Maryland's urban areas.</p>
