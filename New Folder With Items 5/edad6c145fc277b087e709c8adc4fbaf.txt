<h2>Sales Operations Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>At Interos, we are disrupting the way Fortune 500 companies and government agencies identify and respond to risk within their supply chains whether this risk is being caused by cyber breaches, socio economic issues, quality control, natural disasters or unknown, unethical sourcing. We deliver the data and insights to business leaders that help them identify, visualize and understand the ripple effects that could impact their supply chains, before they happen.</p>
<p>
Recently funded by Kleiner Perkins and pivoting to an automated solution, Interos is in essence, a start-up SaaS environment. We need someone who thrives as part of fast-paced team and takes pride in the ownership of their content strategy.</p>
<p><b>
The Opportunity:</b></p>
<p>
The Sales Operations Manager is responsible for supporting the global sales team in achieving the company revenue targets and growing market share. You will establish new and scalable processes for the company to improve sales efficiency and work closely with Sales leadership and Marketing to determine growth goals and recommend and support improvements for pipeline and bookings growth goals.</p>
<p>
This role will be the 'right hand person' for the VP of Revenue and will manage the ongoing reporting and the development of Interos's Salesforce.com deployment. This role is a blend of data analysis, technical project management, and Salesforce administration.</p>
<p><b>
We want to meet you if you:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Have an excellent strategic and operational vision</li>
<li>Have strong process management and analytical skills</li>
<li>Get a kick out of establishing new processes and working in an agile work environment</li>
<li>Want to understand the complete customer journey right from inception of a marketing lead, through to a customer advocate</li>
<li>Get the value of automation and building a lean business powered by data insights
</li>
</ul>
<p>Interos is an early leader in an emerging market space which means this role must be agile, willing to experiment and be incredibly self-disciplined. You should be able and proud to point to examples of your prior work that had a huge impact on your space.</p>

<p><b>Key Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Provide strategic leadership to design and administer all sales processes, policies and programs to drive sales effectiveness and productivity</li>
<li>Partner with Marketing to monitor and report on the sales funnel from lead generation through to deal.</li>
<li>Integrate automated efficiencies where possible to maintain an agile, lean business
</li>
<li>Build prospect processing and sales reporting capabilities to optimize sales performance</li>
<li>Analyze the business and proactively offer insights and recommendations to the sales and executive leadership team</li>
<li>Design and implement sales compensation plans that align with company objectives, motivate sales staff, and drive productive behavior</li>
<li>Collaborate with sales leaders and internal departments to design sales training programs to optimize individual and team performance</li>
<li>Oversee the administration of all enterprise software applications used by the sales team and ensure utilization, timeliness and accuracy of these systems</li>
<li>Prepare accurate real-time, monthly and quarterly performance reporting against key KPIs</li>
<li>Design / evaluate / implement technology and programs to improve sales productivity</li>
</ul>

<li>Ability to independently identify problems, propose solutions and execute on those solutions</li>
