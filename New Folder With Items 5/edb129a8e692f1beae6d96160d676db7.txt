<h2>Service Account Manager - Nonwovens Division</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Summary/Objective</b></p>
<p>The Service Account Manager position acts as a sales representative of the After Sales Service using technical, organizational and customer knowledge to influence customers and assist them in applying the products/services to their needs, resulting in revenue generation.</p>
<p><b>Essential Functions</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Establishes and fosters/maintains customer contacts, researches customer needs and develops application of products and services.</li>
<li>Prepares quotations for possible service packs.</li>
<li>Identifies opportunities at the customer site and finds a suitable solution by communicating with internal departments to satisfy the customer need.</li>
<li>During the Design/Engineering Phase of a new machine, the Service Account Manager looks at required training needs on the machine, maintenance or audit services/contracts needed and communicates with engineering on potential spare parts required and communicates that information with the spare parts team.</li>
<li>The Service Account Manager looks for</li>
<li>Process optimization</li>
<li>Warranty expansion</li>
<li>Spare part support</li>
<li>Upgrade opportunities</li>
<li>Software upgrades</li>
<li>Efficiency improvement programs/financial justification</li>
<li>Service support</li>
<li>Acts as the keeper of the Open Issues list after delivery of the machine and plays a substantial role in the success of the project.</li>
</ul>
