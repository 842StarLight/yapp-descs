<h2>Marketing Operations Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Job Description</b></h3>
<br>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>Why Applitools? </b>We are changing the landscape of software development. Applitools provides the only commercial-grade, visual AI-based test cloud service that validates application user interfaces in a fully automated manner, used by hundreds of engineering teams at top brands worldwide.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>In your new job as our Marketing Operations Manager you will join a growing global marketing team as our leader in marketing ops and analytics.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>You will build out and manage our global marketing operations stack including Marketo, Discover.org, Lean Data, Attribution tools, Google Analytics, and our UTM framework</li>
<li>
You will create and manage the analytical engine we need to support our product led growth strategy ranging from a freemium developer offer to our Enterprise SaaS platform.</li>
<li>
You will help analyze all marketing programs and tactics to determine their effectiveness in driving leads, product engagement, sales pipeline, and revenue.</li>
</ul>
<p>Your career will progress in several important ways:</p>
<ul>
<li>You will be a key part of an integrated sales &amp; marketing operations team helping to analyze and recommend improvements to our marketing efforts including content marketing, social media, paid digital advertising, search marketing, events, website optimization, and more.</li>
<li>
You will support the executive team under the direction of a veteran CMO with special projects focused on our go-to-market strategy and tactics.</li>
<li>
You will have a front row seat to the strategies and tactics vital to driving success from start-up to $50m+ revenue in just 3 years.</li>
</ul>
<br>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>Qualifications</b></p>
<p>We'd love to have someone with some or all of the following experience (don't worry if you don't have all these; we're great mentors!):</p>
<ul>
<li>
You have 3 years or more as a Marketing Analyst, with a B2B or B2D company that loves using data to make good decisions</li>
<li>
We need your experience administering marketing automation tools such as Marketo. Your Marketo certification is a plus, but we will invest in your future and get you more training if you need it.</li>
<li>
You have a Bachelor Degree in Marketing or Mathematics (or related fields like Economics, Statistics, etc.). technical or advanced degrees a plus, but not required.</li>
<li>
We are in need of your exceptional organizational, communication, and team building skills to help us manage the fast-paced world of a high-growth global SaaS start-up.<br>
<br>
Location. Location. Location. Tired of your long commute? We are located in San Mateo, CA at the intersection of 92 and 101, and a close walk from Caltrain. It's a reasonable commute from almost anywhere in the Bay Area!</li>
</ul>
