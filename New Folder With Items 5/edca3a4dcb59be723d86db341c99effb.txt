<h2>SVP Marketing, Communications & eCommerce</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p><i>Basic purpose or primary function of job)</i></p>
<div>
<div>
<div><div>
<p>Reporting directly to the President and CEO, the SVP Marketing, Communications &amp; eCommerce will lead all aspects of the vision and executable strategy for the Foundation’s marketing plan. We will rely on the SVP to oversee all strategic initiatives to increase business leads as well as tactical execution of the strategy for the Foundation. The SVP will also lead the critical effort to use communications and marketing to build relationships with and engage stakeholders, supporters and constituents, to increase the visibility of the Arthritis Foundation and to connect the Foundation’s work to finding a cure for arthritis. As a senior leader in the organization, the SVP Marketing, Communications &amp; eCommerce will participate in overarching strategic planning and decision-making and will help guide efforts to shift the Arthritis Foundation, nationally and throughout the regions, into becoming a high-performing, 21st century communicating, engagement-driven organization.</p>
<p><b>
RELATIONSHIPS AND CONTACTS</b></p>
<p>
Within The Foundation</p>
<p>
The SVP maintains frequent and close working relationships with members of the Foundation’s Senior Leadership Team, other staff, and volunteers as appropriate to accomplish the Foundation’s brand-building objectives. Provides marketing expertise and counsel to staff and volunteers.</p>
<p>
Outside the Foundation</p>
<p>
The SVP Develops and maintains regular and frequent relationships with sponsors, vendors, professional groups and other organizations as appropriate to accomplish the Foundation’s communications and marketing plans. Create a solid network of strategic partnerships to build brand awareness and drive new sales.</p>
