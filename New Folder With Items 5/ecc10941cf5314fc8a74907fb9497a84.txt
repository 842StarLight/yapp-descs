<h2>Senior Merchandising Analyst/Associate Merchandiser</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<div>Grainger is a broad line, business-to-business distributor of maintenance, repair and operating (MRO) supplies and other related products and services. More than 3.2 million businesses and institutions worldwide rely on Grainger for products such as safety gloves, ladders, motors and janitorial supplies, along with services like inventory management and technical support. These customers represent a broad collection of industries including commercial, government, healthcare and manufacturing. They place orders online, on mobile devices, through sales representatives, over the phone and at local branches. Approximately 5,000 suppliers provide Grainger with more than 1.6 million products stocked in Graingerâ€™s distribution centers and branches worldwide.</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<p><b>Primary Function:</b></p>
<p>The primary purpose of this role is to assist the Merchant and Channel Merchandising Manager in driving profitable growth by optimizing our product assortment and improving the Grainger online experience for customers. This includes helping support assortment reviews through data gathering and analytics, analyzing online metrics and leading critical maintenance efforts for a broad set of product lines. In addition to assortment reviews &amp; analytics this role also acts as a technology expert within a specific set of merchandising toolkits.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Principle Duties and Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Lead strategic assortment activities such as analyzing and proposing key additions to merchants</li>
<li>Responsible for partnering with Merchants and Channel Merchandisers to execute off-cycle maintenance opportunities such as product assortment changes and Grainger.com merchandising experiences.</li>
<li>Support the implementation of strategic merchandising initiatives and special projects</li>
<li>Work with the Managers, Channel Merchandising to implement category reviews</li>
<li>Lead testing for new digital software features and functionalities</li>
<li>Provide troubleshooting and technical support of software to the merchandising teams</li>
<li>Maintain Standard Operation Procedure documentation for digital tool sets</li>
<li>Collaborate with business partners in Product Information, Search, and Publishing to optimize online content</li>
<li>Assist in training new hires on our software toolkits</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Preferred Education &amp; Experience:</b></p>
<p>Must have 3-5 years of experience in a large, multi-channel organization with:</p>
<ul>
<li>3-5 years of related ecommerce, product merchandising, or product management experience</li>
<li>Proven success working in teams with dynamic, analytics-driven, multi-channel work environments</li>
<li>Experience collecting and managing product-related digital assets (product imagery, PDF documentation, etc.)</li>
<li>Ability to work independently as well as in a team environment</li>
<li>Strong systemic thinking and problem solving skills</li>
<li>Demonstrated interpersonal and relationship-building skills</li>
<li>Strong communication skills, detail orientation, and business acumen</li>
<li>3-5 years of experience in Stibo STEP, strong technical acumen a strong plus</li>
<li>High-level knowledge of the MRO (Maintenance, Repair, and Operations) industry and industrial products a strong plus</li>
<br>
</ul>
