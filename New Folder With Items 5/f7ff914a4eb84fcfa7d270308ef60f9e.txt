<b>Position Description</b><br>
<p>The Sourcing Specialist II - MLA will support sales by executing customer RFQ’s and multi-location opportunities, source and negotiate solutions for customers in order to drive savings, and complete analytical projects that improve Veritiv’s position with its customers.</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Key Accountabilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Responsible for the completing the Facility Solutions product proposal portion of RFPs for multi-region opportunities:</li>
<li>
Develop customized Facility Solution product programs that meet the customer’s needs and align with Veritiv overall LEAN strategy and preferred suppliers</li>
<li>
Negotiate contract costs, terms, minimums, and incentives in order to gain and/or grow new business and enhance margin position</li>
<li>
Collaborate with sales and product management on sourcing strategies and also in attempt to meet vendor growth incentives</li>
<li>
Meet all internal and external deadlines</li>
<li>
Conduct existing business improvement projects aimed at producing cost savings and margin enhancements, providing solutions, consolidating SKUs and manufacturers with preferred, and simplifying logistic complexities</li>
<li>
Collaborate with program management, sales, and inventory teams to successfully implement awarded programs</li>
<li>
Identify, drive and implement cost reductions through product, pricing, costing &amp; margin analysis. Initiate margin enhancement projects within the customer contract parameters.</li>
<li>
Maintain extensive industry knowledge, product applications, new products, competitive products, pricing and market trends.</li>
<li>
Work closely with Product Management to ensure effective utilization of corporate programs, private brand products, core vendor selection and product transitions.</li>
<li>
Develop and enhance multi-level vendor relationships across geographic markets. Identify and cultivate vendor relationships with a National and/or North American footprint.</li>
<li>
Other related duties and responsibilities as required or assigned</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Qualifications / Knowledge &amp; Experience:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Ability to manage multiple and conflicting deadlines, often under pressure</li>
<li>
Excellent verbal, written, presentation, people skills are required</li>
<li>
Must have the ability to understand distribution and company economics and be able to effectively communicate with a wide variety of internal and external customers and suppliers</li>
<li>
PC proficient in Windows based software programs, intermediate to expert in MS Excel</li>
<li>
Project management skills preferred</li>
<li>
Strong analytical and problem-solving skills are required</li>
<li>
Must exhibit Veritiv Values, specifically customer focus and action oriented</li>
<li>
College degree or equivalent work experience required (3-5 years in supply chain, sourcing, sales, or related field)</li>
</ul>
