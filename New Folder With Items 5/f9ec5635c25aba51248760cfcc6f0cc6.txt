<h2>Senior Director, Professional Services</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Position</b>: Senior Director, Professional Services</p>
<p><b>
Location</b>: San Jose, CA (Headquarters)</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
For over 10 years, Zscaler has been disrupting and transforming the security industry. Our 100% purpose built cloud platform delivers the entire gateway security stack as a service through 150 global data centers to securely connect users to their applications, regardless of device, location, or network in over 185 countries protecting over 3,500 companies and 100 Million threats detected a day.</p>
<p>
We work in a fast paced, dynamic and make it happen culture. Our people are some of the brightest and passionate in the industry that thrive on being the first to solve problems. We are always looking to hire highly passionate, collaborative and humble people that want to make a difference.</p>
<p>
The Senior Director, Professional Services provides leadership and direction to the teams that are executing professional services engagements to Zscaler customers. This highly visible role will bring strong client services experience and technical strength related to security consulting practices to continue growth and reputation as the leading provider of technology solutions to our market.<br>
<br>
<br>
In this role, you will work closely with sales, product, technology teams and customers to ensure Zscaler product deployments are delivered timely and achieve the business objectives for our customers. You will have overall responsibility for the professional services organization financial objectives, including productivity and contribution margin. To be successful in this role, you will need to possess a positive, engaging and goal-oriented management style and must be comfortable working with cross functional teams.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Key Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Responsible for taking an entrepreneurial approach to building and implementing Professional Services Offerings with customer success as the goal</li>
<li>
Develop strategies and operational models to drive services revenue and profitability</li>
<li>
Set goals and initiatives for the team and manage performance and productivity</li>
<li>
Influence cross-functional business units to drive operational excellence and customer satisfaction</li>
<li>
Build and sustain trusting client relationships at the appropriate level</li>
<li>
Hire, develop, motivate, and retain top talent</li>
<li>
Drive financial performance through productivity analysis, contract terms and resource planning</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Desired Skills and Experience</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
10 years of exceptional leadership in leading, managing and scaling a growing professional services organization</li>
<li>
Excellent client relationship management skills, and ability to serve as a trusted advisor</li>
<li>
Developing Service Offerings for customers using all available delivery channels</li>
<li>
A roll-up sleeves approach to the design, development, implementation and measure of Professional Services</li>
<li>
Strong interpersonal skills and a collaborative nature</li>
<li>
A proven performance track record of achieving / overachieving against goals</li>
<li>
Demonstrated ability to communicate well verbally, in small and large presentation settings, and in writing; also, the ability to lead or facilitate technical workshops</li>
<li>
Direct experience in a technical services role highly preferred</li>
<li>
Experience in the SaaS Security domain preferred</li>
<li>
Travel to customers may be required – 20%</li>
</ul>
