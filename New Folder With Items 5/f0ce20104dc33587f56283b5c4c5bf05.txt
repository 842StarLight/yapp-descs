<p><b>Financial Analyst - Dulles VA</b></p>
<p><b>
Job Code: SAS20190310-33761</b></p>
<p><b>
Job Description:
</b></p>
<p>The Specialty Applications (SA) business of L3Harris is seeking a Financial Analyst to support a highly dynamic area consisting of diverse business models including traditional development programs, commercial and government products, and international pursuits and programs. The SA business, situated within the Wireless Solutions Division, is focused on growth through program and product development and market entry leveraging leading edge technologies.</p>
<p>
This role will be responsible for SA management reporting, including, weekly Risk and Opportunities reporting, monthly four-point analysis, sales and profit driver analysis, backlog and book-to-bill reporting, discretionary spending and other duties as listed below. This financial analyst in this position will interact with various levels of management at each business enterprise, with the SA business area and the Wireless Solutions leadership team.</p>
<p><b>
Job Details:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Demonstrate strong interpersonal skills, be capable of interfacing and forming business partnerships with key decision-makers</li>
<li>
Operate effectively and make decisions with ambiguous information</li>
<li>
Excel at communications (oral, written, and listening), provide presentations to management</li>
<li>
Assist with the analysis of business cases; including financial, accounting, and compliance implications</li>
<li>
Drive efficiencies into the weekly closure reporting process of OSPI</li>
<li>
Assist with the Strategic Growth Plan (SGP), Annual Operating Plan and activities</li>
<li>
Responsible for Monthly Orders/Sales/Profit/Investment forecasting and tracking Hyperion; Monthly Financial Reporting consolidation and submission; Monthly Direct Sells and Bid and Proposal budget forecasting and tracking; Monthly Backlog and Book-to-Bill analysis; Monthly Discretionary spending analysis; Capital forecasting and reporting; Balance Sheet forecast</li>
<li>
Support monthly financial reporting requirements</li>
<li>
Support monthly variance analysis reporting</li>
<li>
Responsible for a range of quarterly financial reporting activities</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Essential Functions:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Monthly forecast and reporting</li>
<li>
Main POC between SA and Wireless Solutions</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Qualifications:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>B.S. degree in finance, accounting or related discipline.</li>
<li>
4+ years’ experience in financial analysis and program finance.</li>
<li>
At least 4 years of experience working with Microsoft Office applications (with advanced Excel skills) required.</li>
<li>
At least 4 years of experience working on multiple projects simultaneously.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Preferred Additional Skills:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Experience with CostPoint, Cognos, Peoplesoft, HFM and Hyperion Planning is highly desired</li>
<li>
Demonstrated creative and critical thinking skills</li>
<li>
Strong communication and presentation skills. Hands-on knowledge of the FAR and CAS a plus</li>
<li>
Experience in government contracting environment performing program control analysis.</li>
<li>
Self-motivated and proactive team player with strong analytical and problem-solving skills.</li>
<li>
Strong interpersonal and written communications skills and the ability to communicate with staff members of all levels.</li>
<li>
Detail oriented and organized.</li>
</ul>
