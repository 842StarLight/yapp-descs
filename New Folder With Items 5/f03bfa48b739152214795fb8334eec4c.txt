<h2>Manager of Supply Chain Planning Supplies</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Overview
</b></h2>
<div><div><div><ul>
<li>Manages several small units responsible for production lifecycle, item documentation, demand planning, centralized FG procurement and product costing.</li>
<li>
Establishes operational objectives and work plans, and delegates assignments to staff.
</li>
<li>Receives assignments in the form of objectives and determines how to use resources to meet schedules and goals. Manages human resources and develops procedures and processes to attain unit goals.</li>
<li>
Substantial involvement in developing, modifying and executing policies that affect immediate</li>
<li>
operation(s) but which may also have company-wide effect.
</li>
<li>Represents the unit at meetings or on committees.</li>
<li>
Ensures Supplies products being created and released meet customer and regulatory requirements.</li>
<li>
Provides oversight to Item creation and drawings and ensures the integrity of such.
</li>
<li>Responsible for maintaining and updating regional demand plans.
</li>
<li>Ensures the accuracy of the estimating/product cost structures and that processes are in place to efficiently support Quoting requirements and overall business needs.</li>
<li>
Ensures supply of product and/or services to support Zebra’s desired customer service requirements while balancing operational efficiencies</li>
<li>
Will drive initiatives, programs, clean-up activities and other assignments as directed by the management team. Additionally, working with management team, they will highlight problem areas and make process improvements where possible.</li>
<li>
Planning levels are moderated by the team to ensure that inventory positions are appropriate to meet the demand requirements relative to; product lifecycle, inventory on order and in transit, finished goods and inventory quality.
</li>
</ul></div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities<br>
</b></h2>
<p></p>
<div><div><div>
<ul>
<li>Oversees procurement of materials within defined scope. Centralized purchasing of FG items.</li>
<li>
Provides guidance or manages to –
<ul>
<li>Reviewing and updating forecasts. Determining what items should be forecasted.
</li>
<li>Performing inventory balancing activities.
</li>
<li>Overviews and ensures system requirements for planning levels (MOQ, reorder points, etc) and safety stock levels are implemented.</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>
Develops, implements, and maintains policies, procedures and material control systems.</li>
<li>
May prepare material requirement plans using enterprise resource planning systems.</li>
<li>
Understand supply chain costs and manages key metrics for inventory quality and supply chain. Total costs, inventory turns, IQR and other key metrics used to evaluate overall performance against the supply or demand plan.</li>
<li>
Develops and implements programs/solutions to improve processes, reduce costs and cycle time, and</li>
<li>
improve customer service</li>
<li>
Defines process and business requirements to ensure data quality.</li>
<li>
Manages escalations.
</li>
<li>Responsible for Item Drawing creation processes and tools used to create drawings (Corel, Illustrator, etc.).</li>
<li>
Responsible for Supplies item creation processes and product lifecycle management.
</li>
<li>Support CIient Care, Sales, Product Management, Manufacturing, R&amp;D and other teams with the release and maintenance of sustaining or new products, and changes to such products.
</li>
<li>Support the business for specific projects such as but not limited to - clean-up projects, cost-reduction projects, regulatory initiatives, etc.</li>
<li>
Responsible for the estimating process and product costing structure.
</li>
<li>Optimize human, financial, and other resources to achieve designated goals.</li>
<li>
Effectively distributes work, evaluates performance, provides feedback, assesses and selects</li>
<li>
Recommends items and amounts for inclusion in the budget. Controls expenses.</li>
<li>
Interacts with key internal and external stakeholders in order to solve complex problems.</li>
<li>
Sets KPI’s with subordinates. Sets operational goals for key metrics. Ensure metrics driven to root causes and process changes implemented where needed.</li>
<li>
Responsible for the training, development, and management of talent within the team.</li>
<li>
Internally recognized leader who exercises judgment within broadly defined practices and policies in selecting methods, techniques and evaluation criteria for obtaining results.</li>
<li>
Significant knowledge in function, product, technology service, strategy, and business complexities in order to contribute to the development of creative solutions in area of expertise.</li>
</ul>
