<h2>Coordinator - Supply Chain</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Holman Enterprises, headquartered in Mount Laurel, New Jersey, is an international automotive services company. Beginning in 1924 as a single Ford dealership, Holman has evolved to encompass six business segments that support several diverse sectors of the automotive market:
</p>
<ul>
<li><p>Holman Automotive, one of the largest privately-owned dealership groups in the United States, with more than 38 retail franchises 17 brands from the East Coast to the Pacific Northwest;
</p></li>
<li><p>Steward Financial Services, an auto retail finance company;
</p></li>
<li><p>Holman Insurance Services, a commercial and consumer insurance services company
</p></li>
<li><p>Holman Parts Distribution, a national multi-brand powertrain parts distributor;
</p></li>
<li><p>Auto Truck Group, a truck up-fitting business; and
</p></li>
<li><p>ARI, the largest privately-owned fleet leasing and management company in the world
</p></li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Responsibilities:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Solicit and monitor order pipeline from outside sources, including but not limited to Sales, ADR, AM, RTM’s
</li>
<li>Communicate order expectations to internal and external customers as needed prior to order placement
</li>
<li>Creates, analyzes and distributes custom supply chain status reports to internal and external stakeholders with a focus on identifying process improvements and proactive communication
</li>
<li>Monitor customer order cycle and status to ensure vendor commitments align with actual performance
</li>
<li>Interface with manufacturers, dealers, sales, body companies, and internal and external customers
</li>
<li>Oversees and coordinates the supply chain process to ensure client vehicles are delivered licensed and timely
</li>
<li>Work with Supply Chain Analyst and Client Process Specialist to apply LEAN principles and approaches to uncover more efficiency and increase team capacity
</li>
<li>Support Supply Chain Analyst with global initiatives as needed
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Knowledge and Skill:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Working knowledge and exposure to all areas of Vehicle Supply Chain
</li>
<li>Familiarity with major upfit vendors; constraints; ship through, ship to, licensing and overall Supply Chain processes
</li>
<li>Ability to communicate effectively, in writing and verbally, with all levels of management, customers and vendors
</li>
<li>Must have excellent customer service skills
</li>
<li>Ability to manage multiple priorities, pay close attention to detail, excellent organizational and follow up skills are required; is creative and have advanced problem solving skills
</li>
<li>Must be proficient in the Microsoft Office suite, with knowledge of ARI systems and Business Objects software
</li>
<li>Bachelor’s degree or currently enrolled in college with equivalent and relevant experience
</li>
</ul>
