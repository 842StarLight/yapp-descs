<h2>Integrated Planning And Scheduling Specialist 3 - 38496</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<ul>
<li>Prepares, develops, coordinates, integrates, analyzes and maintains, integrated plans and schedules for proposals, projects, programs and major initiatives at all levels across functional value streams to meet program and/or project objectives.
</li>
<li>Tracks, plans, schedules and performs risk analysis for projects, programs and change activities throughout the lifecycle of a program.
</li>
<li>Performs studies/analysis and planning efforts, within and/or across programs/projects, organizations or business units to determine impacts, constraints and plans involving product development, production rates, process improvement, or program initiatives.
</li>
<li>Develops the Integrated Master Plan, Integrated Master Schedule and supporting elements, and drives program planning through the Enterprise Gated Process in support of Program requirements and objectives.
</li>
<li>Performs integration of program plans and schedules, horizontally and vertically, across company functional, product groups, suppliers and partners. Identifies, analyzes and reports schedule performance variances.
</li>
<li>Performs schedule-related risk and opportunity analyses and provides potential solutions to management.
</li>
<li>Works with customers to develop performance recovery plans and incorporates in relevant documentation where appropriate.
</li>
<li>Performs schedule, event and change impact analysis.
</li>
<li>Oversees implementation planning for change requirements and ensures commitment of stakeholders.
</li>
<li>Develops and posts planning, scheduling and change related metrics/reports.
</li>
<li>Maintains disciplined revision control of all planning/schedule baselines.</li>
</ul>
<p>Education/experience typically acquired through advanced education (e.g. Bachelor) and typically 6 or more years' related work experience or an equivalent combination of education and experience (e.g. Master+4 years' related work experience, 10 years' related work experience, etc.).</p>
