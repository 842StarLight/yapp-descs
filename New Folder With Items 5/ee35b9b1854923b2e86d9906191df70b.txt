<p><b>About IWD</b></p>
<p>
IWD is a retail-thinking company providing software &amp; services to help brands achieving merchandising, excellence, &amp; increase profitability. We are a happy team of Engineers Client success managers, Developers, and Retail experts. We are always looking for positive, hard-working, and talented people to join our teams in LA, NYC, Paris, &amp; Singapore.</p>
<br>
<p>
<b>
Job description</b></p>
<p><b>
What you will do</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Review and expand our current clients &amp; prospects database to identify new business opportunities (including cold calling).</li>
<li>Generate leads to transform opportunities into sales: meet or exceed semester sales goals and follow up the sales.</li>
<li>Discover prospects’ needs and use your sharp communication skills to build and present sales proposals.</li>
<li>Manage your own portfolio of prospects, book, attend and lead meetings with them.</li>
<li>Work closely with the LA Director to discover new opportunities for growth.</li>
<li>Attend networking events and tradeshows to build a strong network within the West Coast retail industry.</li>
</ul>
<b>Preferred experience</b>
<p><i>NO SPONSORSHIP FOR A VISA: YOU MUST ALREADY HAVE THE AUTHORIZATION TO WORK IN THE USA (US citizen or green card, visa)</i><br>
</p>
<p><b>What we are looking for</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Enjoy working in a start-up environment with a great team.</li>
<li>Retail Brand Experience A Plus.</li>
<li>Located in Santa Monica.</li>
<li>A desire to talk about the IWD's offer via multiple channels (phone, email, LinkedIn) without being overly pushy.</li>
<li>Be curious &amp; open-minded, motivated, responsible, independent, flexible and have a can-do attitude.</li>
<li>Education: Bachelor’s Degree or higher.</li>
<li>Skills: Excellent interpersonal, verbal and written communication skills and a good knowledge of Microsoft Office.</li>
<li>Curiosity: staying up to date on the competition as well as industry trends in US retail and technologies.</li>
<li>Organization: This position requires solid organizational skills &amp; strong attention to detail.</li>
<li>Experience: Professional experience in B2B sales required (2-5 years), ideally for a SaaS company.</li>
</ul>
