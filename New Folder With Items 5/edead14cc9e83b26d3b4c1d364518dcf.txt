<h2>CX Developer/Analyst</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div><div><div>

<p><b>Job Summary:</b></p>
<div><div>
<div><b>Job Summary:</b></div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div>
Techtronic Industries Power Equipment, a subsidiary of TTI, has an opening in its Anderson, SC office for a CX Developer to join the CRM &amp; Marketing IT Team supporting the Sales, Marketing, Customer Service, and Warranty teams in their Customer Experience and e-commerce initiatives.</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div><b>
Experience:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Three years' experience working on enterprise software projects employing the ABSL language in the Hybris C4C environment; Both ABSL and BODL languages, and the S/4 environment preferred.</li>
<li>Two years' experience working on utilizing SAP PI/XI, SAP CPI, or equivalent enterprise middleware such as Oracle Fusion or Apache Camel.</li>
<li>Strong working understanding of CRM data patterns and structures, industry best practices, and the compliance and risk management requirements surrounding Personally Identifiable Information.</li>
<li>Previous hands on experience implementing integrations utilizing the RESTful OData and SOAP web services.</li>
<li>Self-initiation of personal or academic projects which demonstrate technical curiosity and eagerness to understand and employ emerging technology.</li>
<li>Awareness of IT best practices regarding code quality, documentation, security, and performance.</li>
<li>In depth understanding of code repositories such as Git or Subversion.</li>
<li>Ability to perform basic Windows and Linux system administration tasks sufficient to independently maintain software packages on development environments and development workstations.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<div></div>
<div>
</div>
<div><b>Job Responsibilities:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Work with IT team members, external partners, and business functional experts to maintain and enhance the integration, automation, user experience, and internal operations of Hybris C4C Platforms and their supporting middleware and micro services.</li>
<li>Collaboratively develop integration plans to support new initiatives and projects. Rapidly analyze new platforms to be integrated, develop and test functional hypotheses, and work independently to implement custom integration scenarios.</li>
<li>Actively participate in performance tuning and debugging sessions within the Hybris C4C landscape and ancillary systems.</li>
<li>Evaluate quarterly cloud platform patches and rapidly develop solutions to maintain integration scenarios and custom functionality.</li>
<li>Develop and maintain technical documentation of enhancements and configuration changes.</li>
<li>Participate in continuous improvement, identifying technology and process improvements to benefit the operations of both the business and IT business systems.</li>
<li>Eagerly learn and adapt to new technologies, languages, platforms, and environments.</li>
<li>Perform independent research and critically evaluate source material for suitability.</li>
<li>Apply analytical thinking in a fast paced environment to develop and implement technical solutions to meet any and all requirements that may arise.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<div></div>
<div>
</div>
<div><b>Minimum Requirements</b></div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div>
Education:</div>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor's Degree from a four year regionally accredited university in Computer Science, Computer Information Systems, or a related information technology field, or five years professional experience in software development and systems integration.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<div>
</div>
<div>Knowledge and Skills:</div>
<ul>
<li>Awareness of IT best practices regarding code quality, documentation, security, and performance.</li>
<li>Ability to effectively document code and configuration changes and communicate technical concepts in a clear and concise manner.</li>
<li>Familiarity with the concepts and applications of RESTful APIs.</li>
<li>Basic understanding of code repositories such as Git or Subversion.</li>
<li>Basic understanding of core IT infrastructure concepts such as server-client architecture, enterprise networking, and the underpinnings of the internet.</li>
<li>Ability to perform basic Windows and Linux system administration tasks sufficient to independently maintain software packages on development environments and development workstations.</li>
<li>Ability to perform basic database tasks sufficient to create schemas, tables, indexes, and sequences, and generate test data for development scenarios.</li>
<br>
</ul>
