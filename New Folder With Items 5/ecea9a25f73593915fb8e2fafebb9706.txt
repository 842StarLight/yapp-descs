<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>About the job</b></h2>
<p>
Google's line of products and services to our clients never stops growing. The Partnerships Development team is responsible for seeking and exploring new opportunities with Google's partners. Equipped with your business acumen and extensive product knowledge, you are right on the front line of interacting with our partners, and helping them find ways to grow using Google's newest product offerings. Your knowledge of relevant verticals and relationships with key industry players will help shape our great applications and content for products such as YouTube, Google TV and Commerce.</p>
<p>
With a particular focus on Broadcast, Media and Entertainment publishers, you drive monetization efforts amidst a rapidly changing landscape as consumer viewing habits shift from linear to digital. Your knowledge of programmatic buying/selling and the video ads ecosystem, combined with your communication skills and analytical abilities shapes how new and existing businesses grow. You leverage your entrepreneurial drive to target, educate and persuade new customers to embrace Google's latest advertising products and technologies. Using your influencing and relationship-building skills, you provide Google-caliber client service, research and market analysis. You know our clients’ business goals and strategies inside and out. You anticipate how decisions are made, understand the details of individual campaigns and persistently explore and uncover the business needs of clients. Working with them, you set the vision and the strategy for how to maximize ad growth.
</p>
<p>Google’s Global Partnerships team powers extraordinary user experiences through partnerships that solve users’ needs, advance our partners’ goals, and further Google’s mission. Our team provides meaningful solutions — from helping publishers and developers grow their businesses through monetization solutions, to helping our long-term partners anticipate and navigate change in a way that delivers economic and reputational value. We build and distribute products through partnerships across a multitude of product areas, including Ads, Search &amp; Assistant, Geo, Platforms &amp; Ecosystems, Devices &amp; Services, Health, Retail, Payments, Next Billion Users, and more. We collaborate across Google, ensuring a coordinated approach when engaging with our most complex and strategic partners.</p>

<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Responsibilities</b></h2>
<ul>
<li>Understand full scope of a publisher partner monetization business including inventory trends, direct and indirect channel priorities, competitors used. Understand how monetization fits into larger business strategy.</li>
<li>Expand Google’s monetization growth by identifying new inventory and monetization products to support the partner’s business growth.</li>
<li>Analyze monetization performance and present findings to key clients, influencing partner strategy by highlighting key opportunities to increase yield through product usage. Identify opportunities to add new/emerging channels (e.g., mobile and video) to product mi and bring data-driven insights to support recommendations.</li>
<li>Drive Quarterly Business Reviews with senior executives.</li>
<li>Optimize usage of Google’s monetization suite in consultation with partners based on performance analysis, e.g. block list management, CPM thresholds, ad positioning; develop frameworks to evaluate success of implemented optimizations.</li>
</ul>
