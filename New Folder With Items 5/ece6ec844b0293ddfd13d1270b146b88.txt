<h2>Senior Sales Trainer - Clinical Products</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Wolters Kluwer Health's Clinical Effectiveness (CE) organization is a fast-growing and innovation-driven healthcare information technology (HIT) provider working on the front lines of clinical care. Our talented team of physician and pharmacist editors, technologists, and product visionaries collaborate to provide advanced clinical decision support solutions that measurably improve clinical effectiveness by helping healthcare professionals provide optimal care for their patients.
</p>
<p></p>
<p>The <b>Senior Sales Trainer - Clinical Products </b>will play a pivotal role in the on-going learning and development of all sales employees within the organization. Using a variety of training methods, the Senior Sales Trainer will have the responsibility of developing, planning and conducting training and certification programs for employees under the leadership of the Director, Global Training &amp; Knowledge Management.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b><i>***This position will ideally be based in Waltham, MA, or Chicago, IL but highly qualified remote candidates will also be considered.
</i></b></p>
<p></p>
<p><b>ESSENTIAL DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Design sales training programs based on adult learning principles through a variety of methods including remote and face to face instructor led training
</li>
<li>Provide support to the Global Sales and Customer Experience Leadership teams
</li>
<li>Responsible for product training within Clinical Effectiveness
</li>
<li>Responsible for working with product teams within Clinical effectiveness to learn about upcoming product launches and updates
</li>
<li>In partnership with SME's develop content to continually enhance and update product training across Clinical Effectiveness
</li>
<li>Develop and maintain the product certification process for Clinical effectiveness
</li>
<li>Support the execution, maintenance, and on-going improvement of new hire on-boarding and certification programs
</li>
<li>Work with Sales and Customer Experience leadership to identify training needs and manage the creation and execution of new training programs across the organization
</li>
<li>Act as a liaison between various departments and build relationships with SMEs within the organization
</li>
<li>Performs other duties as assigned.
</li>
</ul>
