<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Position Summary:</b></h2>
<div>Working closely with the Office of the Chief Scientific Officer/ Internal &amp; External Affairs Communication Leader, the Manager, Enterprise Innovation Communication is responsible for supporting the creation, development and implementation of an integrated strategy to advance the priorities of the Office of the Chief Scientific Officer and advance the reputation of the company as a leading innovator and champion of science.
</div>
<div>You will work to build awareness of Johnson &amp; Johnson as a company that harnesses the best ideas to bring breakthrough healthcare innovations to everyone, everywhere. You also will drive programs that fuel public engagement and trust in science, and celebrate the critical role of scientific innovation in advancing the trajectory of health for humanity.
</div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities:</b></h2>
<ul>
<li>Help develop and lead execution of programs within the Champions of Science initiative designed to position Johnson &amp; Johnson as a company that celebrates science and scientists and their impact on society, including the Dr. Paul Janssen Award for Biomedical Research, the Johnson Medals, and the Champions of Science Storytelling Challenge.</li>
<li>
Support external positioning and internal awareness of the company’s thought-leadership in advancing STEM education, science journalism, science engagement, public funding of basic science and other science policy issues.</li>
<li>
Lead planning and coordination of events and activities: manage vendors, budgets and timelines, collaborate with business and communications partners across the enterprise as well as external partners and stakeholders.</li>
<li>
Working with the Office of the Chief Scientific Officer Communication Leader, develop the organizational communication plan for the Chief Scientific Officer; lead implementation of various plan elements, including presentation development, speech writing and developing social media posts and website content.
</li>
<li>Work with business segment leaders, sector business and R&amp;D communication colleagues to roll up the overall content and materials needed to build the J&amp;J position in innovation and R&amp;D. Work with members of the GCA team to ensure the innovation story reaches all J&amp;J employees and stakeholders across a variety of channels. Work collaboratively with other members of the Innovation, Global Public Health and Science Policy Communication team to ensure consistency across communications initiatives.
</li>
<li>Establish and implement processes to enable the Innovation, Global Public Health and Science Policy Communication team be a best-in-class team and deliver on key objectives, including processes for capturing and reporting progress against goals, processes for effectively reaching key stakeholders, enabling the sharing of information and best practices among team members.
</li>
<li>Help manage a team of freelancers and a broad network of consultants and agency resources.
</li>
<li>Play an active role in global communication initiatives and special projects, as needed.</li>
</ul>
