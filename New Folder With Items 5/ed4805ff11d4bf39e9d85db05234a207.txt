<h2>Division Head, Mechanical</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>The Division Head of our Mechanical team is responsible for leading the division to deliver the highest quality of service to our clients. The Division Head will create a vision for specific objectives to deliver revenue growth, profitability, and market share. The RVP works closely with senior members of operations, business development, and company leadership - interacting directly with clients and prospective clients to understand needs and opportunities.</p>
<p><b>Job Description</b></p>
<ul><li>Responsible for the overall operations, leadership and management of a portfolio of clients within a specific division.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Optimizes strategic partners and relationships to initiate appropriate strategies to enhance customer services and fiscal stability of the business relationship.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Develops and assesses business data necessary to support strategic decision-making including with emphasis on assessing revenue, profitability and business development.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Identify unmet needs in the marketplace and develop strategies to expand or launch offerings.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Determine the market value for service line components and deploy pricing strategies to support profit optimization and market competitiveness.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Standardized Methodologies – Develop standardized methodologies for client solutions, service delivery, pricing and proposing the cleaning and janitorial offering.</li></ul>
<ul><li>P&amp;L management and analysis.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Functional and Organizational Planning.</li></ul>
<ul>
<li>Develop a staffing plan that is supported by financial planning (revenue and growth).</li>
<li>Ensure availability of sufficient labor resources to execute services.</li>
<li>Manage operations team and service resources to achieve excellence in service execution at or above margin targets.</li>
<li>Manage all other aspects of personnel and business.</li>
<li>Other duties as assigned</li>
</ul>
<p><b>DESIRED SKILLS AND REQUIREMENTS</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Technical knowledge of all Mechanical aspects, specifically in the state of Massachusetts.</li>
<li>Good written and verbal communication skills.</li>
<li>Ability to proactively establish and maintain effective working relations with employees and other departments.</li>
<li>Experience with the development and maintenance of budgets, labor costs, and monitoring financial information</li>
</ul>
<ul><li>Strong interpersonal and organization skills required, demonstrating competence in a rapidly changing, growth environment.</li></ul>
<ul>
<li>Strong customer service focus is required.</li>
<li>Strong interpersonal skills; able to effectively influence others through positive, proactive communication style.</li>
<li>Excellent written and oral communication skills and sound problem-solving and decision-making capabilities.</li>
<li>Adept at dealing with all levels of management, possessing strong consultative and communication skills.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>REQUIREMENTS</b></p>
<ul><li>Bachelor's degree, Basic Management Experience.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Over ten years’ experience in the Mechanical industry, with at least five years in a management position.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Competency in all processes and procedures related to the management, sales and service execution in the commercial cleaning/facilities managed services.</li></ul>
<p>Very strong written and verbal skills.</p>
<ul><li>Ability to develop and present a compelling value proposition.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Understanding of Mechanical labor management in Union and non-Union environments.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Able to read and interpret financial data related to service performance.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Microsoft office (Word, excel, PowerPoint and outlook) CRM related software.</li></ul>
<p>Job Type: Full-time</p>
<p>Benefits:</p>
<ul>
<li>Health insurance</li>
<li>Dental insurance</li>
<li>Vision insurance</li>
<li>Retirement plan</li>
<li>Paid time off</li>
</ul>
