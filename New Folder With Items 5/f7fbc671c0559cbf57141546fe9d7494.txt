<p><b>Summary</b></p>
<p><b>Field Service Representative</b></p>
<p>At our Company, we grow People, Brands, and Businesses! We are seeking adynamicField Service Representative (FSR) who will be responsible for in-home pre- and post-installation inspections in their assigned territories to maximize retail customer satisfaction increasing customer commitment and brand sales. He/she will be responsible for making sure a customer’s installation project can move forward. This role will also manage assigned brand store displays and marketing collateral.</p>
<p>Take this opportunity to join North America’s leading business solutions provider and build your career working with amazing people in a growing industry! Apply today!</p>

<p><b>Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>

<li><p>
Ensure that all in-home deliveries include:</p></li>
<li><p>
