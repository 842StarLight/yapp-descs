<h2>Fitness Concierge - early a.m. shift</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<div><div><div>
<p>Are you friendly, flexible and enjoy working as a part of a team? Asphalt Green is looking to hire part time staff for our Fitness Center Welcome Desks at our Battery Park City location. <b>We are specifically looking to fill early morning shifts starting at 5:30 am to noon weekdays with the opportunity for more hours.</b> The role involves both member interaction and support to the management team. The goal is to ensure a high quality member experience. Responsibilities include but are not limited to the following:</p>
<ul>
<li>Meet and greet members and guests. Respond to phones and emails;</li>
<li>Using MindBody software, assist members with class and appointment scheduling;</li>
<li>Process personal training sales;</li>
<li>Data entry/retrieval of membership information via AG database;</li>
<li>Communicate schedule updates to staff and members;</li>
<li>Respond to questions on pricing, trainers, classes, etc.</li>
<li>On a daily basis, monitor supplies and equipment conditions</li>
<li>Maintain studios and Fitness Center spaces and fold/replenish towels</li>
<li>Monitor and report incidents</li>
<br>
</ul>
</div></div></div>
<p></p>
<div>Qualifications</div>
<div><div>
<div><p>Qualified candidates will be highly reliable, positive and professional and enjoy interacting with members and guests. Strong computer skills are a must as that is how we schedule sessions and track business. Good phone and email skills are also required for success in this busy role. Must also be able to stand for long periods of time and lift 50 lbs.</p></div>
