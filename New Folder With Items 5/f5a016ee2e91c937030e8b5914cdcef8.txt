<div><b>
JOB SUMMARY</b></div>
<div>
Individuals in this position will enroll, explain and promote club membership. Player’s Club Hosts maintain positive customer relations and accurate records, track current information on game and customer activity, and provide valet services.</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div><b>
ESSENTIAL DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES</b></div>
<ul>
<li> Must possess knowledge of the games that are entitled to Club benefits (to a level to explain games to new players and general knowledge of department’s operating policies).</li>
<li> Provides coverage for the Player’s Club Center, the Valet area, as well as the entrance to the casino.</li>
<li> Works the gaming area, checking with Club members and recruiting new club members.</li>
<li> Uses the public address system to promote Club and to moderate special events.</li>
<li> Must possess the knowledge of how to check out and return a cash bank from the main cashier.</li>
<li> Must know opening and closing procedures for the Club and be able to train other Team Members on these procedures.</li>
<li> Must possess knowledge of the Infogenesis system including how to ring-up cash and credit sales.</li>
<li> Maintains a neat and well-groomed personal appearance.</li>
<li> Is friendly, outgoing, polite and courteous at all times.</li>
<li> Provides answers and information to patrons, including player cards, flyers, pins, or other media.</li>
<li> Promotes the games and promotes membership into the Player’s Club.</li>
<li> Keeps abreast of all Club benefits and all events of the Casino.</li>
<li> Issues prizes and rewards to Club members.</li>
<li> Accurately enters and updates player information, points earned and points redeemed into the system.</li>
<li> Accurately accounts for all gifts/rewards items distributed and prepares related documentation.</li>
<li> Maintains the security of the Player’s Club.</li>
<li> Maintains the confidentiality of Player’s Club members.</li>
<li> Resolves disputes or finds proper person to resolve problem matters.</li>
<li> Recommends gift items, tracks selection, and takes inventory.</li>
<li> Maintains the advertising literature in the front of the Casino.</li>
<li> Takes promotional pictures and maintains the records of pictures.</li>
<li> Other duties as assigned by management, including any supervisor, manager, or executive.</li>
</ul>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div><b>
ADDITIONAL DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES SPECIFIC TO VALET</b></div>
<ul>
<li> Assists guests promptly and courteously with Valet check-in and checkout.</li>
<li> Welcomes and greets all guests in a friendly, upbeat, and positive manner using the guest’s name when possible and recognizing all VIP guests.</li>
<li> Opens vehicle doors, assists guests in stepping out of their vehicles, and helps guests with luggage or parcels.</li>
<li> Greets everyone at all times. Smiles, makes eye contact and verbally greets guests and open doors for them both at arrival and departure from the property.</li>
<li> Runs/jogs safely to ensure promptness and urgency of vehicle parking/retrieval at all times, in accordance with established safety guidelines.</li>
<li> Drives and parks vehicles in designated areas of the property, then accurately records the location of the vehicle onto a Valet receipt, which is attached to the appropriate keys and stored in the Valet office until the guest is ready to leave the property.</li>
<li> Effectively communicates with the Guest Services and Security staff.</li>
<li> Promptly clears ramp area of cars to be parked.</li>
</ul>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div><b>
Promotes the following within the department and among all Team Members:</b></div>
<ul>
<li> Creates an atmosphere of FUN for all casino guests.</li>
<li> Encourages mutual respect, dignity and integrity with all Team Members, by setting positive examples at all times.</li>
<li> Maintains a professional departmental, company and community reputation.</li>
</ul>

Blue Lake Casino &amp; Hotel requires Team Members, on a continual basis:</b></div>

<li> To maintain a professional departmental, company and community reputation.</li>
