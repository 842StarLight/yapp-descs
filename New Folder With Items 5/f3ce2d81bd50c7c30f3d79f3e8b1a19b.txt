<h2>Senior Email Marketing Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Why you're here: </b></p>
<p>Your savvy when it comes to email marketing. You thrive in a fast-paced environment and love to multitask. You’re looking for an innovative team where your contributions matter in the digital marketing space.</p>
<p><b>What you'll be doing: </b></p>
<p>The Senior Email Marketing Specialist will work within the Salesforce Marketing Cloud platform and perform coding, QA, sending and analysis of up to 10 campaigns per week along with global localizations and translations. The specialist will also have the opportunity to work on broader digital marketing projects across multiple disciplines.</p>
<ul>
<li>Review daily email schedule in Basecamp. Prioritize work.</li>
<li>Create marketing emails using HTML/CSS and Content Builder using email marketing best practices.</li>
<li>Upload, manage settings, test, and deploy marketing emails.</li>
<li>Build and maintain email lists, using a mix of Data Extensions, Filters and SQL queries</li>
<li>Complete ad-hoc marketing email related projects as needed.</li>
<li>QA deployments and email communications to ensure a flawless send.</li>
<li>Measure and analyze email and campaign performance metrics, develop reports and generate insights to continuously iterate and improve engagement</li>
<li>Perform end to end quality checks, ensuring content accuracy, functioning links/landing pages, assessing segmentation, and produce campaigns in a timely manner. Test creative integrity through Email On Acid.</li>
<li>Keep up to date with email marketing best practices and strategies</li>
<li>Create and analyze email consumer journeys and automations.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>What you need: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Minimum 3 year of experience sending marketing emails using one or more of the following platforms: Exact Target/Salesforce Marketing Cloud (strong plus), or other platform such as MailChimp, Cordial, Iterable or other enterprise level system</li>
<li>Strong working knowledge of email specific HTML/CSS</li>
<li>Basic Photoshop understanding</li>
<li>MS Excel Skills</li>
<li>SQL Experience (a strong plus)</li>
<li>Understanding of email marketing principles including CAN SPAM and best practices.</li>
<li>Proficient with MS Office, Basecamp and similar productivity tools.</li>
<li>Excellent teamwork and communication skills; also works well independently</li>
<li>Direct selling, beauty or fashion industry experience a plus</li>
<li>Strong software aptitude and fast learner</li>
</ul>
