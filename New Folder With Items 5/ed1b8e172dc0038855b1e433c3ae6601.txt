The Director is responsible for cost, schedule and technical delivery for their assigned portfolio and will be a primary interface with SMC Directorates. This position manages and delivers on corporate objectives including return on sales, product delivery (to include hours delivered), product quality and business growth. The position is located in the area of El Segundo, CA. The position requires frequent visits to additional BAE Systems and/or Customer locations.<br>
