<p><b>
Position Overview</b></p>
<p>
Drive truck equipped with concrete mixer to deliver ready mix concrete to job sites and perform specific customer service tasks.</p>
<p><b>
Key Responsibilities (Essential Duties and Functions)</b></p>
<p>
The duties and responsibilities include but are not limited to the following:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Be able to ingress and egress truck by navigating 2 steps with grab bars present.</li>
<li>
Be able to manipulate truck steering wheel (power steering), shifter and other various levers and knobs necessary for operation of truck and associated equipment.</li>
<li>
Operate ready-mix concrete truck to deliver to construction sites, as specified, in a safe and efficient manner, obeying applicable laws and following dispatch instructions.</li>
<li>
Maintain communication with Dispatcher or Plant Manager as necessary regarding deliveries. Check delivery tickets for special instructions and complete as required.</li>
<li>
Frequently climb 8 foot ladder to ensure proper materials are loaded in a safe manner, and obtain proper signatures as required.</li>
<li>
Rinse out drum and frequently handle 50 pound mixer chutes safely.</li>
<li>
Collect money from customers and record transactions on customer receipt.</li>
<li>
Respond to customer questions &amp; complaints related to basic product knowledge and basic concrete instruction.</li>
<li>
Monitor various gauges during operation of vehicle, investigate abnormalities and perform operator maintenance and minor field repairs.</li>
<li>
Record sales and delivery information.</li>
<li>
Maintain operator logs, forms and records in accordance with company policy and Department of Transportation regulations.</li>
<li>
Complete written report of problems or necessary repairs and forward to maintenance personnel. Detect and report improper operation, faulty equipment, defective materials and unusual conditions to proper supervision.</li>
<li>
Safely maintain work area and vehicle in a clean and orderly condition.</li>
<li>
Responsible for daily inspection of truck equipment and supplies such as tires, lights, brakes, fuel, oil and water.</li>
</ul>
<p>
Other Requirements:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Display a professional and courteous attitude to co-workers, supervisors, and the general public at all times.</li>
<li>
Must be willing to travel and work away from home when required.</li>
<li>
Must be willing to work nights and weekends when necessary.</li>
<li>
Report to the assigned job site ready to begin work at the designated start time.</li>
<li>
Strict adherence to safety requirements and procedures as outlined in the Manual of Safety Practices and Procedures.</li>
<li>
Strict adherence to Shelly Company policies and procedures as outlined in the Company Book of Policies.</li>
<li>
Willingness to work in a team environment and assist co-workers or supervisors with other duties as required.</li>
<li><b>
Timely and regular attendance is an expectation of performance for all Shelly Company employees. Employees will be held accountable for adhering to their workplace schedule.</b></li>
</ul>
