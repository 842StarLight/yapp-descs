<h2>Solutions Architect Cloud/Hosting -StL/KC</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<div>
<div><div><p>The Solution Architect is leveraged as a technical specialist on large, complex and high potential opportunities with new and existing customers. The SA is accountable for technical sales support of all CenturyLink products and services including: Managed Hosting Services, Public &amp; Private Cloud, Security/Compliance, Outsourced infrastructure solutions (managed and un-managed), and specialized application workload and IT Consulting Services.
</p></div></div>
<div>
<div>
<b>Job Description</b>
</div>
<div><ul>
<li>Generate sales opportunities for specific product/services within prospect accounts and identify and upsell opportunities within existing customers</li>
<li>
Present our vision to clients, support optimization of solution strategy and design, and drive additional revenue within your assigned sales region</li>
<li>
Perform consultative, solutions-based design for business clients who desire outsourced infrastructure options to enhance their own technical capabilities.</li>
<li>
Pre-sales design and implementation expertise in Enterprise/WAN service integration</li>
<li>
Provide technology consulting for internal contacts in Product Management and Marketing and translate prospective customer requirements into recommendations for new or enhanced products and features</li>
<li>
Develop thought leadership and gain industry recognition and credibility.</li>
<li>
Maintain current knowledge of industry trends, topics, methodologies, and core technologies in order to assimilate customer needs and design appropriate technical solutions.
</li>
</ul></div>
</div>
<div>
<div>
<b>Qualifications</b>
</div>
<div>
<ul><li>Bachelor’s Degree in Engineering, MIS, Computer Science, or Telecom plus 10 years of experience as a Sales Engineer, Solution Architect or technical consulting role</li></ul>
<ul>
<li>
Experience in requirements gathering, due diligence, solution development and proposal generation activities unique to large, complex opportunities and consulting engagements in key to success in this position</li>
<li>
Depth and breadth of expertise in Managed Hosting, Pubic Cloud, Private Cloud, Outsourced infrastructure solutions (managed and un-managed), Security, and Consulting Services related to CenturyLink solutions offerings.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Preferred Qualifications</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
10+ years’ experience in Managed Hosting Services, Public Cloud (AWS, Azure, Google), Private Cloud (VMware), Outsourced infrastructure solutions (managed and un-managed), specialized Enterprise application suites or equivalent expertise</li>
<li>
2+ years’ experience in design of distributed, high availability server-based network computing infrastructures, storage, Disaster Recovery and advanced networks</li>
<li>
2+ years’ experience in configuration and support of Microsoft Windows, UNIX, or Solaris operating systems, including remote management.</li>
</ul>
