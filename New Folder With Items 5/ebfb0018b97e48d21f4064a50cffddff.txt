<h2>Project Manager, Employee Benefit Services</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Job Title: Project Manager</b></p>
<p>
Business Services Project Manager is responsible for leading client implementations and annual renewals. They will have direct ownership of client relationships and employee enrollment while providing post enrollment support and handling client / broker / benefit platform escalations. Will be called upon to provide continuous feedback on processes, services and documentation to enhance client / broker / benefit platform experience and engagement.</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Collaborate with external and internal stakeholders to smoothly and timely implement new clients, which includes leading meetings, working with contacts on marketing materials and technical resources on system and data file setups. Goal is to create an exceptional client / broker / benefit platform experience that leads to client retention and additional sales opportunities.</li>
<li>
Manage client renewals and work with marketing resources on enrollment materials to meet client / broker / benefit platform needs and engage with technical resources on various electronic files. Guide external contacts to provide enrollments timely and internal resources to activate employee accounts on-time.</li>
<li>
Serve as client / broker internal advocate by resolving client / broker inquiries regarding benefit services, billing inquiries, employee concerns and file issues.</li>
<li>
Work with internal departments to streamline communications and processes, update CRM system, distribute print and digital marketing materials, and provide additional support as needed.</li>
<li>
Work with internal and external resources so information developed and distributed to employees will increase enrollments.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>
What makes you a good fit for this role?</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Communication Skills: Utilize excellent listening skills to gather insights. Leverage excellent verbal and written skills with the ability to adjust messaging style to influence different audiences and create broker / client / benefit platform engagement. Comfortable speaking in front of groups.</li>
<li>
Project Management: Set direction through planning and leadership, then utilize conflict management and execution to deliver initiatives on-time that meet commitments and goals.</li>
<li>
Adaptability: Ability to work independently and in a team environment. Comfortable wearing many hats and transition between multiple tasks. Adjusts well to changing situations and priorities.</li>
<li>
Technical Skills: Proficient with CRM type software and Microsoft Office, including Word, Excel, PowerPoint and Outlook. Possess technical aptitude with ability to learn new software systems and lead electronic file discussions with external technical resources.</li>
</ul>
