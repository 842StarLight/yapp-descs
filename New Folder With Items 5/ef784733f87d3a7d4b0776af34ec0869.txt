<p>This role will play a major part in driving business results for the Tablets and PC business. The successful candidate will drive and align business and marketing programs, including promotion and customer marketing initiatives and all go-to-market activities for the category within the assigned customer base. The candidate will have a background in a consumer-focused or technology industry with direct experience in product marketing, on-line, retail and customer-facing activations. This individual will work with account teams, insights teams, and retailer and carrier marketing teams to create shopper-centric initiatives to drive growth in the category. The candidate can be expected to execute and track programs that drive results and manage all aspects of market development funds, collaborating with the demand generation, channel, and account teams to ensure effective sales deduction and customer marketing spend. You must be able to establish and maintain effective working relationships with account teams, product management, and SEA and HQ support personnel
</p>
<p>Responsibilities include:
</p>
<p><i>Realize business objectives of revenue and share growth by executing effective promotional and marketing tactics to drive growth and awareness in the Tablets and PC businesses by:
</i></p>
<ul>
<li>Initiate, develop, coordinate and implement customer promotions, marketing plans and programs in conjunction with account teams.
</li>
<li>Execute marketing programs, program management and post-program evaluation. Understand and analyze data to evaluate aggregate and individual marketing programs - such as ROI, sales lift, etc.
</li>
<li>Maximizing marketing investment in channel and retail promotions, training and advocacy, etc.
</li>
<li>Interface with HQ product planning and RPM teams to drive supply chain management KPI’s, inventory management, and channel delivery within target customers.
</li>
<li>Manage weekly promotional planning, financial, and supply chain forecasting and achievement for category and customer responsibility.
</li>
<li>Lead new product launch and assortment decisions within assign accounts. Drive new and innovative product launch, engagement and promotional strategies, tactics and programs.
</li>
<li>Maintain close and frequent working relationship with account teams, product management, demand creation, and SEA marketing support to optimize sell thru initiatives.
</li>
<li>Work collaboratively with customers to understand their strategies and provide shopper insights expertise/recommendations that maximize category sales &amp; profitability.
</li>
</ul>

</p>

</li>
<li>Solid customer relationship skills. Direct retail or carrier channel experience a plus.
</li>
<li>Highly organized/detail oriented with ability to manage multiple priorities

<li>Experience in budget management and tracking.
</li>

</li>
</ul>
