<p><b>
Purpose and Objective:</b>
</p>
<p>SAP America, Inc. seeks a Business Processes Consultant for our Pittsburgh, PA office location (and various client sites nationwide) to perform application and process specific solution consulting for several application and/or process areas, including creation of business blueprints, Implementation based on a blueprint, creation of test cases, test scheduling and execution, key user training, go live support, and post go live support.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Expectations and Tasks:</b>
</p>
<p>Support escalated projects. Perform feasibility studies and solution reviews. Support pre-sales activities. Serve as a substitute project team leader when necessary. Perform detailed planning of process implementation. Define detailed blueprints for development requirements. Perform industry and corporate process implementation across all related applications, including SAP, non-SAP, OnPremise, and Cloud applications. Develop and ensure quality of process models. Utilize knowledge of modeling standards and tools to drive deployment of SAP Model Companies for specific area of business. Up to 70% travel required to various client sites nationwide.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Education and Qualifications/Skills and Competencies:</b>
</p>
<p>Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Engineering, Business Administration, or a related field and 5 years of experience. Will accept a Master's degree and 3 years of experience.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Work Experience:</b>
</p>
<p>Experience must include 3 years in the following: HANA Platform activities, including HANA SP upgrade, Revision upgrade, HANA installations, HANA Performance Tuning, HANA High Availability and Disaster Recovery Setup (HADR), and HANA Administration; Installation and configuration of SAP products, including ECC, MDG, Portal, SRM, CRM, BW, SCM, Solution Manager, Livecache, TREX, SLT, or Web Dispatcher; SAP product upgrades; SAP OSDB migrations (Heterogeneous Migrations), migration optimizations, or HANA DMO migrations; Data Center migrations (Homogeneous migrations); Setup HADR for SAP Netweaver products in Linux environments; Building cutover plans for SAP implementation projects from SAP technology perspective; Re-Architecture of VERTEX product in customer landscape to provide load balancing and improve performance of SAP Billing jobs; installations in UNIX, Linux, AIX &amp; Windows Operating systems and databases; SAP system sizing; SAP Printer management; and SAP Language Installations. Up to 70% travel required to various client sites nationwide.</p>
