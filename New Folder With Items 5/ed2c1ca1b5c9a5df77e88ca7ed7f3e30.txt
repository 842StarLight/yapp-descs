<h2>Account Executive - Strategic</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>SHI has a dynamic career opportunity for a new business development focused Account Executive within our Strategic Account Sales organization. The Account Executive’s role will focus on new business development efforts across named accounts in the Energy Market. This position will focus on new business growth in the Energy Vertical.</p>
<p>This position plays an integral role in the execution of our Enterprise Strategic acquisition strategy and targets large global customers who have strategic relationships with OEM Partners. With the guidance of the Leadership Team and SHI’s Support, Training and Development teams, a successful Account Executive will be equipped to engage and position SHI’s world class services to these customers.</p>
<p>This position will be based in the Houston or Austin areas of Texas.</p>
<p><b>Responsibilities/Job Function</b></p>
<p><b>Responsibilities include, but are not limited to: </b></p>
<p>*Work with prospects to open accounts and build SHI’s market share in the Energy vertical<br>*Uncover new sales opportunities by providing consultation to customers regarding IT solutions<br>*Effectively communicate and develop demand for SHI Services including Cloud services, Integration services, Security services, etc.<br>*Develop partnerships with Vendor Field Sales Representatives and optimize joint selling opportunities within the territory<br>*Build market awareness of SHI through participation in local/regional industry events, organizations and affiliations<br>*Develop solid business relationships within the various decision-makers and influencers at all levels of each target account<br>*Regular onsite customer visits will be required, so must be available to travel regularly to visit various customer sites throughout the US<br>*Uncover and help resolve each prospect’s business problems, map their organization and identify their unique technology needs<br>*Develop, document and execute account sales plans for each target account<br>*Build loyalty and trust within your account base by hosting regular meetings and introducing SHI resources<br>*Meeting sales targets, primarily consisting of attaining new buying accounts (NBA)</p>
<p><b>Qualifications/Minimum Skills Required</b></p>
<p><b>Qualifications: </b></p>
<p>*Minimum of 1 year sales experience with a focus on prospecting and cold calling<br>*Bachelor’s Degree from an accredited 4-year college<br>*Prior experience with IT hardware, software and/or service sales</p>
<p><b>Required Skills: </b></p>
<p>*Ability to cold call and prospect into new customers and business<br>*Ability to travel as required by the business<br>*Ability to present technical information in a way that is understood to varying audiences<br>*Ability to present oneself with a business professional appearance when meeting with customers, during meetings, etc.<br>*Strong organizational and planning skills<br>*Strong written and verbal communication skills<br>*Proficiency in MS Office tools - Outlook, Word, Excel, PowerPoint<br>*Excellent presentation skills</p>
<p><b>Preferred Skills </b></p>
<p>*Working experience and knowledge with Salescenter, BI, CRM, &amp; AX (internal posting only)<br>*Knowledge of how to data mine Linkedin for information</p>

<p><i>Innovative Solutions. World Class Support. </i></p>
