<h2>Collection Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div></div>
<div>
<div>As an AR/Collections Specialist at Syneron-Candela, you will collaborate in the AR/Collections process. Syneron-Candela’s AR/Collections team has the responsibility to accurately and timely make collection calls, post daily cash receipts, research problem</div>
<p></p>
<div>
<p><b>Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Make and receive customer service collection calls</li>
<li>Research unpaid balances</li>
<li>Send monthly statements of accounts to customers and related sales representatives</li>
<li>Document and resolve customer service issues and inquiries</li>
<li>Close and post daily cash batches</li>
<li>Process credit card transactions and post to customer accounts</li>
<li>Maintain compliance with Credit and Collection Policies</li>
<li>Ensure high-quality invoicing and collection procedures</li>
<li>Prepare daily summary of cash and reconcile receipts to the general ledger</li>
<li>Continuously enhance work methods using information technology</li>
<li>Communicate directly with the sales force</li>
<li>Stay updated on industry and legislative changes</li>
<li>Assist in the financial planning with the help of cash forecasting</li>
<li>Conduct credit checks on new customers</li>
<li>Participate in special projects to enhance A/R collections</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Experience/Requirements:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>3-5 years of experience in Credit and Collection</li>
<li>Oracle Experience preferred</li>
<li>Excellent verbal and written communication skills - must be able to work and communicate with cross-functional teams and the salesforce, business owners, and international distributors</li>
<li>Strong customer service skills</li>
<li>Must have the ability to work in an agile environment where priorities change rapidly</li>
<li>Ability to exhibit composure, patience, and confidence when facing difficult situations</li>
<li>Highly accurate and efficient data entry skills</li>
<li>Excellent organizational and time management skills</li>
<li>Problem-solving attitude</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Preferred education</b></p>
<ul><li>Associate’s or Bachelor’s degree in accounting or other business related major preferred</li></ul>
