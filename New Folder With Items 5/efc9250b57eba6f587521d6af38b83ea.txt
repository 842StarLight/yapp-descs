<h2>Inside Sales Intern</h2>

<p>For over 150 years, RR Donnelley has helped businesses connect with audiences. RRD's mission is to enable our clients to better connect with their customers. No other organization gives clients the means to connect to their customers across the entire breadth of the customer journey, from marketing programs to the critical business operations that serve and extend the brand.</p>
<p>
This Internship provides an opportunity to learn how to be successful within a large organization, while developing sales skills and strategies that will benefit you throughout your career. This role is the logical training ground for admittance to our Enterprise Sales Associate program, which is our highly regarded full time job training program. During the internship, you will work closely with our customer support and sales teams as well as management in a strong, but competitive team atmosphere. The focus of this Internship will be on Business to Business Inside Sales. We will provide initial and on-going training to guide you on the path to success.</p>
<p>
If you are seeking college credit for this internship, we can work with your school to meet your requirements.</p>
