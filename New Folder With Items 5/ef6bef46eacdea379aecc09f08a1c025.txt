<h2>Senior Assistant Customer Support</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Job Summary</b><br>Responds to basic, multiple customer inquiries and requests.<b>Responsibilities and Duties</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Answers telephones and responds to basic customer questions and/or forwards call to appropriate personnel.</li>
<li>Records call on account history record with results of the inquiry; initiates required confirmation without direct supervision. Researches customer inquiries and responds to appropriate parties in a timely manner.</li>
<li>Processes customer requests by sending faxes, returning telephone messages, sending mail documents, or using other related communication.</li>
<li>Processes calls in a manner that ensures service level agreements (SLAs) are met or exceeded. Records calls, processes requests and updates account history with results of inquiry to include proper documentation.</li>
<li>Processes and distributes incoming and outgoing mail for multiple clients in accordance with established service level agreements.</li>
<li>Performs data entry and matches documents to appropriate accounts to ensure up to date and accurate information.</li>
<li>Interfaces with team members, management, and customers in reference to customer service issues.</li>
<li>Reviews client reports on a weekly basis to ensure data integrity; prepares and submits report to management.</li>
<li>Reviews and recommends modifications to procedures and workflow as necessary to ensure efficient and effective processing of transactions.</li>
</ul>
