<b>Job Description:</b><br>
The LTG Brand and Comms Director collaborates with Group marketers to lead an integrated brand strategy across our portfolio of businesses.<br>
<br>
Managing a team of writers, communicators and designers, the Brand and Comms Director is an expert strategist who governs quality of deliverables across all brand activity.<br>
<br>
Why you’ll love it:<br>
<br>
- You will be empowered to do what you do best - drive high-quality brand activity across a Group of new and established businesses<br>
<ul><li>Partnering with marketing and service/product individuals, you will guide the creation of impactful campaign assets for lead generation and growth of brand equity</li></ul>
- Motivated by quality - you will be encouraged to make every deliverable the best it could be, whilst keeping to time and budget<br>
<ul><li>You will be working with inspirational professionals who have a shared purpose to bridge the workforce capability gap</li></ul>
- You enjoy collaborating with other disciplines - this role works closely with marketing channel Managers and Directors to ensure high-quality outputs across all brands<br>
<ul><li>You will be joining a unique group of companies that are all leaders in their field and shaping the future of work. Owned by LTG plc, we are a fast-moving company providing fantastic opportunities for exceptionally talented individuals</li></ul>
<br>
In this role you will:<br>
<br>
Ensure that all brands are:<br>
<ul>
<li>Underpinned by a well-curated strategy to bring messages alive across the Group</li>
<li>Supported by a well crafted tone-of-voice to bring business and marketing strategies to life</li>
</ul>
 - Provided with solid content outputs - short and long form, we expect laser-focused accuracy<br>
<ul>
<li>Supported with impeccable visual design</li>
<li>Enabling exceptional customer experience and value from marketing output</li>
</ul>
 - Experiencing high-quality brand application across all channels - we are only as good as what we put into the market, slick representation is essential<br>
<ul>
<li>Partner with LTG Brand and Product Marketing teams to lead and govern an outcomes-focused brand strategy for each LTG brand.</li>
<li>Work alongside LTG Head of Marketing, Marketing Managers and executive leadership to devise a `brand strategy for cross and up-sell outcomes across the Group</li>
</ul>
- Govern quality across every element of brand - for all businesses, daily<br>
<ul>
<li>Line manage a talented global team of content and design professionals for completion of deliverables to agreed deadline</li>
<li>Partner with LTG’s Demand and Digital Director to enforce a solid campaign process, owning all visual and written brand assets for pipeline generation</li>
<li>Support LTG’s Art Director to ensure a cohesive strategy for visual brand application across all marketing channels</li>
</ul>
- Own brand refresh and launch projects - working with the discipline leads to bring together each thread of the process for exceptional strategy and execution<br>
<ul>
<li>Support the public relations efforts across the Group, including news releases and editorial</li>
<li>Collaborate with LTG channel managers to support, analyse and challenge brand representation across all marketing channels (content, events, website, digital etc) for optimum outcome, suggesting innovative ways to improve brand reach where possible
</li>
</ul>

B2B audiences are your motivator<br>
