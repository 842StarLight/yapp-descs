<h2>Director of Media Sales</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>At Devada, we put software developers at the core of our business, and we are passionate about providing software development and technology professionals with the knowledge, tools, and strategies they need to build great businesses for the future! Since 2005, Devada has been empowering developers to learn, share, grow, and succeed via the technology-specific web communities we enable. DZone.com is one of the world's largest communities of user-contributed technical learning resources, and AnswerHub software powers developer relations communities for some of the biggest technology and tech-enabled companies in the world.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
We are actively seeking an experienced Director of Media Sales to build, motivate, and lead our growing media sales team. In this role, you will report directly to the General Manager and will be responsible for managing the activities of our sales team, holding them accountable to performance goals, managing the pipeline on a daily basis, deal engineering to help close sales, and personnel management. This is a highly visible role that gives you the opportunity to exercise situational leadership, develop a team of reps, and be an integral part in the growth and success of DZone's media unit!</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
As the Director of Media Sales, you will partner with the commercially focused DZone media management team, which consists of software development, product management, advertising operations, marketing, and editorial, to deliver on agreed metrics and KPIs, including driving bookings and profitability targets for the media team. This is a fantastic opportunity to join a growing company, lead a great team, and have an immediate impact on how DZone provides a differentiated editorial and marketing experience on DZone.com.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div><b>
Key Accountabilities:</b></div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
<b>
People Management:</b> The Director of Media Sales' most prominent role is their leadership of the media sales team. In this position, you will build, organize, train, and manage a team of account directors and account executives to achieve and exceed our annual bookings plan based on monthly quota achievement. You will ensure the smooth execution of the day-to-day activities within the department and serve as the main point of contact within the media sales department to the rest of the business departments. As a leader, you will drive innovation for the rest of the team, enabling continuous achievement of sales bookings targets as well as the business's overall revenue and profit targets.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
<b>
Strategy:</b> The Director of Media Sales plays a major role in strategic development within the business, where they will ensure we have the right structure for the sales team along with recruiting the right people and effectively managing performance. You will lead the design, development and implementation of a rigorous and effective sales rep training program that establishes rules of engagement, scripts, communication protocols, and closing tools. You will develop programs that drive creativity and productivity; create an atmosphere that stimulates learning, development, and challenge. In this role, you will be responsible for increasing the competencies across the sales team by working directly with individuals and training the team on selling best practices.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
<b>
Collaboration:</b> The role of the Director of Media Sales is highly collaborative and, as such, works closely with the advertising operations, product and editorial/content teams to continuously improve the way we position, message, promote, and sell products. In this capacity, you will work inter-and-cross-departmentally to inform and implement sales operational improvements. The Director of Advertising Operations will liaise with all departments with a view of exceeding sponsor expectations and building long-lasting trusted advisor relationships.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div><b>
What We Look For:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
7+ years of direct experience in selling media products to IT and software marketing organizations combined
</li>
<li>3+ years of proven sales management experience in a fast-paced, high-volume environment
</li>
<li>Proficiency in data-driven approaches to pipeline management leveraging tools such as Salesforce and Tableau</li>
<li>
Exceptional leadership, communication, and change management capabilities, with the ability to move a group towards a unified direction and with a common vision</li>
<li>
Demonstrated ability to establish strong relationships and collaborate with cross-functional peers, managers, subordinates, and partners to achieve business results
</li>
<li>Ability to provide an articulate point-of-view while speaking with diverse audiences, including clients ranging from CMO's (and their teams) as well as CRO's, CCO's and CFO's
</li>
<li>Data driven and analytical mindset with knowledge and passion for media, data and technology
</li>
<li>Highly motivated &amp; driven to deliver results that meet &amp; exceed goals
</li>
<li>Passion for improving global standards for customer satisfaction and a strong desire to maintain a customer focus</li>
<li>
Prior experience defining and managing to compensation plans</li>
<li>
Bachelor's Degree in Business Administration, Marketing, or related field of study required</li>
</ul>
