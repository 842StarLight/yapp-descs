<h2>Office Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Office Manager</b></p>
<p></p>
<br>
<div><b>
About the job</b></div>
<div>
You're at the heart of the company's business operations and activities and the soul that keeps your team moving forward. Our company designs, develops, and manufactures a variety of façade systems, interior wall and ceiling systems and custom art installations. Our widely published company is headquartered in a fast-paced design office and manufacturing facility in LA. You anticipate the needs of your managers and team members and help them stay focused on their projects by resolving operational and administrative issues before they arise. You move quickly with the changing environment and are up to date with the latest Arktura products and services, and connected to our external sales team's needs. You also use that knowledge to strategically support your team. In addition to being organized and analytical, you possess the strong business judgment and communication skills needed to interact with a variety of people and job functions.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div><b>
What you'll achieve:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
Build efficiency and effective responsiveness into existing operations and help define new operational strategies.</li>
<li>
Anticipate current and future needs while creating detailed action plans to get ahead of them.</li>
<li>
Greet and assist guests in a professional, helpful and enthusiastic manner. You will be the face of Arktura!</li>
<li>
Mentor new team members.</li>
<li>
Serve as a technical lead for, and manager of, small projects.</li>
<li>
Provide support on purchasing, printing, meeting notes, presentations, facilities, etc.</li>
<li>
Take ownership of projects by managing logistics for on-boarding, events, client visits, and more.</li>
<li>
Handle inquiries quickly, proactively, and follow through on tasks to successful completion.</li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<br>
<div><b>
About you:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
Have 3+ years of administrative assistant, Office manager, project manager, event planner, or similar experience managing and supporting multiple senior leaders and large teams.</li>
<li>
Are able to prioritize business needs and make trade-offs across all stakeholders accordingly.</li>
<li>
Are proactive and motivated to iterate on current processes and build efficient systems.</li>
<li>
Are technologically savvy with the ability to quickly adapt to new applications and tools.</li>
<li>
Are a self-starter who can determine priorities and make trade offs when needs change.</li>
<li>
Have discretion and sensitivity towards confidential information.</li>
<li>
Have an incredible attention to detail especially when it comes to written communications, projects, and presentations.</li>
<li>
Have professional polish in both internal and external interactions and the ability to exhibit grace and composure under pressure.</li>
<li>
Are committed to collaboration and teamwork—you're a believer in "it's what we do and how we work"</li>
<li>
Understand the highly flexible nature of support work and are comfortable with being available as needed</li>
<li>
Believe customer service is key and that learning what teams need is key to a seamless support role</li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<br>
<div><b>
Preferred qualifications:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
Proven ability to consistently and positively contribute in a high-paced, changing work environment with the ability to prioritize multiple functions and tasks and manage work time efficiently.</li>
<li>
Excellent communication and interpersonal skills; proven ability to take initiative and build strong, productive relationships.</li>
<li>
Affinity towards design and an appreciation of the process of making.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Requirements</b>:</p>
<ul>
<li>
 Bilingual is a plus</li>
<li>
 High school diploma/GED required, AA degree preferred</li>
<li>
 Regularly lift and/or move objects 10-50 pounds</li>
<li>
 Ability to climb, stoop, kneel, reach, push, pull, grasp, and perform repetitive motions</li>
<li>
 Regularly stand, walk, and sit</li>
</ul>

Benefits and Perks</b></div>

The Benefits! - Include health care, 401K, paid time off and professional development.</li>

Working at Arktura is the best! We have a casual dress code, free snacks, and even an on-site gym!</li>
</ul>

<p><b>
About Arktura:</b></p>
<p>
At Arktura, we make design happen. Creativity, expert knowledge and cutting-edge technology shape everything we do whether it's exploring form and function in our standard architectural product, adapting our pre-configured architectural systems to meet project needs, or partnering with clients through our Solutions Studio. Our multidisciplinary team brings together architects, designers, programmers, engineers and artists with a deep understanding of the creative process. We use our collective ingenuity to bridge the gaps between design intent and manufacturing capabilities, creating proprietary software and machinery to meet evolving demands.<br>
