<h2>Vice President, New Business Development</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p><b>Overview</b></p>
<p>This is a high-visibility position within a growing data and analytics provider targeting top B2C marketing organizations. The successful candidate will be a strong business development leader with demonstrated skills in developing a holistic view of each client opportunity and executing new business and/or service expansion strategies. Must be a subject matter expert in selling data and capable of defining effective data-driven marketing strategies and developing a compelling business case for test and adoption of proposed solutions. Alliant is seeking a proven industry professional to lead its sales and business development team. This position requires an innovative mind-set and a strong desire to deliver customer satisfaction while leading a high performing sales team. This position reports to Senior Vice President of Revenue.</p>
<p><b>Principle Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Identify, develop and lead new business development, overseeing all new business account contact and sales activity</li>
<li>Lead and develop a team of high performance new business executives in support of client cultivation and conversion</li>
<li>Set sales goals, develop forecasts, maintain standard pipeline, track opportunities, manage the sales fulfillment process and report regularly on team progress to executive management</li>
<li>Align Alliant audience targeting, campaign optimization and analytic solutions to customer applications; prepare and communicate solution overview, pricing, proposals, and negotiate the terms and conditions of the sale</li>
<li>Update CRM management information daily and document sales activity, transactions, account, client information and revenue opportunities as required</li>
<li>Partner with Account Management team to transition new business as well as grow existing accounts in support of corporate growth strategies</li>
<li>Consistently achieve and exceed assigned sales targets; maximize profitability and new business financial performance by managing costs and customer pricing</li>
<li>Partner with marketing team to research, create and manage prospect funnel</li>
<li>Actively participate in corporate planning, product development and team building activities</li>
<li>Partner with senior leadership to set new business strategy and organizational structure to maximize the business</li>
<li>Work closely with internal and external teams as required to manage client solution fulfillment</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Qualifications and Skills:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree in sales, marketing, or business field; MBA or equivalent preferred</li>
<li>10+ years’ proven experience in data sales and/or account management in a data driven marketing or technical/analytic environment, ideally within marketing-services organizations</li>
<li>Demonstrated mastery of and passion for the art of selling, a love of data and an understanding of how to utilize the power of analytics to support customer goals</li>
<li>Deep knowledge and hands-on experience as a vendor or end-user within direct, digital and/or database marketing; relevant sales contacts within direct-to-consumer digital and direct marketing organizations a plus</li>
<li>A proven ability to build, mentor and lead successful sales teams</li>
<li>Must demonstrate solid listening skills and a consultative sales approach, with proven ability to identify and build relationships with C-Level executives and key decision makers</li>
<li>Demonstrated ability to develop and expand client relationships and have a proven record of exceeding revenue goals</li>
<li>Working knowledge of marketing analytics and reading model gains charts a plus</li>
<li>Experience using a CRM database or similar software applications</li>
<li>Outstanding writing and presentation skills, with an intermediate knowledge of Microsoft Office, including Word, Excel and PowerPoint</li>
<li>Position requires external client interaction, must have the ability to accommodate travel to client sites and industry events</li>
<li>Must be flexible, detailed, team-oriented and committed to having a good time while doing a great job</li>
</ul>
