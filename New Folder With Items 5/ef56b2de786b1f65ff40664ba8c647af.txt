<p><b>The role:</b></p>
<p>
This position delivers services to support the client’s day-to-day needs related to the use of ticketing systems and products. The CSS will be responsible for maintaining agreed upon service levels. They will follow-up as needed with national teams to ensure we are providing timely problem resolution. Responsibilities will also include training, sharing of best practices, and general operational support including installs and upgrades.</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>
What the job is:</b></p>
<p><b>
Client Service</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Develop and maintain excellent client relationships</li>
<li>
Meet and exceed client service level agreements</li>
<li>
Advise and assist with reporting</li>
<li>
Coordinate upgrades and hardware replacements at client sites</li>
<li>
Advise/educate clients within procedural guidelines to ensure a complete solution to their technical or service questions</li>
<li>
Maintain customer contacts to enable accurate tracking and reporting</li>
<li>
Provide high level marketing support on Ticketmaster no-cost solutions</li>
<li>
Provide onsite event support and after hours office support</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Ticketmaster ONE, Host System &amp; Access Control Support</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Working knowledge of Ticketmaster ONE web portal</li>
<li>
Remain current with new software/product releases for Host, Presence, AccessManager</li>
<li>
Create/modify reports, including Autypes, Repgens, Mopreps, and other advanced reporting</li>
<li>
 Support season ticketing, Presence/AccessManager/eEntry support customization (rules, exceptions, etc.)</li>
<li>
Assist with client onboarding and ongoing maintenance</li>
<li>
Act as the expert in all facets of Access Control products</li>
<li>
Basic knowledge of event programming as it relates to sales channels and consumer experience</li>
<li>
Knowledge of the Event Base product suite</li>
<li>
Identifies and assist in resolving event programming related errors</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Product Support</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Communicate product updates, new features, and functionality to client base</li>
<li>
Provide support and best practices to the client for all TM products</li>
<li>
Assist with new manifest creation</li>
<li>
Establish any special client MOP types</li>
<li>
Install Archtics on workstations and ticket printers</li>
<li>
Initial and ongoing training of new features and functionality</li>
<li>
Perform DB backups</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Problem Resolution</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Use troubleshooting techniques and tools to identify the root cause of issues</li>
<li>
Research client/customer complaints about service levels</li>
<li>
Work with National support groups to expedite problem resolution</li>
<li>
Troubleshoot software and hardware issues – Archtics/Host/Presence/AM</li>
<li>
Coordinate networking issues between client IT and TM Field Technology</li>
<li>
Balance Audits/Settlement issues</li>
<li>
Resolve issues with Customer Service for events with problems and/or special circumstances</li>
<li>
Communicate across departments for resolution on Archtics server issues</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>
What a qualified candidate should possess:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
H.S. diploma or equivalent. BA/BS degree is preferred</li>
<li>
1+ years of experience with the Ticketmaster System and/or various ticketing system</li>
<li>
Overall awareness of the entertainment and sports business is important</li>
<li>
Box Office experience is a plus</li>
<li>
Knowledge of how TM departments impact on one another, and on outside clients is a plus</li>
<li>
Knowledge of Presence, AccessManager, REPGEN, and the Event Base product suite is preferred</li>
<li>
Archtics experience is preferred</li>
<li>
Strong Microsoft Word, Powerpoint and Excel skills</li>
<li>
Service oriented, with strong organizational and communication skills.</li>
<li>
Able to successfully handle multiple priorities</li>
<li>
Certain degree of creativity, latitude, and problem solving is required</li>
</ul>
