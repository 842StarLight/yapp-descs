<h4 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>JOB PURPOSE:</b></h4>
<p>The Sales &amp; Marketing Coordinator is responsible for providing support for all areas of the company’s sales and marketing strategies. This includes supporting the development and execution of sales strategies, creating and administering social media content and marketing campaigns, developing content for customer and industry distribution, and supporting our website and CRM strategies. The ideal candidate will have prior experience with a wide range of marketing functions, including communications, advertising, branding, digital marketing, and social media. The Sales &amp; Marketing coordinator must be an organized multi-tasker able to handle many diverse projects at once and meet tight deadlines.</p>
<p><b>
OBJECTIVES OF THIS ROLE:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Assist in the development and implementation of the company’s brand strategy</li>
<li>Ensure all marketing efforts serve to achieve immediate and long-term business goals, identifying and executing improvements for processes, content, and lead generation</li>
<li>Prepare marketing activity reports and metrics for measuring program success</li>
<li>Provide market research, forecasts, competitive analyses, campaign results, and consumer trends</li>
<li>Support the maintenance and development of internal and external tools, including a CRM database, global contact directory, and company website site</li>
<li>Further own knowledge and education regularly through workshops, research, and seminars, and share best practices, and local marketing ideas and tactics with the team</li>
</ul>
