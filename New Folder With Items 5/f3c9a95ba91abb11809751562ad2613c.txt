Summary:<br>
Under general supervision, the Actuarial Analyst performs actuarial services in support of Health Plan operations, and monitors and maintains financial solvency through the understanding of current data &amp; environment and modeling of future events. Interacts with internal and external departments such as Rating &amp; Underwriting, Finance, Network Management, Provider Contracting, Health Informatics, Marketing &amp; Sales as well as BCBSA, NYSDFS and CMS. Establishes insurance rates, rating structures, and rating systems for groups and categories of business. Maintains reserve programs, pricing files, benefit relativity tables and appropriate documentation for work. Develops financial projections, unpaid claim liability estimates and conducts actuarial analysis for well defined projects as directed. Compiles and analyzes data to draw conclusions and make recommendations. Proposes and assists in the development of process improvements utilizing system and software applications to full potential and participates in activities and projects as directed.<br>
<br>
