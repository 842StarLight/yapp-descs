<h2>Applications Technical Trainer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<b>APPLICATIONS TECHNICAL TRAINER</b><br>
<p></p>
<br>
<div>
<p><b>Summary</b><b>:</b></p>
<br>
</div>
<div>
Demonstrate, instruct and provide technical support for metal processing systems</div>
<div>
<p><b>Principle Duties &amp; Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Perform cutting and software demonstrations for potential customers.</li>
<li>
Instruct on metal applications, machine operations and laser/mechanical safety.</li>
<li>
Instruct on BySoft software.
</li>
<li>Provide test cuts and time studies for potential customers.</li>
<li>
Provide technical support via phone assistance: applications, operations and software.</li>
<li>
Instruct on machine maintenance procedures.</li>
<li>
Provide in-house computer hardware and software support.</li>
<li>
Maintain demo area and equipment.</li>
<li>
Maintain training documentation.</li>
<li>
Conduct technical presentations of Bystronic products for potential customers.</li>
<li>
Assist Sales Managers with potential customer visits.</li>
</ul>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>QUALIFICATIONS:</b></p>
<p><b>EDUCATION AND EXPERIENCE:</b></p>
<ul><li>Two (2) year degree or higher preferred in Engineering or Mechanical Engineering Technology.</li></ul>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>MANDATORY REQUIREMENTS:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Minimum two (2) years’ experience either in an Applications role, Technical Sales role, or as a Field Service Engineer.</li>
<li>
Be able to travel 10-20%.</li>
<li>
Have or be able to obtain a Passport.</li>
<li>
Have and maintain a clean and valid driver’s license and be able to rent a car.</li>
<li>
Be able to lift up to 70 lbs.<br>
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>ADDITIONAL REQUIREMENTS:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Excellent communication skills oral and written.</li>
<li>
Working knowledge of Microsoft Word, PowerPoint &amp; Excel.
</li>
<li>Knowledge of trigonometry and metallurgy.<br>
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>SPECIAL SKILS:</b><b>
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Experience in Hydraulics, pneumatics, electronics and blueprint reading.
</li>
<li>CNC programming, CAD software and Ladder Logic.</li>
<li>
Working knowledge of metal applications operations.<br>
</li>
</ul>
