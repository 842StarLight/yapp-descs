<p><b>Marketing Coordinator
</b></p>
<p>At Aqua-Tots Swim Schools we believe that swimming creates opportunities for families! We are seeking a Marketing Coordinator with 1+ years of experience to join our in-house team. The ideal candidate will have experience with various marketing outlets, including grass roots marketing efforts. You should be an individual that can efficiently and effectively manage various projects and tasks at one time. You should possess a strong passion for marketing and its impact on the families we serve!
</p>
<p><b>Duties and Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Coordinate and implement all marketing communication projects including public relations, special event management, advertising, promotional items, creating brand awareness, assist with various office events and projects, as needed.</li>
<li>
Ensure consistent brand identity and messaging internally and externally.</li>
<li>
Work with Aqua-Tots HQ to create, write, edit and design marketing/sales materials such as brochures, presentations, newsletters, surveys, training materials, press releases etc.</li>
<li>
Take photos/create videos of projects in progress and at completion in the schools for use on social media and in marketing efforts.</li>
<li>
Work with schools to develop sales tools and share information and ideas for local markets.</li>
<li>
Vendor management print materials and promotional items.</li>
<li>
Prepare reports on status of marketing and sales efforts survey results, social media/web analytics, sales reports etc.<p><b>
Skills and Abilities:</b></p>
<ul><li>
Superb work ethic, attention to detail and ability to follow-up independently. Team player with exceptional interpersonal skills. Strong organizational and time management skills, displaying poise and professionalism. Proactive, fast-learner and self-motivated/self-starter. Effective and professional written and verbal communication skills.</li></ul>
<p><b>
Experience:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Basic computer skills of Microsoft Office a must, advanced skills preferred
</li>
<li>At least 1 year of marketing experience required</li>
</ul>
</li>
