<h2>Career Counselor</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>The Career Counselor position at the A.B. Freeman School of Business at Tulane University will:
</p>
<p>1) Provide Career Management Education through the acquisition of lifelong career skills,
</p>
<p>2) Facilitate student employment through the acquisition of internship and full-time career positions and
</p>
<p>3) Cultivate both internal and external relationships and partnerships with all stakeholders.
</p>
<p>The Career Counselor will perform these duties in a proactive, collaborative environment that relates to the student as the primary beneficiary or customer.
</p>Required Knowledge, Skills, and Abilities
<ul>
<li><p>Skilled coaching ability
</p></li>
<li><p>Business acumen, executive presence and professional demeanor
</p></li>
<li><p>Effective sales / influencing / recruiting skills
</p></li>
<li><p>Innovative, creative, proactive and effective at problem-solving
</p></li>
<li><p>Effective understanding and management of stakeholders including students, HR and recruiting representatives, university administrators, C-level professionals, executives, faculty, alumni, and parents
</p></li>
<li><p>Ability to work collaboratively, including as a member of a team to build internal and external relationships
</p></li>
<li><p>Ability to work with a variety of companies, organizations and government agencies to connect students for internships and jobs
</p></li>
<li><p>Ability to work through ambiguity
</p></li>
<li><p>Be agile and adaptive to change
</p></li>
<li><p>Effective oral and written communication skills
</p></li>
<li><p>Proficiency in collecting, analyzing, and communicating data
</p></li>
<li><p>Demonstrated or related experience in individual and / or group career consulting
</p></li>
<li><p>Proficiency with Microsoft Office programs and familiarity with other applications such as assessment instruments, recruiting management systems for the delivery of career services, and customer relationship management (CRM) software
</p></li>
</ul>Required Education and/or Experience
<ul><li><p>A Bachelor's degree and at least five years of relevant experience
</p></li></ul>
<p>OR
</p>
<ul><li><p>A Master's degree and at least three years of relevant experience
</p></li></ul>Preferred Qualifications
<ul>
<li><p>International business experience and language fluency in a second language
</p></li>
<li><p>Corporate talent acquisition experience or network
</p></li>
<li><p>Professional certification in career coaching, career management, or related professional credentials</p></li>
</ul>
</div></div>
