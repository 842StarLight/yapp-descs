<h2>Brand Ambassador</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>We are building a team of highly competitive sales conscious brand advocates for a well respected company. We are looking for individuals that can talk to anyone and seek to be involved in their community. Applicants will be honest, courteous, outgoing, competitive, maintain a positive attitude, have a strong work ethic, and thrive off of helping others while working face to face with their customers. This is a flexible full time position offering competitive base per year plus incentives on a robust product offering. Position provides health insurance with employer contribution, life insurance paid by employer, 401K with partial employer match and paid vacation (after 1 year) and personal days (after 6 months). We will train on product offerings and industry standards. Sales experience preferred but not necessary. Base sales goals are very realistic, there is room for growth, and the company is nationally known for it's excellence in their industry. Be proud of where you work and get involved in your community. We are not looking for contractors. We want committed team members.</p>
<p><b><i>
This is an entry level position that is a competitive base salary ($11.50-$14.50 per hour for 40 hours per week) with an incentive per sale.</i></b></p>
<p>
Lee &amp; Cates Glass Brand Ambassador/Sales Development</p>
<p>
Job Description</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
The Lee &amp; Cates Glass Brand Ambassador represents Lee &amp; Cates Glass to targeted customers in order to build awareness, create a consistent image, generate sales opportunities, and build customer preference.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Job Tasks</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Network and build trusting relationships with potential customers</li>
<li>Thoroughly understand our products and services to inform potential customers</li>
<li>Use word of mouth marketing techniques</li>
<li>Assist in organizing marketing events</li>
<li>Track customer preferences and suggest advertising and positioning ideas</li>
<li>Plan and implement awareness creating programs to reach out to potential customers</li>
<li>Engage in outreach programs to involve existing customers into the company’s offerings</li>
<li>Understand the company’s goals and missions, and apply them within a sales and marketing platform</li>
<li>Relay customer feedback to the company in anticipation of improving products and services</li>
<li>Generate ideas to maximize sales to achieve or exceed sales goals</li>
<li>Develop and maintain strong and clear communication with existing and future customers</li>
<li>Assist in creating and installing marketing displays and insuring brand advertising material is placed strategically</li>
<li>Build and sustain business relationships with key customers and accounts in an operating area.</li>
<li>Leave any literature, if any, regarding our products and services</li>
<br>
</ul>
