<div><b>Position Overview</b></div>
<div>
KPA has an immediate opening for competitive, successful software sales professionals with a desire to take on new challenges and work in an environment with unlimited opportunity. The Account Executive is a strategic member of the sales team, responsible for helping KPA achieve top line revenue growth by identifying and closing new sales opportunities.</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
The role is focused on selling KPA’s Environmental Health &amp; Safety (EH&amp;S) compliance software and value-added services portfolio into the small and mid-sized business (SMB) market. Our software serves as the central hub of activity for growing compliance and safety departments seeking an adaptable, quick-to-implement solution featuring built-in best practices that accelerate business processes for compliance and risk professionals.</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
This is a challenging, exciting role that will test your sales skills, strategic thinking capabilities and resilience.</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div><b><i>
More specifically, you will:</i></b></div>
<ul>
<li>Hunt within a defined geographic territory closing sales with existing clients and new logo targets</li>
<li>Actively prospect for new opportunities within the assigned territory and act on inbound leads with urgency</li>
<li>Build and maintain a pipeline that is 3x annual quota target</li>
<li>Meet monthly and quarterly sales bookings targets by effectively managing the full sales-cycle, including contract generation and other deliverables required to close new business</li>
<li>Prioritize opportunities and coordinate internal KPA resources to provide the best client experience</li>
<li>Develop and execute a comprehensive territory plan</li>
<li>Spend minimum of 3 days/week in customer-facing activities and meetings</li>
<li>Use Salesforce to continuously update and maintain active deals for pipeline management, daily activity and sales forecasting</li>
<li>Rapidly become proficient in the value proposition of KPA’s offering and demonstrate the ability to deliver that message in person, via webinar and by phone</li>
<li>Actively participate in team meetings, share best practices and maintain a positive, team player attitude – motivating others as necessary</li>
<li>Job related travel up to 75%</li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>

<div><b>
Requirements</b></div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<ul>
<li>3+ years successful quota carrying software sales experience in a B2B environment</li>
<li>Consistent, proven over-achievement in past quota carrying roles</li>
<li>High intensity and drive to achieve immediate results and “crush” sales targets</li>
<li>Assertive hunter mentality adept in identifying and winning new business</li>
<li>Strong intellectual curiosity to uncover why buyers will buy and how to leverage that information</li>
<li>Highly effective communication skills that convey professionalism in all written, verbal and virtual meetings</li>
<li>Proficient working with Salesforce, MS Office suite including Outlook, Word, Excel and PowerPoint</li>
<li>Positive attitude and a team player</li>
</ul>
