<h2>Contracts Renewals Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Position Title: </b><b>Renewal Retention Associate</b> <b>Department: </b><b>OPIS Sales / Contracts Renewals Team</b></p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
I. Mission of the Position:</b></p>
<p>
Under the direction of the OPIS Contracts Team Manager, this position is responsible for managing and executing a specific range of OPIS Service Accounts renewals for hundreds of customers that purchase OPIS services in a specific annual dollar amount. The Renewal Retention Associate will initiate audit requests on a monthly basis, review audits of customers, analyze current agreements and identify potential monetization issues, propose rate increases for renewals, and will work with the customer and the Contracts Team to ensure that renewal agreements are executed in a timely fashion, and on the correct IHSMarkit paperwork.</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>
II.</b> <b>Ongoing Duties &amp; Responsibilities</b></p>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>
Manages the monthly list of upcoming customer renewals:<ul>
<li>
Ensures that customer audits have been completed on time</li>
<li>
Reviews audits and identifies areas where we are not charging the customer for data, deliveries and/or licensing and approaches the customer to monetize those services</li>
<li>
Works up Flat Rate Reviews and renewal rate proposals</li>
<li>
Communicates effectively with the customer via telephone and email to address questions and/or issues</li>
<li>
Ensures that upon renewal, the customer is signing updated IHSMarkit paperwork</li>
<li>
Ensures all customer invoicing changes are effectively entered into the Sales software systems</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>
Analyze and identify opportunities to achieve maximum value for both the customer and the business</li>
<li>
Works with Contracts Team to prepare and execute renewal agreements, and manage the timeliness of return of executed documents</li>
<li>
Finalize renewals prior to the “price increase date” and commencement of end of month billing</li>
<li>
Maintains and updates notes and documentation on customers within the Sales software systems<ul>
<li>
Manages the accuracy of customer data through reporting, audit requests, record edits, and removal of duplications</li>
<li>
Utilizes software systems to convert opportunities, adjust customer rates, complete Time Series customer adjustments, process renewals, and analyze audits</li>
<li>
Assists the Contracts Team with unique racks/unique users audits</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>
Facilitates routine communication with external customers and internal departments via email, phone, and direct mailings</li>
<li>
Assist customers with questions related to cost, service breakdowns, and cancelation</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>
III. Metrics for Evaluating Performance:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Timeliness of monthly production of customer lists within specified parameters</li>
<li>
Timeliness of audit case requests and audit completions</li>
<li>
Accuracy and timeliness of customer updates and documentation in the Sales software system</li>
<li>
Accurate reconciliation of unbilled items</li>
<li>
Finalization of monthly customer renewals by due date</li>
<li>
Customer retention rate</li>
<li>
Promptness of updating customer billing records.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>
IV. Required Skills, Abilities &amp; Knowledge</b>:</p>
<p><br>
These are skills, abilities and knowledge that a candidate MUST have in order to be considered for the position. Lack of these requirements will preclude candidate from consideration.</p>
<ul>
<li>
Ability to appropriately prioritize tasks and manage time</li>
<li>
Ability to communicate and negotiate effectively both internally and externally, while building and maintaining effective relationships with team members and customers alike</li>
<li>
Ability to work independently and efficiently to complete daily, weekly and monthly responsibilities</li>
<li>
Basic proficiency in Microsoft Excel</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>
V. Preferred Skills, Abilities &amp; Knowledge:</b></p>
<p>
These are skills, abilities and knowledge that you would like a successful candidate to have, but lack of will not preclude candidate from consideration.</p>
<ul>
<li>
NetSuite, or a similar “CRM” system.</li>
<li>
Excellent written skills.</li>
<li>
Advanced Microsoft Excel skills such as pivot tables.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>
VI. Experience</b></p>
<p>
Should have a minimum of one year’s experience in the following:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Negotiating purchase agreements and/or contracts with customers.</li>
<li>
Analyzing a customer’s “profile,” including a breakdown of their “spend.”</li>
<li>
Direct contact (verbal and written) with key customers</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>
VII. Education and Certifications</b></p>
<ul><li>
Bachelor’s degree required</li></ul>
