<h2>Field Service Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Overview
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<p>Waters Corporation's Field Services Organization is comprised of highly skilled, motivated and customer focused professionals that are technical experts on Waters’ product suite. As a Field Service Specialist, working for an industry leader, you'll join a global team of specialists that are crucial to ensure Waters’ legendary service for our customers who utilize our scientific instrumentation and software.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p>In this role, you'll have the opportunity to provide technical expertise on Waters' innovative product suite (LC, LC/MS, HPLC/UPLC, SFC) for multiple customer laboratories spanning across the Pharmaceutical &amp; Life Sciences, Chemical Materials, Clinical, Food, Environmental, Forensics, Academia and Government industries.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
Waters Corporation is seeking a highly skilled, motivated and customer focused individual to join our renown Field Services Organization in the <b>Utah</b><b> </b>area.</p>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<ul>
<li>Installation, performance maintenance, troubleshoot and support Waters' product suite (LC, LC/MS, HPLC/UPLC, SFC, Informatics)</li>
<li>
Establish and maintain effective relationships with external and internal customers ensuring their success</li>
<li>
Manage customer expectations, by communicating work performed and providing follow up plan if needed</li>
<li>
Ensure customer compliance regulations are followed</li>
<li>
Deliver system level training at customer sites</li>
<li>
Manage service inventory according to Waters policies</li>
<li>
Issue field service reports daily to document work performed</li>
<li>
Maintain sound knowledge regarding the technology and customers' application demands</li>
<li>
Work with Sales team in respect to identifying future business development opportunities and needs for service contract sales</li>
<li>
Ensure that quality system procedures and Health &amp; Safety standards are adhered to at all times</li>
</ul>
</div></div></div>

<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Company Description
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<p>Waters Corporation (NYSE: WAT), the world's leading specialty measurement company, has pioneered chromatography, mass spectrometry and thermal analysis innovations serving the life, materials and food sciences for 60 years. With approximately 7,000 employees worldwide, Waters operates directly in 31 countries, including 15 manufacturing facilities, and with products available in more than 100 countries.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
Waters Corporation's businesses include Waters, TA Instruments, VICAM, ERA, and Nonlinear Dynamics.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
Waters creates business advantages for laboratory-dependent organizations by delivering ultra performance liquid chromatography (UPLC), high-performance liquid chromatography (HPLC), chromatography columns and chemistry products, mass spectrometry systems, laboratory informatics solutions, and comprehensive service programs to enable significant advancement in healthcare delivery, environmental management, food safety, and water quality.</p>
