<h2>Senior Financial Analyst, Professional Services</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Mission: </b>The Senior Financial Analyst, within the Professional Services group at IntegriChain, is part of an elite team of consultants who leverage the Life Sciences domain expertise to analyze, model, and synthesize secondary information to provide insight to our Gross to Net (GTN) customers. The Senior Financial Analyst will be the strategic voice to our customers seeking to understand and solve their forecasting and accrual accounting needs. The ideal candidate will demonstrate high-level analytical thinking combined with the technical sophistication and marketing-savvy approach to each business scenario.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Provide in-depth analysis and recommendations to customers around critical GTN business processes</li>
<li>
Assess customers’ current state GTN processes, benchmark against industry norms and best practices, develop recommendations to enhance and automate aspects of the GTN process, and build roadmaps to guide customers down the path to achieve these improvements</li>
<li>
Provide insights on GTN liabilities across government and commercial channels, including managed care contracts, Medicaid, Public Health Service (PHS) 340B, Tricare, returns, copay and Coverage Gap</li>
<li>
Perform accurate &amp; timely accounting and analyses of all sales discounts, rebates, chargebacks, returns and allowances included in the calculation of gross-to-net revenue</li>
<li>
Assist customers in developing sophisticated, practical, and user-friendly forecasting and accrual models to ensure predictable forecasts, accurate accruals and a more efficient close process</li>
<li>
Prepare monthly revenue related journal entries and ledger account reconciliations</li>
<li>
Conduct reconciliations of actual transaction data to the associated accrual assumptions</li>
<li>
Partner with customers to ensure sales deduction accruals are in line with contractual agreements and analyze any variances</li>
<li>
Perform true-ups based on actual versus accrued contra revenue GTN deduction items</li>
<li>
Analyze, explain and meaningfully summarize gross to net variances</li>
<li>
Work with team leads on advising on the implementation of IntegriChain’s industry-leading GTN software through execution of workshop, design of configurations, and advisory support across the project team</li>
<li>
Support customers’ monthly close process, including establishing accruals, refreshing forecasts, providing critical analysis of data, and alerting customers to potential changes that may affect their business</li>
<li>
Continuously communicate with the customer teams in order to stay on top of all developments relevant to future product development and IntegriChain performance</li>
<li>
Create on-time, high quality project deliverables while managing expectations internally and externally</li>
<li>
Demonstrate value to our customers and drive incremental revenue opportunities</li>
<li>
Contribute to IntegriChain’s industry-leading GTN thought leadership and benchmarking capabilities through networking and collaboration with customers and colleagues, independent and project-based research, project delivery, and conference attendance</li>
</ul>
<br>
Requirements:<br>
<ul>
<li>Deep understanding of the GAAP principles related to revenue recognition and gross-to-net accounting practices</li>
<li>
Knowledge of the pharma supply chain and contracting and the relationship to data flows through GTN models – including but not limited to direct sales, chargebacks, rebates, and copay data.</li>
<li>
Experience as the customer-facing consulting lead for complex GTN activities and deliverables – ability to deeply describe all steps, approaches, pitfalls, and how to mitigate risk and achieve success</li>
<li>
Strong interpersonal and communication skills</li>
<li>
Excellent understanding of cross-functional team work</li>
<li>
Mastery of Excel and PowerPoint</li>
<li>
Excellent presentation skills</li>
<li>
Minimum B.A. / B.S., CPA or MBA preferred with majors in Accounting, Finance, Economics, or similar</li>
<li>
5 - 7 years of accounting experience working on the customer side or at a consultancy</li>
</ul>
