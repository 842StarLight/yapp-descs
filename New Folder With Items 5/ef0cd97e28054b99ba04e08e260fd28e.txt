<p>
Skillsoft is currently seeking a Content Marketing Specialist to join our GTM Marketing Team in Nashua, NH. The Content Marketing Specialist is responsible for developing and executing marketing content to create awareness and drive demand for Skillsoft products and services.
</p>
<p>The Content Marketing Specialist will develop a variety of marketing content types, designed to drive demand, including white papers, eBooks, infographics, landing pages and other projects as needed. The ideal candidate will have excellent writing skills and the understanding of the strategic role of content in multi-faceted marketing campaigns.
</p>
<p>Opportunity Highlights:
</p>
<ul>
<li>Work with the Manager of Content Marketing to develop and write thought leadership-oriented material that support the stages of the buyer journey.
</li>
<li>Write and edit content to support lead nurture efforts, including emails, scripts, collateral and sales tools.
</li>
<li>Write and update web content with an eye toward search engine optimization (SEO).
</li>
<li>Collaborate with graphic design and marketing operations on the design of emails, web pages, collateral, etc.
</li>
<li>Work with demand generation team to understand how campaigns are performing and improving over time.
</li>
<li>Analyze existing content and look for ways to repurpose within marketing campaigns and buyer’s journey.</li>
</ul>
<p>Skills &amp; Qualifications:
</p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree in communications, marketing or English
</li>
<li>Minimum 2-4 years of experience in marketing communications or corporate marketing</li>
<li>Experience in enterprise software or eLearning a plus but not required
</li>
<li>Experience working in B2B and/or B2C environment is helpful
</li>
<li>Writing is the primary skill needed – but successful candidate must be a top-notch communicator, able to work with many different constituents across the company
</li>
<li>Strong proficiency in desktop applications (Word, PowerPoint, Excel) and understanding of web best practices
</li>
<li>Occasional travel may be required</li>
</ul>
