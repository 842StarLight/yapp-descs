<h2>Advertising Campaign Coordinator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b><i>Do you have excellent communications skills and possess a natural talent for sales campaign coordination and logistics?</i></b> If so, we'd love to have you take charge of our ad campaigns! The Campaign Coordinator is responsible for coordinating the execution of outdoor advertising campaigns and communicating updates to Lamar account executives and customers. Our Lamar office in Fayetteville, Arkansas is now hiring a Campaign Coordinator to help us bring effective outdoor advertising campaigns to life for brands in Fayetteville, AR and the surrounding areas.</p>
<p><b>
Company Overview:</b></p>
<p>
Lamar Advertising is one of the largest outdoor advertising companies in the world. Lamar offers advertisers a variety of advertising formats, helping both local businesses and national brands stand out from the competition. We live by the Golden Rule, and we operate with honesty and integrity in every aspect of our business. We are open with our employees, transparent with our customers, and loyal to the communities in which we serve. We have been in business over 100 years, and we have stayed true to our mission and values. While Lamar is a large company, each office has its own culture and family atmosphere, making employees feel connected both locally and nationally.</p>

<p><b>
Compensation Overview:</b></p>
<p>
The hourly range for this position is <b>$16 - $18 / hour</b>, dependent on relevant experience and skills.</p>
<p><b>
Position Purpose:</b></p>
<p>
The purpose of the position is to coordinate the execution of advertising campaigns sold by the sales team. Campaign Coordinators are responsible for managing tasks, deadlines, and performing a range of campaign related administrative functions that occur after the contract has been signed. In addition, there may be other tasks assigned by Account Executives, Sales Manager, and/or General Managers.</p>
<p><b>
Essential Functions and Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Coordinating and monitor the ongoing activities and internal communications related to advertising campaigns to ensure on-time and contractual campaign execution while delivering excellent customer service.</li>
<li>Informing Account Executives and customers about the current status of advertising campaigns.</li>
<li>Coordinating, managing, reviewing campaign progress and delivering the customer installation and proof of performance information.
</li>
<li>Coordinate and/or obtain approval from the client on artwork
</li>
<li>Coordinate and communicate panel locations in “to be determined” situations.
</li>
<li>Resolves problems during campaign by investigating; identifying solutions; notifying AE’s, managers and customers.</li>
</ul>
