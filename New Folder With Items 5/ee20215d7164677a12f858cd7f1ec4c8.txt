<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Position Summary
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div>WISH-TV/HYFN Local Indianapolis is seeking a sales superstar with a digital acumen, and a always learning attitude. This individual will be responsible for growing an existing book of business while simultaneously uncovering digital media opportunities with local and regional direct clients, as well as agencies. This position offers high income potential, with uncapped earnings structure. This individual will have all the tools necessary to offer tailored and thoughtful digital solutions to clients, while leveraging local broadcast solutions and sponsorships. It will be their responsibility to be on top of digital media trends, present themselves as a digital leader in the marketplace, and be well versed on the impact digital marketing has on company business models. They will demonstrate an ability to think creatively, sell strategically, and communicate effectively while growing the scope and relationship of the client relationship.</div>
