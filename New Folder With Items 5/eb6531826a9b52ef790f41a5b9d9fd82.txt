<h2>Construction Project Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>As one of the country's leading mechanical contractors, Harris offers you the best of both worlds: the stability, resources and opportunities of a national company, and the team culture, creative spirit and customer loyalty of a local business. If you thrive on variety and new challenges, we want to meet you!</p>
<p>
From stadiums to manufacturing facilities, power plants to hospitals, concert halls to classrooms, we handle projects of all sizes and complexity from multiple regional locations across the country.</p>
<br>
<p>
<b>
RESPONSIBILITIES:</b></p>
<p>
This individual will provide project management support during all phases of HVAC construction to ensure project standards, budget, deadlines and communications are maintained.
</p>
<ul>
<li>Manage Safety Compliance and foster a culture of safety</li>
<li>Document Control and Review</li>
<li>Manage Scope of Work</li>
<li>Basic Schedule Management and Field Resources</li>
<li>Procurement Management &amp; Coordination</li>
<li>Estimation</li>
<li>Financial Management</li>
<li>Develop, Build &amp; Maintain relationships</li>
<li>Support Sales Process</li>
</ul>
<p><b>SKILLS, KNOWLEDGE, QUALIFICATIONS &amp; EXPERIENCE:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
2-year Associate degree required or Journeyman level achieved</li>
<li>4-year Bachelor degree preferred or equivalent years experience.</li>
<li>5+ years knowledge or experience in the building and construction industry with a strong understanding of mechanical systems, design techniques, tools and principles.</li>
<li>Proven ability to read and comprehend construction documents</li>
<li>Proficient knowledge of Microsoft Office Suite and Estimating software</li>
<li>PMP certification and OSHA 30 a plus</li>
<li>LEED accredited/knowledge a plus</li>
<br>
</ul>
