<p>The UPS Store – We are a locally owned and operated! The Assistant Manager will play a large supportive role to the store manager. He or she will be required to open and close the center, help manage productivity and ensure the center’s team delivers world-class customer service to all customers, monitor cost control and expenses. It is a position responsible for achieving all company goals and initiatives through driving sales and developing talent. Assume responsibility for the store and its success by leading an effective customer-focused selling culture by building the team, sales and the brand. Establishing and maintaining store standards through leadership and operational expertise. Have the ability to encourage and demonstrate exceptional customer service while on the sales floor. Motivates and inspires the associates to build brand loyalty and create a positive store environment. Great pay with benefits and incentives, depending upon experience.</p>
<br>
The ideal candidate has a high school diploma or GED (college coursework or a degree preferred), one year of retail store operations experience, strong supervisory/managerial/leadership skills, excellent computer/internet/software knowledge, the physical ability to perform this job (lifting, bending, etc.), and knows how to listen, follow, and lead. He or she must have a dynamic personality and must be able to help motivate a team to optimize performance.<br>
