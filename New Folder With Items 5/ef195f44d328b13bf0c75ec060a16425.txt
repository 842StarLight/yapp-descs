<div><b>Apply Now and move your career forward with Airgas as the Regional Manager, Financial Planning &amp; Analysis at our Regional Headquarters in Appleton, WI!</b></div>

<div></div>
<div><b>What you will do:</b></div>
<div>The Manager of Financial Planning &amp; Analysis (FP&amp;A) is responsible for the Region’s planning, budgeting, forecasting, business reporting and analysis in support of the Region’s profitability and growth. The Manager, FP&amp;A further provides innovative financial leadership to the business unit and functional leaders in the Region.</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div><b>Your responsibilities:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Reports and analyzes on Regional financial and operating metrics, such as: same-store sales, gross margin, same-store and operating expenses, working capital, asset utilization, capital expenditures, etc.</li>
<li>
Works closely with the Regional Company business/finance support teams to better understand historical financial results, trends and validate forecast assumptions.</li>
<li>
Supports the annual budgeting and quarterly forecasting processes, working closely with both the Regional and Divisional business/finance support.</li>
<li>
Leads the Region’s efforts to improve &amp; utilize warehouse reporting tools.</li>
<li>
Performs extensive financial modeling in support of the Region.</li>
<li>
Builds strong relationships with the Division Finance management teams.</li>
<li>
Provides financial training to Regional leaders within the organization.</li>
<li>
Oversees financial audits and control testing of branch operations to insure compliance with Airgas financial policies &amp; procedures.</li>
<li>
Supports the Regional finance organization through goal-setting, evaluations, positive feedback, discipline, and on-going coaching ensuring activities are performed within Airgas policy.</li>
<li>
Other duties and projects as assigned.</li>
</ul>

Minimum of seven years of increasing responsibility in financial planning and analysis demonstrating leadership of and/or management of a team.</li>
<li>
Prior experience utilizing SAP preferred.</li>

<li>
Business and financial acumen.</li>
