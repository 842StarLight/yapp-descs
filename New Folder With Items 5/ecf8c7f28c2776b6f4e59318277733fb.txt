<h2>Pre-Sales Engineer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Summary</b></p>
<p>MDSL Sales Engineers are responsible for supporting the Sales and Account Management teams using highly a combination of a consultative, value-selling process and domain expertise to support the attainment of quarterly booking targets. The professional MDSL Sales Engineer will be expected to perform in a matrix environment in a highly collaborative manner to support a complex solution selling lifecycle. The SE must be able to clearly present the solution and differentiate MDSL to prospects and customers. The Sales Engineer is involved in all aspects of the customer acquisition journey including discovery, demonstrations and the orchestration of defining a SaaS and Managed Service solution that aligns to customer requirements, removes business challenges and provides business value.</p>
<p><b>This remote position can be based in any Western US city near a major airport, such as Denver, CO, Salt Lake City, UT or Portland, OR</b></p>
<p><b>Principal Job Duties: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Consultative discovery and qualification</li>
<li>Create and deliver custom-scripted demonstrations, configured demos and POCs</li>
<li>Clearly articulate Managed Service packages and entitlements</li>
<li>Present customer anecdotes and case studies</li>
<li>Understand and present implementation and integration methodologies</li>
<li>Support the assessment and construction of RFQ, RFIs, and RFPs</li>
<li>Develop customer facing collateral to support the sales process</li>
<li>Disciplined documentation of all sales activities in the CRM</li>
<li>Support hand-off activities to Implementation teams including SOWs</li>
<li>Support the Marketing team with requests for event support, webinars, and written communication pieces</li>
<li>Acquire and maintain deep technical product and solution knowledge related to product releases and enhancements</li>
<li>Maintain deep domain expertise in the Telecom Expense Management and be proficient in at least one vertical</li>
<li>Stay current with changes in the shifting landscape of competitive offerings</li>
<li>Work with leadership to pro-actively manage the complexities of schedules and prioritization of work products within a collaborative matrix environment</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Qualifications</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree or equivalent combination of education and experience required</li>
<li>5+ Years proven pre-sales engineering experience required</li>
<li>Telecom Expense Management domain expertise and/or related expertise in the Source-to-Settle solution domain preferred</li>
<li>Strong communications, presentation and relationship building skills; the ability to accurately and clearly articulate the value of MDSL’s products/solutions is required</li>
<li>Knowledge of SasS, Managed Services and SDLC is required</li>
<li>Working knowledge of Microsoft Excel and Access to manipulate data and PowerPoint and Word to develop materials to communicate ideas</li>
<li>Travel up to 50%</li>
<li>English-fluency is required. Additional language fluency including French, German, Italian, Spanish, or Swedish is highly desirable but not required</li>
</ul>
<p>Eligibility to work in the US without sponsorship required</p>
<p>Job Type: Full-time</p>
<p>Experience:</p>
<ul><li>TEM or Source-to-Settle Domain: 5 years (Preferred)</li></ul>
</div>
