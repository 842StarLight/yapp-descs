<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>About the job</b></h2>
<p>
gTech’s Professional Services team takes a creative, collaborative, and customer-centric approach to provide foundational services and forward-looking business solutions to top advertiser and publisher customers. Through technical implementation, optimization, and key solutions, gTech Professional Services helps customers attain their business goals while building long-term capabilities.</p>
<p>
As the Manager, you will lead a team of product experts and technical consultants, working with Google’s largest Agencies and Partners in the Americas. This team understands Agency and Partner business objectives to uncover collaboration opportunities for technical projects; specific product and feature implementations; and overall ecosystem enablement to up-level the technical capabilities of Google’s Agencies and Partners. You will collaborate with stakeholders in Sales, Commercialization, Product Management and Engineering. With creativity, collaboration, and client-centricity as your guiding principles, you will coach, advocate, and showcase your team’s impact on the business. You will be operating in a dynamic and entrepreneurial environment, and will have the opportunity to help shape and influence how the industry operates in this increasingly technical and complex reality.
</p>
<p>Google creates products and services that make the world a better place, and gTech’s role is to help bring them to life. Our teams of trusted advisors support customers globally. Our solutions are rooted in our technical skill, product expertise, and a thorough understanding of our customers’ complex needs. Whether the answer is a bespoke solution to solve a unique problem, or a new tool that can scale across Google, everything we do aims to ensure our customers benefit from the full potential of Google products.<br>
<br>
To learn more about gTech, check out our video.</p>
