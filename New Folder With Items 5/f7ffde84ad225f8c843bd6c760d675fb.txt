<b>Engineering Manager – Geared Systems</b><div>
The<br>
Engineering Manager – Geared Systems will be responsible for leading the Geared<br>
Systems team to integrate core components such as gears, bearings, shafts and<br>
housings, into an assembly that meets functional requirements. Working with the product groups, the Geared<br>
Systems group provides initial architecture design/direction though core<br>
component design/selection and system level modeling/analysis to ensure<br>
durability, efficiency and NVH performance targets are achieved.<br>
</div>
