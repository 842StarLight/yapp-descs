<h2>Senior Product Marketing Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>The Senior Product Marketing Manager will manage content strategy and execution along with other initiatives to promote and sell the FireMon suite of products to a global audience of new prospects and existing customers as well as to internal sales and partner audiences. Reporting directly to the VP, Product Marketing, the person in this role will make a strong impact on the business by serving as the lead technical marketing specialist across the entire product, service, and solution portfolio.</p>
<p>
Primary responsibilities include:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Cross-functional team lead for conception, execution, and measurement of product collateral, including presentations, data sheets, white papers, web copy, solution briefs, partner materials, ROI calculators, tech briefs, and internal sales tools to support the entire sales cycle and marketing funnel.</li>
<li>
Support training and enablement of our new hires and global sales team to help them effectively articulate and optimally position the value of our solutions to increase win rates.</li>
<li>
Derive market insights from analysts, media, customers, prospects, sales, and primary research and deliver product marketing input to product management and development teams.</li>
<li>
Together with the Product Management, R&amp;D and Marketing teams, educate and present to both internal and external stakeholders about FireMon’s solutions and the FireMon story.</li>
<li>
Perform other duties as assigned.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Required Skills and Experience</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Bachelor’s Degree in Marketing, Engineering, IT, Journalism, or Business Administration, OR equivalent B2B experience required, MBA preferred but not required</li>
<li>
Minimum of five (5) years work experience in the enterprise security space required</li>
<li>
Strong communication, time management, writing and project management skills</li>
<li>
Highly motivated, collaborative, and hungry for the win!</li>
<li>
Experience working with sales teams, engineering teams, media and industry analysts</li>
<li>
Highly organized; superior communication and presentation skills; self-managed, proactive</li>
<li>
Ability to thrive in a demanding environment requiring high degree of deadline-driven productivity, commitment, adaptability, communication, initiative and follow-through.</li>
<li>
Excellent time management and organizational skills, including ability to accurately gauge project duration, deliver on deadlines, and manage a high-volume workload of often-changing priorities.</li>
<li>
Must be strong in translating deeply complex technical concepts into compelling messaging for all audiences, including C-level, security teams, devops, network admins, etc.</li>
<li>
Strong attention to detail and commitment to quality</li>
</ul>
<p>
This position can be located in the FireMon offices in either Overland Park, KS or Dallas TX, or may be based remote in the US, Canada, or UK.</p>
