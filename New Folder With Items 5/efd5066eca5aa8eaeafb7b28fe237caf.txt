<b>
JOB DESCRIPTION</b><br>
UST is looking for a high energy, collaborative, Director Channel Partnerships who will manage existing channel partnerships with large technology leaders, help develop new partnerships and drive revenue growth through the partnerships.</div>
<div>
The Director, Channel Partnerships plays a key role managing all aspects of relationships with channel partners as well as for account customers developed through the partnership. The Director will, within the assigned geography, ensure growth and profitability by focusing strongly on vision alignment and service of the channel. This includes selling to prospects and driving revenue as well as managing partners and clients delivered via the channel.<br>
The expectation of the role is to drive our channel partnerships and revenue growth, through close partnership. This role will report to the global head of sales and bring tactical sales execution expertise to the select channel.<br>
<b>RESPONSIBILITIES</b><br>
Drive revenue. The most important responsibility will be to involves hitting their monthly goals. Channel Managers, along with the senior management team, are responsible for developing the strategy to hit said goals. Build partnership strength and effectiveness. The role will primarily focus on building the strength of our relationship with a major B to B software channel partner and working with that partner s sales force and client base continuing to drive sales force engagement and knowledge or our process automation.<br>
<ul>
<li>Drive sales via partnership channel</li>
<li>Continuously Train and Engage existing partner Sales Team on Company Offerings. Work with sales team to understand their client needs and how our platform can help. Convey to our partners the value of our USTs intelligent AI platform business offering, implementation approach and pricing strategy</li>
<li>Establish alliances with new partners. Onboard new partnerships handle agreements and negotiations work with internal groups to inform them on current and upcoming business opportunities</li>
<li>Become an expert on each partner s organization, business model, and strategic priorities to realize new opportunities.</li>
</ul>
<br>
<ul><li>Lead the sales team with integrity and providing ongoing coaching and feedback, recognizing and developing team members, identifying and managing risks, and completing daily management tasks. Exhibit strong technical domain expertise, being comfortable in understanding and explaining technology and solutions and with ability to guide large RFPs and maintaining response quality
</li></ul>
