<p></p>
<p>Danone North America is looking for a Sr. Manager, Demand Planning - CO in Broomfield, CO.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<div><b>
RESPONSIBILITIES:</b></div>
<br>
<div></div>
<ul>
<li>Serve as the objective voice of Supply Chain by partnering with Marketing, Sales, Trade Management and Supply Planning to drive monthly volume consensus</li>
<li>Facilitate monthly S&amp;OP/GPS forecast process meetings with Sales, Marketing, Supply Chain and Business Unit leadership</li>
<li>Lead and coach team of Divisional Demand Planners to generate regional consumption-based forecast(s) within assigned Business Unit(s)</li>
<li>Consolidate monthly divisional forecasts into Business Unit level view for communication with executive leadership.
</li>
<li>Align with Trade Marketing and Sales teams on promotional planning volumes to build into consumption modeling/shipments forecast</li>
<li>Create ad-hoc reporting as needed to enhance Demand Planning and cross-functional stakeholder understanding of volume drivers and forecast variances</li>
<li>Closely monitor forecast accuracy, Demand Planning and Supply Chain KPIâ€™s to identify gaps and initiate corrections</li>
<li>Lead/Provide oversight for new item forecasting process for assign Business Unit(s)</li>
<li>Document and communicate (monthly) risks and opportunities to plan/forecast</li>
<li>Develop consumption based forecasting models, identify shipment vs. consumption differences, and lead final shipment forecasting process by integrating past/future demand factors (distribution gains/losses, price changes, new/disco products, promotion plans, etc.) from Sales and Marketing and aligning on their impacts to demand</li>
<li>Provide leadership in the annual volume planning process and quarterly updates through monthly 18-month rolling volume forecast</li>
<li>Identify and report on root cause for error at all levels of product-sales hierarchy: SKU-Warehouse-Week-Customer</li>
<li>Master forecasting software and MicroStrategy reporting to improve productivity and reduce forecast error</li>
<li>Work closely with Product Supply groups to ensure alignment on forecast updates and potential operational impacts</li>
<li>Coordinate with Sales, Customer Service and Supply Planning on constrained platform product allocations as needed</li>
<br>
</ul>
