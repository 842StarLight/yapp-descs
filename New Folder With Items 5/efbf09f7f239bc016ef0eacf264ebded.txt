<p><b>Job Description Summary
</b></p>This position performs a variety of functions in underwriting and servicing Residential Construction loans, including builder speculative and presale loans, land loans, townhome loans, condominium loans, revolving lines of credit, participations sold and/or purchased, One-Step, DHHL and Renovation loans as set forth by company policy. Activities may include underwriting Residential Construction loan requests, including borrower credit, property and market analysis, setting up, activating and funding new loans, processing draw requests, monetary processing, releases/reconveyances, insurance tracking, customer service for borrowers, builders and branch staff, file tracking, extension and modification processing, preparing conversion loan documents, reviewing and tracking builder financial file information and other activities of similar scope and complexity within Residential Construction. At this level III, the incumbents are expected to possess a high level of specialized knowledge of the residential construction lending process and to handle the most complex and unusual transactions. Incumbents use a combination of operational and technical skills that are fully developed, and they are proficient in most of the specific work routines within the department and act as a backup throughout the work group as needed. May participate in special projects, monitor and update procedures as necessary, coordinate daily work flows, provide training and technical guidance to lower-level incumbents and review work for conformance to internal procedures.
