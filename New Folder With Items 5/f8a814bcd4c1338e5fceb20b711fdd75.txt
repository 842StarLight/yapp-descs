<h2>Program Manager I - Corporate Relations Strategy</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>BASIC FUNCTION</b></p>
<p>The Corporate Development Program Manager I – Corporate Relations &amp; Strategy will design and execute sponsorship recognition packages and lead the sales process and follow up support, as necessary. The Program Manager will develop professional relationships with potential corporate and industry supporters, both domestically and internationally, of ASPS, PSF, and affiliated organization programming and initiatives with the intention of collaborating on sponsorship and branding opportunities that provide a value add to membership as well as appropriate return on investment for supporters.</p>
<p><b>ESSENTIAL DUTIES</b></p>
<p><b>Corporate Development</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Responsible for securing funding to advance the society’s interests by researching, identifying, and soliciting sources of corporate support and by maintaining strong business relationships with corporate prospects.</li>
<li>Design, implement, and manage a comprehensive proactive development strategy focused on expanding and enhancing corporate relationships by obtaining corporate monetary support for clinical symposia, strategic initiatives, and annual scientific meeting.</li>
<li>Develop highly competitive proposals for corporate prospects and leverage diverse corporate goals and interests in conjunction with organization’s goals. Works cross-functionality to develop and identify opportunities for corporate support and ensure execution of marketing and logistical tactics required for successful strategy deployment.</li>
<li>Partners synergistically with ASPS grants and foundations support team to develop a plan which maximizes the receipt of corporate support via branding opportunities while still securing support for research, public awareness campaigns, educational grants and charitable care by leveraging corporate and industry professional relationships and knowledge of potential supporter strategy.</li>
<li>Collaborates with department leadership and ASPS program-owners to determine corporate support goals and manages associated Corporate Development budgets.</li>
<li>Serves as key contact to corporate sponsors and potential corporate sponsors for branding/sponsorship opportunities and liaises with Exhibits team to assist in securing corporate partners as exhibitors at live meetings.</li>
</ul>
<p>*</p>
<ul>
<li>Follow up with corporate supporters to ensure expectations were met (survey, conference calls, etc.) for all branding/sponsorship involvement as part of an overall strategy to nurture and grow corporate relationships.</li>
<li>Co-Staff liaison to Development Committee and the Corporate Leadership Council (CLC)</li>
<li>Provide guidance to all members to ensure ethical compliance with industry guidelines, such as ACCME Standards for Commercial Support.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>OTHER DUTIES</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Keeps informed of developments in philanthropy and fund development, including funding requirements, and informs ASPS senior staff and the Development Committee on current trends, problems and activities in order to facilitate policy making.</li>
<li>Maintain compliance with all relevant regulations, laws and accountability standards to donors and ensure compliance with code of ethical principles and standards of professional conduct for fundraising professionals.</li>
<li>This position requires 10% travel</li>
<li>Performs other duties as assigned</li>
</ul>
<p><b>POSITION QUALIFICATIONS</b></p>
<p>Education: Bachelor’s degree in Marketing or related field or equivalent work experience</p>
<p>Experience: 3-5 years’ experience in marketing or development fundraising required</p>
<ul>
<li>Positive attitude and team player</li>
<li>Flexible and adaptive to changing priorities</li>
<li>Sound understanding of the principles of marketing and sales</li>
<li>Excellent follow up and customer service skills</li>
<li>Working knowledge of the sales process – prospect/research, present, follow up, execution and evaluation</li>
<li>Ability to identify and inventory abstract concepts and pull these elements together to create innovative opportunities</li>
<li>Proficient w/Microsoft Office software (Word, Excel, Powerpoint); project management software a plus; Aptify competence a plus</li>
<li>Ability to organize and prioritize workload to meet deadlines</li>
<li>Deadline driven with good attention to detail</li>
<li>Stong written and verbal communications skills</li>
<li>Detail oriented and adept at multi-tasking</li>
<li>Excellent technical skills; willingness to learn Aptify/SharePoint</li>
<li>Excellent time and project management skills</li>
<li>Ability to build and maintain productive relationships with peers and cross functional teams</li>
</ul>

<p>Manual Dexterity: Physically operate routine office equipment, such as telephones, computers and copier.</p>
