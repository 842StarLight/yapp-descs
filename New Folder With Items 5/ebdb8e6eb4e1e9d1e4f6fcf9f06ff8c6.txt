<p><b>Agricultural Sales Representative</b></p>
<p>Liphatech currently has an exciting opportunity for an Agricultural Sales Representative with a strong emphasis on Animal Science and Orchard Crops. This territory includes VA, WV, KY, OH, PA, NJ, MD, NY, VT, RI, NH, ME, MA, and CT. The Agricultural Sales Representative covers all sales functions in an assigned territory including; sales growth, development of existing and new end users, working with animal health and field product distributor sales representatives based on a relationship of trust and commitment while delivering a high level of customer service, contributing to the development of an annual District Sales Plan, and communicating to the Business Director, Ag Division and other company management important information regarding the marketplace, customers, and competition.</p>
<p><b>Essential Duties and Responsibilities: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Secure purchase orders from existing and potential customers by promoting product and service benefits.</li>
<li>Establish and maintain professional customer relationships with appropriate customer personnel.</li>
<li>Conduct informative training sessions that educate end user and distributor customers on rodent behavior, baiting techniques, and product benefits.</li>
<li>Submit weekly report as defined by the categories on the Ag Division Report form.</li>
<li>Perform all duties assigned by Business Director, Agricultural Division.</li>
<li>Submit an expense report weekly, if possible, if not possible, a minimum of every other week.</li>
<li>Control expenses to stay within approved district expense budget.</li>
<li>Collaborate weekly by phone with Business Director.</li>
<li>Maintain a rolling three month electronic calendar with key events identified. As part of this 3 month calendar, maintain 2-3 weeks of activities that include: specific topics, people, meeting times and locations.</li>
<li>Use the CRM program to manage customers and your district.</li>
<li>Inform customers of supply and pricing information.</li>
<li>Keep customers informed on new products/services and other general information of interest to customers.</li>
<li>Manage customer complaints by notifying proper department of the organization and following up to ensure complaint is resolved.</li>
<li>Represent Company at trade association meetings to promote products.</li>
<li>Comply with all Liphatech safety policies and procedures.</li>
</ul>
