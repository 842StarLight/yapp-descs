<p>American Music &amp; Sound (AM&amp;S) is a leading independent distributor of Pro Audio, Music Creation, and Musical Instrument products from the world's finest manufacturers. To learn more about American Music &amp; Sound, please visit our Web site at <i>http://www.americanmusicandsound.com/</i> .</p>
<p><b>JOB PURPOSE</b></p>
<p>Work closely with our teams in the US and Italy to increase both brand awareness and demand within the US market, while projecting a positive image for the dBTechnologies brand.</p>
<p>Manage traditional and digital marketing agendas based on local insights in sync with the team at dBT HQ in Italy.</p>
<p>Organize and implement resources to optimize Key Performance Indicators.</p>
<p>Most importantly, we are looking for someone who’s outgoing, self-motivated, has a positive attitude, and understands the professional audio industry culture.</p>
<p><b>DUTIES &amp; RESPONSIBILITIES</b></p>
<p>Duties and responsibilities may include but are not limited to the following:</p>
<ul>
<li>Plan and implement a marketing strategy to continue building the dBTechnologies brand and support sales growth in the Unites States.</li>
<li>Work in close partnership with dBTechnologies global HQ.</li>
<li>Maintain a relevant and contemporary social media presence for the brand.</li>
<li>Initiate and run effective digital marketing campaigns, including pay-per-click, paid social and email marketing.</li>
<li>Travel to live shows to capture visual content and interview leading audio engineers.</li>
<li>Attend tradeshows and work closely with global HQ on tradeshow preparation</li>
<li>Commission and/or execute photography and video of high-profile tours and installations where our product is used.</li>
<li>Create and adapt advertising artwork and other marketing collateral.</li>
<li>Work in partnership with leading audio retailers to identify collaborations and to ensure that our products are presented in the best possible ways.</li>
<li>Plan and manage marketing resources per agreed budgets.</li>
<li>Monitor results of marketing activities and competitor’s activity.</li>
</ul>

</li>

<li>Interest in/familiarity with audio equipment a plus;</li>
<li>Experience with Adobe Illustrator and Photoshop;</li>
<li>Experience within the <b>professional audio industry a plus.</b>
</li>
</ul>
