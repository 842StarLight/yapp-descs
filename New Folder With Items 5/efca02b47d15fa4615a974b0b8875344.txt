<h2>Logistics and Dispatcher Coordinator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Logistics Coordinator/Dispatcher - Power Only Transport </b></p>
<p>Power Only Transport, LLC is growing and is seeking a qualified applicant to fill the <b><i>newly added position </i></b>of Logistics Coordinator/Dispatcher. In this role this individual will solicit/book freight and trailers, coordinate and schedule pick up and deliveries of loads and trailers for dispatching on company trucks and drivers. Other duties include soliciting extra freight and trailers and soliciting carriers to haul excess freight or trailers. Nevertheless, assist Transportation/Logistics Manager and Operations Manager with sales leads generation through cold calling and other sources.</p>
<p><b>What you will be doing: </b></p>
<p><b>Dispatching: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Plan, organize and timely assignment of loads in relation to pick-up and delivery of trailers based on company and customer requirement as well as driver availability making appropriate update/changes as necessary.</li>
<li>Communicates assignment of loads to Operations Manager and drivers providing specific loading and shipping requirements.</li>
<li>Monitor in-transit deliveries managing service issues in order to ensure on-time delivery of loads.</li>
<li>Assists Safety Manager in completing incident/accident reports as necessary ensuring the proper information is obtained from the drivers</li>
<li>Ensures all internal/external paperwork meets all company and DOT regulations and procedures</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Brokerage</b>:</p>
<ul>
<li>Communicate with customers via phone, email, mail or personally</li>
<li>Dispatch, tracking and tracing brokerage loads and trailers to ensure timely pick-up and delivery assist in brokerage billing for carrier payments</li>
<li>Releasing/tracking trailers from the Utility Portal</li>
<li>Data entry of loads in system as well as updating website load database</li>
<li>Verify carrier compliance for authority, insurance etc.</li>
<li>Assist Transportation/Logistics Manager and Operations Manager with sales leads generation through cold calling and other sources</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Other Duties: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Answerable for the procedures of Omni-Tracs Electronic Logging System (ELD). Monitor drivers’ hours as well as educate them on time management.</li>
<li>Be the Go-To person to trouble shoot issues with the system to help the drivers alleviate any problems.</li>
<li>Responsible for processing driver settlements, trip envelopes, calculating and submitting payroll.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>What you will need: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Must have a high degree of knowledge, understanding or ability to learn the “Power Only” transportation business</li>
<li>Prior experience within the trucking, transportation and brokerage industry desired</li>
<li>General knowledge of transportation logistics and coordination</li>
<li>Proficient in data entry</li>
<li>Knowledge of Truckwin software a plus</li>
<li>High School Diploma or equivalent</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Key Competencies: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Must possess and have the ability to interact with a high degree of professionalism</li>
<li>Excellent written and communication skills, able to communicate clearly and concisely</li>
<li>Superior listening and problem resolution skills</li>
<li>Ability to effectively deal with frequent delays or unexpected events</li>
<li>Ability to multi-task</li>
<li>Computer proficiency</li>
<li>Above average customer service skills</li>
</ul>
<p><b>What we offer: </b></p>
<p>We offer excellent pay and a comprehensive benefit package that includes health insurance, HSA, long term disability, and life insurance, 401(k), as well as voluntary options such as dental, vision, and short term disability. Paid vacation. Hours are generally Monday-Friday 8:00am to 5:00pm. Qualified applicants should email resume or complete one online at www.driveforpower.com. You may also fill out an application in person at Power Only Transport, LLC, at 5700 N. Seubert Ave, Sioux Falls, SD 57104. No phone calls please. We are an equal opportunity employer.</p>
<p>Job Type: Full-time</p>
