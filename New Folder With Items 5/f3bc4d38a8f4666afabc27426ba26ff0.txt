<div><b>RESPONSIBILITIES:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Provides timely, courteous service and assistance in response to fleet inquiries.
</li>
<li>Handles HEAT support correspondence to track issues/requests, document updates and resolutions, and maintaining activity reports.</li>
<li>Enforces fleet policy to ensure the efficient and economical use and operation of several hundreds of company-owned or leased vehicles (inclusive of cars, trailers, owned or leased tractors and pickup trucks).
</li>
<li>Conducts scheduled audits of fleet data.</li>
<li>Maintains accurate inventories of the equipment and titles.</li>
<li>Processes invoices by obtaining approvals and providing accurate G/L coding.</li>
<li>Identifies cost savings, cost avoidance and improvement opportunities.</li>
<li>Handles the acquisition and disposal for company vehicles.</li>
<li>Coordinates vehicle inspection, licensing, titles, insurance, safety, and accident reviews.</li>
<li>Monitors maintenance schedules, keeps accurate inventory of vehicles and other activities that support the operations of the equipment.</li>
<li>Develops and maintains performance reporting.</li>
<li>Other duties as be assigned.</li>
</ul>
<div><b>REQUIREMENTS:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>A minimum of 2 years business experience, preferably in customer service, finance, audit, transportation, logistics, or supply chain.</li>
<li>Experience involving company car or truck fleets is desirable.</li>
<li>Good communication skills, written and verbal.</li>
<li>Proficient in Microsoft Office programs.</li>
<li>Self-starter able to work in a team environment.</li>
</ul>
