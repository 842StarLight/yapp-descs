<h2>Lids General Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div></div>
<div>
<div><div>
<p><b>Essential Duties and Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
DRIVE sales by delivering and modeling exceptional customer service.</li>
<li>
Develop store schedules and monitor payroll plans daily to ensure that budget is met.</li>
<li>
Train store personnel on standard operating procedures as well as State and Federal laws and regulations.</li>
<li>
Communicate consistently with store team members to ensure understanding and compliance with company directives.</li>
<li>
Act as the liaison between the District Sales Manager and the store team members.</li>
<li>
Take appropriate actions to ensure each store maximizes sales, minimizes asset loss, and conforms to budgetary requirements.</li>
<li>
Model, support and adhere to company policies, procedures, and guidelines.</li>
<li>
Communicate with employees at all levels of the company.</li>
<li>
Ability to work varying days and hours, based on business needs.</li>
<li>
Ability to maintain an excellent attendance record.</li>
<li>
Other duties as assigned.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Supervisory Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Carry out managerial responsibilities in accordance with LIDS policies, procedures, and applicable laws.</li>
<li>
Supervise store employees through planning, assigning, and directing their overall functions.</li>
<li>
Appraise subordinates on positive performance and assist with formal performance evaluations.</li>
<li>
Recruit, select, develop, and train store personnel on proper store operations and procedures.</li>
<li>
Administer the progressive steps of discipline to include verbal and written warnings.</li>
<li>
Direct compliance of store personnel with established company policies, procedures and guidelines including, but not limited to, safekeeping of company inventory, funds and property.</li>
<li>
Address complaints and problem solve when appropriate with the assistance of the DSM and or RD.</li>
</ul>

Proven managerial skills</li>
<li>
Proven ability to perform independently with minimal supervision</li>

Ability to operate a computer, as well as maneuver relative software programs.</li>

Standing required for up to 90% of the daily work schedule.</li>
<li>
Relative computer hardware and software experience.</li>
</ul>
