<p></p>
<p>The <b>Business Office Manager/Skilled Nursing Facility Biller</b> is responsible for overseeing business functions and day-to-day operations of the billing and financial reporting systems for the Skilled Nursing Facility and Outpatient Therapy. The position will also provide support and act as the backup to the Home Health Billing Specialist.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Essential Functions</b>:</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Oversee the submission of all required Medicare documentation to ensure timely submission of regular billing, demand bills, medical review requests, adjustments, post payment reviews, and denied claims issues.</li>
<li>Coordinate the processing of Medicare claims data to supplemental insurance carriers.</li>
<li>Coordinate or mediate billing issues with the billing software resolution specialists.</li>
<li>Process charges for consolidated billing for the health center and outpatient therapy.</li>
<li>Input/update all charges from vendors or in the billing system that are necessary for the proper billing of skilled nursing claims.</li>
<li>Address any denials or delays in Medicare claim payments in a timely manner.</li>
<li>Submit statistical reports on a monthly, quarterly, and annual basis.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Requirements</b>:</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor or Associate degree in accounting or equivalent experience.</li>
<li>Experience working in a billing department processing charges, invoices and statements.</li>
<li>General office experience.</li>
<li>Intermediate computer knowledge. Able to use MS Word, Excel, Outlook and Windows.</li>
</ul>
