<h2>Jr. Buyer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Position: Jr. Buyer</b></p>
<p><b>
Location: </b>Long Beach, CA</p>
<p><b>
Department: </b>Purchasing</p>
<p><b>
Reports to: </b>Procurement Manager</p>
<p><b>
Schedule:</b> Monday – Friday; Some short-notice travel may be required</p>
<p>
Under general supervision, the Jr. Buyer works as part of Curtin Maritime’s Purchasing team. The Purchasing department is responsible for purchasing Goods and Services for all company projects, vessels, shipyard and inventory needs. Curtin Maritime has 75-100 employees and 20+ vessels.</p>
<p><b>
RESPONSIBILITIES/GOALS:</b><b>
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Lead purchaser for all vessel requests posted in HELM connect, our vessel management software.</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Create, execute and receive purchase orders thru our Quickbooks purchase order system.</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Act as a liaison with our Accounting department by coding, matching and scanning all pertinent documents.</li>
<li>Oversee receiving of shipments and verify packing slips.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>TASKS AND DUTIES:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Lead and/or assist with all company purchases from request to fulfillment</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Create/Maintain company database of necessary parts and supplies</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Work with Accounting team in maintaining a paperless collection of backup documents</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Assist in managing the Tool Crib employees to anticipate the future needs of our fleet</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Ad hoc projects for the Procurement manager, or other company leadership.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p>
</p>
<p><b>SKILLS, APTITUDES, QUALIFICATIONS AND CERTIFICATIONS:</b><b>
</b></p>
<ul><li>Bachelor’s Degree or equivalent preferred</li></ul>
<ul><li>1-2 years purchasing experience; ideally involving tools, hardware, and/or raw materials</li></ul>
<ul><li>Should have a basic knowledge of hand tools, power tools, and common hardware</li></ul>
<ul><li>Experience in QuickBooks or HELM connect a plus</li></ul>
<ul><li>Must have a valid driver license and clean driving record (5+ years)</li></ul>
<ul><li>Must be able to obtain a valid Passport</li></ul>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
Mental Aptitudes Required:</p>
<ul>
<li>Ability to prioritize requests efficiently</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Strong communication skills, verbal and written</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Multitasking</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Strong organizational skills</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Ability to work under pressure of deadlines.</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Negotiating skills in working with vendors<br>
</li>
</ul>
