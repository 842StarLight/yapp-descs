<h2>Underwriting Assistant</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>
<div>Corvus is creating a safer, more productive world through technology-enabled commercial insurance. Utilizing sensors, wearables, IoT and other emerging technologies, Corvus combines data and digital tools with insurance expertise to create relevant commercial insurance products and reduce the total cost of risk. Delivering its solutions worldwide, Corvus is reinventing the commercial insurance buying experience making it accessible and transparent for brokers and buyers. A leader in commercial insurance innovation, Corvus is headquartered in Boston, Massachusetts.</div>
<div>

<div>
REQUIREMENTS</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
Log new business and renewal submissions and assist underwriters with quotes, binders, and policy issuance workflows</div>
<div>
Review submission materials and policies, ensuring that they are complete and accurate</div>
<div>
Frequently interact with brokers to follow up for outstanding underwriting information, process BORs, and respond to endorsement requests.</div>
<div>
Sign up new brokers to work with the CrowBar, the proprietary software platform of Corvus</div>
<div>
Handle and respond to broker queries in a timely fashion (emails the same day and phone calls within 24 hours)</div>
<div>
Maintain account files in accordance with company policy and compliance guidelines</div>
<div>
Assist underwriters in evaluating risks and determining if certain exposures fall within the scope of Corvus’ underwriting guidelines\</div>
<div>

</div>
