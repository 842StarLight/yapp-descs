<h2>Regional Sales Director - Corporate Integrated Risk team</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Led by an action-driven leadership team, employees at Workiva develop and provide cutting-edge solutions and services to the business market. We firmly believe our people are our greatest assets, and our unique culture gives employees the opportunity to not only make a difference, but BE the difference.</p>
<p>
The Corporate Integrated Risk Sales Director (CIRSD) is accountable for driving sales of our Wdesk Integrated Risk platform, services, and solutions to target prospect companies through strategic conversations at all levels within these organizations. The CIRSD will work in conjunction with the Corporate RSD to grow and expand the opportunity. This expansion will focus on opportunities within our existing customer base to incrementally increase sales volume via additional growth engines and Workiva solutions.</p>
<p>
What You'll Do:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Provide market expertise and domain experience to build Value Framework with the Corporate RSD</li>
<li>
Close new business opportunities and expand opportunities within existing customers.</li>
<li>
Conduct needs analysis to uncover cross-selling opportunities.</li>
<li>
Plan and execute sales strategy.</li>
<li>
Provide consistent and accurate forward-looking analysis.</li>
<li>
Probe for objections, remove obstacles and eliminate barriers to gain commitment.</li>
<li>
Naturally lead the sales process to a close by demonstrating Workiva's superior value proposition.</li>
<li>
Maintain the customer relationship as well as grow the business.</li>
</ul>

<ul>
