<h2>Customer Resource Center Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Edupoint Educational Systems, a "Top Companies to Work For" recipient is currently seeking a <b>Customer Resource Center Specialist </b>to join our growing team of professionals. If you enjoy being a part of a <b>dynamic team</b> based environment, working a consistent <b>M-F work schedule,</b> and being rewarded for a strong work ethic, please apply! This position will be responsible for providing technical software support and services for Edupoint’s web based Synergy Student Information System to client K-12 school district personnel by answering calls and email requests from customers, providing training and creating documentation. This includes resolving product issues, identifying product defects and enhancements, creating and communicating workarounds, conducting training, testing software, reviewing documentation, supporting product development, and assisting with sales efforts.</p>
<p>We offer a <b>Comprehensive Package</b> to include generous <b>Paid Time Off</b>, <b>excellent medical, dental, and vision coverage, life insurance, short and long term disability, wellness programs, and 401K with immediate vesting opportunities. </b>If you are a <b>customer service </b><b>rock star</b> and enjoy working with a dynamic team and would enjoy working in the K-12 industry, we want to hear from you!</p>
<p><b>Job Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Acquire and maintain working knowledge of Edupoint’ s products.</li>
<li>Acquire and maintain expert knowledge of the Synergy Student Information Systems applications and tools</li>
<li>Acquire and maintain an understanding of current Customer Resource Center policies and practices.</li>
<li>Provide prompt, accurate and knowledgeable support to Edupoint’s customers by responding to, researching, troubleshooting, diagnosing and resolving highly complex technical questions received via telephone calls, e-mails and through the customer portal.</li>
<li>Handle inbound calls and manage workload to ensure clients receive timely responses.</li>
<li>Use available resources such as the Synergy Help Desk Web Application, knowledge base, documentation etc., to research reported issues.</li>
<li>Use basic query tools to examine data and pinpoint problems.</li>
<li>Communicate effectively in writing all the steps needed to reproduce errors or problematic behavior in applications using specified formats.</li>
<li>Log activity to resolve issues, including customer interactions and e-mails in the Synergy Help Desk Web Application.</li>
<li>Escalate issues when needed by following internal escalation protocol.</li>
<li>Conduct root cause analysis on incidents and communicate the results.</li>
<li>Other duties as assigned.</li>
</ul> <p><b>Job Requirements:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>High School Diploma.</li>
<li>Previous work experience in retail, hospitality, or customer service.</li>
</ul> <p><b>Compensation &amp; Benefits:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Medical/Dental/Vision Insurance</li>
<li>Company Paid for Life Insurance</li>
<li>Short and Long Term Disability</li>
<li>Wellness Programs</li>
<li>401(k) Plan with matching and immediate vesting</li>

</ul>
