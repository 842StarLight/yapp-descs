<h2>Director of Franciscan Retreat Center</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<b>Description:</b><br>
The Director oversees the daily operations of the Franciscan Retreat Center (FRC), assuring a positive experience for all guests. The Director is responsible for all business administration, planning, marketing and staff supervision. All aspects of this ministry are to be done in a manner consistent with the Sisters of St. Francis’ mission and values. .
<br><br>
<b>Requirements:</b><br>
<b>Key Responsibilities:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Generating sales in accordance with the current FRC Marketing Plan.</li>
<li>Providing lodging, conference rooms and food service in a manner consistent with the Franciscan Retreat Center mission, values and policies.</li>
<li>Supervising all employees, including hiring, assigning and directing work.</li>
<li>Managing finances: budget, revenue and expenses.</li>
<li>Handling billing and bookkeeping activities.</li>
<li>Purchasing equipment, supplies and services within budget.</li>
<li>Reporting to CEO and FRC Board of Directors.</li>
</ul>
<b>Qualifications:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Minimum of Bachelor’s Degree; or three years related experience; or equivalent combination of education and experience.</li>
<li>Master’s degree in related field preferred</li>
<li>Minimum of three years’ experience in business administration</li>
<li>Demonstrated leadership and management skills</li>
<li>Knowledge of accounting software, such as QuickBooks, preferred</li>
<li>Experience working/volunteering in a not-for-profit organization; retreat/conference ministry experience preferred</li>
<li>Alignment with the Franciscan Retreat Center’s mission and values</li>
</ul>
<b>Benefits:</b><br>
We offer excellent benefits, competitive wages, excellent insurance plans (medical, dental, short/long term disability and life insurance), Paid Time Off (PTO), Personal Time, Sick Leave, and 401(k) with up to 4% employer match.
<br><br>
<b>Salary:</b><br>
This is a full-time, exempt position. Salary will be commensurate with qualifications and experience.
<br><br>
