<h2>Customer Support Advisor (SatCom Trainer)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p><b>Job Summary: </b>Serve as main customer contact at customer sites and support all requirements and requests from the customer. Work autonomously as part of the Support Services team to ensure customer success, provide training and troubleshooting, encourage follow-on sales, and maintain effective customer relationships. This position typically works under general supervision and direction. Incumbents of this position will regularly exercise discretionary and substantial decision-making authority.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Essential Job Duties and Responsibilities:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Support demonstrations, trade shows, and other deployments to reinforce customer relationships and identify potential new customers
</li>
<li>Travel to customer sites
<ul>
<li>to support new customer deliveries and conduct new operator training
</li>
<li>to verify operator proficiency, provide retraining, and identify additional equipment sale opportunities
</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>Provide basic training to personnel to ensure employees have product knowledge commensurate with job responsibilities
</li>
<li>Performs field troubleshooting, diagnostics, rework and retrofit of electronics hardware and software subassemblies as required in training situations
</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Maintain customer tracking program to maximize use of account metrics
</li>
<li>Participate in scheduled meetings with leadership and travel to headquarters as required to maintain effective relationships
</li>
<li>Assists in developing roadmaps for future product offerings, collecting customer needs for catalog product improvements
</li>
<li>Maintains customer tracking program to maximize use of account metrics
</li>
<li>Assists in the training of Associate CSRs
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Minimum Job Requirements:
</b></p>
<p>Four year college degree in Business, Marketing, or Engineering and two years related experience or equivalent combination of education and experience. Networking, and training experience required. Ability to obtain security clearance required. Military experience preferred. Excellent communication and presentation skills. Strong knowledge of basic network management. Hands-on experience with systems and networking equipment.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><i>The description provided above is not intended to be an exhaustive list of all job duties, responsibilities and requirements. Duties, responsibilities and requirements may change over time and according to business need.
</i></p>
