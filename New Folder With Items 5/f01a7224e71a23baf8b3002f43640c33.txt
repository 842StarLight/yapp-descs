<h2>Operations Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Are you a dynamic leader looking for an awesome opportunity to work for one of the best-known brands in Central New York? That One-Big-Store-In-Solvay is looking for someone to join the leadership team who wants to help us take China Towne to the next level of success. We want someone with big ideas and the vision and skills to implement those ideas, working across departments to help us deliver the best possible customer experience.</b></p>
<p><b>
JOB SUMMARY</b></p>
<p>
The Operations Manager is responsible for coordinating the overall efficiency and effectiveness of all aspects of China Towne Furniture and Mattress by working with the various department leaders, establishing the key metrics for success and implementing plans to support the CEO and with travel arrangements, calendaring, expense reports, etc. Must be resourceful, an independent thinker, and self-sufficient as well as self-motivated.</p>
<p><b>
ESSENTIAL JOB DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES</b></p>
<p>
40% Coordinate departmental operations and communication including developing and implementing procedures and policies. Establish and review key operational metrics. Resolve issues between departments. Lead weekly departmental meetings and staff meetings. Responsible for maintaining inventory accuracy.</p>
<div><p>
40% Administer special projects in cooperation with department leaders and under the direction of the CEO. May include work with suppliers, manufacturers, marketing, community engagement, and/or advertising.</p></div>
<div>
<p>
10% Manage and assist department heads in the human resources operations including coordinating recruiting, interviewing, and hiring. Update personnel policies for compliance and best practices.</p>
<p>
5% Responsible for the day-to-day facilities operations including supervising the maintenance and alteration of office areas and equipment such as the layout, arrangement, and housekeeping of office facilities; purchasing office supplies, furniture, office equipment, etc. for the entire staff</p>
</div>
<div><p>
5% Maintaining confidentiality of sensitive and confidential information</p></div>
<p><b>
MINIMUM QUALIFICATION STANDARDS</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
College diploma</li>
<li>
Bachelor’s degree in business, marketing, or related field preferred.</li>
<li>
One to three years of related management.</li>
<li>
An equivalent combination of education and experience will be considered.</li>
<li>
Must have at the time of hire, and maintain throughout employment, a valid NYS drivers’ license free of major infractions with insurance coverage acceptable to the company’s insurance carrier.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
KNOWLEDGE, SKILLS &amp; ABILITIES</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Ability to communicate effectively, both orally and in writing.</li>
<li>
Knowledge of planning and scheduling techniques.</li>
<li>
Strong interpersonal and communication skills and the ability to work effectively with a wide range of constituencies in a diverse community.</li>
<li>
Ability to develop, plan, and implement short- and long-range goals.</li>
<li>
Ability to analyze and solve problems.</li>
<li>
Ability to gather data, analyze information, and prepare reports.</li>
<li>
Ability to review and analyze financial statements.</li>
<li>
Ability to identify and/or follow up sales leads and referrals.</li>
<li>
Ability to operate a computer to send/receive email messages, post information to a database or spreadsheet, and perform basic word processing and/or data entry.</li>
<li>
Ability to work cooperatively and demonstrate initiative in achieving operational goals.</li>
<li>
Ability to travel throughout the area on a regular basis.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
EQUIPMENT, MACHINES, AND SOFTWARE USED</b></p>
<p>
Ability to operate a computer, printer, photocopier, telephone, fax machine, and calculator.</p>
<p>
Computer Software: Microsoft Office.</p>
<p><b>
MENTAL AND VISUAL REQUIREMENTS</b></p>
<p>
Strong mental and visual attention required for performing work where there is variety and frequent decision making. Work requires constant coordination with others.</p>
<p><b>
PHYSICAL ACTIVITIES AND REQUIREMENTS</b></p>
<p>
While performing the duties of this position, the employee is regularly required to sit, stand, walk, talk, hear, and use hands to finger, grasp, handle or feel.</p>
<p>
The physical requirements of this position call for the ability to exert up to 25 pounds of force occasionally and/or a negligible amount of force frequently or constantly to lift, carry, push, pull or otherwise move objects.</p>
<p><b>
ENVIRONMENTAL CONDITIONS</b></p>
<p>
The employee will be primarily in an office environment.</p>
