<h2>Marketing Coordinator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Responsibilities: </b></p>
<p>· Help plan and coordinate marketing activities and special events</p>
<p>· Assist VP of Marketing and VP of Business Development with the execution and implementation of campaigns by ensuring all deliverables are on track for completion according to deadlines, and creative in adherence to campaign objectives and brand guidelines.</p>
<p>· Generate reports, maintain and update records of marketing metrics and results of past campaigns.</p>
<p>· Assist in setting brand standards, mission, and goals.</p>
<p>· Have strong social media application knowledge.</p>
<p>· Manage the workflow for creation, production and delivery of marketing materials. Work with internal departments and external agencies, draft creative briefs, and provide creative feedback.</p>
<p>· Write resumes, boilerplate information, project descriptions, etc.</p>
<p>· Work with technical staff to develop proposal sections and scopes of work</p>
<p>· Help develop and manage marketing database</p>
<p>· Update social media and website information</p>
<p>· Coordinate specialized consultants (photographers, PR, etc.)</p>
<p>· Research target markets</p>
<p>· Manage CRM database</p>
<p>· Actively participate in professional and community organizations, including SMPS</p>
<p><b>Skill Set: </b></p>
<p>· Experienced proposal writer</p>
<p>· Proficient with MS Office 365, including word processing software</p>
<p>· Proficient with Adobe Creative Suite</p>
<p>· Organizational skills, multi-tasking</p>
<p>· Detail oriented</p>
<p>· Excellent time management skills</p>
<p>· Basic understanding of A/E/C industry terminology and procedures</p>
<p>· Working knowledge of marketing databases and proposal resources files. Cosential experience a big plus</p>
<p>· Understanding of basic marketing principles</p>
<p>· Excellent communication and presentation skills</p>
<p>· Strong analytical skills with a goal-oriented attitude</p>
<p>· Able to analyze problems and strategize for better solutions</p>
<p><b>Education/Certification Requirements: </b></p>
<p>· Bachelor’s degree with one to three years of experience recommended</p>
<p>· Without a degree, three to five years of relevant A/E/C experience required</p>
<p>· Knowledge of marketing digital tools and techniques</p>
<p>· Preferred: Current or preparation to earn the Certified Professional Services Marketer (CPSM) certification</p>
<p>Job Type: Full-time</p>
<p>Experience:</p>
<ul><li>Marketing: 1 year (Preferred)</li></ul>

<ul>
<li>Health insurance</li>
<li>Dental insurance</li>

</ul>
