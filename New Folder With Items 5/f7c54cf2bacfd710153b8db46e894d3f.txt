<p><b>Software Implementation Specialist</b></p>
<p>AINS seeks to expand our software implementation team with the addition of a talented <b>Software Implementation Specialist </b>(Software Configuration Specialist).</p>
<p>The ideal candidate will have excellent writing and communication skills, a strong customer service and engagement ability, some project management experience, a quick learner, and is technically savvy.</p>
<p><b>Responsibilities </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Provide an engaging top notch customer service experience to all customers.</li>
<li>Analyze, capture, document and illustrate “as-is” and “to-be” business processes and requirements.</li>
<li>Prepare documentation such as; functional/business requirements, use cases, and instructional documentation.</li>
<li>Configure, test, and implement business processes and requirements.</li>
<li>Provide onsite/online application training as needed.</li>
<li>Provide product demonstrations onsite/online to support project milestones as well as to support sales team.</li>
<li>Manage assigned project tasks in a timely manner and coordinate resources needed to complete tasks.</li>
<li>Provide weekly reports/updates of assigned tasks and progress to project manager/management.</li>
<li>Be prepared to play multiple roles in the implementation of a project.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Requirements</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Customer service/engagement experience</li>
<li>Excellent writing and verbal communication skills</li>
<li>Quick learner with strong multi-tasking abilities</li>
<li>Advanced knowledge of Microsoft Product Suite; Word, Excel, Project and Visio</li>
<li>Must be available for occasion travel to support project or product promotion efforts</li>
<li>Must have method of transportation to support daily onsite/office transitions</li>
</ul>
<p><b>US Citizen with the ability to obtain a US Federal Government Security Clearance</b></p>
