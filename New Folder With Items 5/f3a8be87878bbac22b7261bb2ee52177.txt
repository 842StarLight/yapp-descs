<h2>Lease Operator 1 or 2</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p>This role is for an 8 days on shift and 6 days off rotation<br>
<br>
Gauge tanks, read meters and record data.<br>
<br>
Inspect equipment and facilities for problems and do minor repairs.<br>
<br>
Perform basic maintenance work on surface equipment.<br>
<br>
Monitor chemical and freshwater usage<br>
<br>
Adhere to all Equinor environmental and safety policies.<br>
<br>
Communicate with production foreman and other lease operators/techs on a daily basis.<br>
<br>
Keep company vehicle maintained and in good working condition.<br>
<br>
Coordinate oil sales and salt water hauling.<br>
<br>
Maintain gauge sheets properly and note reasons for production changes.<br>
<br>
Ability to diagnose well problems and Identify downhole failures<br>
<br>
Conduct business with the highest ethical standards and demonstrate good decision making skills.<br>
<br>
Other duties as assigned<br>
</p>
<h4 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Qualifications</b></h4>
<p>
High school or secondary education<br>
<br>
1+ years experience in production operations.<br>
<br>
Extensive knowledge and experience with Industry and Best-Practices as applicable.<br>
<br>
Demonstrated propensity for identifying and acting on opportunities for innovation and creativity.<br>
<br>
While performing the duties of this job, the employee is regularly required to stand, use hands, and reach with hands and arms.<br>
<br>
The employee is required to walk, stoop, kneel, crouch, or crawl.<br>
<br>
The employee may be required to sometimes sit, climb or balance.<br>
<br>
Ability to lift up to 50 lbs with manual dexterity to lift, bend and jump. The employee is occasionally exposed to fumes or airborne particles.<br>
<br>
The employee may be exposed to a wide range of temperatures.<br>
<br>
The noise level is usually loud.<br>
<br>
Twenty-four hour on-call responsibilities.<br>
<br>
Relevant experience may compensate for formal qualifications.<br>
<br>
</p>
<h4 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Personal Qualities</b></h4>
<p>
Excellent written and oral communication skills and strong interpersonal skills.<br>
<br>
Ability to communicate with all levels. Ability to organize work, engage in a variety of tasks simultaneously and consistently meet deadlines.<br>
<br>
Ability to deviate from established procedures when special problems or opportunities arise.<br>
<br>
Developed knowledge of risk management and safety requirements affecting oil and gas production. Must be detail oriented. Strong mechanical aptitude.<br>
<br>
Team building skills; organizational and staff development skills. Knowledge of budgeting and expense control.<br>
<br>
High degree of initiative and independent judgment.<br>
<br>
Computer skills; spreadsheet software capabilities.<br>
<br>
Ability to travel to various work site locations, meetings and training seminars.<br>
<br>
Ability to live by our safety and security expectations.<br>
</p>
