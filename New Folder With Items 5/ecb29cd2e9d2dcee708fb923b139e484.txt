<div>
<p>Altice USA (NYSE: ATUS) is one of the largest broadband communications and video services providers in the United States, delivering broadband, pay television, telephony services, proprietary content and advertising services to approximately 4.9 million Residential and Business customers across 21 states through its Optimum and Suddenlink brands.</p>
<p>
From high-speed broadband and ultra-HD video, to digital advertising solutions, local news and voice offerings, we are making connections possible around the country - to businesses, to consumers, to communities, to schools, to hospitals, and everyone in between.</p>
</div>
<p></p>
<div>
<p>The <b>Manager, Customer Growth Strategy</b> supports the design and implementation of the acquisition offers, designed to attract new customer relationships, improve product mix and sell in ARPU.</p>
<p>
This Manager will analyze market conditions and detailed customer data to support the creation of end to end product offer strategies and tactics to attract new segments and develop under penetrated areas. This Manager is the subject matter expert at the corporate level of the respective local markets under his/her responsibility.</p>
<p><b><br>
</b></p>
<p>This role will examine the characteristics of the different markets segments, the local competitive environments of the different markets, and monitor the performance of existing acquisition offers to inform the development of offer strategies and tactics that will drive growth to improve subscriber acquisition and profitability.</p>
<p>
The <b>Manager, Customer Growth Strategy</b> is a highly analytical role that should at the same time be able to influence in a highly matrixed environment. This role reports to the VP, Acquisition Strategy and will work cross functionally with key contacts in Sales, Product, Marketing, Marketing Analytics, Business Planning and Operations. Availability to travel frequently is a must.</p>
<p><b>
Main Responsibilities
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Develop detailed performance analysis at the offer, market and segment level;</li>
<li>
Support the development of an effective customer acquisition strategy and plan for highly competitive areas, under penetrated segments or underperforming markets;</li>
<li>
Be the subject matter expert at the corporate level of the respective offers, markets or segments under his/her responsibility;</li>
<li>
Understanding of the regional market performance, the competitive environment and collect customer insights;</li>
<li>
Measure the local effectiveness of marketing programs and tactics;</li>
<li>
Pull data from internal sources, and perform business interpretation of the several sources of raw data coming from other areas, and recommend offer development, marketing and media tactics;</li>
<li>
Monitor and react to the initial customer lifecycle KPIs (ex. gross adds, contact rates, NPS, early churn, etc);
</li>
<li>Perform competitive analysis, offer performance analysis, gather insights;</li>
<li>
Produce executive reporting;</li>
</ul>
