<h2>Director of Security and Compliance</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<b>PURPOSE:</b><br>
<br>
The Director of Security and Compliance will work as a primary operational lead for all Information Technology security and compliance activities. The Director will work closely with multiple cross-functional teams to understand customer and legal requirements, enforce security standards, implement and review designs, and provide solutions that support our business objectives. This role is a strategic leader while remaining hands-on technically.<br>
<br>
<b>RESPONSIBILITIES:</b><br>
<br>
Develop security architecture for highly scalable and fault-tolerant applications that adhere to expected standards and discipline from a security posture.<br>
<br>
Identify security design gaps in existing and proposed architectures and recommend changes or enhancements.<br>
<br>
Work cross-functionally to develop secure business requirements, develop the security architecture and integrate into our long-term platform strategy.<br>
<br>
Own security systems planning and implementation.<br>
<br>
Provide training and awareness on security and information technology compliance-related concerns to the extended team.<br>
<br>
Work directly with project development teams to enable successful project implementation by applying the recommended security tools, technologies and techniques. Provide expertise to engineers as needed.<br>
<br>
Drive comprehensive security policy development and enforce the policies.<br>
<br>
Lead risk management activities, including risk models and analysis of weaknesses.<br>
<br>
Lead incident response activities, tabletop exercises, penetration tests, and remediation efforts.<br>
<br>
Lead penetration testing efforts.<br>
<br>
Design and lead incident management efforts.<br>
<br>
Manage and lead vulnerability assessments.<br>
<br>
<b>SKILLS AND QUALIFICATIONS:</b><br>
<br>
Proven track record and experience in developing information security programs, policies and procedures, including successful implementations in medium to large enterprise environments.<br>
<br>
High degree of initiative, dependability; experience managing multiple, simultaneous, and high-profile information security initiatives and responses.<br>
<br>
Grasp of the “big picture” (company structure and key business initiatives).<br>
<br>
Ability to review internal systems at the code level and make recommendations on that basis<br>
<br>
Strategic mindset with a desire to be hands-on technically.<br>
<br>
Sense of urgency, with a positive attitude.<br>
<br>
Proven excellent organizational, coordination and negotiation skills.<br>
<br>
Ability to effectively prioritize and execute in a high-pressure environment.<br>
<br>
Team player with proven relationship building skills.<br>
<br>
Excellent interpersonal and verbal and written communication skills.<br>
<br>
Ability to interact and work effectively across all functional areas (e.g., Technology, Sales, Marketing, Finance, HR) and at all levels of the organization.<br>
<br>
Effective leadership and influencing skills.<br>
<br>
Sound business judgment. Must be able to set priorities, make plans to organize work, and perform multiple tasks concurrently.<br>
<br>
Experience in designing and managing new and existing security systems.<br>
<br>
Ability to advise infrastructure and applications staff in securing their respective environments.<br>
<br>
Experience with contract and vendor negotiations.<br>
<br>
Ability to provide technical and professional leadership, guidance, and training to others.<br>
<br>
<b>EDUCATION AND EXPERIENCE:</b><br>
<br>
Bachelor’s degree in relevant technical field.<br>
<br>
10+ years IT-related experience, including IT management, with 5+ years in the security area.<br>
<br>
Proven success with security compliance management.<br>
<br>
Experience implementing and managing IT security solutions.<br>
<br>
Software Development environment experience.</div></div>
