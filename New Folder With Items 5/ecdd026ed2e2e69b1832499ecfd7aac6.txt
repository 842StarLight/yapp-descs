<p><b>Role: </b>Global Spread Products is seeking an experienced Data Quality Analyst. Reporting through the COO line, this person will work closely with the front office and technology on multiple fronts to help execute strategic goals. This team sits at the intersection of sales, trading, structuring, capital markets origination and technology and is responsible for implementing process improvements and realizing senior management's vision. Our team oversees a wide array of front office systems including pricing and risk, trade capture, inventory management, origination, securitization, sales dashboards, management reporting, electronic trading and database management.</p>
<p><b>
Background:</b> Global Spread Products is the primary source of capital for corporations, governments, and non-profits, providing liquidity and innovative solutions globally across the credit, municipal, and securitized markets.</p>
<p><b>
Job Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Become familiar with all market, reference and application data sources within Global Spread Products and across the firm. Act as a subject matter expert when data needs or questions arise</li>
<li>
Troubleshoot root cause of data quality issues as they arise and work with technology partners to correct</li>
<li>
Identify problems with data quality by running regular checks</li>
<li>
Provide transparency to upstream data consumers on data owners, permissions, and accessibility. Help end-users to navigate the data landscape</li>
<li>
Develop quality checks for ongoing data ingestion and standard calculation conventions</li>
<li>
Work with Citi partners and vendors to define data needs</li>
<li>
Test and verify data acquisition and changes</li>
<li>
Work with technology and vendors to resolve issues</li>
<li>
Respond to ad-hoc data requests and provide reporting as needed</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
 Required Skills and Qualifications</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Candidate is required to have a BA/BS</li>
<li>
3+ years’ experience in data analysis, data quality, or systems architecture.</li>
<li>
Ability to work in a dynamic environment under tight deadlines with changing priorities</li>
<li>
Experience with Oracle, KDB, Clojure, and Google Cloud is preferred</li>
<li>
SQL, VBA, Python, Qlikview, Cognos or other BI/data visualization tools is preferred</li>
</ul>
<ul><li>
Strong data querying, MS Excel and quantitative skills required</li></ul>
<ul><li>
Excellent communication, organizational and interpersonal skills, as well as the ability to multi-task under tight deadlines</li></ul>
<ul><li>
Must have strong attention to detail, be self-motivated and inquisitive with an interest in financial markets</li></ul>
<ul><li>
Experience with Fixed Income or other Capital Markets a plus</li></ul>
