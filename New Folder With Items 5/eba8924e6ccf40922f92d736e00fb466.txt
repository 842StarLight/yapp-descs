<h2>Project Manager</h2>

<div>
<p>Position:<br>
Project Manager</p>
<p>Job<br>
Description Summary</p>
<p>The Project Manager is responsible for managing the<br>
appropriate resources required to meet client expectations to deliver a<br>
fully-functional system on budget and on time. Specific responsibilities include:</p>
<ul><li> Conduct project kick off meetings with Sales,</li></ul>
Design and Engineering to gain overall project vision and define project<br>
schedule.
<ul><li> Schedule and conduct project walk-through</li></ul>
with appropriate employees, subcontractors, general contractors, and clients.
<ul><li> Facilitate relationship-building with other</li></ul>
trades.
<ul><li> Conduct client interview to gather</li></ul>
information for the Project Site and introduce members of the Linx team
<ul><li> Manage project budget (labor hours,</li></ul>
materials, and equipment costs).
<ul><li> Ensure all equipment and materials are</li></ul>
ordered, received, and staged prior to the start of the project. Coordinate ordering through the Linx<br>
procurement team.
<ul><li> Ensure timely delivery of materials and</li></ul>
equipment to jobsite
<ul><li> Track key project milestones and adjust</li></ul>
project plans and/or resources to meet client needs and requirements
<ul><li> Manage change order requests from the</li></ul>
client. Coordinate with the appropriate<br>
sales resources for pricing and client acceptance as required.
<ul><li> Maintain project documents throughout the</li></ul>
project.
<ul><li> Coordinate Installation Technicians,</li></ul>
Engineering and Programming resources with other Project Managers and<br>
Operations Manager
<ul><li> Daily and/or Weekly updates with the client</li></ul>
on project status, scheduling, and open issues as required for the size of the<br>
project.
<ul><li> Communicate project status and scheduling</li></ul>
with employees and subcontractors
<ul><li> Communicate project status with Operations</li></ul>
Manager on a weekly basis
<ul><li> Overall jobsite cleanliness, organization,</li></ul>
materials disposal and recycling
<ul><li> Overall project quality control and feature</li></ul>
requirements
<ul><li> Provide assistance to Installation</li></ul>
Technicians as necessary to complete the project
<ul><li> Initiate and maintain Linx Field Directive</li></ul>
documents
<ul><li> Daily and Weekly maintenance of Project Task</li></ul>
and Punch List with Project Foreman.
<ul><li> Creation and delivery of Customer Notebook to</li></ul>
the Client including warranty terms and start/end date
<p>Conduct or coordinate appropriate resources to<br>
conduct client training.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>Perform a post close-out project review and<br>
lessons learned.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>Reporting<br>
Relationships</p>
<p>The Project Manager reports directly to the Operations<br>
Manager.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>Qualifications</p>
<p>To perform this job successfully, an individual must be<br>
able to perform each essential duty satisfactorily. The requirements listed<br>
below are representative of the knowledge, skill and ability required.<br>
Reasonable accommodations may be made to enable individuals with disabilities<br>
to perform the essential functions.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>Education<br>
and Experience</p>
<p>Four-year college degree preferred. Two years of project<br>
management experience in a fast paced environment. Experience in the<br>
construction industry, electronics integration, and/or audio/visual installation<br>
business is preferred. Must be<br>
proficient with desktop computer software and systems.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>Organizational<br>
and Time Management Skills</p>
<p>Self directed individual. Ability to develop timeline necessary for installation activities and<br>
product delivery. Ability to manage multiple projects and customers at various<br>
stages of the project life cycle. Assure that all time and resources are<br>
budgeted properly for maximum return on investment.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>Language<br>
Skills</p>
<p>Ability to read, analyze, and interpret general business<br>
periodicals, professional journals, technical procedures, or governmental<br>
regulations. Ability to write project schedules, reports, business<br>
correspondence, and procedures. Ability to effectively present information and<br>
respond to questions from employees, managers and customers.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>Mathematical<br>
Skills</p>
<p>Ability to calculate figures and amounts such as<br>
discounts, interest, commissions, proportions, percentages, area,<br>
circumference, and volume. Ability to apply concepts of basic algebra and<br>
geometry.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>Reasoning<br>
Ability</p>
<p>Ability to define problems, collect data, establish facts,<br>
and draw valid conclusions. Ability to interpret an extensive variety of<br>
technical instructions in mathematical or diagram form and deal with several<br>
abstract and concrete variables.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>Certificates,<br>
Licenses and Registrations</p>
<p>Valid Colorado Driver's License</p>
<p>Ability to obtain CTS Certifications and Project<br>
Management Certifications</p>
<p>Salary to be determined based on related experience and<br>
qualifications.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>Linx<br>
Multimedia is an equal opportunity employer.</p>
