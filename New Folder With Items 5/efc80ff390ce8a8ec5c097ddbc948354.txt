<p><b>Position Summary:</b></p>
<p>
The Director of Sales is responsible for achieving solid sales growth, capitalizing on both existing and new market opportunities. Delivers profitable year-over-year growth through successful new account acquisition, the effective development of existing accounts, the creation of new revenue channels and careful cost management for The Locker Network products and services in the direct sales and revenue share business models. Provides executive representation to customers and develop business opportunities with potential partner organizations. Contributes to shaping the growth strategy for the USA market. This is an individual contributor role with the potential to grow into sales leadership.</p>
<p><b>
Key Responsibilities:</b></p>
<p>
Execute a repeatable and scalable sales processes that ensures predictable results using data analytics and forecasting methodologies. Prescribe, analyze and report on appropriate and effective sales metrics related to sales volume, potential sales, and areas of proposed client base expansion</p>
<p>
Candidates must have proven capability to quickly develop strong relationships across many levels within a company and to understand the opportunities to provide differentiated value and have demonstrated excellence in strategic execution, communication and leadership. The candidate must articulate the capability to define new business opportunities, articulate business cases and drive effective decisions across varying levels of the customers</p>
<p>
Contribute strategic guidance in refining sales and revenue strategy. Maintain current business relationships with key customers, owning the renewal process for existing customers to achieve revenue on long term commitments</p>
<p>
Selected candidate must be a builder, self-starter, a creator, as well as be collaborative, apolitical and a strong team player. Ultimately, this person will thrive on selling our products</p>
<p>
To make regular contacts, business visits and presentations to existing and prospective customers. To attend trade shows, seminars and distributor events to promote The Locker Network brand and to drive sales revenue</p>
<p>
To have an active participation on business development - from the research of developing prospective customers to determine their business potential and to the development of clear and effective written proposals / quotations for current and prospective customers</p>
<p>
To work ensure a high level of coordination and execution of Locker Network-related activities with functional teams in the Dallas office including Support, Operations, Finance, Supply Chain, and Senior Management</p>
<p>
To analyze the market potential and determine the value of existing and prospective customers’ value to The Locker Network</p>
<p>
Provide management with oral and written reports on customers’ needs, problems, interests, competitor activities and potential for new products and services</p>
<p>
Keeps abreast of product applications, technical services, market trends/conditions, competitor activities, advertising and promotional trends through the reading of pertinent literature, attending educational workshops, establishing personal networks and consulting with marketing and technical service areas</p>
<p>
10 or more-years experience in the Locker, Vending, or Amusement business</p>
<p>
Self-motivated, creative thinker who can create new processes from the ground floor</p>
<p>
Experience of direct product sales and experience or good understanding of solution sales including contract negotiation and project evaluation</p>
<p>
Very good presentation and communication skills with an aptitude for problem solving and providing customer solutions</p>
<p>
Excellent organizational and time management skills with the ability to meet deadlines under pressure. The successful candidate as well must be able to work independently and as a team member across varied internal groups and international offices</p>
<p>
Efficient knowledge of the Microsoft Office application suite and ideally familiarity with Netsuite (accounting and CRM software solution)</p>
<p>
Good technical aptitude to understand mechanical and software characteristics of keyless locker products in order to educate customers to their features and benefits</p>
<p>
Be able to work flexible hours. Some work out of normal work hours may be expected by clients Capable of multi-tasking and working under pressure with a fast-moving, scale-up environment</p>
<p>
Leader with integrity and high standards of ethics who can creates an environment of trust, openness and respect.
</p>
<p>Strong organizational skills including process discipline and focus on details, with a demonstrated ability to leverage technology and communication to drive consistent daily activity and relentless command of next actions.</p>
<p>
Curiosity and desire to learn, including embracing emerging best practices.</p>
<p>
Ability to travel, estimated up to 40%
</p>
<p>Effective problem solving and interpersonal skills</p>
<p>
Knowledge of Microsoft Word, Excel and PowerPoint; Salesforce a plus</p>

Bachelor s degree preferred</p>
