<h2>Veterinary Receptionist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>The receptionist is the client’s first contact when calling or visiting the practice and as such represents the professional image of the practice to callers and visitors. The receptionist answers telephones, greets clients, prepares patient files and escorts clients and patients to exam rooms, receives and relays client correspondence, updates client financial records. The exceptional receptionist has the ability to diffuse negative client situations and foster client bonding.</p>
<p><b><i>EXPERIENCE REQUIREMENTS</i></b></p>
<ul>
<li>Significant experience with basic office functions in a busy office environment.</li>
<li>Previous receptionist and veterinary practice experience are desirable but not required.</li>
</ul>
<p><b><i>PERSONAL REQUIREMENTS</i></b></p>
<p>The receptionist must be able to:</p>
<ul>
<li>Be flexible in attitude and work habits.</li>
<li>Quickly learn how to pronounce, know the meaning of and spell commonly used veterinary terms.</li>
<li>Perform basic computer skills, type 30 words per minute.</li>
<li>
<b>Physical Effort</b>: Work requires lifting and carrying records and equipment weighing up to 25 lbs; requires sitting and standing for extended periods or time.</li>
<li>
<b>Working conditions</b>: May be exposed to unpleasant odors, noises and animal feces.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Veterinary Knowledge / Client Education</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Can answer client’s inquiries about basic animal care questions and routine procedures.</li>
<li>Can educate clients on over-the-counter products such as shampoos and nutraceuticals; has full knowledge of heartworm and external parasite preventives to promote client education and sales.</li>
<li>Can guide client’ to make appropriate decisions regarding optimum pet care.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Admitting / Discharging Patients/ Cash Handling</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Can admit patients and handle medical records entries accurately.</li>
<li>Can prepare health certificates, immunization certificates, laboratory requests, and euthanasia certificates.</li>
<li>Can handle client/patient transfers with ease.</li>
<li>Can prepare client invoices for services performed.</li>
<li>Can accurately handle payment transactions.</li>
<li>Can accurately and empathetically communicate estimates/treatment plans to clients.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Schedule Management</b></p>
<ul><li>Can work to maintain an effective veterinarian appointment schedule, screening emergencies and prioritizing cases that need to be seen.</li></ul>
<p><b>Computer Knowledge / Telephone Skills</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Receives and relays telephone, fax, and email messages accurately and promptly.</li>
<li>Answers telephones and handle calls quickly, efficiently and in a professional and friendly manner using a multi-line system.</li>
<li>Can Enter and update client and patient information into practice computer software.</li>
<li>Has good computer skills and shows accuracy in inputting details.</li>
<li>Will be working with AVImark practice management software</li>
</ul>
