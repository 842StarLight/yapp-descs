<h2>Assistant General Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">Founded in 2007, Club Pilates is the nation’s largest and fastest-growing Pilates franchise with locations in 40 states and 2 countries. Class formats target a wide range of clients needs from young to more senior and beginner to advanced. Club Pilates has already sold more than 800 territories throughout the United States and Canada. Last year, Club Pilates was recognized in Entrepreneur magazine’s Franchise 500®, the world’s first, best and most comprehensive franchise ranking.

<b>POSITION:</b><br>
The ideal Assistant General Manager will closely assist our GM in overseeing all Studio functionality from Membership Sales to Operations and everything in between at Multiple Studios in Louisville. Must be passionate about fitness and lead a healthy lifestyle. Very strong sales experience is required. Must be able to generate client leads, discuss the benefits of Pilates naturally and effectively, listen to clients, tailor pitch to their goals, overcome objections and most importantly: Close The Deal.
