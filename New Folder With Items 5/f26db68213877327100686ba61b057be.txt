<h2>Customer Service Representative</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Customer Service Support Team Member</p>
<p>Reports to: Operations Manager</p>
<p>This: position provides customers with product, service information, and basic technical support.</p>
<p><b>Duties and Responsibilities</b></p>
<p>· Greet customers professionally.</p>
<p>· Maintain quality assurance expectations.</p>
<p>· Answer customer request for information and technical support.</p>
<p>· Perform clerical duties.</p>
<p>· Read from provided scripts.</p>
<p>· Perform follow up calls.</p>
<p>· Perform duties of the Billing associate as needed.</p>
<p>· Ability to respond to emails, Technical alerts, and create support tickets.</p>
<p>· Must be able to document all calls.</p>
<p>Light cleaning.</p>
<p><b>Essential Functions</b></p>
<p>1. Serve as a connection between customers and support teams when necessary.</p>
<p>2. Determine customer needs and direct customers to the appropriate personnel or department.</p>
<p>3. Accurately process and document allcalls and customer information.</p>
<p>4. Open/close all tickets.</p>
<p>5. Participate in regular workflow meetings.</p>
<p>6. Accept changes in policies and procedures.</p>
<p>7. Attempt to resolve customer complaints.</p>
<p>8. Work with management and other team members effectively.</p>
<p>9. Wear company branded attire.</p>
<p>10. Follow company safety protocol.</p>
<p><b>Functional and Technical Competencies</b></p>
<p>Skilled communicator who is comfortable talking face-to-face as well as via telephone. Demonstrates attention to detail. Maintain a professional and positive attitude. Good conflict resolution skills. Ability to maintain confidentiality. Open for learning new skills and handling unexpected change. Skilled in using computers and associated business software programs. Ability to perform basic mathematic functions. Admirable written communication skills.</p>
<p><b>Education/Training</b></p>
<p>At least 1 year of related experience in Customer Support, along with Administrative skills required.</p>
<p><b>Environment/Working Conditions</b></p>
<p>Work is generally performed within an office environment with standard office equipment. Sitting for extended periods, walking, lifting up to 40 lbs., touching dexterity, clear vision, clear hearing ,and clear speech.</p>
<p><b>Desired Qualifications</b></p>
<p>Knowledgeable work experience in Customer Support/NOC Technician Level 1 related field. Experienced in basic technical support.Knowledge of product and market demand. Great problem solving skills, phone listening skills, ability to multi-task, and work under stress. Ability to operate and test equipment. Have a valid driver’s license and pass company drug screens.</p>
