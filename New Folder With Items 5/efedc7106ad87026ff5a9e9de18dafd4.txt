<p><b>Job Overview/Summary
</b></p>
<p></p>The Sr. Tax Analyst will report directly to the Sr. Tax Manager and provide strong technical knowledge and experience in support of Clorox’s federal and state income tax compliance team. The ideal candidate will have strong technical knowledge and experience related to federal and state income tax requirements for a large corporate taxpayer. This role requires a computer savvy, process improvement mindset with the ability to collect, filter, and analyze large amounts of data quickly and efficiently. Experience managing data and preparing / e-filing tax returns using Corptax software is highly desired.
<p></p>
<p><b>Key Responsibilities
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Federal compliance including preparation of pro-forma 1120s.
</li>
<li>State compliance consisting of separate and combined returns.
</li>
<li>Preparation of quarterly state tax estimates and extensions.
</li>
<li>Preparation of state amended returns.
</li>
<li>Resolve state tax notices and assist with audits.
</li>
<li>Assist with state apportionment, PL 86-272 analysis, and nexus questionnaires
</li>
<li>Other projects as assigned including ongoing process improvement initiatives
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Key Skills, Abilities and Experience Required
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor's degree in accounting or related field.
</li>
<li>4+ years of industry or public accounting experience.
</li>
<li>Solid understanding of federal and state tax compliance regulations and apportionment.
</li>
<li>Proficiency with Microsoft Office applications.
</li>
<li>Strong tax analysis and research skills.
</li>
<li>A team player with excellent interpersonal skills and the ability to communicate effectively.
</li>
<li>Experience with tax software and e-filing, preferably Corptax.
</li>
</ul>
