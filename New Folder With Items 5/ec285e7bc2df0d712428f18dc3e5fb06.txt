<h2>Product Manager - APM Core</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>New Relic</b><br>
<b>Product Manager, APM Core</b><br>
<b>Portland, Oregon</b></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Your Opportunity</b></p>
<p>
Are you passionate about improving user experience and creating a high quality product? For eleven years, New Relic APM has helped our customers to improve their applications and services by giving them deep visibility into the health of their systems. We’re looking for a Product Manager who can inspire change and improvement to our APM user experience.</p>
<p>
As the Product Manager for APM Core, you will drive a roadmap that improves experiences for our existing APM customers, removes underused functionality, and provides a seamless bridge to our New Relic One platform. You will collaborate side-by-side with the APM engineers and designers to convey customer value, ensure an excellent user experience, and prioritize for customer value.</p>
<br>
<p><b>
What You’ll Do</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Use firm ROI data to build and maintain a rolling 12-month roadmap of features, fixes, and improvements</li>
<li>
Connect with customers around the world about their APM needs and how New Relic can provide a complete solution for them</li>
<li>
Collaborate with designers and other product managers to provide the best experience that bridges between our APM UI and New Relic One platform.</li>
<li>
Work with engineers to provide business context, clear guidance, and success criteria that will delight our customers</li>
<li>
Collaborate with Global Technical Support, Sales, and other groups to ensure smooth deployments and change management</li>
</ul>
<p><br>
<b>
Your Qualifications</b></p>
<p>
Must-have:</p>
<ul>
<li>
2+ years as a product manager and 4+ years as an engineer, technical project manager, or other role involving technology</li>
<li>
Customer-facing product management experience at a company delivering business-to-business SaaS software products</li>
<li>
Experience as a key decision maker for a product, with decisions based on cultivation of data from a variety of sources and stakeholders</li>
<li>
Experience communicating decisions that influence executive-level buy-in</li>
<li>
Ability to lead via influence, patience, and determination</li>
<li>
Excellent analytical/problem-solving skills, including data analysis</li>
<li>
Experience participating in all stages of the software development lifecycle</li>
<li>
Ability to move easily between longer-term goals and day-to-day engagement with partner teams (e.g. Engineering, Sales, Marketing) to keep them focused on current priorities</li>
<li>
Track record of collaborating with PM peers to leverage and build on ideas and knowledge</li>
</ul>
<p>
Nice-to-have:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Experience as a software developer or in technical operations</li>
<li>
Product management training and certification</li>
</ul>
<p>
Qualified applicants may inquire about visa sponsorship (including transfer of a current H-1B visa), but the company does not sponsor candidates for O-1, F-1, or J-1 visas, or candidates who require commencement of an H-1B "cap case" petition for future authorization. This position is to be filled as soon as possible.<br>
</p>
