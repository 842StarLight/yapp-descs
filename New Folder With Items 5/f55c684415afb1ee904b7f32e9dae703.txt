<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Your daily responsibilities include
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<ul>
<li>Responsible for directing the overall operations and staff of the Casino Marketing department. Develops, implements and manages operational goals and monitors achievements of performance and profit objectives</li>
<li>
Develops and implements marketing strategies to maximize volume and market share; investigates potential opportunities for incremental revenue.</li>
</ul>
<ul><li>
Builds loyalty and develops sustained effective relationships among valued VIP customers; establishes and maintains relationships with industry influencers and key community and strategic partners.</li></ul>
<ul><li>
Setting measurable goals for customer development staff with regards to growing high end play, and generating more play out to the current database.</li></ul>
<ul>
<li>
Ensures that scheduling is done in an effective and efficient manner to ensure coverage at peak time to support VIP customers and events, while maintaining labor costs, meeting staffing objectives and achieving guest satisfaction.</li>
<li>
Responsible for preparing, monitoring and adhering to budgets and ensuring compliance to departmental budget initiatives. Reports budget concerns / deviations to the Executive Management / GM. Ensure effective control of marketing results, and takes corrective action to guarantee that achievement of marketing objectives falls within designated budgets.
</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Actively supervises the successful completion of host special events on and off property, collaborating with other department as needed.</li>
<li>
Vendor coordination, management and negotiation. Responsible for accuracy and timeliness of all charges and payments relating to vendor efforts.</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>
Guide preparation of marketing activity reports and present to executive management.</li>
<li>
Supports and maintains a consistent property image throughout all product lines, promotional materials, and events.</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>
Designs and implements a bonus system that provides acceptable incentives aligned to revenue and profitability objectives.</li>
<li>
Ensures effective utilization and maintenance of CRM system</li>
</ul>
<ul><li>
Enthusiastically supports, actively promotes, and demonstrates superior customer service in accordance with department and company standards and programs. Ensures customer service standards are followed by all team members and addresses issues as they arise. Responsible for the overall achievement of department customer service goals.</li></ul>
<ul><li>
Availability for evening, weekend and holiday work is required.</li></ul>
<ul>
<li>
Responsible for ensuring the compliance with all regulatory compliance within area of responsibility and reporting potential issues to Executive Management / GM.
</li>
<li>Maintains strict confidentiality in all departmental and company matters.</li>
<li>
Oversee the Marquee Rewards Programming</li>
<li>
Property representative at internal and external functions.</li>
<li>
Serves as point person in the resolution of customer complaints and service recoveries.</li>
</ul>
