<h2>Production Supervisor for new 2nd shift</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p><b>Come join a stable, growing company with a prominent market position. Due to continued growth we are adding a second shift.</b> <b>With over 25 years’ in business, Rhino Marking &amp; Protection Systems provides innovative damage preventions products that are required to protect vital infrastructure systems across the world. Using your skills and experience, you will be an integral part of our team, helping grow our brand and achieve our goals.
</b></p>
<p><b>Summary</b>: As an integral and critical member of the Rhino team, the Production Supervisor plays a key role in all stages of production/shipping of goods manufactured in our Waseca plants. Ensures accurate, quality products are being produced on time to meet or exceed our customer’s expectations. This position will also be a very hands on role. 50%+ of the time will be spent working directly with the production team and optimizing processes.</p>
<p><b>
Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
 Coordinates with Production Control to create a master schedule to meet shipping dates according to sales forecasts and customer orders.</li>
<li>
 Analyzes production specifications and plant capacity data and performs mathematical calculations to determine manufacturing processes, tools, and human capacity needs.</li>
<li>
 Plans and schedules workflow for each department and operation according to previously established manufacturing sequences and lead times.</li>
<li>
 Plans sequence of fabrication, assembly, installation, and other manufacturing operations for guidance of production workers.</li>
<li>
 Coordinates with other departments to determine status of assigned projects.</li>
<li>
 Expedites operations that delay schedules and alters schedules to meet unforeseen conditions.</li>
<li>
 Prepares production reports.</li>
<li>
 Prepares lists of required materials, tools, and equipment needed for the manufacturing processes.</li>
<li>
 Completes all other duties as assigned</li>
<li>
 Ensures shipping requirements are being accomplished</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Core Competencies:</b></p>
<p>
To perform the job successfully, an individual should demonstrate the following competencies:</p>
<ul><li>
<b>Planning/Organizing</b>- Prioritizes and plans work activities; Uses time efficiently; Plans for additional resources; Sets goals and objectives; Organizes or schedules other people and their tasks; Develops workable implementation plans; Communicates changes effectively; Monitors transition and evaluates results.</li></ul>
<ul><li>
<b>Problem Solving</b>- Focuses on solving conflict, not blaming; identifies and resolves problems in a timely manner; Gather and analyzes information skillfully; Develops alternative solutions; Work well in group problem solving situations; uses reason even when dealing with emotional topics.</li></ul>
<p>
Exhibits sound and accurate judgment; Makes timely decisions.</p>
<ul><li>
<b>Delegation</b>- Delegates work assignments; Matches the responsibility to the person; Sets expectations and monitors delegated activities; Provides recognition for results.</li></ul>
<ul><li>
<b>Teamwork</b>- Gives and welcomes feedback; Contributes to building a positive team work environment; able to build morale and group commitments to goals objectives.</li></ul>
<ul><li>
<b>Leadership</b>- Inspires and motivates others to perform well; Inspires respect and trust. Mobilizes others to fulfill the vision.</li></ul>
<ul><li>
<b>Ethics</b>- Treats people with respect; Works with integrity and ethically upholds organizational values.</li></ul>
<ul><li>
<b>Managing people</b>- Provides regular performance feedback; Develops subordinates’ skills and encourages growth; Fosters quality focus in others; improves processes, products, and services. Demonstrates knowledge of EEO policy; promotes a harassment-free environment.</li></ul>
<ul><li>
<b>Quality Management</b>- Looks for ways to improve the promote quality; Demonstrates accuracy and thoroughness. Contributes to profits and revenue; Conserves organizational resources.</li></ul>
<ul><li>
<b>Safety and Security</b>- Observes safety and security procedures; Determines appropriate actions beyond guidelines; resolves potentially unsafe conditions; Uses equipment and materials properly.</li></ul>
<ul><li>
<b>Oral and Written Communication</b>- Listen and gets clarification; Responds well to questions; Participates in meetings; Presents fact based numerical data effectively.</li></ul>
<p><b>
Desired Skills:</b></p>
<p><b>
Preferred</b>: Bachelor’s degree from four-year College or university; or two years related experience and/or training; or equivalent combination of education and experience.</p>
<p><b>
Language Skills- </b>Ability to read and interpret documents such as safety rules, operating and maintenance instructions, and procedure manuals. Ability to write routine reports and correspondence. Ability to speak effectively before groups of customer or employees of other organizations.</p>
<p><b>
Reasoning Ability- </b>Ability to interpret a variety of instructions furnished in written, oral, diagram, or schedule form.</p>
<p><b>
Computer Skills- </b>To perform this job successfully, an individual should have knowledge of order processing systems, Microsoft Excel, Word, Outlook, Peachtree, or similar software.</p>
