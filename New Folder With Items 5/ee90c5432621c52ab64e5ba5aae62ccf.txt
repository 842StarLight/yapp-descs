<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Job Details
</b></h2>
<p>Salesforce, the Customer Success Platform and world's #1 CRM, empowers companies to connect with their customers in a whole new way. We are the fastest growing of the top 10 enterprise software companies, the World's Most Innovative Company according to Forbes, and one of Fortune's 100 Best Companies to Work For six years running. The growth, innovation, and Aloha spirit of Salesforce are driven by our incredible employees who thrive on delivering success for our customers while also finding time to give back through our 1/1/1 model, which leverages 1% of our time, equity, and
<br>
<br>
You will be responsible for helping to launch a new product Lightning Order Management.
<br>
<br>
To do this, you will be helping to define a narrative for our sales team to show our customers the value of Lightning Order Management on the Salesforce Platform. You will understand the customer journey and help inform and retell the magic of how Salesforce has helped them and their companies transform.
<br>
<br>
Successful candidates will have a passion for taking the complex and making it simple and for marketing through compelling stories.<br>
<br>
<br>
<br>
KEY SKILLS
<br>
<br>
<b>Interpret the need of the sales and distribution teams and translate into scalable solutions
</b><br>
It’s essential that you feel comfortable listening to the needs and requests of our sales team and business partners and are able to quickly interpret them to understand the root of the problem and diagnose what is truly not working or needs to be done.
<br>
<br>
<b>Understand technical products and terminology, simplifying it into clear messaging that empowers sales to be the expert.
</b><br>
We work with complex products and the role of a Product Marketer at Salesforce involves understanding the complexity and being able to distill it into powerful but simple messaging.<br>
<br>
<br>
<br>
<b>Find creative ways to solve problems and collaborate across functions to execute and deliver them
</b><br>
At Salesforce we must work across many, many different departments in order to be successful. It’s essential that you are able to demonstrate great acumen and empathy when collaborating with your peers across different parts of the business.
<br>
<br>
RESPONSIBILITIES
</p>
<ul>
<li>Develop use cases for Lightning Order Management Commerce to our customers' and prospects' unique needs, including defining customer challenges, value proposition &amp; resulting customer benefit
</li>
<li>Drive creation of innovative &amp; compelling content that can be used by our sales teams including pitch decks, messaging videos, data sheets, FAQ’s, playbooks &amp; more.
</li>
<li>Help our sales teams understand our product and solution narrative through impactful enablement &amp; training including leading workshops, participating is sales calls &amp; customer briefings as appropriate.
</li>
<li>Partner with our Campaigns Marketing team to drive an innovative integrated marketing campaign which communicates Sales Cloud to customers &amp; prospects to help build a pipeline for our sales teams.
</li>
<li>Analytics skills to monitor pipeline &amp; revenue across sales segments and ensure the supporting marketing plan is aligned with the specific needs of the business
</li>
</ul>
<p><br>
REQUIRED EXPERIENCE
<br>
<br>
<i>Technical
</i></p>
<ul>
<li>Clear written &amp; verbal communicator who can represent Salesforce offerings to sales teams &amp; customers/prospects
</li>
<li>Hands on ability to lead the creation of innovative marketing tools &amp; assets
</li>
<li>Experience with cloud-based enterprise software such as CRM, Finance or ERP applications
</li>
<li>Experience with Salesforce technologies, either as a customer or partner
</li>
<li>Hands-on ability to create a range of marketing assets, including viral on-message product demo videos and succinct slide pitch-decks.
</li>
<li>Strong skills in communication, writing and ability to work well cross-functionally.
</li>
</ul>
<p><i>Industry
</i></p>
<ul>
<li>2+ years experience in product marketing at a high tech company, or similar. MBA a plus, but not required.
</li>
<li>Demonstrated ability in launching and growing innovative products, preferably with familiarity of the cloud/SaaS and CRM
</li>
<li>Some sales background or experience to sales (sales operations, strategy, customer support, enablement, etc.)
</li>
</ul>
<p><br>
<i>Role
</i></p>
<ul>
<li>Proven track record in developing compelling messaging/positioning and content for sales teams &amp; customers
</li>
<li>Experienced and confident speaking in front of large internal, external, and global audiences.
</li>
<li>Strong collaborator with willingness &amp; drive to help teammates to succeed
</li>
<li>Experience performing well in a fast-paced environment with organizational skills to manage multiple projects and meet tight deadlines effectively.
