<p>
</p>
<p><b>Job Summary:
</b></p>Creates program content and manages the whole production process for both live and recorded radio programs<br>
<p>
</p>
<p>Responsibilities
</p>
<p></p>
<ul><li>Undertakes key administrative responsibilities as well as assists in planning and producing live and/or pre-recorded radio shows which may include news, music, talk, sports, traffic, and feature productions.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Generates original ideas, identifies suitable ideas from others, and carries out thorough research.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Researches information, obtains materials and assists with contacting and scheduling guests.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Gives direction to presenters, content providers, assistants and other crew members.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Manages budgets and ensures the efficient use of resources.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Obtains permissions or licenses for recording or broadcasting on location, and for the use of music, sound effects and audio archive material.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Monitors listener emails, inquiries and responses related to shows.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Works in collaboration with presenters, performers or other program contributors, in order to perform quality assurance to meet established production standards, rules and regulations.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Uses various broadcasting technologies such as soundboards and editing/production software.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Schedules and monitors the radio stream during live recordings.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Assists with time-keeping, saving show recordings, archiving show files and post-production editing as directed.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>May work the board for pre-recorded or live shows.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Performs news, talk, music, comedic or other format shows or program for broadcast entertainment.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Establishes relationship with listeners by providing entertainment and/or information of interest consistent with format, genre, and targeted demographic audience.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Interviews guests, moderates debates, converses with callers, and hosts live events.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Adheres to all guidelines, policies and procedures of the station, iHeartMedia, the FCC and all other federal, state and local laws, including policies and procedures regarding indecency and obscenity.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Prepares for an air shift using creativity, imagination and exercise of independent professional judgment in writing, producing, interviewing, taping or broadcasting on-air material
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Ensures logged commercials, promotions and any other programming essential to the stations operation are aired.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Maintains a website personality page.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Participates in required station Programming, Promotions, and Sales staff meetings and events.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Makes regular approved appearances at paid and non-paid station events; serves as ambassador for the radio station(s) when out in public
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Executes only the interviews and events on air that are approved by Program Director; follows designated show format.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Assists with voice over and production of paid and non-paid spots required to run on the station.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Serves as primary creative voice and directs support staff of show.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Researches and gains knowledge of subject matter that facilitates speaking extemporaneously and providing immediate non-scripted analysis/commentary.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Finalizes content using digital audio editing software.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Prepares written content, visual images, audio material and video footage for websites, blogs, or other social media platforms.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Checks studio equipment for proper functioning and notifies Engineering of technical malfunctions affecting quality of the broadcast.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Handles emergency inserts (e.g. news updates and breaking stories, emergencies, alert system tests, etc.); is on call for possible disasters, breaking news, or acts of nature
</li></ul>
<p></p>
<p>Qualifications
</p>
<p></p>
<ul><li>100% fluent in Spanish with strong English skills.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Engaged in social media and digitally proficient with the ability to read and write in Spanish.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Knowledgeable in music genres including but not limited to Reggaeton, EDM, Salsa, Bachata, Trap and Urban.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Ability to generate original ideas, and to think creatively about how to communicate them to audiences; has comprehensive knowledge of subjects relevant to specific radio genres
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Excellent writing and storytelling skills; ability to tailor and adapt content for different audiences and platforms
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Knowledgeable of the radio market, different station/program styles, audience demographics
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Knows how to use a variety of recording equipment and to operate different radio studios
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Excellent communication skills, complemented by diplomacy, empathy and patience
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Ability to build rapport and draw information from people
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Has confidence and tenacity to pursue information, overcome obstacles and pitch ideas to senior colleagues
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Stress tolerance: ability to work calmly and effectively under pressure, and to react quickly to changes
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Ability to plan and organize, set priorities, multi task and meet tight deadlines
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Full knowledge of the law, rules and industry regulations around radio production; knows when it is necessary, and how to acquire, relevant clearances and licenses
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Proficient in Microsoft Office suite and social networking platforms
</li></ul>
