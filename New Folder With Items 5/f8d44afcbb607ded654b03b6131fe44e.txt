<h2>Digital Media Planner</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>The Digital Media Planner is a critical role in the daily management of the digital signage products within the JCDecaux North America Digital network. As Digital Media Planner, you will work directly with the Director of Sales Administration on the implementation, sales strategy, set up and ongoing maintenance of JCDecaux Digital inventory. This includes the management of day to day sales planning, traffic, charting and sales communications relating to Digital advertising campaigns on Digital billboards, Street Furniture and Malls.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Responsibilities:</b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><i>Sales Planning, Trouble Shooting, Daily Tasks</i></p>
<ul>
<li>Daily monitoring of Digital schedules</li>
<li>Liaise with the Digital Tech Admin team to analyze and troubleshoot field issues</li>
<li>Communicate relevant issues to Ad Operations team and Sales Department as necessary</li>
<li>Prioritize Sales requests, traffic schedules, special requests and content deployments</li>
<li>Assist in the implementation of best practices, reporting tools and strategies to maximize revenues on Digital products</li>
<li>Daily use of internal Content Management System web based tools to monitor all components of digital advertising campaigns</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><i>Content Management</i><i>
</i></p>
<ul>
<li>Review and process all Creative Content &amp; Posting Instructions and provide Campaign Planning &amp; Scheduling confirmations to Sales</li>
<li>Create and maintain support materials on the specifics of each digital product and its sales strategy</li>
<li>Sales support: review, validate and coordinate with sales on all scheduling activity for the Digital Signage Network</li>
<li>Daily use of third-party Content Management System for scheduling and reporting purposes</li>
<li>Works with Sales, Marketing and Operations to review content scheduled to and displayed on digital products.</li>
<li>Submit Creative Content to external parties to acquire Creative Content Approval</li>
</ul>
<p><i>Inventory Management</i></p>
<ul>
<li>Maintain records of the Digital Inventory (Sales packages, Rate card)</li>
<li>Organize and monitor workflow</li>
<li>Manage slot creation and network allocations</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Internal Relationships:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Build and maintain trusting, collaborative relationships with Sales, Management and Digital Tech Admin teams</li>
<li>Manage expectations of Sales and Management teams</li>
<li>Ensure collaboration among various departments to foster great teamwork</li>
<li>Present new business efforts to Sales team when appropriate</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Strategic Thinking &amp; Leadership:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Demonstrate strong analytical and strategic skills</li>
<li>Implement efficient and effective solutions to complex problems</li>
<li>Arrive at solutions that balance the needs of JCDecaux and our clients</li>
<li>Lead the process of strategic media planning/execution and serve as a resource across the Digital team (from CEO through Coordinators)</li>
<li>Be accountable for digital schedule timeliness, accuracy, and overall excellence</li>
<li>Understand and adopt new techniques that are relevant for clients’ digital media strategies</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Desired Skills and Experience</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s Degree</li>
<li>1-3 years of media planning experience, preferably with Digital experience</li>
<li>Client service professionalism, maturity, and diplomacy</li>
<li>Strong understanding of digital strategy</li>
<li>Ability to think strategically, with a balanced analytical and creative approach, and the ability to teach others to think strategically</li>
<li>Collaborative and able to successfully bring people from different disciplines, internal and external together to achieve desired outcomes</li>
</ul>
