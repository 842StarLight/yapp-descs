Bar/Restaurant Auditor - Inventory Control Analyst<br>
<br>
Only serious, qualified applicants who follow the directions in this add will be considered. Applicants resumes who fail to follow these directions will be excluded from consideration.<br>
________________________________________<br>
Bevinco Bar Systems (www.sculpturehospitality.com) is looking for a responsible person with Restaurant or Bar experience for employment as an Inventory Control Analyst for an established auditing/consulting firm in the hospitality industry. If you work late nights Monday - Thursday, do not apply.<br>
<br>
The ideal candidate has an acute attention for detail, excellent written communication skills, reliable transportation, and is able to perform some heavy lifting (30 minutes to 1 hour per day). General computer knowledge of business software such as Microsoft Office is required.<br>
<br>
Responsibilities include taking detailed and precise inventory of bars, hotels and restaurants with handheld computers and digital scales, weighing kegs, and inputing and analyzing Sales and Purchase data. Half of your day will be collecting inventory, half will be computer entry and analysis.<br>
