<h2>Inside Sales Account Executive</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Are you feeling the need for a change in your sales career?
</b></p>
<p>Do you want to sell best-in-class software to a market you can feel great about? Check out Vanco! We have been growing at 30% and are looking for enthusiastic, tenacious Inside Sales Account Executives to connect our communities with unique SaaS solutions.</p>
<p>
We are a U.S. market leader with over 40,000 clients in K-12 education, faith-based and non-profit markets. They trust Vanco to provide streamlined payment and administrative solutions.</p>
<p>
As a sales team, we are exceptional because of our curiosity, agility and creative approach to meeting the needs of our clients. Our leaders are passionate about delivering unparalleled client service through a company culture that treats clients, partners and each other with the highest level of integrity and respect.</p>
<p>
We are open to training sales professionals from a different industry. If you have drive and follow-through, that’s what we need.</p>
<p><b>
What you’ll be doing</b>:</p>
<p>
In this role, you will become an expert in selling our unique software as a solution (SaaS) product line to qualified leads within the specified market. You’ll get to manage the sale start-to-close. Our fantastic Marketing team generates qualified leads (MQLs) to help aid your prospecting and ensure your pipeline success. You’ll develop relationships and perform product demonstrations for key decision-makers within your given market. You will document customer contact in Salesforce and communicate with internal teams regarding customer onboarding.</p>
<p><b>
Who you are:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>You have graduated high school, 2-4-year degree preferred or equivalent experience</li>
<li>You will have 2 years of relatable sales experience, preferably inside sales</li>
<li>You are proficient in MS Office products (Excel, Word, MS Outlook, MS PowerPoint)</li>
<li>You have demonstrated ability to communicate effectively and professionally with a diverse group of people</li>
<li>You display excellent verbal and written communication skills</li>
<li>You feel comfortable making presentations both in-person and via web conferencing</li>
<li>You are can manage details and organize projects and contacts throughout the entire sales cycle</li>
<li>You are able to travel up to 5% of the time for periodic tradeshow exhibits at industry conferences</li>
<li>You can commute to and work in an office environment, use all computer/phone equipment for the hours required and participate in monthly and quarterly meetings</li>
</ul>
<p><b>What we offer:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Competitive base salary plus uncapped commission plan</li>
<li>100% Employer Paid Health Insurance Base Plan; employee only</li>
<li>Dental, Vision, Life and Accident Insurance</li>
<li>Paid Holidays and 4 weeks PTO</li>
<li>401(k) Plan with employer match</li>
<li>Small collaborative teams where you can impact both outcome and culture</li>
<li>Learning opportunities provided by LinkedIn Learning</li>
<li>Ongoing professional development opportunities and career progression</li>
<li>Volunteer opportunities organized by the company</li>
<li>Casual work environment with fun office events</li>
<li>Office Diner with lots of drinks and snacks</li>
</ul>
