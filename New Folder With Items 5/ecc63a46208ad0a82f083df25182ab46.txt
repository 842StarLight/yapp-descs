<b>What’s YOUR Mission?</b><br>
Here at Raytheon, we work together as one global team creating trusted, innovative solutions to make the world a safer place. Our innovation spans all domains: from land and sea to air, space and cyberspace. Raytheon gains inspiration from a noble mission, shared by Raytheon employees around the globe, while providing an inclusive culture empowering employees and celebrating their contributions.<br>
<br>
The Raytheon Integrated Defense Systems (IDS) Engineering Quality (EQ) organization enables engineering quality solutions, capabilities and services to the business. Most important is collaboration with our functional partners and customers (internal and external) to execute and solve problems quickly and efficiently. EQ embeds itself throughout the Engineering lifecycle to prevent defects from reaching the warfighter and to ensure flawless program execution to meet quality, cost, schedule and technical objectives.<br>
<br>
We have an immediate need for a Principal Systems/Software (SW) Quality Engineer (QE) to join the Engineering Integrated Air Missile Defense (IAMD) Section located in Tewksbury, MA. The IAMD Section is responsible to partner with Engineering to ensure work products are free from defects throughout the engineering lifecycle.<br>
<br>
<b>This position is contingent upon Raytheon winning the Lower Tier Air Missile Defense (LTAMDS) contract.</b><br>
<br>
<b>Key Responsibilities:</b><br>
Assess Engineering work products (Peer Reviews, Configuration Control Boards, Code Reviews, Product Readiness and Sale, etc.) to identify and eliminate in-phase defects<br>
Perform project data analysis of program metrics to drive root cause and correction action<br>
Participate in test preparation and execution to ensure verification of requirements<br>
Review Risk and Opportunity data to lead projects to eliminate risks and/or realize opportunities<br>
Investigate failures, participate in failure review boards, support failure investigation teams and drive root cause analysis<br>
Drive continuous improvement<br>
<br>
<b>Basic Qualifications:</b><br>
Minimum of 8 years Systems Engineering, SW Engineering, SW Testing or equivalent SW lifecycle experience<br>
Bachelor’s degree in Engineering or Mathematics<br>
Ability to obtain Department of Defense Secret Clearance<br>
<br>
<b>Preferred Qualifications:</b><br>
<br>
Master’s degree in Engineering<br>
Domain knowledge in Requirements analysis and/or verification, SW Coding and/or Testing<br>
Domain knowledge in Agile, Code Analysis and/or Test Automation techniques<br>
Demonstrated Applied Critical Thinking Skills<br>
Ability to influence without authority, experience with cross functional teams<br>
Experience in multiple phases of the program / product life cycle<br>
Root Cause Analysis and Corrective Action expertise<br>
Existing Secret Clearance<br>
Ability to prioritize work load in a multi-tasking, resource constrained work environment<br>
Minimum of 5 years Quality Engineering experience<br>
American Society for Quality (ASQ) Certification, R6s Expert or Six Sigma Black Belt<br>
Raytheon Six Sigma Principal Specialist or Expert<br>
Mission Assurance Certification Level 4 in Software, Hardware or System Assurance or Mission Assurance Lead Level 3<br>
Excellent written and oral communication skills, including experience presenting to leadership &amp; negotiation skills<br>
Travel domestically or internationally up to 20%<br>
