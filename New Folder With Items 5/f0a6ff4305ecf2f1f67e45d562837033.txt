<h2>Trainer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p><b>Position Summary:
</b></p>
<p>This position exists to develop and enhance the productivity of staff through training and development programs aimed at ensuring an effective, qualified, and dedicated workforce.</p>
<p><b>
General Purpose:</b></p>
<p>
Performs duties related to implementation of credit union sales/service training programs and software applications. Enhance staff individual development through continued assessment of retained knowledge &amp; skills.</p>
<p><b>
Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Trains the Diamond Credit Union culture.</li>
<li>Provides instruction to new employees in system functions necessary for the performance of their position.</li>
<li>Keeps current and provides instruction and training in new software applications, compliance regulations and new products.</li>
<li>Follows training trends and implements best practices.</li>
<li>Assesses each new staff member for achieved progress compared to the developed timelines.</li>
<li>Develops, maintains and updates credit union staff training manuals and templates.</li>
<li>Participate in Diamond's community fundraising events.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Qualifications:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Demonstrated dedication to providing exceptional service.</li>
<li>Associate's Degree in Business or related field required.</li>
<li>Two plus years of lending experience at financial institution.</li>
<li>Three plus years of formal training experience, preferably in a financial institution.</li>
<li>Understanding of training and development processes.</li>
<li>Demonstrated creativity and innovation in formulating training programs.</li>
<li>Understands and identifies adult learning methods.</li>
<li>Strong presentation, interpersonal and communication skills, both oral and written.</li>
<li>Proven leadership with the ability to problem-solve and make good decisions with minimal supervision.</li>
<li>Excellent organizational skills and meet deadlines.</li>
<li>Proficient in Outlook, Word, Powerpoint, Excel and web applications, e-learning a plus (Articulate).</li>
<li>Ability to travel between branches.</li>
<li>Available to work evenings and early mornings to accommodate for training outside normal business hours, including Saturdays.</li>
</ul>
