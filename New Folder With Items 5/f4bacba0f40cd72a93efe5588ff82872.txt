<div>
<p>Secure Code Warrior is the global secure coding company that makes software development better and more secure. Our vision is to empower developers to be the first line of defense in their organization by making security highly visible and providing them with the skills and tools to write secure code from the beginning.</p>
<p>
We have built a powerful platform that moves the focus from reaction to prevention, training and equipping developers to think and act with a security mindset as they build and verify their skills, gain real-time advice and monitor skill development.</p>
<p>
Headquartered in Sydney Australia, were experiencing a period of hypergrowth and backed by our investors Paladin and Airtree. Our customers include Motorola, JPMC, Paypal, Qantas and 20 of the top 100 global banks. With offices in Sydney, London, Boston, and Bruges we’re just getting started, and looking for amazing people to join our team across the globe. We value diversity and aim to attract, retain and develop a talented workforce who thrive in a vibrant high performing culture, with a passion for collaboration &amp; people development.<br>
</p>
<p><b>Talent </b><b>Acquisition Specialist</b><b>, Portland</b></p>
<p>
We’re looking for our Talent Acquisition Specialist to join our people operations team to recruit and deliver on our cultural + people centric recruiting process. You will be a major contributor to our expansion to Portland, OR while operating within a hyper-growth environment working alongside teams across 5+ timezones. We are looking for an empathetic executor who will drive our forward-looking and aggressive hiring plan that enables our company’s strategy and evolves our organization into a high-performing recruiting machine. You will be based out of Portland, OR and report directly to the Head of People and Culture.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
What You’ll Do</b></p>
<p>
You'll work alongside our Head of People and Culture to scale our current recruiting process into the Portland market. You’ll bring your passion for recruiting, process, and growth to help find and hire our inaugural cohort of portland sales team members. You’ll also be responsible for spreading our employer brand into the PDX area - leveraging your talent communities and networks to spread our SCW flag across the cyber security, tech, and sales communities. We’re in the early stages of building out our recruitment arm, so this role will also require travel across North America offices to help with recruitment pushes and support the ownership of all US hiring.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Take the lead on all things recruiting for PDX - this is a full cycle recruiting role and will include sourcing, interviewing, coordinating and virtual and onsite interviews.</li>
<li>Create a sourcing strategy that leverages your networks, the cyber-security space, and diverse and inclusive communities working in technology.</li>
<li>Master our applicant tracking system, scheduling tools and organizational products. We use BambooHR.</li>
<li>Build strong partnerships with candidates, Internal Recruiters, and Sales Leadership in order to drive an efficient process for providing the best candidate experience</li>
<li>Leverage our employer brand - help spread the SCW love to events, conferences, and communities in PDX.</li>
<li>Manage offer letters and assist with onboarding for new warriors in the PDX area.</li>
<li>Help with hiring support and eventually own the recruitment process for all US hires.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
