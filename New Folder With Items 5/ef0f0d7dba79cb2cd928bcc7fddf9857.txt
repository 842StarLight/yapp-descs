<h1 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Position Summary</b></h1>
<p>The Business Development Rep is an essential function in the sales organization. The business development representative is responsible for creating sales opportunities, and supporting the solution sales organization. The Business Development rep will target specific buyer personas for the Travelnet Solutions SaaS product lines. 1 year+ of sales experience preferred.</p>
<br>
<p></p>

<ul>
<li>

</li>
<li>

</li>
<li>
<p>Identify growth opportunities (e.g. client needs) within client base and introduce relevant products/services</p>
</li>
<li>
