<h2>Industry Account Executive</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">----------------------
<br>Team Description
<br>----------------------
<br><br>
Our Sales team shares Dropbox Business and Dropbox Enterprise with enterprises around the world, helping them understand the power Dropbox has to offer teams at scale. We're a collaborative and empathetic Sales team, focused on understanding what businesses need to work better together.
<br><br>
----------------
<br>Role Description
<br>----------------
<br><br>
As an Industry Account Executive for our Central customers, you'll play a pivotal role in crafting the future of Dropbox Business for our customers. You'll join a talented team striving for one thing: using technology to simplify people's lives. To do this, we're looking for smart, high-reaching people who are passionate about revolutionizing the way people work.
<br><br>
----------------
<br>Responsibilities
<br>----------------
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Build strong, long-lasting relationships with Fortune 500 clients and executives to serve as a trusted advisor</li>
<li>Mobilize a broad account team through a complex sales cycles</li>
<li>Present the value of Dropbox Business and our services to C-level executives</li>
<li>Evangelize the Dropbox vision through product demonstrations, field marketing events, and account-specific initiatives</li>
<li>Construct, forecast, and manage your sales activity and pipeline to meet revenue targets and company goals</li>
<li>Drive revenue by identifying and closing a list of named accounts</li>
<li>Develop and scale out a territory-specific Strategic Enterprise strategy</li>
</ul>
------------
<br>Requirements
<br>------------
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>8+ years experience in B2B software sales (preferably Enterprise Content Management, Document Management, Cloud-Based Solutions)</li>
<li>Consistent track record of building pipeline and exceeding sales targets</li>
<li>Hunter mindset. Strong prospecting, discovery, negotiation and closing skills are your speciality</li>
<li>Excellent collaboration skills and the ability to build long-lasting relationships across your team and the company</li>
<li>Successfully sold software into Fortune 500 companies and bringing in top logos</li>
<li>Successfully sold into IT</li>
<li>Must be able to spend 25%+ time in the field calling on clients</li>
<li>Avid Dropbox user and a vocal technology evangelist</li>
<li>Dream to work in a fast-paced, challenging and exciting environment</li>
</ul>
