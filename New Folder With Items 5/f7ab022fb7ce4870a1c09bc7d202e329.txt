<b>Automotive Internet Sales Purpose:</b><br>
Responds to Internet customer inquiries and ensures that the dealership actualizes its maximum profit potential on Internet sales.<br>
<br>
<br>
<b>Automotive Internet Sales Duties and Responsibilities:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Communicates effectively with Internet customers and prospects according to their preferred method of communication, whether by phone, by e-mail, or in person.</li>
<li>Has thorough knowledge of all dealership products and services.</li>
<li>Satisfies the transportation needs of Internet-generated customers.</li>
<li>Directs customers to product information resources, including those available on the Internet.</li>
<li>Assists customers in selecting a vehicle.</li>
<li>Understands the psychology of Internet customers and works with them accordingly to draw them into the store for proper vehicle demonstration and to close the transaction.</li>
<li>Checks email frequently and responds to inquires immediately. Delivers messages intended for other departments promptly. Notifies customers that their messages have been forwarded to the appropriate personnel for prompt attention.</li>
<li>Handles all Internet sales inquiries personally.</li>
<li>Coordinates Internet promotions with other dealership sales promotions and advertising.</li>
<li>Ensures that the Web site address is prominently displayed throughout the dealership; e.g., on business cards, flyers, pens, and all dealership advertising.</li>
<li>Researches auto-buying services on the Internet and acts as liaison with any services the dealership uses to promote its site or advertise.</li>
<li>Works with webmaster to update the Web site frequently to attract new and repeat visitors.</li>
<li>Takes photos of dealership inventory for use on the dealership site.</li>
<li>Maintains an ongoing customer database to capture repeat business.</li>
<li>Knows and understands the federal, state, and local laws which govern retail automobile sales.</li>
<li>Establishes personal income goals that are consistent with dealership standards of productivity, and devises a strategy to meet those goals.</li>
<li>Keeps abreast of new products, features, accessories, etc., and their benefits to customers.</li>
<li>Attends product and sales training as requested by sales manager.</li>
<li>Schedules first service appointment.</li>
<li>Attends sales meetings.</li>
<li>Maintains professional appearance.</li>
<li>Must follow all company safety policies and procedures, and immediately report any and all accidents to a manager or supervisor</li>
</ul>
<br>
<br>
<br>
<b>Automotive Internet Sales Qualifications:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Must have one year of professional sales experience</li>
<li>Dealership experience is preferred</li>
<li>To perform this job successfully, an individual must be able to perform each essential duty satisfactorily</li>
<li>All applicants must be authorized to work in the USA</li>
<li>All applicants must perform duties and responsibilities in a safe manner</li>
<li>All applicants must be able to demonstrate ability to pass pre-employment testing to include background checks, MVR, drug test, credit report, and valid driver license</li>
</ul>
