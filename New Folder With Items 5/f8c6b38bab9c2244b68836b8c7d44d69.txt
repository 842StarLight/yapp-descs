<p><b>Store Manager Job Description</b></p>
<p><b>
Purpose of Position</b></p>
<p><b>
The purpose of the Service Manager is to have a responsible person able to operate the daily business and mange the daily sales activities, either in association with, or in the absence of the business owner.</b></p>
<p><b>
Qualifications</b></p>
<p><b>
All Managers must be able to clearly demonstrate their ability to set and reach performance goals. They must conduct every aspect of their job in an ethical manner. They must have the ability to sell recommended service; interview employment candidates; manage and motivate the company’s personnel; work well under pressure; and resolve customer as well as employee issues in a professional, expedient manner. In other words, “to lead by example”.</b></p>
<p><b>
All managers must be able to work a minimum of 50 hours a week on a regular basis.</b></p>
<p><b>
Store Managers must also have the ability to manage and control the technical productivity as well as be productive themselves.</b></p>
<p><b>
Areas of Responsibility</b></p>
<p><b>
Unless otherwise specified, the Store Manager is responsible for all the daily productivity in the business with the exceptions outlined by the owner. This responsibility includes but is not limited to a dedication: to the company’s mission statement, to employee development, to company profitability and to 100% customer satisfaction. Also, responsible for following the company policies and procedures as outlined in the employee manual and in task-specific procedures.</b></p>
<p><b>
Many of the Store Manager’s responsibilities overlap with the General Manager and Office Manager’s responsibilities. They assist each other toward a common goal where teamwork is key.</b></p>
<p><b>
Specific Duties</b></p>
<p><b>
Daily</b></p>
<ul>
<li><b>Open shop and get business going in the morning. (Refer to Store Opening Tasks)</b></li>
<li><b>Manage daily technical production including producing own work.</b></li>
<li><b>Order parts for specific repair others by telephone, fax, email and internet.</b></li>
<li><b>Use Mitchell Repair and service manuals to look up technical information.</b></li>
<li><b>Write up service jobs.</b></li>
<li><b>Add part numbers and canned jobs into software database as needed.</b></li>
<li><b>Assign work and manage the flow of the shop</b></li>
<li><b>Verify that all inspections are done in correct manner</b></li>
<li><b>Make sure whiteboard is updated</b></li>
<li><b>Edit Inspections and send to customers.</b></li>
<li><b>Write and sell service/repair to assist Shop Manager achieve monthly performance goals.</b></li>
<li><b>Wrap up business and close shop at the end of the day. (Refer to Store Closing Tasks)</b></li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Weekly</b></p>
<ul>
<li><b>Oversee Shop area, Shop equipment and parking lot clean up and maintenance.</b></li>
<li><b>Attend/Hold weekly Shop meeting. Monthly</b></li>
<li><b>Customer Follow-up</b></li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Quarterly</b></p>
<ul><li><b>Review monthly expenditures and performance goals with manager’s team.</b></li></ul>
<p><b>
Annually</b></p>
<ul><li><b>Assist owner in Technician Performance Review.</b></li></ul>
<p><b>
Exceptions to Duties</b></p>
<ul>
<li><b>Daily Bookkeeping/bank deposits/accounts payable/payroll</b></li>
<li><b>Alarm response after hours</b></li>
<li><b>Computer maintenance and software updates</b></li>
<li><b>Telephone systems programming</b></li>
<li><b>Coolant Recycling</b></li>
<li><b>Order parts for Inventory (if no other option)</b></li>
<li><b>Customer pick-up or delivery (if no other option)</b></li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Notice</b></p>
<p><b>
If you have any questions about this job description, contact the owner. You must notify the owner if at any time you can not perform the job or any portion of the job as described above.</b></p>
<p><b>
This job description is not a contract for employment and pertains to all At Will associates only.</b></p>
