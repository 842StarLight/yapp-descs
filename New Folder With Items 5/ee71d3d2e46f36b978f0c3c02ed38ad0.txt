<h2>Commercial Support Analyst</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<b>Company Overview:</b><br>
Hi-Crush is a leading domestic producer, supplier and distributor of premium Northern White frac sand used to enhance recovery rates of oil and natural gas from hydraulic fracturing wells.
<br><br>
Pronghorn Energy Services, a division of Hi-Crush, is based in Denver Colorado. We are focused on bringing a sophisticated, data centric approach to oilfield logistics and providing “best in class” service quality for our growing client base. We are seeking talented individuals with a proven track record in the oilfield industry.
<br><br>
<b>Position Summary:</b><br>
Reporting to the Director, Commercial Support, this position will coordinate the customer sales order process for the Last Mile Service business line. Working closely with the finance, supply chain, operations and trucking logistics teams, will track product sales and usage across jobs and customers, review all sales activities and work with accounting to ensure all sales activities are processed within client guidelines.
<br><br>
<b>Essential Functions:</b><br>
<b>Reasonable Accommodations Statement:</b> To accomplish this job successfully, an individual must be able to perform, with or without reasonable accommodation, each essential function satisfactorily for the loading and unloading of sand containers. Reasonable accommodations may be made to help enable qualified individuals with disabilities to perform the essential functions.
<br><br>
Essential Functions Statement(s)
<br><br>
1. Set up and maintain customers and sales orders within enterprise software (IFS) and frac job database.
<br><br>
2. Consolidate and maintain tables of customer-specific invoicing requirements, billing frequency, and pricing.
<br><br>
3. Ensure all activity reports are received from operations personnel to aid in accurate billing of client.
<br><br>
4. Analyze and prepare job activity logs by job and by customer to ensure inventory is available to meeting short term demand.
<br><br>
5. Review, reconcile and resolve variance of material delivered to well site against load out reports.
<br><br>
6. Measure, prepare and disseminate reports of sand usage across jobs and customers.
<br><br>
7. Develop analytics, reports, and variance analysis to support timely and accurate sales order and invoicing processes with the right product and accurate pricing.
<br><br>
8. Lead root-cause analysis and resolution of major variances between usage and amounts billed to customers.
<br><br>
9. Coordinate &amp; partner with site personnel to streamline and optimize data gathering activity through the process.
<br><br>
10. Coordinate and partner with dispatch on ensuring accuracy of PropDispatch data.
<br><br>
11. Provide input to the development of invoicing policies, procedures and templates to ensure customers have everything they need to pay invoices on a timely basis.
<br><br>
12. Coordinate with AR and Credit and Collections teams to resolve any customer billing and collections issues.
<br><br>
13. Coordinate with AP to resolve any carrier billing and payment issues.
<br><br>
14. Additional ad hoc reports or projects as assigned.
