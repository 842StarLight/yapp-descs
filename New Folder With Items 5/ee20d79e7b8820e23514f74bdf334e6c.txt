<b>The Position</b><br>
The Senior Manager – Area Insights &amp; Analytics is responsible for providing insights and analytics support for a sales area within the NNI diabetes business. The Senior Manager will provide support to an area representing approx. $1.5B in sales across 5 to 6 regions. The Senior Manager will function as a strategic partner to the Area Commercial lead and their leadership team who has responsibility over Diabetes sales representatives, Health System representatives, Health System account executives and Diabetes educators. The Senior Manager will proactively identify critical business drivers / opportunities and develop recommendations to guide strategy. An ideal candidate for this role will have strong experience developing insights by analyzing various sources of information, a proven ability to partner with senior level executives, and a demonstrated ability to drive the development of strategic plans to drive business changes.<br>
<br>
<br>
<b>Relationships</b><br>
The Senior Manager – Area Insights &amp; Analytics reports to the Director – Commercial Insights &amp; Analytics. The senior manager interacts regularly with the Area Commercial Leads, Regional Business Directors, Marketing leadership, Sales Execution leadership and other key stakeholders. External relationships include consultant partners, data providers and information technology partners.<br>
<br>
<br>

Provide strategic guidance to support existing brands and launch plans</li>
