<h2>Customer Success Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>The Splunk <b>Customer</b> <b>Success</b> <b>Manager</b> (CSM) is an entry level Customer Success role as you take first step in your journey to building a career in Customer Success, the CSM will be the first port of call for a range of customers from multiple verticals all looking to better demonstrate Splunk technology and attain maximum Return on Investment (ROI).</p>
<p>
Our Customer Success Manager will guide our Splunk customers in their journey to discover the full value of their machine data and to support the real-decision-making that today's businesses demands. The CSM role is continuous and revolves around supporting our customer to use their Splunk Enterprise software solutions as effectively and efficiently as possible to its maximum potential.</p>
<p><b>
Customer Success Managers will be responsible for:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
The establishment of success plans and standard methodologies with our customers.</li>
<li>
Driving and leading the adoption across business units.</li>
<li>
Ensuring continuous training of Splunk end users, developers and administrators are met.</li>
<li>
Evangelize a Splunk Success Framework within the customer success organization and assisting in its creation. This consists of Splunk domain experts and end users in the organization that provide governance and sponsorship to the Splunk capabilities.</li>
<li>
Industry standard methodology sharing to demonstrate the successes achieved by others in their market sector and knowledge of value realization achieved in similar market sectors.</li>
<li>
The formation of user groups within the company to collaborate, grow and share achievements.</li>
<li>
Adapting the organization in order to get the most from the Splunk platform.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
You will have the following skills and experience:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Willing to travel, based on customer and business needs for extended periods when required</li>
<li>
Experience in a client facing role with a background that could be within support, account management, project management, value selling or any role that sees you strive for successful customer outcomes.</li>
<li>
You have successfully led customer engagements to completion and customer happiness.</li>
<li>
You possess excellent analytical skills &amp; problem-solving skills, combined with the ability to provide quick resolution to problems.</li>
<li>
You also possess exceptional verbal and written interpersonal and presentation skills.</li>
<li>
You can demonstrate success in thinking strategically, executing tactically while providing consistent and high levels of customer satisfaction and retention in a fast-paced environment.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Minimum requirements:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Strong business acumen. Spot additional sales opportunities.</li>
<li>
Highly customer orientated to achieve maximum customer satisfaction</li>
<li>
Have the personal attitude and behaviors that represent the Splunk’ values: Creative, Passionate, Disruptive, Open and Fun.</li>
<li>
Working knowledge of technology &amp; products such as Cloud, Exchange, VM Ware, Log Files, Hadoop, FIX and Mobile &amp; Web applications</li>
<li>
Bachelor’s degree or equivalent experience.</li>
<li>
U.S. Citizenship is required</li>
<li>
Prior DoD / Intelligence experience is desired</li>
<li>
Excellent knowledge of Microsoft Office</li>
<li>
Understanding of Enterprise Architecture, API’s and Networks</li>
</ul>
