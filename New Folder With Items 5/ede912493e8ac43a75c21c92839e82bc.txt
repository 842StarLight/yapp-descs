Curtis Media Group is North Carolina’s largest independent radio broadcasting company, operating 62 AM and FM signals across the state of North Carolina in Raleigh-Durham, Greensboro – Winston-Salem – High Point, Greenville – New Bern, Jacksonville, Boone, Lenior, and Goldsboro. Curtis Media Group also operates the North Carolina News Network, David Glenn Show, Triangle Traffic Network, Southern Farm Network, The ACC Sports Journal, ACCSports.com, and Curtis Marketing Group which produces events including the North Carolina Wine Festivals, the Kid Expo and the Man Expo.</p>
