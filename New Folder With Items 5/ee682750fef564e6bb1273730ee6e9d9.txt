<h2>Night Audit / Front Desk</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<div>The <b>University Club of San Francisco</b> is centrally located atop Nob Hill in San Francisco. We are a private social Club that also has overnight accommodations and banquet facilities for all types of events.
<br>
We are currently looking for a part time Night Audit / Front Desk staffer to work the 11:30 p.m. to 7:30 a.m. shift 16 to 24 hours per week.<br>
<br>
This position is our guest's main point of contact and must present an efficient, courteous and professional manner while maintaining the Club's hgh standards of service and hospitality.</div>
<div>
<b>
Main Job Duties:</b>
<ul>
<li>Record and reconcile charges for previous day's sales.</li>
<li>Monitor Clubhouse and Athletic Facility during shift.</li>
<li>Set out and maintain the daily Continental Breakfast.</li>
<li>Update room inventory, prepare new arrival registration cards, coordinate with housekeeping.</li>
<li>Execute reservations tasks including guest check-in or check-out, posting charges to guest accounts, maintaining rooms status information and receiving payments via cash or credit cards.</li>
<li>Develop thorough knowledge of room locations, types of rooms and room rates and cross-promotes other services that the Club offers to guests.</li>
</ul>
</div>
<div><div><div>
<div><div>
<b>
Skills:</b><br>
Must be an experienced computer user and have familiarity with the internet, operating office equipment, have a friendly demeanor and great attention to detail.<br>
Both verbal and written communication skills are required for customer interactions.<br>
