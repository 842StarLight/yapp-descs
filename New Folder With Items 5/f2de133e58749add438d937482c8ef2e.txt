<h2>Vice President of Legal</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Headquartered in Santa Rosa, CA, Left Coast Ventures is a diversified cannabis and hemp company specializing in cultivation, manufacturing, brand development and distribution. Left Coast Ventures and its subsidiaries are working to shape the future of the legal cannabis industry in the United States through acquisitions, investments, and incubation while building a respected portfolio of top shelf brands. Wholly owned, licensed, and distributed brands in Left Coast Venture’s portfolio include Marley Natural™, Mind Your Head™, Headlight™, Chill Chocolate and New Frontier Cannabrew.</p>
<p>We are seeking a motivated, dynamic and team-oriented attorney to join a growing legal team supporting a growing business. Based in our San Francisco office and reporting to the CEO, the VP of Legal will focus on advising all Left Coast Ventures business units and leadership. Candidates must have excellent business judgment and the ability to work both independently as well as collaboratively, in a collegial, fast-paced team environment.</p>
<p><b>What You’ll Do: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Support the negotiation and documentation of M&amp;A, strategic transactions and partnerships; handle any legal integration.</li>
<li>Advise on legal &amp; regulatory issues applicable to a CPG company, including: new product review (labels, claims, efficacy, etc.); brand and product research and development; supply chain matters; import/export and sales.</li>
<li>Review, draft and negotiate a variety of agreements, templates and other documents, including: procurement and distribution agreements; licensing agreements; contract manufacturing, white label, &amp; co-packer agreements; vendor agreements; advertising agreements; and professional services agreements.</li>
<li>Applicable regulatory matters (FDA, FTC, USDA, CPB, and their relevant state equivalents).</li>
<li>Advertising, content, marketing and promotions review, guidance and sign-off.</li>
<li>Review and prepare documentation and internal policies for risk management, corporate governance, and corporate compliance.</li>
<li>Working with executive leadership and other business units to identify and manage key risks and opportunities.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>What We’re Looking For: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>JD from an accredited law school, admitted to the bar in any US jurisdiction.</li>
<li>Minimum 5 years professional experience as an attorney, preferably with a focus in a highly regulated consumer products industry; prior cannabis experience preferred.</li>
<li>Direct experience working in a company setting as corporate counsel.</li>
<li>General curiosity of the cannabis industry and its evolution.</li>
<li>Ability to work independently while being able to contribute successfully to cross-functional, fast-paced teams.</li>
<li>Relationship-oriented with excellent business judgment, strategic thinking, and project management skills.</li>
<li>Ability to manage multiple projects with competing priorities, follow through and meet deadlines.</li>
<li>Possess a strong work ethic and be able to work well under pressure and with minimal supervision.</li>
<li>Strong analytical skills, as well as strong research, organizational, problem-solving, and project management skills.</li>
<li>Excellent verbal and written communication skills.</li>
<li>Ability to handle challenging situations gracefully.</li>
<li>Position is located in San Francisco, CA. Some travel (&lt; 20%) is required.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>What We Bring: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Extremely competitive benefits including: Health, Dental and Vision coverage</li>
<li>Opportunity to highly contribute to our growth goals</li>
<li>A highly energetic and fast paced environment at a rapidly growing company</li>
<li>Equity options available</li>
<li>Generous vacation policy in addition to 12 celebrated holidays</li>
</ul>
<p>Job Type: Full-time</p>
