Because it’s the best-run businesses that make the world run better and improve people’s lives.</p>
<p>
Our Client Development organization serves as the focal point for our clients to achieve success with SAP Concur. As a Client Success Manager you will be responsible for developing trusted relationships with your clients, measured through revenue growth, retention and relationship health.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p>This is a critical role working with key customers and internal teams and has central importance to SAP Concur’s ongoing success. In this role, you will spend your days partnering with our new clients, providing strategic recommendations to maximize the benefits of their subscription with SAP Concur.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p>A Client Success Manager will:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Build a strategic territory plan to proactively engage customers, and assist in the rollout of their SAP Concur products</li>
<li>
Complete proactive customer outreach through the early stages of the customer lifecycle</li>
<li>
Develop strong relationships with each client using virtual meetings</li>
<li>
Meet or exceed account retention and proactive outreach goals on a monthly basis</li>
<li>
Establish a trusted advisor relationship with each client, driving continued value of our products and services</li>
<li>
Contribute to process development and organizational growth objectives</li>
<li>
Be an expert for industry best practices and the SAP Concur products</li>
<li>
Work in partnership with multiple internal teams to ensure a smooth client journey</li>
<li>
Maintain a clean book of business – ensure data integrity for the business</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
Skills &amp; Qualifications:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Proven track record of exceeding retention and revenue targets</li>
<li>
Successful experience managing a portfolio of 100+ clients in the mid-market or small business space</li>
<li>
Excellent verbal and written communication skills</li>
<li>
Analytical, inquisitive and able to uncover customer needs via open-ended questions</li>
<li>
Focused on establishing, maintaining, and growing broad and deep relationships within each customer account</li>
<li>
Highly organized, able to manage many activities and deadlines</li>
<li>
Ability to uncover how a customer defines success and the ability to align that vision with SAP Concur’s delivery of value via the client’s products and services</li>
<li>
Thrive in an entrepreneurial, fast-paced, and evolving environment</li>
<li>
Mobilize customers and cross functional partners through collaboration and clear communication</li>
</ul>
