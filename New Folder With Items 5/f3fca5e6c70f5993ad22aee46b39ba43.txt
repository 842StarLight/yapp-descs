<h2>Marketing Traffic Coordinator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<b>Description</b><br>
<div><b>Job Summary</b></div>
<div>Monitors the workflow between different departments and vendors to ensure on-time delivery of marketing projects and/or materials. Troubleshoots problems, manages schedules, and simultaneously coordinates multiple activities. Drives multiple creative and design projects in various stages of development for successful outcomes. Ensures all projects are implemented accurately and within agreed upon timeline. Keeps all parties informed of conflicts, delays, or any other unusual situations. Expedites and prioritizes rush projects accordingly maintaining positive and professional relationship with all team members.</div>
<br>
<b>
Qualifications</b><br>
<div><b>Minimum Job Requirements</b></div>
 <ul>
<li>1-3 years of experience in a professional marketing position</li>
 <li>Experience working with creative development of web or advertising</li>
</ul>
<div><b>Knowledge, Skills, and Abilities</b></div>
 <ul>
<li>Associates’ degree preferred.</li>
 <li>Minimum of one-year experience with creative portfolio for review preferred.</li>
 <li>Experience with project management software a plus.</li>
 <li>Spanish a plus.</li>
 <li>Strong creative skills.</li>
 <li>Excellent communication skills.</li>
 <li>Excellent presentation skills.</li>
 <li>Self-starter, ability to multi-task, and organizational skills.</li>
 <li>Team player with positive and collaborative attitude. Interpersonal skills.</li>
 <li>Knowledge of all aspects of printing including application, vendor management, and production cycle.</li>
 <li>Experience working with Adobe Creative Cloud (Photoshop. Illustrator, InDesign, etc.)</li>
</ul>
<div><b>Job Specific Duties</b></div>
 <ul>
<li>Reviews, records, and breaks down marketing requests, creating a production schedule with estimated time needed to complete each phase.</li>
 <li>Inputs marketing requests, broken down steps, and timeline into SharePoint. Assigns tasks to team members based on areas of expertise or responsibilities. Updates SharePoint tasks and timeline. on areas of expertise or responsibilities.</li>
 <li>Prepares and confirms specifications for printing projects. Provides print-ready and approved materials to vendors for production.</li>
 <li>Requests and submits quotes from vendors to be approved by the External Affairs department or department making the request.</li>
 <li>Processes requests for purchase orders and keeps track of open orders.</li>
 <li>Requests and monitors the flow of information, materials, or resources needed between departments to develop a schedule delivery of products/ resources &amp; to ensure on-time delivery.
 </li>
<li>Anticipates problems and develops a plan of action to prevent delays in the workflow. Troubleshoots problems to make sure a project or process remains on schedule.</li>
 <li>Communicate with outside vendors, including designers and printers, to ensure on-time delivery of projects and/or marketing materials.</li>
 <li>Works with the team to assign resources to projects. Monitors the progress of a project, campaign, or marketing request and prepares production reports.</li>
 <li>Ensures all material are properly signed off as approved by the department’s director. Keeps track of expenses per project and reports to the department director at delivery.</li>
 <li>Creates and maintains printing and design archive for future reference.</li>
 <li>Maintains inventory of marketing materials and promotional items.</li>
 <li>Verifies invoices with orders and settles billing discrepancies with vendors.</li>
 <li>Develops the layout and designs of minor projects, updates the design of ongoing projects, and updates copies on current projects.</li>
 <li>Develops and designs materials for signature events, promotions for grassroots events, marketing materials for development officers and outreach programs.</li>
 <li>Monitors quality and accuracy on printing and digital jobs. Advises team members on the execution of printing or design projects.</li>
</ul>
</div></div>
