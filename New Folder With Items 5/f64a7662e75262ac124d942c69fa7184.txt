<h2>Technical Writer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Essential Responsibilities: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Reviewing, analyzing, assessing and summarizing solicitation requirements that are received from the Marketing and Sales departments or other internal channels</li>
<li>Reviewing materials received from bid notification services and redirecting to appropriate areas within NRG based on the type of product or service being solicited</li>
<li>Collaborating with project teams to prepare compelling responses and content to be used in presentations, used for internal and external purposes involving sales or business origination opportunities</li>
<li>Coordinating materials, statistics and information with various groups within the company, including, among others, Marketing Communications, Regulatory, Government Affairs, Supply Chain and Legal</li>
<li>Leading cross-functional team discussions involving solicitation responses</li>
<li>Expanding and maintaining the RFP Team’s Content Library by integrating material developed for responses</li>
<li>Updating the Content Library based on schedules driven by financial reporting dates and public announcements</li>
<li>Assisting business development and sales teams customize presentations and fact sheets for use in sales presentation</li>
<li>Researching and fact checking data and information to ensure consistency in solicitation responses and publicly released information in other company communications</li>
<li>Consolidating, editing, conforming, and integrating modular responses from individual contributors to prepare responses to commodity focused solicitations</li>
<li>Proofreading, performing quality control of written product to ensure accuracy, completeness and consistency with external communication standards * Producing graphics, diagrams and illustrations for proposal material</li>
<li>Ensuring solicitation responses are in compliance with all requestor instructions, schedules, and approval protocols</li>
<li>Multi-tasking in a fast-paced deadline-oriented environment, as well as the proven ability to quickly adapt to shifting priorities and workflow</li>
<li>Focusing and delivering material that is error free through attention to detail</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Education: </b></p>
<ul><li>Bachelor’s degree from an accredited four-year college in English, Journalism, or Communications is required</li></ul>
<p><b>Experience: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Minimum of 5 years of professional work-related experience required</li>
<li>Adobe InDesign desktop publishing software desired, but not mandatory</li>
<li>Adobe Photoshop software desired</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Skills &amp; Knowledge: </b></p>

<li>Ability to maintain composure under pressure</li>
<li>Proficiency in Microsoft Office suite including SharePoint, Visio and other database applications</li>
</ul>
