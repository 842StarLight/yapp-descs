<h2>Business Analyst Corporate Data and Analytics</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p>Covetrus is a global animal-health technology and services company dedicated to empowering veterinary practice partners to drive improved health and financial outcomes. We’re bringing together products, services, and technology into a single platform that connects our customers to the solutions and insights they need to work best. Our passion for the well-being of animals and those who care for them drives us to advance the world of veterinary medicine. Covetrus is headquartered in Portland, Maine, with more than 5,000 employees, serving over 100,000 customers around the globe</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>SUMMARY</b><br>
The <b>BI Business Analyst</b> plays a vital role in the overall creation of requirements, documentation, and development of current and future capabilities across multiple data engineering and analytics teams. This position works in alignment with Business Intelligence, other global teams, and is highly interactive with the executive and commercial business partners. The scope of the role includes requirements gathering and documentation, collaborating with business, analytics, and engineering teams to understand customer needs and assist development and quality engineering teams to translate requirements into actionable, testable user stories. Primarily, this role supports large, strategic initiatives – from idealization through delivery.<br>
</p>
<p><b>ESSENTIAL DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES</b> include the following. Other duties may be assigned.</p>
<ul>
<li>Become a Subject Matter Expert in the field of Covetrus global BI</li>
<li>Gather and document requirements from both internal and external customers</li>
<li>Assist with identifying workflow and process gaps and issues</li>
<li>Organize and conduct requirement reviews with stakeholders and development teams</li>
<li>Coordinate cross-functional requirements across teams- ensuring inter-dependencies are documented, understood, and communicated.</li>
<li>Work with analytics and development managers to ensure sprint and release plans demonstrate traceabiility</li>
<li>Drive a tenacious focus in validating that what the team is building will meet the needs and demands of our customers</li>
<li>Participate in and foster a working environment where creativity, innovation, ownership and teamwork are encouraged and rewarded</li>
</ul>

<li>None</li>
