<h2>( POS )Field Service Technician</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>An Associate Field Engineer (AFE) will work on computer equipment in the Point of Sale (POS) industry. An AFE must have strong computer repair hardware and software skills. A technical degree, technical certifications (A+, Dell, HP, MCSE etc…) or equivalent field experience is required. Equipment serviced include items such as Cash Registers, Printers, Scanners, Self-Checkout Units, EFT Machines and Servers. Products serviced are manufactured by the likes of Fujitsu, HP, Dell, Toshiba, Cisco, NCR, etc.</p>
<p>This position is a long term position with no expected end date. It is a Retail market and requires availability to work 7x24. Most work is done between 8am and 10pm, but there are installs and projects that occasionally require overnight work. The AFE will be scheduled to work 40+ hours/wk (varying days). AFEs will be required to drive throughout a large territory to cover service calls as needed. Overtime will be paid in accordance with state laws.</p>
<p>AFE’s will be provided a laptop, cell phone, tools and a parts inventory. AFEs will be required to have a dependable source of transportation (Car or truck) and a valid driver license. Use of a personal vehicle will be reimbursed based on mileage. Current rate is $0.54/mile and varies. If/when possible, a Corporate Vehicle will be provided and the AFE will need to provide safe parking and care of vehicle.</p>
<p>All AFEs will be required go through a background check and drug screen. You must be able to legally work in the United States and have a valid driver license.</p>
<p>Daily Requirements:</p>
<ul><li>Local and Long Distance Driving</li></ul>
<ul><li>Some lifting up to 75lbs.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Standby and after hrs. availability</li></ul>
<p>Job Type: Full-time</p>
<p>Salary: $20.00 /hour</p>

</ul>
