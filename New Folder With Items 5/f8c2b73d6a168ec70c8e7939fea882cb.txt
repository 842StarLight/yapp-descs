<p><b>ABOUT AGILIS SYSTEMS, LLC</b></p>
<p>Agilis Systems is a fast-growing SaaS provider who develops location-based software solutions for businesses and consumers. Our GPS fleet management solutions serve the needs of thousands of small business customers. We market our services through highly recognized brands such as Linxup.com and MOTOsafety.com.</p>
<p><b>ABOUT THE POSITION</b></p>
<p>The Fulfillment Department is an integral part of Operations, receiving incoming deliveries of material and devices and fulfilling, tracking and coordinating customer orders through the fulfillment process. The Fulfillment Specialist, working with a team, will ensure that orders are processed accurately in a timely manner, verify that incoming/outgoing devices are marked and inventoried, and ensure quality inventory control. The Fulfillment Specialist will also be required to keep the department clean, well-organized, and safe as well as help on other projects as needed depending on order volume. The ideal candidate will be a detail-oriented team player with the proven ability to multitask and problem solve in a fast-paced work environment.</p>
