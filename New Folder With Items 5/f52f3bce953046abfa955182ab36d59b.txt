<h2>Senior Technical Support Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p><b>Essential Functions &amp; Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Sell company products and services to customers by processing orders, price and delivery inquiries, providing technical and application information, soliciting customer orders and suggesting additional products in a fast paced multi-tasking environment.</li>
<li>
Maintains responsibility of orders from quotation to collection:<ul>
<li>
Select and specify a variety of products utilizing a variety of FCX and vendor supplied tools/software.</li>
<li>
Responds to customer inquiries.</li>
<li>
Processes customers’ orders and send order acknowledgements.<ul><li>
Review customer purchase orders for special conditions/documentation</li></ul>
</li>
<li>
Checks availability and verifies ship dates.</li>
<li>
Increases order size by suggesting related items, explaining features, and checking customer’s buying history.</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>
Processes and expedites orders, customer returns and returns to vendors in an accurate and timely manner. Sources product and creates purchase orders. Follows-up with vendors to ensure timely delivery.</li>
<li>
Negotiates pricing and delivery with vendors when necessary.</li>
<li>
Works closely with Outside Salespeople in soliciting business, executing order transactions and maintaining superior customer service levels.</li>
<li>
Communicates effectively and participates positively as a member of the Branch/Division inside sales team (e.g. sharing work load when possible, picking incoming phone calls, etc.)</li>
<li>
Maximizes gross margin by ensuring that FCX gets paid for superior service, problem solving, and technical expertise.</li>
<li>
Reviews open customer order reports and takes action on open items; specifically, those items that may be at risk in meeting the customer’s promised delivery date.</li>
<li>
Reads and reviews customer requests for project proposal, interprets technical specifications, and develops proposals in coordination with the outside sales team. Proposal responsibilities include cover letters, bill of materials, cost work up, pricing, exceptions, clarifications, etc.</li>
<li>
Manages project orders to include order entry, purchasing, supplier/vendor document development and delivery, document control, delivery scheduling and communication of project details with the customer.</li>
<li>
Other duties as assigned by Management.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Essential Physical Functions/Equipment Used:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Able to operate standard office equipment, namely computer, multi-line VoIP phone system, printer/copier/scanner and other equipment as needed.</li>
<li>
Must be able to sit for long periods of time while viewing computer screen.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Essential Functions:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Self-motivated with a can-do attitude that identifies smart, creative methods to complete complex tasks with minimal effort.</li>
<li>
Aggressive in sales tactics and strategies; desire to close sales every phone call.</li>
<li>
Excellent internal &amp; external communication and problem solving skills.</li>
<li>
Strong organizational and task management skills.</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>
Able to tackle multiple projects while being organized, detail, and deadline oriented.</li>
<li>
Ability to relate to all levels of employees within the organization, as well as people customers &amp; vendors.</li>
<li>
Ability to focus on company expectations while continuously striving for continuous improvement.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Preferred Education/Experience:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Bachelor’s Degree <b>preferred</b>.</li>
<li>
5-7+ years of experience in an Inside Technical Sales/Customer Service/Application Engineer role with an industrial distributor or engineered product recommended.</li>
</ul>
