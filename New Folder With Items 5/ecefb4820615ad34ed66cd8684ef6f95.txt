<h2>Copy and Print Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<ul>
<li>Educate customers on business solutions and print product offerings (e.g. digital printing, wide format, business products, specialty products, and binding and finishing services).</li>
<li>Apply knowledge of print products and production methods to make product recommendations.</li>
<li>Effectively apply knowledge of printing concepts such as paper considerations, file formatting, and output settings.</li>
<li>Apply knowledge of color management to educate customer and make file adjustments as needed.</li>
<li>Provide customers with quotes and follow up and consult on complex orders.</li>
<li>Brainstorm and mock-up design ideas.</li>
<li>Meet with customers to present concepts and adjust designs to fit their needs or taste.</li>
<li>Effectively manage production workflow, schedules and production materials.</li>
<li>Manage quality control throughout production process to ensure finished product is correct and meets customer and The UPS Store quality standards.</li>
<li>Follow and understand quality guidelines.</li>
<li>Interpret a variety of instructions furnished in written or oral form.</li>
<li>Proofread orders and understand all job requirements before beginning desktop process.</li>
<li>Operate and maintain print and finishing equipment.</li>
<li>Deliver print sales and production training to other Center Associates.</li>
<li>Greet customers in a friendly manner; determine customer needs, and how to exceed their expectations.</li>
<li>Deliver exceptional customer service through customer engagement and quality product delivery.</li>
<li>Be knowledgeable of products and services offered and properly promote them to customers.</li>
<li>Identify customer needs and offer additional services which could benefit them.</li>
<li>Receive, sort and place mail in mailboxes accurately.</li>
<li>Record all sales transactions completely and accurately in Point of Sale</li>
<li>Pack contents for shipping using established procedures and products.</li>
<li>Receive and prepare documents for printing.</li>
<li>Operate all other software packages.</li>
<li>Receive and process packages for shipping, including lifting and weighing packages, accessing shipment manifesting software, and generating labels. May require retrieving packages from customer's vehicle when requested.</li>
<li>Process and document damaged shipments, late and lost package claims.</li>
<li>Assist in merchandising the store, including: stocking shelves with product, ensuring clean and orderly appearance of product, informing appropriate personnel of inventory levels for re-ordering supplies.</li>
<li>Perform end of day duties: reconciliation of cash, checks, money orders, credit cards, and all other transactions accurately and in accordance with company policy and procedure.</li>
<li>Operate copiers, fax machines, binding equipment and all other office machines in a safe, efficient and productive manner. Help customers operate office machines located in retail area.</li>
<li>General housekeeping duties.</li>
<li>Other duties as assigned.</li>
</ul>
