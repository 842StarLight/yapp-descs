<h2>Insurance Marketing Automation Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Job Description
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<p>Applied Systems, Inc., a worldwide leader in insurance technology, is currently searching for an <b>Insurance</b> <b>Marketing</b> <b>Automation</b> <b>Specialist</b> to facilitate the implementation and optimization of Applied’s Marketing Automation product for our clients, ensuring a smooth implementation and ongoing successful utilization. The Insurance Marketing Automation Specialist interacts with the customer to create a shared sense of direction and establish a trusted working relationship. During the implementation cycle and beyond, services are provided to ensure Applied’s products are fully functional, and remain a value-add, within the customer’s organization.</p>
<div></div>
<br>
<div><b>
RESPONSIBILITIES</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
Coordinate entire project lifecycle with each customer; accurately assess client needs, identifying any unique priorities of the customer and determining appropriate implementation path between “standard” and “customized”
</li>
<li>Develop and maintain implementation project plans for each agency deployment, revising to meet changing needs and requirements</li>
<li>
Handle “customized” projects including but not limited to, applying knowledge of industry best practices, drafting and submitting statements of work (SOW), and providing consulting services as outlined in the SOW</li>
<li>
Communicate project information, including difficult/sensitive information, to customer, team, and management</li>
<li>
Assist with development of training materials, consulting documentation and end user resources through collaboration with internal teams</li>
<li>
Manage customer expectations against industry marketing benchmarks for what is considered successful outcomes, such as open rates, sales conversions, etc.
</li>
<li>Serve as a Subject Matter Expert to share information between customers and various departments at Applied</li>
<li>
Maintain advanced knowledge of product functionality, keep current on insurance trends/legislation, and participate in ongoing product readiness efforts</li>
<li>
Utilize OpenAir to effectively manage Services deliverables, record work effort, etc.<br>
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>

<ul>
<li>
Bachelor’s degree or equivalent work experience</li>
<li>
2-3 years’ experience in marketing automation, general marketing, or lead generation</li>
<li>
Knowledge of group insurance and employee benefits<ul><li>
Property &amp; casualty/risk management knowledge a plus</li></ul>
</li>
<li>
2-3 years’ experience in software training, implementation and consulting or another related customer service field is a plus</li>
<li>
Proven ability to build and cultivate positive client relationships</li>
<li>
Motivated, self-starter individual willing to show their leadership potential and flexibility in developing process and documentation for newly defined role</li>
<li>
Solid interpersonal, written, and verbal communication skills; experience writing SOW’s a plus</li>
<li>
Strong group facilitation skills as well as demonstrated skills in training, group/individual presentations, etc.</li>
<li>
Ability to manage multiple projects with strong organization skills and detail orientation.</li>
<li>
Strong general computer skills, including advanced knowledge of Microsoft Office business software
<ul><li>Intermediate to advanced skills in Internet technology and usage</li></ul>
</li>
</ul>
