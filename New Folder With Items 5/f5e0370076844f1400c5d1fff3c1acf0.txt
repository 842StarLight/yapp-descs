<h2>Manager of Billing & Revenue</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>Job Description<br>
<br>
<p>As the Manager of Billing and Revenue, you will be responsible for managing a team of up to four accountants, the day-to-day billing and collections duties of the company, and the related GAAP accounting and financial reporting related to those operations. This position will get exposure to a broad scope of business activity and work cross functionally with other departments and external customers.</p>
<p>
Responsibilities:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Manage billing and collections team and daily invoicing activity and related revenue recognition</li>
<li>
Coordinate with sales team to resolve outstanding customer issues</li>
<li>
Ability to research and answer accounting and billing questions</li>
<li>
Ensure collections goals are achieved</li>
<li>
Support monthly and annual close by preparing supporting schedules and miscellaneous financial analysis</li>
<li>
Develop, enhance and document existing procedures to streamline department efficiency</li>
<li>
Contribute to and/or leads special projects and other tasks as they arise</li>
<li>
Establish effective working relationships with other employees and areas supported</li>
<li>
Other duties as assigned</li>
</ul>
</div>
<br>
<div>Qualifications<br>
<br>
<p>Experience, Skills and Competencies:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Degree in Accounting</li>
<li>
5 plus years of accounting experience including billing, collections, general ledger, balance sheet account reconciliation in SAAS environment</li>
<li>
People management experience</li>
<li>
CPA or public accounting experience a plus</li>
<li>
Solid understanding of US GAAP, emphasis on revenue recognition principles and application thereof</li>
<li>
Experience with customer service in a professional capacity</li>
<li>
Excellent communication and organizational skills</li>
<li>
Proficiency and aptitude for applicable software (Intacct, Salesforce)</li>
<li>
Problem-solve, prioritize work and meet deadlines</li>
<li>
Enthusiastic about a complex and changing organization and embraces changes in structure and work priorities</li>
<li>
Positive and professional attitude; perform responsibilities with integrity</li>
</ul>
<br>
Additional Information<br>
<br>
<p>All your information will be kept confidential according to EEO guidelines.</p>
