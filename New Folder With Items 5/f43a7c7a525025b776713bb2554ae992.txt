<h2>Engineering (Security Systems Engineer)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>Description:<br>
The Commercial Security Estimator spends their days working with Sales Executives designing Commercial Electronic Security Systems and other low voltage systems. The primary function of this position is to engineer and design electronic security systems to meet functionality and performance requirements, and ultimately to bring the successful completion of specific project goals and objectives while meeting and often challenging deadlines.<br>
<br>
The Core Sales Estimator performs a critical function within the organization to ensure the National Account and Core Commeriacl customers' needs are met through a focus on high quality customer interaction before, during and after the sales process, maximizing customer satisfaction, and retention as well as customer perception of the Johnson Controls value proposition in the market place.<br>
<br>
How you will do it<br>
<br>
The primary focus of this position will be to design complete and accurate electronic security systems while adhering to classic project constraints. Create and depict these systems accurately in CAD format.<br>
<br>
<ul>
<li>50% of the time would be spent with sales people to design and estimate systems for Johnson Control Customers</li>
<li>50% of the time would be spent creating engineered drawing packages for project submittal.</li>
</ul>
<br>
<ul>
<li>Provide on time, accurate site surveys at a local branch level to meet National Accounts and Core Sales survey request timeframes. Will conduct site surveys, and review job estimates with local Install or Branch manager before submitting completed documents in to the system for processing.</li>
<li>Process and complete all required documentation in accordance with approved and standardized procedures. To include UL and AHJ requirements.</li>
<li>May provide functional guidance to less experienced representatives.</li>
<li>Maintain an in depth knowledge of complete line of products/services and customers' issues and needs through in-house training and reading/research.</li>
<li>Activities and responsibilities will adhere to current policies, procedures, products, programs and services.</li>
</ul>
<br>

<li>Self-Motivation with good organizational skills</li>

<li>Valid driver's license with acceptable driving record</li>

</ul>
