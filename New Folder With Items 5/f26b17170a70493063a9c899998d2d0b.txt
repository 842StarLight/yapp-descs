<h2>Traffic Log Editor, Local</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<h1 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Primary Purpose<br>
</b></h1>
<p></p>
<p>Responsible for managing the daily ad log to maximize inventory and revenue.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<h1 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Key Activities<br>
</b></h1>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Edit advertising elements on the daily program log for the current week.
</li>
<li>Ensure that all elements are entered accurately and in accordance with advertiser requirements to ensure quality control.
</li>
<li>Correct log conflicts as required.
</li>
<li>Perform electronic-log changes to finalize logs when necesssary; includes coordinating last minute orders/revisions.
</li>
<li>Generate a daily station log.
</li>
<li>Work closely with sales account executives, sales management, engineering, promotion departments, and central traffic hub.
</li>
<li>Act as back-up to other positions in the traffic department as needed.
</li>
<li>Perform other duties as assigned.<br>
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<h1 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Skills<br>
</b></h1>
<p></p>
<p><b>Education &amp; Experience/Certifications
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>High school diploma or equivalent required.
</li>
<li>Minimum of 2 years related experience preferred. Previous experience as copy editor highly preferable.
</li>
<li>Previous broadcast experience especially with ad management and broadcast traffic systems (i.e. WideOrbit, OSI etc.) desirable.<br>
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Skills &amp; Abilities
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Proficient with MS Office products including Word, Excel, PowerPoint and Outlook.
</li>
<li>Ability to adhere to strict deadlines and complete work assignments in a timely and accurate manner.
</li>
<li>Adaptable and flexible with ability to shift focus in fast-paced, high-pressure environment.
</li>
<li>Strong interpersonal skills.
</li>
<li>Good verbal and written communication skills.
</li>
<li>Team oriented and experienced in working in a team environment.
</li>
<li>Excellent organization and time management skills. Strict attention to detail.
</li>
<li>Strong problem-solving skills.<br>
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<h1 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Work Environment<br>
</b></h1>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>May require limited overtime to cover vacations and holidays.
</li>
<li>Standard office environment.</li>
</ul>
