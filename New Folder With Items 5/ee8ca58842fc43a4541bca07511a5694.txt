<h2>Supply Chain Operator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Job description</b></h2>
<p></p>
<div>
<div><ul><li>Shipping/receiving</li></ul></div>
o Physically load, unload raw materials, complex commodities, capital equipment, customers heat packs and miscellaneous items. Receive, unpack, check, and store goods and materials.<br>
o Verify items against shipping documents, and inspect condition of shipments.<br>
o Process all received materials/commodities/equipment and other items accurately in the system (SAP and Tiscam if necessary), following SLS KY guidelines and procedures<br>
o Update and maintain purchasing receiving databases, ship packages, all outbound shipment records, keep archive records<br>
o Ensure Class A items are received in and certificates forwarded to Quality Department.<br>
o Spare parts and Graphite repairs follow SOP and communicate any issues to Purchasing/Logistics/ end user<br>
o Responsible for correctly shipping/storing materials according to OSHA regulations and SLS KY Safety guidelines<br>
o Receive incoming customer Heatpack Purchase Orders into Tiscam in a timely manner<br>
o Interco deliveries: Ensure Disk deliveries from SRB and MOL are received into both SAP and Tiscam<br>
o WB torque tubes: ensure receipts and shipments for WB are done physically and in the system (SAP)<br>
o Delivery of certain waste stream (IE Scrap fiber, Rejected Disk, Carbon Dust bags, Worn Pucks)<p></p>
<div><ul><li>Cycle counts:</li></ul></div>
o Maintain inventory accountability. Perform cycle counts on all items on a monthly basis.<br>
o Perform spot checks as needed in case of discrepancies<p></p>
<div><ul><li>Warehouse activities:</li></ul></div>
o Operate warehouse equipment including forklift, manual/electric pallet jacks, and scrubber. Load and unload material utilizing hand trucks, forklifts, hoists, conveyors, or other handling equipment.<br>
o Accurately maintain daily check sheets on equipment<br>
o Milk-run: Support daily and weekly Milk runs (Including be not limited to: RFPC Hardware/Production consumables/Critical Maintenance spares/Calibration gases)<br>
o Quality inspection: Items that need inspection must be addressed, segregated and put away in a timely manner.<br>
o Maintenance Spares: once received, Maintenance items must be stored in a timely manner<br>
o System movements: any parts being moved from one warehouse to another (Fiber, Hardware, Maintenance items, Repair items,…) have to be transferred in the system (SAP) as well.<br>
o WB torque tubes: Ensure physical deliveries of torque tubes to WB<br>
o Work orders: prepare any needed items for Production and Maintenance work orders<br>
o Shipment Requests (SAP Purchase orders and SharePoint): prepare, package, weight and measure items to be sold for Logistics<p></p>
<div><ul><li>Communications</li></ul></div>
o Communications with Customer Service, Operations, Purchasing, Welcome Center, Quality and Logistics must be clear and detailed.<br>
o Management of dock hours drop-offs, pick-ups and refusals per policy<br>
o Call and Log for Trash dumpster/Scrap Metal switch outs<br>
o Assist with Recyclables and Waste steams as needed (i.e. Achille Bayart, Shamokin, Wisdom,…)<p></p>
</div>

<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Job requirements</b></h2>
<div>
<p>Experience/Education/Certification Required:<br>
</p>
<div><ul>
<li>High school diploma/GED</li>
<li>Experience in shipping/receiving/storage of products including complex commodities, hazardous materials and international standards</li>
<li>Experience in material/inventory control techniques. (Kanban, MRP, and/or Visual Management, SAP, SharePoint)</li>
<li>Education/training in computer/software applications and HAZMAT preferred</li>

</ul></div>

<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Complementary description</b></h2>
<p></p>
<div>
<div><ul><li>Daily activities</li></ul></div>
o 5S : Maintain a clean and organized shipping receiving dock and warehouse area<br>
o SQCDPI : attend every morning meeting<br>
o Ensure compliance with established internal control procedures<br>
o Assist Operations as assigned for material movement requests<br>
o Waste streams : Assist with removal of Trash hoppers/Quality scrap/Rejected Disks, and Recycle collection units.<br>
o Perform other related duties as required<p></p>

Direct Reports None<br>
Broad Control None</p>

Physical/Environmental Scope of Position:<br>
</p>

<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Company information</b></h2>
<div>
<p>Safran is an international high-technology group, operating in the aircraft propulsion and equipment, space and defense markets. Safran has a global presence, with more than 92,000 employees and sales of 21 billion euros in 2018. Working alone or in partnership, Safran holds world or European leadership positions in its core markets. Safran undertakes Research &amp; Development programs to meet fast-changing market requirements, with total R&amp;D expenditures of around 1.5 billion euros in 2018.</p>
<p>
Safran is ranked among the Top 100 Global Innovators by Thomson Reuters and is featured on the "Happy at work" rankings. The Group places fourth on the Universum ranking for the favorite companies of newly-qualified engineers in France.</p>
<p>
Safran Landing Systems is the world leader of aircraft landing and braking systems. The company has partnerships with 30 airframers in civil, regional, commercial and military transport, and supports 27,000 aircraft that operate around 70,000 landings daily.</p>
