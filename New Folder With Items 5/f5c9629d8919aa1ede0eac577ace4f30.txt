<h2>Bilingual Dealer Orientation Representative II</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<div>The Bilingual Dealer Orientation Representative (DOR) is responsible for ensuring that every bilingual dealer’s onboarding experience to NextGear Capital platform is successful. Enhancing NextGear's consultative approach, the DOR provides an informative onboarding welcome call, with consistent messaging to all bilingual dealers, reviewing all NextGear expectations on dealer managing account and what dealer can expect from NextGear. The DOR is responsible for managing communication with internal/external customers and going over additional products to benefit the dealer. This will include ensuring all tracking of onboarding calls via (CRM) software – Salesforce, completed and in progress, are handled in a thorough and accurate manner, making using their line of credit, convenient and efficient for dealers and NextGear Capital Field employees.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<ul>
<li>
Be a resource on the DOR team for our new bilingual dealers at NextGear Capital
</li>
<li>Create a welcoming experience with each new dealer to NextGear Capital
</li>
<li>Contact and schedule onboarding welcome calls with dealers.\</li>
<li>
Communicate and follow-up regarding scheduling and onboarding welcome calls with NextGear Capital Field Employees.
</li>
<li>Ensure dealer is aware line of credit has been activated and aware of how to utilize line of credit. \</li>
<li>
Execute dealer onboarding welcome calls efficiently and accurately by walking a dealer through their account information and benefits of having a line of credit with NextGear Capital. \</li>
<li>
Go over additional products or add-ons to the line of credit at the dealers disposal.
</li>
<li>Expected to cover all items pertaining to NextGear expectations on the DOR call outline when needed.
</li>
<li>Maintain all dealer contact activity, notes, and onboarding calls through NextGear Capital's Customer Relationship Management (CRM) software – Salesforce.<br>
</li>
</ul>
