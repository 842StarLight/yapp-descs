<p><b>
About US Storage Centers</b></p>
<p>
Westport Properties/US Storage Centers (“USSC”) is a top 10 Self Storage Management company in the country with over 130 properties, operating in multi-states under management totaling over 30 million rentable square feet. Founded in 1985, we have built a premier management platform in the industry. Since its inception, USSC has helped thousands of its customers organize their lives by delivering convenient and affordable self-storage solutions.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<div>We are seeking to fill several open positions available in our self-storage facilities.</div>
<ul><li>
Assistant Manager (full-time): salary range $12.00 to $13.00 per hour</li></ul>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
The open position is a <b>permanent full-time position</b> working with an opportunity for advancement with one of the leading providers of self-storage in the United States. The qualified candidates would be an energetic, outgoing, self-starter, who has excellent sales, marketing and customer service skills.</div>
<div>
<br>
<i>
This position contributes to facility profitability by developing and maintaining mutually beneficial business relationships and being committed to customer satisfaction. This Manager pays close attention to detail and has general sales, marketing and computer skills.</i>
</div>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<p><b><i>Responsibilities</i></b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Responsible for the sales, marketing, occupancy, and delinquency management of the facility</li>
<li>
Provides outstanding customer service including assessing and resolving customer problems and professionally handling telephone and walk-in inquiries</li>
<li>
Shows, rents and cleans self-storage units as well as actively selling and maintaining supplies of moving and packing materials</li>
<li>
General management of the office including but not limited to opening and closing the office on time each day, keeping and balancing petty cash, making daily bank deposits and preparing daily, weekly and monthly reports as required by management</li>
<li>
Keeping the facility rented at optimum levels by effectively utilizing advertising, promotional and customer relation skills as approved by management</li>
<li>
Maintains all required tenant documentation in a neat and orderly manner as directed by management</li>
<li>
Controls delinquencies through telephone contact and letters on a scheduled basis including sending out monthly invoices</li>
<li>
Cleans space(s) as vacated, hallways, and grounds as needed. Clean office and restrooms daily</li>
<li>
Maintains a neat, clean, safe and secure facility including minor maintenance and daily lock checks as well as visual inspection daily</li>
<li>
Assist in preparing units for auction as prescribed by law, and approved by management</li>
<li>
Effectively communicates with management and fellow associates</li>
<li>
Other functional duties as assigned<br>
</li>
</ul>

<ul>
<li>
Basic Computer Skills are required</li>
<li>
Good organizational skills
</li>
</ul>
