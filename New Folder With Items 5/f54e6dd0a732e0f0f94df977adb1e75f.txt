<h2>Digital Advertising Sales Consultant</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Job Description</b></p>
<p>Want to start your sales and marketing career with a relatively small-but-growing company looking for new ideas and energy?</p>
<p>Join our leadership team, and help us get to the next level.</p>
<p>We’re looking for a digital enthusiast who loves talking with people and solving their problems. If you’re good at developing sincere professional connections, understanding client needs and then closing a sale, you could be our next Digital Media Sales Executive. You need to be motivated by money, because your earnings aren't limited with this job. But even more important, you need to be motivated by helping other business owners — that's essential to your success and career growth, as well as ours.</p>
<p>We’ll do our best to help you master your job. We’ll equip you with sales tools and teach you how to target each client and audience. We'll help develop your skills and prepare you for your next challenge.</p>
<p><b>A little about Advocate Media</b></p>
<p>We’re the neighborhood media company covering Lakewood/East Dallas, Preston Hollow, Oak Cliff and Lake Highlands. We know where the best places to eat, drink and play in our neighborhoods. We also know all the quirks, the people to talk to and the best events. We print four monthly magazines, but we run four companion websites and social media sites, and we also publish a city-wide daily email newsletter. Our digital advertising options are almost endless, and we’re always working on a few new ones, too.</p>
<p><b>Your Responsibilities: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Work on managing and building digital sales. You can sell print ads, too.</li>
<li>Consult, develop and present client-specific marketing plans.</li>
<li>Work on digital product development based target audiences.</li>
<li>Meeting with current and potential clients to generate new ad revenue.</li>
<li>Generate new leads and develop new business partnerships.</li>
<li>Achieve and beat monthly, quarterly and annual sales targets.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Requirements: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>You have a Bachelor's Degree, preferably in Marketing, Business, Journalism, Communications or a related field</li>
<li>You have a proven track record of successful sales, preferably in digital and social media</li>
<li>You know how to use Instagram, Twitter, Snapchat and Facebook. You also know which one a grandma, a 17-year-old, your mom and you use the most.</li>
<li>You also know what’s happening in the world of digital media — what’s the next big thing digital?</li>
<li>You are highly self-motivated: Hey, not everyone is!</li>
<li>You <i>really </i>love the Internet, social media and everything related to digital content.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>What We Provide: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Lots of tools for career development</li>
<li>Health Insurance (Health, Vision, Dental)</li>
<li>All the tech you need: MacBook Air, iPad and etc.</li>
<li>Flexible hours. We want you to get your work done, but we want you to be happy, too.</li>
<li>Good work environment. Think the creativity of a startup, stability of a established company, and the relaxed atmosphere of a mom and pop shop.</li>
<li>Great location. You’re literally 15 minutes from everything.</li>
<li>
<b>A solid base salary plus UNCAPPED COMMISSIONS</b> on sales with lots of income growth potential.</li>
</ul>
