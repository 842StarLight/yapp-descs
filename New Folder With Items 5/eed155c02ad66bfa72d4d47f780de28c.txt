<h2>Product Manager – Cable Cloud Applications</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>What You'll Do</b></h2>
<p>
The Cable Group includes the Cloud Native Broadband Router product portfolio comprising software and cloud-based products and services. In this role, you will be responsible for defining the software strategy, roadmap and positioning for cloud based operations applications and management products. Drive solution lifecycle including market research, competitive analysis, product requirements definition, and customer adoption. You will learn to transition from strategy formulation to execution and delivery while driving a stellar customer experience.
</p>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Who You'll Work With</b></h2>
<p>
As a member of the Product Management team, you will collaborate extensively with engineering, technical marketing engineers, user-experience designers, and peer product managers to build a cohesive strategy and see through the execution. You will also work with Cisco sales and customers to drive awareness and adoption of new solutions, and garner customer insights to drive future solution development.
</p>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Who You Are</b></h2>
<p>
You will help further refine Cisco’s Cable application portfolio and building blocks for our customers to operate their networks.
</p>
<p>Articulate and design exceptional customer experience around cable’s operational simplification and management Software. Work closely with partners from Engineering, Sales, Corporate Marketing, and Finance to deliver a world-class product. Partner with sales and customer experience teams to drive adoption.</p>
<ul>
<li>
5+ years of product management experience in cloud, and container frameworks, Network Automation, PaaS or SaaS technologies</li>
<li>
Good understanding of DevOps, Networking Technologies and SDN</li>
<li>
You have a data driven approach to problem solving and pay attention to detail without losing sight of the big-picture.</li>
<li>
Phenomenal written and verbal communications skills (Industry presentations and/or presentations to senior executive).</li>
</ul>
