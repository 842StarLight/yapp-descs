We are currently seeking an <b>Product Service Engineer </b>to provide field service and technical support primarily for Unico product offering: variable speed drives and associated control systems. Position will be located in Franksville, WI.<br>
<br>
<b>Job Summary &amp; Responsibilities:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Analyzes and resolves customer issues in control applications involving Unico / Micromatic / PowerPro hardware and software. Determines if customer is able to resolve the issue or service tech should be dispatched.</li>
<li>Provides frontline customer support such as troubleshooting phone call &amp; emails.</li>
<li>Additionally generates and maintains supporting technical documentation.</li>
<li>Works with sales, engineers, and field tech teams and customers directly to bring issues to resolution.</li>
<li>Participates in after hours tech support rotation.</li>
<li>Gathers accurate and complete information of product, systems, and application failures.</li>
<li>Communicates to various teams through established processes.</li>
<li>Coordinates with customer and/or Aftermarket Parts to determine appropriate replacement parts and dispatch of service support as needed. Determines if in or out of warranty.</li>
<li>Conducts data analysis of present and historical data to identify improvement opportunities.</li>
<li>Maintains pareto/awareness of defects identified by customers and drives communication and resolution of top issues.</li>
<li>Participates in team process improvements, High Energy Teams, and DMAIC processes, etc.</li>
</ul>
<br>
<b>
Minimum Qualifications:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor's degree in a technical discipline or equivalent related experience.</li>
<li>4+ years in application of Unico or similar product.</li>
<li>Green Belt in Lean Six Sigma within 3 years strongly desired</li>
<li>Field service experience with electronic systems including safety training and awareness of electronic and mechanical systems a plus but not required.</li>
<li>Must demonstrate professionalism (appearance, speech, manner, communication) and have good communication and interpersonal skills with the ability to work with customers in a positive and professional manner.</li>
<li>Basic computer skills required.</li>
<li>Reliability, punctuality and excellent attendance a must.</li>
<li>Must demonstrate excellent attention to detail and manual dexterity.</li>
<li>Ability to handle pressure, adversity and change; flexibility and adaptability.</li>
<li>Ability to be part of after hours tech support rotation (i.e. 1 week per month).</li>
<li>Ability to travel 10% of time.</li>
<li>Ability to work independently and consistently meet deadlines.</li>
<li>Requires strong commitment to customer service and teamwork.</li>
<li>Excellent communication skills (oral, written, and listening) and the ability to collaborate effectively with diverse group of professionals.</li>
</ul>
