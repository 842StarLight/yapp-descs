<h2>Business Development Liaison</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<div>
<p>The Outreach Business Development Liaison will be responsible for identifying, qualifying and creating new business relationships among potential Outreach clients in the defined caption area. The Outreach Business Development Liaison will meet regularly with physicians, nurses, practice administrators, office managers, and representatives of nursing homes and industry. The primary purpose is to generate relationships that result in revenues for the Laboratory Department and the Healthcare System.</p>
<p>
Requirements: BA/BS Degree in Administration, Marketing, Communication or Health Sciences preferred. Three to five years experience specific to the physician office market promoting a health care product or service. Also requires a customer service orientation, a teamwork approach, flexibility, organizational and problem-solving skills. Experience using sales contact management systems as well as using basic software programs such as Word, Excel, and Power Point. Must be willing to learn the laboratory and hospital s information systems.</p>
