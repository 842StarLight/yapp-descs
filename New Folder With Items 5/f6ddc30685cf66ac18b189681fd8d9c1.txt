<h2>Subaru Automotive Finance Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p>The statements below are intended to describe the general nature and level of work being performed by persons assigned to this job. They are not intended to be an exhaustive list of all responsibilities, duties, skills and essential functions required of this position.<br>
<br>
<br>
</p>
<p><b>SUMMARY</b><b>:</b> Produces additional revenue for the dealership by selling finance and insurance programs and other appropriate after-sale items to new- and used-vehicle customers.</p>
<p><b>
ESSENTIAL DUTIES: </b>Sells financing and credit life, accident, and health insurance to customers.</p>
<p>
Provides customers with thorough explanation of aftermarket products and extended warranties.</p>
<p>
Converts cash deals to finance.</p>
<p>
Establishes and maintains good working relationships with several finance sources, including the manufacturer.</p>
<p>
Processes finance and lease deals accurately and fairly through financial sources to secure approval.</p>
<p>
Conducts business in an ethical and professional manner.</p>
<p>
Processes all federal, state, and dealer paperwork related to vehicle transaction.</p>
<p>
Understands and complies with federal, state, and local regulations that affect the new- and used-vehicle and finance departments.</p>
<p>
Completes paperwork necessary for vehicle sales and leases in a timely and efficient manner.</p>
<p>
Checks all paperwork for correct title, lien information, taxes, etc., before forwarding to accounting.</p>
<p>
Acquires and maintains current state insurance license, completing continuing credit insurance education as needed.</p>
<p>
Provides customers with complete explanation of manufacturer and dealership service procedures and policies.</p>
<p>
Seeks new lending institutions to secure competitive interest rates and finance programs continually.</p>
<p>
Manages the alternative finance source program to ensure that several sources are available.</p>
<p>
Seeks insurance companies for insurance paper.</p>
<p>
Maintains insurance files.</p>
<p>
Sets up and maintains a program which will ensure 100 percent turnover to the F&amp;I department.</p>
<p>
Provides sales force and sales managers with current information about finance and lease programs continually.</p>
<p>
Trains the sales staff regarding the benefits of financing, insurance and extended service programs.</p>
<p>
Works with sales managers to secure a reasonable profit from every sale.</p>
<p>
Coordinates finance forecasting with sales department forecasting to achieve desired levels of penetration and income.</p>
<p>
 Ensures collection of all finance and insurance fees.</p>
<p>
Takes all credit applications.</p>
<p>
Prepares reports on finance penetration.</p>
<p>
Works closely with general sales manager to establish sales department goals and objectives and ensures that they are achieved.</p>
<p>
Attends managers meetings.</p>
<p>
Handles all rate quotations.</p>
<p>
Develops and monitors guidelines for working with customers to ensure maximum customer satisfaction.</p>
<p>
Supervises and trains designated backup to ensure continued efficiency and profitability in the F&amp;I manager's absence.</p>
<p>
Maintains an adequate and current supply of all required contracts and paperwork needed for completion and finalization of car deals.</p>
<p>
Maintains a professional appearance.</p>
<p>
Maintains a clean and professional work environment.</p>
<p><b>
OTHER DUTIES:</b><b> </b>As may be assigned by supervisor.</p>
<p><b>
SUPERVISORY RESPONSIBILITIES:</b><b> </b>None</p>
<p><b>
QUALIFICATIONS:</b><b>
</b></p>
<p>Excellent communication skills (including listening, writing, speaking).</p>
<p>
Public relations skills. Ability to effectively establish rapport, present information and respond to questions from managers, customers and the general public.</p>
<p>
Skill and ability to meet people and listen</p>
<p>
Ability to read, write, speak and understand English.</p>
<p>
Mental ability to conduct on-going interpersonal interactions.</p>
<p>
Ability to actively participate in team oriented process.</p>
<p>
Ability to interpret a variety of instructions furnished in written, oral, or schedule form.</p>
<p>
Skill and ability to sell.</p>
<p>
Physical ability to use computer hardware/software.</p>
<p><b>
EDUCATIONAL SKILLS</b>: H.S. Diploma/Equivalent.</p>
<p><b>
COMMUNICATION SKILLS: </b>Speak, read and write English. Ability to read and comprehend instructions and information. Excellent communication skills. Has regular contact with others both inside and outside the organization. The most frequent external contacts are with customers and business associations. Internal and external interactions involve information exchange, negotiations and selling.</p>
<p><b>
PHYSICAL ACTIVITIES AND REQUIREMENTS:</b> While performing the duties of this job, the employee is regularly required to sit and stand for long periods of time; use hands to finger, handle controls, and talk or hear. The employee is required to reach with hands and arms. Specific vision abilities required by this job include close vision and the ability to adjust focus.</p>
<p><b>
WORK CONDITIONS:</b><b> </b>The work environment characteristics described here are representative of those an employee encounters while performing the essential functions of this job. This position requires the employee to work in an environment that may include frequent interruptions, evening work, and working long hours with varied hours and days as business dictates. Work with others and may be outside in inclement weather.</p>
<p><b>
WORK HOURS:</b> Sunday through Saturday</p>
<p>
 <b>RESPONSIBILITIES: </b>Care of Equipment: Daily</p>
<p>
 Safety of Others: Daily</p>
<p>
 Vendor Contact: Daily</p>
<p>
 Confidential Data: Daily</p>
<p>
 Decision making: Occasionally</p>
<p>
 Acts in Supervisory Capacity: Occasionally</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
SENSORY REQUIRMENTS</b>: Sight: Full</p>
<p>
 Hearing: Full</p>
