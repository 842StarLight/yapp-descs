<h2>Director of Business Operations</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<div>
<b>Advertising Specialty Institute (ASI)</b><b> </b>is the largest business and technology services company for the promotional product and advertising industry. ASI serves over 24,000 members including trade shows, conferences, events and business tools.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
<b>
The ASI Show!</b> attracts industry suppliers and distributors by offering education, high-profile keynote speakers, networking opportunities and an impressive show floor featuring the leading companies within the industry. The ASI Show provides a complete trade show experience in three host cities per year (Orlando, Fort Worth and Chicago). Additionally, The ASI Show offers the ASI Roadshow, the ad specialty industry’s most popular traveling show, fASIlitate &amp; fASI500, ASI’s hosted buyer events.</div>
<p>
The Director of Operations is responsible for business operations including; managing budgets, contract negotiation, securing venues, financial analysis and logistics for three major ASI Shows , ASI Roadshows, and fASIlitate/fASI500 events.
</p>
<p><b>Position Responsibilities Include:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Manages operations team.
</li>
<li>Expense budgeting/tracking, forecasting, process management and driving revenue generation efforts that maximizes profitability.</li>
<li>
Vendor contract negotiation, vendor management, acting general services contractor, managing housing, registration, AV, security, logistics, etc.</li>
<li>
Successfully identifying, negotiating and securing future dates/venues for all events.</li>
<li>
Work collaboratively with internal stakeholders including Sales and Marketing and ASI teams. Creates a team culture within the department while maintaining full compliance with processes.</li>
<li>
Providing best-in-class customer service to our clients and organizing events which service their needs with a high attention to detail balanced with fiscal responsibility.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Position Requirements:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Bachelor’s Degree required</li>
<li>
Experience with operations of multiple national trade shows and regional shows and managing shows for large associations and/ or member driven organizations.
</li>
<li>Strong financial management skills, strong organizational skills and the ability to communicate like a senior manager.</li>
<li>
Experience with negotiating contracts with conventions centers, major hotels, entertainment venues, key note speakers, transportation, food and beverage, AV, technology, staffing, etc.</li>
<li>
Must be personable, team-oriented, a good communicator, organized, deadline oriented and committed to the success of both our internal and external customers.</li>
<li>
Must be able to travel a minimum of 25% of the year.
</li>
</ul>
