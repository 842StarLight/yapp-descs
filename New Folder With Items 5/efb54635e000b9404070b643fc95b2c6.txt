<h2>Product Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Overview<br>
</b></h2>
<div></div>
<div><div><div>
<div>Welcome to Allscripts! Our Mission is to be the most trusted provider of innovative solutions that empower all stakeholders across the healthcare continuum to deliver worldclass outcomes. Our Vision is a Connected Community of Health that spans continents and borders. With the largest community of clients in healthcare, Allscripts is able to deliver an integrated platform of clinical, financial, connectivity and information solutions to facilitate enhanced collaboration and exchange of critical patient information.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
The primary purpose of this role is to deliver on product roadmap priorities to achieve time to market and time to value targets by managing the product development lifecycle for a given product(s) through proven technical leadership. The incumbent may have budget responsibility or revenue responsibility, i.e. project profitability, sales revenue, training budget, etc.</div>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<ul>
<li>Cross-functionally drive development and launch of the product from concept to delivery</li>
<li>
Defines and writes technical and functional specifications including requirements and detailed use cases for new product features</li>
<li>
Explains designs and constraints to stakeholders, writing User Stories for Dev teams</li>
<li>
Work with engineering, QA, professional services, and technical operations during design and implementation to shape the technical feature set and usability of the product and ensure the highest quality products delivered on-time, on budget and with superior performance and support</li>
<li>
Analyzes and ensures performance requirements are met</li>
<li>
Reviews test plans with software quality assurance team</li>
<li>
Collects and manages requirements from all stakeholders for one or more products across multiple product generations</li>
<li>
Synthesizes requirements from the various stakeholders</li>
<li>
Plans and manages execution of product roadmaps, development schedules and product/component designs</li>
<li>
Manages the cross functional compatibility of software, hardware, and firmware for the solution</li>
<li>
Coordinates business and contractual arrangements with partners</li>
<li>
Trains and supports others on functional area related topics</li>
<li>
Supports advisory boards, user groups and SIGs as needed</li>
</ul>
</div></div></div>
