<p><b>
Job Details:</b></p>
<p>
The Mortgage Loan Officer will be responsible for opening, closing and funding all loans according to the proper product type, investor and agency. All loans must comply with state and federal guidelines as well as UNMB corporate processes, procedures and guidelines and be executed in an accurate and timely manner.</p>
<p>
This position offers flexible scheduling, a supportive/positive management team, substantial earning potential with bonus and commission programs. Additional incentives include contests and rewards, an excellent benefits package including medical, 401(k) and much more.</p>
<p><b>
You Will:</b><br>
</p>
<ul>
<li>
Produce high quality loans which meet investor guidelines, provide excellent customer service and meet minimum production goals.</li>
<li>
Adhere to all federal and state compliance guidelines relative to the position.</li>
<li>
Utilize in-house technical systems and programs, (Encompass).</li>
<li>
Act as advocate for the borrower and as brand ambassador for UNMB.</li>
<li>
Maintain a professional, upbeat and respectful demeanor.</li>
<li>
Attend events to raise referral source awareness of UNMB’s products and services.</li>
<li>
Learn various borrowing programs/processes; stay current with changes, updates and new products.</li>
</ul>
