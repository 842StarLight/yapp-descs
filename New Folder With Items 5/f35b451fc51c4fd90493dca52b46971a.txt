This position
<br>The Senior Product Manager - Contract Lifecycle Management (CLM) will develop the roadmap, vision, definition, design and deliver the underlying platform systems and applications necessary to support continued system scale, a broadening product portfolio, the basis for new innovative product capabilities, and improve operational efficiencies related to these industries. Your objectives will be directly linked to business metrics such as revenue, customer satisfaction, and operational efficiency.
<br><br>
You will lead the integration of our CLM product into DocuSign Agreement Cloud and ensure the delivery of a great experience for both administrators and end users. You are an excellent, high-bandwidth communicator who engages on a daily basis with nearly every function at DocuSign -- from compliance and security to customer support, user experience, engineering and program management. By doing so, you will develop the roadmap, vision, functional specifications and designs of features that satisfy the evolving business needs and requirements of our customers. Your objectives will be directly linked to business metrics such as revenue, customer satisfaction and operational efficiency
<br><br>
This is an individual contributor role reporting to the Senior Manager, Product Management.
<br><br>
Responsibilities
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Lead the strategy and roadmap for DocuSign's core platform document processing and management systems</li>
<li>Drive the vision and requirements, translate them to functional specifications, facilitate a sound design and deliver an efficient developer experience</li>
<li>Own insights on the markets and segments in which these applications compete in order to stay abreast of industry trends and channels to anticipate customer needs</li>
<li>Evaluate and prioritize requirements in partnership with the business, leveraging industry best practices and new innovations</li>
<li>Prioritize and manage the backlog for effective release and sprint planning</li>
<li>Facilitate requirements definition across teams to decompose and document releases, epics, functional requirements to developer-ready user stories and acceptance criteria</li>
<li>Work with Program Management and Engineering to ensure product ships on time and to quality</li>
</ul>
