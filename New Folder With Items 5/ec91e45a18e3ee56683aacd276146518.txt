<h2>Marketing Director</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Tamarack, The Best of West Virginia is accepting resumes for the position of Marketing Director. This position requires passion, creativity, and accountability. Must be result oriented individual to continue to enhance Tamarack’s presence in our local community and traveler’s alike to create a destination to visit, shop and dine local. It will be your responsibility to develop and implement a unique and creative marketing plan with the marketing department, as well as, cohesively partnering with outside sources to execute a successful marketing strategy to increase visitor traffic and sales.</p>
<p>In addition:</p>
<ul>
<li>Ability to successfully manage multiple projects simultaneously while multi-tasking, prioritizing and meeting deadlines.</li>
<li>Event planning to executing and wrap-up</li>
<li>Educate Tamarack team on brand identity</li>
<li>Be involved in the community and work with local and state tourism partners</li>
<li>Find innovative ways to connect with target audiences</li>
<li>Experience in all social media marketing platforms and analytics.</li>
<li>Manage digital marketing campaigns</li>
<li>Increase brand awareness and reach across multiple platforms</li>
<li>Direct and oversee all design and advertising ventures</li>
<li>Train, and manage marketing team, including performance reviews</li>
<li>Present frequent progress reports to colleagues</li>
<li>Strong attention to detail and organization</li>
<li>Understanding and developing budgets and finance, including expenditures, ROI and P&amp;L statements.</li>
</ul>
<p>Notes:</p>
<ul>
<li>Marketing Director reports directly to the executive director</li>
<li>Includes medical, dental, life, vision, 401K (after one year) and PTO accrual</li>
<li>The marketing director is a salaried position based on experience and will require certain weekends and holidays.</li>
<li>Work closely with various departments throughout the organization.</li>
<li>Compensation will be discussed during the interview process</li>
<li><i><b>Deadline to submit resume is September 6, 2019. No phone calls.</b></i></li>
</ul>
<p>Candidate must possess the following:</p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree in marketing or equal experience in the marketing and strategic planning industry.</li>
<li>Proven leadership and management skills.</li>
<li>Strong ability to delegate, oversee for accountability and provide results in a timely manner.</li>
<li>Must be well-versed in Adobe Creative Suite, Microsoft Office, Mac Computer, Constant Contacts, Expression Engine</li>
</ul>
