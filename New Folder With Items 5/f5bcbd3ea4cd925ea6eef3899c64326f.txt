<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Why We Work at Dun &amp; Bradstreet
</b></h2>
<p></p>
<p>We are at a transformational moment in our company journey - and we’re so excited about it. Each day, we are finding new ways to strengthen our award-winning culture, and to accelerate creativity, innovation and growth. Our purpose is to help customers improve business performance with Dun &amp; Bradstreet’s Data Cloud and Live Business Identity, and we’re wildly passionate and committed to this purpose. So, if you’re looking to make an immediate impact at a company that welcomes bold and diverse thinking, come join us!<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p>Are you passionate, almost obsessed, with the potential for data to transform the way Marketers do their job in the 21st century? Do you have “Digital DNA” coursing through your veins? Do you thrive on the excitement of a start-up…yet want the resources that a F500 company can provide? If so, Dun &amp; Bradstreet’s Audience Solutions group is your new home.
</p>
<p></p>
<p>We provide an innovative startup environment built on innovation, hard work and fun. We’re obsessed in providing cutting-edge advertising and marketing solutions for our clients.
</p>
<p></p>
<p>As an Account Manager within Audience Solutions, you will collaborate with the sales team in prospecting, engaging, qualifying and closing digital audience targeting solutions for some of the top marketers, agencies and publishers in the digital advertising space. Account Managers will be product experts and the role requires a high standard of communication (internal/external), multi-tasking, and strategic thinking to provide valuable information and insights to clients
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Key Responsibilities
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Collaborate with Account Coordinators on administrative and operational functions and ensure continuity of pre and post-sale product delivery
</li>
<li>Build customer relationships, acting as day-to-day point of contact during post-sales cycle by answering questions and fulfilling requests with accuracy and urgency
</li>
<li>Execute customer delivery and on-boarding processes for D&amp;B’s Audience Solutions product portfolio including fulfillment, kick-offs and aligning on KPIs; act as first line of defense with client troubleshooting and collaborate with Sales Engineering where necessary to provide solutions
</li>
<li>Manage successful product deployments by monitoring performance, ensuring reach of client’s KPIs and goals, initiating ongoing program optimization and feedback/reporting in order to proactively achieve client satisfaction and secure upsells and renewals
</li>
<li>Strategize in partnership with the Data and Technology Teams to develop new or advanced product offerings
</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Requirements
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s Degree Required along with 3+ years’ experience working in the digital advertising space; must have experience owning client relationships
</li>
<li>Demonstrated ability to achieve results both independently and as part of a team; must be self-motivated and have a great work ethic in order to be able to take initiative to get the job done
</li>
<li>Proficiency in Microsoft Office in addition to strong written, verbal and listening skills. Knowledge of SFDC preferred
</li>
<li>Excellent organizational and time management skills; must be able to multi-task, adapt, prioritize and meet deadlines in a fast-paced environment
</li>
<li>Experience with the data targeting, programmatic/digital advertising and digital campaign planning; important data skills for this role include analyzing data and showing customers the value of products through insights
</li>
</ul>
