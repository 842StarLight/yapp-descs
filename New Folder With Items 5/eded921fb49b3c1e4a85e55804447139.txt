<h2>Pricing Specialist</h2>

<p><b>Company Summary</b></p>
<p>Headquartered in Grand Rapids, Michigan since 1955, Lumbermen’s operates in Michigan, Ohio, Indiana and Kentucky<b>.</b> A leading distributor and manufacturer, we are driven by the goal to be first choice for our employee owners, customers, and suppliers. Lumbermen’s is 100% employee-owned and committed to investing in rewarding relationships and doing the right thing in every interaction.</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Position Summary</b></p>
<p>Located at our Corporate Office in Grand Rapids, MI, the Pricing Specialist maintains product pricing records for both internal and external partners. More specifically, day-to-day responsibilities include:</p>
<ul><li> Developing product knowledge</li></ul>
<ul><li> Creating and maintaining part numbers in our ERP system</li></ul>
<ul><li> Building and maintaining customer-specific list and net pricing pages</li></ul>
<ul><li> Managing pricing changes/exceptions/deviations for all divisions</li></ul>
<ul><li> Entering and maintaining dealer discounts in our ERP system</li></ul>
<ul><li> Working with purchasing team, sales leadership, and vendors to ensure data accuracy and timely updates</li></ul>
<ul><li> Generating and updating pricing for home center partners through their software programs</li></ul>
<ul><li> Assisting with material preparation and vendor commitments for annual buying show</li></ul>
<ul><li> Ensuring timely vendor payments after annual buying show</li></ul>
<ul><li> Assisting with other tasks/responsibilities/special projects as needed</li></ul>
<p></p>
<p>Ideal candidates for this position are very organized, detail-oriented, and able to deliver under tight deadlines when needed.</p>
