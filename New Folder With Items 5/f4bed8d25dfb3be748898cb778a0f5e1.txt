<p>This is an opportunity to produce video, audio, webconferencing, and other media content that help communicate the strategies,initiatives and services of an aligned IT organization at Emerson.</p>
<div></div>
<br>
<p>
The <b><i>Video &amp;</i></b> <b><i>Multimedia Co-Op</i></b> willhelp Enterprise IT deliver media content in new and creative ways. This person should understand the impact ofthe medium used, as well as the variety of distribution methods at theirdisposal.<br>

<p><b>PRINCIPAL FUNCTIONAL RESPONSIBILITIES</b></p>
<div></div>
<br>
<ul>
<li>Take complex technologies and create business-focused videos/animations (script, film, edit, and animate).</li>
<li>Develop creative ways to create and share a message using video, audio, web conferencing and animation.</li>
<li>Interview subject matter experts to extract meaningful quotes and sound bites.</li>
<li>Identify aspects of an IT project or program that would tell the best story.</li>
<li>Record, edit, and distribute video content using the Adobe Creative Cloud, Microsoft Office 365, and other tools.</li>
<li>Review content with subject matter experts and approvers.</li>
<li>Assist in making video, audio, and web conferencing a sustainable communication medium.
</li>
<li>Participate on a team of communicators across the Emerson IT organization.</li>
<li>Develop guidelines and tips for self-service multimedia creation by end-users.</li>
</ul>
