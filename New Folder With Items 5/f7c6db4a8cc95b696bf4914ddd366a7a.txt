<h2>Competitive Intelligence Consultant</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<div>
<p><b>Job Purpose:</b></p>
<p>
This position is responsible for functioning as an internal competitive intelligence (CI) consultant to support leadership on large complex projects from initial client consultation to delivery and presentation and/or managing internal programs. Responsibilities include meeting with internal customers, project management, competitor monitoring, research, analysis, presentations and reports related to competitor activities for internal HCSC stakeholders. The role will support enterprise stakeholders with research for strategic planning, as well as in-depth custom research projects, and white papers.</p>
<p><b>
Job Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul><li>Support coordination between CI and its internal customers, vendors, and associates for all projects as assigned</li></ul>
<ul><li>Prepare clear and compelling project deliverables that present work approach, results and actionable insights to multiple internal/external audiences, in presentations, reports, dashboards, emails and intranet site content</li></ul>
<ul><li>End-to-end project accountability to ensure appropriate deliverables are met. Responsible for ensuring the needs of internal customers are met from intake though the preparation and delivery of executive level presentations</li></ul>
<ul><li>Synthesize and interpret research/analysis findings aligned with project frameworks and hypotheses</li></ul>
<ul><li>Conduct competitor assessments to include strategy, products, capabilities, marketing, sales, service delivery, financials, etc.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Communicate with management and key stakeholders to inform on project deliverables, status updates and issue resolution</li></ul>
<ul><li>Oversee work of less senior resources and serve in a mentoring capacity</li></ul>
<p><b>
Job Requirements:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor Degree plus 3 years marketing research, consulting, or planning experience OR 7 years marketing research, consulting, or planning experience.</li>
<li>Knowledge of financial analysis and interpretation, survey design, market segmentation analysis, consumer research, and psychographic market research concepts.</li>
<li>Conceptual, qualitative and quantitative research, and analytic skills.</li>
<li>Project management skills, including strong organization, prioritization, problem solving and communication skills; ability to accept direction and feedback.</li>
<li>Strong verbal, written and interpersonal communication skills.</li>
<li>Flexibility and responsiveness in managing multiple projects concurrently.</li>
<li>Initiative and persistence, with ability to meet deadlines.</li>
<li>Ability to interact with staff/management across multiple departments.</li>
<li>PC proficiency to include Word, Excel, PowerPoint.</li>
<li>Presentation and group facilitation aptitude.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Preferred Job Qualifications:</b></p>
<ul><li>Experience in a position that required research and/or competitive intelligence expertise</li></ul>
<ul><li>Health insurance or healthcare industry experience</li></ul>
