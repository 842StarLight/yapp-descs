<h2>AVP - Digital Solution Lead</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>As AVP Development – AI Solution Lead for Digital Marketing Technology , responsible for strategy, analysis, design and implementation of modern, scalable, cloud native machine learning and artificial intelligence solutions for Digital Marketing initiatives. . This role primarily will lead delivering machine learning and AI solutions to deliver content search engine, content ranking engine and marketing content to target audience. . This role requires a successful and experienced digital, data and artificial intelligence practitioner, able to lead discussions with business and technology partners to identify business problems and delivering breakthrough solutions.</p>
<p>
The successful candidate will have excellent verbal and written communication skills along with a demonstrated ability to mentor and manage a digital team. You must possess a unique blend of business and technical savvy; a big-picture vision, and the drive to make a vision a reality.</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Key Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Partner with business, product and technology teams to analyze business problems, develop technology solution design, build prototypes and create new offerings.</li>
<li>Define innovative Digital Content (Similar to google search) and targeted Marketing Sales offerings applying machine learning and AI technologies</li>
<li>Evangelize and promote Cloud based micro-services architecture and Digital Technology capabilities adoption across organization</li>
<li>Lead and own technical design and development aspects of knowledge graphs, search engines, personalization engine and intelligent content engine platforms by applying digital technologies, machine learning and deep learning frameworks</li>
<li>Responsible for research, design and prototype robust and scalable target audience models applying machine learning, AI, data mining, and statistical</li>
<li>Experience in event data analysis , sentiment analysis , modelling to deliver targeted digital content and send real time marketing campaigns.</li>
<li>Manage onsite and offshore development teams implementing products and platforms in Agile</li>
<li>Collaborate with business, product , enterprise architecture and cross-functional teams to ensure strategic and tactical goals of project efforts are met</li>
<li>Work collaboratively with QA, DevOPS teams to adopt CI/CD tool chain and develop automation</li>
<li>Work with technical teams to ensure overall support and stability of platforms and assist with troubleshooting when production incidents arise</li>
<li>Be a mentor and leader on the team and within the organization</li>
</ul>
