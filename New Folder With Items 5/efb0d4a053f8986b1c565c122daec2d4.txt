<h2>Retention Marketing Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>This is a newly created position to support our Agriculture Division! Eligible for benefits day 1! 401K match $ for $ up to 6%!</p>
<p></p>
<p>Summary DTN is looking for a seasoned professional to fill the role of Retention Marketing Manager. This role will be responsible for executing effective marketing strategies and programs to effectively retain existing customers in a recurring Saas model.</p>
<p>This position will report to the Agriculture Marketing Director and will also partner with the Customer Success team. The successful candidate will play an integral role in engaging DTN’s customer base within the Agriculture business. Data analysis and ability to see trends will be a must.</p>
<p></p>
<p>Selected candidates will possess the following experience:</p>
<p></p>
<p>Essential Functions</p>
 <ul><li>Develop marketing strategies for retaining existing customers</li></ul>
<ul><li>Drive customer loyalty and retention efforts for DTN in the Agriculture industry</li></ul>
 <ul><li>Promote and drive DTN’s vision to increase retention of existing customers</li></ul>
 <ul><li>Build a marketing plan and gain buy-in from strategic partners.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Organize and execute marketing programs that can include digital email campaigns, on-line advertising, conferences, seminars, webinars, and content syndication, in-app messaging; adoption messaging during on-boarding</li></ul>
 <ul><li>Monitor programs and analyze results to determine what’s working and adapt what isn’t working</li></ul>
 <ul><li>Work closely with the Customer Success team on program development and implementation</li></ul>
 <p>• Create and execute programs • Develop and communicate strategies, plans, results and recommendations to leadership teams • Successful candidate will need to have a high proficiency to understand trends captured by your data analysis.</p>
<p></p>
<p>Preferred Qualifications</p>
<ul><li>Bachelor’s Degree in Marketing or related discipline</li></ul>
 <ul><li>7+ years’ experience in a product marketing role, or demand generation, preferably with software or SaaS</li></ul>
 <ul><li>Strong organization and prioritization skills</li></ul>
<ul><li>Ability to work well to prioritize and multi-task in a fast-paced, collaborative, deadline driven environment</li></ul>
 <p>• Marketing automation, prefer experience with Eloqua and Salesforce • Excellent analytical, communication and presentation skills</p>
 <ul><li>Proven ability to create, lead and execute complex initiatives</li></ul>
 <ul><li>Strong logical, analytical and creative problem-solving skills</li></ul>
 <ul><li>Self-starter with a proactive, results-oriented focus with a desire to assume additional responsibilities</li></ul>
 <ul><li>Excellent written and oral communication skills required</li></ul>
<ul><li>Ability to travel up to 30% of the time</li></ul>
<ul><li>Experience with complex enterprise sales cycles and demonstrating marketing’s impact on the business thru business models, dashboards and data</li></ul>
<p></p>
