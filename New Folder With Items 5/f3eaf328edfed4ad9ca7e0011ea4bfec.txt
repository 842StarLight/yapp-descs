<h2>Project Manager IT - Sales Enablement</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p>As the Project Manager supporting Sales Systems projects, you will be responsible for managing a related set of projects with low to moderate complexity within the Sales Enablement Project Portfolio. This role reports to a Sr. Program Manager. Takes projects from original concept through final implementation. Defines project scope and objectives. Develops detail work plans, schedules, project estimates, resource plans, and status report. Conducts project meetings and is responsible for project tracking and analysis. Ensures adherence to quality standards and reviews project deliverables. Manages the integration of vendor tasks and tracks and reviews vendor deliverables. Provides technical and analytical guidance to project team. Recommends and takes action to direct the analysis and solutions of problems. Incumbent may also have expertise on specific technologies. Frequently reports to a Project Planning Manager/Director or Executive.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Essential Duties and Responsibilities</b> include the following. Other duties may be assigned:</p>
<ul>
<li>Provides project direction and management to a team of resources in order to accomplish common goals defined by the project scope and objectives</li>
<li>Provides project management services and support</li>
<li>Provides input in the development of project management protocols, procedures and guidelines</li>
<li>Provides implementation services including, but not limited to: project management, rollout support, and operational planning. Acts as point of escalation for team members in providing these services.</li>
<li>Develops and maintains project plans to continuously manage project scope, activities, schedules, deadlines, resources and cost/time estimates</li>
<li>Completes project status reports and escalates issues and resource needs to management</li>
<li>Effectively manages within budget constraints, closely monitors and promptly reports on budgetary, resource, or scope issues via the change control process</li>
<li>Serves as a resource for other staff members</li>
<li>Handles multi-project interactions, interfaces, assessments and related decisions while considering project priorities based on resources and funds.</li>
<li>Handles multiple projects and deliverables</li>
<li>Manages vendor resources, resource demand and project funding</li>
<li>Ensures appropriate stakeholders are engaged and committed during the project life cycle</li>
<li>Demonstrates the ability to maintain professionalism and a positive attitude when encountering changes in the workplace</li>
</ul>
