<h2>Partner Program Product Manager – Business Applications</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div></div>
<div>
<div>The team is seeking a strong senior leader to define the business case and opportunity, strategy and value proposition/positioning of our partner offerings and the corresponding infrastructure, aligned to Business Applications, SMB and partner attribution thinking for partner programs.
</div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities</b></h2>
<ul>
<li>
Frame and prioritize market opportunities, analyzing competitive advantages and influence our program plans for Business Applications and SMB</li>
<li>
Define partner overall value proposition, strategy and positioning across solution offerings and design our program accordingly</li>
<li>
Work with OCP Partner Attribution lead on program implications</li>
<li>
Working with multi discipline stakeholders and ability to influence through storytelling or synthesize complex ideas into understandable propositions</li>
<li>
Manage Business Applications and SMB program offers through entire lifecycle, including release, maintenance and retirement</li>
<li>
Work closely with peers in One Commercial Partner (OCP), Business Applications and SMB on ensuring cross Microsoft consistency in our programmatic approach
</li>
<li>Leverage listening systems (self-initiated research on market conditions/field/analysts/partner councils/customers/ formal customer and partner research) to inform strategic proposals</li>
<li>
Own program design, working with Partner Experience Engineering (PX engineering) and our stakeholders on infrastructure
</li>
<li>Contributing to cross team governance to discuss, define and drive decisions regarding program strategy and execution (Director/General Manager), and (Corporate Vice President) cadence as needed.
</li>
<li>Represent Programs GM, Sr Strategy Director and Director in executive forums.
</li>
</ul>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Qualifications</b></h2>
<p></p>
<br>
<ul>
<li>
Product management experience</li>
<li>
Understanding of the partner ecosystem
</li>
<li>Understanding of Dynamics product suite and partner proposition</li>
<li>
Analytical skills</li>
<li>
Understanding of Microsoft’s SMB strategy and offerings and the partner proposition for them</li>
<li>
Excellence in execution from strategy through to experience launch. attention to detail, self-discipline and self-motivation
</li>
<li>Strong cross group collaboration and communication skills
</li>
<li>Business and strategy leadership with excellence in technology planning and marketing
</li>
<li>Highly developed quantitative and analytical skills informing strategic recommendations</li>
<li>
Understanding of business metrics, financial analytics and proficiency with financial calculations for business modeling (in partnership with OCP and Business Applications finance teams)
</li>
<li>Knowledge of Business Applications and SMB business models
</li>
<li>Experience in a complex multi-team environment driving agile business strategy
</li>
<li>Razor-sharp intellect, coupled with strong problem solving, judgment, decision making and strategic capabilities
</li>
<li>Negotiation skills a plus
</li>
<li>Familiarity with Microsoft’s software development life cycle.</li>
</ul>
