<br>
<div>Do you have a passion for the automotive industry? Are you an expert in how car companies and their partners are using technology revolutionize their industry? Do you thrive in a cross-functional environment, working closely with some of the best and brightest in the world?<br>
<br>
As the BD Enterprise Solutions Leader for Automotive, you will have an exciting opportunity to shape and deliver on a global strategy to help the industry ecosystem transform. Your responsibilities will be to develop, implement, and manage technical solution packages for go-to-market growth programs, drive sales enablement, and work with field teams to accelerate customer adoption, measure results, and evolve programs to meet AWS’ customer needs effectively.<br>
<br>
The right candidate will be a passionate technologist, have broad and deep automotive technology experience, and demonstrated go-to-market success. They will have a proven track record of leading complex solutions programs, strong data and metrics bias, a good understanding of industry ecosystems, and a passion for helping them transform using cloud technologies. They will have specific experience and knowledge of the operations, customer service, and IT processes in the largest automotive firms. They will also have deep working knowledge of cloud computing and the related applications across industries.<br>
<br>
Role &amp; Responsibilities<br>
<ul>
<li>Working backwards from customers, identify solutions themes, portfolio of solution(s) for each solution theme, and AWS value proposition(s) for the Automotive markets.</li>
<li>Together with the broader team, develop the technical side of the strategic plan for AWS, to engage customers and partners in this industry.</li>
<li>Develop go-to-market plan for each solution area; including leveraging AWS field teams, technology and consulting partner channels, and self-service.</li>
<li>Establish technical credibilit0.y in sales cycles (personally or with the broader technical team), and credibly drive discussions with CAOs, CCO’s, CTOs, CIOs, Chief Architects, Senior Developers, etc. from both customers and partners.</li>
<li>Collaborate with the technical teams to realize solutions, including reference architectures, patterns, automation for one-click deployment, release plan, etc.</li>
<li>Deploy – pilot and scale – solutions-led growth programs in the field working with sales enablement, regional business development teams, and account teams.</li>
<li>Manage solutions-led growth programs, assess performance, pivot and evolve programs based on observed data to improve programs efficacy and performance.</li>
</ul>
