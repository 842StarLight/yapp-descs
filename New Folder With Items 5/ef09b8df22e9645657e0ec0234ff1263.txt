<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
The Big Picture:</b></h2>
<div>
Mortenson's Sports + Entertainment Group is seeking a Marketing Manager to work with the business development team, marketing coordinators, and construction teams to create compelling responses to RFP’s to meet customer requirements and differentiate Mortenson from competitors; provide oversight of the entire proposal process including coordinating scheduling, assigning marketing resources, monitoring work-flow, and producing and approving final proposal documents.</div>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>What will you be doing?</b></h2>
<div></div>
<br>
<div><b>
Supervision</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
Collaborate with supervisor (Senior Marketing Manager) and marketing team to manage 2-3 specific deadline-driven proposals at any given time</li>
<li>
Facilitate interaction between business development, marketing coordinators, and operations group executives to guarantee the timeliness and quality of each stage of proposal preparation from creation to completion</li>
<li>
Assure that the workloads of marketing coordinators are properly balanced</li>
<li>
Identify process issues affecting the completion of assigned tasks</li>
<li>
Resolve and/or escalate issues related to gaps or errors in content, timeliness, or other potential issues which could compromise the final product</li>
<li>
Supervise the creation, development and production of effective proposals, presentations, and other marketing materials in order to provide a competitive advantage leading to the acquisition of new business.</li>
<li>
Oversee 3rd party public relations and communications consultant.</li>
<li>
Supervise operating group social media accounts and coordinate with Corporate Strategic Marketing team.</li>
</ul>
<div><b>
Proposal Management</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
Assume ownership of content management</li>
<li>
Facilitate meetings with marketing coordinators, business development, Design Phase managers, construction executives and operations group leaders to develop proposals and graphics that address target audience requirements and hot buttons</li>
<li>
Manage the change control process</li>
<li>
Ensure that all proposals use defined documentation methods, processes and tools that meet the highest quality standards</li>
<li>
Act as the principle editor on all proposals</li>
<li>
Develop and implement a structured review process of all proposal efforts</li>
<li>
Establish and closely monitor project timelines to ensure that completed proposals meet scheduling deadlines and stay within budget</li>
<li>
Create and maintain a proposal library for historical reference purposes. Library will include electronic and hard copies of completed proposals</li>
<li>
Organize and maintain past proposal templates in searchable categories to aide in future proposal activity</li>
</ul>
<div><b>
Strategy</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
Guide marketing team in developing and refining a strategy for the proposal process and content, which involves acting as a consultant/facilitator from pre-proposal to presentation stages</li>
<li>
Apply targeted marketing and sales principles to the proposal process in order to customize creative solutions which meet or exceed customer expectations</li>
<li>
Participate/collaborate in overall marketing strategy development and implementation</li>
<li>
Work with business development team to create marketing content for targeted customers and projects well in advance of RFP issuance.</li>
<li>
Perform market research and data analysis to track current market conditions and trends.</li>
</ul>
<div><b>
Sales Enablement through content</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
Mapping customer decision-making paths (in partnership with strategic marketing)</li>
<li>
Developing customer personas/profiles for key segments</li>
</ul>
<div><ul><li>
Identifying the best tactics to achieve desired position with each segment</li></ul></div>
<ul><li>
Partnering with manager to develop cohesive integrated marketing programs/budget to achieve business goals</li></ul>
<ul><li>Including: public relations, website, feature articles, social media, videos, advertising and other mediums</li></ul>
<ul><li>
Measuring results of programs, documenting learning, and driving continuous improvement</li></ul>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
<b>
 </b><b>Other marketing activities</b>
</div>
<ul>
<li>
Identify and coordinate new business development opportunities such as trade shows, speaking opportunities, etc.</li>
<li>
Lead content marketing by nurturing leads through every step of their buying journey – with insights-driven content, engagement events, newsletters, and other activities</li>
<li>
Monitor progress of leads and hand over to business development group at appropriate points in the buying journey</li>
<li>
Create communications to augment customer interaction and events including exhibitions, customer meetings, print collateral, advertising, publicity, awards, website content, social media, email and other channels as needed</li>
<li>
Oversee the implementation of day-to-day marketing efforts including marketing materials development, distribution of press releases, award submittals, customer events, and other marketing activities</li>
<li>
Misc. projects as assigned</li>
</ul>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Qualifications
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>How will you succeed?</b></h2>
<ul>
<li>
Detailed–oriented with exceptional writing and communication skills</li>
<li>
Must be capable of delivering comprehensive proposals by drawing segmented information from construction, operations, and leadership teams and translating the information into solution- based language tailored to each client</li>
<li>
Possess excellent verbal skills and be able to interact with clients, marketing team, and senior management</li>
<li>
Demonstrated ability to adhere to deadlines and identify obstacles which would impact timeliness and quality</li>
<li>
Proficiency at scheduling project assignments and monitoring work flow to optimize results is strongly desired</li>
<li>
Team player with a positive attitude</li>
<li>
Proficiency in Word, Excel, Power Point and InDesign also required</li>
</ul>
<div>
<b>
Education and Experience Requirements</b><b>:</b>
</div>
<ul>
<li>
Bachelor's degree in Marketing, Journalism, English, or Communications strongly preferred</li>
<li>
Minimum 7 years of proposal development and/or marketing management experience</li>
</ul>
