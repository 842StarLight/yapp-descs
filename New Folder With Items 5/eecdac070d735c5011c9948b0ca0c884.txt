<div><b>WHO WE ARE
</b></div>
<div>Looker is on a mission to bring better insights and data-driven decisions to every business. Everything we do is aimed at making sure our customers love every aspect of Looker, from our products and technologies to our ease of doing business and our support. We are looking for curiously brilliant individuals to join our team as we reinvent data analytics. Get data-driven and see yourself at Looker.
</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div><b>WHAT WE'VE GOT GOING ON
</b></div>
<div>The Manager of Sales Engineering will provide direct leadership to our West Coast Pre-Sales team supporting our small and medium business segments. In this role, you will own all aspects of building and managing a scalable, world-class team of Sales Engineers, providing leadership and guidance in SaaS technology, sales strategy, relationship building, enterprise solution development and trial execution / conversion.
</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>The Sales Engineering team within Looker serves as a primary point of contact to drive the successful introduction, adoption and broad usage of Looker’s products within the customer and prospect base. Activities of the team include product demos, technical qualification, identification of customer business / data analysis use cases, trial development and delivery, definition of appropriate business and technical solutions, customer management, account development, technology evangelizing, and business consulting. Key metrics for the team currently include customer activation, adoption, usage rates and trial conversions.</div>
