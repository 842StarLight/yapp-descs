<h2>E-Commerce Product Management Intern</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">As a member of the Product Management team specializing in the component product line and its e-commerce presentation at Logic Supply, you will be focused primarily on global product introduction. You are responsible for selecting and qualifying the products we carry in support of our embedded computer lines, including coordinating testing and ensuring launches on our website. You work with the Product Management and Engineering teams, as well as our suppliers to stay up to date on cutting edge products along with supporting the Sales team on customer projects. We are looking for an intern to work 15-20 hours per week during the fall semester.
<br><br>
<b>This is how you approach your day. You:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Support global product launches and product on boarding for Logic Supply’s website by * Coordinating the enablement of product pages on our global websites</li>
<li>Executing the introduction and testing of new products.</li>
<li>Collaborating with Product Management, Engineering, Marketing, Sales and other key teams to create and maintain product pages, manuals, specification sheets, images, drivers and other collateral.</li>
<li>Coordinate third party partnerships including product listings, software certifications, and partner portal management</li>
<li>Develop and maintain a thorough, high level knowledge of the company’s core products and their applications.</li>
<li>Contact suppliers and manufacturers to get the latest technical information on products and services.</li>
</ul>
<b>Who we are looking for:</b><br>
We are looking for someone who can take on multiple time sensitive projects including delivering product milestones on time and responding to inquiries from Sales and Marketing team members. The ideal candidate is able to adapt to rapidly changing and dynamic responsibilities. You must have a passion for computer knowledge, website management and problem solving.
<br><br>
This is you!
<br><ul>
<li>You are currently enrolled as a junior or senior in a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Product Management, Engineering or related fields.</li>
<li>You have a passion for computer hardware and related market trends that keep yourself at the forefront of new technology.</li>
<li>You have a strong technical knowledge of computers, motherboards, processors, chips and other related components.</li>
<li>You have knowledge of current software applications and basic website management skills (HTML)</li>
<li>You have attention to detail and a focus on quality. Must be organized, with excellent analytical and problem solving skills.</li>
<li>You have the superior ability to manage time, shifting priorities and ability to effectively communicate project milestones or changes across cross functional, global teams</li>
<li>You have excellent written and verbal communication skills.</li>
<li>You have the desire to learn business processes, strategy and “big picture” thinking</li>
</ul>
