<h2>Sr Customer Success Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Do you want to drive that next wave of convenience, comfort and security for consumers? How about enabling businesses to run more efficiently while servicing their customer needs better? Maybe you want to help redefine business models for service providers and manufacturers? Come join our Customer Success team, as we help large brands and enterprises transform their business with the Internet Of Things.</p>
<p>
Ayla Networks is redefining the way brands, enterprises and service providers can develop, monetize and support connected products for the Internet of Things (IoT) in a way that’s open, scalable and economical. Ayla provides the industry’s first Agile IoT Platform, an end-to-end software fabric running across devices, cloud, and apps to create secure connectivity, data analytics, and feature-rich customer experiences.</p>
<p>
As a Customer Success Manager, you will be a key member of our fast growing Customer Success Team. You will be responsible for managing multiple customers ranging from marquee global brands to innovative upstarts to help them successfully launch amazing connected products. The ideal candidate thrives in a fast paced environment and is passionate about being a customer advocate and a trusted strategic advisor by constantly acquiring and upgrading the required skills and domain knowledge. You build strong and long lasting relationships with your customers and your efforts will drive customer satisfaction and happiness.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
What You Will Be Doing</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Own the post-Sales Customer Relationship: Build strong, trusted advisor relationships with key customer contacts to deliver an exceptional Ayla experience</li>
<li>
Manage multiple client relationships with a focus on quality of relationships, adoption, retention and satisfaction.</li>
<li>
Project Manage/Lead technical customer implementations and go to market initiatives</li>
<li>
Work closely with Ayla’s Product, Sales, and Professional Services teams to quickly onboard customers, manage projects, and surface risks before they become real problems</li>
<li>
Drive continued product engagement, including contract renewal and up-sell opportunities through trusted advisor relationships</li>
<li>
Proactively identify and lead team process improvements</li>
<li>
Strategically incorporate customer feedback into the Ayla platform by collaborating with Ayla product management team</li>
<li>
Translate and document customer facing material as needed to achieve business objectives</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Desired Skills and Experience</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
The ideal candidate will have 5+ years experience in a customer facing post-sales role, ideally providing professional services or customer success functions in a technology-related field such as SaaS, PaaS, Mobile, Security or IoT</li>
<li>
Proven track record of managing customer engagements across multiple stakeholders.</li>
<li>
Demonstrated ability to understand technical concepts/solutions, customer use cases and guide strategy to deliver positive results for customers</li>
<li>
Self-starter who thrives under ambiguity in an extremely fast-paced, deadline oriented startup environment</li>
<li>
Excellent verbal and written communication skills</li>
<li>
Creative, resourceful, detail-oriented, and highly organized</li>
<li>
Preferred physical locations are in Northeast US</li>
<li>
Applications/Tools (Optional but nice to have): Experience working with SalesForce CRM and Customer Success software services</li>
<li>
B.S./M.S. degree in a related field</li>
</ul>
