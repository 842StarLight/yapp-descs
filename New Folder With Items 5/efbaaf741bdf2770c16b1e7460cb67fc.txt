<h2>Solution Architect</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>InTouch Health is a leading telehealth solution company enabling remote physician care anytime, anywhere. Our solutions are robust, secure, and provide excellent physician-patient experiences to maximize care and minimize response time, and ensure the right doctor makes the right treatment decision every time.</p>
<p>Under the general direction of the VP, Solution Analysis &amp; Design, the Solution Architect is responsible for technical design and solution validation efforts for pre-sales activity. He/she will work closely with the sales organization to establish sales approaches, present technical presentations to prospects, customers, and company associates.</p>
<p><b>Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Prepare and deliver technical presentations explaining products or services to customers and prospective customers</li>
<li>Confer with customers to assess solution needs and to determine system requirements</li>
<li>Collaborate with sales teams to understand customer requirements and provide sales support</li>
<li>Prepares cost estimates by studying solution plans and related customer documents.</li>
<li>Determines improvements by analyzing cost-benefit ratios of equipment, supplies, or service applications in customer environment; engineering or proposing changes in equipment, processes, or use of materials or services.</li>
<li>Gains customer acceptance by explaining or demonstrating solution benefits and operations improvements.</li>
<li>Prepares sales engineering reports and comprehensive statements of work by collecting, analyzing, and summarizing sales information and engineering and application trends.</li>
<li>Contributes to sales engineering effectiveness by identifying short-term and long-range issues that must be addressed; providing information and commentary pertinent to deliberations; recommending options and courses of action; implementing directives.</li>
<li>Participate in industry events/organizations as thought leader and promote products/business solutions at conferences, forums and seminars.</li>
<li>Work with cross-functional team members to align the Solution Design strategy across the company</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Knowledge, Skills &amp; Ability</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Selling to Customer Needs and understanding their software requirements</li>
<li>Strong presentation skills with the ability to quickly comprehend and explain complex engineering concepts in simple, effective terms.</li>
<li>Successfully tailor demonstrations for customers, trade shows, and special events and work closely with the customer to set up and maintain a successful demo period, being available to answer questions and trouble-shoot as needed.</li>
<li>Demonstrated success working with Healthcare organizations</li>
<li>Results oriented with demonstrable achievement of success</li>
<li>Relentless desire to delight customers and partners</li>
<li>Ability to influence technical decision makers and executives through technical presentations and business case creation</li>
<li>Strong work ethic and result-oriented</li>
<li>Ability to multi-task and work in fast paced environment and function through ambiguity</li>
<li>Works well with team members and cross-departmentally</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Qualifications</b></p>
<ul>
<li>A Bachelor's degree from a four-year college or university (preferred)</li>
<li>5+ years prior customer-facing presentation experience (required)</li>
<li>5+ years working in the healthcare space (required)</li>
<li>Disciplined organizational skills</li>
<li>Proficiency with Microsoft Office</li>
<li>Proficiency with tools to create comprehensive Statements of Work and Solution Designs</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Working Conditions</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Normal office environment</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Notes</b></p>
<p>For the qualified candidate, InTouch Health offers competitive compensation, medical, dental and vision benefits, paid time off, 401(K) and stock options. InTouch Health is based in sunny and beautiful Santa Barbara, California.</p>
