<h2>RFP Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>RFP Specialist-RFP0001Z</p>
<div>
Description<div>
</div>
<div>
Using a strong project management discipline and positive, flexible approach, the Request for Proposal (RFP) Strategist coordinates the completion of RFP responses that accurately and favorably present Prudential Retirement’s products and services to specific client markets, including union, tax-exempt, and corporate. The RFP Strategist is responsible for directing the strategic and custom positioning of RFP responses by actively participating in and facilitating strategy sessions with sales team to better understand the prospect’s needs; how we want to differentiate products and services and communicating the key project milestone dates to the team. Focus on and achievement of all milestone dates to deliver RFP responses on time and accurately is crucial. The expectation is that this individual will be adaptable to changes in process and seek out further process improvements. The RFP Strategist continually seeks to enhance RFP content through continuous outreach to subject matter experts and sharing knowledge gained with the rest of the RFP team. This individual will be a team player, willing to help their peers, and be a strong advocate for the team both within the department and externally.<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<div>Job Responsibilities:</div>
<ul>
<li>
Complete multiple assignments (RFPs, RFIs, proposals, security questionnaires), occurring concurrently, accurately and within established timeframes/milestones</li>
<li>
Effectively conduct productive strategy sessions with sales team to gain understanding of prospect and how to best position the RFP response, with respect to the client’s industry</li>
<li>
Will come to all strategy sessions with value-add information on the prospect and/or information on our book of business that is similar to the prospect</li>
<li>
Provide accurate RFP drafts to appropriate partners by established milestone date</li>
<li>
Develop and maintain effective partnerships with members of the selling team and various subject matter experts
</li>
<li>Demonstrates sense of urgency with all projects and flexibility in responding to last-minute changes</li>
<li>
Proactively learn Prudential’s products and services to be able to represent them effectively and in a persuasive RFP style</li>
<li>
Show creativity as needed to customize responses to address specific questions with the understanding that our primary source of content is the RFP database</li>
<li>
Partner and share knowledge with other members of the RFP team</li>
<li>
Partner effectively with the team’s Production Specialists to ensure all final files are submitted for printing</li>
<li>
Verify accurate licensing and pricing approval on all projects prior to production</li>
<li>
Update the team’s workflow tracking database (Salesforce) to reflect project status<br>
</li>
</ul>
</div>
<div>Qualifications<div>
</div>
<ul>
<li>
Ideal candidates will have a minimum of 3 years Retirement and/or Financial Services experience, and experience in RFP response development</li>
<li>
Retirement plan knowledge, particularly with tax-exempt, Taft-Hartley, and corporate plans</li>
<li>
Demonstrated project management skills with the ability to consistently exceed deliverables in a high-volume production environment; must be able to manage workflow and handle multiple projects simultaneously</li>
<li>
BA or BS degree or equivalent, relevant work experience</li>
<li>
FINRA Series 6 licensed or ability to obtain within 120 days of hire</li>
<li>
Excellent interpersonal skills, written and verbal skills, attention to detail; excellent copy editing, grammar and punctuation skills are essential</li>
<li>
Must possess ability to work independently and within the team to meet deadlines and prepare responses with a high degree of accuracy</li>
<li>
Must be highly organized, detail-oriented, proactive, resourceful, and able to exercise sound judgment</li>
<li>
Intermediate to advanced level of proficiency in MS Word and Excel</li>
<li>
Experience with Qvidian RFP Product Suite or other RFP development software programs, as well as Salesforce.com, is a plus</li>
</ul>
