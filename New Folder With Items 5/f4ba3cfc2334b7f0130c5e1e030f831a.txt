<h2>Sr. Solutions Architect</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Comcast Business offers technology solutions ranging from Ethernet, internet, and WiFi connectivity to voice, television, and managed Enterprise solutions to power businesses of all sizes to perform better. From small businesses to mid-market and large Enterprise organizations, Comcast Business serves business customers across the country. Powered by an advanced, Gig-speed network and backed by 24/7 technical support, Comcast Business is one of the largest contributors to the growth of Comcast Cable. The organization is the nation's largest cable provider to small and mid-size businesses and has emerged as a force in the Enterprise market, recognized by leading industry associations as one of the fastest growing provider of Ethernet services.</p>
<br>
<br>
<br>
<p><b>Summary:</b></p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
Responsible for providing subject matter expertise on the design and implementation of complex Wide Area Data Networks, Network Function Virtualization, and SD-WAN solutions for business class customers across a national footprint. Works as a collaborative member of the sales team to market and sell the Company's product lines. Provides insight to customers on current network solutions. Works closely with account executives on presentations and the technical design of new sales. Integrates knowledge of business and functional priorities. Acts as a key contributor in a complex and crucial environment. May lead teams or projects and shares expertise.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Core Responsibilities:</b></p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
Provide timely, technical support and problem resolution for field service questions on a national level.</p>
<ul>
<li>Assist the Account Management Team and Sales leadership in the development of the overall business strategy to acquire new customers. Contributes to costing and pricing of Complex SD-WAN solutions.</li>
<li>Assist in generating new business and ensure growth of existing accounts.</li>
<li>Design Wide Area Network solutions for the customer that meets their business application needs, optimizes cost, provides disaster recovery situations, and appropriate bandwidth for future growth.</li>
<li>Translate complex technical design requirements into information that customers can understand and respond to.</li>
<li>Serve as a national technical expert to review RFP responses for content and accuracy before they are submitted.</li>
<li>Provide technical support for National events and technical trade shows.</li>
<li>Participate in technology transfer training activities in the Sales Engineering group as new technologies are introduced across the Comcast Business product line.</li>
<li>Develop, deliver and run effective presentations, meetings, client interviews and facilitate a group workshop.</li>
<li>Work with Comcast Business Network Engineering, Product Engineering, Product and the national engineering organization to acquire an intimate knowledge of all Comcast Business products concepts, process and procedures.</li>
<li>Keep up to date on technological and market evolution as well as competitors’ activities.</li>
<li>Consistent exercise of independent judgment and discretion in matters of significance.</li>
<li>Regular, consistent and punctual attendance. Must be able to travel and work nights and weekends, variable schedule(s) as necessary.</li>
<li>Most be a subject matter expert of the following Comcast Data Products: Ethernet Dedicated Internet, Ethernet Network Service, Ethernet Private Line, Ethernet Virtual Private Line, ActiveCore SD-WAN</li>
<li>Obtain customer network details needed to configure and provision service</li>
<li>Develop action list actions needed to be taken by customer to successfully implement service</li>
<li>Other duties and responsibilities as assigned.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>

Years Experience-Generally requires 10+ years related experience with 2 or more customer facing</p>
