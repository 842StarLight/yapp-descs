<h2>Order Processing Coordinator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<b>Description:</b><br>
JOB PURPOSE
<br>The Order Processing Coordinator is responsible for processing orders by creating a base shell. This role will include working closely with other departments to identify orders with issues or lacking documentation that may need to be updated before they can be accepted and processed in a timely fashion.
<br><br>
DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES
<br><ul>
<li>Responsible to enter and process all orders.</li>
<li>Responsible to assign orders accurately to the correct resources i.e. factories, Project Managers, Installation Coordinators, Sales Reps per Fabtex guidelines.</li>
<li>Able to compare documents to verify accurate pricing.</li>
<li>Ability to work with other departments to request additional information if needed.</li>
<li>Create and send acknowledgement letters on orders daily.</li>
<li>Able to work in fast paced environment to meet specific deadlines.</li>
<li>Back-up for order entry when needed.</li>
</ul>
<b>Requirements:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Excellent knowledge of functional area(s) related to the job.</li>
<li>Working knowledge of computers and software programs such as MS Office or other programs specific to the job.</li>
<li>Excellent oral and written communication skills to communicate effectively.</li>
<li>Strong organizational skills with ability to manage deadlines and prioritize workload and make adjustment to meet business needs.</li>
</ul>
</div>
