<h2>External Senior Strategic Relationship Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>External Senior Strategic Relationship Manager-STR000A4</p>
<div>
Description<div></div>
<br>
<div>
</div>
<div>
The External Senior Strategic Relationship Management (Sr. SRM) role will be responsible for the overall management of the annuity and life insurance relationship between Prudential and the assigned national account(s). The Sr. SRM will be accountable for creating market share for Prudential and the deepening of the relationship between Prudential and account(s).</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
The Senior External SRM will work closely with the account(s’) leadership team to develop a strategic plan that aligns the business interests of Prudential and account(s) to grow revenue. The Senior External SRM will partner with the entire Annuity and Life sales organization and the functional leadership within the Prudential home office to deliver high quality sales, service and marketing support to the account(s).</div>
<div>
<br>
<br>
</div>
<div>This position reports to the Vice President, SRM Bank/Wire Team.</div>
