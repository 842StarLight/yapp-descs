The team
<br>Our Marketing team creates world-class content and data-driven marketing solutions that communicates the value of DocuSign's Agreement Cloud to reach more customers and help them understand the potential impact DocuSign can have on their business. We design creative for a new business campaign. Refine messaging around new product launches. Organize captivating customer events. Develop innovative social media campaigns. <br><br>
This position
<br>DocuSign's vision is to help companies be more agree-able by providing the leading apps and platform technologies for modernizing their systems of agreement. We call this the DocuSign Agreement Cloud. When companies connect the Agreement Cloud with their existing systems of record, magic happens. And that's particularly true when that system of record is Salesforce.
<br><br>
As the Senior Partner Marketing Manager, Salesforce, you will lead and engage on messaging, demand generation, content, and enablement efforts for this important partnership. Reporting to the Senior Director, Marketing -- DocuSign Agreement Cloud for Salesforce, you will work closely with cross-functional teams including Demand Generation, Integrated Marketing, Business Development, and Audience &amp; Product Marketing, as well as with your counterparts at Salesforce to accelerate and grow this business.
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Responsibilities</li>
</ul>
Create compelling messaging that portrays how the DocuSign Agreement Cloud fits within the Salesforce ecosystem
<br><ul>

<li>Partner with DocuSign audience marketing, demand generation &amp; integrated marketing and Salesforce to drive SQOs through joint lead gen initiatives for priority segments and industries</li>
<li>Optimize our holistic presence on the AppExchange to ensure it's an effective lead gen source, including owning DocuSign's AppExchange Marketing Programs (AMP) and revamp our nurture sequences, and partnering with product marketers to define appropriate objectives for each product</li>
