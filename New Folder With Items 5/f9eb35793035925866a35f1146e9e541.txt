GENERAL SUMMARY</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
Assist in the development, day to day maintenance, and accurate revisions of digital media plans for Lexus National and the Lexus Dealer Association. This requires strong organizational skills with a high attention to detail/accuracy due to depth and breadth of client's media commitments. The person in this role must effectively communicate the status of projects to Media Planner (MP)/ Media Supervisor (MS).</div>
