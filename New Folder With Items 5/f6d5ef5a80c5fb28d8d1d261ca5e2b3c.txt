<h2>Director of Marketing</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Flight Fit N Fun is: </b> An indoor adventure park for all ages featuring trampolines, ninja warrior courses, dodgeball, climbing structures, laser mazes, ice skating, virtual reality games, and more – all under one roof.</p>
<p><b>Ideal Candidate: </b> A thoughtful leader who is detail-oriented and possesses excellent organizational, communication and interpersonal skills and abilities. Responsible for planning, development and implementation of all the organization’s marketing strategies and communication. Leads and/or oversees the promotions, programming, website, social, ad campaigns of the business.</p>
<p>This position serves as a member of the senior management team.</p>
<p><b>Key Responsibilities: </b></p>
<p>Responsible for creating, communicating and implementing a comprehensive marketing strategy that will enhance the organization’s image and position through…</p>
<ul>
<li>Promotion: Develop, publicize, and execute marketing incentives meant to increase sales for the brand.</li>
<li>Programming: Lead and execute on suitable strategies and objectives that are in congruence with Flight Fit N Fun’s organizational strategy.</li>
<li>Website: Responsible for all online operations and analysis.</li>
<li>Digital: Leads the deliver and delivery of digital marketing programs across digital channels, including email, CRM, SEM, Social and Web.</li>
<li>Ad campaigns: Lead and manage all platform and agency relationships, including Facebook, Google and other channels.</li>
</ul>
<p>Serves as a subject matter expert for the Operations team, both providing strategic direction and support of all stages of marketing.</p>
<p>Responsible for direction, production and distribution of all organizational design materials (e.g. – logo, brand colors, letterhead, etc..), including digital media (e.g. LinkedIn, Facebook, Twitter, Instagram, etc.).</p>
<p>Ensure effective, branded marketing communications, including the company website, print communication and advertising.</p>
<p>Ensure the Flight Fit N Fun regularly conducts relevant market research and coordinate and oversee this activity. Monitor trends.</p>
<p>Develops, then partners with operations on execution of weekly/monthly/annual brand marketing strategies and monitors the performance of those strategies in order to make necessary update and improvements.</p>
<p>Responsible for the quality of the content, updating information layouts and functionality of the website.</p>
<p>Provide ongoing education on opportunities in social media, including industry trends, platform changes and creative opportunities to reach and engage with guests.</p>
