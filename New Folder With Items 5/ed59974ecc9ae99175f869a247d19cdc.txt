<h2>Independent Carquest Store Project Manager - Dallas</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">Job Description
<br><br>
The Independent Project Manager will oversee all aspects of Independent Carquest new store projects, while helping to define and document implementation processes. This role will receive guidance and direction from the Independent Sales Director and Account Executive to ensure the project is completed on time and within budget. The Project Manager will supervise and coordinate the activities required to effectively relocate or remodel an existing store location or open a new store. There are two key functions:
<br><br>
<b>Primary Role:</b> Manage competitive changeovers, acquisitions and green fields, as well as assist in store remodel / relocation projects. Gain consistent alignment and timely execution of all tasks between Advance Store Support Center departments and the field.
<br><br>
<b>Secondary Role:</b> Provide ongoing identification and development to streamline and scale processes that are vital to the growth of the Independent Carquest business.
<br><br>
ESSENTIAL DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES include the following. Other duties may be assigned.
<br>Oversees all aspects of project workflow of all Independent Carquest store projects.
<br>Manages projects using Advance Auto Parts approved project management tools.
<br>Focuses on maintaining consistency and streamlines field process within set timelines.
<br>Interacts, partners and builds relationships with Field Directors, Account Executives, Finance Team, Setup Team Leads, Store Partners (customers) and other Store Support Center teams as required.
<br>Supervises unloading and loading of trucks.
<br>Supervises that fixtures are set according to the floor plan.
<br>Merchandises product on fixtures according to the planogram (POG).
<br>Assists in training store personnel on proper merchandising techniques.
<br>Places prices and descriptive signs on fixtures and merchandise.
<br>Installs and/or relocates computers.
<br>Must be capable of leading and directing a group of employees to successfully set a new store, relocation, or remodel of an existing or acquired store to company and department standards.
<br>Verify all POGs are correct, current and built to plan. This includes signage, display racks, and specialty display items.
<br>Report any unfinished construction issues.
