<h2>Account Executive</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>At this time an <b>Account Executive </b>is needed<b> </b>to develop assigned agencies by promoting NAE products, while maintaining the company’s philosophies and principles. Work with our entire NAE staff to insure the success of assigned agencies with professionalism and enthusiasm and being the “GO TO PERSON” for all concerns, issues, and direction for everything pertaining to assigned agencies.</p>
<p><b>Role and Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>To be the NAE product knowledge and technology expert in ALL environments.</li>
<li>Work with management and Client Relations team to continue to develop processes that will ensure current accounts’ growth and success with NAE.</li>
<li>Takes immediate action to the needs of clients.</li>
<li>Responsible for following the NAE Servicing Matrix for the account management of current accounts.</li>
<li>Responsible to work with others to work through/resolve issues with current clients.
</li>
<li>General Management of current assigned agencies.</li>
<li>Proper delegation of work through CRMs and AM’s.</li>
<li>Accountable to meet NAE's annual production forecast.
</li>
<li>Retro/Reinsurance and custom dealer presentations.</li>
<li>Retro/Reinsurance reviews for ALL assigned agency business.</li>
<li>Managing assigned agencies Dealer’s remittance trends.</li>
<li>Managing assigned agencies attrition for current dealers.</li>
<li>Accountable for keeping expenses within the established guidelines.</li>
<li>Live by the NAE Standards.
</li>
<li>Maintains consistent and accurate records of all documents that pertain to accounts on the server.</li>
<li>Responsible to work with the Accounting department on all accounting issues related to assigned agencies.</li>
<li>Responsible to work with the Claims department on claim issues related to assigned agencies.</li>
<li>Strives to enforce and live the NAE Philosophy of CQSE each and every day.</li>
<li>Other Job Duties as assigned.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Qualifications and Education Requirements:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Strong computer skills including proficiency with Microsoft Office, CRM Software and internal software systems.</li>
<li>High School diploma or equivalent.
</li>
<li>Problem resolution experience.</li>
<li>A strong sales aptitude.</li>
<li>5+ or more years’ experience in an automotive sales related environment.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Work Environment and Physical Requirements:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Travelling position. Travel is typically done via car; however, air travel is frequent as well.</li>
<li>Must have mobility to drive and move about client sites.</li>
<li>Required to talk and hear on a frequent regular basis.</li>
<li>Ability to sit for extended periods of time while travelling.</li>
<li>Ability to stand while making presentations.</li>
<li>Use of hands, reach with hand and arms, stoop, kneel and crouch.</li>
<li>Specific vision abilities required include close vision, peripheral vision, depth perception and ability y to adjust focus.</li>
<li>Ability to lift at least 20 pounds.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Preferred Skills:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Attention to detail and quality of service clients.</li>
<li>Well-developed organizational skills and the ability to successfully manage multiple issues and demands.</li>
<li>Excellent communication skills, both verbal and written.</li>
<li>Excellent in building relationships and partnerships.
</li>
<li>A strong sense of urgency and a desire to succeed.</li>
<li>Driven by Integrity.</li>
</ul>
