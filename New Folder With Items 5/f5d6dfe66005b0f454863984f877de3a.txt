<h2>Electrical Engineering Project Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>If you are looking to work alongside technical experts in the power industry, use your expert knowledge and experience, enjoy traveling, then this position is for you! SEL Engineering Services, Inc. (SEL-ES) seeks a professional, innovative and detailed individual for our Project Engineer - Project Manager position in our Pullman, WA office.</p>
<p><b><i>Essential Duties and Responsibilities</i></b></p>
<ul>
<li>Possesses, understands and applies knowledge and experience in the full life-cycle project management of multiple large multi-discipline projects from proposal conception to commissioning and close-out. Effectively track and stabilize projects, implement solutions for obstacles and anticipate customer needs related SEL products in order to complete complex assignments.</li>
<li>Lead in training and assisting customers with the creation, design configuration, validation, installation, commissioning and operation of automation and/or protection systems.</li>
<li>Lead in assigning, completing and implementing work within agreed upon scope, schedule and budget to a high level of quality and safety.</li>
<li>Lead others to create and finalize customer approved functional design specifications for projects.</li>
<li>Assist in the identification, preparation and presentation of successful proposals for SEL equipment, services, and systems.</li>
<li>Provide support for other SEL marketing, sales, application engineering and development activities.</li>
<li>Mentor and develop professional staff. May supervise as necessary.</li>
<li>Other duties as assigned.</li>
</ul>
<p><b><i>Required Qualifications</i></b></p>
<ul>
<li>B.S. In Electrical Engineering or relevant discipline</li>
<li>6+ years of project management experience related to electric power systems, industry practices, and philosophies</li>
<li>Willing to travel regionally a maximum of 25%.</li>
<li>Strong writing, documentation, and speaking skills</li>
<li>Ability to learn new skills and assume new responsibilities</li>
<li>Ability to work cooperatively in a team environment</li>
<li>Background check results satisfactory to SEL</li>
<li>Negative drug test result(s)</li>
</ul>
<p><b><i>Preferred Qualifications</i></b></p>
<ul>
<li>4+ years relevant application experience with electric power systems, power system protection and control, integration, and/or automation and communications applications.</li>
<li>Professional Engineer (PE), Project Management Professional (PMP) License or equivalent certification</li>
<li>Electrical utility, industrial plant, consulting engineering, or system integrator experience</li>
<li>Working knowledge MS Project or other project management tracking software</li>
</ul>
