<h2>Front Desk Supervisor</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<h1 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Job Description:</b></h1>
<p>The Crunch Sales Supervisor will ensure the members receive the highest quality of service and facilities. He or she will exhibit an ability to achieve the financial targets for the club by motivating, leading, supervising and coordinating the activities of employees engaged in servicing our member needs. He/She will demonstrate an aptitude and command of all company wide policies and initiatives to ensure the integrity of the Crunch brand.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<h1 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Reports to:
</b></h1>
<p>General Manager</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<h1 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Requirements:
</b></h1>
<ul>
<li>Some college degree preferred</li>
<li>2 years management/sales experience required</li>
<li>Current Cardiopulmonary Resuscitation (CPR) required</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p>
</p>
<h1 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Special Skills:
</b></h1>
<ul>
<li>Strong sales skills</li>
<li>Excellent written and verbal communication</li>
<li>Creative management techniques</li>
<li>Strong organizational skills</li>
<li>Strong leadership skills</li>
<li>Strong administrative skills</li>
<li>Strong customer service skills</li>
<li>Strong computer skills</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p>
</p>
<h1 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities:
</b></h1>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Administration/Organization
</b></h3>
<ul>
<li>Demonstrate a working knowledge of sales and service standard operating procedures and policies that pertain to the club.</li>
<li>Communicate and implement club policies and procedures to employees.</li>
<li>Encourage staff to work as a team and be productive.</li>
<li>Illustrate an ability to make decisions.</li>
</ul>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Sales/Revenue Management
</b></h3>
<ul>
<li>Demonstrate the ability to lead, motivate, and manage team.</li>
<li>Achieve desired sales goals.</li>
<li>Achieve desired sales goals thru the leadership and motivation of employees.</li>
<li>Implement and support company programs and promotion to help generate new sales leads for optimum new membership growth.</li>
<li>Ensure that Team maintains proper tracking forms and the daily leads.</li>
<li>Ensure that all promotions are effectively communicated to the team and all other appropriate staff.</li>
<li>Ensure ongoing prospecting and generation of new prospective members.</li>
<li>Review sales-related written communication such as proposal, letters and promotional pieces for effectiveness, spelling, accuracy and distribution</li>
<li>Ensure that the staff has a high level of knowledge about the clubs programs, facilities and equipment.</li>
<li>Emphasize importance of staff involvement in the community and neighborhood businesses.</li>
</ul>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Leadership/Motivation
</b></h3>
<ul>
<li>Serve as a role model for employees.</li>
<li>Communicate effectively by holding weekly and individual meeting with all key club personnel.</li>
<li>Provide an inspirational environment that welcomes honest feed back from employees and takes action to ensure a quality, working environment.</li>
</ul>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Meetings</b></h3>
<ul>
<li>Monthly or Weekly Department Meetings</li>
<li>Employee Training Meetings</li>
<li>Daily “One Minute Meetings” with club staff and General Manager</li>
<li>Weekly Department Meeting</li>
<li>Performance Evaluations</li>
</ul>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Accountabilities</b></h3>
<ul>
<li>Implement and support company programs and promotion to help generate new sales leads for optimum new membership growth.</li>
<li>Keep current in knowledge of key competitors.</li>
</ul>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Measurement Standards
</b></h3>
<ul>
<li>Sales KPI's / goals</li>
<li>Ensure standards of clubs customer service excellence.</li>
<li>Demonstrate professionalism by leading by example.</li>
<li>Membership retention.</li>
<li>Timely completion of assigned tasks and projects.</li>
</ul>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Other Responsibilities</b></h3>
<ul>
<li>Membership Sales</li>
<li>Greet all members &amp; guests with a smile and wish them well as they exit the club</li>
<li>Check in members and guests in accordance with company procedures</li>
<li>Facilitate any messages on club software at member check-in</li>
<li>Answer phones in courteous, helpful, professional manner</li>
<li>Communicate special events to members and guests</li>
<li>Maintain an atmosphere, which makes members feel welcome</li>
<li>Facilitate all member requests or forward to a manager</li>
<li>Maintain professional disposition at all times</li>
<li>Sell retail products</li>
<li>Schedule member services: tanning, etc</li>
<li>Facilitate payment of member services in accordance with company procedures</li>
<li>Know club facility, services, and schedules</li>
<li>Maintain a clean and organized work area</li>
<li>Assist in all projects as delegated by club management</li>
<li>Follow all policies and procedures in the Employee Handbook</li>
<li>Opening and closing duties</li>
<li>Monitor flagged check-in’s to increase revenue and collections.</li>
<li>Demonstrate an ability to increase revenue per member.</li>
</ul>
