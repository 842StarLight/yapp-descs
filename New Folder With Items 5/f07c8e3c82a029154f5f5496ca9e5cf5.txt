<div>Voice-driven AI experiences are finally becoming a reality and Amazon’s Alexa cloud service and Echo devices are at the forefront of this latest technology wave. We deliver world-class products on aggressive schedules that are used every day, by people you know, in and about their homes. We are looking for. Come join the Alexa for Everyone team and help us craft innovative solutions for millions of customers across Alexa/Echo devices.<br>
<br>
In this role, you will be the subject matter expert for helping customers and technical teams design solutions that leverage Alexa to meet accessibility needs. You will partner with product, development, and marketing stakeholders to create scalable technical assets and accelerators for product teams and strategic customers. You will partner deeply with product management and global business development leaders to design the strategy and execution plan for Alexa for Everyone objectives. You may be required to develop technical accelerators, testing and training processes, case studies, white papers, blogs, reference implementations, labs, or presentations.<br>
<br>
You have immediate credibility with technologists and developers at all levels, from students to CIOs. You love to share your passion with others and exhibit good judgment in selecting strategic opportunities to do so. With your unique blend of business and technical skills, you will influence Alexa product and service teams to create the best experience for our customers. You will also be willing to get your hands dirty to demonstrate concepts that will delight our customers. You don’t just want to be part of an industry movement; you want to be out front leading it. If this sounds like you, we would like to speak with you.<br>
<br>
Your roles and responsibilities will include:<br>
<ul>
<li>Work with Alexa product teams to define requirements and product roadmap; work with PM and BD on outbound GTM composition and execution</li>
<li>Act as a technical leader for Alexa for Everyone, including lighthouse customer support, technical asset development, providing mentorship, covering speaking engagements</li>
<li>Provide Thought Leadership – Evangelize Alexa for Everyone and share best practices through forums such as whitepapers, reference architectures and public-speaking events to customers</li>
<li>Act as a technical liaison between customers and Alexa for Everyone engineering teams to provide customer driven product improvement feedback</li>
<li>Build reference architecture, frameworks, and tool-kits for future Alexa partners</li>
<li>Manage complex projects with significant bottom-line impact, executive visibility, and reach to millions of users</li>
</ul>
