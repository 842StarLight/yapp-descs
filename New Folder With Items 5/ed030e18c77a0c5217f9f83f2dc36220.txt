<h2>Brand Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<div><div><div>
<p>PCA SKIN, named one of the top 3 medical skin care lines in 2015 by Aesthetic Everything, is currently looking for a Brand Manager for their Professional channel. Recognized as one of the best companies to work for by Phoenix Magazine, PCA SKIN is looking for a seasoned individual to represent this established and rapidly growing brand.</p>
<p>The Brand Manager will take on a leadership role within the Professional Marketing team and will be the main point of contact for all Professional channel marketing, covering PCA SKIN’s full portfolio of products. This position is responsible for ensuring growth of PCA SKIN products by developing and executing marketing plans in alignment with the company’s strategic priorities. The Brand Manager reports to the Sr. Director, Brand Marketing and partners closely with all functional departments, specifically with the Product Manager, Sales Team, Education Manager and Creative Team.</p>
<p><b>Essential Functions:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Understand the brand’s positioning and ensure it is adhered to in the execution of strategy and tactics; translate brand elements into plans and go-to-market strategies</li>
<li>In partnership with the Product Manager, establish product performance specifications, cost and price parameters, market applications and sales estimates/forecasts</li>
<li>Oversee new and ongoing marketing and advertising activities</li>
<li>Lead project management of all internal and external creative projects and marketing materials that motivate the target audience to “take action,” including but not limited to Collateral, Advertising (print or other), Videos (for commercial or consumer use), Photography, Trade Shows, Presentations</li>
<li>Measure and report performance of all marketing campaigns, and assess ROI and KPIs; Make the required changes to constantly improve campaigns or cancel non-productive projects to invest more heavily in successful efforts</li>
<li>Garner deep understanding of marketplace, inclusive of competitor dynamics, customer and consumer needs and trends; compile analysis and leverage intelligence to support all marketing strategies</li>
<li>Expands PCA SKIN’s brand presence in current customer channels, while significantly strengthening/building our brand presence in the medical field</li>
<li>In partnership with the Education Team and Sales, develops and executes programs that enhance our customers’ business skills/acumen and enhances their profitability</li>
<li>As the lead Brand Manager, align the company around the brand’s direction, choices and tactics</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Desired Skills:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Proven ability to develop brand and marketing strategies and communicate recommendations to executives</li>
<li>Experience in identifying target audiences and devising effective campaigns</li>
<li>Excellent understanding of the full marketing mix</li>
<li>Strong research and analytical skills, partnered with a creative, innovative mind</li>
<li>Data-driven thinking and an affinity for numbers</li>
<li>Budget management skills and forecasting proficiency</li>
<li>Comfort with CRM software</li>
<li>Analytical skills to forecast and identify trends and challenges</li>
<li>Excellent written and verbal communication skills</li>
<li>“Roll-up sleeves” and “can-do” attitude</li>
<li>Up-to-date with latest marketing trends and best practice</li>
</ul>
</div></div></div>

<li>Minimum of 5 years of experience in marketing; minimum of 2 years of experience as a brand manager</li>
<li>Preference for experience in premium skin care / beauty / CPG</li>
</ul>
