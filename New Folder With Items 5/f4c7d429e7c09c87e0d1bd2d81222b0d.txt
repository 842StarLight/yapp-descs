<h2>Principal Systems Engineer (NE Boston)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p>RoundTower Technologies (RTT) is a leading National solutions provider and consulting firm that provides innovative solutions and services in the areas of <b>data center infrastructure</b>, <b>converged platforms</b>, <b>cloud automation</b> and <b>orchestration</b>, <b>DevOps</b>, <b>network</b>, <b>end-user compute experience</b>, <b>IT security</b>, <b>big data</b> and <b>data analytics</b>, <b>Splunk</b>, and <b>ServiceNow</b>. We’re helping enable our customers to drive positive business outcomes by becoming more agile and efficient through the use of foundational and emerging technologies.</p>
<p>
The Principal Systems Engineer (PSE) at RTT is the technical leader for their assigned territory. The PSE is the technical quarterback, working with their sales counterparts to determine the sales strategies and execution plan for their joint territory. As the technical QB, the PSE owns the technical relationships within their customers and has responsibility for driving and directing all technical aspects of the sales strategy, plans, campaigns and extended teams. The PSE strives to become THE Trusted Advisor for their customers, helping them set future vision that supports their transformation journey while helping them to solve current needs leveraging foundational and emerging technology solutions. The PSE focuses on understanding and documenting their customer’s current state, their challenges, initiatives, and transformation goals and leverages this information to position the full RTT portfolio of Services and Solutions.</p>
<p>
The successful PSE at RTT approaches this role with excitement and enthusiasm and a <b>Create</b>, <b>Grow</b>, and <b>Accelerate</b> mindset, constantly considering what they can be doing to create, grow, and accelerate business, but always with the best interest of our customers at the forefront in their minds.</p>
<p></p>
