<h2>Customer Experience Transformation Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div><div><div><div>
<p><b>SageSure Insurance Managers </b>recently moved to a new state-of-the-art office in Cincinnati, OH to support our 30% yearly growth and we are seeking a proven Customer Expereince Transformation Manager to join our team. As a pivotal member of our growing Customer Experience team, you’ll be focused on improving customer experiences in support of our vision to become the #1 choice for residential property insurance in coastal states. We are looking for an action-oriented individual with exceptional customer journey mapping experience, process improvement, project management and analytical abilities.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
What you’ll be doing:</b></p>
<ul><li>
Initiating, planning and leading a portfolio of key process improvement initiatives:
<ul>
<li>Creating project documents to effectively initiate, track and manage both incremental and business transformation initiatives
</li>
<li>Mapping processes, including end to end journeys, to identifying moments of truth, delighters and key drivers of customer satisfaction</li>
<li>
Conducting qualitative and quantitative analysis to make informed recommendations on customer facing improvements to leadership</li>
<li>
Collaborating with stakeholders across the organization to develop and deliver customer facing improvements
</li>
<li>Reporting on progress and providing regular status updates to key stakeholders</li>
<li>
Managing interdependencies, risks, and issues, flexing as needed to keep deliverables on track and aligned with desired business outcomes</li>
</ul>
</li></ul>
<ul><li>
Working with Assistant Vice President of Customer Experience to establish supporting processes:<ul>
<li>
Establishing and managing departmental work intake processes to effectively evaluate, plan, and prioritize initiatives</li>
<li>
Building and implementing standardized project management templates and approaches</li>
<li>
Developing, implementing and managing a process for the collection, tracking, evaluation, and resolution of customer insights</li>
<li>
Developing effective and repeatable processes to communicate initiative status, results, and progress to stakeholders</li>
</ul>
</li></ul>
<p><b>
We're looking for someone who has:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
A proven track record of improving processes and collaborating with others to re-imagine, design, and implement best in class customer experiences</li>
<li>
The ability to influence stakeholders outside of direct reporting relationship to identify and implement customer facing improvements</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Qualified candidates have:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
A bachelor’s degree in business, marketing, sales, operations or related discipline</li>
<li>
A minimum of 5-7 year’s work experience in a business transformation, continuous improvement PMO type function and customer journey mapping skills</li>
<li>
Advanced skills in MS PowerPoint, Excel and Visio</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Highly preferred candidates have:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
A Project Management (PMP), Lean Six Sigma (Green Belt or Black Belt), Customer Experience (CCXP) or Change Management certifications</li>
<li>
A passion for learning and developing new skill sets to help the team in different areas</li>
<li>
Prior insurance industry experience</li>
<li>
Prior experience in consulting</li>
<li>
A MBA</li>
</ul>
