What you'll do
<br><br>
Cloudflare is looking for a Product Counsel to join the Legal team based either in our Austin or San Francisco office. The ideal candidate is a seasoned legal professional with deep technical knowledge and experience working with product and engineering teams to support their innovative efforts.
<br><br>
In this role, you will
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Play a broad role working closely with product and engineering teams on new and existing products to provide guidance on legal issues</li>
<li>Engage throughout the product development life cycle to manage legal risk and ensure compliance in a collaborative fashion</li>
<li>Draft terms of service, disclosures, policies and help implement related processes</li>
<li>Work with outside counsel on patent prosecution and patent litigation strategy.</li>
</ul>
This role is ideal for someone who is a proactive self-starter and strategic thinker, who has a thirst for knowledge and continued growth, all while working as part of a great team. You'll be able to to develop and improve upon our existing processes, and help us pave the way for continued expansion.
<br><br>
Examples of desirable skills, knowledge and experience
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>JD degree with strong academic credentials and admitted to practice law in at least one U.S. jurisdiction (ideally California)</li>
<li>4+ years of relevant experience working in legal roles -- preferably with a technology transactions or intellectual property litigation background and with some experience in-house at a technology company</li>
<li>Experience with compliance issues and standards at all levels (local, state, federal and international) and familiarity with industry-specific and consumer protection regimes (e.g. GDPR, PCI, advertising, data protection, etc.)</li>
<li>A strong mix of legal, technical and business experience that will allow you to understand and discuss complex technical issues and data flows, especially related to software development, networking technology, and cyberlaw</li>
<li>The ability to translate legal advice into actionable guidance for innovative product, engineering and sales teams</li>
<li>Ability to organize, prioritize, and manage many tasks on a complex project</li>
<li>Strong communication skills and cooperative team player. People want to work with you</li>
<li>The ability to work in a cross-functional team, with lawyers and non-lawyers, to establish effective solutions efficiently and in an amenable manner.</li>
</ul>
