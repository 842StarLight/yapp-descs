<h2>Demand Planner</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Description</b></p>
<p>The Demand and Supply Planner is responsible for the development and accuracy of planned capacity and raw material needs, as well as the data analysis and support for Mill Scheduling.</p>
<p><b>Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Closely monitors Inventory levels, customer orders, inventory transactions and forecast requirements to develop required replenishments and timing.</li>
<li>Brass Parts.</li>
<li>Overseas sourced components.</li>
<li>Internally produced products.</li>
<li>Develops and tracks inventory plan, including reporting out information for the Sales and Operations Planning process.</li>
<li>Accumulates purchasing data including purchase receipts, the open POs and purchase requisition. Works with finance on future spending forecasts.</li>
<li>Creates, monitors, and maintains S&amp;OP metrics and key performance indicators, identifying resulting risks and opportunities.</li>
<li>Other duties or tasks as assigned.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Knowledge, Skills, Abilities and Behaviors</b></p>
<p>Through a minimum of 2 years of hands-on experience in forecasting or demand planning related job roles, you must demonstrate acquired skills and measurable success in each of the following areas:</p>
<ul>
<li>Must be responsive to the needs of internal customers, and work well in a team environment.</li>
<li>Must have significant computer skills.</li>
<li>Must be familiar with a major ERP system (Microsoft AX, JDE.)</li>
<li>Must have strong software skills (all MS Office applications: Word, Excel, PowerPoint, Outlook).</li>
<li>Must be detail oriented; accomplished in working with multiple data sources and contributors.</li>
<li>Must have good analytical skills.</li>
<li>Must have the ability to communicate effectively both orally and in writing with various levels of management.</li>
<li>Enthusiastic, positive personality who embraces change. Able to create collaborative relationships.</li>
</ul>
