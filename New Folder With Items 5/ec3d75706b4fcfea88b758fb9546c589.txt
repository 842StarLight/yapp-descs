<h2>IT Business Systems Analyst-Dynamics365</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Our corporate client is a privately owned importer, exporter, processor and distributor of specialty agricultural products.</p>
<p>Under the direction of the Director, Business Applications, the <b>Business</b> <b>Systems Analyst </b>will work with multiple business units to understand their needs and to leverage effective utilization of information technology to manage and profitably grow the business.</p>
<p><b>Primary Function of Role</b>:</p>
<p>· Analyze new and legacy business requirements and system capabilities to propose, direct development of, and implement improvements.</p>
<p>· Consult with business staff at multiple sites to understand their needs and to leverage effective utilization of ERP, CRM, Reporting, and other key systems and interacting technology.</p>
<p>· Conduct project leadership work in a manner consistent with high quality and respect for deadlines and time commitments.</p>
<p>· Provide on-demand responsiveness required to keep business application solutions operating efficiently.</p>
<p><b>Principal Responsibilities and Activities: </b></p>
<p>· Perform duties in concert with Information Technology team.</p>
<p>· Consult with the operational departments to develop and maintain an understanding of business requirements and implement applications to enhance financial results by improving purchasing, operational, and sales functions by reducing errors, improving communications systems, and enabling greater efficiency.</p>
<p>· Identify, report, and help solve project issues, conflicts, and problems. Mitigate risks that jeopardize the project deliverables.</p>
<p>· Perform technology project leadership tasks including planning, design, development oversight, status reporting, task scheduling, and business / technical analysis, testing, rollout, training, and project closure.</p>
<p>· Write functional specifications that document business and technical requirements that are usable by business management and development resources.</p>
<p>· Develop system and user documentation for the purposes of knowledge management and self-service application support.</p>
<p>· Understand the structure of business system databases and work to ensure their integrity and operational efficiency.</p>
<p>· Plan, develop, and maintain management reporting systems including Business Intelligence and SQL reporting platforms.</p>
<p>· Manage interdependencies between projects.</p>
<p>· Deliver comprehensive on-demand status reporting to the Director, Business Applications.</p>
<p>· Attend industry conventions and seminars to learn of new applications and solutions.</p>
<p>· Coordinate with 3rd party vendor relationships as related to services utilized.</p>
<p>· Assess need for 3rd party consulting talent on an as-needed basis.</p>
<p>· Other duties as assigned by management.</p>
<p><b>Education and Experience</b>:</p>
<p>· Four-year Bachelor degree in either a Computer Science or Business curriculum.</p>
<p>· Formal technical training in business application development or equivalent experience.</p>
<p>· Minimum 10 years of professional work experience with increasing levels of responsibility.</p>
<p>· Experience with business system user support, and functional solution development using 3rd party and in-house resources and tools.</p>
<p>· Knowledge of ERP, including CRM. Microsoft Dynamics AX 2012 or D365 is a must. Microsoft CRM is a nice to have.</p>
<p>· Basic knowledge of the Microsoft Power Platform.</p>
<p>· Relational database design and SQL experience.</p>
<p>· Data conversion experience related to system migration, upgrade, or other activities.</p>
<p>· Proficiency in MS Office desktop productivity tools.</p>
<p>· Experience with distribution or manufacturing industry is a plus.</p>
<p>Job Type: Full-time</p>
