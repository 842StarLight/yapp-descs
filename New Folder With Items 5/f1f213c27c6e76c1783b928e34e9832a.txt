<p><b>Job Description Summary:
</b></p>Dedicated Contract Services specializes in total supply chain management solutions, customized to meet each client's needs. We serve customers in over 450 locations, ranging from but not limited to, retail, food and restaurant, agriculture and the oil industry. We are expected to double in size in the next five years - We are not slowing down!
<p></p>
<p><b>Job Description:
</b></p>
<p>As an <b>Operations Supervisor </b>, you will be responsible for managing the daily operations of an account, which includes maintaining revenue generation, safety, equipment maintenance, payroll and billing, and ensuring Customer Value Delivery. The environment is challenging, fast-paced, and very rewarding!
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Qualifications:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Four-year college degree from an accredited educational institution with a preferred GPA of 2.70 or higher, or
</li>
<li>Minimum of 3 years continuous employment with J.B. Hunt demonstrating management potential, or
</li>
<li>Minimum of 3 years recent supervisory experience outside of J.B. Hunt, or
</li>
<li>Military Non-Commissioned Officer with at least 3 years of supervisory experience (i.e., minimum E-4).
</li>
<li>
<i>Preference will be given to candidates who are willing to relocate for future opportunities within the company.
 </i><br>
</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Responsibilities:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Dispatch and supervise a group of drivers
</li>
<li>Maintain performance metrics and participate in customer meetings
</li>
<li>Monitor and train drivers to promote company safety culture
</li>
<li>Route or pre-plan delivery of freight to ensure on-time service
</li>
<li>Involvement in the financial analysis as it relates to the specific profit-and-loss statements
</li>
<li>Monitor compliance with safety and Department of Transportation regulations
</li>
<li>Coordinate with maintenance support personnel to manage service of all account equipment
</li>
<li>Reduce driver turnover by utilizing JB Hunt’s Driver Dedicated program
</li>
</ul>
