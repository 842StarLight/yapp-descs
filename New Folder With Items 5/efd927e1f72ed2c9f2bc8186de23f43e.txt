<h2>Business Development Executive</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p><b>Who We Are:</b></p>
<p>Excelacom is a consulting and technology solutions company with a focus on Communications and Media providers. At our core, we partner with our clients to solve their complex business, technology and operational challenges through a combination of consulting expertise and telecom-focused product solutions. Our consulting teams optimize existing systems and work on an accelerated timeline using innovative approaches and best practices that minimize risk and maximize return to improve business and operational performance without costly technology transformations.</p>
<p>Excelacom is a global company and is headquartered in the United States in Reston, VA, outside of Washington, D.C., with offices in North America and Europe as well as a technology center in Chennai, India.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Job Description:</b></p>
<p>You will lead the charge in finding and securing brand new “Logo” clients in the CSP/MSP/Cloud space.. You will discover, develop, and close new business with qualified prospects having a need for innovative business applications that significantly improve performance and bottom-line results.</p>
<p>We are looking for highly talented and passionate individuals who can effectively drive OSS/BSS/CRM/CPQ Software solutions.
</p>
<p>The Business Development Executive (BDE) will formulate and HUNT utilizing a solutions sales strategy within their region, drive revenue growth &amp; develop new customers. In addition to the excellent sales skills the BDE should possess deep industry knowledge in combination with exceptional customer management experience at the C-level.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Report to VP of Sales</li>
<li>Help Refine sales messaging, prospecting, qualifying, and closing techniques</li>
<li>Create new leads from prospecting efforts and assist others</li>
<li>Leverage business from new and established relationships</li>
<li>Manage complex sales cycles, utilizing internal and external resources as appropriate</li>
<li>Identify and grow sales opportunities within assigned territory</li>
<li>Be willing to travel domestically and internationally as required</li>
<li>Keep current with industry trends and engage customers addressing their business challenges</li>
<li>Develop &amp; execute on CSP/MSP/Cloud sales plans &amp; cycles.</li>
<li>Learn Excelacom Software Products</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Required Qualifications:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>6+ years software sales experience</li>
<li>Bachelor's degree or equivalent</li>
<li>Knowledge of OSS, BSS, CRM, &amp; CPQ a plus</li>
<li>Experience in the telecom vertical</li>
<li>Experience with sales planning and prioritization.</li>
<li>Proven ability in managing complex sales cycles from start to finish with a track record of successful revenue attainment in the relevant vertical</li>
<li>Ability to maintain a high level of productivity, manage multiple competing priorities, and work effectively under the pressure of time constraints in a fast-paced, complex, collaborative, and team-oriented environment</li>
<li>Excellent verbal, written, interpersonal and presentation skills</li>
<li>Highly self-motivated and able to work with little direct supervision</li>
<li>Proven consultative sales solution skills, including the ability to articulate a clear, concise return on investment value statement</li>
<li>Understanding of the impact of mobility and digital strategy</li>
<li>Experience managing the sales cycle from business champion to C-level executives</li>
<li>Extensive background in technology and platform solutions such as application development, database, business intelligence, integration, internet.</li>
</ul>
