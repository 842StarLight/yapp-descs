What You Will Do
<br><br>
The US Regional Engineering Operations Manager (REOM) will lead a team of over 250 personal in a defined US region and will cascade initiatives through Senior Engineering Operations Managers (SEOMs) based on-site who will run the management of their particular sites. Having direct accountability for overall regional performance in line with both internal and client SLA arrangements, as well as general legal and health and safety standards, the REOM will work closely with other Ocado REOM's in country.
<br><br>
The REOM will be a key stakeholder throughout the new site development programs, post-construction phase, and build, inspire and motivate talented on-site engineering teams, creating a loyal, focused and dynamic working environment which is reflective of the Ocado values. The REOM will also serve as the regional business leader/project sponsor for the creation of both regional and site specific strategy for Ocado's Engineering Operations priorities.
<br><br>
As a Senior Manager, you will be a brand ambassador of Ocado as the business becomes more established in the US over the coming years.
<br><br>
As our Regional Engineering Operations Manager, your day-to-day responsibilities could include:
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Support and coach site managers and their respective teams to enhance service and improve overall performance</li>
<li>Promote 'best in class' practices and will ensure US teams provide high quality "end user" feedback to central head office teams.</li>
<li>Provide oversight and leadership for continuous improvement initiatives using Lean, Six Sigma and TPM methodology</li>
<li>Deliver operational objectives through incremental improvement projects with regular contact with Site Leaders to ensure improvements are sustained and performance targets are achieved</li>
<li>Contribute to the development of the business strategy, business planning and execution and should direct the preparation of regional budgets, forecasts and cost management</li>
<li>Adhere to reliability road map by ensuring sites are actively applying best practices in preventive and predictive maintenance</li>
<li>Enforce all aspects of Safety Management by ensuring that local operations, safety professionals and the Ocado Health and Safety team are included in process reviews to guaranty cross functional alignment, while also maintaining adherence to all local and state safety and regulatory requirements</li>
<li>Regularly review effectiveness of equipment handover to Engineering Operations by central project teams, ensuring that appropriate Project Reviews are being conducted in the region and Site Managers are taking ownership of MHE in satisfactory conditions. The REOM will help escalate to central engineering when required</li>
</ul>
