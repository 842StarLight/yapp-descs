<h2>Commercial Account Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>At Interos, we are disrupting the way Fortune 500 companies and government agencies identify and respond to risk within their supply chains whether this risk is being caused by cyber breaches, socio economic issues, quality control, natural disasters or unknown, unethical sourcing. We deliver the data and insights to business leaders that help them identify, visualize and understand the ripple effects that could impact their supply chains, before they happen.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
Recently funded by Kleiner Perkins and pivoting to an automated solution, Interos is in essence, a start-up SaaS environment. We need someone who thrives as part of fast-paced team and takes pride in the ownership of their content strategy.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>The Opportunity:</b></p>
<p>
Supply Chain Risk Management is a hot topic given globalization and Interos is the only company that uses AI to non-invasively map out sub-tier relationships to gain a full picture of a supplier/product ecosystem. To keep up with rapid demand, we need experienced Account Executives to take the Interos AI Platform to SVP and C-Level players in key verticals in the Fortune 1,000. Today, there is nobody providing this type of technology and Interos has a unique 18-month window to exponentially grow its commercial account market share.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Key Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Individual Contributor</li>

</li>
<li>Develops a business plan and sales strategy (new-business) for each Targeted Account in the assignment to ensure tactical penetration and engagement.</li>
<li>Responsible for qualifying, developing and closing new opportunities for F500 accounts in your territory.</li>
<li>Manages sales cycles from VP-Level champions/sponsors to C-level executives.
</li>
<li>Develops a business plan and new-business sales strategy that ensures tactical penetration and attainment of direct sales quota to fleets of 50 or fewer assets.</li>
<li>Management of BDRs to ensure a continuous flow of qualified leads.</li>
<li>Overachievement of quarterly sales quota through selling TPRM, SCRM platform to well qualified prospects using the Interos sales process.</li>
<li>Remains highly knowledgeable as a SME in your specified industry vertical(s). 100% certification of all DInteros solutions and Customer Success offerings.</li>
<br>
</ul>
