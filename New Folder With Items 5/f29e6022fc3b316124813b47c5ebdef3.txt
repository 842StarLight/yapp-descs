<h2>Communications and Promotions Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p>The Communications &amp; Promotions Manager will be responsible for developing and managing internal and external communications for a unique, niche company to build Team engagement, foster community support, educate the market and create a sustainable pipeline of leads to meet sales and marketing goals.</p>
<div>
Strategy &amp; Planning<br>
<ul>
<li>Work with Team to develop strategies for company-wide communication processes that support Musco’s priorities and build Team engagement</li>
<li>Develop strategies, budget and tactical plans for identifying potential projects early in the planning process through an optimal mix of PR and promotions to targeted new and existing customer audiences</li>
<li>Work with HR &amp; community development Team to create strategies for communications that provide education and foster support within communities</li>
<li>Regularly interact with stakeholders and discuss strategies to ensure alignment and accurate, consistent messaging. Stakeholders include developmental and operational sales, marketing, engineering, HR, legal, company leadership, customers, partners and publications</li>
</ul>
Analysis<br>
<ul>
<li>Monitor, analyze, anticipate and manage issues/opportunities for marketplace communications and promotions</li>
<li>Continually explore best methods for delivery tools and approaches that make our interactions with the audience understandable, useful, consistent and convenient</li>
</ul>
Execution<br>
<ul>
<li>Develop and lead Team and vendor resources to execute coordinated communications/promotions using vehicles such as articles, social media, web sites, direct marketing, lead services, advertising, tradeshows, blogs, webinars and newsletters</li>
<li>Report results and success factors</li>
<li>Provide resources to encourage and support customer self-promotions</li>
<li>Other duties as assigned</li>
</ul>
</div>
<div>
<b>
Education &amp; Experience Requirements:</b><br>
<ul><li>A degree in communications, journalism or equal, plus 8-10 years of related experience preferred</li></ul>
</div>
<div>
<b>
Job Specifications:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Enjoy the challenge of niche marketing for a Team with unique product and service solutions</li>
<li>Experience in developing PR, promotion and communication strategies and tactical plans that incorporate stakeholder perspectives and objectives</li>
<li>Experience executing plans within budget and deadlines</li>
<li>Experience leading and mentoring teams</li>
<li>Disciplined in analytics and developing conclusions/actions based on data and instinct</li>
<li>Creative and problem-solving skills</li>
<li>Exceptional written and verbal communication skills</li>
<li>Technical aptitude</li>
<li>Able to prioritize and handle multiple tasks in a fast paced, team environment</li>
<li>Flexible with a sense of humor</li>
<li>Enjoy working in a constantly changing team environment</li>
<li>Some travel and night/weekend work possible</li>
</ul>
</div>
