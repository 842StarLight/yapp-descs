<p><b>ASSISTANT STORE MANAGER
</b></p>
<p><b>You’ve got to…</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Love our brand, customers, and teams and take every opportunity to shout it from the mountaintops.</li>
<li>
Have a track record of setting and achieving goals.</li>
<li>
Enjoy working hard and always see things through from start to finish.</li>
<li>
Make the best first impression; smile, welcome and connect with customers authentically.</li>
<li>
Be aware of what’s going on around you and pick up on subtle cues to adjust approach and tempo.</li>
<li>
Make smart decisions by: actively listening, understanding data, and looking beyond the obvious.</li>
<li>
Look under rocks, be curious, ask questions and think boldly.</li>
<li>
Know when to act and when to partner.</li>
<li>
Generate ideas to evolve the business.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
We’ll want you to…</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Set the pace for the team by connecting with customers and speaking to value.</li>
<li>
Observe Sales Associate performance on the selling floor and assist if necessary to make a sale.</li>
<li>
Help Sales Associates make the most of their customer interactions by sharing specific tips to improve results.</li>
<li>
Provide the communication and tools needed to motivate the team to achieve key KPIs.</li>
<li>
Own the selling floor and ensure that the right people are in the right place at the right time.</li>
<li>
Achieve Enterprise Selling targets.</li>
<li>
Ace training and put skills to use.</li>
<li>
Embrace role ownership and be ready to step in for anyone on the management team when needed.</li>
<li>
Find ways to get things done, even when time and resources are in short supply.</li>
<li>
Maximize opportunities to grow the business and consistently question what can be done better.</li>
<li>
Act in a manner that aligns with our values.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Oh, and by the way, you…</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Have a high school diploma or equivalent combo of education and experience.</li>
<li>
Have 2 or more years of experience with similar scope, specialty retail preferred.</li>
<li>
Communicate effectively and confidently.</li>
<li>
Process information and operate store systems accurately.</li>
<li>
Assist in shipment processing, replenishing, and keeping the store looking great.</li>
<li>
Are available when we are busy, including: nights, weekends and holidays.</li>
<li>
Are adept with technology and apps.</li>
<li>
Must bend, reach, and stretch for product, as well as lift, carry and move at least 40 pounds |18 kilos.</li>
<li>
Must regularly move around all store areas and be accessible to customers.</li>
</ul>
