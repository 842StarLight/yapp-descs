<h2>Leasing Consultant</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<div><div><div>
<p>The Leasing Consultant is responsible for all sales and on-site marketing to increase and maintain a high property occupancy, and will serve as first point of contact for the property office.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>DIMENSIONS:</b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>Acts as the first line contact, verbally and in-person, for all prospects, vendors, fellow employees, management executives and public in general. Attempts to lease apartments shown and/or pre-leases apartments with notices to vacate through new contacts and renewals of existing residents. Increase value of real estate by reaching and maintaining maximum occupancy of apartments and their maximum rates. This is a line position with direct impact on company funds.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>ESSENTIAL DUTIES:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Answers all telephone calls, stands and greets all in-person visitors. Answers inquiries and provides rental information in an enthusiastic and positive manner. Schedules sales appointments. Converts prospect phone calls into property visits.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Tours model or market units daily, confirming readiness for presentation each morning and closing each evening. Demonstrates units to prospects in a manner so as to attempt to close 50% of all prospects shown. Must meet individual goals and property goals set by the property. Goals may fluctuate depending on the needs of each specific property.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Records all traffic and activities daily and applies company guest card and follow-up programs in a prompt and consistent manner.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Notifies prospects of application acceptance or rejection within 24 hours of acceptance determination.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Completes and reviews market survey to maintain current knowledge of local markets.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Receives and records resident service requests ensuring full and accurate information is taken; posts completed service requests. Follows up by phone on completed work orders taken each week.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Completes other administrative, marketing and other reasonable duties as assigned.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Maintains consistent and punctual attendance record.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>MARGINAL DUTIES:</b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Make business and locator contacts weekly to promote the property and generate traffic.</li>
<li>Executes lease paperwork and move-in packet, following company policies regarding move-in details including Day 1 walk through procedures with new resident and targets a zero defect move-in experience.<br>
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Assists in resident retention and relations programs in a manner inviting resident satisfaction and comfort.<br>
</li>
<p></p>
<li>Keeps leasing office and desks neat and orderly at all times and maintains office standardization system, including filing and forms supplies.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Travels to suppliers to pick up parts and supplies as needed.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Greet prospects and qualify by covering all criteria (ask questions; utilize completed guest cards, etc.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Immediately record all marketing traffic including emails, telephone calls and in-person visits on appropriate reports</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Lease apartments and be responsible for all paperwork involved in taking applications.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Files own guest cards and maintain according to established procedures</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Inspect model and available “market ready” units; communicate related service needs to Property Manager</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Demonstrate community and apartment/model and apply product knowledge to clients’ needs by communicating the features and benefits; close the sale</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Have prospect complete application and secure deposit in accordance with the company procedures and Fair Housing requirements</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Update availability report, process applications for approvals. (i.e., credit check, rental history, etc.) Submit processed applications to the Community Manager for approval. Follow up with applicant regarding status</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Ensure apartment is ready for resident to move-in on agreed date</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Immediately follow-up on prospects that did not close and attempt to close sale again. If unable to help prospect, refer them to sister communities to meet prospect’s needs.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Secure new residents signature(s) on appropriate paperwork prior to move-in. Orient new residents to community.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Assist in monitoring renewals. Distribute and follow-up on renewal notices.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Monitor advertising effectiveness. Gather information about market competition in the area and file.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Represent the company in a professional manner at all the times.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Design and implement an outreach (outside marketing) plan with property manager.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<ul><li>Allocate time for outside marketing. (Must provide your own transportation)<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>SCOPE:</b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>The majority of decisions are made using supervisor’s direction and following company policies and procedures. The incumbent has no authority that is not specifically delegated from the Property Manager.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>RELATIONSHIP WITH OTHERS:</b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>Has contact with residents and prospective residents to sell, persuade and exchange information. Has contact with company executives, vendors and other company site and corporate personnel to exchange information. Participation fully in staff meetings and designated company training programs.</p>
</li></ul>
</div></div></div>
<div>Qualifications</div>
<div><div>
<div>
<ul>
<li>A minimum of two years of sales experience is required</li>
<li>Previous leasing experience is highly preferred</li>
<li>High school graduate or GED is required</li>
<li>Proficient in Microsoft Office</li>
<li>Excellent verbal and written communication skills and interpersonal skills</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>PERFORMANCE EXPECTATIONS AND STANDARDS:</b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>The incumbent shall perform his/her duties using standard levels of productivity and dependability, ensuring completion in a timely manner and zero-defect manner. A 50% closing ratio should be consistently attempted. Must be able to provide clear and consistent verbal and written communication to others. Must portray a clean, neat, well-groomed professional appearance. Company-approved attire may be required and a nametag must be worn in plain sight when at work. Must be honest, trustworthy, enthusiastic and team/project oriented. A flexible workweek is mandatory. The incumbent must be customer service oriented and genuinely support and promote good resident relations and service staff and residents. Evening and weekend work is typical and usual.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>PHYSICAL/MENTAL REQUIREMENTS:</b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>The leasing consultant position requires the following physical and mental capabilities to be performed:</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>Constantly (66% to 100% of the time)</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>Writing, keyboarding, grasping, turning, using finger dexterity, giving and receiving verbal communication both in person and over the telephone, giving and receiving written communication, working outdoors in all conditions, lifting and carrying items weighing 10lbs or less, observing things clearly beyond an arm’s reach, performing general and detail observation and driving vehicles.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>Frequently (33% to 66% of the time)</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>Being on foot, bending, stooping, squatting, kneeling, climbing stairs, pushing or pulling, reaching above shoulder height, sitting, lifting and carrying items weighing 20lbs or less, working indoors, operating equipment and tools, following instructions, reading and/or comprehending manufacturer’s and product label directions.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>Occasionally (1% to 33% of the time)</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>Lifting and carrying (with assistance) items weighing 20 to 50 lbs</p>
