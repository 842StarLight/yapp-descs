<p><b>Job Purpose Statement</b></p>
<p>This position is responsible for the accurate preparation of 3d models and working drawings to drive the development of new products, as well as any related tooling as required. Scope of work will include characterization of products under development as well as finished products requiring design changes or updates to support the identified R and D project portfolio goals. In addition, the position will support additional product development and research projects as required. The CAD Designer will prepare working drawings as well as 3d CAD solids and surface models and will need to seamlessly interface with other departments, customers, vendors, and suppliers as needed.</p>

<p><b>Essential Duties and Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Create wheel, component, accessory, and tooling drawing packages</li>
<li>Create parametric 3d CAD models and assemblies</li>
<li>Create styling models and renderings of select NPD projects</li>
<li>Maintain files versions/revisions and process files for production release</li>
<li>Upload and maintain released files in the company master drawing database</li>
<li>Utilize computer tools to optimize the design of related tooling for various performance characteristics</li>
<li>Arrange for tooling and equipment purchases as assigned</li>
<li>Prepare working drawings as needed to purchase and setup equipment</li>
<li>Expediting deliveries when required</li>
<li>Inspect finished products for quality and adherence to customer specifications.</li>
<li>Communicate with suppliers regarding the details of design, logistics, timing, and production</li>
<li>Maintain records of wheel product drawing packages in accordance with specification requirements</li>
<li>Responsible for design or redesign of product, molds, production tooling, machine mechanisms and related equipment as required</li>
<li>Arrange shipment or transfer of molds, equipment, or product between company and vendors</li>
<li>Check incoming equipment, tools, or products for compliance to drawings.</li>
<li>Become knowledgeable in wheel to vehicle fitments and assist in capturing fitment data for use in the creation of new product to meet an identified market need</li>
<li>Test products or subassemblies for functionality or quality.
</li>
<li>Utilize computer skills in a Microsoft Windows environment and other software applications used for development</li>
<li>Participate in DFLS Phase-Gate teams, including participating in design review meetings and assisting in obtaining approvals to facilitate new product launches</li>
<li>Operate with a mindset focused on safety first</li>
<li>Adhere to all applicable regulations, policies, and procedures for health, safety, and environmental compliance.
</li>
<li>Provide advice or training to other technicians.</li>
<li>Keep a clean work area</li>
<li>Assist engineers in developing, building, or testing prototypes and new products, processes, or procedures.
</li>
<li>Perform other duties as assigned</li>
</ul>
