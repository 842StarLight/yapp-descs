<h2>Senior Accountant</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Job Summary:</p>
<p>
The Senior Accountant will work with the Director of Accounting to support all of the Company’s corporate tax functions. This includes personal property tax filings as well as sales and use tax filings for multiple states, federal income tax filing, corporate tax planning, and work with external auditors as it relates to tax audits. This position will also have month-end close responsibilities and some general accounting functions. A solid technical accounting background,especially as it relates to corporate tax and/or public accounting is required. An intermediate level in excel and accounting database software experience, strong written and verbal communications skills, the ability to effectively multi-task, maintain a positive team-oriented environment, and the ability to consistently meet reporting and month-end close deadlines is essential.</p>
<p><b>
Duties and Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Prepare various g/l account reconciliations including: retainage, sales and use tax accounts, various other tax and accrual accounts.</li>
<li>Work with CFO and Director of Accounting in strategic corporate tax planning.</li>
<li>Prepare state/local annual reports, business licenses, contractor licenses and census reporting.</li>
<li>Prepare and reconcile quarterly Canada GST/HST and PST reports.</li>
<li>Assist with federal and multiple state audits.</li>
<li>Prepare various month end close entries.</li>
<li>All other duties and responsibilities as assigned.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Qualifications and Experience Requirements:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Minimum of 3-5 years of experience with month-end and year-end close.</li>
<li>Experienced in general ledger, journal entries, and month end close functions.</li>
<li>Meticulous, detail oriented, dependable and accountable.</li>
<li>Fast learner with willingness to learn new tasks and other accounting functions.</li>
<li>Good interpersonal skills, particularly in working with a team.</li>
<li>Ability to handle multiple projects and adapt to new and changing situations.</li>
<li>Proficient with Excel and be able to use various formulas and functions.</li>
<li>Ability to work extra hours to meet deadlines as needed.</li>
<li>Maintain accuracy with large amounts of accounting data.</li>
<li>Prior experience with using Epicor is preferred, but not required.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Education Requirements</b> – Bachelors in Accounting is required, CPA preferred, but not required if other technical accounting/corporate tax experience has been obtained. Masters in Business Administration, Accounting, or Finanace is preferred, but not required.</p>

Paid time off inclusive of vacation, and holidays. <b>If you are interested in joining the accounting team of a growing company, please send your cover letter, resume, and salary requirements to Human Resources.</b></p>
