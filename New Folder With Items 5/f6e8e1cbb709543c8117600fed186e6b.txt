<p>Description</p>

<div>
<p><b><i>Situated on 2,000 acres, Nemacolin Woodlands Resort, located in the Laurel Highlands of Southwestern Pennsylvania, features luxurious guestrooms, suites, townhomes and luxury vacation homes, including the Falling Rock boutique hotel, a recipient of the AAA Five-Diamond award and Forbes 5-Star rating. Pennsylvania’s luxury, nature-inspired retreat, named one of 2018’s top 58 most luxurious hotels in the world by Forbes Travel Guide!</i></b></p>
<p><i>
We are seeking to fill a key position in our Conference and Catering Department. We are looking for an individual that has a passion in conference services to include corporate and social events. This individual must possess a strong attention to detail and have the ability to multi-task in a fast-paced, deadline-oriented environment.</i></p>
<p><b>
Position Summary</b>:</p>
<p>
The candidate we are seeking will be responsible for managing sales group bookings to determine client needs in the preplanning phase, execution and continued follow up with a diverse group of resort clientele in order to exceed all guest and resort leadership expectations. The ability to successfully coordinate and oversee all assigned functions by working with and effectively communicating client needs with all departments in the resort is essential. S/he will also be responsible for achieving departmental and individual goals in order to be eligible for bonus compensation and to be considered for advancement. The most important part of the job is achieving the highest quality of service for our guests both internal and external and remaining positive when faced with any adversity. The resort is committed to be the best in the world and the right candidate would be eager to be a part of something truly exceptional.</p>
<p><b>
ESSENTIAL FUNCTIONS:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Ensure accurate and detailed information is streamlined between Resort Events and all other Departments</li>
<li>Work closely with the Director of Sales &amp; Marketing in strategizing all group related resort packages, and the assignment of meeting space</li>
<li>Must be able to lead pre-cons and other guest related meetings, and actively support the Resort</li>
<li>Ensure all indoor meeting areas, including adjoining public space, is in good repair, clean, and appropriately maintained</li>
<li>Annually establish/revise/review all non-food and beverage pricing structures to remain competitive and viable</li>
<li>Create accurate and professional quality Group Resumes, Event Orders, Diagrams and other communication forms as necessary</li>
<li>Attend weekly and resort meetings</li>
<li>Develop, implement and maintain accurate budgets and forecasts for all related departments</li>
<li>Plan, develop, and coordinate all Special Events as required</li>
<li>Attend any in-house seminars or training classes which may benefit this position</li>
<li>Ability to constantly be observant of how to improve upon daily operations</li>
</ul>
<p><b>QUALIFICATIONS:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Experience with Delphi and Microsoft Office Software is preferred</li>
<li>A degree or equivalent experience in Sales, Marketing, Catering or Conference Services, Resort Operations or related field is necessary</li>
<li>Telephone, oral and solicitation skills are required</li>
<li>3-10 years of experience in the same field is preferred</li>
<li>Pleasant demeanor, poised, confident, detailed, professional, organized and reliable.</li>
<li>Be able to work flexible shifts, weekends and holidays.</li>
<li>Must be able to speak English.</li>
</ul>
<p><b><i>Be Excellent, Be Engaged, Be Empowered to Create Lifelong Memories!</i></b></p>
