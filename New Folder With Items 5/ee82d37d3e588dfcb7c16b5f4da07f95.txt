<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Job Type: Full Time, Independent Contractor
</b></h3>
<p><b>Company &amp; Location :</b> DIRT Illustrated Communications, Costa Mesa, CA,</p>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Opportunity
</b></h3>
<p>You will be joining a team of experienced communications executives launching a new enthusiast communications platform serving the Off Road powersports market, focusing on motorcycles, all terrain vehicles and side by side utility vehicles. This project is supported by communications partner and off road industry veteran Wright Publishing. The communications platform includes a state of the art web site, a digital magazine and print magazine under the brand DIRT Illustrated. The position is selling advertising, sponsorships and custom marketing opportunities to clients in the off road business interested in selling their products and services the enthusiast that use them.
</p>

</li>
