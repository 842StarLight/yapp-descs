<div>Support operations and maintenance of our clients’ facilities and have a hand in transforming tomorrow.<br>
<div></div>As the Buyer on the Facilities Services Program, you will have the chance to create cost-effective solutions to our client’s industrial manufacturing facilities and equipment-related needs. Working with other members of our team, you’ll serve as a project procurement representative, developing knowledge of client indirect sourcing guidelines, effectively negotiate pricing and terms, procure and ensure timely delivery of goods and services in accordance with corporate strategies, and work collaboratively with internal and external team members to achieve client objectives. During your time with us, you will also have the opportunity to demonstrate your expert knowledge of the procurement process, executing purchases of services and equipment from initial requirement to contract award. If you’re up for the challenge, we’ve been looking for someone just like you. Join our team and help us lay the foundation for human progress. #AF #AdvancedFacilities #SpecializedManufacturing #Procurement
