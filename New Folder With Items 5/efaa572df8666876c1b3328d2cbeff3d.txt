<h2>Maintenance Associate</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">The primary function of a Maintenance Associate is to assure the cleanliness and orderliness of the store to fulfill the company’s commitment to safety and high-quality customer service.
<br><br>
DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES
<br>1.1 Customer Service
<br>1.1.1 Keep the customer first in all aspects of the job; be courteous and polite at all times
<br>1.1.2 Greet all customers
<br>1.1.3 Assist customers while shopping; be knowledgeable of products and store layout and helpful throughout the store
<br>1.1.4 Suggestively sell additional products/services
<br>1.1.5 Thank all customers
<br>1.1.6 Handle customer complaints in a professional manner
<br><br>
1.2 Communication &amp; Teamwork
<br>1.2.1 Be friendly, honest &amp; dependable
<br>1.2.2 Wear proper uniform and maintain neat appearance and presentation to others
<br>1.2.3 Effectively communicate (in English) verbally and in writing
<br>1.2.4 Maintain a positive, cooperative attitude &amp; assist co-workers as needed.
<br>1.2.5 Resolve conflicts in positive, productive manner
<br>1.2.6 Follow proper chain of command
<br>1.2.7 Work independently without waiting to be asked
<br>1.2.8 Accept direction and show willingness to improve performance
<br>1.2.9 Arrive for work on time and prepared to start
<br><br>
1.3 Inventory &amp; Financial Control
<br>1.3.1 Understand and follow cash control procedures, including register security, over/shorts, cash, check, credit card, local credit and coupon policies
<br>1.3.2 Follow all loss-prevention policies at all times, including scanning merchandise, using PLU’s, etc.
<br>1.3.3 Watch for shoplifters &amp; observe pumps for drive-offs
<br>1.3.4 Follow proper vendor receiving policy
<br><br>
1.4 Merchandising &amp; Sales
<br>1.4.1 Be familiar with layout of store and merchandise
<br>1.4.2 Keep sales area on counter free of clutter
<br>1.4.3 Maintain supply of cups, straws, napkins and condiments
<br>1.4.4 Keep all frozen and hot/cold beverage dispensers full
<br>1.4.5 Front shelves during shift; keep merchandise clean, stocked and rotated, including in store room and cooler
<br><br>
1.5 Safety &amp; Security
<br>1.5.1 Adhere to all safety rules and/or precautions.
<br>1.5.2 Wear and/or utilize proper protective equipment or tools, including slip-resistant shoes, safety goggles (when required), gloves, etc.
<br>1.5.3 Properly report all incidents, problems or concerns to management/supervisor.
<br><br>
1.6 Maintenance, Retailing &amp; Multi-tasking
<br>1.6.1 Be willing to do repetitive work
<br>1.6.2 Operate register and other equipment efficiently and effectively
<br>1.6.3 Clean and maintain all equipment according to policies &amp; checklists
<br>1.6.4 Maintain awareness of environment and surroundings and promptly report or correct any exceptions
<br><br>
1.7 Food Service
<br>1.7.1 Follow all health and safety standards, including personal cleanliness and hygiene, food temperature, food storage, food preparation, food serving, utensil &amp; equipment maintenance and use, and safety
<br>1.7.2 Operate all food prep, cooking and storage equipment efficiently and effectively
<br>1.7.3 Follow all recipes and quick reference guides, including portioning standards
<br><br>
1.8 General
<br>1.8.1 Follow all company policies and procedures.
<br>1.8.2 Strive to reduce possible liability exposures for the company.
<br>1.8.3 Ensure that all methods and procedures implemented in the stores meet local, state and federal rules and regulations.
<br>1.8.4 Complete special duties/assignments assigned by management.
<br><br>
STANDARDS OF PERFORMANCE
<br>2.1 Demonstrates consistent, high-quality customer service.
<br>2.2 Works well with others.
<br>2.3 Completes shift duties accurately and efficiently.
<br>2.4 Maintains clean, well-functioning equipment and economizes on supplies.
<br>2.5 Adheres to safety rules and precautions.
<br>2.6 Punctual, observes work break/meal period policies and has an acceptable overall attendance record.
<br><br>
EDUCATION, CERTIFICATION AND SKILLS REQUIREMENTS
<br>3.1 Education/Experience
<br>3.1.1 High School Education and 1 year of experience in retail sales; or
<br>3.1.2 Equivalent combination of education and experience.
<br>3.1.3 Prior experience in retail/convenience store industry preferred.
<br><br>
3.2 Licensing/Registration/Certification
<br>3.2.1 Current, valid Responsible Vendor Certification required (within 45 days of hire);
<br>3.2.2 Food Safety Certification a plus.
<br><br>
3.3 Personal Skills and Abilities
<br>3.3.1 Ability to establish and maintain effective relationships with management staff, employees, and the general public;
<br>3.3.2 Ability to work varied hours/days as business dictates;
<br>3.3.3 Must be detail oriented;
<br>3.3.4 Ability to work independently;
<br>3.3.5 Ability to work well under pressure.
<br><br>
3.4 Analytical Skills and Abilities
<br>3.4.1 Ability to apply concepts such as fractions, percentages, ratios, and proportions to practical situations;
<br>3.4.2 Ability to apply and adapt practices and techniques to the special requirements of senior management;
<br>3.4.3 Ability to effectively utilize technology to streamline processes and increase efficiency.
<br><br>
3.5 General Management Skills and Abilities
<br>3.5.1 Ability to organize and manage multiple priorities.
<br><br>
3.6 Technical Skills and Abilities
<br>3.6.1 Ability to process information/merchandise through system and POS Register system;
<br>3.6.2 Ability to operate and use all equipment necessary to run a store;
<br>3.6.3 Ability to freely access all areas of the store including selling floor, stock area and register area;
<br>3.6.4 Ability to learn and use proprietary software systems and applications.
<br><br>
3.7 Language Skills
<br>3.7.1 Ability to communicate effectively in English with associates and customers;
<br>3.7.2 Ability to read, count, and write to accurately complete all documentation;
<br>3.7.3 Ability to read and interpret documents such as department manual;
<br>3.7.4 Ability to respond to common inquiries or complaints from customers, regulatory agencies, or members of the business community;
<br>3.7.5 Ability to present facts and recommendations effectively in oral and written form.</div>
