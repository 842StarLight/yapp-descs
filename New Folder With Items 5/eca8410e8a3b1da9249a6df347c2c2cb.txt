<h2>Manager, Partnership Sales & Business Development</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<b>Position:</b> Manager, Partnership Sales &amp; Business Development
<br><br>
<b>Reports To:</b> Vice President, Partnership Sales &amp; Business Development
<br><br>
<b>Department:</b> Partnerships &amp; Business Development
<br><br>
<b>Status:</b> Full-Time, Exempt
<br><br>
<b>Position Summary:</b><br>
The San Francisco Giants are seeking an experienced, creative, and innovative sponsorship sales executive who will focus on business development and overall revenue generation in corporate partnerships. She/he will infuse professionalism, leadership, energy and creativity into the sales organization and bring new ideas and value to the existing corporate sales team. The ideal candidate will have strong sponsorship sales experience and a successful track record of prospecting, fostering, and selling, long-term partnerships through a thoughtful, thorough, and structured approach to the market. She/he will have notable experience developing creative marketing platforms and devising compelling solutions to meet partner objectives. As such, the Manager must understand and convey marketing, advertising, brand, and media research and analytics from the client's perspective. This role will be responsible for cultivating leads, developing targeted marketing proposals, securing new partnerships, and retaining and growing existing accounts.
<br><br>
<b>Position Responsibilities:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Develop and present customized proposals and marketing campaigns for brands; to include signage, promotions, asset naming rights, Hispanic marketing, hospitality entitlements and media (TV, radio, print &amp; digital)</li>
<li>Sell sponsorship packages to both new and existing clients (renewals and upsells) based on their marketing objectives and budgets, while maintaining the integrity of the organization and assets</li>
<li>Work within a team environment to achieve the revenue goals for the individual and the organization</li>
<li>Qualify leads and develop a strategy to close business in an efficient and timely manner</li>
<li>Research the internet, publications, television, radio, etc. and generate a list of potential sponsors and advertisers to be contacted regarding marketing opportunities</li>
<li>Draft, review and execute Letters of Agreements (LOA's) and contracts</li>
<li>Maintain account receivables to ensure sponsorship payments are kept up-to-date</li>
<li>Assist Account Service Team including day-of-game responsibilities throughout the season</li>
<li>Heavy emphasis on prospecting and new business</li>
<li>Update and maintain CRM system (Salesforce) for prospects and current partners</li>
</ul>
<b>Knowledge and Skills:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor's Degree required with emphasis in communications, marketing and/or business</li>
<li>Minimum of 5-7 years in sports/entertainment sponsorship sales experience</li>
<li>Proven ability to network within corporate community and sell sports media/sponsorship programs</li>
<li>Goal oriented, self-starter with strong work ethic and ability to manage multiple projects in a fast-paced and time sensitive environment</li>
<li>Demonstrated experience with Outlook, Word, Power Point, and Excel</li>
<li>Experience with Salesforce CRM preferred</li>
<li>Experience with Scarborough Sports Marketing &amp; Neilsen Repucom preferred</li>
<li>Exceptional interpersonal and communication skills and adhere to highest ethical standards</li>
<li>Ability to work non-traditional hours</li>
</ul>
