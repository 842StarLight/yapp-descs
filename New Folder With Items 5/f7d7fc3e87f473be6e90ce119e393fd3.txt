<div>
<div>We’re looking for a driven and passionate <b>Brand Manager</b> to join us in changing people’s lives for the better. Your mission, if you choose to accept it: The Brand Manager is responsible for owning and leading the traditional and digital marketing strategy and campaign development, execution, budget and analysis for the Isopure (including Nature’s Best) and ABB brands. This means you are a strategic, creative, driven, entrepreneurial and marketing-savvy expert that has the ability to influence and lead the creation of breakthrough branded content and control the media investment and strategy to drive brand growth and push for excellence. Think you got what it takes?</div>
<div>Isopure® is all about formulating high-quality, premium, and effective sports nutrition products for people who aspire to be the best they can be so they can enjoy life and inspire others. We are for weight trainers, runners, rock climbers, mothers, fathers, busy professionals - just to name a few. We make the absolute finest fuel to help you conquer your daily obstacles and reach your fitness and health goals. We aim for the highest standards. Beyond quality. Formulas with taste.</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div>ABB has over three decades of proven performance where it matters most: At your gym. The sports nutrition products we offer, from our legendary originals to our latest innovations, stand for taste, convenience, and results. We set the bar in sports nutrition.<br>
<br>
It's a great time to join our team! We have experienced turnaround double-digit growth with a ton of momentum heading into the future. We are positioned for high growth as a lifestyle brand with excellent consumer brand health scores, high celebrity and influencer appeal, strong consumption growth, breakthrough and engaging marketing campaigns and exciting new product innovation. But we need your help. Your strong performance and skillset will substantially contribute and help ensure that we keep raising the bar!</div>

</li>
</ul>
</li>
