<h2>Sales and Data Analyst</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>We are the world’s largest university press. We have both a worldwide reach and a personal understanding of local needs, partnering on the ground to adapt publications and resources to meet the individual needs of communities, societies, and countries. We partner with researches, educators, and technology partners to stay ahead of the changing ways people around the world are accessing and sharing knowledge. With a presence in more than 50 countries and millions of customers around the world, an idea launched at OUP can have global impact.</p>
<p>
To support the production of data, analysis and insights which will be utilized to drive improved performance across the Sales function. Responsible for supporting the development and curation of core data sets which are standardized and provide a holistic view of our sales performance.</p>
<p>
This will include creation of sales enablement tools and analytics packages to provide a greater understanding of customer behavior and directly influence decisions across the sales organisation to increase turnover. Also to seek to improve processes and systems wherever possible utilizing contemporary technology.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
PRINCIPAL ACCOUNTABILITIES/KEY RESULT AREAS</b></p>
<p><i>
Maximum of 10 Accountability Statements</i></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Responsible for maintaining and supporting development of all key data sets relating to sales metrics including sales pipeline analysis, customer segmentation modelling, product performance and customer up selling tools.</li>
<li>Interacts directly with the Sales department to identify key analytical opportunities that deliver valuable sales insight and actionable data.</li>
<li>Delivering end to end campaign analysis to help understand customer behavior and the effectiveness of our sales activity.</li>
<li>Provide automated insight dashboards allowing sales operations to effectively and efficiently enable self-service analytics.</li>
<li>To support the creation of core data structures to drive pricing analytics.</li>
<li>Ensuring data sets and dashboards are produced in a scalable way and are error free.</li>
<li>Support in interrogating key internal data sets to ensure sales data is cleansed, standardized and has all required fields to drive better strategic decision making.</li>
<li>Responsible for delivery of daily, weekly and monthly reporting requirements as defined by the business.</li>
<li>Support improvement in analysis, forecasting and predictive modelling to enable better business decisions.</li>
<li>Rolling out new tools and training users to ensure we embed the new ways of using data to help drive decision making.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p><b>
</b></p>
<p><b>KNOWLEDGE AND EXPERIENCE</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Minimum 1-2 years’ experience in the role of an analyst.</li>
<li>Highly numerate and experienced in the development and maintaining of complex data structures.</li>
<li>Ability to translate data into clear and concise actionable recommendations whilst bringing stakeholders along the data journey.</li>
<li>Experience of cleansing and preparing data with the ability to query using MS SQL.</li>
<li>Microsoft Excel – advanced level</li>
<li>Excellent knowledge of MS SQL and Salesforce</li>
<li>Experience of using Data Visualization software (Tableau, Microsoft PowerBI or QlikView) and modern BI tools (eg, Alteryx).</li>
<li>Resilience, persistence and a willingness to drive issues to a successful conclusion.</li>
<li>Adaptable – focused upon improving business processes and delivering change through the provision of Data &amp; Analytics.</li>
<li>Proven ability to deliver root cause analysis and troubleshoot data &amp; data related processes.</li>
<li>Alteryx Designer Core certified – desirable</li>
<li>Tableau Desktop Fundamentals certified – desirable</li>
<li>com certified – desirable</li>
</ul>
