<h2>Oncology Senior Manager, Global Forecasting</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<b>Site Name:</b> USA - Pennsylvania - Upper Providence<br>
<b>Posted Date:</b> Aug 26 2019<br>
<p><b>**Eligible for Relocation</b></p>
<p></p>
<p>
The Senior Manager, Global Immuno-Oncology Forecaster is an integral member of a high-performing team accountable for developing long-range forecasts for early and late-stage pipeline assets as well as valuation of potential licensing and acquisition targets to drive strategic planning, resource investment, and product planning decisions.</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Key Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Insure accuracy and methodological appropriateness of forecasts</li>
<li>
Insure forecasts address the impact of critical events, are logically designed and well documented, and represent consensus decision making among key stakeholders</li>
<li>
Insures timely completion of regularly scheduled and ad hoc deliverables</li>
<li>
Understands forecast key sales drivers, risks and opportunities. Keeps abreast of news which could impact on forecast drivers, e.g. industry and competitive intelligence, analyst reports, company earnings calls, etc.</li>
<li>
Presents forecasts to internal customers, including VP Marketing, and Business Unit Heads, using effective oral, written, presentation, and data analysis skills.</li>
<li>
Develops strong working relationships and regular productive communication with business partners/stakeholders: Global Brand management, Analytics, Finance, GMS, Business Development, and Corporate.</li>
<li>
Analyzes, evaluates, and recommends most appropriate techniques to use for developing a forecast– epidemiology, demand study, or analog based.</li>
<li>
Evaluates and recommends most appropriate sources for forecast model inputs, including epidemiology sources, prescription and prescriber databases, primary and secondary research, and scientific literature.</li>
<li>
Guides the design of required market research studies to validate forecast assumptions, architecture, and patient populations.</li>
<li>
Identifies, analyzes, applies and adopts a variety of analogs, benchmarks and order of entry models to formulate forecast assumptions.</li>
<li>
Contributes ad-hoc projects to provide strategic insights to product management and/or sales forecasting</li>
</ul>
