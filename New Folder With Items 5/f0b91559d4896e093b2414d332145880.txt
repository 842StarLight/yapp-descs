<p>This is a full-time or part-time, seasonal position.</p>
<p>North America's Largest Franchised Lawn Care Company continues their successful growth and now is the perfect time for you to join our Amazing Team. For over 40 years, Weed Man has been providing exceptional lawn care service to its customers. Together with the greatest products, service, and employees in the world, we will continue to be America's choice when it comes to selecting a Lawn Care Professional. Come to work with your passionate, enthusiastic attitude, and you'll experience the thrill of being a part of a winning team and you'll be rewarded with many opportunities for personal growth!</p>
<p>We are looking for highly motivated, energetic and goal driven individuals to join our Inside Sales team. We focus on promoting our brand utilizing a blend of sales strategies that will ensure a positive experience and provide a high level of customer service.</p>
<p>Sales representatives will be responsible to follow-up with pre-qualified leads generated daily from our outside lead generation team, radio advertising, referrals, social media, and website. There's no "cold calling". All leads are pre-generated. On-going training and coaching will help you reach a high level of success and earnings.</p>
