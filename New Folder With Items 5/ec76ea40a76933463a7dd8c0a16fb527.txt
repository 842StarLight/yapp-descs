<h2>Manager/Associate Client Director - US Retail Collaboration</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">Manager/Associate Client Director - US Retail Collaboration - 40964
<br><br>
Client Services and Sales - USA Chicago, Illinois
<br><br>
Have you ever walked down an aisle in your local retailer and wondered why your favorite beverage or food product moved? At Nielsen, we work with U.S. retailers like Walmart and Sam’s to help them understand their consumers, and to optimize their business down every aisle. On the Nielsen Retail Team we work face-to-face with major retail players and use Nielsen solutions to help answer questions such as finding the right assortment and guiding pricing strategies. Every day brings a new challenge when working in the fast-paced retail environment.
<br><br>
The Client Manager/Associate Client Director is responsible for sales activities for assigned clients/opportunities. Sales activities may include identifying new opportunities, preparing materials for client sales meetings, conducting client sales meetings, managing next steps for opportunities, supporting closure of sales, timing of deals, and deployment &amp; support of sales assets. This position will partner with regional sales teams and the Walmart Collaboration team to execute sales plans and achieve sales targets set for the team.
<br><br>
As a Client Manager/Associate Client Director you will be responsible for:
<br><br>
Supporting development of a Sales Plan for assigned clients/sales target
<br>Achieving the teams’ sales target by managing disciplined action plan for opportunities you support
<br>Supporting the team on updating opportunities to have current status of opportunity pipeline
<br>Conducting an assessment of team’s opportunities to recommend prioritized list of high impact opportunities
<br>Conducting an assessment of wins/losses and provide a recommendation on how to adjust selling approach
<br>Building supporting materials for client proposals
<br>Maintaining an understanding of client stakeholders and building client relationships with key players
<br>Assisting existing clients with training and support to ensure renewal of collaboration products and services
<br>Transferring client feedback and market desires to product teams in support of solution enhancements
<br>Monitoring client contracts to ensure correct and timely delivery of services and contract renewal timelines
<br>Full understanding of Nielsen portfolio and inter-connectivity of different solutions &amp; collaboration assets including, but not limited to, Walmart One Version of Truth, Walmart Retail Beats, Sam’s Club MADRiD and Walmart Clustering
<br>Working collaboratively with Walmart Center of Excellence, Client Headquarter &amp; Walmart Retail teams to ensure all work is connected to client business issues, is delivered on-time, and focused on outcomes
<br><br>
