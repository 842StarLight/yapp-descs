<h2>Senior Account Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>DESCRIPTION:</b></h2>
<p><b>Senior Account Manager</b> to increase Fortigo's customer base and increase revenue.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>RESPONSIBILITIES:</b></h2>
<ul>
<li>Develop customer account strategies and tactical penetration plans.</li>
<li>Develop new customer relationships.</li>
<li>Develop compelling value propositions based on ROI cost/benefit analysis.</li>
<li>Execute against the major steps of the sales process:
<ul>
<li>Find new business opportunities</li>
<li>Qualify new opportunities</li>
<li>Close new business.</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>Serve as a focal point for customer services issue escalation.</li>
<li>Manage closure of escalated customer support issue.</li>
<li>Sell against quarterly and annual revenue targets for subscription and services.</li>
<li>Maintain high levels of customer satisfaction and loyalty with customers.</li>
<li>Manage to detail regarding the customer account contact management information through the entire sales life cycle process.</li>
</ul>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>KNOWLEDGE/SKILLS REQUIRED:</b></h2>
<ul>
<li>Experience and success in selling high value, mid to large company solutions ($125K and above and 3-6 months in duration for individual transactions).</li>
<li>Experience and success in selling high value, long lead time enterprise solutions ($250K and above and 6-12 months in duration for individual transactions).</li>
<li>Experience and success selling implementation consulting.</li>
<li>Proven sales quota attainment track record ($2M annual quota and above).</li>
<li>Proven new business development skills.</li>
<li>Outstanding presentation, facilitation, communication and negotiation skills.</li>
<li>Outstanding customer-focused Account Management skills.</li>
<li>Knowledge and understanding of Logistics industry pain points and related cost drivers is preferred.</li>
<li>Knowledge and understanding of Software-as-a-Service solutions.</li>
<li>High comfort level and presence with senior executives.</li>
<li>Extensive travel required.</li>
<li>Bachelor's degree required.</li>
<li>Experience with Microsoft Office (Excel, Word, PowerPoint) required.</li>
<li>Experience with Salesforce and Google Apps is preferred.</li>
</ul>
<p>Forward your resume and cover letter to jobs@fortigo.com. <i>Please do not call the office.</i> Relocation expenses are not covered by Fortigo.</p>
