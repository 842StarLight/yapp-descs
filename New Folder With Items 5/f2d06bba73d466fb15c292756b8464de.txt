<p>Intralox L.L.C is currently seeking a Technical Support Specialist with the Technical Support Group and is looking for individuals who have strong mechanical/technical aptitude and can provide world class Customer Service and support to Intralox Customers for our Latin America Market.</p>

<p><b>
Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Provide technical assistance to Customer Service, Account Managers and Customers remotely by phone, written correspondence and/or via web-conferencing.</li>
<li>Make industry specific product recommendations.</li>
<li>Remote troubleshooting and correction of any problem installations or applications.</li>
<li>Technical evaluations of conveying systems.</li>
<li>Analyze conveyor design and make recommendations</li>
<li>Run Engineering analysis using proprietary software.</li>
<li>Provide technical training</li>
<li>Utilize telephone sales and support skills to create interest and offer value-added products and services to customers.</li>
<li>Build and expand customer relationships and help ensure replacement and increased business.</li>
<li>Moderate travel for training purposes</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Requirements</b></p>
<ul>
<li>BS degree in Mechanical or Industrial Engineering or a minimum of 3 years experience in an industrial sales/support or industrial equipment specification role.</li>
<li>Strong mechanical aptitude and ability to conceptualize and articulate mechanical concepts</li>
<li>Excellent verbal and written communication skills.</li>
<li>Computer proficiency with demonstrated experience in MS Office (Excel &amp; PowerPoint), and CAD; experience with developing programs and writing code a plus</li>
<li>Fluent in English and Spanish (written and verbal)</li>
</ul>
