<h2>Clinical Program Consultant (National Accounts Northeast Market) (60926BR)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<b>Description:</b><br>
<p>Excellent opportunity for an experienced clinician with a passion for consulting to leverage clinical, analytic and consultative skills to join our team of Clinical Program Consultants (CPC) who offer clinical insights and solutions to help improve health plan performance for our plan sponsors. Highly collaborative position and professional environment working with account teams and customers, Aetna's clinical operation teams and our Plan Sponsor Insights team of consultants.<br>
<br>
Clinical Program Consultants are key members of the Plan Sponsor Insights (PSI) business - a business focused on providing insights and solutions based on analytics to customers in the group commercial business. The work environment supports and thrives on out-of-the box thinking, creative talent and a consultative approach. Our team is highly valued by our internal partners as well as the customers we serve.<br>
