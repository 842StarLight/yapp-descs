<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Position Description<br>
</b></h2>
<div></div>
<div><div><div>
<div><b>At a Glance</b></div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
<b>
Legrand </b>has an exciting opportunity for a Buyer/ Planner to join the <b>Electrical Wiring Systems Team</b> in <b>Concord, NC</b>. The successfull candidate will perform the planning/purchasing for all purchased content and assist with the planning of all finished items and independent sub-assemblies in one of the production value streams.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div><b>
What Will You Do?</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
Maintain effective inventories of purchased material and work in process to support assembly operations</li>
<li>
Communicate with CSR’s about lead times and production inquiries</li>
<li>
Lead and assist in material storage initiatives</li>
<li>
Monitor supplier deliveries and take corrective action as required</li>
<li>
Coordinate forecast changes and deviations with the Forecast Manager, Forecast Analyst and/or marketing team.</li>
<li>
Use capacity planning tools to make recommendations to the SIOP Manager and Value Stream Manager about OT or production changes</li>
<li>
Monitor production to plan and take corrective action where necessary.</li>
<li>
Monitor and control engineering deviations and new product</li>
</ul>
</div></div></div>
