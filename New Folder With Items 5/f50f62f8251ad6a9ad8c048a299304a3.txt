<p><b>About NCR
</b><br>
NCR Corporation (NYSE: NCR) is the global leader in consumer transaction technologies, turning everyday interactions with businesses into exceptional experiences. With its software, hardware, and portfolio of services, NCR enables nearly 700 million transactions daily across retail, financial, travel, hospitality, telecom and technology, and small business. NCR solutions run the everyday transactions that make your life easier. NCR is headquartered in Atlanta, Georgia, with nearly 30,000 employees and does business in 180 countries.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Title: Product Manager Emerald US
</b></p>
<p><b>Location: Atlanta, GA
</b></p>
<p><b>Grade: 13
</b></p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Position Summary </b>.
</p>
<p>As a Product Manager with Digital First Retail, you will be responsible for roadmap planning, development and delivery for the next generation Emerald Point of Sale product. The Emerald POS product is a modern and open “Platform of Sale” combining the power of world class enterprise POS system integrated with cloud business services and APIs to truly deliver a frictionless experience for the retailer and their customers. It is one of the key strategic growth products that is focused on Retail grocery segment within the broader Food, Drug and Mass Merchandise (FDMM) retail market.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Key responsibilities:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Define product roadmap and business requirements required to meet both market and customer needs.
</li>
<li>Collaborate very closely with key stakeholders like Portfolio/Product management, Engineering, UX and other cross functional teams to understand product strategy and translate market requirements into more specific product level features.
</li>
<li>Manage product backlog, priorities and release plans for the product you own. Ensure interlock with both Product and Engineering stakeholders.
</li>
<li>Lead the continuous enhancement and delivery of new product functionality by working closely with cross-functional development teams in an Agile manner
</li>
<li>Act as the voice of the customer and business for Engineering and other technical teams so that they are aligned to the overall product vision and priorities
</li>
<li>Work closely with other product managers within the portfolio to manage dependencies and define product release requirements across your product line.
</li>
<li>Represent NCR as needed at industry events including customer advisory boards, user groups and other forums.
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Basic Qualifications
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree or Master’s degree in Technology or Business
</li>
<li>8-10 years of experience overall, with expertise in Retail store systems &amp; processes
</li>
<li>Proven track-record in managing a successful product
</li>
<li>Willingness to work with cross-functional, offshore and onshore teams
</li>
<li>Excellent communication skills – Presentation, Verbal and Written
</li>
<li>This role would require around 20% travel
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Preferred Qualifications
</b></p>

<li>Master’s in Business Administration (MBA)
</li>
<li>Pragmatic or other product management certifications
</li>
<li>Understanding and knowledge of the Retail grocery market
</li>
<li>Openness to take additional responsibilities and stretch assignments
</li>
</ul>
