<h2>Sr Director, Ad Operations</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>ConsumerTrack™ is unique in the digital marketing and media industry - we combine marketing, digital, content and fintech. Our performance based approach increases brand awareness and generates targeted audience engagement on our internal web properties and partner sites.</p>
<p>
Learn More About What We Do</p>
<p>
ConsumerTrack is looking for a highly-collaborative, sharp Ad Ops leader to drive the business forward. The Senior Director of Advertising Operations works with internal teams (Sales, Media, Analytics, Design, Tech, and Product) and external groups (Publishers, Agencies, and Clients) to lead and scale the end to end ad management process: sales planning, order management, trafficking, campaign management, inventory management, media ops and final reporting. This role manages Ad Trafficking &amp; Campaign Management, Media &amp; Sales support, and Technical Ad Ops. The ideal candidate is extremely detail-oriented, process-driven, and a master at follow-up and follow-through.</p>
<p>
The Senior Director of Ad Ops will lead ad ops innovation, revenue growth, and process improvement efforts. This person is a solutions-oriented problem-solver who isn’t afraid to get hands-on to reach the best answer or resolve an issue. This role sits at the crossroads of supply, demand and data, which will make this role central to the development of innovative ad tech solutions.</p>
<p>
Responsibilities:
</p>
<p>Leadership:
</p>
<ul>
<li>Lead, manage, and train the Ad Operations department, setting and measuring quarterly department goals.
</li>
<li>Recruit and on-board high performing individuals to improve and grow the team.
</li>
<li>Constantly assess and optimize processes to improve performance, quality of work and timeliness while also ensuring we align to IAB standards.
</li>
<li>Coach team to think ahead about potential roadblocks to meet campaign performance goals and work with stakeholders to preemptively address issues.
</li>
<li>Create positive, solutions focused department culture through high communication and collaboration, continuous training and recognition of success.
</li>
</ul>
<p>Process</p>
<ul>
<li>Maintain optimized Ad Taxonomy for our O&amp;O sites to allow Data and Analytics teams to maximize data for reporting and automation.
</li>
<li>Build discipline to look at all initiatives as opportunities for automation, scale and improvement.
</li>
<li>Build processes and train teams to empower individuals, limit human error and evangelize team knowledge on all context around campaigns, on-boarding, opportunities and department goals.</li>
</ul>
<p>Client and Campaign Growth:</p>
<ul>
<li>Work with Analytics and cross-functional team to optimize campaign performance and profitability.
</li>
<li>Manage Sales Planning, ensuring successful client onboarding, tracking, asset management, forecasting, budget fulfillment, compliance and approval tracking.
</li>
<li>Provide team oversight to guarantee accurate campaign set up, trafficking, campaign optimization and data output.
</li>
<li>Partner with Media team to manage publisher relationships, supporting publisher onboarding, forecasting, inventory management, and campaign performance.
</li>
</ul>
<p>Technical</p>
<ul>
<li>In house expert in ad tracking and tagging. Work with ad ops tech lead to facilitate conversations with clients and publishers around tags, pixels and tracking. Educate internal teams and evangelize best practices for tracking and troubleshooting.
</li>
<li>Own Incident Management, advising on business impact and incident resolutions</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Requirements</b></p>
<ul>
<li>10+ years’ experience in digital Ad Operations working with a variety of ad serving and data technologies
</li>
<li>8+ years building and managing a team handling high-volume digital campaign operations (Onsite and remote teams), including direct hiring responsibilities.
</li>
<li>Highly detail oriented, obsessed with accuracy, a strong critical thinker and problem solver, and able to juggle and project manage multiple projects/clients
</li>
<li>Strong experience across digital platforms – Including multiple ad serving platforms (Google Ad Manager, Google Campaign Manager, Sizmek, etc), viewability &amp; verification vendors (IAS, DoubleVerify, etc.), Tag Management Systems (GTM, Adobe, etc.) and programmatic (DMPs, DSPs, Exchanges)
</li>
<li>Expert-level understanding of performance-based digital advertising campaigns (CPA/CPC, Mobile/desktop, display and native) and familiar with post-click attribution tracking and optimizations.
</li>
<li>Experience using proprietary or custom tools and adept at learning new systems quickly while providing feedback for system improvements.
</li>
<li>Technical experience setting up and troubleshooting different tracking solutions: pixels, ad tags, rich media integrations, query string parameters, etc. Ability to explain complex and technical issues to varied audiences, both internal and external.
</li>
<li>Experience making operational decisions based off financial analysis. Some experience in Finance or Analytics is a plus.
</li>
<li>Proficient in basic HTML, data structures, and Advanced MS Excel.
</li>
<li>Experience working in Salesforce. Salesforce Admin experience a plus.</li>
</ul>
