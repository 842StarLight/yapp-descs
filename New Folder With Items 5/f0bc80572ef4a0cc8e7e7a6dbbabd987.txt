<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Position Overview:</b></h2>
<p>
The Assistant Manager assists the Store Manager in maximizing sales and net profit through effective management, operational, and financial methods. You will assist in training and certification of all Cashiers and the Lead Cashier. While always ensuring company standards are upheld, the Assistant Manager builds, coaches, manages and develops teams from a staffing, interviewing, hiring and training standpoint. Responsibilities also include providing prompt, efficient, effective and courteous quality service to the customer, as well as maintain the conditions of the store during the work shift up to the quality and safety standards set by the Company. The Assistant Mgr can be called upon to assume Store Mgr responsibilities.</p>

<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
About Maverik:</b></h2>
<p>
Maverik was founded by Reuel Call in Afton, Wyo., in 1928, and has grown to over 320 stores in 12 states to become the largest independent convenience store chain in the Inter-mountain West. Maverik currently employs nearly 6000 Adventure Guides and has ambitious growth plans, including expansion into markets west of the Mississippi. As Adventure’s First Stop, Maverik lives up to its purpose to build the coolest convenience-experience on the planet.</p>
