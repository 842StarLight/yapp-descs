<h2>Customer Service Representative</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>DryZone LLC, a regional leader in crawlspace, basement, concrete lifting and foundation repair industry, is seeking a positive, energetic individual to join its team of Appointment Center Representatives. The role of the Appointment Center Representative is to schedule sales and maintenance appointments for our salespeople and service technicians.</p>
<p>
 DryZone’s purpose is to redefine the construction industry by pushing beyond the status quo and providing our customers with a remarkable experience.</p>
<p>
If you pride yourself in providing exceptional customer service, and want to be valued for what you bring to the team, apply today to join us as our Appointment Center Representative.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
Job Duties</p>
<p>
 Proactively represent our company primarily through telephone contact by answering calls, setting service appointments, targeting inactive accounts in order to generate new sales opportunities, and following up with homeowners on various issues</p>
<p>
Provide tactical support to other departments of the company</p>
<p>
Keep homeowner records and database current</p>
<p>
Coordinate paperwork for our Service Team</p>
<p>
Provide homeowners with a remarkable experience</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
Job Requirements</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Demonstrable experience in telephone sales or related field</li>
<li>Customer service focused – understands the need, solves problems, and responds in a timely and efficient manner</li>
<li>Exceptional telephone and interpersonal skills</li>
<li>Self-motivated and energetic</li>
<li>Ability to make a large number of calls to prospective clients per day and even with constant rejection, have the persistence and positive attitude to continue in these efforts every day</li>
<li>Organizational skills – ability to manage time and multi-task</li>
<li>Ability to attend to tasks with accuracy in detail</li>
<li>Proficiency in Microsoft Office computer applications and ability to navigate internal software programs</li>
<li>Ability to pass pre-employment drug screen and background check</li>
<br>
</ul>
