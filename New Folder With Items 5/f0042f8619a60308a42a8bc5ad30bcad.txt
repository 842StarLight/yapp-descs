</li>
<li>Possesses strong credibility at a client and/or within Accenture, which involves negotiating or influencing on significant matters
</li>
<li>Comfortable with fast paced projects and high-pressure settings
</li>
<li>Has the flexibility to be capable of working independently at a client or within Accenture.
</li>
<li>Ability to enter new client environments and produce results through the use of practical skills that are easily transported between different industries.</li>
</ul>
