<p>Responsibilities:</p>
<p>As an Account Representative, you will collaborate with business owners to create online marketing solutions. You will be accountable for achieving and exceeding sales goals.</p>
<p>
Additional responsibilities:</p>
<ul>
<li>Prospect for new business</li>
<li>Build and maintain client base</li>
<li>Explain complex technical concepts such as SEM, SEO, Online Display, etc.</li>
<li>Use iPads to present solutions</li>
<li>Work in a virtual environment</li>
</ul>

<p>Requirements:</p>
<p>From bonuses to promotions, we reward performance! Ready to take control of your paycheck and career? We’re looking for a go-getter who will become a media expert on all hibu products. Someone who’s not scared to pound the pavement, network and get involved in the community. If that’s you, we want to hear from you!</p>
<p>
Account Representative requirements:</p>
<ul>
<li>2+ years of sales, marketing, or advertising experience</li>
<li>Strong understanding of SEM, SEO, Online Display, and Social marketing</li>
<li>Proficient use of Microsoft Office, including Power Point, Excel, and Outlook</li>
<li>Technically savvy and proficient user of the iPad</li>
<li>Strong work ethic and highly motivated</li>
<li>Self-disciplined to work independently</li>
<li>Excellent time-management and organizational skills</li>
<li>Ability to walk up to 5 miles per day, carry 25 pounds, sit or drive 2-4 hours, and stand 1-2 hours per day</li>
<li>Successful completion of a drug screen and background check</li>
<li>Reliable, insured transportation in most markets</li>
<li>Valid state driver’s license</li>
<br>
</ul>
