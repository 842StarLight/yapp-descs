<p><b>LOCATIONS WITH POSSIBLE OPENINGS: Albany, Buffalo, Syracuse, Rochester</b></p>
<p>Our clients range from one person boutique firms to large companies with thousands of employees. The mission of our team is to put the proper engineering tools in the hands of our customers so they can realize a shorter time to market, and a reduction in design, manufacturing, and overall operational costs.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>CADimensions, Inc., a SOLIDWORKS Reseller in Upstate New York, has an immediate need for a Data Management (DM) Support Engineer. A DM Support Engineer is responsible for implementations, training, and support activities. They must have the ability to troubleshoot and support the DM product. Ability to troubleshoot basic Windows functions relating to the installation of WPDM &amp; EPDM. Must attain CEPA &amp; CSWS-D certifications.</p>

<p><b>COMPANY INTRODUCTION: </b>CADimensions, Inc. was founded in 1990 in Upstate NY as a Value Added Reseller (VAR) for the CAD/CAM, FEA, and DM industries. CADimensions provides sales, technical support, training, and applications development for the engineering, manufacturing, and educational markets in New York, Vermont, Western Massachusetts and Pennsylvania. In addition to its headquarters in East Syracuse, New York, CADimensions, Inc. also has branch offices in NY Metro, Albany, Rochester, Buffalo, and Philadelphia, Pittsburgh and Erie PA.</p>
<p>Our mission is to put the proper engineering tools in the hands of our customers, so they can realize a shorter time to market, and a reduction in design, manufacturing, and overall operational costs. CADimensions, Inc. has been a SOLIDWORKS reseller since 1995 and is a certified Stratasys and Fortus reseller since 2003.</p>
