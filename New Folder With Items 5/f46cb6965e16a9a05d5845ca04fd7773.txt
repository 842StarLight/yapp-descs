IoT Quality Manager</b></p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Functional Overview:</b><br>
Currently, we are seeking qualified candidates for an Internet of Things (IoT) Quality Manager opening to join our GIS - Global Information Systems organization. This role reports up through the GIS-GPO IoT Director and will have a primary focus on leading Delivery Operations as it relates to Quality Management, Strategic Execution, and General Quality Assurance. This position will be responsible for representing IT among all the IOT Quality elements (Marketing, Global Consumer Design, Legal, Product Firmware, Product Engineering and Delivery, Information Systems), to ensure product quality for the consumer.<br>
<br>
<b>Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
This role reports up through the IT Global Product Organization Leadership with dotted line accountability to the Global Quality Organization and will have a primary focus on leading Quality Practices as it relates to Software and Application Quality Management, Strategic Execution of Quality initiatives, and General Quality Assurance</li>
<li>
Lead and provide governance over the Quality Assurance activities associated with the Global IoT Platform, Mobile Applications, and Connected Product Features (i.e. Consumer Quality Metrics, Product Performance, drive resolution of critical quality product issues, Pre Product Launch Quality Assurance).</li>
<li>
Work closely with IOT Operations Team to ensure quality is part of Change, Incident Management. This includes working closely with the Operations Manager to develop and execute a quality driven test strategy.</li>
<li>
Evaluate, recommend and implement best practices and technologies that will drive high quality digital deliverables across the IoT value chain</li>
<li>
Share accountability to manage outsourcing partners as well as internal teams, and foster active collaboration across various stakeholders</li>
<li>
Working Knowledge of new technologies, trends, processes and industry strategies in order to drive best in class digital product quality.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
</b><br>
