<p>
With more than $2 billion in revenues, CDK Global is a leading global provider of integrated information technology and digital marketing solutions to the automotive retail and adjacent industries. Focused on enabling end-to-end automotive commerce, CDK provides solutions to dealers in more than 100 countries around the world, serving approximately 28,000 retail locations and most automotive manufacturers. CDK Global solutions automate and integrate critical processes from pre-sale targeted advertising to the sale, financing, insurance, parts supply, repair and maintenance of vehicles, with an increasing focus on utilizing data analytics and predictive intelligence.</p>

<div>
The <b>Director, Implementation </b>is responsible for the successful implementation of products for all competitive takeaway clients (New Name Unhooks and competitive Buy/Sells) by providing leadership and direction to an integrated national Implementation team, including effective management of staff and resources, services, and solutions that will ensure achievement of timely and effective implementations with a high level of customer satisfaction.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
Provides strategic direction and expert project management knowledge while making decisions and solving complex problems to ensure the right products are sold in partnership with sales and installation of backlog.</div>
<ul>
<li>
Responsible for the integration of implementation activities for all new Unhooks and competitive (non-CDK) buy/sells for all of the U.S. and Canada; Responsible for client satisfaction, Net Promoter Score (NPS) and issue resolution for new client portfolio.<br>
</li>
<li>
Maintains a DAILY rigorous focus on implementation backlog ensuring hands on detail knowledge of project in his/her regions – ensures projects are scheduled quickly and that projects on hold are escalated quickly.<br>
</li>
<li>
Is responsible for running successful discovery on all client installations ensuring accurate inputs are captures and proper expectations set with the client.<br>
</li>
<li>
Partners cross functionally as required, to ensure the delivery of world-class implementation services for CDK’s client. This includes coordination with the regional Implementation teams to ensure a smooth transition of the client from the Unhook team to the baseline region teams.<br>
</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>
Partners and collaborates with New Business (Unhook) sales organization to increase market share and reference-able client base.<br>
</li>
<li>
Identifies and mitigates high-level risks across the team and implementation projects; Manage and diffuse escalations<br>
</li>
<li>
Responsible for review and approval of proposed business, starting with sales discovery phase through resource estimates to ensure delivery of effective and efficient solutions; Allocates resources based on the project demand<br>
</li>
<li>
Accountable for making tactical day-to-day and long term strategic decision and activities performed by the team<br>
</li>
<li>
Responsible for ensuring adherence to Sarbanes Oxley guidelines<br>
</li>
<li>
Develops a highly skilled team of Client Implementation Managers; The Client Implementation Manager will be a new role combining the responsibilities of the traditionally separate Project Manager/Specialist and Client Manager roles. Responsible for hiring, developing associates, building bench strength, monitoring performance, driving associate retention, and delivering performance reviews.</li>
</ul>
