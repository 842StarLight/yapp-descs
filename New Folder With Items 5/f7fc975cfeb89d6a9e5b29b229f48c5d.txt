<h2>Technical Finance Project Manager / Business Analyst</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div></div>
<div>
<div>Invesco is one of the world’s leading global investment managers, entrusted with managing $1.2 trillion* in assets on behalf of clients worldwide. We are the 6th largest US retail asset manager and the 13th largest investment manager globally, and our more than 8,000 employees worldwide are dedicated to delivering an investment experience that helps people get more out of life. We are purely focused on managing a comprehensive range of active, passive and alternative investment capabilities, which we draw on to provide customized solutions aligned to client needs, our most important benchmark. (*As of May 31,2019)</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div>
<b>
Job Purpose (Job Summary):</b>
</div>
<div>The primary role of the Business Analyst position is to review, analyze, and evaluate technology projects that Invesco has deemed important to the success of achieving its business goals. Provide efficient and cost-effective software systems and/or business processes to facilitate the design, testing, and implementation in accordance with company methodology. Document business requirements, define scope, write functional specifications and formulate systems to parallel with overall business strategies. This position requires strong business analytics, communication, and critical thinking skills. It is a position that is important and unique for it faces both internal business clients and technology professionals assisting with implementing tools and processes to achieve viable, appropriate, and sometimes market leading solutions to business strategies.<br>
</div>
<div>
<b>
Key Responsibilities / Duties</b><b>:</b>
</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
</div>
<ul>
<li>
Business Analyst working in financial systems environment with project management skills.</li>
<li>
Work with EPM (Enterprise Performance Management) software EPBCS, PCMCS and interact with FP&amp;A.
</li>
<li>Analyze and write new business requirements and/or enhancements to existing Invesco financial systems and / or business processes.</li>
<li>
Clearly document solutions and recommendations that aligns with Invesco future operating model.</li>
<li>
Write functional specifications and work closely with IT development &amp; support teams.</li>
<li>
Create cost and time estimates along with project plans to enable prioritization and execution.</li>
<li>
Assist and/or coordinate user testing, ensuring issues are resolved in a pragmatic and appropriate manner.</li>
<li>
Work as part of a project team, reporting progress and escalating issues to project management in a timely manner to ensure successful completion of projects &amp; reviews.</li>
<li>
Adopt Agile and adhere to standards set by the Invesco project methodology.</li>
<li>
Take a major role on small, medium or larger projects.</li>
<li>
Assist with developing improvements to Invesco technology systems, processes and procedures.</li>
<li>
Participate in team improvement sessions on team mentoring, training, and communication.</li>
<li>
Keep abreast of current development and trends in financial systems and processes through networking, continuing education, and industry/technology publications.</li>
<li>
Communicate effectively via phone, email or in person. Interface with clients at all levels.</li>
<li>
Functional – Technical responsibilities within the Finance EPM team.<br>
</li>
</ul>
