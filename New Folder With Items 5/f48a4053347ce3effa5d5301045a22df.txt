<h2>Product Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>
<div>Cape Analytics uses AI and geospatial imagery to provide instant property intelligence for buildings across the United States. Cape Analytics enables insurers and other property stakeholders to access valuable property attributes at time of underwriting—with the accuracy and detail that traditionally required an on-site inspection, but with the speed and coverage of property record pre-fill. Founded in 2014, Cape Analytics is backed by leading venture firms and innovative insurers, and comprised of computer vision and risk analysis experts.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
</div>
<div><b>
Product Manager</b></div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
As Product Manager, you will own strategy, roadmap, and execution of the Cape product line for a key vertical market. You will use your expertise in building and shipping data-focused enterprise products to advance Cape’s property intelligence product line, building on strong momentum to grow the business.</div>
</div>
<div><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>What you'll do:</b></h3>
<div><ul>
<li>
Drive strategy, roadmap, and execution of Cape’s data and analytics product line for commercial property insurers, mortgage lenders, and/or real estate investors</li>
<li>
Develop a deep understanding of customer pain points, use cases, and workflows by working directly with Cape’s current and prospective customers</li>
<li>
Team with data scientists, machine learning researchers, and software engineers to design and prioritize solutions to critical customer problems</li>
<li>
Drive execution of the product roadmap by clearly defining and communicating product requirements and priorities and ensuring that development teams have the critical inputs to ship high quality features on time</li>
<li>
Partner with Sales, Marketing, and Customer Success on positioning, competitive landscape, key deals, customer onboarding, and quantifying return on investment</li>
</ul></div>
</div></div>
<div><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>What you'll need:</b></h3>
<div><ul>
<li>
4+ years of enterprise data or software product management experience</li>
<li>
Excellent skills and talent for communications in all forms, formal and informal—spoken, written, visual, presentations</li>
<li>
Ability to think strategically and execute tactically</li>
<li>
Proven history of successfully managing and delivering, large cross-functional projects on time and with high quality, including experience with agile software development</li>
<li>
Strong technical competence and depth of experience with data science and/or machine learning</li>
<li>
BA/BS/MBA etc. in a technical domain</li>
<li>
Sense of humor, fun, and empathy for teammates and customers</li>
</ul></div>
</div></div>
