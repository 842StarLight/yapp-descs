<h2>On Air Talent</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Cox Media Group—Dayton Radio WHKO/K99.1FM is seeking an innovative and competitive on-air personality for an extremely rare opportunity on the decades long #1 rated music station in the area as co-host for the equally long #1 rated morning show, New Country Mornings with Nancy. The next superstar to join K99.1FM must be passionate about country music, the artists, the format and be ready to be a part of creating some of the most unique content in the country. K99.1FM’s next personality lives the lifestyle, understands how to connect to a listener today and beyond, can easily connect with people on-air, in person and on social media to relate to the lifestyle in the Miami Valley area in Ohio, understands being local and relatable, the importance of serving our community with a “team first” attitude.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Responsibilities:</b></p>
<p></p>
<br>
<ul>
<li><div>
Show prep, including format related content, pop-culture content and local content; writing and posting web and social media content; writing and delivering commercial or personal endorsement content.</div></li>
<li><div>
Co-hosting regular air shifts, plus other voice-tracked shifts as assigned. Duties include working closely with program director to develop relatable content, execute format, and deliver ratings goals.</div></li>
<li><div>
Attend all relevant station meetings</div></li>
<li><div>
Must be able to work control board operation, including editing phone bits, interviews and other audio.</div></li>
<li><div>
Remotes and appearances at station, community events, or life group functions, as assigned.</div></li>
<li><div>
Must know how to set-up operate equipment and promotion gear during remote broadcasts</div></li>
<li><div>
Work with sales and promotions to maximize revenue opportunities, including promotions and personal endorsements.</div></li>
</ul>
