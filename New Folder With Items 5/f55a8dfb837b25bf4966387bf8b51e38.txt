<h2>Marketing Coordinator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader">
<b>OVERVIEW</b><b>
</b>
</h2>
<div><div><div>
<div>In an extremely fast-paced environment, creates and organizes presentation materials and proposal graphics in support of sales and marketing department, as well as graphic work for other departments and projects.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
Responsible for the creation and submittal of advertising and brochure copy material for professional printing, utilizing knowledge of design, pre-press, press, and finishing requirements. NT operating system experience, network basics including printing, and software.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
</div></div></div>
<div><div><div>
<div>
<div><b>SUPERVISORY RESPONSIBILITIES</b></div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
May provide guidance for sales support personnel on an as-needed basis for content and design.
</div>
<div>Provide guidance to any support staff to accomplish assigned tasks.
</div>
<div>Budget and scheduling for communications/design pieces.</div>
<div>
Maintain relationships with vendors for all of the printing, supply, design, and equipment needs of the marketing department.
</div>
<div>Provide technical instruction and maintenance of equipment.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div><b>
EDUCATION</b></div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
Requires a four-year degree or equivalent, preferably in Graphic Communications, Graphic Design, or Multi-Media. Knowledge of the A/E industry is a plus.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
<b>
Q</b><b>UALIFICATIONS AND EXPERIENCE</b>
</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
Need at least five (5) years of high-end project/proposal experience in a fast-paced, on-demand marketing environment. Experience with printed process and all accompanying aspects of that process. Experience working within the engineering industry and knowledge of proposal process is preferred. Should be graphically inventive and a team player with strong sales instinct. Must be proficient in Microsoft PowerPoint, PhotoShop, Illustrator, QuarkXPress, Excel, Word, Outlook, and knowledge of NT operating system and software. Experience with Premier, After Effects, CorelDraw, a plus. Must be skilled in use of animation sequences, video, and audio for electronic presentations. An understanding of GIS, AutoCAD, and Microstation, a definite plus. Must have knowledge of pre-press procedures including scanning, file types, and separations. Must have knowledge of offset lithography, screen-printing. In addition, should have some knowledge of preflighting, traps, pagination, bleeds, press page layout, and estimating, as well as finishing procedures such as trimming, folding, and binding.</div>
</div>
<div></div>

Research and development.</div></li>

Maintain and manage supplies for presentations and proposals, including paper supply, toner, transparencies, badges, etc.</div></li>
