<p><b>Summary:</b><b> </b>The Sr. Project Manager (PM) is responsible for providing basic to moderate range of project coordination from initial planning through any software development requests and all implementation processes. The person for this position exhibits leadership qualities that enable them to manage small &amp; large multi-functional and geographically distributed teams. The PM/Coordinator manages the high-level business objectives for the project and is “hands-on” with assessing requirements, end-user testing, client delivery, and the software development life cycle. While assisting with the implementation, the PM will lead the design of project plans, fine-tune clinical workflow, and help to create world-class software products and solutions for our customers. These innovative technology solutions make our customers’ lives easier and ensure that no patient falls through the cracks.</p>
<p><b>Education:</b> Bachelor’s preferred; Associates degree with project management position will be considered.
</p>
<p><b>Experience:</b> Five (5) years professional work experience managing major IT projects or working in IT positions using the organizational and professional skills used in project management.</p>
<p><b>Skills and Abilities:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Ability to adapt and respond to changes both inside and outside the projects/program</li>
<li>Ability to predict risk areas, design mitigation/contingency strategies and execute on these strategies to avoid issues</li>
<li>This strategic position collaborates routinely with management/staff with expertise in software development, quality assurance, account management, client services, finance, marketing, sales, commercial business partners, and subject matter experts from various industries. The individual in this role needs to be self-driven and have a passion for understanding how to solve problems efficiently with our health software technology solutions.</li>
</ul>
