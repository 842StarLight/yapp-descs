<h2>Distribution Sales Rep (Security/Pro AV)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>D-Link Systems, Inc. (“D-Link”) is a global leader in the design, manufacture and marketing of advanced networking, broadband, digital, voice and data communications solutions. Following our company motto, “Building Networks for People”, D-Link continually meets the global networking and connectivity needs of digital home consumers, small office professionals, small to medium-sized businesses, and enterprise environments.</p>
<p>Join our team at D-Link as our technology makes new advances and changes the world! D-Link is looking for people who enjoy challenges and cutting-edge technology. If you are looking for a culture that supports the expression of ideas, then look no further!</p>
<p><b>Job Summary and Location Information: </b></p>
<p>This position is located within the Distribution department and reports to the Director of US Distribution. Ideal candidate will be located on west coast (Southern California preferred) with ability and access to travel.</p>
<p><b>Job Responsibilities: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Maximize D-Link mind share and market share at assigned distributor accounts (Almo)</li>
<li>Manage and grow sales in the Security and Pro AV Channels through reseller and end user engagement</li>
<li>Build and manage a multi-million dollar pipeline</li>
<li>Accurately forecast expected sales quantities for D-Link products</li>
<li>Build relationships with key integrations partners to maintain and grow sales</li>
<li>Build relationships with distribution sales staff and tech support, be the brand, the face of D-Link within the accounts</li>
<li>Develop and drive promotions, bundles, and special pricing</li>
<li>Train and educate channel partners and integrators on the value of D-Link</li>
<li>Train distributor sales representatives on product and value of D-Link</li>
<li>Track sell through and report to D-Link Management</li>
<li>Facilitate opportunities for D-Link field representative interactions with resellers and Distribution Field Representatives</li>
<li>Drive solution sales and new project business back through the channel to D-Link</li>
<li>Educate resellers at distributor sponsored reseller shows</li>
<li>Engage and educate industry rep firms</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Qualifications: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>3 years’ experience in the Physical Security and or Pro AV industry</li>
<li>Experience with IT Sales</li>
<li>Strong written and verbal communication skills</li>
<li>Ability to travel as needed (50% +)</li>
<li>Experience with and relationships within A&amp;E firms preferred</li>
<li>Associates or bachelor’s degree in a related field preferred but not necessary (in lieu of relevant experience)</li>
<li>Applicant must be proficient in Microsoft excel, access, word and other office programs. Salesforce knowledge is a plus</li>
<li>Applicant must be organized and have a basic understanding of the components of a complete network solution</li>
<li>Strong in both data collection and analytics</li>
</ul>
