 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">About MIMS
<br><br>
MIMS by Arrow Labs, is an innovative field workforce management solution, designed to improve efficiencies across companies' processes, workflows and human capital. The software can be accessed across web, mobile and its smartwatch, thus allowing all levels of employees to create and update on crucial work-related matters on a daily, real-time basis. Companies deployed with MIMS consistently experience gains of up to 40% in business efficiencies and 20% in operating costs.

About Arrow Labs
<br><br>
Founded in 2011, Arrow Labs strives to transform the way companies manage field resources through smart technology wearables and services. The company’s mission is to disrupt traditional business models with solutions to manage effectively, automate processes and increase revenue on a whole new level. The systems, built on machine learning, process automation and workforce optimization technology, are designed to elevate the typical work day for the thousands of employees that engage with us.
