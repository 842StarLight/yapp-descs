<p><b>Job Description</b></p>
<p>CallPotential (CallPotential.com) is a Naperville based provider of software to help businesses with lead management, collection and performance. Our goal is to provide our customers with a giant easy button to have better processes and controls in place to run their sales and receivables operations as they continue to grow.</p>
<p>If you are sick of working at a large company where your efforts end up buried or barely noticed, come join us. Get some great experience across many different areas with the ability to see direct and immediate impact from your efforts.</p>
<p>This job is not the right fit for everyone. . .we are looking for highly motivated individuals who have excellent customer service, strong analytical abilities, are tech savvy, and are looking to gain a variety of different experiences.</p>
<p>CallPotential has been busy developing new products and offerings that have continued to make life easier for our customers. We are in need of new members of our customer support team. We have a fun, flexible work environment and try and bring in people that not only do good work but also fit our company culture. If you feel you have what it takes, drop us a line and we would love to talk.</p>
<p><b>Job Responsibilities: </b></p>
<p>As a member of our team, you will</p>
<ul>
<li>Confidently understand and navigate our software to assist and guide end users.</li>
<li>Field incoming chats and phone calls from our end users, answer questions, demonstrate proper use of our product, and ask questions to better understand possible issues.</li>
<li>Analyze information, troubleshoot issues, and help provide solutions.</li>
<li>Use SQL to access database information.</li>
<li>Reach out to customers via phone to proactively identify and solve problems.</li>
<li>Access and assist with continual update of internal knowledge base.</li>
<li>Work closely with account management team on new customer set-up.</li>
<li>Offer ideas for product updates/improvements based on interaction and feedback from our customers.</li>
<li>Utilize ticketing system for issue escalations and product requests. . .we use Zendesk.&amp; JIRA. . .don't worry, if you haven't used either, we can teach you.</li>
</ul>
