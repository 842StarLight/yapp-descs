<h2>Digital Training Manager - Consumer Sales</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<div>
<div>As a leading worldwide provider of navigation devices and wearable technology, our focus is on developing, designing and supporting superior products. Our advanced technology promotes performance, safety and ease of use in every market we serve. That makes us an engineering and technology company with a keen focus on recruiting talent in those fields. But the same vertical integration business model that keeps all design and manufacturing processes in-house also gives us the incentive to hire top performers from different backgrounds, including operations, finance and accounting, information technology, sales, marketing and communications.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
We are looking for a Digital Training Manager for our consumer sales segment. They will lead the development and delivery of instructor led and web-based training courses and curriculum to meet the needs of Garmin associates, leaders and business partners. The Digital Training Manager will apply his/her proven experience and industry best practices to design, develop and implement innovative performance support solutions including but not limited to eLearning platforms, video content development, course development and written communication and training materials.</div>
</div>
<p></p>
<div>
<b>
Qualifications</b><div>
</div>
<div>
<div>
ESSENTIAL FUNCTIONS:</div>
<div><ul>
<li><div><div>
Identifies training and development needs and develops and implements client specific solutions where needed</div></div></li>
<li><div><div>
Leverages training program certifications and course and curriculum development skills to effectively meet associate and leader professional development needs</div></div></li>
<li><div><div>
Assesses business situations and develops goals and training and development strategies to improve business results and performance</div></div></li>
<li><div><div>
Proactively identifies threats and opportunities and leverages training expertise to design creative solutions which align with business strategy</div></div></li>
<li><div><div>
Assist the Director with the development and implementation of the various training programs for the organization</div></div></li>
<li><div><div>
Utilize adult learning theories to follow an established instructional design process, including needs analysis, design, storyboarding, and development with iterative review cycles.</div></div></li>
<li><div><div>
Conduct quality reviews of current learning programs to assess the need for modifications to content and structure/format and to ensure courses are current, effective, and meet business needs.</div></div></li>
<li><div><div>
Work collaboratively and be willing to mentor team members to teach best practices in instructional design concepts and adult learning theory. Participate in and/or lead multiple project teams.</div></div></li>
<li><div><div>
Develops content for eLearning, video and written training.</div></div></li>
<li><div><div>
Proactively coaches’ leaders on leadership practices and techniques and develops coaching skills in others</div></div></li>
<li><div><div>
Develops new partnerships and manages relationships with training vendors to maximize value to the organization</div></div></li>
<li><div><div>
Applies a sense of urgency, commitment and focus on the right priorities by developing solutions in a timely manner</div></div></li>
<li><div><div>
Exemplifies Garmin’s Mission, Vision, Values &amp; Quality Policy and proactively works to improve Garmin’s image and culture</div></div></li>
