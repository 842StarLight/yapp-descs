<h2>Senior AP Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<h1 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>POSITION SUMMARY</b></h1>
<p>
View is a growing company that is changing the landscape of the building industry with our Dynamic Glass. With built-in intelligence, View Dynamic Glass automatically transitions between clear and variable tint, providing control over the amount of light and heat entering a building. In all conditions, View Glass remains transparent, keeping you connected to the outside world. It’s a simple idea. But one that will revolutionize the way we build.</p>
<p>
View, Inc. seeks a candidate for the newly created position of AP Specialist within the Finance department of our rapidly growing organization. If you are the type of person who is detail oriented and thrives in a collaborative and fast paced start-up environment, View, Inc. might be the place for you!</p>
<h1 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
ESSENTIAL POSITION RESPONSIBILITIES</b></h1>
<p>
Accounts Payable Responsibilities:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Process PO and non-PO related vendor invoices in Oracle</li>
<li>
Ensure all invoices are coded to the correct GL accounts and departments</li>
<li>
Interact professionally, collaboratively but persistently, with management and other departments for invoice dispute resolution, obtaining approvals, and voucher processing</li>
<li>
Answer vendor inquiries and interact with vendors regarding issues relating to invoices</li>
<li>
Maintain and organize filing of vendor invoices</li>
<li>
Reconcile vendor statements and work with buyers to resolve discrepancies</li>
<li>
Process check requests and wire transfers for AP payments</li>
<li>
Assist with check runs</li>
<li>
Assist with year-end audits relating to AP</li>
<li>
Comply with and promote corporate policies, procedures, and ensure proper internal controls are being followed</li>
<li>
Special projects as required</li>
</ul>
