<p><b>
FUNCTION OVERVIEW</b></p>
<p>
Whirlpool is seeking qualified candidates for an Associate, Engineering role to work at our Benton Harbor location. The ideal candidate is someone from an electronics background who has designed high volume mid power, high quality products. Whirlpool is looking for a strategic thinker, someone with the ability to create a strategy and a vision for this role.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
JOB RESPONSIBILITIES</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Develop and validate embedded hardware applications</li>
<li>
Design hardware solutions to drive system optimization, improve module/component design and enhance product reliability</li>
<li>
Interact with system architects/ integrators to review and consolidate application requirements</li>
<li>
Select and integrate hardware modules to build embedded hardware applications</li>
<li>
Electronic / Electrical circuit model simulation</li>
<li>
PCB (Printed Circuit Boards) layout design, applied simulation and component selection for hardware execution</li>
<li>
Prototype assembly, bring up, debugging, and functional verification</li>
<li>
Manage project KPI’s (cost, quality, schedule)</li>
<li>
ECM (Electronic Control Manufacture) management (technical) until pilot (connecting with procurement as needed)</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
MINIMUM REQUIREMENTS</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Bachelor’s Degree in an engineering discipline</li>
<li>
1 plus years’ experience in a manufacturing environment</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
PREFERRED REQUIREMENTS</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Master’s degree in an engineering discipline</li>
<li>
Electrical engineering background (both hardware and software)</li>
<li>
Experience with Hardware and PCB, (Printed Circuit Boards ) layout development and tools</li>
<li>
Work with both Global and regional groups within Whirlpool</li>
<li>
Familiar with OPEX (6Sigma) EMC (Electromagnetic Compatibility) and EMI (Electromagnetic Immunity) theory, A3 Methodology, Power supply Theory</li>
<li>
Experience in prototype bring up and debugging, functional verification and manufacturing</li>
<li>
Experience designing high volume products</li>
<li>
Experience in small embedded systems, 8-bit and 16-bit controllers</li>
<li>
Experience in system integration, processor selection, various communication protocols, analog integration, AC loads (&lt;1000W), power supplies, LED driving circuits</li>
<li>
PSPICE knowledge</li>
<li>
Experience with project assessment and risk management, developing projects</li>
</ul>
