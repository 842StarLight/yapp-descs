<h2>Client Success Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Overview:
</b></p>
<p>At Salsa, we’re not your typical SaaS company - we represent a community of more than 3,000 nonprofit organizations. We support our clients’ efforts to do everything from ending hunger to eliminating nuclear weapons to providing playgrounds for children. We want our clients to use our software to change the world – and to do that, we need Client Success Managers to inspire each and every client, identify their goals, and act as a trusted advisor to help them adopt Salsa’s tools.</p>
<p>
Client Success Managers report to the Manager of Client Success and will “own” a portfolio of premier Salsa clients. Our Client Success Managers are responsible for building and maintaining strong working relationships with Salsa users, acting as an escalation point for issues that impact client success and responding to retention risks and opportunities. As a member of the Client Success Team, this position will aim to expand Salsa’s adoption, retention, and ultimately, success. Client Success Managers work to become not just Salsa experts, but a valued resource in SaaS organizing technology, best practices, and digital campaign management.</p>
<p><b>
Job Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Professionally manage customer relationships with a portfolio of assigned accounts</li>
<li>
Meet customer retention goals and ensure consistently high retention rates.</li>
<li>
Build and maintain strong, trusted working relationships with key decision makers and power users from onboarding to end of client lifecycle.</li>
<li>
Gain a thorough understanding of assigned client’s needs, campaign objectives, and processes to ensure that they successfully adopt and deploy Salsa.</li>
<li>
Ensure that customers derive maximum value from Salsa and collaborate with the sales team to help upsell additional services, integrations, and features.</li>
<li>
Identify risks to client success and aggressively/proactively engage the client whenever there’s an identified retention issue.</li>
<li>
Develop and execute retention plans for customers who may be at risk</li>
<li>
Closely track client engagement activity and risk potential in Salesforce and Gainsight</li>
<li>
Drive resolution of escalated account issues in coordination with Billing, Support, and other departments.</li>
<li>
Gain and maintain an expert knowledge of Salsa’s products and services</li>
<li>
Be a voice for accounts internally and help senior leadership understand product concerns, shortcomings, and missing features that are retention risks.</li>
<li>
Manage contract related inquiries, questions, and issues from clients and other departments.</li>
<li>
This position may require occasional travel to client sites and Salsa locations.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Some Must Have’s:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
2+ years proven success in Customer Success role for a SaaS company with a designated portfolio of clients</li>
<li>
Experience with account portfolio planning, management, and prioritization</li>
<li>
High attention to detail and willingness to get “in the weeds” to fix a problem</li>
<li>
Comfortable navigating and mediating conflict while being able to foster honest dialog</li>
<li>
Knowledge of customer success best practices</li>
<li>
Experience driving client adoption of technology or software product</li>
<li>
Exceptional communication and relationship management skills</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Bonus Points:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Comfort with web technologies such as CSS, Javascript, JQuery</li>
<li>
Experience with Salesforce, Zendesk and Gainsight</li>
<li>
Direct experience working at a nonprofit, or in the fundraising, advocacy, or digital marketing industry</li>
</ul>

Benefits of Working at Salsa Labs:</b></p>
