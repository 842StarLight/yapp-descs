<h2>Corporate Sales Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">Reporting directly to the Vice President, Corporate Sales, the Corporate Sales Manager at BLADE will source and cultivate relationships and execute agreements with potential corporate partners, travel service professionals and other enterprise accounts. This candidate will be a strong and persuasive communicator who is well-organized, proactive and highly motivated. Additionally, the Corporate Sales Manager will support the Chief Revenue Officer and Vice President, Corporate Sales in tracking to and meeting BLADE's enterprise sales goals, and Commercial goals more broadly.
<br><br>
QUALIFICATIONS
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>1+ year in a corporate sales or business development capacity</li>
<li>Experience working in a fast-paced environment and ability to deliver high quality work on a deadline</li>
<li>Strong, persuasive communication skills both in-person, on the phone and via email</li>
<li>Ability to cultivate and grow relationships with key prospects and customers</li>
<li>Proficient in Microsoft Office Suite, Google's G-Suite; Experience working with sales automation software (Outreach.IO), sales lead databases (Zoominfo) and CRM's (Zendesk Sell) is a plus</li>
<li>Team player; interest and willingness in participating in activities that support BLADE's commercial goals</li>
<li>Bachelors degree required</li>
</ul>
