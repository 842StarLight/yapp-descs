<h2>Employee Customer Care Specialist (11 P.M. EST- 7 A.M. EST, Thu-Mon)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<div>
<div>The Employee / Customer Care Specialist (ECCS) is an integral part of the Centralized Services Team. This position is responsible for resolving support requests from Field Operations personnel and assisting the Sales / Key Account Management Customer Service Team with Operations tasks to ensure company goals / objectives are achieved. This process includes, inputting specific requests into a ticketing system, answering the phone or alternative technologies, fielding multiple requests, ensuring follow-up thru a tiered escalation structure and bring closure to the request.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
Job Responsibilities</div>
<ul>
<li> Resolve Employee / Customer Care requests thru a Contact Center approach in the service level agreements defined.</li>
<li> Communicate in a clear, professional manner with internal employees and external customers via phone, email, app or other technologies.</li>
<li> Meet customer / employees assigned deadline expectations on follow-up requests.</li>
<li> Complete all tasks with a high level of attention to detail, sense of urgency, care and compassion.</li>
<li> Ability to use a tiered escalation resolution system with follow-up to completion.</li>
<li> Conduct work efforts with a sense of urgency according to RGIS core values.</li>
<li> Responsible for managing a personal ticket assignment queue (including following a standard categorization method per ticket).</li>
<li> Utilizing standard procedures to resolve requests aligned with RGIS core values.</li>
</ul>
