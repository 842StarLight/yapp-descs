About you:</b></div>
<div>
You are eager to get your foot in the door at a fast-paced, rapidly growing startup. Working within our Business Operations &amp; Sales Strategy team, the Shipper Implementation Manager is responsible for managing the complete onboarding and implementation process for new enterprise shippers onto the Convoy platform.</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
You’ll do this by ensuring we understand shipper KPIs, operational nuances, and areas of risk. You’ll also be tasked with developing operational processes to ensure we deliver on new shipper SLA’s &amp; KPI’s. To guide you through the process, you’ll need to leverage project management skills, customer relationship acumen, and demonstrate superior communication skills by working across several different teams within Convoy.</div>

<br>

As the company grows, implementation is increasingly essential to ensuring our shippers receive world-class service. It starts with you.</div>

<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Role &amp; Responsibilities:</b></h3>
<div><ul>
<li>
Utilize financial models to determine and communicate new account acquisition and account growth goals for sales teams to drive towards overall company growth goals.</li>
<li>
Align internal stakeholders around goals.</li>
<li>
Conduct both short term and long term sales growth forecasts based on multiple forecasting methodologies. Where necessary, make improvements to these methodologies.</li>
<li>
Proactively communicate forecasts to internal stakeholders including sales, pricing, operations, and finance teams.</li>
<li>
Model expected year over year company growth to guide investment and hiring decisions.</li>
<li>
In collaboration with stakeholders across the demand org, establish key business metrics for tracking company growth</li>
<li>
Work with internal stakeholders to project future headcount needs for sales and demand org</li>
<li>
Through analysis and business intelligence, Identify and effectively communicate key levers to accelerate sales growth.</li>
<li>
Design, launch, and oversee new initiatives to sales drive levers.</li>
</ul></div>
