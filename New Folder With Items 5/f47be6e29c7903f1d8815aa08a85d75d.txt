<h2>Director Marketing Operations</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div><div><div><div><div><div>
<p>AM General is a global leader in the development, production and maintenance of ground vehicle systems. We offer a diversified product suite of market leading subsystems, chassis, and fully integrated mission systems for military and commercial customers. We are seeking a for a candidate who will contribute to the successful execution of AM General’s tradeshow program and communications strategy.</p>
<ul>
<li>The role has high visibility to the customer and to the executive team, working together with the leaders of the business units and the CEO.</li>
<li>This role will lead in the development and execution of the company’s marketing strategy, specifically around key customer engagements, trade shows and events.
</li>
<li>The role will manage the overall trade show strategy working with the events team to ensure coordination of budgets, optimization of the events calendar (discussion with Customer Facing Executives to ensure AM General maximizes exposure via shows/events), general branding execution, relationship management with military partners, management of show assets, and coordination of press and social media calendars as they relate to shows/events.
</li>
<li>The candidate will be tasked with reviewing existing operational process and developing plans for improvement and creating new processes where needed. We are looking for an energetic and creative self-starter who can act independently to make important decisions. This role will elevate the comprehensive tradeshow experience — from planning through execution — by creating exceptional experiences based on the company’s strategic priorities.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Collaborate with cross functional groups to establish the marketing strategy, trade show schedule and budget</li>
<li>Ensure tradeshow messaging is aligned with customers’ vision and maximizes corporate visibility in relation to strategic growth/sales goals</li>
<li>Review/enhance vehicle and accessory logistics processes (includes securing international licenses for shipping)</li>
<li>Coordinate with the business unit to decide what will be shown at the tradeshows</li>
<li>Travel to trade show event locations for booth oversight and customer engagements</li>
<li>Lead booth layout, according to marketing and sales strategy, budget, and brand guidelines</li>
<li>Lead new booth design efforts, in collaboration with tradeshow team and according to marketing and sales strategy, budget and brand guidelines</li>
<li>Manage detailed budgets per event; provide monthly reporting
</li>
<li>Track and process accounting, to include accuracy of invoices and timely payment processing</li>
<li>Collaborate on advertising strategy for print/digital publications for shows/events</li>
<li>Ensure that web content is consistent with AM General marketing and communication strategy</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Knowledge, Skills and Experience:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>15 years of experience in logistics management, strategy development, or related field</li>
<li>Bachelor’s degree in marketing, strategy, logistics or equivalent experience</li>
<li>Demonstrated skills in trade show marketing, logistics management, and/or strategy development</li>
<li>Ability to gear written and spoken communication to differing audiences and publics</li>
<li>High level of communication skills – written and oral</li>
<li>Ability to maintain workflow of several projects simultaneously under the pressure of multiple deadlines</li>
<li>Must show demonstrated success managing non-reporting cross functional teams</li>
<li>Must be able to work independently and take initiative in problem solving and decision-making</li>
<li>Must be able to travel a minimum of 25% a year, for a week at a time. Working relationships with our global counterparts must be established and maintained.</li>
<li>Experience in managing military trade shows a plus</li>
<li>Defense or military background is a highly desired for this position.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Physical Effort:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>While performing the duties of this position, the employee is regularly required to talk or hear.</li>
<li>The employee is occasionally required to stand, walk, sit; reach with hands and arms; climb or balance; and stoop, kneel, crouch or crawl.</li>
<li>The employee must occasionally lift and or move objects up to 25 pounds.</li>
<li>Specific vision abilities required by this position include close vision, distance vision, color vision, peripheral vision and the ability to adjust focus.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Working Conditions:</b></p>
<p>
This job operates in a professional office environment. This role routinely uses standard office equipment such as computers, phones, photocopiers, filing cabinets and copy, fax and scanning machines.</p>
