<h2>Senior Account Executive, Strategic Brands</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>
<div>Affirm is reinventing credit to make it more honest and friendly, giving consumers the flexibility to buy now and pay later without any hidden fees or compounding interest.</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
We're excited to share that financial services startup, Affirm is setting up shop in Chicago's River North neighborhood! This office will provide us with a much needed midwest outpost for our Sales, Marketing, Engineering, and Product teams. Join us on our mission to build honest and transparent financial products that improve lives.
</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>We're looking for a dynamic Senior Account Executive who will identify and drive new opportunities to build out our rapidly-growing merchant business. You will operate on the front-lines and be closely engaged with the market - developing and expanding relationships with business influencers and decision-makers. You will engage with partners across the spectrum from more established players to new, high-growth brands, creating customer value and forging new commercial partnerships in an efficient timeframe.</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
You're a resourceful leader who thrives in a challenging, fast-paced environment. Your role will have a direct impact on top-priority, company-wide goals and will provide accelerated learning opportunities with a world-class team.</div>
</div>
<div><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>What You'll Do</b></h3>
<div><ul>
<li>
Drive revenue and market share in a defined territory or industry vertical</li>
<li>
Develop and execute against a comprehensive account/territory plan</li>
<li>
Drive new business by finding and developing new leads</li>
<li>
Generate revenue by selling, managing, and developing client relationships</li>
<li>
Meet or exceed targets for revenue and sales activity</li>
<li>
Create &amp; articulate compelling value propositions around Affirm solution</li>
<li>
Prepare a variety of status reports, including activity, closings, follow-up, and adherence to goals</li>
<li>
Manage pipeline and follow through with presentations, proposals and contracts</li>
<li>
Liaise with other teams and functions both administratively and as part of new launches, etc.</li>
</ul></div>
</div></div>
<div><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>What We Look For</b></h3>
<div><ul>
<li>
Bachelor's degree or equivalent experience</li>
<li>
5+ years of closing experience on high performing sales teams</li>
<li>
Experience in investment banking, consulting, growth equity or retail e-commerce</li>
<li>
Proven experience using consultative approach to partner with e-commerce merchants (ideally tech/finance products)</li>
<li>
Strong strategy, research, analysis, presentation and negotiation skills</li>
<li>
Strong knowledge, interest and experience in retail, startups and enterprise sized companies preferred</li>
<li>
Experience and/or comfort in managing deals and speaking with decision makers at e-commerce companies</li>
<li>
Willingness to step up to greater responsibility, strong desire to contribute to overall goals</li>
<li>
Excellent ability to communicate and extremely detail-oriented</li>
</ul></div>
