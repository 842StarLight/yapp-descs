<h2>Dental Client Advocate - Customer Care</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p><b>Let’s do great things, together</b><br>
<br>
Founded in Oregon in 1955, Moda is proud to be a company of real people committed to quality. Today, like then, we’re focused on building a better future for healthcare. That starts by offering outstanding coverage to our members, compassionate support to our community and comprehensive benefits to our employees. It keeps going by connecting with neighbors to create healthy spaces and places, together.<br>
<br>
Moda Health is searching for Customer Service Reps who will provide customer service to our dental members of multiple benefit plans by analyzing the caller’s needs and by providing timely and accurate responses and answer calls from policyholders, agents, members, providers, hospitals, pharmacists and others regarding benefits or other issues.<br>
</p>
<p><b>Primary Functions</b><b>:</b><br>
01. Answer claim benefits from Moda/Group employees specific to their benefits and/or plan coverage via telephone and email.<br>
02. Provides solutions to problems, confirm eligibility and explain benefits, networks, plan designs and coverage accurately, timely, and in a professional manner.<br>
03. Apply mathematical skills to determine correct benefit information.<br>
04. Exercise judgement, initiative and discretion in confidential and sensitive matters.<br>
05. Performs related duties:<br>
 A. Review, update and become familiar with new and revised benefit information or claim processing procedures.<br>
 B. Resolve and record complaints, appeals and inquiries.<br>
 C. Contact physicians, dentists, hospitals and other providers when necessary to answer questions and obtain or provide information.<br>
 D. Provide timely follow up and return calls when required.<br>
 E. Research eligibility issues and facilitate urgent adds as needed.<br>
 F. Facilitate report requests and help to review reports with Client.<br>
 G. Track issues and look for trends to help Moda/Client relationship.<br>
06. Assist in the coordination of benefit design changes, annual enrollment meetings/health fair, design and development of literature, and coordination of new ID cards and brochures working closely with the Senior Account Executive.<br>
07. Directs thoughtful, customer specific initiatives which deliver value added services through the execution of client specific business planning working closely with the Senior Account Executive.<br>
08. Travel to Alaska as needed.<br>
09. Perform other related duties as assigned.</p>
<p><b>Working Conditions</b><b>:</b><br>
01. Work remotely at Clients location when and if needed.<br>
02. Ability to commute to Moda Tower for in person meetings as needed.<br>
03. Constant sitting and telephone use.<br>
04. Must be able to navigate multiple screens in a complex and changing environment.<br>
05. Constant interaction with others on the phone and in person.<br>
06. Work in excess of 37.5 hours per week, including evenings and weekends, to meet business need<br>
