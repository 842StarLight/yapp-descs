<p><b>COMPANY SUMMARY:</b></p>
<p>
McCann Health Echo is a full-service healthcare advertising agency solely focused on healthcare professionals and their patients. There is a sweet spot that exists at the intersection of HCP and patient – a conversation that happens, which paves the way for treatment plans and goals – and that’s where we live. We build smart, original brands and present ideas that delight and inspire our partners, encouraging them to reach further to connect with the consumer and reimagine healthcare.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
We are a collection of naturally creative, instinctively insightful, and authentically strategic people who care deeply about brands and one another. Our vibe is genuine and relaxed – more backyard barbecue than boardroom. We pride ourselves on being independent but inclusive, down-to-earth but driven.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
Standing behind us at all times is McCann Health, the largest global agency network. McCann Health is a part of McCann Worldgroup and the Interpublic Group (NYSE: IPG).</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
POSITION SUMMARY:</b></p>
<p>
The Copy Supervisor’s primary responsibility is to write creative copy (sales pieces, secondary “pick-up” pieces, etc.) on assigned accounts and develop powerful creative marketing tools that enable clients to succeed at selling their products. In addition to copywriting, the Copy Supervisor is responsible for the creative work produced by assigned copywriters and contributes to the performance review of assigned copywriters, alongside the Associate Creative Director.</p>
<p>
Active in new business and client presentations, the Copy Supervisor is a conceptual thinker that is confident presenting big ideas, both internally and externally. Additional responsibilities include referencing and highlighting copy, and attending all relevant internal product-related meetings.</p>
<p>
A perfect match for us would be a great team player who loves the opportunity to be involved with creating fresh ideas, wants to play a key role in developing engaging digital solutions, and has the keen ability to become an asset to the assigned pharmaceutical brand and therapeutic category. We are seeking a copywriter with recent exposure to/experience working on a <b>immunology </b>brand.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
ADDITIONAL RESPONSIBITIES:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Lead a key client relationship and develop strategic direction for our pharmaceutical clients’ brands</li>
<li>Manage the agency team to proactively and regularly create ideas that deliver on the client’s business objectives</li>
<li>Oversee the strategic and executional quality of all client materials</li>
<li>Work with assigned team to grow the client’s business at McCann Health Echo to ensure financial profitably</li>
<li>Mentor and develop direct reports to fulfill their career growth goals</li>
<li>Strive for excellence and have an eye on what’s best for the brand at all times</li>
<li>Enjoy your job and want to bring out the best in those around you.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p>
 </p>
<p><b>POSITION REQUIREMENTS:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Completed Bachelor’s Degree in Advertising or a related field</li>
<li>Minimum 5 years of copywriting experience</li>
<li>Minimum 2 years of pharmaceutical copywriting experience, pharma ad agency experience required</li>
<li>Recent experience working on a <b>immunology </b>brand; familiarity with scientific information related to this therapeutic category</li>
<li>Must possess a thorough understanding of the healthcare market and competition, with an eye on trends and innovative thinking that ensures leadership-quality deliverables</li>
<li>Confident presenting creative work to clients</li>
<li>Solid writing, analytical thinking and presentation skills</li>
<li>Previous mentorship or supervisory experience required</li>
<li>Proactive and confident, with a sense of humor and desire to work with talented team members</li>
<br>
</ul>
