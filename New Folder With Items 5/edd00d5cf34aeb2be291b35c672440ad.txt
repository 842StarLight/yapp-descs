<h2>Automation Sales Engineer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>G7 Automation is currently seeking an Automation Sales Engineer to work with the Principal of the company. The individual will train almost exclusively as a member of the operations team for the first 6 months.<br>
</p>
<p>
As a member of the operations team, you will be required to wear many hats. These projects will involve system detail design documentation, SCADA software development, PLC programming, panel and server communications/networking configuration, factory testing and system startup onsite. During startups you will work on construction sites with mechanical and electrical installation teams as well as interact with<br>
employees at our customer's facility. Some overnight travel will be required for onsite startup.<br>
After 6 months the individual will begin to transition to the role of Sales Engineer by working with the<br>
Principal to help manage accounts, estimate and prepare proposals. You will learn strategic selling and negotiation first hand. After 2 years, the plan is for this individual to transition to a Business Development Engineer compensated by a Salary plus commission.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p>
Required:<br>
</p>
<ul>
<li>BS degree in Mechanical, Electrical, Chemical Engineering or Computer Science is preferred</li>
<li>Excellent interpersonal communication skills are required</li>
<li>Aptitude for relationship building is required</li>
<li>Experience with sales is NOT required</li>
<li>Aptitude for technical writing is required</li>
<li>The ability to work independently as well as on a project team is required</li>
<li>Proven presentation skills are a plus</li>
<li>Demonstrated leadership ability is a plus</li>
<li>Experience with Rockwell RSLogix PLC programming software is a plus</li>
<li>Experience with AutoCAD is a plus</li>
<li>Strong information technology infrastructure skills are a plus</li>
<li>Aptitude to troubleshoot the instrumentation/equipment with which our systems interface is a plus</li>
</ul>
<p><br>
G7 Automation LLC offers competitive salary and benefits, as well as, a modern, flexible work environment, and close-knit team approach.<br>
</p>
<p>
Submit cover letters and resume to careers@g7automation.com.</p>
