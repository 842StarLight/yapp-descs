<h2>Associate Project Manager – Spectrum Enterprise</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>Spectrum Enterprise is a national provider of scalable, fiber-based networking and managed services solutions. Solutions include Internet access, Ethernet, Managed Network Services, Voice solutions, TV solutions, Managed Application, Cloud Infrastructure and Managed Hosting Services. We work closely with clients to achieve greater business success by providing these right-fit solutions designed to meet their evolving needs. For more information, visit enterprise.spectrum.com.
</div>
<br>
<b>Highlights</b><br>
<div>This position is responsible for assisting in the management, planning, monitoring and reporting on key projects from conception through implementation. This role manages project details, data collection, reporting and communication to successfully drive project efforts and Develops and administers special projects in support of Sales Operations key organizational initiatives. This position also assists with testing and performs quality checks on system enhancements, improvements and processes both pre- and post-deployment. This role documents results and recommendations for senior leadership and conducts impact assessments while working with partner teams to develop and document processes.</div>
<br>
<b>
What you will do
</b><ul>
<li>Assist in planning, managing and monitoring projects from concept through implementation.
</li>
<li>Subject matter expert on Spectrum Enterprise's sales and Sales Operations policies, processes, systems and tools; products, features and attributes; and pricing and promotional offers. Review processes to ensure tool and policy compliance.
</li>
<li>Manage day-to-day operational aspects of all assigned projects and gains consensus from stakeholders to ensure successful implementation.</li>
<li>
Assist in establishing project management processes and methodologies to ensure assigned projects are delivered on time, within budget and meet high-quality standards and Leadership expectations using the appropriate tools.</li>
<li>
Meet with internal clients of an assigned project to provide status updates and reports and gather project-related feedback. Manage assigned initiatives to include planning, overseeing, and documenting all aspects of the project. Work with leadership to define the scope, plan key activities and sequencing, ensure the project is on schedule and meets targeted deployment dates.</li>
<li>
Responsible for handling multiple projects simultaneously.</li>
<li>
Perform Operational Readiness Testing (ORT) and User Acceptance Testing (UAT) in support of project deployments. Collaborate with Systems and Tools and IT teams to identify and correct system defects.</li>
<li>
Support and enhance sales referral programs to increase lead generation activity and accelerate revenue growth.</li>
<li>
Track costs and performance, service levels and other metrics required to ensure project goals and objectives are met.</li>
<li>
Assist with gathering data and preparing a variety of reports covering project estimates, project status and other management reports.</li>
<li>
Assist with the development of tools and applications to create efficiencies and improve organizational performance.</li>
<li>
Performs additional duties related to this position as assigned.</li>
</ul>
<b>

</b><ul>
<li>Ability to read, write and speak the English language to communicate with employees, customers, suppliers, in person, on the phone, and by written communication in a clear, straight-forward, and professional manner</li>
<li>
Ability to prioritize and organize effectively and manage multiple projects and assignments</li>
<li>
Ability to develop strong working relationships with peers and project members
</li>
<li>Ability to perform effectively in a fast-paced environment</li>
<li>
Knowledge and ability to use computer and software applications</li>
<li>
Zero or more years of project management experience</li>
</ul>
