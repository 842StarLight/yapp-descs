<h2>VIS Systems Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>VISibility Workflow Specialist Objectives:</p>
<p>As a VISibility workflow Specialis you will become a critical part of the VISibility Managed Services platform. With the use of software in conjunction with the VISibility framework, your ability to be as effective and efficient as possible will directly impact the throughput and time to market criteria that VISibility provides to franchise automotive dealerships.</p>
<p>The Scope of duties and responsibilities requested of a VISibility Workflow Specialist may include but are not limited to :</p>
<ul>
<li>Manage Workflow software.</li>
<li>Daily reviews of customers workflow queue always accessing progress of units moving through the system.</li>
<li>Identifying issues and bottlenecks and addressing those issues.</li>
<li>Using proprietary software to maximize impact, performance and throughput of units in the system.</li>
<li>Assessing Vehicles for cosmetic damage</li>
<li>Ability to prioritize and identify alternate pathways an inventory unit may need to flow through the system</li>
<li>Auditing dealers Inventory showing in the VISibility queue.</li>
<li>Verifying status/requesting action items to be performed, to get units to the most efficient flow.</li>
<li>Quality control and checks of services provided by vendors.</li>
<li>Train clients and vendors on proper interaction on the software.</li>
<li>Ability to provide Basic support to client and vendor users of the system.</li>
<li>Reviewing 3rd party dealership systems and software and comparing that data with what is reflecting in the VISibility platform to insure accurate data is relayed to the client.</li>
<li>Onsite pre launch discovery at new client facilities.</li>
<li>Onsite launch of new accounts.</li>
</ul>
<p>Minimum 2 years experience in Franchise automotive environment. Sales, sales manager, F&amp;I, service advisor etc...</p>
