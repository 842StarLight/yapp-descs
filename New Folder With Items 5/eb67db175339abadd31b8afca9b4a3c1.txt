<h2>Assistant Project Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<b>Job Description:</b><br>
<p>Beckwood leverages over 40 years of engineering and manufacturing experience to produce high-quality hydraulic press systems for a wide range of industries and applications. Our customers are in a variety of industries including aerospace &amp; defense, energy/oil &amp; gas, automotive, construction, agricultural and more ranging from small, independent manufacturers to multi-national corporations with global footprints.
</p>
<p>It is Beckwood’s employees who have continually set us apart. Our team’s belief system in customer satisfaction, communications and a can-do attitude make us a great place to work!<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>PRIMARY RESPONSIBILITIES</b>:
</p>
<ul>
<li>Assists Lead Project Manager in all facets of the project management process in the design and build of customized hydraulic presses</li>
<li>
Enters new orders received from the Sales Department into the Company’s ERP system</li>
<li>
Communicates project details with internal departments</li>
<li>
Determines project budgets and enters into company software</li>
<li>
Creates Gantt charts to communicate important milestones and overall project schedule</li>
<li>
Submits invoices</li>
<li>
Submits various documents generated by Engineering or Lead Project Manager to the customer for review</li>
<li>
Reviews data in company ERP system to ensure processes and schedule is maintained</li>
<li>
Communicates with vendors for timely deliveries of components</li>
<li>
Participates in scheduling meetings</li>
<li>
Prepares reports including budget vs actual and on time tasks data</li>
<li>
Communicates both internally and externally with the customer to coordinate shipping and installation of finished presses</li>
</ul>
<br>
Required Experience:<br>
<ul>
<li>Must have experience working in a manufacturing environment</li>
<li>
Excellent verbal and written communications</li>
<li>
Analytical problem solver</li>
<li>
Ability to develop project plans and coordinate projects</li>
<li>
Ability to understand complex and technical data</li>
<li>
Team oriented</li>
<li>
Strong computer skills including Word, Excel and Microsoft Project with the ability to learn new software programs</li>
<li>
Experience with ERP systems</li>
<li>
College or Technical degree or manufacturing experience equivalent</li>
<li>
Background in technical field helpful</li>
</ul>
