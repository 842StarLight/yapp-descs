<h2>Member Relationship Associate-Insurance</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Are you looking to change the financial services industry for the better? Do you thrive working in a challenging fast paced environment?</b></p>
<p><b>Only Candidates with a Valid Life Insurance License will be Considered</b></p>
<p><b>About the Company: </b></p>
<p>Founded in 2014, DPL Financial Partners has established its unique, financial footprint with an innovative solution to transform the relationship between insurance carriers and RIAs (Registered Investment Advisors). We consult with RIAs to help them strategically implement commission-free insurance solutions to ultimately provide greater client value. DPL also consults with insurance carriers to develop solutions that will support the growing market shift to a fee-only, fiduciary standard.</p>
<p><b>What we’re looking for: </b></p>
<p>A dynamic, detail-oriented individual with a financial services background to join our firm. In this role, you will work with the team of consultants to support field efforts, case analysis, and provide overall excellent customer service to our member firms.</p>
<p><b>Duties and Responsibilities</b></p>
<p><b>New Business Efforts</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Maintain and monitor membership opportunities.</li>
<li>Coordinate with and support DPL Consultants along with Advisors and their Clients to complete applications and obtain necessary documents in a timely manner.</li>
<li>Develop an understanding of Firelight software.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Analysis / Policy Review</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Provide illustrations and analyses of life insurance and annuity statements.</li>
<li>Become proficient user of carrier software and internally developed tools.</li>
<li>Engage in summary of recommended solutions that address client needs.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Coordination with RIAs</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Assist in scheduling on-boarding calls/meetings with new RIA members.</li>
<li>Participate in on-boarding calls/meetings to ensure proper introduction to DPL team and services.</li>
<li>Aid in periodic follow up calls, emails, or meetings with existing RIA members.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Room for growth includes advanced case design, lead generation, operations, or sales.</b></p>
<p><b>Education and Experience </b></p>
<p>Best qualified candidates will possess:</p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s Degree from accredited college or university.</li>
<li>Life insurance license required, health insurance license preferred.</li>
<li>Two to four years of experience in insurance sector with focus in account support or internal sales.</li>
<li>Customer support-oriented with excellent communication skills. Must be able to work in a dynamic and fast-paced environment.</li>
<li>Proficient in Microsoft Office – Microsoft Word, Excel, and Power Point required. Salesforce, Pardot, and/or Firelight experience preferred.</li>
</ul>
<p>Job Type: Full-time</p>
<p>Salary: $35,000.00 to $45,000.00 /year</p>

<ul><li>Bachelor's (Required)</li></ul>
