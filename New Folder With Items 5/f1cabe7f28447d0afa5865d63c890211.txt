<h2>Financial Systems Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Overview
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div>Frost &amp; Sullivan is growing its finance department and adding a Financial Systems Manager in San Antonio, TX who is looking to make an immediate impact. You will play a key role in creating, implementing, and overseeing out financial systems. You will also develop and create financial reporting and proactive actionable metrics that can help drive the strategy of the business. This position will work closely with the global finance, accounting and leadership teams to compile, consolidate, and analyze data to support decision-making and executive presentations. This position will also serve as the expert in our new Netsuite Global ERP, analyzing, developing and documenting company requirements and translating those requirements into automated solutions to solve business issues and/or needs. You will partner with stakeholders to lead, plan, and execute business system initiatives across the global business units. Additionally, you will also play a key role in ongoing system analysis and maintenance, troubleshooting and making best practice recommendations as it relates to NetSuite and other business &amp; financial systems.</div>

<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<ul>
<li>Ownership of NetSuite and Adaptive Insights financial systems – continually creating and suggesting ways to maximize logic and efficiencies within the tool</li>
<li>
Execute implementation of systems, associated processes, and change management across the global organization</li>
<li>
Advise leadership on potential business impact of new technologies and systems towards improving efficiencies and/or strengthen internal controls</li>
<li>
Creation and optimization of monthly financial reporting, constantly looking for new metrics and ways of enhancing the reporting</li>
<li>
Work with internal stakeholders to analyze complex needs and define best practices to translate business requirements into functional system processes</li>
<li>
Ensure clear and complete documentation of procedures, processes</li>
<li>
Create &amp; maintain training materials and facilitate NetSuite end-user global training</li>
<li>
Keep abreast with new technologies, with an eye towards their relevancy to the company's future architectural needs</li>
<li>
Perform functional configuration and enhancements, system maintenance and business process testing for NetSuite maintenance, which includes managing custom fields, forms, records, workflows, searching and reporting, and user roles to support business requirements</li>
<li>
Perform continuous assessment of the close process and process re-engineering to reduce the timing to close the financial period</li>
<li>
Support month end and year end close procedures, along with internal and external reporting</li>
<li>
Support the adoption of additional new systems in finance including the integration of HR and payroll systems or other related software</li>
<li>
Participate in other projects as required</li>
<li>
Structured analytical approach to problem solving and looks for process improvement opportunities</li>
<li>
Ability to work in a fast paced, deadline oriented environment across multiple time zones in a global organization</li>
<li>
Must be able to manipulate large amounts of data from disparate sources and ensure accuracy and integrity of sales and financial numbers; exhibit a willingness to track down data</li>
<li>
Excellent communicator, both verbally and written, who can effectively communicate process changes and improvements to all levels of the team cross-functionally as well as present findings to management</li>
<li>
Develops, recommends, and implements ideas and projects that will result in improved efficiency, productivity, quality, and operational savings</li>
</ul>
