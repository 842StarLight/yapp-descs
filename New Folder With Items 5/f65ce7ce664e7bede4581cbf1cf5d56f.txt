<p><b>Key Responsibilities</b>
</p>
<p>The Assistant Marketing Manager assists the Marketing Manager in maintaining daily scheduled emails, social media platform and banner ad campaigns. He/She oversees the product development to ensure proper SEO, page formatting and functionality. They are also responsible for overseeing the associates’ progress and reporting to the Marketing Manager for better department workflow. Reasonable accommodations may be made to enable individuals with disabilities to perform the essential functions.
</p>
<ul>
<li>Responsible for copy writing and editing the marketing material for a B2B audience.
</li>
<li>Creates email marketing with Constant Contact and Mailchimp, Social Media campaigns and push notifications geared towards mature audience.
</li>
<li>Manages sales and marketing campaign schedules with the vendors on a daily basis on CBDtoStore.com website.
</li>
<li>Assists the Marketing Manager with the interior facing company graphics including but not limited to Business cards, Warehouse Signs, Employee Rewards, Certificates, Employee of the Month etc.
</li>
<li>Assists with developing and implementing new creative ideas to ensure the department productivity.
</li>
<li>Collaborates with other team members and answers any questions and/or concerns regarding the assigned tasks.
</li>
<li>Leads the Marketing Department in the absence of the Marketing Manager and provides necessary updates regarding the current tasks and deadlines.
</li>
<li>Posts marketing content on the Social Media Platform frequently to ensure productivity and increase in customer acquisition. (Facebook, Twitter, Instagram)
</li>
<li>Responsible for SEO auditing using the SEMRush and Google Analytics.
</li>
<li>Manages deadlines to ensure successful workflow and uses Project Management Software.
</li>
<li>Assists the Marketing Manager with Ecommerce Product Development.
</li>
<li>Performs other duties as assigned by the Marketing Manager.
</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Skills &amp; Experience</b>
</p>
<ul>
<li>A minimum of Associate’s degree and three to four years of related experience and/or training, or equivalent combination of education and experience.
</li>
<li>Experience with MS Suite including Word, Excel, Google Sheets/Docs.
</li>
<li>One year of supervisory experience.
</li>
<li>Bigcommerce experience is a must
</li>
<li>Experience with Wordpress, Magento, HTML, CSS and SEO.
</li>
<li>Experience with Adobe Suite (Photoshop, Illustrator, InDesign etc.)
</li>
</ul>
