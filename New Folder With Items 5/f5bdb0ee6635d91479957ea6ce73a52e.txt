<h2>Operations Team Manager- Shelter Cove</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">The Operations Team Manager position is responsible for managing the team that performs non-selling duties to support the selling function of the store. The Operations Team Manager position is exempt and reports to the Assistant Store Manager and/or Store Manager.
<br><br>
<b>Essential Functions:</b><br>
Presents merchandise consistent with visual and merchandising standards
<br>Ensures the timely floor setup, including signage, for promotional events and seasonal business activity
<br>Ensures direction of playbook and seasonal merchandise meetings
<br>Maintains floor and stock areas consistent with store standards
<br>Work in conjunction with the Sales Team Manager (STM) and visual coordinators, where appropriate to support the overall management presentation of the store
<br>Receiving Management
<br>Supervise the team performing the process of unloading and sorting merchandise cartons received on trailers. Move new merchandise to the appropriate processing drop zone on the sales floor in a safe and timely manner utilizing equipment such as rolling flats, carts and z-racks.
<br>Ensure Workload Planning and Forecast tools are being used weekly to drive productivity of the operations team.
<br>Processing / Placement Management
<br>Supervise the team performing merchandise unpacking and unwrapping to ensure readiness for merchandising on the sales floor. Includes applying sensor tags per company standard.
<br>Properly merchandises new and replenished goods in accordance with plan-o-grams and store direction
<br>Price Change Management/ Merchandise Return Management
<br>Supervises the team executing all types of markdowns on the sales floor. Set sales and national ad events.
<br>Ensures accuracy of signing and markdowns.
<br>Supervises the team pulling and processing transfers, returns to vendors (RTV), damages, and mark out of stock (MOS) merchandise in partnership with LP.
<br>Communicates price change inaccuracies and signage direction to the price change office via the help desk
<br>Maintenance &amp; Housekeeping Management
<br><br>
<b>Supervise the team performing the following:</b><br>
Maintenance tasks including light bulb replacement, ceiling tile replacement and replacing sales floor and maintenance supplies.
<br>Removal of packing materials from processing drop zones. Bail cardboard and prepare plastic for recycling.
<br>Ensure the cleanliness of the sales floor and the receiving area following processing on truck days.
<br>Housekeeping includes being responsible for the cleanliness of the facility specifically the restrooms, associate lounge, and office areas. (Note: if applicable, specific stores may have 3rd party cleaning service)
<br>Maintaining a safe shopping and working environment
<br>Be responsible for communication to and from BSS Store Operations that would include but not limited to Facilities Management, JLL repair, ROCC the Dock, etc.
<br>Responsible for the Workload Planning process.
<br>Sales &amp; Profit
<br>Meet or exceed sales goal for entire store
<br>Meet or exceed all operational goals including cartons per hour and shrinkage control.
<br>Proper execution of all existing systems and procedures, and ownership of new system implementations and processes.
<br>Non-Core Store Operations
<br>Seasonal sets
<br>Floor moves
<br>Daily maintenance
<br>Fill Ins
<br>Daily Standards
<br>Recovery
<br>Driving support the RCC
<br>Supporting the store to achieve Belk Rewards goal
<br>General Business Performance
<br>Ability to ring the register and provide relentless customer care
<br>Support Team Development
<br>People Management
<br>Recruiting, interviewing, selecting and retaining quality associates and ensures all positions are filled in a timely manner.
<br>Maintaining high store associate engagement through efficient, timely communications of policies and other information. Follow through on all concerns or problems that develop within the store.
<br>Ensuring all associates receive and complete all necessary paperwork.
<br>People Development
<br>Train support associates in correct processes and procedures. Provide effective coaching in order to improve performance of all associates.
<br>Conducting timely performance management process and communicate development needs with subordinates. Set goals and identify areas for improvement.
<br>Utilize the corrective action process and or the monthly status checks to document and coach poor performers consistently throughout the store.
<br>Communicate with associates on possible career paths and advancement opportunities.
<br>Develop and promote a diverse group of associates.
<br>Communicate consistently with truck day sales floor management and store manager. Escalate issues as appropriate.
<br>Accept additional duties or share responsibilities during busy times and/or as requested by Management
<br>Manager on Duty Assignment
<br>Supervise customer service during assigned period.
<br>Open and close the store, including weekends
<br>Approve select point of sale transactions such as returns and voids.
<br>Resolve customer service issues as needed.
<br>Understand and be able to use all building operating systems
<br>Follow all policies and procedures related to loss prevention and safety â€“ such as floor sweeps.
<br>Use safety equipment at all times in accordance with OSHA regulations.
