<h2>Program Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<div>The Program Manager position will be responsible for organizing programs and projects for the Business Intelligence program, ensuring alignment with customer and company objectives. This role will work closely with various business units (such as Operations, Sales &amp; Marketing, Product Development, Finance, and Legal) to develop operating plans and budgets for programs.<br>
<br>
<ul>
<li>Organizing programs and activities in accordance with the mission and goals of the organization</li>
<li>Developing new programs to support the strategic direction of the organization</li>
<li>Creating and managing long-term goals</li>
<li>Developing a budget and operating plan for the program</li>
<li>Developing an evaluation method to assess program strengths and identify areas for improvement</li>
<li>Working within a team that has a diverse array of talents and responsibilities</li>
<li>Ensuring goals are met in areas including customer satisfaction, security, quality and team member performance</li>
<li>Implementing and managing changes and interventions to ensure project goals are achieved</li>
<li>Meeting with stakeholders to make communication easy and transparent regarding project issues and decisions on services</li>
<li>Producing accurate and timely reporting of program status throughout its life cycle</li>
<li>Analyzing and communicating program risks</li>
<li>Working on strategy with all appropriate teams
</li>
</ul>
</div>
<div>Position Requirements
<br>
<br>
• Lottery industry preferred<br>
<ul>
<li>5+ years’ experience with project management</li>
<li>Technical background and experience with enterprise software preferred</li>
<li>Innate curiosity for information technology and security</li>
<li>Strong knowledge of Project Management practices</li>
<li>Must be able to apply and grow necessary skills to meet objectives in a fast-growing enterprise environment</li>
<li>Bachelor’s Degree required</li>
<li>Ability to work in a fast paced environment on multiple projects</li>
<li>Strong analytical, problem-solving and time management skills</li>
<li>Excellent verbal and written communication skills and the ability to interact professionally with a diverse group to include executives, managers and subject matter experts</li>
<li>Willingness to learn new tools, complex concepts and processes</li>
<li>Ability to work in a dynamic, team-oriented, collaborative environment</li>
<li>Ability to use Microsoft Office Tools (MS Word, Excel, PowerPoint, Visio)</li>
<li>Strong customer service orientation
</li>
</ul>
