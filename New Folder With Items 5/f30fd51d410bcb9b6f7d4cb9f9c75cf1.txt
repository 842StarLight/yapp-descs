<h2>Regional Account Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>The Globalscape Regional Account Manager (RAM) is responsible for building new business relationships with existing and new logo small to medium-sized customers. This includes prospecting for new customers, building adequate pipeline enough to consistently reach a monthly and quarterly objective, up selling existing and new product lines to new and existing customers, selling newly released GlobalSCAPE products to an existing customer base. Because this position requires a high volume of outbound phone calls and emails and an occasional on site visit, the selected candidate must have excellent communication and presentation skills as well as a strong consultative sales and software experience in their background.
</p>
<p>This position requires a high volume of outbound phone calls and emails and an occasional on-site visit, the ideal candidate must have excellent communication and presentation skills as well as a strong consultative sales and software experience.</p>
<p><b>
As a RAM you will;</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Build a pipeline building through individual’s network, existing customers, and hunting new prospects</li>

<li>Provide accurate forecasting</li>
<li>Track activity with our Salesforce system</li>
<li>Seamless and productive account management</li>
<li>Attain quarterly/annual sales goals</li>
</ul>

<li>An understanding of the major software applications in the market</li>
<li>Proven strategic selling to large enterprises, account management skills, and problem-solving methodologies</li>
