<h2>Vice President, Business Development & Partnerships</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">Do you enjoy building businesses that disrupt conventional performance marketing? Does the challenge of unlocking and growing a new vertical with strong product-market fit invigorate you? Do you enjoy a fast-paced environment with some of the brightest minds in technology? Ibotta is fundamentally changing how the world's leading brands think about payments and digital marketing. We are looking for a Senior Director of Business Development to help guide our payments vision and to sell that vision the biggest players in retail and mobile commerce.
<br><br>
<b>Responsibilities:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Own the business development process and client relationships, gaining leadership buy-in, selling and negotiating partnerships, launching new platforms, and evolving those platforms based on data-driven results</li>
<li>Unlock challenging, high-potential verticals, using a combination of innovative tactics to capture spend from competitors, traditional digital players, and legacy tactics</li>
<li>Beat annual revenue targets through the development, advancement, and closure of partnerships and execution of new businesses and expansion of existing business</li>
<li>Embrace the Lean Startup approach to quickly evaluate new opportunities at a low cost</li>
<li>Develop and deliver innovative sales presentations that educate prospective clients and partners on Ibotta's value proposition, roadmap, and partnership opportunities</li>
<li>Grow, maintain and leverage your network. Attend conferences, trade shows, and industry events to build relationships, close deals and maintain expertise in our industry. Establish yourself as an industry expert</li>
<li>Use innovation, creativity, process, and entrepreneurialism in every interaction you have, both externally and internally</li>
<li>Listen to clients (existing and potential) and identify opportunities to grow and enhance the business and our products</li>
<li>Negotiate terms. Create win-win partnerships</li>
<li>Ensure that sales tactics align with the values, goals, and resources of the business. Work with leadership to establish appropriate targets</li>
<li>Ensure retention and growth of existing accounts through adept post-campaign account management and education around new product enhancements</li>
<li>Work closely with other functional teams to ensure client partnerships are successfully executed from start to finish</li>
<li>Continually refine your skills and expertise to stay ahead of the demands of your role</li>
</ul>

<b>About Us:</b><br>
Headquartered in Denver, CO, Ibotta ("I bought a...") is a free app that's transforming the shopping experience by making every purchase rewarding. The company partners with leading brands and retailers to offer real cash back on groceries, travel, electronics, clothing, gifts, home and office supplies, dining out, and much more. Ibotta is the ultimate starting point for savings, and having paid out more than $500 million in cash rewards to its Savers, it's no surprise why Ibotta is one of the most downloaded shopping apps in the United States
