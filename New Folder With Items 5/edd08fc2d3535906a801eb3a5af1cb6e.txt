<h2>Warehouser II</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Overview
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>This position is responsible for receiving in stock, pulling, checking, and packing orders, operating a UPS Worldship computer system, processing transfers, assisting in the returns process, assisting in conducting inventories, operating a forklift, maintaining the proper cleanliness of the warehouse, and all other warehouse duties assigned. Some heavy lifting is required.
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities
</b></h2>
<div><div><ul>
<li>2-5 years of service and/or meeting assigned targeted performance.</li>
<li>Receives in stock: includes unloading trucks, inspecting for damage, confirming items and item codes, serial numbers, and quantities are correct according to Packing Slip, then receive in the system via handheld scanner, creating barcode labels for 3rd party item; assigning stock locators, moving stock into appropriate locators within the warehouse.</li>
<li>Releases orders for all assigned organizations on a scheduled basis and works with Order Management.</li>
<li>Based on released orders, pulls all items for unpacking. Each order will be picked and processed (create barcode labels) via hand held scanner. After the order has been picked all serialized items &amp; accessory quantities are verified and processed into the Shop for pre-installation.</li>
<li>Operates a UPS Worldship computer system: includes scanning a label on the order, verifying the label scanned matches the order, checking the order for special shipping instructions and processing as requested, and verifying that the cost via UPS is less expensive than another mode of delivery.</li>
<li>Processes transfers: includes moving stock from one warehouse status/location to another.</li>
<li>Assists in the return's process: includes checking return orders and performing all return related functions as assigned.</li>
<li>Assists in conducting inventories: includes counting and recording the quantity on hand for each item on the inventory report, identifying all items in stock that are not on the inventory report, recounting and helping to research all discrepancies, and correcting stock locations.</li>
<li>Processes all RMA's received on a daily basis, coordinating modifications to orders as needed.</li>
<li>Maintains the proper cleanliness of the warehouse: includes keeping all work areas clean and safe throughout the day, placing garbage only in a garbage container, immediately removing shrink wrap and empty boxes from stock, picking up garbage when seen, sweeping when needed, and removing snow and salting when needed.
</li>
</ul></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Qualifications
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>Typically requires general education and/or vocational training plus 1 to 2 years of related experience.<br>
<ul>
<li>Forklift certified in accordance with Canon and OSHA safety guidelines.</li>
<li>Must be able to lift 50lbs. Must be able to operate a forklift.
</li>
</ul>
</div></div></div>
