<h2>Consulting Practice Lead</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>At Sphere Software we design, develop, support and enhance solutions and systems that help our customers run their business operations, grow and take advantage of efficiencies by leveraging cutting edge technologies.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>The current consulting practice includes the following disciplines: project management, business/application consulting, custom software engineering, system integration and data analytics consulting using team augmentation and outsourced software delivery approaches. The work profile of the consulting practice includes team augmentation of onsite and remote software development teams, software development projects, cloud based projects and solutions to our clients to build modern technology products and platforms of tomorrow by blending design, product engineering, analytics, and smart automation.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>We are seeking for an entrepreneurial and progressive leader to lead the growth and success of our solutions offerings.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p>Sphere team works to define technology strategies and implement solutions that provide tangible business value to our clients. Our multi-capability approach enables us to bring innovative thinking to address problems and challenges from all angles.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>Practice Leader will be accountable for operational/financial metrics and overall business results of practice. Responsible for setting and driving strategic direction, ensuring profitable growth of the practice, quality of consulting delivery, and maintaining customer referenceability. Actively develops new approaches and opportunities for expanding customer base. Creates and implements operational processes to drive consistency and support achievement of practice business strategy.</p>
<br>
<p>
</p>
<p><b>The key responsibilities for this role will include:</b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>
<p>Business plan and road map development for the practice</p>
</li>
<li><p>Accountability for the practice, including driving sales, revenue, recruiting, delivery, consulting, and all operational aspects, including:
</p></li>
<li><p>Practice Building – Drive overall growth of practice area through a combination of business development, talent management, oversight of delivery work, and thought leadership.
</p></li>
<li>
<p>Build relationships with new and existing clients.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Work effectively with alliance partners</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Work with teams to capture best practices and develop case studies and other core deliverables to build solution offerings</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Financial, strategic and tactical management against goals for the global consulting practice</p>
</li>
<li><p>Participate in industry forums, events, and speaking engagements; manage production of project deliverables, budget, and presentations to client personnel and leadership
</p></li>
<li>
<p>Ownership of processes and metrics to support consulting practice</p>
</li>
<li><p>Engagement Management – Manage engagement risk, project economics including planning and budgeting, managing accounts receivable, defining deliverable content, and ensuring buy-in of proposed solutions from top management levels at the client.<br>
</p></li>
<li>
<p>Proactively lead the practise by setting strategy, defining business plan and road map, drive the development of new business in the market, and provide advice across disciplines</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Creation, introduction and management of new service offerings and capabilities to meet market and growth opportunities</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Forecasting revenue and hours demand by resource type</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Internal status on consulting operations</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Service Planning</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Cross group collaboration on commercial management of consulting engagements (bid to delivery)</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Resource capacity management against the on-going project demands</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Negotiation of cross group resource utilization with peers to accommodate anticipated peaks in demand</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Collaboration with project leadership in project planning, resourcing, risk management and quality management</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Service Delivery</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Provide oversight to project leadership for risk &amp; quality management across all engagements</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Provide oversight to team leadership for the technical and business work-order project delivery process</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Establishing a consulting culture that balances financial goals with customer satisfaction.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Escalation point for client engagement relationship, resourcing, risk management and quality management</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Ownership of hiring process of new consulting team members</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Daily leadership of consulting practice managers and team members</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Training, professional development and career planning for consulting team</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Manage and deliver large projects by developing the project team, assessing engagement risks throughout, driving conclusions, and reviewing / challenging the output produced by the team</p>
<br>
</li>
