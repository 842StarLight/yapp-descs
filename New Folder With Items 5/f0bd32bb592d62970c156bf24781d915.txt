<h2>Call Center / Reservation Agent</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>Pirates Dinner Adventure is looking for part-time Reservation Agent that is customer service oriented and requires communicating with hundreds of different individuals in a pleasant and prompt manner. The main communication for this position stems from phone calls and emails, and so we're looking for someone who is enjoyable over the phone and also well-written.</p>
<p><b>
Job Duties:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Establish clear expectations for customers throughout the sales process.</li>
<li>
Be the benchmark example for service delivery and execution – personalize products and services.</li>
<li>
Transfers accurate, complete and timely information to operating departments within the reservation department.</li>
<li>
Effectively resolves guest issues that arise as a result of the sales process. Brings issues to the attention of the Leadership Team as appropriate.</li>
<li>
Share recommendations to eliminate customer service issues.</li>
<li>
Demonstrate strong organization and problem-solving skills.</li>
<li>
Maintain knowledge of our terms and conditions.</li>
<li>
Use strong communication skills (verbal, listening, writing); utilize confident telephone skills and etiquette.</li>
<li>
Maintain a broad understanding of the products and calendar to act as a Reservation Agent and sell a memorable experience to customers in all capacities.</li>
<li>
Work cohesively with other departments and co-workers as part of a synergistic team.</li>
<li>
Adhere to all standards, policies, and procedures.</li>
<li>
Perform other duties as assigned to meet business needs as assigned.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Minimum Qualifications:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
High School Diploma or equivalent</li>
<li>
Excellent selling skills and understanding of sales processes; can bring a sale to closure quickly at a high return rate</li>
<li>
Knowledge of basic revenue management and need time strategies</li>
<li>
Ability to influence others to upsell products and services with excellent negotiation skills</li>
<li>
Strong communication skills (verbal, listening, writing) – possesses confident telephone skills and etiquette</li>
<li>
Ability to use reservations software</li>
<li>
Ability to accurately input and access data</li>
<li>
Must be able to prioritize, organize and follow-up in a timely manner</li>
<li>
Must be able to work cohesively with other departments, co-workers as part of a synergistic team</li>
<li>
Ability to work a varied schedule including, days, nights, weekends, and holidays</li>
</ul>
