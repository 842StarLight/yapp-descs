<h2>Photo Booth Production Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Our SMF team is looking for a Production Manager to join ourCompany. The Production Manager’s role is to safeguard and manage all events across the Bay Area. You will also work closely with the sales team to aid in client pitches by demonstrating our ever-evolving capabilities and developing budgets for custom activations and builds.</p>
<p>All resumes must include email. Interviews will be conducted in person. DO NOT CALL about the job.</p>
<p><b>RESPONSIBILITIES: </b></p>
<p>You will manage logistics, production, hardware and software to execute the event and preside over the post-production deliverables to our client. It will be your responsibility to holistically know the capabilities of our software and hardware.</p>
<p><b>ONGOING RESPONSIBILITIES: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Ensuring all equipment is in proper working condition</li>
<li>Working events as a Technician</li>
<li>Inventory tracking</li>
<li>Ordering supplies need for events and office purposes</li>
<li>Coordinating logistical needs with other areas</li>
<li>Participating in new software release testing and training of staff</li>
<li>Maintaining the organization and overall cleanliness of the office</li>
<li>Monthly equipment audits</li>
<li>Approval of operator time sheets</li>
<li>Communicating with clients and projects before and after the event</li>
</ul>
<p><b>QUALIFICATIONS: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>You have previous event production experience</li>
<li>You have some experience in digital photography</li>
<li>You’re at your best when you’re working as part of a team but can work independently as well</li>
<li>You’re tech savvy in both hardware and software</li>
<li>You’re reliable and always on-time</li>
<li>You're proficient in Windows</li>
<li>You’re a problem-solver and calm under pressure</li>
<li>You have excellent interpersonal and communication skills and enjoy interacting with new people.</li>
<li>You can provide tech support</li>
<li>Available to work some afternoon/evenings on Thurs/Fri/Sat for events</li>
<li>Outgoing personality (comfortable talking to people, staff and clients)</li>
<li>Access to a vehicle</li>
<li>21+</li>
<li>Able to lift 50 lbs</li>
<li>Able to work 8 hour day on your feet</li>
<li>Pay dependent upon experience and qualifications.</li>
