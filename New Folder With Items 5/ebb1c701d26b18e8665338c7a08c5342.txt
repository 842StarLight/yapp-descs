<h2>Leasing Consultant- Ponderosa Ranch</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Avanti Residential is a rapidly growing property management company comprised of 26 apartment communities and approximately 5,500 units in the Colorado and Arizona markets. The work environment is challenging, fast-paced, multi-faceted and dynamic. We are a privately held company with our corporate offices in a downtown, professional setting poised for tremendous growth in the coming years giving a successful candidate a wide range of future career growth opportunities.</p>
<p>JOB SUMMARY:</p>
<p>The Leasing Consultant assists the Community Manager and Leasing Manager with showing and leasing of available apartment homes to prospective residents. This position ensures a positive working environment, quality community living, and a high standard of operations to meet Avanti Residential objectives and marketing plans.</p>
<p>ESSENTIAL DUTIES</p>
<p>· Completes lease form or agreement and collects rental deposit.</p>
<p>· Responsible for insuring the “model” apartments and target apartments are ready for show; responsible for maintaining work area and office in clean and orderly manner; need to have an awareness of the “curb appeal” of the community.</p>
<p>· Thorough knowledge of lease terms, specifications and all community policies.</p>
<p>· Knowledgeable of required telephone and sales techniques.</p>
<p>· Knowledgeable of current market conditions.</p>
<p>· Fully informed of current rental rates, sizes, locations and all amenities of community.</p>
<p>· Maintain contact with all apartment locator services and local businesses to provide informational materials.</p>
<p>· Responsible for “shopping” competitive properties.</p>
<p>· Develop full knowledge of application information required, screening processes and policies regarding rentals.</p>
<p>· Responsible to conduct out-reach marketing at least once per week.</p>
<p>· Develop and maintain an on-going resident retention program under direction of Community Manager.</p>
<p>· Responsible to play an active role in the renewal process.</p>
<p>· Responsible for proper maintenance of all resident and community lease files.</p>
<p>· Responsible for maintaining and updating Leasing and Property Handbook and Advertising Log Book.</p>
<p>· Efficient and timely processing of all required administrative reports and related information.</p>
<p>· Responsible for reporting unusual or extraordinary circumstances regarding the community or residents.</p>
<p>· Responsible for assisting Community Manager in collection of delinquent rents.</p>
<p>· Courteous, efficient handling of resident requests and complaints.</p>
<p>· Represent the company in a professional manner at all the times.</p>
<p>· Perform other related duties and responsibilities as required.</p>
<p>KEY COMPETENCIES:</p>
<p>· Must have excellent organizational and time management skills to successfully navigate frequent interruptions and changing priorities</p>
<p>· Proficient computer skills including knowledge of property management software preferably YARDI</p>
<p>· Work independently with minimal supervision and be accountable for actions, self-motivated</p>
<p>· Excellent communication skills - written and verbal</p>
<p>· Demonstrated knowledge of phone and email procedures and etiquette</p>
<p>· Thrive in a fast-paced environment; Ability to prioritize and meet deadlines</p>
<p>· Positive attitude, professional demeanor, team player</p>
<p>EDUCATION AND EXPERIENCE GUIDELINES - Any combination of education and experience that would likely provide the required knowledge and abilities is qualifying:</p>
<p>· High School Diploma or GED</p>
<p>· One to two-year of Customer Service experience preferred</p>
<p>REQUIREMENTS</p>
<p>· Possession of valid driver’s license and up to date vehicle insurance OR reliable transportation</p>
<p>· Prior to employment pass criminal background screening</p>
<p>Salary for this full-time position will be commensurate with experience. We offer excellent benefits including medical, dental, vision and life insurance, short-and long-term disability insurance, flexible spending account and a 401(k). Housing discount is offered.</p>
<p>Job Type: Full-time</p>
<p>Experience:</p>
<ul>
<li>leasing: 1 year (Preferred)</li>
<li>Customer Service: 1 year (Preferred)</li>
</ul>
<p>Education:</p>
<ul><li>High school or equivalent (Preferred)</li></ul>

<ul><li>One location</li></ul>
