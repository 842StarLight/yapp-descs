<p>
Oldcastle BuildingEnvelope®, a CRH company, is the leading supplier of products specified to close the building envelope. They include: custom-engineered curtain wall and window wall, architectural windows, storefront systems, doors, skylights and architectural glass.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Job Summary</b></p>
<p>
Our location in Middleburg Heights, OH is looking for a <b>Project Manager</b> to join our team. A perfect candidate will demonstrate leadership in safety while maintaining world-class quality and optimum productivity. This is a training position with intended progression into a Project Manager role based on performance and business volume. This candidate will work closely with a Senior Project Manager and support their roles and responsibilities. This position may have daily or weekend overtime as dictated by production needs.
</p>
<p><b>Essential Duties and Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Review of architectural, structural drawings, fabrication and shop drawing submittals against scope of work in the proposal.</li>
<li>
Manage the development of shop drawing plans, elevations, wall sections and details for architectural glazing products using estimates, take-offs, project specifications, and/or architectural and structural drawings</li>
<li>
Prepare, update and communicate project schedules. Meet scheduled completion dates as outlined by the program schedule</li>
<li>
Reviewing/revising drawings, per red line markups and written directions from customer, and other submittal documents as needed.
</li>
<li>Create and manage fabrication documents from the approved shop drawings or inputs provided</li>
<li>
Create and manage tag drawings for packing and installation</li>
<li>
Coordinate outside resources as needed to achieve project delivery goals.</li>
<li>
Create material order paperwork, quantifying glass, aluminum parts, steel, etc.</li>
<li>
Coordinate project information/documentation distribution, storage, and retrieval.</li>
<li>
Coordinate software and data interface between multiple internal and external customers and departments.
</li>
<li>Coordinate materials, inventory requirements and shipments through product design, fabrication and installation.</li>
<li>
Resolution of issues with internal and external customers that may arise during the project.</li>
<li>
Complete program support responsibilities for clients relating to the coordination of shop drawings/submittals, fabrication documents, and material orders.</li>
<li>
Use AutoCAD, Inventor and Revit software to create and manage project documents.</li>
<li>
Work directly with clients on design or cost initiatives under the direction of our sales consultants.</li>
<li>
Provide support to sales and estimating for new and ongoing projects.</li>
<li>
Regular and predictable attendance, safely perform tasks and adherence to all safety policies and procedures are essential functions of this job.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Skills, Knowledge, and Ability Requirements</b></p>
<p>
To be considered for the Project Manager position, the following conditions must be met:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Minimum Four (4) year B.S. Degree in Construction, Engineering or related discipline</li>
<li>
Must be capable of reading architectural plans and structural drawings.</li>
<li>
Must be functional in AutoCAD and Microsoft Excel/Word/Project.</li>
<li>
Functional knowledge of Revit or Inventor is a plus</li>
<li>
Experience with AlphaCam/CNC programming and panel fabrication documents a plus</li>
<li>
Experience in Engineering, Estimating, Construction related Manufacturing, or Commercial Glazing, Curtain Wall or Skylight industry is a plus.</li>
<li>
Experience in supporting estimating and material coordination</li>
<li>
Must be self-motivated and flexible, with the ability to function in a team environment.</li>
<li>
Ability to travel periodically to job sites and /or customers.</li>
<li>
Good communication skills with the ability to write concise technical business correspondence.</li>
<li>
Ability to work under pressure and comfortable with change and time constraints.</li>
<li>
Ability to negotiate in a fair, firm, professional and friendly manner.</li>
</ul>
