<h2>Sr. Integrated Marketing Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Crawford Group (www.crawfordgroup.com) is looking for a Senior Integrated Marketing Manager for a client based in Cary, NC. The qualified Senior Integrated Marketing Manager will drive revenue marketing strategy, programs and outcomes for our business, specifically in the Healthcare and Manufacturing verticals. This role is heavily focused on the full client lifecycle including new client acquisition and client relationship expansion. Will partner and build relationships with members of the sales, marketing, product, and client success teams to ensure alignment on go-to-market strategy, plans and execution. This position will report to the Senior Director of Revenue Marketing.</p>
<p>The ideal candidate is someone with the proven ability to grow revenue through the development, and execution of targeted, multi-touch, multi-channel campaigns that speak to the hearts and minds of our prospects and clients. Will own campaign performance through to revenue, will develop strong relationships with sales partners and prioritize the most impactful marketing efforts to achieve those joint objectives. Will have a full understanding of the range of marketing programs available to reach the targeted audience – be it through highly targeted account based marketing or broader brand to demand campaigns spanning traditional and digital marketing channels. This is an ongoing role that sits onsite at the client's office in Cary, NC.</p>
<p>Responsibilities:</p>
<ul>
<li>Devise integrated marketing campaign strategy by with the aim of acquiring and retaining clients to meet pipeline targets in the Healthcare and Manufacturing Verticals</li>
<li>Create and lead the execution of campaign plans – including objectives, segmentation and messaging by persona and lifecycle stage, activation, sequence planning, performance metrics, and reporting. This includes being “hands on” with things like partner media buys, email, web page, webinar production</li>
<li>Partner, partner, partner. With product and sales to design and prioritize marketing programs and campaigns. With product marketing to craft the strongest positioning and launch new products. With revenue marketing to flawlessly execute, test and adjust. With brand and content to define the content and assets required at each stage of the campaign journey. With sale people to understand how leads are performing. With analytics to understand performance and frequently provide the right altitude of insights on results to stakeholders</li>
<li>Define budget needs, work within budget and measure ROI to marketing investment</li>
<li>Provide regular reports of campaign status, results, and tracking and measurement. Collect and use data to inform new campaigns and the evaluation of existing campaigns</li>
<li>Success metrics/KPIs for this role include new Annual Recruiting Revenue (ARR) booked from prospects and current clients, ROI on marketing campaigns, and a qualitative component of partnership effectiveness with cross functional teams</li>
</ul>
<p>Requirements:</p>
<ul>
<li>8+ years of high-tech B2B marketing, experience in SaaS and Account Based Marketing a plus</li>
<li>Bachelor’s degree in business or marketing; MBA preferred</li>
<li>Proven integrated campaign experience in a multi-product, persona and vertical environment</li>
<li>Excellent cross-functional partnership skills, with proven ability to drive alignment and accountability</li>
<li>Strong strategic thinking coupled with the ability to roll up your sleeves and make things happen</li>
<li>Exceptional communications and storytelling abilities, including written and verbal</li>
<li>Deep analytical skill set to identify insights and optimize execution of campaigns/programs</li>
<li>Marketo experience highly desired and knowledge of Salesforce</li>
</ul>
<p>Location: Onsite at the client's office in Cary, NC<br>
Duration: Ongoing<br>
Rate: DOE, W2</p>
