<h2>Customer Care Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Overview
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div>Challenger Lifts, Inc., a division of Snap-on is seeking a candidate that would be responsible to supervise a staff of Inside Sales Associates, Service/Inspection Team Associates and the Service Tech Department to provide customer support to end-users, distributors, representatives, sales managers and other Snap-on organizations. Seek to improve customer relationships by responding to inquires in a timely manner. Ensure pricing accuracy of purchase orders and order processing. Support Challenger organization as required.</div>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<ul>
<li>Lead a team of 17 associates in the areas of Inside Sales, Managed Care, and Service</li>
<li>Create an environment that enables associates to perform key tasks in a timely and professional manner</li>
<li>Coordinate and lead the response to customer inquiries involving billing or delivery in a timely and professional manner.</li>
<li>Define process to complete the review of customer purchase orders for accuracy of information in preparation of data entry.</li>
<li>Manage data entry processing of orders into the ERP database.</li>
<li>Ensure all order acknowledgements are issued as required.</li>
<li>Monitor orders, back orders, expedite and prioritize orders for equipment and accessories, as needed.</li>
<li>Generate metrics or reports and participate as part of management team as required.</li>
<li>Perform Associate Performance Reviews and Goal Setting in accordance with Company Policies and Procedures</li>
</ul>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Qualifications
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<ul>
<li>College graduate preferred</li>
<li>Familiarization of Microsoft Office, Excel and Access software.</li>
<li>Strong interpersonal skills in a team environment.</li>
<li>Good problem solving skills</li>
<li>Excellent organizational skills with ability to prioritize and manage time effectively. Must be multi-task oriented.</li>
<li>Excellent written and oral communication skills with ability and experience in communicating with all levels of Management.</li>
<li>Working knowledge of contracts and contract negotiation skills</li>
<li>Able to work in high-stress, fast-paced environment.</li>
<li>Good attendance record.</li>
<li>Able to work flexible hours</li>
</ul>
