<h2>Assistant Property Manager/Self-Storage</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Jack Rabbit Storage has a great opportunity</b> available for a full-time Assistant Store Manager. Our Assistant Managers are second in charge assisting our Managers in the day to day operation of the facility and filling in for them in their absence. <i>This position will require you to fill it at two stores; reliable transportation is necessary.</i></p>
<p><b>Primary Responsibilities include: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Answer customer inquiries via phone, in person and internet.</li>
<li>Assist customers through showing available units, merchandise sales and truck rental.</li>
<li>Follow up on reservations and inquiries.</li>
<li>Prepare vacant units for rent.</li>
<li>Process daily payments and complete daily bank deposit.</li>
<li>Contact delinquent customers for payment.</li>
<li>General office neatness &amp; light cleaning.</li>
<li>Property upkeep &amp; cleanliness</li>
<li>Other Duties &amp; Responsibilities as needed.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Minimum Requirements: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Customer service experience in retail or office environment.</li>
<li>Excellent Customer Service Skills and enjoy assisting customers.</li>
<li>Ability to work well independently when required and as part of management team.</li>
<li>Proficient skills with Microsoft Office applications.</li>
<li>Able to quickly learn and navigate specialized software.</li>
<li>Work during all four seasons in varying weather conditions.</li>
<li>Type 35 WPM and be proficient with computer skills.</li>
<li>High School Diploma</li>
<li>Able to stand, walk or sit for a combined eight hours daily.</li>
<li>Able to walk property daily, lift 25lb garage door, use step stool or small ladder.</li>
<li>Able to lift and move inventory.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Hours: </b> FULL TIME 40 hours. Scheduled hours will be a mix of weekdays 8:30am - 5:30pm and Saturday 8:30am - 3pm. Only highly motivated individuals need apply.</p>
<p><b><i>Only Qualified applicants will be contacted</i></b></p>
<p><b>Qualified Applicants will be asked to complete a computer skills &amp; knowledge test and to pass a pre-employment screening.</b></p>
