<p><b>
Function</b></p>
<p>
As a member of the Global Advanced Manufacturing Engineering team one of your key functions is to develop and execute the strategy for Industrial Digital Transformation / Industry 4.0. The focus of the role will be to identify, prioritize, and help drive global implementation across Whirlpool’s manufacturing sites all the work-streams for Industrial Digital Transformation.
</p>
<p>You will spend time supporting implementation of production data tools, automation, production intelligence capabilities, and Smart Factory Industry systems, coordinating reviews of potential systems with other engineers in Global Advanced Manufacturing Engineering and other groups. You may spend time at any of our facilities worldwide to review existing capabilities, making recommendations for Industry 4.0 technologies, digital transformation implementation or developing a global standard. You may be part of a cross-functional team with Advanced Manufacturing, Information Systems and Operations Excellence group, working to drive execution of an Industry 4.0 technology from Scouting, Trial, Pilot, Deployment and Rollout.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Responsibilities</b></p>
<p>
This is a broad-based engineering application in the emerging Industrial Digital Transformation and Industry 4.0 discipline. You will play a lead role in investigating and implementing a global strategy to establish connectivity, traceability, and data visibility within our manufacturing environment; taking into account the best possible solution from aspects such as cost, simplicity, effectiveness and agility. You will work globally with Engineering &amp; Technology, Platform, and Manufacturing teams to distinguish ‘hype from reality’ within emerging Industry 4.0 technologies and develop plans to pilot and implement scalable production solutions that will reduce costs and improve product quality. You will function as the smart factory specialist, clearly communicating and explaining different technologies’ capabilities and limitations in both technical and non-technical manners. You will collaborate with regional I4.0 teams, who drive implementation initiatives, to provide technical expertise.</p>
<p>
We are seeking a “hands-on” engineer with exceptional system design and analytical skills to play a key role in conceptualizing, developing and validating new concepts for existing and next-generation smart factory manufacturing systems. The successful candidate must excel in a high-energy, team environment and be capable of making sound decisions when facing time pressures, ambiguous, and incomplete information typical of research and development work. You must also have a proven track record of bringing new concepts into reality in a timely and cost-effective manner.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
You will:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Develop and maintain an expert level of technical knowledge in multiple Industry 4.0 / Smart Factory technologies. These may include automation, vision systems, artificial intelligence, data analytics (visualization &amp; predictive modeling), and edge technologies</li>
<li>
Project Manage industrial digital transformation execution workstream, collaborate with regional global leads, while removing roadblocks to implementation.</li>
<li>
Build initial Business Case and Advanced Bill of Process activities within the Smart Factory related technologies, gaining participation and agreement from manufacturing experts globally</li>
<li>
Develop technology roadmaps and drive a metric-driven implementation for Whirlpool manufacturing on key Smart Factory technologies</li>
<li>
Identify potential opportunities for beneficial application of I4.0 systems within the plants and develop plans for implementation</li>
<li>
Identify new technologies developing Proof of Concept and implementation projects</li>
<li>
Evaluate capital equipment purchases to ensure that appropriate investments are made to enable connectivity, traceability, and data visibility</li>
<li>
Support manufacturing by ensuring proper design for cost and maintainability</li>
<li>
Assume various Project Management roles on projects as assigned or required</li>
<li>
Must be willing to travel 30-40% of the time; work will require travel to Asia, Mexico, Europe, and/or Latin America in addition to U.S. locations.</li>
</ul>
