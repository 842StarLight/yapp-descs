<h2>DSD Pricing Clerk</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Overview
 </b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div>To assist in keying and maintenance of all pricing files and support of the Retail Services Team</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
This is a level 3 hourly position.</div>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div>Areas of responsibility include, but are not limited to:</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<ul>
<li>
Responsible for keying and maintenance of the DSD pricing files to ensure customer's needs and gross profit objectives are met.</li>
<li>
Provide efficient and courteous support, problem resolution, and follow-up on customer pricing issues and concerns.</li>
<li>
Assist retailers with vendor support: delivery issues, cost discrepancies, contact information, etc.</li>
<li>
Coordinate and communicate with DSD vendors for pricing updates, allowances, new item information, store set schedules, etc.</li>
<li>
Assist with keying of sales plans and specific MDI commodities.</li>
<li>
Provide support to MDI pricing department.</li>
<li>
Participate in special projects as required by management.</li>
<li>
May be required to perform other tasks as assigned by management.</li>
</ul>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Qualifications
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div>Must have at least one year of customer service or supermarket/retail sales experience and rated fully effective on performance review.</div>
<div>
Above average organizational skills.</div>
<div>
Ability to meet deadlines and work in a fast paced environment.</div>
<div>
Must be self-motivated.</div>
<div>
Must have a sense of urgency in customer satisfaction and be able to keep matters and pricing confidential.</div>
<div>
knowledge of mainframe applications and IMS software applications preferred.</div>
<div>
Must be dependable, have an excellent attendance record, ability to work flexible hours, and work independently.</div>
<div>
Knowledge of GTIN/UPC/PLU identifiation; department and tax classifications necessary for scanning operations preferred.</div>
</div></div></div>
