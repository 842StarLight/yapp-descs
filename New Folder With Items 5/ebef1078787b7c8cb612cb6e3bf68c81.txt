<h2>Department Lead - Manufacturing</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>Herff Jones, A Varsity Achievement Brand, is looking to hire a Department Lead - Manufacturing. The Department Lead will assist supervisors in coordinating, tracking, and training activities of employees in the factory gown mover department. The Department Lead will regulate, balance, and expedite the work throughout the departments. In the absence of the supervisor or when needed, may take on supervisor’s responsibilities such as overseeing other employees duties and any other duties assigned.<br>
<br>
<b>What you’ll be doing on a daily basis:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Supports supervisor in ensuring proper work flow through the department</li>
<li>Assists with balancing Eton Mover and minor equipment repair</li>
<li>Works with all operators to maintain quality and efficiency standards</li>
<li>Assists in the training of new personnel</li>
<li>Assists in checking finished product quality to meet standards</li>
<li>Logs production information, including errors</li>
<li>Works in Oracle, Eton, and other software programs as needed</li>
<li>Helps conduct daily/weekly, department meetings to discuss quality &amp; efficiency</li>
<li>Aids and assists supervisor as needed</li>
</ul>
<b>What you will bring to the table:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>High school diploma or equivalent required, bachelors’ degree preferred</li>
<li>3-5 years production experience (manufacturing preferred); experience with planning and tracking production/work flow experience desired</li>
<li>Proficiency with MS Office, specifically Excel</li>
<li>Must be able to work in a fast paced environment, must be highly organized and disciplined to complete tasks independently. Must have strong leadership abilities and communication skills.</li>
</ul>
<b>Physical Requirements:</b>Daily activity includes but is not limited to lifting, carrying, sitting, fingering, grasping, reaching, standing, kneeling, walking; standing or moving throughout an 8 hour+ shift; be able to regularly lift, push, and pull up to 50lbs; noise level in the work environment is usually moderate to above.<br>
