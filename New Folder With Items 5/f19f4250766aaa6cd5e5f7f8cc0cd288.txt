<b>Summary/Objective:</b><br>
The main purpose of this role is to work in a team selling environment with more experienced enterprise sales reps on the team to grow and close bookings of higher velocity, lower sized (inbound) sales opportunities and to create, develop and close new (outbound) opportunities. Candidates will be responsible for achieving bookings goals on a continuous basis by managing, building and developing a pipeline of sales opportunities. This position requires a hunter sales acumen.
<br><br>
<b>What you will do:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Manage (inbound) or find (outbound), qualify and close higher velocity and volume opportunities within team sales territory</li>
<li>Own and manage the sales cycle leveraging a value-based selling approach</li>
<li>Present solutions to meet client's business needs and fulfill requirements</li>
<li>Responsible for accurate and timely opportunity updates and bookings forecast</li>
<li>Interaction with sales development reps to drive opportunity creation, revenue generation</li>
<li>Utilize telephone communication as the primary means of contacting customers, articulating solutions, developing and closing sales opportunities</li>
<li>Meeting or exceeding company standards for customer phone contact and documentation of customer interactions</li>
</ul>
