<h2>Channel Account Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Perimeter 81 is looking for highly professional, personable and tech-savvy Channel Partner Manager to join our rapidly expanding, venture-backed startup with offices in NYC.</p>
<p>
Reporting to the VP Sales, the Channel Partner Manager (CPM) will be responsible for maximizing partner adoption and ensuring the expansion of Perimeter 81’s services, all while giving value to the partner. As the CPM, you will act as a trusted advisor and guide partners through their SaaS journey — from service onboarding to product review — ensuring all customers get the full value of their investment with Perimeter 81.</p>
<p>
If you are a skilled communicator, thrive on building customer loyalty, and are passionate about promoting account growth, this job is for you.<br>
</p>
<p><br>
<b>Job Description</b><br>
</p>
<p>As the Channel Partner Manager, you will be responsible for:</p>
<ul>
<li>Managing new and existing channel partners to increase sales</li>
<li>Building a positive working relationship with partners to ensure smooth partner services</li>
<li>Identifying, recruiting and onboarding new channel partners to build the business</li>
<li>Collaborating with partners to identify new business opportunities for revenue generation</li>
<li>Educating partners on the product portfolio and complimentary services offered</li>
<li>Working with partners to conduct product presentations to end-users</li>
<li>Providing proposals, quotes and pricing to partners</li>
<li>Analyzing sales contracts with respect to legal and financial perspectives</li>
<li>Developing a profitable business model based on market trends and competitor activity</li>
<li>Implementing sales programs for product positioning and promotions</li>
<li>Ensuring partners are up-to-date with product information</li>
<li>Introducing new Perimeter 81 services and features to partners</li>
<li>Conducting business reviews with partners and recommending improvements</li>
<li>Working with each partner to ensure the fit of our solutions to their needs and addressing business outcomes for our common customers</li>
<li>Maintaining highly collaborative relationships with the Presales, Operations, Product, Marketing and Customer Success teams</li>
<li>Performing detailed reviews and regular reporting on an ongoing basis – and including the wider teams when necessary communicating opportunities and risks</li>
<li>Promoting partners’ Perimeter 81 sales and marketing initiatives, overseeing and maintaining the partner PRM portal</li>
<li>Creating and executing a business plan with the American sales team, building your vision, direction, and priorities to achieve/exceed Y/Y growth objectives</li>
<li>Developing effective go-to-market channels in the networking ecosystems. Creating and managing initiatives to increase the partner base, market penetration, and drive revenue streams across new consumption models.</li>
<li>Accurately forecasting monthly and quarterly sales revenue with all your partners</li>
<li>Fostering strong relationships with the key players within the new/existing partner base</li>
</ul>
<p>Requirements:<br>
</p>
<ul>
<li>3-5 years of experience as a Channel Partner Manager - working in the SaaS or cybersecurity sector</li>
<li>Proven ability to develop and cultivate lasting partner relationships</li>
<li>Technological mindset – able to have a technological discussion with IT managers and senior stakeholders, as well as high-level technical meetings</li>
<li>Exceptional written and verbal communication skills and the ability to convey complex technical messages in a clear and simple way</li>
<li>Strong team-player and collaborator</li>
<li>Proven experience meeting and exceeding sales quotas and accurately forecasting the business</li>
<li>Demonstrated success selling and supporting within an indirect channel or partner-based sales program</li>
<li>Skilled at nurturing strong collaborative relationships with partners</li>
<li>Strong understanding of the industry climates</li>
<li>Track record of collaborating and demonstrated success in sales organizations that fostered teamwork</li>
<li>Startup experience is an advantage</li>
<li>Experience in selling cloud-based solutions is an advantage</li>
<li>BA/BS Degree, MBA or Computer Science or Engineering degree is an advantage</li>
</ul>
