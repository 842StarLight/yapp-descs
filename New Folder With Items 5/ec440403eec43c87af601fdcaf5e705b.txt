<p>Inbound Sales Professional - Product Specialist</p>
<p>Wayfair Sales is a team that has reinvented the way you shop for the home you love! Our dedicated sales agents are committed to providing a genuine, personal shopping experience by helping customers Discover More, Rest Assured and Feel Confident in their buying decisions. Our sales agents begin their career at Wayfair with an in-depth, paid training program that prepares them to help consumers feel confident in choosing to shop with Wayfair. Wayfair Sales agents focus on providing world-class customer experiences while building revenue for the family of Wayfair brands.</p>
<p>What You'll Do</p>
<ul>
<li>You work to achieve metrics tied to your own financial gain (revenue, close/conversion, CSAT)</li>
<li>You explore our products and services with customers and help them make buying decisions, through upselling and cross-selling</li>
<li>You provide solutions and ask for the sale on every call</li>
<li>You show professionalism in every interaction</li>
<li>You navigate many tools including multiple software applications and communication technologies</li>
<li>You efficiently manage your time and always put the customer experience first</li>
<li>You are comfortable working in a competitive and ever-changing/fast-paced environment</li>
</ul>
<p>What You'll Need</p>
<ul>
<li>2+ years of sales experience, preferably in a solutions-based selling environment (Retail Management, SaaS, Account Manager, etc.)</li>
<li>High School diploma or equivalent required</li>
<li>Excellent communication and relationship building skills</li>
<li>Ability to respond to inbound phone calls frequently to meet customer requests</li>
<li>Self-motivation to drive metrics, results and key performance indicators (KPIs)</li>
<li>Regular and reliable attendance</li>
<li>Salesforce Customer Relationship Management (CRM) software experience is a plus</li>
</ul>
