<h2>Drafter & Prepress Technician</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Job title</b></p>
<p><i>
Drafter and Prepress Technician</i></p>
<p><b>
Reports to</b></p>
<p><i>
Graphics Manager</i></p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Job purpose</b></p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
This is a newly created hybrid role that will play a big part in the future success of our organization. Overarching responsibilities include the creation of detailed panel and elevation drafting including high-resolution graphics along with the preparation of print files from standard and customer supplied art for digital print production.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Duties and responsibilities</b></p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
Panel and Elevation Drafting</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<ul>
<li>
Analyze internal request details along with customer supplied details.</li>
<li>
Work with Inside Sales department to rectify issues with customer-supplied information.</li>
<li>
Prepare individual panel drawings and elevations in Adobe Illustrator.</li>
<li>
Create low-resolution FPO images in Adobe Illustrator or Adobe Photoshop from standard or customer supplied art files for placement in individual panel drawings and elevations.</li>
<li>
Ensure all drawings and elevations accurately display the products we intend to make.</li>
<li>
Verify that all drawings and elevations show only what we can produce.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
Preparing High-Resolution Graphics for Production and Archiving</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<ul>
<li>
Analyze customer supplied art files for correct format and resolution.</li>
<li>
Work with Inside Sales and Manufacturing departments to ensure all aspects of the job are being considered for timely and correct completion.</li>
<li>
Prepare full-size high-resolution graphics in Adobe Illustrator and Adobe Photoshop for production by sizing and scaling appropriately.</li>
<li>
Monitor job output and ensure color match and consistency to house standards.</li>
<li>
Document all pertinent settings used for job production to ensure repeatability.</li>
<li>
Perform archive and file maintenance duties as needed.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Qualifications</b></p>
<p></p>
<br>
<ul>
<li>
Proficient in the latest versions of Adobe Illustrator and Adobe Photoshop, preferably on Mac.</li>
<li>
Advanced skills and knowledge of Adobe Illustrator, 3 years' experience or greater.</li>
<li>
Prior drafting experience.</li>
<li>
Ability to work cross-platform Mac and PC.</li>
<li>
Strong color correction skills in Photoshop.</li>
<li>
Strong problem-solving skills.</li>
<li>
Strong communication skills, both verbal and written.</li>
<li>
Attention to detail and ability to manage multiple assignments concurrently.</li>
<li>
Ability to work in a fast-paced environment.</li>
<li>
Digital and/or Commercial Print production experience a plus.</li>
<li>
Experience with RIP software, specifically Onyx and Wasatch a plus.</li>
<li>
Familiarity with construction drawings.</li>
</ul>
