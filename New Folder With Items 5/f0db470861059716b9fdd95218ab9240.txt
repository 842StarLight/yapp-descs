<p><b>About Can Can Wonderland</b></p>
<p>Located in St. Paul’s Hamline Midway neighborhood and the Creative Enterprise Zone, Can Can Wonderland combines art and play to create a one-of-a-kind arts immersive environment for people of all ages to enjoy.</p>
<p>We offer a truly unique, 18-hole artist designed, indoor mini golf course. But Can Can Wonderland isn’t just mini golf; it’s a wonderland full of arts-based fauna and flora for you to explore and delight in! Our boardwalk is full of attractions and amusements (created by/with artists). Our two stages offer a wide array of local and regional talents. We have two bars and possibly a secret bar. We have noshes! We even have a few unicorns.</p>
<p>We’re looking for people who…</p>
<ul>
<li>Have a guest-focused, friendly approach towards service</li>
<li>Have a passion for hospitality and providing exceptional guest service</li>
<li>Want to learn everything possible about Can Can Wonderland</li>
<li>Are passionate about our social purpose</li>
<li>Have a warm personality</li>
<li>Have a solid work ethic</li>
<li>Love people</li>
</ul>

<li>Ability to navigate technology well and use a POS system</li>
<li>Maintain excellent cleaning standards by performing cleaning dues such as scrubbing and polishing counters, equipment, dishes, sweeping, and mopping</li>
<li>Replenish food at serving stations</li>
<li>Maintain inventory</li>
<li>Attend all scheduled employee meetings and offer constructive suggestions for improvement</li>
<li>Work well in a team</li>
