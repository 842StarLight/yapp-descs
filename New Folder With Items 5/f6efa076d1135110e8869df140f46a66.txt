<div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Inbound Marketing Manager (Boston, MA office or Boulder, CO remote)</b></h3>
<br>
<div>
</div>
<div>Our mission is to transform the way online learning experiences are created, managed, and delivered on a global scale. Our goal is to become the premier platform for organizations growing their online learning businesses. We are a fast-growing tech company - a people-first organization with a flexible, fun, hard working environment.</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div>As our Inbound Marketing Manager you will work with product, technology, and sales / marketing peers to educate website visitors about the value of partnering with Thought Industries. Your primary responsibilities will be increasing high quality organic traffic to the Thought Industries site, converting those visitors to leads, and shepherding those leads down funnel to become loyal customers.</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div>This position mashes up creative and analytical skills, and requires an agile intellect that can blend customer intelligence, storytelling, data manipulation, and technology that converts intent into action.</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div><b>In this role, you’ll be successful if you can:</b></div>
<br>
<div></div>
<ul>
<li>
<div>Build and execute a successful Inbound strategy that meets Thought Industries goals of quality leads.</div>
<br>
</li>
<li>
<div>Use SEO best practices to scale the creation of authoritative, entertaining original content optimized for search and distribute that content via partner sites to drive qualified traffic to the Thought Industries site.</div>
<br>
</li>
<li>
<div>Increase high-quality, organic social engagement and visits via LinkedIn, Facebook, and other relevant social channels.</div>
<br>
</li>
<li>
<b>
</b><div>Use technical SEO best practices to ensure the Thought Industries public site architecture meets Google search expectations; use CRO best practices to optimize the user experience and improve the site lead conversion rate.</div>
</li>
<br>
<li>
<b>
</b><div>Maintain Thought Industries’ leadership position via industry awards and continue to build awareness for the Thought Industries brand with impactful PR.</div>
</li>
<br>
<li>
<div>Use Google Analytics, Salesforce and other data sources to track Inbound performance, and focus on continuous improvement.</div>
<br>
</li>
<li>
<div>Manage a distributed group of contractors responsible for delivering critical aspects of your Inbound strategy.</div>
<br>
</li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<div><b>Sound like something you’d love to do? Awesome! Your background and experience should include, but is not necessarily limited to the following:</b></div>
<br>
<div></div>
<ul>
<li>
<div>2-3 years of experience working in a B2B / SaaS-focused, data-driven online marketing role, with a strong understanding of best practices throughout all facets of the customer funnel.</div>
<br>
</li>
<li>
<div>Strong left brain / right brain execution that combines creativity with analytical rigor.</div>
<br>
</li>
<li>
<div>Intimate knowledge of content marketing tools (SEMRush, HubSpot, Salesforce preferred), basic HTML / CSS, and Google Analytics.</div>
<br>
</li>
<li>
<div>Demonstrated knowledge of SEO and CRO best practices.</div>
<br>
</li>
<li>
<div>Management of part-time contractors with high expectations for performance.</div>
<br>
</li>
<li>
<div>Excellent writer and content creator (blogs, press releases, eBooks, etc.)</div>
<br>
</li>
<li>
<div>Social media saavy.</div>
<br>
</li>
<li>
<div>Previous experience with online learning software a plus.</div>
<br>
</li>
<li>
<div>Passionate, motivated and hungry to succeed.</div>
<br>
</li>
</ul>
<div><div><div>Thought Industries is based in Boston, MA,. This is a full-time position that can work from the home office or (for the right candidate) remotely in the Boulder, CO area.</div></div></div>
