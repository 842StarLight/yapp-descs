<h2>SHOP SERVICE MANAGER (Heavy Equipment)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>Since 1936 Power Equipment Company (PECO) has been a distributor of heavy construction and aggregate equipment serving the construction, mining and aggregate industries in multiple locations. PECO is looking for an experienced<b> </b><b>Shop Service Manager</b><b> </b>for the Colorado Springs, Colroado location<b>. </b>All locations provide equipment rentals and sales as well as complete parts and service support for all of our product lines. We pride ourselves in our commitment to our customers and are dedicated to supporting your equipment now and in the future in order to guarantee your success.</p>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Position Purpose:</b></h2>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>As the direct point of contact for both internal and external customers, the shop service manager is responsible for directing and supervising the daily activities of the shop service staff, with the goal of ensuring customer satisfaction through timely and accurate completion of repair and maintenance work orders. Timely customer response and timely invoicing with detail is critical to customer satisfaction.
</b></h2>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Essential Job Functions:</b></h2>
<ul>
<li>Maintain a safe and compliant working environment</li>
<li>Schedule all shop service work and assign technicians to specific jobs.
</li>
<li>Oversees all work to ensure work is completed to customer's satisfaction.</li>
<li>Answer incoming calls from customers; provide technical advice, advise customers of estimated repairs, costs and expected completion of work.</li>
<li>Charge out all work orders (internal &amp; external), in a timely, profitable manner.</li>
<li>Maintain and produce accurate records of discussion or correspondence with customers, vendors and associates.</li>
<li>Sees that shop tickets and work orders are properly handled, that proper entries are made concerning time and overtime and that parts and equipment used are accounted for.</li>
<li>Supervises the quality of work performed and sees that standards of performance are maintained.
</li>
<li>Sees that a proper library of manuals, service bulletins, instructions and references for mechanics, equipment and service operations is maintained and properly used.</li>
<li>Notifies the status of current progress of jobs for the sales and rental department, along with activity updates to management.</li>
<li>Work with customers to resolve any complaints/issues.</li>
<li>Recruit, interview and hire any new Shop Service Technicians as needed.
</li>
<li>Mentor existing staff and drive a spirit of respect, cooperation and teamwork.</li>
<li>Develop and monitor ongoing training and retention programs for existing technicians.</li>
<li>Develop and implement processes and procedures to improve operational efficiency.</li>
<li>Purchase and maintain supplies and services for use in the shop.</li>
<li>Ensure company assets are maintained for optimum reliability, safety, while controlling costs.</li>
<li>Work closely with the parts department on delivery, pricing, and returns.</li>
<li>Work closely with the sales and rental department to provide technical and repair support.</li>
<li>Maintain current and valid logs, registrations, licenses for all service vehicles and rental equipment.
</li>
<li>Complete annual reviews of assigned employees</li>
</ul>
<p> <b>Supervisory Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>

Assigned Service Writer(s), and Shop Technicians.</li>
</ul>
<p> <b>Education and/or Experience:</b></p>

High School diploma; Trade School (2 yr. degree) preferred</li>
<li>3 to 5 years' practical experience in the Heavy Equipment Industry.</li>
<li>Supervisory Experience Preferred.
</li>
<li>Volvo equipment experience preferred</li>
<li>QuipWare Experience Preferred.</li>
</ul>

<ul>

<li>Ability to lift up to 60 lbs. and work inside and outdoors in various climate and temperatures.</li>
</ul>

 </li></ul>

 </li>
</ul>
