<div>About Us</div>
<div>
<div>
Beckman Coulter develops, manufactures and markets products that simplify, automate and innovate complex biomedical testing. Our diagnostic systems, found in hospitals and other critical care settings around the world, produce information used by physicians to diagnose disease, make treatment decisions and monitor patients. Scientists use our life science research instruments to study complex biological problems including causes of disease and potential new therapies or drugs. More than 275,000 Beckman Coulter systems operate in both diagnostics and life sciences laboratories on all seven continents. For 80 years, our products have been making a difference in peoples’ lives by improving the productivity of medical professionals and scientists, supplying critical information for improving patient health and reducing the cost of care.</div>

<div>
Description</div>
<div>
<ul>
<li>
Work closely with Sr. Manager, Digital Strategy and Global Marketing leadership to manage the overall website as a product, including feature development, roadmap, backlog of feature requests and implementation plan</li>
<li>
Managing a product through the entire product lifecycle and develop action plans to address technology issues hindering marketing strategy and objectives</li>
<li>
Lead web project estimation and integration sessions; report to marketing leadership regularly site development to show progress toward and impact on projects or goals</li>
<li>
Drive day-to-day project prioritization and sprint planning/grooming with the website development team</li>
<li>
Proactively identify, prioritize and drive resolution for project issues</li>
<li>
Actively address ambiguity and change in the product backlog</li>
<li>
Ensure website layout/user interface implements standard HTML/CSS best practices</li>
<li>
Oversee integration of datasets from various back-end services and databases</li>
<li>
Gather and refine specifications and requirements based on business and/or technical needs</li>
<li>
Manage the implementation of website projects with internal teams and third-party vendors to ensure successful completion within appropriate budget guidelines and project timeline</li>
<li>
Work closely with stakeholders across the company to align strategies, roadmaps, dependencies, and releases with business priorities for maximum impact</li>
<li>
Collaborate with Design, Engineering, Sales, Product, Legal, and Customer Support to rapidly conceptualize, prototype, market validate, and launch new product ideas and innovations.</li>
<li>
Create business cases to be prioritized on the roadmap and collaborate with the engineering, design, marketing, and sales teams to build business cases, requirements/services, and determine the best technical implementation method and schedule. Making thoughtful tradeoffs to balance quality, customer value, and schedule.</li>
<li>
Effectively create user stories, UX/front-end requirements, back-end/functionality requirements, and testing acceptance criteria for project Confluence &amp; JIRA documentation</li>
<li>
Ensure requests, website feedback, and leadership prioritization are integrated into the product planning and development processes as well as vetted through (where applicable) VoC, User Experience and User Research Teams</li>
<li>
Use data and analytics to identify opportunities to improve user experience, service quality, efficiency, and effectiveness for the site</li>
<li>
Define measurable goals and KPIs, set priorities and manage a backlog of features and functionality; work with engineering, design, and QA to meet those requirements.</li>
<li>
Drive planning and vision refinement sessions with business, design and engineering teams; gain support for your product vision through influence and collaboration with peers, partners, and business stakeholders</li>
<li>
Performs user acceptance testing for code deployments and helps to document issues across the website; perform testing in DEV and UAT environments ahead of release to ensure feature enhancements and bug fixes are being delivered to spec</li>
<li>
Monitor network conditions and assist with IT teams to troubleshoot service disruptions for the website.</li>
<li>
Maintain a strong understanding of the industry and enterprise-wide trends, staying up to date on Sitecore, emerging technologies and apply to daily work</li>
<li>
Create and maintain software documentation for ongoing website management/maintenance.</li>
<li>
Direct the work of outside agencies or contractors to supplement internal development</li>
<li>
Train and mentor web developers and web operational support</li>
<li>
Develop and communicate with product and development teams, and Sr. Leadership the business cases, product roadmaps, vision, and strategy.</li>
<li>
Reporting on key performance indicators (KPIs) for code deployments and project roadmaps</li>
<li>
Identify customer needs and use the information to define user personas, user stories, use cases, and product requirements for product features.</li>
<li>
Design and conduct product discovery and optimization experiments using quantitative (A/B tests) and qualitative techniques.</li>
</ul>
