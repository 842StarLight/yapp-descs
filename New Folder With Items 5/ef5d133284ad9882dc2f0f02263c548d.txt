<p><b>Outside Sales Account Manager - Auto Dealer Floor Plan Financing</b></p>
<p><b>
CarBucks Floor Plan, New Orleans/Baton Rouge</b></p>
<p>
Great career opportunity for candidates who have worked in <b>Outside Sales positions such as: Account Managers, Territory Managers, Business Development Professionals, and Sales and Field Representatives</b> who have at least <b>3-5 years of successful B2B </b><b><i>outside sales experience</i></b> to join a <b>premier Auto Floor Plan Financing Company</b> and receive a <b>base salary</b><b> plus </b><b>commission</b><b> and fabulous benefits, including </b><b>company car!</b></p>
<p><b>
Experience selling products or services, such as warranties, advertising, equipment and supplies, etc. to independent auto dealers</b><b> is preferred,</b><b> </b>but not required. Bi-lingual (Spanish) is a plus.</p>
<p>
Candidates with a <b>successful track record</b> in <b>B2B/outside sales roles</b> or <b>field representatives who have experience generating new business, managing a territory, as well as tracking and meeting daily, weekly and monthly sales quotas to achieve bonuses/commissions</b> are generally very successful in this position.</p>
<p><b>
CarBucks Floor Plan</b>, a subsidiary of <b>Grand South Bank</b>, headquartered in Greenville, SC, has retained <b>Smart Work | Network, Inc.,</b> Greenville, SC-based Search Firm, to recruit an experienced <b>Outside Sales Account Manager</b> <b>who lives in or near New Orleans or Baton Rouge, LA to work a territory that runs from Lafayette to Baton Rouge to Hattiesburg, Mississippi, including both Biloxi and Gulfport, MS and the entire New Orleans Metropolitan and surrounding area.</b></p>
<p><b>
You will offer reasonable rates and excellent service to independent auto dealers</b> for the purchase of their inventory with a long-term plan and <b>NO CURTAILMENTS</b> to maximize the dealer’s cash flow.</p>
<p>
As <b>Outsides Sales Account Manager</b>, you will report to the National Sales Manager and be responsible for growing your assigned territory by establishing name recognition for CarBucks and <b>calling on independent used vehicle dealers</b> and <b>developing and maintaining dealer accounts.</b></p>
<p>
Position responsibilities include researching the territory to identify all independent used car dealers, attending auto auctions and establishing a plan to generate applications which convert to approved contracts to meet daily, weekly and monthly sales quotas.</p>
<p>
Successful candidates will have a high degree of initiative, excellent sales skills, be self-directed, able to set own goals, build a plan for achieving those goals and create a workable schedule to consistently work the plan and meet the company’s expectations.</p>
<p><b>
ESSENTIAL DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES</b> include the following:</p>
<ul>
<li>Establishing name recognition for CarBucks in the territory</li>
<li>Calling on independent used vehicle dealers in the territory</li>
<li>Developing and maintaining dealer relationships to build accounts for financing their floor plans</li>
<li>Attending local independent auto auctions regularly to build relationships with dealers</li>
<li>Performing cyclical audits of dealer inventory</li>
<li>Tracking your sales activities through Salesforce.com</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Qualifications: Successful candidates have some or all of the following experience</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Outside sales experience, preferably with variable income (commissions)</li>
<li>Great communication/presentation/relationship/influencing skills</li>
<li>Excellent organizational/time management skills</li>
<li>Professional appearance and behaviors</li>
<li>Interest in independent auto sales industry and auto auctions</li>
<li>Bi-lingual (English-Spanish) is a plus</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Requirements:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Ability to travel and stay occasional overnights</li>
<li>Valid Driver’s License with Clean Driving Record</li>
<li>Basic Microsoft Office Proficiency</li>
<li>Experience with SalesForce.com helpful</li>
<li>Ability to pass Credit Worthiness, Background and Drug Testing</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Compensation/Benefits:</b> Base salary + commission, 401K plan, medical, dental and vision benefits, life/accidental insurance, long term disability, flexible spending account, vacation, holidays, paid personal days, company vehicle and cell phone reimbursement</p>
