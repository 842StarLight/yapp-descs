<h2>Technical Sales Executive</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>Connection is looking to hire a <b>Technical Sales Executive</b>. It is a Fortune 1000 Global Technology Solutions Provider is adding to our family of passionate individuals. We work together to connect people with technology that enhances growth, elevate productivity, and empower innovation. If you would like to discover how over the 35 years of collaboration has shaped our culture of integrity, teamwork, and mutual success, we would love to speak with you.<br>

Working under general supervision of the Manager of Solution Architects, relying on experience and judgement to plan and accomplish goals, the Technical Sales Executive (TSE) grows revenue and drives profitability for targeted federal accounts. The TSE solves Storage business problems with technology solutions for our federal customers, and pulls in appropriate Connection resources to deliver these solutions. The TSE works within Connection's Federal business unit supporting Connection's federal DELL Technologies storage business.<br>
