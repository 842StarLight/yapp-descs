<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities
</b></h2>
<div></div>
<div><div><div>
<div>Reporting to Field Service Supervisor, the person in the position performs service activities at job sites and customer locations for customer and/or sales and rental fleet equipment.</div>
<div>
</div>
<div><ul>
<li>
Analyze and diagnose equipment malfunctions.</li>
<li>
Repair, replace or rebuild engines, transmissions, fuel systems, final drives, steering clutches, cross-shafts, hydraulic components including pumps, motors, valves, and all related components, electrical and air systems.</li>
<li>
Write service reports that accurately document diagnosis and repairs performed.</li>
<li>
Possess basic computer knowledge and ability to use service software required in position.</li>
<li>
Prepare required documentation including work orders, parts lists, and time cards in an accurate and timely manner</li>
<li>
Ensure assigned company vehicle maintains a clean professional appearance and is in proper working order.</li>
<li>
Perform assigned tasks in a timely, accurate, and efficient manner.</li>
<li>
Follow manufacturer and company service guidelines and procedures to ensure quality work and to preserve and document component failure information for accurate failure analysis.</li>
<li>
Project a positive professional image with customers.</li>
<li>
Develop and maintain a strong customer service relationship with all customer contacts.</li>
<li>
Other duties as may be assigned by the supervisor</li>
</ul></div>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Qualifications
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<ul>
<li>Prefer Associates in Diesel Technology and six (6) years heavy equipment repair experience with an emphasis on paving equipment. Education and experience must meet minimum job requirements to be considered.</li>
<li>Experience in troubleshooting and diagnosing equipment malfunctions</li>
<li>Ability to become DOT certified with DOT Medical Card</li>
<li>Ability to obtain lift truck operator certificate</li>
<li>Maintain a tool inventory sufficient to perform assigned tasks</li>
<li>Physical requirements include ability to lift 50 pounds, standing, walking, and bending</li>
<li>Maintain valid CDL and a clean driving record</li>
<li>Excellent attendance and punctuality essential</li>
</ul>
