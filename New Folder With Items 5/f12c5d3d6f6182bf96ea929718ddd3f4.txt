<b>Who We Are:</b><br>
Woda Cooper Companies, Inc., is a rapidly growing development, construction and property management organization headquartered in Columbus, Ohio. The organization specializes in providing quality affordable housing through the Midwest and Central Atlantic regions. Woda Cooper currently owns and managers over 10,000 housing units in over 300 housing communities. The management company has a concentration specifically on communities funded via the LIHTC program, Rural Development, and HUD Section 8, and other affordable programs. We have implemented Yardi Software throughout our portfolio.<br>
<br>
</div>
<div>
<b>Job Duties and Responsibilities:</b><br>
We are searching for a Regional Manager to join our Property Management team. This position is responsible for directing and overseeing the overall operations of their apartment communities, within an assigned geographical area. The right person will provide exceptional leadership, direction, and support to their team. You will also need to be well versed in sales, marketing, and analytical reporting; as a goal is to produce the maximum cash flow and improve the property's long-term value. This position will consist of 60-75% travel throughout the region. The Regional Manager is organized, analytical, goal-oriented, and a self-starter.<br>
<br>
</div>
<div><b>Qualifications:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Experience managing multiple communities.</li>
<li>Strong leadership skills.</li>
<li>Bachelor degree or equivalent experience required.</li>
<li>Excellent written and verbal communication skills.</li>
<li>5+ years experience working in affordable housing programs including one of more of the following: LIHTC, USDA Rural Development, Section 8, HOME required.</li>
<li>Strong computer skills</li>
<li>Experience with Yardi Voyager.</li>
<li><b>Position requires travel to communities throughout the entire state of WV</b></li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<div><b>In this role you will:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Oversee all aspects of the operations of the Communities assigned.</li>
<li>Direct supervision of the Community Managers within the portfolio.</li>
<li>Some overnight travel is required.</li>
<li>Ensure all actions on site are conducted within the company policy and procedures.</li>
<li>Responsible for ensuring that all personnel respond to resident requests or complaints in a timely, efficient and courteous manner.</li>
<li>Ensure all residents adhere to the terms of the lease and rules/regulations.</li>
<li>Ensure each resident file is in compliance with all regulatory commitments and any other governmental commitments.</li>
<li>Manage the selection process of residents.</li>
<li>Review all expenditures and ensue all Communities within their portfolio operate within budget.</li>
<li>Ensure the Communities in their portfolio have the highest and greatest curb appeal.</li>
<li>Staff any open positions in their portfolio with qualified employees.</li>
<li>All management functions for employees reporting to them, including timecard approval, PTO approval, attendance monitoring, reviews, etc.</li>
<li>Develop operating budgets.</li>
<li>Conduct regular community inspections.</li>
<li>Evaluate cost efficiency of maintenance operations.</li>
<li>Manage advertising and marketing plans for the Communities in their portfolio.</li>
</ul>
