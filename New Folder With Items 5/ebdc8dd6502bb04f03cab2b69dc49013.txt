<b>JOB SUMMARY</b><br>
The Spectrum Reach Sales Software Development team is currently seeking an ERP/CRM Solutions Architect. Spectrum Reach is expanding its enterprise ERP System capabilities integrated with a cadre of Media Sales Lifecycle tools. The Sales Software Development team is responsible for the technology strategy, sourcing, development, deployment, maintenance, and Tier 3 support for these enterprise capabilities. Existing responsibilities include the technology and software applications for enterprise, multi-channel campaign management. This person will be actively involved in all of the above areas and specifically responsible for creating and executing the ERP application integration strategy. Experience with relational marketing databases and technical development and deployment of enterprise ERP solutions is required.<br>
