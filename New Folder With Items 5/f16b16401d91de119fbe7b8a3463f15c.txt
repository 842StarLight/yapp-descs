<h2>Senior Loan Officer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Real estate-based private/direct lender seeking a Senior Loan Analyst to join our wonderful team.</p>
<p><b>Responsibilities: </b></p>
<p>· Take full lead detail sheet from incoming leads along with 1003 loan application for residential and commercial loans</p>
<p>· Qualify borrowers based on their private capital financing needs.</p>
<p>· Call and obtain proper documentation from borrowers/developers or brokers needed to satisfy underwriting requirements.</p>
<p>· Properly analyze important deal inquiry details when presenting loan requests during committee meetings.</p>
<p>· Guide borrowers and be able to explain our loan programs accurately.</p>
<p>· Follow up with clients on missing items needed for pre-qualification.</p>
<p>· Set up file using our checklist and turn into processing.</p>
<p>· Track and manages new leads efficiently.</p>
<p><b>Qualification Requirements: </b></p>
<p>· Ability to multitask and organize files in pipeline.</p>
<p>· Ability to make at least 100 phone calls a day to clients who have already contacted us having interest in doing loans. 90% of your success here will be with you being proactive and building long term relationships with your clients on the phone.</p>
<p>· Excellent communication skills, both verbal and written.</p>
<p>· Customer service oriented.</p>
<p>· Must have computer skills and feel comfortable learning new software.</p>
<p>· Proficient in Outlook, Adobe, Word and basic internet tools.</p>
<p>· Self-motivated and driven.</p>
<p>· Team player.</p>
<p>· Reliable.</p>
<p>· Sales experience (2 years required).</p>
