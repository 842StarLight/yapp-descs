<h2>Branch Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Overview</p>
<p>As part of a journey to develop a best-in-class sales coverage model to enable stronger sales growth, Harvey Building Products is adding new talent within the Distribution Products organization during this exciting transformation. Harvey Building Products is seeking to hire a seasoned Branch Manager to cultivate and manage a branch location. S/he will manage and train Inside Sales personnel within the branch location and oversee day-to-day operations and inventory.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Deliverable Outcomes</b><b>
</b></p>
<p>The Branch Manager will be responsible for delivering certain critical outcomes,<br>
including:</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Ensure branch achieves and exceeds invoiced sales goals</li>
<li>Drive branch profitability through ownership and execution of appropriate operational and pricing decisions</li>
<li>Produce metrics-driven branch summaries and synthesize opportunities for improvement to be reported to your respective Regional Branch Manager</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Specific Responsibilities</b><b>
</b></p>
<p>Core responsibilities will include:</p>
<p>1. Manage and oversee all daily operations of the Branch</p>
<p>2. Execute proper selling strategy to drive growth of small customers</p>
<p>3. Champion data-driven, continuous improvement and personal accountability culture</p>
<p>4. Provide accurate and timely performance reporting as required by senior leadership</p>
<p>5. Develop and maintain an environment of professionalism and safe working practices, both within the branch and throughout the service area</p>
<p>6. Ensure the proper execution of all operating processes as well as adherence to company policies and procedures</p>
<p>7. Ensure accurate management of data in CRM, SAP and other systems</p>
<p>8. Oversee inventory, product availability and coordinate transfers of merchandise to and from other branch locations</p>
<p>9. Engage in ongoing performance management and development of branch employees</p>
<p>10. Coach and develop Inside Sales team members on selling, service and account management best practices through weekly coaching and 1:1s</p>
<p>11. Lead Inside Sales team members in understanding, articulating and promoting marketing programs and sales promotions to customers</p>
<p>12. Promptly communicate all vital branch-specific information to Regional Branch Manager</p>
<p>13. Collaborate with Regional Branch Manager to develop strategies that combat competitive challenges</p>
<p>14. Engage with customers in the field to build strong relationships and perspective on selling opportunities</p>
<p>15. Possess and maintain current knowledge of the builder and remodeler market and competitive space</p>
<p>16. Identify opportunities for increasing share of wallet and driving additional sales in current customer base<br>
</p>
<p><b>The Person</b></p>
<p>Qualifications &amp; Experience</p>
<p>The successful candidate must have the following experience and capabilities:</p>
<p>1. Minimum of 5 years experience successfully leading teams, with 3-5 years operations/logistics management experience</p>
<p>2. 3-5 years experience in a fast-paced sales/customer service role</p>
<p>3. Proven track record of delivering process-driven results</p>
<p>4. Experience with data-driven analytics, metrics and KPIs to measure and drive results</p>
<p></p>
<p>Additional preferred qualifications include the following:</p>
<p>5. A broad understanding of the building products business, with more specific knowledge of the local market – as it relates to product types,<br>
customer trends, and competitors</p>
<p>6. Bachelor’s degree in related field</p>
<p>7. CRM experience</p>
<p>8. Strong working knowledge of SAP enterprise software and CenterPoint or related software</p>
<p>9. Working knowledge of MS Office (Word, Excel and Outlook)</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Skills&amp; Personal Attributes</b></p>
<p>The skills that will be key to success in this role include:</p>
<p>1. Effective leadership and coaching skills</p>
<p>2. Excellent communication/presentation skills and ability to build relationships</p>
<p>3. Good time management skills</p>
<p>4. Ability to work to a process</p>
<p>5. Ability to work as part of a team to achieve desired results</p>
<p>6. Strong quantitative and analytical skills</p>
<p>7. Knowledge of market research, sales and negotiating principles</p>
<p>8. Strong customer service skills</p>
<p>9. Negotiation and interpersonal skills</p>
<p></p>
<p>The personal attributes that will be key to success in this role include:</p>
<p>10. High integrity</p>
<p>11. Visionary and critical thinker</p>
<p>12. Highly motivated &amp; energetic</p>
<p>13. Collaborative and team oriented</p>
<p>14. Able to adapt and excel in a<br>
high-pressure environment undergoing considerable change</p>
