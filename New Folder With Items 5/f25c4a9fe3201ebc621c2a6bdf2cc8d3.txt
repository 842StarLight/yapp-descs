<p>At Wells Fargo, we want to satisfy our customers’ financial needs and help them succeed financially. We’re looking for talented people who will put our customers at the center of everything we do. Help us build a better Wells Fargo. It all begins with outstanding talent. It all begins with you.</p>
<p>
Corporate Risk helps all Wells Fargo businesses identify and manage risk. The team focuses on several key risk types, including conduct, credit, financial crimes, information security, interest rate, liquidity, market, model, operational, regulatory compliance, reputation, strategic, and technology risk.</p>
<p>
The group provides leadership, enhances communications, assists with problem identification and solutions, and shares best practices. In addition, the group provides an enterprise-wide view of risk, assists management and our Board of Directors in identifying and monitoring risks that may affect multiple lines of business, and takes appropriate action when business activities exceed the risk tolerance of the company</p>
<p>
The Team:</p>
<p>
Conduct Management is a centralized team within Corporate Risk. Conduct Management has enterprise-wide responsibilities to address allegations related to team member misconduct. This includes reporting on the entire allegation life cycle including case intake, triage, research and disposition of a wide variety of allegations including behavioral misconduct, theft, sales practices, fraud, money laundering, etc.</p>
<p>
Job Responsibilities:</p>
<p>
This leader will be part of the Enterprise Conduct Management Analytics, Insights and Reporting team (CMAIR). S/he will report to the Business Solutions Leader in CMAIR and will be responsible for building and leading a team of analytic consultants to drive the implementation of an overarching conduct management data quality assurance function. This Data Quality Assurance team is responsible for partnering within Conduct Management as well as with other key stakeholders to ensure consistency, quality and effectiveness of CMAIR data, analytics and reporting across Wells Fargo.<br>
Specifically this manager will be responsible for leading the team to</p>
<ul>
<li>Create and implement quality assurance processes and reporting</li>
<li>Perform quality assurance on data for reports, analytics and online dashboards</li>
<li>Measure and communicate data quality status and improvements</li>
<li>Produce evidence of quality checks and process that are auditable</li>
<li>Align process with CMAIR and Enterprise governance guidelines</li>
</ul>
<p>
The successful candidate will also be able to...</p>
<ul>
<li>Collaborate across people, processes, and technology/tools within a highly intensive and complex data environment</li>
<li>Communicate effectively, in both written and verbal formats</li>
<li>Coordinate completion of multiple tasks and meet aggressive time frames</li>
<li>Negotiate, influence, and collaborate to build successful relationships and deliver</li>
<li>Be a strong influencer and consensus builder with a positive attitude, who motivates others</li>
<li>Focus on team member development and engagement</li>
</ul>
<p>
As a Team Member Manager, you are expected to achieve success by leading yourself, your team, and the business. Specifically you will:</p>
<ul>
<li>Lead your team with integrity and create an environment where your team members feel included, valued, and supported to do work that energizes them.</li>
<li>Accomplish management responsibilities which include sourcing and hiring talented team members, providing ongoing coaching and feedback, recognizing and developing team members, identifying and managing risks, and completing daily management tasks.<br>
</li>
</ul>
