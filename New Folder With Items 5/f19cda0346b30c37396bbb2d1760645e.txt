<p>About You…</p>
<p>You are a motivated self-starter who needs little or no supervision, and you manage your time well with a focus on getting results. You thrive on setting daily goals and leading a team to achieve these objectives. You have the desire to win and help others succeed. You’re a quick thinker, fast learner and great problem-solver who is confident examining metrics to interpret performance indicators.</p>
<p>You have sound general business skills and are comfortable with the Internet and basic computer software. You are a confident, friendly “people person” who can establish and maintain solid relationships. Passion, integrity, professionalism and empathy are values that you live by.</p>

<p>1-800-GOT-JUNK? is making the ordinary business of junk removal exceptional. We are proud to be the leaders of our industry, and we recognize that every member of our team is a vital contributor to our success. High performance is expected, achieved and rewarded. Our culture of achievement is grounded in hard work, fun, professionalism, enthusiasm and teamwork. 1-800-GOT-JUNK? is dedicated to helping employees realize and fulfill their potential.</p>
<p>About the Job…</p>
<p>1-800-GOT-JUNK? Team Leaders/Managers are an important part of our business. Successful candidates will:</p>
<ul><li>Lead by example with time spent on truck performing junk removal duties and customer service duties with staff. <b>This is a field position and daily time on a truck is expected.</b> <b>Physical work loading/unloading trucks, similar to a moving company, is part of the job on a daily basis.</b>
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Run franchise operations periodically to ensure revenue and profitability targets are met. <b>General business experience would be beneficial but we will train you in our systems.</b>
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Work closely with the Franchise Partner and/or the Operations Manager to implement and build new systems and processes</li></ul>
<ul><li>Supervise Truck Team Members periodically. No previous supervisory experience is required, but helpful.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Oversee employee training in areas such as sales, marketing, customer service, profitability, and safety</li></ul>
<ul><li>Be comfortable with operational reports to make operational decisions</li></ul>
<ul><li>Execute marketing plans as needed.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Assist with customer concerns to ensure customers are always left as enthusiastic advocates of 1-800-GOT-JUNK?</li></ul>
<ul><li>Maintain inventory of equipment and ensure that proper equipment is used on the job</li></ul>
<ul><li>Maintain fleet, including inspections, regular maintenance, and minor fixes so that all issues with trucks are resolved quickly and in a cost effective manner.</li></ul>
