<p>JOB SUMMARY</p>
<p>
Reports To: Engineering Manager</p>
<p>
The Integration Engineer position is comprised of handling the technical design and conﬁguration effort for multiple projects and service calls active within our organization.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
ESSENTIAL FUNCTIONS</p>
<p>
I. Ability to handle the implementation of plant automation using our in-house integration methods and vendor supplied hardware and software.</p>
<p>
2. Interacts with our customers to meet the needs of the project or service call.</p>
<p>
3. Interacts with our sales staff to be sure the requirements of the project are understood.</p>
<p>
4. Ability to interact with our customers after the project or service call is completed in order to support post project issues.</p>
<p>
5. Ability to work as a functioning team member to support large projects.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
ADDITIONAL RESPONSIBILITIES</p>
<p>
1. Sales:</p>
<p>
a. Occasionally supports the sales staff by reviewing sales proposal prior to a presentation to the</p>
<p>
customer.</p>
<p>
b. Occasionally supports the sales staff by acting as the technical consultant for potential projects.</p>
<p>
2. Engineering:</p>
<p>
a. Acting as a team member for the engineering group for some of the larger projects.</p>
<p>
b. Acting as a project manager for some of the smaller projects.</p>
<p>
3. Administrative</p>
<p>
a. Completes the associated ISO paperwork for projects and service calls on a monthly basis</p>
<p>
b. Enters their time in the engineering database on a weekly basis.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
EDUCATION &amp; EXPERIENCE</p>
<p>
I. A four year engineering degree or equivalent related experience is required.</p>
<p>
2. Must have plant process control experience</p>
<p>
3. Must be familiar with Programmable Logic Controllers, Distributed Control Systems and various SCADA packages.</p>
