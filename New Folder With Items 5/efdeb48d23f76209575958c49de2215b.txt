<h2>Medical Aesthetician</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Work at Juvly Aesthetics as a Medical Aesthetician!</b></p>
<p>Work for the fastest growing aesthetics practice in the country! You will have access to our industry-leading paid training. Perform aesthetic procedures including but not limited to Coolsculpting, microneedling, chemical peels, facials, microdermabrasion, etc. The ideal applicant will have experience performing these services and will share our values. The Juvly Aesthetic values are excellent results, transparent pricing, and providing amazing service.</p>
<p>As a dynamic cosmetic dermatology company on a fast-moving mission, we are looking for someone with an energetic "make it happen" attitude, someone who encourages others to be their best and explore their growth. Someone who can stretch themselves and be flexible with changing priorities and stays focused during times of uncertainty and change. The position requires intuitive interpersonal skills and a positive mindset, which requires you to join forces with others across functions and geographies while building and maintaining trust.</p>
<p><b>Description of Role as Medical Aesthetician: </b></p>
<p>You will perform CoolSculpting, Microdermabrasion, Microneedling, Facials, Chemical Peels, Dermaplaning, and other standard aesthetics services as needed. Ideal applicant will have the ability to ensure total customer satisfaction. Applicants must also drive sales towards skincare and other aesthetics services offered.</p>
<p>*CoolSculpting and/or Microblading experience is a plus!</p>
<p><b>Education and Experience for Medical Aesthetician: </b></p>
<p>1. High school diploma or equivalent. College diploma preferred.</p>
<p>2. Must have active Ohio Aesthetician’s License.</p>
<p>3. Must have experience working at a medical spa or similar environment.</p>
<p><b>Essential Skills and Abilities for a Medical Aesthetician: </b></p>
<p>1. Able to work well in a dynamic, fast-paced environment.</p>
<p>2. Possesses a wide-ranging fund of knowledge applicable to skin care. While possessing the willingness and desire to learn more.</p>
<p>3. Demonstrable skills in sales, customer service, and time management.</p>
<p>4. Must have flexible schedule (every Saturday from 10AM-6PM).</p>
<p>5. Enthusiastic, energetic, personable, friendly, passionate, intelligent, and knowledgeable regarding the Aesthetics industry.</p>
<p>6. Ability to prioritize among multiple requests.</p>
<p><b>Responsibilities of a Medical Aesthetician: </b></p>
<p>1. Greet guests and members in a professional and friendly manner. Must be able to suggest the appropriate services as well as skincare based on the customer’s needs and skin concerns.</p>
<p>2. Creates an excellent experience for customers with a friendly attitude. Demonstrates product and service expertise.</p>
<p>3. Actively recommends, promotes, and sell skincare &amp; aesthetic services.</p>
<p>4. Maintain regular communication with front desk staff members. This will ensure promotion of products and optimization of scheduling. Provides clients with best customer service experience.</p>
<p>5. Maintain professional appearance at all times.</p>
<p>6. Provides back-up for front office staff as needed.</p>
<p>7. Interacts with patients, physicians, and other departments to resolve any issues.</p>
<p>*Please, no calls. Any calls will immediately disqualify your application.</p>
