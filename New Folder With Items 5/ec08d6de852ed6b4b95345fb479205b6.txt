<div><b>
BRIEF POSITION SUMMARY:
</b></div>
<div>The major role of the Customer Administration Specialist is to provide account support amongst associates within the Sales Team and other functional areas in support of meeting the obligations we have set forth with our customers. The role also requires assisting with activities the MSC sales teams are working on related to special projects within the company. Heavy interaction with internal customers and the Sales Team to ensure world class customer service and support in reference to the maintenance of contract obligations in a timely and accurate manner<b>.</b>
</div>
<div>
<b>DUTIES and RESPONSIBILITIES:</b>
</div>
<ul>
<li>Provides administrative customer account support to the MSC Sales Team.</li>
<li>Builds internal and external customer relationships via exceptional levels of service to ensure customer satisfaction.
</li>
<li>Interact and advise internal customers, through the application of specific program knowledge and subject matter expertise, in the execution of Customer Administration tasks.</li>
<li>Comprehend customer contracts to determine the business terms that need to be implemented within the system.</li>
<li>Responsible for the implementing/maintaining and validating of customer business terms to ensure contractual obligations are met or exceeded at all times.</li>
<li>Works with cross-functional teams to provide customer data in support of strategic sales opportunities.</li>
<li>Efficiently utilizes reporting tools to provide information to assist the Sales Team with decision making in support of the customer.</li>
<li>Analyze customer account modifications to determine required system updates to minimize impact to customer.</li>
<li>Leverage established team processes to ensure compliance.</li>
<li>Communicates process improvements to provide better service to our customers and streamline process workflow.</li>
<li>Responsible for timely, accurate and professional responses to account-specific deliverables.
</li>
<li>Fosters the MSC Culture in the department and throughout the company to ensure unity of purpose and fulfillment of MSC’s mission.</li>
<li>Participates in special projects and performs additional duties as required.</li>
</ul>
<div>
<b>EDUCATION and EXPERIENCE:</b>
</div>
<ul>
<li>Associate’s Degree required or the equivalent and a minimum of three years’ experience in a sales/customer service environment are required.</li>
</ul>
<div>
<b>SKILLS:</b>
</div>
<ul>
<li>Excellent communication, organizational and planning skills are required.</li>
<li>Excellent oral and written communication skills are also required.</li>
<li>Computer literacy and proficiency of computer applications such as AS400, SAP, SFA, Excel, Access, and presentation software is required.</li>
<br>
</ul>
