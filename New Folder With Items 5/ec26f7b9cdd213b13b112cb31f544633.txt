<h2>Product Support Manager, Cash in Transit</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>Brink’s, Incorporated is the premier provider secure logistics solutions, including transportation, ATM servicing, currency processing and other value added services to financial institutions, retailers and other commercial and government entities. The company has a proud history of providing growth and advancement opportunities for its employees. We have a challenging opportunity for a Product Support Manager, Cash in Transit.
<br>
<br>
Job Summary:
<br>
The Product Support Manager, Cash in Transit is responsible for support and coordination of the product line. This position will plan and perform customer implementations, internal startups, process improvement initiatives, on-going support, and other product-line related tasks. Working in a matrix environment, the incumbent collaborates with the Product Team, Sales Team and Operations Team to ensure Product Line support and objectives are met. The incumbent will provide expertise and guidance in product-related processes and procedures for operations activities at all levels to ensure effective and efficient execution of product processes.
<br>
<br>
Key Responsibilities:
<br>
<ul>
<li>Provide expertise and guidance in operations product-related processes and procedures
</li>
<li>Support business objectives related to product line Quality initiatives
</li>
<li>Ensure that operational policies, procedures, workflows and reporting formats for product line are established and maintained
</li>
<li>Manage expectations of customer and field operations in cooperation with Sales
</li>
<li>Create/improve operations key indicators (KPI) and quality assurance measures by monitoring and reporting performance at established intervals
</li>
<li>Achieve results by communicating and working across functions and teams
</li>
<li>Serve as a product consultant and point of escalation for Product Line support issues
</li>
<li>Share knowledge and best practices with team, customers, and other groups, including documentation and training
</li>
<li>Conduct operational training on product line(s)
</li>
<li>Create and support development of timely corrective actions to product line issues in operations
</li>
<li>Analyze key indicators and quality data to identify opportunities to improve overall operations performance
</li>
<li>Improve processes directed toward reducing costs, improving cycle time, increasing sales/gross profit, and enhancing customer satisfaction
</li>
<li>Manage projects
</li>
<li>Travel as necessary, 50% or more depending on primary location.
</li>
<li>Perform other duties as assigned or necessary
</li>
</ul>
