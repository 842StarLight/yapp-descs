<h2>New Car Sales Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>At Midstate Auto Group of Vermont, we believe that purchasing a car should be fast, fun and easy. We strive for a process that makes the customer's experience enjoyable and transparent. Our employees work hard to build and establish relationships with our customers. Midstate is family owned with 3 stores locate in Vermont, a great place to raise a family with abundant outdoor recreational activities available. Our Sales Managers normally work a 5 day work week, with four weekdays and a Saturday scheduled.We are looking for a highly motivated individual to share our passion for success and help build a stronger team. A Sales manager is responsible for planning, organizing, and controlling the activities of the Sales Department and in measuring the performance of the employees in the department. Our Sales Managers oversee both new and used vehicle sales in their buildings, working with the Used Car team to increase overall sales. Applicants must be able to utilize our technology. If you have Hyundai and AutoMate, V-Auto or ProMax experience it is a plus. Must be comfortable with training and monitoring sales staff, making phone calls and growing a team. Experience with used vehicle sales and appraisals is a plus.</p>
<p></p>
<div>
Responsibilities</div>
<ul>
<li>Hire, train, motivate, counsel, and monitor the performance of all sales employees under your supervision.</li>
<li>Direct and schedule the activities of all new &amp; used sales department employees under your supervision.</li>
<li>Help salespeople close deals when necessary, including meeting and greeting customers during our sales process.</li>
<li>Running the sales desk for new Hyundai's and used cars in conjunction with the float manager.</li>
<li>Set sales and gross objectives for salespeople under your supervision.</li>
<li>Forecast goals and objectives for the department and strive to meet them.</li>
<li>Develop, implement, and monitor the use of a prospecting program.</li>
<li>Understand, keep abreast of, and comply with federal, state, and local regulations that affect new &amp; used-vehicle sales.</li>
<li>Assist with new and used vehicle advertising as requested.</li>
<li>Follow company rules for safeguarding customer information at all times.</li>
<li>Conduct sales training meetings in conjunction with FCA Store.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<div>Qualifications</div>
<ul>
<li>Proven track record of successful people management skills in a sales environment.</li>
<li>High Energy with a positive out-going attitude.</li>
<li>Excellent communication skills through phone, text, email as well in person.</li>
<li>Professional personal appearance.</li>
<li>Minimum three (3) years of automotive sales management experience.</li>
<li>Valid drivers license and clean driving record.</li>
<li>Must be able to work weekdays and Saturdays.</li>
</ul>
