<h2>Fluid Power Account Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<ul>
<li>We are looking for a Fluid Power Account Manager to create long-term, trusting relationships with our customers</li>
<li>Account management responsibilities include developing strong relationships with customers, connecting with key business executives and stakeholders and preparing sales reports</li>
<li>In this role, you will liaise with cross-functional internal teams (including Customer Service and Product Development departments) to improve the entire customer experience</li>
<li>This position may require occasional travel</li>
<li>The Account Manager’s role is to oversee a portfolio of assigned customers, develop new business from existing clients and actively seek new sales opportunities</li>
<li>Account Managers answer client queries and identify new business opportunities among existing customers</li>
<li>If you are familiar with account management software (CRM), have a flair for client communication and understand consumer behavior, we’d like to meet you</li>
<li>Ideally, candidates for this role will know how to meet ambitious individual and team-wide sales quotas</li>
<li>Ultimately, a successful Account Manager should collaborate with our sales team to achieve quotas while keeping our clients satisfied and engaged with our products and services in the long-run</li>
<li>Serve as the lead point of contact for all customer account management matters</li>
<li>Build and maintain strong, long-lasting client relationships</li>
<li>Negotiate contracts and close agreements to maximize profits</li>
<li>Develop trusted advisor relationships with key accounts, customer stakeholders and executive sponsors</li>
<li>Ensure the timely and successful delivery of our solutions according to customer needs and objectives</li>
<li>Clearly communicate the progress of monthly/quarterly initiatives to internal and external stakeholders</li>
<li>Develop new business with existing clients and/or identify areas of improvement to meet sales quotas</li>
<li>Forecast and track key account metrics (e.g. quarterly sales results and annual forecasts)</li>
<li>Prepare reports on account status</li>
<li>Collaborate with sales team to identify and grow opportunities within territory</li>
<li>Assist with challenging client requests or issue escalations as needed</li>
