<h2>General Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>WestRock (NYSE: WRK) partners with our customers to provide differentiated paper and packaging solutions that help them win in the marketplace. WestRock’s 50,000 team members support customers around the world from more than 320 operating and business locations spanning North America, South America, Europe, Asia and Australia. Learn more at www.westrock.com.</p>

<div></div>
<div><div>
<div>
<b>
Career Opportunity</b>:</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
The General Manager position will have responsibility for the Sioux Falls Plant.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
Manage the entire operational structure of the Sioux Falls Plant which includes the functional areas of safety, sales, human resources, financial, environmental, quality, production, purchasing, transportation, maintenance, engineering and community relations. Manage and coach sales and customer service team members with the objective of achieving budgeted, margin growth or further expansion of our business. The General Manager reports to the Business Unit General Manager.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div><b>
How you will impact Westrock:</b></div>
<ul>
<li><div>
Assure on a daily basis all WestRock values, standards, and customer commitments (i.e.; sales, quality, R&amp;A, safety, employee relations, on-time delivery, reporting etc.) are followed.</div></li>
<li><div>
Build effective relationships with all departments to produce strong customer relationships and superior products.</div></li>
<li><div>
Develop and implement action plan for increasing plant income (i.e.; sales, creating efficiencies, etc.).</div></li>
<li><div>
Control cost within budgetary limits to achieve profitability.</div></li>
<li><div>
Review and understand annual budget, forecasts, and monthly financial summaries.</div></li>
<li><div>
Work closely with sales representatives in developing and maintaining relationships with key accounts.</div></li>
<li><div>
Foster employee development through training and other resources.</div></li>
<li><div>
Establish goals, track results uptime, quality productivity.</div></li>
<li><div>
Ensure all customer feedback is promptly dealt with and any complaints are corrected immediately.</div></li>
<li><div>
Maintain assets including building, land, equipment, and other assets.</div></li>
<li><div>
Ensure all environmental compliance issues are addressed on a timely basis.</div></li>
<li><div>
Provide a vision for the plant strategically linking resources of the facility with its respective markets.</div></li>
<li><div>
Promote diversified recruiting, hiring, training and managing of sales representatives, customer service, and operations staff for both facilities.</div></li>
<li><div>
Maintains a sufficient sales pipeline to meet budgeted volume growth objectives.</div></li>
<li><div>
Ensures target accounts match margin, capacity, and equipment requirements of the plant.</div></li>
<li><div>
Leads Talent Management initiative.</div></li>
<li><div>
Approve pricing within the appropriate levels.</div></li>
<li><div>
Assists in management of all aged inventories ensuring zero controllable write-offs;</div></li>
<li><div>
Management of all accounts receivable ensuring zero controllable write-offs.</div></li>
<li><div>
Assists with establishing budget for sales, margin and expenses within the assigned plant.</div></li>
</ul>
<div>
Ensures that appropriate policy and procedures are followed for tooling, outside purchases, etc.</div>
<ul><li><div>
Performs other duties as assigned.</div></li></ul>
