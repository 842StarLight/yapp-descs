<h2>Business Development Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div><div>
<p>Join us on our exciting journey! IQVIA™ is The Human Data Science Company™, focused on using data and science to help healthcare clients find better solutions for their patients. Formed through the merger of IMS Health and Quintiles, IQVIA offers a broad range of solutions that harness advances in healthcare information, technology, analytics and human ingenuity to drive healthcare forward.</p>
<p><b>
Business Development Manager</b></p>
<p><b>
Location: US – Greater Boston area, NY/NJ , VA/DC Metro, CA areas</b></p>
<p>
The Business Development Manager works collaboratively with other colleagues to increase sales within the life sciences industry. Responsibilities include full cycle sales including: target identification, securing and managing client meetings, listening and understanding client needs, addressing client concerns/objections, managing Subject Matter Experts in defining opportunities, proposing solutions, negotiating, obtaining SOWs and closing the deal.</p>
<p>
Our Sales Professionals are expected to quickly develop credibility with customers/prospects and build trust through professionalism and knowledge of services and products.</p>
<p><b>
Essential Duties and Responsibilities:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Prospect accounts focusing on products and services</li>
<li>
Identify and develop new accounts and follow up on existing leads</li>
<li>
Develop and maintain productive relationships with key target accounts</li>
<li>
Manage assigned accounts to generate both incremental and repeatable annual business</li>
<li>
Initiate and manage RFI and RFP process and negotiate terms for all account programs and projects</li>
<li>
Work collaboratively with the Professional Services Organization to ensure a complete and professional delivery of software and service solutions</li>
<li>
Expand product portfolio in the PV market</li>
<li>
Collaborate with Executives and SMEs to maximize account coverage</li>
<li>
Maintain knowledgeable understanding of changing regulatory guidelines and mandates affecting the industry community</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Job Experience:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Must demonstrate a sustained (3+ years) and successful track record selling professional services to Pharmaceutical, Biotech, Medical Device, and/or Clinical Research Organizations</li>
<li>
Demonstrated track record of success working with senior level management in a fast paced growth environment is essential</li>
<li>
Full sales cycle experience is required: Prospect, Identify opportunities, Propose Solutions (writing proposals), Negotiate, Close</li>
<li>
Strong written and verbal English skills, quality-oriented proposal writing and proposal management experience</li>
<li>
Ambitious, driven and self-motivated personality adaptable to a fast paced high volume and high velocity sales organization</li>
<li>
Bachelor’s degree or higher required</li>
<li>
Willing to travel throughout the US, estimated up to 90%</li>
<li>
Reside in the greater Boston area, NY &amp; NJ area, VA/DC metro area or CA</li>
<li>
Highly preferred capabilities:<ul>
<li>
Possess an understanding of the regulatory pharmacovigilance process for both pre-marketed and post-marketed stages of a product</li>
<li>
Background in Software as a Service (SaaS) market</li>
<li>
Selling drug safety consulting services and products</li>
<li>
Experience working with Regulatory Affairs Departments, Product Safety related departments, or Regulatory Submissions.</li>
<li>
Experience with data-mining or statistical signal detection products</li>
</ul>
</li>
</ul>
