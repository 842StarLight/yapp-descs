<h2>Marketing and Group Sales Coordinator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">SUMMARY
<br><br>
Under direct supervision, this position will assist the Marketing Department with promoting all INTRUST Bank Arena events through advertising and group sales campaigns, and will manage all aspects of email marketing and the arena's website.
<br><br>
ESSENTIAL DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Carry out specific marketing procedures related to events through advertising, public relations and group sales as requested by the Director of Booking and Marketing and Marketing Manager.</li>
<li>Lead and manage group sales efforts for events including soliciting, managing and maintaining group sales accounts.</li>
<li>Execute graphic design and creative projects in Photoshop in an effective and aesthetically pleasing manner.</li>
<li>Recruit, hire, train and supervise interns within the Marketing Department (1-2 per semester) to assist in the development and implementation of all marketing aspects for events.</li>
<li>Manage all INTRUST Bank Arena e-mail marketing communications, including list management, content management and campaign execution through our email campaign software.</li>
<li>Maintain the venue's website, ensuring all information is up-to-date and accurate.</li>
<li>Create and update all digital venue advertising.</li>
<li>Assist in developing and delivering social media marketing strategies and campaigns for INTRUST Bank Arena. In the absence of the Marketing Manager manage content of social media channels.</li>
<li>Coordinate street team and grassroots marketing efforts with local market contacts including the distribution of posters and flyers.</li>
<li>Develop and coordinate efforts to enhance Fan Experience at INTRUST Bank Arena events.</li>
<li>Assist with building and implementing overall marketing plans for SMG Special Events.</li>
<li>Create and distribute the monthly Employee Newsletter.</li>
<li>Regular attendance is an essential job function.</li>
<li>Other duties/responsibilities as assigned.</li>
</ul>
QUALIFICATIONS
<br><br>
To perform this job successfully, an individual must be able to perform each essential duty satisfactorily. The requirements listed below are representative of the knowledge, skill, and/or ability required. Reasonable accommodations may be made to enable individuals with disabilities to perform the essential functions.
<br><br>
SUPERVISORY RESPONSIBILITIES
<br>Directly supervises interns in the Marketing Department in accordance with SMG's policies and applicable laws. Responsibilities include recruiting, interviewing, selecting, and training interns, planning assigning and directing projects, appraising performance at the conclusion of each semester.
<br><br>
SKILLS REQUIRED
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Demonstrate knowledge of principles, practices and terminology of marketing, advertising, public relations programs and sales.</li>
<li>Graphic design experience required with working knowledge of Photoshop.</li>
<li>Comfortable cold-calling for group sales and meeting with group leaders to maintain client relationships.</li>
<li>Ability to effectively work in a fast paced environment, handling multiple projects and details simultaneously, while meeting tight deadlines.</li>
<li>Attention to detail is a must.</li>
<li>Ability to think "outside the box" and come up with creative ideas to set INTRUST Bank Arena apart.</li>
<li>Ability to troubleshoot and problem solve independently.</li>
<li>Ability to establish and maintain effective working relationships with promoters, media users, employees, and the general public.</li>
<li>Excellent communication skills, both written and orally.</li>
<li>Organized with strong time-management skills and ability to meet strict deadlines.</li>
<li>Demonstrate high energy level with a positive attitude, motivation and a strong work ethic.</li>
<li>Operate Word, Excel and PowerPoint and other related office programs.</li>
<li>Ability to work long, irregular hours as dictated by programming including weekends and holidays.</li>
<li>Willingness to respond in a timely fashion during normal business hours, after hours and on weekends via mobile phone.</li>
<li>Be licensed and insured to operate a motor vehicle in the state of Kansas.</li>
<li>Must be able to pass a background check.</li>
</ul>
EDUCATION AND/OR EXPERIENCE
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>College degree required.</li>
<li>Minimum 2 years' experience in marketing/advertising, live entertainment marketing preferred.</li>
<li>Sales experience preferred.</li>
</ul>
This is a fast-paced office. Ideal candidates will have music knowledge, show marketing experience, familiarity with the market, a thick skin and able to work at a fast pace.
<br><br>
<b>Other:</b><br>
Requires ability to work flexible hours, including nights, weekends and holidays, in addition to normal business hours.
<br><br>
<b>Physical Demands:</b><br>
This position requires personal mobility for getting around the facility. Must be able to use a computer, write, type; and use phone and cell phone. Ability to lift up to 30 lbs.
