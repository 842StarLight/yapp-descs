<h2>Search Engine Optimization Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">

<p>DoctorLogic is looking for a talented Search Engine Optimization Manager to help our growing customer base succeed online! The DoctorLogic Website Marketing Platform has helped hundreds of doctors, including plastic surgeons, dentists, and dermatologists to increase their number of new patients, grow their practice and outperform their competition online. This is a well-funded company with an industry-shaping platform started by proven entrepreneurs with a successful track record for this type of application.</p>
<p>
As a Search Engine Optimization Manager at DoctorLogic, you will play a critical role in coordinating and implementing search engine optimization programs for clients. You will work directly with client services, sales, marketing, and product teams to ensure that the best practices are implemented and adopted to ensure that the goals of DoctorLogic customers are achieved.</p>
<p>
In addition to creativity and strategy, the right person will bring a serious passion for SEO, the ability to problem-solve and communicate effectively within a team environment, and experience in developing programs and mentoring individuals.</p>
