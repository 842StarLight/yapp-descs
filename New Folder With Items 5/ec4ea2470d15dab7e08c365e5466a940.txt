<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities

<div><div><div>
<div>
<b>JOB SUMMARY:</b><br>
<br>
Provide technical assistance to computer end users in a call center environment. Answer questions or resolve technical problems for clients via telephone and email. Provide assistance concerning the use of computer software, including but not limited to business systems, reporting, printing, connectivity, productivity applications (Word, Excel, Outlook), and operating systems.<br>
<br>
<b>THE OPPORTUNITY:</b><br>
<br>
<ul>
<li>Provide first-level business and productivity application support to end users</li>
<li>Analyze critically and priority of reported issues, and escalate as necessary</li>
<li>Evaluate documented resolutions and analyze trends for ways to prevent future problems</li>
<li>Provide guidance and training on the use of Burns &amp; Wilcox applications</li>
<li>Experience working in a team-oriented, collaborative environment</li>
<li>Enter, track, and route all support requests and document their resolutions within the help desk software</li>
<li>Document processes and procedures</li>
<li>Research support requests using available information resources</li>
<li>Have excellent verbal and written communication skills</li>
<li>Ability to communicate in user-friendly language</li>
<li>Participate in after-hours rotation</li>
<li>Exhibit professional behavior</li>
<li>Possess keen attention to detail</li>
</ul>
