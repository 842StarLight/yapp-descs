<h2>Production Planning Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Establish vision and direction for the Cable Planning Organization by developing, leading and mentoring a dynamic team who constantly search for creative ways to elevate the capabilities of resources and systems to meet business needs and customer demands.</li>
<li>Oversee the master planning process (forecasting through order fulfillment) for manufacturing ensuring that each phase is accurate and reflective of capacity, material supply, and other constraints.</li>
<li>Ensure planning outputs provide stakeholders with the relevant information they need to execute including volume and revenue forecasts, production line schedules, material demand and resource requirements.</li>
<li>Provide input and support for the annual business plan and the monthly sales &amp; operations planning processes by modeling production line capacity, identifying opportunities, and reconciling schedule conflicts. Work closely with the operations and sales teams to proactively develop and implement joint plans to relieve constraints and meet business plan objectives including outsourcing opportunities.</li>
<li>Closely monitor production schedule performance and schedule compliance. Utilize schedule analytics to proactively identify and communicate schedule constraints and areas of focus to improve. Work with the operations and sales teams to make necessary adjustments that maximize customer experience.</li>
<li>Provide transparent and clear reporting of schedule performance to highlight customer satisfaction, capacity and inventory utilization, financial impact, and workflow/technology opportunities. Utilize these KPIs to drive continuous improvement initiatives and investment decisions.</li>
<li>Execute projects to improve schedule accuracy, scheduling models, workflows, data integrity, reporting, and the functionality needed to meet business objectives and advance operational performance.</li>
<li>Provide supervision and direction including performance oversight and skills development. Cultivate an environment of teamwork, customer service, and continuous improvement.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p><b>
</b></p>
<p><b>Personal Qualities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Self-starter with integrity and confidence who strives to achieve even the most challenging objectives with limited supervision.</li>
<li>Team player with well-developed leadership and interpersonal skills that is comfortable working in a fast-paced, dynamic, cross-functional, and multi-cultural environment.</li>
<li>A results-oriented professional that is comfortable in a challenging professional environment where complex problem solving is required.</li>
<li>Strong communicator with the ability to convey complex issues in simple terms to all levels of the organization.</li>
<li>Strong analytical, time management, and technical writing skills.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p><b>
</b></p>
<p><b>Qualifications</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Strong background in leading a planning organization across multiple manufacturing sites with 10 or more years of experience with demonstrated strategic thinking and relationship management across all levels of an organization.</li>
<li>Able to oversee planning processes across multiple manufacturing sites with an emphasis on demand management and master production scheduling in a complex, build-to-order manufacturing environment with relatively short lead times.</li>
<li>Experience leading the sales and operations planning process to determine the optimal use of capacity in support of customer demand and business performance.</li>
<li>Demonstrated ability to apply advanced scheduling techniques including constraint management to optimize throughput.</li>
<li>Hands-on understanding of the complexities and challenges that arise in the day-to-day process of creating detailed production schedules for manufacturing.</li>
<li>Practical experience and knowledge of the material requirements planning process including inputs, outputs, and methods used to create material demand information.</li>
<li>Practical understanding of inventory management processes including finished goods/consignment replenishment methods, accuracy controls, and schedule inputs.</li>
<li>Practical understanding of shop floor production controls including inputs required for scheduling and work-in-process tracking and controls.</li>
<li>Able to develop, implement, and improve all closed-loop planning and control processes needed to optimally meet customer demand with available materials, labor, and production capacity.</li>
<li>Solid understanding of basic statistics and analytical methods to measure and monitor planning processes and system performance.</li>
<li>Working knowledge of lean manufacturing including a basic understanding of six sigma methods and techniques.</li>
<li>Experience in the ongoing assessment and continuous improvement of the planning process and associated systems using established performance evaluation benchmarks.</li>
<li>Experience with Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP) systems preferably Oracle R12 related manufacturing modules.</li>
<li>Experience with forecasting, planning, scheduling, and shop floor control systems such as APS, MES, MOM etc.</li>
<li>Experience in the identification, implementation, and improvement of IT systems to better support the planning, scheduling, and control processes.</li>
<li>Intermediate or higher user of MS Office including Excel, Outlook, Access, and Word applications.</li>
<br>
</ul>

<p><b>Working Conditions</b></p>

<li>Factory office environment with occasional weekend and off-shift support required.</li>
</ul>
