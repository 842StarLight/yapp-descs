<h2>Project Manager - Advanced Systems Group</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>Job Description<br>
<br>
<p>Boston Engineering's Advanced Systems Group is experiencing high growth in response to continued demand for its engineering development services for our nation's military. We are currently seeking a customer focused <b>Project Manager</b> who is a results-oriented leader and who has demonstrable success in managing high-tech product developments and working with other high-tech professionals in meeting challenging technical goals. Our projects range in complexity and involve some or all of our engineering disciplines which include mechanical, electrical and software engineering. The primary focus of this project management role is <b>defense and security technology development</b> and will require technical fluency in system level engineering.</p>
<p>
This position will include project management over the full cycle of our phase-gate product development process including:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Requirements and specifications development</li>
<li>
Concept/system architecture development</li>
<li>
Technology development support</li>
<li>
Engineering analyses and design</li>
<li>
Prototype build and testing</li>
<li>
Design verification</li>
<li>
Preparation for design transfer to manufacturing</li>
</ul>
<p>
In addition to the PM role, significant technical contribution on projects is required.</p>
<p>
To be successful, you must have demonstrated capabilities in the following:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Establishing project plans and tracking scope, schedules, budgets and deliverables for multi-disciplinary Defense and Security development projects</li>
<li>
Systems-level engineering expertise with electromechanical systems</li>
<li>
Demonstrated experience managing small multi-disciplinary engineering teams</li>
<li>
Ability to direct several projects simultaneously in a fast-paced environment</li>
<li>
Ability to prepare and present at project reviews identification of project risks and development of risk mitigation strategies/approaches</li>
<li>
Ability to prepare proposals and develop estimates to support sales</li>
</ul>
</div>
