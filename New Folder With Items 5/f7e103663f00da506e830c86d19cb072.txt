<h2>Administrative Manager/Accounts Receivable Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Administrative Manager/Accounts Receivable Specialist Responsibilities: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Supervising day-to-day operations of the administrative department and staff members.</li>
<li>Developing, reviewing, and improving administrative systems, policies, and procedures.</li>
<li>Ensuring office is stocked with necessary supplies and all equipment is working and properly maintained.</li>
<li>Working with accounting and management team to set budgets, monitor spending, and processing payroll and other expenses.</li>
<li>Planning, scheduling, and promoting office events, including meetings, conferences, interviews, orientations, and training sessions.</li>
<li>Collecting, organizing, and storing information using computers and filing systems.</li>
<li>Overseeing special projects and tracking progress towards company goals.</li>
<li>Building new and expanding existing skills by engaging in educational opportunities.</li>
<li>Researching and resolving account discrepancies.</li>
<li>Processing and recording transactions</li>
<li>Maintaining records regarding payments and account statuses.</li>
<li>Obtaining information from other departments to ensure records are accurate and complete and that accounts receivable ledgers and journals are up to date.</li>
<li>Working with the collections department to review accounts and client payment and credit history to develop new or better repayment terms.</li>
<li>Performing administrative and clerical tasks, such as data entry, preparing invoices, sending bill reminders, filing paperwork, and contacting clients to discuss their accounts.</li>
<li>Generating reports and statements for internal use.</li>
<li>Engaging in ongoing educational opportunities to update job knowledge.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Administrative Manager/ Accounts Receivable Specialist Requirements: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Experience in related field, such as management, bookkeeping, accounts receivable or financial reporting, preferred.</li>
<li>Combination of education and experience may be accepted.</li>
<li>Exceptional leadership and time, task, and resource management skills.</li>
<li>Strong problem solving, critical thinking, coaching, interpersonal, and verbal and written communication skills.</li>
<li>Proficiency with computers, especially MS Office and Quickbooks.</li>
<li>Ability to plan for and keep track of multiple projects and deadlines.</li>
<li>Strong math, typing, and computer skill, especially with bookkeeping software.</li>
<li>Excellent communication, research, problem solving, and time management skills.</li>
<li>High level of accuracy, efficiency, and accountability.</li>
<li>Attention to detail.</li>
<li>Ability to build relationships with clients and internal departments.</li>
</ul>
<p>Job Type: Part-time</p>
<p>Experience:</p>
<ul>
<li>Accounts Receivable: 2 years (Preferred)</li>
<li>Bookkeeping: 1 year (Preferred)</li>
</ul>
<p>Education:</p>
<ul><li>Associate (Preferred)</li></ul>
<p>Work Location:</p>
<ul><li>One location</li></ul>
<p>Hours per week:</p>
<ul><li>20-29</li></ul>
<p>Schedule:</p>
<ul><li>Monday to Friday</li></ul>
<p>Dutie(s):</p>
<ul>
<li>Reconciling the accounts receivable ledger</li>
<li>Generating financial statements and A/R reports</li>
<li>Responding to customer questions and resolving issues</li>
<li>Clerical functions such as billing, estimates, monthly statements, etc.</li>
<li>Treasury functions such as bank deposits, manual wire payments, and credit card transactions</li>
<li>Posting payments to the general ledger and conducting daily cash reconciliation</li>
<li>Analyzing customer payment plan, payment history, and credit line</li>
<li>Collecting customer payments in accordance with payment due dates</li>
<li>Making collection calls on outstanding accounts</li>
<li>Comply with federal, state, and company policies, procedures, and regulations</li>
<li>Submitting delinquent accounts to attorney or outside agency for collection</li>
</ul>
