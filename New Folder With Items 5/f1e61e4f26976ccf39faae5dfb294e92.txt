<h2>Senior Financial Analyst</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>This position will support Advanced Development Programs (ADP) in Palmdale, CA and will be responsible for Financial Planning and Analysis in support of ADP Programs. This position requires extensive knowledge in the underpinnings of the major financial measures of orders, sales, backlog, EBIT and cash flow and the systems used to develop, analyze and report them. Understanding of the financial ramifications on these measures by varying contract geometries is also required. Previous experience in Long Range Plan development as it relates to a volatile, quick-turn R&amp;D environment is highly prized. This position supports a multitude of program finance functions and preparing analysis for presentation to various levels of leadership.<br>
