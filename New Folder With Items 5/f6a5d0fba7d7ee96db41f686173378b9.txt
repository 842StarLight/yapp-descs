<b>Job Description</b><br>
The Senior Product Manager is responsible for the product planning and execution throughout the<br>
Product Lifecycle, including: gathering and prioritizing product and customer requirements,<br>
defining the product vision, and working closely with engineering, sales, marketing and support to<br>
ensure revenue and customer satisfaction goals are met. The Senior Product Manager’s job also<br>
includes ensuring that the product supports the company’s overall strategy and goals.
</p>
<ul>
<b>Position Requirements</b>
<br>
<br>
<b>Essential Job Functions</b><br>
<li>Define the product strategy and roadmap</li>
<li>Define and quantify business opportunities, insuring development resources are devoted to the most</li>
<li>profitable prospects.</li>
<li>Create and coordinate responses to customer RFPs, deliver MRDs and PRDs with prioritized features</li>
<li>and corresponding justification.</li>
<li>Work with external third parties to assess partnerships and product licensing opportunities</li>
<li>Be an expert with respect to the competition</li>
<li>Act as a leader within the company</li>
