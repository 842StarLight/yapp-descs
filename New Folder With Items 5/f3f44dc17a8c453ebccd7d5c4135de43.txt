<h2>BOX Ticket Sellers</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Job Summary:</p>
<p><b>
Unity in Diversity
</b></p>
<p>Our team members are as diverse as our music venues. With its owned/operated/managed venues, Live Nation Clubs and Theaters, a division of Live Nation Entertainment, is wide-ranging, including the legendary Fillmore venues and the intimate House of Blues clubs throughout the United States. Live Nation Clubs and Theaters is the country’s preeminent group of intimate music venues, featuring superior sound and lighting technology in one-of-a-kind custom-designed environments aimed to bring fans and artists together in unparalleled musical environments.</p>
<p></p>
<p>
Our mission is to create a profitable and principled global entertainment company. To celebrate the diversity and brotherhood of world culture. To promote racial and spiritual harmony through love, peace, truth, righteousness and non-violence. Our venues bring fans as close to artists as possible and our aim is to create teams that are just as close.</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>
The Role:</b></p>
<p>
Ticket Sales, both over the phone and walk-up</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>
What You’ll Need:</b></p>
<p></p>
<p>
 <b>Minimum Requirements:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Working Knowledge of Phone Handling, Cashiering, Customer Service</li>
<li>
Entry level knowledge of basic computer operation and Point of Sales systems</li>
<li>
Skill in guest relations, phone etiquette, cash handling, filing, multi-task environment, attention to detail</li>
<li>
Ability to communicate, work well with others, work in a high-volume and sometimes stressful environment</li>
<li>
Ability to type 30 wpm</li>
<li>
High School Diploma</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b><i>
Preferred:</i></b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Previous Ticketing experience</li>
<li>
Working knowledge of Windows based PC programs</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Physical Demands/Working Environment:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Working environment is fast-paced.</li>
<li>
Position requires repetitive use of arms for answering busy phones and typing.</li>
<li>
Must be able to lift or move up to 25 lbs using proper lifting techniques</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>
What You’ll Do:</b></p>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>
Cash Handling; Bank Procedures and Check-out Procedures</li>
<li>
Maintain superior customer service both in person and over the phones</li>
<li>
Answer phone with prompt and courteous attitude</li>
<li>
Guest Relations</li>
<li>
Will Call Window</li>
<li>
Running Ticket Program on Computer for Sales</li>
<li>
Processing Opening and Closing Paperwork</li>
<li>
Knowledge of Talent and Entertainment</li>
<li>
Have a positive attitude</li>
<li>
Keep a clean, well-groomed appearance</li>
</ul>
