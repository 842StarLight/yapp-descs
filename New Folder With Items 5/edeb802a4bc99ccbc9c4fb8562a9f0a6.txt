<h2>Western Media Sales Director</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>The Company: </b></p>
<p>Human Capital Media is an award-winning, multimedia and marketing services organization servicing the Human Resources marketplace through <i>Chief Learning Officer</i>and <i>Workforce.com.</i>HCM specializes in original content, custom content, custom research and live events.</p>
<p><b>The Role: </b></p>
<p>We are seeking an experienced, highly motivated sales professional with a proven track record in digital media sales and a passion for marketing to represent us in the western half of the United States and Canada. You will own a substantial territory and be responsible for generating a significant percentage of media revenues for the organization.</p>
<p><b>Responsibilities: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Retain and grow our client base through consultative selling and upselling.</li>
<li>Work closely with our marketing team to prospect and cultivate leads.</li>
<li>Identify and recommend new products, services and improvements to our existing product portfolio.</li>
<li>Stay current on industry trends, market activity and knowledgeable of direct and indirect competition.</li>
<li>Prepare regular sales progress reports.</li>
<li>Maintain professional and technical knowledge, stay up on industry trends, abreast of professional/competitive landscape and proficient at establishing professional networks.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Requirements: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Minimum 5 years of digital media sales experience with a well-documented and verifiable history of success selling webinars, e-blasts, e-newsletters, digital advertising, custom content and research.</li>
<li>Firm grasp of integrated marketing, lead gen strategies and content marketing initiatives.</li>
<li>Experience in cultivating new business opportunities by identifying prospects and evaluating their position in the industry and conducting a needs analysis.</li>
<li>A keen understanding and history of consultative, solution-based selling and be able to think strategically and creatively.</li>
<li>Proficient in MS Office and G Suite. HubSpot experience is a plus.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>The Person: </b></p>
<p>You must possess the ability to engage at all levels, from assistant media planners of digital media agencies to C-suite media and marketing executives at <i>Fortune</i>100 companies.</p>
<p>You must be able to think strategically, creatively, know what consultative selling means and how to employ it. Your primary responsibility will be to drive media revenue by helping HCM clients and prospects achieve their goals by identifying and formulating marketing strategies that align with HCM products and services.</p>
<p>To be successful in this role, <b>you must possess the following: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>High energy and the ability to meet or exceed goals continually.</li>
<li>Able to maintain and build relationships with HCM’s existing client base and provide exceptional service, support, information and guidance.</li>
<li>Must be highly effective in presenting to, retaining and negotiating with clients and prospects.</li>
<li>The ability to work remote while maintaining a high level of productivity and results.</li>
</ul>
<p>If your experience fits the above requirements and you are looking for a stimulating, challenging and rewarding opportunity in a positive, upbeat work environment, please forward your résumé along with a cover letter that clearly articulates why you believe you are the best candidate for the job. <b>Résumés submitted without a cover letter will not be considered. </b></p>
