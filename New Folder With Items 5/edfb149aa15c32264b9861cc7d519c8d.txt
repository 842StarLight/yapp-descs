<h2>Commercial Account Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Liberty Ford is growing and we are in need of a full time Commercial Account Manager for our Commercial Vehicle Center! Come be part of Northeast Ohio's largest Ford dealer group. Excellent pay with unlimited earning potential, opportunity for growth, flexible hours, medical benefits, 401K, life insurance, and paid time off. Voted one of NEO's Top Workplaces 10 years in row!</b></p>
<p><b>SUMMARY</b></p>
<p>Sells Commercial Vehicles by understanding and demonstrating vehicle characteristics, capabilities, and features in addition to developing relationships with business to qualify buyers and close sales. B2B Sales.</p>
<p><b>ESSENTIAL DUTIES</b></p>
<ul><li>Approaches, greets, and offers assistance or direction to any customer who enters the Commercial Sales Center.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Build relationships with local businesses</li></ul>
<ul><li>Offers test drives to all prospects. Follows dealership procedures to obtain proper identification from customer prior to test drive</li></ul>
<ul><li>Demonstrates automobiles by explaining characteristics, capabilities and features and explaining warranties and services</li></ul>
<ul><li>Satisfies the transportation needs of the buyer by understanding buyer’s requirements and interests and by developing rapport with the customers</li></ul>
<ul><li>Completes quotes and explains financing options in accordance with established dealership policies</li></ul>
<ul><li>Delivers vehicles to customers, ensuring that the customer understands the vehicle’s operating features</li></ul>
<ul><li>Follow up with prospective customers and return email/voicemail</li></ul>
<p><b>JOB REQUIREMENTS</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Ability to read and write</li>
<li>Ability to comprehend simple instructions, short correspondence, memos, safety rules, operating, maintenance instructions and procedure manuals</li>
<li>Ability to respond to common inquiries or complaints from customers</li>
<li>Ability to calculate figures such as discounts, interest and percentages</li>
<li>Basic MS Office knowledge, computer software and internet proficiency</li>
<li>Excellent appearance, verbal/written communication, strong negotiation and presentation skills</li>
</ul>
<p><b>EDUCATION</b></p>
<ul>
<li>High School degree or equivalent preferred</li>
<li>Valid Driver’s license that meets dealership insurability requirements</li>
</ul>
<p><b>EXPERIENCE</b></p>
<p>The ideal candidate must have a strong desire to succeed. Our Commercial Account Manager come from a variety of backgrounds and industries. You don’t have to have automotive sales experience to be successful but you should have experience providing exceptional customer service and previous sales experience is a plus</p>
<p><b>PHYSICAL REQUIREMENTS</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Must be able to walk the sales lot and showroom to review inventory, and greet customers for at least 8-12 hours in a shift</li>
<li>Must be able to drive company vehicles</li>
</ul>
