<p><b>Responsibilities and Duties</b><br>**<br>HR Professional</p>
<p><b>Job description</b><br>Consult with line management and provide daily HR guidance.</p>
<p><b>Roles and Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Develops innovative, creative, and proactive recruitment / sourcing strategies to develop high quality active and passive candidate pipelines.</li>
<li>Effectively builds and engages with talent communities through social media outlets.</li>
<li>Provides support to regions that are experiencing a significant number of talent vacancies or where the recruiter position is vacant (i.e. assisting regional Recruiters with recruitment and retention plans).</li>
<li>Identify and source appropriate internal talent for current and future open roles within the organization.</li>
<li>Develops, coordinates and implements community-based recruiting initiatives with colleges, churches, local community organizations, etc. May give presentations at colleges, attend student group meetings, and increase college awareness of Bethesda before and after career fairs and events.</li>
<li>Assists in branding in establishing a recognizable "employer of choice" reputation for Bethesda, both internally and externally.</li>
<li>Manages HR Data and Analytics.</li>
<li>HR Reports and Presentation.</li>
<li>Understanding upcoming vacancy for national and international region and do research on Salary benchmarking and Industry Mapping.</li>
<li>Other duties as assigned.</li>
<li>MIS preparation and analyzing data of attrition, retention, etc. · Participates in evaluation and monitoring of success of training programs. Follows-up to ensure training objectives are met.</li>
<li>Manage key HR processes in close partnership with managers (e.g. performance cycle, Performance linked incentives (PLI), employee engagement activities, etc.).</li>
<li>Ensure that KRA/KPI’s of the employees are aligned as per the business objective. Drive continuous performance improvement / Support Top talent program.</li>
<li>Provide coaching and guidance to managers on promotions, demotions, and transfers.</li>
<li>Provides HR guidance wrt policies and procedures, wherever, appropriate to new managers.</li>
<li>Analyze trends and metrics in partnership with the HR group to develop solutions, programs and policies</li>
<li>Works closely with management and employees to improve work relationships, build morale, and increase productivity and retention.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Required Experience and Qualifications</b><br>Experience - 6 to 10 years.</p>
