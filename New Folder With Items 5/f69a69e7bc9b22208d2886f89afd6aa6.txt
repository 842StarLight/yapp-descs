<br>About Us
<br>********
<br><br>
Remind is a communication platform that helps educators reach students and parents where they are: their phones. With 31 million active users, we're one of the fastest-growing companies in education technology, but we have our sights set on something bigger: giving every student the opportunity to succeed.
<br><br>
Our investors include First Round Capital, GSV, Kleiner Perkins Caufield Byers, and Social Capital, and we want you to join us.
<br><br>
Responsibilities
<br><br>
Extensive knowledge of the K-12 market and selling SAAS products to districts.
<br><br>
The ideal candidate would have experience in selling educational technology to the K-12 market. The candidate will ideally be based in the Carolinas or a neighboring state and will call on district targets in the Southeast to sell Remind District Plans and build use cases for Remind in the market. The Account Executive is expected to develop a portfolio through prospecting and existing contacts, be comfortable with cold outreach, and developing a territory strategy. This candidate will report to the Regional Sales Manager, Southeast and work with Remind's Marketing Team to target specific users and use cases.
<br><br>

<br><br>
<ul>
<li>2+ years of successful selling into the K-12 market, SAAS preferred</li>
<li>History of hitting &amp; exceeding goals/quota</li>
<li>Experience portfolio building</li>
<li>Excellent cold-calling skills</li>
<li>Strong verbal and written communication skills</li>
</ul>
