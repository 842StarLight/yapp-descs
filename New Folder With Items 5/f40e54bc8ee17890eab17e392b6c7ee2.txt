<p><b>Visual Merchandiser General Objectives:</b><br>
<br>
At Furniture Row, our Visual Merchandiser will implement and maintain appropriate floor displays, in accordance with the Visual Merchandising Guidelines. Develop and grow accent sales (plants, pictures, lamps, mirrors, rugs, etc.).</p>
<p>
The Visual Merchandiser should have a strong understanding of the marketing and visual merchandising budget and work within the budget parameters. They will implement visual merchandising displays and fixture presentations &amp; will be involved in preparing for and presenting at retail meetings.</p>
<p><b>
Job Duties:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Communication with store leadership to discuss and plan floor design, layout formats, display points, items to be displayed, and creative design flow of the store merchandise.</li>
<li>Design window or internal displays based on a theme, style/trend, and advertising components.</li>
<li>Obtain accessories in congruence with styles and trends while constructing displays to include: painting, wiring, design, layout, etc.</li>
<li>Actively optimize lighting to display merchandise to reflect style/trends as well as showcasing the presentations to maximize customer engagement.</li>
<li>Arrange ticketing and signage to enhance presentation of merchandise while reflecting advertising, promotional opportunities, and customer engagement.</li>
<li>Set-up, maintain, and remove displays based on store leadership and advertising trends.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Education/Experience:</b></p>
<p>
A minimum of 1 – 2 years practical interior design, display merchandising experience and/or an Interior design degree from an accredited institution.<br>
<br>
<b>Other Requirements:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Strong communication and organizational skills</li>
<li>Ability to train others to accessorize through knowledge of design, fashion, and new product/ development trends</li>
<li>Possess a valid Driver’s License and provide own transportation</li>
<li>At least 18 years of age</li>
<li>Possess a High School Diploma or Equivalent</li>
<li>Available to work evenings, weekends, and holidays</li>
</ul>
