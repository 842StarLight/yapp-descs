<p></p>
<p>Under limited supervision, responsible for researching, designing, and producing instructional content for eLearning and instructor led materials supporting the Retirement Solutions Group within Empower while leveraging skills in needs analysis, project management, course development, and evaluation methodologies. Manages development of large training projects independently from beginning to end and successfully incorporates subject matter experts, other team members and leadership in the process.
</p>
<p><b>DUTIES / RESPONSIBILITIES / ESSENTIAL FUNCTIONS:
</b></p>
<p>Design and Development
</p>
<ul>
<li>Develops learning solutions inclusive of instructor-led sessions, self-paced e-learning, virtual classroom experiences and assessments while applying instructional strategies that align with the intended outcomes.
</li>
<li>Uses industry standard e-learning authoring tools to create and upload LMS compliant courses; researches and stays current on industry standard tools and technologies.
</li>
<li>Translates and organizes complex content into formats conducive to learning with a strong focus on internal and external customers.
</li>
<li>Applies modern instructional strategies according to intended behavioral outcomes and for high engagement.
</li>
<li>Develops interactive media including audio, video, interactions, animation and simple gamification.
</li>
<li>Creates and modifies images for use in learning materials.
</li>
<li>Progressively learns business systems, policies and procedures to better design effective training solutions.
</li>
<li>Leads or participates in all phases of the ADDIE process.
</li>
</ul>
<p>Project Management and Evaluation
</p>
<ul>
<li>Provides project management and leadership for course development and maintenance projects, ensuring timeline and quality requirements are met; communicates project timelines, statuses, and action items to stakeholders.
</li>
<li>Conducts evaluations and assessment measurements of learning programs to ensure continuous improvement.
</li>
<li>Manages workload responsibly and meets project goals and deadlines.
</li>
</ul>
<p>Team Work
</p>
<ul>
<li>Researches and shares learning and development best practices and proactively identifies new technologies and tools that improve organizational performance.
</li>
<li>Performs peer reviews to edit, standardize, improve, and enhance team content.
</li>
<li>Participates in developing and follows team design standards and templates.
</li>
<li>Interfaces with project sponsors, subject matter experts, and training colleagues during all phases of system analysis, testing, training, and deployment.
</li>
<li>Other duties as assigned.
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>EDUCATION:
</b></p>
<ul><li>Bachelor's Degree in Education, Instructional Design or a related field or equivalent experience.
</li></ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>OTHER PREFERRED QUALIFICATIONS:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>5+ years working as an instructional designer or courseware developer with experience in instructor-led, virtual classroom delivery and e-learning formats.
</li>
<li>Solid understanding of visual design principles; experience with graphic editing tools such as Illustrator and Photoshop.
</li>
<li>Ability to thrive in a collaborative environment in addition to working well independently.
</li>
<li>Strong project management and time management skills with a focus on efficiency.
</li>
<li>Ability to gracefully manage multiple projects, competing priorities and a dynamic business environment.
</li>
<li>Ability to develop collaborative and diplomatic working relationships with a variety of people; ability to influence without authority.
</li>
<li>Excellent knowledge of learning theory, brain science, adult learning principles, and instructional design principles.
</li>
<li>Experience with each phase of the ADDIE process.
</li>
<li>Strong technical writing and grammatical skills; ability to simplify complex topics and write concisely in an active voice.
</li>
<li>Strong and proactive communication skills.
</li>
<li>Advanced experience with Captivate or Storyline.
</li>
<li>Proficient in Microsoft Office Products, including PowerPoint and Word.
</li>
<li>Strong technical aptitude with desire and ability to learn new technologies and tools.
</li>
