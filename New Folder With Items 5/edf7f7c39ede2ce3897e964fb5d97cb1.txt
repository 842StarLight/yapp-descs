<h2>Territory Sales Executive - New York</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">Passionate about making a difference in the world of cancer genomics?
<br><br>
With the advent of genomic sequencing, we can finally decode and process our genetic makeup. We now have more data than ever before but providers don't have the infrastructure or expertise to make sense of this data. We're on a mission to connect an entire ecosystem to redefine how genomic data is used in clinical settings.
<br><br>
<b>Responsibilities:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Drive strategic business expansion/collaboration opportunities with the following:</li>
<li>Major U.S. cancer centers and clinics / Top 20 largest oncology practices in the territory</li>
<li>Key Opinion Leaders (KOLs) and Academic Medical Centers (AMCs) within the specified territory.</li>
<li>Structure detailed strategic plans for gaining and retaining new and existing clients.</li>
<li>Maximize client-bill contracting opportunities</li>

<li>Collaborate and coordinate with all sales positions (VP, Sales, RSD's, DSM's, SAM's, and GL's) to ensure successful attainment of company goals and objectives</li>

<li>Continually analyze competitive landscape and environment within assigned accounts to determine trends and provide customer feedback to Tempus leadership</li>
<li>Monitor performance of sales to ensure objectives are met</li>
<li>Develop and implement a comprehensive business plan for the territory that will be inclusive of budgets, travel, territory management, goal setting, etc.</li>
