<h2>Greenpoint Interim Controller</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Job description</b></h2>
<div>
<p>Summary<br>
Directs the general financial activities of all Greenpoint companies.</p>
<p>
Essential Duties and Responsibilities<br>
</p>
<div><ul>
<li>Makes recommendations to management regarding corporate economic strategies, objectives, and policies for the company and its subsidiaries.</li>
<li>Recommends modifications to existing corporate programs.</li>
<li>Prepares financial statements in accordance with GAAP, which summarizes and forecasts company business activity and financial position in areas of income, expenses, and earnings based on past, present, and expected operations.</li>
<li>Prepares and uploads financial data to meet parent company reporting requirements in SAP Business Objects Financial Consolidation module.</li>
<li>Works with sister companies to reconcile on a monthly and quarterly reporting basis.</li>
<li>Ensures compliance with parent company policies and works on streamlining Greenpoint companies to corporate procedures.</li>
<li>Prepares consolidated job schedules and necessary elimination schedules for all intercompany transactions.</li>
<li>Directs preparation of budgets, reviews budget proposals and prepares necessary supporting documentation and justification.</li>
<li>Provides management with timely reviews of organization's financial status and progress in its various programs and activities.</li>
<li>Provides implementation and administration oversight and maintains integrity of systems and controls, processes and data and enhances the Company's value.</li>
<li>Advises management on desirable operational adjustments due to tax code revisions, intercompany and interstate transactions.</li>
<li>Arranges for audits of company's accounts.</li>
<li>Prepares reports required by various regulatory agencies.</li>
<li>Leads staff by assisting with planning and direction of their work.</li>
<li>Manages staff to ensure high quality service are provided in a timely manner.</li>
<li>Schedules one on one meetings with staff to distribute information, gather feedback, evaluate progress and lend support.</li>
<li>Promotes and supports the Finance team.</li>
<li>Performs recruiting, hiring, training and evaluations of staff.</li>
<li>Makes recommendations, initiates promotions, transfers, coaching and disciplinary action as necessary.</li>
<li>As part of management team, understands company goals, vision and mission statement and delivers management message to employees.</li>
<li>Provides implementation and administration oversight and maintains integrity of ERP systems across parent and subsidiary companies.</li>
<li>Enhances departmental and cross-company efficiencies.</li>
<li>Maintains regular attendance including being at work, being on time to work and working full shifts.</li>
<li>Other duties and special projects as assigned.</li>
</ul></div>

<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Job requirements</b></h2>
<div>
<p>Required Knowledge, Skills &amp; Abilities<br>
</p>
<div><ul>
<li>Thorough knowledge of GAAP and IFRS.</li>
<li>Thorough knowledge of all local, state and federal mandated laws related to company finance.</li>
<li>Strong written skills with ability to produce professional business communications and thorough employee relations documentation.</li>
<li>Ability to organize effectively and prioritize work to meet deadlines.</li>
<li>Ability to ensure effective follow-up and follow-through.</li>
<li>Strong attention to detail and quality.</li>
<li>Ability to motivate, lead and inspire performance.</li>
<li>Ability to work well independently and cross-functionally as part of an established and growing team.</li>
<li>Strong sense of ethics, character and interpersonal skills with displayed professionalism and confidentiality.</li>
<li>Ability to read, analyze, and interpret general business periodicals, professional journals, technical procedures, or governmental regulations.</li>
<li>Ability to write reports, business correspondence, and procedure manuals.</li>
<li>Ability to effectively present information and respond to questions from groups of managers, clients, customers, and the general public.</li>
<li>Ability to calculate figures and amounts such as discounts, interest, commissions, proportions, percentages, area, circumference, and volume.</li>
<li>Ability to apply concepts of advanced algebra and geometry.</li>
<li>Ability to define problems, collect data, establish facts, and draw valid conclusions.</li>
<li>Ability to interpret an extensive variety of technical instructions in mathematical or diagram form and deal with several abstract and concrete variables.</li>
<li>Experience with project accounting and percentage of completion revenue recognition.</li>
<li>Demonstrated experience with Internet software; MS Suite; Database software, Human Resource Information Systems (HRIS); Payroll systems, and Accounting software.</li>
<li>Ability to sit for extended periods of time, use hands to lift and/or move up to 10 pounds, occasionally lift and/or move up to 25 pounds and have close/color vision ability to adjust focus.</li>
<li>Ability to travel as required.</li>
<li>Ability to work overtime as required.</li>
</ul></div>
