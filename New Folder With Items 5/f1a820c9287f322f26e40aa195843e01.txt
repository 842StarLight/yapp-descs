<h2>Sales Associate, In-home Flooring</h2>

<p>The In-Home Sales Consultant will be responsible for making product presentations in customers’ homes, negotiating and closing the sale for the flooring purchase. Candidates with in-home sales experience from other industries (windows, siding, roofing and etc.) would be considered.
