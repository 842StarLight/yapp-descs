<h2>Senior Interior Designer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>We imagine futures and empower people to create them. We are G70.</b></p>
<p>
In the past 47 years, G70 has grown into a thriving, sustainable, forward-thinking, multi-disciplinary firm specializing in the art of <b>creating</b>. We are thought leaders, technologists, cultural practitioners, visualizers, and members of the community dedicated to the design and planning of innovative and community focused places.</p>
<p><b>
We are currently looking for an INTERIOR DESIGNER to join our team.</b></p>
<p><b>
In this role, a typical day may involve:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Communicating with clients on design intent and progress</li>
<li>
Programming and space planning</li>
<li>
Working alongside architects, engineers, consultants and other team members to find the best design solution to meet the client’s needs</li>
<li>
Development of color and materials palette</li>
<li>
Preparing and reviewing construction documents</li>
<li>
Keeping up to date with products by attending manufacturer representative meetings</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
YOU’VE GOT…</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
A Bachelor’s Degree in Interior Design or Interior Architecture</li>
<li>
At least two years of practical work experience in the field</li>
<li>
Proficiency in using the following software programs – AutoCAD, Revit, Microsoft Office, Adobe Photoshop, and Adobe InDesign</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
BUT ARE YOU ALSO…</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Keen on <b>details</b>? Do you have a skilled eye to notice when something is out of place, missing or incorrect?</li>
<li>
Able to <b>multitask</b>? Are you <b>organized </b>and able to manage multiple deadlines and project teams?</li>
<li>
<b>
Open-minded</b> to work collaboratively with others and welcome others’ ideas?</li>
<li>
<b>
Driven </b>and motivated to excel in your work?</li>
<li>
Able to effectively <b>communicate </b>your ideas to others, both verbally and visually?</li>
<li>
Strong desire to <b>learn &amp; grow</b>?</li>
</ul>
<p>
If so, <b>we want to hear from you</b>.</p>

Requirements:<br>
<p><b>Essential Duties (Major Responsibilities/Activities):</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Development and management of appropriate project work plans.</li>
<li>
Maintain project schedules and deliverables.</li>
<li>
Manage multiple projects at the same time.</li>
<li>
Development or management of concept sketches and renderings (by hand or computer).
</li>
<li>Development or management of concept finishes, mood board and presentation materials.
</li>
<li>Oversee and/or participates in programming interviews with end user and documentation.
</li>
<li>Oversee and/or participates in the selection of finish materials.</li>
<li>
Oversee and/or prepare material finish boards (digital and/or physical).</li>
<li>
Oversee and/or participate in the selection of furniture and equipment.</li>
<li>
Lead client meetings and presentations.
</li>
<li>Client communication management.
</li>
<li>Lead project teams.</li>
<li>
Lead project AEC team meetings.
</li>
<li>Communicate with AEC team members for cohesive design.</li>
<li>
Oversee selection of material and finishes for compliance with local codes and constructability.
</li>
<li>Oversee and/or prepares the schedules and plans for finish materials.</li>
<li>
Oversee and/or prepares construction drawings, working drawings and specifications for buildings.</li>
<li>
Oversee and/or reviews plans for completeness and accuracy.</li>
<li>
Oversee and/or reviews plans and specifications of buildings and other structures for compliance with building, plumbing, electrical, fire and health regulations.</li>
<li>
Oversee and/or confers with officials on requirements of projects; makes site surveys to obtain information and other requirements for projects.</li>
<li>
Integrates engineering systems and components into unified design.</li>
<li>
Oversee and/or prepare millwork drawings for custom designs.</li>
<li>
Oversee and/or prepare FF&amp;E millwork drawings for custom designs.</li>
<li>
Oversee and/or prepares FF&amp;E specifications and budgets.</li>
<li>
Oversee and/or participate in procurement and tracking of FF&amp;E and accessories.</li>
<li>
Assist or Oversee the bidding process for FF&amp;E.</li>
<li>
Assist or oversee the management tracking of FF&amp;E fabrication.</li>
<li>
Oversee and/or review shop drawings for conformity in dimensions, materials and design with contract plans and specifications.</li>
<li>
Oversee and/or review FF&amp;E shop drawings for conformity in dimension, materials and design.</li>
<li>
Conducts periodic on-site observation of work during construction to monitor compliance with plans.
</li>
<li>Oversee installation of furniture and equipment at job site in accordance with FF&amp;E plans and specifications.
</li>
<li>Participate and/or oversee sales office, model rooms and other similar space outfit with FF&amp;E and accessories.
</li>
</ul>

</li>

Other duties as assigned.</li>
