<p>Job Description:</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>
About Loop Commerce:</b></p>
<p></p>
<p>
Ready to work on an exciting product that is at the forefront of changing an entire e-commerce industry and touches millions of consumers across leading retailers? Loop Commerce, a Synchrony Solution, has transformed the retail experience with GiftNow® by allowing retailers to offer gift givers enhanced choices at check out and gives gift recipients personalized options. We’ve recently expanded our capabilities with our Gift360™ suite of products, to drive further value to retailers and consumers. As we continue to grow, we need talented, passionate and innovative people to join our team and help redefine how people buy and receive gifts across the digital and physical worlds.</p>
<p></p>
<p>
We offer a vibrant culture as being a family-like and fun-loving atmosphere, agile, and focused on defining the leading edge of something meaningful for millions of people. We’re located in the heart of Silicon Valley in downtown Menlo Park, CA within a two minute walk from the Caltrain station. And now, while maintaining our start-up culture and fast-pace innovation, Loop has the backing and support of Synchrony to fuel and accelerate its growth and success.</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Essential Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Lead the Loop Commerce marketing efforts for assigned retail partners</li>
<li>
Partner with retailer marketing teams to develop integrated marketing plans that optimize the use of the Loop Commerce suite of gifting solutions</li>
<li>
Create and execute upon shared financial growth goals that align with both the Loop Commerce and retailer business strategies</li>
<li>
Develop and manage a shared annual marketing calendar with retail clients focused on growth metrics, target opportunities &amp; delivery against gifting penetration targets</li>
<li>
Ensure shared marketing calendar aligns with the Loop Commerce business marketing strategy, business objectives, vision &amp; roadmap</li>
<li>
Partner cross functionally with Relationship Managers to cultivate strong partner connectivity/relationship</li>
<li>
Strategic thinker that can see beyond the day-to-day to help reach marketing and sales goals</li>
<li>
Experience with B2B2C solutions within retail and commerce</li>
<li>
Create and leverage playbooks to drive the acquisition and repeat gift purchases across the Loop Commerce suite of product (GiftNow, Digital Gift cards…)</li>
<li>
Execute on retail specific/retailer customer specific marketing campaigns</li>
<li>
For existing SYF clients- identify opportunities to convert GiftNow customers to SYF card customers</li>
<li>
Work collaboratively within Loop/ SYF / client organization to achieve growth goals</li>
<li>
Identify primary path to conversion when shopping for others and integrate Loop Commerce gifting solutions across key customer touch points</li>
<li>
Develop &amp; manage the acquisition and lifecycle marketing strategies and execute seamlessly</li>
<li>
Create marketing campaigns aimed at converting GiftNow recipients to GiftNow users</li>
<li>
Perform other duties and/or special projects as assigned</li>
</ul>
