<p><b>Job Summary:</b></p>
<p>
The Marketing Coordinator / Graphic Designer is responsible for managing our tradeshow presence as well as all tradeshow related scheduling and booking logistics. In this role, your secondary focus will be to support our marketing team as a designer and copywriter, creating meaningful imagery and documents. This position will help develop marketing/communications campaigns and spearhead their implementation. You will be responsible for the day-to-day coordination and execution of the RPO marketing strategy.
</p>
<p>The Marketing Coordinator / Graphic Designer will be part of a motivated team within a fast-paced, energetic and results-driven environment. The right person will have a strong aptitude for trade show administration, a solid design foundation and portfolio, basic copywriting skills and excellent attention to detail. This position reports to the Sales &amp; Marketing Manager.
</p>
<p><b>Summary of Essential Duties:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Coordinate the exhibition logistics and company attendance of various tradeshows and events</li>
<li>
Assist in the ideation, graphic design, copywriting, printing and distribution of all promotional and product related collateral</li>
<li>
Coordinate with printers and other outside venders as needed</li>
<li>
Closely work with senior staff to strategize marketing and advertising campaigns and deliverables</li>
<li>
Perform day-to-day marketing department tasks</li>
<li>
Update the website as needed</li>
<li>
Create and coordinate all social media posts</li>
<li>
Set an example as a front-line representative for all other event staff</li>
<li>
Maintain regular contact and communication with event coordinators and client partners</li>
