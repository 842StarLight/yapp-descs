<h2>Digital Sales Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Cumulus Media | Huntsville AL</b> is searching for a digital sales expert that has an accomplished record of delivering client solutions coupled with successful sales experience in a related digital or broadcast field. The role of the <b>Digital Sales Manager</b> is to maximize revenue opportunities by assisting in the sales of complementary digital products under the Cumulus C-Suite umbrella as well as the market’s/stations’ digital assets (email, website, app, streaming, podcasts, video and social).</p>
<p>Cumulus stations in the area include: WZYP 104.3, 730 AM &amp; 103.9 FM WUMP Sports Radio, News Talk 770 AM &amp; 92.5 FM, NASH ICON 93.3, and 94.1 WHRP. The cluster of 5 stations reaches thousands of listeners on a daily basis.</p>
<p>This individual will integrate digital products and services into our robust radio asset offerings and identify innovative ways to meet the marketing needs of new and existing clients. This is a leadership position and will be responsible for collaboratively working with the cluster’s overall sales leadership and coaching fellow sales team members on selling our suite of digital products.</p>
<p><b>Key Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul><li>Resident expert in all digital products offered by Cumulus including:
<ul>
<li>Our vast marketing extension programs including targeted email, targeted display and geo-fencing, video and social solutions</li>
<li>Our local digital marketing services including website creation, social media and reputation management</li>
<li>Our Station Digital Assets – display and mobile ad positions, pre-roll video. Social media, podcasting and in-stream ads</li>
<li>Act as primary liaison with all market selected third-party vendors (e.g. email and ad targeting vendors) for sending and fulfilling orders, RFP’s</li>
<li>Train and coach AE’s on digital advertising tactics and products – as well as work with vendors to fulfill client needs</li>
<li>Collaborate with sales leadership to develop and execute digital packages and solutions</li>
<li>Measure, grow and exceed digital sales and market share goals</li>
<li>Assist in the preparation, creation and delivery of compelling presentations to clients</li>
<li>Analyze and provide necessary sales reports, market analysis and identify growth opportunities for digital assets</li>
</ul>
</li></ul>
<p><b>Core Competencies:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Ability to develop, demonstrate and train people in digital products and sales strategies</li>
<li>Demonstrate knowledge of selling digital products and applications, including; SEO/SEM, mobile, social media, reputation management, targeted ads (geo-fenced, behavioral) and how they work in tandem with radio assets</li>
<li>Creative thinker and problem solver</li>
<li>Highly organized with ability to support multiple ad buys at the same time</li>
<li>Skilled in oral/written communications with the ability to articulate ideas and guidance concisely and professionally</li>
<li>Comfortable presenting to a diversified array of prospects/clients</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Qualifications:</b></p>
<p>Bachelor’s Degree in Radio and Broadcasting, Communications, Journalism, English or closely related field and (2) years of related experience preferred.</p>

<ul>
<li>Competitive pay</li>
<li>Collaborative and creative work environment</li>
<li>Recognition and reward for outstanding performance</li>
<li>Medical, Dental &amp; Vision Insurance coverage</li>
<li>401K with company match to plan for the long-term</li>
<li>Paid Vacation &amp; Holidays</li>
</ul>
