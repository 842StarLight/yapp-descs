<h2>Sales Representative for Kitchen Remodeling</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Fast growing kitchen remodel company is looking for a Sales Representative.</p>
<p>
We are looking for a Salesperson that exhibits professionalism, passion and drive, to achieve sales goals and grow with us. Experience in selling kitchen remodeling is preferred but not required. Previous sales experience for in-home sales is preferred.</p>
<p><b>
Commission Rate</b></p>
<p>
10% of Revenue and 15% from self-generated leads. 1st three months a base salary of $1,500 will be offered.</p>
<p><b>
Reporting Relationship</b></p>
<p>
The salesperson reports to the owner of Kitchen Tune-Up.</p>
<p><b>
Duties and Relationships</b></p>
<p>
1. Follows up on all leads via phone, e-mail and/or text.<br>
2. Creates outside leads and sales other than those generated by existing advertising.<br>
3. Qualifies each prospect’s wants and needs into a plan of actions.<br>
4. Performs job site measuring and gathers the information necessary to create a cabinet layout.<br>
5. Photographs jobs that are being measured to provide “before” photographs for the showroom album.<br>
6. Gathers information on all job-related products in order to provide a presentation to the customer.<br>
7. Assembles a quote and related information to present to the customer with the goal of creating a sale.<br>
8. Revises quotes or drawings as customers’ needs are assessed. Communicates to design services (where applicable) to enable a customer rendering to be completed.<br>
9. Completes and place orders on behalf of the business, initiates any job change orders.<br>
10. Meets the installer at the job prior to installation to inspect and cover all necessary details.<br>
11. Continues customer contact during the job and monitor all related activities.<br>
11. Processes all payments from the customer.</p>
<p>
Must be 21 years of age, and able to work in the United States, with a valid driver's license. Must be willing to provide their own transportation to job site(s).</p>
