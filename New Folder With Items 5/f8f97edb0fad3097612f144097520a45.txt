<p>
Advantech (www.advantech.com ), the leading Embedded Platform Hardware provider, has been an innovator in the development and manufacturing of high quality, high-performance embedded solutions in the industrial computing and automation markets since 1983. We are looking for an energetic team player to join Advantech Embedded &amp; Networking Computing Group in Irvine.</p>

You will partner closely with the strategic CDS partners and key stakeholders in the Microsoft field including WCB, OCP and product groups in driving platform and service reference for OEMs, and ultimately driving CDS Azure services consumption.</p>

Responsibilities</b></p>

<li>Provide partner go to market and on boarding assistant as needed</li>
<li>Support the channel ecosystem owner in the recruiting and activating regional partner ecosystem to drive IoT sales</li>
<li>Lead a virtual team of technical, marketing, partner and consulting resources to advance in sales process.</li>
<li>Work with partners to build a plan to increase sales of Azure Services</li>
<li>Orchestrate relevant and applicable internal resources to drive maximum coverage on key opportunities and strategies.</li>

<li>Other duties as required</li>
