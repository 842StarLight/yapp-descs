<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities
 </b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div><b>Duties &amp; Responsibilities</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Establish or manage and grow key partner and sponsor relationships.</li>
<li>Increase key sponsors engagement and activations to the properties they are licensed to.</li>
<li>Integrate and support all leagues new partner acquisition and current partner retention programs through development of merchandise strategies.</li>
<li>Work with NAM and VP Client Services to set and implement sales strategies for growth of assigned accounts.</li>
<li>Work within assigned budgets to maintain profitability.</li>
<li>Work with teams to develop creative ideas for client’s external marketing projects, sales promotions, tradeshows, &amp; internal corporate promotions to ensure meeting or exceeding of teams’ sales budgets.</li>
<li>Research, analyze, and capitalize on market opportunities. Minimize effects of competitive activity.</li>
<li>Encourage overall client relations – exhibit high levels of customer service to ensure smooth operations and long-term client partnerships. Develop positive, respectful, long-term relationships between BDA and clients.</li>
<li>Produce high quality service and products. Results driven, flexible and agile.</li>
<li>Perform your job with energy, accountability, urgency, deadlines, and passion.</li>
<li>Other performance expectations as defined by NAM of New Business.</li>
</ul>
