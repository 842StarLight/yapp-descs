<h2>Senior Business Operations Manager - IT</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">The Position
<br><br>
Description –
<br>The primary mission of the Genentech Research &amp; Early Development (gRED) organization is to discover and rigorously assess promising molecule candidates for viable therapeutic benefits. The gRED Business Operations group is responsible for oversight and improvement of key business processes and technological solutions to advance the gRED’s mission.
<br><br>
We are looking for a strong leader, collaborator and team player who is passionate about technology and improving user experience by bringing innovative, valuable and usable technical solutions to our business partners.
<br><br>
<b>Responsibilities:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Managing the suite of portfolio management applications from strategic planning to tactical activities</li>
<li>Determining and specifying organizational needs and requirements for current and future products by leading and facilitating meetings with Director and VP level customers</li>
<li>Driving a technical solution set across development teams through entire lifecycle, from ideation, planning, analysis, design, development, testing, release and communication</li>
<li>Leading cross-functional teams and collaborating with Genentech/Roche IT resources to successfully launch technical products, communicate project goals and manage projects’ scope, timelines and costs</li>
<li>Assessing future business needs and opportunities/improvements for technical solutions</li>
<li>Expanding the reach and scope of our applications to target additional customer needs</li>
</ul>
Qualifications –
<br><br>
<b>Minimum Qualifications:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>BS or BA degree in Computer Science, Information Systems, or Engineering</li>
<li>8-10 years of experience managing and delivering process and system solutions to business customers</li>
<li>A professional focus on solution delivery, solution ownership, and customer-service</li>
<li>A well-organized individual who can communicate effectively in both written and verbal form</li>
<li>A strong understanding of information systems management and modern technology platforms and trends</li>
<li>Willing to work / facilitate meetings across time zones with colleagues in Europe</li>
</ul>
<b>Preferred qualifications:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>M.S. in Computer Science, Information Systems, or Engineering</li>
<li>Previous experience working in an Agile/Scrum environment / technical product development organization</li>
<li>Previous experience working in a life sciences or drug development company is a plus</li>
</ul>
