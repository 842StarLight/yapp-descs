Deli Clerk
<br><br>
<b>Duties &amp; Responsibilities other duties may be assigned:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Practice safe food handling procedures at all times.</li>
<li>Serves customers at the deli counter by listening to the request and obtaining the desired product.</li>
<li>Accurately scales all weighable products, including the use of correct tares &amp; codes to determine price.</li>
<li>Sets up advertising displays or arranges merchandise on counter and tables to promote sales.</li>
<li>Serves customers with a smile and willing attitude. Assures high sales through establishing relationships with customers to ensure repeat business.</li>
</ul>
Qualifications
<br><ul>
<li>No experience is necessary; on the job training is provided.</li>
<li>Strong communication skills</li>
<li>Posses the ability to handle high stress and volume of customers while maintaining a positive shopping experience for the customer.</li>
<li>Availability requirements include; but are not limited to; days, evenings, weekends, &amp; holidays.</li>
</ul>
Physical Demands While performing the duties of this job, the employee is regularly required to:
<br><ul>
<li>Stand for long periods of time; walk; frequent use of hands.</li>
<li>Occasionally required to climb or balance and stoop, kneel, or crouch.</li>
<li>Frequently lift and /or move up to 25 pounds and occasionally lift and/or move up to 75 pounds.</li>
</ul>
