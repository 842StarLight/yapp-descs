<b>About the Position:</b><br>
As a member of the Ceridian Customer Support organization for its fast growing Dayforce SaaS product, the Technical Account Manager (TAM) will provide tactical account management services to assigned key large enterprise clients and partner with Client Support Analysts to deliver best-in-class support. The TAM is hands-on with the Ceridian Dayforce product and demonstrates a strong technical aptitude in order to collaborate with R&amp;D, Implementation, and other key stakeholders ensuring client success and satisfaction. In addition to managing day-to-day client incidents, the TAM responsibilities include: relationship management, identifying improvement opportunities and recommending best practices, architecting effective application solutions, incident management, upgrade planning and coordination, resource and effort prioritization, and other activities that drive client success. The TAM is a skilled partner and the client’s trusted advisor, and finds opportunities to develop a long term relationship with customers by understanding their business operations and optimizing the Ceridian solution to meet their strategic goals and functional needs. This is an excellent opportunity to join a rapidly growing leading innovator in next-generation Human Capital Management solutions in a challenging and rewarding role. The ideal candidate is highly energetic, passionate about client management, and thrives in a highly collaborative and fast paced environment.
<br><br>
<b>Responsibilities:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Maintain the highest level of client satisfaction by developing relationships at all levels within the client organization and delivering high quality solutions</li>
<li>Meet or exceed targeted Net Promoter Score (NPS) levels and client retention metrics</li>
<li>Ensure adherence to Client Service Level Agreements (SLAs)</li>
<li>Provide detailed information about Ceridian products, updates, issues, workarounds, and product release announcements</li>
<li>Investigate functional and technical issues and deliver best practice recommendations to the client</li>
<li>Plan, coordinate, and support application upgrades changes and hotfixes</li>
<li>Solicit client feedback and leverage internal partners to drive product and operational improvements</li>
<li>Partner with Support Management to continuously improve efficiency and quality of service to clients</li>
<li>Engage with resources in Hosting, R&amp;D, and Implementation to drive issues to closure</li>
<li>Provide status reports and account specific KPIs and metrics, as required</li>
<li>Establish recurring support targets and monitor performance</li>
<li>Manage priorities and timely updates for ongoing incidents through the use of Support Portal and other standard tools</li>
<li>Continually advocate for Clients and find new ways to add value to the Client</li>
</ul>
<b>Qualifications:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor's Degree in Computer Science, Engineering, or relevant software/computer related fields</li>
<li>3-5 years of support, education, or consulting/implementation experience on the Dayforce HCM platform</li>
<li>Strong technical background with a proven ability to resolve highly complex issues</li>
<li>Outstanding oral and written communication skills</li>
<li>Proven ability to motivate and lead a team of analysts</li>
<li>Excellent organizational and decision making skills</li>
<li>Deep familiarity with Microsoft Technology (.Net / Windows Server / SQL Server)</li>
<li>Thorough understanding of Software-as-a Service (SaaS) business model and exposure to hosting and change management protocols</li>
<li>Occasional travel to client sites is required</li>
</ul>
<b>Significant assets:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Multiple levels of HCM domain experience (Workforce Management, HR, Payroll, Benefits)</li>
<li>Strong understanding of relational database systems and proficiency with SQL, XML, and/or XSLT</li>
<li>Dayforce HCM solution (all modules) / C# / .NET / Silverlight / HTML5</li>
<li>Prior experience in SaaS, Managed Services, or Hosting services is an asset</li>
</ul>
