<h2>Engagement Manager II - Web</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<div>The Engagement Manager II is focused on our most strategic customers. The function’s goal is to maximize customer satisfaction, retention, and growth throughout the relationship. In this role, you will use utilize your program management skills, strategic thinking, technical acumen, pre-sales skills, and leadership to help evolve our customer’s cloud strategies, moving them from concept to implementation that best leverages both the customer’s and Akamai’s strengths. You will drive business value and innovation and develop strong customer relationships, along with a deep understanding of the customer’s business models.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
As an Engagement Manager II you will:</div>
<div>
<br>
<ul>
<li>Serve as the central point of contact for the customer, expand client relationships, oversee integrated account team execution, and ensure we meet or exceed customer expectations.</li>
<li>Work independently and lead small technical teams with Akamai customers/resellers to evaluate the customer’s web and security architecture to deliver the appropriate integration solution.</li>
<li>Manage the customer implementation portfolio, including architecture design, portfolio planning, creation and maintenance of operational controls and tracking metrics and deliverables.</li>
<li>Participate in operational issues related to specific engagements, including escalations, staffing, utilization management, among other client-facing activities.</li>
<li>Work closely with sales and pre-sales partners to educate customers on Akamai's services and solutions. This includes conference calls, site visits, presentations, technical evaluations, technical objection handling, proposals, and follow up on all customer related issues.</li>
<li>Strategically develop and technically manage the customer’s environment and use of Akamai’s products and services.</li>
<li>Create and expand the customer relationship through exploration of the customer’s business.</li>
<li>Educate the customer on Akamai’s portfolio and roadmap.</li>
<li>Up to 25% travel required.</li>
</ul>
<br>
</div>
<div>
<b>
About the Team</b><br>
The Services team is an experienced group of industry experts and Internet technologists working with the top industry companies. From a range of backgrounds across these key verticals, they engage with our customers to consult on industry best practices, design and build quality solutions, and always maintain the highest quality customer service.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
<div><b>Required Education and Experience</b></div>
<ul><li>Minimum 5 years of relevant experience and a Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science, MIS, Engineering, another related field or its equivalent.</li></ul>
<div></div>
<br>
<div><b>
Required Skills</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Minimum 3 years of experience delivering consulting services.</li>
<li>Minimum 3 years of experience with enterprise-level project management (client-facing experience, task management, risk management).</li>
<li>Minimum 3 years of experience and familiarity with one or more of the following: web or security methodologies, technologies, and/or development languages, such as HTTP, DNS, TLS, Web Application Firewalls, etc.</li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
<b>
Desired Skills</b><br>
<ul><li>Detailed knowledge of and experience in web performance, web security, DevOps, and application performance analytics.</li></ul>
</div>
<ul>
<li>Internet technology experience and/or experience with products and processes related to web application architectures, application security, and automation.</li>
<li>Experience leading a small team and/or acting as a mentor.</li>
<li>A proven track record demonstrating business acumen, business management, strategic agility, and a passion for excellence.</li>
<li>Experience in selling products and/or services and developing successful client relationships.</li>
<li>Must be able to work independently as part of a remote team.</li>
<li>Strong written and verbal communication skills, including the ability to effectively present technical and business-related subjects to all audiences, from the user to the executive level.</li>
</ul>
