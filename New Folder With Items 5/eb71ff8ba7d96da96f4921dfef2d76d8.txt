<h2>Business Development Representative</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">Alpha ( https://workable.com/nr?l=http%3A%2F%2Fwww.alphahq.com ) (alphahq.com) is a venture-backed and growth-stage company based in New York, NY whose on-demand insights platform empowers clients to rapidly validate growth opportunities. Our clients use Alpha to accelerate experimentation, inform business decisions with customer wants and needs, deliver and scale agile research capabilities, and develop better products faster. By integrating traditionally manual tasks like audience sourcing, concept designing, test scripting, and data reporting, the platform accelerates time-to-insight from months to hours.
<br><br>
In short, we're the fastest way for organizations to learn more about their future customers.
<br><br>
As a Business Development Representative at Alpha, you'll be an integral part of the marketing team, a partner to the Sales team, and essential to the revenue growth of the company. You'll be working alongside an experienced team who are dedicated to fostering meaningful personal and professional growth.
<br><br>
To make sure you are set up for success, you will go through an initial 30-day training period where you'll get equipped with various sales techniques and gain a basic understanding of Alpha's offering, the competition, product management principles, and the transformational outcomes we help customers achieve.
<br><br>
RESPONSIBILITIES
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Develop and qualify inbound marketing leads - Email and call prospective clients using templated and personalized emails and messages; build and manage a pipeline of prospective clients</li>
<li>Prospect and build client lists - Research and determine prospective client lists using objective criteria; identify the right individuals to reach out and how to reach out to them</li>
<li>Run qualification calls - Qualify prospective clients based on qualification criteria</li>
<li>Source and schedule meetings for our Enterprise Sales team - Deliver qualified prospects to our sales team and meet or exceed opportunity generation quotas</li>
</ul>
