<h2>Content Marketing Associate</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">Because you belong at Twilio
<br><br>
The Who, What, Where and Why
<br><br>
Who?
<br><br>
Twilio SendGrid is looking for a creative and data-driven writer to join the Content Marketing Team as a Content Marketing Associate. You are a recent college graduate and strong writer who has an interest in driving traffic and revenue through content. You are excited to experiment with different content forms, both written and visual, and are ready to make an immediate impact!
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Super detail-oriented and leave no stone unturned when editing.</li>
<li>Driven to see your writing make a direct impact on revenue and have demonstrated verbal and written communication skills.</li>
<li>Strategic in your approach to content creation.</li>
<li>Able to write under short deadlines.</li>
<li>Excited to contribute your voice to SendGrid's content.</li>
<li>Ready to join an energetic, fast-paced team.</li>
<li>Creative in how you develop and write content pieces.</li>
<li>Bechelor's degree in English, Journalism or Marketing</li>
</ul>
What?
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Contribute original articles to our best practices blog.</li>
<li>Identify areas for optimization in our resources and blog</li>
<li>Help edit and write our customer and prospect email communications</li>
<li>Assist with the creation of new visual and media releases (webcasts, guides, and thought leadership videos)</li>
<li>Help schedule our monthly editorial calendar and attend relevant product, marketing, and sales meetings to help with planning</li>
<li>Assist with cross-departmental copy editing requests</li>
<li>Research and write best practices guides that will be showcased in our resources library</li>
<li>Help identify new content channels for us to distribute our visual and written content</li>
</ul>
Why?
<br><br>
Twilio is a company that is empowering the world's developers with modern communication in order to build better applications.
<br><br>
Twilio is truly unique; we are a company committed to your growth, your learning, your development and your entire employee experience. We only win when our employees succeed and we're dedicated to helping you develop your strengths. We invest in weeks dedicated to tackling hard problems and creating your own ideas. We have a cultural foundation built on diversity, inclusion and innovation and we want you and your ideas to thrive at Twilio. Come join us.
<br><br>
Where?
<br><br>
This position will be located in our office at 1801 California St in Denver, CO. You will enjoy our office perks: catered meals, snacks, game room, ergonomic desks, monthly All Hands and more. What you will also get to experience is a company that believes in small teams for maximum impact; that strives to balance work and home life, that understands that this is a marathon, not a sprint; that continuously and purposefully builds an inclusive culture where everyone is able to do and be the best version of themselves. We seek people who naturally demonstrate our values ( http://www.twilio.com/values ), who are challenged by problems, empower others to thrive, people who can draw the owl and not be beholden to one playbook.
