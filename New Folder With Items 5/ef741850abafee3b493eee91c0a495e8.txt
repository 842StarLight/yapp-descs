<h2>Solutions Engineer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Solutions Engineers interface directly with our enterprise-grade customers via ticket system, live chat, video, and telephone to assist them with technical support issues or inquiries. As an agile team solutions engineers must be able to “think outside the box” and be comfortable performing a wide variety of tasks, some outside the scope of your standard “IT admin”. Other duties may include creating our video-based customer facing documentation, making technical product recommendations, recreating customer scenarios in the lab, and serving as the “go to” technical resource for the pre-sales process.</p>
<p>An ideal candidate for this role will have a wide breadth of knowledge and experience with a variety of knowledge domains in IT including networks, systems, and cybersecurity. An outstanding candidate will have previously been the “go-to” network engineer, system administrator, or security engineer in a previous role. If you have an innate curiosity to understand how systems work (and could possibly be abused), a drive to find creative solutions to problems others may think are impossible to solve, are comfortable in a fast paced, challenging, and exciting environment, and are driven to provide customer satisfaction, we want to hear from you. We’ll provide the opportunity for you to work with some of the largest and most complex networks in the country.</p>
<p><b>DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Assist customers with technical requests via phone, chat, and ticket systems to ensure support issues are resolved in accordance with SLAs.</li>
<li>Act as a ‘project manager’, taking full ownership of technical support cases from inception to solution coordinating escalations as necessary.</li>
<li>Recreate customer scenarios in a lab environment.</li>
<li>Be a technical subject matter expert on the Education Solutions product suite and the underlying infrastructure of the ecosystem</li>
<li>Perform primarily remote (infrequently on-site) product installations, initial configurations, and product training</li>
<li>Create and manage internal and external product and training documentation</li>
<li>Train and mentor other team members.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>SKILLS &amp; EXPERIENCE</b></p>
<ul>
<li>A degree in an IT, Computer Science, or MIS discipline.</li>
<li>Experience working in a K-12 environment.</li>
<li>Solid understanding of networking, server, and web technologies</li>
<li>Expert-level Linux and Networking knowledge.</li>
<li>Experience architecting complex solutions</li>
<li>Experience with IT management products and services</li>
<li>Domain expertise with IT operations, application management and/or security/compliance is desirable</li>
<li>Some experience with network directories is desirable</li>
</ul>
<p><b>QUALIFICATIONS</b></p>
<ul>
<li>The ability to communicate effectively and efficiently to customers over the phone and written channels (ticket\chat\email) is critical.</li>
<li>5+ years of experience in a senior role in either system administration, network administration, security, solutions architect, or a tier 3 support role.</li>
<li>Experience designing and supporting multilayer IP networks; routing and switching</li>
<li>Experience with network security; firewalls, intrusion prevention, risk assessment, pen testing, content filtering, PKI, SSL/TLS</li>
<li>Experience managing large ( &gt;1k seat) Windows and Mac workstation deployments</li>
<li>Experience managing Windows Server or Red Hat Linux server environments in an enterprise setting.</li>
<li>Experience with acquiring and analyzing packet captures</li>
<li>Experience/familiarity with managing enterprise deployments of iOS, Android, and Chromebook devices</li>
</ul>
