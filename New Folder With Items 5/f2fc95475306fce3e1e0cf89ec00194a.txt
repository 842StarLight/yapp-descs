<h2>Finance and Operations Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Skagit Fisheries Enhancement Group </b><br><b>Job Announcement: Finance and Operations Manager </b></p>
<p><b>Posted: </b>October 18, 2019<br><b>Closing Date: </b> November 1, 2019</p>
<p><b>Position Title: </b>Finance and Operations Manager<br><b>Supervised by: </b>Executive Director<br><b>Classification: </b>Regular, Full Time (40 hours), Non-Exempt (hourly)<br><b>Pay Range: </b> $21.00-28.00 per hour (based on experience &amp; qualifications)<br><b>Benefits: </b>WA sick leave, 2 weeks vacation leave, 10 holidays, medical paid at 100% for employee, vision insurance, retirement plan matched up to 3%</p>
<p>The Skagit Fisheries Enhancement Group is a nonprofit organization dedicated to engaging our community in restoring salmon populations for future generations. Established in 1990, we have a long history working with our community to ensure the future stewardship of our watersheds through habitat restoration, volunteer engagement, and community education. For more information about SFEG, please visit our website at www.skagitfisheries.org.</p>
<p><b>Position Summary: </b><br>The Finance and Operations Manager is a cooperative team member working to ensure the long- range vitality of the Skagit Fisheries Enhancement Group through careful, accurate management of all organizational finances. An understanding of non-profit finance and accounting is essential to communicate SFEG’s fiscal health to board and staff at an organizational, grant, and project level.<br>The Finance and Operations Manager plays a critical role in the management of the Skagit Fisheries<br>Enhancement Group by processing payroll, analyzing financial data and ensuring that all reporting is up to date and accurate to facilitate budget compliance and staff planning efforts. The Finance and<br>Operations Manager will provide leadership by ensuring internal controls, policies and procedures are adhered to and improved upon to accommodate organizational growth. The Finance and<br>Operations Manager also manages business operations and provides competent, accurate tracking of grant expenditures, compliance with grant requirements and tracks complex grant budgets to ensure effective expenditures of funds.</p>
<p><b>MAJOR DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES: </b><br><b>Finance: </b></p>
<ul><li>Work with Board of Directors and Executive Director to assure accounting system and</li></ul>
<p>QuickBooks procedures are set-up and running efficiently, minimizing time required to perform duties</p>
<ul>
<li>Ensure day to day bookkeeping tasks are executed in an effective and cost-efficient manner</li>
<li>Adhere to SFEG’s Financial Management Policies and Procedures and update as may be necessary to assure top quality internal controls, fiscal management and grant compliance</li>
<li>Ensure compliance with GAAP, OMB standards and other regulatory requirements as may be required for proper non-profit fiscal management and compliance with grant regulations</li>
<li>Prepare and process bi-weekly payroll, ensuring accuracy of all timesheets and data entry</li>
<li>Ensure timely and accurate payment of all payroll taxes</li>
<li>Set up new employees in QuickBooks and maintain accurate payroll accruals, rates and tax liabilities</li>
<li>Provide timely and accurate month-end financial closing</li>
</ul>
<ul><li>Prepare and clearly communicate monthly, quarterly and annual financial statements for board of directors and staff</li></ul>
<ul>
<li>Manage accounts payable and accounts receivable</li>
<li>Coordinate with payroll vendor including L&amp;I and SIMPLE IRA and employee benefits, including complex back-end allocation of payroll between grants</li>
<li>Verify accuracy of financial data and make recommendations</li>
<li>Manage, track, &amp; pay quarterly sales tax as needed</li>
<li>Reconcile bank accounts and project cash flow</li>
<li>Process and manage all donations and tracking of donations along 990 specifications</li>
<li>Coordinate with staff to prepare financial reports needed to manage projects</li>
<li>Create complex, grant-specific invoices required for grant reimbursements using</li>
</ul>
<p>QuickBooks</p>
<ul>
<li>Serve on SFEG’s Finance Committee, attend Executive Committee and Board Meetings as needed</li>
<li>Reconcile and analyze daily cash account balances</li>
<li>Maintain risk management area back-up</li>
<li>Coordinate annual budget development, revision, and tracking process</li>
<li>Analyze budget vs. actual statistics</li>
<li>Manage cost recovery accounts including determination of recovery costs, billing and utilization of funds for replacement/repair</li>
<li>Manage monthly cost allocation</li>
<li>Prepare and submit necessary items for annual 990 preparation to accountant</li>
<li>Prepare annual state reports and renewals (e.g. WA corporate &amp; charitable renewals, etc.)</li>
<li>Preparation necessary items for completion of annual financial review as well as other audits required by external entities including Federal A-133 audits when needed</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Human Resources: </b></p>
<ul><li>Administer health insurance, retirement plan, and other benefits as needed for employees.</li></ul>
<p>Ensure timely updates as necessary</p>
<ul>
<li>Manage paid time off, sick pay, and holiday pay system</li>
<li>Maintain employee files, employment records, and benefit plan in coordination with</li>
</ul>
<p>Executive Director and supervisors</p>
<ul>
<li>Prepare Annual Employee Forms with updated information</li>
<li>Prepare and update composite rate forms for all employees as needed</li>
<li>Stay current on employment laws</li>
<li>Help to provide a welcoming and supportive environment</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Operations Management: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Create and maintain organizational office systems</li>
<li>Process all new funding contracts for projects and programs. Manage SFEG’s contract filing system, including financial and contractual documents for grants and projects</li>
<li>Understand and adhere to all contract compliance guidelines and create and/or refine internal instructions for grant billing</li>
<li>Communicate with staff to ensure contract compliance, accurate expenditures, adherence to all rules and regulations, deliverables are met and contract provisions are followed</li>
<li>Manage matching funds, provide match documentation, track match to ensure accurate utilization of matching funds, communicate with staff to comply with internal matching fund strategies</li>
</ul>
<ul><li>Track individual grant budgets, provide reports to staff, assist with grant or contract spending and close-out plans</li></ul>
<ul>
<li>Ensure accurate coding of bills to comply with grant or contract rules and scope eligibility</li>
<li>Work with external grant managers to ensure compliance and accurate tracking</li>
<li>Prepare, and oversee preparation of, regular grant or contract billings for state, federal and private funders</li>
<li>Prepare monthly, quarterly, annual and end-of grant reports with project managers and coordinators</li>
<li>Coordinate troubleshooting and maintenance of SFEG computers and computer network and coordinate computer repair and replacement needs with contractor(s)</li>
<li>Track and order office supplies as needed</li>
<li>Track equipment purchases and manage SFEG merchandise and equipment inventory</li>
</ul>
