<h1 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>DESCRIPTION</b></h1>
<p>Kimball Midwest, a national distributor of maintenance, repair and operation products, is searching for a Customer Care Representative in our Marketing Development Department for our Columbus, OH location. We are looking for an assertive, hardworking, motivated, and customer focused individual.</p>
<p>
Our Customer Care Representative will research sales leads, make contact with potential customers through daily calls and email leads and enter data obtained from the leads for the sales force.</p>
<p>
As a Kimball Midwest employee, you will experience why we have been listed as one of the Top Workplaces in Columbus six years in a row! Our sales revenue growth is dynamic, increasing from $1 million in 1983 to more than $240 million today. Throughout all our growth we have kept the family owned and operated culture alive. At Kimball Midwest, you are a name and not a number and we pride ourselves on our unique culture.</p>
</div></div>
<div>
<h1 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>QUALIFICATIONS</b></h1>
<ul>
<li>This position requires some completed college coursework in business administration or other areas</li>
<li>This position requires prior customer service experience and the ability to communicate efficiently on the phones</li>
<li>This position requires basic understanding and proficiency in standard Microsoft computer software programs, Excel, Word, Access and Outlook</li>
<li>This position requires the ability to work independently, respond quickly to problems, and work in a stressful environment</li>
<li>This position requires excellent writing and editing skills</li>
<li>This position requires the ability to adapt and support the Sales Team and their mission; requires the understanding and adherence to the ‘Two Customer Philosophy</li>
<li>This position requires a high degree of accuracy and attention to detail</li>
<li>This position requires the ability to communicate effectively with co-workers, supervisors, and managers—both orally and in written form</li>
<li>This position requires the ability to read and write small letters and numbers and the ability to make arithmetic calculations</li>
</ul>
