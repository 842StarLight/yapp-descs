<b>Position Summary:</b><br>
</div>
<div>The primary focus of this position is to lead and manage the facility's sales, marketing and admissions, to provide the number of referrals and admissions that are required to meet or exceed facility census and revenue goals, by accomplishing the specific business goals, reporting to the facility Administrator and Regional DOBD.<br>
<br>
</div>
<div><b>Responsibilities:
</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Oversee a focused team of payor relations staff aligned with the assigned markets</li>
<li>Directs the coordination and integration of managed care services with other departments and the primary functions of their market facilities.</li>
<li>Responsible for managed care team building, company managed care marketing initiatives.</li>
<li>Development/Management of financially viable managed care contracts.</li>
<li>Maintains regular and open lines of communication with key customers by developing and managing close, internal and external, business relationships in order to monitor market conditions while sourcing new business opportunities and to ensure on-going account servicing in a manner that maximizes customer satisfaction while elevating Windsor’s facility profile in the marketplace.</li>
<li>Works closely with the senior staff in developing inclusive sales and business plans as well as quarterly and annual revenue forecasts designed to meet and exceed the profit and growth objectives of the company.</li>
<li>Meets with vital industry influencers including contracted and non-contracted medical groups, workers compensation organizations, health plans and hospitals.</li>
</ul>
<div><b>Knowledge/Skills/Abilities:
</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Self-motivated and results oriented.</li>
<li>Ability to apply knowledge of medical terminology and payor reimbursement methodologies including managed care requirements and strategies.</li>
<li>Strong organizational skills.</li>
<li>Excellent oral and written communication and interpersonal skills.</li>
<li>Ability to communicate in English effectively through verbal and written means.</li>
<li>Ability to work under stress with conflicting priorities and deadlines.</li>
<li>Ability to think critically, review and understand contract language.</li>
<li>Computer Skills including Microsoft Office software.</li>
</ul>
<div><b>Experience:
</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Knowledge of managed care contracting required in any of the following settings: Long Term Acute Care, Skilled Nursing/Transitional Care, Inpatient Rehab, Home Health/Hospice</li>
<li>Minimum of 5+ years Healthcare experience, including at least 3+ years experience in healthcare administration</li>
<li>3-5 years experience Sales and Marketing and/or Admissions experience in a Hospital or Long-Term Care facility</li>
</ul>
