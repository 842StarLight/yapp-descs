<h2>Branch Sales Director</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Overview
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>Manages a branch that includes sales teams, who sell specific business system products and services including: black &amp; white and color printers and copiers, facsimile equipment, document management solutions and related software. Responsible for strategic planning to ensure that the company objectives for their market are achieved, as well as the professional development of sales managers and overall profitability of the branch.
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities
</b></h2>
<div><div><ul>
<li>Responsible for the overall profit and loss of the business unit.</li>
<li>Manages the entire local sales organization within the assigned territory.</li>
<li>Responsible for the overall branch morale and satisfaction of both internal and external customers.</li>
<li>Works closely with Senior Executive Management to ensure that corporate directives are clearly communicated and achieved on a local, branch level.</li>
<li>Provides direction and leadership toward the achievement of the organization's philosophy, mission, strategy, and its annual goals and objectives.</li>
<li>Displays excellent human relations skills as judged by intellect, communications ability, approachability, and integrity.</li>
<li>Responsible for devising creative ways to recognize outstanding performance and effort from branch employees, i.e. dinners, trophies, recognition meetings, etc.</li>
<li>Insures consistent pricing for sales orders, manage special pricing arrangements and discount approval process within guidelines.
</li>
</ul></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Qualifications
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>Bachelor's degree in a relevant field or equivalent experience required, plus 12 years of related experience.<br>
<ul>
<li>Progressive responsibility in sales, industry and marketing experience.</li>
<li>Extensive knowledge of Canon products and competitors.</li>
<li>Strong communication skills including the desire to develop and lead a sales team, an interest in learning new technology in an evolving industry.</li>
<li>Excellent time management skills.</li>
<li>May require up to 50% travel locally, regionally and/or nationally (valid driver's license and acceptable driving record necessary).
</li>
</ul>
</div></div></div>
