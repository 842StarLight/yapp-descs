<h2>Product Support Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>We are looking for a talented Product Support Manager who will be accountable for the oversight of a team of approximately 30 team members, the management of day to day workflow, and reporting to the Director of Client &amp; Product Support. The main role of this position will include but not be limited to: best in class customer experience, developing &amp; deploying execution strategies, training, managing and developing staff; collaboration with other departments, and working in cross functional teams in the successful execution of cross-departmental projects; providing feedback, reporting and proposals to leadership to assist executive decisions.</p>
<br>
<p><br>
<b>What You’ll Be Doing:</b></p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Leadership Presence and Management</b>:</p>
<ul><li> Consistently demonstrate skills and experiences in dealing with non-exempt (hourly) employees in creating an effective team environment.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Work to successfully create a culture for the hourly workforce that is consistent and compatible with CU Direct Operations, policies, and procedures.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Represent Client Support Operations and effectively work with other organizations areas, i.e. Sales, Product Management, Product Development, Marketing, and Accounting.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Recruit, hire, provide or identify development opportunities for the staff. This includes technical, product and soft skill training linked to increase in knowledge, service levels, responsibilities, initiatives, and personal development.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Fully support the Enterprise Risk Management Program and the various risk projects that will be launched.</li></ul>
<p><b>Customer Care Operations</b>:</p>
<ul><li> Design, manage, control, and monitor the day-to-day Customer Care operations to ensure business objectives are being met.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Assist in generating business and system reports for management.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Collaborate with other departments on credit union or dealer issues (on-line product system issues, workflow, staff conflict, marketing/communications coordination, etc.) to find effective solutions that result in timely and efficient delivery of services and support.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Ensure timely communication to clients of product and system issues.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Promote and help implement technologies aimed at improving overall department efficiency and client experience, such as Computer Telephony Integration (CTI), Interactive Voice Response (IVR), effective ticketing systems and self-service capabilities by the clients.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Provide business analysis support on CEM related to information on Credit Unions and Dealers and work to develop a Knowledge Base process to capture information to be organized, shared, searched and utilized by Customer Care staff to properly and consistently respond to client questions.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Engage in improving and monitoring critical end to end processes involving multiple organizations especially related to billing and funding.</li></ul>
<p><b>Product Knowledge</b></p>
<ul><li> Maintains a high level of technical and operational expertise for CU Direct products and systems issues.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Oversee the client support knowledge base to ensure staff has the information they need to effectively support our clients and products.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Works with other staff to develop comprehensive operational training programs and materials for Client &amp; Product Support staff related to product support.</li></ul>
<br>
<p><br>
<b>Operational Standards, Policies &amp; Support:</b></p>
<ul><li> Identify, develop, implement, and manage operational standards resulting in (1) system security; (2) data integrity; (3) workflow process efficiencies; and (4) accurate and timely delivery of services.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Ensures policy and procedural changes are communicated to the staff and appropriate departments. Participate in department audits, working with auditors and updating or report recommended solutions.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Apply working knowledge of operational procedures and processes to product processing systems and associated databases.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Actively participate in the product management, product development, product sales end to end life cycle from a Customer Care Operations perspective.</li></ul>
<p><b>Culture and Teamwork:</b></p>
<p>Work to foster and enhance teamwork within the organization. Provide the highest quality products and services to each other and our clients. Consistently promotes and models the culture and values of CUDC. Demonstrates the behaviors aligned with each value with clients and employees across the organization.</p>
<br>
<p><br>
<b>The Ideal Candidate:</b></p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Education:</b></p>
<p>Bachelor’s Degree in Business, or combination of related courses and experiences.</p>
<p>Specialized courses, seminars and/or training classes related to Agile Methodology and/or SQL database</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Experience:</b></p>
<p>A minimum of seven (7) years technical experience supporting applications</p>
<p>Five years of management experience in a customer service environment including track record of team development.</p>
<p>A minimum of three (3) years technical support management experience</p>
<p>Experience with collaborating and coordinating with internal IT teams to resolve application issues</p>
<p>Familiarity on windows architecture, including .NET, Windows Server, &amp; IIS is a plus</p>
<p>Successful track record in working in growing business and technology environments.</p>
<p>Experience with evaluating and integrating technology solutions in customer support environments.</p>
<p>Previous experiences in leading and managing a customer support department in a SaaS company.</p>
<p>Knowledge of the credit union, mortgage banking and auto sales industries a plus.</p>
<p>Experience with Consumer and Mortgage LOS software strongly preferred.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Specialized Skills:</b></p>
<p>Excellent customer service skills</p>
<p>Strong prioritization skills and excellent time management and organizational skills required to handle multiple high priority tasks simultaneously</p>
<p>Proven ability to train and development staff.</p>
<p>Strong verbal, written communication, analytical and interpersonal skills</p>
<p>Strong aptitude for problem-solving and the ability to resolve complex issues while retaining a strong customer service focus</p>
<p>Exceptional trouble-shooting and problem-solving analytical skills and ability to identify potential technical causes of product and application issues.</p>
<p>Innovative mindset change agent in operational processes.</p>
<p>Excellent knowledge on PC based computer systems, including Microsoft Word, PowerPoint and Excel</p>
<p>Working knowledge of remote access tools and browsers such as: Bomgar, Webex, Firefox, Chrome, and IE.</p>
<p>Knowledge of Agile Methodology and SQL database functions</p>
<p>Knowledge of programming languages such as C# and JavaScript a plus.</p>
