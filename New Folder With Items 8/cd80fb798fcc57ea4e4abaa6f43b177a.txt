<h2>Client Executive - Philadelphia, PA</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>What makes Gartner Sales a GREAT fit for you? When you join Gartner, you’ll set your career on track for outstanding achievement with a company that knows no limits. We’re the world’s leading research and advisory company, achieving consistent double-digit growth by steering clients toward the right decisions with business and technology insights they can’t find anywhere else. Our sales associates earn a competitive base salary, uncapped commissions and exceptional benefits — along with top training and support, and all-expenses-paid luxury incentive trips for high performers. Responsible for detailed understanding of large, global account client business strategy, drivers, goals and initiatives and translating these into Gartner selling opportunities</b></p></li>
<li><p><b>
Account management with outcome of increased customer happiness and increase in retention &amp; account growth</b></p></li>
<li><p><b>
Quota responsibility aligned to specific multinational accounts</b></p></li>
<li><p><b>
Deep understanding and consistent execution of Gartner’s internal sales methodology</b></p></li>
<li><p><b>
Proficient in global account planning and understanding of territory management</b></p></li>
<li><p><b>
Lead forecast accuracy on a monthly/quarterly/annual basis</b></p></li>
<li><p><b>
Maintain high-reaching knowledge &amp; focus</b></p></li>
<li><p><b>
Fiscal responsibility with regards to expense management</b></p></li>
<li><p><b>
In-depth knowledge of Gartner’s products and services</b></p></li>

10-15 years external experience with shown consultative sales, preferably experience in high technology (services, software or hardware), with evidence of prior success in Sales.</b></p></li>
