<h2>Sales Support Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<b>The Opportunity:</b> We are seeking a Sales Support Specialist for a full time position with Technologent.
<ul>
<li>
<b>Role:</b> Sales Support Specialist</li>
<li>
<b>Experience:</b> 5+ Years</li>
<li>
<b>Work Location:</b> Irvine, CA</li>
<li>
<b>Project Duration:</b> Full Time</li>
</ul>
<b>This is an office based position.</b><br>
<br>
Responsible for teaming with the Technologent field, customers, and suppliers to provide pre-sales configuration, costing, and quoting support in delivering Technologent solutions to the customer. It is the responsibility of the Sales Support Team to understand the Technologent quoting and pricing strategies, the technical requirements of the customer solution, and supplier pricing and program offerings.<br>
<br>
<b>Job Responsibilities:</b>
<ul>
<li>Configuration of vendor solutions, based on direction of the Technologent Field. <i>Note: As Technologent’s solution offerings include many diverse products and platforms, some configuration work is managed inhouse and some offerings are configured externally via the various manufacturer or vendor technical resources.</i>
</li>
<li>Assisting the Technologent field with vendor specific opportunity registrations.</li>
<li>Submitting pricing requests, per the vendors defined pricing programs.</li>
<li>Understanding all new transactional based vendor programs and applying them, in order to maximize margin.</li>
<li>Initial product sourcing review (ie: readily available product to meet potential implementation dates).</li>
<li>Accurately process customer transactions such as quotes, orders, or returns.</li>
<li>Additional point of contact for customer sales inquiries and requests.</li>
<li>Manage time effectively, meet personal goals and work effectively with other members of the Technologent team.</li>
<li>Communicate to the Business Operations department the customer needs for each opportunity won.</li>
</ul>
<b>Desired Skills:</b>
<ul>
<li>Proficiency with Cisco, EMC, HDS, HP, IBM, Oracle, Symantec, VCE, or VMware products and programs</li>
<li>Prior experience with NetSuite ERP/CRM tool</li>
<li>5+ years of Sales Support/Customer Service experience</li>
<li>Applicable training or certifications for the above supplier lines</li>
<li>Exceptional verbal and written communication</li>
<li>Detail oriented</li>
</ul>Technologent is an Equal Opportunity Employer - EEO/AA Employer/Vet/Disabled.<br>
