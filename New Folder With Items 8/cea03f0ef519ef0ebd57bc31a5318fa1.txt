<p>Position Summary
</p>
<ul>
<li>Own and drive the full cycle recruitment strategy and results for F5
</li>
<li>Develop, drive, and execute comprehensive search strategies - including driving a recruitment plan which is geared at building key competitive research, industry profile development, networking, and direct sourcing
</li>
<li>Work with the HR Business Partner(s) and clients to advise on specifics for the position, including leveling/compensation strategy, immigration and market trends
</li>
<li>Utilize strategic advising and influencing skills; demonstrate staffing expertise and provide recommendations to hiring managers
</li>
<li>Serve as the talent specialist on critical recruiting initiatives – craft and implement sourcing and pipeline strategy and timeline – drive the dedicated talent sourcing to hire sales professionals to meet the business need
</li>
<li>Educate/train hiring managers and Sr Management on recruiting processes, use of recruiting software systems, compliance requirements and hiring manager best practices.
</li>
<li>Design and provide on-going training to managers on hiring/interviewing practices and procedures, with a focus on interviewing techniques and EEO/OFCCP/Sarbox compliance.
</li>
<li>Mentor, provide training and encourage knowledge share and teamwork with fellow recruitment teammates and HR Partners
</li>
<li>Deliver results with little supervision and effectively handle multiple projects and candidates.
</li>
<li>Meet standard measurements of success for staffing, including (but not limited to recruitment metrics, annual hiring plan, diversity goals, and cost/time to hire standards
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p>Qualifications
</p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree in related field or equivalent work experience required
</li>
<li>5+ years of experience working in Human Resources / Recruiting environment
</li>
<li>Direct experience at organizations in the following areas of technology is a plus: Application Delivery/Software Defined Networks, Cloud Applications, and/or Security
</li>
<li>Proven experience and passion toward targeting high-producing talent skillsets; ability to creatively find top talent using advanced search techniques, passive sourcing, social media, personal network, referrals, and the internet
</li>
<li>Full-cycle and strong sourcing experience is required with a proven ability to attract, recruit and close
</li>
<li>Proficiency in MS Office applications, Applicant Tracking System usage, and internet candidate search techniques and tools
</li>
<li>Excellent communications both written and verbal required; ability to effectively communicate at various levels of the management chain
</li>
<li>Proven influence &amp; leadership skills; ability to effectively collaborate, lead and drive the recruitment process with hiring managers and candidates
</li>
</ul>
