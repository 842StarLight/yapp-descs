<div>The Plant Controller directs financial activities of the plant and manages dissemination of financial information about the company activities that will assist management. Applies principles of cost accounting to conduct studies which provide detailed cost information not supplied by general accounting systems. Assists other departments in maintaining accurate inventory records. This position is located at our plant in Monroe, Ohio.</div>
<div></div>
<div>Responsibilities:</div>
<ul><li> Maintain manufacturing database, including, but not limited to:</li></ul>
<div>Add new products as required;</div>
<div>Establish standards for raw materials and trade items, if applicable;</div>
<div>Establish standards for purchased finished goods;</div>
<div>Establish production rates for all products;</div>
<div>Establish standard costs and routing for all products;</div>
<div>Establish departmental labor and overhead rates.</div>
<div></div>
<ul><li> Coordinate the measuring and reporting of cost reduction activities for the plant.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Chair biweekly cost reduction meetings.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Communicate financial results with plant management and employees.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Prepare projected monthly sales forecast and profit and loss reports.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Produce journal entries needed for month end close.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Resolve financial transaction and report discrepancies.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Produce various weekly, monthly and quarterly financial reports.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Produce and/or review daily efficiency and material usage reports.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Coordinate and oversee semi-annual physical inventories.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Prepare monthly journal vouchers for accrual and expense accounts.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Prepare annual budget and participate in annual audit.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Participates in monthly corporate accounting meetings.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Supervises subordinate positions in the Accounting Department such as a Cost Accountant, or Junior Accountant. Carries out supervisory responsibilities in accordance with UGN's policies and applicable laws. Responsibilities include interviewing, hiring and training employees; planning, assigning and directing work; appraising performance; rewarding and disciplining employees; addressing complaints and resolving problems.</li></ul>
<div></div>
<div>Qualifications</div>
<ul><li> Bachelor degree in accounting or finance. CPA or MBA is a plus.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Five plus years of related experience and/or training.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Previous Plant Controller experience within the automotive industry or the manufacturing industry desired.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Effective communicator with all levels.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Knowledge of Accounting software; Database software; Manufacturing software; Payroll systems.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Proficiency with Microsoft applications-Word, Excel, Power Point, and Microsoft Outlook, and Manufacturing ERP system (Oracle).</li></ul>
