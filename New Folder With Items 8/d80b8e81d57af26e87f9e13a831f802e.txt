<p>The Customer Service Escalation Specialist will work collaboratively with internal stakeholders to deliver excellent customer service. The incumbent works independently to respond timely, accurately, and sensitively to members' issues by thoroughly researching and analyzing each case, collaboratively working with other departments as necessary, and resolving issues with providers and members or their agents.</p>

<p>DISPUTE INVESTIGATION, RESOLUTION, PREVENTION AND PROCESS IMPROVEMENT</p>
<ul>
<li>Process escalated member complaints received via phones and written correspondence (i.e., portal inquiries and web email).</li>
<li>Investigate facts surrounding member disputes utilizing all available system information, established guidelines and procedures, policy provisions, and consultation with relevant internal or external experts.</li>
<li>Resolve disputes and prevent grievances. Perform analysis on each dispute including all relevant claims and applicable medical records, coding, and charges.</li>
<li>Evaluate relevant member and provider information or documentation.</li>
<li>Determine when to pursue exceptions.</li>
<li>Educate members, providers, and staff, as applicable.</li>
<li>Confer with or refer cases requiring policy exceptions to leadership and organize case information and provide summaries and chronologies to the Office of General Counsel (OGC), the independent review agency, or other internal committees, as appropriate.</li>
<li>Maintain regular communication with the member during the resolution process, gather facts from or educate the member about the determination or policy, and communicate the final resolution.</li>
<li>Record complaint and exception data to identify root causes, timelines, and resolutions.</li>
<li>Analyze medical records for assigned claims and determine if documentation supports coverage for in-network reimbursement. Make recommendations for claims processing or reprocessing.</li>
<li>Work closely with other staff to ensure guidelines are current and reflect up-to-date policy provisions.</li>
<li>Analyze member liability for high dollar claims balances prior to release of member's explanation of benefits (EOB). Review medical records to determine if denied claims need to be reprocessed and, if so, direct staff to do so.</li>
<li>Contact member and provide education on EOB and claim status as appropriate.</li>
<li>Share expertise and knowledge and provide support to key internal staff.</li>
<li>Assist OGC staff on difficult/sensitive claims issues and on claims payments affected by contracting/network contracts.</li>
<li>Draft and review member correspondence as requested.</li>
<li>Maintain accurate complaint/dispute log for OGC and identify trends to leadership for staff training, as appropriate.</li>
<li>Improve complaint/dispute prevention through the identification of claims adjudication errors or incorrect/inaccurate information provided by staff.</li>
</ul>

<p>DISPUTE PREVENTION AND SALES AND CUSTOMER RELATIONS PARTNERSHIP:</p>
<ul>
<li>Serve as liaison to resolve complex inquiries received from customer service, account management, customer solutions or sales teams regarding benefits and claims (including but not limited to telephone, email, fax, chat, social media and written correspondence) by performing research independently to provide accurate and timely responses.</li>
<li>Conduct analysis of medical records in conjunction with benefit plan documents to accurately and effectively communication medical coverage decisions to prevent grievances.</li>
<li>Educate and coach members on their rights within the grievance process.</li>
<li>Collaborate with internal resources to obtain information and help resolve issues in support of the customer service, account management, customer solutions or sales teams, including but not limited to wellness, claims, account collection, or other areas across the organization.</li>
<li>Maintain continuous communication with the applicable sales team members to keep them informed of all escalated member complaints, inquiries, and recommendations handled for their assigned accounts.</li>
<li>Develop and sustain productive relationships with key departments.</li>
<li>Attend monthly meetings for key departments when appropriate.</li>
<li>Provide support for and attend new member meetings or other events as requested.</li>
<li>Attend and assist with employer and prospect site visits including, but not limited to enrollment meetings and other group or broker meetings.</li>
<li>Make recommendations to management and appropriate team members regarding opportunities to improve customers' experience.</li>
<li>Proactively identify transaction and service issues which could impact the customer experience and make recommendations to management for resolution and addressing the root cause.</li>
</ul>
