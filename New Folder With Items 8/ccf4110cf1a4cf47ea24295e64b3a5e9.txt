<h2>Accounts Payable Coordinator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>The Accounts Payable Coordinator position is a full-time employment opportunity. The Accounts Payable (A/P) Coordinator is responsible for processing Accounts Payable activity for Bridgepoint and subsidiaries. The position requires regular data entry of vendor invoices, obtaining approvals, coding, processing payments, filing and reporting.</p>
<p>Essential Job Duties:</p>
<ul>
<li>Review expense reports for compliance with company policy, process approved reports for payment using Concur software</li>
<li>Provide guidance to Concur users on the system itself as well as relevant company policies</li>
<li>Review incoming invoices and check requests for approval and coding requirements</li>
<li>Determine the appropriate coding; compare recommendation from approver against standard accounting practice, including reviewing for capitalization or prepaid requirements</li>
<li>Ensure that sales tax is properly recorded when applicable</li>
<li>Data entry of payable invoices and expenses</li>
<li>Process payments, including recording electronic payments</li>
</ul>
<p>Additional Job Duties (include but are not limited to):</p>
<ul>
<li>Monitor timeliness of payments, including weekly payment recommendations</li>
<li>Respond to payment inquiries by internal and external customers</li>
<li>Review employee reimbursement and company card reconciliations against company policy and process accounting approvals</li>
<li>Filing payables records</li>
<li>Assist during internal and external review by obtaining documents, preparing reports or responding to inquiries</li>
<li>Document Imaging, including document preparation, scanning, indexing, compiling and assembling documents in accordance with company standards</li>
</ul>
<p>Minimum Requirements:</p>
<ul>
<li>General Accounting knowledge</li>
<li>Experience with MS Office and accounting software</li>
<li>At least 2 years of related accounts payable experience, including the use of a document imaging system</li>
</ul>
<p>Preferred Qualifications:</p>
<ul>
<li>Experience with Great Plains software</li>
<li>Knowledge of prepaid accounts, construction in process and deposits</li>
<li>Ability to work in a fast paced, high volume environment</li>
</ul>
