<h2>Team Coordinator - Customer Service Rep</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Cascade Maverik Lacrosse is seeking a Team Coordinator, Gloves (Customer Service Representative),<b> </b>for our business sales operations, located in Liverpool, NY</p>
<p>The Team Coordinator for Gloves will be responsible for delivering pleasant, professional, accurate and timely information to our customers’ inquiries, building and maintaining long term business relationships with both internal and external customers and sales representatives through prompt, efficient and anticipatory service thus promoting loyalty to our brands.</p>
<p>He/she will be responsible for order management including taking accurate information, entering order information using the SAP system, ensuring compliance to current sales programs, ensuring customer delivery requirements are met or exceeded, ensuring adherence to vendor guidelines and maintaining an open order file updating information of current inventory availability. He/she makes appropriate suggestions for substitutions of unavailable products, informs customers of new products in stock and makes order suggestions based on extensive knowledge of both product and customer history. The Team Coordinator is responsible for researching and validating claims, preparing the appropriate credits and/or debits, and creating warranty returns and authorized stock returns.</p>
<p>The Team Coordinator is a shared resource working with the sales department (60%) and the customer service team (40%). He/she is responsible for the coordination and follow-through for team gloves orders and deliveries and performs as a customer service representative in support of the customer service department and may perform activities to proactively source new sales opportunities through inbound lead follow-up and outbound cold calls and emails.</p>
<p>The Teams Coordinator is responsible for understanding and executing actions to produce and deliver custom equipment (gloves) to teams and customers.</p>
<ul>
<li>
<p>Collect information and detail from customers to prepare customer orders</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Collaborate closely with graphics department ensuring Team logo file images meet quality factory production standards.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Prepare detail specification tech packs and deliver to factory.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Track and ensure follow up on factory purchase orders.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Track shipping of finished product from factory to delivery to customer (teams).</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Manage customer warranties.</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p>The customer base includes athletic directors, equipment managers, coaches of the NCAA teams and sales reps.</p>
<p>The Team Coordinator will strive to obtain information on the latest developments in products and maintain superior working knowledge of Cascade and Maverik products and product catalogs in order to analyze, train and support customers’ needs. He/she will maintain frequent communication with all departments (credit, product, warranty, sales, supply &amp; demand) to ensure customer satisfaction for all issues and will work in close collaboration with the distribution center to ensure execution of product launches and timely deliveries.</p>
