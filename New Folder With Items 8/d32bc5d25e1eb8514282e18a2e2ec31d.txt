<h2>Senior Contract Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">BluJay Solutions delivers supply chain software and services to the world's most progressive retailers, distributors, freight forwarders, manufacturers, and logistics service providers. Transforming supply chain logistics with the BluJay Global Trade Network, we enable customers to unlock the power of more than 40,000 universally connected partners. This role allows for remote work in Atlanta.
<br><br>
The Sr. Contract Specialist's primary tasks will consist of preparing and negotiating a variety of customer, vendor and general corporate agreements and amendments for BluJay in a way that complies with company policies to reduce risk and maximize revenue. Experience in dealing with confidential information and interfacing with sales teams and multiple levels of management and employees is required.
<br><br>
<b>Primary Responsibilities:</b><br>
Serve as a liaison among various Company departments to ensure timely and accurate completion of contractual documents.
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Responsible for timely preparing first drafts and handling redlines and negotiation of Company contracts including, but not limited to, Software License &amp; Services Agreements, Amendments/Addenda, Services Agreements, Non-Disclosue Agreements and various partner agreements with support from the General Counsel and the EVP of Legal &amp; HR.</li>
<li>Assist with review, negotiation and processing of vendor agreements, such as technology, marketing and other commercial contracts.</li>
<li>Review signed agreements received for handwritten or non-highlighted revisions and approve for signature. Facilitate signature by official Company signatory.</li>
<li>Serve as primary liaison among various Company departments to ensure timely and accurate completion of contractual documents and field cross-departmental questions.</li>
<li>Direct internal clients in obtaining any necessary approval(s) for non-standard contractual requests. Monitor the approval(s) from the appropriate subject matter experts and release agreement when all approvals are received.</li>
<li>Maintain a proficient working understanding of Company's day-to-day functions and products/services.</li>
<li>Understand the policies, procedures and initiatives of the Company Legal Department and implement them.</li>
<li>Ensure that Company's corporate policies and directives are vetted and incorporated into contractual documents.</li>
<li>Determine whether contract requests which vary from the established standards can be accepted or require additional authorization.</li>
<li>Ensure compliance with financial policies and objectives of Company, spot pertinent business and financial issues in contracts and raise to internal clients/General Counsel/EVP of Legal &amp; HR as appropriate, for review.</li>
<li>Update, maintain and ensure the accuracy of an internal customer information, assist Legal Department staff with various contract-related projects and handle all administrative aspects of contract processing.</li>
<li>Work closely with the General Counsel and EVP Legal &amp; HR on revisions to contract templates.</li>
<li>Other projects as may be requested from time to time by the General Counsel and EVP Legal &amp; HR.</li>
</ul>
<b>Qualifications:</b><br>
Required Education and/or Experience
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor's degree (B.A.) from four-year college or university, or equivalent combination of education and experience.</li>
<li>Five (5) or more years of experience drafting and negotiating commercial contracts in a software company; familiarity with SaaS agreements desired.</li>
<li>High proficiency with MS Office Programs including Word, SharePoint, Excel and PowerPoint and with Adobe Acrobat.</li>
<li>Proactive planning and organizational skills with high attention to detail and desire for excellence.</li>
<li>High energy, creative, self-motivated, and able to complete tasks with minimal supervision.</li>
<li>Ability to prioritize and handle multiple projects in a fast paced environment with detail and sensitivity to confidential matters.</li>
</ul>
<b>Other Qualifications:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Familiarity with commercial contract concepts.</li>
<li>Team player who provides excellent client service, builds internal and external client confidence, sets achievable expectations, ensures commitments are met, solicits opinions and ideas from others, responds in a timely manner and shows tact and diplomacy.</li>
<li>Excellent written and oral communication skills. Actively communicates in an open, clear and consistent manner, explains concepts and procedures clearly and completely and delivers information effectively in a variety of formats.</li>
<li>High level of accountability. Seeks out new responsibilities, acts on opportunities, generates ideas, holds themselves as well as other team members accountable for performance.</li>
</ul>
</div>
