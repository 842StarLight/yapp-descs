<h2>Business Development Representative (Contract/Internship)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p><b>Position Overview</b><br>
The Business Development Representative Contractor/Intern will perform activities to generate leads and new business for the Real Estate business within ExteNet; targets may include multi-tenant corporate real estate, industrial, healthcare, sports/entertainment, and hospitality. Job duties will include cold calling, networking, attending industry events, attending trade shows, and performing research on potential customers.</p>
<p>
This is considered a contract or internship role and the duration is expected to last 6-9 months. The pay is competitive and this is a great opportunity for someone who wants to begin a career in sales.</p>
<div>
<b>
Detailed Responsibilities:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Qualify leads from marketing campaigns as sales opportunities</li>
<li>Contact potential clients through cold calls and emails</li>
<li>Present our company to potential clients</li>
<li>Identify client needs and suggest appropriate products/services</li>
<li>Customize product solutions to increase customer satisfaction</li>
<li>Build long-term trusting relationships with clients</li>
<li>Proactively seek new business opportunities in the market</li>
<li>Set up meetings or calls between prospective clients and more senior-level sales managers.</li>
<li>Present weekly reports to Executive Director of Sales with a pipeline of potential clients, activity of contact with those clients, and related details.</li>
<li>Update company’s CRM with sales activity.</li>
<li>Update the sales pipeline data base with relevant information related to potential clients.</li>
<li>Perform related job duties as needed and assigned.</li>
</ul>
</div>
<p></p>
<br>
<div>
<b>
Qualifications:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree in Business, Marketing, Communications, or similar field.</li>
<li>Previous internship experience in sales or marketing is strongly preferred.</li>
<li>Excellent verbal and written communication skills with a professional presence.</li>
<li>Outgoing demeanor with the confidence and persistence to make cold calls on a daily basis.</li>
<li>Experience delivering presentations in front of a group or via web-based software.</li>
<li>Ability to build professional and cordial relationships with senior-level decision makers.</li>
<li>Good Internet research skills and networking ability.</li>
<li>High degree of teamwork and client service focus.</li>
<li>Proficiency in MS Office tools including Outlook, Word, Excel, and PowerPoint.</li>
<li>Organized, thorough, and detail-oriented.</li>
<li>Able to work well independently without constant guidance from supervisor.</li>
<li>Willingness to travel up to 20%, including some travel by plane.</li>
</ul>
</div>
