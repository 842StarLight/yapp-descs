<h2>Analytics Product Marketing Lead</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div></div>
<div>
<p><b>About MSCI</b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>MSCI, Inc. is a leading provider of critical decision support tools and services for the global investment community. With over 45 years of expertise in research, data and technology, we power better investment decisions by enabling clients to understand and analyze key drivers of risk and return and confidently build more effective portfolios. We create industry-leading, research-enhanced solutions that clients use to gain insight into and improve transparency across the investment process. Our line of products and services includes indexes, analytical models, data, real estate benchmarks and ESG research. For more information, visit us at www.msci.com.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Position overview</b>:</p>
<p>The Analytics Marketing Lead will play a leadership role in driving the growth of MSCI and its Analytics business globally, bringing tangible value in helping management to communicate the firm’s vision and deliver on its strategies. The Marketing Lead will partner closely with executive leadership, product management, research, sales and all marketing functions to develop and execute marketing plans globally that are focused on delivering tangible results. You will be joining an organization that is growing at an extraordinary pace with impressive global marketing, commercial and sales teams.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p>The role reports to the Global Head of Product Marketing based in New York and involves working closely with Marketing Leads located across the globe. You will be responsible for leading and driving innovation in the positioning and marketing of the firm’s Analytics capabilities and innovative platform to a wide range of institutional clients globally. You will be responsible for creating and executing multi-channel marketing programs, with a significant focus on digital channels, to drive demand generation with new clients, as well as existing clients through upsell and cross-sell strategies.<br>
</p>
<p><br>
Critical to your success is the ability to work within Marketing (Product Marketing, Segment Marketing, Corporate Communications, Digital Marketing, Design and Sales Enablement) to execute on these marketing programs. Strong partnership and alignment with the Product, Coverage, Client Service and Research teams is required.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Scope of Responsibilities:</b><br>
</p>
<ul>
<li> Develop and manage marketing programs to stimulate ongoing revenue growth and to support new product launches / key initiatives / partnerships and ensure all content is aligned with the value proposition and brand guidelines</li>
<li> Drive a 360 Marketing Approach to develop and execute marketing programs integrating the majority of marketing mediums, in particular digital marketing—paid and social media, videos, websites and e-marketing—advertising, PR and events</li>
<li> Utilize networking skills to make connections and partner closely with client-facing teams and subject-matter experts to leverage the firm’s intellectual capital and create content that’s relevant, easy to understand and, importantly, conveys the benefit to the client</li>
<li> Partner with other Product Marketing Leads to deliver MSCI solutions and ensures regional marketing plans and strategy are consistent and integrated with the global plans</li>
<li> Produce meaningful metrics for the business and offers fact-based insights to help guide marketing strategies and programs for brand positioning, client retention and development, industry partnerships, and cost-effective methods for attracting new business to the firm</li>
</ul>
<br>
<p></p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Desired experience and qualifications:</b></p>
<ul>
<li> Strong background in product marketing in the FinTech space with an understanding of the investment process. Knowledge of analytics tools are a bonus</li>
<li> 7-10 years marketing communications experience, with an emphasis on creating messaging, competitive positioning and sales enablement activities.</li>
<li> Degree in marketing and/or other marketing related professional qualifications, MBA or CIM</li>
<li> Confident self-starter with leadership qualities and ability to work in a matrix work environment and establish strong partnerships</li>
<li> Experience in engaging with clients and/or building client relationships at many different levels</li>
<li> Proven ability to give concise, compelling verbal presentations to senior decision-makers, and to respond quickly and thoughtfully to challenges and pushback in a meeting</li>
<li> Ability to translate complex and sometimes contradictory themes and opinions to create unified, consistent messaging that effectively communicate the firm’s core values and goals</li>
<li> Strong attention to detail and excellent power point and excel skills</li>
<li> Ability to handle significant workload with excellent organizational skills, work well under pressure and prioritize to meet deadlines</li>
<li>Must be self-motivated, adaptable and able to work in a changing environment</li>
</ul>
