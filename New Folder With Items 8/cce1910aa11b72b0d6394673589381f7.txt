<h2>Global Sales Compensation Program Manager</h2>

<p><b>About NCR
</b></p>
<p>NCR Corporation (NYSE: NCR) is a leading software- and services-led enterprise provider in the financial, retail, hospitality, telecom and technology industries. NCR is headquartered in Atlanta, Ga., with 34,000 employees and does business in 180 countries. NCR is a trademark of NCR Corporation in the United States and other countries.
</p>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li><p>Position: Global Sales Compensation Program Manager<br>
</p></li>
<li>
<p><br>
Location: Atlanta, GA
</p>
<p>Position Summary
</p>
<p>This role reports directly to the Senior Director, Global Sales Compensation, Strategy, Operations and Analytics. Works with the Sales Compensation Committee on the overall NCR and Divisional strategies, design, communication, implementation, and execution of Sales Compensation Plans for the Financial Services and Services Sales organizations. The individual in this role will be required to team with leaders in other areas of the HR function, as well as the legal, tax, payroll, finance and IT functions; and will also interact and communicate with senior leadership on a regular basis for some Industries.
</p>
<p>In addition, they will support the migration to Anaplan for Quota and Territory management.
</p>
<p>Key Areas of Responsibility
 </p>
<div>* Subject Matter Expert and thought leader in all areas of sales/solution incentive compensation –overall philosophy, including plan design, plan mechanics, eligibility criteria, administration, legal compliance, etc.
 <br>
<ul>
<li>Provides direction to the sales/solution administration team, responsible for all aspects of the solution compensation tool, key processes and deployment.
 </li>
<li>Responsible for leading Sales Compensation Committee governance process to ensure incentive plan decisions adhere to the design principles and strategic goals established annually.
 </li>
<li>Actively participates in the design and plan modeling of sales/solution incentive pay programs
 </li>
<li>Drive implementation and coordinates communication and training efforts for variable plans (sales incentive &amp; solution compensation plans).
 </li>
<li>Consults and interacts with Finance partners and other members of Sales and Solution leadership teams on design principles, communication tools, and sales/solution compensation administration tools &amp; processes.
 </li>
<li>Participates in benchmark surveys &amp; studies
 </li>
<li>Stays current on market practices
 </li>
<li>Performs internal and external compensation data analysis to ensure competitiveness and proper resource allocation within our solution incentive compensation programs
 </li>
<li>Conducts data analysis, develops interpretations and recommendations based on findings and NCR’s strategic business needs.
 </li>
<li>The individual in this role will be required to team with colleagues in other areas of the HR function, as well as the legal, payroll, finance, Sales Operations and IT functions; and will also interact and communicate with the leadership team on a regular basis.
 </li>
<li>Ensures that NCR local practices and processes conform to all local and national regulations; May work with local unions / works councils.<br>
 </li>
</ul>
</div>
<div>
<br>
* Participates on special project teams to support new or changing compensation or HR initiatives.
 <br>
<ul>
<li>Work with Sales / Sales Operations/ Finance and the HR community to participate in the creation / execution of the NCR Sales compensation policies
 </li>
<li>Actively participate and lead discussions on such topics as High Achievement, SPIFF, Accounts Receivable, Quota coverage, etc. for Financial Services and Services Sales Organizations.
 </li>
<li>Work with Sales Operations and IT on the migration to Anaplan for Quota and Territory Management.
 </li>
<li>Ensure smooth transition and project plan for our Sales Team while ensuring our existing Professional Services associates are appropriately supported in 2020 (outside of Anaplan).
 </li>
<li>Thorough understanding of various projects/tools throughout NCR to Lead and ensure Sales Compensation teams are aligned to future requirements: i.e.: CDM, ERP, Sales and Services Order Transition
</li>
</ul>
</div>
