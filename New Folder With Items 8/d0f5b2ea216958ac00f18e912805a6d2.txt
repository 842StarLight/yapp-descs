<h2>FIELD ENGINEER</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<h4 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Job Title:</b></h4>
<p>
Field Engineer</p>
<h4 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Job Type:</b></h4>
<p>
IT/Service</p>
<h4 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Location:</b></h4>
<p>
Buffalo</p>
<h4 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Employment:</b></h4>
<p>
Permanent, Full-Time, Salary</p>
<p><b>
Job Description Includes</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Assess/Maintain customer’s network(s) and infrastructure(s).</li>
<li>
Integrate new hardware and software into existing infrastructure(s).</li>
<li>
Configure and maintain WAN connectivity including routers, switches, firewalls and VPN Tunneling.</li>
<li>
Maintain network/data security within the environment, managing identity and access needs.</li>
<li>
Remote and on-site administration of Windows Infrastructure including AD, GPO, Security, Files shares, backups and Printing.</li>
<li>
Familiar VMWare and virtual desktops a plus.</li>
<li>
Responsible for successful Exchange Mail administration/migrations.</li>
<li>
Develop and write procedures for installation, use, and troubleshooting of IT assets.</li>
<li>
Recommend support solutions including hardware and software technology, site locations, and integration of technologies and systems.</li>
<li>
Conduct on-site surveys to determine present and future user needs.</li>
<li>
Direct customer interaction to understand problems, requests, and recommendations per the needs of the client.
</li>
<li>Participate in R&amp;D efforts to both enhance and develop services and products.</li>
<li>
Document troubleshooting and maintenance procedures for new services and products.</li>
<li>
Train Sales and Engineers in new services\products.
</li>
<li>Train users in use of equipment.
</li>
</ul>
<p><b>To Be Successful in this Role, the Selected Candidate will Have/Display:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Bachelor’s degree or Associate’s degree in IT related curriculum.</li>
<li>
3+ years of experience in an Engineer or Field Engineer role.</li>
<li>
A+/Net+ certifications are a plus.</li>
<li>
Ability to travel 75-100% of the time (mostly local).</li>
<li>
Positive attitude with a drive to learn and grow.</li>
<li>
Excellent verbal and non-verbal communication skills.</li>
<li>
Ownership of tasks, issues, and assignments.</li>
<li>
Strong ability to work both as an individual and in a team environment.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Required Physical Activity:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Sitting- Ability to sit for a long period of time in front of a computer screen.</li>
<li>
Lifting- Ability to lift 50lbs or more.</li>
<li>
Repetitive Motion- Ability to type, write, and pick up items.</li>
<li>
Hearing- Ability to receive detailed information through oral communication.</li>
<li>
Talking- Ability to express or exchange ideas by means of spoken words.</li>
</ul>
