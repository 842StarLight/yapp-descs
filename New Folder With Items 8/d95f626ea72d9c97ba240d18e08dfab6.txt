<h2>IT QA Analyst I</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>Summary: The Mobile Quality Assurance Analyst is primarily a technical testing role where the candidate will be working with an agile technical product team for mobile applications and websites. They will perform functional, device, regression, and user interface testing for the product for each release on iOS, Android, and web. This individual should be able to develop test plans, test reports, and perform preliminary debugging of issues identified. This individual would also provide leadership and consulting on quality assurance methodology. In addition, they will lead quality improvements to requirement, development and test procedures and be responsible for the development and implementation of quality assurance processes and tools.
<br>
<br>

<ul>
<li>Create manual and automated test cases/scripts and record bugs in mobile applications.</li>
<li>Collaborate with cross-functional teams throughout the SDLC and agile ceremonies and take ownership of the QA process. This includes daily interactions and collaboration with the team.</li>
<li>Participate in the development process from definition, design, build, test, release, and maintenance.</li>

<li>Front-end testing of mobile applications in both iOS and Android</li>
<li>Back-end testing (API layer and back-end architecture)</li>

</ul>
