<b>Overview:</b><br>
Avanade is seeking Manager to expand its advisory capabilities. You will support engagements advising clients at the highest strategic level with big-picture and tactical guidance on how technology enables a wide range of business outcomes. You must possess exception analytical, interpersonal and creative thinking capabilities. You will need to be entrepreneurial, enterprising and personable, possessing exceptional leadership qualities, that allows you to assume a critical role in the Advisory Practice with an ability to scale over time.
<br><br>
<b>Key Role Responsibilities:</b><br>
Operating with senior client executives (C-levels, VPs, Directors), you will assist our clients in successfully defining their technology strategy, investments and operating models to support their business priorities. As a successful Manager, you will:
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Support the delivery of a broad range of IT strategy and multiple technical advisory engagements</li>
<li>Identify and Leverage approaches, appropriate tools and methodologies</li>
<li>Possess deep understanding of trends and strategies for identifying solutions to meet client business, financial and technical objectives</li>
<li>Conduct detailed analyses to understand and explain client problems and utilize standard consulting management skills such as facilitation, interviewing, gap analysis, etc.</li>
<li>Identify, develop and document issues, perform analysis, draw conclusions and formulate strategic recommendations</li>
<li>Create clear and actionable strategic roadmaps and supporting business cases that both delineate a broader end-game, while also prioritizing quick wins with near-term measurable impact</li>
<li>Actively develop and maintain client relationships at senior levels</li>
<li>Enthusiastically contribute towards industry specific offerings/ thought leadership development</li>
<li>Assist senior team members with sales efforts including development of client proposals and pitch mechanics</li>
</ul>
<b>Required Skills &amp; Experience:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>6+ years of Management Consulting experience from a top management or IT professional services firm</li>
<li>Demonstrated experience interfacing with executive level clients</li>
<li>Willingness to travel 80% domestically</li>
<li>Previous experience working on multiple workstreams/engagements in a highly matrixed external client facing environment, with Talent &amp; Project &amp; Budget Management responsibilities</li>
<li>Excellent communication, presentation and facilitation skills</li>
<li>Aptitude for critical and analytical thinking as well as the capacity to accept out-of-the box solutions</li>
<li>Autonomous, positive thinker, highly flexible and willing to work independently as well as part of a multi-disciplinary team</li>
<li>Experience discussing a wide range of strategic imperatives across functional, operational and technological issues</li>
<li>Bachelor’s degree in related field, MBA preferred</li>
</ul>
<b>Preferred Experience and Skills:</b><br>
The following skills and capabilities are central to the role, depending on Service Area expertise:
<br><br>
<ul>

<li>CIO Strategy and IT Strategy</li>

<li>Digital Strategy and Transformation, Customer Experience Strategy and Experience Design</li>
