<h2>HVAC Truck Base Controls Technician</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<div>
<p><b>What you will do</b></p>
<p>
Under specific direction, performs preventive maintenance, repair, installation, and commissioning and general servicing of electronic control systems (including troubleshooting of systems). Works with assigned Controls Technician mentor to achieve high levels of customer satisfaction through direct, on site, customer interface. Properly completes required project and service documentation.</p>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<p><b>
How you will do it</b></p>
<p>
1. Works with Controls Technicians on diagnostics and repair of complex system malfunctions requiring extensive knowledge of a variety of electronic or digital controls systems. Tests system software in multiple programming languages.<br>
2. Works with mentor to develop the ability to draw sketches and control design functionality.<br>
3. Communicates with customer upon arrival and before leaving the work site. Assists the Controls Technician to ensure high levels of customer satisfaction. Meets regularly with the assigned mentor and customer to become familiar with operating problems. Keeps customer informed on the nature of service provided and outstanding issues. In conjunction with the Controls Technician, promotes the sale of add-on work.<br>
4. Adheres to all Johnson Controls and customer safety standards.<br>
5. Compiles assigned job documentation, such as preventative maintenance documentation, service tickets and punch lists.<br>
6. Participates in training classes as assigned and conducts extensive self study (reading, research and practice) to develop technical proficiency in company's product lines. Completes certifications and develops technical proficiencies as required by the company.</p>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<p>
ADDITIONAL DUTIES:<br>
Other duties related to assisting in commissioning and servicing of building controls and facilities management systems as assigned.</p>
</div>

Qualifications</b><div>

What we look for</b></p>
<div><b>
Required</b></div>
