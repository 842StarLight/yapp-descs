<p><b>
JOB DESCRIPTION</b></p>
<p></p>
<br>
<div>
<b>
Summary/Objective</b><br>
The Phone Repair Tech provides service, oversees inventory, markets available services, manages repair tickets, and handles customer service issues.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div><b>
Essential Functions</b></div>
<div></div>
<br>
<ul>
<li>
Manage, track, and order parts inventory</li>
<li>
Tag and label incoming POs—setting cost and MSRP</li>
<li>
Manage tools and supplies needed for repairs</li>
<li>
Troubleshoot/diagnose/repair phones, tablets &amp; other small wireless electronics</li>
<li>
Marketing/advertising the repair center to acquire business</li>
<li>
Manage repair tickets to ensure repairs are done in a timely manner</li>
<li>
Ensure quality customer service experience</li>
<li>
Customer communications</li>
<li>
Setup and organization of the repair center for efficiency</li>
<li>
Design the repair center in a manner that is efficient for each technician to work and a comfortable atmosphere for the customer</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Competencies
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Adaptability: Maintaining effectiveness in reaching set goals by adapting to changed circumstances, tasks, responsibilities, and people.</li>
<li>
Attention to Detail: Taking responsibility for a thorough and detailed method of working.</li>
<li>
Customer Focus: Knowing the (internal and external) customer business needs and acting accordingly; anticipating customer needs, and giving high priority to customer satisfaction and customer service.</li>
<li>
Planning and Organizing: Setting priorities and defining actions, time, and resources needed to achieve predefined goals.</li>
</ul>
