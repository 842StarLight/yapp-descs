<h2>Customer Experience Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div></div>
<div>
<div><div><div>
<div>The Customer Experience Manager’s (CEM) responsibility is to optimize the quality of a service experience delivered to PCM/Insight Services clientele. Through oversight and coordination of sales and service delivery initiatives/channels, the CEM will ensure all Services deliverables are connected and consistent ultimately leading to an elevated customer experience and long term loyalty of PCM/Insight clientele.</div>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<ul>
<li>Manages execution of the customer experience through effective planning, prioritizing, communication and follow up.</li>
<li>
Develops and maintains the ”customer journey map” for each client within their assigned portfolio.
</li>
<li>Aligns customer value moments and required communications to PCM service delivery strategy</li>
<li>
Establish relationships with primary client stakeholders as a trusted advocate and services planning and delivery advisor</li>
<li>
Facilitates internal/external communications “touchpoints” to ensure messaging strategies and toolsets are consistent and align to target service and customer business objecives.</li>
<li>
Guide the customer throughout all onboarding of PCM service efforts identifying their business requirements and mitigating implementation barriers</li>
<li>
Define the necessary analytics/toolsets to track, monitor, and evaluate service delivery efforts impacting the customer experience</li>
<li>
Utilization of performance analytics to review and measure the quality of the customers experience through all “journey stages.”</li>
<li>
Monitor the customer health status for required intervention and mediation of service delivery improvements.</li>
<li>
Implement case management protocol when required to address customer health status declared as “red” involving possible loss of revenue.</li>
<li>
Develop and maintain an annual growth strategy focusined on increasing the customers consumption of PCM services.</li>
<li>
Drive client penetration efforts by sharing of all insights with a multi-functional team consisting of sales, pre-sales, and services leadership.</li>
<li>
Work with CEM and service delivery teams to define/implement/manage customer experience best practices, based on insight into customer behaviors and propensities.</li>
<li>
Work with PCM and client teams to identify the people, process, and technology capabilities required to deliver desired customer experiences in market, and then plan for the implementation of those experiences.</li>
</ul>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Qualifications<br>
</b></h2>
<div></div>
<div><div><div>
<ul>
<li>Strong experience with <b>IT Service Management</b> <b>(ITSM)</b> and business analytics platforms.</li>
<li>
<b>
High level Service Delivery</b> experience.</li>
<li>
Minimum 8 years of working experience post-college. Minimum 4 years experience in customer or program management in a technical services environment.</li>
<li>
Demonstrated ability to identify and communicate with multiple personalities, roles, and decision-makers</li>
<li>
Excellent time management and organizational skills</li>
<li>
Excellent communication, presentation, delegation, follow-up skills</li>
<li>
Strong analytical and quantitative skills</li>
<li>
Demonstrated ability to manage complex and competing priorities</li>
<li>
Demonstrated conflict management and resolution skills</li>
<li>
Bachelors Degree</li>
</ul>
