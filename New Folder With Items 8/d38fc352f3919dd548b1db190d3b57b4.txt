<h2>Senior Account Manager, Demand Partnerships</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>Job Description<br>
<br>
<p>Smaato is looking for a Senior Account Manager who will work with and support our growing DSP business. This is a great opportunity for someone interested in a fast-paced energetic environment with immense growth potential.</p>
<p><b>
What You'll Do</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Day-to-day lead for managing top tier accounts.</li>
<li>
Work closely with Smaato’s demand partners to optimize their current spending.</li>
<li>
Maximize the potential of Smaato’s demand partners by conducting weekly calls and keeping an open line of constant communication.</li>
<li>
Exceed individual and team sales goals.</li>
<li>
Build custom reports and host Quarterly Business Reviews.</li>
<li>
Clearly communicate to Business Development, Sales Engineering, Market Quality, and Revenue Operations teams regarding revenue opportunities and execute on them in a timely fashion.</li>
<li>
Analyze revenue and performance data daily to ensure targets are on track and proactively provide solutions if targets are missed or projected to be off.</li>
<li>
Identify new approaches to solving problems as well as new processes to improve team workflows.</li>
<li>
Assist in training other Account Managers.</li>
<li>
Attend local conferences.</li>
<li>
Effectively use various sales tools including Salesforce and internal dashboards.</li>
</ul>
</div>

<p></p>

 2+ years of previous Account Management experience at a digital/web/mobile advertising/media company, with solid understanding of advertising terminologies and pricing structures (CPA, CPC, CPM, etc).</li>
<li>

<li>
Pro-active, autonomous go-getter who takes ownership and is accountable for their work.</li>

Strong time management and communication skills.</li>
<li>
Proven ability to analyze and optimize opportunities to maximize ROI.</li>
<li>
Capable of prioritizing tasks and switch gears on the fly.</li>

Comfortable dealing with clients in a professional manner, including presentations and hosting conference calls.</li>
<li>
Startup experience a PLUS.</li>

BA or BS from an accredited 4-year university.</li>
</ul>
