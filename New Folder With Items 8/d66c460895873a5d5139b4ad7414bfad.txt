<h2>Account Manager, Data and Research Products</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">Position Description
<br><br>
As Account Manager, Data and Research Products, you’ll take the lead on selling Recargo’s electric vehicle-focused data and research products to automakers, electric utilities and other industry stakeholders. This includes electric vehicle (EV) charging infrastructure data (PlugShare) and SaaS products, business intelligence tools, consumer research services and subscription research products.
<br><br>
<b>Responsibilities :</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Lead in account planning and forecasting activities for PlugShare's electric vehicle market data and research products</li>
<li>Participate in the entire deal-making process from prospecting to qualifying inbound leads through to contract negotiation and closing</li>
<li>Creatively develop and present multi-product offerings to clients that are sculpted to their specific needs</li>
<li>Achieve revenue growth by meeting quarterly sales targets</li>
<li>Provide "voice of the customer" feedback to the product development and marketing teams</li>
<li>Actively mine and expand a comprehensive database of industry decision-makers</li>
<li>Maintain a disciplined approach to lead and contact management, using Salesforce and similar CRM tools</li>
<li>Support in product pricing and contract approval for customized data deliverables, working directly with product and engineering teams</li>
<li>Help to build and manage a team of sales professionals</li>
</ul>
<b>Required Skills/Experience :</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s Degree required, advanced degree is better</li>
<li>4-7 years of experience in a Sales/Business Development role preferred</li>
<li>Great verbal and written communication, presentation, and leadership skills</li>
<li>Experience selling data-driven products and software services preferred</li>
<li>Willingness to travel: this is a personal selling position that will require frequent face-to-face meetings</li>
<li>Strong understanding of financial and contract management</li>
<li>Appreciation for the benefits of vehicle electrification</li>
</ul>
Candidates must be authorized to work in the United States as a pre-condition of employment.

About Recargo
<br><br>
Founded in 2009, Recargo’s mission is to help electric vehicle (EV) drivers find the way forward. We make the world’s most popular EV app, PlugShare: A top reviewed, driver-sourced guide to finding and using public charging globally. We are one of the EV industry’s most important sources of driver opinions and public charging infrastructure data, paving the way automakers, government, utilities and charging networks make crucial decisions about future vehicles, features, policies, and more. Recargo is a wholly-owned subsidiary of the sustainable transportation powerhouse, Innogy eMobility, and with their support, we are aggressively expanding our team and launching into innovative new product categories.</div>
