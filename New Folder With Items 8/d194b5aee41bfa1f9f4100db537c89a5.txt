<h2>Sales Coordinator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p><b>Job Summary
</b></p>
<p>The Sales Coordinator is responsible for providing day-to-day administration related to pre and post-sale activities.
</p>
<p>This role will support the General Managers and Sales team to ensure timely delivery and execution of the sales process.
</p>
<p>The Sales Coordinator must possess strong communication and organization skills and a willingness to learn and grow in a fast-paced environment.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Essential Functions
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Partner with the General Managers and Sales team on all facets of client relations including proposal completion, media plans, and customer service.
</li>
<li>Provide outstanding customer service through product knowledge, industry news and market expertise.
</li>
<li>Administer the contract process from creation to execution for direct clients or agencies including creating and processing any related forms.
</li>
<li>Facilitate daily communication between account teams, creative, and inventory managers to help fulfill client requests.
</li>
<li>Manage the internal Proof of Performance process and liaise with the Operations team to manage quality control of the photos.
</li>
<li>Act as the Telmar “specialist” and liaison between sales and research. Assist the team with issues pulling the reports and assist in training.
</li>
<li>Liaise with sales, billing and the collection teams to assist with the billing process.
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Additional Functions
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Establish a good working relationship with clients.
</li>
<li>Develop and maintain strong relationships with clients and account, finance, and operations teams; and contribute to the achievement of quality results.
</li>
<li>Think creatively while juggling several projects and ideas at once.
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Minimum Qualifications
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Must possess strong organizational skills, attention to detail and ability to prioritize.
</li>
<li>Must be able to multi-task.
</li>
<li>Proficiency with Microsoft PowerPoint, Word and Excel.
</li>
<li>Strong analytic capabilities and interest in advertising, marketing and sales.
</li>
<li>Self-motivated with 1-2 years of related work experience.
</li>
<li>Bachelor’s Degree preferred. Course of study in Marketing, Digital Marketing, Communications or similar a plus.
</li>
</ul>
