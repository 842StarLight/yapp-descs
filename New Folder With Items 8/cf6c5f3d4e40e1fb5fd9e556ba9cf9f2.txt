<b>Job Summary:</b>
</div>
<div>
<div>Supermicro is seeking a Product Manager who can lead the promotion and development of high end workstation motherboard solutions for a diverse range of vertical markets. This is a key product management role focused on delivering and promoting workstation product solutions where communication between cross-functional teams is critical to fulfill roadmap and volume expectations. In addition to developing new workstation motherboard products, Supermicro product managers are expected to collaborate with marketing and sales departments to promote products as well as represent the organization to external customers directly. As a product manager, you will use strong technical and communication skills towards product positioning within Supermicro's core product portfolio including research into vertical-based solutions and the creation of aggressive go-to market plans. Ultimately, a Product Manager will become a core member of the motherboard product management team and engage in technical product development, marketing, and pre/post-sales support activities.</div>
</div>
</div>
