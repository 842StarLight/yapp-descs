<div><i>Summary of Job:</i></div>
</div></div>
<br>
<div><div>
<div>SharedLABS is currently seeking a full-time Sales Executive.</div>
<div>As our Outbound Sales, Business Development Specialist you will be responsible for bringing in new sales for all digital and web services offered. Our fast, fun paced service-focused business model means it is imperative that you are a people person. You must possess an energetic and entrepreneurial spirit. A positive demeanor effective time management, networking skills: online and offline will all be assets that contribute to your success. If you have a proven track record in sales &amp; are a closer, we want you!</div>

<div><b>Essential Job Functions:</b></div>
</div>
<div>
<div>Reasonable accommodations may be made to enable individuals with disabilities to perform the essential functions.</div>
<div></div>
<ul>
<li>Develop new business for SharedLABS Digital &amp; Web Services Division.</li>
<li>Prospect for potential new clients and setup meeting.</li>
<li>Set up qualified meetings between client decision makers and SharedLABS sales.</li>
<li>Cold call as appropriate within your market or geographic area to ensure a robust pipeline of opportunities.</li>
<li>Meet potential clients by growing, maintaining, and leveraging your network.</li>
<li>Identify potential clients, and the decision makers within the client organization.</li>
<li>Strategically build relationships to fill your sales funnel.</li>
<li>Negotiation skills</li>
<li>Present an image that speaks to both the client and our company.</li>
