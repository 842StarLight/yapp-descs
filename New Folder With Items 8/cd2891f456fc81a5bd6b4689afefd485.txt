<h2>Account Executive</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>We are seeking a high-energy and self-motivated individual with a proven track record in media sales for our sales team. The Account Executive will be responsible for servicing existing local and agency accounts and developing new business. Duties include maintaining proper payment schedules, working with production, traffic and accounting to process sales orders. The Account Executive will also need to provide competitive market analysis, forecast sales revenue activity and be a strong presenter of qualitative research data.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Responsibilities:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Prepare proposals and avails.
</li>
<li>Prepare and Present client sales presentations.
</li>
<li>Expedite necessary paperwork such as: contracts, production orders, traffic instructions and collections of monies due to the station from clients.
</li>
<li>Responsible for selling and collecting local advertising in Bakersfield.
</li>
<li>Detail oriented, with ability to utilize qualitative and quantitative tools to facilitate selling process.
</li>
<li>Work closely in a team atmosphere with multiple departments including news, research, traffic and production from beginning to end of the selling cycle.
</li>
<li>Responsible for directing and exceeding companies monthly individual sales goals.
</li>
<li>Other duties as deemed necessary.
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Required Skills &amp; Experience:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Must be goal oriented, able to work in a fast-paced environment and meet deadlines.
</li>
<li>Strong desire to find and develop new business.
</li>
<li>Requires strong negotiation and presentation skills plus a positive team mentality.
</li>
<li>Premium attention given to client service and new business development is a must.
</li>
<li>Must be organized, highly motivated, detail oriented and have excellent communication skills.
</li>
<li>Must work well in a team environment
</li>
<li>Minimum of 1-3 years of working sales experience
</li>
<li>Must be computer literate with solid working knowledge of Word, Excel, PowerPoint and Outlook software
</li>
<li>Must have working knowledge of windows based operating systems
</li>
<li>Must be comfortable with cold calling and prospecting new business.
</li>
<li>Must have a valid driver’s license and an acceptable driving record.
</li>
</ul>
