<h2>Marketing Coordinator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<div><b>SUMMARY/OBJECTIVE</b></div>
<div></div>
<div>NOTS Logistics is seeking a Marketing Coordinator to be an integral member of our Marketing team. The Marketing Coordinator will plan, create and manage all both internal and external marketing material, social media content, and web presence; helping to ensure continuous follower growth and engagement within each community. This person will help build and manage relationships with key logistics industry trade media to generate buzz and maximize coverage for NOTS Logistics and will help promote company culture to all active and future employees. The ideal candidate is a creative social networking wizard with prior experience managing B2B brand pages and branded websites as well as a sound writer who can create and maintain all company marketing materials. To be successful in this position you must be a proactive team player, detailed oriented, organized and have a passion for brand development.</div>
<div></div>
<div><b>ESSENTIAL FUNCTIONS / DUTIES</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Drive compelling multi-platform Social Media strategy, designed to accomplish larger company objectives.</li>
<li>Day-to-day owner for all social networks; including content creation, scheduling, optimizing and strategy.</li>
<li>This role is responsible for tracking social media performance, directing continuous improvement, reach and engagement.</li>
<li>Manage the updating and/or development of both digital and print marketing materials ensuring consistent branding across all channels maintaining consistency in appearance.</li>
<li>Develop and deliver external communications including website, intranet, social media, press releases, announcements, advertising, etc.</li>
<li>Manages all editorial calendars, writes and distributes compelling press releases and proactively engages with the media to maximize brand coverage</li>
<li>Manages all ongoing media requests for images, product descriptions and samples</li>
<li>Update and expand media contact list</li>
<li>Brand liaison to all industry associations and groups</li>
<li>Assist in the Coordination of all marketing event related initiatives (parties, launches, market events, round table sessions, etc.)</li>
<li>Proof reading and editing of marketing department materials on an ongoing basis</li>
</ul>
<div><b>REQUIRED EDUCATION AND EXPERIENCE</b></div>
<ul>
<li>BA degree in Marketing, Communications, Journalism or relevant field</li>
<li>2+ years of concentrated experience with content management; proven ability to significantly drive engagement and conversion</li>
<li>1+ years of experience managing brand pages, including B2B</li>
<li>Impeccable writing skills and attention to detail</li>
<li>Excellent project management skills, must be able to multi-task and be well organized</li>
<li>Able to work in an ambiguous, 'internet-speed' environment</li>
<li>Proven ability to manage multiple, competing priorities simultaneously</li>
<li>Proven analytical and quantitative skills; ability to use hard data and metrics to back up assumptions and develop business cases</li>
<li>Some experience using graphic design software such as InDesign, Photoshop or Illustrator to edit, resize and crop images</li>
<li>Creative thinker who can be both innovative while execution oriented</li>
</ul>
<div><b>POSITION TYPE / HOURS OF WORK</b></div>
<div>This is a full-time position, 40 hours per week. Schedule is Monday- Friday, 8:00 a.m. to 4:30 p.m. This position regularly requires long hours.</div>
