<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>About the job</b></h2>
<p>
The Business Strategy &amp; Operations organization provides business critical insights using analytics, ensures cross functional alignment of goals and execution, and helps teams drive strategic partnerships and new initiatives forward. We stay focused on aligning the highest-level company priorities with effective day-to-day operations, and help evolve early stage ideas into future-growth initiatives.</p>
<p>The Product and Solutions Activation (PSA) team is part of the Google Customer Solutions (GCS) organization and is responsible for creating performance and brand solutions for small- and medium-sized businesses (SMBs). We are the product and business experts behind GCS Googlers and advertisers. We’re responsible for driving sustainable business growth and product adoption across the GCS channel for our product areas.<br>
<br>
Google Customer Solutions Product and Solutions Activation’s role is to bring product to market and market to product. We’re responsible for driving the commercialization strategy for Google’s business and shaping the product roadmap with the specific requirements of our diverse group of SMB advertisers. Our mission is to grow global product by shaping product direction, launching new products to market, empowering our Sales teams with impactful resources and capturing and channeling customer feedback to product. We work closely with Product, the Global Product teams and the GCS commercial organizations, providing exposure and insight to these teams.<br>
<br>
In this role, you will be responsible for working on complex, cross-functional initiatives that drive strategic product and go-to-market decisions to grow the GCS business. You are independent and can work in a rapidly changing industry, work through ambiguity and demonstrate problem-solving leadership with limited oversight.<br>
</p>
<p>
When our millions of advertisers and publishers are happy, so are we! Our Google Customer Solutions (GCS) team of entrepreneurial, enthusiastic and client-focused members are the "human face" of Google, helping entrepreneurs both individually and broadly build their online presence and grow their businesses. We are dedicated to growing the unique needs of advertising companies. Our teams of strategists, analysts, advisers and support specialists collaborate closely to spot and analyze customer needs and trends. In collaboration, we create and implement business plans broadly for all types of businesses.</p>

Responsibilities</b></h2>
