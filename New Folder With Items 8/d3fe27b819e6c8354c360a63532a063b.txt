<h2>Senior Business Systems Analyst</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>
<p><b>Primary Purpose:</b></p>
<p>Works as part of the product management team and engages business units, IT, internal departments and external clients to analyze, understand and document business requirements and implement solutions. Continuously strives to improve efficiency, reduce costs, ensure compliance with regulations, and work cooperatively and jointly to provide clients with quality service.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Essential Duties and Responsibilities:</b></p>
<p>Following is a summary of the essential functions for this job. Other duties may be performed, both major and minor, which are not mentioned below. Specific activities may change from time to time.</p>
<ul>
<li>Ensure business requirements are researched, developed and implemented in line with Business Analysis best practices and methodology</li>
<li>Elicit, understand, and document complex business processes, systems, and related business rules for software applications</li>
<li>Decompose high-level concepts and information into detailed, workable deliverables</li>
<li>Develop user test plans and support their execution to ensure that requirements and quality objectives are satisfied for product owners</li>
<li>Provide clarification concerning business requirements throughout project execution</li>
<li>Act as a liaison between Program Managers, Project Managers, Product Owners, Marketing, Support and IT teams as appropriate</li>
<li>Facilitate inter/intra team communication and coordination</li>
<li>Expand understanding of business domain, products, and processes</li>
<li>Report time and track tasks</li>
<li>Performs other business tasks or functions as assigned<br>
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Minimum Skills and Competencies:</b></p>
<p>The requirements listed below are representative of the knowledge, skill and/or ability required. Reasonable accommodations may be made to enable individuals with disabilities to perform the essential functions.</p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s Degree or in-lieu of degree equivalent education, training and work-related experience</li>
<li>7+ years in a Business Analyst role</li>
<li>Strong experience and working knowledge of best practices for Business Analysis</li>
<li>3+ years working in an Agile process</li>
<li>Write clear and concise requirement artifacts</li>
<li>Create, clarify, and manage user stories</li>
<li>Translate technology terms into language clients can understand</li>
<li>Demonstrate expert level of facilitation skills in medium to large group settings and with senior level management</li>
<li>Work effectively and interact with all levels of the organization including executive leadership</li>
<li>Adept in analysis and problem-solving</li>
<li>Can deal with ambiguity and conflicting goals</li>
<li>Ability to conceptualize, apply, analyze, synthesize, and evaluate information to determine solutions</li>
<li>Question assumptions to ascertain their validity and evaluate appropriate solutions</li>
<li>Exhibit the ability to be flexible and work in a rapidly and constantly changing environment</li>
<li>Manage multiple priorities and assist other in prioritization</li>
<li>Show systematic and orderly planning skills with a high attention to detail</li>
<li>Proficient in Microsoft Office (Word, Excel, Outlook, PowerPoint)</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Desired Skills:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Health Care industry experience</li>
<li>Insurance Sales experience</li>
<li>Familiar with SaaS delivery</li>
<li>Experience using Team Foundation Server (TFS) or similar tools for SDLC tracking</li>
<li>Certification(s) in Business Analysis, Systems Analysis, or Business Requirements gathering</li>
<br>
</ul>
