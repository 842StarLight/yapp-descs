<h2>Pre Press Technician</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Scalable Press is on an incredible growth trajectory and we're looking for a Pre-Press Technician to join our operations team. If you are eager and love working with artwork, we encouraged you to apply! This position is based out of Indianapolis, Indiana, and reports to our Director of Quality and Compliance.</p>
<p>What you'll do:</p>
<ul>
<li>Review all-digital production artwork within specified production queues to defined standards</li>
<li>Utilize experience and knowledge of Adobe Photoshop, Adobe Illustrator to rectify issues identified with digital production artwork</li>
<li>Utilize experience and knowledge of Adobe Photoshop, Adobe Illustrator to recreate production digital artwork for high-value sales opportunities</li>
<li>Utilize experience and knowledge of Adobe Photoshop, Adobe Illustrator to develop digital separations for direct use in a production environment</li>
<li>Utilize experience and knowledge of digital techniques to determine mesh selection, color sequencing, pass speed, and additional digital press functions</li>
<li>Utilize production equipment to create pre-production samples for sales/customer approval</li>
<li>Maintain tracking of digital pre-production and production artwork files</li>
</ul>
<p>Additional skills needed:</p>
<ul>
<li>Must have demonstrable graphic art/screenprint artwork separations experience with Adobe Photoshop, Adobe Illustrator, and Adobe Acrobat software</li>
<li>Experience with Microsoft Office software</li>
<li>Ability to learn/adapt to specifications</li>
<li>Ability to understand and adhere to standard operating procedures (SOPs)</li>
<li>Detail-oriented</li>
</ul>
