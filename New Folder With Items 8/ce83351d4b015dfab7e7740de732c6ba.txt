 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">

<div><div>
<div><div>Gardner Denver Nash, a global leader in the manufacture of Liquid Ring Vacuum Pumps and Centrifugal Blowers, is looking for a motivated rotating equipment <b>Authorized Service Center (ASC) Coordinator</b> to facilitate workflow, technical direction and general day-to-day needs in support of our ASC partner businesses throughout North America. This position is based in Bentleyville, PA or can be remote.<br>
<div><div>
<div><div>
<b>Essential Functions:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Build sustainable trust through open and interactive communication with ASCs.</li>
<li>Facilitate the timely resolution of ASC-related product and service issues by clarifying the ASCâ€™s area of concern, determining root cause of the problem, selecting and explaining the best solution to solve the problem, expediting the corrective action and diligently following-up to ensure resolution and that the highest standards of customer satisfaction are maintained.
</li>
<li>Daily follow-up of outstanding open issues, maintenance for quotations and other key metrics as required.
</li>
<li>Communicate and coordinate with colleagues as necessary at all levels of the organization, from field personnel to senior management.</li>
<li>Provide accurate and complete information by using the right methods/tools for quoting, ordering, processing forms, applications and other requests made by the ASC.</li>
<li>Answer questions about warranties or terms of sale.</li>
<li>Work with the OE &amp; AM teams to plan &amp; schedule ASC Technicians visits to customer sites for commissioning, technical and warranty services, as needed.</li>
<li>Parts planning and confirmation for ASC partners.</li>
<li>Perform quoting, planning, scheduling, reporting and billing for field services.</li>
<li>Manage incoming call from ASCs.</li>
<li>Work with Customer, ASEs, PMs and Service managers to ensure proper customer service is being delivered at all times.</li>
<li>Manages, maintains and plans training, as required, for ASC partners.</li>
<li>Utilize computer technology to handle high call volumes from ASCs.</li>
<li>Periodically reviews and offers improvement suggestions on sales and support materials</li>
<li>Travel is not expected but some training, other company site visits could involve limited travel</li>
<li>Other responsibilities as assigned or required by manager</li>
