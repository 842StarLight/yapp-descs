<h2>Manager of Customer Support</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>About the role</b><br>
This is an exciting opportunity for a strong enterprise-software Manager of Customer Support, with proven technical software and client liaison experience, to be part of a growing Customer Experience team at PetDesk. This experienced professional will manage a team of 6 Technical Support Representatives in the San Diego office, who provide post-sales technical assistance to our customers in North America. Experience with Jira, ZenDesk, and FreshDesk is a must!</p>
<p><b>Apply if you're excited to:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>As well as the day-to-day management and development of the team, the Manager of Customer Support will be responsible for the overall satisfaction of customers, overseeing escalations, and representing the needs of customers back to the Product/Engineering organizations.</li>
<li>
Contribute to PetDesk's initiatives which include establishing and implementing tools and metrics which drive and deliver a proactive, customer-focused experience. In addition, you may also be involved in directly providing technical support services to customers, as appropriate to your technical skills.</li>
<li>
Drive customer satisfaction and advocacy through data and leading by example.</li>
<li>
Prioritize, escalate, and manage customer critical issues.</li>
<li>
Establish and maintain proactive communication with key customers.</li>
<li>
Build strong relationships with Engineering and Product Management teams to ensure customer needs are well represented.</li>
<li>
Implement the technology, champion the process, and scale the team to support both businesses and consumers.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>About you</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Minimum 2-3 years of demonstrable success managing a multi-faceted Technical Success team at a technology-related company.</li>
<li>
Experience in a highly technical support environment.</li>
<li>
Expert working knowledge with Jira, ZenDesk, and FreshDesk.</li>
<li>
Demonstrable team leadership experience, and the ability to monitor trends and use data to drive decision-making and team strategy.</li>
<li>
Thrive in a fast-paced, high-growth environment, with a strong drive, sense of accountability, and attention to detail.<br>
</li>
</ul>

<p><b>About us</b><br>
PetDesk entered the pet care market in 2013 with one idea in mind—to extend pets' lives by bringing value to pet care providers and pet parents. This principle has guided us through various obstacles along the way. Today, we are a key player in the industry with over 1,800 customers and 1 Million app users. Our team of over 70 people is committed to the success of our customers and to improving the quality of life for all pet companions.</p>
<p>Our mission is to improve the quality of life for pets. We do that by providing pet care businesses with top notch customer communication software and pet parents with engaging native mobile experiences.</p>
