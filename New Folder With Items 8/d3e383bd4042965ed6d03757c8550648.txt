<h2>Customer Success Consultant</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Overview
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<p>Ensure highest customer satisfaction and return on investment while helping our partners LOVE us.</p>
<p>
Proactively manage and prioritize needs of assigned book of business.</p>
<div>
Achieve best in class status for Zywave and all products, service and customer experience.</div>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>What You Will Do
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<p><b>ESSENTIAL FUNCTIONS:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Demonstrate deep knowledge of all products through online consulting sessions.</li>
<li>
Possess intermediate project management skills to confirm understanding of project goals, scope, resources, work plan and deliverables with customers and internal teams.
</li>
<li>Answer basic corporate and navigation questions on behalf of the customer.
</li>
<li>Recommend free &amp; fee-based resources to provide scalable solutions for partner needs including: self-service videos, instructor-led webinars, and Take Action series.</li>
<li>
Execute customer strategies including: drip marketing campaigns, partner and client demos and running Mods.
</li>
<li>Fulfill customer connection goals to contribute to Zywave’s overall retention goal.</li>
<li>
Maintain accurate time tracking and record keeping of events using Salesforce.com</li>
<li>
Engage with customer to analyze current process and product utilization, creating Action Plans that result in increased product utilization.
</li>
<li>Deliver annual business reviews.</li>
<li>
Demonstrate customer utilization expertise, focused on the ability to build and deliver reports from the Partner Scorecard.</li>
<li>
Exhibit the ability to manage overall book and prioritize partners accordingly.</li>
<li>
Execute proactive campaigns to increase utilization, announce new software products or version releases.</li>
<li>
Interact with Account Executives and other departments to deliver a consistent service experience.</li>
<li>
Propose and execute new ideas to improve the customer’s experience for assigned book or business.</li>
<li>
Suggest process recommendations to improve the Customer Success team’s operations.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
ADDITIONAL RESPONSIBILITIES:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Identify and share lessons learned and apply to future customer scenarios.
</li>
<li>Keep current on insurance trends and legislative developments that impact our customers.
</li>
<li>Proves consistent performance with partners through call shadowing program involving management feedback.</li>
<li>
Willingness to assist internal teams with problem resolution.</li>
<li>
Adaptable to change.
</li>
<li>Perform other tasks on project as needed/assigned by management to make Zywave the best in our business.</li>
</ul>
</div></div></div>

</b></h2>
<div></div>

Comfortable working on high-profile assignments as well as behind-the-scenes administrative projects. Ability to demonstrate value of business applications to customers.</li>
<li>
Strong verbal and written communication skills. Demonstrated ability in persuading and influencing others. Facilitation and presentation experience.</li>

Demonstrated computer systems knowledge, CRM, employee self-service and a general understanding of larger business system applications. Working knowledge and understanding of Microsoft Word, PowerPoint, Excel, etc.</li>
