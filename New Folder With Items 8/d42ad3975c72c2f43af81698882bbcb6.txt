<p><b>ER&amp;I – Oil, Gas and Chemical - SAP PM/EAM – Senior Consultant<br>
</b></p>
<p></p>
<p>Are you an SAP professional experienced with clients in Oil, Gas &amp; Chemicals sector? Our practice is undergoing heavy growth and high demand for SAP talent, while giving our talent the chance to work with world-class organizations solving key business issues in a team environment.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<div>
</div>
<p><b>
Work you’ll do</b></p>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<ul>
<li>Deliver OG&amp;C (Oil, Gas, &amp; Chemicals) SAP projects</li>
<li>Lead and support SAP pursuits and OG&amp;C practice development initiatives</li>
<li>Promote and participate in forums for sharing SAP and OG&amp;C expertise, strengthening Deloitte’s collective knowledge and helping resolve our clients’ challenges</li>
<li>Collaborate on innovative solutions to support the future of SAP and OG&amp;C</li>
<li>Develop your knowledge of and gain project experience in SAP’s new S/4HANA and future solutions</li>
</ul>
<div>
</div>
<p><b>Required Qualifications</b></p>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<ul>
<li>Functional/technical proficiency with at least 2+ full lifecycle SAP implementations and 4+ years of industry and/or consulting experience</li>
<li>At least 4 years of SAP experience implementing <b>SAP PM and EAM with S4 HANA</b>
</li>
<li>Experience defining systems strategy, developing systems requirements, designing and prototyping, testing, training, defining support procedures and implementing practical business solutions under multiple deadlines</li>
<li>Adept at implementing technology-enabled business solutions for clients as part of a high-talent team and as a team lead or Project Manager on at least 1+ full cycle implementation</li>
<li>Able to collaborate with clients and have a strong desire to excel and be committed to gaining exposure to multiple industries while further developing your career</li>
<li>Strong configuration and design skills</li>
<li>Ability to travel 80-100% is REQUIRED</li>
<li>Bachelor’s degree or equivalent required</li>
<li>No sponsorship restrictions</li>
</ul>
