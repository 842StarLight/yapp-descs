<h2>Production Planner</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p>The Production Planner’s primary responsibility is demand planning – to ensure all products required by internal and external customers are manufactured, shipped, and delivered on-time.</p>
<br>
<p>
</p>
<br>
<br>
<p></p>
<b>RESPONSIBILITIES</b><ul>
<li>
DEMAND PLANNING</li>
<li>
Develop demand forecasts. Use and maintain the demand planning software as the primary tool. Plan, schedule, release, and control production orders based on MRP requirements.</li>
<li>
Review sales orders/trends and collaborate with sales, marketing, production, and other groups to understand demand forecast drivers. Ensure information used for demand forecasts is current and accurate.
</li>
<li>Evaluate forecast results. Coordinate cross-functionally to reconcile variances, resolve potential schedule interruptions, and refine forecast.
</li>
<li>INVENTORY
</li>
<li>Provide input into development of inventory strategies on current, new, and to-be-obsoleted items.</li>
<li>
Manage raw material, WIP, and finished goods inventory levels to align with business objectives and requirements. Develop and maintain safety stock strategy.</li>
<li>
Investigate inventory discrepancies, identify root cause, and initiate corrective actions to prevent future variances.</li>
<li>
Support cycle counting and inventory control issues.</li>
<li>
PURCHASING</li>
<li>
Purchase standard and non-standard items and services to support production and customer needs.</li>
<li>
Monitor supplier performance. Follow up on late orders, and expedite as necessary. Identify causes of supplier delays and propose corrective actions to resolve.
</li>
<li>Maintain supplier material certification database.<br>
</li>
</ul>
<br>
<br>

Self-directed, reliable, strong initiative, and bias for action to conduct the necessary investigation, collaboration, and educational activities to ensure responsibilities are met.</li>
