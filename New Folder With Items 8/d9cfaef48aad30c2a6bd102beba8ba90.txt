<div><b>Job Description:</b></div>
<div>
<ul>
<li>Currently seeking a MEAN Stack developer with expertise in design/development of new high performance web applications.</li>
<li>This position will support a confidential project for the dealerships which will allow dealers to utilize data that has never been available to them in the past to create a Omni-Channel marketing solutions to drive vehicle sales.</li>
<li>Currently, dealers are able to purchase several different product or services ( Email, Direct Mail etc.) without knowing how their customers want to be marketed to.</li>
<li>This new “Omni-Channel Solution” will allow the dealers to pick specific multi-channel, all encompassing solutions based on data which will give them better return on their investment.</li>
<li>They are currently creating a portal for the dealers to access that will be piloting to several markets.</li>
</ul>
