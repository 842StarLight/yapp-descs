<h2>Marketing Coordinator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>We are seeking a Marketing Coordinator to play a critical role in brand and agent marketing. This position reports up through the Regional Agent Marketing Manager.</p>
<p><b>
Responsibilities include, but are not limited to:</b></p>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>
Working proactively and collaboratively to guide agents to effectively incorporate the Corcoran brand into their personal marketing efforts</li>
<li>
Consulting with agents on business development strategies, creation of marketing plans, sales presentations, media plans, ads and social media tactics</li>
<li>
Supporting Marketing Managers in the field on agent projects and materials which may include copywriting/editing and design</li>
<li>
Staying abreast of current trends and tools (internal and external) to serve as a constant source of support and intelligence for agents</li>
<li>
Support the larger Corcoran Marketing team on company initiatives which involves product development, testing and rollout</li>
</ul>
<p>
 <b>Qualifications:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Bachelor Degree required</li>
<li>
2+ years Marketing/Advertising experience, specifically in a sales environment</li>
<li>
A balance of strategic planning and hands-on execution (with an all-hands on deck mentality)</li>
<li>
Customer service oriented by nature. Accustomed to managing multiple clients simultaneously. Must be able to handle difficult or challenging situations with diplomacy</li>
<li>
Positive can-do attitude and a willingness to take initiative on new projects and a priority to support the team and jump in wherever needed</li>
<li>
Excellent organization, verbal and written communication skills</li>
<li>
Adept at juggling multiple priorities and deadlines without sacrificing quality</li>
<li>
Demonstrated ability to thrive in a self-motivated and fast-paced environment</li>
<li>
Proficiency in Adobe Creative Suite (specifically InDesign, Illustrator Photoshop)</li>
<li>
Strong Microsoft Office Suite (Word, Excel, Powerpoint)</li>
<li>
Real estate experience a plus</li>
</ul>
</div></div>
