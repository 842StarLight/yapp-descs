<p><b>Description:</b><br>
In this role you will be responsible for day-to-day operations of general diagnostics,<br>
repair, and resolution of hardware and software related problems for PC Laptop,<br>
Desktop, and Server components.
<br>
<br>
A successful candidate will lead by example and demonstrate a passion for<br>
technology, quickly grasp and retain new concepts and ideas, support field sales,<br>
work together with and assist other technicians.<br>
<br>
Such a person should possess: - Excellent organizational and problem solving skills -<br>
A strong sense of standards and standardization - Attention to detail - Excellent<br>
documentation and research skills - Good interpersonal skills and ability to<br>
communicate and work with others – Juggle multiple tasks.<br>
<br>
<b>Minimum Certifications:
</b><br>
Certifications are not required however; basic electrical, basic electronics, A+, MCP,<br>
and additional vendor and industry certifications are a plus for this position.<br>
Additional certifications that may also be helpful include: MCSE, MCSA, MCSD.<br>
<br>
<b>Minimum related experience:</b> 2+ Years
<br>
Candidate will have experience with hardware analysis, detection, diagnosis, and<br>
repair. Additional experience and knowledge of operating Systems include but is not<br>
limited to Microsoft® Windows® 98, ME, 2000, XP, 2003, 2003SBS, Linux, and<br>
Apple based systems as well as support to major applications and databases that<br>
may include Access, MSDE, SQL, Oracle, DB2, and Microsoft® Office suites.<br>
<br>
Additional skills should include; Networking Essentials, Cable and Wiring, Antivirus /<br>
Spyware detection and removal, peer-to-peer workgroup networking, remote<br>
desktop support, Printer sharing, IP addressing, Router configuration, Security, and<br>
wireless concepts.<br>
<br>
Additional skills may also include experience with any of the following areas; IIS,<br>
ISA, Exchange, Active Directory, Group Policies, Small Business Server, Live<br>
Communications Server, Point of Sale Systems, Document Imaging &amp; Scanning,<br>
Multi-Line Fax Servers, SAN, TAPE, backup and disaster recovery concepts /<br>
practices, alternative storage devices, handheld devices, printers and peripherals.<br>
