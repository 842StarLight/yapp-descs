<h2>Marketing Coordinator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Marketing Coordinator for an up and coming global cyber security company with a cutting-edge SaaS security software. This position will report to the Marketing Manager and will be responsible for managing a broad range of activities central to the execution of AppGuard’s marketing plan.</p>
<p><b>
Day-to-Day Responsibilities Include:</b></p>
<ul>
<li> Support the development and execution of marketing plan</li>
<li> Assist with managing company website – add content, monitor performance with google analytics, suggest enhancement</li>
<li> Manage marketing calendar and execute email campaigns</li>
<li> Assist with creation and execution of press releases and public relations campaigns</li>
<li> Develop and implement effective strategies to maximize trade show participation including preshow planning and follow up activities</li>
<li>Assist with the planning, logistics, coordination for all of AppGuard’s events, webinars, and industry-related engagements</li>
<li>Create, proofread and edit copy for various marketing channels</li>
<li> Coordinate deliveries and shipments of marketing supplies to various office locations</li>
<li> Oversee ordering, maintenance and inventory levels of the marketing promotional and print materials</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Requirements</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s Degree</li>
<li>1-3 years marketing experience</li>
<li>Experience with the following or similar marketing and sales channels/tools: Marketo, HubSpot, SalesForce, Google Adwords, WordPress</li>
<li>Previous marketing experience in cybersecurity or SaaS products a plus.</li>
</ul>
</div></div>
