<p><b>Strategy Analyst, Cisco Managed Services</b></p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
The Business Entity</b></p>
<p>
At Cisco Managed Services (CMS), we enable the digital transformation of Cisco's most strategic customers by accelerating the adoption and maximizing the promise of Cisco technologies through a premium managed experience, while driving recurring revenue and profitable growth for Cisco. Many companies are working through key technology challenges of our time, including the increasing complexity, accelerating innovation, and talent shortages. CMS’s premium offerings enable customers to leverage the innovation of Cisco and refocus on their business challenges.</p>
<p></p>
<p><b><br>
The Team</b></p>
<p>
The CMS Strategy Team works with the CMS senior leadership to devise CMS’s long-term plans to deliver enhanced innovation, speed, and value to our customer in the new and accelerating era of technology disruption. We touch all parts of CMS’s business, from go-to-market to operations to delivery, to structure, analyze, and plan for the next phase of CMS’s business. We also support any ad hoc strategic work requested by the SLT, including market assessments, due diligence, and project management. We are a small, nimble team with a broad scope.</p>
<p></p>
<p><b><br>
Who You Are</b></p>
<p>
We are looking for someone that wants to shape the future of the managed services industry. You are willing to dive in, do the research and analyses, and then challenge the status quo with new ideas about for an innovative future. You are ready to use your background in business and ideas about technology to help us chart a path for the future of CMS.</p>
<p></p>

</li>

<li>

</ul>
<ul><li>Program Management of leadership initiatives:</li></ul>
