<h2>Telephone Products & Systems Outside Salesperson</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<ul>
<li><b>Find and contact potential customers</b></li>
<li><b>Sell SIP Trunking, Hosted Phone Systems, and other IP related products and systems through a consulting selling approach that includes the following steps: </b></li>
<li><b>Evaluate clients needs and determine how our product and systems will meet those needs.</b></li>
<li><b>Confirm buying points with the customer.</b></li>
<li><b>Arrange demonstrations on how system will perform for client </b></li>
<li><b>Complete the sale by receiving a signed contract and deposit check or an approved purchase order.</b></li>
<li><b>Implement the installation of the equipment sold according to “Installation Worksheet.”</b></li>
<li><b>Assist existing customers with upgrades and /or replacement of equipment that they already have in house.</b></li>
<li><b>Train on various product installations to improve sales</b></li>
<li><b>Conduct at least two customer seminars annually to promote new products and services. </b></li>
<li><b>Prepare product/service sales documentation and distribute to appropriate departments. </b></li>
</ul>
