<h2>Area Sales Director, Enterprise Accounts</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div><b>SUMMARY</b></div>
<div>The Area Sales Director (“ASD”) is responsible for maximizing revenue through the direction of field sales and inside sales activities. The ASD is responsible for the relationship and performance of Channel Partners in coordination with the overall ANSYS Channel Partner program. The ASD provides direction and guidance directly to regional sales managers/directors, sales reps and other sales professionals. The ASD develops plans and strategies to promote the success of the field sales and inside sales functions. Maintains effective communications with executives and managers across the company to ensure proper sensitivity to the needs of the field sales force. The ASD is committed to growing the business in accordance with company goals and objectives.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div><b>RESPONSIBILITIES</b></div>
<ul><li>Oversees an assigned geographic area or product/service line to maximize sales revenues and meet corporate objectives</li></ul>
<ul><li>Accurately forecasts annual, quarterly, and monthly revenue streams</li></ul>
<ul><li>Develops specific plans to ensure revenue growth in all company's products/services</li></ul>
<ul><li>Provides assessments of field sales, inside sales, and sales support staff’s productivity</li></ul>
<ul><li>Coordinates appropriate company resources to ensure efficient and consistent sales</li></ul>
<ul><li>Conducts long-range studies of changes in sales and strategies</li></ul>
<ul><li>Directs and develops managers/supervisors in the field sales, inside sales, and sales support area</li></ul>
<ul><li>Hires, coaches, reviews, rewards, motivates, disciplines, and terminates managers/supervisors/field sales representatives</li></ul>
<ul><li>Evaluates effectiveness of managers/supervisors/field sales representatives; makes necessary changes</li></ul>
<ul><li>Educates field sales and inside sales area in terms of sales techniques and tactics</li></ul>
<ul><li>Establishes and manages effective programs and seminars to address the following issues:</li></ul>
<ul><li>New account sales and field sales growth</li></ul>
<ul><li>Enhancement of field sales and inside sales revenues</li></ul>
<ul><li>Sales of emerging products/services</li></ul>
<ul><li>Pro-active competitive strategies and targeted sales campaigns</li></ul>
<ul><li>Proper use and level of sales support</li></ul>
<ul><li>Management of sales department expenses</li></ul>
<ul><li>Business/financial issues on contracts</li></ul>
<div></div>
<br>
<div><b>MINIMUM QUALIFICATIONS</b></div>
<ul><li>Bachelor's degree in business, sales, or marketing
</li></ul>
<ul><li>A minimum of 10 years of sales experience with four years of sales management experience</li></ul>
<ul><li>Highest level of knowledge of company's products/services, pricing practices, and selling skills</li></ul>
<ul><li>Effective management skills with ability to manage a major portion of the company's field sales operations</li></ul>
<ul><li>Excellent time management, communication, decision-making, human relations, presentation, and organization skills</li></ul>
<ul><li>Excellent PC knowledge and various software applications</li></ul>
<ul><li>Extensive Travel required</li></ul>
