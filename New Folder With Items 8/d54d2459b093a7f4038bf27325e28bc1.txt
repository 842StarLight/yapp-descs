<p><b>What you’ll be doing...</b>
</p>

<p>

As a division of Verizon, we combine the fun and excitement of a start-up environment with the resources, operational excellence, and brand recognition of an established tech giant.</p>
<p>
Position Summary:</p>
<p>
The Sales Executive for Enterprise accounts will be responsible to successfully expand/upsell software adoption with customers that utilize some existing Verizon Connect services both by adding services and solutions to existing user groups and by expanding solutions adopted to additional business units within a customer business hierarchy.</p>
<p>
Responsibilities:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Sell to large and complex customers.</li>
<li>
Customer complexity requires custom integration, professional services, AP, etc.</li>
<li>
Drives revenue from new accounts.</li>
<li>
Leads entire sales process from organic generation through deal negotiation and closing.</li>
<li>
Negotiates pricing and contractual agreement.</li>
<li>
Implements creative sales strategies, performing extensive customer needs analyses, surveys, proposals, presentations and product demonstrations.</li>
<li>
Accountable for sales process including account plans, Salesforce.com discipline, consistent and professional communication.</li>
</ul>
