<h2>Service Manager (Camera Store)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Metro Denver's premier camera store, Englewood Camera, is looking to hire a Camera Service Manager.</p>
<p>This is predominately a back-office position that requires a high degree of attention to detail, mechanical aptitude, organization, and follow through to meet equipment service deadlines. Camera repair experience is not necessary.</p>
<p>The service responsibilities include: interacting with customers to determine the service and repair requirements of their equipment. Sending repairs to the appropriate facilities, acquiring approvals, cleaning the exterior of camera equipment, the mirror-box, and image sensors, calibrating lenses to cameras utilizing docks or in-camera software. The ability to problem solve older equipment for repair.</p>
<p>Administrative tasks include: working with spreadsheets to maintain organization, contacting customers and repair facilities through email or phone, and interacting with customers and staff members to best solve repair issues.</p>
<p>This position also involves occasional help on the sales floor.</p>
<p>Previous camera repair experience is not necessary, but interest in photography and knowledge of camera gear is helpful. Experience with analog film processes, digital image processing, lighting, studio, and video will also be strongly considered in hiring for this position.</p>
