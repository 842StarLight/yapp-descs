<h2>Leasing Professional (Pacific Pointe)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>**Experience with OneSite strongly preferred**</b></p>
<p>Sunrise Management, a growing Property Management Company, is seeking a strategic, passionate, forward thinking team player with a rock star personality to join our team and make a strong impact on the ongoing growth of our company.</p>
<p>The Leasing Professional has the responsibility, under the supervision of Property Manager for demonstrating apartments and the community to prospective renters and securing commitments and deposits from prospective residents. They will also be responsible for providing customer service support to residents and prospective residents at the property site, including answering telephones, greeting customers, performing move-in procedures, processing rental applications and maintenance service requests, and other duties as assigned.</p>
<p><i><b>This is a Full Time position, offering an hourly wage of $16.00 per hour. Work schedule and hours are to be determined. As a full-time employee you will also receive Benefits (medical, dental and vision) + 401k + 2 week vacation pay + holiday pay.</b></i></p>
<p>Typical Schedule is as followed - subject to change</p>
<p>Days Off: Sunday/Monday</p>
<p><b>Hours: Thur/Fri/Sat 8:00am-4:30pm; Tu/Wed 9:30am – 6:00p</b></p>
<p>** 2 years experience in Leasing or Sales is required**</p>
<p><b>**</b> All inclusive retirement living and experience working with seniors is preferred**</p>
<p><b>EXPERIENCE AND/OR SPECIAL SKILLS DESIRED</b><br>* Proficient at Microsoft Office, including Word and Excel<br>* Experience with OneSite strongly preferred</p>
<p>* 2 years Previous Experience in Sales</p>
<p><b>Essential Duties and Responsibilities</b></p>
<p>Include the following. Other duties may be assigned.</p>
<p>* Market apartments by promoting rentals on the phone and in person; demonstrate apartments and community to prospective renters, sell apartment features/benefits, overcome objections, secure commitments and deposits from prospective residents and follow up on undecided prospects.<br>* Lease administration under the supervision of the Property Manager.<br>* Review the property management data base of assigned rentals to insure accuracy of data input.<br>* Transport and set up marketing collateral needed such as signs, flags, banners and such to and from rental.<br>* Prepare rental before showings (lightly clean and make ready for show).<br>* Review advertising of rentals for accuracy and make recommendations as needed.<br>* Inspect community while on-site noting any deficiencies.<br>* Assist the Resident Manager in ensuring that residents are provided with a clean, safe, well maintained community.<br>* Assist in preparation of market surveys when assigned.<br>* Accept maintenance requests as professionally, efficiently and effectively as possible when needed.<br>* Assist in performing move-in and move-out administration with particular attention to consistent apartment inspections.<br>* Represent Sunrise Management in a professional manner. Maintain a positive businesslike attitude in person and on the telephone and neat, clean, professional dress and grooming appropriate for greeting the public.<br>* Adhere to Fair Housing Policy, ADA, Injury and Illness Prevention Policy and Asbestos and Hazardous Materials Policy.<br>* Assist in promoting Sunrise Management and securing new business.</p>
<p><b>Other Duties and Responsibilities</b></p>
<p>Include the following. Other duties may be assigned.</p>
<p>* Effectively provide excellent resident services through day-to-day interaction and correspondence with residents.<br>* Serve as first point of contact for residents and potential residents; greet all customers by telephone or in person in a friendly, timely and professional manner.<br>* Provide complete and accurate rental information to prospective residents.<br>* Accurately process rental applications in a timely manner.<br>* Prepare and executed leases and move in paperwork.<br>* Assists with other assigned projects as needed.</p>
<p><b>Supervisory Responsibilities</b>:</p>
<p>This job has no supervisory responsibilities.</p>
<p>Specializing in the management of residential real estate, Sunrise Management AMO®, oversees a portfolio of 215 properties and over 14,000 units throughout the West, primarily focusing on conventional housing, senior living, affordable housing and acquisitions.</p>

Through exceptional service, accountability and innovation, we transform our client's investment goals into realities.</i></p>
