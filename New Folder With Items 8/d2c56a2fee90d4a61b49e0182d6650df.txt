<h2>Senior Client Development Director</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div></div>
<div>
<div>
<div>Do you want to work in a fun, smart, and energetic environment where you will be challenged, rewarded AND inspired? Come work with top performers at CJ Affiliate to make a real difference in how top brands run their businesses. We are the world’s leading affiliate marketing network - we transform digital marketing with the help of big data and business intelligence. We help advertisers drive more sales and expand their reach … and we want to hear from you!</div>
<br>
<div>
</div>
<div>Client Development Directors are responsible for ensuring client teams meet advertiser objectives by attending advertiser planning and review meetings, building trusted adviser relationships with advertisers and internal staff, and elevating affiliate marketing strategies. Account Directors play a key role in the hiring, developing, and retention of successful account teams.</div>
<br>
<div>
</div>
<div><b>Do these things interest you? You will:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Lead and develop a high performance team of 5-10 account management professionals in a fast paced, detail oriented and team environment.</li>
<li>Execute strategic initiatives to enhance the competitiveness of CJ’s managed services offering and health of advertiser relationships.</li>
<li>Develop and manage financial plans, and meet financial expectations for your portfolio of business.</li>
<li>Ensure client teams successfully meet advertiser objectives.</li>
<li>Serve as a trusted adviser and collaborator in handling client objections and all complex issues that cannot be solved by account teams.</li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<div><div><b>What we look for:</b></div></div>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s Degree</li>
<li>5 - 10 years of experience working in a customer success or digital performance marketing role</li>
<li>Ability to develop annual and quarterly plans, and ensure successful execution of these plans across multiple client teams, delivering high quality service and results to clients while delivering against CJ financial expectations.</li>
<li>Self-initiative and development- you are results focused and eager to enhance your skills to take your career to the next level.</li>
<li>Proven leadership, staff management and coaching skills.</li>
<li>Critical thinking and problem solver</li>
<li>Strong written and verbal communication skills.</li>
<li>Proficient knowledge of Microsoft suite of products, including Windows, Word, Excel, and PowerPoint.</li>
</ul>

Epsilon® is an all-encompassing global marketing innovator. Epsilon’s digital media arm, Conversant, is a leader in personalized digital advertising and insights through its proprietary technology and trove of consumer marketing data, delivering digital marketing with unprecedented scale, accuracy and reach through personalized media programs.
