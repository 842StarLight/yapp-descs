<p>Description</p>
</div>
<div><div>
<p>At OneAmerica®, we deliver on our promises when customers need us most. We believe the best way to serve our customers is to know that every individual, employee, family and business we work with has unique personal and financial goals. We keep our promises so we can help them achieve their goals and realize their definition of financial success.</p>
<p>
This position is responsible for defining and driving the service and education delivered to OneAmerica’s Retirement Services plan participants, to meet desired outcomes. Strategic leader who directs a national team, and works across business areas to direct, develop, prioritize and deliver service and education to our defined contribution customers across our phone, digital and in-person channels.</p>
<p><b>
Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li> Define and deliver on the participant journey, across all media, accounting for all services (enrollment in retirement plan, maximizing retirement income potential, utilization of advice and guidance, rollovers, financial wellness, national campaigns, etc.).</li>
<li>Accountable for driving and developing strategic roadmap for participant enrollment and education – both in person and virtual.</li>
<li>Lead Asset Acquisition team and strategy responsible for facilitating asset transition into the plan.</li>
<li>Coordinate participant outreach efforts with the Customer Concierge team responsible for asset retention.</li>
<li>Connects to other divisional and enterprise projects.</li>
<li>Seek to build participant connection to OneAmerica's brand and the Company’s commitment to help Americans to a secure retirement.</li>
<li>Support Retirement Service sales specific to participant marketing and communication deliverables - includes pricing, RFPs, presentations.</li>
<li>Assist with client retention by meeting with other members of the servicing team on a regular basis to review the current status of all assigned client/advisor relationships and to communicate with the team on an ongoing basis regarding all client issues and opportunities.</li>
<li>Forges positive working relationships and synergies across the organization, creating transparency and communicating frequently the decisions and activity within the Participant Experience Team.</li>
<li>Identify new opportunities/ideas across the participant channels to drive positive outcomes, both in plan and outside the plan.</li>
<li>Key contributor to RS Leadership around strategic imperatives and initiatives touching the participant.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Required Qualifications</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Minimum of 7 - 10 years of experience; including people leadership</li>
<li>In-depth knowledge of retirement service industry, financial wellness trends and profit drivers.</li>
<li>Industry accreditation (or working toward), e.g., QPA, RPA, or CEBS or significant work-related experience with demonstrated knowledge.</li>
<li>Series 7 and 63 licenses required</li>
<li>Strong communication and presentation skills, written and oral.</li>
<li>Ability to work well cross functionally to build alignment and reach objectives</li>
<li>Ability to manage in unstructured situations with innovative and creative thinking.</li>
<li>Strong knowledge of ERISA. regulatory compliance, fiduciary responsibility and disclosure requirements as they relate to qualified retirement plans.</li>
<li>Familiarity with the types and features of investment vehicles utilized in retirement plans.</li>
<li>Expert relationship management skills with a strong client and advisor focus and self-motivating drive.</li>
<li>Able to multi-task and thrive in a rapidly changing environment.</li>
<li>Regional travel requirements, including some overnight travel.</li>
</ul>
