We are fueled by a commitment to innovation, customer-centric approach and accountability. Headquartered in San Francisco, with offices around the globe, we are connected by a culture that promotes creativity, teamwork, and transparent leadership.</div>
<div>

</div>
<div>
We are looking for an experienced Implementation Project Manager to join our San Francisco team of creative and talented people.</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
Position Description:</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
We are looking to hire our first Implementation Project Manager, within the Customer Success organization reporting to the Director of Customer Success. It will be this individual’s responsibility to effectively and professionally transition our clients from the sales cycle through implementation and into Customer Success.</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
The Project Manager will drive the success of our largest customers through a detail-oriented approach to Enterprise implementations, which includes cross-functional process development and tracking across three international offices and multiple key stakeholders. This individual must be a self-starter, with excellent prioritization, time management and presentation skills.</div>
</div>
<div><p><b>
Responsibilities:</b></p></div>
<p></p>
<li>
Track and manage all phases of the implementation and integration lifecycle, from sales handoff and customer kickoff to launch, in conjunction with Customer Success, Product, Integration and Solutions Engineering Teams;</li>
<li>
Build, develop and maintain Humanity project management templates and documentation - we are looking for someone to take complete ownership of this new role who is prepared to demo preferred toolset as part of the interview process;</li>
<li>
Proactively identify significant project risks, develop mitigation strategies, escalate to leadership, and implement approved mitigation steps;</li>
<li>
Provide recurring implementation project status updates to internal stakeholders;</li>
<li>
Work with Sales on SOW and timeline expectations;</li>
<li>
Facilitate and lead interaction between internal and external project stakeholders with a bias towards expediting the time-to-value cycle and lowering project risk;</li>
<li>
Ensure configured product offerings match client specifications;</li>
<li>
Understand and effectively communicate product features, functions, and benefits to client, emphasizing Humanity’s Best Practices and seeking support appropriately when client requirements deviate from product capabilities;</li>
<li>
Demonstrate keen awareness to gaps in rolled-out processes, solutions and tools, canvassing teams and stakeholders for opportunities to improve or remediate shortcomings;</li>
<li>
Demonstrate full accountability for assigned domain of responsibility, even in the face of events outside of one’s own control.</li>
<div>
<br>
</div>

Proven experience in providing service to internal stakeholders to achieve successful project outcomes;</li>
<li>
Understanding of project delivery and acceptance processes within a fast-paced environment;</li>
<li>
Demonstrated capability for problem-solving, decision-making, sound judgment, and assertiveness;</li>
<li>
Highly adaptable and a self-starter, able to operate in complex environments while successfully managing simultaneous projects;</li>
<li>
Strong relationship-building and interpersonal skills.</li>
