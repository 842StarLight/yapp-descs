<h2>Orpheum Theatre House Manager - Part-time</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div></div>
<div><div><div>
<p>This position coordinates and directs the work of event support personnel, including: ushers (paid and volunteer), security guards, caterers, decorators, and temporary labor working on contract with the Orpheum Theatre. Duties include: ensuring that chairs, tables, and lobby equipment are set-up to meet the client's needs and the facility's lease requirements; explaining policies and procedures to client tenants and the general public; settling merchandise sales with tenants; opening and securing the facility before and after events; obtaining necessary permits; and completing all necessary event paperwork and keeping event management computer system up-to-date. This position requires extensive face-to-face customer service and problem-solving. Currently, there is one vacancy at the Orpheum Theatre.</p>
<p>
The ideal candidate is self-motivated, proactive, and detail-oriented and must have the ability to accept responsibility, quickly make decisions, and work in a fast-paced, high-volume atmosphere.</p>
