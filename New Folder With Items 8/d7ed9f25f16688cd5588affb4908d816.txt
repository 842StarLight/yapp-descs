<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>About the job</b></h2>
<p>
We are now starting the second stage of our channel partnerships and invite you to be a part of the New Business Development team. The mission of this team is to help expand Hardware into new channels such as Enterprise, targeted verticals such as ISPs, hospitality, EDU as well as to explore new business models. The group will collaborate with stakeholders from Sales, Marketing, Operations and Product within Hardware and also across Product Areas.</p>
<p>
The Google Devices and Services team focuses on Google and Nest branded hardware across all product categories. Our team is responsible for managing partnerships with our top carriers, retailers and enterprise verticals, including developing channel strategy, negotiating commercial terms, marketing and merchandising, and overall market share goals. We partner cross-functionally with marketing, product and engineering, finance and retail marketing teams internally, and with our partner counterparts across these disciplines.
</p>
<p>As a Solutions Lead, Business Intelligence sitting within our Business Tools &amp; Intelligence group, you will be responsible for owning and delivering internal reporting and analytics solutions for the Devices and Services Partnerships organization. You will assess current tools and data infrastructure, align business requirements with technical capabilities, outline implementation plans, and own the end-to-end deployment of reporting and business intelligence tools for our teams. This role will maintain a broad perspective by defining a holistic vision for our business intelligence capabilities, while also partnering internally to design and deliver innovative solutions. Google Devices and Services is just at the beginning of its journey, and this role will be pivotal in enabling our teams to scale.</p>
<p>
The Hardware Partnerships team was formed with the purpose of bringing the best of Google’s software and hardware to our users by bringing to market and distributing a Google-branded hardware portfolio. The team is responsible for managing all partnerships with carriers, retailers and resellers worldwide. This includes the partnerships for products such as Pixel, Daydream View, Google Wifi, Google Nest Thermostat, Chromecast Ultra and Google Home.</p>
