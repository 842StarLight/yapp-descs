<p><b>
 Logistic Coordinator Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Surpass weekly minimum standard of loads matched to carrier by booking assigned Account Manager’s freight as well as other freight on the available load board.</li>
<li>With timeliness and attention to detail, thoroughly track and document all shipments assigned and immediately report any issues to Account Manager.</li>
<li>With a high sense of urgency, answer incoming phone calls within two rings and assist callers or transfer to appropriate personnel.</li>
<li>Build carrier lane capacity by strengthening carrier relationships, assisting to resolve carrier issues and negotiating carrier pricing.</li>
<li>Locate carrier capacity by utilizing multiple carrier databases within operating system and web based applications.</li>
<li>Participate in employee reviews during and post training, using feedback to continually improve job performance. P</li>
<li>Proactively work to assist other in achieving the organizations objectives.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Education and Skills Requirements:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Associates/Bachelor’s degree with preference to sales related training.</li>
<li>Strong sense of urgency to handle multiple tasks concurrently and maintain focus during fast paced and moderately stressful environment.</li>
<li>Possess ability to work in a team-oriented environment by excelling at handling individual workload to support the greater good of the team.</li>
<li>Exemplary experience in using and learning technology including the internet, computer software and telephone systems.</li>
<li>Excellent skills for communicating interpersonally through written and verbal means to co-workers, carriers and customers.</li>
