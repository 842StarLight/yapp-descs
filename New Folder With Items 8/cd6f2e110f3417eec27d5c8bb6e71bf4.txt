<h2>Manager-Corp Quality Assurance</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p><b>Job Summary:
</b></p>Provides technical expertise and oversees the implementation evaluation, and validation of Blue Diamond Growers’ Food Safety and Food Quality Programs and product quality. Oversees the growth and development of Quality Assurance team members.
<p></p>
<p><b>Job Description:
</b></p>
<p>ESSENTIAL JOB FUNCTIONS
</p>
<ul><li>Collaborates with the Customers, Sales, Production Managers, Planning, Scheduling and other department heads to ensure product quality standards are clearly defined and that the finished product quality attributes are within the defined specifications.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Oversees the Complaint System; manages responses to customer forms and surveys to maintain or become a certified or qualified supplier to customers around the world.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Reports quality trends to stakeholders; resolves quality issues and reports back to customers on a timely basis.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Serves as the liaison between sales and customers regarding quality issues.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Works with sales managers and customers on defining what product quality attributes are required.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Partners with production to ensure that manufacturing processes are capable of meeting the customer quality requirements.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Coordinates corrective action plans to drive quality initiatives.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Oversees 2nd and 3rd party audits; engages in regulatory inspections for non-conformances.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Provides technical information on BDG products and processes to customers.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Manages the product specification system.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Serves as a resource to production improvement teams on technical quality issues; aids in the development of QC worker instructions, calibration procedures, test procedures, SPC interpretation, and computer set up and data input.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Collects data, analyzes, and reports on key quality attributes; track trends, average performance against the specifications, and overall process capability.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Implements, maintains, verifies, and validates the company’s SQF System (includes HACCP, Food Safety, Food Security and Food Quality).
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Provides back-up support for other SQF Practitioners.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Develops, audit and enforce QC policies and procedures that will result in consistent quality products that fulfill internal and external customer needs.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Trains and develops Quality Assurance team members.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>This job description reflects management’s assignment of essential functions; it does not prescribe or restrict the tasks that may be assigned.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Manage product holds and dispositions in SAP for all BDG products.
</li></ul>
<p></p>
<p>MINIMUM JOB REQUIREMENTS
</p>
<ul><li>Bachelor's Degree in Chemistry, Biology, Food Processing, or Business Administration.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>5 years of quality assurance experience in a food/beverage manufacturing environment.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Supervisor/ management experience.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Experience presenting concepts and ideas to employees at all levels of the organization as well as external customers in business and technical fields.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Experience writing and interpreting complex documents in a clear and concise manner.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Proficient use of computer and analytical software. Able to develop, interpret and apply specification parameters.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Expert knowledge of statistics and SPC.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Knowledge of USDA Grades and standards for the almond industry.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Strong technical background and ability to analyze laboratory reports and technical data.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Proficient in SAP
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Required to keep Quality Assurance documents and specifications current.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Certification as a SQF Practitioner.
</li></ul>
<p></p>
<p>PREFERRED SKILLS AND EXPERIENCES (Specialized education, specific software experience, etc.)
</p>
<ul><li>Experience in the nut industry preferred.
</li></ul>
