<h2>Design Assistant/Junior Designer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Career Opportunity – Design Assistant/Junior Designer </b></p>
<p>The Design Assistant/Junior Designer will be an integral part of the design team and our customer service commitment to ensuring all showroom visitors, telephone callers and digital contacts have an exceptional experience here at Braam’s Custom Cabinets.</p>
<p><b>Skills For Success</b></p>
<ul><li>Preferred minimum of 2 years of successful work experience in a design-oriented showroom.</li></ul>
<ul>
<li>Design creativity with exposure in the high-end kitchen, bath or remodeling industry. A thoughtful articulate, passionate, and compelling communicator.</li>
<li>Experience in building trust-based relationships with customers.</li>
</ul>
<ul><li>A team player who is highly motivated, energetic &amp; driven to succeed in our industry.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Meticulous attention to detail</li></ul>
<ul><li>Must be able to handle multiple projects proficiently</li></ul>
<ul><li>Must be able to prioritize, work quickly, accurately and produce design projects within deadlines</li></ul>
<ul><li>Preferred minimum Associates Degree in related design, architecture or construction program or related work experience.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Preferred software skills in Cabinet Vision, 20/20, Vortek, AutoCAD, and MS Office</li></ul>
<p><b>Key Responsibilities </b></p>
<ul><li>Delivering genuine hospitality ensuring every visitor has an exceptional showroom experience.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Tracking showroom leads and assisting in pre-qualifying with accurate information for sales/designers to follow up.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Ensuring we obtain information from each visitor/caller/digital inquiry and appropriately document information for marketing purposes.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Assisting designers and other team members on finish material selections, project samples, researching alternative or new products materials and preparing for meetings.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Assisting with project coordination including ordering, tracking, documenting, maintain documents and project information.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Assisting in maintaining project schedules, project records and project design files.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Coordinator setting and confirming meetings for sales/designers as well ensuring the showroom and meeting spaces are clean, prepared and ready to accommodate each customer.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Provide excellent customer communications in all areas from telephone calls, online inquiries and in person opportunities.</li></ul>
<ul>
<li>Showroom operations including managing and ordering supplies, ensuring showroom/meeting rooms clean and organized, and keeping orderly library of product samples and materials.</li>
<li>Provides design support by utilizing proprietary systems and CV, Vortek and 20-20 Design software to fulfill all aspects of jobs.</li>
</ul>
