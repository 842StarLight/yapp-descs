<h2>Strategic Accounts Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>POSITION SUMMARY:</b></p>
<p>
The Strategic Account Manager is responsible for successfully managing all operational execution of current client accounts as well as identifying and generating new opportunities for profitable growth. The position will work closely with IT, Accounting, customer solutions group and operations to drive tailored solutions to increase efficiency add value and maximize value for clients. Will participate with the implementation of new customers.</p>
<p><b>
ESSENTIAL FUNCTIONS:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Ensuring existing client accounts are achieving budgeted objectives.</li>
<li>Develop account specific metrics and scorecard.</li>
<li>Manage customer expectations and communicate with client on regular schedule.</li>
<li>Create, review and update all client statements of work, functional requirements, aligning technology needs and educate impacted groups within internal departments. Align contract rate structure to include profitability analysis and billing set up.</li>
<li>Work closely with marketing team to develop and execute lead generation campaigns.</li>
<li>Collaborate with customer solutions group to develop winning client-centric proposals and ensure smooth client onboarding.</li>
<li>Maintain and manage account SWOT analysis to help identify and secure additional opportunities throughout the life of the contract.</li>
<li>Provide accurate and timely updates of all sales and marketing activity in Salesforce.com.</li>
<li>Develop and maintain rapport and effective internal and external relationships to support business success.</li>
<br>
</ul>
