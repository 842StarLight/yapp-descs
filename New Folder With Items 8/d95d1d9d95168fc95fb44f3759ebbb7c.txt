<h2>Associate Technical Account Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>Associate Technical Account Managers at Yardi will provide consultative, implementation and technical support to a group of assigned Yardi clients in order to manage business operations and financial accounting functions in Yardi’s Voyager software.
</p>
<p>Responsibilities</p>
<ul>
<li>Implements the Yardi suite of software products by assisting clients with system implementation, training, and technical support</li>
<li>Researches, analyzes and resolves software issues</li>
<li>Supports software product customization based on client requirements</li>
<li>Performs data mapping and conversions</li>
<li>Performs system and business process reviews and other service reviews and makes recommendations on best practices</li>
<li>Works on a regular basis with Yardi internal resource teams to include: training, conversion, sales and development
</li>
</ul>
<p>Requirements</p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree in Economics, Business, Math, Statistics or Information Management preferred</li>
<li>Experience setting and managing client expectations</li>
<li>Superior verbal and written communication skills</li>
<li>Project Management, Accounting, or Property Management experience/background</li>
<li>Solid Proficiency in MS Office Suite and Outlook</li>
<li>Knowledge in three of the following areas: Property Management, Accounting, Microsoft Windows, Technical Support, SQL, HTML/ASP</li>
<li>Prior experience with property management software or real estate software highly desirable</li>
<li>Prior experience with Yardi software highly desirable</li>
<li>Regular attendance and a regular work schedule is an essential function of this job.</li>
</ul>
</div>
