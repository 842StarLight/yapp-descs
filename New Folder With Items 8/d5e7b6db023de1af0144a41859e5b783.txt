<h2>Membership Advisor</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Change lives working with the best! Orangetheory uses the latest technology and exercise science to provide our clients with unparalleled results. The only thing missing is YOU</p>
<p>The Membership Advisor role assists in running all “front of house” fitness studio operations, with a strong focus on premium customer service as well as meeting sales expectations. Candidate must have strong customer service skills and sales expertise. The Membership Advisor is responsible for meeting and exceeding all key performance sales indicators for the fitness studio, including membership sales and renewals, retail and concession sales, maximizing workout traffic, and maintaining premium customer service levels.</p>
<p>JOB QUALIFICATIONS:</p>
<p>- Passion for fitness, contagious positive attitude, excellent communication skills and work ethic</p>
<p>- Must be able to attend two training sessions per week minimum</p>
<p>- Reliable and resourceful</p>
<p>- Excellent teamwork</p>
<p>- Excellent customer service skills and a passion for helping others succeed</p>
<p>- Previous sales experience, with strong sales skills (experience working in a sales quota bearing structure highly preferred)</p>
<p>- Able to multi-task and excel in a busy environment.</p>
<p>- Functional computer skills required</p>
<p>– MS Office basic programs (word, excel), Internet, Basic computer program software use</p>
<p>- Ability to work and function in an ORANGE TEAM environment (Orange is more than a just a color, it’s an attitude)</p>
<p>- Basic fitness knowledge (anatomy, physiology, program design etc)</p>
<p>- Flexible to work day, evening and/or weekend hours as needed and attend external events</p>
<p>JOB DUTIES INCLUDE:</p>
<p>* Establishes and maintains an effective referral program</p>
<p>* Maintains accurate records using established OTF sales systems.</p>
<p>* Conducts telephone inquiries/follow up calls/customer care calls</p>
<p>* Leads OTF studio previews with prospects and/or fitness program holders</p>
<p>* Greets members and guests promptly, enthusiastically and with a smile to create a friendly positive entrance into the OTF studio</p>
<p>* Accurately follow the daily studio cleaning checklist ( pre/during/post ) shift activities</p>
<p>* Must be able to participate in 2 OTF scheduled workouts per week</p>
<p>* Maintains an organized and clean lobby/front desk area</p>
<p>* Responsible for processing accurate cash and credit card transactions</p>
<p>* Follow up and follow through activities with all prospective clients</p>
<p>* Responds immediately to member requests, inquiries and concerns.</p>
<p>* Proper onboarding all OTF clients through the use of Client Intake Forms and FP Agreements</p>
<p>* Works closely with Fitness Team to ensure that processes are fulfilled * Responsible for attending and participating in all relative OTF training programs</p>
<p>Job Type: Full-time</p>
<p>Experience:</p>
<ul><li>Sales: 3 years (Required)</li></ul>
<p>Education:</p>
<ul><li>High school or equivalent (Required)</li></ul>
<p>License:</p>
<ul><li>Personal Trainer (Preferred)</li></ul>
