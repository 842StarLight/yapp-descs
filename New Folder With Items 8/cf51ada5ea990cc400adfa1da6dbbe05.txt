<p><b><i>Roles and Responsibilities </i></b></p>
<p>· Directly supervise and be accountable for all aspects of Retreat operations when the Retreat Director is not present.</p>
<p>· The Assistant Retreat Director generally works 45 hours per week (full-time) to cover all shifts, especially evenings and weekends.</p>
<p>· Create an environment that is passionate about providing Guest service.</p>
<p>· Meet and exceed Retreat goals.</p>

<p>· Schedule new and return appointments based on Guest preferences.</p>
<p>· Present Lifestyle Programs, Gift Certificates and retail products.</p>

<p>· Stay updated on all retail products and promotions available in the Retreat</p>
<p>· Achieve goals for retail, re-booking, and memberships</p>
<p>· Create and maintain positive working relationships with Team Members.</p>
<p>· Take pride and accountability in all aspects of your job.</p>
<p>· Create a Team environment.</p>
<p>· Provide a clean environment for Team Members and Guests.</p>
<p>· Required to work evenings and weekends</p>
