<b>ESSENTIAL FUNCTIONS:</b><br>
The Account Executive’s primary role is to achieve assigned annual revenue and gross margin quotas by establishing relationships and contracts with NEW and existing customers in first the<br>
territorial hot zone or priority 1 areas, then the geographic region or priority 2 areas, and finally nationally. This position will be compensated by a base salary and a yearly commission plan.<br>
The ideal candidate will be comfortable selling Outside Plant Construction services to ILEC’s, CLEC’s, Wireless carriers, Municipalities, Power Companies/Cooperatives, and enterprise<br>
customers.</div>
