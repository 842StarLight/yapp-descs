<b>
Responsibilities</b>
</div>
<div>
<p>The Vendor Relationship Manager is responsible for all technology and IT vendors for IGT globally. This person will be the primary resource responsible for a combination of IT vendor contract, performance relationship and risk management. must have an understanding of contractual, commercial and operational working relationships with vendors to ensure effective delivery of committed goods and services. This role reports directly to the Senior Director – Global IT Services</p>
<p>Operational duties:</p>
<ul>
<li>Establish and manage internal and external policies, standards and processes for vendor interaction.</li>
<li>Collaborate with internal stakeholders — including IT and business resources, sourcing and procurement.</li>
<li>Provide assistance to business and procurement teams to optimize the cost-effectiveness of negotiations and ensure compliance of negotiated agreements to established vendor management policies and practices (such as articulation of service levels and deliverables).</li>
</ul>
<p>Contract management:</p>
<ul>
<li>Manage the creation and maintenance of a repository of vendors in a contract management database or similar solution to track key dates (expirations, renewals) and assess demand.</li>
<li>Maintain an inventory of vendor spend in collaboration with business, finance and procurement teams by category (software, hardware, telecom, staff augmentation, services), cost center and region.</li>
<li>Performance management:</li>
<li>Oversee the measurement and reporting of vendor performance, including the development of evaluation tools based on constituent requirements, and the creation of scorecards and dashboards that will accurately present this data.</li>
</ul>
<p>Risk management:</p>
<ul>
<li>Maintain vendor risk assessments or risk registers in cooperation with risk, privacy, business continuity and security teams.</li>
</ul>
