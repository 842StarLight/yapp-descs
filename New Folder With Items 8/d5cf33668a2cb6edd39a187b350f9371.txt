<h2>Office Assistant/Customer Service Rep</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Daniel Smart Manufacturing, Inc. has been making and distributing high quality motorcycle gear and fashion leather apparel for almost 20 years.</p>
<p>Daniel Smart Manufacturing is hiring a full-time <b>Office Assistant/Customer Service Rep.</b></p>
<p><b>Job Summary: </b></p>
<p>This position is responsible for assistance in all office duties and daily operations.</p>
<p><b>General Accountabilities: </b></p>
<p>Assistance in all office operations, other administrative duties and day to day customer service.</p>
<p>Servicing existing customers.</p>
<p>Develops and maintains outstanding working relationships with customers to ensure customer satisfaction.</p>
<p>Resolves customer complaints regarding sales and service.</p>
<p>Prepare and modify documents including correspondence, reports, drafts, memos and emails.</p>
<p>Processing orders in Quick Books and Invoicing.</p>
<p>Prepare shipping documents for LTL Shipping, UPS, DHL and USPS.</p>
<p>Collaborate with warehouse, purchasing and other managers to optimize processes.</p>
<p>Maintain office supply inventories.</p>
<p>Coordinate maintenance of office operation.</p>
<p>Quickly adapt to our software system.</p>
<p><b>Job Qualifications: </b></p>
<p>· Experience: At least 4 years of related experience</p>
<p><b>Skills</b></p>
<p>· Excellent verbal and written communication skills.</p>
<p>· Proven customer service skills.</p>
<p>· Strong administrative skills.</p>
<p>· Well organized and able to do multi tasks in a fast-paced environment.</p>
<p>· Attention to detail and accuracy</p>
<p>· Manage time effectively.</p>
<p><b>Compensation: </b></p>
<p>Compensation package includes a competitive hourly rate and a company benefits package.</p>
<p>Equal Opportunity Employer.</p>
