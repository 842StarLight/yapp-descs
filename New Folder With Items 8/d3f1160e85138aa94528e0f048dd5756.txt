<h2>Supply Chain Optimization Senior Analyst</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>The primary purpose of the role is to support the execution and analysis of channel decisions and optimization of the Supply Chain Networks. The senior analyst will determine the most efficient and optimal flow of product from supplier to store and customer, while balancing service and cost. The senior analyst serves as the functional expert for supply chain product flow analytics. The position will also lead analysis initiatives to support the supply chain stocking strategy and will assist the Manager, SC Optimization with the development and refinement of the network stocking strategy.<br>
<br>
RESPONSIBILITY STATEMENTS<br>
</p>
<ul>
<li>
Lead product flow and channel analysis and coordination with SC Planning, Capacity, Operations, and Merchandise Logistics Management teams for execution</li>
<li>
Develops and supports weekly reporting to identify items and programs that need additional analysis and/or to have their stocking strategy adjusted based on changing business conditions
</li>
<li>Conducts central channel analysis using the appropriate optimization tool and model to review alternatives and trade-offs within business and strategy guardrails
</li>
<li>Completes analysis to support the development of processes and governance of channel business rules and decisions that ensure costs, capacities, and capabilities are accounted for to ensure timely flow of goods through the network.</li>
<li>
Expert in advanced channel modeling capabilities (including data management, modeling and analytics, knowledge management, and talent development) to support channel and flow decisions.
</li>
<li>Provides supply chain flow analysis support across the enterprise (SC Strategy, SC Planning, SC Replenishment, SC Engineering, etc.)</li>
<li>
Supports participants with User Acceptance Testing (UAT) development/execution and training for optimization tools, process updates, and workflow tools
</li>
<li>Develops deep understanding of supply chain analytics and optimization concepts and stays up to speed on industry best practices</li>
<li>
Develops and documents best practices and content to support decision making, including the supporting modeling results, alternatives considered, timelines and financial impacts/requirements.
</li>
<li>Proactively identifies process improvement activities including designing, developing, and communicating improved processes through SC organization including end to end workflow updates.
</li>
<li>Supports training and development of the analysts on the team to ensure that SOPs are followed, and the team adheres to industry best practices</li>
</ul>
<p><br>
Required Education/Experience<br>
Bachelor’s Supply Chain Management, Business or Related field and 5+ yrs<br>
*Performing Supply Chain Analysis *Replenishment, Planning, Forecasting or transportation process experience *Product Flow Optimization and analytics CrossFunctional team experience *Data Manipulation- Large data sets *Strong experience with Data (Industrial Engineering, Supply Chain Analysis, Financial Analysis, Microsoft Excel, Access and SQL, Power BI, Tableau a plus)<br>
