<h2>Event and Membership Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<div>Execute planning and logistics for the National Center for Spectator Sports Safety and Security events, with close attention to planning, design and production. The candidate will be able to provide outstanding customer service and organize events that meet the intended goals and expectations both creatively and consistently. The candidate is responsible for developing and implementing membership plans, strategies and initiatives for retention of current members and recruitment of new members.<br>
<br>
<b>Primary Duties and Responsibilities</b><br>
<br>
<ul>
<li>Effectively lead the National Center of many concurrent events and membership program</li>
<li>Coordinate, manage, track and evaluate all membership renewal activities. Develop innovative membership marketing and promotional programs to increase member value</li>
<li>Responsible for building and maintaining industry membership program</li>
<li>Design and build a cohesive and individual look and feel for each event</li>
<li>Provides input into the development of event strategy, content and communications</li>
<li>Negotiate vendor contracts; facilitation and management of relationships with vendors and internal event stakeholders</li>
<li>Initiate event timelines; identify and work with local partners and vendors; secure appropriate venues; clearly communicate status updates and priorities; and effectively manage changes in events</li>
<li>Ensure timely and clear communication to event stakeholders regarding all necessary information related to events through various means of communication, including initiating and running effective efficient meetings.</li>
<li>Manage logistical tasks for producing events, including recruitment, guest registration, facility needs, audio/visual, catering, etc.</li>
<li>Anticipate and arrange all logistics related to VIP speakers and guests, including travel,</li>
</ul>
 accommodations, and transportation<br>
<ul>
<li>Determine or recommend staffing needs and guides day-of event staff and vendors</li>
<li>Other duties as assigned</li>
</ul>

<p><b>About The University of Southern Mississippi</b>
<br>
The University of Southern Mississippi (USM) is a comprehensive public research institution delivering transformative programs on campuses in Hattiesburg and Long Beach, at teaching and research sites in central and southern Mississippi, as well as online. Founded in 1910, USM is one of only 130 universities in the nation to earn the Carnegie Classification of Institutions of Higher Education's "R1: Doctoral Universities - Very high research activity" designation, and its robust research enterprise includes experts in ocean science and engineering, polymer science and engineering, and sport venue safety and security, among others. USM is also one of only 40 institutions in the nation accredited in theatre, art and design, dance and music. As an economic driver, USM generates an annual economic impact of more than $600 million across the state. USM welcomes a diverse student body of approximately 15,000, representing 71 countries, all 50 states, and every county in Mississippi. USM students have collected four Truman Scholarships and 36 National Science Foundation Graduate Research Fellowships, while also leading Mississippi with 24 Goldwater Scholarships, an honor that recognizes the next generation of great research scientists. Home to the Golden Eagles, USM competes in 17 Division I sports sponsored by the National Collegiate Athletic Association (NCAA). For more information, visit www.usm.edu.
</p>
