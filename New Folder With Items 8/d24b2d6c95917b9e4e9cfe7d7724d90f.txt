<h2>Customer Success Manager - Healthcare</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<div>As a Customer Success Manager focused on our Healthcare vertical, you will work with our strategic on-premise enterprise customers. CSMs oversee the post sale customer relationship and support customers in achieving the full value of their Kronos investment by building a deep understanding of their goals and workforce management objectives and mapping Kronos solutions and capabilities to those goals. CSMs build strong relationships with executive sponsors and key contacts within customer organizations and monitor and drive customer health and satisfaction. The ideal candidate is hands-on and results oriented with excellent communication and problem solving skills.<br>
<br>
<ul>
<li>Establish strong relationships with executive sponsors and key contacts within customer organizations in the Healthcare space</li>
<li>Build knowledge and expertise of customer organizations and their WFM plans and objectives and monitor progress against those objectives on an ongoing basis</li>
<li>Develop success plans that outline customer’s critical business issues and workforce management plans and develop success metrics to measure progress</li>
<li>Provide guidance in best practice and identify optimization opportunities</li>
<li>Monitor and track customer heath</li>
<li>Collaborate with the Kronos customer account team including sales, support and professional services</li>
<li>Provide feedback to product management and educate customers on product enhancements and roadmap</li>
<li>Facilitate contacts and communication within Kronos and assist in resolving issues and concerns when needed
</li>
</ul>
</div>
<div>
<p>Qualifications</p>
<ul>
<li>Strong relationship manager with a minimum of 7-10 years of experience in enterprise account management or consulting services with particular focus on Healthcare customers</li>
<li>Ability and experience with building credibility and relationships with senior leaders in enterprise organizations</li>
<li>Experience with workforce management or relevant functional experience highly desired</li>
<li>Excellent communication and presentation skills</li>
<li>Ability to work cross functionally in a global organization</li>
<li>Able to travel up to 25-30%</li>
<li>Ideal candidate will be located in MST or PST</li>
</ul>
