<h2>Executive Assistant</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Madison Environmental Resourcing Inc. (known as MERI) is a licensed infectious transporter and treatment facility in Waunakee, WI. We assist various customers with safe transportation and disposal of medical waste. MERI is seeking a qualified Executive Assistant to provide administrative support to the Chief Executive Officer, other team members, as well as external customers. The ideal candidate demonstrates versatility, initiative, and resourcefulness. You will contribute to MERI by:</p>
<p>· Managing and maintaining the calendar of the CEO</p>
<p>· Scheduling, coordinating, and preparing for Board of Director meetings</p>
<p>· Answering incoming calls and direct to appropriate team members</p>
<p>· Administering benefits for employees (e.g. at hire, at termination, qualified events)</p>
<p>· Preparing timesheets for CEO review</p>
<p>· Managing electronic correspondence and filling systems (paper and electronic)</p>
<p>· Creating truck/client notes in our software program</p>
<p>· Preparing and processing hazardous waste manifests, MWRS orders</p>
<p>· Managing and updating Standard Operating Procedures (SOPs) as needed</p>
<p>· Preparing sales packets and presentations as needed</p>
<p>· Assist sales team with proposals and contracts</p>
<p>To be successful in this role you will need:</p>
<p>· 3+ years of experience providing high-level administrative support</p>
<p>· Strong organizational and time management skills with excellent attention to detail</p>
<p>· Strong interpersonal, verbal and written communication skills, including strong listening skills</p>
<p>· Strong problem-solving and critical thinking skills</p>
<p>· Ability to multitask, quickly change direction and independently prioritize work</p>
<p>· Ability to take initiative and manage multiple projects through successful completion within set timelines</p>
<p>· Highly versatile and resourceful team player</p>
<p>· Proficiency with Microsoft Office Suite (Word, Excel, Power Point, Outlook) and Google Suite</p>
<p>This is a full-time position with a great benefits package including health, dental, vision, and 401(k). This position will work Monday-Friday and allows for flexible hours for a total of 30-40 hours with a salary of $40,000 - $50,000/year depending upon experience. To be considered for this opportunity, please forward your resume and pay requirements. We look forward to hearing from you!</p>
