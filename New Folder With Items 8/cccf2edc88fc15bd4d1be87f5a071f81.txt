<h2>Marketing Rep</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>SERVPRO® of South Colorado Springs and of Northern Colorado Springs/Tri-Lakes is seeking a skilled marketing representative. We’re seeking someone who is comfortable meeting new people, who has excellent communication skills, and who is a serious multi-tasker. If you are self-motivated and have superb interpersonal skills, then you’ll thrive in this work environment. Our idea of the ultimate candidate is one who is proactive, is experienced, truly enjoys providing superior service, and loves taking ownership.</p>
<p>Primary Responsibilities:</p>
<ul>
<li>Assist with social media accounts and search engine optimization</li>
<li>Meet or exceed assigned sales quota by executing the sales cycle, setting up closing appointments, maintaining assigned contact lists, participating in professional associations, providing lunch-and-learns, and promoting continuing education courses</li>
<li>Complete Emergency Ready Profiles (ERPs) and discuss benefits of emergency event preparation</li>
<li>Conduct objective-to-objective daily marketing contacts, build customer relationships and rapport by educating them on the reasons SERVPRO® is the best cleaning and restoration company in the world, compile and maintain center of influence information., identify “Target 25” (Top 25 contacts to develop into clients)</li>
<li>Provide owners and marketing managers with one-on-one meetings (closing appointments) with COIs to encourage SERVPRO® referrals</li>
<li>Increase sales territory revenue by consistently achieving sales territory goals</li>
</ul>
<p>Requirements:</p>
<ul>
<li>A minimum two years of progressively responsible business-to-business sales experience</li>
<li>Experience with sales and marketing within the service sector</li>
<li>Superb sales, customer service, administrative, verbal, and written communication skills</li>
<li>Strong business and financial background and process-and-results-driven attitude</li>
<li>Experience in the commercial cleaning and restoration or insurance industry is desired</li>
<li>Working knowledge of current business software technologies is required</li>
<li>Bachelor’s degree in marketing or business or equivalent experience is preferred</li>
<li>Ability to successfully complete a background check subject to applicable law</li>
<li>Reliable Vehicle and good standing driving record</li>
</ul>
<p>Competitive base plus activity-based commission and increases based on merit.</p>
<p>Job Type: Full-time</p>
