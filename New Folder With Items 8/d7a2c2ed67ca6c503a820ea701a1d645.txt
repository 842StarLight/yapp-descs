<p><b>Financial Services Industry Product Marketing Director</b><br>
<b>
</b><br>
</p>
<p><b>Job Type: </b>Full-Time<br>

<p><b>
Job Description:</b><br>
<b>
</b><br>
RingCentral is looking for a dynamic Financial Services Industry Product Marketing Director who will be responsible for leading go-to-market execution RingCentral’s solutions for the Financial Services industry. This experienced individual will drive the definition, and delivery of our go-to-market strategy, messaging, content, activities and marketing campaigns in the Financial Services space. This role requires the alignment of industry and positioning across the entire go-to-market approach, from themes, campaigns and programs, and considering all our routes to market. Deep Financial Services industry go-to-market knowledge combined with a strong understanding of cloud and/or SaaS businesses including experience with communications platforms is a plus.</p>
<p>
This industry and product marketing role highly visible and cross-functional, requiring regular communication with RingCentral leadership, product management, and sales. Successful candidates will be experienced product marketers who roll up their sleeves, have a ‘can do’ attitude while working with dynamic teams in a fast-paced, cross-functional environment. This position will require out of the Belmont, CA headquarters office.</p>
<p><b>
Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Develop a market-winning Financial Services go-to-market strategy and marketing plans necessary to meet company goals</li>
<li>
Collaborate with Sales, Sales Enablement, Industry Principals, Marketing, Product, Channel, ISV and Carrier teams</li>
<li>
Provide broad Industry and targeted Sub-Industry messaging and positioning</li>
<li>
Lead cross-channel marketing campaigns to extend growth in the Financial Services market</li>
<li>
Execute integrated Themes, Campaigns, and Programs that drive awareness and pipeline through innovative and results-oriented tactics, including PR/AR/ strategies, compelling and fresh content, data-driven digital investments, ABM campaigns, smart use of field marketing, amazing customer case studies, and innovative thought leadership programs</li>
<li>
Enable sales and channel teams on Financial Services industry solutions, as well as on best practices and sales plays</li>
<li>
Proven ability to create crisp, executive-level presentations, synthesizing actionable recommendations from quantitative and qualitative data</li>
<li>
Develop appropriate sales and partner training materials, collateral, and pricing guides</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Qualifications:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Bachelor's degree and 8 years of marketing experience</li>
<li>
Proven success developing and executing go-to-market plans for SaaS or cloud services in the Financial Services market</li>
<li>
Experience in leading cross-functional teams</li>
<li>
Strong business acumen, as well as strong analytical and strategic thinking abilities to draw conclusions based on data</li>
<li>
Self-motivated and driven individual skilled both planning and hands-on execution</li>
<li>
Excellent written, verbal, and presentation skills</li>
</ul>
