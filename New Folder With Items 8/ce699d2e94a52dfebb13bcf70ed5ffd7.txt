<p>
At Ajinomoto Bio-Pharma Services, our mission is to help improve the health of humankind. As a leading, global contract development and manufacturing organization, with sites in the US, Belgium, Japan and India, we work together to empower our clients to bring drugs to market.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
Every team member, from manufacturing and quality programs to development and facilities services are a critical component to this effort. At our US location, our focused expertise and capabilities makes us one of the industry’s top leaders for large molecule manufacturing and aseptic drug product fill in vials and syringes.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
We believe our people are our greatest asset and we are committed to providing our employees with an environment that supports personal and professional growth. We take pride in knowing our efforts are helping countless patients for years to come.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
Do you enjoy working in a dynamic, inspiring team with an amazing culture? If you do, then Ajinomoto Bio-Pharma Services is the place for you.</p>
<p>
We are looking for a <b>Marketing Strategist</b> responsible for the execution, management and monitoring of various marketing activities for the company. These include print &amp; online advertising, conferences, press releases, thought leadership, and collateral.</p>
<p><b>
The Marketing Strategist is a contract role with an expected duration of 6 months.</b></p>
<p><b>
Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Manages marketing related activities that support the sales channel including the maintenance and development of sales collateral.</li>
<li>Executes brand and website strategy by incorporating and leading new imagery and messaging into all marketing tactics.</li>
<li>Handles tradeshow and conference events hosted by Aji Bio-Pharma. Activities include advertisements, shipping of tradeshow materials, management of external vendors and communication to conference attendees.</li>
<li>Supports internal clients in their departmental marketing needs and internal branding projects.</li>
<li>Runs Aji Bio-Pharma social media accounts and postings.</li>
<li>Crafts a strategic advantage in marketing communication activities.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Requirements:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree in a business discipline or equivalent experience required.</li>
<li>Minimum of four (4) years of meaningful experience in marketing.</li>
<li>Experience within the Life Sciences industry is a plus.</li>
<li>Strong knowledge of the following concepts and technologies: HTML, CRM Database (Salesforce), Adobe Creative Suite: Illustrator, Photoshop, graphic design, WordPress.</li>
<li>Familiarity with online marketing and email campaigns, online culture, social media, social engagement, mobile devices, etc.</li>
<li>Detail oriented with strong interpersonal skills.</li>
<li>Ability to work independently, within prescribed guidelines, or as a team member.</li>
<li>Proficiency with Microsoft Office applications, especially PowerPoint and Excel.</li>
<br>
</ul>
