<b>Job Description:</b><br>
An exciting opportunity to strategize and write content for PeopleFluent’s suite of enterprise software products:<br>
<br>
<ul>
<li>Listen and be heard – Play an integral role in shaping content strategy to elevate an established brand and associated offerings</li>
<li>Create work you’re proud of – Be creative in presenting the PeopleFluent brand and products across media and channels</li>
<li>Put your best foot forward – Take on the exciting challenge of developing high-quality content for lead generation, nurture, and client relations</li>
</ul>
<br>
We are looking for a proven writer to craft quality content for our enterprise talent and learning business, PeopleFluent. The mission of this role is to collaborate with the PeopleFluent Product Marketing team and LTG channel specialists (design, digital demand, UX/web, and events) to plan and create well-crafted content that builds brand equity and supports pipeline growth.<br>
<br>
What you’ll be doing every day<br>
<br>
<ul>
<li>Planning and creating high-quality content in support of PeopleFluent and its best-of-breed talent solutions</li>
<li>Researching, writing, and editing copy for websites, blogs, landing pages, emails, and events, as well as sales enablement materials, as assigned</li>
<li>Collaborating with PeopleFluent marketers and subject matter experts to support communication of value propositions and campaign messaging</li>
<li>Coordinating content reviews with subject matter experts throughout PeopleFluent and LTG’s centralized marketing team</li>
<li>Defining and documenting the PeopleFluent brand voice and encouraging consistency in its use</li>
<li>Ensuring all content is optimized for SEO (in collaboration with the LTG SEO Specialist)</li>
<li>Creating content that has a purpose and will generate leads, in consultation with the LTG Digital and Demand Director</li>
<li>Working with the LTG Art Department to create effective assets for PeopleFluent across digital and print media</li>
<li>Managing multiple assignments with overlapping deadlines</li>
<li>Establishing and maintaining strong relationships across LTG Central Marketing, PeopleFluent leadership, and other departments</li>
<li>Sharing knowledge and best practices in content marketing across the content team and wider LTG Central Marketing team</li>
<li>Keeping up to date with online communication technologies and content strategy trends through relevant blogs, professional memberships, events, and training opportunities</li>
</ul>
<br>
Why you’ll love it<br>
<br>
<ul>
<li>You’ll have a voice and integral role in shaping messaging into content that drives results</li>
<li>You’ll be part of a fun and collaborative team of content writers, and an extended team of marketing channel experts who strive every day to create their best work</li>
<li>You’ll work directly with subject matter experts who appreciate the rarity and value of writing talent</li>
<li>You’ll get to write on a range of timely and interesting topics in talent management and learning, with a view toward improving the working lives of people around the world</li>
<li>You’ll get to craft authentic content that you can be proud of</li>
<li>You’ll see the impact of your work on the success of PeopleFluent as business
</li>
</ul>
