<p><b>Company Overview: </b></p>
<p>Lunar Cow Design is a national destination marketing company that supports travel and tourism organizations across the United States. Our clients represent a national footprint and are fun destinations to work with.</p>
<p><b>The Job: </b></p>
<p>Lunar Cow's Inside Telephone sales representatives call small businesses in the regions that we represent. We call current and prospective business clients that can benefits from advertising in a travel guide that will help the business drive more tourism customers. We speak to businesses that want to place advertisements in our guides so that the business can attract new customers from tourists that are traveling to the regions we represent.</p>
<p><b>Training Provided: </b></p>
<p>Training for this position is provided initially and ongoing. We review sales training, techniques and sales applications on a daily basis. Our commitment to our inside sales representatives focuses on driving sales knowledge and sales skill that allows our reps to close sales at a high rate.</p>

<p>$9.00 Per Hour Plus Commission, Bonus and Incentives. Part Time 20 Hours Per Week. The average hourly rate is $20 per hour.</p>
