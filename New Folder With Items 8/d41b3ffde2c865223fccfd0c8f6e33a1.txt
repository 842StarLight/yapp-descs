<p>Are you a persuasive, enthusiastic, driven person interested in an Inside Sales position selling print and digital advertising space for a quality-driven media and publishing company?</p>
<p>Brehm Communications (BCI), a customer-focused, multi-state media company producing daily and weekly newspapers, special publications and magazines throughout California, Arizona and Utah, has openings on our Inside Sales team in Auburn, California.</p>
<p>Gold Country Media (GCM), a subsidiary of BCI, is based in Auburn and is seeking self-driven, outgoing individuals for its Sales staff at The Auburn Journal.</p>
<p>The Inside Sales duties involve making brief, winning sales pitches over the phone a to prospective customers, utilizing marketing materials and bundling products to fit the customer’s needs. Ideal qualities for this position include a patient and pleasant demeanor, since exceptional customer service and quality sales presentations are the pinnacle of success at our company. A willingness to go the extra mile and remain flexible is essential. Lastly, the ideal candidate will provide quality communication in manner of speech, remaining cognizant to customer marketing solutions!</p>
<p>As a member of the team, you will focus on selling ad space within the company’s products and bundling services to extend the greatest marketing opportunities to our customers.</p>
<p><b>Responsibilities: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>You will be responsible for selling display and classified advertising, writing sales proposals, using MS Excel and MS Word, entering insertion orders and applying rate cards and research data to further the sales process. Knowledge of customer management systems is a plus.</li>
<li>You will be expected to meet and exceed sales goals, make outgoing cold calls, prospect for new customers, and build upon and service existing advertisers in your assigned territories.</li>
<li>When speaking with prospects, you will need to identify the key advertising placement decision makers and initiate and pursue productive, consultative relationships.</li>
<li>Ability to close sales, redirect the customers focus to the product(s) or proposal inquiries, up-sell frequency and maintain a viable working relationship with your customer accounts.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Qualifications: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>You should be articulate, tenacious, persuasive, prepared, meticulous, organized, energetic, a team player and have previous retail, inside and/or outside sales, or telephone sales experience.</li>
<li>The inside advertising sales team is an excellent place to enhance your career in advertising sales, manufacture a series of successes, gain notice, contribute to community events and be promoted to higher levels of responsibility.</li>
</ul>
