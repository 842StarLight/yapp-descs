<b>Job Summary:</b><br>
The Collision / Body Shop Estimator is responsible for providing customers with automotive repair estimates and relating this information to the Collision Technician so that the unit can be repaired in a timely fashion.
<br><br>
<b>Job Responsibilities include but not limited to:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Prepare all damage reports in an accurate and efficient manner</li>
<li>Obtain all signatures, work authorizations and powers of attorney</li>
<li>Negotiate an agreed price on every job prior to processing the job</li>
<li>Review all final bills and supplemental billings prior to delivery of the vehicle Review the completed vehicle to check quality and thoroughness</li>
<li>Greet each customer in the process of getting a vehicle repaired</li>
<li>Make every effort to satisfy the customer's needs in regards to their automotive repair</li>
<li>Assertively inform the customer that we feel we are the best choice for them to have their car repaired</li>
<li>Make every effort to make a customer or potential customer feel comfortable and welcome during the sales and estimate process or whenever visiting the shop</li>
<li>Demonstrates behaviors consistent with the Company's Vision, Mission, and Values in all interactions with customers, co-workers and suppliers Adheres to all company policies, procedures and safety standards</li>
</ul>
