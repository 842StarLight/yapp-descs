<p><b>
Career Opportunity:</b></p>
<p>
The <i>Beverage Manager </i>of <i>Sawmill Market</i> will:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Lead and mentor a team of Beverage professionals to create an unforgettable mixology experience.</li>
<li>
Set the creative mixology vision for Sawmill Market, implementing a Beverage program in collaboration with the Director of Operations.</li>
<li><i>
Use essential hospitality and customer service standards to inspire and celebrate our guests: smile, speak first, and engage in polite conversation (if time permits).</i></li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Supervisory Responsibilities:</b> None</p>
<p><b>
Qualifications:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Four-year college degree, Hospitality preferred; or equivalent combination of education and experience.</li>
<li>
2-3 years of beverage management or equivalent combination of education and experience; luxury branded hospitality experience preferred.</li>
<li>
Experience with ordering, proper storage and taking inventory of liquor, wine, and beer.</li>
<li>
New Mexico Food Handler Certification and Alcohol Server Certification.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Essential Functions &amp; Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Work with the Director of Operations in creating the Sawmill Market beverage program, including cocktail menu development, wine and beer selections, and seasonal changes.</li>
<li>
Lead, train, guide, and mentor the team of bartenders and outlet supervisors.</li>
<li>
Write and post schedules for all beverage team members in a timely fashion.</li>
<li>
Maintain relationships with vendors to ensure best pricing on all products ordered.</li>
<li>
Assist outlets with service, including working behind the bar when necessary to ensure smooth operation and a great guest experience.</li>
<li>
Communicate all beverage changes, specials, and updates to all effected outlets.</li>
<li>
Conduct weekly inventory of all bars, order and receive product, process all invoices.</li>
</ul>
