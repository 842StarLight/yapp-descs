<p></p>This job is responsible for the development and optimization of digital campaign landing pages and campaign assets with the goal being to drive customer engagement and acquisition. Responsibilities include creating and updating content to increase Search Engine Marketing performance, analyzing and reporting campaign results and ensuring necessary metrics are being tracked. This position will also support the departmentâ€™s various digital advertising efforts.<br>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>Education
</b></p>
<p>Required - High School diploma or equivalent and additional degrees, business training experience and/or certifications may be combined to meet minimum qualifications.
</p>
<p>Preferred - Bachelor's degree in Marketing, Advertising, Business Administration or related field<br>
</p>
<p><br>
<b>Work Experience
</b></p>
<p>Required - 3 years related marketing experience
</p>
<p>Preferred - 5 years of marketing experience including three years of project leadership experience
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Knowledge Skills and Abilities (KSAs)
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Proficiency in SEO and SEM techniques, execution and performance reporting
</li>
<li>Comfort navigating web pages with a user mindset
</li>
<li>Solid understanding of performance marketing, conversion, and online customer acquisition
</li>
<li>Working knowledge of the design/development of websites
</li>
<li>Working knowledge of basic HTML, Adobe Photoshop, Adobe Illustrator, Adobe Acrobat and Microsoft Office Suite
</li>
<li>Proficient in Microsoft Office Suite (Word, Excel, PowerPoint, Adobe, etc.).
</li>
<li>Effective verbal and written communication skills and the ability to present information clearly and professionally to varying levels of individuals throughout the patient care process.
</li>
<li>Must have computer skills and dexterity required for data entry and retrieval of information
</li>
<li>Must be proficient with Windows-style applications, various software packages specific to role and keyboard
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Job Duties
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Perform ongoing keyword research, expansion and optimization
</li>
<li>Create, enhance and monitor landing pages to improve search engine rankings with a focus on user experience
</li>
<li>Ensuring landing page content is consistent in terms of style, quality and tone of voice, and optimized for user experience
</li>
<li>Managing content publishing within structured workflow, maximizing quality of page content, display, links, and metatags
</li>
<li>Work with digital partners to ensure each campaign meets desired ROI goals
</li>
<li>Collaborate with cross-functional teams to develop, execute, and analyze results for digital marketing campaigns
</li>
<li>Compile and distribute various marketing campaign reports
</li>
</ul>
