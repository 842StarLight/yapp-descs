<div><b>
About the role</b></div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
The online offer incubator was established to test and develop new wireline offers and develop more creative/efficient ways to execute. This role would lead online offer ideation and work across various online teams to prioritize and bring concepts to life. Role would require close partnership with Marketing offer team to establish strategic focus, a quarterly offer proposal, and high level business cases needed to secure approvals for implementation.</div>
<div></div>
<ul>
<li><div>
Role is also critical in providing the broader enterprise with expertise in how to test various offer concepts online prior to scaling.<br>
</div></li>
<li><div>
Role would require creativity in leveraging/developing online’s unique targeting and deployment abilities.<br>
</div></li>
<li><div>
Role requires familiarity with online testing tools and knowledge of AT&amp;T processes as it would require persistence in navigating concepts across AT&amp;T teams towards successfully implement.</div></li>
<li><div>
Role requires strong analytics as decisions for funding or future scaling have to be driven by results and the ability to effectively communicate those results.</div></li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<div><b>
Overall Purpose</b></div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
Responsible for the success of a product or group of products through development and execution of comprehensive marketing plan. Typically viewed as product owner throughout the life cycle. Conducts multi-year product planning to determine product life-cycle decisions, ensure consistency with overall marketing and corporate strategy, and achieve product performance, revenue, market share, and profitability objectives. Identifies, monitors and analyzes internal (e.g. financials, movement, revenue, cost, sales) and external (e.g., technology, market share, competitive) performance parameters to position, modify features and identify new uses for the product. Provides continuing product surveillance and management of established product to attain financial objectives. Accountable for revenue attainment and profitability. Leads cross functional teams in presentation and resolution of marketing deliverables as needed. Provides readout to leadership level regarding products and plans as assigned. Acts as team lead as needed to provide leadership to less experienced professionals. Considered an expert within the discipline.<br>
</div>
