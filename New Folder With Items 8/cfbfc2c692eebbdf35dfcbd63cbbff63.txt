<h2>Fitness Membership Sales – Prime Fitness</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>CORPORATE SUMMARY – June 2019 </b></p>
<p>Tivity Health®, Inc (tivityhealthcom) is a leading provider of health improvement, nutrition, fitness and social engagement solutions at scale to improve clinical outcomes, reduce healthcare costs and create opportunities to feel better, work better and live better. With decades of clinical and operational expertise, the company touches millions of consumers through its integrated portfolio of brands and works directly with hundreds of healthcare practitioners and many of the nation’s largest payers and employers. Tens of millions of Americans are currently eligible for Tivity Health’s SilverSneakers®, Fitness, WholeHealth Living™ and flip50™ programs and millions of people have lost weight with Nutrisystem®, South Beach Diet® and DNA BodyBlueprint™. As part of its commitment to tackling social isolation and loneliness, in 2017, Tivity Health launched a rural aging initiative to address challenges unique to older adults in rural communities.</p>
<p><b>Position Summary: </b></p>
<p>This position is responsible for enrolling new members, retaining and advising existing members of preventive care and other benefits they are entitled to through their plan coverage. Our Member Services Advisor empowers members to take charge of their health and wellness needs and get the most value from their plan. This person needs to demonstrate an ability to quickly identify member needs, draw out concerns and use persuasive skills to enroll or retain plan participants on either inbound or outbound calls when applicable. Must be motivated to generate sales and consistently provide exceptional customer service in a highly competitive call center environment. Average number of calls could be 30+ / daily. Calls will vary based on time of year.</p>
<p><b>Essential Duties and Responsibilities</b>: Other duties may be assigned by supervisor or management. In the event of absence, duties for this position will be overseen by the position to which it reports.</p>
<ul>
<li>Answer incoming calls to enroll prospects from qualified leads for Prime Fitness and use persuasive sales skills to retain plan members</li>
<li>Build rapport with members to identify need &amp; recommend appropriate solutions</li>
<li>Answer questions and provide guidance to new members on amenities, facilities, billing etc.</li>
<li>Assist, support, or offer alternate solutions to members who wish to cancel</li>
<li>Be considerate of challenges faced by callers or members and use good judgment when handling sensitive information</li>
<li>Handle each member interaction with courtesy, professionalism and respect to guarantee exceptional service while supporting company values and policies</li>
<li>Disclose to members who enroll, the required terms and conditions for all applicable plans</li>
<li>Meet or exceed all monthly defined targets or quotas as issued by supervisors or director. Metrics include but are not limited to % of enrollments, member retention rates, up sells &amp; revenues generated</li>
<li>Metrics are evaluated throughout the year reflective of our business</li>
<li>Be an advocate of Tivity Health, Inc and the member by doing what is best for both parties</li>
<li>Other duties may be assigned by supervisor or management</li>
</ul>
