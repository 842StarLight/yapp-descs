<h2>New Business Development</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p><b>Position Summary:</b> Bluewater Media℠ is growing direct response advertising agency building a New Business Development team. This team will be responsible for prospecting and developing new business opportunities, negotiating/closing business deals and maintaining extensive knowledge of current market conditions.<br>
As a senior sales professional, it is their job to work with the internal team to increase sales opportunities and thereby maximize revenue for Bluewater Media℠. In addition, it is important to help manage the relationship with the clients they bring in to ensure they stay satisfied and positive about the services and deliverables provided by our Team.</p>
<p>
They should have excellent verbal and written communication skills, with a proven record in generating significant revenues.</p>
<p><b>
Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Generate leads/cold calling to ensure a robust new business pipeline</li>
<li>Efficiently maintain regular contact with existing prospects</li>
<li>Identify potential clients, and the decision makers within the client organization</li>
<li>Set up meetings between client decision makers and company’s leaders/Principals</li>
<li>Build and maintain strong relationships with your clients</li>
<li>Cross sell additional Bluewater℠ services to your clients</li>
<li>Independently plan and implement initial services and capabilities pitches</li>
<li>Work with the internal team to develop proposals that address the client’s challenges, needs and objectives</li>
<li>Close new business and met sales goals in first 12-15 months of employment</li>
<li>Forecast sales targets and ensure they are met by you and the team. Create weekly/monthly status reports to business leaders/Principals to track progress against sales revenue goals</li>
<li>Using knowledge of the market and competitors, identify and develop the company’s unique selling propositions and differentiators</li>
<li>Identify industry categories to prospect. Assist in developing marketing materials required</li>
<li>Attend industry events and conferences. Set meetings with decisions makers ahead of time. Be prepared</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Job Requirements:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>5-10 years advertising agency and/or sales experience. Direct Response preferred.</li>
<li>2-3 years TV Media and Creative sales experience</li>
<li>Must have existing business contacts and relationships</li>
<li>Strong writing and verbal skill. Attention to detail</li>
<li>Strategic thinker</li>
</ul>
