<h2>Sr. ScrumMaster</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Help us make Health Care better for our members! As a <b>ScrumMaster</b> on the Product Development, Sales and Marketing team, you will be supporting scrum teams working with many different customers across the organization. You will apply Agile principles, practices and values to lead one or more cross-functional Scrum teams.</p>
<p>As a servant leader you will coach your team(s) through the delivery lifecycle and relentlessly advocate for continuous improvement in solution quality as well as the team’s Agile and technical practices. Premera is undergoing a technology transformation and you will help drive the future of applications. A successful candidate will not only drive for success in their individual teams!</p>
<p><b>What you’ll do </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Guide and coach the team and organization to follow Agile/Scrum principles, values and practices</li>
<li>Grow our Agile maturity within development team members as well as our product manager and product owners</li>
<li>Coach the Product Manager in the management of the Portfolio backlog with respect to priorities and scope</li>
<li>Implement scaling best practices</li>
<li>Identify gaps and develop new practices and measures to demonstrate a complete and accurate picture while coaching the organization to adopt and use the practices and measures appropriately</li>
<li>Collaborate with other Scrum Masters/Project Manager to deliver functioning software together</li>
<li>Identify, manage and escalate issues and risks to</li>
<li>Prevent and remove impediments and distractions that interfere with the team or organization’s ability to deliver on their goals</li>
<li>Foster team health, creating a trusting, safe and collaborative environment; guiding and coaching the team to become self-organized and empowered</li>
<li>Coach teams through decision making, problem solving, and conflict resolution to improve methods and practices</li>
</ul>
<p>Job Type: Full-time</p>
<p>Experience:</p>
<ul>
<li>Agile principles: 4 years (Required)</li>
<li>Coaching: 4 years (Required)</li>
<li>SAFe Methodology: 2 years (Required)</li>
<li>ScrumMaster: 4 years (Required)</li>
</ul>
<p>License:</p>
<ul><li>ScrumMaster (Required)</li></ul>
<p>Work authorization:</p>
<ul><li>United States (Required)</li></ul>
