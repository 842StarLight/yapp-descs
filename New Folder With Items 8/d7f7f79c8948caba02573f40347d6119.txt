<h2>Sign Shop Operations and Client Relationship Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Well-established branding/advertising company in the Northern Chicago suburbs is looking for a full-time manager, who is multi-talented, possessing exceptional interpersonal and communication skills along with graphic design experience to complement our small, dynamic team. The ideal candidate must be proficient in Adobe Illustrator, be creative, analytical, organized, detail-oriented, self-motivated, and operate with a positive attitude. The position will involve both graphic design, and customer communications and support.
</p>
<p>Responsibilities include, but are not limited to building and maintaining customer relationships, conceptualizing and designing signage and other marketing materials, organizing and managing client engagements, social media, and marketing/admin tasks, as well as:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Manage all customer communications and relationships from initial call to product delivery.</li>
<li>
Organize daily activities and tasks for yourself as well as for team members.</li>
<li>
Produce appropriate job quotes for bids and ensure information is clearly communicated both verbally and in written form to clients.
</li>
<li>Prepare design proofs for clients and then launch into production</li>
<li>
Determine best branding solutions through client phone interviews and on-site visits</li>
<li>
Coordinate all in-house production processes</li>
<li>
Track sales volumes</li>
<li>
Meet all project deadlines</li>
<li>
Work independently as a key team player</li>
</ul>
<p>
Credentials: Graphic design or related degree a definite advantage. Strong customer service skills a must.</p>
<p>
Please submit a resume. WE ARE NOT ACCEPTING PHONE CALLS.
</p>
<p>ABOUT US:<br>
Our local Signarama store serves as the brand building and visibility experts in our local business communities. We don't just sell signs: We provide extensive corporate marketing experience to help deliver solutions for clients to build brand awareness and grow their businesses.
</p>
<div>Signarama got its start in 1986 when father and son team Roy and Ray Titus opened a store in Farmingdale, New York. After the store showed signs of success, they decided to open a store in North Palm Beach, Florida. In 1987, Signarama began franchising and has been a prominent force in the sign industry. Signarama was named the No.1 sign franchise in the 2014 Franchise 500 in Entrepreneur magazine.</div>
