<h2>Beverage Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div><div><div><div>
<p>We love what we do – and it shows! Time Out Market is the division of Time Out that brings our food and cultural experiences to life for our guests, readers and clients.</p>
<p><b><i>Responsibilities</i></b></p>
<ul><li> Coordinate the development and implementation of beverage programs that support concept clarity and take advantage of local beverage opportunities</li></ul>
<ul><li> Ensure the implementation of all TOM annual beverage program initiatives</li></ul>
<ul><li> Oversee and participate in the hiring, training, supervision, management, coaching, counseling, and evaluation of TOM bar staff</li></ul>
<ul><li> Provide necessary beverage training with emphasis on sales, service, and product knowledge to ensure operational success</li></ul>
<ul><li> Create concept appropriate beverage lists enhancement, recipes and menu collateral</li></ul>
<ul><li> In conjunction with the location Alcohol Safety Coordinator, maintain on-site Responsible Alcohol Service training
</li></ul>
<ul><li> Control and Provide the necessary beverage cost management tools to operations for successful cost management</li></ul>
<ul><li> Ensure beverage cost and operational efficiency and great guest beverage experience by providing standard operating procedures and ongoing training for team members</li></ul>
<ul><li> Obtain beverage sales targets and controls assigned cost
</li></ul>
<ul><li> Ensure local competitive pricing and product through regular research
</li></ul>
<ul><li> Ensure all security, safety, and sanitation standards are met</li></ul>
<ul><li> Work with local suppliers to ensure ‘most favored’ pricing, discounting and allocation</li></ul>
<ul><li> Ensure continuity of retail pricing throughout location</li></ul>
<ul><li> Work with Market managers and team members to ensure proper product control and handling of all inventory and equipment is practiced</li></ul>
<ul><li> Maintain and perpetuate strong partnerships with vendors, and assists in the development of mutually beneficial programs</li></ul>
<ul><li> Liaise with the General Manager and able to assist in various property operations, training and development as requested</li></ul>
<ul><li> Interact with and support Market management team through active communication and participation in meetings</li></ul>
<ul><li> Maintain regular contact with team members and Market concessionaires to promote a positive guest experience</li></ul>
<ul><li> Promote a cooperative work environment while maximizing productivity and morale</li></ul>
<ul><li> Maintain all beverage standards and ensure the highest standards of food and beverage quality, guest service, cost control, and consistency in accordance with TOM expectations</li></ul>
<ul><li> Delegate responsibility to the management team as needed and enforce existing policy consistently</li></ul>
<ul><li> Oversee the weekly schedule for both TOM staff and contracted staff
</li></ul>
<ul><li> Excellent communication skills required, both verbally and in writing, to provide clear direction to the management and service teams</li></ul>
<ul><li> Assign and instruct the employees and managers in the details of their work; observe performance and encourage improvement where necessary</li></ul>
<ul><li> Operate with an open-door policy, listen to and understand requests of the team, respond with appropriate actions, and provide accurate information</li></ul>
<ul><li> Remain calm and alert, especially during emergency situations and/or heavy restaurant activity, serving as a role model for the management team and other TOM and Vendor employees</li></ul>
<ul><li> Interact with all department personnel, restaurant staff and Vendor staff as needed</li></ul>
<ul><li> Ensure inventory levels are maintained for facilitating proper restaurant operations, enforce strict inventory controls, and participate in monthly inventory reconciliations in conjunction with purchasing and finance teams</li></ul>
<ul><li> Make decisions and take actions based on previous experience and good judgment, sometimes revising procedures to accommodate unusual situations</li></ul>
<ul><li> Maintain positive and professional relations with vendors, managing vendor interaction on the property with our staff</li></ul>
<ul><li> Ensure health, safety, and sanitation requirements are in compliance with the Department of Health, OSHA, and any city or state agencies such as the Department of Buildings and the Fire Department</li></ul>

<ul><li> Ability to lead by example</li></ul>
<ul><li> Ability to act in a professional manner always</li></ul>

<ul><li> Ability to demonstrate a positive attitude always</li></ul>
<ul><li> Ability to keep an open and objective view</li></ul>
<ul><li> Ability to listen empathetically and be respectful always</li></ul>

<p><b>Company Overview – Time Out Market</b></p>
<p>Time Out Group comprises two business divisions. Time Out Digital and Time Out Market. In 2014, Time Out Market Lisbon opened in Lisbon – a food and cultural market bringing the best of the city together under one roof: its best restaurants, bars and cultural experiences based on editorial curation. In 2017, Time Out Market Lisbon saw 3.6 million visitors. Now Time Out Group is rolling the format out to other cities as part of its growth strategy. Time Out Market Miami is set to open in 2018, Boston and Chicago in 2019 and the Group is scoping locations around the globe.</p>
