<h2>Salesforce - Midwest Practice Leader</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Slalom is a purpose-driven consulting firm that helps companies solve business problems and build for the future, with solutions spanning business advisory, customer experience, technology, and analytics. We partner with companies to push the boundaries of what’s possible—together.</p>
<p>
Founded in 2001 and headquartered in Seattle, WA, Slalom has grown organically to more than 6,000 employees. We were named one of Fortune’s 100 Best Companies to Work For in 2018, and we are regularly recognized by our employees as a best place to work. You can find us in 27 cities and 7 technology Build Centers across the U.S., U.K., and Canada.</p>
<p>
As a Platinum Salesforce partner, we help our clients transform the way they connect with their customers. Our team is made up of Salesforce-certified experts with experience across the Salesforce Customer Success Platform and multiple industries. As the 3rd largest global Salesforce partner, we are committed to growing and developing our Salesforce talent, offering continued growth opportunities and exposing our people to interesting and diverse projects</p>
<p><b>
Job Title: Practice Lead</b></p>
<p>
Slalom Consulting is looking for an entrepreneurial and progressive leader to lead the growth and success of our Midwest Regional Salesforce team based in Chicago. The Practice Leader will provide thought leadership to our enterprise clients, set strategic direction for the Salesforce practitioners on the client site, and generally be accountable for ensuring the overall quality execution of business and technology consulting across all Salesforce engagements.</p>
<p><b>
Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Accountability for the practice, including driving quality, sales, recruiting, account management, consulting, and all operational aspects, including:<ul>
<li>
Practice Building – Drive overall growth of practice area through a combination of business development, talent management, oversight of delivery work, and thought leadership.</li>
<li>
Team &amp; Individual Development – Maximize team performance through an effective team approach that increases productivity and job satisfaction.</li>
<li>
Engagement Management – Manage engagement risk, project economics including planning and budgeting, managing accounts receivable, defining deliverable content, and ensuring buy-in of proposed solutions from top management levels at the client.</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>
50–60% target utilization at the designated enterprise client account</li>
<li>
Responsible for the profitability of all Salesforce offerings, with revenue management expectations of $2–5 million per year.</li>
<li>
Identifies opportunities for growth and maturation of Salesforce offerings</li>
<li>
Provides oversight and governance of all sold and managed Salesforce projects</li>
<li>
Drives business development with the proper information, tools, and subject matter expertise to sell engagements within the offering</li>
<li>
Builds and develops relationship / partnership with local market teams, aligning on sales pursuits, resource capacity and capabilities, and awareness across the region.</li>
<li>
Builds and develops relationships with Salesforce and owns joint sales pursuits</li>
<li>
Develops or supports the development of case studies and training materials; conducts brown bags and provides guidance for Salesforce Practice</li>
<li>
Develops or supports the development and delivery of best practices, delivery templates, and point-of-view papers</li>
<li>
Maintains awareness of industry best practices and business levers for cloud offerings and understands how Slalom practice areas interact</li>

Oversees quality assurance of project delivery</li>
