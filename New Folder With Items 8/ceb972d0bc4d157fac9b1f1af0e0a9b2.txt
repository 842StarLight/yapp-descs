<h2>Junior Sales Associate</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>The <b>Junior Sales Associate </b>position is a valued team member in the marketing and sales department. The position is considered entry level to start during training which includes a comprehensive overview of basic marketing strategy, direct advertising, and promotional sales techniques. After successful completion of the program, an individual would be considered a team lead in the marketing and sales department.</p>
<p><b>Why we win business: </b>Our customers love us. We're one of the highest-rated marketing and advertising firms for our local and national clients. You'll be <i>proud </i>to be apart of our team.</p>
<p><b>Hot leads: </b>We do not cold call. Every prospect wants to speak with you and every prospect wants a face-to-face interaction. Your job is to enlighten them why we are the BEST.</p>
<p><b>Sweet compensation: </b>We offer a generous compensation, with uncapped commissions. Every Sales team member has the ability to earn industry leading commissions, bonuses, and prizes.</p>
<p><b>Career path: </b>Our Sales management, including the Director of Sales Training, started as Sales Associates. Our Sales hires have also advanced into other corporate departments. If you're looking for a career, our team is the clear choice.</p>
<p><b>Tools to reach your goals: </b>We'll provide everything you need to succeed: paid training from the best in the business, a steady stream of warm leads, weekly coaching and world-class analytics We'll make you an expert in your field, so you'll sell with confidence.</p>
<p><b>Responsibilities</b>:</p>
<p>· Perform full-cycle sales role with a focus on closing sales</p>
<p>· Using a consultative sales approach</p>
<p>· Set appointments and follow up with prospects via phone</p>
<p>· Maintain a high degree of product and service expertise</p>
<p>· Promote and enhance company reputation, providing professional, effective assistance</p>
<p><b>Qualifications</b>:</p>
<p>· There's no one "right" profile. If you're a superstar now, or one in the making, let's talk</p>
<p>· High level of motivation to achieve personal goals and improve earning potential</p>
<p>· Strong communication skills, with the ability to engage and persuade</p>
