<p><b>Your Challenge</b></p>
<p>
HighQ is looking for an experienced Enterprise Sales Executive based in Portland, who has demonstrated the ability to establish, lead and grow revenues for industry leading software solution providers. Your ability to win new business, gain "C" Level access and sell complex solutions will be key factors in your success. You’ll be expected to leverage this skillset and experience with a business that is experiencing exceptional growth with technology and revolutionizing the way organisations run their business!</p>
<p><b>
Your Role</b></p>

<li>You will have significant experience navigating through complex organisations and selling to multiple decision makers, including the “C Level” and specifically CIOs and CMOs</li>

<li>Enterprise / SaaS Sales</li>

<p><b>About Us</b></p>
<p>
HighQ provides innovative enterprise collaboration and content management solutions to the world’s leading law firms, financial services companies, governments and corporations. The company’s blue-chip client base includes over 40% of the global top 100 law firms and some of the largest global financial institutions. HighQ combines secure, enterprise-grade technology with an amazing user experience to transform the way businesses collaborate, communicate and share information securely in the cloud. Founded in 2001, HighQ is headquartered in London, with offices in New York, Kansas, Portland, Amsterdam, Frankfurt, Sydney and Ahmedabad. Having seen considerable growth in recent years, we are looking to expand our sales team and are seeking talented individuals to join the team in the worldwide so that we can continue to meet the requirements of a fast growing business.</p>
