<h2>Video/Multimedia Marketing Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div></div>
<div>
<div>
<div>Command Alkon is bringing innovative technologies and systems to the concrete, asphalt and building materials industry globally. Our technology helps our customers improve the efficiency and quality of the foundation of where we live, work and play. What we do best is combine next-gen technologies with over 40 years of industry expertise. We are not a “here today; gone tomorrow” software company, we will be here for a long time and a good foundation for your career…. like concrete.<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<br>
<div><b>The Role</b></div>
<div>
Command Alkon is searching for a Video/Multimedia Marketing Specialist with demonstrated success in captivating an audience and driving content engagement through video and multimedia creation. We are looking for someone who is self-motivated, enthusiastic, results-driven, consistent, takes project ownership, and embraces a healthy competitive mind-set.
</div>
<div></div>
<div>Candidates who possess expertise in creating and managing digital multimedia assets, along with the ability to work with a fast-paced team, will find this position highly rewarding. If you are a creative with a knack for telling stories through self-shot video footage and eye-popping motion graphics, while also holding exceptional editing skills and a wish to work on a highly collaborative team – this position is for you.
</div>
<div></div>
<div><b>Scope</b></div>
<div>
Reporting to the Senior Creative Designer, the Video/Multimedia Marketing Specialist is responsible for the development, growth, quality, and performance of Command Alkon’s multimedia assets. Experience with shooting, editing, and production of videos, podcasts, web-streaming, webinars, digital photography, and other multimedia-oriented content are critical to the role’s success. The role will also help measure asset performance KPIs for continuous improved direction and resource allocation recommendations.</div>
</div>
<div><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Skills Required</b></h3>
<div><ul>
<li>
An associate degree or above in marketing, multimedia, video, or related discipline.</li>
<li>
2+ years of video and multimedia experience.</li>
<li>
Professional experience with Final Cut/Premiere Pro, After Effects, WordPress, and some HTML5.</li>
<li>
Extensive skills in video production, video editing, audio production, and digital content creation</li>
<li>
Experience with video output, hosting, and collaboration tools.</li>
<li>
Knowledge of live-streaming production and technologies is highly desired.</li>
<li>
Hardware and software confidence to lead the entire life-cycle of a video project.</li>
</ul></div>
</div></div>
<div><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Who you are
</b></h3>
<div><ul>
<li>You are excited to work in a high output, fast paced, collaborative team environment.</li>
<li>
You hold strong project management skills, a will to be hands-on during projects, and you can manage multiple projects simultaneously with seamless execution.
</li>
<li>You have strong storytelling skills and a natural ability to apply creativity, innovation, and offer unique perspective on projects.</li>
<li>
You are able to embrace unanticipated projects within tight deadlines with passion and enthusiasm.</li>
<li>
You carry analytical skills, professional communication skills, and an efficiency for creative problem solving.</li>
<li>
You are technology focused and have a passion for learning.</li>
<li>
You’re excited to work for a global organization and would thrive despite language and cultural differences.</li>
<li>
You can flex your schedule as needed and are comfortable with some travel.</li>
<div>
</div>
</ul></div>
</div></div>
<div><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>A Day in the Life
</b></h3>
<div><ul>
<li>Execute Command Alkon’s journey to modernize and digitize its content marketing strategy through multimedia-rich assets.</li>
<li>
Ensure the successful creation and management of a multimedia-rich content library, fueling marketing campaigns, sales enablement, automation, and personalization.</li>
<li>
Collaborate with other Command Alkon team members – with focus on Marketing, Sales, Product Management, and Customer Success teams - to ensure participation in the creation and execution of the overall multimedia content strategy</li>
<li>
Help surface and accelerate sales opportunities with existing customers, in addition to attracting new customers, through insight-rich content, storytelling, emotive connections, and multisensory experiences.</li>
<li>
Turn performance data into insights to monitor and measure the effectiveness of multimedia assets.</li>
<li>
Understand customer, market, and industry dynamics to identify new and prioritize existing assets, channels, and devices.</li>
<li>
Deploy creative thinking and storytelling, with the ability to identify and develop story ideas to cover, capture, and edit for both internal and external audiences.</li>
</ul></div>
