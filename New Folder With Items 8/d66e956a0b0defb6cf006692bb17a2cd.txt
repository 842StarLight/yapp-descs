<h1 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Digital Marketing Specialist, Paid Search &amp; SEO
</b></h1>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>The Role
</b></h2>
<p>In this role, you’ll be responsible for the development of SEO, SEM and analytics strategies to help generate quality leads for our clients. We are looking for the person that wants to help build a program around their knowledge.
</p>
<p></p>
<p>You’ll have a unique opportunity to join a growing and dynamic team focused on being the premier marketing agency for the entire club industry. Your contribution to the Marketing Services team will play a direct and critical role in driving the entire business forward.
</p>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities
</b></h2>
<ul>
<li>Support Marketing Services Manager, Team Lead in all aspects of Search Engine Optimization (SEO), Search Engine Marketing (SEM), and Web Analytics. Collaborate with clients on website content to improve keyword rankings and organic search traffic
</li>
<li>Conceptualize, develop, implement, monitor and report on various marketing campaign initiatives
</li>
<li>Competition analysis in SEO, PPC and Social Media to produce areas of opportunity
</li>
<li>Stay up-to-date on both onsite/offsite best practices for global SEO and white hat/black hat SEO strategies to ensure website search best practices to avoid penalizations.
</li>
<li>Analytics:
<ul>
<li>Monitor and analyze client sites and organic search results to provide proactive recommendations and adjustments based on objectives.
</li>
<li>Monitor website analytics to develop recommendations to increase conversions
</li>
<li>Develop timely reports/audits/dashboards that present areas of optimization
</li>
<li>Evaluate performance across a variety of SEO tools
</li>
<li>Server work - Building sitemaps in HTML &amp; XML and robots.txt
</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>Run one-time seo projects for new implementation websites
</li>
<li>Monitor site health and use tools to track error pages and implement proper redirects
</li>
<li>Develop backlinking strategies that work for our clients where appropriate
</li>
<li>Optimize internal linking structures and site architecture to maximize rankings
</li>
<li>Own paid media efforts across all channels (including paid search, paid social, and display
</li>
<li>Set up keyword lists, campaigns, ad copy and optimize landing pages
</li>
<li>Own ongoing optimization efforts including daily management of campaign budgets, on-site conversion actions, ad creative, bid management and negative keyword strategies
</li>
<li>Growing new quality leads by converting site traffic through calls-to-action, landing pages, and lead generation content
</li>
<li>Executing tests/QA, collecting and analyzing data, identifying trends and insights in order to achieve maximum ROI in paid search campaigns
</li>
<li>Proactively make campaign and keyword optimization changes as needed
</li>
</ul>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Qualifications
</b></h2>
<ul>
<li>3+ years experience in Search Engine Optimization (SEO) and Paid Search (PPC, Social Media)
</li>
<li>Excellent understanding of technical on-page SEO including structured data, rich text snippets, and content mark-up
</li>
<li>Familiar with website optimization tools like MOZ
</li>
<li>Google Search Console/Webmaster/Tag Manager/Google Analytics experience
</li>
<li>Familiarity with display advertising and remarketing (Google Ads, Bing Ads)
</li>
<li>Basic experience with HTML, and Content Management Systems (CMS)
</li>
<li>Desire to grow in digital marketing career
</li>
<li>Outstanding ability to think creatively, strategically, and identify and resolve problems
</li>
<li>Collaborative team player, working across various teams within the organization
</li>
<li>Strong attention to detail, organized and goal-oriented
</li>
<li>Confident client-facing skills
</li>
<li>Can effectively cope with change/ambiguity and shift gears comfortably
</li>
<li>Has solid understanding about which creative ideas will work and has the ability to bring those ideas to market
</li>
<li>Sought out by others for advice and solutions based on strong decision quality .
</li>
<li>Enjoys learning on the fly . Learns quickly when facing new problems; analyzes both successes and failures for clues to improvement; enjoys the challenge of unfamiliar tasks
</li>
<li>Strong perseverance : pursues everything with energy, drive, and a need to finish; seldom gives up before finishing
</li>
<li>Self-motivated for personal development . Is committed to and actively works to continuously improve him/herself
</li>
</ul>
