<h2>Design Drafter</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<div><div><div>
<p><b>SUMMARY:</b></p>
<div>Drafts detailed manufacturing drawings and specifications used in the manufacture of electro-mechanical food processing equipment and related devices by performing the following duties.</div>
<p><b>ESSENTIAL DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES: </b>include the following. Other duties may be assigned.</p>
<ul>
<li>Reviews rough sketches, drawings, specifications, and other engineering data received from Design Engineers, Engineering Technicians, and/or Project Engineers.</li>
<li>Drafts detailed drawings of all necessary elements of a required design including but not limited to assemblies, sub-assemblies, weldments, sheet metal flat patterns, and installation drawings.</li>
<li>Computes weights of components, center of gravity, speed, and stroke using computer aided design tools.</li>
<li>Develops detailed Bills of Materials.</li>
<li>Works in cooperation with Sales, Sales Engineers, Manufacturing Engineering, Manufacturing, External Customers, and Suppliers as required.</li>
<li>Works with infrequent supervision.</li>
<li>Must recognize areas of technical requirements that need the advice and/or attention of qualified personnel.</li>
<br>
</ul>

<div>4. Machinery which may start and stop without warning.
