<p><b>The Role
</b></p>
<p></p>
<p>We are seeking an experienced, innovative thought partner with exceptional managerial and analytical skills to join our team as Manager, FP&amp;A - Revenue/Billings/SaaS. This is a highly visible role that will lead a team of analysts tasked with driving all top-line forecasting and analysis as it relates to short/long-term revenue, billings, expected renewals, and customer retention. The opportunity reports to the Director, Financial Planning &amp; Analysis, and will work to develop, manage, and refine the design of company-wide revenue, billings, and other top-line performance related reporting processes. You should be comfortable working with executives across multiple business units (Finance, Accounting, Sales, Sales Operations, Etc.) to identify, define, scope, solve, and implement scale-able processes to help meet the businesses growing demands.
</p>
<p></p>
<p>The role requires an execution-focused candidate who can quickly become familiar with our SaaS business and associated metrics and is able to support meaningful business decisions through in-depth analysis coupled with sound business judgment. The right culture fit will balance a process-oriented, analytical approach with a strategic point of view and a mentality to win.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Your day-to-day
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Gather, consolidate, and analyze key operating, financial, and sales performance results – including in-depth analysis of revenue and bookings trends, expected renewals and customer retention , variance analysis and retention analysis in support of internal and external reporting
</li>
<li>Develop short-term and long-term revenue and billings outlook with a particular focus on internal product initiatives and appropriate accounting principles (606)
</li>
<li>Contribute to annual and quarterly expense planning and forecasting processes and long range strategic planning - Billings, Revenue, etc.
</li>
<li>Lead, motivate, and mentor growing team of financial analysts to manage and execute growing portfolio of dashboards and reporting projects
</li>
<li>Combine your analytical presence with innate soft skills to proactively initiate, develop, and maintain effective working relationships with various cross-functional leadership teams across the company
</li>
<li>Build and maintain complex business and forecast models used to drive business insights and help support informed recommendations to c-level executives – deliver the right information, to the right people, at the right time
</li>
<li>Contribute to annual and quarterly expense planning and forecasting processes and long range strategic planning - Billings, Revenue, etc.
</li>
<li>Leverage track record of process improvement by identifying new projects or continuous improvement for existing processes to do more, faster
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>What you bring to the team
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>5+ years of experience in financial analysis or comparable analytical role at a high growth public tech company - preferably SaaS software
</li>
<li>BA/BS degree with strong academic record – preferably in Economics/Business/Finance ; commensurate real-world experience in any of these areas
</li>
<li>Knowledge of revenue accounting standards including ASC 606 a plus
</li>
<li>MBA plus but not necessary
</li>
<li>Experience working in a high-growth, public tech environment (SAAS preferable)
</li>
<li>Proven experience in managing large datasets, developing insights from ambiguous data and facilitating team decisions
</li>
<li>History of execution managing project timelines and requirements from multiple stakeholders
</li>
<li>Superior statistical, analytical, and data modeling skills including advanced proficiency in Excel (pivot tables, Vlookups, etc.) and the ability to quickly manipulate and analyze large data sets and build reports
</li>
<li>Working knowledge of Salesforce.com, Microsoft 365, RevPro, and Adaptive Planning a plus
</li>
<li>Strong interpersonal, organizational, and communication skills a must
</li>
</ul>
