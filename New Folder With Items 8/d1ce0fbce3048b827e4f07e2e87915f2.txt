<b>Job Description</b>
</div>
<div>Accenture is seeking a <b>GRC Integrated Risk Consultant</b> to join <b>Accenture Security Strategy &amp; Risk</b>.
</div>
<div>
<b>Accenture’s Security Strategy and Risk</b> professionals develop and deliver solutions for managing the risk of enterprise systems, applications and data through policies and controls, risk assessments, and issues and exception management. These professionals advise clients on building robust risk management and security programs, identifying and mitigating risks, establishing security policies and practices, implementing security controls, and educating stakeholders.
</div>
<div></div>
<div><b>Key Responsibilities:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Drive high-profile and high-impact projects involving complex GRC and risk management challenges.
</li>
<li>Perform gap assessments between security and risk leading practice frameworks and clients’ risk and control frameworks and advise on remediating gaps.
</li>
<li>Create budgets, staffing requirements, and privacy and security direction for long-term, 5+ year strategies.
</li>
<li>Design and establish or transform IT risk management, governance, and compliance programs based on clients’ business structures, strategies, and priorities.
</li>
<li>Advise on, develop, and implement processes around risk identification, assessment, and remediation, including issues management, exception management, vendor risk management policy management, and security incident and vulnerability response.
</li>
<li>Advise on and assist clients to implement GRC platforms and solutions.
</li>
<li>Collaborate and lead meetings with diverse high-level stakeholders, including C-suite, IT operations, system architects, security professionals, and business leads.
</li>
<li>Develop training materials and other communications to increase employee understanding and awareness of security and risk issues.
</li>
<li>Translate business requirements into technical requirements.
</li>
<li>Communicate GRC technology capabilities into business terms for executive stakeholders.</li>
</ul>
</div>
