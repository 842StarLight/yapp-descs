<h2>Legal Analyst</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>K1 seeks a Legal Analyst for our Southern California office. The Legal Analyst will be instrumental in structuring, negotiating and executing K1’s private equity investments in leading enterprise software companies across the country and around the world. The right candidate will be able to apply both a “business” and a “legal” perspective to complex business problems on behalf of both K1 and its portfolio companies. Most importantly, the right candidate will fit the values of K1 – Passion, Persistence, Humility and Excellence.<br>
<br>
<b>Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Transaction execution - review, negotiate and draft NDAs, letters of intent, purchase agreements, corporate documents and other investment-related legal documentation</li>
<li>
Due diligence. Review and opine on critical legal due diligence documentation and risk areas</li>
<li>
Existing investments. Manage legal issues with regard to existing portfolio companies including employment, compensation, contracts and disputes</li>
<li>
Investment structure. Manage complex legal structuring involving multiple entities, investors, lenders and equity incentive holders</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Qualifications:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
JD degree with a strong academic background</li>
<li>
Active membership of an established Bar</li>
<li>
Transactional M&amp;A experience with a reputable law firm or in a top corporate or investment management legal department</li>
<li>
Significant experience in legal practice including drafting, reviewing, and negotiating a variety of investment agreements; drafting and reviewing legal memoranda; analyzing complex legal and factual issues; performing substantive legal research; and advising stakeholders</li>
<li>
Excellent interpersonal, analytical, problem-solving, and organizational skills</li>
<li>
Excellent written and verbal communication skills</li>
<li>
Excellent judgment; able to handle multiple projects and set priorities</li>
<li>
Able and willing to handle a variety of tasks across a broad range of substantive areas and to learn new areas quickly</li>
<li>
Creativity and positive attitude</li>
</ul>
<p>
K1 is a Los Angeles-based private equity investor with over $4.1B in assets under management focusing on high-growth enterprise software companies globally. We seek to help dynamic businesses achieve successful outcomes by identifying and executing organic and acquisition-based growth opportunities that can position our companies as industry leaders. Our team has been investing in high-growth software and technology companies for over 20 years. We typically invest alongside strong management teams that continue to guide their organizations on a day-to-day basis. As partners, we seek to provide meaningful strategic and financial support to help our companies reach their full potential. Our investments vary in the level of ownership in order to meet the needs of entrepreneurs and managers. We look to help our portfolio companies navigate strategic opportunities such as executive recruitment, external financing, mergers and acquisitions, strategic sales and public offerings.</p>
