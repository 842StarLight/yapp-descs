<h2>Lead Coordinator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>American Equipment Finance, a commercial lender for business equipment nationwide, is looking to hire a Lead Coordinator to help support a sales team spread across Oregon, South Dakota, and Missouri. The ideal candidate will possess exemplary written and verbal communication skills, a positive attitude, and the ability to learn and adapt with change. Candidates interested in the field of finance and with either a degree in business or a social science field OR a year of experience in an office environment will move to the top of the list.</p>
<p>The duties associated with this position include the following:</p>
<ul>
<li>Operating and managing an Artificial Intelligence marketing platform</li>
<li>Handling social media and basic customer service</li>
<li>Verifying customer application information, including credit and cash flow, to place into the proper program</li>
<li>Answering basic customer questions and obtaining signed terms sheets</li>
<li>Sending emails and followup reminders using multiple software platforms</li>
<li>Building relationships with customers and sellers of equipment and interacting with and supporting Finance Officers on transactions</li>
<li>Interacting with credit departments and understanding basic criteria for approval or decline</li>
<li>Responding to customer requests for financing and redirecting to a prequalification system to help figure out if we can help</li>
</ul>
<p>All responses will be treated with the strictest of confidence. Pay commensurate with experience.</p>
<p>Job Type: Full-time</p>
<p>Experience:</p>
<ul><li>Office: 1 year (Preferred)</li></ul>
</div>
