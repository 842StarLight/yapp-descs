<h2>Analytic Consultant 5 - Conduct Risk Analytics</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<b>Job Description</b><br>
<p>At Wells Fargo, we want to satisfy our customers’ financial needs and help them succeed financially. We’re looking for talented people who will put our customers at the center of everything we do. Join our diverse and inclusive team where you’ll feel valued and inspired to contribute your unique skills and experience.</p>
<p>
Help us build a better Wells Fargo. It all begins with outstanding talent. It all begins with you.</p>
<p>
Wholesale Banking provides financial solutions to businesses across the United States and globally. Our four major business lines include Corporate &amp; Investment Banking, Commercial Banking, Commercial Real Estate, and Wells Fargo Commercial Capital. We also have groups in credit risk, group risk, finance, marketing, human relations, and the Wholesale Chief Operating Office that support our businesses.</p>
<p>
The Conduct Risk Analytics team is part of Wholesale Strategic Insights in the Wholesale Insights &amp; Reporting (WI&amp;R) organization. This team will support Wholesale and Enterprise initiatives for improved and consistent risk management for Conduct Risks (Allegations, Ethics, Sales Practices, etc). This team partners closely with Wholesale Conduct Risk and this position will be a senior analytic consultant responsible for analytics in support of the Wholesale Conduct Risk strategy. This role will be crucial in helping devise and execute measurement of KRI (Key Risk Indicator) and KPI metrics around this program. This role will also be responsible for analytical statistical modeling to help establish additional/adapt existing metrics and KPIs/KRIs in an ever-changing environment.</p>
<p>
Additionally, this team member will need to engage with the different LOBs within Wholesale as well as Front Line and Independent Risk Management (IRM) teams to ensure alignment of Conduct Risk metrics across the business. The role will require flexibility and adaptability to different leadership styles of its many business partners. It is imperative this team member is able to work with disparate data sources and understand how to bring this data together so results are consistent and can be rolled up to the top level.</p>
<p>
This will be a highly visible (and scrutinized) job function so the team member must exhibit exemplary analytic accuracy, communication and presentation skills. This team member must be able to take complex concepts and summarize them concisely and creatively for an executive audience. This also requires the team member to excel at strategic thinking to recognize the most salient information quickly, and possess creativity to display this information in a visually pleasing, impactful, often innovative way. The team member must have strong verbal communication skills and be comfortable speaking to business partners at all levels of the organization.<br>
Specific responsibilities of this role include (but are not limited to):</p>
<ul>
<li>Identification, monitoring, and reporting of KRIs, KPIs, and other metrics, including sourcing information for analytics development</li>
<li>Root cause analytics to provide clarity as to the underlying drivers of performance</li>
<li>Partner with Data Management &amp; Insights (DMI) and Strategic Infrastructure teams to provide requirements for a data model through. This will require acquiring a robust understanding of multiple data sources both inside and outside of Wholesale.</li>
<li>Establish relationships with Wholesale Conduct Risk Control and other Wholesale LOB leaders through ongoing engagement related to the formation and subsequent measurement and analytics of Conduct Risk metrics.</li>
<li>Remaining consistent with the broader Wells Fargo risk and control standards, build key metrics and reporting around Conduct Risk for each of the Wholesale LOBs. Maintain consistency in reporting for all LOBs while still addressing the unique concerns of each, to ensure the ability to aggregate performance at the Wholesale Business Group.</li>
<li>Devise new and measurable Conduct Risk metrics, where appropriate, exceed the guidelines set at the Enterprise level. Utilize advanced analytics to track the success of these implementations and adjust accordingly.</li>
<li>Partner with the WI&amp;R reporting team to design executive dashboards with interactive functionality allowing management to track their KRIs in real time.</li>
</ul>
<p>
Our preferred locations are Charlotte, Des Moines or Minneapolis; however, other Wells Fargo hubs may be considered.</p>
<p>
Team members support our focus on building strong customer relationships balanced with a strong risk mitigating and compliance-driven culture which firmly establishes those disciplines as critical to the success of our customers and company. They are accountable for execution of all applicable risk programs (Credit, Market, Financial Crimes, Operational, Regulatory Compliance), which includes effectively following, and adhering to and if applicable Wells Fargo policies and procedures, appropriately fulfilling risk and compliance obligations, timely and effective escalation and remediation of issues, and making sound risk decisions. There is emphasis on proactive monitoring, governance, risk identification and escalation, as well as making sound risk decisions commensurate with the business unit’s risk appetite, and all risk and compliance program requirements.</p>
