<p><b>Profile Summary:
</b></p>
<p></p>
<p>Leads the development and management of brand guidelines, programs and platforms to ensure understanding of, adherence to and availability of brand-approved assets and processes for use by the company and its distribution partners.
</p>
<p>Assists in leading the creation of the company’s strategic road map for growing its brand. Leads the execution of all brand-related activities comprising the totality of the brand’s cultural, client-facing and employee-engaging visual identity, graphic expressions, voice, messaging, narratives and experiences. Manages an internal team focused on advancing Voya’s brand awareness, familiarity, favorability, engagement and advocacy — at the enterprise level and in support of its business segments.
</p>
<p>Responsible for establishing and ensuring compliance to brand standards in order to maintain and protect the equity of the brand. Provides leadership relative to the development, roll-out and oversight of brand management guidelines, programs and platforms
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Profile Description:
</b></p>
<p></p>
<p>1. Ensure consistent alignment of the brand with the organization’s goals, mission and brand promise.
<br>
2. Manage brand standards, brand architecture, naming conventions, logos and other visual and voice identifiers.
<br>
3. Partner with business marketing, functional leads, compliance, legal, and brand and communications teams to align marketing communications, both internal and external, with brand standards and guidelines.
<br>
4. Responsible for the development and updating of brand guidelines, identity standards, brand assets and brand tools (covering traditional and digital channels) and the training of all users in how to apply them.
<br>
5. Manage brand repositories and tools and act as primary brand lead on shared repositories (eg, Brand Central, Ad Central, AdMaster, VMZ, etc.)
<br>
6. Manage brand strategy initiatives to include: brand culture, brand identity, brand positioning, brand awareness and supporting proof points, and brand governance.
<br>
7. Act as a key promoter of the Voya brand, ensuring consistent brand expression through all client and cultural touch points, across all external and internal audiences and platforms.
<br>
8. Strategic Integration – Help influence the digital marketing and sponsorship strategies to ensure brand consistency and ability to grow brand value.
<br>
9. Enact deliverables for ongoing programs to engage employees in dialogue about the Voya brand and its implications for the behaviors and experiences required to support the company’s overarching brand promise.
<br>
10. Help establish guidance for all creative marketing communications and collateral across all channels, including: print, digital, video, brochures, reports, marketing materials, templates and all related branded communications vehicles.
<br>
11. Actively support a brand “center of excellence” that the organization can rely upon for consistent collateral support, messaging guidance, brand strategy integration, sales or recruiting kits, and to operationalize client insights.
<br>
12. Manage all processes, including vendors, relating to the production of brand applications, such as business cards and stationery, forms, signage, merchandise and associated statutory or discretionary uses of the visual brand.
<br>
13. Provide strategic and tactical brand and creative-services support for key company initiatives.
<br>
14. Budget/Financial Management – Determine appropriate investment levels that are consistent with the overall needs of the enterprise and business goals.
<br>
15. Other duties and projects as assigned.
</p>
