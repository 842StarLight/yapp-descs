<h2>iTero Marketing, Associate Director</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>This position will be responsible for driving the penetration and usage of the iTero intraoral scanner in the General Practitioner (GP) Channel. Specifically, the individual will develop and execute GP strategy and program(s) that strengthen our product utilization while redefining and delivering our value proposition.</p>
<p>
This person must be customer-centric and a self-starter with proven ability to execute and work independently. She/he must work effectively cross-functionally, leading collaborative efforts with sales, clinical education, legal, and outside agencies. Role includes all aspects of marketing such as positioning, messaging, creative development, sales force partnership &amp; enablement, pricing and promotions, trade shows, and key opinion leader programs.</p>
<p>
The position will also work in close partnership with the Director of iTero Marketing and extended team in managing projects from commercialization, tracking and analyzing business results, using sound strategic judgment, working well in cross-functional teams, proactively recommending improvements, and communicating effectively.</p>
<p></p>
<p><b><i>
ESSENTIAL DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES</i></b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Strategic leadership of assigned product commercialization programs for the iTero portfolio.</li>
<li>
Become the market expert for the iTero product portfolio for the GP Channel.</li>
<li>
Keep abreast of the latest market trends and the wider macro-environment and maintain awareness of how this may affect the iTero product and broader business.</li>
<li>
Develop and maintain intimate knowledge of clinical techniques, scanning techniques and workflows that relate to the use of the iTero system by the general dentist, hygienist, and laboratory.</li>
<li>
Act as the ‘champion’ for new product commercialization endeavors internally, providing support and training to all internal stakeholders in the company to aid in the learning and uptake of critical information related to new innovations.</li>
<li>
Transform the voice of the customer into business and commercialization plans.</li>
<li>
Develop commercialization strategy and programs that effectively launch programs to commercial availability and life cycle management. This includes creative development of print and digital collateral for dental professionals and patients, media planning, and effective management of associated budgets to support launch and commercialization efforts.</li>
<li>
Understand customer mindset and barriers to adoption &amp; utilization, and develop and drive successful marketing programs that ladder to business objectives and drive business plan.</li>
<li>
Responsible for developing and executing scalable pilot initiatives with our sales partners that drive GP channel strategy</li>
<li>
Serves as primary contact and marketing support for GP-specific sales training and selling structure.</li>
<li>
Manages a range of projects that support our strategic and tactical efforts, including market research, development of customer-facing marketing collateral (brochures, emails, web pages, trade advertising, etc.), and creation of new reports to analyze progress in building the business</li>
<li>
Leads cross-functional teams in creating and executing tactical plans that support our business strategies</li>
<li>
Track overall business results including identification and monitoring of key metrics and other data sources that gauge the health and growth of the business, and future business trends.</li>
<li>
Understand the incremental impact of marketing programs including ROI and the ability to analyze reports</li>
<li>
Monitors competitive activity that could prevent the achievement of business goals, and develops proactive and reactive plans to counter competitive threats</li>
<li>
Manages trade show and promotion efforts for iTero in the GP Channel.</li>
<li>
Collaborate with the sales organization and iTero trainers to understand their needs along with those of the customer to ensure product development and marketing programs align with the requirements of the teams to deliver business objectives. Regular field sales and training co-travels and sales/marketing planning sessions should be established and executed.</li>
<li>
Analyze and distribute reports tracking the growth of the business. Including forecasting of forthcoming business trends.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>

Extensive knowledge of the dental practice environment including clinical techniques, workflows and interactions in dental practices.</li>
<li>
Knowledge of the dental manufacturer landscape</li>
<li>
Self-starter who needs minimal supervision</li>
<li>
Strong project management skills and ability to demonstrate leadership</li>
<li>
Strong analytical skills for business analysis</li>
<li>
Ability to work with individuals at all levels in an organization</li>
<li>
Strong Communicational skills (writing, oral, &amp; presentation)</li>
<li>
Strong marketing commercialization and creative skills</li>
<li>
Manages execution of and prioritize multiple tasks</li>
<li>
Shows initiative, takes action, and maintains ownership</li>
<li>
Understands cost/benefit relationships</li>
<li>
Thinks strategically and uses sound judgment</li>
<li>
Delivers a high customer experience in all aspects of the job</li>
<li>
Drives for results</li>
</ul>
<br>
<p>
EDUCATION and/or EXPERIENCE</p>
<ul>

10+ years of dental marketing or related experience, with an emphasis on taking products to commercialization is required.</li>
<li>
Preferred: 7 or more years of experience in marketing professional dental products to general practitioners, hygienists and/or dental laboratories.</li>
<li>
BS or BA required, MBA a plus</li>
</ul>
