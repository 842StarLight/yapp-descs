<h2>Account Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Harmony Plus is an exciting education startup that leverages its diverse ecosystem in Silicon Valley and Asia to provide world-class education and training programs to corporations, executives and high school students. Our growing company is in need of a dynamic and resourceful Account Manager to work closely with parents and high school students to understand their needs and recommend the right programs and services. The right candidate is self-driven, creative, a good problem-solver, customer-focused and enthusiastic to serve the needs of our customers. As we are a fast-growing startup, the candidate will also need to be flexible and adaptable.</p>
<p>Responsibilities include:</p>
<ul>
<li>Collaborate with VP of Growth to formulate go-to-market and sales strategies</li>
<li>Achieve quarterly sales targets and KPIs</li>
<li>Follow up with leads</li>
<li>Work with parents and students to understand their learning and college counseling needs</li>
<li>Explain Harmony Plus programs and services to parents and students</li>
<li>Create proposals</li>
<li>Negotiate sales contracts</li>
<li>Maintain ongoing relationship with customers to ensure high customer satisfaction</li>
<li>Maintain updated CRM and provide sales forecasts</li>
<li>Assist with planning and conducting events</li>
<li>Participate in road shows and seminars</li>
<li>Assist other teams and co-workers when needed</li>
</ul>
<p>Qualifications &amp; Skills:</p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree</li>
<li>At least one year of work experience, preferably in education or college counseling industry</li>
<li>Ability to cold call new prospects</li>
<li>Ability to establish rapport and build relationships with parents and students</li>
<li>Ability to discover customer needs and formulate appropriate solutions</li>
<li>Outstanding communication skills</li>
<li>Planning skills with strong attention to detail</li>
<li>Ability to operate in a fast-paced environment</li>
<li>Willingness to “wear different hats” and be flexible with job responsibilities</li>
<li>Fluency in written and spoken English and Mandarin Chinese</li>
<li>Knowledge of productivity tools and software</li>
</ul>
<p>Harmony Plus offers:</p>
<ul>
<li>Competitive salary plus commissions</li>
<li>Medical, dental and vision insurance</li>
<li>Opportunities for rapid growth and merit-based promotion</li>
<li>Free coffee and lunches</li>
</ul>
