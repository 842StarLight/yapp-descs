<h2>Strategic Account Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p><b>What you’ll be doing...</b>
</p>
<div>This person will develop, build and manage programs for the Verizon Business Group (VBG) Organization, particularly the Executive Engagement Team, Strategic Account Management Program. The Strategic Account Manager will develop and manage relationships and drive Executive level meetings to ensure we build 360 degree Executive level relationships within our top and most wanted accounts. In addition you will need to be able to coach VBG Sales teams to understand Customer’s Business Goals in order to qualify and recommend a top Executive as a Sponsor for the account to drive business discussions. You’ll research, strategize and support the specific Sales team to assist in uncovering and driving additional revenue streams across the business. Being part of this team means that you will manage several complex key initiatives and programs dealing with cross functional organizations and leaders at multiple levels. You will potentially have to deal with highly sensitive information and contribute to strategic discussions regarding the account and organization and their development.</div>
<div>
Key Responsibilities:</div>
<ul>
<li>
Build and grow Strategic Account Management Program working directly with Sales Teams and Senior Level Executives building relationships that help grow the business and revenue within account.</li>
<li>
Adept at building relationships with leaders at multiple levels across the organization including Executive Management and potentially within the Customer.</li>
<li>
Develop and Project Manage Strategic Initiatives which requires strong analytical and problem solving skills</li>
<li>
Handle highly sensitive, confidential, and non-routine information supporting a rapidly changing organization.</li>
<li>
Coordinate across multiple organizations to complete Customer specific Executive Briefs for Senior Leaders enabling them to build relationships and drive additional business.</li>
<li>
Effectively collaborate with a variety of people and job functions, accomplishing tasks of high complexity and scope.</li>
<li>
Produce insights that will be utilized in executive level presentations and help drive critical business decisions</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
What we’re looking for...</b>
</p>
<div>You are Savvy in Sales Strategy, Customer research, Product and Marketing and Sales systems, e.g. Salesforce.Efficiency is your middle name – you analyze in order to be successful in making recommendations to achieve specific sales driven business goals. You are detailed oriented and have the ability to work under pressure with tight timelines.</div>
<div>
You’ll need to have:</div>
<ul>
<li>
Bachelor’s degree or four or more years of work experience.</li>
<li>
Six or more years of relevant work experience.</li>
<li>
Six or more years in business or sales operations environment</li>
<li>
Six or more years of experience in sales strategy and programs with enterprise or Mid-Tier Enterprise Client Base</li>
<li>
Experience working with multiple cross-functional teams in Global Operations Organization</li>
<li>
Program management experience</li>
<li>
Willingness to travel</li>
</ul>
<div>
Even better if you have:</div>
<ul>
<li>
A degree in Marketing and/or Business Administration</li>
<li>
Presentation skills for executive level management, public groups, etc.</li>
<li>
Excellent written and verbal communication skills</li>
<li>
Computer skills including Excel, PPT, etc.</li>
<li>
Ability to communicate knowledge and expertise to provide insight and direction to the business and Sales teams.</li>
<li>
Ability to coach, mentor and train others within Verizon and have the ability to educate key stakeholders on the benefits of a proposed approach.</li>
</ul>
