<div><b>SUMMARY OF PURPOSE</b></div>
<div>The Associate, Product Management is a key member of the Product Team that optimizes Kasasa products to ensure client and consumer satisfaction, while identifying opportunities for new product innovation. This role will support the Director, Brand Management (Cross-Sell Products), Senior Product Managers, and Product Leadership as well as work cross functionally throughout Kasasa to identify and fill product gaps, achieve team and corporate goals, and research opportunities to provide new and enhanced products to Kasasa’s clients while embodying the Patch Values. The Associate Product Manager will be willing to pitch in on multiple projects and has a thirst for learning. Kasasa's Product Team is very fast-paced and is looking for a team member who can keep up.</div>
