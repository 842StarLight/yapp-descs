Want to be part of our team?</b></p>
<p></p>
The Managed Services Technical Delivery Lead will proactively support the strategy and innovation in the evaluation of existing services, systems and technologies and will take a lead in the development of strategic solutions and roadmaps to solve challenges of service delivery to the client. This role will provide oversight over design, research, testing and evaluation of services that improve and enhance Dimension Data’s managed services reliability, availability and serviceability. The Managed Services Technical Delivery Lead will collaborate with internal and external partners to ensure that technology decisions and roadmaps align with the overall enterprise architecture strategy.<p></p>
