<h2>General Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p>Title: General Manager</p>
<p>
Reports to: District Manager</p>
<p>
Supervises: All Fitness Consultants, Sales Managers, Front Desk Staff, Kid’s Club Staff, and Group X instructors at their location.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Position Summary:</b></p>
<p>
The General Manager has the overall responsibility to ensure the gym achieves its revenue, expense control and thereby profitability goals. The General Manager will achieve this by effectively managing all areas facets of operations to include memberships, service and product sales and promotions, facility management, personnel management and ensuring that the facility is running efficiently and in compliance with company policies and procedures.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Duties &amp; Responsibilities:</b></p>
<p><b>
Membership Sales and Promotions:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
<p>Achieve revenue goals</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Communicate sales and promotions plan to sales team (Sales Manager, Weekend Sales Manager, and Program Director)</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Continue to sell membership services and supplements as part of the regular sales presentation</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Plan and promote special events for the gym in conjunction with the sales team</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Manage and direct all sales team activities to ensure achieving goals and that they are being run in compliance with Company policy and procedure</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Facilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
<p>Conduct daily two walk thrus to ensure the cleanliness of the facility</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Maintain a safe and functioning workout and work place environment</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Interface with retail vendors and service contractors as needed</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Daily cleaning inspections of the facility and equipment and evaluations of the cleaning log</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Twice a week safety and operational inspections of the facility and equipment as well as evaluation of the maintenance log</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Report all maintenance issues on facility maintenance log.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Review the maintenance log throughout the month to ensure no unresolved issues</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Alert RVPS when cleaning and equipment issues persists where outside vendors or regional staff are responsible for the delinquency</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Personnel:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
<p>Select and grow staff in a professional environment while maintaining payroll to budget levels</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Cross interview all hires before starting the new hire process</p>
</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>
<p>Perform and delegate audits and reviews of Membership, Front Desk, Maintenance and Kid’s Club monthly and possibly bi-weekly</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Submit recommended hires for approval to appropriate Regional Managers</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Final approval for all schedules</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Ensure that new employees information, employee changes, terminations and related information is submitted to Human Resources correctly and on time</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Contacts Human Resources as appropriate in avoiding or encountering problem situations with employees or related issues</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Monitoring payroll against budget</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Customer Service:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
<p>Provide high quality of customer service in a fast-paced, energetic environment</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Handle, delegate Front Desk Supervisor or direct to corporate any and all customer service issues</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Retail/Proshop:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
<p>Achieve revenue goals while controlling inventory margins</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Ensure staff is properly trained in promoting and selling products</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Monitor proper levels of inventory and profitability</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Front Desk/Kid’s Club</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
<p>Ensure departments are staffed to budget with well-trained teammates that are executing our vision</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Front Desk:</p>
</li>
<ul>
<li>
<p>Greeting members properly</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Stopping and addressing any member issues</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Promoting and selling retail items</p>
</li>
</ul>
<li>
<p>Kid’s Club:</p>
</li>
<ul>
<li>
<p>Greeting members properly and signing children in and out according to Company policy and procedure</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Providing a safe and engaging environment for the children according to Company policy and procedure</p>
</li>
</ul>
</ul>
<p><b>Operational:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
<p>Inspect membership contracts for completion and correct pricing; addressing any issues in a timely manner</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Responsible for monitoring and managing all incident reports filed at gym involving members and staff and sending them to Corporate immediately</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Oversee maintenance of inventory levels of office, cleaning and retail supplies</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Administers the company’s policies and procedures</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Be prepared (trained) to complete Paperwork &amp; Deposit duties in the OM’s absence</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Facilitate weekly Department Head Meetings (to include all club level supervisors of FC, UBF and Cleaning Connection)</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Physical Demands:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
<p>Position duties require standing for long periods of time, daily walking, sitting, lifting, bending, squatting and reaching.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>The physical demands described here are representative of those that must be met by an employee to successfully perform the essential functions of this job. Reasonable accommodations may be made to enable individuals with disabilities to perform the essential functions.</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Qualifications:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
<p>High School Diploma or GED required</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>College Degree or 5-year industry experience</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Minimum of one year at the AGM or Fitness Consultant level or equivalent experience</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Must complete the SRX Certification Training through the National Academy of Fitness &amp; Sports Nutrition (NAFSN).</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Must have a positive attitude and be able to motivate staff in a positive manner and promote team spirit.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Have through knowledge of company policies and procedures and enforce them accordingly</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Possess strong leadership abilities</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Possess strong motivational, communication, team building and training skills.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Must be self-motivated and have strong organizational &amp; management skills.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Must have an understanding of basic cash procedures.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Must have a complete understanding of Front Desk, Sales and Kid’s Club policies and procedures and software.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Excellent verbal and written communication.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Excellent training skills.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Must have the ability to handle challenging customer issues with patience, tact, and professionalism.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Must have a high level of professionalism, honesty, integrity, and work ethic.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Exhibit willingness to work flexible hours.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Must be able to work under pressure and meet deadlines in a fast paced environment</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Ability to complete multiple tasks</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Willing to transfer between locations if needed</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Willingness to fill in shifts at your Home Club and other locations if needed</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Willingness to Support &amp; Participate with Club Promos, On-sites, Community Events, etc.</p>
</li>
<li><p>Other duties as assigned</p></li>
</ul>
