<p><b>
Strategist Overview</b></p>
<p>
Icreon is seeking an enthusiastic, consultative, and motivated individual to join our New York based sales team. This is a mid-level opportunity for someone with 2 - 3 years of agency work experience, who will have a direct impact on Icreon's growth and success as one of the top Innovation Agencies in the country!</p>
<p>
This role presents an enormous opportunity to build a foundation in business strategy. You will lead conversations to establish needs, recommend solutions and set meetings for the management team. The right person for this job will meet and crush weekly, monthly and annual call/appointment/opportunity goals. Work side by side with sales management to constantly develop your skills and ensure you are always growing professionally and personally. Initial and ongoing training and call guides will be provided.</p>
