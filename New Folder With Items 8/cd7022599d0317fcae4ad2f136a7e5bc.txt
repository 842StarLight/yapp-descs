<h2>Event Planner</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div><b>JOB SUMMARY</b></div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
We are searching for an enthusiastic Events Planner, who enjoys working collaboratively and creatively with the Events and Continuing Education team, other cross-functional teams and external parties to carry out key functions of the education and commercial events and tradeshows. The Event Planner's overall responsibility is to provide outstanding support to the organization's stakeholders through the planning and execution of quality events.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
The individual will stay abreast of industry trends and technology and share ideas and best practices to innovate, improve and contribute to a successful Events and Continuing Education team. He or she will bring strong program, project, time management and problem-solving skills – highly organized, detail focused, results driven, accountable with the ability to handle stress and last-minute, urgent requests.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
We are an equal opportunity employer and all qualified applicants will receive consideration for employment without regard to race, color, religion, sex, national origin, disability status, protected veteran status, or any other characteristic protected by law.</div>
<div>
<br>
<b>
JOB RESPONSIBILITIES</b>
</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<p>
Event Management:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Demonstrate event planning and execution standard methodologies by managing all project delivery elements within time and budget limits.</li>
<li>
Throughout the event cycle, consistently evaluate options under circumstances that may be outside defined procedures and select the best solution to the problem with minimal support and guidance.</li>
<li>
Work with the Events and Continuing Education Director to create and manage timelines for all assigned events, including those that involve outside vendor and cross-functional teams.</li>
<li>
Work collaboratively in a team environment, sharing responsibilities and the success of assigned events.</li>
<li>
Work directly or indirectly with various levels of management, sales team, outside vendors and customers.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
Logistics:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Plan and manage all event logistics including venue, guests, programs, catering, décor, entertainment, transportation, equipment, etc.</li>
<li>
As needed, provide onsite support or supervision on the set-up, execution, and break down of an event, proactively identifying and resolving issues.</li>
<li>
Work with hotels to manage housing lists, audio visual, banquet orders, room set-ups for regional events.</li>
<li>
Review accuracy of invoices and process payments accordingly, while tracking event expenses under budget requirements.</li>
<li>
Initiate, review and negotiate contracts.</li>
<li>
Ensure compliance with insurance, legal, health and safety obligations of each event.</li>
<li>
Select and vet vendors for the different needs of an event.</li>
<li>
Direct and supervise contracted vendors and staff before and during the event.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
Marketing and Metrics</p>
<ul>
<li>
Support the coordination of all promotional efforts with the Marketing team.</li>
<li>
Assist in developing and proofing content for event pages, marketing communications and collateral.</li>
<li>
Create and manage event registration using the provided registration management software.</li>
<li>
Support efforts to develop and maintain relevant event databases, mailing lists and lead generation efforts.</li>
<li>
Support survey summary report development and other post-event reporting to identify success metrics areas of opportunity.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
Non Essential Job Functions:</p>
<ul><li>
Other duties and responsibilities as assigned</li></ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
JOB REQUIREMENTS AND COMPENTENCIES:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Bachelor's degree, with focus on Event Management or Hospitality Management a plus.</li>
<li>
3 years of experience working in a dynamic environment and track record of successful execution of multiple events with opposing priorities.</li>
<li>
Excellent time management and communication skills, both verbal and written.</li>
<li>
Demonstrated experience leading by example, exhibiting integrity, enthusiasm, and commitment to results.</li>
<li>
MS Office and Google Office applications proficiency.</li>
<li>
Willingness to travel about 30 – 40 %.</li>
<li>
Ability to work overtime and weekends as necessary to accommodate heavy workloads that tend to occur in the few months before an event.</li>
<li>
Ability to work long hours while onsite for an event, often on your feet for the majority of the day and ability to lift and move boxes of reasonable size/weight.</li>
</ul>
