<h2>Sales Enablement Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>We are seeking an energetic, passionate, and creative professional to help develop and drive a world class global Sales Enablement program here at Apttus. In this role, you will be supporting Apttus’s customer-facing teams through effective and efficient on-boarding initiatives, ongoing initiatives, and coaching programs. The position is based in San Mateo, CA (Apttus HQ) and reports to the Director of Sales &amp; Technical Enablement.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
Enablement at Apttus means far more than sales training! The Sales Enablement team’s mission is to foster and empower excellence at scale throughout Apttus’ customer-facing organizations. Our business and customers require sales and technical sales staff who are deeply knowledgeable about our industry, technology, and the value to be delivered from our solutions.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
Leveraging ‘Enablement’ technology to develop, deploy, measure, and action on data will be a fundamental part of this job, so you will be responsible for partnering with the cross-functional leadership and customer facing employees to help define strategy, drive execution, and capture and communicate the business impact.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Primary Responsibilities Include</b></p>
<p></p>
<br>
<ul><li>
Development, execution, &amp; measurement of a ‘state-of-the-art’ sales onboarding program, through automated ‘role-specific’ online training paths (Sales Enablement Software), in-person instruction (Bootcamps), &amp; virtual classroom (Strategic Webinars)</li></ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<ul><li>
Manage the day-to-day administration of the Sales Enablement Solution(s); create comprehensive courses, ‘just-in-time’ updates/trainings, adaptive reinforcement &amp; retention programs, &amp; innovative coaching programs</li></ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<ul><li>
Actively source &amp; ‘creatively’ deliver routine ongoing ‘just-in-time’ and ‘virtual on-demand’ enablement around sales techniques, Apttus’s sales process, Apttus solutions, internal sales tools, competition, account management &amp; delivery, and much more.</li></ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<ul><li>
Develop and maintain strategic and effective reporting systems for sales personnel &amp; management; to drive accountability, engagement, identify enablement needs, and measure program success</li></ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<ul><li>
Effectively design content and tools to increase our employees' level of knowledge, competence, &amp; confidence in serving our customers and the business</li></ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<ul><li>
Maintain a framework &amp; governance process for sales enablement content, partnering closely with cross-functional departments</li></ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<ul><li>
Serve as a trusted advisor to our sales organization, by partnering with key stakeholders and ensuring enablement program alignment with the organization’s goals</li></ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<ul><li>
Help orchestrate &amp; execute sales enablement ‘big-ticket’ events</li></ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Requirements</b></p>
<p></p>
<br>
<ul>
<li>
 Demonstrated passion for competition and winning</li>
<li>
 Experience developing &amp; executing sales onboarding programs</li>
<li>
 Experience developing &amp; executing on-demand enablement/learning strategies</li>
<li>
 Experience delivering instructor-led training</li>
<li>
 Experience with and strong understanding of how to effectively leverage modern learning management platforms and enablement tools</li>
<li>
 Confident &amp; engaging presentation and communication skills</li>
<li>
 Comfort engaging with all levels of the organization</li>
<li>
 Self-motivated team player able to prioritize and manage projects in a fast-paced environment</li>
<li>
 Strong organizational skills; ability to manage multiple cross-functional projects simultaneously</li>
<li>
 Self-learner who can learn to use new technologies with minimal guidance as the role's tech-stack shifts to meet evolving needs</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Company Description</b></p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
With more than 300 enterprise customers and 450,000+ users worldwide, Apttus is the industry’s fastest-growing provider of Quote-to-Cash solutions. Apttus delivers category-defining software that drives the vital business process between the buyer’s interest in a purchase and the realization of revenue. Delivered on the industry's most advanced cloud infrastructure, Force.com, Apttus’ Quote-to-Cash solutions include Configure-Price-Quote (CPQ), Proposal Management, Contract Management, and Revenue Management. Additionally, Apttus’ patent pending X-Author technology enables Microsoft Office to be a user-interface for Salesforce.com with full interaction and control between Salesforce and Microsoft Office. Apttus is based in San Mateo, California, with additional offices in London, UK and Ahmedabad, India.</p>
