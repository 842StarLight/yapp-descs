<h2>Senior Trading Support Associate</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Description</b></h2>
<br>
<div></div>
<div><b>
Job Summary:<br>
</b></div>
<div></div>
<div>Under limited supervision, uses intermediate knowledge and skills obtained through experience and/or training to provide support to branches, advisors and other trading groups in specific trading activities within an assigned functional area. Serves as a resource to advisors in resolving routine to intermediate problems and issues. Extensive contact with internal and external customers is required to identify, research, and resolve problems.</div>
<div></div>
<div><b>Essential Duties and Responsibilities:<br>
</b></div>
<div></div>
<ul><li>Manually facilitates trade order processing when required.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Ensures that trades are executed and confirmed in accordance with organizational policies and procedures.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Facilitates the price adjustment and trade correction process.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Ensures trades are confirmed and researches and resolves unconfirmed trades.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Reviews trade problem, analyzes and interprets data and determines appropriate corrective action.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Contacts internal and external customers to resolve trading problems.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Maintains currency in trading policies and procedures.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Provides trading support to financial advisors and responds to inquiries regarding fund suitability, products and services.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Establishes and maintains periodic orders. Resolves periodic order issues, cancels and corrects as needed.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Ensures trades are receiving appropriate breakpoints/sales charges.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Reviews, uploads and researches rejects on bulk trades for discretionary accounts, works with advisor to reprocess as needed.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Coordinates and processes share class conversions, works with both internal and external departments and funds companies to ensure successful conversions.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Ensures that all the varied and complex fund family rules and procedures are adhered to, and that the firm's regulatory obligations are met.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Processes various trade related reports and utilizes tools including trade related databases and 3rd party applications.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Researches and corrects Asset Management Services portfolio rebalance issues.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Provides Inbox support.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Performs other duties and responsibilities as assigned.</li></ul>
