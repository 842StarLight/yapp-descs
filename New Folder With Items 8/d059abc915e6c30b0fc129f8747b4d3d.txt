<h2>Marketing Manager - The Pfister Hotel</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<div>
<div>
<b>Do you have a passion for Marketing and Social Media? </b>This position contributes to and supports the development of marketing materials and requires a proactive marketing professional to promote Mason Street Grill and the Wells Spa. Experience and good working knowledge of social media, public relations, web materials, video, and email marketing is essential to succeeding in this role.</div>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>ESSENTIAL FUNCTIONS:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Participate in the development and execution of comprehensive marketing plans/calendars and programs, both short and long range, to support sales &amp; revenue objectives of organization.</li>
<li>Serve as liaison to PR agencies, media agency, and creative agencies regarding promotional activities to include print, online, electronic media, and direct mail.</li>
<li>Oversee the development and production of promotional and collateral materials.</li>
<li>Research, analyze, and monitor various environmental factors to capitalize on market opportunities.</li>
<li>Event promotion, event production, and Event Management for events at the Pfister hotel and Mason Street Grill</li>
<li>Participate in the development and execution of comprehensive marketing plans for wine dinners, chef dinners, interactive guest events, and chef demonstrations in Mason Street Grill</li>
<li>Coordinate and manage online listings for properties, including Google, Yelp, etc.</li>
<li>Oversee ad and email creative for property, ensuring advertising deadlines are met</li>
<li>Oversee and create content for the property’s social media channels for hotels, restaurants and other business units.</li>
<li>Oversee and analyze online reputation management through revinate or other equal service.</li>
<li>Manage/write blogs for property</li>
<li>Establishes and maintain a consistent image throughout all promotional events and materials.</li>
<li>Participate in marketing/revenue and sales meetings and events as needed.</li>
<li>Leverage relationships with local venues and events to create additional marketing opportunities using social media and email.</li>
<li>Perform other duties as assigned.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>POSITION REQUIREMENTS:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s Degree in Marketing, Communications or PR related field is preferred.</li>
<li>Two or three year’s experience in the marketing field required; experience in a hospitality environment highly desired.</li>
<li>Experience with social media.</li>
<li>Experience with Adobe InDesign and Photoshop preferred.</li>
<li>Interest in producing video content.</li>
<li>Excellent written and verbal communication skills.</li>
<li>Strong analytical skills with the ability to evaluate various options and quickly make recommendations.</li>
<li>Outstanding organizational and project management skills with strong attention to detail.</li>
<li>Proactive with a proven ability to take initiative and follow-through.</li>
<li>Ability to follow verbal directives and apply to multiple projects.</li>
<li>Demonstrates flexibility, adaptability, and working effectively with changing/competing work priorities.</li>
<li>Professional demeanor.</li>
<br>
</ul>
