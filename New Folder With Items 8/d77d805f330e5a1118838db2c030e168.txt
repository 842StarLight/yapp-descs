<h2>Software Applications Engineer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p>Essential Role and Responsibilities</p>
<ul>
<li>
Provide software support to customers and NAI Applications Engineers to support NAI board products for Windows, Linux and VxWorks operating systems.</li>
<li>
Create sample programs for customers utilizing NAI product software support kit and libraries.</li>
<li>
Assist customers in debugging application programs.</li>
<li>
Provide pre- and post-sales product information, advice, and technical assistance to prospects towards the goal of achieving new design wins.</li>
<li>
Via telephone and email correspondence with customer, understand project technical system requirements.</li>
<li>
Work with customers to identify software issues with NAI products.</li>
<li>
Advise and participate with the prospect during customer test and evaluation activities.</li>
<li>
Coordinate additional support activities and post-sales technical support with engineering departments.</li>
<li>
Communicate, internally, product challenges and customer specific requirements for the purpose of resolution.</li>
</ul>
<p>
Qualifications and Education Requirements</p>
<ul>
<li>
Bachelor of Science Degree in Computer Engineering or related discipline required.</li>
<li>
Minimum two-years of engineering and/or software programming experience.</li>
<li>
Experience with bus architectures (VME, cPCI, PCIe, VPX), processors and/or related products is a plus.</li>
<li>
Concepts of real time operations systems (i.e. VxWorks) is a plus.</li>
<li>
Strong C programming skills a must, TCP/UDP Ethernet Programming, Windows, Linux and/or VxWorks.</li>
<li>
Experience with C++ and C# is a plus.</li>
</ul>
<p>
Other Skills and Abilities</p>
<ul>
<li>
Must have good oral and written communication skills.</li>
<li>
Ability to work with hardware to debug programs.</li>
<li>
Strong analytical and problem-solving skills.</li>
<li>
Good organizational and time management skills.</li>
<li>
Ability to take initiative and multi-task within a fast-paced environment.</li>
<li>
Ability to work independently towards focused goals.</li>
<li>
Familiarity with Microsoft Office (Outlook, Word, Excel, PowerPoint)</li>
<li>
Prior Applications Engineering experience is a plus.</li>
</ul>
