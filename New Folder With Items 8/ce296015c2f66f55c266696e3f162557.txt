<h2>Sr. Supervisor Call Center</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Overview
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<p>The Call Center Senior Supervisor / Manager will oversee productivity of a new growing team of agents who are responsible for turning inquiries into sales leads via inbound and outbound calling strategies (voice, email and online) with the goal of setting in-home appointments for American Standard sales specialists.</p>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<ul>
<li>Plan, coordinate and conduct training for new hires as well as ongoing training for established agents.</li>
<li>Monitor inbound and outbound calls with all agents, to ensure adherence to best practices, quality and efficiency. Provide feedback and motivation.</li>
<li>Be proactive with observations, by reporting trends, adjustments and/or recommendations for the program on a regular basis.</li>
<li>Provide coaching and mentoring to agents, delivering feedback on call performance and other key performance indicators.</li>
<li>Motivate call center agents to achieve performance objectives and team goals.</li>
<li>Manage inbound and outbound call strategies.</li>
<li>Review trend reports against program goals and ensure metric goals are met.</li>
<li>Participate in regular business reviews with the American Standard team.</li>
<li>Manage the day-to-day communication and successful operation of the program.</li>
<li>Understand all aspects of the offered products and programs.</li>
<li>Phone support for inbound client accounts, as needed.</li>
<li>Identify, train, equip, coach, and mentor Call Center Team Leads to create additional layers of leadership and accountability for agents.</li>
<li>Directly manage Call Center Team Leads to ensure program(s) productivity, quality and customer satisfaction/business performance objectives are met and performed in an efficient manner.</li>
<li>Motivate the team to work toward the sales target by promoting a result-driven sales culture in the call center team</li>
<li>Partner with the Business Manager and Human Resources to execute strategic recruiting and employee engagement programs to attract and retain top performers</li>
<li>Ensure all positions are staffed appropriately to meet business requirements and effectively manage inbound and outbound call traffic handling throughout spikes and ebbs of volume</li>
<li>Responsible for implementing all FTC and state directed policies and regulations, keeping the business within compliance parameters both nationally and locally, as directed by business managers.</li>
<li>Monitor call center systems to support IB and OB strategies which include an auto-dialing platform, procedures and business rules, CRM, dialing strategies, reporting, scheduling and staffing requirements</li>
</ul>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Qualifications
</b></h2>
<div><div><div><ul>
<li>3-5 years of related experience (call center sales management and call center training experience highly desirable).</li>
<li>Proven track record of successful management of call center agents, for inbound and outbound lead generation or sales.</li>
<li>Minimum 1 year experience as manager in direct response call center environment.</li>
<li>Must have excellent oral, written, communication and presentation skills.</li>
<li>A sales background with the ability to close.</li>
<li>Proven leadership skills and solid account or project management experience.</li>
<li>Highly organized and detail-oriented.</li>
<li>Proficient in the MS Office Suite (Excel, Word).</li>
<li>A self-motivated team player who interfaces well on all levels with minimal direction.</li>
<li>A positive attitude with high enthusiasm and a desire to provide top-notch service.</li>
<li>Ability to prioritize tasks and resolve issues quickly.</li>
<li>Experience using Five9 software preferred</li>
<li>Experience in using a CRM or lead management database, such as Lead Perfection, preferred.</li>
</ul></div></div></div>
