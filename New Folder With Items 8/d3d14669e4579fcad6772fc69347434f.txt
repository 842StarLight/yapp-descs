<div><b>Marketing Director</b></div>
<div>Summary</div>
<div>The Marketing Director holds ultimate responsibility for the organization’s marketing activities and oversees the development and delivery of a fully integrated marketing strategy for the business. Develops marketing strategy by studying economic indicators; tracking changes in supply and demand; identifying customers and their current and future needs; monitoring the competition.</div>
<div>Essential Duties of the Marketing Director:</div>
<ul>
<li>Development and implementation of the Brand strategy.</li>
<li>Developing the marketing strategy for new and existing products</li>
<li>Responsible for digital marketing (marketing products or services using digital technologies, on the Internet, mobile phones, display advertising, and any other digital medium.</li>
<li>Overseeing implementation of the Marketing strategy - including campaigns, events, digital marketing, and PR.</li>
<li>Working closely with the company’s Sales team; enabling them to meet their commercial objectives by providing them with appropriate tools, materials and presentations</li>
<li>Manage social media presence and direct programs to improve social media reputation and recognition.</li>
<li>Developing and delivering marketing and communications strategies for the organization.</li>
<li>Undertake continuous analysis of competitive environment and consumer trends</li>
<li>Provides short- and long-term market forecasts and reports by directing market research collection, analysis, and interpretation of market data.</li>
<li>Achieves financial objectives by preparing an annual budget, scheduling expenditures, analyzing variances, and initiating corrective actions.</li>
<li>Handles RFPs for vendors for all marketing materials, creative work, services, software and so on.</li>
<li>Manages the performance of marketing vendors.</li>
<li>Responsible for the identification, development and management of lead generation sources.</li>
</ul>
