<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Essential Duties and Responsibilities
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<p><b>Lifestyle Multimedia Journalist (MMJ) - Full Time</b></p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
WTNH/News 8 is looking for a self-starting MMJ to work on our lifestyle show.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Details</b></p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
The successful candidate must have MMJ skills including shooting, writing and editing both long and short form client videos, including packages, vignettes, social videos, and other on air or digital assignments as needed. Additionally, this position will also be responsible for assisting the producer with booking guests, going out on sales calls, writing segments for the show and all other duties as assigned related to the daily production of “Connecticut Style,” a daily marketer-friendly show that focuses on health, fitness, fashion, cooking and a variety of family friendly topics.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p>The position requires a proven track record as a successful MMJ with a minimum of three years’ experience, preferably with direct on-camera experience plus solid remote shooting, lighting and editing skills. You must be able to work independently and work on multiple projects on a deadline. The ability to work as part of a unique production team with a host and other on-camera talent is essential. Must be able to assist the show producer with writing solid copy on a deadline basis, as required. Knowledge of local and national trends is important, as is an ability to communicate extemporaneously with CT Style team, news, production and clients.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p>May be assigned as general assignment news reporter as needed.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Requirements</b></p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Education/Experience: </b>Bachelor’s degree in Communications/Journalism or related field preferred, with minimum of (3) three years’ experience as a lifestyle or news feature reporter. Training/Equipment: Operate newsroom computer systems; Software skills required include Word Press, Adobe CC Suite production package including; Premiere Pro, After Effects, Photoshop.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<div>
<b>
Additional Information: Work Environment/Mental/Physical Requirements:</b> High stress environment with deadline pressures. Local travel will be required on a regular basis. Position requires the transport of equipment weighing up to 50 lbs. Position often requires work in extreme outdoor temperatures. Must be willing and able to work all shifts, including nights, overnights, early mornings, mornings, weekends, and holidays.</div>
<p>
The Lifestyle MMJ must be able to sit, conduct telephone conversations, use electronic mail, write letters and memos, conduct face-to-face discussions with individuals or groups, make decisions without supervision, make decisions that impact the results of co-workers, work near others, work outdoors occasionally in all weather conditions, and work indoors in environmentally controlled conditions. In addition, must be able to lift, set up and operate equipment weighing up to 50 pounds, and be able to handle, control or feel objects, tools or controls. Repetitive movements are required. Work may be conducted in extremely bright or inadequate lighting conditions and the demands of breaking news, which can happen at any time.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p>A valid driver’s license and an acceptable driving record are required. Background and DMV check are required.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
No telecommuting.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p>This position falls under the umbrella of the AFTRA Collective Bargaining Agreement with WTNH.</p>
