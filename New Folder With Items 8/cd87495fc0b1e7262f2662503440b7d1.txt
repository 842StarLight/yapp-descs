<h2>Director Planned Giving</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<b>Position Overview:</b><br>
<div><b>PRIMARY PURPOSE:</b></div>
<div>
<div>
To direct a comprehensive Gift Planning program; offer legal counsel to donors, prospects, and Foundation staff; and, to promote, secure, and protect planned gifts designated to California Pacific Medical Center, California Pacific Medical Center Foundation, and related programs. In recent years, revenue from planned gifts amounts to about one-third of the funds raised annually by the Foundation.</div>
<div></div>
<div>
<b><br>
SUPERVISION: (Reporting Relations) </b>Supervises the Senior Gift Planning Officer and the Gift Planning Officer.</div>
</div>

<br>
<b>
Qualifications:</b><br>
<p><b>Education: </b>Bachelors degree required; Juris Doctorate (law degree) preferred.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Knowledge:</b><b> </b>Must possess mastery of current tax law, estate planning law, and probate judicial process. Expertise in the use, application, communication, and law related to planned giving instruments, including but not limited to wills, trusts, pledge agreements, contracts, and annuities. Knowledge of fundraising, word processing, and other software programs, including PG Calc or Crescendo, Sales Force Donor Database, Microsoft Word, Outlook, and Excel.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<div>
<b>Experience:</b><b> </b>Minimum 3 years experience in gift planning fundraising; or 5 years of law practice; or a combination there of. Track record of successfully and efficiently supervising a multi-tasked environment, interacting with lawyers and other professional advisors, stewarding donors, cultivating prospective donors, and collaborating with development staff. History of matching appropriate planned giving instruments with donors while putting the donorsâ€™ well-being above all other considerations. Ability to determine whether gift is a wise arrangement for the Foundation from a cost-benefit standpoint. Position requires excellent personal, written, and verbal communication skills.</div>
