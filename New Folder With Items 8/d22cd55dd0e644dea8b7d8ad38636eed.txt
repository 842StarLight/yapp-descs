<h2>Account Executive</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>Raleigh, NC</div>
<div><div>
<p>
Are you interested in an exciting opportunity with a fast growing company in the lucrative field of IT sales? Infranet is hiring information technology account executives to help grow our revenue and market presence through prospecting, lead response and relationship building. Our partnerships with leading-edge technology manufacturers keep us on the cutting edge of delivering transformative IT solutions and services. We feel strongly about being a champion of technology for our clients. By listening to each client’s business objectives and applying technical knowledge and career experience we strive to understand, recommend and implement leading technologies that fit their needs. We take a proactive approach to client support utilizing preventative and monitoring services while always maintaining a posture and readiness to deliver fast, friendly, responsive and accurate assistance.</p>
<p>
Candidate must love technology, sales and working with area businesses. Experience with information technology or related sales is preferred but not required.</p>
<p>
Education: Bachelor’s Degree</p>

<ul>
<li>Ethical and trustworthy approach</li>
<li>Ability to handle objections</li>
<li>Knowledge on how to identify and transverse “gate keepers”</li>
<li>Excellent listening skills</li>
<li>Open to coaching</li>
<li>Ability to manage time</li>
<li>Ability to prioritize tasks</li>
<li>Understanding of the sales cycle</li>
<li>Determined prospector</li>
<li>Constantly active in prospecting</li>
<li>Ability to quality prospective customers</li>
<li>Differentiation from the competition</li>
<li>Experience responding to RFPs</li>
<li>Experience selling to committees</li>
</ul>
