<h2>Technical Account Manager (TAM) - Enterprise Data</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">Infoworks is scaling! Our Technical Account Manager team is expanding rapidly into multiple markets to support our growth and amazing customers. We are hiring remote TAMs on the East Coast - NY or Boston and Atlanta; Central - Texas and Chicago; West Coast - Los Angeles or Orange County, CA. You will be responsible for building and growing post-sales relationships with our customers as a trusted advisor of our to drive successful implementations. Ensure our customers achieve targeted business benefits from their investment resulting in them becoming our biggest champion and evangelist.
<br><br>
The TAM role is a unique way to have a huge impact with some of the largest enterprise forward-thinking companies where you get to help them solve high-visibility problems as the voice of the customer.
<br><br>
<b>What you get to do:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Identify solutions, troubleshoot issues, and answer technical questions</li>
<li>Act as customers primary liaison and accountable for identifying and directly addressing business and technical issues</li>
<li>Marshal resources across the organization, coordinate timely interactions and escalate as needed</li>
<li>Develop and maintain a working knowledge of the technical architecture and business requirements of our customers and become a trusted resource for them</li>
<li>Partner with the other Customer Engagement and Delivery matrix resources to ensure proper technical guidance, project management and functional support</li>
<li>Be a great listener, taking time to understand the problem to better help clients resolve any issues</li>
<li>Identify, document, triage and track issues to ensure resolution (FreshDesk)</li>
<li>Play an active role contributing to the growth and scalability of the Customer Delivery and Training and Education teams through robust documentation, continuous process optimization, and capability cross-training</li>
<li>Apply industry/domain/technology expertise to client implementations</li>
<li>Gather intelligent product feedback and recommendations from customers to design and inform new features and capabilities</li>
<li>Integrate customer internal applications and systems with Infoworks Autonomous Data Engine</li>
<li>Monitor accounts to ensure usage of Infoworks product stack and optimal performance</li>
<li>Perform quarterly Business reviews and assist in product trainings needed</li>
</ul>
<b>Your Background Likely Includes:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>5+ years experience in enterprise data management or data warehouse domain</li>
<li>5+ years experience in a "enterprise customer facing" environment such as sales – solution architect, technical consulting or enterprise customer support</li>
<li>Executive presence, comfort, and ease communicating throughout a top fortune company from executives down.</li>
<li>Strong organizational skills with an ability to manage competing customer demands, multiple priorities, commitments and projects</li>
<li>Strong analytical skills regarding technical and project management issues</li>
<li>Expert level knowledge of big data ecosystem</li>
<li>Good understanding of Data Management on Cloud ecosystem, with expertise in at least one Cloud system</li>
<li>Ability to troubleshoot technical issues at a high level to determine the appropriate path to resolution</li>
<li>Exemplary customer service attitude with business development and customer relationship management experience</li>
<li>Ability to facilitate and harness cross-team collaboration working with teams in person and virtually</li>
<li>Wear the 'Customer Advocate' hat at all times</li>
<li>Work in a fast-paced environment with multiple projects under tight deadlines with a can-do attitude</li>
<li>Articulate technical issues in a meaningful way to both team and executive level management</li>
</ul>
<b>Bonus Points:</b><br>
<ul>

</ul>
