<h2>HR & Payroll Systems Analyst</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p>The HR &amp; Payroll Systems Analyst is responsible for the administration, security, and design of our HR information system, the administration of US and Canada semi-monthly and US biweekly payrolls, and the administration of commissions via our commission software for Dude Solutions. This is a technical role that requires attention to detail, data accuracy, advanced payroll knowledge, and experience managing systems and vendor relationships. As a member of this team, this role will share in the accountability for ensuring effective data management and process and procedural documentation.</p>
<p><b>
What you'll be doing...</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
 Manages the administration and system optimization of HR technology and software solutions in support of HR programs.</li>
<li>
Processes 6 payrolls a month: 2 Semi-monthly US, 2 semi-monthly Canada, and 2 bi-weekly US. This includes managing time-off balances, deduction and earning codes, garnishments, relationship management of vendor services with our HRIS.</li>
<li>
Participates in audits as needed for payroll purposes.</li>
<li>
Processes monthly commissions for the sales organization. This includes administering employment changes and maintaining records in the commission software, collaborating with the business systems team to ensure correct functionality of commission plans in the software, and the ability to troubleshoot errors and communicate them to the sales team members efficiently and effectively.</li>
<li>
Serves as Tier 1 support for all HR system and payroll related requests.</li>
<li>
Manages all employment verification and unemployment requests.</li>
<li>
Maintain relationship with tax team of HR software to ensure all payroll taxes are up to date and paid in a timely fashion. This includes understanding and executing the process to set up new states for taxation and unemployment.</li>
<li>
Responsible for understanding the benefits plans including 401k to ensure proper administration as it relates to processing payroll.</li>
<li>
Responsible for creating user guides, training content, process documentation, and identifying efficiencies for HR operational functions.</li>
<li>
Audits talent data contained in HR systems of record to ensure integrity.</li>
<li>
Evaluate HR System and Commissions system and make recommendations for improvements, automations, and optimizations.</li>
<li>
Works in cross functional teams as a subject matter expert to ensure that the HR System is serving the needs of the business.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
What you need...</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Bachelor’s degree or commiserate years of experience</li>
<li>
7 plus years of payroll experience and at least 5 years of experience with HR systems</li>
<li>
CPP certification preferred</li>
<li>
Ability to develop and maintain effective working relationships with other departments</li>
<li>
Ability to exercise independent judgement with guidance from manager</li>
<li>
Demonstrates strong problem-solving, critical thinking, and organizational skills</li>
<li>
Demonstrates a sense of urgency in sensitive situation</li>
<li>
Ability to think through processes, identify areas for improvement, and implement necessary change</li>
<li>
Advanced Excel skills</li>
<li>
Experience with Ultimate Software a plus</li>
</ul>
