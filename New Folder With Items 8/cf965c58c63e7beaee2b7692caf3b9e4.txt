<h2>Account Executive</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>
<p><b>What type of people are we looking for?</b> If you love people, love sales, and love freedom then this might be a great job for you. Some of our other sales people that have been extremely successful are retired and on their second career; second income folks that need flexibility in theirr daily schedule; have owned their own business but did not like going at it alone!</p>
<p><b>
What will you do as an Account Executive?</b> You will be working with clients on a one-on-one basis to ultimately help them build their brand utilizing promotional advertising products. You will have the support of our entire in-house team and over 100 years of combined product knowledge of our account executives and management team.</p>
<p><b>
What requirements do you need to meet?</b> The ability to use Outlook, Microsoft Office and learn proprietary software is required. You must be of good character, highly motivated, have an expectation of success and sell with integrity.</p>
<p><b>
What is your earnings potential?</b> This position is full commission and there are no limits on what you can earn.</p>
<div>
<b>
What will be some of my responsibilities?</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Prospecting new business relationship opportunities</li>
<li>Developing key accounts</li>
<li>Attending monthly sales meeting to acquire product knowledge</li>
<li>Attending end user, national and regional industry trade shows</li>
<li>Sending out quotes, product information, and entering orders</li>
<li>Following up with clients after the order is complete</li>
</ul>
