<p>The Intranet Specialist is responsible for oversight of the BluePearl intranet homepage. In this position, you will work across the organization to ensure appropriate content is provided to be both informative and engaging.</p>
<br>
</div></div>

<div><b>
As the Intranet Specialist, you will</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Manage the intranet homepage to maintain a proper balance of content, tools and editorial voice.</li>
<li>
Oversee authoring and proper use of online channels for official company news.</li>
<li>
Oversee intranet content and engagement strategy.</li>
<li>
Develop and maintain editorial calendar of fresh, relevant and compelling content that informs and inspires Associates.</li>
<li>
Work with Communications Team and other departments to create, write and edit content with a focus on story telling.</li>
<li>
Management and creation of new SharePoint pages and content.</li>
<li>
Liaise with the IT Department and software vendors on technical questions of implementation and upgrades that drive intranet adoption, streamline processes and facilitate communications.</li>
<li>
Monitor user-generated content for appropriateness.</li>
<li>
Monitor intranet site analytics and report on key performance indicators (KPIs).</li>
<li>
Perform typical administration and site maintenance activities such as user permissions management, search configuration, etc.</li>
<li>
Develop and maintain the curricula and schedule for training of users at all levels.</li>
<li>
Maintain mechanisms to assess the level of success of the training programs.</li>
<li>
Other duties as assigned.</li>
