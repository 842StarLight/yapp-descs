<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Job Description
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div>Omni Barton Creek Resort &amp; Spa is seeking a dynamic sales leader who brings leadership, revenue driven hotel sales experience and is interested in joining and industry leading company. You will be a senior sales manager, leading our efforts managing an assigned territory and engage key account relationships.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
To assist in implementing all strategies to generate and maximize overall revenue, through marketing, sales, promotion, advertising, and public relations that will impact positively on all operating departments of the hotel. To assist the Director of Catering Sales in support of the sales operation and work closely with Catering Sales, Conference Services Department, and Reservation Sales Department to maximize revenues</div>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<ul>
<li>Solicitation of direct-selling in the Social Catering market.</li>
<li>
Event planning and detailing for booked events.</li>
<li>
Maximize Catering revenues wherever possible and minimize erosions of check averages from price-sensitive groups.</li>
<li>
Communication of function-arrangement to all responsible hotel departments.</li>
<li>
Communication with clients prior to events to initiate, negotiate, and close contracts for all catered functions.</li>
<li>
Creations of profitable menus and distribution of client expectations to Food and Beverage Departments.</li>
<li>
Coordinate Catering support service (flowers, entertainment, decoration, A/A, etc.) to provide complete client satisfaction.</li>
<li>
Physically double check all function arrangements against specifications of client. Correct where necessary.</li>
<li>
Client contract and effective communication</li>
<li>
Update and increase client prospects through active prospecting of key local wedding planners</li>
<li>
Maximize revenues for the hotel through up-selling techniques.</li>
<li>
Be involved in and/or conduct departmental training.</li>
<li>
Collaborate with Director of Catering on creating weekly and monthly forecasting procedures.</li>
<li>
Other duties as assigned by Director of Catering.</li>
</ul>
</div></div></div>

</b></h2>
<div><div><div>

The ability to delegate, effectively train and coach</li>
