<h2>Relationship Manager/ Business Development Analyst</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>Job Description<br>
<br>
<p>Our Verisk Strategic Alliances business is thriving! We are seeking an experienced and professional Relationship Mgr/ Business Development Analyst to build business with Software/ Technology/ Consulting vendors in the P&amp;C markets and manage licensing and contracts with other consultants. You'll work to protect our intellectual property, maximize opportunity for revenue, and enhance products and services for our customers. This non-commission role benefits from broad exposure to various departments, product and services and personnel.</p>
<div>
Responsibilities<br>
<ul>
<li>Manage a consultative sales/business development process to successfully generate leads and close opportunities involving the sale of risk decision information, analytics, products and services focused on the underwriting and policy administration discipline within the insurance carrier environment to consulting companies, software and technology vendors and others, as applicable</li>
<li>Assist with the administration of the Consulting Agreement Process with 3rd party vendors and consulting companies</li>
<li>Assist with administering amendments to insurer contracts when working with 3rd party vendors</li>
<li>Draft and negotiate contracts and licenses as necessary</li>
<li>Work with various levels of management and departments including: Law Department, Business and Product areas, Customer Service, Sales, Finance</li>
<li>Network to create opportunities to offer new and expanded products and services to vendors who service the Insurance industry and other industries as applicable</li>
<li>Properly and accurately maintain sales pipeline revenue forecasts</li>
<li>Maintain positive relationships and help to ensure the satisfaction of existing client vendors for the purpose of maintaining or growing their revenue base with ISO/Verisk</li>
<li>Participate in the research, design and implementation of new products and services that meet customer needs</li>
<li>Assist Relationship Managers and Team in executing on its goals</li>
<li>Research market and industry trends</li>
</ul>
</div>
</div>
