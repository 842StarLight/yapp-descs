Main Responsibilities
<br>Specific Duties and Essential Functions
<br><br>
Primary liaison between G4S Secure Solutions (USA) Inc. and client management; manage the daily security operations of the data center construction site; implement security directives; build community partnerships with local law enforcement agencies; provide emergency response as necessary
<br>General Operations management; review post orders and make recommendations to Client and G4S management when updates should be considered; review daily log reports and provides pass downs as required; responsible for officers meeting company standards pertaining to quality of work performed on an ongoing basis, performing all work related tasks in a manner that is in compliance with all company policies and procedures including worldwide business standards; ensure the security team receives required training on a consistent basis; implement security programs; ensure physical security measures and practices are enforced to insure Google directives are carried out continuously
<br>General Human Resources management; participate in selection and placement, conduct performance reviews, manage attendance, and conflict resolution; scheduling; payroll
<br>Compile exception reports and forward to appropriate security manager/operations manager for review
<br>Provide monthly and as-needed reports to the construction organizations detailing all pertinent activities that have occurred in the previous month concerning any security and safety issues
<br>Administer G4S Safety Program; semi-annual review and revise the security Emergency Response plan; conducting risk assessments; participate in safety meetings, attend any/all on-site meetings involving security related processes
<br>Conduct and document monthly testing of security systems; follow through on any corrections/maintenance issues; conduct weekly/ monthly/quarterly audits
