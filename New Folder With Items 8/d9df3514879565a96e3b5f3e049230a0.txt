<h2>Ad Operations Analyst</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>BrideClick is seeking a talented and enthusiastic Ad Operations Analyst who wants to join our team in New York. The primary function of this role is to work with BrideClick advertisers and internal teams to ensure consistent and smooth delivery of advertising campaigns. The ideal candidate will have strong problem solving skills, experience with analyzing reports, advanced excel functions, extreme attention to detail, be diligent on execution and possess strong organizational habits. The position is not for a passive order taker, processing orders/emails. The position is a revenue generator for the company and will require full hands on involvement. As the company’s product offerings evolve this role will be essential in translating sales opportunities to advertising system formats to allow for clear, concise, and consistent processes.</p>
<p><b>Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Analyze campaigns, implement and offer optimization suggestions of campaign that are performing below benchmarks</li>
<li>Address day-to-day technical issues and questions from clients and internal personnel</li>
<li>Ensure creative adheres to technical specifications, including testing and troubleshooting third party ads</li>
<li>Weekly reporting and trafficking Q/A of campaigns</li>
<li>Identify opportunities to implement solutions that increase the effectiveness and efficiencies of the Ad Ops department, including the development of tools, process and reports related to job responsibilities</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Qualifications:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>A bachelor’s degree from 4 year university, or equivalent experience</li>
<li>Minimum 1-2 year working in ad trafficking, or data analysis</li>
<li>Strong data analysis skills and experience working with large data sets.</li>
<li>Fundamental understanding of HTML/Javascript is preferred</li>
<li>Strong organizational, project management, problem-solving skills with proven ability to identify and prioritize critical issues, and the ability to self-prioritize tasks</li>
<li>Ability to work cross functionally with excellent communication and teamwork skills</li>
<li>Knowledge of DFP/Exchange/RTB platforms and online advertising a plus</li>
<li>Entrepreneurial spirit and full of initiative, and ready to roll up your sleeves</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Compensation:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Competitive Base salary</li>
<li>Health &amp; Benefits</li>
</ul>
<p>This position is based in New York City.</p>
<p><i>Please send a cover letter and resume to </i><i>hr@brideclick.net</i><i>.</i></p>
