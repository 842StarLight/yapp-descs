<h2>Vice President of Sales, Health Plans</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Ovia Health is the leading family health and technology company for women and families on their reproductive health journeys. Our mobile apps for fertility, pregnancy, and parenting empower people to take control of their health and navigate parenthood with confidence. We are on a mission to improve fertility and maternity healthcare, and to help women and families live fuller, healthier lives at home and at work.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
You will succeed at Ovia Health if you are innately entrepreneurial and passionate about our solutions, like being held accountable to hard performance goals, and have a lot of hustle. This position will report to the Senior Vice President of Enterprise Sales.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
In your role as Vice President of Health Plan Sales, you will be focused on growing the Health Plan footprint through the creation of partnerships with leading Health Plans in their <b>Commercial ASO, Fully insured and Medicaid business</b>. You will increase our booked business within the health plan space and leverage your strong experience and network. One of your primary objectives will be to position Ovia Health as an embedded benefit in the Fully Insured and Medicaid products by 2020. This will be an individual contributor role.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p>
This dynamic, professional and energetic team player will bring a stellar background on solution selling, customer acquisition marketing and account management to the role. You enjoy interacting with people and building relationships, thinking strategically and are relentless in the purpose of a strong pipeline that ultimately leads to business revenue.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Manage and grow Ovia's relationships with Commercial ASO, Fully Insured and Medicaid health plans</li>
<li>Build and demonstrate an in-depth understanding of Ovia Health solutions for health plans, including Medicaid.</li>
<li>Help create the strategic roadmap for Ovia Health in the Medicaid market</li>
<li>Understand competitive landscape, technology, and market needs to craft and communicate appropriate value proposition.</li>
<li>Maximize, drive and achieve revenue and sales goals, including management of the entire end-to-end sales process.</li>
<li>Ability to build sales plan by strategically and targeting prospects and identify opportunities that will lead to pipeline growth. (i.e.,email campaigns, cold calls, trade shows, LinkedIn Sales Navigator, work with inside sales.
</li>
<li>Secure meetings with necessary buying influencers including C-level audiences across a matrix of roles and areas including Sales, Product, Clinical.</li>
<li>Prepare and perform all product demonstrations and presentations to key stakeholders. Demonstrate consultative selling experience by assessing and capturing needs of prospective clients and defining the unique value of Ovia Health solutions for each health plan prospect.
</li>
<li>Understand and help define the decision making process for each prospect including identification of champion, decision maker(s), and influencers, clear identification of next steps with appropriate urgency to close opportunities.</li>
<li>Demonstrated ability to craft and present proposals, complete RFPs, RFIs and negotiate for a win.
</li>
<li>Collaborate with business development, clinical, product management, inside sales, and account management during the sales process and through handoff.</li>
<li>Provide feedback and input for product/service enhancements.
</li>
<li>Maintain up to date account information in Salesforce including contacts, opportunities, pipeline with appropriate placement of opportunities by sales stage and close date to ensure accurate forecast.
</li>
<li>Be curious, self-motivated, outgoing and engaging with a self-directed entrepreneurial spirit
</li>
<li>Have a proven history of being highly organized and detail oriented
</li>
<li>Motivate other team members and lead by example
</li>
<li>Work with minimal supervision, ability to adapt and be flexible</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Requirements</b></p>
<ul>
<li>8+ years experience selling enterprise solutions to health plans
</li>
<li>Successfully sold Software or Wellness Programs directly into commercial ASO, fully-insured and Medicaid health plans</li>
<li>Top 20% in your field
</li>
<li>Exceptional sales performance history that can be confirmed through references and documentation
</li>
<li>Proven experience in new business development in the health plan space</li>
<li>Have run the entire sales process from prospect to closure for health plans
</li>
<li>Excellent communication skills and analytical skills
</li>
<li>Has proven grasp of Value and Solution Selling
</li>
<li>Has worked in both large and small companies selling business application software
</li>
<li>Proven network within the health plan vertical
</li>
<li>Takes initiative and drives for measurable revenue results
</li>
<li>Strategic thinker and sharp problem solver who does not shy away, but rather is motivated by seemingly intractable challenges</li>
</ul>
