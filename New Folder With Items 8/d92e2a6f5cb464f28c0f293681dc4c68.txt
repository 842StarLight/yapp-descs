<h2>Regional Warehouse Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div><div><div><div>
<p>Thrasher is seeking a purpose driven Regional Warehouse Manager to help us redefine our industry by constantly challenging the status quo. The Regional Warehouse Manager is responsible for overseeing all of our warehouse operations at our five locations to ensure we are operating with safe, effective, and efficient processes. This position is responsible for making recommendations to improve productivity, quality and efficiency to the company’s operations. Additionally, we expect employees to show enterprise commitment and be willing to do duties outside of their normal job responsibilities.<br>
</p>
<p></p>

</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>WHAT YOU’LL DO
</b></p>
<p><b>1. Lead, Manage, Accountability (LMA)
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Develop, lead and manage associates within the warehouse, tool room and fleet shop. Manages performance of warehouse and fleet associates and recognize, train, coach, counsels, and takes corrective action when warranted.
</li>
<li>Develop a strategy that outlines the capacity planning requirements, including facilities, equipment, and staff, to meet the current and anticipated growth goals of the company. Leader for satellite locations.
</li>
</ul>
<p><b>2. Right Materials in the Right Place at the Right Time
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Develop and implement the replenishment plan, organization, and maintenance for all warehouse locations. Ensure all materials are returned to stock locations weekly.
</li>
<li>Manage the material movement process to be efficient and effective. Communicate to material coordinator slow moving and obsolete inventory.
</li>
<li>Be proactive to prevent challenges and root cause problems for our production team. Ensure morning crew loading runs smoothly and is timely.
</li>
</ul>
<p><b>3. Systematic Processes completed Correctly and Timely Across the Network
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Ensure the following is done accurately – train if needed and audit to verify.
<ul>
<li>Inbound Receiving – Receive the paperwork and ensure physically stored in the right place.</li>
<li>Cycle Adjustments – Correct reason codes are used and understand they why of the adjustment
</li>
<li>Requisitions/Transfer Orders/Sales Orders – Complete on a weekly basis and ensure material flow matches.
</li>
<li>Seek improvements to streamline processes and gain efficiencies. Drive inventory accuracy.</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>Subject matter expert in our WMS system to train, use and audit our system applications.
</li>
</ul>
<p><b>4. Expense Management
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Analyze and drive best material flow balancing warehouse working capital, transportation expenses and labor costs to drive the lowest cost scenario to meet business needs.</li>
<li>Develop the annual budget spends for various expenditures.</li>
<li>Drive actions to minimize expenditures while driving best practices in how our teams utilize tools.</li>
<li>Evaluate monthly spend against the budget and prior year providing value-added information use to favorably impact costs<br>
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>KNOWLEDGE, SKILLS, ABILITIES, AND REQUIREMENTS
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree in Business, Operations, Supply Chain or a related field; or equivalent experience.
</li>
<li>Minimum 3 years’ experience in supply chain processes and systems.
</li>
<li>Minimum 3 years’ experience to managing complex projects and working independently to achieve goals.
</li>
<li><b>Minimum 5 years’ experience managing a diverse team in multiple locations.
</b></li>
<li>Demonstrated ability of critical thinking and analytical problem-solving skills to create efficiencies.
</li>
<li>Strong interpersonal skills with ability to collaborate effectively with all functions and levels of the organization.</li>
<li>Excellent written and verbal communication skills.
</li>
<li>Organized and detail-oriented with superb follow-through.
</li>
<li>Ability to multi-task and manage multiple complex issues.
</li>
<li>Ability to roll sleeves up and get work done.
</li>
<li>Adept with Microsoft Office Suite, Google platform (Email, Drive, Groups, etc.)
</li>
<li>Working knowledge of budgets, forecasting and metrics.<br>
</li>
</ul>
