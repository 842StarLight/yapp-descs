<h2>Senior HR Business Partner</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>At Rackspace, HR is more than just solving people problems. It’s having a seat at the table, partnering with business leaders, and delivering results. Rackspace is a dynamic company with opportunities to grow in multiple areas whether it’s supporting product/technology, government services, sales &amp; marketing, M&amp;A activity, or overall people and culture strategy, Rackspace is a place to learn and grow your career. Here, we call our employees "Rackers." That's not a title or a label - it is an attitude and reflection of who we are, what we do, and most importantly how we do it. Rackers are the voice behind the call, the brains behind our expertise and innovation, and the heart behind our service to customers. Exceptionally talented, creative, smart people helping the world leverage technology to achieve a greater purpose - that's who we are.</p>
<p><b>
ESSENTIAL DUTIES &amp; RESPONSIBILITIES:</b></p>
<p>
We are seeking a Senior HR Business Partner in San Antonio, TX – someone who loves to advise leaders, understands the needs of a technology organization, activates to create people strategies that support the business plan, and wants to be part of an amazing team.</p>
<p>
Specific duties include:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Translating business strategies and issues into appropriate HR actions.</li>
<li>
Coaching key business leaders on leading and managing their organizations effectively.</li>
<li>
Diagnosing organizational effectiveness issues and implementing solutions.</li>
<li>
Understanding HR and compliance requirements of a federal business and advising leadership on related people practices.</li>
<li>
Running large scale HR processes including annual compensation cycle, performance management cycle, talent reviews, engagement surveys, and more.</li>
<li>
Maintaining Workforce and Organizational Planning</li>
<li>
Managing employee relations concerns and escalations.</li>
<li>
Partnering with Talent Attraction to recruit world-class talent, including representing Rackspace at talent events.</li>
<li>
Influencing a winning culture in our San Antonio, TX office and driving culture and engagement initiatives alongside business leaders that align to Rackspace Core Values.</li>
<li>
Working directly with the senior leadership team, as well as cross-functionally with other key internal stakeholders, to promote the internal and external brand of your business segment.</li>
</ul>
<p>
The right candidate will:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Exercise excellent judgment and make recommendations that balance the needs of employees as well as the company.</li>
<li>
Our HR Business Partners are the single point for HR for their assigned business unit, so an ability to manage multiple competing and important priorities quickly, and effectively communicate back to other HR stakeholders and business leaders is critical.</li>
<li>
Above all, a strong sense of integrity and impeccable professional maturity is a must.</li>
<li>
Maintain a holistic knowledge of all major HR functional areas (compensation, talent acquisition and talent management, benefits, etc.).</li>
<li>
Have proven HR business partner skills.</li>
<li>
Demonstrate knowledge and experience with team dynamics.</li>
<li>
Demonstrate expert knowledge of applicable employment laws, compensation philosophies, and the HR operating mechanisms.</li>
<li>
Apply excellent interpersonal skills both in one-on-one situations with all levels of personnel, and in facilitating group meetings and keeping them on track.</li>
<li>
Use seasoned judgment to suggest approaches that optimize among customer needs, business/technological constraints.</li>
<li>
Understand the principles of change management and is able to architect and execute a change effort.</li>
<li>
Consistently apply effective process management and project management skills.</li>
</ul>
<p>
 Other must-haves:</p>
<ul>
<li>
7+years of HR experience, including recent experience supporting federal/government teams.</li>
<li>
Experience managing clearance processes, including knowledge of onboarding and offboarding requirements.</li>
<li>
BS/BA in Management, Human Resources, Organizational Psychology, or equivalent experience.</li>
<li>
PHR/SPHR preferred.</li>
<li>
Advanced knowledge of Microsoft Office; experience with an HRIS System-based software.</li>
<li>
Strong organizational and planning skills.</li>
<li>
Ability to maintain confidentiality.</li>
<li>
Ability to work well with others both in person and across other offices through remote communications.</li>
<li>
Excellent communication skills, including the ability to effectively communicate and integrate with geographically dispersed leaders and teams.</li>
<li>
Strong problem solving ability with prioritization and attention to detail.</li>
</ul>
