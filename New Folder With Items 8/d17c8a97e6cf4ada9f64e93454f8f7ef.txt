<h2>Epic Assoc Analyst</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<div><div>
<p><b>Job Summary:</b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>Under direct supervision, configures programs, screens, pathways, reports, and/or job instructions to meet detailed user specifications. Supports I/S Epic Analysts in providing technical assistance to customer departments and maintains databases for one or more applications supported by the team. Works with assigned departments to prioritize development initiatives and activities. Maintains system hardware and /or software for one or more of the supported applications. Working with more advanced Analysts, provides analysis of workflow, recommends solutions, and implements application solutions to meet departmental needs. Coordinates and resolves system problems related to technology used by assigned systems. Supports internal customers in such functional areas including, but not limited to, nursing, ancillary departments, Physician practice, patient accounting, medical records, finance, human resources, purchasing, sales, and contracts. Assists in managing system upgrades and other small projects.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><i>All team members are responsible for bringing life to our Purpose Statement – Inspire health. Serve with compassion. Be the difference – through every action and every interaction at work.<br>
</i></p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Responsibilities:</b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>Performs the following under moderate to heavy supervision by the Project Manager and/or IS Epic Senior Analyst: Provides user functionality by maintaining application tables/ profiles/ dictionaries, builds screens and/or pathways, and develops application support procedures to support customer requirements.</p>
<p>Performs the following with moderate supervision by the IS Project Manager and/or IS Senior Epic Analyst: Supports customer relations and resolving customer issues related to software applications before involving the Manager. Interacts with users to develop plans to resolve their problems regarding the use of applications.</p>
<p>Functions as a technical liaison with the respective vendors for all implementation and post implementation activities of specific Epic modules. Functions as a department contact and provides project management support as required.</p>
<p>Completes tasks on time and meet assignment dates. Accurately checks processes and tasks and ensures data integrity for assigned Epic and/or legacy applications, testing to ensure new code does not impact previous version or other systems.</p>
<p>Performs the following with moderate supervision by the I/S Project Manager and/or I/S Sr Epic Analyst. Participated in Prisma Health's disaster recovery planning for assigned applications of support.</p>
<p>Optional where appropriate: Creates training materials. Incorporates various learning techniques to achieve desired outcomes. Continually assesses curriculum to ensure users are able to pick up the knowledge needed to succeed when using the systems.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Requirements:</b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>Education: Bachelor's degree in Information services, health administration, business administration, health sciences or a related field. In<br>
lieu of the educational and experience requirements listed above an Associate's Degree in Computer Science or a directly related field and 2 years of Related Information Technology experience in a Business, Computer Science, or Clinical field/setting; or a High School Diploma or equivalent and 5 years related Information Technology experience in a Business, Computer Science, or Clinical<br>
field/setting may be considered.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>Experience: No previous experience required.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>Licensure, Registry or Certification Required: Required to obtain and maintain an EPIC module certification as required by the position.<br>
Certification must be obtained within 6 months of hire date.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>Knowledge/Skills/Abilities: SQL database knowledge; Project management software, concepts and tracking; Health Care Software Applications - Preferred Related Information Technology experience in a business, computer science or health care setting. Related Information Technology experience in a business, computer science or health care setting preferred. Microsoft Office products.</p>
