<p>GSS a leading importer of Video Security products, is currently recruiting a Sales Executive to join our City of Industry, CA Sales Team. This is a new and exciting opportunity within the organization for energetic, creative and dynamic sales professional to develop sales on national basis.<br>
The ideal candidate should come from the security products sales industry, in a current or previous Sales/Business Development role. A second language Spanish, Korean or Chinese is desirable but not essential.</p>
<p>
Summary of Duties:<br>
– Achieve assigned targets<br>
– Present and sell company products and services to current and potential clients on an international level<br>
– Construct personal sales lead list<br>
– Develop new accounts and expands customer base in the international market<br>
– Prepare action plans and schedules to identify specific targets and to project the number of contacts to be made<br>
– New business development<br>
– Provide customers with sales materials and current product knowledge<br>
– Execute special sales activities to reduce stock<br>
– Establish and maintain current client and potential client relationships<br>
– Account Management<br>
– Identify and resolve client concerns<br>
– Prepare a variety of status reports, including activity, closings, follow-up, and adherence to goals.<br>
– Communicate new product and service opportunities, special developments, information, or feedback gathered through field activity to appropriate company staff<br>
– Ability to travel &amp; achieve sales targets<br>
– Assumes the responsibility for the creation of notes, personal schedules and reminders to ensure effective and efficient time management.</p>
<p>
Essential Operational Experience<br>
– Strong negotiation and professional communication skills (verbal and written)<br>
– Exhibits excellent time management skills and very detail oriented.<br>
– Previous Sales experience essential, at least 1 yrs+ experience in CCTV industry<br>
– Customer relations experience<br>
– Experience dealing with customers</p>
<p>
Candidate Requirements:<br>
– Role will suit self starter, energetic, passionate person, who is looking to add value to the team<br>
– Excellent technical proficiency<br>
– Energetic, confident and motivated with the ability to think on your feet.<br>
– Ability to persuade and influence others. Ability to develop and deliver presentations<br>
– Ability to create, compose, and edit written materials<br>
– Strong interpersonal and communication skills. Knowledge of advertising and sales promotion techniques.<br>
– 1 to 5 years of sales, installer or technical consulting experience<br>
– Bilingual is preferred not a must<br>
– Visibility requires maintaining a professional appearance and providing a positive company image to the public<br>
– Excellent Negotiation Skills</p>
