<p><b>Job Description
</b></p>
<p></p>
<p><b>JOB SUMMARY
</b></p>
<p>A Dealer Setup Administrator receives, examines and ensures documentation is complete, accurate and in compliance with state and company guidelines. This position will work with dealerships, external, and internal customers to obtain and verify information.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>JOB DUTIES
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Dealer set-up to include assigning new dealer numbers, ensuring the dealer agreement documents are filled out completely and signed, entering dealer information into proper systems.
</li>
<li>Contacts external and internal customers to obtain missing or inaccurate information.
</li>
<li>Maintains the Dealer Services Support line by answering and resolving dealer calls in a professional, timely and efficient manner while providing effective customer service.
</li>
<li>Responds to a variety of internal and external customer inquiries in a timely and thorough manner.
</li>
<li>Partners with the sales team to meet and exceed dealer’s service expectations.
</li>
<li>Maintain thorough knowledge and understanding of Exeter’s policies and procedures, as well as, state and federal laws.
</li>
<li>Develop processes, tools and job aids that will help provide the most efficient and effective operations.
</li>
<li>Assists with the training of new employees.
</li>
<li>Adherence to the attendance policy.
</li>
<li>Other duties as assigned.
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>QUALIFICATIONS-EDUCATION AND WORK EXPERIENCE
 </b></p>
<ul>
<li>High school diploma or equivalent
 </li>
<li>Auto finance experience preferred
 </li>
<li>Strong attention to detail and sense of urgency
 </li>
<li>Ability to build rapport and relationships
 </li>
<li>Typing and ten key skills
 </li>
<li>Good written and verbal communication skills
 </li>
<li>Must be highly organized and able to prioritize workload
 </li>
<li>Ability to handle high volume of paperwork
 </li>
<li>Ability to use MS Office products, proprietary software and general office equipment
</li>
</ul>
