<p><b>Position Summary
</b></p>
<p><b>MUST have Credit Union Experience</b></p>
<p><b>
CU*Base experience Preferred</b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
This position plays a critical role in the success of client relationships and the overall conversion process with our clients. This individual works with other members of the Conversions team on project management, vendor communications, performing data entry, and configurations related to a credit union's conversion.
</p>
<p>CU*Answers is seeking an individual with excellent project management skills, extensive knowledge of credit union operations, and the ability to take ownership of critical functions within the organization.<br>
</p>
