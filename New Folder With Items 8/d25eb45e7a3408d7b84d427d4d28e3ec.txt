<h2>Cyber Security Engineer II</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<b>Job Description Summary</b><br>
Security is foundational to Transamerica and we are growing our Information Security Team. We are looking for a multi-talented Cyber Security Engineer II. We are a fast paced energetic team dedicated to making our enterprise more secure. So if you have at least 3-5 years cyber experience, are an exceptional team player have great Franken-skills; come join our industry leading, global organization.<br>
<b>Job Description</b><p><b>
Functional Job Activities:</b></p>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>
Gathers data, analyzes and drafts reports, including department, project, key indicators and dashboards. Ability to quickly develop excellent working relationships with peers and key stakeholders, such as business partners, legal, internal audit, risk, and technology specialists</li>
<li>
Working within our Information Security team, the Cyber Security Engineer II plays a key role in the identification, assessment, prioritization and remediation processes of cyber security risks and vulnerabilities</li>
<li>
Evaluates cyber security threats, risk, vulnerabilities and processes to determine relative risk to the product, system and organization</li>
<li>
Applies knowledge of controls, threats, vulnerabilities, risk and technical systems to build strategic planning and roadmaps to harden systems and close vulnerabilities</li>
<li>
Help transform control culture from current detective controls to preventative controls</li>
<li>
Help build security as competitive differentiator for sales enablement</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Essential Skills:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
3-5 years cyber security engineering experience with a focus on Vulnerability Management</li>
<li>
Information Security vulnerability management experience; ideally with code, application and infrastructure vulnerabilities</li>
<li>
Experience with one or more of the following cyber security domain areas: STIGs, security within SDLC, data level encryption, key management systems, web certifications, application awareness, Data handling and classification, cyber resiliency, secure coding</li>
<li>
Experience in threat intelligence and persistent threat management, incident response and/or crisis management</li>
<li>
Information Security controls experience with NIST CSF, NIST SP 800-53, FISMA, ISO 27001 or COBIT or other applicable control frameworks</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Additional Required Skills:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Proficiency with Microsoft Office, Preference given to candidates with deep Excel and PowerPoint skill sets</li>
<li>
Proficiency in one or more GRC systems including Service Now, Archer, RiskVision, RSAM or equivalent</li>
<li>
Experience with systems such as Remedy, JIRA, Tanium, and equivalent</li>
<li>
Experience with scanning tools such as Fortify, Web Inspect, Nexpose, Twistlock, Nessus, Service Now, Kenna</li>
<li>
Experience with penetration testing exercises, tools and objectives</li>
<li>
Ability to fluently read, write and speak English</li>
<li>
Experience with leading group discussion and presenting to varying levels and audiences</li>
<li>
Self-motivated and self-management skills</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Preferred Skills, Education and Certifications:</b></p>
<p>
Strong knowledge of Information Security and Cyber Defense demonstrated by one or more of the following:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Bachelorette degree in Information/Cyber Security, Information Risk, Information Risk Management</li>
<li>
Bachelorette degree in Information Systems, Computer Science, Information Management or similar four year technical degree combined with one or more of the following:</li>
<li>
Active CISSP, CISM or CISA certificates</li>
<li>
Experience in Insurance, Payments, Banking or other Fin-Tech Industries</li>
<li>
Strong preference for candidate with excellent Excel and PowerPoint skills</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Preferred Skills, Education and Certifications:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
The preferred location for this role is in Denver, CO with a secondary location option of Cedar Rapids, IA</li>
<li>
Must be available to travel up to 20%</li>
<li>
Must be able to pass extensive security background check</li>
</ul>
