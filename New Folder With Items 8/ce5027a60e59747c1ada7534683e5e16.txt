<h2>Product Marketing Specialist I, Industrial Fluids (757)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Position Summary:</b></p>
<p>The Product Marketing Specialist is responsible for developing and delivering marketing plans and programs to support the growth and expansion of company products and services. Develops and executes communication programs that effectively describe and promote the organization and its products, including presentations, brochures, company or product fact sheets, and/or other promotional assets. Researches and develops market intelligence and content for publication of products. Provides reports based on information collected such as market trends, competition, new products, and pricing.</p>
<p><b>Job Responsibilities:</b></p>
<p>This role has primary support for the following markets: Offshore; Specialty Coatings; Metal Finishing; Cans; Mining</p>
<ul>
<li>Manages development and implementation of marketing plans for the purpose of achieving specific marketing strategies and sales targets. Ensuring that project schedules and budgets are met</li>
<li>Creates communications programs that effectively describe and promote the organization and its products including presentations, brochures, company or product fact sheets, or other promotional assets.</li>
<li>Develops and executes plans for all major types of advertising media, such as print, digital and social, and advises on techniques to ensure that advertising is engaging and aimed at target users.</li>
<li>Maintains communications with outside entities including media and agencies to maintain good public relations. • Gathers, analyzes and reports on the business results of recently introduced or enhanced products and marketing initiatives. •</li>
<li>Analyzes competitors' products, prices, sales, and methods of marketing, summarizes and publishes reports.</li>
<li>Monitors, analyzes, and documents market trends. Observes social, economic, and political trends (PESTLE) that might have an effect on the organization, and makes recommendations to enhance the organization's image based on those trends.</li>
<li>Integrates market research data with Pipeline, CRM or other systems to create new lead</li>
<li>Conducts and supports market research</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Education, Experience &amp; Skills:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor's Degree from four-year college or university; or minimum six years related experience and/or training; or equivalent combination of education and experience. Familiar with a variety of the field's concepts, practices, and procedures</li>
<li>3+ years related experience and/or training; or equivalent combination of education and experience.</li>
<li>Knowledge of marketing/commerce with emphasis on industrial marketing.</li>
<li>Experience in industrial fields related to metalworking (machine tools, tools, lubricants, other consumables, etc.) preferred • Experience in channel management (Sales, marketing, etc) preferred.</li>
<li>Experience in organizing seminars, lectures, workshops and trade fairs and developing and presenting commercial justification</li>
<li>Must possess commercial creativity.</li>
</ul>
