<br>
<b>Job Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Customarily and regularly provides technical expertise and recommendations in assessing new software solutions to meet the company’s business requirements.</li>
<li>
Implements security standards and ensures adherence to compliance standards.</li>
<li>
Prepares communications and creates presentations on system enhancements and / or alternatives.</li>
<li>
Responsible for assisting in evaluating, optimizing, harmonizing, and standardizing existing systems.</li>
<li>
Responsible for analyzing information and possible solutions, making independent judgments, decisions, and recommendations.</li>
<li>
Customarily and regularly assists development leaders designing applications using appropriate systems architecture, database structures, communication, and technologies.</li>
<li>
Assists in developing and managing the maintenance of assigned systems, applications, and processes, including back-up, downtime procedures, disaster recovery, and process monitoring.</li>
<li>
Performs software solution Systems Analysis; interpreting and documenting product and business requirements.</li>
<li>
Manages Solution Delivery Lifecycle for products, running release planning, sprint planning, and daily standup meetings.</li>
<li>
Serves as communication / reporting liaison between sales, product management, and IT for status of SDLC Project Management.</li>
<li>
Customarily and regularly monitors quality standards ensuring failure-free operation.</li>
<li>
Responsible for trouble-shooting technical issues and identifying modifications needed in existing applications.</li>
<li>
Performs other related duties as assigned.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Education/Qualifications</b><b>:</b></p>
<ul><li>
Bachelor’s Degree in Computer Science, IT or combination of education and work experience in a related field required.</li></ul>
<p><b>
Job Knowledge/Work Experience</b><b>:</b></p>
<ul><li>
0-2 years of actual on the job experience with increasing responsibility in Systems Analysis, SDLC or IT development required.</li></ul>
<p><b>
Technical Skills</b><b>:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Proficiency in Microsoft Office applications, especially Visio, Project, and TFS.</li>
<li>
Basic understanding of Computer Science and Software Development lifecycle.</li>
<li>
Working knowledge of personal computer / network server hardware, assorted computer peripherals, including printers and networking equipment, such as switches and routers.</li>
</ul>
