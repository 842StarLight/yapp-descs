<h2>2020 Spring - Partnership Operations Intern</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Workiva is searching for talented Interns to join Partnerships team. This is an exciting opportunity to help internal and external customers be as successful as possible while building relationships across the organization. During your time at Workiva you will learn what it’s like working at a high-energy and fast-paced software company. Workiva has a fantastic summer internship program where our interns are given opportunities to grow professionally and create connections across the company.
</p>
<p><b>What You'll Do:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Support partnerships team in data collection and interpretation of data to drive increased efficiency</li>
<li>
Evaluate partnership program performance (win/loss, efficiencies, etc) to identify areas for improvement
</li>
<li>Complete tracking and reporting tasks as assigned
</li>
<li>Contribute to lifecycle and automation functions of partnerships</li>
<li>
Develop working relationships across the organization to better integrate Strategic Partnerships with teams we support and that are supported by us. (i.e. Sales Operations, Services, Information Technology, Legal, Finance, and Accounting)</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
What You'll Need:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Ability to work independently and take initiative</li>
<li>
Currently pursuing a degree in finance, management, business analytics or a related field</li>
<li>
Grade Point Average of 3.0 or higher</li>
<li>
Must be available to work 15-20 hours per work during the semester</li>
</ul>
