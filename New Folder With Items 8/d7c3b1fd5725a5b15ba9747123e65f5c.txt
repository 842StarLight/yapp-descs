<b>What will you do?</b><br>
<br>
The primary responsibility of the SAP Business One Consultant is to ensure that the software system design and client roll out are executed with a high level of customer satisfaction. On a per-project basis, the consultant will maintain their work, documentation, estimates, communications, and facilitate successful implementations.<br>
<br>
<b>Your Essential Duties and Responsibilities as an SAP Business One Consultant:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Ensure the successful implementation of SAP Business One and software for Softengine’s clients</li>
<li>Identify statement of work, including customizations and reports, workflow automations, training, and process design</li>
<li>Utilize project tracking software and other tools to document specific decisions and workflow processes as required</li>
<li>Perform discoveries with prospects, clients to gather business requirements and processes</li>
<li>Configure various modules within SAP Business One (i.e. Advanced Financials, Advanced Inventory, Order Management, Demand Planning, Fixed Assets, Revenue Recognition etc.)</li>
<li>Perform data migration tasks from legacy systems to SAP Business One</li>
<li>Perform system testing and issue resolution support</li>
<li>Troubleshoot and resolve SAP Business One solutions</li>
<li>Complete project tasks as assigned, on time and budget</li>
<li>Provide project status updates to project manager on a regular basis</li>
<li>Develop User Acceptance Testing scripts and Perform customer training both on-site and remotely</li>
</ul>
