<h2>Advertising Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>About Everywhere Wireless</b></p>
<p>Everywhere Wireless is Chicago's fastest growing Internet Service Provider (ISP) changing the way internet is being delivered. The company competes with Comcast and AT&amp;T to provide internet access to business and residential customers throughout Chicago and its first ring suburbs. Using the most advanced microwave and fiber-optic technology, EW has disrupted the status quo in the ISP space by providing customers with a superior Internet experience, all-the-way-around. Just see our 5-star Google and Yelp! Ratings.</p>
<p><b>Position</b></p>
<p>We’re looking for a passionate and creative individual with fearless ideas to capitalize on the potential of our department for B2B and B2C marketing. Our customer base, spanning from affordable housing communities to the luxury homes of 9 West Walton (arguably Chicago’s most premier condo building), and popular landmarks such as the Shedd Aquarium and Chicago beaches, offers you a massive playground to execute varied projects.</p>
<p>You will serve as a master of our digital platforms, using your expertise with Google Adwords, Analytics, and other digital marketing platforms. Task your team to intensify the reach of our current marketing, including our puppy mascot – Spotty, visible throughout much of Chicago. Overall, you will develop and manage marketing programs across different channels, foster customer retention, advance brand building, and support business objectives.</p>
<p><b>What You'll Do: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Craft the company’s digital marketing landscape including website, mobile, social media, and email marketing</li>
<li>Lead the execution of marketing programs from start to finish, leveraging internal support and driving collaboration</li>
<li>Analyze customer insights, consumer trends, market analysis, and marketing best practices to build successful strategies, increase customer conversions, and generate more qualified leads</li>
<li>Coordinate with all departments to create and execute campaigns for business development, including lobby and convention-level events</li>
<li>Create and publish all marketing material in line with marketing plans; plan and implement promotional campaigns</li>
<li>Work in partnership with the support and sales team to streamline direct-customer marketing events</li>
<li>Effectively manage communication throughout departments, passing information to all parties to avoid gaps in new procedures</li>
<li>Manage content and updates for customer and internal touch points using our CRM</li>
<li>Present ideas and final deliverables to internal and external teams, and communicate with senior leaders about marketing programs and strategies</li>
<li>Be a hands-on, leading by example and encouraging teamwork</li>
</ul>

<li>Experience utilizing website analytics tools such as Google Analytics</li>
<li>Experience with Google Adwords and SEO</li>
<li>Excellent written and verbal communication skills</li>
<li>Proven experience developing and instituting successful marketing campaigns</li>
<li>Confident, outgoing personality</li>
<li>Strong problem-solving skills</li>
<li>Strong project management skills</li>
<li>Great listener willing to work with the team in place and participate in new ideas</li>
</ul>

<p><b>Why EW: </b></p>
<p>Everywhere Wireless is an ideal place to work. If you have a strong work ethic, an entrepreneurial spirit, and a common-sensed approach to business, you will fit right in with the company culture. EW values initiative and rewards employees that consistently exceed goals, objectives and expectations. EW is headquartered in the West Loop technology hub alongside Google, Echo, Belly, Jump Trading, Lightbank and many other fast growing technology companies.
