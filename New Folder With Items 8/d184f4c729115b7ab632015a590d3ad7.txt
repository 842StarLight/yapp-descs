<h2>Global Alliances Strategy and Execution Leader</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>The Business Development Portfolio and Execution Manager will support the Enterprise Alliances Global Alliances and Business Development team, in “running business development” and in establishing best practice business development and partner innovation processes and collaboration across the entire Equifax Enterprise. This position will identity and execute on opportunities to build and mature the business development and partner innovation functions for all verticals and regions. This role will establish routines, metrics, and cadences to ensure initiatives meet desired objectives according to specifications and cost, as well as identify opportunities to increase productivity and efficiency and enable increased revenue growth.</p>
<p></p>
<p>
This role will manage highly complex, cross regional programs including activities such as process development, portfolio management of Global Alliance and Business Development initiatives, documentation and tracking, building a socialization and overall communication plan for Global Alliances, regional business development, and communication across multiple senior management stakeholders.</p>
<p></p>
<p>
This position will drive executional excellence, liaising with other Global Regions, Business Units and Centers of Excellence, representing Global Alliances and Business Development on corporate projects, as needed, to ensure high performance by addressing roadblocks.</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Works with leadership and other global cross functional teams to ensure priorities are aligned with the strategic objectives and growth initiatives of the Equifax enterprise and enhance cross divisional coordination</li>
<li>
Develops, plans and provides secondary implementation support of Global Alliance business development and innovation across the enterprise</li>
<li>
Leads and facilitates projects and key BD&amp;I activities in support of all regions, divisions, verticals and capabilities</li>
<li>
Designs, plans, communicates internally and to customers, while managing risk, establishing and ensuring on time execution, within budget</li>
<li>
Develops business cases, evaluates execution and reports on ongoing global projects and partnerships</li>
<li>
Performs project assessments by defining the project objectives, requirements and establishing a high level road map in order to ensure project alignment with the strategic plan, customer or vertical initiative</li>
<li>
Works with key internal global partners to develop and drive goals, define parameters and implement detailed plans for projects and initiatives for regions, divisions, verticals and capabilities</li>
<li>
Ensures the work is being performed according to required quality standards, and changes are managed appropriately</li>
<li>
Establishes global project and program management standards, using industry best practices and enterprise standards, in order to drive efficiency and consistency within projects throughout all phases of project management</li>
<li>
Consolidates project/process data using predefined reporting tools and methods in order to monitor project/process performance and provide leadership team performance metrics to drive business group performance</li>
<li>
Meets all business unit/vertical budget targets</li>
<li>
Makes decisions guided by policies in nonstandard situations that impact the achievement of customer, operational, or project objectives</li>
</ul>
