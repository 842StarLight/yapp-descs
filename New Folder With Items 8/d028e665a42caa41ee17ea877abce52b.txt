<h2>Patient Account Representative- IL- Dental Medicine Clinic</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p><b>Summary
</b></p>
<p>The Patient Account Representative is one of a team of Patient Account Representatives who are responsible for performing a variety of front office procedures. The position reports to the Manager of Patient Accounts for the Midwestern University Dental Medicine Clinic. Must be able to adapt to work schedule changes based on the hours of the clinic.</p>
<p><b>
Essential Duties and Responsibilities
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Greet and direct patients and visitors</li>
<li>Assist with patient scheduling and registration</li>
<li>Answer phones and patient questions</li>
<li>Enter patient information into the computer system</li>
<li>Perform cashiering, billing, refunds to patient and third parties, payment plans, and collections</li>
<li>Work in conjunction with the Manager of Patient Accounts to help reach and maintain financial and accounts receivable goals for the clinic</li>
<li>Other duties may be assigned</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Supervisory Responsibilities</b></p>
<p>
This position has no supervisory responsibilities.</p>

Reasonable accommodations may be made to enable individuals with disabilities to perform the essential functions.</p>
