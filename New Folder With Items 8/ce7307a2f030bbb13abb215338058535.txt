<h2>Sales Executive</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div><b>SALES EXECUTIVE</b></div>
<div><b>POSITION SUMMARY</b></div>
<div>At Quest Oracle Community, you’ll join an exciting team dedicated to creating a digital community and online experience that is changing the way users of Oracle enterprise software solutions connect, share and learn. Take a look at this position and if you’re ready to join one of the best employers in Lexington, KY - apply below!</div>
<div></div>
<div><b>ESSENTIAL FUNCTIONS AND RESPONSIBILITIES</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Manage the sales and fulfillment of contracted inventory</li>
<li>Develop and execute sales strategies to achieve sales goals</li>
<li>Proactively pursuing new accounts</li>
<li>Managing and ensuring growth of existing accounts</li>
<li>Building presentations and proposals specifically designed to grow existing and developmental business</li>
<li>Develop strategic plans for prospecting and cultivating new business growth</li>
<li>Using Salesforce to create and track contracts</li>
<li>Accurately reporting of activity, projections, and forecasting</li>
<li>Serve as the primary contact for exhibit and sponsorship sales for Quest conferences and support with show floor management function</li>
<li>Represent Quest at meetings and events as assigned</li>
<li>Perform other duties and assist with periodic special projects</li>
<li>Ensuring the receipt of all payments associated with but not limited to contracted items</li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<div><b>QUALIFICATIONS REQUIRED</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree</li>
<li>Proven sales experience; especially in sponsorship sales, event exhibit sales, and digital sponsorship sales</li>
<li>Strong history of new business development</li>
<li>Excellent organizational skills, with ability to manage multiple priorities and to exhibit a moderate amount of self-sufficiency around the tasks associated with the position</li>
<li>Highly motivated individual with exceptional communication and customer service skills</li>
<li>Positive attitude and comfortable working in a fast-paced environment</li>
<li>Enjoy working in a true team environment</li>
<li>Ability to travel as needed</li>
<li>Proficiency in Microsoft Office and Salesforce</li>
</ul>
