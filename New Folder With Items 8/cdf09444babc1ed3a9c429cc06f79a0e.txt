<div><p>ESSENTIAL FUNCTIONS:<br>
<br>
? Maintain customer relations to the highest level possible by making personal visits to enhance company presence as required. If appropriate, meet with retail customer and/or insured to present an insurance program. Explain policies and/or coverage issues to retail customers and negotiate modifications with underwriters. Identify opportunities to leverage synergies with customers and insurance companies<br>
? Learn and increase systems skills to improve efficiency and Suggest changes to production-related procedures<br>

Through the submission process, help the Broker meet and exceed client expectations by clearly defining and managing client coverage goals, priorities, and overall expectations. Frequently update the client on marketing progress, as directed by the Broker. Gather and develop complete loss summaries and analysis. Prepare high quality, error free submissions and provide effective verbal presentations to underwriters. Ultimately responsible for the quality, timeliness, and accuracy of quotes, binders, checklists and other marketing file documentation. Identify and correct inconsistencies or errors in relevant documents and promptly correct/clarify same with the client and/or underwriter. Assist Broker in soliciting timely and substantive responses to submissions and effectively negotiate pricing and terms with most underwriters<br>
? Meet quality measurement goals by delivering high quality service in servicing assigned clients and accounts. Accurately communicate coverage terms and all other information between clients and underwriters. Above all, ensure that the policy delivered to client is what was promised by Brown &amp; Riding<br>
? Works with management and broker to establish revenue expectations<br>
