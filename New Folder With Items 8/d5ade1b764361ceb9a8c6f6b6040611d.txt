<p><b>Project Manager</b></p>
<p>Sunpro is seeking a <b>Project Manager</b> for our <b>Dallas, TX</b> location! This is a fast-paced leadership position overseeing all day to day operations and overseeing multiple installation crews. The Project Manager is ultimately responsible for organizing, motivating, and leading the installation team as well as ensuring the safety, quality, and efficiency of the crew, and ultimately the customer experience.</p>
<p><b>Duties &amp; Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Ensure customer satisfaction above all else!</li>
<li>Confirm that staff is aware of their daily duties and assignments</li>
<li>Monitor and oversee daily production of the office and its employees</li>
<li>Manage employee and subcontractor schedules and time reporting</li>
<li>Schedule projects and service work</li>
<li>Maintain and enforce safety and training program</li>
<li>Acquire necessary project materials, tools and equipment</li>
<li>Inspect job sites of each installation for quality and accuracy</li>
<li>Resolve project, customer, and staff escalations</li>
<li>Review project packets for completeness and accuracy</li>
<li>Oversee inspections and commissioning</li>
<li>Update appropriate statuses in project management software</li>
<li>Communicate and coordinate with territory sales manager on a regular basis</li>
<li>Oversees P&amp;L for territory</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Qualifications &amp; Requirements</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Must be a motivated team player, looking to succeed</li>
<li>5+ years construction management experience</li>
<li>Must have electrical experience/knowledge</li>
<li>Residential construction experience
</li>
<li>Excellent verbal and written communication skills</li>
<li>Excellent organizational and time management skills</li>
<li>Demonstrated ability to manage projects; installation, operations, and troubleshooting</li>
<li>Excellent people skills, able to manage, motivate and discipline if necessary</li>
<li>Professional demeanor when customer-facing</li>
<li>Experience with MS Excel, MS Word and project management tools (MS Project) preferred</li>
<li>Familiarity with Salesforce is a plus</li>
<li>Ability to manage a $1-10M P&amp;L</li>
<li>Strong work ethic and grit</li>
<li>Entrepreneurial attitude</li>
</ul>
<p><b>What’s in it for You?</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Competitive Pay and Incentives</li>
<li>Opportunities for Self-development and Career Progression</li>
<li>Health Benefits, Allowances, 401(k) with Company Match</li>
<li>Paid Holidays &amp; Vacation</li>
</ul>
