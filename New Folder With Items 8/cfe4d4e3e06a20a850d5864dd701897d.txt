Panzura is the leader in multi-cloud data management, a $68B market opportunity. Its portfolio includes Panzura Freedom NAS and Vizion.ai. Freedom NAS provides software-defined, multi-cloud file services to enterprises in over 5,500 sites globally across 33 countries. The Vizion.ai SaaS platform enables enterprises to meet the most demanding requirements for data search, analytics, and governance in a multi-cloud world. Panzura’s cloud partners include AWS, Google, IBM and Microsoft and has over 400 resellers worldwide. Organizations like Sony, Avis, American College of Radiology, LiveNation, Prosper Marketplace and Chevron use Panzura to unlock the power of multi-cloud data.</div>

<div>
Panzura’s Technical Account Manager provides personalized support for a customer’s Panzura infrastructure.</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div><b>
Job Summary:</b></div>
<div>
As Technical Account Manager you will utilize your strong technical competencies, account and escalation management skills to provide the highest level of personalized, proactive, and preventative Enterprise-Class support services. You will build a partner relationship with the customer to better serve their business needs. The Technical Account Manager role is critical to the customer in furthering the Panzura/customer relationship, and in helping to increase sales by coordinating support activities for the account. Working very closely and collaboratively with members of the Account Team and customer contacts, you will become intimately acquainted with the customer business requirements, technical needs, systems, environment and service history.</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
The Technical Account Manager educates the customer’s IT team about Panzura infrastructure best practices, tools, and resources needed to minimize risk and enable greater storage system efficiency. Because the TAM is a team member both in the customer’s business and in Panzura, business objectives are well understood and communication is streamlined.</div>
<div><b>
</b></div>
<div>
The Technical Account Manager acts as a liaison with various Panzura groups and resources to assist in providing the highest level of proactive and “reactive-ready”support services. The TAM reviews and oversees all of Panzura support activity and regularly monitors the Panzura cloudfs environment. Based on deep knowledge of both Panzura and the customer’s IT environment, the TAM is strategically positioned to help customers obtain the most from their Panzura systems. A TAM provides:</div>
<div>
</div>
<div>
1. Personalized Service</div>
<ul>
<li>Provides informed strategic planning, storage support best practices, and upgrade advice</li>
<li>Understands the customers' environment and apply Panzura knowledge to improve the overall support experience</li>
<li>Conducts regular operational service reviews and provides personalized best practice recommendations</li>
</ul>
<div>
</div>
<div>
2. Centralized support management</div>
<ul>
<li>Strategically positioned to review and oversee all of assigned customers' Panzura support activity, enabling maximum efficiency while minimizing risk</li>
<li>During any critical events, facilitates issue resolution</li>
</ul>
<div>
</div>
<div>
3. Customized Proactive Care</div>
<ul>
<li>Through a combination of expertise, analytics, tools, and an understanding of customers' operating environment, will consult and provide deliverable aimed to mitigate risk and ensure stability on Panzura Solution</li>
<li>Help educate the customer as needed on the various tools that are on Panzura Support Site and help them understand their Panzura products better.</li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
</div>
<div><b>
Qualifications:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Excellent written and verbal communication skills.</li>
<li>Good interpersonal communication and customer service skills are needed in order work successfully with customers and within Panzura</li>
<li>Escalation management experience is strongly desired.</li>
<li>Experience in handling customer expectations</li>
<li>Ability to integrate diverse perspectives in critical situations to aid issue resolutions</li>
<li>In-depth technical knowledge in Cloud, Storage, or Virtualization Technology</li>
<li>Strong aptitude for learning new technologies and understanding how to utilize them in a customer facing environment.</li>
<li>Available to travel to the customer domestic sites and other global sites as necessary.</li>
</ul>
<div>
Education &amp; Experience:</div>
<ul>
<li>Minimum of 5 years of relevant experience is required. Prior experience as a Technical Support Engineer, Escalations Engineer, Technical Account Manager, and/or a Project Manager is desired.</li>
<li>A Bachelor of Science Degree in Computer Science, Electrical Engineering, or related field is required.</li>
</ul>
