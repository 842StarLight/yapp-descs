<h2>Public Utilities Business Development Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Job Description: </b></p>
<p>Build market position by locating, developing, and defining, negotiating and closing business relationships in the public utilities sector. This position is responsible for all aspects of managing and servicing current and future clients within the Public Utility Sector. This position will also ensure business growth is delivered in accordance with the company strategy.</p>
<p><b>Responsible to: </b> Sr. Vice President of Business Development</p>
<p><b>External relationships: </b> Existing clients and new clients</p>
<p><b>Internal relationships: </b> Regional Vice Presidents, Project Managers, Regional Managers, Marketing Assistant</p>
<p><b>Location: </b>Denver, Tulsa or Houston</p>
<p><b>Rolls and Responsibilities: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Develop, review and report on current clients, contacts and opportunities to the Sr. Vice President of Business Development via weekly BD Reports and group conference call.</li>
<li>Contribute to the development of the company’s vision and goals.</li>
<li>Work within the confines of the company strategy with regards to all BD activities</li>
<li>Support the overall company goals to ensure the company maximizes it’s short, medium and long-term profitability goals</li>
<li>Marketing assistance through advertising and show attendance recommendations to the Director of Business Development or the Marketing Assistant</li>
<li>Work to develop personal knowledge of LW Survey services in order to better market clients and assist with proposal preparation</li>
<li>Work within business development budget</li>
<li>Work with other Business Development Managers on opportunities outside of the Public Utility space</li>
<li>Locate and propose potential business deals by contacting potential partners</li>
<li>Close new business deals by developing and negotiating new and existing clients</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Required Skills: </b></p>
<ul><li>Experience selling survey services in the Public Utility sector, Closing Skills, Motivation for Sales, Prospecting Skills, Sales Planning, Selling to Customer Needs, Market Knowledge, Presentation Skills, Professionalism</li></ul>
<p><b>Key Performance Indicators: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Number of real opportunities developed</li>
<li>Number of opportunities realized</li>
<li>Dollar value of realized opportunities</li>
<li>New Clients Developed</li>
</ul>

<ul>
<li>Sales: 5 years (Preferred)</li>

</ul>
