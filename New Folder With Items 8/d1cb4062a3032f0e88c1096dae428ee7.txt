<h2>Account Development Director - 1830</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Location: </b>Marlborough, MA</p>
<p><b>
Job Description:</b></p>
<div>
KIOXIA America Inc., formerly Toshiba America Inc., is looking for an Account Development Director in Marlborough, MA. As a global memory components leader, KIOXIA designs and manufactures high-quality flash memory-based storage solutions, Solid State Drives (SSDs), Application Specific NAND components that make possible today's leading edge Smartphones, Tablets, Communications Equipment, Camera Solutions, Medical devices, Enterprise server, storage solutions and more.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
Reporting to the Alliance East Sr. Director of Sales and Account Development, the ideal candidate is responsible for the leading the direction of Technical Sales Managers, Account Development Managers in daily tactical, strategic sales and account development approach to large OEM Storage/Server providers and Financial Market and Data Center customers in Eastern Canada, New England, NY/NJ, Mid Atlantic, and Southern states geography. This position will direct all sales and technical account development activities, work closely with our business units, engineering and overseas sales/marketing teams to provide best in class customer support while also promoting our leading edge products and driving sustainable growth for the responsible customers and markets. This includes developing &amp; working on sales/marketing programs, regular corporate and customer presentations, proactive account development, account planning, participation in sales meetings, and training throughout the outlined geography.<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<div>The successful candidate will develop and maintain expertise on customers, products, solutions, programs and competitive local industry intelligence. The candidate will also engage in regular customer calls, provide guidance on pre-sales and presentations.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div><b>
Responsibilities:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
Directing pre and post sales activities, support engineering on design in activities, establish and nurture relationships, identify and report on market and customer trends.</li>
<li>
Develop and maintain strong relationships with large OEM Storage/Server Providers, Financial Market and Data Center customers.</li>
<li>
Facilitate promote and support KIOXIA products business growth (i.e. SSD, Application Specific NAND ASN and complementary products) for sale to IT, CIO and Storage/Server Architects by understanding customers' strategy for development / design in and aligning with KIOXIA growth strategies.</li>
<li>
Coordinate and expose all local account developments as it translates to indirect and worldwide KIOXIA management and sales teams</li>
<li>
Work closely with Sr. Director, assigned Sales people, Business Units, local Technical Support and global Sales/Marketing team.</li>
<li>
Regular Travel (~50%) within the Eastern Canada, New England, NY/NJ, Mid Atlantic, and South East states geography is expected.</li>
</ul>
<div><b>
Requirements:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
Minimum 10 years' experience in NAND SDD or HDD sales management, account development, and account penetration at all levels.</li>
<li>
Experience, background and understanding of NAND Memory &amp; SSD's is required.</li>
<li>
Self-Starter, strong communication, presentation, organizational and relationship skills.</li>
<li>
Experience and knowledge of GA OEM Storage/Server Providers and Financial and Data Center customers' business is required.</li>
</ul>
<div><b>
Education:</b></div>
<ul><li>
Bachelor's Degree in Engineering or related degree</li></ul>
