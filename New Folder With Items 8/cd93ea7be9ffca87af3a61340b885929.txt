<p><b>Job Description</b></p>
</div></div>
<br>
<div><div>
<p>Ready to join an amazing team of marketing and communication professionals? We are looking for an experienced <b>Product Marketing Manager </b>to create powerful positioning and messaging for our suite of products.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>Our ideal candidate is a creative professional with a strong background in B-to-B product marketing. You must be able to learn new markets quickly and develop engaging messaging for various buyer personas to drive demand. In addition, you must be able to work across internal departments to facilitate go-to-market activities, including rolling out new products to the market and product updates to existing customers. For this position, it’s also essential to be up-to-date with new technologies and marketing trends.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>Responsibilities include:</p>
<ul><li>Be the expert on our buyers; Create and update buyer personas and be the voice of the buyer internally and externally</li></ul>
<ul><li>Collaborate with cross-functional teams to define and execute go-to-market strategies that launch and commercialize our products in a differentiated light</li></ul>
<ul><li>Be able to demonstrate our top differentiators and articulate the strengths, weaknesses, opportunities and threats for various products</li></ul>
<ul><li>Support and guide internal marketing partners in developing, producing, and delivering high quality materials in demand campaigns</li></ul>
<ul><li>Partner with Product Management to guide the evolution of our product roadmaps</li></ul>
<p>· Ability to learn quickly in a fast-paced environment and multi-task effectively</p>
<br>
</div></div>
<br>
