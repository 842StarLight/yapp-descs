<h2>Account Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Our ideal Account Manager is someone who is positive, energetic, and focused on delivering an extraordinary experience for clients at MuniBilling!<br>
</p>
<p>Your role will be working to create an exceptional client experience while also managing, prioritizing, and troubleshooting Tier 1 &amp; II client requests via phone, email and ticketing system. Assists in converting new accounts to the MuniBilling software program, product testing, user training, writing support documentation, and managing the customer relationship.</p>
<p>
Customer Engagement &amp; Account Management</p>
<ul>
<li>
Serve as the lead point of contact for all client account management matters</li>
<li>Respond in a timely manner to client questions, requests, and potential software incidents.</li>
<li>Effectively communicate and demonstrate new software features to current clients.</li>
<li>Build and maintain strong, long-lasting client relationships through proactive outreach and engagement.</li>
</ul>
<p>Training &amp; Support</p>
<ul>
<li>
Assist in training new clients on the MuniBilling platform.</li>
<li>Help clients meet their needs and objectives by ensuring they are effectively utilizing MuniBilling products and services to their full potential.</li>
<li>Manage support tickets and inbound calls to meet realistic client expectations.</li>
</ul>
<p>Platform and Product</p>
<ul>
<li>
Achieves and maintains a comprehensive understanding of the full line of software and services provided by MuniBilling.</li>
<li>Be a client advocate by collecting feed</li>
<li>back and recommendations to drive continuous improvement across software and services.</li>
<li>Develop new business with existing clients and/or identify areas of improvement to assist in generating additional sales.</li>
</ul>
<p>Knowledge, Skills, and Abilities:</p>
<ul>
<li>
3+ years technical account management/customer support experience, preferably in a high-tech SaaS industry.</li>
<li>Ability to prioritize, multitask, unitask, and perform effectively under pressure.</li>
<li>Ability to work in a fast-paced, team-oriented, collaborative environment.</li>
<li>Exceptional analytical and problem-solving skills with demonstrated ability in managing multiple projects and meeting deadlines.</li>
<li>Exceptional organizational, presentation, and communication skills, both verbal and written.</li>
<li>Proficient user of Microsoft Office applications; strong Excel skills.</li>
</ul>
<p>Required Education and Experience:</p>
<ul>
<li>
BS Degree</li>
<li>A minimum of 3+ year technical customer support experience</li>
</ul>
<p>Location: Office-based Greensboro, North Carolina or Working Remotely- prior experience required</p>
<p>
Hours: Normal business hours (8:00 5:00) with extended hours, as necessary</p>
<p>
Headquartered in Greensboro, North Carolina, MuniBilling provides utility billing software for government, property management and private companies. We have an informal, collaborative environment and understand the value of feedback and open communication. Come join our team!</p>
