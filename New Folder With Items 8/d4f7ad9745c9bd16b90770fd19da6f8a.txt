<div></div>
<br>
<div><b>
Position Summary</b></div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
Sr. Systems Administrator will be responsible for supporting Office 365 and our internal Windows System infrastructure. Additionally, this person will be responsible for supporting our primary data center, our Disaster Recovery site, global remote sites and users. Responsibilities will include management of VMware, Backups, Storage, Active Directory, Databases, Windows &amp; SCCM infrastructure, Alerts, Monitoring and IT tickets.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
Sr. Systems Administrator must understand and adhere to company’s security policies and procedures. The Sr. Systems Administrator will be part of the global team and will be responsible to identify the infrastructure requirements, propose solution, take a lead and keep up with growing and dynamic environment needs.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
Sr. Systems Administrator will be required to demonstrate a strong commitment to customer service and superior communication and organization skills essential in fostering excellent relationships with our team members and employees.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
<b>
Essential Duties and Responsibilities:</b><br>
</div>
<ul>
<li>
Manage and administer O365</li>
<li>
Microsoft System Center Suite – patch management, application packaging &amp; distribution, troubleshooting, OSD, task sequencing, deployment testing, log files analysis and reporting</li>
<li>
Must be able to automate using PowerShell scripting</li>
<li>
Proofpoint or other SPAM management experience</li>
<li>
SSO management experience</li>
<li>
Manage and maintain server, storage and virtual infrastructure which includes VMware Hyper Visor, SRM, EMC and Dell SANS, Direct Attached Storage, Blade Servers</li>
<li>
Proactively monitor, troubleshoot and report backups of VMs, Servers, Databases and Files Shares through Backup Exec, vRanger, Double-Take and Netwrix</li>
<li>
Work closely with other departments to build and troubleshoot virtual systems</li>
<li>
Proactively monitor and troubleshoot systems with Solarwinds and Nagios</li>
<li>
Manage and maintain Servers, DFS, Active Directory, DHCP and DNS infrastructure</li>
<li>
Load balance power and manage UPS and PDUs</li>
<li>
On-call support as part of a rotation with the rest of the IT Systems team</li>
<li>
Lead &amp; timely completion of Projects</li>
<li>
Off-hours maintenance support as needed</li>
<li>
Other job-related duties and responsibilities that may be assigned from time to time</li>
</ul>
