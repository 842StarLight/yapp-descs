<h2>Regional Operations Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>About Us</b><b>:</b></p>
<p>
Have you lived in or visited an apartment community where the resident places the trash/recycling right outside their front door and it magically disappears? It’s no surprise that this service has become the #1 amenity for apartment residents! This is what we do in over 26 states nation-wide!</p>
<p>
Trash-Butler is a doorstep trash and recycling pickup service for multifamily (apartment) communities. Trash-Butler™ is part of an INC 500 company that has been featured in The Wall Street Journal, TIME Magazine and Oprah.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Are you a service oriented, enthusiastic team player? Then we want to talk to you!</b></p>
<p>
The Regional Operations Manager (RM) is responsible for managing overall operations within an assigned region, including leading a team of District Managers responsible for driving operational excellence and customer satisfaction throughout their assigned apartment communities.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
ESSENTIAL DUTIES &amp; RESPONSIBILITIES</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Participate in the selection, hiring and training of District Managers for your region</li>
<li>Evaluate performance of your management team</li>
<li>Investigate and respond to employee and escalated customer matters in your region</li>
<li>Monitor and analyze service levels and trends to identify areas of opportunity</li>
<li>Ensure the effective and proper use of resources within region (inventory, travel etc..)</li>
<li>Ensure new properties are onboarded (rolled out) according to Trash Butler's roll-out procedure</li>
<li>Communicate and facilitate the effective implementation of Trash Butler policies and procedures within assigned region</li>
<li>Provide ongoing support, including tools and resources to help team succeed and filling in where needed for District Managers that are out on leave/vacation etc...</li>
<li>Promote ongoing development of your team through team building and other employee engagement activities</li>
<li><i>Other duties as assigned</i></li>
<br>
</ul>
<p><b>
</b></p>
<p><b>REQUIRED EDUCATION &amp; EXPERIENCE</b></p>
<ul>
<li>3 years of people management/leadership experience including developing others</li>
<li>Prior experience in multi-site operations and remote management of full-time employees</li>
<li>Working knowledge of personnel policies and procedures including recruiting, interviewing, hiring, training, evaluating performance and terminations</li>
<li>Prior experience and success with business-to-business customer service and problem solving</li>
<li>Excellent oral and written communication skills</li>
<li>Working knowledge of CRM software and MS Word, Excel and Outlook email</li>
<li>BS degree preferred</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p><b>
</b></p>

<li>Strong work ethic and quality improvement orientation</li>
<li>Strong interpersonal skills; ability to build trust and rapport and effectively communicate with co-workers, employees and customers</li>
<li>Self-motivated and resourceful</li>
<li>Ability to work independently</li>
<li>Collaborative/team player</li>
<li>Embody our core values: <i>Building leaders, Always branding, Fun, enthusiastic team environment, Listen, fulfill &amp; delight</i><br>
