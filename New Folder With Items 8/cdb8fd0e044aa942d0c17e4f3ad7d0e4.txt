<h2>Social Media Account Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Account Managers are the heart and soul of what we do at Chatterkick. They are responsible for the nurturing and enhancing relationships with new and existing clients by meeting (or exceeding) their needs and goals. This Chatterkick team member will drive and develop content calendars and social campaigns for our clients by creating, curating, and ultimately deploying new content.</p>
<p><b>We are actively seeking a candidate with the following experience: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>2+ years of inbound marketing experience – including producing relevant SaaS and/or B2B content and executing lead generation strategies</li>
<li>Basic knowledge of WordPress and HTML</li>
<li>Must be active on social media and/or blogs</li>
<li>HubSpot Inbound Marketing Certification preferred</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Who also possesses these fantastic skills: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>An independent collaborator who thrives in a fast-paced, high-energy environment and owns responsibilities like a boss</li>
<li>A savvy, quick-thinker who can not only manage customer relationships but can also play a mean game of Heads Up</li>
<li>An excellent written and verbal communicator, including client correspondence and marketing copy</li>
<li>A results-oriented self-starter, with strong analytical skills to measure and evaluate campaign effectiveness</li>
<li>A positive, big-picture thinker who eats details for breakfast</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Here’s how the position breaks down: 80% – Manage Existing Client Relationships</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Work with clients to create a social media strategy that collaborates with their established marketing, PR, and advertising campaigns, research top influencers, competitors, and trends in clients’ industries</li>
<li>Create timely and engaging content optimized for platform used and intended audience</li>
<li>Monitor sites for customer service opportunities and initiate conversations on behalf of the client</li>
<li>Analyze and report social media actions on a monthly basis for successes and new opportunities</li>
<li>Coordinate creation and alignment of engaging and professional visuals that accurately reflect each client and their brand (Customize social media pages (ex. Facebook, Twitter, Google+, YouTube), develop supporting offline collateral, create original content, etc.)</li>
</ul>
<p><b>20% – Research Industry Trend and Implement Innovative Digital Strategies</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Stay current with social media trends and tools – includes attending networking and educational events, reading blogs, and listening to podcasts</li>
<li>Build and manage a rich content/editorial calendar that attracts a qualified audience to websites (including blog posts, whitepapers, ebooks, reports, webinars, infographics, etc.)</li>
<li>Consistently represent the company values, culture, and thought leadership through all external interactions</li>
<li>Create a presence on behalf of the company through various means such as presenting at conferences, providing input to the larger discussions as a conference attendee, interacting on social media, etc.</li>
</ul>
<p>In addition to being competitively compensated, the right candidate will learn from some of the best minds in social media advertising, sales, and graphic design. We created this position for a game-changer, one who can see it for the career-enhancing opportunity it is, not only for the skills and abilities we will continually hone, but also for the lasting relationships and strategies that will impact our team, community, and industry.</p>
