<h2>Marketing & Sales Representative</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>We are a ServiceMaster By Enterprise restoration servicing the Miami Dade and Upper Keys we are looking for qualified individuals to join our team as a full-time Sales &amp; Marketing Representative to build relationships with those in the South Florida area. Ideal candidates will be enthusiastic, career-minded, with a strong work ethic and a commitment to teamwork. This position reports to the President as well as the Director of Sales and has responsibility for growing the company’s sales and marketing effort including: supporting sales goals, being active in networking organizations, developing and extending various marketing programs, building and maintaining relationships with local insurance companies and agents, property managers, business owners, and other commercial disaster restoration targets.</p>
<p><b>Required Qualifications: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Meet or exceed assigned sales quota by executing the sales cycle, setting up closing appointments, maintaining assigned contact lists, participating in professional associations, hosting lunch-and-learns, and promoting continuing education (CE) courses</li>
<li>Complete Emergency Ready Profiles (ERPs) and discuss benefits of emergency event preparation</li>
<li>Conduct objective-to-objective daily marketing contacts build customer relationships and rapport by educating them on the reasons SERVPRO® is the best cleaning and restoration company in the world, compile and maintain center of influence information., identify “Target 25” (Top 25 contacts to develop into clients)</li>
<li>Provide owners and marketing managers with one-on-one meetings (closing appointments) with COIs to encourage ServiceMaster Referrals</li>
<li>Increase sales territory revenue by consistently achieving sales territory goals</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Position Requirements</b></p>
<ul>
<li>A minimum two years of progressively responsible business-to-business sales experience (preferable)</li>
<li>Experience with sales and marketing within the service sector is ideal</li>
<li>Superb sales, customer service, administrative, verbal, and written communication skills</li>
<li>Strong business and financial background and process-and-results-driven attitude</li>
<li>Experience in the commercial cleaning and restoration or insurance industry is desired</li>
<li>Working knowledge of current business software technologies is required</li>
<li>Ability to successfully complete a background check subject to applicable law</li>
</ul>
<p>If you have the required skills to succeed with a growing company, please apply by submitting a resume and cover letter. Must have a clean background and driving record.</p>
<p>Job Type: Full-time</p>
<p>Salary: $20,000.00 to $100,000.00 /year</p>
<p>Experience:</p>
<ul><li>Sales: 2 years (Preferred)</li></ul>
