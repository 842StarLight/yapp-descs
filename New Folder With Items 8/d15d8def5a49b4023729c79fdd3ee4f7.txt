<h2>Administrative Assistant</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Position Summary:</b></p>
<p>
Open Systems is seeking an Administrative Assistant to join our team. In this role, your primary responsibilities are to assist company operations by executing a variety of duties each day. This includes all daily receptionist tasks such as answering incoming phone calls, e-mails, and upholding all front office responsibilities. You'll be working closely with a variety of departments in addition to our client base. Successful candidates will have excellent customer service and must be very organized. The ability to juggle multiple tasks with strong attention to detail is essential. We can assure you, there will be variety!</p>
<p><b>
Core Duties: </b>Other duties may be assigned to meet business needs.</p>
<p><b>
Reception:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Ensure all phone calls are redirected accordingly also field/answer all routine and non-routine questions</li>
<li>
Greet guests in a professional, friendly and hospitable manner</li>
<li>
Performs opening and closings of reception area and employee breakroom each day (lights, phone system, start coffee, water plants, etc.)</li>
<li>
Works in cooperation with backup administrative staff to cover phones</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Administrative Support:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Maintain inter-office documents (business card orders, recognition program awards, etc.)</li>
<li>
Performs monthly billing reconciliation and receipt management</li>
<li>
Maintain mail station including bringing out mail each day, sort and distribute incoming mail, sign for packages and send external staff and client correspondence as needed</li>
<li>
Performs requisition of office and lunchroom supplies</li>
<li>
Collaborates with a variety of internal departments and staff to ensure smooth business operations</li>
<li>
Assist Director of Operations in executing employee engagement initiatives and other company sponsored events</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Other:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Ongoing maintenance of company owned townhouse including light cleaning to upkeep facilities as new tenants arrive/depart; occasionally, this may require weekend work</li>
<li>
Fleet management including performing car checkout and return procedures</li>
<li>
Coordinate travel arrangements, managing complex itineraries, booking flights, hotels, ground transportation and restaurants</li>
<li>
Schedules and organizes complex activities such as meetings, conferences and department activities</li>
<li>
Vendor management including communications with cleaning company that provides daily building cleaning services</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Required Qualifications:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
High School diploma or equivalent education</li>
<li>
One to three years of receptionist and/or administrative assistant experience</li>
<li>
Working knowledge of basic PC skills and proficiency with specific programs including; Microsoft Office Suite and database maintenance</li>
<li>
Professional appearance and presentation as the first impression of our business</li>
<li>
Excellent phone etiquette, written and verbal communication skills</li>
<li>
Ability to communicate effectively with all levels of management, customer, and vendors</li>
<li>
Strong time management and calendar management skills</li>
<li>
Ability to work independently and multitask as needed</li>
<li>
Ability to sit/stand for long periods of time and periodically lift up to 40 pounds</li>
<li>
Ability to perform occasional weekend work to meet business needs</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Preferred Qualifications:</b></p>
<ul><li>
Some post-secondary education with coursework focused on areas of Public Relations, Communications and/or Business</li></ul>
