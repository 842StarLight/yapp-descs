<br>The Role
<br>--------------
<br><br>
In this role, you'll be a key business partner to our VP of Sales, Head of Operations, the CEO, and the entire Modern Health commercial team. The Head of Client Success &amp; Strategy is responsible for ensuring customer delight, leading to a satisfied and highly referenceable customer base that renews year over year. This individual will have proven success leading a Client Success team that serves Fortune 500 clients in a fast-paced, highly cross functional environment.
<br><br>
The Head of Client Success &amp; Strategy will report to the CEO and be responsible for hiring, training, and motivating a team of Client Success Managers. The ideal candidate will have a background in large-scale client relationship management or account management, with demonstrated success in partnering with clients to achieve their goals. As the Head of Client Success, you'll support your team of Client Success Managers in managing direct relationships with senior HR executives and business leaders, driving engagement with the product/service, and owning renewals. This role is highly visible internally and externally. Above all, we are looking for someone motivated to help us positively impact the lives of millions.
