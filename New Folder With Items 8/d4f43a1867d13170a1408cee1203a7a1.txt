<h2>Junior Graphic Designer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Junior Graphic Designer - Boston or Newburyport, MA</b></p>
<p>
Matter is an independent and thriving brand elevation agency unifying PR, social media, creative services, and digital marketing. From fast growth startups to established brands, we work with clients across diverse industries, including technology, consumer and professional services. We immerse ourselves in our clients' worlds so that we can develop and execute strategic, content-rich campaigns that inspire action and build value through measurable results.</p>
<p>
As we've grown, we've maintained focus on building and nurturing a team-oriented and creative environment because we know that when we tap into our collective brainpower we come up with inspired solutions for our clients and because it makes for a happy place to work. We're proud of the many awards that we've garnered as a Top Place to Work and as a Top Agency in the US, but we're equally as proud of the many organizations that we've helped through our agency-wide volunteer initiative. If you're the kind of professional that takes their work seriously, but also doesn't take themselves too seriously, this could be the place to grow your career.</p>
<p>
We're looking for a full-time Junior Graphic Designer to join either our Boston or Newburyport, MA office. This is an exciting opportunity for this designer to join our creative team and be integrated into all phases of the design process. This person will work alongside five other designers and our Creative Director on a wide variety of story-telling initiatives, across many industries. The design team works with PR account teams, as well as independently, to manage projects from proposal stage through final delivery, collaborating with other staff members and clients regularly. It's a great opportunity for a Junior Designer to work in a very collaborative team to grow and expand their technical skills, while at the same time being able to leverage many different design styles to expand their creative thinking.
</p>
<p>The successful candidate will work with Designers and Creative Director to:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Translate PR/marketing concepts into viable print and digital designs and campaigns for a wide range of clients and industries, including B2B, food &amp; beverage, consumer, etc.</li>
<li>
Produce unique and engaging visuals for online advertising, microsites and landing pages, digital/printed lookbooks, social media channels</li>
<li>
Create a wide variety of brand concepts and deliverables, including brand identities like logos, color palette, font book, style guide, corporate collateral, etc.</li>
<li>
Produce sales/marketing campaign collateral including advertisements, brochures, invitations, reports, tradeshow booth graphics, etc.</li>
<li>
Produce visually compelling infographics, PowerPoint presentations, sales decks, conference presentations, etc.</li>
</ul>
<p>
Along with a great portfolio of work, your previous experience and attributes will include:</p>
<ul>
<li>
1-2+ years full-time graphic and web design experience</li>
<li>
Agency experience a plus</li>
<li>
Ability to work independently and be a self-starter</li>
<li>
Expert knowledge of the most recent versions of Adobe Creative Cloud</li>
<li>
Experience in PowerPoint layout and design including master slides, transitions and animations</li>
<li>
Proficient in Microsoft Office programs</li>
<li>
Working knowledge of the realities of executing designs within an online environment (HTML, CSS, Javascript) a plus</li>
<li>
Experience contributing to a creative team, including designers, UX designers, Creative Directors and copywriters</li>
<li>
Willingness to learn; receive, process and act on feedback
</li>
<li>Friendly, flexible, and energetic. Ability to thrive in a fast-paced environment, and shift gears from creative development to production</li>
<li>
High attention to detail and the ability to work within deadlines and independently</li>
<li>
A strong portfolio that showcases strong graphic design, typography, and layout skills for a variety of clients and industries</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Please include a link to your online portfolio in your resume/cover letter.</b></p>
