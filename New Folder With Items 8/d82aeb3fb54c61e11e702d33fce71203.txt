<p><b>NOVA Thrift Shop Assistant Manager - Fulltime</b></p>
<p>Do you have a passion for our organizational mission (<i>www.novabucks.org</i>)?</p>
<p>Are you a team player?</p>
<p>Would you enjoy working in a fast-paced thrift/retail environment?</p>
<p>Do you have experience supervising volunteers?</p>
<p>If you answered yes to all of these questions, we want you to apply with a cover letter, resume and salary requirements.</p>
<p>Network of Victim Assistance (NOVA), a nonprofit organization County, seeks a fulltime Assistant Manager for the NOVA Thrift Shop to join its team. The Thrift Shop located on Route 313, just north of Doylestown in Hilltown township. The NOVA Thrift Shop is a social enterprise and is the third-largest revenue source of NOVA! The culture of NOVA Thrift team reflects that of the larger organization – a supportive, collaborative and work environment of staff and volunteers who are committed to the mission of NOVA.</p>
<p>Primary responsibilities:</p>
<ul>
<li>Supporting and training volunteers</li>
<li>Direct sales</li>
<li>Preparation of donations from triage to completing sales</li>
<li>Marketing</li>
<li>General upkeep of the shop.</li>
</ul>
<p>Physical Requirements</p>
<ul>
<li>Bend, stretch and stand on feet for 8 hours.</li>
<li>Ability to carry 30 pounds unassisted.</li>
<li>Flexible schedule with availability to work weekends. The shop is open 7-days a week 10-5. Work hours range between 9 am – 6 pm. Fulltime position is 37.5 hours per week.</li>
<li>You must meet all the required clearances for employment (Child Abuse Clearance, Criminal Record Check, and FBI Clearance).</li>
</ul>
<p>Benefits</p>
<p>Salary, excellent health insurance, 3-weeks PTO and other exceptional benefits</p>
<p>To be considered you must provide:</p>
<ul>
<li>Cover letter explaining describing why you are the right candidate for this position.</li>
<li>Resume</li>
<li>Salary requirements</li>
</ul>
<p>Applicants must send a cover letter with salary requirements in addition to their resume. AA/EOE.</p>
