<h2>Sales Director West</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<b>About us:</b><br>
Want to join a company on the cutting edge of technology and travel? Want to be part of a fantastic and fun company that’s revolutionizing the online travel advertising space?
<br><br>
Built on a decade of expertise analyzing the complete traveler path to purchase, Sojern drives travelers from dream to destination. The company delivered $13B in bookings for its clients to date by activating multi-channel branding and performance solutions on the Sojern Traveler Platform.
<br><br>
Recognized on the Top Company Cultures list by Entrepreneur Magazine, Sojern is headquartered in San Francisco, with teams based in Dubai, Dublin, Hong Kong, London, Mexico City, New York, Omaha, Paris, Singapore, Sydney, and Istanbul.
<br><br>
<b>The Role:</b><br>
Sojern’s Enterprise Solutions team is looking for a driven, outgoing, fun and experienced digital advertising sales professional with demonstrated success of developing, managing and growing a high-opportunity territory. This Sales Director will be part of our rapidly growing team and will be responsible for growing Sojern’s non-travel endemic and travel endemic clients in Nevada and Pacific Northwest Territories.
<br><br>
The position requires outstanding prospecting, sales, communication, and organizational skills. The candidate will be responsible for maintaining deep relationships on the client and agency side and must have a full understanding of programmatic digital advertising. Optimal candidates will have existing ad agency relationships in stated territories and be willing to prospect with purpose and a competitive edge.
<br><br>
Sojern is one of the most forward thinking, collaborative and exciting companies in travel. Sojern has been called "the most successful Big Data marketer in travel" and earned the fastest growing travel technology company distinction on the Deloitte Technology Fast 500 for several years.
<br><br>
The position is full-time and can be based in our San Francisco office or filled remotely in Las Vegas.
<br><br>

<ul>
<li>Grow current book of travel and non-travel advertisers while simultaneously closing new accounts</li>

<li>Travel is required and you must be able to travel whenever necessary 50% travel is expected</li>
