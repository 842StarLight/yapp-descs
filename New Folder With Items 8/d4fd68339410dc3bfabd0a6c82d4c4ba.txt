<h2>Business Development Commercial Vehicle Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Location(s)
</p>Colorado Springs CO, Denver, CO
<p></p>
<p><b>Details
</b></p>
<p></p>
<p>Acts as a Company Representative for Commercial Vehicle. Prospects, appoints, orients and trains new agents and encourages existing agencies to increase writing and profitability for the Company.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Position Responsibilities:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>This team member will develop a field sales plan by agent to support the Company sales goals.
</li>
<li>Expected to spend 90% of their time in the field visiting existing agents, prospecting for new agents and training agents/front line producers.
</li>
<li>Once qualified professional agent prospects are appointed this team member will develop an orientation plan to ensure the success of each appointment.
</li>
<li>Responsible for developing and maintaining partner relationships with a goal of maximizing profitable growth within each of our target product offerings and reporting results as assigned.
</li>
<li>Activates and maintains a flow of risk submissions compatible with product line and production goals.
</li>
<li>Provides continuing education and training to the agent regarding Company products, programs, underwriting guidelines and pricing.
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Position Qualifications:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>4 year college degree (Business or Marketing preferred) or 2+ years of outside sales/marketing experience. Internal candidates may be considered if they have 1 year of experience in a position during which they have made agency visits.
</li>
<li>Strong written and verbal communication and organizational skills.
</li>
<li>Working knowledge of use of PC/software program use including, but not limited to, spreadsheet and word processing software.
</li>
<li>Bilingual skills (typically English/Spanish) may be required for specific positions.
</li>
<li>Must have/maintain an acceptable driving record.
</li>
</ul>
