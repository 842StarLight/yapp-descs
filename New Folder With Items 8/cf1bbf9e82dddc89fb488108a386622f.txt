<h2>Oncology Solutions Director -Somatic/Informatics</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Sema4 is a next-generation health information company, spun out of the Mount Sinai Health System, that provides<br>
advanced genomic testing and merges big data analytics with clinical diagnostics. Our team creates practical tools<br>
that help patients, clinicians, and researchers better diagnose, treat, and prevent disease. We are striving to<br>
construct a more comprehensive picture of health by integrating three key elements: a wealth of clinical experience<br>
that informs the answers that patients and providers are seeking, the world-class academic research that illuminates<br>
new directions, and the pioneering information science that puts all the pieces together.</p>
<br>
<p><br>
Sema4 is seeking a Product Director to join our Oncology team to launch and own products across the lifecycle and<br>
support the development of the overall strategy for the Oncology franchise. In this role, you will be primary<br>
responsible for identifying and bringing these to market partnering across the organization with research, lab, sales,<br>
marketing, digital, operations and business development. You will need to define key markets, understand<br>
competition, set strategy and manage new product launches, including tracking KPIs and adjusting strategy as<br>
needed. Your success will be measured by revenue generated by products launched to market as well as their<br>
contribution to Sema4’s strategic objectives.</p>
