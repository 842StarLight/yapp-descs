<h2>Team Lead, Sales Development</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Read something interesting online today? There's a good chance it reached you because of our technology.</p>
<p>
Taboola is the world's leading content discovery platform, serving 360B recommendations to over 1B unique visitors each month on the web's most innovative publisher sites, including NBC, USA Today, The Weather Channel, Tribune and Fox Sports.</p>
<p>
Taboola, the world’s largest content recommendation platform, is hiring a Team Lead, Sales Development</p>
<p><b>
Key Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Organize and project manage a team of up and coming, junior and mid-level colleagues.</li>
<li>Expedite onboarding on Media SDR Team; by way of weekly individual meeting, conducting training workshops.</li>
<li>Support Senior, Enterprise Level Sales Teams directly</li>
<li>Generate scalable, high quality leads and manage the lead generation process</li>
<li>Identify decision makers and research prospect’s contact information</li>
<li>Drive revenue growth through outreach emails &amp; phone calls with a focus on setting appointments for Enterprise Sales Team</li>
<li>Conduct outreach while educating prospects on Taboola advertising solutions</li>
<li>Accompany supported sales managers to client events, developing key client relationships</li>
<li>Work with Sales to keep Salesforce fully up to date and the pipeline current</li>
<li>Work closely with sales managers on various projects that are in support of the entire sales team</li>
<li>Gather feedback from sales team leaders and implement change throughout the SDR Team</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p>
</p>
<p><b>Requirements:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Graduate of a 4-year college</li>
<li>1-2 years experience, preferably in a sales or sales research related role (1 year of previous team-lead i.e. player/coach experience in a sales and/or sales support capacity preferred, but not required)</li>
<li>Experience in digital media advertising, or tech</li>
<li>Strong business acumen, and an ability to present and communicate ideas/concepts effectively</li>
<li>Prior experience with Salesforce (or another CRM system) and related productivity tools</li>
<li>Proficiency in Google Apps, MS Office</li>
</ul>
