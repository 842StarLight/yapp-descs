<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div><b>Job Commitment</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
Build a trusting relationship with prospects by identifying and showing exciting, beautiful units based on their needs, while providing <b>stellar customer service</b>
</li>
<li>
Manage the leasing process from start to close while demonstrating your organization and people skills</li>
<li>
Because of your exceptional customer service skills, you will be readily available to assist the prospective resident with any questions or concerns</li>
<li>
We are going to provide you with best management and training to be a seasoned Leasing Consultant and show you how to operate our property management software</li>
<li>
You are going to be such an engaged Leasing Consultant by always making sure that the property is prepared and in pristine condition for your new resident to move in
</li>
<li>Working closely with your new resident, you will make sure that you issue keys, move-in package, and anything else that is necessary to ensure that the move-in is successful</li>
<li>
Use your amazing marketing skills to seek out new residents through various sources of outreach such as social media, shopping other properties, cold calling, and community organizations in specific areas</li>
<li>
Build and maintain meaningful relationships with the residents by providing exceptional customer service and assisting with their day-to-day needs</li>
</ul>
