<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Job Description
</b></h2>
<p>We have world-class cross-functional staff across the globe including sales and marketing, regional medical liaisons, oncology nurse specialists and customer account teams to ensure our discoveries go from bench to bedside.
</p>
<p></p>
<p>The Forecasting Director of Oncology, Hematology will lead the development and communication of revenue forecasts for in-line and pipeline products in hematology.
</p>
<p></p>
<p>Specifically, this person will:
</p>
<ul><li>Develop revenue forecasts for US and Global teams based on key assumptions and in collaboration and agreement with relevant stakeholders (global marketing and product strategy teams for pipeline products; brand teams for in-line products)
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Lead collaboration and partnership with brand leadership; Commercial Insights teams; regional forecasting teams; access, pricing and reimbursement teams; R&amp;D/Center for Observational Research (CfOR) and other cross-functional teams who provide data and/or assumptions for the forecast
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Allocate forecasting team resources to effectively load-balance across in-line and pipeline assets, according to business needs
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Ensure translation of key insights, patient journey and other critical information into product forecasts
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Perform analyses and assessments of market size, penetration, market dynamics, etc. that lead to potential worldwide forecasts
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Synthesize and incorporate findings based on triangulation of multiple sources
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Ensure implementation, collaboration and communication of forecast outputs to all key functions and regions impacted by the forecast
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Present forecasts to relevant product / brand strategy teams, review boards and senior management
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Work with cross-functional teams to ensure smooth integration of forecasts into downstream business processes (e.g., Finance, Operations)
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Identify and communicate key risks and uncertainties of forecasts
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Translate and elevate forecasts at senior levels and push back on thinking where appropriate, given robust understanding of forecast drivers
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Support adoption of forecasting best-practices, including incorporating new tools and methodologies as appropriate and with particular emphasis on increasing transparency around forecast assumptions
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Develop forecasting team capabilities via training and mentorship
</li></ul>
<p></p>
<p>Basic Qualifications:
</p>
<p>Doctorate degree and 4 years of forecasting and/or analytics experience
</p>
<p>OR
</p>
<p>Master’s degree and 8 years of forecasting and/or analytics experience
</p>
<p>OR
</p>
<p>Bachelor’s degree and 10 years of forecasting and/or analytics experience
</p>
<p>AND
</p>
<p>4 years of managerial experience directly managing people and/or leadership experience leading teams, projects, programs or directing the allocation of resource
</p>
<p></p>
<p>Preferred Qualifications:
</p>
<ul><li>Experience with quantitative techniques including smoothing, regression, time series analysis, and simulation
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Disease area experience in hematology
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Strong oral, written and presentation skills; able to explain complex concepts clearly to a variety of audiences
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Strong project management skills—able to organize and complete multiple projects efficiently and on time
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Demonstrated analytical skills combined with a client service orientation
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Ability to work collaboratively in team-based environment
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Demonstrated ability to lead cross functional teams
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Demonstrated ability to develop and retain staff
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Good understanding of the latest developments in the biopharmaceutical industry, and unmet medical needs and opportunities in the assigned therapeutic area
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Broad, working knowledge of commonly used biopharmaceutical industry databases
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Demonstrated ability to think strategically to support key business decisions
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Background and demonstrated understanding of global markets
</li></ul>
