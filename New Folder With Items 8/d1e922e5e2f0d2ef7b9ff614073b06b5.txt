<h2>Social Media Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Ark Ideas is a Marketing and Technology company that works closely with National Wireless Retailers, Channel Distributors, and Store Owners to provide marketing, CRM, reporting, and development solutions. We are growing rapidly and are looking to add a motivated, customer-centric Social Media Brand Specialist to our team to help drive our Social Media program.</p>
<p>
The Social Media Brand Specialist will be responsible for providing online customer service on social media by responding to incoming messages and using basic sales techniques to drive conversion. Additional duties include managing weekly and monthly ad budgets, target audiences, and ad campaign performance for clients. A successful candidate will have a strong passion for customer service, digital marketing, and technology. Prior experience in wireless retail and/or social media advertising is a plus.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Onboard new clients to our Social Media program by creating Facebook pages and publishing weekly ad content.</li>
<li>Work with the Social Media team to determine budgets and target audiences for clients.</li>
<li>Maintain an in-depth understanding of new products and promotions within the wireless retail channel.</li>
<li>Verify, input, organize, and audit client data to maintain proper records.</li>
<li>Engage with new and existing customers on various social media platforms to qualify, upsell, and drive traffic to physical store locations.</li>
<li>Manage and track customer complaints and success stories to provide additional insights into the customer experience.</li>
<li>Assess campaign performance using sales data and ad metrics reporting.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Requirements</b></p>
<ul>
<li>High School Diploma/GED required (College degree preferred, but not required)</li>
<li>1+ year(s) experience in a customer service role</li>
<li>Excellent verbal and written communication skills</li>
<li>Ability to work well in a team environment and independently</li>
<li>Must be available to work occasional nights, holidays, and weekends</li>
<li>Strong sense of professionalism and time-management</li>
<li>Interest in advertising, social media, or brand management</li>
<li>Prior experience in social media advertising is a plus, but not required</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Benefits</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Competitive Salary</li>
<li>401k Plan with Company Match</li>
<li>Paid Vacation and Sick Leave</li>
<li>Health, Vision, and Dental Insurance</li>
<li>Opportunities for growth</li>
</ul>
