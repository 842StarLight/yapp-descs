<p><b>Spa Sales Associate</b></p>
<p><b>
*APPLY TODAY*</b> <b>Hand &amp; Stone Massage and Facial Spa</b> is looking for a passionate, ambitious individual to join our expanding team. We provide massages, facials, and waxing services 7 days a week. Located off Woodruff Rd next to Academy Sports, our location has been open for 6 years. We pride ourselves on creating an excellent work environment and unmatched client experience.</p>
<p>
Compensation includes an hourly rate from $10-$14 per hour (including sales commissions performance incentives).</p>
<p>
Below are some additional details regarding the position.</p>
<p><b>
Spa Associate Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Meeting membership sales goals (there is a minimum requirement to sustain employment)</li>
<li>Detailed knowledge of the menu of services</li>
<li>Effectively communicate sales, promotions, and services options with clients</li>
<li>Execute the daily operational tasks, day-to-day goals and priorities assigned by management.</li>
<li>Uphold store cleanliness standards</li>
<li>Scheduling appointments and balancing the daily schedule</li>
<li>Communicating the continually changing schedule and operational needs with teammates</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Spa Associate Qualifications:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Capable of working flexible hours (evenings and weekends required)</li>
<li>Special consideration given to candidates with prior retail or membership sales experience (Gym, Tanning, or Salon) or prior Spa experience such as Massage Envy, Elements or Massage Heights</li>
<li>Experience with Millennium software a plus</li>
<li>Critical thinking skills, including conflict resolution</li>
<li>Excellent verbal and written communication skills</li>
<li>A fast learner, with a positive attitude, and remain calm in fast pace environment</li>
<li>Computer proficient (a knowledge of retail/appointment booking computer systems, a plus.)</li>
<li>A strong team player with ability to work independently with minimal supervision</li>
<li>Exceptional organizational and prioritizing ability, high attention to detail, and ability to multi-task</li>
</ul>
