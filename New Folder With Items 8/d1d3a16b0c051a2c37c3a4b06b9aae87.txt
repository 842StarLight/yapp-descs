<h2>Sales/Business Development Representative</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">Sprout Social is used by over 20,000+ brands worldwide including Evernote, GrubHub, West Elm, and Edelman. These businesses rely on Sprout to create stronger relationships with their customers through social media.
<br><br>
Sprout Social is looking for a highly motivated, outgoing and tech savvy sales representatives with strong business acumen to join our growing Sales/Business Development team. As a Sales/Business Development Representative, you will generate new business by prospecting inbound/outbound leads.
<br><br>
Joining the Sales/Business Development team is an opportunity to fast track your career in sales. Contributing at a high level in the Sales/Business Development role will open opportunities to move through our growing organization.
<br><br>
The Experience
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor's degree in business, marketing, related field OR equivalent experience</li>
<li>1+ years of relevant experience in B2B sales - client facing, consulting or marketing</li>
<li>SaaS experience preferred</li>
<li>Driven to succeed and ability to work in a fast-paced environment</li>
<li>Proficient in time management, setting priorities, and taking initiative</li>
<li>Track record of overachieving against defined metrics</li>
<li>Exceptional organizational, presentation, and communication skills - phone, email, and webinar</li>
<li>Technical expertise and ability to acquire knowledge of new products</li>
<li>Understand and apply effective business development strategies and techniques</li>
<li>A passion for building new business</li>
<li>Experience working with Salesforce.com or other CRM platform</li>
<li>Willingness to work in a collaborative work environment</li>
</ul>
Within 1 month, you will…
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Complete Sprout Social's new hire training &amp; onboarding program alongside other new Sprout team members. You'll gain a broader understanding of our products and how your role fits into the organization.</li>
<li>Partner with the Manager of Sales/Business Development to define key success metrics for your role and how you will measure against them.</li>
<li>Begin developing familiarity with our business, platform, and applications, as well as our company's key metrics.</li>
<li>Acclimate yourself with the day to day responsibilities of Sprout's Sales/Business Development team by shadowing team members, listening to recorded customer calls and working closely with our dedicated Enablement Manager.</li>
</ul>
Within 3 months, you will…
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Identify and contact prospective clients to build the pipeline through a variety of methods including, but not limited to cold-calling, emailing, and mass email campaigns.</li>
<li>Approximately 60% of time spent will be on the phones prospecting.</li>
<li>Achieve department standards for activity that includes:</li>
<li>​Making a minimum number of quality outbound calls per day, of which at least 20% of those calls should result in quality conversations.</li>
<li>Scheduling a minimum number of new quality discovery calls per week.</li>
<li>Create a professional and engaging first impression with our prospects and customers.</li>
<li>Create, maintain and update database of prospects with complete information and emails.</li>
<li>Thoroughly qualify all outbound opportunities and collaborate with Account Executives for a smooth customer experience.</li>
<li>Generate new business through outbound prospecting.</li>
<li>Prioritize leads, research prospects and position relevant product features to solve business needs.</li>
<li>Run initial discovery calls to identify customer requirements and expectations in order to make tailored product recommendations.</li>
<li>Manage follow-up with calls-to-action to consistently build a sales pipeline.</li>
<li>Exceed activity and pipeline goals on a monthly basis.</li>
</ul>
Within 6 months, you will…
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Consistently achieve your opportunity and revenue targets.</li>
<li>Become an expert in all things social media, including advanced listening and employee advocacy.</li>
<li>Have mastered the uses cases of successful customers across different verticals and sophistication on social media.</li>
<li>Consistently reflect on your own skill and development gaps along with your Manager to identify personal and professional coaching areas.</li>
<li>Step up as a leader in the Sales Development organization by mentoring new hires.</li>
<li>Have a clear understanding of the SMB Account Executive organization and how to position yourself as an ideal candidate.</li>
</ul>
Within 12 months, you will…
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Have mastered the necessary skills needed to work a consistent inbound/outbound process, including time management, sense of urgency, and account prioritization.</li>
<li>Be a leader on the floor by sharing best practices and ways that you have been successful as an SDR/BDR.</li>
</ul>
Of course what is outlined above is the ideal timeline, but things may shift based on business needs and other projects and tasks could be added at the discretion of your manager.
