<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Job Description</b></h3>
<div></div>
<div>
<div>The purpose of this job description is to report the functions, requirements, working conditions, and supervisory responsibility for the Production Planning and</div>
<div>Materials Manager at Smith Interconnect Stuart.</div>
</div>
</div></div></div></div>
<div><div><div><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Duties &amp; Responsibilities</b></h3>
<div></div>
<div><ul>
<li>Responsible to provide training and instituting best practices aimed at achieving best in class management of the MPS.</li>
<li>Responsible for instituting and defining supply and demand activities including but not limited to: Capacity planning, forecasting, strategic inventory planning and the like.</li>
<li>Responsible for managing day to day activities of the production planners and their performance to support delivery and financial objectives.</li>
<li>Responsible for establishing the production schedules and assessments of lead times of each operation to meet delivery and financial commitments.</li>
<li>Analyzes production specifications and plant capacity data and performs mathematical calculations to determine strategic inventory levels, resource constraints and</li>
<li>mitigation plans to ensure delivery commitments are met.</li>
<li>Analyzes established manufacturing sequences and lead times.</li>
<li>Responsible to work with supplier quality and global sourcing to align strategic supplier partnerships</li>
<li>Confers with marketing and sales to determine sales forecasts and inputs for planning model.</li>
<li>Expedites operations that delay schedules and alters schedules to meet customer delivery requirements</li>
<li>Prepares and maintains productions reports.</li>
<li>Utilizes computerized inventory and materials requirements system to support company operations.</li>
<li>Research and resolve problems connected to production scheduling and planning.</li>
<li>Effectively communicate with management and staff ensuring an understanding of scheduling procedures.</li>
<li>Instill lean manufacturing principles and planning tools, i.e. Kanban, one piece flow, Heijunka and other visual management systems</li>
<li>Initiates inter-company purchase orders to obtain materials and services.</li>
<li>Responsible for procurement and purchasing activities to support production</li>
<li>Initiates multi-site dialog on materials and delivery requirements for both purchase and make products</li>
</ul></div>
</div></div></div></div>

<li>Understanding of lean principles, continuous improvement methodologies, process mapping and advanced problem solving is required</li>

<li>Well-developed MS office skills to produce and communicate high level &amp; detailed reports and presentations.</li>
