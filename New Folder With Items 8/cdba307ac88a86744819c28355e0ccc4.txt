<h2>Media Sales Representative</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">We're looking for a driven Media Sales Representative to join our growing team here in West Hollywood. As a Media Sales Representative, you are the first point of contact for Grindr's advertisers. You'll generate demand, interest, and excitement for advertising to Grindr's 12M monthly active users.
<br><br>
Based in our WeHo headquarters, you will be responsible for closing new business with Direct and Agency Advertisers. The primary responsibility of this role will be to establish and maintain relationships with media agencies and advertisers, with a secondary focus on programmatic buyers and trading desks. The ideal candidate will have a good understanding of digital advertising and a strong desire to make an impact.
<br><br>
ABOUT THE TEAM
<br><br>
Grindr's Direct Ads team is a tight-knit group with diverse backgrounds - we are seasoned sellers, account managers, and veterans of digital media. We are charged with all aspects of the sales process, from cold-calling and emailing prospects to creating pitch decks, presenting in-person to agency groups or brand managers to client entertainment and y/y account growth. We work with companies of all kinds to help them reach their goals by targeting our users with their branding, products and services.
<br><br>
KEY RESPONSIBILITIES
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Generate and develop partnerships with advertisers, agencies, &amp; trading desks to drive revenue/sales for Grindr through direct and programmatic solutions.</li>
<li>Proactively prospect, qualify, grow and maintain an account list</li>
<li>Remain current on industry trends including: rates, ad products, and success metrics</li>
<li>Quickly develop a deep understanding of Grindr's community and culture in order to consultatively communicate our value proposition effectively with brands – mapping our solutions to client's objectives</li>
<li>Work cross functionally with Programmatic Sales team and Ad Operations to develop Direct, PMP and programmatic sales strategies</li>
<li>Serve as an industry expert in direct and programmatic mobile advertising for sales and advertiser/agency partners</li>
<li>Participate on sales calls to help streamline the sales approach and insure the available tools and platforms are being discussed and utilized</li>
<li>Develop case studies of successful campaigns in order to share insights with customers</li>
</ul>
DESIRED SKILLS AND EXPERIENCE
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>2 or more years experience in media sales a phone sales role as part of a SaaS or B2B company</li>
<li>Ideal candidate will have at least 2 years of experience in a sales or Account Manager role within mobile, online media, or similar</li>
<li>A consultative and human sales approach -- every conversation is different, and there are no scripts</li>
<li>A demonstrated track record of exceeding sales targets.</li>
<li>Stellar account management skills with the ability to proactively engage new clients through email and phone</li>
<li>Familiarity with DSP space, ad serving, data exchanges or cross-device performance advertising is preferred</li>
<li>A knack for asking great questions to uncover issues and opportunities with a prospect's advertising budget</li>
<li>Strong consultative sales, relationship building, and customer services skills</li>
<li>Driven, self-motivated and goal-oriented</li>
<li>Proficient using: Google business suite, Word, Excel, and PowerPoint</li>
<li>Proficiency with Salesforce is ideal</li>
<li>Excellent written and verbal communication skills</li>
<li>BA in Marketing, Communications, Media, Advertising, or other related fields strongly preferred</li>
</ul>
