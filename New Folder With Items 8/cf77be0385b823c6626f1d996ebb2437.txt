<div><b>What You Will Be Doing:</b></div>
<div></div>
<br>
<ul>
<li>
You will be responsible for marketing and renewal activity for fully-insured and self-insured clients</li>
<li>
You will assist in discussion with Account teams to set marketing objectives and assess risk to best present strategic options to client.</li>
<li>
You shall direct marketing activities to ensure service model timetables are met.</li>
<li>
You will interpret marketing strategy and develop RFP to meet objectives.</li>
<li>
Be responsible for executing marketing instructions and requests to carriers.</li>
<li>
You’ll develop and maintain key relationships critical to the sales process and negotiates with carrier contacts to seek competitive options for clients.</li>
<li>
You will direct carriers throughout the sales process.</li>
<li>
You’ll analyze proposals for both compliance and for desired provisions that will meet our clients’ needs.</li>
<li>
You will need to illustrate options and provide recommendations to Account Managers and Account Executives.</li>
<li>
You’ll log and review proposals and loads essential data into renewal template.</li>
<li>
Upon analysis of proposals, you’ll recommends a revised strategy in response to marketing conditions.</li>
<li>
You shall evaluate, compare benefit plans, and market trend data to develop specific recommendations to best support clients’ overall benefit strategy.</li>
<li>
You will review and compare cost information to meet common goals and deliver high quality products and service to the client.</li>
<li>
You will participate in peer review process which includes reviewing renewal documents, and presentations to ensure information is free from typos, mistakes, errors before client receives information. Addresses consistency, style, tone, format, etc. Offer constructive feedback to generate a quality output.</li>
<li>
You will be responsible (with other Benefits Analysts) for managing the fully-insured and self-insured financial monitoring report process for Data Analytics department.</li>
<li>
You will help build and manage self-insured templates and process.</li>
<li>
You’ll assist with building of ad-hoc templates, processes, direction for the Data Analytics department.</li>
<li>
You will manage data delivery process with Data Analytics utilization platforms.<ul><li>
Establish key contacts and progression of data exchange capabilities.</li></ul>
</li>
<li>
You will review, manage and update claims trends for WS offices enterprise-wide.</li>
<li>
Gain ability and competency in marketing of self-insured lines of coverage.</li>
<li>
You shall expand knowledge and abilities in following areas:<ul>
<li>
Medical, dental, and vision renewal evaluations/projections</li>
<li>
Data Analytics department specific sales and marketing materials</li>
<li>
Ad-hoc template creation and maintenance</li>
<li>
Self-insured financial monitoring reports (FMR)</li>
<li>
Captive renewal calculations</li>
<li>
Demographic and census evaluation</li>
<li>
Stop loss marketing and analysis</li>
<li>
Pharmacy benefit manager (PBM) marketing management and analysis</li>
<li>
Utilization review</li>
<li>
Network discount review</li>
</ul>
</li>
</ul>
