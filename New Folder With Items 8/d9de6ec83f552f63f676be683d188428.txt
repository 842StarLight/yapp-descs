<h2>Business Development - Market Lead</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p><b>Job Summary: </b>Performs duties which identify, advocate and pursue business development opportunities/strategies related to the promotion and sale of systems. This position typically works under limited supervision and direction. Incumbents of this position will regularly exercise discretionary and substantial decision-making authority. This position may be located within the continental US.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Essential Job Duties and Responsibilities:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Identifies “new” business opportunities. Ability to identify, pursue, and capture niche markets in DoD and Commercial business areas.
</li>
<li>Develops and executes (achieving) strategic business plan related goals including revenue, bookings, and profit for business area.
</li>
<li>Develops and implements business area expansion plans and strategies; including ongoing long-range strategic planning and development of a robust opportunity pipeline.
</li>
<li>Recommends and manages domestic and international representatives and agents.
</li>
<li>Develop and manage strategic business relationships with key government customers such as the Navy, Army, Air Force, Marine Corps, and prime contractors. Garner customer and leadership buy-in, interact with key senior executives and stakeholders within the customer community.
</li>
<li>Develops and executes opportunity specific capture plans and strategies.
</li>
<li>Maintains a productive relationship with business area and associated engineering personnel.
</li>
<li>Develops accurate and realistic business (bookings) forecast.
</li>
<li>Develops and maintains positive customer relations.
</li>
<li>Develops accurate discretionary resources requirements.
</li>
<li>Develops business area advertising plan.
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Required Qualifications:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor's degree in engineering field with at least 12+ years of related DoD and commercial business development experience including management and leadership experience.
</li>
<li>Active Top-Secret security clearance and SCI eligibility.
</li>
<li>Must have extensive knowledge of and experience in the procurement process for complex military systems.
</li>
<li>Experience and knowledge of government programs and working with command senior leadership. Ability to effectively deal with technical and management representatives of U.S. and foreign government and industrial customers.
</li>
<li>Working knowledge of DoD program environment such as FAR, ITAR and DSS requirements.
</li>
<li>Demonstrated strong judgment/decision-making skills, and political acumen.
</li>
<li>A strong commitment and ability to work cooperatively in a team environment.
</li>
<li>Excellent organizational, verbal, and written communications skills, as well as project management, analytical, planning, presentation and human relations’ skills.
</li>
<li>Domestic and international travel as required, to exceed 50%. Location is flexible.
</li>
<li>US Citizenship
</li>
</ul>
<p><br>
<b>Desired Qualifications:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>A minimum of twelve years experience in the marketing of electronic systems and/or products to U.S. and foreign government customers.
</li>
<li>Prior military service highly desired.
</li>
<li>Prior project/program management desired; PMP qualification desired.
</li>
<li>The ability to analyze a broad range of issues and a demonstrated ability in strategic thinking to best influence senior DoD civilian and military decision makers.
</li>
<li>A high level of confidence, integrity, and hands on approach to problem solving are required as well as a professional executive presence for interacting with and influencing potential customer decision makers, senior management and executives internal and external to the company.
</li>
</ul>

</i></p>
