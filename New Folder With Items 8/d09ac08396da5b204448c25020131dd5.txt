<h2>Shift Leader/Supervisor</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Ramen Ray is the best restaurant to enjoy Sapporo style ramen noodle, which was born in Hokkaido, Japan. Our goal is to create exceptional guest experiences through high-quality food and warm hospitality. Please join our team and enjoy working with our great people!</p>
<p>Job Responsibilities</p>
<p>Shift Supervisor to accomplish the following things.</p>
<ul>
<li>Ensure that proper safety procedures are in place to protect employees, guests, and company assets to establish and maintain a safe working and guest environment to reduce the risk of injury, liability, and accidents.</li>
<li>Enforce and maintain sanitary practices and procedures for food handling, general cleanliness, and maintenance of kitchen equipment and also for dining areas. Ensure compliance with operational standards, company policies, ServSafe guidelines, federal / state / local laws, regulations, and ordinances.</li>
<li>Ensure consistently high quality of food preparation and service. Maintain professional restaurant image, including restaurant cleanliness, proper uniforms, and appearance standards. Supervise portion control and quantities of all food and beverage to minimize waste.</li>
<li>Oversee inventory level and receiving from vendors and suppliers; review and evaluate their performance. Take corrective action if needed for any interruption, quality, or delivery problems.</li>
<li>Enforce and maintain company policies and standard operating procedures (production, safety, quality, and customer service standards); identify a problem, and suggest an improvement.</li>
<li>Maintain customer satisfaction by monitoring, evaluating, and auditing food, beverage, and service offerings; build and maintain relationships with preferred customers. Ensure positive guest service in all areas. Respond to complaints, taking all appropriate actions to address complaints or concerns from dissatisfied guests. Take all steps to create long-lasting, loyal customers.</li>
<li>Assist with company human resource objectives by orienting, training, assigning, and disciplining employees. Enforce policies and procedures and ensure that the shift is adequately staffed.</li>
</ul>
