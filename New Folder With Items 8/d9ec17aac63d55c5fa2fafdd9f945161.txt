The Implementation Consultant, Pulse/Salesforce is the key technical expert on the implementation team. The Implementation Consultant is responsible for understanding business and technical requirements, developing solutions, configuring the Pulse application and potential modification to the client’s Salesforce org. The Implementation Consultant is critical to driving the overall technical solution on projects.</div>
</div>
<div><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Summary of Responsibilities</b></h3>
<div><ul>
<li>
Perform development lifecycle activities such as analysis, design, configuration, documentation, testing and issue resolution based on system requirements</li>
<li>
Interact with client users and demonstrate understanding of business/technical needs</li>
<li>
Provide expertise around Salesforce and Pulse integration/synch functionality</li>
<li>
Facilitate meetings/sessions and drive consensus on technical approach and solution for both Pulse and Salesforce environments</li>
<li>
Provide knowledge transfer regarding Salesforce/Pulse integration/synch best practices to client team</li>
<li>
Work closely with client project team and with other Ellie Mae project team members and internal resources such as Product, Engineering or Technical Support</li>
</ul></div>
