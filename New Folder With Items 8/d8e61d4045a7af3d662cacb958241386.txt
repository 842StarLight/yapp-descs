<p><b>
Job Purpose:</b></p>
<p>
Under the general guidance of the Cost Controller, perform all Storeroom and receiving duties for hotel guests, owners and company mail, and patrol all areas of the property. Completes the daily beverage storeroom operations, adhering to established storage, receiving, stock rotation, requisition, sanitation and security procedures. Additional responsibilities include preparing reports and taking inventories</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Duties &amp; Functions:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Able to receive proper counts and record any discrepancies on paperwork</li>
<li>Follow and comply with all company policies, OSHA &amp; SNHD standards</li>
<li>Be proficient in order picking, stocking &amp; delivery of goods via power equipment &amp; truck</li>
<li>Pass and maintain forklift safety test license for use with all types power equipment</li>
<li>Working in temperatures below zero degrees</li>
<li>Prepare and ship out items from hotel. Domestic and International</li>
<li>Receive shipments and inventory packages</li>
<li>Schedule pickups with FedEx, UPS, etc. for outgoing packages</li>
<li>Deliver all incoming shipments to perspective departments</li>
<li>Transports materials to appropriate storage area in a timely manner.</li>
<li>Completes daily receiving reports and transmits receiving documentation to accounts payable.</li>
<li>Notifies Management team of any receiving related discrepancies, to facilitate request for credit or replacement or damaged or discrepant goods.</li>
<li>Maintains organized purchase order and receiving documentation files.</li>
<li>Issues beverage requisitions to all F&amp;B outlets according standard operating procedures.</li>
<li>Performs all operations necessary to maintain the cleanliness and safety of the receiving and Beverage Storeroom area.</li>
<li>Assists in the performance of weekly /monthly/annual beverage inventories.</li>
<li>Other duties as assigned.</li>
</ul>
