<p><b>Leasing Manager (LM)</b></p>
<p>The Leasing Manager (LM) is responsible for the consumer life cycle including acquisition of consumers, excellent resident service, retention and separation of residents. Responsible for enhancing the residential experience for residents residing at WAM communities.</p>
<p><b>Essential Duties and Responsibilities:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>A flexible work week schedule is required.</li>
<li>Supervise leasing personnel such as leasing consultants and/or make ready personnel.
</li>
<li>Provides information about the apartments and the community to the prospective resident and schedules visits to the community. Responsible for leasing apartments at an acceptable determined percent of total prospects.</li>
<li>Effectively explains all lease and community policies to new and current residents.</li>
<li>Accurately and efficiently prepares and assists with all leasing paperwork (i.e. applications, lease agreements, addenda, employment checks, credit checks and leasing move-in packets.)
</li>
<li>Completes follow-up thank you notes and callbacks with prospects after initial community visits in a timely fashion (defined) within 24 hours.</li>
<li>Supports the overall marketing efforts and offers input and suggestions in regards to promotions, advertisements, etc.
</li>
<li>Maintains an accurate and in-depth knowledge of all aspects of the community, particularly in areas such as rent and pricing information, market comps, vacancies, apartment availability, lease expirations, square footage, etc.</li>
<li>Understands and complies with state landlord-tenant laws, federal fair housing laws and company standards.</li>
<li>Monitors all current and future vacancies to proactively manage future occupancy levels.</li>
<li>Analyzes concerns associated with apartments that are slow to lease and offers recommendations to the Community Manager.</li>
<li>Attends and assists in the coordination and organization of resident relations and activities, such as newsletters, parties, renewal campaigns, etc.</li>
<li>Accompanies prospective residents either on a walking or motorized vehicle tour of property grounds including demonstrating the show apartments and all amenities.
</li>
<li>Outreach marketing campaigns as assigned by Community Manager and Regional Property Manager.</li>
<li>Performs any additional functions as assigned by supervisor.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Qualifications &amp; Experience:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>High school diploma or GED equivalent is required.
</li>
<li>A college degree or related coursework in business management, hospitality management or property management is beneficial.
</li>
<li>Property management and leasing experience essential. Sales experience desirable.
</li>
<li>Excellent communication, organizational, and leadership skills necessary.
</li>
<li>Computer literacy required. Experience with computerized property management and/or accounting software is preferred.</li>
</ul>
