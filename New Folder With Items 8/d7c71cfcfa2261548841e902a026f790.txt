<p><b>On Boarding &amp; Badging Specialist</b></p>
<p><b>
Company Overview</b></p>
<p>
We’re Grassroots, a new cannabis company that’s all about living deeply. We started in Illinois and we’re growing nationally from the ground up. We care deeply, about our plants, our products and the people they serve. Our team brings an unmatched depth of expertise and commitment to perfection to every cannabis seedling we cultivate, and every product we invent. We may be one of the largest medical cannabis dispensary owners in the country with licenses in a growing number of states, but we operate like a hometown business in the same communities where we live with our families and friends. In doing so, we connect deeply, to one another, to our communities and to a larger movement. We’re always looking for new talent to join our team and to move our growing industry forward.</p>
<p><b>
Role Overview</b></p>
<p>
This newly created role on our Human Resources team will work closely with our new hires to complete our internal on-boarding process and facilitate the badging process with all required state entities. This is an important role that will set the tone for our employee entrance to the Grassroots.</p>
<p><b>
Role Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Execute a compliant on-boarding and badging process for all new hires throughout the organization</li>
<li>Provide a bridge between the talent acquisition team and the human resources team to ensure all required on-boarding and badging steps are completed within tight deadlines</li>
<li>Communicate on-boarding and badging expectations and requirements to each new hire; follow up to ensure completion</li>
<li>Coordinate and conduct first day corporate on-boarding program for all new hires at Chicago office</li>
<li>Create a framework for Day 1 onboarding at all cultivation and retail locations</li>
<li>Work cross functionally with IT, security, human resources and talent acquisition to ensure all hardware, software and physical requirements are meet prior to new hire’s first day</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Role Requirements</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
2 plus years of applicable work experience</li>
<li>Bachelor’s degree in business, communications or similar discipline</li>
<li>Impeccable attention to detail and organizational skills</li>
<li>Strong written and verbal communication skills</li>
<li>Experience with and passion for in-store guest experience, sales and service</li>
<li>Ability to balance multiple competing priorities and maintain composure under pressure</li>
<br>
</ul>
