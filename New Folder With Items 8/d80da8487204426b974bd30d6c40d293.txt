<h2>Senior Systems Engineer</h2>

<div><div><div><div><div><div>
<p>Spectra7 is offering a unique opportunity for a highly motivated, hands-on individual with a passion for excellence and innovation to lead all aspects of chip debugging and validation and systems engineering. This position reports to the Director of Systems Engineering.</p>
<p><b>
Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Design and test specifications for multi gigabit ICâ€™s for use in high speed datacenter products.</li>

<li>
<div>Analysis of multiple components of active cable assemblies in systems such as IEEE 802.3cd, HDMI 2.0, DisplayPort 1.4, USB 3 Gen 2, and PCI Express.</div>

</p>
<p>Work Experience: A minimum of five years of high-speed analog circuit design and/or PCB design is a requirement for this position.
</p>
