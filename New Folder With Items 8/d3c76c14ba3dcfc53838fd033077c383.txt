<p><b>Reservation Specialist/Receptionist II</b> <b>Job Description</b></p>
<p>Bellís Therapeutic Spa is seeking an energetic Reservation Specialist/Receptionist to facilitate daily tasks. This part-time position not only requires clerical duties but some light housekeeping as well. You must have a delightful personality and professional phone voice. Customer service, problem solving skills, working collaboratively as part of a team and the ability to work well with little to no supervision are key. This position requires being able to multi-task as well as the willingness to step out of your comfort zone and learn new things. You will need to have the ability to adapt to change quickly and move on to the next priority.</p>
<p><b>Job Requirements: </b></p>
<p>A positive attitude is a must. A minimum of a high school diploma or equivalency is required. Must have flexible weekend (Fri-Sun) availability. You must be an excellent communicator with exceptional customer service skills. Strong interpersonal skills are necessary to service our clientele. Prior cash handling and office experience is helpful. You should have an aptitude of servicing others and the ability to anticipate the guests’ needs as they arise. Personal hygiene and a professional appearance are very important in this role. Daily housekeeping including laundry duties are to be performed of a daily basis. Must be able to sit, stand, walk and bend for extended periods of time. Must have the ability to carry laundry of up to 30 lbs. 20-30 feet as needed.</p>
<p><b>Duties: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Answer phone calls and take messages.</li>
<li>Must be able to type a minimum of 30 wpm</li>
<li>Greet guests with a pleasant smile</li>
<li>Up selling and recommending additional products and services</li>
<li>Must have a willingness and ability to achieve monthly sales goals(for memberships and retail products)</li>
<li>Thoroughly explain spa services and benefit</li>
<li>Effectively schedule appointments and rebook guests for services</li>
<li>Must be able to sell each therapist skills to each guest’s request</li>
<li>Print and distribute spa schedules to therapist</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Checking out guests</li>
<li>Providing change to guest</li>
<li>Responsible for administrative projects such as mailings, advertising and sales calls, developing a monthly newsletters and other guest communications</li>
<li>Data entry, record keeping and filing guest history forms daily</li>
<li>Other responsibilities as assigned by manager/owner.</li>
</ul>
<p>Job Type: Part-time</p>
<p>Experience:</p>
<ul><li>Customer Service and Retail: 2 years (Required)</li></ul>
