<p><b>
Fantastic Sams Cut &amp; Color </b>hair salons have immediate openings for cosmetologists/hair stylists that want to join our fun, creative and busy salons and work in a team-oriented environment.</p>
<p><b>
Fantastic Sams Cut &amp; Color </b>provides regional marketing and advertising campaigns to bring guests through the door. You can focus on putting your skills to use creating beautiful styles for men, women and kids. Walk-ins are welcome, appointments accepted, and online check-in available.</p>
<p><b>
Fantastic Sams Cut &amp; Color </b>provides television, radio and print advertising regionally, as well as a strong presence on social media, like Facebook and Instagram. Customer loyalty and email programs are also included.</p>
<p><b>

Free continuing education is available to you to take your skills to the next level!</p>
