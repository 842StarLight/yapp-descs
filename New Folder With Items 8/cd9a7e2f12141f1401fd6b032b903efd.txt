<p>Menemsha Development Group, Inc. is seeking a motivated, driven, Project/Account Executive to be responsible for the development of new and existing clients. While working hand in hand with ownership, the overall goal is to expand business across each of our primary service offerings (Survey, Architecture &amp; Construction).</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>This role will consist of identifying markets and opportunities through internal and external research, direct prospecting (cold calling), networking, attendance and participation with various industry and professional groups and networking associations. ? While at the same time working hand in hand with the owners to manage the business strategy, client relationships and industry relationships to achieve sales and revenue goals.</p>
<p><b>Key Duties</b><b>:</b></p>
<p>The Project/Business Development Account Executive will be responsible for the following:</p>
<ul>
<li>Develop both new and existing accounts and expand business across each of our primary service offerings (Survey, Architecture &amp; Construction).</li>
<li>New client acquisition through market research and existing contacts.</li>
<li>Act as a liaison between departments to share information and ensure client needs are addressed.</li>
<li>Works collaboratively with the project teams to ensure<b><i> </i></b>the<b><i> </i></b>client retention by driving/demanding superior client service thru the organization.</li>
<li>Successful Onboarding of new client accounts. Effectively communicate client needs, requirements and contractual obligations.</li>
<li>Effectively and efficiently contribute to the quality of the clients’ experience with Menemsha.</li>
<li>Conduct client sales presentations (face-to-face, via phone or web meetings, attend industry tradeshows, as required.</li>
<li>Presents proposal(s) while selling the features and services of our business to close deals.</li>
<li>Understanding of internal and external research tools, and ways to analyze this research data into leads.</li>
<li>Effectively utilizes CRM system (Dynamics 365), on daily basis, to manage sales pipeline, client contacts, sales activities, and lead activity.</li>
<li>Maintain strong marketplace awareness of potential construction projects.</li>
<li>Partner with the marketing team to create effective marketing strategies to win in the marketplace, including market segmentation, branding strategy, and communications strategy development. ?</li>
</ul>
