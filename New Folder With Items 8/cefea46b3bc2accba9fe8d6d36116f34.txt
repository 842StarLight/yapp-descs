<p>
Position Title: Marketing Specialist</p>
<p>
Requirements: (resumes that do not reflect this will not be considered)</p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor's Degree</li>
<li>4 years of relevant experience</li>
<li>Ability to obtain a US Public Trust Clearance</li>
<li>Must be a US citizen</li>
<li>Candidate must demonstrate strong Marketing skills</li>
<li>Proficiency in Microsoft Office Suite (i.e., Word, Excel, PowerPoint, Access, Project)</li>
<li>Excellent oral and written communication skills</li>
<li>The capacity to adapt to a fast-paced, dynamic environment</li>
<li>The ability to work well on a team</li>
</ul>
<p>Position Type: Full-Time</p>
<p>
Location: Crystal City, Arlington, VA; Please ensure you have reliable transportation before you apply</p>
<p>
Salary: Commensurate with experience</p>
<p>
Position Description:</p>
<ul>
<li>Conduct market research to find answers about consumer requirements, habits and trends</li>
<li>Brainstorm and develop ideas for creative marketing campaigns</li>
<li>Assist in outbound or inbound marketing activities by demonstrating expertise in various areas (content development and optimization, advertising, events planning etc.)</li>
<li>Liaise with external vendors to execute promotional events and campaigns</li>
<li>Collaborate with marketing and other professionals to coordinate brand awareness and marketing efforts</li>
<li>Plan and execute initiatives to reach the target audience through appropriate channels (social media, e-mail, TV etc.)</li>
<li>Assist in analyzing marketing data (campaign results, conversion rates, traffic etc.) to help shape future marketing strategies</li>
<li>Undertake individual tasks of a marketing plan as assigned</li>
</ul>
<p><br>
Additional Qualifications Strongly Desired:</p>
<ul>
<li>The ability to conceptualize and draft marketing and advertising campaigns</li>
<li>Prepare advertising strategies, plans, and objectives</li>
<li>Able to analyze the effectiveness of campaigns by collecting, analyzing, and summarizing data</li>
<li>Able to meet project deadlines</li>
<li>Identify and help to resolve problems</li>
<li>Has experience with industry best practices</li>
<li>Current understanding of digital platforms, and customer experience</li>
<li>Knowledgeable of applicable government regulations specific to marketing</li>
</ul>
