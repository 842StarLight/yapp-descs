<div>Facebook is looking for exceptionally talented and experienced engineers to join GMS Technology team in Menlo Park, California. Our team provides analytics and workflow tools for Global Marketing Solutions (GMS), partnering with sales, marketing, measurement, support and operations teams. In this role, you’ll work with some of the brightest minds in the industry, work with one of the richest data sets in the world, use cutting edge technology, and get an opportunity to solve some of the most challenging business and engineering problems, at a scale that few companies can match. You will do so by partnering with stakeholders/teams and building scalable, reliable solutions that provide business critical insights and metrics, while ensuring the best uptime and responsiveness. This is a full-time position based in Menlo Park, CA.
