<b>Software Engineer-Payments</b><br>
<b>We're looking for geniuses. Dreamers. Inventors. Innovators. People with the will to get stuff done. Doers who'll rip an idea out of thin air. Make it a reality. Own it.And amaze their peers. Sound like you? Welcome home.</b><br>
<br>
<b>As a Software Engineer, you will be a member of the Hy-Vee Payments delivery team following an agile delivery cadence. Your primary focus will be building secure, highly available, and scalable payments solutions. You will work in a secure, hybrid cloud environment with fully automated CI/CD pipelines and infrastructure as code. We are looking for people who can not only be great individual contributors, but also pair program and mentor other engineers.</b><br>

</ul>
<br>
<ul><li>Pair with other team members to cross-pollinate ideas and skills</li></ul>
<br>
<ul><li>Champion code quality, maintainability, and reliability</li></ul>
<br>
<ul><li>Find and address performance issues</li></ul>
<br>
<ul><li>Take features from conception to final production deployment</li></ul>
<br>
<ul><li>Contribute heavily to user story definition that takes our technology stack into consideration, but also continue to push the bounds of what is possible</li></ul>
