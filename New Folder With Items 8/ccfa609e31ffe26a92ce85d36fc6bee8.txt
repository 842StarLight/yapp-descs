<h2>Credit & Collections Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<b>Description:</b><br>
The Credit &amp; Collections Manager will be responsible for managing, developing, and maintaining a centralized credit management and receivables collection department. This individual will lead a team of experienced professionals, collaborate with company leaders, and establish positive professional relationships with customers. The successful candidate will improve the company’s collection statistics and establish effective customer credit parameters.
<br><br>
Duties &amp; Responsibilities
<br><ul>
<li>Provide day-to-day supervision and support to collection team members.</li>
<li>Oversee workforce needs of the department including coaching, training, and evaluating employee performance. Provide input into hiring, termination, compensation, and promotion decisions.</li>
<li>Analyze new and existing customer’s credit worthiness and collectability risks.</li>
<li>Review financial information and credit history reporting agency reports to establish credit worthiness.</li>
<li>Monitor the AR Aging report to determine potential payment issues and promptly follow up for resolution.</li>
<li>Responsible for the analysis, reconciliation and collection of accounts receivable and cash receipts.</li>
<li>Manage and direct 3rd party collection agencies and attorneys.</li>
<li>Assign tasks, organize and schedule all necessary resources required to accomplish daily, weekly, and monthly activities.</li>
<li>Maintain detailed collection and cash management statistics.</li>
<li>Manage and resolve collection issues for select at risk accounts.</li>
<li>Research documentation to resolve and process appropriate forms to facilitate processing of credit memos, debit memos, and customer refunds.</li>
<li>Apply Company’s credit, collection, and accounting procedures ensuring compliance.</li>
<li>Monitor and evaluate departmental performance and productivity, identify areas for improvements, and implement strategies to improve process performance.</li>
<li>Works closely with sales and support team to evaluate and resolve customer disputes</li>
<li>Maintain open communication with other departments and leadership to ensure fluid and effective operations.</li>
<li>Communicate and enforce company policies and procedures.</li>
<li>Determine training needs and provide training to new and/or lesser experienced employees.</li>
<li>Perform other duties as assigned by leadership. .</li>
</ul>
<b>Requirements:</b><br>
<b>Required:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree in management, finance, accounting, or related field</li>
<li>Four (4) plus years of credit and collections experience with knowledge of UCC, bankruptcy and fair credit and reporting requirements</li>
<li>Management experience, which includes the demonstrated ability to manage, coach, counsel, discipline, and develop employees</li>
<li>Basic understanding of accounting principles</li>
<li>Ability to prioritize a high-volume workload efficiently and able to meet deadlines</li>
<li>Excellent organizational, analytical, attention to detail, and problem-solving skills</li>
<li>Excellent listening, verbal and written communication skills with the ability to communicate clearly with team members, management, employees and external customers in an efficient and professional manner</li>
<li>Demonstrate strong customer service communication skills, effectively addressing and resolving customer concerns with positive business impact</li>
<li>Flexibility to accept and adapt to change as well as the commitment to seek continuous improvement</li>
<li>Microsoft Office: Outlook, Excel, Word</li>
<li>Accounting and CRM software</li>
</ul>
<b>Preferred:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Professional accreditation</li>
<li>Solid Waste Industry or ancillary experience</li>
</ul>
