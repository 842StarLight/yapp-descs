<b>
A Day in the Life</b><br>
Reporting to the Global Director of Advanced Energy, you will help support the Advanced Energy strategy, to ensure short and long-term objectives are met thoroughly for all upstream and downstream initiatives. You will also help create and execute successful tactical plans to build profitability and achieve targets. You provide leadership and support Marketing planning, operational health, product launches, product management, market development, training programs and market research activities.</div>
<ul>
<li>Supports the operational health of the Surgical Energy portfolio, coordinating communication and operational activities with manufacturing sites, overseeing product backorders, forecasting and maintaining two-way communication between regions and key internal departments
</li>
<li>Leads product lifecycle management and portfolio optimization for assigned products.</li>
<li>
Supports current advanced energy marketing strategies and executes yearly business plan tactics</li>
<li>
Leads and coordinates product planning, product launches, product management, market development and market research activities.</li>
<li>
Provides support to Advanced Energy cross-functional team to ensure PD projects are led and executed successfully in the regions</li>
<li>
Develops deep knowledge of market attractiveness, competitive landscape &amp; market analytics in the different Advanced Energy markets</li>
<li>
Supports research to stay ahead of competition and communicates and provides competitive materials to regional partners</li>
<li>
Facilitates the implementation of marketing strategies and tactics, being the example of execution standards and time and quality of implementation</li>
<li>
Works individually or with a team in recommending action, scheduling and planning projects, estimating cost and managing projects to completion</li>
<li>
Facilitates and coordinates marketing activities with geographic areas to increase teamwork and build partnerships.</li>
</ul>
