<div><b>
About the Role</b></div>
<div>
WalkMe is looking for a Lead / Sr. Lead Analyst to join our growing Revenue Strategy &amp; Operations team! You will report directly to the VP of Revenue Strategy &amp; Operations, and will focus on the pipeline health of the company - working with sales, marketing, partner, account management and BDR organizations. Specifically, you will be in charge of the pipeline model, setting pipeline targets, evaluating pipeline funding options, monitoring trends across the funnel and making recommendations to leadership. WalkMe is growing fast, and you will be at the epicenter of the business, following your intuition and pursuing hunches to uncover deep insight that steer our go-to-market investments.</div>
</div>
<div><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>What You’ll Own</b></h3>
<div><ul>
<li>
Building, iterating and maintaining our pipeline model</li>
<li>
Monitoring, reporting on and interpreting metrics and trends across the pipeline life-cycle</li>
<li>
Helping manage our attribution models and workflows</li>
<li>
Evaluating pipeline funding strategies</li>
<li>
Developing pipeline targets across the organization (generation, coverage, conversion)</li>
<li>
Optimizing our pipeline funnel</li>
</ul></div>
</div></div>
<div><div>

At least 1 or more years in a sales or marketing operations capacity, preferably in SaaS or B2B</li>
<li>
Advanced Excel skills (can perform complex functions)</li>
<li>
Experience modeling complex problems, both conceptually and tactically</li>

Passion to move the needle</li>

Team player (This is a WalkMe core value)</li>
