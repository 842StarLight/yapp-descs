<p>
Senior Internal Strategic Consultant<br>
<br>
Do you thrive in high-profile, high-growth, and creative environment? Do you enjoy solving complex problems – requiring strategic insight combined with crisp execution? SAP Concur has just opened a rare job opportunity for an Internal Strategic Consultant working to transform our Global Distribution organization. Join the Business Transformation team as we take on the biggest ideas and challenges and turn them into solutions that will transform our Business. This high-profile team with board level exposure, requires an experienced (10+ years) team member with background in consulting, program management, product marketing and/or product management. If this sounds like you, please apply!
</p>

<li>
Drive a holistic effort to transform the business by ensuring cross team alignment across Strategy, Operations, Distribution, Product Management, Sales &amp; Marketing, Finance &amp; Accounting, Human Resources, Client Services, IT, Legal, and Facilities.</li>
<li>
Define and successfully manage all project planning activities, including, but not limited to: scope and priority setting, building business requirements, identifying interdependencies, risk mitigation, issue tracking, quality management, resourcing, success measurement, KPI monitoring, budgeting, training, and communications.</li>

Travel up to 30% possible, including international.</li>
<li>
Be aware of, and comply with, all corporate policies.<br>
