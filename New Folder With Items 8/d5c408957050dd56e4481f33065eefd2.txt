<h2>Corporate Recruiter</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Erus Energy, LLC, a solar energy company, is looking for a Corporate Recruiter to lead the acquisition of top talent for our company. Will are actively filling positions across the company to support our growing operations. This will include both leadership and sales positions.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
The person we hire will have:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
A magnetic personality. A good recruiter can build great relationships and sustain them.
</li>
<li>Fantastic critical thinking skills. Able to evaluate and assess candidates' skills and potential.</li>
<li>A great sense of humor. We joke and laugh a lot, even when our jokes aren't that funny.</li>
<li>The desire to pick up the phone and listen to voicemails. Most of our job is communication and a lot of that is done over the phone.</li>
<li>The ability to sell. We don't care if you've sold time shares or clothing. If you know how to get people to sign on the dotted line, you are good to go.<br>
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Responsibilities:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Manage recruitment processes: sourcing, screening, interviewing, background checks, reference checks, and onboarding.</li>
<li>Evaluates applicants by studying job descriptions and job qualifications, discussing job requirements and applicant qualifications with managers; interviewing applicants on a consistent set of qualifications.</li>
<li>Build and maintain a successful recruiting process, including the utilization of social and digital technologies that attracts top talent and a diverse workforce. Research and recommend new sources for recruiting.</li>
<li>Develop a pool of qualified candidates in advance of need by building networks of candidates.</li>
<li>Arranges interviews by coordinating schedules; arranges travel, lodging, and meals as appropriate.</li>
<li>Develop and prepare technical and behavioral interview questions based on position needs.</li>
<li>Fostering relationships with talent to ensure they have a positive candidate experience.</li>
<li>Ensure regulatory aspects of the full cycle recruitment process are compliant with federal and state laws. Responsible for tracking and analyzing data regarding applicant statistics.
</li>
<li>Maintain accurate and current applicant data base.</li>
<li>Manages employee referral program.</li>
<li>Prepares monthly and weekly departmental reporting for management.</li>
<li>May perform other related duties as required to meet the ongoing needs of the organization.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Other Requirements:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Ability to work and communicate effectively with employees at all levels of the organization.
</li>
<li>Demonstrated excellence in communication skills, both written and verbal required.
</li>
<li>Ability to communicate in a pleasant, tactful, and professional manner required.
</li>
<li>Excellent administrative skills such as ability to organize projects, strong attention to detail, and demonstrated follow-through with projects required.
</li>
<li>Accuracy in punctuation, composition, and grammar required.
</li>
<li>Strong interpersonal skills and ability to work both independently and as part of a team.<br>
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Qualifications:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
3+ years of full-cycle recruiting experience.</li>
<li>Experience in behavioral based interviewing.</li>
<li>Excellent interpersonal and communication skills.</li>
<li>Experience in applicant tracking software programs.</li>
<li>Proficient in written and spoken English.</li>
<li>Able to get work done on time and with high quality.</li>
<li>Passion to deliver hiring commitments on time and within agreed service levels.</li>
<li>Ability to lift files, open filing cabinets, and bend or stand as necessary. In addition, employees in this position are regularly required to walk and stand; talk or hear, both in person and by telephone; use hands repetitively to finger, handle, feel, or operate standard office equipment; reach with hands and arms; and occasionally lift up to 20 pounds.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>

Exempt, Salary</p>
