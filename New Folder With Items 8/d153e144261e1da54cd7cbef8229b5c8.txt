<h2>Service Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p>Virginia Tire &amp; Auto’s Service Managers are true auto repair professionals, expert problem solvers and workflow managers. They are knowledgeable about cars, how to diagnosis them and the parts and repairs needed to fix them. But most of all, they enjoy leading a team of service advisors and technicians, driving sales and taking care of the customer. They love to engage with people and they love to sell. To them, it’s about learning what a customer’s vehicle truly needs and presenting a solution. And they want to teach their team to do the same.</p>
<p><b>
WHAT YOU’LL DO</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Work alongside your service team in order to drive auto repair sales, procure parts and to ensure that every customer’s car care experience is stress-free</li>
<li>
Ensure that all of your team’s vehicle recommendations represent an accurate diagnosis and viable solution and are communicated in a clear manner to the customer</li>
<li>
Become the expert on Virginia Tire &amp; Auto’s sales procedures, engage with customers and coach employees on selling</li>
<li>
Manage your team to keep pace with customers, balance multiple priorities and use good judgment to manage time and workflow</li>
<li>
Distribute work to technicians and ensure that the technicians are appropriately efficient</li>
<li>
Champion the Virginia Tire &amp; Auto brand, values and people and ensure your team delivers a best in class customer experience</li>
<li>
Own the training and development of your service and technician team, including identifying training opportunities to assist with their continuous development (with the support of our learning department)</li>
<li>
Be the point person for communications to and from your team</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
QUALIFICATIONS</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Sales experience in the automotive industry</li>
<li>
Understanding of complex vehicle systems and how to properly diagnosis vehicles and the proper repairs needed</li>
<li>
Ability to lead and manage in a fast-paced, multi-tasking retail environment with a smile and a<br>
positive attitude</li>
<li>
A professional personal appearance</li>
<li>
Ability to learn our internal software applications</li>
<li>
Have and maintain a valid driver’s license</li>
<li>
Virginia Tire &amp; Auto has a strong commitment to our people, providing extensive training, a healthy culture, and opportunities for growth. We are seeking applicants interested in making a commitment to Virginia Tire &amp; Auto and their career.</li>
</ul>
