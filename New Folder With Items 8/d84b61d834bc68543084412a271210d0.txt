<h2>Video Production Generalist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">Motion Inc. is looking for a video production generalist. The main duties of this position include online video editing using our in house Avid systems and going out on location for shoots. Your average day will be a mix of cutting videos, shooting / crew out in the field, assisting in the creation of distribution deliverables and assisting senior editors. Candidates should have a strong sense of visual storytelling, experience animating with After Effects, have a strong graphic design sense and feel comfortable creating video quickly and efficiently from start to end.
<br><br>
<b>Requirements:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>2 or more years of video production experience or a Bachelors degree in video production or a related field</li>
<li>Strong interest in innovative video storytelling</li>
<li>Software skills with Adobe After Effects / Photoshop and Avid is a significant plus</li>
<li>Ability to shoot video is a significant plus</li>
<li>Ability to fly a drone is a significant plus</li>
<li>Ability to animate in After Effects is a significant plus</li>
<li>Graphic design &amp; animation experience a plus</li>
<li>Edit and manage video post-production projects and assets.</li>
<li>Work with internal and external video producers, writers, and creative directors on execution of video projects</li>
<li>Have a high degree of attention to detail especially when managing multiple video projects, versioning, and receiving feedback from project owners</li>
<li>Manage communication between project stakeholders and the video production team</li>
<li>Collaborate well with other editors, videographers, animators, writers, and designers</li>
<li>Be self-motivated to continually raise the creative bar</li>
<li>Acquire new video production skills and skillsets on a continual basis.</li>
<li>Applications should include a resume, cover letter, and a link to a demo reel</li>
</ul>
<b>Company Overview:</b><br>
Motion Inc. is a full service film, video and media production company located in Rocky Hill, Connecticut. We provide Corporate Video, Documentary Video, Television Commercials, Non-profit Video, Sales &amp; Marketing Videos, Industrial Video, Medical Video, Music Videos, Training Videos, Corporate Communications, Video Services and more. Serving Connecticut CT, Massachusetts MA, New York NY, Boston, Hartford, New York City, New England and clients worldwide.
<br><br>
This position is a contract position with the intent to lead to a full time position. The ability to travel in the CT area and nationwide is required. Potential candidates must have a car.
