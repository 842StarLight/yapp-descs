<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div>Customer Service:</div>
<ul>
<li>
Responsible for the customer relationship activities by providing quality customer interface, proactive customer management, issue resolution, and customer satisfaction</li>
<li>
Ensure accurate PM scheduling and follow up, breakdowns and vehicle status updates; oversee Technician work planning for efficiency, training, and flexibility</li>
<li>
Coordinate with the rental department to ensure maximum utilization without compromising lease customers</li>
<li>
Partner with Sales staff on customer calls for new business and increased customer satisfaction</li>
<li>
Accountable for coordinating with Maintenance, Asset Management, Sales and Marketing to ensure customer satisfaction</li>
<li>
Ensure policy and processes are followed to optimize running costs and maintenance overhead</li>
<li>
People: Effectively develop and lead employees to increase productivity and morale</li>
<li>
Support and build knowledge with the location's leadership team for future Succession Planning</li>
<li>
Work Flow Management: Ensure branch productivity through effective work scheduling and planning specifically around preventative maintenance, repair campaigns, and OOS vehicles</li>
<li>
Accountable for coordinating with rental counter to identify repair requirements, available substitute units and vehicle wash requirements</li>
<li>
Ensure appropriate use of outside repair with vendors and customers</li>
<li>
Location Management: Lead execution of parts inventory management process to include: physical inventory, parts ordering, receiving, stocking, managing purchase orders and parts invoices, and coordinating parts pick-up and delivery</li>
<li>
Work closely with inventory planning team for changes to min-max levels</li>
<li>
Ensure a safe and productive operation through proper implementation and administration of company policies and procedures</li>
<li>
Review, analyze, and proactively develop steps to attain maintenance metrics</li>
<li>
Create and execute action plans for continuous improvement to align with quality objectives</li>
<li>
Financial Management: Financial reporting and cost control related to maintenance and asset management</li>
<li>
Understand financial statements in order to create action plans to manage running costs and overheads</li>
</ul>
