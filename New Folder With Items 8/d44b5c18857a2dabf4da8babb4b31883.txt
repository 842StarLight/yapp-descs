<h2>Events Assistant</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p>Northstar Travel Group is looking for a highly motivated and sales-oriented Events Assistant. This entry-level position is a great opportunity for candidates who want to build their sales or marketing career, while gaining exposure and insight to travel trade events and event management.</p>
<p>
In this role, you will support the event sales, marketing and operation team(s) with a core focus on sales. You will sell under the direction of a senior sales executive while also serving as administrative sales/event team support. This position will allow you to gain significant sales/event experience while also learning the components of sales and event management. If the travel &amp; tourism events industry is something you want to learn more about, this role will provide you the opportunity to touch all facets of it.</p>
<p>
The ideal candidate will have strong listening and communication skills, be proactive, have great problem-solving skills and can keep calm under pressure. This person will learn extensively from the events and operations team and be an integral part of a fast-paced, rapidly growing company. This person will be working directly with clients and supporting the business development team. This position supports a small but diverse team so a willingness to step out of your comfort zone to cold-call potential customers, helping to run busy events, and more is a must.</p>
<p><b>
This position is based in our Secaucus, NJ office and involves domestic travel for a total of about 5 weeks throughout the year.</b></p>
