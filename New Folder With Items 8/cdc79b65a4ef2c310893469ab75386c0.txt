<h2>Branch Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>OVERVIEW:</p>
<p>Under the general supervision of the Branch Administrator, organizes, coordinates and directs the ongoing activities of the branch to provide for the optimal delivery of service to the Bank’s customers in conformance with established Bank policies, strategies and procedures.</p>
<p>PRIMARY ACCOUNTABILITIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES:</p>
<p>Performs any functions necessary, within the scope of authority and expertise, to provide the highest level of customer service and responsiveness to the financial needs of individuals, families, businesses, organizations and stockholders served by the Bank.</p>
<p>1. Plans, coordinates and directs the daily activities of assigned staff. Ensures the efficient, effective and courteous delivery of customer service by assigned staff. Conducts and/or oversees the periodic performance review process for assigned staff. Provides support, training, development, motivation and disciplinary action as needed. Participates in the hiring, salary and promotional recommendation process.</p>
<p>2. Oversees the direction of all Branch activities involved with the development of new business and promotion of Bank services. Works personally in the development of new business relationships, large accounts, and strategic high priority accounts. Establishes and implements programs designed to generate new deposit business and promote banking relationships. Coordinates relationships with the Business Development departments within the Bank, to include Lending and Wealth Management.</p>
<p>3. Educates, coaches and motivates branch staff to recognize and pursue cross selling opportunities as they arise. Researches daily transactions of staff to determine if there were any lost sales opportunities, and turns them into training opportunities.</p>

<p>4. Maintains the branch sales goals on a daily basis by observing staff, following up on sales leads, mailing information to current or prospective customers, phone calls and other customer contacts. Utilizes CRM data and reporting.</p>
<p>5. Maintains an intricate knowledge of bank products and services, and is proficient in the benefits to, and integration of, those products for our customers.</p>
<p>6. Performs all required teller and/or customer service duties as necessary. Assists teller line, opens and closes accounts and cross sells Bank products and services. Provides servicing to large depositors and/or complex accounts. Troubleshoots and reconciles problems and complaints referred by customers and/or subordinates.</p>
<p>7. Coordinates staffing and ensures proper branch coverage at all times. Assists and supports other bank branches as necessary.</p>
<p>8. Ensures the branch’s compliance with bank policies, procedures, State and Federal regulations including currency transaction reporting and Customer Identification Program requirements. Monitors documents and procedures and ensures appropriate training. Reports compliance activities.</p>
<p>9. Provides for the preparation of various reports and records for management, compliance, auditing, etc.</p>
<p>10. Ensures proper upkeep of assigned facilities and equipment and provides for routine maintenance of operational equipment and systems. Ensures the branch facility is maintained in a neat, attractive and functionally efficient manner.</p>
<p>11. Provides for branch security in conformance with Bank’s established security program and procedures; ensures that all security equipment is in proper working order. Provides staff with security training and information as necessary.</p>
<p>12. Serves as Loan Officer. Originates installment and commercial loans. Meets with applicants and takes applications. Makes loan decisions within assigned lending limits and makes recommendations for approval of loans in excess of individual lending authority. Is responsible for the collection process for own loan portfolio.</p>
<p>13. Keeps abreast of and conforms to changes in bank policies, procedures, products, etc. Ensures that branch staff is informed; conducts periodic meetings to provide updated information.</p>

<p>14. Keeps abreast of developments within the banking industry and regulatory environment for assigned area and apprises staff of same. Ensures compliance with State and Federal regulations. Attends meetings and seminars as needed. Recommends procedural changes as needed to support prescribed requirements.</p>
<p>15. Actively participates in the community and represents the Bank through participation in various community activities. Promotes the best interest of the Bank whenever and wherever possible.</p>
<p>SOX COMPLIANCE:</p>
<p>1. Comply with all SOX policies and procedures.</p>
<p>2. Assist managers with day-to-day compliance with internal and external controls.</p>
<p>3. Report any changes in internal controls over financial reporting to department manager or Executive Officer as they occur; ensure process flows and procedures are kept current with the practices of the department.</p>
<p>OTHER ACCOUNTABILITIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES:</p>
<p>1. Performs related customer service, supervisory and administrative functions as may be required to ensure the ongoing effectiveness of assigned branch.</p>
<p>2. Performs duties of Head Teller as needed.</p>
<p>3. Adhere to the Bank’s Core Values and Moments of Truth.</p>
<p>The above is a description of the ordinary duties of the position. It should be expected that from time to time other duties both related and unrelated to the above may be assigned, and therefore, required.</p>
<p>POSITION REQUIREMENTS:</p>
<p>Associate’s degree level of education or its equivalent in retail management and related course work or training. Three to five years banking experience, with at least two years within the lending area and/or retail branch management. Proven management and problem solving skills. Strong math, organizational, customer service and communication skills. Able to use a variety of office equipment including a computer terminal and Bank program software.</p>
<p>QUALIFICATIONS:</p>
<p>To perform this job successfully, an individual must be able to perform each essential duty satisfactorily. The requirements listed above are representative of the knowledge, skill, and/or ability required. Any physical demands or work conditions described here are representative of those that must be met by an employee to successfully perform the essential functions of this job. Reasonable accommodations may be made to enable individuals with disabilities to perform the essential functions.</p>
