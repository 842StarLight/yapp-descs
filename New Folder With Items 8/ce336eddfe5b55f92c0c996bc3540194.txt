<h2>Representative, Billing I</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Overview
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>Responsible for the administration and billing of service agreements under close supervision. Requires working in a collaborative environment with Sales, other administrative departments and Service Management while always keeping the customers’ requirements and satisfaction as a top priority.
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities
</b></h2>
<div><div><ul>
<li>Works in a busy, dynamic, professional office environment.</li>
<li>Manages billing and takes ownership of an appropriate number of commercial customers.</li>
<li>Interacts with customers regularly to ensure accurate and timely billing.</li>
<li>Reviews and validates pricing and terms of service agreements and purchase orders.</li>
<li>Responsible for data entry by entering and updating service agreement billing and terms in custom operating system.</li>
<li>Handles routine dispute resolution and resolves all internal and customer inquiries through phone, email and customer ticketing system in a timely manner.</li>
<li>Escalates unresolved issues to Lead or Supervisor.</li>
<li>Works hand in hand with Sales, Service and other internal customers.</li>
<li>Responsible for retrieval and entry of meter reads.</li>
<li>Responsible for retrieval of purchase orders for contract renewal.
</li>
</ul></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Qualifications
</b></h2>
<div><div><ul>
<li>Associates degree or equivalent.</li>
<li>Prior administration experience with strong Microsoft Excel skills required along with effective analytical ability.</li>
<li>Oracle or SAP experience preferred.</li>
<li>Ability to complete a high level of data entry with robust problem solving skills are necessary.</li>
<li>Limited processing errors.</li>
<li>Some overtime required, especially at month end.
</li>
</ul></div></div>
