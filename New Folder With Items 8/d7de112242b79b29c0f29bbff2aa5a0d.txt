<h2>Job Cost Accountant</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>We are recruiting motivated candidate to join our Accounting Team as Job Cost Staff Accountant. The Job Cost Staff Accountant will be responsible for completing on a timely basis, all aspects of construction payables, contractors’ payments, including coding, preparation of loan draws and capital calls, job cost, GL, bank reconciliation, monthly close and financial analyses.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Essential Duties and Responsibilities (but are not limited to):</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Perform month-end accounting close</li>
<li>Maintain checkbooks and cash flow.</li>
<li>Administer deposits.</li>
<li>Process accounts payable on weekly basis.</li>
<li>Maintain records on condo fees.</li>
<li>Maintain records of monthly utilities.</li>
<li>Record and track real estate taxes and personal property taxes</li>
<li>Record and track yearly business licenses, SCC licenses, etc.</li>
<li>Record and track sales – maintain closing statements – Book Closings.</li>
<li>Calculate and post costs of goods sold.</li>
<li>Maintain accuracy of escrow accounts.</li>
<li>Book bi-weekly and semi-monthly payroll along with payroll job costing as required.</li>
<li>Manage bank and general ledger reconciliations.</li>
<li>Review general ledger accounts, prepare and adjust journal entries.</li>
<li>Perform account analysis, reconciliation and inter-company general ledger accounts.</li>
<li>Prepare trial balance.</li>
<li>Track fixed assets and prepare necessary schedules.</li>
<li>Prepare financial statements.</li>
<li>Assist with internal control evaluations.</li>
<li>Research and correct account discrepancies.</li>
<li>Preparation of all required lender monthly draw requirements.</li>
<li>Maintain job cost reports and provide to project manager as requested.</li>
<li>Maintain subcontractor contracts and change orders.</li>
<li>Maintain subcontractor certificates of insurance and W-9s as they pertain to each job.</li>
<li>Maintain job historical data such as completions dated, closing dates, certificates of occupancy, warranties, etc.</li>
<li>Prepare wire transfers and ACH payments</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Job Knowledge, Skills and Abilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Must possess three years previous job cost accounting experience with considerable knowledge of basic accounting principles</li>
<li>Must possess attention to detail</li>
<li>Must be able to multitask effectively.</li>
<li>Strong communication, organizational, and customer service skills essential.</li>
<li>Proficiency with Microsoft Office applications, particularly Excel.</li>
<li>Prior use of Sage Timberline software required.</li>
</ul>
