<h2>Creative, Sales & Special Events Internship</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>As the leading company reaching Hispanic America today, Univision delivers premier entertainment, news and sports content to U.S. Hispanics through its television, radio and interactive platforms. Univision is looking to recruit top talent for our Internship program to join our mission of informing, entertaining, and empowering U.S. Hispanic consumers. The ideal candidate is at the top of their class, is a self-starter, and is eager to learn in a team environment. Our internship program strives to grow our interns’ knowledge of the media industry and expand their networking opportunities.<br>
</p>
<p><br>
<b>PROGRAM DETAILS </b>:
</p>
<ul>
<li>Runs 10 weeks; maximum of 20 hours/per week
</li>
<li>Projects designed to build knowledge, skills, and experience in the Media Industry
</li>
<li>Exposure to various business leaders and company initiatives
</li>
<li>Additional training and development workshops to build transferable business skills
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>DEPARTMENT INFO – </b>Local Sales Creative Solutions<br>
</p>
<p><br>
<b>RESPONSIBILITIES </b>:
</p>
<ul>
<li>Meet with Sales Account Executives, Analyze the creative request, Brainstorm for Creative Concepts.
</li>
<li>Design Sales Presentations in PowerPoint, Keynote and other presentation platforms.
</li>
<li>Present Creative decks, coordinate with production and develop recaps.
</li>
<li>Other projects and responsibilities.
</li>
</ul>

</li>

</li>

 </li>
