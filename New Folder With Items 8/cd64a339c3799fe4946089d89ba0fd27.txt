<h2>General Manager - Studio</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>COMPANY OVERVIEW: </b></p>
<p>At YogaSix we believe everyone deserves the mind-body experience of yoga. We offers six different class types designed to accommodate students of all levels in a way that is empowering, encouraging, and fun. YogaSix was established in 2012 and is the fastest growing boutique yoga company in the United States.</p>
<p><b>POSITION: </b></p>
<p>The General Manager is responsible for the overall success of the studio which includes delivering a superior customer experience, running successful operations, and leading and supporting a team of advisors and teachers.</p>
<p>The General Manager is also accountable for the financial performance of the studio. They will lead and deliver on marketing and sales programs for the studio and will be responsible for meeting and exceeding all key performance sales indicators for the studio including memberships, renewals and retention, retail sales, and maximizing workout traffic.</p>
<p>This position is ideal for a candidate who is a self-starter, and entrepreneurial in spirit and does not need much direction. They love the challenge of building something from scratch and watch it grow. They see opportunity in good ideas and have the ability to see them through.</p>
<p><b>REQUIREMENTS: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Must be an independent thinker, decision-maker, and a creative problem solver</li>
<li>Previous experience as a Manager preferred. Proven ability to manage store operations independently.</li>
<li>3+ years of retail/service sales or fitness sales experience, with the willingness to reach out to the community and ability to foster great relationships</li>
<li>Experience in implementing grassroots level marketing and sales programs, and a great track record of generating and closing leads</li>
<li>Proven leadership skills with the ability to lead and motivate teams and create a great environment for team members. Ability to mentor wellness advisors</li>
<li>Strong customer service skills, with the ability to stay calm at all times and act respectfully and professionally, even with customers who may become angry or raise their voices. Demonstrate excellent conflict resolution and mediation skills.</li>
<li>Ability to manage and drive all revenue streams: memberships, retail, and teacher training</li>
<li>Experience with Club Ready of similar software is a plus</li>
</ul>
<p>Other skills include</p>
<ul>
<li>Highly organized, proficient in data management, ability to prioritize and meet deadlines</li>
<li>Professional, punctual, reliable and neat</li>
<li>Trustworthy and ability to handle confidential information</li>
<li>Solid writing and grammar skills / Must be fluent in English and have excellent communication and strong interpersonal skills in person, on the telephone and via email</li>
</ul>
<p><b>DUTIES</b></p>
<p><b>Responsible for the overall customer experience and customer satisfaction</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Responsible for maintaining professional and impeccable studio environment</li>
<li>Managing guest concerns and questions, inventory, associates, and reports</li>
<li>Implement structure, policies, and procedures.</li>
<li>Participate in studio, regional and company-wide meetings and trainings as needed</li>
<li>Conduct surveys and improve based on customer feedback</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Build, lead and motivate a team of wellness advisors and teachers</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Recruit, hire, train, supervise, coach, mentor all studio staff</li>
<li>Work with owner and director of education to optimize class schedule on an ongoing basis</li>
<li>Participate in or conduct weekly meetings with PTs to review performance and offer direction, inspiration, and guidance toward achieving individual and club goals.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Profitability</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Work closely with Studio Owner and National Sales Director to ensure health and profitability of the studio</li>
<li>Manage and grow all revenue streams including Memberships, Retail and Teacher Training</li>
<li>Manage studio budget, spending, and reporting</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Develop and implement the marketing plan and sales general plan with a focus on</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Lead generation and closing leads</li>
<li>Schedule/participate in grassroots marketing partnerships and events</li>
<li>Hold promotions to help with customer engagement</li>
<li>Social media engagement</li>
</ul>
<p><b>PHYSICAL REQUIREMENTS</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Must have open flexibility to work overtime or during weekends to open and/or close shifts, if required</li>
<li>The physical demands described here are representative of those that must be met by an employee to successfully perform the essential functions of the job. Employee must be able to work hours that may exceed 8 hours per day and/or 40 hours per week including nights and weekends, especially during peak activity periods.</li>
<li>May be required to sit or stand for up to four hours at a time. May be required to reach with hands and arms. Must be able to bend, lift, kneel, and drive a car. This job requires the person to climb up and down flights of stairs as elevators are not present at all locations. May be asked to occasionally lift up to 30 pounds.</li>
<li>Specific vision abilities required by this job include Close vision, Distance vision, Peripheral vision, Depth perception and the ability to adjust and focus</li>
<li>Must be able to effectively hear in person and via telephone</li>
</ul>
