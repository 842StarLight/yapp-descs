<p><b>
Job Summary</b></p>
<p></p>
<p>
This position will have life cycle responsibilities for new military avionics software products, with an emphasis on developing reusable product line software components into complete solutions that will be deployed on legacy and next generation military aircraft. The product Life Cycle Value Stream Manager (LCVSM) coordinates technical product development, integrating the work of research and development, marketing, sales, and service functions for a product line.</p>
<p></p>
<p>
Primary responsibilities for this position include strategic planning for their assigned product lines, execution of product developments, and promoting cross-business product synergies to support business unit objectives. The product LCVSM directs all phases of product development from inception through completion.</p>
<p></p>
<p>
Qualified candidates should have a strong technical background in avionics software products and systems, with experience leading and managing complex product or system developments. Successful candidates will demonstrate an ability to balance technical knowledge and business acumen to manage this product line.</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Principle Accountabilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Successful candidate will have responsibilities for managing products through their life cycle and creating cross-business product synergies to support business objectives.</li>
<li>
Leads medium size programs to ensure adherence to program plans, schedules and budgets for programs under his/her responsibility and recommends appropriate adjustments.</li>
<li>
Supports annual strategic planning efforts for the business.</li>
<li>
Identifies product requirements for key program pursuits and development of the product line.</li>
<li>
Supports the development of new business growth strategies.</li>
<li>
Develops product roadmaps and identifies opportunities to generate product reuse across multiple programs.</li>
<li>
Identifies and reacts to program problems and supports solutions, such as allocation of resources and changing contractual specifications.</li>
<li>
Supervises the appropriate use of all resources allocated to the program to ensure efficient use of resources.</li>
<li>
Participates in and leads the negotiation of contracts and contractual changes, including coordinating preparation of proposals, business plans, proposal work statements, operational budgets, specifications, and financial conditions of contracts to ensure legal compliance and to protect the organization’s interests.</li>
<li>
Execution responsibilities include cost, schedule, technical performance, risk management, product support, manufacturing, and quality of all assigned product lines.</li>
<li>
Work with all business units to ensure customer commitments are met or exceeded.</li>
<li>
Interfaces with customers for project specifications to ensure successful completion.</li>
<li>
Coordinates responses to customer action items.</li>
<li>
Prepares and presents value-stream strategy, progress, and results.</li>
<li>
Consolidates and presents progress reports at customer, program, or product reviews.</li>
<li>
Manages supplier relationships in coordination with Subcontracts Management</li>
<li>
Develops milestones, program plans, schedules and budgets for programs.</li>
<li>
Ensures successful transition of products into the manufacturing environment.</li>
