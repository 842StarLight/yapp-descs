<h2>BDC Appointments Agent</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p>Primarily responsible for lead generation activities in support of the company’s sales and service goals.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Supervisory Responsibilities: </b>This job has no direct supervisory responsibilities.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Essential Duties and Responsibilities </b><b>include the following:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Answer all incoming phone calls according to a proven, pre-set script, and schedule a sales or service appointment.
</li>
<li>Log all customer comments into Dealership Management System
</li>
<li>Schedule follow-up contact if no appointment is made in Dealership Management System
</li>
<li>Confirm scheduled appointments with future customers.
</li>
<li>Post scheduled appointments on appointment board in the Business Development Center.
</li>
<li>Re-schedule “no-show” customer appointments.
</li>
<li>Follow-up with sales or service department to determine if the appointment was kept and what the outcome was. Schedule future contact as needed.
</li>
<li>Maintain and update customer changes in database.
</li>
<li>Contact current customer base on current marketing incentives.
</li>
<li>Respond to customer website request (internet inquiries).
</li>
<li>Contact internet clients via e-mail and phone to schedule a sales or service appointment.
</li>
<li>Notify necessary departments to inform of appointments set.
</li>
<li>Forward any customer concerns to the correct department Manager and follow-up.
</li>
<li>Maintains CSI at or above Company standards
</li>
<li>Maintains an organized, clean and safe work area
</li>
<li>Participates in required training
</li>
<li>Follows Safeguards rules and regulations.
</li>
<li>Demonstrates the Company’s Core Values
</li>
<li>Maintains accurate timekeeping record in timekeeping system.
</li>
<li>Complies with Company policies and procedures
</li>
<li>Observes all Federal, State, Local and Company safety rules and regulations in the performance of duties.
</li>
<li>Other duties as assigned
</li>
</ul>
