<h2>Marketing Operations Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">We're looking for a strategic and driven Marketing Operations Manager to scale and optimize Lob's marketing tech stack and operations program. As a Marketing Operations Manager, you'll have a direct impact on the growth of our business by enabling operational rigor within the Marketing team.
<br><br>
You will work with our Sales Operations and Data team to conduct data analyses that will be used to inform strategic decisions by stakeholders from across the company. This is a great opportunity for an ambitious, hands-on marketing ops professional to roll up their sleeves and build something from the ground up.
<br><br>
As a Marketing Operations Manager, you'll...
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Develop and execute on a forward-thinking vision for marketing operations, measurement, and technology implementation.</li>
<li>Manage the implementation of Marketing Operations programs (e.g. marketing automation implementation, CRM enhancements, database enrichment &amp; cleansing, etc.)</li>
<li>Utilize Marketo to build and execute marketing campaigns including campaign flows, developing dynamic content and complex nurture streams, form creation, lead scoring, and more.</li>
<li>Own, measure, and iterate on processes required to achieve lead qualification and lead velocity milestones.</li>
<li>Refine integration between salesforce and marketo to ensure campaign tracking and proper ROI measuring for campaign analysis.</li>
<li>Own marketing reporting including dashboards, QBR reports, and budgeting.</li>
<li>Identify marketing funnel improvement areas, and develop process/engagement solutions with the marketing and sales team.</li>
</ul>
