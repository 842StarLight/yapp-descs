Elavon is seeking a <b>Salesforce Marketing Cloud Technical Specialist</b> to lead the ongoing development of our Salesforce Marketing Cloud environment. The Marketing Cloud Specialist will work closely with functional leaders, organizational units, and subject matter experts to identify develop and deploy new marketing campaigns and marketing automation strategy. This role is part technical project manager, part administrator and part Salesforce.com analyst. The Salesforce Marketing Cloud Specialist will be responsible for configuration, support, maintenance and improvement of our marketing automation platform.</div>

 -Serve as primary system administrator for the Salesforce.com Marketing Cloud environment.</div>

<li>Coordinate the evaluation, scope and completion of new development requests including campaigns and marketing automation.</li>

Configure and implement marketing automation schemas, workflows, and campaigns ensuring operational success. Including customer journeys, 3rd party platform integrations, user access/management, data mapping and configurations.
