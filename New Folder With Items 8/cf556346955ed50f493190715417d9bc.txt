Incumbent will also be responsible for generating new business opportunities all while driving client satisfaction</div>

<p><b>
Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>

<li>
Responsible for embracing, communicating and executing the mission of A4
</li>
<li>Canvas merchants/businesses on a daily basis to discover new advertisers

<li>

</li>

</li>

Collect any and all outstanding balances on advertising accounts</li>
