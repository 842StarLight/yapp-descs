<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Position Summary
</b></h2>
<div><div><div><div>WKRG.com has an immediate opening for a Digital Content Manager (DCM)/Reporter. The DCM/Reporter should be passionate about local news, innovative, reader-focused, and full of creativity and initiative. Working directly with the Digital Content Producers, the DCPs and DCM/Reporter bring the day’s content to life in a way that engages and captures the attention of our online audience.
</div></div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Essential Duties and Responsibilities
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div>The Digital Content Manager should be very well versed in website architecture, user experience design and development including data management and optimization scripting languages and system administration. And should be able to act as our Digital reporter for “live” and recorded segments online, mobile, and on Social Media as needed.<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<div>The position must be able to create and implement a flow of creative solutions for daily operations, including training, tools, efficiencies and processes. In addition to site and product maintenance, the position is responsible for creating and executing product and project solutions to meet the strategic needs of the operation.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
The DCM should be completely at ease with a digital lifestyle; should be comfortable learning, analyzing and recommending new software and service providers; have an advanced knowledge of CMS-based publishing platforms; an optimized and organized digital content for a good user experience; and have experience working with different digital video formats.</div>
