<h2>Digital Account Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>ABOUT THE COMPANY</b></p>
<p>
We help connect students with educational opportunities at some of the country's leading educational "brands." Passion for higher education is what drives us as we help colleges and universities – of all sizes and missions – uncover demand for their programs and help students make decisions about their futures.</p>
<p>
We support our clients with marketing automation software (SaaS), eCRM services, and consulting. Using our deep knowledge of higher education enrollment marketing, our staff becomes an extension of our clients' team providing actionable solutions to the marketing issues they face and the wherewithal to get the work done.</p>
<p><b>
ABOUT THE JOB</b></p>
<p>
This position is responsible for the client relationship and manages client projects from start to finish, oversees daily account operations and leads a small team of internal and external resources. We are looking for someone who can hit the ground running with the stated responsibilities of the job.</p>
<p>
Our client projects are considered digital direct response (sometimes called Drip campaigns or eCRM) using web landing pages, email and text message workflows and social media postings.</p>
<p>
Our team supports "full-service" clients - where we create college recruitment digital messaging and deploy the campaigns — as well as "self-service" software clients that use our marketing automation platform, EduConverse.</p>
<p>
Ask yourself these five questions:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Are you a marketing ninja who can plan, develop creative concepts, and write and edit?</li>
<li>
Do you understand Statements of Work, creative briefs and deliverables?</li>
<li>
Do you have demonstrated project management experience, preferably in an agency environment?</li>
<li>
Do you possess the poise and qualities for a heavy client-facing role?</li>
<li>
Are you equally "left-brain" and "right-brain?"</li>
</ul>
<p>
If you answered yes to all of these questions, then yes, please Apply immediately!</p>
<p><b>
JOB RESPONSIBILITIES</b></p>
<p>
Working in a fast-paced setting, the Digital Marketing Manager has three primary areas of responsibility:</p>
<p><b>
Managing client relationships</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Manages client relationships including project timelines, follow-up, and delivering client work.</li>
<li>
Conduct regular client meetings and documents outcomes.</li>
<li>
Tracks and reports project performance — budgeted versus actual.</li>
<li>
Performs training on marketing automation software and conversation planning services for employees and clients.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Creating value</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Leads collaborative sessions to create digital communication strategies using landing pages, email, text messaging and social media to achieve client goals.</li>
<li>
Develops creative briefs and manages marketing client discovery and research process.</li>
<li>
Provides art direction as necessary.</li>
<li>
Provides personal accountability for final proofing of all creative assets to ensure accuracy, clarity and consistency of style.</li>
<li>
Reports on campaign performance versus client goals.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Coordinating resources</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Performs account management in a heavy-client facing role.</li>
<li>
Ensures that internal quality assurance practices for message copy, design and marketing automation message workflows are implemented consistently.</li>
<li>
Supervises client support requests for our marketing automation software.Participates in new business pitches with senior leadership.</li>
<li>
Leads a small internal team and develops relationships with external contract resources.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Skills and Experience</b></p>
<p>
We are accepting applications from local metro Atlanta candidates only. We are looking for a star on the rise — ready to take the next step in his/her career.</p>
<ul>
<li>
3 - 8 years of digital marketing experience.</li>
<li>
A four-year college degree from a regionally accredited institution.</li>
<li>
Strong Excel skills and experience with project management platforms, (Preferably with Wrike software).</li>
<li>
Excellent presentation and communication skills.</li>
<li>
Strong attention to detail.</li>
<li>
Preferred experience with marketing automation software.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
COMPENSATION</b></p>

The midpoint salary for this position is $55k.</p>
<p>
We offer a competitive compensation package that includes medical, dental, and vision benefits, short and long term disability, PTO, and a retirement plan with a company match.</p>
<p><b>
Please note that this is an in-office position.</b></p>
