<h2>Adobe Experience Manager (AEM) Digital Developer III</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<b>Job Summary</b><br>
<br>
Leads the development of cost-effective information technology solutions by creating new, and modifying existing, software applications. Analyzes complex systems requirements, existing business processes, and information systems. Codes, tests, and debugs documents, and implements software applications. Undertakes complex projects requiring additional specialized technical knowledge. Coordinates with users to determine requirements that are more complex.<br>
As our Adobe Experience Manager developer, you will hands-on develop AEM 6.3 building blocks with an emphasis on components, templates, and will integrate AEM with our front-end ecommerce codebases and ecommerce platforms. Our ideal candidate will be AEM Developer Certified with a proven track record of enhancing and deploying mission-critical Digital projects. Candidate should have a strong and proven track record of both AEM and front-end development experience and can operate as a full tech-stack developer with an emphasis on AEM-based projects. As an AEM developer, you will enhance and maintain code for our transactional web sites and will be enhancing web functionality in accordance with Adobe best practices. Candidate will require in-depth knowledge of frontend/backend integration methods; experience with advanced javascript frameworks and design patterns; and ability to integrate AEM with RESTful APIs. Candidate will drive and influence technical web discussions across Digital supplier teams and will hands-on code web site enhancements meant to improve our customer experience and drive sales conversions. Candidate should be able to communicate effectively across all levels of management and should be able to lead brainstorming conversations and participate in architectural discussions. Candidate may supervise the work of other developers to ensure accountability for goals and objectives, with a high focus on AEM code quality and integrity.<br>
