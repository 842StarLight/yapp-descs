<h2>Sports Nightly Anchor/MMJ</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p><b>:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>The Sports Nightly Anchor/MMJ is responsible for all facets of sportscast production including writing, editing, and reporting for BCSN’s flagship nightly sportscast, as well as the regional sports network’s digital and social media platforms.
</li>
<li>From stacking a nightly 30 minute newscast to enterprising and producing feature storytelling for multi-platforms, the Sports Nightly Anchor/MMJ is a proven self-starter and team player who thrives in the high-energy, fast paced culture of sports journalism.
</li>
<li>An experienced storyteller, the Sports Nightly Anchor/MMJ is tasked with all facets of managing the concept-to-broadcast sports news gathering process, from asset assignment to nightly show debriefs.
</li>
<li>The Sports Nightly Anchor/MMJ reports to the BCSN Market Manager.
</li>
<li>There are no direct reports for this position.<br>
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>ESSENTIAL JOB FUNCTIONS:
</b></p>
<p>The Sports Nightly Anchor/MMJ is accountable for the following functions for Buckeye Broadband:
</p>
<ul>
<li>Contributing to the Sports Nightly team that develops, produces and delivers the relevant broadcast, digital and/or social media assets required for linear television, digital and social media engagement including linear and digital platforms as well as Facebook, Instagram, YouTube, Instagram, Twitter, and Snapchat applications;
</li>
<li>Using production and editing equipment to make skilled edits to show segments, creative videos, and in-house promotions for BCSN and Local Stations;
</li>
<li>Using creativity to write and coordinate scripts and creative graphics for videos used for promotions and videos;
</li>
<li>Contributing to the Sports Nightly team that ensures adherence to quality broadcast, digital and social media standards, deadlines, and proper procedures;
</li>
<li>Contributing to the production of various promotional materials created by the creative work group;
</li>
<li>Collaborating with the creative team to take projects from concept to final execution within established deadlines;
</li>
<li>Participating in community outreach and promotional activities sponsored by the company;
</li>
<li>Maintaining an expert working knowledge of all production and postproduction resources including studios, cameras, lighting, audio, editing systems and graphic programs;
</li>
<li>Following and enforcing the Company’s policies and procedures, including the EEO guidelines and safety, at all times;
</li>
<li>Performing any miscellaneous duties as assigned.<br>
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>JOB REQUIREMENTS
</b></p>
<p>Education and Experience
</p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor degree in Communications or other relevant field - Preferred
</li>
<li>2 Years of experience producing, editing and feature story writing – Required
</li>
<li>2 Years of experience working in sports/news environment – Required
</li>
<li>Experience with Newsroom Production Systems like ENPS or iNews – Required
</li>
<li>Working Knowledge of Adobe Photoshop and codecs for web video – Preferred
</li>
</ul>
<p>Core Competencies
</p>
<ul>
<li>Accountable – See it, Own it, Solve it, Do it; Hold each other accountable.
</li>
<li>Agile – Embraces change; adaptable and flexible; sense of urgency;
</li>
<li>Collaborative – Teamwork, Proactive knowledge sharing, Constructive Conflict;
</li>
<li>Customer Focused – External/Internal; Creates the exceptional customer experience; demonstrates a sales and service mentality;
</li>
<li>Innovative – Uses critical thinking; Creativity; Continuous learning; Challenges the status quo;
</li>
</ul>
<p>Other Skills &amp; Requirements
</p>
<ul>
<li>Understanding of the Buckeye brand as well as local client advertising and marketing needs;
</li>
<li>Background record that meets Company standards;
</li>
<li>Reliable means of transportation at all times;
</li>
<li>Strictly maintains confidentiality of financial and/or other information acquired in the course of work; discloses only when authorized, unless legally obligated to do so.<br>
</li>
</ul>

</b></p>

</p>

<p><b>WORKING CONDITIONS
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>The Sports Nightly Anchor/MMJ is a full time, 3pm- 11pm, salaried/exempt position. The regular work may vary to accommodate the needs of the department and includes weekends and/or holidays.
</li>
<li>The Sports Nightly Anchor/MMJ works in a television news gathering, newsroom, studio environment.
</li>
<li>The Sports Nightly Anchor/MMJ is required to drive on the job as needed and is subject to regular MVR checks. He/she must maintain a valid driver’s license and maintain a driving record that meets Company standards for an insurable driver at all times. Any time these conditions are not met, he/she must not drive on the job and must immediately report it to their supervisor.
</li>
<li>The Company is a drug-free workplace. All employees are required to adhere to the Company’s drug-free workplace programs and policies. Pre-employment testing is mandatory along with random testing throughout continued employment.
</li>
<li>The duties assigned, the hours worked, and the status of this position is subject to change as the needs of the company changes.<br>
</li>
</ul>
