Responsibilities:
</p>
<p>The AP/AR Specialist is an essential piece of the Accounting team. The ideal candidate is comfortable performing day to day tasks and is willing to take on the challenge of being an investigator: researching customer deductions, charge backs, PO discrepancies and the like. It is essential that this position ask questions and work collaboratively with sales personnel and customer service to understand and possibly dispute unauthorized deductions. Clear and concise communication is key, as this position provides summary information to management for review. In addition to these things, this position is responsible for posting cash receipts, verifying authorization of purchases, processing employee expense reports, classifying deduction amounts to the correct general ledger accounts, and reviewing, entering and posting routine accounts payable vouchers for check runs.</p>
<p>
The 80:</p>
<ul>
<li>Enter and post cash receipts, write off authorized deductions to appropriate accounts, make collection calls to customers.</li>
<li>Match invoices with receivers, enter invoices into accounting software, obtain signatures or approval for payments, process check/ACH payments and review outstanding checks.</li>
<li>Prepares month end reports: AP sub ledger, Received Not Invoiced Report, Invoiced Not Received Report, with adequate support for reconciliation when applicable, AR Aging Report, Write-off Report when applicable, Monthly Cash Posting Register and Report with proper documentation for reconciliation</li>
</ul>
<p>
The 20:
</p>
<ul>
<li>Release hold orders.</li>
<li>Regularly generate and send monthly Customer Account Statements to customers and other internal parties.</li>
<li>Assist in Month-end and Year-end close processes.
</li>
</ul>
