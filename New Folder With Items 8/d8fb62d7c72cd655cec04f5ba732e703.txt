<h2>Tennis Shop Attendant</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<b>Description:</b><br>
Job Summary (Essential Functions)
<br>Sell tennis merchandise in the Tennis Shop. Maintain the Tennis Shop and manage court reservations.
<br><br>
Job Knowledge, Core Competencies and Expectations
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Able to operate a point-of-sale (POS) system.</li>
<li>Outstanding customer service skills.</li>
<li>Enthusiastically promote the game of tennis.</li>
<li>Knowledge of and ability to perform required role in emergency situations.</li>
</ul>
<b>Requirements:</b><br>
Job Tasks/Duties
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Supplies towels, cups and ice to members and guests.</li>
<li>Records all charge tickets and charges guest fees, if applicable.</li>
<li>Responsible for demonstrator tennis rackets loaned to members and their guests.</li>
<li>Builds creative and appealing displays and rotates merchandise periodically.</li>
<li>Restocks merchandise when necessary.</li>
<li>Inventories and prices in-coming merchandise.</li>
<li>Ensures that tennis accessories are properly inventoried and maintained.</li>
<li>Takes and records court reservations and assists in finding cooperative partners for players.</li>
<li>Informs members about club programs and tournaments.</li>
<li>Directs members and guests to their courts.</li>
<li>Promotes and sells Tennis Shop merchandise.</li>
<li>Assists members and guests with Tennis Shop merchandise selections and purchases.</li>
<li>Fills special orders for members.</li>
<li>Empties trash on each court and cleans courts.</li>
<li>Maintains Tennis Shop and keeps storage area tidy.</li>
<li>Vacuums and dusts tennis shop daily.</li>
<li>Takes towels to and from laundry area.</li>
<li>Reports any complaints, feedback, or concerns from membership to Assistant Tennis Professional or Tennis Professional.</li>
<li>Completes other appropriate assignments made by the Assistant Tennis Professional or Tennis Professional.</li>
</ul>
Licenses and Special Requirements
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>None.</li>
</ul>
Physical Demands and Work Environment
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Able to meet and perform the physical requirements and to work effectively in an environment which is typical of this position.</li>
<li>Frequent lifting, bending, climbing, stooping and pulling.</li>
<li>Frequent repetitive motions.</li>
<li>Continuous standing and walking.</li>
</ul>
Education and/or Experience
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Some High School required.</li>
<li>Competitive tennis experience as an athlete preferred.</li>
<li>Background in retail, sporting goods preferred.</li>
<li>Familiarity with POS software.</li>
</ul>
</div>
