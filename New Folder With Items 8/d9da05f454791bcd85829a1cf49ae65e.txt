<h2>Design Consultant</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p><b>Job Description</b></p>
<p>The Design Consultant is responsible for meeting or exceeding sales goals set by management while determining and understanding the needs and wants of clients. The position is tasked with suggesting design ideas that incorporate functionality and aesthetics and utilizing knowledge of Summer Classics ordering system and inventory flow. The Design Consultant will also be responsible for greeting the client(s) within the store and providing product knowledge as well as assisting the customer throughout the sales process including providing expected delivery dates and troubleshooting issues as they arise.</p>
<p><b>Essential Job Duties</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Sales – Meets or exceeds sales goals set by management through in-store sales, in-home consultations, sales leads through outreach and events, designers and customer referrals; provides quotes and keeps customers informed of promotions and attempts to collect information from every guest.</li>
<li>Customer Service – Welcomes our guests into the store by providing a friendly, informative atmosphere and assist with their needs; Also provides updates to the guests’ order on a regular basis, follow-through after delivery and provides answers to product care, warranty, and Summer Classics sales and programs.</li>
<li>Inventory Management / Information Systems – Correctly inputs of all custom sales for customer.</li>
<li>Merchandising– Assists in the implementation of the sales floor plan. Merchandises accessories and rooms within the store.</li>
<li>Accuracy – Regularly checks order acknowledgements as provided by Retail Operations Manager. 6. Communication – Communicates the history of the company. Additional Job Duties &amp;</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Responsibilities</b></p>
 <ul>
<li>Cleans and helps with upkeep of store as assigned by management.</li>
 <li>House calls offered and performed with a minimum of 1-2 per month.</li>
 <li>Insures proper tagging and visual requirements are met.</li>
 <li>Assists with annual inventory and periodic cycle counts.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Expected Hours of Work</b></p>
 <ul>
<li>Hours are set by the assigned store with frequent weekend hours expected; PT – less than 30 hours per week; FT – 38-42 hours per week.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Roles</b></p>
 <ul>
<li>Sales</li>
 <li>Client Focus</li>
 <li>Client Satisfaction</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Knowledge, Skills &amp; Abilities</b></p>
 <ul>
<li>High school diploma or equivalent; some college preferred; Four-year degree in Design, Industrial Design or trade membership in Design field highly preferred;</li>
 <li>One year of experience in design or retail environment minimum required;</li>
 <li>Ability or aptitude to sell luxury products;</li>
 <li>Excellent written, verbal and interpersonal communication skills;</li>
 <li>Good computer skills – knowledge of MS Outlook, Excel and Word required;</li>
 <li>Technical knowledge of space planning and ability to use planning software preferred;</li>
 <li>Knowledge of the design industry including knowledge of fabrics and applications and general design practices preferred;</li>
 <li>Problem solving skills and positive, professional demeanor are required;</li>
 <li>Computer – Syspro ERP system &amp; Microsoft/Apple Systems &amp; Planning Software, iPad.</li>
</ul>
