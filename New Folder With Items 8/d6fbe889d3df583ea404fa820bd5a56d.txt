<div>Job Description for FLOATING TELLER POSITION<br>
<br>
THIS POSITION REPORTS TO:<br>
Retail Branch Manager<br>
<br>
MINIMUM REQUIREMENTS FOR POSITION:<br>
<br>
<ul><li>High school graduate or active student</li></ul>
<br>
<ul>
<li>Basic math aptitude and proficiency</li>
<li>Demonstrated positive academic record</li>
<li>Achievement in social and/or extracurricular activities preferred</li>
<li>Post high school education and/or two years work experience with the public preferred</li>
<li>Must have a professional appearance and manner</li>
<li>Data entry skills</li>
<li>Basic sales experience</li>
<li>Provide prompt quality service to all customers within acceptable time limits</li>
<li>Balance cash and meet the Bank’s standards for both number and amount of cash differences</li>
<li>Maintain a working understanding of, and comply with, bank policies, procedures, and applicable banking regulations</li>
<li>Knowledge of accounting principles helpful</li>
<li>Demonstrate excellent verbal and written communication skills</li>
<li>Maintain satisfactory attendance record</li>
<li>Process transactions up to designated limits, using sound judgment in order to serve the customer and protect the Bank from loss</li>
<li>Able to inquire and perform transactions on the system</li>
<li>Must be flexible with time and location for the hours worked</li>
<li>Must have transportation to each of our locations</li>
</ul>
<br>
<br>
PRIMARY ACTIVITIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES:<br>
<ul><li>Have an awareness of general banking services.</li></ul>
<br>
<ul>
<li>Gain knowledge of First National Bank and Trust’s products/services, pricing and internal requirements (through printed material and attendance at meetings, etc.) to communicate to customers.</li>
<li>Successful completion of all required Teller 1 training during first 90 days of employment.</li>
<li>Learn about competitor bank products/services and advantages of First National Bank and Trust products/services.</li>
<li>Operate network computers/software, and other equipment necessary to handle financial transactions.</li>
<li>Accept deposits and withdrawals up to designated limits while keeping cash within set limits.</li>
<li>Process transactions up to designated limits, using sound judgment in order to serve the customer and protect the Bank from loss.</li>
<li>Provide prompt quality service to all customers within acceptable time limits.</li>
<li>Have knowledge about the importance and requirements of, and how to complete Currency Transaction Reports (CTRs).</li>
<li>Balance cash and meet the Bank’s standards for both number and amount of cash differences.</li>
<li>Accept loan payments of all types.</li>
<li>Sell cashier’s checks, bank money orders and gift/travel cards.</li>
<li>Process cash advances on credit/debit cards.</li>
<li>Redeem savings bonds.</li>
<li>Balance cash drawer.</li>
<li>Participate in on-going training.</li>
<li>Review Bank referral plan and learn the importance of making qualified referrals.</li>
<li>Maintain a working understanding of, and comply with, bank policies, procedures, and applicable banking regulations.</li>
<li>Maintain confidentiality of customer information.</li>
<li>Know where the Community Reinvestment Act (CRA) notice, statement and public disclosure file is located in the Branch, and who the CRA Officer is.</li>
<li>Support CRA through community and civic involvement.</li>
<li>Know when to report suspicious transactions/activity/situations to appropriate personnel.</li>
<li>Open/close branches as required while complying with security and confidentiality requirements.</li>
<li>Participate in the general upkeep and appearance of the Branch.</li>
<li>Understand and comply with Bank mission, organizational structure, employee handbook, bank security, policies and procedures, standards of conduct and incentive program.</li>
<li>Other duties as needed or assigned.</li>
</ul>
