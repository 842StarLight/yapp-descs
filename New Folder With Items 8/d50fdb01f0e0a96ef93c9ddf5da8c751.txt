<h2>Account Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Position Summary: </b></p>
<p>The Account Manager will serve as a primary contact for pre-assigned Company existing customers . He/She is responsible for understanding client needs, delivering targeted advice/solutions, achieving client ROI objectives and working seamlessly with Operations initiatives.</p>
<p>The AM serves as the day-to-day primary point of contact with key client decision-makers, ensuring the development and implementation of strategies, projects, campaigns, growth opportunities and retention efforts. The AM collaborates with all Company departments to manage the relationship, ensure expectations and tasks are delivered, negotiate business outcomes, and address escalations.</p>
<p>The AM is responsible for high levels of client satisfaction, retention and account growth.</p>
<p><b>Essential Functions: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Utilizes current company reporting mechanisms for account progress and accountability.</li>
<li>Works with Director of Operations and Information Services to develop new report functions and analysis.</li>
<li>Proactively works accounts in Company's Customer Stratification file.</li>
<li>Assesses client needs and objectives, develops account plans, manages day-to-day needs and transitions up-sell service opportunities for existing clients.</li>
<li>Coordinates with various departments to ensure high level operational issues are resolved completely and in a timely manner.</li>
<li>Escalates complex issues appropriately to maintain superior customer satisfaction.</li>
<li>Proactively prepares strategies to prevent recurring issues from impacting customer retention.</li>
<li>Knowledgeable in Procurement Functions and open Service Partner vacancies.</li>
<li>Assists with tasks when other AM(s) are out or on assignment.</li>
<li>Understands the sales pipeline and impact on Company; whether new business or renewal opportunities, and the timing of each to ensure the support teams are aware and prepared to execute at the highest level of service.</li>
<li>Follows expectations, tasks, benchmarks and priorities for existing client services.</li>
<li>Works with Director of Operations to determine expectations and builds the relationships with the clients.</li>
<li>Attends all Client Pre-Communication meetings as necessary.</li>
<li>Assumes Company mobile phone 24/7/365 (less PTO time) to answer phone, texts or e-mails in case of service emergency. Responsible to notify service managers/on-call reps and close loop with the corporate customer</li>
<li>Travel as necessary</li>
<li>Create and maintain a calendar of customer meetings (face to face or virtual)</li>
<li>Additional duties as assigned</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Qualifications: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>High school diploma or equivalent required; Bachelor of Arts/Science in Business or related field preferred.</li>
<li>2-3 years’ experience in the Hospitality, Construction or Facilities Management arenas</li>
<li>At least 2-3 years in account management or relationship management.</li>
<li>Exceptional relationship management and account management skills required; strong negotiating skills and escalation management are critical.</li>
<li>Demonstrated ability in ensuring achievement of client objectives and company financial objectives through understanding of client needs.</li>
<li>Proven strong sales skills with the ability to influence decision makers.</li>
<li>Excellent analytical, problems solving and troubleshooting skills – ability to define problems, collect data, establish facts and draw conclusions.</li>
<li>Strong overall business and people skills, including planning, presentation skills and business acumen.</li>
<li>Excellent communication skills with the ability to effectively interface with all levels and departments (internal and external) on a formal, informal, written and verbal basis.</li>
<li>Demonstrated ability to:</li>
<li>Flourish in a fast paced, deadline driven work environment.</li>
<li>Process customer requests in a timely, professional manner.</li>
<li>Analyze customer problems and aggressively pursue solutions which benefit the customer.</li>
<li>Think creatively and act independently within scope of assigned responsibilities converse, listen and take notes simultaneously.</li>
<li>Provide world-class customer service.</li>
<li>Strong negotiation skills.</li>
<li>Knowledge of Windows, Word, Excel, Outlook and Internet; able to create spreadsheets, emails and other departmentally required documents. Willingness and ability to learn the Company’s proprietary database program.</li>
</ul>
<p>Knowledge of principles and processes for providing customer service including customer needs assessment, meeting quality standards for service, and evaluation of customer satisfaction.</p>
