<h2>Customer Success Engineer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>The Role</b></p>
<div>
We are looking for a Customer Success Engineer that wants to join our inside sales team in Pittsburgh, PA. The mission of the Customer Success Engineer is to help our users get great value adopting NetBeez. In this role, you will build and maintain strong relationships with NetBeez prospects and customers, ensuring that they are benefiting from the products and services acquired. You will assist new customers in successfully implementing the NetBeez solution, while also making sure that existing customers are engaged and continue getting value from it. This position requires that you possess technical and project management skills, while being effective in communicating and collaborating with internal and external teams.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div><b>
Responsibilities</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
Handle and ensure that post-sales activities are successful</li>
<li>
Cross-selling and up-selling new products and services</li>
<li>
Handle the renewal process of NetBeez customers</li>
<li>
Maintain regular communications and QBRs with customers to assure that value is consistently delivered by products and services offered</li>
<li>
Identify and address any impediments that may challenge the successful use of products and services</li>
<li>
Gathering quotes, customer testimonials, and identify customer references</li>
<li>
Maintaining accurate customer records on the company's CRM tool</li>
<li>
Track and be responsible for customer satisfaction metrics</li>
<li>
Any other duties assigned</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<div><b>
Requirements</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
A degree in Electrical Engineering, Computer Science or a related field, although if you don't have one and you think that you have the necessary network engineering skills then you shouldn't exclude yourself</li>
<li>
2+ years of experience and proven track record in technical support or sales</li>
<li>
Familiar with networking principles, WiFi, TCP/IP, DNS, HTTP, and Voice-over-IP</li>
<li>
Working knowledge of Linux, virtualization, and software containers</li>
<li>
Exceptional written and verbal communication skills</li>
<li>
Experience with tools like Salesforce or similar CRMs</li>
</ul>

<div><b>
About NetBeez</b></div>
<div>
At NetBeez we are building the next generation of network monitoring tools that enable organizations to reduce IT downtime. Our dedicated sensors, called Beez, simulate end-users and proactively identify issues in the most complex and large enterprise networks. We serve well-known customers like Kingston, Zillow Group, Oath (a Verizon company), and other Fortune 1000 companies. With presence in both Pittsburgh, PA, and San Jose, CA, our team is welcoming, collaborative, and always willing to learn. NetBeez investors include Y Combinator, Carnegie Mellon University, Partech Ventures and Innovation Works.</div>
