<h2>Office Manager/Customer Service</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Our health &amp; wellness office is looking for a person who is positive, friendly, enthusiastic, efficient and an excellent communicator. Previous experience in a chiropractic office is appreciated but not required.</p>
<p>This is a customer service oriented practice and a willingness to go the extra mile for our clients is a must. There are many changing priorities day to day so flexibility in tasks is necessary. We have many new projects/initiatives to increase new &amp; existing clientele like accepting health insurance and moving from paper based to software and electronic records. So technology use and project management skills are preferred.</p>
<p>You must be a team player with an ambition for growth and great work ethic. Must be a self starter with an ability to multitask and take ownership of scheduling, managing client visits, and keeping all parties on track.</p>
<p>Part time to begin with goals to develop this position into a career opportunity. You can grow into an opportunity for: salary, bonuses, vacation time, health insurance and more.</p>
<p>Skills: Customer Service, Phone, scheduling, problem solving, social media posting and response, note taking, assisting doctor with exams, will discuss plans and comfortable talking about insurance, money and costs.</p>
