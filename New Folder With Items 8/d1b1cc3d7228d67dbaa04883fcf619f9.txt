<h2>Director of Business Development - Pharma Services</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>Almac is an exceptional, award winning, drug development solutions provider at the forefront of the pharmaceutical industry. We are a privately owned organization that has organically grown over 50 years now employing almost 5,000 highly skilled personnel worldwide. Our Global headquarters is located in Craigavon, Northern Ireland with additional operations throughout Europe (UK, Ireland), across the US (Pennsylvania, North Carolina and California) and in Asia (Singapore and Japan).</div>
<div></div>
<div>Our drive and passion for the values championed by our Founder, and enshrined in the ethos of Almac, ensures that ‘Partnering to Advance Human Health’ is more than just a tagline – it is our way of life.</div>
<div></div>
<div>We are currently looking for a <b>Director, Business Development</b> for our Almac Pharma Services business unit.The Almac Pharma Services Business Development team are field sales personnel with responsibility for managing business relationships and achieving assigned revenue targets within their allocated client base.</div>
<div></div>
<div><b>Responsibilities include but are not limited to:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Preparation, execution and follow-up of client engagements which include face-to-face sales visits, industry seminars, tradeshows, on-site customer visits, teleconferences, etc. The explicit goal of promoting and growing the service offerings of Almac Pharma Services to its current and prospective clients. (Valid License Required for travel). The candidate will at first be focusing on selling commercial packaging and potentially later Development services.</li>
<li>Generate and present detailed proposals to the client, either personally or with the assistance of Technical colleagues. This will involve liaising with several internal departments including Project Management, Operations, Design, Logistics, Procurement, etc. in order to generate costs and lead time information used in the proposals.</li>
<li>Develop, maintain and optimize territory business plans whereby the goal is to generate business opportunities, capture those opportunities and provide ongoing analysis via our CRM (Salesforce).</li>
<li>Work with other Pharma departments (globally if necessary) to ensure that clients’ requirements as represented in their proposal and such requirement are clearly understood both internally and externally before commencement of work.</li>
</ul>
<div><b>Qualifications</b></div>
<div>Education:</div>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree within a Life Science or Business related discipline required.</li>
<li>Master’s degree within a Life Science or Business related discipline preferred.</li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<div>Required:</div>
<ul>
<li>3+ years of B2B experience in the life sciences market, specifically for a pharmaceutical, biotech, CRO, clinical supplies or similar organization</li>
<li>Business planning and account management territory plan development &amp; execution experience as well as experience with quote or contract preparation</li>
<li>Previous experience of managing client accounts within a business development role (to include relationship maintenance and development and revenue generation) within a target driven environment</li>
<li>Previous experience of presenting to individuals or groups of key decision makers on business solutions, service offerings etc (to include both preparation and delivery of presentation materials)</li>
<li>Strong understanding of sales strategy, sales tactics, and business development processes, including but not limited to business planning, sales forecasting, sales funnel life-cycle management, account management principles</li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<div>Preferred:</div>
<ul>
<li>Knowledge of clinical trials and the pharmaceutical industry OR knowledge of the contract packaging industry OR knowledge of logistics/supply chain management.</li>
<li>Previous practical experience with CRM software</li>
</ul>
