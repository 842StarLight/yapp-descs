<h2>Technical Support Agent</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p>Job description</p>
<p><b>
DUTIES and RESPONSIBILITIES:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Answer and address all incoming support calls from customers; both internal and external.</li>
<li>
Answer and address incoming web chats, emails and voicemails from customers.</li>
<li>
Take ownership of each issue assigned and effectively bring it to resolution or escalation.</li>
<li>
Own and follow through on all requests for support from customers; both internal and external.</li>
<li>
Ensure complete resolution of issue(s) in order to prevent customers having to call back.</li>
<li>
Assist customers with basic program setup and administrative issues.</li>
<li>
Effectively troubleshoot, resolve and properly document support calls and cases.</li>
<li>
Work closely with other departments to resolve outstanding issues.</li>
<li>
Assist in documenting solutions for our Knowledgebase for training and information purposes.</li>
<li>
Perform remote analysis and diagnosis of customer issues and recommend and/or implement corrective solutions.</li>
<li>
Assist with identifying, troubleshooting and cleaning of viruses and/or malware within customer's operating environment.</li>
<li>
Provide backup phone/case/voicemail/chat support for other teams when necessary.</li>
<li>
Document and present technical support and/or training material to internal customers - i.e. Customer Care , Sales, etc.</li>
<li>
Work closely with the Product Management, Quality Assurance, Research, and Knowledgebase teams to test, investigate, and document all aspects of existing and upcoming products.</li>
<li>
Project a positive attitude and be a mentor for other employees.</li>
<li>
Maintain a favorable image of ESET when interfacing with outside sources.</li>
<li>
Accurately record and submit timesheets and paid time off requests.</li>
<li>
Accurately track, log and otherwise tag support requests using the approved CRM, and/or other management approved tools.</li>
<li>
Ensure the successful onboarding of new, recently renewed, or upgraded partners, partner customers and direct customers.</li>
<li>
Ensure customer needs are met by ensuring customer awareness of relevant ESET products and services when appropriate.</li>
<li>
Provide excellent support by ensuring customers are aware of products and services relevant to their needs as identified during the support interaction.</li>
<li>
Effectively collect and submit relevant data to appropriate groups for analysis. Based on findings follow up with customers regarding ticket statuses in a timely manner.</li>
<li>
Document and report all escalated issues to appropriate groups. (i.e. weekly report)</li>
<li>
Communicate escalated and trending issues to other Customer Care Teams .</li>
<li>
Submit and maintain escalated/trending content within SharePoint sites.</li>
<li>
Test and replicate reported issues in test environment then report/document findings and results.</li>
<li>
Serve as Primary Contact and Subject Matter Expert for ESET Technical Alliance Partners as needed.</li>
<li>
Assist in creating and delivering internal processes and documentation to internal customers.</li>
</ul>
