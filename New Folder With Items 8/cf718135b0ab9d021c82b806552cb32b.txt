<h2>Advertising Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Position Overview</b></p>
<p>The Advertising Manager will be responsible for the development of annual advertising plan, including both print and online ads. Under supervision of the Vice President of Marketing, the Advertising Manager will develop a thoughtful and effective school-year ad plan.</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Duties and Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Ensure strategic coordination on featured product selection/timing with other marketing department members.</li>
<li>Stay abreast of new tools and propose test ad units and test media</li>
<li>Develop relationships with ad sales reps from appropriate media.</li>
<li>Negotiate best possible terms for Benchmark Education.</li>
<li>Maintain an advertising budget.</li>
<li>Meet all reservation deadlines.</li>
<li>Provide regular analysis of advertising effectiveness.</li>
</ul>
