<div>
Yamaha Motor Corporation, U.S.A. is a growing and dynamic organization with superb products that includes motorcycles, outboard motors, ATVs, personal watercraft, snowmobiles, boats, power assist bicycles, outdoor power equipment, race kart engines, accessories, apparel, and much more! We are a company of enthusiasts and have passion for our products!<br>
</div>
<p></p>
<div>Yamaha has an excellent opportunity for a <b>Credit Underwriter </b>to join our <b>Financial Services team in Marietta, GA. </b>The Credit Underwriter is responsible for researching and analyzing credit risks, assessing credit history, and approving or denying credit to potential consumers.<br>
 </div>
<p></p>
<p><b>Specific duties include, but are not limited to:</b><b>
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Decision applications by placing emphasis on response time, quantity of applications processed, and quality of applications approved.
</li>
<li>Structure approvals and handle rehashes to maximize profits and enhance dealer relationships.
</li>
<li>Maintain and establish dealer relationships and educate and train dealerships on YMFUS programs and policies.
</li>
<li>Initiate sales calls to capture applications and where appropriate, prepare written follow-up. Provide prompt accurate response to all internal and external customer inquiries.
</li>
<li>Review and calculate income documentation and perform verification calls on employment and consumers.
</li>
<li>Participate in loan origination software program testing, for development and improvements.
</li>
<li>Perform related duties as needed.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<div>
</div>
<p><b><i>Qualifications:</i></b></p>
<ul>
<li>BA / BS degree in Finance, Business or other related course of study is preferred. (High School Diploma Required.)
</li>
<li>Requires 3+ years underwriting full-spectrum credit experience in the automotive or motorsports industry.</li>
<li>Must be highly skilled in credit decisioning and have a solid understanding of consumer credit, and applicable federal and state regulations relative to consumer lending.</li>
<li>Must have strong analytical and quantitative skills, organizational, negotiation, interpersonal skills and acute problem-solving ability.</li>
<li>Must be results oriented and possess a high level of personal initiative.</li>
<li>Strong proficiency in MS Office (i.e., Word, Excel, Power Point, Outlook, etc.)</li>
<li>Ability to work flexible hours including weekends and overtime, as necessary.</li>
</ul>
<div><b><i>Must perform all other duties outstandingly and consistent with guidelines, as assigned<br>
</i></b></div>
<div></div>
<div><b>Reports to: Credit Underwriting Manager</b></div>
