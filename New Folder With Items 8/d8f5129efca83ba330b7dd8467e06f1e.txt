<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Role Overview:</b></h2>
<p>
McAfee Consumer is seeking a data-driven, customer-focused, and energetic individual for a Director, Product Management role for the Secure Home Platform product line. You will partner with Engineering, Sales, and Marketing to accelerate market traction for our next-generation products that redefines how we protect consumers.</p>
<p><br>
<b><i>
Company Overview</i></b></p>
<p>
From device to cloud, McAfee provides market-leading cybersecurity solutions for both business and consumers. McAfee helps businesses orchestrate cyber environments that are truly integrated, where protection, detection, and correction of security threats happen simultaneously and collaboratively. For consumers, McAfee secures your devices against viruses, malware, and other threats at home and away. With the mission of capturing the biggest market share in the area of cyber security, network security, endpoint security, threat research, malware research, cloud security, we work together for a common goal of shaping the company’s future by designing and building best in class cyber security solutions.<br>
</p>
<p><b><i>
About the role:</i></b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Analyze market and product trends and drive business cases for new opportunities.</li>
<li>
Validate product direction by interacting with customers and internal stakeholders.</li>
<li>
Work closely with executive and development teams to develop and maintain a product road-map.</li>
<li>
Serve as a product leader in pre and post sales customer engagements.</li>
<li>
Responsible for defining and communicating product strategy and road-map.</li>
<li>
Assumes leadership for team that functions as a tightly integrated, accountable, and customer-focused unit.</li>
<li>
Partner with Engineering, Customer Experience, Sales, Marketing, and Partner Product Solutions teams to accelerate market traction.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b><i>
About you:
</i></b></p>
<ul>
<li>Extensive experience leading cross functional teams including product management and engineering</li>
<li>
Excellent organizational and management skills to balance analytical and execution demands of the role</li>
<li>
Exceptional background presenting at Executive briefings, Sales kickoffs, Industry analyst discussions and Customer meetings</li>
<li>
Strong verbal, written, and presentation communication skills</li>
<li>
You will have comprehensive knowledge of the home network security or IoT security market, a proven track record in developing winning products and extensive team leadership qualities.</li>
<li>
Experience working with enterprise level customers, including Internet Providers</li>
<li>
Possess both the ability to think strategically as well as act with urgency to deliver new capabilities to market.</li>
</ul>
