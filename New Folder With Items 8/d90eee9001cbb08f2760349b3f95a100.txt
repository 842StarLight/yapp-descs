<h2>Senior Manager, Marketing, SiJCP</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>J.C. Penney Company, Inc.</b></p>
<p><b>
Plano, Texas</b></p>
<p>
The <b>Sr. Manager, </b><b>Marketing SiJCP </b>drives in partnership with JCPenney and Sephora the development, implementation and analysis of SiJCP Marketing Communications projects, programs, strategies and activities which support achievement of sales and profit objectives. Provides counsel and assistance on related matters and ensures the effective and consistent communication across customer touch points. Execute Sephora marketing through integration with JC Penney.</p>
<p><b>
Primary Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Partners with Sephora Marketing on planned events, integration, digital, social, and ensures alignment for both companies.</li>
<li>
Collaborates with Research, Planning and Promotions and agencies to develop Marketing Communications briefs that define projects for stakeholders</li>
<li>
Implement and executes marketing ideas with Sephora while maintaining the brand image and enterprise objectives, strategies and media plans.</li>
<li>
Ensure the brand story has consistent alignment across all customer touch points, ensuring a 360 view of the customer.
</li>
<li>Provide cross-functional strategic approach for all channels of marketing.</li>
<li>
Communicates out to cross-functional team (JCP and Sephora) to keep them up to date and aware to facilitate integration and all marketing collateral.</li>
<li>
Exhibit expert communication and leadership skills to establish a common understanding of project timelines, milestones and expectations on delivery.</li>
<li>
Motivate and build collaborative project team with internal and external stakeholders. Set expectations among these group regarding scope, timeline and deliverables.</li>
<li>
Ensure availability of project resources, monitor project efforts, assemble key project deliverables, and intelligently and proactively identify issues requiring escalation when reporting status.</li>
<li>
Work with all cross-functional teams and agencies to ensure that project priorities are clear, and process requirements (proper documentation, ability to deliver with available resources) are met.</li>
<li>
Executes tactical jobs including store communications , marketing collateral tracking, event planning, goal setting for stores.</li>
<li>
Executes In Store Events and planning</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Core Competencies &amp; Accomplishments:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Bachelor’s Degree in Marketing, Mass Communications or related field.
</li>
<li>8+ years of Marketing Project Management working with internal and agency project partners to successfully complete large scale, high visibility projects.</li>
<li>
Demonstrated experience in managing people and/or project management is a prerequisite, as is experience in working with senior management of a large organization.
</li>
<li>Proven ability to interface effectively and comfortably with Associates at all levels of the organization utilizing project management ability and the aptitude to work with and negotiate with other departments.
</li>
<li>Demonstrated ability to implement programs through effective use of communication skills in formal and informal settings.
</li>
<li>Relationship building</li>
<li>
Microsoft Word, Excel, Powerpoint</li>
</ul>
