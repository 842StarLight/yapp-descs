<h2>Marketing Programs Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>About Vertos Medical Inc.</b></p>
<p>Vertos Medical is a leader in the treatment of patients suffering with lumbar spinal stenosis<br>(LSS), a degenerative, age-related narrowing of the lower spinal canal. Its proprietary technologies include <i>mild®</i>, which offers an outpatient, minimally invasive, fluoroscopically guided therapeutic treatment of LSS. For more information, visit www.vertosmed.com.</p>
<p><b>Job Summary</b></p>
<p>Our business is growing and we are looking for a <b>Marketing Programs Manager </b>to join our team. In this role, you will be responsible for developing and executing programs focused on provider education to stimulate local markets. This includes professional and patient education, in-and-out of office to increase awareness/interest and generate physician desire to offer treatment and patient preference for the <i>mild®</i> procedure. You will engage pain physicians, advanced practice providers and referral physicians to educate and drive adoption, and you will also collaborate with the sales team to achieve key metrics of success.</p>
<p><b>Responsibilities and Duties</b></p>
<ul><li>Support compliance with the company quality standards, FDA regulations and guidance, applicable EU Medical Device directives, applicable ISO standards, and other pertinent country specific medical regulatory requirements.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Assess and identify geocentric markets to target for local peer-to-peer advanced practice provider programs or education and direct-to-consumer tactics to educate on treatment option.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Work with external agencies to assess markets, opportunities, costs and potential ROI to determine tactical execution plan at local level.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Work with marketing team and external agencies to ideate on and test patient and provider messaging.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Develop education content about lumbar spinal stenosis and the benefits of the <i>mild® </i>
</li></ul>
<p>procedure for advanced practice providers and other referral specialties.</p>
<ul><li>Execute local programs to educate advanced practice providers and engage them to get feedback on education and barriers to incorporating <i>mild® </i>into the practice.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Generate and execute communication and follow-up with sales team and practices to drive mindshare and momentum.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Champion education/marketing campaigns with sales team and act as a conduit between marketing, sales and practices for evaluating impact of messaging and tactics.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Work with Field Integration Specialists to assess needs and execute tactics to drive</li></ul>
<p><i>mild®</i> adoption and penetration at the practice level. This includes, but is not limited to, educating all in-office providers on patient identification, patient education, patient consultation best practices, and educating on outcomes.</p>
<ul><li>Maintain and increase professional and technical knowledge by on-the-job training, attending educational workshops, reviewing professional publications, establishing personal networks, and participating in professional societies.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Represent Company at various business meetings to promote the company.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Perform special projects and other duties as assigned.</li></ul>
<p><b>Do you have the qualifications &amp; skills for this role?</b></p>
<p>As a qualified candidate, you will have a Bachelor’s degree in a relevant field. In addition, you will have at least three years' experience in marketing and/or advertising roles and demonstrate base knowledge in the fundamentals of marketing (positioning, branding, strategic marketing, tactical execution and business analysis). You will also have intermediate to advanced knowledge of Microsoft Office applications.</p>
<p>You will also have the following:</p>
<ul>
<li>Flexibility to travel up to 50% of the time, including overnight.</li>
<li>Out-of-the-box thinking.</li>
<li>Analytical skills with an emphasis on market analysis.</li>
<li>Desire to champion execution with enthusiasm and frequent interaction with sales and providers.</li>
<li>Ability to use influence skills in situations without authority.</li>
<li>Strong verbal, written, and training communication skills, including the ability to listen to customer needs.</li>
<li>Critical feedback/coaching and development skills with peers, physicians, and others.</li>
<li>Self-motivated with an understanding of entrepreneurs and entrepreneurial environments.</li>
<li>Exceptional command of leadership, presentation, and interpersonal skills.</li>
<li>Ability to foster constructive relationships with internal and external customers.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Having these qualifications is a plus: </b></p>
<ul>

<li>Experience in a medical-related industry in a practice support, patient marketing, or equivalent marketing position.</li>
<li>Direct-to-consumer marketing: Social media, radio, patient marketing.</li>
<li>Basic knowledge of anatomy, medical and surgical terminology.</li>
</ul>
