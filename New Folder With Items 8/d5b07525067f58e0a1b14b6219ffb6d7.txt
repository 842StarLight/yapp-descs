<h2>Product Marketing Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">We are looking for an all-star to play a critical role in driving Teamworks’ rapid growth. The Product Marketer will report directly to the VP, Marketing and will craft strategy to position and promote new and existing products across the elite athletics market. The Product Marketer will also play a key role in Teamworks’ customer education efforts, ensuring users are aware of and leveraging value-add use cases and best practices.
<br><br>
Our ideal candidate has a background in Product Marketing, preferably in a SaaS environment, and has experience managing products across a diverse array of personas and market segments. Experience in the athletics industry is a plus. Most importantly, the Product Marketer must have strong alignment with Teamworks’ core values: Be honest , humble , hard working , committed , innovative , and exceptional.
<br><br>
<b>Success Within 3-6 Months:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Fully integrate into the Marketing team and Teamworks culture, building strong relationships across Marketing, Product, Sales, and Customer Success</li>
<li>Gain a deep understanding of product usage patterns, mapping use cases and documenting best practices as they apply to each of our market segments</li>
<li>Map personas across the industry and establish clear messaging around value props</li>
<li>Working cross-functionally with Product, Sales, and Customer Success, map and improve upon our product launch processes</li>
<li>Build strong processes with the Product department to ensure internal communication around new products and features and the product roadmap is seamless</li>
</ul>
<b>Activities to Achieve Success:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Build and execute product promotion strategy, targeting priority personas and market segments</li>
<li>Leverage new and innovative marketing tactics and distribution methods to reach a diverse set of users</li>
<li>Collaborate with the Customer Success team to build and execute a customer education strategy that is built for scale</li>
<li>Manage Teamworks’ release communications process, ensuring that new feature enhancements and new products are strategically positioned, messaged, and promoted</li>
<li>Be the in-house expert on our customers and buyers and their psychology. Understand what value props they respond to and what drives them to action.</li>
</ul>
<b>Experience We’re Looking For:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>5+ years Product Marketing experience</li>
<li>Startup experience and/or experience in the athletics industry a plus</li>
<li>Proven history of creative innovation</li>
<li>Ability to connect market and product strategy to tactics</li>
<li>Experience managing multiple products or multiple brands a plus</li>
</ul>
</div>
