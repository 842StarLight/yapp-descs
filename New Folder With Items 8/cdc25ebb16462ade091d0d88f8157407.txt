<h2>Pricing Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Overview
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div>Support the Marketing Managers and regional Sales teams in North and Latin America in regional price development and ensure consistent and effective implementation of Pricing processes, Pricing Systems (PROS) and champion Value Based Pricing (VBP) in the region.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div><b>
KEY DELIVERABLES:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
Margin improvement – Global Pricing team is responsible for leading actions to deliver business goal of 1-2% ($10-20m) margin improvement year over year
</li>
<li>Drive team awareness of the pricing strategy and actions they can take to support the strategy
</li>
<li>Broad understanding of VBP and its importance to developing pricing and optimizing margins</li>
<li>
Contractual and legal compliance in regional pricing</li>
</ul>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<ul><li>Price Administration and Compliance (~60% of Time)<ul>
<li>
Calculate and implement non-standard Pricing Mechanisms on time and accurately per the contractual obligations with customers</li>
<li>
Ensure regional compliance and governance for price approvals, Most Favored Customers, and internal controls</li>
<li>
Lead and champion usage and training of PROS pricing system including:<ul>
<li>
Price Optimizer in marketing</li>
<li>
Deal Optimizer in sales</li>
<li>
Scientific Analytics modules</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>
Apply Lean / Six Sigma methodologies to improve proficiency of Americas Pricing Processes</li>
</ul>
</li></ul>
<ul><li>
Margin Management (~30% of Time)<ul>
<li>
Proactively analyze Americas results to:
<ul>
<li>Track Pricing Strategy implementation and assess relevance of Pricing Architecture with Sales organization, for instance lead implementation of Market General Price Changes</li>
<li>
Identify areas of potential margin improvement</li>
<li>
Define action for corrective measures</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>
Follow Global guidance for Quote Management, steering the regional Americas Sales and Marketing team to comply with the process and ensure a proactive approach to quotation development</li>
<li>
Execute price reviews, including supporting justification and documentation for use internally and externally<ul>
<li>
Raw Material Driver Overviews</li>
<li>
Package Cost Trends</li>
<li>
Finished Fluid Price Trends</li>
<li>
Key Customer Share of Wallet &amp; Profitability Analysis</li>
<li>
Sensitivity analysis (historic vs future pricing expectations)</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>
Identify and implement projects to reduce value leakage, recommending actions e.g Product Portfolio optimization, drive Americas’ WIG (Wildly Important Goals) initiative</li>
<li>
Support Sales staff at customers as a Pricing expert, providing “consultancy” as appropriate to help customers maximize the value of their portfolio</li>
</ul>
</li></ul>
<ul><li>
Pricing Strategy (~10% of Time)<ul>
<li>
Implement and support the regional pricing strategy in line with the Global Pricing Policy &amp; be a regional champion for Value Based Pricing<ul>
<li>
Be knowledgeable in Value Based Pricing methodologies</li>
<li>
Coach and develop skills base across the full customer-facing team</li>
<li>
Understand product and customer segmentation for each BU and work with Marketing Managers to identify and measure value drivers for each product/customer segment</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>
Monitor pricing and coach product marketers to ensure that all three elements of pricing (market competition, customer derived value, product cost) are factored into SBU and product pricing</li>
<li>
Work with Sales and Marketing to support a system to gather and store competitive intelligence as allowed by local Laws and regulations</li>
<li>
Responsible for auditing regional adherence to the Global Pricing Policy and Most Favored Customer(MFC) Policy</li>
</ul>
</li></ul>

<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Qualifications
 </b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div>
<b>EDUCATION</b><b> &amp; EXPERIENCE MINIMUMS:</b>
</div>
<ul>
<li>
BS/BA and/or MBA in marketing, business or finance
</li>

</li>
</ul>

Experience working with price optimization software, such as PROS or Vendavo</li>

Subject matter expert in SAP a plus</li>

SKILLS/ABILITIES:</b></div>

Effective leadership skills &amp; experience in a cross-functional, matrixed team environment</li>

Knowledge of the principles of value based pricing, strategic (market) thinking and related financial impacts</li>

Strong tenacity in delivering tangible outcomes</li>
<li>
Effective influencing &amp; negotiating skills</li>

Experience in the principles of market competition, product value extraction &amp; customer analysis

</ul>
