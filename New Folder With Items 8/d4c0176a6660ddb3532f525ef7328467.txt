<h2>Senior Project Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">Role Summary
<br><br>
The Professional Services Senior Project Manager will be responsible for managing all aspects of ServiceMax software implementation projects. Your focus is ensuring a successful and expedient go-live for our customer in order for them to gain the long term benefits of our software. Projects are expected to be complex implementations, often involving integrations, middleware and potential partner or other 3rd party involvement. In this role you will utilize your experience or expertise to solve problems, develop and execute objectives for self and others, and have the ability to effect short-term and some long-term business goals.
<br><br>
The successful candidate will demonstrate leadership in understanding customer objectives, appropriately representing ServiceMax, effective and timely communication, setting expectations, accurate planning, gaining mutual agreement and managing to budget, scope and schedules.
<br><br>
<b>Responsibilities:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Project owner for overall customer and ServiceMax success</li>
<li>Primary customer contact for the duration of each project and owner of all project communication</li>
<li>Plan and implement projects at various customer work sites in the US and abroad, including the activities owned by ServiceMax, customer, 3rd parties and any subcontractors</li>
<li>Track and manage the operational, financial, and deliverable aspects of projects based on timelines and work plans.</li>
<li>Proactively communicate both internally and externally, including project risks, issues, or any information that may affect success.</li>
<li>Translate customer needs / requirements into detailed project plans while driving internal execution to meet project targets.</li>
<li>Understand the client's information technology governance structure and incorporate in the project plan / budget. Provide feedback to Scoping team as required</li>
<li>Leverage appropriate stakeholder involvement throughout the life of the project to ensure success</li>
<li>Identify opportunities to expand the project in ways that bring value to our customer and satisfy our profitability requirements</li>
<li>Participate in the development of proposals for projects</li>
<li>Manage budget and communicate status, including burn rate, to project executives on a weekly basis or as contractually required</li>
</ul>
Understand the client's information technology governance structure and incorporate in the project plan / budget. Provide feedback to Scoping team as required
<br><ul>
<li>Resolve, document, and / or escalate critical issues as needed in a timely manner to minimize project risk factors</li>
<li>Ensure that delivered products and services achieve highest quality and performance levels</li>
<li>Negotiate change orders with the customers</li>
<li>Professionally represent ServiceMax during all customer communications. Document and evangelize customer product requests and requirements. Be a compelling and trusted "voice of customer" when evangelizing the features that will yield the biggest impact in current and future implementations</li>
</ul>
<b>Qualifications:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor's degree from an accredited university or college (or a high school diploma/GED with at least 5 years of experience in an engineering or project management position</li>
<li>At least 5 additional years of experience in a project management, engineering or manufacturing role</li>
<li>At least 5 years of experience leading enterprise software implementations with one or more Customer</li>
<li>Relationship Management Solution (such as Oracle, SalesForce.com, SAP, Siebel or other CRM software) configuration experience desired</li>
<li>Experience with enterprise integration platforms (such as Informatica, IBM Cast Iron, or MuleSoft)</li>
<li>Experience with technologies supporting SaaS applications, including XML, Web Services, HTML, Java and JavaScript</li>
<li>Exposure to Salesforce.com CRM. Salesforce.com certification is a big plus</li>
<li>Experience with field service operations or customer service management and call center support a big plus</li>
<li>Adept at interactions with C Level executives</li>
</ul>
