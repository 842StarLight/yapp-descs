<h2>Lead Generation Coordinator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>The Lead Generation Specialist will work directly with the Regional Managers and will be responsible for identifying potential business opportunities for the sales team. Activities will include account research as well as outbound prospecting via cold calling, telephone and email. This position is responsible for generating highly qualified leads by engaging in business to business cold calls with marketing materials to physically determine, validate and discover possible opportunities in our target markets. Creativity, positive attitude, and persistency applied to researching, interacting with and qualifying prospective customers is essential. The Lead Generation Specialist must demonstrate enthusiasm, tenacity and possess strong interpersonal skills. This position also requires excellent written and oral communication skills, organizational ability and strong work ethics.<br>
<br>
<br>
<b>Essential Functions</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Identify and interact with leads via cold calls, emails or phone solicitations</li>
<li>
Serves as initiator for each potential sales opportunity.</li>
<li>
Works closely with members of the sales team.</li>
<li>
Set realistic, daily goals and manage a leads pipeline.</li>
<li>
Consistently collects, inputs and updates information into the CRM software.</li>
<li>
Provide timely periodic results to the Regional Manager.</li>
<li>
Generate lead activity that contributes toward achieving annual sales goal.</li>
<li>
25-30 minimum accounts contacted per day.</li>
<li>
Represents Flagship in accordance with the Company’s Code of Business Ethics and Conduct.</li>
</ul>
