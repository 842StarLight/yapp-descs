<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div>The Senior Business Analyst-Life Insurance/Illustrations is the individual who will have primary responsibility to analyze, validate, and specify the needs of the project stakeholders, customers and end users. The Business Analyst will serve as a conduit between the customer community and the software development team through which the project requirements will flow. The Business Analyst will provide specifications and produce outline designs of IT requirements, specifying the data, files and logical operations the system will perform and delivery expectations of the end user.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div><b>
Primary Responsibilities</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
Work with the project sponsor to identify and document the product or service vision and project scope.</li>
<li>
Collaborate with Project Managers to evaluate reported defects and identify scope creep.</li>
<li>
Collaborate with Project Managers to ensure software releases meet release dates set by clients.</li>
<li>
Identify project stakeholders and user groups.</li>
<li>
Solicit requirements by conducting interviews, performing data and document analysis, surveys, site visits, etc.</li>
<li>
Write requirements specifications according to organization practices using language that is clear, concise and encompassing for a diverse audience.</li>
<li>
Decompress high-level customer and end user requirements into functional requirements, suitable in quality and detail to be used by team members who must base their work on these requirements.</li>
<li>
Define quality benchmarks, external interfaces, and other project constraints.</li>
<li>
Lead the requirements analysis and validation ensuring that the requirement statements are complete, concise, feasible, and verifiable.</li>
<li>
Participate in requirements prioritization.</li>
<li>
Conduct peer reviews and inspection of documentation at declared stages.</li>
<li>
Manage and control requirements status throughout the project.</li>
<li>
Manage changes to baseline requirements ensuring company change requirement rules are followed.</li>
<li>
Identify ways to provide enhanced training solutions to the end user community.</li>
</ul>
