<p><b>Job Description</b>:</p>
<p>
Who We Are</p>
<p>
We are playmakers, game-changers, and go-getters who are motivated by the relationships we create and the products and services we write about. We love data. We believe in big ideas. And we always fight for the user. At the core of everything we do is the desire to elevate the customer experience and create something worth talking about. We take our work—and our dreams—seriously.<br>
<br>
<b>Who We’re Looking For</b></p>
<p>
Technology Reporter / Home Security or Mobile, Salt Lake City, Seattle, Orem<br>
<br>
If polished prose and meticulous research send shivers of excitement down your spine, you’ll want to keep reading. And if you’re a home security geek who nerds out over smart home technology or you’re a mobile guru always looking for the best carrier to support your unlimited data lifestyle, well, you may have found your new home.<br>
<br>
As a Technology Reporter specializing in Home Security or Mobile technology at Clearlink, you’ll help folks find the services and products that are right for them. You’ll take complex ideas and information, and make that data accessible for different audiences. Engaging landing pages. Persuasive service and product reviews. Informative blog posts. You’ll write it all—working across multiple channels and industries with a team of like-minded professional marketers.<br>
<br>
It’s a tough gig. But if you love throwing words on the page and making them play nice together, we should meet.<br>
<br>
<b>Breaking Down Your Day-to-Day</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Research and write about Home Security or Mobile technology products and services for our websites</li>
<li>Write expert-level articles for outside websites</li>
<li>Script and perform informational videos</li>
<li>Collaborate with designers, SEO specialists, project managers, web developers, and marketing managers</li>
<li>Write across multiple digital marketing channels (organic search, PPC, email, social, display, etc.)</li>
<li>Track and analyze content performance</li>
<li>Ensure content aligns with brand positioning and voice</li>
<li>Juggle numerous projects with competing deadlines</li>
<li>Adjust voice and tone for audience and project type</li>
<li>Study digital marketing and UX best practices</li>
</ul>
<p><br>
<b>What You Need</b></p>
<ul>
<li>3+ years’ experience in journalism, content marketing, or related field</li>
<li>A demonstrable passion for all things Home Security or Mobile related (preferably in direct relation to journalism or blogging)</li>
<li>Bachelor’s degree in Communications, Creative Writing, Journalism (or similar discipline) from an accredited college/university or equivalent work experience</li>
<li>Experience with Microsoft Word, Microsoft Excel, Adobe Acrobat, Google Suite, Airtable, and similar software applications</li>
<li>Excellent spelling and grammar skills</li>
<li>The ability to take / provide constructive and positive feedback</li>
<li>An insatiable drive to learn and grow</li>
<li>Solid communication and organizational skills</li>
<li>An agile mindset</li>
<li>The desire—and ability—to collaborate</li>
<li>A knack for solving complex problems</li>
<li>Strong grasp of marketing trends</li>
<li>An understanding of what language, structure, and value propositions will resonate with our readers</li>
<li>A strong portfolio with a variety of samples</li>
</ul>
