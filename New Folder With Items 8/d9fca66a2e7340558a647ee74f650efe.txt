<h2>Merchandiser, Nashville TN, 2019-09-04</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Empire Distributors Inc, A Leading Beverage Distributor is now hiring a <b>Merchandiser</b>. This person will represent our company to retail customers. This person will assist customers in stocking and displaying Company products. Our company offers excellent benefits, competitive wages and the potential for growth opportunities.</p>
<p><b>Essential Duties and Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul><li> Meets delivery trucks at accounts to put up stock from deliveries.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Refurbishes existing displays, stocks shelves and cold boxes with product.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Delivers and setup up promotion display items and product.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Arranges display of items in customer's establishment.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Forwards customer comments and sales opportunities from customer to assigned Sales Representative.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Designs, typesets and produces Point of Sale and advertising for internal and external customers.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Proofreads and edits customer materials for accuracy and adherence to state guidelines.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Assists with finish work, including cutting, laminating, binding, large format printer and printing press jobs.</li></ul>
<p><b>Education and/or Experience</b></p>
<p>High school diploma or general education degree (GED); or one to three months related experience and/or training; or equivalent combination of education and experience.</p>
<p><b>Certificates, Licenses, Registrations</b></p>
<p>TN ABC Wholesaler's Employee Permit</p>
<p>Valid Driver License with F Endorsement</p>
<p>Current DOT Medical Card.</p>
<p><b>Computer Skills</b></p>
<p>None</p>
