<h2>Production Scheduling Specialist</h2>

<p><b>BACKGROUND</b></p>
<p>
Home Market Foods HMF is a dynamic enterprise that has become the market leader in a number of prepared meat-based meal and snack categories. The company has grown substantially across its markets through excellence in innovation, manufacturing, and customer service. This success has been nurtured by its ownership group and an expanding team of world-class executives. HMF is building on this track record through complementary acquisitions and a growing number of business development initiatives. HMF's business model is poised to continue to thrive and offer greater opportunities for its customers and team.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
We are based in Norwood, Massachusetts, which is comfortably within the Boston metropolitan area. HMF's facilities include a state-of-the-art plant, and R&amp;D facility, as well as warehouse and Corporate functions. We are focused on continued growth and are executing against various initiatives that include additional product lines, new sales channels, greater penetration of our extensive customer relationships, as well as opportunistic acquisitions. Our success is directly dependent on attracting and retaining the best possible talent across our functions. To this end, we are posting for a strong Production Scheduling Specialist.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
OVERVIEW OF ROLE</b></p>
<p>
The Production Scheduling Specialist is a value-added member of our Supply Chain team. The specialist will become familiar with our ERP systems and develop an understanding of how sales forecasting coincides with the schedule and our material requirement planning process. An understanding of the production process will be key to success. Data accuracy for this position is a must, as is a desire to learn and continuously improve.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
RESPONSIBILITIES
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Work with the procurement team to track deliveries and changes to delivery appointments
</li>
<li>Update the scheduling and MRP tool with daily production and sales orders</li>
<li>
Input and understand changes to both raw material and finished goods inventory balances within the scheduling and MRP tool
</li>
<li>Analyze discrepancies between production consumption and theoretical BOMs</li>
<li>
Create and manage production orders in Microsoft Navision
</li>
<li>Issue purchase orders to co-packers and monitor completion through a reconciliation between expectations and receipts
</li>
<li>Maintain VMI spreadsheets and to ensure inventory is available for all promotions</li>
<li>
Solve short term scheduling issues with a cross functional team including Production Managers and Customer Service</li>
<li>
Work with Demand Planner and Sales Planning team to understand impacts of forecast changes</li>
<li>
Manage scheduling and tracking of all R&amp;D test runs</li>
<li>
Work with interdepartmental teams, including sales, marketing, R&amp;D, regulatory, engineering, logistics, purchasing and finance departments to efficiently launch new items</li>
<li>
Provide support to the Operations Planning Manager through ad hoc projects</li>
<li>
Other duties as assigned</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
QUALIFICATIONS</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Bachelor's degree with GPA of 3.5 or higher or</li>
<li>
2+ years of experience in supply chain to include master scheduling/material planning in a food/beverage environment preferred</li>
<li>
Understanding of sales and operations planning, including direct correlations to MPS/RCCP/MRP</li>
<li>
Exposure to ERP systems in relationship to multiple level BOM's, Work Centers, Forecasts, Capacity Planning, MPS, and MRP</li>
<li>
General knowledge of MRP, including lot size, lead time, scrap factors, yield factors, and safety stock</li>
<li>
Proficiency in Microsoft Excel</li>
<li>
Strong communication and project management skills</li>
<li>
Proven prioritization skills and attention to detail
</li>
<li>Ability to work in a fast-paced, ever-changing</li>
</ul>
