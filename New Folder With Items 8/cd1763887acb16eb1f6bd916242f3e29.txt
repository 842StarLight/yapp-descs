<h2>Retail Mortgage Lender</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p><b>POSITION PURPOSE</b></p>
<p>
To support the company’s mission statement, strategy and sales goals by establishing new customer relationships and retaining and expanding existing customer relationships through the development of affiliations with Centers of Influence and participation in the company’s sales management program. Demonstrate and implement our Trusted Advisor strategy to all internal and external customers.</p>
<p><b>
ESSENTIAL FUNCTIONS AND BASIC DUTIES</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Originate mortgage and consumer loan applications using the bank’s loan origination software systems at the point of sale.</li>
<li>
Exercise good decision making up to credit or authority limits.</li>
<li>
Outline loan terms and conditions and inform borrowers of loan commitments; Assist borrowers by making appropriate recommendations.</li>
<li>
Maintain compliance with loan policy, procedures and documentation, including loan package preparation in accordance with First Federal Bank and our partners, including Freddie Mac, Fannie Mae, MGIC and others.</li>
<li>
Produce quality referrals to all business units through regular calls on existing customers and prospective customers and referral sources.</li>
<li>
Cross-sell additional bank products and services to customers.</li>
<li>
Meet the expectations of First Federal Bank’s Service Level Agreement for lenders.</li>
<li>
Provide on-going service to existing customers and branch personnel regarding loan questions and issues.</li>
<li>
Complete other duties as assigned.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
WORK ENVIRONMENT</b></p>
<p>
This job operates in a professional office environment. Work is normally performed in a typical interior work environment which does not subject the employee to any hazardous or unpleasant elements.</p>
<p><b>
QUALIFICATIONS</b></p>
<p><b>
Education/Certification:</b></p>
<p>
High school diploma or equivalent; Bachelor’s degree in business or finance, or equivalent combination of education and work experience.</p>
<p><b>
Required Knowledge:</b></p>
<p>
Thorough knowledge of Company and provider services and products, including secondary market; Understanding of related legal and regulatory requirements; complete understanding of lending functions, processes, policies, and procedures.</p>
<p><b>
Experience Required:</b></p>
<p>
Three years of related experience in a financial institution, with a minimum of two years of lending experience.</p>
<p><b>
Skills/Abilities:</b></p>
<p>
Strong interpersonal, presentation and organizational skills; excellent verbal and written communications skills; sales and goal-oriented personality, with high degree of professionalism; ability to maintain an effective and efficient workflow; strong problem solving and critical thinking skills.</p>
