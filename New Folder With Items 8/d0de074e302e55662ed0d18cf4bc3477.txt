<h2>Strategic Account Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>Do you love taking a data-driven approach to problem-solving and selling? If you have large scale, Strategic Account Management experience with an outstanding, record of success. Read on all of you data enthusiast!<br>
Do you currently work in the BI/Data Analytics space and desire an uncapped quota? Have insightful product solutions, but are unheard?<br>
We need you, a self starter who can creatively build out existing business within a short period of time, while winning over business partnerships<br>
All Strategic Account Managers, ready to drive the adoption and evolution of the GoodData Platform, please apply.</p>
<p>
RESPONSIBILITIES:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Develop an account strategy to drive revenue growth, business and partnership development, and community building</li>
<li>
Lead and own enterprise accounts, serving as the single point of contact across all work with the customer, maintaining relationships and keeping stakeholders engaged</li>
<li>
Serve as both "Consultative Sales Professional" and "Consultative Sales Coach" across all customers and internal teams</li>
<li>
Manage complex sales cycles and multiple engagements simultaneously while growing pipeline</li>
<li>
Manage a portfolio of projects for the assigned customer, ensuring coordination of shared resources, common activities, and other dependencies across project</li>
<li>
Provide context to Sales with respect to contract renewals, negotiations, documentation and knowledge sharing</li>
<li>
Report on sales activities and forecasts to senior management.</li>
</ul>
<br>
ABOUT YOU:<br>
<br>
<ul>
<li>4+ years experience selling in a customer-facing enterprise, data analytics/BI, program/project delivery, technical project delivery discipline</li>
<li>
Exceptional written and verbal communication skills, ability to simplify complex concepts, extensive experience creating and delivering executive level presentations</li>
<li>
You thrive in a fast-paced, collaborative, team selling environment</li>
<li>
You are successful when given the opportunity to grow business with a client, and can set and execute on a multi-year growth strategy with the client</li>
<li>
You have demonstrated the ability to manage and exceed quota and key metrics</li>
<li>
Empathetic to customer and end-user needs, with the ability to translate those needs into solutions that deliver value</li>
<li>
Management consulting and start-up experience strongly preferred</li>
<li>
You are able to bring the level of ownership, entrepreneurship, creativity, commitment, time, patience, and humor it takes to work with an evolving and growing organization (startup experience)</li>
</ul>
<br>
REQUIREMENTS:<br>
<br>
MUST LOVE DATA! MUST LOVE DATA ANALYTICS! MUST LOVE SOLVING PROBLEMS! MUST LOVE UNCAPPED QUOTAS!<br>
<br>
PLEASE NOTE: ONLY DATA ENTHUSIASTS NEED APPLY!<br>
