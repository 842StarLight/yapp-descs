<h2>Restaurant Managers and Assistant Managers</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<div><div><div>
<p><b>A. POSITION IN ORGANIZATION</b></p>
<p>1. Reports directly to Area Manager/Supervisor</p>
<p>2. Directly Supervises restaurant employees.</p>
<p><b>B. SUMMARY OF RESPONSIBILITIES AND ACCOUNTABILITIES.</b></p>
<p>1. Maintain highest level of Quality, Service, Cleanliness and Hospitality.</p>
<p>2. Takes an active role in customer service and customer relations, monitors and corrects restaurant employees' appearance, courtesy and suggestive selling techniques and takes part in company marketing efforts.</p>
<p>3. Tracks restaurant labor and product costs to meet set targets, orders supplies, paper, and food products needed, maintains proper inventory levels and controls waste, plans weekly schedule for restaurant employees.</p>
<p>4. Follows published procedures for receiving, preparing, holding, packing and serving products.</p>
<p>5. Has all menu items available at all times.</p>
<p>6. Maintains high level of productivity through effective recruiting, training, and motivation of hourly employees.</p>
<p>7. Has routine preventive maintenance program in operation, checks, adjusts and makes minor repairs of restaurant equipment, building and grounds.</p>
<p>8. Prepares and submits required reports, uses approved projection techniques; cash control techniques and security measures.</p>
<p>9. Recruits, interviews, recommended hiring, disciplinary action, and termination of hourly restaurant employees and keeps required personnel and performance information.; develops, motivates and effectively trains and maintains a safe work environment.</p>
<p>10. Performs other tasks as required by area manager/supervisor.</p>
<p><b>C. SPECIFIC RESPONSIBILITIES</b></p>
<p><b>1. Customer Service/Suggestive Selling Role</b></p>
<p>a. Ensures quality service by routinely working front counter and packing areas.</p>
<p>b. Takes a personal interest in customer satisfaction by talking with customers and checking product quality.</p>
<p>c. Effectively trains all restaurant employees in customer service skills and on the job coaching.</p>
<p><b>2. Sales Building</b></p>
<p>a. Develops employee understanding and commitment to sales building.</p>
<p>b. Periodically conducts employee sales meeting to review results and establish new goals.</p>
<p>c. Effectively trains restaurant employees in suggestive selling techniques using on the job coaching and regular performance evaluations.</p>
<p><b>3. Customer Relations</b></p>
<p>a. Becomes involved in the community to show company concern and support of the communicates in which we operate.</p>
<p>b. Ensures courtesy of restaurant employees to customers and fellow employees.</p>
<p>c. Monitors and evaluates customer service times and takes corrective action to meet QSCH guidelines when necessary.</p>
<p>d. Controls uniform and dress code of all employees assigned to the restaurant by supplying proper uniforms and stressing grooming and neat appearance.</p>
<p>e. Handles customer complaints and informs the Area Manager/Supervisor of actions and decisions.</p>
<p><b>4. Cost of Operations</b></p>
<p>a. Reviews and analyzes restaurant P &amp; L compared with restaurant records and takes corrective action where needed.</p>
<p>b. Orders all necessary supplies (such as food and paper products as well as cleaning and general supplies) from distributors and supplies approved by the Area Manager/Supervisor.</p>
<p>c. May order additional outside services after approved by the Area Manager/Supervisor.</p>
<p>d. Reduces energy costs by monitoring effective utilization of lighting, cooking and air tempering equipment.</p>
<p><b>5. Cost of Sales.</b></p>
<p>a. Checks the quality of all goods received at the restaurant to conform to specifications.</p>
<p>b. Informs the Area Manager/Supervisor of shipments rejected because they do not meet specifications and takes action as directed to assure product quality.</p>
<p>c. Assures product freshness and prevents spoilage by maintaining proper storage rotation and levels of inventory.</p>
<p>d. Projects correct amount of product and supplies needed, monitors yields and takes corrective action to maintain standard yields.</p>
<p><b>6. Product</b></p>
<p>a. Has all menu items available at all times.</p>
<p>b. Follows all published procedures and specifications, uses only approved ingredients, maintains inventory or required utensil and equipment, uses only approved set points.</p>
<p>c. Follows published procedures for holding prepared products.</p>
<p>d. Follows First-In-First-Out method of rotation.</p>
<p><b>7. Labor</b></p>
<p>a. Uses company approved guidelines to determine labor needs.</p>
<p>b. Develops and posts weekly schedule to restaurant employees and Assistance Managers assigned to restaurant.</p>
<p>c. Adjusts labor to meet sales volume.</p>
<p>d. Obtains prior approval of Area Manager to set hourly rate of pay of restaurant.</p>
<p>e. Submits justification to Area Manager/Supervisor for any exception to labor guidelines before the exception is made.</p>
<p><b>8. Sanitation</b></p>
<p>a. Uses only approved cleaning products and procedures unless otherwise directed by Area Manager/Supervisor.</p>
<p>b. Has available and uses all necessary cleaning supplies and equipment.</p>
<p>c. Achieves the highest possible rating of Health Department inspections.</p>
<p><b>9. Maintenance</b></p>
<p>a. Has restaurant level preventive maintenance program in operation.</p>
<p>b. Identifies needed repairs and forwards requests for service to Area Manager/Supervisor.</p>
<p>c. Maintains record of maintenance department inspections and trouble calls for cost verification purposed.</p>
<p><b>10. Administration</b></p>
<p>a. Prepares and submits required reports on a timely basis.</p>
<p>b. Uses product projection chart properly.</p>
<p>c. Uses Action-Needed program.</p>
<p>d. Follows all cash control and security procedures.</p>
<p>e. Complies with payroll policies and submits all payroll requisitions according to company policy.</p>
<p>f. Follows all procedures and policies for computer processing of restaurant records.</p>
<p><b>11. People</b></p>
<p>a. Keeps the Area Manager/Supervisor informed of recruitment, selection and orientation of hourly employees and obtains Area Manager/Supervisor approval on decisions and actions taken.</p>
<p>b. Develops and motivates hourly restaurant employees, assistance managers, shift leaders, and manager trainees assigned to the restaurant.</p>
<p>c. Tracks, analyzes and takes necessary action to reduce employee turnover costs.</p>
<p>d. Understand and maintains personnel files for each hourly employee assigned to the restaurant, maintains the following compliance documents: OSHA, EEOC, and other Wage and Hour documents as required in the restaurant location.</p>
<p>e. Informs the Area Manager/Supervisor of disciplinary actions.</p>
<p>f. Maintains a safe work environment that meets OSHA and local fire codes and follows Corporate Policy and Procedures on safety.</p>
<p>g. Takes action necessary to minimize Workman Compensation claims as well as unemployment claims against the company.</p>
<p>h. Takes corrective disciplinary actions to improve employee behavior.</p>
<p><b>12. Marketing</b></p>
<p>a. Performs in restaurant marketing efforts directed by Area Manager/Supervisor, such as employee sales contests, effective uses of point of sales advertising materials, coupon redemption's etc.</p>
<p>b. Maintains awareness of growth, promotions, sales trends and products of local competitors.</p>
<p>c. Participates in catering program as direct by the Area Manager/Supervisor.</p>
