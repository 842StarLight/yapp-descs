<div><b>
About the Role</b></div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
We are looking for a Senior Strategic Finance Associate as part of our Sales Finance team for Uber Eats. Uber Eats is a fast growing business and you would be partnering closely with the Sales and Sales Ops leadership for Uber Eats on key strategic decisions that drive restaurant selection on our platform.</div>
<div>
If you're ready to go deep, working on a rewarding set of challenges and if you've got the finance skills, experience, and passion - read on and get in touch!</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
<div><b>What You'll Do</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
Business partnerships - work closely with Sales leaders to develop financial sales forecasts and operational goals, apply business judgment and financial insights to assist with decision making</li>
<li>
P&amp;L planning - you will be the primary point of contact for all global sales finance initiatives (including building the P&amp;L for restaurant segments, designing the forecasting process, goal setting, and modeling support)</li>
<li>
Sophisticated financial modeling - build models from scratch, improve existing models, break complex models down into components that consolidate - you can do it all</li>
<li>
Analyze variances between actuals, forecast, and plan - use insights to iterate on forecasts and create action</li>
<li>
Think strategically and then execute - the models we build aid in decision making and strategy - we operationalize our insights.</li>
<li>
Help us scale / transition from startup to enterprise - Part of this is maturing our planning processes, part is ensuring finance best practices spread through the organization.</li>
<li>
Special projects and deal support - new initiatives always come up, as strengths emerge you will have the chance to chip in beyond traditional FP&amp;A</li>
<li>
Innovate - we will push the boundaries beyond traditional forecasting. Be creative, adaptive, and eager to learn new technical skill sets</li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<br>
<div><b>
What You'll Need</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
Bachelor's degree (e.g,. Business Administration, Economics, Mathematics, Finance, Accounting)</li>
<li>
Minimum 5+ years of relevant analytical and management experience in the technology industry, investment banking, or management consulting and with 2 years of relevant experience in Sales Finance or Operations preferably in a fast growing B2B environment</li>
<li>
Exceptional analytical skills - Best practices are second-nature, your models are efficient, scalable, and intuitive; hands-on, detail oriented, positive attitude, and be able to summarize and translate complex models into initiatives and actions</li>
<li>
Excellent communication and people skills - numbers are key, but a business is built with people, be able to challenge and align with the key stakeholders</li>
<li>
Self starter who embraces rolling up your sleeves; skilled at process optimization; thrives on change and comfortable with ambiguity</li>
<li>
Systems - working knowledge of enterprise-level ERP systems (Oracle, Tableau etc)</li>
<li>
Be able to deep dive into the details while not losing the big picture</li>
<li>
Strong personal code of ethics, integrity, diversity and trust. Strong references from former employers and business partners; passion for building relationships</li>
</ul>
