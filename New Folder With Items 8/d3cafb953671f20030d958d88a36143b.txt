<h2>Manufacturing Compliance Specialist (Contract)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p><b>AGC Biologics</b> is a leading global Contract Development and Manufacturing Organization, with a deep commitment to improving life quality by bringing new biopharmaceuticals to market. Because of our dedication to building and empowering our internal teams, AGC Biologics is an innovative leader in the industry with an extensive network of cGMP facilities in the US, Europe and Asia. As such, we deliver a deep expertise, dynamic solutions and technologies, as well as customized services for the scale-up and cGMP manufacturing of protein-based therapeutics. We forge exceptionally strong partnerships with our clients, and we never lose sight of our pledge to deliver a reliable and compliant drug substance supply, Right, On time. For more information, visit www.agcbio.com</p>
<p><b>
SUMMARY:</b></p>
<p>
AGC Biologics is looking for a <b>Manufacturing Compliance Specialist</b> to support Manufacturing activities including deviation investigation and report composition, CAPA creation and closeout, and communication of compliance strategy to stakeholders.</p>
<p><b>
PRINCIPAL RESPONSIBILITIES:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
<p>Perform root cause analysis on manufacturing deviations; create deviation reports.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Maintain facility GMP compliance; assist in ensuring regulatory inspection readiness.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Work closely with other team members to ensure project progression; represent the Manufacturing department’s interests on multidisciplinary project teams.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Serve as liaison between multiple teams including manufacturing, quality, and process development.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Clearly communicate compliance strategy and rationale, both verbally and in written communications.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Lead investigations for manufacturing issues, bringing them to a resolution under minimal direct supervision.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Initiate appropriate corrective action/preventative action (CAPA) to prevent recurrence of deviations.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Work closely with manufacturing operations and QA team to drive CAPAs to timely closure.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Work within site quality and asset management systems.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Support quality system programs such as Deviation, CAPA, and Change Management.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Review records and assist in record closure for Lot Disposition process.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Update and revise site manufacturing SOPs.</p>
<p><b>
KNOWLEDGE, SKILLS &amp; ABILITIES:</b></p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Strong knowledge of FDA and EU regulations for APIs and Drug Products.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Ability to understand and apply GMP regulations as they relate to manufacturing.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Strong technical writing skills.</p>
<p><b>
EDUCATION / EXPERIENCE:</b></p>
</li>
<li>
<p>B.S. in Chemistry, BioChemistry, Biology or related scientific discipline preferred.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Minimum of 5+ years’ experience conducting investigation and root cause analysis in a pharmaceutical or FDA/GMP environment required.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Experience using GMP compliance in a clinical/commercial manufacturing environment required.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Experience using quality management software (Mastercontrol, Trackwise, etc.) preferred.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Experience with asset management software (Blue Mountain, Maximo, etc) preferred.</p>
<p><i>
AGC Biologics offers a highly competitive compensation package and a friendly, collaborative culture that values personal initiative and professional achievement. EOE</i></p>
</li>
</ul>
