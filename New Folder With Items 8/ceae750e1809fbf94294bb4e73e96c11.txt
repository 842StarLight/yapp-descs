<h2>Marketing Competitive Intelligence Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><ul>
<div>
<p><b>KEY ROLES &amp; RESPONSIBILITIES (</b>required<b>):
</b></p>
<ul>
<li><p><b>Provide market and competitive intelligence, insights and strategy recommendations to Product and regional affiliates</b></p></li>
<li><p><b>
Develop a strategy and framework for segmentation, how to monitor market trends, competitors, technology partners, and other macro- and micro forces that could impact NAVIFY’s business</b></p></li>
<li><p><b>
Develop a market book and gaining alignment from the regional affiliates</b></p></li>
<li><p><b>
Develop and apply the right programs, platforms, tools that to help surface competitive and market intel in accordance with all legal and ethical boundaries</b></p></li>
<li><p><b>
Implement the right processes, systems, programs, platforms, tools to formulate insights and develop and support a cadence of communications for timely dissemination of information to best arm NAVIFY</b></p></li>
<li><p><b>
Be the resident go-to in-house subject matter experts regarding market strategy, competition, and potential beneficial or adversarial partnerships</b></p></li>
<li><p><b>
Work closely with Product Marketing and regional affiliates to create sales plays and competitive take-out programs to drive greater NAVIFY revenue</b></p></li>
<li><p><b>
Deliver presentations with relevant, actionable, and up-to-date accurate information.</b></p></li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>
KNOWLEDGE/ SKILLS/ATTRIBUTES (required):
</b></p>
<ul>
<li><p><b>Bachelor’s degree in business, marketing, or related field required.</b></p></li>
<li><p><b>
5-10 years of experience in business strategy, market research, or competitive intelligence</b></p></li>
<li><p><b>
Deep knowledge and understanding of the healthcare SaaS business model landscape</b></p></li>
<li><p><b>
Previous experience setting up a growing market intelligence practice in house and/or supplementing information via external vendors</b></p></li>
<li><p><b>
Strong business acumen, understanding of strategies, plays, and take-out tactics</b></p></li>
</ul>
