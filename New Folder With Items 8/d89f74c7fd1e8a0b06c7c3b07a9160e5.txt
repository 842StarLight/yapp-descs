<h2>Coord, Eating Disorders Outreach</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p><b>Requires extensive travel, weekend, and evening hours.</b></p>
<p><b>Job Summary: </b>The Coordinator, Eating Disorder Outreach is responsible for creating awareness and demand for the nationally recognized Laureate Eating Disorders Program (LEDP) through planning, developing, and implementing marketing and outreach programs. These activities are fundamental to maintaining an adequate pool of referral sources and potential patients by remaining a leader in the competitive national ED market for financial viability and growth of the LEDP.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Essential Functions and Responsibilities</b></p>
<p>Responsible for development, presentation, and implementation of long and short range strategic marketing and outreach plans to promote and ensure admissions to the nationally recognized LEDP specialty services.</p>
<p>Serves as expert consultant for the highly specialized and rapidly changing niche ED market to ensure alignment of organization's operational strategic plan, mission, vision, and values.</p>
<p>Collaborates with senior leadership in development of annual marketing and outreach budget. Provides oversight of approved budget to monitor and maximize generated revenue. Provides expert recommendations for sponsorships with national organizations and selection of national events in which to participate.</p>
<p>Researches, compiles, analyzes, and manages LEDP outreach database, admissions demographics, and national ED market trends.</p>
<p>Revises marketing strategies to maintain relevance and strategic placement in competitive national eating disorders market.</p>
<p>Develops, supervises, and serves as consultant for local events. These include events such as referral and potential referral source monthly clinical site visits and participation in Eating Disorders family week events, webinars, National Eating Disorders Awareness community events, networking lunches, and physician presentations at professional and community events. Requires availability after hours and on weekends.</p>
<p>Manages national outreach activities to capture maximum market potential. Coordinates and oversees eating disorders staff at national conferences, speaking engagements, networking events, and sales calls. Requires extensive travel, weekend, and evening hours.</p>
<p>Responsible for all copy writing and dissemination of program collateral materials and marketing communications, to include physician marketing, monthly newsletter, brochures, national and local print advertising, proposals for speaking. Designs and manages content of LEDP website. Coordinates with SFHS Marketing department to update and revise website content as needed.</p>
<p>Serves as the sole liaison to the media for the LEDP services. Works with the national, local print, and broadcast media on news stories as requested.</p>

Knowledge of internet marketing, marketing concepts. Ability to work independently and adjust to changing priorities.</p>
