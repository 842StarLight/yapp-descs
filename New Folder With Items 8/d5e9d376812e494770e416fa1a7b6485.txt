<b>POSITION SUMMARY:
</b></p>
<p>The Income Auditor’s primary responsibility is to ensure that the daily revenue from all outlets
</p>
<p>is captured and recorded accurately then post to resort accounting system Actual Ledger. The
</p>
<p>Income Auditor Contributes to the success of Resort.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>ESSENTIAL DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITES:
 </b></p>
<p>On daily basis collects the previous night’s package compiled by Night Auditor from<br>
Front Desk.
 </p>
<p>Checks the daily revenue report, credit card worksheet and various city ledgers to make<br>
sure all revenues are entered correctly.
 </p>
<p>Communicates to the Night Auditor or Front Desk Manager when city ledgers are out of<br>
balance and require investigation and/or additional postings to correct.
 </p>
<p>Verify daily, month to date, plan and last year figures are correct on the Daily Sales<br>
Report for actual and plan, then distribute if not already distributed by Night Auditor.
 </p>
<p>Review or prepare the Income Audit Journal, by retrieving the information (including<br>
statistical data) from various sources, daily revenue report, SMS, Micros and other excel
</p>
</div></div>
<div><div>
<p>reports, make sure both revenues and settlements are balanced. Import the income<br>
journal to resort accounting system actual ledger.
 </p>
<p>Have monthly spreadsheets set up prior to the start of each month for use by the Night<br>
Auditor. This may include Daily Sales Report, Credit Card Report.
 </p>
<p>Finish the cash report prepared by Central Cashier by completing other half of the<br>
information from SMS, Micros and other reports. Double check any overages or<br>
shortages resulted and issue over/shortage notices and follows up on daily basis. Inform<br>
accounting manager of any major variances
 </p>
<p>Ensure that all gratuities are recorded according to Hotel Policy. Prepare gratuities<br>
sheets, attach general ledger account analysis and ensure that payments made do not<br>
exceed amounts collected. Obtain signatures from the outlets and the Controller before<br>
forwarding to Payroll for bi-weekly payroll processing.
 </p>
<p>Check on daily basis, to ensure all the void checks are given proper explanation and<br>
authorized by their manager. Follow up if any void checks are not properly explained or<br>
approved.
 </p>
<p>Reconcile total credit card transactions for each day and ensure funds are transmitted<br>
to the bank. Reconcile credit cards accounts balance in general ledger balance with the<br>
bank statement. Investigate and correct any variance including chargeback’s and non-<br>
payment advises received from various credit card providers. Make sure the total<br>
amounts posted to the general ledger are balancing to the total amount transmitted<br>
and deposited to bank.
 </p>
<p>Check total amounts posted to the general ledger, against Guest Ledger, City Ledger,<br>
Reservation Deposits and Gift Certificates are balanced to the SMS Report
 </p>
<p>Make sure all the GST, PST and any other taxes are been booked as per the Hotel Policy.<br>
Any variances are investigated and rectified
 </p>
<p>Make sure all Guest Activities &amp; Services (coordinated with outside contractors)<br>
revenues are split correctly according to the agreement between the hotel and the<br>
contractor, payable to balance Sheet account and hotel share to revenue account
 </p>
<p>Investigate and make sure on daily basis, that all vouchers for rebates/ allowances are<br>
properly explained and signed by Front Office manager or assistant. Any unusual<br>
amounts must be reported to the account manager. Provide direction when required to<br>
assist other associates in completing vouchers correctly.
 </p>
<p>On daily / monthly basis (based on Hotel fiscal Calendar year) check the revenues<br>
posted to the actual ledger are balanced to daily revenue report.
 </p>
<p>Make sure all documents related to Revenue Journal are stored in a proper place on<br>
monthly basis.
 </p>
<p>Conduct surprise cash counts of house fund holders on regular basis with a witness.
 </p>
<p>Ensure that the night auditors adhere to the Night Audit checklist and income audit<br>
checklist is adhered to.
 </p>
<p>Complete cross training as required to provide assistance/vacation coverage for other<br>
positions within the department.
 </p>
<p>Some stress resulting from daily guest interactions (internal and external), budgetary,<br>
and time constraints
 </p>
<p>Other job related duties may be assigned.<br>
</p>
</div></div>
