<h2>Weekend Real Estate Sales Assistant</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Job Summary: </b>Assist in day-to-day operation of the Sales Office and support of the Sales Team. Answer phones and ensures showing schedules and reports are prepared and properly maintained. Open homes for showings and run errands as needed. <b>Weekend work required</b>.</p>
<p><b>
Essential Duties &amp; Responsibilities </b>include the following, but not limited to<b>:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Assist with answering phone &amp; directing to appropriate person.</li>
<li>
Greeting guests in the Real Estate Office.</li>
<li>
Assist in maintaining general files.</li>
<li>
Assist and coordinate operational needs with the Sales Team.</li>
<li>
Opening, staging, and closing homes to be shown.</li>
<li>
Meeting with service persons &amp; contractors on site.</li>
<li>
Scheduling showings with Owners and outside Brokers.</li>
<li>
Ensure cross training of job responsibilities with Sales Administrator.</li>
<li>
Assist with listings, invoices and advertising/marketing as needed.</li>
<li>
Reporting</li>
<li>
Update and maintain Sales reporting as needed.</li>
<li>
Create new reporting forms as needed.
</li>
<li>Completes other appropriate tasks as assigned by Broker and/or General Manager/COO</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Job Requirements:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Excellent communications verbal and written skills.
</li>
<li>Innovative and forward thinking.</li>
<li>
Outstanding people skills.</li>
<li>
Guide the day-to-day operations of the office in a business-like, positive, professional and ethical manner.</li>
<li>
Seek to improve self in knowledge and skills to remain knowledgeable about the market and best practices.</li>
<li>
Ability to maintain good working relationship with employees, members, vendors and all clients.</li>
<li>
Effective use of time and efficient multi-tasker; flexible in work habits and work schedule.</li>
<li>
Demonstrated record of good work attendance and reliability.</li>
<li>
Must be highly organized and detail-orientated.
</li>
<li>Experience in Microsoft Word, Excel, Publisher, Power Point, Outlook, Social Media Platforms.</li>
<li>
Requires weekend work Saturday &amp; Sunday Full Day.</li>
<li>
Real Estate office experience and MLS experience helpful but not required.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Physical Demands and Work Environment</b></p>
<p>
Working conditions are normal for an office environment. Must be able to reach, bend, stoop, stand and lift up to 40 pounds. Must be able to sit for prolonged periods of time. Work schedule will include some evenings and/weekends.</p>
