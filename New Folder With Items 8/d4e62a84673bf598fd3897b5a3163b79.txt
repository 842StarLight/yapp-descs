<p><b>Job Description:</b></p>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Line of Business Role Description;</b></p>
<p>
Manages the client service and fulfillment activities for treasury management products, programs and services for a large team or geographic area. May act as the primary interface with the client to establish all connectivity between organizations, organizing all internal and external project related activities to ensure a timely and smooth client implementation process. Exceptionally skilled at developing and deepening relationships with clients and business partners. Participates in the development of short and long-range client service enhancement strategies that drive client delight and revenue growth. Monitors internal processes and leverages six sigma methodologies to implement enhancements that drive client delight. Has expert knowledge in the end-to-end sales, service and fulfillment processes. Must be a high impact player who exhibits forward thinking, promotes team effort, and demonstrates initiative.<br>
Key Responsibilities - Must align enterprise capabilities, create competitive business plans while maintaining client focus, balances risk and reward and acts as a change agent. Digest complex issues and develop creative solutions. Creates unit vision, lead people to perform and develops employees. Manages staffing requirements, performance management, budget/expense management, reporting and forecasting for areas managed. Participate in the resolution of operational and/or product servicing issues of a sensitive/high risk nature for major clients.</p>
<p></p>
<p>
This position will lead the Treasury Fulfillment &amp; Service teams supporting CREB clients. This position will serve as the TFSO engagement leader for the CREB Senior Sales Executives.</p>
