<h2>Graphic Designer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>GRAPHIC DESIGNER POSITION </b></p>
<p><b>DELSEY Luggage</b> is one of top largest brands of luggage distributed worldwide, we looking for a full time creative and hardworking individual with a minimum 5-year experience in graphic design to join its US team.</p>
<p>The Graphic Designer will report to the US Director of Marketing, and is responsible for the design and production of all marketing communications materials for the US market, in alignment with the overall corporate identity, messaging and strategy. This position requires full knowledge of all design software systems as well as photography.</p>
<p>Key responsibilities include:</p>
<ul>
<li>Design, develop and produce printing communication and marketing materials, including, but not limited to, hangtags, brochures, feature sheets, press kit, forms, direct mail, posters, store signage, promotional coupons, advertising materials and client presentations.</li>
<li>Create a variety of graphics to be used on the company websites, social media outlets, and email campaigns.</li>
<li>Photograph new product collections and maintain the content library for all imagery. This involves retouching, resizing, renaming images and uploading them to the company and clients’ data servers.</li>
<li>Help the team with the preparation of sales meetings and trade shows by creating large-scale print graphics for wall panels, lightboxes, and presentation boards.</li>
<li>Ensure all materials created are aligned with the corporate branding and style guide.</li>
</ul>
<p>Desired skills and competencies:</p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree in graphic design, plus minimum 5 years of experience.</li>
<li>Intermediate to advanced knowledge of Adobe Creative Cloud products including Photoshop, Illustrator, InDesign and Acrobat Pro (Mac platform).</li>
<li>Web design code/software experience such as Adobe Dreamweaver, FTP (filezilla), and coding emails.</li>
<li>Working knowledge of Adobe After Effects</li>
<li>Firm grasp of print processes including file preparation and color check. Must be able to oversee a project from conception to completion. Must understand how to optimize files for all marketing mediums, both digital and print.</li>
<li>Strong technical proficiency in digital photography. Knowledge of image processing and digital enhancement techniques. Experience in editing, color correcting and retouching product and lifestyle photography.</li>
<li>Organized and detail oriented even in a fast-paced environment.</li>
<li>MUST be a strong team player with strong communication, organizational and time management skills.</li>
<li>Portfolio demonstrating expertise required.</li>
<li>Must be comfortable working in an international environment.</li>
</ul>
<p>We offer a competitive base salary and a full benefits package including health insurance, life, dental and a 401(k) plan with a company match.</p>
