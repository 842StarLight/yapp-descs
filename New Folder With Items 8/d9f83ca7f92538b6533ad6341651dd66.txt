<h2>Assoc Sales Dir</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div><div>
<p>IQVIA™ is the leading human data science company focused on helping healthcare clients find unparalleled insights and better solutions for patients. Formed through the merger of IMS Health and Quintiles, IQVIA offers a broad range of solutions that harness the power of healthcare data, domain expertise, transformative technology, and advanced analytics to drive healthcare forward.</p>
<p>
Position Summary / Responsibilities:</p>
<ul>
<li>Thorough command of company portfolio offerings including both product and services lines; with specific experience selling to Pharmaceutical companies</li>
<li>Identify and generate new business and potential target clients through outside sales activities such as cold calls, prospecting, telemarketing, territory planning, scheduling meetings and relationship building to grow account base.</li>
<li>Partner with specialty sales and other BU’s, both global and regional, to drive results and manage client relationships.</li>
</ul>
<br>
Requirements:<ul>
<li>7+ years of recent Sales and Business Development experience; business to business sales selling pharmaceutical product and Consulting Services to clients.</li>
<li>Should consistently exceed Sales Quota/Sales Targets, and have the ability to drive business development with an established record of closing new accounts.</li>
<li>Competent in all aspects of the sales process, prospecting, cold/warm calling, scheduling meetings, client presentations, gathering requirements and managing results.</li>
<li>Team Player with his/her Colleagues' and Customers' best interest in mind at all times.</li>
<li>Capable of selling and leading in a fast-paced environment.</li>
<li>Has successfully sold Information Technology Staffing, Products and/or Services in excess of $3Million gross revenue annually.</li>
<li>Possess a " hunter" mentality with the ability to manage existing accounts as well as forge new account business opportunities.</li>
</ul>
