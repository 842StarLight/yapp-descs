<h2>Shift Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Wings over Storrs is seeking a Shift Manager for the upcoming school year!</p>
<p>Our Shift Managers work with our General Managers and Assistant Managers oversee the daily operations of Wings Over Restaurant by working with team members to ensure outstanding customer service, a high level of productivity and respect for each other; as well as to maximize sales and to control food, labor and other operating expenses.</p>
<p><b>WHAT'S IN IT FOR YOU!</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Full-time positions with 5 days per week, 8 hour per day</li>
<li>All hours over 40 worked each week are paid at time and one half</li>
<li>FLEXIBILITY!</li>
<li>Discounted employee meals</li>
<li>Paid Sick time</li>
<li>Group Health Benefits, including Medical, Dental and Vision</li>
<li>Paid Holidays</li>
<li>Paid Vacation</li>
<li>401k with a 4% match</li>
<li>Tuition Reimbursement</li>
<li>Advancement opportunities – we prefer to promote from within to AM or GM</li>
<li>Performance reviews and potential pay increases after 3 and 12 months and annually thereafter.</li>
<li>Employee Referral bonus</li>
</ul>
<p><b>What you will be doing while Running your own shifts: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Maintains shift operations by following established policies and standard operating procedures; implementing production, productivity, quality, and patron-service standards</li>
<li>Controls food costs by reviewing portion control and quantities of preparation; minimizing waste; ensuring high quality of preparation.</li>
<li>Conducts and accounts for precise daily inventory of restaurant food products.</li>
<li>Assists GM to attract customers by developing and implementing marketing, advertising, public and community relations programs.</li>
<li>Works with Manager to achieve restaurant operational objectives by contributing information and recommendations to action plans; assists with implementing production, productivity, quality, and customer-service standards and resolving problems.</li>
<li>Is learning to assists Managers in accomplishing restaurant human resource objectives by recruiting, selecting, orienting, training, assigning, scheduling, coaching, counseling, and disciplining hourly staff; communicating job expectations; assisting with planning, monitoring, appraising, and reviewing job contributions; planning and reviewing compensation actions; enforcing policies and procedures.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>What you need to be successful with us?</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Availability during our hours of operation which includes evenings, late night, weekends and some holidays</li>
<li>Skillful to provide outstanding customer service and maintain customer focus to increase sales</li>
<li>Experience controlling food, labor and other operational costs to show increase profits</li>
<li>Experience with people management and an the able to nurture, coach, mentor and hold team members accountable</li>
<li>Capable to develop team member schedule and flex based on sales volume demands</li>
<li>Experience resolving conflict with customers and team members</li>
<li>Ability to identify poor process and knowledge to create new and more efficient to processes</li>
<li>Possess solid communication (verbal and written) and interpersonal skills</li>
<li>Sound decision making and problem solving skills</li>
<li>High level of initiative and able to multitask</li>
<li>Time management and deadline oriented</li>
<li>Self-Motivated and a High Energy Level</li>
<li>Average minimum 1 years’ experience in food service management, required</li>
<li>Experience with fast casual restaurant with delivery, preferred</li>
<li>High school diploma/GED, required</li>
<li>Associate’s degree or higher in Hospitality or Food Service, preferred</li>
<li>Must be legally eligible to work in the United States and able to provide appropriate documentation to complete the form I-9 within 3 days of their first day of employment</li>
<li><b>Must be 18 years of age, acceptable criminal background check, possess a valid driver’s license, maintain an acceptable motor vehicle record (MVR), proof of insurance and have a satisfactory vehicle</b></li>
</ul>
