<h2>Sales Development Representative</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>
<div>As Sales Development Representative (SDR) you will be the first point of contact of our prospective customers, and will be responsible for generating and qualifying new enterprise customers.</div>
<div>
You will help prospect and qualify potential clients for our growing sales team. The ideal candidate has a keen eye for new business and can rapidly qualify, convert, and assign those opportunities. This role will be a part of a new team that will build the process of expanding our enterprise business.</div>
</div>

<li>
Develop ideal customer profiles for outbound prospecting</li>
<li>
Qualify inbound inquiries</li>
<li>
Build new methods to attract potential customers and partners</li>
