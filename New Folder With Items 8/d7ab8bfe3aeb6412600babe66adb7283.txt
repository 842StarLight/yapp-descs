<h2>Marketing Planning & Program Specialist III</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<b>Job Description</b><br>
<br>
<div>The Auto Club Group (ACG) provides membership, travel, insurance and financial services offerings to approximately 9 million members and customers across 11 states and 2 U.S. territories through the AAA, Meemic and Fremont brands. ACG belongs to the national AAA federation and is the second largest AAA club in North America.</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div><b>Primary Duties and Responsibilities:</b></div>
<div>Participates in the planning, development and implementation of strategic, corporate marketing programs or projects that support Auto Club Group (ACG) marketing goals and objectives.<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>Collaborates with key stakeholders, staff and vendors, as appropriate to develop/document marketing or project plans. May utilize statistical procedures and complex analysis tools to measure and analyze results. Recommends new or enhanced strategies and tactics based on results as appropriate.</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>Develops and maintains effective working relationships with internal partner work groups (e.g., product lines and business areas) and vendors, as appropriate, within a diverse product, cross-functional environment. Works in cooperation with internal or external resources to ensure the accuracy and timeliness of campaign or report rollout. Identifies any potential operational issues and possible solutions.<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>Maintains and applies current and emerging marketing knowledge for assigned area(s) of expertise. Ensures compliance with applicable marketing related laws, regulations, company policies and guidelines.</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div>
<br>
<div><b>Direct Response-Direct Mail and Direct-Response-Email
</b></div>
<div>Develops comprehensive direct response campaign components and project plans and defines target audience. Facilitates the process of producing the campaign target audience file, and ensures list integrity. Develops and distributes lead lists for field agents and staff. Ensures campaign is executed in accordance with budgetary guidelines. Manages, organizes and archives documentation for all campaigns. Monitors activity and market results against plan and provides ongoing support by channel, product and market. Compiles data and prepares reports for use by Marketing management in determining appropriate course(s) of action.</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div><b>Direct Response-Email:</b></div>
<div>Uses available email marketing software (e.g., ExactTarget) to create, test and launch campaigns. Maintains and ensures the updating of email address database.<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<div><b>Direct Response-Reporting</b></div>
<div>Facilitates the Auto Club Group’s (ACG) marketing planning process. Formats, monitors and communicates marketing plan components to ensure plans align with corporate business objectives and with marketing and communication activities. Monitors activity and market results against plan and provides ongoing reporting by channel, product and market. Develops and maintains performance reporting for direct response and website business performance. Consults with direct response and internet marketing staff to identify reporting needs and documents report objectives, research available data sources and propose reporting solutions. Maintains and publishes reports as required.<br>
</div>
</div>
