<p>
VMware is the leader in virtualization and cloud infrastructure solutions that enable our more than 350,000 enterprise and SMB customers to thrive in the Cloud Era/ A pioneer in the use of virtualization and automation technologies, VMware simplifies IT complexity across the entire data center to the virtual workplace, empowering customers with solutions in the software-defined data center to hybrid cloud computing and the mobile workspace.</p>

If you are ready to accelerate, innovate and lead, join us as we challenge constraints and problem solve for tomorrow today.</p>
<p>
VMware is creating a dedicated sales team to assist our field with our new AppDefense solution in the Network and Security Business Unit (NSBU). AppDefense is a breakthrough new solution launched last September to protect applications and data running on virtualized and cloud infrastructure. It leverages the hypervisor itself to ensure the only thing running is what the application intended – flipping the security model to “ensuring good” versus “chasing bad”. This simple yet powerful solution is being marketed and sold into VMware’s massive installed base – both into their InfoSec teams and vAdmin/compute teams.</p>

Job Role and Responsibilities:</p>
<p>
The Account Executive for AppDefense will be responsible for a coordinated sales approach with VMware’s NSX and Core sales teams. They will own the AppDefense bookings target. They will engage in direct competitive selling opportunities. Their mission is to accelerate sales velocity and growth of this new solution. They will focus on selling into key accounts, building sales momentum, helping identify early use cases, refine messaging, and ultimately codify the ideal selling motion for the broader field organization. They will work closely with the product teams, providing critical customer and market feedback. And they will work to enable the broader VMware sales organization, raising awareness of the opportunity, helping them get proficient in selling this new solution</p>
