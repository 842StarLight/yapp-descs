<h2>Agency Partner</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>We are looking for a US Agency Partner to join Team Snapchat! You'll build and manage relationships with Independent Agencies to help them expand their digital presence on Snapchat. Working from our New York, NY office, you'll need to be strategic, have great presentation and people skills, the ability to manage many projects at once, and a deep understanding of the digital advertising world.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>What you’ll do:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li><p>Hold responsibility for the financial performance of your Independent Agency roster in the US; work to ensure that Agency and Account meets targets while also activating new accounts
</p></li>
<li><p>Firmly establish Snapchat as a leading brand building and business driving platform in the eyes of multiple Agency constituents at both established Independent Agencies to those redefining Agency models
</p></li>
<li><p>Act as the primary point of contact, escalation point and thought leader for your Agency Roster in the US
</p></li>
<li><p>Develop and deliver education &amp; inspiration focused meetings that have an impact on multiple roles (media, digital, strategic planning) across named list of Independent Agencies
</p></li>
<li><p>Develop status as a trusted peer by leadership across the Independent Agencies, partner with the Sales team to develop key accounts and to activate new accounts
</p></li>
<li><p>Conduct periodic business reviews with Agency leadership and important constituencies
</p></li>
<li><p>Understand Agency’s businesses and develop appropriate solutions to meet their needs
</p></li>
<li><p>Provide best-in-class client service and partnership across all facets of Agency investment, planning, social and research
</p></li>
<li><p>Manage and activate US trading agreements with key Independents. Develop strategic frameworks and incentive plans to drive growth for full agency purview
</p></li>
<li><p>Drive Agency growth by initiating and developing relationships across Agencies, developing strategic frameworks and plans, and focusing on both top accounts and new advertisers
</p></li>
<li><p>Work collaboratively with cross-functional teams to drive revenue growth with new and existing customers
</p></li>
<li><p>Track revenue performance across the Agency portfolio in the US and determine corrective measures where needed<br>
</p></li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Minimum qualifications:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li><p>Bachelor’s degree in business, communications, marketing, or another related area of study
</p></li>
<li><p>10+ years of marketing, brand advertising, agency, media sales and/or online advertising experience leading businesses or accounts<br>
<br>
</p></li>
</ul>
<p><b>Preferred qualifications:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li><p>Experience working with account contacts and growing revenue through the development of relationships
</p></li>
<li><p>Exceptional communication and presentation skills
</p></li>
<li><p>Experience in sales and marketing in a sales/commission-based role to advertising agencies
</p></li>
<li><p>Ability to influence and communicate cross-functionally
</p></li>
<li><p>Understanding of Snapchat value proposition, and the ability to describe technical or complicated ideas into plain language
</p></li>
<li><p>Experience in working for matrix organizations &amp; amp; ability to win the support of key internal cross-functional stakeholders
</p></li>
<li><p>Experience in a highly dynamic, rapidly changing environment
</p></li>
<li><p>Proven ability to build relationships at the C-level across the business both internally and externally with market reputation
</p></li>
<li><p>Direct agency management experience</p></li>
</ul>
