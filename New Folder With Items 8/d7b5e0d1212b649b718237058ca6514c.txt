<h2>Travel Agent Assistant</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Summary: </b></p>
<p>The Travel Assistant is responsible for the guiding and educating of clients in their decision to find the best travel accommodation and destination solutions to ensure a positive travel experience. The Travel Assistant will assist the Travel Consultant and possess a flair for exceptional customer service and aptitude for learning.</p>
<p><b>Duties/Responsibilities: </b></p>
<p>· Guide clients from start to finish based on their travel destination needs and preferences.</p>
<p>· Provide high-end customer service to our clients and vendors.</p>
<p>· Build a positive rapport with our clients and vendors.</p>
<p>· Build long-term relationships with our clients and vendors.</p>
<p>· Assist the Travel Consultant as directed.</p>
<p><b>Required Qualifications: </b></p>
<p>· Proven ability to provide excellent customer service.</p>
<p>· Natural consultative skills with the ability to offer confident recommendations.</p>
<p>· Resilience and composure to remain positive under pressure and constantly evolving circumstances.</p>
<p>· Ability to work independently, exercising discretion and good judgement.</p>
<p>· Previous Microsoft Office and other software experience.</p>
<p>· Exceptional communication skills, both written and verbal.</p>
<p><b>Preferred Qualifications: </b></p>
<p>· Excellent geography knowledge is preferred.</p>
<p><b>Compensation/Benefits: </b></p>
<p>· Hourly wage is dependent upon experience.</p>
<p>· One week paid time off after ninety days and two weeks after one year of full time employment.</p>
<p>· Health, dental, vision and life insurance are available effective the first of the month following sixty days of employment.</p>
<p>· Seven paid holidays per year.</p>
<p>· Life insurance policy paid by the company.</p>
<p>· 401k eligibility at annual enrollment after the first year of employment.</p>
