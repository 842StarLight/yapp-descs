<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>About the job</b></h2>
<p>
As a Marketing Strategy Lead, you'll be involved with product marketing strategy from beginning to end. You will the strategy for Google’s advertising narratives in a way that connects Google’s core business objectives with what matters most to our customers. You’ll establish the roadmap, rally the stakeholders, and define the program requirements to ensure Google is established as the trusted authority on the future of marketing.</p>
<p>As Marketing Strategy Lead, your strategic vision and persuasion will be instrumental in driving both internal alignment and momentum, and impact for the business. You’ll foster deep relationships with our most valuable advertising/business contacts in order to distill our business objectives into storytelling objectives, and you’ll collaborate deeply with our research partners to identify the insights that will unlock new ways of thinking. With equal commitment to business goals and what matters to our customers, you’ll ensure our creative choices and communications reflect our strategic goals with empathy, finesse and impact, and you’ll continually absorb new inputs and prioritize how they’re applied. In close partnership with sales, research, creative, editorial, and events, you’ll have the chance to create new initiatives that ultimately shift audience mindsets and drive them to take action.</p>
<p>
Know the user. Know the magic. Connect the two. At its core, marketing at Google starts with technology and ends with the user, bringing both together in unconventional ways. Our job is to demonstrate how Google's products solve the world's problems-from the everyday to the epic, from the mundane to the monumental. And we approach marketing in a way that only Google can-changing the game, redefining the medium, making the user the priority, and ultimately, letting the technology speak for itself.</p>
