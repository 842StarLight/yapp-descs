<h2>PMO Team Lead - TTS North America</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<ul>
<li>Establish the Trade and Treasury North American Project Management Office – to identify, provide, and manage project management resources for highly complex and transformative projects.</li>
<li>
Establish curriculum and toolkit for self-service project management approach for smaller initiatives.</li>
<li>
Increase Senior Management visibility over all in flight initiatives via reporting including status reports/trackers, executive summaries and steering committee decks. Be able to effectively deliver the key message around the project updates/risks to business and senior leaders</li>
<li>
Partner with Regional and Global Product Management to ensure sufficient funding to achieve project goals (e.g. technology budget-related).</li>
<li>
Ensure risk and control commercialization deliverables are completed, documented and approved.</li>
<li>
Establishing appropriate governance model for transformative projects involving multiple teams within Trade and Treasury Services and Citigroup enterprise.</li>
<li>
Orchestrating the execution of the project plan across multiple leads, implementers, vendors, and consultants in different groups such as Operations, Technology, Legal, Risk, Compliance, Product, Sales and many others in multiple geographical locations</li>
<li>
Act as key project manager and single point of contact for the coordination and successful delivery of key projects across the PMO team. Overseeing the execution and completion of the various work streams of the project across the globe.</li>
<li>
Identifying and forming strong working relationships with key internal and external stakeholders. Obtaining agreement and signoffs on all objectives and milestones with the stakeholders</li>
<li>
Creating metrics and monitoring/reporting project status on a regular basis. Abstracting the key details and business relevance to make the updates meaningful for a wide range of audience including senior executives</li>
<li>
Managing scope through change control process. Being flexible while setting clear expectations after careful analysis of the scope change</li>
<li>
Diligently creating and managing relevant document repository ranging from Communication Plan, SOW/PID, Project Charter, Project Plan, Risk Register, Issue Log, Status Trackers and various other PM artefacts to provide clear communication, transparency and updates to the extended project team</li>
<li>
Ensuring effective transition from development to commercialization.</li>
</ul>
