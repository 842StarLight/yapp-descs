<h2>Assistant Club Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>JOB OVERVIEW:</b></p>
<p>
An Assistant Club Manager is more than just the person who helps run the day to day operations of the club; they are our future leaders! They have the unique opportunity to work side by side with our management team, ask questions and enhance their skill set as they work towards taking the next step in their career. The Assistant Club Manager will have the opportunity to improve their ability to drive sales, own the execution of operational processes and inspire their team to deliver an exceptional experience to members.</p>
<p><b>
RESPONSIBILITIES</b><b>:</b></p>
<p>
The Assistant Club Manager is responsible for:</p>
<ul>
<li>Conducting weekly inventory, including retail, beverage, marketing collateral and maintenance supplies</li>
<li>Placing monthly supply orders and retail orders as needed via the Blink web portal</li>
<li>Creating Front Desk and Maintenance schedules</li>
<li>Ordering drinks weekly</li>
<li>Managing entire cash reconciliation and deposit process</li>
<li>Receiving and adjust all retail inventory via club management software</li>
<li>Onboarding all new associates</li>
<li>Touring and signing up new members</li>
</ul>
<p>The Assistant Club Manager will partner with the Club Manager on:</p>
<ul>
<li>Driving sales through successful tour execution and lead generation activity</li>
<li>Executing all initiatives that support the delivery of the Blink member experience</li>
<li>Managing the staff on day to day performance</li>
<li>Assist in driving Personal Training revenue</li>
<li>Interview, hire, train and develop new staff members</li>
</ul>
<p><b>QUALIFICATIONS</b><b>:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Minimum high school education or equivalent.</li>
<li>Previous management and/or key holder experience is preferred.</li>
<li>Proven ability to direct and train team</li>
<li>Excellent verbal and written communication skills</li>
<li>Enthusiastic, energetic, friendly and personable</li>
<li>Passionate and knowledgeable regarding the fitness industry</li>
<li>Current CPR/AED certification</li>
<li>Computer literate on the Microsoft Suite</li>
<li>Excellent time management, organizational, problem solving and follow-up skills</li>
</ul>
<p><b>COMPENSATION AND BENEFITS</b><b>:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Competitive Compensation</li>
<li>Complimentary Blink Membership</li>
<li>Benefits</li>
</ul>
