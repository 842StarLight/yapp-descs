<h2>Manager, Professional Services</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>ROLE IMPACT</p>
<p>
Vertafore develops and delivers software to the Insurance industry, helping our clients save time and money. In support of our products and our clients, the Senior Manager, will be immediately involved in the following initiatives:</p>
<ul><li>Leading a team of project managers in a high energy, technical environment.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Responsible for ensuring budgets, schedules and performance standards are attained.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Responsible for serving as the primary customer escalation point for customer changes and/or issues during the implementation process.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Assists the sales teams with project scoping exercises and preparing statements of work.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Responsible for providing project oversight to ensure all project management processes are followed.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Drives process improvements to streamline work and provide a higher value to our customers.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Inspire team with drive and motivation needed to aggressively meet commitments.</li></ul>
<p>
 Work with sales, product management, development and cross functional teams to improve customer experience.</p>
<p>QUALIFICATIONS/DUTIES</p>
<ul><li>Minimum of 3 years managing a high performing Professional Services team in a high tech environment with services delivery as primary focus</li></ul>
<ul><li>Minimum of 5 years project management or consulting experience preferably in a SaaS company</li></ul>
<ul><li>Strong knowledge of program management</li></ul>
<ul><li>Broad knowledge of process improvement methodologies (Six Sigma, Lean, BPM)</li></ul>
<ul><li>Demonstrated ability to drive large, highly complex programs in a fast-paced, matrix management organization</li></ul>
<ul><li>Strong interpersonal skills with the proven ability to effectively engage and influence at all levels</li></ul>
<ul><li>Ability to effectively and efficiently analyze data to identify potential issues that aid in decision making by mitigating risks</li></ul>
<ul><li>Ability to effectively prioritize workload and tasks to meet deadlines</li></ul>
<ul><li>Manage confidentiality without exception</li></ul>
<ul><li>Excellent presentation and communications skills, both written and verbal</li></ul>
<ul><li>Outstanding time management skills</li></ul>
<ul><li>Ability to interact with clients of all levels, from C-level to IT individual contributor</li></ul>
<ul><li>Ability to be creative and innovative</li></ul>
<ul><li>Experience with Salesforce, Project Management software and Professional Services Automation tools</li></ul>
<ul><li>Insurance industry experience preferred</li></ul>
<p><i>
Travel would be 25% or less</i></p>

Education Requirements</p>
<ul><li>Bachelor’s degree in Business Administration/Management or Computer Sciences</li></ul>
