<h2>Implementation Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Requirements</b></h3>
<ul>
<li>3-5 years of experience in the enterprise software space working directly with customers, preferably leading implementations or owning the customer relationship</li>
<li>
Prior experience in a Consulting or Professional Services organization</li>
<li>
Prior experience with enterprise scale software implementations and systems integration is required</li>
<li>
Demonstrated experience working with global F500 organizations is desirable</li>
<li>
Strong written and verbal communication skills including C-Level presentation experience</li>
<li>
Ability to build consultative relationships with senior members of marketing and IT management</li>
<li>
Ability to identify business challenges and shape solutions that fit with the Percolate platform vision</li>
<li>
Ability to understand enterprise software products, and talk fluently about the product to non-technical users and buyers</li>
<li>
PMP certification is a plus, but not required</li>
<li>
Willingness to travel - usually only 25% - but may vary by customer</li>
<li>
Bachelor’s Degree (preferably in Business, Marketing or Information Systems)</li>
<li>
Understanding of common digital marketing technology practices preferred</li>
</ul>
</div>
<div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities</b></h3>
<ul>
<li>Drive customer implementations to successful launch by managing scope, budget, timeline, resources and the customer relationship</li>
<li>
Plan, manage and communicate the progress of the implementation, both internally and externally</li>
<li>
Lead and run alignment workshops to to map customer’s requirements to the Percolate solution</li>
<li>
Provide internal coaching and leadership to team members on implementations, as well as on delivering world-class enterprise implementations</li>
<li>
Demonstrate cross-functional leadership to ensure alignment between Product launches and project objectives</li>
<li>
Partner with Sales teams to provide expertise and advice during the sales cycle</li>
</ul>
</div>
