<h2>Mechanical Engineering Project Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Overview:</b></p>
<p>
The primary role of the Mechanical Engineering Project Manager is to produce detailed engineering product submissions for standard and custom-designed mechanical systems and for commercial HVAC, Plumbing, and Industrial Process applications.</p>
<p><b>
Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Produce accurate content for submittal documents.</li>
<li>Support the production/fabrication teams with product engineering information and calculations.</li>
<li>Participate in the development of standard products.</li>
<li>Meet customer design goals.Perform calculations for mechanical, electrical, and structural sizing.</li>
<li>Coordinate with the CAD department to develop the custom project and standard product drawings.</li>
<li>Review consultant engineering construction documentation (plans &amp; specifications) and perform equipment take-offs required for selecting and pricing equipment.</li>
<li>Visit job sites as required to determine site conditions and provide advice for future/retrofit equipment packages.</li>
<li>Support customers with application engineering and documentation including preliminary system layouts and equipment selection.</li>
<li>Coordinate with manufacturer vendors to design, price, and build custom equipment packages.</li>
<li>Perform and review calculations for mechanical and electrical equipment sizing and selection using standard engineering practices and manufactures product selection software.</li>
<li>Assist engineers, sales, business development in the design of fluid handling and heat transfer systems.</li>
<li>Develop and maintain standard technical documentation required for manufacturing.</li>
<li>Write/review specifications for system packages.</li>
<li>Develop control schemes and sequence of operations (SOO) for equipment packages.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Competencies:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Detail-oriented</li>
<li>Follow through</li>
<li>Time Management</li>
<li>Microsoft Office</li>
<li>3D AutoCAD and Revit proficiency</li>
</ul>
