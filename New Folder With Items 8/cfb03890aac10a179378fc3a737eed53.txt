<h2>Digital Product Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div><b>Digital Product Manager</b></div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
<div><b>Job Summary:</b></div>
<div>
The Digital Product Manager is responsible for defining the product vision and managing the roadmap for our websites. They are responsible for optimizing the online experience for our customers and increasing digital engagement, online utilization and online orders.</div>
<div><b><br>
Reports to:</b></div>
<div>
Director of Digital Marketing</div>
<div><b><br>
Company:</b></div>
<div>
Direct Supply is the nation’s leading supplier of equipment, services and eCommerce systems to the Senior Living profession. Since 1985, we have assembled one of the brightest, hardest-working teams in America. Our high-tech culture is fast paced, and our business continues to grow at an astounding rate year after year. Best of all, we are a Milwaukee-based, 100% employee-owned company that offers great opportunities for ambitious, motivated people.</div>
<div><b><br>
Essential Job Functions and Leadership Responsibilities:</b></div>
<div>
<ul>
<li>
Utilize customer feedback, competitive analysis, industry best practices and internal feedback to define and manage the product roadmap for our websites</li>
<li>
Drive User Experience improvements and feature enhancements to optimize the customer experience</li>
<li>
Increase customers digital engagement with our digital products; including adoption, utilization, ordering and self-serve</li>
<li>
Drive improvements in KPIs (leads, new customers, online orders, conversion rate, AOV)</li>
<li>
Identify new features/functionality and evaluate 3rd party software and tools. Manage the implementation of new features and tools/software.</li>
<li>
Work with cross functional team to meet goals and improve KPIs; including Sales, Marketing, CIS, Ops and 3rd parties</li>
<li>
Utilize analytics to understand customer behavior and to measure the impact of new features</li>
<li>
Work with category management to plan and execute effective online merchandising campaigns. Coordinate these campaigns with other online efforts and our print and sales channels to ensure a consistent marketing message across channels.</li>
<li>
Manage the positioning of products, promotions and content on our websites to increase the conversion % and the average order size</li>
<li>
Identify and execute unique digital marketing campaigns to improve the customer experience and increase sales, such as personalization and affinity marketing.</li>
<li>
Create landing pages to improve the effectiveness of the marketing campaigns</li>
<li>
Responsible for analyzing KPIs and customer behavior and generating reports for key stakeholders. Recommend changes to our websites and merchandising based on the data.</li>
<li>
Understand the voice of customer through market research and usability testing to make recommendations on improvements to the websites</li>
</ul>
