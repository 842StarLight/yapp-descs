<b>About NBCU Portfolio Creative Partnerships:</b><br>
Portfolio Creative Partnerships (PCP) Collaborates, Curates, Creates and Connects all networks and platforms on behalf of advertising sponsors across the NBCUniversal ecosystem. Our integrated marketing solutions span from local to global and typically including 3 or more networks and platforms within the Entertainment, Lifestyle, News, Sports and Hispanic networks, Strategic Partnerships, Universal Pictures, Parks, Comcast and Sky Media.<br>
<br>
<b>Duties and Responsibilities</b><br>
<ul><li>
This position reports to the Senior Vice President of Portfolio Creative Partnerships. The candidate will support and contribute to the development and implementation of integrated ad sales marketing strategies and solutions for brand clients that leverage the NBCUniversal and Sky Media portfolio of local to global properties, products, and strategic partners: multi-network, multi-genre (sports, entertainment, lifestyle, Hispanic), scripted, reality, and multi-platform initiatives. Candidates ideally will have a full-bodied background of agency, brand advertiser, TV and digital experience while providing category-specifc knowledge.
</li></ul>

Candidates will be responsible for the following:<br>
<ul>
<li>
<b>
Proactive Solution Development:</b> Collaborate on developing strategically-led creative ideas and campaign program constructions that combine the NBCUniversal portfolio assets into sellable packages for advertisers</li>
<li>
<b>
Marketing Strategy:</b> Liaising with NBCU strategy teams to further define the value proposition of the partnership. Synthesizing brand advertiser client direction, sales strategy, cultural/market trends, and consumer insights into compelling strategic platforms that focus thinking and spark creative ideation</li>
<li>
<b>
Project Management</b>: Oversee project timelines and work streams ensuring timely delivery of RFP responses and other client deliverables</li>
<li>
<b>
Communication</b>: Cultivate relationships, gaining trust and respect, and facilitate efficient, transparent communication with internal cross-functional partners: sales, strategy, [vertical] network marketing, strategic partner liaisons, design, research, and content creative.</li>
<li>
<b>
Category expertise: </b>Develop expertise in categories that lend themselves to global activation: Studio, Tech, Travel, Finance (global business health, development of proactive solutions, uncovering potential pre-sale leads)</li>
<li>
<b>
Cross-platform dexterity</b>: ability to develop and communicate ideas that stretch across platforms leveraging them creatively and effectively in service of the idea, the client advertiser, and the consumer (linear, digital, mobile, social, VOD, second-screen, etc.)</li>
<li>
<b>

<li>

Portfolio Ambassador:</b> Educates and informs entire cross portfolio team on all updates regarding their assigned verticals: programs, personnel changes, new products, etc.</li>

Executor of Process Changes for the team they oversee</li>

Back up for team’s proposal overflow.</li>
