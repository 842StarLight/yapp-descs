<h2>Contract Technical Sourcing Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">We're seeking a technical sourcing specialist with extensive experience recruiting C++ software developers to work in a fast paced and high performance computational environment.
<br><br>
This is a 6-12 month contract sourcing opportunity with flexibility to work remotely.
<br><br>
Candidates should be familiar with the requirements of highly selective, data intensive, quantitative research-driven organizations, and possess an established recruiting network.
<br><br>
<b>Primary Responsibilities:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Identify potential candidates through web sourcing, social media, networking, candidate referrals and other creative ways to fill the candidate pipeline</li>
<li>Utilize knowledge of multiple recruiting sources and advanced sourcing techniques to identify and engage passive candidates.</li>
<li>Maintain weekly sourcing activity reports.</li>
</ul>
<b>Requirements:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Successful track record recruiting C++ developers and achieving targets, deadlines and recruiting goals.</li>
<li>Excellent research/sourcing skills with ability to dive deep into searches for hard-to-fill requisitions.</li>
<li>Demonstrable history of using creative sourcing approaches, including leveraging strategies that are uniquely your own.</li>
<li>Finance industry recruiting experience an asset.</li>
<li>Excellent communication and interpersonal skills.</li>
<li>Established recruiting contacts in software development and the related academic communities.</li>
</ul>
</div>
