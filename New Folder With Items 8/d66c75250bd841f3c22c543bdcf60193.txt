 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">Townsquare Media Group (Bozeman, MT)
<br><b>Townsquare Media Bozeman includes the well-known brands:</b> XL Country 100.7 – KXLB, The Moose 95.1 – KMMS-FM, KISS-FM 96.7 – KISN, MY 103.5 – KZMY, and KMMS-AM 1450.

Media and Digital Sales Executive
<br><br>
Townsquare Media Bozeman is looking for a dynamic sales professional or someone looking to learn and grow with us that is driven to help businesses reach their goals!
<br><br>
The ideal candidate for this position is someone who is eager to prospect for new local businesses to work with, while holding meetings to uncover how we can help them with our media and digital multi-platform offerings. You will be selling local advertising for our innovative stations and be able to live in beautiful Bozeman, Montana! This is an outdoor enthusiast paradise mixed with the sophistication of one of the fastest growing communities in the country. Townsquare Media provides a great opportunity for the right candidate to engage on the front line of bringing cutting edge media and digital approaches to all size local businesses. Help businesses target the right customers and enjoy fly-fishing, skiing, hiking, and the adventures of Yellowstone National Park in your backyard.
<br><br>
Responsibilities
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Prospect for qualified local and regional businesses; conduct thorough Customer Needs Analysis (C N A); present and close appropriate marketing solution campaigns. These campaigns may include any of Townsquare Media's many assets for clients: Broadcast and Online radio, digital products such as display, streaming, loyalty programs, e-commerce, audience extension and digital marketing services.</li>
<li>Leverage our live event platform through sponsorship and sales campaigns to new and existing clients.</li>
<li>Make a high-level of new business calls, while creating new relationships with local and regional businesses in our area.</li>
<li>Build custom high-quality campaign proposal plans. Work with local and corporate marketing teams to develop campaign support materials.</li>
<li>Responsible to accurately project revenues, meet and exceed monthly budgets for all product lines and overachieve annual budgets.</li>
<li>Enter new customer data and other sales contract details for station clients.</li>
<li>Follow accountabilities set forth by the Director of Sales to help guide you to success achieving monthly sales quotas consistently.</li>
<li>Provide insight and value to executive management to shape the future of our organization.</li>
</ul>
The candidate will need to be a team player who is willing to help others, while being able to work independently to complete tasks by deadline for your clients and for sales management. This candidate must be creative, take time to ask the right questions to prospective businesses and then manage the advertising campaign to the desired results.
<br><br>
Qualifications
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Goal oriented, a strong work ethic and a strong desire to learn.</li>
<li>Previous sales experience preferred, but not required.</li>
<li>Knowledge and experience with digital media.</li>
<li>The successful candidate will be smart, curious, tenacious, entrepreneurial, independent, passionate, and enthusiastic, and work with urgency to meet deadlines.</li>
<li>Accomplished at prospecting and qualifying, while conducting a high volume of in-person new business meetings.</li>
<li>Ability to engage clients quickly and develop rapport, with excellent communication and problem-solving skills.</li>
<li>Associates/Bachelor's business/marketing-related degree or equivalent experience.</li>
</ul>
