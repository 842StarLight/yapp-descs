<h2>Executive Chef</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b><i>Innovate Small Businesses, Inspire Our Communities, Celebrate Local Artisans</i></b></h3>
<p><b>
Career Opportunity:</b></p>
<p>
The <i>Executive Chef </i>of <i>Sawmill Market</i> will:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Lead a team of culinary professionals in creating an unforgettable food experience.</li>
<li>
Implement the creative culinary vision for Sawmill Market.</li>
<li>
Plan and direct food preparation and all Kitchen activities.</li>
<li><i>
Use essential hospitality and customer service standards to inspire and celebrate guests: eye contact, smile, speak first, and engage in polite conversation (if time permits).</i></li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Supervisory Responsibilities:</b> Sous Chef, Line Cooks, Prep Cooks, Dishwashers</p>
<p><b>
Qualifications:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Four-year college degree, Hospitality preferred; or equivalent combination of education and experience.</li>
<li>
5+ years of kitchen management or equivalent combination of education and experience; luxury branded hospitality experience preferred.</li>
<li>
New Mexico Food Handler Certification and ServSafe Manager Certification.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Essential Functions &amp; Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Create and implement menus that capture the spirit of the Sawmill Market artisanal food hall experience.</li>
<li>
Plan and direct food preparation and culinary activities, including estimates of food requirements and food/labor costs.</li>
<li>
Perform management responsibilities according to policies and applicable laws, including recruitment, training, assigning work, appraising performance, and addressing complaints and resolving problems.</li>
<li>
Work closely with the events team to plan and execute all private events.</li>
<li>
Uphold company food safety, food handling, cleanliness, and sanitation requirements to ensure the health and safety of our guests and staff.</li>
<li>
Arrange for equipment purchases and repairs.</li>
<li>
Rectify arising problems or complaints.</li>
<li>
Give prepared plates the “final touch”.</li>
<li>
Maintain a positive and professional approach with coworkers and customers.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Knowledge/Skills/Abilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
<b>
Administration &amp; Management:</b> Knowledge of financials and operations; detail-oriented and highly organized.</li>
<li>
<b>
Visionary Leadership: </b>Actively listens, identifies plan of action; communicates ideas clearly and inspires action. Willingness to live and breathe our culture and to hold oneself to the highest standards.</li>
<li>
<b>
Position &amp; People Passion</b>: Passion for the service industry. Desire to make people happy, both guests and co-workers. Team player who is willing to come in early, stay late, and jump in wherever and whenever needed.</li>
<li>
<b>
Personalized Service:</b> Resolves issues with guest and coworker satisfaction in mind; multi-tasks with ease; strong interpersonal skills including keeping emotions under control; open to others’ ideas.</li>
<li>
<b>
Communication Skills:</b> Well-spoken, personable, outgoing, listens and gets clarification; responds well to questions; great communicator.</li>
<li>
<b>
Office Sensibility:</b> Ability to manage records, accomplish tasks as directed; cash handling skills.</li>
<li>
<b>
Technology:</b> Tech savvy with computers and software - word processing, PMS, point of sale.</li>
<li>
<b>
Image:</b> Ability to maintain professional image standards, including any uniform requirements.</li>
</ul>
