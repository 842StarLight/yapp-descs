<b>Our Ideal Leasing Consultant:</b><br>
What does a Mark-Taylor Leasing Consultant look like? You're passionate about the multi-family industry and excited to learn about the business from the ground-up! You have awesome communication skills, with a knack for presenting in front of others. You are a people-person who loves maintaining and developing relationships. You are service-oriented, always going above and beyond for those you serve.
<br><br>
<b>The Work You Do:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Greet prospects and qualify by covering all criteria set forth by Mark-Taylor Residential</li>
<li>Demonstrates community and apartment and/or model by actively touring the community and amenities and apply product knowledge to future residents needs by communicating the features and benefits; close the sale.</li>
<li>Guide future resident through the complete application, process application credit/criminal. Submit processed applications to the Community Manager for approval. Follow up with future resident regarding status.</li>
<li>Immediately follow up on prospects that did not lease and attempt to close the sale again. If unable to help prospect, refer them to sister communities that meet prospects needs.</li>
<li>Receive all telephone calls and in-person visits. Listen to resident requests, concerns and comments.</li>
<li>Inspect models and available "market ready" apartment homes and communicate related service needs to the Manager and/or Service Manager.</li>
<li>Complete maintenance Service Requests and inform the service team. Answer questions for residents about community, repairs, rent, policies etc. Follow up on a timely basis if unable to respond to residents on all matters.</li>
<li>Secure new residents' signature(s) on appropriate paperwork prior to move in and ensure apartment home is ready for resident to move in on agreed date.</li>
<li>Represent the company in a professional manner at all times.</li>
<li>Maintain resident files, accept rental payments, and actively manage apartment availability/pricing.</li>
<li>Complete paperwork and input information in Management Software system accurately and on a timely basis.</li>
<li>Contribute to cleanliness and curb appeal of the community on continuing basis.</li>
<li>Assist in planning and participating in resident functions as directed by the Community Manager.</li>
<li>Assist management team with other tasks as required.</li>
</ul>
<b>The Skills You Bring:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>At least 1 year of customer service experience in a related industry</li>
<li>College degree or attending college classes, preferred</li>
<li>Strong problem-solving abilities</li>
<li>Significant experience with conflict-resolution</li>
<li>Excellent verbal and written communication skills</li>
<li>Detail-oriented and proactive with the ability to pick up on new concepts quickly</li>
</ul>
