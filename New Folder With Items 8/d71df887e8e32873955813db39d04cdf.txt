<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Position Overview
</b></h2>
<p></p>At PNC, our people are our greatest differentiator and competitive advantage in the markets we serve. We are all united in delivering the best experience for our customers. As a Private Equity Associate within PNC Bank, you will be based in Pittsburgh, PA
<br>
<br>
PNC Riverarch Capital is seeking to hire an Associate to join its team. The Associate will work closely with the firm’s other investment professionals and will be involved in all aspects of the investment and portfolio management process. An Associate’s primary roles include:
 <br>
<br>
Deal screening and evaluation
 <br>
Financial modeling and analysis
 <br>
Managing third-party due diligence providers
 <br>
Securing deal financing from third-party lenders
 <br>
Supporting transaction negotiations
<br>
<br>
Importantly, we expect an Associate’s role to expand over time as they demonstrate an ability to take on more responsibility.
<br>
<br>
PNC Riverarch Capital, the private equity affiliate of PNC Financial Services Group, is focused on making control investments in privately held middle-market companies headquartered in North America. The firm seeks well-positioned companies where it can invest $10 million to $100 million of equity capital, primarily as a control investor in leveraged and management buyouts. The firm invests across a variety of industry sectors, including outsourced services, niche manufacturing, and value-added distribution. PNC’s private equity program was started in 1982 and has provided over $1.5 billion of equity capital to more than 100 companies. We are investing from a committed capital pool of $650 million, a substantial majority of which remains uninvested and available.

<p><b>Job Description
</b></p>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Performs analytical and operational activities related to proposed deals and existing investments under the direction of senior staff professionals. Participates in the identification, origination, analysis, closing and monitoring of investment transactions.
</li>
<li>Participates in the marketing effort directed toward sources of investments in support of senior staff as needed. May develop sources and perform sales calls.
</li>
<li>Performs in-depth analyses of prospective new investments including historical/projected financial results, industry research and risk factors. Determines acceptability of potential investments in conjunction with team.
</li>
<li>Evaluates various opportunities for portfolio companies including evaluation of potential acquisitions, analysis of various comparable companies in contemplation of public offering and determination of fair market value of companies contemplating recapitalization and other exit scenario analysis as required.
</li>
<li>Evaluates various existing opportunities for portfolio companies. Participates in the review of legal documentation prior to and following the transaction closing in conjunction with senior staff. Monitors compliance issues after closing. Works with internal finance staff to track portfolio performance, valuation and reporting.
</li>
</ul>
