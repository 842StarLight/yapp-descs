<h2>Campaign Lead, Programmatic</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Hulu is the leading premium streaming service offering live and on-demand TV and movies, with and without commercials, both in and outside the home. Operating at the intersection of entertainment and technology, Hulu has a unique opportunity to be the number one choice for TV. We captivate and connect viewers with the stories they love, and we’re looking for people who are passionate about redefining TV through innovation, unconventional thinking, and embracing fun. Join us and see what Hulugan life is all about.</p>
<p><b>
SUMMARY</b></p>
<p>
Hulu’s Automated Operations team is seeking a Campaign Lead who will be an extraordinary addition to our Automated Operations team. In this role, you will report into The Manager of Automated Ops and will work closely with the ATV Ad Sales and other sales support teams at Hulu.</p>
<p>
The primary responsibility for the Campaign Lead is to ensure accurate campaign setup, 100% delivery and invoicing of Hulu’s most high profile and complex advertising campaigns. Additionally, you will be providing outstanding service to both an Account Executive and our clients! The right person for this role is detail orientated, thrives in a dynamic and fast-paced collaborative environment, wants an opportunity to make a significant impact and jumps at the opportunity to tackle new challenges!</p>
<p><b>
WHAT YOU'LL DO</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Support high profile and complex programmatic video ad campaigns</li>
<li>
Work with outstanding advertising customers, the ad agencies that represent them, The Demand Side Platforms that they transact through, and internal cross-functional groups</li>
<li>
Help ensure client’s programmatic ad specs are met and communicate out changes to our client’s and their DSPs</li>
<li>
Ensure accurate campaign set up between Hulu’s ad server and our SSP</li>
<li>
Lead client communication related to our programmatic offering and optimizations around that.</li>
<li>
Troubleshoot campaign performance by investigating ad server bugs, third party discrepancies and under-delivery issues</li>
<li>
Optimize for campaign delivery by pulling campaign reporting, reviewing delivery insights and providing recommendations to our clients</li>
<li>
Provide after-hours support for any client-related emergencies, as necessary</li>
<li>
Become an authority in our SSP and the programmatic advertising ecosystem</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
WHAT TO BRING</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
At least 1-2 years of digital advertising and online media experience, with strong emphasis in client communication</li>
<li>
1 year of digital ad trafficking experience</li>
<li>
Experience within the digital online ecosystem, including familiarity with third-party ad servers such as DCM, Sizmek, Innovid, etc., as well as ad serving technologies such as VAST and VPAID</li>
<li>
Professional communication skills, with internal and external customers. Ability to articulate thoughts and concepts fluidly.</li>
<li>
Ability to quickly build rapport and professional relationships</li>
<li>
Ability to think critically; Has analytical and problem-solving skills</li>
<li>
Strong proficiency with Microsoft Excel and PowerPoint; ability to quickly learn productivity software application tools</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
NICE-TO-HAVES</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Strong interest in the digital media, advertising, and emerging technology industries</li>
<li>
Bachelor’s degree with a focus in media, advertising or other meaningful experience</li>
<li>
Familiar with all things tech, including, consumer websites, mobile/tablet, over-the-top and connected TV platforms, as well as video game devices</li>
<li>
Experience working with ad sales teams, creative production studios, and/or marketing departments</li>
<li>
Exposure to yield management and inventory planning</li>
<li>
Familiarity with Advertising Research and Verification vendors like Nielsen, ComScore, Moat, Integral Ad Science, Millard Brown, etc.</li>
<li>
Familiarity with the Programmatic advertising landscape</li>
</ul>
