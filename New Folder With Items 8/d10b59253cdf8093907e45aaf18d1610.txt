<h2>Marketing Assistant/Administrative Assistant</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>Founded in 1969, Kidder Mathews (www.kidder.com) is the largest, independent commercial real estate firm on the West Coast, with more than 800 real estate professionals and staff in 22 offices in Washington, Oregon, California, Nevada, and Arizona. We offer a complete range of brokerage, property management, appraisal, consulting, and debt &amp; equity finance services for all property types. The firm performs over $9 billion om transactions, manages over 60 million square feet of space, and conducts of 1.600 appraisals annually.<br>
<br>
Kidder Mathews is an expanding, dynamic, vibrant and entrepreneurial firm and has been consistently recognized as a "best place to work" in multiple markets on the West Coast.<br>
<br>
Our Brokerage Division is Kidder Mathews largest division. This position supports our Brokerage team by providing administrative, marketing and facilities support. Our compensation is competitive with a full suite of benefits offered. In addition, we offer a fun work environment where getting to know one another and off-site and in-office gatherings are the norm.<br>
</div>

<p>Job Summary: (Overall purpose and objective of the job)</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
The Marketing Assistant/Administrative Assistant works as part of a team that supports the firm's commercial real estate brokers with their daily administrative needs, graphics work, property marketing, maps and aerial imagery, and neighborhood research.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
Essential Functions: (The reason this job exists is to perform these functions. These functions are performed regularly, seriously impacts other jobs, and there are a limited number of employees to whom performance of the function can be distributed).</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<ul>
<li> Work well and maintain good relationships with coworkers in a close-knit team environment to support a fast-paced, high profile sales force</li>
<li> Provide high level customer service and an overall can do, service oriented attitude</li>
<li> Use InDesign templates within various software applications to produce and assemble marketing presentations, proposals, flyer, e-mail flyers, newsletters, postcards, etc.</li>
<li> Use Photoshop to edit and enhance property photographs and other images</li>
<li> Use various mapping and demographic software applications to run reports and create customer maps, aerials and presentations</li>
<li> Prepare informational packages and maps for property tours</li>
<li> Provide administrative support for legal documents, proposals, invoices, executive summaries and other long and short documents</li>
<li> Compose and/or transcribe various correspondence, some of which may be highly confidential</li>
<li> May be required to operate and troubleshoot office equipment like fax machine, postage meter, etc.</li>
<li> Perform research, special projects, record keeping and other miscellaneous duties as needed for the office</li>
<li> Cross-train on front desk responsibilities to act as back up to the Administrative Assistant when they are on break or out of the office</li>
</ul>
