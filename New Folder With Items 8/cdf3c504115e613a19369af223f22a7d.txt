<h2>Sales Associate, Financial Services</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">Cuebiq is a Location Data and Intelligence company that provides actionable insights to help clients understand the offline consumer journey. In addition to its market leading business in the Advertising and Marketing realm, Cuebiq has a rapidly growing presence in Financial Services, Real Estate, Government and Transportation.
<br><br>
Cuebiq is headquartered in New York with offices in San Francisco, Chicago, Italy, and China.
<br><br>
The company is backed by Goldman Sachs, Nasdaq, DRW and Balyasny.

<b>About the Role:</b><br>
Cuebiq is looking for an exceptional sales person to join its Financial Services team in NYC. The individual will have a track record of proven success selling into Asset Managers (Hedge Funds and/or Private Equity) with bonus points for candidates that have experience in subscription sales of alternative data products. Entrepreneurial, get-it-done attitude is a must as well as an innate ability to build long-term relationships. This person works effectively with little hand holding, but is also looking for a fast-paced team environment with a good sense of humor.
<br><br>
<b>Responsibilities:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Evangelize Cuebiq as an expert on alternative data and the geolocation data landscape</li>
<li>Take over existing pipeline opportunities</li>
<li>Build new pipeline through outbound prospecting efforts</li>
<li>Engage daily with sophisticated clients that ask difficult questions (CIOs, PMs, Analysts)</li>
<li>Manage the entire subscription sales cycle (lead generation, qualification, testing/evaluation, negotiation, contract closure, relationship management)</li>
<li>Execute against a quarterly quota</li>
<li>Tailor your pitch, collateral and proposals to fulfill a variety of client types (i.e. Quants, Fundamental Investors, Channel Partners, Private Equity, Real Estate, etc)</li>
<li>Collaborate with Product Management, Marketing and Quantitative Research teams to ensure new and existing client needs are being met</li>
<li>Stay up to speed on equity market dynamics and how Cuebiq’s offering can provide insight to company performance</li>
<li>Maintain client activity records in SalesForce.</li>
</ul>
<b>Experience:</b><br>
