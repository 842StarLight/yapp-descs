<h2>Sr Account Executive</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div><div>
<p>The core mission and goal of the Sr Account Executive is to make an impact as an individual contributor to maximize the company’s reputation and revenue generation. With the assigned territory of: ME, DC, TN, NY, PA, VA, NJ, KY and WV, responsible for building and executing on the territory plan; effectively building a pipeline of $8M+ in sales opportunities; and closing on at least $2.0M in new sales on an annual basis. The ideal and strongest candidate has superior interpersonal, organization, and self-starter skills and in addition must be resilient, results-driven, and be an exceptional team player.</p>
<p>
Note: This role is salary plus a competitive and generous commission plan. To fully take advantage of the potential this person must be interested in working in a highly coordinated, collaborative, focused organization, and be prepared for client on-site engagements, sales conventions and demonstrations, and annual company user conferences.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
Responsibilities and Competencies:</p>
<ul>
<li>Prospect and secure initial discussions with new accounts</li>
<li>Thorough communication with generated leads through company marketing activities</li>
<li>Qualify opportunities and build sales pipeline</li>
<li>Present and demonstrate strategic solutions to potential clients in a knowledgeable and attractive way</li>
<li>Know accurate product capabilities, enhancements and road maps</li>
<li>Engage in marketing and conference planning activities</li>
<li>Communicate with product team on prospect/market needs and trends in territory</li>
<li>Update and maintain accuracy of Salesforce information</li>
<li>Powerful communication skills; verbal, written and presentation capabilities with both clients and leadership</li>
<li>Demonstrate negotiation skills in developing, closing and managing deals</li>
<li>Strong demonstration of intellect, strategic insights, drive, executive presence and sales acumen</li>
<li>Work multiple campaigns and manage multiple deliverables at a time</li>
</ul>
<p>Education and Experience:</p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree in Business or related discipline desired</li>
<li>Strong proven track record exceeding sales quota by using a relationship and consultative approach</li>
<li>Minimum 5-8 years software sales experience</li>
<li>3+ years’ experience with Higher Ed software or educational software</li>
<li>Strong computer literacy, including Salesforce and MS Office Suite experience</li>
</ul>
