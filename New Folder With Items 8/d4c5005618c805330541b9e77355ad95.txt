<h2>Assistant Community Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p>CAPREIT, a nationally known property management company that owns and manages multifamily housing communities across the U.S. has an opening for an Assistant Community Manager for the Valle Vista Armes apartment community located in Greenwood, Indiana. We are seeking a high energy professional who must excel in sales, enjoy working with people, and be detail oriented. The successful candidate will work closely with and support the Community Manager.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>Responsibilities include, but not limited to:</p>
<ul>
<li>Provides a high level of service to residents while promoting great resident relations.</li>
<li>Assists the community manager in meeting and exceeding the budget by achieving occupancy and rental rates goal, and controlling expenses.</li>
<li>Collects rent promptly and conducts unlawful detainer procedures, as necessary.</li>
<li>Shows and leases apartments to respective residents. Assists with inspections during check-in and check-out.</li>
<li>Assists in accurate preparation and processing of all leasing forms, maintenance orders, rent collection, bookkeeping, and company reporting.</li>
<li>Works with the service team to ensure the property is well-maintained and secure.</li>
</ul>
<p>Requirements:</p>
<ul>
<li>At least two years of experience in property management and sales is required.</li>
<li>Excellent customer service skills and strong communications skills.</li>
<li>The successful candidate must be very organized, detail oriented, self-motivated, outgoing, energetic, professional, and customer focused.</li>
<li>Willing to work a full-time schedule that may include weekends, evenings, holidays and overtime.</li>
<li>Must have a working knowledge of Microsoft Office and the Internet; ability to learn specialized property management software.</li>
<li>Experience with Realpage and Onesite is a huge asset!</li>
<li>Ability to work every other Saturday.</li>
</ul>
