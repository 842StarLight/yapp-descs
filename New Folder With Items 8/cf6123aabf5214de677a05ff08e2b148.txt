<h2>Senior Account Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Job Summary</b></p>
<p><b>Location: </b>San Francisco Bay Area (Preferred), Chicago, Sacramento, Denver, Dallas, Salt Lake City, Toronto</p>
<p>MondayCall is looking for team-members to join a fast growing, highly collaborative Salesforce consulting company. MondayCall is headquartered in San Francisco and is looking for sales people in California as well as in areas that MondayCall is planning to expand in the next year. The Senior Account Manager / Executive is a role that requires experience in IT environments with Salesforce knowledge highly recommended. Senior Account Managers must be able to understand business requirements, conduct high level solutioning and estimation of projects with the assistance of technical team-members as needed. Many successful MondayCall sales executives have experience as former Salesforce administrators, managers, project managers and developers, not just in sales.</p>
<p><b>Responsibilities and Duties</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Accurately forecast and deliver on sales goals</li>
<li>Develop and maintain strong working relationships with salesforce.com’s and other partner’s Account Executives and sales teams</li>
<li>Identify business requirements and craft a consulting solution to meet business needs</li>
<li>Manage all phases of the business development cycles to successfully close (including proposal development, implementation estimation, scope construction, and client presentations)</li>
<li>Maintain and nurture client relationships to ensure additional business</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Qualifications and Skills</b></p>
<ul>
<li>BA/BS or equivalent; MBA is a plus</li>
<li>5+ years experience in software, SaaS or Services sales organizations</li>
<li>Hands on experience with Salesforce.com. Salesforce.com certification not required but a significant plus.</li>
<li>Proven track record of successful solutions-oriented services sales</li>
<li>Superior communication skills (both for internal and external clients), very strong leadership qualities, and team oriented business values are essential</li>
<li>Strong relationship building skills with customers and partners and delivery team</li>
<li>Ability to work in an entrepreneurial environment</li>
</ul>
