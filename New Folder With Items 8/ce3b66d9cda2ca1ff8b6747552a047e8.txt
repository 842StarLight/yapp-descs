<h2>Email & Social Marketing Analyst</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Major Position Responsibilities and Accountabilities</b>:</p>
<ul>
<li>Lead email strategy and execute all production processes with the goal to grow profitable sales, control discounts, increase the file, and grow AOS.</li>
<li>Collaborate with all relevant parties to ensure all email elements are aligned with brand strategies.</li>
<li>Manage the email calendar to ensure all plans are created, updated, and scheduled.</li>
<li>Manage the email service provider on day-to-day email marketing initiatives including regular communication and feedback on maintenance and hygiene of databases, on-going use of email application software, and ad hoc projects as assigned.</li>
<li>Serve as the liaison to site merchandising, product buyers, and brand designers to assist in product selection and creative design and ensure that all emails adhere to branding guidelines.</li>
<li>Conduct extensive testing and analysis of email marketing efforts including delivery time, creative layout, promotional offers, subject line testing, and segmentation performance to develop best practices for ongoing email campaigns.</li>
<li>Partner with vendor to monitor deliverability and overall account reputation to ensure messages are being delivered to the inbox.</li>
<li>Evaluate new email approaches, competitive benchmarking, best practices, new technology, and vendor capabilities to identify opportunities to improve email relevance, customer engagement, and sales.</li>
<li>Manage file of marketable email addresses. Develop acquisition and segmentation strategies to optimize email performance.</li>
<li>Conduct quality check tests to ensure links, images, subject lines, and lists function properly.</li>
<li>Stay current with email best practices, deliverability and data hygiene.</li>
<li>Prepare weekly and monthly reports, including circulation data, budget projections and sales results.</li>
<li>Manage Social Media programs, including Facebook, Instagram, Pinterest, and YouTube.</li>
<li>Strategize, execute, and maintain a content creation plan for all social platforms.</li>
<li>Partner with coordinator and digital marketers to build social media campaigns and events.</li>
<li>Manage and support paid social campaigns with third-party vendors.</li>
<li>Monitor social metrics and report on weekly, monthly, quarterly basis.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Required education, skills, abilities, and experience</b>:</p>
<ul>
<li>S. or B.A. Business, Marketing or related field.</li>
<li>3+ years of experience in email marketing or 5+ years of digital marketing experience.</li>
<li>Excellent writing and communication skills.</li>
<li>Ability to create email content with a compelling value proposition.</li>
<li>Proficient in the mechanics of email creation.</li>
<li>Experience working with Email Service Providers (ESPs)</li>
<li>PC Skills - Microsoft Office - all applications. Proficiency in Microsoft Excel required, Database experience is a plus.</li>
<li>Strong analytical skills.</li>
<li>Excellent reasoning and problem-solving skills.</li>
<li>High attention to detail and strong organizational skills.</li>
<li>Excellent team player with strong project management skills.</li>
</ul>
</div></div>
