<p>
The mission of Wells Fargo <b>Marketing</b> is to build the best possible brand, experiences, and engagement with our customers and stakeholders that reflect our commitment to our customers and their needs and to driving key business and stakeholder outcomes. In support of this mission, Marketing is responsible for developing integrated marketing strategies across Wells Fargo’s business lines and geographies; leading brand strategy; leveraging deep insights and analytics about consumers and businesses; executing customer messaging across all channels and touchpoints; and building out capabilities that move toward real-time, always-on, personalized and compelling messaging and digital experiences.</p>
<p>
Within Marketing, this role is part of the <b>Marketing Strategic Capabilities &amp; Insights (MSCI)</b> organization. MSCI’s role is to lead the creation of world-class marketing technologies, social capabilities &amp; platforms, digital customer experiences and insights and analytics to enable industry leading revenue growth, scale, and efficiency for Wells Fargo.</p>
<p>
Specifically this position reports to <b>Marketing Measurement</b>, which is a critical group within MSCI. The team brings accountability to the Marketing organization by empirically estimating the incremental contributions of Marketing to the business and the performance of each campaign and marketing initiatives. Its charter also includes providing guidance to the organization as to the optimal levels of program investments and allocations across businesses, marketing channels, sales channels, audiences and time to achieve forecast sales goals. To that end, the team is charged with developing and maintaining all the Marketing Mix Modeling, Multi-touch Attribution, Test and Control Models, Audience, Targeting Models, Opportunity Sizing, Sales Planning and Forecasting as well as Numerical Optimization Tools that provide the backbone of past Marketing performance measurements and future investment optimizations. All while reducing risk and driving operational efficiencies.<br>
<br>
KEY RESPONSIBILITIES INCLUDE:</p>
<ul>
<li>Build, maintain and evolve geographic level marketing mix models to estimate the causal impacts of marketing and non-marketing drivers on Wells Fargo's business outcomes in the short and long term</li>
<li>Develop a project plan and timeline to support modeling related activities, such as data preparation and validation, model development and validation, model documentation, model performance tracking, and work with model governance to obtain necessary approvals from Corporate Model Risk and Legal and Compliance</li>
<li>Measure the long-term impacts on customer behavior through evaluation of the relationship marketing spend has with Brand Equity and how Brand Equity influences business outcomes</li>
<li>Develop a robust and repeatable data preparation process that incorporates the automation of input ETL, and creates an auditable trail, including data development documentation</li>
<li>Ability to extract critical insights to identify growth opportunities and effectively communicate outcomes and recommendations</li>
<li>Develop collaborative partnerships and leverage strong influencing skills to communicate insights and recommendations</li>
<li>Engage with marketing partners to establish mutual objectives, priorities, and measures of success</li>
<li>Provide analytical thought leadership and bring cutting edge statistical knowledge to solve complex marketing problems</li>
<li>Lead effective data analysis that may suggest risk or opportunities for Wells Fargo</li>
<li>Provide ongoing support for existing models’ key performance metric reporting</li>
</ul>
