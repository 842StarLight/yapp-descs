<h2>Cost Engineer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div></div>
<div>The Cost Engineer/Estimator supports the Interconnect Operations &amp; Marketing team and is accountable for the complete estimating life‐cycle: Request for Quotation (RFQ) to Quote, to Marketing &amp; Sales. This position will primarily focus on custom products for Automotive and Industrial products on a global basis.<h6 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Reports To:</b></h6>
General Manager, Interconnect<h6 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Duties and Responsibilities:</b></h6>
<ul>
<li>
Define Cost Estimate Scope: Define the project scope, time period, phases to be estimated, communication channels, and establish any boundaries for estimates or project requirements.</li>
<li>
Maintain and update DRL (RFQ forms).</li>
<li>
Define assumptions or constraints: Identify any schedule or budget constraints, and how those will be resolved.</li>
<li>
Establish and update a local and global database of quotes and RFQs .</li>
<li>
Build up a software system to electronically calculate new projects and therefore atomizes the quoting and cost break down procedure.</li>
<li>
Develop Cost Element Structure: Identify everything defined in the scope to be costed.</li>
<li>
Collect and Monitor Data in the System to verify STD cost equals calculations and adjust if needed.</li>
<li>
Manage risks, identify (if necessary) contingency plan, control changes and communication. Measure and report on progress.</li>
<li>
Develop Cost Estimating Relationships: Use cost data to develop formulas/equations entered into the cost model.</li>
<li>
Work close with advanced purchasing and purchasing to drive the right costs and supplier portfolio</li>
<li>
Customer Review: Ensure that all expected costs have been properly presented to Customer for Review/Approval/Acceptance of the estimate if needed face to face.</li>
<li>
Maintain Smartsheet and follow up with Sales on the status.</li>
<li>
Assist in preparing cost data for proposals and contract negotiations, internally with the technical and operational team.</li>
<li>
Coordinate the needed production concept for best price in NA or China that meets deadlines and customer requirements.</li>
<li>
Establish quote priorities based on evaluation and analysis of overall opportunities.</li>
<li>
Verify processes needed for production to decide if this can be external or if there is a need for integration internally.</li>
<li>
Monitor P&amp;L quarterly with Operations team.</li>
<li>
Performs performance monitoring and control functions for specific applications, including the consistent use of applicable procedures.</li>
</ul>
<h6 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Job Requirements:</b></h6>
<ul>
<li>
Bachelor's Degree is required; PMP / PRINCE2 Certification is a plus</li>
<li>
Minimum of two (2) years experience in the Connectors industry with Marketing or Sales experience.</li>
<li>
Good interpersonal skills and ability to work effectively with customers, suppliers, manufacturing personnel, and development engineers.</li>
<li>
Must have strong written and verbal communication skills and have profound attention to detail and perform with high accuracy.</li>
<li>
Must be able to handle multiple projects and possess strong organizational and analytical skills.</li>
<li>
Demonstrate leadership and integrity to bring all resources to bear to achieve project success in the committed timeframe while openly acknowledging difficulties and roadblocks.</li>
<li>
Willing to travel in order to support Marketing and Sales in front of the customer.</li>
<li>
Intermediate level of proficiency in MS Excel and other MS Office programs.</li>
<li>
Functional knowledge of accounting, financial accounting.</li>
<li>
Operating knowledge and experience of SolidWorks, design software and engineering tools.</li>
<li>
Strong customer service orientation.</li>
<li>
Positive leadership style and ability to create a high morale manufacturing environment.</li>
<li>
Autonomous, self‐starter. Holds self personally accountable.</li>
</ul>
