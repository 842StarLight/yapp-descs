<p><b>Who We Are</b></p>
<p>
Twitter has built one of the fastest growing advertising businesses in the world and we’re looking for an extraordinary PM leader to join our talented team.
</p>
<p><b>What you’ll do</b></p>
<p>
You will be the internal champion for your product area and will develop and articulate your vision for these products to internal and external stakeholders.</p>
<ul>
<li>
Convey your product vision through bold product roadmaps, leading a cross-functional team of engineering, product, research, design and sales teams to deliver products that drive Outcomes advertisers care about, starting with Performance outcomes</li>
<li>
Develop empathy for the needs of both external and internal customers, working with research and talking to customers directly to inform your view</li>
<li>
Engage with senior stakeholders across the business to convey your vision and manage expectations</li>
<li>
Drive execution with cross functional teams (especially engineering, design, data science and research) to deliver on your vision and roadmap</li>
<li>
Collaborate with your fellow product managers to deliver a holistic set of solutions across the entire product stack that align with the advertisers’ needs</li>
<li>
Make, build, buy, or partner recommendations for various aspects of the product solution.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Who you are</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
4+ years of product management experience</li>
<li>
Computer science or engineering background strongly preferred</li>
<li>
Ability to think strategically and craft a compelling product vision.</li>
<li>
Scrappy, “impact-focused” attitude</li>
<li>
Experience driving impact through cross-functional leadership.</li>
<li>
Exceptional written and verbal communication skills.</li>
<li>
Strong analytical abilities</li>
</ul>
