<h2>Marketing Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>Mt. Bachelor's Marketing Manager oversees the company's advertising, social media, graphic design, content creation, website and digital marketing initiatives. With guidance from the Director of Marketing and Communications, this position manages the Visual Design Coordinator, the Marketing Coordinator and the Content Coordinator in a manner that stimulates professional development while advancing Mt. Bachelor's mission, goals and objectives. This is a fast-paced and demanding position - ideal for someone who has their marketing chops, likes to manage people, brings new ideas to the table, is able to work outside of a 9-5 day, and who wants to work hard in the office and play hard outside. The successful candidate will be passionate and knowledgeable about snow sports, mountain biking, rafting, marketing strategy, social media and will have a minimum of 5 years of experience in marketing.</p>
<p><b>
Essential Functions/Major Responsibilities:</b></p>
<p><i>
Staff Management</i></p>
<ul>
<li>Evaluate performance, coach for improvement and facilitate professional development for direct-reports</li>
<li>Jointly develop action plans with direct-reports to advance sales, marketing and communication objectives</li>
<li>Manage project timelines and track progress</li>
</ul>
<p><i>Content, Brand and Visual Design Management</i></p>
<ul>
<li>Maintain and enforce company brand look and messaging across all platforms and locations</li>
<li>Act as primary liaison with creative design agency to develop and implement new, creative ideas that amplify the company's brand position and elevate the brand reputation</li>
</ul>
<p><i>Paid Advertising Management</i></p>
<ul>
<li>Act as primary liaison with media planning/buying agency to develop and implement seasonal advertising plans that amplify key messages and drive revenue growth</li>
<li>Solicit and evaluate local advertising partnerships and opportunities to advance local initiatives</li>
</ul>
<p><i>Digital Platform Management</i></p>
<ul>
<li>Manage all aspects of email marketing plan; develop email marketing strategy and give content direction/support to the Marketing Coordinator to deploy email content</li>
<li>Manage all aspects of company websites; update sites as needed with assistance from the Marketing Coordinator; work with POWDR team to advance website initiatives</li>
</ul>
<p><i>Social Media Management</i></p>
<ul>
<li>Manage all social media platforms; research and implement current best-practices</li>
<li>Actively respond to customer comments and questions using Mt. Bachelor's brand voice</li>
</ul>
</div>

<p><b>Specific Job Skills:</b></p>
<p>
License/Certifications- Valid driver's license required</p>
<p>
Technical-</p>
<ul>
<li>Sales, marketing and reservations systems</li>
<li>Deep understanding of all Creative Cloud design apps (most importantly InDesign, Illustrator, and Photoshop)</li>
<li>Intermediate knowledge of Premiere Pro and After Effects</li>
<li>Strong grasp of resort/hospitality business and financial models/metrics</li>
<li>HTML and website content management</li>
<li>Creative and descriptive writing</li>
<li>Photo and video art direction</li>
</ul>
<p>Physical Capabilities-</p>
<ul>
<li>Ability to sit for up to 8 hours</li>
<li>Must be able to lift and carry items up to 25 lbs.</li>
<li>Ability to stand for 4 hours consecutively</li>
<li>Skiing/snowboarding at an advanced level</li>
<li>Ability to work outdoors in extreme heat or cold</li>
</ul>
<p>Additional-</p>
<ul>
<li>Positive "can do" attitude and a collaborative leadership style</li>
</ul>
<div>

<p>
Minimum education required- Four-year college degree, preferably with a marketing, advertising or communications major</p>
<p>
Minimum time in related position- At least 5 years in a marketing, sales or advertising position with demonstrable experience managing staff</p>
