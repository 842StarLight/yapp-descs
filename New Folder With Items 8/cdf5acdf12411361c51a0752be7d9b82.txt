<h2>Implementation Project Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Job Title: </b>Implementation Project Manager</p>
<p><b>
Position Summary: </b>Perspectium is looking for a top-tier Implementation Project Manager professional with excellent judgement and personal integrity. The position manages all customer implementations worldwide. Organization, communication and multi-tasking are essential to be successful in this role. As an Implementation Project Manager at Perspectium, you put technology, problem solving, and project management skills to work in a highly consultative role. The right individual understands the importance of solving complex business problems, yet recognizes the value in a balanced approach which emphasizes the success of the customer relationship. This role is focused on taking new customers from contract signing through a complete adoption of the Perspectium solution. In this role, you'll get to work closely with all Perspectium teams – from Sales to Product to Engineering, and more - to enhance and accelerate the satisfaction and growth of our user base. Analytic rigor, strategic thinking, and outstanding interpersonal skills come together to characterize what we're looking for in our Implementation Project Manager. You're a great team player that's excited to help Perspectium scale to become the category leader in integration technology solutions.</p>
<p><b>
Company Overview</b>:
</p>
<p>Perspectium offers comprehensive systems and application integration solutions to the enterprise. We solve process and data integration, monitoring, and replication problems with the lowest impact and highest data throughput – all the while retaining control of the data with the data owner. Architected from the ground up with high availability and throughput in mind, Perspectium solutions power the most intricate integrations of some of the world's largest enterprises.</p>
<p>
Perspectium is solving enterprise integration challenges for some of the most data-reliant businesses in the world … and we're growing fast. To fuel that growth, we are looking for the best and brightest in the world to join our company. We offer highly competitive benefits and compensation and are growing rapidly.</p>
<p><b>
Essential Duties and Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Successfully deploy the Perspectium solutions with new customers, from contract signing to full adoption through go-live</li>
<li>
Fully understand customer processes, workflow and requirements</li>
<li>
Identify and solve complex process and technology-related problems during the roll-out process through methodologies that rely on best practice as much as creative thinking</li>
<li>
Clearly communicate the status of all projects, issues with implementation, and solutions</li>
<li>
Manage customer and internal resources to drive toward project milestones and overall project execution</li>
<li>
Engage with a variety of stakeholders within each customer to ensure buy-in at all levels within the organization</li>
<li>
Service as an ambassador of the Perspectium brand in all customer interactions, maintaining the highest level of professionalism and world class service our customers have come to expect</li>
<li>
Participate in cross-functional teams, acting as an advocate for the user, to ensure customer needs are met through a more powerful Perspectium solution</li>
<li>
Communicate functionality and technical concepts to technical users</li>
<li>
Work to meet deadlines in a small entrepreneurial team</li>
<li>
Manages a team of implementation specialists that are responsible for the implementation of technical solutions.</li>
<li>
Oversees and supports multiple implementation projects.</li>
<li>
Assess client's product demands, develops project plans and ensures that implementations are completed on time, within budget, and meet client expectations.</li>
<li>
vCalendar management and fluidity to adapt to changes in scheduling and priority</li>
</ul>
