<h2>Electronic Security Technician</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Overview
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div>Looking to join a rapidly growing company that offers diverse work and is committed to your safety? Now is a great time to apply for an Electronic Security Technician position at Pillar Innovations. Here at Pillar, we are a service company committed to offering a diverse product portfolio to fulfill the needs of energy, mining, environmental, manufacturing, pharmaceuticals, wireless communications, and food &amp; beverage sectors.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
Pillar is currently on the lookout for a full-time Electronic Security Technician to join our Morgantown, WV team. This individual will handle first level support of service requests, field installation of network, security and other related electrical systems, building access control camera systems, troubleshooting and repairing cabling systems and equipment. This relates to all technology, to include; workstations, servers, printers, networks and vendor specific hardware and software. The Electronic Security Technician will be based out of the Morgantown office, and will report directly to the Fie/Security Manager.
</div>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<ul>
<li>Diagnosing and providing solutions for system failures and troubles</li>
<li>
Analyzing blue prints, schematics and drawings of network, security and/or other related electrical systems
</li>
<li>Programming low voltage security products such as cameras, NVRS, DVRS, access control, intercoms, gate operators and networks</li>
<li>
Inspecting electrical, security and networking systems and their components for possible repairs or maintenance
</li>
<li>Installing electrical system components as assigned (i.e. panels, audible/visual alarms, pull stations, detectors, switches, cameras, servers…etc.)
</li>
<li>Preparing written materials to document activities and/or conveying information</li>
<li>
Providing basic technical support at the network level: WAN and LAN connectivity, routers, firewalls and security</li>
<li>
Remaining detail oriented and methodical when troubleshooting issues</li>
<li>
Maintaining a professional and courteous manner while representing Pillar in the field, and answering customer questions as necessary
</li>
<li>Identifying potential value-added sales opportunities to increase company revenue</li>
<li>
Working patiently and maintaining communication with customers
</li>
<li>Assisting with paperwork and documentation for accurate record keeping, billing and inventory
</li>
<li>Maintaining company tools, equipment and vehicles in a professional manner</li>
<li>
Responding to emergency situations during and after hours</li>
</ul>

<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Qualifications
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<ul>
<li>Valid driver’s license required</li>
<li>
High school diploma or equivalent</li>
<li>
1+ years of experience with electronic security/CCTV</li>
<li>
Manufacturers training is a plus</li>
<li>
Experience reading blueprints desired</li>
<li>
Well-rounded experience in cameras, networking and configurations</li>
<li>
Strong verbal and written communication skills with the ability to effectively and professionally communicate with co-workers and customers</li>
<li>
Must be dependable; trustworthy; and possess a positive and productive attitude
</li>
<li>Possess a working knowledge of personal computers and software applications to aid in installation process</li>
<li>
Be able customer focused, and able to work independently and/or with co-workers</li>
<li>
Willing to participate in the company’s required background and drug screening process</li>
<li>
Willing to work a flexible schedule that may include travel, out-of-town work, weekends, extended hours and call-out work</li>
</ul>
