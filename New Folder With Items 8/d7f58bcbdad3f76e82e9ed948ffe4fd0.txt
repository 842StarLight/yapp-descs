<h2>Underwriter</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<b>OVERVIEW</b><p>
We are currently seeking an individual with an appetite for meeting challenges and delivering results to fulfill a critical part in the creation of policies for our customers. As one of our premier Underwriters, you’ll review insurance applications and evaluate their potential risk to Sentry.</p>
<p>
This position can be filled as an Associate Underwriter or Underwriter depending on the selected candidate’s related qualifications and experience.</p>
<b>
WHAT YOU'LL DO</b><p>
As an Underwriter on our Commercial Lines team, you’ll work closely with our sales personnel in order to obtain information needed to underwrite, price, and program individual policies/accounts. In addition, you’ll:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Utilize manuals, procedures, and collected information to handle basic to complex underwriting assignments while referring business outside of authority limits</li>
<li>
Perform placement of basic reinsurance assignments</li>
<li>
Work closely with other business functions to ensure service and quality objectives are met</li>
<li>
Maintain privacy and confidentiality of policy holder information</li>
<li>
Travel to and communicate with insureds on present issues and opportunities</li>
<li>
Attend and partake in company or industry sponsored continuing education seminars</li>
<li>
May assist with training other Underwriters on underwriting procedures</li>
</ul>
<b>
WHAT IT TAKES</b><ul>
<li>
Bachelors Degree or equivalent experience</li>
<li>
2 or more years of experience in commercial insurance is desirable</li>
<li>
Completed insurance courses such as IIA and/or CPCU beneficial</li>
<li>
Prior knowledge of underwriting systems and/or various software applications beneficial</li>
<li>
Excellent verbal, written, and interpersonal communication and human relations skills to provide exceptional customer service</li>
<li>
Ability to meet limited travel requirements
</li>
</ul>
