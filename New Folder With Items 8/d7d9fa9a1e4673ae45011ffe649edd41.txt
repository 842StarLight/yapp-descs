<h2>Salesforce Tech Lead</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">JOB DESCRIPTION
<br><br>
About the Domain
<br><br>
The Salesforce Competency Center is accountable for the delivery of all Salesforce initiatives across the Enterprise. It also provides oversight and governance of platform technology, defines standards, and owns platform support and release management functions. As part of the broader Enterprise Strategy, Data, and Architecture organization, the Salesforce CoE owns the strategic roadmap for Salesforce and its surrounding ecosystem at the bank.
<br><br>
About the Job
<br><br>
The Salesforce Tech Lead will join the Salesforce Delivery team and be part of multiple Salesforce delivery initiatives. This role envisions a broad array of Salesforce centric skills combined with experience working in large and complex implementation initiatives spanning across multiple business units and technology divisions.
