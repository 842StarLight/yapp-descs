<h2>Sales Development Specialist II</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Want to get in on the ground floor of a fast-growing startup? We thought so! At Bump Boxes, we are passionate about our mission: making Mom’s life easier. We work hard to offer unparalleled customer service and amazing products for pregnancy and beyond. We take care of each other and foster an environment of encouraged professional development, creative thinking and growth.<br>
</p>
<p>
Bump Boxes is looking for a positive, excited, motivated, outgoing, energetic, health conscious, adventurous, self-motivated, risk-taker with the desire to be a part of something big in the early stages!
</p>
<p>The Sales Development Specialist will be play a key role in our marketing and sales efforts through the development of qualified leads that results in more proposals and overall sales for the companies and having fun while doing it. The ideal candidate will possess exceptional communication skills, be high-energy, self-disciplined, and self-motivated to work in an environment where higher standards of performance are expected and achieved. Prior experience in B2B sales a plus.<br>
</p>
<p><b>
Responsibilities will include, but not limited to:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Source new sales opportunities through inbound lead follow-up and outbound cold calls and emails</li>
<li>Contact leads to set up appointments for Senior Account Executive</li>
<li>Conduct account-based research to personalize outreach</li>
<li>Collaborate with and leverage teammates to develop additional call and email strategies, and messaging to overcome objections and drive new opportunities</li>
<li>Learn and maintain in-depth knowledge of Bump Boxes’ core products</li>
<li>Be a brand ambassador by communicating clearly and confidently about Bump Boxes</li>
<li>Utilize Zoho to identify potential opportunities, manage daily interactions and conversations, and track progress through sales pipeline</li>
<li>Collaborate with Sales and Marketing teams to communicate prospect needs and challenges in preparation for first meeting; provide support as needed throughout sales cycle</li>
<li>Provide feedback on quality of contacts, prospect needs, industry trends to help shape campaign messaging and future company offerings</li>
<li>Conduct research on prospective customer by using LinkedIn Sales Navigator and other research tools</li>
<li>Coordinate and set lead follow up protocols with Account Executives</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Requirements</b></p>
<ul>
<li>1-3 years of sales related work experience</li>
<li>Self-starter able to deliver high call volume and appointment setting for our Senior Account Executive</li>
<li>An aptitude for learning information quickly and applying it in sales conversations</li>
<li>Exceptional listener with an ability to ask the right questions and identify pain points of the customer</li>
<li>Deep knowledge of business process, roles, and organizational structure</li>
<li>ZoHo, SaaS, and sales automation experience a plus</li>
<li>Experience cold-calling and cold-emailing a plus</li>
<li>LinkedIn Sales Navigator experience a plus</li>
</ul>

<li>Employee Stock Option Plan</li>
<li>Employee Recognition Program</li>

<li>Progressive Vacation Policy</li>
<li>Family Friendly Atmosphere</li>
</ul>
<p>...and the pride of working at the fastest growing company in the region! :-)</p>
