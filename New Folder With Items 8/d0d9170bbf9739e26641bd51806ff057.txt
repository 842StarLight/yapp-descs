<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
About the job</b></h2>
<p>
gTech’s Professional Services team takes a creative, collaborative, and customer-centric approach to provide foundational services and forward-looking business solutions to top advertiser and publisher customers. Through technical implementation, optimization, and key solutions, gTech Professional Services helps customers attain their business goals while building long-term capabilities.</p>
<p>
Working within the broader Google Professional Services (gPS) organization, the Google Customer Solutions (GCS) Implementation Program Management team provides implementation solutions for Google’s small and medium-sized business (SMB) advertising clients. Our team designs and scales programs that accelerate strategic GCS growth opportunities by helping our customers overcome implementation barriers, wherever they exist (e.g., within our products or on their websites).</p>
<p>
As a Global Program Manager on our team, you will design and lead complex, multidisciplinary projects. This includes analyzing the needs of our advertisers and the GCS sales force, creating and implementing solutions, managing cross-functional stakeholders, and clearly communicating goals and results to executive leadership. Projects often span offices, time zones and hemispheres, and you will keep all the players coordinated on the project's progress and deadlines. In this role, you will have a mandate to help our advertisers and the freedom to define the specific actions you will take to accomplish this goal. On our team, you will have exposure and visibility to senior executives and cross-functional leaders as you drive alignment on and excitement for strategic initiatives.</p>
<p>
Google creates products and services that make the world a better place, and gTech’s role is to help bring them to life. Our teams of solution-oriented trusted advisors support millions of customers globally. Our solutions are rooted in our technical skill, product expertise, and a thorough understanding of our customers’ complex needs. Whether the answer is a bespoke solution to solve a unique problem, or a new tool that can scale across Google, everything we do aims to ensure our customers benefit from the full potential of Google products.<br>
<br>
To learn more about gTech, check out our video.</p>

Responsibilities</b></h2>
