<h2>Finance Director</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Overview
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<p>Best Reviews, a profitable and rapidly growing online product review company, is seeking an energetic, highly motivated, and entrepreneurial finance professional to be our Finance Director in our San Francisco office. This individual serves as Finance partner to management and plays a crucial role for the company by focusing on business margin improvement while ensuring sound financial reporting, practices, and analysis that supports major business decisions of the CEO, COO, and the Board of Directors.</p>
<ul><li>
Participate in strategic planning and model revenue growth and cost optimization initiatives</li></ul>
<ul>
<li>
Plan and prepare the annual budget and monthly forecasts and report on variances</li>
<li>
Actively participate in month-end close process, including preparation of journal entries, review of results and preparation of monthly, quarterly and annual financial statements for internal stakeholders</li>
<li>
Take ownership of assigned tasks, meet all deadlines and work towards objectives without undue supervision.
</li>
<li>Reports to corporate planning and interacts closely with CEO/COO.</li>
</ul>

<div>
Who Are We?

From heated blankets to trampolines to cordless drills, we compare the top products in thousands of categories to give you the most reliable purchasing advice. Our goal is to make it easy for you to pick the best product and be confident in your decision.</div>

<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<ul>
<li>
<b>Strategic Planning &amp; Initiatives Supports:</b><ul>
<li>
Creating, updating, and maintaing finacial models and detailed forecasts of the company’s future operations</li>
<li>
Considering opportunities for the company to expand or grow. Mapping out growth plans, including capital expenditures and investments. Updating three- to five-year financial forecasts.</li>
<li>
Develop forecasting models including online traffic, sales activities, and other mission-critical metrics as well as the related revenue &amp; expenses</li>
<li>
Maintain current knowledge of overall company operations, policies, and organization to provide effective customer service.</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>
<b>
Annual Budget &amp; Monthly Forecasts</b><ul>
<li>
Improve and streamline the planning and forecasting process</li>
<li>
Partner with the senior management, key internal customers, and corporate planning to develop inspiring but achievable annual budget that aligns with the long-term strategic plan</li>
<li>
Identify key risks and opportunities, and provide actionable recommendations for financial improvements vs. budgets</li>
<li>
Comparing actual results against budgets and forecasts, and performing variance analysis to explain differences in performance and make improvements going forward</li>
<li>
Prepare monthly cashflow forecast and maintain capital requirement model</li>
<li>
Keep the senior management informed on issues and trends, recommending appropriate resolution or seeking assistance where required.</li>
<li>
Present financial results with business insights to various levels of stakeholders</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>
<b>
Period-end Close and Financial Reporting</b><ul>
<li>
Responsible for the accurate and timely recording of monthly financial transactions to ensure that all financial statements are properly recorded</li>
<li>
Prepare revenue, expense accruals and reclass general ledger journal entries during a compressed monthly close</li>
<li>
Develop comprehensive variance analysis and explanations on both income statements and balance sheets</li>
<li>
Optimize the financial and data tracking process, and provide key metrics reporting to measure and track online performance</li>
<li>
Prepare the monthly, quarterly and annual financial statements for senior management and the Board of Directors</li>
<li>
Track and approve CAPEX
</li>
<li>Ensure compliance with internal controls and take a lead role in assessing/designing control procedures. In addition. participate in process walkthroughs with external and internal auditors and provide quarterly certification of controls effectiveness</li>
<li>
Continually review and enhance internal controls, and facilitate internal audit</li>
</ul>
</li>
</ul>
