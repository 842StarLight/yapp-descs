<h2>Community Relations Coordinator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<b>Position Summary:</b><br>
P. Responsible for evaluating, developing, implementing, and coordinating resident admissions and marketing processes according to statutory, regulatory, and Community policies. Using a strong hospitality focus, implements various admission-generating activities including networking with potential referral sources, creating and maintaining relationships with community organizations, and coordinating regularly scheduled and special event planning.
<br><br>
<b>Essential Duties:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Responsible for receiving inquiries regarding admission into the Community and organization and for dispensing information about the services and mission to relevant parties. Provides appropriate follow-up to inquiries made by prospective residents and families.</li>
<li>May be directed to create and foster relationships within the outside community by calling on potential referral sources, by networking with local churches and their senior groups, and meeting with discharge planners, etc.</li>
<li>As part of the sales team, is flexible in developing and implementing marketing and sales strategies to meet defined move-in objectives, census and sales goals. Also includes participation in event planning within the Community and external events.</li>
<li>Coordinates and schedules admissions and in-house move-ins and move-outs with all related departments.</li>
</ul>
Coordinates paperwork, records and files relating to current and prospective residents.
<br><ul>
<li>With a professional and friendly approach, presents and explains admission packet to residents and family members. Ensures paperwork process is accurate and completed on a timely basis.</li>
<li>Conducts tours of the Community as needed for all interested parties and potential residents.</li>
<li>Coordinates assessment of potential admissions and communicates assessment result with relevant parties.</li>
<li>Ensures timely admission of residents to maintain cash flow and census.</li>
<li>Participates in surveys and other inspections made by authorized governmental agencies; is responsible for complying with statutory and regulatory standards.</li>
<li>Leads and models collaborative and supportive behaviors between and among all Community teams, CLC business entities (i.e. Cappella Living Solutions and all Rhythms service lines.)</li>
<li>Responsible for encouraging, participating and integrating Masterpiece Living and Eden Alternative initiatives by supporting successful aging and person-centered programs and culture.</li>
<li>Other duties as assigned.</li>
</ul>
