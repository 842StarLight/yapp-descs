<h2>Service Manager $1,500 Sign on Bonus ( paid first 30 days)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>FleetPride is the largest after-market distributor of heavy-duty truck and trailer parts in the U.S. with some of the best and brightest people in the business! We are looking for a results-driven, detail-oriented Service Manager to join our growing Service organization. In this position, you will provide excellent customer service experiences and maintain strong relations with employees, dealers, sales representatives, and vendors. As such, you must be a positive, self-motivated, energetic leader who excels in working in a collaborative environment and one who can thrive in a fast-paced leadership role.</p>
<p><b>Key Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul><li>Oversee all Service functions and workspaces in the branch.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Delegate and direct Service tasks; monitor the Service department workflow processes and progress of all current and pending work assignments.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Consistently deliver incredible customer service experiences by offering knowledge, advice, and answering questions and concerns</li></ul>
<ul><li>Proactively coach employees to achieve a high-performance level and manage costs and assets of the branch to achieve financial performance targets.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Act as the highest source of information for all service, inventory, and product-knowledge concerns and work process matters (for both employees and customers)</li></ul>
<ul><li>Manage and update work orders and invoices to ensure quality and timeliness of work performed and accurate invoicing.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Provide a quality check process on all work performed and the accuracy of Invoices.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Communicate and maintain good relations with vendor reps, dealers and subcontractors.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Keep a safe and clean workspace at all times</li></ul>
<ul><li>Monitor employee work and provide frequent progress reports in order to improve employee satisfaction and efficiency</li></ul>
<ul><li>Follow and abide by all industry standards, regulations, and laws</li></ul>
<ul><li>Help train new employees in company procedures, including safety and work processes</li></ul>
<ul><li>Stay up to date on industry standards and any new innovations, materials, tools and/or processes that can benefit the company</li></ul>
<ul><li>Provide direction and oversight to employees and staff and accurate reporting of business unit performance as directed by senior management.</li></ul>

<ul><li>A dynamic leader with a proven track record in Service operations and customer service</li></ul>
