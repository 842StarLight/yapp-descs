<p>Job Description
</p>
<div><div><div>
<p>We are seeking several talented individuals to join the Pharmacy Solutions team. Our Pharmacy Service Specialists assist pharmacies, patients and insurance companies in prescription processing in accordance with established policies. Responsibilities include but are not limited to the following:</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<ul>
<li>
Answer incoming calls to assist customers with a variety of inquiries</li>
<li>
Place calls to gather data to resolve pharmacy claim and coverage inquiries</li>
<li>
Obtain authorization from insurance companies to process new transactions</li>
<li>
Partner with Claims Adjusters to determine claim eligibility and claim status</li>
<li>
Enter and maintain accurate records in our information system</li>
<li>
Respond to customer requests regarding service, products, and account information</li>
<li>
Analyze and rectify customer concerns using established procedures</li>
</ul>
</div></div></div>
