<h2>Senior Accountant</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<b>SG Services, Inc. seeks a Senior Accountant based in our Bloomington office.</b><br>
<br>
<b>Company Summary:</b><br>
SG Services, Inc. provides Management, Accounting, Human Resources, Information Technology, and Legal services to affiliated companies operating within the music industry. These companies include Secretly Group, which is a family of independent record labels (Dead Oceans, Jagjaguwar, Numero Group, Secretly Canadian,). Secretly Services, Inc. also provides Accounting support to Secretly Publishing, Fort William Artist Management, and Bellwether Properties.<br>
<br>
<b>Position Summary:</b><br>
This position will be an integral part of the Accounting team and will play a key role in providing outstanding accounting and financial services to affiliated companies. Day to day, this individual will focus on general ledger accounting, tax compliance, payroll, and financial reporting.<br>
<br>
<b>Responsibilities:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Payroll processing, distribution, withholding tax compliance</li>
<li>Bank Statement Reconciliations</li>
<li>Monthly Balance Sheet account reconciliations &amp; review of general ledger activity</li>
<li>Assists with monthly close process and financial statement preparation</li>
<li>Prepares year end income tax schedules</li>
<li>Prepares property tax returns for various state and local jurisdictions</li>
<li>Prepares sales &amp; use tax returns for numerous states</li>
<li>Develops and documents workflow procedures and accounting polices.<br>
</li>
</ul>
<b>Requirements:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Strong work ethic, able to produce a large volume of work and meet deadlines</li>
<li>Exceptional organizational skills and attention to detail</li>
<li>Excellent communication and writing skills, ability to respond to inquiries and requests promptly</li>
<li>Strong interpersonal skills, able to build authentic relationships easily</li>
<li>Self-motivated and goal-driven</li>
<li>Accounting degree or related experience</li>
<li>CPA or CMA preferred but not required<br>
</li>
<li>Interest in music industry a plus</li>
</ul>
<b>Benefits:</b><br>
Health, Vision, Dental and Life insurance<br>
401K<br>
Paid holidays<br>
Paid vacation<br>
