<h2>Area Manager / Regional Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<b>Description:</b><br>
<b>Company:</b> Headquartered in Houston, Texas, Hilltop Residential is a fast-growing real estate investment firm that sources, acquires and renovates multi-family properties located in the southern United States. The founders of Hilltop collectively have 35 years of experience in the multifamily industry and have completed over $4 Billion in multifamily transactions. The founders of Hilltop’s most recent company were recently recognized by Achievers as a “Top 50 Most Engaged Workplaces” and also named a “Top 100 Best Workplaces” by Great Place to Work Institute as published in Fortune Magazine.
<br><br>
<b>Job Title:</b> Area Manager / Regional Manager – Dallas / Fort Worth, Texas
<br><br>
<b>Position Summary:</b> Hilltop Residential is seeking a highly motivated, experienced Area Manager or Regional Manager who will be responsible for continuing the development and build out of property management functions for Hilltop’s assets in the DFW market. Beginning with Hilltop’s core values as the foundation, our next Area Manager or Regional Manager will set the tone for, and continue the high standards of, customer service at Hilltop Residential. .
<br><br>
<b>Requirements:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Expand the implementation of our property management strategies that support Hilltop’s core values throughout the DFW market.</li>
<li>Execute a property management strategy that sets Hilltop’s customer service and cost controls at levels that will maximize profitability of the Hilltop‘s DFW assets.</li>
<li>Directly supervise all their portfolio’s current onsite property teams.</li>
<li>Evaluate how to continue to deliver resident focused technology to our residents.</li>
<li>Assist in implementing site level operating policies and procedures as already established by Hilltop Residential.</li>
<li>Assist in continuing to develop the highest maintenance standards and methods throughout Hilltop’s portfolio.</li>
<li>Assist in managing programs for cost controls and cost management.</li>
<li>Contribute to the development of onsite personnel to help build upon Hilltop’s culture of a place for hard work, integrity, and opportunity.</li>
</ul>
<b>Qualifications:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>First-hand experience in all facets of multi-site multifamily property management and operations, including financial and customer service performance</li>
<li>Employment history that demonstrates the application and usage of an exceptional sales, marketing, and customer service background sufficient to quickly and consistently resolve customer complaints and issues, maximize revenues, manage spending, and complete financial and other reports and documents with accuracy and punctuality</li>
<li>Developed management and supervisory skills to lead, direct, evaluate, and mentor subordinate team members.</li>
<li>Demonstrated proficiency in using property management software (preferably OneSite / Real Page), word processing, spreadsheet, and database management applications to complete and produce required reports and other employment documents.</li>
<li>Self-starter, team player, hardworking, humble, always looking for the very best way to do things</li>
</ul>
Compensation – Commiserate with Experience</div>
