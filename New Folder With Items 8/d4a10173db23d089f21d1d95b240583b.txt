<h2>Service Technician</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Come grow with us! Hoj Innovations is a growing and dynamic company with a bright future, and we are looking for smart, reliable people to join our exceptional organization. <b>HOJ/HESCO Services</b> seeks outstanding candidates for a <b>Service Technician</b> position to trouble shoot, repair and maintain crane, conveyor and material handling systems.</p>
<p>
About us- We are the intermountain leader in comprehensive warehouse design, building world-class fulfillment centers and provide everything from design, engineering, software, installation, parts, sales and maintenance to the high growth fulfillment industry. If you have a desire to grow professionally in a fast-paced, challenging and supportive company, read on!<br>
</p>
<p><b>Essential Duties and Responsibilities:</b><br>
</p>
<ul>
<li>Visually inspects and listens to machines and equipment to locate causes of malfunctions</li>
<li>Dismantles machines and equipment to gain access to problem area</li>
<li>Inspects and measures parts to detect wear, misalignment, or other problems</li>
<li>Removes and replaces worn or defective parts of drive mechanism or hydraulic system</li>
<li>Realigns and adjusts components such as spindles and clutches</li>
<li>Starts machines and equipment to test operation following repair</li>
<li>Repairs broken parts</li>
<li>Sets up and operates metalworking tools such as lathe, drill press, or grinder, to make or repair parts</li>
<li>Performs other duties as assigned.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Qualifications:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>High school diploma or general education degree (GED); and five years related mechanical and electrical experience with some troubleshooting experience</li>
<li>Ability to work on-call rotations</li>
<li>Ability to read and interpret documents such as safety rules, operating and maintenance instructions, and procedure manuals</li>
<li>Ability to write routine reports and correspondence</li>
<li>Ability to communicate and interact effectively with customers or other employees</li>
<li>Ability to calculate figures and amounts such as discounts, interest, commissions, proportions, percentages, area, circumference, and volume</li>
<li>Ability to apply concepts of basic algebra and geometry</li>
<li>Ability to solve practical problems and deal with a variety of concrete variables in situations where only limited standardization exists</li>
<li>Ability to interpret a variety of instructions furnished in written, oral, diagram, or schedule form</li>
<li>To perform this job successfully, an individual should have basic knowledge in the use of computers and basic data entry skills</li>
<li>Requires a valid driver's license with a good driving record</li>
<li>Must be able to pass pre-employment and ongoing random drug screenings</li>
</ul>
