<h2>TABA Sourcing Partner</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>Position Overview<br>
The TABA Sourcing Partner is responsible for screening and submitting qualified applicants to locations for per diem positions. This role tracks applicant progress through the on-boarding process and reports results to the Sourcing Manager and applicable Hiring Manager and VP. This role is responsive to sourcing needs identified by locations.<br>
<br>
Essential Job Functions<br>
<ul>
<li>Screen candidates for minimum qualifications as defined by state and location guidelines; work with location staff to ensure an efficient on-boarding process</li>
<li>Post requisitions for per diem needs in areas designated by location</li>
<li>Initialize, review and follow up on background and Fraud Abuse Control Information System (FACIS) checks; seek guidance of appropriate Aveanna staff regarding sanctions/criminal records; provide guidance to location on company policies regarding hire</li>
<li>Utilize CareerBuilder to source candidates for locations with insufficient applicant traffic</li>
<li>Consistently document and maintain applicant information in Applicant Tracking System and in Excel reports such as communication, dates and changes in status, etc.</li>
<li>Participate in regular Sourcing department call and in additional group calls as requested</li>
<li>Track and report applicant progress to Sourcing Manager, Hiring Managers and VPs</li>
<li>Generate new business through conversations with prospective caregiver candidates</li>
</ul>
<br>

</ul>
