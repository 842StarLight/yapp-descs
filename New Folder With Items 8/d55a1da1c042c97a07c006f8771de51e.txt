<h2>Account Manager - ANSYS</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>Job Description<br>
<br>
<div>SUMMARY<br>
At EAC we strive to provide the world’s best product development technology and services to our customers by building long-term relationships that provide continuously increasing value. We are looking for someone that is assertive, motivated, self-disciplined, engaging and business savvy. As an Account Manager your primary function is to develop new business as well as develop relationships with key personnel at existing customers. In addition you are responsible for selling the full product suite EAC has to offer in the assigned territory. This includes sales of software and services; implementation, training, process consulting and engineering. In addition, you are responsible for selling the full ANSYS product suite in the assigned territory. This includes sales of software, consulting and engineering, as well as identifying additional opportunities for other services EAC provides.<br>
<br>
ESSENTIAL JOB FUNCTIONS<br>
SALES ROLE<br>
<ul>
<li>To achieve assigned revenue quota by selling ANSYS software products and EAC solutions to the engineering sector of the discrete manufacturing companies with a focus on new business</li>
<li>Investigate and understand the internal business processes of potential clients; and strategize, present and demonstrate a tailored technology solution</li>
<li>Build relationships with key customers and departmental managers</li>
<li>Prospect and develop new accounts while maintaining sales within existing accounts</li>
<li>Develop revenue forecast in coordination with Regional Sales Manager</li>
<li>Expected to develop a pipeline that is 3x of quota in all quota categories</li>
<li>Ability to manage territory through marketing development (Seminars, web casts, customer appreciation events, etc…)</li>
<li>Engage and develop entire territory with the EAC PDS vision, this includes working with the rest of the EAC. Utilize the proper resource for the proper opportunities.</li>
<li>Expected to talk to every EAC customer quarterly (between the AM and AAM this needs to be accomplished)</li>
<li>Develop Long Term Relationships</li>
</ul>
</div>
