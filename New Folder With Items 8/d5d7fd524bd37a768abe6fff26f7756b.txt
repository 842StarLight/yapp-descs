<h2>Air Dispersion Modeler/Project Manager (Mid-Senior Level)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>ERM </b>is seeking a motivated mid to senior level client facing <b>Air Dispersion Modeler/Project Manager </b>to join our consulting team in <b>Raleigh, NC </b>. The successful candidate will work on a variety of interesting technical projects for clients in the power, manufacturing, oil &amp; gas, and chemical sectors and be responsible not only for technical project delivery but also project and client management activities.
</p>
<p></p>
<p>This is an excellent career opportunity to work with an expert consulting team on challenging environmental management projects for large industrial and global clients. Access to ERM's national Air Quality &amp; Climate Change experts provide knowledge sharing of best practices across the industry and ongoing learning opportunities for our team.
</p>
<p>ERM’s Air Quality &amp; Climate Change practice community pursues and wins highly innovative engagements for the most interesting clients, providing the full breadth of air quality and climate change services. With annual global sales in excess of $50 million, ERM’s Air Quality &amp; Climate Change practice is one of the largest among its peers in our industry and is steadily growing at a double-digit pace. In North America alone, ERM has more than 300 dedicated air quality and climate change professionals assisting clients with projects as diverse as developing responses to emerging Low Carbon Fuel Standards, to leading complex capital projects through the many hurdles associated with the NSR permitting program.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>RESPONSIBILITIES:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Provide technical expertise in the air permitting field, with special emphasis on air quality dispersion modeling development, execution, and analysis.
</li>
<li>Perform air quality modeling analyses using standard regulatory air quality models (e.g., AERMOD) to evaluate state and federal health and air quality impacts as required for Title V, PSD, and New Source Review compliance.
</li>
<li>Communicate technical work products clearly and effectively to clients and state and federal agencies at both a technical and business level.
</li>
<li>Perform and manage multiple tasks within scope/budget/schedule expectations and ensure quality standards on project deliverables.
</li>
<li>Interact and maintain successful relationships with clients, ERM employees, and subcontractors.
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>REQUIREMENTS:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>BS in environmental, or atmospheric sciences/meteorology or related degree; MS preferred.
</li>
<li>4-8 years of direct consulting experience in the air quality or atmospheric science field.
</li>
<li>Experience in managing large technical projects and interacting with clients regarding all aspects, technical and financial, related to the project.
</li>
<li>Hands-on knowledge and interest in Clean Air Act issues such as NAAQS, Title V, and PSD permitting issues, especially in how air dispersion modeling requirements interact with these regulations.
</li>
<li>Experience in performing air quality modeling analyses using standard regulatory air quality models (e.g., AERMOD, CALPUFF, AERSCREEN).
</li>
<li>Familiarity with Microsoft office software and other related software programs.
</li>
<li>Excellent written and verbal communication skills.
</li>
<li>Ability to succeed in a fast-paced consulting environment, handling multiple project assignments, meeting strict deadlines, and traveling to client facilities as needed.
</li>
</ul>
