<h2>Non-Qualified Client Service Manager (Private Label)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p><b>Company Overview:</b></p>
<p><i>
Newport Group is a leading provider of retirement plans, insurance, and consulting services. We help companies offer their employees a more secure financial future by offering comprehensive plan solutions and consulting expertise to plan sponsors and the advisors who serve them.</i></p>
<p><b><i>
Job Description:</i></b></p>
<p>
Client Service Manager who will administer and maintain service and communications with Private Label Non-Qualified plan clients.</p>
<p><b><i>
You are the right person for this role if:</i></b></p>
<ul>
<li>You have a Bachelor’s degree in a business related field or an equivalent combination of education and industry experience.</li>
<li>You have 4 or more years of experience in Retirement Services. Work with non-qualified deferred compensation or 457(b) and 457(f) plans a plus.</li>
<li>You have the ability to read, analyze, and interpret general business periodicals, professional journals, technical procedures, or governmental regulations. Ability to write reports, business correspondence, and procedure manuals. Ability to effectively present information and respond to questions from groups of managers, clients, customers, and the general public.</li>
<li>You are a “self-starter” and have effective time management and organization skills as you will work in a fast-paced, demanding environment often working under tight deadlines.</li>
<li>You are able to apply advanced mathematical concepts utilizing Excel and accounting database software programs.</li>
<li>You can travel up to 10% of the time or as needed.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p>
</p>
<p><i>The Client Service Manager will:</i></p>
<ul>
<li>Administer and maintain; documentation and record keeping, plan statements, and communication of information to client, and assist to modify and enhance plan administration processes within the limits of established policy.</li>
<li>Provide client support and customer service on day-to-day inquiries from clients.</li>
<li>Respond to client and partner requests in both oral and written communication.</li>
<li>Act in a pro-active manner with assigned clients, advisors and partners to build and retain relationships.</li>
<li>Responsible for interpreting plan documents for client plan administration.</li>
<li>Provide calculations and amounts to plan sponsors, communication of fund actions and communicate and consult with clients to answer inquiries, research and resolve issues, provide legal updates, and respond to requests for specialized reports.</li>
<li>Assist plan sponsor, partners and intermediaries on the utilization of web-based applications and deliver web demonstrations for financial advisors and plan sponsors.</li>
<li>Work with clients to correct and fund payroll items and handle distribution requests.</li>
<li>Manage enrollment events with Plan Sponsors and necessary internal teams including coordinating the development of enrollment materials and education.</li>
<li>Participate in sales finals presentations and other client meetings.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p>
</p>
<p>It is a requirement of this job to consistently report to work as scheduled, be on time, and remain at work for entire assigned shift.</p>
