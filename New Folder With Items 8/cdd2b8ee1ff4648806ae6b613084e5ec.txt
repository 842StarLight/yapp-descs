<p><b>Nielsen Products, LLC</b></p>

<p>Seeking a strategic product marketing specialist to develop and implement effective product marketing strategies to communicate the key features and benefits of our products, as well as increase sales and lead generation. In the role of Product Marketing Specialist, based in Boulder, CO, you will be in a key position to help further evolve our brands and how we position our products in the global marketplace. <ul>
<li>Expert in content creation for email, blog, video scripts, sales promotions, social media, advertising, and websites</li>

<li>Implement and interpret conversion rate tracking and optimization</li>
<li>Perform ongoing keyword research, selection and optimization for websites, pay-per-click campaigns and re-marketing, as well as other SEO activities</li>
<li>Track, report, and analyze website performance and pay-per-click initiatives and campaigns</li>
<li>Research and analyze competitor websites and SEO strategies</li>
<li>Write, copy, and create images for websites, Display Network Ads, CTA banners, and more</li>
<li>Ability to perform research, analyze websites, document changes, and make decisions regarding optimization approach</li>
<li>Ability to compile and analyze search data and metrics, and make decisions regarding campaign direction</li>
<li>Contact B2B clientele through Email Marketing</li>
<li>Finding and vetting new leads and generating leads through strategic market analysis</li>
<li>Enthusiastic about growing a brand on social media with the goal to sell more product in creative ways</li>
