The Customer Account Specialist serves as a customer liaison for assigned customers within the Database Services business, managing several elements of customer relationship activities. Has direct responsibility of billing for Database subscription contracts and subsequent monthly renewals. This role directly affects the relationship between the company and the customer.</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Job Responsibilities:</b></p>
<p>
Process customer requests for database subscriptions using pre-approved pricing and previously negotiated Ts &amp; Cs.</p>
<p>
Initiate monthly subscription renewals and ensure that they are billed in a timely manner.</p>
<p>
Utilize an established network of internal contacts in other areas of the business.</p>
<p>
Interacts with customer prior to and/or after sale developing and maintaining positive partner relationships.</p>
<p>
Assist the Finance organization with the review of monthly revenue reports.</p>
<p></p>
<p>
Receives general instructions on new projects or assignments. Work is reviewed for soundness of judgment and overall adequacy. Develops solutions to a variety of problems of moderate scope and complexity, referring to defined procedures and practices to determine appropriate action. Responsible for measuring performance to customers on many dimensions. Areas of focus is root cause and development of corrective action. Continue to develop and use analytical skills to perform data analysis to create resolutions to positively impact performance to all customers and/or serve as a precedent for future decisions.</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Job Skills &amp; Attributes:</b></p>
<p>
Customer Service Orientation - Generally applies fundamental service techniques and adheres to proper customer procedures when interacting with customers.</p>
<p>
Dependability - Demonstrates the ability to follow through with work assignments and support management's policies and procedures.</p>
<p>
Detail Orientation - Is able to provide the status of work assignments at any time and can quickly produce any documentation or information that is needed.</p>
<p>
Information Management - Uses various software packages to maintain and update documents and records.
</p>
<p>Judgment - Demonstrates the ability to base decisions on factual information and sound logic.
</p>
