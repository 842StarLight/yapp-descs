<h2>(Winmo) Sales Leader</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>Job Description<br>
<br>
<p>As we continue to grow, we’re looking for a Sales Leader to come in to exceed our sales goals, develop people and help with overall strategy. The ideal Sales Leader will be comfortable with technology, have an entrepreneurial spirit, and is team-oriented with management and teaching capability. The ideal candidate will demonstrate the ability to connect at high levels, carry engaging conversations regarding sales leadership, revenue generation, and strategy.</p>
<p>
The Sales Leader should be up to the challenge of developing a world-class sales organization, establishing third party relationships, and providing follow-up services for clients on customer experience. The Sales Leader will be responsible for coaching, training and managing the new sales team.</p>
<p>
Sales Leader Job Function:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Identify and target strategic or major/national accounts for the team to implement prospecting against</li>
<li>
Provide expert advice, recognition and accountability on the selling process.</li>
<li>
Maintain product/industry expertise and share best practices with other Sales team members.</li>
<li>
Be a trusted mentor to Sales team members.</li>
<li>
Build and maintain an effective personal network to further build access to information, leads, contacts, etc.</li>
<li>
Develop effective relationships with key decision-makers in assigned target customers.</li>
<li>
Conduct negotiations on behalf of the company to secure sales that represent good business within current sales framework, authorities, and practices.</li>
<li>
Support other necessary corporate activities including forecasting, product management and marketing initiatives, development of sales strategies, and departmental planning.</li>
<li>
Build and maintain industry expertise by participating in vertical-specific user groups and by monitoring and reporting on competitor activities and offerings.</li>
<li>
Travel as needed for business development, deal closings, and client retention</li>
</ul>
</div>
