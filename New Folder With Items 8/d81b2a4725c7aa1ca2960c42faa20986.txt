<div><b>About Scout</b></div>
<div>
Scout is the sourcing and supplier engagement platform trusted by procurement teams to streamline their process, manage a unified pipeline of projects, and collaborate with stakeholders and suppliers. Scout’s powerful platform drives profitability and makes enterprise commerce faster, less risky, and more transparent, giving CPOs the tools they need to transform their organizations.</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
Over 250 global brands trust Scout to provide greater visibility across stakeholders and suppliers, centralize core processes, measure effectiveness, and make procurement teams crazy successful by delivering better business outcomes. Our simple, effective interface enables companies to streamline supplier selection, manage vendors, centralize data, and make more informed purchasing decisions, faster.</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
Headquartered in San Francisco, CA with additional offices in Chicago, IL and Toronto, Canada. Scout is funded by Menlo Ventures, New View Capital, Scale Ventures, Salesforce Ventures, and Workday Ventures.</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
Scout RFP is a strategic sourcing software company that helps procurement departments become heroes within their companies. We are on a mission to demystify and reinvigorate sourcing in every industry and vertical. But we can’t do it alone! We need self-starting Senior Enterprise Account Executives to join our San Francisco team and help us make sourcing smarter, simpler, and more streamlined by evangelizing Scout’s full-stack platform.</div>

</div>
<div><i>
Overview:</i></div>
<div>
As a Senior Enterprise Account Executive, you will collaborate with our BDR, Marketing, and Customer Success teams to close new business and drive revenue in our largest markets. Our number one value is Obsess over our Customers. To us, that means discovering and solving for pain points that are crippling procurement departments as well as building relationships with key stakeholders that last well after the papers are signed. Another value of ours? Promote our Employees. We are seeking growth-oriented team members looking for opportunities to excel. If this rings true for you, apply now! We can’t wait to meet you.</div>
