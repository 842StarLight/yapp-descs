<h2>Full Time Teller</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>Position Summary:</p>
<p><br>
</p>
<p><br>
Provides exceptional customer service and basic product information through transactions including a variety of savings, checking and loan account transactions, as well as money orders and official checks. Establishes rapport and identifies cross-sell opportunities and refers customers to appropriate business partners.</p>
<br>
<p>
Major Duties &amp; Responsibilities:</p>
<ul>
<li><div>
Responsible for comprehensive, prompt and efficient customer service. Ensures the accurate processing of basic customer transactions including savings, checking and loan account transactions while adhering to the financial institutions policies and procedures. Transactions also include but not limited to bond redemption, cash advances and safe deposit box payments.</div></li>
<li><div>
Assist customers with account maintenance questions both in the branch and via telephone.</div></li>
<li><div>
Responsible to remain up to date on bank products, services, policies, practices and procedures.</div></li>
<li><div>
Promote bank products and services by referring customers to appropriate business partners based on needs and captures customer interactions using our computer software program PCV. This includes but does not limited to loan, business and investment services.</div></li>
<li><div>
Responsible to balance cash drawer daily. Ensures discrepancies are to be reported to the supervisor.</div></li>
<li><div>
Assist supervisors in daily branch operations and sales activities.</div></li>
<li><div>
Ensures that the teller station and branch properly stocked with forms, supplies, etc.</div></li>
</ul>
<p>
NOTE: This job description is not designed to cover or contain a comprehensive listing of activities, duties or responsibilities that are required of the employee for this job. Duties, responsibilities and activities may change at any time with or without notice.</p>
<br>
<p>
Education &amp; Experience:</p>
<ul>
<li><div>
High school diploma or equivalent.</div></li>
<li><div>
Successful completion of in-house training program.</div></li>
<li><div>
Knowledge, Skills, &amp; Abilities:</div></li>
<li><div>
Good communication and interpersonal skills.</div></li>
<li><div>
Superior delivery of customer service.</div></li>
<li><div>
Excellent attention to detail and computer skills.</div></li>
<li><div>
Ability to recognize customer needs in order to refer products.</div></li>
</ul>
