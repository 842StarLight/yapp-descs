<p><b>Essential Functions and Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Must be able to prioritize work to achieve or exceed stated collection metrics.</li>
<li>
Responsible for using available collection software tools to assist in executing effective strategies and initiatives to achieve continuous receivable delinquency improvement that contributes to a lower DSO.</li>
<li>
Ensures timely and accurate responses and resolution to customer inquiries, questions, documentation issues, billing errors, payment application errors, disputes, program changes, new sale requests and opportunities to close new business.
</li>
<li>Must be proficient in Bond &amp; Lien laws for the given states within the given AR portfolio. Complete waivers of lien as required to obtain payment from customers when required</li>
<li>
Must be knowledgeable in State Tax rates for billing
</li>
<li>Responsible for making decisions to hold &amp; release orders based on credit line exposures and risk determination</li>
<li>
Responsible for creating workout plans for financially distressed customers that mitigate loss exposure to Ingersoll Rand.</li>
<li>
Use appropriate level of authority for account concessions (determine if amount is owed or written off to GL)</li>
<li>
Must be able to promote, negotiate and sell pre-payment options to customers to expedite payment when needed</li>
<li>
Must be knowledgeable in the creation of AIA documents for billing customers. In some cases, create amended AIA billing documents to secure payment.</li>
<li>
Provide phone support by handling incoming calls from sales offices and customers on a variety of items.</li>
<li>
Responsible for developing and maintaining business relationships with customers (contractors and owners) and Sales Office personnel that promotes future business.</li>
<li>
Monitors assigned portfolio to mitigate loss exposure by ensuring each assigned customer is in full compliance with their approved program. Recommends changes to the existing program where warranted; exceptions are corrected and/or brought to the attention of Manager. Prepares and recommends over line approval requests.
</li>
<li>Responsible for account reconciliation and identifying and tagging disputed items within assigned portfolio. Tracking and ensuring timely clearing of these disputed items is required.</li>
<li>
Initiates and maintains follow-up to ensure all operating and action conditions placed on approved credit files are completed within established time frames.</li>
<li>
Compiles all information necessary to comply with the Business Unit's reporting in a timely and efficient manner.</li>
<li>
Follows up on legal documentation required for a given customer account (if needed).</li>
<li>
Responsible for risk management for accounts &amp; projects within a given level of authority once training is completed.</li>
<li>
Running Dun &amp; Bradstreet/ Equifax reports and calling for credit references as needed with approval authority for credit lines up to $35K.</li>
<li>
Initiates travel to visit field sales office teams as well as customers to build relationships and educate them on current issues and expectation for prompt payment.</li>
<li>
As required, perform all other related functions &amp; projects as assigned.</li>
</ul>
