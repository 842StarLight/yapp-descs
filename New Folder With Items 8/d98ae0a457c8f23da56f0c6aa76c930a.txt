<h2>Real Estate Transaction Coordinator</h2>

<p>Real Estate Transaction Coordinator</p>
<p><b>Job Overview</b></p>
<p>Provides high level customer service to clients in the sale and/or purchase of real estate</p>
<p><b>Key Responsibilities</b></p>
<p>· Provides superior client customer care</p>
<p>· Provides a great first impression by answering client calls; conveying empathy, kindness &amp; enthusiasm</p>
<p>· Implements the details of each transaction process related to contractual sales and listings</p>
<p>· Coordinates details with clients, agents, and title companies in the contract-to-close cycle</p>
<p>· Demonstrates a high level of positive verbal &amp; written interaction with clients</p>
<p>· Troubleshoots client service challenges with high level of diplomacy</p>
<p>· Maintains information technology processes; liaison with IT provider</p>
<p>· Serves as a liaison with vendors</p>
<p>· Develops &amp; maintains data spreadsheets &amp; procedures manuals</p>
<p><b>Duties </b><br>· Transaction Coordination Activities:</p>
<p>o Creates and maintains client transaction binders and tracks action steps</p>
<p>o Ensures all contractual documents are complete and correct</p>
<p>o Maintains contract calendar &amp; countdown checklists</p>
<p>o Implements computer tracking systems for all transactions</p>
<p>o Develops &amp; maintains buyer packets</p>
<p>o Maintains closing spreadsheet</p>
<p>o Cross trains &amp; assists with the listing/marketing coordinator tasks</p>
<p>o Coordinates activities of Team Runner</p>
<p>· Manages flow of activity in computer systems in order to maintains databases, transactional records, &amp; archives</p>
<p>· Troubleshoots minor technology issues and coordinates with telephone &amp; IT provider</p>
<p>· Purchases and maintains office supply inventories</p>
<p>· Assists agents with scheduling showing appointments and open house events</p>
<p>· Monitors team emails and ensure all client communications are carried out in a timely &amp; professional manner</p>
<p>· Responsible for keeping Team Leader and all agents informed regarding client issues</p>
<p><b>Required Knowledge, Skills, Abilities </b><br>· Mastery of the Microsoft Office Suite: Excel, Word, Publisher</p>
<p>· Mastery of the Multi List System (MLS), Keller Williams systems (eEdge &amp; Command)</p>
<p>· Outstanding ability to multi-task &amp; prioritize</p>
<p>· Excellent grammatical &amp; proof-reading skills</p>
<p>· Excellent communication &amp; time management skills</p>
<p>· Excellent project management skills</p>
<p>· High degree ability to maintain strict confidentiality regarding all proprietary &amp; client information</p>
<p>· Outstanding listening skills and ability to communicate compassionately with mature clients</p>
<p><b>General Job Requirements</b></p>
<p>· Must live within 20 minutes of zip code 75036</p>
<p>· Must be able to accurately type 50 wpm</p>
<p>· Must have cell phone</p>
<p>· Must have a late model operating vehicle required</p>
<p>· No smoking or gum chewing permitted</p>
<p>· Ability to lift 25 pounds</p>
<p>· Business dress required</p>
<p>· Flexibility to occasionally work extended hours</p>
<p>· <i>Valid Texas Real Estate Agent License is a plus</i></p>

<p>· Pay is commensurate with qualifications</p>
