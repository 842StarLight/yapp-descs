<h2>Sales Rep</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p><b>POSITIO</b><b>N TITLE: DEALERSHIP SALES REPRESENTATIVE</b>
</p>
<p><b>DEPARTMENT: SALES
</b></p>
<p><b>REPORTS TO: </b><b>SALES MANAGERS</b>
</p>
<p><b>FLSA STATUS: EXEMPT (salespeople)</b><br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>PURPOSE:</b>
</p>
<p>The sales representative is responsible for the <i>sale </i>of new and used cars.
</p>
<p><b>CORE DUTIES:</b>
</p>
<ul><li><p><b>Aggressively and cheerfully perform or ensure performance </b>of any and all activities necessary to improve and maintain the highest possible Dealership Customer Satisfaction level.
</p></li></ul>
<ul>
<li>
<p><b>Generate and maximize unit </b><b><i>sale:</i></b></p>
<ul>
<li><p>work to achieve <i>sales</i> goals set by management on a weekly, monthly, and yearly basis and review daily the results vs goals</p></li>
<li><p>
Maintain accurate records in the CRM.</p></li>
<li><p>
 Follow the CRM follow up procedures without fail.</p></li>
<li><p>
 utilize phone, mail, civic organizations, personal advertising, and other forms of prospecting</p></li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>
<p><b>Total understanding of the </b><b><i>selling</i></b><b> process</b>:</p>
<ul>
<li><p>demonstrate profitable use of floor time to effectively close first time prospects.</p></li>
<li><p>
 100% of the time you must have a introduction of customer to the appropriate manager to insure effective close of vehicle transaction.</p></li>
<li><p>
Be knowledgeable of questions concerning extended warranty.</p></li>
<li><p>
check condition of vehicles scheduled for delivery, always leaving ample time for corrective service if necessary.</p></li>
<li><p>
 deliver all vehicles sold personally.</p></li>
<li><p>
 always introducing customer to service facility and personnel.</p></li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>
<p><b>Acquire and maintain total product knowledge</b>:</p>
<ul>
<li><p>capability of communicating all features and benefits of all models</p></li>
<li><p>
Take all factory tests and become a certified salesperson</p></li>
<li><p><i>
maintain current </i>knowledge of competition’s features, benefits, and shortcomings;
</p></li>
<li><p>use effective demonstration rides and walk aounds to reinforce product features</p></li>
<li><p>
knowledge of complete inventory, both in stock and in process;
</p></li>
<li><p>participate in training programs, product seminars, competitive comparison meeting, etc.
</p></li>
</ul>
</li>
</ul>
<p><b>OTHER DUTIES:</b>
</p>
<ul><li>
<p><b>Demonstrate professional personal behavior</b>:</p>
<ul>
<li><p>treat all customers in an enthusiastic, courteous and helpful manner always striving for complete customer satisfaction</p></li>
<li><p>
 dressing appropriately, in uniform supplied by company and as defined by dealership policies and procedures</p></li>
<li><p>
 refraining from smoking in presence of customers</p></li>
<li><p>
 proper and timely greeting of customers entering the dealership</p></li>
<li><p>
 dealing honestly and accurately with both customers and management</p></li>
<li><p>
 complete all paper work in a timely manner
</p></li>
<li><p>Perform other duties as assigned by management</p></li>
</ul>
</li></ul>
<p><b>PHYSICAL QUALIFICATIONS:</b>
</p>
<ul><li><p>Ability to assist in snow removal on sales lot and vehicle</p></li></ul>
<ul><li><p>
Ability to lift 20 lbs<br>
</p></li></ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>QUALIFICATIONS:</b>
</p>
<ul>
<li><p>Know and understand the federal, state and local laws which govern retail auto sales.
</p></li>
<li><p>Must follow Company safety policies and practices, and immediately report any and all accidents to a Manager/Supervisor
</p></li>
<li><p>High school diploma or the equivalent.
</p></li>
<li><p>Ability to read and comprehend instructions and information.
</p></li>
<li><p>Must have a valid in-state driver’s license and have and maintain an acceptable, safe driving record.
</p></li>
<li><p>Must be insurable by Company insurance company.</p></li>
<li><p>
Excellent communication skills.
</p></li>
<li><p>Ability to sell a minimum quota according to dealership standards.
</p></li>
<li><p>Must be willing to work flexible hours</p></li>
</ul>
