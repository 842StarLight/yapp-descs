<h2>Field Engineer - Prosper Press</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>Kodak is Hiring<b>!</b><br>

We are looking for an experienced<b> Field Engineer </b>in <b>White Plains, MD area</b><br>
<br>
Our service team is looking for an individual which will help Kodak grow in the future. We are looking for an experienced Field Engineer to repair, maintain and install Kodak Inkjet Printing Systems. These products may include Prosper Presses, S-Series Printers and other legacy inkjet systems. Such an individual would need to drive customer satisfaction by advising customers on preventive maintenance, configurations, and operations which may impact product performance. We are looking for an individual who will manage service calls (in real time) in our state of the art system, including following up on service calls, identifying potential growth opportunities, and helping to ensure the site’s profitability.<br>
