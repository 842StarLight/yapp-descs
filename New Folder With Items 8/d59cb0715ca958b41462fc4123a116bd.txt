<div><div><div><div><p>General Ledger &amp; Accounting Ops Director - GROWMARK, Inc. - Bloomington, IL
</p></div></div></div></div>
<div><div><div><div>
<div>GROWMARK is a regional cooperative with annual sales of $7.0 billion providing agronomy, energy, facility planning, and logistics products and services, as well as grain marketing and risk management services in more than 40 states and Ontario, Canada. GROWMARK is ranked fourth on the NCB Co-op 100 listing of the nation’s 100 highest revenue-earning cooperative businesses. GROWMARK owns the FS trademark, which is used by affiliated member cooperatives. GROWMARK is large enough to offer solid career opportunities, but small enough to provide a family-oriented, welcoming environment and great benefits.</div>

<br>
<div></div>
<div><b>
PURPOSE AND SUMMARY STATEMENT</b></div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div>Responsible for generation of the GROWMARK consolidated GAAP (Generally Accepted Accounting Principles) financial reports and annual financial statement audit process. Directs the accounts payable and receivable processes and related functions.</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div> <b>SUPERVISION AND LEADERSHIP</b>
</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div>Directs, leads and develops the Supv Accounting &amp; Control, Mgr Accounting &amp; Systems and their direct reports.</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div><b>ESSENTIAL JOB FUNCTIONS</b></div>
<br>
<div></div>
<p>Responsible for maintaining accounting systems and controls to account for assets, liabilities, receipts and disbursements of GROWMARK and certain subsidiaries.</p>
<p>Directs the general and administrative expense and other income and expense functions including general ledger maintenance, balance sheet and overhead account analyses.</p>
<p>Directs the receivables and payables accounting for corporate GROWMARK.</p>
<p>Directs the fixed asset accounting functions for corporate/retail GROWMARK and certain subsidiaries.</p>
<p>Directs the implementation of technology to improve accounting processes and financial information reporting and to provide access for decision making.</p>
<p>Coordinates the annual audit by independent auditors including preparation of GROWMARK Consolidated Financial Statements, footnotes and supplementary schedules. Performs same for GROWMARK sponsored employee benefit plans.</p>
<p>Ensures GROWMARK’s compliance with Generally Accepted Accounting Principles including research and communication of accounting standards, implementation and documentation of accounting policies.</p>
<p>Consults with corporate and division management in providing interpretation of financial information.</p>
<p>Directs the collection of subsidiary and retail division accounting information and its consolidation into the GROWMARK financial statements.</p>
<p>Directs accounting for business acquisition and other major business transactions for GROWMARK and subsidiaries. Performs discounted cash flow or other valuation methods to determine valuation of certain assets and liabilities.</p>
<p><b>OTHER JOB FUNCTIONS</b></p>
<div>Participates in divisional strategic planning and budgeting.</div>
<br>
<p><br>
Provides consultation on business model, acquisitions, regulatory, and technology developments to maintain internal controls and compliance with GAAP.</p>
<p>Follows GROWMARK’s Environmental Health and Safety, OSHA and DOT policies and procedures, as they apply.</p>
<div>Performs all other duties as assigned.</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div><b>REQUIREMENTS</b></div>
<br>
<div></div>
<p>Normally requires a college degree in accounting, finance, or business administration or equivalent, plus a CPA and at least 10-12 years of related experience to demonstrate the ability to provide corporate accounting leadership to the GROWMARK System.</p>
<p>Advanced understanding and experience with accounting principles, financial decision making, accounting information systems, cost accounting, general ledger maintenance, account analyses, fixed asset, receivables, and payables accounting, internal controls, audit strategy, policies and procedures, and compliance along with accounting strategic planning, program execution and budgeting.</p>
<p>Proven management and leadership skills including staffing, coaching, team-building and performance evaluation and the ability to achieve results through others.</p>
<p>Proven professional communication, interpersonal, persuasion, leadership, and customer service skills.</p>
<p>Demonstrated planning, organization, problem-solving, and project management skills.</p>
<p>Proven IT skills including competency in recommending and implementing systems development initiatives, and in the use of accounting software, software query tools, and databases along with the ability to use technology to enhance business processes.</p>
<p>Demonstrates essential abilities including business knowledge, collaboration, communication, customer focus, decision making and skill development.</p>
<div>Must have and maintain a valid driver’s license and passport, and have the ability to travel independently and overnight as necessary.</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div>May be required to work hours beyond what is considered “normal”.</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<p>Ability and willingness to participate in required training that may include education on GROWMARK’s policies and procedures and additional training as it relates to the requirements of the position.</p>
