<div>FULL-TIME KEYHOLDER - Mashpee, MA</div>
<p></p>CORE RESPONSIBILITIES
<br>
<ul>
<li>Meet and exceed daily store sales and profit goals</li>
<li>Analyze and react to business trends regarding assortment, communicating sell through, stock levels, customer feedback and opportunities to increase sales to corporate office
</li>
<li>Motivate staff to meet store goals and comply with company policies and procedures</li>
<li>Ensure merchandising concepts are set up and maintained.</li>
<li>Perform and supervise store opening/closing procedures including counting register funds, completing bank deposits, opening and closing registers, and securing facility</li>
<li>Conduct daily store meetings to ensure accurate and consistent brand communication with employees</li>
<li>Perform inventory responsibilities including the timely and accurate processing of all markdowns, transfers, damages, shipping and receiving in order to maintain operational integrity</li>
</ul>
<br>
SKILL SET REQUIREMENTS
<br>
<ul>
<li>Professional sales development and seasoned motivator to staff</li>
<li>Excellent interpersonal skills</li>
<li>Clear communication, both written and verbal</li>
<li>Strong leadership skills including the ability to communicate internally at all levels within the organization and externally with clientele
</li>
<li>Ability to read and analyze selling reports, identify sales trends, and react to the needs of the business</li>
Comfort in making decisions and mediating conflict in a team environment Proficient in PC based software including Outlook, Excel, and Word</ul>
<br>
EDUCATION AND EXPERIENCE
<br>
<ul>
<li>Strong personal selling and customer relations experience</li>
<li>Six months of customer service experience, retail experience preferred</li>
<li>Must be available to work store schedule, including nights and weekends on an as-needed basis</li>
<li>Can stand comfortably for long periods of time; able to lift boxes up to 50 pounds</li>
</ul>
