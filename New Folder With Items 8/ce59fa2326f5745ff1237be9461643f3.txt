<div>Do you have the ability to think critically, analyze and decompose complex systems to identify risk and drive improvements to enhance security processes and controls? You will be a be a member of a high performing Information Security &amp; Risk Management team that is responsible for the security of Big Data platforms and Supply Chain Applications.</div>

<div></div>
<div>Responsibilities:</div>
<ul>
<li>Perform application security questionnaires/assessments, identifying security gaps and working with customers to develop appropriate remediation actions.</li>
<li>
Advise customers on the secure use of new technologies related to big data platforms.</li>
<li>
Advise customers on the secure use of Supply Chain ERP &amp; Planning applications.</li>
<li>
Develop and maintain secure standards for Big Data &amp; Supply Chain ERP/Plan Applications.</li>
<li>
Lead large security projects spanning many organizations.</li>
<li>
Work with security teams and customers to identify and remediate vulnerabilities.</li>
</ul>
