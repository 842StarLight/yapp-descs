<h2>Virtual Accountant</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>We are looking for an accounting professional to become apart of our growing CPA Firm as a virtual accountant at our West Covina Office. This is a flexible, part-time position which will allows you to work from home virtually.</p>
<p>The accepted candidate will enjoy using cutting edge software and technology, along with a cloud infrastructure in a paperless work environment. The position allows one to work on various business industries and perform tasks related to tax, accounting, and administrative duties.</p>
<p><b>Position Role: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Guide new clients through the Onboarding Process</li>
<li>Process and organize client documents</li>
<li>Bookkeeping, Accounting Journal Entries, and General Ledger Updates</li>
<li>Bank Reconciliations and Month End Close </li>
<li>Accounts Payable and Receivable Operations </li>
<li>Seeing through client day-to-day financial operations and performing ad-hoc projects at the clients request</li>
<li>Business and Individual Tax Preparation for client groups</li>
<li>Payroll processing, tax payments and filings</li>
<li>Preparation of Sales Tax Returns and Nexus</li>
<li>Administrative duties and assistance to management, including ad-hoc tasks and projects</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Position Qualifications</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Must be available during normal business hours, 20-30 hour a week with internet connection.</li>
<li>Preferred Bachelors in Accounting, Finance or Business</li>
<li>Preferred at least 1 year of tax and accounting experience</li>
<li>Must be proficient in Microsoft Office, experience in Quickbooks a plus</li>
<li>Need to be able to communicate via email professionally</li>
</ul>
<p>Job Type: Part-time</p>
<p>Salary: $15.00 to $20.00 /hour</p>
</div>
