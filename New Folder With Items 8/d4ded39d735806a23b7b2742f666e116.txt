<h2>Technical Support Representative NHR 515</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<b>Summary:</b><br>
Hawk Ridge Systems is adding a Technical Support Representative based in our Portland-area office. This is an entry level, customer-facing position that requires exceptional communications skills and proven proficiency in troubleshooting and resolving tier one, licensing, administrative and fundamental Windows technical issues that affect users of SOLIDWORKS applications. The role of Technical Support Representative requires regular and consistent interaction with external customers.
<br>Hawk Ridge Systems Technical Support agents become experts with SOLIDWORKS products and gain many SOLIDWORKS certifications along the way. This position will have an emphasis on SOLIDWORKS Product Data Management.
<br><br>
<b>Key Responsibilities:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Provide technical support to external customers in a post-sales capacity.</li>
<li>Develop and maintain technical expertise in SOLIDWORKS applications.</li>
<li>Learn new skills and applications as new products are launched.</li>
<li>Constant communication with customers via phone, chat, and e-mail to solve installation, crashing, and general usage type cases.</li>
</ul>
<b>Qualifications:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>At least two years of work experience in a customer support and client-facing environment.</li>
<li>Experience in troubleshooting Windows networks in a help desk role.</li>
<li>Familiarity with customer support best practices, including support case lifecycle.</li>
<li>Bachelor's degree in Computer Information Systems or Information Technology preferred. Equivalent experience will be considered.</li>
<li>Strong familiarity with Microsoft Operating Systems &amp; MS Office products.</li>
<li>Experience with TCP/IP troubleshooting would be a bonus.</li>
<li>Self-starter with the ability to take initiative and accomplish tasks.</li>
<li>Possess excellent problem definition and problem-solving skills.</li>
</ul>
