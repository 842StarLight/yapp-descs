<h2>Demand Generation Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p><b>Rapid7 is helping organizations around the globe advance securely. Our technology, services, and community-focused research simplify complexity for security teams, helping them reduce vulnerabilities, monitor for malicious behavior, investigate and shut down attacks, and automate routine tasks. </b><b>With more than 7,400 customers across 120 countries, Rapid7 is a recognized leader in cybersecurity that has proudly earned numerous industry accolades and strong recognition for our technology and culture. </b><b>Learn more at www.rapid7.com.</b></p>
<h1 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
The Opportunity</b></h1>
<p><b>
Rapid7 is looking for a Demand Generation Manager to support our Central Sales and Business Development teams based in Austin, Texas. Reporting to the Senior Director, Americas Growth Marketing, you will be a key member of the Growth Marketing team and a critical partner to Sales and other cross-functional teams. Growth Marketing at Rapid7 is a unique blend of science and creativity. With a passion for analytically-driven marketing and a desire to blaze new trails and challenge convention, you'll translate our global mission, vision and innovation into scalable growth initiatives that resonate with customers and deliver out-sized results. This is a highly visible role with substantial ability to directly impact Rapid7's market success.</b></p>
<h1 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Essential Responsibilities</b></h1>
<ul>
<li><p><b>
Develop and execute a growth marketing strategy and program to support our customer acquisition goals</b></p></li>
<li><p><b>
Build and scale an account-based marketing program to target and engage the accounts our sales team have prioritized</b></p></li>
<li><p><b>
Work closely with sales to track account engagement and ensure alignment between the two groups</b></p></li>
<li><p><b>
Drive attendance to our field events through an integrated promotional campaign</b></p></li>
<li><p><b>
Work with our key channel and technology partners in-region, building and executing on joint go-to-market plans</b></p></li>
<li><p><b>
Identify opportunities to improve marketing effectiveness through better audience segmentation, marketing techniques, and processes</b></p></li>
<li><p><b>
Monitor and communicate campaign performance so we know what's working, what's not, and how we can best beat our goals. You'll ensure we have detailed insight into all funnel metrics and ROI across every acquisition channel</b></p></li>
</ul>
