<h2>Retail Partnership Business Development Analyst - CHICAGO</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<div>
<p><b>**POSITION IS LOCATED IN THE CHICAGO OFFICE**</b></p>
<p>
Job Purpose:<br>
This position is responsible for supporting Rx networks and the retail strategy/execution of partnerships with retailers. Executes partnerships with retailers that leads to improving health outcomes, reducing cost of care, and enhancing member experience for HCSC. Assess and evaluate partnerships with retailers, contributing to the development of RFPs and negotiation of potential pilots. Supports the development and implementation of new Rx networks and partnerships for HCSC, and other innovations that drive awareness, conversion and sales.</p>
<div>
Required Job Qualifications:<br>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor Degree in Business, Marketing, Communications or similar field</li>
<li>5 years marketing/advertising experience or retail marketing/sales experience</li>
<li>1 year in acquisition, marketing, partnerships and/or new business development</li>
<li>Experience leading major business, planning, and development efforts</li>
<li>Financial acumen</li>
<li>Verbal and written communications skills to negotiate, present and communicate effectively with individuals at all levels of the organization and with external agencies</li>
<li>Leadership presence and skills to drive collaborative work processes across multidisciplinary teams</li>
<li>Travel required</li>
</ul>
</div>
<div>
Preferred Job Qualifications:<br>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor Degree in Marketing, Finance, or Health Administration</li>
<li>Master Degree in Business, Health Administration, Marketing or similar field.</li>
<li>Prior experience in healthcare, consulting, product managment or project implementation</li>
<li>Knowledge of managed care/health care products, operations and sales channels.</li>
</ul>
