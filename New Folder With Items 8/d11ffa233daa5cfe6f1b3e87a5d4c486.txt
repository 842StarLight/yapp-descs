<h2>Health, Safety & Environmental Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Position Summary:</b></p>
<p>The Health, Safety, Environmental and Facilities Manager is responsible for the development, implementation and monitoring of the Company’s Safety and Environmental programs as well as overall equipment and facility maintenance. This position is responsible for meeting all the Governmental requirements as well as the Company’s philosophies and optimizing costs.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Essential Duties &amp; Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul><li> Proven ability to develop and implement the Company’s Health, Safety and Environmental programs</li></ul>
<p>o Establish and lead a Safety Coordinator group, assigning a qualified Safety Coordinator at each Hufcor location</p>
<p>o Ensure that necessary programs are in place to reduce employee risk</p>
<p>o Maintain compliance with all federal, state and local regulations</p>
<p>o Develop appropriate HSE policies and procedures</p>
<ul><li> Ability to maintain consistency for all Health, Safety and Environmental efforts throughout the Company</li></ul>
<p>o Ensure a consistent and cost effective approach</p>
<p>o Direct HSE audits and programs at headquarters, locations and subsidiaries</p>
<ul><li> Oversee the development and implementation of effective industrial hygiene programs</li></ul>
<p>o Address in a timely fashion all requests to evaluate air quality, illumination standards, respirator protection, and/or hearing conservation issues in the workplace</p>
<p>o Review and approve occupational investigations to ensure that all workplaces are eliminating potential health hazards</p>
<ul><li> Develop strategic HSE planning for Company operations</li></ul>
<p>o Analyze the impact of HSE compliance on proposed projects</p>
<p>o Work within appropriate levels of the organization to improve workplace safety and reduce hazardous waste</p>
<p>o Maintain a thorough understanding of relevant laws/regulations</p>
<p>o Assist in administering and maintaining compliance programs such as LOTO, Hazard Communication, EAP, Hearing Conservation, Machine Guarding, Industrial Hygiene, etc.</p>
<ul><li> Conduct regular evaluations of the impact of Company products/facilities/operations on its environment</li></ul>
<p>o Work with Sales Team to assist in safety compliance documentation</p>
<p>o Ensure field installation teams are compliant to company-wide safety program requirements</p>
<p>o Conduct regular safety audits of Hufcor field installation procedures and processes</p>
<p>o Work closely with the Director of Service Sales to ensure consistent safety quality nationally for our service technicians</p>
<ul><li> Perform facility HSE inspections</li></ul>
<ul><li> Evaluate Personal Protective Equipment (PPE) and ensure proper use and maintenance of PPE</li></ul>
<ul><li> Direct the development of HSE training programs to ensure objectives are met</li></ul>
<ul><li> Serve as an advisor for any accident. Coordinate accident investigation for root cause and corrective action</li></ul>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Essential Duties &amp; Responsibilities cont’d</b></p>
<ul><li> Serve as the principal community liaison with all local, state and federal regulatory agencies</li></ul>
<ul><li> Service as a resource for all Hufcor office and staff</li></ul>
<ul><li> Coordinate and prioritize buildings &amp; grounds repair, maintenance and projects (including CAPEX)</li></ul>
<ul><li> Design and build controls for tooling and products</li></ul>
<ul><li> Identify and develop cost justifications for equipment and processes, estimating costs and organizing resources</li></ul>
<ul><li> Other duties as assigned</li></ul>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Knowledge, Skills and Abilities:</b></p>
<ul><li> In-depth knowledge of HSE&amp;F practice, procedures and compliance</li></ul>
<ul><li> Ability to maintain confidentiality</li></ul>
<ul><li> Excellent communication skills, both verbal and written</li></ul>
<ul><li> Ability to interact and influence others in a positive manner</li></ul>
<ul><li> Computer proficient including MS Office</li></ul>
<ul><li> Organized, detail oriented, and able to set priorities while managing multiple projects</li></ul>
<ul><li> Professional demeanor and positive attitude</li></ul>
<ul><li> Able to travel to various domestic sales offices</li></ul>
<ul><li> Working knowledge in designing and building PLCs for tooling and products</li></ul>
<ul><li> Proven leadership and influencing skills, with the ability to facilitate teams and manage projects</li></ul>
<ul><li> Strong interpersonal skills, both written and verbal communication</li></ul>
<ul><li> Strong mathematical skills, including metrics conversions</li></ul>
<ul><li> Experience in negotiating contracts and managing subcontractors for outsourced projects</li></ul>
<ul><li> Ability to work hands-on in a physically demanding environment, operating machinist’s hand tools, manual mills, lathes, saws, drills, welding equipment</li></ul>
<ul><li> Ability to identify and implement process improvements</li></ul>
<ul><li> Strong organizational skills with the ability to set priorities and manage multiple projects</li></ul>
<ul><li> Self-motivated with the ability to work independently with minimal supervision</li></ul>
<ul><li> Proficiency in 3D drawing software, such as AutoCAD, Solidworks, Pro-E , 3ds Max, etc Ability to work in a rapid changing, fast-paced environment on strategic and tactical elements</li></ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Minimum Education and Experience:</b></p>
<ul><li> Bachelor’s degree in Occupational Safety, Environmental Science, Chemistry, or related technical equivalent</li></ul>
<ul><li> 4 – 6 years HSE or related experience</li></ul>
<ul><li> 2 years of supervisory / management experience preferred</li></ul>
