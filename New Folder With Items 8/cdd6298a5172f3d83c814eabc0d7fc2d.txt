<p><b>
About the Role</b></p>
<p></p>
<p>
The Partner Business Manager is responsible for managing a $25 - $30M book of business in the Channel Support segment.</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>
The Partner Business Manager will
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>close assigned support renewal sales,</li>
<li>
protect renewal sales revenue streams</li>
<li>
maintaining partner relationships.</li>
<li>
Work strategically with partners and distributors within a defined account assignment or territory.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Education and Experience</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Four year university/ Bachelor's degree preferred or equivalent experience.</li>
<li>
Typically at least 2 years’ of selling or account management experienced; preferable in IT industry.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Skills and Knowledge:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
General knowledge of Channel business, preferred in enterprise software industry with general understanding of related technologies</li>
<li>
Proven track record in sales, with specific knowledge of Maintenance Renewal sales</li>
<li>
Demonstrated ability to move leads through entire sales process independently.</li>
<li>
Accurate and timely forecasting to management</li>
<li>
Leadership skills and strategic planning</li>
<li>
Strong communicator and relationship builder with proven negotiation skills</li>
</ul>
