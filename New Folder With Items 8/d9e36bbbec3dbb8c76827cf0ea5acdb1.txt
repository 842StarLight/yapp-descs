<h2>Account Executive</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>MicroRam Electronics, Inc. is a stocking distributor of Electronic and PC Components. MicroRam provides unmatched quality products and services to our customer base of OEM's, CEM's, brokers and distributors. We are currently looking to expand our Sales team! Don't live in the Tampa Bay area? No problem, you can work from home! This position offers a competitive base salary and commission with an unlimited earning potential.</p>
<p>Candidates would be responsible for successfully selling electronic components into various industry segments, customers and territories.</p>
<p>MicroRam employees enjoy a fast paced, trade floor environment in a professional yet business casual setting.</p>
<p>Responsibilities and Duties</p>
<ul>
<li>Maintaining an active customer base.</li>
<li>Meeting established goals, budgets and objectives established by management.</li>
<li>Outbound phone calls to develop and qualify new leads and prospects.</li>
<li>Establishing relationships with buyers and management at OEM, CEM and EMS companies. (Both domestic and international)</li>
<li>Manage incoming requirements and quoting procedures.</li>
<li>Coordinate all customer order transactions and order management.</li>
<li>Data entry into ERP and CRM software.</li>
<li>Occasional limited customer site visits and travel may be required.</li>
</ul>
<p>Qualifications and Skills</p>
<ul>
<li>Proven track record of at least 1-5 years of successful sales experience in franchised or independent distribution preferred.</li>
<li>High school diploma or GED required.</li>
<li>Bachelor's degree preferred.</li>
<li>Committed to quality and customer service.</li>
<li>Ability to make proactive decisions and handle customer service related issues.</li>
<li>Excellent written and verbal communication skills.</li>
<li>Career driven with a professional attitude.</li>
<li>Self-motivated with an attention to detail.</li>
<li>MS Office experience with Excel, Outlook and Word.</li>
<li>Bilingual a plus.</li>
</ul>
