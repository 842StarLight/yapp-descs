<h2>Marketing Associate</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>Magnolia Capital is currently seeking a Marketing Associate for its newly formed property management company, <b>STYL Residential</b>. The Marketing Associate oversees the marketing efforts for the STYL Residential portfolio of apartment communities. This individual works directly with property teams and Operations leaders to develop and optimize marketing plans to help meet occupancy goals and other targets. This position provides support and training for property teams as it relates to marketing best practices. This role will work closely with the Managing Director of Property Management to build out the marketing function within the new company.</p>
<p>
This position is based in Chicago.</p>
<p><b>
Essential Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Develop and optimize property-level marketing plans by assessing local market conditions and analyzing advertising performance data. Summarize recommendations and implement changes as needed.</li>
<li>
Facilitate regular marketing calls with property teams and regional managers to ensure best practices are being followed provide additional training and support to new team members as needed.
</li>
<li>Remain knowledgeable about current marketing trends, particularly as they relate to digital advertising, AI, automation, and predictive analytics.
</li>
<li>Maintain accuracy of online content (photos, copy, special offers, etc.) for all property websites and advertising sources, and make updates as needed.
</li>
<li>Manage social media and online reputation management (Yelp, Apartment Ratings, Google Reviews, Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn, etc.).
</li>
<li>Assists with establishing and implementing social media accounts, email templates, craigslist/free online advertising templates, websites, resident portals and other on-line tools.</li>
<li>
Travel to properties as needed to help execute marketing plans, provide training for property teams, assess signage needs, and evaluate local competition.
</li>
<li>Assist in the acquisition/set up of new properties as it relates to marketing.
</li>
<li>Manage ILS Contracts.</li>
<li>
Ensure site teams are meeting assigned marketing goals inclusive of assigned Knock CRM metrics.</li>
<li>
Guides design and production of branded community collateral marketing materials (business cards, uniforms, and other STYL branded items), coordinates production with vendors and maintains tracking, invoices, etc. Assist site teams with marketing collateral, business cards, uniforms, and other STYL branded items.
 </li>
</ul>
<p><b>Who We Are Looking For:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Completed college coursework in Marketing, Communications, or a related discipline.</li>
<li>
2+ years experience in marketing.</li>
<li>
Proficiency in Microsoft Office Suite (Excel, Word, Outlook, PowerPoint).</li>
<li>
Proficiency in RealPage products (OneSite, Marketing Center, Active Building, etc.) a plus.
</li>
<li>Excellent written and verbal communication skills</li>
<li>
Exceptional organizational skills and attention to detail.</li>
<li>
Ability to thrive in a fast-paced office environment and adjust priorities based on evolving needs of the organization
</li>
<li>Demonstrated project management experience.</li>
<li>
Working knowledge of multifamily property management software (Onesite) and graphic design software (Adobe Creative Suite).</li>
<li>
Previous experience with copywriting, digital marketing channels, and/or social media management.</li>
</ul>
