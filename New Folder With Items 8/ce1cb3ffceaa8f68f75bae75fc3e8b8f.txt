<h2>Director, Integrated Marketing</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<b>Job Title:</b> Director, Integrated Marketing
<br><br>
<b>FSLA:</b> Classification: Exempt
<br><br>
<b>Reports To:</b> Chief Revenue Officer
<br><br>
<b>Location:</b> New York
<br><br>
<b>Summary / Objective:</b><br>
The Director, Integrated Marketing will identify and prioritize campaigns, promotions, and strategies to drive demand generation and sales enablement to our key audiences. We are looking for someone who possesses a passion for marketing, demand generation and data-driven decision making with a high level of energy seeking to grow and learn in an exciting and rewarding team environment.
<br><br>
<b>Responsibilities:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Support sales, marketing and new business opportunities with innovative and strategic sales strategies and tactics.</li>
<li>Support brand position as innovative healthcare company and top-tier point of care brand through insight-driven marketing.</li>
<li>Manage and or/collaborate with internal creative studio team staff and ensure quality of design, marketing, advertising, provider and sales support creative and materials.</li>
<li>Work in conjunction with communications team to develop and manage social strategy for editorial and marketing social strategy.</li>
<li>Partner closely with business partner leads on strategy development in order to create recommendations of marketing support approaches.</li>
<li>Oversee agency and/or vendor management for key campaign projects, ensuring strategic briefing and assessment of creative development aligns with the brand.</li>
<li>Work closely with product team to develop and support key launches and initiatives.</li>
<li>Drive Outcome Health awareness and leadership via promotional programming.</li>
<li>Oversee campaign planning, development and accurate execution of promotional</li>
</ul>
Qualifications and Experience
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>5+ years developing highly digital integrated marketing communication and demand generations strategies and campaigns in a B2B technology environment.</li>
<li>3+ years in digital healthcare marketing.</li>
<li>Experience with customer relationship management (CRM) systems (i.e. Dynamics 365, Sales Force, etc.)</li>
<li>Demonstrated ability to manage multiple projects simultaneously and to create and maintain project lists and timetables for a large team with strong attention to detail.</li>
</ul>
