<p><b>Job Description</b></p>
<p>
Akili is expanding its Cloud Solutions practice, and we need qualified candidates to help us grow! If you have strong analytical skills and enjoy creating complex financial models, we want you to join our team. Whether your background is in financial planning and analysis, demand planning, sales and operations planning, workforce planning or actuarial planning, we need problem solvers who enjoy building planning solutions.</p>
<p>
Anaplan is a cloud-based planning platform that is disrupting the world of enterprise planning management. Akili is a premier Anaplan partner with expertise in helping our clients implement complex planning solutions using the Anaplan platform. The Anaplan Modeler will be trained to use the Anaplan platform to build planning models, then join other Akili planning team members to build solutions for our clients. We work in teams. We need people who are goal oriented and work well with others to achieve successful outcomes for our clients.</p>
<p><b>
Basic Qualifications:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s Degree in Finance or Information Systems or related discipline</li>
<li>Required 2+ years’ experience as a financial analyst or financial systems developer</li>
<li>Strong experience using advanced financial formulas to build complex financial models in MS Excel</li>
<li>Good understanding of general accounting and financial principles</li>
<li>Detail oriented and commitment to building high-quality solutions</li>
<li>Eagerness to learn and work with a dynamic, fun team</li>
<li>Experience implementing software applications a plus</li>
<li>Previous experience with Anaplan a plus</li>
<li>Ability to travel as required</li>
</ul>
