Legal
<br><br>
We seek team members who share our interest in engaging legal issues and who apply a pragmatic, creative, and thoughtful approach when providing legal advice to our business teams. As an internet business, we often operate on the leading edge of the legal sphere, where the law struggles to keep up with technology and the rules get fuzzy. This makes our work very interesting. So, if this is something you'd be passionate about and you want to work in a hands-on start-up environment we'd love to have you on board.
<br><br>
The Role
<br><br>
SurveyMonkey is seeking a bright, talented attorney to join its legal team. We are looking for a Senior Corporate Counsel - Commercial Transactions with experience in SaaS business issues and transactions. This is an exciting opportunity to be involved in helping to scale a growing internet business. This role will require you to focus on enterprise commercial transactions and will involve extensive collaboration with members of SurveyMonkey's sales, finance, operations, and executive teams.
<br><br>
<b>A successful candidate will:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Have primary responsibility for negotiating, drafting and advising on all IT contracts, including SaaS Enterprise Agreements and ancillary documentation related to sales deals.</li>
<li>Proactively provide training and ongoing support to internal teams on sales and contractual matters, including developing internal playbooks and training.</li>
<li>Collaborate closely with internal clients and key stakeholders (including the sales, sales operations, security, finance, and management teams) by providing legal advice on relevant legal topics.</li>
<li>Negotiate and advise on customer and vendor privacy related agreements.</li>
<li>Actively support the Procurement team to navigate complex contractual negotiations, including security and privacy related matters.</li>
<li>Gain in-depth product knowledge of all SurveyMonkey products and services.</li>
<li>Take the initiative to recommend and implement improvements to commercial policies, procedures, and standard form contracts.</li>
<li>Build strong relationships with and support other members of the legal team as required.</li>
</ul>
Requirements
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>7+ years of relevant experience in a top-tier law firm and/or in-house in a technology company.</li>
<li>Law degree from a reputable institution with a strong academic record.</li>
<li>Excellent negotiation, drafting and communication skills with the ability to foster relationships and collaborate with internal clients.</li>
<li>Strong general commercial contracting background with experience of IT and SaaS contracts preferred.</li>
<li>Experience with and a demonstrated ability to negotiate data-privacy related agreements. Ability to give succinct, commercially driven legal advice.</li>
<li>Razor sharp organizational skills, with the ability to manage multiple projects and drive them through to completion within required timeframes.</li>
<li>Works well autonomously and in a multidisciplinary team environment.</li>
<li>Strong communication skills, both verbal and written.</li>
<li>Experience with lease and other commercial real estate matters a plus.</li>
</ul>
