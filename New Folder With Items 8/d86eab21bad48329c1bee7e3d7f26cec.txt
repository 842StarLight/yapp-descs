<h2>Sr. Overdraft & Fee Income Program Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>This senior subject matter expert is responsible for overseeing all phases of the overdraft program including monitoring the program from a financial standpoint and customer behavior trends. This position will also be responsible for managing fee income generated from Branch Banking deposit products. This position is responsible for the overdraft and fee income products for all three regions of branch banking, encompassing 160 depositories and approximately 500,000 accounts. The Senior Overdraft &amp; Fee Income Program Manager is responsible for the regulatory compliant products that generate approximately $35 million of non-interest income each year, 20% of revenue for the retail bank annually.
<br>
<br>
To ensure appropriate overdraft programs for the Bank, this position will:
<br>
<ul><li>Solely responsible for partnering with key stakeholders enterprise-wide to effectively
</li></ul>manage the following areas of the program: operational administration, core system(s)
<br>
execution, frontline training and coaching, customer/banker support, reporting, and risk &amp;
<br>
compliance.
<br>
<ul><li>Regularly report out the program performance by collecting, creating, analyzing, and
</li></ul>presenting documents and reports to the Overdraft Steering Committee and management.
<br>
<ul><li>Serve as a leader within the Bank by chairing of the Overdraft Program Steering Committee
</li></ul>Program with leadership across the enterprise.
<br>
<ul><li>Ensure outcomes encompass fair and responsible customer treatment, and transparent
</li></ul>disclosures by having subject matter expertise of all regulations related to overdraft
<br>
