<h2>Agile Software Development Business Analyst</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Job Description:</b></p>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Facilitate discussions related to developing technical requirements for systems that support business processes; translate business requirements into technical requirements</li>
<li>Actively participate in the creation of technical requirements; at times, owning the publication of those requirements
</li>
<li>Developing use cases to explain/ demonstrate specifications to business partners</li>
<li>Support ad hoc report and/or ad hoc strategic efforts/projects</li>
<li>Support weekly, monthly and quarterly routines</li>
<li>Partnering closely with technology team to elicit and document the functional requirements, generating process/data flow diagrams, and crafting user guides</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Desired Experience</b></p>
<ul>
<li>8+ years process design and/or technology development</li>
<li>Strong business partnering experience / skills including the ability to interact, communicate and influence equally well at all levels of the organization</li>
<li>Ability to deliver high quality work in a dynamic environment with overlapping deadlines and tight time constraints
</li>
<li>Ability to effectively juggle multiple high priority activities and deliver in a fast paced environment</li>
<li>Strong data analytics skills</li>
<li>MS Office proficiency (Outlook, Excel, Power Point, Word, Visio); ability to quickly learn how to use new tools</li>
<li>Proven ability to think strategically and execute tactically</li>
<li>Motivated self-starter who can deliver results with minimal direction</li>
<li>Highly organized and motivated to deliver results with minimal direction
</li>
<li>Creative and proactive problem solver – ability to understand what the team needs and offer suggestions above and beyond what they desire
</li>
<li>Naturally curious individual with the ability to quickly become the authority in the various procedures, data and systems used by the team</li>
<li>Experience writing user stories for Agile development (user story and acceptance criteria)</li>
<li>Knowledge of software acceptance testing including regression testing</li>
<li>Experience with end-to-end Change Management and Delivery</li>
<li>Working knowledge of Agile or Waterfall methodology and a deep understanding of various phases of the Software Development Life Cycle</li>
<li>prior software development/coding experience a plus</li>
<li>Information Security, Auditing, or Control Function background is desired, but not required
</li>
<li>Bachelor’s degree from an accredited institution (Not Required but you can list as desired or helpful. Also, equivalent/related work experience)<br>
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>

1st shift (United States of America)<p></p>
