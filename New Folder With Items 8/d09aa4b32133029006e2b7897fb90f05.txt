<p><b>Job Summary</b></p>
<p>
This Account Rep- Retail Space Support position contributes to the development and growth of ALL our brands and packages in three primary ways. 1) Constant communication with Sales and Marketing counterparts to assure WP Beverage Plan-o-Gram integrity is maintained within all channels of business. 2) Weekly account level focus on improving our presence and location within beverage sections and cold vault sets. 3) This position also is a resource for best practice sharing around current merchandising trends with internal team members. The Account Rep-Retail Space Support position is required to be on-site and physically complete all sets, and is responsible to order various merchandising materials including shelving, glides, etc. Every member of the WP Team is a sales person for our brands and company. Therefore, the Retail Space Coordinator must be focused on providing WP Beverages with profitable growth through the sale and efficient distribution of our refreshing beverages, superior customer service and innovative customer focused marketing programs.</p>
