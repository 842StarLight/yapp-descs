<h2>Sales Associate</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p>Our Sales team is responsible for introducing our one-of-a-kind products to community-centered business owners and set appointments for our Sales Executives to convert them into future CGI clients. A solid over-the-phone handshake game is a must! Don't worry, hand stretching breaks are encouraged.</p>
<p><b>For a Sales Representative, a typical day might look like…</b></p>
<ul><li>Channeling your inner detective and conducting market research in your assigned communities to target local business prospects.</li></ul>
<p>-Calling potential clients and getting them excited about CGI’s exclusive community partnerships and amazing mobile-digital marketing strategies that could potentially change the way they do business forever (NO cold calling - we work off of referrals and warm leads!)</p>
<ul><li>High-fiving your Sales Executive on a Friday afternoon after a having stellar week.</li></ul>
<p><b>Perks...</b></p>
<ul><li>Uncapped monthly commissions! (trust us, these bring a BIG smile to your face)</li></ul>
<p>-TRAVEL - you can earn the opportunity to travel every month</p>
<ul><li>Amazing team culture</li></ul>
<ul><li>Quick upward mobility into a Sales Executive position (within the first year)</li></ul>
<ul><li>All the coffee you can drink (you know, for the days you might need it!)</li></ul>
<p><b>This job might be for you if…</b></p>
<ul><li>You WANT to be in sales!</li></ul>
<ul><li>You enjoy helping people and you care about digital advertising. CGI is all about progressive technology and official community partnerships.</li></ul>
<ul><li>You have the ability to juggle multiple projects at one time with ease and grace (they won't be on fire, we promise).</li></ul>
<ul><li>You enjoy earning uncapped performance commissions</li></ul>
<ul><li>You possess strong written and verbal communication skills (texting does not count).</li></ul>
<ul><li>You have the ability to deliver a strong handshake over the phone.</li></ul>
