<h2>Accountant 1</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>We are currently seeking an Entry-Level Accountant for our NYC office. The position is responsible for accounts payable functions as well as processing expense reports to employees.</p>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Essential Duties and Responsibilities</b></h3>
<ul>
<li>
Maintain petty cash accounts and log all outgoing disbursements</li>
<li>
Monitor all vendor payment agreements to find discounts that can be used to reduce invoice amounts</li>
<li>
Responsible for paying all sales tax on appropriate invoices and making a note of each tax payment made</li>
<li>
Keep track of credits owed to the company and ensure all credits are properly applied to vendor payments</li>
<li>
Reconcile payable reports each month to confirm that all amounts paid were accurate</li>
<li>
Process payments to vendors</li>
<li>
Upload files to the firm’s banking institution</li>
<li>
Process expense report payments to employees</li>
</ul>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Requirements</b></h3>
<ul>
<li>
Bachelor’s Degree in Accounting, Finance or related discipline</li>
<li>
0 to 2 years of experience</li>
<li>
Ability to use Microsoft productivity software such as Excel and Word. Experience with Deltek Vision a plus</li>
<li>
Excellent attention to detail</li>
<li>
Strong organizational ability</li>
<li>
Effective corporate communications skills</li>
<li>
Ability to consistently meet all deadlines</li>
</ul>
