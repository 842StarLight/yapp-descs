<h2>Retail Space Planner</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<div>
<p>We have a wonderful opportunity to join our City Furniture Marketing team as a Retail Space Planner. The Retail Space Planner is accountable for planning product layout and collection transitions in City Furniture and Ashley Furniture Home Store showrooms and assisting in determining optimal product assortment. In addition, this position is well suited for a candidate who is detail oriented and an analytical thinker, with an aptitude for working collaboratively on multiple projects and building relationships across the organization.</p>
<p>The Retail Space Planner is responsible for the creation of product layout in AutoCAD for showroom remerchandising and new store opening projects utilizing visual and merchandising standards. Our Retail Space Planner Conducts quarterly evaluations of product lineup per showroom, collaborating with the buying office, visual merchandising and sales GM’s.</p>
<p>The ideal candidate is looking for a company to build a career. With 28 City Furniture and Ashley Home Store locations throughout Florida, City Furniture continues to be recognized as one of the top furniture companies in South Florida and in the top 30 nationwide. A place where you can be yourself, find success and grow into the career you've always dreamed about. The opportunity requires a 40-hour workweek Monday through Friday in a dynamic corporate office environment. Occasional travel to showrooms is required. The City Furniture team is excited about the future. Join our fun, family-spirited team to build a long-lasting career at a company that will continue to challenge, develop, and appreciate its Associates.</p>
