<h2>Actuary</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p><b>JOB SUMMARY:</b>
</p>
<p>Sharing with peer actuary, responsibilities related to reserving, pricing, reinsurance, and data analysis support for Underwriting, Claims, Marketing, Sales, Accounting and IT. Will work collaboratively across all teams and departments within Olympus Insurance Company to understand business process and changes in business environment.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
ESSENTIAL FUNCTIONS:</b></p>
<p>
The list of essential functions below is intended to be representative of the tasks performed within this job. Other duties may be assigned based on business need.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Comprehensive monthly reserve analysis. Related tasks include data validation and reinsurance accounting.</li>
<li>Provide rate filing report</li>
<li>Review work product of peer actuary</li>
<li> Recurring and ad-hoc reporting for production, retention, exposure management, loss trends, etc.</li>
<li> Granular profitability analyses to support Underwriting, Marketing and Sales initiatives.</li>
<li> Participate in developing reinsurance program and monitoring risk profile.</li>
<li> Rating and underwriting research – identify and analyze variables and algorithms for improved risk segmentation</li>
<li> Work with vendors and regulators as necessary to ensure expeditious regulatory approval of company filings. Assist product development team on efforts to obtain regulatory approval for new products.</li>
<li> Research and stay current on program changes and rate revisions that impact Marketing, Policy Services, Claims and financial results</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p>
</p>
<p><b>MINIMUM QUALIFICATIONS:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree</li>
<li>ACAS, + Exam 7 or FCAS</li>
<li>10+ years of actuarial experience which includes both reserving and ratemaking</li>
<li>Advanced skill in the use of Microsoft Office products (Word, Outlook, and Excel)</li>
<li>Intermediate SQL programming skills</li>
<li>Homeowners Insurance experience required</li>
<li>Reinsurance knowledge</li>
</ul>
<p>In addition to meeting the minimum qualifications listed above, an individual must be able to perform each of the established essential functions to perform this job successfully.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
KNOWLEDGE, SKILLS, AND ABILITIES:</b></p>
<ul>
<li> Knowledge of products and concepts relating to the Florida homeowners market
</li> <li>Experience with mapping software a plus.
</li> <li>Experience with mapping software a plus</li>
<li> Extensive experience with predictive modeling in Florida personal property
</li> <li>Programming in R a plus
</li> <li>Skill in adapting to a changing work environment, competing demands and ability to deal with frequent change, delays or unexpected events
</li>
<li>Ability to establish and maintain effective and collaborative working relationships with those contacted in the course of work</li>
<li>Ability to read, analyze, and interpret program related laws and regulations and establish necessary work processes and procedures</li>
<li>Ability to exercise good judgement and maintain confidentiality in maintaining critical and sensitive information, records and reports</li>
<li>Ability to use critical thinking skills to arrive at solutions and suggest improvements to processes</li>
<li>Ability to prepare clear, accurate and concise financial records and reports</li>
<li>Ability to manage multiple priorities to ensure that deadlines are met</li>
<li>Ability to communicate effectively verbally and in writing</li>
<li>Ability to organize work for timely completion</li>
<br>
</ul>
