<h2>Professional Services Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Position Information</b></p>
<p>SIMCO has an immediate opportunity for a talented Professional Services Manager who wants to have a measurable impact on the successful growth of SIMCO’s Service Management Software as a Service (SaaS). As our customer base continues to grow rapidly, we’re looking for a Professional Services Manager to manage our team, lead and execute customer implementations, and ensure customer satisfaction to maximize annual recurring revenue.</p>
<p>The Professional Services Manager is responsible for managing the team to execute the end-to-end SaaS customer onboarding process while partnering with the sales organization to ensure customer satisfaction to secure the software renewal. The Professional Services Manger will lead the team and provide sales support to capture customers, generate quotations, and SOWs. The Professional Services Manager is the customer’s advocate and communicates customer enhancement requests to the software development team. The Professional Services Manager provides ongoing management, training, and other related tasks to ensure the professional services team delights existing customers and expand software’s annual recurring revenue.</p>
<p>This opportunity offers all the benefits of a start-up environment within an established and profitable company that serves a diverse and growing global customer base. SIMCO promotes work/life balance and offers a rich set of employee benefits.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Responsibilities and Duties</b></p>
<p>Customer Responsibilities</p>
<ul>
<li>Delight the customer with an exceptional software implementation experience</li>
<li>Deliver customer satisfaction to ensure securing of the software renewal</li>
<li>Be the customer’s advocate and communicates customer enhancement requests to the software development team</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p>Manager Responsibilities</p>
<ul>
<li>Manage professional services team and provide team leadership</li>
<li>Develop team members to perform expected tasks &amp; become future leaders</li>
<li>Hire &amp; expand the team as sales forecast exceeds team capacity</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p>Professional Services Team Responsibilities</p>
<ul>
<li>Lead all aspects of software customer onboarding</li>
<li>Clarify customer requirements, configure the solution to meet those requirements, define customer workflows, and upload data</li>
<li>Develop user training material and deliver user training</li>
<li>Provide post-implementation support and issue resolution</li>
<li>Develop SOWs, cost models and quotations for custom configurations</li>
<li>Successfully deploy tailored solutions based on SOWs</li>
<li>Demonstrate software solutions and features to software customer prospects</li>
<li>Provide sales support and quotations in timely manner</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Other Responsibilities</b></p>
<p><b>Marketing Support</b> – be the subject matter expert on software configuration to support the development of marketing content and videos</p>
<p><b>Solution Development</b> – Identify and package new configuration solutions for sales to offer customers</p>
<p><b>Collaboration</b> – collaborate with other SIMCO business development team members</p>
<p><b>Software Proposals</b> – support marketing and software sales team proposal development</p>
<p><b>Client-Ready Presentations</b> – develop scripted narratives in collaboration with marketing to deliver customer implementation presentations</p>
<p><b>Reporting</b> – manage and communicate software customer metrics on sales support, quotes, implementations, satisfaction, etc…</p>
<p><b>Customer Satisfaction Tracking</b> – develop and implement a customer satisfaction tracking system to provide advance notice of customer dissatisfaction</p>
<p><b>Promotional Campaigns</b> – work closely with marketing team to develop email/web material</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Qualifications</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Team player with close collaboration between customers, sales, and software development</li>
<li>Strong organizational and time management skills</li>
<li>Ability to balance multiple projects simultaneously and stay on top of all details</li>
<li>Excellent business, technical, and presentation communication skills</li>
<li>Self-starter who learns quickly and is able to work independently</li>
<li>Experience generating SOWs to meet customer requirements and implementing tailored solutions</li>
<li>Knowledge of web application architecture and technologies</li>
<li>Positive attitude and customer-centric</li>
<li>1-2 years’ experience deploying SaaS solutions and end-user training and support</li>
<li>Enterprise Customer Success experience</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>How Will Success Be Measured</b></p>
<p>Team and Individual performance to deliver:</p>
<ul>
<li>Retaining customer’s annual recurring revenue</li>
<li>Professional Services revenue generated</li>
<li>Completion of value add projects</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Working Environment and Travel</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Office environment, some U.S. and International travel required to deploy customer solutions and end-user training (15%)</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>What We Offer</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Full-time, exempt position</li>
<li>Excellent benefits package includes; medical, dental, vision, disability, life insurance, 401(k) with company matching, employee funded pre-tax health, child care spending accounts and tuition reimbursement</li>
<li>Vacation, sick and paid holidays</li>
<br>
</ul>
