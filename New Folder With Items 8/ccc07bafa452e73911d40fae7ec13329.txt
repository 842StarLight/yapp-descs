<p></p>As a Personal Mortgage Advisor (Outside), the employee’s primary duties involve making sales or obtaining orders or contracts for services or for the use of facilities for which a consideration will be paid by the client or customer. Employee will be procuring and originating consumer-based residential mortgage loans. Employee will solicit, receive, and originate applications for the extension of credit to purchase and refinance 1-4 unit residential properties, secured by a first-lien mortgage or deed of trust, and provide supporting service to consumer applicants. Ensures exceptional customer experience by overseeing loan processing from origination to closing, and providing on-going communication to customers.
