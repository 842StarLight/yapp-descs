<h2>Marketing Automation Email Program Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">Projectline is looking for a contract Marketing Automation Programs Specialist to own marketing campaign execution; enhancing customer and prospect engagement programs, ensuring the quality of marketing campaign communications, and providing Marketing Automation support to the global marketing team for one of our technology clients in Seattle.
<br><br>
Are you a Marketing Automation professional looking for your next role? Do you have plenty of experience using marketing technology? Do you want to love where you work? If yes, then we would love to hire you! You will have the opportunity to work with one of our biggest clients and grow your career and knowledge within this role.
<br><br>
<b>Primary Responsibilities (Essential Functions):</b><br>
<ul>
<li>You will plan, design and execute email marketing communications; in order to execute end-to-end campaigns.</li>
<li>You will partner with the design team to improve engagement within marketing emails.</li>
<li>Use Marketing Automation tools to deliver timely and meaningful communication to audience segmentation you have identified and run through QA.</li>
<li>Build reporting around program performance, week over week engagement and year over year performance, using data visualization tools.</li>
<li>You will maintain data integrity of within marketing systems and provide standard methodologies around email privacy and compliance.</li>
<li>You will provide consultation and education around email Marketing scheduling, frequency, and retention strategies.</li>
<li>You will develop and support the email program for the clients annual conference, being the liaison between the events team and Marketing Automation team.</li>
<li>You will build and document process for delivering and optimizing effective campaigns programs.</li>
</ul>
<b>Knowledge and Skill Requirements:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>You have deep technical knowledge of marketing operations, automation, data flows, and campaign execution as well as best practices for sales and marketing processes.</li>
<li>Excellent written and oral communication skills.</li>
<li>Ability to think both strategically and tactically and comfortable doing both kinds of work.</li>
<li>Proven track record writing customer-facing content that is on-brand, relevant, and tailored to the target audience.</li>
<li>Ability to analyze data, seek opportunities, and assess the efficacy of marketing programs.</li>
</ul>
<b>Education/Experience:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Bachelors degree in Business or Marketing related field, or meaningful work experience</li>
<li>3-5+ years experience in marketing/marketing operations</li>
<li>Experience with email marketing and automation software preferably Eloqua</li>
<li>Experience with CRM and/or relational databases preferably Salesforce</li>
<li>Experienced with HTML and other programming languages</li>
<li>Familiar with Tableau Software or another data visualization platform</li>
</ul>
