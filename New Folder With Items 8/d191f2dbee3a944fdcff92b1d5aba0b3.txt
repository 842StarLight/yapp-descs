<h2>Accounting/Administrative Assistant</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p>American Concrete Company (ACC), a related entity to Hamilton Construction Co., is based in Coburg, OR and is a specialty construction contractor. ACC has been in business since 1984 and is reputable, respected and licensed in 13 Western States. ACC offers a fast-paced but casual work environment where self-starters have the opportunity to succeed. With continued growth, a new, part-time, position – <b>Accounting/Administrative Assistant</b> — is needed, to help our current Contracts Administrator and Payroll Administrator with their duties. Also, when time allows, to help the dispatcher schedule jobs when out on job sites.
</p>
<p><b>Responsibilities:</b></p>
<p>
<b>Financial and Accounting:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Accounts receivable Invoicing and Accounts payable Processing</li>
<li>Cost coding of payable</li>
<li>Generate and distribute monthly financial
</li>
<li>Account reconciliations and adjusting journal entries</li>
<li>Prepare monthly sales/use tax report</li>
<li>File quarterly tax reports by county and/or state</li>
<li>Provide documentation for auditors</li>
<li>Generate 1099 s</li>
</ul>
<p><b>General Office Management:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Maintain business licenses and/or registrations in various cities, counties, states as
</li>
<li>Maintain records and licensing for equipment</li>
<li>Assist with website content and/or coordinate website updates</li>
<li>Office equipment – computers, copiers, cell phones – assure maintenance, replacement, service
</li>
<li>Notarize documents., general mailing and postal activities</li>
<li>Assist with job scheduling, helping the dispatcher at times</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Human Resources:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Assure employee handbook updates and distribution</li>
<li>Receive and distribute employment applications</li>
<li>Coordinate new hire on-boarding and orientation training</li>
</ul>
<p><b>The ideal candidate is or has:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>A detail-oriented multitask-er with excellent oral/written communication skills and some payroll experience</li>
<li>A strong accounting background and experience</li>
<li>Strong interpersonal skills to work collaboratively and effectively with management, customers, and field workers</li>
<li>A notary or have the ability to become one with the help of the company</li>
<li>Able to pass a background check and has an acceptable past work history and record, given the sensitive nature of the position in regards to payroll and human resources records</li>
<li>Proficient with MS office, Word, Excel, Outlook, Viewpoint or similar accounting / job costing software</li>
<li>Somewhat familiar with construction—helpful but not required</li>
<li>A valid Oregon driver's license
</li>
</ul>
<p>Position is part-time. There is a potential for full time, depending on the candidates skill set.</p>
