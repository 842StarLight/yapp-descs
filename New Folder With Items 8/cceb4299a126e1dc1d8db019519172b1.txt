<b>Investment Analyst - </b><b>Omaha, NE</b>
</div>
<div></div>
<div><b>NorthMarq is seeking an Investment Analyst to support the Production office in Omaha, NE. We provide capital solutions for owners of commercial real estate. With regional offices coast-to-coast, we are the nation’s largest privately held servicer and provider of commercial real estate debt and equity. We continually establish new standards of excellence in serving a nationwide client base while focusing on the importance of relationships and service.</b></div>
<div></div>
<div><b><i>**Inquiries about this position, please contact Maria Sather (in Human Resources) at NorthMarq Capital.</i></b></div>
<div><b><i>**All candidates interested must apply online to be considered.</i></b></div>
<div></div>
<div><b>Position:</b></div>
<div><b>The Analyst will work with a group of senior analysts building expertise in commercial real estate and commercial mortgages. The analyst team support four mortgage bankers in developing investment packages for distribution to institutional investors. This entails running pro forma analysis on commercial real estate (apartments, office buildings, retail, and industrial), valuation, analysis of borrowers, analysis of real estate markets / economic data, and an investment narrative explaining strengths and weaknesses of debt and equity transactions. The analyst will have the opportunity to learn all aspects of commercial real estate, finance, economics, and supporting client relationships.</b></div>
<div></div>
<div>
<div><b>Responsibilities:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
<div>
<b>Organization </b>– Responsible for providing support to Omaha office through underwriting, loan request/investment report packaging, due diligence, closing, servicing and various support functions.</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>
<b>Planning </b>– Plans and schedules own workload to best utilize time and most efficiently provide commercial real estate research, analysis, packaging and loan closing services.</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>
<b>Underwriting</b> – Prepares underwriting of commercial real estate properties based on historical financial statements, projections, and market standards to determine a projected income, value and loan amount.</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>
<b>Packaging</b> - Prepares investment reports in compliance with company guidelines and investor requirements. These reports include analysis of the borrower, property, underwriting, valuation, location, market, leases/rent roll, photographs, aerials, maps, etc.</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>
<b>Market Research</b> – Gathers site, neighborhood, and city data including demographics, sales and rent comparables, absorption surveys and occupancy studies.</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>
<b>Closing</b> – Provides loan closing coordination including collecting due diligence items, ordering third party reports, coordinating with all parties including borrowers, lenders and legal counsels, and acting as liaison with the servicing department.</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>
<b>Servicing</b> – Performs regional office servicing functions including annual inspections, investor portfolio analysis, lease reviews/briefs, and normal routine regional office servicing duties through local market knowledge.</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>
<b>Customer Service</b> – Responsible for providing investors and borrowers with prompt quality service in a friendly and professional manner.</div>
</li>
</ul>
<div><b>QUALIFICATIONS</b></div>
<ul>
<li>One to Three years’ experience in Commercial Real Estate Finance preferred; appraisal/valuation background also considered.</li>
<li>Four-year college degree required with a major in real estate, finance, accounting or business area preferred</li>
<li>Demonstrated analytical skills with ability to calculate NOI, DSC, ROC, IRR, etc.</li>
<li>Strong computer knowledge and skills, including in-depth expert knowledge of Microsoft Office (Excel, Word, PowerPoint etc.) and graphic software in relation to real estate (knowledge of ARGUS software a plus)</li>
<li>Strong organizational skills with the ability to multi-task while under pressure</li>
<li>Customer service oriented</li>
</ul>
<div></div>

<li>Demonstrated ability to work independently with minimal supervision</li>
<li>Demonstrated work ethic and willingness to work extended hours when necessary</li>
<li>Demonstrated ability to handle multiple projects and assignments; able to prioritize and meet deadlines</li>
<li>Effective oral and written communication skills</li>
<li>Problem-solving skills to reflect level of responsibilities</li>
