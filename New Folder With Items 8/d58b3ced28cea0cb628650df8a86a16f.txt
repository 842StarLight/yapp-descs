<h2>Customer Service Representative</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Position overview:</b></p>
<p>
We’re seeking for a driven, self-starter <b>Customer Service Representative</b> to join our dynamic organization.</p>
<p>
Acting as a customer advocate within Optimas for assigned Customer accounts, the Customer Service Representative is responsible for ensuring the customer continually receives professional world-class service from all Optimas departments and personnel.</p>
<p>
If you are looking for a fast paced environment with lots of opportunity for personal and career growth then this is the ideal place for you!</p>
<p><b>
Main Responsibilities</b></p>
<p>
The<b> Customer Service Representative</b> will be responsible for the following:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Management of assigned customer account responsibilities include;</li>
<li>
Providing communication between assigned customers and all other Optimas Departments and personnel as necessary.</li>
<li>
Expediting parts for customers</li>
<li>
Working with Materials and Production Control, Plan &amp; Schedule assigned account orders.</li>
<li>
Managing and coordinating all assigned account RFQ’s</li>
<li>
Providing and documenting Feedback on Optimas relationship.</li>
<li>
Providing regular materials status reports to customer and ensuring customer inventory liabilities appropriately</li>
<li>
Providing sales information on assigned accounts such as monthly sales, bookings, on time delivery, and forecasts.</li>
<li>
Identifying new opportunities and using these to increasing sales with assigned accounts</li>
<li>
Taking appropriate actions to resolve quality issues related to service and product.</li>
<li>
Reporting to senior management account status as required</li>
<li>
Proactively interact with each account on a regular and ongoing basis, utilizing a wide array of available communication channels, to maximize their purchasing with Optimas.</li>
<li>
Research and analyze the strategic business direction and needs of each assigned account to determine how Optimas can best align itself as a product and service partner for all current and future business needs of the account.</li>
<li>
Review how Optimas can add value and help with each account’s purchasing needs.</li>
<li>
Regularly exercise independent discretion and judgment to determine whether a potential customer qualifies to do business with Optimas, as well as negotiating transaction terms, conditions and pricing for each existing and new account.</li>
<li>
Evaluate and determine the appropriate marketing and promotional approaches to grow the business and sales for each account.</li>
<li>
Coordinate and lead activities and communication between each account and Optimas business departments to achieve appropriate business solutions, respond to customers’ purchasing needs and coordinate sales.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Key Competencies:</b></p>
<p>
The ideal candidate should have the following competencies:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Strategic Vision.</li>
<li>
Building organizational capacity.</li>
<li>
Results driven.</li>
<li>
Embrace change.</li>
<li>
Collaboration and Influence.</li>
<li>
Entrepreneurial spirit.</li>
<li>
Customer value and Market focus.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Skills &amp; Qualifications</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
A. / B.S. in Business administration or any other relevant degree.</li>
<li>
At least <b>3+ </b>years of relevant working experience in a customer-facing role.</li>
<li>
Strong sales/business acumen generally attained through complex B2B selling.</li>
<li>
Expert in Customer Service. Working to achieve all department objectives and goals set by senior management, following all company policies and procedures, and fostering a positive and constructive attitude with all company personnel and customers.</li>
<li>
Knowledge of business and management principles involved in strategic planning, resource allocation, leadership technique, production methods, basic arithmetic and resources. Achieved with life experience and recipient of a High School Diploma.</li>
<li>
Good Knowledge of raw materials, production processes, quality control, costs, and other techniques for maximizing the effective manufacture and distribution of goods.</li>
<li>
Computer Knowledge— the ability to operate software and electronic devices. Intermediate knowledge of Microsoft Office products (inclusive of Excel, Access, Word and Outlook).</li>
<li>
Oral and Written Expression — Ability to solve practical problems and deal with a variety of concrete variables in situations where only limited standardization exists. Ability to interpret a variety of instructions furnished in written, oral, diagram, or schedule form.</li>
</ul>
