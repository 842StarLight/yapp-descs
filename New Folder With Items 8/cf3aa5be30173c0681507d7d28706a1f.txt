<h2>Sr Tax Systems Analyst</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>Expedia<p></p>
<p>
Who you are</p>
<ul>
<li>
Bachelor's degree in Finance, Accounting, Business or a related field.</li>
<li>
Minimum of 3-5 years of applicable Finance or Accounting experience in a technology or dynamic, travel-related organization.</li>
<li>
Proficient with all applications of the Microsoft Office suite, including: Excel, Word, MS Project, Visio, and Outlook preferred.</li>
<li>
Experience with charts, graphs and visualization of data; Tableau and Alteryx are a plus.</li>
<li>
Exemplary and proven organizational and interpersonal skills with a focus on the details.</li>
<li>
Ability to communicate highly complex and multi-faceted technology and data concepts to non-technical colleagues in a simple, clear and concise way, both in written and verbal formats.</li>
<li>
You possess a comfort and demonstrated ability to communicate to large audiences and ability to persuade and create action with peers, upwards and downwards in the organization.</li>
<li>
You take pride in excellent analytical skills, coupled with self-motivation and an ability to exceed expectations in a dynamic, results-oriented environment.</li>
<li>
Working knowledge of tax engines (Vertex) and/or prior experience with sales/hotel occupancy taxes a plus.</li>
<li>
Experience with data analysis tools including SQL, TeraData, Tableau and Business Objects.</li>
<li>
Working knowledge of software development processes with a focus on eCommerce preferred.</li>
<li>
Prior proven Finance Business Analyst and/or hands on Project Management experience preferred.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p>
What you’ll do</p>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>
You are a SQL and Python expert; have experience with related data analysis tools to deliver information and support the business</li>
<li>
You will become an expert in Expedia data and deliver against ad hoc and ongoing requests for information.</li>
<li>
Analyze changes to business process and systems throughout Expedia brands; deliver solutions to Tax related to reporting, systems, and compliance operations.</li>
<li>
Develop solutions to automate, streamline, and enhance data access, data integrity, and business processes.</li>
<li>
You have passion around improving processes and systems.</li>
<li>
Collaborate with Finance partners, business sponsors, product managers, developers, and other parties to deliver project designs and business requirements.</li>
<li>
Represent Expedia Tax Department in multi-functional business meetings concerning data and systems requirements.</li>
<li>
Prepare data to support compliance and controversy, including incorporation of multiple data sets to create an end-to-end view of data flows.</li>
<li>
Manage project delivery details, including coordinating with project team members and ensuring deadlines are achieved.</li>
<li>
Work effectively, both on your own and as part of a team; collaborative working demeanor is critical to success.</li>
<li>
You effectively mentor and develop junior staff and enjoy sharing your knowledge</li>
<li>
You possess attention to detail and passion around digging deep into data and resolving issues.</li>
<li>
You have automation experience with Alteryx, Tableau, RPA, bots is desired. A real passion for learning and applying new tools is a substitute.</li>
</ul>
