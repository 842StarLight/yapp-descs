<h2>Account Executive, Senior</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Overview
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>Responsible for selling Canon's hardware and software technology-based solutions within an assigned account list.
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities
</b></h2>
<div><div><ul>
<li>Develops strategies to penetrate accounts with the key decision makers within assigned account list. The focus is on placing Canon equipment and solutions in new accounts.</li>
<li>Reports customer activity to management identifying: customer requirements, competitive trends, and changing environments.</li>
<li>Develops strategic plans to address customer's requirements on a local basis. Strategy should include short term as well as long-term goals.</li>
<li>Provides marketing, technical and administrative support to the named accounts' internal departments to ensure outstanding relations and excellent customer support in all facets of daily activity.</li>
<li>Develops plans to introduce/place Canon technology, service, software, and 3rd party solutions to address customer requirements.</li>
<li>Establishes high level relationships with customer base that will enhance long term working partnerships.</li>
<li>New market share is gained through strategic prospecting and ability to meet customer requirements by utilizing all resources available efficiently.
</li>
</ul></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Qualifications
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>Bachelor's degree in a relevant field or equivalent experience required, plus 7 years of related experience.<br>
<ul>
<li>Minimum 3 years office technology, business to business, outside sales experience.</li>
<li>Strong communication skills including the desire to build solid working relationships.</li>
<li>An interest in learning new technology in an evolving industry.</li>
<li>The ability to work autonomously and excellent time management skills.</li>
<li>May require a significant amount of travel (valid driver's license and acceptable driving record necessary).
</li>
</ul>
</div></div></div>
