<h2>Strategic Alliance Specialist/ Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p><b>Business Title</b>: Strategic Alliance Manager<br>
<b>Location</b>: Milpitas, CA
</p>
<p><b>Description</b><br>
Based in Silicon Valley, ZL Technologies (ZL) is an industry leader in enterprise governance and analytics software. ZL Unified Archive (ZL UA) is the industry’s most comprehensive information governance solution, combining eDiscovery, Compliance, Records Management, Storage Optimization and Analytics under one unified system. ZL’s ability to provide such a unique offering is rooted in its history, its dedication to the corporate vision and its commitment to attracting and retaining high caliber talent.
</p>
<p><b>Responsibilities</b>
</p>
<ul>
<li>Develop, implement, and manage alliance-based offers/relationships with several large strategic alliance partners in support of ZL strategies.</li>
<li>Lead the development of a joint business plan, value proposition and legal contracts, and gain commitment from stakeholders.</li>
<li>Manage alliance launch(es) and drive the enablement of the alliance including completing the joint offer, work with partner on developing sales kits, training, and marketing and communications plans.</li>
<li>Attract qualified alliance partners to participate with ZL on selected initiatives, understand alliance partner needs and partnering drivers, manage and help ensure fulfillment of expectations.</li>
<li>Support ZL and partner sales team to follow the agreed rules of engagement and guide lead generation and management activities for the joint offer.</li>
<li>Manage alliance governance though executive contact facilitation, results and operations reviews, and frequent communication between the parties.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Qualifications</b>
</p>
<ul>
<li>5+ years of experience in solutions selling and consultative business development; three years of experience managing strategic alliances preferred; information governance experience a definite plus.</li>
<li>Bachelor’s degree in a related field from accredited college/university; MBA desirable.</li>
<li>Demonstrated success in building and nurturing win-win alliance relationships from planning through execution.</li>
<li>Advanced alliance management certification such as Association of Strategic Alliance Professionals CAAM or CSAP preferred.</li>
<li>Strong networking and influence management skills that translate into building commitment and driving actions across organizational boundaries; ability to work well with sales organization.</li>
<li>Highly credible communicator with excellent organization and project management skills and track record of delivering results.</li>
<li>Adaptability and flexibility in the face of changing circumstances; natural curiosity and ability to both listen well and delve to the fundamental business drivers.</li>
</ul>
