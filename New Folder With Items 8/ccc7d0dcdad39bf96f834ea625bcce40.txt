<h2>Complex Marketing Manager - sbe Miami</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p>A global tribe of individuals, partners and progressives, devoted to creating extraordinary experiences for our community throughout our proprietary brands - <b>we are sbe</b>. Visionaries at the forefront of hospitality, cuisine, design, residences and entertainment, our lifestyle moments are forged with highly-curated and passionate service. We are committed to <i>authenticity, sophistication, mastery</i><i> </i>and<i> </i><i>innovation</i>. Our stage is the world. Our time is now.</p>
<p>
With this in mind, we foster a family environment built on values that describe our lifestyle, experiences and how we see the world. When you have a tribe such as ours and place it in and around the collection of accommodations, restaurants, nightlife, retail and gaming, you create a force that cannot be replicated by anyone else. We are an equal opportunity employer.</p>
<p><b>
Job Purpose:</b></p>
<p>
Responsible for coordinating, implementing and managing marketing programs and events that uphold the corporate image and property identity.</p>
<p><b>
Duties &amp; Functions:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Execute marketing tactics, partnerships, programming and activations.</li>
<li>Manage annual marketing activation plan and calendar focused on driving business into low &amp; shoulder seasons while enhancing the guest experience during peak seasons</li>
<li>Create a content strategy for the website, social media platforms &amp; email communications</li>
<li>Plan and communicate marketing plans and activities with operators</li>
<li>Support PR, social media and digital media tactics</li>
<li>Attend key operational meetings to ensure alignment with all company policies.</li>
<li>Perform other duties deemed necessary by management to support the organization</li>
<li>Execute marketing plans and programs in conjunction with the Sales departments to ensure profit growth</li>
<li>Manage all advertising, public relations and promotional activities</li>
</ul>
<p><b>ADDITIONAL RESPONSIBILITIES</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Communicate effectively, both verbally and in writing, to provide clear direction to the venue teams.</li>
<li>Communicate with employees using a positive and clear speaking voice, listen to and understand requests, respond with appropriate actions and provide accurate information.</li>
<li>Remain calm and alert, especially during emergency situations and/or heavy restaurant activity, serving as a role model for team and other employees. Interact with other department personnel and venue staff as needed.</li>
<li>Make decisions and take actions based on previous experience and good judgment, sometimes revising procedures to accommodate unusual situations.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>To be aware of and ensure constant compliance with all necessary operational policies including:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Health and Safety</li>
<li>Food Hygiene</li>
<li>Maintenance</li>
<li>Emergency Procedures</li>
<li>Liquor Licensing</li>
<br>
</ul>

<p><b>
SPECIFIC JOB KNOWLEDGE, SKILL AND ABILITY</b></p>
<p>
The individual must possess the following knowledge, skills and abilities and be able to explain and demonstrate that he or she can perform the essential functions of the job, with or without reasonable accommodation.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor's degree in Marketing, Advertising, Public Relations or related field is strongly desired but not necessary</li>
<li>Three to Five (3-5) years related experience marketing, advertising or public relations, preferably in an upscale or lifestyle brand hotel</li>
<li>Must have comprehensive knowledge of food and beverage, catering sales, conference service and marketing management experience, guest relations and etiquette</li>
<li>Basic understanding of legal contract language</li>
<li>Proven team leader with a high level of energy and motivation with a proven track record of living the company's values</li>
<li>An intermediate to proficient understanding of Computer systems such as: Opera, Microsoft Word, Excel &amp; Outlook is preferred</li>
<li>Enter and locate work related information using computers and/or point of sale systems</li>
<li>Ability to spend extended lengths of time viewing a computer screen</li>
<li>Possess a gracious, friendly, and fun demeanor</li>
<li>Ability to multitask, work in a fast-paced environment and have a high level attention to detail</li>
<li>Maintain positive and productive working relationships with other employees and departments</li>
<li>Ability to work independently and to partner with others to promote an environment of teamwork</li>
<li>Must be able to stand or walk a minimum eight-hour shift.</li>
<li>Must be able observant and quick to respond to various situations while also multitasking and handling stressful situations.</li>
<li>Must be able to twist, tow (push or pull), reach, bend climb and carry objects as necessary.</li>
<li>Must have excellent communication skills and be able to read, write, speak and understand English.</li>
<li>Must be able to work inside and outside at all times of the year as needed, based upon business volumes.</li>
<li>Knowledge of federal, state and local laws governing equal employment opportunity and civil rights, occupational safety and health, wage and hour issues, and labor relations</li>
<br>
</ul>
