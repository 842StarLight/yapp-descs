Job Description
<br>At KS StateBank, our team is focused on creating positive experiences for our clients through providing incomparable knowledge, outstanding solutions, and exemplary service. With that in mind, we have an opportunity within our growing Residential Mortgage Lending team in Overland Park for a hard-working individual who aspires to succeed in a professional organization to join our team as a Residential Mortgage Systems Help Desk Assistant.
<br>The Residential Mortgage Systems Help Desk Assistant will work in a customer service oriented environment and will assist with answering questions and providing technical support to Mortgage Lending and Operations employees as they relate to mortgage processes and systems. This role also coordinates across a variety of teams including Compliance, Marketing and Mortgage Operations.
<br><br>
Familiarity with the mortgage process will help with understanding the functionality of our various mortgage software systems making it important that you are committed to learning all aspects of our mortgage systems. You will be challenged to work independently, grow in your knowledge of compliance with governing authorities as well as agency and industry guidelines when recommending solutions to current software.
<br><br>
Job Responsibilities
<br>Assist with Help Desk requests for residential mortgage sales and operation processes which can be addressed through corrections, changes and/or additions to software applications
<br>Assist with defining and documenting workflow and application requirements and recommend solutions
<br>Assist with performing and/or coordinating testing, implementation (including coordination with Information Technology staff) and user training material for such solutions on an initial and ongoing basis
<br>Coordinate with and support sales, operational and compliance requirements
<br>Assume responsibility for related duties as required or assigned
<br><br>
Knowledge, Skills and Abilities
<br>Education - High school GED or diploma required. Bachelor's degree in a business-related field from an accredited institution of higher learning.
<br>Skills - High proficiency in Microsoft Word and Excel, required
<br>Knowledge - Experience with Encompass. Basic coding and/or form building experience would be helpful
<br>Skills - Excellent attention to detail; proven outstanding work ethic; takes initiative; positive interpersonal skills; good problem solving skills
<br>Skills - Excellent client service skills and ability to communicate effectively with employees
<br><br>
