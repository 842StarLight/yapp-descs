<h2>Inside Sales Executive (Boston, MA)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>
<div>HUBER+SUHNER Polatis designs and manufactures all-optical fibre-optic switches which make and maintain their optical connections by means of our patented DirectLight™ beam steering technology. Working closely with our remotely based sales team, your primary responsibility will be to develop new accounts in the Service Provider, Data Center, Intelligence / Government, and Enterprise market segments and other markets where HUBER+SUHNER Polatis is not currently selling solutions.<br>
<br>
You will need to develop a target account list for outbound lead generation campaigns, contact new prospects inside a campaign to introduce them to HUBER+SUHNER Polatis, and develop relationships with key account contacts, introducing the business to our regional account managers. In addition:<br>
<br>
<ul>
<li>Maintain personal customer relationships as required
</li>
<li>Collaborate with the application and solutions team to customize our value proposition
</li>
<li>Drive the development of collateral needed to further position joint solutions with our partners</li>
<li>
Drive the development and implementation of webinars as needed to support the campaign
</li>
<li>Manage regular updates for sales management and account teams on developing business
</li>
<li>Utilize Salesforce.com or another CRM platform as the depository for contact and status information to enable productive collaboration</li>
</ul>
</div>
<div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Your profile</b></h3>
<div>To be successful, you’ll ideally have an accredited college degree in a relevant subject, plus 2 to 5 years of strategic sales experience as well as cold calling and prospecting sales skills.<br>
<br>
You’ll be highly organised with excellent interpersonal, presentation and verbal/written communication skills.<br>
<br>
You must be located in the Boston area and be willing to travel both domestically and internationally as required.<br>
</div>
