Job Description
<br>---------------
<br><br>
Vlocity, Inc. is recruiting a UI Designer and Developer to help build Vlocity product demonstration capabilities for Vlocity Solution Consultants. The UI Designer and Developer will lead the creation of attractive and business relevant visual layouts for our demos. The goal is to enable our Solution Consultants to deliver repeatable, reliable and reusable demo experiences that speak to customer work flows, process flows and business requirements.
<br><br>
This role will be part of the Demo Engineering Team. We work within the broader Vlocity Solution Consulting organization and our goal is to provide a trusted single source of truth regarding the latest demo environments – so that our Solution Consultants can confidently demo the latest, stable product capabilities to customers. This role will report to the VP Demo Engineering, and will be part of Vlocity's first dedicated Demo Engineering team – helping Solution Consultants deliver faster and more accurate demos – directly contributing to the hyper-growth of our company.
<br><br>
We seek an experienced and talented UI designer with solid front-end coding capabilities to design and shape unique, Enterprise level experiences.
<br><br>
.Responsibilities
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Collaborate with product teams (Communications, Media, Utilities, Health Insurance, Insurance &amp; Public Sector) and Solution Consultants to understand demo requirements</li>
<li>Define and implement innovative solutions for demo visuals and user experience</li>
<li>Build designs for the Demo Org; which can then be fed back into Engineering</li>
<li>Conduct testing of completed applications, websites and software to assess user experience</li>
<li>Bias to making those around you successful and are comfortable working as a team player</li>
<li>Contribute to building a positive, can-do, high-performance culture</li>
</ul>
Experience
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Ideal candidate has experience working in agile teams, with developers, UX designers and product management – must enjoy the 'small company' challenge</li>
<li>Account for and track HCI (human-computer interaction) element of design [preferred]</li>
<li>UI design and development experience in Mobile environments is a plus</li>
<li>5+ years of Enterprise experience – UI design work related to enterprise platforms such as Vlocity, Salesforce.com, Oracle, Siebel, Microsoft, Amdocs, Netcracker, etc.</li>
<li>Excellent communication skills, both written and verbal</li>
<li>Experience and desire to work within a fast-paced, iterative sales and development environment</li>
</ul>
Technical Skills
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Hands-on experience creating wireframes, prototypes, storyboards, user flows, process flows and site maps</li>
<li>Proficiency with basic front-end languages: HTML5, CSS3 Javascript, for rapid prototyping</li>
<li>Proficiency in Photoshop, Sketch, Illustrator, InVision, UXPin, Quartz or other visual design and wire-framing tools</li>
<li>Strong portfolio to be presented and defended during interview</li>
<li>Creative ideas with problem solving mindset</li>
<li>Experience working in an agile/scrum development process</li>
</ul>
Desired Certifications / Qualifications
<br><br>
<ul>
