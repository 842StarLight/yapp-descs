<h2>Restaurant Supervisor</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Job Responsibilities</p>
<p>Restaurant Supervisor</p>
<ul>
<li>Oversea the dining area, supervises food and beverage service staff in accordance with operating policies that he or she may help establish.</li>
<li>Maintains records of staff periodic manner and operating costs.</li>
<li>Provides feedback and coaching to the Team regularly.</li>
<li>Sets high standards for appropriate team behaviour on shift.</li>
<li>Works with food and beverage staff to ensure proper food presentation and proper food-handling procedures.</li>
<li>Handle guest complaints in restaurants.</li>
<li>Schedules periodic food and beverage service staff meetings to ensure correct interpretation of policies and obtain feedback from staff members.</li>
<li>Maintain budget and employee records, prepare payroll, and pay bills, or monitor bookkeeping records.</li>
<li>Check the quality of deliveries of fresh food</li>
<li>Meet with sales representatives to order supplies such as tableware, cooking utensils, and cleaning items.</li>
<li>Arrange for maintenance and repair of equipment and other services.</li>
<li>Total receipts and balance against sales, deposit receipts, and lock facility at end of day.</li>
<li>Ensures new products are executed properly the following roll-out.</li>
<li>Is capable of handling irate customers with a friendly/calm attitude.</li>
<li>Ensures product quality and great service.</li>
<li>Shows enthusiasm about guest within the restaurant.</li>
<li>Is flexible in dealing with changes/problems (e.g., being short staffed).</li>
<li>Has effectively forecasted restaurant needs.</li>
<li>Shifts priorities and goals as work demands change.</li>
<li>Priorities tasks effectively to ensure most important tasks are completed on time.</li>
<li>Delegates and follow-up effectively.</li>
<li>Taking Ownership of issues or tasks and also give detail update of the F&amp;B manager and the General Manager.</li>
<li>Seeks, listens and responds to Guest feedback.</li>
<li>Coaches team on how to exceed Guest expectations.</li>
<li>Does not blame others; takes accountability for problems.</li>
<li>Effectively identifies restaurant problems through reports and can ideate &amp; execute to resolve the same.</li>
<li>Proficiency in using computer software to monitor inventory, track staff schedules and pay, and perform other record keeping tasks.</li>
<li>Proficiency in Point of sale ( POS ) software, inventory software, Restaurant guest satisfactory tracking software etc.</li>
<li>Assist in planning regular and special event Menu.</li>
</ul>

<p>Degree, High schooling or one year's Diploma in Hotel Management or equivalent.</p>

<p>Has effectively managed Team Members for 12+ months.</p>
<p>Previous experience in similar Job role.</p>
<p>Coached and improved performance of at least one poorly performing Team member/Manager.</p>
