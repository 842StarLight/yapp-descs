<h2>Account Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Company Overview</b></p>
<p>
Medinas is a rapidly growing healthcare company based in Berkeley CA. Our goal is to use software to reduce hospital waste by creating one click, technology-driven asset management and remarketing solutions.</p>
<p><b>
Job Summary</b></p>
<p>
Our account manager acts as the face of the company for potential buyers of equipment through our online marketplace. The account manager works with buyers to make offers, helps onboard new buyers to the platform, and works with our Chief Marketing Officer to create and execute strategies to get equipment sold.</p>
<p>
We are growing rapidly and there is huge potential for growth within the company. We're invested in helping you develop skills and grow as a professional.</p>
<p><b>
Responsibilities and Duties</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Calling Buyers to help match them with equipment listed on our marketplace</li>
<li>
Working with hospital account managers to update listings and get questions answered for buyers</li>
<li>
Onboarding new buyers to our software, helping explain the features and how they can use the software to purchase equipment</li>
<li>
Partnering with our CMO to build and run email marketing and ad campaigns</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Qualifications and Skills</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
A natural talent for communication</li>
<li>
Sales/tech experience is a plus</li>
<li>
Attention to detail and being process oriented</li>
<li>
A customer service-focused mindset</li>
</ul>
