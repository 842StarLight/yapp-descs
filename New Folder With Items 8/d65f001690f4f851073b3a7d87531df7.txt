<b>Job Summary:</b><br>
Responsible for installation, configuration and performance monitoring of data network communications equipment including, servers, routers, switches, software and other network equipment. Working under general supervision, the Data Network Technician will perform and/or assist in the routine testing, analysis, troubleshooting and maintenance of operational network resources.
