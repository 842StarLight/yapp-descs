<h2>Receiving Assistant</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Overview
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div><b>Job Summary</b></div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
The Receiving Assistant is responsible for assisting the Receiving Manager in the successful operation of the receiving department.</div>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div><b>Key responsibilities include the following essential functions, but are not limited to:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
Providing World Class Customer Service as a number one priority.</li>
<li>
Exemplifying integrity, responsibility, and excellence and adhering to all policies.</li>
<li>
Assisting the receiving department manager in accurately receiving all incoming products including date, accuracy and damage checking.</li>
<li>
Scanning all incoming and outgoing product.</li>
<li>
Staging products for efficient delivery to the sales floor.</li>
<li>
Processing POs, RPOs, mispicks, invoices and paperwork timely and accurately.</li>
<li>
Researching and processing all discrepancies.</li>
<li>
Communicating with department managers regarding delivery issues and vendor out-of-stocks.</li>
<li>
Following up on special orders.</li>
<li>
Operating a forklift and pallet jack as required.</li>
<li>
Maintaining a clean and organized back room.</li>
<li>
Working with the department managers to address performance issues within the department.</li>
<li>
Supporting store opening and closing activities including Daily Sales Report (DSR) and cash handling and cashier closeout responsibilities.</li>
<li>
Handling register functions including backup cashiering, managing customer returns, addressing customer complaints, and covering register shift changes, including those for scheduled breaks.</li>
<li>
Maintaining the safety and security of customers and employees.</li>
<li>
Assisting in training and monitoring of department personnel including assigning and following up on tasks.</li>
<li>
Taking the initiative to provide additional help and backup in the store and providing store backup on the sales floor when receiving duties are complete.</li>
<li>
Answering customer questions per company standards and policies, including the use of Structure/Function statements and/or statements of nutritional support.</li>
<li>
Continually increasing product knowledge.</li>
<li>
Using SAP and inventory management software, emailing and utilizing other IS programs as needed.</li>
<li>
Working a schedule based on store needs which includes evenings, weekends, holidays. Position requires working five days, 40 hours per week and attending mandatory store meetings.</li>
<li>
This position has limited Manager on Duty but is never intended to be the overall Manager on Duty for the store.</li>
</ul>
<div>
Although this is a general outline of job responsibilities all employees are expected to be ''hands on'' and do whatever it takes to get the job done and make the company thrive.</div>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Qualifications
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div><b>Education, Skills &amp; Experience</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
High School diploma, GED or equivalent preferred.</li>
<li>
1 year of experience in grocery/retail or receiving environment preferred; natural foods background is a plus.</li>
<li>
1 year of experience supervising others preferred.</li>
<li>
Ability to pass forklift certification.</li>
<li>
Ability to pass food safety training courses and/or certifications as required by law and maintain compliance by keeping certifications up to date and registered if applicable.</li>
<li>
Ability to manage changing priorities and to stay focused with the task at hand.</li>
<li>
Possess a sense of urgency in the completion of tasks.</li>
<li>
Possess excellent customer service skills.</li>
<li>
Highly organized with great attention to detail.</li>
<li>
Ability to take direction and follow through.</li>
<li>
Must be cashier trained and able to count currency.</li>
<li>
Proficient in MS Word, Excel and Outlook</li>
</ul>
