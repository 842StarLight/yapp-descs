<h2>Senior Visual and Interaction Designer (UI)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">There is no hotter field right now than Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning. DataRobot is at the forefront of this trend, having established market leadership in a category called Automated Machine Learning. We're Boston-based and we've raised $225M to fuel our expansive ambitions. We are growing quickly and need talented, motivated people to join the team. Our software helps organizations gain a competitive edge with AI. We've transformed how data science is done at the highest level, and we're looking to extend that disruption.
<br><br>
********************
<br>What you'll do
<br>********************
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>You'll work hands-on to create sleek and simple visual solutions for complex problems involving data visualization and heavy technical information</li>
<li>You'll help build a library of extensible UI patterns and interaction guidelines to solidify/improve user experience and accelerate delivery from concept to code</li>
<li>You'll interact directly with project stakeholders to collect design feedback and iterate (a lot) on mockups then ultimately deliver pixel-perfect, hi-fidelity, annotated designs</li>
<li>You'll work with product managers and other designers to ensure that user interaction in any given design is efficient, understandable, and falls within a technical reality</li>
<li>You'll provide design feedback and guidance to engineers and other designers throughout the process on visual design and interaction execution</li>
<li>You'll apply your design expertise to help build team-focused processes that boost productivity, reduce waste, improve communication, and aid in our ability to scale</li>
<li>You'll help to solve previously unsolved problems within a new, hot, and growing market</li>
<li>You'll learn from functional groups with a vast array of backgrounds in data science, software engineering, product management, marketing, sales, and business</li>
<li>You'll work with cutting-edge technology, world-class teammates, and learn how organizations are using their data across nearly every vertical</li>
</ul>
*****************************
<br>What will make you successful
<br>*****************************
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>You have 5+ years of digital interface visual and interaction design experience in delivering high-volume, high-profile or award-winning products; preferably experience with enterprise SaaS</li>
<li>You have diverse experience and deep knowledge/skill designing complex applications focused on data, decision-making, and/or exploration</li>
<li>You're a pragmatic, systematic, user-first thinker and problem solver with a passion for turning complex ideas into something simple, comprehensible, and pleasant</li>
<li>You're passionate about pushing the boundaries of conventional visual design but you're able to recognize valuable user patterns and utilize them within your work</li>
<li>You're proficient in a variety of methods to convey ideas, collaborate, and collect feedback (e.g. storyboards, wireframes, prototypes, etc)</li>
<li>You've demonstrated the ability to create and maintain a system of UI resources for designers and engineers</li>
<li>You are comfortable iterating through quick low-fidelity sketches to amazing hi-fidelity mockups on tight schedules</li>
<li>You have your fingers on the pulse of best practices in typography, color theory, iconography and data visualization</li>
<li>You have a strong knowledge of visual and technical accessibility and how it affects your designs</li>
<li>You're organized, self-directed, efficient, and able to manage multiple complex projects in a timely manner</li>
<li>You have expertise with modern design suites like Figma, Sketch, Framer, InVision, Adobe CC, but you also know when and how to use a whiteboard or a sketch pad</li>
<li>You have strong familiarity with web technologies and their impact on the feasibility of design solutions; you'll preferably have some coding experience</li>
<li>You have experience working with and visualizing data; data science and machine learning knowledge is a huge plus</li>
<li>You have examples of high-quality, diverse, dazzling work that demonstrate the above</li>
</ul>
