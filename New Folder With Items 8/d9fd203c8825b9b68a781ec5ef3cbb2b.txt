<h2>Branch Ops / Counter Trainee</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p><b>Reports to: Branch Manager</b></p>
<p>Electric Supply Center is looking for talented, hard-working people to help us maintain high levels of customer satisfaction in every area of our business. As a Branch Operations &amp; Counter Sales Trainee you will be responsible for assisting both counter sales &amp; the branch manager, as well as spending time learning how to work on the sales counter to provide excellent customer service to our customers.</p>
<p><b>Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Fulfilling customer orders placed at the sales counter</li>
<li>Cycle counting</li>
<li>Receiving material into stock and shipping out transfers</li>
<li>Assisting counter and inside sales team as needed.</li>
<li>Moving around to different locations/departments as needed.</li>
<li>Ensures compliance with company policies and procedures including but not limited to compliance in facilities maintenance, and safety.</li>
<li>Place, arrange and maintain merchandise displays.</li>
<li>Perform any and all physical requirements necessary to lift and move inventory around the warehouse.</li>
<li>Operate and maneuver warehouse equipment and any material contained in the manner consistent with the intended purpose.</li>
<li>Update knowledge of products through self-study and attending training classes.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Qualifications</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Individual must have the ability to multitask and communicate effectively to all functions of our business.</li>
 <li>Desire to learn electrical distribution, product applications and sales experience</li>
<li>Ability to perform the physical requirements necessary to receive, pick, pack, ship, and move materials throughout the warehouse.</li>
<li>Ability to operate and maneuver warehouse equipment including pallet jacks and forklifts.</li>
<li>A minimum of 1 year experience working in either a counter sales or operations position in the electrical distribution or related industry is preferred.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Language Skills</b></p>
<p>Ability to read, write and speak English, analyze, and interpret general business correspondence. Ability to respond to common inquiries or complaints from internal/external customers, vendors, or manufacturers.</p>
<p><b>Computer Skills</b></p>
<p>To perform this job successfully, an individual should have knowledge of Solar Eclipse and RF Inventory scanning technology.</p>
<p><b>Physical Demands</b></p>
<p>The physical demands described here are representative of those that must be met by an employee to successfully perform the essential functions of this job. Reasonable accommodations may be made to enable individuals with disabilities to perform the essential functions. While performing the duties of this Job, the employee is regularly required to speak read or hear. The employee is frequently required to stand; walk; sit and use hands to handle, or feel. The employee is occasionally required to reach with hands and arms and climb or balance. The employee must occasionally lift and/or move up to 75 pounds. Specific vision abilities required by this job include ability to adjust focus.</p>
<p><b>Working Conditions</b></p>
<p>The work environment characteristics described here are representative of those an employee encounters while performing the essential functions of this job. Reasonable accommodations may be made to enable individuals with disabilities to perform the essential functions. Responsibilities are performed in an office environment, at the branch counter interacting with customers, in the attached warehouses services orders when needed and at company/customer functions outside the facility. The noise level in the work environment is usually moderate. This position will require approximately 40% local domestic travel and ability to travel by air and motor vehicle transportation. There may be occasional exposure to extreme weather conditions during travel or in a warehouse environment at company branches. This is a high stress and fast paced environment.</p>
