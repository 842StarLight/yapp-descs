<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>JOB REQUIREMENTS:</b></h3>
<div></div>
<div>
<br>
<div><div>
<p><b>Education:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>High School Diploma or GED Certificate.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Additional Requirements:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Advanced knowledge of table games. MUST BE ABLE TO PASS AN AUDITION
</li>
<li>Knowledge of applicable regulations, rules, and procedures.</li>
<li>Previous experience in hospitality, sales, marketing, promotions, and/or public relations is preferred</li>
<li>Excellent interpersonal skills with demonstrated patience and tact.</li>
<li>Exceptional detail and follow-up skills.</li>
<li>Must be able to work a flexible schedule, including nights, weekends and/or holidays.</li>
<li>Must be able to work overtime as business necessitates.</li>
<li>Must be able to read and write legibly.</li>
<li>Must apply for, receive, and maintain a Gaming License from the Tribal Gaming Agency.</li>
</ul>
