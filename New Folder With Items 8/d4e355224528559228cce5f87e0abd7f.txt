<h2>Parts Director - Warehouse</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<div><b>Position Overview</b></div>
<p>
The Director – AutoNation Parts Warehouse is responsible for developing wholesale business to ensure adequate sales volume to meet operating profit targets; as well as managing a profitable and efficient wholesale parts department, through the use of proper distribution procedures, inventory control, staff utilization, security, pricing, merchandising, displaying and advertising.</p>
<div></div>
<div><b>
Who Would I Interact With?</b></div>
<p>
Reports to the Area Wholesale Director/Lead (with a Dotted Line to the Corporate Director of ACP). Responsible for all activities regarding wholesale parts. Interacts with region and corporate management teams, and various levels of store associates.</p>
<div></div>
<div><b>
What are the day-to-day responsibilities?</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
Establish and communicate a clear vision of the wholesale parts operations</li>
<li>
Forecast goals and objectives for the department and facilitate their attainment</li>
<li>
Prepare and administer an annual operating budget for the parts department</li>
<li>
Ensure parts and accessories inventories are maintained and available for resale; and generate gross profit through the sale of parts and accessories.</li>
<li>
Establish and maintain purchasing policies including purchase orders, inventory guide figures, order codes, etc</li>
<li>
Understand, keep abreast of, and comply with federal, state, and local regulations that may affect wholesale parts sales</li>
<li>
Ensure parts department associates are provided technical assistance as needed. Monitor parts department associates’ payroll records</li>
<li>
Develop, enforce and monitor guidelines for working with customers to ensure maximum customer satisfaction</li>
<li>
Establish and enforce a pricing policy that balances volume, customer loyalty, and the cost of doing business</li>
<li>
Monitor and adjust inventory to minimize obsolescence</li>
<li>
Ensure that processes are in place to measure, maintain and improve customer service</li>
<li>
Use CSI and customer feedback to determine specific opportunities for improvement and ensure that needed action is taken</li>
<li>
Provide for and facilitate the recruiting, selection, on boarding and training of associates; planning/scheduling/assigning, monitoring and appraising job results and associate performance; and the coaching, counseling and disciplining of associates so as to develop and retain a qualified workforce</li>
<li>
Create a work environment that improves associate satisfaction and loyalty while reducing turnover</li>
<li>
Build management team that demonstrates a high degree of teamwork and makes decisions that are focused on customer needs and the good of the dealership</li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<div><b>
What are the requirements for this job?</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
High School diploma or equivalent</li>
<li>
Two years’ experience as a Parts Manager at a wholesale parts department</li>
<li>
Minimum 5 years of supervisory experience</li>
<li>
5+ years of experience in an automotive parts department</li>
<li>
Excellent communication and managerial skills</li>
<li>
Valid in-state driver’s license and have and maintain an acceptable, safe driving record</li>
</ul>
