<h2>Automotive Service Advisor Bill Harris Dealerships - Ashland, Ohio, US</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">Automotive Service Advisor
<br><br>
If you believe Customer Service is a true craft - one built on charisma, communication, and thorough product knowledge - we want to talk to you!
<br><br>
<b>As a Service Advisor, you will be responsible for:</b> Meeting and greeting service customers promptly in a professional, warm, and welcoming manner. Scheduling service appointments using dealership software, balancing customer needs, requests, and shop capacity. Inspecting vehicles and refer to service history to accurately identify and verify customer’s service needs. Promoting the sales of appropriate services, parts, and accessories by thoroughly understanding the product and associated service requirements. Providing accurate cost and time of completion estimates for the services recommended; communicating and getting proper authorization from customers. Checking on progress of services to vehicles throughout the day and contacting customers to communicate and receive approval regarding any changes in the cost-estimate, promised time of delivery, or recommended services. Follows up with customers frequently and consistently concerning the status of their vehicle. Communicating with parts department to ensure procurement of parts and offers the customer options presented by the parts department concerning pricing and availability. Administering new and used vehicle warranty repairs in accordance with manufacturer warranty guidelines. Confirming service appointments by telephone and conducting post-repair follow up. Inspecting vehicles prior to each customer delivery to ensure vehicles services and/or repairs are performed correctly; communicating the results with the customer. Meeting requirements of state and federal laws for automobile repair and consumer protection. Demonstrating behaviors consistent with the company’s values in all interactions with customers, co-workers, and vendors.
<br><br>
<b>Job Requirements:</b><br>
<b>Preferred:</b> two or more years of previous experience as a service advisor. High school diploma or equivalent. Valid driver license Must be available to work a schedule that will include Saturdays. Great attitude with high-energy personality. Excellent customer service skills. Professional appearance and work ethic. Ability to work well in a process driven environment. Outstanding communication skills, both verbal and written.
