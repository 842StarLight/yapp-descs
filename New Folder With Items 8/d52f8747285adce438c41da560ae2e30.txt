<p><b>Job Summary:
</b></p>
<p>This position will be responsible for the graphic design and production of digital media including email and web based creative, print media, direct mail, advertising, collateral and signage. The Graphic Designer will collaborate with the marketing team to grasp the concepts, strategy, and information to be communicated. This person will need to provide innovative creative design recommendations, have strong technical abilities, and be able to apply his/her comprehensive functional knowledge of graphic design to be a key contributor to the marketing team.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Job Responsibilities:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Create original design solutions consistent within the MileOne brand and creative standards for email marketing, promotional digital campaigns, direct mail, collateral and logo development.
</li>
<li>Work collaboratively with our dynamic team to achieve company goals. Be willing to pitch in on any job.
</li>
<li>Ability to strategize solutions, based on objectives and audience, under tight deadlines.
</li>
<li>Proven ability to create original design concepts for both print and web.
</li>
<li>Attend weekly meetings with Design team to discuss ideas and current work.
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Requirements:
</b></p>
<p></p>
<p><b>*PLEASE SUBMIT A PORTFOLIO FOR CONSIDERATION, THOSE CANDIDATES WITHOUT A PORTFOLIO WILL NOT BE CONSIDERED*
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>The ideal candidate will have a minimum of 2 years’ experience as a graphic designer.
</li>
<li>Excellent communication, presentation, and interpersonal skills.
</li>
<li>Understand the principles of design, marketing and advertising and appreciate the need for consistency within company's branding and marketing mix.
</li>
<li>Adept at visual design and in touch with modern design aesthetics, including typography, iconography, layout, color theory and user interface elements.
</li>
<li>Ability to manage multiple deadlines on various projects at a time.
</li>
<li>Experience and proficiency in a MAC environment.
</li>
<li>Proficient in Adobe's Creative Suite, specifically InDesign, Illustrator, Adobe Photoshop, and Dreamweaver.
</li>
<li>Working knowledge of HTML &amp; CSS is a must.
</li>
<li>Knowledge in Microsoft Office.
</li>
<li>Ability to work in a fast paced environment with high design standards.
</li>
<li>Strong organizational skills.
</li>
<li>Experience with video editing and motion graphics a plus.
</li>
</ul>
