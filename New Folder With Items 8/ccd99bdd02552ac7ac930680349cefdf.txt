<div><b>
About the Role</b></div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
As a Senior Account Executive at Clinc, you will play an instrumental role in our growth by developing new business within the conversational AI enterprise market. You will be expected to establish trusted advisor status with our key prospects and customers, and demonstrate innovation through the Clinc conversational AI platform. Your responsibilities include prospecting within the top Financial, Automotive, QSR, and Healthcare markets in the world, positioning Clinc as the conversational AI platform of choice for digital consumer experiences, and securing new business that helps our customers drive digital innovation. You will focus on a named account territory based on a mixture of geographic and vertical accounts. You'll have access to a dynamic team of Business Development Managers and Solutions Consultants for sales support plus an extended team that consists of the best industry talent in engineering, project management, and customer experience.</div>
</div>
<div><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
You’ll help our customers drive digital innovation by</b></h3>
<div><ul>
<li>
Providing customer experience and platform demonstrations showing the possibilities of the Clinc experience.</li>
<li>
Effectively differentiating the Clinc experience versus competitors, and selling Clinc solutions to Global 1000 customers.</li>
<li>
Share customer and industry best practices related to business value and elevated consumer experience use cases.</li>
<li>
Facilitating meetings to understand customer’s business and digital initiatives, plus identify best use cases to apply conversational AI.</li>
<li>
Work with customers to scope use case requirements and success criteria for Proof of Concepts, Pilots and Production deployments.</li>
<li>
Traveling to customer sites on a regular basis (50-75% is not unusual).</li>
</ul></div>
</div></div>
