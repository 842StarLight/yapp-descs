<p>
Position Summary</p>
<p>
ALL Sunshine Crane Rental Corp. is seeking a Service Technician &amp; Mechanic with a CDL and active medical card. This is a full-time, non-exempt position.</p>
<p>
Essential Functions</p>
<ul>
<li>
Performs off site lubrication, service, and preventative maintenance work on mobile cranes</li>
<li>
Performs minor mechanical field repairs on equipment</li>
<li>
Maintains records and preventative maintenance schedules for assigned equipment</li>
<li>
Performs related work as required</li>
<li>
Maintain conformity to safety requirements and other regulations</li>
<li>
Prepare proper documentation of actions taken</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Skills and Experience Requirements</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Must have a Class A or Class B CDL with an acceptable MVR</li>
<li>
Must have a current Medical Examiner's card</li>
<li>
Must be able work safely around moving machinery</li>
<li>
Able to work in conditions with marked changes in temperature &amp; humidity</li>
<li>
Strong initiative required; ability to work independently with minimal direct supervision</li>
<li>
May be asked to perform related work such as crane assembly/disassembly and rigging</li>
</ul>
