<h2>Project / Design Engineer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<b>Description:</b><br>
This multifaceted position is focused new product development with an emphasis on developing and continuously improving customer relationships while bringing new designs from concept to production. Innovation on all levels, open and honest communication, and leading collaboration with all internal teams including manufacturing, sales, supply chain, estimating, and customer service. Responsible for the design of parts and accessories from concept to production meeting goals for performance, budget and time.
<br><br>
Essential Job Functions
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Develops strong working relationships with customers and serves as their primary engineering contact for active projects. Overcommunicates budget, timeline, and design progress.</li>
<li>Ability to manage multiple projects at one time, facilitate customer requests, organize priorities, and drive resolution in an industry leading timeframe.</li>
<li>Leads design related component styling, purchasing and manufacturing issues with emphasis on quality, reliability, timely delivery, and cost.</li>
<li>Leads and organizes cross functional team meetings to drive product development deadlines as required.</li>
<li>May involve direct management of people to distribute project load in order to achieve pre-determined deadlines and meet or exceed customer expectations.</li>
<li>Manages project development timeline for all owned projects from concept to production in line with overall project requirements.</li>
<li>Ability to work within the following digital platforms (CAD, File Management and ERP System) to ensure efficient product development.</li>
<li>Determines ideal component manufacturing direction to achieve overall design, performance, and cost goals.</li>
<li>Analyze existing and new designs to identify and implement cost reduction opportunities.</li>
<li>Accountable to PDP (Product Development Process) and ownership of tasks within.</li>
<li>Prepares preliminary sketches and/or schematic diagrams and/or presentations to aid in the understanding and solution of engineering problems.</li>
<li>Analyzes field and production problems and recommends possible solutions.</li>
<li>Performs other miscellaneous project related assignments.</li>
</ul>
<b>Requirements:</b><br>
Minimum Requirements
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor's Degree in Mechanical or Industrial Engineering (equivalent work experience may be considered)</li>
<li>2-5 years related design experience required, plastics and/or sheet metal preferred</li>
<li>Experience designing and bringing product into production</li>
</ul>
Abilities Required
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Customer-facing effective communicator focused on building relationships</li>
<li>Excellent problem-solving skills</li>
<li>Results driven performance/work ethic</li>
<li>Leadership attributes centered around team collaboration</li>
<li>Personal Development mindset</li>
<li>Proficiency in 3D CAD Software (preferably Solidworks)</li>
<li>GD&amp;T, FMEA and dimensional tolerance stack ups</li>
<li>Familiar with design benefits / constraints in multiple manufacturing methods (sheet metal, injection molding, machining, etc.)</li>
<li>Application of engineering principles to ensure product performance</li>
<li>Experience managing customer and vendor relationships</li>
<li>Demonstrated ability to implement change in a way that positively impacts overall department and company performance</li>
<li>Must be able to handle multiple projects simultaneously and manage priorities</li>
<li>Excellent verbal and written communication skills</li>
<li>Must excel at working in a team environment with a high level of people/interpersonal skills and demonstrated ability to cross functionally drive results</li>
</ul>
</div>
