<h2>Marketing Engineer II</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>The Marketing Engineer II will report to the Product Planning Manager and will be located in Plymouth, MN, where they will have direct access to Product Management, Applications, and Engineering. In this role, the candidate will be responsible for:</p>
<ul>
<li>Management of selection software changes including the creation &amp; prioritization of Fogbugz cases, validation of engineering data, investigation/resolution of technical issues, creation of SmartSpec, software testing, and weekly status communications.
</li>
<li>Project analytical support for NPI and Non-NPI projects including specification definition and targets, participation &amp; input at team meetings, order-to-cash testing, and technical coordination of changes to new and existing products.
</li>
<li>General literature support (as needed) for the creation of Marketing News, webcasts, white papers, newsletters, and creative briefs.
</li>
</ul>
<p>Key skills needed for this position include a mechanical engineering background with strong analytical, problem solving, and computer skills. Must understand advanced mathematical concepts and have solid written and oral communication skills.</p>
<p><b>
Education/Experience:</b><br>
Bachelor's degree in Mechanical Engineering from four-year college or university and 3-5 years related experience working in the HVAC industry. Marketing experience or MBA degree, preferred. LEED, environmental, "Green" experience. LEED AP preferred or willingness to achieve certification.</p>
