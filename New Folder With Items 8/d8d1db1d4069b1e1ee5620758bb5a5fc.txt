<div><b>Grow above and beyond.<br>

<div></div>
<div>As a Dealer Sales Consultant, you’re responsible for building relationships with our Dealership Clients in a specific territory. This includes Cold Calling, Dealership visitations, along with Telephony and email consultation. Responsibilities include managing and increasing revenue through a consultative approach, along with visiting your Dealership Client base on a regular schedule and managing Dealer performance by reviewing key metrics. Your time will also include adding new Client business acquisitions and performing account maintenance in order to maximize Dealership growth opportunities for Retail and Service traffic.<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<div>What you’ll do as a <b>Dealer Sales Consultant:</b>
</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<ul><li>
<div>
Plan, maintain and manage a specific area of responsibility:</div>
<ul>
<li><div>
Visit and consult with 2 or more Dealership Clients daily to bring value and support on Epsilon product portfolio.</div></li>
<li><div>
Actively consult with Owners, General Managers and other key Dealership management on all program participation.
</div></li>
<li><div>Assess opportunities within Dealerships in order to drive Retail and Service traffic.</div></li>
<li><div>
Consistently meet or exceed sales objectives by implementing Epsilon products that will help Dealerships target customers.</div></li>
<li><div>
Provide exceptional problem resolution to dealerships’ needs by maintaining consistent support and service to all existing clients.</div></li>
<li><div>
Work with our Automotive Partners and Clients to help develop customized plans for each Dealership in order to drive and maintain consistent revenue opportunities.</div></li>
<li><div>
Facilitate training and best practices on all programs to ensure Dealerships are maximizing benefits of all Epsilon products.<br>
</div></li>
</ul>
</li></ul>
