<h2>Enterprise Campaign Architect</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>The Global Demand Team is seeking an energetic, creative, and passionate leader with great campaign development, management, and execution skills to undertake our new Campaign Architect role.
</p>
<p>The Campaign Architect role understands the needs &amp; trends of today’s buyers and delivers messaging, audience strategies, and content that is used to uncover new pipeline opportunities. By focusing on strategic planning, cross-functional collaboration and launching integrated marketing programs, the Campaign Architect will make a material difference to the growth of our Enterprise business.</p>
<p><br>
As Campaign Architect, you will use your expertise to help manage, execute &amp; monitor campaigns across multiple channels for Juniper’s Security &amp; AI-Driven Enterprise campaigns. In this role, you will leverage your marketing expertise, organizational and leadership skills to help achieve overarching marketing and demand generation goals. You will collaborate closely with your product marketing counterparts, field marketing, web, social, brand, etc. to manage the execution deliverables across multiple campaigns designed to generate pipeline &amp; awareness.<br>
<br>
The successful candidate will have knowledge of demand creation best practices &amp; the buyer’s journey, strong project management skills, the ability to drive campaign execution, as well as a natural inclination to measure the effectiveness of marketing activities, while managing the expectations of various stakeholders across the organization. They will have experience in building, delivering, and optimizing digital campaigns which include (but not limited to) digital/social media, email nurture, webinars, content asset development, and paid reach (search, content syndication, etc.). The person will be proficient with data and comfortable with creatively investigating and solving challenges in the business using data.
</p>
<p><b>Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Working closely with your product marketing counterparts you are responsible for identifying goals &amp; objectives, architecting marketing strategies and then conceptualizing integrated campaigns to generate market demand to exceed annual pipeline goals
</li>
<li>Understand how to interpret &amp; apply various campaign approaches based on the goal, objective and audience: new contact acquisition, upsell/cross-sell and install base base / account marketing
</li>
<li>Ensure successful implementation and execution of campaigns by ensuring clear goals, expectation and accountabilities are understood and well managed</li>
<li>Knows the buyer’s journey well and is comfortable developing contact journeys across awareness, consideration and decision stages
</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Intimately understands how to use content marketing as a tool to attract &amp; nurture active buyers – has the ability to suggest content mediums to communicate key messages
</li>
<li>Manage the execution &amp; launch timelines of campaigns and tactics to drive opportunities that contribute to sales pipeline</li>
<li>Ensure that campaigns can be executed within budget guidelines and/or resources and assist in making critical budget balancing recommendations to ensure overall campaign effectiveness</li>
<li>Understand how to monitor and optimize campaign data performance, i.e. conversion rates and maintain effective control of marketing results, and take corrective action, if needed, to achieve marketing objectives</li>
<li>Team up with other organizations which contribute to the success of campaign activities (such as Field Marketing, Product Marketing Teams, Marketing Operations, Web, Events, Media, etc.)</li>
<li>Stay on the cutting edge of marketing best practices by researching the latest trends and gathering new ideas from industry analysts and experts, vendors/agencies, and peers/competitors</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Minimum Qualifications:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s Degree in Business, Marketing or related field with 6+ years marketing experience, preferably B2B with a technology or software company</li>
<li>Comprehensive knowledge of the rapidly changing digital marketing landscape and the latest online marketing, campaign and content strategies</li>
<li>Experience with integrated marketing campaign planning across digital, email, social, and other media</li>
<li>History of developing and executing demand generation and pipeline-building marketing campaigns (including digital, direct marketing, events, etc.)</li>
<li>Ability to think strategically, prioritize tasks, and execute with attention to detail</li>
<li>Marketing campaigns or programs management experience
</li>
<li>Knowledge of security and enterprise business lines
</li>
<li>Understanding on how to interprept and optimize campaign performance data
</li>
<li>Excellent project management and process development skills; you drive collaboration and make projects and teams work more effectively by providing standard tools, structure and process. Manage timelines and deliverables and raise issues as they arise</li>
<li>Creative thinker who is adept at working on multiple projects simultaneously in a fast-paced environment, and comfortable adding new ideas</li>
<li>Must use and incorporate data as part of regular processes</li>
<li>Must have the proven ability to successfully manage and execute multiple projects, priorities, and engagements simultaneously</li>
<li>Project management skills with experience driving deliverables with multiple entities</li>
<li>Understanding of the modern-day buyer’s journey and digital marketing techniques and technologies (including marketing automation platforms, such as Eloqua)</li>
<li>Experience in collaborating with and managing marketing agencies and vendors to develop creative assets for marketing campaigns</li>
<li>Energetic, high performer with a willingness to take initiative and be resourceful</li>
<li>Understanding of content marketing best practices across the buyer’s journey
</li>
<li>Excellent written and verbal communication skills and presentation abilities</li>
<li>Team oriented, motivated self-starter with relationship building skills with a positive attitude and passion to learn</li>
</ul>
