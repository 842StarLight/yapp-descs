<b>Who you are:</b><br>
As Associate Corporate Counsel, you will report to the General Counsel and will be primarily responsible for drafting and negotiating customer contracts in support of a fast-moving, global sales organization. You will also provide advice and counsel on a broad range of issues in areas such as privacy, employment and intellectual property.
<br><br>
<b>Responsibilities:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Review, draft, and negotiate a wide variety of commercial agreements, including SaaS, master services, licensing, marketing and other vendor agreements; partner with relevant business stakeholders as necessary</li>
<li>Assist with employment, privacy, intellectual property, governance, litigation, international and other legal matters</li>
<li>Prepare and enhance company policies and procedures</li>
<li>Advise internal teams, including HR, Sales, Product and Marketing teams, on legal matters and provide general corporate and commercial legal advice</li>
<li>Research and anticipate new and emerging legal issues</li>
<li>Provide strategic legal advice and counsel on a broad range of legal topics</li>
</ul>
<b>Desired Skills and Experience:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Juris Doctor (JD) from an accredited U.S. law school, and a license to practice law in at least one U.S. jurisdiction</li>
<li>At least 2-4 years of experience practicing law at a law firm or in-house with a focus on general corporate matters</li>
<li>Experience drafting and negotiating complex commercial agreements, including SaaS agreements</li>
<li>Business judgment and ability to assess legal risk while providing practical advice</li>
<li>Experience and/or strong interest in areas such as employment law, data privacy and protection and/or intellectual property</li>
<li>Ability to organize, prioritize and manage deadlines in a fast-paced and demanding work environment</li>
<li>Thoughtful and persuasive interpersonal skills and ability to build and maintain strong working relationships</li>
<li>Enthusiastic team member who enjoys taking ownership of projects, building relationships cross-functionally and working on cutting-edge legal issues</li>
</ul>
