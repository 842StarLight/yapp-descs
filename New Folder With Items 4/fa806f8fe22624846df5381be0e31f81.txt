<h2>Customer Service Representative</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p><b>We are currently looking to hire Part-Time Customer Service Representatives to work
</b></p>
<p><b>Thursday Evenings (approx 4-8 PM) and
</b></p>
<p><b>Saturdays (8 AM - 1 PM)</b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
Summary: We are seeking a Customer Service Representative with excellent standards of customer care. A qualified candidate will have the ability to help maintain a fun and inviting atmosphere for swimmers and families as their first point of contact. If you have a passion in saving lives by learning to swim and want to make a difference by supporting young and old to learn a life skill, then you may have the opportunity to join our SwimWest family!</p>
<p>
Benefits:</p>
<ul>
<li>Excellent Pay.</li>
<li>Flexible hours.</li>
<li>Save Lives.</li>
<li>Valuable work experience.</li>
<li>Future job character reference.</li>
<li>Management training opportunities.</li>
</ul>
<p>Requirements:</p>
<ul>
<li>Ability to work with children.</li>
<li>Excellent interpersonal communication and organizational skills.</li>
<li>Must pass background examinations (included with training).</li>
</ul>Duties and Responsibilities:
<ul>
<li>Answers telephones and responds to inquiries professionally.</li>
<li>Processes student registrations with effective sales/customer service.</li>
<li>Resolves customer concerns/complaints professionally.</li>
<li>Assists members with sales of swim gear.</li>
<li>Greets parents and students as enter the building.</li>
<li>Maintains cleanliness of the facility.</li>
<li>Checks voicemail and email and responds in a timely manner.</li>
<li>Provides support for management personnel.</li>
<li>Makes collection calls to resolve open account problems.</li>
<li>Enforces safety rules and regulations to prevent accidents. Administers first aid when necessary.</li>
<li>Fulfills other duties and responsibilities as assigned by the Employer.</li>
</ul>
<p>Education/Experience: High school diploma or GED required. Some college preferred. Two years of previous customer service and/or administrative office experience required. Intermediate level computer skills required using Word, Excel and other software systems.</p>
<p>
Certifications and licenses: CPR, First Aid, and AED certifications required. Can be obtained through SwimWest Swim School after training is complete.</p>
<p>
Work Environment: While performing this job, the employee is exposed to heat and humidity occasionally. Noise level is usually moderate.</p>
