<h2>Commercial Coordinator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Overview</b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
The primary responsibility of the <b>Commercial Coordinator</b> is to assist the Account Executive with bidding, submittals, material research, and other administrative needs.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Responsibilities</b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Work with vendors to gather pricing, submittal documentation and physical samples of materials for new bids and contracted work</li>
<li>Prepare order forms, gather information for crew leaders/workers, drivers, quality control, Area/Production Managers and accounting</li>
<li>Coordinate with the Account Executive to maintain pricing and product in operating system</li>
<li>Communicate with Production, Sales, and Warehouse staff to avert problems</li>
<li>Compile and submit closeout information at completion of the project</li>
<li>All other job duties as assigned</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p>
</p>
<p><b>Qualifications</b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>High school diploma or general education degree (GED)</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Minimum of two years related experience and/or training; or equivalent combination of education and experience</li>
<li>Strong written and verbal communication skills</li>
<li>Interpersonal skills to develop and maintain professional working relationships with builders, vendors, subordinates and co-workers</li>
<li>Communicate effectively with customers, co-workers and supervisors in a professional and courteous manner</li>
<li>Proactively identify, analyze, address, and resolve problem areas, conflicts, and issues</li>
<li>Exhibits sound and accurate judgment, supports and explains reasoning for decisions, includes appropriate people in decision-making process, and makes timely decisions</li>
<li>Strong organizational skills to insure adequate tracking systems are utilized to maintain and track all required information associated with the bidding and production process</li>
<li>Working knowledge of internet search tools, spreadsheet and word process software</li>
</ul>
