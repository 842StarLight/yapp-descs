<p><b>Description</b></p>
<p>
This person will work hand-in-hand with Fox Sports Regional Networks (RSNs) sales teams in identifying, pitching and servicing key advertising clients and prospects. This will include identifying potential clients, setting sales strategies for clients, providing tactical support like presentation development, attending sales meetings and maintaining sales performance metrics. Additionally, this person will support the RSNsâ€™ fast-growing digital ad sales effort and day-to-day operations. This person will report to the Senior Vice President, Regional Networks and be expected to lead key initiatives with limited supervision.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Support local sales teams with key clients and prospects (e.g., identify sales opportunities, conduct client research, design key selling points, build and quality control sales presentations, attend sales meetings)</li>
<li>Analyze RSN sales performance on on-going basis (e.g., maintain up-to-date pricing and pacing reports, maintain sales performance metrics)</li>
<li>Facilitate best practice sharing between RSN sales teams (e.g., distribute client lists and success stories, provide marketplace updates)</li>
<li>Lead growth initiatives such as new category growth plans and implementation of new sales tools</li>
<li>Support RSN digital ad sales, which includes driving the digital ad sales strategy and support as well as operations and campaign execution</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Qualifications/Requirements</b></p>

<li>Deep analytical skills to bring robust insight to RSN sales teams</li>

<li>Extensive experience with Microsoft Excel and PowerPoint</li>
<li>Relevant experience and deep interest in advertising sales</li>
<li>Expected to spend approximately 20% of time traveling to regional offices</li>

</ul>
