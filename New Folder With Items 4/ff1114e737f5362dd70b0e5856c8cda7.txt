<h2>Guide</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Gather is a community-oriented boutique play space and enrichment center in Evanston, IL that is deeply focused on creativity and learning for 0-5 year olds.</p>
<p>
Because we believe heavily in the value and power of diversity, our team comes from a wide variety of backgrounds, working together to build unique experiences for children and their favorite grown-ups centered around creativity and connection.</p>
<p>
We are currently looking for part-time guides with the flexibility to work 5-15 hours per week, mostly on the weekends.
</p>
<p>If you love working around children and families, value creativity, and understand the important role community plays in leading meaningful lives, this job may be right for you!</p>
<p><b>
Responsibilities</b></p>
<p>
*Ensure a clean and inviting space by keeping the store tidy and germ-free, maintaining and organizing craft supplies, and disinfecting toys *Welcoming families *Anticipating and addressing customer needs *Managing the point of sales system and customer software *Encouraging fun, play, and creative expression through games, play, and art projects *Opening and closing store *Implementing drop-in activities, as needed, such as engaging children and their caregivers in dance parties, art projects, and story time.
</p>
<p><b>Qualifications</b></p>
<p>
Our ideal candidate is attentive, motivated, and engaged. Additionally you should have / be :</p>
<p>
*Experience keeping a clean and safe environment *Experience being around children *Strong people skills, and enjoy solving problems and being helpful *Ability to maintain composure and multi-task when the store gets busy *Self-starter *Detail oriented *Warm and fun
</p>
<p>We are very much looking forward to your application!</p>
<p>Available shifts and compensation: We have available shifts all days of the week. Compensation is $12.00 - $15.00/hour.</p>
