<div>
 <b>Qualifications</b>
</div>
<div></div>
<ul>
<li>
Functional/technical proficiency with at least 2+ full lifecycle SAP implementations and 4+ years of industry and/or consulting experience</li>
<li>
Experience implementing SAP HANA Analytics, plus SAP S/4 HANA, plus HANA Cloud Platform (HCP) - Cloud for Analytics, Digital Board Room, plus BOBJ. ]</li>
<li>
Qualified candidates will have experience with HANA Data Modeling, BW/4, and Native HANA</li>
<li>
Experience defining systems strategy, developing systems requirements, designing and prototyping, testing, training, defining support procedures and implementing practical business solutions under multiple deadlines</li>
<li>
Adept at implementing technology-enabled business solutions for clients as part of a high-talent team and as a Team Lead or Project Manager on at least 1+ full cycle implementation</li>
<li>
Able to collaborate with clients and have a strong desire to excel and be committed to gaining exposure to multiple industries while further developing your career</li>
<li>
Strong configuration and design skills</li>
<li>
Ability to travel 80-100% is REQUIRED</li>

Bachelor’s degree or equivalent required - Master’s degree preferred</li>
