<h2>Inside Sales Coordinator (Bilingual, Spanish)</h2>

<div>
<p><b>Inside Sales Coordinator (Bilingual, Spanish)</b></p>
<p></p>
<p><b><br>
Location:</b> Miami, FL</p>
<p><b>
Opportunity</b></p>
<p>
KOHLER Power Systems, one of the largest power generator manufacturers in the world, has delivered energy solutions worldwide since 1920. KOHLER Power Systems offers power generation equipment for residential, industrial, mobile and marine applications as well as automatic transfer switches, switchgear, monitoring controls and accessories. For more information about KOHLER Power Systems, please visit <b>www.kohlerpower.com</b>.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
Kohler Power Systems is hiring for an Inside Sales Coordinator in our Miami, Florida office. Reporting to the General Manager (SDMO LATIN AMERICA), you will act as the liaison between the distributor and the factory, providing a high level of customer service.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
 <b>Specific Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Quickly and accurately process incoming orders. Order processing includes tasks such as checking the bill of material for factory changes, holding quote pricing and discounts, setting lead times for delivery, and setting up the order to reflect the customers shipping and billing preferences.</li>
<li>
Create deliveries and shipments when system does not generate or requires consolidation. Create air shipments.</li>
<li>
Maintain the orders pending processing list, production note updates, customer inquiry logs, shipping status reports, complaint tracking reports and any other assigned reports.</li>
<li>
Resolve invoice issues such as credit requests and customer deductions promptly.</li>
<li>
Learn to look up and use sales order, schedule, production, shipping, and invoice information in Kohler Power Systems Enterprise System (QAD).</li>
<li>
Follow up at least weekly on all assigned distributors’ open orders and provide distributors with timely updates about order status changes.</li>
<li>
Understand and use customer service reference documents including ISO procedures, product dimension sheets, product specification sheets, price lists, inventory reports, and lead-time reports.</li>
<li>
Administer policies that govern pricing, order entry, order change, order status inquiry, and any other policy that relates to the Sales Administration Department.</li>
<li>
Learn the responsibilities of coworkers in order to back them up in their absences.</li>
<li>
Prepare and submit field complaints as required to document field problems.</li>
<li>
Create custom invoices for national accounts and comply with other special handling requirements which may include working in vendor portals.</li>
<li>
Assist other sales coordinators with their workload and perform other related duties as assigned.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Skills/Requirements</b></p>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>
<br>
Minimum 2 years related inside sales experience is required</li>
<li>
Fluent in Spanish required</li>
<li>
Microsoft Office 365 (Outlook, Word, Excel, and Access) skills are required</li>
<li>
Ability to travel up to 25% of the time is required</li>
<li>
Bachelors OR Associates Degree is strongly preferred</li>
<li>
A background in make-to-order manufacturing or distribution is preferred</li>
<li>
Power Generation Industry experience is preferred</li>
<li>
Must be able to work out of our office at: 3801 Commerce Parkway Miramar, FL 33025</li>
</ul>
