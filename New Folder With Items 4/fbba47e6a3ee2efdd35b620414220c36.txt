<p><b>Job Summary</b></p>
<p>
We are looking for a <b>Project Engineer</b> to design, service, and supervise the installation, and commissioning of HVAC Control Systems in South Central Wisconsin. Our ideal candidate strives for excellence in customer satisfaction, has experience in customer interaction, and provides continuous communication. He/She must be technically competent and be able to mentor fellow technicians on the topic of Building Automation, and basic service activities.</p>
<p><b>
Description of responsibilities</b></p>
<p>
PRINCIPAL DUTIES:</p>
<ul>
<li>Design of HVAC control systems based upon project requirements or industry standards.</li>
<li>Develop graphical user interfaces based upon project requirements using industry graphical assembly tools.</li>
<li>Consult with the customer, provides proposals for retrofits, upgrades and design enhancements to systems.</li>
<li>Provide technical recommendations for sales leads.</li>
<li>Manage assigned work to meet or exceed customer expectations. Provides sketches of field changes and discrepancies for engineering corrections and drawings.</li>
<li>Promote the sale of additional technical support and work for the customer.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Qualifications</b></p>
<p><b>
The Ideal Candidate will possess</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Experience with Niagara Framework, Lonworks, BACnet and graphical programming languages.</li>
<li>5+ years applications engineering experience in Building Management Systems and HVAC principles and basics.</li>
<li>Working knowledge of Internet related technologies and programming along with web based graphics.. Ability to read blueprints and interpret specifications.</li>
<li>Knowledge of heating, ventilating and air conditioning equipment and related DDC and building management systems</li>
<li>Bachelor of Engineering Degree in Mechanical or Electrical Engineering, Knowledge of Computers and computer software packages including but not limited to (AutoCAD, VISIO, MS Office, Explorer, and HTML).</li>
<li>Requires a valid driver license.</li>
<li>Must be authorized to work in USA.</li>
</ul>
