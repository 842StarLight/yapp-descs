<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Job Description
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<p>Conduent is seeking a director-level new logo sales executive to champion profitable BPO/BPS sales growth through consultative sales, closing opportunities by leveraging deep Business Process Services/ Outsourcing knowledge and expertise. The Incumbent will have demonstrated experience and background in dealing within the Retail, Travel, and Logistics (RTL) industry.</p>
<p>
This position is remote and the incumbent must reside within the Atlanta metro area.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Duties and Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Will be responsible for driving sales of business process services.</li>
<li>
Sales to new clients in the Retail, Travel and Logistics industry verticals.</li>
<li>
Building a very select pipeline of key prospects with the goal of closing large, strategic opportunities.</li>
<li>
Engage product marketing and solutions expertise to refine service offerings for specific market segments.</li>
<li>
Cross-sell and collaborate on across enterprise solutions.</li>
<li>
Recommend and initiate enhancements to the sales and marketing operation.</li>
<li>
Establish goals, analyze growth and provide accurate, complete and timely submission of required progress reports, forecasts, quotations, budgets and rates.</li>
<li>
Demonstrate advanced knowledge of consultative sales strategy.</li>
<li>
Demonstrate experience with Challenger sales.</li>
<li>
Strategize, recommend and consult with partners in operations, product groups and support groups to leverage Conduent’s capabilities to close sales deals.</li>
<li>
Work closely with pricing and finance to develop profitable solutions.</li>
<li>
All other duties as assigned.</li>
</ul>
<p>
 <b>Experience:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
5-10 yrs. of demonstrated Hunter/New Logo sales Experience.</li>
<li>
5+ years Business Process Services and Solutions sales experience.</li>
<li>
Experience in selling Finance, Accounting and Procurement (FA&amp;P) outsourcing, Human Resources Services, AND/OR End User Experience solutions (EUE) is <i>required</i>.</li>
<li>
Demonstrated success of selling into Retail, Travel and/or Logistics Fortune 1000 companies.</li>
<li>
Strong consultative seller.</li>

An aggressive sales executive who can forge key relationships with CEO's, CFO’s and other C-suite executives as well as other key business contacts, along with internally aligning support for contract execution.</li>

Proven ability to develop new BPS business and proven success in meeting sales quotas ($3 million + ACV).</li>
<li>
A strong and capable leader that is self-motivated and driven to win the confidence and trust of his/her prospects, clients, global operations, and pursuit teams alike.</li>
<li>
Track record in closing complex BPS deals and building relationship with C-suite.</li>
<li>
Innovative team player with the energy, creativity and an entrepreneurial spirit to achieve success. A professional who is respected for his/her leadership, intelligence and expertise.</li>
<li>
Superb relationship builder, negotiator, and communicator.</li>
<li>
Bachelor’s degree or equivalent experience.</li>
