<h2>Full Desk Recruiter - Healthcare</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">AtWork Medical is seeking a successful Healthcare Account Manager to join our internal healthcare staffing team in Phoenix, AZ. Join the fun and be part of an award-winning, growing organization. AtWork Medical has achieved the coveted Diamond Award, Best in Staffing - Client Satisfaction six years in a row and recently recognized for the fifth consecutive year with the Best in Staffing - Talent Satisfaction Award. <br>Our Healthcare Account Manager will join the spotlight in our Medical Division and manage full desk responsibilities with a core focus on business development and balancing recruiting initiatives to service your healthcare clients. Having the ability to adapt to change, work in a fast paced environment, execute with precision, and perform with a "quality first" mindset are keys to success in this role.
<br>The ideal Healthcare Account Manager will possess the following:
<br><ul>
<li>Identify new business relationships through prospecting techniques to define a narrowed target list in a niche industry.</li>
<li>Negotiate staffing agreements for optimal pricing values for both agency and client and track close ratios against fills and cost of vacancy.</li>
<li>Conduct client presentations to educate hiring managers and key stakeholders the value of AtWork Medical and our full-service portfolio.</li>
<li>Manage all account activity to include administrative, follow-up calls, site visits, payroll coordination, etc. as needed to ensure 100% client satisfaction.</li>
<li>Uncover new opportunities within the account and initiate introductions via email and in person monthly while keeping records in CRM database.</li>
<li>Proactively build candidate pipeline for open positions.</li>
<li>Manage accurate records in ATS database for applicant workflow.</li>
<li>Communicate with national sales teams and sales executives on account metrics, analytics, and trends as requested.</li>
<li>Ensure all open seats are filled to exceed client expectations.</li>
<li>Conduct interviews, reference checking, administer assessments, and background checks as required to ensure and uphold quality candidate screening requirements.</li>
<li>Work in tandem with other recruiters as needed.</li>
<li>Manage the reporting for all account management and recruitment initiatives and present to the executive team.</li>
<li>Manage all medical scheduling responsibilities to ensure client shift coverage needs are met.</li>
</ul>
Healthcare Account Manager Qualifications
<br><ul>
<li>Minimum 3 years of experience in healthcare recruiting with business development and account management responsibilities.</li>
<li>College degree or equivalent education and training.</li>
<li>Performance-based experience measured by KPI's.</li>
<li>Staffing agency experience a plus!</li>
<li>Excellent written and verbal communication.</li>
<li>Advanced knowledge of MS Office, Social Media Platforms, and ATS/CRM software.</li>
<li>Innovative, growth mindset with a "get it done" work ethic.</li>
<li>Ability to work extended hours as needed.</li>
</ul>
