<h2>Logistics Project Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Summary: </b></p>
<p>The Logistics Project Manager is responsible for the overall management of the development and the coordination of operational implementation for new therapy centers. The Project Manager will provide end-to-end project management oversight for all activities that support the completion of a specified project effort. The project manager will need to make quick decisions, be able to work independently in a fast paced, challenging environment and be able to lead the project team through the project execution process. This will include, but not limited to, scope development; design reviews; some construction inspection; coordination of systems and coordination of the installation of the communications systems; final walkout and acceptance of the project; and closing out the project.</p>
<p><b>Responsibilities: </b></p>
<p><i>Facility Development: </i></p>
<ul>
<li>Coordination of potential facility sites with executive team</li>
<li>Coordination of site visits of potential sites</li>
<li>Coordination of lease negotiation and execution</li>
<li>Coordination of floor plan development</li>
<li>Development, adjustment and maintenance of the furniture, fixtures and equipment (FF&amp;E) budget (to include IT, Phones)</li>
<li>Coordination of the acquisition and accounting for all FF&amp;E items</li>
<li>Management of the delivery and set up of all FF&amp;E items</li>
<li>Set up of all utilities, phone and data lines, and cable service (if needed)</li>
</ul>
<p><i>Operational Implementation: </i></p>
<ul>
<li>Effectively coordinate with related departments (patient intake, clinical care, scheduling, etc.) to ensure positive patient and referral source experiences</li>
<li>Coordinate and manage opening day with all staff</li>
<li>Communicate with executive staff opening day and all necessary announcements</li>
<li>Collaborate with the leadership team to develop relevant metrics that provide reliable insight into the performance of the development process</li>
</ul>
<p><i>Regulatory and Payer Notifications: </i></p>
<ul><li>Coordination with executive staff all payer and regulatory notifications for the new locations</li></ul>
<p><b>Requirements: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Proven, measurable track record with minimum of 3 years of project management experience in “operationalizing” business model strategy (healthcare services industry experience is preferred)</li>
<li>Bachelor’s degree required in related field</li>
</ul>
<p><i>Technical Skills: </i></p>
<ul>
<li>Demonstrated technical competencies in MS Word, Excel, PowerPoint; proficiency in sales management software such as Salesforce or MS Dynamics</li>
<li>Effective management – organizational skills regarding goal setting/ measurement/ monitoring</li>
</ul>
<p><i>Behavioral Skills: </i></p>
<ul>
<li>Strong emotional intelligence</li>
<li>Highly self-motivated</li>
<li>Strong organizational skills</li>
<li>Ability to set goals and stick to strict deadlines to achieve them</li>
<li>Leadership and management</li>
<li>Team-building</li>
</ul>
<p><i>Physical Requirements: </i></p>
<ul>
<li>Travel to company facilities, referral sources, professional meetings and company-sponsored events as required</li>
<li>Occasionally lift and/or move up to 30 pounds facilitating move-in to new facility</li>
</ul>
<p>Job Type: Full-time</p>
<p>Work authorization:</p>
<ul><li>United States (Preferred)</li></ul>
