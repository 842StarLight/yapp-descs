<h2>General Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Key Contribution:</b> The <b>General Manager</b> contributes to the success of McAlisters through the direction and control of restaurant operations to ensure a positive guest experience and company profitability. The <b>General Managers</b> responsibility is to direct the operation of the restaurant, attain sales and profit objectives, maintain the highest standards of food quality, service, cleanliness, safety and sanitation by directing and supervising managers and team members.</p>
<p>
<b>Reporting Relationship:</b> The General Manager reports to the Area Director</p>
<p>
<b>Essential Functions:</b></p>
<ul>
<li><p>Ensure a High Quality Operation
</p></li>
<li><p>Maintain Controls
</p></li>
<li><p>Manage Team Members
</p></li>
<li><p>Advocate a Team Environment
</p></li>
<li>
<p>Manage Personal Development
</p>
<p><b>Ensure a High Quality Operation</b>
</p>
</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li><p>Provides Team Members, Managers, and Guests with a positive experience and atmosphere.
</p></li>
<li><p>Ensures prompt, friendly service according to McAlisters guidelines.
</p></li>
<li><p>Ensures the production of high quality food on a consistent basis.
</p></li>
<li><p>Directs overall activities and performance of team members on a shift-by-shift basis.
</p></li>
<li><p>Drives positive Guest experience through a front door and floor presence and timely delivery of accurate food orders.
</p></li>
<li><p>Ensures positive Guest experience by monitoring and ensuring appropriate steps of service and delivery of the McAlisters Touch.
</p></li>
<li><p>Maintains the proper atmosphere through music and lighting levels for varying business periods.
</p></li>
<li><p>Promotes an atmosphere of positive Guest Relations. Build Guest relationships and respond to Guest complaints or concerns professionally and in a timely manner. Responsible for written communications with Guests.
</p></li>
<li><p>Establishes a positive relationship with the local authorities and seek their help in managing regulatory and governance related issues.
</p></li>
<li><p>Responsible for assisting with the development of sales and marketing strategies and for implementing, advertising, and promoting campaigns. Responsible to support and implement Support Center - corporate initiatives.
</p></li>
<li><p>Maintains and inspects dining room, food receiving, preparation, production, and storage areas to ensure that health and safety regulations are adhered to at all times. Corrects unsafe practices or conditions.
</p></li>
<li><p>Supervises cleaning and maintenance of equipment and arrange for repairs, contracts, and other services. Maintains restaurant cleanliness and organization for both the interior and exterior of the restaurant.
</p></li>
<li>
<p>Follows the standards as described in the Policy &amp; Procedures Manual, Red Line Crisis Guide, and Operations Manual.
</p>
<p><b>Maintain Controls</b>
</p>
</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li><p>Reviews financial information such as sales and costs and monitor budget to ensure efficient operation and that expenditures stay within budget limitations. Takes action to correct any deviations from the budget. Use the computer for review and analysis of information.
</p></li>
<li><p>Responsible for the financial results of the restaurant.
</p></li>
<li><p>Maintains and controls restaurant assets. Monitors restaurants on-hand inventory for waste and theft.
</p></li>
<li><p>Controls service contract; follow through on all work done, ensure proper billing and compare costs on a semi-annual basis with other purveyors.
</p></li>
<li><p>Conducts line checks to ensure product standards and specifications.
</p></li>
<li><p>Ensures adequate inventory levels that allow for Guest needs but do not tie up company assets in unneeded and costly inventory.
</p></li>
<li><p>Completes daily paperwork and projects on a timely basis.
</p></li>
<li><p>Complies with cash control policies and procedures. Ensures all managers, supervisors, and team members comply with cash control policy and procedures.
</p></li>
<li><p>Drives positive results for labor through proactive scheduling and reacting to business trends.
</p></li>
<li><p>Controls operating costs by instituting awareness through the restaurant.
</p></li>
<li><p>Manages capital expenditures within the restaurant.
</p></li>
<li><p>Conducts inventories and calculate food and beverage costs. Use computer to facilitate collection and analysis of information. Respond to discrepancies from budgeted costs.
</p></li>
<li><p>May at times be responsible for ordering food and beverage products. May be responsible for receiving and checking orders to ensure that they adhere to company specifications.
</p></li>
<li><p>At times will direct the kitchen activities and performance of back of the house team members on a shift-by-shift basis.
</p></li>
<li>
<p>Responsible for submitting accurate information to the Support Center and Area Director.
</p>
<p><b>Manage Team Members</b>
</p>
</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li><p>Ensures quality recruiting and training of new managers and supervisors.
</p></li>
<li><p>Hires, trains, motivates, and evaluates all team members.
</p></li>
<li><p>Creates positive relationships with team members and managers by treating all members with respect.
</p></li>
<li><p>Minimizes turnover by responding to and resolving team member and manager issues.
</p></li>
<li><p>Supports and practices an open door policy.
</p></li>
<li><p>Implements programs to reward positive contributions. Recognizes and promotes positive performers. Coaches and counsels team members whose performance is below expectations.
</p></li>
<li><p>Develops team members. Ensures that timely performance reviews of team members are conducted. Conducts performance reviews of management team.
</p></li>
<li><p>Maintains a trained staff through effective use of employment orientation, individual training sessions, employee meetings and implementation of company training programs for team members and managers.
</p></li>
<li>
<p>Assures compliance with company policies, practices and procedures; communicate changes. Ensure compliance with all local, state, and federal laws, regulations, and guidelines.
</p>
<p><b>Advocate a Team Environment</b>
</p>
</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li><p>Displays, encourages, and inspires high morale and motivation in the restaurant.
</p></li>
<li><p>Conducts team meetings to plan menus and related activities, plans special events, shares information, or conducts supplemental training.
</p></li>
<li><p>Communicates with other managers using the managers log Red Book on a daily basis.
</p></li>
<li><p>Shows daily attention, participation and sensitivity needed to establish credibility and promotes unity in the team.
</p></li>
<li><p>Holds management team accountable for areas of responsibility.
</p></li>
<li><p>Responds to team conflicts professionally and work towards positive resolutions.
</p></li>
<li><p>Sets a good example through attitude, involvement, and positive influence.
</p></li>
<li><p>Establishes an environment that does not permit or promote sexual or any other kinds of harassment.
</p></li>
<li><p>Ensures restaurant is staffed to avoid work load and poor morale issues.
</p></li>
<li><p>Reports employee relation issues of a sensitive nature to the Human Resources department.
</p></li>
<li>
<p>Works with Human Resources and the Area Director for the positive outcome of such issues.
</p>
<p><b>Manage Personal Development</b>
</p>
</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li><p>Provides effective leadership.
</p></li>
<li><p>Demonstrates organizational skills. Completes assignments and duties on time.
</p></li>
<li><p>Develops personal goals for professional growth.
</p></li>
<li><p>Maintains a pleasant, positive, and professional attitude in the eyes of team members, management team, and Guests.
</p></li>
<li><p>Exhibits a neat and clean appearance consistent with a professional image.
</p></li>
<li>
<p>Executes company programs and decisions with support and commitment.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>General Manager Qualifications:</b>
</p>
<p><b>Aptitude</b>
</p>
</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li><p>Professional oral and written communication skills. Must be able to speak, read, write, and understand the primary language of the restaurant.
</p></li>
<li><p>Organization and time management skills.
</p></li>
<li><p>Works well under pressure.
</p></li>
<li><p>Can effectively solve problems.
</p></li>
<li><p>Able to take and give direction.
</p></li>
<li><p>Can be flexible as needs dictate.
</p></li>
<li><p>Works well with other people in a team environment.
</p></li>
<li><p>Service driven.
</p></li>
<li><p>Excellent attendance is required with schedule flexibility determined by business needs.
</p></li>
<li>
<p>Must be able to work all shifts when the restaurant is open.
</p>
<p><b>Experience</b>
</p>
</li>
</ul>
<ul><li><p>Two (2) year associates degree or a bachelors degree from a four (4) year college (desirable).
</p></li></ul>
<ul>
<li><p>Minimum of Two (2) years prior experience as a General Manager for a full service, quick casual, or fast food high volume restaurant.
</p></li>
<li>
<p>If being considered for internal promotion, must have Three (3) years Assistant Manager experience, or Area Director approval.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Physical Requirements</b>
</p>
</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li><p>Must be able to work in a fast paced, high energy, and physically demanding environment.
</p></li>
<li><p>Must have the stamina and ability to work 50 to 60 hours a week. Must be able to occasionally work more than a 60 hour work week during short management staff situations.
</p></li>
<li><p>Must be able to spend 90% of working time standing.
</p></li>
<li><p>Must be able to spend 10% of working time sitting.
</p></li>
<li><p>Will be required to use all sensory capabilities such as: vision, hearing, tasting, smelling, touching, and speaking.
</p></li>
<li><p>Will be required to use physical capabilities such as: walking, bending, kneeling, handling, hand flexibility, reaching, squatting, crawling, lifting, climbing, and stooping.
</p></li>
<li><p>Must be able to carry loads greater than 35 pounds, and be able to transport up to 70 pounds regularly.
</p></li>
<li><p>Must be able to communicate clearly with our Guests in the primary language of the restaurant, specific to location. (Primarily English).
</p></li>
<li><p>Must be able to hear with 100% accuracy with correction.
</p></li>
<li><p>Must be able to see to 20/20 vision with correction.
</p></li>
<li><p>Travel may be required for occasional deliveries, visits to other locations, or company meetings. The General Manager is required to have reliable transportation to meet these travel requirements.<br>
</p></li>
</ul>
