<h2>Contract Administrator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Reports to:</b> Director of Contracts</p>
<p><b>
Summary:</b></p>
<p>
USA Millwork is looking for a Contract Administrator to complete contract reviews and revisions company wide.</p>
<p><b>
Essential Duties &amp; Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Manages the receipt, multiple reviews, and execution of a high volume of contracts, notices to proceed, letters of intent, purchase orders, and other related documentation and their revisions for multiple entities ensuring all conditions are timely satisfied</li>
<li>
Tracks and enforces internal review deadlines and meets all customer submission deadlines with exceptional accuracy</li>
<li>
Maintains the correct status of contracts at all times across all levels of review for all entities—complete an audit of existing files</li>
<li>
Reports to Director of Contracts and works with and assists company Vice Presidents, Estimators, Project Managers, customers, Sales Managers, Executives, and others to keep contracts moving, provide proposed revisions, and accurately convey and coordinate feedback to the appropriate party</li>
<li>
Works closely with and communicates regularly with all departments for compliance, reporting, accounting, insurance, bonding, and other contract requirements</li>
<li>
Maintains electronic contract databases and reporting with correctness—uploading documents and entering data to include the use of SharePoint, Word, Adobe, Excel, and other similar software.</li>
<li>
Assists with and maintains the execution, organization, and reporting of internal subcontractor agreements</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Supervisory Responsibility</b><br>
<br>
This position has no supervisory responsibilities.</p>
<p><b>
Physical Demands</b></p>
<p>
This is a largely sedentary role; however. some filing is required. This would require the ability to lift files, open filing cabinets and bend or stand as necessary.</p>
<p><b>
Other Duties:</b></p>
<p>
Please note that this job description is not designed to cover or contain a comprehensive listing or activities, duties or responsibilities that are required of the employee for this job. Duties, responsibilities and activities may change at any time with or without notice.</p>
<p><b>
Preferred Education and Skills (in addition to the required):
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor's degree</li>
<li>
Intermediate to advanced MS Excel skills</li>
<li>
Contracting experience with general contractors, subcontractors, millwork, and/or the construction industry</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Required Education and Skills:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>High school diploma</li>
<li>
Ability to manage a high volume of contracts and correspondence to include emails and phone calls with extraordinary accuracy in a fast-paced environment</li>
<li>
Strong administrative, organizational, and time-management skills</li>
<li>
Solid and professional verbal, interpersonal, and written communication and negotiation skills—must enjoy picking up the phone and calling others to negotiate, follow-up, and resolve problems</li>
<li>
Exceptional attention to detail and professionalism in all tasks</li>
<li>
Ability to review contracts, manage the contract life cycle, and extract data with correctness</li>
<li>
Efficiently utilize software and databases such as SharePoint, Word, Excel, and Adobe with ease</li>
<li>
Ability to recognize risks, problems solve, and analyze data</li>
<li>
Self-motivated and reliable with a strong work ethic</li>
</ul>
