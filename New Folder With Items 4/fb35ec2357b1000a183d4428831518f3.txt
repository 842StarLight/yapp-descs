SUMMARY:<br>
This position is responsible for administering the Occupational Health Services for three chemical plants in the upstate of South Carolina. The primary location will be the Allen Plant in Blacksburg, SC with additional coverage at the Dewey and Keystone Plants in Inman, SC. The position provides clinic services during the regular work week, Monday through Friday. Additional hours may be required for incidental testing and routinely scheduled annual events, such as Emergency Response Team physicals. This position will report to the Director of Human Resources.<br>
<br>
ESSENTIAL FUNCTIONS:<br>
1. Conduct and coordinate health assessments and examinations as needed for:<br>
a) Pre-employment physicals, annual/bi-annual/compliance employment physicals, return-to-work physicals, and incidental and chronic health conditions (as the condition impacts ability to perform regular job duties)<br>
b) Coordinate FMLA administration, ADAA evaluation, medical leave documentation and return-to-work case management.<br>
2. Conduct pre-employment, random, post-incident and "for cause" substance abuse screening for all associates.<br>
3. Provide coordination of care to workers with chronic health conditions and provide maintenance of care within established protocols, to assist associates and ensure work does not negatively impact the conditions.<br>
4. Communicate with management regarding associate medical leave of absences and return to work timelines.<br>
5. Manage all aspects of the location health clinics, including ordering supplies, ensuring current equipment calibrations and ensuring proper disposal of expired supplies.<br>
<br>
QUALIFICATIONS:<br>
1. Current license to practice as a registered nurse (RN) in the State of South Carolina required.<br>
2. Bachelor's Degree in Nursing or equivalent education and experience required.<br>
3. Certified Occupational Health Nurse (COHN) or experience in Occupational Health nursing environment desired.<br>
4. Instructor level Certifications in First Aid / CPR / AED preferred.<br>
<br>
Candidates should possess the following:<br>
1. Strong written and verbal communication skills<br>
2. Knowledge of MS Office Software: Outlook, Word, Excel and PowerPoint.<br>
3. Ability to work effectively and collaboratively with a wide range of staff while maintaining strict confidentiality.<br>
4. Ability to create effective training materials and present training to small and medium-size audiences.<br>
<br>
WORK SCHEDULE:<br>
Typical week is Monday to Friday, 40 hours, Flexible hours but normal hours would be 8AM-5PM<br>
