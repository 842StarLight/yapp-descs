<h2>Business Solution Architect.1278810.SP</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p><b>What You'll Do</b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>Cisco seeks a passionate Customer Solutions Architect (CSA), that will be working as a trusted advisor and partner to a leading Global Investment Firm. You will be an integral member of the team, working towards our common goals and demonstrating the business value of Cisco Solutions and Architectures by aligning opportunities with a 2-5 year architectural vision.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Responsibilities:</b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>You will provide a consultative relationship with the customer and collaborate with Product teams to develop customer focused architectures driving industry-wide innovation.</li>
<li>You will lead the architectural design associated with Cisco's technology strategy in alignment with key business drivers and market issues for individual customers.</li>
<li>You will demonstrate business relevance to executive stakeholders.</li>
<li>You will identify top opportunities and design top-down future state architectures and associated Cisco technical strategy.</li>
<li>You will build creative architectural solutions to take on customer business problems.</li>
<li>You will deliver and/or support delivery of technical presentations to customers, as well as deliver architecture selling concept and methodology in Partner CTO/SE events to committed Cisco partners.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Who You'll Work With</b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>You will work with a closely aligned Client Executive, relevant account teams across the globe as well as serve as the lead advocate for these customers within Cisco. You will coordinate with senior client leadership, industry partners and other appropriate services and organizations to provide a vision terms of technology focus and relationship development.</p>
<p><b>Who You Are</b></p>
<ul>
<li>You've got at least 7 years of networking / architecture and field experience working with named accounts as a Systems Engineer or equivalent.</li>
<li>You are a senior customer focused technical and architectural engineering sales professional with knowledge of customer specific business initiators and networking environment, including adjacent technology and solution areas.</li>
<li>You have deep understanding of the financial industry trends.</li>
<li>You are a strong collaborator, and a great communicator.</li>
<li>You have a Bachelor's degree from a reputable university.</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>You have Software Development Experience</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p>
</p>
<p><b>The key performance traits for this role include:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Creating Business Relevance</li>
<li>Solution and Architectural Selling</li>
<li>Building Competitive Intelligence</li>
<li>Working Across Boundaries</li>
<li>Co-ordination of technical teams to get the best result for the customer</li>
<li>Driving the Cisco Vision</li>
<br>
</ul>
