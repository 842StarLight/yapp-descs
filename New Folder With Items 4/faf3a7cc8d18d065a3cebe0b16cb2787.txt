<h2>Business Analyst</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<div>
<p>The Business Analyst I translates detailed business needs into application software requirements. The primary focus is gathering and documenting business requirements, as well as writing technical requirements. Business Analysts acts as a liaison with users of the software to address questions/issues. They determine specific business application software requirements to address business needs and work with programming staff to ensure requirements are incorporated into system design and testing.</p>
<div>
Responsibilities<br>
<ul>
<li>Reviews, analyzes, and evaluates business systems and user needs.</li>
<li>Documents requirements, defines scope and objectives, and formulates systems to parallel overall business strategies.</li>
<li>Proactively communicate and collaborate with external and internal customers to analyze information needs and functional requirements</li>
<li>Deliver the following as needed:</li>
</ul>
 o Eliciting and writing software requirements.<br>
 o Requirements organization.<br>
 o Translating and simplifying requirements for technical and non-technical staff.<br>
 o Requirements management and communication.<br>
 o Requirements analysis<br>
<ul>
<li>Assist in developing test scripts, use cases and testing.</li>
<li>System and user documentation.</li>
<li>Assist in development of design documents</li>
<li>Adhere to Service Delivery Life Cycle processes</li>
<li>Template and/or business process development, as needed</li>
<li>Management of multiple initiatives and projects</li>
<li>Reviewing material and assisting with sales presentations</li>
</ul>
</div>
<div>
Qualifications:<br>
<ul>
<li>BA/BS in Computer Science, Information Systems, Business Administration or other related field or equivalent experience</li>
<li>Experience with Microsoft Suite</li>
<li>Minimum of 3 years of experience with business analysis that includes writing business requirements</li>
<li>Reference manual content in compliance with the life cycle methodology</li>
<li>Experience in the development of application specifications and flowcharts</li>
</ul>
</div>
