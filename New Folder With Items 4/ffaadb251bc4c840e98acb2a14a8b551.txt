<p><b>About Us: </b></p>
<p>Highlands Energy Services an Alvarado Group company is a coalition of companies that includes civil engineering and construction company Vanguard Construction Services, general contracting company Highlands Diversified, and Highlands Energy Services, which focuses on energy conservation.</p>
<p>Highlands Energy is a Minority Business Enterprise (MBE), Disabled Veteran Business Enterprise (DBVE) and is fully bonded, licensed, and insured.</p>
<p><b>Job Summary</b></p>
<p>Our company Highlands Energy Services provides no-cost weatherization, home repairs, and appliance upgrade services to qualified renters and homeowners with PG&amp;E and SoCal Gas.</p>
<p>Our Energy Specialist, through an in-home audit, verify eligibility of our PG&amp;E customer-base through the Energy Savings Assistance Program.</p>
<p>Our goal is to provide energy conservation through energy efficient measures through home improvement upgrades and replacements.</p>
