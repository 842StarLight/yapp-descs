<h2>Worldwide Account Manager-Specialty Sales</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<b>Company:</b> FedEx Services<br>
<b>Job Title:</b> Worldwide Account Manager-Specialty Sales<br>
<b>Job Requisition Number:</b> RC199283<br>
<b>Category:</b> Sales/Solutions<br>
<b>Locations:</b><br>
East Boston, Massachusetts 02128<br>
United States<br>
<br>
<p>Responsible for new business development, account implementation, and maintaining and deepening customer relationships within a defined geographic territory. Provides face-to-face customer contact, with a focus on achieving personal sales targets and market goals to close and activate new business with mid to large sized local and national customers. The face-to-face service to customers is in a particular geographic area, and living within a reasonable Commuting distance of the territory is an important determinant of effectiveness and success in this role.<br>
<br>
<b>General Duties and Responsibilities</b><br>
This is a representative list of the general duties the position may be asked to perform, and is not intended to be all-inclusive:<br>
</p>
<ul>
<li>
Meet or exceed sales quotas in existing accounts and territory</li>
<li>
Schedule and conduct face-to-face sales calls to include cold calling activity</li>
<li>
Create and present value-added business solutions to accounts, within prescribed guidelines</li>
<li>
Build rapport with customers using a consultative sales approach</li>
<li>
Serve as the subject matter expert for SDC and support the primary relationship owner(s) for accounts in territory to ensure proper service and retention</li>
<li>
Build and maintain a pipeline of business at established standards</li>
<li>
Interact with Sales leadership to develop and review sales strategies for sales accounts and prospects</li>
<li>
Drive target products and promotions, as directed</li>
<li>
Work with other sales resources in a team selling capacity to include involvement with other FedEx operating companies</li>
<li>
Maintain accurate customer relationship management systems, including daily activities and account updates</li>
<li>
Prospect for new accounts and provides relevant information to be considered for the account base</li>
<li>
Meet all minimum activity standards consistently</li>
</ul>
<br>
<b>
Domicile: Boston metro area</b><br>
<b>Pay Band: SL</b><br>

<b>Minimum Qualifications:</b><br>
Bachelor's Degree/equivalent. Five (5) years directly related commercial or industrial sales experience. Demonstrated ability to achieve account sales goals and to plan and execute major customer sales strategies. Strong human relations, communication and negotiation skills. Minimal overnight travel required.<br>
