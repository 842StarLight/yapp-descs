<h2>Office Administrator/Marketing</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>Accident Cleaners &amp; Restoration, LLC is currently seeking a full time professional for our growing remediation and restoration company. Main service line includes emergency remediation services for biohazard cleanup and construction related restoration services. Our goal is to restore homes affected by events such as homicide, suicide, and unexpected death.</p>
<p>
The Office Administrator is responsible for administrative duties associated with processing insurance claims, working as a liaison with clients, day to day administrative and marketing support and coordination. This is an excellent opportunity for a highly motivated, obsessively organized individual with real people skills.</p>
<p>
If you enjoy working as a part of a cohesive team built on integrity, passion and commitment with a proven company culture, this could be your opportunity.
</p>
<p><b>Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
General office upkeep and organization of files, including ordering office supplies and marketing materials</li>
<li>
Provide client support by assisting in explaining services, fielding questions, intake of new job information and coordinating services</li>
<li>
Maintain high level of attention to detail and accuracy</li>
<li>
File system management, organizing and maintaining physical paper files and electronic documentation</li>
<li>
Accounts receivable support, preparing and submit claims packets to insurance companies and follow through to collection of payment</li>
<li>
Maintain accurate data entry records into CRM system for all activities related to claims submission, processing and payment</li>
<li>
Represent the company with a professional, helpful, friendly and relationship driven attitude</li>
<li>
Manage the company's social media presence, email communications, and direct mail with the assistance of marketing and sales</li>
<li>
Manage external vendors for creation of marketing material</li>
<li>
Assist in scheduling conferences and educational events</li>
<li>
Other tasks as assigned to aid in special projects completion</li>
</ul>
