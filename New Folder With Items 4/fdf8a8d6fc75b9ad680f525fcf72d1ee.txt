<h2>Division Director of Finance</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>MiTek® USA Inc., a subsidiary of Berkshire Hathaway, Inc. is the world's leading supplier of state-of-the-art, engineered connector products, engineering services, and computer-driven machinery for the building component industry. We provide the industry's most advanced, most comprehensive, and most innovative software for design and manufacturing. Our products and services are backed up by a team of industry professionals who are dedicated to one goal – making our customers successful.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
POSITION SUMMARY
</b></p>
<p>Conduct quantitative analyses of information affecting financial performance and strategic planning for the business organization. Enforce all policies and procedures related to financial controls, gathering and reporting of financial information. Directs and advises the overall financial strategy of the organization.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
ESSENTIAL FUNCTIONS:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Analyze financial reports to assess performance as aligned with forecasted or budgeted goals.</li>
<li>Direct and advise management and staff with respect to the overall financial strategy of the organization.</li>
<li>Recommend investment decisions by analyzing financial information to forecast business, industry, or economic conditions.</li>
<li>Monitor developments in the fields of industrial technology, business, finance, and economic theory.</li>
<li>Guide and direct sales with trending data collection and analysis.</li>
<li>Monitor fundamental economic, industrial, and corporate developments by analyzing information from financial publications and services, investment banking firms, government agencies, trade publications, company sources, or personal interviews.</li>
<li>Prepare plans of action for investment, using financial analyses.</li>
<li>Present oral or written reports on financial performance, general economic trends, individual corporations and entire industries.</li>
<li>Conduct financial analyses related to green construction or green retrofitting projects.</li>
<li>Forecast or analyze financial costs associated with potential acquisitions, building expansions, capital expenses, etc.</li>
<li>Provide contractual reviews and coordination with necessary legal resources.</li>
<li>Prepare and/or approve financial related forms and manuals for the organization.</li>
<li>Attendance at work during regularly scheduled shifts is an essential duty. Scheduled additional hours may be required at times depending on specific deadlines, projects, etc. This includes but is not limited to being at the work station ready for work at the start of the shift.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p>
</p>
