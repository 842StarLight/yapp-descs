<h2>Sales Executive – Banks and Insurance</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p>The Impact:</p>
<p>
Your contribution and dedication to your role is critical as you have a direct impact on business generation. You will be able to lead from the front and have direct impact for future growth. In doing so you will get help clients identify needs while providing solutions utilizing our products and services. You are responsible for promoting and consultatively selling a portfolio of products and services to current and target customers. You will identify key decision makers and influencers with target prospects and execute sales strategy collaboratively with sales members, client services, product management, operations, technology, and legal to optimize product and service delivery.</p>
<p>
The Team / The Business:</p>
<p>
We value partnership and it is crucial that you are a great teammate in order to be successful within our organization. We reward hard work, dedication, and self-starters. We believe that no one part is more valuable than the sum of the whole; we need everyone to achieve our goals.</p>
<p>
What's in it for You? As a key player within the sales team, you will have the opportunity to work with major key clients. This is a great opportunity for you to grow professionally as well as contribute to the growth of the business.</p>
<p>
Responsibilities:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Promote and sell a portfolio of products to current and target Financial Institutions. Develop and consultatively sell appropriate product/service solutions</li>
<li>
Focus on managing and selling to larger and more complex clients and prospects. Establish relationships with senior level executives of these organizations</li>
<li>
Communicate regularly with team members, senior sales management, and business units regarding client activity, results, competitive information, and market trends</li>
<li>
Practice a diagnostic selling approach</li>
<li>
Coordinate and provide sales demonstrations</li>
<li>
Negotiate contract terms and pricing</li>
<li>
Maintain focused approach to daily sales activity pipelines and systems-based workflow (i.e. Salesforce.com)</li>
<li>
Develop industry,persona and product specific knowledge necessary to have credible diagnostic conversations</li>
</ul>
<p>
 What We're Looking For:</p>
<p>
Minimum Qualifications:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Bachelor’s Degree or equivalent experience</li>
<li>
Experience working with Financial Institutions in a client service, sales and product support role/setting</li>
</ul>
<p>
Preferred Qualifications:</p>
<ul>
<li>
3-5 years of previous experience in Sales, Relationship Management, Client Development and/or quota carrying role</li>
<li>
A track record of exceeding sales targets</li>
<li>
Experience working with Banks &amp; Insurance firms</li>
<li>
Interest in solving problems for clients through the delivery of financial market data solutions and analytical tools</li>
<li>
Proficiency in MS Office, and Salesforce.com</li>
<li>
Strong interpersonal communication skills and confident in delivering product demonstrations</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
About RiskSpan</b></p>
<p>
RiskSpan is a leading source of analytics, modeling, data and risk management solutions for the Consumer and Institutional Finance industries. We solve business problems for clients such as banks, mortgage-backed and asset-backed securities issuers, equity and fixed-income portfolio managers, servicers, and regulators that require our expertise in the market risk, credit risk, operational risk and information technology domains.</p>
