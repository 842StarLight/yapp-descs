<h2>Account Manager- Home Health and Hospice</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">This is a dynamic, customer-facing, account relationship and retention role that requires proactive communication and strategic discussions at the C-Suite level. An Account Manager in our Home, Health and Hospice division will be responsible for providing a personalized approach to developing customer relationships while delivering processes and best practices to our largest and most valuable hospital customers. This role ensures that strategic customers receive the highest possible return on investment and satisfaction from our suite of products and services. As an Account Manager, you'll be responsible for understanding the Home Health and Hospice business at large so the relationship, solutions and associated revenue stream translate into profit and value for the account and WellSky, while ensuring the client is healthy.
<br><br>
This exciting opportunity provides vast outlets for professional development, growth and success. You'll use strong communication skills, heavy organizational ability and support of internal groups through a matrix environment to bridge the gap between potential software shortcomings. Apply today to be apart of this growth opportunity!
<br><br>
<b>Position Responsibilities:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Relationship-building with key stakeholders throughout the account lifecycle.</li>
<li>Serve as the single point of contact for all things related to WellSky.</li>
<li>Ability to network across an organization; establish relationships with key decision makers.</li>
<li>Develop a high-level strategy to grow the breadth and depth of the relationship</li>
<li>Lead ad hoc teams from cross-functional departments to develop customer programs, materials, processes, and best practices to ensure continued utilization, growth, and adoption.</li>
<li>Assist in measuring success metrics, ROI, and leading indicators of growth and/or risk potential.</li>
<li>Provide reporting to cross-functional areas and senior management.</li>
<li>Provide detailed summaries of the overall needs, health, and key issues of the supported customers.</li>
<li>Provide periodic health checks and business reviews to key customer stakeholders; review metrics, anticipated ROI, articulating areas of strength, and developing programs for improvement and/or remediation opportunities.</li>
<li>Identify at-risk customers and work cross-functionally to mitigate customers concerns.</li>
<li>Works independently and with subject matter expert to maintain thorough awareness of the product and new product releases and participates in cross-training as needed.</li>
<li>Other duties as assigned.</li>
</ul>
<b>Position Skills and Experience:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor's Degree in technical, medical, financial, or communications discipline, or related field or relevant, equivalent work experience</li>
<li>3+ years of previous account management experience (preference for Healthcare experience)</li>
<li>A passion to serve customers with an ability to deliver exceptional customer experiences.</li>
<li>Exceptional training and/or public speaking experience; software/technology training experience is strongly preferred.</li>
<li>Excellent verbal and written communication skills.</li>
<li>An ability to think critically and strategically while considering the needs of both the customer and company.</li>
<li>Strong organizational skills with the ability to handle multiple projects and escalations.</li>
<li>Detail-oriented with strong problem solving, crisis management and negotiation skills.</li>
<li>Demonstrable proficiency utilizing the Web, MS Office, Salesforce, and/or other CRM tools.</li>
<li>Ability to travel 30% of the time.</li>
</ul>
WellSky Corporation
