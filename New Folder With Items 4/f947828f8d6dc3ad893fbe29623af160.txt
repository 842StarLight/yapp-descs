<h2>Product Manager - Data Science Products</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>Our Product Design and Management teams work tirelessly to understand what our customers need and make it a reality. They’re the ones who make our deals come to life and our user experience simple and intuitive.
</p>
<p></p>
<p>At Priceline we're the best travel dealmakers in the world because we want everyone to experience the moments that matter in life.
</p>
<p></p>
<p>We are looking for a data science product manager to join our data product team.
</p>
<p></p>
<p>Are you passionate about data and using the scientific method to improve customer experiences and to develop new products?
</p>
<p></p>
<p>Do you have a background in scientific research or as a data scientist as well as product and product management?
</p>
<p></p>
<p>Do you innately think like a scientist?
</p>
<p></p>
<p>Do you have experience doing A/B testing?
 </p>
<p></p>
<p>Have you implemented human centered design thinking on an enterprise level?
</p>
<p></p>
<p>Do you want to work with talented people who love to travel and are passionate about enabling people to experience special moments with those that they care about?
</p>
<p></p>
<p>If you have answered yes to these questions we should talk.
</p>
<p></p>
<p>We are looking for a product manager who is a true team player and enjoys working within and across teams. You will be the voice of the customer for our internal A/B testing and associated platforms. You will be responsible for working with key stakeholders and internal customers to develop our ability to quickly iterate and make data driven and scientifically sound product decisions. Open to Norwalk, CT or NYC locations with at least once per week travel to the other office.
</p>
<p></p>
<p>Responsibilities :
</p>
<ul>
<li><p>Be the voice of the customer to prioritize development epics, stories and tasks for technology teams
</p></li>
<li><p>Partner with UI/UX design, marketing, engineering, and sales to deliver the best product for our customers, partners, and internal teams
</p></li>
<li><p>Glean insights about our users and sellers from qualitative and quantitative research which will inform future product decisions
</p></li>
<li><p>Work with engineers to identify the optimal solutions to meet current and future strategic needs while keeping data vision roadmaps in mind
</p></li>
<li><p>Drive growth and revenue using a data driven approach
</p></li>
<li><p>Represent your team in communication with product, engineering, and marketing leadership
</p></li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p>Qualifications :
</p>
<ul>
<li><p>Solid understanding of how to grow products and experience running A/B tests
</p></li>
<li><p>2+ years of product management experience or a relevant strategic role
</p></li>
<li><p>Solid understanding of human centered design thinking
</p></li>
<li><p>Experience implementing experimentation using the scientific method
</p></li>
<li><p>Experience with data science or as a data scientist a significant plus
</p></li>
<li><p>Servant leader mentality
</p></li>
<li><p>Experience working with engineers in an agile environment
</p></li>
<li><p>Enjoys working cross-functionally across teams and departments
</p></li>
<li><p>Exceptional written and verbal communication skills
</p></li>
<li><p>Demonstrated ability in relationship-building, interpersonal skills
</p></li>
<li><p>Team player with a positive, collaborative attitude and a ‘roll up your sleeves’ attitude
</p></li>
<li>Demonstrated history of living the values important to priceline: Customer, Innovation, Team, Accountability and Trust. Unquestionable integrity and ethics.
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p>Bonus points :
</p>
<ul>
<li><p>Experience performing user research, market/competitive research, and soliciting customer feedback
</p></li>
<li><p>Working with or in a data science or machine learning engineering role
</p></li>
<li><p>Previous startup experience
 </p></li>
</ul>
