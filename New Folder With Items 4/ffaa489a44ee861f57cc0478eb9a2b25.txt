<h2>Leasing Consultant</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Local Property Management Company is looking for a full time Leasing Consultant for a BRAND NEW lease-up community located in Hawk's Prairie. This is a full time position with hours Tuesday - Saturday.</p>
<p>The person in this position is responsible for leasing, marketing and maintaining positive resident relations.</p>
<p>Responsibilities:</p>
<ul>
<li>Ability to convert phone calls to tours, and tours to leases.</li>
<li>Provide first-class, top-notch customer service relationships with prospects and residents.</li>
<li>Be able to manage and resolve resident concerns.</li>
<li>Clear knowledge and understanding of all lease-related paperwork from application to move-in.</li>
<li>Prepare lease files, assist with the renewal process, move-out requests, and all other documents utilized as they relate to leasing and resident relations.</li>
<li>Assist manager in developing and implementing effective marketing strategies to increase property traffic and internet leads</li>
<li>Maintain thorough knowledge of the property, sister properties, and competitors.</li>
<li>Standard administration work, including but not limited to answering phones, taking messages, managing the facilities/work orders in the property management software, filing, and ordering office supplies.</li>
<li>Plan, organize and conduct resident functions as needed and as directed by Property Manager.</li>
</ul>
<p>Required Qualifications</p>
<ul>
<li>A minimum of one year leasing experience</li>
<li>High school diploma/GED</li>
<li>Strong professionalism, communication and “people skills”</li>
<li>Must possess attention to detail</li>
<li>Well organized with the ability to prioritize and multi-task</li>
<li>Superior sales skills</li>
<li>General knowledge of computers, Microsoft Word, Internet, Email and Social Media</li>
</ul>
<p>Job Type: Full-time</p>
<p>Salary: $20.00 /hour</p>
