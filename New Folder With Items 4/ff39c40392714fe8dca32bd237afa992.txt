<b>Your Next Challenge:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Responsible for the development and measurement of digital advertising strategies and best practices that maximize client performance</li>
<li>Work with Sales Operations and Google Partners to measure growth of the DA business and proactively identify opportunities to sell into the install base</li>
<li>Works with Sales to develop tailored Digital Advertising solutions for Key or VIP accounts</li>
<li>Standardize client facing material working with Product Marketing, Account Management, and Sales to highlight the value of Digital Advertising with G5</li>
<li>Determine which paid channels yield the best returns and allocate client budget accordingly and monitor/adjust campaign controls to achieve optimal client results</li>
<li>Participates in customer facing business and performance reviews with clients on an as needed basis to discuss digital strategies in depth</li>
<li>Review &amp; monitor competitive landscape and client's place within that landscape for each search engine / paid channel</li>
<li>On-going mentoring and training of Digital Advertising team members</li>
<li>Responsible for the execution of ad network betas, and driving ongoing testing of updates &amp; changes in the digital landscape</li>
<li>Continuous testing of current G5 best practices; developing and monitoring test plans, implementing testing protocol, and analyzing results</li>
<li>Define team KPIs and strategies for goal achievement, tactical accountability and performance evaluation</li>
</ul>
<b>What You'll Need:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>3-5 years of experience managing high-volume, large spend digital advertising campaigns</li>
<li>Proven track record of being a thought leader and mentor within a digital advertising team</li>
<li>Comprehensive understanding of targeting and campaign structure best-practices within each network (Google, Microsoft and Facebook Ads) and channel (search, display, remarketing and social)</li>
<li>Achieve and maintain certain professional qualifications, including but not limited to: Google Ads Professional, Google Analytics Individual Qualification, and Microsoft Ads Accredited Professional</li>
<li>Inherent desire and ability to seek out and learn cutting edge industry trends</li>
<li>Ability to solve problems and identify opportunities through data analysis and critical thinking</li>
<li>Demonstrated leadership skills and the ability to rally the team around new ideas</li>
<li>Strong attention to detail, excellent organizational skills, and ability to manage multiple projects and responsibilities</li>
<li>Advanced Microsoft Excel / Google Sheet skills (pivot tables, charts, v look-ups, "if" statements, etc.)</li>
<li>Knowledge of Google Analytics or other web analytics tools</li>
</ul>
