<h2>Senior Salesforce Developer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p><b>Job Purpose:</b></p>
<p></p>
<p>
SalesForce Developer with strong analytical and technical ability with experience in customizing SalesForce applications, and knowledge of Core Java, Java EE, and JavaScript . Able to work independently, excellent communication skills and ability to lead a team across multiple time zones.</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Key Responsibilities:</b></p>
<p></p>
<p>
Candidate should be hands on and have a strong analytical and technical ability with experience in customizing SalesForce applications. Extensive years involved with complex medium to large projects in a fast paced financial environment is expected.</p>
<p><br>
Strong communication skills are a key. The position is required to manage day to day delivery work, ensure adherence to process and quality, and identify project/program delivery risks and works on risk mitigation. Requires good analytical skills in order to filter, prioritize and validate potentially complex material from multiple sources and applies skills and knowledge of the business to develop creative solutions to meet client and business needs.</p>
<p><br>
<b>
Job Description</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Understanding the business area and user requirements.</li>
<li>
Study and understand the existing programs.</li>
<li>
Performing program design, coding and Development.</li>
<li>
Performing unit and integration testing.</li>
<li>
Producing documentation based on the company's standards.</li>
<li>
Evaluation and Recommendation for new technologies and updating versions of existing ones.</li>
<li>
Design reusable frameworks.</li>
<li>
Environment management and layout design.</li>
<li>
Unified Development environment (Developers systems as well as servers).</li>
<li>
Providing Technical road map.</li>
<li>
Establish effective working relationships with client representatives.</li>
<li>
Achieve continuous improvement on all aspects of work - including areas outside direct accountability/responsibility.</li>
<li>
Appropriately apply architecture &amp; design patterns in design and code.</li>
<li>
Comply with mandated policies and procedures.</li>
<li>
Achieve extensibility, reliability, and reusability in design and code.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Job</b><b>Qualifications</b>:</p>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>
Bachelor’s degree or equivalent work experience;</li>
<li>
5+ years of hands-on application development experience on Salesforce.com Sales Cloud platform;</li>
<li>
Excellent verbal and written communication skills.</li>
<li>
Experience with Enterprise implementations of complex Salesforce applications;</li>
<li>
Proven experience with Salesforce point and click development features. SalesForce Lightning framework.</li>
<li>
Hands-On Technical experience in HTML5, CSS, JavaScript, JQuery, Visual force , APEX programming (Triggers and batch class ), SOQL – Salesforce object query language , Data loader</li>
<li>
Strong initiative and passion in learning new functionality, and the ability to work both individually and part of large teams</li>
<li>
Background with capacity management and scheduling; Plainview, JIRA and OPPM tool knowledge a plus</li>
<li>
Ability to collaborate with technology and business in ambiguous situations, meeting or exceeding customer and partner expectations.</li>
<li>
Experience working with offshore consultants and will have had success in increasing and improving their turn-around times and upgrading skill levels</li>
<li>
Ability to adapt to a rapidly changing environment</li>
</ul>
