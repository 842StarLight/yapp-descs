<h2>Contract Specialist (Advanced level - Secret)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>CONTRACT SPECIALIST<br>
<br>
<b>LOCATION:</b> Dahlgren, VA<br>
<br>
<b>SUMMARY: </b>The Contract Specialist will support the Missile Defense Agency (MDA) Contract Directorate.<br>
<br>
<b>ESSENTIAL DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES: </b>Reasonable accommodations may be made to enable individuals with disabilities to perform the essential functions of this position.
<ul>
<li>Performs a variety of contracting duties to support the research, development, testing and evaluation of highly specialized weapon systems, software systems, and materials. The work involves both fixed price and cost-reimbursement contracts, phased procurements with negotiated options, extensive subcontracting, and complex competitive and sole source proposals. These duties are performed using the Federal, Department of Defense, and MDA acquisition regulations, policies, procedures and practices.</li>
<li>Assists the Senior Contract Specialists and Contracting Officers in assignments such as analyzing procurement requirements, preparing solicitation packages, evaluating proposals, analyzing costs, researching regulations, identifying procurement sources, evaluating contractor performance, and monitoring expenditures.</li>
<li>Gathers and organizes data, prepares specific contract documents, and drafts reports for review by supervisor or senior specialist.</li>
<li>Supporting contract(s) closeout activities by analyzing, collecting appropriate documentation and correspondence, validating ULOs and de-obing excess funding, finalizing the official contract file.</li>
</ul>
<b>NON-ESSENTIAL DUTIES: </b>Perform other duties as assigned<br>
<br>
<b>EDUCATION AND EXPERIENCE:</b>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor's degree and/or 15 years of Government Contracting experience required</li>
<li>Must have strong verbal and written communication skills</li>
<li>Ability to work independently and prioritize/organize workload to meet Government established milestones</li>
<li>Experience with Request For Proposal (RFP) preparation and proposal evaluation to include cost and price analysis</li>
<li>Experience with Federal Government prime contract administration</li>
<li>Experience in preparing, administering and managing pre-award, award, and post-award contractual documentation</li>
<li>Must have knowledge and previous use of Standard Procurement System (SPS) Procurement Desktop-Defense (PD2) software</li>
</ul>
<b>ADDITIONAL KNOWLEDGE, SKILLS, ABILITIES AND COMPETENCIES (Desired)</b>:
<ul>
<li>Prior MDA experience</li>
</ul>
<b>COMPUTER SKILLS:</b>
<ul>
<li>Skill in operating a personal computer and standard office equipment</li>
<li>Proficient in Microsoft Office (MS Excel, Word, Outlook, PowerPoint)</li>
</ul>
<b>CERTIFICATES, LICENSES, REGISTRATIONS, CLEARANCE:</b>
<ul>
<li>Must possess and maintain an active DoD Secret (or Interim) security clearance</li>
</ul>
