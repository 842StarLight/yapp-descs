<h2>Senior Accountant</h2>

<div>
<div>There’s a gap between when systems go sideways and when the consequences take hold. In that gap, there’s a narrow opportunity to stave off catastrophe. That is what Moogsoft is for. We are the leader in AIOps. We provide a platform with purpose-built algorithms and automation to solve the systems failures of the largest banking, finance, energy, insurance, transportation, and entertainment companies in the world.</div>
<div>
</div>
<div>
The Moogsoft Finance team is searching for our perfect Senior Accountant. Do you have a passion for high-growth start-ups and have excellent technical accounting skills? Do you go to sleep at night dreaming of spreadsheets? If the idea of getting in on the ground floor excites you, then we want to hear from you!</div>
<div>
</div>
<div>
This role will report directly to the Accounting Manager and work closely with partners in the Sales and HR teams to ensure accurate recording of transactions. You will participate in, and help develop, an efficient close process, facilitate annual audits, work cross functionally on various projects, and play an important role in supporting financial operations. If you thrive under pressure and have experience in a Software/SaaS company, we want to talk to you.</div>
<div>
</div>
</div>
<div><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>You will...</b></h3>
<div><ul>
<li>
Participate in month-end closing procedures in all areas of accounting</li>
<li>
Review GL entries and ensure accuracy of financial statements in accordance with GAAP</li>
<li>
Prepare and review account reconciliations, journal entries, and supporting schedules</li>
<li>
Implement new lease accounting policy</li>
<li>
Help ensure 606 compliance</li>
<li>
Review and maintain stock option records</li>
<li>
Assist with ad hoc flux analysis</li>
<li>
Prepare audit support schedules Assist with system implementations and/or system enhancements.</li>
<li>
Work cross functionally with internal teams, as well as external teams, to drive high quality reporting results</li>
</ul></div>
</div></div>
<div><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>You have...</b></h3>
<div><ul>
<li>
A Bachelor’s degree in Accounting, CPA is a plus</li>
<li>
Working ideally for a SaaS or Software Company</li>
<li>
Working knowledge of Stock Based Comp - ASC718 and 606 Revenue Recognition</li>
<li>
6+ years of experience with a preference to candidates with audit experience.</li>
<li>
Solid balance sheet reconciliation experience, with an acumen for debits and credits</li>
<li>
Technically savvy with software systems (such as Netsuite, Salesforce, Mineral Tree, Expensify, Avalara, Carta, Excel)</li>
<li>
Strong customer service orientation, including excellent oral and written communication skills</li>
<li>
Examples of being self-sufficient in reviewing work and ability to take ownership for projects and drive to completion</li>
<li>
Strong time management skills and are proactive</li>
</ul></div>
