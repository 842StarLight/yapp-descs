<h2>MPS Analyst</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Overview
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div>We are looking for an MPS Analyst to join our fast-paced and growing sales support team! The Managed Print Services (MPS) Analyst is an integral member of the pre-sales, post-sales and operations teams.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
Our MPS division specializes in finding solutions for our clients documented related expenses. Document related expenses are one of, if not the largest, unmanaged expense for many organizations. Our Managed Print Services program uncovers hidden costs associated with printing while providing an ROI-driven improvement plan. The first step is to define all costs associated with printing and break it down to a simple cost per page metric. The next is to implement an on-going plan that will manage both the performance of the devices and the behavior of employees who print; to ultimately increase efficiencies and reduce costs.</div>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div>
<b>The main objective is to support sales and operations staff in the following MPS processes</b>:</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div><b><i>
Pre-Sale</i></b></div>
<ul>
<li>
Travel required to perform customer walkthroughs, mapping, and obtain necessary financials</li>
<li>
Compiles and analyzes gathered data from assessments and enters into our Total Cost of Ownership (TCO) tool.</li>
<li>
Installing Data Collection Agent (DCA) at customer locations, remotely or in-person</li>
<li>
Generate working files and preparing presentation documents</li>
</ul>
<div><b><i>
Post-Sale
</i></b></div>
<ul>
<li>Works with supply and contract teams to ensure accuracy of account management and data integrity</li>
<li>
Assists in setting up customers and their fleet of devices to communicate with our back end software (e-automate)</li>
<li>
Work directly with key contacts at our customers to on board new accounts onto our MPS remote monitoring program</li>
</ul>
<div><b><i>
Operations</i></b></div>
<ul>
<li>
Coordinate with Logistics/Service teams for device deployment and installations</li>
<li>
Provide on-going management of customer’s equipment fleet: adding new devices, researching stale devices-DCA, and troubleshooting.</li>
<li>
Device and Software Training for customers</li>
<li>
Contact current clients to offer our MPS value add components</li>
<li>
Consistently demonstrate exceptional customer service skills and attitude</li>
<li>
Performs other duties assigned</li>
</ul>
</div></div></div>
