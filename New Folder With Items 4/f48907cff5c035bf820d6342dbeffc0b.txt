<h2>HubSpot Data Analyst (Located in Johnston, Iowa)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>AKC Marketing has an opening for a Data Analyst who will work with a team to produce data and insights required to make strategic decisions for our clients. In this role, you will define the data requirements and reporting infrastructure to accelerate sales growth and improve sales rep productivity. The focus of this work will be designing and executing strategic reporting initiatives as well as providing operational and analytical support for high-growth sales segments.</p>
<p>Must be willing to relocate to the Central Iowa area!</p>
<p><b>In this role, you'll get to: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Work with executives and sales leadership to define go-to-market strategies for a sales organization</li>
<li>Partner closely with sales leadership to develop actionable, measurable projects and programs that accelerate sales growth and improve sales reps’ productivity</li>
<li>Work with the sales representatives to improve the performance of the sales organization through data insights</li>
<li>Work cross-functionally with marketing, content and other departments to ensure alignment on data and measurement</li>
<li>Understand industry best practices of core sales operations functions focused on data structure, modeling, and business analytics</li>
<li>Build a strong internal network of experts and resources that can help drive effective change</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Responsibilities</b></p>
<p>Essential duties and responsibilities include the following.</p>
<ul>
<li>Manage technical aspects of key sales and marketing systems (marketing automation, CRM, etc.) used to generate, distribute and report on leads using HubSpot</li>
<li>Establish and maintain scalable processes that ensure best practices in campaign and lead management</li>
<li>Create and maintain metrics reports on marketing and sales activities, as well as effectiveness and business impact</li>
<li>Monitor and maintain data quality within the marketing database, including list creation</li>
<li>Evaluate new technologies and add-on applications to improve and optimize marketing team performance and measurement</li>
<li>Compile, analyze, report and interpret data to support key sales data metrics</li>
<li>Engage in data cleansing activities to ensure that sales data is consistent and accurate for management reporting</li>
<li>Monitor campaigns to optimize performance based on industry best practices</li>
<li>Develop robust KPIs and scorecards for marketing performance monitoring</li>
<li>Analyze marketing and sales data to develop insights and make recommendations on areas for optimization</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Qualifications</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Marketing Automation (i.e. HubSpot) experience a plus</li>
<li>Business Intelligence tool experience a plus</li>
<li>Excellent written and verbal communication and interpersonal skills</li>
<li>Ability to organize, multi‐task, work independently on several projects and meet deadlines</li>
<li>Skill in defining problems, collecting data and interpreting information</li>
<li>2+ years of experience in sales and/or marketing operations functions</li>
<li>Bachelor's degree in marketing, communications or business</li>
<li>Data-driven, analytical mindset</li>
<li>Working knowledge and understanding of Microsoft Office Suite</li>
<li>CRM experience preferred</li>
<li>Travel may be required</li>
</ul>
<p>We offer a relaxed, yet highly motivated work environment, where each individual’s contribution to the business is truly valued. Qualified candidates should provide a resume and cover letter.</p>
