<p>If you are looking for a challenging and rewarding career, we’d like you to join our security Bank of Kansas City team! Our banking professionals receive paid training and ongoing education, opportunities for growth and advancement, great benefits, and use the latest in technology to help make the experience at Security Bank a pleasant one for our staff as well as our customers!</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Basic Job Functions:</b></p>
<div>
<i>Under the direction of the Corporate Training Department Manager, the </i><i>Corporate Training Coordinator </i><i>will:</i><br>
<ul>
<li>Provide training to Security Bank employees on a variety of bank regulations, policies, procedures, and system changes/enhancements etc.</li>
<li>Provide refresher training to employees on policies and procedures of the Bank.</li>
<li>Provide feedback to the Corporate Training Department Manager concerning the progress of the Teller Trainees.</li>
<li>Provide documentation to the Corporate Training Department Manager and/or Human Resources on the training completed.</li>
<li>Facilitate training solutions to meet Security Bank’s needs.</li>
<li>Assist in aligning business initiatives and operational goals with creating and maintaining</li>
</ul>
</div>
<br>
<p></p>
<div>
<b>Job Responsibilities:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Create programs and provide training on the bank’s policies and procedures.</li>
<li>Online Learning System Administration, which includes assigning New Hire Training with all new employees in their assigned area(s) of responsibilities as well as creating, assigning and tracking annual training in accordance with a schedule of courses to maintain and meet employee training requirements.</li>
<li>Schedule and organize training sessions, which includes setting up equipment and materials needed for training sessions</li>
<li>Assist employees over the phone with training needs and questions</li>
<li>Coordinate and implement all levels of training programs and techniques that support company initiatives to drive sales, build customer loyalty, and develop the skills of all employees.</li>
<li>Coordinate with management to determine training needs and schedule training sessions</li>
<li>Design effective training programs</li>
<li>Partner with Retail, Operations, HR, Loans and other functional departments with communication of learning, development initiatives and expectations.</li>
<li>Interact with all levels of leadership, gather feedback to evaluate training programs to ascertain that requirements are met and that the programs are vigorous and are accomplishing the goals established for Company performance.</li>
<li>Acts as a resource to provide coaching, developmental support and guidance to all employees.</li>
<li>Conduct seminars, workshops, individual training sessions etc.</li>
<li>Other duties as assigned.</li>
</ul>
<br>
</div>
<p><b>Knowledge, Skills, and Abilities Required to Enter the Job:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Attainment of this position may be contingent upon successful mastery of the skills and responsibilities inherent in 2 years bank experience or equivalent education in bank operating procedures.</li>
<li>Must satisfactorily meet all necessary requirements of the Corporate Training Coordinator position.</li>
<li>Knowledge, skill and mental development equivalent to the completion of a high school education.</li>
<li>Good math, reading and writing/typing skills.</li>
<li>Patience, and the ability to teach/train.</li>
<li>Ability to impart knowledge of tasks to be performed to trainee, and identify learning performance.</li>
<li>Excellent verbal communication skills, including presentations and over the phone with co-workers.</li>
<li>Ability to use tact and diplomacy when dealing with customers and co-workers.</li>
<li>Adequate knowledge of learning management system and web delivery tools.</li>
<li>Extensive knowledge of instructional design and implementation.</li>
<li>Familiarity with traditional and modern job training methods and techniques.</li>
<li>MS Office proficiency.</li>
<li>Advanced organizational skills with the ability to handle multiple assignments.</li>
<li>Positive and Cheerful Attitude.</li>
<li>In addition to possessing the necessary skills to perform this job, the employee must be willing and able to demonstrate those skills by coming to work on a daily basis, as scheduled.</li>
</ul>
