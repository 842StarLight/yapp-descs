<h2>Director of Athletics Marketing</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div><div><div><div><div><div><div><div>
<div>
<p>The mission of the NC State Department of Athletics is to prepare student-athletes to compete at the highest level and to inspire them to be leaders now and for the future by providing the best environment to achieve their athletic, academic and personal aspirations as well as providing support for the professional development of our coaches and staff, all within the role of the greater institutional relationships to the University. North Carolina State University competes as an NCAA Division I team in the Atlantic Coast Conference.</p>
</div>
<br>
<div>
<div><b>Essential Job Duties</b></div>
<div>
<p>The incumbent will serve as Director of Marketing for the Athletics Department and will serve as the primary marketing contact for Football and Baseball. He/She will also help oversee and develop select Marketing Assistants and the Marketing Coordinator.<br>
Under the direction of the Assistant Athletics Director for Marketing and Fan Development, duties and responsibilities will include, but not limited to the following:</p>
<ul>
<li>Developing and executing a comprehensive external plan inclusive of marketing, promotions, advertising, budgeting, communications and social media for Football and Baseball. This also includes advising support staff and coaches on marketing strategies</li>
<li>Coordinating with the in-house sales team on aggressive ticket and attendance strategies to meet revenue goals. Collaborate with communications team to promote ticket sales and promotional opportunities</li>
<li>Serving as gameday producer for Football and Baseball inclusive of drafting gameday scripts and collaborating with Broadcast &amp; Video Services and Wolfpack Sports Properties (IMG/Learfield) on engaging in-game content</li>
<li>Managing and executing gameday operations and promotions for Football and Baseball including working with Wolfpack Sports Properties, the Wolfpack Club, NC State Alumni Association, Marketing Assistants, Student Volunteers/Interns and other campus constituents for all in-game activities</li>
<li>Coordinating with Creative Services to conceptualize and develop print, digital, social and e-mail campaigns for designated sports with strong brand consistency</li>
<li>Assisting with Digital Marketing Strategies and ROAS in coordination with Paciolan</li>
<li>Responsible for hiring and overseeing Marketing Assistants and Marketing Coordinator that handle select sports
</li>
<li>Coordinating trade contracts for media outlets in partnership with advertising agency</li>
<li>Providing guidance and vision for fan experience efforts across all Varsity Sports</li>
<li>Representing the Athletics department on select campus-wide committees</li>
<li>Assisting with overseeing Marketing Assistants on student promotional efforts, retention and engagement through key University student organizations (i.e. Fraternity &amp; Sorority Life, Housing, Student Government) and maintain athletics as a strong campus partner</li>
</ul>
</div>
