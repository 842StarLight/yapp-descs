<h2>Wealth Management Associate</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Live on or near the beautiful lakes and mountains of New Hampshire. Embark on a professional career path. Work with great people, helping them fulfil their personal financial goals. Make a difference in the lives of our clients.</p>
<p>The Foundry Financial Group, Inc. is currently accepting resumes for the positions of <b>WEALTH MANAGEMENT ASSOCIATE</b> on the career path towards becoming a client advisor for <b>financial planning</b> and <b>investment management</b> services. This is NOT a Sales Position. Working with an experienced advisory team, you will be responsible for preparing financial plan presentations, participating in client meetings, and following up on service activities. You will be engaged in training to become an advisor, as you attain important professional designations and licenses (CFP®, CFA®, CIMA®, SIE, Series 65).</p>
<p>You will be expected to stay abreast of trends and issues in financial planning and communicate with the FFG Team. You will work extensively with our financial planning software and our investment analytical tools.</p>
