<h2>Customer Care Representative</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p>The Customer Service Representative offers excellent customer service by responding to customers' calls, emails and fax inquiries; places product orders; follows up on inquiries and orders; ensures timely shipment of orders; verifies credit standing; provides or recommends solutions to a wide variety of problems; provides information about the product lines to customers; promotes all selling initiatives and works with general supervision and direction to ensure Wow! Customer Careâ„¢.</p>
<h4 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Principal Accountabilities:</b></h4>
<ul>
<li>Proficient in SAP ERP software and Salesforce as it relates to the order entry process; notates important customer information, including inventory management (backorders/allocations), list sales, and confirmations/invoices.</li>
<li>Proven attendance record.</li>
<li>Process department faxes, emails and mail orders within one (1) hour of receipt.</li>
<li>On-call rotation for influenza vaccine call queue. Includes answering customer requests, checking product availability, prices and account status.
</li>
<li>Deliver excellent customer service on every call, incorporating all necessary call requirements.</li>
<li>Maintain all customer files.</li>
<li>Answer the department minimum for calls and place the department minimum for orders.</li>
<li>Maintain a high order accuracy rate with minimal to no errors, consistently meeting the department minimum order accuracy percentage of 99.80%.</li>
<li>Work with other departments to manage and ship product in most efficient manner.</li>
<li>Comply with the policies and procedures stated in the Injury and Illness Prevention Program by always working in a safe manner and immediately reporting any injury, safety hazard, or program violation.</li>
<li>Ensure conduct is consistent with all Compliance Program Policies and procedures when engaging in any activity on behalf of the company. Immediately report any concerns or violations.</li>
<li>Display dedication to the position responsibilities and achieve assigned goals and objectives.</li>
<li>Other duties as assigned.</li>
</ul>
<h4 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Dimension:</b></h4>
<p>Annual sales of $1 billion.</p>
<p>Approximately 275 customer service calls per day.</p>
<h4 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Supervisor Responsibilities:</b></h4>
<p>None.</p>
<h4 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Knowledge, Skills and Abilities:</b></h4>
<p>Represent the company in a professional manner and appearance at all times.</p>
<p>Understand and internalize the company's purpose; display loyalty to the company and its organizational values.</p>
<p>Display enthusiasm and dedication to learning how to be more effective on the job and share knowledge with others.</p>
<p>Work effectively with co-workers, internal and external customers and others by sharing ideas in a constructive and positive manner; listen to and objectively consider ideas and suggestions from others; keep commitments; keep others informed of work progress, timetables, and issues; address problems and issues constructively to find mutually acceptable and practical business solutions; address others by name, title, or other respectful identifier, and; respect the diversity of our work force in actions, words, and deeds.</p>
<p>Encourage teamwork by treating all subordinates fairly; keep employees informed of department/Company plans; Encourage group participation; Value employees' input; Acknowledge achievements; Lead by example.</p>
<h4 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Education and Experience Desired:</b></h4>
<ul>
<li>High school diploma or equivalent.</li>
<li>Minimum two (2) years' experience in a customer service environment, preferably call center.</li>
<li>Experienced in order entry, processing live customer call orders and researching account information as well as handling complaints and billing inquiries.</li>
<li>Excellent oral and written communication skills.</li>
<li>Positive attitude and team player.</li>
<li>Follows established procedures and processes with little deviation.</li>
</ul>
<h4 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Typical Office Environment:</b></h4>
<p>No unusual exposures.</p>
<h4 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Equipment Used:</b></h4>
<p>Operate office equipment.</p>

<p>While performing the duties of this position, the employee is required to regularly use hands to finger, handle or feel objects, tools, controls and equipment. The employee must be able to walk, sit and stand. The employee occasionally lifts up to 20 pounds and occasionally kneels and bends.
</p>
