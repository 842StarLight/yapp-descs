<p><b>
Job Title:</b> Administrative Assistant
</p>
<h1 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Summary:</b></h1>
<p>This is a skilled position requiring the employee to perform a variety of office support functions.</p>
<h1 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Essential Duties and Responsibilities:</b></h1>
<ul>
<li>Typing, filing, receipting cash sales and preparing and reconciling bank deposit, data entry, copying, preparing, shipping, and distributing courier packages, composition and editing correspondence, etc…
</li>
<li>Operate office equipment including, but not limited to: telephone switchboard, computers, copiers, fax machines, printers, etc…</li>
<li>Knowledge of Microsoft Office software preferred: Word, Excel, etc…</li>
<li>Minimum typing speed of 45 words per minute.</li>
<li>Knowledge of basic office practices and protocol.</li>
<li>Other duties as assigned per supervisor.</li>
</ul>
<h1 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Qualifications:</b></h1>
<p>To perform this job successfully, an individual must be able to perform each essential duty satisfactorily. The requirements listed below are representative of the knowledge, skills, and/or ability required. Reasonable accommodations may be made to enable individuals with disability to perform the essential functions.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>High school diploma or general education degree (GED) required; and one to two years related experience and/or training; or equivalent combination of education and experience.</li>
<li>Ability to read and comprehend instructions, correspondence, and memos. Ability to write clearly and effectively</li>
<li>Ability to add, subtracts, multiply, and divide in all units of measure, using whole numbers, common fractions, and decimals.
</li>
<li>Ability to apply common sense understanding to carry out detailed, but uninvolved written or oral instructions</li>
<li>Knowledge of Command Alkon, Internet software, Microsoft Office including Excel, Word, Outlook, etc.</li>
<li>Minimum typing speed of 45 words per minute.
</li>
<li>Ability to operate office equipment including, but not limited to: telephone switchboard, computers, copiers, fax machines, printers, etc.</li>
</ul>

<li>TEAMWORK – Contributes to building a positive team spirit.</li>
