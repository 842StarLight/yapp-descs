<h2>Marketing Coordinator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<b>Description:</b><br>
Socket is seeking a self-driven, motivated individual to join our growing marketing team! Candidates should posses a proven record of marketing strengths, including traditional print, radio and digital mediums, as well as a solid work ethic and exceptional organizational skills. Experience in advertising, public relations and/or event planning is a plus. Must maintain a clean driving record.
<br><br>
<b>Position Responsibilities:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Coordinate a wide variety of advertising and marketing campaigns primarily digital mediums and print</li>
<li>Contact vendors and media representatives to coordinate projects and to promote events</li>
<li>Attend and coordinate Socket presence at community events</li>
<li>Assist with copy-writing for website, social media channels and company blogs</li>
<li>Support external and internal marketing through graphic design, written communication and desktop publishing.</li>
</ul>
<b>Requirements:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Bachelors degree in marketing or related field , or applicable experience, required</li>
<li>At least 2 years or professional experience in marketing related role</li>
<li>Proficient in creating content, including publications and images, using the Adobe suite</li>
<li>Working knowledge of social media platforms and supporting software for business/marketing purposes</li>
</ul>
