<p><b>Job Description
</b></p>
<div><div><div><div>
<p>Strategic Customer Engagement is a senior role that works with 10-15 key/marquis strategic customers or prospects. This role requires strong interlock and communication with Sales, Services, Support, and Product Management to ensure product adoption and referenceabilit y at varying levels of the customer lifecycle.
</p>
<p>Strategic Customer Engagement leader works with General Manager, GVP, &amp; ISE leader to select and prioritize strategic accounts. Monitors and manages customer satisfaction and status through customer lifecycle stages (sign to go-live of phased deployment). Will be responsible to provide data driven insights for Product leadership regarding the health of our strategic customers. In addition to this, the Strategic Engagement leader tracks any strategic account product commitments, customer issues, and escalations. The Strategic Customer Engagement leader is the customer champion and acts as the liaison to GM, Product Strategy, &amp; Product teams.</p>
