<h2>Accounting Assistant- Experienced</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Do Not Apply if you have no prior accounting experience.</p>
<p>Job Title: Accounting Assistant</p>
<p>Job Category: Experienced</p>
<p>Department/Group: Accounting Location: Stockton</p>
<p>Travel Required: None Level/Salary Range: $12.77 – $21.46</p>
<p>We are looking for an Accounting Assistant to undertake payable/receivables operations in our accounting department. Our Accounting Assistant needs to have a keen eye for detail and the ability to look at the bigger picture. Ability to work with little supervision to meet tight deadlines is essential. Advanced knowledge of QuickBooks is required.</p>
<p>Responsibilities and Duties</p>
<p>Daily data entry of invoices, reconcile and verify invoices.</p>
<p>Pays vendors, scheduling and preparing checks, resolving purchase order, contract, invoice, or payment discrepancies and documentation; insuring credit is received for outstanding memos</p>
<p>Verifies vendor accounts by reconciling monthly statements and related transactions.</p>
<p>Post customer payments by recording cash, checks, and credit card transactions.</p>
<p>Posts revenues by verifying and entering deposits.</p>
<p>Verifies validity of account discrepancies by obtaining and investigating information from sales, trade promotions, customer service departments, and from customers;</p>
<p>Resolves invalid or unauthorized deductions by following pending deductions procedures.</p>
<p>Resolves collections by examining customer payment plans, payment history, credit line; coordinating contact with collections department.</p>
<p>Summarizes receivables by maintaining invoice accounts; coordinating monthly transfer to accounts receivable account; verifying totals; preparing financial reports.</p>
<p>Providing administrative support to the Controller and Accounting as needed.</p>
<p>Assists with maintaining budget with close communication with executives.</p>
<p>Qualifications and Skills</p>
<p>Proven accounting experience, preferably as an Accounts receivable clerk or Accounts payable clerk.</p>
<p>Familiarity with bookkeeping and basic accounting procedures.</p>
<p>Competency in MS Office, QuickBooks and accounting software.</p>
<p>Hands-on experience with spreadsheets and financial reports.</p>
<p>Accuracy and attention to detail.</p>
<p>Aptitude for numbers.</p>
<p>Ability to perform filing and record keeping tasks.</p>
<p>Data entry and word processing skills.</p>
<p>Well organized.</p>
<p>High school degree required.</p>
<p>Associate’s degree or relevant certification is a plus,</p>
