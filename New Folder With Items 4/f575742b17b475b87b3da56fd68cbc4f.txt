<p><b>zoovu</b> makes complex look simple for 15M+ people every year.</p>
<p>
We offer the leading SaaS platform to build and manage digital sales assistants for the web, mobile, chatbots, voice-enabled devices, and in-store use. These intelligent, conversational solutions engage buyers, ask the right questions in the right moment, and guide them to the right products, services or solutions.</p>
<p>
There's a lot that goes into "making complex look simple", which is why we're looking for an <b>Account Executive</b> to join our Sales team. This is a unique opportunity to work in our buzzing office in Boston together with our team of open-minded, genuinely caring professionals with many different backgrounds.</p>
<p>As an <b>Account Executive</b>, you will identify, source and close good-fit prospects. Using strong consultative selling skills, you will balance contacting inbound leads and driving self-sourced leads for your pipeline, while also partnering closely with internal stakeholders to achieve team goals and work towards zoovu’s mission.<br>
<br>
<b>In this role, you’ll get to</b>:<br>
</p>
<ul>
<li>Position the value of zoovu’s software and the conversational AI methodology to mid-size businesses</li>
<li>Educate and guide prospects through the buyer’s journey to help them learn how zoovu can grow their business</li>
<li>Manage a pipeline of inbound and self-sourced leads to identify, engage, and develop relationships with potential buyers</li>
<li>Dissect and qualify prospects’ business goals to determine if zoovu can be a strategic investment for their business’ growth</li>
<li>Close business with new and existing customers at or above quota level</li>
<li>Partner with marketing and technology departments to execute sales strategy as the company introduces enhancements to existing solutions and/or releases new products</li>
<li>Bring your thinking, strategies, and ideas to advance our company’s values, unique culture, and vision for the future<br>
</li>
</ul>
<p><b>We are looking for people who:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Have the desire and commitment to do what it takes to be successful in sales</li>
<li>Have a positive outlook and a strong ability to take responsibility for their successes and failures</li>
<li>Have exceptional consultative selling and closing skills</li>
<li>Are Top Producers in their current role</li>
<li>Have a sharp focus on their goals and a belief that their daily, weekly and monthly activities will help achieve them<br>
</li>
</ul>
<p><b>What we offer:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Competitive salary, based on your skills</li>
<li>Innovative working environment at a global market leader</li>
<li>Flat and clear structure with go-for-it attitude, international and friendly teams</li>
<li>Fun and collaborative environment, open and friendly organizational culture</li>
<li>Opportunity to grow your career in a rapidly growing company</li>
<li>Benefits package (paid vacations / time-off, health care/ insurance, 401K plan, commuting benefits)</li>
</ul>
