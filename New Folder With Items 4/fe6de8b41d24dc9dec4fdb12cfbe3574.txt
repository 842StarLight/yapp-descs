<h2>Water Restoration Sales Representative</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>OVERVIEW
</p>
<div><div><div>
<div>Roto-Rooter is the name that home owners and businesses have turned to for over 80 years for their plumbing and drain cleaning troubles. Our newest division, water restoration, is growing quickly, and we are adding additional team members.</div>
</div></div></div>
<p>RESPONSIBILITIES
</p>
<div><div><div>
<div>As a Water Restoration Sales Representative, your responsibilities will include:</div>
<ul>
<li>
Promote our commercial and residential services to insurance agents for properties that have been damaged by water</li>
<li>
Front line and first responder for our company</li>
<li>
Inspect site and present solutions to the customerl</li>
<li>
Be the point of contact for the customer and insurance agent throughout the project</li>
<li>
Ensure project is completed in established time frame and to the customer’s satisfaction</li>
</ul>
</div></div></div>
<p>REQUIREMENTS
</p>
<div><div><div>
<ul>
<li>Sales experience preferred</li>
<li>
Strong customer service focus and able to communicate effectively with customers</li>
<li>
Able to work in a fast paced environment</li>
</ul>
