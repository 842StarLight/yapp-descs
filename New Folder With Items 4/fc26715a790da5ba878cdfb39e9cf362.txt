<p>The PROS UX Team is seeking an aspiring User Experience Designer for our 2020 internship program. You’ll be mentored on a variety of techniques and tools from experienced User Research, UX Design, and UI Design professionals. You’ll get first-hand knowledge on what it takes to successfully collaborate in a design team. Plus, you’ll get practical work experience from a technology company creating advanced enterprise software products.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p>You may be the right person for this internship if you’re highly self-motivated and possess excellent time management skills. You should have a keen ear for coaching from senior team members and quick in applying it. Being articulate and observant is also essential.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p>We take the learning and development of our interns seriously. We have fun. We look out for each other. And we always get the job done. Come join us!<br>

<p>Responsibilities
</p>
<p>We’ll adjust this internship’s responsibilities according to your skills, but may include the following:
</p>
<ul>
<li>Assist in design sessions with product managers, developers and others
</li>
<li>Assist in user research activities, such as interviews or usability testing
</li>
<li>Design workflows and prototypes based on business requirements and user research
</li>
<li>Design high-fidelity user interfaces and interactions
</li>
<li>Collaborate and integrate contributions from Researchers, Writers, UI and UX Designers
</li>
<li>Write microcopy and other types of instructional text
</li>
<li>Craft user surveys, analyze data and report results
</li>
<li>Design and deliver presentations to an audience
</li>
<li>Seek coaching and apply feedback from senior team members
</li>
<li>Plus any other responsibilities deemed reasonable and necessary by management<br>
</li>
</ul>
