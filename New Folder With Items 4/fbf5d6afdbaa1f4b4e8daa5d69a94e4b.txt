<h2>Technical Recruiter</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">Join SADA Systems as a Technical Recruiter!
<br><br>
SADA Systems is adding a talented technical recruiter to the Talent Acquisition team to contribute to the growth of our Google and Microsoft practices.
<br>You'll have high visibility in our company and work on solving complex hiring issues with a seat at the table with senior leadership daily. Total ownership of your day-to-day and room for upward mobility. We are looking for someone with strong sourcing skills who can quickly bring in the best-qualified candidates. Agency or start-up experience is preferred. This person should be passionate about recruiting!
<br><br>
<b>What you'll be doing:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Post new requisitions on internal and external sites</li>
<li>Actively source for all open positions (main focus on technical positions)</li>
<li>Review submitted resumes and select qualified candidates</li>
<li>Conduct prescreen interviews and qualify independently</li>
<li>Work very closely with hiring managers through full lifecycle</li>
<li>Attend job fairs and Meetups as necessary to find qualified candidates</li>
<li>Follow company recruiting process to fill open positions</li>
</ul>
<b>You Would Be Great For This Position If You Have:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>BA/BS from an accredited university</li>
<li>Proven experience with 2 years of sourcing and recruitment</li>
<li>Experience hiring developers, engineers, technical architects or tech sales</li>
<li>A successful track record in IT recruiting - (measured by hires per month)</li>
<li>Prior experience using Jobvite or a similar ATS system</li>
<li>Excellent communication and written skills</li>
<li>Ability to negotiate salary with candidates</li>
<li>Process oriented</li>
<li>Thrive under pressure (easily work on 13 positions at a time)</li>
<li>Ability to work independently and must be proactive</li>
<li>Agency experience preferred</li>
</ul>
