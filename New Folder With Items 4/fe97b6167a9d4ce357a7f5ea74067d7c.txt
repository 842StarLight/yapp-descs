<p>KB Home, a Fortune 1000 NYSE company and one of America's premier homebuilders, has an immediate employment opportunity available. The Company operates in 38 markets in 8 states across the nation.</p>
<p>
KB Home (NYSE: KBH) is one of the largest homebuilders in the United States, with more than 600,000 homes delivered since our founding in 1957. We operate in 38 markets in eight states, primarily serving first-time and first move-up homebuyers, as well as second move-up and active adults. We are differentiated in offering customers the ability to personalize what they value most in their home, from choosing their lot, floor plan, and exterior, to selecting design and décor choices in our KB Home Studios. In addition, our industry leadership in sustainability helps to lower the cost of homeownership for our buyers compared to a typical resale home. We take a broad approach to sustainability, encompassing energy efficiency, water conservation, healthier indoor environments, smart home capabilities and waste reduction. KB Home is the first national builder to have earned awards under all of the U.S. EPA's homebuilder programs - ENERGY STAR®, WaterSense® and Indoor airPLUS®.</p>
<div>
<b>
JOB SUMMARY:</b> Prepare financial and operational forecasting, reporting and analyses, managing land development budgeting, contracting and cost accounting and various accounting-related and administrative responsibilities.</div>
<div><b>
</b></div>
<div><b>
ESSENTIAL DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITES:</b></div>
<div><ul>
<li>
Preparing Financial and Operational Forecasts, Reports and Analyses</li>
<li>
3-Year, Annual and Quarterly Business Plans/Forecasts/Flashes</li>
<li>
Internal weekly/monthly/quarterly financial reporting packages and variance analyses</li>
<li>
Land Development Budgeting and Cost Accounting</li>
<li>
Manage land development contract documentation</li>
<li>
Process land development draw/payment requests</li>
<li>
Maintain land development budgets</li>
<li>
<b>
Accounting and Administrative Functions</b><ul>
<li>
Maintaining summary-level house gross margin projections, by floorplan</li>
<li>
Processing/coding certain SG&amp;A costs</li>
<li>
Review coding prepared by other department managers</li>
<li>
Processing miscellaneous cash receipts</li>
<li>
Sarbanes Oxley compliance and information requests from corporate and centralized accounting</li>
<li>
Month/Quarter-end SOX compliance checklist</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>
Other Duties and Responsibilities as Assigned</li>
</ul></div>
