<h2>Customer Representative Service I</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div></div>
<div>
<ul>
<li><div>Light clerical work including answering / screening / telephone calls to the appropriate person, resolution of calls (orders, quotes, etc).
</div></li>
<li>
<div>Ability to use hand tools and assist or complete modifications to products i.e. glazing, section width changes, insulating, etc. and fork truck operation.</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Respond to customer requests for information concerning order tracking, order expediting and product availability.</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Perform order entry at both sales invoicing level and request for products from supply plants.</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Notify customers of potential backorders, credit issues, order availability and any other information regarding orders.</div>
</li>
<li><div>Act as a liaison between sales center and plant departments, gather the necessary information and respond appropriately.
</div></li>
<li><div>Process all necessary paperwork for the daily operation of the sales center as required.
</div></li>
<li><div>Delivery responsibilities as required. Including driver qualified (valid license).
</div></li>
<li>
<div>Assist in unloading and loading vendor and customer vehicles.</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Monitor completion of daily customer LTL shipment including UPS.</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Assist the GM and AGM with inventory controls including but not limited to cycle counts, daily review inventory levels, scrap processing and loss prevention.</div>
</li>
<li><div>Other duties as assigned.</div></li>
</ul>
<br>
<div></div>
<div></div>
<div>
<div><b>Qualifications</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
<div>1-3 years Customer Service experience, door industry preferred.</div>
</li>
<li><div>Attention to detail and problem solving.
</div></li>
<li><div>Excellent telephone, written and verbal communication skills while interfacing with all levels of staff and customers.
</div></li>
<li><div>Working knowledge of PC’s and related software including MS Word, Excel, Access, Lotus Notes, etc.
</div></li>
<li><div>Basic math skills required.
</div></li>
<li><div>Willingness to learn product and processes.</div></li>
</ul>
<div><b>Education</b></div>
<ul>
<li><div>High School Diploma or GED
</div></li>
</ul>
<div><b>Physical / Work Environment Requirements</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
<div>Ability to stand for long periods of time in a non-climate controlled environment.</div>
</li>
<li><div>Ability to lift up to 50 pounds.</div></li>
</ul>
