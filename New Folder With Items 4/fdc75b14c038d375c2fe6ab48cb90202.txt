<h2>SENIOR GLOBAL DEMAND PLANNER</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div><div>
<p>Abbott is a global healthcare leader that helps people live more fully at all stages of life. Our portfolio of life-changing technologies spans the spectrum of healthcare, with leading businesses and products in diagnostics, medical devices, nutritionals and branded generic medicines. Our 103,000 colleagues serve people in more than 160 countries.</p>
<p>
We are seeking an experienced, high caliber Senior Global Demand Planner. The Senior Global Demand Planner works without appreciable supervision, develops and issues the finished goods forecast per value stream assigned which translates into manufacturing plans by partnering with all or some of the following: marketing, supply chain management, supply planning, purchasing, finance, program management, regulatory, research and development and clinical.</p>
<p></p>
<p>
Impact this role will have:</p>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>
Prepares data and graphs and leads monthly pre-S&amp;OP meetings with cross-functional stakeholders (marketing, production, engineering, regulatory, clinical, R&amp;D) within applicable value streams; drives action items as result of meeting.</li>
<li>
Schedules and ensures proper attendance of pre-S&amp;OP meetings.</li>
<li>
Prepares S&amp;OP template for S&amp;OP management review and is prepared to justify actions.</li>
<li>
Facilitates meetings to ensure clarity between supply and demand.</li>
<li>
Develops new product launch plans (including phase ins and phase outs) with input and collaboration from selling divisions, production, marketing, R&amp;D and regulatory; works with Supply Chain Manager for production plan.</li>
<li>
Reviews data from various sources to create a finished goods forecast which translates into manufacturing build plans while giving consideration to the following: backorder mitigation, minimization of time finished goods sits in inventory, minimizing finished goods/raw material/subassembly scrap, finished goods safety stock, new product introductions and product phase outs.</li>
<li>
Leads cross functional meetings to ensure key stakeholders provide feedback and updates which could influence finished goods demands.</li>
<li>
Leads continuous improvement project teams, proactively and without prompting, pulling in stakeholders from across the entity.</li>
<li>
Performs as a systems super-user to investigate and recommend system efficiency improvements.</li>
<li>
Provides training and guidance to new members of the team as well as Regional Demand Planners</li>
<li>
Review demand submissions from Regional Demand Planning team to finalize global demand plan and prepare monthly hand off to Supply</li>
<li>
Analyzes the following on a consistent basis to review accuracy, identify trends and capture issues, developing plans of actions based on findings: actual shipments versus the net required forecast plans; finished goods actual results versus the forecast; actual sales versus the forecast</li>
<li>
Loads and maintains the demand forecast into the appropriate system.</li>
<li>
Prepares various reports as needed which are moderately to highly complex.</li>
<li>
Facilitates communication with key stakeholders regarding finished goods supply issues. Partners with stake holders to resolve outstanding issues.</li>
<li>
Complies with U.S. Food and Drug Administration (FDA) regulations, other regulatory requirements, Company policies, operating procedures, processes, and task assignments.</li>
<li>
Maintains positive and cooperative communications and collaboration with all levels of employees, customers, contractors, and vendors.</li>
<li>
Performs other related duties and responsibilities, on occasion, as assigned.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p>
Your experience(s), education and knowledge:</p>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>
Bachelor’s degree in Supply Chain, Business Administration, Finance or related field.</li>
<li>
4 years’ minimum experience in forecasting/demand planning.</li>
<li>
3 years of experience working in forecasting/demand planning/ERP/MRP systems and/or software</li>
<li>
Ability to work in a highly matrixed and geographically diverse business environment.</li>
<li>
Ability to work within a team and as an individual contributor in a fast-paced, changing environment.</li>
<li>
Ability to leverage and/or engage others to accomplish projects</li>
<li>
Strong verbal and written communications with ability to effectively communicate at multiple levels in the organization.</li>
<li>
Ability to travel approximately 10%, including internationally.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p>
Your preferred qualifications and education:</p>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>
1 year in medical device.</li>
<li>
Experience working in a broader enterprise/cross-division business unit model.</li>
</ul>
