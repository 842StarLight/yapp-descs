<p><b>Data Protection Analyst</b></p>
<p><b>Summary of Responsibilities</b></p>
<p>The data protection analyst role is accountable to deliver prompt and reliable security prevention, monitoring and response services to enable business productivity focusing on cloud and endpoint security. This role will have responsibility to implement and monitor data protection solutions and provide recommendations to manage information technology risks. As member of the global information security team, responsibilities include:</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Provide subject matter expertise for tools and services that protect sensitive information and prevent data loss</li>
<li>Provide ownership for operational, administrative support, and full product lifecycle management of specific security tools (e.g. DLP, CASB)</li>
<li>Participate in the selection and implementation of security tools</li>
<li>Develop business relationships and recommendations to achieve maximum business value from those solutions</li>
<li>Enhance and support data classification program throughout the company</li>
<li>Refine, enforce, and manage discipline surrounding information security policies and standards balancing organizational risk and need against industry best practices</li>
<li>As part of a global information security team, advocate for information security management and provide security insights and recommendations to leadership</li>
<li>Work with management to develop key usage reports and policy infringement alerts to support each business area and the Information Security team (e.g. SOC, SIEM, Legal, Ethics, Compliance, etc.)</li>
<li>Inspect and fulfill requests for exceptions to security policies and standards related to protecting sensitive information</li>
<li>Assist with investigating security breaches and other cyber security incidents; document incident artifacts and business impacts/concerns</li>
<li>Administer portable media program, policy, and periodic review to meet the needs of the business</li>
<li>Work with Security Awareness for information handling best practices</li>
<li>As necessary, support or participate in penetration testing and remediation efforts</li>
<li>Stay up-to-date on information technology and security trends to drive information protection needs</li>
<li>Provision application and network security access per user requests</li>
<li>Other information security activities as needed</li>
<br>
</ul>
