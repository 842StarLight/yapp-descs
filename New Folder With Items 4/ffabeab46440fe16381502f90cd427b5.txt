<p><b>ABOUT US</b></p>
<p>For more than 20 years, WINFertility has been managing family-building benefits that are delivered with care and support by the highest quality providers. WIN is the largest, most experienced fertility benefit management company in the country. It offers inclusive family-building solutions to employers, health plans and individual patients. From integrated fertility management, including medical treatment, pharmacy and reproductive genetics, to surrogacy and adoption, WIN’s mission is to help build families by providing access to the best network doctors, technology and emotional support at the lowest cost.</p>
<p>WIN’s managed fertility programs include an integrated medical and pharmaceutical model that is unique to the industry. Its dedicated nurse care managers, all with experience in reproductive endocrinology clinics, guide employees through every step of their fertility journey, and are available 24/7.</p>
<p><b>SUMMARY</b></p>
<p>The Project Manager will define the current and future state of WIN’s implementation processes, identify issues and risks, suggest strategies and drive towards solutions. This person is responsible for all aspects of the planning and delivery of strategic programs and client implementations. They will manage initiatives through advanced skills in project management and strong organizational relationships.</p>
