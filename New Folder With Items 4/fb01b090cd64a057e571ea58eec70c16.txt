<h2>Tax Partner - South Florida</h2>

<p>Cherry Bakaert is a nationally rank public accounting firm that is celebrating over 70 years! . We seek a Tax Partner, with an active CPA license and current experience in public accounting with a large regional or national firm, to join our growing firm in our Coral Gables or Ft. Lauderdale office. Relocation assistance may be provided.</p>
