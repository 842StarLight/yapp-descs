<h2>Ulta Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div><div>
<p><b>Ready to bring passion into your career?
</b></p>
<p>Clarins Group is not only a fast-growing, leading Beauty Company, we’re also a committed, family-owned enterprise with a fiercely independent and pioneering spirit. As one of the top skin care companies worldwide, with a strong position in Europe and the Americas and growth accelerating in Asia, we’re looking for an <b>Ulta Specialist for the Los Angeles, California market.</b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
The role:</b></p>
<p>
Supporting the growth of Clarins, this role is responsible for contributing to the development of the brand at Ulta by using all available tools and collateral to achieve targets.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
What you’ll do:</b></p>
<p>
By fulfilling the main mission of this role, you will contribute to all of the 5 pillars of the Group strategy: people-centric, brand-builder, consumer-obsessed, digitally minded, and committed to responsible beauty.</p>
<ul><li>Provide in-store selling and training to Ulta store management, staff and vendor partners.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Represent the Clarins brand by planning, executing and participating in corporate events and achieving sales targets.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Hire, develop and coach a selling team to achieve their personal sales productivity.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Provide weekly recaps to reporting manager on sales and performance from the previous week and action plan for upcoming week on store support, events, sales</li></ul>
plans and focus.
<ul><li>Provide in-store training to Ulta management and Beauty Advisor teams on Clarins products and applications.</li></ul>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
You are:</b></p>
<ul><li>Curious, with the desire to learn constantly.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Analytical and have strong problem-solving skills.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Collaborative and succeed in motivating a team.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Genuinely looking forward to new challenges.</li></ul>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
You have:
</b></p>
<ul><li>An Associate’s degree. Bachelor’s degree preferred.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Minimum of 4-6 years of successful in-store cosmetics and sales experience.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Excellent communication and organizational skills plus the ability to work in a team environment.</li></ul>
<ul><li>The ability to handle multiple projects, review and prepare event schedules and meet critical deadlines.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Proficiency in Microsoft Office</li></ul>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
We have:</b></p>
<ul><li>Standout brands: Clarins, My Blend, Mugler and Azzaro Fragrances</li></ul>
<ul><li>Distinctive, high-quality products and services</li></ul>
<ul><li>A longstanding commitment to social, economic and ecological initiatives</li></ul>
<ul><li>Growth opportunities</li></ul>
<ul><li>Learning &amp; Development</li></ul>
<ul><li>An attractive compensation and benefits package</li></ul>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
If you have a curious mind and are always questioning how things can be done better, the ability to take risks and accept new challenges, join us!</p>
