<h2>CAS Technical Forensics Team Lead</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>As part of Cellebrite Advanced Services, the <i>Technical Forensic Team Leader</i> (TFTL) provides expert support to our customers by performing specialized services within the Cellebrite Forensics Lab, and on-site at customer labs within the region, including logistics and equipment installations and maintenance. The TFTL will manage a team of Digital Forensics Specialists and Operations Specialists.</p>
<br>
Requirements:<br>
<ul>
<li>Manage a Advanced Services’ activities in the USA including 2 forensic labs and the professional team</li>
<li>
Perform advanced mobile forensics processes using Cellebrite’s industry exclusive tools
</li>
<li>Deploy Cellebrite systems at customers’ site and provide training and support</li>
<li>
Be the Cellebrite Advanced Services “go-to-person” in the region</li>
<li>
Respond to immediate investigational needs of the customer</li>
<li>
Deliver feedback to the CAS Management for quality process improvement</li>
<li>
Constantly improve operation effectiveness and quality</li>
<li>
Brief and train the local Sales team in the region.</li>
<li>
Gather product feedback for Research &amp; Product planning needs</li>
<li>
Escalate complex and problematic cases to CAS Technical Director</li>
<li>
Record lab activity in case management system in real-time</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
