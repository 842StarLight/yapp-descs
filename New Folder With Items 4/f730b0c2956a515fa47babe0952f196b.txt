Sales
<br><br>
We're looking for salespeople to manage accounts and skyrocket customer growth. If you're a self-starter with a consultative approach to selling — with experience pitching sales to any audience, and cultivating great client relationships — this job's for you!
<br><br>
The Role
<br><br>
The Inbound Business Development Representative plays a critical role in growing our business. The main focus of this position is in managing SurveyMonkey's inbound sales process by utilizing our suite of internal tools.
<br><br>
Our Ideal Candidate
<br><br>
Our ideal candidate is a self-motivated team player with high social intelligence and a natural inclination for selling. S/he is very driven and can show evidence that they thrive in a competitive, merit-based environment. They also value networking and the benefits of being part of an agile, growing team.
<br><br>
Responsibilities
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Manage various social media tools for inbound prospect development</li>
<li>Interact with prospects and provide high level offering information</li>
<li>Generate qualified leads and set sales appointments</li>
<li>Manage prospecting status and forecasting in Salesforce.com</li>
</ul>
Qualifications
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Excellent instincts for conversing with technology executives and ability to think on the fly</li>
<li>Strong work ethic – possess the desire to master the art of selling</li>
<li>Fluency in English along with excellent phone skills, phone etiquette, interpersonal, written and oral communication skills</li>
<li>BA/BS degree is preferred</li>
</ul>
At SurveyMonkey, we offer competitive salaries, medical/dental benefits, PTO, 401k, paid holidays, and equity compensation.
