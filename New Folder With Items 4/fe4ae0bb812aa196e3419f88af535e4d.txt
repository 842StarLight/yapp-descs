<h2>Business Transformation Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<b>Division Information</b><br>
<p>Spectrum Brands utilizes a Shared Services model that enables resources to be leveraged across the organization. The Shared Services team is made up of information technology, human resources, indirect sourcing, finance &amp; accounting professionals that support all business units. Shared services teams are headquartered in Middleton, Wisconsin but have members across the country.</p>
<p><b>
This position can be based out of our Middleton, WI or St. Louis, MO offices.</b></p>
<br>
<b>
Job Summary</b><br>
<p>The Business Transformation Manager leads cross-functional business transformation initiatives through the program definition/requirements to set the program expectations, benefits and timing. This includes defining stakeholders, key resources, and tool/process needs. To define the program, usually requires a strong technical knowledge across multiple functions, tools and processes to capture the current “as is” state as well as the desired future state. Often includes benchmarking across one or multiple industries to identify industry best practices and incorporate them into the proposed program solutions. Program often includes IT investments larger than $1M and/or large organizational change.</p>
<p><b>
This is a Limited Term Employment (LTE) position, expected term is 12-18 months.</b></p>
<p>
SPB 20/20 is our strategy to build a stronger future with strong brands and exciting innovation as an efficient low-cost provider. With every employee engaged in delivering great products and exceptional service and creating a culture where we speak up, take action, grow talent and hold ourselves and each other accountable, we are building an even stronger future for ourselves and our shareholders.</p>
<br>
<b>
Primary Duties &amp; Responsibilities</b><br>
<p>Lead cross-functional business transformations</p>
<ul>
<li>
Lead cross-functional efforts to transform key aspects of the SPB products, processes and technology portfolio leveraging global, industry-standard base design points best-practice aligned with core corporate strategic objectives</li>
<li>
Manage large scale, complex projects with multiple stakeholders, often competing priorities and aggressive timelines</li>
<li>
Stage activities with relevant milestones and schedules. Anticipate and adjust effective contingency plans</li>
<li>
Navigate and influence executive priorities in order to achieve the most desirable outcome for SPB</li>
<li>
Unlock value within defined budget parameters - can identify impact of changes and tie to larger program/portfolio budget/benefits across the business</li>
<li>
Present phase exit approvals and updates directly to SPB leadership</li>
<li>
Coordinate work efforts of multiple internal teams across SPB as well as 3rd party consultant agencies and contractors with clear direction and accountabilities. Monitors progress by maintaining dialogue on work and results</li>
<li>
Identifies and creates processes necessary to get work done. Separates or combines activities to create efficient workflow</li>
</ul>
<p>
Demonstrate behaviors aligned with our core values</p>
<ul>
<li>
Step up to address difficult issues; have the courage to say what needs to be said</li>
<li>
Hold self and others accountable to meet commitments</li>
<li>
Take on new opportunities and tough challenges with a sense of urgency, high energy and enthusiasm</li>
<li>
Inspire by gaining the confidence and trust of others through honesty, integrity and authenticity</li>
<li>
Cultivate innovation by introducing new ways of looking at problems. Demonstrate ability to put a creative idea into practice. Encourages divers thinking to promote and nurture innovation</li>
<li>
Talk about future possibilities in a positive way. Create milestones and symbols to rally support behind the vision Partners with others to establish and implement change and communication plans and tactics</li>

<p>
Apply functional and business expertise</p>

Understands and can articulate the interconnections between direct procurement, indirect procurement, supply chain, marketing/new product development, and other business teams</li>
