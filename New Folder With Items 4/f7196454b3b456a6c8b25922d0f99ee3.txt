<h2>Office Administrator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>The outdoors is a big place and just as it offers endless adventures, so does our company.</p>
<p>
Camping World is the premier one-stop-shop for everything RV. Along with RV parts and accessories, we also offer a full selection of new and used RVs for sale; service centers with over 1,900 bays and over 1,600 technicians on staff; over 100 collision centers; RV Spa detail and refurbishment centers; technical advice from knowledgeable experts and hard-to-find parts.</p>
<p>
As the RV industry leader, Camping World is always looking for new and better ways to enhance your RV and outdoor experience. Camping World is a big place, just like the great outdoors. We make it our mission to equip you for it. We've got an awesome team already, but there's always room for more!</p>
<p>
Camping World is looking for an Office Manager.</p>
<p><b>
Essential Job Functions:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Breakdown, post and submit funding paperwork for all Sales transactions</li>
<li>Process and post all cash receipts, credit card payments, scanned checks and ACH payments</li>
<li>Maintain Dealership inventory including stocking in new and used inventory, intercompany transfers and monthly floor plan audit</li>
<li>Process and facilitate all payroll and Human Resource paperwork for submission to corporate office</li>
<li>Sort, review and post all vendor invoices and credit card transactions with correct GL coding</li>
<li>Perform /oversee title work process and procedures including MSOs, POAs and other documentation, fees and related sales tax submissions</li>
<li>Prepare/ provide reports, schedules or requests for additional information on a timely basis to the Area Controller, corporate office or General Manager</li>
<li>Assist the General Manager in running an efficient, organized dealership</li>
<li>Maintain high standards of accuracy and assure compliance with company and other applicable policies and procedures</li>
<li>Provide excellent customer service and maintain vendor/customer relations</li>
<li>Other duties / projects as assigned for the overall benefit of the Dealership</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p>
</p>
<p><b>Essential Job Skills:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Excellent knowledge of a variety of computer software applications in maintaining calendars, word processing, spreadsheets (MS Outlook, Word, Excel, Access, PowerPoint).</li>
<li>Knowledge of auto dealership accounting systems (IDS, ADP, Reynolds &amp; Reynolds) strongly desired</li>
<li>Strong attention to detail in composing, typing and proofing materials, establishing priorities and meeting deadlines.</li>
<li>High level of interpersonal skills to resolve A/P issues.</li>
<li>Ability to handle sensitive and confidential information and situations.</li>
<li>High level of demonstrated poise, tact and diplomacy.</li>
<li>Strong written and verbal communication skills.</li>
<li>Ability to interact and communicate with individuals at all levels of the organization.</li>
<li>Knowledge of office administrative procedures and knowledge of use and operation of standard office equipment.</li>
<li>Ability to work in a fast-paced environment with demonstrated ability to manage multiple competing tasks and demands</li>
<li>We promote a drug-free work environment.</li>
</ul>
<p>Competitive pay, based on experience.</p>
