<p><b>Responsibilities and Duties</b></p>
<p>The duties of a Sewer/Matt Borders Operator include the following:</p>
<ul>
<li>Uses computerized sewing equipment to make borders for mattresses.</li>
<li>Places border onto spindle, feeds into machine.</li>
<li>Monitors machine operation and verifies product meets specifications. Inspects and trims loose threads.</li>
<li>Places finished borders in designated area.</li>
<li>Clears material jams and threads and resets computer as needed.</li>
<li>Changes thread and loads bobbin as needed.</li>
<li>Performs operator maintenance on equipment. Operates vertical stitched.</li>
</ul>
