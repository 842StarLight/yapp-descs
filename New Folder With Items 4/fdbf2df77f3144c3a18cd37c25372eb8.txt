<h2>MPS Coordinator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>ARC is looking for a full-time <b>FM/MPS Technician Coordinator </b>to help us continue to provide the best employment experience for employees; and, continued excellent service for our customers.</p>
<p><br>
<b>
Description:</b><br>
We’re searching the market for a <b>FM/MPS Technician Coordinator</b> to maintain and manage equipment fleet management system for responsible locations, verify and meet service level agreements on equipment contracts, and provide technical assistance and training to system users by performing the following duties:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Schedule and/or execute print production or mailroom solutions; and, troubleshoot digital imaging devices (printers, copiers, fax, scanners, multi-function) at customer sites.</li>
<li>
Manage and schedule customer print production resources to meet the volume of workflow; this may include scheduling overtime for onsite staff to meet customer expectations.</li>
<li>
Provide customers with clear proactive information on all issues concerning possible delays in service.</li>
<li>
Responsible for responding to service calls for clients who need repair on their office technology equipment.</li>
<li>
Travel to the customer location, diagnose the problem and make necessary repairs on large and small format print machines.</li>
<li>
Inspect and perform the necessary routine and preventive maintenance; and, replace any parts that are nearing expiration. Communicate the status of repairs to the customer; and, order parts when necessary.</li>
<li>
Schedule service repairs and dispatch appropriate field service technicians to customer locations for service based on location, skill level of the technicians, and facility response guidelines.</li>
<li>
Respond to customer inquiries concerning systems operation and diagnoses system hardware, software, and operator problems.</li>
<li>
Issue the appropriate service limitations per ticket assigned.</li>
<li>
Develop and maintain a positive relationship with customers.</li>
<li>
Follow -up with customers to gauge satisfaction.</li>
<li>
Recognize and report extended or costly service events.</li>
<li>
Other duties as assigned.</li>
</ul>
<p><br>
<b>
Experience and Qualifications:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Must have at least 2 years Hardware/IT experience in reprographic field.</li>
<li>
Must have experience operating and repairing all formats of printer/copier machines including Oce, Canon, HP, Kip and/or other Wide Format B&amp;W or Color machine</li>
<li>
Must have a working knowledge of Microsoft Excel, Word, Outlook, PowerPoint, Adobe/Bluebeam, and basic Color Management.</li>
<li>
A valid driver’s license and acceptable driving record</li>
<li>
Reliable transportation to travel to various customer locations</li>
<li>
Ability to push and/or pull heavy equipment, up to 100 pounds</li>
<li>
Ability to work a 40-hour week, Monday – Friday and overtime as needed</li>
<li>
Strong customer service and communication skills</li>
<li>
Strong attention to detail</li>
<li>
Ability to balance multiple priorities in a fast-paced environment and align operations and sales processes to achieve business results</li>
<li>
Self-motivated with the ability to work independently, resourcefully with minimal oversight</li>
<li>
Demonstrated experience in developing strong working relationships with internal and external customers</li>
</ul>
