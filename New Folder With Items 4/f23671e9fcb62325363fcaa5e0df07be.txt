<h2>Associate Product Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><i>Challenging convention. Trying new things. Reshaping an industry.
</i></p>
<p></p>
<p>The team at Duck Creek helps insurance companies bring amazing ideas to life quickly and easily with software that thinks and works like they do. We're fueled by sharing ideas openly, challenging conventions, trying new things, and valuing "Why not?" over "Why?" Our certainty that there is always a better way to do things keeps carriers ahead of their competition and is helping to reshape an industry.
</p>
<p></p>
<p>If having a hand in transforming one of the world’s oldest and largest industries into a standard for innovation, open exchange, and peerless user experience sounds exciting, let us know. We may be looking for you.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Product Management </b><b>- Associate Manager
</b></p>
<p></p>
<p>A Product Management Associate Manager for Duck Creek Technologies will support the Digital Engagement Product Manager related to product vision and product roadmap for our enhanced customer experience and digital enabler program. The Product Manager will also work with team leadership to define scope, costs and timelines for updating existing products and implementing new products according to Duck Creek’s product roadmap. In addition to an analytical skillset, the ideal candidate will be a self-starter who exhibits creativity, integrity, teamwork, and a great sense of humor.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Basic Qualifications:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li><p>Bachelors plus 3-5 years’ experience.
</p></li>
<li><p>Deep understanding of insurance company workflows and processes
</p></li>
<li><p>Strong analytic skills and ability to synthesize large datasets into meaningful insights
</p></li>
<li><p>Strong interpersonal and team building skills - ability to work with a cross-functional team and influence change across functional and business units
</p></li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li><p>Strong project management, planning, and organization skills, including ability to handle multiple projects simultaneously in a fast-paced environment
</p></li>
<li><p>Superior communication skills, both verbally and in writing
</p></li>
<li><p>Unwavering attention to detail
</p></li>
<li><p>Ability to effectively manage multiple competing priorities without sacrificing quality or impacting deadlines
</p></li>
<li><p>Will have a basic understanding of SaaS solutions, API and integration strategies, and value proposition of SaaS to customers.
</p></li>
</ul>
<ul><li><p>Will have a basic understanding of digital solutions, customer experience programs and UX design
</p></li></ul>
<p><b>Responsibilities:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li><p>Hands-on definition of the product requirements and the prioritization process, including: Assisting with the managing of product backlog and tracking of market demand
</p></li>
<li><p>Participate in defining and prioritize backlog items necessary to deliver targeted new capabilities into the product
</p></li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li><p>Developing use cases, business scenarios, user personas and other design artifacts to support requirements and development specifications
</p></li>
<li><p>Responsible for serving as the voice of the customer to UX and Engineering
</p></li>
<li><p>Manages product release schedules and work with Product Manager and Engineering to insure releases are on time and issues are communicated and resolved
</p></li>
<li><p>Serves as touch point and provides product related expertise for activities managed by other product teams
</p></li>
<li><p>Supports development of training, documentation, or tools as required
</p></li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li><p>Supports product input and documentation needed for pre-sales and client related activities as required
</p></li>
<li><p>Stays current on trends related to digital products and relates that information into product visioning and solutions</p></li>
</ul>
