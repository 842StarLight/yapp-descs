<h2>Senior Business Process Consultant – SAP SD</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>The Senior SD Business Process Consultant supports the maintenance of the existing SAP Sales &amp; Distribution (SAP SD) solution for North American Sales, Customer Service, and Marketing. The Senior SD Business Process Consultant provides the day-to-day configuration and technical support for Sales and Marketing solutions that are deployed to the Production Environment. This position will work closely with other IT Business Process Consultants and user departments to understand and document business requirements. The business requirements must be translated into technical requirements that can be used as a guide to develop SAP SD solutions. The Senior SD Business Process Consultant is also responsible for implementing the solutions in the global SAP system.
