<h2>Service Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p>TEC Equipment is the West’s leading full-service, heavy-duty truck and trailer dealership, with over 1,200 employees in 26 locations in Washington, Oregon, California, Nevada, and Arizona. Since our humble beginnings in 1976, we continue to be family-owned and operated. Click Here to Learn More About TEC Equipment.</p>
<p>
We are seeking a<b> Service Manager </b>for our Oakland, CA location. The best fit for this role will continue to drive our success and status as a leader in the industry through the efficient and profitable operations of the Service Department. We are seeking an individual with proven leadership and management capabilities who will lead all members of the service team in a positive and cohesive manner toward set goals that result in strong, steady, and sustainable business growth.</p>
<p>
At our Oakland, CA location we offer new and used trucks sales, leasing, parts and service and finance. We are a Mack/Volvo-certified Uptime Service Center licensed dealership. We support all makes of trucks, and more in our service department.</p>
<p>
We are seeking someone who has successful experience building and maintaining solid, positive relationships with internal partners and customers.</p>
<p>
As a fast-paced growing company with a unique business model that allows you the opportunity to use your knowledge and creativity to make business decisions to drive the business forward, we seek candidates with an entrepreneurial spirit who will work collaboratively with team members to achieve continued growth and success.</p>
<p><b>
In this role you will:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
<b>
Serve as a Leader and Role-Model to the Team -</b> Conduct self with highest of ethical and professional standards and model the TEC Core Values of Teamwork, Entrepreneurship, and Customer Focus. Set standards and expectations in place to ensure smooth operations and relationships between all team members.</li>
<li>
<b>
Employee Resource Management </b>- Manage team of approx. 20 diesel technicians, service advisors, service foremen and supervisor, warranty admin, and drivers. This includes workforce planning, staffing, employee development, team building/morale, and performance management.</li>
<li>
<b>
Strong Working Relationships</b> – Commit to a strong and mutually beneficial relationship with all internal business partners (parts, leasing, etc.) to maximize financial performance and customer satisfaction. Seek out, develop, and maintain excellent vendor relationships.</li>
<li>
<b>
Customer Focus </b>- Build and grow customer relationships and loyalty by assuring prompt high-quality repairs, meeting Volvo/Mack Uptime standards, and proactively resolving complaints and problems.</li>
<li>
<b>
Safety and Quality </b>– Provide a safe working environment and reinforce a culture of shop safety. Employ lean process improvement concepts to optimize and streamline operations. Ensure the highest standards of service to internal and external customers.</li>
<li>
<b>
Metrics &amp; Profitability</b> – With guidance from General Manager, take full accountability for the profitability, risk management, and growth of Service department. Forecast long and short term objectives for the department and manage team toward goals utilizing metric objectives scorecard. Actively review financial statements; monitor and manage department expenses appropriately.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Qualifications we seek:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Successful experience managing a team (preferably 20+ employees) with proven history of achieving sales and business objectives</li>
<li>
Excellent people management and leadership skills; ability to build effective relationships with internal and external partners</li>
<li>
Entrepreneurial ability – able to create successful business opportunities and solve problems</li>
<li>
Sound business acumen with ability to analyze, prioritize, identify, and execute solutions</li>
<li>
Experience with operational metric and P&amp;L management</li>
<li>
Knowledge of diesel truck repair and quality standards</li>
<li>
Proficient with computers; ability to utilize all required systems and software</li>
<li>
Valid driver’s license</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Preferred Qualifications:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Experience in transportation industry, repair shop, or dealership environment</li>
<li>
Experience with CDK and ASIST</li>
</ul>
