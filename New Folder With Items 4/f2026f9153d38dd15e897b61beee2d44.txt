<h2>Warehouse Lead</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Fast growing Palm Desert company seeks experienced Warehouse Lead to improve operational efficiency and help manage inventory.</b><br>*<br>Josh Rosebrook Skin and Hair Care LLC is an independent brand of personal care products which has been in the Coachella Valley for 7 years. We started in a garage in Palm Springs and today our products are offered in more than 250 retail stores worldwide.</p>
<p>The Warehouse Lead is a full-time (40 hours/week) position that reports to the Operations Manager and will oversee 2-4 individuals in the warehouse. This position has the following key responsibilities:<br>*<br>*<br>--Manage inventory levels for all finished goods and components.</p>
<p>--Perform periodic inventory counts quickly and accurately.</p>
<p>--Communicate the above information to the Operations Manager to ensure appropriate inventory levels and eliminate out-of-stock situations.</p>
<p>--Coordinate the timely and accurate shipping and receiving of more than 1000 orders per month: inbound shipments from manufacturers, suppliers and vendors, as well as outbound shipments for wholesale and retail customers.</p>
<p>--Communicate with the Sales Manager as needed to prioritize outbound shipments and reduce shipping delays.</p>
<p>--Maintain quality control standards.</p>
<p>--Continually refine warehouse processes to maximize efficiencies for time, labor and materials.</p>
<p>--Maintain a safe and clean working environment consistent with company values as well as state and local regulations.<br>*<br>*<br>Computer literacy and proficiency is a must. The ability to learn new software systems while maintaining a positive attitude is required.</p>
<p>The ability to work in a small team is essential. We are a tight group with a start-up mentality and an entrepreneurial spirit. We encourage new ideas and different perspectives, we are always looking to improve and we welcome new challenges.</p>
<p>Experience operating forklifts, labeling machines, shrink wrappers, bar code scanners and other warehouse machinery would be helpful. This job will require the ability to move boxes weighing up to 50 pounds on a regular basis.</p>
<p>Pay will be determined based on experience. We offer a benefits package which includes paid holidays, paid vacation and health care reimbursements.</p>
<p>Qualified applicants please provide a resume and cover letter for further consideration.<br>*</p>
