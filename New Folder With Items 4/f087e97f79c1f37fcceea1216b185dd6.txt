<h2>Senior Manager Accounting</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>

<p><b>
BASIC FUNCTION OF JOB</b></p>
<p>
The Senior Manager of Accounting (Revenue) is primarily responsible for the accounting for revenue, inventory, and related financial statement line items. Included in the role is the responsibility for a daily report of revenue (for Product lines of business), cost of goods sold and gross margin, which is distributed to company leaders. The role is also responsible for the accuracy of the General Ledger and adherence to SOX-related processes and controls associated with these areas. In addition, the position will be responsible for leading and developing individuals within the team (currently 1 manager and 5 individual contributors) and supporting the Director, Finance and VP - Controller.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>WORK PERFORMED</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Manage the monthly financial close process related to revenue and inventory, including ensuring all revenue/costs incurred are properly recorded, reviewing journal entries, account balance reconciliations and report preparation</li>
<li>
Manage the compilation and review of the daily report of revenue, cost of goods sold and gross margin.</li>
<li>
Analyzing individual sales transactions and applying generally accepted accounting principles in order to:<ul>
<li>
Recognize or defer revenue and cost in the proper period.</li>
<li>
Record appropriate revenue and cost at a line of business and profit center level.</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>
Coordinate with business owners on new solution offerings to ensure proper alignment of operational and accounting treatment.</li>
<li>
Review quarterly cut-off testing procedures performed by the team to ensure proper period revenue recognition.</li>
<li>
Reviewing and performing income statement and balance sheet analytics.</li>
<li>
Oversight and ownership of key processes and related controls subject to SOX testing.</li>
<li>
Reviewing and assist with various internal and external audit requests.</li>
<li>
Identify and drive continuous process improvement efforts.
</li>
<li>Various ad-hoc analysis and special projects as assigned.
</li>
<li>Support development and maintenance of Accounting Policies and Procedures.</li>
<li>
Identify improvements for the accounting close process and develop/document solutions for improvements.
</li>
<li>Assist with the North America close the books accounting processes. Ensures month end closing schedules are maintained.
</li>
<li>Performs other duties as assigned by the Director, Finance and the VP Controller, North America.</li>
</ul>
