<p>ADVERTISING SALES &amp;PUBLICATION MANAGEMENT<br>VISITOR PUBLICATION<br>WELCOME GUIDE-MAP - ORLANDO &amp; KISSIMMEE</p>
<p>Excellent sales opportunity for someone who is responsible, professional, well-organized, and loves selling for an established, proven visitor publication. This is a key position selling advertising and managing the <i><b>Welcome Guide-Map.</b></i></p>

The Orlando &amp; Kissimmee <i>Welcome Guide-Map</i> is a leading visitor guide in this market with an excellent track record and reputation.</p>
<p>The hospitality/tourist industry profession is uniquely fun and rewarding. We truly enjoy helping visitors and the businesses that rely on them. If you are a sales professional and this appeals to you, we are looking for someone like you to join us.</p>
