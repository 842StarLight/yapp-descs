<h2>Corporate Copywriter</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<b>Description</b><div>
<b>
J</b><b>ob Overview:</b>
</div>
<div>
Develop engaging, strategic and on-brand copy for SPP’s corporate marketing, sales and online initiatives. Including but not limited to, video marketing scripts, blog and website copy and content curation for StaplesPromo.com. Help support business development department for new business win and RFP retention presentations.</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div><b>
Primary Duties and Responsibilities:</b></div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div><b>
Impact on Business</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Develop copy for key Marketing initiatives across multiple sales channels and internal teams
</li>
<li>Act as both a copywriter and proofreader. Attention to detail is paramount.</li>
<li>Maintain the integrity of the Staples/SPP voice while pushing our creative copy forward</li>
<li>Effectively communicate the power and rationale of trends and products as determined by the merchandising team(s)</li>
<li>Immerse yourself in the client’s brand and write to their tone/voice when responding to client RFP’s</li>
<li>Ability to write RFP responses, executive summary reports and business proposals</li>
<li>Manage content and tone/voice for our corporate blog and social media channels</li>
<li>Strategize on social media campaigns/efforts and bring new ideas to the table for development of these online channels</li>
<li>Create engaging headlines and body copy for eComms, flyers and presentations</li>
<li>Effectively communicate with the Project Managers and Marketing Managers to ensure copy is on-target and on-brand</li>
<li>
Research trend, product, design and technology insights and adapt them to be communicated effectively for our industry</li>
</ul>
<br>
<p></p>
<b>
Qualifications</b><div><b>
Basic:</b></div>
<div></div>
<ul>
<li>
Bachelor’s degree in communications, marketing or business field preferred.</li>
<li>
2-4 years previous experience writing for either business or industry publications, web writing, technical writing or advertising agency clients.</li>
<li>
1-2 years proofing and editing copy and providing constructive feedback to other writers and coworkers inside and outside the department</li>
<li>
Familiarity with all the latest design and communication trends, the ability to monitor these trends to ensure continuous improvement and sharing new knowledge with others</li>
<li>
Proficiency and experience in Microsoft Office Suite.</li>
<li>
Self-starter with good organizational skills and ability to prioritize numerous projects simultaneously.</li>
<li>
Flexibility and teamwork is essential.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<div><b>
Professional Knowledge/Experience</b></div>
<div></div>
<ul>
<li>
Demonstrated knowledge of grammar, writing and editing skills</li>
<li>
Ability to independently prioritize and tackle multiple projects on a daily basis</li>
<li>
Experience with both creative and corporate writing styles</li>
<li>
Ability to learn new systems, applications or skills on the job</li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<div><b>
Other Skills</b></div>
<div></div>

Proficiency in MS Office, including PowerPoint and Excel</li>
<li>
Strong problem solving skills</li>
<li>
Excellent self-discipline</li>
<li>
Collaborative and positive attitude with an outgoing nature</li>
</ul>
