<h2>Service Specialist (HPE)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>Connection, a Fortune 1000 Global Technology Solutions Provider is adding to our family of passionate individuals. We work together to connect people with technology that enhances growth, elevate productivity, and empower innovation. If you would like to discover how over the 35 years of collaboration has shaped our culture of integrity, teamwork, and mutual success, we would love to speak with you!<br>
<br>
<b>Responsibilities:</b>
</div>
<ul>
<li>
Working under minimal supervision of the Manager Warranty Programs, referring only exceptional problems and issues for management review or approval, the Services Specialist works with PC Connection sales subsidiaries on new warranty service plan opportunities and on defining programs.</li>
<li>
In addition, the Services Specialist collaborates with the vendor(HPE) to drive more sales on open quotes, trains the sales team on vendor(HPE) service offerings, utilizes PC Connection's systems to find new and missed opportunities and follows up on large opportunities with services quoted to help close deals.</li>
<li>
The Services Specialist attends vendor(HPE) training's, business review planning meetings and service conferences.</li>
</ul>
<div>
<br>
<b>
Qualifications:</b>
</div>
<ul>
<li>
Bachelors degree in Business Administration, Computer Science or related field preferred.</li>
<li>
Three plus years of sales, sales support, service plan sales or IT sales experience required.</li>
<li>
Basic computer skills using Microsoft office Excel, Word, and PowerPoint.</li>
<li>
Strong desire to continually learn and apply latest technologies.</li>
<li>
Knowledge of vendor specific technology (HPE) preferred.</li>
<li>
Working knowledge of sales strategies including up-selling.</li>
<li>
Proven success in a goal driven oriented environment.</li>
<li>
Good analytical thought process with ability to determine effectiveness of programs.</li>
<li>
Self- motivated with ability to meet quarterly goals.</li>
<li>
Excellent relationship building skills with ability to create positive account managers and vendor relationships</li>
<li>
Excellent verbal and written communication skills with ability to assist and/or write effective programs for the sales team.</li>
<li>
Excellent time management skills with ability to prioritize escalated or urgent issues and projects.</li>
<li>
Knowledge of warranty service plans, sold or support of plan.</li>
<li>
Understanding of OEM programs to be able to drive attach rates, revenue, and margin.</li>
</ul>
<div>
<br>
Base compensation for this position begins at $18/hour, DOE.</div>
