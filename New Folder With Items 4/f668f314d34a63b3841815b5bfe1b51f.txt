<h2>Eng III, Intergration</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Safran </b>is an international high-technology group, operating in the aircraft propulsion and equipment, space and defense markets. Safran has a global presence, with more than 92,000 employees and sales of 21 billion euros in 2018. Working alone or in partnership, Safran holds world or European leadership positions in its core markets. Safran undertakes Research &amp; Development programs to meet fast-changing market requirements, with total R&amp;D expenditures of around 1.5 billion euros in 2018.
</p>
<p><b>Safran </b>is ranked among the Top 100 Global Innovators by Thomson Reuters and is featured on the "Happy at work" rankings. The Group places fourth on the Universum ranking for the favorite companies of newly-qualified engineers in France.
</p>

<div>
<div><div>A.
</div></div>
<div><div>Utilizes CAD software to construct, modify and/or interrogate 3D models, in accordance with aerospace industry standards (ASME Y14), for transport elements and aircraft installations.<br>
</div></div>
</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div>
<div><div>B.
</div></div>
<div><div>Utilizes PLM/PDM software to control CAD data configuration. Documents drawing changes per company practices. Ensure data input into PLM/PDM tools are meeting Monogram requirements and Industry Standards.<br>

<br>
<div></div>
<div>
<div><div>C.
</div></div>
<div><div>Ensures quality deliverables for the installation and integration. All products should be interference free and install without rejection tags. Strives to create Drawings and Models free of mistakes and errors.<br>
</div></div>
</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div>
<div><div>D.
</div></div>
<div><div>Supports installation and integration design projects. Performs detail design of transport elements. Follow Monogram policies and procedures. Looks for opportunities for efficiencies in the engineering processes, and makes recommendations on improvements to peers and management.<br>
</div></div>
</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div>
<div><div>E.
</div></div>
<div><div>Performs basic or preliminary engineering tasks under the guidance and supervision of senior engineering staff.<br>
