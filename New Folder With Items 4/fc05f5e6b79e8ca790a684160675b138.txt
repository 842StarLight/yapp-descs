<div>Cost Analyst (GFEBS-19-0918-W)<br>
<br>
Bowhead seeks a Cost Analyst to supports it's customer in Fort Belvoir, VA. The Cost Analyst will prepare financial and business related analyses and research in such areas as financial and expense performance, rate of return, depreciation, working capital, and investments.<br>
<br>
Other duties may include:<br>
<li>Prepare forecasts and analyze trends in manufacturing, sales, finance, general business conditions and other related areas. Utilizing PC and/or mainframe based systems and software;
</li>
<br>
<li>Compile and prepare reports, graphs and charts of data developed. Assists in the development of business policies, conducts special financial and business related studies and cooperates with other departments in the preparation of analyses.</li>
<br>
<li>Perform economic analysis of projects and other tasks in support of the GFEBS Portfolio, using models, standards and tools consistent with DoD CAPE, CADE and Army requirements.</li>
<br>
<li>Assist the Government in developing Rough Order of Magnitude (ROM) and other financial estimates based upon available GFEBS and other sources cost and economic data. The main purpose of the ROM and financial estimates is to provide PM leadership with the information necessary to make decisions on whether it makes sense to move forward with projects or other tasks based on the estimated level of effort, in terms of completion time and costs. ROMs and financial estimates shall be accurate, transparent, and reliable.</li>
<br>
<li>May provide work leadership for lower level employees.</li>
<br>
