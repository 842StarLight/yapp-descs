<h2>OFF SHIFT MAINTENANCE TECHNICIAN</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">Founded in 1925, MacLean-Fogg is a worldwide enterprise currently operating 40 global manufacturing facilities across 5 continents with annual sales in excess of one billion (USD) and a workforce of over 3,200 people. MacLean-Fogg is a privately held enterprise headquartered in Mundelein, IL USA and is comprised of two primary businesses, MacLean-Fogg Component Solutions and MacLean Power Systems. MacLean-Fogg Component Solutions (MFCS) is a leading manufacturer of fastener components, engineered components and engineered plastics for automotive, heavy truck, and other diverse industries. MacLean Power Systems (MPS) is a leading manufacturer of products for electric utility, telecommunications and civil markets.

Responsibility Summary
<br>Job Summary
<br>Perform duties to maintain, repair and install production, building, and material handling equipment, and restore machines to manufacturing standards.
<br><br>
Responsibilities
<br><ul>
<li>Maintain and repair a variety of plant production equipment such as presses, belt sanders and grinders, involving mechanical, electrical, pneumatic, resin handling, emergency, cooling tower, compressed air and hydraulic functions</li>
<li>Diagnose the nature of a problem, disassemble equipment, replace worn or defective parts and components such as motors, bearings, shafts and belts, rebuild unit, test operation and make final adjustment</li>
<li>Maintain and repair building, inspect and service electric controls and replace defective components as necessary</li>
<li>Perform scheduled preventive maintenance activities throughout the plant as required, oil and lubricate equipment and change fluid and filters as required</li>
<li>Fabricate parts as needed for repairs as feasible</li>
<li>Perform plumbing and welding maintenance activities as required</li>
<li>Report deficiencies of replacement parts and maintenance supplies to Maintenance Supervisor</li>
<li>Manage area specific system procedures</li>
<li>Adhere to Company Quality Policy and follow appropriate procedures as documented in training agreement</li>
<li>Follow safety rules and keep work area in a clean and orderly condition</li>
<li>Perform other related duties as assigned</li>
</ul>
Qualifications
<br><ul>
<li>High School education or equivalent experience plus 1-3 years of applied training</li>
<li>Must be able to work from verbal instructions, written instructions, sketches, blueprints, electrical, PLC, schematics, manuals and other reference materials</li>
<li>Use hand power tools, machine tools, drill press, grinder, sander, gas and electric welding equipment, lift truck, forklift and hand trucks, overhead crane and acetylene torch and a variety of hand tools</li>
<li>Must be a self starter with good organizational skills and a high level of reliability</li>
<li>Must be a self-motivated team player</li>
</ul>
