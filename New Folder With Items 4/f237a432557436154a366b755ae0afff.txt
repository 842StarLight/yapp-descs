<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>About Our Company
</b></h2>
<p>C&amp;S Wholesale Grocers, Inc. , based in Keene, NH, is the largest wholesale grocery supply company in the U.S. and the industry leader in supply chain innovation. Founded in 1918 as a supplier to independent grocery stores, C&amp;S now services customers of all sizes, supplying more than 6,000 independent supermarkets, chain stores, military bases, and institutions with over 150,000 different products. At C&amp;S, We Select the Best® – those with the motivation, pride, and drive to succeed in our fast-paced world.

<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Position Overview
</b></h2>Job Title: Accounts Receivable,Cash Application Supervisor
<br>
Function: Finance
<br>
Management Level: Manager/Supervisor
<br>
Location: Keene, NH
<br>
<br>
Role overview:
<br>
Assist in management of billing and cash application functions for the company, ensuring that customer payments are applied and customers are billed in a prompt and accurate manner
<br>
<br>
Accounts Receivable overview:
<br>
Manages credit and collections function for C&amp;S in order to extend terms appropriate to risk associated with each customer, and maximize cash flow and minimize Days Sales Outstanding (DSO) &amp; bad debt risk
<br>
Responsible for prompt and accurate customer billing and application of cash received from customers; maintains customer master file data for billing
<br>
Maintains customer contract master file data and manages the compliance audit function to ensure customer billing is in compliance with contract
<br>
<br>
Responsibilities:
<br>
<ul><li>Manage cash application function, including oversight and guidance of:
</li></ul>o Apply lock box receipts, checks and electronic wire payments to the accounts receivable Oracle general ledger
<br>
o Enter manual billings and credits into the C&amp;S mainframe system or Oracle general ledger either via manual input or upload
<br>
o Research and resolve short and over payments, including
 <br>
Work with the customer to gather documentation
 <br>
Work with collections to ensure accurate application
 <br>
Work with contract compliance team/reference contract terms – e.g. customer may have a standard percentage based credit agreement
 <br>
Escalate to team lead and/or collections if unresolved
<br>
o Provide payment schedule to customer per contract terms to encourage compliance with contract
<br>
<ul><li>Manage billing function, including oversight and guidance of :
</li></ul>o Ensure proper control of manual and credit data entry processed in department
<br>
o Ensure all fixed weekly charges and credits are processed on a timely basis
<br>
o Provide ad hoc reporting re billing to senior management as necessary
<br>
o Prepare and provide weekly billing statements with appropriate back-up to each customer
<br>
<ul>
<li>Review and approve manual billings as threshold/limits dictate
</li>
<li>Partner with customers, IT and AR customer set up team on customer start-ups to ensure following parameters are correctly displayed in Oracle AR :
</li>
</ul>o Invoice &amp; statement formats
<br>
o Payment terms
<br>
o Adjustments (rebates, discounts etc)
<br>
<ul>
<li>Carry out periodic review of customer payment terms to ensure that Oracle AR is in line with supply agreement
</li>
<li>Complete/review balance sheet account reconciliations as required
</li>
<li>Assess and optimize processes including:
</li>
</ul>o Regularly gather input from billing and cash application leads re drivers of issues
<br>
o Partner with IT/customer set up/collections/team to develop and implement process changes as necessary
<br>
<ul>
<li>Assist in the coordination and management of documentation/information as required for external audits
</li>
<li>Recruit, manage and develop employees including :
</li>
</ul>o Identification of annual objectives and performance assessment thereof
<br>
o Provide training and development opportunities consistent with objectives
<br>
<br>
Qualifications:
<br>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor's degree in Accounting/Finance or a related field required
</li>
<li>Minimum of three years of applicable experience, including understanding key accounting principles
</li>
<li>Demonstrated proficiency in Excel; experience with financial systems and software (e.g. GL package, Financial reporting system) preferred
</li>
</ul>Individual will need to demonstrate and/or develop proficiency in:
