<p>Due to rapid growth in our e-commerce division, we’re looking to add Full Time team members to our Tax Exemption team.</p>
<p>
 Join one of Pennsylvania’s fastest growing companies today! WebstaurantStore, a Clark Associates company, is looking for proactive, detail oriented candidates to join our growing company as a Tax Exemption Coordinator within our Logistics department. This role focuses on attention to detail and accuracy and is a key part of ensuring WebstaurantStore is providing quality assistance to customers while processing their tax exemption documents in compliance with state sales tax laws.</p>
<p><b>
As a Tax Exemption Coordinator, you will:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Review and approve all incoming exemption certificates, ensuring they are complete, correct and appropriate</li>
<li>Manage customer requests related to taxability of certain items</li>
<li>Manage and maintain the exemption certificate website/software</li>
<li>Ensure all exemption certificates are cataloged and easily retrievable</li>
<li>Perform periodic reviews of exemption certificates and collection/approval processes</li>
<li>Continue education related to changes in exemption certificate law</li>
</ul>
<p><b>We are looking for motivated candidates who:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Are able to work accurately at a fast pace</li>
<li>Provide excellent communication</li>
<li>Have a strong work ethic</li>
<li>Can work well independently or as part of a cohesive team</li>
<li>Are able to work at a computer for extended periods of time</li>
<li>Have proficient typing skills</li>
</ul>
<p>No prior sales tax exemption knowledge is required.</p>
