<h2>Account Manager II</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Job Summary:
</p>This position is accountable for the acquisition of new business (Medicare Advantage Employer Group), conservation of existing accounts and selling additional product lines where appropriate for the SHBP Local, FEP, Small Group Broker, Medicare, Midsize, and Dental markets.
<ul>
<li>Acquires new business (Medicare Advantage Employer Group).
 </li>
<li>Manages contract renewals (all markets for Dental) and ensures services are provided that will maximize the potential for account retention and initial member enrollment.
</li>
<li>Ensures proper installation of new accounts (Medicare Advantage Employer Group) as well as changes to existing accounts.
 </li>
<li>Plans and negotiates terms and conditions of contracts with existing accounts.
 </li>
<li>Directs the implementation process for new clients and/or employers.
</li>
<li>Sells additional lines of business to existing clients.
</li>
<li>Compares and evaluates various products to make appropriate recommendations based on client needs assessment.
</li>
<li>Modifies existing contracts that require term changes that may be outside of predetermined guidelines or standards.
</li>
<li>Develops and conducts workshops for clients/groups relative to enrollment, claim procedures, and health industry regulations.
</li>
<li>Weighs factors and outcomes to make binding financial, operational, and service-related decisions.
</li>
<li>Ensures that all accounts and brokers are promptly informed of relevant legislative and regulatory issues and their associated impact.
</li>
<li>Performs other duties as assigned by management.
</li>
</ul>
<p>Education/Experience
</p>
<ul>
<li>Requires a bachelors degree.
</li>
<li>Requires a minimum of five (5) years of professional sales or contract negotiation experience, preferably in the health insurance industry, with a documented track record of success.
</li>
</ul>
