<p>Inviting applications for the role of <b><i>Customer Service Representative I</i></b></p>
<p>The primary objective of the <b><i>Customer Service agen</i></b>t position is to provide a great customer experience and first call resolution support to internal and external customers. This position requires strong decision-making skills to assist in resolving customer problems, communication issues and maintaining customer master data in the order entry / processes system. This position has multiple skills to manage handling overflow calls from many divisions.</p>
<p><i>Responsibilities: </i></p>
<p><b>Maintain work expectations for Pest division, which may include: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>May work in more than one operating system: contact center phone system, Outlook, workforce management scheduling, internal website</li>
<li>Serve as communication liaison entering customer messages on behalf of client customers</li>
<li>Accurately and efficiently enter customer and field and sales associate orders</li>
<li>Respond to credit related inquiries: enter card payments and initiate dispute / credit re-bill process</li>
<li>Research and respond to customer and field complaints</li>
<li>Log all call transactions into the designated computer software system</li>
<li>Meet minimum call volume and quality requirements for the position; Adhere to daily and weekly call goals as directed</li>
<li>Meet attendance expectations</li>
<li>Maintain accurate record keeping and data management</li>
<li>Maintain an excel spreadsheet of daily work as directed</li>
</ul>
