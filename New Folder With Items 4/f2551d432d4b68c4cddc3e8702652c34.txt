<h2>CAD Drafter</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">Description
<br><br>
Position at NAPA Auto Parts
<br><br>
Job Description
<br>Are you looking for a job in which your efforts are visible and tangible? If so, come join our team as a CAD Drafter and be responsible for creating store plans for our remodeled and new stores! In this position, you will join our Retail team and play a critical role in drafting store plans, for our space planning team, which helps increase our sales and store appearances.This position is ideal for someone who has experience with AutoCAD and is able to translate blue prints into our system software, where required. 3 years of experience working with AutoCAD and experience in fields such as, visual merchandise planning, commercial/retail store planning or drafting for commercial/retail, would make this person successful.
<br><br>
Responsibilities
<br>References blueprints, electrical schematics, architectural, and engineering drawings. Answers questions regarding store plan layouts. Reviews documents for accuracy and makes recommendations as required.
<br>Creates detailed interior floor layouts of stores in AutoCAD, including, but not limited to sales area, backroom, office, bathrooms, racking, etc. Reads store layout sketches, and drafts in AutoCAD system.
<br>Receives direction on updates and design revisions as needed, creates or modifies layouts to match company specifications, as well as building code requirements, and suggests alternatives for any issues encountered.
<br>Tracks project correspondence and documents, follows up with necessary parties to obtain documentation, and files all information in such a way as to be easily viewable by team members. Ensures updates are made as needed to track and report on project progress.
<br>Assists with organizing AutoCAD files including revisions. Adds all AutoCAD files required to the project management systems.
<br>Assists with obtaining all necessary information for each store requirements and layouts, reaching out to the field for more information requests and following up on those requests to ensure deliverables are identified, met and reflected.
<br>Performs other work as assigned.
<br><br>
Qualifications
<br>3 years' of experience with coordinating projects and working with AutoCAD or in a similar field (commercial/retail store planning, or drafting).
<br>Working knowledge of applicable federal, state and city laws, ordinances, codes and regulations a plus, particularly how to apply to store plan layouts.
<br>Working knowledge of ADA requirements and how to apply when creating floor plans.
<br>Bachelor's Degree or equivalent work experience required. Associates with drafting focus highly preferred.
<br>Must be proficient with AutoCAD 2D 2010 or newer.
<br>Working knowledge on how to use project management systems (Costar) a plus.
<br>Working knowledge of Microsoft Office products including Excel.
<br>Working knowledge of Adobe products including Photoshop and Illustrator, a plus.
<br>Ability to communicate effectively, efficiently, and professionally required.
<br>Must have a strong attention to detail both visually and verbally.
<br>Ability to prioritize and manage multiple tasks, based on priority and business needs.
<br>Demonstrates both the ability to work well with others in a team environment and work independently where required.
<br>Work is performed in a typical office environment.
<br>Minimal travel required.
