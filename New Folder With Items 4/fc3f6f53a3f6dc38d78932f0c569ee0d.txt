<p><b>RV Sales Job Description</b></p>
<p>
Due to unbelievable demand, we are needing to add to our talented sales team! You don’t need previous sales experience to take advantage of this RV sales opportunity with unlimited earning potential. What you need is a great attitude, a talent for dealing with people and a knack for paying attention to even the smallest details. We will provide the training you need to succeed! Candidates who have excelled in this position include former business owners, competitive athletes, educators, insurance agents, and door-to-door sales, but we are open to anyone with passion, drive, and anyone with an unyielding desire to succeed.
</p>
<p><b>Job Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li> Understand and implement dealership sales process</li>
<li> Own every aspect of the sales process (from new client prospecting to vehicle delivery)</li>
<li> Present and demonstrate inventory and build value in product</li>
<li> Review, update and manage daily inventory reports</li>
<li> Create and execute strategies for increasing market share</li>
<li> Prepare and review monthly plans with sales leadership</li>
<li> Maintain Customer Service Index, Gross Average and monthly units to dealership standards</li>
<li> Remain up-to-date on products, market trends and certification</li>
<li> Must be familiar with Client Relationship Management (CRM) software</li>
<li> To maximize earning potential; you must be willing to work weekend’s</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Education and/or Experience</b></p>
<p>
Previous sales experience is not necessary, but relevant education and prior sales training are a PLUS.</p>
<p><b>
Compensation</b></p>

Compensation is based on an industry aggressive pay scale. Top producers can earn up to $200K per year.</p>
