<p><b>Principle Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Execution of assigned incoming orders and change orders.</li>
<li>Review new orders for compliance.</li>
<li>Electronically transfer the internal order within the ERP Systems.</li>
<li>Handle all assigned sales order tasks and ensure the order is handled within the appropriate time standard.</li>
<li>Create purchase orders and reconcile invoice issues.</li>
<li>Track order communications and log order status/delay information.</li>
