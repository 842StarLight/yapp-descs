<h2>Technical Product Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Job description</p>
<p>Compete to Create was founded by Pete Carroll and Dr. Michael Gervais to train mindset skills for people to find their best. Come join us to accelerate the products that deliver this vision. Our mission is to help 1 in 5 people train their mind and create a positive impact the world.</p>
<p>We’re looking for an outstanding Technical Product Manager, someone who thrives on the intersection of customer requirements, market opportunities, engineering excellence and ease-of-use. We have a rapidly growing educational platform and online course. This is a full-time position based in Seattle, WA.</p>
<p>Your Responsibilities:</p>
<p>· Communicate directly with and manage development team</p>
<p>· Create a product strategy that realizes company vision</p>
<p>· Develop a product roadmap and product feature briefs to guide execution that incorporates priorities and balances trade-offs</p>
<p>· Evaluate and measure product performance</p>
<p>· Analyze results to define new versions of the product as well as changes in course</p>
<p>· Research and evaluate competitive products</p>
<p>· Mock-up product feature concepts, user functional flows, and high-level wireframes; author clear user story boards; and write concise user stories to define not just what the products are but also how our products work to delight our customers</p>
<p>· Define quantitative and qualitative success metrics and constantly evaluate if our solutions and features meet our business goals both before and after their release.</p>
<p>· Triage bugs and work with Engineering to schedule fixes; communicate with internal stakeholders and external customers.</p>
<p>· Own Product Management processes: continuously improve efficiency and effectiveness to improve quality and time to market.</p>
<p>· Collaborate with sales and marketing for insights on customer experience</p>
<p>Qualifications and Skills Required</p>
<p>· 2-4 years minimum building product for a startup software product or large corporation experience</p>
<p>· Bachelor's degree in a STEM subject preferred</p>
<p>· Passion for a mission-driven organization</p>
<p>· Experience with B2C and B2B products, education or training a strong plus</p>
<p>· Passion for User Experience Design</p>
<p>· Natural instinct for creative problem solving</p>
<p>· History of data-driven decision-making</p>
<p>· Strong communication skills; oral and written</p>
<p>· Flexible; a skilled juggler</p>
<p>· Team and relationship-oriented</p>
<p>· Curious, hard-working and a problem-solver</p>
<p>· Ability to work in the United States without employee sponsorship</p>
<p>· Demonstrated success working with geographically disparate teams</p>
<p>· Ability to look at the big picture while maintaining attention to detail</p>
<p>Benefits &amp; Additional Information</p>
<p>· Medical, Dental, Vision Insurance</p>
<p>· Creative environment and creative freedom</p>
<p>· Passionate team working to change the landscape of corporate training</p>
