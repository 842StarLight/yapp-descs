<h2>Quality Engineer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<ul>
<li>Coordinates with the quality assurance and engineering department in the development of data collection and analysis techniques including measurement system analysis, and computerization of all systems and studies directed at improving overall plant quality and productivity.</li>
<li>
Develops and operates plant wide quality assurance programs in conjunction with quality assurance and engineering departments and provides corrective action when necessary.</li>
<li>
Develops and maintains customer approved statistical process control program and evaluates application of metrology and statistical methods used to diagnose and correct quality deficiencies.</li>
<li>
Establishes reliable vendor relationships and coordinates the receiving of all raw materials to ensure quality standards, timely delivery and cost efficiency.</li>
<li>
Establishes and coordinates testing procedures used in production to ensure acceptable quality levels in conjunction with engineering specification requirements to include development of technique, frequency, and proper documentation and reporting of the results.</li>
<li>
Develops and conducts training programs related to quality assurance which includes proper principles and procedures for statistical process controls.</li>
<li>
Conducts customer and supplier quality surveys at various manufacturing facilities, which maintains a standard of quality in raw materials and finished goods.</li>
<li>
Researches and develops techniques for solving quality related engineering problems, which may include identifying cost causes, instituting corrective action and monitoring the results.</li>
<li>
Tests product samples documenting results for ISIR/PSW submission and approval, coordinates with suppliers for similar submission and approval, and maintains necessary documentation and files for customer quality rating programs.</li>
<li>
Determines the deposition of rework and rejected products from customers and suppliers, and prepares the necessary documentation.</li>
<li>
Assists engineering by providing technical support in the development and standardization of quality assurance aspects</li>
<li>
of new products, gauging inspection procedures, supplier quality assurance, supplier audits, and problem solving and at the customer plant level.</li>
</ul>

<p><b>
ABOUT DURA AUTOMOTIVE SYSTEMS</b></p>
<p>
DURA Automotive Systems, a Lynn Tilton company, is a global automotive supplier specializing in the design, engineering and manufacturing of innovative solutions that propel the vehicle development initiatives of automakers.</p>
<p>
DURA is focused on advancing global sustainability technology in three core areas:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Driving fuel economy improvement through vehicle lightweighting and intelligent mechatronics</li>
<li>
Advancing safety through innovative structural vehicle architectures and advanced driver control solutions</li>
<li>
Preserving our environment through sustainable manufacturing practices</li>
</ul>
<p>
The company’s products include mechatronic control systems, including shift-by-wire systems; electronic systems, including actuators and Advanced Driver Assist Systems (ADAS); lightweight structural body systems, including aluminum closures; and exterior systems, including window systems and exterior trim.</p>
<p>
DURA is headquartered in Auburn Hills, Michigan, USA; with global sales of $1.4 billion. The company has 9,400 employees worldwide and operates 31 facilities in 14 countries. Strong investments in new innovation programs and access to the extensive network of Lynn Tilton technology companies that span virtual gaming, electronics, fashion and aerospace industries have catapulted DURA’s future product offering to anticipate and satisfy the next generation of customer needs. DURA is nationally certified in the United States by the Women's Business Enterprise Council.</p>
