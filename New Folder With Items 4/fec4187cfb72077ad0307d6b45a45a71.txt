<h2>Marketing Associate</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Eurofins Central Laboratory is committed to providing the highest quality services, as well as accurate, timely results capturing the planned completion date, and expert advice from our highly qualified team of experienced scientists</b></h3>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>ECL is searching for Marketing Associate in Lancaster, PA. The Marketing Associate is responsible for ensuring branding via all possible delivery vehicles (advertising, conferences, social media, web pages, press releases, e-mail campaigns) are consistent and delivered with quality. This is a critical role within Eurofins Central Laboratory, with responsibility of branding of services to the industry and ensuring first impressions of Eurofins, is accurate and received favorably.

</h3>

</h3>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader">
