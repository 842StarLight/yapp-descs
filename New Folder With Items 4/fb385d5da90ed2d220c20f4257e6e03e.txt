<div><b>Job Summary:</b></div>
The Hospital EMS Coordinator is responsible for strategically planning and implementing outreach efforts focusing on local EMS providers who treat and transport patients to acute care facilities based on services needed by the patient. The position is responsible for increasing volume and revenue for an assigned territory by targeting EMS customers through new business development, service line sales and communiction. The main goals of this position is to improve processes and care coordination with community EMS providers by providing education regarding quality and service indicators at AdventHealth facilities. The coordinator will also serve as an advocate of the hospital with private, local and regional EMS agencies. They will develop and implement a business plan to drive service-line growth, share market intelligence with hospital leadership and help to ensure ongoing process improvements<br>
