<p><b>SUMMARY</b></p>
<p>
Are you a problem solver, who thrives in challenging scenarios? Are you driven to be successful, and chase an unlimited earning potential? Are you interested in a lucrative career in sales, but want to be more than just another person on a huge sales floor?</p>
<p>
LeSaint Logistics has openings for sales professionals of all levels. Our National Account Executive Freight Broker position could be the perfect fit! Whether you have ten years of sales experience or just getting started, LeSaint can provide the opportunity for YOU to excel, and earn you way to the top of the income totem pole. We offer a base salary that is negotiable based on your experience. Additionally, you have the opportunity to earn uncapped commission on top of the salary. The base salary NEVER goes away, and LeSaint management is committed to investing in our people to help them maximize their commission earnings.</p>

This is what separates LeSaint from other broker positions: you can truly offer full service for your clients.</p>

If you've ever thought about a career in logistics, or even just sales in general, the small-business type atmosphere will facilitate a place where you can learn, and grow, both in experience, and income. Our thorough, hands on training program will cover both operations, and sales, and we will support you every step of the way as you build your book of business. Don't forget, you'll have the network and resources of a nationwide company with facilities in 10 states.</p>
<p>
Interested? We want to talk to you about becoming a National Account Executive Freight Broker on the Brokerage Team here at LeSaint Logistics!</p>
<p><b>
ESSENTIAL DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES</b> include the following.</p>
<p>
Other duties may be assigned.</p>
<ul>
<li>
Generate and pursue new sales leads through various avenues, primarily by cold calling</li>
<li>
Consistently meet sales activity metric goals, including new client acquisition</li>
<li>
Meet quarterly and annual revenue and gross profit goals for the role, or other Key Performance Indicators KPIs as defined and assigned by Brokerage Manager</li>
<li>
Provide exceptional customer service to clients, carriers, and internal customers</li>
<li>
Utilize phone, email, and other avenues to communicate with clients, carriers, and internal customers as required</li>
<li>
Negotiate, dispatch and assign carriers to customer shipments in accordance with LeSaint brokerage guidelines.</li>
<li>
Shipment management tracking and tracing to ensure on time and safe delivery of loads.</li>
<li>
Problem order management/resolutions - major load issues must be communicated to the customer and Brokerage Manager</li>
<li>
Able to think analytically and strategically with a process and systems orientation</li>
<li>
Able to work as a team player</li>
<li>
Follow all policies and procedures necessary to do the job properly.</li>
<li>
Maintains High integrity - honors commitments and the Values of LeSaint</li>
<li>
Regular attendance and timeliness is an essential function of the job.</li>
</ul>
