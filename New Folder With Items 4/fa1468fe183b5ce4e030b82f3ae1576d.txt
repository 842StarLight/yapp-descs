<p><b>SUMMARY</b></p>
<p><b>
 </b>Reporting to the CHRO, the Recruiter will practice creative and innovative ways of sourcing, screening, interviewing and ensuring qualified candidates are processed for hiring managers throughout clinic locations. They perform follow-up meetings with hiring managers to ensure new hires are secured and retained. Special emphasis is to be placed on sales, billing office associates, medical office and ancillary staff associates, such as X-Ray Technicians or Technologists, Medical Assistants, Front Office Medical Receptionists, etc.</p>
<p><b>
 </b><b>ESSENTIAL DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES</b></p>
<p><b>
 </b>1. Build partnerships with managers to gain a better understanding of staffing plans and develops recruiting plans accordingly</p>
<p>
2. Review resumes; screen/interview candidates in person or over the phone, and forward qualified candidates to the appropriate hiring manager; check references as requested</p>
<p>
3. Recruit qualified candidates through newspaper advertising, approved recruiters, internet and other recruiting sources.</p>
<p>
4. Attend job fairs and trade shows when appropriate</p>
<p>
5. Assist in placement of print advertisements</p>
<p>
6. Maintain ad placement on websites and specific trade journals</p>
<p>
7. Negotiate contracts per company policy</p>
<p>
8. Develop innovative, creative and proactive recruitment strategies to meet the business needs of Practice Managers, focusing on clinical staff, ie: Medical Assistants, Front Office Medical Receptionists, Histotechs, etc..</p>
<p>
9. Monitor salary administration, in partnership with compensation, to ensure compliance and equity within the organization</p>
<p>
10. Interact with search firms and employment agencies, as necessary for temporary, permanent placement and locum tenens (per diem) positions</p>
<p>
11. Obtain directories for mailers of hard-to-fill positions</p>
<p>
12. Consult with managers to gain an understanding position requirements</p>
<p><b>
KNOWLEDGE, SKILLS AND ABILITIES</b></p>
<p><b>
 </b>1. Solid knowledge of Human Resources personnel practices and principles</p>
<p>
2. Demonstrated knowledge of technical skills and recruiting techniques with a proven track record and experience in finding candidates for hard-to-fill positions</p>
<p>
3. Solid planning, time management, communication, decision-making, presentation, organization and interpersonal skills</p>
<p>
4. Computer skills, including Microsoft Word, Excel and PowerPoint.</p>
<p>
5. Experience with UltiPro Recruiting and On-Boarding modules</p>

 <b>WORK ENVIRONMENT AND CONDITIONS</b></p>
<p>

Must be able to work efficiently under high pressure. Position often requires long and irregular hours and may involve dealing with angry, upset or uneasy people. Work may be stressful at times. Travel required: up to 25% of time.</p>

Normal or corrected vision to acceptable levels for common office work. Hearing must be in the acceptable range for telephone contacts. It is necessary to view and type on computer screens for variable periods of time.</p>
