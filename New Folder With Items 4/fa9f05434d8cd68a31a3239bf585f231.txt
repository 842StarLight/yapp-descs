<h2>Director of Franchise Development</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>**Candidates must currently work in the San Francisco Bay Area**</p>
<p>Interested in being part of the ground foundation of a new and unique company with unlimited potential? Join the CodeFu Team and do something that truly benefits the world by shaping future generations with skills they'll need to flourish!</p>
<p>CodeFu: Coding for Kids delivers quality after school coding classes for kids ranging from Pre-School to 8th grade. In the past 4 years in the Bay Area, we have grown to over 50+ schools! Developed by Software Engineers &amp; Teachers, we are unique in that our curriculum is designed for the After School Setting alleviating parents from having to drop their kids off at a Coding Center that's not located at their school.</p>
<p>While experience is important to us, being a key member of the CodeFu Team is the most important element. You will be working closely with our CEO &amp; will be the primary point of contact for franchise development.</p>
<p>This unique opportunity is for candidates who see the long term potential of CodeFu and envision themselves being part of a company that will be around for decades to come.</p>
<p><b>Compensation Includes: </b></p>
<p>Base Salary: $45,000 - $55,000 DOE</p>
<p>Commission: 10% of every unit sold (each unit is $20,000 and franchisees often purchase multiple units) There is no cap on how many units you can sell. Depending on performance, the possiblity of commission increase is available.</p>
<p><b>Your duties and responsibilities will include: </b></p>
<ul><li>Achieving all goals/objectives of the franchise sales and development plan within a targeted region. The incumbent will plan, develop, receive approval for and execute the sales/marketing strategies and tactics required to meet stated goals/objectives.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Conducting full life-cycle franchise sales for CodeFu both individually and as part of the franchise development team.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Identifying and cultivating sources for new franchise candidates as well as existing franchisees for additional growth.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Following a disciplined but flexible process to close franchise deals according to expected timelines.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Coordinating implementation of all programs through Franchise Development, Legal, Operations, Marketing, and other Franchise Growth staff.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Developing and executing business plan to sell Franchise and/or Development Agreements to new and/or existing Franchisees as required by the applicable strategic and franchise development growth plan.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Attracting qualified franchise candidates through social media, paid search, local advertising, sales flyers/brochures, open houses, industry groups, direct mail, trade shows, etc. Developing and utilizing creative new approaches to attract candidates.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Cultivating expansion and/or business transition (re-sale) opportunities for existing franchisees; refer leads to other franchise development team members as appropriate.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Delivering executive-level sales presentations to multi-unit franchise candidates.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Tracking and reporting on the progress of each prospective candidate through the qualification process. Maintaining accurate and timely records in CRM/pipeline report(s).</li></ul>
<ul><li>Performing and/or hosting initial meeting, Discovery Day and/or other orientation with prospective CodeFu franchisee candidates.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Presenting CodeFu franchise procedures and company standards to potential franchisee candidates.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Responding to all requests for additional information regarding CodeFu system to potential franchisee candidates.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Examining and studying all potential franchisee candidate credit and background information and recommend action to Approval Committee.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Coordinating closing preparation with franchisee candidates and CodeFu legal department.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Under the guidance of senior executives, negotiating key elements of the agreement, including at times pricing and other key financial terms, as required.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Serving as the key market functional expert and as the go-to resource for franchise and licensing questions and issues in a market.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Leading marketing ideas and initiatives for assigned geographies/territories.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Representing CodeFu at industry functions, presenting the CodeFu franchise value proposition and positively representing the brand and team.We require:</li></ul>
<p><b>Required Experience, Skills, &amp; Credentials: </b></p>
<ul><li>A BS/BA or equivalent and relevant experience in sales of franchises for personal services or in sales of complex business services. Franchise Development/Sales Experience or close equivalent is required.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Experience in a multi-unit franchise environment with 200 or more locations is desirable.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Experience achieving and maintaining high levels of results in a retail environment is required. * Multi-year success in consultative or franchise sales and/or business development.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Ability to travel up to 50% of the time within assigned region. Experience in generating leads using technology/web-based strategies.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Goal-oriented sales professional with discipline to work independently Demonstrated success in implementing new strategies and procedures.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Ability to analyze, assess, and recommend business development needs, such as new franchise sales or franchise marketing tools.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Strong relationship-development skills.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Strong influencing skills with senior management.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Ability to close deals/sales. Success in cross-functional/matrixed implementation.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Professional written and verbal communication.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Experience in a multi-line/site/state global franchise business experience is preferred.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Must demonstrate a high-level of accommodation and flexibility to work in a fast-paced environment of constant change; able to perform well under pressure and deadlines</li></ul>
<ul><li>The ideal candidate will be well organized and confident to work independently but also a strong team player</li></ul>
<ul><li>Demonstrated ability to effectively communicate with all levels of the organization</li></ul>
