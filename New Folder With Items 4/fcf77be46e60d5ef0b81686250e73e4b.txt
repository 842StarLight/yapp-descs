<h2>Client Solutions Associate</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>HMI Technical Solutions, LLC, (HMITS)</b> provides results-driven engineering, design and integrated solutions to the power, oil &amp; gas distribution, and communications industries. HMITS’ best-in-class services include design, asset integrity management, feasibility studies, surveys, constructability reviews, and turnkey delivery services within these core utility segments. With a genesis in construction and a skilled team of multidiscipline engineers and project management professionals, the privately-held firm brings a deep understanding of infrastructure to every project.</p>
<div></div>
<br>
<div><b>
Position Summary</b></div>
<br>
<p></p>
<div>The Client Solutions Associate will contribute to the planning and execution process for identifying and serving new clients for utility (power, oil &amp; gas distribution, asset integrity management, communications) engineering and construction services. This position is responsible for market penetration through direct client development activities, market branding activities and understanding industry trends aimed at advancing HMI Technical Solutions’ power, oil &amp; gas distribution, asset integrity management and communications offerings within the utility infrastructure markets. Additionally, this position is responsible for providing various levels of marketing and proposal support to HMI Technical Solutions to maximize business development activities within the utilities market.<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<div>This is an excellent opportunity for an entry level or mid-career professional looking to expand their business development skills as a member of a high-performance team experiencing significant growth in the utility infrastructure engineering and construction market.</div>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Position Primary Outcomes</b></p>
<ul>
<li>New Business Lead Generation / Strategic Account Management through personal contact and key industry involvement</li>
<li>Brand Management; through industry branding, industry networking, industry communications, industry association membership, etc.</li>
</ul>
<div><b>Major Job Elements</b></div>
<p>Targeted Market / Business Development and Relationship Generation:</p>
<ul>
<li>In conjunction with senior management and department leadership, actively support the development of specific business opportunities</li>
<li>Develops key relationships directly with current customers, potential customers or through network affiliations</li>
<li>Closing new business, including assisting in contract or alliance partner negotiations.</li>
<li>Gains, fosters, and maintains proactive relationships with key national and regional accounts. Assists local management with relationship development of local accounts.
</li>
<li>Participates in major proposal and presentation efforts for regional and national accounts.</li>
<li>Utilizes Salesforce.com as the company’s primary business development tracking platform and insures customer sales activity is updated and reported on a monthly basis</li>
<li>Executes account plans that align the organization on execution tactics for strategic accounts</li>
</ul>
<p>Market and Competitive Research:</p>
<ul>
<li>Helps identify market trends through research in support of business development activities</li>
</ul>
<ul><li>Participates with Executive Management in the development of short, medium and long term business plans<br>
</li></ul>
<p><b>Specific Skills</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Leadership: a demonstrated ability to lead people and get results through others.
</li>
<li>Planning: an ability to think ahead and plan over a 3-6 month time span.</li>
<li>Strong analytical and analytical interpretative skills
</li>
<li>Time Management: the ability to organize and manage multiple priorities.
</li>
<li>Problem analysis and problem resolution at both a strategic and functional level.
</li>
<li>Strong customer orientation</li>
<li>Excellent interpersonal and communication skills including presentation skills.
</li>
<li>High performance teams and a strong team player.
</li>
<li>Ability to deliver support in a decentralized environment</li>
<li>Commitment to company values.
</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Qualifications</b></p>
<p>Education:</p>
<ul><li>BA / BS / MBA in Business Administration, Marketing, Engineering or related field</li></ul>
<p>General Experience:</p>
<ul>
<li>A minimum of 3 years’ experience and a demonstrated track record of accomplishments in utility or construction industry (engineering, products / services sales, or marketing).
</li>
<li>Must work effectively with Senior-level executives, leaders and the staff of internal operating regions.
</li>
<li>Broad functional experience in areas of strategic planning and marketing, business and market development, market research and planning and promotions/advertising.</li>
</ul>
