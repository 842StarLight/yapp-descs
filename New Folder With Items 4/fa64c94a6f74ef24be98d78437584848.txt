<h2>Senior Account Executive, Austin, TX</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">LotLinx, the leading automotive AI-powered digital marketing tool, is experiencing tremendous growth and has an exciting opportunity for an Senior Account Executive, Austin, TX. Role details
<br>The Account Executive is responsible for generating sales with new dealerships, increasing market penetration, and contributing revenue. They will work with client launch teams, sales development, account management, and LotLinx business partners.
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Uncover dealer pain points/sales opportunities</li>
<li>Align LotLinx’s solution to the dealer’s business needs</li>
<li>Build consensus on a proposal and secure an agreement to work together</li>
<li>Ensure dealer activation and smoothly transition the relationship to account management</li>
<li>Build and work a sales pipeline</li>
<li>Meet activity and revenue targets</li>
<li>Additional projects as needed</li>
</ul>
