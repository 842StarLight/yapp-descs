<h2>REGIONAL FABRICATION PROJECT MANAGER</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div><div><div><div><div>
<p><b>KloecknerMetals </b>has an immediate job opening for a Regional FabricationProject Manager at our American Fabricators facility in Nashville, TN.
</p>
<p>Job work location is 570 Metroplex Drive, Nashville, TN 37211</p>
<p><b>
Summary</b></p>
<p>
Incumbent in this role will be responsible for compiling and buildingquotations for fabricated parts, and managing current projects includinginventory management, order entry, forecasting review, and pricing accuracy</p>
<p><b>
Jobresponsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li> Assist in building quotations for fabricated parts reviewing customerspecifications and drawings</li>
<li> Participate in drawing and manufacturing feasibility reviews</li>
<li> Detailed follow up required with supply base and customers to keepprojects progressing on compressed timelines</li>
<li> Daily sales management of current projects including inventorymanagement, order entry, forecasting review, pricing accuracy, etc.</li>
<li> Project management for new launches on awarded fabrication business and keeping all projectsupon agreed upon timing</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Jobrequirements</b></p>
<ul>
<li> Some travel to customers and fabrication partners to support projectlaunch, part buyoff, tooling and equipment review – typically 20% of time andmostly local</li>
<li> Excellent communication skills, above average MS office suite knowledgeand computer skills</li>
<li> Experience with quoting software is a plus</li>
<li> College degree preferred or suitable combination of years of experienceand education</li>
<li> Sheet metal fabrication or stamping experience required</li>
<li> Lean manufacturing training preferred</li>
<li> Project management skills preferred</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<ul><li> Ability to read and understand prints is preferred</li></ul>
