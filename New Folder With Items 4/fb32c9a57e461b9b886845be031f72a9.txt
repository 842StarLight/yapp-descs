<h2>Associate Contract Administrator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">An Associate Contract Administrator is responsible for the administration of contracts and agreements between Industrial Scientific, and our distributors, re-sellers and customers. This position resides within the Americas Sales Team and the role of those in the Contract Administration Team is assisting the outside sales team with the closure of sales.
<br><br>
<b>Duties and Responsibilities:</b><br>
Preparing, examining, analyzing, negotiating, and revising contracts that involve the purchase or sale of goods or services such as equipment, services, supplies, and/or software
<br>Work side-by- side with sales to drive deals to closure and ensure execution of agreement
<br>Work in capacity of project manager upon execution and through implementation of contract
<br>Interface directly with customer counterparts to track deliverables, monitor contractual obligations, and prepare documentation
<br>Review and revision of draft contractual agreements and additional documentation
<br>Assure accuracy and appropriateness of contract documentation and attachments; set-up new contracts; terminate expired contracts; and general maintenance of active contracts
<br>Receive, review and accurately enter customer data into internal systems
<br>Prepare and distribute information to appropriate employees regarding contract status
<br>Ad hoc project work as needed
<br><br>
<b>Required Qualifications:</b><br>
BS/BA in Business, Finance, or related field
<br>Experience in customer service or sales
<br>Excellent communication skills both orally and in writing; strong analytical skills; strong interpersonal skills
<br>Demonstrated success working in a teaming environment both internally and externally
<br>Proficiency in MS Excel, MS Word, MS PowerPoint, Adobe Acrobat as well as Salesforce.com and Oracle ERP systems
<br><br>
<b>Preferred Qualifications:</b><br>
Gas detection experience (sales, customer service, or technical support)
<br>Experience working with distribution
