<h2>Sr. Specialist, Project Planning (Bilingual Japanese)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Overview
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>Responsible for assistinig in and supporting large and complex projects for Canon Solutions America. Acts as a liaison between project teams and Canon Solutions America management to keep management informed of project status and any 'red flags' that may require management attention.
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities
</b></h2>
<div><div><ul>
<li>Supports project planning by establishing clear ownership for project tasks.</li>
<li>Tracks progress and reviews project tasks to attain project goals.</li>
<li>Prepares timely project progress updates, including Project phases/milestones. Proactively communicates project status to Canon Solutions America management. Provides management with ongoing and realistic assessments of project status, potential issues and red flags.</li>
<li>Utilizes change control processes as needed. Collects and documents lessons learned.</li>
<li>Utilizes company systems to ensure weekly status reports are continuously in view.</li>
<li>Coordinates and facilitate communication of project objectives to stakeholders by holding regular status meetings.</li>
<li>Leads both internal and external projects.</li>
<li>Assists in managing the day-to-day operational and tactical aspects of all technology and innovation projects.</li>
<li>Responsible for the accuracy of all Project documentation and deliverables.</li>
<li>Manages implementation lifecycle.</li>
<li>Utilizes pre-existing systems to enhance project deliverables</li>
<li>Develops procedures for implementation of systems as needed.</li>
<li>Adheres to departmental policies, procedures.
</li>
</ul></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Qualifications
</b></h2>
<div><div><ul>
<li>Bachelor's degree in a relevant field, or equivalent experience</li>
<li>5+ years related experience</li>
<li>Must be bilingual (Japanese/English)</li>
<li>Advanced MS Office Suite, Visio and project</li>
<li>Must be fluent in Japanese and understand Japanese business culture</li>
<li>Experience in coordinating and facilitating large IT projects
</li>
</ul></div></div>
