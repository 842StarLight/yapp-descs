<p><b>
Job Description:</b></p>
<p>
UNMB is looking for a team-oriented individual with a graphic design background to join our Marketing Team! The Graphic Designer is an essential team member of the marketing group.</p>
<p>
The Graphic Designer will help cultivate the creativity and brand image of UNMB. The Graphic Designer supports multiple external and internal campaigns and is responsible for creating all printed materials, advertisements, logos, signage and online graphics. This position offers exposure to multiple channels used to market to the general public as well as staff recruitment. Candidates must have an enthusiastic attitude, bring a creative approach to the job and provide a portfolio of previous work upon request.</p>
<p><b>
Position Responsibilities:</b></p>
<p><b>
Web Content &amp; Design</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Design impactful graphics for UNMB’s three websites (including banners and landing pages), social media and e-mail campaigns.</li>
<li>
Photograph and produce video for web and print.</li>
<li>
Manage and design trade show graphics including printed promotional materials.</li>
<li>
Graphically promote and evolve our brand image in a legally compliant manner consistent with customer needs.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Marketing Support</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Assist Marketing Manager with annual advertising production schedule and trade show schedule.</li>
<li>
Help develop and create effective presentations for Sales and Executive members.</li>
<li>
Support Sales with marketing materials as their needs change.</li>
<li>
Support corporate needs including signage, logos, graphics for interdepartmental projects and event photography.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Skills/ Requirements</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Bachelor’s degree in Graphic Design</li>
<li>
Working knowledge of Adobe Creative Collection (predominantly Illustrator/Photoshop/InDesign/Acrobat/Flash).</li>
<li>
Exposure / Experience with HTML and Wordpress.</li>
<li>
Strong creative design, conceptual planning, the ability to follow art direction and collaborate on layouts.</li>
<li>
Print Design and Web Design.</li>
<li>
Proven ability to multi-task and interface with all levels of management.</li>
</ul>
