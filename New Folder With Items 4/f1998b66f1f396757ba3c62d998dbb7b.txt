<h2>Director, Advance Analytics (East)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">Komodo Health is transforming healthcare predictive analytics with its own proprietary data cloud and streaming data analytics platform. Our real-time healthcare intelligence engine provides healthcare business leaders the ability to modernize and improve the way they engage and serve the medical community, and the result is a more transparent, efficient and productive healthcare ecosystem, ultimately leading to reduced patient disease burden.
<br><br>
Our Life Sciences Advanced Analytics practice seeks experienced and entrepreneurial leaders who are ready to lead and grow an Advanced Analytics team. This is an excellent opportunity for accomplished Life Sciences leaders to have massive impact at a fast-growing technology company.
<br><br>
The Director, Advanced Analytics, will work with Komodo Health customers to ensure successful delivery of Komodo analytics services, leading to customer value and attainment of their desired business outcomes. The role requires comfort interfacing with a diverse set of Life Sciences customers ranging from Commercial (Marketing, Sales, and Analytics), Medical Affairs, Clinical Operations, Market Access, and Real-World Evidence, and an ability to engage, communicate, consult, and partner effectively.
<br><br>
At Komodo Health, we value our culture of encouraging growth, collaboration, and constructive debate as well as delivering innovative solutions and services that "wow" our customers. Komodo Health is backed by leading technology investors on the East and West Coasts, and is partnered with leading academic institutions, technology leaders and life sciences thought leaders.

Internal Collaboration and General Leadership
