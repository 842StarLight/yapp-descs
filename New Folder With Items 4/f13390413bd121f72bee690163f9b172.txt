<h2>AP Clark</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Hamilton-Ryker, we are seeking to hire a talented Accounts Payable Clerk, if you’re a hard working professional and looking for additional income. This is the opportunity you’ve been looking for:<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p>The AP provides management with financial data for use in maintaining accounting and statistical records by performing the following duties. This position is a Part-time - job average 24 hours/per week, pay $15.50 Only qualified applicants will be contacted to schedule an interview</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Responsibilities and Duties:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Process invoices by ensuring all invoices are stamped, matched with receiving paperwork and purchased order where needed, coded with proper GL number, and approved by appropriate individual and CFO, and enters invoice data into accounting software.</li>
<li>Generates and submits Trial payment report to CFO for approval of check run</li>
<li>Generates, print and mails checks approved for payment to vendors and files paid invoice</li>
<li>Investigate and missing invoices, unapplied payments, credits or unusual activity on vendor account</li>
<li>Maintains accounts payable files through the year, boxes and stores files at year end, and creates files for new year</li>
<li>Calculates sales and use tax payables for the state of Kentucky on a monthly basis and prepares related quarterly sales and use tax returns.
</li>
<li>Prepares daily bank activity reports, including borrowing base reports.
</li>
<li>Maintain insurance certificates, titles, and tax information on all company vehicles</li>
<li>Special projects as assigned by the CFO</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Qualifications:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Must be 18 years of age and over</li>
<li>Must comply with Hamilton-Ryker substance abuse policy</li>
<li>High degree of accuracy and attention to detail</li>
<li>Ability to read, analyze and interpreter general business periodicals, professional journal, technical procedures and manual.
</li>
<li>Must have BA college or University/and three years related experience or equivalent combination of education and experience.<br>
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>

</b></p>

</ul>
