<div><b>Purpose<br>
</b></div>
<div></div>
<div>Qualified individuals will have a IT and/or Telecommunications technology background and have a background or interest in developing commercial acumen. These individuals will be responsible for the management and ownership of a BID process for SITA Small-Medium size opportunities including but not limited to: validation and selection of appropriate solution technical design including assembly of bills of materials and services, validation and selection of the appropriate solution delivery approach, effort and time schedule, validation of solution operations support model, commercial modeling, , management of internal approval processes, supporting of Account Managers during customer presentations and contract negotiation.</div>
<div></div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<div><b>THE ROLE</b></div>
<div>
<br>
<br>
You will increase customer satisfaction, delivering value to customers, through timely, professional, and compelling proposals for products and services in the travel and transportation industry. By doing so, you will help us retain and grow our existing customer base, in addition to transforming new prospects into customers. You will be the prime interface for sales and customers on commercial and technical matters.</div>
<div>
<br>
<br>
</div>
<div>You will be the primary contributor and owner of the assembly of small to medium size bids, bridging technical, professional services, and commercial components. You will work closely with internal groups to build and approve solutions, as well as ensuring their successful transition into delivery and operations. You will validate, select, scrutinize, assemble, review, and seek approvals for various components of the bid as part of small orders, unsolicited proposals, and RFP responses.<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>You are a self driven individual who will be responsible and take accountability. You will learn quickly on your own and on the job, with support of other team members. You will garner strong air transport industry knowledge, customer business processes, general information technology trends and advancements, as well as internal product knowledge. You will establish and maintain a good working relationship with internal and external customers, and engage with both, to obtain a better understanding of their requirements and preferences in order to tailor and deliver a technical solution, within their risk tolerance and price thresholds.</div>
