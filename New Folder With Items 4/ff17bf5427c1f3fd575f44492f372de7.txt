<p>Responsibilities:</p>
<p>As a Digital Account Executive you will partner with small to medium sized business clients to create effective Digital Marketing programs that bring increased revenues to their business. You will be accountable for achieving and exceeding sales objectives which are a result of selling our diversified Digital programs.</p>

Additional responsibilities:</i></b></p>
<ul>
<li>Prospect for new business to increase client base</li>
<li>Build and maintain client base through renewal, increased and new business opportunities</li>
<li>Explain Digital Marketing concepts to clients and the benefits of how our products work better together in order to help our clients find success</li>
<li>Demonstrate and present to clients using the latest version of the iPad Pro with a proven successful sales presentation, approach and solutions packages</li>
