<h2>Contract Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>POSITION OVERVIEW: </b></p>
<p>The purpose of this classification is to perform technical accounting and clerical duties with the Clerk's agency. Duties include auditing and processing accounting documents; entering, updating and retrieving data in the automated financial systems; testing financial system upgrades or updates; preparing accounting and third party documents; and maintaining accounting records.</p>
<p><b>ESSENTIAL JOB FUNCTIONS: </b></p>
<p><b>The following duties are normal for this position. The omission of specific statements of the duties does not exclude them from the classification if the work is similar, related, or a logical assignment for this classification. Other duties may be required and assigned.</b></p>
<p>1. Physically show up for work and provide services as outlined in Job Description.</p>
<p>2. The Contract Specialist will audit and process accounts payable and payment application documents (e.g. invoices, credit memos, statements) to ensure items:</p>
<ul>
<li>are matched between the purchase order, invoice, and receiving document from the ordering department and supporting packing slip (if available) for procured items</li>
<li>are supported by the required documentation and authorizations for direct payments</li>
<li>have been received and properly authorized by the ordering departments</li>
<li>properly reflect authorized change orders or extensions of time within the parameters of the original contract and the purchasing policy.</li>
<li>contain all required evidence and certifications of completion prior to final payment</li>
<li>conform with the guidelines of AIA phased payment application and certificate of payment</li>
<li>conform with the contractual or other authorized payment terms</li>
<li>are paid with the correct payment method accompanied by requested remittance advices or confirmations</li>
<li>comply with Florida statutes, County policies and generally accepted accounting principles</li>
<li>are accurate regarding dates, information, pricing, discounts, mathematical calculations, previous balances, change orders or other adjustments</li>
<li>have not already been paid by AP or with a purchasing card</li>
<li>are consistent with the previous levels of activity for that expenditure, or documented if otherwise</li>
<li>are complete and contain all needed authorized documentation, including grant, Form 1099 and other reporting requirements</li>
<li>are paid for a valid public purpose</li>
<li>have been screened for proper classification as capital assets, if applicable</li>
<li>reflect records of legible, complete images for all associated documents</li>
<li>are free of information that would need to be redacted for privacy laws, with any hard copies of confidential support items being filed according to policy</li>
<li>have been allocated, if necessary, under consistent, reasonable guidelines</li>
<li>reflect reconciled outstanding balances</li>
</ul>
<p>3. Acts as a liaison or Subject Matter Expert (SME) for system testing and enhancements.</p>
<p>4. Prepare periodic reconciliations of outstanding contracts and retention.</p>
<p>5. Compiles data for reports and/or records.Researches information in files upon request.</p>
<p>6. Performs general clerical tasks in support of assigned functions such as assisting with mailings; review of checks and inclusion of remittance advices as needed; maintaining office supplies; preparing correspondence, letters and notices to departments, outside agencies, vendors, etc.</p>
<p>7. Provides customer service and/or assistance to vendors, contractors, outside agencies, fellow employees, and the general public. Provides information and explains policies and procedures.</p>
<p>8. Operates a personal computer, telephones, copiers and other general office equipment as necessary to complete essential functions, to include the use of word processing, spreadsheet, database, or other system software.</p>
<p><b>EDUCATION AND EXPERIENCE: </b></p>
<p>Requires an Associate's Degree in accounting, business or a related field; supplemented by at least three years of fiscal experience; or any equivalent combination of education, training, and experience which provides the requisite knowledge, skills, and abilities for this job. Additional relevant certifications or degree preferred. Fingerprinting is required.</p>
