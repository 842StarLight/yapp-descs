<p>
Vocera has one mission: to simplify and improve the lives of healthcare professionals, patients, and families. Founded in 2000, we changed the way care teams communicate. Today we continue to offer the leading platform for clinical communication and workflow. About 1,400 hospitals and health systems around the world use Vocera. Our platform provides software for voice, secure text messaging, and patient engagement. It integrates with more than 140 clinical and operational systems. Our wide choice of devices includes smartphones and hands-free wearables. Vocera is headquartered in San Jose, Calif., with offices in San Francisco, Indiana, Canada, India, United Arab Emirates, and the United Kingdom. Learn more at www.vocera.com, and follow @VoceraComm on Twitter</p>
<p><b>
Position Description: </b>Vocera is currently looking for a leader to join our Product Management team. In this role, you will own the overarching strategy and execution of our analytics products. This includes not only how our customers can drive faster actions and decisions with better, more relevant insights but also how the platform can be used internally at Vocera to help us build better products for our customers.</p>
<p>
You should be a data-driven individual who is always curious about “what the data says” and how they can drive improvements based on insights gathered from data.</p>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Responsibilities</b></h3>
<ul>
<li>
You will lead Vocera’s efforts to help customers deliver better patient experience and outcomes by using insights gained from the data</li>
<li>
Collaborate with product teams on how data will be generated, collected, and consumed</li>
<li>
Translate requirements between data scientists, engineers, designers, marketers, and other product managers</li>
<li>
Translate user challenges and needs into actionable data points/metrics to help identify opportunities.</li>
<li>
Be the voice of the customer by connecting with customers regularly to understand their challenges</li>
<li>
You will stay current and be responsible to the organization on market trends that will impact product success, including but not limited to legislation, regulatory requirements, reimbursement</li>
<li>
Work with the team to deliver effective launch plans and key messaging and collateral to the sales team and customers</li>
</ul>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Competencies</b></h3>
<ul>
<li>
Shown experience building analytics solutions from back end to visual display</li>
<li>
Experience turning data sources / sets into analytics/reports that customers value</li>
<li>
Excellent problem solving and strong analytical abilities</li>
<li>
Proven experience working with dynamic cross-functional teams including engineering, sales, operations, customer service, and senior management</li>
<li>
Passionate and customer focused, comfortable meeting and speaking with customers at the executive level</li>
<li>
Strong communication, written, verbal, organizational skills and like helping people have those “lightbulb” moments</li>
</ul>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Requirements</b></h3>
<ul>
<li>
Bachelor’s degree in Mathematics, Statistics, Computer Science, Computer Engineering, or equivalent, MS a plus</li>
<li>
8+ years of experience in a customer-facing product management or equivalent role</li>
<li>
3+ years’ experience leading and managing a team</li>
<li>
Consistent track record of executing the launch of platform products, plus solid understanding in data science, heuristics, machine/deep learning frameworks and technologies</li>
<li>
Successful background growing and developing your team, building and mentoring product managers</li>
<li>
Experience in healthcare operational metrics and data desired</li>
<li>
Approximately 10% - 20% travel is required</li>
</ul>
