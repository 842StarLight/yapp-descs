<h2>MAINTENANCE SUPERVISOR - AWESOME TEAM!</h2>

<p><b>Premium Properties &amp; Development</b> (www.premiumpd.com), established in 2002, is a full service property management and real estate investment, sales &amp; development firm. Currently, we manage over 1,000 units (mostly residential) over 65 building all in the Berkeley/Oakland/Albany area for our clients, investors and ourselves. Our properties are mostly high-end market rate apartments, though are also subject to rent control. Our mission is to always provide Absolutely Premium service to our customers (residents and tenants) and clients. Each member of our staff plays an integral role in fulfilling this goal.</p>
<p>
We are currently seeking an experienced<b> full time Maintenance Supervisor.</b> The person in this position will be responsible for all maintenance aspects of our portfolio and will supervise their team of resident managers, vendors, and contractors, as well as our in-house full-time Maintenance Coordinator/Administrative Assistant. While our main priorities are the safety of our residents and health of our properties, you will be expected to help implement policies and procedures to speed our maintenance turnaround, and reduce costs, without compromising quality. This position reports to the Director of Operations and the two partners.</p>
