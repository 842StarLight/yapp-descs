<h2>Water Restoration Project Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>OVERVIEW
</p>
<div><div><div>
<div>Roto-Rooter Services Company is the name that home owners and businesses have turned to for over 80 years for their plumbing and drain cleaning troubles. As we have continued to evolve and grow, we have added a new division to meet even more of our customers needs. Water Cleanup &amp; Restoration is that division and it's growing quickly. Because of that growth, we are adding additional team members including a Water Restoration Project Manager.</div>
</div></div></div>
<p>RESPONSIBILITIES
</p>
<div><div><div>
<div>As a Water Restoration Project Manager, your responsibilities will include:</div>
<ul>
<li>
Interact with customers, develop scope of work and work plan, monitor progress</li>
<li>
Service as primary contact for the customer</li>
<li>
Ensures customer satisfaction</li>
<li>
Prepares/reviews estimates, acts as liaison with insurance companies, and interacts with other company personnel on collections</li>
<li>
Ensures that technicians perform work in keeping with IICRC and RRSC guidelines</li>
<li>
Performs water restoration sales and technician functions when needed</li>
<li>
Ensures all documentation is complete</li>
<li>
Manage and supervise all water restoration crews</li>
</ul>
</div></div></div>
<p>REQUIREMENTS
</p>
<div><div><div>
<ul>
<li>Knowledge of water Restoration industry and practices and insurance reimbursement for water mitigation claims</li>
<li>
Strong customer service focus and able to communicate effectively with customers</li>
<li>
Able to work in a fast paced environment</li>
<li>
Fluent using Xactimate software</li>
</ul>
</div></div></div>
