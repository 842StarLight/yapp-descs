<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>About the job</b></h2>
<p>
Businesses that partner with Google come in all shapes, sizes and market caps, and no one Google advertising solution works for all. Your knowledge of online media combined with your communication skills and analytical abilities shapes how new and existing business grow. Using your influencing and relationship-building skills, you provide Google-caliber client service, research and market analysis. You anticipate how decisions are made, persistently explore and uncover the business needs of Google's key clients and understand how our range of product offerings can grow their business. Working with them, you set the vision and the strategy for how their advertising can reach thousands of users.</p>
<p>The Services and Distribution sales sector (Technology, Business Industrials, Dating, Careers, Home Services, etc.) is paving the way across Large Customer Sales in creating thought leadership narratives, publications and maximizing our Directors point of view. All of the content the external communications lead will create will be rooted in third-party research and insights.</p>
<p>
Our Large Customer Sales teams partner closely with many of the world’s biggest advertisers and agencies to develop digital solutions that build businesses and brands. We enjoy a bird’s eye view on the massive transformation occurring as advertising shifts to mobile and online platforms. We're uniquely situated to help shape how companies grow their businesses in the digital age. We advise clients on Google's broad range of products across search, video and mobile to help them connect instantly and seamlessly with their audiences.</p>

<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Responsibilities</b></h2>
<ul>
<li>Assist in the scope and overarching plan for how to organize against positioning our Industry Directors as thought leaders.</li>
<li>Seek out stories relevant to our Directors expertise and find story angles through which Google can provide meaningful insight.</li>
<li>Work closely with PR, Marketing, and Legal teams to ensure initiatives follow Google Communications Guidelines.</li>
<li>Create an amplification strategy/playbook for Directors and their teams they run (e.g., one sheeters based on research, webinar series for teams and their clients, etc.) and continually look for ways to measure our thought leadership/PR strategy to improve performance and results.</li>
<li>Draft and edit documents such as blog posts and quotes for articles. Optimize the prep doc process for Industry Directors.</li>
</ul>
