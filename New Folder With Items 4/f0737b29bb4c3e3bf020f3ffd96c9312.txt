<h2>Full-Time Center Associate</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>The Center Associate delivers world-class customer service to all retail customers by receiving and processing packages for courier shipment and operating copiers, fax machines, binding equipment, laminating machines, and point-of-sale devices. The Associate expertly advises our valued customers by providing accurate information on our array of products, services, and best-value options and exhibits confidence by knowing best practices related to the industry.<br>
<br>
The ideal candidate has previous retail sales experience, strong computer and internet skills, a high school diploma or GED, a friendly and genuinely helpful demeanor, and a professional appearance. He or she must be a fast learner who makes an effort to learn all aspects of the business in the shortest possible time.<br>
<br>
All applicants must pass a criminal background check and become a California Notary Public and a Certified Fingerprinting (LiveScan) technician within the first 60 days of employment.<br>
<br>
<b>Persons with previous experience in high-end/luxurious retail, hospitality and print shops (FedEx office, Staples) are highly encouraged to apply.</b><br>
</p>
