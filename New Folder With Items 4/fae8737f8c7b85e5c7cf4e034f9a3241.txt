<h2>Senior Manager, DVD & Blu-ray Production</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p><b>SUMMARY</b><br>
Oversees all production aspects related to the creation of DVD &amp; Blu-ray products for distribution. Leads a team of producers working with various internal teams and external partners to meet company revenue and budget goals.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
PRINCIPAL DUTIES</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Provides management and guidance to producers. Determines product timelines by coordinating with internal teams and external partners including manufacturing, marketing, sales, planning, approvals, design, and localization.</li>
<li>
Works with SVP of Animation to establish annual product budgets and timelines to meet company goals.</li>
<li>
Manages vendor relationships, negotiates pricing, budget, and timeline expectations.</li>
<li>
Works with marketing and sales teams on product specs, build, special features, extras, packaging, pricing, as well as other production elements.</li>
<li>
Utilize project management software (WRIKE) to effectively manage staff workload, product planning, and timelines.</li>
<li>
Manage product P&amp;L working with SVP of Animation to ensure all product costs fall in line with company margin goals.</li>
<li>
Manage process and pipeline to ensure best practices and maximize efficiency.</li>
<li>
Manage administrative processes – title set up, invoicing, vendor, freelance, and independent contractor set up.</li>
<li>
Manage the “special features” process and create for use on disc.</li>
<li>
Represents DVD &amp; Blu-ray Department during relevant meetings.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p>
In addition to these typical responsibilities, performs other activities as necessary.</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>
MINIMUM ABILITY, SKILL AND KNOWLEDGE REQUIREMENTS</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Background with DVD &amp; Blu-ray and familiarity with video formats A MUST</li>
<li>
Knowledge of video/audio, broadcast, encoding, codec, or other media practices, terminology, materials, technology, and deliverables A MUST</li>
<li>
Self-starter, ability to solve complex problems, excellent project management skills, highly organized, a strong communicator and extremely detail-oriented A MUST</li>
<li>
Proven track record working with and managing 3rd party vendors A MUST</li>
<li>
Experience with post-production editing software – Final Cut Pro, AVID, Premiere, &amp; Pro-tools PREFERRED</li>
<li>
Familiarity with Japanese anime and manga A PLUS</li>
<li>
Ability to speak and write effectively and interact with culturally diverse clients and staff</li>
<li>
Experience using WRIKE project management software A PLUS</li>
<li>
Ability to effectively supervise others and to adhere to all employment regulations</li>
<li>
Ability to work effectively in a small organization with an emphasis on teamwork</li>
<li>
Ability to work with minimal supervision</li>
<li>
Ability to interact with culturally diverse clients, service providers and staff</li>
<li>
Ability to work legally in the United States</li>
<li>
Ability to live in/commute to the SF Bay Area</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>
MINIMUM PREVIOUS EXPERIENCE, TRAINING, AND EDUCATION</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
5-7 years of experience in development and management of interactive entertainment</li>
<li>
Strong background with DVD &amp; Blu-ray, TV production, video games, and/or mass-market animation production</li>
<li>
Equivalent of a Bachelor's Degree from an accredited college or university, or equivalent, with a record that demonstrates suitable preparation for this position</li>
</ul>
<p><br>
<b>
SUPERVISORY RESPONSIBILITIES</b></p>
<p>
This position supervises members of the Production staff. This position coaches the work of others and requires the ability to effectively supervise staff and to adhere to all applicable federal, state, and organizational employment regulations.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
WORKING ENVIRONMENT/MINIMUM PHYSICAL REQUIREMENTS</b></p>
<p>
This position works predominantly in an office environment. Approximately 75% of time spent on the job involves use of a video display terminal; therefore, must be able to sit for extended periods and to utilize standard ergonomics practices. Must be able to use standard office equipment such as copiers, faxes, mailing machinery, in addition to required video equipment.</p>
<p>
May require periodic airline travel, both domestically and internationally, to attend conferences and meetings. Ability to carry small amounts of consumer material on business trips necessary.</p>
<p>
As part of a multi-media entertainment company, employees may be subjected to work-related images including content such as: sexually explicit images, nudity, and graphic violence. Employees should be willing to work with this type of content. VIZ Media does not condone the viewing of this content where not work-related.</p>
<p></p>
<h4 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
OTHER INFORMATION</b></h4>
<p>
Employees must adhere to all applicable VIZ Media policies and procedures. Regular and predictable attendance is required. Willingness and ability to work non-standard business hours, such as nights, on an as-needed basis, is required. Employees must be able to cope effectively with deadlines and multiple demands.</p>
