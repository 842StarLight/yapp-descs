<h2>Marine Technician (Experience Preferred)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><i>General Summary</i><br>Responsible for performing maintenance and repair work on marine equipment (boat, motor, trailer, accessories). Engines serviced include outboards, inboard-outboards (I/O's), and trolling motors. Qualified candidate will have an understanding of the internal combustion engine, mechanical systems, fuel systems, electrical systems, drive systems, and diagnostic tooling.</p>
<p><i>Duties and Responsibilities</i></p>
<ul>
<li>Perform seasonal (winterizing) and preventative maintenance (oil changes, tune ups, water pumps)</li>
<li>Evaluate and repair all components of marine units (boat, motor, trailer, accessories)</li>
<li>Prep new and used units for sale (rigging) including installation of accessories</li>
<li>Installation and testing of accessories on customer units</li>
<li>Maintain updated knowledge of product and industry trends and methods</li>
<li>Attend manufacturer sponsored technical training and workshops</li>
<li>Maintain a safe and clean work environment</li>
<li>Maintain care and cleanliness on all customer property</li>
<li>Maintain care and cleanliness of all marina equipment and tools</li>
<li>Maintain accurate records both paper and electronic, related to repair orders including hours worked, and parts used on repair</li>
<li>Assist other areas of marina as needed (hauling boats to/from area lakes, moving boats throughout marina property, look up of technical parts, answering customer technical questions)</li>
</ul>
<p><i>Required Knowledge, Skills, and Abilities</i></p>
<ul>
<li>Able to utilize and comprehend technical service manuals</li>
<li>Physical ability to maneuver in, on and around units being serviced</li>
<li>Physical ability to maneuver in, on and around marina equipment</li>
<li>Able to safely operate stationary and portable power tools</li>
<li>Able to safely operate customer property here for repair and on open water when lake testing is required</li>
<li>Able to use computer equipment for diagnostic evaluation, part lookup, and internal marine software system</li>
</ul>
<p><i>Education &amp; Experience</i></p>
<ul>
<li>Prior mechanical training or background</li>
<li>Basic computer skills</li>
<li>
<i>Desired but not required</i> Technical training on any of the following engine brands: Yamaha, Mercury, Mercruiser, Volvo, Honda, Evinrude, Minnkota</li>
</ul>
<p><i>Salary/Wage</i></p>
<ul>
<li>Salary/Wage based on experience</li>
<li>Worked holiday's are paid at time and a half (Memorial Day, 4th of July, Labor Day) and holiday hours are typically reduced to a 4 hour day</li>
<li>Salary/Wages are reviewed on an annual basis</li>
</ul>
