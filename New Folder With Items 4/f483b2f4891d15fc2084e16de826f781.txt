<h2>Salesforce Architect</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>AST is seeking a Salesforce Architect, who is clever, curious and energized by solving problems and improving our customers' lives. You'll collaborate with a talented and supportive team to continuously deliver new and exciting innovations to our clients.</p>
<p><b>
Why Join Us? </b>AST is a global technology brand with a long history of creating innovative solutions as a systems integrator. Our team has the outstanding ability to motivate, inspire, and collaborate, delivering ground-breaking results to our clients. At AST, we're lifelong learners, who tackle challenges together and win together. If you are a smart, driven person, who thrives on taking technology to the next level, you've come to the right place.</p>
<p><b>
What You'll Do:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Provide hands-on technical support in sales calls, solution presentations, and assist the Client Executive with understanding customer requirements</li>
<li>
Take joint ownership of the assigned accounts/opportunities with the Client Executive on technical issues.</li>
<li>
Provide input for RFI and RFP responses, including the effort estimate, project team, timeline, implementation methodology and solution components</li>
<li>
Lead and facilitate discovery and scoping sessions with clients</li>
<li>
Anticipate customer needs and identify appropriate solution alternatives.</li>
<li>
Incorporate past experience, business savvy, creative and out-of-the-box thinking in designing solutions; offer multiple, potential solutions</li>
<li>
Create and defend solution estimate and Statement Of Work</li>
<li>
Coordinate with Certified Technical Architect, integration, and data consultants, and others, as needed, for specific technical design requirements</li>
<li>
Provide recommendations on third-party applications</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
What You Bring:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
BS in Computer Science, Software engineering, MIS or Information Technology is preferable.</li>
<li>
Minimum of 8-10 years of consulting experience delivering complex solutions, particularly cloud-based solutions including but not limited to Salesforce</li>
<li>
Ideally three Salesforce Certifications including Sales Cloud, Service Cloud and Advanced Admin 301 or Developer 401</li>
<li>
Prior experience with sales processes including architecting and estimating solutions.</li>
<li>
Ability to understand and explain related technical issues, and skills related to working and resolving problems in customer accounts.</li>
<li>
Candidate must demonstrate excellent verbal and written communication skills. They will spend a good part of their time in verbal one-on-one and one-to-many situations discussing a business and/or technical approach to solving a customer issue.</li>
<li>
Ability to coordinate and lead technical resources to architect a complete business and technical solution during the sales process.</li>
<li>
Ability to travel to clients as needed, because it's part of the gig.</li>
<li>
Local to the Chicagoland area.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
What We Bring:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
You'll work alongside gifted professionals—the best in the business—and they love to collaborate and share ideas!</li>
<li>
No matter what your role, you have the opportunity to shape the company's future. If you have an awesome idea, say so!</li>
<li>
Competitive salary, BCBS benefits (medical, vision, dental), 401K, and all that great stuff! You can even insure your pets!</li>
<li>
Access to AST Academy, so you can sharpen your skills in tech and management.</li>
<li>
Awesome company events, think Top Golf and casino nights!</li>
<li>
Room to grow. We want our team to thrive in their careers at AST. You invest your time and talent with us and we'll invest in you.</li>
</ul>
