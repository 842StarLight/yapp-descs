<p>Commercial Sales<br>Full Time Position<br>Salary / Draw against commission</p>
<p>POSITION OVERVIEW<br>Are you looking to “upgrade” your career?</p>
<p>Norman Carpet is now accepting applications from experienced professionals to join our commercial sales team. Our success has been built upon quality flooring and installation, an excellent reputation, market presence and most importantly a team of in-house installers and support personnel dedicated to customer service. We are the largest in-stock flooring retailer in the Delaware Valley.</p>
<p>Commercial sales is a highly competitive business – you must be a self motivated and disciplined individual who wants to earn a minimum of $100,000 a year.</p>
<p>BASIC DUTIES<br>Canvas/cold call new accounts.<br>Handle job site visits.<br>Attend sales meetings.<br>Maintain existing accounts.<br>Follow up and follow through with customers.<br>Work cooperatively with the installation, administration and accounting departments.</p>
<p>WHY WORK FOR NORMAN CARPET?<br>Training/coaching by our top salesperson.<br>Excellent reputation in the community.<br>Repeat business from loyal customers.<br>The highest quality flooring.<br>Selection – carpet, area rugs, wood, resilient, ceramic.<br>Unbeatable pricing.<br>The best installation team.<br>Original and creative advertising campaigns.<br>Management team committed to your success.<br>Norman, the owner. A great person to work for.</p>
<p>REQUIREMENTS</p>
<ul>
<li>A demonstratable track record for success.</li>
<li>Verifiable references.</li>
<li>Excellent computer skills -- MS Office, database management, etc.</li>
<li>Able to read plans and take off measurements to bid new jobs.</li>
<li>Existing client base a plus.</li>
<li>Professional dress and demeanor.</li>
<li>Able to pass a drug screening and background check.</li>
<li>Selling to building trade or construction experience helpful.</li>
</ul>
<p>COMPENSATION<br>Expect to make $100,000 depending on experience and work ethic. Benefits include paid vacation, Personal Choice health insurance (with participation).</p>
<p>CONTACT US<br>To arrange for a confidential interview, email your resume with cover letter (MS Word) explaining how your skills and experience are a match for our company. All qualified resumes will be given the attention it deserves and will be personally reviewed by the sales manager.</p>
<p>Visit our web site at www.normancarpet.net</p>
