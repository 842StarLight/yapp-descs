<h2>Senior Client Executive Acquisition</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p><b>What you’ll be doing...</b>
</p>
<p>Summary</p>
<p>
Verizon Enterprise Solutions is looking for a passionate, articulate and energetic Acquisition Account Executive to bring in new logos and drive incremental new opportunities and revenue within a defined geographic based territory consisting of high growth accounts. This is a true hunter role for a proven sales leader that has strong prospecting and relationship building skills. The Acquisition Account Executive will develop account team credibility and trust-based client relationships by understanding our customers external drivers, business objectives and internal challenges in order to develop a comprehensive solution consisting of Verizon’s World-Class assets in IP-Networking, Cloud, Security, Mobility, Professional Services and Advanced Communications.</p>
<p>
Responsibilities:
</p>
<ul>
<li>Identify and develop C-level executive relationships with newly identified customers and within assigned account portfolio</li>
<li>
Develop strategic solutions based on industry trending and customer analysis, which includes preparing executive profiles, and continuous study of assigned accounts’ industry, business challenges and trends.</li>
<li>
Prepare accurate sales forecasts, account status reports, and recommendations to enhance account growth and new revenue potential</li>
<li>
Participate in contract negotiations.</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>
Articulate client business value with consultative selling approaches</li>
<li>
Develop strategic solution proposals and recommendation to present directly to your clients</li>
<li>
Work in a dynamic environment with a proven ability to balance competing demands and priorities as well as operate independently while building a successful sales pipeline/channel</li>
<li>
Team with colleagues and channel partners to develop creative solutions</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
What we’re looking for...</b>
</p>
<p>Qualifications
</p>
<p>Bachelor's degree or equivalent directly related work experience required</p>
<p>
3-5 years proven industry experience with quantifiable successful sales track record</p>
<p>
Strong relationship building skills, presentation skills and negotiation skills</p>
<p>
Strong interpersonal and communications skills coupled with the ability to act independently and as part of a large team</p>
<p>
The preferred candidate must have demonstrated in previous roles, strong leadership skills and an ability to operate in positions requiring significant self-direction and motivation.</p>
<p>
Ability to understand and demonstrate effective negotiation techniques and then apply them in most situations. Is focused on achieving mutually beneficial, "win-win" results and is alert to customer buying signals and identifies and acts on closing opportunities.</p>
<p>
Creates and maintains detailed and accurate Account/Oppty plans and reviews/updates and communicates these on a regular basis.</p>
<p>
Work in a highly ambiguous, dynamic environment with a proven ability to balance competing demands and priorities as well as operate independently while building a successful sales pipeline/channel</p>
<p>
Excellent communication skills, able to interact with C-Level executives, polished and professional, ability to work effectively on their own and work well under tight deadlines. Able to effectively work on multiple projects at a time. Must be organized. Must be able to identify short and long term goals to achieve overall team/company objectives.</p>
<p>
Experience with Saleforce.com or CRM equivalent Forecasting and Customer Relationship Management</p>
<p></p>
<p>
In addition:</p>
<ul><li>
Willingness to travel.</li></ul>
<p>
Valid driver’s license</p>
