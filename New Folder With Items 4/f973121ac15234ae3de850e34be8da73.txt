<b>Cost Specialist</b>
<p><i>Kalmar keeps global trade moving. Our innovative solutions contribute to every fourth container movement in the world and help transform the future of cargo handling. We believe in collaboration, delivering on our promises and that people make the difference.
</i></p>
<b>Making your next move count</b>
<p>Kalmar is looking for Cost Specialist who will be primarily responsible for cost accounting and general accounting activities, including monthly reports, weekly reports, expense reporting, asset tracking, inventory, and providing relevant and reliable financial information to management.</p>
<b>
Main tasks and responsibilities</b>
<ul>
<li>Planning and collecting data to determine costs of business activity</li>
<li>Analyzing costs of raw materials, etc. and preparing reports</li>
<li>Ensure accurate recording of financial transactions, posting within the general ledger.</li>
<li>Reconcile accounts and resolve anomalies within accounts of the general ledger.</li>
<li>Analyze financial data and communicate with management.</li>
<li>Assist with month-end closing and back-up to Controller for all closing duties.</li>
<li>Accurately analyze and maintain the returned goods to vendor inventory account and make adjusting journal entries at month end.</li>
<li>Analyze all after shipment adjustments for accuracy and completeness and to report to Top Management the cost of returning customer units to the organization.</li>
<li>Audit daily Cost of Shipments report and complete necessary corrections including the development of standard unit costs for all un-costed items and the completion of journal entries necessary to correct applicable General Ledger accounts.</li>
<li>Investigate all sales invoices for inter-company and intra-company orders and make journal entries to transfer sales and cost of sales amounts to the appropriate accounts.</li>
<li>Participate in the Cost Accounting month end closing and make appropriate journal entries as necessary.</li>
<li>Assist in the yearly standard setting cycle for purchase and expensed parts</li>
<li>Monitor Cycle Counting of physical inventory.</li>
<li>Monitor the scrap reporting system and prepare monthly scrap reports and related journal entries.</li>
<li>Provide an accurate accounting of the Returned Goods Program to Management.</li>
<li>Provide an accurate analysis of after shipment invoice to ascertain cost of customer returned units.</li>
<li>Provide proper coding to new parts so they are reported in the proper general ledger accounts.</li>
<li>Investigate and review all purchased and expensed parts when setting standards for next fiscal year to ensure that policies are adhered to.</li>
<li>Analyze daily and monthly Cost of Shipments reports and complete necessary corrections including the development of standard unit costs for all un-costed items and the completion of journal entries necessary to correct applicable General Ledger accounts.</li>
<li>Analyze on a daily basis bill of materials and routings for all new parts to ensure accuracy so that products are properly costed.</li>
<li>Monitor and work with CBS team to timely clear items from GRNI.</li>
<li>Back-up to Controller for all closing duties.</li>
</ul>
