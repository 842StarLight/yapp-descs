<h2>Project Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Project Manager</b></p>
<p><b>Covetrus - Portland, ME</b></p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Covetrus</b> is a global animal-health technology and services company dedicated to empowering veterinary practice partners to drive improved health and financial outcomes. We’re bringing together products, services, and technology into a single platform that connects our customers to the solutions and insights they need to work best. Our passion for the well-being of animals and those who care for them drives us to advance the world of veterinary medicine. Covetrus is headquartered in Portland, Maine, with more than 5,000 employees, serving over 100,000 customers around the globe.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>SUMMARY</b><br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p>The <b>Project Manager</b> will work with on a variety projects across the business.? We expect that you will be adept at communication - providing management and other stakeholders with timely information about progress, issues, and risks.? The key to your success will be the ability to act as steward for your projects and ensure that the interactions and dependencies between multiple teams projects is understood and well managed.??<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>ESSENTIAL DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES</b><b> </b><b>include the following. Other duties may be assigned.</b><b>
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Partner with others to identify opportunities and prioritize project activities based on pre-defined criteria.
</li>
<li>Manages activities to resolve problems in a timely and accurate fashion ensuring effective communication to leadership and stakeholders.
</li>
<li>Responsible for developing detailed project charters and plans, specifying goals, objectives, strategy, scope, budget, scheduling, risk mitigation strategies, contingency plans, approach, requirements, deliverables, timelines, user story maps, test planning and execution, and training needs, as necessary for all project assignments.
</li>
<li>Identifies, tracks and manages project risks and issues. Serves as escalation point to resolve issues, remove roadblocks, formulate contingency plans and communicate status to programs executive leadership team.
</li>
<li>Functions as central point of project communication and status for team members, stakeholders, management and executives. Communications are professional, timely, and clear and present the correct level of information based on audience.
</li>
<li>Supports Product Engineering and other Enterprise integrated planning and reporting requests.
</li>
</ul>
