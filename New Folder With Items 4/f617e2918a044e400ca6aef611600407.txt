<h2>Client Integration Hardware Analyst #3034</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<div><b>Position Summary
</b></div>
<p>The Client Integration Hardware Analyst performs analysis and other tasks necessary for design, integration, maintenance and support of client hardware systems. This team responds to and proactively assesses the evolving needs of the business and designs client hardware solutions to fit. The Hardware Integration Services Team owns the proof of concept, plus full implementation/integration of new technology and hardware systems throughout all Costco businesses. We are active in the development of enterprise level strategies, cost and return analysis, and technology refresh initiatives.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<div><b>
Job Duties/Essential Functions</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Designs and develops new client hardware technology solutions to meet business needs.</li>
<li>Partners with Operations, IS and Vendors to create solutions and solve business problems.</li>
<li>Tests new client hardware or applications on existing infrastructure.</li>
<li>Researches, analyzes and recommends changes required for growth.</li>
<li>Recommends and supports security management practices, as well as following established security standards.</li>
<li>Understands and adheres to Costco’s project methodology and framework.</li>
<li>Understands and adheres to Costco’s Mission Statement and Code of Ethics.</li>
<li>Helps formulate project schedules and provides detailed status reports of tasks and deliverables.</li>
<li>Documents processes and project details as well as provide second level support documentation.</li>
<li>Vendor relationship management, working closely on product road map, and hardware availability.</li>
<li>Lab coordination, focusing on keeping space current and orderly.</li>
<li>Stays current on retail industry and technology directions as well as all areas of Costco business.</li>
<li>Regular and reliable workplace attendance at your assigned location.<br>
</li>
</ul>
