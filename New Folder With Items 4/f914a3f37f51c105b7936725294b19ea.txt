<h2>Marketing Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<b>Description:</b><br>
The Marketing Manager is an exciting opportunity for someone looking to help launch the Karoun Marketing department. This role will allow you to work on variety of tasks from creative projects to providing analytical insights. This role will assist in organizing and controlling Karoun Dairies product line(s) from conceptual stages through product life cycles to optimize profit and meet marketing, financial, and corporate growth objectives by performing the following duties.
<br><br>
Essential Duties and Responsibilities include the following. Other duties may be assigned
<br>1. Utilize syndicated and other sources of data to develop expertise on the product, category, consumer, customers and competitive set
<br>2. Manage in house and external Demo activity (manage the full demo cycle from scheduling to providing analysis)
<br>3. Responsible for Ethnic Market survey project
<br>4. Identify consumer trends to be leveraged in new products and line extensions
<br>5. Participate in building methodology and evaluating results from key research projects
<br>6. Lead creation and execution of digital marketing strategy for key brands
<br>7. Develops and implements integrated marketing campaigns and oversees creation of marketing collateral including, but not limited to: brochures, digital assets, print ads, videos, photographs, presentations, and direct mail. Assists in the production of promotional videos.
<br>8. Participates in creating brand awareness through advertising and social media
<br>9. Organizes and participates in customer events, including trade shows
<br>10. Provide contributions to the business strategy to move business ahead and achieve high level objectives/metrics; demonstrates an understanding of brand strategy and the competitive landscape in which the brand operates, including key competitors, general market conditions such as milk pricing, the dynamic retail environment, etc. This would include “real time” understanding of what is driving our products and category trends in the market place.
<br>11. This description reflects management’s assignment of essential functions, it does not proscribe or restrict the tasks that may be assigned.
<br><br>
<b>Requirements:</b><br>
Education
<br><ul>
<li>Ideally incumbent will possess a minimum of a Bachelor’s Degree (ideally in Marketing, Management, or related field)</li>
</ul>
Experience
<br><ul>
<li>5+ years of experience in a marketing role</li>
<li>Experience in a consumer food marketing position or food experience</li>
<li>Experience with social media campaigns, website creation and management, demo management, ad campaigns, and sales/marketing analysis.</li>
</ul>
Specialized Knowledge
<br><ul>
<li>Familiar with the foods from various cultures (ideally Mediterranean, South Asian, and Hispanic).</li>
</ul>
Skills/Abilities
<br><ul>
<li>Must have the ability to manage multiple projects simultaneously.</li>
<li>Ability to write reports, business correspondence.</li>
<li>Candidate needs to possess strong communication and analytical skills; must be able to coordinate a number of activities internally with a variety of departments and functions.</li>
<li>Needs to have significant computer skills and background.</li>
<li>Interest in foreign cultures and in food as part of a lifestyle, cheese amateur.</li>
</ul>
