<b>Company Overview</b><br>
<b>
</b><br>
Our Client is a leading developer of osteobiologic products designed for posterolateral spine<br>
fusion. Founded in 2009, they are led by a team of scientists and industry veterans<br>
committed to developing unique products for bone regeneration. The company designs,<br>
manufactures and distributes all of its products from its Irvine facilities in the heart of<br>
Southern California’s “Technology Coast”.<br>
</p>
<p><br>
<br>
<b>Position Summary</b><br>
<b>
</b><br>
Responsible for providing leadership and management of the Company’s marketing
<br>
organization and in-bound and out-bound marketing activities, which include: corporate
<br>
marketing, partner/channel marketing, product marketing, and product management. The<br>
Senior Marketing Manager will drive the Company’s efforts to position itself as a visionary
<br>
leader and expand potential in its emerging market to achieve its revenue goals.<br>

<br>
