<h2>Network Administrator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>DH Pace Company, Inc. is seeking to hire a <b>Network Administrator</b> in our Olathe, KS office to implement and support the company’s IT Network infrastructure. You will perform general network administration tasks per project timelines as required.</p>
<p><b>
Job responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Daily monitor and support IT infrastructure including, but not limited to, general server support, administration, and management for Microsoft Active Directory environment. As well as a wide range of products (SQL Server, Oracle, Cisco, Dell/EMC, Aruba Wireless, VMware, and VoIP).</li>
<li>Daily maintain systems administration and network systems support at our various sites in accordance with company best practices and industry standards.</li>
<li>Support of our ERP system, including software updates and Oracle database administration tasks.</li>
<li>Server configuration and administration skills required.</li>
<li>VoIP configuration, setup and general administration.</li>
<li>Serve as Level II support for both local and remote users.</li>
<li>Analyze and resolve Server, Desktop and General Network issues timely and accurately.</li>
<li>Manage and maintain 3rd party relationships with IT vendors.</li>
<li>Assist with network security; policy and procedure creation and execution, product review and implementation.</li>
<li>Support management of security defense products.</li>
<li>Maintain accurate Asset Management for all divisions.</li>
<li>Assist in implementing new products and technologies to ensure success.</li>
<li>Monitor and ensure compliance with company standards and policies as needed.</li>
<li>Perform additional network administration tasks as defined and requested by IT management.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Qualifications:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree preferred</li>
<li>Excellent communication and customer relations skills and high sense of urgency with exceptional problem solving skills</li>
<li>Ability to multi-task and work efficiently in a fast-paced work environment</li>
<br>
</ul>
