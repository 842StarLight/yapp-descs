<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Your Opportunity
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div>Schwab Asset Management Solutions (SAMS), the asset management arm of Charles Schwab Corporation, has approximately $400 Billion in assets under management (AUM) and is one of the nation’s largest retail money managers. CSIM provides a wide variety of products ranging from money market funds, index mutual funds, actively managed mutual funds, exchange traded funds and multi-asset fund-of-funds. As a provider of core investment products, CSIM is both an important enabler in fulfilling Schwab’s mission of helping everyone be financially fit and a critical component of the company’s profitability and future growth. This is a <b>Data Architect</b> role based in Denver, CO, and member of the technology team that provides solutions to the Sales, Marketing &amp; Product Strategy business units.</div>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>What you’re good at
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div>The role will lead our team’s data practice as a data architect, which includes facilitating data modeling sessions with business and technical users, reviewing source system layouts and schemas, analyzing the business and technical requirements and creating data models to meet our client’s business needs. This individual must be a strong data architect and be focused on assessing our client’s environment and requirements and design the Data architecture in conformance to best practices and support our software development lifecycle. The design responsibilities will involve conceptual, logical and physical data modeling, with an emphasis on the Kimball Dimensional Modeling methodology.</div>
<ul>
<li>
Facilitate client assessment engagements to gather requirements, points of pain, gaps and other business and technical input that will support recommendations and a future state Data Architecture</li>
<li>
Lead activities around data analysis, data profiling, data extraction (validation and transformation rules) and integration with data from multiple disparate sources into a robust and scalable data model. Provide means for ensuring data quality and integrity. This position will work closely with clients and end-users to examine existing processes and develop automated solutions, including specifying program and data flow</li>
<li>
Own this data model and work with architects across our broader technology group to adhere to the established guidelines and principles established as best practice activities</li>
<li>
Provide Data architecture and Business Intelligence subject matter expertise and leadership.</li>
<li>
Ensure common application architectural framework across different subject areas and conformance with best practices. Proven experience in designing and deploying conformed dimensions</li>
<li>
Consult with key individuals across multiple projects regarding the usage and application of Business Intelligence and Data Warehousing architectural standards</li>
</ul>
