<h2>Senior Manager Tax</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<b>Job Summary</b><br>
<br>
The Senior Manager of Tax will lead sales/use tax compliance and taxability team ensuring accuracy and timeliness in the preparation and filing of monthly tax returns and related G/L accounts, as well as the taxability of all products sold in 8,500 store locations and online. Establishes vision and leads implementation of automating the sales/use function to focus resources on data analytics to mitigate risk and increase accuracy of tax return reporting and taxability of products and services. Ensures expertise is provided to advise business teams and other tax teams in identifying tax impact to transactions and operations. Ensures development of team members’ tax technical and business acumen skills. Ensures that tax planning initiatives are properly incorporated into the tax compliance processes. Authority to sign returns, reports, filings and other communications on behalf of the Company under penalties of perjury, as necessary. Serves as a leader on cross functional team(s), providing leadership, direction, knowledge and advice to team members and senior management. Develops departmental plans and organizational priorities; is accountable for the performance and results of tax stewardship. Addresses technical issues and recommends solutions. Develops and executes our tax strategy through innovative &amp; creative thinking in a manner which reduces our overall tax cost/tax burden to the Company.<br>

Walgreens Boots Alliance is the first global pharmacy-led, health and wellbeing enterprise. Our purpose is to help people across the world lead healthier and happier lives.<br>
Walgreens Boots Alliance was created through the combination of Walgreens and Alliance Boots in December 2014. This transaction brought together two leading companies with iconic brands, complementary geographic footprints, shared values and a heritage of trusted healthcare services through pharmaceutical wholesaling and community pharmacy care, dating back more than 100 years.<br>
<br>
Walgreens Boots Alliance is the largest retail pharmacy, health and daily living destination across the USA and Europe. Walgreens Boots Alliance and the companies in which it has equity method investments together have a presence in more than 25* countries and employ more than 400,000* people. The company is a global leader in pharmacy-led, health and wellbeing retail and, together with the companies in which it has equity method investments, has over 13,200* stores in 11* countries as well as one of the largest global pharmaceutical wholesale and distribution networks, with over 390* distribution centers delivering to more than 230,000** pharmacies, doctors, health centers and hospitals each year in more than 20* countries. In addition, Walgreens Boots Alliance is one of the world’s largest purchasers of prescription drugs and many other health and wellbeing products.<br>
