<h2>Purchasing Agent</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Purchasing Agent</b></p>
<p><b>Description</b></p>
<p>Reports to the Contracts Liason. Responsible for planning and executing the most cost-effective procurement from external suppliers of products and services necessary for operation, in compliance with government and management directives. The candidate will be responsible for identifying, analyzing and resolving purchasing issues (e.g. shipping discrepancies, product not meeting specified characteristics, pricing discrepancies, etc.). They will identify and evaluate sources of supply and prepare purchase orders for final review before submission to vendors. Candidate will create and track all purchase orders thru JobBoss software.</p>
<p><b>Essential Functions: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Review RFQ’s to determine what vendors are to be contacted for price quotations.</li>
<li>Review all assigned PO’s for raw materia or hardware purchases, and place orders thru appropriate vendors.</li>
<li>Ensure vendor adherence to quality standards, required delivery schedules and contractual terms.</li>
<li>Conduct follow‑up on purchase orders past due with appropriate vendor.</li>
<li>Maintain vendor contacts and vendor supply lists.</li>
<li>Coordinate with accounting department relative to accuracy of pricing data.</li>
<li>Ensures accurate and effective bidding with all vendors.</li>
<li>Maintain a safe and clean work area that models high standards for a work environment.</li>
<li>Assist in the development and implementation of new and revised policies and procedures affecting procurement.</li>
<li>Coordinate with other staff pertaining to procurement matters.</li>
<li>Provide positive, quality customer services to customers.</li>
</ul>
<p>It is expected that the incumbent shall perform other duties as assigned within his/her capabilities as determined by management.</p>
<p><b>Competencies / Skills: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Solid working knowledge of purchasing policies and procedures</li>
<li>Effective written &amp; oral communication skills with established interpersonal skills</li>
<li>Proficient experience in the appropriate range of Office Suite programs such as Word, Excel, Project, PowerPoint and JobBoss or a similar system</li>
<li>Strong time management skills and be deadline oriented</li>
<li>Demonstrate the ability to handle multiple tasks, changing priorities</li>
<li>Experience in analyzing data and providing recommendations</li>
</ul>
