<h2>Graphic Design & Multimedia Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<b>Description:</b><br>
This person will support all marketing efforts for each of the Innovance companies. This person will work closely with the Marketing Specialist and General Managers of each company and be responsible for directly executing the various design and technical aspects of each companies marketing plan. The role requires an individual capable of managing multiple on-going projects and campaigns, attention to detail, outstanding communication skills, and technical expertise. Skills and experience in graphic design for print and online, WordPress management, HTML and social media are required. This position reports to the Director of Sales &amp; Marketing.
<br><br>
<b>JOB FUNCTIONS:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Work with internal customers with Innovance subsidiaries to develop and execute effective marketing projects that lead to profitable sales.</li>
<li>Creation of promotional print, video and online marketing collateral. You will be the main creative force in creating a variety of content for each of the Innovance companies.</li>
<li>Skill set in working with the Adobe suite of products; InDesign, Photoshop, Illustrator.</li>
<li>Skill set in web site development and back end management of WordPress. Content editing for six existing company websites.</li>
<li>Occasionally work with outside marketing agencies and contractors as needed.</li>
<li>Ability to work effectively managing time and shifting priorities to meet deadlines.</li>
<li>Able to help create and execute Social Media activities with a focus on LinkedIn. This could include content creation, posting content, advertising and re-marketing.</li>
<li>Coordinate marketing efforts within the Microsoft Dynamics 365 CRM system as needed.</li>
<li>Travel on occasion to all Innovance companies, and 2-4 trade shows per year.</li>
<li>Reports regularly to the Director of Sales &amp; Marketing. .</li>
</ul>
<b>Requirements:</b><br>
<b>The ideal candidate will have the following:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>2-year or 4-year degree in Graphic Design and/or Multimedia Arts.</li>
<li>Portfolio of relevant creative content that matches the job description.</li>
<li>Experience working in an advertising or PR agency or other large company with multiple concurrent projects is a plus.</li>
<li>Excellent interpersonal and communication skills</li>
<li>Technology skills including, MS Outlook, MS Word, MS Excel, MS PowerPoint, MS CRM and website content management systems like WordPress.</li>
<li>A strong ability to set and prioritize goals.</li>
<li>Ability to multi-task within a team environment.</li>
<li>Travel as needed to the Innovance companies, and on occasion trade shows in support of the various companies.</li>
</ul>
