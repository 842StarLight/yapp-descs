<h2>Systems Support Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>SUMMARY</b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
The Systems Support Specialist performs skilled and specialized work, with a primary focus on providing assistance to users in the areas of desktop computing and distributed computing. The Systems Support Specialist identifies, tracks, and resolves technical problems with software and hardware, and plans and implements corrective and proactive solutions. The Systems Support Specialist position requires flexibility, and the ability to master quickly new concepts, procedures, hardware and software.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
ESSENTIAL DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES </b>include the following:</p>

</ul>
<div>
<ul>
<li>Logging, addressing, and tracking (through resolution) support requests</li>
<li>Proactively troubleshooting and resolving problems both at the user and system levels</li>
<li>Coordinating with other department members and vendors to obtain problem resolution</li>
<li>Providing courteous, responsive, and effective user support</li>
<br>
</ul>
