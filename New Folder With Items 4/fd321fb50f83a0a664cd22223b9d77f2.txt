<h2>Director of Product</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Director of Product
</b></p>
<p><b>The Marcus Buckingham Company (TMBC)</b>, powered by ADP, turns <b>talent into performance</b> through innovative solutions designed <b>specifically for team leaders and their team members</b>. Grounded in decades of research on what drives engagement and performance in teams, our technology, coaching and education offerings help companies build more teams like their best teams.</p>
<p><b>
Position Description</b></p>
<p>
At <b>TMBC</b> we <b>fuel engagement. </b>We <b>accelerate performance</b>, leader by leader, team by team<b>.</b> Why? Because we are passionate about transforming people and organizations through Strengths. We believe that, in order to be your best, you need to start with a strength-based approach. And that is why we're ridiculously excited to wake up and go to work every day.</p>
<p>
Do you want to be part of something amazing? The Product Team is looking for a <b>Director of Product.</b></p>
<p>
We are seeking a <b>Director of Product </b>to lead the way in developing and maintaining StandOut, a cloud-based application designed to drive team leader effectiveness. You will lead the team responsible for existing and new functionality that effectively support the core of our product. The <b>Director of Product </b>will own the vision and execution of the functionality that supports the rituals of the best team leaders. We're looking for an experienced product leader who is a self-directed creative thinker, and driven to contribute to TMBC's reputation, growth and long-term success. You are knowledgeable on Agile methodologies and best practices.</p>
<p>
We are headquartered in Beverly Hills, and the <b>Director of Product</b> role must work onsite due to the highly collaborative and iterative nature of the work.</p>
<p><b><i>
Do the following activities make you feel STRONG?</i></b></p>
<ul>
<li>Developing and executing on a product strategy that anticipates the needs of users, clients, and the market and is aligned with business goals and priorities, proactively helping achieve our company vision</li>
<li>Making data-driven decisions about the product - and strategically delivering decisions and implications to internal stakeholders and clients</li>
<li>Providing daily strategic guidance, management, and leadership to a team of dynamo Product Owners, ensuring that all scrum teams are working effectively and in a way that drives the overall product vision forward</li>
<li>Leading a team of designers to ensure the user experience is world class</li>
<li>Collaborating closely with Engineering to ensure that all technical standards are met in the development of the product and that security, scalability, and data architecture are always top of mind</li>
</ul>
<ul><li>Collaborating with marketing, sales and service on the development of product positioning, demo design, training courses, sales collateral, etc. to ensure that business objectives are achieved</li></ul>
<ul>
<li>Teaching stakeholders to 'fall in love with the problem" to make the best product decisions that truly meet the needs of the business, vs. managing intake of feature requests</li>
<li>Being a "ruthless prioritizer" - organizing input/feedback from a range of stakeholders, including clients and users, and translating into product features</li>
<li>Partnering with other product leaders within ADP to make strategic decisions regarding product integration, and driving that work forward</li>
</ul>
<br>
<p></p>
<p></p>
<p><b><i>Is it YOU we're looking for?</i></b></p>
<ul>
<li>Has ability to translate business objectives and unmet customer needs into a clear product vision with rigorous requirements, then support the execution of that vision</li>
<li>Self-starter, able to drive work forward within an agile environment</li>
<li>Outstanding communication and interpersonal skills, including experience working directly with clients and collaborating across internal teams to deliver complex projects</li>
<li>Strong bias for action with a demonstrated track record of owning and delivery products successfully</li>
<li>Dedicated leader of product people with a passion for building intuitive, user-centered products</li>
<ul>
<li>Ability to understand technical concepts / software applications and to use data, metrics, and user research findings to back up assumptions and drive actions</li>
<li>Critical thinker with strong problem-solving skills and a tendency to challenge the status quo</li>
<li>Relentless focus on delivering product enhancements that will drive real, measurable impact to our users</li>
<li>Excellent team management skills and prior product leadership experience</li>
</ul>
</ul>
<p><b><i>Education and Experience</i></b></p>
<ul>
<li>BA/BS required in applicable field with 8+ years' experience in Product Management of a market-facing software or enterprise solution</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Experience with Agile development methodologies, working on scrum team(s)</li>
<li>Familiarity with product localization and proven experience scaling functionality to new markets</li>
<li>Experience leading product teams and collaborating with leaders of Engineering, QA, and Technical Infrastructure functions</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Ideal candidate has experience in talent management or HCM domain</li>
<li>Ideal candidate has experience supporting a growing organization and product</li>
</ul>
