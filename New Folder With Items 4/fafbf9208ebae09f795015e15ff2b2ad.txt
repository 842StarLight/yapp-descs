<p><b>Position Summary
</b></p>
<p></p>
<p>TaxAct is seeking a uniquely innovative and highly motivated <b>Manager, Pricing and Business Analytics </b>to measure operational performance, analyze customer behavior and trends, mine data, synthesize and communicate findings efficiently. Must be able to transform “big data” into financial models to drive pricing, SKU, and Mix recommendations. You must be a talented, passionate analyst with uncanny “big thinking” skills, proven ability to innovate and simplify, and an impressive ability to build bridges and collaborate with other teams and designers.
</p>
<p>At their core, Pricing and Business Analysts are strong in quantitative analysis and modeling. They think critically to tackle complex challenges, thrive in a fast-paced environment and are seeking a high-growth opportunity where they will have an immediate impact on day one. Must be strong communicators who are eager to learn, endlessly curious, and take pride in hard work. These individuals strive to lead the business with their output rather than just inform it.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Essentials Duties &amp; Responsibilities
</b></p>
<p></p>
<p>Major responsibilities of the position are listed below. To perform the job successfully, the individual must be able to execute each essential duty satisfactorily. Reasonable accommodations may be made to enable individuals with disabilities to perform the essential functions.
</p>
<ul>
<li>Designing and implementation of complex budget and forecast financial models that help leadership take action on data.
</li>
<li>Turning data into realistic, achievable financial pricing recommendations and clearly communicating those to the appropriate people, including executive management.
</li>
<li>Working closely with internal customers to understand their information needs and develop analyses that lead to actionable insights to maximize key performance indicators.
</li>
<li>Integrating and cleansing data from multiple sources to create complex views that can be used to drive decision making and financial models.
</li>
<li>Telling a story with data by preparing and presenting executive level summaries of analyses in a concise fashion.
</li>
<li>Working on a team helping to define and develop requested business intelligence deliverables.
</li>
<li>Other job duties as defined.
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Education &amp; Experience
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s Degree in business analytics, accounting, mathematics, statistics or equivalent.
</li>
<li>Minimum of 5-7 years of proven working experience as a data analyst or business data analyst.
</li>
<li>Strong analytical skills with the ability to collect, organize, analyze, and disseminate significant amounts of information.
</li>
<li>Some knowledge of statistics and experience using statistical packages for analyzing datasets (Excel, R, SAS, SPSS, etc.).
</li>
<li>Big data analytics experience is desired.
</li>
<li>Strong proficiency in Microsoft Excel.
</li>
<li>Ability to work independently as well as partner with others and work well within a team.
</li>
<li>Strong organizational and time management skills; ability to adhere to deadlines.
﻿ </li>
</ul>
