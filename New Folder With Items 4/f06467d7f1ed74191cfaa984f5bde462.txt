<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>About the job</b></h2>
<p>
Our Sales Strategy and Operations teams ensure Google’s complex and ever-evolving Ads businesses run smoothly. They design, enable and support key customer segments and sales teams and drive strategic initiatives forward across the globe. Team members are analytical and strategic with a pragmatic sense of how to get things done.</p>
<p>In this role, you'll manage continuous improvement throughout the ads business. You'll combine analytical rigor, thought leadership and operational excellence to drive improvements in incentives, customer segmentation and prioritization, engagement strategy, product go-to-market, and organization design. You will have exposure and visibility to leaders as you present your proposals and demonstrate their impact.</p>
</div>
<div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Responsibilities</b></h2>
<ul>
<li>Structure and execute operational and strategic initiatives by developing work plans, gathering and synthesizing relevant data, leading analyses and developing final recommendations.</li>
<li>Action on analysis and modeling that translates data into actionable insights.</li>
<li>Garner impactful advertising product insights through your deep knowledge of the user, workflows, sales processes, and the strategic initiatives of sales teams.</li>
<li>Work with cross-functional partners to identify opportunities and drive ongoing program improvements and enhancements to completion.</li>
<li>Advise senior executives on strategic topics and deliver insights on business performance, go-to-market plans, growth opportunities, resource allocation, and more.</li>
</ul>
