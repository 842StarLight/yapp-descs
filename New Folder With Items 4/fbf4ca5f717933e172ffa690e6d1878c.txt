<h1 class="jobSectionHeader">JOB DESCRIPTION
</h1>
<div>
<p>KFMB Stations - a TEGNA media company located in San Diego, CA – is home to two (2) radio stations (100.7 San Diego and 760 KFMB-AM) as well as two (2) television stations (CBS 8 and The CW San Diego).</p>
<p>We have openings for part-time/fill-in <b>Promotions Assistants</b> to help the promotions and marketing departments with daily activities to promote KFMB and/or our clients.</p>
<p>If you are an outgoing, fun and energetic person and would like to help serve as a brand ambassador for KFMB, please apply online (no phone calls please).</p>
<p>If you’re eager to incorporate your inspirational leadership, creative, digital and strategic skills to develop and execute “mind blowing” ideas and programs across our various media platforms, then we encourage you to apply! We want a courageous leader who understands the importance of multi-platform audience and revenue growth.</p>
<p><b>Responsibilities include:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Set up/take down and transport equipment to/from events, including banners and other staging elements</li>
<li>Run audio and other type of equipment</li>
<li>Engage with clients and listeners at promotional events in the community</li>
<li>Record events (i.e. photos, videos, audio and social media for station promotions)</li>
<li>Drive promotional vehicles</li>
<li>Assist with station contests and prize fulfillment</li>
<li>Work with the Programming and Sales departments as well as clients</li>
</ul>

<p><b>About TEGNA</b><br>
TEGNA Inc. (NYSE: TGNA) is an innovative media company that serves the greater good of our communities. With 47 television stations and two radio stations in 39 markets, TEGNA delivers relevant content and information to consumers across platforms. It is the largest owner of top 4 affiliates in the top 25 markets, reaching approximately one-third of all television households nationwide. Each month, TEGNA reaches 50 million adults on-air and approximately 35 million across its digital platforms. TEGNA has been consistently honored with the industry’s top awards, including Edward R. Murrow, George Polk, Alfred I. DuPont and Emmy Awards. TEGNA delivers results for advertisers through unparalleled and innovative solutions including OTT local advertising network Premion, centralized marketing resource Hatch, and G/O Digital, a one-stop shop for local businesses to connect with consumers through digital marketing. Across platforms, TEGNA tells empowering stories, conducts impactful investigations and delivers innovative marketing solutions. For more information, visit www.TEGNA.com.</p>
