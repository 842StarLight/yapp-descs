<h2>Sales Development Representative</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div><div>
<p>Ever dreamt of disrupting one of the biggest and fastest growing industries? Wunder Mobility is one of the fastest growing mobility technology companies in Europe, with 150 employees of over 30 nationalities. Our HQ is in Hamburg with further presence in Dortmund.</p>
<p>
We are the only global company that conceptualizes and scales all variations of new mobility services as part of its technology solutions. Our products connect end-to-end services to create an ecosystem of sustainable mobility.</p>
<p>
Join Wunder and together let’s build our team and culture to fulfill our vision of being the best mobility tech company in the world.</p>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
WHAT YOUR ROLE WILL BE</b></h3>
<p>As Sales Development Representative, you have the opportunity to become a key contributor to our remarkable growth story as founding part of our new US office. In this role, you will be at the forefront of our Sales Activities by creating, attracting and qualifying demand for Wunder’s full stack mobility platform. You will connect with a broad range of different companies (from established Automotive OEMs to tech startups) and the public sector to explore how our products can add value to their businesses and their most important stakeholders.</p>
<p>
Thereby, you will generate and develop a high value pipeline with new projects for Wunder. In your window function between Marketing and Sales, you identify high-potential customers and execute engagement strategies to win them for our solutions through all sorts of in- and outbound channels. This is a very entrepreneurial role in which you are asked to actively identify and act on market opportunities in close collaboration with our technical teams, based in Germany.</p>
<p>
To make you an expert on Wunder’s offerings for mobility solutions, we will invite you to our HQ in Hamburg, Germany, for a few weeks onboarding and training at the beginning of your employment.</p>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
WHAT YOU WILL DO</b></h2>
<ul>
<li>Build a best-in-cass Sales Qualification pipeline by executing multi-channel, customer centric campaigns. This entails both inbound lead qualification in close collaboration with Performance Marketing as well as high levels of outbound activity to add prospects to our sales funnel.</li>
<li>Ensure a constant stream of sales qualified leads towards our business development. Collaborate closely with our sales Team to find and win more suitable clients for our products.</li>
<li>Identify high-potential customer segments, research their pain-points and how Wunder solutions can add value to their operations. Establish lists of target companies and collect the data you need to engage them successfully.</li>
<li>Build and refine collateral such as for example Market Intelligence, Case Studies or engagement strategies and share best practices within and across Business Units</li>
<li>Constantly monitor and analyse (y)our sales efforts and proactively improve our targeting, content, communications and processes.</li>
<li>Accurately keep track of all opportunities in our CRM tool. Forecast potential revenue impact of your activities.</li>
</ul>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>WHAT YOU BRING TO THE TABLE</b></h2>
<ul>
<li>Demonstrated success in a customer facing role such as a Sales, Account Management, Customer Service and/or Marketing position</li>
<li>Comfort in maintaining a high level of outbound activity, including starting an initial contact to cold leads</li>
<li>A team-oriented mindset and the ability to work with a wide range of individuals</li>
<li>High levels of sales persistence and work ethic</li>
<li>Comfortable with active change in a startup environment</li>
<li>High EQ, with excellent interpersonal skills and ability to drive consensus with both internal and external stakeholders</li>
<li>Strong references from former employers and business partners and a passion for building relationships</li>
<li>Proficiency in English</li>
</ul>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>WHAT GIVES YOU BONUS POINTS</b></h2>
<ul>
<li>Prior experience at Internet, SaaS, AdTech startups or the mobility space is a major plus</li>
<li>Experience with CRM and BI tools</li>
<li>Experience with market research</li>
<li>Proficiency in German, or other languages</li>
</ul>

<p>...being part of a team that celebrates diversity, supports inclusiveness and encourages individual expression. You will find yourself surrounded by talented people who are passionate about what they do and who feel empowered and trusted to take ownership in their roles. We are growing quickly in a challenging industry, which means we act smart and work at a high speed.</p>
<p>
We are able to do this because of the agile, adaptive, and supportive environment we work in, with an aim to grow with people who share these values and are better than ourselves. As part of Wunder, every day is a new chance for you to make an impact and help us be better than we were yesterday.</p>
