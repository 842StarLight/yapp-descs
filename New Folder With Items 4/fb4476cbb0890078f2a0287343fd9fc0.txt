<h2>Landscape Enhancement Crew Leader</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Here at The Grounds Guys, we abide by the simple values outlined in our company acronym "C.A.R.E.", which are: Customers first, Attitude, Respect, and Enjoy life in the process!</p>
<p>
As Enhancements Crew Leader, you are responsible for leading a crew of 2-3 people. Our clients trust to us to provide quality services and you are key in helping us live out our values every day.</p>
<p>
You are a proactive leader and self-starter who can interact with the public and our employees. You have a strong work ethic and are able to manage time to effectively meet deadlines. You have experience in landscaping and at least two years of supervisory experience, as well as proven communication skills with supervisors, employees, and customers.</p>
<p><b>
Specific Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Train your crew to provide the highest quality service and performance standards in the industry.</li>
<li>Show your crew and educate them on best practices for scope of work and field safety.</li>
<li>Manage projects and services to completion while providing consistent communication to the customer throughout the process.</li>
<li>Maintain consistent communication with sales staff to ensure their vision for the customer is carried out to perfection.</li>
<li>Contribute to company sales through lead generation by utilizing our Box 9 system and provided word of mouth referral.</li>
<li>Maintain a clean and well-stocked work rig to include clean and inspected equipment.</li>
<li>Improve upon current processes to increase efficiency without sacrificing quality.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Job Requirements:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Background in landscape construction industry</li>
<li>At least 2 years supervisory experience</li>
<li>Valid Driver's License with the ability to safely and efficiently maneuver a truck and trailer rig.</li>
<li>Strong written and verbal communication skills</li>
<li>Computer literate, with working knowledge of work processing, business software and spreadsheet applications</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Benefits:</b> Benefits package varies by location</p>

We are actively interviewing for this position - Apply today and our hiring manager will follow up!</b></p>
