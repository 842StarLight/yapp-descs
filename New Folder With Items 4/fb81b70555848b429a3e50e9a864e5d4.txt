<p><b>Job Summary: </b></p>
<p>TheSenior Designer is responsible for conceptualization and implementation of omnichannel design solutions that meet marketing strategies from concept tocompletion. This Designer will need to have experience working in catalog production environment and the ability to establish catalog conventions and design elements as well as up to date retail advertising and signage. The candidate must demonstrate a level of proficiency in design, and software applications, such as Adobe Creative Suite and familiarity with asset management systems. It is essential to have knowledge of printing, photography, illustration, verbal and written communication. This position requires someone who can work well with a team under tight deadlines with minimal supervision exhibiting flexibility in a fast-paced environment. Team member will sit in Littleton, MA.</p>
<p><b>Responsibilities: </b></p>
<p>· Provides and recommends conceptual direction, templates and conventions, for all assigned projects.</p>
<p>· Designs and produces annual and sale catalogs, inserts, retail signage and POP, email and web support.</p>
<p>· Communicates ideas with team member son upcoming catalogs, photo shoots, concepts and projects.</p>
<p>· Processes vendor art, studio art, and location photography.</p>
<p>· Creates and uploads finalized PDF files for each catalog according to the printer’s specifications.</p>
<p>· Approves color Epson proofs to ensure content accuracy prior to press approval.</p>
<p>· Approves dbl online prior to press approval.</p>
<p>· Helps to maintains image resource files and database.</p>
<p>· May attend location shoots providing art direction to capture the concepts that have been created to convey the brand and product message.</p>
<p>· Works with studio photographer to ensure that studio concepts are met through art and styling direction.</p>
<p>· Maintains software knowledge and seeks out new creative materials</p>
<p>· Maybe called upon to go on press check approvals.</p>
<p><b>Qualifications.</b></p>
<p>· 5-7+ years of experience in graphic design.</p>
<p>· Catalog experience required.</p>
<p>· Proven success in fast-paced, business to consumer, retail and catalog business</p>
<p>· Photography direction experience in studio and on location</p>
<p>· Proficiency in: Adobe Creative Suite - which is InDesign,Photoshop, Illustrator, Acrobat</p>
<p>· Equestrian experience strongly preferred</p>
<p>· Adept in presenting complex concepts in a simple, relatable way with the ability to receive and implement feedback openly.</p>
<p>· Understanding of company branding with ability to create concepts, comps, layouts and finalart.</p>
<p>· A knowledge of methods and techniques of graphic design and layout. A clean design esthetic and advanced typography skills.</p>
<p>· High level communication skills and ability to work with a team.</p>
