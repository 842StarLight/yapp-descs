<b>Position:</b><br>
The ABM Manager is a new role at CodeScience and a very welcome addition to the team, reporting to the head of Marketing. Your background will be well-suited to this job if you have 5+ years of account based marketing and solid demand generation skills.
<br><br>
The ABM Manager is responsible for planning, organizing and executing programs that generate new prospects and advance existing opportunities for the sales team within specific, targeted accounts and contacts. They will act as the liaison between marketing and sales teams to develop programs designed to penetrate targeted accounts. An ideal candidate must be strong both on the strategic level and on the tactical/execution level. The ABM manager leads and builds multi-touch marketing plans including both inbound and outbound tactics as well as managing 3rd party vendors to meet pipeline requirements of account segments.
<br><br>
Contract is full-time through December 31, 2019 with potential for hiring to W2.

<b>Duties &amp; Responsibilities:</b><br>
<b>In the role of ABM Manager, you will:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Develop and implement, jointly with sales, pipeline generation and acceleration programs for specific targeted accounts and contacts</li>
<li>Interface with sales to determine sales enablement needs, and work with content team to develop tools, programs, and training</li>
<li>Proactively engage with the Sales team to coordinate, communicate, and optimize the impact of marketing activities</li>
<li>Design &amp; execute 1:1, 1: few, and broad-based marketing initiatives to meet pipeline requirements of varied account segments</li>
<li>Track lead flow to ensure sales is following up on marketing identified acoounts in a timely manner</li>
<li>Work with sales to identify gaps in key personas and contact information within strategic accounts; work with marketing operations to develop data and insights to inform addition of key personas and contacts within accounts</li>
<li>Identify opportunities to improve marketing effectiveness through better audience segmentation, marketing techniques, and processes</li>
<li>Guide and manage the attainment of up-to-date market &amp; account intelligence to decipher the most attractive accounts in their vertical across multiple key measures</li>
<li>Vertical campaign planning, build, execution &amp; measurement with a carefully weighted bias towards the most attractive key accounts in those segments</li>
<li>Build, manage and leverage a database of all key contacts for all target (and existing) accounts</li>
<li>Responsible for all account metrics to show positive engagement, pipeline and revenue growth at the industry and account level</li>
</ul>
