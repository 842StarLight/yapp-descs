<h2>Customer Service Representative - Front Desk & Inside Sales</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">The Customer Service Representative serves an important role in the company, by having direct contact with our customers/clients. The CSR will greet all visitors and customers that come to our facility. Once trained, should be proficient in providing customers with product and service information, along with knowing our capabilities. This individual will assist walk-in customers, helping completing job orders or assisting them with their needs.
<br><br>
Will also answer incoming calls and take detailed and accurate messages. The CSR will process work orders and sales receipts and do cash handling with QuickBooks. Will perform End-of-day closing procedures, which includes cash balance reports.</div>
