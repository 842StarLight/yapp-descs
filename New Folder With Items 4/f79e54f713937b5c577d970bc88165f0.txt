That’s a ton of missed opportunity!</p>

Enter Botify.</b></p>
<p>
We are building the new standard in enterprise search marketing.</p>
<p>
Our unified, data-driven, and user-friendly platform provides companies with the insights they need to impact every stage of the search process from the underlying technical requirements to content optimization, ranking, and real keywords.</p>
<p>
Botify is used by 500+ leading global companies across industries including e-travel (Expedia, Luxury Retreats, Odigeo), e-commerce (Jet.com, Macy’s, Farfetch, Zalando), media (Axel Springer, Condé Nast, Refinery29), marketplace (eBay, RetailMeNot, Monster), and digital agencies (Merkle, iProspect, GroupM).</p>

<p>In late 2018, we raised a Series B round of $20MM and we need more help on our Marketing team! Botify offers a fun, fast paced learning environment with a great culture. We look for people who are curious, team-spirited, ambitious and who want to grow with us.</p>
<p>
Do you have a passion for content marketing, SEO, and making complex concepts simple and accessible? Then we want to hire you for Botify’s Content and Community position!

<p>Who you are:</p>
<ul>
<li>You’re the person your friends turn to when they need help reviewing their writing.
</li>
<li>People often describe you as “meticulous” and “organized.”</li>
<li>You understand the business value of content, and are passionate about using it to connect with key audiences.
</li>
<li>You’ve never been able to tell whether you’re right-brained or left-brained, since you feel equal parts creative and analytical.
</li>
<li>You’ve been described as a great teacher, able to break down complex concepts in a way that feels approachable and understandable to a variety of audiences.
</li>
<li>You love making connections with people and aren’t afraid to hop on a call to chase down a great story.</li>
</ul>
<p>Your experience:</p>
<ul>
<li>You’ve spent at least 3 years in a role where you were responsible for producing content that drove brand awareness, traffic, and lead generation goals.</li>
<li>You have a basic knowledge of how search engines work, and preferably 1-2 years of applied experience in SEO (ex: former SEO Manager)
</li>
<li>You know your way around major social channels, and have an established social presence (professional or personal)
</li>
<li>You’re skilled at both copywriting &amp; long-form article writing
</li>
<li>You’ve worked in a fast-paced tech company before (bonus points if you have B2B SaaS experience!)</li>
<li>You possess a Bachelor's degree (preferred)</li>
</ul>
<p>Sound like you? Great! Then let’s dive into what your day-to-day would look like at Botify.</p>
<p>
What you’ll do:</p>
<p>
This role will report to the Director of Brand Content &amp; Communications, working with them to drive Botify’s content and community efforts.
</p>
<p>There are three main components to this job:
</p>
<ul>
<li>You’ll work on researching, producing, and promoting content for the Botify Blog.
</li>
<li>You’ll produce punchy copy for a variety of marketing materials, such as white papers, sales sheets, and landing pages.
</li>
<li>You’ll use your people skills to grow our audience into an engaged, active community.</li>
</ul>
<p>A typical day in this position might look something like this:
</p>
<ul>
<li>Finalize the draft for the next post on the Botify Blog</li>
<li>Work with a guest contributor to polish their article</li>
<li>Work with the revenue team to produce some ad copy for a new campaign</li>
<li>Come up with an engaging post for our social channels and engage with our audience’s responses</li>
</ul>
