<h2>Valuation Associate</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<b>JOB SUMMARY</b><br>
<br>
Researches and provides market information to senior appraisers as it relates to the appraisal assignment. Assists senior appraiser in the preparation of real estate appraisal reports, including entering data in the property database, taking photos of the subject and comparables, and driving the neighborhood.<br>
<br>
<b>ESSENTIAL DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES</b><br>
<br>
Does preliminary research on a property including verification of comparables, demographics, market area analysis, etc.<br>
<br>
Assists in preparing simple portions of an appraisal report.<br>
<br>
Assists in examining and analyzing income producing properties.<br>
<br>
Researches market area and attempts to determine what may impact the subject's value.<br>
<br>
Assists in making comparison analysis based on sales of similar properties.<br>
<br>
Analyzes past revenue and expense performance<br>
<br>
May be trained to use Argus (cash flow software) as needed.<br>
<br>
Other duties may be assigned<br>
<br>
<b>SUPERVISORY RESPONSIBILITIES</b><br>
<br>
No formal supervisory responsibilities in this position.<br>
<br>
