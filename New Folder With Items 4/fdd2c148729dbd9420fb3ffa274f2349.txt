<div><b>Job Overview</b></div>
<div>Working closely with cross-functional teams of designers, developers, content specialists, customer success representatives and website implementation consultants, you will lead and manage multiple client projects through each stage of the process. As Project Manager, you will manage all aspects and phases of client engagements including kickoff meetings, requirements definition, site mapping, site development and configuration, design review, content publishing, quality testing, site launch. The key to success in this role will be confident and competent leadership of timeline management, setting of client expectations, task coordination and accountability, issue resolution, quality assurance, and proactive communication and reporting to both internal resource teams and external clients.</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div></div>
<div><b>RESPONSIBILITIES &amp; DUTIES:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Clarify and fully understand project goals and objectives as communicated from sales and confirmed by clients.</li>
<li>Facilitate and direct all client meetings and interaction relating to the initial development and completion of the project.</li>
<li>Appropriately set and manage client expectations of project tasks, critical sign-off stages, and milestone schedules.</li>
<li>Consult with client on best practices to use in implementing the new system.</li>
<li>Interact with and coordinate the involvement of cross-functional teams as needed to meet project objectives.</li>
<li>Establish and drive project timeline and desired results.</li>
<li>Ability to handle multiple projects across different product lines. (Website, Mobile App, Reservation System, Accounting/POS System)</li>
<li>Ensure compliance and accountability by internal team.</li>
<li>Identify opportunities for additional sales and forward those to appropriate sales personnel.</li>
<li>Record, track and manage projects through weekly project status reviews.</li>
<li>Construct intuitive site map structures that support client objectives and facilitate easy accessibility and use by site visitors.</li>
<li>Work with client to devise an appropriate cross-marketing plan and ensure that the relevant structures are implemented in the website.</li>
<li>Oversee the collection, organization, formatting and publishing of client content on site, whether through Clubessential or client staff.</li>
<li>Perform rigorous site review and testing prior to site launch to ensure the highest quality appearance, completeness and functional operation.</li>
<li>Utilize the appropriate channels for client and internal communication (phone, email, project management platform,..) to ensure responsiveness and clarity throughout implementation.</li>
<li>Provide near-term client support immediately following launch and facilitate a clear transition to the support team for ongoing support needs.</li>
</ul>

</ul>
