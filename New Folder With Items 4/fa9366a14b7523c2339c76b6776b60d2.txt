<h2>Sales Representative - Health IT / Insurance - Remote/Part Time</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Our small global company (pVerify) is a leader in healthcare eligibility insurance reporting, and is looking for a sales/onboarding person. We've experienced significant growth and need a person for those roles. We are looking for a person with 2-5 years inside sales experience, with knowledge of CRMs. Ideally you will have healthcare insurance experience. We see this role as expanding, if you have marketing, customer support, and/or account management experience this is a huge plus.</p>
<p>This ideal candidate will partner with Account Executives to find, contact, and follow-up with prospective clients. Once they discover the client's needs, they will discuss our product's technological capabilities and business value with the client. The ideal candidate should be able to clearly articulate highly technical concepts to all prospective clients.</p>
<p><b>Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Clearly articulate technical capabilities and give technical demonstrations</li>
<li>Onboard qualified leads onto our software</li>
<li>Provide general technical support to clients</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Qualifications</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Ability to discuss highly technical concepts with prospective leads</li>
<li>Strong verbal, written, and interpersonal skills</li>
<li>CRM experience is a MUST</li>
<li>Sales experience is a MUST</li>
<li>Medical billing / verification is a huge plus</li>
<li>Able to handle multiple streams of communication (phone, web chat, email, skype)</li>
</ul>
<p>This is a 100% remote position but ideally will be situated near Durham, NC or Austin, TX. This is a contract position due to our strong growth but could convert to perm. We are looking for someone with at least 20 hours a week availability up to 40 hours.</p>
