<p>
KPMG is currently seeking an Associate to join our Federal Advisory practice.</p>
<p>
Responsibilities:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Help execute process and strategy improvements related to supply chain and operational improvement projects</li>
<li>
Accomplish complex studies requiring application of analytical and statistical methods and techniques</li>
<li>
Provide Continuous Process Improvement (CPI) program management across an enterprise</li>
<li>
Conduct and synchronize enterprise-wide projects on moderate to complex problems related to Supply Chain operations that require As Is and To Be process models derived from root cause/ gap analysis</li>
<li>
Study trends in Business Transformation efforts and Continuous Process Improvement efforts to formulate recommendations to ensure Client Directorates and aligned with the supply chain enterprise</li>
</ul>
<p>
Qualifications:</p>
<ul>
<li>
A minimum of two years of management consulting experience providing operational advisory services related to supply chain, cost reduction, and operational restructuring; U.S. Federal government consulting experience preferred</li>
<li>
Bachelor's degree in finance, engineering, operations analysis, or a related field from an accredited college/university Lean Six Sigma Black Belt Certification preferred</li>
<li>
Ability to assess, improve, and reconstruct the information, physical, and financial flows across the client's supply chain</li>
<li>
Deep knowledge of demand planning and replenishment, inventory optimization, logistics, transportation, distribution, and sales and operations planning</li>
<li>
Demonstrated experience developing relationships with senior clients</li>
<li>
U.S. citizenship required</li>
<li>
Ability to obtain a U.S. Federal government security clearance within a reasonable period of time</li>
</ul>
