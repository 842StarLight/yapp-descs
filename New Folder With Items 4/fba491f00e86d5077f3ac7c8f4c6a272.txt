<p><b>
Job Description</b><br>
</p>
<p>
The successful candidate will be responsible for preparing and modifying drawings per specifications and customer requirements. Prepare/maintain accurate catalog drawings and digital library for the website. Work closely with General Manager to ensure the product is built according to the latest part or shop drawings. Other duties will include, but are not limited to:<br>
</p>
<ul>
<li>
Create 3D models with AutoCAD Inventor from 2D drawings</li>
<li>
Create special manufacturing drawings for immediate production</li>
<li>
Adhere to customer (Internal and External) volumes, timelines and expectations</li>
<li>
Prepare and modify catalog drawings from existing and new products</li>
<li>
Maintain accurate drawing books for plant production and Quality Control</li>
<li>
Prepare product assembly or shop drawings for existing/new products as needed for sales staff and special drawings for plant projects as needed</li>
<li>
Work with<b> </b>General Manager and Plant Manager to learn requirements of process control, inspection, and testing in production</li>
<li>
Update/maintain digital database, i.e. Autodesk vault, hard-copies, and engineering documents</li>
<li>
Other duties may be assigned as needed<br>
</li>
</ul>
