<h2>Marketing Copywriter - Ocala</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>R+L Global Logistics</b> is seeking a <b>Marketing Copywriter</b> to join our growing logistics company. The ideal Marketing Copywriter candidate will be creative, motivated and sales oriented. Individuals who are strategy driven and have a desire to be a part of our growing organization are encouraged to apply.</p>
<p><b>
 </b><b>Marketing Copywriter</b> <b>Summary of Key Responsibilities</b></p>
<p>
 <b>Responsibilities and essential job functions include, but are not limited to the following:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Develop marketing content that is purposeful, on-brand, and high-quality, including emails, advertising, video scripts, landing pages, signage, print media, social, and event copy</li>
<li>
Collaborate with stakeholder's company-wide to ensure content is accurate and effective</li>
<li>
Create video scripts and support video production from concept through execution</li>
<li>
Support and influence Marketing's traffic, performance, and engagement goals</li>
<li>
Participate in brainstorming and contribute ideas to influence the Marketing strategy</li>
<li>
Determine how best to incorporate analytics results into content</li>
<li>
Incorporate SEO organically and purposefully</li>
<li>
Understand and evaluate the competitive landscape</li>
</ul>
<p>
 <b>Marketing Copywriter Knowledge, Skills, and Abilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Ability to write in our brand voice in long and short form across a variety of mediums</li>
<li>
Excellent copywriting and editing skills; strong verbal communicator and presenter</li>
<li>
Ability to think creatively and work effectively to quickly changing priorities and processes</li>
<li>
Self-directed and collaborative - ability to work autonomously and as part of a team as needed.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Marketing Copywriter Qualifications</b></p>
<p><b>
Summary of Experience and Education</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
High school Diploma or GED required</li>
<li>
Bachelor's degree in English, Journalism, Marketing, Communication, or related field preferred</li>
<li>
1-3 years of relevant experience in marketing preferred</li>
<li>
Previous experience working in supply chain or transportation industry a plus</li>
<li>
Ability to read, write, and speak English fluently; Spanish is a plus</li>
<li>
Experience working with SEO tools such as SEMrush, WordPress, Yoast, Google Analytics</li>
</ul>
