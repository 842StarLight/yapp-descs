<h2>Logistics Supervisor</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Position Summary:</b></p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
The Logistics Supervisor will be responsible for managing a team comprised of 3 to 4 logistics coordinators and planners as well as a fleet of company drivers / owner operators to ensure the drayage, transportation and logistics service offerings in our Chicago operation meet PFS Logistics growth and profit targets. This role will also be responsible for managing carrier relationships, as well as building the relationship with the freezer operations and sales teams. This role is directly responsible for executing programs for both new business and managing existing business for this Preferred Freezer Services division. A successful candidate will act as the focal point in service and cost management for the Port to Preferred Freezer Services drayage business, while offering support on customized transportation consolidation, TL / LTL programs from Freezer to retail and food service customers, ensuring customers a sustainable service solution. The manager will drive profitability and growth in line with company vision and values. Establishes strategies to expand and support the customer base while contributing to the development of the logistics operations team.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Primary Tasks &amp; Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Assist in the recruitment efforts of company drivers and owner operators for intermodal drayage (as well as regional and outbound TL / LTL initiatives) – working together with LTL/TL Operations.</li>
<li>
Face to face engagement with Freezer SM's, GM's, Shipping/Receiving departments to discuss PFSL service levels and identify opportunities for service improvement towards operational excellence.</li>
<li>
Create a daily dispatch plan on all shipments to avoid rail / port demurrage, container per diem, and ensuring optimum driver/unit utilization and performance in generating revenue.</li>
<li>
Complying with Federal Motor Carrier Safety Regulations, namely HOS.</li>
<li>
Host periodic driver meetings to promote a positive PFSL driver experience and culture.</li>
<li>
Provide our customers and PFS Freezers with the highest level of operational excellence, customer service; maintaining constant communication with a solution based "can do" attitude, to represent professionalism throughout the transportation lifecycle.</li>
<li>
Assist in the development sustainable cost-effective solutions that leverage Preferred Freezer Services inbound and outbound volume, systems, and partnerships.</li>
<li>
Assist in the creation and implementation a transportation capacity strategy that includes contingency plans to support varying levels of company sales and changes to supply/demand patterns and market driven fluctuations.</li>
<li>
Adhere to existing processes and procedures to ensure daily execution of transportation policies, operating standards, and service level agreements.</li>
<li>
Assist in the development and support the use of key performance indicators (KPIs), metrics and periodic reporting to help drives execution /process uniformity and compliance across all regions.</li>
<li>
Build and foster relationships with Railroads, Marine Terminal Operators and 3rd Party Carriers to improve PFSL operational efficiency.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Education/Experience:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Associates degree, required.</li>
<li>
Bachelor's degree in Engineering, Business and/or Supply Chain/Logistics discipline, preferred.</li>
<li>
Minimum 5 years' experience or equivalent in transportation and/or supply chain required in a DOT Compliance, Dispatcher / Customer Logistics / Transportation Manager or similar role/function.</li>
<li>
Experience with both optimization/transportation software packages. (ex. Profit Tools, TMW, PiNC &amp; Accellos / HighJump preferred)</li>
<li>
Must possess thorough understanding of supply chain and transportation methods/modes, cost structures, service requirements, tariffs, fuel scales, accessorials, equipment and infrastructure in order to ensure optimal day-to-day management of clients.</li>
<li>
Ability to effectively communicate with individuals/groups from varied backgrounds and skill levels.</li>
<li>
Detail and multi-task oriented. Strong analytical, critical thinking and problem-solving skills.</li>
<li>
Strong interpersonal skills, written and verbal communication skills, and implementation and facilitation skills.</li>
</ul>
