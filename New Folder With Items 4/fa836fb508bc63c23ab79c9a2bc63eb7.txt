<h2>Service Desk - FCPAC ( PT)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<b>HOURS:</b> 18-28 hours per week, days, evenings, and weekends.<br>
<br>
<b>MINIMUM AGE:</b> Must be at least 18 years of age<br>
<br>
<b>DISTINGUISHING FEATURES OF WORK</b><b>:</b><br>
This is year-round, part time clerical and cashier work.<b> Employees must be able to work at least three weekday afternoon/evening shifts (2-10PM) and weekends. Employees may be required to work on some City Holidays.</b> Emphasis is placed on customer service, sales, cashiering, and computer related functions. The employee is responsible for handling customer concerns while projecting a positive image of the organization. The employee performs cashier related duties including collecting fees; distributing tickets, tags, and passes; and completing daily cash reports. The employee also performs word processing, data entry or other computer-related tasks, as well as general office clerical functions.<br>
<br>
 DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES: <b>ESSENTIAL FUNCTIONS:</b><ul>
<li>Delivers superior customer service; performs cashier related duties.
</li>
<li>Handles membership sales as well as activity/program registration
</li>
<li>Performs word processing, data entry, or other computer-related tasks.
</li>
<li>Files documents in proper order according to established filing systems.
</li>
<li>Performs daily record keeping tasks; prepares reports.
</li>
<li>Enforces the rules of the Center with social responsibility.
</li>
<li>Attends mandatory staff meetings.
</li>
<li>Demonstrates regular and predictable attendance as scheduled.
</li>
<li>Performs other duties as assigned.</li>
</ul>
<br>
 KNOWLEDGE, SKILLS AND PHYSICAL REQUIREMENTS: <b>KNOWLEDGE, SKILLS AND ABILITIES:</b><ul>
<li>Ability to work independently under general supervision, and to demonstrate initiative in performance of the work.
</li>
<li>Ability to follow established departmental procedures, learn new tasks and adapt to operational changes readily.
</li>
<li>Ability to communicate both verbally and in writing in an effective manner.
</li>
<li>Ability to establish and maintain effective and cooperative relationships with other employees, program participants, and the general public.</li>
</ul>
<br>
<b>QUALIFICATIONS</b><b>:</b><ul>
<li>Customer service and sales experience, cash handling and knowledge of computer business applications are necessary.</li>
</ul>
<br>
 EXPERIENCE AND TRAINING/LICENSING REQUIREMENTS: <b>NECESSARY SPECIAL QUALIFICATIONS</b><b>:</b><br>
Must submit to a criminal history check and drug screen.<br>
<br>
<b>EXAMINATION PROCESS</b><b>:</b><br>
Written Exam 40%<br>
Oral Exam 60%</div></div>
