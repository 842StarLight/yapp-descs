<b>What to expect in this role:</b>
</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div>We’re looking for a talented professional with a drive to grow the overall business within an assigned territory of responsibility. As Business Development Manager, your focus will be on the application, sales and administration of business across multiple markets. You’ll be responsible for the management of accounts which will include business sustainment and developmental opportunities. Your strategic business focus ensure territory growth over numerous market sectors. This position calls for an outstanding communicator who thrives on managing multiple projects and a team player equally comfortable working on your own initiative. You’ll be interfacing with customers, field sales and interdepartmental personnel on technical and administrative issues.<br>
<br>
</div>
<div><b>Within the role you will be responsible for these principal accountabilities:</b></div>
<br>
<div></div>
<ul>
<li><div>Be responsible for implementation of business strategies and continuous improvement.
</div></li>
<li><div>Take the lead as the proposal manager for key proposal activities.
</div></li>
<li><div>Be responsible for managing the sales and business development activities to support overall territory growth.
</div></li>
<li><div>Establish and manage a bookings forecasts.
</div></li>
<li><div>Promote a common understanding of Moog products and technologies throughout various markets.
</div></li>
<li><div>Provide marketing analysis as appropriate to help guide continued refinement of business strategy, product and technology roadmaps.
</div></li>
<li><div>Improve profitability and customer satisfaction on new and recurring sales by creating alignment of sales objectives and operations’ capability.<br>
</div></li>
</ul>
