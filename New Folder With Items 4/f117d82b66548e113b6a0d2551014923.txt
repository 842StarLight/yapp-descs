<h2>Regional Manager Specialty Pharmacy Operations</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>Cornerstone Health Solutions (CHS) is one of the Northeast’s largest specialty pharmacy partners. The Regional Manager of specialty pharmacy operations is responsible for overseeing and managing day to day specialty operations at up to several contracted hospital and clinic partner locations. The ideal candidate will possess knowledge of specialty pharmacy, focused on developing and managing key programs supporting client clinical and financial performance. A key trait of this candidate will be to work well under time pressures, establishing credibility with clients, and be service oriented in their work with client sites.<br>
<br>
This position will report directly to the Director of Pharmacy Strategy and Business Development, where they will work closely in developing, deploying and managing tactics to grow client programs, alongside identifying immediate growth or optimization opportunities. He or she will be directly responsible for partner P&amp;L’s and be incentivized for the quality of care and financial performance of the region.<p>
On-site account management, partner site P&amp;L’s and new and established growth programs at client sites</p>
<ul>
<li>
Manages all areas of partner accounts, including full financial performance responsibility of: total revenues, growth, patient recruitment and conversion rates</li>
<li>
Understanding of clinical service needs, translating those needs into program standards and addressing challenges and roadblocks by devising solutions and alternative approaches</li>
<li>
Maintain a deep understanding of client environment, including clinic operations, 340B eligibility and other onsite support networks or programs</li>
<li>
Possess ability to influence others without formal authority</li>
</ul>
<p>
Coordination of clinical care programs aligned with partner health system objectives through the management of on-site staff</p>
<ul>
<li>
Provides day to day performance management, and accountability to all dedicated account staff in conjunction with the Manager of Advanced Teams and Director of Pharmacy Strategy and Business Development</li>
<li>
Participates in interviewing, hiring, orientation, onboarding, and recurring training of staff</li>
<li>
Creates a great work environment and inspires teamwork and partnership at all levels</li>
</ul>
<p>
Driving growth through program performance, including operating effectiveness (patient recruitment and retention) and operational efficiencies, and standardization/optimization of programs post initial implementation phase</p>
<ul>
<li>
Process focused and decisive in ensuring effective execution of strategy and initiatives to meet client expectations by translating company strategy into operational tactics without heavy senior oversight</li>
<li>
Engages with internal teams to optimize performance integrated with support areas, including but not limited to; patient call center or support staff, CHS specialty pharmacy, CHS mail order pharmacy</li>
<li>
Working knowledge of pharmacy regulations, local, state and federal laws, and adherence to accreditation and contract standards (URAC, payer contracts, etc.)</li>
<li>
Understands market trends and environment, including partner changes, drug access, prescribing trends, and evolving clinic landscapes</li>
</ul>
<p>
Maintaining strong relationships with business units, clinical staff, and C-suite leadership</p>
<ul><li>
Addresses partner hospital satisfaction promptly and with high level of service</li></ul>
<p></p>
<p>
Must adhere to all of BMC’s RESPECT behavioral standards.</p>
