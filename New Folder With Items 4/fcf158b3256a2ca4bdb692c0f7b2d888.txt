<h2>Parts Coordinator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Position Summary</b></p>
<p>
Parts Coordinator is responsible for assisting the Parts Manager in variety of ways including sales accounting functions, logistics management and support, sales quotes, and training staff, handling customer concerns and shop workflow.</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Essential Duties &amp; Responsibilities</b></p>
<p><i>
Duties:</i></p>
<ul>
<li>Assists the Parts Manager with various tasks including the organization of the parts division</li>
<li>Identify and maintain inventory; create and maintain items in the system to include minimum and maximum stock levels and reorder items</li>
<li>Coordinate and perform physical inventories to assure accuracy and completeness of all inventory reports as well as managing detailed accounting records.</li>
<li>Work with accounting department to reconcile inventory numbers between all software systems.</li>
<li>Responsible for recording sales and purchase transactions by means of creating invoices, purchase orders and other accounting data entry documents, both in computer programs and in paper form.</li>
<li>Assist warehouse personnel with receiving duties including processing inbound shipments; stocking material and notifying management of any damaged shipments for freight claims.</li>
<li>Assist coordinating the activities of receiving, shipping in the warehouse/yard</li>
<li>Provides leadership in the Parts Warehouse department and verifies compliance with customer specifications.</li>
<li>Provides backup for Parts Manager in customer matters and/or while Parts Manager is out of office.</li>
<li>Sourcing and purchasing new and used parts for each division</li>
<li>Website listings and reconciliation of listings between various marketing websites</li>
<li>Assist Marketing Coordinator with various web listings and targeted email blasts.</li>
<li>Provides timely responses to customers and co-workers requests.</li>
<li>Seeks to improve internal processes and the overall customer experience by sharing their ideas with management</li>
<li>Report all recognized unsafe conditions or acts to Management</li>
<li>Attends training sessions as required</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Qualifications</b></p>
<p><i>
Education &amp; Experience</i></p>
<ul>
<li>2-4 years of experience in related field required</li>
<li>High school diploma or GED or equivalent years of relevant experience and/or training</li>
<li>Person must be able to pass a drug test and have a valid Texas driver’s license and a clean driving record</li>
<li>Must be a self-starter, willing to listen and work with others, and possess a teamwork philosophy</li>
<li>Knowledge of CAT parts a plus</li>
<li>Previous experience using forklifts a plus</li>
</ul>

<p><b>Physical Demands</b></p>
<p>
Some physical activity performing daily activities primarily technical in nature. Moving about on foot to accomplish task, particularly for long distances; occasional ascending or descending on ladders.</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Work Environment</b></p>
<p>
Combination of both office and shop work. Office work involves working closely with the Parts Team. Shop work and occasional outdoor work involves environment with fumes or airborne particles and very loud noise (examples: forklift, engine noise, load banks). Typical schedule is Monday through Friday with regular working hours, possible evening and weekend schedules</p>
