<h2>Principal Consultant – Penetration Testing</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>
<b>Principal Consultant – Penetration Testing</b><br>
Multiple cities – Remote position<br>
<br>
<b>Overview:</b><br>
As a leader in TUV Rheinland OpenSky Penetration Testing services group, you will provide technical thought leadership, transform our testing methodologies to stay ahead of threats, and mentor team members in new and advanced techniques. Moreover, you will be ‘hands-on’ and participate in the delivery of our most innovative testing projects throughout North America. In addition, you will be responsible for developing and presenting technical presentations at world leading conferences, develop new testing solutions, and provide technical authority to develop and enhance TUV Rheinland’ s testing portfolio.</div>
<div></div>
<div><b>Responsibilities:</b></div>
<div>The right candidate will help establish relationships and influence organizational leaders throughout North America and TUV Rheinland’ s broader global organization, including regional business heads (EVP-level), regional practice leaders, and service line managers to execute on the product and business strategy.</div>
<div>In addition, you will:</div>
<ul>
<li>Develop and deliver thought leadership for North America’s penetration testing services strategy</li>
<li>Lead research and development activities to synthesize new testing services (methodologies, tool sets, test case documentation, etc.)</li>
<li>Develop early stage proof of concepts scripts (exploits, automation scripts, etc.), and publish technical white papers to present at industry leading conferences in order to grow TUV Rheinland’ s revenue and brand awareness in the cybersecurity sector</li>
<li>Act as the regional SME for the strategic projects (including technology strategy, innovation, and roadmap for the capability)</li>
<li>Support and enable regional delivery of cybersecurity services and capability development through delivery oversight, training, and documentation development</li>
<li>Support pre-sales, as expert overlay, and provide hands on delivery as primary tester for new cybersecurity services</li>
<li>Collaborate with marketing and sales teams in support of revenue growth, and service line expansion, through the development of value propositions, services descriptions, sales presentations, standard statements of work</li>
<li>Identify and evaluate potential delivery and technology partnerships</li>
<li>Assist in business case development, and investment recommendations</li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<div><b>Required Skills:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>At least 7 years experience working in the cybersecurity industry, preferably in consulting or professional services.</li>
<li>Minimum 7 years experience leading and performing network and web application vulnerability assessments and penetration testing services for a consultancy or large enterprise organization.</li>
<li>Demonstrated hands on experience testing IoT/Embedded systems and hardware that includes reverse engineering, wireless technologies and physical components.</li>
<li>Demonstrated ability leading and influencing in a complex and multi-faceted corporate environment.</li>
<li>Must be a change agent with experience driving accountability toward positive outcomes across a diverse team, as well as a cross-functional team player.</li>
<li>Experience supporting sales as a technical expert with the ability to lead an opportunity from initial conversation to signed statement of work</li>
<li>Exceptional communication skills.</li>
<li>Demonstrated ability to solve large and complex problems.</li>
<li>Awareness of industry trends and developments.</li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<div><b>Preferred Skills:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Offensive Security Certified Professional (OSCP) qualification preferred</li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<div><b>Education:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor's degree in Computer Science or another technical field</li>
<li>Advanced degree in Computer Science or related field preferred</li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<div><b>Travel Required:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Up to 40% travel</li>
</ul>
