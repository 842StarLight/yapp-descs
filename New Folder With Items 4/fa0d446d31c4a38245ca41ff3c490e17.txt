We're looking for an innovative Head of Marketing who will develop and execute dynamic messaging, communications, and campaign strategy to help drive the next phase of growth for Text IQ. The ideal candidate should bring proven B2B lead generation experience, coupled with intelligence and business savvy, to assume a key role in our management team. He or she will be responsible for all market strategy implementation and for achieving measurable marketing targets for our enterprise customers and prospects.
<br><br>
<b>As Head of Marketing, you will:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Manage and monitor all lead generation activities (events, paid media, content, etc.) in close conjunction with Sales and Sales Operations</li>
<li>Collaborate with Product Management team to position our features and innovation to benefit new and existing customers and differentiate our company from the competition</li>
<li>Own branding and messaging across all our marketing collateral (website, ads, email drips, social media channels, etc.) and communications</li>
<li>Define marketing KPIs, create and implement a top-of-the-funnel inbound marketing strategy, and create dashboards to present progress to company leaders</li>
<li>Manage public and media relations, including developing affiliations with related publications</li>
<li>Hire new members and develop the marketing team as our needs grow</li>
</ul>
<b>We are looking for a teammate who has:</b><br>
<ul>

<li>An eagle eye for detail, never letting anything slip through the cracks</li>
<li>Technical capability, excellent communication skills, and a desire to improve processes</li>

<li>Capacity to build a high-performing results-driven team</li>
