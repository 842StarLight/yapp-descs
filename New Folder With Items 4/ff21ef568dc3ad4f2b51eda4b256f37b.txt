<h2>Key Account Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<b>Job Description</b><br>
<br>
Mattel, Inc., the largest toy company in the world, is seeking a Key Account Manager to drive revenue and profitability by selling the most iconic toy brands in the industry to top retailers in the US.<br>
<br>
The Key Account Manager will be responsible for developing and executing our Strategic Account Plans for multiple categories. The role will require a deep understanding of the retail environment and how to align our portfolio of products with the account needs.<br>
<br>
This position will be located at one of our Sales offices around the country or remote based. The position will require periodic travel to the account(s) and corporate headquarters.<br>
<br>
Key Responsibilities:<ul>
<li>
Account Base consists of:<ul>
<li>
Focus on the Fisher Price, Mega and AST lines.</li>
<li>
Direct interaction with buying, planning, advertising, merchandising and finance teams at each customer.</li>
<li>
Multiple national customers</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>
Achieve targeted sales objectives.<ul>
<li>
Consistently deliver the quarterly and annual business plan.</li>
<li>
Increase overall revenue in conjunction with profitable growth at account level. (includes ROI analysis to justify funding spends)</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>
Develop and execute strategic business plan for the account base that drives brand sales levels as well as customer profitability growth.</li>
<li>
Accountable for weekly in-depth analysis of customer performance while generating incremental shipments and opportunities at SKU level.</li>
<li>
Increase market share at customers by maximizing in aisle, feature areas and other key promotional levers.</li>
<li>
Manage monthly forecast and pledge review process to ensure in-line with open to build thresholds.</li>
<li>
Identify business opportunities and market share gaps with accounts, work to design business plans to achieve objectives.</li>
<li>
Oversee &amp; manage budgets covering market development, trade and over &amp; above funding.</li>
<li>
Build and merchandise full-line Spring and Fall planograms to increase SKU and brand productivity for each customer.</li>
<li>
Maintain item set up and fill/cut reports within account base</li>
</ul>
<br>

<li>
Experience with Consumer Goods and/or National Retail Corporation(s).<ul><li>
Background in the Toy industry preferred.</li></ul>
</li>
<li>
Relationship building skills with proven sales success managing key account partnerships-to include strong communication, interpersonal and project management areas</li>
<li>
Knowledge of syndicated data analysis, data metrics, brand, competitive and category volumes.</li>
<li>
Understanding for competitive landscape and toy industry.</li>

Proven ability to lead, own and drive programs</li>

Action oriented, proactive and well versed in decision-making</li>
<li>
Strong economic thinking: financial acumen and entrepreneurship skills.</li>
<li>
Results /execution driven with emphasis on sales metrics achievement.</li>
<li>
Ability to work in a fast pace team environment.</li>
<li>
Bachelor’s degree in Business Administration, Marketing or related field; MBA preferred.</li>
<li>
5+ year’s related work experience</li>
</ul>

ABOUT US</b><br>
<br>
Mattel is a leading global children's entertainment company that specializes in design and production of quality toys and consumer products. We create innovative products and experiences that inspire, entertain and develop children through play. We engage consumers through our portfolio of iconic franchises, including Barbie®, Hot Wheels®, American Girl®, Fisher-Price®, Thomas &amp; Friends® and MEGA®, as well as other popular brands that we own or license in partnership with global entertainment companies. Our offerings include film and television content, gaming, music and live events. We operate in 40 locations and sell products in more than 150 countries in collaboration with the world's leading retail and technology companies. Since its founding in 1945, Mattel is proud to be a trusted partner in exploring the wonder of childhood and empowering kids to reach their full potential.<br>
