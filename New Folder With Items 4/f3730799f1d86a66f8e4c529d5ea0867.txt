<h2>Mgr - Regional Production Technology Product Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<div>
<div>The Mgr - Regional Production Technology is accountable for implementing and directing Technical Innovation and Development, Customer Facing Technology Communications, and Global Technology Consistency and Efficiency. This role bridges Production Technology with Enterprise IT. The Mgr - Regional Production Technology supports Brand Point Management by implementing standard operating procedures created in partnership with Enterprise IT.<br>
</div>
</div>

<div>Regular

<div>Full-time
</div>

<div>
<p><b>Job Responsibilities</b></p>
<div><ul>
<li>Interacts with client to better understand their business needs and develop technical solutions to meet those needs.
</li>
<li>Implements the strategic vision and innovation processes that consistently deliver operational benefits for the company and its clients within a business segment.
</li>
<li>
Partners closely with Brand Development, Brand Deployment and Enabling Practices partners to drive operational excellence and an exceptional client experience.
</li>
<li>
Works with the Enterprise IT and Regional business leaders to influence technical and architectural aspects of enterprise wide IT solutions.
</li>
<li>
Participates in the Technology Leadership Council.
</li>
<li>
Ensures resources are efficiently managed and available to support and deliver the corporate and functional technical innovations and initiatives.
</li>
<li>
Develops and maintains relationships with the business’ key suppliers, ensuring the company is given priority on technical innovation and is represented on the suppliers technical roadmap.
</li>
<li>
Assures that the Regional Production Technology team delivers effective and timely services to the business segments.
</li>
<li>
Assists with technical support and training for corporate enterprise products.
</li>
<li>
Ensures the business has effective selling tools in the areas of technical innovation that demonstrate the value to both current and prospective clients.
</li>
<li>Assists in the selling process where appropriate.</li>
<li>
Plans, directs, and coordinates activities of employee(s) to ensure goals or objectives are accomplished. Mentors, coaches, trains and develops team.</li>
<li>
Additional duties as assigned.</li>
</ul></div>
</div>
<div>
<p><b>Qualifications / Requirements</b></p>
<div>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s Degree in in Information Technology, Graphic Arts, Business Management or related field</li>
<li>
Advanced degree (MBA, Master’s) preferred</li>
<li>
8+ years progressive in Information Technology, Graphic Arts, Business, Management experience</li>
<li>
5+ years in a leadership role</li>
<li>
Occasional travel either locally, nationally, and/or internationally may be required.</li>
<li>
High degree of proficiency in MS Office Suite, Outlook &amp; Internet applications.</li>
<li>
Strong analytical, prioritizing, interpersonal, problem-solving, presentation, budgeting, project management (from conception to completion), &amp; planning skills.</li>
<li>
Strong verbal and written communication skills (including analysis, interpretation, &amp; reasoning).</li>
<li>
Solid understanding and application of mathematical concepts.</li>
<li>
Ability to develop and maintain collaborative relationships with peers and colleagues across the organization, as well as, internal and external clients.</li>
<li>
Ability to work well autonomously and within a team in a fast-paced and deadline-oriented environment.</li>
<li>
Ability to work with and influence peers and management.</li>
<li>
Self-motivated with critical attention to detail, deadlines and reporting.</li>
</ul>
