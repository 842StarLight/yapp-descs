<h2>Sr. Product Marketing Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>
<div>Arm Treasure Data provides an end-to-end, fully managed cloud service (data acquisition, storage and</div>
<div>
analysis capability) for Big Data that is trusted and simple. As the original developers of Fluentd, an advanced open-source log collector specifically designed to solve the big data log collection problem, Arm Treasure Data solves the problems for companies wanting the ability to manage their big data needs. The differentiation of Arm Treasure Data is based on the underlying data platform to ingest and unify customer data, perform machine learning on customer data and syndicate with other software products for activation. Data from IoT devices is a key source for bringing physical and digital data together. Numerous large global customers, like Anheuser Busch, Subaru, Panasonic, and Wish trust Treasure Data to handle their complexity, scale and security needs.</div>
</div>
<div><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>The Role</b></h3>
<div><ul>
<li>
Reporting to the Director, Product Marketing, you will be an integral part of the global product marketing team, collaborating closely with product managers, sales engineers and sales. As part of a high-performance product marketing team, you will have the opportunity to:</li>
<li>
Build product positioning and messaging in close partnership with product management and collaborate with internal teams to ensure its implementation across all customer and sales channels.</li>
<li>
Define target audiences, build a deep understanding of, and empathy with, audiences through market research, insights and engaging with the customer base.</li>
<li>
Work with the enterprise marketing team to co-develop integrated marketing global marketing campaigns for lead generation and build audience targeted content.</li>
<li>
Generate content for prospects, evaluators and channel partners and use internal and external agencies to build high quality creative assets.</li>
<li>
Partner with Product Management to drive product launches and inform product roadmap. Enable our sales and business development teams with audience personas, targeted collateral, playbooks, and messaging and drive consistency through all channels.</li>
<li>
Propose, test and implement improvements to increase conversion rate at every stage of the sales funnel.</li>
</ul></div>
</div></div>
<div><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Your Background will include:</b></h3>
<div><ul>
<li>
6+ years product marketing experience in the B2B SaaS space, preferably with big data or analytics platforms.</li>
<li>
Experience developing clear and compelling messaging, delivering engaging presentations and creating high quality content for customers, evaluators, advocates and channel partners.</li>
<li>
Proven ability to collaborate with product, sales and marketing across a wide variety of product marketing-related activities.</li>
<li>
Adept at influencing peers and managers and building consensus in a fast-paced environment.</li>
<li>
Can understand and simplify complex, technical topics for non-technical audiences.</li>
<li>
Expertise conducting market researching and using audience insights for segmentation and persona development.</li>
<li>
Outstanding follow-through, time management, and project management skills to deliver results.</li>
<li>
BA/BS in business or technology field. MBA from a top university preferred.</li>
<li>
A positive attitude and a sense of humor.</li>
</ul></div>
