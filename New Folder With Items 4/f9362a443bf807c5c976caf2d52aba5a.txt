<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>About the job</b></h2>
<p>
One of the key problems that needs to be solved to enable the shift from traditional TV to YouTube is helping advertisers measure and understand the effectiveness of YouTube ads. To solve this problem; we are building products that are used by advertisers to measure ad spend on YouTube. These products help advertisers understand and optimize for ad performance; and decide how and where they want to allocate future spend.<br>
<br>
As the Product Management Director for YouTube Ads Effectiveness, you will help advertisers understand this critical area. You will be responsible for our strategy and execution and will lead a highly talented, experienced and technical team of product managers.<br>
<br>
Google runs one of the highest quality and largest scale advertising platforms in the world. This platform works because users trust Google and trust that the ads we show are honest and appropriate. The scale of our platform also makes us a target for abuse from highly sophisticated attackers. We have a responsibility to our users, advertisers and partners to create a platform that is trustworthy, protects our user and advertiser interests, and also sets the standard for the industry on how to prevent and defend against attacks.There are billions of dollars at stake, billions of ads in our system and an ever increasing footprint of advertising products that need our support.<br>
</p>
<p>
YouTube as a platform is incredibly strategic to Google's future growth. It is a phenomenal platform with 1B+ daily active users and has empowered an entire generation of creators and artists. As the platform continues to experience double digit growth its becoming strategically more important to Google. As viewership moves away from traditional TV to online video platforms - brand advertising dollars will also move which gives Google access to a new source of advertiser budgets.</p>
