<h2>Payroll, Benefits, Accounts Payable Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>JOB DESCRIPTION</b></p>
<p>The Virginia Symphony Orchestra is seeking a full-time <b>Payroll, Benefits, Accounts Payable Manager </b>who reports directly to the CFO. Primary responsibilities include payroll, benefit administration, accounts payable, and daily deposit reconciliation. The ideal candidate is a highly organized individual with a positive attitude, who combines their knowledge and love of accounting with a desire for helpful and regular interaction with our employees.</p>
<p><b>Principal Responsibilities: </b></p>
<p>Payroll:</p>
<ul>
<li>Accurate and timely entry of bi-monthly payroll information from time sheets into Payday Payroll database including entry of new employee information</li>
<li>Meet with new employees and review payroll forms and benefit forms and information</li>
<li>Develop familiarity with Musician CBA</li>
<li>Reconciliation of payroll deductions against monthly invoices</li>
<li>Timely entry and processing of payroll related benefits (Health Equity and Sentinel)</li>
<li>Prepare monthly musician pension payable from payroll report</li>
<li>Review of quarterly payroll reports</li>
<li>Timely completion and submission of unemployment benefit forms</li>
<li>Provide employee contributions for entry into Tessitura each payroll</li>
<li>Distribute and collect keys, parking fobs etc. from new and terminated employees</li>
<li>Prepare general journal entry to allocate production and per diem/travel to projects</li>
</ul>
<p>Benefits:</p>
<ul>
<li>Enter and terminate benefits for employees into benefit databases including health, vision, dental, FSA, HSA, dependent care and 403B</li>
<li>Update payroll deductions for changes in benefits for an employee</li>
<li>Enter new employees into COBRA benefit database; Enter terminated employees into COBRA and inform COBRA company as to such</li>
<li>Assist CFO with annual benefit renewal and enrollment including collection and review of forms from employees; Enter new benefit amounts into payroll and database systems for each employee</li>
<li>Vacation and sick time tracking and distribution</li>
</ul>
<p>Accounts Payable:</p>
<ul>
<li>Enter stamp on all AP invoices for approval and coding</li>
<li>Enter invoices into AP system on a weekly basis including accurate account and project codes.</li>
<li>Provide AP aging with recommended invoices to be paid and review with CFO</li>
<li>Maintain on-going AP projection</li>
<li>Cut checks on a weekly basis from appropriate checking account</li>
<li>Post AP General Journal weekly and reconcile with GL account</li>
<li>Void and replace checks as needed</li>
<li>Follow up older uncashed checks with vendors.</li>
</ul>
<p>Deposits:</p>
<ul>
<li>Prepare daily check deposit for pick up by deposit service or take to banks as needed</li>
<li>Enter deposit information onto daily cash sheet</li>
<li>Daily reconciliation of Tessitura GL Summary for gift and ticket sale entry against batch detail and check deposits</li>
</ul>
<p>Month End Entries:</p>
<ul>
<li>Record miscellaneous deposits</li>
<li>Prepare and reconcile other month end entries per month end checklist</li>
<li>Track and record debit card purchases</li>
<li>Monthly account reconciliation including benefit liability, debit card, and AP</li>
<li>Prepare general journal entry reclasses as needed</li>
</ul>
<p>Other:</p>
<ul>
<li>Prepare annual 1099 forms using Payday Payroll</li>
<li>Review and distribute W2s</li>
<li>Assist with annual audit</li>
<li>Assist CFO with special projects</li>
<li>Other duties as required</li>
</ul>
