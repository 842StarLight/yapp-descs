<h2>Clothing Processor</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<b>Job Title:</b> Clothing Processor
<br><br>
<b>Reports To:</b> Assistant Store Manager
<br><br>
<b>Job Location:</b> Store Location
<br><br>
<b>FLSA Status:</b> Hourly Non-Exempt. Positions may be Full or Part-Time; Seasonal or Non-Seasonal
<br><br>
JOB SUMMARY
<br><br>
The Clothing Processor follows the standard operator procedures to receive and process apparel at the store. This position ensures the apparel racks are merchandised correctly and fully stocked.
<br><br>
JOB DUTIES AND ESSENTIAL FUNCTIONS
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Follow receiving SOP’s to accurately scan and receive purchase orders</li>
<li>Use scan gun and computer-based software to ensure accuracy in receipts</li>
<li>Track purchase order shortages daily by scanning and emailing short invoices to Store Support</li>
<li>File all receiving paperwork daily</li>
<li>Assist with sorting of DC trucks</li>
<li>Verify the vendor’s price tag descriptions and prices match the item</li>
<li>Place security tag on select apparel items based on the SOP</li>
<li>Add price labels to select apparel items</li>
<li>Hang and fold apparel according to merchandising standards</li>
<li>Maintain sufficient amount of supplies (hangers, price labels, security tags, etc.)</li>
<li>Receive and ship store-to-store transfers</li>
<li>Research past purchase orders to find variances in paperwork</li>
<li>Assist the apparel department in stocking apparel merchandise on the sales floor when apparel receiving is caught up</li>
<li>Assist the receiving manager with regular receiving when apparel work is caught up</li>
<li>May be required to run the register and work in other departments on the sales floor</li>
<li>Use general office equipment such as telephone, copy machine, fax machine, calculator, computer</li>
<li>Operate Telxon gun, forklift, pallet jack, ladder, and other retail equipment</li>
<li>May be required to cross train and perform other duties</li>
</ul>
QUALIFICATIONS
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Must be highly organized and display a great attention to detail</li>
<li>Must have problem-solving skills, be self-motivated, and team-oriented</li>
<li>Ability to work independently without supervision</li>
<li>Flexibility with hours: ability to work a 40-hour workweek with varied hours, days, night, and weekends as business dictates.</li>
<li>Ability to access and use the in-store computer, scanning system, and wireless handheld unit</li>
<li>Ability to participate in our online applicant and new hire on-boarding process remotely</li>
<li>Ability to complete computer-based training</li>
</ul>
SUPERVISORY RESPONSIBILITIES
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>None</li>
</ul>
PHYSICAL REQUIREMENTS
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Good visual acuity</li>
<li>Ability to lift, push, and/or pull a minimum of 30 pounds repetitively and 31-50 pounds intermittently</li>
<li>Ability to stand and/or walk for long periods of time</li>
<li>Ability to use a ladder and/or pallet jack</li>
<li>Close vision for computer work</li>
<li>Repetitive wrist movements on keyboard</li>
<li>Ability to stand and/or walk for long periods of time</li>
<li>Ability to walk up and down stairs multiple times per day</li>
<li>Ability to verbally communicate effectively and professionally with all audiences (in-person or via handheld transceiver)</li>
</ul>
Reasonable accommodations may be made to enable individuals with disabilities to perform essential job functions.</div>
