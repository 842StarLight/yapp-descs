<div>Job Summary</div>
<p>
The Senior Account Manager I, Client Partnerships is responsible for growing and cultivating client relationships while managing multiple portfolios. Partners closely with critical internal cohorts to drive the coordination and development of value-add programs that fit within our key initiatives while meeting the needs of the client. Responsible for achieving budget goals within the department through tactics informed by cross-functional and matrix partners. Provides coaching to Account Manager I/ Account Manager II and creates mentoring opportunities. Other duties as assigned to meet function/business needs</p>
</div>

<p>
Essential Job Functions</p>
<p>
Portfolio Management - Oversees and manages the execution of tactics to support strategic focus as it relates to Credit Risk, Marketing Analytics, and financial performance. Frequently communicates within the Practice and internal partners to understand best practices and drive synergies as needed. Represents each respective brand need and manages accordingly through enterprise-wide initiatives. Partners closely with many internal departments such as Credit Risk, Finance, Marketing Analytics, Care Center, Compliance, Legal and Operations</p>
<p>
Relationship Management -Salesforce client record owner. Maintains productive relationships with matrix internal partners. Works in close synthesis with peers on Partnership Marketing and Field teams. Facilitates new brand partner onboarding, and develops strong day-to-day working relationship with brand partners. Oversees enhancement Marketing and third party marketing efforts. Some travel required.</p>
<p>
Expense Management -Point of contact for escalated accounting issues. Builds and manages the portfolio strategy throughout the annual budget process. Also identifies incremental needs by brand, advocates for business support of those needs and develops strategy for execution. Accountable for updating forecast as it relates to expenses and budget goals. Accountable for appropriately allocating the use of contractual and non-contractual marketing funds, as well as for the maintenance and accuracy of fund tracking</p>
<p>
Portfolio Operations -Point of contact for the business related to any large scale changes. Delivers initial and ongoing communication of initiatives regularly to internal partners. Accountable for accuracy and management of all portfolio reporting</p>
<p>
Talent Management - Provides coaching and mentoring opportunities to team members. Works with direct reports to build and follow through on development plans and goals based on their level of performance. Guides associate behavior by demonstrating attitudinal leadership</p>
