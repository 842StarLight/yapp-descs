The Compensation Workforce Rewards Consultant - Senior Associate is central to our project teams. They manage client projects, draft client presentations, assist in providing consulting advice, and formally and/or informally manage a team of analysts and Associates, providing feedback, technical review, and development. Key responsibilities include: Plans and manages execution of projects, likely with multiple work streams Executes project delivery in line with strategic direction from senior consultant Independently facilitates client meetings and acts as a trusted advisor to clients Appropriately delegates assignments to junior colleagues and reviews output for alignment with project needs Leads the development of client ready reports, with minimal guidance Identifies new business opportunities with clients and manages proposal process What’s in it for you? Opportunity to grow your career and showcase your talent in a global organization This role is responsible for: Prepares project charts and templates using project management tools, estimates hours for the project team, and prepares timelines for project milestones. Serves as an independent project manager for more straightforward projects or work streams. Ensures client projects adhere to budget. Immediately addresses out-of-scope issues or budget overruns and manages the client’s expectations.
<br><br>
Prioritizes project activities to manage the workload and meet project deadlines. Delegates project tasks in line with individual capability, development needs and available analyst and associate resources. Leads both internal and client project status meetings. Serves as a primary client contact and builds relationships with clients.
<br><br>
Writes and reviews client PowerPoint presentations/reports including Excel exhibits and initial conclusions on the data analytics completed by Analysts and Associates. Works closely with senior consultants on the project to determine the exhibits and findings to be included in the final client presentations, which typically involves a high level of customization. Conducts client interviews, facilitates focus groups, and delivers training. Is responsible for the technical part of client presentations to HR and management or during client meetings.
<br><br>
Writes project proposals and answers to Requests for Proposals. Suggests project extensions based on existing work. Attends sales calls. Writes statements of work including project steps for senior consultants’ review.

Develops new tools and templates that can be leveraged in multiple markets. Ensures intellectual capital generated within own projects is captured and available for others to draw on. Provides proactive and just-in-time feedback, coaching and mentoring to junior staff. Acts as a mentor and resource (technical or consulting) for colleague questions.
<br><br>
Participates in development and performance conversations with senior consultants to help ensure individual and team success. We would like you to have: Knowledge Management Knowledge of business and management principles involved in strategic planning, resource allocation, human resources modeling, and coordination of people and resources. Compensation: Knowledge of compensation principles, e.g., job benchmarking, salary structure design, job architecture Skills Active Listening: Giving full attention to what other people are saying, taking time to understand the points being made, asking questions as appropriate, and not interrupting at inappropriate times.
