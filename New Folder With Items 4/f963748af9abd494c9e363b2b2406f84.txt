<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Locations:
</b></h2>US-New York, NY (3 Park)
<p></p>
<p><b>Summary:
</b></p>
<p>Responsible for assisting the project management team with ongoing translation projects, assessing project specifications, and performing a thorough, non-language final-eye of documents before the conclusion of the project.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Description:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Understanding TransPerfect’s quality standards and client expectations to ensure they are upheld
</li>
<li>Consulting with the Project Managers and sales team to discuss the client’s expectations or specifications
</li>
<li>Pre-flighting source files and discussing any findings with the project manager
</li>
<li>Properly utilize all appropriate supplemental materials (refer to client PAQ, reference material, glossaries, etc.)
</li>
<li>Proofread all documents with a strong focus on names, numbers, missing text, punctuation/spelling, grammar, adherence to instructions, proper use of glossaries/memory/reference materials or other significant issues
</li>
<li>Organizing and networking documents
</li>
<li>Formatting using .doc, .PDF, and .xls files; importing images from PDF files to Word document, etc.
</li>
<li>Data entry; responsible for tracking and accurately logging all job submissions into the departmental spreadsheet
</li>
<li>Perform other special projects or duties when required
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Required Skills:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree
</li>
<li>Excellent communication skills (both written and verbal)
</li>
<li>Strong MS Office/computer skills
</li>
<li>Meticulous attention to detail
</li>
<li>An ability to learn new skills quickly and exceptional problem solving/critical thinking skills
</li>
<li>Able to effectively multitask in order to simultaneously execute multiple projects
</li>
<li>Maintain professionalism in all situations, especially under tight deadlines
</li>
<li>Prior copy-editing experience a plus
</li>
<li>Prior experience with translation memory software desired, but not required
</li>
</ul>
