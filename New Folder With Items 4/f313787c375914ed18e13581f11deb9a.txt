<h2>General Manager - Innovation</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b><i>If you’re passionate about becoming a Nationwide associate and believe you have the potential to be something great, let’s talk.
</i></b></p>
<p></p>
<p><b><i>Number 57 on the Fortune Magazine 100 Best Places to Work.
</i></b></p>Build and grow initiatives that generate from the Innovation team. Manage a cross functional, partly matrixed team that is responsible for the end-to-end business, including customer experience, sales performance, operations, and technology. Build and execute the strategy and vision while also managing the P&amp;L in a growth and scale environment. Comfortable with all aspects of general business management and understands the diverse perspectives of stakeholders and works with them to resolve differences. Prioritize effectively and communicate clearly and persuasively. Drive focus and excellence within the programs owned, with colleagues across the organization. Lead multiple teams through both actions and direction.
<p></p>
<p><b>Job Description
</b></p>
<p><b>Reporting Relationships: </b>Reports to Innovation Executive. Typically leads a team of 10-20 direct and indirect reports.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Core Duties and Responsibilities:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Manage business profit and loss and conduct reviews through effective written and verbal updates on product, key projects to senior leadership and stakeholders.
</li>
<li>Create and drive customer experience / product strategy and drive new business opportunities, with a focus on leveraging modern technology to solve problems.
</li>
<li>Influence the organization design structure for successful execution.
</li>
<li>Prioritize, communicate strategy and plans to ensure execution across Sales, Marketing, Finance, Operations, and Tech stakeholders.
</li>
<li>Ensure customers are engaged through a variety of channels and serve as the voice of the customer internally. Provide thought leadership and use Design Thinking to identify opportunities for improving the customer experience.
</li>
<li>Build great teams, including hiring and developing leadership and individual talent.
</li>
<li>Move initiatives from concept to reality: develop product, negotiate agreements, create &amp; validate P&amp;L, gain internal buy-in within organization and own all decisions required to execute with speed and precision.
</li>
<li>Establish great working relationships across the company to help invent progressive ways to improve how we come to market and how the business can further expand and support the channel.
</li>
<li>Hypothesize, measure and learn-break insights and opinions into hypotheses, drive the right test(s), measure with scientific discipline, and make consumer backed decisions.
</li>
<li>Own weekly and monthly reporting on the state of the business. Utilize metrics to deep-dive into issues and uncover business-driving trends and patterns. Regularly present to senior executives regarding the status, strategy, and funding needs of your business.
</li>
<li>Be the face of the business both internally and externally to create momentum, excitement, and results.
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Typical Skills and Experiences:
</b></p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Education: </b>College degree, preferably in a business or technical / engineering discipline, MBA preferred
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Experience: </b>10+ years of professional experience, ideally in complex and dynamic environment ideally in e-commerce, technology, retail or management consulting firm. Experience leading top-performing teams and managing growing teams, strong ability to operate in a matrix environment, collaborate with various stakeholders in different locales and functions effectively, and manage multiple priorities simultaneously.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Knowledge, Abilities and Skills: </b>Familiarity with agile software development practices. Familiarity with direct-to-consumer marketing strategies. Team management, including people management, development process and mechanisms, recruiting, and performance management.
</p>
<p></p>
<p>Other criteria, including leadership skills, competencies and experiences may take precedence.
</p>
<p></p>
<p>Staffing exceptions to the above must be approved by the hiring manager’s leader and HR Business Partner.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Values: </b>Regularly and consistently demonstrates the Nationwide Values and Guiding Behaviors.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Job Conditions:
</b></p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Overtime Eligibility: </b>Not Eligible (exempt)
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Working Conditions: </b>Normal office environment. Some travel may be required.
</p>
