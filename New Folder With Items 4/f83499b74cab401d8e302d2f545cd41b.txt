<h2>Account Services Representative</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>Exciting career opportunities in the language access industry are waiting for you at LanguageLine Solutions. Whether you work in the corporate office, in one of our satellite offices, at our Interpreting Call Centers, or work from home, you can help make a difference in someone’s life every second of every day.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Language Required:
</b></p>
<p></p>
<p>In this position you will work directly in support of the Company's sales team, including the Regional Vice President of Sales (RVP), Account Executives (AE), and the Regional Support Executive (RSE). The role primarily supports pre-sales and revenue generation, with some post-sales responsibilities, by setting up and managing all account information in the Company's databases, by ordering equipment or materials and helping to coordinate implementation activities, and by providing general AE and customer assistance. You may also perform sales follow-up to support ongoing account activity within assigned territories based on need.
</p>
<p>The Account Services Representative will be based in Monterey, California. If possible, you would be willing to travel on a limited basis in the initial two months to meet the regional sales team you will support. Depending on experience, a career path within the North American Sales organization as an Account Executive may be available for you as we build a new team.
</p>
<p><b>THE ROLE
</b></p>
<p>Interact with the RVP, AE, RSE, and customers to set up new and expansion accounts in software systems, including Salesforce.com. [Approximately 40% of time] Interact with the Customer Contracts Administration, Customer Service, Interpreter Resources, Workforce Management, and Information Technology regarding account set-up and to ensure adequate testing is completed prior to customer start-up. Interact with Billing and Accounting teams to ensure correct billing procedures are followed. [Approximately 40% of time] Place orders for equipment or materials, and help coordinate implementations for customers as needed. [Approximately 10% of time] Work with Customer Service to modify account information in the systems based on requests from the RVP, AE, RSE, or customers, such as customer contact information, billing information, prompts/scripts, access codes, etc. [Approximately 5% of time] Provide customized and standard reports to customers and sales teams on an as needed basis, and participate in sales team meetings [Approximately 5% of time]
</p>
<p><b>THE REQUIREMENTS
</b></p>
<p>To be a good fit for this opportunity, you must have:
</p>
<ul>
<li>At least a Bachelor’s Degree, preferably in a business-related field
</li>
<li>At least two years relevant work experience; business/sales customer service experience preferred
</li>
<li>Excellent verbal and written communication skills
</li>
<li>Superior ability to utilize Microsoft Office Suite products, excellent ability to develop and manage Excel spreadsheets, and ability to use customized programs and databases
</li>
</ul>
