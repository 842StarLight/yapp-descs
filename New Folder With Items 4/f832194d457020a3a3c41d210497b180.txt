<h2>Business Development Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>
<p>Bitwise IO is a specialized information technology services firm headquartered in Minneapolis, Minnesota. We deliver enterprise architecture and enterprise software development consulting, with specific expertise in software development projects, blockchain solutions, architecture consulting, and High Performance Computing (HPC).</p>
<p>We are currently looking for a Business Development Manager with both an engineering background as well as business development experience including strong interpersonal and communication skills and a focus on organization. The ideal candidate has a technical degree as well as experience in building business relationships, writing technical proposals, negotiation, and closing deals.</p>
<p>Responsibilities</p>
<ul>
<li>Research current and prospective accounts to target potential future projects</li>
<li>Create and present project proposals to prospective clients</li>
<li>Write first draft technical proposals for software development projects</li>
<li>Manage customer meetings</li>
<li>Price, negotiate, and close customer deals</li>
<li>Maintain relationships with current clients and identify new prospects</li>
<li>Possess a strong understanding of our consulting work and positioning</li>
</ul>
<p>Qualifications</p>
<ul>
<li>BS degree in Computer Science or Engineering</li>
<li>3-5 years experience in technical business development</li>
<li>Superior presentation and communication skills, both written and verbal</li>
<li>Strong proficiency in MS Word, Excel and Powerpoint</li>
<li>Technical skills required to understand and propose software consulting services</li>
<li>Ability to communicate information, whether technical or non-technical to staff members and customers, in a clear and concise manner</li>
</ul>
</div>
<div>
<p>
Bitwise IO offers a flexible work environment with offices in the heart of the north loop of Minneapolis downtown. We offer a great compensation package for developers that want to experience the opportunity to work on exciting, industry leading technology projects.</p>
<p>To apply, send your resume in .pdf format to careers@bitwise.io.</p>
