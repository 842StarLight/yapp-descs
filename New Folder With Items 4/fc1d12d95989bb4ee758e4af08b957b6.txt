<h1 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Job Description</b></h1>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>Position Title: Teller<br>
IV (Head Teller)</p>
<p>Division: Retail/Operations</p>
<p>Reports Directly To: Assistant<br>
Manager<br>
</p>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>BASIC PURPOSE:</b></h2>
<p>This position is responsible for,<br>
but not limited to, being the all-important front line ambassador of<br>
CenterState Bank. Has powerful influence<br>
on the perception of the Branch to new and existing customers. Performs all paying and receiving cash<br>
functions of the Branch. Must possess<br>
excellent verbal and communication skills and work well with deadlines. Must adhere to all of the Bank’s policy and<br>
procedures while maintaining accuracy, consistency, and security. This position supervises the other personal<br>
banker positions and holds the responsibility for their actions, attitude and<br>
conduct.</p>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>ESSENTIAL FUNCTIONS:</b></h2>
<p>This<br>
position is expected to perform the specific duties, tasks, and<br>
responsibilities as outlined below:</p>
<p>In addition to performing the paying and<br>
receiving cash functions of the Branch this position supervises the tellers<br>
behind the line</p>
<p>Performs all duties assigned to a teller as<br>
defined by the job description</p>
<p>Provides on the job training</p>
<p>Monitors teller’s to ensure compliance for<br>
policy and procedures</p>
<p>Assist teller’s in balancing cash drawers and<br>
locating cash discrepancies</p>
<p>Orders cash for the Branch</p>
<p>Performs monthly audits on each teller’s cash<br>
supply</p>
<p>Assumes responsibility for overall service<br>
quality of subordinates to customers</p>
<p>Responsible for performance evaluations and<br>
disciplinary actions if necessary</p>
<p>Delivers Quality Customer Service</p>
<p>Answer phone calls within three rings</p>
<p>Assist customers by guiding them to proper<br>
personnel to address concerns</p>
<p>Attend all Training as defined by Management</p>
<p>Will assist Branch Team in achieving deposit,<br>
loan and investment goals</p>
<p>Cross sells banking Products and services</p>
<p>Actively seeks opportunities for new deposit<br>
growth</p>
<p>Refers business to CenterState Bank subsidiaries</p>
<p>Able to lift heavy coin</p>
<p>Ensure that the Bank’s policies and procedures,<br>
code of conduct, and regulatory guidelines are strictly complied with
</p>
<p>Provide assistance to other employees by<br>
liaising with them through healthy and positive interactions</p>
<p>Be involved in performing marketing<br>
endeavors/efforts
</p>
<p>Continuously update skills by participating in<br>
professional training</p>
<p>Seek opportunities to improve skills through<br>
cross-training offered by the Bank</p>
<p>All other tasks, responsibilities or duties, as<br>
directed by management</p>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>OTHER SKILLS/QUALIFICATIONS:</b></h2>
<p>Proficient<br>
with general office machines and programs to include Microsoft Office Word,<br>
Excel, Outlook, and other software that might be utilized in the department</p>
<p>Ability to use the computer efficiently and the<br>
capacity to learn new software programs as they are rolled out by the Bank</p>
<p>Must have good knowledge of business English,<br>
including spelling and punctuation</p>
<p>Must possess basic English language skills in<br>
order to write and speak clearly, and effectively with coworkers and customers</p>
<p>Must be well-organized, accurate, and attentive<br>
to detail</p>
<p>Must be cooperative and willing to assist<br>
coworkers and customers on a regular basis</p>
<p>Must have effective listening skills<br>
demonstrated by the ability to listen to others talk (without interruption),<br>
understand them, and then propose solutions or make contributions based on the<br>
points made by others</p>
<p>Must possess excellent multi-tasking skills and<br>
be able to function well under pressure</p>
<p>Must be able to remain composed under pressure<br>
and respond to customer and coworker concerns regularly
</p>
<p>Must have a keen eye for detail and follow<br>
instructions to the letter</p>
<p>Must be patient and willing to help others in<br>
solving problems while maintaining a positive attitude at all times</p>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>ESSENTIAL PHYSICAL DEMANDS:</b></h2>
<p>Must<br>
be able to remain seated or work standing in a confined area on a computer for<br>
a minimum of four (4) hours each work day</p>
<p>Must<br>
be able to constantly use hands and fingers to enter data through or on a computer<br>
or answer a phone throughout most of an eight (8) hour shift</p>
<p>Must<br>
be able to walk frequently throughout the day to obtain supplies from other<br>
areas in the Bank and to cover for other employees, on break or on lunch</p>
<p>Must<br>
be able to use hands and fingers to count money throughout each workday</p>
<p>Must<br>
be able to hear and communicate with coworkers and customers throughout the day</p>
<p>May<br>
need to lift up to 25 pounds one to two times a day to perform job</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>The physical<br>
demands described here are representative of those that must be met by an<br>
employee to successfully perform the essential functions of this job. Position<br>
may require regular before/after normal business hours, or regular out-of-town<br>
travel.</p>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>WORK ENVIRONMENT:</b></h2>
<p>Must<br>
be able to function in a professional, business office environment and act and<br>
dress appropriately for same, consistent with the Bank’s grooming and dress<br>
codes</p>
<p>Must<br>
demonstrate excellent interpersonal skills with customers and coworkers</p>
<p>Must<br>
be willing to function as a team member</p>
<p>Must<br>
be willing to demonstrate commitment to CenterState Bank’s mission and goals</p>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>MINIMUM QUALIFICATIONS:</b></h2>
<p>High School diploma or equivalent</p>
<p>One to Two years of job related experience</p>
<p>Supervisory / Management experience</p>
<p>Excellent verbal and written communication<br>
skills</p>
<p>Sales Experience</p>
<p>Problem solver, resourceful, independent</p>
<p>Works at faster than average pace</p>
<p>Optimistic, enthusiastic</p>
<p>Attentive to details</p>
<p>Organized, careful. Multi-tasked</p>
<p>Confident, competitive, communicative</p>
<p>These specifications are general<br>
guidelines based on the minimum experience normally considered essential to the<br>
satisfactory performance of this position. The requirements listed above<br>
are representative of the knowledge, skill and/or ability required to perform<br>
the position in a satisfactory manner. Individual abilities may result in<br>
some deviation from these guidelines.</p>
