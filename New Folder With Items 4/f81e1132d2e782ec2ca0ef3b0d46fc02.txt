<h2>Sr. Process Control Engineer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p><b>Portfolio Business</b><b><i>:</i></b> Huber Engineered Materials</p>
<p>
J.M. Huber Corporation is one of the largest privately held, family-owned companies in the United States. Established in 1883, we are a diversified, global supplier of specialty and commodity chemicals, hydrocolloid solutions, engineered wood products and natural resources to customers spanning a wide variety of industries. With approximately $2.3 billion in sales and 4,000 employees worldwide, we have a material presence in more than 20 countries.</p>
<div>
<div>
<div>
Position Summary
</div>
<div><div>Improve plant capacity and efficiency for all GCC Plants though Process Control Optimization. Process Control Optimization will include the development of process control algorithms for existing processes as well as capital projects and manufacturing expansions.
</div></div>
</div>
<div>
<div>Principal Duties &amp; Responsibilities
</div>
<div><ul>
<li>Develop processes with safety and environmental performance and compliance in mind</li>
<li>
Develop and implement engineering standards for all GCC plants</li>
<li>
Work with Plant Personnel to troubleshoot process control issues</li>
<li>
Work with Plant Personnel to optimize existing process control logic</li>
<li>
Directs contractor activates during project execution
</li>
<li>Evaluates new process control technologies</li>
<li>
Manages projects and supervises sub-contractors</li>
</ul></div>
</div>
<div>
<div>
Decision Making Authority
</div>
<div><ul>
<li>Works on projects that are identified and prioritized by the GCC Management team</li>
<li>
Evaluate and develop processing control processes</li>
<li>
Identify plant process control improvements</li>
<li>
Accurately determines the cost of capital and makes recommendations on expenditures based on the analysis of several alternatives</li>
</ul></div>
</div>
<div>
<div>
Specialized/Technical Knowledge or Required Skills
</div>
<div><ul>
<li>A minimum of a 4-year engineering degree at an accredited university with at least 7 years Process Control experience in manufacturing – Electrical or Instrumentation Engineering background a plus
</li>
<li>Working knowledge and programming of the following types of automation hardware: Allen Bradley PLC’s, Contrologix 5000, FactoryTalk HMI, as well as other power distribution and control circuit development and wiring systems such as VFD’s.</li>
<li>
Ability to install and troubleshoot Ethernet/IP and DeviceNet systems</li>
<li>
Ability to manage data collecting and reporting of database software systems using Historian or VanatagePoint is a plus</li>
<li>
Proficient with ladder logic control, PLC operations and systems interface, instrumentation schematics, one-line diagrams, electrical drawings, etc</li>
<li>
Proficient in Word, Excel, PowerPoint, AutoCAD design
</li>
<li>Must have the ability to manage time and priorities in order to effectively handle multiple competing projects with tight deadlines and budget requirements</li>
<li>
Ability to practice good management skills required to direct and supervise contractors</li>
<li>
Demonstrated ability to communicate, plan, organize, and document project requirements</li>
<li>
Good communication skills and team skills</li>
<li>
Experience in mineral processing is preferred</li>
<li>
Must be able to travel up to 20% of the time from base location to support other GCC Plants</li>
<li>
Must be able to read and comprehend company safety manual policies, SOP’s, MSDS, and various production schedules/ reports and engineering drawings.</li>
</ul></div>
