<h2>Director of Sales - Discovery Golf</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Position Summary
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div>In the U.S., Discovery Golf will invest in Golf Digest to grow and enhance its digital offering utilizing Discovery’s experience in developing digital platforms and creating compelling and engaging content. Importantly, Discovery Golf will be creating a global sales force in partnership with the PGA TOUR to offer advertisers and sponsors a global golf platform with which to tell their stories.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
The Director of Sales in Discovery Golf’s commercial team will be responsible for advertising and sponsorship revenue generation for the Golf Digest property and will be tasked with developing the commercial strategy to drive revenue growth for the business.<br>
S/he will be expected to develop direct relationships with brands and agencies and build a portfolio of business. This person will strategically identify potential clients, develop a relationship, pitch advertising and sponsorship opportunities, and close business.<br>
This role will initially be responsible for the North American region and is expected to work closely with Discovery Golf’s international properties to help maximize revenue across all properties.</div>
<div>
<br>
This person must have a strong digital sales background with experience in closing complex, multi-platform deals. A proven track record in selling digital advertising and sponsorship with experience in selling sports properties is required.
</div>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities
</b></h2>
<div><div><div><div>1. Achieve revenue and budget objectives for digital advertising and sponsorship sales on Discovery’s Golf platforms, specifically focused on Golf Digest<br>
2. Develop a go-to-market strategy for Golf Digest’s digital assets<br>
3. Build a strong network of agency relationships to drive agency led business<br>
4. Manage client and agency relationships to ensure year on year growth of revenue<br>
5. Create and develop new advertising and sponsorship concepts, enabling incremental revenue growth<br>
6. Coordinate and manage relationships with agency resellers and other golf stakeholders such as PGA TOUR to ensure there is a coordinated approach to sales<br>
7. Enable and encourage team coordination and continuous communication across the sales team in all Discovery global regions, specifically with GOLFTV
</div></div></div></div>
