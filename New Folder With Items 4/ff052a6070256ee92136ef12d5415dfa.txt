<h2>Technical Trainer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p><b>This position can be #remote or located at one of our offices in San Francisco, CA or Manchester, NH.</b><br>
<br>
Sitecore is the global leader in experience management software that combines content management, commerce, and customer insights.<br>
<br>
The Sitecore Experience CloudTM empowers marketers to deliver personalized content in real time and at scale across every channel—before, during, and after a sale.<br>
<br>
More than 5,200 brands––including American Express, Carnival Cruise Lines, Kimberly-Clark, and L’Oréal––have trusted Sitecore to deliver the personalised interactions that delight audiences, build loyalty, and drive revenue.</p>
<b>
Position Summary</b><br>
<br>
We are looking for enthusiastic, passionate and outgoing advocate for the Sitecore Learning Transformation team.<br>
<br>
You will be part of a global training team, delivering outstanding training experiences to our customers and partners throughout the US and Canada.<br>
<br>
The role involves a minimum 50% travel throughout North America for in-person trainings, along with training in your local area, and online, instructor-led trainings.<br>
<br>
You must demonstrate an ability presenting, mentoring and teaching to a variety of audiences including business users, marketers, developers and administrators.<br>
<br>
The successful candidate has demonstrated being the lead on several successful implementations of the Sitecore Experience Platform and preferable an advocate in the Sitecore community.<br>
<br>
Beside that it is expected that you have a passion for teaching, and mentoring others in the same industry.<br>
<br>
<b>Experience</b><br>
<br>
<ul>
<li>2+ years of experience in implementation of the Sitecore Experience Platform and knowledge of the Sitecore product family</li>
<li>Industry experience in Web Content Management and Commerce</li>
<li>Experience in #C and ASP.NET</li>
<li>Solid understanding of the Microsoft .NET technology stack</li>
<li>JavaScript coding and CSS experience is a plus</li>
<li>Excellent presentation and mentoring skills</li>
<li>Demonstrate the ability to explain concepts to both technical and non-technical audiences</li>
<li>Teaching/Training experience is an advantage</li>
</ul>
<br>
<b>
Responsibilities</b><br>
<br>
<ul>
<li>Instruct the Sitecore Certified Developer and Commerce courses, including virtual classrooms.</li>
<li>Be an advocate of the Sitecore product family</li>
<li>Advise customers and partners on how to best use Sitecore products</li>
<li>Assist other members of the Sitecore Learning Transformation team and Education Service team in the generation of new and upcoming training materials/best practices</li>
<li>Participation in online developer communities</li>
<li>Being a subject matter expert on the Sitecore Experience Platform</li>
<li>Miscellaneous minor technical tasks as need arises</li>
</ul>
<br>
Sitecore pays competitive salaries and offers great opportunities within a booming company for growth both professionally and technically. We are seeking people located in the US. The role requires a minimum of 50% travel.<br>
