<h2>Product Manager, Analytics (Solar)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>WHO WE ARE</b></p>
<p>
We are Power Factors, developer of the world’s leading cloud platform for renewable energy companies. Our SaaS platform, DRIVE, is used to manage over 25 Gigawatts of renewable energy across the planet (enough to power approximately 1.5 million homes). By driving down the operations costs of wind and solar power plants, we’re tackling one of the world’s most important challenges: making renewable energy the world’s leading source of power. Our company is going through accelerated growth, fueled by the unprecedented transition to clean, renewable energy.</p>
<p>
The DRIVE platform continues to gain traction throughout the renewable energy market. Our platform collects millions of IIoT and other data points, cleans up the data, detects issues, identifies leading indicators to predict future issues, and adds full advanced analytic capabilities on top of this rich, high value dataset. In addition to issue detection and analytics, DRIVE provides the tools needed to turn analytic insight into action, such as case management and work order management.</p>
<p>
To our clients, we deliver excellent customer service and an exceptional system of engagement that provides the results they are looking for: lower operations costs and higher output from their renewable energy power plants. We are an agile development company – big enough to make an impact, but small enough to move quickly and execute in a growing industry, taking advantage of rapidly evolving technologies. To our employees, we provide a culture that values respect, teamwork, transparency, and achievement.</p>
<p><b>
THE ROLE</b></p>
<p>
Power Factors is growing rapidly and we are looking for a driven candidate to own the IoT analytics capabilities for our Drive platform. The successful will have a mix of Product Management or Product Owner experience with understanding of Data Science. As the Product Manager, Analytics you are the glue between customer performance engineering teams, internal analytics data scientists, and the software development team by turning market needs into products that pleases our users.</p>
<p>
Your role will add value for customers and end users by creating a product backlog that reflects the company’s overall product strategy and long-term roadmap. You will be focused on your product’s adoption and the successful realization of product features that solve customers’ problems. More precisely, your mandate consist in analyzing multiple sources of information, owning key features from development to release, and creating organizational transparency into a product’s prioritization and engineering efforts.</p>
<p>
The successful candidate has relevant experience with enterprise software in the field of Cloud/SaaS, Big Data Analytics, or Industrial IoT along with technical knowledge of Solar/Wind assets in the Renewables industry.</p>
