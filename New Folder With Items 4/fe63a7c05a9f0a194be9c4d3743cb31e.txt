<h2>Purchasing & Fulfillment Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Job Summary</b></p>
<p>
Work closely with a production team to ensure quality and productivity objectives are exceeded. Work with Plant Manager and production employees to develop new and creative ideas to improve productivity in a team environment. Communicate regularly with Office and Sales staff regarding production demand and delivery schedules to ensure the highest level of customer satisfaction. This position assists in maintaining a safe, efficient, and productive work environment.
</p>
<ul>
<li>Develop and implement efficient and effective daily production planning and scheduling. Communicate production schedules to lead person or production staff in a timely and effective manner. Direct workforce to appropriate location for daily production.</li>
<li>Directing and coordinating activities of workers engaged in dispatching, warehousing, inventory management, and shipping to facilitate a timely and accurate order-fulfillment process.</li>
<li>Assists the Plant Manager to ensure that company quality objectives are being met on a consistent basis. Train production workers on quality assurance procedures and policy.
</li>
<li>Monitor, plan, and place orders for raw materials based on projected production requirements as directed by Plant Manager.</li>
<li>Monitor, reorder, and purchase empty bags and plant supplies needed for inventory replenishment for the facility and other TCC</li>
<li>Collaborate with technical team to source and Purchase raw materials through reliable vendors that deliver consistent quality at competitive pricing.</li>
<li>Create purchase orders, monitor delivery dates, including calls and emails to suppliers for costing, status updates and expediting.</li>
<li>Proactively manage obsolete and/or aged inventory</li>
<li>Support cycle counts and annual physical inventory process.</li>
<li>Ability to multi task, willingness to do multiple jobs and learn new processes.
</li>
<li>Contribute to a cross-functional team, including both plant personnel and corporate resources.</li>
<li>Collaborate with corporate resources to ensure to timely and effective new product roll-out. Communicate any potential or imminent roadblocks so they can be addressed quickly.</li>
<li>Collaborate with corporate resources to maintain and grow the success of e-commerce program(s). Communicate issues and successes in order to improve fulfillment and margin.</li>
<li>Provide assistance to dispatch function as required to ensure timely and efficient delivery service for the organization and its customers.</li>
<li>Assist in entering daily production MAS 500 in a timely and accurate manner. Fill out production reports, accurately recording downtime and reason codes.</li>
<li>Assist in entering all raw material and finished goods receiving into MAS 500 in a timely and accurate manner. Conduct physical inventories/cycle counts as required to ensure accurate perpetual inventory.</li>
<li>Develop, implement, and verify standard operating procedures (SOP) for all job-related tasks.</li>
<li>Maintain effective working relationships with company employees, suppliers, and customers. Promote and maintain the professional image and reputation of the company and ensure timely problem resolution and customer satisfaction.</li>
<li>Other duties as assigned by TCC Management.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Minimum Qualifications</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor s degree in business, finance, engineering, logistics, or related field required</li>
<li>5 years supervisor experience and 3-5 years purchasing experience in a manufacturing industry preferred
</li>
<li>Must have excellent organizational and communication skills</li>
<li>Computer proficient and strong knowledge of PC Window s environment, including Microsoft Office (Outlook, Excel, Word). Ability to learn inventory forecasting software and ERP software.</li>
<li>Must be able to multi-task in an accurate and timely manner with superior time management skills</li>
<li>Must be self-motivated with demonstrated ability to work successfully without close supervision, as well as in a collaborative team environment</li>
<li>Proven analytical and problem-solving skills with strong attention to detail.</li>
</ul>

<p><b>About Us</b></p>
<p>
Akona Manufacturing®, a TCC Materials® company, is a leading manufacturer of spec blended construction products located in Rosemount, Minnesota. Akona Manufacturing provides the Upper Midwest and Canada with pre-mixed liquid, mastic, and powdered specialty products including concrete repair, floor preparation, tile setting, and specialty products. We are seeking a Production Worker to carry out various jobs in the manufacturing plant. The ideal candidate will be reliable, detailed orientated, self-motivated, and have the ability to understand and follow detailed instruction/directions.</p>
