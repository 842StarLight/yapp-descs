<h2>Sr. Data Analyst, Commercial Analytics</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">The Senior Analyst, Commercial Analytics uses analytical, financial and quantitative methods to understand, track and measure customer behavior. This position is responsible for assembling data, creating reporting, and leveraging statistical methodologies to developed recommendations in support of critical business decisions. Essential Functions: • Coordinate with all levels of sales and marketing teams as well as vendors to gather, analyze, summarize, prepare and present recommendations and solutions regarding sales results, business plans, trended future requirements and profitability improvement strategies. • Use various predictive analytics techniques to analyze current and historical data. • Compile, validate, analyze, translate and present performance-tracking information. • Provide insight to sales &amp; marketing personnel on market &amp; organizational trends that will support brand performance. • Champion and support adoption of BI tools across organization. Monitor usage statistics for BI tools, working with customers to provide input to future enhancements. Provide data validation and testing for upgrades, provide ad hoc training to end users. • Support sales &amp; marketing objectives to help the organization develop, implement and meet its strategic goals. • Compile and coordinate use of in-house and vendor provided sales &amp; marketing databases. • Track and document progress of sales and marketing programs based on research results. • Evaluate front-end and back-end promotions and analyzes customer purchasing/prescribing patterns. • Other duties may be assigned. The work environment characteristics and physical requirements described here are representative of those an employee encounters while performing the essential functions of this job.
