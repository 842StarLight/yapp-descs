<br>Job Description
<br>---------------
<br><br>
Invoca is seeking an experienced Events Marketing Manager to own Invoca's event marketing strategy and execution. The ideal candidate will have exceptional leadership and communication skills, a strong understanding of marketing and operations, and validated experience executing best-in-class events. We're looking for a passionate individual to drive our event efforts by making them eye-opening and unforgettable. This is a fantastic opportunity to drive the strategy, execution, and value of trade shows / conferences, local field events, company conferences, and internal events nationally. This highly strategic position works very closely with internal customers and is a valued partner to the organization.
<br><br>
This opportunity requires someone with excellent organization and planning skills who can work autonomously with minimal supervision, but can also work effectively with others collaborating within a team environment. Seeking candidates who thrive in a very high-growth, high-energy corporate environment that demands and rewards leadership, initiative, resourcefulness, and individual contribution.
<br><br>
----------------
<br>Responsibilities
<br>----------------
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Oversee yearly events calendar and budget and all operations related to these.</li>
<li>Plan, execute, and lead all aspects of conference sponsorships, including coordination of logistics such as equipment, catering, marketing collateral, promotional give-aways, and company travel requirements</li>
<li>Work with sales leadership to develop strategy and oversee execution of territory-based regional sales events</li>
<li>Plan and execute Invoca's annual conference in Santa Barbara</li>
<li>Lead and execute bi-annual internal company wide events</li>
<li>Work with marketing demand generation team to lead all aspects of the pre, during, and post event nurture and engagement programs</li>
<li>Run individual event budgets, with focus on delivering high quality leads and generating pipeline, while maximizing the value of each event</li>
<li>Coordinate external vendor relationships, inclusive of negotiating budgets and contracts</li>
<li>Partner with internal departments to ensure each event meets the overall goals of the company</li>
</ul>
---------------------------------
<br><br>
<b>Required Skills &amp; Experience:</b><br>
---------------------------------
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Experience in managing event participation/sponsorship contracts and fulfillment of deliverables, including booth selection, lead retrieval, bag drops, advertisements, etc.</li>
<li>Direct pre-event operations, including: venue selection, budget creation and forecasting, outstanding contract negotiation, booth preparations, event staffing, signage creation, ordering collateral and promotional items, purchasing and shipping supplies, housing and travel coordination, on-site services, etc.</li>
<li>Experience managing the multi-channel event experience to boost event engagement and return on investment, including email, direct mail, web, and social marketing programs</li>
<li>Experience running large scale events (ex customer conferences), including planning, execution, and reporting.</li>
<li>Strong ability to operate in a start-up environment and manage a high-volume event calendar.</li>
<li>Able to work in a complex, highly collaborative team environment</li>
<li>Excellent written and spoken communication with the ability to write marketing collateral in multiple tones/voices as appropriate</li>
<li>Outstanding organization skills and be deadline and schedule oriented, yet able to flex with dynamically changing priorities.</li>
<li>Demonstrated ability to be poised and effective during fast paced situations; ability to anticipate issues and have (or quickly develop) contingency plans.</li>
<li>Strong knowledge of the tradeshow industry and 'must-attend' digital marketing events, knowledge of B2C industry-specific (Insurance, Financial Services, Healthcare, etc) events a plus</li>
<li>Min 5 years of successful field marketing experience for B2B high tech, SaaS and/or Cloud industry.</li>
<li>Experience with Salesforce, Marketo, and event management platforms</li>
<li>Travel: Approximately 1-2 events per month.</li>
<li>Bachelor's degree in marketing, communications, or related field</li>
</ul>
