<h2>Social Media Engagement Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>About LionDesk: </b></p>
<p>LionDesk is a fast growing and thriving startup that provides an integrated CRM platform to make it easy and affordable for sales and marketing professionals to connect, communicate with, and close more deals leveraging today's most sophisticated technology.</p>
<p>LionDesk has an open API development platform that integrates with 100s of the best in class tools so professionals can run their operations from a single centralized hub. For more information visit www.liondesk.com</p>
<p><b>Position Overview: </b></p>
<p>Our new team member will join an established technology company who is continuing to grow, they will have the opportunity to learn innovative skills within the real estate technology industry.</p>
<p>As LionDesk’s <b>Social Media Engagement Specialist</b>, you will be responsible for managing our Facebook presence. We are looking for a candidate who has technical aptitude, great writing skills, and superb follow through.</p>
<p>This is a unique position that will be within our Support department to communicate efficiently with our LionDesk Facebook group on updates on our product, be the voice of the company, and keep a pulse on our user engagement.</p>
<p><b>**Position is PT (20 hrs. a week) at our awesome Carlsbad office**</b></p>
<p><b>Responsibilities: </b></p>
<p>· Daily management of LionDesk Facebook presence, post’s and responses.</p>
<p>· Monitor LionDesk’s social media channels, initiate conversations with and respond to community comments referencing established brand and community guidelines.</p>
<p>· Consistent communication with Support Hero QA Coordinator on updating online portal for bug updates within Beamer to maintain ongoing communication with customers of duration for fix along with update once issues are resolved on social media platforms.</p>
<p>· Work cross functionally with Support QA Coordinator and Support Operations to develop and distribute LionDesk quarterly newsletters and feedback reports on items. I.e. bugs that were submitted and resolved vs. pending bugs.</p>
<p>· Monitor LionDesk Facebook customer conversation and develop recommendations for client as appropriate.</p>
<p>· Incorporate best social practices to drive engagement.</p>
<p><b>Qualifications: </b></p>
<p>· Academic background in communication, marketing, or any related program preferred.</p>
<p>· Experience with CRM software</p>
<p>· Experience in the main social media tools and channels, with a cutting-edge interest in new trends and releases.</p>
<p>· Excellent writing and interpersonal communication skills.</p>
<p>· Ability to be organized, take initiative, and follow up independently.</p>
<p>· Willingness and flexibility to work various shifts including after hours and weekends.</p>
<p>· Problem Solving: Identifies and solves problems in a timely manner, gather and analyze information skillfully, develop alternative solutions. Strong customer service orientation</p>
