<h2>Insurance Advisor - Education</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p><b>POSITION SUMMARY: </b>This position is responsible for growing an enterprise in the early learning and education niche for an established independent agency with a 35-year history in this market. This sales professional will utilize his/her phone skills, presentation acumen, relationship development, attention to detail, CRM management, urgent follow up and networking skills to further develop a revenue book that exceeds growth targets for the agency.</p>
<p><b>
IDEAL CANDIDATE: </b>An Insurance Advisor with CCIG is motivated and competitive. Successful candidates in this role need to have a true entrepreneurial spirit and a work ethic to deliver results. This is not a high-pressure sales environment. We’re interested in individuals with excellent relationship-building skills, the adaptability to attract new business and the ability to articulate resource needs and collaborate with a wide variety of constituents. The ideal candidate will be able to develop new revenue that would not otherwise exist. Successful candidates have previous sales experience with a demonstrated ability to convert leads into clients through a relatively short sales cycle (less than 1 year).
</p>
<p><b>KEY RESPONSIBILITIES:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>New Business Development:
<ul>
<li>Develops and acquires new business revenue through sales to new and existing clients with emphasis on early learning and educational institutions.</li>
<li>Works with an existing service team to grow enterprise through business development and retention strategies.</li>
<li>Ability to interact with all levels of buyers with an emphasis on educators and owner.</li>
<li>Able to influence outcomes beyond the scope of their individual function.</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>Manage Client Relationships:
<ul>
<li>Manages overall client relationships, supported by day-to-day account supervision.</li>
<li>Prepares and implements an individual business plan annually.</li>
<li>Provides service to clients according to their needs, retaining them as clients.</li>
</ul>
</li>
</ul>
<p><b>EDUCATION, EXPERIENCE &amp; SKILLS:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor's degree, OR high school diploma/GED and 3-5 years’ insurance production experience.</li>
<li>3+ years related experience</li>
<li>Must possess extensive knowledge of the insurance industry and our products and services</li>
<li>Acts independently with minimal direction.</li>
<li>Strong communication and interpersonal skills.</li>
<li>Must have or obtain a State of Colorado P&amp;C Insurance License to practice.</li>
<li>Must provide a copy of their most recent two production reports.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>EXPECTED HOURS OF WORK: </b>This is a full-time position, and hours of work and days are Monday through Friday, 8:00 a.m. to 4:30 p.m. This position regularly requires long hours and frequent weekend work. Must be available for emergency on-call support.</p>
<p><b>
WORK ENVIRONMENT:</b> This job operates in a professional office environment. This role routinely uses standard office equipment such as computers, tablets, phones, multi-function printers and fax machines.</p>
<p><b>
PHYSICAL DEMANDS: </b>The physical demands described here are representative of those that must be met by an employee to successfully perform the essential functions of this job. While performing the duties of this job, the employee is regularly required to talk and/or listen, frequently stand; walk; sit for extended periods of time, use hands to handle or feel; and reach with hands and arms. The individual should be capable of lifting large items up to 40 pounds.</p>
<p><b>
TRAVEL:</b> Travel is primarily local during the business day, although some out-of-the-area and overnight travel may be expected. Out of town travel is minimum.</p>
