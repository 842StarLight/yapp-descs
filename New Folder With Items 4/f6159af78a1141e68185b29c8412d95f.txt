<h2>Manager, Merchandise Reporting</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div><b>Job Overview:</b></div>
<div></div>
<div>The Manager, Merchandise Reporting plays a lead role in the prioritization and development of corporate reporting not available in standardized reporting systems by using various technical systems and applications. The Manager is responsible for reviewing/prioritizing/sizing reporting requests and determines if the requests are best solved through maintenance to enterprise reporting, new enterprise reporting, or ad-hoc, Excel based reports. The Manager will report to the Senior Director of Merchant &amp; Vendor Operations &amp; Support and will have direct responsibility over a team of Business Analysts overseeing the team’s projects, deliverables and workload. The Manager is the primary liaison with Merchant and Macy’s Technical (MTECH) teams and is the point person for requested projects. Perform other duties as assigned.</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div><b>Essential Functions:</b></div>
<ul>
<li><div>Assists in development and delivery of reporting strategy, streamlining ad-hoc reports for the organization.</div></li>
<li><div>
Supervise the improvement and enhancement of established databases, processes and reports based on evolving business needs.</div></li>
<li><div>
Oversee the development of customized reporting projects as well as enterprise reporting solutions.
</div></li>
<li><div>Manage the development of dashboards and report in business intelligence tools (Tableau, Power BI, Cognos Analytics, etc.).</div></li>
<li><div>
Develop large scale reports, reporting tools and analyses to understand significant variances to plan and/or history while providing relevant metrics and filtering capabilities.</div></li>
<li><div>
Actively pursue expertise in established Macy’s analytical tools, especially: Standard Reports, cube-based self-serve analytics, Cognos/Rubi, and SQL querying of the Macys’ Enterprise Data Warehouse.
</div></li>
<li><div>Acts as the primary liaison with site/digital merchant and senior management teams.</div></li>
<li><div>
Regular, dependable attendance and punctuality.</div></li>
</ul>
