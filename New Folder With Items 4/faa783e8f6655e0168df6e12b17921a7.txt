<p><b>Position Summary</b></p>
<p>Under general supervision from the VP of Maintenance, manages the operation and maintenance of all the tractors and tank trailers ensuring assets are maintained in accordance with federal (DOT), state and local regulations and Company policies.</p>
<p><b>Duties and Responsibilities: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Oversee shops and equipment in the assigned region to ensure a safe, efficient, timely, cost effective and enforce company maintenance program.</li>
<li>Enforce and comply with all established safety policies and standards to include OSHA regulations and company policies, and the identification, use, and proper procedures for handling or disposal of hazardous waste.</li>
<li>Monitor and address all issues concerning corrective action of direct reports and employee performance and be accessible to all areas of the region.</li>
<li>Forecast manpower to meet workload.</li>
<li>Perform oversight of mechanical condition of vehicles and equipment, including diagnosing component malfunctions, and initiating repair actions.</li>
<li>Manage special equipment projects assigned in region.</li>
<li>Review scheduled weekly, monthly, quarterly and annual preventive maintenance.</li>
<li>Properly annotate all maintenance performed on prescribed forms and enter data into TMT and other data collection systems.</li>
<li>Manage all relationships with service vendors in assigned region.</li>
<li>Provide support to the terminal operations team regarding all equipment-related issues.</li>
<li>Directly responsible for cost controls, equipment, process improvement, and contributing to the Company’s revenue growth..</li>
<li>Perform administrative functions’, including timesheet approval, repair order maintenance and compliance documentation.</li>
<li>Utilize sound judgment to determine when vehicle repairs should be completed by an outside agency that is authorized and qualified to perform such work.</li>
<li>Comply with all applicable company policies and procedures and federal/state/local laws and regulations.</li>
<li>Produce and maintain accurate inspection forms and their records.</li>
<li>Regularly Communicate with Terminal Managers, Operations, and Safety</li>
<li>Perform additional duties as assigned.</li>
</ul>
