<h2>Showroom Coordinator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Description</b></h3>
<div>
<i>Position at HBF</i>
</div>
<p></p>
<p><b>HBF,</b> an industry leader in office furniture design and manufacturing, and a top brand in the <b>HNI Corporation </b>enterprise, is in search of a<br>
<b>Showroom Coordinator - HBF</b> to join our team in Hickory, NC!</p>
<p><i>
Some of the essential aspects of the position include:</i></p>
<ul><li>
<p><b>
Primary Purpose:</b> Manage the complete customer service experience in the showroom; inside sales support, sales fulfillment and project management.</p>
<p>
Manage and maintain the showroom. Service customer needs and requests daily including walk-ins. Answer phones, provide quotes, fill sample and literature requests, and fill textile bags and other sales materials. Maintain appearance of the HBF showroom. Maintain showroom office inventory, mail/fax distribution, and maintain sample chair log, weekly textile and sample inventory, showroom furniture inventory.</p>
</li></ul>
<p><i>
What it takes:</i></p>
<ul><li>
<ul>
<li>
Creates positive and brand consistent customer experience.</li>
<li>
Maintain product knowledge and business literacy to service walk-in customers</li>
<li>
Coordinate events in the showroom. Schedule caters, sets up and clean up, per sales team request.</li>
<li>
Maintains showroom calendar and effectively facilitates schedules in showroom. Notifies sales team of major events, installs, etc. Track visits and traffic.</li>
<li>
Constantly seeks new ideas to make the space more functional for the needs of the showroom.</li>
</ul>
<ul><li>
Ensure all areas of the facility (paint, carpet, equipment, electrical, etc.) are maintained to the highest quality standards. Schedule contractors for major installs and as needed to resolve any identified needs and communicate any need for support.</li></ul>
<ul>
<li>
Handles IT set up within showroom.</li>
<li>
Maintains overall appearance of showroom, including management of cleaning service, flower/plant service and building services.</li>
<li>
Give showroom tours daily.</li>
<li>
Promote the showroom through special mailings and events.</li>
<li>
Apply organizational policies and assures adherence to departmental procedures.</li>
<li>
Maintains and orders all office supplies, equipment supplies and café supplies.</li>
<li>
Effectively manages facility invoices. Drives showroom cost efficiencies.</li>
<li>
Handles incoming and outgoing correspondence and packages.</li>
<li>
<p><b>
Minimum Qualifications and or experience required:</b></p>
<p>
Strong customer service skills</p>
<p>
Strong communication, organization and analytical skills.</p>
<p>
Industry experience – project management, showroom management, facilities management or interior design experience.</p>
<p>
Understands industry language, manufacturer/dealer structure, the role of A&amp;D in selling process.</p>
<p>
Experience with furniture or finishes sales, furniture or finishes installation or facilities maintenance issues a plus.</p>
</li>
<li>
<div>Giza, CAP or other computer design program experience a plus.</div>
<br>
</li>
</ul>
</li></ul>
