<h2>Commercial Design Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p>Occupational Summary:</p>
<p>
Commercial Design &amp; Project Coordinator’s role is to produce the Project designs, validations, drawings quotations and time line schedule for the Contract Sales Department. The coordinator leads the support team for the Contract Division Sales Team. This position develops the project quotations, executes approved projects into the FLS SYSTEM and organization of the installation process.</p>
<p>
Essential Functions:</p>
<div><ul>
<li>
Interface with Sales staff for preliminary project requirements and identification of sales services that are needed for each opportunity.</li>
<li>
Record design hours on all projects completed.</li>
<li>
Record Project Numbers in Shared File for identification of all projects going through FLS Contract Sales department.</li>
<li>
Assist in collecting the Sales Funnels with this project file numbering system.</li>
<li>
Daily use of CAD, 2020 Giza, CAP Worksheet and /or CET design specification software</li>
<li>
Utilize software systems such as Microsoft Office – EXCEL, WORD, POWER POINT, SHARED DOCS</li>
<li>
Provide furniture drawings, plan and 3-D; specifications, finishes, validations, installation drawings and BOM breakdown for Contract Sales and client review.</li>
<li>
Attend vendor training, updates and any continuing education opportunities.</li>
<li>
Responsible for maintenance of library materials and finishes.</li>
<li>
Collect all sign off paperwork (quotes, validations, etc) and file in the project file.</li>
<li>
Complete time sheets and other administrative responsibilities.</li>
<li>
Assist with updates of the Contract Showroom</li>
<li>
Assist in projects installations: Attending weekly meetings with Design Supervisor to discuss projected billing and scheduling of the installations</li>
<li>
Complete punch lists, warranty orders and service calls to ensure communication to clients occurs in a timely manner.</li>
<li>
Occasional client site visits will be required</li>
</ul></div>
