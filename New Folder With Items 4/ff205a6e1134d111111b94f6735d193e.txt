<p><b>
POSITION PURPOSE:</b></p>
<p>The Director, Sales Development (SDR Team) will be responsible for leading a Sales Development Rep team that develops and expands our key markets. The ideal candidate has 3-5 years’ experience leading an SDR team focused on B2B enterprise software sales. This is an opportunity to join a high growth company that is rapidly expanding into new markets. We are looking for a high-performance self-starter who wants to take the team to the next level.
</p>
<p>The SDR team is responsible for sales growth as measured by the creation of net new sales opportunities and pipeline. Ownership of new business prospecting, territory planning, campaign ideation, pre-call planning, and discovery meetings. The director is responsible for hiring and training a high output team of top performers. Experience in B2B software sales to manufacturing, healthcare, and/or financial services industry targets is desired but not required. This role should understand how to penetrate new markets and launch new product effectively.
</p>
<p>This role reports to the CMO and works closely with sales leadership daily.
</p>
<p></p>
