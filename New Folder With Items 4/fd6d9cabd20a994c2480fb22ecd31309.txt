<h2>Senior Client Success Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>MedBridge was recently awarded in Seattle Magazine's 2018 Leaders in Health Care Awards and the 2017 Tech Impact Awards. We were also named one of Seattle's top companies by Inc. 5000 2018!</p>
<p>
MedBridge is a fast-growing company at the intersection of online education and healthcare. Our online educational ecosystem empowers physicians and clinicians to stay up-to-date with the latest research, refine their clinical skills, and facilitate improved patient outcomes. Our customers include major hospitals and clinics, encompassing thousands of healthcare providers. We wake up every day to build technology that will directly improve people's lives.</p>
<p>
Are you looking for a job that has a positive impact on the world - somewhere you can contribute, continue to develop and also make a real difference in people's lives? We are looking for a passionate and experienced Sr. Account Manager who wants to make a big impact while experiencing the excitement of a fast-growing company at the intersection of online education and healthcare.</p>
<p>
In this role you will:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Own the client experience at MedBridge from launch onward, including renewal and growth.</li>
<li>
Successfully onboard clients to foster long-term partnerships and product utilization.</li>
<li>
Take client satisfaction to the next level by acting as your clients' internal advocate.</li>
<li>
Create meaningful and long-lasting relationships at all levels of client organizations to drive renewals and growth at strategic accounts.</li>
<li>
Engage with new clients via web demonstration to ensure successful implementation and activation.</li>
<li>
Drive feedback-based technical enhancements and content creation with our clients and your team members.</li>
<li>
Develop client referrals through providing exceptional and engaging client service.</li>
<li>
Collaborate with Sales to secure account expansions.</li>
<li>
Conduct regular business reviews with the leadership team of enterprise clients.</li>
</ul>
<p>
About You:</p>
<ul>
<li>
4+ years of relevant experience in enterprise account management or client success management.</li>
<li>
Bachelor's degree is required.</li>
<li>
Exceptional communication, organization, and prioritization skills.</li>
<li>
Expert in proactively driving activation, process change, business value, and delight among enterprise clients.</li>
<li>
Adept at securing renewals early by making strong case for ROI.</li>
<li>
Experienced at driving expansion after initial implementation or pilot, in collaboration with sales.</li>
<li>
Comfortable driving conversations with all levels of customers, from individual clinicians to the executive suite.</li>
<li>
Demonstrated success at turning around client relationships and reframing value as your product evolves.</li>
<li>
Data-driven decision-maker: confident in analyzing data, spotting trends, and identifying prioritized next steps.</li>
<li>
A track record of teamwork and willingness to roll up one's sleeves to get the job done.</li>
<li>
An ability to hit the ground sprinting in a fast-paced environment where constant innovation is required.</li>
<li>
Ability to travel up to 5% - 15%, to include overnight travel for meetings.</li>
</ul>
