<h2>Guest Service Coordinator Leader</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Job Description</p>
<p>The Guest Services is clearly the first and final impression for all clients. We are looking for a Team Player who will support and collaborate with ALL other team members.</p>
<p>This individual performs a variety of guest services at the front desk and retail space. Candidates must possess strong customer service and client relationship skills, have retail sales experience with the ability to multi-task in a fast-paced environment, all while maintaining a their professionalism at all times. We are looking for a loyal, long term employee.</p>
<p>Duties/Responsibilities:</p>
<p>Guest Services staff members must be able to prioritize all tasks in order to maintain superb customer service while maintaining accuracy in scheduling, transaction services receipts and retail sales. Staff members greet and check in clients, answer phones, schedule appointments, confirm and rebook appointments, cash out clients, keep/update records, maintain a clean work-space and perform daily opening and closing.</p>
<p>To perform this job successfully, an individual should have the following qualifications:</p>
<ul>
<li>At least 2 years of customer service experience with excellent communication skills are a must.</li>
<li>Experience with Millennium Software is a PLUS!</li>
<li>Computer skills proficiency with a high comfort level are required to utilize Spitale’s web based Millennium and Demand Force software programs.</li>
<li>Ability to multi-task in a fast-paced environment.</li>
<li>Must be a TEAM player.</li>
<li>Prior experience working in salon/spa environment preferred but not required.</li>
<li>Be willing to work days, nights and Saturday’s.</li>
</ul>
