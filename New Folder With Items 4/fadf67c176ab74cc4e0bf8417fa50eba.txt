<h2>Sales Representative</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Reflexion Interactive Technologies, LLC is an award-winning sports-technology company that has developed a service to track and improve neurofitness in athletes. Using the "Edge", Reflexion measures and trains an athlete's neurocognitive and psychomotor status in about 30 seconds. The platform is designed for monitoring changes over time to help them be the best they can be.</p>
<p>
Due to company growth, Reflexion is recruiting for a highly skilled Sales Representative who will work collaboratively with the Leadership Team in Lancaster, PA to provide innovative business development initiatives that catapult new business reach and revenue, capitalize and advance opportunities nurtured from existing client relationships, and solidify profitable, long-term business relationships that align with the company's business strategy.</p>
<p>
This is a multi-disciplinary role that requires expertise in brand-building and developing strategic partnerships, sales and marketing, and providing a world-class customer service experience.</p>
<p><b>
YOUR DAY-TO-DAY COLLABORATIVE WORK COULD LOOK LIKE THIS</b><b>:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Assist in sales strategy development/implementation and the forecasting of annual, quarterly, and monthly sales revenue; designing tactical plans to meet (or exceed!) those goals</li>
<li>
Analyze customer research, current and trending market conditions and competitor information to properly position service offerings</li>
<li>
Assist in the planning and execution of sales and marketing initiatives and business development strategies to drive sales, including but not limited to: email, social media and display advertising campaigns, maximizing exposure at trade shows, conferences and association events, etc.</li>
<li>
Produce timely, accurate sales reports, pipeline information and other business development and sales metrics; measure, analyze and report sales performance metrics (ROI and KPIs); recommend appropriate course adjustments based on new client data, market trends or other acquired industry intelligence</li>
<li>
Develop effective sales presentations and proposals</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
YOUR DAY-TO-DAY PERSONAL CHALLENGES COULD INCLUDE, BUT WOULD NOT BE LIMITED TO</b><b>:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Identify suitable new business leads; effectively and promptly qualify and follow-up on leads, nurture pipeline prospects through the sales cycle, and secure valid, enforceable signed purchase contracts for the company's products</li>
<li>
Effectively demonstrate company products at site visits, trade shows, meetings and other venues</li>
<li>
Refine/implement "getting to yes" strategies; closing new business deals by coordinating requirements; developing and negotiating contracts; and integrating contract requirements with business operations</li>
<li>
Conduct post-sale analysis and de-brief, including insightful evaluation of budget variance and other sales metrics</li>
</ul>
<p>
<b>MINIMUM REQUIREMENTS:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Must be self-directed and able to work independently to meet/exceed company goals; ability and willingness to work flexible/extended hours and travel to accomplish objectives and meet prospect/client needs is a must</li>
<li>
"Day 1" comfort level with strategic cold-calling, being able to take the initiative and thrive in a fast-paced, performance-driven environment; taking personal ownership of business activities and outcomes is expected.</li>
<li>
Proven ability to collaborate and build strong working relationships with others is imperative, especially forging alliances with decision makers in the athletics space, school district administrators, and selection committee members</li>
<li>
Effective, persuasive and compelling communications skills are absolutely critical, as are social perceptiveness and active listening skills</li>
<li>
Bachelor's degree from an accredited four-year college or university and/or equivalent work experience to include:</li>
<li>
3-5 years of professional/technical sales experience in the athletics or health care fields with demonstrated organization, planning, strategy, implementation, and problem-solving skills are also essential OR</li>
<li>
3-5 years of progressively responsible experience in athletics, health care, and/or school district administration with connections, memberships in associations, and marketing, customer service and research experience</li>
<li>
High comfort level with software and spreadsheets, including use of financial, metric-driven performance reporting, all Microsoft office products, on-line calendar and meeting tools, smart phones, CRM and a wide variety of other technology tools</li>
<li>
Regular on-going day and overnight travel is required, primarily within the Mid-Atlantic territory (PA, DE, NY, NJ, MD, District of Columbia, VA; roughly four days per month). Having a valid driver's license, registered and insured vehicle, and the ability to drive to and from prospective/current accounts is mandatory. Occasional, less frequent</li>
</ul>
