<h2>Business Development Rep</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p>THE POSITION
<br>
<br>
This position acts as an extension of the Cylance sales and marketing organizations, generating qualified sales opportunities by responding to inbound interest via events, web inquiry, trade shows, digital, social and direct marketing initiatives at Cylance.
<br>
<br>
WHAT YOU WILL DO
<br>
<br>
May be required to travel domestically and internationally.
<br>
Nurture and send marketing and sales literature to prospective clients.
<br>
Generate and manage opportunities through high-volume reactive outbound calling and conducting pre-call preparation.
<br>
Provide Field Marketing support and promote Cylance’s sales events associated with, but not limited to conferences, tours, and tradeshows.
<br>
Demonstrates success through sales lead acceptance and contribution to pipeline and revenue.
<br>
Meet monthly, quarterly, and annual MBO’s and metric-based target(s).
<br>
Stay up-to-date on the latest Cylance products and services, industry best practices, and customer needs; attends applicable training, conferences, and learns new tools as needed.
<br>
Learn and develop new skills quickly as needed.
<br>
Protect Cylance operations by keeping information confidential.
<br>
Perform other duties as assigned.
<br>
<br>
WHO WE ARE LOOKING FOR
<br>
<br>
Bachelor’s or high level degree preferred; relevant experience can be substituted for educational degree
<br>
Two years minimum of related experience in business to business (B2B) tele-sales, business development, and/or other related experience.
<br>
Intermediate ability to generate leads over the phone.
<br>
Intermediate ability to provide customer-service and build/maintain relationships.
<br>
Advance ability to use business tools and software such as Microsoft 365 office programs and CRM (i.e., Salesforce.com (SFDC)).
<br>
Effectively manages a large territory and multiple concurrent business processes.
<br>
Effectively communicates in verbal and written form to various stakeholder’s who have differing levels of technical knowledge.
<br>
<br>
ABOVE AND BEYOND
<br>
<br>
May spend 15-25% of the time traveling to Cylance conferences and events
<br>
May be required to travel domestically and internationally.<br>
