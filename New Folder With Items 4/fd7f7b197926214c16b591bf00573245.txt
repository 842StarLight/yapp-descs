<h2>Tax Analyst (Fuel Tax)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Job Brief</b></p>
<p>This position is responsible for ensuring TravelCenters of America LLC and all of its subsidiaries maintain in compliance with all taxing jurisdictions for all Motor Fuel Excise tax laws. This position will work as part of a greater team charged with the responsible for all aspects of motor fuel tax accounting, compliance and audits.</p>
<p><b>Duties and Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Maintain and update motor fuel tax compliance calendar for responsible jurisdictions.</li>
<li>Understand, obtain, review and validate necessary documentation for filing motor fuel and related tax returns in numerous jurisdictions.</li>
<li>Accurately prepare, timely file, and pay motor fuel and related tax returns for various jurisdictions.</li>
<li>Reconcile monthly posting of sales and use tax payments and journal entries for responsible jurisdictions.</li>
<li>Respond to inquiries from tax authorities.</li>
<li>Create and update standard operating procedures (SOP’s) for responsible tasks.</li>
<li>Monitor motor fuel tax rates and law changes related to the motor fuel industry.</li>
<li>Research and document relevant tax laws affecting TA’s motor fuel taxes.</li>
<li>Identify risks, obstacles and issues; recommend solutions and work with team to resolve escalated issues.</li>
<li>Identify, develop, and implement, process improvement opportunities.</li>
<li>Proactively pursue professional development activities (attending training, reviewing professional publications, establishing personal networks, etc.).</li>
<li>Assist in updating tax rates in SAP.</li>
<li>Special projects as needed.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Qualifications</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Associates Degree or three plus years’ experience in corporate tax or accounting;</li>
<li>Strong analytical skills</li>
<li>Detailed oriented</li>
<li>Critical thinking skills to interpret information and accurately</li>
<li>Must be highly motivated and communicate in a highly professional and articulate manner verbally and in writing</li>
<li>Proficient in the use of MS Office Suite (Word, Excel, PowerPoint, Outlook)</li>
<li>Ability to manage multiple complex assignments</li>
<li>Strong written and verbal communication skills required</li>
<li>SAP experience a plus</li>
<li>Avelara software and SAP experience a plus</li>
<li>OneSource software and SAP experience a plus</li>
</ul>
<p>Job Type: Full-time</p>
