<h2>Returns Clerk</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p><b>Position:</b> Returns Clerk</p>
<p><b>
Department:</b> Returns Department</p>
<p><b>
Reports To:</b> Returns Manager</p>
<p><b>
FLSA Status:</b> Non-Exempt</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Purpose:</b></p>
<p>
Performs clerical support in order to ensure productive and accurate processing of completed customer orders, out of carton, defective and returned product.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Essential Duties &amp; Responsibilities;</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Verifies all customer delivery orders are completed. Identifies and corrects discrepancies.</li>
<li>Finalizes completed delivery orders in the GERS system.</li>
<li>Prepares reports regarding inventory cycle reports for truck, box and return dock locations.</li>
<li>Keys refused product information into Enterprise</li>
<li>Updates work orders for customer owned product that is received at the warehouse for repair.</li>
<li>Communicates with internal and external personnel in a professional and timely manner.</li>
<li>Maintains accurate files and processes in order to maximize productivity.</li>
<li>Performs clerical support as needed.</li>
<li>Other duties as assigned.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p>
</p>
<br>
<br>
Required Skills<br>
<p><b>Skills and Abilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Excellent verbal and written communication, and listening skills.</li>
<li>Basic reading and comprehension skills.</li>
<li>Basic numerical reasoning skills.</li>
<li>Ability to complete paperwork in an accurate, neat and efficient manner.</li>
<li>Demonstrated knowledge of software, including Microsoft Office.</li>
<li>Excellent organizational skills.</li>
<li>Outstanding customer service skills.</li>
<li>Knowledge of GERS, Red Prairie and Sales Force system preferred.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p>
</p>

<li>Ability to communicate effectively with customers and co-workers using telephone.</li>
<li>Ability to use vision to read documents and computer screen.</li>
<li>Ability to use hands to type information into computer.</li>
<br>
</ul>
