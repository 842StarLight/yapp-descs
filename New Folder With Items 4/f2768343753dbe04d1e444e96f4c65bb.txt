<h2>Product Administrator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>JOB SUMMARY</b></p>
<p>Accurately process customer product orders. Answer customer inquiries and resolve complaints. Follow up with customers on product orders, while providing support to Account Managers and Customer Service. Support and coordinate delivery activities and obtain status updates on purchase orders.</p>
<p><b>MAJOR DUTIES &amp; RESPONSIBILITIES</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Ensure product are delivered according to supply agreements.</li>

<li>Review orders daily for availability and shipping status and communicate as needed to assure timely delivery. Ensure backorders are filled when product is received.</li>
<li>Maintain working knowledge of products and inventory.</li>
<li>Provide technical support and advice to customers, as well as our Sales department</li>
<li>Support and assist Sales in conceptualizing and pricing of customer fleet agreements as well as technical inquiries</li>
<li>Support and assist global Customer Coordinators with service inquiries / orders</li>
<li>Register and maintain fleet agreement information into the ERP systems</li>
<li>Report and take corrective action; help in ensuring sales budgets and contractual obligations are fulfilled</li>
<li>Provide Account Managers with regular updates regarding inquiries and quality issues raised by customers</li>
<li>Coordinate with third party suppliers for purchasing parts when necessary</li>
<li>Gather information required to investigate and resolve invoice disputes</li>
