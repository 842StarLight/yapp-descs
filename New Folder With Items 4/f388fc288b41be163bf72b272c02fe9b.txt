<h2>Senior Product Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p><b>About Us</b></p>
<p>
Datameer is a well-funded, global software company headquartered in San Francisco with global offices across Europe backed by top tier investors including KPCB, Repoint Ventures, and Next World Capital among others. Our customers include the who’s who of the fortune 100 and the list is growing!</p>
<p>
We are an agile enterprise big data platform that fuels faster, trusted data-driven insights anywhere. Datameer’s advanced data preparation, exploration, and governance functionality makes it easy to transform big data into curated data sets to feed any analytics initiative. Using a hybrid architecture that spans on-premises and the cloud, Datameer helps organizations deliver new insights in days and operationalize them immediately, increasing the agility and responsiveness of the analytics that drive the business.</p>
<p>
Datameer is looking for a <b>Senior Product Manager</b> to join our team in San Francisco. Are you ready to be part of Fast Company's 50 most innovative tech companies? Do you want to be part of shaping the most cutting-edge big data analytics product?</p>
<p><b>
Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Help drive efforts on Datameer's core big data analytics product</li>
<li>Gather, validate, translate and prioritize buyer and user needs into a logically sequenced product and feature roadmap by utilizing internal and external sources for market, customer, competitive and product research.</li>
<li>Act as a subject matter expert and product manager for data analytics use cases pushing the boundaries of typical analytics using disparate structured and unstructured data</li>
<li>Write detailed and Agile product requirements documents and ensure clear communication of those requirements to the business, design, usability, and development teams</li>
<li>Adopt the role as a scrum based Product Owner and partner closely with development team and team lead</li>
<li>Understand and incorporate competitive landscape from the standpoint of strategy and functional capabilities</li>
<li>Monitor and maintain the ongoing customer acceptance and satisfaction of products</li>
<li>Closely collaborate with marketing, sales, services and engineering managers</li>
<li>Serve as the subject matter expert for the products and features and acts as a product evangelist to build internal awareness across the organization</li>
<li>Develop, maintain, and communicate the vision of product</li>
<li>Validate strategy, vision, and plans with senior management, key stakeholders and customers</li>
<li>Duties and projects as assigned</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p>
</p>
<p><b>Qualifications:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>5-7 years of work experience in a hands-on technical role.</li>
<li>3-4 years of relevant experience in a technical product management role.</li>
<li>Prior work experience in a technical customer-facing or engineering role.</li>
<li>BS in Math/Computer Science, analytics, business intelligence or related fields</li>
<li>The successful candidate must be seen as a consultative and effective communicator, presenter, thought leader in data analytics</li>
<li>Excellent knowledge of ETL, Excel and any other BI / analytic tools</li>
<li>Ability to take a proactive approach to product management, with strong follow-through, resourcefulness, attention to detail and excellent written and verbal communication skills</li>
<li>Ability to work closely with individuals at the customer's organization, from management to end users</li>
<li>Hands-on experience with application server or BI server platforms.</li>
<li>Literate, communicative, client-friendly, and flexible</li>
<li>Ideal experience as a technical Product Manager on a Big Data engine i.e. Stream processing, Map-reduce, machine learning, noSQL, in-memory processing</li>
<li>Alternately experience as a Technical Product Manager on a software platform technology such as DBMS kernel, Application Server, Integration, BPM, BI or event processing servers</li>
<li>Experience working in a dynamic startup environment</li>
</ul>
