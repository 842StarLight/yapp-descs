<h2>Accounts Payable/Receivable Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<b>Overview:</b>
<br>
<br>
As Accounts Payable/Receivable Manager , you will be accountable for the revenue operations of the company, to include processing the billing to customers; paying invoices by verifying transaction information; scheduling and preparing disbursements; obtaining authorization of payment, managing revenue recognition, the production of periodic revenue reports, and maintenance of an adequate system of revenue records. You will be expected to ensure that reported results comply with generally accepted accounting principles and/or international financial reporting standards.
<br>
<br>
You must be comfortable working closely with the Executive and Sales teams. You’ll need to thrive in a fast-paced startup environment, be hardworking, bold, ambitious, and analytical to thrive in this position.
<br>
<br>
Please note that submissions without cover letters will not be reviewed.
<br>
<br>
<b>Detailed Job Description:</b>
<ul>
<li>Generate invoices for new accounts using both quotes and purchase orders.</li>
<li>
Gather information for billing as needed from other team members</li>
<li>
Notify customers of upcoming license expirations and bill accordingly</li>
<li>
Proactively follow up with unresponsive accounts</li>
<li>
Work with customers who have gotten behind in billing to bring them into compliance</li>
<li>
Update various documents to track invoice and customer information</li>
<li>
Collaborate and coordinate with sales and support teams to achieve accurate billing, software installation, and license subscription updates</li>
<li>
Maintain and update existing billing manual of best practices and procedures</li>
<li>
Carefully record and track all customer interactions and events</li>
<li>
Research and maintain a database of correct billing contacts as well as billing procedures and preferences for each account</li>
<li>
Generate tasks in Zoho for support team based on billing events</li>
<li>
Respond to account status inquiries from sales and support team members</li>
<li>
Integrate and interface with multiple third party billing vendors. This will, at times, require learning those systems</li>
<li>
Monitor email and respond promptly to customer inquiries</li>
<li>
Provide banking and W9 information to customers on request</li>
<li>
Respond to special billing inquiries as requested on a per customer basis</li>
<li>
Attend weekly meetings to discuss status of accounts as they relate to product implementation</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Respond to vendor invoices</li>
<li>
Ensure that all payments are made in accordance with company policy</li>
<li>
Ensure that all payments are sent on time</li>
<li>
Resolve payment discrepancies and disputes on behalf of the company</li>
</ul>
