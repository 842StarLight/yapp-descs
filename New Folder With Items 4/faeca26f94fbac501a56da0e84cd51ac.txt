<b>About the Role: </b><b>W. L. Gore is seeking a Project Manager to join our team at our Elkton, Maryland facility. In this role, you will work within Gore’s IT Project Management Organization (PMO) and will be responsible for creating and utilizing best practice PM methodologies to ensure IT projects are delivered on time, within budget, adhere to high quality standards and meet customer expectations. You will be responsible for driving global IT projects such as software implementations or redesigns.<br>
</b>
</div>
<div></div>
<div>
<b>Responsibilities </b><b>:
</b>
</div>
<ul>
<li><div><b>Facilitating the creation of project scope, goals, and deliverables that support project goals
</b></div></li>
<li><div><b>Defining and creating project plans for project/program prioritization and implementation
</b></div></li>
<li><div><b>Participating in the creation and communication of PM best practices and standards
</b></div></li>
<li><div><b>Utilizing Gore’s PPM process for portfolio management and resource commitment
</b></div></li>
<li><div><b>Building, leading, and coaching multiple project teams throughout project lifecycles
</b></div></li>
<li><div><b>Assessing and facilitating resolution of project risks and issues as they are encountered
</b></div></li>
<li><div><b>Monitoring financial delivery and issue management processes, being sure to escalate issues and variances to cost and schedule variances where necessary
</b></div></li>
<li><div><b>Monitoring progress and continually updating relevant business stakeholder of status
</b></div></li>
<li><div><b>Tracking milestones and deliverables using MS Project and other appropriate tools
</b></div></li>
<li><div><b>Facilitating all operational checkpoint activities throughout the project lifecycle such as requirement analysis, vendor selection, design, development, testing, implementation and hand-over to post-go live support
</b></div></li>
</ul>
</div>
