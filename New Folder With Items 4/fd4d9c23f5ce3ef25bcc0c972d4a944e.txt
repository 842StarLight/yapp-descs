<h2>Senior Account Manager-Retirement</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div></div>
<div>
<div>
<div>Position Objective</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
Responsible for the servicing Lockton Retirement clients under the direct supervision of the Practice Leader/ Dir. of Client Services and in accordance with Lockton procedures.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
Essential Duties</div>
</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<p>
Maintain primary responsibility for book of business (revenue $500,000).</p>
<p>
Execute service plan based on client meeting frequency</p>
<p>
Lead presentations to client</p>
<p>
Investment reviews</p>
<p>
Fiduciary best practices</p>
<p>
Plan design recommendations</p>
<p>
Fee benchmarking and analysis</p>
<p>
In addition, support Account Executive(s) in Account Manager role on assigned clients (revenue $500,000)</p>
<p>
Maintain strong client relationships</p>
<p>
Maintain knowledge of products available in market</p>
<p>
Possess strong presentation skills, including preparation, execution, and delivery of materials</p>
<p>
Effectively manage service/project plan timeline for each client</p>
<p>
Prepare RFPs for bid solicitation</p>
<p>
Manager vendor responses questions during bid solicitation</p>
<p>
Clarify any questions with carriers</p>
<p>
Review and summarize proposals</p>
<p>
Negotiate fees with vendors</p>
<p>
Initiate and manage all administration activity relative to implementing a new provider</p>
<p>
Contract reviews</p>
<p>
Implementation meetings/calls</p>
<p>
Ensure all necessary contracts/documents are executed in a timely fashion</p>
<p>
Review Plan Documents, SPD and other Contracts for accuracy</p>
<p>
Provide updates and response to employer compliance issues/questions as needed</p>
<p>
Respond to client requests in a timely fashion</p>
<p>
Assume additional responsibilities from Practice Leader/ Dir. of Client Services/Account Executives as appropriate</p>
<p>
Ability to operate independently with clients on certain initiatives</p>
<p>
Listen to clients and colleagues, provide meaningful feedback, and collaborate to identify problems and solutions</p>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
<div><div>Qualifications</div></div>
<div></div>
<br>
<p>
Bachelor’s degree in business or equivalent education and/or experience.</p>
<p>
Five+ years’ working knowledge of qualified retirement plans</p>
<p>
Series 6, 63 and 65 or ability to obtain within 90 days of employment</p>
<p>
Organizational and time management skills to prioritize and project manage tasks and workloads to meet time sensitive deadlines</p>
<p>
Strong knowledge of Microsoft products (Word, Excel, PowerPoint, Outlook) or similar software applications</p>
<p>
Demonstrated interpersonal communication skills and ability to interact with Associates at all levels of responsibility</p>
<p>
Demonstrated understanding of when to proceed alone and when to involve the Account Executive, Practice Leader, Producer, and others</p>
<p>
Demonstrated professional, tactful negotiation and persuasion skills to achieve objectives</p>
<p>
Ability to present proposals to clients in a professional manner</p>
<p>
Ability to travel by automobile and aircraft, and stay for more than one day and night</p>
