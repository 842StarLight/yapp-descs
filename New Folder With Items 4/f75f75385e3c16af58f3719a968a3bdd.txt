<p>JOB TITLE/ POSITION:</p>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Marketing, Graphic Design and Communication Specialist</b></h2>
<p>Status: Full time</p>
<p>Benefits: Yes</p>
<p>Hours: 8:30 am – 5:30 pm</p>
<p>Location: ONR’s corporate office: 1101 South Capital of TX Hwy, G-200, Austin, TX 78746, www.ONR-INC.com</p>
<p>Please submit your resume and portfolio for consideration to: ncvitanov@onr-inc.com. No recruiters or calls permitted.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>Company/ Position Description:</p>
<p>Orthopaedic &amp; Neurological Rehabilitation, Inc. (ONR) is a therapist owned rehabilitation company that is nationally recognized for the quality care we provide primarily in the long term care/ skilled nursing facility sector as well as in other healthcare settings. We are seeking an accomplished professional to be our Marketing and Communication Specialist. This position is based at ONR’s corporate office in Austin, TX.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>The primary duty of this position is to create and design internal and external marketing and recruitment collateral for both ONR and ONR clients. This position must successfully work, interact, and partner with ONR’s business development, recruitment, operations, clinical, and administrative teams, with the ONR brand consistent, professional, and unique.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>ONR is seeking an individual that initiates creative designs and incorporates the gift of language to achieve the “wow factor” deserving of our entrepreneurial organization. Initial and on-going exploration of ONR’s sector of the healthcare business is a must.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>This individual must strive, relentlessly, to better understand ONR’s business, clients and competitors.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>Position is based in our Austin corporate office with possible telecommuting option.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li><b>ESSENTIAL FUNCTIONS</b></li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p>The statements below reflect the general duties considered necessary to describe the principal functions of the job as identified and shall not be considered as a detailed description of all the work requirements that may be inherent in the job:</p>
<ul>
<li>Manage daily administrative tasks to ensure that the Marketing department runs smoothly.</li>
<li>Copy edit, proofread, and revise communications.</li>
<li>Work with manager and various departments to determine collateral budget and manage expenses to that budget.</li>
<li>Review all existing ONR collateral sales and marketing material; catalogue and file all materials in a logical and easily accessible manner.</li>
<li>Create and develop, on an ongoing basis, marketing brochures, sales presentations, ONR newsletter and mailers, as requested.</li>
<li>Produce time sensitive presentations for sales calls in an expedient and timely manner.</li>
<li>Assist with organizing promotional events, i.e., recruitment and sales tradeshows, as well as all other events.</li>
<li>Procure extensive knowledge of ONR’s services, sales strategies, and core values.</li>
<li>Monitor competitors’ marketing activities.</li>
<li>Partner with the ONR Business Development team to complement sales goals with associated sales and marketing tools.</li>
<li>Work closely with ONR’s recruitment department to establish mailers and national hiring strategies.</li>
<li>Coordinate media and social networking placement to enhance ONR’s public image and brand.</li>
<li>Actively participate in weekly Business Development calls.</li>
<li>Offer expert advice to develop innovative advertising, public relations and events.</li>
<li>Provide direction and expertise in social media networking (Facebook, Twitter, LinkedIn).</li>
<li>Oversee ONR web page and ensure the marketing efforts to direct traffic back to the ONR site.</li>
<li>Coordinate all collateral material with customers, related to Census Development.</li>
<li>Collaborate with Clinical Services to develop educational training resources, flyers, and company webinars.</li>
<li>Assist in the negotiation with media partners to guarantee advantageous contracts.</li>
<li>Demonstrate exceptional communication and interpersonal skills.</li>
<li>Gate keep all active Clinical and Business forms; design and update all organizational flow charts, in cooperation with key department heads.</li>
<li>Assist with posting Recruitment positions on social media sites.</li>
<li>Performing other related duties as required.</li>
<br>
</ul>

<li>Must have professional experience overseeing a company's social media marketing utilizing FaceBook, LinkedIn, Twitter, Instagram, Snapchat, and any new evolving platforms.</li>
