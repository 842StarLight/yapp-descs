<p><b>
Company Overview</b>
</p>
<p>Spirit Human Resources is a Professional Employer Organization (PEO). Our mission is to empower and enable business owners in their pursuit of success through hassle-free outsourced HR services including human resources, employee benefits, payroll and risk management.</p>
<p>
Spirit HR provides meaningful work where team members are valued. We support each other in the pursuit of providing extraordinary customer service to our clients and to each other.
</p>
<p><b>Opportunity</b>
</p>
<p>Spirit HR is seeking an impactful sales professional with proven sales track record who is self-motivated, results-driven, understands the needs of small business owners and thrives on helping those businesses succeed.
</p>
<p><b>We are looking for</b>
</p>
<ul>
<li>3-5 years selling B2B. PEO, Payroll, Group Insurance or HR Outsourcing experience preferred.
</li>
<li>Relationship-based/consultative sales experience.
</li>

</li>
<li>Local marketing experience; lead generation, prospecting and networking.
</li>
<li>Strong written and oral business communication skills.
</li>
<li>Bachelor’s degree or equivalent experience and education.
</li>
<li>Business owner experience a plus.
</li>
<li>Valid Oklahoma life and health insurance license preferred.
</li>
</ul>
