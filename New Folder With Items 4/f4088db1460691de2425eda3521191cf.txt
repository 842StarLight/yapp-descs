<p><b>Customer Service Specialist-Idaho (Remote)</b></p>
<p><b>
Commercial Sales</b></p>
<p></p>
<p>
Dell provides the technology that transforms the way we all work and live. But we are more than a technology company — we are a people company. We inspire, challenge and respect every one of our over 100,000 employees. We also provide them with unparalleled growth and development opportunities. We can’t wait for you to discover this for yourself as <b>a Customer Service Representative in Oklahoma City.</b></p>
<p><br>
Not even digitally ground-breaking products sell themselves. At Dell, we’re determined to give every one of our customers and partners a high-level, white-glove experience. This experience begins with our ground-breaking technology portfolio of products – client solutions, high-end electronics, accessories, mobility products, enterprise solutions, services and packaged software. Our Inside Sales Account Reps breathe life into our technology portfolio. They take great pride in developing lasting relationships, building great rapport and using their flawless communication skills to sell our products to customers by phone, web and email.</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Summary</b></p>
<p>
Responds to customer service inquiries regarding company products, features and/or services. Maintains ownership of customer issues directing customer to the appropriate agent when required. Supports sales team by developing and maintaining positive customer relations with clients/customers, which can substantially affect service and/or product revenue(s). Works with various departments to meet maintenance services sales goals through constant process alignment. Works with customers and/or distributors to understand and document customer issues. May be involved in pre or post-sales or both.</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Consults with customers on escalated issues and provides service to effectively resolve escalated customer issues.</li>
<li>
Works under limited supervision with little direction from management and adheres to administrative policies and escalation procedures.</li>
<li>
Provides support to leadership to effectively improve the overall service levels provided to the customer and recommends departmental improvements to leadership.</li>
<li>
If applicable, provides coaching, guidance and direction to team members</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Requirements</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Solid telephone etiquette skills.</li>
<li>
Solid knowledge of products, customer service, policies and procedures.</li>
<li>
Solid computer skills.</li>
<li>
Complex problem-solving skills.</li>
<li>
Ability to articulate and simplify customer issues and solution.</li>
<li>
Ability to influence others to achieve results; negotiation skills.</li>
<li>
Ability to prioritize projects and/or deliverables.</li>
<li>
Customer focused Communication skills.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Education and Experience</b></p>
<p>
Typically requires 4+ years of related experience.</p>
