<h2>Project Data Assistant</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Position Summary</b></p>
<p>Everlight Solar is seeking a data-minded assistant to help our Solar Project Manager work with our team located in Madison, WI. This will be a work-from-home "remote" position.</p>
<p>Our project managers and assistants oversee and coordinate the entire process for company projects, assisting customers, team members, and contractors, ensuring that each project is completed in a timely manner with high-quality results.</p>
<p><b>Key Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Validates expectations with customers before, during and after project completion.</li>
<li>Exceed customer’s expectations on a regular basis while performing excellent customer service.</li>
<li>Successfully manages multiple projects through all phases.</li>
<li>Ability to work independently, under pressure, to organize and prioritize workloads to keep several projects progressing to completion simultaneously with little or no direction; ability to weigh options, foresee consequences, and employ good judgment.</li>
<li>Serves as a liaison between field technicians, department management, government bodies, and customers.</li>
<li>Use independent judgment to handle and troubleshoot all inquiries, questions, and complaints.</li>
<li>Proactively completes projects on time.</li>
<li>Reviews and oversees complete plans and project documentation for accuracy.</li>
<li>Monitors equipment production to ensure product integrity.</li>
<li>Consistently meets the overall project deadlines in a timely manner.</li>
<li>Builds trust and confidence with contractors and the Project Management Team.</li>
<li>Assists with managing multiple subcontractors across multiple states.</li>
<li>Recruits and develops business relationships with new subcontractors.</li>
<li>Driving sales and profitability through effective and efficient project execution.</li>
<li>Understanding of principles and methods for showing, promoting, and selling products or services, including marketing strategy and tactics, product demonstration, sales techniques, and sales control systems.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Required Skills, Qualifications, and Competencies</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Excellent organizational and time management skills.</li>
<li>Excellent communication and interpersonal skills, both written and verbal.</li>
<li>Extraordinary commitment to the highest level of customer service. Ability to work well with customers; responds verbally and in writing to their inquiries, remains calm when dealing with an upset or angry customer, listens to their concerns/questions.</li>
<li>Positive, out-going, energetic team player that enjoys working in a collaborative team environment.</li>
<li>Must be fluent with the Apple ecosystem of software (iOS, macOS, iWork, etc)</li>
<li>Proven ability to function in all phases of the project development from sales proposals and design through project completion and closeout.</li>
<li>Knowledge/Proficiency in Microsoft Office programs.</li>
<li>Knowledge /Proficiency in Salesforce.</li>
<li>Demonstrated sense of urgency and ownership in all assignments.</li>
<li>Ability to collaborate closely with other team members on a wide variety of projects.</li>
<li>Prepares timely and accurate paperwork.</li>
<li>Participates in marketing activities and business development efforts.</li>
<li>Experience with Solar.</li>
</ul>
<p>The successful candidate will be versatile in all areas of project management, sales support, be able to multi-task and have experience working in a highly collaborative environment. Candidates must have strong leadership and work ethic and must be able to thrive in a strong, dynamic team environment. Project Managers require help as they investigate and find potential new projects and sales opportunities while ensuring that every step of the quote, scheduling and fulfillment process of the company’s projects are executed flawlessly. This position is responsible for the overall success of the projects they manage and will be required to be the day-to-day contact person for their clients.</p>
