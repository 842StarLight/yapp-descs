<h2>Non-DOT Delivery Associate Fayetteville Territory - Valid DL Needed</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">Job Description
<br><b>Job Title:</b> Non-DOT Delivery Associate (Valid DL Needed)
<br><b>Industry Code:</b> Wholesale Trade 424000 Non-Durable Goods
<br><b>Job Code:</b> 53-3031(05)
<br><b>Reports To:</b> Route Sales Supervisor
<br><b>FLSA Status:</b> Non-Exempt FWW + Commission Available
<br><b>Department:</b> Sales Distribution
<br><b>Division:</b> Piedmont Cheerwine Bottling Company
<br><b>Location:</b> Raeford, NC
<br><b>Summary:</b> Services, orders, and delivers products to customers by performing the following duties for a Presales Delivery Associate.
<br>Essential Duties and Responsibilities include the following. Other duties may be assigned.
<br>A Presales Delivery Associate services customers assigned to route based on scheduled time-stopped ranking. Must adapt to any changes in work assignments as well as supporting organizational restructuring efforts.
<br>Follow accounts receivable guidelines set by trade/customer when checking in orders (DSD, PO#, Military, Receipt, or stamp) with customer.
<br>Ensures all deliveries are properly worked by stocking our designated shelves and displays; ensuring that back stock be properly broken down and placed on appropriate pallets, bunkers, or trailers in an organized fashion; gather all shells/flats, pallets to be picked up and loaded, and damage or out of date be bundled and picked up; and then review our promotions and new sales orders with the necessary managers at our customer's location before finalizing the order and delivery sheet.
<br><b>Communicates with each account:</b> New product offering(s), sales events/promotions, and display opportunities.
<br>Audits and pulls out-of-date or damaged products and rotates product in order to maintain the freshest product level possible on a regular basis.
<br>Forecasts sales based on previous sale reports and upcoming promotions/sales to ensure accuracy for our customer and minimize pallet pick-ups.
<br>Follows all company policies and procedures set forth by management.
