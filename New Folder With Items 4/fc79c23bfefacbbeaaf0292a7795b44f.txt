<h2>Client Support Representative</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Imagine working to propel the mission of an amazing nonprofit organization. Now imagine working to propel the missions of thousands of amazing nonprofits across the country.</p>
<p>That’s exactly what we do here at DonorSearch.</p>
<p>DonorSearch is a dynamic, rapidly-growing prospect research company that is changing the way people fundraise. We make waves because we only hire the best of the best. Better still, we recognize that our supremely talented employees are the lifeblood of our organization. If you join the DonorSearch team, expect a fun environment with room for growth and a wonderful work-life balance. We strongly believe that companies can only thrive if they meet the needs of their employees first, and that’s exactly what we strive to do at DonorSearch. Join us!</p>
<p>The Regional Account Executive evaluates and prioritizes a client portfolio, based on a geographic region, to ensure retention and revenue goals are obtained. Regional Account Executives are expected to have a hands-on, client centered approach by having meaningful renewal conversations, ensuring multi-year commitments and expanding existing relationships.</p>
<p>Responsibilities:</p>
<ul>
<li>Responsible for customer renewals with a goal to achieve and exceed the monthly, quarterly, and annual revenue and retention targets.</li>
<li>Generate proposals and contracts with accuracy and following all proper protocols and internal systems.</li>
<li>Own the critical patch of the entire renewal transaction to create action and urgency in closing renewals timely and accurately.</li>
<li>Identify target accounts for contract expansion and upgrade.</li>
<li>Plan and execute an upgrade process.</li>
<li>Monitor overall account health and satisfaction and proactively engage internal teams to ensure that satisfaction remains high.</li>
<li>Work with the Retention Specialist on a daily basis with open streams of communication to achieve the highest level of revenue and retention.</li>
<li>Use Salesforce to capture client information and track opportunities.</li>
<li>Use internal billing system to report client usage, finding “at-risk” clients that have stopped using their account.</li>
<li>Understand all aspects of using ZenDesk ticketing system and answer tickets accurately and timely.</li>
<li>Conducting monthly webinars for clients – including Online Tools Training, Major Gifts, FAQ’s and more.</li>
<li>Handle regional one-on-one account training.</li>
<li>Keep up with updates and changes within the tool to answer questions and concerns of the client.</li>
<li>Schedule and travel to territory accounts for 1-on-1 training’s conferences and/or User Groups.</li>
<li>Other duties that align with the mission and vision of DonorSearch.</li>
</ul>
<p>Qualifications:</p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree preferred or equivalent in work experience.</li>
<li>3 to 5 years of related experience supporting a sales organization in a technology or related environment.</li>
<li>3 to 5 years of customer service experience.</li>
<li>Experience in the nonprofit space strongly preferred</li>
<li>Demonstrated ability to work in a team-oriented, collaborative environment.</li>
<li>Ability to effectively communicate over the phone and using computer for training purposes.</li>
<li>Strong work ethic, attention to detail and ability to work independently.</li>
<li>Excellent organizational skills and attention to detail; ability to manage multiple priorities and tasks simultaneously.</li>
<li>Ability to operate within an ever-changing environment.</li>
<li>Proficiency in MS Office applications (Word, Excel, Powerpoint, and Outlook).</li>
</ul>
<ul><li>Salesforce CRM experience a plus (or some experience with a customer management software).</li></ul>
<p>Job Type: Full-time</p>
<p>Salary: $45,000.00 to $65,000.00 /year</p>
<p>Experience:</p>
<ul>
<li>working in the nonprofit sector: 2 years (Required)</li>
<li>customer service for a software or technology based company: 3 years (Required)</li>
</ul>
<p>Education:</p>
<ul><li>Bachelor's (Required)</li></ul>
