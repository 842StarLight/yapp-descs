<h2>Sales Development Representative</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Levelset (previously known as <i>zlien</i>) is one of the South's fastest-growing tech companies. Our headquarters in New Orleans has been named one of CityBusiness's "Best Places to Work" two years in a row. Backed by venture funding, we are growing revenue (and our headcount) 100% year by year, and recently expanded into Austin in mid-2018. We empower contractors to always get what they earn, and our software platform makes lien rights in the construction industry simple and stress-free, so contractors and suppliers can get paid.</p>
<p>
The Sales Development Representative role is responsible for generating qualified opportunities and breaking records. The best representatives are creative and passionate team players who can handle the challenges of cold calling and objections with grit and enthusiasm. This position will give someone beginning their career in sales significant opportunities to grow and a clear learning and earning path to become an Account Executive. You will have direct access to the VP of Revenue, dedicated sales leadership, and the executive team to help in supporting your sales goals and learning more about a successful SaaS business.</p>
<p>
We'll provide the mentorship to take your growing professional career to an entirely new level. Are you ready?</p>
<p><b>
Your responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Generate new business opportunities to fuel Levelset's sales growth by calling outbound and inbound leads to set up demos for Account Executives</li>
<li>
Prospect and identify key decision-makers at target companies and lay the foundation for a strong, long-lasting relationship</li>
<li>
Use a value-oriented consultative approach to listen to prospective customers and educate them on what Levelset solutions will best meet their needs</li>
<li>
Work closely with Account Executives to develop targeted lists, call strategies, and messaging to drive opportunities for new business</li>
<li>
Achieve or exceed monthly quotas of qualified opportunities</li>
</ul>
<p>
This position is a perfect way to jump start a successful and lucrative career in sales. You'll receive world-class training, and learn a suite of new and marketable skills, including sales principles, strategic design of SaaS customer acquisition models, business development strategy, and an understanding of a high-growth business environment.</p>
