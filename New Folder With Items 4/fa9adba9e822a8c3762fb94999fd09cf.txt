<h2>Director of Sales & Marketing</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<b>Summary:</b> SMG, the leader in privately managed public assembly facilities, has an excellent and immediate opening for a Director of Sales &amp; Marketing responsible for developing new and existing business by conducting on-going sales solicitation efforts for national, regional and state conventions, tradeshows, consumer shows and social functions, as well as maintaining client relationships for renewal contracts. Must also work closely with Visit Corpus Christi. Oversee Sales Managers and Marketing staff including advertising, public relations, and related tasks.
<br><br>
Essential Duties and Responsibilities
<br><br>
Establish strong client base ensuring quality guest services.
<br><br>
Identifies and implements sales goals and objectives.
<br><br>
Assist team in accomplishing sales and marketing goals.
<br><br>
Maximize total room revenues and profits.
<br><br>
Participate in industry related tradeshows and meetings.
<br><br>
Market facility through direct advertising, print collateral and internet.
<br><br>
Develop sales contracts and addendums and monitor compliance.
<br><br>
Prepare statistics, reports and budget presentations for top management.
<br><br>
Participate with staff in pre-event correspondence and meetings until the account can be turned over to an Event Coordinator.
<br><br>
Prepare Marketing Plan and ensures deadlines are met.
<br><br>
Directs a wide-range of marketing campaigns for all the facilities (arena, auditorium, and convention center) which are inclusive of all the concerts, and family shows taking place at the facilities.
<br><br>
Lead the sales and sponsorship program in which includes corporate sales (suites, club seats, event sponsors, etc.)
<br><br>
Work directly with facility partners such as tenants, promoters, sponsors, suite holders, and advertising clients to make sure all their needs are met.
<br><br>
Perform common managerial functions; planning, organizing, communicating, supervising, controlling and other general managerial functions including acting as Director on Duty as needed.
<br><br>
Perform other duties as assigned by the General Manager.
<br><br>
Supervisory Responsibilities
<br><br>
Directly supervises employees in the Sales &amp; Marketing Department. Carries out supervisory responsibilities in accordance with SMG's policies and applicable laws. Supervisory responsibilities include interviewing, hiring and training employees; planning, assigning, and directing work; appraising performance.
<br><br>
Education and/or Experience
<br><br>
Bachelors' degree preferred in Marketing, Sales, Business, or related field along with 7+ years' experience in hospitality industry, related to booking national group sales in a similar market as well as managing a sales, sponsorships, and marketing team or equivalent combination of education and experience.
<br><br>
Knowledge, Skills, and Abilities
<br><br>
Excellent communication, problem solving and organizational skills.
<br><br>
Knowledge of supervisory principles and practices.
<br><br>
Result oriented with exceptional leadership and motivational skills.
<br><br>
Ability to multi-task under extreme deadline pressure in a fast-paced environment.
<br><br>
Maintain an effective working relationship with clients, employees, exhibitors, patrons, and others encountered during employment.
<br><br>
Strong computer skills.
<br><br>
Ability to work with minimal supervision.
<br><br>
Ability to work flexible hours based on events, including daytime, evening, weekends, and holidays as needed.
