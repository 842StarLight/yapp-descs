<h2>Sr. Specialist, Accounting</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Our Company:
</b></p>
<p>Media Solutions Corporation (MSC) is a Chandler, Arizona subsidiary of Kansas City based Associated Wholesale Grocers, Inc. (AWG). AWG is a food distributor to several thousand grocery stores throughout the US and the largest cooperative distributor in the nation with sales of about $10 billion annually.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p>MSC provides digital marketing solutions to about 1,000 grocers in the US. This includes store websites, email services, hosting services, social media services, store apps, in-store TV screen digital signage networks, text message programs and other digital services. All of these services are created with MSC's development team in our Chandler office.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Job Focus:</b></p>
<p>
This position oversees and helps to complete the functions in support of operations, reporting and accounting management for Media Solutions Corporation. Although most of MSC's accounting needs are fulfilled by our parent company (Associated Wholesale Grocers in Kansas City, Kansas), this new position will be responsible for coordinating all local billing and accounting needs for our Chandler, Arizona office.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Key Job Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Responsible for all invoice and statement billings to MSC's customers. Coordinate credits when required.
</li>
<li>Responsible for Accounts Receivable reporting and follow-up.
</li>
<li>Responsible for Bill Pay to third party service providers.
</li>
<li>Coordinates Estimates and Proposals for new business requests. Works closely with MSC staff members to determine appropriate pricing for our services.
</li>
<li>Review and research monthly operating statements for accuracy.
</li>
<li>Assist in the development of the annual MSC business plan, budget and business plan narrative.
</li>
<li>Coordinates Human Resource needs for MSC with our parent company. Maintain current job descriptions for MSC employees. Coordinate annual review process with all MSC employees.
</li>
<li>Responsible for all time and attendance elements for non-exempt employees.</li>
<li>Receives and coordinates approvals for all requests for time off for the company</li>
<li>Oversee acquisition of office related supplies, coordinates third party services like janitorial services, pest control, office maintenance and repairs.</li>
<li>Coordinate CAPEX related duties with our parent company.
</li>
<li>Oversees MSC budgeting process with our parent company.
</li>
</ul>
