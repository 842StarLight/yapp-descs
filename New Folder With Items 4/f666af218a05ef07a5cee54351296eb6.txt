<h2>Real Estate Photographer/Videographer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Due to volume of candidates, we will only respond those who can send samples of Real Estate Photos only. We need to see your angles and composition. Please email your samples to jobs at hometourvision.com</b></p>
<p>HomeTourVision is looking to add experienced Real Estate Photographers as independent contractors to our growing team. This is an excellent way to supplement your income. We are looking for immediate coverage in Orange and Rockland Counties. Must live in the the areas or close to the areas we need covered. Immediate work for the right individuals.</p>
<p>We are looking for candidates with an eye for detail, an understanding of bracketing photos for HDR, understanding composition and angles, and own a full frame DSLR with wide angle lens. (16-35) Some cropped sensor cameras will be acceptable with a minimum of a 10-22 lens. <b>All cameras must be able to auto-bracket at least 5 exposures.</b></p>
<p>Videographers will need a motorized gimbal and be able to operate it smoothly.</p>
<p>Photographers may also be trained to operate our Floor Plan Mapping Software System to provide floor plan/ 360 degree virtual tours, and walk-through video tours.</p>
<p>All sales, scheduling, and editing are done by our in-house team. All you need to do is take the media and send it to us by the end of the day.</p>
<p><b>This is a fast paced, volume based business so must work quick and efficiently.</b></p>
<p>We need excellent photographers with the required gear who are presentable and personable and can be flexible during the week and occasional weekends and are willing to go through a training period and be critiqued on their performance.</p>
<p>If you are an FAA Certified Drone Operator, please let us know also.</p>
<p>Very competitive pay. Depending on service performed, jobs can range from 2-5 per day/10-20+ per week.</p>
<p>Media must be uploaded to our FTP server the same day as the shoot so high speed internet is also a must.</p>
<p>Requirements:</p>
<ul>
<li>Full frame camera with wide angle lens (Some cropped acceptable)</li>
<li>Camera must be able to auto-bracket at least 5 exposures</li>
<li>Understanding of HDR Bracketing</li>
<li>Sturdy tripod with Ball head</li>
<li>Understanding of Real Estate Photography</li>
<li>Flexible weekday schedule (be available some weekends)</li>
<li>Reliable and insured vehicle</li>
<li>Pass a background check</li>
<li>Excellent customer service skills</li>
<li>Extremely friendly and can always work with a smile when with our clients and homeowners.</li>
</ul>
<p>Job Type: Contract</p>
<p>Experience:</p>
<ul><li>Real Estate Photography: 1 year (Preferred)</li></ul>
<p>Work Location:</p>
<ul><li>On the road</li></ul>
