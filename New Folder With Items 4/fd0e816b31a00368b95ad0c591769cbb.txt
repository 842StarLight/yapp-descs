<p>Corning is one of the world’s leading innovators in materials science. For more than 160 years, Corning has applied its unparalleled expertise in specialty glass, ceramics, and optical physics to develop products that have created new industries and transformed people’s lives.<br>
<br>
Corning succeeds through sustained investment in R&amp;D, a unique combination of material and process innovation, and close collaboration with customers to solve tough technology challenges.<br>
<br>
As a leading developer, manufacturer, and global supplier of scientific laboratory products for 100 years, Corning’s Life Sciences segment collaborates with researchers seeking new approaches to increase efficiencies, reduce costs and compress timelines in the drug discovery process. Using unique expertise in the fields of materials science, surface science, optics, biochemistry and biology, the segment provides innovative solutions that improve productivity and enable breakthrough discoveries.</p>
<p><b>
Scope of Position:
</b></p>
<p>In this role, you will have global commercial responsibility for the permeable supports business. You will be responsible for the development of specific marketing plans for products to establish, enhance or distinguish product placement within the competitive arena. You will analyze sales and customer data to enable the sales team to protect current business and drive growth. Additional responsibilities for forecasting, financial performance tracking and business modeling. We look for you to develop solutions to commercial, quality or supply chain problems. Develop/review marketing strategies, advertising plans, long-range plans, goals, and objectives and develop sales tools and customer facing materials.</p>

</b></p>
<ul>

</li>

</li>

</li>

</li>

</li>

</li>
<li>Pricing management and approvals
</li>
<li>Work with Business Director and Senior Product Line Manager to develop the overall business strategy

<li>Develop appropriate business plans – supported by financial, manufacturing, channel, etc. data and analysis – in support of recommendations
