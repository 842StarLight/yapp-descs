<div><div>
<div>
<p>Label Tech, a Fortis Solutions Group company, is in search of a buyer to support our Merced, California facility! If you have at least 3 years of prior purchasing or buying experience in a manufacturing environment, this job could be perfect for you! Ideal candidates will also have good communication skills, be detail-oriented and proficient in MS Office and other business systems such as ERP, CRM.</p>
<p><b>General Description:</b></p>
<p>The Plant Buyer is responsible for all purchasing-related functions that support production and administrative needs. They are also responsible for leading in the development and implementation of cost savings opportunities, minimizing working capital and obsolescence through disciplined raw material inventory, and establishment of efficient purchasing methods. Other key responsibilities include the determination of item-based min/max levels, handling supplier quality complaints, and driving the necessary supply chain standardization processes across all Fortis locations.
</p>
<p>Position is salaried and will work days, 8:00am to 5:00pm, Monday through Friday. Fortis Solutions Group provides a full suite of benefits including medical, dental, vision, short term disability, long term disability, life insurance, 401k with match and generous PTO.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Essential Duties and Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Directly responsible for the accurate issuance of plant purchase orders to vendors on a daily basis per the plant administrative and manufacturing needs. Accurately provide the necessary tracking of purchases to ensure on-time delivery. Follow up with suppliers in the case of delays, incorrect quantities, or damaged goods.</li>
<li>Participate in monthly conference calls and quarterly department meetings with other plant buyers to develop best practices.</li>
<li>Coordinate forecasting with major raw material suppliers and FSG manufacturing plants, minimizing overall system inventory while not impacting customer deliveries.</li>
<li>Complete monthly spending report for plant location and analyze areas to conduct bid packages.</li>
<li>Keep aged raw material to below 90 days and support Director of Supply Chain initiatives for SKU and web width consolidation.</li>
<li>Fill out and file all vendor claim documents and ensure tracking and credit receipt. Ensure materials are properly disposed of and removed from MRP system.</li>
<li>Serve as point of contact for the local plant level supplier relationships.</li>
<li>Conduct bid packages &amp; consolidate on indirect materials, plant services &amp; supplies where possible.</li>
<li>Work with fellow plants to prioritize and follow up on raw material consolidations and substitutions while ensuring accurate system data.</li>
<li>Complete plant related vendor scorecard tracker from the “approved” vendor database.</li>
<li>Be able to back up &amp; assist other FSG plants as needed</li>
<li>Assist as needed with Engineering on bid packages and RFI’s</li>
<li>Be able to interact and share information as needed with other departments.</li>
<li>Issue purchase orders as required to FSG approved outsource vendors. Point of contact for all outsource vendor services &amp; relationships.</li>
<br>
</ul>
