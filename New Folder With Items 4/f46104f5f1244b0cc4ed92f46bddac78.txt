<div>
Harvard Pilgrim Health Care is currently seeking highly motivated individuals to join our organization as a Senior Key Account Executive. Reporting to the Sales Director, the Senior Key Account Executive is responsible for the management of HPHC’s most significant (membership, impact, political, influential) accounts. The responsibilities include increasing membership penetration in existing accounts, retaining existing membership, identifying most appropriate product and funding mechanism and developing strong relationships with the designated account contacts. The Senior Key Account Executive manages the relationship between HPHC Senior Management, the consultant or TPA if applicable and internal operations.</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div><b>
WHAT YOU WILL BE DOING:</b></div>
<div><ul>
<li>
Serves as primary contact between account, broker and Harvard Pilgrim Health Care for information including products, benefits, and the value and quality of Harvard Pilgrim Health Care. Acts as consultant on plan design, negotiates rates, and works with internal service areas such as Account Services to provide customer service solutions.</li>
<li>
Manages strategic broker and customer relationships with Harvard Pilgrim Health Care’s elite accounts within the key account segment.</li>
<li>
Develops and implements an overall strategic sales plan with a long-term view. Actively participates in product development and other strategic initiatives in the key account segment.</li>
<li>
Acts as a team resource for escalated issues.</li>
<li>
Understands all aspects of rating and underwriting strategies and can deliver and explain complexities of rate development.</li>
<li>
Develops a strategic sales plan with a long-term view. This may include membership growth or retention strategy.</li>
<li>
Responsible for complex RFPs, reports, membership projections; updates sales data systems; gathers competitive information. Works directly with Sales Associate to coordinate appropriate information.</li>
<li>
Maintains current and thorough knowledge of Harvard Pilgrim Health Care products, benefits, systems, health care industry, legislation etc.</li>
<li>
Attends health fairs and other client meetings and coordinates site visits. Prepares and delivers presentations. Accountable for appropriate level of entertaining clients and monitoring</li>
<li>
Perform out-of-the-box thinking, collaborate with others, and make a difference every day!</li>
<li>
Other duties and projects as assigned.</li>
</ul></div>
