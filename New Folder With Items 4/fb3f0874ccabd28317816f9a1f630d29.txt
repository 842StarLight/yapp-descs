<b>WHO WE ARE LOOKING FOR:</b><br>

<b>WHAT YOU WILL BE DOING:</b><br>
-----------------------
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Acquire, build, and cultivate relationships with senior leadership at the client</li>
<li>Work collectively with client services and with The Trade Desk trading team to cultivate accounts</li>
<li>Communicate the value of The Trade Desk media buying platform by walking through detailed demonstrations of the product</li>
<li>Participate in product road map build out discussions based on client needs with senior leadership, product, &amp; engineering</li>
<li>Take lead in responding to RFP's or new inner agency opportunities, including qualification &amp; win strategy</li>
<li>Coordinate global account discussions with counterparts in APAC &amp; EMEA</li>
<li>Work closely with product, marketing and revenue teams to constantly optimize efforts relating to company-wide goals</li>
<li>Identify and close strategic up-selling opportunities by understanding core client business needs</li>
<li>Using knowledge of the market and competitors, identify and develop the company's unique selling propositions and differentiators</li>
<li>Mentor and manage individuals in the organization with account responsibility</li>
<li>Contribute to The Trade Desk culture</li>
</ul>
