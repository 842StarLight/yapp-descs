<h2>Business Analyst</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Position Overview:</b></p>
<p>
We are looking for a Business Analyst who will be the vital link between our development team, our business objectives and our user community. The business analyst will work closely with stakeholders and clients to define and document software requirements for our development team.<br>
<br>
<b>Job Duties:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Work directly with medical clients to support the software and develop requirements for product improvements</li>
<li>
Create and design specifications as requested by sales and account management</li>
<li>
Liaise with internal and external consultants and managers to document business processes effectively</li>
<li>
Communicate with internal teams and external clients regarding projects and effectively convey solutions along with any impediments</li>
<li>
Ensure that various appropriate infrastructure elements, including documents, periodicals, manuals, policy documents, etc. are kept up to date and are available to stakeholders</li>
<li>
Create and develop functional requirements of systems to improve quality, performances, external interfaces, and constraints</li>
<li>
Validate requirements by confirming they meet customer specification</li>
<li>
Manage requirements and ensure they are stored in automated systems</li>
<li>
Record, manipulate, and make reports using database technologies if needed</li>
<li>
Make contributions in the development of Test Plans and Test Scripts, and execute both as required</li>
<li>
Learn new technologies to develop, maintain, and support existing and new software</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Qualifications</b>:</p>
<ul>
<li>
2-3 years experience as a business analyst in an Agile environment</li>
<li>
At least 1 year of SQL experience</li>
<li>
General understanding of software architecture</li>
<li>
Ability to plan, organize, and structure work</li>
<li>
Ability to multitask</li>
<li>
Ability to work in a team-oriented environment</li>
<li>
Excellent written and verbal communication skills</li>
<li>
Ability to learn new technologies, processes, and methodologies</li>
<li>
Ability to take initiative and manage multiple detailed tasks in a fast-paced and ever-changing environment</li>
<li>
Ability to work onsite at the Revenue iQ office location in Richmond, VA</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Preferred Qualifications:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Healthcare industry experience preferred</li>
<li>
Experience using Jira to capture requirements</li>
</ul>
