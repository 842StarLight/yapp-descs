<p>
Arrow Electronics, Inc. – a Fortune 110 company with global headquarters in Denver - helps the world’s best technology companies think “Five Years Out,” working together to continually innovate the “next big thing”. <p>At present, we have an excellent opportunity for an Applications Developer in our Reno, NV location. Specifically, we are seeking an individual with expertise in Supply Chain/ Logistics applications with technical experience in the areas of Warehouse management/ execution solutions like Manhattan, Oracle WMS or custom warehouse solutions. Responsibilities include, but are not limited to:</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>
RESPONSIBILITIES</b>:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Design and Development of applications built with the Oracle tool set.</li>
<li>
Analyze solution design and create technical specifications based on business/functional requirements.</li>
<li>
Development activities including analysis, design, coding, data migration and testing for production and development environments.</li>
<li>
Customize and develop Interfaces, Reports and Extensions as needed to support business requirements.</li>
<li>
Participate in system-level tests responsible for comparing actual results with expected results.</li>
<li>
Maintenance and Provide support for the existing systems as needed.</li>
<li>
Performs solution design, bug verification, and beta support, which may require research and analysis.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>
COMPETENCIES:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Proven experience in supporting large IT initiatives</li>
<li>
Advanced knowledge and Demonstrated experience in the implementation and support of Oracle applications including SQL, PL/SQL, Oracle workflow, XML Publisher, Forms &amp; Reports, UNIX, Java, C, Pro*C.
</li>
<li>Proven experience on working and developing Oracle Application Development Demonstrated proficiency in SQL performance tuning.</li>
<li>
Ability to work in a fast-paced environment coordinating with internal customers to help support and enhance business systems.</li>
<li>
Demonstrated ability to work in a team environment including the ability to work collaboratively with business users.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>
BASIC QUALIFICATIONS:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
8+ years of experience with at least 5 years of experience as an Oracle Developer in Applications framework using SQL, PL/SQL, Oracle Forms 6i, Oracle Workflow, Oracle Reports, and XML reports.</li>
<li>
Experience and hands-on work with UNIX, Java, C, Pro*C.
</li>
<li>Distribution/ Logistics experience.</li>
<li>
4 year degree (minimum) in Computer Science, Information Systems, Engineering or related field.</li>
</ul>

PREFERRED QUALIFICATIONS</b><b>:</b></p>

Oracle technologies in solutions.</li>

Functional experience – specifically within Supply Chain/Manufacturing experience, including Order to Cash and Quote to Order.</li>

Experience with Oracle Applications would be a plus</li>
