<h2>Account Success Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<b>The Company:</b><br>
Headquartered in Birmingham, AL (Riverchase), ProctorU a growing software business (recently partnered with Google) on a mission to uphold the integrity of academic and professional testing. We use technology - not to "catch cheaters" - but to build trust in the expansion of access to education. Our team is responsible for building and supporting digital solutions trusted by over 1,000 institutions in 129 countries who administer over 2,000,000 exams per year and we plan to continue growing!
<br><br>
<b>The role:</b><br>
The Account Success Manager (or Account Manager, internally) is responsible for ad hoc and highly visible problem-solving, ongoing strategic engagement, and long-term adoption in assigned academic institutions. The Account Manager will consult with assigned partners to design, develop, and drive their institution strategy after the Implementation Manager has completed go-live and the hand-off process.
<br><br>
<b>The things you’ll do:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Drive customer success as the primary point of contact for and sustain exam volume in ProctorU’s largest partner institutions.</li>
<li>Lead creative and business ideation sessions and workshops to develop long-term roadmaps, business cases, and strategies that support future business and current organizational goals</li>
<li>Develop and implement processes and procedures to improve operational efficiency</li>
<li>Manage quotes to customers for software and services work while acting as an escalation point for support issues or other customer concerns</li>
<li>Research, report on root cause of issues, and escalate the resolution of high priority issues and determine when an issue should be marked as out of scope or chargeable (i.e. a feature request)</li>
<li>Take ownership of projects which affect assigned institutions and manage partner expectations</li>
<li>Perform requirements analysis, functional specification of the requirements, and liaise with the Software Development Team to deliver a quality and timely release; work with customers to implement new features</li>
<li>Work with marketing to provide ideas for new customer facing collateral, slides, case studies, etc.</li>
<li>Establish a rhythm of status reporting and issue resolution in the form of weekly status meetings with the Client Development Director, and Regional Services Director</li>
<li>Schedule review meetings with individual project stakeholders and quarterly business reviews to evaluate the overall status of each account and to revisit and adjust (if necessary) the priorities of each account</li>
</ul>
<b>The skills you’ll need:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Approximately 3-6 years experience in a customer success or similar role</li>
<li>Outstanding relationship and rapport building abilities with ability to “command a room”</li>
<li>Process-oriented with greater than average ability to multi-task, prioritize, and balance time across clients, multiple partner relationships and internal initiatives on a daily basis while maintaining a profound attention to detail</li>
<li>Comfortable working autonomously with greater than average ability to discern the difference between situations that require escalation, those that require immediate attention without escalation, and those that are important but not immediate</li>
<li>Professional character that represents ProctorU’s brand and service standards well</li>
<li>Greater than basic technical aptitude with the ability to do minor troubleshooting, testing, and QA</li>
<li>Is patient with and respectful of people and processes. Tries to understand the people and the data before making judgments and acting. Expresses sensitivity and empathy when appropriate.</li>
<li>Focused on getting results for the customer and company; resourceful in solving problems and comfortable making decisions</li>
<li>Strong business acumen, problem-solving skills, and strategic foresight</li>
<li>Advanced written and verbal communication skills</li>
<li>Willing to travel a least 20% to visit accounts</li>
</ul>
<b>The values you already have:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Integrity. Honest, open and ethical practices</li>
<li>Service. Remarkable service and personal leadership</li>
<li>Innovation. Anticipate, challenge, and create</li>
<li>Respect. Debate, collaborate and appreciate each other</li>
<li>Excellence. Quality through relentless improvement</li>
<li>Results. Uncompromising accountability and outcomes</li>
</ul>
