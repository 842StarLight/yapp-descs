<h2>Director, Professional Services Joint Solutions Strategy and Planning</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<h1 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Business Summary</b></h1>
<p>
VMware is the leader in cloud infrastructure, business mobility and virtualization software, and empowers customers with solutions in the software-defined data center to hybrid cloud computing and the mobile workspace. The Dell Technologies Synergy Acceleration Team’s (DSAT) mission is to accelerate our customers’ business outcomes by leveraging Dell Technologies scale and reach; resulting in VMware and Dell Technologies accretive growth.</p>
<p></p>
<h1 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Job Role and Responsibilities</b></h1>
<p>
Our Dell Technologies Synergy Acceleration Team (DSAT) needs a Solution Trains - Director Professional Services to drive GTM strategies, joint account planning and sales initiatives for our joint solutions with Dell Technologies. This role will function as a change agent across a matrix organization, reinforcing and advocating “<b>first and best</b>” principles and improving Sales and Delivery field collaboration. This role will provide Services Sales and Delivery impact and leadership through the creation and execution of strategic programs/approaches that drive alignment between the VMware Field Sales and their Dell Technologies counterparts at a theater, segment and account level.</p>
<p></p>
<p>
He or she should be highly motivated, organized, analytical and foster close working relationships with external (Dell Technologies Services) and internal VMware Professional Services leadership and Geo/Theater leadership teams.</p>
<ul>
<li>
Define, develop, implement and reinforce <b>Services </b>GTM strategies aligned to our key integrated solutions with Dell with an objective of including volume-based deployment and support capabilities for the solutions that we bring to market together.</li>
<li>
Facilitate technology-specific alignment conversations and capability mapping around Solution Trains (SD-WAN, EUC, etc) and ensure delivery and portfolio teams are connected</li>
<li>
Assist in best practice and processes for account planning and Core-to-Services collaboration</li>
<li>
Develop appropriate leadership interlocks and governance to drive tighter in-field alignment and review blockers to mutual success</li>
<li>
Working <b>with global and Geo/Theater leadership</b>, develop strategies to support and meet Sales/Delivery objectives of the region, VMware and the SABs as a whole</li>
<li>
Collaborate with EMC/VMware Sales/Delivery field teams and field leadership to address, document, and <b>resolve field friction and escalations</b>
</li>
<li>
Support and <b>facilitate consistent leadership collaboration between VMware and Dell Technologies</b>, including the joint review of business performance against goals (bookings/revenue forecasting and achievement, trends)</li>
<li>
Along with other VMware executive leaders, represent VMware PSO as part of the Dell Technologies Services Council</li>
<li>
Utilize understanding of company-wide operational and financial goals to make contributions to WW leadership priorities</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<h1 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Required Skills:</b></h1>
<ul>
<li><p>
10+ years of Professional Services experience with a working knowledge of Delivery/Consulting and Services Sales challenges, goals, operating principles and culture</p></li>
<li><p>
Experience working with and reporting to exec and C-level leaders as part of a complex, multifaceted, multinational and distributed organization</p></li>
<li>
Ability to build and maintain internal and external relationships resulting in the expansion of new and existing Services business</li>
<li>
Ability to work independently as well as part of a larger team</li>
<li>
Demonstrated track record of success in organizational leadership and Services strategy</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<h1 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Preferred Skills:</b></h1>
<ul><li><p>
Knowledge, understanding, and past experience with Dell, Dell Technologies and DT Strategically Aligned Businesses (VMware, Pivotal, RSA, Secureworks, Virtustream) strongly preferred</p></li></ul>
