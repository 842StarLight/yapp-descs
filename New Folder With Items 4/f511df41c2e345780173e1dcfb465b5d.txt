<h2>Supplier Quality Engineer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p><b>About Daikin Applied</b></p>
<p>
Daikin Applied is a member of the world's largest HVAC manufacturer, Daikin Industries. Daikin Applied designs and manufactures technologically advanced commercial HVAC systems for customers around the world. Customers turn to Daikin with confidence that they will experience outstanding performance, reliability and energy efficiency.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
Led by the principles of people-centered management, Daikin employees have unlimited potential to influence the business. For years, we've redefined HVAC technology, innovating and pushing to never settle. Together with our customers, we're delivering the solutions to reach new heights of success.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
Daikin Applied, formerly Daikin McQuay, was started in 1924 and has an extensive history of developing new, industry-leading innovations and technology. Our award-winning Rebel rooftop units, Magnitude magnetic bearing chillers, Pathfinder air-cooled chillers and Intelligent Equipment set new standards for efficiency and value for building owners. Also, our extensive water source heat pump and applied technologies, aftermarket service, and parts operations provide support to keep our customers' operations running smoothly. Daikin Applied equipment, solutions and services are sold through a global network of dedicated sales, service, and parts offices.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
Daikin Industries, Ltd. is a Fortune 1000 company with more than 67,000 employees worldwide and revenues in excess of $17 billion in 2016 with nearly 30% of that coming from the U.S. In the Americas we have a strong position in the global portfolio, and the credibility and confidence of Daikin Industries' leaders who are fueling our future with investment.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Specialties:</b></p>
<p>
HVAC equipment, Internet of Things (IoT) for HVAC, energy management, aftermarket service support and replacement parts, and turnkey solutions.</p>
<p><b>
Summary: </b>This position will drive the processes governing supplier quality. The SQE will interface with the supplier base and other business unit departments to qualify new suppliers and parts, resolve nonconformities, and drive continuous process and product improvements.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Essential Duties and Responsibilities </b>include the following. Other duties may be assigned.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
The position supports the business unit with regard to their supplier quality needs.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
Assesses supplier performance by performing analytical analysis on performance data and provides reports/communicates findings regarding this performance to the Business Unit.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
Has regular contact with current DAA suppliers and potential new suppliers.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
Responsible for assessing potential new suppliers and making recommendations regarding approval.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
Responsible for issuing supplier level corrective actions and providing assistance to suppliers in implementing the corrective actions. Also responsible for tracking the corrective actions to ensure response and effectiveness.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
Represents quality in all required aspects of new product development (NPI).</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
Perform Process Capability to determine CpK about a process.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
Understands Six Sigma and Lean Enterprise concepts and the ability to apply related tool sets.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
Some travel may be required.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
