<br>
<br>
<b>POSITION SUMMARY:</b><br>
At SanMar, this team member has a passion for product and finds satisfaction in being a part of the product creation process. This person will be responsible for assisting the Sourcing/Product Development Manager with a wide range of product categories and tasks. The ideal candidate demonstrates leadership skills, is a problem solver, has a positive attitude and willingness to adapt and grow.<br>
<br>
<br>
<b>PRIMARY DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES:</b><br>
<br>
<b>Product Development</b>
<ul>
<li>Demonstrate the ability to lead the product creation process through a complete cycle.</li>
<li>Effective independent communication with Suppliers and Cross Functional Team (CFT) partners.</li>
<li>Collaborate with CFT on design and engineering of seasonal development.</li>
<li>Build and maintain PD portion of technical product specifications (BOM, hangtag /label art, packaging and associated technical images as needed).</li>
<li>Develop Fabric, Trims and Styles with multiple sources and facilitate approval during product development stage.</li>
<li>Review and approve Lab Dips and 1st bulk with color team, involving merchandising team and manager when needed.</li>
<li>Lead product development process improvement on continuous basis with CFT.</li>
<li>Initiate testing with quality team during development and beginning production stages.</li>
<li>Understand ITS test reports at the development and production stages. Communicate with Suppliers, Quality Assurance and Merchandisers on testing results and approvals.</li>
<li>Lead and monitor the review of Product Creation Calendar deliverables across CFT.</li>
<li>Maintain the fabric swatch library and sample racks.</li>
<li>Manage ELC chart and maintain seasonal costing quotes with managers’ guidance.</li>
<li>Complete compliance worksheets on seasonal basis to ensure correct classification of product with compliance team.</li>
<li>Manage prototypes, photo shoot and sales sample reviews with CFT.</li>
<li>Participate in weekly CFT meetings and fit sessions; review and comment on Pre-Production/Top of Production samples with Tech Design partners.</li>
<li>Proof read marketing materials for accuracy of all content.</li>
<li>Execute embellishment testing with Decorator Relations team on new development styles.</li>
<li>Maintain all tested materials and samples and review results or issues with Manager and CFT team.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<br>
<br>
<b>Production</b>
<ul>
<li>Execute Photo shoot samples, Salesman samples seasonally.</li>
<li>Facilitate or execute the writing of purchase orders for all assigned styles, place PO’s with suppliers monthly and confirm capacity and delivery.</li>
<li>Manage production status through reviewing Order Status Reports (OSR) weekly, working with planning and logistic for on-time delivery.</li>
<li>Collaborate with color team on reviews of Bulk color submits, providing business demand and inventory status into decision making.</li>
<li>Administer the resourcing process of current products and programs as required.</li>
<li>Identify and resolve any issues that arise during production.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<br>
<br>
<b>General</b>
<ul>
<li>Special projects as needed or assigned to.</li>
<li>Travel as needed to Factories and Mills for development of new products and to resolve production issues.</li>
<li>Key Partnerships: Suppliers, Technical Design, Merchandising, Quality Assurance, Color, Compliance/Legal, Planning, Logistics, Supply Chain, Accounting and Decorator Relations.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<br>
<br>
<br>
<b>REQUIREMENTS FOR POSITION:</b>
<ul>
<li>Knowledge of garment construction and raw materials.</li>
<li>High level ability to organize, communicate effectively and meet deadlines.</li>
<li>Strong verbal and written communication skills with the ability to clearly articulate goals and objectives.</li>
<li>Strong information seeking and problem solving skills, with a drive to make things happen.</li>
<li>Comprehension, and ability to lead understanding of the design and development process from inception through final adoption and production delivery with the ability to meet deadlines.</li>
<li>Ability to work independently and in a team environment.</li>
<li>Computer skills, proficient with Microsoft Excel, other Microsoft office applications and general data base software.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<br>
<br>
<br>

<br>
</ul>
