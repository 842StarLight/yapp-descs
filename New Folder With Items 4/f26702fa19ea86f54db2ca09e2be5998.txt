Position Description
</div>
<div>
<p>Works as a dedicated customer service resource aligned to a specific customer and manages indirect inventory at the customer’s location. In addition to standard KeepStock, duties can include managing Grainger and Non Grainger inventory within a customer tool crib using Grainger or customer specific software and processes.</p>
<p>Partners with the customer to provide Grainger inventory management services aligned with the Grainger or customer’s internal processes on the customer’s site.</p>
<p>Performs hybrid Onsite Services, Customer Service, and Sales support activities established through custom scope of work processes designed specific to a customer.</p>
<p>Onsite Services: Manages and stocks customer’s inventory levels for Grainger and Non Grainger inventory through KeepStock programs and/or Crib management. Supports the customer’s inventory management and product procurement processes by managing the day to day integration of all in-direct inventory into the customer’s work stream processes. Guides customers to other internal resources such as Technical Support, Credit, Grainger Parts and Sourcing or Sales as necessary.</p>
<p>Customer Service: Provides excellent customer service by resolving problems promptly and accurately in a manner that will retain and promote customer loyalty.</p>
<p>Seller Support: Performs product search and select activity directly with customer in house for end users. Provides quotes based on customer request. Provides specialized reports requested by the customer.</p>
<p>Utilizes continuous improvement methodologies to drive process costs from the Managed MRO operating model.</p>
<p>Executes the standard work detailed for the specific customer requirements which they are assigned.</p>
<p>Ensures compliance to documented policies for customer site and processes in order to provide superior customer service with the on-site service and to exceed customer expectations.</p>
<p>Comply with the safety and inspection requirements of the facility where the service is being provided.</p>
<p>Directly impacts the short and long-term financial results of KeepStock Solutions and customer account growth</p>
<p>Uses independent judgment to quickly solve a customer’s problem using limited standardized practices</p>
<p>Empowered to make decisions that support Grainger’s Service Promise and commitment to the customer’s installed inventory management solution</p>
<p>Work conditions are typical of a warehouse environment on a consistent basis</p>
<p>Significant daily interaction with customers, OSS support team, branch team members and members of the Sales staff</p>
<p>Reports directly to Manager, Onsite Services</p>
<p>Some interaction with vendors, freight carriers and other internal business partners</p>
<p>Will work at customers’ facilities. This is typically a plant type of facility. Conditions can be noisy and space can be limited</p>
<p>High School Diploma or equivalent preferred</p>
<p>3-5 years of experience in related field preferred</p>
<div>Will use computers, scanners, and be in contact with Grainger parts &amp; supplies on a daily basis</div>
