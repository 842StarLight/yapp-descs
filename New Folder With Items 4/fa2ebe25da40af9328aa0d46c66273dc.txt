<h2>ERP Application Consultant (III)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Picture yourself building a successful career at a company that has been awarded “Madison Best Places to Work” two years in a row. Work alongside other talented, driven individuals to help fast growing organizations and mid-size companies enhance productivity, improve efficiency and grow profits.</p>
<p><b>
goVirtualOffice</b> is an award winning, top Oracle NetSuite Solution Provider with over 14 years of experience helping companies with the selection, implementation and successfully expand their CRM, ERP, Accounting and Ecommerce solutions.</p>
<p>
The ERP Application Consultant (III) performs work of considerable difficulty in implementing and consulting on Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP) system implementations. Primary contributor with all requirements gathering, workflow analysis, system design, setup, configuration, general consulting, data mapping/ conversion, custom report development, testing, training and support. Evaluates and recommends changes to current and future ERP system requirements to meet client needs. Has extensive knowledge and experience relating to implementing and consulting with ERP solutions.</p>
