<h2>Business Development Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<h4 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Business Development Manager</b></h4>
<p>Focuses on increasing company sales by developing business with new and existing customers. Primary activities include in-person visits to customers and prospects and outbound calling from the office.</p>
<h5 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Essential Duties and Responsibilities:</b></h5>
<ul>
<li>Identifies and expands potential business development opportunities on sales calls. Builds new relationships with key industries receptive to our products.</li>
<li>Attends Industry Trade Shows and schedules specific site visits to meet with customers and prospects. Existing customer visits to leverage opportunities for business development at current facilities and additional company facilities.
</li>
<li>Works closely with Sales and Marketing Departments to support and advance Marketing Material Content. Works with marketing department to plan marketing and promotions, including advertising, review media and specials sales.</li>
<li>Expected to reach out to existing customers and prospects when working from the office. Use house files and other resources to generate lists of contacts.</li>
<li>Provides support to upper management in deal negotiations, contract development, due diligence, and other business development or alliance development projects.</li>
<li>Truss verticals may include: Compost / Fertilizer; Equine; Grain / Feed; Athletic Facility; Aviation / Aerospace; Bulk Storage / Aggregates; Government/Municipal; Marine/Seaport; Military; Mining / Exploration; Oil / Gas; Recycling / Waste; Sand and Salt; Farming; Zoo / Aquarium.</li>
<li>Greenhouse verticals may include: Aquaponic Grower; Cannabis Grower; Flower Grower; Hydroponic Grower; Nursery Grower; Research; Retail Garden Center; School K-12; College; Vegetable Grower.</li>
</ul>
<h5 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Supervisory Responsibilities:</b></h5>
<p>This job has no supervisory responsibilities.</p>
<h5 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Competencies:</b></h5>
<p>To perform the job successfully, an individual should demonstrate the following competencies:</p>
<ul>
<li>Business Acumen - Understands business implications of decisions; Displays orientation to profitability; Demonstrates knowledge of market and competition; Aligns work with strategic goals.</li>
<li>Judgement - Displays willingness to make decisions; Exhibits sound and accurate judgment; Supports and explains reasoning for decisions; Includes appropriate people in decision-making process; Makes timely decisions.</li>
<li>Planning/Organizing - Prioritizes and plans work activities; Uses time efficiently; Plans for additional resources; Sets goals and objectives; Organizes or schedules other people and their tasks; Develops realistic action plans.</li>
<li>Professionalism - Approaches others in a tactful manner; Reacts well under pressure; Treats others with respect and consideration regardless of their status or position; Accepts responsibility for own actions; Follows through on commitments.</li>
<li>Adaptability - Adapts to changes in the work environment; Manages competing demands; Changes approach or method to best fit the situation; Able to deal with frequent change, delays, or unexpected events.</li>
</ul>
