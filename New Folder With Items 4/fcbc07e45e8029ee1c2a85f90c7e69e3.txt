<b>JOB SUMMARY</b><br>
<br>
The Advanced Advertising Senior Product Manager will be responsible for further defining and launching new capabilities for our industry leading impression based TV Everywhere advertising products. This includes growing and monetizing our Multi-screen TV product with the inclusion of Linear and OTT inventory, scaling support for Attribution reporting, expanding automation and growing our use of data within the product offering. This role will assume leadership in a highly matrixed environment and involves working in concert with, and in support of, other Product leads and managing cross-functional teams, gathering requirements, and driving progress at the center of a fast paced, highly visible strategic endeavor. The role will report to the Sr. Director of Product Management.<br>
