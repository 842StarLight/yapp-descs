<div>
<b>
Your Career</b><p>
As a Competitive Intelligence Engineer, you will play a key role in differentiating our security operating platform from our key competitors. You will be hands-on in competitive analysis, primary and secondary research and will also spend time engaging with internal and external key stakeholders to understand and highlight our unique value proposition. Your content will be used by Sales, Systems Engineering and Partners to successfully position our products and solutions against the competition.</p>
<p>
You must also be a self-starter, keen to learn and experiment with new technologies, be able to work independently and as a team player. Be passionate to drive revenue growth by assisting Sales, Partners, and Systems Engineering teams in large and strategic deals. The job requires 10% travel.<br>
<br>
<b>Your Impact</b></p>
</div>
<ul>
<li>
Create and demonstrate replicable proof of concepts that highlight the key value proposition of our enterprise security products</li>
<li>
Research, analyze and keep up to date tab on competitive landscape, solution positioning, and market strategies to deliver competitive business insights and thought leadership</li>
<li>
Create compelling competitive collaterals in the form of battle cards, technical briefs, webinars and blogs for Sales, Systems Engineering and Partners</li>
<li>
Perform hands-on testing of Palo Alto Networks Next-Generation Firewall capabilities and compare with competitor’s product offerings</li>
<li>
Partner with Product Marketing, Product Management and Sales to strengthen our leadership in enterprise security</li>
<li>
Drive the success of customer engagements by assisting our field personnel with competitive differentiation both on a tactical and strategic level</li>
</ul>
<div><b>
Your Experience</b></div>
<div>
<ul>
<li>
3+ years of hands-on experience with next-generation firewalls</li>
<li>
2+ years of work experience in Sales, Systems Engineering, Professional Services or Consulting in a technical capacity</li>
<li>
Experience in building a successful competitive intelligence framework and maintaining a test lab for technical analysis</li>
<li>
Competitive intelligence certifications such as CIP preferred</li>
<li>
Strong understanding of the cybersecurity industry and current threat landscape especially in network security</li>
<li>
Excellent written and verbal communication skills</li>
</ul>
