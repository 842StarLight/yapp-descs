<p></p>
<p><b>How you will contribute:
</b></p>
<p>This is a great opportunity to leverage your design talent and leadership skills as you lead, mentor &amp; direct the design team on various fire protection system projects. As the Design Manager you will exercise your attention to detail while designing automatic sprinkler systems using HydraCAD, an AutoCAD based software product. You will ensure quality of submittal drawings, calculations, monitor workload of other designers and maintain technical certifications to obtain necessary permits and certificates. In conjunction with Labor Leadership and Sales Project Managers, you will plan, organize and execute projects through activities such as; on-site field surveys, generating hydraulic calculations, submitting drawings to obtain the required building permits, 3D BIM coordination using NavisWorks, preparing stock lists, ensuring the projects adhere to the proposed timeline, and preparing project close-out, operation and maintenance documents. You will be challenged to excel beyond your expectations in team-oriented, results driven environment.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>What you need to qualify:
</b></p>
<p>You will have a minimum of four years of prior relevant experience working with fire sprinkler design. Two years of leadership experience is preferred. Post-secondary degree or completed apprenticeship preferred. You can stand out above other candidates by demonstrating the ability to thrive in a fast-paced, deadline driven work environment. A commitment to providing exceptional customer service, maintaining cutting edge industry knowledge and being able to execute tasks with a great amount of detail will be crucial. If you are ready to demonstrate this expertise, Ahern wants you!<br>
</p>
