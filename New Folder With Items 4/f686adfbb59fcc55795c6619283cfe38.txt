<h2>IT Sales Executive</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div><div><div>
<p>We are looking for a seasoned IT sales professional. This is a new Commercial Account Manager/Business Development role responsible for the entire sales lifecycle. In this position, you will sell technology solutions to mid-sized companies. The successful candidate is a focused and enthusiastic individual with a track record of integrity and success in technology sales and someone who excels in developing new business and managing multiple accounts with the highest level of customer service.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Job Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Identify new opportunities</li>
<li>Develop qualified leads</li>
<li>Perform Discovery and Client Needs Analysis</li>
<li>Effective present our services and solutions to clients</li>
<li>Close deals</li>
<li>Achieve sales targets</li>
<li>Client follow up and relationship development</li>
<li>Maintain CRM Activity and Sales Pipeline (familiarity with ConnectWise &amp; QuickBooks are a plus)</li>
<li>Demonstrate sales professionalism</li>
<li>Maintain sales certifications for key vendors</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p>
</p>
<p><b>Professional Qualities &amp; Experience</b></p>
<ul>
<li>3 years’ sales experience in the technology field</li>
<li>Demonstrated ability to build ‘C’ level executive relationships</li>
<li>Knowledge of enterprise solutions – HPE, Cisco &amp; Vmware sales experience or equivalent</li>
<li>Proven track record of exceeding annual revenue and profit targets</li>
<li>Proficiency with using social selling tools such as LinkedIn</li>
<li>Solid professional communication skills, with demonstrated ability to develop compelling presentations, reports, proposals and price quotations</li>
<li>Seasoned negotiator and strong closer</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Personal Qualities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Highly motivated, takes initiative</li>
<li>Strong ability to influence and persuade others</li>
<li>Wants challenge; enjoys attempting difficult tasks or goals</li>
<li>Persistent and resilient; tenacious when encountering obstacles</li>
<li>High degree of self-confidence</li>
<li>Thrives in a fast-paced collaborative environment</li>
<li>Excellent people and management skills</li>
<li>Flexible attitude and ability to adapt to changing priorities</li>
<li>Enjoys working on teams</li>
</ul>
<p>InaCOMP offers a highly competitive salary based on experience, 401K, and medical insurance.</p>
