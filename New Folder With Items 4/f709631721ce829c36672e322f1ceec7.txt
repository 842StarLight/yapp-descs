<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibility</b></h3>
<div>
<br>
AkzoNobel Inc.–has an outstanding opportunity for an Indirect Tax Specialist. The Indirect Tax Specialist will support and execute in an accurate and timely way the required ongoing, monthly, quarterly, and annual indirect tax related activities for the entities in the region.<br>
<br>
Specific Tasks:<br>
<br>
Maintain and monitor the (indirect)tax compliance of the entities in the region.<br>
<br>
<ul><li>Advise and support specialists and operations analysts related to indirect tax matters tax accrual account. Any interest or penalty payments should be separately identified in the invoices.</li></ul>
<br>
<ul><li>Hold periodic meetings with the RPTTP to discuss the real property valuations that may need to be appealed. Notify and collaborate with the Corporate Tax Department (“ATD”) related to any potential appeal</li></ul>
<br>
<ul><li>Add additional business units to the property tax outsource engagement as needed.</li></ul>
<br>
<ul><li>Maintain and monitor filing calendar to track the timely filing of all returns whether prepared by SUTPP or internally.</li></ul>
<br>
<ul><li>Liaison with Real Estate Manager on a quarterly basis to identify any changes in locations.</li></ul>
<br>
Manage Indirect Tax Audits (Sales &amp; Use – Canadian GST/HST/QST/PST)<br>
<br>
<ul><li>Upon receipt of an audit notification advise management and Tax Department (ATD) Completing an audit will be a collaborative effort with Regional Accounting Center (RAC) and ATD.</li></ul>
<br>
<ul><li>Provide data as requested by the auditor which includes sales and purchase information, copies of tax filing, charts of accounts, exemption certificates and trial balances. Analyze data before providing information to auditor to ensure it is relevant and responsive to auditor’s request. Also determine if any exposure exists related to the information to be provided.</li></ul>
<br>
<ul><li>Collaborate with various departments to obtain the information needed to answer information data requests from the auditors.</li></ul>
<br>
<ul><li>Maintain an audit log to track progress of all audits.</li></ul>
<br>
<ul><li>Identify and implement process changes required to correct areas of noncompliance identified from audits.</li></ul>
<br>
Advise business units on indirect tax matters<br>
<br>
<ul><li>Respond to BU requests for tax advice including requests for exemption certificates from vendors.</li></ul>
<br>
<ul><li>Identify opportunities to streamline and improve indirect tax processes and implement as appropriate. Review monthly tax law update notices from third party sources to keep abreast of any changes that will impact AkzoNobel and advise business units accordingly. Identify and implement process changes required to comply with law changes.</li></ul>
<br>
<br>
Support and execute in an accurate and timely way the required ongoing, monthly, quarterly, and annual indirect tax related activities for the entities in the region. These high-level activities for NAM (US &amp; Canada) are:<br>
<br>
<ul><li>Prepare and file 1099s with the IRS.</li></ul>
<br>
<ul><li>Support the TTL with management of AkzoNobel’s third party sales and use tax (including Canadian GST/HST/PST) compliance provider (SUTPP).</li></ul>
<br>
<ul><li>Run monthly sales and use tax reports from AkzoNobel’s tax software, reconcile to general ledger, review for abnormal variances and provide to SUTPP by the established deadline.</li></ul>
<br>
<ul><li>Obtain monthly sales and use tax reports from AkzoNobel’s accounting third party provider, Infosys, for companies not yet operating tax software, reconcile to general ledger, review for abnormal variances and provide to SUTPPT by the established deadline.</li></ul>
<br>
<ul><li>Resolve any variances identified in the reconciliation between the tax reports and the general ledger and reconcile the tax payments made by the SUTPP to the general ledger. Resolve any variances at month end close.</li></ul>
<br>
<ul><li>Process all funding requests from the SUTPP to ensure timely filings and payments of AkzoNobel’s sales&amp; use tax returns.</li></ul>
<br>
<ul><li>Prepare all intercompany invoices to pass the taxes remitted by the SUTPP to the related business units and legal entities in order relieve the tax liability account. Any interest or penalty payments should be separately identified in the invoices.</li></ul>
<br>
<ul><li>Review and forward indirect tax notices related to returns prepared by SUTPP from the variance states and Canadian authorities to the SUTPP for resolution. Handle urgent indirect tax notices on an as needed basis. Also handle notices related to tax returns not prepared by SUTPP.</li></ul>
<br>
<ul><li>Collaborate with the SUTPP to build a teamwork atmosphere and hold regular status meetings to assure all notices are being handled on a timely basis.</li></ul>
<br>
<ul><li>Inform the SUTPP of tax filing frequency changes received by the various taxing authorities.</li></ul>
<br>
<ul><li>Monitor the Canadian input tax credits that are posted to the tax liability account to assure the business units are properly posting the GST that is paid upon importation to Canada. Take any necessary action in the event the postings abnormally high or low.</li></ul>
<br>
<ul><li>Maintain and monitor filing calendar to track the timely filing of all returns whether prepared by SUTPP or internally.</li></ul>
<br>
Support the TTL with management of AkzoNobel’s third party real estate and personal property tax compliance provider (RPTPP).<br>
<br>
<ul><li>Provide on an annual basis all asset and inventory data requested by the RPTPP. This data must be reconciled to the general ledger prior to hand off to the RPTPP. Review and analyze data to ensure it is accurate and fulfills the RPTPP’s request before providing to RPTPP.</li></ul>
<br>
<ul><li>Review all personal property tax filings before they are submitted to the taxing authorities by the RPTPP assuring the filings balance to the data provided.</li></ul>
<br>
<ul><li>Process all funding requests from the RPTPP to ensure timely filings and payments of AkzoNobel’s property tax returns.</li></ul>
<br>
<br>
Prepare all intercompany invoices to pass the taxes remitted by the RPTPP to the related business units and legal entities in order relieve the property.<br>
