<h2>Seasonal Garden Center Merchandiser</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Love to work with plants? Looking for a seasonal position to earn extra income? Gardenvision is searching for hard working individuals who like plants and working outdoors. This is the busiest time of year in the plant business, and we need people to work 20-40 hours per week from October-February with the possibility to stay on as an Apprentice Merchandiser.</p>
<p>This position will be responsible for the care and presentation of the live goods in the local garden center, restocking, as well as providing customer service.</p>
<p>Our most successful team members work HARD, love plants, know that details matter, follow directions easily and completely, thrive on working outdoors, and are self-motivated to reach for greater levels of achievement and efficiency every day.</p>
<p><b>The 4 Pillars (Work Responsibilities): </b></p>
<p><b>Safety: </b></p>
<p>Work safely, and demand the same of your teammates</p>
<p>Wear recommended Personal Protective Equipment (PPE)â€”boots, gloves and glasses.</p>
<p><b>Presentation: </b></p>
<p>Follow the Area Merchandising process by working the store from front to back</p>
<p>Pull bad product, dead leaves and garbage from fixtures</p>
<p>Add new product according to merchandising standards, pulling racks/pallets to the table.</p>
<p>Shine the presentation by adding signage &amp; pricing, straightening lines and grids, and reorganizing if needed for the greatest effect.</p>
<p>Sweep the area where you have worked, discard garbage, and remove empty racks.</p>
<p><b>Productivity: </b></p>
<p>Insure that every task is completed at every visit</p>
