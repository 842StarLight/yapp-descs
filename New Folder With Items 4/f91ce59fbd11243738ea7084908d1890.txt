<h2>Cook Assistant/Prep Cook</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p><b>The Cook Assistant/Prep Cook prepares food in accordance with company standards and federal, state, and local standards, guidelines, and regulations to ensure that quality nutritional services are provided on a daily basis and that the culinary services are maintained in a clean, safe, and sanitary manner.</b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Essential Activities and Tasks</b></p>
<div>
<div>
<div><div>
<p><b>Operations Management
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Prepares and serves all products and meals so they are palatable and appetizing in appearance by following planned menus and using the recipe cards.</li>
<li>Ensures timely and efficient meal service by preparing food in accordance with quality standards, sanitary regulations, and established policies and procedures.</li>
<li>Stocks and maintains sufficient levels of food products at line stations to ensure a smooth service period.</li>
<li>Portions food products prior to cooking according to standard portion sizes, recipe specifications, and portion control procedures.</li>
<li>Reviews production for raw quantities to prepare and estimate required portions.</li>
<li>Informs supervisor immediately of product shortages.</li>
<li>Communicates inventory of leftover foods to manager for integrating into weekly menu.</li>
<li>Handles, stores, and rotates all products properly.</li>
<li>Refers to daily prep list and opening checklist at the start of each shift and the closing checklist for the end of each shift for assigned duties to complete.</li>
<li>Assists in maintaining department work areas and equipment in a clean and safe manner by participating in cleaning duties, following sanitation schedule, and disposing of food and waste properly for tables, shelves, walls, grills, broilers, fryers, pasta cookers, saut√© burners, convection oven, flat top range, and refrigeration equipment.</li>
<li>Assists departmental leadership in development of cooking staff with training and coaching as directed.</li>
</ul>
<br>
</div></div>
<p></p>
<div>
<div>
<p><b>Quality, Compliance, and Risk Management</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Ensures food production of consistently high quality food, always delivering and presenting a quality plate every time.</li>
<li>Ensures that the resident's individual needs are met by following special diet orders and serving food in accordance with established portion control procedures.</li>
<li>Ensures that culinary services work areas and equipment are maintained in a clean and sanitary manner.</li>
<li>Ensures that established quality assurance, infection control, and universal precaution practices are maintained when performing dietary procedures.</li>
<li>Willingly and consistently shares observations and insights of departmental operations that may improve quality and efficiencies of department.</li>
<li>Attends all-employee and departmental meetings and in-service training as scheduled or as directed.</li>
</ul>
<br>
</div>
</div>
<p></p>
<div>
<div>
<p><b>Customer Relations and Service Delivery</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Listens and responds to the needs of residents and staff members.</li>
<li>Maintains the confidentiality of resident care information.</li>
</ul>
<br>
</div>
</div>
<p></p>
<div>
<div>
<p><b>Administration and Reporting</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Maintains current and accurate records through use of computers and/or other documentation in accordance with company standards and federal, state, and local standards, guidelines, and regulations.</li>
<li>Maintains production orders and usage logs.</li>
</ul>
<br>
</div>
</div>
<p></p>
<div>
<div>
<p><b>All other duties as assigned.</b></p>
<br>
</div>
</div>
</div>
</div>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Qualifications</b></p>
<p>
Education</p>
<ul>
<li>Tenth grade education required.</li>
<li>High school diploma or equivalent preferred.</li>
</ul>
<p>Experience</p>
<ul>
<li>One year experience in a direct food service environment as a cook or in a service position preferred.</li>
<li>Knowledge of cooking methods and food and catering trends with focus on quality, production, sanitation, and presentation a plus.</li>
<li>Proficiency with Point of Sale (POS) and diet management software a plus.</li>
</ul>
<p>Other Requirements</p>
<ul>
<li>Must be able to read, write, speak, and understand the English language.</li>
</ul>
