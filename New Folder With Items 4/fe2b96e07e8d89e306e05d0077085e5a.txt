<h2>Associate Program Manager Digital Marketing</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>The Digital Marketing Associate Program Manager will be responsible for managing the strategy, day-to-day execution, and maintenance of the Paid Search (SEM) and display channels for both the US and international regions. They will optimize these channels towards driving strong profit and net revenue numbers, as well as driving customer acquisition through various tactics. This role provide budget management support and acts as a liaison to internal teams and agencies on implementation, daily management, and campaign analysis.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Essential Functions and Key Responsibilities:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Work in Adwords and Bing to run the Paid Search channel
</li>
<li>Work closely with our Display vendor on retargeting ads and prospecting ads
</li>
<li>Use Google Analytics and other 3rd party software to track performance in each of the channels
</li>
<li>Planning, project management, and implementation of strategies for digital marketing tactics.
</li>
<li>Ownership and continuous review of paid search performance including keyword analysis, landing page and ad copy testing, new product support, etc.
</li>
<li>Consolidate metrics for all digital marketing tactics and provide ongoing reports to communicate performance against goals (ROAS and other KPIs). Provide insights on performance and guidance for program optimization.
</li>
<li>Partner with 3rd party agencies and vendors to further develop shopping feed.
</li>
<li>Run A/B tests in all channels for creative, copy, etc. and report back on findings and implement optimizations based on findings
</li>
<li>Responsible for working closely with vendors to ensure all insertion orders/statements of work and invoices are maintained and processed properly.
</li>
<li>Communicate with internal and external parties regarding all promotions, site optimizations and brand activations, ensuring that opportunities are maximized to support these initiatives within performance marketing channels.
</li>
<li>Maintain digital marketing calendar for respective channels that aligns with Ecommerce, Global Marketing, Online Merchandising and key events.
</li>
<li>Work cross-departmentally to gather assets, business requirements, marketing goals and objectives, etc.
</li>
<li>Stay current with best practices, strategies, legislation, and industry standards related to digital marketing tactics.
</li>
<li>Track and analyze trends, competitive landscape, consumer insights and current state of the business.
</li>
<li>Performs other responsibilities as required
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Knowledge and Skills Requirements:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>3-5 years’ experience using Adwords and Bing required
</li>
<li>Experience working with enterprise reporting tools a must, Google Analytics preferred
</li>
<li>Experience with Google Shopping Feed preferred
</li>
<li>Excellent written and oral communication skills with ability to communicate ideas in both technical and user-friendly language.
</li>
<li>Excellent listening and interpersonal skills.
</li>
<li>Highly self-motivated and directed, with keen attention to detail.
</li>
<li>Able to prioritize and execute tasks and work independently in a dynamic and fast-paced organization
</li>
<li>Experience working in a team-oriented / collaborative environment.
</li>
<li>Strong knowledge of emerging digital media trends and “consumer needs” orientation.
</li>
<li>Passion for golf and/or sport
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Education, Work Experience, and Professional Certifications:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree in Marketing, Digital Media, Business or related field
</li>
<li>3-5 years’ experience in working with a paid search program and digital platforms
</li>
<li>Project management skills and exposure to project-based work structures.
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Work Environment / Physical Requirements:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Office environment, heavy computer use
</li>
<li>Light physical effort equal to frequent lifting or moving of lightweight materials.
</li>
<li>Regularly required to sit or stand, bend and reach.
</li>
</ul>
