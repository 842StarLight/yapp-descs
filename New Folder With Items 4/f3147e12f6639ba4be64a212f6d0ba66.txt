EDUCATION, CERTIFICATION AND SKILLS REQUIREMENTS</b></p>
<p><b>

Ability to work varied hours/days as business dictates;</li>
<li>

<li>
Ability to work independently;</li>

<p><b>
Technical Skills and Abilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Ability to process information/merchandise through system and POS Register system;</li>

Ability to operate and use all equipment necessary to run a store;</li>

<p><b>
DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES</b></p>
<p><b>
Customer Service</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Keep the customer first in all aspects of the job; be courteous and polite at all times</li>
<li>
Greet all customers, assist customers while shopping; be knowledgeable of products and store layout and helpful throughout the store, thank all customers</li>
<li>
Suggestively sell additional products/services</li>
<li>
Handle customer complaints in a professional manner</li>
<li>
Resolve conflicts in positive, productive manner</li>
<li>
May be asked to work independently or in a group</li>
<li>
Accept direction and show willingness to improve performance</li>
<li>
Be familiar with layout of store and merchandise</li>
<li>
Keep sales area on counter free of clutter</li>
<li>
Maintain supply of cups, straws, napkins and condiments</li>
<li>
Keep all frozen and hot/cold beverage dispensers full</li>
<li>
Front shelves during shift; keep merchandise clean, stocked and rotated, including in store room and cooler</li>
<li>
Clean and maintain all equipment according to policies &amp; checklists</li>
<li>
Perform cleaning activities such as dusting, mopping etc.</li>
<li>
Perform minor fixes</li>
<li>
Conduct maintenance tasks such as replacing light bulbs, collecting trash etc.</li>
<li>
Inform supervisors when supplies are getting low or equipment is not working properly.</li>
</ul>
