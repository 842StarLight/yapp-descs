<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div>We're seeking a <b>Reception Manager</b><b> </b>to join our team.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<p>
As our Reception Manager you will:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Consistently offer the highest level of personalized member customer service while maintaining a positive, enthusiastic and helpful attitude</li>
<li>
Greet and welcome new center members, assist with membership and program questions, and front desk operations</li>
<li>
Process payroll</li>
<li>
Assist and provide information for financial tracking reports</li>
<li>
Assist and maintain accurate tracking of locker service program and inventory items</li>
<li>
Respond to Member’s concerns, suggestions and complaints in a timely, professional and consistent manner.</li>
<li>
Assist with keeping the reception, retail, and lobby areas well stocked and clean</li>
<li>
Create reception team schedule within budget, approve time off requests</li>
<li>
Hire, train and develop reception team</li>
<li>
Review information in software for accuracy</li>
<li>
Assemble information for Manager for daily/weekly/monthly reports</li>
<li>
Oversee inventory and sales for retail
</li>
<li>Develop excellent communication system for reception team</li>
<li>
Additional duties when needed</li>
</ul>
</div></div></div>
