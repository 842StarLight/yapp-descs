<p>
GENERAL FUNCTION:</p>
<p>
Provide support to a few relationship managers, with small portfolios, in their daily duties in underwriting and monitoring credit, existing and proposed credit requests, providing high level customer service support, assisting in cross sell effort and performing special projects for the department manager. Prepares documentation required for the processing of loans, reviews status of existing loans, takes appropriate action on loans that are mature or past due, and assists relationship managers in loan closings.</p>
<p></p>
<p>
Responsible and accountable for risk by openly exchanging ideas and opinions, elevating concerns, and personally following policies and procedures as defined. Accountable for always doing the right thing for customers and colleagues, and ensures that actions and behaviors drive a positive customer experience. While operating within the Bank's risk appetite, achieves results by consistently identifying, assessing, managing, monitoring, and reporting risks of all types.</p>
<p></p>
<p>
ESSENTIAL DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Supports the Department Manager, Team Leader, and/or Relationship Managers in their sales and credit underwriting efforts including industry and company research, approval document preparation and assisting in the sales proposal process, including accompanying the Relationship Managers on customer and prospect calls, and various other sales/customer oriented projects.</li>
<li>
Completes all requirements of loan bookings, to include all preparation, review, documentation and filing.</li>
<li>
Develops a working knowledge of all customers, businesses, and prospect opportunities.</li>
<li>
Responsible for all loan document preparation, including third party document coordination, completion and review.</li>
<li>
Collects and reviews all required collateral information.</li>
<li>
Reviews current account status, to include but not limited to, Past Due accounts and Notes Matured.</li>
<li>
Makes appropriate recommendations for these accounts.</li>
<li>
Supports the Relationship Manager in monitoring on n on-going basis information concerning customer performance, abilities, and industry to determine that loan is an acceptable risk. This will consist of but not be limited to statement, covenant and insurance tracking, Dual Risk Rating updates, and Admin Loan Status Report updated and other commercial scorecard items.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p>
MINIMUM KNOWLEDGE, SKILLS AND ABILITIES REQUIRED</p>
<ul>
<li>
Bachelor's degree in accounting, finance or economics preferred.</li>
<li>
1 to 3 years of credit analysis and/or loan review experience, preferably in the banking industry or an advanced business degree with 1 to 3 years of business related experience.</li>
<li>
Must be proficient in basic software applications such as Microsoft Word and Excel.</li>
<li>
Familiarity with the Bank's internal applications is a plus.</li>
</ul>
