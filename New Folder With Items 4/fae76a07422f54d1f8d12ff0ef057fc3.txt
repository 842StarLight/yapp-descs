<h2>Merrill Digital Sales Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Job Description:</b></p>
<p></p>
<p>
In partnership with the Digital Marketing team and the Merrill Line of Business, the Merrill Edge Digital Sales manager will be responsible for driving the management and optimization of the digital sales strategy acrossThe websites. The team lead will work closely with LOB and segment partners to identify, prioritize and successfully deliver new and innovative solutions to continue to meet and exceed the Merrill Edge Self Directed and Merrill Guided Investing digital sales goals. They will work to identify issues to eliminate risk, as well as opportunities for enhancements to the client experience and report out on performance of tests and ongoing weekly and monthly performance of the digital sales funnel.</p>
<p></p>
<p>
This role will support the Merrill Edge Self Directed and Merrill Guided Investing digital sales experience which is a critical distribution channel for Consumer Investments LOB and supports key business initiatives including acquisition and deepening efforts. The role will manage overall strategy and optimization of sales pages, new request intake process and prioritization, QA and publishing via the platform.</p>
<p></p>
<p>
Required Skills:</p>
<p></p>
<ul>

<li>Ability to identify key drivers of business performance and clearly outline trends, risks, and opportunities within ongoing performance management routines</li>
<li>Proven analytical and strategic problem-solving skills with emphasis on creating effective data-driven, customer-centric strategies</li>

<li>5-7 years of work experience in digital and leadership roles</li>
<li>Strong critical thinking skills and business acumen with understanding of digital banking or related industries</li>
</ul>

Directly involved with the largest and/or most complex projects and/or initiatives. Has management responsibility. Is responsible for hiring, managing, training and developing associates.</p>
