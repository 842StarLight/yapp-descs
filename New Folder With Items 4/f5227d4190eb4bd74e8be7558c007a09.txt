<p><b>Job Description Summary:
</b></p>Final Mile Services specializes in professional and cost effective final mile deliveries throughout the United States. With over 100 distribution centers nationwide and 1 million square feet of warehouse space, we are able to provide commercial, business to business and home delivery services. Our solutions range from jobs site deliveries for construction and remodeling projects to retail and home deliveries including appliances, furniture, mattresses and many other products. Our network is growing and we are flexible to fit every client’s need.
<p></p>
<p><b>Job Description:
</b></p>
<p>As a Builder Specialist, you will be responsible for managing the installation process of commercial appliances and ensuring Customer Value DeliveryTM. You'll work closely with the installers to ensure proper application and installation. The environment is challenging, fast-paced, and very rewarding!
</p>
<p></p>
<p>Responsibilities
</p>
<ul>
<li>Work with the sales team to understand new Builder jobs
</li>
<li>Perform installation audits to fulfill customer's monthly requirement
</li>
<li>Builder delivery scheduling
</li>
<li>Pre and post delivery installation inspections
</li>
<li>Delivery team safety
</li>
<li>Parts Inventory Management
</li>
<li>Appliance installation and product handling training
</li>
<li>Damage assessment from customer complaints, property
</li>
<li>Launch new projects/Builder site "Welcome Process"
</li>
<li>Site Assessment with sales team and job Superintendent
</li>
<li>Review safety requirements, policy B2000
</li>
<li>Review installation policies per installation manuals
</li>
<li>Review site readiness
</li>
<li>Pre-call procedure review
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Qualifications:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>High school diploma or general education degree
</li>
<li>5 - 10 years related experience
</li>
<li>Have an understanding of Builder and Home service SKU's
</li>
<li>Understand Builder Site Requirements
</li>
<li>Possess appliance installation knowledge
</li>
<li>Able to cross train all office functions
</li>
<li>Good customer service skills, both written and oral
</li>
<li>Basic computer skills in Word, Excel, PowerPoint and email
</li>
<li>Understand and use the various product websites
</li>
<li><b><i>Must have a clean MVR upon review to be eligible for company vehicle
