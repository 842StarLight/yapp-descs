<b>Sales Engineer</b><b> -</b><b> </b><b>Commercial Accounts, Central</b>
</div>
</div>
<div></div>
<div>As a key member of the Clari team, you will be responsible for supporting our Commercial sales team with Clari’s prospects and customers in a pre-sales focused role. As the solution expert for the Clari Platform, your efforts will begin early in the sales cycle making sure that we understand the customer’s priorities and showing how Clari is the best solution to ease that pain. The goal is to drive the technical side of the sales process, and ensure that the customer truly loves the Clari product.</div>
<div></div>
<div>
<div>
<div><b>About Clari</b></div>
<div>Clari uses AI and automation to drive growth and retention for high-performing revenue teams. Clari’s Revenue Operations platform is currently processing over $300 billion in pipeline, and is used by over 50,000 marketing, sales and customer success professionals across 170 countries. Customers include market leaders like Symantec, Adobe, Alteryx, Workday, Lenovo, Zoom, Medallia, and hundreds of others. Clari harvests and analyzes activity signals from dozens of different business systems, including email, calendar, CRM, marketing automation, to shorten sales cycles, increase win rates, and make revenue more predictable.</div>
<div></div>
<div>The result is passionate and frankly humbling customer loyalty. We consistently hear from our customers how we’ve changed their lives - just check out the reviews on G2 Crowd. It never gets old, and we never take it for granted.</div>
</div>
<div></div>
<div>Day-to-day, you’ll uncover customer pain points, build out our solution to match their needs, present the value of our industry-leading technology, and cultivating relationships with some of the brightest sales minds in the industry. It’s satisfying and enjoyable, and you’ll never be bored. We think you’re going to really like it here.<br>
<br>
The ideal candidate can be based out of Austin, Denver, Phoenix, or Chicago. They will possess a strong work ethic and be upbeat, intelligent, well organized, articulate, and excited about working in a fast paced environment. If you are a “people person” looking for a dynamic role where you get to work alongside professionals that share that same passion for customers, then please apply!</div>
</div>
<div></div>
<div><b>Responsibilities</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Working with the fastest growing prospects and customers to understand company initiatives and map to Clari solutions</li>
<li>Designing and managing the product and technical strategy in the most complex deals</li>
<li>Owning all aspects of custom demos and proof of concepts</li>
<li>Developing and maintaining product expertise, including best practices to provide value to customers by working closely with Product Management and Customer Success</li>
<li>Partnering with other departments (Sales, Customer Success, Product Management…) on strategic company projects focused on scalability and growth</li>
<li>Collect customer product requests to help the Product Management team prioritize product development.</li>
<li>Participating in marketing events and industry conferences by leading presentations, building relationships with customers and helping marketing create content</li>
<li>Contribute to the growth of Clari by being a Brand Ambassador and assist in the hiring of great talent</li>
</ul>
<div><b>Requirements</b></div>
<ul>
<li>3+ years of experience in a technically oriented, pre-sales, customer-facing role: Sales or Solutions Engineer, Solutions Consultant, etc.</li>
<li>Experience with Salesforce, and ability to understand the details of complex SFDC installations</li>
<li>Unique balance of technical and sales skills, as well as pre- and post-sales knowledge</li>
<li>Experience with training sales organizations and driving change management in an organization is preferred</li>
<li>Highly reliable self-starter with a can-do attitude and the ability work with minimal supervision</li>
<li>Successful track record selling products into complex enterprise environments</li>
<li>Technical proficiency installing and administering mobile, web, and desktop applications</li>
<li>Proven track record in delivering value based presentations and demos to prospects and customers</li>
<li>Superior communications skills (presentation, written, and verbal) and demonstrated ability to communicate/present effectively at all levels of the organization</li>
</ul>
