<p><b>Who we are:</b></p>
<p>
ARLANXEO, established in April 2016, is a world-leading synthetic rubber company with sales of around EUR 3.2 billion in 2017, about 3,800 employees and a presence at 20 production sites in nine countries. The company’s core business is the development, manufacturing and marketing of high-performance rubber for use in, for example, the automotive and tire industries, the construction industry, and the oil and gas industries.</p>

<p>
ARLANXEO has a new career opportunity available for an <b>Asset Integrity/Reliability Engineer.</b></p>
<p>
As an <b>Asset Integrity/Reliability Engineer</b> you will be responsible for providing reliability and inspection support for the manufacturing units, have technical oversight of all activities related to mechanical integrity, reliability programs, and maintenance training, mechanical integrity evaluation, fitness for service determination, new equipment inspection, and developing and implementing risk based inspection practices.</p>
<p>
Duties include, but are not limited to:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Providing comprehensive subject matter expertise for PCT and mechanical maintenance and design.</li>
<li>
Advise the maintenance department for technical support of all fixed and rotating equipment. To ensure mechanical integrity and structural integrity of all units and site infrastructure.</li>
<li>
Provide necessary expertise pertaining to PSV inspections and repairs, vessel inspections and repairs, material of construction compliance, and structural integrity.</li>
<li>
Work with Reliability Coordinator to initiate, monitor, maintain, and improve the preventative and predictive maintenance programs for the rubber units and off-sites. Take corrective actions as required.</li>
</ul>
<p>
In order to accomplish this you will possess:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Bachelor's degree in Mechanical Engineering</li>
<li>
Minimum 5 years’ experience with manufacturing equipment asset integrity and reliability.</li>
<li>
Experience with integrity and reliability management programs; continuous improvement of risk based inspection programs maintains compliance with applicable codes and regulations; troubleshooting and resolution of technical issues.</li>
<li>
Ability to lead on incident investigations and root cause analysis on equipment failure investigations.</li>
<li>
Knowledgeable use of SAP and Capstone RBI software.</li>
</ul>
