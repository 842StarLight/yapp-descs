<h2>General Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<b>Position Title:</b> General Manager
<br><br>
<b>Job Description:</b> The Crunch Manager will ensure the members receive the highest quality
<br>of service and facilities. He or she will exhibit an ability to achieve the
<br>financial targets for the club as outlined in the annual budget by
<br>motivating, leading, supervising and coordinating the activities of
<br>employees engaged in servicing our member needs. He/She will
<br>demonstrate an aptitude and command of all company wide policies and
<br>initiatives to ensure the integrity of the Crunch brand.
<br><br>
<b>Reports to:</b> District Manager
<br><br>
<b>Requirements:</b> 4 year college degree preferred
<br>4 years management experience required
<br>Fitness management experience preferred
<br><br>
<b>Special Skills:</b> Excellent written and verbal communication
<br>Creative management techniques
<br>Strong organizational skills
<br>Strong leadership skills
<br>Strong administrative skills
<br>Strong customer service skills
<br>Strong computer skills
<br><br>
<b>Responsibilities:</b><b>Administration/Organization:</b><br>
Demonstrate a working knowledge of all standard operating procedures
<br>and policies that pertain to the club .
<br>Communicate and implement club policies and procedures to employees
<br>Encourage staff to work as a team and be productive.
<br>Illustrate an ability to make decisions.
<br>Recruit and hire the highest possible caliber of staff.
<br><br>
<b>Sales/Revenue Management:</b><br>
Demonstrate the ability to lead, motivate, and manage team.
<br>Achieve desired sales goals.
<br>Achieve desired revenue goals thru the leadership and motivation of
<br>employees.
<br>Implement and support company programs and promotion to help
<br>generate new sales leads for optimum new membership growth.
<br>Ensure that Team maintains proper tracking forms and the daily leads.
<br>Ensure that all promotions are effectively communicated to the team and
<br>all other appropriate staff.
<br>Ensure ongoing prospecting and generation of new prospective members.
<br><br>
Review sales-related written communication such as proposal, letters and
<br>promotional pieces for effectiveness, spelling, accuracy and distribution.
<br>Ensure that he staff has a high level of knowledge about the clubs
<br>programs, facilities and equipment.
<br>Emphasize importance of staff involvement in the community and
<br>neighborhood businesses.
<br><br>
<b>Personal Training/Revenue Management:</b><br>
Demonstrate ability to lead, motivate and manage personal training
<br>department.
<br>Achieve desired personal training revenue and session production goals.
<br>Achieve desired revenue and production results thru the leadership and
<br>motivation of employees.
<br>Implement and support company programs and promotion to help generate
<br>new PT client acquisition for optimum member base penetration.
<br>Facilitate integration of Personal Training products into point of sale.
<br>presentations to maximize the number of orientation sessions scheduled
<br>and packages sold.
<br>Ensure Personal Training team follows proper procedures in session
<br>redemption.
<br>Oversee PT manager in ensuring all components of departmental
<br>objectives are satisfied.
<br><b>Operations:</b> Coordinate and work within club support functions of Fitness, Sales and
<br>Marketing, Accounting, Information Technology.
<br>Support personnel related problems or difficulties by following club
<br>procedure and documentation.
<br>Resolve member complaints in an expeditious and tactful manner following
<br>club procedure and documentation.
<br>Promote a professional and welcoming atmosphere that enhances the
<br>quality of service and care offered to the members.
<br>Ensure the club meets standards for cleanliness, maintenance, safety, and
<br>security.
<br>Conduct weekly detailed inspection of the club with the Maintenance.
<br>Manager utilizing cleaning checklist and forward information to appreciate
<br>staff.
<br>Ensure visible maintenance items are repaired promptly, proper signage is
<br>posted and if possible out of service equipment removed from the floor.
<br>Reinforce to staff the cleanliness is everyone’s responsibility, not just the
<br>maintenance staff.
<br>Ensure proper inventory of maintenance parts.
<br>Track completion of opening/closing checklists, stage management, repair
<br>&amp; maintenance log, locker log and cleaning checklist.
<br>Assist in the processing/submission and approval of payroll.
<br><br>
<b>Financial:</b> Exhibit an understanding of budgets and income statements.
<br>Establish controls of expenses and purchasing of club supplies.
<br>Display an ability to keep expenses at or below budget.
<br>Demonstrate an ability to articulate variances in revenue/sales/expenses
<br>versus budget.
<br><br>
<b>Leadership/Motivation:</b><br>
Serve as a role model for employees.
<br>Communicate effectively by holding weekly and individual meeting with all
<br>key club personnel.
<br>Provide an inspirational environment that welcomes honest feed back from
<br>employees and takes action to ensure a quality, working environment.
<br>Oversee, support, direct and develop department heads.
<br><b>Profit Centers:</b> Illustrate an ability to drive profit center revenue such as personal training,
<br>retail, etc…
<br>Monitor flagged check-in’s to increase revenue and collections.
<br>Demonstrate an ability to increase revenue per member.
<br><b>Meetings:</b> Monthly or Weekly Department Meetings
<br>Employee Training Meetings
<br>Daily “One Minute Meetings” with club staff
<br>Daily Personal Training Manager Meeting
<br>Weekly Club Management Meeting
<br>Annual Performance Evaluations
<br><b>Accountabilities:</b> Implement and support company programs and promotion to help
<br>generate new sales leads for optimum new membership growth.
<br>Oversees expense goals by managing payroll and general and
<br>administrative expenses.
<br>Ensure that the clubs meet Crunch standards for cleanliness,
<br>maintenance, safety, security and physical plant operations.
<br>Keep current in knowledge of key competitors.
<br>Conduct frequent walk thrus.
<br><br>
<b>Measurement Standards:</b><br>
Successful management of all financial budgetary goals.
<br>Ensure standards of clubs cleanliness and customer service excellence.
<br>Demonstrate professionalism by leading by example.
<br>Membership retention.
<br>Timely completion of assigned tasks and projects.
<br>Follow all policies and procedures.
<br>Above description may be subject to change or alteration at any time.</div>
