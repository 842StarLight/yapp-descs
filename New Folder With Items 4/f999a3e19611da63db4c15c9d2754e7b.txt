<h2>Software Support Representative</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Job Summary</b></p>
<p>Capture 3D, Inc, www.capture3d.com , is seeking an Application Software Support Representative to expand our current Customer Support team. Our Customer Support team is a critical component to Capture 3D's overall success by providing knowledgeable, consistent, timely and excellent support to our clients. <b>This opportunity is in our Farmington Hills MI facility</b>.</p>
<p>Our 3D Non-Contact measurement solutions have emerged as a new standard and have been embraced by a wide range of companies such as Activision, Alcoa Howmet, Barnes Aerospace, Boeing , Chrysler, Disney, Ford, GE, General Atomics, GM, Honda, Honeywell, Joe Gibbs Racing, Microsoft, NASA, NIKE, PCC, Rolls Royce, Siemens, Tesla, Toyota Racing Development, and Pratt &amp; Whitney to name a few.</p>
<p>Headquartered in Santa Ana CA with offices in Farmington Hills MI (Automated Solution Center), Charlotte NC, Hartford CT and Seattle WA area offices. Capture 3D is the exclusive North American provider of the industry leading ATOS 3D Non-Contact Scanning and inspection solutions in both robotic fully automated and manual configurations.</p>
<p><b>Responsibilities and Duties</b></p>
<ul><li>Provide client support for Capture 3D software and hardware solutions via phone, Email, face to face and via other electronic medium</li></ul>
<ul>
<li>Perform solution end user training's both in-house and onsite</li>
<li>Maintain client and Capture 3D systems with current application releases, OS and other required software and networking solutions</li>
<li>Support Capture 3D's technical staff (Application Engineers), sales team and management efforts</li>
<li>Utilize Capture 3D's CRM system to log support cases and ensure account information, contacts and problem resolution are updated</li>
<li>When required, assist the AE team for demonstrations, projects, tradeshows and workshop preparation etc.</li>
<li>Help keep required support spare parts stocked, inventoried and replenished</li>
<li>Application software training and onsite visits when required</li>
<li>Travel required</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Qualifications and Skills</b></p>
<p>The ideal candidate should posse the following skills:</p>
<ul>
<li>3 years of industry experience in customer application support and electronics troubleshooting &amp; repair</li>
<li>Computer hardware and software knowledge with established troubleshooting methodologies</li>
<li>Competency with required tools to diagnose, troubleshoot and repair electrical and mechanical issues</li>
<li>Proficient oral and written interpersonal communication, organizational and presentation skills</li>
<li>Ability to work both independently and efficiently to meet deadlines and also be able to work as part of a team</li>
<li>Achieve required knowledge of Capture 3D hardware and software solutions to be able to provide timely support</li>
<li>Ability to promptly answer support related emails, phone calls and other electronic / verbal communications</li>
<li>Become proficient with Capture 3D's Microsoft CRM system</li>
<li>Self-motivated, detailed oriented and resourceful</li>
<li>Knowledge of metrology related hardware and software is a plus</li>
</ul>
