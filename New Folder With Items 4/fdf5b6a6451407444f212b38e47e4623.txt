<h2>Technical Sales Architect</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Who You’ll work With</p>
<p>The Global Specialists (GS) organization was set up to operationalize a consistent, scalable model for Cisco’s sales and SE specialists globally. It will grow the business as Cisco’s tip of the spear for strategic offers vs. many of our toughest competitors, and it will transform our specialist model such that we most effectively put the right GTM and selling resources on Cisco’s most critical opportunities and transitions.</p>
<p>Supporting the Global Specialist team for Service Provider. We are seeking a Technical Solution Architect to join the team to engage with the Service Provider leadership teams and the Geo field Services leadership teams to ensure the teams are aligned in their GTM strategy, fiscal year plan and enablement plan; interlocked. This will accelerate the field’s capability and selling readiness to deliver sustainable growth.</p>
<br>
<p>What You’ll Do</p>
<p>You will drive technical business initiatives, solution offers, and drive the GTM for Service Provider solutions. You will also ensure the successful scaling of our messaging to the Service Provider, Enterprise, Public Sector and Commercial teams.</p>
<p>There will be three main areas of focus:</p>
<ul>
<li>Drive technical and commercial messaging to deliver relevant, competitive, high value offers</li>
<li>
Guide field readiness on existing technology trends and lead the development of transformational technology architectures</li>
<li>
Engage relevant customers to ensure Cisco success in winning SP complex solutions.</li>
</ul>
<br>
<p>Who You Are</p>
<p>You are a strategic thought leader with a depth of experience in driving transformation across a broad set of technologies globally. You have the ability to view the current environment and develop insights for the future requirements of the organization in a 6, 12 and 18-month horizon</p>
<ul>
<li>A Bachelor’s Degree is required Masters in technology is preferred with a minimum of 10+ years’ related experience
</li>
<li>Demonstrated knowledge of IP, Optical Networks and 5G network transitions</li>
<li>Create relationships and credibility at all levels of Cisco and Customer organizations</li>
<li>Proven experience in Technical Leadership roles across Cisco</li>
<li>Excellence in all areas of network architecture, key customer networks and market transformations driven by technology
</li>
<li>Experience in implementing new product introduction processes and customer and employee input/feedback methodologies</li>
<li>Clear success in prior roles, with increasing levels of responsibility and independence</li>
<li>Excellent communication, interpersonal and presentation skills and a desire to work in a team-oriented environment</li>
</ul>
