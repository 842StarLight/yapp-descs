<h2>Account Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">We are looking for a qualified Account Manager ("AM") to oversee and address our customers' digital needs. The chosen candidate will be tasked with generating new revenue at existing iX.co accounts by providing accurate technical counsel and designing digital solutions to business problems.
<br><br>
The AM we hire will be a digitally-savvy professional who will be be results-driven and aspire to achieve specific goals.
<br><br>
Ideal candidates should be able to provide technical, product and business knowledge to support sales process and strengthen customer relationships. The AM is the primary front-line customer facing role within iX.co, and is responsible for the overall growth, quality, and satisfaction of the customer's support services relationship. This role offers an opportunity to be front and center with our customers supporting them in their digital transformation, while accelerating your career by driving business impact.
<br><br>
The Account Manager will report to the Account Management Lead, Professional Services. This role is located in New York.
<br><br>
Responsibilities
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Generate new revenue at existing accounts by providing digital counsel and creating value based on the digital business objectives of the account</li>
<li>Keep track of sales performance metrics</li>
<li>Provide support pre-sales and post-sales processes</li>
<li>Establish and maintain ongoing client relationships</li>
<li>Address all customer queries in a timely fashion</li>
<li>Provide internal team with customers' feedback and help identify potential upsell opportunities</li>
<li>Identify solutions to reduce customer costs</li>
<li>Analyze customers' needs and suggest approaches that meet their requirements</li>
<li>Establish best practices</li>
</ul>
Requirements
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Proven work experience as an Account Manager, ideally in a services business (at least 5+ years experience)</li>
<li>Solid digital background with hands on experience in digital technologies (experience in Project Management a plus)</li>
<li>Familiarity with front-end and backend software development, familiarity with mobile applications, connected devices, etc.</li>
<li>An ability to grasp customers' needs and suggest timely solutions</li>
<li>Excellent verbal and written communication skills</li>
<li>Strong analytical and problem-solving skills</li>
</ul>
Ideal Candidate Skills &amp; Background
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Sales, Project Management, or Development background</li>
<li>Experience delivering digital products/platforms, specifically content/video experiences</li>
<li>Experience in the sports, media or entertainment industries.</li>
</ul>
