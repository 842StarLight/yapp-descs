<h2>Corporate Hotel Sales Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Overview
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<p>Hotel sales manager networks with potential clients, manages large accounts, works to maintain a loyal customer base, and oversees hotel sales and marketing strategies.</p>
<p><i>
This list of duties and responsibilities is illustrative only of the tasks performed by this position and is not all-inclusive.</i></p>
<p><b>
MINIMUM QUALIFICATIONS:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Must be at least 21 years of age</li>
<li>
Bachelor’s degree in Business Administration, Hospitality, related field or four (4) years of industry specific work experience required preferably in a luxury hotel/resort environment</li>
<li>
Proven track record of consistently exceeding sales quotas and targets for a minimum of 4 years</li>
<li>
Ability to represent the organization at a variety of industry and non-industry events. Serve as “face of the company.”</li>
<li>
Experience with cold calling and telemarketing skills</li>
<li>
Must be punctual and dependable in reporting to work as scheduled and completing assigned tasks</li>
<li>
Must maintain a neat and hygienic appearance.</li>
<li>
Required to pass a pre-employment drug screening and obtain and maintain an Osage Nation Gaming license.</li>
<li>
Required to maintain a valid Driver’s License.</li>
<li>
Required to provide documents to show the applicant is eligible to work in the United States.</li>
<li>
Osage, Native American, and Veteran preference shall apply to all positions at Osage Casinos.</li>
<li>
Applicants must be able to perform all essential functions of job duties with or without reasonable accommodation.</li>
</ul>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<p><b>BUSINESS GROWTH/DEVELOPMENT:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Business solicitation, generate sales, manage sales for designated Osage Casino property and other properties as needed.</li>
<li>
Develop and implement an effective strategic sales plan.</li>
<li>
Receives and delegates incoming leads and RFPs. Oversees follow-through in a timely manner.</li>
<li>
Represents sales team at numerous networking events, community events, and organization meetings. Assigns sales team members to attend specific networking events in order to generate more sales leads and community awareness.</li>
<li>
May be required to prepare detailed daily, weekly, and monthly operating reports as required by the reporting senior.</li>
<li>
Makes decisions regarding scheduling of activities and facilities related to banquet room blocks.</li>
<li>
Coordinates sales operations with other departments.</li>
<li>
Oversees banquet space calendar, sales reports, lead lists, and mailing lists.</li>
<li>
Consults with all involved parties of sales to establish goals, services required, and fee schedules.</li>
<li>
Prepares and maintains any required documentation.</li>
<li>
Prepares and provides post-activity evaluation data as appropriate to the objectives of the position.</li>
<li>
Conducts weekly BEO meetings with appropriate members of management.</li>
<li>
Prepares promotional materials and correspondence to all or some participants.</li>
<li>
Responsible for notifying Marketing Department, in a timely manner, of the need for advertising media for press releases, mailings, posters, flyers, newsletters, brochures and other promotional materials.</li>
<li>
Collaborate with the Marketing Department to create effective sales materials and advertising pieces to drive sales to Osage properties. Develop further online presence by examining online resources and evaluating potential ROI.</li>
<li>
Actively seeks and brings in additional business.</li>
<li>
Develop creative sources, programs, and client groups for new revenue generation. Identify segments for growth potential and develop strategies to achieve that growth.</li>
<li>
Creates and initiates action plan to approach and secure new business for sales team. Creates a focused selling message with sales strategies, scripts, sales tactics and techniques.</li>
<li>
A willingness to learn new programs to support future job responsibilities.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
COMMUNICATION:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Makes efforts to keep informed of company information and communications by reviewing property bulletin boards, digital signage, and company newsletter.</li>
<li>
Conducts at least bi-weekly One-on-One meetings with direct reports, soliciting input from employees.</li>
<li>
Effectively relates ideas for improvements to management in accordance with chain of command.</li>
<li>
Ensures company information provided by management is effectively communicated to assigned staff and ensures staff concerns, requests for information, and ideas for improvement are effectively relayed to management.</li>
<li>
Facilitates the flow of information throughout the department by presiding over scheduled meetings with the members of the departmental team as required.</li>
<li>
Performs all other duties and projects as assigned by the Sales Manager and or Director of Hospitality.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
DEPARTMENT OPERATIONS:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Assists internal and external clients in developing and implementing their banquet or room block.</li>
<li>
Provides on-site coordination including problem-solving and decision-making.</li>
<li>
Approves banquet and room block sales contract negotiations to ensure negotiated rates are in line with the overall financial goals of the company.</li>
<li>
Acts as liaison to event participants and casino departments.
</li>
<li>Manage relationships with area vendors to provide services for clients and hotel guests.</li>
<li>
Analyzes current client base or target market for the hotel.</li>
<li>
Devises new ways to expand that client base.</li>
<li>
Develops promotional and advertising materials for the hotel.</li>
<li>
Oversees distribution of marketing materials.</li>
<li>
Reaches out to organizations and businesses that might require a hotel for conferences and events.</li>
<li>
Meets with representatives from these companies to explain the hotel’s merits and amenities.</li>
<li>
Networks with wedding planners and special event planners to encourage hotel bookings.</li>
<li>
Designs and advertises special group rates and packages to increase larger bookings.</li>
<li>
Overcomes hesitations of clients to close a sale.</li>
<li>
Manages accounts of high-profile clients.</li>
<li>
Processes payments and applies discounts as necessary.</li>
<li>
Addresses and resolves any issues or complaints regarding sales or marketing.</li>
<li>
Ensures that guests are enjoying their stay at the hotel and that large group needs are met.</li>
<li>
Maintains positive relationships with clients to encourage repeat and recurring business.</li>
<li>
Responsibly represent the Osage Casino and Hotel through conducting conferences, making proposals and other external sales presentations to prospective customers to solicit, explain, negotiate and close hotel and banquet sales. Utilize discretion and authority to negotiate contracts, commit Osage with customers, and establish agreements with vendors.
</li>
<li>Travel and engage in sales activities outside of the property to meet with and entertain customers and prospective customers, participate in tradeshows, and industry and community events to promote facilities, to make proposals, maintain contacts, and to close deals.</li>
<li>
Write proposals and or contracts to advise prospective groups for meeting space and or banquet date, space and rate availability for group’s accommodations for moderately complex meetings. Develop and quote prices.</li>
<li>
Attend weekly sales departmental meetings and other scheduled meetings to support business operations, e.g., tentative review, lost business review, group-pick up etc.</li>
<li>
Conduct site inspection and property tours with potential customers, visiting guest rooms, meeting space and banquet facilities, as well as other hotel facilities.
</li>
</ul>
<p><b>GUEST SERVICE:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Provides excellent internal and external guest services, treating internal and external guests with courtesy and consideration at all times.</li>
<li>
Cooperates and communicates with all fellow employees, always exhibiting mutual respect and consistently projecting a positive, helpful image/attitude.</li>
<li>
Arranges banquets, meetings, trainings, receptions and any and all other applicable sales activities.</li>
<li>
Develops, manages, and nurtures new business relations with all clients and casino guests.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
REGULATORY COMPLIANCE:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Monitors all relevant activities of the department, to ensure that all applicable laws, rules, regulations and controls of the organization, and the National Indian Gaming Commission (NIGC) and the Osage Nation Gaming Commission (ONGC) are understood and enforced by department personnel.</li>
<li>
Maintains excellent working relationships with other Company staff and all applicable regulatory commissions and other agencies as may be applicable to ensure compliance.</li>
<li>
Performs all duties in accordance with company core values, objectives of the Osage Nation, internal policies and procedures, as well, as applicable laws and gaming regulations, including but not limited to, the state-tribal compact, IGRA, MICS, ONGR, the Bank Secrecy Act, Office of Foreign Asset Control, USA Patriot Act and Privacy Act.</li>
<li>
Ensure assigned staff is aware of, understand and comply with regulatory requirements and enforces regulatory standards through discipline when necessary.</li>
</ul>
