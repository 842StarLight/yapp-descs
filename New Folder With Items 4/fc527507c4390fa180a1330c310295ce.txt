<p><b>General Summary</b></p>
<p>The Engineering Rotational Program will provide high potential entry-level engineers the opportunity to professionally develop, participate in training and gain experience through a series of rotations, under the leadership of an assigned mentor, in the following areas: Product Service Line, Engineering, Manufacturing and Sales &amp; Operations. The program will also provide numerous opportunities to network, participate in social events and develop leadership skills, in addition to business acumen. At the conclusion of the program, program graduates will transition into one of the previously mentioned areas.
</p>
