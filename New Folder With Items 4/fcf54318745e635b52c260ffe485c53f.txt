<h2>Multimedia Sales Executive</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>MediaNews Group is seeking a Multimedia Sales Executive to join the team! MediaNews Group offers news reporting and third party advertising and marketing opportunities through its multi-platform products which include: web, mobile, tablet, and print. We are one of the largest media groups in the U.S., serving high-value audiences and connecting advertisers to them. MNG publishes award-winning content (49 Pulitzers) that amasses and engages a nationwide audience via 800 multi-media platforms including web, mobile, social, and print. We are transforming the news industry.</p>
<p>We’re looking Multimedia Sales Executive to join our fast-paced, professional team. Our Sales Execs focus on selling our portfolio of advertising solutions, with a heavy concentration on print and growing digital initiatives. Candidates in this position are responsible for targeting new business on a weekly/monthly basis and need to work well within a deadline environment, have the ability to problem solve and must be able to learn quickly and adapt to a changing marketplace. We are looking for talented, motivated individuals in the Greater Montgomery County area.</p>
<p>Primary goal is build business that drives sales revenue while maximizing results for the customer and company.<br><b>Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Sells display and digital advertising to new and assigned local businesses.</li>
<li>Additionally, would be responsible for gaining the customers' confidence, building rapport with all levels of decision-makers, understanding the accounts' marketing goals and learning more about how our products can meet their needs.</li>
<li>Contacts all approved businesses on a regular basis by telephone or in person.</li>
<li>Develops and creates marketing campaigns based on selective use of competing media to complement strengths of available print and digital advertising. Maintains and develops a thorough knowledge of the category, industry, and assigned client base.</li>
<li>Achieves at least the minimum sales goals in areas to be determined by sales manager.</li>
<li>Provides courteous and efficient service to any advertiser and adheres to all company policies, procedures and standards of acceptance.</li>
<li>Monitors advertising in competing media and growth from new businesses within the category to generate new advertisers and suggest opportunities for new sales efforts.</li>
<li>Demonstrates consistent follow-through with accounts, management, production and other applicable departments at the paper.<b>Qualifications</b>
</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s Degree preferred</li>
<li>Ideal candidate has experience in face-to face sales</li>
<li>Proficient in Microsoft Office (Word, Excel, PowerPoint)</li>
<li>Media including digital advertising familiarity is a plus but not required.</li>
</ul>
