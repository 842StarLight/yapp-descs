<h2>Senior Product Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>
<div>We believe that a great writing experience is fast, outcome-focused, and alive with your ideas. Going from start to finish on a document in three minutes. Knowing who will respond to anything you've written before you even press send. Writing software that puts your ideas at the center, with language that is certain to land the way you want.</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
Our augmented writing platform is delighting customers, and we’re looking for a creative and passionate product manager to join our Seattle team.</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
In this role, you’ll engage with customers and our executive team to shape the vision and strategy for Augmented Writing. You’ll be an expert on our products, the competitive landscape, and the needs of our customers. You will confidently champion our vision, both internally and to the outside world. And you’ll also be hands on, working closely with designers and engineers to build the future product you’ve imagined.</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
Are you excited to lead the evolution of a remarkable user experience that is already changing how people write? Do you want to be involved from initial brainstorming through completed sales? Would you like to create something new that really makes a difference? If so, Textio is a great place to work, to learn, and to experiment.</div>
</div>
<div>
<p><b>
We’d love to hear from you if:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
You have experience partnering with engineers and designers to build and ship products people love</li>
<li>
You can work face to face with customers to learn about their needs and envision product enhancements to solve their problems in innovative and valuable ways</li>
<li>
You can develop a product roadmap that aligns with current customer needs and the long term Textio vision, and prioritize a backlog to efficiently meet those goals</li>
<li>
You are an excellent storyteller, who can confidently communicate product vision to both small internal teams and large external audiences</li>
<li>
You know how to translate customer feedback and strategic product goals into a spec-level plan for engineering execution
</li>
<li>You are the project expert and the best representative of ongoing work in your area of ownership</li>
<li>
You ensure that the teams you work with feel confident, informed, and efficient. You actively identify issues and remove hurdles before they disrupt the team from meeting goals</li>
<li>
You have the organizational skills to coordinate the launch of features and products across engineering, sales, marketing, customer success, and the executive team</li>
<li>
You measure the success of product changes with usage data, and respond to the surprises and opportunities that the data reveals</li>
<li>
You’re excited by data, written communication, and the chance to pioneer the emerging field of augmented writing</li>
<li>
You’re eager to become an expert on the shifting landscape of hiring, productivity, and successful business writing</li>
<li>
You’re committed to quality as a top priority—you pay attention to what you make, and take pride in it</li>
<li>
Your track record demonstrates a love of experimenting, building, and learning new things</li>
<li>
You have a point of view but are low ego</li>
</ul>
</div>
<div>
<div>
Bonus points for experience working on products where experimentation, machine learning, and data science are core to development.</div>
