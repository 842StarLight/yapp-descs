<h2>Account Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Who you are</b></p>
<p>
Work4 is seeking an Account Manager to help organizations revamp their social recruitment strategies. Ideally, you have experience with renewing, up-selling and cross-selling B2B technology to existing clients. Your clients will see you NOT as a sales person, but as a trusted advisor and expert in your domain area. You understand that showing professional courtesy and having a customer centric approach is key to winning business.</p>
<p>
We are looking for someone who:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Has the ability to learn quickly and to apply new information in customer-facing scenarios.</li>
<li>
Is independent, self-motivated and success-driven, yet willing to work within a team environment.</li>
<li>
Thinks that excellent human relation skills and a sense of customer service are the key to a company's success.</li>
<li>
A data focused, ambitious attitude - the numbers tell a story</li>
<li>
Passion to educate and sell while focused on solutions - you're not easily intimidated</li>
<li>
Is process oriented</li>
<li>
Has experience in account management</li>
<li>
Is tech savvy and has a background in software sales or software management</li>
<li>
Pays strong attention to detail, is extremely organized, and has strong 'follow-up' skills</li>
<li>
Is keen on working in a startup environment, where processes and products are constantly changing and evolving</li>
<li>
Has excellent written and verbal communication skills and can relay information in a timely manner</li>
<li>
Desires to become knowledgeable about the Work4 products and continuing learning our account management methodology</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
What You'll Do</b></p>
<p>
You will be working along with the US New Business Sales team, and will be responsible for generating new business.</p>
<p>
More precisely, you will:</p>
<ul>
<li><p>
Build long-term client relationships by listening to client needs, demonstrating the benefits of Work4 products, and recommending appropriate solutions</p></li>
<li><div>
Help in the retention and growth of your book of accounts.</div></li>
<li><div>
Know the current status of both account relationship and performance for each of your assigned accounts.</div></li>
<li><div>
Work closely with key client decision makers helping them to articulate their goals and success metrics.</div></li>
<li><div>
Assist our clients in gaining clarity for their recruitment marketing strategies.</div></li>
<li><div>
Develop real connections through phone, email, and professional networking</div></li>
<li><div>
Identify client's needs</div></li>
<li><div>
Provide timely and accurate information to clients regarding their requests, complaints, and questions</div></li>
<li><div>
Keep all clients up to date with product enhancements, changes, or glitches</div></li>
<li><div>
Ensure that customers receive top of the line customer service and are fully satisfied through communicating proactively</div></li>
<li><div>
Generate proposals and negotiate with prospects while knowing how to tackle objections</div></li>
<li><div>
Build and develop account relationships and close sales quickly</div></li>
<li><p>
Light travel when appropriate</p></li>
</ul>
