<h2>Account Executive, Merchant Business</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">Join Plastiq at a time of rapid growth!

As our dedicated Account Executive, Merchant Business will be responsible for new commercial merchant acquisition across targeted verticals. You will work from a targeted leads list as well as identify prospects that fit our target merchants. A successful Account Executive will be someone who is customer-centric in their approach, obsessed with helping their clients grow their businesses, and has a strong entrepreneurial mindset. As a part of a fast-growing financial technology company, there is a need for frequent iteration and adaptation of your approach while maintaining an unrelenting positive spirit as you do so.
<br><br>
About You
<br><br>
<ul>

<li>Experience selling new payment concepts to finance teams, accounts receivable and payments professionals at merchants</li>
<li>Demonstrated understanding of Business to Business billing and payments processes</li>
<li>Previous sales methodology training and full sales cycle management</li>
<li>You thrive in a constantly changing environment. You can start your own initiatives, can quickly shift focus when met with new data and are not afraid to experiment with new approaches</li>
