What You'll Do
<br><br>
The primary responsibility of the Contracts Analyst will be to assist the in-house attorney with customer and vendor contracts, legal compliance, and general legal department operations. This includes responsibility for maintaining the company's contracts and other files, assisting with the contracting process, including contract execution and tracking, preparing draft contracts, filing, and providing other assistance as needed.
<br><br>
The Contracts Analyst position reports to the Contracts Manager/In-House Attorney and will not have direct reports.
<br><br>
Key responsibilities &amp; duties
<br>---------------------------------------
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Support customer and vendor transactions, including contract review, document preparation, and facilitation of signatures for vendor deals</li>
<li>Organize and maintain legal documents in paper and/or digital format as required</li>
<li>Provide administrative support as required, including document filing, record-keeping, compilation of spreadsheets/data</li>
<li>Respond to internal queries from sales and other departments as necessary</li>
<li>Other duties as assigned</li>
</ul>
