<h2>Sales Development Representative (Software/SaaS)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>ChiroTouch, a healthcare software company, is the market leader in the chiropractic practice management space. Our software helps doctors create a positive in-practice experience for patients—from sign-in to sign-out. Our software also makes a noticeable impact in the chiropractor's front office by providing a convenient, automated, and intuitive workflow for staff.</p>
<p>
As a Sales Development Representative (SDR) on our growing Inside Sales Team, you'll bring ChiroTouch's platform to practices across the U.S, spending about 80% of your day on the phone. You'll do this by qualifying prospective customers through targeted questions about their practice, leading you to learn how likely they are to be a good match for ChiroTouch's services. Your initiative, persistence, phone and email skills will be a critical part of your ability to quickly determine customer opportunities.</p>
<p>
As an SDR at ChiroTouch, you'll benefit from a defined career path that will develop your sales and business skills, empowering you to self-promote into more senior roles and higher pay opportunities. Since we're constantly growing and creating new opportunities for employees, it truly is an exciting time to be part of our growing company!</p>
<p><b>
Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Maintain a high number of outbound calls (minimum, 80-100 calls a day)</li>
<li>
Achieve monthly quota of demonstrations completed for software</li>
<li>
Articulate ChiroTouch's value proposition to decision makers to assess buying interest</li>
<li>
Answer prospect's questions with the goal of moving them to an appointment</li>
<li>
Schedule appointments between prospects and Account Executives</li>
<li>
Maintain up-to-date software and industry knowledge</li>
<li>
Record prospect information in CRM database, Salesforce.com</li>
</ul>

Have met or exceeded personal/team goals</li>

Customer service skills with the ability to listen and empathize</li>
<li>

</ul>
