<b>Technical Architect - Red Hat</b><br>
<br>
Ready to OPEN a new world?<br>
<br>
Red Hat's complete, open source portfolio runs up, down, and throughout the stacks of the world’s most demanding datacenters. If airlines, telcos, commercial banks, healthcare companies, and every cabinet-level agency in the federal government rely on Red Hat, you can too. The challenge is most organizations do not have the resources, knowledge or support to navigate the disruption this rapid technology growth is causing.<br>
<br>
Clients need our help. Their IT infrastructures cannot keep up with the pace of change their internal and external stakeholders demand. You can solve these issues for our clients. As a Technical Sales Engineer you will work with Softchoice sales reps and customers on Red Hat renewals, deal registrations and overall Red Hat program management.<br>
