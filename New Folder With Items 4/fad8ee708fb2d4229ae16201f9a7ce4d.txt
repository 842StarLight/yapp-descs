<p></p>Maxar is seeking a hands on Technical Project Manager to manage a program comprised of projects that perform data science activities and develop application software for the collection, processing, visualization, analysis, management, and dissemination of intelligence data. The Technical PM will interact directly with end user customers and customer executives, and manage individual projects and report back on resources, budget, and client expectations. There will be multiple projects running simultaneously and technical support will need to be provided. The Technical PM will mentor and coach team leads, interact with corporate staff, and assist with business proposals, planning workforce, and report overall metrics to company leadership.
