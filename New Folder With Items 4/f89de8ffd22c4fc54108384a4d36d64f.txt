<h2>Associate Product Manager - API</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div><div><div>
<div>The Product Management team is responsible for setting and driving the company strategy for all platforms, applications, and features. The team provides cross-functional leadership that bridges gaps within the various groups in the company such as engineering, sales and marketing, and support. We shape the roadmap our products, create clearly-defined requirements documents, and work closely with our technology teams to continually advance the Company’s product portfolio to be best in class.<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<div>The <b>Associate Product Manager - API</b> is critical to the success of Stamps.com’s product offerings and will directly contribute to drive the design and deployment of new features for our API Platforms. The role will act as intermediary between internal and external stakeholders (senior management, partners, and customers) to drive the design and to deploy new features, improvements. The APM-API will work closely with developers, QA engineers, product managers, and internal stakeholders to develop creative solutions to address our partners’ and customers’ needs.
</div>
<div>The APM will develop a deep understanding of each feature and how it affects key metrics (such as revenue) as well as customers’ and partners’ workflows. The APM must have the ability to articulate and justify their implementation against competing features and priorities across each of the stakeholder groups. This position offers the opportunity to work on market-leading products at an established, yet rapidly growing tech company.</div>

Stamps Company Overview:</b></div>
<div>
Stamps.com (NASDAQ: STMP) is the leading provider of postage online and shipping softwaresolutions to nearly 750 thousand customers, including consumers, small businesses, e-commerce shippers, enterprises, and high volume shippers. Stamps.com offers solutions that help businesses run their shipping operations more smoothly and function more successfully under the brand names Stamps.com, Endicia®, ShipStation®, ShippingEasy® and ShipWorks®. Stamps.com’s family of brands provides seamless access to mailing and shipping services through integrations with more than 475 unique partner applications.</div>
