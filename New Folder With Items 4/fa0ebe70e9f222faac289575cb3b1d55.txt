<div><div>Position Overview<br>
The role of the FTZ Administrator is to properly log and account for all FTZ imports, exports and subsequent corrections that may be needed to process in the Future Electronics FTZ software.<br>
<br>
Responsibilities<br>
 Examine product information to ensure compliance with regulations.<br>
 Account for goods into and out from the MADC FTZ.<br>
 Process any value or quantity discrepancies.<br>
 Stay abreast of changes in import or export laws or regulations by reading current. literature, attending meetings or conferences, or conferring with colleagues.<br>
 Update knowledge of legal or regulatory environments.<br>
 Maintain proper recordkeeping in compliance with all CBP regulations.<br>
 Coordinate regulatory documentation activities.<br>
 Advise others on legal or regulatory FTZ compliance matters.<br>
 Monitor or trace the location of goods, and work with carriers to ensure goods can move in customs bond to and from the FTZ.<br>
 Liaise with CBP Memphis port officials regarding FTZ compliance.<br>
<br>
 Maintain relationships with customs brokers in other ports to expedite clearing of cargo.<br>
 Prepare regulatory or compliance documentation as needed.<br>
<br>
General Administrative Functions :<br>
 Daily data entry processing with in Questaweb FTZ software.<br>
 Prepare reports and communicate with Trade Compliance management teams.<br>
 Maintain CBP compliant recordkeeping process.<br>
 Create and maintain SOPs as needed.<br>
