<h2>FinancialForce & Salesforce Admin</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">The Revenue team is the driving force of Weedmaps and the Sales Enablement team has been tasked with improving the sales productivity of our salesforce through enabling our sales team by automating manual tasks and configuring our ERP and CRM systems to reflect business needs. To achieve these goals, we are looking for a FinancialForce &amp; Salesforce admin.
<br><br>
As a Financialforce Administrator, you will effectively be part of the Sales Enablement team and have the opportunity to partner with other teams across the business organization. In this role, you will collaborate with a wide variety of internal teams on projects including: closely working with stakeholders on business and build teams to drive process and execution. You will have the opportunity to interface and partner with several teams including Sales, Marketing, Finance, and Accounting.
<br><br>
<b>The impact you'll make:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Tending to the everyday requests and questions from Finance, focused on FinancialForce PSA with some additional Salesforce Platform responsibilities</li>
<li>Administrative tasks, including but not limited to assigning user profiles, reviewing access, creating fields, workflows, etc</li>
<li>Participating in our agile development process with our team of Salesforce developers to design, test, and implement new features on FinancialForce</li>
<li>Responsible for end-user administration and support of the Salesforce.com platform, Financial Force and other installed or related applications</li>
<li>Duties involve diagnosis and resolution of issues impacting employee productivity and business throughput as well as end-user training.</li>
<li>Assists with the overall configuration, design, and functionality of enterprise applications</li>
<li>Configure and maintain custom objects, record types, fields, page layouts, formulas, data validation rules, custom workflows, approval processes, installed packages, etc. within Salesforce.com</li>
<li>Maintain user roles and profiles, security settings, access settings, etc. (User Profiles, Role Hierarchy, Sharing Rules and Security)</li>
<li>Support change control and best practices with regard to system maintenance, configuration, development, testing, data integrity, etc.</li>
</ul>
<b>What you've accomplished:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>2+ years of FinancialForce administrator experience supporting core ERP and RevRec Modules</li>
<li>2+ Years Salesforce administration on Sales Cloud and/or Community Cloud platforms</li>
<li>Think in terms of simplicity and scalability - no need to create hundreds of profiles per micro use-case</li>
<li>Have a knack for listening and interpreting user requirements and then turning them into action</li>
<li>Experience with Agile development practices and a sprint delivery model</li>
</ul>
<b>Bonus points:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Salesforce.com certification(s)</li>
<li>Experience with SOQL/SQL - familiarity and comfort with querying data</li>
<li>Any developer experience on the Salesforce platform or other web application platforms</li>
</ul>
