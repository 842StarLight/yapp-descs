<h2>President</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Opportunity</b></p>
<p>Our mission is to provide the highest quality durable medical equipment and outstanding patient service, every day. Our team of well trained and compassionate care representatives provide the standard in patient and family education on all equipment delivered and in multiple languages. We understand the importance and strength of hospice relationships. Our partners have entrusted Montgomery DME with end of life patients for over 22 years. We are an extension of the hospice plan of care. We offer a competitive compensation package, including bonus equity shares.</p>
<p>We are looking for a person who can assume this key leadership role responsible for strategic planning, sales management, and financial performance. Lead, motivate and develop executive team: Operations, Finance and Business Development. This position will lead the team to ensure optimal customer satisfaction by establishing goals and objectives that are defined as a company standard for industry growth; for increasing revenue, increasing market share with effective sales team management and utilizing an effective marketing strategy to enhance the overall success and growth. They will be responsible for ensuring and improving the performance, productivity, efficiency and profitability of the organization through the execution of effective methods and strategies.</p>
<p>Please visit our web site: https://www.montgomerydme.com</p>
<p><b>Responsibilities include: </b></p>
<p>· Develops, implements, and executes a strategic growth business plan for the company including achievement of assigned metrics such as those related to financial forecasts and customer satisfaction.</p>
<p>· Collaborates and communicates at all levels of the organization to meet corporate goals.</p>
<p>· Ensures compliance with local, state, and federal regulations, and company policies, procedures, and compliance programs.</p>
<p>· Approves contracts, actively assisting with negotiations.</p>
<p>· Maintains knowledge of trends, developments, new technologies, and market conditions relevant to the industry.</p>
<p>· Keeping client satisfaction as the primary focus of the team</p>
<p>· Exceeding client expectations = job #1</p>
<p>· Cultivating and developing referral relationships</p>
<p>· Regularly communicating with staff on opportunities, results, and challenges</p>
<p>· Training staff and overseeing the continuing education and development of manager</p>
<p>· Overseeing marketing and advertising while keeping a consistent and clear message through the use of print, mail and digital</p>
<p>· Developing new markets, products and services</p>
<p>· Maintaining an active profile in the referral community – local and regional</p>
<p><b>Knowledge, Skills and Abilities required: </b></p>
<p>· 10-15 years of progressive leadership work experience in durable medical equipment industry preferred, but not required.</p>
<p>· Thorough knowledge of compliance requirements and governmental regulations.</p>
<p>· Ability to produce sales and implement marketing strategies.</p>
<p>· Ability to develop policies and procedures that are effective and clear.</p>
<p>· Proficient in Microsoft Office applications required.</p>
<p>· Experience in managing several projects at the same time.</p>
<p>· Excellent interpersonal and negotiation skills.</p>
<p>· Excellent communication skills – verbal, writing and listening.</p>
<p>· Hands-on attitude.</p>
<p>· High sense of urgency, results-oriented, decisive, organized, diplomatic and tactful.</p>
<p>· Strong analytical and problem-solving skills with a proven ability to exercise initiative, judgment, and discretion.</p>
<p>· Ability to function well in a high-paced and at times stressful environment.</p>
<p>· Ability to be flexible and to adapt to change.</p>
<p>· Ability to apply business acumen to service opportunities and challenges.</p>
<p><b>Education and Experience: </b></p>
