<p><b>Media Storm (A Division of Water Cooler Group) New York, NY (SOHO)</b></p>
<p><b>Manager, Advanced Data Modeler</b></p>
<p><b>Job Summary</b>: The Manger, Advanced Data Modeler will leverage technology and advanced analytical methods to organize, analyze, evaluate and discover new insights hidden in large scales of data, and help us make smarter decisions to deliver even better products. Your primary focus will be in applying data mining techniques, doing statistical analysis, and building automating data collecting and reporting system integrated with our products.</p>
<p><b>Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Statistical modeling and data mining on both structured and unstructured data to extract insights in a timely manner</li>
<li>Work with Market Analysts to refine the methodology, data collection and modelling</li>
<li>Investigate and evaluate new datasets</li>
<li>Design and build robust and scalable solutions for structured and unstructured data conducive for business insights, reporting and analytics</li>
<li>Use business intelligence tools and applications to deliver research findings to market teams</li>
<li>Develop software and websites to deliberate our analytic products
</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Requirements:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Strong communication skills in interpretation of data to insights</li>
<li>Interpretation of data to insights in PowerPoint formats
</li>
<li>Work with internal media team to implement and apply insights in media
</li>
<li>Hold an advanced degree in Statistics, Math, Engineering or Economic degrees
</li>
<li>3-5 years of relevant hands-on work experience</li>
<li>Demonstrated ability to conduct independent research utilizing large data sets</li>
<li>Solid understanding of data structures and databases in structured &amp; unstructured environments</li>
<li>Extensive knowledge with machine learning, statistical models and data mining tools.</li>
<li>Strong programming skills in statistical software: Python or R is preferred</li>
</ul>
