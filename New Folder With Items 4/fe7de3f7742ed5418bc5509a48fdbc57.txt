<h2>Wholesale Sales Representative</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>POSITION SUMMARY</b></p>
<p><b>Must have previous outside sales experience </b></p>
<p>We are seeking highly motivated and experienced cannabis salesperson to prospect, sell our brand of cannabis products to dispensaries. Cultivate is a successful fast-growing cannabis company and we are looking for top performers only!</p>
<p>Must be a self-starter and be willing to work full-time. Experience with sales and CRM experience. Previous cannabis sales experience is required. We are a successful fast-growing cannabis company and we are looking for top performers only.</p>
<p>Your role will involve creating and maintaining business relationships and creating a greater network of industry clients. YOU will be on the front line for advertising the company's brand.</p>
<ul>
<li>Establish, develop and maintain strong relationships with customers through personal visits, texts, social DMs, emails, and phone calls</li>
<li>Ability to close sales through building strong relationships</li>
<li>Harness existing dispensary relationships to generate additional product placement</li>
<li>Ensuring customers have the proper merchandising displays and products are presented professionally</li>
<li>Strive to get more space/product selection into each account</li>
<li>Be the most professional/knowledgeable sales person in the industry</li>
<li>Handle all customer-related questions, inquiries, and orders professionally and promptly</li>
<li>Ensure territory growth</li>
<li>Educate budtenders on products and industry</li>
<li>Meet or exceed monthly sales goals</li>
<li>Daily, high level of communication with management</li>
<li>Ensure Sales are collected when product is delivered</li>
</ul>
<p><b>About You: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Detail oriented and organized</li>
<li>Excellent verbal communication &amp; presentation skills</li>
<li>Outstanding interpersonal and persuasive skills</li>
<li>Comfortable in a fast-paced environment</li>
<li>Ability to learn and apply product-related information in a professional manner</li>
<li>High level of integrity, personal motivation, and sense of urgency</li>
<li>Willingness to work after hours and occasional weekends for business meetings and industry trade shows &amp; events</li>
<li>Social media experience is a plus</li>
</ul>
