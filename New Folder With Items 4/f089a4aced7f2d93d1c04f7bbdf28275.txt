<h2>Client Engagement Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Position Summary: </b></p>
<p>Works directly with our clients to provide an exceptional brand experience while assisting them with their inquiries. In this position, you will be part of a team whose primary objectives are to retain our current clients and welcome our new clients. As a critical part of our rapidly growing organization, Client Engagement Specialists will ultimately acquire a deep understanding of our products and solutions. They will be responsible for educating and supporting our clients on product usage, and will garner client feedback for future product development purposes.</p>
<p><b>Responsibilities Will Include: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Onboarding new clients relative to the applicable products and/or solutions they will be utilizing</li>
<li>Working with existing clients relative to the products and/or solutions they use in their practice on a daily basis, as well as educating clients on best industry practices</li>
<li>Act as a liaison between our clients and internal colleagues within the organization</li>
<li>Ensure all inquiries are resolved as quickly as possible, leveraging all resources across Fi360</li>
<li>Act on opportunities to direct clients to other product/service offerings</li>
<li>Connect all enterprise and/or prospect inquiries to the appropriate colleagues</li>
<li>Communicate our clients’ needs/objectives/feedback for consideration</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Knowledge/Abilities: (at full job performance)</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Professional étiquette in client relations</li>
<li>Phone and email correspondence</li>
<li>Computers, i.e., software programs including Office, Salesforce.com</li>
<li>Troubleshooting/problem solving</li>
<li>Attention to detail</li>
<li>Progressively learn / understand:</li>
<li>fi360 products and solutions</li>
<li>Financial industry terminology and concepts</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Expectations: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>100% Client Satisfaction is the top priority</li>
<li>Delivery of product education in various formats, such as: email, phone, webinar, etc.</li>
<li>Inter-organizational/Team Collaboration, as well as the ability to work independently</li>
<li>Multi-tasking</li>
<li>Professional communications, both verbally and in writing</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Requirements: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Minimum Educational Level Required: Bachelor’s degree in Business, Finance, Marketing, Accounting, or a related field</li>
<li>Minimum Experience Level Required: 1+ years client relations and/or sales experience, including professional phone and email correspondence. Financial industry and investments experience is a plus.</li>
</ul>
<p>Job Type: Full-time</p>
<p>Salary: $35,000.00 to $40,000.00 /year</p>

<ul><li>relevant: 1 year (Preferred)</li></ul>

</ul>
