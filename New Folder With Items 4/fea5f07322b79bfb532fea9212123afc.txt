<h2>Business Development Representative</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>About Rental Beast: </b></p>
<p>Rental Beast is the real estate tech firm that powers the residential rental business of leading agents and brokerages. Our clients include Coldwell Banker, Century21, Berkshire Hathaway Home Services, Long &amp; Foster, Better Homes &amp; Gardens and many others. We are a young, fast growing company looking for talented individuals ready to make an impact.</p>
<p>We’re looking for a Business Development Representative to join our high-performance team in our journey to help real estate agents start and build their businesses. Rental Beast is seeking a sharp, agile, and energized Sales Development Representative to take part in the dramatic growth currently underway. In this role, you will make outbound calls to potential clients who have expressed interest in learning more about Rental Beast or appear to be strong potential customers based upon our general market focus.</p>
<p>The Sales Development Representative qualifies prospective customers through targeted questions about their business strategy to include informing them about Rental Beasts offering and ascertains the likelihood of the prospect being a good match for Rental Beasts services. Eighty percent of one's time will be on the phone and you’ll play an integral role in building our sales pipeline and our business.</p>
<p>Key Responsibilities:</p>
<ul>
<li>Respond quickly and effectively to inbound sales inquiries via phone calls and emails.</li>
<li>Proactively build, maintain, and manage a highly productive pipeline.</li>
<li>Prospect and identify key decision-makers to develop sales opportunities.</li>
<li>Explain how Rental Beast brings value to real estate professionals’ growth efforts.</li>
<li>Document all activities, lead dispositions and insights within Salesforce.com.</li>
<li>Consistently achieve qualified quotas to ensure revenue objectives of the sales department.</li>
<li>Maintain clear communication and work through challenges with sales executives.</li>
</ul>
<p>Job Requirements:</p>
<ul>
<li>Experience successfully managing a high volume of client interactions, preferably over the phone.</li>
<li>Ability to remain calm and use ingenuity when facing challenges.</li>
<li>Experience working in a team environment.</li>
<li>Comfortable with the phone as your primary communication tool.</li>
<li>Proven history of success and achievement.</li>
<li>A desire to build a career in sales.</li>
</ul>
<p>Preferred Qualifications:</p>
<ul>
<li>You’ve worked on an inside sales team as a BDR for a SaaS-based solution company.</li>
<li>You have strong achievements in sales.</li>
<li>You’re proficient with CRM and opportunity management systems, preferably Salesforce.com.</li>
<li>You have an understanding of the different channels of online marketing, such as paid search, social media marketing, SEO, etc.</li>
<li>You have an understanding of the residential real estate industry.</li>
</ul>
