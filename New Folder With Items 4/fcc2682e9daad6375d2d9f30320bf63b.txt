<b>About the Opportunity</b><p></p>
<p>
A pharmaceutical company in New Jersey is actively seeking a new Assistant Marketing Communications Specialist for a promising opportunity with their growing staff. Apply today!</p>

<b>
Company Description</b><p></p>
<p>
Pharmaceutical Company</p>

Job Description</b><p></p>
<p>
The Assistant Marketing Communications Specialist will:</p>
<ul>
<li>Develop and follow up on the agenda and ensure reviewer input is captured for all LMR review meetings</li>
<li>and facilitate efficient LMR review meetings to ensure all appropriate stakeholders are in attendance</li>
<li>Help prioritize LMR submissions based on business needs</li>
<li>Manage submission process through LMR software application</li>
<li>Implement changes based on the comments made by all stakeholders</li>
<li>Prepare and upload documents for final submission to the LMR system of record</li>
<li>Provide project support to business unit initiatives</li>
</ul>
