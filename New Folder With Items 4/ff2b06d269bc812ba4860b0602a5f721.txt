<p><b>About us:</b></p>
<p>
Headquartered in Ann Arbor, Michigan, Truck Hero, Inc. is a fast-growing designer and manufacturer of accessories for pickup trucks.</p>
<p>
We provide pickup truck owners America's best-selling selection of truck bed covers and caps. Truck Hero also offers truck bed liners, nerf steps and boards, suspension lift kits and a variety of other truck accessories. Our products enhance the functionality and aesthetics of pickup trucks and enable you to customize and personalize your truck with innovative products born from high-quality materials, superior engineering and cutting-edge manufacturing. Truck Hero is recognized as one of the leaders by sales in the pickup truck accessory market.</p>
<p>
Our brands include: Extang, TruXedo, BedRug, UnderCover, Advantage, Retrax, BAK Industries, Rugged Liners, Husky Liners, A.R.E., N-FAB, Superlift, Omix-ADA, Backrack, and Lund International.</p>
<p><b>
Professional Recruiter – Temporary position, with potential for full-time placement. Full or part-time candidates encouraged to apply.</b></p>
<p>
This individual will be responsible for full cycle recruiting for a number of front-line professional positions at a key manufacturing location.</p>
<p><b>
Duration: </b>6-12 months</p>
<p><b>
Location:</b> This position may work out of our Massillon, Ohio location, or from Truck Hero corporate headquarters located in Ann Arbor, Michigan. Successful candidate also may be permitted to work from a home office.</p>
</div>
<br>
<div>
<p><b>Position Requirements:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Work closely with local management team to understand key attributes, success factors, and experience for targeted position candidates.</li>
<li>Leverage all aspects of sourcing and candidate development including job posting, job board mining, internet sourcing, social networking, employee referrals, and networking events</li>
<li>Utilize a wide range of promotion and advertising techniques matched to the types of positions and candidate flow requirements.</li>
<li>Effectively utilize the ATS system to ensure an organized and prioritized approach to candidate management.</li>
<li>Schedule candidate visits and interview schedules, ensuring productive and professional processes.</li>
<li>Ensure a positive candidate experience through professional communication and timely feedback.</li>
<li>Effectively screen candidates against position requirements employing effective interview techniques and other evaluative tools.</li>
<li>Actively coordinate and support the process of selecting top candidates and presenting employment offers.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Education and Experience Required:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree preferred</li>
<li>Three or more years of professional recruiting experience, preferably in a manufacturing environment.</li>
<li>Experience effectively utilizing applicant tracking systems.</li>
</ul>
