<h2>Cabinet Design Assistant (Tampa, FL)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Position Profile
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div>Norcraft Companies, <i>a division of MasterBrand Cabinets,</i> is currently searching for a <b>Cabinet Design Assistant</b> for our Tampa, Florida facility. This position reports to the Operations Manager and is responsible for initiating and closing sales of approved company products.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div><b>
Key Accountabilities:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Initiates and closes sales of company approved products.</li>
<li>Initiates and closes sales of company approved products during visits to targeted builders as assigned by the sales manager.</li>
<li>Designs projects using company software.</li>
<li>Prospects for potential sales leads.</li>
<li>Completes and processes all required documentation.</li>
<li>Walks all installed jobs to drive the project to a successful completion.</li>
<li>Makes presentations to potential customers in the store or in the field as required.</li>
<li>Educates each customer about the Company’s products and services and about the processes involving the customer related to the purchase and installation of the customer’s project.</li>
<li>Represent self and the Company in a professional manner.</li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
<b>
Key Competencies:</b><br>
<ul><li>Sales including prospecting and closing.</li></ul>
</div>
<ul>
<li>Design of kitchens and bathrooms as per the Company standards using 20/20 computer application.</li>
<li>Interpersonal communication and presentation skills.</li>
<li>Personally well-organized.</li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<br>
<div><b>
Requirements:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>High school diploma/equivalent is required.</li>
<li>Bachelor’s-level degree preferred or equivalent experience in a related field.</li>
<li>Knowledge of 32 NKBA Guidelines</li>
<li>Experience in the kitchen and bathroom design industry</li>
<li>Familiarity of Company products</li>
<li>Insight into kitchen and bathroom product manufacturing and installation</li>
<li>Comprehension of kitchen and bath sales process and language</li>
</ul>
