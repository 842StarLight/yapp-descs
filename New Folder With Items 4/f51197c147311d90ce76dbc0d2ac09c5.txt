<h2>Technical Fulfillment Associate</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Alteryx, Inc. has an immediate opening for a Technical Fulfillment Associate. This position is responsible for entering and validating customer purchase orders into the order and fulfillment system. These range from small orders to complex server &amp; cloud based solutions. The Technical Fulfillment Associate communicates with Sales, Finance, Customer Success and the customer in order to ensure a high level of customer satisfaction.
<br>
<br>
Responsibilities:
</p>
<ul>
<li>Prepares domestic and international orders for shipping: Interacts with Sales Operations to ensure orders are processed, shipped and delivered.
</li>
<li>Provides basic-to-intermediate technical services concerning software licensing and license key management. Interacts with Technical Staff for advanced questions.
</li>
<li>Responsible for constant monitoring of orders to ensure customer delivery expectations are met.
</li>
<li>Ensures receipt of all relevant documentation and agreements.
</li>
<li>Compiles work and presents final deliverables and assists in identifying areas of improvement in order processing and documentation.
</li>
<li>Analyze and produce reports using business applications.
</li>
<li>Evaluates a variety of common business issues relative to fulfillment and recommends course of action.
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p>Requirements:
</p>
<ul>
<li>College graduate with a degree in Business Administration or a related field or equivalent experience is preferred
</li>
<li>Basic understanding of software licensing models
</li>
<li>Attention to detail
</li>
<li>Ability to work on own initiative, action oriented
</li>
<li>Customer focus and problem solving
</li>
<li>Time management and organizational skills
</li>
<li>Team orientation
</li>
<li>Candidates must be proficient in Microsoft Office applications.
</li>
<li>Applicants with previous experience using SalesForce and NetSuite will be highly considered</li>
</ul>
</div></div>
