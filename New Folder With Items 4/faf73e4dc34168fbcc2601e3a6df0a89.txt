<h2>Property Manager (must be Licensed Realtor)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b><i>Property Manager Job Responsibilities </i></b> Maintains and bring new property rentals by advertising and filling vacancies; negotiating and enforcing leases; maintaining and securing premises.</p>
<p><b><i>Property Manager Job Duties: </i></b></p>
<ul>
<li>Establishes rental rate by surveying local rental rates; calculating overhead costs, depreciation, taxes, and profit goals.</li>
<li>Tenant acquisition by advertising vacancies; obtaining referrals from current tenants; and REALTOR explaining advantages of location and services; showing units.</li>
<li>Acquire new homes and develop marketing strategies to increase inventory.</li>
<li>Contracts with tenants by negotiating leases; collecting security deposit.</li>
<li>Maintains Owners and tenants relations and proactive and prompt communication.</li>
<li>Conducting move in and move out and quarterly property inspections.</li>
<li>Maintains property by investigating and resolving tenant complaints; enforcing rules of occupancy; inspecting vacant units and completing repairs; planning Maintains building systems by contracting for maintenance services; supervising repairs.</li>
<li>Enforces occupancy policies and procedures by confronting violators.</li>
<li>Proficiency in company software and organizing and prioritizing management duties</li>
<li>Proficient knowledge of Owner agreement, lease and Landlord Tenant act.</li>
<li>Eviction process when it necessary.</li>
</ul>
<p><b><i>Property Manager Skills and Qualifications: </i></b></p>
<ul>
<li>Motivation for Sales</li>
<li>Meeting Sales Goals</li>
<li>Negotiation</li>

<li>Territory Management</li>
<li>Closing Skills</li>
<li>Prospecting Skills</li>
<li>Professionalism</li>
<li>Internal Communications</li>
<li>Listening</li>

</ul>
