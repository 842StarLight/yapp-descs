<div><b>
About the role</b></div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
We are looking for a top-notch Marketing leader to build out a high-velocity demand gen machine, and bring Cmd to Linux-based DevOps teams everywhere. We are starting from a blank slate - so it’ll be up to you to define our program, get it operational, and build out the supporting team over time. Cmd’s marketing and sales teams are joined at the hip in Boston, and the opportunity in front of us is real and enormous.</div>
</div>
<div><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Responsibilities
</b></h3>
<div><ul>
<li>Define, build and execute Cmd’s demand gen plan; owning campaigns end-to-end, &amp; working closely with key stakeholders to align against sales and marketing goals</li>
<li>
Develop &amp; execute top-of-funnel marketing programs (webinars, content syndication, digital display, etc.)</li>
<li>
Define lead-nurture programs that drive engagement, conversion and funnel velocity</li>
<li>
Monitor, track and report on campaign performance against set KPIs, and provide insights to improve current campaigns &amp; inform future strategy</li>
<li>
Manage data; including segmentation and reporting, testing, cleansing and optimization</li>
<li>
Own our marketing automation and analytics stack</li>
<li>
Work closely with sales to communicate new campaigns, gain feedback on lead quality and optimize accordingly</li>
<li>
Collaborate with agencies and manage vendor partners</li>
<li>
Evaluate emerging technologies and provide perspective for adoption</li>
</ul></div>
</div></div>
<div><div>

Your skills
</b></h3>
<div><ul>

<li>
Strong communication skills, both written and oral</li>
<li>
Proficient with marketing automation systems and associated technologies</li>
<li>
Self-directed, comfortable taking the initiative and plotting a path forward</li>
<li>
Detail-oriented, with excellent reasoning and planning skills</li>
<li>
Team player who is curious and adept at experimenting with new initiatives quickly</li>
<li>
Exudes a positive attitude and level-headed composure, even in a fast-paced, high-growth startup environment</li>
<li>
Experience in cybersecurity is a plus</li>
</ul></div>
