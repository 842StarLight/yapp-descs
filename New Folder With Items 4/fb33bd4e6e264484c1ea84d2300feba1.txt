<h2>Marketing Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p><b>Statement of Position</b><b>:</b></p>
<p>
As Olmsted Medical Center (OMC) continues its growth, marketing has become increasingly important. Predictable, sustainable, and profitable revenue growth requires careful planning as well as constant, proactive demand generation, especially for certain service lines. Well-coordinated plans and teamwork between internal stakeholders, client organizations, and business partners for service lines are essential.</p>
<p>
A top-performing marketing professional will provide management for the external and internal marketing process, collection, and analysis of internal stakeholder information, oversight for marketing and external relations plans, as well as developing the healthcare business to business arena. Candidates should have marketing and communications experience, business development expertise, an entrepreneurial spirit, and other relevant industry and professional services experience..</p>
<p>
This position is responsible for working with clinical departments on their business and marketing plans and creating strategic and tactical plans to develop business to business opportunities.</p>
<p><b>
Job Responsibilities:</b></p>
<p>
Planning &amp; Presentation (60% of time)</p>
<ul>
<li>Assists in the development of marketing plans, reports, procedures/processes, proposals/recommendations, and evaluations of plan implementation.
</li>
<li>Creates awareness, building external relationships with key decision makers and developing/pursuing leads.
</li>
<li>Supports direct marketing campaigns - including following up on direct response efforts.
</li>
<li>Assists internal stakeholders with external service line promotion as needed.
</li>
<li>Adheres to sales and promotion process including healthcare business pipeline development and accurate forecasting via internal data collection (utilization, revenue, population studies, market research, etc.).Performs proposal writing and works with colleagues to develop customized materials for clients.
</li>
<li>Attends trade shows, human resource meetings, health fairs, chamber and community events, and performs continuous marketing efforts to new and existing customers and relationship building efforts with new and existing customers.
</li>
<li>Participates in regular reporting with departments and communicates marketing objectives with departments and operations leadership.
</li>
<li>Participates in professional development to ensure satisfactory performance, staying abreast on emerging technologies and practices.
</li>
<li>Promotes organizational culture and ethics and establishes name recognition.
</li>
<li>Works in a team environment with marketing, operations, and finance department staff to enhance all aspects of the marketing strategy.
</li>
<li>Serves on OMC’s Revenue and Expense Committee, Marketing Action Team, External Relations workgroup, and other committees as required to regularly report to these committees regarding performance.
</li>
</ul>
<p>Business Development Execution (15% of time)</p>
<ul>
<li>Works with designated clinical services to review market research and develops business development plans and meet goals.
</li>
<li>Executes marketing strategies by identifying and qualifying healthcare customer needs and positioning the appropriate solutions to businesses, allied healthcare providers, and other organizations through direct services or referrals.
</li>
<li>Increases market share and develops business through multiple marketing and business-to-business techniques including but not limited to cold calling, conducting in-person meetings, and partnering with internal department representatives.
</li>
<li>Maintains a targeted understanding of clients’ needs and shows the ability to offer solutions, creating strong relationships to effectively generate demand and build loyalty.
</li>
</ul>
<p>Account Management (15% of time)</p>
<ul>
<li>Manages individual marketing and promotion objectives.
</li>
<li>Manages ongoing client account maintenance.
</li>
<li>Works with internal stakeholders to ensure that proposals are provided and requests are fulfilled accurately.
</li>
<li>Works with clinical and operations staff to accurately scope and fulfill marketing projects
</li>
<li>Works with process improvement and patient relations to ensure appropriate feedback and action taken, given patient or client feedback.
</li>
</ul>
<p>Staff Supervision (10% of time)</p>
<ul>
<li>Supervises assigned marketing staff including evaluating work performed through performance appraisals and handling other personnel issues; designs and monitors appropriate work schedules for staff; handles interviewing, hiring, and vacation schedules for assigned staff.</li>
</ul>
</div>
