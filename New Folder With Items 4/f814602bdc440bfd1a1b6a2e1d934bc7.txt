Job Summary: Effectively connect and engage Partners through visual storytelling with video and motion graphics.<br>
<br>
Essential Functions / Process Responsibilities include the following; other duties may be assigned as necessary<br>
<br>
<ul><li>May lead cross functional teams with other departments to create and write scripts, shoot, edit and distribute video products for Internal departments</li></ul>
<br>
<ul><li>Communicates effectively across the organization on best practices, developing production capabilities and video communication strategies</li></ul>
<br>
<ul><li>Reviews drawings and other information relating to natural or artificial conditions to determine required lighting, film, shutter angles, filter factors, camera distance, depth of field and focus, angles of view, and other variables to produce desired effects</li></ul>
<br>
<ul><li>Designs creative brief and storyboards for corporate and senior leadership specific events</li></ul>
<br>
<ul><li>Develops content and theme for video production</li></ul>
<br>
<ul><li>Coordinates production schedules to include location, set, props, and talent</li></ul>
<br>
<ul><li>Leads the edits and designs of video footage according to creative brief outline</li></ul>
<br>
<ul><li>May help guide or mentor the work of other partners to ensure scripts and products meet the requirements expressed by management</li></ul>
<br>
Education and Experience preferred<br>
<br>
<ul><li>Bachelor's Degree in Communication, Radio, Television, and Film, or related field or comparable experience</li></ul>
<br>
<ul><li>3-5 years experience in studio/video production and lead a media production team</li></ul>
<br>
<ul><li>5+ yrs of creative brief design and storyboard development with an established portfolio</li></ul>
<br>
<ul><li>7+ years of professional experience with editing and motion graphics software</li></ul>
<br>
<ul><li>Working knowledge and experience in project management</li></ul>
<br>
<ul><li>Experience with live video production and content streaming</li></ul>
<br>
<ul><li>Advanced knowledge of waveform, video levels and color balancing</li></ul>
<br>
<ul><li>Advanced knowledge and skill as a DV, HD, and DSLR video camera operator, with expertise shooting with multiple camera and formats</li></ul>
<br>
- Advanced PC skills including Microsoft Office Suite software - Word, Excel, PowerPoint, Access and Outlook - as well as computer software programs / applications and programming such as Adobe After Effects, Adobe Photoshop, develop knowledge of current video and audio equipment including lighting</div>
<div>
<br>
<ul><li>Advanced working knowledge and expertise in graphic editing software, e.g. Adobe Illustrator, Adobe Photoshop, etc.</li></ul>
