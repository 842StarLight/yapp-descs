The Aparavi Business Development team consists of a small group of seasoned professionals with one purpose…change the world of data management through significant revenue growth and market expansion of the Aparavi solution. As Director - Business Development, Sales &amp; Partnerships you will work with customers, product management, product marketing and the leadership team to define Aparavi’s Enterprise strategy and build and execute on a plan.
