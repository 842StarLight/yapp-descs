Joining Intuitive Surgical means joining a team dedicated to using technology to benefit patients by improving surgical efficacy and decreasing surgical invasiveness, with patient safety as our highest priority.<br>
<br>
<b>Position Overview:</b><br>
<br>
The Group Product Manager for Digital Learning will be responsible for both upstream product management, and downstream product marketing of digital learning products. The primary functions will be to manage and execute to a comprehensive digital learning roadmap that enables a continuous learning experience around minimally invasive care tailored for surgeons, OR staff and hospital administrators.<br>
<br>
<b>Key Responsibilities:</b><br>
<br>
<b>Key Objectives</b><div>
<br>
</div>
<div><ul>
<li>Partner with Learning &amp; Development team to develop comprehensive digital learning roadmap that closely aligns with the customer, business and company’s long term needs</li>
<li>Partners closely with business teams to research learning solutions, and identify trends
</li>
<li>Understand competitive and emerging technologies and their impact on robotic surgery and education</li>
<li>Develop business case, return on investment and key performance indicators for all new learning products</li>
<li>Creates project proposals to define scope, strategy, time frame, budget, development plan, responsibilities, risks, and allotment of available resources to various phases of the project</li>
<li>Drive alignment with all key stakeholders including customers, c-suite, business, design and development teams
</li>
<li>Establish market and product requirements; including product positioning, value proposition and pricing – as needed</li>
<li>Create functional mockups, epics and user stories to enable development of digital learning products using agile/scrum methodology</li>
<li>Ensure all products follow regulatory compliance as defined by HIPAA, FDA Labeling etc.
</li>
<li>Ensure data privacy and security compliance leveraging Intuitive Surgical Standard Operating Procedures and Health Information Technology (HIT), as required</li>
<li>Partner with business and development teams to key milestones are met throughout the execution phase; including milestones, resources and budgets</li>
<li>Manage the entire product rollout and implementation process from internal and external alignment and change management to global product launch.
</li>
<li>Partner with user research teams to conduct usability and customer preference studies
</li>
<li>Refine requirements with feedback from customers, sales, marketing, services, operations, clinical development engineering and product engineering teams.
</li>
<li>Partner with business teams to measure success by monitoring key performance indicators and seeking post-launch product feedback.</li>
<li>Partner with development teams to manage, monitor and prioritize product backlog
</li>
</ul></div>
