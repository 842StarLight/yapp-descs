<li><p>Develop literature and customer communication to promote the offer value proposition

<li><p>Enhance a highly dynamic and collaborative environment with: engineering, product architects, project management, quality, purchasing, industrialization, manufacturing, customer care center, business development and sales force<br>
</p></li>
