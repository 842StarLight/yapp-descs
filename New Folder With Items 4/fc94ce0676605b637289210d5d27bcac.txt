<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Company Overview</b></h2>
<br>
At Motorola Solutions, we create technologies our customers refer to as their lifeline. Our technology platforms in communications, software, video and services help our customers work safely and more efficiently. Whether it’s helping firefighters see through smoke, enabling police officers to see around street corners, or reliably keeping the lights on in homes and businesses around the world, our work supports those who put their lives on the line to keep us safe. Bring your passion, potential and talents to Motorola Solutions, and help us usher in a new era in public safety and security.<br>

<b>

<b>Department Overview</b>
</h2>
This department is responsible for selling Motorola Solutions public safety applications to city, county and state agencies.<h2 class="jobSectionHeader">
<b>
</b><br>
<b>Job Description</b>
</h2>
<br>
The Sr Account Manager is responsible, but not limited to the following:
<ul>
<li>Responsible for sales, strategic account planning and overall relationship management for a number of target customers</li>
<li>
Develop and implement a comprehensive sales plan for each “target” and “geographic” account covering the full portfolio of Motorola products and services (core and expansion technologies)</li>
<li>
Act as a trusted advisor to influence customer’s technology platform decisions and develop preference and loyalty for Motorola Solutions</li>
<li>Proactively develop large project opportunities encompassing a wide range of products and services</li>
<li>Develop strong relationships with key decision makers and influencers within and outside of public safety; Sheriff, Mayor, CIO, Police Chief, Fire Chief and other officials in order to understand and influence technology and funding priorities</li>
</ul>Specific Knowledge/Skills:
<ul>
<li>Bachelors Degree with 4+ years of sales/public safety experience OR 8+ years of sales experience</li>
<li>
Seeking prevous outside sales experience selling solutions into government organizations preferred</li>
<li>
Strong relationship development and management skills</li>
<li>
Experience utilizing strategic account management practices</li>
<li>
Excellent communication skills (oral, written &amp; presentation)</li>
<li>
Ability to quickly learn new key characteristics of products/solutions and communicate the value of those solutions to customers</li>
<li>
Excellent negotiation skills</li>
<li>
Ability to develop and maintain relationships with a C-Level executives</li>
<li>
Ability to establish oneself as a trusted advisor</li>
<li>
Ability to lead a diverse group in addressing customer requirements</li>
<li>
High tech solutions sales experience a plus</li>
<li>
Knowledge of industry trends and best practices a plus</li>
<li>
Highly motivated and results oriented</li>
<li>
Experience utilizing channel partners to address customer requirements</li>
<li>
Ability to quickly learn Salesforce as the project reporting tool and forecast method</li>
</ul>
