<h2>Store Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Position Title: </b> <b>Store Sales Manager</b></p>
<p><b>Reports To: </b> <b>District Sales Manager</b></p>
<p><b>Essential Position Responsibilities: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Meet and exceeds sales goals.</li>
<li>Analyze store metrics to improve store performance.</li>
<li>Coaching and motivating your team to inspire top performance.</li>
<li>Enhance brand loyalty by empowering store staff to offer a personable customer experience.</li>
<li>Maintains high level of customer focus.</li>
<li>Recognize talented staff and develop them for growth within the company.</li>
<li>Provide feedback and coaching to all employees.</li>
<li>Responsible for conducting performance evaluations of all store staff.</li>
<li>Ensure proper execution all of visual directives and initiatives.</li>
<li>Responsible for managing sales incentive contest within the guidelines set by the Company.</li>
<li>Responsible for ensuring payroll hours, wage dollars and staff schedules are within the set operating constraints.</li>
<li>Monitor shrink to ensure establish parameters are met or exceeded.</li>
<li>Completes daily inventory cycle counts.</li>
<li>Maintains adherence to all company policies and procedures.</li>
<li>Maintains a safe and clean retail environment.</li>
<li>Proactively communicate local events to District Sales Manager that can potentially increase store traffic.</li>
<li>Manage, access and minimize expenses at store level.</li>
<li>Proficiently utilize position related tools or company provided software.</li>
<li>Utilize ladders for the purpose of merchandising or addressing minor maintenance issues.</li>
<li>Work minimum 48 hours (with meal break) per week. Weekly schedule includes nights and weekends.</li>
<li>Maintain designated staffing requirements.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Qualifications: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Demonstrated leadership ability with experience in customer service.</li>
<li>2+ years of footwear or retail management experience.</li>
<li>Excellent interpersonal and customer service skills.</li>
<li>Strong mathematical, problem solving and decision making skills.</li>
<li>Candidate will be required to stand for long periods of time throughout their shift.</li>
<li>Lifting between 15-30 pounds.</li>
</ul>
