<h2>Service Technician</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>As a promoter of sustainable mobility, Alstom develops and markets systems, equipment and services for the transport sector. Alstom offers a complete range of solutions (from high-speed trains to metros, tramways and e-buses), passenger solutions, customised services (maintenance, modernisation), infrastructure, signalling and digital mobility solutions. Alstom is a world leader in integrated transport systems. The company recorded sales of €8.1 billion and booked €12.1 billion of orders in the 2018/19 fiscal year. Headquartered in France, Alstom is present in over 60 countries and employs 36,300 people.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<div>
<div>
<div><div>
<p><b>Objective
</b></p>
<p>The role is multi-skilled requiring a high degree of competency in mechanical skills with basic electrical skills. The role involves diverse mechanical and electrical work on rail cars and other vehicles including body work repairs, welding repairs, fabrication and basic electrical installation tasks. The role has responsibilities for Environmental Health and Safety (EHS) inspections as assigned and the maintenance of documentation for the vehicles and the work site. The ability to drive a road vehicle and fork lift truck is desirable.</p>
</div></div>
<br>
<div><div>
<p><b>Mission</b></p>
</div></div>
<br>
<div><div>
<p><b>Responsibilities and Accountabilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Body work and repairs: For rail passenger cars, locomotives and components</li>
<li>Fabrication: Parts and special tooling</li>
<li>Welding: The ability to weld different types of metals, steel, stainless steel, aluminum is desirable but not essential</li>
<li>Cutting: Ability to cut metals using mechanical tools, gas and other thermal tools</li>
<li>Mechanical: Mechanical disassembly/assembly of passenger rail car interior panels and general interior fittings</li>
<li>Mechanical: Conduct repairs and installation of pneumatic and water systems on passenger rail cars and locomotives</li>
<li>Mechanical: The installation of snow plows, fiberglass noses and other large components on locomotives</li>
<li>Electrician: Installation of high and low voltage also WIFI and communications conduit and cabling. The installation of communication systems on buses and passenger rail cars</li>
<li>Environmental Health and Safety: Performs EHS duties as assigned</li>
<li>Electrician: Basic electrical wiring installation, testing and troubleshooting of passenger rail cars and locomotives</li>
<li>Documentation: Maintains vehicle history books and other production documentation</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Supervisory Responsibilities</b></p>
<p>This role has no supervisory responsibilities.</p>
<p><b>Competencies</b></p>
<p>To perform the job successfully, an individual should demonstrate the following competencies</p>
<ul>
<li>The ability to work in a safe and secure manner using mechanical and electrical safety isolations</li>
<li>The fabrication of parts &amp; special tooling using standard industrial tooling</li>
<li>The ability to install complex mechanical systems, structures, flooring, brackets etc. on passenger rail cars and locomotives</li>
<li>Certification as a welder desirable</li>
<li>Ability to read mechanical and electrical schematic and manufacturing drawings</li>
<li>Ability to follow written work, process and testing instructions</li>
<li>Mechanical skill including the selection and use of both hand and power tools for drilling, tapping, removal and replacement of mechanical parts</li>
<li>Basic electrical skills for installation of conduit and cabling</li>
<li>Basic computer skill, word processing and spreadsheet</li>
<li>Interpersonal skills, able to work in a small team or alone with minimal supervision</li>
<li>To perform this job successfully, an individual should have basic knowledge of internet software, word processing and spreadsheet software</li>
<br>
</ul>
