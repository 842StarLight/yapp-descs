<h2>Product Manager - Broadcast Market</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Overview
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div><b>We have an exciting opportunity for an experienced Product Manager to join our Global Product Management team at Shure!</b></div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
This position champions the development of new products for <b>Broadcast</b> market segments. In this role, the Manager works with customers, all levels of Engineering, and all functions within Global Marketing and Sales to build a shared understanding around critical customer needs and the product attributes that will solve for them. Drafts product requirements, working directly with engineering teams to ensure successful implementation. Performs thorough quantitative analysis and provides recommendations on new product forecasts and pricing. The Manager, Product Management is the company expert on the needs of target end users and thoroughly understands the strengths and weakness of all competitors in the category. Merges business needs, customer needs, product design, and Engineering cost/time/risk considerations into successful products. Contributes to decisions about Shure’s product roadmap and marketing strategy.</div>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<ul>
<li>Represents the Global Marketing and Sales Division during product development projects, ensuring that product attributes will create a positive end user value proposition.</li>
<li>
Initiates and participates in market research to define target segments and product positioning for new product development efforts.</li>
<li>
Writes detailed functional requirements for product development teams, ensuring the development team has clarity on the measures of success in meeting requirements, which include technical specifications if necessary.</li>
<li>
Provides forecast and pricing analysis and recommendations to key stakeholders in the Company’s business units.</li>
<li>
Formulates and communicates the value proposition to the Company global marketing teams.</li>
<li>
Executes timely Alpha and Beta testing with end users and analyzes findings to determine the appropriate course of action.</li>
<li>
Reviews user documentation for accuracy regarding product features, proper usage, and expected performance.</li>
<li>
Provides internal training regarding operation, performance, usage environments, and ordering convention on products prior to launch and serves as the product champion at trade exhibitions during launch.</li>
<li>
Maintains expert knowledge of the worldwide market for competitive products, and maintain contact with end users to validate requirements for products, based on competitive offerings and existing Shure products.</li>
</ul>
</div></div></div>
