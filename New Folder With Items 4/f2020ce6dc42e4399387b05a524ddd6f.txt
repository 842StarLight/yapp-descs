<p><b>Manager of Strategic Partnerships </b></p>
<p>The Manager of Strategic Partnerships role is responsible for identifying, developing, and expanding relationships with solutions providers to create business development opportunities and increase the value of MJ Freeway to the cannabis industry with complementary integration solutions.</p>
<p>The right candidate should have excellent communication skills and be a strong negotiator to maintain strong relationships with multiple channel partners simultaneously. He/she must be knowledgeable about the software product and be innovative in the quest to help partners understand how to effectively position the value.</p>

<li>Negotiating agreements and growth plans with current and prospective partners.</li>
<li>Working with the product team to qualify and implement Platform integration opportunities to increase value to the industry.</li>
<li>Manage new and existing partner programs to increase front end sales opportunities and backend technical integration partnerships.</li>
