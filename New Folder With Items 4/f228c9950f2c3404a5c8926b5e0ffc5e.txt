<h2>Marketing Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>The Marketing Manager - Carolinas, under the direction of Senior Leadership, is responsible for the comprehensive marketing efforts of the region within budget with the objective to generate consumer interest that supports branch efforts to meet or exceed budgeted sales goals.</p>
<p>Using marketing-related analytics and industry best practice and using regular reporting of activities and ROI, the Marketing Manager-Carolinas will be responsible for increasing the exposure of the Bath Fitter brand and its market share through multiple media platforms as well as directing the Events Coordinator team to ensure brand presence at shows, exhibits, and retail locations.</p>
<p>ESSENTIAL DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES</p>
<ul>
<li>Understand and manage budgets, including expenditures, return-on-investment and staffing activities for the Marketing team in the Carolinas.</li>
<li>Direct, plan and coordinate all marketing activities for the region, including but not limited to, digital and television exposure as well as brand representation at retail outlets, trade shows and special events. Ensure alignment across all platforms.</li>
<li>Responsible for ongoing reporting of all marketing activities through analytics as requested as well as providing regular updates and communications of marketing campaigns and activities to the organization.</li>
<li>Maintain relationships with Branch Managers of the region through digital and physical presence.</li>
<li>Continual research to identify new opportunities for brand exposure, including assessments of competitors, economic conditions, and consumer trends.</li>
<li>Manage relationships with vendors and media contacts, including negotiation and continual evaluation of contracts and conditions to ensure maximum exposure and ROI.</li>
<li>Lead the Carolinas marketing team to ensure maximum productivity through engagement and accountability.</li>
<li>Daily management of social media activities to ensure timely response to consumer feedback.</li>
<li>Lead professional development of Event Coordinators.</li>
<li>Develop and maintain a professional network to promote the BathFitter brand.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>QUALIFICATIONS</b></p>
<p>To perform this job successfully, an individual must be able to perform each essential duty satisfactorily. The requirements listed below are representative of the knowledge, skill, and/or ability required.</p>
<ul>
<li>Education – Minimum Bachelors Degree in a business program with marketing focus.</li>
<li>Media Marketing- Must demonstrate a comprehensive understanding of best practices in levering and coordinating use of media across all platforms.</li>
<li>Experience- Demonstrated history of success managing all stages of marketing strategy.</li>
<li>Language/ Data Comprehension Skills – Ability to read and interpret documents such as contracts, marketing related analytics, and internal policies and communications. Ability to write and present reports and communciations as required to demonstrate results and maintain awareness within the organization of all marketing activities.</li>
<li>Mathematical Skills – Ability to add, subtract, multiply, and divide in all units of measure. Ability to compute rate, ratio, and percent.</li>
<li>Reasoning Ability –Ability to solve practical problems and deal with a variety of concrete variables in situations where only limited standardization exists. Ability to interpret a variety of instructions furnished in written, oral, diagram, or schedule form.</li>
<li>Computer Skills –Knowledge of computer workstations and peripheral equipment; knowledge of database, spreadsheet, and word processing software to manage marketing related analytics.</li>
<li>Experience managing a team for productivity through engagement and accountability.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>This position is based out of the Charlotte branch but will be required to travel 100% to Greensboro, Raleigh, &amp; Wilmington.</b></p>

</ul>
