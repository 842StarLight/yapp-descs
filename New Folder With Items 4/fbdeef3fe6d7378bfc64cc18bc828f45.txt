<b>Position Overview:</b><br>
Amobee is seeking a motivated and detail-oriented Senior Proposal Specialist to join our Proposal Development Team. The primary responsibility of this position is to project manage, write &amp; compile on-brand RFx (RFI, RFP, etc.) responses that showcase Amobee’s bespoke solutions for leading brands &amp; agencies. The ideal candidate will have prior demonstrated success in proposal development or project management roles, preferably within the digital advertising industry. Candidates should be able to manage multiple projects at once and enjoy collaborating with a variety of cross-functional teams (including sales, sales engineering, product, etc.). This individual will possess a strong technical aptitude and the communication skills necessary to answer complex customer requests in a clear and compelling way.
<br><br>
<b>Responsibilities:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Lead the compilation of RFI/RFP responses for global advertisers &amp; agencies under tight timeframes</li>
<li>Coordinate all aspects of the proposal preparation, from kick-off through final submission</li>
<li>Collaborate with key stakeholders and relevant SMEs to ensure responses are factually correct, tailored to customer need, and completed on time</li>
<li>Establish relationships with SMEs to continuously improve the quality of content</li>
<li>Work with Proposal Development team members to build, maintain &amp; expand the team’s central database of master content, including a list of questions, answers &amp; images for RFx FAQs</li>
<li>Ensure content library materials are edited regularly to reflect the latest product offerings and capabilities</li>
<li>Own the team’s work tracking metrics, including post-submission success, SLA adherence, and wins/losses</li>
<li>Maintain a current understanding on technical solutions, products and positioning offered by Amobee</li>
<li>Train other teams on the proposal response process and content management software</li>
</ul>
<b>Requirements:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>B.S. or B.A. preferably in Business, Marketing, Advertising, Communications, Journalism, or a related field</li>
<li>3-4+ years experience as a Proposal Writer, Coordinator, or Manager</li>
<li>Experience in the digital advertising industry preferred</li>
<li>Exceptional verbal and written communication skills,</li>
<li>Strong technical aptitude, comfort with technical concepts &amp; ability to clearly communicate product benefits</li>
<li>Proficiency in proposal and presentation production software, including Microsoft Office and Google Suite</li>
<li>Experience using Salesforce.com, Ariba, Prizm and other proposal management tools</li>
<li>Experience using Ombud, Loopio, Qvidian or other content management software</li>
<li>Experience using Smartsheets, Tableau and data analysis are pluses</li>
<li>Interest in graphics &amp; visual design is a plus</li>
<li>Strong work ethic, high attention to detail, and a desire to work in a dynamic industry with rapidly changing environment</li>
<li>Must be a team player with ability to work independently while demonstrating a high level of prioritization, organizational, and time management skills.</li>
</ul>
<b>Location:</b> New York, NY
