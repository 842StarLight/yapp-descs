<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Please review the following job description:
</b></h3>
<p></p>Primary focus on growing retail households through promoting and improving financial wellness, while providing meaningful value to business clients and prospects. Responsible for promoting the BB&amp;T@Work Financial Wellness tools and resources and program benefits to companies and their employees through a variety of activities, and through referral relationships with internal and external partners. Serve as BB&amp;T@Work Financial Wellness liaison in Community Bank Regions to create awareness of program, increase product knowledge, and foster presentation activity.
<p></p>
<p><b>Essential Duties and Responsibilities:
</b><br>
Following is a summary of the essential functions for this job. Other duties may be performed, both major and minor, which are not mentioned below. Specific activities may change from time to time.
<br>
1. Develop call strategies with internal partners and through self-prospecting efforts. Focus efforts on Corporate and Commercial segments.
<br>
2. Identify and prioritize qualified BB&amp;T@Work Financial Wellness company prospects.
<br>
3. Make calls to the decision-makers of companies to promote the BB&amp;T@Work Financial Wellness benefits package and establish employee communication strategies.
<br>
4. Conduct Financial Wellness presentations and account opening sessions for employees of participating businesses, and meet behavior and sales goals.
<br>
5. Maintain comprehensive knowledge of the BB&amp;T@Work Financial Wellness Program, account opening procedures and competitive programs and products in local markets.
<br>
6. While a specialist in BB&amp;T@Work Financial Wellness and deposits, also act as a generalist banker and share the BB&amp;T value story on all calls, carefully listening for other financial needs clients may have and getting them connected with the appropriate team members.
<br>
7. Develop relationships with internal partners in order to maintain their awareness of BB&amp;T@Work Financial Wellness and foster referrals and client introductions.
<br>
8. Provide leadership and training to retail teammates to develop an extended sales force.
<br>
9. Integrate retail and business teams for seamless delivery of the Perfect Client Experience.
<br>
10. Represent the Bank through community and professional involvement in groups and associations.
<br>
11. Proactively cultivate Centers of Influence.
</p>
