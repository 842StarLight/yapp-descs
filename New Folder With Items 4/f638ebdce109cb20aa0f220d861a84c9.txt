<h2>Manager, Business Intelligence Enablement</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Join Our Community of Food People!
</p>
<p></p>"The Manager of Enterprise Business Intelligence (BI) Enablement leads the BI Governance and Enablement team within the Insight and Analytics (I&amp;A) department, managing priorities of the team and is accountable for the team’s work and success. The individual in this role:
<br>
<ul>
<li>Develops the BI roadmap, both in the form of quarterly/periodic segments from an execution standpoint and as a partner in the long-term strategy development
</li>
<li>Partners with other Managers inside Business Analytics as well as business leaders to develop, refine and execute on a BI roadmap that delivers maximum value for all stakeholders
</li>
<li>Assists the Sr. Director with change management and training within the department, as well as with business partners to ultimately drive and sustain a self-service BI model
</li>
<li>Leads reporting certification, development and visualization standards and rationalization as well as metrics stewardship for US Foods to leverage best practices
</li>
<li>Collaborates closely with the Business Analyst Managers/teams of assigned business functions (e.g., Category Management, Marketing/ eCommerce and Sales; Supply Chain Replenishment; Supply Chain Logistics, etc.) when developing and ensuring the BI policies and processes are utilized throughout the department
</li>
<li>Understand how Business Analyst Managers/teams work and what each role/function needs from the BI Governance and Enablement team
</li>
<li>Leads RFPs and tool implementation for I&amp;A and therefore will require a strong understanding of technology in the BI space as it pertains to new tools and trends
</li>
<li>Closely integrates and partners with IT organization on a day-to-day basis as well as on longer-term projects"
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>

Strategic Solution Development

<ul>

<li>Drive standardization of tools, metrics, reporting and diagnostic processes to drive more efficient access to business intelligence and link decision making contextually across the enterprise
</li>
<li>Assist Sr Director in driving continuous improvement through the management of BI tools, visualization capabilities and data transformation

</li>
<li>Provide leadership in BI industry trends, including new technology and visualization techniques
