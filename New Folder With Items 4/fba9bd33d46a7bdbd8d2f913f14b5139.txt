<h2>Graphic Designer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p>The Graphic Designer is responsible for conceptualizing, planning, designing, and producing a wide variety of high quality graphic designs to fulfill the conceptual vision that meets strategic and creative objectives for each project. Provides and executes technical and creative solutions for internal marketing teams, in strong partnership with Creative Director, strategists, and marketing managers. The quality of the design outputs are critical components of this role in helping the team anticipate and leverage the best ways to execute the work â€“ while being mindful of time, cost, and quality.</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Primary Responsibilities</b><b> </b>:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Produces both print and online sales and marketing collateral, advertising, conference, direct response, informational, and other corporate collateral materials.</li>
<li>
Attends and actively participates in creative meetings to determine strategic and creative objectives of each project.</li>
<li>
Collaborates with copywriter(s) to fulfill strategic and creative objectives.</li>
<li>
Determines size and arrangement of graphics, layout, and features based on media type, knowledge of layout principles, and aesthetic design concepts.</li>
<li>
Researches and executes custom graphic needs, including acquisition of stock photos, and arrangements for custom illustration.</li>
<li>
Responsible for the delivery of high-quality work (e.g. spell-check, construction check, asset optimization, consistency, and final output).</li>
<li>
Responds to project requests in a timely fashion to meet time-sensitive project deadlines and oversee project through completion.</li>
<li>
Proofreads design work and graphic files, distribute PDF proofs to project requestors, and make all necessary corrections to files before considering a project complete.</li>
<li>
Responsible for juggling multiple deadlines, prioritizing work, and keeping projects moving forward.</li>
<li>
Meets deadlines as requested in a fast-paced project environment.</li>
<li>
Maintains current knowledge of computer design software applications and emerging technologies in new media.</li>
<li>
Participates in special projects and performs other duties as assigned.</li>
</ul>
