<h2>Assistant Retreat Director</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>Become part of the leadership team at <b>Massage Heights</b> and help build and guide your team to inspire, educate and promote a balanced lifestyle for every member and guest that walks through our door. We are in search of an <b>Assistant Retreat Director</b> who is seeking an entry level management position. The ideal candidate loves sales, is responsible, ambitious and diligent. This position is the beginning to building a rewarding career in a dynamic industry.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
Here’s why you’ll love working at <b>Massage Heights</b>:</p>
<ul>
<li>
<b>Robust</b> leadership training and development: we want to support you in your role and set you up for success</li>
<li>You’ll experience a culture that invests <b>positive energy</b> into everything we do</li>
<li>Employee benefits, flexible schedules and a <b>rewarding</b> career path</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p>
</p>
<p>The essential duties and responsibilities of the <b>Assistant Retreat Director</b> position are designed to assist the Retreat Director to help ensure exceptional guest service and team member satisfaction, these duties include but are not limited to the following:</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
Assist the Retreat Director in managing all aspects of the <b>Daily Operations</b>:</p>
<ul>
<li>Directly assist the Retreat Director to supervise all aspects of Retreat operations, from driving revenue and profitability to managing inventory, ensuring brand systems and processes are adhered to with <b>consistency</b> through each shift</li>
<li>Assist as assigned with recruitment, hiring, <b>training</b>, developing and retaining top talent</li>
<li>
<b>Plan</b> and <b>implement</b> Front Desk Sales Associate training; in-the-moment coaching, role playing, and training is essential for the <b>success</b> of the Front Desk Team</li>
<li>Stay updated on all services, products and promotions, be <b>diligent</b> in setting the example for your team</li>
<li>Effectively <b>manage</b> team member schedules to ensure proper execution of daily operations</li>
<li>
<b>Collaborate</b> with the Retreat Director to set individual team member and overall Retreat goals; create plans and convert plans into action to meet and exceed goals</li>
<li>Lead by example, demonstrating the ability to achieve personal as well as team goals</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p>
</p>
<p>Adhere and align the team and Retreat to our <b>Brand Standards</b>:</p>
<ul>
<li>Create an environment that is passionate about providing supreme guest service</li>
<li>Focus on building guest loyalty by consistently following the proven operating system of Massage Heights</li>
<li>Enhance the value of the Massage Heights brand name in the minds of current and future guests by supporting and implementing the brand’s Mission, Core Values and Strategies</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p>
</p>
<p><b>A strong candidate will possess the following</b>:</p>
<ul>
<li>At least 1-year face to face sales or retail management experience</li>
<li>Self-starter mentality with leadership, organization, communication and interpersonal skills</li>
<li>At least 1-year experience in training, coaching, and conflict resolution</li>
<li>Capacity to develop positive relationships with employees to manage coaching/mentoring with discipline</li>
<li>Demonstrate working knowledge of computers and standard software</li>
<li>Basic business math skills</li>
<li><b>Must be able to work varied hours to include weekends, evenings, holidays, etc</b></li>
<br>
</ul>
