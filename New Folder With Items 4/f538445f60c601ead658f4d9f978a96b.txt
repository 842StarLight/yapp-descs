<h2>Golf Marketing Coordinator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p><b>SUMMARY</b></p>
<p>
The Marketing Coordinator will oversee and implement the execution and production of marketing initiatives. The Marketing Coordinator will work with the Vice President of Sports Marketing to contribute and help drive the development of the Golf brands: Grand Slam and Jack Nicklaus. He or she will maintain, direct and be responsible for the execution of day-to-day Marketing department activities.</p>
<div>
<p><b>DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES:</b></p>
</div>
<div>
<ul>
<li>Help coordinate the creative development process and all projects that flow through the department</li>
<li>Help coordinate all production, planning and on-set follow through of photo shoots, in studio (look book) and on location (ad campaigns) – model selection, photographers, locations, studio, artists, etc.)</li>
<li>Work collaboratively with cross functional departments</li>
<li>Manage and develop all website, social media strategies, promotions and daily updates</li>
<li>Manage and develop all social media paid ads including content strategy, copy and budget</li>
<li>Facilitate in developing copy for e-comm, catalogs, social media as well as all other marketing needs.</li>
<li>Coordinate requested sample shipping</li>
<li>Manage and submit creative requests and trademark approval forms.</li>
<li>Liaison with legal to ensure timely execution of contracts and project approvals</li>
<li>Coordinate across departments collateral and in-store marketing needs</li>
<li>Support in coordination of needs for trade shows and sales meetings</li>
<li>Update/educate internal teams on marketing communications, best practice strategies.</li>
<li>Collaborate with Vice President of Sports Marketing on development of brand strategy, marketing initiatives, and updating processes.</li>
<li>Maintain brand marketing calendar</li>
<li>Work with creative team on all assigned projects to track and maintain and accurate status reports</li>
<li>Coordinate scheduling meetings for both internal and external resource</li>
<li>Other duties as assigned</li>
</ul>
<p><b>SKILLS, KNOWLEDGE AND ABILITIES</b></p>
</div>
<div><ul>
<li>Strong writing and presentation skills</li>
<li>Understanding of graphics and video production</li>
<li>Highly organized with keen attention to detail</li>
<li>Possess a strong sense of urgency</li>
<li>Be a sports enthusiast, preferably the game of golf and tennis</li>
</ul></div>
<p><b>MINIMUM REQUIREMENTS</b></p>
<div>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s Degree in Marketing, Advertising preferred</li>
<li>1-3 year Marketing experience preferred</li>
<li>Proficient in with Google Apps, Social Media, Microsoft Office</li>
<li>Golf / Sports Marketing experience is a plus</li>
</ul>
</div>
