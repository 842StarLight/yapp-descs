Want to join a company on the cutting edge of technology and travel? Want to be part of a fantastic and fun company that’s revolutionizing the online travel advertising space?

<br><br>
<b>The role:</b><br>
Sojern is seeking an Associate Account Manager to work with major travel advertisers in a fast-paced, online advertising environment. The Associate Account Manager will play a critical role within the company through hands-on management of advertiser campaigns while developing and maintaining our partner relationships. Key duties of the role include:
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Responsible for coordinating pre-sale activities</li>
<li>Enterprise Team is responsible for working closely with the sales team during the RFP process to ensure accounts are set up for success</li>
<li>Lead the day-to-day, post sales relationship with a portfolio of digital advertising clients</li>
<li>Take ownership of your book of business to drive relationships with your clients leading to renewals and upsells, ensuring campaign success and client satisfaction</li>
<li>Grow revenue through pitching new products and channels</li>
<li>Coordinate with AdOps, analyze campaign performance and provide recommendations to ensure client satisfaction</li>
<li>Meet client KPIs, goals and objectives</li>
<li>Lead post-campaign analysis and review</li>
<li>Coordinate billing for clients on a monthly basis</li>
<li>Partner closely with internal teams on day-to-day operational processes and larger, high-impact strategic improvements</li>
<li>Present campaign results and insights, as well as Business Reviews to clients on a regular basis</li>
<li>Monitor and influence campaign delivery, troubleshoot issues and provide optimization and/or upsell opportunities across products.</li>
</ul>
To succeed in this role, you must work well with people, be comfortable making data-driven decisions, refining processes and have an aptitude for learning!
<br><br>

<li>Managing Relationships - Meeting the personal needs of individuals to build trust, encourage two-way communication, and strengthen relationships internally and externally.</li>

<li>Managing Work - Effectively managing one’s time and resources to ensure that work is completed efficiently.</li>
</ul>
