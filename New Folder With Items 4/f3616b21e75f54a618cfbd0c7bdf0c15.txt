<h2>Inside Sales - Enterprise Software</h2>

<p>HireFaster is a secure, SaaS cloud solution for global pre-employment screening. The portal allows Hiring Firms to strengthen internal controls and mitigate fraud risks. Products include:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Real-time biometric ID and official document authentication</li>
<li>
5yrs real-time employment verification</li>
<li>
Professional reference checks</li>
<li>
Global criminal and credit background checks</li>
<li>
Qualification, Membership and Licence Verifications</li>
</ul>
<p>There is an opening for an articulate inside sales professional, with at least 2 years experience in a similar role, to handle initial inquiries in a consultative manner. Business acumen matters more than a tech background. Over time you may also be involved in software demos. Our target market is large corporate clients and big ticket sales. You should be comfortable dealing directly with department heads. Target sectors include: Financial Services, Information Technology, Healthcare and Industrials, although other industries with stringent hiring criteria are also relevant.</p>
