<div><b>ACCOUNT MANAGER, FIELD SALES</b></div>
<div><b>(Chicago)</b></div>
<div></div>
<div>
<p>Atmosphere is the first-of-its kind streaming portal containing original, owned and operated ambient TV channels. From the team that built and introduced CHIVE TV to thousands of businesses and homes worldwide, Atmosphere includes a variety of contoured audio-optional channels, updated regularly. While CHIVE TV remains a flagship channel, new channels such as Happy TV, Alpine TV, Motiv8 and many more have been curated to fit any vibe and integrate seamlessly into any environment, from bars and gyms to offices and waiting rooms.</p>
<p>This is a Field Sales Position that requires "face-to-face" cold calling and STRONG presentation skills! We need someone who lives off of rejection and thrives on closing deals. We need a hustler!</p>
<p>Atmosphere is seeking a Field Sales Account Manager to drive Atmosphere TV installs and Pro digital signage sales/upgrades, in all relevant verticals and SMB enterprise groups. The position will be responsible for regional sales growth within the assigned territory.</p>
<p>This position is based in Chicago, IL.</p>
<p>JOB DESCRIPTION:</p>
<ul>
<li>Grow Atmosphere distribution (installs/Pro Sales) within necessary verticals and qualified SMB chains in assigned region(s)</li>
<li>Create unique ways to generate lead and target lists for region/territory</li>
<li>Under general direction, solicit business by “Cold-Calling” clients, attempting to interest prospective buyers and explaining the merit of product and its application.</li>
<li>Drive the market penetration strategy.</li>
<li>Keep information on competitive products, promotional matters, sales techniques, pricing and marketing policies.</li>
<li>Inform company of competitive activity and conditions that would affect company interests.</li>
<li>Ability to work nights and weekends on promotional activities and other account activities is a must.</li>
</ul>
<p>REQUIREMENTS:</p>
<ul>
<li>Not afraid of rejection</li>
<li>2+ years of prior sales experience is a must.</li>
<li>Bar or liquor rep/distributor experience preferred.</li>
<li>Proven ability in pitching and closing face-to-face deals.</li>
<li>Working knowledge of sales pipeline management tools (SalesForce, NetSuite, other).</li>
<li>Strong interpersonal skills with the ability to communicate with the stakeholders at all levels.</li>
<li>Driven, team player motivated to take responsibility and ensure successful outcomes.</li>
<li>Ability to survive and thrive in a fast-paced environment.</li>
<li>Ability to work long hours and during weekend frequently.</li>
<li>Expect +50% travel time managing the team.</li>
<li>This position is in Chicago, IL - Living in the Chicago area is a must.</li>
</ul>
<p>COMPENSATION &amp; BENEFITS:</p>
<ul>
<li>Competitive salary.</li>
<li>Lucrative Commission Structure (Work Hard=GET PAID!)</li>
<li>Profit sharing.</li>
<li>401(k) with a company match plan (after 6 months with the company).</li>
<li>Open vacation policy.</li>
<li>Competitive medical/dental/vision insurance plans.</li>
</ul>
