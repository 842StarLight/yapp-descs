<h2>Quality Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>Lead Quality Assurance for the Stanley Access Technologies’ Farmington Plant, reporting into the Director of Manufacturing Operations. Responsible for maintaining customer satisfaction, the ISO 9001, supplier quality, and product quality from conception through manufacturing. Key interface with customers and manufacturing associates for resolving external and internal failures. Coordinate and support corporate-wide efforts to improve quality and profitability of the business.<br>
<br>
<b>Job Description</b>:<br>
<ul>
<li>
Effectively communicate with customers and assist in the resolution of customer inquiries regarding product quality, product reliability, customer complaints and other questions or concerns.</li>
<li>
As the Management Representative, maintain the ISO9001 registration including communication and implementation of standard requirements. Coordinating efforts to simplify and advance the Quality System so that it provides business value and a solid base for continuous improvement.</li>
<li>
Enhance supplier quality via systemic supplier approval process, purchase part specifications, and using metrics and rhythms to assure quality of purchased components.</li>
<li>
Provide support for attainment of productivity, safety and other goals as set forth by the Organization.</li>
<li>
Work with manufacturing on developing and sustaining daily, weekly, monthly, and annual rhythms for scrap, rework, aluminum fall-off and other essential quality improvement efforts.</li>
<li>
Analyze and translate information from individual customers, customer surveys, focus groups, quality function deployment (QFD) teams, and other sources. Use the information to develop quality standards for products to increase customer retention and sales revenue.</li>
<li>
Through application of team-oriented improvement programs and other tools provide systems and processes for effective and economical in-process and final inspection of product.</li>
<li>
Engage hourly employees to grow the quality culture through presentations, training, awards, postings, product audits, quality teams, and other approaches.</li>
<li>
Provide dedicated support to and follow up with associates engaged in assembly, inspection, and testing activities to ensure material and product quality.</li>
<li>
Maintain business ethics and integrity per Corporate guidelines.</li>
<li>
Coach and mentor on continuous improvement tools.</li>
</ul>
<br>
<b>Special Requirements</b>:<br>
<ul>
<li>
Effective communication (written/verbal) and presentation skills.</li>
<li>
Team player, able to interact at all levels of the organization.</li>
<li>
Analytical and problem solving skills.</li>
<li>
More than 5 years experience in quality assurance.</li>
<li>
Application of Six Sigma methodology and statistical tools, Black Belt certification preferred.</li>
<li>
Strong organizational and project management skills.</li>
<li>
Business and financial acumen with a technical manufacturing background preferred.</li>
<li>
In-depth knowledge of Microsoft Office software with Microsoft Access database design skills required.</li>
<li>
Two or more years of managerial experience preferred.</li>
<li>
Quality background in “make-to-order” environment or electromechanical assembly desired.</li>
</ul>
<br>
<b>Education Requirements</b>:<br>
<br>
BA/BS (Master’s preferred) in Business Administration or Engineering (Mechanical, Manufacturing, Electrical or related field)</div></div>
