<h2>Senior Director Information Technology</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">The Sr. Director of Information Technology (IT) for Pacific Scientific EMC is a senior leadership position responsible for setting strategy and direction for a highly capable, collaborative IT organization. The Sr. Director of IT will lead a team defining and driving the overall direction, vision, deployment and management of both hardware and software for the company, with a strong focus in support of integrated supply chain manufacturing and quality across the business.
<br><br>
<b>Role and Responsibilities:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Lead, manage, coach, and grow the members of the IT team in multiple locations.</li>
<li>Administer and control the information systems expense budget to contribute to a cost-effective operation.</li>
<li>Maintain knowledge of developments in the area of systems and hardware to assure that new developments are incorporated in future systems of the Company.</li>
<li>Identify and present hardware and software to ensure that computer capabilities are responsive to the needs of corporate growth and changing corporate objectives.</li>
<li>Continued liaison with other senior leaders in order to answer all questions, evaluate needs, monitor service, advise on problems, encourage data processing education and understanding, and ensure smooth conversion from manual to computerized systems, or from existing computer systems to new improved ones.</li>
<li>Partner with vendors and suppliers to help deliver services provided by our IT organization.</li>
<li>Own information security for the organization which includes compliance with corporate standards.</li>
<li>Manage, maintain, and improve full portfolio of services we deliver. This includes:</li>
</ul>
o Continuing transformation to paperless manufacturing o Datacenter that houses most of the equipment we use.
<br><br>
o Large high-performance compute grid used for IP development and testing. Refresh compute nodes regularly.
<br><br>
o Large storage footprint used for both personal productivity and for IP design and development. This includes backup and archive of critical data.
<br><br>
o All networking services within the organization.
<br><br>
o Vendor selection for large purchases of storage and compute pools used by the organization.
<br><br>
o Laptops, desktops and associated software and services for our large organization.
<br><br>
o IT helpdesk and 24x7 support for our organization. Maintain strong customer satisfaction and rapid turn-around time on issues and requests.
<br><br>
<b>Skills and Qualifications:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree in Information Technology or related field. Masters degree a plus.</li>
<li>15 years experience in the IT field, to include 5+ years of managerial experience managing an IT organization</li>
<li>Experience in Aerospace and Defense or other compliance driven industry a plus</li>
<li>Experience leading IT team working across geographies and cultures.</li>
<li>Proven track record of delivering complex IT services and maintaining strong teams.</li>
<li>Strong communication skills from executive level to individual contributor level.</li>
<li>Ability to plan and execute complex programs,</li>
<li>Ability to travel as required by role.</li>
<li>Knowledge of Info Visual or other ERP system and Aegis or related systems</li>
</ul>
