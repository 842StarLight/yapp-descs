<h2>MBA Intern - Product Marketing</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>MBA Summer Associate – Future of Work, Product Marketing Associate</b></p>
<p></p>
<p>
HP is looking for a full-time summer associate to join us in Houston, Texas. As part of the future solutions team, you will be responsible for helping define new product and service concepts that will realize HP’s vision for the Future of Work.</p>
<p></p>
<p>
The nature of work is changing: demographic shifts and new business models are changing the workforce, the blur of where work happens is changing the workplace, and new collaboration tools and interfaces are changing how people work.</p>
<p></p>
<p>
The team helps define HP’s opportunities, products, and go-to-market strategies in this Future of Work, and we need your help.</p>
<p></p>
<p>
Business Personal Systems (BPS), part of HP's Printer and Personal Systems Group (PPS), is a dynamic growing business. The Commercial PC Business is a high-growth, fast paced business within BPS responsible for $18B across notebooks, desktops, and convertibles. As part of the future solutions team, you will be responsible for helping to develop sales tools and marketing assets to communicate HP’s vision for Office of the Future.</p>
<p></p>
<p>
Office of the Future is a company wide initiative building on the trends in the future workforce, workplace, and work style. Here you will be working across the different business units to build tools to enable the sales force to communicate and deliver against this vision. In addition you will have responsibility to develop key future experiences and concept definitions to be tested by customers. Strong customer and user experience/design thinking background, as well as product knowledge and program management skills critical. You will get a unique end to end view from concept development to GTM enablement.</p>
<p></p>
<p>
We have:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Massive impact: as the #1 market leader for PCs, HP has resources and reach to scale</li>
<li>
Global perspective: &gt;70% of HP’s business is outside the U.S., and the Future of Work is truly a global opportunity</li>
<li>
Team of intrapreneurs: this nimble team of strategists, engineers, and product managers works directly with HP’s leadership team</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p>
You have:</p>
<ul>
<li>
A creative, design thinking mindset to tackle new challenges from different perspectives</li>
<li>
Ability to take insights from tech trends and raw research to actionable product concepts and user experiences</li>
<li>
Project management skills to juggle many moving pieces while prioritizing for big picture</li>
<li>
Communication skills to convey complicated ideas clearly, in person and on paper, to diverse audiences</li>
<li>
Motivation to take ownership of your time with us to challenge yourself and learn</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p>
You might also have:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Experience in product management, product marketing, experience design, or management consulting – can be through work experience, school projects, club leadership, etc.</li>
<li>
Experience in B2B, SaaS, or hardware</li>
</ul>
