<h2>Client Service Coordinator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>WHO WE ARE:</b></p>
<p>
Premier Healthcare Services is a leading provider of skilled in-home nursing care for patients. We are currently looking for a passionate, empathetic, motivated and COMMITTED individual to fill our Service Coordinator Position! Our success is largely based upon hiring ambitious team members who provide outstanding customer care.</p>
<p><b>
JOB DESCRIPTION:</b></p>
<p>
The Service Coordinator is a sale and customer service oriented individual who builds/retains relationships with currently employed nurses, potential nurse candidates, and patients. They are responsible for the staffing, recruitment, screening, hiring and retention of external personnel.
</p>
<p><b>WHAT PREMIER OFFERS:</b><br>
</p>
<ul>
<li>WEEKLY pay (competitive rate).</li>
<li>BENEFITS: Medical, Dental, Vision, vacations/sick days, life insurance, and 401(k).</li>
<li>Quarterly bonus potential of $1,500.</li>
<li>Generous referral BONUSES.</li>
<li>Make a difference in a child/adult's life.</li>
<li>FUN, POSITIVE, and PROFESSIONAL work environment.</li>
<li>Employee appreciation events &amp; employee recognition awards.</li>
<li>Weekly core values meeting, regular paid trainings &amp; orientations.</li>
<li>Monthly and quarterly office outings, and an annual company-wide beach party.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>SERVICE COORDINATOR RESPONSIBILITIES:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Staffing/scheduling of nurses (assign nurse shifts to patients).</li>
<li>Manage &amp; maintain nurse/patient schedules.</li>
<li>Answer high volume of calls.</li>
<li>Provide EXCEPTIONAL customer service to staff and clients.</li>
<li>Actively recruit, post jobs, interview &amp; staff nurse personnel.</li>
<li>Accompanying the Homecare Manager on sales/customer service calls.</li>
<li>Meet goals &amp; deadlines.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>SERVICE COORDINATOR QUALIFICATIONS:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Must enjoy sales and business development, while providing customer service to diverse clients.</li>
<li>Bachelor's Degree preferred or relevant industry experience required.</li>
<li>Proficient in English required, Bilingual a PLUS.</li>
<li>Highly organized.</li>
<li>Effective communication skills.</li>
<li>Ability to analyze information through problem-solving.</li>
<li>Computer literate and able to adapt to various software.</li>
<li>Able to perform under pressure in a fast-paced environment.</li>
</ul>
