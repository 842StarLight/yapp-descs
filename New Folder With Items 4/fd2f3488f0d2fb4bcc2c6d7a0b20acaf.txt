<h1 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>POSITION PURPOSE</b></h1>
The controller position is accountable for the accounting operations of the company, to include the production of periodic financial reports, maintenance of an adequate system of accounting records, and a comprehensive set of controls and budgets designed to mitigate risk, enhance the accuracy of the company's reported financial results, and ensure that reported results comply with generally accepted accounting principles or international financial reporting standards.<br>

<h1 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>ESSENTIAL FUNCTIONS AND BASIC DUTIES</b></h1>
<ul>
<li>
<b>Assumes </b><b>responsibility for </b><b>maintaining </b><b>the financial integrity and controls of the </b><b>company. Specific </b><b>tasks include but are not </b><b>limited</b><b> </b><b>to:</b>
</li>
</ul>
<div><div><ul>
<li>Monthly, quarterly, and annual financial reporting.</li>
</ul></div></div>
<div><div><ul>
<li>Overseeing or handling personally all accounting and finance activities for the company, including general ledger, inventory, A/R, A/P, Payroll, employee benefits and budgeting for the company.</li>
</ul></div></div>
<div><div><ul>
<li>Lead all cost account efforts including variance analysis and cost roll ups.</li>
</ul></div></div>
<div><div><ul>
<li>Works closely with President and accountant regarding financial data. Works closely with accountant to file quarterly state and local tax and year-end tax estimates and returns. Responsible for filing sales use and property taxes.</li>
</ul></div></div>
<div><div><ul>
<li>Responsible for internal ERP accounting software. Maintains current package and researches alternatives when feasible. Responsible for managing outsourced functions and providers including but not limited to internet, telephone, etc.</li>
</ul></div></div>
<div><div><ul>
<li>Help to ensure adherence to Group policies and procedures by company employees. Help to ensure adherence to all laws and regulations by all company employees.</li>
</ul></div></div>
</div>
<div>
<ul>
<li>
<b>Oversee </b><b>the </b><b>operations </b><b>of the </b><b>accounting team, </b><b>including the </b><b>design </b><b>of an </b><b>organizational </b><b>structure </b><b>adequate </b><b>for </b><b>achieving </b><b>the </b><b>department’s goals </b><b>and</b><b> </b><b>objectives.</b>
</li>
</ul>
<div><div><ul>
<li>Supervise and develop existing accounting team of three people: Jr. Accountant, Payroll/AP and Credit/AR. Corrects variances in performances.</li>
</ul></div></div>
<div><div><ul>
<li>Ensures that accounts payable are paid in a timely manner and that all reasonable discounts are taken.</li>
</ul></div></div>
<div><div><ul>
<li>Ensures that accounts receivable are collected promptly and in accordance with Company standards. Assists in collections activities and resolution of all delinquent or problem accounts.</li>
</ul></div></div>
<div><div><ul>
<li>Process payroll in a timely manner.</li>
</ul></div></div>
<div><div><ul>
<li>Maintains the chart of accounts and that periodic bank reconciliations are completed.</li>
</ul></div></div>
<div><div><ul>
<li>Maintains an orderly accounting filing system and an orderly system of controls over accounting transactions.</li>
</ul></div></div>
<ul>
<li>
<b>Assumes </b><b>responsibility for company </b><b>legal </b><b>and </b><b>compliance</b><b> </b><b>objectives.</b>
</li>
</ul>
<div><div><ul>
<li>Issue timely and complete financial statements. Comply with local, state, and federal government reporting filing requirements and tax filings.</li>
</ul></div></div>
<div><div><ul>
<li>Initiates and maintains database of all intellectual property, Non-Disclosure Agreements (NDA’s), trademarks, customer contracts and vendor contracts.</li>
</ul></div></div>
<div><div><ul>
<li>Analysis of both strategic and tactical activities of the company. Provide financial analyses as needed, in particular for capital investments, pricing decisions, and contract negotiations.</li>
</ul></div></div>
<div><div><ul>
<li>Helping in the establishment of policies and procedures for the company as they become necessary.</li>
</ul></div></div>
<div><div><ul>
<li>Ensures that the Company’s quality reputation is maintained.</li>
</ul></div></div>
<ul>
<li>
<b>Assumes </b><b>responsibility for </b><b>related </b><b>duties </b><b>as </b><b>required </b><b>or</b><b> </b><b>assigned.</b>

<h1 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>PERFORMANCE MEASUREMENTS</b></h1>
<ul>
<li>All internal accounting functions are accurate, orderly and filed on a timely basis. Expenses and costs are controlled and well-managed.</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Employee benefit plans and programs are appropriate and effective. Accounting objectives are executed in accordance with approved policies and procedures.</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Current managed accounts are maintained or expanded and additional ones solicited. Good business relationships exist with accountant and bank/finance.</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Accounting team personnel are well-trained, efficient, and effective.</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>President is appropriately informed of accounting activities and significant problems.</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>All local, state and federal legal requirements and reports are accurate and filed on a timely basis.</li>
<br>
</ul>
