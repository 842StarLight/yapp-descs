<h2>Redmond - Customer Service Representative</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p>Brown Bear Car Wash, is looking for a full-time customer service representative for our car wash and store in Redmond WA at 17809 Redmond Way in Redmond. You must be 21 to work in the store. Various positions are available c-store: Monday, Wednesday, Friday and Saturday from 7:00 am to 3:00 pm. Also, Thursday, Friday and Saturday closing shift from 2:00 pm to 12:00 am. Car wash position Monday through Sunday 7:00 am to 3:00 pm and 12:30 pm to 7:30 pm.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
Starting rate is $17 per hour</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
Job Description</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
FLSA STATUS: Non-Exempt</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
REPORTS TO: Site Manager</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
QUALIFICATIONS AND REQUIREMENTS:</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
High School diploma or GED preferred.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
Experience in retail sales or customer service preferred.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
Ability to communicate effectively with customers and coworkers.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
Ability to read and follow written instructions and documents, like site procedures, safety manuals, etc.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
Ability to freely access all areas of store/site.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
Ability to operate and use all equipment necessary to run site, including shut offs for gas pumps or car wash.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
Ability to understand and apply basic arithmetical concepts like addition, subtraction, multiplication, division, fractions, and percentages.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
Ability to work with minimal direction and supervision under stressful conditions.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
Ability to climb ladders.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
Ability to regularly and predictably report to work, including irregular hours if needed.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
ESSENTIAL DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES (WILL VARY BY SITE COMPLEXITY):</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
Provide prompt, courteous customer service.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
Obtain merchandise requested by customer or receive merchandise selected by customer.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
Acknowledge, forward and/or resolve customer questions/complaints.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
Ring up all sales on cash register properly and accurately, and handle money and credit/debit card payment for products sold. Wrap or bag merchandise for customers.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
Accurately operate the cash register, fuel console, lottery machine, car wash console, and safety‑shut offs.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
Prepare and finalize shift closeout. Remove, secure and record amount of cash in register at end of shift.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
Solicit customers for upsell at both c-store and car wash.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
Perform inside/outside cleaning duties necessary to maintain site cleanliness and basic upkeep of all equipment at the site.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
Inspect site facilities and equipment for safety, cleanliness, and proper working order.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
Perform preventative maintenance duties as required.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
Receive and verify vendor deliveries.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
Control merchandise, cash shortages, and other expenses.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
Stamp, mark and/or price tag merchandise.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
Assist in maintaining proper inventory levels and shift audits. May be taught to assist with inventory and/or ordering of merchandise. Set up merchandise and/or advertising displays.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
Guide cars into car wash, pre-wash vehicles, and towel dry where required.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
On occasion, may be required to work irregular hours, including unscheduled shifts, overtime and attend job-related meetings.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
Perform other duties as assigned by the Person in Charge, Assistant Manager or Site Manager.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
PHYSICAL DEMANDS:</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
Must be able to stand and/or walk as needed throughout shift.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
Must be able to lift and/or carry up to 30 pounds from ground to overhead when stocking/maintaining merchandise and/or supplies.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
Able to lift and/or carry up to 60 pounds from ground to waist level when replenishing fountain syrups.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
Occasionally push and pull hand truck up to a force of 20 pounds.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
Able to bend and twist at waist when stocking/maintaining merchandise and/or supplies within c-store.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
Safely able to move car wash solution barrels weighing 100+ pounds, with proper equipment.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
Able to grasp, reach, and manipulate objects with hands continuously, such as while serving customers, making change, processing credit/debt transactions, etc.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
Able to climb a ladder to perform preventative maintenance, retrieve materials and/or place and remove signs.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
WORKING CONDITIONS:</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
In the c-store environment, approximately 95% of work performed indoors. However, also endure exposure to cold temperature extremes while performing needed work in store’s walk-in cooler and/or freezer.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
Work in conditions with exposure to occasional noise, such as traffic, sirens, car alarms, car wash equipment, emergency or security alarms.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
In the car wash or gasoline kiosk sites, work will be 95% outdoors, with exposure to varying temperatures, and including wet weather while performing duties. Able to reach, bend and twist for entire shift, where wipe down of vehicles is performed. Where wipe down of vehicles is performed, employees will be exposed to noise, requiring hearing protection.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
The above statements are intended to describe the general nature and level of work performed by those assigned to this job. They are not intended to be an exhaustive list of all responsibilities, duties, and skills required of the position.</p>
