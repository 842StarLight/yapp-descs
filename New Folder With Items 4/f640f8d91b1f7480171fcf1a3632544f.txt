<h2>IT Project Manager (IT Services Business)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>ESSENTIAL DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES</p>
<ul>
<li>Manage the daily activities of the project/engineering team</li>
<li>Improve usage of resources and increase productivity of the team</li>
<li>Act as the decision maker on technical solutions and client’s business strategy</li>
<li>The final authority on resolution to technical issues</li>
<li>Function as the customer’s single point-of-contact for projects.</li>
<li>Communicate with Service Delivery Manager on issues escalated by the managed services and support team</li>
<li>Communicate with all parties in a constructive manner to ensure customer expectations are met</li>
<li>Maintain awareness of all outstanding customer issues both pre- and post-project delivery and provide status to clients as necessary</li>
<li>Perform post-project review of all projects</li>
<li>Interface with appropriate technical personnel for customer problems that cannot be resolved effectively</li>
<li>Provide accurate reports and metrics to company management on status and budget of on-going projects and agreements</li>
<li>Understand overall team objectives, as well as the role and function of each team member</li>
<li>Manage the development of the team by ensuring that daily tasks and activities are in line with their career interests</li>
<li>Drive root cause problem investigation and resolution as required</li>
<li>Ensure that risks are identified, communicated, and mitigated and that projects are delivered successfully through to production</li>
<li>Design and maintain process documentation for the engineering/project team</li>
<li>Manage the process of implementing change efficiently and effectively</li>
<li>Provide guidance to the sales team for prospect and client project/opportunities</li>
</ul>
<p>ADDITIONAL DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES:</p>
<ul>
<li>Set strategy and own P&amp;L</li>
<li>Participate in, and manage, the hands-on engagement with targeted existing customers to build confidence in the value of our tools and confidence in meeting their goals</li>
<li>Ensure consistency of existing systems through creating, maintaining, and enforcing standards/procedures for implementing technical solutions</li>
<li>Identify areas for improvement and make constructive suggestions for change</li>
<li>Continually seek opportunities to increase customer satisfaction and deepen customer relationships</li>
<li>Communicate escalated issues through Service Delivery Manager: keeping him/her informed of progress, notifying of impending changes or agreed outages, etc.</li>
<li>Ensure consistency of existing systems through creating, maintaining, and enforcing standards/procedures for implementing technical solutions</li>
<li>Business awareness: specific knowledge of the customer and how IT relates to their business strategy and goals</li>
<li>Develop in-depth knowledge of the service catalog and how it relates to customer’s needs</li>
<li>Involvement in the design and building of new services</li>
<li>Conduct performance evaluations and mentor those with less experience</li>
<li>Develop training programs to develop and refine the skills of the field service and support team</li>
<li>Document internal processes and procedures related to duties and responsibilities</li>
<li>Responsible for entering time and expenses in ConnectWise as it occurs</li>
<li>Review and approve the project/engineering team time and expenses sheets in ConnectWise</li>
<li>Enter all work as activities, service tickets, or project tickets into ConnectWise</li>
<li>Review IT publications and online materials to remain up to date with current and future technologies emerging in the industry</li>
</ul>
<p>KNOWLEDGE, SKILLS, AND/OR ABILITIES REQUIRED</p>
<p>To perform this job successfully, an individual must be able to perform each essential duty satisfactorily. The requirements listed below are representative of the knowledge, skill, and/or ability required. Reasonable accommodations may be made to enable individuals with disabilities to perform the essential functions.</p>
<ul>
<li>Ability to maintain an extreme sense of urgency in all interactions with the customer</li>
<li>Ability and desire to encourage a team culture that promotes accountability while maintaining enthusiasm and creating growth opportunities for each person on the team</li>
<li>Foster a positive culture, with a focus on helpful client service and support</li>
<li>Professional IT Certifications, such as: Microsoft and VMware</li>
<li>Knowledge and experience in cross-functional management methods and techniques</li>
<li>Knowledge of IT applications, processes, software and equipment</li>
<li>Strong organizational, presentation, and customer service skills</li>
<li>Skill in strategic planning with an ability to think ahead and plan over a 6-12 month time span</li>
<li>Skill in planning and preparing written communications</li>
<li>Skill in leading people and getting results with a strong customer orientation</li>
<li>Interpersonal skills: such as telephony skills, communication skills, active listening and customer-care</li>
<li>Ability to multi-task and adapt to changes quickly</li>
<li>Ability to work in a team and communicate effectively</li>
<li>Service awareness of all organization’s key IT services for which support is being provided</li>
<li>Understanding of support tools, techniques, and how technology is used to provide IT services</li>
</ul>
