<h2>Wood Scientist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>POSITION OVERVIEW</p>
<p>The Sterling R&amp;D team finds solutions to the most difficultengineering challenges across all divisions of the company. Projects are seldom the same and often havelittle to no precedent. We’re seeking a versatile,experienced wood scientist who will help drive enhancements in the current Sterlingproducts and processes and develop innovative design concepts for emergingproduct lines.</p>
<p>The R&amp;D Wood Scientist must be task-oriented, extremelyadaptable and possess exceptional, out-of-the-box problem solving ability. This position will lead critical R&amp;Dprojects through the product development stage-gate process and will report toour Director of R&amp;D. As a R&amp;D WoodScientist in our rapidly growing enterprise, you will be on the fast track tobecoming a R&amp;D Project Lead.</p>
<p><b>ESSENTIAL FUNCTIONS &amp;RESPONSIBILITIES: </b></p>
<p>· Manage all phases of the <b>PRODUCT DEVELOPMENT STAGE-GATEPROCESS</b> including:</p>
<p><b>DISCOVERY</b></p>
<ul><li>Research new technologies and markets for long range expansioninitiatives.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Assess high-level business case.</li></ul>
<p><b>SCOPE</b></p>
<ul><li>Identify necessary technical requirements, restraints, specifications&amp; certifications.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Establish cost targets, supply/manufacturing strategy, marketing/salesstrategy.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Create and manage project timeline.</li></ul>
<p><b>DEVELOPMENT</b></p>
<ul><li>Engineer solutions, produce prototypes, obtain samples.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Determine sourcing, tooling, cost monitoring and test plan.</li></ul>
<p><b>TESTING / VALIDATION</b></p>
<ul><li>Execute test plans, manage field studies and validate certificationactions.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Process data and analyze results.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Iterate through scope, development and testing phases until projectoutcomes are confirmed.</li></ul>
<p><b>DEPLOYMENT / COMMERCIALIZATION</b></p>
<ul><li>Integrate new products and processes into all facets of theorganization, including supply chain, finance, manufacturing and construction services.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Act as subject matter expert for the marketing/sales organization.</li></ul>
<p><b>EDUCATION, TRAINING, CERTIFICATIONS &amp;EXPERIENCE: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor of Science in Wood Sciences</li>
<li>Minimum of 3 years experience</li>
</ul>
<p><b>SKILLS &amp; KNOWLEDGE: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Subject matter expertise in wood characteristics and behavior, mass timber products etc.</li>
<li>Represent company in technical organizations, trade shows and sales demos.</li>
<li>Proven leadership and collaboration skills with the ability to effectively supervise, coach, and influence employees, vendors and service providers.</li>
<li>Strong analytical skills to analyze complex business processes and identify risk in high visibility, and high stress situations; quickly and accurately apply sound, well-balanced reasoning to each decision.</li>
<li>Ability to identify opportunities to increase accuracy, optimize resources, and develop, recommend, and implement solutions.</li>
<li>Strong time and project management skills with ability to multitask and prioritize workloads.</li>
<li>Excellent verbal and written communication skills; able to present clean, organized and thorough information and data appropriate for intended audience.</li>
<li>Experience or trainable in forklift operation, wood working equipment and hand tools.</li>
<li>Proficiency with Microsoft Office Suite (Word, Excel, PowerPoint) and MS Project; ability to learn and achieve proficiency in new software applications.</li>
</ul>
