<h2>Senior Systems Engineer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Ziosk</b> is revolutionizing the $472 billion restaurant industry by providing on-the-table payment and guest ordering experiences.</p>
<p>
Today there are over 180,000 Ziosk tablets deployed nationwide at over 3,000 restaurants including Chili’s, Olive Garden, Red Robin, Outback, TGI Friday’s, Yard House, and processing over $12 Billion in payments per year.</p>
<p>
We are expanding our team in the areas of development, sales and customer service to support this growth.</p>
<p><b>
Summary</b></p>
<p>
Ziosk is looking for a customer focused, <b>Senior Systems Engineer</b> to join our growing IT organization. This role will manage and support our internal IT systems and servers deployed in customer environments. A successful candidate must be dependable, self-motivated, possess a can-do attitude, and understand the rigors of working in a high paced environment. Must be highly organized and willing to work with the team to achieve a great outcome for our internal and external customers.</p>
<p><b>
Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Manage and administer all aspects of the all Ziosk corporate, test and PoC Labs, and customer systems and software environments</li>
<li>Architect, design, and deploy solutions to support internal and external customer environments</li>
<li>Troubleshoot complex customer and corporate issues and propose long term preventive measures</li>
<li>Interact with other teams to provide escalation support during outages and customer issues, including driving resolution for issues outside the department</li>
<li>Facilitates cross team problem management for timely resolution</li>
<li>Collaborate with Ziosk Engineering teams for new features, problem management, and new initiatives</li>
<li>Determine operational weaknesses within the existing infrastructure and present execution plans to overcoming those concerns in a timely fashion</li>
<li>Review current technology suite and evaluate solutions and alternatives to either improve efficiencies, management, or cost savings</li>
<li>Evaluate various 3rd party tools and systems to determine the most cost-effective solution</li>
<li>Actively monitor and react to real-time network conditions and make recommendations on improving monitoring and reporting</li>
<li>Manage team project milestones, ticket queue, and report status and delays</li>
<li>Review current processes and propose steps that will improve efficiency</li>
<li>Participate in capacity reviews, metric reporting and budgeting</li>
<li>Documents IT activities such as downtime, problems, standards, and device configurations</li>
<li>Create and update detailed architecture or environment diagrams and knowledge base articles</li>
<li>Guides and mentors other engineers to allow them to expand their capabilities</li>
<li>All other duties as assigned</li>
<li>Must participate in an 24X7X365 on call rotation</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Qualifications</b></p>
<ul>
<li>8+ Years experience working as a server IT / Support engineer</li>
<li>5+ Years working with Windows Server, Hyper V (or VMware), AD and other server-side technologies</li>
<li>3+ Experience administering MS Exchange</li>
<li>Strong knowledge of Windows Server / domain management systems and monitoring tools</li>
<li>Knowledge of Powershell and Windows scripting</li>
<li>Strong general knowledge of networking technologies</li>
<li>Strong troubleshooting skills</li>
<li>Must be a strong problem solver</li>
<li>Excellent verbal and written communication skills</li>
<li>Knowledge of Visio and experience creating architecture diagrams</li>
<li>Must be willing to document work effort on an ongoing basis</li>
<li>Must be willing to learn how to setup Ziosk systems with many different types of POS (Point of Sale) systems</li>
<li>Linux knowledge is not required but is a plus</li>
<li>Knowledge of Docker or other technologies is a plus</li>
<li>Familiarity with Payment Card Industry standards a plus</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Ziosk</b> is an Equal Opportunity employer offering competitive benefits and compensation. Candidates must be eligible to work in the U.S. and be able to commute daily to office. <b>No agencies or third party recruiters, please</b>.</p>
