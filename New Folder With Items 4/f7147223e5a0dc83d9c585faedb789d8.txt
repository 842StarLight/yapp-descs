<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Overview
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div><b>Who We Are:</b></div>
<div>
Guest Worldwide is a leading global manufacturer and distributor to the Travel and Leisure industry, providing products to over 25,000 hotels in 109 countries. We manufacture personal care amenities and a full range of textiles, and, we distribute nearly everything else you find in the hotel public guest areas in the hotel room. Recently achieving a billion dollars in annual revenue, Guest Worldwide has grown its business by over 200% in the last 7 years. Guest Worldwide is also a wholly owned subsidiary of Sysco. Sysco is a 54 billion dollar, industry leading, global food and beverage distribution company, employing thousands of employees worldwide and home to the 13th largest sales organization in the world.</div>
<div></div>

<div>
<b>
The Opportunity:</b><br>
As an Account Manager, this is a unique opportunity to join a growing and dynamic company whose strategic business initiatives have propelled it to the forefront of the Travel &amp; Leisure industry. As a member of the Guest Supply team, you will cultivate relationships within the industry to increase product penetration and contribute to growing Guest Supply’s overall market share. The continued and rapid growth of Guest Supply’s business offers many long term career growth opportunities, across multiple functions, for the right candidates. Those roles may include opportunities globally within sales, specialty/manufacturing sales, luxury sales, marketing, product innovation &amp; design, supply chain, finance, and human resources, in addition to opportunities at Sysco.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
<b>
Are You The One?</b><br>
If you are a person who has demonstrated a strong work ethic, a customer-first mindset, and have experience assisting in strategic planning while also managing the development and tactical implementation of integrated digital marketing programs, we’d love to hear from you. The ideal candidate will also have a proven track record of building internal and external relationships, a commitment to continuous personal capability development, exceptional time management and analytical skills, and the ability to positively adapt to change in a highly dynamic work environment.<br>
If you are highly organized and detail-oriented with an entrepreneurial mindset you can have a career at Guest Supply that challenges your intellect, builds your business acumen, and develops your personal and professional career goals.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div><b>
Position Summary:</b></div>
<div>
This position will primarily nurture and strengthen long-term relationships with current accounts within their assigned book of business. In cooperation with the Account Management team, the Account Manager establishes and maintains strong relationships with property-level purchasing and operations decision makers. The Account Manager acts as the primary point person in addressing all customer issues with respect to inventory, shipping, order entry, billing, etc. in an expedient and courteous manner. Also, this position monitors account attrition risk, implements retention plans, and works to increase spend throughout their account book.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
This position will be based out of the Guest Supply office in Plainfield, Indiana and will occasionally travel when approved by the Director of Key Accounts. Most customer interaction will be through the use of communication mediums such as telephone, email, fax, etc.</div>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<ul>
<li>Manages and maintains strong relationships with property-level amenity, textile, and accessory purchasing decision makers in cooperation with the Corporate Accounts, Key Account Managers, and field sales.
</li>
<li>Acts as the point person in addressing all customer issues with respect to inventory, shipping, order entry, billing, etc. in an expedient and courteous manner.</li>
<li>
Develops and maintains multiple relationships with current clients through strong communication.</li>
<li>
Actively monitors accounts at risk of attrition and reports promptly and accurately.</li>
<li>
Supervises and manages inventory on all custom programs, communicates to clients and internal departments all forecast trends (over/under) to contract specifications.</li>
<li>
Contacts General Managers and Executive Housekeepers regularly to analyzes current business needs and offer solutions within the Guest Supply portfolio.</li>
<li>
Markets Gilchrist &amp; Soames and Manchester Mills collections and proactively converts customers to more profitable product lines. Identifies upsell opportunities for additional OS&amp;E categories.</li>
<li>
Provides superior customer service, maintains customer data, and keeps detailed, and accurate records of all communication with accounts utilizing Salesforce (CRM).</li>
<li>
Proactively monitor customer purchasing trends and pursues opportunities to grow existing book of busines.</li>
<li>
Participates on project teams, communicates cross-functionally with internal departments, and performs other duties as assigned.</li>
</ul>
