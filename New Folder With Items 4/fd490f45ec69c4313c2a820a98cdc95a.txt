<b>
About H-E-B</b><div>
</div>
H-E-B is one of the largest, independently owned food retailers in the nation operating over 400 stores throughout Texas and Mexico, with annual sales generating over $25 billion. Described by industry experts as a daring innovator and smart competitor, H-E-B has led the way with creative new concepts, outstanding service and a commitment to diversity in our workforce, workplace and marketplace. H-E-B offers a wealth of career opportunities to our 109,000+ Partners (employees), competitive compensation and benefits program and comprehensive training that lead to successful careers.<div></div>
<br>
<div>
</div>
<p>
H-E-B Advertising and Marketing Partners work together with cross-functional teams from consumer insights to graphic design to orchestrate marketing campaigns that span print and digital spaces and speak to millions of Texans each day. As Senior Marketing Account Manager, you’ll lead all aspects of the cross-functional marketing and advertising activity for specific H-E-B work groups / accounts.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
Our Partners thrive <b>The H-E-B Way</b>. In the Senior Marketing Account Manager job, that means you have a…</p>
<p><b>
HEART FOR PEOPLE</b>… you relish leading others in pursuit of common goals</p>
<p><b>
HEAD FOR BUSINESS</b>… you can translate data, customer insights, trends and competitive activity into strategic marketing and advertising plans</p>
<div>
<b>
PASSION FOR RESULTS</b>… you have the agility and drive to manage a number of initiatives / initiatives simultaneously</div>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
What you’ll do (Strategy / Leadership / Project Management)</b></p>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>
Develop always-on marketing and advertising plans for merchant organizations and strategic initiatives</li>
<li>
Lead strategic discussions; develop briefs and presentations; assess campaign success; recap results and learnings for senior leaders</li>
<li>
Lead cross-functional teams in data and analytics, targeted / digital marketing, traditional advertising, media, promotions, external agency team management</li>
<li>
Lead development / execution of all aspects of marketing and advertising campaigns for a large business unit or complex project</li>
<li>
Execute tactical implementation of plans for specific projects; work closely with a cross-functional team of designers, media planners, and in-Store merchandising and signage teams</li>
<li>
Understand the client’s business, industry, and competition; serves as a trusted advisor to strategically grow that line of business</li>
<li>
Collaborate / engage coworkers and team members to build powerful and effective campaigns that are on strategy, on time, and on budget</li>
</ul>
