<p><b>Job Description</b></p>
<p><b>Dry Grocery – Senior Food &amp; Beverage Buyer</b></p>
<p><b>Job Title: </b>Dry Grocery – Senior Food &amp; Beverage Buyer</p>
<p><b>Reports To: </b>Division Purchasing Manager - Food &amp; Beverage</p>
<p><b>FLSA Status: </b>Exempt</p>
<p><b>Revised: </b>04/15/2019</p>
<p><b>Summary </b>The Buyer is responsible for purchasing products from assigned vendors to maintain appropriate inventory levels as specified by Service Level Objectives set by the Division Purchasing Manager resulting in maximum customer service levels and increased profitability. Overall responsibility is for the asset management of these vendors as well as providing team leadership for the buying team. The Buyer will ensure the appropriate levels of inventory are available. Other day to day responsibilities include but are not limited to the following:</p>
<p><b>Essential Duties and Responsibilities </b>include the following. Other duties may be assigned.</p>
<ul>
<li>Work with the Sales Team to provide accurate forecasts for each SKU as applicable</li>
<li>Evaluate and provide up-to-date sales trend information and analysis</li>
<li>Monitor sales by SKU to ensure that each SKU is achieving sales rates</li>
<li>Ensure that appropriate levels of inventory are in stock at company warehouse(s)</li>
<li>Regularly review the inventory position of each SKU in replenishment system</li>
<li>Ensure that the appropriate levels of inventory are available for all promotions</li>
<li>Work with Division Purchasing Manager to appropriately manage vendors through line transitions to maximize sales while minimizing the risks of excess and obsolete inventory</li>
<li>Work regularly with vendors to return non-productive inventory</li>
<li>Work with individual sales reps on overstock and obsolete inventory</li>
<li>Responsible for new SKU creation</li>
<li>Create product descriptions following company guidelines</li>
<li>Ensure accurate costs records for each SKU</li>
<li>Ensure Special order SKU’s are sent to correct customer</li>
<li>Responsible to resolve all invoice discrepancies</li>
<li>Analyze, identify, recommend and implement purchasing strategies and inventory management techniques to reduce inventory while maintaining service levels</li>
<li>Gain an understanding of and monitor market conditions for critical commodities used in the business to determine both timely buying opportunities and risk</li>
<li>Assist in other day-today functions and special projects as required</li>
<li>Must possess excellent negotiating skills</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Supervisory Responsibilities </b></p>
<p>Buyer could supervise a purchasing clerk. Will have daily interaction with AP, Customer Service, Credit, Sales Team, Transportation and Warehousing. Ability to build effective relationships internal and external to the organization</p>
