<h2>Qu Implementation Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Duties to include the following:</p>
<ul>
<li>Manage and coordinate the install and launch process of Qu beginning with initial customer greeting through the install and launch of Qu.</li>
<li>Prompt and accurate correspondence with customers and partners via phone, email, web, and in person if necessary</li>
<li>Assist customers with configuration of menus, employees, store settings and other details to ensure their Gusto system is optimized for their specific needs and location</li>
<li>Assist customers with periodic hardware and software updates</li>
<li>Provide “go live” support and training</li>
<li>Assist with demos and troubleshooting and provide clear communication for resolving POS questions</li>
<li>Assist customers with basic networking questions</li>
<li>Overall ensures that customers are satisfied with Qu by taking a proactive approach; manage customer expectations</li>
<li>Add to Qu's industry leading customer experience reputation within the restaurant industry</li>
</ul>
<p>Qualifications:</p>
<ul>
<li>Detail oriented, energetic, and organized with a desire to make a major impact to a rapidly changing industry</li>
<li>Excellent communication skills and ability to build a rapport with customers</li>
<li>Punctual and efficient with flexibility in scheduling if necessary</li>
<li>Ability to assess, understand, and solve customer problems</li>
<li>Ability to work independently under tight deadlines</li>
<li>Willingness to travel, up to 50%, to customer sites for installation and training of Qu.</li>
<li>Must be able to meet physical demands of position including standing, bending, twisting, repetitive motion and lifting between 10-40 lbs. (POS equipment)</li>
<li>Computer knowledge and skills including Microsoft Office, Windows 10 and basic networking</li>
<li>Ability to work full time from our Bethesda, MD head office; must be located within commuting distance</li>
<li>Exudes optimism and wants to join our mission of providing the most reliable technology hub for restaurateurs</li>
</ul>
<p>Preferred but not required:</p>
<ul>
<li>Previous career experience working in restaurants</li>
<li>Knowledge of point of sale system design, installation, configuration, operations, and maintenance.</li>
<li>Experience in the hospitality industry deploying, troubleshooting, using, or maintaining POS systems.</li>
</ul>
<p>Job Type: Full-time</p>
<p>Experience:</p>
<ul><li>relevant: 1 year (Preferred)</li></ul>
