<h2>Application Engineer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>Mitutoyo America Corporation is a leader in the field of precision measuring tools and instruments. Since our beginning in 1963, our employees have felt pride in the Company and its products. We serve an important role in providing state-of-the-art measurement products to manufacturing companies throughout North America. Metrology is the science of measurement. We are the only full-line provider of Metrology solutions in North America that can satisfy every measurement challenge faced by our customers.</p>
<p><b>
Position Summary</b></p>
<p>
Perform functions to promote sales and effective use of Mitutoyo America products by providing instructions, software demonstrations, and recommendations. Provide software support to customers and Mitutoyo America personnel.</p>
<p><b>
Essential Job Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Analyze customer problems and applications for PMI, CMM, Form and/or QV/QS products. Recommend and assist in developing parameters for appropriate software.</li>
<li>Provide instructions to, conduct software demonstrations for, and answer technical questions from customers and other Mitutoyo America personnel.</li>
<li>Assist in correction of software problems.</li>
<li>Conduct or assist distributor personnel with end-user sales calls.</li>
<li>Participate in product knowledge training sessions.</li>
<li>Prepare and submit required reports in a timely manner.</li>
<li>Participate in Mitutoyo America and distributor trade shows.</li>
<li>Keep informed on Mitutoyo America products, effective demonstration methods, and competitor activities. Communicate new developments.</li>
<li>Attend regional meetings and participate in sales training and product knowledge sessions as required.</li>
<li>Update Customer Management Tool (CRM) as directed.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Supervisory Responsibilities</b>: There are no supervisory responsibilities for this position.</p>
<p><b>
Work Environment</b>: Office Environment. Showroom floor.</p>
<p><b>
Physical Demands: </b>The person in this position needs to occasionally move about inside the office to access file cabinets, office machinery, etc. This person will be using various Mitutoyo equipment to create solutions for customers.</p>
