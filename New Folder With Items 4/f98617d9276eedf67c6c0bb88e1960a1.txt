<h2>R&D Chemist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Every day, Kelly Scientific Resources connects scientific professionals with opportunities to advance their careers, and we currently have a direct hire opening for a Research and Development Chemist in Port Huron, MI.</p>
<p>Responsibilities:</p>
<ul>
<li>Identifying any all customer needs that are not being met – use that knowledge to design a product solution that meets those needs</li>
<li>Work with Marketing and Product Management group – explore and document new product opportunities</li>
<li>Develop product design using extensive lab and pilot facilities – work with operations group to translate the design into a commercially viable process</li>
<li>Follows new products into the field, works with Technical Service and Sales</li>
<li>Assigns, oversees and reviews the experimental work performed by team members</li>
<li>Writes reports and memos with executive summaries</li>
<li>Will be managing 2-3 projects at a time with applying hands on benchwork.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p>Requirements:</p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree or higher in a Science or Engineering discipline</li>
<li>3-5 years R&amp;D background</li>
<li>Adhesive industry experience preferred</li>
<li>Must have good project management skills</li>
<li>Innovative, organized self-starter with good problem-solving skills</li>
<li>Collaborative interpersonal style</li>
<li>Knowledge of DOE principles and statistical analysis software</li>
<li>Excellent written and oral communication skills</li>
<li>Demonstrated customer focus</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p>Relocation assistance offered</p>
<p>Position offers lots of internal growth opportunity.</p>
