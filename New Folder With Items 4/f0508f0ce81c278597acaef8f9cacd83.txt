<p><b>Job Summary:
</b></p>Are you a strategic and creative thinker who knows how to bring together traditional and digital media to create amazing returns for your clients? Do you have the ability to prospect and present your ideas to the biggest and most innovative brands and agencies? iHeartMedia is looking for the next Advertising Sales Executive to harness the power of incredible lifestyle events (including Jingle Ball, iHeartRadio Music Festival, etc.), roster of big name talent (From Elvis Duran to the Breakfast Club), iHeartRadio digital and its over 100 million registered users and powerhouse amplification of 850 radio stations to build impactful event marketing campaigns for advertisers and agencies.<br>
<p>
</p>
<p>Responsibilities<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p>• Identifies and solicits new business; builds and maintains a full pipeline of sales prospects.
</p>
<p>• Services and grows relationships in existing base of clients.
</p>
<p>• Identifies client/agency needs and develops persuasive proposals to meet needs and opportunities.
</p>
<p>• Delivers effective sales presentations.
</p>
<p>• Steers clients based on market, platform and station information.
</p>
<p>• Maintains client communication and ensures client satisfaction.
</p>
<p>• Monitors competition to continually find new account leads.
</p>
<p>• Negotiates rates based on iHeartMedia ’ s budgets.
</p>
<p>• Works collaboratively with internal partners to drive revenue.
</p>
<p>• Ensures prompt payments.
</p>
<p>• Follows all station procedures for preparing orders, resolving billing issues, submitting regular reports regarding sales, pipeline lists, forecasts and competitive analysis.
</p>
<p>• Creates effective marketing campaigns in cooperation with iHeartMedia resources.
</p>
<p>• Generates revenue and meets/exceeds established sales targets.
</p>
<p>• Candidate must drive their own vehicle with a valid driver ’ s license and state-mandated auto insurance<br>
