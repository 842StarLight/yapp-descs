<p>Dynamics CRM Project Manager</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
Job Description:
</p>
<p>Project Manager for Dynamics CRM Client Implementations, working in partnership with Menlo Technologies, Senior CRM Engineers you will be responsible for managing the process of:</p>
<p>
Developing strategy and producing project plans (scope, risk, quality, cost, change management, communications, procurement, work breakdown structure and schedule) for our clients</p>
<p>
Working with our client sponsor to develop project charter, goals and priorities</p>
<p>
Facilitating risk assessment/mitigation sessions and follow through risk management with a balanced and business priority driven approach</p>
<p>
Leveraging a solid understanding of the project management life cycle and related principles, methodologies and best practices and improves such processes and execution</p>
<ul>
<li>
Establish Issue Management Process – Define process for identifying and escalating issues throughout the project.</li>
<li>
Establish Change Control Process – Define process for managing and executing change requests throughout project.</li>
<li>
Establish Status Tracking Process – Define process for evaluation of ongoing project progress through meetings, or verbal/written communications.</li>
<li>
Review Requirements and Reconcile Conflicts – Iterative review process whereby requirements may be modified and refined during the engagement.</li>
<li>
Obtain Sign-Off on Requirements – Review and sign-off by customer SMEs and stakeholders.</li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<br>
<p>
Qualifications:</p>
<p>
Minimum of 3+ years’ experience in a Project Management role successfully leading full life-cycle business process and/or Information Technology projects, on time and on budget to client’s satisfaction</p>
<p>
Proven Project Management or Lead Consultant experience implementing Enterprise CRM solutions for customers (strong preference with Dynamics CRM)</p>
<p>
Knowledge of best practices in Project Management with prior experience in delivering solutions utilizing both Agile and SCRUM</p>
<p>
Ability to communicate effectively with Senior Management (including business and IT C-level staff) and clients/vendors</p>
<p>
Bachelor’s Degree in Information Technology, Business Administration, Accounting or related field</p>
<p>
PMP certification desirable</p>
<p>
Balance of business knowledge and people management skills</p>
<p>
Ability to develop project proposals, comprehensive project budgets and plans and to contribute extensively through entire engagement lifecycle from sales to delivery</p>
<p>
Ability to travel as required</p>
