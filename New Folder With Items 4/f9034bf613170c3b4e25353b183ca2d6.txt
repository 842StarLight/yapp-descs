<h2>Sales Executive II</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<b>Job Title:</b> Regional Sales Executive (Sales Executive II)
<br><br>
<b>Position:</b> Full-time
<br><br>
<b>Location:</b> Roseville, CA
<br><br>
Are you a hunter and solution-driven seller who loves the thrill of cultivating new business and delivering results
<br><br>
Begin a New Sales Career at Sterling Talent Solutions
<br><br>
We’re a fast-moving world leader in cloud-based background screening and identity services. Our clients use our services to create safer workplaces, build trusted brands and protect vulnerable populations. We are financially thriving, investing in growth and grounded in authenticity. If you are motivated and passionate, you will love working here. Apply today!
<br><br>
We have great people here and are looking for more. Come join us - you will love it!
<br><br>
As a Regional Sales Executive, you will source, call on, and sell Sterling Talent Solutions to new prospects and clients with an employee range of 1,000-5,000 within your assigned territory. You will work in our regional office with an incredible team of Sales Professionals and receive excellent coaching and support from our Sales Leadership Team.
<br><br>
Every day, our amazing sales team provides solutions to their clients, gaining their trust while building long-term partnerships. When you join us, you'll be part of a high-performing team that truly values your contributions, and in return, will reward you handsomely. If you're a self-starter who can meet the challenge, keep reading!
<br><br>
<b>This Is the Job For You, If:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>You're a relationship builder who connects with people and values friendship and fun.</li>
<li>Competition drives you. You were involved in sports, love a good game of trivia, and you won't be beat at a friendly game of Monopoly.</li>
<li>You're a natural seller. You had the best lemonade stand on the block as a kid and you're consistently a top seller in your current role.</li>
<li>Insatiable appetite to learn. Driven by continuous learning with an insatiable hunger to grow, become, do, share, and give more.</li>
<li>Trusted Adviser. Lives integrity and delivers on promises ... every time.</li>
<li>You'll talk to anyone at a party. You're a natural networker and can make conversation with everyone.</li>
</ul>
<b>This Is What You'll Do:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Work within a defined geographic territory to close sales, win business, and reach sales goals. It's that simple.</li>
<li>Close sales by recommending the right solutions to help our clients understand the real needs of their business.</li>
<li>Earn referral business by connecting with existing and soon-to-be clients, and forging partnerships with key decision makers at the C-level.</li>
</ul>
<b>This Is What We're Looking For:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>5 or more years Business to Business (B2B) sales experience within a results-oriented environment selling to mid-market prospects</li>
<li>1+ years of SaaS Sales and comfortable using and demonstrating technology</li>
<li>Strong customer orientation, dedication, and passion for delivering a great customer experience</li>
<li>Demonstrated cold calling sales ability, with assertive, positive and persistent style</li>
<li>Excellent verbal and written communication skills, including with C-level executives</li>
<li>Motivated, self-starter with effective time management and presentation skills</li>
<li>Ability to work independently, as well as being a collaborative team member</li>
<li>Excellent prospecting, negotiating, demonstration and relationship-building skills</li>
<li>Trained and practiced in the art of consultative, needs-benefits selling</li>
<li>College Degree is strongly preferred</li>
</ul>
