<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities
</b></h2>
<div>
<div>The Document Control System Administrator will assist in the implementation, training and management of the enterprise wide document management system. As part of the Document Management team they will work with users across the companies different business lines to ensure the successful implementation of the Document Management strategy. This position will be responsible for implementing, maintaining and updating the systems technical requirements such as the classification scheme, meta data, folder structures, integrations and other technical system requirements.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
The Document Control System Administrator will assist in the implementation, training and management of the enterprise wide document management system. This includes;</div>
<div><ul>
<li>
Set up folder structures, meta data, integrations and classification sheme for document management program.
</li>
<li>Train and assist users in setting up accounts and getting their documents into document management system</li>
<li>
Respond to user requests and troubleshoot and resolve issues with users and coordinate response and solutions with other team members</li>
<li>
Maintain system accounts to include account creation, account changes, and account deletion, upgrades, and server patching</li>
<li>
Update system to fix defects or to complete minor enhancement requests</li>
<li>
Maintain Box environment and roll out to other business groups or projects as needed</li>
<li>
Perform system upgrades when required</li>
<li>
Ensure performance of changes in software applications or business practices, work with others resolve resulting issues</li>
<li>
Report issues or delays to management and work on solutions and recovery strategies</li>
<li>
Maintain program and site security and confidentiality of data</li>
</ul></div>
</div>
