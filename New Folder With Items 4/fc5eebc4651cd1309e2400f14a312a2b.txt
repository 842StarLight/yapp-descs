<h2>Disney Celebrations & Events Internship - Spring 2020</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<b>Disney Celebrations &amp; Events Internship - Spring 2020</b><br>
<br>
<b>Job Overview:</b><br>
<br>
<b>“We make magic.” </b>That’s our motto at Walt Disney Parks and Resorts. And it permeates everything we do. At Disney, you’ll help inspire that magic by enabling our teams to push the limits of entertainment and create the never-before-seen!<br>
<br>
<b>Department Overview:</b><br>
<br>
<b>We Are…</b><br>
A leader in the Event Industry. We sell, plan, and execute weddings as well as custom events at The Walt Disney World Resort.<br>
<br>
<b>Responsibilities :</b><br>
<b>Be a part of a transformative team and culture…</b><br>
<br>
<b>By applying to this posting you will be considered for an Internship in one of the roles listed below. There may be opportunities to assist on various projects under each role heading regardless as to which role you are primarily placed. In this role, the individual will be involved in a wide variety of Event Planning and Sales activities – including, but not limited to the following:</b><br>
<br>
<b>Event Services:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Assisting the Event Managers and Coordinators through all phases of planning from pre-event to actual execution</li>
<li>
Create and update room diagrams, floral proposals, and Banquet Event Orders</li>
<li>
Partnering closely with operational areas including Catering, Culinary, Audio/Visual, Floral, Entertainment, and Transportation to ensure that guest expectations for set-up, service and flow of catered functions are delivered in an exceptional manner</li>
<li>
Supporting Event Managers by serving as a liaison with wedding and custom events guests</li>
<li>
Directly support Event Services Assistants with administrative processes and phone coverage</li>
<li>
Execute and assist with projects assigned by the Event Managers</li>
<li>
Utilizing existing Disney software systems</li>
<li>
Miscellaneous Office and Technical support.</li>
</ul>
<br>
<br>
<br>
<b>Wedding Sales:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Following up on and qualifying Wedding sales opportunities</li>
<li>
Providing Guests with follow-up Wedding information</li>
<li>
Scheduling sales phone appointments and site visits</li>
<li>
Utilizing existing Disney software systems</li>
<li>
Miscellaneous office and technical support with emphasis on reporting and data compilation</li>
<li>
Conducting follow-up site visits</li>
<li>
Preparing contracts and event concepts</li>
<li>
Projects assigned by Wedding Consultants.</li>
</ul>
<br>
<br>
<br>
<b>Franck’s Studio (Sales and Planning Center located at the Wedding Pavilion)</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Representing the brand “Disney’s Fairy Tale Weddings &amp; Honeymoons” by greeting and welcoming our guests</li>
<li>
Maintaining Franck’s to be show-ready during operating business hours</li>
<li>
Partnering closely with Operational areas including the custodial. security, horticultural, engineering, bakery, and other show-keeping teams</li>
<li>
Creating magical experiences for our Guests during their visit to Franck’s Studio</li>
<li>
Organization of couple’s wedding items</li>
<li>
Company vehicle upkeep</li>
<li>
Assisting Sales Consultants and Event Services Managers as needed.</li>
</ul>
<br>

Internship Eligibility:</b><ul>

<li>

<li>

<li>
Must provide full work availability</li>
<li>
Must provide own transportation to/from work</li>
<li>
Current Active Disney cast members must meet Professional Internship transfer guidelines (for Walt Disney World cast members this is no more than four points and one reprimand in the last six months; for Disneyland cast members this is six months of consecutive service and a performance record clear of any disciplinary issues (warnings, suspensions, etc.) for at least six months)</li>
</ul>
