<h2>Event and Meeting Coordinator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<b>OVERVIEW</b><p>
At Sentry, our business is built around people. We pride ourselves on the promise to deliver efficient and effective services to our customers. We have an opportunity for an Event &amp; Meeting Coordinator to support coordination activities related to planned company meetings and events. In this role, you will be a key member in assisting with event planning and execution, along with coordination of promotional products/gifts and assistance with event registrations.
</p>
<b>WHAT YOU'LL DO</b><ul>
<li>Provide clear, concise and timely communication of detailed requirements to operational departments.</li>
<li>Generate ideas and order process for promotional products/gifts within given budget and timeframe.</li>
<li>Provide onsite support of meetings and events as needed; may require occasional travel</li>
<li>Assist with meeting and event-related research to include: event activities, transportation, restaurant and private dining options.</li>
<li>Submit ePro requisitions and eVouchers as requested for procurement and payment.</li>
<li>Ensures all charges, deposits and sales of the event are properly recorded and collected on a timely basis.</li>
</ul>
<br>
<b>
WHAT IT TAKES</b><ul>
<li>Bachelors Degree or equivalent work experience</li>
<li>Related work experience.</li>
<li>Experience in event management and travel industry preferred.</li>
<li>Excellent organizational, planning, interpersonal and problem-solving skills.</li>
<li>Strong communication and organizational skills</li>
<li>Excellent customer service and leadership skills</li>
<li>Ability to work effectively under time constraints and deadlines</li>
<li>Proficient in the use of personal computers, automated systems, and software applications.</li>
</ul>
