<h2>Operations Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p>We are looking for an experienced Parlor Operations Manager to oversee the operational and financial responsibility of a Doc Burnstein’s Ice Cream Lab parlor in <b>Sacramento, Ca</b>. The Parlor Operations Manager is responsible for ensuring that the parlor operates efficiently and profitably while maintaining the company’s mission and quality of service.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>This is a full time, exempt position that requires frequent weekend and holiday work to align with the nature of retail operations. This position reports to the <b>Sacramento Regional Director</b>. Duties will include retail &amp; parlor sales, parties, budgeting, merchandise sales, hiring and training parlor employees.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Take ownership for the business performance of the parlor, and for providing a consistent Guest Experience</li>
<li>Upholding the Values and Principles of Doc Burnstein’s Ice Cream Lab in daily interactions with Staff, Guest and the Community. Maintain company’s high standards of quality control, hygiene, and health and safety.</li>
<li>Maintain community outreach through hosting of fundraisers and managing charitable donations to strengthen the community connection.</li>
<li>Prepare reports at the end of the shift/week, including staffing, food inventory and sales.</li>
<li>Hiring, training, appraising and development of employees</li>
<li>Train all employees and ensure adherence adherence to the company’s work policies and standards with direction from the Human Resources Manager.</li>
<li>Maintaining statistical and financial records</li>
<li>Participation in strategic level discussions about business direction, new business development and marketing initiatives</li>
<li>Creation of operational procedures</li>
<li>Dealing with customer queries and complaints</li>
<li>Overseeing pricing and stock control</li>
<li>Maximizing profitability and setting/meeting sales targets, including motivating staff to do so.</li>
<li>Ensuring compliance with health and safety, CalOSHA standards</li>
<li>Conduct any other activities required by Doc Burnstein’s Corporate Management as deemed necessary to meet all personal and company goals.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Required Education and Experience</b>
</p>
<p>College degree or equivalent experience.</p>
<p>Knowledgeable in essential food service standards, equipment, and processes.</p>
<p>Have a high level of accuracy and attention to details.</p>
<p>Fully proficient in POS and scheduling software.</p>
<p>Have a high level of computer skills on PC and MAC</p>
<p>Collaborative leader and manager.</p>
<p>Experience in a retail environment preferred.</p>
<p>Knowledge of performance evaluation metrics and principles.</p>
<p>Sound understanding of optimization of store operations and standards for success.</p>
<p>Knowledgeable in essential food service standards, equipment, and processes.</p>
<p>Qualifications &amp; Personal Attributes</p>
<p>Kind-hearted, dynamic personality with excellent problem-solving skills.</p>
<p>Communication proficiency, oral and written.</p>
<p>Active role in community and business organizations.</p>
<p>Computer Skills to include Word, Excel, Other Programs,</p>
<p>Honest, trustworthy, reliable, respectful.</p>
<p>A cultural awareness and sensitivity.</p>
<p>Organization and time management skills.</p>
<p>Demonstrated initiative &amp; leadership competence.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Supervisory Responsibility</b></p>
<p>This position is responsible for managing the Sacramento Parlor employees assigned to their parlor.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Work Environment</b></p>
<p>This job operates in a retail food setting. This role routinely uses standard office equipment such as computers, phones, photocopiers, filing cabinets and fax machines. This job also requires travel to various indoor and outdoor locations including community centers, restaurants, office space, ice cream parlors, and others.</p>
<p>While performing the duties of this job, the employee is frequently exposed extreme temperatures, moving mechanical parts and vibration. The noise level in the work environment and job sites can be loud.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Physical Demands</b>
</p>
<p>While performing the duties of this job, the employee is regularly required to talk or hear. This is largely a sedentary role; however, some filing is required. This would require the ability to lift files, open filing cabinets and bend or stand as necessary. The employee is occasionally required to sit; climb or balance; and stoop, kneel, crouch or crawl. The employee must frequently lift and/or move up to 10 pounds and occasionally lift and/or move up to 25 pounds.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Position Type</b></p>
<p>This is a full-time position. Evening and weekend work may be required as job duties demand. Due to the nature of the job, the employee must be available on-call early/late hours to deal emergencies in the business.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Travel</b></p>
<p>This position is based in the <b>Sacramento Area in California</b>, with regular local travel in the region.</p>
<p>Additionally, training and onboarding for this position requires significant travel to the Central Coast region.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Other Duties</b></p>
<p>Please note this job description is not designed to cover or contain a comprehensive listing of activities, duties or responsibilities that are required of the employee for this job. Duties, responsibilities and activities may change at any time with or without notice.</p>
