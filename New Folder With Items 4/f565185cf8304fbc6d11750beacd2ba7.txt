<h2>Business Development Representative</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>QAD is looking for insightful, sales driven and skilled<b> Business Development Representatives </b>looking to advance their career with a Next Generation ERP company that is Rapid, Agile, and Effective. The function of this role is to conduct research, prospect and engage appropriate C-suite contacts in assigned<b> Large Enterprise Automotive Accounts</b> with <b>Commercial Insights</b> to uncover new business opportunities. This position requires a true hunter mentality, the ability to qualify prospects, add value qualify and partner with stakeholders to uncover new business opportunities that align with our Automotive ERP and supply chain solutions.<br>
<br>
 Working in a collaborative environment with field sales and marketing, the BDR is responsible for generating fully qualified leads that will contribute to the growth of the sales pipeline and revenue, and help ensure QAD’s success in the marketplace. A successful BDR will engage in social selling, emailing and telephone-based prospecting, identify and qualify quality leads with senior level decision makers that the sales teams will develop into sales opportunities.<br>
<br>
<b>What You’ll Do:</b>
</div>
<ul>
<li>Prospect into large enterprise net new target accounts</li>
<li>Research potential vertical targets and key contacts within targeted accounts</li>
<li>Identify prospects’ challenge areas and determine decision makers and contacts</li>
<li>Evangelize QAD’s value proposition</li>
<li>Hand off fully qualified opportunities to Sales</li>
<li>Achieve monthly, quarterly and yearly qualified opportunity goals</li>
</ul>
<div>
<br>
<b>What You’ll Need</b>
</div>
<ul>
<li>Original/creative thinking and superior problem-solving abilities</li>
<li>Bachelor’s degree preferred in Sales, Marketing, Social Science or related field</li>
<li>Previous business development and prospecting experience in the B2B technology industry and/or the automotive industry is highly desirable. Preferably calling on technology decision makers of large enterprises.</li>
<li>Expertise in navigating all social media sites, i.e. LinkedIn, twitter.</li>
<li>Exceptional phone, written, organizational and interpersonal skills</li>
<li>Self-motivation and ability to work with minimal supervision</li>
</ul>
<div><b>Who You Are:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Insatiably curious, coachable and committed to lifelong learning.</li>
<li>A growth-oriented professional who is eager to join a fast-paced and entrepreneurial team.</li>
<li>Have a strong will to achieve - willing to push harder, have a competitive spirit, and willing to be challenged.</li>
<li>A high achiever, marked by perseverance, humility and a positive outlook in the face of challenges.</li>
<li>Generous with your time and genuinely interested in helping other team members succeed in their roles.</li>
<li>Enjoy working in a collaboratively competitive environment. Make others around you better while achieving your own goals</li>
<li>Demonstrates QAD core values - partnership with our customers, commitment to excellence, innovation, global teamwork, integrity and respect for the individual</li>
</ul>
