<h2>Guest Service Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>We are hiring for an enthusiastic Guest Service Manager who is passionate about providing supervision and direction to the front-end team while maintaining high standards of guest service throughout the front-end operations. A typical shift will include bookkeeping tasks and assisting the Sr. Guest Services Manager with Guest Services, self-check-outs, regular checkouts, bagging area, parcel pickup, cart room, click and collect, and parking lot areas.</p>
<p>A day in the life of the Guest Service Manager may include any combination of the below duties (and more!):</p>
<ul>
<li>Assist the Senior Guest Service Manager in supervising the front-end team to accomplish human resource objectives by selecting, training, assigning, scheduling, coaching, counseling and disciplining employees, communicating job expectations and appraising job contributions.</li>
<li>Ensure that the duties, responsibilities, and authority of each direct report are clearly defined and communicated.</li>
<li>Maintain a “Say Yes to the Guest” attitude and develops employees to do the same.</li>
<li>Responsible for ordering supplies, maintaining point of sale system/machines, balancing tills, safety and cleaning of front end area.</li>
<li>Ensure the front end meets goals including, but not limited to goals related to labor, RFG, MORE Rewards, CTO, key staff coverage, etc.</li>
<li>Responsible for daily/weekly bookkeeping activities.</li>
<li>Provides backup services to employees in all areas of responsibility including Guest Services, Cashiering, bagging, and pushing carts.</li>
<li>Provides backup to the Senior Guest Service Manager in his/her absence.</li>
</ul>
<p>The ideal candidate for the Guest Services Manager position:</p>
<ul>
<li>is a people person with excellent leadership and relationship building skills;</li>
<li>is a self-motivated and enthusiastic guest service “rock star” with excellent communication skills;</li>
<li>is able to provide and facilitate training to and development conversations with employees;</li>
<li>has the ability to efficiently and accurately operate multiple POS systems and accurately handle all forms of payment transactions; and</li>
<li>has a working knowledge of the Microsoft Office Suite and email systems.</li>
</ul>
<p>Must have a one year certificate from a college or technical school or three to six months related experience and/or training or equivalent combination of education and experience.</p>
<p>Prefer previous experience with point of sale systems, including troubleshooting; a history of working with scheduling software and experience working with various guest service machines such as Western Union, Money Orders, fax machine, etc.</p>
<p>Must be able to work one evening per week, rotating holidays, and every other weekend. All other shifts will be based off guest/location needs.</p>
