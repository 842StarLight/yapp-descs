<h2>Senior Precision Agriculture Lead (Req 75661)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">The Senior Precision Agriculture Lead develops, maintains and implements Precision Farming Programs in an assigned territory through any applicable Southern States business operation.
<br><br>
<b>Responsibilities include:</b><br>
Front line support for Southern States member cooperatives and COR precision ag coordinators
<br>Trains and supports member co-op and company-owned field staff on implementation of the Precision Farming Programs.
<br>Define the needs for sales and training materials for implementation of products, such as Quick Guides, Information Sheets, Communication Sheets, etc. for software and equipment products.
<br>Identify new business opportunities for products and services available in Southern States network and communicate to the Precision Ag group in Richmond for evaluation.
<br>Front line support for sales and management of Southern States Coop imagery program. Including ground, aerial (plane or UAV) and satellite.
<br>Work with Precision Ag Coordinators and others to develop and fine-tune agronomic recommendation equations for Seed, Crop Nutrients and Crop Protection products to make proprietary equations that are marketable.
<br>Develop and implement new and enhanced Precision Ag Programs to producers and growers.
<br>Works with service locations, managed coops and private dealers to perform periodic "market research" of technology practices and needs within the designated territory.
<br>Monitors performance of precision agronomy sales, operations, and goals for assigned region.
<br><b>.mceHideBrInPre PRE BR { DISPLAY:</b> none }
<br><br>
<b>Qualifications include:</b><br>
College degree, bachelors or associates, in Crops Science, Business Management or applicable curriculum. (Associates degree would require more experience for consideration)
<br>Minimum of five years of experience in the Ag Technology industry
<br>CCA preferred, but not required
<br>High level of communication abilities, written and verbal.
<br>Proficient in MS Office, especially Excel and PowerPoint
<br>High level of self-motivation to see opportunities and ability to motivate others to action.
<br>High level of ability to work in cross-functional groups.
<br>High level of innovation and problem solving
<br>Travel approximately 50%
<br>Provide after-hours and weekend support as needed.
