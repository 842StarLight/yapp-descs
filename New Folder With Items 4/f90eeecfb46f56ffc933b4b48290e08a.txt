Reporting to the Assistant Controller, the Senior Accountant will assist with the general accounting activities of Demandbase, and play a critical role in the accounting/finance department. You will have the opportunity to interpret and apply emerging accounting principles to a variety of issues and transactions.
<br><br>
<b>What you'll be doing:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>General Ledger - assist with month-end close process, including preparation of journal entries and month-end bank/GL account reconciliations</li>
<li>Consolidations - ability to prepare consolidating trial balances, inter-company eliminations and foreign currency adjustments</li>
<li>Sales/use tax administration – manage Avalara returns</li>
<li>Stock compensation administration – Assist with record keeping of Demandbase's stock option plans, and quantifying compensation expense</li>
<li>Support external auditors and tax accountants</li>
<li>Other duties as assigned</li>
</ul>
<b>What we're looking for:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>3-5 years accounting experience at a SaaS company</li>
<li>Understand the concept of GAAP versus non-GAAP reporting &amp; reconciliation</li>
<li>Data analysis/management/transformation capabilities</li>
<li>BS/BA degree in accounting or related field</li>
<li>Intaact and/or Avalara experience are a plus</li>
<li>Professional certification (CPA) is a plus</li>
<li>SQL/report-writing skills would be ideal</li>
</ul>
