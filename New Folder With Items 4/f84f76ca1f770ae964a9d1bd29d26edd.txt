<h2>Mgr 1, SMB Direct Sales (Outside Sales)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Comcast Business offers technology solutions ranging from Ethernet, internet, and WiFi connectivity to voice, television, and managed Enterprise solutions to power businesses of all sizes to perform better. From small businesses to mid-market and large Enterprise organizations, Comcast Business serves business customers across the country. Powered by an advanced, Gig-speed network and backed by 24/7 technical support, Comcast Business is one of the largest contributors to the growth of Comcast Cable. The organization is the nation's largest cable provider to small and mid-size businesses and has emerged as a force in the Enterprise market, recognized by leading industry associations as one of the fastest growing provider of Ethernet services.</p>

<b>Job Summary:</b><br>
Responsible for effectively managing and monitoring the sales of an<br>
integrated communication structure to small-to-medium business customers<br>
in an effort to maximize sales revenues and meet corporate objectives<br>
on a consistent basis. Assures optimal sales team staffing and training<br>
readiness of sales professionals.<br>
<br>
Employees at all levels are expect to:<br>
<br>
<ul><li>Understand our Operating Principles; make them the guidelines for how</li></ul>
you do your job<br>
<ul><li>Own the customer experience-think and act in ways that put our</li></ul>
customers first, give them seamless digital options at every touchpoint,<br>
and make them promoters of our products and services<br>
<ul><li>Know your stuff-be enthusiastic learners, users and advocates of our</li></ul>
game-changing technology, products and services, especially our digital<br>
tools and experiences<br>
<ul><li>Win as a team-make big things happen by working together and being</li></ul>
open to new ideas<br>
<ul><li>Be an active part of the Net Promoter System-a way of working that</li></ul>
brings more employee and customer feedback into the company-by joining<br>
huddles, making call backs and helping us elevate opportunities to do<br>
better for our customers<br>
<ul>
<li>Drive results and growth</li>
<li>Respect and promote inclusion and diversity</li>
<li>Do what's right for each other, our customers, investors and our</li>
</ul>
communities</div>
<div>
<b>
Core Responsibilities:</b><br>
<ul><li>Maintains expertise on Company's products/services to effectively</li></ul>
manage team sales of Comcast Ethernet, Internet, Voice, and TV services<br>
to small-to-medium business customers.<br>
<ul><li>Ensures competence and continuity of qualified small-to-medium</li></ul>
business Account Executives through optimum selection, training and<br>
development, appraisal, and motivation techniques.<br>
<ul><li>Develops, plans, and coordinates sales promotions and incentives to</li></ul>
meet business goals and objectives. Ensures team and individual rep<br>
achievement of all sales, plus quality, goals, and standards.<br>
<ul><li>Monitors employee performance, and counsels and advises to ensure</li></ul>
compatibility, maximum effectiveness, and continued growth on a constant<br>
basis. Addresses personnel issues/performance issues in accordance with<br>
Company policy.<br>
<ul><li>Prepares, analyzes, and maintains records of individual and group</li></ul>
sales and performance activities relative to business goals and<br>
objectives. Ensures accurate forecasts of annual, quarterly, and monthly<br>
revenue and unit numbers through experience with processing and<br>
analyzing of data.<br>
<ul><li>Designs, implements, and manages overall territory team structure.</li></ul>
Coordinates efforts with other internal teams and groups to ensure<br>
effectiveness and efficiency. Possesses excellent written and oral<br>
communications, interpersonal skills, and planning and organizational<br>
skills.<br>
<ul><li>Develops and implements best practices that contribute to improved</li></ul>
performance and overall success through leading by example and modeling<br>
the Comcast Credo, Touchstones, and Promise.<br>
<ul><li>Coaches, develops, appraises, and motivates individual sales</li></ul>
representatives to achieve and exceed assigned objectives. Educates<br>
sales professionals in sales planning tactics to support their small to<br>
medium business success by assuring compliance with organizational<br>
training requirements.<br>
<ul><li>Consistent exercise of independent judgment and discretion in matters</li></ul>
of significance.<br>
<ul><li>Regular, consistent and punctual attendance. Must be able to work</li></ul>
nights and weekends, variable schedule(s) as necessary.<br>
<ul><li>Other duties and responsibilities as assigned.</li></ul>
