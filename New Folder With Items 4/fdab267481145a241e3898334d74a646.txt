<h2>Field Marketing Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div><div>
<p><b>The Opportunity:</b></p>
<p>We’re looking for a Field Marketing Manager to help us shape the future of education. With nearly 20,000 organizational customers and millions of student users, Blackboard is the world's leading education technology company with the industry's most comprehensive student-success solutions. Our mission is to partner with the global education community to enable student and institutional success by leveraging innovative technologies and services.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>As Field Marketing Manager, you will report to the Senior Director of North America Field Marketing and play a pivotal role in developing, managing, and executing the marketing programs that focus on creating awareness, demand, engagement, and renewal related to Blackboard’s Teaching and Learning products and services. You will work closely with a multi-disciplinary team of sales, client success, solution engineers, product marketing, and others to deliver programs that drive pipeline towards sales and renewals across our existing and prospective clients.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p>Specific responsibilities will include:</p>
<ul>
<li>Supporting the design and development of the annual field marketing plan for North America Teaching &amp; Learning, partnering with Product Marketing, Sales, Central Marketing, PR, and other key stakeholders</li>
<li>
In partnership with an additional Field Marketing Manager, executing the marketing plan, monitoring results and reporting on marketing performance</li>
<li>
Creating and organizing marketing content for programs, leadership pieces from initial copy development to asset and campaign production for strategic campaigns in the region</li>
<li>
Delivering and managing multi-channel campaigns leveraging a range marketing mix tactics including PR, social media, events, online marketing, direct marketing, and other field programs
</li>
<li>Becoming fluent in the marketplace dynamics, client segment needs, roles, and personas and developing and helping to execute scalable marketing activities based on the client-centric and segmented approach</li>
<li>
Generating reports and analysis to support marketing key performance indicators such as lead generation, lead follow-up, and lead conversion</li>
<li>
Defining and reporting on key program metrics to stakeholders and executive leadership</li>
<li>
Managing and developing the creation of effective sales tools and assets including sales training materials</li>
<li>
Coordinating with numerous groups including Sales, Customer Success, Communications &amp; PR, Product Marketing, and Product Management</li>
</ul>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>The Candidate:</b></p>
<p><i>Required skills:</i></p>
<ul>
<li>5+ years of prior relevant marketing experience</li>
<li>
Ability to drive demand for enterprise software, leveraging a variety of techniques including content marketing, drip campaigns, digital/web, etc.</li>
<li>
Proficient with developing integrated, multi-touch marketing plans</li>
<li>
Proficient in HTML email, marketing automation platforms (e.g. Eloqua) and use of CRM tools like Salesforce</li>
<li>
A deep understanding of customer segmentation and experience using data and analytics to inform and influence decision-making</li>
<li>
Outstanding writing, presentation, story-telling, and organizational skills</li>
<li>
Outstanding project management and coordination skills and ability to track multiple simultaneous projects and deliverables</li>
<li>
Ability to excel in a fast-paced, cross-functional environment</li>
<li>
Self-motivated, innovative, collaborative, multi-tasker, creative, and analytical</li>
<li>
Travel: approximately 30% across the US and Canada</li>
</ul>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><i>Preferred skills:</i></p>
<ul>
<li>University degree in Marketing or another business-related field</li>
<li>
Prior experience at a technology company and/or in the education industry</li>
</ul>
