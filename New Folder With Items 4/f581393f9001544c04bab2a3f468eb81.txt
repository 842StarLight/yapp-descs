<h2>Account Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<div>
<p>We are looking for an Account Manager to create long-term, trusting relationships with our customers. The Account Manager’s role is to oversee a portfolio of assigned customers, lead projects from implementation through go-live and the entirety of the client relationship, provide timely and accurate problem-solving responses, and be proactive in identifying and providing new opportunities back to each existing customer.</p>
<p><b>
Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Serve as the singular point of contact for all customer matters</li>
<li>Responsible for managing several accounts and being the face of the company to all clients</li>
<li>Build and maintain strong, long-lasting client relationships</li>
<li>Manage multiple projects at a time during varying stages of implementation and maturity</li>
<li>Ensure the timely and successful delivery of data and reports to fulfill customer needs and objectives</li>
<li>Provide In-person/web software training</li>
<li>Clearly communicate the progress of client’s monthly performance and status of project implementations</li>
<li>Forecast and track key account metrics (e.g. monthly volume)</li>
<li>Prepare reports on account status</li>
<li>Perform analytics in order to identify and provide opportunities for each client</li>
<li>Assist with challenging client requests or issue escalations as needed</li>
<li>Keeping accurate records pertaining to history of service</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Required Skills</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Proven work experience as an Account Manager, Sales Account Manager, Supervisor, Junior Account Manager or relevant role</li>
<li>Proficient in MS Office products: Outlook, Word, Excel, and PowerPoint.</li>
<li>Exceptional verbal and written communication skills.</li>
<li>Experience delivering client-focused solutions for customer needs</li>
<li>Proven ability to manage multiple projects at a time, while maintaining close attention to detail</li>
<li>Excellent listening and presentation abilities</li>
<li>Ability to collect, track, and analyze large amounts of data.</li>
<li>Adaptability and strong problem-solving skills.</li>
</ul>
