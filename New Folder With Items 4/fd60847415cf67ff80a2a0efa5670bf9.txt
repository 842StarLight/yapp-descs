<h2>Information Security, Risk & Compliance Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>The Risk and Compliance Manager of Information Security will work collaboratively across the organization to plan, direct and coordinate security compliance initiatives to ensure compliance with regulatory, legal and contractual obligations. The Risk and Compliance Manager will align the security policy and standards with business needs and will clearly communicate those needs into requirements actionable by the organization. The Risk and Compliance Manager will also identify and report on the compliance, the effectiveness of the security controls and will support security posture improvement efforts of the organization.</p>
<div>
ESSENTIAL FUNCTIONS<br>
<ul>
<li>Partner closely with engineering, product management, legal, network and other stakeholders to build flexible and robust security programs that continuously evaluate, track, and manage privacy and security risks</li>
<li>Lead Cross-functional efforts to support the growth of LogistiCare's infrastructure in a manner that maintains data protection regulatory compliance</li>
<li>Develop standards, processes, and documentation to improve privacy and security practices</li>
<li>Establish metrics and regular reporting mechanisms for assessing and measuring security, privacy, compliance risk across the organization</li>
<li>Mature the vendor risk management program</li>
<li>Develop and deliver privacy and security training</li>
<li>Develop a cadence for ongoing communication of planning, project status, issues and risks in a timely fashion to stakeholders.</li>
<li>Support the sales and integration team in responding to customer requests regarding the security controls of our products and infrastructure</li>
<li>Take initiative to ensure the organization is successful in its Information Security objectives</li>
<li>Follow through on all tasks with little to no guidance or supervision</li>
</ul>
</div>
