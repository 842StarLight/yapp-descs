<div>
<p><b>Inside Sales Associate</b></p>
<p><b>
ALT Sales Corp.</b></p>
<p><b>
Richfield, OH (44286)</b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Position Summary</b></p>
<p>
ALT Sales Corp. has an entry-level opportunity for an Inside Sales Associate – taking photos, listing, and selling our products. Prefer local candidates or those willing to relocate. This is a full-time, non-exempt position.</p>
<p><b>
Essential Functions</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Marketing and sales of our products.</li>
<li>Coordinating sales efforts by studying existing and potential volume of customers.</li>
<li>Communicating with management by submitting activity and results reports on a regular basis.</li>
<li>Analyzing current marketplace information on pricing, available products, marketing and merchandising techniques, etc.</li>
<li>Submitting recommendations on changes in products, services, policies, etc., based on evaluation of current competitive developments.</li>
<li>Providing great Customer Service by investigating customer complaints, developing solutions and making recommendations to management.</li>
<li>Maintaining records on area and customer sales; providing historical reports. The successful candidate would be required to maintain professional and technical knowledge by attending technical workshops, reviewing professional and industry-related publications, establishing personal networks, etc.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Skills and Experience Requirements</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Customer Service oriented; good communication skills; closing skills, territory management; prospecting, presentation and negotiation skills; self-confidence and motivation; product knowledge and ability to maintain client relationships.</li>
<li>Construction Equipment Rental/Sales experience is bonus but not required.</li>
<li>Intermediate computer and phone technology skills required.</li>
<li>Ability to travel up to 10%.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>ALL Crane Family of Companies</b></p>
<p>
The ALL Family of Companies and its affiliated branches are Equal Opportunity Employers with competitive wage and benefits packages in a drug-free environment. The ALL Family of Companies is the largest privately-owned crane rental and sales enterprise in North America, with 38+ strategically located branches operating under the ALL, Central, Dawes, and Jeffers names – including specialized divisions for aerial lifts, boom trucks (ALT Sales), and parts. Since 1964, the ALL Family has been a leader in the lift industry – with market strengths that include commercial construction, roads &amp; bridges, power generation, plants &amp; processing, facility maintenance, and more. We're ALL you need ®</p>
<p>
The ALL Family of Companies is an Equal Opportunity Employer – M/F/Veteran/Disability/Sexual Orientation/Gender Identity.</p>
