Position Overview<br>
In this dynamic role, you will establish and lead the marketing and business development to realize booking quota for the Gas and Power, Services Power Transmission group focusing on Transmission Product equipment. You will also strategically drive the business development and the proposal process, will develop a pipeline that will include defined strategies to retain/grow existing, and acquire new accounts to achieve greater market share.</div>
<div>
This is an outstanding opportunity to join a strong team selling the best Transmission solutions and services in the market. Siemens is leading the charge in providing the following innovative solutions to the Transmission Market:<br>
Flexible AC Transmission Systems (FACTS)<br>
High Voltage Substations – GIS, AIS, Mobile Solutions and Transformers<br>
High Voltage Direct Current (HVDC)<br>
Offshore Substations</div>
