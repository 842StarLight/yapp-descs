<h2>Assistant Lead</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<div><div>
<p><b>(S):</b>
</p>
<p>Responsible for all duties related to the preparation and serving of student meals and snacks<br>
(daily). Adheres to the National Breakfast, Lunch, and Snack Programs guidelines and<br>
requirements in accordance with Federal, State, and Local regulations. Provides excellent<br>
customer service to all. May be required to leave the main work site on occasion for meetings,<br>
workshops, and trainings, etc.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>JOB DUTIES / ACCOUNTABILITIES:</b>
</p>
<p>1. Assists Lead with the distribution of monthly menus to students.<br>
2. Assists Lead with the weekly projections and ordering of meals, snacks, and supplies from
</p>
<p>the Commissary.<br>
3. Shares responsibility for proper receiving and storage of products upon delivery.<br>
4. Assists Lead with maintaining accurate production and inventory data. Reports on a daily,
</p>
<p>weekly, and monthly basis.<br>
5. Assists Lead with preparing and distributing meals and snacks following local, state, and
</p>
<p>federal guidelines.<br>
6. Assists Lead with the completion of daily bookkeeping tasks, including sales reports, class
</p>
<p>lists, student/staff meal payments, and reconciling totals for accuracy.<br>
7. Assists Lead with banking monies collected.<br>
8. Assists Lead with performing and documenting sanitation checks/verifications as required
</p>
<p>by law.<br>
9. Shares the responsibility for marketing the Breakfast and Lunch programs as directed by
</p>
<p>the Manager of Elementary Schools.<br>
10. Assists Lead with the formation of a Youth Advisory Council (YAC).<br>
11. Exhibits knowledge relating to all aspects of the Leadâ€™s responsibilities to provide
</p>
<p>coverage during absences.<br>
12. Other duties as assigned.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>EXPERIENCE / KNOWLEDGE:</b>
</p>
<p>1. Minimum of two (2) years experience in a position of responsibility in the Food Service<br>
industry and Customer Service (preferred)
</p>
<p>2. Computer proficiency required. Knowledge of district and department software programs<br>
preferred.
</p>
<p>3. Accuracy in handling money (cashiering, banking, etc.).

<b>EDUCATION:</b>
</p>
<p>1. High School Diploma or equivalent (required).<br>

3. State of Illinois Sanitation Certificate required within six months of hire.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>PHYSICAL DEMANDS:</b>
</p>
<p>1. Ability to work indoors in a busy kitchen environment with exposure to noise, heat, cleaning<br>

</p>
<p>2. Ability to stand for long periods of time.<br>
3. Ability to assist in lifting up to 50 lbs. with proper technique.<br>
4. Wears appropriate uniform and shoes as provided.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>TERMS OF EMPLOYMENT:</b>
</p>
<p>This is a 4 hour, 10-month position. Hours vary due to specific building breakfast and lunch<br>
schedule. Salary and benefits are as established by SEIU collective bargaining agreement.</p>
