<h2>Business Development Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Business Development-Specialty Gases </b></p>
<p><b>JOB SUMMARY: </b></p>
<p>The Specialty Gas Business Development role is responsible for developing new business within assigned territory. This position will develop and implement strategic marketing plans within assigned territory. In addition, planning and implementing micro-bulk and specialty gas sales training to Inside Sales Reps and Account Managers. Responsibilities will include acting as primary Airgas contact for specialty gas portfolio within assigned area.</p>
<p><b>ESSENTIAL FUNCTIONS: </b></p>
<p> Develops and executes sales campaigns for new business development within assigned territory.</p>
<p> Responsible for the initiation, coordination, and fulfillment of the complete, specialty gas sales cycle including sales calls, needs analysis, preparing sales proposals, order entry, product sourcing, product delivery and, if applicable, complaint resolution.</p>
<p> Prepares sales proposal by quoting pricing, credit terms, estimated date of delivery to customer based on knowledge of company’s production and delivery schedules using software program set up by company.</p>
<p> Develops long-term supply relationships through contracts and/or agreements.</p>
<p> Identifies, monitors, and documents competitive market activity by gathering current marketplace information on pricing, products, new products, delivery schedules, merchandising techniques, etc.</p>
<p> Represents company at trade association meetings to promote products.</p>
<p> Implements specialty gas “Target Account Planning and Tracking System” and provides updates on a monthly basis to sales and product management.</p>
<p> Meets or exceeds the specialty gas and equipment sales goals specified in the individual market plans.</p>
<p> Examines current Airgas industrial and/or medical accounts for opportunities to add specialty gas business.</p>
<p> Acts as a sourcing and technical resource for retail sales associates (inside and outside).</p>
<p> Helps identify and converts current Airgas industrial and/or medical accounts for opportunities to add specialty gas business.</p>
<p> Works with the specialty gas product manager to coordinate the procurement and trucking of product to branch and/or customers.</p>
<p> Works with regional company product manager and ASG management to identify and to develop new products and/or service to meet changing customer and market requirements.</p>
<p> Attends and participates in specialty gas and equipment training sessions and seminars.</p>
<p> Assists distribution salespeople where needed.</p>
<p> Maintain a professional presence and demeanor that is reflective of a corporate image.</p>
<p><b>ADDITIONAL RESPONSIBILITIES: </b></p>
<p> Other duties and projects as assigned.</p>
<p><b>EDUCATION AND EXPERIENCE: </b></p>
<p> Bachelor’s degree from a four-year college or university in Chemistry, Engineering or related field required.</p>
<p> 3 to 5 years of experience selling medical, specialty or industrial gases and equipment to independent distributors and customers while working for an industrial or specialty gas producer.</p>
<p> Working knowledge of specialty gas pure gas and mixture production methods.</p>
<p> Industry experience preferred and related product knowledge is essential.</p>
<p> Negotiating and selling skills required with a proven record of achieving or exceeding assigned sales goals.</p>
<p> Computer proficiency and intermediate knowledge of MS Word, Excel and Google Kite.</p>
<p> Prior experience with SAP a definite plus.</p>
<p> Valid driver’s license and reliable personal vehicle with insurance coverage as required by company.</p>
