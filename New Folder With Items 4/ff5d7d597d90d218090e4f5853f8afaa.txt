<p><b>Trans World Entertainment Corporation</b></p>
<p><b>Assistant Buyer - Media</b></p>
<p><b>The Position</b></p>
<p>We are looking for a Assistant Buyer - Media who will responsible for creating an exciting and timely product assortment for the Retail business that meets or exceeds the sales volume and profits for all categories. They are to execute the direction of the Merchandising Leadership, and develop action plans that drive growth and increase profitability.</p>
<p><b>What You Will Do</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Manage the sample request and receipt process, ensuring samples arrive with time sufficient to be approved, ordered, and received prior to seasonal deadlines.</li>
<li>Collaborate with Planner and Buyer on assortment planning tools, purchasing system input and maintenance.</li>
<li>Manage new vendor set-up including EDI set up and communication of policies. Maintain communication with vendors to ensure 100% understanding of our needs and intentions related to their product and to have up-to-date delivery information.</li>
<li>Work collaboratively with the Visual Team in the mock store developing the optimum merchandise presentation for the assigned category. Assist in preparing SKU index and preparing visual boards to present assortments.</li>
<li>Track shipping on all orders to ensure on time delivery. Remains constantly aware of expected product arrival times by using available reports such as weekly PO Status report and being in contact with our suppliers.</li>
</ul>

<p><b>About Us</b></p>
<p>Trans World Entertainment Corporation (TWMC) is an omni-channel specialty retailer of entertainment related products and services. Revenue for the fiscal year ending 2018 was $418 million. The company was founded in 1972 and is headquartered in Albany, New York. The Company went public in 1986 and is traded under the symbol TWMC on the NASDAQ stock exchange. Trans World offers its products primarily under the brand f.y.e. (For Your Entertainment.) Trans World offers value-added services to attract customers. In addition to offering personalized customer service at its stores, the company offers a loyalty program, Backstage Pass card, which provides a discount on everyday selling prices on most products. The company also regularly engages in live events around the country with studio and artist participation including in store, festivals, conventions and numerous other interactive opportunities. This interactive capability coupled with Trans World Entertainment's more than forty years developing relationships in the entertainment industry affords a unique capability to delight both customers and artists alike.</p>
<p>The company currently operates over 200 stores comprised primarily of mall based, and to a lesser degree, free standing f.y.e. brand stores in the United States, and Puerto Rico. TWEC also operates a web platform including fye.com and secondspin.com.</p>
<p>The company enjoys a competitive and entrepreneurial culture that promotes innovation, excellence and ownership. Trans World employs over 2,500 associates nationwide, with about 400 of those associates working out of its corporate headquarters and distribution center in Albany, NY.</p>
<p>In October of 2016, the Company acquired etailz, the independently operated digital marketplace retailer across all product categories. Etailz utilizes a data-driven approach and proprietary software to grow brands on Amazon, Jet, eBay, Walmart, and Alibaba both within the U.S. and internationally.</p>
<p>Blending this online marketplace with access to Trans World’s 200+ brick and mortar locations and direct websites, the Company has created a complete retail experience like you’ve never seen before.</p>
