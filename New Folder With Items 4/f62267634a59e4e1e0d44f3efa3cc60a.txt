<h1 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Introduction</b></h1>
<p>This is a great opportunity for a high energy, leadership minded individual that has a passion for education and working in a fast paced SaaS environment. As an Inside Sales Manager, you will be responsible for ensuring your respective team members are successful in developing an assigned sales territory and meeting sales goals. You will support your designated inside sales reps through the entire sales and renewal process (prospecting, product demonstration, sales order, implementation and contract renewal). Target market will be K-12 schools, public, charter and virtual, to provide digital content solutions for credit recovery, blended learning and intervention.
</p>
<h1 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>About Edgenuity</b></h1>
<p>Imagine a world where all children have an equal opportunity to acquire an excellent education. That's the Edgenuity mission. Founded in 1998, today Edgenuity partners with schools to provide award-winning curriculum to millions of students in school districts across the U.S., and we’re growing! As the leading provider of digital curriculum and instructional services, Edgenuity empowers students, educators and school districts to achieve success in a variety of online and blended learning models.
</p>
<h1 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>A Day in the Life as an Inside Sales Manager</b></h1>
<p>An Inside Sales Manager is responsible for managing all aspects of their respective inside sales team. In this position you will be responsible for training and coaching Inside Sales employees; monitoring and reporting on sales metrics/expectations; and managing the sales process. You will be responsible for a growth percentage in your book of new business annually as well as an assigned current account book of business (renewals). An Inside Sales Manager is responsible for fully understanding and being able to speak to the education initiatives and opportunities for their reps assigned territories including state and school funding initiatives.
</p>

</li></ul>
<ul><li> Demonstrated history of landing new business for a SaaS offering and/or hitting team or personal sales goals
</li></ul>
<ul><li> Outstanding interpersonal and relationship-building skills with a high degree of prompt responsiveness and integrity.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Self-starter with the ability to work in a fast-paced environment.
</li></ul>
<ul><li> Exceptional verbal and written communication skills.
</li></ul>
<ul><li> Strong analytical skills to assess complex situations and exceptional creativity to provide and execute on solutions.
</li></ul>
<ul><li> A comfort level with presenting over the phone and via the web and potentially in person.</li></ul>
