<h2>District Manager - AUS - Knoxville, TN</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>Description
<div><div><div>
<p>Aramark Uniform Services (AUS) provides uniforms and related products to more than 400,000 customers nationwide, in virtually every industry. From designing and manufacturing to laundering and delivering, AUS works with local and national clients to create and maintain a total uniform solution that promotes teamwork and establishes a professional identity. AUS operates from over 200 locations nationwide.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Establish and maintain outstanding customer service within assigned geographic area</li>
<li>Recruit, select, hire, develop and educate Route Sales employees</li>
<li>Effectively manage district’s top 100 accounts through Quality Control Monitoring</li>
<li>Aid in conducting evaluations of staff to provide feedback on their overall performance, growth and customer service skills</li>
<li>Create a pervasive sales culture that fosters a strong brand orientation and supports achievement of sales objectives</li>
<li>Proactively follow up on accounts in jeopardy and lost accounts</li>
<li>Identify growth areas and retain 100% of the district’s customers</li>
<li>Make direct service calls and resolve service concerns expeditiously</li>
<li>Maintain close coordination with production department regarding service and customer issues</li>
<li>Perform route observations weekly</li>
<li>Partner with sales organization to identify areas of growth</li>
</ul>
</div></div></div>
<p></p>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Qualifications</b></h2>
<div><div><div><ul>
<li>High School Diploma or equivalent; Bachelor’s Degree preferred</li>
<li>Two years of service and route-based industry experience, with proven track record for growing accounts</li>
<li>Operate a Step Van or similar vehicle</li>
<li>Previous P&amp;L accountability and/or Contract-Managed service experience preferred</li>
<li>Strong interpersonal, analytical, communication and customer service skills</li>
<li>Must be able to frequently lift and maneuver up to 50 pounds and occasionally up to 100 pounds, as well as load/unload product from a truck without assistance</li>
<li>Solid computer skills including working knowledge of MS Office software</li>
<li>Open to relocation for advancement opportunities</li>
<li>Successful completion of Criminal Background, Motor Vehicle Record, and Drug Screen checks</li>
</ul></div></div></div>
