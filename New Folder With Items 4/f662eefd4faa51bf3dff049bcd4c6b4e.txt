<p><b>PURPOSE and OBJECTIVES</b><br>
As a member of the Audit Coordination Team within Operations, Processes and Controls, you will be challenged with audit assignments that not only provide exposure across the entire SAP Concur enterprise, but also enhance your skills to effectively evaluate business requirements against governance structures, policies and procedures, and strategic &amp; operational execution plans.</p>
<p>Operations, Processes and Controls is one of the integral elements of the SAP Concur governance and compliance framework. We evaluate strategic, financial, compliance, and operational aspects of SAP Concur business to ensure adequate governance exist. We provide advice and guidance to improve the effectiveness and efficiency of SAP Concur’s business. We are playing an increasingly important role as a strategic business advisor to stakeholders and leadership across SAP Concur, who seek our guidance to diagnose, improve, and accelerate performance.</p>
<p>We interact closely with the R&amp;D, Security, Corporate Functions, and our Distribution teams. The results of our work are presented to these stakeholders making our contribution highly visible.</p>
<p>We rely on the diversity and expertise of our highly qualified audit professionals to drive business outcomes for SAP Concur.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>EXPECTATIONS AND TASKS</b></p>
<ul> <li>Coordinate follow-up activities with process and service owners to ensure corrective and preventive actions from audits are properly resolved</li>
<li>Support harmonization of ISO Audits &amp; Data Center Audits</li>
<li>Support SAP Concur Units reaching Certifications (e.g. ISO 9001, ISO 27001 and PCI)</li>
<li>Support internal and external audits for units, customers, or regulatory body inspections</li>
<li>Work closely with Development, Data Center and Cloud teams, Delivery team, sales team and Global Security management in the planning, development, and execution of short and long-term goals</li>
<li>Demonstrate initiative and creativity in independently recommending and implementing solutions.</li>
<li>Build and maintain effective and trusted relationships with the Global Security Group and other internal partners.</li>
<li>Maintain knowledge of corporate changes, external trends, and the global regulatory landscape that may impact business activities and clients.</li>
<li>Frequently meet with key stakeholders to evaluate and adjust the direction of the audit engagement plan as appropriate.</li>
<li>Strong understanding of writing comprehensive audit reports</li>
<li>Effectively communicate status and results of overall audit activities and the audit plan to various levels of management</li>
<li>Support cross projects in cooperation with other teams</li>
<li>Be willing to drive and support a variety of assignments across the SAP Concur Enterprise</li>
<li>Actively engage and/or lead in all phases of audits from planning to execution through reporting</li>
<li>Deliver impactful and timely reports suitable for Control Owners, executives, and business managers</li>
<li>Actively utilize and share your knowledge and insights across the Audit Coordination team</li>
<li>Demonstrate professionalism and objectivity with a focus on the customer</li>
<li>Deliver results that are high quality, fact based, and with execution excellence</li>
</ul>

<b>EDUCATION AND QUALIFICATIONS | SKILLS AND COMPETENCIES</b></p>
<ul> <li>Bachelor’s Degree in Computer Science, MIS, Engineering, Information Security, or a related discipline.</li>
<li>Profound knowledge of industry standards (ISO Norms, particularly ISO 9001 and ISO 27001, best practices (e.g. ITIL, COBIT)</li>
<li>Auditor qualification would be a plus</li>
<li>Very good know-how about Quality and Information Security Management Systems, auditing and certification standards</li>
<li>Previous experience with Data Center or Development Audits and/or ISO 9001 or ISO 27001, SOC1, SOC2, or PCI Audit</li>
<li>Demonstrated experience in cloud, IT, and infrastructure technologies</li>
<li>Complex thinking, strong analytical skills and creativity in solving complex problems required</li>
<li>Understanding of infrastructure build-out and infrastructure components (e.g. servers, operating systems, data storage, network monitoring tools, Building Management System (BMS), Directory services (Active Directory), and networking concepts</li>
<li>Willingness to assist in areas outside of direct assignments when necessary, and commitment to self-improvement and completion of team objectives.</li>
<li>Strong communication skills in English is a requirement</li>
<li>Proactive behavior, high willingness to learn and the ability to drive in challenging projects</li>
<li>Exposure to internal audit, consulting, risk management, or compliance experience is a plus</li>
<br>
</ul>
