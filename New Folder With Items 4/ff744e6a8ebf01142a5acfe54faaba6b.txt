<b>What we are Looking for:</b></p>
<p>
The Contracts Representative at Dalco is primarily an administrative role responsible for daily maintenance and updating of contracts that are received from manufacturers, Network Services, or sales reps. Load contract information into the contracts management system (AS400). Send notification’s to account manager and customer service of any contract changes.</p>
<p><b>
Specific Duties and Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Upload and maintain customer contracts to ensure properly updated. Notifications of new/updated contracts are sent to account manager and customer service rep.</li>
<li>Assist customers and account manager by phone, email, fax, etc., with questions related to contract cost or sells. Obtains information from the AS400.</li>
<li>Execute reports from AS400 to find vendor cost versus rebate cost. Analyze output of reports to determine cost variance. Will be directed to suspend or reactivate an item based on manager review.</li>
<li>Run reports on sku replacements or obsolete items via AS400 (gather new contracts to replace).</li>
<li>Assist in researching best cost for bids/quotes for account managers.</li>
<li>Displays a courteous and helpful attitude in order to provide a positive experience for our customers.</li>
<li>Promptly follows up with customer after complaint to ensure they are satisfied with the action that was taken.</li>
<li>Listens to customers in an empathetic manner, ensuring an understanding of questions, concerns and requests and their feeling associated with them.</li>
<li>Recommend potential departmental changes and improvements.</li>
<li>Assist all customer support departments’ resolution of customer problems and satisfaction of needs.</li>
<li>The company may at any time change functions and requirements of the position to meet the needs of the business</li>
<li>Perform additional duties and projects as assigned.</li>
</ul>

<li>Ability to work independently and build strong relationships</li>
<li>Effective written and verbal communication skills.</li>
<li>Detail oriented.</li>
<li>Organized</li>
<li>Basic computer and math skills</li>
<li>Able to meet monthly deadlines.</li>
<li>Self-motivated</li>
