<p><br>
<b>Job Responsibilities</b><br>
The size and scope of our business and technology has grown and evolved exponentially over the past five years and with that, our end users have as well. This position is a result of many ongoing and upcoming technological initiatives being rolled out to the entire organization.</p>
<br>
<p><br>
Please make sure you can work the schedule as required by the position before applying.</p>
<br>
<p><br>
<b>General Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Support of our internally developed Ruby on Rails enterprise application</li>
<li>Responds to telephone calls, email and personnel requests for technical support</li>
<li>Monitors ticket queue for high priority tickets</li>
<li>Documents, tracks and monitors the problem to ensure a timely resolution</li>
<li>Works with end users to identify issues and document potential resolution</li>
<li>Identifies, researches, and resolves technical problems</li>
<li>Ability to learn new processes and procedures</li>
<li>Coordinate with the development team for testing and deployment of new features</li>
<li>Knowledge of commonly-used tech support concepts, practices, and procedures</li>
<li>Ability to use problem management databases and help desk systems</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Hours
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Monday - Friday: 11 am - 9 pm</li>
<li>Saturday &amp; Sunday on a rotating basis (7:30 am - 2:00 pm; on-call until end of business day)</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Required Education and Experience</b></p>
<ul>
<li>A working knowledge of business process and work flow a plus</li>
<li>Knowledge of commonly-used tech support concepts, practices, and procedures</li>
<li>Ability to use problem management databases and help desk systems</li>
<li>Must have excellent communication skills and ability to multitask</li>
<li>An Associate's degree in Computer Science or related discipline, Bachelor's degree preferred</li>
<li>At least one, but typically three years of experience in an IT role or an equivalent combination of education and work experience</li>
<li>Position requires sufficient knowledge of IT operations, responsibilities, workflow processes, and procedures to resolve most inquires independently</li>
<li>Good analytical and communication skills, sound judgment, and the ability to work effectively with client and other Systems Division staffs</li>
<li>An Articulate and Professional Demeanor</li>
<li>Self motivation and discipline</li>
<li>Excellent organizational and interpersonal skills</li>
<li>Resourceful problem solving abilities</li>
<li>Highly evolved customer service skills</li>
</ul>
