As a part of the Information Security Risk Management Supply Chain group, the ISRM Senior Analyst – Customer Connectivity is responsible for supporting the development and implementation of a green-field security program which spans e-commerce, hospital systems integration, contracts management, and customer call center security.<br>
<br>
<b>Key Responsibilities:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Provide security consulting and assurance via secure architecture design reviews on projects and new implementations</li>
<li>
Shape security user stories and provide security solution/remediation guidance across both on premise and cloud/SaaS hosted platforms</li>
<li>
Provide mentorship on secure coding; lead and support adoption and implementation of secure CI/DC and DevSecOps practices including integration of static analysis, software composition analysis, and dynamic analysis tools</li>
<li>
Provide consulting on security patterns for microservices, APIs and serverless architectures.
</li>
<li>Assist in site, vendor and full-stack technology assessments existing customer call center sites, systems and vendor relationships. Rank risks; provide security remediation guidance across Information Technology solutions (applications, databases, infrastructure, networks, industrial Internet of Things); facilitate penetration testing.</li>
<li>
Provide consulting to IT teams on secure application architecture and design, secure coding, adoption and implementation of secure Continuous Integration/Continuous Delivery practices, and security patterns for microservices, APIs and serverless architectures.</li>
<li>
Perform assessments and design reviews of solutions and environments, analyzing and triaging output of scan tools and penetration testing, ranking risks and providing and guidance, solution design and technology options for remediation.</li>
<li>
Contribute SME knowledge to help shape the strategy and security program for J&amp;J Customer facing IT solutions.</li>
<li>
Monitor the industry landscape for emerging threats, technologies and capabilities.
</li>
</ul>
<br>
