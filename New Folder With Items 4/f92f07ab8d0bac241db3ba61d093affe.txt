<h2>Sales Representative</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Matrix Global Services is a leading Public, Hybrid and Private Solution provider. With over 8 years of experience Matrix Global Services provides its customers with a complete service package that includes consulting, professional services and managed services including a 24/7 hours NOC services.</p>
<p>Matrix Global Services is part of Matrix, a public company (MTRX: TV) which employs 9000 IT professionals, is the leading information technology company in Israel with a growing presence in the US.</p>
<p>We are a fast-paced company with a success driven culture, always looking for ways to grow and expand our business ventures and build creative technologies.</p>
<p>As we continue to grow our business in the US, we are seeking a Sales Rep, with experience selling multiple IT service solutions to both new and existing customers. We will look for this individual to build strong, long term relationships that are a reflection of the high quality solutions, large scale complex deliverables and top service level for which we have become known.</p>
<p>An inside sales rep will play a fundamental role in achieving our ambitious customer acquisition and revenue growth objectives. You must be comfortable making dozens of calls per day, working with channel partners, generating interest, qualifying prospects and closing sales.</p>
<p>Responsibilities:</p>
<ul>
<li>Source new sales opportunities through inbound lead follow-up and outbound cold calls and emails</li>
<li>Understand customer needs and requirements</li>
<li>Route qualified opportunities to the appropriate executives for further development and closure</li>
<li>Close sales and achieve quarterly quotas</li>
<li>Research accounts, identify key players and generate interest</li>
<li>Maintain and expand your database of prospects</li>
<li>Team with channel partners to build pipeline and close deals</li>
<li>Perform effective online demos to prospects</li>
</ul>
<p>Requirements</p>
<ul>
<li>Proven inside sales experience</li>
<li>Track record of over-achieving quota</li>
<li>Strong phone presence and experience dialing dozens of calls per day</li>
<li>Proficient with corporate productivity and web presentation tools</li>
<li>Experience working with Salesforce.com or similar CRM</li>
<li>Excellent verbal and written communications skills</li>
<li>Strong listening and presentation skills</li>
<li>Ability to multi-task, prioritize, and manage time effectively</li>
<li>BA/BS degree or equivalent</li>
</ul>
<p>Job Type: Full-time</p>
<p>Experience:</p>
<ul>
<li>Sales: 3 years (Preferred)</li>
<li>Customer Service: 3 years (Preferred)</li>
</ul>
