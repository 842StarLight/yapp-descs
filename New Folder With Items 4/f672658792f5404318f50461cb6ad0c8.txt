<div><b>
Description</b></div>
<div></div>
<div>Emerson provides an excellent environment for engineering creativity and design. The candidate will be immersed in an environment that nurtures individualism, creative problem-solving and intellect. The purpose of this position is to design, develop, modify, and evaluate electronic parts, components, or integrated circuitry for electronic equipment and other hardware systems.<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<div><b>Responsibilities</b></div>
<div>Design, Administrative and Project Tasks</div>
<ul>
<li>Design of novel analog and digital circuitry, including current-generation applications processors, MCUs, and switched-mode power supplies, for highly challenging industrial control systems</li>
<li>
Perform complex analysis, design, and development tasks with a high degree of autonomy, efficiency and accuracy</li>
<li>
Test designs and apply rigorous methodology to design and design optimization</li>
<li>
Ensure safety regulations are met</li>
<li>
Interface with UL and Regulatory agencies</li>
<li>
Anticipate, identify, and solve problems independently</li>
<li>
Develop and write technical documents and white papers</li>
<li>
Track ECNs and follow up to ensure sign off in a timely manner</li>
<li>
Research and implement improvement techniques to enhance product performance.</li>
<li>
Be self-motivated and willing and able to operate with little direct guidance or supervision, work effectively with incomplete or un-defined requirements, and have familiarity with protection of intellectual property.</li>
<li>
Support engineering department activities including, but not limited to, design reviews, presentations, production support, training, field application support, and budgeting.</li>
<li>
Interface with customers, partners, and vendors globally<br>
</li>
</ul>
<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<div>
<div><b>Requirements</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree in Electrical or Computer Engineering required; Master’s preferred.</li>
<li>
8 + years of work experience, preferably in an industrial control environment</li>
<li>
Expertise with EDA software, preferably CADENCE. Must be able to do schematic capture, perform power and signal integrity analysis, and high-speed PCB design/layout.</li>
<li>
Experience with configuration management and product lifecycle management tools such as Omnify.</li>
<li>
Hands-on test and debug experience with oscilloscopes and similar test equipment in a lab environment;</li>
<li>
Communication Networking experience a plus; RS485, Ethernet, Bluetooth, WIFI, USB.</li>
<li>
Basic understanding or willingness to learn refrigeration systems.</li>
<li>
Knowledge of IAR embedded Workbench is a plus</li>
<li>
Basic Office Skills (Word, Excel, Outlook)</li>
</ul>
