<h2>Marketing Intern</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>The marketing intern participates in and organizes marketing activities, resources and schedules for positive, creative and successful accomplishment of proposal production, public relations/advertising and marketing/sales objectives of the business unit. This role would begin Spring 2020.</p>
<p><b>
Job Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Assist in researching and coordinating production of qualifications, proposals, presentations, and other marketing materials and submittals.</li>
<li>Researches/designs/writes/edits various proposal/business development documents (i.e., brochures, flyers, postcards, project profiles, advertisements).</li>
<li>Assist with social media posts (Facebook, Twitter, LinkedIn, YouTube).</li>
<li>Assist with updates to current website.</li>
<li>Develop background research on target markets and potential clients.</li>
<li>Research industry organizations, conferences/seminars, client news, and in-the-news trends.</li>
<li>Collects, organizes and maintains the upkeep of the marketing/business development information, resources, files and schedules.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Education/Experience:</b></p>
<p>
High school diploma and some college education preferred.</p>
<p><b>
Specific Knowledge, Skills, License, Certifications, Etc.:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Knowledge of word processing software</li>
<li>Experience with desktop publishing and editing software (i.e. InDesign, Photoshop)</li>
<li>Knowledge of social media platforms</li>
<li>Detail oriented and attention to accuracy</li>
<li>Ability to work independently</li>
<li>Knowledge of database concepts</li>
<li>Time management skills, reviews items promptly and has the ability to adjust accordingly</li>
<li>Ability to work within group and project teams</li>
<li>Organizational skills, multi-tasking</li>
</ul>
</div></div>
