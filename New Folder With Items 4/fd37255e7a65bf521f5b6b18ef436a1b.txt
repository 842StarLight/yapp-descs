<h2>Program Manager, Business continuity</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<b>Location:</b> San Francisco
<br><br>
Anaplan is looking to hire a talented Manager, Business Systems within our growing Global IT organization.
<br><br>
Our office is located in the heart of the eclectic SOMA district, you can feel the excitement and energy of what we do at Anaplan when you step into our San Francisco office. There are open floor plans, fully stocked kitchens, and great collaboration spaces, and we focus on making sure that you have everything you need to work well from the right lighting to the latest technology.
<br><br>
<b>What you'll do:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Work with multiple stakeholders across Anaplan to ensure effective understanding of Business Continuity Plans and Disaster Recovery strategies</li>
<li>Manage the Business Continuity Management program including both Business Continuity and Disaster Recovery</li>
<li>Meet with senior management and ERM leadership to conduct Business Impact Analyses</li>
<li>Work with various departments enterprise-wide to develop and maintain up-to-date Business Continuity plans using a risk based approach</li>
<li>Build and scale a technology Disaster Recovery program</li>
<li>Develop and conduct training and exercises for both Business Continuity and Disaster Recovery</li>
<li>Serve as a point of contact for any identified resilience issues and risks within the business</li>
<li>Provide management, leadership, and direction to build, maintain and enhance resilience practices</li>
<li>Assume a leadership role to understand dependencies in the business and incorporate them into Business Continuity plans</li>
<li>Be accountable for identification, understanding, management, mitigation, and remediation of continuity risks and issues assigned to the organization globally</li>
<li>Communicate with senior executives on plan status including significant enhancements or issues that could impact continuity planning</li>
</ul>
<b>You have:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Minimum 7 years progressive experience in Business Continuity and Disaster Recovery, with at least 3 years if directly managing a Business Continuity program in IT / technology company</li>
<li>Experience with global Business Continuity standards and frameworks</li>
<li>Ability to analyze, communicate, articulate Business Continuity governance and compliance trends and program requirements</li>
<li>Experience in conducting tabletop and functional exercises across business functions and with technology teams</li>
<li>Experience with Disaster Recovery in a cloud centric environment including Infrastructure-as-a-Service (IaaS), Software-as-a-Service (SaaS), and Platform-as-a-Service (PaaS) environments</li>
<li>Excellent written and verbal communication, critical thinking skills, effective interpersonal skills, strong formal presentation abilities, and strong project management experience</li>
<li>Ability to be flexible and work effectively with ambiguity and change</li>
<li>Bachelor's or Master's degree in business, management, information systems, or a related discipline</li>
<li>Industry certifications such as ABCP, CBCP, MBCP, ISO22301 Lead Implementer are preferred</li>
</ul>
