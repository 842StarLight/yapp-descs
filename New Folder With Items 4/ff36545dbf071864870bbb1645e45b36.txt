We maintain all original equipment manufacturers for material handling equipment, BHS, PBB, Facilities, GSE and vehicles. Our hybrid programs efficiently cross utilize technicians between equipment types to provide seamless coverage that protects the best interest of our customers. Whether this is getting full warranty value for new parts and systems or applying experienced and specialized technical support for mature equipment needs, ELS has you covered.</div>

<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div><b>POSITION SUMMARY
</b></div>
<div>The MHE Tech I will be responsible for assisting in the housekeeping, general maintenance, and repair of the material handling equipment within the building.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div><b>
ESSENTIAL FUNCTIONS
</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Repairing and cleaning the material handling equipment to include conveyors, SLAM machines, etc.
</li>
<li>Maintaining and repairing distribution center racking</li>
<li>
Assists other MHE Techs in repairing and maintaining material handling equipment</li>
<li>
Assists other MHE Techs in repairing and maintaining building pneumatic system</li>
<li>
Completing preventative maintenance routines, documentation and procedures</li>
<li>
Perform battery changes, and preventive maintenance on batteries</li>
<li>
Ability to do e-mail communication and date entry for database</li>
<li>
Enter downtime data in daily log, complete PM sheets</li>
<li>
Electrical, knowledge of basics components</li>
<li>
Ability to ID problems and understand resources, needed to resolve them</li>
<li>
Entering parts into computer software as parts are used.</li>
<li>
Supporting continuous improvement in department</li>
<li>
Develop and maintain a good working relationship across all levels in the organization.</li>
<li>
Completes all safety training as assigned by the Company.</li>
<li>
Compliance with all ELS safety programs, policies and procedures.</li>
<li>
Perform additional duties/assignments that may be required by management from time to time.</li>
</ul>
</div></div></div>

<div><b>BASIC QUALIFICATIONS</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
PC competency, Word &amp; Excel and Windows OS</li>
<li>
Experience with conveyors, understand flow of system, start, stop, and un-jam. Check zones, belt tracking, and oilier, minor repairs, PM's with assistants</li>
<li>
Slam, Print and Apply, able to start system and know key components with basis adjustments. Ability to repair printers adjusts photo eyes and scales</li>
<li>
Box Makers- start and stop load fill and change tape, know key components and make adjustments. Perform PM’s and do basic repairs</li>
<li>
Electrical knowledge of basics components.</li>
<li>
Basic knowledge of Pneumatics- knows what they do and how to take readings</li>
