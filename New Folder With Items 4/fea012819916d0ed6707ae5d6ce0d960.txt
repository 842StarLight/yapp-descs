<h2>Customer Service - Account Coordinator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Melitta is a global premium coffee company with our North American headquarters located in Clearwater, Florida. In North America, Melitta is the leading brand of coffee filters, coffee and pour-over coffee brewing systems. Melitta is known in the market for its high-quality products that address the needs of discerning customers. Melitta is celebrating 111 years in business and is a state-of-the-art roaster of specialty coffees.</p>
<p><b>SUMMARY</b></p>
<p>We are looking for an Account Coordinator that will extend the highest level of customer service to our Business to Business trade partners. They will utilize a variety of software tools to navigate partner accounts, research, review and process orders. This position works closely with customers and internal teams to provide best in class service. Take a proactive stance anticipating &amp; heading off problems with orders in keeping ongoing communication flow with in-house and outside customers in the order fulfillment process. This position requires a high attention to detail.</p>
<p><b>RESPONSIBILITIES AND DUTIES</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Direct contact/liaison with customer on orders, availability</li>
<li>Process customer orders – varies based on individual customer requirements on how their orders are handled, Receive, edit and process all B2B sales orders, via phone, fax or EDI</li>
<li>Work closely with operations to ensure timely delivery of customer orders.</li>
<li>Research order issues for distribution, traffic, internal depts. Communicate regularly with the B2B business team</li>
<li>Any periodic customer or internal reporting as required</li>
<li>Liaison between customer and operations/scheduling/shipping/order issues/forecasting</li>
<li>Communicate with internal departments – operations, customer service management, logistics</li>
<li>Invoice research as needed</li>
<li>Special projects as needed by the company</li>
</ul>

</ul>
