<h2>Display Coordinator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div></div>
<div><div>
<div><div>
<div><b>MAJOR FUNCTION</b></div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div>Maintains display standards of showroom in accordance with Corporate Display Standards.</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div>
<b>ESSENTIAL DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES</b> include the following. Other duties may be assigned.</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<ul>
<li>Arranges and presents merchandise on showroom floor based on Best Seller Report and Display Plan.</li>
<li>Aids sales associates with customers in selection of furniture and/or accessories. May provide in-home design services.</li>
<li>Coordinates and displays accessories and accent pieces based on Corporate Display Standards.</li>
<li>Completes Display Coordinator Weekly Checklist and reviews with Display Manager and/or General Manager.</li>
<li>Tags accessories according to Corporate Display Standards.</li>
<li>Tracks accessories sales using Best Seller Report.</li>
<li>Marks down, appropriately displays and moves out accessories that are not selling as indicated by Best Seller Report.</li>
<li>May be required to provide administrative and/or housekeeping support.</li>
<li>SUPERVISORY RESPONSIBILITIES</li>
<li>This job has no supervisory responsibilities.</li>
</ul>
</div></div>
