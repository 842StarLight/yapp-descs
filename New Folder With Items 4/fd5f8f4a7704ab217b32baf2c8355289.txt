<b>Job Description:</b><br>
We are looking for a resourceful, driven, team player with a competitive spirit, and strong business acumen to join our Account Management team. This role will partner with our Health Plan customers reach new levels of customer success. The ideal candidate has experience building and maintaining executive-level relationships at health plans while taking a consultative approach to growing and retaining customers. This role requires engaging with health plan customers at every level of the sales cycle, from pre-sale to implementation to ongoing operations. If you have a curiosity for solving business problems, a passion for making your customers successful, and the ability to articulate the ROI of an enterprise solution across multiple stakeholders, this could be the role for you.
<br>The Health Plan Director, Account Management role is responsible for account satisfaction, retention and growth with a focus on ensuring strong engagement with the DOD platform to meet utilization, cost savings and customer satisfaction targets. This role owns the creation and execution of strategic account plans that position Doctor On Demand to optimize our opportunities across a health plan’s portfolio. In addition, this role works with our in-house marketing agency to develop and implement marketing/account strategies and tactics which enhance Doctor On Demand’s position in the market and lead to account growth. This is position reports to Doctor On Demand’s President and Chief Commercial Officer. Position can be based in our Minneapolis, MN office or remote in the Central or Eastern time zone.
<br><br>
<b>Job Responsibilities:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Develop and execute strategic and tactical plans to achieve optimal satisfaction, retention, and growth in each health plan account</li>
<li>Proactively identifies membership growth, retention, and expansion opportunities through analysis of our health plan customers' business objectives, industries, and the dynamics of the healthcare/benefits market</li>
<li>Lead growth initiatives within existing accounts as well as renewal efforts designed to expand and deepen customer relationships</li>
<li>Identifies and analyzes current industry and competitor trends and applies knowledge of marketplace to account planning processes</li>
<li>Build and cultivate executive-level relationships with health plan stakeholders as well as collaborate with internal stakeholders to develop and execute strategic account plans</li>
<li>Build and present a compelling value proposition for our offering to customers, including ROI analysis, clinical opportunities, and member engagement strategies</li>
<li>Provides strategic recommendations to the design of new products, benefits offering, or pricing models that will address customer needs and business objectives</li>
<li>Continuously monitors performance against plan adjusting as market and Doctor On Demand imperatives dictate</li>
</ul>
