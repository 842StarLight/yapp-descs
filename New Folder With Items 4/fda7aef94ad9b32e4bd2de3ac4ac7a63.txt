<h2>Logistics Solutions Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div></div>
<div>
<div>
<div>The Logistics Solutions Manager will grow revenue by positioning brokerage services with enterprise accounts, leading to greater share of wallet, diversification of services within account and higher account retention.</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div><b>
ESSENTIAL DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Service expert
<ul>
<li>Develops deep knowledge and expertise about brokerage services and how they can benefit customers businesses.</li>
<li>Serve as service experts for internal enterprise sales teams and large enterprise accounts.</li>
</ul>
</li>
</ul>
<ul><li>Dedicated account management
<ul>
<li>Develops deep understanding of enterprise accounts they are assigned to and serves as a key member (customer and sales facing) for the customer team</li>
<li>Proactively identifies customer needs and provides reliable information and solutions to meet those needs.</li>
<li>Works directly with customers to solve logistics problems, provide consulting and solutions.</li>
<li>Provides support directly to customers, serving as the quarterback of the customer POD support team to make sure solutions are proactively provided and issues resolved.</li>
<li>Serve as a strategic resource for CAE and other account team members to provide solutions, ideas and problem solving.</li>
<li>Increase share of wallet and retention with enterprise accounts.</li>
</ul>
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Strategic support
<ul>
<li>Provide strategic insights and BI for customer meetings, new business pitches and QBRs.</li>
<li>Identify issues or opportunities (internal, customer &amp; market) and share with CAEs to help them compile information that helps them create deeper, strategic relationships with current point of contact or new points of contact within accounts.</li>
</ul>
</li></ul>
