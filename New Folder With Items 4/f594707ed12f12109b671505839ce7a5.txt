<p>
Honeywell Building Solutions (HBS) has an exciting opportunity for a Global 3rd party Outcome Based Services (OBS) Breakthrough Initiative Leader. This position will report to the Global HBS Vice President of Services and will be responsible for developing and implementing strategies to develop and grow the 3rd party OBS solutions and related service business. A key area for growth of our business aligns to Global Breakthrough Initiatives. These are initiatives that have not been traditionally “core” to our business, but are now being driven to deliver breakthrough growth. We are seeking a seasoned Business Leader with demonstrated growth results to provide the leadership required in this role.</p>
<p>
Honeywell Building Solutions (HBS) is a $2.5B strategic business unit within Home and Building Technologies (HBT). HBS installs and maintains the systems to help keep buildings and facilities safe, secure, comfortable and cost-efficient, and is a leading provider of energy efficiency solutions worldwide. HBS specializes in the installation and service of critical and connected building systems, including Heating, Ventilation and Air Conditioning (HVAC), Building Automation, Fire, Security and Energy Management.</p>
<p>
As the <b>Global 3</b><b>rd</b><b> party Outcome Based Services Breakthrough Initiative Leader </b>for HBS, you will be challenged to use business acquisition judgment and leadership skills in achieving the financial / non-financial annual goals. This requires implementation of customer acquisition sales strategies, tactics and processes to solve our customer’s most critical business problems. The value proposition includes the full cycle offering from assessments, configuration and design, implementing appliances/software, monitoring and performing predictive maintenance services. You will utilize your experience in the Software, Building Services, Software as a Service (SaaS), solutions industry to hire, direct, coach, lead and mentor a sales force of Business Consultants that provide high quality, innovative Outcome Based Services and solutions to our customers.
</p>
<p>This position interfaces with the Global Marketing &amp; Strategy team for HBS/HBT, Honeywell Technology Organization and the Honeywell Building Solutions sales teams on financials, processes, operational initiatives and core install and service leads, to ensure OBS growth objectives are achieved.<br>
<br>
<b>Position Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Develop new business opportunities with the HBS Outcome Based Services Portfolio of new offerings through understanding of market trends, key players in the market and next generation technologies in assigned markets.</li>
<li>
Responsible for achieving Annual Operating Plan (AOP) targets (Orders, Rev/GM and Profit) while monitoring and optimizing pricing policies.</li>
<li>
Provide profitable and strategic sales growth for 3rd Party OBS, drive customer acquisition, establish market demand and grow business exclusively with new customers.</li>
<li>
Provide Direct or Indirect management of Sales reps as applicable. Dedicated Sales reps accountable for 3rd party OBS revenue will report directly to this role.</li>
<li>
Monitor strengths and weaknesses of the 3rd Party OBS value proposition and make required adjustments based on market intelligence working closely with the Marketing, Technology and Regional Sales teams.</li>
<li>
Work closely with Technology &amp; Marketing teams to enable successful Product launches and NPI targets achievement (related to this Breakthrough Initiative of 3rd Party OBS).</li>
<li>
Manage sales activity by means of a Sales Management Operating System to include forecasting calls, opportunity reviews, Plan to Make Plan (PtmP) development and monitoring.</li>
<li>
Leverage strength of HBT/HBS by utilizing available resources and 3rd party suppliers and software eco-system partners, while aligning corporate sales initiatives to HBS strategies.</li>
</ul>
