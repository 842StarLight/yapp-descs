<p><b>
Why Us?</b></p>
<p>
This is an incredible opportunity to bring your selling expertise and creativity to this newly created position. You'll help us identify new opportunities, and conduct outreach into these prospective markets. You'll qualify, demo and close leads that our Marketing team is generating. You'll constantly gather feedback from your wins and losses and contribute to our market competitive research to help our product team with new dev releases. You'll be a direct contributor to help grow a startup, and incorporate your creative flair in our processes.</p>
<p>
We're VC backed and have a good foundation for you to plant your feet on. Sometimes the entire office spends the morning wrestling with ideas and high-level strategies over coffee and bagels. Other days we strap on headphones and get deep into our work. This is the kind of place you can get your hands dirty, hone your skills, stimulate your brain daily, learn a ton, and feel like you belong.</p>

<p><b>
What You'll Do - Job Duties:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Pipeline Management - Maintain a substantial pipeline of qualified opportunities. Organize daily, ultra-responsive follow up cadence on all sales cycles.</li>
<li>
Conduct online software demos of BlueCart platform(s).</li>
<li>
Consistently maintain an accurate forecast.</li>
<li>
Track and give regular feedback on the market, competition, and win/loss feedback. Your feedback will help influence future product releases.</li>
