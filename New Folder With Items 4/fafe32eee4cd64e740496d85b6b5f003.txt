<p><b>The opportunity:</b>
</p>
<p>The Project Resource Coordinator acts as the link between the Services, Sales, and Finance groups as it relates to initiating a project kick-off and gathering and aligning the appropriate resources. Resources include but are not limited to contracts, artifacts (pre-sales solution diagrams, specifications, requirements, etc.) and project team staffing (Project Manager, technical lead, etc.)<br>
<br>
The individual will be required to organize project contracts and initiate introductory calls with customers to ensure a smooth hand-off to the assigned project manager. The coordinator will assess individual projects and work with management to align and assign team members meet project staffing requirements. The Coordinator must ensure project resourcing is managed to meet timely delivery and must communicate to appropriate project managers resourcing issues that may impact scheduled deadlines. A secondary responsibility of the project resource coordinator is to assist in maintaining reporting on utilization and project resource forecasting needs.</p>
