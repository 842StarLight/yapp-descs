<p></p>
<p>The Customer Master Data Steward will be responsible for daily maintenance of global customer master records, ensuring the highest integrity of Customer Master Data according to LS&amp;Co data governance standards and established best practices. This position will be responsible for handling service requests from all geographic regions according the established service level agreements (SLA's). This individual will regularly communicate with other LS&amp;Co departments including Sales, OTC, Finance, Accounts Payable, and other stakeholders, providing clarity and information as part of delivering high quality customer service. This individual will also continually seek process improvements in delivering customer value, and will participate in corporate initiatives as a subject matter expert if and when requested to do so.
</p>
<p></p>
<p>As a Customer Master Data Steward, you must have:
</p>
<ul>
<li>Overall, high level understanding of the Customer Master, and its components supporting the LS&amp;Co business
</li>
<li>Strong understanding of Customer Master structure and business processes
</li>
<li>Understand the broad business and potential global implications of Customer Master data
</li>
<li>Strong analytical skills and the ability to move forward with some ambiguity
</li>
<li>Strong computer skills with the ability to learn new technologies as required
</li>
<li>The ability to work effectively with cross-functional teams
</li>
<li>The ability to make decisions under significant time pressure
</li>
<li>A passion for detail and delivering customer value and meeting expectations
</li>
<li>Ability to stand firm on data quality principles while also balancing the needs of the business
</li>
<li>Eagerness to help the team succeed and thrive
</li>
<li>Passion for one’s own professional development
</li>
<li>Obsession for data accuracy
</li>
<li>Desire to drive process improvement
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p>As a Customer Master Data Steward, you will perform the following main functions:
</p>
<ul>
<li>Create and modify Customer Master records as requested for all regions
</li>
<li>Manage daily and weekly work schedules to ensure maximum productivity
</li>
<li>Prioritize work based on business needs
</li>
<li>Tenaciously maintain Customer Master data quality
</li>
<li>Enforce Data Governance rules as required in the regular course of work
</li>
<li>Proactively resolve Customer Master data issues when encountered
</li>
<li>Identify and escalate potential concerns related to Customer Master data and related business processes
</li>
<li>Work closely with other team members to create a unified effort and deliver highest business value
</li>
<li>Participate in business initiatives as a subject matter expert when required
</li>
<li>Continually improve your professional skill set
</li>
<li>Meet or exceed SLA’s
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Qualifications
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Minimum 5 years in a master data role, or related experience preferred
</li>
<li>Prior experience with Customer Master
</li>
<li>Competence with SAP ECC Customer Master preferred
</li>
<li>Prior experience with SAP ERP, or a similar enterprise ERP system
</li>
<li>Competence with MS Office Suite
</li>
<li>Experience with one of more of the following preferred: MS SharePoint, MS InfoPath, Winshuttle
</li>
</ul>
