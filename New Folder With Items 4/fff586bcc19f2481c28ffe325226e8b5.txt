<div>
As a <b>Sr. Application Engineer</b>, you will assist in defining business processes and enabling end-to-end capability from design to implementation across various applications (Salesforce, Jira, Confluence, etc…). You will be an active participant through the software development life-cycle, from design, build, deploy and change management. Focus will be delivering a holistic solution not based solely on the merits of any one technology. This individual would work under minimal direction from managers and senior team members and reviews work and provides guidance for less experienced colleagues.</div>
</div>
<div><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Role Responsibilities</b></h3>
<div><ul>
<li>
Ensure proper security models are in place and maintained for data access</li>
<li>
Work with departments on change requests to ensure changes align with business processes and mitigate risk of those changes</li>
<li>
Stay current on new features of application to ensure company is utilizing the application as effectively as possible</li>
<li>
Ensure new releases are rolled out smoothly to the organization</li>
<li>
Gather requirements from users, propose solutions that meet business needs, and deliver agreed upon solutions</li>
<li>
Apply business best practices and consider business goals/objectives when developing solutions</li>
<li>
Manage monthly feature releases via rotation with other Application Administrators and Engineers</li>
<li>
Regular customer engagement and strategic discussion via: running monthly stakeholder meeting(s), and other activities</li>
<li>
Develop solutions that are scalable, and flexible</li>
<li>
Create comprehensive business workflows utilizing internal tools such as Salesforce, Jira, Workato, etc… to empower internal teams and meet business requirements</li>
<li>
Provide technical expertise across small, medium, and large-scale projects as related to supported applications</li>
<li>
Coordinate with Business Service Managers to ensure timely delivery, execution and function of service implementations</li>
<li>
Understand system architecture of upstream and downstream applications and identify potential impact when implementing solutions</li>
<li>
Proactive recommendation of process improvements within role, team and across other Relativity departments Troubleshoot and resolve user issues as they arise</li>
<li>
Manage data security within applications</li>
<li>
Provide application expertise to team members through education and review</li>
<li>
Train, educate and empower business team to use supported applications effectively and efficiently</li>
<li>
Ensure all work is thoroughly documented in the appropriate format (requirements, tickets, project plans, workflow diagrams, test cases, user stories, etc.)</li>
</ul></div>
</div></div>
