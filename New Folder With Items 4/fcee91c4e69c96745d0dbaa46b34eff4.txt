<h2>Budtender - Lead Evans</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div><div><div><div>
<p>The Budtender Lead is passionate about Marijuana, provides product knowledge to our patients, and explains the benefits of buying the product. The Budtender Lead is responsible for supporting the Retail Manager and Assistant Manager with all aspects of the daily retail operations, coaching and developing the retail team that delivers the LivWell retail promise to every customer. LivWell operates under the highest standards of professionalism.</p>
<div></div>

Essential Duties</b></div>

<br>
<ul>
<li>
Execute LivWell selling standards to meet team and individual sales goals and metrics.</li>
<li>
Assist with the conduction of training of all new employees as well as the re-training of existing employees when needed.</li>
<li>
Ensure the sales floor is properly stocked and the presence of the store is well maintained.</li>
<li>
Model best-in-class service experience that consistently exceeds our customer’s expectations.</li>
<li>
Inspire customer confidence and create loyalty to our brand.</li>
<li>
Promote a work environment that is positive, customer-service oriented, and compliant with established policies and procedures.</li>
<li>
Accountable for accurately utilizing LivWell’s Point of Sale system.</li>
<li>
Provide motivation and keep employee morale high.
</li>
<li>Other duties as assigned by the Management Team.</li>
</ul>
