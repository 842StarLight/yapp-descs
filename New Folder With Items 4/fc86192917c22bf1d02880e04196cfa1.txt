<p><b>Job Title:
</b></p>MKTG - Market Associate (Alcohol) - Boston, MA
<p></p>
<p><b>Job Description:
</b></p>
<p>The Market Associate is focused on the daily management of nightlife and retail consumer engagement programs in market. Attention to detail is fundamental, and the implementation of controls, process, policy, and compliance is essential. The Associate position supports all aspects of activity in market and reports to the Market Manager. The Market Associate in this role must be comfortable working with the marketing and promotions of wine and spirits products.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>PRIMARY RESPONSIBILITIES:
</b></p>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Recruit and hire part-time brand ambassadors and event specialists to execute alcohol sampling events in bars, retail outlets, and event spaces
</li>
<li>Train staff to actively engage consumers and sample consumers at the point of purchase
</li>
<li>Book the right staff for the right accounts, events, and brand experiences
</li>
<li>Manage point of sale inventory and materials for events
</li>
<li>Coordinate kitting, shipping, and delivery of POS and assets for all event activations
</li>
<li>Ensure brand staff enter event recaps into online report portals in an accurate and timely manner
</li>
<li>Attend and audit events to ensure executional excellence
</li>
<li>Make ongoing recommendations to improve event quality
</li>
<li>Demonstrate flexibility and resourcefulness
</li>
<li>Adhere to standard operating procedures, policies, and protocols for issue resolution
</li>
<li>Communicate and socialize success, learnings, and opportunities
</li>
<li>Report and recap data and insights for real time measurement &amp; evaluation
</li>
<li>Be content minded and able to manage social media channel(s) to amplify events and event execution
</li>
<li>Approve and submits staffing payroll and expenses
</li>
<li>Provide ongoing market insights and competitive intel
</li>
</ul>
