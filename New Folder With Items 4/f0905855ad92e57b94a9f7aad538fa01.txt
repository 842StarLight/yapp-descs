<h2>Digital Account Executive</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><i>Do you thrive in a fast paced, dynamic environment? Does the thought of working for a leading digital agency and directly contributing to its growth excite you?</i></p>
<p>
If so, we have a new opportunity for a Digital Account Executive who can provide superior customer service through consultative communication. The Digital Account Executive will be responsible for up selling and cross selling, improving the customer experience, and positively interacting with other departments to resolve customer concerns and complaints. As a Digital Account Executive, you will be a critical component of maximizing sales and growing accounts nationwide.</p>
<p><b>
Position Accountabilities:</b></p>
<p><i>
Account Management</i></p>
<ul>
<li>
Has a firm understanding of the clients' industry, marketing strategy and goals as it applies to lead generation for the client</li>
<li>
Works with Client Service Account Executive to grow revenue in assigned territory: appointment setting, upselling, retention</li>
<li>
Prospect potential clients in a designated market territory</li>
<li>
Presenting an array of products, and ensuring proper follow up with clients and leadership</li>
</ul>
<p><i>
Client Relationship Management and Communication</i></p>
<ul>
<li>
Provides professional, informed, and timely responses to internal and external contacts</li>
<li>
Establishes customer rapport, building trust, and maintaining long term relationships</li>
<li>
Develops a thorough knowledge and understanding of current products, and the ability to communicate them effectively to our customers</li>
<li>
Responsible for the effective and efficient documentation, communication and dissemination of information regarding client activities and client administrative procedures</li>
</ul>
<p><i>
Knowledge and Industry Relations</i></p>
<ul>
<li>
Demonstrates in depth knowledge of DAC's product offerings, including digital and directional products</li>
<li>
Remains knowledgeable about the on-going program activities after a program (print or digital) has been launched</li>
<li>
Ability to explain basic advertising, digital media and marketing concepts (SEM, SEO)</li>
<li>
Maintain Google AdWords Certifications</li>
</ul>
