<h2>Regional Account Manager - Specialty</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<b>Job Summary</b><br>
<br>
Responsible for the promotion of Walgreen’s specialty pharmacy services to targeted physicians, nurses, practice managers and other referral sources in assigned territory. Works with clients to support and understand their overall business objectives and critical success factors. Establishes and utilizes professional relationships, internally and externally, to generate support for key initiatives, challenges, and opportunities within the territory. Develops and implements strategies and tactics to consistently met or exceed quarterly sales goals. Works across highly matrixed field organization including, but not limited to, store operations, managed markets sales, government affairs, etc.<br>
<br>
<b>Job Responsibilities</b><ul>
<li>
<b>
Business Acumen </b>– Demonstrates the analytical, marketplace, and clinical knowledge and skills required for the development of strategies to grow revenues. Exhibits strong technical proficiency in terms of the specialty pharmacy space and the services provided by Walgreens and AllianceRx Walgreens Prime. Has a strong understanding of the competitive landscape and how to position Walgreens and AllianceRx Walgreens Prime as a primary choice of pharmacy. Has a strong understanding of the market access environment and knows how to be successful with local challenges and opportunities. Uses all analytical resources and tools available to identify opportunities and challenges within territory and build an action plan.
</li>
<li>
<b>Business Planning</b> - Demonstrates an ability to build, adapt and follow-through on an effective business plan to achieve and exceed quarterly sales goals. Re-evaluates and adapts with changing market conditions. Invests time and effort in the areas with the greatest return on investment. Prioritizes and stays focused on end goals in order to maximize productivity efforts.</li>
<li>
<b>
Account Management</b> – Uses all available information to understand customers’ needs and to identify opportunities. Understands the complexity of an account and connect the dots on where and who can influence decisions. Creates account specific strategies and goals to maintain, grow and/or stabilize each individual account. Evaluates, tracks and adjusts plan and goals as needed.
</li>
<li>
<b>Influence and Impact</b> - Uses a wide variety of communication methods, selling skills, and promotional tools to appropriately influence and impact results. Can adjust messaging and style based on customer relationship (i.e. prospect, emerging, established). Listens, communicates and shares information around Walgreens Specialty Pharmacy services and AllianceRx Walgreens Prime to develop trust that ultimately results in customer commitment.</li>
<li>
<b>
Business Partnering</b> - Leverages resources within Walgreens sales and operations organizations—including Retail Management, Community Pharmacy, AllianceRx Walgreens Prime, Sales Operations, Marketing, senior management, clinical staff and other functions to assist in both addressing customer issues and expanding local business.</li>
</ul>
Walgreens, one of the nation's largest drugstore chains, is included in the Retail Pharmacy USA Division of Walgreens Boots Alliance, Inc., the first global pharmacy-led, health and wellbeing enterprise. More than 10 million customers interact with Walgreens each day in communities across America, using the most convenient, multichannel access to consumer goods and services and trusted, cost-effective pharmacy, health and wellness services and advice. Walgreens operates 8,175 drugstores with a presence in all 50 states, the District of Columbia, Puerto Rico and the U.S. Virgin Islands. Walgreens omnichannel business includes Walgreens.com. Approximately 400 Walgreens stores offer Healthcare Clinic or other provider retail clinic services.<br>
<br>
As the neighborhood drugstore and retailer, our goal is to make health and happiness simpler, easier and within reach. And we remain a trusted wellness provider offering convenient access to important health services, such as immunizations and an array of pharmacy services that can help patients improve their health. To our team members, Walgreens represents a unique opportunity to excel in their careers in a welcoming and inclusive environment. We offer the chance to work in a truly supportive environment, and be a part of a progressive organization dedicated to the well-being of our customers, team members and the communities we all call home.<br>
