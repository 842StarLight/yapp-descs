<h2>Director of Enterprise Sales</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>About The Opportunity
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div>Here at Grubhub we’ve been dedicated to giving diners the most convenient way to order food from their favorite restaurants (whether that’s a late night Chalupa from Taco Bell or a salad for lunch from a local restaurant the day after they enjoyed said late night Chalupa).</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
While we are food-obsessed, we are also customer-obsessed. We look to constantly innovate our technology so our diners’ food experience is memorable, restaurant owners get more business and individuals across the country looking for part-time work can deliver the food from the restaurant to the diner flawlessly. We take great pride in knowing that we are a part of 17+ million diners food ordering experience and we are partnered with 105,000 restaurants in 2,000 US cities across our suite of apps (Grubhub, LevelUp, Seamless, Tapingo, Eat24).</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
Want to be a part of the biggest movement in the US that is moving eating forward? If so, we want to talk to you - and hear what’s your favorite restaurant for food delivery!</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div><b>
Summary:</b></div>
<div>
Leads the Enterprise Sales Team for the full suite of Grubhub Enterprise Cloud products including Marketplace listing, rewards &amp; loyalty, Delivery-as-a Service, and more. Targets include any restaurants with over 25 units and $50M in annual sales. This role is responsible for establishing sales territories, quotas and goals, and establish training programs for sales representatives.
</div>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Some Challenges You’ll Tackle
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<ul>
<li>Accelerating the growth of the Enterprise team and revenue through selling Grubhub’s products and services</li>
<li>
Will be responsible for building the sales strategy and the team to support this plan</li>
<li>
Building a team compensation structure that drives productivity, loyalty, and sound economics</li>
<li>
Building relationships and selling consultatively to drive buying decisions of VP, Director, and C-Level executives at Enterprise restaurants</li>
<li>
Participating and leading client and prospect meetings</li>
<li>
Managing pipeline and sales activity with an ability to accurately forecast</li>
<li>
Fostering a learning culture, focused on high-performance, agility, and creativity within the team</li>
<li>
Recruiting, developing, training, and motivating a group of high achieving A-players, based on Grubhub’s company values</li>
<li>
Finding creative solutions to challenging problems</li>
<li>
Effectively communicating thoughts and ideas with peers and leaders</li>
<li>
Willing to risk comfort level and step into unknown projects/tasks</li>
<li>
And other exciting projects assigned by your manager</li>
</ul>
</div></div></div>

</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
