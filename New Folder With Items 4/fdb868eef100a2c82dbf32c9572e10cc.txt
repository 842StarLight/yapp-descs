<h2>Enterprise Customer Account Executive</h2>

<p>At Khoros, our passion is to help the world’s best brands create customers for life. We build products we’re proud of, and we’re passionate about customer success.</p>
<p>
As an <b>Enterprise</b> <b>Customer</b> <b>Account Executive</b>, you will work closely with Khoros customers, helping them understand the value of our solutions and platform. The successful candidate will understand the customer’s definition of business value and be able to challenge them to think about new ways to differentiate and transform their business. You'll be able to inspire customers by providing thought-provoking and consultative strategies to help some of the world’s most iconic brands to better engage with their customers. The perfect candidate is an intelligent team player with high-energy and a strong interest in social business. As part of the Vista Equity family, you’ll receive best in class development opportunities and the ability to work with customers like Google, Microsoft, Disney and Sprint.</p>
<p>
The Khoros engagement platform comprises Online Community, Social Media Management and Messaging products for social customer service, social support and social media marketing teams to listen, respond and act on customer conversations – creating deep relationships and fostering brand loyalty and advocacy.</p>
<p>
The best Fortune 1000 companies in the world trust Khoros to help them build closer, more personalized and trusted relationships with their customers.
