<p>IQVIA™ is the leading human data science company focused on helping healthcare clients find unparalleled insights and better solutions for patients. Formed through the merger of IMS Health and Quintiles, IQVIA offers a broad range of solutions that harness the power of healthcare data, domain expertise, transformative technology, and advanced analytics to drive healthcare forward.</p>
<p><b>Overview</b></p>
<p>
IQVIA R&amp;D Technologies provides the leading cloud-based AI-powered data and analytics clinical technology platform that harnesses big data for clinical integration, predictive analytics, and business intelligence. The Orchestrated Clinical Trials SaaS platform is the first of its kind to empower organizations to more effectively utilize their data to improve trigger a series of orchestrated automation around patient-centric workflows and supporting trial automation to competitively lead precision therapies to market smarter and faster. At IQVIA everything we do is centered around the patients. From Virtual Trials in bringing the patient to the trials to leveraging connected device to improve the patient experience to improve engagement and retention. By connecting disparate data and unlocking incorporating valuable actionable insights from multiple sources of data, fundamentally reimaging the patient experience radically improving the patient experience, enhancing clinical trial productivity. Reimagine and evaluate the possibilities to unify your data in a novel meaningful way via IQVIA’s Orchestrated Clinical Technology platform.</p>
<p><b>
About the role</b></p>
<p>
We are currently hiring a <b>Director</b> to join the R&amp;D Digital Strategy team in the UK.</p>
<p>
For Global Pharma, Biotech and Medical device companies, the platform enables them to pull clinical, regulatory, compliance and commercial data in new ways. As a member of the R&amp;D Digital Strategy team you will join us in our mission to improve lives and give hope by delivering innovative solutions that address the most pressing patient and site challenges through data and cognitive insights. As an IQVIA R&amp;D Digital Health Solution Sales Director, you will be informing and advancing the digital health strategy of Life Sciences and Biopharma companies, leveraging the IQVIA Orchestrated Clinical Technology platform.</p>
<p>
You will be defining, scoping and closing deals that help our clients engage with patients. You will be an expert in digital health, someone who has a proven track record in using digital health to engage with stakeholders (patients, providers, payers, caregivers) to deliver meaningful results and business value. You are looked upon as a digital health expert, a pioneer of new technology and a change agent. You are a trusted adviser, someone who can lead clients from education, through ideation to implementation.</p>
