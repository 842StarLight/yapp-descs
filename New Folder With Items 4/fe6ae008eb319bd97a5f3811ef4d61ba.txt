<b>Job Description</b><br>
<p>At Wells Fargo, we want to satisfy our customers’ financial needs and help them succeed financially. We’re looking for talented people who will put our customers at the center of everything we do. Join our diverse and inclusive team where you’ll feel valued and inspired to contribute your unique skills and experience.</p>
<p>
Help us build a better Wells Fargo. It all begins with outstanding talent. It all begins with you.</p>
<p>
The <b>Payments, Virtual Solutions, and Innovation (PVSI) </b>team includes Operations, Consumer Credit Card, Retail Services, Consumer Deposits, the Innovation Group, Treasury Management, Merchant Services, Marketing and Wells Fargo Virtual Channels and the professional services teams that partner with these businesses. PVSI's focus is on delivering the next generation of payments and deposit capabilities and advancing our digital and online offerings to design new customer experiences and products. Our guiding principles are to start with the customer, move faster, and partner effectively.</p>
<p>
Treasury Management is a key part of our Payments, Virtual Solutions and Innovation Team. Treasury Management includes managing and optimizing our customers' cash flow and mitigating its operational, financial, and reputational risk. We focus on creating operating efficiencies, cost savings and time savings for our customers by delivering treasury solutions through a consultative approach.</p>
<p>
We put our customers at the center of every decision we make by focusing on their needs, being a leader in innovation, keeping things simple, and making our solutions easy to implement.</p>
<p>
This role sits on the Commercial Banking team, focusing on various industries within the Sacramento CA market.</p>
<p>
Experienced sales consultant position responsible for development and retention of Treasury Management business for an assigned customer base, geographic area, market segment or series of product lines. This position partners with relationship managers in a consultative selling role and is responsible for meeting annual sales activity targets by developing and executing strategy to retain and fully penetrate existing relationships. Develops sales plans for territory and key customer accounts and analyzes customer needs. Provides training on Treasury Management products and services to relationship managers as needed. Increased autonomy in managing assigned territory. Actively shares knowledge and market experience with peers and is a participant in Treasury Management initiatives.</p>
<p>
Location: Sacramento CA</p>
