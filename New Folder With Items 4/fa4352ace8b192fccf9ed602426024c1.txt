<h2>Physician Recruiter-PRN</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>Supports the physician and advanced practice provider recruitment, onboarding and retention functions for the Physician Enterprise.<br>
<br>
KEY RESPONSIBILITIES:<br>
1. Identifies potential sources of qualified candidates by researching job fairs/conferences to attend, searching the candidate database for prospects, posting positions on the PHC web site as well as association web sites.<div>
<br>
2. Publishes ads in association publications, creates a list of targeted candidates to send mailing or email campaigns to, as well as networking with program directors and other external sources used for physician recruitment.</div>
<div>
<br>
3. Analyzes the effectiveness of ads placed and sources used.</div>
<div>
<br>
4. Plans, participates and sets up recruitment activities including job fairs, campus visits, conferences/seminars, etc.</div>
<div>
<br>
5. Screens candidates for appropriate skills and abilities and completes a thorough phone interview prior to presenting to hiring manager.</div>
<div>
<br>
6. Escorts candidate to all of their interviews during his/her site visit.</div>
<div>
<br>
7. Manages the offer, contract, and onboarding of the candidate.</div>
<div>
<br>
8. Accountable for retention metrics.</div>
<div>
<br>
9. Maintains relationships and communicates often with hospital and practice executives.</div>
<div>
<br>
10. Coordinates, develops and leads on-boarding process for all newly hired providers.</div>
</div>
<br>
