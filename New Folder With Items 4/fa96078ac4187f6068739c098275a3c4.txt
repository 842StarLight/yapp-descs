<h2>E-Commerce Business Analyst</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>The E-Commerce Business Analyst is responsible for helping to drive growth of Weiman’s retail e-commerce business across Amazon and their international marketplaces, Walmart.com, Jet.com, eBay, Home Depot.com, etc. by supporting the day-to-day activities of these key accounts. This role will manage all product listings (content, bullets, etc.) to ensure we’re up-to-date and best in class to optimize our sales across the platforms. Additionally, the analyst will monitor competitive threats and pricing across the various retail sites and search out new ideas from products to content listing improvements. This role will report to the Retail E-Commerce Business Manager but will interface with many other members of Weiman’s Digital Business Team, a fast-moving autonomous organization within the company.</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Experience and Skill Sets:</b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<ul><li> 1+ year experience in digital marketing/merchandising, ideally in a consumer product environment</li></ul>
<ul><li> Digitally-savvy with a passion for e-commerce businesses</li></ul>
<ul><li> Familiarity with business and data analytics, including understanding profit margins and ROI</li></ul>
<ul><li> Exhibits superb attention to detail</li></ul>
<ul><li> Possess strong verbal and written communication skills</li></ul>
<ul><li> Brings high-energy attitude and embodies perseverance in daily work</li></ul>
<ul><li> Prior experience selling products on Amazon International Marketplaces, Walmart.com, Home Depot.com or EBay a plus</li></ul>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Job Responsibilities:</b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<ul><li> Manage product listings across retail e-commerce platforms of Amazon, Jet, Walmart.com, Home Depot.com etc. to ensure content and bullet points are up to date</li></ul>
<ul><li> Develop and optimize PPC advertising strategy on Amazon and Walmart platforms</li></ul>
<ul><li> Write bullet points and content descriptions to optimize search ranking on each relevant platform</li></ul>
<ul><li> Research competitive listings to highlight threats and opportunities from a product, pricing, and listing/content perspective</li></ul>
<ul><li> Execute product merges and variations in the Amazon portal under direction from manager</li></ul>
<ul><li> Serve as lead “case manager” within Amazon portal to drive issue resolution in a timely fashion</li></ul>
<ul><li> Learn all aspects of the Amazon portal and develop expertise with the reports and functionality contained therein</li></ul>
<ul><li> Pitch in to help out in key areas of need that arise within the Digital Business Team</li></ul>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Reports To: </b>Retail E-Commerce Business Manager</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Education:</b> Bachelor’s Degree Required</p>
<p><b>Working Location:</b> Gurnee, Illinois</p>
<p><b>Travel Required:</b> None<br>
<b>Employment Status:</b> Full-Time</p>
