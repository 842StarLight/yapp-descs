<h2>IT Services Architect</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>Offering an opportunity to be part of a high-performance team, enjoy exceptional benefits, and a supportive work-life balance, the <b>Gordon Flesch Company</b> is an employer of choice throughout WI, IL, IN and OH. The <b>Gordon Flesch Company</b> strives to recognize and appreciate our associate’s commitment, creativity, and the results they deliver every day for our customers and the community.</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>In this role you will:</b>
</p>
<ul>
<li>Assist the Managed IT sales team in winning new business that aligns with GFC’s target customer profile
</li>
<li>Design and present technical solutions aligned to customer needs; advising prospects on applicable value and trade-offs with solution options
</li>
<li>Collaborate with market vCIO on final technical solution to ensure long term client success
</li>
<li>Deliver effective recommendation presentations to prospective clients
</li>
<li>Ensure a smooth transition for clients from presale design into the implementation process; partnering with vendors and internal groups as needed
</li>
</ul>
<p><b>You should have:</b>
</p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree in computer science, information technology or related field or related experience
</li>
<li>5+ years experience with infrastructure technologies, networking, and security
</li>
<li>Excellent client interaction skills with the ability to articulate recommendations to decision makers including C-Level executives
</li>
<li>1-2 years of experience in cloud technologies
</li>
<li>25-40% travel required, some may be overnight
</li>
<li>Valid driver’s license
</li>
</ul>
<p>The <b>Gordon Flesch Company</b> offers Medical, Dental, Personal Time, Vacation Time, Holiday Time, Tuition Reimbursement, 401(k) and 401(k) match, a Wellness Program and many more!</p>
