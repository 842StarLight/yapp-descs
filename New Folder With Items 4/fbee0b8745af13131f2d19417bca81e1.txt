<h2>Territory Assistant (Part-Time)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">Jyve is a Skills-as-a-Service platform that matches enterprise businesses' in-store execution needs with the right skills at the right time. Operational in most of the U.S., Jyve offers distributors, brands, and retailers fast access to skilled Jyvers who are certified to handle tasks ranging including merchandising, ordering, brand ambassadorship, and e-fulfillment shopping.
<br><br>
The company was founded in 2015 and is based in San Francisco. Jyve recently closed its Series A round of funding, raising a total of $35M from notable investors including SignalFire, Crosscut Ventures, Ridge Ventures, and New Enterprise Associates.
<br><br>
The Territory Assistant is responsible for all aspects of executing the day-to-day relationship with the stores in an assigned territory. The Territory Assistant provides back-up territory coverage and assistance for Regional Sales Supervisors and Retail Sales Consultants during their vacations, absences or as otherwise needed.
<br><br>
<b>Responsibilities:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Accurately assess inventory needed in the store; write an accurate and timely order(s); review and approve all orders placed within the store with appropriate store personnel</li>
<li>Stock and rotate supplied product during the backup or coverage in the assigned territory. When applicable, identify lost sales opportunities (i.e. item voids, missing shelf tags, out of stocks at store-level, planogram integrity)</li>
<li>Ensure high levels of customer service by responding quickly and appropriately to customer inquiries in the assigned territory and providing product education and expertise to customers and consumers</li>
<li>Refer sales leads, customer feedback, and information on competitor activity and other pertinent findings to the Regional Sales Supervisor or Retail Sales Consultant to help them respond to changing market conditions and customer demands</li>
<li>Perform additional duties as assigned by the Regional Sales Supervisor or customers including, but not limited to: new item cut-in, category resets, code checks, shelf and product maintenance, emergency deliveries, and attend and participate in sales meetings as requested</li>
</ul>
<b>Qualifications:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>High School Diploma required; Bachelor' degree preferred</li>
<li>Minimum of two (2) year experience in sales of grocery products to chain accounts</li>
<li>Proficiency with Microsoft Office (Word, Excel, PowerPoint) required</li>
<li>Demonstrated strength in oral and written communications with people at all organizational levels</li>
<li>Demonstrated ability to effectively manage a variety of tasks simultaneously and prioritize those with the highest business impact</li>
<li>Can travel up to 90%, have a valid driver's license, clean driving record, and reliable transportation</li>
<li>Ability to perform repeated bending and lifting of up to 50 pounds</li>
</ul>
