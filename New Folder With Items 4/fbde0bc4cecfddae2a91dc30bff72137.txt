<h2>Sales and Marketing Operations Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<ul>
<li>3+ years of experience with Seller Engagement or Marketing</li>
<li>Bachelor degree or equivalent experience</li>
<li>Experience leading cross functional projects</li>
<li>Experience working autonomously</li>
</ul>
<br>
<div>The Sales and Marketing Operations Specialist will work on solutions to drive automated program adoption at scale to support 3P US Sellers moving into Amazon’s international markets. You will work directly with sales teams and other relevant stakeholders to identify areas of opportunity. You will be responsible for developing all aspects of the solution, including creating target lists, mass marketing materials and metrics tracking. You will also be responsible for any post-launch feature announcements and document writing.<br>
<br>
Roles and Responsibilities:<br>
<ul>
<li>Understand Seller expansion blockers and concerns and develop scalable solutions to address these issues</li>
<li>Collaborate cross-functionally with other internal teams to develop material and assets that can impact merchants at scale</li>
<li>Develop, deploy, and manage outbound merchant engagement campaigns</li>
<li>Identify patterns in merchant and customer data and behaviors, provide analysis to influence internal business decisions, and help develop engagement programs based on that analysis</li>
<li>Create, execute, and report on mass marketing campaigns that aim to educate and drive Seller action at scale</li>
<li>Utilize Salesforce to create and track relevant Seller records</li>
<li>Identify patterns among merchants’ business challenges and develop targeted, repeatable solutions</li>
<li>Identify common merchant interests and represent those interests to internal stakeholders</li>
<li>Develop a deep understanding of the current and future functionality of Amazon Global Selling to find and execute new opportunities</li>
</ul>
</div>

<li>Ability to work in fast-paced environment and resourceful in achieving success in the face of ambiguity</li>
