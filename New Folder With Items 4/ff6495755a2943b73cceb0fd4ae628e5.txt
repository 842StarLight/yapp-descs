<h2>Customer Service Representative</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Farmers insurance is hiring Customer Service Representatives and for several locations. Positions available in Escondido, Vista, Valley Center, Oceanside, San Marcos, La Jolla, and Temecula. We are looking for motivated individuals to join and grow with our local agencies. This is a great opportunity for those looking for a rewarding career in the insurance industry. Individuals bilingual in Spanish preferred.</p>
<p>Ideal candidates will have:</p>
<ul>
<li>Critical thinking and researching skills</li>
<li>Computer software skills</li>
<li>Written and oral communication skills and loves interacting with clients</li>
</ul>
<p>Compensation can vary greatly based on experience and we do hire at the entry level. Must pass state license exam within 60 days of hire at entry level. If you are already licensed, send in your resume!</p>
<br>
Salary Range: $30000.00 - $45000.00 per year<br>
<b>Responsibilities</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Process customer policy change requests.</li>
<li>Verify phone numbers, addresses and email addresses with each customer contact and update customer information.</li>
<li>Answer incoming phone calls on the first ring.</li>
<li>Schedule appointments for sales staff to meet prospective customers.</li>
<li>Responds to all inquiries, cancellation requests, and sales requests within specified timeframe.</li>
</ul>
<br>
<b>Requirements</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Possess an upbeat, positive and enthusiastic attitude.</li>
<li>Proficiency to multi-task, follow-thru and follow-up.</li>
<li>Great Customer Service Skills.</li>
<li>Driven and goal-oriented individual.</li>
<li>Ability to tactfully handle stressful and difficult situations.</li>
</ul>
