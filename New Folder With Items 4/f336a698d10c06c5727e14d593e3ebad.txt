<h2>VP, Technology Infrastructure & Operations US</h2>

<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Overview
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div>EXL Service (NASDAQ: EXLS) is a global end-to-end Insurance Administration provider with Life, Annuity and Health operations, headquartered in New York. EXL was incorporated in 1999 and has since grown to be one of the leading insurance service provider companies with its 29000 employees including 7000+ talented insurance domain experts and 300+ FLMI, CLU, CPCU, ChFC, RHU, REBC, Series 6 and Series 26 licensed professionals, 35+ service delivery centers across US, India, The Philippines, Malaysia, Czech Republic, Romania and Bulgaria.</div>
<div>
The LifePRO division of EXL is a team dedicated to the development and implementation of the EXL LifePRO insurance administration system for Life, Health and Annuity Business. LifePRO streamlines policy administration for insurance companies through features such as a flexible product engine, real-time customer service and extensive agent compensation support.</div>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div>The VP of Technology Infrastructure and Operation will be responsible for Technology Infrastructure Services, Design Architecture, Project Management, IT Security, Compliance, Technology Financial Management and Technology Operational Delivery to EXL Platform business units. Role includes implementation of industry technology best practices, standardization of tools &amp; services, compliance to regulatory &amp; client contractual requirements, effective implementation of IT general computing controls, excellence in operational delivery, technology financial management, drive technology modernization, supplier governance and client/business stakeholder management. The role will require to bring in very strong focus to alignment of IT general computing controls, IT process implementation &amp; adherence and any day audit readiness with in Platform Business units inline with EXL Enterprise Security Controls and Practices.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
The position operates in a matrix environment reporting directly to EXL’s Head of Technology for US, UK and LATAM Geography. The position will be responsible for managing delivery and driving governance with key stake holders such CTO’s Office, Platform Business Leaders and Platform Technology Leaders, etc. The position will be directly and indirectly responsible for management of 25+ employees across locations US, UK and India. At EXL, we emphasize innovation, world-class delivery and leading-edge technologies and thinking as a competitive advantage in our markets.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
