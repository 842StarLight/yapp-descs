<p></p>
<div><b>
Position Overview</b></div>
<div>
As a Sales Compensation Analyst/Manager, you will work to lead the financial modeling, development of sales compensation plans/incentive-based programs and subsequent performance management reporting for a large and diverse technology sales infrastructure. You will serve as the primary coordination point for sales, sales operations, finance and human resources relative to sales compensation models, performance management and strategies.<br>
<br>
</div>
<div>You will prepare financial models, ratios and metrics to actual performance and systematically document sales compensation plans within Executive Leadership’s existing strategy and framework. This position will exercise a high level of financial, sales, and business acumen.<br>
<br>
</div>
<div><b>Key Responsibilities</b></div>
<div><i>
The requirements listed below are representative of the knowledge, skill, and/or ability required. Reasonable accommodations may be made to enable individuals with disabilities to perform the essential functions.</i></div>
<div>
The <b>Sales Compensation Analyst/Manager</b><b><i> </i></b>is primarily responsible for:</div>
<div><ul>
<li>
Assisting in the short and long-term compensation strategy and execution, including, but not limited to job leveling, career-pathing, attraction, retention, reward and recognition, incentive design, communication methods, and education.</li>
<li>
Partnering with recruiting, HR business partners and management in evaluating jobs and benchmarking new hire and ongoing compensation.</li>
<li>
Partnering with finance, legal, and business development teams on relevant compensation issues.</li>
<li>
Assisting in annual and mid-year review cycles including design, administration, mentoring, distribution, and communication of compensation programs.</li>
<li>
Monitoring and interpreting FLSA and other international, federal, state and local laws regulating compliance for salary and pay policies.</li>
<li>
Providing excellent customer service to employees, management, and business partners.</li>
<li>
Documenting all compensation plans within executive framework and partner with HR for rollout and delivery to the sales organization</li>
<li>
Analyzing and reporting compensation expense based on monthly, quarterly and annual performance of individual performers, sales managers, sales directors and related indirect sales performers based on performance metrics</li>
<li>
Building financial models to measure changes in incentive compensation plans for annual plans</li>
<li>
Providing metrics and business analysis and recommendations to executive leadership to support decisions affecting sales compensation</li>
<li>
Regularly benchmark competitor pay practices and compensation plans</li>
</ul></div>
<div><b>
We are seeking candidates with the following experience and skills</b></div>

</ul>
