<h2>Quality Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>Thank you for your interest in Applied Thin-Film Products. Please send resumes to: hr@thinfilm.com.</div>
<br><div><div>
<p>Seeking a Quality Manager that will be responsible for the management of Quality Assurance operations, including inspection, testing, and customer interactions. This position will report directly to the President. The individual will be accountable in daily production meetings and involved in significant interface with Sales/Marketing, Production and Materials departments. This is a direct hire, full-time, regular day shift position with the full package of benefits offered the first of the month after hire.</p>
<div><b>Required Qualifications</b></div>
<ul><li>Bachelor’s degree or related experience.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Minimum five (5) years management experience.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Minimum ten (10) years working in a production/manufacturing environment.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Prior experience with ISO 9000 or AS9100 quality systems is required.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Experience in quality assurance of manufactured products. Preferably thin film, thick film, or pc board industry experience. Semiconductor manufacturing will also be considered.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Capable of creating and implementing improvements with procedures and personnel.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Epicor experience is very desirable; other software experience (such as Oracle, SAP, ASK) may substitute for Epicor experience; extra consideration given if individual participated in the implementation.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Experience with MIL-STD-883 is highly desirable.</li></ul>
<div><b>Desired Qualifications</b></div>
<ul><li>Change agent with a track record and experience in leading, developing, and implementing changes in operations that create efficiency.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Assertive and collaborative self-starter who can coach and organize the staff and hold them accountable.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Someone who can present messages and ideas in a confident and convincing manner. Ability to communicate effectively with subordinates, both verbally and in writing.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Problem solver who can collect information, determine root cause, and create a solution or plan for change.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Individual with good organizational skills who can create clear paths and stay on top of the department.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Individual who follows through to complete tasks in a timely manner and delivers on action items without prompting.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Team player with ability to work collaboratively with supervisor, peers, subordinates, and other departments.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Individual who is willing to “get their hands dirty.”</li></ul>
