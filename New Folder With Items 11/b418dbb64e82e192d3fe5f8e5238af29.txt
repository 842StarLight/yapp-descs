<h2>Customer Success Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>About Skyword</b></p>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
Skyword moves brands – and their content – forward. By embracing a sustainable, scalable approach to original storytelling, Skyword liberates brands from ineffective marketing practices and inspires them to create deeper connections with their customers. With a powerful combination of enterprise-class technology, authentic voices, and professional services, Skyword propels clients' content marketing programs to new levels of excellence. Skyword is a privately held company headquartered in Boston, MA, with offices in Washington DC, and Pittsburgh, PA.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div><b>
Job Summary</b></div>
<div>
The Customer Success Manager works with their Director to oversee a portfolio of clients and ensure each client is fully trained on the Skyword platform and set up for success. The most qualified candidates will be problem solvers, have a track record of getting things done and thrives off of customer satisfaction.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div><b>
Primary Duties and Responsibilities</b></div>
<ul>
<li><div>
Work with the Director, Customer Success to support and grow a portfolio of upwards of 50 clients by acting as primary point of contact for customer relationships</div></li>
<li><div>
Learn and master all aspects of current software platforms sold by Skyword</div></li>
<li><div>
Conduct both in-person and virtual trainings for a broad range of client stakeholders</div></li>
<li><div>
Support the Sales team by providing platform expertise during pitch process</div></li>
<li><div>
Configure and customize Skyword platforms to address client challenges and support their content marketing objectives</div></li>
<li><div>
Partner with internal and external technology teams and facilitate seamless integrations of technologies</div></li>
<li><div>
Use layered-in technology to monitor and oversee clients' usage of our platforms and encourage adoption of all features</div></li>
<li><div>
Educate the client on feature enhancements and evolution of current platforms</div></li>
<li><div>
Identify opportunities to expand the client relationship and drive contract renewals</div></li>
<li><div>
Work internally, across departments, to deliver compelling solutions for our clients</div></li>
<li><div>
Report and escalate issues and delays to management as needed</div></li>
<li><div>
Occasional travel is required</div></li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<br>
<div><b>
Qualifications</b></div>
<ul>
<li><div>
2-4 years of client management experience</div></li>
<li><div>
2-4 years of Customer Success experience at SaaS or technology company</div></li>
<li><div>
Familiarity using different CMSs (e.g., WordPress, Drupal, Adobe) and technologies (e.g. HTML, JIRA, Asana)</div></li>
<li><div>
Experience conducting software trainings for customers</div></li>
<li><div>
Experience in digital marketing, online content, and/or SEO preferred</div></li>
<li><div>
Advanced knowledge of Microsoft Word, Excel and PowerPoint</div></li>
<li><div>
Strong organizational skills, written and verbal communication are critical to success</div></li>
<li><div>
Self-starter approach to work, with an eagerness to take responsibility and consistently exceed objectives</div></li>
<li><div>
Able to analyze issues and develop a clear action plan</div></li>
<li><div>
Able to work individually and build partnerships and foster teamwork</div></li>
<li><div>
Able to thrive and be flexible in a rapidly changing start-up environment</div></li>
<li><div>
Comfort with technology and the ability to discuss basic Internet technologies with clients</div></li>
</ul>
