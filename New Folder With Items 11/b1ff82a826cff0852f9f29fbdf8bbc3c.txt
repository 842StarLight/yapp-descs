<div><b>
Job Summary</b></div>
<ul><li><div>
Manages the day-to-day responsibilities associated with accommodating guests' arrivals, departures and requirements in the bathing areas and locker rooms of the spa.</div></li></ul>
<br>
<div><b>
General Accountabilities</b></div>
<ul>
<li><div>
Greets and directs guests to bathing areas/lockers.</div></li>
<li><div>
Schedules, cancels and confirms reservations for services.</div></li>
<li><div>
Makes self available for any guest need while in the locker/Bathing areas. (towels, uniforms, slippers, services, etc)</div></li>
<li><div>
Upholds and enforces Island Spa rules &amp; regulations within the bathing area, ensuring the safety of all the customers without direct surveillance.</div></li>
<li><div>
Transmits and/or receives messages.</div></li>
<li><div>
Tests and chemically balances the tubs every two hours to comply with health code</div></li>
<li><div>
Double checks tub temperatures every two hours</div></li>
<li><div>
Keeps records of occupied and vacant therapist rooms and guests' accounts.</div></li>
<li><div>
Answers inquiries pertaining to spa services and accommodations of spa.</div></li>
<li><div>
Handles guest complaints or concerns, seeks help from Floor Manager if needed.</div></li>
<li><div>
Responds to inquiries and requests for information requiring knowledge of departmental and company policies and procedures and services</div></li>
<li><div>
Driving sales of products and services offered</div></li>
<li><div>
Keeps all work areas up to Island Spa Standards of cleanliness and hygiene.</div></li>
<li><div>
Able to work flexible schedule, varied shifts and days, including holidays and weekends.</div></li>
<li><div>
Highly suggested to try spa services to better serve customers</div></li>
<li><div>
Handles reservation turning for body scrub employees</div></li>
</ul>
