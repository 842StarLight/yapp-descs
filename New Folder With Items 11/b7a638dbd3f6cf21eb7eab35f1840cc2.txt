<h2>Freight Claims Analyst</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>Tronex International, Inc., headquartered in Mt. Olive, New Jersey, is a world-class global sourcing, sales and marketing, and distribution enterprise. As a multiple award-winning global brand and innovator of disposable safety and Personal Protective Equipment (PPE), Tronex delivers optimal cross-contamination control and infection-prevention solutions for major Healthcare, Foodservice, Hospitality, Manufacturing, Retail, and Government organizations worldwide for 30 years. Our advanced PPE offerings include full lines of superior quality disposable examination, food-safe, multipurpose, and specialty work gloves; head-to-toe disposable protective apparel; face masks; plus wound care and surgical/operating room infection-control products.<br>
<br>
Tronex produces world-class products, best-in-class services and customer experiences, and delivers unparalleled value and brand experiences for our customers. It is our dedication to these core values that enables Tronex to excel and sustain three decades of growth and progression in an ultra-competitive marketplace.<br>
<br>
The Freight Claims Analyst will have a variety of responsibilities, including, but not limited to freight claims research, processing, reporting and analytics. This includes the timely and accurate resolution of OS&amp;D issues and deductions, communication with the Sales, Customer Service, Operations, and Accounting teams, other business partners and customers, and analysis of trends to protect company assets. While freight claims will be the initial focus for this position, there is considerable opportunity for advancement and growth within the Supply Chain group.<br>
<br>
<b>Key Responsibilities</b><b>:</b>
<div>
<ul><li>Review, research, validate and resolve customer OS&amp;D deductions in a timely manner, recognizing the urgency of dealing with these situations quickly</li></ul>
<ul><li>Coding and entry of validated deductions in the system
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Monitor and follow through all pending claims and supporting documents promptly. Ensure proof of claim / PODs are properly filed for audit purposes</li></ul>
<ul><li>Analysis, issue resolution and problem solving of delivery and deduction issues with customers related to OS&amp;D, including timely communication with internal customers</li></ul>
<ul><li>Respond quickly as the first point of contact for internal stakeholders with questions regarding OS&amp;D claims. This includes working with Sales, Customer Service, Operations, and Accounting personnel, external carriers, Logistics and AR team as needed to ensure high customer service levels</li></ul>
<ul><li>Based on trending analysis and insights, provide timely recommendations to internal stakeholders on actions to leverage opportunities or mitigate risks</li></ul>
<ul><li>Work with other departments to determine root cause for issues</li></ul>
<ul><li>Analyze, identify and implement new procedures to prevent errors from occurring</li></ul>
<ul><li>Establish and refine Standard Operating Procedures (SOPs) to ensure consistency and high service levels</li></ul>
<ul><li>Continuously evaluate carrier performance based on total cost comparison and present findings and recommendations to the Freight Manager</li></ul>
<ul><li>Provide routine reporting relative to OS&amp;D</li></ul>
<ul><li>Perform ad hoc analysis as needed</li></ul>
</div>
