<h2>Director of Marketing</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">Position Summary
<br>-
<br><br>
Atlanta-based insurance brokerage specializing in commercial and professional lines for architecture, engineering and law firms is seeking a Marketing Director. This position will support our Greyling Insurance brand, which is a division of EPIC Insurance Brokers &amp; Consultants and will be responsible for the marketing process from research &amp; planning to execution &amp; analysis. Qualified candidates must have a portfolio of successful marketing plans, ability to be a hands-on self-starter that can develop an effective marketing practice by working with the Greyling business for content creation, EPIC corporate marketing for partnership and collaboration and report directly to the Greyling Managing Principals.
<br><br>
Position Responsibilities
<br>-
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Collaborate with leadership and marketing team to develop and implement marketing strategies that generate leads and support business initiatives</li>
<li>Participate in the development of branding and corporate identity efforts and drive adherence to brand standards</li>
<li>Responsible for developing and implementing content for print or digital media, including sales collateral, articles, web pages, presentations, social media, interviews, case studies and more</li>
<li>Define and scale digital plans for social, search, email, web &amp; advertising that drive business efforts</li>
<li>Understand the changing landscape of marketing platforms and how to effectively launch multi-channel campaigns</li>
<li>Plan and manage events for clients, speaking engagements and other similar opportunities</li>
<li>Develop analytics reports and presentations to showcase audience growth, campaign success, and ROI</li>
<li>Responsible for developing and managing a marketing budget</li>
<li>Activate and apply marketing concepts (brand positioning, SWOT analysis, competitive assessment, market research, brand experience) to the business</li>
<li>Coordinate with corporate marketing team on leveraging tools, support, processes, and best practices</li>
</ul>
