What You'll Do
<br><br>
We are currently seeking a highly motivated, customer support lead to join our global Customer Support Team. The ideal candidate will have a strong background and a proven track record in resolving customer issues in a fast-paced environment. The candidate will be accountable for driving the resolution of product issues, through a documented process, and ensuring the highest level of customer satisfaction.
<br><br>
Uptake Customer Support team offers 24x7 support coverage for our customers via Email and Phone. We have Customer representatives across 2 different cities (Chicago &amp; Bangalore) and our mission is to create delightful experiences for our customers through every interaction that they have with Uptake Support.
<br><br>
Responsibilities
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Resolve product support tickets directly or through first-level customer support representatives.</li>
<li>Troubleshoot and test products to replicate problems reported from the customer.</li>
<li>Demonstrate attention to detail when coding and documenting tickets, which is essential for accurate reporting and maintaining historical ticket data.</li>
<li>Monitor assigned open tickets, contact customers, and update tickets on a timely basis.</li>
<li>Provide responsive support to clients, either during normal business hours or after normal business hours (when scheduled).</li>
<li>Work early/late shifts or be on-call when required.</li>
<li>Ensure a fast time to contact and time to resolution on all support issues, as well as the highest level of customer satisfaction.</li>
<li>Maintain expert domain knowledge of Uptake products, as well as any new future solutions.</li>
<li>Collaborate and communicate with support team members to troubleshoot customer issues or transfer knowledge.</li>
<li>Help improve customer service processes when we identify gaps.</li>
</ul>

<li>A team player attitude with an eagerness to learn more</li>
