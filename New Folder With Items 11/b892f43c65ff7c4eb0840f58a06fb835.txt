<h2>Community Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>High Associates LTD. residential property management division is seeking an experienced individual to manage our Bentley Ridge community located in Lancaster, PA. The Community Manager shall be responsible for the managing, operating, and maintaining the luxurious multifamily community. Will work with the Regional Manager on the company’s objectives, annual budget, and with corporate leadership to determine property operating objectives and plans to achieve them, developing the property team members to maximize performance, and taking a hands-on approach to any necessary duties to accomplish objectives as well as to facilitate the successful performance of others by performing the following duties personally or though subordinates.</p>
<p><b>
Responsibilities:</b></p>
<p><b>
Operations</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Inspect each building at least once each week. Maintain daily records of inspections and provide follow-up supervision to ensure corrective action is taken as needed. Inspection areas include, but are not limited to, all buildings, grounds, and other common and public areas to ensure that employees are performing their assigned duties.</li>
<li>Continuously be aware of safety practices and procedures. Ensure that the Community Safety Program is effective and proactive.</li>
<li>Conduct and have recorded monthly meetings with all staff members.</li>
<li>Coordinate with the Regional or District Manager on all housing violations, any special tenant arrangements or potential problems.</li>
<li>Ensure that HIGH Company’s policy regarding Fair Housing is understood and followed by all employees and contractors and that necessary Fair Housing notices are posted as required by local, state and federal regulations.</li>
<li>Follow emergency procedures as outlined in HIGH Company manuals and policy guidelines. Notify next level of management and proper authorities immediately of emergencies (e.g. fire).</li>
<li>Inspect vacated apartments and decide the extent of turnover repairs/redecorating that will be required. (Inspections may only be delegated with the written approval of the Regional or District Manager).</li>
<li>Inspect and approve vacant apartments that are ready for move in.</li>
<li>Monitor stock levels and project supplies and replacement items required for property operations and building maintenance in coordination with the maintenance superintendent.</li>
<li>Monitor work of contractors and report findings/concerns to the Regional or District Manager.</li>
<li>Actively manage and participate in our Signature Legendary Sales and Service Training program. Achieve 90% or better as a team on all 14 measured components of the magic formula for sales calls.</li>
<li>Approve, approve with conditions, or decline all rental applications. Assure compliance with Scorex guidelines. Exceptions must be presented to Regional or District Manager for consideration.</li>
<li>Assure that lease or rental agreements are being properly completed and renewed in a timely manner.</li>
<li>Assure that all offices are kept clean and neat.</li>
<li>Assure that equipment is kept in proper working condition and repairs are made as necessary. Replace equipment as necessary as coordinated and approved with the Regional Manager.</li>
<li>Keep common areas (e.g. halls, stairways, community room, grounds, play grounds, etc.) clean and in good repair. Repair unsafe items immediately, or mark to ensure no person is injured by the item.</li>
<li>Assure that policy and procedure changes are communicated to the employees who need to know or as directed by the District Manager.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Human Resources</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Hire, train, and equip the best employees in the industry.</li>
<li>Supervise all on-site personnel (either directly or indirectly).</li>
<li>Provide staff with leadership and supervision which motivates employees to effectively perform.</li>
<li>Constantly coach and mentor employees on individual and community goals.</li>
<li>Provide new employees with a thorough introduction of the community and HIGH Company so that they understand what is expected of them, the importance of their contribution to the community, how they fit into the team, and the importance of working safely.</li>
<li>Ensure employees receive on the job training and other training opportunities.</li>
<li>Review employee performance and schedule training programs as needed.</li>
<li>Make recommendations for the advancement and promotion of above average employees.</li>
<li>Make recommendations for the termination of non-qualified employees in coordination with the Regional or District Manager and the Director of Human Resources.</li>
<li>Ensure employees understand the content and follow HIGH Company’s Employee Handbook.</li>
<li>Report all on the job employee injuries to the HR department at the Main Office the same day that they occur.</li>
<li>Assure that employee bulletin board(s) are orderly and up-to-date, and all necessary federal and state posters and HIGH Company notices are posted for employee information.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Marketing</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Oversee the daily marketing/sales/customer service effort to ensure maximum potential is reached.</li>
<li>Have proper “tools” available for the marketing effort (floor plans, area information, transportation schedules, etc.)</li>
<li>Keep vacant units clean and in market ready condition at all times.</li>
<li>Supervise all outreach programs.</li>
<li>Review first impression items monthly, e.g., entrance signs, office, professional attire, and model appearance to ensure a positive impression.</li>
<li>Review shopping reports with Marketing Representatives and provide guidance on how to improve.</li>
<li>Review monthly sales/financial reports with the Regional or District Manager and recommend adjustments as needed.</li>
<li>Ensure that all residents receive a resident referral reminder bimonthly.</li>
<li>Participate in the development of the advertising plan for the community.</li>
<li>Review and analyze market survey information and make pricing recommendations.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Resident Relations</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Insure active resident relations programs are in place<b>.
</b>
</li>
<li>Aggressively pursue resident retention.</li>
<li>Ensure staff is providing superior customer service to all residents.</li>
<li>Supervise resident services.</li>
<li>Ensure that someone on the management team meets all new residents prior to, or at, occupancy.</li>
<li>Interview those residents who present challenges/problems that the Assistant Community Manager or other staff employees cannot resolve and attempt to resolve them and/or make recommendations to the Regional or District Manager on how the matter can best be resolved.</li>
<li>Assure prompt and positive action on all resident complaints.</li>
<li>Continually stress customer service and courtesy by all staff members in their contact with residents and prospective residents.</li>
<li>Assure proper dress neatness and personal cleanliness by the entire staff is always maintained, (e.g. uniforms for grounds/maintenance, proper business/career apparel for administrative employees).</li>
<li>Follow-up with residents after maintenance has been performed through phone calls, personal notes, etc.</li>
<li> Communicate with your residents about community news. Note: newsletters and all nonstandard letters to resident are to be approved by the Regional or District Manager.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Accounting Procedures</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Insure that purchases are made in accordance with current HIGH Company policy. Assure that vendors are approved and that purchasing procedures are followed.</li>
<li>Process all property invoices timely. Invoices over $1000 other than routine maintenance agreements, require second approval by the Regional or District Manager.</li>
<li>Assure that all parts, equipment and supplies purchased are delivered and that any outside service work is performed satisfactorily before processing the invoice for payment.</li>
<li>Assure that there is a daily bank deposit of all checks and money orders received.</li>
<li>Assure that petty cash is secured and properly accounted for.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Supervisory</b></p>
<ul>
<li>May manage several subordinate supervisors who supervise the community’s employees. Is responsible for the overall direction, coordination, and evaluation of these supervisors. May also directly supervise non-supervisory employees.</li>
<li>Carries out supervisory responsibilities in accordance with HIGH Company policies and applicable laws. Responsibilities include, but are not limited to, interviewing, hiring, and training employees; planning, assigning, and directing work; appraising performance; coaching, rewarding and disciplining employees; addressing complaints and resolving problems and issues.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>The successful candidate will possess the following qualifications:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Associate degree (A.A.) or equivalent from two-year college or technical school is preferred.</li>
<li>5 years’ experience in residential property management.</li>
<li>Familiar with federal, state, and jurisdictional laws and regulations concerning multi-family housing, Fair Housing and employee safety.</li>
<li>Ability to read, analyze and interpret general business periodicals, professional journals, technical procedures, or government regulations.</li>
<li>Ability to write reports, business correspondence and procedure manuals.</li>
<li>Ability to effectively present information and respond to questions from groups of managers, clients, customers and the public.</li>
<li>IREM certification is high desirable.</li>
</ul>

</ul>
