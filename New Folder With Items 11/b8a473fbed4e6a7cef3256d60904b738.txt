<h2>Outside Sales Representative</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<div><div><div>
<p>Generates revenue and/or profit margin in assigned industrial accounts, product lines or territories. Interacts with established customers and develops new prospects.</p>
<br>
<p><br>
<b>Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Ensures high post-sales satisfaction facilitating positive long-term relationships and high potential for repeat business with customers.</li>
<li>Establishes account and territory sales plans to meet annual, quarterly, or monthly goals.</li>
<li>Maintains knowledge of all major product lines and the specifications of each. Prices items and accessories, and produces estimates and quotes.</li>
<li>Prepares sales report regarding quotes and sales activity for Sales Manager.</li>
<li>Gathers preliminary lien information from customers on all sales over $3,000.</li>
<li>Manages contacts and opportunity pipeline using the Company’s Customer Relationship Management (CRM) software. Produces quotes using the Company’s Configure Price Quote (CPQ) and Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP) software.</li>
<li>Participates in entertainment functions with customers, trade shows and seminars.</li>
<li>Assists in the semi-annual physical inventory.</li>
<br>
</ul>
</div></div></div>
<p></p>
<div>Qualifications</div>
<div><div>
<div>
<ul>
<li>3+ years of experience in outside sales - Required</li>
<li>Bachelor’s Degree in Engineering or Construction management from an accredited university, or equivalent precast/construction work experience - Preferred</li>
<li>Proficient with Microsoft Office (Email, Spreadsheet, Word Processor) - Required</li>
<li>Strong communications skills to include both verbal and written communication - Required</li>
</ul>
<p>Equal Opportunity Employer</p>
<p>We offer competitive compensation, a comprehensive benefits package that includes medical, vision and dental insurance, paid holidays, 401k plan and more.</p>
