Fireye – Workday Posting Template Fireye - Derry, NH Customer Support / Project Manager Fireye, located in Derry, NH is part of the Carrier organization; a leading provider of heating, ventilating, air conditioning and refrigeration systems, building controls and automation, and fire and security systems leading to safer, smarter, sustainable and high-performance buildings. Carrier is a unit of United Technologies Corp., a leading provider to the aerospace and building systems industries worldwide. The Fire &amp; Security group at Carrier is dedicated to providing product and technology designed to protect people and property from the effects of fire and its related hazards. As the world’s largest manufacturer of fire safety products, the Fire Safety Solutions group meets the safety needs of homes and businesses by providing fire extinguishers, smoke alarms, carbon monoxide alarms, mechanical locking systems, fire alarm control panels, gas and flame detection, fire suppression systems and mass notification solutions. Notable brands within the group are Kidde, Firex, Badger, Vigilant, Fenwal, Det-Tronics, Fireye and Edwards. Description: The Customer Support / Project Manager is responsible for assuming the lead on “special projects” that require providing actions above what is required for processing a typical order (e.g., generating drawings, specialty items, mutually agreed upon terms and conditions, etc.). This position will interface internally with diverse business unit functions, (e.g. engineering, sales, operations, service, etc.), as well as external customers and various sales/distribution channels on a global basis. Key secondary role will be performing Desktop Publishing functions as required for the Marketing Department. General Responsibilities of the Role:  Manage special projects from the initial customer Request for Quotation, clarification of customer requirements, proper selection of equipment, determine pricing based on previous quotes and acceptable Gross Margins, submittal of quote within requested time frame, through the acceptance and subsequent completion of all project deliverables (project specific drawings, certificates of compliance, hazardous area certifications, detailed information for ID tags, etc.).  Generate new and modify existing user’s manuals, product bulletins, cut sheets, 4-color brochures as necessary in compliance with the UTC / Carrier policies.  Photograph products as needed for manuals, cut sheets, brochures, presentations, etc.  Provide Sales and Service support through product demonstrations, trade show assistance, training, customer visits as required.  Work with Marketing team to produce launch materials and documentation, and establish and develop product and marketing advertising and promotional programs for the various product lines.  Working knowledge of Microsoft Publisher, Excel, Word, PowerPoint, Adobe, and FrameMaker.  Perform other duties as assigned. Education: Minimum Requirements:  Associate or Bachelor’s Degree in Marketing and or Graphic Arts helpful Experience/Qualifications:  Excellent written and verbal communication skills  Highly motivated; ability to interface with multi-functional teams;  A proven desire for continuous improvement  3+ years in Project Management / Desktop Publishing Physical Limitations: Must be able to lift up to 30 lbs. and;  The employee is periodically required to perform repetitive movements;  Employee is required to occasionally use mechanical controls including buttons, knobs, levers, cranks, and pedals in performance of a function;  Occasionally required to use personal protective equipment;  The employee is occasionally required to reach overhead with hands and arms; rotate body from sitting and standing position and lift and carry weight up to 30 lbs.;  Normal amount of sitting and standing, average mobility to move around an office and plant environment, able to conduct normal amount of work on a computer; and  The employee is regularly required to walk and move around; use of hands to feel, touch, grasp or grip tools, objects, or handles.
