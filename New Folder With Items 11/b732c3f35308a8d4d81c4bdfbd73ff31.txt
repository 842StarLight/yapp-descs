<h2>Senior Customer Success Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">Liveperson is an expert based culture, built on the drive and passion of smart people! Our business is growing and we are looking for people like you!
<br><br>
LivePerson is the global leader in conversational commerce solutions and we aim to make life easier by transforming how people communicate with brands. Our 18,000 customers, including leading brands like Citibank, HSBC, Orange, Virgin Atlantic, RBS and Liberty Global, use our conversational commerce solutions to orchestrate humans and AI, at scale, and create a convenient, deeply personal relationship — a conversational relationship — with their millions of consumers.
<br><br>
Over the next three years, our goal is to transform the 268 billion analogue phone calls between a brand and its consumers to digital on the LiveEngage platform. By doing this, we enable consumers to get back time and experience a more connected relationship with the brand in which sales, service, marketing, branches, stores, and contact centre's become a unified experience.
<br><br>
The successful candidate has an opportunity to join a highly outstanding team within a fast-paced and successful organisation.
<br><br>
<b>In this role you will:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Maintain a high level of customer response and satisfaction based upon both survey and attrition indicators.</li>
<li>Increase reference ability.</li>
<li>Ensure all aspects of scope, time, quality and cost are achieved or bettered through our comprehensive project management methodology.</li>
<li>Communicate planned milestones and general progress.</li>
<li>Identify applicable use cases and business needs for LivePerson capabilities and successfully deploy into customers account.</li>
<li>Coordinate relevant performance review data at regular and agreed review points. The data must be interpreted to determine customer performance and support recommendations to increase the business benefits and usage of LivePerson's products.</li>
<li>Proactively suggest and drive the adoption of new and innovative tools and features for increasing value of the LivePerson solution for customers centered around connection center operations.</li>
<li>Continuously optimize and improve the development and sharing of best practices and lessons learned for increasing overall program efficiency.</li>
<li>Collaborate with the Account Manager &amp; Customer to establish a success plan consistent with the customer's needs and LivePerson's reasonable expectations. The success plan will be reviewed regularly in accordance with the needs of the company.</li>
<li>Maintain an excellent relationship with the key stakeholders of the account and convert these relationships and conversations into significant outcomes, such as account growth and usage increase.</li>
<li>Serve as the escalation point for any items within the account.</li>
<li>Act as head of Project Management Office for the account, defining and maintaining standards for overall projects and activities within the account.</li>
<li>Support the contract and renewal process.</li>
<li>Own, drive and/or support activities that transform business through thought leadership, vision and organizational development.</li>
</ul>
<b>You should be an expert in:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>BS or BA degree, MBA preferred</li>
<li>3+ years managing the SAAS software solutions within Mid-Market client organizations</li>
<li>Demonstrated leadership, strong negotiation and conflict resolution skills</li>
<li>Operational knowledge of contact centers</li>
<li>Proficient understanding of LivePerson's products &amp; services as well as general web-based technologies</li>
<li>Strong analytical and problem-solving skills</li>
<li>Outstanding interpersonal, relationship building skills conducive to team collaboration</li>
<li>Consistent track record of success and progressive achievement in career to date</li>
<li>Well spoken, articulate and capable with business and technical writing &amp; presentations in English</li>
</ul>
