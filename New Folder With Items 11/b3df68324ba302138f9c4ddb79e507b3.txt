<h2>Agronomy Account Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>WORK PROUD. Are you up for a challenge, doing a job that matters? Looking for a company where work/life balance is respected, honest effort is rewarded, and your contribution is appreciated? You’re looking for Landmark Services Cooperative. Find your place in a positive, supportive atmosphere where opportunity is always growing. Join Landmark, a team that believes in what it’s doing. Enjoy a job that grows along with you as your skills and talents evolve, while fueling the needs of your family, friends, and billions of others who depend on agriculture.</p>
<p>Landmark is looking for an Agronomy Account Manager to maintain positive relationships with growers that results in sales of fertilizers, chemicals, and seeds that enhances our member’s business while also conducting on farm sales calls. The ideal candidate takes pride in their work and always goes the extra mile to guarantee customer satisfaction. This position will be based near our Southern Wisconsin Territory (Janesville, Evansville, Rockford, Beloit, etc.).</p>
<p><b>Essential Responsibilities: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Recommends to growers the fertilizers, chemicals, and seed that will result in the greatest yield and profitability.</li>
<li>Writes up orders for products and verifies price to maintain margins.</li>
<li>Submits orders for processing and follows up on product procurement.</li>
<li>Ability to arrange for product delivery or application.</li>
<li>Analyzes soil through sampling and observation.</li>
<li>Ability to conduct tests and communicate the results to the supplier.</li>
<li>Develops plans for pest infestations and other crop problems.</li>
<li>Communicates with producers on crop development and makes recommendations to improve crop production.</li>
<li>Communicates with operations manager regarding timing of materials.</li>
<li>Communicates with Applicators regarding field characteristics, product placement, and other application concerns.</li>
<li>Follows up with producers after application and resolves any application problems.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Required Skills: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Excellent communication and interpersonal skills with and enthusiasm for building relationships.</li>
<li>Able to embrace goals and goal setting as a means to a successful career in sales.</li>
<li>Self-directed and action oriented.</li>
<li>Ability to negotiate/close while identifying objectives aiming for the best solutions.</li>
<li>Possess a strong set of core values and beliefs.</li>
<li>Accepts responsibility for own actions and is honest.</li>
<li>Demonstrates persistence and recovers quickly from setbacks.</li>
<li>Demonstrates confidence in approaching new customers and presenting new ideas.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Physical/Work Demands: </b></p>
<ul><li>Must be able to walk/stand for long periods of time.</li></ul>
<p><b>Education and Experience: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree or previous experience.</li>
<li>Customer service knowledge preferred.</li>
<li>Experience in communications preferred.</li>
</ul>
