Opportunity Highlights:<br>
<ul>
<li>Complete involvement in all aspects of post-contract execution activities.</li>
<li>Confirms terms, values, products, pricing, and other contract related issues are compliant with company guidelines</li>
<li>Responsible for analyzing and entering customer contracts into PeopleSoft Financials</li>
<li>Accurate and prompt entry of contracts and customers into PeopleSoft - This includes: Business Unit, products, pricing, terms, deferred dates, commissions, titles, site id, line status, referrals, correct spelling of customers name, contact, addresses, zip codes, and country</li>
<li>Accurate and prompt delivery of invoices to customer.</li>
<li>Understands basic accounting and revenue rules to restructure contracts according to company guidelines</li>
<li>Send notifications to PSG, Sales, and Order Administration</li>
<li>Fulfilling/maintaining orders in the internal order management systems</li>
<li>Research and resolve contract/data entry issues that arise from Accounts Receivable, Commissions, Billing, Order Administration, Sales Representatives, and customers in a timely manner</li>
<li>Ensure compliance with all Skillsoft policies and procedures including financial, business, revenue recognition and Sarbanes-Oxley.</li>
</ul>
</div>
