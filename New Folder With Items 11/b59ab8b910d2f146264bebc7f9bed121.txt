<h2>Customer Service Order Entry Administrator - Maryville, Tenn</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div></div>
<div>
<div>
<div>Schneider Electric™ creates connected technologies that reshape industries, transform cities and enrich lives. Our 144,000 employees thrive in more than 100 countries. From the simplest of switches to complex operational systems, our technology, software and services improve the way our customers manage and automate their operations. Help us deliver solutions that ensure Life Is On everywhere, for everyone and at every moment:<br>
</div>
<div></div>.<br>
<p></p>
<div><b>Great people make Schneider Electric a great company.<br>
</b></div>
<div></div>
<p><b>Job Description: </b>Manage and organize large amounts of incoming calls and ensure orders are entered accurately and on-time. This includes both domestic and international orders. Identify and assess customers’ needs to achieve correct catalog number verification, pricing, delivery, and shipping information for each order. Responsible for customer accounts, change orders, expediting, credit memos/limits, RMAs and acknowledgements. Responsible for communication to other departments such as production, purchasing and finance. Assist company in achieving goals set for revenue, profit, receivables, on-time and fill-rate, vitality, quality and safety. Build sustainable relationships and trust with customers through open and interactive communication.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Qualifications: </b>High School Diploma required, two year degree or higher desired. Experience in customer service or order entry in a manufacturing environment desired.
 </p>
<p>Advanced PC, Microsoft Office, Excel, MACPAC or SAP ERP system knowledge is a
</p>
<p>plus. Also needs to be skilled using company and customer web-based tools. Excellent communication skills, including people skills, leadership skills and the ability to train and be a role model to others. Must be organized and possess a strong customer-focused attitude: highly personable, accurate, relates well with people, service oriented, and a positive attitude.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Accountabilities:
 </b></p>
<p>1. Order Management – Ensure orders are entered on-time, entered with 98% accuracy and
 </p>
<p>shipped on-time.
 </p>
<p>2. Customer Addresses – Create new customer bill-to, sold-to and ship-to addresses. Ensure
 </p>
<p>address information is entered and maintained accurately. Modify as necessary based on
 </p>
<p>information provided by the customer, Sales and/or the Credit department.
 </p>
<p>3. Salesperson Addresses – Create new salespeople addresses for all positions: 1, 2, 3 and 4.
 </p>
<p>Ensure address information is entered and maintained accurately in all applicable databases.
 </p>
<p>Modify as necessary based on information provided by Sales.
 </p>
<p>4. Commissions (only applicable to those who serve commissionable sales rep agencies) –
 </p>
<p>Create and maintain accurate commission rates for salesperson1, and process monthly
 </p>
<p>commission payments for your rep agencies.
 </p>
<p>5. Master Reference Comments – Update accordingly based on conversations, promotions,
 </p>
<p>customer freight or shipping requirements, or changes made to maintenance tables (ex. customer
 </p>
<p>addresses, salesperson addresses, orders pricing, etc).
 </p>
<p>6. Programs and Promotions – Assist in executing new or changes to existing Sales and
 </p>
<p>Marketing programs through our customers, the rep agencies and Operations. Coordinate with
 </p>
<p>our customers to ensure that their systems are properly set-up with all our vendor information
 </p>
<p>and program specifications.<br>
 </p>
<p></p>
<p>7. RMAs, credits and rebills – Ensure they are processed and entered accurately, entered on-
 </p>
<p>time, and invoiced on-time.
 </p>
<p>8. Customer Performance and Sales Information – Provide sales and marketing information as
 </p>
<p>requested. Continuous communication on customer issues, trends and opportunities that arise to
 </p>
<p>appropriate internal customer(s). Respond to inquiries received within 4 hours or by end of day,
 </p>
<p>whichever is sooner.
 </p>
<p>10. Compliance to all customer Service and company standards and policies. Improve service
 </p>
<p>through continually meeting and interacting with our customers and other internal departments to
 </p>
<p>maintain your knowledge of products, programs, customer's needs, new products, stock
 </p>
<p>availability and the production plan.<br>
</p>
<br>
