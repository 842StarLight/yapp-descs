<h2>Trade Show Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p>Tyler Technologies participates in approximately 800 trade shows annually, which represents a significant part of our brand strategy. Successful administration of every trade show is critical to the success of our company’s image and brand awareness.</p>
<p>As a Trade Show Specialist, you will be responsible for supporting and executing multiple aspects of trade show administration for Tyler’s civic services software, including but not limited to: initial registrations to booth configuration, shipping, rentals, on-site exhibit support, budget recommendations, lead management and reporting, inventory, and internal communications related to shows.</p>
<p>Work with our solutions that enable new businesses to safely build, open their doors, and stay in compliance. Our solutions facilitate community development, business management regulation, and code compliance while supporting fire prevention and structural safety.</p>
<div>

<p>Duluth, GA</p>
</div>
<div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Travel</b></h2>
<p>5%</p>
</div>
<div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities</b></h2>
<p><b>Specific duties include:</b></p>
<ul><li>Responsible for identifying at which trade shows the company will participate
<ul>
<li>Research trade shows to obtain the latest vendor information.</li>
<li>Assist with budgetary reports and budget recommendations to the marketing lead</li>
<li>Track trade show demographics: # of attendees, job titles, etc.</li>
<li>Explore, analyze and recommend sponsorship opportunities</li>
</ul>
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Responsible for all trade show logistics including:
<ul>
<li>Selecting booth location</li>
<li>Selecting/purchasing show services (electricity, materials handling, Internet, etc.)</li>
<li>Secure pre- and post-show lists</li>
<li>Sending and consolidating pre and post-show sales surveys</li>
<li>Ensuring that all appropriate materials arrive at the show for installation by the salespeople</li>
<li>Post-show budget versus expense reporting</li>
<li>Working with sales to determine who will represent the company</li>
<li>Distributing show information within the company and determining any special requests that might be needed for each show</li>
</ul>
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Accountable for:
<ul>
<li>Awareness of marketing communications campaigns and their impact on trade shows</li>
<li>Budget versus expenses: identify discrepancies in a timely manner in order to make course corrections prior to trade show</li>
<li>New show requests: basic due diligence, work with trade show specialist and analyst as appropriate</li>
<li>Addressing excessive cancellations/substitutions made by sales</li>
<li>Managing challenges, obstacles, suggested changes/modifications to processes</li>
</ul>
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Responsible for maintaining trade show data in Eventuosity
<ul>
<li>Comprehensive files for each trade show</li>
<li>All associated costs</li>
<li>Pre- and post-show attendee lists</li>
<li>Marketing materials developed by Marcom departments</li>
</ul>
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Other responsibilities:
<ul>
<li>Maintain booth inventory to ensure availability and appropriateness for each show</li>
<li>Code card statements with the proper account code for expense tracking</li>
<li>Auditing/managing literature and promo item inventory</li>
<li>Executing special events/meetings that take place in conjunction with trade shows</li>
</ul>
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Tier 1/Tier 1 Regional Show responsibilities include:
<ul>
<li>Facilitating strategy meetings ensure alignment between marketing/sales/events</li>
<li>Ensure trade show best practices are followed</li>
<li>Facilitate the creation/development of strategic sales and marketing goals, objectives and tactics for each trade show</li>
<li>Work with marketing to identify and execute engagement programs that align with established goals that drive booth traffic</li>
<li>Initiate and/or lead pre-show meeting to communicate established goals for show and discuss all relevant activities surrounding the show</li>
<li>Responsible for negotiating value adds and/or discounts for sponsorships to garner additional value</li>
</ul>
</li></ul>
<ul>
<li>Occasionally travel to various trade shows to assist and/or supervise logistics and management of the show</li>
</ul>
</div>

<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree in a related field or equivalent experience</li>
<li>Typically requires 3-5 years of marketing and/or trade show experience</li>
<li>CTSM or CTSM study required</li>
<li>Ability to manage trade show budgets and understand basic financial concepts</li>
<li>Excellent organizational skills</li>
<li>Basic understanding of our industry (Public Sector Software) and marketing concepts</li>
<li>Strong communication skills, with the ability to speak confidently to all groups such as sales, vendors, executives, marketing and branding staff</li>
<li>Strong proficiency in Microsoft Office products</li>
</ul>
