<h2>Woodshop Technician</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Reporting to the Owner &amp; Directive of Creative, the Woodshop Lead is responsible for creating the vision sold by the Sales department and bringing each piece to a reality. Tasked with shop organization, providing creative insight to inventory builds, repairing existing inventory, and assisting with warehouse/inventory organization. The Woodshop Lead will work closely with the Owner to achieve departmental goals along with all over vision of each product send out to clients.</p>
<p>RESPONSIBILTIES</p>
<ul>
<li>Organizing/cleanliness of the shop including all physical tools, supplies, lumber, etc.</li>
<li>Prioritizing product builds per event by date and importance</li>
<li>Understanding departmental revenues, expenses, and fiscal policies</li>
<li>training other employees who will assist with mass builds</li>
<li>Exercising all safety protocols of department</li>
<li>assisting warehouse with inventory organization (as needed)</li>
<li>Assisting operations team with load/unload trucks (as needed)</li>
<li>Creating new products and bringing new ideas to the management team for approval</li>
<li>Understanding other department concepts, needs, and areas of assisting</li>
<li>Learning operating software to gain an understanding of the allover vision of Tavolo Rental</li>
<li>Aiding the concept of Tavolo Home</li>
<li>Being an active ambassador within the community</li>
</ul>
<p>REQUIREMENTS</p>
<ul>
<li>Customer Service – Hospitality Back Ground Is a Plus</li>
<li>Teamwork skills</li>
<li>Creative Mind</li>
<li>Excellent written and verbal skills including proper etiquette</li>
<li>Ability or Willingness to learn how to drive a box truck (up to 26’ in length)</li>
<li>30-40 hour work week commitment (some working from home may be required)</li>
<li>Goal and project oriented</li>
<li>Will need to utilize personal device for company related activities</li>
<li>Positive Attitude</li>
</ul>
