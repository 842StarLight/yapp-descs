<h2>Brokerage Administrator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>
<div>Marcus &amp; Millichap, the nation's leading commercial real estate investment sales company, is seeking a talented, career minded professional to work as a Brokerage Administrator for their Dallas office.</div>
<div>
</div>
<div>
The Brokerage Administrator provides a broad range of administrative assistance to Sales Agents and Management and is responsible brokerage tasks relating to the listing and sales of commercial real estate, marketing, as well as performs various administrative tasks related to commercial real estate brokerage.</div>
</div>
<div><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Essential Duties and Responsibilities:</b></h3>
<div><ul>
<li>
Marketing support including proposals, press releases, event coordination, advertising, etc. using software such as Adobe InDesign and Photoshop</li>
<li>
Brokerage administration and transactional responsibilities including managing online listings</li>
<li>
Maintain cleanliness and organization of the office and conference room areas</li>
<li>
Perform a variety of administrative duties as required to support agents including data entry and proofreading of marketing packages and other client materials</li>
</ul></div>
</div></div>
<div><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Inter-personal Skills:</b></h3>
<div><ul>
<li>
Ability to communicate extremely well with a diverse group of personality types</li>
<li>
Possess a strong desire to learn new concepts and challenge yourself to learn and grow</li>
<li>
Have a natural willingness to be helpful and be very service oriented</li>
<li>
Reliable and punctual</li>
<li>
Adaptable and willing to take on tasks as needed</li>
<li>
Strong organizational skills and attention to detail</li>
</ul></div>
</div></div>
<div><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Requirements:</b></h3>
<div><ul>
<li>
Proficient in Office 2010 Suite</li>
<li>
Some experience with InDesign and Photoshop required</li>
<li>
Professional front office appearance, dress and demeanor</li>
<li>
High School Diploma</li>
<li>
Bachelor's degree (preferred)</li>
</ul></div>
</div></div>
