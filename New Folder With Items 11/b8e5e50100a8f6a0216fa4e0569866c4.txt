 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Controller/Accounting Manager</b></p>
<p>If you are an accounting professional with manufacturing environment experience and looking for a great opportunity that provides the ability to be a key contributor to the management team, Solar Management Services may be the place for you. We are a family owned company that is built on traditional values and offers a stable and positive outlook with strong projected long-term growth.</p>
<p>We are currently seeking an enthusiastic individual to fill the position of Controller/Accounting Manager. The successful candidate will work closely with Senior Management and be a strategic partner in helping to maintain, plus grow the business. This candidate should possess a hands-on approach and have a strong background in cost accounting principles, ERP systems, cash flow planning and analysis, forecasting, financial reporting and variance analysis, insurance negotiations, plus familiarity with payroll and tax reporting.</p>
<p>Responsibilities</p>
<ul>
<li>Prepare, compile and analyze all financial reports, which include but are not limited to income statements, balance sheets, cash flow, inventory and cost allocation reports.</li>
<li>Recommend, establish and maintain internal financial controls, procedures and policies in accordance with the company’s operational objectives to ensure integrity of the financial data.</li>
<li>Responsible for overseeing, development and administration of general accounting, cost accounting, payroll, 401K, plus accounts receivable and accounts payable.</li>
<li>Communicate financial metrics to Senior Management.</li>
<li>Supervise, coach, motivate and develop accounting staff with goal of retention, creating a positive work environment and improving skill sets.</li>
<li>Work with administration, engineering, sales and purchasing to assist them in meeting the company’s sales and production goals.</li>
<li>Perform contract administration for the Companies by reviewing all contractual obligations, especially in sales contracts and advising the Company on best course of action.</li>
<li>Participate and advise management about corporate insurance coverage for protection against property loss, potential liabilities and maintain relationships with providers.</li>
<li>Complete and manage company audits with our external accounting firm.</li>
<li>Maintain job cost analysis and develop overhead rates for job costing and inventory valuation.</li>
<li>Perform other duties as assigned</li>
</ul>
<p>Qualifications</p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree in Accounting or Business Finance</li>
<li>Over 8 years accounting experience that includes GAAP accounting principles, cost accounting in a manufacturing environment, producing and analyzing financial statements package, budgeting and forecasting.</li>
<li>CPA or CMA certification preferred</li>
<li>Experience with ERP systems and manufacturing computer software (Sage MAS preferred).</li>
<li>Excellent critical thinking and problem-solving skills with ability to adapt quickly to business changes.</li>
<li>Previous proven supervisory experience.</li>
<li>Intermediate to advanced user knowledge of Microsoft products (Excel, PowerPoint, Word, etc.)</li>
</ul>
<p>We offer a friendly work environment, plus generous compensation and benefits. Some of our benefits include but are not limited to Medical, Dental, Vision, Short and Long Term Disability, 401K with Company Match, Employee Incentive Plan and paid time off. If interested, please submit resume with cover letter.</p>
