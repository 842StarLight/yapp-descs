<p><b>Galls</b> , <b>LLC</b> is your reliable source for quality, in-stock public safety equipment and apparel. We are seeking a <b>Part -Time Sales Associate (Retail)</b> to join our <b>Los Angeles, CA Store team</b> .</p>

<p><b>Summary:</b> The Sales Associate provides excellent consistent customer service. Processes sales, bags merchandise and services customers by helping them select products for their individual needs. Assists customers by measuring and fitting uniforms and providing product information to meet customer’s request and per contract specifications; adheres to purchase order specifications and ensures customer receives outstanding customer service.<br>
</p>
<p>Although we are a retail operation, we don't have typical retail hours. Store hours are Monday through Friday from 9:00 - 5:00 and <b>CLOSED WEEKNIGHTS ANS WEEKENDS.</b> All associates are expected to be available for work during core store hours.</p>
