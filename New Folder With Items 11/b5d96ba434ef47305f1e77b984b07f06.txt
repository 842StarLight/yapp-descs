<h2>EXPERIENCE PLANNER ON-SITE</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<div><div>
<p>Miraval Austin is seeking an On-Site Experience Planner to join our growing team. The primary responsibility is to support experience planning and the guest services teams in delighting VIP guests on property. This includes developing relationships with guests upon arrival and during their stay and offering helpful suggestions and solutions to ensure a memorable visit. The purpose of this position is to optimize the guest experience with spa and programs bookings, assist with in-person guest relations, and provide superior customer engagement with a thoughtful approach to preserving the Miraval experience.</p>
<p><br>
</p>
<p>
Duties will also include helping VIP guests plan their stay and maximize their experience; review guest itineraries to ensure they meet guest’s documented goals; act as contact for escalated guest issues; perform in-depth property tours and participate as needed in the guest experience; and provide face to face interaction with guests during their visit.</p>
