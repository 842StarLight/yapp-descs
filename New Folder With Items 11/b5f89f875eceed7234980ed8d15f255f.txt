<h2>Sr Planner Coach</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>Edelman Financial Engines is conducting a Nation Wide search for our next Sr Development Coach. In this role, you will identify, design, develop and deliver innovative, scalable, sustainable learning experiences that support the performance improvement of our national financial advisor team.
<br>
<br>
<b><i>What you will do:</i></b>
<ul>
<li>Engage as an internal consultant with leadership to identify business initiatives and training needs to continuously improve advisor performance</li>
<li>
Responsible for sustaining and improving performance by proactively identifying emerging trends and creating action plans to improve performance</li>
<li>
Completes analyses, measures program effectiveness and recommends activities and program strategies</li>
<li>
Facilitate workshops, training programs, and leadership coaching to improve advisor results</li>
<li>
Design, develop and deploy learning programs utilizing adult learning theory and cutting edge technology</li>
<li>
Identify and consult with subject matter experts to develop accurate learning materials</li>
</ul>
<b><i>What you will need:</i></b>
<ul>
<li>Ability to travel up to 50%</li>
<li>
Bachelor’s degree or equivalent experience</li>
<li>
5 years’ experience in Sales Training, developing learning solutions for retail, contact center and remote/distance learning</li>
<li>
Previous experience in financial services</li>
<li>Ability to work cross functionally and establish influential relationships at all levels of the enterprise</li>
<li>
Strong learning and organizational development consultation skills with the ability to identify performance gaps, evaluate knowledge gaps and design solutions to develop skills or transfer critical knowledge</li>
<li>
Demonstrate a positive, proactive and self-directed approach</li>
<li>
Flexibility to respond quickly and positively to shifting demands and opportunities; ability to work under tight deadlines; plan, organize and carry out multiple, detailed tasks</li>
</ul>
