<h2>Brand Marketing Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Chetu is seeking a full-time, experienced branding specialist to join our dynamic and fast paced marketing team. As Chetu’s Brand Marketing Specialist you will maintain the Chetu brand identity and improve the brand reputation across all review sites and other channels.</p>
<p>Leveraging contemporary strategies in digital marketing, the candidate will regularly optimize branding efforts to help achieve department and company goals. This position will be responsible for areas including branded articles and reputation management, public relations, CSR, plus web and graphic design.</p>
<p><b>Brand Marketing Specialist Responsibilities Include: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>The ideal applicant is an excellent writer, with a high level of creativity, and able to research and produce content on a variety of topics.</li>
<li>Use data-driven insights, analyzing our brand positioning and consumer awareness to create superior branded materials.</li>
<li>Audit, QA, maintain, and improve Chetu.com and related domains to conform to best practices in SEO, UI/UX, plus design.</li>
<li>Enhance the tech fabric of Chetu’s digital channels by researching and implementing contemporary technologies to improve lead generation and branding efforts.</li>
<li>Analytical skills to forecast and identify trends and challenges</li>
<li>Create, design, and coordinate marketing collateral to support key messaging and brand identity for marketing and sales teams through flyers, graphics, videos, etc. and manageassociated rights and permissions.</li>
<li>Translating brand elements into strategies, helping shape and communicate our vision, and mission.</li>
<li>Demonstrate In-depth understanding of the company’s current offerings and future expansions.</li>
<li>Write and issue DMCA infringement notices and cease and desist orders whenever appropriate.</li>
<li>Monitor the web for new occurrences of the Chetu name in real-time, mitigating negative results and acquire plus promote positive ones.</li>
<li>Clean up SERP results for brand keywords and farm positive links to populate first and second page results.</li>
<li>Create and edit storytelling content for PR and marketing initiatives and bring to fruition proactive thought leadership opportunities such as identifying speaking engagement/award opportunities, opportunities to engage with influencers, events, etc.</li>
<li>Responsible for CSR strategies and Corporate Communications campaigns to raise awareness of social responsibility events plus coordinate participation.</li>
<li>Continually identify new directories, awards, PR opportunities, and other channels for brand exposure.</li>
<li>Ensuring marketing campaigns are delivered on brand, on time, and on budget while reducing redundancies and complexities.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Requirements: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree in Advertising, Marketing, Communications, Business, or related fields</li>
<li>Must have at minimum 2 years of related experience in digital marketing</li>
<li>Strong written and verbal communication skills, and great attention to detail.</li>
<li>Ability to communicate clearly and consistently in person or through a variety of channels</li>
<li>Excellent understanding of contemporary digital marketing principles, strategies, and best practices</li>
<li>Ability to approach challenges with resilience, resourcefulness, optimism, creativity and strategic agility</li>
<li>Excellent understanding of the full marketing mix</li>
<li>Synergized understanding of other forms of digital marketing including content marketing, SEO, conversion optimization, landing page optimization, and paid advertising</li>
<li>Solid understanding of performance marketing, conversion, and online customer acquisition</li>
<li>In-depth experience with website analytics tools Including Google Analytics, SEMrush, etc.</li>
<li>Google Analytics Certified a plus</li>
<li>Preference will be given to those with a strong writing portfolio.</li>
</ul>
