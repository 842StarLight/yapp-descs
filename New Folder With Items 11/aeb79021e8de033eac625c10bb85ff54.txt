<h2>Security & Fire Systems Technician - Richmond, VA</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<div>
<div>
<b>What you will do</b><br>
<br>
Under general supervision, possesses technical competence on security &amp; CCTV systems and the ability to resolve or guide others through systems problem resolution. Able to perform programming, commissioning, troubleshooting and preventive maintenance of fire/security systems per contract obligations. Provides detailed information to communicate design to customers, installation or service team and subcontractors.<br>
<br>
<b>How you will do it</b>
</div>
<ul>
<li><div>
Provides solutions to systems problems by troubleshooting the system or advising others on how to resolve problems. Requires thorough understanding of core and third party fire /security products, software and systems / electrical technology.<br>
</div></li>
<li><div>Configures fire/security systems, which utilize both core and third party security products and systems /electrical technology as defined by the project specification or on retrofit projects while maintaining quality and ensuring customer satisfaction.<br>
</div></li>
<li><div>Orders material from both internal sources and / or external vendors, and expedites as necessary. Ensures timely delivery to appropriate locations. Completes all required commissioning documentation. Provides accurate information for as-builts documentation.<br>
</div></li>
<li><div>Assists engineering and sales by providing technical recommendations to produce job layouts and bid proposals.<br>
</div></li>
<li><div>Performs site-specific training for owner / operator on the total system.<br>
</div></li>
<li><div>Adheres to safety standards and local and national codes, with a high degree of regard to employee and subcontractor safety.<br>
</div></li>
<li><div>Maintains appropriate local and National Security Industry Certifications.<br>
</div></li>
<li><div>Communicates with customer upon arrival and before leaving the work site.<br>
</div></li>
<li><div>Continuous proactive customer interactions including an in-depth understanding of customer(s) needs wants and systems currently in place. Plans for future needs with customer that include recommending complete systems enhancements and promoting the sale of add-on work.<br>
</div></li>
<li><div>Assists in monitoring project execution per plan. Reports problems or changes to management.<br>
</div></li>
<li><div>Creates and presents field proposals for small projects and labor and material.<br>
</div></li>
<li><div>May mentor other Security Technicians on tools and technology. Serves as a Security Team technical resource.<br>
</div></li>
<li><div>May assist sales in development of cost estimate on complex projects.<br>
</div></li>
<li><div>Performs other duties as assigned.<br>
</div></li>
</ul>
</div>
