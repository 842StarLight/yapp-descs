<h2>Sr. Financial Analyst, Guardsman</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>We’re thrilled that you are interested in joining us here at the Amynta Group!
</p>
<p></p>The Senior Financial Analyst provides financial reporting and analysis for the Guardsman organization. This includes the production of recurring reports, tracking of key financial data and analysis of business strategies for the company. This position mines, analyzes, and reports data for a variety of business managers and executives. Also, this position identifies opportunities to analyze the business and pursues those opportunities with general supervision. The Associate may interact with his or her supervisor several times a week to receive guidance and feedback. Many non-routine activities will require a supervisor’s advance approval.
<p>Provide daily, weekly, monthly, and quarterly reporting for the executive team regarding many aspects of the company’s performance and is typically used to manage the business and act as a liaison between Financial Planning and Analysis and other Finance groups.
</p>
<ul>
<li>Perform financial forecasting, reporting, and operational metrics tracking
</li>
<li>Analyze financial data and create financial models for decision support
</li>
<li>Report on financial performance and prepare for regular leadership reviews
</li>
<li>Analyze past results, perform variance analysis, identify trends, and make recommendations for improvements
</li>
<li>Work closely with the accounting team to ensure accurate financial reporting
</li>
<li>Assist with the implementation &amp; completion of the annual budgets. Work with various departments to incorporate information into the budget
</li>
<li>Evaluate financial performance by comparing and analyzing actual results with plans and forecasts
</li>
<li>Guide cost analysis process by establishing and enforcing policies and procedures
</li>
<li>Provide analysis of trends and forecasts and recommend actions for optimization
</li>
<li>Recommend actions by analyzing and interpreting data and making comparative analyses; study proposed changes in methods and materials
</li>
<li>Identify and drive process improvements, including the creation of standard and ad-hoc reports, tools, and Excel dashboards
</li>
<li>Increase productivity by developing automated reporting/forecasting tools
</li>
<li>Perform market research, data mining, business intelligence, and valuation comps
</li>
<li>Maintain a strong financial analysis foundation creating forecasts and models
</li>
<li>Perform budget and forecast to actual variance analysis, investigation and commentary for the executive team and business leaders
</li>
<li>Seek continuous improvements for all processes on FP&amp;A team by sharing best practices with other FP&amp;A team members at every level
</li>
<li>Perform cost/benefit and sales promotions analyses including the use of ROIC, IRR, and ROI valuations.
</li>
</ul>

</b></p>

</li>

</li>

</li>

</li>

</ul>
