<h2>WEB DESIGN/IT SPECIALIST</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p><b>Position Description:
</b></p>
<p>This position is responsible for creating, designing and maintaining websites and providing IT support to workstations, including software/hardware updates and repairs, for our growing business. The ideal candidate is a hard-working individual who has a creative mindset to help with the development and execution of branding/marketing on the web. This candidate must be knowledgeable of the newest technology and its implications in the industry. Natural motivation, positive attitude/aptitude, a continuous-improvement mindset and an outstanding attention to detail are recommended.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Responsibilities:
</b></p>
<div>· Create clean, modern, functional and easy-to-navigate website and landing pages.
</div>
<div>· Produce creative digital media objects for company website and social media channels.
</div>
<div>· Integrate web technologies to support our organization’s needs.
</div>
<div>· Create scripts using a variety of web-oriented scripting tools.
</div>
<div>· Research and recommend new opportunities for enhancing our software, hardware and IT processes.
</div>
<div>· Provide support to employees through ticketed requests and face-to-face interactions.
</div>
<div>· Balance workday between multiple projects and respond immediately to technical support requests from staff.
</div>
<div>· Become an expert on Imperial products and develop sales support content.
</div>
<div>· Troubleshoot software and hardware issues, and provide recommendations.
</div>
<div>· Report any issues and concerns immediately to Information Systems Manager.
</div>
<div>· Conduct end-user training when needed.
</div>
<div>· Follow strict safety regulations.
</div>
<div>· Assist with other projects as needed.<br>
</div>
<p></p>
<p><b>Requirements </b><b>:
</b></p>
<div>· Associates degree in IT- Web Design or related field.
</div>
<div>· A solid, cohesive and comprehensive online portfolio.
</div>
<div>· 2+ years’ experience in the Web Designer field desired.
</div>
<div>· Proficient in web technologies like HTML, CSS and JavaScript, jQuery, content management systems, user experience design and branding/marketing on the web.
</div>
<div>· Experience with Big Commerce, WordPress or other e-commerce software a plus.
</div>
<div>· Advanced knowledge of Windows operating systems and Microsoft software.<br>
</div>
<p></p>
<p><b>Knowledge, Skills, and Abilities:
</b></p>
<div>· Must have a strong work ethic with excellent verbal and written communication skills.
</div>
<div>· Knowledge of website analytics tools.
</div>
<div>· Ability to focus on multiple projects at once and to troubleshoot problems quickly.
</div>
<div>· Must be well organized, a self-starter with a driven personality.
</div>
<div>· Ability to work in a team environment and/or on an individual basis.
</div>
<div>· Flexible and capable of successfully performing in a fast-paced environment.<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<p>This position offers competitive wages and benefits package that includes: Medical, Dental, Vision, Prescription and Life Insurance, Short Term Disability, Paid Holidays, Vacation, Personal Days, 401K and Profit Sharing, and more.
