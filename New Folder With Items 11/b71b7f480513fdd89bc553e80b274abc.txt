<h2>Assistant Manager, Brand Activation</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>
<b>Position Title:</b> Assistant Manager, Brand Activation
</div>
<div>
<b>Location:</b> Boca Raton, Florida</div>
<div>
<b>Reports to: </b>Manager, Brand Activation</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div>Responsible to participate and collaborate in the developing of the brand shopper strategies for a product (s) grouping(s) within a brand (s) to build consumer demand and market share, grounded in deep consumer, communicate products, and market understanding.<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>Specific Responsibilities
</div>
<div>Specific responsibilities will include but are not limited to:</div>
<div><b>Activation</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Assist on the execution of the marketing shopper strategies for the upcoming year</li>
<li>
Familiar with test and learn opportunities within communication vehicles to find leverage points for scale and impact.</li>
</ul>
 <div><b>Brand Stewardship</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Performes brand activities in accordance to the brand identity and guidelines process i.e. consumer promotion materials, POS, displays, retailer website content</li>
<li>
Ensures marketing communications and marketing adaptations are on-strategy and consistent.</li>
<li>
Implements assets, including digital, physical and copy to Trade and Sales partners
 </li>
</ul>
<div><b>New Product and Package Launch Support</b></div>
<ul><li>Support product launches and assista the manager with the the cross-functional teams communication.</li></ul>
 <div><b>Integrated Marketing Plan and Execution</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Team up with the Brand Activation Manager to implement the overall integrated shopper marketing activities
</li>
<li>Support the Brand Activation Mgr. by monitor the integrated marketing programs and adjusts programming as needed, included visiting stores and analyze the POS reports.
</li>
<li>Contributes to best practices in the industry through networking internally and externally and keeping up with industry best practices.</li>
</ul>
 <div><b>Event Marketing</b></div>
<ul><li>Partner with Sales and Trade Marketing to ensure all communications are on-brand and meeting the set promotional support needs per campaign and event.</li></ul>
 <div><b>Promotions Planning</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Participates and support the Brand Activation Manager in the development of the annual consumer promotional plan.</li>
<li>
Supports on the consumer promotion plan and the execution elements.
</li>
<li>Works closely with the Brand Activation Manager to develop full year promotional calendar to meet brand and portfolio priorities.</li>
</ul>
 <div><b>Project and Process Management</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Works with marketing to forecast and prioritize initiatives and reviews and validates marketing requests based on priorities.
</li>
<li>Facilitates collaboration and communication across all stakeholders.
</li>
<li>Measures and analyzes ROI of marketing programs and recommends improvements.</li>
</ul>
