<h2>Finance Director</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Habitat Bucks seeks a Finance Director to administer the funds raised by Habitat for Humanity of Bucks County for operations, construction and home repair projects. The Finance Director will serve as the key staff member in managing all finance functions, including accounts receivable, accounts payable, budget development, cash flow management, grant reporting, and audit preparation. The Finance Director will provide leadership in all banking transactions, bookkeeping and daily accounting processes, working closely with staff, financial institutions, Treasurer and auditors to ensure compliance with all established procedures and GAAP.</p>
<p><b>Specific Duties include</b></p>
<p><i>Financial accounting and reporting</i></p>
<ul>
<li>Develop and maintain timely and accurate financial statements and reports</li>
<li>Ensure that all statutory requirements of the organization are met</li>
<li>Prepare all supporting information for the annual audit and liaise with the Board's Audit Committee and the external auditors as necessary</li>
<li>Develop and maintain financial accounting systems for cash management, accounts payable, accounts receivable, credit control, and petty cash</li>
<li>Oversee the bookkeeping function including maintenance of the general ledger, accounts payable, accounts receivable and payroll</li>
<li>Prepare annual charitable return</li>
<li>Liaise with the Treasurer, Finance Committee and/or Audit Committee as appropriate</li>
</ul>
<p><i>Payroll preparation and administration</i></p>
<ul><li>Oversee all payroll functions</li></ul>
<p><i>Budget preparation</i></p>
<ul><li>Establish guidelines for budget and forecast preparation, and prepare the annual budget</li></ul>
<p><i>Project management accounting</i></p>
<ul>
<li>Maintain financial records for each project in a manner that facilitates management reports</li>
<li>Ensure that accurate and timely financial statements are prepared in accordance with contract agreements with funders</li>
<li>Ensures the integrity of all sales, inventory tracking and valuation controls in the ReStores</li>
</ul>
<p><i>Information technology</i></p>
<ul><li>Evaluate the need for new technology to meet the organization's financial data processing, control, and reporting requirements</li></ul>
<p><i>Risk management</i></p>
<ul>
<li>Monitor risk management policies and procedures to ensure that program and organizational risks are minimized</li>
<li>Maximize income where possible and appropriate</li>
</ul>
<p><i>Office administration</i></p>
<ul><li>Oversee the management of all leases, contracts and other financial commitments</li></ul>
