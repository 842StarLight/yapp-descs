About you
<br><br>
As part of Boxy Charm's creative team, the Creative Production Coordinator will support the creative Project Manager and Creative Director by ensuring the organization of all productions. You will manage talent schedules, organize photoshoots, and see the entire creative life cycle for a project.
<br><br>
A successful Production Coordinator is energetic, creative and highly organized. Equally as important, the Production Coordinator is dead-line driven, flexible and resilient.
<br><br>
About you
<br><br>
Boxy Charm is looking for a Merchandise Coordinator to accelerate business results by providing tactical and organizational support to the Merchandise team. The primary focus is being a liaison with internal and external teams to ensure the successful completion of multi-million-dollar purchase orders and delivery of millions of products. In addition, you will also be responsible for partnering with internal teams to make things happen.
<br><br>
A successful coordinator thrives in a fast-paced, detail-oriented, and comfortable in a varied role is perfect for someone who has some experience in sales support within the eCommerce, media or advertising world.
<br><br>
What you will be doing
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Support the buying team by generating and processing Purchase Orders (POs) as agreed upon with each Brand Manager for all boxes and ecomm</li>
<li>Gather information required from internal and external teams including interaction with Beauty and Lifestyle brands</li>
<li>Ensure accuracy, proper recording, documentation and timely signage</li>
<li>Enter all Purchase Orders once signed into our ERP System</li>
<li>Work with supply chain to detect early issues with delivery and vendor product quality concern</li>
<li>Manage Fedex/UPS shipments when needed</li>
<li>Send gifts on a monthly basis to our Brand Partners, as needed</li>
<li>Complete Ad Hoc administrative duties as needed by Senior Team</li>
</ul>
