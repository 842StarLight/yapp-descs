<h2>Communications and Marketing Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div><div><div>
<div>
<p>Reporting to the Assistant Director of Public Relations, the Communications and Marketing Manager will support the Office of Communications and Marketing’s goal of communicating the college’s strategic initiatives and guiding principles through planning, executing, and reporting on short and long-term communications and marketing campaigns for college-wide initiatives and the Center for Performing Arts. In addition to working closely with the President’s Area on college-wide initiatives, this position will work directly with the Center for Performing Arts to create and execute communications and marketing campaigns for the center and its portfolio of shows as well as oversee the center’s brand presence and promote its mission and programs. This position will also analyze the results of campaigns to improve and increase stakeholder engagement.</p>
</div>
<br>
<div>
<div><b>Minimum Qualifications</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree in a marketing or communications-related discipline.</li>
<li>A combination of education and related experience in marketing and communications for a total of five to six years.</li>
<li>A background in performing arts marketing preferred, but not required.</li>
</ul>
</div>
<br>
<div>
<div><b>Criteria</b></div>
<div>
<p>CRITERIA: The following criteria, which are not the sole criteria used in the final hiring decision, will be used to review the applications of those persons who meet the stated minimum qualifications and to select those qualified applicants who will be interviewed. Other information and observations made during the interview process may be considered in the selection process. <b>Please ensure that your application and attached documents address the criteria listed below.</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Experience in developing and executing communications and marketing campaigns.</li>
<li>Experience buying paid media (television, radio, print, and digital) to support a marketing campaign.</li>
<li>Demonstrated abilities in writing and editing copy for print, TV, radio, social media, and the web.</li>
<li>Experience working with executive and senior level staff.</li>
<li>Outstanding organizational skills. Ability to coordinate and manage multiple projects under tight deadlines.</li>
<li>Demonstrated experience in AP Style.</li>
<li>Excellent writing and verbal communication skills. Strong proofreading skills.</li>
<li>Knowledge of public relations and marketing principles and best practices.</li>
<li>Demonstrated ability to use research and analytics to support and evaluate communications and marketing campaigns; experience executing and measuring campaigns against sales goals.</li>
<li>Experience using software packages including Microsoft Office Suite and Adobe Creative Suite as well as Content Management Software.</li>
</ul>
</div>
</div>
<br>

<ul>

<li>Ability to communicate effectively in spoken and written standard English.</li>
<li>As required by the 1986 Immigration Act, be prepared to present acceptable documentation showing your identity and that you are a U.S. citizen or an alien who is authorized to work.</li>
</ul>
