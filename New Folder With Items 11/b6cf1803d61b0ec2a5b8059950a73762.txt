<h2>Project Manager (VO)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p><b>SUMMARY</b><b>:</b></p>
<p>
The Project Manager position manages the communication plan and project management process flow for the various Intelisys Business Units. The Project Managers primary responsibilities include providing project support complex network implementations for Enterprise customers, Indirect Sale Agents (Partner Sales &amp; Mobility Solutions) by developing and managing timelines and project-related activities; managing project priorities and risks; communicating to key stakeholders and project team members; developing processes and procedures for high-volume order entry and order flow; and, managing supplier, vendor &amp; customer expectations. The Project Manager will also provide tier 1 product support and serve as first point of contact for low- and mid-level escalations. POSITION IS AVAILABLE FOR REMOTE WORK THROUGHOUT UNITED STATES</p>
<p><b>
ESSENTIAL FUNCTIONS</b><b>:</b> Includes the following:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Provides LEC and carrier product and process support information to Partners by responding to inquiries and prioritizing concerns to help set expectations.</li>
<li>
Develops and maintains project plans and implementations schedules to provide timely and accurate updates.</li>
<li>
Interfaces with sales partner &amp; customer to verify order information and assists with completing requirement documents for new and existing customers (i.e., quotes, complex translations forms, order forms, contracts, LSRs, ASRs).</li>
<li>
Subject matter expert on data and complex voice products.</li>
<li>
Identifies resources needed and assigns responsibilities as needed.</li>
<li>
Ensures project documents are completed, accurate, accessible and stored appropriately.</li>
<li>
Tracks project deliverables using Project Management tools (i.e., RPM field, spreadsheets)</li>
<li>
Keeps project team members well informed by participating in regularly scheduled status meeting.</li>
<li>
Determines appropriate revenue recognition; insures accurate billing; and monitors commission payout.</li>
<li>
Responsible to making sure all order fulfillment activities are completed - order entry, order tracking, product implementation, problem resolution.</li>
<li>
Provide Tier 1 for Order Fulfillment Specialists and Partner Support Team.</li>
<li>
Provide training to Order Fulfillment Specialists, Project Managers &amp; Partner Support Team.</li>
<li>
First point of contact for low &amp; mid-level escalations.</li>
<li>
Develop effective working relationships with customers, key suppliers and vendor representatives.</li>
<li>
Participate in company meetings and trainings as required.</li>
<li>
Perform other tasks and special projects as required.</li>
</ul>
