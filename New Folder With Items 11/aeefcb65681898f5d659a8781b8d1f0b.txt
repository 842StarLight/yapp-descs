<h2>Director of Data Center Operations</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>As a member of the company’s operations team and reporting to the VP of Data Center Operations, the Director of Data Center Operations for our Northern VA and Washington D.C. markets is responsible for the operation of the data centers, ensuring that facilities problems are identified and repaired quickly, ensuring that contractors are delivering quality services, ensuring the security program is implemented, and making sure that all customer service delivery requirements are being met. This individual will own the data center operations and capital budget for their markets and will be expected to remain abreast of best-in-class industry practices and work with team members to improve efficiency and implement new data center operational strategies.</p>
<p><b>Duties:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Leads the development and supervision of the facility maintenance program and equipment/system operation under the authority of the Senior Vice President of Data Center Operations</li>
<li>Demonstrates technical knowledge and experience of data center power, mechanical, network, and security systems</li>
<li>Acts as a technical point of contact for Sales and the Executive team concerning all aspects of the maintenance, troubleshooting, and operation of site support systems</li>
<li>Participate in the overall facility design and construction from a security, operating, and maintainability point of view</li>
<li>Develops and implements new maintenance and service installation standards, as well as work to improve existing standards to support site operations</li>
<li>Review, supervise and critique work performance by operations team members to ensure adherence to standards, schedules and specifications; This includes any third party contract hires to support data center and base building infra-structure</li>
<li>Engage and address any issues that arise day-or-night, and provide on call support in case of emergencies or other off-hours issues</li>
<li>Responsible for incident management and reporting, including internal communication to peers and leadership</li>
<li>Manages regional operating and capital budgets and expenses, including maintenance contracts, repair costs, salary/benefits, overtime, and travel</li>
<li>Ensures that operational, maintenance and emergency Methods of Procedures (MOP’s) are developed, reviewed and audited through all stages of the maintenance process for electrical and mechanical support equipment</li>
<li>Mentors and provides training for new hire personnel in the operation of data centers to include security, telecom, and facilities programs</li>
<li>Develops and grows the Data Center Operations department processes, policies, procedures, and documentation to support company goals in a changing environment</li>
<li>Facilitates cross-functional communication and interaction, including setting up ways to track and report on the fulfillment of cross-functional activities</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Requirements</b></p>
<p><b>
Knowledge, Skills &amp; Abilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Candidate must demonstrate in-depth technical knowledge of security, telecom, mechanical and electrical support systems in a critical environment</li>
<li>Candidate must have a proven track record of standardized process and policy implementation, specifically in the area of critical maintenance and system operation</li>
<li>Candidate must be able to develop and manage a budget</li>
<li>Candidate must be able to speak professionally with clientele, senior leadership, and executives</li>
<li>Candidate must be able to provide technical training</li>
<li>Candidate must be able to travel to the corporate office and company locations within their assigned region</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Education/Experience:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>8-10 years of relevant experience in the operation, design, troubleshooting, and maintenance of power distribution and mechanical support systems</li>
<li>3-5 years’ experience in administering and managing facility preventative maintenance programs and budgets</li>
<li>3-5 years of direct management experience is required.
</li>
<li>Experience managing remote teams will be considered attractive</li>
</ul>
