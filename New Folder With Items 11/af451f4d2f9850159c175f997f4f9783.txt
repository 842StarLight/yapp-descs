<h2>Program Manager II(US)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">TCG Customer Success is looking for a solution oriented, innovative and proactive leader to help transform and scale our *** B cloud collaboration business, and ensure Cisco's collaboration customers fully realize the value of our products. The ideal candidate will partner with the organization's senior leaders and the broader cross-functional teams to plan and operationalize the transformational strategy needed to take this growing cloud business to the next level. The individual will help drive growth by defining optimization solutions to rehome non-adoption activities. They will define automation requirements, product requirements, and process changes while continuing to ensure our customer's needs are being service. This individual will act as a "general manager” for multiple optimization efforts, and is responsible for ensuring the success of the solution. This position will focus on the Webex Meetings and Teams related non-adoption activities. This position will have significant direct visibility and engagement with senior executives.

Who We Are
<br><br>
Cisco's Team Collaboration Group (TCG) is helping people transform how they collaborate globally through solutions targeted at enhancing the user experience and driving collaboration and productivity to unprecedented levels, thus helping drive revenue growth and defining new business models. We are the only vendor that can truly connect conferencing hardware, software, and SaaS offerings to provide the best end-to-end user experience regardless of whether they are internal or external, while making it secure and scalable for IT. We are Customer Success for TCG. Our broader team of Customers and Partner Success Managers work with Customers and Partners to ensure the highest levels of utilization of our collaboration products are achieved. The Optimization Team within Customer Success is driven to make CSMs and PSMs insanely productive and our customers realize unprecedented value and delight from our products and their CSM.
