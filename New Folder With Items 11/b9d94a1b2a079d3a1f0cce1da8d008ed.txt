<h2>Associate Territory Manager (ATM), Diabetes - Orlando/Lakeland, FL</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>CAREERS THAT CHANGE LIVES:</b></p>
<p>The Associate Territory Manager (ATM) will partner with the field and inside team to service and support key markets. This role will drive operational efficiencies within specified territories (i.e., paperwork collection, marketing campaign support, CareLink software/technical support, EPNP conversion, NPT generation). This role will serve as a liaison between the field sales and clinical team, inside sales teams, and customers.</p>
<br>

<p></p>
<p><b>A DAY IN THE LIFE:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Partner with the field sales and clinical team across an assigned district and /or specific customer accounts to meet or exceed sales targets.</li>
<li>Collaborate with TM and DTS to review and manage pipeline. Responsible for pursuing leads, assessing needs and providing product services to maximize the benefits derived from Medtronic's products and/or services.</li>
<li>Facilitate any paperwork (Medtronic or Dealer) collection needs with the office/patient and coordinates with the inside team. This includes paperwork collection for patient insurance processing, contacting patients for information and making sure all insurance requirements are fulfilled.</li>
<li>Assist TM and CTM with EPNP and NPT production.</li>
<li>Contact out of warranty (OOW) and ship when ready (SWR) leads.</li>
<li>Partners with Field Marketing Manager, DM, TM, and CTM to drive local marketing campaigns and events.</li>
<li>Fulfill and distribute marketing collateral to specified accounts.</li>
<li>Provide CareLink technical support as needed.</li>
<li>Implement strategies to streamline processes to create a pathway for new patient growth.</li>
<li>Responsible for developing, building, and strengthening long-term relationships with stakeholders including distributors and healthcare professionals.</li>
<li>Promotes and establishes education of the company's products and/or services.</li>
<li>Conducts market research including customers and competitor’s activities.</li>
</ul>
<div><div>
<p><b>MUST HAVE - MINIMUM REQUIREMENTS:</b></p>
<p><i>TO BE CONSIDERED FOR THIS ROLE, PLEASE BE SURE THE MINIMUM REQUIREMENTS ARE EVIDENT ON YOUR RESUME</i></p>
<ul><li>Bachelor's degree</li></ul>
<p><b>NICE TO HAVE:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>2 years of sales or customer support experience (field or inside) OR 1-year clinical experience in a sales environment.</li>
<li>Knowledge of sales techniques typically gained through education and / or on the job learning.</li>
<li>Introductory knowledge of company products and services.</li>
<li>Prior medical device, business to business or pharmaceutical sales experience highly preferred.</li>
<li>Demonstrable, consistent success in previous roles indicated by high level of sales performance</li>
<li>Experience being accountable for sales quotas in a challenging, fast paced, changing environment.</li>
<li>Prior experience and knowledge of diabetes, unmet needs of diabetes therapies, and benefits of insulin pump therapy and continuous glucose monitoring.</li>
<li>Previous experience in a high volume telesales call center environment.</li>
<li>Strong customer focus in delivering sales and building relationships.</li>
<li>Demonstrated proficiency with Microsoft Office software. Ability to effectively navigate through databases and retrieve information electronically. Ability to quickly learn new programs and systems.</li>
<li>Ability to multi-task effectively, and to work both independently and in a team environment.</li>
<li>Must possess strong organizational skills and the ability to successfully handle multiple priorities.</li>
<li>Hardworking driven individual with effective persuasion skills.</li>
<li>Ability to understand and solve underlying causes of simple or complex problem.</li>
<li>Strong attention to detail by being exact, precise, and accurate.</li>
<li>Must successfully complete training.</li>
<br>
</ul>
