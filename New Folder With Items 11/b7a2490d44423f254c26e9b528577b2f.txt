<h2>Programmatic Advertising Platform Associate</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>The Programmatic Platform Associate will work within the Winstar operations team to actively manage client campaigns to include data/inventory analysis, booking, optimization and assisting sales team with client related activities. The PPA will assist the Programmatic Platform Manager with campaigns using a variety of digital inventory suppliers, 3rd party data solutions and DSPs with which Winstar is partnered. The responsibilities include campaign organization, data analysis, discrepancy reporting, and basic operational support in our DSP platforms. The ideal candidate will have experience with DSPs and the digital media landscape including 3rd party data integrations.</p>
<p>The Platform Associate reports to the Platform Manager.</p>
<p><b>Position Responsibilities: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Support of all media campaigns including implementation through optimization and reporting.</li>
<li>Establish best practices and optimization strategies for campaigns utilizing DSP platforms</li>
<li>Drive campaign ROI</li>
<li>Contribute to clients' business growth by identifying new opportunities and encourage planning teams to implement creative solutions</li>
<li>Maintain knowledge of media technology buying platforms and analytics tools</li>
<li>Deliver insight into the implications of campaign constraints, such as scale, ramp up time, creative used and cost per conversion</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Skills &amp; Requirements: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor Degree Required (quantitative field preferred)</li>
<li>Understanding of campaign success metrics: conversions, pacing, target CPAs, etc.</li>
<li>Experience with DSP platforms</li>
<li>2-4 years minimum experience in display campaigns, DSP and/or SEM, Social</li>
<li>Expert with various ad-server technologies</li>
</ul>
