<h2>Staff Accountant</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Do you sweat the details and maintain a high level of integrity in your work?</b></p>
<p>Aqua Finance, Inc. is looking for sharp, good-humored <b>Staff Accountant</b> who enjoys working in a constantly changing environment and streamlining a hectic day. You will be working in a fast-paced, creative environment with a nimble team that is focused on quality. We're taking a fresh approach to building an environment that brings out the best in Aqua Finance, Inc. and our employees! We're looking for people who are jacks of all trades: super organized and great at working with people.<br>
<br>
The <b>Staff Accountant</b> will perform a wide range of timely accounting duties and responsibilities, including but not limited to: general ledger reconciliation, journal entry creation, financial statement and report preparation, account reconciliation and analysis, general sales and cost accounting support, and project work.</p>
<p>Are you a self-starter with the ability to work independently, meet deadlines, drive improvement and lead successful change within the organization?</p>
<p><b>If this sounds like you, we'd love to talk!</b><br>
</p>
<p><b>Requirements &amp; Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Associates Degree in Accounting, Bachelors of Science in Accounting Strongly Desired, or</li>
<li>4 Years Related Accounting Experience, or</li>
<li>Understanding of GAAP financials is desired</li>
<li>Any combination thereof will be considered</li>
<li>Monthly closing of Financial Statements including A/P Accruals and other General Journal entries</li>
<li>Weekly, Monthly, Quarterly, &amp; Annual internal and external reporting duties</li>
<li>Monthly Bank Reconciliations</li>
<li>Run Daily/Weekly/Monthly Sales, Loan Collateral Assignments, Writeoffs, &amp; Account Buyback processes.</li>
<li>Create/update reports as assigned by the Accounting Manager</li>
<li>Assist with Year End Audit and Bank Exams</li>
<li>Limited Internal Audit duties</li>
<li>Monitor Daily, Weekly, Monthly Cash Collections for Securitizations &amp; Lines of Credit</li>
<li>Must be able to work effectively with co-workers in a professional manner</li>
<li>Must be able to communicate clearly in writing and verbally</li>
<li>Must have the ability to use a telephone, and computer terminal/software packages</li>
<li>Must have sufficient experience or training to demonstrate knowledge and ability to perform the job functions as defined above</li>
<li>Great communication skills and the ability to work effectively with a spectrum of personalities as well as excellent listening skills</li>
<li>Self-starter with ability to identify needs and areas of impact, as well as potential solutions</li>
<li>Team player who works well under pressure, and is flexible in the face of changing priorities and needs</li>
<li>Superb attention to detail and organizational skills</li>
<li>Strong prioritization skills and a track record of consistently going above and beyond with outside-the-box thinking</li>
<li>Strong Computer skills with working knowledge of Windows applications</li>
<li>Incredibly bright individual who works well under pressure and has a passion to learn and contribute</li>
</ul>
