<h2>Taco Bell Crew Member</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div><div><div>
<div>
<div>UNDER DESIGNATED SUPERVISION, PREPARES AND SERVES A LIMITED VARIETY OF SIMPLE FOODS THAT REQUIRES VERY LIMITED AND MINIMAL COOKING SKILLS. THIS POSITION REQUIRES ASSOCIATE TO BE FRIENDLY AND PASSIONATELY FOCUSED ON DELIVERING A PERSONALIZED CUSTOMER EXPERIENCE. AS REQUIRED BY CHANGING WORK STATIONS IN THE FACILITY, ON A REGULAR AND RECURRING BASIS, PERFORMS THE FOLLOWING:<br>
<br>
1. OPERATES A VARIETY OF KITCHEN EQUIPMENT, SUCH AS AN OVEN, GRILL, DEEP-FAT FRYER, OR OTHER, IN A SITUATION WHERE SET PROCEDURES ARE FOLLOWED, WHERE COOKING SKILLS ARE MINIMIZED AND WHERE OPERATIONS PERFORMED ARE REPETITIVE.<br>
<br>
2. TAKES CUSTOMERS FOOD SELECTION ORDERS BY LISTENING TO NEEDS, OFFERING SUGGESTIONS AND THANKING CUSTOMERS FOR THEIR BUSINESS. DEMONSTRATES KNOWLEDGE BY MAKING RECOMMENDATIONS OF NEW FOOD ITEMS.
<br>
3. ASSEMBLES SANDWICHES AND OTHER FOOD PRODUCTS.<br>
<br>
4. PARTICIPATES, ALONG WITH SUPERVISORS/MANAGEMENT STAFF, IN THE ESTABLISHMENT AND ACHIEVEMENT OF SERVICE AND SALES GOALS FOR THE FOOD ACTIVITY. ASSISTS IN INSURING OPTIMUM STOCK AVAILABILITY BY TAKING PERIODIC STOCK COUNTS; ADVISING MANAGEMENT STAFF OF SLOW-MOVING, OUT-OF-STOCK, DEFECTIVE, AND DAMAGED MERCHANDISE. RESOLVES CUSTOMER COMPLAINTS AT THE LOWEST LEVEL AND INFORMS MANAGEMENT OF BARRIERS TO MEETING THE OPTIMAL CUSTOMER EXPERIENCE.<br>
<br>
5. INSURES REQUIRED LEVEL OF SANITATION IS MAINTAINED WITHIN THE ASSIGNED WORKING AREA AND ASSISTS IN CLEANING OTHER AREAS, AS REQUIRED.<br>
<br>
6. ASSISTS IN UNLOADING, RECEIVING, AND STORING FOOD AND EXPENSE SUPPLIES.<br>
<br>
7. PERFORMS OTHER RELATED DUTIES AS ASSIGNED.<br>
<br>
<br>
ACTIVELY COMMUNICATES/PROMOTES THE BENEFITS OF THE IN-STORE CREDIT CARD TO CUSTOMERS IN ORDER TO DRIVE SALES GROWTH AND INCREASE CUSTOMER LOYALTY</div>

<p><b>Who We Are</b></p>
<div>The Army &amp; Air Force Exchange Service, also known as “the Exchange,” is the retailer on US Army and Air Force installations worldwide. An $8 billion enterprise ranked among the top retailers, the Exchange operates department stores, convenience stores, restaurants, movie theaters and an internet shopping site to serve Soldiers, Airmen and their families. In addition to US bases in Germany, Italy, England, Turkey, South Korea and Japan, the Exchange operates stores for US troops in Iraq, Afghanistan and other countries in the region. The Exchange ensures Soldiers and Airmen have access to US goods and services, wherever they serve.</div>
