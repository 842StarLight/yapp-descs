<p><b>Overview:<br>
</b></p>
<p></p>
<p>Chevron Oronite Company is a leading developer, manufacturer, and marketer of performance additives for fuels and lubricating oils. It is a subsidiary of Chevron Corporation. We market a broad range of products in 16 different product lines. Headquartered in San Ramon, Chevron Oronite is organized into three primary functions; Supply &amp; Manufacturing, Sales, and Products &amp; Technology that operate globally across several regions that include: Americas, Asia Pacific, Europe, Africa, and the Middle East.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Responsibilities for this position may include but are not limited to:<br>
</b></p>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Functions as a project team member that is responsible for generating and evaluating alternatives for material movements as well as transportation equipment selection and management within a global supply chain network.
</li>
<li>Coordinates logistics from source to destination utilizing a network of internal and external stakeholders including; customer service, planning and scheduling, operations, quality assurance, terminals, 3PL’s, freight forwarders, customs brokers, transportation companies, etc.
</li>
<li>Responsible for generating, distributing, and filing documentation for all modes of transportation in accordance with company and regulatory requirements.
</li>
<li>Maintains Key Performance Indicators of logistics service providers.
</li>
<li>Supports the evaluation, selection and onboarding processes of logistics service providers.
</li>
<li>Selects and/or specifies equipment based on source and destination handling requirements.
</li>
<li>Facilitates SAP setup for new supply chain execution processes for inventory and quality management.
</li>
<li>Manages the logistics and maintenance of ISO and railcar fleets that are dedicated to the transport and staging of materials.
</li>
<li>Focal point of contact for all communications regarding issues with assigned modes of transportation.
</li>
<li>Leads root cause analysis and corrective action development and implementation processes.
</li>
<li>Provides “on call” support as needed for twenty-four-hour operations.
</li>
<li>Self-motivated with the desire and ability to shape the position and optimize existing and new supply chains.
</li>
<li>Strong communication with internal and external stakeholders
</li>
</ul>
