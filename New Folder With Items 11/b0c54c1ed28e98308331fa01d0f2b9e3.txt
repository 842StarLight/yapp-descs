<h1 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Overview:
</b></h1>Position requires a person who is strong in practical and technical skills and analytical skills in the field of meat and poultry processing. The person must be able to lead a team to drive conclusions and communicate findings to Sr. Management through data collection and reporting. Requires the ability to direct and handle multiple tasks at one time and work within a project timeline. It is essential, that the person have the ability to communicate the department mission and objectives in a clear and persuasive manner. Requires the ability to adapt to unforeseen circumstances and the ability to resolve conflict with minimal assistance.

<ul>
<li>Take all measures to ensure internal and external customers total satisfaction.
</li>
<li>Must be able to independently work in a meat processing lab environment.
</li>
<li>Supervise and perform the daily projects assigned by department.
</li>
<li>Exercises good communication skills.
</li>
<li>Independently run the R&amp;D lab and maintain all equipment.
</li>
<li>Help build and maintain an effective R &amp; D team.
</li>

</li>
