<h2>Sales Director</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>RADAR (www.radarfirst.com), the recognized leader in privacy incident response management software, is seeking an experienced and highly motivated sales professional to drive revenue growth in a selected geographic territory. Reporting directly to the Chief Revenue Officer, the successful candidate will have a proven track record of articulating business value to executives in SasS-based enterprise software sales environment.</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div>
The Sales Director will be responsible for new client/revenue acquisition. You will be executing a repeatable sales model and delivering predictable and reliable sales performance through territory development, sales process advancement, deal strategy, objection handling, and closing skills. Additionally, you will work closely with cross-functional teams to collaborate on opportunities and provide feedback to the marketing and product team.</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div>
This role is focused on generating new business in an emerging, high-growth market segment with a strong emphasis on prospecting and effective pipeline management. As a part of your role you will be tasked with accelerating the market awareness of RADAR within your geography as a market leader. This will include public speaking events, networking, participation in industry groups and establishing relationships with influencers in the market.</div>
<br>
<br>
 <div><b>What You’ll Do</b></div>
<ul>
 <li>Drive new customer acquisition within an assigned territory</li>
<li>Drive revenue growth by meeting and exceeding quota objectives</li>
<li>Gain access and influence C-level executives</li>
<li>Grow awareness through public speaking events, networking, participation in industry groups, and establishing relationships with influencers in the market.</li>
<li>Manage all aspects of the sales process</li>
<li>Ensure CRM (SalesForce) records are kept current</li>
<li>Participate in sales forecasting and planning</li>
<li>Ensure effective handoff of new client at the close of the sales process</li>
<li>Become recognized as a subject matter expert in the company’s business space</li>
<li>Maintain the highest ethical standards</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p>
 </p>
<div><b>What You Must Have:</b></div>
<ul>
 <li>Highly motivated and ambitious self-starter with a competitive approach to closing business</li>
<li>Exceptional active listening abilities</li>
<li>Strong curiosity and an interest in learning customer’s needs</li>
<li>Effective skills in delivering sales presentations and product demonstrations</li>
<li>Strong business acumen, negotiation skills, and conceptual and analytical abilities</li>
<li>Experience selling enterprise software in the financial services, insurance, and healthcare industries</li>
<li>Outstanding communication skills, both written and verbal</li>
<li>Willing to challenge the status quo while gaining the customer’s trust and respect</li>
</ul>
<div><b>What We’d Like You to Have:</b></div>
<ul>
 <li>Proven track record selling enterprise SaaS software solutions in a startup or growth environment with a proven ability to meet and exceed sales objectives</li>
<li>3-8 years of SaaS-based enterprise software field sales experience</li>
<li>Experience in privacy, security, and/or compliance is highly desirable</li>
<li>College degree</li>
</ul>
<div>This is an opportunity to join a fast-paced company with demonstrated leadership and success in a new and exciting industry. RADAR’s clients include numerous Fortune 500 companies who rely on RADAR to maintain compliance with data breach notification laws.</div>
