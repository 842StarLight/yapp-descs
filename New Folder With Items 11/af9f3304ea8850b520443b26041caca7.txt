<h2>Channel Marketing & Enablement Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>ABOUT THE ROLE:</p>
<p>
Greenlight Guru is looking for an experienced Channel Marketing &amp; Enablement Manager to ramp up our channel marketing &amp; enablement programs. You will collaborate with our channel sales and demand generation teams to drive a broad range of go-to-market initiatives spanning partner recruitment, events, webcasts, incentives, partner communications, enablement and demand creation.</p>
<p>
Reporting to the VP of Marketing, this role requires a “jack-of-all-trades” mentality that is proactive, can create their own solutions when needed, and can work across departments. Your creativity will help strengthen our partner relationships through the creation of unique brand experiences, and your analytical mindset will serve you well as you collaborate with our field and inbound marketing teams to drive demand with and through our partner community.</p>
<p>
The ideal candidate will bring strategic vision, marketing savvy, strong communication skills and a passion for execution. This individual will play a key role in our partner organization and help expand Greenlight Guru's market reach by growing our partnerships with key QA/RA consultants, consulting groups and our ecosystem partners.</p>
<p>
RESPONSIBILITIES:</p>
<ul>
<li>
<p>Define, develop, and execute Greenlight Guru Partner/Ecosystem/Channel marketing and enablement strategy</p>
</li>
<li>Focus on 3 key areas:
<ul>
<li>
<b>Market to partners</b> to maintain a consistent flow of inbound partner interest through project managing webinars, email campaigns, and content initiatives</li>
<li>
<b>Market through partners</b> in order to help our partners bring leads to Greenlight Guru by building scalable marketing kits, sponsoring field marketing events, etc.
</li>
<li>
<b>Market for our partners</b> to our customer ecosystem through project managing webinars, email campaigns, and content initiatives
</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>
<p>Be measured on contribution to the lead generation pipeline to/through/for partners (3 key areas above)</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Own a consistent partner communication program (monthly webinar, monthly newsletter, partner community, Partner of the Month program, etc.)</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Work with key stakeholders to prioritize Partner Enablement needs based on key behaviors that will successfully drive channels business</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Collaborate closely with all members of the GTM enablement team to understand what assets exist, or need to be re-purposed or created</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Drive cross functional collaboration, in particular with product marketing, channels and direct sales</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Prioritize, design, develop, deliver and measure impact of partner enablement initiatives in conjunction with the partner sales teams</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Continue and improve current programs: Partner portal training, monthly partner webinars, regional instructor-led partner training</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Create and implement an effective Partner Certification program</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Develop new and innovative programs for additional impact</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Self-starter who is able to strategize, execute and build Greenlight Guru partner enablement into a world class program using best practices and pushing the envelope on new ways to be more successful</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p>REQUIREMENTS</p>
<ul>
<li>
<p>3+ years of direct experience building and managing partner marketing and enablement programs, or 5+ years of experience working alongside Partnership teams in related roles: marketing campaign manager, field marketing, partner development (sales/bizdev), etc.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>A successful track record of developing partner lead pipeline through webinars, email campaigns, events, and other creative programs</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Experience working for a fast-growing B2B software company, where channel sales was done in conjunction with a direct sales team</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>A team builder, and know how to get multiple departments to play well together towards the same end goal</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Strong project management and organizational skills, ability to manage multiple projects in tandem</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>A passionate individual with a knack for solving problems and building/delivering solutions, and you’re OK with taking risks (and learning from failure)</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>An entrepreneur at heart. You’re flexible and can thrive in environments that don’t have defined processes and you’re excited to help, build, launch - and market - minimum viable products (it’s a scaleup after all!)</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Humble, but your colleagues would say that you’re a game-changer and an “A player”</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>A generally curious person and you love to listen and learn</p>
</li>
</ul>

<p>
Voted one of the top places to work in Indiana, Greenlight Guru' innovative SaaS offering is revolutionizing the way medical device companies invent, design, manufacture, sell, and support their products. With a consistent focus on providing fanatical support for our clients, being innovative, and fostering a culture of closing, Greenlight Guru is poised for exceptional growth and looking for the right people to help facilitate this growth and help us achieve our mission of improving the quality of life.</p>
