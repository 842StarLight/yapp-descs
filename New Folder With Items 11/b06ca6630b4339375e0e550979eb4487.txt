<h2>Product Manager, Cervical Spine</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div></div>
<div>
<div>As a Product Manager at NuVasive, you spearhead product development, leading a product from inception through the development process partnering with engineers, surgeons and a diverse population of internal/external teams on design iterations through commercialization and launch. This is an amazing opportunity for someone with passion and drive to manage products in several stages of development (i.e. near-term launch, pending FDA approval and new products). You will come up the product name, associated branding, technique, sales training and product launch with revenue goals in mind. Because you are result-oriented, you take pride in optimizing profit as well as meeting marketing, financial and corporate growth objectives by working across diverse internal and external teams. Your business plans focus on balance, emphasis, profitability, thoroughness, and attention to long and short range consequences and optimization of both marketing share and profitability of existing and new products.
<p><b>Essential Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Conduct marketing analysis to develop product definitions, coordinate technical product development, estimates of potential profits, and release to production as well as a prioritized list of customer and market requirements for the product</li>
<li>
Conduct market research to identify/track market trends, produce competitive analysis/materials to compare like products from our competitors and product selection/definition</li>
<li>
Perform duties in compliance with applicable FDA and state regulations as well as standards.
</li>
<li>Perform management and leadership responsibilities for new shareowners including interviewing, training, assigning work, appraising performance, addressing complaints, and resolving problems</li>
</ul>
</div>

<b>About NuVasive</b><div>
NuVasive, Inc. (NASDAQ: NUVA) is the leader in spine technology innovation, focused on transforming spine surgery and beyond with minimally disruptive, procedurally integrated solutions designed to deliver reproducible and clinically-proven surgical outcomes. The Company's portfolio includes access instruments, implantable hardware, biologics, software systems for surgical planning, navigation and imaging solutions, magnetically adjustable implant systems for spine and orthopedics, and intraoperative monitoring service offerings. With more than $1 billion in revenues, NuVasive has approximately 2,600 employees and operates in more than 50 countries serving surgeons, hospitals and patients. For more information, please visit www.nuvasive.com.</div>
