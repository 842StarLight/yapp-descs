<div><b>The Role</b></div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div>The Sr. Consultant, Advisor Technical Services-Estate &amp; Business Succession will provide subject matter expertise and direction to Lincoln Financial Network advisors and a national financial planning team seeking counsel, direction &amp; comprehensive personal financial planning. The position will support the advisors and team with advanced tax and legal functions needed for the estate, business succession, and income tax planning marketplace. S/he will collaborate with stakeholders to develop financial models, plans, and project materials using Lincoln approved software to support advisors' external client estate, business succession, and income tax planning needs. S/he will support case design, interpret legal and income tax matters, provide clear guidance on client solutions/problems, and research matters related to complex financial planning topics.</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div><b>Responsibilities</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Provides direction on complex case assignments, projects, and/or initiatives to build and enhance the capability of his/her assigned area(s) of responsibility.</li>
<li>Collaborates and consults with advisors and other team members to build complex financial plans and models to support external client needs.</li>
<li>Acts as a subject matter expert in design and debrief meetings to identify case-specific observations and makes appropriate recommendations to advisors.</li>
<li>Reviews executed estate / business succession documents and income tax returns and provides summaries, observations, and/or recommendations based on the specific client fact patterns.</li>
<li>Reviews, identifies, and communicates recommendations on complex and high net worth cases to advisors and other team members.</li>
<li>Utilizes Lincoln approved financial planning software tools to create complex models for advisors' client needs.</li>
<li>Acts as the point of contact for field advisors and staff seeking technical and/or planning support from LFN's National Planning Center.</li>
<li>Reviews advisor, client, and management sales materials to assure technical compliance.</li>
<li>Develops and analyzes new case designs to satisfy a constantly changing market environment.</li>
<li>Maintains a national speaking presence at company-wide training events.</li>
<li>Coaches advisors on estate, business succession, and income tax issues.</li>
<li>Directs training on financial planning &amp; timely estate, business succession, and income tax planning topics to team members, advisors, and office staff when necessary.</li>
<li>Maintains knowledge on current and emerging developments/trends for assigned area(s) of responsibility, assesses the impact, and collaborates with senior management to incorporate new trends and developments in current and future solutions.</li>
<li>Directs and enhances organizational initiatives by positively influencing and supporting change management and/or departmental/enterprise initiatives within assigned area(s) of responsibility.</li>
<li>Identifies and directs the implementation of process improvements that significantly improve quality across the team, department and/or business unit for his/her assigned area(s) of responsibility.</li>
<br>
</ul>

</b></div>

</ul>
<div><b>Experience</b></div>

<li>5 – 7+ years of financial planning experience in a law or accounting firm or financial services provider dealing with estate, business succession, and income tax planning.</li>
<li>Preferably including tax, business law, tax accounting, business planning (structuring entities), qualified plans, and/or real estate. Having advanced marketing/advanced underwriting experience or equivalent is also desirable.</li>
<li>Strong legal and tax background with an ability to apply and communicate that information in a variety of settings.</li>
<li>Familiarity with insurance products (e.g., life insurance, annuities, and long-term care).</li>
