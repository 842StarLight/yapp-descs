Alys Beach is carefully crafted to celebrate the public realm of streets, parks, pedestrian paths, and great civic buildings in a manner reminiscent of the world’s most beloved seaside towns. Planned by Duany Plater-Zyberk &amp; Company, Alys Beach is a Traditional Neighborhood Development (TND) whose architectural vision has its roots in the style of Bermuda architecture and in the courtyards of Antigua, Guatemala, the colonial capital of Central America.</div>

<div>
<p>Project manager – Residential construction</p>
</div>
<div><p>Job Summary
</p></div>
<p>EBSCO Industries is seeking a Project Manager to join the Project Team with the oversight of construction projects from start to finish utilizing computer and communication skills as well as working in a team environment with an established and growing residential construction company. This position is with Alys Beach Construction, a residential Contractor located in Alys Beach, FL.
</p>
<div><p>Job Responsibilities
</p></div>
<ul>
<li>Perform a key role in project planning, budgeting, permitting, and identification of resources needed</li>
<li>Project accounting functions including developing an estimate, establishing the budget, managing the budget, tracking of team expenses, and minimizing exposure and risk in the project</li>
<li>Ensure that construction activities progress per predetermined schedule (i.e. using BuilderTrend software)</li>
<li>Periodic inspection of construction sites</li>
<li>Communicate effectively with the subcontractors responsible for completing various phases of the project</li>
<li>Coordinate the efforts of all parties involved in the project, which include the sales team, architects, interior designers, suppliers, sub-contractors and laborers</li>
<li>Maintain strict adherence to the budgetary guidelines, quality and safety standards</li>
</ul>
<div><p>Job Requirements
</p></div>
<ul>
<li>Proficient in the use of all Microsoft Office applications (i.e., Outlook, Word, Excel)</li>
<li>Minimum 15 years of project management experience to include high end residential construction, commercial construction, and renovations/remodels</li>
<li>Ability to communicate effectively both verbally and in writing with team members, clients, subcontractors, and interdepartmental personnel</li>
<li>College Degree, B.A.</li>
</ul>
<div>
<p>Essential Job Functions</p>
</div>
<ul>
<li>Possesses good time management, negotiation, and analytical/problem-resolution skills with respect for co-workers</li>
<li>Written/verbal communication for project team coordination (Homeowners, Builder, Designers, Architects, Consultants, and Subcontractors) to address challenges promptly</li>
<li>Electronic device competency for usage of computer, MS Surface, and/or smart devices</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Committed to doing quality work with attention to detail that can leave a paper/electronic trail easily understood by other team members while meeting deadlines</li>
<li>Current with new industry trends/ practices and willingly to participate in industry events
</li>
</ul>
<div><p>Preferred Skills
</p></div>
<ul>
<li>Possess strong mathematical and computer skills. Proficiency with all MS Outlook, Office products (Word, Excel, PowerPoint), scheduling software (i.e. BuilderTrend)</li>
<li>Ability to breakdown construction operations into a logical sequence of activities</li>
<li>Ability to plan and organize a team effort, as well as work in a team environment</li>
<li>Must be able to meet deadlines
</li>
<li>Effective oral and written communication skills amongst team members, clients, as well as interdepartmental personnel</li>
</ul>
