<h2>Customer Project Manager I</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>ABOUT SICK:
</b></p>
<p>SICK is a leading global provider of intelligent sensors, systems and services for factory, logistics and process automation applications. With more than 1,000 patents, innovation and technology are at its core. This focus on innovation and “Sensor Intelligence” have allowed SICK to develop products for every phase of production in the automotive, packaging, electronics, food and beverage, consumer goods, storage and conveyor, robotics, material handling, oil and gas, chemical, power, maritime industries and more. In addition, SICK’s focus on Sensor Intelligence allows us to make Industry 4.0, or the Industrial Internet of Things, a reality for their customers.</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>POSITION SUMMARY:
</b></p>
<p>Responsible for the development of and preparing complete system’s configurations, accurate proposal/quote and service estimates, complete bid packages and commercial documents using Sick’s best practices, standard tools/templates and procedures. Review and coordinate with Engineering, Solutions Engineering and additional resources to establish a complete and accurate solution, ensure competitive positioning, and pricing strategies to maximize revenue and gross margin dollars while minimizing risk based on scope identification of system requirements in RFP documents. Meet all required Bid and Proposal deadlines, works closely with Sales and Legal to vet Contracts, Terms &amp; Conditions, and Service Agreements.</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>RESPONSIBILITIES:
</b></p>
<ul><li>Utilization and overall responsible for the SICK CPM processes. Allowing effective management and reporting on customer quotations and proposals that exist at SICK and to provide transparency for CPM process until a purchase order is received or project is deemed closed by customer.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Support the Process Automation and Oil &amp; Gas sales channels through the proposal process, providing pricing analysis and guidelines as well as technical solutions that fit the needs of the customer.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Develop the technical and commercial solution for full project scope; including installation planning with project or other project specific special requirements to fulfill the project scope.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Develop templates and tools to be utilized by the sales team to support project scope definition, project hand-over and the CPM process.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Maintain quotation data, quotation status, and perform calculations pertaining to customer quotations assigned. Prepare detailed and summary reports pertaining to customer quotations and the Process Automation Technical Services team.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Coordinate with other disciplines/functions in the company to manage work packages necessary to fulfill identified scope of projects. This includes, but is not limited to, legal reviews of terms and conditions and controlling functions of the project budgets.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Stay up-to-date on industry advancements and competitor’s offerings as well as customer history in order to better assist in the guidance of the Pre-sales customer project management process phases.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Total understanding of the entire CPM Process from CPM 0 – CPM 8 across the multiple phases and milestones.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Regular attendance and timeliness is a must.</li></ul>
<p></p>

</b></p>
<p>Education and Experience</p>
<ul><li>Four-year degree or equivalent experience required.</li></ul>
<ul><li>3+ years of experience in customer project management and/or customer proposals; preferred in the Process Automation, Chemical, Petrochemical, EPC and Oil &amp; Gas Industries.</li></ul>
<p>Other Qualifications</p>

<ul><li>Excellent verbal and written communication skills.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Ability to manage multiple simultaneous tasks and responsibilities.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Industry and computer software (Access, SharePoint, EDB, SAP, etc.) experience is a plus.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Process development and tracking is a plus.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Contract, legal and logistical, experience is a plus</li></ul>
<p>Computer Skills</p>
<ul><li>Computer proficiency with Microsoft (Word, Excel, Outlook, PowerPoint) and Internet applications</li></ul>
