<p><b>Sales Account Executive</b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>IT/ Network Sales Account Executive specializing in the Education Vertical.</b></p>
<p>International IT company located in New York seeks a qualified Sr. Sales account executive with a minimum of five years’ experience promoting and negotiating contracts for IT/ Network sales, to sell our IIX, managed IT services, and network products for our U.S. sites. Qualified person must have experience with the Education (college or university) verticals, be a team player, well organized, detailed in managing work assignments, and service orientated when dealing with clients. The candidate must have excellent written, verbal and pc network and application skills; four-year degree in business, marketing; or a 2-year degree with five years of telecommunications or related sales and administrative experience.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Desired Skills and Experience</b></p>
<p><b>Specific Responsibilities Include:</b></p>
<ul><li> Must have min 5-year experience with IT/Network sales</li></ul>
<ul><li> Must be familiar with college or university IT/Network market in NY/NJ</li></ul>
<ul><li> Must know the right IT contact of SUNY and/or CUNY</li></ul>
<ul><li> Must know the right IT contact of colleges and/or universities in Philadelphia</li></ul>
<ul><li> Successfully prospect new opportunities via established relationships</li></ul>
<ul><li> Solid Education vertical decision-making contacts to approach</li></ul>
<ul><li> “Hunter” role requiring background of cold calling prowess, i.e. someone who can go out and find own potential clients, booking multiple appointments in a day</li></ul>
<ul><li> Also requires a degree of “Farmer” mentality in order to retain and grow individual customers after concluding initial deal</li></ul>
<ul><li> Build your personal sales funnel through:</li></ul>
<ul><li> “Cold lead development – telesales, hosted industry events (open-houses, etc), cold-calling, following up on marketing lead generated opportunities, etc. “Warm lead development – existing account expansion, RFP response, former account recovery, etc.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Meet or exceed monthly sales quotas.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Identify and develop partners to accelerate sales (including agents, datacenter partners, etc).</li></ul>
<ul><li> Develop familiarity with Salesforce.com and utilize as basis for: contact management database, sales opportunity tracking, sales forecast and report generation.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Strong commitment to maintain &amp; update weekly reports on Salesforce.com for management</li></ul>
<ul><li> Close sales to new and existing customers</li></ul>
<ul><li> Manage long-term relationships with customers</li></ul>
