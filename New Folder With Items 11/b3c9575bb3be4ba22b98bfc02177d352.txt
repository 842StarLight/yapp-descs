<p><b>General Summary of Position:</b></p>
<p>We are seeking business-oriented, customer service driven professionals to provide high quality service to Fortune 500 level customers.</p>
<p><b>Duties and Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Maintain and manage Granite’s Premier accounts.</li>
<li>24 x 7 availability to support your assigned accounts.</li>
<li>Conduct weekly meetings with assigned Premier accounts.</li>
<li>Minute each meeting weekly and follow up on all actions</li>
<li>Areas of responsibility for each account include but are not limited to maintenance of phone lines, outages, billing, reporting, etc.</li>
<li>Communicate directly with customers, sales and customer service staff.</li>
</ul>
