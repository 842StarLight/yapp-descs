<h2>Texas Educational Courseware Representative</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<b>Description:</b><br>
Hawkes Learning Systems, a fast-growing software company and textbook publisher, is seeking candidates for a full-time Educational Courseware Representative in Texas (Dallas, TX or Houston, TX). Representatives will be responsible for building the presence of our educational courseware at colleges and universities in regional territory that includes the entire state of Texas.
<br><br>
Candidates must also be available to relocate for a training period of up to 6 months at company’s Charleston, SC headquarters upon job offer or be willing to relocate to Texas after training period.
<br><br>
<b>Duties include, but not limited to, the following:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Visit college campuses within assigned territory developing relationships with faculty members to sell our educational materials and maintain current accounts</li>
<li>Travel to sales presentations, trade shows, implementation meetings, and customer maintenance visits</li>
<li>Develop and coordinate sales strategy for designated geographic market</li>
<li>Maintain contact database</li>
</ul>
Competitive salary plus commission structure and excellent benefits provided.
<br><br>
Hawkes Learning offers a casual work environment that encourages collaboration and teamwork, within and across departments. Our team is comprised of extremely talented and highly motivated members, who make working at Hawkes a very rewarding experience.
<br><br>
Company events, including food trucks, birthday and ‘Hawkes’iversary celebrations, volunteer opportunities and team milestone festivities keep our office environment lively and exciting. We invite you to take a closer look and see if joining the Hawkes family is the right fit for you.
<br><br>
<b>Requirements:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s Degree with a minimum cumulative 3.3 GPA</li>
<li>1 to 5 years of sales experience</li>
<li>Ability and willingness to travel (extensively during selling season)</li>
<li>Ability to work independently from a home office</li>
<li>Public speaking experience</li>
<li>Excellent organizational and time management skills</li>
</ul>
