<h2>Business Development Executive (Enterprise)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>MicroD is seeking an experienced Business Development Executive to be responsible for growing assigned accounts and selling new accounts to achieve a sales plan that supports the strategic growth of MicroD. The Business Development Executive will cultivate and close new business by developing relationships with industry contacts and promoting MicroD's broad range of progressive digital solutions and services.</p>
<p>
Are you passionate about taking great care of customers? Are you a sales hunter? Do you love finding, cultivating and closing new business? Want to contribute to a rapidly growing organization? We want you to join our team! The ideal candidate will successfully:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Conduct solution-oriented sales with new business prospects</li>
<li>
Gather and understand customer needs and propose a long-term value proposition</li>
<li>
Prospect for new clients in-person, via Social Media, email, and telephone in collaboration with our Marketing department</li>
<li>
Research, identify and qualify potential new business leads</li>
<li>
Follow a proven sales process to make sales in a disciplined way</li>
<li>
Develop trusting relationships with various contacts within furniture manufacturers and retail furniture stores</li>
<li>
Close deals priced at acceptable profitability levels</li>
<li>
Maintain strong customer relationships and work closely with the Project Management team to fulfill client requests</li>
</ul>
<p>
POSITION REQUIREMENTS</p>
<ul>
<li>
Bachelor's Degree or equivalent experience</li>
<li>
4+ years' experience conducting solution-sales</li>
<li>
Demonstrated ability to meet or exceed sales goals with large quotas</li>
<li>
Demonstrated passion for technology, people and customer service</li>
<li>
Experience using Salesforce or similar CRM</li>
<li>
Experience selling technology, experience with SaaS is a plus</li>
<li>
Furniture industry knowledge is a plus</li>
<li>
Occasional travel outside of the local area and overnight required</li>
</ul>
<p>
