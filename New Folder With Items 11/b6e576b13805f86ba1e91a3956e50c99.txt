<h2>Social Media Associate</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Inspire and deliver exceptional value to travelers around the globe as part of the Apple Leisure Group (ALG) team! Our vision is to be the best-in-class and largest all-inclusive resort management and travel services provider, differentiated by the collaboration of our unique resort and distribution brands.</p>
<p>
Grow your career as a <b>Social Media Associate </b>within AMResorts, one of our 14 industry-leading brands. In this role you will work across all brands, sharing your love of social media and the latest insights and trends in the digital media space that can be used to promote all AMResorts brands. You will collaborate with other sales &amp; marketing departments. You will also assist with the coordination of specific communications campaigns, and integrated mobile technologies.</p>
<p><b>
A successful Social Media Associate will:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Execute content gathering and editing of video and imagery for social media channels, when necessary.</li>
<li>Drives the content calendar proofing efforts for brand content.</li>
<li>Reach out and engage target audiences through various social mediums to create online conversations about the brands, promotions and services.</li>
<li>Execute the coordination of social media campaigns for multiple brands including, but not limited to blogger outreach, social network initiatives, social news participation, keyword optimization, press release optimization, mobile device marketing and virtual worlds participation.</li>
<li>Drive the tracking and monitoring of the success of online initiatives (i.e. impressions, reach influence, etc.), and provide reports for executive management.</li>
<li>Coordinate the specific duties necessary to educate internal &amp; external staff on the implementation of new technologies and platforms in the social space.</li>
<li>Assist Marketing Department in creating and implementing social media campaigns to promote the brands and AMResorts.</li>
<li>Identify and report on digital/social media trends to Senior Manager.</li>
<li>Identify and execute implementation of digital/social media tools as needed.</li>
<li>Develop the digital/social media portion of initiatives and incentives.</li>
<li>Execute tactics to provide input to manage our online presence – web sites, blogs, etc.</li>
<li>Actively participate in relevant forums to elevate our position in digital/social media with a working knowledge of social media paid advertising campaigns including Facebook, LinkedIn and Twitter</li>
<li>Stay up to date with all Social Media industry developments and best practices</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Requirements:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s Degree in Communication, Marketing or equivalent work experience</li>
<li>Experience in content creation including but not limited to Facebook, Instagram, Twitter, Pinterest, Snapchat and emerging social networks</li>
<li>1-3 years’ experience in Marketing with a focus in social and digital media</li>
<li>Bilingual in Spanish preferred</li>
<li>Strong understanding of user-generated content management, content marketing and reputation management</li>
<li>Knowledge of resort hospitality will be helpful</li>
<li>Ability to multi-task</li>
<li>Sales/Marketing experience</li>
<li>Must be friendly and approachable both online and offline</li>
<li>Ability to build rapport internally and externally</li>
<li>Must be able to travel internationally when required</li>
<li>Excellent written and oral communication skills</li>
<li>Excellent PC literacy skills, with a strong emphasis in PowerPoint (Excel, Word, etc.…)</li>
<li>Excellent time management and project management skills</li>
<li>Ability to achieve defined objectives with minimum supervision</li>
<li>Attention to detail, proactive and good organization</li>
</ul>
