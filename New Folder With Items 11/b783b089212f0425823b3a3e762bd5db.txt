<h2>Revenue Analyst</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Revenue Analyst with the overall responsibility for initial set-up, documentation, and tracking of sales contracts in accordance with the Company’s policy. Individual will be part of a team effort to perform proper compliance and documentation of all new sale global transactions.
</p>
<ul>
<li>Review customer contracts for new and add-on license and/or SaaS sales by completing deal analysis sheet:
<ul>
<li>Perform initial review of sales contracts
</li>
<li>Input contract detail, pricing, and customer information
</li>
<li>Document contract key data points
</li>
<li>Highlight contract points for additional review in accordance with company policy and authoritative accounting guidance
</li>
<li>Follow-up with Contracts, Sales, and Professional Service Teams on any open items related to deal completion
</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>Contract processing:
<ul>
<li>Upload contract to accounting system
</li>
<li>Post invoices based on proper invoice type
</li>
<li>Submission of invoice to Finance Ops Team for customer delivery
</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>Create new bookings report for all new contract activity for the month, outlining each contract by obligation
</li>
<li>Stand-alone Selling Price (SSP) Monitoring – maintain on-going detail and analysis database of transactions to support SSP by revenue stream
</li>
<li>Assist with analysis and establishment of credit limits, including review of partner history, financial statements, and other relevant data
</li>
<li>Assist with monitoring and tracking qualitative deal information used for analysis under accounting revenue guidance
</li>
<li>Review stand-alone statement-of-work(s) and document initial revenue conclusion related to impact with any related license or SaaS contract and coordinate proper revenue treatment
</li>
<li>Familiarity with ASC 606 Revenue from Contracts with Customers
</li>
<li>Assist with processing of credit memo requests related to new sales and maintain supporting documentation/approvals
</li>
<li>Part of team to proactively work account collections as needed
</li>
<li>Assist in the documentation, creation, and maintenance of internal policies, procedures, and controls
</li>
<li>Create detailed reporting as needed for management purposes
</li>
<li>Assist Finance Ops Team with any transactional questions and renewals analysis as requested
</li>
<li>Prepare supporting documentation for audit and tax requests as needed
</li>
<li>Perform other duties and projects related to role as assigned
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p>Required Skills
</p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree in Accounting preferred
</li>
<li>Up to 2 years of relevant experience in industry, public accounting, academic, or combination
</li>
<li>Software industry experience a plus
</li>
<li>Excellent organization skills and detail oriented with the ability to handle multiple, competing tasks simultaneously
</li>
<li>Experience with an Accounting ERP system, experience with Intacct a plus
</li>
<li>Excellent communications skills and the ability to develop effective working relationships within and across departments
</li>
<li>Strong Excel skills and computer skills required
</li>
<li>Be part of a team that helps the company develop processes and procedures as the team and company grow, willingness to get into the detail
</li>
<li>Familiarity with Sarbanes-Oxley (SOX) requirements and documentation
</li>
<li>Strong analytical skills and leadership qualitie
</li>
</ul>
