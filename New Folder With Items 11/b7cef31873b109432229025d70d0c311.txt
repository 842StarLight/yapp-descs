<h2>Business Development Representative (FireMon)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">Description
<br><br>
<b>About the Position:</b><br>
We need motivated, self starters with a positive outlook to join our Business Development team. Ideally we would like candidates to have some exposure to corporate prospecting, but more importantly we are targeting individuals with high energy, great communication skills and the ambition to work hard. New graduates are urged to apply!
<br><br>
We are strong believers that cutting edge cybersecurity can have a huge impact on making the world a safer place and we want other people who feel that same passion. In return we can offer you the opportunity to work with and learn from a fast-growing, high performance team.
<br><br>
Your role will be to seek new business opportunities by contacting potential customers. In this position you are a driving force for the company by boosting sales and contributing to the long-term business growth.
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Set and execute on a cadence of phone, email, and social communications to build a pipeline of qualified opportunities within large enterprise organizations.</li>
<li>Manage the top for the sales funnel to ensure sales has pipeline to support goals.</li>
<li>Prospect and identify key executives at target companies.</li>
<li>Promptly follow-up and qualify all leads.</li>
<li>Prioritize strategic target account lists within a defined territory.</li>
<li>Research and build new and existing accounts.</li>
<li>Run discovery calls with prospects to qualify opportunities .</li>
<li>Maintain consistent levels of activity to reach assigned goals.</li>
<li>Manage, track, and report sales activities and results using internal CRM.</li>
<li>Coordinate marketing activities for your assigned territory with the Marketing team.</li>
<li>Report on trends and information discovered during the prospecting and qualification process. Coordinate marketing activities with the Marketing team.</li>
<li>Achieve monthly quotas of qualified opportunities.</li>
</ul>
<b>Required Skills and Experience:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor's degree</li>
<li>Excellent written/verbal communication skills</li>
<li>Strong problem solving skills</li>
<li>Motivation, drive and a self-starting attitude</li>
<li>Ability to work in a fast paced, team environment</li>
<li>Ability to understand customer needs and meet that need</li>
<li>Good time management with strong organizational skills</li>
</ul>
<b>Preferred Skills and Experience:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>CRM experience</li>
<li>6 Months of corporate prospecting experience</li>
</ul>
