<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<ul>
<li>Develops and manages new processes and improving current processes around customer setup, maintenance, and monitoring to manage the global customer master.</li>
<li>
Develops master data enrichment processes including working with outside service providers.
</li>
<li>Works with the Business, functional areas, and IT to analyze the effects of process and technical enhancements that interact with the customer master data from end to end from marketing &amp; sales to quote, order, invoice as well as fulfillment and field services.
</li>
<li>Develops and manages the global training materials for all customer master setup, maintenance, and monitoring processes as well as conducting global training when necessary.</li>
<li>
Develops monitoring reports and validation processes for the customer master data.</li>
<li>
Project manages the development of technical enhancements including trouble shooting solutions, business user testing, and production deployment.</li>
<li>
Manages mass migration of customer master data from acquisitions and supports the deployment efforts.</li>
<li>
Supervises and trains maintenance data stewards on customer maintenance processes.</li>
<li>
Develops business automation as needed.</li>
<li>
Performs other duties as directed.</li>
</ul>
</div></div></div>
