<h2>Guest Service Agent/Front Desk</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>SUMMARY STATEMENT</b></p>
<p>Assures highest possible level of visitor and guest satisfaction by providing superior customer service. Responsible for checking guests into and out of the hotel as well as answering phone calls and customer inquiries</p>
<p><b>PRIMARY DUTIES AND ACCOUNTABILITY</b></p>
<p><b>Room Assignments, Changes, and Issues </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Checks guests into and out of the hotel in an accurate, speedy, and courteous manner.</li>
<li>Efficiently handles all payments received whether they are cash, credit card, checks, or traveler’s checks. Balances/verifies all monetary transactions in the appropriate accounts.</li>
<li>Make guest reservations in a prompt and courteous manner, following the standard reservation systems, software and expectations of the company and of the brand</li>
<li>Ensures that all hotel keys are distributed to the appropriate parties and that guest privacy is maintained.</li>
<li>Responsible for maintaining all documentation regarding room registration, changes, charges, credit card transactions, customer service and maintenance issues.</li>
<li>Perform Night Audit functions as needed, and/or as requested by supervisor</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Guest Service </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Offers guests assistance whenever possible.</li>
<li>Responsible for resolving escalated customer relations issues.</li>
<li>Answers inquiries pertaining to hotel policies and services.</li>
<li>Must respond to and follow-up on all guest requests to ensure customers are satisfied with the hotel’s services and accommodations.</li>
<li>Responsible for positively representing and promoting the property.</li>
<li>Ensures systems and procedures are in place and followed for guest safety and security.</li>
<li>Deliver guest invoices if working overnight shifts</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Ongoing Other Duties as Assigned</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Ensures that the lobby and front desk area is clean, organized, visually appealing, and well stocked with all necessary supplies.</li>
<li>Properly compiles, completes, and distributes reports to all management and relevant staff.</li>
<li>Reports any maintenance, security, or safety issues to the appropriate staff.</li>
<li>Assist in other departments when the need arises, as directed by supervisor. This may include assisting with breakfast set up or clean-up, assisting with basic property cleanliness such as trash removal, assisting with basic clerical tasks to assist the sales, revenue and marketing efforts, and assisting with housekeeping and/or laundry needs</li>
</ul>
<p><b>SKILLS, EDUCATIONAL BACKGROUND AND EXPERIENCE</b></p>
<ul>
<li>High School Diploma or GED required.</li>
<li>Previous front desk experience highly preferred.</li>
<li>Prior experience using Windows based software including Microsoft Word, Excel, and Outlook highly preferred.</li>
<li>Excellent organizational and prioritization skills.  Ability to work well and collaborate with others.</li>
<li>Superior customer service skills.</li>
<li>Excellent communication skills.</li>
<li>May be required to stand for an extended period of time.</li>
<li>Able to work a flexible schedule.</li>
</ul>
<p>This position will also include a variety of multiple shifts from 7:00 AM-3:00 PM, 3:00 PM-11:00 PM, 11:00 PM-7:00 AM. Weekends are a must!! Prior hotel front desk experience preferred but not required</p>
