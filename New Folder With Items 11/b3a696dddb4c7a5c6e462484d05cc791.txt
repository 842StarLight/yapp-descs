 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div><b>Sr. Implementation Consultant</b></div>
<div></div>
<div>Cincinnati, OH</div>
<div></div>
<div>Job Summary</div>
<div></div>
<div>Paycor is a key player in the HCM software as a service market. We build, sell, and service our own proprietary payroll, time, and HR platform. The heart of our mission is to Take Care of the Client First and to Take Care of Each Other so that we can provide an equally rewarding client AND associate experience.</div>
<div></div>
<div>Client onboarding is at the crux of our business. It’s the key to lasting client satisfaction and retention. Our mission is to solidify clients’ decision for choosing Paycor as a trusted business partner. We utilize cloud-based project management software and a dedicated team structure to enable collaboration, free-flowing communication, and efficient problem-solving to avoid surprises for both clients and associates. To help us succeed in this mission, we’re seeking candidates who have that unique service orientation and are capable of providing “an amazing experience” to new clients to ensure long lasting satisfaction and retention.</div>
<div></div>
<div>Paycor’s Sr. Implementation Consultants are responsible for utilizing best-in-class implementation practices to onboard Paycor’s small market client base. Regardless of level, Implementation Consultants serve as the primary client contact to execute project tasks and ensure timely completion of key milestones. They’re responsible for providing consultative input on our products and services to effectively meet clients’ business requirements.</div>
<div></div>
<div>Successful Implementation candidates will demonstrate a dedicated focus to clients. More specifically the ability to establish immediate rapport and connection, quickly understand clients’ needs, and tactfully address challenges and concerns. Additionally, they will possess demonstrated influencing skills to help clients understand and fully utilize Paycor’s software application to maximize their business productivity, client satisfaction, and ultimately retention.</div>
<div></div>
<div>Paycor offers a rewarding employment environment; one where we work and play equally hard. If you’re interested in being a part of a winning team with people who have a similar passion for servicing clients this is the right opportunity for you.</div>
<div></div>
