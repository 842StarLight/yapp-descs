<h2>Chief Officer, Coleman Data Solutions - FT - Summit County</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">

<p>Coleman Data Solutions (CDS), a social enterprise of Coleman Professional Services, is a certified state use and SOC-certified non-profit organization located in Akron, Ohio. CDS provides cost-effective and compliant data conversion and document management solutions (such as document imaging and scanning, data entry and indexing, and secure storage and destruction) with a commitment to extraordinary customer service. We seek a Chief Officer to lead and transform the agency as technology develops and service models adapt. Chief Officer duties include, but are not limited to:</p>
<ul>
<li>Selling and marketing CDS services</li>
<li>Achieving at least 15% net return</li>
<li>Managing multiple contracts with a variety of different customers</li>
<li>Adhering to contract standards for quality, accuracy, and other measures</li>
<li>Leading and managing direct reports</li>
<li>Managing a diverse and blended workforce in which approximately 75% are employees with disabilities</li>
</ul>
<p>Qualified candidates will have the following:</p>
<ul>
<li>5+ years experience with document management business</li>
<li>3+ years of supervisory experience</li>
<li>Experience in marketing, sales, and Association for Information and Image Management (AIIM)</li>
<li>Working knowledge of hardware and software</li>
</ul>
<p>This position will work Monday – Friday, 50 hours per week. Hours worked are based on business needs. Benefits options include health, life, pension, paid time off, and more. Qualified applicants will receive consideration for employment without regard to race, color, religion, sex, national origin, sexual orientation, gender identity, disability or protected veteran status. E.O.E. Persons with a disability can request an accommodation to complete the application process by emailing Kimberly.Jackson@colemanservices.org with the subject line “Accommodation Request.”</p>
