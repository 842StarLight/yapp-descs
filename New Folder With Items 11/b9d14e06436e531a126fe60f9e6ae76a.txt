<h2>Corporate Account Executive</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>United Leasing &amp; Finance, a division of United Companies, is hiring Corporate Account Executives (CAEs) for our growing Sales team at our Evansville, Indiana corporate headquarters location. These full-time, benefits-eligible, salaried positions are non-supervisory roles and are responsible for developing and maintaining the equipment leasing business for assigned and developed accounts. Working closely and in conjunction with the company’s administrative and accounting staff, the CAE is responsible for generating sales that meet and exceed company criteria which will align with United Leasing’s strategic growth initiatives.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Job Responsibilities Include:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
<b>Sales</b>: Achieve sales goals as defined at least annually in the Annual Business Plan by working the existing client base for additional business as well as establishing new customer, vendor and manufacturer accounts. Participate in activities such as outside lunches, trade shows, and client visits in the development of new opportunities and to grow the existing customer base.</li>
<li>
<b>Client Relationship Management (CRM):</b> Field incoming inquiries from customers, serving their needs for new leases, answering questions on existing portfolio, quoting, pricing and locating equipment as well as continual development of those relationships. Contact existing customers in at least 90-day intervals for additional business. Manage the pre-termination contact, disposition details and operational notifications to both Accounting and Billing departments respective to all assigned mature accounts. Assist in collection of existing customer accounts, as required. Using Aspire lease management software, follow up on all lease prospects and current customers as defined in the Business Plan. Closely monitor and report on competitor activities, marketplace concerns and other activities which could impact the company in your areas of responsibility.</li>
<li>
<b>Credit Analysis:</b> Obtain required trade, credit and financial information on customer applicants and prepare a credit overview of each new sales opportunity. This requires basic knowledge of the interpretation of financial statements, balance sheets, profit/loss and income statements and other documents that are required in the completion of management approval documentation.</li>
<li>
<b>Miscellaneous:</b> Writing documents, such as marketing and specialty letters. Prepare presentations and relationship documentation. Effectively utilize Aspire lease management software system, Microsoft Office applications, and other business systems and tools. Travel as business needs will require. Perform other appropriate tasks as may be assigned by Management.</li>
<li>
<b>Quality &amp; Continuous Improvement:</b> Personally commit to quality in all aspects of work. Provide “World Class Customer Service” for internal and external customers. Participate in and promote the established Quality Improvement Process (QIP) for continuous improvement. Participate on teams to research, measure, and correct problems and to strive for process improvement. Communicate and exemplify the Company’s Mission Statement, Vision Statement, Values, and Philosophy. Attend at least ten (10) credit hours of training annually to continue development of work-related skills. Membership and networking within the Equipment Leasing &amp; Finance Association (ELFA) is preferred.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p>
</p>
<p><b>Education, Experience, &amp; Skills:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
<b>Education</b>: Bachelor’s degree from an accredited institution in Business, Finance, Marketing, or a related field is required. Background and/or coursework in Finance and/or Accounting is preferred.</li>
<li>
<b>Experience:</b> This position does not require prior experience, however we also welcome applicants with equipment leasing and finance experience. Prior sales and customer relationship-building experience is preferred, but not required.</li>
<li>
<b>Skills &amp; Abilities: </b>Strong customer service orientation and relationship-building skills. Strong communications skills, including in written, verbal, presentation, and active listening instances. Willingness to learn from in-house expertise. Demonstrated ability to consistently and effectively work with details. Ability to multi-task in a fast-paced environment and to shift priorities quickly. Demonstrated competency in Microsoft Office applications, such as Word, Excel, Outlook, and PowerPoint. Also, ability to work within databases. Strong data entry, proofreading and administrative skills. Consistently demonstrate a professional presence. Strong negotiations, problem-solving, and creative thinking skills, to develop solutions for customer needs. Demonstrate initiative. Membership with the Equipment Leasing &amp; Finance Association (ELFA) is preferred.</li>
<br>
</ul>
