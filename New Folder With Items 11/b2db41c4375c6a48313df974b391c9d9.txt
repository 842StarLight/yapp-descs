<h2>Technical Support Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Are you searching for a great career with a fast pace, innovative company that offers high growth, excellent benefits and provides exceptional opportunities? If so, look no further!!</p>
<p>We are looking for a Technical Support Specialist for the worldwide leader in the dental manufacturing industry, located in San Diego.</p>
<p><b>ESSENTIAL TASKS AND RESPONSIBILITIES: </b></p>
<p>As the Technical Support Specialist, you will provide technical support for dental lab customers through the phone and case queues. This role will work in conjunction with all departments through their supervisors to support the customer needs throughout the sales and fulfillment processes.</p>
<ul>
<li>Provide support to customers to answer technical questions related to the company’s full product suite.</li>
<li>Manage heavy call and email inquiry and uphold 3-hour customer service level agreement.</li>
<li>Record all customer inquiries in Salesforce and ensure all technical cases are solved.</li>
<li>Process and/or analyze product returns (RMAs) to provide production team with appropriate quality feedback.</li>
<li>Interact with digital production team daily to provide continuous customer feedback on quality and production standards.</li>
<li>Support outside sales team in digital sell-through processes, providing clients education with the company’s digital product suite.</li>
<li>Update digital retention and outside sales team with all interactions on their accounts.</li>
<li>Communicate effectively with other departments to ensure account order timelines, customer inquiries, and overall satisfaction exceeds expectations.</li>
<li>Provide feedback to business development on additional products to add to digital product suite.</li>
<li>Provide feedback on the company’s Digital website to improve usability and customer adoption and retention.</li>
<li>Continuously update the existing processes to improve the digital processes and general purchasing procedures.</li>
<li>Perform other duties as may be assigned.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>EXPERIENCE AND SKILLS: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>5+ years’ experience interacting with customers.</li>
<li>3+ years’ 3Shape design experience.</li>
<li>CDT certification preferred.</li>
<li>Extensive knowledge of PFM and crown &amp; bridge alloys.</li>
<li>Knowledge of metal finishing and porcelain application.</li>
<li>Aptitude to effectively maintain quality control and testing measures.</li>
<li>Digital experience and college degree is preferred.</li>
<li>Experience working with CRMs (Salesforce) and ERPs (QAD).</li>
</ul>
