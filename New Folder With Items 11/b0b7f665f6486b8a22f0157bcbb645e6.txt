<h2>Sales Manager - Englewood, CO</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Overview<br>
</b></h2>
<div></div>
<div><div><div>
<p><b>Sales Manager</b></p>
<p><b>
Hall’s Culligan of Denver/Englewood</b></p>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
Hall’s Culligan is searching for a talented and dynamic <b>Sales Manager</b> to lead a team of sales professionals representing the most trusted brand in the water quality improvement industry, Culligan Water. Working in close collaboration with the General Manager and other dealership team members, the <b>Sales Manager</b> is accountable for achieving budgeted revenue targets and other key performance metrics.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
The <b>Sales Manager</b> is further responsible for coaching, retaining, and growing a staff of full time high- performance salespeople sufficient to cover the household markets we serve in the Denver metro area. The <b>Sales Manager</b> position is vital to the growth of our dealership, and is supported by a peer group of fellow Sales Managers in the Hall’s Culligan Midwest Region, and by a dedicated Regional leadership team.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div><b>
Experience and proven skills of the candidate being sought for the Sales Manager position include:
</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Sustained record of meeting/exceeding team sales goals and other key benchmarks</li>
<li>
Demonstrated capacity to recruit, train, develop, and retain successful salespeople</li>
<li>
Experience in application of progressive disciplinary action to address underperformance</li>
<li>
Skilled in building positive relationships with customers, employees and strategic partners</li>
<li>
Established record of effective and efficient use of marketing and advertising resources</li>
<li>
Strong background in creative lead sales lead generation and activity management</li>
<li>
Expertise in the crafting and administration of sales incentive programs</li>
<li>
Developed skills in the inspection of sales and finance agreements for accuracy and compliance</li>
</ul>

<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities<br>
</b></h2>
<div></div>
<div><div><div>
<ul>
<li>Ensure profitably by achieving revenue budgets and other key sales performance metrics</li>
<li>
Develop, implement, and monitor growth Initiatives within the sales department</li>
<li>
Retain recruit, hire, train, mentor, and support an effective, fully staffed sales department</li>
<li>
Coach team in the field to ensure continuous improvement of sales skills and territory management</li>
<li>
Monitor, support and coach team members on their field activity such as demonstrations and lead generation actions</li>
<li>
Daily inspection of all sales contracts, financing, and other related paperwork to ensure full compliance to all processes</li>
<li>
Weekly, Monthly, Quarterly, and Annual Sales Performance Reviews; initiate progressive disciplinary actions when necessary</li>
<li>
Work collaboratively with Advertising Agency and Coop members to implement and monitor an efficient, effective, and comprehensive marketing plan</li>
<li>
Manage incentive, rewards and recognition programs for sales professionals</li>
<li>
Establish and maintain effective working relationships with all levels of business and public</li>
<li>
Maintain budget and P&amp;L responsibilities</li>
</ul>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Qualifications
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<ul>
<li>A minimum of 3 years effective sales management experience and education/experience equivalent *</li>
<li>
Above average stats with a proven track record in residential sales with a history of meeting and/or exceeding monthly, quarterly and annual sales goals</li>
<li>
Experience with in-home demonstration and negotiation, selling to consumer needs and providing solutions</li>
<li>
Technology savvy and Microsoft Office proficient</li>
<li>
10% travel outside territory /frequent travel within</li>
<li>
Ability to lift up to a minimum of 50lbs</li>
</ul>
<ul><li>Please note: Special consideration will be given to those with direct related experience in providing residential water conditioning / water treatment solutions to in-home consumers</li></ul>
