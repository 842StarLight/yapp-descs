<h2>Dynamics NAV Network Engineer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>** This is a remote position **<br>
<br>
ArcherPoint is currently seeking a Network Engineer to join our Professional and Customer Support teams. This role is responsible for addressing urgent technical issues with clients as well as working internally on the RDM (Remote Desktop Manager) tool. The ability to analyze and understand internal and external client issues through the use of excellent communication and customer service skills is a must. The ability to remain focused in high-pressure situations and exhibit a high level of knowledge, assurance and empathy are keys for success in this role.</p>
<p>
Specifically, the Technical Customer Support Consultant is accountable for:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Implementing and configuring software on clients D365BC servers (Dynamics NAV) both on-prem and SaaS</li>
<li>
Providing ongoing preventative server maintenance to ensure client data security and optimum performance</li>
<li>
Establishing client connectivity standards</li>
<li>
Establishing and maintaining client connections</li>
<li>
Responding to client requests with appropriate urgency</li>
<li>
Resolving and following-up on tickets with appropriate urgency</li>
<li>
Providing pre-sales technical recommendations in support of sales pursuit of new business</li>
<li>
Publishing and updating knowledge-based articles</li>
</ul>
<p>
The ideal candidate for this role will have at least 3 years of help desk experience in a customer service or consulting environment. Experience troubleshooting and modifying with Microsoft Dynamics NAV and SQL are also required. Candidates with experience working with Azure and TSQL are strongly encouraged to apply!</p>
<div>
In addition to providing world-class customer service for our clients, the successful candidate for this role must align well with our very defined, active, and dynamic organizational culture. ArcherPoint is not your typical company, culture, or business environment, so we seek candidates that are ready for a significant change and opportunity.</div>
