<h2>LOCAL SERVICE COORD20</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Position Overview
</b></h2>
<p>Responsible for providing local branch support for intra-day activities and escalations within the branch to ensure quality customer service experiences and prompt resolution to customer issues. Cultivates and maintains on-going Customer, Branch, and Contact Center relationships. Collaborates with various field and corporate departments to resolve challenging customer issues.
</p>
<p></p>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Job Responsibilities
</b></h2>
<ul>
<li>Provides support for escalated customer issues by ensuring prompt answering of transferred phone calls from the contact centers.
</li>
<li>Provides support for technicians needing assistance with shifting appointments and/or making other modifications to customer accounts .
</li>
<li>Assists branch leadership with scheduling issues and communicates changes to the customer. Includes managing intraday schedule changes and Pest Control new sales.
</li>
<li>Proactively resolves Customer Follow-up Requests submitted from the contact center.
</li>
<li>Monitors the Customer Incident Response Tool (CIRT) queue for 5-Star and NPS survey low scores and addresses each accordingly.
</li>
<li>Processes manual tickets approved by the Service Manager.
</li>
<li>Reviews and resolves both PC &amp; TC cancel requests.
</li>
<li>Posts NPS Surveys.
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Job Requirements
</b></h2>
<ul>
<li>High school diploma or general education degree (GED); or one to three months related experience and/or training; or equivalent combination of education and experience
</li>
<li>1-2 years clerical or customer service experience preferred
</li>
<li>Ability to read and comprehend complex instructions, correspondence, and written instructions
</li>
<li>Excellent verbal, written, and interpersonal skills
</li>
<li>Ability to effectively present information to customers &amp; field workforce
</li>
<li>Familiar with Microsoft Office applications – Word, Excel, PowerPoint, Outlook, SharePoint, Microsoft teams
</li>
<li>Experience with AS400 software preferred but not required
</li>
<li>Ability to multi task
</li>
<li>Ability to type 35 words per minute
</li>
<li>Ability to work in a stressful, fast-paced environment
</li>
<li>Strong problem-solving skills
</li>
<li>Ability to overcome objections
</li>
</ul>
