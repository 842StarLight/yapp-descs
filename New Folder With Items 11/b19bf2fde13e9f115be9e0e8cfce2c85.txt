<h2>Implementation Manager I (San Jose, CA)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Job Overview:
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div>Interested in technology? Love working with people? We’re seeking a business-savvy, time management superstar to join our team as an Implementation Manager I, where you’ll work closely with our customers to implement our platforms, offering continued guidance and consultation to help them adopt best practices and rollout successfully. In this role, you’ll be responsible for executing the iCIMS implementation methodology and handling multiple projects of varying levels of complexity, collaborating with other Implementation Managers or Project Managers, and on occasion traveling to our customers to provide onsite assistance and training. We’re looking for someone who knows how to navigate tough conversations and scenarios to influence and drive projects while keeping our customers on track. If you’re a highly-organized, driven, professional team player who shares our belief in customer commitment, you’ve come to the right place!</div>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>About Us:
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div>Headquartered in Holmdel, NJ, iCIMS is a high-growth Software-as-a-Service (SaaS) company that was recently named #16 on Glassdoor’s 2019 Best Places to Work list. With multiple locations across the U.S. and around the globe, we are the industry’s #1 recruitment software provider, delivering technology that supports approximately 4,000 contracted customers. Dedicated to maintaining an inclusive, inspirational and innovative work environment, and committed to our consistent growth, we have a wide range of opportunity for career advancement within our organization. Come grow with us—apply today!</div>

<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities:
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<ul>
<li>Carry out implementation projects from beginning to end stages for all types (Emerging, Majors, and high profile Enterprise clients)</li>
<li>
Conduct analysis of customer requirements</li>
<li>
Work directly with customers to consult and configure iCIMS products in an exemplary manner, with a focus on best practices</li>
<li>
Assume a leadership role in the development and management of project scope, issues and escalations while continually setting customer expectations</li>
<li>
Partner with Enterprise Project Team members to complete project action items</li>
<li>
Provide project planning, management, and status reporting, internally and externally</li>
<li>
Work with customers to configure systems and coordinate training</li>
<li>
Manage data conversion process, data validation, and system testing</li>
<li>
Supports customer during initial “live” period and transition to the Account Management and Support organizations</li>
<li>
Take significant responsibility for the overall success of the project and satisfaction of the customer</li>
<li>
Manage several implementations of various sizes simultaneously</li>
<li>
Consistently ensure business is conducted with integrity at all times and that behavior aligns with iCIMS policies, procedures, and core competencies</li>
</ul>
