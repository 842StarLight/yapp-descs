<h2>Estimator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">As a key member of the Design and Estimating function, the Wall Panel Design/Estimating Lead will have significant responsibility and a driver for the continued growth of wall panel production within the organization. Attention to detail, manufacturing knowledge, and the ability to effectively communicate with internal and external customers will be keys to success in this role. With a company focus on a consultative approach, delivery on-time and quality wood components, this role with be an integral part of growing the business offerings. This role initially will be an individual contributor but would be expected to manage a team in the near future. A collaborator, and mentor approach will be necessary for he/she be effective in this position.
<br><br>
The Wall Panel Design/Estimating Lead is responsible for design, estimating, and customer service for the wall panel projects Trussway offers. He or She will use Mitek software, understand automated manufacturing equipment, and engage in field visits for complex and critical projects during delivery. The right candidate will be able to develop, evaluate, and report on KPIs for the Wall Panel projects primarily out of the Acworth facility.
<br><br>
<b>Key Responsibilities:</b><br>
Design / Estimating
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Design complex multi-family and light-commercial wall panels.</li>
<li>Develop processes to ensure accuracy and in turn profitability for the wall panel division.</li>
<li>Function as the point of contact on wall panel projects to leadership, manufacturing, sales, and customers.</li>
<li>Computing cost factors and preparing estimates used for management and sales in the preparation of proposals and value engineering cost saving opportunities (VE opportunities)</li>
<li>Collecting data to determine costs of project in a short time span; analyzing data obtained and recording results for proposals</li>
<li>Pre and Post-Estimate meetings with sales and management as needed; communicating with sales representatives and operations managers to ensure scope is appropriately met</li>
<li>Cross department or cross division communication and assistance with Admin (draw schedule development), Production (batching opportunities), other divisions (share bid information as needed), and BOE (audit and problem solving)</li>
<li>Participating in pre-design meetings once a project is sold</li>
<li>File maintenance and TOS update</li>
</ul>
Customer Interactions
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Effectively communicate with internal and external customers regarding wall panel design, estimating, along with changes during specific projects.</li>
<li>Ability to aid customers with field inspections to ensure quality and proper installation</li>
<li>For critical or complex projects, participate is kick-off project deliveries to customers</li>
</ul>
Management / Thought Leadership
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Provide leadership and insight on best practice and competitive practice on wall panel design, estimating, and production</li>
<li>Ability to attract and retain a strong team of designers and estimators.</li>
<li>Work with team members to develop process improvements, metrics, and innovation for wall panel design, production, and delivery.</li>
</ul>
</div>
