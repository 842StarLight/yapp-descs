<h2>Customer Success Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>Job Description<br>
<br>
<p>Are you passionate and driven? Do you understand what it takes to deliver excellent service and built valuable partnerships?</p>
<p><b>
Your role:</b></p>
<p>
ProspectNow is seeking a Customer Success Specialist to help build and strengthen our relationships with the real estate professionals we serve.</p>
<p>
As a Customer Success Specialist, your main focus will be onboarding and training clients by hosting webinars. It is your role to have engaging and educational conversations to make sure agents understand what it takes to be successful with our software.</p>
<p>
You will need to understand every part of the business cycle from the perspective of the real estate agents and be able to explain to them how they can profit best when using our software.</p>
<p>
Since the real estate agents are located throughout the country, your conversations will be on the phone and through webinars from our new Roseville, CA office location.</p>
