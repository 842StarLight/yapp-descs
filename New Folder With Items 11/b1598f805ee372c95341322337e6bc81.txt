<h2>New Business Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Overview
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div>New Business is not a department. It’s a mindset. It’s the process that the agency (and the entire advertising industry) goes through to win a new client. Everyone at Ogilvy &amp; Mather is encouraged to embrace a hunting New Business culture. Based in New York, Ogilvy’s Business Development group is a small team of new business experts. We drive growth for the company, setting the ambition, bringing in prospect leads to orchestrate the presentation of strategy and creative ideas during pitches.</div>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<ul>
<li>Manage high volume of new business activity across the matrix</li>
<li>
Research, identify, target and refine prospects</li>
<li>
Follow through with all presentations, including follow-up letters, proposals, etc.</li>
<li>
Oversee Business Development staff</li>
<li>
Distribute a weekly Press Roundup to top 100 staff</li>
</ul>
<div>
<b>
Prospecting:</b><br>
</div>
<ul>
<li>
Work in tandem with Business Development Director and agency principals to develop prospect targets and prospect plan by market and service.</li>
<li>
Report on market climate, competitive position and specific sales opportunities, as part of annual and long-term business plan development and implementation.</li>
<li>
Research, develop and maintain a list of targeted prospects</li>
<li>
Put together credentials presentations and case studies</li>
</ul>
<div>
<b>
Pitching:</b><br>
</div>
<ul>
<li>
Review and write prospect briefs with company background, ad spend, current agency, marketing situation and analysis of opportunity for Ogilvy</li>
<li>
Managing pitch process and internal and client deliverables for small scale pitches and assisting in the management of large integrated pitches from RFP to Final Presentation</li>
<li>
Overseeing and maintaining statuses and pipelines</li>
<li>
Maintaining new business files and inventory</li>
<li>
Initiating status meetings on all sales/new business opportunities</li>
<li>
Some travel required for pitches</li>
</ul>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Qualifications
</b></h2>
<div><div><div><ul>
<li>3-5 years experience in an advertising agency</li>
<li>
Passion and knowledge of agency business, industry trends</li>
<li>
Ability to own small projects and pitches, liaising directly with senior staff</li>
<li>
Enthusiastic and resourceful, and attentive to detail</li>
<li>
Ability to problem-solve and offer creative solutions</li>
<li>
Excellent communication skills, written and oral</li>
<li>
Management expertise and ability to work with senior staff and WW CMO</li>
</ul></div></div></div>
