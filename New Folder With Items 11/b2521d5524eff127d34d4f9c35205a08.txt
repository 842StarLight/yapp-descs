<li>Provide daily support to the Enterprise Sales Executives, including:

<li>Schedule appointments via phone, web or onsite meetings

<li>Deliver on follow-up items from meetings/presentations
</li>
<li>Assist in the preparation and signature of MNDAs, business proposals and sales orders
</li>
<li>Follow-up with prospects and customers
</li>
</ul>

</li>
<li>Provide explanations, web meetings and collateral to support the sales process
</li>
