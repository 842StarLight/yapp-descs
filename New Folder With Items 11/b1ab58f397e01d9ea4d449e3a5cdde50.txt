<h2>Budtender</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<div><b>BUDTENDER</b></div>
<div></div>
<div>MED (Marijuana Enforcement Division) Badge required to apply!!</div>
<div></div>
<div>FLSA Status: Non-Exempt</div>
<div></div>
<div><b>Position Objective:</b></div>
<div></div>
<div>The Denco, LLC Budtender is the face of the company and is responsible for representing the Den.Co brand at a retail level. Budtender is the primary employee that will leave a lasting impression on our patrons, provide a friendly experience and knowledge behind the marijuana dispensary counter who guides our patrons through the process of understanding, selecting and purchasing products, creating a long lasting customer that is willing to return to our establishment. The candidate is responsible for maintaining functionality, efficiency, and fluidity throughout the store and among all levels of Den.Co staff, vendors and others.</div>
<div><b>Essential Responsibilities:
</b></div>
<div></div>
<ul>
<li>
<div>Acting as the face of the dispensary, greeting customers</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Facilitate sales</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Educate customers about a variety of cannabis products, including flowers, edibles, and concentrates</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Willing to solve problems and take on new challenges daily</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Welcome customers with warm friendly greeting</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Verifying proper identification and paperwork from customers</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Patient check-in, and all ID checking (front desk and before purchase)</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Stay abreast of new products, strains, changes in laws, and industry trends in order to provide the highest level of service to our customers</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Provide recommendations on cannabis products based on customer needs and requests</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Responsible for all money and inventory on the sales floor or in designated POS system</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Checking in Primary Patients and Den.Co’s customers in POS.</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Ensure medical patient’s paperwork is filed and entered into POS properly</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Adhere to Denco cash handling policies and procedures</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Assist with daily inventory audit procedures</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Responsible for daily store opening and closing duties, along with completing the daily checklists and maintaining the store appearance</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Maintain workstations in an orderly manner</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Ability to make proper recommendations based on experience with products sold</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Allocate guest badges and oversight on all vendor/visitor sign-in to remain compliant</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Adhere to operational guidelines and standards.</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Comply with all safety, security and MED compliance policies.</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Maintain a positive attitude and calm demeanor during periods of high volume or unusual events to keep store operating at a high standard and must be able to work as a team</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Maintain current and accurate store records of patient data, MED compliance and other data as directed</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Other duties as assigned</div>
</li>
</ul>
<div><b>Minimum Qualifications:</b></div>
<div></div>
<ul>
<li>
<div>Experience in a similar capacity of retail and customer service</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Knowledge of basic computer functions and working on various software and programs</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Good written and verbal communication skills</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Must have a valid support or key badge from the Colorado Marijuana Enforcement Division</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Ability to lift-up to 25 lbs</div>
</li>
<li><div>Must be able to sit, stand, stoop, crouch, reach and lift for long periods of time</div></li>
</ul>
