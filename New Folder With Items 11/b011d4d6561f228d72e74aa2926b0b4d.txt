<div><p><b>
Description</b></p></div>
<div><p><br>
<br>
HP has ambitious goals for the next generation of connected, intelligent, digital manufacturing. The software portfolio and platform we deliver to our customers is a critical piece of that goal. As a Software Product Manager you will be part of a new and dynamic team responsible for products across our 3D business. You are passionate about the digital manufacturing space and how the role of data in decision making, a customer and design-centered focus, and how the evolution of connected IoT frameworks can transform the way the world manufactures.</p></div>
<p></p>
<br>
<div><p>
This is a highly impactful role within HP that requires a combination of skills and experience to succeed. The ideal candidate will bring a highly successful track record of software or system product management learnings to the role and leverage the substantial resources of HP to identify and solve key customer problems at a level not yet seen in industry. Working across teams with key support from finance, marketing, engineering, and customer insights is critical and will require the ability to be a great peer while delivering the best product possible. Passion for owning a product E2E and maintaining an entrepreneurial spirit will be required for success in this role, as well as the vision to help define and champion the entire portfolio of SW products across the HP business.</p></div>
