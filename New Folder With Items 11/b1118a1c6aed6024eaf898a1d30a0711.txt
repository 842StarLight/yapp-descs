<div><b>What is Real Contact?
</b></div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>Real Contact helps agents and teams quickly respond to and engage with leads through their text messaging lead response services. Run by real people who act as a branded extension of a real estate agent, Real Contact concierges respond to leads from any source within 90 seconds, 24/7. They work to qualify leads and nurture unresponsive leads, allowing agents to step in at any time via the app.
