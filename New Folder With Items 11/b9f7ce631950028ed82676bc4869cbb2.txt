<p>Primary Function
</p>
<p></p>
<p>A.O. Smith is seeking a passionate and motivated manager to oversee the company’s IT Operations and Service Management capabilities. The job requires working with a diverse community of business partners and stakeholders across multiple international locations. It not only includes the day to day management of these capabilities, but the design and deployment of additional ITIL services supported by a recently implemented ITSM solution. The role comes with an enormous opportunity to grow professionally, functionally, technically and personally in an efficient and collaborative team atmosphere.</p>
<p></p>
<p>Responsibilities
</p>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Ensures all sites are supported through the management of a centralized Service Desk and local end user support personnel.
</li>
<li>Responsible for the lifecycle management of IT assets to include: capital acquisition, production deployment, maintenance of a global inventory, and licensing compliance of hardware and software assets.</li>
<li>
Provides strategic leadership and tactical oversight in the management of enterprise operational personnel, processes, and technologies such as: workplace productivity solutions, an IT Service Management platform, and infrastructure and application monitoring solutions.
</li>
<li>Builds high performance teams through staff development, recruitment and retention</li>
<li>
Acts as the organizational champion of the IT Service Management (ITSM) program and lead the design, implementation, and continuous improvement of ITIL capabilities such as: incident management, request management, change management, problem management, the Service Catalog, event management, and service level management.</li>
<li>
Lead the design and implementation of a centralized infrastructure and application monitoring solution.</li>
<li>
Work with senior IT leadership to create and publish an IT scorecard that measures overall departmental performance, improvement, and value contribution towards the strategic objectives of A.O. Smith.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p>Qualifications
</p>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree in Information systems, computer science, or related field
</li>
<li>Five or more years of management experience, including running infrastructure, operations, and service desk teams with breadth from multiple IT disciplines
</li>
<li>Knowledge of and experience implementing ITIL / Service Management frameworks and best practices</li>
<li>
Demonstrated ability to think strategically and drive the design and implementation of enterprise projects within the scope of responsibility</li>
</ul>
