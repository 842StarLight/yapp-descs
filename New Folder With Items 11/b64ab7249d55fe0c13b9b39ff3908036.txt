The Enterprise Account Executive, will be tasked to develop, own and manage closing new business in the Northeast including the Media, Entertainment and Publishing vertical. Responsibilities include ensuring that prospective customers have the best possible experience and finding the right mix of Acquia offerings for their needs. The ideal candidate cultivates deep relationships with prospective customers, partnering with them to determine their needs and delivering world class service.
<br><br>
The ideal candidate will have deep experience with solution selling of complex enterprise software with preferable background around web development, Cloud/SaaS, Web Analytics, Marketing Automation, Web Content Management or Social Software.
<br><br>
<b>Responsibilities:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Develop and execute a go-to-market strategy for Acquia's offerings in the US East</li>
<li>Identify and establish relationships with key corporate C-level representatives through prospecting, marketing leads and expansion of existing contacts within accounts</li>
<li>Build and maintain strong sales pipeline and forecast, through discovery calls and meetings</li>
<li>Drive the sales process and manage the contract and approval process of new deals, including negotiating on price and getting approvals including contract terms</li>
<li>Meet or exceed set Quota targets and Key Performance Indicators (KPI's) for sales based activities</li>
<li>Conduct opportunity and account review sessions with Sales Leadership</li>
<li>Completes required reports by deadline, maintains accurate forecast</li>
<li>Manage all information in Salesforce.com</li>
<li>Contribute to the development of sales strategy through input of ideas and suggestions to senior management team</li>
<li>Build awareness for Drupal and Acquia within large Enterprises and System Integrators</li>
<li>Build and maintain relationships with new and existing Acquia partners</li>
</ul>
<b>Qualifications:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>History of consistently exceeding quota</li>
<li>Experience with SaaS technologies. Preferably around Marketing Automation and/or Web Content Management</li>
<li>A clear understanding of current social media capabilities and their impact on businesses of all sizes</li>
<li>Demonstrated ability to manage both direct and indirect opportunities</li>
<li>Significant experience in a new business Sales role ideally within a similar start up and high growth organization</li>
<li>Experience with Media, Entertainment and Publishing a major plus</li>
<li>Based in San Francisco or West Coast area</li>
</ul>
