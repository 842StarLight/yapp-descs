<h2>Senior Project Manager - Business Intelligence</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<div>Role Purpose:</div>
<div></div>
<div>The Project Manager, Senior – Business Intelligence will be responsible for simultaneously managing various large and complex system-wide technical projects and initiatives impacting multiple entities throughout our region (Dean Medical Group, SSM-W Hospitals and affiliates and independent physician practices). Project oversight will include Epic and other healthcare system upgrades, new product implementations, the implementation of ancillary clinical systems, extract and reporting development, and system optimizations. This position will be a hands-on individual that will become familiar with all aspects of the data-related systems supported by Business Intelligence. This role requires building a strong working relationship with executives, physicians, management and staff as well as the ability to coordinate the efforts of resources from multiple entities. The Project Manager, Senior will be responsible for project specific budget creation and management, leadership of the project team, vendor management, effective use of project management tools and communication to stakeholders. This individual must have significant meeting facilitation experience and strong written and oral communication skills.</div>
<div></div>
<div>Responsibilities:</div>
<ul>
<li><div>Lead cross-functional project teams on both local and enterprise-wide business intelligence projects that span multiple business and IT areas and include numerous applications.</div></li>
<li><div>Create and manage project plans, scope, budget, communications, risks, etc. for multiple concurrent projects of various sizes and complexities.</div></li>
<li><div>Manage projects by following the Dean/SSM tools, templates and methodologies.</div></li>
<li><div>Liaise with project sponsors, stakeholders, management, technical staff and customers, ensuring all project requirements and/or objectives are appropriately documented.</div></li>
<li><div>Develop and present formal presentations on progress, justifications and details for specific projects.</div></li>
<li><div>Work with management staff to acquire resources for projects and ensure tasks are assigned to appropriately skilled resources.</div></li>
<li><div>Identify and resolve dependencies and constraints between and across projects.</div></li>
<li><div>Conduct software evaluations and selection processes for vendor supported software.</div></li>
<li><div>Coordinate internal resources, vendors, and customers to deliver quality projects on schedule and within budget.</div></li>
<li><div>Facilitate workflow analysis and process redesign. Document processes and procedures developed during the course of projects.</div></li>
<li><div>Work with the appropriate members of the sponsor group to balance the needs of individual organizations with the collective needs of the delivery system as a whole.</div></li>
<li><div>Train and share expertise with less experienced staff and help to further improve the project management methodology.</div></li>
<li>
<div>Coach less experienced peer staff:</div>
<ul>
<li><div>Orient new staff to our project management methodology and tools.</div></li>
<li><div>Provide coaching on communication methods, and quality assurance of written communications</div></li>
<li><div>Coach new staff on how to be expert advisors to their customers</div></li>
<li><div>Coach new staff on the cultural differences for each customer base and how those differences need to be recognized and mediated</div></li>
<li><div>Give guidance on how to ensure that each stakeholder has an equal voice in decision making while doing no harm to existing workflows and functionality</div></li>
</ul>
</li>
<li><div>Partner with fellow project managers across the organization to help establish and refine standard templates, methodologies and processes.</div></li>
<li><div>Potential travel to stakeholder, customer, or vendor sites.</div></li>
<li><div>Maintain strict confidentiality of Personal Health Information and personnel information. Proactively protects confidentiality of information and identifies potential risks.</div></li>
<li><div>Complete other duties assigned by manager.</div></li>
</ul>
<div>
Minimum Qualifications:</div>
<ul>
<li><div>Bachelor’s degree with 5 years of experience in healthcare project management or technical project management OR an Associate degree with 10 years of experience in healthcare project management or technical project management.</div></li>
<li><div>Of the above experience, 3 years of project management experience and software systems management.
</div></li>
<li><div>Must be goal oriented and self-disciplined with demonstrated project management and meeting facilitation experience.</div></li>
<li><div>Excellent cross-functional team leadership skills, including providing clear direction, conflict resolution, task assignment/tracking and establishing a collaborative environment</div></li>
<li><div>Strong verbal and written communication skills with the ability to effectively speak with both technical and non-technical partners.</div></li>
<li><div>Strong organizational, interpersonal, and analytical skills.</div></li>
<li><div>Process development and continuous improvement expertise with proven experience in implementing impactful change in an organization.</div></li>
<li><div>Ability to quickly adapt within an ever-changing business and technical environment while maintaining a professional image.</div></li>
<li><div>Experience with the Microsoft Office suite and project management software.</div></li>
<li><div>Commitment to exceptional customer service. Through collaborative partnership.</div></li>
</ul>
<div>Preferred Qualifications:</div>
<ul>
<li><div>Project management (PMP) certification
</div></li>
<li><div>Previous project management or leadership experience in a healthcare setting.</div></li>
</ul>
