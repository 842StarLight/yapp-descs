<h2>Sales Systems Lead (Platform)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">About the Team
<br><br>
The Business and Revenue Operations team at AppDirect drives strategic and operational decision-making across our Platform Services Organization. Our team's mission is to help Platform Services operate more effectively and efficiently with regards to driving revenue. We leverage data to report on the growth engine of our business and generate insights to drive actionable results.
<br><br>
About You
<br><br>
As a member of a fast-growing team, you will help define, execute and own our Platform Services technology roadmap. You will partner with senior leaders from our Platform Services teams to scope out business requirements and architect best-in-class systems solutions. Ultimately, you will drive the growth engine and efficiency underpinning our global teams through productivity initiatives.
<br><br>
This role will have significant visibility into the growth strategy of our company and will carry exposure to all critical functions of the business, including the executive suite. The ideal candidate will be a data-driven individual with an analytical eye toward problem solving and have extensive business systems experience to drive sustainable impact across the company.
<br><br>
<b>How you'll make an impact &amp; what you'll do:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Define, execute and own our business operations technology roadmap</li>
<li>Serve as a business partner to senior Platform Services leaders to scope out business requirements and architect best-in-class systems solutions</li>
<li>Drive business process efficiencies related to technology systems across the Platform Services organization.</li>
<li>Support growth initiatives across teams, regions and verticals</li>
<li>Define system best practices through trainings and documentation through partnership with our Sales Enablement team</li>
<li>Support special projects</li>
<li>Administer our Platform Services business operations systems including, but not limited to, Salesforce.com, OpenAir, Zendesk, Clari, Outreach.io</li>
</ul>
<b>What you'll need:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>3-5 years of work experience, preferably in systems administration, sales systems strategy, or business operations.</li>
<li>Experience with Salesforce.com administration and other sales/business tools preferred</li>
<li>Salesforce certifications preferred</li>
<li>Experience in high growth SaaS environments</li>
<li>Sound business judgment: an ability to formulate and test hypotheses about business questions utilizing data</li>
<li>Strong organizational skills, attention to detail and track record of driving projects to completion</li>
</ul>
<b>About AppDirect:</b><br>
AppDirect is the leading platform for selling, distributing, and managing cloud-based products and services. Its flexible, modular technology enables organizations of all sizes to get to market quickly and cost effectively with an offering that meets their individual business needs. AppDirect-powered marketplaces, billing, distribution, reselling, and premium technical support services help providers— including Comcast, ADP, Zendesk, Deutsche Telekom and others — connect more than 30 million businesses to solutions from Microsoft, Google, GoDaddy, and more. AppDirect is headquartered in San Francisco with 13 global offices.
