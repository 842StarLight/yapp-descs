<h2>Principal Solutions Architect, Alexa</h2>

<ul>
<li>Bachelor's degree in a technical field such as computer science, MIS, engineering or related discipline</li>
<li>10+ years of experience in the design, coding and implementation of IT systems</li>
<li>Experience with two or more development languages such as Java, JavaScript or Python</li>

<br>
<div>Amazon support third party hardware makers who wish to integrate the Alexa Voice Service (AVS), the brain behind Amazon Echo, into their products. The Alexa team is seeking an experienced solutions architect to help integrate Alexa into partner devices and services. Are you the kind of hands-on technical professional who can help our partners? In this role, you will wear many hats, including technical adviser, software developer and evangelist. You must possess a unique combination of deep technical skills and business acumen as well as a do-what-it-takes attitude to ensure our partners are able to take full advantage of Alexa’s skills and are delivering a delightful end user experience.<br>
<br>
Ideally, you’re someone who can write code with the best of them, but also wants to help guide the broader industry towards a technology you believe in. You have immediate credibility with technologists and developers at all levels, from students to CIOs. You love to share your passion with others and exhibit good judgment in selecting strategic opportunities to do so. You don’t just want to be part of an industry movement; you want to be out front leading it. If this sounds like you, we’d like to speak with you.<br>
<br>
Responsibilities<br>
<ul>
<li>Engage with partners’ developers, architects, hardware engineers and project managers to facilitate the integration of Alexa into their products and services</li>
<li>Educate partners of all sizes on the Alexa service and participate in deep architectural discussions to ensure their solutions are designed for successful deployment</li>
<li>Spend time as required on-site with partners helping them integrate with Alexa</li>
<li>Brief Amazon executive teams on progress in adopting Alexa</li>
<li>Work with the Alexa product teams to define platform requirements</li>
<li>Capture and share best-practice knowledge that future Alexa partners can leverage</li>
<li>Help Alexa business development identify leads for new potential partnerships</li>
</ul>
