<h2>Sales Planner</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Position Summary:</b></p>
<p>The Sales Planner will develop sales plans and packages for clients that meet the needs of the network/digital clients and sales staff. They will support the Account<br>
Executive with deal execution and work with the P&amp;I team to maximize yield by advertiser for both linear and digital sales.</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Essential Duties and Responsibilities:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Prepare and revise sales plans that maximize the use of inventory while fulfilling agency/client requirements.</li>
<li>Manage and process all cancellation and expansion options.</li>
<li>Stewardship: maintain client schedules to ensure delivery of buy, build/schedule ADU packages as needed, work with assistant and traffic to manage preemptions, reaffirm quarterly estimates and create account posts.</li>
<li>Account Servicing: work closely and communicate directly with appropriate agency/client counterparts as needed to ensure excellent service at all times.</li>
<li>Generate, track and distribute assigned sales reports, via all sales systems.</li>
<li>Work closely with the inventory manager to discuss Inventory/Recaps, and enforce all pricing and planning deadlines, policies and procedures for both linear and digital.</li>
<li>Handle various miscellaneous duties as assigned, including the coordination of training for sales assistants and preparing pitches.</li>
<li>Assist with custom deck presentations and incorporating research data and marketing sponsorships.</li>
<li>Oversee execution of marketing elements across platforms.</li>
</ul>
