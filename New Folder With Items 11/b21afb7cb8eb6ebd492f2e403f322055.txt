<p><b>Job Summary:</b></p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
Responsible for managing the packaging and documenting of new product ideas and conducts user needs analyses for ancillary products so that subscriber and financial objectives are achieved. Conducts the budgeting, planning, analysis, and monitoring required to ensure unit and financial targets are met for assigned products. Manages ongoing product management of new and existing automation products. Develops financial and operational objectives. Ensures operational plans are aligned with business objectives. Contributes to functional strategy development. Has a greater degree of impact on business results, and typically manages one or more groups of professional employees.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Core Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Extensive experience with and enthusiasm for the digital and linear media industry and operational systems, including but not limited to media planning, currency, targeting, data, reporting, inventory, campaign effectiveness.</li>
<li>Drive and optimize inventory and audience-targeted campaign effectiveness through ability to analyze, interpret and navigate a variety of complex system configurations.</li>
<li>Ability to author detailed technical requirements, processes and workflows that resulting in clear, concise and unambiguous system requirements.</li>
<li>Determination to bridge and lead the transformation of the digital/linear media industry convergence by balancing current business needs against a future state lens.</li>
<li>Effectively navigate and partner with stakeholders across a matrixed sales-minded organization, while maintaining an operational and execution driven approach to product management.</li>
<li>The innovative and collaborative mindset in order to develop ideas, validate hypothesis, pinpoint underlying business problems, and execute on a plan that includes both data analysis and sound business sense.</li>
<li>Detailed, organized and process-driven, while simultaneously promoting and pursuing iterative improvements in business processes and tools.</li>
<li>Clear communication skills in order to distill the true nature of enhancement and feature requests by uncovering the underlying ask, and doing the proper due diligence to ensure customer needs are met and aligned with the solution provided.</li>
<li>Successful outside vendor management and industry partnership skills required.</li>
<li>Experience with SAFe methodologies (certifications preferred).</li>
<li>Creates documents for architecture and design, technical requirements and service specifications. Presents and defends architecture, design, and technical choices to internal audiences.</li>
<li>Demonstrates technical leadership and mentoring of others on the application of new technologies and system development methodologies.</li>
<li>Manages department's performance, budget responsibilities, development of staff, resource planning and goal setting.</li>
<li>Develops requirements to be used by the vendor community to develop solutions to meet Company application needs.</li>
<li>Evaluates various architectural solutions and implementations.</li>
<li>Identifies trends, services and/or capabilities that may be beneficial to various product offerings.</li>
<li>Participates consultatively in the testing and certification process.</li>
<li>Keeps current with technological developments in the industry.</li>
<li>Establishes and maintains productive relationships with peer organizations and also equipment and software vendors.</li>
<li>Consistent exercise of independent judgment and discretion in matters of significance.</li>
<li>Regular, consistent and punctual attendance. Must be able to work nights and weekends, variable schedule(s) as necessary.</li>
<li>Other duties and responsibilities as assigned.</li>
</ul>
