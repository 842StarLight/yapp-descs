<h2>General Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>If you're a born leader, then you've stumbled across the right job for you. As the General Manager, you'll enjoy plenty of work-related perks, such as advancement opportunities, provided uniforms, paid vacation days, meal discounts, and company medical benefits will be offered.</b><br>
<br>
<b>The perfect General Manager is a confident decision-maker who has a proven track record and experience in guest and customer service, employee management, business and financial management, and people motivation. He or she is an excellent communicator, both in written form and verbally.</b></p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Job Purpose Statement: </b>To develop, support and lead the restaurant team. To be accountable for sales growth by maintaining the highest standards of quality, service, and cleanliness with a constant focus on profitability.</p>
<p><b>
Job Functions:</b></p>
<p><b>
 </b>Enthusiastically greet every guest with the "Welcome to Moe's entering the restaurant. Interview and hire adequate staff to operate the restaurant based on company standards and in compliance with state and federal regulations.</p>
<p>
 Schedule sufficient staff to operate the restaurant and accommodate guest flow. Control payroll to meet company labor cost standard.</p>
<p>
Train the management team and crew using current company training system. Evaluate performance at company designated intervals. Design a development plan with each employee to improve performance.</p>
<p>
Maintain a cooperative, harmonious working relationship with management team and crew. Provide coaching, counseling, and discipline as warranted.</p>
<p>
Conduct crew and management team meetings as needed to discuss operations procedures, promotions, safety, security, and training.</p>
<p>
Maintain an adequate supply of products and items necessary to operate the restaurant. Control inventory to meet company cost standards.</p>
<p>
Maintain reports and records based on company standards and in compliance with state and federal regulations.</p>
<p>
Maintain building and equipment using proper operating, cleaning, and preventive maintenance procedures.</p>
<p>
Responsible for all cash. Ensure that cash procedures are adhered to.</p>
<p>
Ensure personal appearance meets company standard and displays professionalism at all times. Ensure all sanitation, safety and security policies and procedures are recognized and enforced that creates a safe environment for all.</p>
