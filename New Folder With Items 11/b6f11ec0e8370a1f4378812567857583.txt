<h2>Industrial Billing Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Casella Waste Systems, Inc. [Nasdaq: CWST] is a regional solid waste, recycling and resource management services company that provides solid waste collection, transfer, disposal, recycling, and organics services. Casella operates facilities in 6 northeastern states, with 2,400 employees, and annual revenues of approximately $670 million.</p>
<p>Casella Waste Systems, Inc. has opened a search for an accomplished, experienced Industrial Billing Specialist to join our team located in Rutland, VT.</p>
<p>Essential Job Functions/Key Accountabilities:</p>
<p>Monthly Invoicing</p>
<ul><li>Obtains an understanding of the Casella Resource Solutions (CRS) group’s operations.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Understands the software systems involved in the billing of CRS accounts.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Maintain monthly invoicing for all CRS assigned accounts.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Understand and be able to complete the Auto Charge function of monthly billing.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Understand and be able to run billing cycles in the software systems involved in billing CRS accounts.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Audit weight tickets for current month prior to month end close to ensure accurate billing.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Understand correct procedure for processing sales adjustments for assigned accounts in Softpak/Core.</li></ul>
<p>Reporting</p>
<ul><li>Maintain customized monthly reporting requirements for customers specific to their needs. These reports can involve multiple locations per report, consolidated reporting, multiple graphs, pivot tables, green sheets, diversion summaries with complicated formatting and formulas. Mid to high level understanding of Excel is needed to complete these.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Complete the end of month customer by customer P &amp; L spreadsheet after billing completion at month end if assigned industrial accounts.</li></ul>
<p>Administrative Processes</p>
<ul><li>Reconcile rebate accounts associated with assigned customers/vendors monthly.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Assist Collection department with collection and billing questions regarding assigned accounts.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Process Accounts Payable invoices for assigned accounts if industrial- done monthly as invoices arrive.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Increase efficiency and value to our billing process.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Assist the Finance team with special project assignments that come up throughout the year.</li></ul>
<p>Position Qualifications:</p>
<p>Skills &amp; Knowledge</p>
<ul><li>Advanced Excel</li></ul>
<ul><li>Microsoft suite</li></ul>
<ul><li>Excellent verbal, written, analytical and formal presentation skills</li></ul>
<ul><li>Ability to work under pressure</li></ul>
<p>Experience</p>
<p>Required</p>
<ul><li>2-4 years Accounting or related experience</li></ul>
<p>Preferred</p>
<ul><li>Billing experience</li></ul>
<p>Education</p>
<p>Required</p>
<ul><li>Bachelor’s Degree or equivalent experience</li></ul>
<p>Preferred</p>
<ul><li>BA in Accounting or Finance Related field</li></ul>
<p>Other Requirements</p>
<ul><li>Can work with specific timelines</li></ul>
