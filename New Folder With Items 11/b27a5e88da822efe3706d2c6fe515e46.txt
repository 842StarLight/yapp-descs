<h2>Network Account Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><ul>DES is seeking a motivated <b>Account Manager</b> to join our team. The successful candidate must have prior outside sales experience as well as experience in enterprise technology sales. This role focuses on mid-market to enterprise-level clients including those in the healthcare, education, financial, and manufacturing sectors.<p></p>
<p>Custom compensation plan includes base-salary based on experience, plus commission for successful sales of any assigned accounts. Technologies include enterprise switching and routing, wireless, servers, storage, voice, and cybersecurity solutions as well as other services.</p>
<p><b>
Position Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Identify, qualify, and develop new prospects in the Northeast region of the US, with a focus on Western Pennsylvania, West Virginia, Maryland, and Ohio initially. Territory is encouraged to be expanded into other locations of this region over time.</li>
<li>Manage portfolios of key accounts, nurture and source existing leads for these customers as well as any new prospects</li>
<li>Recommend products and solutions to prospective customers that best suit their needs and environment
</li>
<li>Attend tradeshows and customer-facing events to build and maintain strong relationships with clients and enhance brand recognition in the region</li>
<li>Prepare and deliver sales proposals and presentations to customers with the ability to follow up with C-level or other key decision makers</li>
<li>Prepare price quotations, and work with vendors and partners on special pricing, delivery dates, etc.</li>
<li>Establish and maintain client relationships to ensure total customer satisfaction</li>
<li>Develop and maintain a thorough knowledge of DES’ Networks Division’s products and service offerings</li>
<li>Participate in preparation of sales forecasting, including accuracy of monthly, quarterly and annual reports</li>
<li>Develop and manage partner relationships to identify customer opportunities and ensure sales enablement</li>
<li>Monitor sales performance metrics and adjust activities accordingly</li>
<li>Ensure data is entered in a timely and accurate manner in the CRM</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Essential Skills and Experience</b></p>
<li>
3+ Years minimum of outside sales experience required</li>
<li>Prior Enterprise or Solution-based technology sales experience in B2B environment is preferred</li>
<li>Proven ability to build and maintain relationships, influence others, and create consensus to close deals</li>
<li>Ability to make recommendations and/or determine the best solutions for customers and prospects</li>
<li>High level of initiative and drive – engages in proactive behavior and looks for new opportunities</li>
<li>Must be comfortable meeting prospects and customers, hosting meetings, and independently leading conversations</li>
<li>Demonstrate expertise in formal sales techniques: prospecting, cold calling, up-selling, cross-selling and closing.</li>
<li>Excellent verbal and written communication skills</li>
<li>Experience working with an ERP/CRM database</li>
<li>Candidate must have reliable transportation</li>
<p><b>Schedule and Hours</b><br>
This position is an outside sales position. Outside sales team members will work towards monthly and quarterly quotas and goals.</p>
<p><b>
Attitude</b><br>
We believe finding the right personality fit for our team is the most important aspect. Successful candidates are courteous, responsible, fun, team-oriented, curious and willing to jump in to learn, research and share.</p>
<p><b>
Benefits</b><br>
We offer a competitive compensation and benefits plan. Join the team which is the Preferred Technology Provider for the Pittsburgh Steelers, and winner of numerous Customer Satisfaction and Top Work Places awards.</p>
