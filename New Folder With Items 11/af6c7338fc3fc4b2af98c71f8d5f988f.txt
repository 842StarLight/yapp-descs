<h2>Retail Supervisor</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p><b>Position Summary:</b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
Supervises Retail Sales Floor activities to ensure that goals are met. Will serve, at times, as “Supervisor in Charge” of entire facility. Responsible for assuring a high level of guest and donor service. Must ensure that Easter Seals-Goodwill policies and procedures are uniformly enforced and followed. Cross training required in all aspects of Goodwill. Performs duties according to the established Best Practices of ESGW.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Conducts regular staff meetings that improve communication with staff, such as daily tool box talks and monthly department meetings</li>
<li>Provides superior guest service by handling guest transactions in a timely and courteous manner, and responding to guest complaints/concerns with a sense of urgency to see to their resolution</li>
<li>Assures that cash register, transaction, and cash handling procedures are strictly followed and enforced. Corrects any register or transaction errors according to established procedures</li>
<li>Acknowledges and greets guests who come within 10 feet</li>
<li>Ensures consistent use of “Guest Ready” book</li>
<li>Follows-up on Store Visit Reports and completes action plans</li>
<li>Completes or delegates tasks, as required, to bring store into compliance with “guest service” standards</li>
<li>Monitors potential safety hazards, reports them and helps correct them</li>
<li>Ensure retail store and donation areas are organized and clean</li>
<li>Assist with Payroll management (i.e.: keeping staff busy doing productive tasks, and/or by asking Team Members to volunteer to go home when business is very slow)</li>
<li>Comply with, train staff, and ensure that all policies and procedures outlined in the Retail Associate Policy and Procedure Handbook are consistently enforced</li>
<li>Ensures display standards are met. Adhere to established recovery best practices. Daily monitoring of the Push Down &amp; Hanger rules</li>
<li>Control inventory by performing rack rolling, maintenance, and stock rotation</li>
<li>Conduct weekly sizing review and take appropriate actions to correct problems</li>
<li>Ensures timely and accurate completion of paperwork and electronic “forms” in areas which are required of all supervisors and/or in any areas that have been specifically assigned by manager including those related to HR, Safety/Asset Protection and Accounting</li>
<li>Completes administrative tasks, which include; accurate daily/weekly/monthly reporting; opening and closing procedures, cash management, and stock on hand reporting</li>
<li>May assist with completion of performance logs, and/or performance evaluations</li>
<li>Responsible for store performance in the following areas:</li>
<li><ul>
<li>Retail Sales floor</li>
<li>Controllable expenses, such as payroll (Ensure staff stay busy &amp; productive)</li>
</ul></li>
<li>Ensures success by motivating and engaging the team; set the example.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p>
</p>
<br>
<br>
Required Skills<br>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Sensitive to guest needs and wishes.</li>
<li>Ability to work effectively with people with disabilities or other special needs preferred.</li>
<li>Must have computer experience and be competent in internet use and a variety of computer software applications.</li>
<li>Must have strong interpersonal, communication, monetary, organizational and decision making skills.</li>
<li>Must be able to read, write and speak the English language in order to communicate with staff and guests.</li>
<li>Applicant must pass background check.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p>
</p>
<p><b>Physical Requirements:</b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Ability to move independently or with reasonable accommodation within the facility and community.</li>
<li>Applicant must be physically and mentally able to perform all requirements of the job.</li>
<li>Able to lift and carry 20-100 pounds with team lifting or mechanical assistance.</li>
<li>Able to stand, stoop, bend, or carry for extended periods</li>
<li>Able to stand and walk entire shift.</li>
<li>Tolerance of extreme changes in temperature and humidity.</li>
<li>Must be able to work flexible hours, evenings, and weekends or longer shifts if needed.</li>
<li><b>May be required to work more than 40 hours per week</b></li>
<br>
</ul>
<br>
Required Experience<br>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>0-2 years previous experience</li>
<br>
</ul>
