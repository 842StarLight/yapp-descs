<p>
Immediate opening for a <b>Customer Service Representative</b> at the Denver Branch.
</p>
<p><b>Primary Function:
</b></p>
<p>As a Customer Service Representative for Tolin, your primary objective is to utilize the dispatch software to effectively maintain work schedules for service representatives and assure that customer commitments are met in a timely manner. This position must maintain a working knowledge of all maintenance contracts that are assigned to them including type of coverage, which services are included, and which services are billable.</p>
<p><b>
Job Description:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Daily coordination of Service Technicians to perform scheduled maintenance and repairs to assure Customer commitments are met.</li>
<li>Daily, weekly, monthly scheduling of Technicians.</li>
<li>Daily interaction with Customers to meet their needs as well as Service Managers, Field, Supervisors, Technicians, Parts Coordinator, Service Accountant, and Sales Reps to facilitate meeting customer needs.</li>
<li>Quoting repair work to Customers and follow up procedures.</li>
<li>Working with A/R Department on current Customer status.</li>
<li>Providing support with warranty issues.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Requirements:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Two years of post-secondary education and/or minimum of two years commercial HVAC dispatch experience preferred</li>
<li>Computer literate and technology savvy</li>
<li>Proficient in Microsoft Suite</li>
<li>Excellent verbal and written communication skills</li>
<li>Excellent Customer Service Phone Skills</li>
<li>Ability to understand technical terminology and mechanical processes</li>
<li>Key2Act/Great Plains software experience a plus</li>
<li>Understanding or 3rd party work order systems (Service Chanel, etc.)</li>
<li>Strong Organizational skills</li>
<li>Ability to multitask, detail oriented, positive attitude</li>
</ul>
