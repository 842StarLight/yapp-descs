<p><b>About Fujitsu Network Communications, Inc.</b></p>
<p>
Fujitsu Network Communications, Inc., is a trusted partner to a broad spectrum of customers across all industries, enabling them to realize the maximum value from their communications networks. We are a market-leading U.S.-based manufacturer of network equipment and a top U.S. patent holder in optical networking. Our solutions combine the best wireline, wireless, and software technology with extensive multivendor services expertise to deliver custom, end-to-end network integration and management solutions. For more information, please see http://us.fujitsu.com/telecom, connect with us on LinkedIn at www.linkedin.com/company/fujitsu-network-communications, and follow us on Twitter @FujitsuFNC.</p>

<p>
This planner will use his or her optical expertise to help plan and market Fujitsu’s optical product offerings.</p>
<p>
Key job responsibilities include:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Engage senior technical and business members at Fujitsu’s customers to share industry insights and determine their business needs.</li>
<li>
Represent Fujitsu’s optical product offerings at trade shows and other industry events.</li>
<li>
Develop requirements and high-level roadmaps for Fujitsu’s optical offerings.</li>
<li>
Define product release cadence and target schedules.</li>
<li>
Lead development of business cases.</li>
<li>
Define product release features and priorities.</li>
<li>
Ensure content-resource-schedule tradeoffs to achieve financial goals.</li>
<li>
Ensure appropriate management and control support.</li>

This planner will work closely with Fujitsu’s Sales, Software Business Unit, Regional Business, and Development organizations.</p>

<p>
This position requires a BS degree (EE or CS preferred). A Master’s Degree (technical or MBA) is also preferred.</p>
<p>
12+ years of industry experience is required. Most of that experience should be at an optical equipment supplier.</p>

Candidate must display a good blend of big-picture understanding and command of technical details – to understand and assimilate customer issues and have enough technical knowledge and business sense propose solutions that benefit the customer and Fujitsu.</p>

<p>
Deep and wide understanding of the optical industry</p>

<p>

Credibility</p>
