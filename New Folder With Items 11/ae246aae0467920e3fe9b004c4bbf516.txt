<h2>Marketing Specialist - Macon-Bibb HD</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div><div>
<div><b>Description</b></div>
<div><p>Pay Grade: J</p></div>
<div><b>Job Responsibilities</b></div>
<p></p>
<div>
<div>Marketing</div>
<div>
<div>Develops and implements a marketing plan for Macon-Bibb Health Department (MBCHD)
<div>
<div>Develops annual marketing plan in support of county goals and seasonal priorities (i.e. flu shots, back to school, cancer prevention).
</div>
<div>Targets marketing and outreach to high risk groups including teens.
</div>
<div>Coordinates with district programs to ensure that there is consistency in marketing even as individual program and county needs are addressed.
</div>
<div>Collaborates with various members of the MBCHD team including health education, clinical services and environmental health to ensure consistency in messaging and marketing of programming efforts.
</div>
<div>Creates marketing materials such as brochures, flyers and posters in soft and hard copy for distribution during events and through social and local media outlets.
</div>
<div>Works with Marketing Coordinator to establish on-brand messaging that speaks to local audiences.
</div>
<div>Works with local media salespeople to identify advertising opportunities.
</div>
<div>Assists in the analysis of marketing/media budget, opportunity cost and potential return on investment.
</div>
<div>Seeks continued education in marketing trends, including media consumption trends; and local media and website analytics.
</div>
</div>
</div>
<div>Develops relationships with external marketing partners and vendors
<div>
<div>Coordinates communications and relationships with external marketing partners and vendors in collaboration with the District PIO and Marketing Coordinator.
</div>
<div>Serves as the MBCHD media buyer using program funding
</div>
<div>Establishes and maintains a working relationship with internal and external customers and other related contacts.
</div>
</div>
</div>
</div>Community Relations<div>
<div>Builds and maintains relationships with community members and organizations that share and support mutual goals to improve the health and well-being of the community.
<div>
<div>Builds rapport through effective communication to ensure community engagement.
</div>
<div>Represents MBCHD at various events and meetings.
</div>
</div>
</div>
<div>Develops a strategic plan for promoting MBCHD programs, services and partnerships.
<div><div>Utilizes community assessment information and other data to evaluate the needs of the community.
</div></div>
</div>
<div>Plans and implements community outreach programs, organizes events and coordinates partner participation.</div>
</div>
Skills:<div>
Strong presentation and negotiation skills<br>
Strong interpersonal skills<br>
Excellent writing skills<br>
Experience with various software platforms for marketing, social media use</div>
</div>
<p></p>
<div><b>Minimum Qualifications</b></div>
<div><div>Bachelor's degree in a related field from an accredited college or university.<br>
<br>
<b>Preferred Qualifications:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Experience leading event planning or programmatic initiative.</li>
<li>One year in public health or corporate relations.</li>
</ul>
</div></div>
