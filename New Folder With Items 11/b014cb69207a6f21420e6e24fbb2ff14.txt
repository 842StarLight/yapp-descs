<h2>Accounting Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Avi-on Lighting Controls power major commercial installations in the United States, plus the white label platform for Eaton’s Halo Home. Avi-on is the perfect place for people with owner/builder attitudes: the opportunity to own a specialized area and continually build it to match the growth to the company.</p>
<p>This role is an opportunity to own the day to day accounting, financial, and operational functions for a rapidly growing, international technology company located in Park City, Utah. Your contribution and proficiency will enable our teams to make key strategic decisions that impact both Avi-on, and the lighting and controls industry.</p>
<p>&gt; Attention to detail and the ability to communicate in writing are critical to success in this role. First consideration will be given to candidates who read this job description and include a written cover letter describing their relevant experience and interest in this role.</p>
<p><b>JOB RESPONSIBILITIES: </b></p>
<p>This role manages and performs all of the following functions. You are both the person setting daily priorities AND touching the buttons to make it happen. We will be adding accounting support depending on the skills of the person in this specific role.</p>
<ul>
<li>Accounts Payable processing – invoice review, approval process, entry and documentation tracking.</li>
<li>Customer Purchase Order entry and Sales Order confirmation.</li>
<li>Customer invoicing and payment processing/bank deposits.</li>
<li>Monthly bank reconciliations and general ledger account reconciliation.</li>
<li>Expense report processing.</li>
<li>Monthly, quarterly and annual inventory reconciliation and adjusting entries.</li>
<li>Semi-monthly payroll processing and coordination of HR information with our PEO.</li>
<li>Quarterly Sales Tax reporting.</li>
<li>Budget tracking and actual to budget reporting.</li>
<li>Reconcile monthly general ledger and produce a reconciled Trial Balance.</li>
<li>Produce monthly financial reporting package for management.</li>
<li>Customer Purchase Order entry and Sales Order confirmation.</li>
<li>Electronic document filing.</li>
<li>Budget and forecasting process management.</li>
<li>Additional financial reporting and projects as needed.</li>
<li>General office management and administrative duties.</li>
<li>Support other departments and team members as needed.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>REQUIRED SKILLS: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Experience using Quickbooks Online accounting software is required.</li>
<li>Experience using SOS Inventory Management Software and Expensify preferred.</li>
<li>Experience in a product and/or project accounting environment required.</li>
<li>Inventory management and/or construction industry experience required.</li>
<li>Understanding of the accounting process through a reconciled Trial Balance and Financial Statements.</li>
<li>Strong attention to detail and accuracy.</li>
<li>Ability to manage multiple priorities in a fast-paced environment.</li>
<li>Strong knowledge of Microsoft Excel, Word, Powerpoint as well as Google Sheets, Docs and Slides.</li>
<li>Ability to recognize discrepancies in written information and recorded data.</li>
<li>Ability to meet multiple deadlines and adapt to changing priorities.</li>
<li>Ability to communicate effectively with management, co-workers and outside contacts.</li>
<li>Interest and ability to use digital tools like email, text, phone, Slack, Confluence, and others.</li>
<li>Able to establish priorities, work independently and proceed with objectives without supervision.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>MINIMUM QUALIFICATIONS: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>5+ years of senior level accounting experience.</li>
<li>Bachelor’s Degree in Accounting (may consider an Associates Degree with additional years of job experience)</li>
<li>Advanced skills in Quickbooks Online and Excel/Google Sheets</li>
</ul>
<p>This role reports to the Controller with additional direction from the COO. The position is located at the Jeremy Ranch Exist on I-80, northwest of Park City, Utah. This is a full-time position with benefits that include paid time off, sick leave, paid holidays, health and supplemental insurance, stock options and 401(k). Travel is possible, but minimal. Compensation will be commensurate with experience.</p>
