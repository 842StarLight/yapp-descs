<b>The Role</b><br>
<br>
Job Summary &amp; Responsibilities:<br>
The Director of Integrated Sales (for GM) (full-time, salaried, regular) will manage the year long, monthly and daily strategic sales strategy, revenue and goal achievement on behalf of the MotorTrend Group specific, but not limited the General Motors business.<br>
<br>
Essential duties and responsibilities include but not limited to:<br>
<ul>
<li>Be able to articulate and sell the overall MotorTrend Group properties as both individual units, as well as 360 integrated marketing programs including (Print, Digital, Social, Video, Linear, Programmatic, Data, etc.)</li>
<li>Understand and identify role of MTG amongst General Motors and all divisions.</li>
<li>Understand and identify GM media “tiers” and MTGs role within GM tiers.</li>
<li>Lead and manage MTG GM Business Plan.</li>
<li>Work directly with MTG Marketing to develop category, brand specific or custom solutions achieving the goals and objectives of GM.</li>
<li>Translate and manage approved MTG strategies and products into sales programs incorporating an aggressive, systematic plan for approaching GM.</li>
<li>The ability to negotiate and close the complex deals involving Carat Planning, Investment, etc.</li>
<li>Manage and execute all GM RFPs</li>
<li>Plan, organize and lead activities and entertainment focused on establishing and maintaining mutually beneficial relationships with key decision markers at Client and Agencies.</li>
<li>Participate in trade show, special events and other promotional programs as required</li>
<li>Partner with VP/GMs on special projects, sales planning and sales budget and forecasting</li>
<li>Work with Account Manager as point of contact responsible for providing execution status for assigned advertising campaigns</li>
<li>Work responsibly and maintain productive working relationships with multiple internal teams (brand general managers, engineering, web development, social media, product, ad operations, billing, etc.) to facilitate execution of advertising campaigns.</li>
<li>Assist Account Manager with the management of client expectations throughout the life of a campaign.</li>
<li>Work with the Account Manager and Advertising Operations team to oversee the ongoing tracking and optimizations of GM advertising campaigns.</li>
<li>Assist Media Planning, Campaign Management, and Account Management teams in strategic analysis of completed campaigns and provide recommendations for future GM campaigns.</li>
</ul>
