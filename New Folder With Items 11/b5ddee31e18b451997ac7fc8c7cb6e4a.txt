<h2>New Home Consultant</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div><div>Overview
<br>
<br>
<p><b>Summary of Position Requirements</b></p>
<p>
Responsible for the daily operation of a Welcome Home Center. Working with customers, processing sale agreements and closing new home sales with the final goal of creating a satisfied customer. #CB</p>
<br>
<br>
<br>
<b>Responsibilities
</b><br>
<br>
<p><b>Primary Duties and Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Engage visitors, establishing a relationship and determine their home buying needs by utilizing the Company’s selling philosophy</li>
<li>
Through self generated or referred leads, sell, process and close homes in accordance with company business plans using the 10-5-2-1
</li>
<li>Travel throughout the local community, self-generating leads from Realtor/Co-Broker businesses, referrals and self-prospecting
</li>
<li>Develop an in-depth knowledge of the competitive marketplace, including product, site, local community, sales, advertising and demographics</li>
<li>
Customer-generation process consisting of each week generating 10 new customers; 5 initial appointments; 2 follow-up appointments; and creating 1 net sale, with the final goal of creating a satisfied customer</li>
<li>
Accompany visitors through the Welcome Home Centers, models, and inventory homes answering questions and providing information about the community and discussing features of the home.
</li>
<li>Provide timely and consistent follow-through with customers from initial contact; the purchase and financing process, through all closing and post closing activities. Maintain accurate records of all communications during this process.</li>
<li>
Participate in weekly sales meetings reviewing neighborhood status and sales strategies, including Division Phone Banks.</li>
<li>
Monitor and record daily customer traffic utilizing company designated tracking tools.</li>
<li>
Periodically gather data and prepare reports for management review.</li>
<li>
Responsible for maintaining the condition of the Welcome Home Center, models and inventory homes communicating with field and office staff of maintenance issues and ensuring issues are resolved</li>
<li>
Participate in the homeowner meetings and orientation(s) required by the Division</li>
<li>
Required to cross-train on other communities within the division as needed</li>
<li>
Required to have iPad 2 or newer, capable of 3G service</li>
</ul>
