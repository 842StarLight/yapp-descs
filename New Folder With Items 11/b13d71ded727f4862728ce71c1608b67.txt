<p><b>
Start Your Journey with Midas Hospitality:</b></p>
<p>
Midas Hospitality is seeking a Sales Manager to join our sales team at the brand new and soon to be opened Courtyard by Marriott located in Steele Creek, NC. This person will assist the Director of Sales with all aspects of the hotel sales department. Daily activities will include booking social/SMERF groups, meeting rooms and conferences while providing exceptional customer service to hotel guests.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
What You Will Be Doing:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Complete weekly telemarketing calls to solicit new and untapped business leads within the Sales Coordinator's specified market segments.</li>
<li>
Be fully acquainted with all lead systems Meeting Broker, Lanyon, etc. and support the Director of Sales in follow-up of all leads within the specified time guidelines.</li>
<li>
Direct sales leads to the Director of Sales within the DOS's established market segments.</li>
<li>
Assist the Director of Sales in the development of new programs and sales campaigns in an effort to obtain additional sales from the various market niches.</li>
<li>
Assist Director of Sales with the implementation, follow-through and updating of the quarterly action plans.</li>
<li>
Participate in communication and professional organizations to maintain high visibility and promote sales.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
The Ideal Candidate:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Exceptional communication and sales skills in order to solicit new business and ensure the satisfaction of current business.</li>
<li>
At least one year of sales experience in a hotel or related setting.</li>
<li>
Willingness to complete outside sales calls and cold-calling.</li>
</ul>
