<h2>Chief Executive Officer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<b>ESSENTIAL FUNCTIONS</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Directs all programs and operations.
</li>
<li>Establishes and implements departmental strategic plan, budget, policies, programs and procedures.
</li>
<li>Exercises supervision concerning senior management team and other employees as needed.</li>
</ul>
EXAMPLES OF DUTIES: <b>EXAMPLES OF THE WORK</b> (These statements are intended to be illustrations of the<br>
work of this class.)<br>
UNDER POLICY DIRECTION:<br>
<ul>
<li>Directs activities and operations including budget, fixed assets, accounting, payroll, marketing, gift shop, private and public cruise booking, ticket sales, food and beverage, advertising, publicity and purchasing in accordance with the Agency President.
</li>
<li>Plans and develops the departmental strategic plan, goals, objectives, policies and procedures.
</li>
<li>Prepares the annual budget in conjunction with the Agency President and monitors expenditures and funds received.
</li>
<li>Advises the Agency President on accounting, financial and budgeting matters.
</li>
<li>Represents the agency to the public, media, schools, community groups, tourism leadership, U.S. Coast Guard and other government agencies to provide information concerning activities and operations.
</li>
<li>Approves and signs group charter contracts and other major partnerships.
</li>
<li>Selects, trains, evaluates, counsels and disciplines employees.
</li>
<li>Seeks funds for the Belle of Louisville and Andrew Broaddus foundations, prepares grant requests; develops capital improvement plans and approves contracts awarded for such programs.
</li>
<li>Reviews purchases, approves paying of bills and signs checks.
</li>
<li>Prepares and submits reports and completes special projects.
</li>
<li>Performs related work.</li>
</ul>
MINIMUM REQUIREMENTS: <b>MINIMUM REQUIREMENTS</b><br>
Bachelor's degree in Business Administration, Human Resources Management,<br>
Communications or a related field.<br>
Five years of budget preparation and analysis, marketing, employee relations, public relations, communications or business administration experience, tourism industry experience including minimum of five years management experience.</div></div>
