<p>Job Description:

<p></p>
<p><b>About Loop Commerce:
</b></p>
<p></p>
<p>Ready to work on an exciting product that is at the forefront of changing an entire e-commerce industry and touches millions of consumers across leading retailers? Loop Commerce, a Synchrony Solution, has transformed the retail experience with GiftNow® by allowing retailers to offer gift givers enhanced choices at check out and gives gift recipients personalized options. We’ve recently expanded our capabilities with our Gift360™ suite of products, to drive further value to retailers and consumers. As we continue to grow, we need talented, passionate and innovative people to join our team and help redefine how people buy and receive gifts across the digital and physical worlds.
</p>
<p></p>
<p>Loop Commerce, leader in the fast-growing Gift Commerce category, is looking for a highly motivated, results-driven sales professional to manage a given territory. We are looking to add seasoned digital commerce sales experts to the team with at least 10 years of experience winning and forging relationships with the C-suite of retail organizations. As an Account Executive, you will be responsible for generating sales opportunities through outbound prospecting and converting marketing leads. The ideal candidate will have a track record of selling multi-faceted engagements in the retail industry, be comfortable navigating IR100 organizations and deal cycles, have a background selling into retailers, and be passionate about the digital commerce industry.
</p>
<p></p>
<p>We offer a vibrant culture as being a family-like and fun-loving atmosphere, agile, and focused on defining the leading edge of something meaningful for millions of people. We’re located in the heart of Silicon Valley. And now, while maintaining our start-up culture and fast-pace innovation, Loop has the backing and support of Synchrony to fuel and accelerate its growth and success.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Essential Responsibilities:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Generate and drive new business opportunities through prospecting and networking within a specific territory or set of named Retail and Brand accounts
</li>
<li>Conduct well-informed and consultative meetings with senior level executives (C-suite) to demonstrate the business value of the Loop Commerce solution(s)
</li>
<li>Own all aspects of the sale from aggressively prospecting and generating new business opportunities to navigating the legal review of a contract
</li>
<li>Leverage and coordinate cross-functional internal teams to efficiently navigate complex sales cycles
</li>
<li>Provide up to date and accurate forecasts by actively updating all things Hubspot/Salesforce related
</li>
<li>Responsible for developing data-driven processes such as sales programs, major account strategy, outbound initiatives, lead gen/management, and continue to innovate in the sales process.
</li>
<li>Perform other duties and/or special projects as assigned
</li>
</ul>
