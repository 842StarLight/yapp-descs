<h2>Navitaire-Content Marketing Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Navitaire, a wholly owned Amadeus Company, delivers industry-leading technology services supporting growth, profitability and innovation to airlines and rail clients worldwide, including many of the world’s most successful airlines. Navitaire offers a full suite of proven solutions focused on revenue generation and streamlining costs in the areas of reservations, ancillary sales, loyalty, revenue management, revenue accounting and business intelligence. Each day, more than 500 employees based in five global offices develop, support and operate Navitaire solutions to help our clients continue to move the travel industry forward.
</b></p>
<p>If you are passionate about the travel industry, and talented at promoting solution benefits to business customers then our Content Marketing Specialist is a great opportunity to join Navitaire, a growing company that works with many of the world's fastest growing airlines. In this role, you’ll be responsible for creating compelling, persuasive content for digital, print and other channels, plus managing marketing projects for tradeshows, events, website, research, PR, social media, and communications. You’ll work closely with the creative designer, and collaborate with product, sales and account team members across the company as you contribute to the development, execution and success of our marketing programs, strategies and initiatives. The ideal candidate will have prior experience with a range of marketing functions, including communications, branding, digital marketing, events and social media. If you like variety, are organized and able to handle many concurrent, diverse projects with short deadlines, then our Content Marketing Specialist may be a good fit for you.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Responsibilities:<br>
</b></p>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Develop content, research, write, edit and update for use in diverse channels including print and digital media, collateral, presentations, video, communications, emails, campaigns, social media and more
</li>
<li>Draft, edit and execute internal and external communications including e-newsletters, targeted direct mail and press releases
</li>
<li>Assist with development, coordination, content and execution of marketing events such as tradeshows, conferences, seminars, speaking engagements and workshops
</li>
<li>Coordinate and support key internal projects and related promotional project activities
</li>
<li>Help create and maintain consistent brand messaging and voice across sales channels, customer and internal communications
</li>
<li>Assist with branding strategy, along with defining key messages to connect with audiences. Uphold and manage brand identity standards, company disclosures and trademarks.
</li>
<li>Support social media and PR initiatives, industry trend research, corporate information requests and other duties as assigned<br>
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Experience:<br>
</b></p>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor's degree in Marketing, Communications, English, Journalism or related field
</li>
<li>Minimum of 3 years of experience in content creation and management, marketing communications, event planning and coordination, internal communications and social media, writing experience for multiple purposes and for various formats
</li>
<li>Demonstrated competency in marketing, communications and project management
</li>
<li>Industry experience in a commercial related area and/or technology experience in the airline or travel industry
</li>
<li>Exceptional PC proficiency, especially with Microsoft Office PowerPoint, Word and Excel, also SharePoint and Teams
</li>
<li>Experience with Cvent (conference and emarketing), Adobe Acrobat editing and optimizing, CrowdSignal (survey) highly desired; familiarity with Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, InDesign and other marketing platforms helpful
</li>
<li>Strong project management skills
</li>
<li>Experience working with budgets
</li>
<li>Good aptitude with new software tools
</li>
<li>Knowledge of traditional and digital marketing, especially in the IT and/or airline industries
</li>
<li>Awareness and understanding of primary airline and travel related practices, players, roles and trends
</li>
<li>Identify and prioritize key messages for effective audience centric B2B communications
</li>
<li>Focus on process improvement, development and maintenance of tracking tools for marketing data, projects and activities. Apply a business operator mindset to projects and execution.
</li>
</ul>
