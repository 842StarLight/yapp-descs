<h2>Partner Promotions Operations</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>We are a global marketing services group specializing in the execution of print, digital and retail media. Providing services to brands and retailers, both directly and via their nominated agencies, HH Global delivers a seamlessly integrated Customer Brand Experience and a greater measurable return on marketing investment (ROMI).</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
With offices across Europe, the US, Latin America, the Middle East and Asia-Pacific, HH Global delivers for some of the most successful and challenging brands in the world and is a market leader in the coordinated, leveraged, management of global marketing supply chains to unify and synchronize all customer touchpoints across digital, print and retail media.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
The HH Global proposition includes: Marketing Technology; Media Asset Management; Creative Production; Artwork Management; Trans creation and Adaptation; Marketing Procurement; Production Management and Marketing Fulfilment.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b><i>
Responsibilities</i></b></p>
<ul>
<li>Manage communications with internal partners requesting Promotions - including timelines, capabilities, status, risks, etc.</li>
<li>Track status of campaign execution</li>
<li>Evaluate and explore the feasibility of new promotion ideas
</li>
<li>Validate promotion compliance at the country level using existing capabilities matrix or verifying directly with legal team</li>
<li>Validate contractual requirements are met for each promotion</li>
<li>Manage all approvals (including business case, legal / compliance, T&amp;Câ€™s, etc)</li>
<li>Mint redemption testing codes &amp; share developers or other stakeholders to support redemption testing
</li>
<li>Validate the redemption testing done by developers or other stakeholders</li>
<li>Activate and sync campaigns after approvals</li>
<li>Identify bugs and tool functionality issues &amp; report them</li>
<li>Develop Terms and Conditions applied to partner promotion(s) &amp; submit a localization request</li>
<li>Create Campaign Details Pages (CDP) or microsites to support promotions (as needed, not frequent)</li>
<li>Monitor promotion results to track results, spend thresholds, and to flag potential user abuse or issues</li>
<li>Conduct campaign performance report &amp; share with partners (or enable partners to self-serve reports)</li>
<li>Create invoices or purchase orders to settle promotion funding
</li>
<li>Track a status of campaign invoicing and provide performance data as needed</li>
<li>Terms &amp; Conditions Tool Kit - own maintenance of the tool kit including updating changes, managing regular review cadence with legal, etc.</li>
<li>Track and maintain promo capabilities matrix as things change due to regulatory changes, new information, new launches, etc.</li>
<li>Assist with new capabilities such as Tax, Treasury, Legal (TTL) review submissions, new country launch requests, etc.</li>

<li>User Acceptance Testing for enhancements to tools and systems</li>

</ul>
