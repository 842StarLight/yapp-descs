<b>Position Summary:</b><br>
The FOH Manager is responsible for ordering supplies, regulatory control, ensuring high guest service, building maintenance, meeting budget, quality control, training staff, and dealing with personnel according to human resource guidelines.
<br><br>
<b>Essential Job Duties &amp; Responsibilities:</b><br>
Reports directly to General Manager
<br>Ensures that each customer receives outstanding service by providing a customer-friendly environment which includes greeting and acknowledging every customer in a positive manner, solid menu and product knowledge, and all other components of the restaurant’s customer service standards in order to take care of a customer’s needs.
<br>Estimates food consumption, place orders with suppliers, and schedule delivery of fresh food and beverages.
<br>Resolves customer complaints about food quality or service.
<br>Direct cleaning of kitchen and dining areas to maintain sanitation standards, and keep appropriate records.
<br>Monitors actions of staff and customers to ensure that health, cleaning, safety standards, and liquor regulations are obeyed. Ensures that all building codes and city health department regulations are met, and that proper documentation is available upon request.
<br>Maintain budget and employee records, prepare payroll, and pay bills, or monitor bookkeeping records.
<br>May use computer software to monitor inventory, track staff schedules and pay, and perform other record keeping tasks.
<br>Checks quality of deliveries of fresh food and baked goods.
<br>Meets with sales representatives to order supplies such as tableware, cooking utensils, and cleaning items.
<br>Arranges for maintenance and repair of equipment and other services.
<br>Totals receipts and balance against sales, deposit receipts, and lock facility at end of day.
<br>Selects or creates successful menu items based on many considerations, and assign prices based on cost analysis.
<br>Recruits, hires, and oversees training for staff. Ensure low turnover and maintain high morale.
<br>Schedules work hours and coordinate activities for server staff.
<br>Monitors food preparation and methods.
<br>Updates and use job-related knowledge.
<br>Records information about inventory, staff, equipment, ordering, and health practices.
<br>Maintains an awareness of food and product information, merchandise promotions, special offers and advertisements
<br>Implements ideas or products
<br>Assists in floor moves, merchandising, display maintenance and restaurant housekeeping.
<br>Assists in processing and replenishing food and merchandise; participate in receiving and monitoring floor stock; ensures re-wraps are done promptly.
<br>Accurately and efficiently completes all sales transactions and maintains proper cash account-abilities at registers.
<br>Communicates customer requests in a prompt, efficient manner.
<br>Cooperates and interacts with colleagues in a positive, respectful manner and with a team approach, offering to assist as needed in order to ensure customer service.
<br>Reports to work on time and as assigned per the schedule on a regular, consistent basis.
<br>Properly uses materials and equipment to effectively and efficiently complete varying workloads in a timely fashion.
<br>Any other duties as assigned by upper level management.
<br>Aid General Manager with evaluations for hourly counter/cashier staff according to handbook parameters.
