<h2>Compliance Analyst</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Overview
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div><b>Position requires 1-2 days per week in Lyndhurst, NJ and 3-4 days per week in Somerset, NJ.</b></div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div><b>
POSITION SUMMARY:</b></div>
<div>
The Compliance Analyst works under direct supervision of the Compliance Manager or Compliance Director and is responsible for daily operations of the Regulatory Affairs and Compliance department. The Compliance Analyst operates primarily at the tactical level, but is required to know and understand FDA, DEA, HIPAA, Part 11 regulatory requirements in order to effectively support company compliance activities on a day-to-day basis.</div>

<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div><b>KEY RESPONSIBILITIES:</b></div>
<ul><li>
Non-compliance by Sales Representatives</li></ul>
<ul>
<li>
Assists Regulatory Affairs and Compliance management on incident analysis and trending.</li>
<li>
Conducts internal audits</li>
<li>
Handles any updates to company’s mid-level prescriptive authority table for Rx and DEA sampling (review FDA, DEA, HHS websites, state websites, and SHALL)</li>
<li>
Handles any updates to company’s State Loss / Theft Reporting
</li>
<li>Aids Compliance Manager in monitoring changes to PDMA requirements and state requirements for drug sampling and sales representative activities.</li>
<li>
Monitors the PDMA Alliance website and regulatory database (i.e. SHALL) for legislative and regulatory updates.</li>
<li>
Conducts regulatory research and preliminary analysis on federal and state investigation and reporting requirements.</li>
<li>
Conducts regulatory research on mid-level prescriptive authority.</li>
<li>
Assists with preparation of materials for client audits alongside Compliance Administrator.</li>
<li>
Assists with preparation of training materials for sales reps alongside Compliance Administrator.</li>
<li>
Maintains client-related Regulatory Affairs and Compliance files alongside Compliance Administrator.</li>
<li>
Document archiving as required alongside Compliance Administrator.</li>
<li>
Maintains Resource hours accurately to capture billable, non-billable and overtime hours.</li>
</ul>
<ul><li>
Schedule/assist/conduct Annual Regulatory trainings for employees.</li></ul>
