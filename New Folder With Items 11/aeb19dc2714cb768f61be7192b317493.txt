<b>About Us</b><br>
<br>
Kroenke Sports &amp; Entertainment (KSE) is an American Sports and entertainment holding company based in Denver, Colorado. Kroenke Sports &amp; Entertainment is committed to providing world class sports and entertainment for both live and broadcast audiences. We will welcome fans into our venues as family, providing respect and care from the purchase of a ticket, to the drive home. We will celebrate the best in sport and entertainment by recognizing the diversity and human spirit around us, and by working within our community to improve the lives of all those within the community. We will strive to perform this mission within a viable and self-sustaining business model.<br>
<br>
<b>About Our Job</b><br>
<br>
KSE Radio Ventures, LLC is seeking a full-time Sales Coordinator. This position provides administrative assistance to the Sales team by compiling advertising agreements, sales materials, and maintaining advertiser schedules in the traffic system.<br>
<br>
Job responsibilities will include:<br>
<ul>
<li>Input advertiser commercial schedules/packages into WideOrbit Traffic System.</li>
<li>Double check and verify all contracts are entered into Traffic System correctly.</li>
<li>Ensure that all client contracts are filed properly and copied for sales staff.</li>
<li>Assist Sales with any requests and day to day functions.</li>
<li>Print bi-weekly reports and work with Traffic Manager on processing make-goods.</li>
<li>Pull pre and post logs reports as needed.</li>
<li>Complete monthly co-op invoices for AE’s and/or clients.</li>
<li>Track all sponsorships and deliver weekly information to sales team.</li>
<li>Trouble shoot problems and or discrepancies for AE’s and clients.</li>
<li>Process new client info to accounting group.</li>
<li>Build team packages in traffic system.</li>
<li>Back up traffic manager and other team members on an as needed basis.</li>
<li>Troubleshoot log issues with Traffic Manager, Program Directors and Sales.</li>
<li>Performs other duties and responsibilities as directed by supervisor(s), including but not limited to special projects.</li>
</ul>
<br>
