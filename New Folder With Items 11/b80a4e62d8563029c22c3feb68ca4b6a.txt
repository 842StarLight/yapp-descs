<h2>Account Executive, Commercial SMB</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>The challenge
</b></h2>
<p>Adobe is looking for an Account Executive for our Commercial Business for our Marketo business unit who will focus exclusively on new business acquisition in the Small and Medium Size Business segment. If you are an enthusiastic salesperson, looking to join a software company with plenty of room to grow, apply today!
</p>
<p>This role sits in our Denver office and reports to the Manager, Commercial Sales.
</p>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>What you’ll do
</b></h2>
<ul>
<li>Nurture and sell to a defined territory segment consisting of accounts up to $100M in business revenue
</li>
<li>Prospect, develop, qualify, and close new and existing leads for Adobe Campaign and Marketo’s entire suite of products resulting in $25k-$400k in ARR.
</li>
<li>Manage pipeline hygiene in Salesforce to provide forecasts on weekly cadence to your Commercial Sales Manager
</li>
<li>Navigate deal cycles 2-4 months in length
</li>
<li>Negotiate business terms with line-of-business, senior management, and C-level executives
</li>
<li>Own and manage existing accounts for additional cross-sell and upsell opportunities
</li>
<li>Work closely with Customer Success, Support, Solutions Consultants, and Professional Services to satisfy customer’s implementation and servicing needs
</li>
<li>Travel as needed to close business
</li>
</ul>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>What is needed to succeed
</b></h2>
<ul>
<li>2+ years of on-quota sales experience selling B2B SaaS solutions or 3-4 years sales closing experience in highly competitive industries
</li>
<li>History of consistent quota over-achievement
</li>
<li>Previous experience navigating lengthy and complex sales cycles using a value-based selling methodology
</li>
<li>Ability to work in a fast-growing and changing environment
</li>
<li>Teamwork, good communication, and internal partnership skills are strong qualities you'll have
</li>
<li>Four-year university/college degree or equivalent experience required
</li>
</ul>
