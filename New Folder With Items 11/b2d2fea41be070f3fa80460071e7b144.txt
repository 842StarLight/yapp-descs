<h2>Ad Operations Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>As the Ad Operations Specialist, you oversee Google Ad Manager and the ad stack for indirect &amp; direct monetization at Trusted Media Brands. You own the set up and execution of programmatic partners and deals, while supporting the execution and management of direct sold campaigns. You’ll lead technical integrations and new ad product implementations, making you a crucial part of Trusted Media Brands’ digital growth and overall success.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>ESSENTIAL DUTIES/RESPONSIBILITES<br>
</b></p>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Manage technical DFP setup of new demand partners via header bidding and exchange bidding. Provide recommendations for optimization of partner performance.
</li>
<li>Lead set up of programmatic deals for both direct sales team and indirect programmatic monetization strategy
</li>
<li>Work with Digital Account Managers to support the execution of digital campaigns
</li>
<li>Act as a point of escalation for Digital Account Managers when technical ad ops knowledge is needed
</li>
<li>Troubleshoot ad serving errors and issues for both programmatic and direct IO business
</li>
<li>Assist in ad &amp; site product QA for product rollouts
</li>
<li>Collaborate with and support cross-functional teams within Revenue Operations, Product, Marketing, Programmatic, &amp; Yield
</li>
<li>Lead integrations, targeting innovations, and implementations for indirect and direct revenue growth
</li>
<li>Collaborate with and support cross-functional teams within Revenue Operations, Product, Marketing, Programmatic, &amp; Yield
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>REQUIRED SKILLS/QUALIFICATIONS<br>
</b></p>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>4-5 years ad operations and programmatic experience
</li>
<li>Deep technical knowledge of header bidding and more complicated DFP configurations
</li>
<li>Expert knowledge of programmatic deal set up and optimizations
</li>
<li>Experience managing online advertising campaigns using Google Ad Manager, Index, Appnexus, Amazon, and other partners
</li>
<li>Strong understanding of HTML, Flash, and JavaScript, rich media vendors, mobile and video ad serving s olutions, programmatic technologies and exchanges, OMS &amp; CRM tools
</li>
<li>Technical mindset to absorb ad-serving technologies with an eye for detail and precision
</li>
<li>Strong analytical skills with the ability to distill data into actionable insights and decisions
</li>
<li>Able to manage multiple projects and campaigns with conflicting priorities in a deadline driven environment
</li>
<li>Must be a self-starter; able to work with minimal supervision to initiate and complete tasks
</li>
<li>Strong interpersonal skills, ability to collaborate with cross-functional teams
</li>
<li>Must be enthusiastic, friendly and work well under pressure</li>
</ul>
