<p><b>POSITION SUMMARY &amp; KEY AREAS OF RESPONSIBILITY
</b></p>
<p>We’re seeking a “Make is Happen” passionate, creative and persuasive sales professional with experience in total solution sales and forming tight customer partnerships with focus on Software Solution Sales. The primary objectives of this position are to grow the region’s business, drive solution opportunities and continually enhance the breadth and depth of relationships with one of NCR’s premier strategic customer partners.
</p>
<p></p>
<p>If you have experience creating highly complex software, hardware, and consulting solutions for Hospitality accounts in the restaurant, travel and entertainment segments, have a history of success developing an opportunity funnel, influencing decisions and selling across multiple offices, we want to talk to you.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Key Areas of Responsibility include:
</b></p>
<p></p>
<ul><li>Communicate and inspire a vision for the business to customers
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Responsible for managing the NCR Hospitality accounts to ensure both high customer satisfaction as well achievement of the account financial goals
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Develop account specific plans focused on maximizing customer satisfaction on solutions already deployed in the account and to position and introduce the entire portfolio of solutions offered by
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Builds and maintains senior-level customer relationships through consultative selling and promoting customer confidence in NCR
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Must be able to strike the right balance between building and maintaining high level of customer satisfaction with existing products and services and identifying ways to expand the business portfolio within the named accounts
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Coordinates with other parts of the NCR organization to be able to meet client commitments
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Implements structured and consistent communication framework to ensure the relevant stakeholders have up-to-date visibility into the account status and opportunities to identify and implement corrective actions early in the process
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Partners with other business units to expand cross-sell opportunities
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Negotiates multi-year software and services contracts
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Work with senior management to develop territory strategy
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Understands the customer’s business objectives and ensuring NCR is viewed as a strategic partner to help meet these goals
</li></ul>
<p></p>
<ul><li>Manages the sales process:
</li></ul>
<ul>
<li>Build funnel – know how to prospect and run lead campaigns
</li>
<li>Qualify leads – can the customers use our solutions and do they have budget
</li>
<li>Identify decision makers - have a discussion about business issues
</li>
<li>Develop relationships outside IT – tie our solutions to business problems
</li>
<li>Close – negotiate contractual and financial terms
</li>
<li>Ensures ongoing customer satisfaction during presales and post sales engagements
</li>
<li>Communicates effectively and driving cross functional teams to ensure customer success
