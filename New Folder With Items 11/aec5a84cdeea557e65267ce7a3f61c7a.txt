<h2>Account Executive</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>W are looking for a driven, passionate <b>Account Executive</b> who will engage and increase sales in the region. You will use a pro-active strategy and concerted effort to reach out to potential members for FoodBAM.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div><b>
Who are we?</b></div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
FoodBAM is a restaurant inventory management software serving up simple tools for the everyday chef, to general manager of a region, to a COO at a 1,000 unit chain. We offer software and a service that can be adapted in a matter of days, comes with great training and constant access to foodservice consultants helping operations save time and save money. Technology becomes a friend to the restaurant operations, we make sure of that!</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
With over 20 years of experience in Foodservice, our mission is to equip an account with a device that replaces the need for clipboards, invoices and recipe books. This device then becomes the digital doorstep into a blueprint that thousands of locations across the country have followed to advance their missions, optimize their food cost and employer their staff.</div>
