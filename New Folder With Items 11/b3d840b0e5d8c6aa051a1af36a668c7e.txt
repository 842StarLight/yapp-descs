<h2>General Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>Tuff Shed is recruiting for a results-driven <b>General Manager at our Factory Store in </b><b>Little Rock, AR</b> This position reports to the Regional Director and is responsible for operating the Store profitably. This includes providing leadership and management of the following functions: <b>retail and wholesale</b> <b>sales, production, installation, customer service, safety, human resources</b>, and <b>accounting.</b></p>
<p>
<b>ABOUT TUFF SHED</b></p>
<p>
Founded on an entrepreneurial spirit and an unwavering commitment to quality, Tuff Shed was established in 1981, and has since developed into America's largest manufacturer and installer of storage buildings and garages, having built more than one million buildings for satisfied customers. We utilize cutting edge green technology in our materials and manufacturing processes, and hold multiple U.S. Patents.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
DO YOU HAVE WHAT IT TAKES TO BE A SUCCESSFUL TUFF SHED GENERAL MANAGER?</b></p>
<p>
We are seeking an <b>entrepreneurial business person</b> who can take on the tasks of <b>managing</b> <b>our local Factory, installation teams, and sales force</b>; this is an excellent opportunity for a proven leader who can run a <b>dynamic and unique business</b> like it's his/her own</p>
<p>
Enthusiastic, passionate and have great management, selling, marketing, and customer service skills</p>
<p>
Proven <b>sales and service experience in the home improvement industry</b></p>
<p>
A <b>strong local network</b> and experience <b>recruiting and managing a network of independent</b> <b>installers</b> highly preferred</p>
<p>
Significant experience in <b>safety management</b>, including: OSHA; fire prevention; machine and tool usage; PPE; Hazard communication; lifting techniques; fork lift; electrical safety; fall protection; and safety training documentation management</p>
<p>
Experience <b>managing employees</b> and resolving employee relations issues, and an understanding of the importance of <b>"leading by example"</b></p>
<p>
A focus on <b>customer service excellence</b>; the ability to <b>work with and listen to customers</b>, understand their needs, design their buildings, and deliver a great product!
</p>
<p>Hands-on computer skills in Microsoft Office and Internet applications required; experience in utilizing <b>Customer Relations Management (CRM) software</b> including Oracle, JDE, Onyx, Salesforce.com, Goldmine, or similar systems highly preferred</p>
<p>
Must have a current valid driver's license and a satisfactory Motor Vehicle Report</p>
