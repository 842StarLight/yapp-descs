<h2>Order Management Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>At Bose, we’re compelled to push things forward. We need to make things better. It’s in our blood. We’re expert engineers, researchers, and designers. We believe a healthy obsession is something to celebrate because it leads to greatness — not just in what we do, but in art, music, sports, science, everything.
</p>
<p></p>
<p>In the Global B2C Order Management Team, we obsess over the details that matter most to our customers. We collaboratively manage consumer orders in a fast-paced, challenging environment, which requires excellent attention to detail and communication skills. Through processing orders inquiries for Bose Direct consumers in the Americas, Europe, and Australia/New Zealand, we provide our customers and the organizations we support with the highest level of service possible, while maintaining efficiency and driving continuous improvement. Key activities are order review and release, logistics, credit refunds/reconciliation, fraud review, and inventory management.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Key Accountabilities:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li><p>Responsible for order management of external and internal direct-to-consumer (B2C) orders.
</p></li>
<li><p>Provide data analysis around customer KPIs
</p></li>
<li><p>Leverage system solutions to increase automation of standard processes
</p></li>
<li><p>Drive continuous improvement to maximize efficiency in processes, and reduce the cost of managing orders to customers
</p></li>
<li><p>Manage high volume of daily order processing with accuracy and efficiency
</p></li>
<li><p>Perform key tasks according to service levels, and communicate risks to participate in mitigation plan
</p></li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Key Skills:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li><p>Self-motivated, self-starter, and ability to work independently
</p></li>
<li><p>Strong analytical skills
</p></li>
<li><p>Ability to manage multiple priorities and meet deadlines
</p></li>
<li><p>Knowledge of supply chain, distribution, and sales processes.
</p></li>
<li><p>Excel and Outlook proficiency
</p></li>
<li><p>Previous experience with SAP, CRM, EDI, or related enterprise software
</p></li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p>You may be required to work second shift on occasion to cover other geographic areas to support the global business.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Education and Experience:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li><p>Bachelor’s Degree in Business, Finance, Operations, Supply Chain, or equivalent experience
</p></li>
<li><p>2+ years of experience in Order Management
</p></li>
</ul>
