The world's leading private security organization, G4S, has an immediate job opportunity for a Scheduling Specialist. As a Scheduling Specialist, you will be directly responsible for the communication, dispatching and/or scheduling various levels of field security personnel/security officers for regular weekly contractual service hours and service requests/changes provided by G4S Secure Solutions National Communications Center and G4S customers. This position includes maintenance of schedules in G4S Labor Scheduling System and the oversight of data entry in the Labor Scheduling System.
<br><br>
G4S is a security provider for the United States government, fortune 500 companies, nuclear power plants, oil and gas companies, airport, ports, banks, hospitals, factories, warehouses, commercial facilities, residential communities and much more.

Main Responsibilities
<br>Specific Duties and Essential Functions
<br><br>
Proactively communicate and confirm scheduling posts, shifts, and scheduled times
<br>Correctly map and maintain a schedule on a weekly basis
<br>Communicate with the Ops managers, clients, site supervisors, area supervisors, and flex officers on a consistent basis to ensure overall satisfaction and efficiency with G4S and the operations team
<br>Create Master Schedule
<br>Create three-week schedule look ahead and one-week locked schedule
<br>Identify accurately number of open positions
<br>Provide assistance to the General Manager and Operations Manager in monitoring non-billable overtime
<br>Coordinate OJT (on the job training) for Officers with Site Supervisors, Account Managers, and Clients
<br>Schedule and manage call offs and vacation requests for the employees
<br>Work with Area Supervisors (Territory Manager/Ops Manager) to address any overrides/errors that need to be addressed
<br>Data entry in the G4S Labor Scheduling System
<br>Dispatch service requests to field supervision
<br>Provide response to G4S National Communications Center with resolutions to schedule/service requests
<br>Provide assistance to customers, employees, and visitors in a courteous and professional manner
