<h2>OPERATOR - PLASTIC</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">Founded in 1925, MacLean-Fogg is a worldwide enterprise currently operating 40 global manufacturing facilities across 5 continents with annual sales in excess of one billion (USD) and a workforce of over 3,500 people. MacLean-Fogg is a privately held enterprise headquartered in Mundelein, IL USA and is comprised of two primary businesses, MacLean-Fogg Component Solutions and MacLean Power Systems. MacLean-Fogg Component Solutions (MFCS) is a leading manufacturer of fastener components, engineered components and engineered plastics for automotive, heavy truck, and other diverse industries. MacLean Power Systems (MPS) is a leading manufacturer of products for electric utility, telecommunications and civil markets.
<br><br>
Operate injection molding machines and auxiliary equipment. Handle, inspect, and package molded parts according to written instructions and provided specification. Take responsibility for quality of parts produced.
<br><br>
Responsibilities
<br><ul>
<li>Verify that proper materials and colorant are at the workstation as per provided specifications.</li>
<li>Read and follow all work instructions provided.</li>
<li>Operate molding machines in the prescribed manner to avoid injuries and prevent damage to machine and molds.</li>
<li>Maintain prescribed cycle times insuring 100% efficiency.</li>
<li>Package finished goods using established standards.</li>
<li>Accurately and neatly maintain production documentation records.</li>
<li>Handle, inspect and package molded parts per written instructions and requirements. Responsible for visual quality according to written and visual requirements.</li>
<li>Communicate with the other shifts regarding quality and process concerns and issues.</li>
<li>Communicate issues as they arise.</li>
<li>Support environmental system specific responsibilities as assigned by system procedures.</li>
<li>Use hand tools such as clippers and utility knives to trim molded parts. Perform secondary operations when required by the provided instructions.</li>
<li>Manage area specific system procedures. Follow all Safety, Environmental, and Quality policies and guidelines.</li>
<li>Other duties may be assigned or required for this position.</li>
</ul>
