<h2>Digital Marketing Analyst</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">**Description**
<br><br>
At Pearson, we’re committed to a world that’s always learning and to our talented team who makes it all possible. From bringing lectures vividly to life to turning textbooks into laptop lessons, we are always re-examining the way people learn best, whether it’s one child in our own backyard or an education community across the globe. We are bold thinkers and standout innovators who motivate each other to explore new frontiers in an environment that supports and inspires us to always be better. By pushing the boundaries of technology — and each other to surpass these boundaries — we create seeds of learning that become the catalyst for the world’s innovations, personal and global, large and small.
<br><br>
Join our fast evolving team for Marketing Technologies. Bring together platforms like Salesforce, Sprinklr, marketing content tools, to further expand our digital education experience. You will also support researching and analyzing new marketing tools to bring more marketing to our users. By going more and more digital, Pearson has the key ability to provide education the far reaches of the globe! That is a job worth being a part of. Here in our RTP office, we enjoy lots of camaraderie, social events, walking trails, game room, wellness room, and access to numerous restaurants within walking distance. Come join the team &amp; change our education world at Pearson!
<br><br>
**Qualifications**
<br><br>
The successful candidate has a passion for learning about the technology needs of our various teams, identifying gaps, and executing the best possible solutions. Experience should include large-scale IT project and program execution, as well as familiarity with marketing workflows.
<br><br>
The ideal candidate is someone who has prior experience delivering solutions as digital marketing software or marketing technology software such as Digital Asset Management, campaign management tools, social media management, etc. The specialist will interface with a variety of business partners, along with a tech team to ensure well-managed execution of processes and deliverables. They will also closely align with legal and data privacy teams to ensure that Pearson meets all corporate, international, and contractual standards.
<br><br>
Our ideal candidate is more than just the software; he or she knows people. The right person has a clear understanding of Pearson’s overall mission and is ready to apply technology toward that goal in a way everyone can understand. High-level communication and presentation skills as well as impressive data analysis abilities make this possible.
<br><br>
With a background in both business and IT, and a passion for marketing technology trends, our ideal candidate is comfortable educating others on the latest technology, which helps make transitions to new platforms and tools smoother for all key stakeholders by speaking the language of both marketers and hard-core technologists.
<br><br>
