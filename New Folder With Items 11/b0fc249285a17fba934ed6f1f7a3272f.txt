<h2>Business Development Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>General Purpose: </b></p>
<p>Liaise with government procurement officials (local, city, state, county, federal…) to develop employment opportunities for PWD/Persons with Disabilities through GW’s GoodTemps Division</p>
<p><b>Essential Functions: </b></p>
<p>· Develop a procurement strategy/plan to expand GW/GT services to government agencies.</p>
<p>· Develop relationships with government agencies Procurement Officers in order to expand temping opportunities.</p>
<p>· Create and/or modify procurement/marketing materials to present GW services to potential customers.</p>
<p>· Develop an understanding of preferred source contracting in New York versus New Jersey, so as to maximize each State’s potential</p>
<p>· Build relationships with NYSID, ACCSES-NJ, to maximize opportunities.</p>
<p>· Expand the number of temping opportunities in existing contracts and agencies</p>
<p>· Open more agencies (City, state, local…) to temping opportunities</p>
<p><b>Qualifications: </b></p>
<p>· Bachelor’s degree</p>
<p>· 3+ years’ experience in procurement, temping or marketing</p>
<p><b>Skills Required: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Must have excellent people skills to work in a contracting and human services/rehabilitation environment.</li>
<li>Must have demonstrable organizational, program and financial management, administrative skills and ability to work effectively in an extensive inter-agency collaborative operation.</li>
<li>Must have excellent verbal and written communication skills, professional presentation and excellent interpersonal/customer service skills.</li>
<li>Must have knowledge of the current software including Microsoft Office</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Scope of Responsibility &amp; Positions Supervised: .</b></p>
<p>· Able to analyze opportunities to develop contract possibilities</p>
<p>· No supervisory, budgeting or fiscal responsibility</p>
<p><b>Special Working Conditions: </b></p>
<p>· Must be able to travel to various locations in New York City, NY State and New Jersey</p>
<p>· Must be able to work in a fast paced environment under pressure</p>
<p>Job Type: Full-time</p>
<p>Salary: $60,000.00 to $65,000.00 /year</p>
<p>Experience:</p>
<ul>
<li>Business Development: 3 years (Required)</li>
<li>Procurement: 3 years (Required)</li>
<li>Marketing: 3 years (Required)</li>
</ul>
<p>Education:</p>
<ul><li>Bachelor's (Required)</li></ul>
<p>Work authorization:</p>
<ul><li>United States (Required)</li></ul>
