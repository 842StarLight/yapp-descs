<p><b>Description</b></p>
<p>Do you want to truly make meaningful and positive impacts on society in your work? If you are looking for an exciting high-growth start-up culture, then Agrivolution is the place for you. We are seeking an enthusiastic and hard-working intern who is passionate about sustainable agro-technologies to assist in our digital marketing activities. In this role, you will be assigned various marketing projects including email and social media marketing, content generation, website management and more. The intern will assist in launching and monitoring multiple campaigns to generate traffic and sales leads. The candidate will also assist in creating analytic reports to gain new insights based on marketing data. The ideal candidate would be familiar with various internet marketing concepts. The candidate should be highly analytical, goal-oriented, a self-initiator who likes working in a fast-paced environment.<br><b>Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul><li>Create, develop, and manage a variety of content types – written, videos, presentations, etc. – as needed to fuel business and marketing success.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Leverage and influence existing customers by creating their compelling success stories to amplify marketing effectiveness to reach more prospects.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Conduct marketing research and competitive analyses that will identify and engage new marketing prospects.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Strategize and implement multiple phases of digital marketing campaigns including email marketing, website development, internet advertising, and social media.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Update WordPress websites and e-commerce platforms.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Establishing our brands through active marketing and a consistent message throughout all digital platforms.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Design and run weekly and monthly data-driven reports that will enable effective execution of all digital marketing efforts.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Improve SEO through data-mining Google Analytics data and growing organic and paid traffic.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Evaluate social performance trends &amp; opportunities based on data.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Assist in monitoring active marketing campaigns and developing programs to improve those performances and increase ROAS.</li></ul>
