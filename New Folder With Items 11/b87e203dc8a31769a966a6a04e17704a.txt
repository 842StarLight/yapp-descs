<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Summary</b></h3>
<p></p>
<div>
<p>This National Guard position is for a IT SPECIALIST (SYSADMIN) (TITLE 32), Position Description Number <b>D1617000</b> and is part of the FL JFHQ, National Guard.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
</div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities</b></h3>
<div><p>As an <b>IT Specialist (SR SYSADMIN), GS-2210-12</b>, you will develop, modify, and test overall system backup and recovery strategies for all systems; serve as the technical expert and focal point for installation, configuration and testing of hardware and software associated with multiple operating systems and computer platforms; provide technical assistance and analysis to other IT personnel involved in system design, programming, database design, and adapting commercially available software; determine equipment requirements, communications, and interfaces with other systems; evaluate machine usage based on current usage patterns and develop plans for the acquisition of hardware and software needed to support future IT requirements.<br>
</p></div>

<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Qualifications</b></h3>
<div>
<p><b>AREA OF CONSIDERATION:</b>This position is in the Excepted Federal Civil Service "under the authority of 32 U.S.C. 709", and is open to Category 1 &amp; 3 Only. Category 1: All permanent/indefinite (T32 and T5) technicians employed by the agency WITH dual FL National Guard military membership; Category 3: All current military members of the Florida National Guard.<br>
<br>
<b>PCS: </b>Permanent Change of Station (PCS) MAY be authorized. Authorization for payment of PCS expenses will be granted ONLY after determination is made that payment is in the best interest of the Florida National Guard.<br>
<br>
<b>NOTE #1:</b> This position is categorized as IAT Level II IAW DOD 8570.01. Selectee must obtain required formal training within 6 months of hire date.<br>
<br>
<b>Military Grades:</b> <b>Warrant/Enlisted: CW4 and Below</b><br>
<br>
<b>GENERAL EXPERIENCE</b>: Must have experience, education or training that has provided a basic knowledge of data processing functions and general management principles that enabled the applicant to understand the stages required to automate a work process; experience may have been gained in work such as computer operator or assistant, computer sales representative, program analyst, or other positions that required the use or adaptation of computer programs and systems.<br>
<br>
<b>SPECIALIZED EXPERIENCE:</b><b> </b><b>GS-2210-12</b>, must have at least <b>36 months</b> experience, education, or training that approaches techniques and requirements appropriate to an assigned computer applications area or computer specialty area in an organization; experience planning the sequence of actions necessary to accomplish the assignment where this entailed coordination with others outside the organizational unit and development of project controls; experience that required adaptations of guidelines or precedents to meet the needs of the assignment; experience preparing documentation on cost/benefit studies where is involved summarizing the material and organizing it in a logical fashion; experience in managing the function of the work to be performed; experience which includes leading, directing and assigning work of personnel.</p>
