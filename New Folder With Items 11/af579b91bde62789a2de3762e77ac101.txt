<h2>Senior Vice President, Information Systems</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>
<p>Location:
</p>
<div>Chicago, Illinois</div>
</div>
<div>
<p>Founded in 1978, Levy has pioneered premium sports and entertainment dining and remains one of America’s fastest growing and most critically acclaimed restaurant companies. Named one of the 10 most innovative companies in sports by Fast Company magazine, Levy’s diverse portfolio includes award-winning restaurants, iconic sports and entertainment venues, and convention centers as well as the Grammy Awards, PGA Championship, US Open Tennis Tournament, Kentucky Derby, and NHL, MLB, NBA All-Star Games, and has been named 2015 NHL Retailer of the Year.</p>
<br>
<br>
<p>The Senior Vice President, Information Technology drives the IT vision and strategy to enable achievement of the organizations strategic objectives. They own and develop the IT road-map designed to enable company growth and transformation, partner closely with the executive team to define and implement specific technology strategies including but not limited to our digital, data, marketing, research, programs, supply chain, development, finance, HR and others necessary to meet our strategic imperatives as well as builds, develops, and collaborate with the IT team for long-term success.</p>
<br>
<br>
<p><b>Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Translate business into enterprise technology strategy; establish team goals, objectives and success measures.</li>
<li>Provides direction on what emerging technologies should be introduced, integrated and assimilated within the enterprise to ensure IT capabilities respond to the needs of the business strategy.</li>
<li>Collaborate with executive leadership and business partners to define, develop and prioritize execution of IT strategy</li>
<li>Lead IT related policy development; drive the development of enterprise technology standards, governance processes and performance metrics to ensure IT delivers value.</li>
<li>Oversee large capital investment projects for enterprise-wide IT initiatives to maximize the return on investment</li>
<li>Develop annual expense and capital budget; manage budget and prioritize capital investments.</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Lead the IT team and its business partners in effective execution of IT strategy and road map.</li>
<li>Communicate and provide an on-going scorecard of IT performance against business objectives.</li>
<li>Build strong and transparent relationships with key departments and their leaders across the enterprise, instilling a detailed understanding of their issues, challenges and opportunities across the I.T. team.</li>
<li>Partner with E15 to establish a vision for the integration of data analytics in support of the business</li>
<li>Partner with Finance and our parent company, Compass Group in the development of the IT sourcing strategy and provide oversight for strategic vendor and partner relationship management</li>
<li>Maintain oversight of all IT projects, ensuring that commitments are properly planned, staffed, monitored and reported</li>
<li>Lead change, collaborate, build partnerships and achieve tangible results by engaging and aligning with a wide range of stakeholders</li>
<li>Establish appropriate service levels and the processes to ensure that KPIs are met.</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Establish and enforce IT policies, processes, portfolio management, development standards and methodologies.</li>
<li>Collaborate with the team to develop and implement systems, processes and the workforce capable of achieving the objectives of the business.</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Build a knowledgeable, effective and trusted technology organization combining strong technical competency with effective consulting and customer service skills.</li>
<li>Develop and maintain a plan that provides for succession and continuity in the most critical IT management positions.</li>
<li>Enhances the capabilities of IT by leveraging a multitude of resources, both internally and externally.</li>
<li>Ensure that the organization has strong systems analysis and project management skills, and the ability to execute business-critical projects.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<br>
<br>
<p><b>Competencies</b></p>
<br>
<br>
<p>Focusing on Clients &amp; Consumers</p>
<ul>
<li>Identify and shapes strategic initiatives to improve the service to Partners, Guests and Stakeholders</li>
<li>Ensure an understanding of customer and stakeholder needs is incorporated into strategic thinking</li>
<li>Lead development of new internal services which are strategically important to the business</li>
<li>Use an understanding of sources of competitive advantage to target or support sales activity</li>
<li>Support account management and retention strategies for major clients</li>
<li>Monitor the marketplace to identify opportunities for new business</li>
<li>Work to build relationships with prospective clients and secure an opportunity to bid</li>
</ul>
<p>Developing Winning Teams</p>
<ul>
<li>Manage a diverse mix of activity through a delegated team structure</li>
<li>Empower challenge and support managers to manage their teams</li>
<li>Personally coach and mentor High Potential individuals</li>
<li>Plan and manage resources for the medium term to support growth</li>
<li>Ensure team performance is accurately measured and reported</li>
<li>Acknowledge and celebrates outstanding performance</li>
<li>Drives cross-business team working to build understanding and collaboration</li>
<li>Maintain a positive, winning spirit and role models personal resilience</li>
</ul>
<p>Building Strong Relationships</p>
<ul>
<li>Build strategic networks which add value to the business</li>
<li>Develop influencing strategies which take account of the organization’s culture and dynamics</li>
<li>Use personal credibility and common ground to influence effectively</li>
<li>Demonstrate personal gravitas in dealings with senior stakeholders</li>
<li>Manage complex contract negotiations to balance a range of conflicting objectives</li>
<li>Act as a role model for open, honest and effective communication</li>
</ul>
<p>Seeing the Bigger Picture</p>
<ul>
<li>Demonstrate a broad and detailed understanding of external activity in the Sector or Function</li>
<li>Identify medium and long term trends in external thinking which will shape strategy</li>
<li>Lead the team to think broadly and flexibly about the strategic opportunities and challenges facing the business</li>
<li>Understand the wider Group strategies relevant to own Sector/Function</li>
<li>Show the confidence and courage to challenge and influence wider business strategy</li>
<li>Lead the development of strategies for own Sector or Function to take the business forward</li>
<li>Articulates ‘where we are going’ to build understanding and support</li>
<li>Lead the team to monitor progress against strategy and take action to keep things ‘on track’</li>
<br>
</ul>
<br>
<br>
<p>Innovating &amp; Improving</p>
<ul>
<li>Interpret business information (e.g. consumer insights) to identify strategic opportunities for improvement</li>
<li>Focus major change where it will deliver most business benefit</li>
<li>Manage the load on the business by prioritizing change and cutting out unnecessary activity</li>
<li>Harness operational excellence to shape innovation and change</li>
<li>Use an in depth understanding of change management to deliver major change projects</li>
<li>Encourages individual initiative and flair at all levels in the business</li>
<li>Lead activity aimed at sharing innovation</li>
</ul>
<p>Delivering Results</p>
<ul>
<li>Demonstrate passion and resolve to achieve business objectives</li>
<li>Develop performance frameworks for the business (e.g. KPIs, Scorecards)</li>
<li>Lead the use of data and analytics to evaluate commercial opportunities and risks</li>
<li>Understand the commercial frameworks that operate in own Sector/Function and how these are changing</li>
<li>Lead thinking on innovative new commercial models which help the business to stay ahead of the competition</li>
<li>Make sound commercial decisions, which recognize a range of complex factors and ambiguities</li>
<li>Constantly challenges self and others to improve business performance</li>
</ul>
<p>Experience &amp; Qualifications</p>
<ul>
<li>15 years of technology experience, 10 years’ experience in progressively responsible leadership IT roles, 5 years of experience in a senior leader role.</li>
<li>Bachelor’s degree in related technical/business area required. Master’s degree preferred.</li>
<li>Demonstrated experience and leadership in managing a team of professionals to meet the strategic business needs.</li>
</ul>
