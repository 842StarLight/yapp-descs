Getting the right products at the right price in the right store at the right time is no small task. That's what our merchandising Partners do every day. They bring expertise in the areas of procurement, merchandising, culinary, analysis, pricing, quality assurance, demo programming, and inventory planning.<br>
<br>
As a Business Development Assistant I, you will review, prioritize, track actions, and resolve complex issues in support of H-E-B's Business Development Managers (BDMs).<br>
<br>
Once you're eligible, you'll become an Owner in the company, so we're looking for commitment, hard work, and focus on quality and Customer service. 'Partner-owned' means our most important resources-People-drive the innovation, growth, and success that makes H-E-B The Greatest Retailing Company.<br>
<br>
Do you have a:<br>
HEART FOR PEOPLE... communication, collaboration, and customer service skills?<br>
HEAD FOR BUSINESS... an ability to research and resolve moderately complex issues on your own?<br>
PASSION FOR RESULTS... capability to perform multiple tasks simultaneously in a fast-paced, many-deadline environment?<br>
<br>
We are looking for:<br>
<ul>
<li>a bachelor's degree</li>
<li>experience in business, merchandising, procurement, or store operations</li>
<li>understanding of product information data flow (item, vendor, cost, price)</li>
</ul>
<br>
What is the work?<br>
Administration:<br>
<ul><li>Ensures products are competitively priced, meeting price image and financial objectives as directed by the BDM</li></ul>
- Executes consistent pricing strategy for BDM in communication materials / branding throughout pricing programs, processes, policies, and other H-E-B initiatives<br>
<ul>
<li>Manages data entry of cost and retail relationships to support marketing and merchandising strategies</li>
<li>Utilizes existing templates / processes to track category performance and / or run category reports</li>
<li>Identifies, researches, and resolves simple to moderately complex issues with minimal BDM involvement</li>
<li>Represents BDM in meetings (note taking, presenting updates, clarifying, notifying BDM of priority action steps)</li>
<li>Creates promotional product surveys in tool for promotional events including forecasting, store allocation</li>
<li>Promotional event execution including coupons, PI tracking, and OMS offers</li>
<li>Executes broker's functions when no broker exists (e.g., secure product samples)</li>
<li>Coordinates test sets</li>
<li>Supports BDM</li>
<li>Drafts communications / presentations</li>
<li>Solves minor process problems; implements solutions to routine issues</li>
<li>Develops / coordinates agenda and travel itinerary for key BDM supplier or retail visit trips</li>
<li>Manages new item submission and set up process after items have been selected by Business Development Manager including eCommerce</li>
<li>Performs basic analysis for supplier negotiation</li>
</ul>
<br>
What is your background?<br>
<ul><li>Bachelor's degree in business, marketing, finance, or related field, or comparable experience</li></ul>
- 1 - 3 years of experience in business, merchandising / procurement or store operations<br>

<li>Ability to prioritize and manage workload with multiple deadlines; ability to multi-task</li>
<li>Ability to work on cross-functional teams</li>
<li>Ability and willingness to learn, adapt to, and use new systems and processes</li>
<li>Ability to make good decisions</li>
</ul>
