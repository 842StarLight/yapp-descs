<h2>Project Manager, Sustaining Technology Deployment</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Power Electronics is a Spanish multinational manufacturer of solar inverters (500KW to 3 MW) and heavy industrial equipment for Water, Gas and Mining industries. Founded in 1987, the company is a major force in the global market place with subsidiaries in over 30 countries and over 1,000 employees worldwide. At Power Electronics, we believe in a customer-oriented focus and understand a strong service organization is critical to retain customers. Power Electronics service philosophy is a key element to our continued success.</p>
<p>Power Electronics is currently seeking a Project Manager for Sustaining Technology Deployment to join our team in the United States. The ideal candidate for this position will be have project management experience and be a machinery technology generalist with some knowledge in multiple disciplines, such as, mechanical systems, power, electronics, communications, and controls.</p>
<p>Power Electronics US operations are based in Phoenix, successful candidates will attend training in Phoenix. Power Electronics would prefer the candidate to relocate to work from the Gilbert, AZ office, but Power Electronics would entertain remote employment options for the right candidate.</p>
<p>The Project Manager will be managing the launch and deployment of technology upgrades for the Power Electronics fleet of inverter, storage, and power conversion products. In addition, this person will advise the Sustaining Engineering team in Spain of opportunities for further enhancements. The Project Manager will be coordinating with production and engineering resources in Spain and the USA, field service assets in the USA, and customers to train, plan, and execute the deployment of technology enhancements. This person will be responsible for the timely and accurate execution of quality and technology deployments in the USA; and assist with customer communications and education.</p>
<p>We are seeking hard working, motivated, and well-organized individuals with a focus on continuous improvement to exceed expectations.</p>
<p><b>Primary functions and responsibilities</b></p>
<ul><li>Coordinate an International team comprised of engineering, production, sales, field service, quality, and customer resources to develop work breakdown structures and schedules for the deployment of fleetwide quality and technology upgrades.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Manage deployment costs and scheduling for the planned deployments.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Report progress to Senior Management, project owners, and O&amp;M customers. Manage priority changes and minimize the impact to customers and owners from deviations to planned activities.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Help educate the customers and project stakeholders on the enhancements, the impact to their product performance, and benefits to them for the upgrades.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Develop a detailed knowledge of Power Electronics products and customer needs.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Work with team members to ensure all regulatory, safety, training, and local utility requirements for the deployment process and enhancements are met by the project teams and deployment process.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Identify opportunities for further enhancements and build business cases to qualify the potential benefit for customers and Power Electronics; and report opportunities to Sustaining Engineering.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Other duties may be required or assigned.</li></ul>
<p><b>Skills/Qualifications</b></p>
<ul><li>Former experience (5 years) or formal training plus 1 year of experience in project management.</li></ul>
<ul><li>A bachelor’s degree in an engineering or science discipline. Ideally the formal education will be in power engineering or electrical engineering, but experience in these industries will substitute.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Experience with management software, such as MS Project, Trello, Basecamp, etc. will be required.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Proven success with upward management of customers and Senior Management, customer communication, and managing cross-functional teams in multiple locations is a critical skill.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Familiarity with one-line drawings, three-line drawings, and electrical schematics is a plus.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Knowledge of solar market is an asset.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Ability to travel up to 10-20% of time required for customer and site visits.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Positive ‘can-do’ attitude.</li></ul>
<p><b>Abilities</b></p>
<ul><li>Ability to coordinate the activities of a diverse, multi-national, and multi-disciplinary team.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Ability to communicate effectively with team members at all levels of the company, as well as regularly report progress to customers and project owners.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Ability to adjust timelines and sequencing to manage changing priorities, minimize impacts of plan alterations on the customers and stakeholders, and hold individuals accountable to deliverables.</li></ul>

<ul><li>The physical demands of this position are limited to those found in a normal office environment.</li></ul>
<ul><li>The employee must be able to travel via automobile or commercial travel to off-site locations across the USA, and occasionally travel internationally to coordinate with team members.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Employee must occasionally lift and/or move up to 25 pounds.</li></ul>
