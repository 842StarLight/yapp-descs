<h2>Visitor Services Assistant I - Mill City Museum</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div><div>
<div>
<b>JOB TITLE:</b> #1296 Visitor Services Assistant I – Mill City Museum<br>
<br>
<b>OPEN TO:</b> This job is open to all applicants.<br>
<br>
<b>LOCATION:</b> Mill City Museum, 704 South 2nd St., Mpls, MN 55401<br>
<br>
<b>SALARY:</b> $14.42 hourly minimum<br>
<br>
<b>STATUS &amp; HOURS:</b> Part-time, regular (780 annual hours) position.
<br>
<br>
<b>BENEFITS: </b>Eligible to participate in retirement program with employer contribution.<br>
<br>
<b>CLASSIFICATION:</b> 55L Service<br>
<br>
<b>HIRING MANAGER:</b> Site Manager III, Mill City Museum<br>
<br>
<b>POSTING DATE:</b> August 23, 2019<br>
<br>
<b>DEADLINE DATE:</b> September 6, 2019
<br>
<br>
<b>TO APPLY:</b> Interested applicants must apply online at the Minnesota Historical Society’s career center at www.mnhs.org/jobs and include a resume and cover letter by the application deadline date.
<br>
<br>
<b>DESCRIPTION: </b>The Minnesota Historical Society (MNHS) seeks applicants to provide positive customer service while performing a variety of duties related to admissions, the retail store, and visitor information.<br>
<br>
<b>SUMMARY OF WORK:</b> 1) provide customer service through admission sales, as well as provide visitor information; 2) sell merchandise and provide operational assistance in the museum store; and 3) assist with school programs.<br>
<br>
<b>MINIMUM QUALIFICATIONS:
</b><ul>
<li>High school diploma or equivalent.</li>
<li>One year of cash handling and retail or customer service experience.</li>
<li>Good organizational and personal computer skills.</li>
<li>Demonstrated ability to communicate with a diverse public in a positive and friendly manner, in person and over the phone.</li>
<li>Demonstrated ability to independently problem solve and respond to visitor concerns with patience, empathy, and grace.</li>
<li>Availability to work a flexible schedule that includes weekends, evenings, and holidays, as necessary.</li>
</ul>
<br>
<b>
DESIRABLE QUALIFICATIONS:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>One year experience with ticketing and point of sale software.</li>
<li>Foreign language skills.</li>
<li>Knowledge of retail display and marketing techniques.</li>
<li>Knowledge of Minnesota and area history.</li>
</ul>
