<h2>Area Safety Manager I</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p>Forterra is a leading manufacturer of pipe and products in the U.S. and Eastern Canada for a variety of water-related infrastructure applications, including water transmission, distribution and drainage. The company generated pro forma net sales of $1.3 billion in 2018. Based in Irving, Texas, Forterra employs more than 5500 people and operates more than 85 facilities, with products available throughout the U.S. and Eastern Canada.</p>
<p><b>
ESSENTIAL RESPONSIBILITIES &amp; TASKS</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Assist Regional “We Lead with Safety Initiatives”</li>
<li>
Implement and support all S&amp;H programs, including Training, Inspections/Audits, Industrial Hygiene, implementation of Company S&amp;H Policies, Accident Investigation/Reporting and Regulatory Compliance programs.</li>
<li>
Provide on-going S&amp;H support and guidance to plant management and employees</li>
<li>
Continuous interpretation and compliance planning for current and emerging regulatory requirements</li>
<li>
Work closely with other Area Environmental Managers to ensure a team approach is used to effectively monitor all ESH activities</li>
<li>
Monitor and proactively report S&amp;H performance</li>
<li>
50% - 75% local travel within the assigned area</li>
<li>
5% travel outside assigned area</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
MINIMUM QUALIFICATIONS</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Bachelor of Science Degree in related field or a minimum of 10 years of relevant safety experience.</li>
<li>
Experience and competence in developing and implementing all aspects of an industry leading Safety and Health Program</li>
<li>
Strong management, communication and influencing skills from Senior Management to plant level employees</li>
<li>
Competent in coaching and teaching</li>
<li>
Ability to form, facilitate and maintain internal and cross functional teams and relationships</li>
<li>
Competent in federal, state and local S&amp;H regulations and the regulatory agencies governing the assigned facilities</li>
<li>
Competent in Microsoft Office software including Word and Excel</li>
</ul>
