<h2>Marketing Asst / Social Butterfly!</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><i><b>Are a TRUE people person? Do you have strong &amp; NATURAL social skills?</b></i></p>
<p><i><b>Do you ENJOY a fast-paced team environment with daily challenges, goals and REWARDS? </b></i></p>
<p><i><b>Do you LOVE to meet people? And do THEY love to meet you?</b></i></p>
<p><i><b>Can you FIND a conversation with ANYONE, even in a loud crowded room? </b></i></p>
<p>If you said yes after yes, then you are exactly the right fit to creatively and strategically build alliances and seek out potential clients for our construction services within our new Minneapolis office!</p>
<p>“Building Relationships First”</p>
<p>Yes, we are a construction company, so our business is building things. But it starts with people. Those on our team, our business partners, vendors &amp; affiliates and of course, OUR CLIENTS.</p>
<p>This is where you fit in. You will work closely with our marketing and management team, reporting directly to the Founder &amp; CEO on a day-to-day basis. Your number one goal will be expanding the company message &amp; offerings while building relationships with our clients and business partners.</p>
<p>This opportunity requires a naturally driven personality who thrives in a challenge, all the while being provided the highest-level training to exceed such expectations. 30-plus years of invaluable sales &amp; marketing knowledge will be openly shared with you (some of which in confidentiality) for you to grow and exceed in your own expectations within this role on our growing team!</p>
<p>Your Skills &amp; Talents are:</p>
<ul>
<li>Highly organized</li>
<li>Self-starter / naturally energetic</li>
<li>Thrives in a fast-paced environment, while always exciting, many tasks are related to hard deadlines. Must be proficient at managing stress</li>
<li>Daily meetings notes / project discussions typed into memos &amp; documents to be shared back within our team</li>
<li>Excellent communicator- both written &amp; verbal, strong multi-tasker</li>
<li>Strong phone skills, comfortable with structured prospecting (a step above a “cold call”)</li>
<li>Highly efficient with computer programs, MS Office, setup &amp; customizing Excel, etc.</li>
<li>Strong social media understanding of and knowledge of updating platforms</li>
</ul>
<p>Daily Tasks will include:</p>
<ul>
<li>Phone work, including prospecting calls, managing multiple e-mail accounts</li>
<li>Prospecting Letters with introduction &amp; follow-up phone calls</li>
<li>Data entry, construction management &amp; Infusionsoft CRM software (will train)</li>
<li>Directly responsible to create and report daily marketing reports to management &amp; CEO</li>
<li>Scheduling corporate travel &amp; trade shows, limited travel required with ample notice</li>
<li>Seeking out publicity: PR engagements, media opportunities, community interaction</li>
<li>Working closely in a mission of expanding the company’s “Giving Back” program</li>
<li>Handling the unexpected daily needs of a busy environment in an exciting mission-driven &amp; rapidly growing company</li>
</ul>
<p>-Just to be clear on this point, there will be times opportunities will arise to be accomplished immediately while still maintaining the steady flow of the day-to-day activities / follow-up in play.</p>
<p>This is clearly a position for only the right person, if anything above sounds like a stretch, don't worry- as we will support your growth and teach you fast- so long as you love a challenge and are ready to grow from it head on!</p>
<p>Flexibility is highly valued and offered in your schedule. Ultimately, this is a full-time position while PT / transition to FT schedules are welcomed and open for discussion.</p>
<p>While there are often early morning &amp; after 5pm networking opportunities, in the same we are flexible to your needs as well.</p>
<p>Compensation is not based solely on experience and is generous for the right candidate. Inherit abilities &amp; willingness and openness to learn quickly are strong attributes for this position. The expectation is that of a very specific and honored leadership role within the organization encompassing strong advancement opportunities.</p>
