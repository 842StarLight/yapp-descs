<h2>Senior Coordinator, Meetings and Exhibits</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Senior Coordinator, Meetings and Exhibits</b></p>
<p><b>
Position Summary:</b> The Senior Coordinator, Meetings and Exhibits, assists with all departmental activities relating to meetings and exhibits at NAFSA, including providing timely front-line service responses regarding the NAFSA Annual Conference &amp; Expo. This position supports the conference logistics team in facilitating the successful execution of meetings, exhibits and educational offerings for the association.</p>
<p><b><i>
Major Functions and Responsibilities</i></b></p>
<p><b>
Working with the Associate Director, provide meeting and logistical support to all in-house meetings including Board of Directors meetings, Washington Leadership Meeting, Advocacy Day, departmental committee meetings, and local events in Washington, DC (25%)</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Meeting and logistical support includes reserving meeting/function space, food and beverage/catering arrangements, audio visual setup, on-site room and meal checks throughout meeting, and working closely with hotel/venue staff to ensure a smooth program</li>
<li>Manage staff communications and resources for in-house meetings</li>
<li>Review and update requests for proposals (RFP) for in-house meetings
</li>
<li>Maintain internal meeting request system</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Support all activities related to annual conference logistics (35%)</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Provide consistent answers to daily phone and e-mail inquiries through the conference inbox, based on department guidelines
</li>
<li>Manage visa invitation letter process for annual conference attendees</li>
<li>Travel to conference site two to three times per year for planning purposes, taking an active role as part of planning team</li>
<li>Communicate pertinent conference information to staff through Staff Resource Guide
</li>
<li>Manage signage production and placement with guidance from Senior Director</li>
<li>Partner with Local Area Team (LAT) Communications chair to submit blog posts according to schedule</li>
<li>Work with LAT Special Events chair to arrange campus visits and manage volunteers for special events</li>
<li>Manage conference web pages with Conference Program team</li>
<li>Draft NAFSA news stories for the operations team as needed</li>
<li>Manage the shipping process for annual conference</li>
<li>Review and update RFPs for annual conference and expo
</li>
<li>Manage and produce floor plans for all space at convention center facility, working closely with decorating vendor and Associate Director, to include administrative space, networking spaces, sessions and workshops</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Coordinate and support planning and execution of exhibits management functions (35%)</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Collaborate closely with vendor to manage set up of online system for exhibitors
</li>
<li>Monitor daily exhibits sales in online system to make sure all pieces are accurate and reflected properly</li>
<li>Manage check, credit card and wire transfer payments with accounting</li>
<li>Provide consistent, accurate, and timely exhibitor communication</li>
<li>Work with Exhibits Director on maintaining business practices for successful exposition</li>
<li>Partner with various vendors to manage Exhibitor Service Kit and Lead Retrieval
</li>
<li>Coordinate exhibitor registration with Registration Specialist
</li>
<li>Work with Marketing Services to develop expo sales strategy and communication plan</li>
<li>Research and develop comprehensive resources for exhibitors
</li>
<li>Assist with pre-sales of future years</li>
<li>Work with expo team in producing <i>Navigating the NAFSA Expo</i> brochure</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Assist with general office administration (5%)</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Attend all meetings within logistics and content departments</li>
<li>Prepare letters, memos, and labels to support all departmental work</li>
<li>Provide back-up support to the Membership Frontline Specialist within the rotation of other NAFSA staff members who cover the front reception desk</li>
<li>Assist CFO and Senior Director as required</li>
</ul>
