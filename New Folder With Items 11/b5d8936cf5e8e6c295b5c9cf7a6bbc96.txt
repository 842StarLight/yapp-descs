<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilties</b></h2>
<b>
Job Description</b><br>
<p>At Wells Fargo, we want to satisfy our customers’ financial needs and help them succeed financially. We’re looking for talented people who will put our customers at the center of everything we do. Join our diverse and inclusive team where you’ll feel valued and inspired to contribute your unique skills and experience.</p>
<p>Help us build a better Wells Fargo. It all begins with outstanding talent. It all begins with you.</p>
<p><b>Wells Fargo Technology </b>sets IT strategy; enhances the design, development, and operations of our systems; optimizes the Wells Fargo infrastructure footprint; provides information security; and enables continuous banking access through in-store, online, ATM, and other channels to Wells Fargo’s more than 70 million global customers.</p>
<p>Creates, prepares and implements systems quality assurance reviews for Wells Fargo's image applications, specifically for: Image File Import, WellsImage Viewer and Image Media. Responsible for primary development of testing strategies for image applications. Consults with users, providing advice and direction. Uses independent judgment to perform complex analysis of business requirements and technical system specifications for Image applications. Leads and prioritizes testing activities for projects involving onshore and offshore teams. Executes test plans and scripts to facilitate complex testing activities. Provides specific technical guidance on defects to developers. Gives direction to less experienced analysts.</p>
