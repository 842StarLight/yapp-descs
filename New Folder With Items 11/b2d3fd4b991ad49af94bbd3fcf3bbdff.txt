<h2>Outsourced Product Program Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">SCOPE
<br>Drives business growth within Hennessy by owning the daily working relationship with our Outsourced Product (OSP) partners, executing new product development and sustaining projects on time and on budget. The Program Manager owns process discipline, leading a cross-functional geographically dispersed team, documentation and tracking of all programs including tollgate process compliance, regular cadence of meetings and reviews, reporting, program metrics, counter measures and overall performance. The role requires a person who is comfortable communicating at all levels of the organization, in managing conflict and tradeoffs and is an enabler for cross-functional clarity, communication and execution through followership and driving accountability.
<br><br>
ESSENTIAL FUNCTIONS
<br>Accountable to program sponsors for schedule, budget, and quality of all program elements; escalates decisions to program sponsors as necessary.
<br>Leads the program planning process, tools and schedule development.
<br>Reviews/approves program plans for conformance to program strategy and schedule.
<br>Acts as the communications conduit to sponsors and program steering committee and conducts periodic briefings/status updates. Prepares program status, program risk analyses, and customer VOC collection with a goal to drive issues to closure and assure achievement of program goals and KPIs.
<br>Directs and coordinates resources throughout respective company functions and conducts regular program reviews to make sure that program development and key performance indicators stay on track.
<br>Assures that the program achieves the cost, quality, performance, production rate, and yield metrics defined during the proposal stages.
<br>Leads program through all stages from idea to commercial execution.
<br>Proactively manage changes in project scope, identify potential crises, and devise contingency plans.
<br>Collaborates cross-functionally to establish performance, schedule, cost and quality objectives for internal teams and OSP partners.
<br>Travel up to 25% of the time both nationally and to corporate headquarters as required.
<br>Other duties as assigned.
<br>EDUCATION, SKILLS, AND EXPERIENCE
<br>Bachelor's degree required (business, marketing, engineering or related field desired preferred); PMI/PMP certification preferred.
<br>A minimum of 5+ years of experience developing system level products, which include hardware, firmware and software in a fully integrated system.
<br>A minimum of 5+ years of progressive and broad based strategic development of new business programs and reinvention of current programs, technical marketing, brand and product leadership experience.
<br>Demonstrated experience in leading aggressive and results-driven brand management and product planning strategies utilizing Voice of the Customer (VOC) to identify growth opportunities for the company.
<br>Proven experience in developing long-range strategic goals with the ability to establish competitive strategy based on market intelligence, financials, and technology drivers.
<br>Demonstrated ability to work in a team-oriented, collaborative environment including leadership of geographically dispersed, cross-functional development teams.
<br>Basic understanding of hardware and software development processes, electronics manufacturing, and materials procurement.
<br>Ability to effectively prioritize and execute tasks in a high-pressure environment.
<br>Proven experience in risk management and change management.
