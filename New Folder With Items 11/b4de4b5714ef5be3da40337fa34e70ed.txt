<h2>Director of Clinical Services</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>
<p><b>Interstate Electronics Company</b>, a leading communications systems integrator, seeks a Director of Clinical Services for its Chicago location.</p>
<p>
Founded in 1964, Interstate Electronics Company designs, installs and supports a wide range of technology solutions, including: data networks, audio/visual systems, professional audio systems, video distribution systems, communication systems, paging systems, security systems, control systems, etc.</p>
</div>
<div>
<p>
The Director of Clinical Services is responsible for the successful clinical implementation of Responder hardware/software/integrated solutions and clinical services provided and sold by Interstate Electronics Company. Day-to-day responsibilities include clinical account management, sales support, Responder implementation activities, and clinical service delivery.</p>
<p>
This role also provides general supervision of the Interstate Electronics Company clinical implementation team (Clinical Application Specialist), including staff training/development, scheduling, and providing team appropriate resources to ensure a superior customer experience.</p>
<p>
The Director of Clinical Services is responsible for making consultative recommendations to corporate and hospital-level nursing leaders regarding clinical workflow, informed by evidence-based practice, and the impact of system implementation decisions on the organizations intended outcomes.</p>
<p>
Typical Responder implementations utilize a multi-step approach; assist sales team during discovery and proposal development, conduct the needs analysis for system design, provide the end-user training program, support system go-live, and deliver value-add, outcomes-focused clinical services.</p>
<p>
Performance of this role involves traveling to multiple job sites and demonstrating system use for healthcare executive leadership, super-users and customers (end-users) and engaging with key staff to facilitate these services.</p>
<p>
Employee will maintain complete understanding of the company policies, services, products, administrative procedures, sales strategy, philosophy, and business objectives.</p>
</div>
<ul>
<li>
Train and provide support to clinical implementation team on the use of all systems (education, healthcare, fire alarm) installed by Interstate Electronics Company</li>
<li>Contact customers to arrange meetings/training/services and engage key customer staff</li>
<li>Develop/review all training curriculum – update as necessary to address new system features/applications</li>
<li>Act as resource to help review system configuration plans, confirm facility layout &amp; design of installed systems, program product demonstration units for training</li>
<li>Direct best practice in application of system solutions per manufacturer recommendations</li>
<li>Supervise CAS’s as they work closely with the assigned service and installation technicians to assure proper system configuration per needs assessment</li>
<li>Act as an advisor to the Sales Department to assess customer needs and expectations from systems purchased from Interstate Electronics Company</li>
<li>Presents Interstate Electronics Company image to the community in a positive manner</li>
<li>Provide timely responses to internal and external customer queries</li>
<li>Locate appropriate company resource to assist customers when needed</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>
Documentation of system design decisions, education plan, outcomes engineering clinical services needed and/or provided, etc.</li>
<li>Produce, duplicate, and assemble customized end-user facing materials</li>
<li>Coordinate with Project Management and assign CAS’s to projects based on skillset and experience</li>
<li>Maintain records of hours provided against hours allotted per job</li>
<li>Coordinate installation completion forms with Accounting to assist billing process</li>
<li>Assist with completion of project sign-off process, including the user acceptance testing and warranty initiation</li>
<li>Other Duties as assigned.</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>
RN, experienced* in acute care 10+ years’ preferred (*in order of preference: med-surg, ED, OR, specialty)</li>
<li>Ideally seeking candidate who has held a leadership role within an acute hospital/healthcare environment.</li>
<li>Strong project management experience</li>
<li>Some experience implementing technology in the healthcare environment</li>
<li>General US healthcare market knowledge as it relates to the clinical and operational impact of the evolution of fee-for-service to value-based care</li>
<li>Ideally actively involved in a nursing association and/or clinical advisory board</li>
<li>Experience with the application of Lean/QI in the healthcare environment a plus</li>
<li>Strong classroom training skills; minimum of 2-4 years’ experience. Curriculum design expertise a plus</li>
<li>Ability to work a flexible schedule based on customer need</li>
</ul>
<div>
<div><div>
<div><div><div><div><div>
<ul>
<li>
Excellent verbal and written communications skills. Experience authoring case studies or clinical research papers a plus.</li>
<li>Strong interpersonal skills; ability to work with diverse groups</li>
<li>Experience in developing and preparing custom presentations</li>
<li>Computer literacy at the GUI (Graphical User Interface) operating system level</li>
<li>Knowledge of Microsoft operating systems, windows applications and networking</li>
<li>Ability to manage customer experience with credibility and guide them toward evidence-based, best practices in technology utilization and staff education to achieve their organizational goals</li>
<li>Ability to demonstrate planning and organizational skills which allow the successful completion of a project with multiple stakeholder by a specific due date.</li>
<br>
</ul>
