<h2>Consumer Insights and Reporting Analyst</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p><b>Outstanding career opportunity in highly desirable Sonoma County in Northern California, working with a growing and very progressive organization where you will have visibility with high level stakeholders, up to the Executive Vice President of the organization! This is your chance to make an impact utilizing your business skills, well-developed communication skills, and your technical ability!</b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
We are looking for a Consumer Insights / Reporting Analyst, Data Analyst, or Business Analyst with intermediate to advanced experience with an enterprise reporting platform (Power BI preferred) and a solid understanding and experience creating database-driven report templates and dashboards, as well as modifying existing report templates and dashboards... an individual who possesses a strong work ethic and commitment to quality. Most importantly, we are seeking a polished individual that has good business experience and excellent communication skills, with a comfort level working with VP level staff.</b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Reporting to the Director of Business and Technology Solutions, this is a great opportunity if you're interested in working in a team environment chartered to shape and design a new enterprise analytics platform.</b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b><i>
Overview / Position Summary:</i></b></p>
<p>
Awesome opportunity to work at a successful company known for being innovative and focused on quality. The Consumer Insights and Reporting Analyst will capture, manage, analyze, and apply customer knowledge to help the organization win, support and retain customers. To succeed, this person must capture customer data from all sources and manage it across customer-facing systems and functions as well as analyze it to obtain actionable insights and apply it to the business. A successful candidate will have proven experience communicating directly with senior leadership along with experience producing reporting, dashboards and visualizing insights; expertise in pattern mining and business analytics. This role requires both a broad knowledge of existing data mining and processing along with the creativity to invent and customize as needed.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b><i>
What You'll Need to Be Successful:</i></b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelors' degree required.</li>
<li>5 - 10 years of exemplary experience in a Business Analyst, Data Analyst or Consumer Insights / Reporting Analyst role.</li>
<li>Experience and strong skills on the business side, first, technical skills second. You should be someone with a strong ability to communicate with key business stakeholders, including up to EVP levels, as this is critical in this role.</li>
<li>CPG (Consumer Packaged Goods) experience preferred, but not required. Preference for someone with experience in Sales &amp; Marketing, IT, Retail, Finance, Consumer Packaged Goods, Food &amp; Beverage, or Banking.</li>
<li>Intermediate to advanced experience with an enterprise reporting platform (Power BI preferred).</li>
<li>Solid understanding and experience creating database-driven report templates and dashboards, as well as modifying existing report templates and dashboards.</li>
<li>Willingness to produce reports on a regular weekly, monthly, quarterly, or annual basis ultimately utilized by leaders at all levels.</li>
<li>Desire to take proactive ownership of the accuracy and timeliness of reported data.</li>
<li>Desire to work with colleagues to design the most effective data visualizations in reports and dashboards that identify trends and unlock key insights.</li>
<li>Familiarity with databases and basic programming skills in Structured Query Language (SQL).</li>
<li>Understanding of statistical concepts such as significance testing, weighting, Z-Test, etc., is preferred.</li>
<li>Comfortable working with modern data technologies and familiarity with database modeling and data warehousing principles.</li>
<li>Ability to operate comfortably and effectively in a fast-paced, highly cross-functional, rapidly changing environment.</li>
<li>Ability to work effectively with data, BI developers and consumer research and insights partners to meet the data needs of the business, translating business needs into analytical requirements.</li>
<li>Experience engaging with the business and leadership on problem definition.</li>
<li>Ability to communicate the results of analyses in a clear and effective manner with product and leadership teams to influence the overall strategy of the product.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p>
</p>
<p><b><i>What You'll Be Doing:</i></b></p>
<ul>
<li>Responsible for business analytics work through all phases, including data quality, data analysis, data visualization, and presentation of results and deliverables.</li>
<li>Take ownership of both recurring / standardized reporting as well as ad hoc projects and requests.</li>
<li>Lead the analysis of transactional and customer data, including cross-tabulations and statistical analysis, provide insights into purchasing patterns, develop hypotheses about trends, and translate results into actionable recommendations for business partners.</li>
<li>Perform independent data mining and database research to identify customer opportunities.</li>
<li>Quantify the impact of DTC sales and marketing initiatives on customer success and future behavior.</li>
<li>Develop reusable analytics and assets working closely with the Data Warehouse/BI team to ensure scalability of analytical outputs.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p>
</p>
<p><b>Local candidates preferred, but hiring company is very open to providing relocation assistance for a well-qualified candidate.</b></p>
