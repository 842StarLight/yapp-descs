<p><b>
REQUIREMENTS: TECHNICAL SUPPORT ADVISOR</b></p>
<ul>
<li>2+ years working in a technical contact center environment</li>
<li>Must have practical professional electrical experience.</li>
<li>Able to pass a drug screen and background check.</li>
<li>Refrigeration experience is a big plus</li>
<li>Being Bi-Lingual is a big plus</li>
<li>Able to work in an office environment.</li>
<li>Able to take inbound phone calls and make outbound phone calls to customers</li>
<li>High mechanical ability, good working knowledge of electrical theory, water treatment best practices and hydraulic systems preferred.</li>
<li>Mechanical — Knowledge of machines and tools, including their designs, uses, repair, and maintenance</li>
<li>Good soft skills</li>
<li>Knowledge of principles and processes for providing customer service. This includes customer needs assessment, meeting quality standards for services, and evaluation of customer satisfaction.</li>
<li>Reasoning Ability: Very High Skills - Ability to define problems, collect data, establish facts, and draw valid conclusions. Ability to interpret an extensive variety of technical instructions in mathematical or diagram form and deal with several abstract and concrete variables.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>OVERVIEW: MECHANICAL TECHNICAL SUPPORT ADVISOR</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Responsible for the ongoing maintenance of internal and external service and support documentation and resources for relevant equipment lines.</li>
<li>Plays a key role in the distribution of and adherence to the current product specifications, providing relevant guidance and corresponding documentation, especially in key account scenarios.</li>
<li>Responsible for the quality and execution of technical phone assistance for the equipment.</li>
<li>Completes projects and tasks to ensure the implementation and efficacy of after-sales support with regard to best practices, preventive maintenance programs, parts stock recommendations, and field service activities.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>RESPONSIBILITIES: MECHANICAL TECHNICAL SUPPORT ADVISOR</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Communicates highly technical information to service personnel.</li>
<li>Maintain high-level customer satisfaction by clarifying customer needs and ensuring they are met.</li>
<li>Provide technical assistance to answer questions or resolve problems for customers and technicians over the phone.</li>
<li>Maintain a mindset of continuous improvement in terms of customer satisfaction and the development of resources for field support.</li>
<li>Work to ensure that the product know-how (technical competence) of Franke products is transferred and understood by internal and external service partners.</li>
<li>In collaboration with relevant engineering and support teams, provide and/or release relevant direction and documentation to internal service departments as updates to current and past equipment are made available.</li>
<li>Work to facilitate customer resolution for escalated issues and engage the necessary internal and external stakeholders to find a resolution that meets the customer’s satisfaction.</li>
<li>Recommends changes to products or services to fulfill customer and service provider needs.</li>
<li>Collect and communicate a repetitive failure analysis to aid in the quality improvement process.</li>
<li>Collaborate with Technical Product Support team to implement and modify equipment maintenance programs, in cooperation with relevant engineering and support teams, that meet the cost and reliability needs of the particular market.</li>
<li>Develop relevant relationships with operational and technical department personnel of key customers.</li>
<li>Assist in technical troubleshooting questions in relation to service activities.</li>
<li>Provide part and sub-system identification and assist in the placement of parts orders as necessary.</li>
<li>Distribute technical bulletins and resources as field scenarios dictate.</li>
<li>Verify warranty status.</li>
<li>Assist in quoting of large scale extensive repair.</li>
<li>Document details of calls taken to maintain an accurate customer and equipment service history.</li>
<li>Dispatch Service Providers as necessary.</li>
<li>Handle and resolve more complex customer requests or complaints when necessary.</li>
</ul>
