<h2>Capacity Procurement Representative - Fort Myers</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>R+L Global Logistics</b> is seeking a <b>Capacity Procurement Representative </b>to join our team<b>.</b> The Capacity Procurement Representative is responsible for track and trace functions on freight and is an entry level role great for those interested in starting a career in logistics.</p>
<p><b>
Capacity Procurement Representative </b><b>Summary of Key Responsibilities</b></p>
<p><b>
Responsibilities and essential job functions include, but are not limited to the following:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Make outbound calls on loads that have a check call set in the system to obtain the status of the load.</li>
<li>
Document the status of all loads with the results of the calls made into transportation management software</li>
<li>
Maintain contact with carriers and internal business partners to ensure the shipper and consignee are aware of any challenges or issues that may arise</li>
<li>
Escalate any issues that are abnormal to the proper CPE or Supervisor on duty.</li>
<li>
Provide professional and courteous customer care to R+L Global's carrier base.</li>
<li>
Work with partner carriers to determine the status of loads and facilitate on-time delivery of orders</li>
<li>
Monitor email on an ongoing basis for communications from the CPE team regarding order status as well as communications from the carrier regarding load</li>
<li>
Support the strategic objectives of the business</li>
<li>
Perform other duties as assigned by Leadership</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Capacity Procurement Representative </b><b>Knowledge, Skills, and Abilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Have the drive, assertiveness, passion, and dedication to succeed in the logistics industry</li>
<li>
Ability to work independently while supporting an overall team mentality</li>
<li>
Ability to multitask in a fast paced environment with rapidly changing priorities</li>
<li>
Ability to work in a blended role of operations and sales in an office environment</li>
<li>
Manage complex transactional or emotional customer situations promptly and professionally</li>
<li>
Ability to handle and prioritize high call volumes and customers inquiries</li>
<li>
Ability to read, write, and speak English fluently</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Capacity Procurement Representative </b><b>Qualifications</b></p>
<p><b>
Summary of </b><b>Capacity Procurement Representative Ex</b><b>perience and Education</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
High school Diploma or GED</li>
<li>
Basic transportation or logistics knowledge is a plus</li>
<li>
Basic knowledge of the following modes a plus: dry van, expedited, flatbed, oversized loads, refrigerated and intermodal</li>
</ul>
