<h2>Restaurant Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">Run a successful restaurant!
<br><br>
Kigo Asian Kitchen serves Wok-Fired Eats—Asian inspired bowls, made to order in our growing chain of Seattle restaurants. We take overwhelming pride in our food and in our team, creating a thriving culture. At Kigo, we believe that happy people create productive, positive work environments, which is why we provide our team with the tools to reach their full potential both in life and work. We offer competitive pay and benefits, equaling or exceeding that of the largest fast casual restaurants in Seattle.
<br><br>
As a restaurant manager you will...
<br>Oversee and coordinate the planning, organizing, training and leadership necessary to achieve restaurant success in sales, costs, employee retention, guest service and satisfaction, food quality, cleanliness and sanitation.
<br><br>
Customer Service and Teamwork
<br> Hold self and team accountable to create an environment where all customers feel welcome and are given responsive, friendly and courteous service that exceeds expectations.
<br> Develop and carry out plans to continuously improve store performance against company objectives in sales, service, quality, appearance of facility, sanitation and cleanliness, and store profitability.
<br> Create a positive, productive work environment that encourages problem solving and engages team members in continuous improvement. Model professionalism. . Maintain respectful, professional relationships and effectively communicate across diverse audiences.
<br> Hire, train and develop employees in accordance with Kigo guidelines. Provide ongoing feedback and coaching to staff and ensure that performance appraisals are completed in a timely manner.
<br> Administer prompt, fair and consistent corrective action, up to termination of employment, for any and all violations of company policies, rules and procedures, in line with Kigo standards and policies regarding personnel.
<br> Implement restaurant marketing, advertising, promotional activities and campaigns as directed.
<br><br>
Process and Procedure
<br> Ensure store operations comply with all Kigo policies, procedures, standards, specifications, guidelines and training programs and food products are prepared and served according to Kigo standards.
<br> Ensure that all team members comply with cash handling and reconciliation procedures, as well as other safety and security procedures. Tasks action to ensure the safety and security of all team members, customers and company assets.
<br> Monitor product inventory and store orders to improve store profitability ensuring that products are available at all times; food cost and waste align with company targets.
<br> Schedule labor as required by anticipated business activity and as needed, while maintaining labor cost aligned with company target. Anticipate and monitor store staffing to ensure business needs are met.
<br> Prepare all required paperwork, including forms, reports and schedules in an organized and timely manner.Comply with all federal, state, county and municipal regulations that pertain to health, safety and labor requirements of the restaurant, employees and guests.
<br><br>
Qualifications
<br> Be 21 years of age.
<br> Reliable and dependable.
<br><br>
 Be able to communicate and understand the predominant language(s) of the restaurant’s trading area.
<br> Have knowledge of service and food and beverage, generally involving at least three years of front-of-the-house operations and/or assistant management positions.
<br> Possess excellent basic math skills and have the ability to operate a cash register or POS system.
<br> Be able to work in a standing position for long periods of time (up to 5 hours).
<br> Be able to reach, bend, stoop and frequently lift up to 50 pounds.
<br> Must have the stamina to work 50 to 60 hours per week.</div>
