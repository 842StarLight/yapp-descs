<h2>Associate Director, Business Development</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>General Summary
</b></p>
<p>The Associate Director, Business Development is responsible for the management of external opportunities with sponsor/CRO and the assessment of customers through direct and indirect interaction. This position focuses on the promotion, growth and development of market share in a competitive life science industry. This position is responsible for providing oversight and tactical direction to the Director, Business Development in providing customer service and building client loyalty.</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Principal Duties &amp; Responsibilities</b></p>
<p></p>
<p>Management of External Opportunities</p>
<ul>
<li>Identify and develop new business relationships within targeted organizations</li>
<li>Penetrate high revenue pharmaceutical and clinical research organizations (CROs) resulting in increased protocol and site volume for Advarra</li>
<li>Provide the vision and necessary hands-on leadership to take advantage of the company’s growth opportunities</li>
<li>Partner with other Advarra business units such as IBC and Consulting to ensure penetration of Advarra’s services into our customers</li>
<li>Utilize Salesforce to manage accounts, opportunities, contacts and all corresponding activities</li>
<li>Facilitate contract negotiation process between Advarra and the client</li>
<li>Maintenance of CRM tool (Salesforce)</li>
</ul>
<p>Assessment of Customers</p>
<ul>
<li>Portfolio planning and review: Working directly with customers and prospects to understand product service gaps and then drive solutions through business development activities, towards completing the appropriate transaction</li>
<li>Provide feedback and guidance to internal marketing groups in the development of sponsors and CRO focused business development tools</li>
</ul>
<p>Business/Strategic Planning</p>
<ul>
<li>Participate in the annual and quarterly business planning process and frequent strategic discussions with cross-functional teams</li>
<li>Other duties will include working with key internal parties to capitalize on existing technologies and processes in evaluating strategic fit and potential for operational synergy</li>
<li>Work with other Business Development Executives and key internal decision makers to identify strategic target organizations which contribute to overall growth</li>
<li>Identify, recommend, and implement new ideas through technology or process changes to continuously improve service delivery</li>
<li>Maintain productive and collaborative working relationship to resolve interdepartmental issues in a timely manner</li>
<li>Maintain accessibility and availability for staff to bring questions or complaints</li>
</ul>
<p>Other</p>
<p>Position requires domestic travel up to 50% of the time</p>
<p></p>

</li></ul>

</li></ul>

</li>

</li>
