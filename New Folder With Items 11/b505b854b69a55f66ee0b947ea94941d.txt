<h2>Senior Digital Sales Lead - Bismarck</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">Townsquare Media
<br><br>
<b>Townsquare Media (NYSE:</b> TSQ) is seeking a seasoned Sales Executive, with a true "hunter" mentality to bring our full suite of cross - platform Digital Media Solutions to market. This front-line seller will be responsible for building out new revenue channels at the local level to both agencies and direct clients. We're looking for a domain expert with proven success at selling Advanced Audience Targeting and Extended Reach Digital solutions as well as superior knowledge of new/emerging digital advertising technologies and techniques.
<br><br>
The ideal candidate has an entrepreneurial spirit, who works with a sense urgency and passion, and thrives in a fast paced, competitive sales environment. The Digital Sales Executive will be a key contributor to defining sales strategy, offering, programming, packaging and collateral. This is an ideal role for an experienced, solutions-based seller who is comfortable building new lists, prospecting and closing new business.
<br><br>
Core Responsibilities
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Build out new revenue channels at Regional and Local Key Account levels</li>
<li>Create, implement &amp; execute on a Sales Territory/Account Plan to drive direct and agency sales efforts, with a focus on Advance Audience Targeting and Extended Reach Digital solutions</li>
<li>Work independently from, and long-side, Local cross-platform Sellers in individual markets</li>
<li>Perform extensive needs assessments with prospects (acquisition), and existing customers (cross sell) to determine how TSQ Solutions offer the best results for your customers</li>
<li>Deliver compelling presentations that highlight the TSQ competitive advantages</li>
<li>Develop needs-based solution proposals</li>
<li>Identify, organize and focus internal resources (pre-sales and post sales) that are required to affect close of new sales opportunities</li>
<li>Meet and Exceed Revenue Targets</li>
<li>Thrive in a business development role which includes prospecting within a defined geography as well as expanding, thru cross-sell, existing client relationships</li>
<li>Be successful with cold calling, prospecting and scheduling your own appointments as this is a front-line seller role focused on Local Agencies and Direct Clients</li>
<li>Research opportunities thoroughly and proactively, identifying an advertiser's needs while working with Client Services to propose efficient solutions</li>
<li>Expert level understanding of exchange platform and advanced audience targeting capabilities and their value proposition for customers to drive business unit growth and revenue</li>
<li>Establish strong relationships with advertising agency and client-direct personnel, including all decision-makers and key influencers from junior media planners to top management</li>
<li>Act as a digital media mentor and resource to our client base and general sales staff</li>
<li>Become a positive and influential presence in your region</li>
</ul>
Qualifications
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Minimum 2 years sales experience as an individual contributor in a sales (hunter) role</li>
<li>Digital Sales background (i.e. mobile, video, display, social, email and paid search)</li>
<li>Proven experience selling Advanced Audience Targeting, Extended Reach, and Programmatic offerings</li>
<li>Consultative/Solutions selling experience vs. a single Product selling background</li>
<li>Proven track record in meeting/exceeding monthly, quarterly and annual revenue goals</li>
<li>Consistent performer and responder to daily, weekly and monthly Sales KPI's</li>
<li>Demonstrable experience at new market and revenue development</li>
<li>Previous Radio, Events and Sponsorship sales experience a huge PLUS</li>
<li>Deep contacts with Regional Agencies and Direct Clients with focus on Healthcare Services, Retail, Travel/Tourism, Automotive, Energy and Government</li>
<li>Independent, Entrepreneurial, comfortable in a fast pace, early stage environment</li>
<li>Detail-oriented, someone who rolls up their sleeves and gets the job done</li>
<li>Intellectual curiosity to surface insights &amp; implications and use this knowledge to think creatively about how to advance TSQ offerings</li>
<li>Exceptional communication skills with the ability to clearly articulate compelling value propositions for TSQ's Digital Solutions</li>
<li>Excellent verbal and written communication skills</li>
<li>Superior knowledge of new/emerging digital advertising technologies and techniques</li>
<li>entrepreneurial spirit, a self-starter, who works with urgency and passion</li>
<li>Bachelor's Degree or equivalent experience is required, Advanced degree a plus</li>
</ul>
