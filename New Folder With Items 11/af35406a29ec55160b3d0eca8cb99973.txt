<h2>Senior Business Systems Analyst, Post Sales (Salesforce Service Cloud)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>
<div>8x8, Inc. (NYSE:EGHT) communication solutions help businesses transform their customer and employee experience. With one system of engagement for cloud voice, video, collaboration and contact center and one system of intelligence on one cloud communications platform, businesses can now communicate faster and smarter to exceed the speed of customer expectations. For additional information, visit www.8x8.com, or follow 8x8 on LinkedIn, Twitter, and Facebook.</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
We’re looking for a “solution-minded” Senior Business Systems Analyst that will be responsible for delivering the company’s most important projects to drive our strong top-line growth. The main responsibility of this position will be to partner directly with our business leaders and key stakeholders to implement new enhancements in <b>Salesforce Service Cloud </b>to support and automate our processes.</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
This is a challenging role, but you will get to own the key business processes that will ensure we meet our aggressive growth goals. We want our customers to buy our products seamlessly while ensuring the proper provisioning, customer support, accounting and billing is taking place.</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
Questions you will tackle head on:</div>
<div>
“How do we bring more automation to the customer onboarding experience (post sales)”</div>
<div>
“How do we get our customers set up and using 8x8 product within minutes?”</div>
<div>
“How can our support agents resolve customer trouble tickets faster?”</div>
<div>
“What applications can we leverage to make processes more pleasant for internal agents and our customers?”</div>
</div>
<div><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities</b></h3>
<div><ul>
<li>
Manage roadmap: Meet with decision makers, systems owners, and end users to define business requirements, and systems goals</li>
<li>
Facilitate discussions among business owners to determine the best solution to roadmap of enhancements through working sessions, new process mapping, and process redefinition</li>
<li>
Document business process flows and requirements for the roadmap of enhancements</li>
<li>
Lead the delivery of key enhancements by directly making configuration changes in <b>Salesforce</b> or Developing the requirements / design specifications and delivering it to our internal development team for completion</li>
<li>
Assist in creating reports and dashboards for users</li>
<li>
Identify test criteria and test new functionality/enhancements</li>
<li>
Provide training to end users</li>
<li>
Evaluate implementation options/vendors and provide recommendation on scope and scale of effort required to develop solution.</li>
</ul></div>
</div></div>
<div><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Required Skills</b></h3>
<div><ul>
<li>
Degree in Computer Science, Business Process, MIS or comparable technical degree</li>
<li>
Minimum of 7 to 10 years experience as a business systems analyst on the Salesforce platform</li>
<li>
Knowledge of Boomi a big plus but not required</li>
<li>
Strong knowledge of system and software quality assurance best practices and methodologies</li>
<li>
Flexible and adaptable in regards to learning and understanding new technologies</li>
<li>
Excellent project delivery skills</li>
<li>
Excellent written and oral communication skills</li>
<li>
Excellent interpersonal skills</li>
<li>
Excellent problem-solving, analytical and investigative skills</li>
<li>
Ability to conduct research into software-related issues and products</li>
<li>
Highly self-motivated and directed</li>
<li>
Keen attention to detail</li>
<li>
Proven analytical and problem-solving abilities</li>
<li>
Ability to effectively prioritize and execute tasks in a high-pressure environment</li>
</ul></div>
