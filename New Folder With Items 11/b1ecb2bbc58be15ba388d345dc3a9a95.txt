<p><b>Who We Are</b></p>
<p>
At Equify, there’s more to us. Whether commercial equipment lending, auctions, insurance or asset services; it all means the same thing – relationships. Our customers are not a number, they are a name, a family member, a friend. In the good times and in the difficult times we treat them all the same – exactly how we want to be treated. An individual or a business should never be measured solely on the basis of a number or group of numbers. We believe that business is not just about profit – it’s personal too.</p>
<p>
At Equify, we value the people that make our business grow: our employees and our customers.</p>
<p>
We are Equify Auctions, LLC and we are based in Fort Worth, Texas, USA. Our job is to develop mutually beneficial partnerships, and our specialty is offering auctions for construction, energy service, and transportation equipment. Our core values include: Integrity, Service, Listening, Passion, and Engagement.</p>
<p><b>
Primary Duties</b></p>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Place values on diverse equipment and other related assets for potential sale.</li>
<li>Ensure equipment valuations meet industry expectations.</li>
<li>Conduct market research on equipment valuations by region, as well as global market. Compare competitor results with internal results, utilizing all tools available to you for current and historical data.</li>
<li>Stay on top of market trends and fluctuations.</li>
<li>Review inspections as completed with field inspectors to confirm quality control standards have been achieved to provide accurate values related to equipment.</li>
<li>Work with sales team on equipment values and other related assets.</li>
<li>Some travel will be required to value equipment and train standards with sales team.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Requirements:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s Degree preferred.</li>
<li>A minimum of three years of industry-related skill, knowledge, or experience is preferred.</li>
<li>Have well developed written, telephone and personal presentation skills.</li>
<li>Critical Thinking — using logic and reasoning to identify the strengths and weaknesses of alternative solutions, conclusions or approaches to problems.</li>
<li>Judgment and Decision Making — Considering the relative costs and benefits of potential actions to choose or recommend the most appropriate one.</li>
<li>Complex Problem Solving — identifying complex problems and reviewing related information to develop and evaluate options and implement solutions.</li>
<li>Safeguarding confidential and protected information covered by privacy law and company policy.</li>
<li>Must work independently and as a member of a team</li>
<li>Must be self-motivated and self-disciplined.</li>
<li>Occasional travel will be required to value equipment or assist in development of Sales staff.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Mental and Physical Requirements:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Able to read, speak and write English.</li>
<li>Ability to read, interpret and present recommended equipment valuations.</li>
<li>Gripping, reaching, writing, data entry, handling (paper, files, etc)</li>
<li>Willing to learn and utilize company software.</li>
<li>Able to work under stressful conditions at times.</li>
<li>Communication - phone communication, communication with personnel, customers and vendors</li>
<li>Vision - able to see the computer screen and drive safely</li>
<li>Position will require a minimum of 40 hours per week unless otherwise directed by supervisor.</li>
<li>Have current driver’s license, access to late model automobile in full operating condition and cell phone.</li>
</ul>
