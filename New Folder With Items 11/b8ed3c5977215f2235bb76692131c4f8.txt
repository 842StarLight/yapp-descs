<h2>Inside Sales (SaaS)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>B2B SaaS company is looking for a Sales Development Rep with a passion for prospecting, qualifying outbound leads, and employing creative tactics to overcome objections and set meetings for our account executives.</p>
<p>You will have click-to-dial, local number dialing, recorded voicemails, automated email sequences, and access to the CRM contact database all in your arsenal, but ultimately what will make you successful is your dedication to relentlessly pursuing the win.</p>
<p><b>Responsibilities: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Make 75+ purposeful, outbound dials per day and send targeted emails to prospects to initiate and nurture meaningful business relationships and to uncover new opportunities</li>
<li>Conduct pre-call research to gather company and contact intelligence</li>
<li>Coordinate and participate in qualified product demos</li>
<li>Develop and nurture lead lists</li>
<li>Work closely with the marketing department to support marketing campaign efforts in conjunction with your own sourcing of leads.</li>
<li>Work with Sales Leadership and other critical company resources to meet or exceed company revenue goals</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Qualifications: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>1 year of software sales experience, preferably in B2B technology</li>
<li>High energy - High professionalism</li>
<li>Intensely goal-oriented and motivated by a strong compensation plan</li>
<li>Proven track record</li>
<li>Excellent written and communication skills</li>
<li>Familiarity with CRM platforms or related software - HubSpot or Salesforce preferred</li>
</ul>
