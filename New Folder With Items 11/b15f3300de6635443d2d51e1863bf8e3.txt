<p><b>
ABOUT VENTURE CAFÉ CAMBRIDGE + THE VENTURE CAFÉ FOUNDATION:</b></p>
<p>
Venture Café Cambridge is a social platform that enables relationship-building that is critical for innovation to happen. We educate and connect local entrepreneurs, investors, and enterprises to catalyze innovation and entrepreneurship in the Greater Boston area. Best known for its flagship Café Night event held weekly on Thursday, Venture Café Cambridge is an essential stop for entrepreneurs and innovators who have open access to office hours, workshops, startup demos, information tables and a variety of networking opportunities every week.</p>
<p>
Founded in 2010 in Cambridge, MA, Venture Café has now grown into a global network of sites designed to forge relationships within innovation communities around the world. Venture Café Kendall is run by the Venture Café Foundation, a 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization focused on entrepreneurship and innovation-based economic development, with a mission that every person with an idea should feel empowered to become a successful entrepreneur. The Venture Café Foundation operates District Hall, the Roxbury Innovation Center and Starthub.org.</p>
<p>
Find out more information at <b>www.vencaf.org</b> and <b>venturecafefoundation.org</b></p>
