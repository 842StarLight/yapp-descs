<h2>SharePoint Web Designer*</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<div><div><div>
<div>DMI (Digital Management, LLC.) is looking for a <b>SharePoint Web Designer </b>to help support a government customer on a program. The candidate must have experience with website design, development, testing, management and support to developing end-to-end SharePoint solutions using repeatable methodologies. The role will perform all aspects of the development tasks including front-end development. The role will utilize in-depth understanding of the capabilities of SharePoint (including SharePoint templates, web parts development and custom workflow) to integrate enterprise-wide SharePoint solutions within a dynamic customer environment. The role requires a disciplined candidate with experience re-engineering and/or reverse engineering customized SharePoint 2010 and 2013 applications. The candidate will provide expert web content development services for existing and future our client's own web content on current and future platforms, environments, and networks to all users.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div><b>
Duties and Responsibilities:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
Troubleshoot and resolve web content issues within one working day.</li>
<li>
Provide basic knowledge of web technologies.</li>
<li>
Create, modify, and delete scripting and programming languages including, but not limited to, CSS, HTML, and JavaScript.</li>
<li>
Create, modify, and delete images or graphics including, but not limited to, BMP, GIF, JPEG/JPG, and PNG.</li>
<li>
Create, modify, and delete SharePoint objects including, but not limited to: Announcements, Asset Libraries, Calendars, Custom Lists, Discussion Boards, Document Libraries, Form Libraries, Issues, Links, Lists, Pages, Picture Libraries, Project Tasks, Surveys, Tasks. Must have experience in IDEs that support both SharePoint and Salesforce.</li>
<li>
Create and modify secure basic configurations, workflows and dashboards using SalesForce, ServiceNow or similar No-Code/Low Code web based environments.</li>
<li>
Provide basic analytics and monitoring web performance</li>
</ul>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Qualifications
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div><b>Education and Years of Experience:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>

<li>
5 years of advance experience with designing and developing Web sites using HTML5, CSS, JQuery and JavaScript</li>
