<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"></h2>
<p><b>PRIMARY PURPOSE OF THE POSITION
</b></p>
<p><b>The Client Engagement Associate will provide education and/or advice to T. Rowe Price’s Core Personal Investors or Terminated Workplace Participants. The primary objectives of the Client Development Client Engagement Associate is to drive Net New flows, Retain assets, and/or Refer opportunities to Individual Investors or RPS. The Client Engagement Associate will establish relationships with Core Personal Investors or Workplace Participants in an effort to understand their broader investment planning needs, distribution options, and the potential for consolidation, resulting in high client satisfaction as well as retention of assets and relationships to the firm. Client Engagement Associates serving in this capacity require strong financial acumen, and the ability to manage multiple clients in different stages of the buying process.
</b></p>
<p></p>
<p><b>PRINCIPAL RESPONSIBILITIES
</b></p>
<p><b>Takes inbound calls from, or places outbound calls to, Core investors or Workplace Participants (lower dollar thresholds than Financial Consultants) through direct call routing, appointment scheduling, or referrals from other associates. Using our Consultative framework, the Client Engagement associate educates investors/participants so they can make informed decisions related to their financial plan or 401k all in an effort to move clients to action of consolidating additional monies at TRP or retaining their assets.
</b></p>
<p></p>
<p><b>List of specific tasks includes:

</b></li>

</b></li>

</b></li>

</b></li>

</b></li>

</b></li>
<li><b>Serve as a single point of contact and assist investors/participants through challenging transitions or complex circumstances; is able to effectively drive investors/participants through decisions with education or advice
