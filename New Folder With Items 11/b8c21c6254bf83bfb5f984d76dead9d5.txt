<div><div><div>Senior Manager of Business Systems Transformation – Finance - Record to Report<br>
<br>
We’re looking for a Senior Manager of Business Systems Transformation - Finance who brings technical, process and strategic leadership as well as a service-oriented mindset in pursuit of bringing business value to our internal stakeholders and customers. In addition to leading a team of product owners that supports our core business functions in Finance, the individual will partner with the revenue &amp; reporting organization and IT team to create a digital roadmap which delivers state of the art revenue and reporting digital capabilities in support of our subscription business. Working with multi-functional PTC teams, you will bring expertise in defining and delivering enterprise process and systems that transform the PTC ecosystem. Your ability to work effectively with a cross-functional team, that crosses the organization, and is geographically distributed will make a deep impact at all levels of the company.<br>
<br>
PTC is undergoing a transformation to transition our business into a best in class, cloud-based subscription software company. The Business Systems and Analysis group is responsible for driving the transformation related to core business systems in Finance. Specifically, we are undertaking a modernization of our Record to Report platform in support of the transformation. If you’re an individual who is zealous about the digital age enabling an organization’s business capabilities and you are eager to have a meaningful impact on people, process and systems in Finance, then this could be the role for you!<br>
<br>
Day-To-Day:<br>
<div>
<div>
Attract, develop, and lead a team of Product Owners who drive the definition, planning, and implementation of our core reporting business systems in Finance</div>
<div>
Own the delivery and management of the prioritized roadmap and governance approved technology investments for the Record to Report business capabilities. Ensure that it aligns with the overall corporate direction and company vision.</div>
<div>
Be the champion and voice of our internal customers and have impactful relationships with business partners.</div>
<div>
Collaborate across teams. We win or lose as a team. Play a critical role as a liaison in creating alignment between IT teams and business partners. A collaborative attitude and ability to develop win/win solutions is essential to the job</div>
<div>
Serve as a “trusted advisor,” and function as a key point of contact for IT and business stakeholders and helps generate the system roadmap for stakeholders</div>
<div>
Provide thought leadership to business stakeholders in understanding emerging technologies.</div>
<div>
Measure success. Define what success means for each business solution and each capability before it goes to development. Define and set expectations for IT and business teams, holding them accountable to success and failures.</div>
<div>
Serve as a servant leader for the Porduct Owners and serve as a Product Owner for certain critical business capabilities leveraging agile methodology.</div>
