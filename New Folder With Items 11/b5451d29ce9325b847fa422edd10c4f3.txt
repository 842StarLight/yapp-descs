<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Company Overview</b></h2>
<br>
Dematic is a leading supplier of integrated automated technology, software and services to optimize the supply chain. Dematic employs over 7,000 skilled logistics professionals to serve its customers globally, with engineering centers and manufacturing facilities located around the world. Dematic is one brand under the KION Group of companies and has implemented more than 6,000 integrated systems for a customer base that includes small, medium and large companies doing business in a variety of market sectors.
<br>
<br>
Headquartered in Atlanta, Georgia, Dematic is a member of KION Group, a global leader in industrial trucks, related services and supply chain solutions. Across more than 100 countries worldwide, the KION Group designs, builds and supports logistics solutions that optimize material and information flow within factories, warehouses and distribution centers. The company is the largest manufacturer of industrial trucks in Europe, the second-largest producer of forklifts globally and a leading provider of warehouse automation.<br>
<br>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>The Role</b></h2>
<br>
<h1 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Position Summary:</b></h1>
<br>
The Director Mobile Automation – North America (MA NA) is responsible for leading the strategy, execution and management of the Mobile Automation business on a North American basis. This position will be responsible for the virtual P&amp;L, best practices, support, and growth of the business associated with our Mobile Automation solutions in partnership with our regional Sales and Engineering leadership. This position will support all KION Group Operating Units (OUs) and Brands in , including but not limited to: KION NA, Linde, STILL, and Dematic. The position will provide leadership for our go-to-market strategy, regional and market input for the product roadmap, strategic positioning of key opportunities, and collaboration with other Mobile Automation regions (APAC, EMEA, ANZA).
<br>
<br>
<b>Key Responsibilities:</b>
<ul>
<li>Build and manage a high-performance MA business team consisting of Sales, Solution Development, Project Engineering and Project Execution focused on our KION Group Mobile Automation platform, partnering with regional sales leadership to enhance our MA content in solutions</li>
<li>Develop and manage the overall Mobile Automation sales strategies and related cadence and processes</li>
<li>Develop and manage metrics/success criteria for adoption of our standard software platforms</li>
<li>Support the Global Product Management function within MA, including roadmap development for customer needs, internal needs, market needs</li>
<li>Chair the Mobile Automation North America region and its adoption across key accounts and KION Group OUs within NA</li>
<li>Work with the executive team to identify and develop strategic alliances and acquisition targets that enhance our overall customer solution</li>
<li>Represent the company at customer, partner, and industry events</li>
<li>Consistently coach and develop Sales, Solution Development, Project Engineering and Project Execution resources on positioning and deploying our MA platforms</li>
</ul>
