<h2>Job 1768, Advertising Account Manager - Sky-Hi News</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p>Do you have a natural ability to build relationships? Do you get satisfaction from helping others succeed? Are you a multi-media advertising guru? The Sky-Hi News is looking for an account manager to help local businesses grow and thrive through innovative advertising.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p>Solicit advertising on the telephone as well as handling call in business. Solicit business from accounts currently assigned to you as well as prospects. Sales will be into Sky-Hi News and other CMNM products, and Sky-Hi special initiatives.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>Responsibilities Include (but are not limited to):</b><br>
</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<ul><li>Sell display advertising to assigned account list and prospect for new business as directed by advertising director. Prospecting may include sales into certain short-term and long-term initiatives, as well as actively pursuing new business from inactive accounts
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Sell special theme pages and directories.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Keep accounts payable within company goals.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Develop creative advertising campaigns on a regular basis for assigned accounts that will publish in the Sky-Hi News or other CMNM newspapers, and special sections.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Prospect your entire account list and sell, make recommendations on our entire line of expanding products as a regular part of your day. These would include the Vail Daily, the Summit Daily News etc.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Turn in all paperwork and ad copy in a legible and timely basis, meeting all established deadlines.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Attend sales and other meetings as required (on a timely basis).
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Handle all customer service issues effectively and quickly
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Do whatever it takes to get the job done.<br>
</li></ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>Qualifications:</b><br>
</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<ul><li>College degree preferred.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Sales experience helpful.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Type a minimum of 40 wpm accurately.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Good spelling and grammar skills.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Basic computer skills.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Good telephone and communication skills.<br>
</li></ul>
<p></p>
<br>
