<h2>Events Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>The Opportunity
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div>Workhuman is changing the way the world is thinking about rewards and recognition. We are redefining what social recognition means in the workplace and beyond. Workhuman seeks an Event Specialist to support our customer marketing efforts. This person will work with our events team to provide a memorable experience at our tradeshows.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
Will be responsible for coordinating all aspects of corporate tradeshows and events to ensure successful execution. This includes logistical planning and equipment asset management of large scale tradeshows as well as supporting internal events and meetings. This position is based in Framingham, MA and requires at up to 50% travel.</div>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities, Skills &amp; Qualifications
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div><b>Main duties and responsibilities:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
Support the execution trade show management for all US based tradeshows including securing booth space, creation of registration badges, managing housing, packing and sending exhibition materials</li>
<li>
Maintain show schedules, project checklist and processes</li>
<li>
Develop staffing and pre-conference training schedules</li>
<li>
Work with exhibit house vendor and transportation companies to ensure timely, cost effective execution and delivery of equipment and materials</li>
<li>
Travel to and support onsite execution and management of tradeshows and special events, including show set-up and dismantle and ensuring demonstration equipment is in proper condition and working order</li>
<li>
Work closely with marketing and sales to understand the demand generation strategy in order to promote tradeshows, including booth invitations, distribute pre- and post- attendee registration lists, website landing pages and email blasts</li>
<li>
Develop and deliver pre and post conference communication to internal team to ensure effective execution of staffing, housing, training and overall tradeshow messaging</li>
<li>
Manage lead retrieval systems during the conference and ensure processing of show leads in CRM system and distribution to Salesforce.com</li>
<li>
Evaluate tradeshow ROI and provide recommendations for attendance at various tradeshows</li>
<li>
Assist in the development of trade show strategy and budget preparation</li>
<li>
Assist in developing tradeshow related marketing communications and efforts</li>
<li>
Assist in the sales functions as necessary</li>
</ul>
<div><b>
Required skills and experience:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
BA/BS in Communications, Marketing or other related discipline preferred</li>
<li>
3-5 years of experience in supporting tradeshows or other corporate events</li>
<li>
Ability to work in a fast-paced environment, with multiple and changing priorities while maintaining strong focus on execution and results</li>
<li>
Adept at handling multiple complex projects simultaneously under pressure to meet tight deadlines</li>
<li>
Strong interpersonal and collaboration skills with an ability to communicate at all levels of an organization both internally and externally</li>
<li>
Excellent written and verbal communication skills, including presentation skills</li>
<li>
Strong organizational and planning skills</li>
<li>
Detail oriented with excellent follow-up, budgeting and time management skills</li>
<li>
Knowledge of marketing communication and demand generation tactics is preferred</li>
</ul>
