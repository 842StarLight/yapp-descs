<h2>Photo Sample Coordinator - Rejuvenation</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p><b>About the Team</b></p>
<p>
You will be a part of the Photo Warehouse team, working to actively maintain and improve the photo sample inventory and systems. You will work with the team to ensure samples are in peak condition and that teams in need of our product are given the best internal customer service possible. Our team plays an important behind the scenes role in bringing the aspirational aesthetic of Rejuvenation to life.</p>
<p><b>
About the Role</b></p>
<p>
Maintain and update sample database systems. Act as liaison between multiple teams to communicate product sample needs, status updates and additional related information. Receive, properly tag, and input all inbound product samples into inventory database. Work with cross functional partners to facilitate the movement of samples between company locations. Communicate needs for pulling, packing, and preparation for shipment, of all outbound product samples. Maintain systems of tracking photo sample data to ensure all necessary teams have accurate information.</p>
<p><b>
You're excited about this opportunity because you will...</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Maintain and update Photo Sample Inventory database to accurately reflect physical inventory.</li>
<li>Track, consolidate and update product records in spreadsheets and sample database</li>
<li>Receive, tag, organize and facilitate proper storage of new samples and related documentation</li>
<li>Collaborate with cross functional teams to track, share, and update samples</li>
<li>Communicate sample status and any related conflicts quickly and accurately</li>
<li>Assist with pulling and packing samples for transfer between teams</li>
<li>Maintain and improve sample storage to ensure items remain in new condition</li>
<li>Facilitate photo sample purges with input from Merchant teams via spreadsheets and data from retail database (Microsoft DAX)</li>
<li>Assist with Sample Sale and special product projects as needed by Studio teams</li>
<li>Keep warehouse/work area safe and clean according to general warehousing practices and OSHA requirements</li>
</ul>
