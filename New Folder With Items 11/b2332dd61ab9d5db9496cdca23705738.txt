<h2>Business Development Associate</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>The Opportunity
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div>Workhuman’s Inside Sales organization is looking for a Business Development Associate with determination and grit to professionally grow in a fast paced, energetic and dynamic Sales environment. The Business Development Associate will be charged with building the qualified sales pipeline to support our rapidly growing business. In this role, you will coordinate closely with our Inside Sales organization to support research and prospecting activities for new accounts to secure a steady flow of new qualified opportunities. The Business Development Associate will be responsible for starting a memorable experience for prospects that will set the stage for effective sales follow up. The successful candidate will be a self-starter, naturally driven and motivated, professional with positive energy and a passion for a customer facing career path. This is an excellent opportunity to gain hands-on experience working on challenging, meaningful projects with guidance and coaching from a mentor and other members of the Inside Sales team.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div><i>
This position will report to the Senior Inside Sales Manager.</i></div>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities, Skills &amp; Qualifications
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div><b>Responsibilities:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
Work alongside our sales team and a mentor to gain knowledge and have hands on training within the HR industry</li>
<li>
Research and generate lists of potential customers and contacts</li>
<li>
Provide input on account initiatives and campaigns</li>
<li>
Assist in data management (via Salesforce)</li>
<li>
Get certified in Social Selling/Deck/Demo</li>
<li>
Respond, engage and qualify leads and inquiries</li>
<li>
Help in driving attendance and engagement to the Workhuman Live annual conference</li>
<li>
Proactively look for opportunities to improve and optimize sales and marketing activities</li>
<li>
Regularly achieve or exceed set activity targets for qualified opportunity generation on a monthly basis</li>
<li>
Conduct account and industry research on an on-going basis</li>
<li>
Learn and apply sales techniques to your day to day</li>
<li>
Work on increasingly challenging and engaging sales projects</li>
<li>
Gain hands-on experience in each stage of the sales funnel</li>
</ul>
<div><b>
Requirements &amp; Qualifications:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
Recent college graduate</li>
<li>
Proficient in Microsoft Office (Word, PowerPoint, Excel, and Outlook)</li>
<li>
Experience in customer facing positions a plus</li>
<li>
Detail oriented a must</li>
<li>
High interest in following a customer facing career path</li>
<li>
Excellent communication skills (verbal and written)</li>
<li>
Strong interpersonal skills to build positive rapport with prospects and team members</li>
<li>
Professional, hard-working, committed and determined to succeed and grow</li>
<li>
Strong time management skills; ability to meet deadlines and priorities while multi-tasking in a fast-paced environment</li>

Works well and thrives under pressure</li>
<li>
Positive energy, enthusiastic team player</li>
</ul>
