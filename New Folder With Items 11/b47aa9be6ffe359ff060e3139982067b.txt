<p></p>
<p>

<p>
GDIT is seeking candidates to support Task Order Managers to join GDIT's opportunity for an FAA National Airspace System (NAS) program and integration of new systems into FAA facilities. Administers task orders issued by FAA customers. Responsible for ensuring work requested under task order is tracked and performed to schedule, budget and customer satisfaction. There are multiple Task Order (TO) managers positions planned for a program to support the Federal Aviation Administration's (FAA) in regions and locations across the US. These leadership positions will include preparing task order proposals, specifications, budget estimates, and manage multi-discplinary teams that can include various including Program analysts, Schedulers, Engineers, Computer Programmers and subject matter specialists. The TO Managers will provide technical, program financial and schedule information to customers on regular basis and provide FAA with GDIT management timely status and reports. Having an understanding and committment to the critical mission of the FAA and flexiblity in addressing customer needs and requirements is important to success in this position.
</p>
