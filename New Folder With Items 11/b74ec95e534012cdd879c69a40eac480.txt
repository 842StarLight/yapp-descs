<p>Job Summary:</p>
<p><b>
Who are we?</b></p>
<p>
Live Nation Entertainment is the world’s leading live entertainment company, comprised of global market leaders: Ticketmaster, Live Nation Concerts, and Live Nation Media &amp; Sponsorship. Ticketmaster is the global leader in event ticketing with over 500 million tickets sold annually and more than 12,000 clients worldwide. Live Nation Concerts is the largest provider of live entertainment in the world promoting more than 30,000 shows and 95+ festivals annually for nearly 4,000 artists in over 40 countries. These businesses allow Live Nation Media &amp; Sponsorship to create strategic music marketing programs that connect over 900 brands with the 86 million fans that attend Live Nation Entertainment events each year. For additional information, visit www.livenationentertainment.com.</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Who are you?</b></p>
<p>
Passionate and motivated. Driven, with an entrepreneurial spirit. Resourceful, innovative, forward thinking and committed. At Live Nation Entertainment, our people embrace these qualities, so if this sounds like you then please read on!</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>
The Role:</b></p>
<p>
In this position you will be responsible for executing all aspects of Talent booking for our North Florida venues and handling show financial settlements the night of the concerts. Involves all aspects of music booking: Budgets, Scheduling, Contracts, Promotion, Marketing, Ticket Sales, Daily Administrative Reports, and Staff &amp; Production Coordination. The role will be based in St. Petersburg, FL.</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Establish, Build and Maintain Positive Relations with Agents, Musicians and Industry People.</li>
<li>
Must use knowledge of band histories and make contact and follow-up</li>
<li>
Negotiate all contractual agreements with artists</li>
<li>
Handle all regional and local agents, assisting with national acts whenever possible</li>
<li>
Establish relationships with industry people, (agents, band management, and label reps)</li>
<li>
Formulate deals and determine percentage splits</li>
<li>
Compiling and distributing monthly musical calendar</li>
<li>
Communicate calendar updates/changes to staff and appropriate media and providing them with promotional material</li>
<li>
Working with promotions on ticket give-aways/marketing materials</li>
<li>
Ensures that flow of venue and corporate information gets to all team members in an orderly and timely fashion</li>
<li>
Formulate deals and percentage splits, negotiating all contractual agreements with artists, manage budget of each event, responsible for forecast projections of events booked</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Qualifications:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Bachelor’s Degree or equivalent is preferred.</li>
<li>
Minimum 2 years or progressive administrative experience in a fast paced environment, preferably in the Entertainment Industry.</li>
<li>
Superior organizational skills; ability to multi-task, collaborate, and prioritize.</li>
<li>
Must have strong computer skills, with proficiency in MS Word, Excel, PowerPoint and Outlook.</li>
<li>
Excellent communication skills, both oral and written; strong attention to detail.</li>
<li>
Must be a strong team player, with a consistent professional demeanor and the ability to deal with all types of individuals.</li>
<li>
Entertainment Industry and/or ticketing experience, a plus.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p>
Applicants for employment in the U.S. must possess work authorization which does not require sponsorship by the employer for a visa.</p>
