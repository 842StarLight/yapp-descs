<h2>Senior Director, Product Management</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Mission:</b></p>
<p>
IntegriChain is seeking an accomplished product manager that has successfully launched, scaled, and maintained a portfolio of products. The successful candidate for this role is passionate about improving our healthcare system through the use of data, analytics, and applications. Successfully bringing products and enhancements to market within our Payer segment is a critical part of us being able to realize our vision.</p>
<p>
The expectation is that you will use a market driven approach to roll-in, develop, and rollout new features, functions, and enhancements across IntegriChain’s products in the Payer segment, based on a roadmap that supports the company’s top and bottom line objectives. You have a bias for action, know how to lead without authority, and can wrangle a team to drive results. Having experience within the payer, market access, and/or finance groups within the life sciences industry is highly desirable.</p>
<p>
What’s it like to work at IntegriChain? We operate with a start-up mentality: we are committed to the true velocity of the organization, we quickly identify and remove blockers, and we help each other out all day, every day. We hire A players, so you’ll be working alongside the best in the industry – smart, effective, passionate, collaborative. Belief in the mission of IntegriChain and its customers is essential. We and our customers believe that we can truly make a meaningful difference in the lives of patients by helping them start therapy sooner and stay on it longer.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Duties:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Proactively engage with existing and prospective customers to understand their problems, challenges, and inefficiencies to inform product roadmap</li>
<li>
Organize and lead 1-1 market stakeholder interviews, product councils, advisory boards, and surveys to inform definition of market problems</li>
<li>
Develop and own the three quarter product roadmap for the Payer segment, translating market problems into sized and prioritized epics</li>
<li>
Maintain a backlog of potential features and enhancements beyond the next three quarters, informed by the external and internal stakeholder feedback</li>
<li>
Define the actionable insights that customers are seeking from their data assets and partner with IntegriChain’s data scientists to design analytic solutions that surface those insights in intuitive and easy to understand ways</li>
<li>
Manage, develop, and grow a team of off-shore product owners that can partner with development team to build and release product enhancements that drive sales, profitability, and customer satisfaction</li>
<li>
Work with on-shore and off-shore development team, in an agile process, to build out a three sprint groomed backlog that helps ensure successful delivery of product enhancements on budget, on scope, and on time</li>
<li>
Drive execution of all cross functional go-to-market activities to ensure successful roll-out of product releases across Sales, Marketing, Customer Operations, Data Operations, and Delivery and Advisory Services</li>
<li>
Establish and measure key performance indicators for each product release</li>
<li>
Define and maintain all aspects of Payer products including, service levels, support models, communication plans, implementation plans, go-to-market plans, and all associated product documentation</li>
<li>
Drive internal awareness of product roadmap and releases through creation and maintenance of Payer product wall, release presentations, brown bag lunches, and company All-Hands meetings</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
Requirements:<br>
<p>
</p>
<ul>
<li>
Minimum of 8 years of product experience with 2+ years experience applying data science and analytics solutions to complex business challenges, preferably in commercial healthcare market</li>
<li>
Experience with healthcare data and analytics strongly preferred, ideally in the payer, claims, gross to net, government pricing, and/or rebate market</li>
<li>
Proven track record of working in and leading cross-functional teams of SMEs, software engineers, data engineers, data scientists, industry solutions, and marketing to solve market problems</li>
<li>
Expertise in building products using off-shore teams located across multiple time-zones</li>
<li>
Proven ability to manage, build, and grow off-shore product teams</li>
<li>
Experience using data visualization tools like Tableau, Spotfire, Crystal Reports, Qlikview, etc.</li>
<li>
Understanding of and experience with data model design as well as data cleansing/integration activities such as master data management, preferably with healthcare data</li>
<li>
An “n=many” mindset that naturally identifies trends across a market and the needs of the many</li>
<li>
A highly consultative and diagnostic mind that is able to rapidly map and understand new business processes and the drivers of business pain</li>
<li>
A deep passion for healthcare business, patient outcomes, clinical workflow, and health tech innovation</li>
<li>
Strong problem solving skills and willingness to roll up one's sleeves to get the job done</li>
<li>
Exceptional written and verbal communication skills</li>
</ul>
