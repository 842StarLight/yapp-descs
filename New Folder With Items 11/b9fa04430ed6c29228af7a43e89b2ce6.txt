<h2>Enterprise Account Executive, SaaS - Secureworks Sales - North Central US</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>Role Responsibilities</p>
<ul>
<li>Consistently achieve quarterly and annual sales targets.</li>
<li>Responsible for overall sales strategies and generating positive business outcomes for our customers, who often have complex environments.</li>
<li>Articulate and evangelize the vision and positioning for the cybersecurity SaaS solution to customers and within Secureworks.</li>
<li>Develop a deep understanding of both the customers and competitive landscape, enabling a business dialogue to be conducted with C-level contacts and their teams.</li>
<li>Acts as a source of software/SaaS positioning and selling expertise for account managers throughout the Secureworks organization.</li>
<li>Masters all SaaS sales-related training materials, and stays current on enablement track and competency development.</li>
<li>Drive creative business development activities to penetrate white space accounts.</li>
<li>Work with Secureworks partners to propose differentiated solutions to help our clients.</li>
<li>Develop, maintain and share Customer Account Plans for your top strategic accounts, ensure theses are current, factual and easily accessible by the virtual team.</li>
<li>Manages current quarter business and subsequent quarters' pipeline proactively and consistently.</li>
<li>Directs and prioritizes own work and provides accurate and timely forecasts in SFDC.</li>
<li>Builds and maintains network of colleagues and customers to share information and obtain prospects.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p>
Requirements</p>
<ul>
<li>Minimum 7+ years of enterprise selling/account management experience.</li>
<li>Demonstrable success selling SaaS solutions at start-ups or young companies.</li>
<li>5+ years selling cybersecurity software and/or SaaS solutions to enterprises and large enterprises.</li>
<li>Proven ability in closing complex deals within the IT/Security sector.</li>
<li>Proven ability to learn quickly in a very fast-paced environment.</li>
<li>Must be a team player - ability to interact with teammates and partners fairly, and respectfully; proactive / results-driven / strong problem solving skills.</li>
<li>Must work well in an entrepreneurial environment; be a self-starter, and be a visionary seller.</li>
</ul>
