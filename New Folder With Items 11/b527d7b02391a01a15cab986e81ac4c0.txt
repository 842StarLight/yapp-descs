<h2>Field Service Technician</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<b>Description:</b><br>
Lombart Instrument is the largest authorized distributor in the United States of diagnostic instrumentation for the eyecare industry. We have an opening for a customer-focused individual to join our team as a Field Service Technician. In this role, you will perform set-up and installation, preventive maintenance, emergency maintenance and troubleshooting on a variety of electronic, mechanical, and computer based medical equipment. Most of these services are performed in the field at our client’s location.
<br><br>
<b>Job Responsibilities:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Provide installation and commissioning services of newly purchased equipment at customer facilities</li>
<li>Provide service and support to customers including preventative maintenance, troubleshooting and repair, both in-house (10%) and on-site (90%)</li>
<li>Deliver world-class customer service to all customers with professionalism</li>
<li>Team with Sales to meet customer requirements</li>
<li>Coordinate business information between customers, sales, and other associates</li>
<li>Submit all paperwork in a timely and professional manner</li>
<li>Maintain a close professional relationship with all internal/external customers</li>
<li>Participate in developing and maintaining a sense of teamwork within the service department</li>
<li>Seek and participate in training to improve technical and professional skills.</li>
</ul>
<b>Requirements:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Minimum of 2 years of experience with electro/mechanical repair and troubleshooting</li>
<li>Minimum High School diploma or GED required</li>
<li>Able to handle the physical requirements of loading, unloading, and moving of equipment up to 100 lbs required</li>
<li>Experience with electronics (computer) repair a plus</li>
<li>Previous experience with diagnostic tools and equipment</li>
<li>Must have an active and valid US driver’s license</li>
<li>Ability to pass a background check and drug screening</li>
<li>Self-starter (the ability to work unsupervised)</li>
<li>Proficient in Microsoft Office or similar business software/applications.</li>
<li>Willing to travel approximately 65% of the time</li>
<li>Excellent communications skills to interface with customers and coworkers</li>
<li>Willingness to seek and participate in training to improve technical and professional skills</li>
<li>Team player</li>
</ul>
</div>
