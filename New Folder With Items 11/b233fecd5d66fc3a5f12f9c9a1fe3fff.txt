<h2>Publishing Sales Representative, Greater Salt Lake Region</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p><b>Educational Sales Opportunity - Walsworth Yearbooks, the nation’s most innovative and fastest-growing yearbook publisher, is hiring outside sales representatives!</b></p>
<p>
Work alongside students and teachers providing journalistic advice, creative guidance and critique, as well as technical training and assistance. Serve as a mentor to both students and teachers, while also working as a project manager to guarantee their publication’s success from start to finish.</p>
<p>
You will receive a group of existing customer accounts to manage and grow. These schools will view you as the ultimate resource for creating their yearbook. Delivering hands-on support, you’ll ensure each school’s yearbook business is successful by assisting with everything from deadline and budget management to team building and marketing challenges. Your customers will look to you for training, guidance and trouble-shooting on topics such as photography, design, making money and keeping staffers on task.</p>
<p>
In addition to managing and supporting existing customers, you’ll also prospect and sell other surrounding schools on partnering with you, and Walsworth, for their next publication. This provides the rare opportunity to work in a creative and educational capacity, while also enjoying the earning and growth opportunity of a sales professional.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
Responsibilities:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Meet or exceed sales quotas</li>
<li>
Support customers through every aspect of yearbook production, keeping them engaged, on schedule, and loyal</li>
<li>
Provide advice and critique on photography, design, journalism, technology, classroom management and business</li>
<li>
Teach and engage students in a classroom setting</li>
<li>
Grow market share within your assigned territory</li>
<li>
Stay a step ahead of customer schools, keeping them on track with deadlines and budgets</li>
<li>
Daily travel to schools, both customers and prospects, within the territory, minimal overnight stays</li>
<li>
Maintain organized customer files and detailed records on every school</li>
<li>
Collaborate with other local Yearbook Consultants to host training workshops and seminars for students and teachers</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
Qualifications:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Minimum of Bachelor’s Degree required</li>
<li>
Prior customer service and sales experience (ideally outside sales)</li>
<li>
Experience with high school or college yearbooks is desirable</li>
<li>
Ideal candidates will also have experience in related fields, such as: teaching, coaching, journalism, graphic design, photography or printing.</li>
<li>
Creative thinking and advanced problem-solving skills</li>
<li>
Excellent computer literacy skills and proficiency in Adobe design software</li>
<li>
Strong communication and organizational skills</li>
<li>
Competitive and driven personality with an influential spirit</li>
<li>
Ability to lift and carry up to 50 lbs.</li>
<li>
Must have a valid drivers’ license and access to a reliable vehicle for daily travel</li>
<li>
Willingness to establish a home office and work independently</li>
<li>
Maintain confidentiality with respect to student data and customer accounts</li>
</ul>
<p><br>
Compensation:</p>
<p>
 We offer a competitive salary and bonus plan, plus an expense account, as well as full benefits including: 401(k), medical insurance, exceptional training and an unbeatable corporate culture.</p>
<p>
In business more than 80 years, Walsworth Yearbooks is the only American family-owned yearbook publisher in the country. Never bought or sold, we’re among the 35 largest printing companies in the U.S., and one of the top 10 book printers, servicing more than 4,400 schools nationwide. Our commitment to providing quality printing with a focus on customer service consistently earns us stellar customer satisfaction scores and ensures our continued national expansion.</p>
<p>
We lead the industry in technical innovation and are the fastest-growing yearbook company in the country. Investing heavily in our employees, we start our Yearbook Consultants with in-depth classroom training and continue with intensive on-the-job training, ongoing education, mentoring and coaching. Every member of our management team started in the field and was promoted from within. Many of our employees stay for decades, with good reason. Help us extend our legacy!</p>
