<h2>Site Manager</h2>

<p>A Self Storage Facility in Colorado Springs, CO is looking for a motivated and dependable person to fill an available full time Site Manager position. Self-Storage experience is preferred, but not required. Being available to work most Saturdays is required. A proven sales background is a plus.</p>

<ul>
<li>Showing, renting, and vacating of storage units.</li>
<li>Greeting customers, answering phones, and responding to emails for rental inquiries</li>
<li>Making collection phone calls to delinquent tenants.</li>
<li>Performing all aspects of running an office including, but not limited to the use of a computer software program, making decisions, reconciling cash receipts, following existing procedures, and the interpreting of reports.</li>

</ul>

<li>Excellent customer service skills and the ability to work well with the public to create an exceptional experience for every customer.</li>
<li>Good time management skills and the ability to solve problems in a courteous and timely manner.</li>
<li>Knowledge of computers, sales, the ability to communicate well on the phone, and enjoy working with people as a team.</li>
<li>A positive attitude and a willingness to learn existing procedures to maximize sales and customer service.</li>
<li>A keen eye for cleanliness and curb appeal to ensure this property is maintained as a premiere storage property in the market.</li>
