Who Are We?
<br><br>
Headquartered in Toronto, Canada, Avison Young is a collaborative, global firm owned and operated by its Principals. Founded in 1978, with legacies dating back more than 200 years, the company comprises approximately 5,000 real estate professionals in 120 offices in 20 countries. The firm’s experts provide value-added, client-centric investment sales, leasing, advisory, management and financing services to clients across the office, retail, industrial, multi-family and hospitality sectors.
<br><br>
Senior Transaction Manager – Chicago Private Equity Advisory Group
<br><br>
We are looking for a new Senior Transaction Manager to join Avison Young’s Private Equity Advisory Group.
<br><br>
The Private Equity Advisory Group specializes in working with private equity firms and their portfolio companies to help accelerate growth, enhance operational efficiency and create long-term value through strategic real estate and operational transformation initiatives. These operational initiatives include day-to-day portfolio and transaction management, and M&amp;A due diligence for multiple clients. Transformation projects include consolidations, facility expansions, acquisitions and/or relocations, sale leasebacks and evaluation analysis, network and supply chain optimization, and economic incentives. For capital intensive transformation projects, this position will be in a support role to Principals.
<br><br>
Manage Client and Account Support Relationships (internal and external)
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Lead key portfolio and transaction responsibilities for global private equity clients</li>
<li>Manage lease administration support team and drive real estate portfolio dashboard customization for each client</li>
<li>Advise on client projects and transactions</li>
<li>Attend regular client meetings and join/lead conference calls</li>
<li>Identify and implement best practices to improve client transaction management processes</li>
<li>Work directly with clients and team members to resolve financial modeling, transaction and landlord issues</li>
<li>Collaborate local market teams and manage building tours with clients</li>
<li>Develop and manage KPI reporting to clients</li>
</ul>
Transaction Support
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Strategize, manage and execute small, mid-size and large complex transactions</li>
<li>Collaborate with internal and external team members to develop customized metrics and KPIs</li>
<li>Lead transaction procurement for client and private equity team on a vast array of projects throughout the globe</li>
<li>Track project progress from strategy through implementation of transactions</li>
<li>Collect client requirements, obtain market data, review market reports, retain real estate broker partners nationally, oversee site selection and evaluation, create requests for proposals (RFP’s), negotiate letters of intent, leases, purchase and sale agreements, and prepare approval packages for client’s senior management</li>
<li>Lead the development and implementation of occupancy cost savings strategies while working with the workplace strategy to develop and evaluate alternative occupancy solutions</li>
<li>Refine existing transaction processes and procedures</li>
<li>Review commission invoices for accuracy and progress on payment</li>
<li>Additional duties may be included around the portfolio and account management process</li>
</ul>
