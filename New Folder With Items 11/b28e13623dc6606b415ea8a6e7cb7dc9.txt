<h2>Director, eCommerce</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>BenSoleimani.com is a digitally-native luxury home brand which launched in early 2019. Ben Soleimani, the founder and creative director, comes from a long history of rug purveyors and has made a name for himself in the design community over the past 20 years. With the launch of Bensoleimani.com, Ben is expanding his design point of view to the entire home.<div><div>
<p><b>
The Ideal Candidate:</b><br>
The <b>Director of eCommerce</b><b> </b>is responsible for leading strategic development of consumer and brand experience, revenue and profit growth for BenSoleimani.com direct to consumer website. This role requires a proven track record in growing eCommerce businesses through functional innovation and user experience, employing effective traffic-driving tactics and campaigns, creating engaging on-site content and merchandising opportunities through cross-functional collaboration.</p>
<p>
As a member of the management team, the Director of eCommerce provides leadership and vision to both drive the business forward and provide exceptional brand experience to our consumers. The Director of eCommerce will partner with corporate shared services functions that span eCommerce development, IT, customer service, and warehousing.</p>
