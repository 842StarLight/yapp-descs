<h2>High Velocity Sales Representative</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">Looking for a sales job where you can do well and make a difference? Level Access, a leading provider of IT Accessibility compliance product and services, is looking for energetic and talented High Velocity Sales Representative with experience selling high-tech products and services. Candidates must be motivated, self-starters and work well in a fast-paced entrepreneurial environment. The High Velocity Sales Specialist will build new relationships and expand business with existing customers within assigned territory or industry.
<br><br>
The High Velocity Sales Representative will be responsible for all aspects of the sale, including price negotiations, contract development, and order fulfillment. This position is a great opportunity to work in a high-growth environment and partner with sales and services organizations to develop and carry out accessibility Initiatives.
<br><br>
<b>Responsibilities:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Find, manage, grow and close revenue in existing and net-new accounts.</li>
<li>Inside, outbound sales, primarily by telephone; must have excellent oral communication skills</li>
<li>Establish, develop, and grow new business relationships over the telephone with influential contacts</li>
<li>Develop and maintain relationships within assigned territory</li>
<li>Use outbound calling to prospects, build relationships with potential clients, uncover the true needs of their business, and recommend the right products and services</li>
<li>Must be able to listen effectively, assesses the situation, determine relevant issues, identify solutions and gain consensus.</li>
<li>Develop comprehensive knowledge of customer and market needs</li>
<li>Managing a complex sales cycle, from prospecting to closing</li>
<li>Responsible for pre-sale planning and post-sale execution</li>
<li>Meet or exceed Monthly, Quarterly and Annual sales objectives</li>
<li>Utilize and leverage SalesForce to manage marketing leads effectively</li>
</ul>
<b>Qualifications:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>2+ years working experience in a professional B2B sales environment in the software industry</li>
<li>College degree - BA/BS - or equivalent</li>
<li>General understanding of Information Technology and SaaS offerings</li>
<li>Strong sales drive and aspiration to win</li>
<li>Demonstrated market knowledge of competitive opportunities</li>
<li>Strong organization and time management skills</li>
<li>Ability to multitask.</li>
<li>Effective communication skills to aid in forming relationships with your customers and coworkers</li>
</ul>
Salary is commensurate with experience. This is a full-time, salaried position with a competitive benefits package. It is being offered in the Vienna, VA office. Please submit your resume and cover letter for immediate consideration.
