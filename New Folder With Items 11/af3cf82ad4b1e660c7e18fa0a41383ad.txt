<p><b>
Position Summary</b></p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
ServiceNow Developer will be responsible to develop, architect solutions, design, enhance, administer and support ServiceNow suite of applications that consists of CSM, ITSM, ITOM, ITBM services using best practices and industry standards. This position will closely work with ServiceNow Lead Engineer and implement the solutions by developing the required soultions and configuring ServiceNow platform for Rimini’s business processes and requirements.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Essential Duties &amp; Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Design, develop and configure out of the box workflows, forms, scripts and create / maintain custom objects to meet business requirements using Agile methodologies.</li>
<li>
Rapidly prototype new solutions based on user requirements.</li>
<li>
Collaborate with team members, to understand the requirements and design solutions by following the industry best practices.
</li>
<li>Build test scripts and perform unit testing for various business case scenarios, and support User Acceptance tests and implementation on production.</li>
<li>
Integrate ServiceNow with other on-premiese and clould Applications as needed.</li>
<li>
Integrate other client ServiceNow instances with internal instance.</li>
<li>
Actively collaborates with other teammates to perform code reviews, discuss new developments and projects.</li>
<li>
Support, develop and configure ServiceNow for Customer Service Portal, Incident, Problem, Change management, Asset Management, Configuration Management and Service Catalog.</li>
<li>
Ensure proper maintenance, administration, version upgrades, performance issues for ServiceNow application.</li>
<li>
Serve as technical expert of ServiceNow platform.
</li>
<li>Enhance data integrity through effective design, maintenance and security practices.</li>
<li>
Deliver detailed TDD (Techincal design Document) for all the solutions worked on.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
 </b><b>Additional Duties &amp; Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Work cross-functionally to integrate ServiceNow with key business applications such as SharePoint, Salesforce, etc.</li>
<li>
Other job related duties and responsibilities may be assigned from time to time.</li>
</ul>
