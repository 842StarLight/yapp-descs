<h2>Marketing and Training Representative</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div></div>
<div>
<div>
<div><b>:</b></div>
<div>
Advanced Professionals (AP) is seeking a Marketing and Training Representative responsible for building awareness of Wholesale Benefits (WB), its clients, customer education and training on the benefits of WB client services, and to serve as the public-facing representative of both AP, WB and its clients. The ideal candidate is outgoing, highly organized and tech savvy.</div>
<div>
</div>
<div><b>
FROM THE TEAM:</b></div>
<div>
Our team is looking for someone who is entrepreneurial, enjoys a fast-paced environment, can think quickly on their toes and likes to take initiative and gets things done. Exciting aspects of the role; client variety you'll be working and interacting with, and the opportunity to grow and develop the position. As a brand ambassador for AP, and one of the most visible roles at ABD, each day will bring new and unique opportunities to market ABD and AP brand. If you have a "can do" attitude and like to find solutions and streamline processes, this is the role for you!</div>
</div>
<div><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>GENERAL INFORMATION:</b></h3>
<div><ul>
<li>
This position is a salary, exempt, and full-time role.</li>
<li>
This position is located at ABD’s Seattle office.</li>
<li>
The position reports to the Wholesale Benefits Director of Marketing.</li>
</ul></div>
</div></div>
<div><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>WHAT YOU'LL BE DOING</b></h3>
<div><ul>
<li>
Plan and execute training events (live and digital), appreciation events, client meetings, and marketing events.</li>
<li>
Network with clients at industry and sponsorship events, trade shows, and client appreciation events while representing and promoting AP/ABD.</li>
<li>
Facilitate, educate and train on WB’s technology platform to retail health insurance brokers and distribution partners .</li>
<li>
Educate and provide training to retail health insurance brokers and other distribution partners on the features and benefits of various WB offerings.</li>
<li>
Coordinate and facilitate annual accreditation of retail health insurance brokers and other distribution partners.</li>
<li>
Communicate and promote new products and services to enhance existing relationships.</li>
<li>
Identify opportunities for marketing campaigns to increase sales.</li>
<li>
Assist marketing with the design, writing and execution of client communications, including PowerPoint presentations, fliers, website updates and email blasts as they relate to their role.</li>
<li>
Using knowledge of the market and competitors, assist in development of company and client selling propositions and differentiators and new opportunities to grow the business.</li>
<li>
Manage client relationships through regularly scheduled meetings.</li>
<li>
Act as a liaison between clients, sales and marketing teams to increase sales. Report on monthly activities.</li>
<li>
May be assigned other responsibilities and tasks as needed to support strategic vision of the client.</li>
<li><b>
To perform this position successfully, an individual must be able to perform each essential duty satisfactorily. Reasonable accommodations may be made to enable individuals with disabilities to perform these essential functions.</b></li>
</ul></div>
</div></div>
<div><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>WHAT YOU BRING TO THE TABLE</b></h3>
<div><ul>
<li>
Excellent communication skills: written, public speaking and presentation preparation and comfortable with engaging c-suite level professionals.</li>
<li>
Excellent customer service skills, professionalism and tact.</li>
<li>
Proficient knowledge and use of MS Office Suite, strong skills in PowerPoint required .
</li>
<li>Ability to work independently, be resourceful, take initiative, solve problems and offer solutions.</li>
<li>
Works well with others in a fast-paced environment and responsive to co-workers and colleagues. Ability to train, delegate, provide oversight and give feedback.</li>
<li>
Adaptability and flexibility to address issues and respond to client needs.</li>
<li>
Excellent time and project management skills and ability to manage competing priorities and high volumes.</li>
<li>
Ability to learn and adopt use of technology systems and software applications.</li>
<li>
Ability to multi-task, understand urgency and manage shifting priorities and deadlines.</li>
<li>
Ability to build rapport and trust with clients, carriers and internal stakeholders.</li>
<li>
Strong understanding of insurance industry knowledge and the ability to analyze data.</li>
</ul></div>
</div></div>
<div><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>EDUCATION AND EXPERIENCE</b></h3>
<div><ul>
<li>
Bachelor’s Degree in Business, Marketing, Communications, or related education or experience is required.</li>
<li>
2-3 years of conducting live seminar and webinar trainings required.</li>
<li>
Experience in designing, writing, and producing training materials.</li>
<li>
Wholesales insurance knowledge, Employee Benefits insurance knowledge are a plus.</li>
<li>
Able to travel for 15% of work (primarily on the West Coast of USA)</li>
<li>
Salesforce and/or Marketo experience is a plus.</li>
</ul></div>
