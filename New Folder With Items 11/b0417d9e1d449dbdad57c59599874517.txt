<h2>Civil Engineer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Wessler is currently looking to fill Project Manager positions in our Drinking Water, Stormwater and Wastewater Groups.</b> These positions entail the study, design and construction administration of water, wastewater and stormwater projects.</p>
<p><b>Responsibilities for the position include: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Project management, including management and technical mentoring of staff.</li>
<li>Client management</li>
<li>New business development</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Job Requirements for the position are as follows: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>5-8 years experience with the study, design and construction administration of water, wastewater and stormwater engineering projects.</li>
<li>Bachelor's of Science Degree in Civil or Environmental Engineering from an accredited institution.</li>
<li>Indiana P.E. license (or ability to obtain through reciprocity).</li>
<li>Familiarity with municipal funding agencies (SRF, OCRA, RD and related).</li>
<li>Experience with sales and client relations management software essential.</li>
<li>Must be team oriented with good communication, planning and organizational skills.</li>
<li>Qualified candidates must be legally authorized to be employed in the United States. Wessler does not anticipate providing sponsorship for employment visa status for this employment position.</li>
</ul>
<p>This position can be based out of our Indianapolis or Carmel, IN office.</p>
<p>Wessler offers a flexible schedule and summer hours May through September where the office closes at noon on Fridays.</p>
<p>The salary range for this position is $75,000-$100,00+ annually based off years of experience and previous job history.</p>
<p>We offer a competitive wage, paid holidays, paid employee health &amp; disability insurance and 401(k) profit-sharing.</p>
