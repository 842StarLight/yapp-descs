<h2>Sales Manager - Midwest USA</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>RYCOR Sales Manager Midwest USA</b></p>
<p><b>Job Description</b></p>
<p>The ideal candidate is a motivated, well-organized individual who has a deep understanding of prospecting and is an excellent closer. This is a work from home position.</p>
<p><b>Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Develop &amp; manage a sales territory</li>
<li>Sell RYCOR to targeted school districts</li>
<li>Achieve sales, revenue and profit targets</li>
<li>Establishing, developing and maintaining positive business and client relationships</li>
<li>Reach out to customer leads through cold calling</li>
<li>Achieve agreed upon sales targets and outcomes within schedule</li>
<li>Coordinate sales effort with sales team members and other departments</li>
<li>Analyze the territory/market’s potential, track sales and status reports</li>
<li>Supply Executive Team with reports on customer needs, problems, interests, competitive activities and potential for new products and services.</li>
</ul>
<p>*</p>
<ul>
<li><b>Requirements</b></li>
<li>College degree in business administration or a related field</li>
<li>Experience in selling to K-12 market preferred</li>
<li>Excellent &amp; established “sales hunting skills”</li>
<li>High degree of competency with SaaS SW</li>
<li>Financial acumen</li>
<li>Successful previous experience as a sales representative or sales manager, consistently meeting or exceeding targets</li>
</ul>
<p>*</p>
<ul>
<li>
<b>The Ideal Candidate will have</b><b>: </b>
</li>
<li>Demonstrated ability to communicate, present and influence credibly and effectively at all levels of the organization</li>
<li>Proven ability to drive the sales process from plan to close</li>
<li>Strong business sense</li>
<li>Strong analytical skills, high degree of competency with SalesForce</li>
<li>Excellent people management skills</li>
<li>Superior competency in Excel &amp; Power Point</li>
<li>Ability to work under pressure and achieve targets</li>
<li>Strong work capacity and ability to get a lot of work completed in a timely manner</li>
<li>A commitment to continuous education through workshops, seminars and conferences</li>
</ul>
