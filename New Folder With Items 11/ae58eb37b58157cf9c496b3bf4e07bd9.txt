<b>Min Qualifications</b><br>
Bachelor's degree or equivalent in related field and 1 year related experience.JOB DESCRIPTION:To provide the technical skills required to generate, modify and maintain computer operating system software and application system software within well defined limits.<br>

<b>
Specific Job Related Duties</b><br>
<p></p>
<ul><li><b>Preferred Candidate to have creativity and technical skills to generate, modify and maintain multiple consumer-focused, web-based resources and applications.</b></li></ul>
<p><b>
General Web Support: 25%</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Support web content updates and provide general website development and maintenance support using the university's content management system</li>
<li>Support ADA web accessibility compliance work</li>
<li>Provide search engine optimization efforts, to include populating keywords and metadata, on various UTMB web properties</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Locations &amp; Wayfinding 25%</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Maintain and enhance UTMB locations in Google My Business (GMB), an interactive campus map, locations on web pages, locations in directory, and coordinate information for use in print</li>
<li>Maintain (or develop) consumer-focused floorplans for each campus and major UTMB site</li>
<li>Co-develop and maintain interior wayfinding system</li>
<li>Contribute to the development of a virtual tour for each major UTMB site</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Physician Info Management 25%</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Actively maintain physician and provider information in our patient directory (bios, locations, photos, education info, specialty and appointment status)</li>
<li>Ensure that information is consistently represented on our patient portal (utmbhealth.com)</li>
<li>Engage and maintain lines of communication with key campus contacts to facilitate timely flow of information</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Social Media: Content and Promotion; Reputation Management &amp; Engagement 10%</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Provide general support and assistance for UTMB's social media initiatives, to include generating and posting some original content</li>
<li>Assist with management of review sites and consumer contact via social channels</li>
<li>Under direction, support the university's reputation management program and communicate with key stakeholders as issues warrant</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Digital Signage, Patient TVs &amp; Monitors 5%</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Aid in the effective use of the campus and patient television system, campus monitors, digital billboards and other public visual display channels to support communication, promotion, wayfinding and related objectives</li>
<li>Assist with the quarterly updates of UTMB's Voice on Hold system</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Analytics Support: 5%</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Develop familiarity with analytical tools and datasets to generate web traffic reports and other metrics, as requested</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Other Duties as Assigned 5%</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Help address assorted tasks and functions managed by members of the digital team.</li>
</ul>
<br>
<b>
Preferred Skill/Competency</b><br>
<p>The ideal person for this role has strong organizational skills, superior attention to detail and demonstrated creativity. He or she is comfortable with a wide variety of projects, and has a high level of digital proficiency. They will have used a content management system for web publishing, and have some experience managing social media. They will be comfortable working as part of a highly motivated and very collaborative team, and equally at ease in intense, self-directed work. Some graphic design experience and image editing skill would be a major asset.</p>
