<h2>Corporate / Mid-Market Sales Director</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">DataRobot is looking for a driven, results oriented sales leader with experience in big data analytics or other enterprise software categories. This sales leader is expected to lead a team of Corporate / Mid-Market Account Executives in meeting and exceeding revenue and market penetration goals for the company. The successful candidate will be expected to hire, lead, and develop a team of high performance individual contributors to rapidly scale our presence in the region.
<br><br>
<b>Duties and Responsibilities:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Analyzes, develops, and implements sales strategy across key accounts to attain company strategic objectives.</li>
<li>Responsible for customer acquisition, client retention, and market expansion for the company, overseeing sales teams across the team to meet or exceed goals.</li>
<li>Drives revenue growth for new and renewing subscription based business.</li>
<li>Develops strategic plan, quarterly revenue forecast, territory design, and resource planning for sales of products in all stages of lifecycle.</li>
<li>Pro-actively manages team talent and pipeline including attracting, identifying, developing, rewarding, managing, and coaching.</li>
<li>Designs and implements an accounts focused sales organization structure to achieve optimal results.</li>
<li>Ensures individual contributors are focused on customers and markets to maximize revenue, billing, and business opportunities.</li>
<li>Leads teams in finding new opportunities and seeks ways to improve processes and products to increase revenue, profitability, and customer experience.</li>
<li>Works effectively across other functional teams such as legal, pre-sales, product, customer success, and finance.</li>
</ul>
<b>Ideal Qualifications:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>BA/BS degree and 5 plus years with direct Mid-Market or Enterprise Software selling and Management experience.</li>
<li>Experience selling analytics, data, databases, predictive modeling, or business intelligence software.</li>
<li>Extensive experience developing, implementing and managing strategic plans and growth strategies.</li>
<li>Strong analytical skills, ability to problem-solve, and apply critical thinking to inform recommendations and decision-making.</li>
<li>Proven track record of attracting, managing, and developing high-performing teams to cover key accounts.</li>
<li>Proven results with products in all stages of lifecycle; experience selling SaaS products and solutions.</li>
<li>Successful experience negotiating large contracts, including working with internal partners (legal counsel, product management, senior management) to drive business results and timely responses.</li>
<li>Understanding of sales methodologies and techniques for selling complex, content-rich information products for multiple users/sites.</li>
<li>Strong interpersonal skills, with ability to professionally interact with a diverse blend of personalities to reach resolution and maintain strong relationships.</li>
<li>Excellent verbal and written communication and presentation skills.</li>
<li>Genuine willingness and temperament in terms of working for a high energy and rapidly growing start-up.</li>
</ul>
<b>Other important information about this position:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>This position will be based out of the Boston HQ</li>
</ul>
