<p><b>BASIC FUNCTION:</b></p>
<p>
This position will provide leadership, planning and management for all areas of information technology strategy, development and implementation for the Enterprise Functions; Finance, HR, and Marketing. The scope of these responsibilities includes: partnering with executive functional leadership, developing an IT strategy, commissioning new efforts to deliver incremental value, leading the implementation of new capabilities, agreeing and delivering on yearly budgets with the functional leadership, and owning the relationship with 3rd party vendors providing support of existing IT Services for the functions.
</p>
<p>This position requires a highly motivated, proactive leader with the leadership, collaboration, and communication skills necessary to forge a partnership with the executive functional leaders.</p>
<p>
The Sr. Director, Enterprise Functions BRM will be a member of the IT leadership team and report directly to the Vice President, CIO.</p>
<p><b>
ACCOUNTABILITIES:</b></p>
<ul>
<li><p>
Lead the successful use of information technologies to help achieve the Function’s goals, including its cost targets. Work closely with functional executives to define an information technology vision and strategy aligned to their strategy</p></li>
<li><p>
Gains alignment on the appropriate and cost-effective investment of financial resources in Information Technology</p></li>
<li><p>
Works with the broader IT organization to drive down costs for provided Services, to develop new offerings that meet the strategic information technology vision, and to provide only the service capabilities deemed critical</p></li>
<li><p>
Recommends creative new technology, processes, or data to drive more value and lower cost</p></li>
<li><p>
Provides guidance to functional executives for proposed new information technology systems, or major enhancements</p></li>
<li><p>
Responsible for the design, development and support of information technology applications supporting the Enterprise Functions</p></li>
<li><p>
Ensures project commitments made to the Enterprise Functions are met, including delivering on scope, schedule, and budget</p></li>
<li><p>
Manages a team of professionals delivering incremental value for the Enterprise Functions</p></li>
</ul>
