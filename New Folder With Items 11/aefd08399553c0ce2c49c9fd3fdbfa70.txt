<div><b>
Senior Enterprise Sales Executive:</b></div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div>We are seeking sales professionals to join the world’s leading and fully integrated cloud applications, platform services enterprise software company. Oracle has a track record for hiring and developing the very best sales professionals in the industry and investing generously in employee benefits and resources. If you have a passion for technology sales, a great attitude, thrive in an environment that is fast-paced and challenging and are looking for an employer who rewards employees that deliver results, you should consider a career at the world’s leading enterprise software company, Oracle.</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div>Oracle America, Inc. currently has Reston VA based openings for the Senior Enterprise Sales Executive position reporting to the Oracle University global business unit. Utilizing a programmatic approach, this person is responsible to build and sell complete education services programs inclusive of cloud-based learning subscriptions, IP solutions, change management services and traditional instructor lead training events to the Oracle customer base through face to face and virtual meetings. The Sr. Enterprise Sales Executive is responsible to ensure all technical and Oracle technology end users are fully functional with Oracle technology.</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div><b>Preferred Sales Experience:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>7+ years of individual quota carrying, direct sales experience selling to the senior executive level within any one of the following three areas:</li>
<ul>
<li>
State and Local market.</li>
<li>
Higher Education market.</li>
<li>
Fortune 500 companies.</li>
</ul>
<li>
Experience with both phone and in-person selling.</li>
<li>
Ability to identify new and expansion sales opportunities - strong business acumen for uncovering, evaluating, growing and closing sales opportunities within a dynamic and extremely time sensitive environment.</li>
<li>
Ability to manage a large number of active accounts while simultaneously uncovering and closing new sales opportunities and new customers.</li>
<li>
Documented, consistent success exceeding quarterly and annual sales targets through thorough account planning and weekly forecasting sessions.</li>
<li>
Understanding of Oracle’s integrated technology solutions including Oracle HCM &amp; ERP SaaS applications software, PaaS and IaaS solutions.</li>
<li>
Impeccable verbal and written business communication skills, and the ability to present effectively to executive leadership teams.
</li>
<li>Travel expectations: 10-25%.</li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<div><b>Preferred Qualifications:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Ability to effectively work in both a teaming and direct role in complex selling environments.</li>
<li>
Experience with target account selling, solution selling, and/or consultative sales techniques.</li>
<li>
Ability to articulate specific examples of how you have hit, or exceeded your documented yearly quota, key wins, length of sale cycle and closing deals.</li>
<li>
BS or BA degree.</li>
