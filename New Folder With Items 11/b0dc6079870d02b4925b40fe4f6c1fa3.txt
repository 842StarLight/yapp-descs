<p><b>Teller – Part Time</b></p>
<p>
The Consumers Credit Union team is looking to hire a passionate Teller – Part Time to join our team. As a Teller – Part Time, you will provide exceptional service to enhance member relationships and exceed member expectations.</p>
<p>
Our ideal candidate has a great attitude, strong attention to detail and can effectively cross-sell products and services to our members to meet their financial needs. This role needs to have the ability to make decisions.</p>
<p>
As an employee of Consumers Credit Union, you are genuine and passionate. You are purposeful and like to be the solution, but more importantly, you are a servant leader.</p>
<p><b>
What you will do:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Provide excellent member service.</li>
<li>
Process member transactions such as deposits, withdrawals, loan payments, cash checks, credit card and line of credit advances.</li>
<li>
Recognize and act upon referral opportunities to achieve personal sales goals.</li>
<li>
Provide excellent member service.</li>
<li>
Process daily teller area operations including money orders, ATMs, night depository, and performing select overrides.</li>
<li>
Sustain teller balancing standards and accuracy.</li>
<li>
Demonstrate knowledge of all Consumers products and services.</li>
<li>
Uphold member confidentiality.</li>
<li>
Follow security guidelines.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
What you'll bring to the table:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
High school graduate or equivalent.</li>
<li>
Professional appearance, builds rapports with members, and enjoys working with people.</li>
<li>
Excellent written and verbal communication skills.</li>
<li>
Cash handling and basic math skills.</li>
<li>
Knowledge of current technology. Able to easily navigate multiple software environments.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
What you didn't know about the team:</b></p>
<p>
At Consumers, you're joining a team that works closely together and counts on one another. You'll find the people here enjoy an unusual high level of camaraderie.</p>
<p><b>
Perks:</b></p>
<p>
Consumers offers generous paid time off, paid gym memberships, in-house training, wellness programs, life coaches, loan discounts and more.<br>
</p>
