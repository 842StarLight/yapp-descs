<h2>Sr Management Consultant, Health & Human Services Practice</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Public Consulting Group, Inc. (PCG) is a leading public sector management consulting and operations improvement firm that partners with health, education, and human services agencies to improve lives. Founded in 1986 and headquartered in Boston, Massachusetts, PCG has over 2,000 professionals in more than 50 offices across North America and in Europe. The firm has five designated practice areas with extensive experience in all 50 states, clients in six Canadian provinces, and a growing practice in Europe. Often combining resources from two or more practice areas, PCG offers clients a multidisciplinary approach to solve their challenges or pursue opportunities. To learn more, visit www.publicconsultinggroup.com.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
Key member of consulting team supporting public clients across the country and reporting directly to Senior Management. Will be responsible for developing and executing programmatic knowledge in health and human services, particularly the Medicaid program; preparing complicated financial spreadsheets and using database software tools designed to support government cost accounting projects; maintaining contact with clients; supervising staff on project components and developing junior staff; and maintain a book a business and growing the business through client relationships and sales and marketing efforts. Will be part of dynamic team and willing to work on both projects and business development efforts.</p>
<p>
Travel (50%) and work over 40 hours per week is required. This is a salaried position. <b>This position will be located in our office in Boston, Massachusetts.
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Manage multiple projects on behalf of PCG, both in the New England area and across the country, related to government cost accounting in the health and human services area.</li>
<li>
Manage a team of direct reports, as well as additional staff working across assigned projects.
</li>
<li>Manage client relationships related to these and other projects.
</li>
<li>Willingness to learn Medicare and Medicaid Cost Principles, and/or cost allocation methodologies used by government agencies and/or health facilities.</li>
<li>
Gain understanding of the Federal Medicaid program and other health and human services programs, including Medicaid, SNAP, Title IV-E, Title IV-B, Temporary Assistance to Needy Families (TANF), and/or Workforce Development.</li>
<li>
Interact with technology team responsible for supporting software tools used to execute software.
</li>
<li>Manage sales and marketing efforts necessary to support and grow business.
</li>
<li>Participate and support Center of Excellence, Practice Area, and firm-wide initiatives related to various non-project related initiatives.
</li>
<li>Collaborate on other PCG projects as necessary.</li>
</ul>
