Our greatest asset is our people, and nothing is more important to us than ensuring that everyone knows that. Each of our 90+ offices has its own individual identity, and each also has its own unique rewards.

<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Locations:
</b></h2>US-Philadelphia, PA
<p></p>
<p>In this high profile role, you will initiate sales opportunities with targeted enterprise level clients to develop new business relationships. The Director of Business Development will be responsible for building and maintaining client relationships while identifying, pursuing and closing sales for TransPerfect’s entire suite of services. The individual should have experience in consultative sales with an emphasis on sales or consulting directly to industry firms and corporations.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Description:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Source and close new clients through referrals, cold calling, networking, and other means (tradeshows, regional organizations, etc.)
</li>
<li>Build a productive pipeline and manage each phase of the sales process
</li>
<li>Cultivate and maintain long term relationships with clients by delivering elite client service and engaging them by using knowledge of each industry and organization
</li>
<li>Formulate sales plan to achieve monthly, quarterly, and annual sales targets
</li>
<li>Generate daily outreach efforts (direct mail, digital marketing, cold calls) to prospective clients
</li>
<li><p>Develop an effective understanding of the capabilities, benefits, and competitive advantages of TransPerfect's services
</p></li>
<li><p>Present TransPerfect's solutions via one-on-one, group, online and in-person meetings.
</p></li>
<li>Set and manage client expectations while consulting with each client for best practices
</li>
<li>Manage and control pricing and contractual issues
</li>
<li>Travel (including multiple annual conferences, client meetings) - domestic and/or international
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Required Skills:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li><p>Minimum Bachelor’s Degree from a 4-year college or university
</p></li>
<li>At least 3-7 years of sales experience
</li>
<li><p>Proven track record in hunting new business and supporting an existing client base
</p></li>
<li>Sales life cycle experience—from lead generation through business closing
</li>
<li><p>Ability to manage and develop a team
</p></li>
<li>Proven track record in achieving sales targets
</li>
<li><p>Ability to travel for qualified business opportunities and trade shows</p></li>
</ul>
