<h2>Senior Business Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Position Overview:</b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>The Sr. Business Manager will manage our national network of Alliance brokers providing direction and guidance for profitable growth. The Sr. Business Manager will work collaboratively with the Candy Alliance for joint meetings and other projects that arise. This position will have direct responsibility for a select group of brokers/accounts (select regional and drug accounts) and will be responsible for coaching and managing Regional and National Sales Managers. The National Sales Manager reporting to the Sr BM is responsible for Special Markets which includes Theater, Vending and Micro Markets as well as the Natural COT. The SR BM will lead and coach Sales Managers to build, grow and develop these channels. This role is responsible for establishing goals and objectives for profitable brand development. This role has leadership responsibility within the Sales team and is expected to make decisions to manage regional and national sales.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Essential Job Functions:</b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Coach, develop and train Sales Managers responsible for managing our broker partners, national accounts (Special Markets and Natural COT) and regional sales.</li>
<li>Work with Director of Sales to establish goals and priorities for profitable growth in top accounts within the area of responsibility.</li>
<li>Develop plans with Brokers to build profitable business in top regional accounts.</li>
<li>Notify and arrange joint broker meetings with Candy Alliance members within ALC region of responsibility (VP’s territory).</li>
<li>Improve and enhance management of broker network– establish best practices and set standards. Collaborate with Candy Alliance team members for ideas on best practices.</li>
<li>Responsible for all communication to our broker partners.</li>
<li>Direct accountability for key accounts in Geography managed. Responsible for achieving specific sales and distribution objectives on specific accounts, while maintaining spend goals.</li>
<li>Monitor workloads to ensure optimal productivity throughout the regional sales teams.</li>
<li>Monitor trade spend – ongoing involvement with trade spend criteria and budgets.</li>
<li>Involvement with major account promotional spend evaluations as needed.</li>
<li>Entering promotional contracts and forecasting into JDE system as needed.</li>
<li>Participation in annual planning; develop volume objectives, ensure accuracy and timelines are met, help guide and develop key account strategies.</li>
<li>Review as needed key account presentations.</li>
<li>Attend account calls with Regional and National Sales Managers</li>
<li>Establish and monitor training, travel and meal budgets</li>
</ul>
<ul><li> Report highlights monthly: BKM to VP of Sales on a monthly basis; Drug to Director of Sales.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Special Markets and Natural team highlights to be completed by National Sales Manager.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Lead the annual development of broker quotas and incentives.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Keep Distribution and sales objectives active with consistent communication to other Sales team members responsible for Brokers.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Responsible for all Corporate Communication including Marketing and Sales updates</li></ul>
<ul>
<li>PDCA business problems.</li>
<li>Work with other departments in clearing obstacles for Regional and National Sales Managers</li>
<li>Ensure projects and reports are completed on time</li>
<li>Build relationships with Sr. executives at strategic accounts within the regions.</li>
<li>Participate in trade events as needed</li>
<br>
 </ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Requirements:</b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s Degree; MBA or other advanced degree preferred.</li>
<li>Six to nine plus years of sales management experience in a CPG company.</li>
<li>Direct selling experience to top regional accounts</li>
<li>Three to five years of Broker Management required.</li>
 <li>Three years of managing Account managers</li>
<li>Approximately 50%- 75% travel.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Qualifications:</b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Successful track record of managing people and achieving goals and objectives.</li>
<li>Strong experience in MS Office and IRI/Nielsen scan data required.</li>
<li>Ability to prioritize and handle multiple projects.</li>
<li>Ability to problem-solve complex issues.</li>
<li>Demonstrate a high level of independence, and vision; take initiative and show resourcefulness while working in a flexible team environment.</li>
<li>Foster team participation</li>
<li>Experience in generating, interpreting and analyzing syndicated data (IRI/Nielsen, Retail link, etc.) and have used forecasting software.</li>
<li>Computer proficient with ability to comprehend new systems.</li>
</ul>
