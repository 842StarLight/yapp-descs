<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Company Overview</b></h3>
<p>BardyDx is an innovator in digital health and remote patient monitoring, with a focus on providing the most diagnostically-accurate and patient-friendly cardiac patch monitors in the industry. Currently, BardyDx has FDA 510(k) clearance and CE Mark approval to market the Carnation Ambulatory Monitor (CAM) patch and is presently launched in the U.S. and multiple international countries.</p>
<p>
The CAM patch is the industry’s only P-wave centric™ ambulatory cardiac monitor and arrhythmia detection device that is designed to improve patient compliance with its lifestyle-enabling patch design. Designed to be worn comfortably and discreetly by both adults and children, the CAM patch is placed on the center of the chest, directly over the heart for optimal ECG signal collection. Distinguished from the competition by its state-of-the-art sensing technology, the CAM patch provides optimal detection and clear recording of the P-wave, the part of the ECG waveform that is essential for accurate arrhythmia diagnosis.</p>

<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Position Overview &amp; Purpose</b></h3>
<p>BardyDx is searching for a Sr. Marketing Manager to join our Marketing Team who is ambitious and passionate about successfully developing, improving, and marketing our products. The right individual will join a dynamic and fast-paced start-up environment and work with cross-functional teams to design, implement, and roll-out products that deliver the company’s vision and strategy.</p>
<p>
Under the purview of the Director of Marketing, the Sr. Marketing Manager’s role incorporates a range of downstream marketing functions, including traditional marketing (collateral, magazines, journals, trade shows, etc.), digital marketing (website, email, search, display, SEO, etc.), campaign development &amp; deployment, market research, and marketing analytics. This individual will combine creative, strategic, and tactical thinking with an analytical approach to successfully execute marketing programs and campaigns that improve the commercialization of our products. The Sr. Marketing Manager will be tasked with planning, executing, and measuring lead generation activities (e.g., trade shows, digital advertisements, search engine optimization, etc.) that will support the overall sales strategy. This individual will work closely with the Product Manager to coordinate product/feature launch plans and communications.</p>
