<h2>In-Store Sales Representative (SF)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>In-Store Sales Representative </b></p>
<p>PureCA, LLC (“Moxie”) is a manufacturer, packer, and shipper in the medicinal and recreational cannabis industry, offering best-in-class products for its clients and customers. With its state-of-the-art manufacturing facilities in Lynwood, California, and corporate offices in Long Beach, Moxie is poised to become a national leader in the emerging cannabis industry. We are a fun and exciting company to work for who truly values our employees and offers industry-leading wages and benefits.</p>
<p><b>Responsibilities</b></p>
<p>The primary responsibility is to ensure that Moxie products are sold-through to consumers. Engage with licensed retail store-front customers, to ensure that Moxie products are prominently displayed and properly priced and that store staff is trained to promote and sell Moxie products. To deliver in-store consumer promotions and events as required. To generate content for social media posting. The role is the “go-to” person for any budtender question or consumer question. Develop personal skills to grow with Moxie as the business grows.</p>
<p><b>Merchandising</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Understand the culture and vibe of Moxie to be able to represent the face of Moxie in each retail store-front</li>
<li>Build strong relationships with store managers and budtenders in each store</li>
<li>Ensure that Moxie products are prominently displayed in each retail location</li>
<li>Communicate Moxie product retail pricing (before tax and OTD) to Moxie area sales manager and record in CRM</li>
<li>Determine the opportunities for in-store advertising, signage, and customized retail displays and communicate to Customer Services manager</li>
<li>With customer’s permission, create a photographic record of in-store product display and save in CRM Notes section</li>
</ul>
<p><b>In-Store Product Management</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Schedule store visits to ensure that new product drops are made available for immediate sale</li>
<li>Gather Moxie product inventory data and share with sales and customer service</li>
<li>Encourage the customer to engage with Headset Bridge to enable the automated collection of inventory and sell-through data</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Training &amp; PADs</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Ensure that all budtenders are actively engaged with TeamMoxie platform</li>
<li>Conduct in-store promotional events/PADs to encourage consumers to purchase Moxie products</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Customer Engagement</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Respond to customer requests in a timely manner, engaging other Moxie resources as required</li>
<li>Manage store-level customer issues to resolution</li>
</ul>
<p><b>CRM Usage</b></p>
<ul><li>Use Microsoft CRM to record information and share with the rest of the internal Moxie team</li></ul>
<p><b>Web/Social Media</b></p>
<ul><li>Generate store-level content and share with the social media team</li></ul>
<p><b>Requirements</b></p>
<p>The store merchandising role requires a self-starter who is highly personable and can relate well to budtenders and consumers.</p>
<p><b>Personal Traits</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Demonstrates high levels of initiative and innovation</li>
<li>Ability to learn</li>
<li>Positive and engaging</li>
<li>Excellent communication skills</li>
<li>Flexible and adaptable to do what is needed to achieve results inside a high growth company</li>
<li>Proven prioritizing, time management and organizational skills</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Experience</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Familiarity with CRMs and Microsoft tools is required</li>
<li>Knowledge of cannabis products and processes is a plus</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Metrics</b></p>
<p>The primary metrics for this role are:</p>
<ul><li>Weekly sales-out</li></ul>
<p>The metrics will be developed for each store/chain of stores and will be updated weekly.</p>
