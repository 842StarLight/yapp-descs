<h2>Water Play Designer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>ESSENCE OF THE ROLE</b></p>
<p>The main function for this position is to assist sales with proposals and bid responsibilities, primarily by creating 2D and 3D designs. This role ensures proposals and services are delivered in a professional and timely manner by preparing proposal packages and providing support to sales representatives.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>VALUES</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Innovation</li>
<li>Commitment &amp; Passion</li>
<li>Integrity</li>
<li>Team &amp; Relationships</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>POSITION COMPETENCIES</b></p>
<ul><li> Able to meet or exceed proposal deadlines.</li></ul>
<ul><li> All to take customer request and visions and translate in a designed product</li></ul>
<ul><li> Ability to resolve issues in a timely manner</li></ul>
<ul><li> High quality of work, accuracy, and attention to detail</li></ul>
<ul><li> Ability to work well with others</li></ul>
<ul><li> Ability to handle multiple tasks and flexibility to shift priorities.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Excellent people, communication and organizational skills are necessary to perform this job</li></ul>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>POSITION EXPECTATIONS</b></p>
<ul><li> Create project (2D &amp; 3D) renderings based on sale instructions</li></ul>
<ul><li> Prepare cost estimates/quotes of projects</li></ul>
<ul><li> Prepare proposal package for presentation</li></ul>
<ul><li> Make revisions to any proposal designs and quotations as requested</li></ul>
<ul><li> Interaction with sales team and clients to obtain photography and any other marketing support materials</li></ul>
<ul><li> Other projects or duties as needed</li></ul>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>QUALIFICATIONS</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree in graphic design, industrial design, environmental design, architecture, interior design, or related field</li>
<li>2 years’ previous experience in a related role</li>
<li>Proficiency on MS Office applications (Excel, Word, etc.)</li>
<li>Experience with AutoCAD and 3D software required</li>
<li>Experience 3DsMax, InDesign, and Photoshop preferred</li>
<li>Must maintain a dependable and reliable work schedule and attendance record</li>
<br>
</ul>
