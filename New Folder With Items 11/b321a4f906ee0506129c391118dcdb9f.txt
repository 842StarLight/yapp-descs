<h2>Life Underwriter</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>A company built to serve you. It's your career, Shelter it!
</b></p>
<p></p>The Life Underwriter gathers information from appropriate sources to determine acceptability of risks and proper risk classification for Life Insurance policies. Provides education to the sales force in all aspects of Life Underwriting. Communicates with the sales force, applicants and medical facilities regarding determination of risk. Requires the ability to perform the essential functions of the position with or without a reasonable accommodation.
<br>
<br>
Minimum starting salary: $41,259.00 - $53,636.00
<p></p>
<p>If interested, please apply by:
</p>08/27/2019
<h1 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Essential Duties
</b></h1>
<ul>
<li>Identifies proper rate class and rating based on Life Underwriting tools of medical exams, blood profiles, Attending Physician Statements, consumer reports, financial statements, MVRs, MIB Coding System, and various questionnaires in addition to the Life application.
</li>
<li>Responds orally and in writing to agents, sales managers, applicants, policyholders, doctors' offices, paramedic companies, reinsurers and others regarding application completion, rate classification changes, exclusions or rate ups, medical records, lab results, Life Portrait assistance and any information needed based on the underwriting decision.
</li>
<li>Analyzes information on the Life Insurance application and about the applicant's medical history, occupation, avocations, lifestyle, driving record and finances.
</li>
<li>Decides if policy can be issued, at what rate and inform agent of decision.
</li>
<li>Reevaluates sub standard risks on issued Life policies to determine if rate can be lowered. Makes decisions on reinstatement applications and increases in coverage to existing Life policies.
</li>
<li>Be knowledgeable of company Life plans we offer and their coverages; medical diseases, conditions and terms; government legislation such as Privacy Act and state laws; world travel warnings; Medical Information Bureau procedures.
</li>
<li>Teaches on the Training Department's Life School program, participates with in house medical seminars, serves and heads committees for Life Underwriting changes and procedure updates; understands and works with Reinsurers.
</li>
<li>Attends Life Kick Off Meetings to assist the sales force by discussing ways to improve and expedite issuance of Life policies, to meet those in the field and to develop a team relationship with Marketing.
</li>
<li>Performs related duties and special projects as requested by the Underwriting Supervisor.
</li>
</ul>
<h1 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Skills
</b></h1>
<ul>
<li>Superior communication skills for effective interaction with agents, sales managers, applicants, policyholders, doctors' offices, paramedical companies, reinsurers and others in explaining company underwriting policies and procedures.
</li>
<li>Proficient operation of PC and applicable software including, but not limited to: Internet Basics, Document Imaging, Lotus Notes, Microsoft Word, Xnet, Concur, Life Portraits System, and the Spotlight and ABC websites.
</li>
<li>Must be able to research, analyze and interpret information from various sources to reach a practical and logical decision.
</li>
<li>Must be consistent in evaluations but possess the ability to negotiate and compromise.
</li>
<li>Must have accuracy, organizational and time management skills.
</li>
</ul>
<h1 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Training
</b></h1>
<ul>
<li>General studies and business administration courses as would be acquired at the college level.
</li>
<li>Completion of LOMA 1 &amp; 2 courses in the first year and LOMA 3 &amp; 4 courses in the second year with continuous successful completion of additional courses is expected until FLMI designation is achieved.
</li>
<li>Participates in underwriting training program to learn company policies and procedures, rating manuals, state laws, and medical terminology along with medical conditions and diseases.
</li>
</ul>
<h1 class="jobSectionHeader">
<b>Responsibilities/Principal </b><b>Accountabilities
</b>
</h1>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Works under general supervision of the Life Underwriting Manager.
</li>
<li>Accountable to assess the Life risks and apply appropriate premiums and conditions to Life policies.
</li>
<li>Exercises sound judgment and moderate discretion in risks accepted, using guidelines provided by Life underwriting manuals and company directives.</li>
</ul>
