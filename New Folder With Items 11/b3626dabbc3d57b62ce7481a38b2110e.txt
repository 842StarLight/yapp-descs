<h2>Field Service Representative 0037</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p>We are seeking a Field Service Representative experienced in the maintenance and repair of high technology, high production machinery. The position does require extensive travel to customers locations throughout the week.</p>
<ul>
<li>
Install and service automated industrial machinery represented by our company</li>
<li>
Assist our customers in the resolution of problems they encounter</li>
<li>
Identify and correct hardware failures, software discrepancies, or programming problems</li>
<li>
Instruct on proper use regarding applications, operation and maintenance</li>
<li>
Provide telephone consultation for customers and Stiles personnel with problems on our equipment</li>
<li>
Recommend corrective action and troubleshooting approaches</li>
<li>
Assist in training other Stiles Field Service Representatives and the customers in the use, operation, adjustments, and proper maintenance procedures for our equipment.</li>
</ul>
<p>
The successful candidate should possess the following:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Mechanical, electrical/electronics skills with industrial/capital equipment</li>
<li>
Experience with computers, pneumatic and hydraulics</li>
<li>
Drive systems, PLC, programming logic</li>
<li>
Knowledge and experience with KEB, Indramat, STOBER and/or FANUC</li>
<li>
Experience in the woodworking industry a plus</li>
</ul>
