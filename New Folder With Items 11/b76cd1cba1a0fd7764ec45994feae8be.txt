<h2>Leasing Consultant</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><i>Wilhoit Properties Inc.</i> <i>is currently seeking a Leasing Professional for our Kensington Court apartment community in Kansas City, MO.</i></p>
<p>The selected candidate will be responsible for assisting the property manager in leasing apartment units, giving tours of the property, completing lease paperwork, and greeting guests at the property.</p>
<p><b>Essential Duties: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Manage busy leasing office</li>
<li>Handle property collections including the filing of evictions</li>
<li>Assist the Property Manager in developing &amp; maintaining marketing plans</li>
<li>Resolve resident issues</li>
<li>Work with outside vendors, procure bids for authorized work</li>
<li>Coordinate vacant unit turnover process with maintenance</li>
<li>Show apartments &amp; process applications</li>
<li>Post rental payments &amp; transport to the bank</li>
<li>Schedule rent increases, re-certifications and lease renewals</li>
<li>Daily property walk/inspection</li>
<li>Directly Report to the Property Manager</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Desired Qualifications: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Comparable property management experience is essential</li>
<li>Supervisory experience is a benefit</li>
<li>Experience using property management software is a huge plus</li>
<li>General knowledge of repair and apartment maintenance</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Requirements: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Proficient with MS Word, Excel and Outlook</li>
<li>Strong administrative &amp; organizational skills</li>
<li>Excellent verbal &amp; written communication</li>
<li>The ability to multi-task</li>
<li>Analytical &amp; problem solving skills</li>
<li>Ability to speak effectively to all clients, ability to write clear and professional business correspondence, ability to understand contracts and documents typical to the management of real estate</li>
<li>Professional attire and demeanor</li>
</ul>
<p>Job Type: Full-time</p>
<p>Salary: $11.50 to $12.50 /hour</p>
<p>Work authorization:</p>
<ul><li>United States (Required)</li></ul>
</div>
