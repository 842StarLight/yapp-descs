<h2>Paid Search and Social Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">Paid Search Social Managers are the day-to-day focal point of the account. They are expected to have a complete and unquestioned understanding of their assigned clients search/social initiatives and client's overall business and marketing objectives. The SSM is steward of the planning process and is expected to provide mid-level strategic recommendations to the SSS, sales and/or the client. The position is usually supported by the ASSM and reports to at least a Search and Social Supervisor level
<br><br>
Skills/Qualifications
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor's degree in Advertising, Marketing, Business, Statistics, Finance, or Economics preferred</li>
<li>3+ years' experience in buying and optimizing campaigns in the Paid Search and/or Social digital space</li>
<li>2+ years' experience and proven track record of managing and exceeding client's goals on digital advertising campaigns</li>
<li>Strong experience buying media through Google and Bing and Facebook</li>
<li>Direct Response experience preferred but not required</li>
<li>Strong analytical and project management skills</li>
<li>Exceptional written and verbal communication skills</li>
<li>Professional and advanced skills in excel – pivot tables, chart making, etc.</li>
</ul>
<b>Duties:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Provide recommendations on budget planning for renewals and new accounts based on the Google keyword and Matchcraft estimator tools</li>
<li>Keyword analysis – build keyword lists, analyze search volume and make recommendations</li>
<li>In-platform setup of paid social and search campaigns</li>
<li>Text creative copywriting for paid search &amp; social</li>
<li>Day-to-day management of performance including oversight of bid recommendations, execution details, budgets/accounting to meet goals and KPIs of clients</li>
<li>Maintain and track ROI and ad spend across all accounts on a monthly basis</li>
<li>Keep up to date with best practices, bid management systems, keyword development tools and industry research</li>
<li>Pull and analyze daily campaign reports</li>
<li>Generate monthly reporting and performance insights that lead to gains against client business objectives</li>
<li>Join and lead client kick off calls</li>
<li>Training of associate search and social managers and oversight of their deliverables, inclusive of 1:1s</li>
<li>Support CS Managers, Leads and Sellers on all search and social issues</li>
<li>Interface weekly with platform reps for smooth planning, execution and optimization of campaigns</li>
<li>Assist the senior team members with product roll outs and presentations</li>
</ul>
