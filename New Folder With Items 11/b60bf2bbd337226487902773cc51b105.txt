<h2>Sales Development Representative</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>ACGI Software is in search of a bright and highly motivated professional to play a key role on its sales team. This individual will report directly to the Vice President of Sales and Marketing.</p>
<p>As a Sales Development Representative ("SDR"), you will be the initial point of contact with prospective customers, working at the top of the sales funnel on a variety of lead generation campaigns and marketing initiatives. We will invest in helping you be successful by educating you on ACGI’s software products, our industry, and how to sell our solutions. It’s a team effort. Regular interaction and collaboration with members of ACGI’s sales and marketing team are critical to meeting your success and the company’s overall revenue objectives.</p>
<p>ACGI is a thriving software company which continues to expand. We believe in rewarding employees for their contributions and proven results. This challenging position offers the opportunity to grow your career with a stable company, earn respect from your coworkers, and make a great income based on your performance.</p>
<p>It’s not all about the hard work. Our culture encourages maintaining a balance between work and home, and is part of the recipe of success. We provide a rewarding work environment that’s family-friendly, and has lots of camaraderie. We enjoy a professional, yet calm office atmosphere to make the work day both productive and comfortable for employees.</p>
<p><b>Primary Responsibilities: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Prospecting is number one – generate leads and qualify incoming demand from call-ins, website inquiries, trade shows, referrals, etc. Establishing contact, developing relationships, and disciplined follow up with prospects by phone, email, and social networks.</li>
<li>Schedule in-person meetings and demonstrations for outside sales reps</li>
<li>Distribute and fulfill requests for product information and other marketing materials</li>
<li>Enter, track, and maintain sales opportunities in the sales database</li>
<li>Assist the marketing team to review content, enhance campaigns, and drive demand</li>
<li>Participate in trade shows, industry events, and ACGI’s software users conference</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Qualifications for Success in this Position: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Strong work ethic, determination, and persistence to meet all responsibilities</li>
<li>Goal-oriented - must be able to reach and exceed quarterly and annual objectives</li>
<li>Aptitude to learn and be able to articulate ACGI’s business and technical</li>
<li>Interpersonal skills and business savvy to develop relationships over the phone</li>
<li>Disciplined to document sales-related activities weekly, monthly, and quarterly</li>
<li>Exceptional oral and written communication skills</li>
<li>A self starter with a positive attitude and ability to work in an entrepreneurial environment</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Experience and Education: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Minimum of 3 years of experience in competitive business-to-business sales (technical / software sales preferred)</li>
<li>Has received some training in competitive selling (solution-based selling a plus!)</li>
<li>Proven track record of meeting quota</li>
<li>An understanding of software, the Internet / Cloud, enterprise systems (ie. CRM, AMS, content management, etc.)</li>
<li>PC Proficient with office productivity applications - Microsoft Windows, Microsoft Office Suite, and the Internet</li>
<li>Experience using sales contact database or salesforce automation systems (Salesforce, Zoho, NetSuite, ACT, etc.)</li>
<li>Basic presentation skills</li>
<li>Knowledge of professional and trade associations, non-profit organizations, and/or certification industries (a plus, but not required)</li>
<li>Undergraduate degree preferred</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Base Salary &amp; Bonuses: </b>Commensurate with experience and proven skills</p>
<p><b>Employee Benefits: </b>Paid holidays; paid vacation; sick leave; group life insurance; group health insurance, dependent coverage available; 401K retirement plan; profit sharing; free parking</p>
