<h2>Consumer Insights Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>Gamut, a subsidiary of Cox Media Group, is a solutions-based digital advertising organization focused on connecting brands to their most relevant consumers and communities, across all platforms. Gamut and its OTT product – Gamut TOTAL – empower advertisers through guaranteed premium inventory, advanced fraud-free options and maximized campaign performance. Founded on a century of success laid down by our parent company Cox Enterprises, Gamut delivers effective advertising campaigns that combine over 20 years of experience in the digital space with data, insights and quality inventory. For more information on Gamut, please visit: http://gamut.media/.</div>
<br>
<div>
</div>
<div>
The Consumer Insights Manager will lead data-driven research projects that align with sales objectives and client needs. Through analysis of multiple research sources, he/she will support the media ad sales team to consult clients on their advertising strategies. The right candidate will possess the curiosity to gain a deep understanding of consumer behavior and leverage their research expertise to inform and influence. The Consumer Insights Manager will report to the Senior Insights Manager, oversee a team of analysts and collaborate with the Sales, Marketing, Analytics, and Strategy Departments.<br>
<br>
</div>
<div>
<b>Ad-Hoc Research Analysis &amp; Present Findings</b>
</div>
<ul>
<li><div>Synthesize raw data culled from various syndicated research providers to transform findings into creative, powerful and actionable insights</div></li>
<li><div>
Strategically leverage insights that support Account Executives to drive sales revenue growth by aligning client needs with research analysis to uncover marketing solutions</div></li>
<li>
<div>
Clearly display insights through written, designed and/or presented deliverables that effectively communicate the message</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
</li>
</ul>
