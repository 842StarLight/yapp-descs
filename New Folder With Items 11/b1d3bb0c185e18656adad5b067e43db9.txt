<h2>Entry-Level Solution Architect</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>Our Associate Solution Architects use their technical knowledge/expertise to support, maintain and enhance software tools, applications for our insurance software products and services. Emphasis is on designing solutions on existing products/services as well as involvement with phases of new product development under the mentorship of our senior architects.</p>
<p><b>
Responsibilities include:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
<p>Support/maintenance of internal tools by troubleshooting/debugging issues and proposing development solutions; testing/executing performance analysis; creating/maintaining technical documentation; suggesting solutions for process improvement.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Project work related to the implementation of existing internal tools and new development/architecture which includes: Scope analyzation, requirements determination and estimate preparation; designing/architecting solutions, writing/implementing development specifications, etc.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Provide technical subject matter expertise for complex issues, lead and mentor others with reviewing designs</p>
</li>
<li>
