<h2>Customer Support Representative</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<div><div><div>
<p>Golden Customer Care's mission is to deliver a world-class experience to every single customer with whom we interact. We are the human touch-point for some of the largest health, wellness and beauty brands online, and we take that responsibility seriously. Through superior knowledge of our products and a drive to solve concerns quickly and thoroughly, we are able to maintain high levels of customer satisfaction.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>POSITION OBJECTIVE:</b></p>
<p>The Customer Service Representative will assist customers with questions concerning products, shipping, billing activities on customer accounts. This position takes calls on the phone queue and is expected to meet or exceed goals set in; quality assurance, number of calls taken, wait times and length of call. Ability to consistently deliver positive customer service is key. High level of teamwork and cooperation within the Inbound – Customer Service team is required to meet our customer satisfaction goals.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>ESSENTIAL FUNCTIONS:</b></p>
<p>Handle heavy call volumes.</p>
<p>Correct any errors on the customer accounts when encountered.</p>
<p>Research and reconcile problems concerning charges and payments</p>
<p>Correct/update customer information as appropriate</p>
<p>Document accounts with actions taken/customer requests.</p>
<p>Place orders for customers as requested.</p>
<p>Track shipments</p>
<p>Meeting occupancy goals and QA’s scores on each call.</p>
<p>Supporting and back filling customer relations team as needed.</p>
<p>Other related duties as assigned.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>OTHER REQUIREMENTS AND PHYSICAL REQUIREMENTS:</b></p>
<p>Regular attendance is required.</p>
<p>Must be able to cope with typical job stress.</p>
<p>May require variable work schedule or extra hours.</p>
<p>Must be able to develop and maintain effective working relationships with manager, other managers, supervisors, other team members and sales agents.</p>
<p>Maintain strictest confidentiality and discretion regarding customer information.</p>
<p>Supporting and back filling customer relations team as needed.</p>
<p>Other related duties as assigned.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
