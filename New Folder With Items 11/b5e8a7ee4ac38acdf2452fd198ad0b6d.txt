<b>About the team:
</b></p>
<p>ACBS (Advanced Commercial Banking System) is a comprehensive commercial lending and trading system that consists of integrated functional modules that support the lending process from deal building through servicing and trading. The key to the ACBS product suite is connection– between front office and back office, arrangers and investors, and buyers and sellers. Our connected suite of products makes loans easier to originate, syndicate, trade, and service for both sell side and buy side firms – and we are continually adding new connections between products and market participants.<br>
</p>
