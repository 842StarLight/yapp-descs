<div><div>
<p>Do you have an interest in sales but want to apply your technical skills? Launch your sales career with the CenturyLink Sales Academy – Sales Engineer Program! We are currently seeking driven and highly motivated individuals to join our sales team and grow your career with a Fortune 200 Company. Participate in an immersive CenturyLink Enterprise Sales Academy to learn the technical and professional fundamentals to facilitate success. Learn through classroom, Virtual Instructor-Led (VILT), and On-the-Job Training (OJT) of CenturyLink’s full product and solution portfolio. Sales, Business and Technical training courses will increase overall knowledge of CenturyLink solutions and go-to-market strategies. The possibilities are endless!</p>
<p><b>
About Our Sales Academy Program Experience:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Temporary relocation to our Broomfield, CO office for our classroom based – 12 week training program.</li>
<li>
Learn technical and professional fundamentals for success from proven sales leaders.</li>
<li>
Understand CenturyLink’s full product and solutions portfolio to increase overall knowledge for go-to market strategies.</li>
<li>
Network with a group of other sales trainees, sales leaders and business executives.</li>
<li>
Transition into an Engineering Support Associate role in a major U.S. market territory Nashville, TN after the completion of the Sales Academy training program.</li>
</ul>
</div></div>

<b>
Job Description</b>
</div>
<div>
<p><b>Sound Interesting? Specifics for our Sales Engineer Role:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Work with the Sales and Sales Engineering teams to develop and document customer requirements, solution designs, service configurations, and project scopes of work in support of the delivery of network and IT solutions to Customers.</li>
<li>
Assist field Sales Engineering teams to identify and develop additional revenue opportunities.</li>
<li>
As needed, provide general discovery, support, and education to customers and internal Centurylink personnel on questions or issues arising from services sold.</li>
<li>
Research, define and document service change requests where needed in support of service modification.</li>
<li>
Work with customer and company personnel to help identify and define technical problems, determine and design the solution and assist customer in implementing the fix.</li>
<li>
Collaborate with the Sales and Sales Engineering teams to uncover and define customer solution and business development strategies, including the recommendation and communication of technology roadmaps to Customers.</li>
<li>
Where needed, present Centurylink capabilities, solution concepts, and technology roadmaps to both internal and external audiences in both formal and informal business settings</li>
<li>
Upon request, participate in marketing and networking events and activities with Customer technical and business contacts.</li>
<li>
Provide continuous feedback to Sales Engineering leadership on where processes, services, tools, and capabilities can be improved or evolved to ensure ongoing improvement in Sales Engineering efficiency and service innovation</li>
</ul>
