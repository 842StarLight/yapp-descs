<p><b>We currently have an immediate need for a Customer Care Specialist</b><b>.</b></p>
<p></p>
<p><b>JOB SUMMARY</b></p>
<p>The Customer Care Specialist is a key contributor to our overall Customer Service performance. Processes advance replacements/repairs under existing Service Contracts and pay as you go. Upsells to Service Contracts by offering current plans and providing leads to the Services Business Development team. The ideal candidate would be customer focused, able to follow defined processes and assertively deal with different departments within the company, able to quickly deal with new and different situations, while staying focused on our overall commitment to customer satisfaction.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>RESPONSIBILITIES</b></p>
<ul><li> The Customer Care Specialist will work closely with our Repair &amp; Return, customer support and sales teams to ensure Aviat Networks is delivering overall superior service and support to our customers. Analyze customer specific situation and discuss different Service Contracts options.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Acting as main point of customer contact for RMA via phone, web and email.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Respond to customer inquiries regarding the status of current RMAs /orders /shipping.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Input and manage customer RMA data in Aviat’s Clarify system.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Verify Customer entitlement, Service Contracts and Specific turnaround times agreed upon.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Track inbound and outbound RMA shipments.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Co-ordinate the return of product between our customer and our different warehouses/inventory locations.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Work with our vendors for OEM RMA’s ups regarding product traceability, quality concerns and statistical failure information.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Understand different level of Maintenance Level Agreements (MLA) offering and discuss with pay as you go (PAYG) customers benefits of having a Support Contract in place and will handover to Business Development team when needed.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Run Status/RMA reports</li></ul>
<ul><li> Communicate internally as to the current status of all RMA/Investigative activity</li></ul>
<ul><li> Identify opportunities for process improvement.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Escalate issues to Management when needed.</li></ul>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>EDUCATION/EXPERIENCE</b></p>
<p>Candidate will interface directly with hardware, firmware and software engineers. The ideal candidate will be resourceful, enthusiastic, self-motivated, and possess excellent communication and interpersonal skills.</p>
<p></p>
<ul><li> High School degree required; Associate and/or college degree preferred</li></ul>
<ul><li> 3+ years as a Customer Service or Inside Sales representative with direct contact with customers in a high-tech environment preferred.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Strong written and verbal communications skills</li></ul>
<ul><li> Knowledge of and experience in working with Windows-based computer applications, including Microsoft Outlook, Word, and Excel</li></ul>
<ul><li> Proven ability to multi-task and adapt to change, flexible, assertive and willing to help grow the business</li></ul>
<ul><li> Pleasant telephone manner and professional etiquette</li></ul>
<ul><li> Self-starter with the ability to work with minimal day to day supervision</li></ul>
