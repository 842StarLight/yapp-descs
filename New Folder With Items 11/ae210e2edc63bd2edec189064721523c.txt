<div>The Johnson &amp; Johnson Supply Chain Digital &amp; Analytics (“JJSC DNA”) group is seeking a Senior Manager of Data Engineering to enable Johnson &amp; Johnson Operating System (JJOS) to make better business decision using unified data and democratizing the data model. In addition, senior manager would work closely with business and IT functions to deliver data projects in timely and cost effective manner.<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<div><b>Responsibilities</b></div>
<div></div>
<ul>
<li>Manage portfolio of data projects and work closely with business and IT functions to deliver data projects in timely and cost effective manner
</li>
<li>Manage development of provisioning data pipes for reporting and data science
</li>
<li>Map the base data elements which needs to be transformed and provisioned
</li>
<li>Work with business to get understanding of their data requirements</li>
<li>
Help Business improve process and help business to make data driven decisions</li>
</ul>
<br>
<br>
<br>
<b>Qualifications</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s and/or Master’s Degree in engineering or related discipline, e.g. statistics, operations research, computer science, mathematics; computer science degree highly preferred</li>
<li>
Proven ability to understand massive disparate data sets and transform the information into clear insights for the business.</li>
<li>
Experience of working with IT and application development functions</li>
<li>
Knowledge of ERP systems
</li>
<li>Experience in one or BI software tools like Tableau, Qlik, Power BI, etc.</li>
<li>
Strong communication and influencing skills.</li>
<li>
8 years of work experience in relevant work experience in data analysis or related field.</li>
<li>
Strong demonstrated understanding of data lifecycle required around data ingestion (ingest data from disparate systems into cloud computing environment), data contextualization (merging large data sets, developing algorithms to merge and clean data); data insights and analytics is required.</li>
<li>
Familiarity with different types of databases and business applications of distributed computing (e.g. MapReduce, HIVE, HADOOP).</li>
<li>
Demonstrated experience in setting up and managing data in cloud computing environment (AWS or MS Azure) highly preferred.</li>
<li>
People leader experience required.</li>
<li>
Familiarity with large datasets, and understanding of data analysis workflows is required.</li>
