<h2>Business Development Representative</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>The Business Development Representative is responsible for the generation of qualified sales leads for the VIPRE malware protection line.</p>
<p>
In this role, it is essential to be result-oriented, versatile, and motivated to drive and grow business for a region or sector within the US or Canada.</p>
<p><b>
Duties and Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Generating leads in a designated geographical area or business sector through high-volume outbound calling campaigns</li>
<li>
Creation of targeted call lists</li>
<li>
Take the initiative to thoroughly learn the market as well as products and services</li>
<li>
Use knowledge of company products and services to promote product sales</li>
<li>
Accurately document and manage opportunities in SalesForce.com</li>
<li>
Transition prospective leads to Territory Account Manager for approval</li>
<li>
Performs other duties as required</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Essential Qualifications</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Must have working knowledge of Windows, MS Office and CRM</li>
<li>
Excellent phone presence</li>
<li>
Ability to present value add for products and services in a concise fashion to generate interest</li>
<li>
Ability to work independently and in a team environment</li>
<li>
Excellent communication and human relation skills, including verbal and written communications</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Preferred Skills</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Experience of working within a high-velocity inside sales environment</li>
<li>
Knowledge of Salesforce and Software Sales</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Education and/or Certification Requirements</b></p>
<ul><li>
HS Diploma required. Two (2) or Four (4) year college degree preferred.</li></ul>
