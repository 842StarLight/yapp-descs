<h2>Audio Visual Director</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>NATURE OF THE WORK</p>
<p>Direct all processes, employees and equipment of the Audio Visual Department including sales, production and hiring. This work can be very mentally and physically challenging as well as require long hours at certain times of the year.</p>
<p>POSITION TASKS AND ACTIVITIES</p>
<ul><li>Management of all areas of Audio Visual Production, inventory and sales.</li></ul>
<ul><li>In charge of all scheduling, management and expectations being met of all audio-visual employees.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Attends weekly Monday Production Meeting.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Attends weekly Monday meeting with General Manager and Warehouse manager to coordinate weekly joint delivery-pickups.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Sets the weekly route log previous day. Office manager will make appropriate adjustments to daily changes when necessary.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Supervises all training of employees in department.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Manages all pay being received for events.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Manages all employee hours and having them recorded correctly and on time for payroll.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Manage labor of department keeping in contact with management about issues.</li></ul>
<ul><li>In charge of all customer service for the Audio Visual Department.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Supervises AV Tech 1 in IT work for the entire company and assists when necessary.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Supervises AV Tech 2 in live sound related items and assists when necessary.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Research industry standards and new technology to further inventory and knowledge in the company.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Complete tasks being given by management and the owners in the deadlines given.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Create and manage an avenue of good communication for the the department throughout the company.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Take steps to assure department is growing and that we are doing everything possible to acquire new business.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Develop department tools such as venue information, job layouts, client preferences etc. and have those tools easily available to all AV staff.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Responsible for training of all staff members in department as well as ongoing job training.</li></ul>
<p>EDUCATION SKILLS AND REQUIREMENTS</p>
<ul><li>Able to fluently utilize all Microsoft office applications, the rental software used by PCP, google docs and email. Must be able to head up IT for the company training and assuring knowledge levels of the Office Manager and AV Tech 1.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Must be able to do basic math and computer problem solving.</li></ul>
<ul><li>College degree or equal job experience required.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Must be able to type at least 35 words a minute.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Knowledgeable of all A/V services and products.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Must be able to climb a ladder, drive a forklift and operate a scissor lift.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Able to communicate effectively with A/V staff as well as all other departments for Peter Corvallis Productions.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Must be able to fill in for director as department head in their absence.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Must be able to lift 70 pounds.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Must have full range of motion and dexterity.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Must have a clean driving record and able to safely operate company vehicles.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Must have a flexible schedule including nights, mornings, weekends, weekdays and holidays.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Must be able to take responsibility for your own actions and the actions of others in the department</li></ul>
<p>Job Type: Full-time</p>
<p>Experience:</p>
<ul><li>relevant: 2 years (Preferred)</li></ul>
<p>Work Location:</p>
<ul><li>One location</li></ul>
<p>Benefits:</p>
<ul>
<li>Health insurance</li>
<li>Dental insurance</li>
<li>Vision insurance</li>
<li>Paid time off</li>
</ul>
