<h2>Mortgage Advisor</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<h1 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Salal Credit Union is looking for a Mortgage Advisor to join our team!</b></h1>
<p>
Our Mortgage Advisors receive sales leads from branch employees and other departments and serve as the liaison between back-office mortgage lending staff and members by providing excellent communication between all parties. To generate more business and mortgage opportunities, Mortgages Advisors establish and develop outside contacts with organizations, realtors, lenders, and other affinity sources to market mortgage loan services to increase lending market share with quality loan volume. We expect these folks to connect with coworkers, members and/or external customers by providing friendly and efficient service while working together to solve problems quickly. This role is a great opportunity for someone who is interested in helping our members meet their goals of owning a home! With a base salary plus bonus compensation structure, the earning potential can vary. This advisor would be expected to do some traveling between our 5 area branches.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Essential duties include:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Assist branch staff and members with mortgage questions and inquiries.</li>
<li>
Outreach to existing members via phone or e-mail for sales opportunities.</li>
<li>
Follow-up on assigned mortgage referrals from branch staff and other departments, and work to develop leads into loan applications.</li>
<li>
Meet with members at various branch locations and report to the corporate office for meetings as requested.</li>
<li>
Collection of documentation and completion of assigned loan applications. Review new mortgage applications to ensure compliance and accuracy.</li>
<li>
Monitor mortgage rates daily, communicate with members regarding rate locks, and submit rate lock requests to back-office staff.</li>
<li>
Expedite loans in process by regularly reviewing pipeline. Correspond with borrowers, back-office team, and other third-parties throughout transaction to ensure a smooth and transparent loan process.</li>
<li>
Proactively refer members and prospective members to Personal Financial Representatives and Member Service Representatives by marketing all Credit Union products and services as opportunities are identified.</li>
<li>
Establish and develop outside contacts with organizations, realtors, lenders, and other affinity sources to market mortgage loan services to increase lending market share with quality loan volume.</li>
<li>
Provide exceptional customer service by maintaining thorough knowledge of lending programs, policies, procedures and regulatory requirements.
</li>
<li>Demonstrate a commitment to professional ethics in the performance of all lending activities and ensures compliance with all Federal and State regulations.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Qualifications:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
One year of financial industry or related sales experience required.
</li>
<li>Mortgage industry experience preferred.</li>
<li>
High School Diploma or GED</li>
</ul>
