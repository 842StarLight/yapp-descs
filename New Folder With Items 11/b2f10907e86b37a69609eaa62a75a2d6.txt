<h2>Sales Engineer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Mailgun is seeking a customer-facing hands-on technologist with a track record of success to join our growing team. As a <b>Sales Engineer</b>, you will represent Mailgun to prospective customers and the development community at-large, develop a deep understanding of customer requirements in pre-sales conversations, and develop a comprehensive solution for how Mailgun with integrate with a customer's application.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p>This position will report directly into our Head of Product and will be directly responsible for driving a tight feedback loop between what our customers need and what we are building.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Our Team</b></h3>
<ul>
<li>We believe in contributing back to the community. Many of the tools and libraries we’ve developed are open source (https://github.com/mailgun)</li>
<li>We move fast and don’t believe in having unnecessary silos and red tape that keep teams from shipping software.</li>
<li>We have a passion for solving hard problems – our services are responsible for processing billions of messages each month and have to not only scale, but be highly reliable.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities</b></h3>
<ul>
<li>
<b>Engage </b>with customers, partners, and prospects to understand and propose solutions using Mailgun technologies.</li>
<li>
<b>Manage</b> the technical sales process for numerous customers day to day.</li>
<li>
<b>Create</b> and execute strategic account strategies and uncover additional sales and business opportunities</li>
<li>
<b>Partner</b> with sales executives to respond to respond to RFPs and technical questionnaires and help design an implementation plan and answer questions about best practices around integrating our products.
</li>
<li>
<b>Enable</b> and support sales executives in the design of technical solutions for opportunities</li>
<li>
<b>Train</b> the sales and marketing teams on new product features and ensure the team is enabled to answer questions from prospects.
</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>
<b>Monitor</b> trends in feedback from prospects and relay this information to the product management and engineering teams.</li>
<li>
<b>Develop</b> a deep understanding of competitive solutions, understand how they compare to Mailgun, and use that intelligence to respond to customer objections and win deals.</li>
<li>
<b>Author </b>content, in collaboration with Marketing, that describes the Mailgun product in a way that is authentic and resonates with developers.</li>
<li>
<b>Present </b>webinars for prospective customers to learn more about our products and features and dig deeper into real customer use cases.</li>
<li>
<b>Evangelize</b> the development community through the authoring of technical blogs and whitepapers that represent how Mailgun can best be leveraged.</li>
<li>
<b>Communicate</b> Mailgun’s technical leadership and value at conferences by partnering with marketing to develop speaking proposals for conferences where Mailgun should have a strategic presence.
</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Requirements</b></p>
<ul>
<li>At least 2+ years experience and success working in a customer-facing technical role</li>
<li>Experience seeing the entire lifecycle from conception to design to technology evaluation to pilot program to implementation to maintenance and support</li>
<li>A strong understanding of the software development lifecycle, with the ability to represent customers needs to our CTO and Product Team with the purpose of influencing our product development roadmap</li>
<li>Experience in one or more of the following areas: web development, hosting services, email development, and real-time communications.</li>
<li>A firm understanding of enterprise integration best practices, service oriented architecture and models for sharing IT services across the enterprise.
</li>
<li>A world class communicator. Confident dispensing knowledge to a highly skilled and experienced audience including C-level executives.
</li>
<li>Ability to switch context quickly throughout the day with numerous competing demands.</li>
<li>Demonstrated experience gathering and understanding customer business requirements</li>
<li>Bachelor's degree in Engineering, Computer Science, MIS or a comparable field is preferred.
</li>
</ul>
