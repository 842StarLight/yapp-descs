<p><b>About Us</b></p>
<p>At Displayr, our mission is to make it easier for everyone to discover and share the stories in their data. We have two products we’re pretty proud of – Displayr, a revolutionary BI app (data science + data visualization), and Q, a stats package for market research.</p>
<p>From humble start up beginnings, we now have strong global footprints! We are built on the rock-solid foundation of our awesome products. The sales of Q and Displayr fund our (competitive) salaries, and even more importantly - allows us to keep growing safely. Our team of creative and hard-working employees has grown from 12 to 47 in the last four years.</p>
<p><b>About The Job</b></p>
<p>If you are a quantitative marketer researcher looking for change in direction, this is the perfect role for you.</p>
<p>You’ll be joining some of the best and brightest in the data science and market research space. This is a totally unique role and will suit someone looking to blend their technical knowledge and skills with their passion for helping others learn and be successful.</p>
<p>Reporting to the Head of User Support &amp; Enablement , you’ll be:</p>
<ul>
<li>Providing technical support for Displayr and Q users – a chance to flex your creative problem-solving muscles.</li>
<li>Building interactive online dashboards and reporting solutions to illustrate the benefits of Displayr to clients.</li>
<li>Creating content to support the software (e.g. webinars, videos, blog posts, and technical documentation)</li>
</ul>
<p><b>About You</b></p>
<p>Essential</p>
<ul>
<li>Strong understanding of data analysis involving respondent-level data. (ideally survey data).</li>
<li>Two years' experience analyzing data and creating data visualizations in a commercial environment.</li>
<li>Technically proficient in using software (and teaching yourself how to use it) – ideally SPSS, R, Q</li>
<li>Passion for helping people learn technical skills/programming.</li>
<li>Strong communication skills, both written and verbal.</li>
<li>Based in London/UK with full working rights with the proven ability to work remotely.</li>
</ul>
