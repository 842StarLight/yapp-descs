<h2>Digital Director</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>PURPOSE of JOB: </b></p>
<p>The digital director will serve as the driving force behind Hour Media’s digital presence. This person must possess big-picture vision with the drive and attention to detail to make their vision a reality. This candidate will be responsible for managing digital advertising opportunities for a publishing company with over 20 consumer websites. They will lead and oversee a digital staff and hold them accountable for implementing, executing and delivering successful results.</p>
<p><b>RESPONSIBILITIES: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Define and communicate a product roadmap aligned with company objectives, and supported by business cases, market research, customer data and competitive analysis.</li>
<li>Work closely with advertising operations, advertising sales, and editorial-balancing efficiency, quality customer experience, and a long-term value generation.</li>
<li>Create high-end digital recommendations including social media marketing, online promotions, mobile and website solutions, SEO/SEM, online targeted display and video advertising.</li>
<li>Provide ongoing sales training and support to their specified advertising team.</li>
<li>Lead strategic planning and implementation of company digital goals and objectives.</li>
<li>Assist markets in planning for long-term growth by analyzing current digital markets trends and staying informed on our competition and new opportunities.</li>
<li>Work efficiently and proactively throughout all departments within Hour Media, while collectively sharing a common goal for overall company success.</li>
<li>Work with business and editorial teams to conceive and execute integrated "go-to-market" client facing strategies for all things digital</li>
<li>Be accountable for achieving digital advertising and traffic goals.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>EDUCATION &amp; REQUIRED EXPERIENCE: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s Degree or equivalent combination of education/experience and training.</li>
<li>At least 3 years of prior sales experience in media is preferred.</li>
<li>At least 3 years of prior experience providing a variety of digital multimedia solutions such as website sales, desktop/mobile display, native content, SEM/SEO, social media and email marketing.</li>
<li>Must have the ability to identify the current need of our clients and communicate the benefits to our digital marketing solutions as they relate to each need.</li>
<li>Possess the ability to research problems and provide client focused solutions.</li>
<li>Demonstrate the ability to grow market share.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>DEMONSTRATABLE SKILLS: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>
<b>Leadership – </b>Takes responsibility from inception to outcomes. Determines needs, aligns priorities, delivers to market, measures outcomes, and seeks to build upon success.</li>
<li>
<b>Strategic Thinker </b>– Understands business objectives and converts them into clear product plans, follows through in execution.</li>
<li>
<b>Goal-Oriented</b> – Analytically oriented, this person must use hard data to assess opportunities, metrics driven, ruthless in prioritization, overcomes obstacles to achieve business outcomes.</li>
<li>
<b>Driven and motivates others</b> – Takes Initiative, sets context, listens and finds compromises.</li>
<li>Understands technology and keeps current with the latest trends.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>TECHNICAL SKILLS: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Knowledge of WordPress, Photoshop and a working knowledge of HTML.</li>
<li>Must have excellent computer skills and be proficient in Microsoft Word, PowerPoint, Excel, and Outlook.</li>
</ul>
