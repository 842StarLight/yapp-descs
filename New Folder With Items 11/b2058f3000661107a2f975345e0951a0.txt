<h2>Regional Account Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>I. Spiewak &amp; Sons is currently seeking a Regional Account Manager for our corporate office in New York City. The ideal candidate will provide optimal customer support while working closely with Outside Sales Representatives to effectively manage and grow both existing accounts and potential accounts. This job will be an exciting opportunity for anyone who enjoys building strong client relationships, is organized and skilled at problem solving, and is an ambitious customer oriented person who can thrive in a fast paced office environment. This position will report directly to the Director of Sales Administration.</p>
<p>Essential duties include:</p>
<ul>
<li>Customer account management for multiple accounts offering the highest level of customer service.</li>
<li>Work closely with an assigned Outside Sales Representative to actively manage and grow a dedicated multi-state territory that includes pre-existing customer base and an extensive list of new business lead opportunities.</li>
<li>Approve and process orders, inventory management, pricing and quotations, stock style plan management.</li>
<li>Maintain contact with and develop plans for the continued growth and development of the customer accounts.</li>
<li>Study and learn the Spiewak product line to effectively demonstrate and explain important product features and benefits to customers.</li>
<li>Help oversee managed contracts and pricing agreement for key accounts.</li>
<li>Assist in the development and implementation of customized programs for large accounts.</li>
<li>Help management create new avenues for growing business where our products could be utilized.</li>
<li>Work closely and effectively communicate with internal support departments – including Marketing, Finance, IT, Design and Manufacturing.</li>
</ul>
<p>Other job requirements and skills include:</p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree a must. Master’s degree a plus.</li>
<li>3-5 years of customer service experience with a proven track record of successfully managing large accounts.</li>
<li>Account management experiences a plus.</li>
<li>Energetic, hard-working team player that can also thrive working independently.</li>
<li>Great organizational and effective time management skills to function in a fast paced, multi-task environment.</li>
<li>Strong oral and written communication skills.</li>
<li>Excellent computer software skills, i.e. Word, Excel, and Internet skills.</li>
<li>Industry experience is not required.</li>
</ul>
