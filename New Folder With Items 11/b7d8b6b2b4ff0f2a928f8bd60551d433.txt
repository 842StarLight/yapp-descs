ABOUT THE ROLE</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
This is an exciting time for Centro, and a unique opportunity to join a new team, help define a new role within the Platform Solutions and Implementation organization, and increase Basis platform’s exposure in the marketplace! As a Basis Solutions Specialist, you will be responsible for providing strategic pre-sale technical support to Centro’s sales team pitching Basis Platform. As enthusiastic experts on Basis products, you will share valuable insight and recommendations on how our products can meet customer’s needs, solve or improve challenges, and create workflow efficiencies. You will utilize both your technical aptitude and exemplary communication skills to help us bring on new platform partnerships. The Basis Specialists will also work closely with the product development teams, ensuring customer feedback and needs are communicated as we learn from our conversations in the marketplace.</div>
