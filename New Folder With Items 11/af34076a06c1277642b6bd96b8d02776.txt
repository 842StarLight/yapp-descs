<h1 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Outside Security Consultant</b></h1>
<p>VideoSurveillance.com secures facilities and at-risk assets for our clients. Our team manages a rapidly-growing business that is widely considered an innovator in our industry. We offer a dynamic <i>work</i> environment and excellent compensation and benefits alongside world-class people. Our company is backed <i>by</i> a leading investment firm, providing stability and future growth <i>opportunities</i> for our team.
</p>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>The Position</b></h3>
<p>VideoSurveillance.com is hiring energetic and professional Account Executives to serve our clients in Portland. Our Account Executives are outside security consultants, working with our clients in the field to assess their risks and implement security measures to prevent losses and criminal activity before they happen. Successful Account Executives make a great first impression, work tirelessly on behalf of clients, and thrive in a competitive environment.
</p>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities:</b></h3>
<p>Our Account Executives' specific responsibilities include:
</p>
<ul>
<li>Generating profitable revenue and meeting sales targets.</li>
<li>Managing the business and sales relationship with key <i>client</i> accounts.
</li>
<li>Developing and qualifying leads within territory to close sales <i>opportunities</i>.</li>
<li>Coordinating <i>client</i> services with key company personnel, including Operations, Support, Central Station, and Leadership teams.</li>
<li>Continuously learning and applying new technologies, products and services, security approaches, and <i>career</i> development.</li>
</ul>

<li>Sales mentality; "Fire in the Belly".</li>
