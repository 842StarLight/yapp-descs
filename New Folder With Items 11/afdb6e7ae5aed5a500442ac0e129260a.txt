<h2>Business Applications Specialist Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>The Business Applications Specialist Manager is a senior leader within our US Financial Services sales organization. The Business Applications Specialist Manager leads, develops and manages a team of high performing Specialist Managers and Specialists seller.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
You will accelerate business by building ambitious sales plans to capitalize on transformational shifts occurring in the market place. You will develop smart selling solutions with partners to focus on business value, make deals bigger, faster, and more impactful. You will be a market-influencer able to leverage a strong and active Business Decision Maker network and challenge Sellers with insights and perspectives that empower them to adopt new practices, think differently and accelerate business growth. You are disciplined in business management and thrive in a complex solution selling world.</div>
