<h2>Video Producer, Jr.</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<div><div><div>
<h4 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>The Video Producer, Jr is responsible for conceptualizing, storyboarding, shooting, editing, and designing on-screen graphics for all video content, designed to engage, educate, or entertain target audiences.<br>
</b></h4>
</div></div></div>
<p><b>Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Collaborate with the creative group, marketing team, and sales department to develop video solutions for magazine content (editorial and social media videos) as well as internal training, company promotion, and customer communication</li>
<li>Develop creative briefs and storyboards for video solutions</li>
<li>Plan, write, and produce scripts</li>
<li>Coordinate all aspects of video production</li>
<li>Plan and facilitate studio and field-based video production including camera operation, editing, set and lighting design, and audio production</li>
<li>Work cross functionally to offer media production support for all areas of the sales and marketing team</li>
<li>Edit video content, making revisions as needed, following brand guidelines</li>
<li>Work on multiple projects at once</li>
<li>Design on-screen graphics for social media and editorial videos using style guide</li>
<li>Post videos to internal and external hosting for content delivery</li>
<li>Maintain directory organization of all original, working, and final files in internal server</li>
<li>May be required to travel</li>
<li>Communicate with various department heads regarding needs and improvements</li>
<li>Keep up to date with digital video trends and new platforms in an effort to remain relevant and effective in video communication</li>
<li>Apply knowledge about company policies, procedures, and pricing to best meet client needs</li>
<li>Perform all other related duties as assigned</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Education:</b><br>

<p><b>Experience:</b></p>

<li>Experience in marketing and communications a plus</li>

<li>Experience with studio and field lighting, green screen, chroma-keying, video compression, and audio recording</li>
<li>Passion for storytelling and a creative mind to make ideas happen</li>
<li>Highly detail oriented and attentive to details</li>
<li>Ability to perform in a high-pressure environment while adhering to strict deadlines</li>
