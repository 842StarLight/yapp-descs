<p>
Mountain News (MNC) is seeking a Digital Content Marketing Specialist for it’s North American and Southern Hemisphere digital properties. MNC, the publisher of OnTheSnow.com and Skiinfo, has the largest media-addressable database of Mountain Travel consumers in the world, with over 36M users. The Digital Content Marketing Specialist at Mountain News Corporation, reporting to the Senior Manager, Content Strategy, will manage and grow the properties by executing the digital content strategies to drive audience acquisition, retention and engagement. You will enhance our core asset of mountain resort and snow data and deliver best in class digital content to drive audience engagement. The job will require agility, a data driven approach and dedication and passion to the Mountain Travel Lifestyle to be successful.</p>
<p><b>
Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Research, write and edit new content, as well as update existing content for our websites</li>
<li>
Proactively leverage analytics to execute content and content distribution strategies with the goal to improve audience engagement driving growth in traffic and email lists.</li>
<li>
Develop a thorough yearly content calendar for the North American and Southern Hemisphere digital properties.
</li>
<li>Ensure the quality of resort and snow reporting data.</li>
<li>
Maintain the site layout, data and content via the CMS
</li>
<li>Optimize content distribution across all channels including SEO, SEM, social, app, and email.
</li>
<li>Execute and help to improve the SEO strategy.</li>
<li>
Create and send digital newsletters and push notifications</li>
<li>
Manage social accounts, internal user generated content as well as user and resort feedback emails</li>
<li>
Collaborate with the development team to improve user experience through A/B testing, page optimizations and improved hierarchy.</li>
<li>
Partner with sales and operations teams to create &amp; deliver on content marketing products for our advertisers.</li>
<li>
Manage SEO writing and snow reporting contractors</li>
</ul>
