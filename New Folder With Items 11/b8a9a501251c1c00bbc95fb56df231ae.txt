<h2>Part-time Talent Acquisition Coordinator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Checkpoint Technologies – a growing IT solutions provider focused on the professional development of its <i>team</i> members – is seeking to <i>hire</i> a driven, self-motivated, focused <i>part</i>-Talent Acquisition Coordinator. We are pushing the boundaries of the Quality Assurance technology industry and are backed <i>by</i> the top software companies within the industry. Checkpoint Technologies has assembled a world-class <i>team</i> of engineers who serve our clients in capacities such as short-term consulting engagements to long-term, <i>staff</i> augmentation projects. We have shown an uptick in engineer needs, hence our need to add an additional resource to find those world-class engineers.</p>
<p>
As the Talent Acquisition Coordinator, in addition to the <i>full</i> cycle recruiting and maintaining a pool of candidates, you will be tasked with contacting procurement and Human Resource departments to identify IT <i>contract</i> staffing needs. You must have the skill to identify and understand the <i>contract</i> staffing processes and requirements of our client organizations.</p>
<p>
If you are a professional who is: undaunted <i>by</i> technology, independent, ambitious, and money-motivated, and willing to take ownership of <i>full</i> cycle recruiting to source and introduce the best candidates – then this is the position for you! You will collaborate and strategize with all aspects of our company including Sales, Engineering, Human Resources and Management personnel to recruit amazing talent.</p>
