<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Main Purpose of Job</b></h2>
<div>The position is responsible for business attraction, expansion and retention opportunities, providing site location assistance to attract new or relocating companies, providing assistance for business growth, and identifying high growth companies within the state of Maryland.
</div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>POSITION DUTIES</b></h2>
<div>
<div>Specific job functions include lead generation, responding to marketing inquiries, managing project requests for information, providing incentive assistance and coordinating prospect visits by key decision makers to Maryland. This position works closely with county and local economic development officials, realtors, private land developers, bankers, and others to maintain knowledge of properties and buildings that are available or are proposed and monitors site and building information; analyzes location specifications and business needs and identifies target markets; recommends communities, commercial and industrial sites or buildings for possible site locations; maintains databases and records of project activities; prepares responses to Requests for Proposals; often within short frame times and also stays abreast of developments in the economic development field. The position serves as a representative of the Department and may coordinate meetings, tours and accommodations with directors of local economic development agencies, utilities and transportation representatives, business executives and local and State elected officials.<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<div>The selected employee will possess excellent interpersonal skills to work with high level public and private sector officials and maintain effective working relationships; excellent written and oral communication skills; the ability to handle multiple, concurrent and high priority projects; and proficiency with the Microsoft Office Suite for Windows including Word, Excel, Access, and PowerPoint, Google Mail and/or other relevant software. This position involves travel throughout the State and occasional national and international travel.</div>
</div>

<p>
 AND</p>
