<h2>Property Manager I</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Are you looking for an exciting challenge? Do you love managing a team to success?</b></p>
<p>ROSS Companies is one of the top tier Property Management firms in the Mid-Atlantic area and we are seeking your expertise to motivate and lead a team of highly skilled team members to provide extraordinary customer service to prospects and residents at one of our lovely multi-family communities.</p>
<p>As the Property Manager, you set the stage for our community success. In this leadership role you will lead the day-to-day operations at a multi-family community, build and nurture a strong team environment, develop, implement and handle the property budget, lead both team members and vendor relations, and demonstrate a passion for sales. With your demonstrated attention to detail, ability to multi-task and strong leadership skills ROSS will continue to be one of the leading residential management companies in the DC Metro Area and beyond.</p>
<p><b>What we would like from you:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>1-5+ years’ experience running a hotel property or multi-family housing property leasing and/or sales experience and maintenance knowledge, along with Bachelor’s Degree in Residential Property Management or Business Administration, preferred.</li>
<li>Handle Employee supervision, and administer resident, prospect, client relations</li>
<li>Hold knowledge of appropriate and effective conduct during emergency situations</li>
<li>Your excellent organizational and administrative abilities</li>
<li>Your excellent social, written, and verbal communication skills; Detail oriented with ability to analyze issues and utilize superb analytical skills in decision-making</li>
<li>Your excellent computer skills and familiarity with property management software</li>
</ul>
<p>This position will require Saturday and possibly Sunday hours.</p>

<ul>

<li>We provide 9 paid holidays.</li>
<li>401k Retirement: Save for your future with a 100% company match through investments in 16+ funds.</li>
<li>Receive a 20% discount on your rent with our Apartment Rental Discount program.</li>
<li>We provide reimbursement for part of your tuition in either undergrad or graduate degrees.</li>
<li>We offer Term Life Insurance: $10,000 without cost to you, with option to purchase additional.</li>
</ul>
