<p><b>About Digital Science</b></p>
<p><b>
Digital Science is a technology company working to make research more efficient. It invests in, nurtures and supports innovative businesses and technologies that make all parts of the research process more open and effective. The portfolio companies include admired brands including Altmetric, Dimensions, Figshare, ReadCube, Symplectic, IFI Claims, GRID, Overleaf, CC Technology, Labguru, BioRAFT, TetraScience and Transcriptic.</b></p>
<p><b>
 About the role</b></p>
<p><b>
We're growing and have created a new role to develop and lead our end to end approach to bid and proposal management. The remit ranges from working with the global sales, business and product teams to support growth of new business, to how we submit quality and compelling RFP/tender responses on time. Working cross functionally with our market segments (Institutions, Corporate, Funder &amp; Government, Publishers), Portfolio businesses and geographies it's a pivotal role that will drive change and improve our operational efficiency.</b></p>
<p><b>
 Role Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li><p><b>
Identify and/or coordinate the assessment of new business opportunities worldwide (RFIs, RFPs, Tenders, etc.)</b></p></li>
<li><p><b>
Work with our global sales team to identify new business opportunities.</b></p></li>
<li><p><b>
Project manage the process from receipt of the request through to submission and provide first line (non-sales) review of RFP documentation.</b></p></li>
<li><p><b>
Coordinate all internal resources in support of responses eg Product Management, Technical Sales, Sales Operations, Contracts, etc.</b></p></li>
<li><p><b>
Central point of contact for bids from all sales territories and the Government sector.</b></p></li>
<li><p><b>
Collaborate with the Portfolio businesses and sales to build financial models for all proposals.</b></p></li>
<li><p><b>
Oversee all aspects of proposal preparation - from coordination to edition - and develop new content as we create new products and/or features.</b></p></li>
<li><p><b>
Develop sustainable processes and content to ensure we can scale efficiently and effectively and make the best use of our resources eg build proposal handbooks, knowledge bases with boilerplate content.</b></p></li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Key Experience:</b></p>
<p><b>
You will be a key member of our new Global Business Operations team and work directly with the SVP for Global Business Operations. We are growing quickly so it's a fast paced environment and you will need to thrive working to tight deadlines across several different time zones. We are looking for people with;</b></p>
<ul>
<li><p><b>
Significant experience managing the proposal preparation process, including the ability to understand and analyze the details of an RFP/tender. Ideally this will be in the academic and/or government space for SaaS.</b></p></li>
<li><p><b>
Ability to coordinate and edit proposal input from a variety of different resources.</b></p></li>
<li><p><b>
Excellent process, time and project management skills and experience of process redesign.</b></p></li>
<li><p><b>
Strong communication skills both written and verbal English language skills.</b></p></li>
<li><p><b>
Leadership experience with confidence in dealing with all levels of management and experience of working with sales teams across different segments / markets / geographies.</b></p></li>
<li><p><b>
Extensive previous exposure to contracts and an understanding of basic contract language is a plus</b></p></li>
</ul>
<p><b>
What we offer you:</b></p>
<p><b>
An opportunity to build new ways of working and make a difference in a growing business. As well as;</b></p>
<ul>
<li><b>Start-up environment and mindset that values innovation, initiative, and energy</b></li>
<li><b>Competitive salary in line with market rate</b></li>
<li><b>The opportunity to work flexibly or from home where feasible</b></li>
<li><b>A good benefits package.</b></li>
</ul>
