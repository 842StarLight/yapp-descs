<h2>Marketing and Elementary School Admissions Assistant</h2>

<p>Immediate Open Position: Full time Marketing and Elementary School Admissions Assistant!</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
Schedule: M-F, 8 hours per day</p>
<p>
FLSA: Hourly/Non Exempt position</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
The International School provides a warm, multicultural environment where children are fully immersed in Spanish, Japanese or Chinese from Preschool through 5th grade. We are Portland’s first International Baccalaureate elementary school. Our school was founded in 1990, and today educates approximately 400 students.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
We are seeking a highly motivated full time hybrid Marketing &amp; Admissions Assistant to join our creative team to help make a difference in the lives of our children and families, and to attract new market share to our diverse community. This position reports to the Marketing and Communications Director, and the Director of Admissions.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
Are you friendly, creative, and a flexible thinker? We are in search of the right candidate with</p>
<p>
attention to detail, organized, team oriented, able to handle a creative work load, and go the</p>
<p>
extra mile in supporting our Global community. This position will work alongside to support the</p>
<p>
Marketing Director and School Admissions Director.</p>

<p><b>
Main job responsibilities:</b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><i>
Marketing duties:</i></p>
<ul>
<li>Assist as needed in the marketing and communication strategies that meet the needs of the school by utilizing best practices in graphic design to create ads, brochures, posters, annual print magazine, newsletters to maintain school identity and branding standards for all communications and marketing campaigns.</li>
<li>Coordinates the class performances at of the school’s annual International Festival.</li>
<li>Assists with the creation and production of the weekly digital newsletter, The Owl, targeted at the current parent community</li>
<li>Manages data associated with the marketing department including filing assistance, listservs, database of contacts, vendor lists, market research and survey data, photo Library, etc.</li>
<li>Point of contact for general questions from prospective and current families.</li>
<li>Supports all public relations efforts to give the school visibility and attract prospective families</li>
<li>Social Media promotion to highlight classroom activities, including photo and simple video creation.</li>
<li>Website updates / form updates</li>
<li>Support Parent Network with event planning, meetings, and community building events</li>
<li>Photograph and short video creation about school life and events.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p><b>
</b></p>
<p><b><i>Admissions duties:</i></b></p>
<ul>
<li>Support Admissions through targeted communications/ prospective family interactions (through Ravenna Software, etc)</li>
<li>Assist the Director of Admissions to cultivate relationships with prospective families through school tours, events, and other communications.</li>
<li>Admissions event preparation and support.</li>
<li>Supports Admissions on communications for Re-enrollment and newly enrolled families</li>
<li>Support Admissions Ambassador Volunteer program.</li>
<li>Filing assistance</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p>
</p>
<p><b>Knowledge, Skills &amp; Abilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Passionate about the mission and vision of the school</li>
<li>Ability to communicate effectively and professionally, orally and in writing, with a broad range of internal and external constituents</li>
<li>Self-motivated, proactive, resourceful and creative problem solver</li>
<li>Strong organizational and project management skills with the ability to meet deadlines, respond to parent and emails, and create communications that anticipate audience questions</li>
<li>Exhibits best practices and ethics in communication</li>
<li>Excellent knowledge of the English language including sentence structure, grammar and punctuation</li>
<li>Comfort and experience with communicating with people from a variety of cultures and nationalities</li>
<li>Preferred solid knowledge of: InDesign, Photoshop Elements, iMovie or Photoshop Premier Elements.</li>
<li>Skills using basic html, Wordpress, or another web content management system</li>
<li>General Understanding of various social media platforms</li>
<li>Experienced skills in MS Office (Word, Excel, PowerPoint)</li>
<li>Experienced skills utilizing online database software. Specific experience using Volgistics, Microsoft Access, and/or Ravenna is a plus but not required.</li>
<li>Comfortable proactively learning new technology and suggesting technology-related solutions</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p>
</p>

<li>Ability to travel occasionally to attend off site events with the Marketing/Admissions team.</li>
<br>
</ul>

<li>Comfortable interacting in an international community</li>
<br>

</p>
