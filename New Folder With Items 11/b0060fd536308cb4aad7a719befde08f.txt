<h2>Salesforce Administrator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Responsibilities</p>
<ul>
<li>Lead the administration and implementation of Salesforce.com</li>
<li>Create processes and automations to assist with global sales operations including account, opportunity, lead and contract management</li>
<li>Partner with internal business groups to understand their business processes, challenges, strategic objectives and translate their stories to produce requirements and design processes on the Salesforce platform that addresses their needs</li>
<li>Lead the discovery of data from various internal sources and the creation of reports and dashboards</li>
<li>Lead the integration of internal applications and systems to Salesforce</li>
<li>Train and support end users on new or existing internal and third-party software systems and processes</li>
<li>Conduct appropriate analysis of existing Salesforce data to help identify patterns and aid in data quality improvements</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Research and recommend third-party applications to enhance the operational efficiency of business users on the salesforce platform</li>
</ul>
