<h2>Store Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>About the Brand - Seletti</b></p>
<p>Seletti is one of the fastest growing and more unique home décor brands in Europe. A charming place that links design to pop art. From its very first year in 1964 in Cicognara, Italy, Seletti has followed its principle of professional service and constant research to strive for betterness innovation and originality.</p>
<p>Seletti creates collections that contain art blended with daily life icons; objects that send a message of shape, function while conveying to end users the possibility to create a unique, personal, fun lifestyle.</p>
<p><b>About the Opportunity</b></p>
<p>Attention Home Décor and Pop Art Lovers! Seletti is opening its first pop-up store in Tysons corner and we are looking for a highly motivated store manager that can help us grow the brand and transition to a permanent location once the pop-up store closes.</p>
<p>The Shop Manager will be accountable for driving profitable business results and operational excellence through the training of Sales Team, Clienteling, management of stock and scheduling, and maintaining corporate standards of shop image and customer service. Must possess the ability to successfully manage in the below areas:</p>
<p><b>Sales</b></p>
<p>· Meet and exceed target sales (implementing sales guidelines).</p>
<p>· Maximize selling by offering optimal client service.</p>
<p>· Maximize the rotation of stocks between the stockroom and the store.</p>
<p>· Analyze the business, create and communicate clear action plans that optimize results and ensure effective execution of all operational activities</p>
<p><b>Staff</b></p>
<p>· Maximize sales potential by continually training the Sales Team on the Corporate Selling Experience</p>
<p>· Ensure store is properly staffed according to peak hours</p>
<p>· Build a store environment that is sharply focused on consistently delivering exceptional, positive in-store customer experience which adheres to Corporate standards</p>
<p><b>Store Image</b></p>
<p>· Ensure visual standards are set in accordance with brand direction</p>
<p>· Maintenance of standards and aesthetic of the store interior.</p>
<p>· Assembly of window displays.</p>
<p>· Training and supervision of the staff in regards to merchandising strandards.</p>
<p><b>Logistics, Management of Stock and Cash</b></p>
<p>· POS software management and staff training</p>
<p>· Sending selling/business report as needed</p>
<p>· Management of shipments, returns and holds</p>
<p>· Follow established cash policy and manage cash deposits</p>
<p>· Ensure loss prevention and safety policies and practices are being followed</p>
<p><b>Desired Skills &amp; Experience</b></p>
<p>· Positive and strong energy level</p>
<p>· Proven ability to drive and nurture sales results</p>
<p>· Proven strong supervisory, communication and client service skills</p>
<p>· Prior retail management experience between 2 to 5 years</p>
<p>· Ability to work retail part time hours including days, evenings, weekends, holidays and special events in a fast paced work environment</p>
<p>· Ability to maintain presence on selling floor for long periods as needed</p>
<p>· Physical ability to stand for extended periods and to move and handle boxes of merchandise up to 40lbs. as well as fixtures, throughout the store, which entails lifting and climbing stairs</p>
<p>· A professional appearance</p>
<p>· Ability to work both independently and in a team</p>
<p>· Ability to work across cultures and interact effectively with offshore crew</p>
<p>· Ability to positively and proactively handle guest concerns and prioritize multiple tasks in a fast-paced environment</p>
<p>· Ability to cultivate and maintain strong relationships</p>
<p>· Hands-on, initiator, and hard worker</p>
<p>· High performer, driven through self-motivation</p>
<p>· Detail-oriented</p>
<p>· Ethical, honest and accountable</p>
