<h2>Sales Operations Analyst</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>Devo unlocks the full value of machine data for the world’s most instrumented enterprises, putting more data to work now. Only the Devo data analytics platform addresses both the explosion in volume of machine data and the new, crushing demands of algorithms and automation, enabling IT operations and security teams to realize the full transformational promise of machine data to move the business forward.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
Founded as Logtrust in 2011 in Madrid, Spain, there are now 190+ Devos across our Cambridge, Massachusetts headquarters, our Madrid European headquarters, and in locations in the UK, Brazil, and across the US – all passionate about helping customers gain real-time actionable insight from their data, at petabyte scale. From the largest financial services companies, to global telecoms, to world-wide security organizations, Devo collects trillions of events every day to help customers speed time to action and gain the insight they need to drive their businesses.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
Reporting to the Vice President of Sales, the <b>Sales Operations Analyst</b> will be responsible for all aspects of sales operations and strategy. Responsibilities include field enablement for all geographies, capacity modeling, and quota setting. The Sales Operations Analyst will be responsible for collecting and evaluating sales information and using that information to maximize sales productivity. The role will partner with the VP of Sales to help build a high-performance global Sales team.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>The Sales Operation Analyst will work closely with the VP of Sales to put processes and structures in place that help the organization grow revenue and support future headcount growth. This impactful role will collaborate cross-functionally to design and implement solutions, systems, data and analytics, and sales enablement methodologies.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>Responsibilities:</b></p>
<p><b><br>
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
<b>Data Management:</b> Manage and evaluate data to determine the effectiveness of a product or campaign.</li>
<li>
<b>
Contract Proposals:</b> Creates contracts and RFP's that satisfy both company and customer needs.</li>
<li>
<b>
Application Management:</b> To produce data for the sales team, sales operation must oversee the use of various applications and platforms.</li>
<li>
<b>
Sales Forecasting:</b> Enable SFDC adoption across the Sales organization. Utilize SFDC to help the Sales and Finance organizations forecast sales. Analyze sales information to improve understanding of the business, our customers, customer personas, and sales cycles in order to increase revenue and improve sales efficiency.
</li>
<li>
<b>Territory Definition:</b> Outline and define various sales territories. Update the territories in SFDC. Assist with sales planning, inclusive of updates to quotas, headcount and sales revenue plan.</li>
<li>
<b>
Training:</b> Help facilitate the training of new and current sales employees.</li>
<li>
<b>
On-Boarding:</b> Collaborate with other functions to ensure a smooth on-boarding process for new employees.</li>
<li>
<b>
Organize QBR's (Quarterly Business Reviews):</b> Work with the Vice President of Sales to maximize the productivity of the QBR and all sales meetings.</li>
<li>
<b>
Sales Team Organization: </b>Support the VP Sales by proactively identifying, developing, and documenting sales process improvements, facilitate implementing changes and help drive enablement efforts in support of the Sales teams.</li>
<li>
Provide project management support to Sales management to help successfully execute new programs.</li>
<li>
Collaborate with internal teams in the creation of programs to drive revenue.</li>
<li>
Document essential processes and engagement rules; help enforce those processes.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Requirements:</b></p>
<p></p>
<br>
<ul>
<li>
Five plus years of sales operational experience in software companies, preferably in high-growth environments.</li>
<li>
Knowledgeable in sales methodologies.</li>
<li>
Able to work effectively within a sales team environment.</li>
<li>
Strong problem solving, analytical, planning, and reasoning skills.</li>
<li>
Excellent verbal and written communication skills.</li>
<li>
Proven experience handling conflicting priorities.</li>
<li>
Proven experience with continuous process improvement.</li>
<li>
Able to handle highly sensitive, confidential, and non-routine information.</li>
<li>
Experience working closely with Enterprise or Strategic Account sales leaders in a SaaS business.</li>
<li>
Salesforce.com mastery: complex dashboards reports, custom report types, calculated fields, workflows.</li>
<li>
Experience with sales force automation tools.</li>
<li>
Exceptional quantitative, MS Excel skills, business intelligence and reporting experience.</li>
<li>
Deep experience modeling complex problems, both conceptually and tactically (spreadsheets, analysis tools, visualization).</li>
<li>
Endless curiosity and a need to dive deeper for understanding.</li>
<li>
The desire and capacity to work independently and collaboratively.</li>
</ul>
