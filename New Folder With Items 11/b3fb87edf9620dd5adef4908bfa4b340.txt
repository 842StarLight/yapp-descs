<h2>Civil Project Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Civil Project Manager:</p>
<ul><li>Primary Purpose of Job:</li></ul>
<p>Manage all aspects of Civil Engineering and Geotechnical Design of QuikTrip Sites by ensuring compliance with QuikTrip &amp; Industry Standards. Offer solutions to our internal and external customers for civil engineering, geotechnical, and technical design expertise for site development plans and cost projections while complying with federal, state, and local municipal requirements.<br>B. List the major functions for this position starting with the most important and ending with the least important (maximum of 10 functions)<i>.</i> <b> </b>Develop preliminary civil engineering plans in-house and manage the completion of final civil engineering plans through outside consultants.<b> </b> Establish project direction with conceptual and preliminary plans.</p>
<ul>
<li>Provide conceptual, preliminary and final plans (plus expertise) to facilitate QuikTrip’s business and operational decisions.</li>
<li>Utilize plans that meet with QuikTrip standards and comply with applicable municipal codes to permit and construct new stores, or to construct fuel / parking / building additions to existing stores.</li>
<li>Produce plans to include comprehensive site-specific civil engineering and subsurface design to minimize cost and time through value engineering, taking into consideration Geotechnical and Environmental issues associated with each site.</li>
<li>Utilize technical skills to provide cost effective design alternatives and enhance the customer experience on QuikTrip sites (present all options available).</li>
</ul>
<ul><li>Expedite resolution of permitting issues through knowledge and use of standard QuikTrip design, civil, engineering principles, and municipal codes.</li></ul>
<ul>
<li>20%</li>
<li>
<b>Create and maintain detailed preliminary and final cost estimates, internal approval packages and technical expertise necessary for QuikTrip to make business decisions during the store development process. </b> * Create and maintain detailed cost estimates and evaluate costs ensuring they are in-line with market averages supporting QuikTrip’s long term business plan.</li>
<li>Coordinate with Environmental Project Manager to capture any environmental costs by holding Environmental/Geotechnical one-on-one meetings.</li>
<li>Coordinate with Construction Department to capture any construction concerns, alternate construction methods, or a-typical costs</li>
<li>Coordinate with other departments to capture any non-standard site items that may impact costs.</li>
<li>Attend and provide necessary documents and technical expertise for all development and construction meetings with the appropriate real estate and construction employees.</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>20%</li>
<li><b>Manage and Maintain Civil, Geotechnical, and Utility Verification Consultants.</b></li>
<li>Prepare and manage Professional Service Agreements.</li>
<li>Evaluate quality of services and costs for Consultants and hire/terminate relationships as needed.</li>
<li>Conduct Annual Evaluations utilizing the QT Consultant Evaluation Form and Procedures.</li>
<li>Negotiate and finalize Engineering related Professional Service fees associated with QT provided Scope of Work and Change Orders.</li>
<li>Facilitate training for newly hired Consultants. Training is a continuous process and retrains must be provided as necessary.</li>
<li>Provide QA/QC for Consultants work/product to ensure it meets QuikTrip Standards.</li>
<li>Ensure professional services agreements and invoices are reviewed for accuracy and paid on time.</li>
<li>Determine if project will utilize internal or external resources.</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>15%</li>
<li>
<b>Manage the completion of the Geotechnical Engineering Report on all stores in the Civil Project Manager’s Market(s). </b> * Serve as QuikTrip’s subject matter expert working in conjunction with the Geotechnical Consultant</li>
<li>Review Geotechnical reports to ensure that the recommendations are in line with QuikTrip Standards &amp; Industry Practice.</li>
<li>Utilize technical skills to provide cost effective design alternatives on QuikTrip sites. (present all options available)</li>
<li>Submit the reports, recommendations, and project specific geotechnical information to internal QuikTrip personnel.</li>
<li>Capture and explain extraordinary costs related to geotechnical issues in cost estimates.</li>
<li>Coordinate with Environmental and Construction for One-on-One and Contingency Meetings to ensure all Geotechnical and Environmental issues are identified for the cost estimate.</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>10%</li>
<li>
<b>Support the Facilities, Marketing &amp; Sales and Operations Departments before, during, and after construction. </b> * Support corporate initiatives, and provide engineering direction on corporate projects including but not limited to * Establish civil project direction with preliminary plans.</li>
<li>Provide final engineered civil drawings, details, and specifications to minimize cost and time through value engineering taking into consideration Geotechnical and Environmental issues associated with each project.</li>
<li>If needed, collaborate with Real Estate to expedite resolution of permitting through knowledge and use of standard QuikTrip design, civil, engineering principles, and municipal codes.</li>
<li>Create and maintain detailed cost estimates and evaluate costs ensuring they are in-line with market averages supporting QuikTrip’s long term business plan.</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Determine and execute Engineering &amp; Design resolutions on open store issues as reported by Operations, Facility Support, or SDPMO. * Work directly with the Engineering Manager, Operations, Facility Support, SDPMO, Projects Group, and Construction to determine costs, timelines, and implement field changes based on recommended engineering and design resolutions – including but not limited to: * Research and investigate each store issue that is reported to Engineering.</li>
<li>Coordinate with Operations, Facility support, Construction, and/or Project Group to develop a resolution that meets the needs of the customer.</li>
<li>Provide cost estimates and timelines by working with construction and projects group.</li>
<li>Keep up-to-date reporting by entering all project status updates into the Engineering Communication Log.</li>
