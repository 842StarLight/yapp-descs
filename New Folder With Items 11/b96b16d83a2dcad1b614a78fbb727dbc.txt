<div><b>
OFFICE MANAGER/ADMIN</b></div>
<div>
At SAI Global, the Office Manager/Admin is a hybrid role with a primary responsibility for managing the administrative functions required to keep the Watertown office premises open, well-kept and operating efficiently. The Office Manager/Admin will also provide executive support to the Vice President-Learning, to the Chief Revenue Officer, and to the EVP AMER Sales, including calendar management, travel &amp; accommodations, and expense report management.
</div>
</div>
<div><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Key Responsibilities:
</b></h3>
<div><ul>
<li>Provide reception and administrative support from the Watertown office daily,during core office hours(eg 8.30am-5pm)</li>
<li>
Provide admin support to executive and office staff: proactive calendar mgt, in-hours/out-of-hours travel support, expense mgt. basic reporting, functions, meetings support meetings planning</li>
<li>
Book car transport, accommodation, travel for guests and visitors to the Watertown office as required</li>
<li>
Proven, secure experience managing confidential Executive communications</li>
<li>
Maintain office security, passes, parking access, and safe, secure access, egress to /from Watertown office</li>
<li>
Maintain adequate supply of stationery, office supplies, kitchen supplies</li>
<li>
Cater food and beverages in for client, vendor, management and staff meetings as approved</li>
</ul></div>
</div></div>
<div><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Qualifications and Experience:</b></h3>
<div><ul>
<li>
2+ years experience in an office manager support, project coordination or administration - preference for confidential, executive level support</li>
<li>
Ability to independently multi-task, efficiently prioritize tasks, and drive projects and company initiatives to completion</li>
<li>
Demonstrated ability to learn quickly when facing problems and ability to translate knowledge to new situations</li>
<li>
Solid project management skills to coordinate and lead a variety of initiatives</li>
<li>
Effective verbal and listening communications skills</li>
<li>
Ability to interact effectively with all levels of the organization</li>
<li>
Strong work ethic and integrity to handle confidential/sensitive information</li>
<li>
Ability to make recommendations and decisions independently</li>
<li>
Pleasant and positive attitude with a focus on helpfulness and customer service</li>
<li>
Highly organized and able to work in a fast paced, agile environment</li>
<li>
Strong technical ability with Microsoft Office 365 products, and various word-processing, and spreadsheets</li>
</ul></div>
