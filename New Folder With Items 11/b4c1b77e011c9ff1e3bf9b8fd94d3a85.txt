<h2>Customer Service Representative</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<b>Description:</b><br>
<b>Reports to:</b> CSR AM Supervisor
<br><b>Department:</b> AM
<br><b>FLSA Status:</b> Non-Exempt
<br><b>Shift:</b> 1 (Days)
<br><br>
<b>JOB SUMMARY:</b><br>
Processes customer orders using enterprise processing system. Ensures order accuracy and answers customer inquiries regarding order status. Helps customer with general account inquiries such as HVAC troubleshooting, transportation, shipment issues, lead times/pricing/availability of parts and directs them to the appropriate departments for any warranty or quality issues. Communicates customer needs to the rest of the Red Dot Organization.
<br><br>
<b>ESSENTIAL FUNCTIONS:</b><br>
1. Processes customer orders for products and literature using computer electronic data interchange, mail, and telephone.
<br>2. Excellent communication skills and proficient in using business management tools that include all MS Office applications and high volume order entry in Oracle.
<br>3. Informs customers in a timely manner regarding product shipping dates, inventory levels, prices, part numbers and location of distribution centers.
<br>4. Accurately enters customer purchase orders into computer system; transfers orders to remote warehouse locations; provides follow-up to customers as required.
<br>5. Investigates and Identifies typical replacement parts
<br>6. Negotiates delivery performance metrics with customers
<br>7. Other duties as assigned.
<br><br>
<b>Requirements:</b><br>
<b>JOB QUALIFICATIONS:</b><br>
1. High school diploma or GED, required. Associates Degree, preferred.
<br>2. Minimum of two (2) years of customer service experience required.
<br>3. Inside Sales, procurement, distribution or expediting experience preferred.
<br>4. Knowledge of vehicle heating and air conditioning systems preferred.
<br>5. Basic understanding of how to use a personal computer including popular applications such as Word, Excel and Microsoft Office Products.
<br>6. Good organizational and personal relations skills.
<br>7. Able to successfully communicate verbally and in writing.
<br>8. Able to Read Engineering Drawings / Blue Prints, preferred.
<br>9. Ability to write, read and communicate effectively in English
<br>10. Good attendance</div>
