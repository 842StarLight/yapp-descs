<p><br>
Lifestyle Communities’ (LC) Marketing team lives at the heart of the company’s purpose to bring people together to connect. You’ll join a team of collaborative marketers that elevate and reinforce the brand in every community through compelling content and advertising, beautiful design and innovative creative, and carefully crafted customer experiences, just to name a few.<br>
<br>
The Marketing team at LC is in the midst of an exciting period of growth, and we’re looking for talented marketers to build upon our impact from strategy through execution. In this role, you’ll work with the Brand Marketing team - and partner with Brand, Operations and Community leadership - to advance the company’s One Community approach to consistent reinforcement and delivery of our brands.<br>
<br>
Make an impact through these primary responsibilities:</p>
<ul>
<li>Supports the development of a service / hospitality standard and operating model shared across brands, creating a clear expectation for hospitality while empowering and recognizing team members who rise above the standard.</li>
<li>Collaborates with Operations leaders to develop and support sales pipeline / campaign management initiatives to drive prospective customer conversion, win back, and existing resident / guest / member retention across brands.</li>
<li>Formalizes team member and customer feedback mechanisms to proactively identify opportunities to build upon strengths and address challenges with coordinated problem solving.</li>
<li>Supports local marketing tactics for each Community team to own, execute and refine in coordination with overarching strategic marketing plans.</li>
<li>Advances One Community experience model, wherein community teams create and execute a combination of experiential and surprise and delight resident and guest interactions consistently throughout the year.</li>
<li>Breaks down silos and create a unified experience across each community. Leverages trusted relationships, structured problem solving, and creativity to help us plan, operationalize and deliver a seamless customer experience.</li>
</ul>
<p>Leverage the following experience in pursuit of our collective goals:</p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree in Marketing and/or related field.</li>
<li>3-5 years’ experience leading or partnering with multiple brands or business units within an organization. Prior background in marketing, strategic planning and operations is desired.</li>
<li>Ability to work in a fast-paced project environment that allows you to take initiative with your own work while collaborating with a team dedicated to accomplishing shared goals.</li>
<li>Local and out of state travel required.</li>
</ul>
<p><br>
Lifestyle Communities (LC) is an Equal Opportunity Employer. For more information, send a message to our HR team at www.lifestylecommunities.com/careers.</p>
