<h2>Senior Manager, Change Management</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader">
<b>Healthcare’s helping hand.</b><b>
</b>
</h2>
<div><div><div>
<p>Love what you do and make a difference in people’s lives at Weatherby Healthcare, a division of CHG Healthcare. CHG is one of the largest providers of healthcare staffing in the country. We touch the lives of millions of patients every year. When your day-to-day routine contributes to this important work, it’s easy to get excited about what you do!</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
This leader will be an integral member of the Business Applications/Support team as they partner with cross-functional business leaders to champion company initiatives through large-scale change. They will bring their expertise in organizational change management, strategic planning, and business process optimization experience to the role in order to ensure successful implementation and adoption of programs. Upon thorough understanding of the history/current state of all stakeholders, this leader will work to ensure all stakeholder voices are considered and integrated into initiatives. In addition, this leader will also focus on developing and mentoring direct reports as well as informal mentorship of others within the team.</p>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader">
<b>Your role:</b><b>
</b>
</h2>
<div><div><div>
<ul>
<li>Maintains current knowledge of sales, processes, tasks, and technical aspects in order to perform duties</li>
<li>
Responsible for contributing to continuously improving and developing standards and best practices surrounding business support and implementation efforts</li>
<li>
Works effectively with sales and business partners towards accomplishing business objectives by enabling stakeholder engagement, ensuring leadership alignment, understanding business impact, assist with proper communications/business readiness, deployment, and adoption measurement</li>
<li>
Form business relationships that will support growth objectives, externally and internally</li>
<li>
Responsible for evaluating and balancing the support staff needed for CHG and Weatherby initiatives/projects by anticipating requirements and working hand-in-hand with Project Management, GM Optimization, Business Analysis and Lean groups in order to develop and implement solutions</li>
<li>
Identifies and helps gather critical information from meetings with various stakeholders to guide team in producing useful dashboards and reports</li>
<li>
Assists in participating in user acceptance testing</li>
<li>
Responsible for managing sustainment programs for the various projects to ensure compliance with the sustainment plans set by the Project Managers</li>
<li>
Partners with Learning and Development to strategize on operational training to ensure new/updated information is pulled through the company</li>
<li>
Leads project review bi-weekly meeting with BI, Contracts, L&amp;D, and Business Development</li>
<li>
Conducts Industry research/training and becomes a SME advisor as needed in order to partner with team, so that there is contribution to knowledge learning initiatives</li>
<li>
Works with sales leaders to research, clarify, and resolve concerns as related to business support</li>
<li>
Interact on a regular, collegial basis with other CHG offices' managers and leader</li>
<li>
Travel as needed to represent Weatherby including regular visits to CHG in Salt Lake City and North Carolina</li>
<li>
Facilitate ongoing and regular one-on-one interactions with direct reports, designed to enhance awareness, support and accountability</li>
</ul>
