<h2>Regional Finance Director</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>We are searching for the right candidate to join our team as a Regional Finance Director located at our West Florida Division.
<p></p>
<p><b>Job Details
</b></p>
<p>Reporting: Direct to Vice President Field Finance and indirect to Area President, the incumbent will
</p>
<ul>
<li>Ensure Division Finance teams accurately develop, analyze and report financial information timely to Division, Region and Corporate management
</li>
<li>Lead Division Finance teams through the budget and forecast process serving as liaison between Corporate and Field
</li>
<li>Review Division and consolidated Region financials, budgets and forecasts, including overhead costs
</li>
<li>Monitor key performance indicators and communicate actual and forecasted trends
</li>
<li>Monitor operational metrics to communicate risks and challenges including, but not limited to, sales pipeline, production cycle, backlog, sell-to-close, spec inventory, model inventory, over/under performing communities, community opening schedule and project closeout reports
</li>
<li>Provide leadership support with division teams to drive improvement efforts as needed
</li>
<li>Prepare ad hoc analysis as requested by Area President and Corporate
</li>
<li>Ensure that Corporate financial policies and procedures are adhered to in Divisions and Region, including SOX compliance
</li>
<li>Assist Division Finance teams with investment committee applications and pro forma analysis
</li>
<li>Provide feedback to the Vice President Field Finance and Area President to help manage the Division and Region operations
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p>Qualifications:
</p>
<ul>
<li>7+ years experience in Finance/Accounting
</li>
<li>Accounting experience, preferably in the homebuilding, land development and/or construction industry
</li>
<li>Knowledge of cost accounting (specifically construction industry)
</li>
<li>Certified Public Accountant (CPA) preferred
</li>
<li>Bachelorâ€™s degree in Accounting or Finance
</li>
<li>Experience with finance and accounting systems and software
</li>
<li>Computer literacy, including excellent Excel skills
</li>
</ul>
