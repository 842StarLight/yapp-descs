<p>Job Description
</p>
<p></p>
<p>As the Associate Product Manager at MRI-Simmons you will provide a critical support role to the product team, with ownership of a variety of tasks and projects that ensure successful execution of the organization’s product roadmap. You will partner with product owners on a range of activities that support Insights, the SaaS platform that delivers MRI-Simmons data to our client-base. This is an ideal position if you are looking to transition into a product management role.
</p>
<p></p>
<p>The role includes the following
</p>
<ul>
<li>Act as the primary product contact for client-facing individuals that raise platform or product concerns; responsible for initial troubleshooting and diagnosis, documentation of concern, assignment to appropriate teams and follow through to ensure timely and appropriate resolution.
</li>
<li>Own feedback loop for platform and product requests, synthesizing needs into actionable requirements and sharing with appropriate owners.
</li>
<li>Assist product managers with all aspects of agile product development lifecycle including but not limited to writing user stories, developing wireframes, managing backlogs, conducting follow-up from scrums and working with engineering and QA teams to confirm appropriate implementation
</li>
<li>Champion the Insights user experience by actively investigating the live platform for bugs and conducting user experience reviews that ensure consistent approach to platform implementation; work with product owners to integrate those recommendations into their priorities backlog.
</li>
<li>Create and/or maintain support documentation (i.e. User Guides) and training materials (including how-to videos) for the solutions accessible in the platform.
</li>
<li>Collaborate with marketing in the development and improvement of new and existing marketing collateral as it pertains to the platform; own coordination with marketing of platform release notes.
</li>
<li>Track platform usage to identify areas of high usage versus low and develop follow up questions to address with sales, account managers and clients ultimately to determine priorities for addressing platform adoption, retention, improvements to user experience and flow.
</li>
<li>Monitor product team KPI’s and follow up with sales to support renewals and upsells.
</li>
<li>Conduct competitive analysis, identifying potential competitors for our platform by researching companies/tools/solutions used by brands, media companies and agencies to address jobs our platform and data can solve.
</li>
<li>Assist in a range of ad-hoc projects that support the teams’ goals.
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>

</li>
<li>Able to handle multiple concurrent tasks/projects, easily pivoting as needs and priorities are adjusted
</li>
<li>Proactive, organized and detail oriented
</li>
<li>Dedicated problem solver who operates effectively and independently, even in areas of uncertainty and ambiguity
</li>
<li>Previous experience at a marketer/brand, media organization, advertising agency or market research vendor is a plus
</li>
<li>Experience working with JIRA a plus
</li>
<li>Hunger to learn!
</li>
</ul>
