<h2>Salesforce Business Intelligence and Analytics Lead</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div><div>
<div>
<div><div>Currently we are seeking a talented Salesforce Business Intelligence and Analytics Lead be a part of the Pacific Life Next Generation Customer Relationship Management (CRM) Platform Center of Excellence (CoE). This transformational team is helping to redefine how customer relationships and data are managed on a single platform for Pacific Life’s retail businesses across marketing, sales, and service utilizing tools from Salesforce. The CRM CoE is rooted in an agile based project management environment with iterative development, where requirements and solutions evolve through collaboration between self-organizing, cross-functional teams. Capabilities are being deployed in a series of foundational phases with the CoE responsible for ongoing operations support, governance, and managing the program back log and capability enhancements.<br>
<br>
The Salesforce Business Intelligence and Analytics Lead will act a central liaison to help the Pacific Life retail business units with the development and improvement of reports and dashboards that provide reliable insights and visualization for in-context utilization by Users on the Salesforce Platform across marketing, sales and service.<br>
<br>
POSITION SUMMARY<br>
<br>
Analysis and Reporting<br>
<ul>
<li>Understand business data models and provide guidance on data migration, access, data changes</li>
<li>Work with business stakeholders to define integrated business intelligence, analytics and operational reporting needs supported within the Salesforce platform to enable sound data-driven business decisions to shape impactful outcomes</li>
<li>Develop reports and dashboards with standard Salesforce Lightning reporting</li>
</ul>
Development Process<br>
<ul><li>The Salesforce Business Intelligence and Analytics Lead should have breadth of knowledge in both established and emerging technologies like big data, data sciences, predictive and prescriptive analytics in addition to experience with data visualization tools (e.g., Salesforce, Einstein Analytics, Tableau, Power BI, etc.).</li></ul>
Strategic Improvements<br>
<ul>
<li>Establish governance around Salesforce reports and dashboards. Collaborate with business stakeholders to audit and enforce reporting and dashboards within Salesforce</li>
<li>Trains on reporting and analytics use cases and best practices</li>
<li>Support the design of effective visualization of reports and dashboards, based on best practice in user experience design</li>
<li>Consults with stakeholders to understand current and future strategies, initiatives and goals. Aligns to upcoming Salesforce iterative releases to support User needs.</li>
</ul>
<br>
FACTORS FOR SUCCESS<br>
<ul>
<li>10+ years of experience</li>
<li>Experience using industry-leading BI tools for reporting and visualization (e.g., Einstein Analytics, Tableau, Power BI, etc.).</li>
<li>Technical expertise regarding data models, database design development, ETL, data mining and segmentation techniques</li>
<li>Hands on experience in producing datasets and reports for analysis using SQL, Excel or other reporting tools verifying their accuracy and providing insights</li>
<li>Salesforce certifications preferred and/or prior experience with Salesforce Platform</li>
<li>Financial Services Cloud, Service Cloud, or Marketing Cloud a plus, Einstein Analytics highly preferred</li>
<li>Excellent analytical and problem-solving skills combined with strong business judgment and an ability to clearly present results</li>
<li>Must be comfortable in agile environment with rapidly changing and sometimes ambiguous work situations</li>
<li>Requires strong analytical, organizational and problem-solving skills</li>
<li>Experience with Agile Scrum delivery framework with basic working knowledge of the key phases across the software delivery lifecycle, including analysis, design, development, testing, and deployment</li>
<li>Strong project management skills, including influencing skills and the ability to manage multiple tasks and deliverables efficiently</li>
<li>Excellent communicator and presenter with proven success in developing client relationships and managing client expectations</li>
<li>Experience in creating and maintaining technical documentation including specifications, data flows, data mapping and data dictionaries</li>
<li>Strong aptitude for learning quickly and applying sound logic when analyzing problems or defining solutions</li>
<li>4-year degree or equivalent experience</li>
</ul>
