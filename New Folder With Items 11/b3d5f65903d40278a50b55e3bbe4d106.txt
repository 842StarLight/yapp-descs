<b>Position Overview:</b><br>
We are currently seeking an energetic Controller, to oversee and manage all of the day-to-day accounting functions of the business. The successful candidate must have proven experience as a strong accounting and financial manager in a B2C e-commerce company.
<br><br>
<b>Responsibilities:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Manage and oversee the accounting department of a company that sells products through retailers and e-commerce direct to consumers</li>
<li>Experience in all aspects of accounting including but not limited to bookkeeping, inventory control, payroll, tax, financial reports, incentive programs and benefits</li>
<li>Oversee the month-end financial close process</li>
<li>Develop and mentor the accounting team</li>
<li>Financial planning and analysis of cash flow, cost controls and expenses to guide business</li>
<li>Ensure all financial reports are prepared in an accurate and timely manner</li>
<li>Create, coordinate, and evaluate the financial programs and supporting information systems of the company to include budgeting, tax planning and conservation of assets</li>
<li>Building budgets and keeping departments accountable by measuring and monitoring</li>
<li>Business process analysis for continuous improvement</li>
<li>Develop and mentor the accounting team</li>
<li>Perform ad hoc projects and reporting</li>
</ul>
<b>Requirements:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s Degree in Finance, Accounting or Business</li>
<li>CPA (or working towards becoming a CPA) preferred, not required</li>
<li>Minimum of 6 years of relevant experience required</li>
<li>Proven leader with strong accounting and management experience</li>
<li>Consumer sales tax understanding within various states</li>
<li>Quick learner with a high drive for results</li>
<li>Technologically savvy</li>
<li>Experienced with Excel and QuickBooks Enterprise</li>
<li>Detail-oriented, critical thinker and problem solver</li>
</ul>
