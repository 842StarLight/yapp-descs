 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>

<p><b>Basic Requirement</b></p>
<p>
To integrate existing software, hardware, services and expert consultation to almost every kind of retail business, including Food, Drug and Mass Merchandise, Department and Specialty Retail, Petroleum and Convenience Retail, and Wholesale Distribution.</p>
<p>
The Goal is to work with existing technologies, working with what you have and know and to enhance output print systems (cash register receipts) and telecom support for any retail operation to utilize a section of the front face of the cash register receipt.</p>
<p>
Develop integration for input to Cash Register receipt and a method of print-to-mobile- internet connectivity.</p>
<p>
You possess a strong technical background in Point-of-Sale systems software. You think creatively out of the box and can deliver on schedule.</p>
<p><b>
Your Day will consist of</b>
</p>
<p>Developing software-hardware connectivity in retail environments for a variety of Cash Register (P-O-S) systems initially on a small scale 5-20 locations.</p>
<p>
Working closely with an Advertiser, Retailer and Manufacturing Product Manager to advertise their products on the front face of the Cash Register receipt to position Belcom as a world leader in “Front Face” Cash Register Receipt Advertising.</p>
<p><b>
Must Haves</b></p>
<p><i>
Experience</i> in a successful software-hardware integration role and a strong passion/interest in exploiting an opportunity to enhance an existing technology on the verge of change. Knowledge of fully featured Point-of Sale application. Knowledge of retail management and support of the GS1 DataBar (barcode tech). Knowledge of backend integration-to-site, telecom, mobile, internet outputs. The ability to work in a team environment and manage outsourced services. The desire to be the best at what you do or aspire to become.</p>
