<p>Description</p>
</div>
<div>
<div>
<p>Yamaha employees are passionate about music and sound! As the world’s largest musical instrument manufacturer, we are known for our quality, stellar customer service and innovation. Yamaha has been named a Top 100 Global Innovator for the past three years. Are you someone who can get genuinely excited about music? Can you manage marketing initiatives from concept to completion? If so, then we have a great opportunity for you!</p>
<p>
Yamaha is looking for a <b>Marketing Specialist - Winds &amp; Strings </b>to execute marketing initiatives that will deliver on the department and company goals and objectives. In addition, the successful candidate will work with internal and external agencies to launch campaigns, develop metrics, and analyze the effectiveness of each campaign in order to drive sales and customer satisfaction.</p>
<p>
The Winds, Strings, and Accessories department consists of people who are dedicated to helping people love music. Our dedication to the players, performers and creators of the world is the driving force behind our innovation and quality. Our award-winning gear fuels genuine musical exploration and expression for the individual musician and all the way through to performers who pack stadiums.</p>
<p><b>
Here's What You’ll Do:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Partner with Marketing Managers to conceive, develop and execute initiatives that support their department’s marketing plans</li>
<li>Review statuses of projects and drive their progress utilizing project management tools</li>
<li>Initiate development of marketing materials such as digital and social media advertisements, clinic and trade show signage, and point of purchase materials</li>
<li>Write creative briefs and work with internal and external creative partners to generate marketing collateral</li>
<li>Plan and execute tactical initiatives to reach the target audience through appropriate marketing channels</li>
<li>Interpret data to determine campaign efficiency and make adjustments to achieve intended results</li>
<li>Ensure creative marketing materials undergo regular review and adhere to brand guidelines</li>
<li>Create, review and update web content for product pages and series pages</li>
<li>Supports sales and marketing by providing advertising and promotional content and researching market trend data</li>
<li>Copy-writing and proofreading of department communications across all channels including, but not limited to: websites, emails, blogs, advertising, POP, and press releases</li>
<li>Administrative support related to day-to-day marketing tasks</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Here’s What You’ll Bring to the Table</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Undergraduate or graduate degree in Marketing, Business, Communications or equivalent</li>
<li>1 -3 years’ experience in a marketing related function or equivalent</li>
<li>Proven experience managing several projects concurrently</li>
<li>Passionate about your field and knowledge of specialized marketing concepts, principles and tactics</li>
<li>Relies on experience and judgment to plan and accomplish goals</li>
<li>Thorough understanding of marketing elements and market research methods</li>
<li>Exceptional communication and writing skills</li>
<li>Demonstrated experience in marketing data analytics and tools preferred</li>
<li>Solid computer skills, including MS Office and project management software (e.g. Wrike)</li>
<li>Well-organized and detail-oriented</li>
<li>Retail and consumer awareness partnered with a creative mind</li>
<li>Ability to travel up to 10% of the time (Domestic) required</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Preferred:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Experience with marketing software (Adobe Creative Suite and CRM) and applications (web analytics, Google AdWords, etc.)</li>
<li>Omnichannel marketing experience</li>
<li>Passionate about music</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Here's What We'll Bring to the Table:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Comprehensive benefits package including Medical, Vision, Dental, and 401k with match and other perks</li>
<li>Performance based bonus program</li>
<li>Robust employee wellness programs including free onsite music lessons</li>
<li>Gym membership reimbursement program</li>
<li>Tobacco cessation reward program</li>
<li>Free concerts from award winning artists</li>
<li>Discounted hotel, travel, entertainment, and other attractions</li>
</ul>
<p>If this opportunity resonates with you, apply now!</p>
