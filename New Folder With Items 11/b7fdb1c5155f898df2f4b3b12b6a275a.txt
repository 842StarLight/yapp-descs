<h2>Senior Strategy Analyst</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<b>ADP is hiring a Sr. Strategy Analyst ;</b> In this position you will be an integral member of the team and add value from day 1. They will report directly to the Vice President of Corporate Strategy and work on projects that have high visibility and are paramount to ADP's continued success.

<br>
<b>RESPONSIBILITIES:</b>
<br>
<br>
<b>Description: This is a high-visibility role in the Corporate Strategy team working directly with ADP's Senior leadership on key strategic issues:</b>
<ul>
<li>Work closely with ADP's Chief Strategy Officer and Corporate Strategy team to manage the enterprise-wide strategic planning process for ADP: develop short, mid, and long-term strategies using fact-based analyses</li>
<li>
Structure complex business problems and undertake analysis to uncover recommendations</li>
<li>
Partner with functional leaders (e,g., Product Management, Sales, ADP Business Units) to build and implement strategies that grow market share and revenue</li>
<li>
Drive market research efforts and track market / competitive trends with a specific focus on potential disruptive threats, changing client preferences, and keys to differentiation and report out on regular basis</li>
<li>
Support business development and acquisition opportunities including business case development</li>
<li>Distills insights for analysis, translate the output into recommendations, drive clear well thought-through conclusions based on findings</li>
<li>Presenting to and engaging with ADP's Senior Leadership to share your analyses and recommendations</li>
<li>
Ability to manage multiple workstreams</li>
</ul>
<b>QUALIFICATIONS REQUIRED:</b>
<ul>
<li>BA/BS degree or graduate from a top tier school with majors in Business, Economics, Finance, or Mathematics/Statistics; MBA preferred</li>
<li>
2-3 years corporate strategy or consulting experience preferred, not required</li>
<li>
Experience developing and evaluating strategies: Identifying and framing issues, developing hypotheses, conducting market research and analyses to test key hypotheses, developing execution plans</li>
<li>
Strong conceptual problem solving skills</li>
<li>
Strong analytical skills (financial modeling, basic statistics, regression analyses, etc.)</li>
<li>
Excellent written and verbal communication</li>
</ul>
