<b>Your job:</b><br>
About Indeed Hire
<br><br>
Indeed Hire is a pay-per-hire service that gives companies access to easy and affordable recruiting. We combine powerful technology with attentive Hiring Specialists to help employers find the best people to join their teams. We are a passionate, diverse, entrepreneurial team building a well-funded startup under the Indeed brand, aiming to help employers find great talent faster.
<br><br>
We are looking for a qualitative-focused UX researchers who can help the Hire team understand the "why" behind behaviors and attitudes of our diverse user groups - employers, candidates, sales, and hiring specialists. These efforts will ensure the experiences we create are easy to use, useful, and efficient. On Indeed Hire, researchers have the opportunity to collaborate with a wide spectrum of stakeholders, including Product, UX, Marketing, Sales, and Business Operations.
<br><br>
So what would you be doing?
<br>Indeed helps people find jobs. We are best known for our job search (www.indeed.com), but we do other things related to jobs and hiring, too.
<br><br>
You'll work with other members of the UX team supporting our Product and Engineering teams in Austin, validating new ideas and helping teams answer their questions through qualitative research. We are a small group because we are incredibly picky with who we hire and we prioritize the things that matter.
<br><br>
<b>Some of the ways you may support the team include:</b><br>

<li>Build relationships with key stakeholders and clients to gain deeper insights.</li>
<li>Establish and manage relationships with research-related vendors.</li>
<li>Conduct research activities as needed to support your particular product area.</li>
