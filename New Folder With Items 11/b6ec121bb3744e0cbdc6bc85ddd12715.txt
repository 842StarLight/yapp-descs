<p>We are looking for a motivated and passionate technical product manager who can develop and expand our Zero Trust Privilege platform through strategic partnerships. You will play a critical role in helping Centrify build strategic partnerships with some of the largest companies in the industry. This role requires that you have a solid understanding of the current IT and security markets and can quickly establish and build strong technical relationships (integrations, APIs, interfaces, scripts, automation and analytics) with industry leaders Amazon, Microsoft, Google, ServiceNow, Splunk, etc. to solve key customer use cases. Specifically, this position is expected to collaborate with various stakeholders (product management, engineering, sales and marketing) at Centrify and our strategic partner organizations on a regular basis. This is a highly visible role and has the potential to make a significant impact on our business. This role reports directly to VP of Product Management.
