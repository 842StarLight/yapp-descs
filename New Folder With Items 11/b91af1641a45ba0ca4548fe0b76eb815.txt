<p><b>Function:</b></p>
<p>Leads and collaborates with Regional and HQ-level personnel to drive and secure strategic sales opportunities within the current and potential customer base. Develops and plans sales strategies to optimize sales results. Supports initiatives within our reseller and OEM distribution channels and a crossed all product lines: HCIT, Ultrasound, X-Ray and Services.</p>
<p>This position requires strong interpersonal, strategic selling and leadership skills. Collaborates with Headquarters, Field Sales and Enterprise Healthcare sales teams on specific initiatives and program roll-outs, as well as coordinates field activities with all channel partners within the region. Prospects are identified based upon a focused strategic plan, with this position playing a pivotal role in the execution and management of key opportunities. Must be knowledgeable of X-Ray, HCIT and US technologies, and possess the necessary skills to manage and lead through multi-level/functional negotiations. Specific emphasis is to grow sales through the regional reseller and OEM distribution channels.</p>
<ul>
<li>Hire, lead and coach a team of AE and OEM team members, and manage Region Product Managers in a matrix management environment.
</li>
<li>Develop and lead a high performance sales team and manage timelines for execution of sales strategies</li>
<li>Maintain and lead relationships with all channel partners on behalf of KMHA</li>
<li>Provide product technical knowledge, support and training for sales channel team members and KMHA sales members</li>
<li>Understand &amp; support technical and legal requirements necessary to optimize sales strategies</li>
<li>Provide clear communication to channel partners on goals and objectives, and drive sales accountability with metrics and reporting of sales achievements</li>
<li>Communicate KMHA technology advancements to appropriate KMHA channel partners.</li>
<li>Develop the KMHA Value Proposition, measure program results, and manage dealer contracts for accuracy and effectiveness</li>
<li>Evaluate next generation technological and provide HQ with strategies to ensure KMHA's competitive edge.</li>
<li>Support regulatory communication requirements on behalf of KMHA and in support of FDA requirements</li>
<li>Develop and maintain necessary reports to effectively manage &amp; execute successful business relationships</li>
<li>Identify and communicate information technology requirements necessary to maintain/enhance KMHA sales results</li>
