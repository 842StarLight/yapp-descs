<p><b>Field Service Engineer ( </b><b>Therapeutic Imaging)<br>
</b></p>
<p></p>
<p><b>About the role:
</b></p>
<p>The Therapeutic Imaging Field Service Engineer team is an integral part of the business unit’s customer activation strategy. This position is responsible for providing site assessments, system designs and technical support for equipment evaluation, implementation, and post go-live activities. Installation support will be provided both remotely and onsite, depending on the level of assistance necessary. This position will collaborate with the Field Sales Organization to develop a “best-in-class” customer activation experience. This will be done via comprehensive facility assessments, working closely with the end users to understand their current processes, then proposing and leading the implementation of the agreed-upon solution. This position’s success will rely heavily on the ability to engage with multiple internal and external stakeholders.
</p>
<p><b><i>** This is a remote opportunity, candidates must live in </i></b><b><i>a metropolitan city in the Midwest </i></b><b><i>with access to a major airport. You will travel </i></b><b><i>85% of the time, sometimes on short notice **<br>
</i></b></p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Your responsibilities will include:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Proposal of system configurations that co-exist with other endoscopic procedure room equipment
</li>
<li>Manage the relationship and expectations of the clinical and technical customer
</li>
<li>Ensure a white glove evaluation period and removal of process-related adoption barriers
</li>
<li>Ensure customer issues and resolution are captured, tracked, and reported through the Quality system
</li>
<li>Work onsite at the customer location, and remotely, in collaboration with customers, field sales, customer care and others within the Therapeutic Imaging team to provide technical and process support
</li>
<li>Lead efforts to graphically and verbally document systems and procedure rooms to facilitate remote support
</li>
<li>Accurately update account information, create installation and field service reports, and other pertinent information in Salesforce
</li>
<li>Work with R&amp;D and Commercialization Teams to develop procedures for new product launches that exhibit the white-glove approach
</li>
<li>Other duties as assigned<br>
</li>
</ul>
