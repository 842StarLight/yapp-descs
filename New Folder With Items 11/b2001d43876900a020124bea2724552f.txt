<p><b>Duties:
</b></p>
<p>The Senior IT Business Systems Analyst will be responsible for working closely with partners from Portfolio Analytics, Market Analysis &amp; Strategy organization and the IT group in order to understand and document strategic drivers and tactical needs to ensure that the implementation teams can provide solutions that meet the business need with the necessary stability, usability, and scalability.</p>
<p>The IT Business Systems Analyst will be supporting projects in the Field Customer Engagement / Advanced Analytics portfolio, experience at SENIOR level is required.</p>
<ul>
<li>Facilitate workshops - story-boarding workshops, translate story-boarding artifacts into an electronic format and provide input in design sessions</li>
<li>Clearly communicate keeping IT and business partners apprised of developments and requirements, key metrics</li>
<li>Analyze and document current and future business process models</li>
<li>Analyze and document requirements (scope), business rules (use cases)</li>
<li>Analyze and document data requirements (from business needs perspective)</li>
<li>Plan and manage user acceptance test (UAT), create UAT scripts and facilitate UAT; report out on UAT progress and outcomes</li>
<li>Collaborate with business partners, IT Project Managers, User Experience Designers, Solution Architects, Developers &amp; Software Quality Assurance on scope, solutions, constraints and risks</li>
<li>Mentor the teams on UX best practices and oversee the creation of delightful prototypes &amp; dashboards</li>
<li>Perform knowledge transfer to training and communication teams</li>
<li>Support user deployment activities as needed</li>
<li>Lead business stakeholders and other business systems analysts thru the business analysis lifecycle</li>
<li>Work closely with Project Manager, Technical Lead, Developers, Software Quality Assurance Lead to accomplish a successful project delivery, focused on Minimum Viable Product (MVP)</li>
<li>Use Data Analysis tools and strategies to understand data trends and issues, guiding and enabling business users to understand data and data structures and draw key insights for business decisions</li>
<br>
</ul>
