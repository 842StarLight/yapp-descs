<h2>Vacation Planning Center Supervisor</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>To meet the minimum qualifications for this position the qualified candidate must possess at least one year of <b>both Call Center, and Hospitality experience from vacation rental company or hotel. </b></p>
<p><b>Summary: </b> In this role, you will assist to manage and coordinate daily activities of employees providing reservations sales services to potential guests and inquiring owners, while ensuring the accuracy of all information as it directly relates to the reservations sales process of our vacation rental environment. You will input and maintain information in the Property Management System (PMS) in relation to reservations environment; rate schedules, MLOS calendar, property description, etc. You will help to develop and communicate to the operating team procedures that ensure reservations practices are adhered to in order to produce maximum occupancy and revenue, guest and owner satisfaction.</p>
<p><b>Duties and Responsibilities: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>To work in conjunction with the Vacation Planning Manager to manage the day to day operations of the vacation planning department. Ensure that employees have the necessary training and resources to successfully accomplish their jobs.</li>
<li>Ensure that company and department policy &amp; procedures are communicated, understood and maintained.</li>
<li>To work in conjunction with the Vacation Planning Manager as it pertains to Vacation Planning and Guest/Owner services including but not limited to; training, and developing Vacation Advisors and Guest/Owner Service teams.</li>
<li>To manage the systems within the Vacation Planning department to ensure accurate handling of reservation inquiries.</li>
<li>Oversee and perform tasks as it relates to the accurate input of all pertinent information into the PMS; including rates, property descriptions, guest information, and terms and conditions.</li>
<li>Use of other software systems NAVIS and Streamline. NAVIS- track booked lead compliance, call compliance, and phone audits. Streamline- Guest sales reports, and info emails.</li>
<li>To maintain departmentâ€™s quality assurance program; includes monitoring frequency of errors, the performance of reservations agents and daily review of all reservations made, canceled or changed.</li>
<li>Assist Vacation Planning Manager in the ongoing development of reservations agents through the use of policy manuals, call monitoring and verbal reinforcement; using the ACD data as a resource tool.</li>
<li>Must demonstrate the confident ability in sales conversion techniques while maintaining acceptable performance standards and meeting productivity goals.</li>
<li>Responsible for maintaining incoming reservation inquiries through webchat and inquiry/information website.</li>
<li>Work in conjunction with Vacation Planning Manager &amp; Revenue Manager in maintaining all 3rd party and online agency relationships.</li>
<li>May direct the work of other Vacation Advisors.</li>
<li>All other duties as assigned.</li>
<li>Regular and reliable attendance and your physical presence at the worksite.</li>
</ul>
