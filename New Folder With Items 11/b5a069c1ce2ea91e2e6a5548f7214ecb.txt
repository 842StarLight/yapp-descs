<p><b>
POSITION SUMMARY</b>:</p>
<p>
The Analytics Implementation Engineer will be responsible for tagging implementation related to a multi-faceted client program. The clients sponsoring these programs are major manufactures in the automotive industry.</p>
<p>
We're looking for someone who can provide technical leadership, direction, and Tagging Implementation expertise to our development team. You will work closely with other team members on technical issues and complex problems; take ownership of the tagging framework; and participate in analysis of client needs.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
Key responsibilities include: Architect and Maintain the tagging implementation. Collaborate with several teams in building reporting requirements for several projects. Handle communication between several agencies to maintain tagging contracts and reporting requirements.</p>
<p>
Play a vital role in the architecture design, configuration, testing and deployment of Adobe solutions, including Audience Manager, Campaign, Analytics, Target and Experience Manager. You will work closely with marketing departments, IT, developers, system administrators, and end users to build state-of-the-art implementations for large customers</p>
<p>
Also, build a robust monitoring solution to ensure tracking across various networks.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
PRINCIPAL DUTIES &amp; RESPONSIBILITIES</b>:</p>
<p><b><i>
Essential Functions:</i></b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Have in-depth knowledge of all components of Adobe Analytics platform implementation including tags, s_code, plug-ins, Campaigns, server call types, cookies, Site Catalyst variables (props, eVars, events), universal tags, Data Connectors, SAINT classifications, calculated metrics, Link Tracking, JavaScript, HTML, and packet sniffing</li>
<li>
Develop innovative strategies for tracking and tagging, and partner with development teams to define and perform quality assurance testing activities to ensure accurate and efficient data collection</li>
<li>
Work with developers and business teams, as needed, to communicate what the implementation is designed to accomplish; define JavaScript functions that need to be executed and provide consultative direction to the development team around best practices and standards.</li>
<li>
Diagnose issues with existing platform configurations and tags, and provide recommendations for improvement</li>
<li>
Direct data layer implementation on site and pages across website properties</li>
<li>
Work with third parties/Agencies to resolve discrepancies or platform issues</li>
<li>
Maintain working knowledge of industry best practices and changes in analytics platforms, and identify how they impact and can grow the capability</li>
<li>
Good knowledge of established digital marketing solutions, and of the corresponding architecture – including knowledge of how these are embedded within a client’s landscape (e.g. tag management, DMP, Agency, etc.)</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
