<h2>eDiscovery Review Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Overview
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div>Xact Data Discovery is looking for a <b>Review Manager</b> who can join our Troy, Michigan team. The successful candidate will be committed to delivering a great customer experience, open communication, and will be a trusted team player.</div>
<div>
<br>
The Review Manager is responsible for simultaneously managing multiple client matters as well as developing company relationships with existing and prospective clients. Should be able to provide high level substantive review workflow and review strategy advice to clients. Should be able to independently manage reviews, and review workflows. Other responsibilities are to provide: input for general management, including scheduling, staffing, resource allocation, revenue projections, and operational process and procedures.</div>
<div>
Specific duties include supporting, coordinating, managing, and participating in document reviews at the direction of clients. As part of this role, this position will primarily utilize the Relativity hosted review platform as an administrative-level user, and will frequently employ analytics and assisted review functions to streamline review processes and increase efficiency.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<ul>
<li>Lead and manage multiple review projects independently.
</li>
<li>Communicate with client and/or outside counsel to establish project objectives, scope substantive parameters of the project, select proper review workflow, and estimate project timelines and budgets.</li>
<li>
Work closely with the client, the Xact Data Discovery service delivery team, and project review team to ensure that lines of communication between all project participants are maintained.</li>
<li>
Provide detailed substantive review support for the client and project review team on the use of review tools, including Relativity, Relativity Analytics, and Relativity Assisted Review, whether on site, at client locations, or remotely.</li>
<li>
Train and guide review teams.</li>
<li>
Assist sales team and consultants in Managed Review sales pursuits.</li>
<li>
Be able to suggest appropriate uses of technology and review workflows, including the ability to integrate quality control workflows involving the client and senior project reviewers.</li>
<li>
Oversee progress of work to ensure delivery of all work product within established timeframes and standards of quality.</li>
<li>
Manage expenditures within budget estimates.
</li>
<li>Maintain communication with client and project team regarding changes to proposed work and budget.</li>
<li>
Establish and maintain good working relationships with other offices and departments in the company.</li>
<li>
Promote uniformity of office procedures and consistent high quality of final work product.</li>
</ul>
</div></div></div>
