<h2>Community Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>
<div>Hayes Gibson Property Services is excited to add a new Community Manager to our team! The Community Manager (CM) leads and supervises property operations, financial activities, regulatory compliance, and guides interactions with all stakeholders – residents, HGPS corporate personnel, regulatory agency officials, clients, community officials, vendors, and customers at two properties; Hiawatha Commons and Gateway Lofts, located in Minneapolis, Minnesota. The CM possesses a passion for customer service that inspires associates and vendors to deliver the highest quality product, comprehensive services, respect, and hospitality HGPS customers expect.</div>
<br>
<b><i>Client Service:</i></b> The CM is responsible for ensuring the delivery of excellent client service, oversight of marketing, sales, leasing, customer retention, financial, administrative, and staffing responsibilities,
<div></div>
<div><b>Essential Functions for the Community Manager</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Client Service</li>
<li>Ensure the delivery of excellent client service</li>
<li>Compile information for clients and owner reports accurately and on time</li>
<li>Respond in a timely fashion to questions, correspondence, e-mails and phone requests from clients, lenders, investors, contractors, vendors, and regulatory agency personnel</li>
<li>Anticipate resident needs and promptly respond to them</li>
<li>Treat all customers, visitors, vendors, and associates with respect</li>
<li>Ensure best-in-the-industry response times to unit issues, repairs, and resident needs</li>
<li>Work through resident disputes in a fact based and solution driven way</li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<div>
<div><i>Marketing/Sales/Leasing</i></div>
<ul>
<li>If applicable, develop, implement, and monitor marketing plan for the lease-up of the property that maximizes lease up pace, net revenue of the property and client requirements</li>
<li>If the property has a regulated housing program, ensure that all eligibility requirements are met for applicants and residents.</li>
<li>Generate a sustaining flow of qualified prospective residents</li>
<li>Regularly conduct marketing surveys to be aware of competitors’ activities in the surrounding marketplace and make recommendations based on survey analysis</li>
<li>Show and lease apartments</li>
<li>Achieve and maintain designated occupancy goals / objectives</li>
<li>Create and monitor occupancy and associated reports in order to monitor traffic and closing performance</li>
<li>Participate in community affairs and maintain positive public image for the property</li>
</ul>
<div>
<br>
<i>Customer Retention</i>
</div>
<ul>
<li>Develop, implement, and monitor marketing strategy to maximize resident retention</li>
<li>Develop and implement positive resident relations program for the community</li>
<li>Respond to resident needs and input</li>
<li>Resolve any conflicts between residents and address issues promptly</li>
</ul>
<div>
<br>
<i>Staffing Responsibilities</i>
</div>
<ul>
<li>Assist Human Resources with employee issues following company policies</li>
<li>Hold staff accountable to the highest standard of customer service and to meet their job requirements</li>
<li>Interview, hire, train, recommend performance evaluations, resolve problems, provide open communication and recommend discipline and/or termination when appropriate</li>
<li>Develop and delegate improvement plans for operations and review performance of management team</li>
<li>Recruit, train and develop qualified talent to achieve property goals</li>
<li>Create and sustain a positive work environment at the community and accomplish the objectives of the client</li>
<li>Inspect the property on a regular basis and schedule improvements and repairs</li>
</ul>
<div>
<br>
<i>Financial Responsibilities</i>
</div>
<ul>
<li>Prepare and manage the operating budget in order to control expenses and meet financial objectives</li>
<li>Supervise the collection of rents and all monies on site and ensure prompt deposit and accurate record keeping of these funds</li>
</ul>
<div>
<br>
<i>Administrative Responsibilities</i>
</div>
<ul>
<li>Complete and submit all records, reports, and documents required by the company and federal or state agencies in a timely and accurate manner</li>
<li>Process applications timely</li>
<li>If applicable, ensure compliance with all HUD, LIHTC, and Fair Housing regulations</li>
<li>Process move in and move out documents</li>
<li>If applicable, ensure that the property is prepared for any HUD or agency audit or inspection</li>
<li>If applicable, work collaborative with the HGPS Compliance Department to complete all required compliance documentation in an accurate and timely manner.</li>
</ul>
</div>
</div>
<div>
<div><b>Other Responsibilities</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Tour and visually inspect property daily</li>
<li>Monitor property condition, cleanliness, cost control, and quality of product and service throughout property</li>
<li>Assist and support other members of the management team to make sound business decisions</li>
<li>Execute the financial objectives of the property</li>
<li>Maintain regular attendance and follow HGPS rules and regulations governing attendance</li>
<li>Communicate effectively and display a professional manner when dealing with supervisors, co-workers, residents and individuals inside and outside the organization</li>
<li>Work effectively as a team member, participating actively and constructively</li>
<li>Perform all duties in a safe manner by following all HGPS safety procedures for the operation of equiCMent, machinery and hazardous substances and chemicals</li>
<li>Ensure clear communication and quantifiable reporting</li>
</ul>
</div>
<div></div>
<div><b>Requirements for the Community Manager</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Education/Training</li>
<li>College diploma preferred; high school diploma or equivalent combination of education required. Industry designations such as CAM, ARM®, CAPS and CCM® are strongly encouraged.</li>
<li>For affordable properties regulated by the Department of Housing and Urban Development (HUD) or a state finance agency, industry designations such as COS, HCCP, TCS, SHCM, C3P are strongly encouraged.</li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<div>
<div><b>Special Requirements</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Positive attitude and customer service focus</li>
<li>Sales, leasing, and marketing ability</li>
<li>Experience in social media and technology</li>
<li>Skills in time management, organization, problem solving, and judgment</li>
<li>Keen attention to detail and accuracy</li>
<li>Strong written and verbal communication skills</li>
<li>Flexibility to work weekends, overtime, and in cases of emergency</li>
<li>Stress tolerance in a fast-paced environment</li>
</ul>
</div>
<div>
<br>
<b>Experience/Skills</b>
</div>
<ul>
<li>A minimum of 3 years’ experience in property management</li>
<li>Valid driver’s license in the state of residence</li>
<li>Proficiency in Microsoft Excel, Word, or other property management software (Yardi, RealPage, AMSI, MRI, Entrata).</li>
<li>For affordable properties regulated by HUD or a state agency, proficiency in understanding the compliance requirements of the specific housing programs(s) in place on the assigned property.</li>
</ul>
<div>
<br>
<b>Compensation</b>
</div>
<div></div>
<div>The position is full-time with benefits and offers a competitive salary structure based on applicable experience.</div>
