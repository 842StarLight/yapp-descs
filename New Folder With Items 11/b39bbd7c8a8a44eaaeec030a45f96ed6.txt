<div></div>
<div><b>Position Responsibilities</b></div>
<div>
<br>
<ul>
<li>Financial performance, including profit, rate, retention, and growth, of an approximate $5 million book of mid-market multi-line Life Sciences business.</li>
<li>Responsibilities include a new business goal of $1.25mm+ each year.</li>
<li>Identifying opportunities for growth within new and existing production sources and developing and executing a new business growth strategy including developing prospects.</li>
<li>Successful market penetration and agency management including building, maintaining and managing producer and customer relationships to over 25 agents.</li>
<li>Developing agency strategy and goals with continual monitoring of progress.</li>
<li>Identifying cross sell opportunities within commercial products and services.</li>
<li>Soliciting, selecting and analyzing risk within Chubb Commercial Insurance guidelines and ensuring proper documentation.</li>
<li>Developing and negotiating price, coverage, and terms and conditions for all new business and renewals and actively identifying account rounding opportunities.</li>
<li>Meeting with producers and new and renewal customers to make sales presentations including product education and new product roll out.</li>
<li>Collaborating with underwriters, operations, claims, marketing and home office product management.</li>
<li>Consistently meeting service standards.</li>
<li>Collecting and sharing industry intelligence with team, including industry trending and development.</li>
</ul>
