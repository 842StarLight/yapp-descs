<h2>Creative Leader</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">At Nextiva, we don't study industry trends and build our products and services reactively; instead, we look to be the leader in defining the trends. We strive to be the best of the best across multiple technology channels – UCaaS and SaaS to name a few. We are determined to help write the future for businesses around the globe by creating innovative products and an agnostic platform that delivers first-class communications tools in the hands of every business. We are a global team of 1,000+ and are the fastest-growing privately held provider of cloud communications. We are looking for amazing people, like you, to join our team!
<br><br>
The Creative Leader will work closely with marketing, sales, product and broader business teams to build and maintain Nextiva's brand narrative. You are the maestro of an internal agency that facilitates all things digital - UX, design, video, multimedia, and more.
<br><br>
The Creative Leader will also create a consistent end-to-end brand experience across multiple marketing touch-points. Working closely with marketing, sales, product, engineering, and account management - you will conceptualize, build, and engage a wide audience of employees, partners, customers, and potential customers.
<br><br>
We're seeking an original thinker and expressive brand storyteller — someone who cares deeply about the work they do with a driving curiosity to push boundaries within an already dynamic and authentic brand.
<br><br>
<b>Nextiva needs someone who will:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Be the fearless authority on all-things ownership of the Nextiva brand. Makes the final decision on all high impact design projects and deliverables.</li>
<li>Possess ultra-sharp digital design acumen - You are able to defeat opinion with fact by breaking down the science behind premium quality digital design.</li>
<li>Craft impressive digital deliverables on traditional and non-traditional channels focused on acquiring, engaging, and educating a diverse audience of businesses, channel partners, and employees.</li>
<li>Constantly evolve and push boundaries in developing Nextiva's brand and creative via content, channel, and experiences. Nextiva is disrupting and innovating the business communications industry, and our creative should, too.</li>
<li>Partner cross-functionally with key stakeholders to ensure creative work is addressing Nextiva's strategic goals. This means working closely with marketing, sales, and product to bring concepts to final execution.</li>
<li>Exhibit strong leadership skills - You will manage a sizable internal team, meaning you should have mastery level project management skills that enable you to delegate and deliver projects in a timely manner while maintaining our quality standards.</li>
</ul>
<b>Your background:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>10+ years exp. working either in-house (SaaS or telecom preferred) or an agency.</li>
<li>You have deep, mastery level expertise in 1-2 areas of digital design but also strong proficiency in adjacent design channels and mediums such as graphic design, photography, color, typography, iconography, UX Design, illustration, prototyping and multimedia skills.</li>
<li>Significant experience in developing marketing &amp; sales specific deliverables e.g. - landing pages, email templates, PDFs, eBooks, infographics, banners, blog graphics, social media creatives, customer communications, videography, and print collateral.</li>
<li>A strong portfolio demonstrating a passion and ability to lead creative initiatives across a wide range of platforms. You've led projects through full strategic and creative development — research, strategy (why and how), naming, copywriting, and content development.</li>
<li>The ability to inspire others and effectively manage teams with clear goals and measurable impact.</li>
<li>The belief that effective creative direction to get to the best work is achieved through collaboration in small, fast-moving teams.</li>
<li>You are a design technologist - up to date with all the latest cutting-edge software, such as the entire Adobe Creative Suite (ACS), multimedia tools for Mac, etc.</li>
</ul>
