<h2>Marketing Manager - Life Sciences Industry</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div></div>
<p></p>
<div><div>
<div><div>
<p>PSC Biotech Corporation (PSC), a global employee-owned firm, is anchored by three primary business lines in the life sciences industry: consulting, enterprise software development and sales, and contract pharmaceutical manufacturing. As such, PSC supports clients worldwide through a variety of comprehensive services designed to provide the ability to perform to the highest industry standards with efficiency and meet all regulatory compliance requirements. The <b>Marketing Manager </b>performs a key role in achieving that goal. The position is responsible for day-to-day activities related to messaging and assets to support demand generation and customer marketing activities in the United States and globally. The incumbent is also responsible and accountable for the execution of the PSC mission, “to maintain the integrity and professionalism of our products and services while enabling steady growth and development.”</p>
</div></div>
<br>
<div><div>
<div><b>Responsibilities:</b></div>
</div></div>
<br>
<div><div>
<p><b>DEMONSTRATED COMPETENCIES:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Marketing – Ability to identify and expand company brand and awareness.</li>
<li>Assess industry business trends to capitalize on emerging opportunities for business growth.</li>
<li>Relationship Building – Work with and across formal and informal professional and social networks to build long-term partnerships and connections in the life science industry.</li>
<li>Negotiation Skills – Active listening needs assessment and present vision to build a consensus.</li>
<li>Analytical Skills – Identify, research, and demonstrate logical reasoning to communicate critical information.</li>
<li>Rational Persuasion – Present key factors, influencing factors, and strategic goals to assist stakeholders in identifying solutions.</li>
<li>Interpersonal Awareness – Ability to be aware of how actions, ideas, and communication can/does affect people and their outcomes.</li>
<li>Agility – Meet objectives by responding to competing and changing priorities and multiple tasks under inflexible timelines.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p>
</p>
<p><b>DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Existing knowledge of the life science marketplace and the demonstrated ability to quickly ramp up and build that experience on-the-job.</li>
<li>Existing knowledge of life science software, such as enterprise quality management system software or similar enterprise solutions for the life science industry.</li>
<li>Work with subject matter experts to create compelling marketing collateral for the sales team, including white papers, video (to be embedded in website), as well as, private YouTube Channel for specific marketing initiatives.</li>
<li>Develop and execute commercialization strategies for each PSC business unit.</li>
<li>Collaborate with each business unit to develop marketing/business development materials to effectively position and market/sell our products and services.</li>
<li>Develop and execute worldwide marketing plans, including market assessment, prioritization and development, etc.</li>
<li>Own a detailed understanding of competitive landscape and continually re-assess competitive hypotheses.</li>
<li>Develop external relationships with key trade organizations related to branding of each business unit.</li>
<li>Define and champion brand strategy within organization and secure needed resources.</li>
<li>Effectively partner and collaborate with key members of the management team to ensure appropriate inputs into marketing strategy.</li>
<li>Develop product message to be used across all media and customer engagement channels.</li>
<li>Collaborate with internal departments to develop PSC event strategies, along with event sponsorships.</li>
<li>Manage event/trade show operations, including the booth marketing and branding</li>
<li>Create and manage marketing collateral, which includes brochures, flyers, sell sheets, data sheets, product briefs, case studies, and white papers.</li>
<li>Manage the PSC web presence and social media campaigns.</li>
<li>Analyze and interpret marketing data, both quantitative and qualitative, using website analytic tools.</li>
<li>Lead and manage SEO/SEM, marketing database, social media and advertising campaigns.</li>
<li>Create marketing best practices, maintain marketing campaigns, and create drip and nurture programs.</li>
<li>Analyze data and provide recommendations for marketing effectiveness.</li>
<li>Develop and provide competitive intelligence on competing products and services.</li>
<li>Contribute to the growth of PSC by contributing the overall strategy of the company.</li>
<li>At all times, demonstrate the highest level of ethical behavior and personal integrity. Operate with the highest professional business ethics when dealing with clients, colleagues, and government entities.</li>
<li>Analytical skills, coupled with strong technical abilities, and a genuine passion for existing and new technologies and scientific advances.</li>
<li>Excellent communication and people skills are needed to succeed in this role.</li>
<li>Ability to travel as need.</li>
</ul>
