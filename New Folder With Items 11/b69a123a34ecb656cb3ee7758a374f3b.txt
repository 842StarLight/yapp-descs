<h2>Marketing Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Education changes lives forever. It broadens horizons, breaks down barriers, and fires imaginations. Like the University of Oxford of which we are a part, we are committed to uncompromising standards, freedom of expression, and the enrichment of lives through education. This is our motivation, our purpose, and our mission. It is why 100% of the money we make is reinvested into education and research. It is why all 6,000 of us in 52 countries care deeply about reaching more people, in more places, with world-class learning and research materials and services.</p>
<p>
As a member of the Academic Division’s Marketing Team, this role develops and ensures the successful implementation of integrated marketing strategy across several disciplines our print &amp; digital publishing program in the Humanities.</p>
<p><b>
PRINCIPAL ACCOUNTABILITIES/KEY RESULT AREAS</b></p>
<p><i>
 Strategic Marketing</i></p>
<ul>
<li>Develop and implement global integrated marketing strategy for assigned product areas, including but not limited to:
<ul>
<li>Content Marketing</li>
<li>Social Media and Community</li>
<li>E-mail</li>
<li>Partnerships</li>
<li>Event strategy</li>
<li>Digital Marketing</li>
<li>Advertising</li>
<li>Merchandising</li>
<li>SEO / SEM</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>Develop and execute product-specific global / regional marketing plans for assigned products</li>
<li>Manage assigned marketing budget; ensure team spend accordingly and stay within assigned budgets</li>
<li>Develop &amp; execute successful strategy to build community and lead content marketing strategy for key humanities disciplines and oversees key humanities social media channels</li>
<li>Work with Senior Marketing Management to set targets for the assigned products and track these accordingly; deliver marketing performance against targets and key metrics, demonstrated through in-depth analysis and reporting; ensure marketing KPIs are met</li>
<li>Implement author communication plan in discipline to ensure author understanding of market and marketing techniques; maintain high level of author satisfaction with marketing services/support; partner with Editorial to deliver marketing programs that support author acquisition</li>
</ul>
<p><i>Product Marketing/Management</i></p>
<ul>
<li>Suggest pricing for all products within disciplines and maintain global pricing for all products</li>
<li>Present key titles to key stakeholders (e.g. sales, key partners) with an in-depth understanding and knowledge of products, including core audience.</li>
<li>Contribute feedback to packaging for all products in discipline.</li>
</ul>
<p><i>Contribute to Publishing Strategy</i></p>
<ul>
<li>Keep abreast of all industry trends, including new products, applications, and competitive moves; and effectively communicate findings to internal partners.</li>
<li>Communicate market feedback to editorial</li>
</ul>
<p><i>Team Management &amp; Development</i></p>
<ul>
<li>Manage 2-3 junior marketing staff; effectively manage the team through day-to-day supervision, coaching, training, and recruitment.</li>
<li>Ensure high levels of performance and morale across the team; ensure the team functions efficiently and understands strategic goals</li>
<li>Ensure team stays educated on trends in Marketing and is adaptable and flexible to necessary changes in strategy or structure</li>
<li>Ensures team work well with central Marketing and is able to represent the Marketing strategy to Sales and Editorial</li>
</ul>
<p><b>KNOWLEDGE AND EXPERIENCE</b></p>
<ul>
<li>5+ years of marketing experience</li>
<li>Bachelor’s degree required</li>
<li>Ability to demonstrate excellent communication skills (written and verbal), influence decisions, and deliver impactful presentations.</li>
<li>Have experience of managing and/or mentoring staff</li>
<li>Demonstrate strong digital marketing and content marketing skills</li>
<li>Be able to balance a variety of responsibilities and handle many projects simultaneously, efficiently, and accurately.</li>
<li>Be willing and able work remotely from manager, and to represent the marketing team when required</li>
<li>Ability to work collaboratively with stakeholders in multiple locations and across functions</li>
</ul>
<p><b><i>GJC Level: I5 (for internal purposes only)</i></b></p>

We are committed to supporting diversity in our workforce and ensuring an inclusive environment where all individuals can thrive. We seek to employ a workforce representative of the markets that we serve and encourage applications from all.</i></p>
