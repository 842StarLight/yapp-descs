<h2>Identity Access Management Analyst</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>General Responsibilities
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<p><b>Job Purpose</b></p>
<p>
Hertz is looking for an Identity Access Management Analyst to manage and provision access to Data, Systems, and Applications. This is a great opportunity with a well-respected company in a demanding technical environment to work hands-on managing Windows security stack, and participating in enterprise-level projects. If you are looking for a position dealing with information security, and handling NTFS and application permissions, we would love to speak with you.</p>
<p><b>
Skill and Knowledge Requirements of Identity Access Analyst:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Provision and de-provision network accounts for associates through formal procedures</li>
<li>
Provision and de-provision application accounts for associates through formal procedures</li>
<li>
Aid in management of quarterly access reviews for network and application accounts</li>
<li>
Aid in provisioning Internet and VPN access</li>
<li>
Strong background in a Windows environment, with hands-on experience in Windows Security Stack</li>
<li>
Experience working with IAM tools like: Oracle IAM, MIM, Okta, RSA, IBM IAM or Courion.</li>
<li>
Mainframe experience a plus.</li>
<li>
Sales Force experience a plus.</li>
<li>
Foundation in documenting requirements using Structured Analysis Techniques, such as Use Cases, and Entity Relationship Diagrams</li>
<li>
Experience working with IT partners</li>
<li>
Ability to perform in a team environment, problem solving, and executing permission changes based on evolving information</li>
<li>
Previous experience working with O365, NTFS permissions, and Active Directory Groups</li>
<li>
Good customer service skills</li>
<li>
Ability to work in a global environment with US and offshore teams.</li>
<li>
Experience working with auditors and compliance teams.</li>
</ul>
</div></div></div>
