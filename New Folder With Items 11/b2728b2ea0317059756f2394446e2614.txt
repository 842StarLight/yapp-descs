<h2>Education Consultant - Napa Valley Unified School District</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Who is Promethean?</b></p>
<p>
Promethean is a leading global education technologies company who is re-inventing the way learning is being delivered. Our mission? To create immersive learning solutions that enable teachers to engage and energize students across the world! Based out of Seattle Washington, with regional centers of gravity in Atlanta (GA), Blackburn (UK), Shenzhen (China) and offices in 10 countries, our aim, is to foster a global community of practice where tools, resources, “how-to” knowledge, experience, and the wisdom to use them intelligently are all available to use and share.</p>
<p>
While Promethean is an established – well capitalized – and recognized EdTech brand, it feels like a startup with a fun, entrepreneurial energy and spirit! This is leading edge development in a fun and challenging atmosphere!</p>
<p><b>
A bit on this role…</b></p>
<p>
The Education Consultant is responsible for working directly with the Sales Teams and Project Teams to present the integrated solutions of the Promethean portfolio, including all Hardware, Software and Professional Development/training in Napa Valley Unified School District.</p>
<p><b>
Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Provides Professional Development (PD) sessions for Napa Valley Unified School District on Promethean hardware and software (as well as other technologies within the district)</li>
<li>
Master all courses in the Promethean professional development catalog / provide feedback for development of future courses / assist with development of customized PD coursework with Napa Valley Unified School District</li>
<li>
Works closely to build relationships with Napa Valley Unified School District and support their training and implementations of Promethean technology</li>
<li>
Builds long term relationships securing future Hardware and Software sales in Napa Valley Unified School District through effective PD and ongoing support</li>
<li>
Interacts with all levels of Napa Valley Unified School District administration and leadership to maintain communication</li>
<li>
Raise awareness in Napa Valley Unified School District of Promethean products and Promethean professional development offerings</li>
<li>
To strive to operate in line with our Values &amp; Ways of Working</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
The right candidate has:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
This position is a 24-month full-time position (with the likelihood of renewal)</li>
<li>
Must live locally to Napa Valley Unified School District to travel in/out of schools daily</li>
<li>
5 - 10 years of teaching experience in a US classroom</li>
<li>
Promethean/Interactive Flat Panel experience in both training and content/lesson development</li>
<li>
Strong presentation skills and the ability to excite/relate to educators at all levels in Napa Valley Unified School District</li>
<li>
Excellent public speaking skills with the ability to speak in front of large groups of educators is essential</li>
<li>
Firm understanding of the pedagogy of teaching and learning – shown through the ability to develop / design / deliver exceptional professional development</li>
<li>
Understanding of the business side of education to support the pre-sales organization</li>
<li>
High levels of knowledge on all aspects of educational technology, technology integration, and current education technology trends</li>
<li>
A demonstrated understanding of and experience with research-based approaches to the teaching of reading, writing, mathematics and Common Core</li>
<li>
Minimal travel required for regional/national meetings</li>
<li>
Results driven, well organized, flexible, and have strong follow-up skills</li>
</ul>
