<h2>Product Manager, Content Library</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>As Product Manager for our Content Library, you will be responsible for our core product – The BizLibrary Collection, which consists of approximately 7,000 video lessons, video courses, and interactive video programs covering a wide variety of business and professional development topics. This product includes content from more than 40 third-party producer partners as well as our internal BIzLibrary Productions team.</p>
<p>The Product Manager is responsible for full lifecycle ownership of the Content Library, including developing and maintaining product roadmaps, and managing relationships with both external and internal producer partners. The ability to communicate well with all areas of the company will be key in this role, as you'll work with other Product Managers and owners to define product release requirements. You will work with marketing communications to define the go-to-market strategy, helping them understand the product positioning, key benefits, and target customer. You will also serve as the internal and external evangelist for your product offering, occasionally working with the sales channel and key customers.</p>
<p>You must possess a unique blend of business and technical savvy; a big-picture vision, and the drive to make that vision a reality. You must enjoy spending time in the market to understand the problems of our target market, the current competitive landscape, and find innovative solutions for the broader market.</p>
<p>Guided by our shared values, we thrive in an environment where collaboration and openness are valued. We believe that innovation is powered by perspective and that teamwork and respect for each other lead to superior results. We elevate each other and obsess about doing the right thing. Our associates serve with humility and a deep respect for their responsibility in helping our customers achieve their goals and realize their dreams. Together, we are on a quest to improve workplace learning and retention forever.</p>
<p>KEY RESPONSIBILITIES</p>
<ul>
<li>Managing the entire product line life cycle from strategic planning to tactical activities including establishing and maintaining a product roadmap.</li>
<li>Specifying market requirements for current and future products by conducting market research supported by on-going visits to customers and non-customers.</li>
<li>Overall responsibility of maintaining and developing relationships with Producer Partners including acquisition and implementation of prospective producer partners.</li>
<li>Driving a solution set across development teams (primarily Development/Engineering, and Marketing Communications) through market requirements, product contract, and positioning.</li>
<li>Developing and implementing a company-wide go-to-market plan, working with all departments to execute.</li>
<li>Weekly/Monthly/Quarterly goal tracking, analysis, and reporting.</li>
<li>Analyzing potential partner relationships for the product.</li>
<li>Manage and professionally develop individual team members.</li>
<li>Provide internal support and resources to support BizLibrary’s strategic initiatives.</li>
</ul>
<p>REQUIREMENTS</p>
<ul>
<li>5+ years of software marketing/product management experience.</li>
<li>Strong interpersonal skills</li>
<li>Strong communication, written, and presentation skills</li>
<li>Smart, Driven, Curious, and Caring</li>
<li>Ability to establish a budget and analyze spend</li>
<li>Knowledgeable in technology</li>
<li>Strong Project Management skills while meeting deadlines</li>
<li>Bachelors/Master’s degree or equivalent work experience a strong plus.</li>
</ul>
