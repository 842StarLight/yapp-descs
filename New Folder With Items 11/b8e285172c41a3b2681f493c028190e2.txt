<div><b>A day in the life of an Acquisition Assistant – Inspection Management</b></div>
<div>The Acquisition Assistant – Inspection Management is a key contributor that helps facilitate the acquisition of targeted properties. Aside from working with a fun and hardworking team to accomplish this task, the Assistant will also support many of our business partners (Chief Investment Officer’s team, Accounting, Closings, Real Estate agents, and Escrow companies) during the transaction of real estate and effectively manage the administrative tasks involved in closing a sale. This is a full-time position. <b>The schedule for this position will be Monday - Friday 7:00 AM - 3:00 PM.</b> This position will report to the Inspection Management Project Manager.</div>
<ul>
<li>Support role for Project Manager. Will be trained to stand in for Project Manager when out of the office</li>
<li>Assist in training other back up team members as requested by management</li>
<li>Build and maintain relationships with internal customers in the field and regional level management</li>
<li>Follow up with regional sales and acquisition teams on short DDP Approvals nearing expiration</li>
<li>Manage the calendar for Short DDP and Pre-Inspect properties</li>
<li>Request pre-inspect counter offers and board inspections for approved pre-Inspect properties in proprietary software</li>
<li>Coordinate with construction teams to schedule Pre-Inspect and Short DDP properties for approval</li>
<li>Track short DDP properties throughout the process from initial request with the construction team, through boarding and assignment in proprietary software</li>
<li>Generate working reports for the regional acquisition assistants (as needed):
<ul>
<li>DDP extension follow-ups</li>
<li>Concession follow-ups</li>
<li>COE extension requests</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>Adhere to Standard Inspection Management procedures for:
<ul>
<li>Short DDP approval and tracking</li>
<li>Do Not Buy processes</li>
<li>Offerpad and Opendoor access needs</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>Assist with workflow for the Outstanding Inspection Daily Report</li>
<li>Adhere to processes to prevent loss of potential contracts, earnest money, and reputation</li>
<li>Monitor "Do Not Buy" properties by communicating with CIO to ensure they are addressed in a timely manner</li>
<li>Support role for the construction teams and specialists (as needed):
<ul>
<li>Take over communication with agents when escalated issues arise</li>
<li>Monitor properties that are stalled in the process, enabling the teams to focus on accomplishing prioritized goals</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>Assist project manager with monitoring inspection management inbox as needed</li>
<li>Weekly DEA Check Report</li>
<li>Provide support to regional acquisitions assistants as time permits</li>
<li>Provide constructive feedback to project manager regarding current processes and possible solutions for better time management</li>
<li>Monitor Outstanding Inspection Management Daily report as needed</li>
<li>Other tasks as requested by management</li>
</ul>
