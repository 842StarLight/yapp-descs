<h2>Technology and Digital Manager (RPA)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>As a Tech &amp; Digital Manager Consultant at RSM, you will work with businesses in diverse industries while working on numerous projects per year, you will work on several pieces of any particular assignment - not just one small part - and you will feel a great sense of accomplishment when you are done. Working in a mutually respectful team environment helps our consultants perform at their best and advance their career and professional capabilities.</p>
<p>RSM's Tech &amp; Digital Consulting Group provides strategy and advisory assistance to improve the overall effectiveness of the Tech &amp; Digital function.</p>
<p><b>Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Provide analysis, development and documentation of improvement opportunities</li>
<li>
Facilitate user workshops to gather and document business needs, requirements, weaknesses and challenges</li>
<li>
Continual learning to better understand RSM's array of services, with the intent to enhance value to the client through integration of services</li>
<li>
Aspire to career growth by assuming more active roles in technology assessments over time, as knowledge and experience progress</li>
<li>
Staying up-to-date on the latest process and IT advancements to automate and modernize systems and regulations</li>
<li>
Effectively documenting and communicating your insights and plans to cross-functional team members and management</li>
<li>
Serving as a liaison between stakeholders and users</li>
<li>
Evaluating business processes, anticipating requirements, uncovering areas for improvement, and developing and implementing solutions</li>
<li>
Prioritizing initiatives based on business needs and requirements</li>
<li>
Working closely with clients, technicians, and managerial staff</li>
<li>
Lead all client delivery activities and discovery session that include requirements gathering and desired workflows with clients to understand and document their business needs (i.e. budget forecasting, financial reporting, and consolidation)</li>
<li>
Establish yourself as a trusted advisor to clients, while managing their expectations</li>
<li>
Manage multiple projects and project teams to deliver exceptional client experience</li>
<li>
Define business requirements for a number of different types of technology engagements</li>
<li>
Interact and communicate effectively with Managers and middle management executives</li>
<li>
Assisting the project teams through agreed upon phases on a variety of projects which could include: best practices, process re-engineering, finance strategy and organizational development, business integration planning and execution, performance management software selection, design and implementation, ERP software selection, implementation, and optimization or operational and IT strategy assessments and due diligence</li>
<li>
Demonstrate quality project execution and delivery by successfully working with manager in monitoring project/program team economics, performance against the job arrangement and budget, and proactively</li>
<li>
Familiar with methodologies, tools, approaches, points of view, thought leadership to support the practice</li>
</ul>
