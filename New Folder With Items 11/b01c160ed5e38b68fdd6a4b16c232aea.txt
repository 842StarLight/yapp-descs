<h2>Advertising Sales Account Executive</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Job Summary </b></p>
<p>The position of Advertising Sales Account Executive will sell and service local and regional accounts as assigned and/or located within a geographical territory. The primary sales effort will be to solicit current and new business prospects to use one or more of the publications and website in our product portfolio. Generally, the Account Executive will be focusing on several military publications at any given time that are published monthly, quarterly or annually. This position will report directly to the Director of Military Publications as well as work with members of their team, and the Chief Revenue Officer of the region. The ideal candidate will be self-motivated, have effective presentation skills, creativity, strategic thinker, idea generator, good oral and written comprehension, deductive reasoning, strong work ethic, high computer aptitude, effective public speaking and ability to present to senior marketing executives, military executives and business owners. Preferred primary location will be the DC Metropolitan Region area or in the nearby communities spending 75% of their time on outside sales.</p>
<p>Previous military experience a plus but not required.</p>
<p><b>Job Functions </b></p>
<ul>
<li>This position will sell solutions to both current and prospective clients using a variety of approaches including in-person, via the telephone, email solicitation, and online presentations.</li>
<li>This position will work with other military staffers to sell various initiatives.</li>
<li>This position will perform back-office administrative tasks as appropriate to ensure that advertising runs according to schedule.</li>
<li>This position will work with employees of other departments to process advertising materials and collect payment.</li>
<li>This position will spend 75% of their time in the sales field.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Minimum Requirements</b></p>
<p>The successful candidate will have a preferred minimum of 2+ years of experience in the print and/or internet media industry.</p>
<p><b>Technology Skills</b></p>
<p>Proficiency in Microsoft Office, Word, Excel and Outlook is required.</p>
<p>Ability to learn new programs required.</p>
<p><b>Education</b></p>
<p>Bachelor's degree in Marketing or an equivalent work experience.</p>
