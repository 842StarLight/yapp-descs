<p>
The VMware DICE team (Data Integrated Customer Engagement) is hiring. DICE is a VMware sales strategy that enables the use of customer data to analyze datacenter infrastructure, model business value associated with an investment in VMware’s portfolio of solutions and track the delivery of identified milestones after the sale.</p>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Job Role and Responsibility:</b></h2>
<p>
We are looking for senior level full stack web developer to apply their expertise in browser-based app development and expansive knowledge of latest technologies to deliver ongoing enhancements for a high-quality sales application using best practices for user experience. Candidates should have the ability to write clean, Angular 5+, JavaScript /Typescript based code, collaborate with internal product management organizations and key VMware Partners. This is a dynamic role with opportunities for career advancement outside of traditional developer job functions.</p>

<li>
Develop integrations to various 3rd party API endpoints, applications and cloud services</li>
<li>
Proficiency with SQL and Relational Databases</li>

Ability work and collaborate with a global team</li>
