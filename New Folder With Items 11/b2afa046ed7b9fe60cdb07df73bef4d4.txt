<h2>Help Desk Coordinator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Kelly Government Solutions is hiring for a Help Desk Coordinator on a long term contract for our customer in Lincolnshire, IL.</p>
<p>Important: Candidate must be a US citizen and will be required to apply and qualify for a US Government Sensitive Clearance. Experience with the US Post Office indirect or direct is highly desirable!</p>
<p>Selected candidate will have keen time management skills and be able to focus on the tasks at hand. Also, someone who is not afraid to raise their hand and ask for help if need be.</p>
<p><b>Duties and Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>IMDAS (Intelligent Mail Data Acquisition System) is a customer service organization and our customers have very specific service contract requirements and the agents are responsible for ensuring that we are managing customer expectations toward contractual compliance.</li>
<li>Provide primary interface for all incoming IMDAS calls / emails pertaining to technical support provide coordination of technical resources for all severity issues.</li>
<li>Flexible 8 hr. shift. Hours of operation are 7:00 am to 7:00 pm (CST), 5 days a week (Monday - Friday), 365 days a year, including U.S. holidays.</li>
<li>Gathers all relevant caller information and inputs information into the departmental database in a timely and accurate manner.</li>
<li>Exercise multi-tasking skills when managing multiple systems and applications during customer interaction Create/Track/Route issues via Service Now and EIMS software application.</li>
<li>Assists clients in installing and effectively using the product for their application.</li>
<li>Identifies bugs and makes recommendations and/or enhancements based on user feedback.</li>
<li>Ensures customer satisfaction by promptly answering customer requests for assistance.</li>
<li>Works to achieve targeted service metrics related to average speed of answer, abandonment rates, and average inbound call length.</li>
<li>Escalates cases to a Technical Advisor or Team Leader after exhausting all methods to resolve the case within the context of the customer issue.</li>
<li>Completes quarterly goals on technical training and development.</li>
<li>Escalates customer complaints related to fit, form, or functionality to the Quality Assurance department in a timely manner. Models corporate and departmental policies, ISO procedures, and terms &amp; conditions of sale.</li>
<li>Performs other duties as assigned.</li>
<li>Maintain Departmental/Organizational service level requirements.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Requirements:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>AA/AS degree in Computer Science or demonstrated working knowledge of installation and configuration of personal computers or PC peripheral devices.</li>
<li>1 year of technical support troubleshooting experience in a help desk environment.</li>
<li>Strong PC operating system knowledge, Windows, and driver experience related to supporting hardware.</li>
<li>Strong written and verbal communication skills.</li>
<li>Basic programming and Network communications skills.</li>
<br>
</ul>
