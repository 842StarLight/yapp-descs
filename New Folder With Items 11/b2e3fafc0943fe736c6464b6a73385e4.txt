<div>As a Microsoft Gold Certified Partner with Certified for Microsoft Dynamics (CfMD) advanced supply chain solutions, Blue Horseshoe is aggressively building our Microsoft Dynamics AX consultant practice. We are currently looking for a Microsoft D365 Production Consultant who can address key business challenges such as complexity reduction, lean manufacturing and operational excellence, process improvement, enterprise transformation and growth through fast innovation. As a Consultant with Blue Horseshoe, you will have the opportunity to shape value-added consulting solutions that enable our clients to meet the changing needs of the global landscape

<br>
<b>Position:</b> Microsoft D365 Production Consultant
<br>
<br>
<b>Job</b> <b>Responsibilities:</b>
<br>
<br>
As a Microsoft D365 Production Consultant, you will be responsible for the following:
<ul>
<li>Facilitate discovery, design, development, and deployment activities</li>
<li>Configure and implement Dynamics Manufacturing/Production module</li>
<li>Manage resources and deliver project tasks, both external and internal</li>
<li>Perform gap analysis between system functionality and client requirements</li>
<li>Communicate and document system specifications for clients and team members</li>
<li>Document test scenarios and perform unit, system, and data conversion testing tasks</li>
<li>Configure application as appropriate to conform to business process definition.</li>
<li>Provide excellent customer interface skills throughout the project life cycle</li>
<li>Provide periodic status reports and accurate estimates and timelines of work</li>
<li>Train client on use and maintenance of Dynamics AX</li>
<li>Ability to work on multiple projects</li>
</ul>
<b>Qualifications:</b>
<ul>
<li>Minimum of 2 years Functional knowledge, using Microsoft Dynamics AX ERP (AX 2009, AX 2012, R1/R2/R3 and D365) in full cycle implementation.</li>
<li>Knowledge of MRP, lean manufacturing, master planning, etc.</li>
<li>Previous supply chain management and software experience is also helpful</li>
<li>Bachelor's degree in a business or technically oriented field</li>
<li>Working knowledge of relational databases and data migration concepts</li>
<li>Team player with strong communication, organization, and client interaction skills</li>
<li>Experience in Product creation and management, Trade agreements, Procurements and Sales</li>
<li>Ability to travel up to 60-80% domestically</li>
<li>Must be legally eligible to work in the US on a permanent basis without sponsorship</li>
</ul>
<b>Certification(s), desired but not required, in any of the following disciplines:</b>
<ul>
<li>Dynamics AX Trade &amp; Logistics</li>
<li>Microsoft Distribution and Trade Certifications</li>
<li>Dynamics AX Production</li>
</ul>
