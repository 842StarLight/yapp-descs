<h2>Project Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>Job Description<br>
<br>
<p>Conga is looking for a talented Project Manager to join our Professional Services organization. Working in a fast-paced, creative environment, the Project Manager will be primarily responsible for the success of Conga implementations. The Project Manager will be the main contact for client projects, create project plans, coordinate cross-functional deliverables meetings, ensure milestones are completed and delivered on schedule, and drive to a successful client go-live.</p>
<p>
Responsibilities:</p>
<ul>
<li><p>
Accountable for all elements of the project including scope, schedule, risks, dependencies, and issues to deliver exceptional value and client satisfaction</p></li>
<li><p>
Facilitate a smooth transition for new clients from the time of sale through the implementation process</p></li>
<li><p>
Report on project success criteria results, metrics, test and deployment management activities</p></li>
<li><p>
Participate in establishing practices, templates, policies, tools and partnerships to expand and mature these capabilities for the organization</p></li>
<li><p>
Prepare estimates and detailed project plan for all phases of the project</p></li>
<li><p>
Manage project scope and changes</p></li>
<li><p>
Monitor, track and control outcomes to resolve issues, conflicts, dependencies and critical path deliverables</p></li>
<li><p>
Identify and develop trusted advisor relationships with project and program stakeholders and sponsors</p></li>
<li><p>
Ensure projects are proceeding according to scope, schedule and quality standards via internal team collaboration and client communication</p></li>
</ul>
</div>
<br>
<div>Qualifications<br>
<br>
<ul>
<li><p>Bachelor’s degree or greater</p></li>
<li><p>
Strong task and client management skills</p></li>
<li><p>
Ability to work with and manage multiple clients / projects simultaneously</p></li>
<li><p>
3+ years of high tech SaaS project management experience, including gathering requirements from the client, tracking and planning projects, and working with business stakeholders within a cross-functional matrix environment</p></li>
<li><p>
Proven ability to demonstrate and drive for results and accountability of business needs</p></li>
<li><p>
Experience in successfully leading projects and programs to on-schedule close</p></li>
<li><p>
Familiarity with PMI standards</p></li>
<li><p>
Previous Salesforce experience or certification preferred</p></li>
<li><p>
Experience with Conga’s products is preferred</p></li>
<li><p>
Contract Lifecycle Management experience</p></li>
<li><p>
Travel expectations - 25%</p></li>
</ul>
