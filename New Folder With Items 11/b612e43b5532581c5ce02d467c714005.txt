<h2>Business Development Executive</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<b>About LeafLink:</b><br>
LeafLink is a SaaS marketplace platform that provides licensed cannabis dispensaries the ability to order from their favorite brands, as well as a suite of software tools for those brands to manage their operations and scale. With 2,600+ dispensaries and more than 900+ leading brands in 15 States, LeafLink is setting the industry standard for how cannabis brands and retailers work together. Our team, backed by funding from leading VC's, is poised to define the cannabis wholesale market. LeafLink was recently one of Fast Company's 'Top 10 Most Innovative Companies in Enterprise' for 2018, joining the ranks of Amazon, Slack, and VMWare and we are hungry for more!
<br><br>
<b>The Role:</b><br>
LeafLink is seeking an experienced Business Development Executive to join our fast-growing team. In this role you will be responsible for building LeafLink Financial’s relationship with the largest cannabis brands in the industry and create a network effect across the LeafLink platform, setting a new standard on how the industry transacts. This role will allow you to build relationships with executive teams and strategize on ways LeafLink can improve and simplify their business operations. This role is highly collaborative, requires creative thinking, sales expertise, and an understanding of all product offerings and platform features on LeafLink with a focus on our financial services offerings. This role will require you to interface directly with our executive team, client experience, sales, marketing, and engineering teams. You need to be a self-starter who is comfortable with change, enjoys a very fast pace, and can juggle many tasks and deadlines.
<br><br>
<b>Qualifications:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>5+ years of experience in a sales or business development role working with middle market or enterprise level companies</li>
<li>2+ years working within financial services. Experience working in the FinTech industry is a plus</li>
<li>2+ years in B2B sales or business development</li>
<li>4+ years working in and meeting monthly forecasted KPIs</li>
<li>Direct experience selling to or interfacing with C-Level relationships</li>
<li>Experience in professional relationship building and negotiation skills</li>
<li>Must demonstrate the ability to understand and articulate various products related to loans, lines of credit, trade credit, factoring, treasury management and other financial services</li>
<li>Ability to work proficiently within Microsoft office is a must</li>
</ul>
<b>What You’ll Do:</b><br>
<ul>

<li>Understand market trends and competition within the FinTech and Cannabis spaces</li>
<li>Ability to put together and analyze data to effectively strategize with C-Levels at cannabis brands and other LeafLink partners</li>
<li>Create and recommend sales process and product/technology improvements</li>
</ul>
