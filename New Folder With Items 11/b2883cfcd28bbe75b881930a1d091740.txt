<p><b>Marketing and Advertising Coordinator</b></p>
<p>MediaBrains is seeking a full-time Marketing and Advertising Coordinator with a strong interest in digital advertising operations. We are looking for a motivated and highly energetic self-starter with an understanding of online marketing and copy writing to join our team. This position is highly visible and plays a critical role in supporting new sales growth of the firm. If you are looking for a ground floor opportunity to grow your career with an industry leader, MediaBrains may be the place for you.</p>
<p>Key responsibilities include:</p>
<p>· Create, edit and manage digital content for company profiles and service fulfillment within our business directories.</p>
<p>· Manage and perform all aspects of advertisement fulfillment.</p>
<p>· Act as advertiser’s first point of contact to communicate digital requirements and content for advertisement setup. This is achieved through phone calls and emails.</p>
<p>· Provide excellent customer support and respond to advertiser inquiries in a timely and professional manner.</p>
<p>· Assemble all creative deliverables for publisher-sponsored print advertisements.</p>
<p>· Review, edit and approve submissions for new companies and new products, questions and answers.</p>
<p>· Manage email campaign schedule and reporting.</p>
<p>· Analyze and report on email campaign results, highlighting successes, trends and opportunities.</p>
<p>Requirements:</p>
<p>· Bachelor's Degree in Marketing, Communications, or Journalism.</p>
<p>· Digital advertising or online marketing experience is preferred.</p>
<p>· Ability to work independently with superior attention to detail.</p>
<p>· Strong organizational, analytical and communication skills.</p>
<p>· Writing, editing and proof reading skills.</p>
<p>· Strong MS Office proficiency.</p>
<p>· Understanding of writing for search engine optimization.</p>
<p>Compensation:</p>
<p>· Salary range: $34,000 to $40,000 based on experience.</p>
