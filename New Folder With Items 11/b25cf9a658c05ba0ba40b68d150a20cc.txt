<h2>Estimator, Multifamily Cabinetry (Lansing)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>ACProducts, Inc. (acpi) is a national kitchen and bath cabinetry company with six brands serving the stock and semi-custom, framed and frameless cabinetry markets. ACProducts, Inc. (acpi) is the largest independent manufacturer and distributor of cabinets in the United States with 14 brands serving the stock and semi-custom, framed and frameless cabinetry markets. acpi has been owned by American Industrial Partners (AIP) since October, 2012. Headquartered in The Colony, Texas, acpi has 11 manufacturing operations across Pennsylvania, Indiana, Texas, Minnesota, Colorado, Oregon, and California.
</p>
<p>We are looking for a full-time <b>Multi-Family Estimator</b> to join our growing company.</p>
<p><b>
** Please note: This is only 1 position posted to multiple cities, so you only need to apply to one position for consideration. **</b></p>
<p><b>
Position Purpose of the Multi-Family Estimator:
</b></p>
<p>The purpose of this position is to prepare and estimate casework bids for Multi-family projects. The position will also provide support and direction to account managers and customers.</p>
<p><b>
Major tasks, responsibilities and key accountabilities </b><b>of the Multi-Family Estimator</b><b>:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Manage / assist select Account Managers bidding and ordering operations to include estimation assistance and Salesforce.com reporting</li>
<li>
Read blueprints and prepare reports</li>
<li>
Work with all sales support personnel (including contract employees) in accurate estimating, and designing projects</li>
<li>
Use SFDC to make sure bids are logged and updated</li>
<li>
Use provided lead source help to generate qualified leads within the sales team</li>
<li>
Build effective business relationships with internal customer team</li>
<li>
Ensure compliance to operational standards, company policies, and safety standards across the channel</li>
<li>
Monitor and ensure compliance with company financial policies concerning profitability and bidding policies</li>
<li>
Ensure feedback to NPD team regarding products needed
</li>
<li>Prepare and review kitchen cabinet layouts and designs for function, anesthetics, and cost</li>
<li>
Place orders, read blueprints, and prepare reports.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
External Interface </b><b>of the Multi-Family Estimator</b><b>:</b></p>
<p>
Will interface with a variety of groups to include multi-family, senior living, military, hospitality, assisted living, and architectural</p>
