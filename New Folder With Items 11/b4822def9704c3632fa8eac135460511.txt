<h2>Senior Financial Analyst Sales Ops</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div></div>
<div>
<div>
<div>Senior Financial Analyst with experience forecasting revenues/expenditures and reporting monthly results. Able to multi task and work in a fast pace environment. 3-5 years of Financial Analysis experience along with 2-3 years of SAAS software experience desired.</div>
<div>
</div>
<div>
Responsibilities</div>
<ul>
<li>Develop financial models through benchmarking and process analysis</li>
<li>Forecast Revenue quarterly
</li>
<li>Participate in budgeting (for departments and projects)</li>
<li>Develop and maintain weekly reporting package</li>
</ul>
<div>
</div>
<div>
Requirements</div>
<ul>
<li>Proven work experience as a Senior Financial Analyst, Financial Analyst or similar role</li>
<li>Hands-on experience with financial software and Salesforce</li>
<li>Expertise in MS Excel and Tableau</li>
<li>Familiarity with finance databases</li>
<li>Understanding of Enterprise Resource Planning systems (ERP)</li>
<li>Excellent analytical skills</li>
<li>Ability to present financial data using detailed reports and charts</li>
<li>Confidentiality in handling sensitive financial information</li>
<li>BS degree in Finance, Accounting or Economics</li>
<li>Able to communicate across all levels of the organization</li>
<li>Creative and by nature a problem solver</li>
<li>Self starter, independent, willing to travel up to 25% of time.</li>
</ul>
