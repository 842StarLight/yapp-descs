<h2>Local Spot Sales Account Executive</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Overview
</b></h2>
<div><div><div><div>This position is responsible for generating revenue among all agencies and direct clients in the Texas region for ION O&amp;O local spot stations. Position is focused on developing new and growing business among major national and local agencies via strong relationships and superior client service by selling ION value to achieve target rates and quarterly target revenue goals.
</div></div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<ul>
<li>Generate and increase revenue of new and developed accounts working with advertising agencies and clients in and around the Texas-OK region.</li>
<li>
Meet quarterly and annual revenue goals with the primary focus on rate achievement and activity growth without compromising rates.</li>
<li>
Achieve pricing objectives set by commercial operations and P&amp;I management.</li>
<li>
Own, manage, and develop relationships with clients, agency planner,s and buyers through day-to-day interactions; actively grow industry relationships in order to gain marketplace intelligence.</li>
<li>
Understand customer needs and opportunities to develop creative solutions.</li>
<li>
Customize sales presentations and proposals utilizing research to create compelling value propositions for clients and agencies.</li>
<li>
Generate sales activity, forecasting, and target-pending reports.</li>
<li>
Communicate effectively and ensure clients receive superior customer service.</li>
<li>
Excellent negotiating and presentation skills. Ability to garner target rates.</li>
</ul>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Qualifications
</b></h2>
<div><div><div><ul>
<li>Minimum of 5 years local-national spot advertising sales experience with local broadcast or cable.</li>
<li>
Proven track record of increasing revenue; developing new business; established agency-client relationships.</li>
<li>
Ideal candidate must have large client, buying, and planner contact list and established relationships.</li>
<li>
Must possess analytical skills deemed vital to the sales process and ION culture including Nielsen, research information, and sales-account sales metric data.</li>
<li>
Must be computer literate with command of Microsoft Excel and PowerPoint.</li>
<li>
Must be very detail-oriented and able to meet deadlines on a regular basis.</li>
<li>
Some travel required within the Texas region for sales and the East Coast for corporate meetings.</li>
<li>
College degree required.</li>
</ul></div></div></div>
