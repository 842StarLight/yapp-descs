<h2>Marketing Coordinator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p><b>Intro</b></p>
<p>
Bodhala is the leading machine learning-based technology startup focused specifically on the $400b legal market. Bodhala’s best-in-class technology brings data and insights to Fortune 500 companies across the country.</p>
<p>
Headquartered in the Financial District of Manhattan, Bodhala has tripled its headcount year to date and is positioned for continued fast-paced growth through the remainder of the year, with locations across the United States and in Europe.</p>
<p>
Bodhala is looking for a Marketing Coordinator to join our fast-growing marketing team. The person coming into this role will be responsible for managing the day-to-day strategy and execution of marketing projects and campaigns. This position will lead efforts across the go-to-market organization to help drive pipeline – from supporting events to running point on nurture campaigns.</p>
<p>
Reporting to the Head of Marketing, and working closely with the rest of the marketing team, the Marketing Coordinator will play a critical role in supporting the delivery of efforts that support the broader marketing and business goals.</p>
<p>
This is an exciting opportunity to be part of building something from the ground up and put your own mark on the company.</p>
<p><b>
Responsibilities</b></p>
<p>
Campaign Strategy &amp; Execution</p>
<ul>
<li>Own and manage cross-channel campaign plan across various audiences including launch dates and key milestones.</li>
<li>Create and analyze reports to determine a campaign’s performance.</li>
<li>Use data to influence the content strategy and prioritization.</li>
<li>Manage social media platforms.</li>
</ul>
<p>Events</p>
<ul>
<li>Manage hosted events – from planning and logistics to negotiating with vendors and driving event attendance.</li>
<li>Coordinate and support trade shows and conferences, including registration, booth and equipment ordering and logistics, promotional items, and collateral support</li>
</ul>
<p>Sales Enablement</p>
<ul>
<li>Order and distribute sales promotional items and client gifts on an as-needed basis.</li>
<li>Organize digital and physical materials so that internal teams have easy access to our collateral, brochures and swag.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>First 30 Days</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Get to know the business through informational interviews with members across the organization.</li>
<li>Set individual KPIs for the remainder of the year.</li>
<li>Take inventory of current campaigns YTD and performance.</li>
<li>Familiarize self with current marketing materials.</li>
<li>Understand 6 month marketing plan.</li>
<li>Communication of complex technical information and concepts in a business-friendly approach</li>
</ul>
<p><b>First 60 Days</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Strategize how to support marketing plan.</li>
<li>Take over all event evaluation and management.</li>
<li>Take ownership of marketing calendar – including campaigns, events, and social media.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>First 90 Days</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Create format for monthly and quarterly internal business reviews.</li>
<li>Manage resources across internal and external team to ensure timely delivery of projects.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Qualifications</b></p>
<ul>
<li>2- 3 years of experience in general or field marketing, ideally in B2B SaaS.</li>
<li>Solid understanding of marketing fundamentals, including marketing / sales funnel, personas, campaign-level KPIs, etc.</li>
<li>Exceptional writing and verbal communication skills.</li>
<li>Strong project management skills with an acute attention to detail.</li>
<li>Comfortable working in a results-driven, revenue-focused, metrics-based organization.</li>
<li>Driven, analytical nature, with entrepreneurial spirit that excels in lean organizations.</li>
<li>Creative, assertive and solution-oriented when faced with obstacles.</li>
<li>You have an uncanny ability to ramp up quickly on complex technology, and are great at translating features and functions into business benefits, defensible differentiation and value.</li>
</ul>
