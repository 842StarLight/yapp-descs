<b>Position Description:</b><br>
As a member of the Risk Management team, the EHS Risk Management Consultant will play a crucial role in developing and maintaining strong relationships with clients to establish and demonstrate exceptional industry and regulatory knowledge through onsite field audits, employee trainings, and regulatory interaction. The objective of an EHS Risk Management Consultant is to minimize risk to increase profitability for the client. This position develops and maintains relationships with district clients, potential clients, dealer group management, and state dealer associations. A professional, likable, persuasive, and customer service-oriented demeanor is essential for success.
<br><br>
<b>In the first 30 days you will:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Inherit a customer service base and immediately establish relationships with Mangers and Management teams across many different businesses</li>
</ul>
<b>In the first 60 days you will:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Complete new hire orientation training to be fully operational as a KPA Risk Management Consultant</li>
</ul>
<b>In the first 6 months you will:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Establish and demonstrate exceptional industry and regulatory knowledge through onsite field audits, employee training, and regulatory interaction</li>
<li>Organize a quarterly visit schedule to maximize travel efficiency based on local and distant client groupings and service frequency</li>
<li>Assist with and/or prepare various compliance documentation as needed (i.e., Hazard Communication, Respiratory Protection, Tier II reporting, OSHA 300 logs, Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) ID numbers, tank permitting, and Spill Prevention Control &amp; Countermeasures (SPCC) Plans)</li>
</ul>
<b>By the end of the first year you will:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Assist in developing, implementing, and monitoring accident prevention programs, policies, and procedures to ensure continuous safe working conditions in accordance with State, Federal, and Local laws and regulations to assist the client in striving to achieve Zero Accident / lost time injuries</li>
<li>Help communicate and implement environmental management compliance systems</li>
<li>Support the ongoing growth of the company by performing exceptional customer service, documenting and submitting 20 referrals or sales leads per year to your area Account Executive</li>
<li>Responsible for promoting productive relationships with dealer group management for KPA clients and State Dealer Association Management. Maintains effective internal and external public relations</li>
</ul>
Performs other duties and fulfills other responsibilities, as assigned.
