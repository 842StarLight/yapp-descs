About the Team:</p>
<p>
Rally Health is seeking a Brand Design Operations Manager to oversee the daily operations of our Brand Design team in San Francisco. The position will support a variety of talent including art directors, designers and writers while assigning jobs, setting priorities to meet deadlines and troubleshooting. The role is a partnership with the brand creative director, working closely with marketing managers, marketing project managers, sales and the corporate website development team.</p>
<p>
Your day to day:</p>
<p></p>
<p><br>
The qualified individual should have experience managing resources and projects for print (event booths, signs, and collateral, print advertising etc), digital (web, email, social media, animation, video production) against marketing roadmaps with cross functional teams. This person should also be able to approach problem solving in a composed, solution oriented, and highly creative manner, as there is a continuous need for original and forward thinking with respect to evolving needs of the team and company process. The right candidate will possess strong preference for working in an intimate, collaborative, and open work environment. This role reports into the brand marketing team and it’s established process.</p>
