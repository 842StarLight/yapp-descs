<p><b>About the Team</b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
The International Technology team is responsible for the architecture, engineering, implementation, support, and operations of international systems portfolio of Williams Sonoma brands like Williams Sonoma, Pottery Barn, West Elm, Mark &amp; Graham &amp; Rejuvenation in multiple channels like e-commerce, stores, franchises and wholesale.</p>
<p>
We currently operate company-owned retail stores in the United States, Puerto Rico, Canada, United Kingdom, and Australia and have franchise operated retail stores throughout the Middle East, Mexico, the Philippines, and South Korea. Wholesale partnerships in UK and Ireland support our UK based stores.</p>
<p><b>
About The Role</b></p>
<p>
Williams Sonoma is seeking a candidate for 'Production Support Operations Engineer' role within its international systems team. This role will require a combination of technical skills along with a detailed understanding of business processes to support day to day business operations, address technical issues and fixes. The candidate should have good knowledge of business processes and methodologies in the retail space, along with a strong analytical mindset. You will partner with key stakeholders, developers, architects and product managers to describe production issues, participate in solutioning and design, testing and validation as well as implementing workarounds while issues are being resolved.</p>
<p><b>
Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Assume lead role in troubleshooting system level problems in production environment</li>
<li>Proactively maintain and ensure integrity (completeness) of system and sub-system data</li>
<li>Support business teams with queries and implementing fixes to production issues.</li>
<li>Mentor and train new users on the system functionality</li>
<li>Administer the system users, permissions and assignments</li>
<li>Lead and support development efforts to operationally improve business process through systems</li>
<li>Analyze, design and implement process improvement changes in collaboration with cross-functional teams</li>
<li>Support and actively participate in a full systems implementation, including the development of BRD's, FRD's, Process Flows, Training, UAT and Change Management.</li>
<li>Drive process improvements in the support team and leverage ServiceNow to manage support metrics and workflows.</li>
<li>Monitor trends of repeating issues and engage with a wider team for permanent resolution.</li>
<li>Develop automated support procedures to reduce the amount of manual effort in supporting production operations.</li>
</ul>
