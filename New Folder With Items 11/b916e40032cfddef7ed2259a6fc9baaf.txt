<h2>Office Operations Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Are you looking for something rewarding and challenging? Do you want to be a part of an elite team? Look no further. We are looking for motivated, passionate, customer service oriented and driven Office Operations Manager to help our team achieve their best. This critical role at Greenlight Guru is part of the operation’s team that makes everything run smooth and seamless. Our team is everything. You will work side by side with the COO to help make the experiences of our team, customers and vendors the ultimate experience.</p>
<p>
Primary Responsibilities:</p>
<ul>
<li>
<p>Define, document, standardize and manage all operational processes to create clarity and a systematic approach to how we operate. Make things clear, easy and efficient for the team. Includes finding new solutions and software to automate our processes.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Work with the finance team to manage and streamline accounts payable, accounts receivable, and payroll processes.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Assist with accounting system, forecasting, budgeting and other financial responsibility including service as a liaison to our external finance team.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Assist with the tax year end taxes, reviews, 606. international and sales tax.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Maintain, assemble and submit data for our training and R&amp;D grants.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Maintain and optimize financial and operational systems including day to day updates to executive reporting such as cashflows.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Point of contact between our leadership team, company employees, customer and vendors when necessary. Manage information and communication flow.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Coordinate customer and vendor visits, communications, preparation, office organization and cleanliness, food, drinks. Ensure they feel welcome and special.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Serve as the point person for office related duties including space, maintenance, supplies, equipment, etc.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Ensures the office is very organized, clean and efficient.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Plan and coordinate employee engagement activities, special events and employee meetings.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Assists team with employee recruitment, interviews, onboarding and retention.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Support activities around training requirements and development activities through communication with management and employees.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Tracks assets and supplies.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Assist with event travel and schedules for the team for important events.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Assist with purchasing of supplies, equipment and new hire equipment.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Communication with external vendors and contractors to include tracking projects and ensuring they meet contractual/ agreement obligations.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Own and manage new locations, moves, equipment/furniture and vendors.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Completes various work assignments requiring analytical ability, independent judgement, creativity and problem-solving skills with minimal supervision.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Communication skills must be at a level of effectively communicate with executives as well as internal/external clients both on-site and remotely to maintain workflow.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Handle customer payment deposits that are received through the mail.</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p>Requirements:</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>
<p>5+ years of recent office management or executive assistant experience required.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Must be detail-oriented, extremely organized and able to multitask and deliver results in a high-volume, fast-paced work environment with very strict, changing deadlines.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Have excellent interpersonal and writing skills with the ability to communicate and connect with all levels of the organization providing a high level of customer service.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Must be extremely professional and poised, exercising independent judgement and discretion while maintaining a high level of confidentiality.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Must have excellent computer skills, including proficiency in Microsoft Office and G-Suite.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Must be willing to work overtime when necessary.</p>
</li>
<li><p>Demonstrate and uphold all of Greenlight Guru’s core values. Must be the model of our Vision, Mission, Values, Expected Behaviors and Vulnerability based Trust in the Ultimate Employee Experience.</p></li>
</ul>
</div></div>
