<h2>Security Leader</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>
<p>Ideal candidates for this senior consulting role will have functioned as a security leader (CISO, VP or Director of Security) or executive security consultant and possess an executive mindset with a firm grasp of the technical components surrounding the information security and risk management landscape.</p>
<p>
Candidates should also have excellent presenter skills with the ability to convey complex information security concepts to both technical and non-technical audiences and the capacity to influence and help security executives through the maturation of their organization’s security posture.</p>
<p></p>
</div>
<div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Primary Responsibilities</b></h3>
<p><b>Executive Advisory</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Operate as trusted advisor to increase ITPG’s presence at the security executive level for marquee and enterprise accounts</li>
<li>Assist client security executives in the planning, creation and execution of their security strategies</li>
<li>Liaison between the client and ITPG Sales and Services delivery organizations</li>
<li>Initiate and engage other ITPG subject matter experts in support of client’s needs and questions·</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Services Engagement</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Provide billable executive sponsorship and delivery for various client-facing projects</li>
<li>Security strategy assessments</li>
<li>Risk management or compliance initiatives</li>
<li>Programmatic security operations (primarily plan and build phases)</li>
<li>Collaborate with practice thought leaders on the development of new services/offerings</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Sales and Marketing Alignment</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Align with Sales team strategies and support them in closing and exceeding their sales quotas</li>
<li>Drive services revenue and sales quota achievement through client-facing business development activities</li>
<li>Describe Services offerings in detail, understand pricing models, reference accounts, past engagements and demonstrate offering differentiators and value.</li>
<li>Identify pre-engagement requirements (goals, budgeting, timing, etc.), assist with technical scoping, project pricing and proposal development</li>
<li>Lead internal and client-facing account management strategy and planning sessions and formal proposal presentations</li>
<li>Leverage personal network to increase exposure for ITPG and help drive additional opportunities and revenue</li>
<li>Assist sales teams in understanding CISO personas, current issues/trends and how to better position and sell security solutions at security executive levels</li>
<li>Provide customer feedback and observations to sales, marketing, and product groups</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Community Engagement</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Create and publish thought leadership content (magazine articles, blogs, presentations, etc.) in support of security executive interests, missions and other relevant topics</li>
<li>Host customer/prospect roundtable events in region</li>
<li>Speak at and attend security conferences</li>
<li>Perform other duties and projects as assigned</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Required Qualifications</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree or equivalent industry experience</li>
<li>Minimum of 7 years of experience</li>
<li>Experience as an information security or risk management executive in a large enterprise environment OR experience functioning as a high-performing consultant focused on security strategy advisory to the security executive suite</li>
<li>Passion about security and building relationships</li>
<li>Strong knowledge of security strategy and risk management</li>
<li>Strong knowledge of cyber threat landscape and hacker tactics</li>
<li>Experience conducting research on security topics</li>
<li>Excellent verbal and written communication and ability to conduct presentations to technical and non-technical groups</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Must Have</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Valid Driver’s License</li>
<li>Ability to work greater than 40 hours per week as needed</li>
<li>Ability to travel at least 50 percent within assigned region</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Desired Qualifications</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Prior experience in a security executive role (CISO, VP, Director, etc.)</li>
<li>Vertical specific expertise as it relates to information security, risk management or compliance</li>
<li>Prior recognition as a published writer in the Information Security space</li>
<li>Demonstrated sales skills and achievement of quota driven result</li>
</ul>
