<h2>LANDSCAPE ARCHITECT</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Rossen Landscape, headquartered in Great Falls, VA, has become the premier, comprehensive landscape company in the Washington DC metro area. Our mission over the last 16 years in business has been to create an environment that nurtures happy clients and happy employees. Specializing in upscale residential properties, we use only the highest quality materials and practices to deliver unique landscapes with a particular focus on what we call, the boutique experience, for each and every client. Join our growing and dynamic team to take your career to the next level.</p>
<p><b>The Opportunity</b></p>
<p>We are currently seeking a full-time, talented Landscape Architect with a proven track record of closing sales by producing outstanding creative landscape and hardscape designs for fine homes and properties.</p>
<p>As a Landscape Architect on the Rossen Landscape team, you will be responsible for:</p>
<p>1. Creating landscape design renderings, CAD designs, proposals and cost estimates for high-end residential and commercial properties based on accurate field verification, measurements and analyses.</p>
<p>Design scope will include, but is not limited to:</p>
<ul>
<li>Plantings and landscape designs</li>
<li>Pools, spas and water features</li>
<li>Structures - decks, screened porches, pool houses, pavilions, pergolas</li>
<li>Patios, walkways, retaining walls</li>
<li>Fireplaces and fire pits</li>
<li>Outdoor kitchens</li>
<li>Landscape lighting</li>
<li>Drainage solutions, etc.</li>
</ul>
<p>2. Serving as one of the primary contacts for new and existing clients and ensuring those relationships are fostered and maintained through the boutique experience.</p>
<p>3. Coordinating and collaborating with team members within the company to ensure our clients are taken care of from beginning to end.</p>
<p>4. Being a voice and face for Rossen Landscape while representing the company out in the community, in front of clients and with our vendors.</p>

<ul>
<li>Loyalty</li>
<li>Passion</li>
<li>Professionalism</li>
<li>Relationships</li>
<li>Integrity</li>
<li>Drive</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Qualifications</b></p>
<ul>
<li>A Bachelor’s Degree in Landscape Architecture or Landscape Design. Candidates must be able to demonstrate an in-depth knowledge of construction and horticulture from prior work experience. State or national landscape industry certification(s) preferred.</li>
<li>A minimum of three years of landscape and hardscape design experience in a position that involved working directly with residential and commercial clients as well as coordinating installations.</li>
<li>Prior experience increasing sales through vetted lead generation and community involvement strongly preferred.</li>
<li>An in-depth understanding of landscape construction products, methods, and procedures. Knowledge of horticultural materials and planting techniques specific to the Washington DC metro area as well as prior experience installing plants and/or hardscaping materials are a plus.</li>
<li>Experience in reading scaled landscape plans with the ability to calculate labor costs and material requirements and quantities.</li>
<li>Proficiency with CAD, DynaScape, and/or Landscape Pro software as well as Microsoft Office.</li>
<li>A valid driver’s license.</li>
</ul>
<p>To further reiterate, because this position serves as one of the primary contacts for new and existing clients, the ideal Landscape Architect candidate will be friendly, professional, confident, genuine, and reliable with a talent for building rapport quickly.</p>
