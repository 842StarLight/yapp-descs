<h2>Sr. Analyst, Business Process Management (Finance)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Comcast Business offers technology solutions ranging from Ethernet, internet, and WiFi connectivity to voice, television, and managed Enterprise solutions to power businesses of all sizes to perform better. From small businesses to mid-market and large Enterprise organizations, Comcast Business serves business customers across the country. Powered by an advanced, Gig-speed network and backed by 24/7 technical support, Comcast Business is one of the largest contributors to the growth of Comcast Cable. The organization is the nation's largest cable provider to small and mid-size businesses and has emerged as a force in the Enterprise market, recognized by leading industry associations as one of the fastest growing provider of Ethernet services.</p>
<br>
<br>
<br>
<p><b>Summary:</b><br>
Supports changes to accounting and finance processes and systems on behalf of the Comcast Business Finance organization. Participates in design activities related to Comcast's Sales, Order and Billing platform for Mid-Market commercial customers. Participates in design workshops, gathers business requirements and lends subject matter specialization in accounting and finance. Supports multiple and simultaneous projects of varying complexity.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<div>
<b>
Core Responsibilities:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Support the Sr. Manager, Business Process Management on major business process transformation projects and system developments, driving change in a quickly evolving business unit.</li>
<li>Participate in workshops and requirements gathering sessions for finance, accounting, FP&amp;A, tax and sales compensation SMEs and stakeholders.</li>
<li>Capture requirements for revenue recognition, A/R, credit &amp; collections, taxation, sales compensation, accounting &amp; financial reporting business processes.</li>
<li>Consult with business and technology SMEs to recommend functional designs that maximize efficiency and internal control.</li>
<li>Participate in test cycles, ensuring that all scenarios are identified and tested.</li>
<li>Map existing finance and accounting processes and recommend solutions that work with changing systems and platforms.</li>
<li>Provide subject matter specialization in revenue recognition, accounting and design of general ledger interfaces.</li>
<li>Implement business processes that take full advantage of system automation.</li>
<li>Implement and monitor internal controls to ensure compliance with company financial policies, accounting practices and adherence to the Company's SOX program standards.</li>
<li>Participate in program launches, ensuring that financial and operational issues are identified and resolved and that changes are implemented successfully.</li>
<li>Cooperate with other work streams and external consultants to ensure alignment of tasks and high-quality work product.</li>
<li>Demonstrate high quality oral and written communication. Deal regularly with all levels of Company management.</li>
<li>Maintain a continuous presence with business partners in the program to understand risks, issues and actions.</li>
<li>Pursue continuous professional development through training, certifications and education.</li>
<li>Actively participate in Comcast and other external community activities / service.</li>
</ul>
</div>
