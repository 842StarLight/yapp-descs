<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Job Description:</b></h2>
<p>
Leidos offers its knowledge and expertise over a wide area of cutting-edge energy technologies and independent oversight, combined with proven business acumen. Building our work for publicly and privately-owned utilities, financial institutions and local governments, we are able to provide our clients with business insight into all sectors of the infrastructure industry.</p>
<p>
We are seeking a Section Manager for our Distribution Engineering Section who will lead a dynamic team of engineering consultants for electrical distribution projects.</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Lead the Section’s project engineering teams.</li>
<li>Lead proposal efforts and preparation including final approval for the Section for the Power Delivery Services Division.</li>
<li>Partner directly with a variety of utility clients to gain an understanding of both the scale/scope of project: from engineering design through construction support.</li>
<li>The successful candidate will have an interest in developing construction work packages for the electric &amp; gas distribution system using various software systems and construction standards.</li>
<li>
<li>
<li>Develop an engineering team with direct report responsibilities.</li>
<li>Responsible for managing client relations, project scope creation, and project management.</li>
<li>Provide ongoing sales support</li>
<li>Lead the growth of the section and ensure financial profitability</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Typically requires a BS in Electrical Engineering and 12+ years of prior relevant experience or a Master’s degree with 10+ years or prior relevant experience.</li>
<li>10+ years of experience supervising or leading teams or projects.</li>
<li>Professional Engineering Registration (NCEES Record preferred).</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Desired Skills and Attributes:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Capability to lead all aspects of a large engineering team.</li>
<li>Sales pipeline creation.</li>
<li>Work effectively in team environment with the ability work also independently.</li>
<li>Proven ability to communicate with clients and project teams.</li>
<li>Excellent verbal and written communication skills.</li>
<li>Ability to develop staff through mentoring.</li>
<li>Experience with complex projects in distribution design:</li>
<li>Experience with development of civil &amp; electrical packages associated with complex cable/conduit in dense urban environments</li>
<li>Successful project management experience (initiate, plan, execute, monitor &amp; control, closeout)</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Knowledge of engineering estimates, construction cost estimating, specification, material procurement process, project schedule creation, and construction work packages.</li>
<li>Ability to travel periodically to visit with clients and to visit Leidos offices.</li>
</ul>
