<h2>Associate Product Manager - Pneumatic tools</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Associate Product Manager - Pneumatic</b></p>
<p>Koki Holdings America Ltd. - Metabo HPT</p>
<p>Braselton, GA</p>
<p><b>SUMMARY: </b></p>
<p>Responsible for the oversight of day-to-day product management, new product development, and marketing of the product line. This includes leading new product initiatives and product launch, conducting market and end-user research, specifying product design criteria, assisting with planning and execution of promotions, as well as other strategic objectives. This position develops the business and channel strategy and drives the execution of this strategy, marketing and new business development.</p>
<p><b>ESSENTIAL DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Manages the new product development initiatives based on trades assigned including preparing strategy, expert in trades, competitive analysis, target customer/end-user and trade, end-user insights to resolve unmet needs and pain points, budgetary estimates, product design and merchandising criteria.</li>
<li>Lead market research initiatives including job site visits, store visits, ethnographic, internet based and other effective techniques to discover unmet needs leading to products innovation.</li>
<li>Drive the execution of new product launches, including well planned merchandising and marketing initiatives appropriate for each channel or customer as needed.</li>
<li>Clearly understand the home center/retail and independent channels, identify all new opportunities and prioritize based on internal product plans and strategy.</li>
<li>Liaison with Sales Management in development of line review strategies, customer presentations, programs, pricing, and key customer special event activities, as well as participate in key account line reviews as required.</li>
<li>Research macro-economic and market trends to support new product direction and selling activities.</li>
<li>Market analysis including share, customer segmentation and product landscape insights for use in selling and internal planning activities.</li>
<li>Analyze competitor product and marketing activities and develop intelligent solutions to combat and increase company’s market share.</li>
<li>Manage existing product SKU productivity and maintenance, ensuring profitability and rationalization.</li>
<li>Work closely with Marketing Communications team to ensure signage, packaging, and all collateral material for product line are accurate and executed in a timely manner.</li>
<li>Assist in the development of promotions and coordination with internal and external teams.</li>
<li>Responsible for pricing and profitability.</li>
<li>Negotiate and work with manufacturing vendors that support OEM product lines, including obtaining and managing vendor support funding.</li>
<li>Work closely with Japan office on new product development, market intelligence and day to day operations.</li>
<li>Analyze sales figures and report back trends, issues, and overall activity.</li>
<li>Liaison to all cross-function areas of company as related to product line knowledge, presentation content, market research, competitive landscape, and analysis.</li>
<li>Other’s duties as assigned.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>QUALIFICATION REQUIREMENTS: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Working knowledge of new product development, design, and marketing.</li>
<li>Ability to organize and direct a project to completion.</li>
<li>Experience working directly with key customers in a dynamic and professional manner</li>
<li>Detail-oriented self-starter, able to manage own time effectively and meet deadlines within a highly dynamic work environment.</li>
<li>Demonstrated planning, organization, work prioritization skills, as well as proficiency in working on multiple projects and meeting goals.</li>
<li>Ability to communicate effectively (both orally and in writing) and present information publicly.</li>
<li>Demonstrated desire for satisfying internal and/or external customers.</li>
<li>Ability to analyze, develop, and propose strategies that will help the organization be more effective, competitive, and otherwise achieve its goals.</li>
<li>Willingness to travel</li>
</ul>
<p><b>EDUCATION and/or EXPERIENCE: </b></p>
<p>Bachelor’s degree (BA) from four-year College or University (Preferably with concentration in Marketing) and a minimum of 4-year product management experience.</p>
<p><b>COMPUTER SKILLS: </b></p>
<p>Highly proficient in Microsoft Word, Excel and PowerPoint programs; software for word processing, generating spreadsheets/graphs/charts, and creating presentations.</p>
