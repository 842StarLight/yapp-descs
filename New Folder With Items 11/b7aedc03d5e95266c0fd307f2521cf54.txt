<p>This is a critical role that provides an opportunity to grow in a leadership position, and serves to help broaden a career path that is expected to culminate in a senior financial role within our organization. As such, the candidate must have the potential, ambition, and commitment to explore his or her capabilities and to learn what makes our company tick. On a routine basis, the cost accountant will perform a variety of functions to ensure that cost of materials, inventory levels, and contracts in progress are accounted for accurately. The successful cost accountant will, like all members of the team, become the subject matter expert in all assigned areas. This position is exempt, and reports to the Controller.</p>
<p><b>Critical job functions</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Daily processing of all activities related to procurement and production of liquid asphalt and asphalt emulsions</li>
<li>Daily monitoring of perpetual inventory for liquid asphalt</li>
<li>Interfacing and closing contracts as often as necessary</li>
<li>Weekly monitoring and reporting of asphalt exchanges</li>
<li>Weekly monitoring of material activity at Hot Mix Asphalt plants and treated stone stockpiles</li>
<li>Monthly accruals of energy expenses, inventoried operating costs, and contracting work in progress</li>
<li>Monthly update of standard cost and internal pricing systems</li>
<li>Yearly oversight of physical inventory reconciliation, and updates of inventoried operating cost factors</li>
<li>Yearly analysis of raw material consumption and production variances</li>
<li>Yearly preparation of various reports to support income tax returns and audited financial statement</li>
<li>Ensure accuracy and timeliness of general ledger accounts related to sales and direct cost of sales</li>
<li>Provide general supervision to and leadership of assigned personnel</li>
<li>Other analytical and accounting tasks may be assigned</li>
</ul>

<li>Intermediate knowledge of US GAAP</li>
<li>Exposure to multi-state tax issues, excluding income taxes</li>
<li>Commitment to continuous improvement</li>
<li>Proactive nature, with a sense of urgency to handle multiple tasks and deadlines in a fast-paced, ever-changing work environment</li>
<li>Analytical focus to be able to identify trends, discover causes of variances, and identify improvement opportunities</li>
<li>Advanced skills with standard office software (MS Excel, MS Word, MS Outlook)</li>
<li>Ability to analyze and follow detailed procedures, establish new procedures as needed</li>
<li>Basic skills with Crystal reports required, intermediate skills preferred</li>

</ul>
