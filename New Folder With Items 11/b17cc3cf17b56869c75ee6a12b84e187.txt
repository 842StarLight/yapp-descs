<p><b>POSITION SUMMARY
</b></p>
<p></p>
<p>The Marketing Automation Senior Specialist is responsible for orchestrating and executing marketing programs delivered through the Marketing Automation Platform (MAP). Working with the Manager, Marketing Automation, key responsibilities include project management, platform administration and data integrity, quality assurance, as well as coordinating with Campaign Management, Segment Marketing, and Digital Strategy. Additionally, the candidate will play a key role in overall marketing automation operations and technology strategy and deployment.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>KEY RESPONSIBILITIES
</b></p>
<p></p>
<p>Orchestrate and execute marketing campaigns and respective tactics aligned to marketing strategy and objectives
</p>
<p><b>50%
</b></p>
<p>Co-manage the technical conceptualization and execution of lead nurturing, lead scoring and dissemination of qualified leads to Sales via multiple CRM platforms
</p>
<p><b>25%
</b></p>
<p>Optimize and monitor programmatic functionality and data cleansing exercise to ensure our marketing automation platform meets business requirements and expectations
</p>
<p><b>25%
</b></p>
<p></p>
<p><b>RESPONSIBILITIES
</b></p>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Collaborate with Demand Gen team to continually improve marketing automation operations to enhance Marketing’s impact on the bottom-line
</li>
<li>Work with team to optimize and standardize marketing automation processes across the organization, including documentation
</li>
<li>Work with the Data Analysis team and support their marketing automation data and activity and contact engagement reporting needs
</li>
<li>Collaborate with Campaign Management to ensure there is a cohesive creation, execution and testing/optimization process
</li>
<li>Communicate and educate Marketing stakeholders with regards to marketing automation best practices
</li>
</ul>
