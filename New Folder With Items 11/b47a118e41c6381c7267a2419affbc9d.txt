<h2>Call Center Representative- Rockford, IL</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Our company continues to grow so we are currently seeking additional work from home agents in Rockford, Illinois and surrounding areas who will handle inbound or outbound calls. We have found that people from the Illinois area share our same values, have a strong work ethic and place a higher premium on spending quality time with their friends and family so that is why we want people from your hometown to join our growing team.</p>
<p>
Our reps handle calls from multiple clients, helping them enroll students in institutions of higher learning, securing appointments for nationally known and trusted home improvement companies, or signing up new drivers for ride share services. As a part of our team you may work on a single or multiple programs during a typical work day. Our agents assist with the following projects:</p>
<ul>
<li>Capture additional information from the prospective student</li>
<li>Warm transfer prospects to various clients</li>
<li>Assist with employment applications and the prequalification process</li>
<li>Using multiple scripts and rebuttals to overcome rejections</li>
<li>Meeting company metrics and sales targets</li>
<li>Participate in company contests and other programs based around quality work- not impossible quotas</li>
</ul>
<p><b>To be a great fit for the job, you must also meet these requirements:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Value the opportunity to set the schedule you work out with your manager that matches your and our clients' needs</li>
<li>Have strong listening skills, professional telephone manner and ability to deal tactfully with callers in a fast paced call center environment</li>
<li>Comfortable with and driven to meet and exceed goals and metrics as it relates to performance and quality of work</li>
<li>Receptive to and quick to implement constructive feedback and a strong desire to continuously improve</li>
<li>Able to multi-task: talking on the phone while on the computer for long periods of time</li>
<li>Have above average computer and typing skills; 35 WPM minimum keyboarding required</li>
<li>A team player that works well with your coworkers and enjoys helping people succeed</li>
</ul>

</ul>

<p><b>Important Home Office and Computer Requirements:</b></p>
<p>
As a representative, you will log into our dialing system from your home using your own computer and Internet connection. You don’t need to use your own phone for this job. You MUST meet all of these requirements or your computer will not work with our dialing system.</p>
<ul>
<li>High speed and wired internet connection only - wireless/Wi-Fi NOT accepted. Your computer must plug directly into the modem in order to work with our phone system.</li>
<li>Your computer must have at least 4 GB of RAM; 2GHz processor</li>
<li>If hired you will need to download MS Office or OpenOffice and have Google Chrome, Firefox or Safari browsers installed (Technical help is available if needed to assist with free downloads).</li>
<li>A phone headset will be provided.</li>
</ul>
