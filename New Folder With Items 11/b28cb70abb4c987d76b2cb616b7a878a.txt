<h2>Service Desk Administrator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p><b>Job Summary</b></p>
<p>
The basic job function is to provide support services in conjunction with our internal service desk teams. Daily activities may include re-cabling patch panels, inventory functions for deprecated assets, supporting end-users who report computer problems, helping with backup tapes and other similar functions. The candidate will need to have a very positive attitude to supporting these types of services in accordance with whatever is needed on a daily basis.</p>
<p><br>
<b>
Job Duties</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Setup workstations and laptops according to guidelines</li>
<li>
Provide phone support for internal customers</li>
<li>
Perform preventative maintenance on workstations and laptops</li>
<li>
Produce documentation and provide training for the end user relating to the technologies we use</li>
<li>
Troubleshoot and resolve networking issues in conjunction with other staff and vendors as needed</li>
<li>
Install, configure, and patch various operating systems (Windows 7/10, OSX) for laptops/desktops</li>
<li>
Manage hardware and software asset lists</li>
</ul>
<p>
 <b>Qualifications</b> (absolute must haves walking in the door)</p>
<ul>
<li>
5+ years of Helpdesk\Service desk experience</li>
<li>
Good communication and excellent Customer Service</li>
<li>
Troubleshooting skills for windows/Mac based platforms, smart phones, and A/V equipment</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>
Willingness to learn, develop, and contribute in a fun, fast-paced environment</li>
<li>
Self-starter that brings both interpersonal skills and creative problem solving skills along with great attention to detail</li>
<li>
Ability to manage time and keep organized</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
 Preferred Qualifications</b> (great to have but not absolutely necessary)</p>
<ul>
<li>
VMware ESXi/Horizon experience</li>
<li>
Ring Central voip</li>
<li>
Jira and Confluence experience</li>
<li>
Office365 experience or exposure on the administrative side</li>
<li>
Knowledge of domain management applications</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>
Demonstrated knowledge of networking and hardware standards</li>
<li>
Solid understanding of security concepts, principles and practices, including fundamental knowledge of OSI model</li>
<li>
A+/Microsoft certification preferred (or working towards)</li>
</ul>
