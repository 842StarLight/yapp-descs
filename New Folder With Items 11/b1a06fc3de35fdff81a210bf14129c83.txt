The Day-to-Day</b></p>
<div>
<div>
<div><div>
<p><b><i>Key Purpose of the Role:</i></b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b><i>
The portfolio operations leader will oversee a function of supply management across the Worldpay segment as well as the broader enterprise (FIS wide). The leader will also have responsibility for key tools and processes supporting the EPM team and stakeholders.</i></b></p>
</div></div>
<br>
<div><div>
<p>
</p>
<p>Key Responsibilities:</p>
<ul>
<li>Manage engineering supply as demand changes to align with business priorities</li>
<li>Manage infrastructure and security supply and ensure alignment of resources to priorities</li>
<li>Track operating expenses required to launch priority products in operations, sales and marketing ensure alignment to capital funding and priority</li>
<li>Oversee various operations functions including project set up and closer</li>
<li>Manage the enterprise plan of record</li>
<li>Maintain/enhance the project tracking portal</li>
<li>Coordinate the forecast validation efforts within the portfolio team</li>
</ul>
