<h2>Technical Project Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">HPOne, named CT Magazine's 2018 Great Places to Work, is looking for a Technical Project Manager to join our team.
<br><br>
The ideal candidate for the project manager position will be responsible for the implementation and management of new projects and processes for new and existing external clients as well as internal business units. Manage and coordinate projects and key initiatives from start to finish. Drive enterprise, functional and/or departmental initiatives and ensure project timelines and expectations are met. Ensure projects deliver on established goals for business value creation. Responsible for creating project plans/timelines and documenting all business process, requirements and expectations of the client, work with internal resources to communicate the client's needs and expectations. Work closely with all levels of management throughout the project to ensure all responsible parties and resources understand the scope and deliverables of the project. Present findings and make recommendations to all levels of management.
<br><br>
Description of accountabilities
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Coordinate and manage project planning and execution for multiple projects</li>
<li>Interface with counterpart at client or vendor and serve as primary point of contact for HPOne</li>
<li>Manage key project initiatives including the development of detailed work plans, set deadlines, assign responsibilities, monitor and summarize project progress, and create detailed business requirements that define the business process, requirements and expectations of the client</li>
<li>Provide supervision and leadership for project team members</li>
<li>Monitor work plans and enforce accountability by key stakeholders</li>
<li>Prepare strategic analysis of potential business and operational opportunities</li>
<li>Communicate purpose and vision of the project and prepare summary reports and recommendations for management</li>
<li>Hold daily/weekly meetings with clients/vendors/internal resources on project initiatives and deliverables</li>
<li>Perform other duties as assigned</li>
</ul>
Required qualifications for this role
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor's degree, or equivalent related industry experience</li>
<li>5+ years technical project management experience including managing projects from start to finish, preferably in a healthcare environment</li>
<li>Familiarity or proficiency in the following areas: Net, Ajax, Analysis, Analyst, ASP, ASP.NET, C#, CSS, Database, Developer, Development, HTML, Javascript, jQuery, Lifecycle, Linux, OOD, Programmer, Project, SQL, SQL Server, UML, Web Developer, Windows, XML</li>
<li>Ability to interpret technical solutions for business process and procedure and be able to effectively communicate it to the client/vendor</li>
<li>Experience leading a project team, keeping team focused and discussions moving forward, resolve conflicts with both internal and external audiences as well as invoke a level of confidence and authority to various department leaders and levels of management</li>
<li>Analytical skills to effectively research solutions, problem solve, define and process business processes, and the ability to make suggestions to business leaders</li>
<li>Organizational skills to include time management, scheduling, coordinating meetings and using time efficiently, as well as managing multiple projects at one time</li>
<li>Superior oral and written communication skills</li>
<li>Knowledge of email systems such Microsoft Outlook sufficient to communicate and schedule both internal and external contacts</li>
<li>Knowledge of MS Word sufficient to create complex documents, which may contain graphics, form fields, sub-headings and indexes</li>
<li>Knowledge of MS Excel sufficient to create moderately complex spreadsheets and data gathering analysis tools containing pivot tables, formulas, links and graphs</li>
<li>Knowledge of Microsoft Visio and Project sufficient to create moderately complex depictions and project plansLI-VB1</li>
</ul>
Preferred qualifications for this role
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>PMP license/certification</li>
</ul>
