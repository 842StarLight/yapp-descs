<h2>Implemention Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<div>The Implementation Specialist is responsible for executing new software integrations with prospective channel partners and diagnosing issues throughout the integration process. The successful candidate will provide ongoing support as an escalation path for ISVs post-integration and must be able to articulate technology and processes to both business and operational users. S/he must be able to proactively manage the integration process and ensure timely delivery throughout.</div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Key Responsibilities:</b></h3>
<ul>
<li>
Configuration, testing, troubleshooting</li>
<li>
Partner-facing technical resource (before, during and after integration)</li>
<li>
Support new integrations</li>
<li>
Maintain new ISV pipeline and deliver to target go-live dates</li>
<li>
Act as a liaison-internally an externally to achieve results</li>
<li>
Able to perform other tasks as needed</li>
</ul>
<br>
<div></div>
<b>Desired Skills and Expertise:</b>
<div></div>
<div><ul>
<li>Integrated payments experience preferred</li>
<li>Technical background or inclination is a must</li>
<li>Must be self-motivated with a proven track record in technology industry</li>
<li>Comfortable in the dynamic atmosphere of a technical organization with a rapidly expanding customer base</li>
<li>Organized and analytical, able to eliminate sales obstacles through creative and adaptive approaches</li>
<li>Extreme attention to detail</li>
</ul></div>
