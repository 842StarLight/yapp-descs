<h2>SaaS Sales Representative</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">Openbay is seeking a high-energy SaaS Sales/Partner Development Representative with established relationships and a track record of success selling face-to-face to automotive service businesses. In this revenue-generating role, you will promote and sell our SaaS and marketplace offerings to automotive service businesses, owning the entire sales process from initial inbound lead contact through close. This is a fantastic opportunity for motivated, self-starters to join a hyper-growth tech company and sales team.
<br><br>
What You’ll Do
<br><ul>
<li>Drive our SaaS sales process by engaging with prospective customers and driving the negotiation and close of the sale within a week sale cycle.</li>
<li>Recruit automotive service providers to join our marketplace.</li>
<li>Achieve and exceed monthly, quarterly and annual sales targets while maintaining daily and weekly activity metrics.</li>
<li>Engage with qualified potential business customers via phone and email, perform online product/system demonstrations, and conduct other activities to build our customer base and company revenue.</li>
<li>Leverage CRM to track and prioritize sales activities and build/update our customer and prospect database.</li>
<li>Manage administrative issues related to account billing, refunds, and disputes as necessary.</li>
</ul>
Who We’re Looking For
<br>You’re a high-energy, motivated, self-starter who can work independently to achieve results. You are also a collaborative team member who desires to contribute to the growth and success of Openbay.
<br><br>
<b>Our ideal candidate has:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>1+ years of inside sales experience. Previous experience selling to automotive service providers a plus.</li>
<li>Passionate desire to exceed goals and achieve consistent results.</li>
<li>Self-starter who has a desire to learn, grow, and excel in their role and play a key role impacting the value of a fast-growing company.</li>
<li>Ability to listen, earn trust, persuade, consult, and confidently close sales with customers.</li>
<li>Organized sales approach and handles rejection with resilience.</li>
<li>Outstanding written, verbal communication and interpersonal skills.</li>
<li>Excellent computer skills with ability to expertly navigate between CRM and online meetings and demo sites.</li>
<li>Previous experience with CRM systems a plus.</li>
<li>Previous experience conducting system demos a plus.</li>
<li>Flexibility working outside of normal hours in order to close deals and get it done.</li>
<li>A multi-tasker with the ability to wear many hats in a fast-paced environment and to prioritize, negotiate, and work with a variety of internal and external stakeholders.</li>
</ul>
</div>
