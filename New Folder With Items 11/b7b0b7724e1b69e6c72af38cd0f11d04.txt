<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Company Overview
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div>Nuance Communications, Inc. is the pioneer and leader in conversational AI innovations that bring intelligence to everyday work and life. The company delivers solutions that understand, analyze and respond to human language, amplifying human intelligence. With decades of domain and artificial intelligence expertise, Nuance works with thousands of organizations – in healthcare, telecommunications, automotive, financial services, retail, and more – to create stronger relationships and better experiences for their customers.</div>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Job Summary
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<p><b>Position summary:</b></p>
<p></p>
<br>
<div>
The Sales Development Representative will drive new and existing business in the defined territory through strong solution selling skills, reporting to the Director of Sales Commercial Account in the US. Must have successful track record of independent selling in a geographic territory and working with partners and channels.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
Primary responsibility will be to drive new sales activity and business in PA, WV and VA territory and with named accounts through partners by solution selling. Must possess strong interpersonal skills and deal at the C Level and below. Enterprise selling success is key.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
As an Associate Sales Representative (ASR), you will focus on both the qualification of “warm” inbound leads as well as prospecting to identify opportunities to support the sales funnel and help build a sales pipeline that leads to your ability to close business to attain quota retirement. You will focus on Nuance solutions in the areas of both on premise and SaaS based Intelligent Engagement, AI, Digital, Voice (speech recognition,) Security, Fraud, professional services and Omni-Channel solutions. As an ASR, your job will be to communicate with prospects by leveraging email prospecting, face to face and advanced phone qualifications skills. You will do all associated activities in a B2B model, selling to executives throughout the PA, WV and VA region closely collaborating with your experienced sales management team member. You will play a key role in support of your assigned territory and will be responsible for fostering deep relationships with customers, channel partners and internal team members through strategic leadership and solution selling all leading to addressable closed pipeline to ensure quota attainment.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div><b>
Responsibilities:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
Lead and collaborate with a team of seasoned internal resources to help move enterprise sales deals through the entire sales life cycle.
</li>
<li>Act as the first line of communication for customer leads that result from Nuance’s marketing activity.</li>
<li>
Receive leads from Marketing, qualify them, and engage with your Field Sales management to develop a strategy and approach.</li>
<li>
Represent the complete portfolio of Nuance Enterprise Software products to prospects and channel partners.</li>
<li>
Develop sales strategies and approaches for specific named accounts, territory accounts and verticals in collaboration with your Field Sales management.</li>
<li>
Set/coordinate customer meetings and/or conference calls, delivery of follow-up materials (marketing material, presentations, case studies, etc.), and maintaining on-going relationships with warm prospects.</li>
<li>
Prospect into these accounts to identify key roles, individuals and business needs
</li>
<li>Perform thoughtful and resourceful research to gather contact and company intelligence &amp; develop an approach for each account</li>
<li>
Meet with and develop relationships with external partners, and channel representatives to promote the Nuance message and encourage and close joint sales of Nuance products and services.</li>
<li>
Learn the Nuance sales methodology and approach.</li>
<li>
Accurately forecast deals on a weekly basis, delivering on committed deals in a timely manner.</li>
<li>
Maintain all prospecting and lead response activity in SFDC.</li>
<li>
Travel in territory required</li>
<li>
Anticipated quota $1.25-3M annually</li>
<li>
Perform tasks related to securing and keeping the products, tools, and processes that you are responsible for securing</li>
</ul>
