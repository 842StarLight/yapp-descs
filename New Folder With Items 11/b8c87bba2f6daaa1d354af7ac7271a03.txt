<h2>Data Analyst - Tableau</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>For more than 30 years, CDPHP has been providing outstanding health care coverage in the Capital Region of New York and beyond. Based in Albany, NY, the physician-guided not-for-profit offers plans in 24 counties. CDPHP cares deeply about the health and well-being of its members as well as its employees. The company values integrity, diversity, and innovation, and its corporate culture supports those values wholeheartedly. At CDPHP, the employees have a voice and are encouraged to make an impact at both the company and community levels through engagement and volunteer opportunities. CDPHP invests in employees who share these values and invites you to be a part of that experience.</p>
<p></p>
<p>The Market Analyst II is responsible for consulting, suggesting, implementing solutions, program administration and analytics that address the goals of CDPHP. The analyst will provide analytical and process support and guidance to the Sales and Marketing department. Analysis and processes may include marketing metric requests, campaign analytics, corporate strategy project and model implementation, business associate reporting and implementation, vendor relationships, and other duties as requested.</p>
<p><b>QUALIFICATIONS:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree required. 7 years relevant experience may be substituted for degree.</li>
<li>Additional consideration will be given for an advanced degree in mathematics, statistics or other related analytic field.</li>
<li>Additional consideration will be given for data, business or analytical certifications.</li>
<li>3 years of relevant qualitative experience in market data, market research, financial analysis, big data, or other form of business intelligence required.</li>
<li>Advanced experience with databases, spreadsheets, statistical software, query design, report design, or ad hoc reporting required.</li>
<li>Advance experience in one or more of the following required: SAS, Tableau, Excel, Cognos, SQL, or other statistical/analytical based software</li>
<li>Advanced understanding of health care administration platform preferred(i.e. member and group setup, claims adjudication, eligibility processing).</li>
<li>Ability to independently prepare, present and interpret data analysis required.</li>
<li>Advanced skills in Microsoft applications required (Access, Excel, Word and PowerPoint), which includes the ability to summarize data concisely, compile charts and graphs, spreadsheet formatting, writing basic mathematical formulas, executing pivot tables, basic query design.</li>
<li>Experience with web-based reporting application and/or customer relationship management (CRM) tool preferred.</li>
<li>Experience with mapping software preferred.</li>
<li>Demonstrated ability to design advanced reporting and analysis through web-based and/or Microsoft applications, which includes but is not limited to complex filters, advanced logic, statistical functions and visual presentation.</li>
<li>Ability to leverage complex information into organized, illustrative presentation formats to influence decision-making is preferred.</li>
<li>Demonstrated ability in writing business correspondence and producing reports, which exhibit appropriate grammar, punctuation and sentence structure, as presented in a letter of interest and resume required.</li>
<li>Demonstrated understanding of and experience with the broker market preferred.</li>
<li>Effective oral and written communication skills required.</li>
<li>Effective time management skills required.</li>
<li>Ability to create positive working relationships with external stakeholders who are responsible for work that directly / indirectly impact Market Analytics.</li>
<li>Ability to follow direction and implement procedures which bring about quality control and /or efficiencies.</li>
<li>Ability to clearly document required work instructions, process flows, and desk levels as well as maintain them annually.</li>
<li>Ability to recognize issues with systems and applications that are pertinent to their own daily work tasks, and submit tickets for resolution, and continuously monitor and track them.</li>
<li>Ability to meet with internal stakeholders to identify the requirements of the work task, present them in a clear and logical manner, and produce output to match the stakeholder's expectation</li>
</ul>
