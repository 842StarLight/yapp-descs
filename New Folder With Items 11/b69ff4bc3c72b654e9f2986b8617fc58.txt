<h2>Account Executive - TV</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>
<b>Cox Media Group</b> <b>Orlando</b> is currently seeking an experienced <b>Account Executive</b> for their Television Group <b>WFTV/WRDQ </b>that has the ability to build influential relationships with existing clientele and has a proven track record of developing new revenue streams.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
As an Account Executive, you will be highly compensated for your performance as you work closely with local business owners to create and implement advertising campaigns using station resources such as commercial advertising, event marketing, promotions, and internet advertising. At Cox Media Group Orlando TV, the Sales and Marketing staff believe in accountability, dedication, professionalism, honesty, communication, resources and integrity.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div><b>
Responsibilities:</b></div>
<div></div>
<ul>
<li>
The ideal candidate will have the skill set and drive to build a full pipeline of sales prospects and be responsible for closing the business and achieving their revenue goals.</li>
<li>
This Account Executive will also handle a list of accounts that is built strategically for this position.</li>
<li>
The candidate will analyze clients’ business goals and develop needs-based solutions to achieve them, will oversee campaign execution, as well as work collaboratively with our properties.</li>
<li>
Develop and manage revenue producing relationships with clients and agencies</li>
<li>
Prospect for new business and build a full pipeline of sales prospects</li>
<li>
Analyze client marketing goals and develop needs based solutions to achieve them</li>
<li>
Oversee campaign execution and analysis</li>
<li>
Provide consultation to sales managers, sales teams and clients</li>
<li>
Work collaboratively with internal partners to drive revenue</li>
</ul>
