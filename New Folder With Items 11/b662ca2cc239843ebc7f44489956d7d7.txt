<b>WHO ARE WE?</b><br>
FIS connects consumers to their money through leading-edge payments solutions. We're the development engine of FIS: the largest, Fortune 500, Fintech company in the world. Our solution is branded by our customers - banks and retailers - so you might not have heard of us. But, if you've used a mobile banking app, interacted with an ATM, or used a debit card to complete a financial transaction, you're most likely using FIS software. Our product is one that touches the average consumer on a regular basis, and makes the financial world go 'round.<br>
Headquartered in Jacksonville, Florida, FIS serves more than 20,000 clients in over 130 countries, and our technology powers billions of transactions annually that move over $9 trillion around the globe. FIS is a Fortune 500 company, ranked 392 on the Fortune 500 list and is a member of Standard &amp; Poor's 500® Index. Named a 2016 "World's Most Admired Company" by Fortune Magazine.<br>
<b>About this team:</b><br>
The Corporate Applications team at FIS is looking to add a dynamic, motivated member to the Case Management System Application Team. Corporate Applications provides custom-developed solutions to our internal client service teams around the globe. Our focus is on delivering quality customer support to our internal clients.<br>
<b>WHAT IS THE POSITION?</b><br>
FIS is looking for a <b>Business Systems Analyst</b> to join our <b>Corporate Applications</b> team in <b>Orlando/Maitland.</b> This is an exciting opportunity for someone with a solid background in <b>business systems analysis</b> who likes to contribute new ideas and is willing to put in the work to build their career.<br>
<b>General Duties and Responsibilities:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Includes analysis of business and user needs, documentation of requirements and translation into proper system requirement specifications.</li>
<li>
Analyzes and documents business processes and metrics, and identifies improvement opportunities.</li>
<li>
Writes test plans and test cases to ensure changes which are made to the application meet client needs and that application integrity is maintained.</li>
<li>
Conducts a variety of tests such as system, integration, readiness and acceptance tests. Ensures changes made by programmers are correct.</li>
<li>
Considers business implications of the application of technology to the current business environment.</li>
</ul>
