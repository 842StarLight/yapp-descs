<h2>Account Executive</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Modern Worldwide is a growing full-service brand activation agency specializing in impactful brand experiences. We create effective and efficient experiential campaigns that bring brands to life while leaving a lasting impression on consumers. We are thrilled to have recently been named by Chief Marketer as one of the top 200 experiential and brand activation agencies in the U.S. for the 2nd year!</p>
<p>Modern Worldwide is looking for an Account Executive who wants to help grow a scrappy, enthusiastic and energetic experiential marketing agency. Providing full service experiential programs to our brand clients and quality event staffing to our agency partners is what we do and we need team members who can sell the concept and nurture our clients. As an Account Executive, you will share our capabilities with prospects and work alongside our Client Services team to ensure success for those clients and encourage long term partnerships.</p>
<p>Description:<br>You are outgoing and can sell through listening to client goals and matching them with our solutions. You are detail oriented to track the process. You are a great communicator both with clients and our internal teams. You are proactive and have a sense of urgency to make your mark on our agency. This position earns a base salary with sales incentive.</p>
<p>Required Qualifications:<br>Flexibility: a number of our clients have short lead times and urgent projects<br>Team-oriented mindset: you are in charge of your accounts, but will need support to execute the programs<br>Detail driven: beyond successful programs, you will be responsible for staying in touch with clients and mining future opportunities<br>Independent thinking: you look for new ways to reach people and communicate our solutions<br>Positivity: we are a small agency and everyone needs to support each other<br>Organization: ability to track, follow up and project manage as needed<br>1-3 years of documented successful sales experience, account management or blend of the two (preference to related industries including media, advertising, events, services)<br>Proficiency in MS Office/Google Drive products and CRM software (e.g. Hubspot)</p>
