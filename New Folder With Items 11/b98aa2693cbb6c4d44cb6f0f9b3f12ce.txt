<h2>Front of House Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>We are currently accepting resumes for the Front of House Manager position at our Westchase location. If you have Front of House Manager experience working in a restaurant environment and experience with craft beer, <b>we want to hear from you! At TBBC, each beer and meal are served in a fun, casual environment by a craft beer knowledgeable team. We are looking for a FOH Manager that will embody the craft beer experience and team leadership that will ultimately ensure customers are happy and employees are successful. </b></p>
<p><b>Summary of Position: </b></p>
<p>Responsible for all aspects of operations and management of Front of House operations in the restaurant. The front of house manager must be high energy, passionate and hospitable, you would be expected to lead by example. Oversee and coordinate the day-to-day operations within the policies and guidelines set forth by Tampa Bay Brewing Company. <b>Be passionate about our beer, our food, and how they are served together.</b></p>
<p><b>Duties &amp; Responsibilities: </b></p>
<p>Included but not limited to:</p>
<ul>
<li>Maintains operations by preparing policies and standard operating procedures; implementing production, productivity, quality, and patron- service standards; determining and implementing system improvements. Understand all policies, procedures, and Standards.</li>
<li>Meets restaurant financial objectives by developing and preparing strategic and annual forecasts and budgets; analyzing variances; initiating corrective actions; establishing and monitoring financial controls. Attracts patrons by developing and implementing marketing, advertising, public and community relations programs in conjunction with the Director of Community Engagement and Creative Director; evaluating program results; identifying and tracking changing demands and <b>ensuring beer knowledge and competency of staff in the craft beer area – to include current knowledge of new TBBC beers and their characteristics</b>
</li>
<li>Accomplishes restaurant and bar human resource objectives by recruiting, selecting, orienting, training, assigning, scheduling, coaching, counseling, and disciplining staff; communicating job expectations; planning, monitoring, appraising, and reviewing job contributions; planning and reviewing compensation actions; enforcing policies and procedures. Make employment and termination decisions under the direction of the owner.</li>
<li>Ensure that all guest feels welcome and are always given responsive and courteous service.</li>
<li>Ensure that all beer, food, and other products are consistently served according to the brewpub’s recipes, portioning, cooking, and serving standards.</li>
<li>Achieve company objectives in sales, service, quality, appearance of facility and sanitation and cleanliness through training of employees and creating a positive, productive working environment.</li>
<li>Control cash and other receipts by adhering to cash handling and reconciliation procedures in accordance with brewpub policies and procedures.</li>
<li>Maintains patron satisfaction by monitoring, evaluating, and auditing food, beverage – <b>especially TBBC beers</b>, and service offerings; initiating improvements; building relationships with preferred patrons.</li>
<li>Fill in where needed to ensure guest service standards and efficient operations.</li>
<li>Continually strive to develop your staff in all areas of managerial and professional development.</li>
<li>Ensure that all equipment is kept clean and kept in excellent working condition through personal inspection and by following the brewpub’s preventative maintenance programs.</li>
<li>Schedule labor in a timely manner while ensuring that all positions are staffed when and as needed and labor cost objectives are met.</li>
<li>Fully understand and comply with all federal, state, county and municipal regulations that pertain to health, safety and labor requirements of the brewery employees and guests.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Qualifications: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Be 21 years of age.</li>
<li>Be able to communicate and understand the English language.</li>
<li>Have knowledge of service and food and beverage, generally involving at least five years of front-of-the-house operations and/or assistant management positions.</li>
<li>Have a working knowledge of the brewing process.</li>
<li>Have knowledge, enthusiasm, and experience in Craft Beer.</li>
<li>Full-time up to 50 hours per week. Perform job responsibilities with the utmost professionalism and responsibility</li>
<li>Possess excellent industry standard math skills and have ability to operate the company POS system (ALOHA).</li>
<li>Possess excellent skills in electronic communication systems and office management software including MS Word and MS Excel</li>
<li>Be able to work in a standing position for long periods of time (up to 8 hours).</li>
<li>Be able to reach, bend, stoop and frequently lift to 50 pounds.</li>
</ul>
<p>Job Type: Full-time</p>
<p>Salary: $45,000.00 to $55,000.00 /year</p>
<p>Experience:</p>
<ul><li>relevant: 1 year (Preferred)</li></ul>
<p>Education:</p>
<ul><li>Bachelor's (Preferred)</li></ul>
