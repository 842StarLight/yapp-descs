<div><b>Who is GeoStabilization International®?</b></div>
<div>
GeoStabilization International® (GSI) develops and installs innovative solutions that protect people and infrastructure from the dangers of geohazards. We specialize in emergency landslide repairs, rockfall mitigation, and grouting using cutting edge design/build and design/build/warranty contracting. GeoStabilization International is the leading geohazard mitigation and bridge rehabilitation firm operating throughout the United States, Canada, and Australasia. Our expertise, proprietary tools, and worldwide partnerships allow us to repair virtually any slope stability or foundation problem in any geologic setting.</div>

<div><b>What You Will Do</b></div>
<div>
As a Master Scheduler/Planner working for GSI, there is no typical day. You will be planning all crew activity and assigning company equipment resources based on forecasted projects companywide. You will also be monitoring and analyzing data in our ERP and scheduling software to bring harmony between operations and sales to balance capacity with demand. When GSI hires you, we believe we are hiring the next leader and everything we do is about setting you up to be personally and professionally successful. We offer the best total rewards in the industry. We share our success with a meaningful profit-sharing plan plus Medical, Dental, Vision, Life, Short- and Long-Term Disability as well as a matching 401K and not to mention our Employee Equity Program.</div>

<h2 class="jobSectionHeader">
<b>Responsibilities</b><b><br>
</b>
</h2>
<div></div>
<div><div><div>
<div><b>How You Will Make a Difference</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
Translate projected forecasts, incoming volume, and capacities into a production plan with a time-phased build schedule.</li>
<li>
Work with customer base to determine weekly inbound forecast and develop consumption models to improve accuracy.</li>
<li>
Establish key performance indicators of forecast accuracy. Ensure the interaction of a firm’s functional managers, and track and maintain the forecast through timely and accurate feedback.
</li>
<li>Schedule demand based on the demand mix and provide accurate commitment dates to customer service team across multiple crew types</li>
<li>
Assemble master schedule reporting regarding performance to the business plan, and the demand plan</li>
<li>
Provide input to finance on operating plans for the sales and operations planning (S&amp;OP) process, concerning incoming volume and capacity</li>
<li>
Identify current planned level of demand and create tactical crew plans to meet the identified customer demand. Calculate production lead times.</li>
<li>
Manage load leveling and bottleneck scheduling and utilize theory of constraints (TOC) for more accurate scheduling.</li>
<li>
Coordinate information with other functional staff, including project management, engineering, accounting, and human resources.</li>
<li>
Establish reporting to determine labor and productivity performance statistics.</li>
<li>
Establish, measure, monitor, and adjust capacity levels to execute all manufacturing schedules.</li>
</ul>
