<h2>Outside Sales Agent</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Are you looking for a high-income outside sales position? Then our Outside Sales Agent opportunity is the perfect fit for you. SLICE is looking for experienced sales people who can market out services to meet and exceed their personal income and production goals.</p>
<p>SLICE Business Marketing provides our Sales Agents with preset, qualified, and triple confirmed appointments. We are able to accomplish an industry leading closing ratio from our in-house marketing channel. We generate 2-3 daily appointments within a 15-20 mile radius from your home zip code. Sales Agents are assigned a dedicated Sales Manager who will train and offer 100% support to you on a daily basis.</p>
<p>SLICE Business Marketing is a full payment solution company, based out of Midtown, NYC. We provide small to medium sized businesses with the service and equipment they need to process credit card transactions. We are able to offer businesses a wide range of services such as terminals, POS systems, loyalty programs, business financing and much more.</p>

In addition, all Sales Agents are entitled to monthly residuals on all active accounts. Production bonuses are offered on a daily, weekly and monthly basis.</p>
<p>SLICE is also leading the industry with our NEW Cash Discount/Credit Surcharge Programs, helping merchants eliminate processing fees altogether. Sales Agents now have an edge when prospecting, to offer a solution to the merchant that has never been offered before.</p>
