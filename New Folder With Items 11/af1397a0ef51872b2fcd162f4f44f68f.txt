<p></p>
<p>SUMMARY:
</p>
<p></p>
<p>The Associate Brand Manager (ABM) is a key contributor to the overall brand team, responsible for leading cross functional teams in planning, execution and analysis of various business initiatives. The ABM role is a general management role with P&amp;L responsibility and includes development of strategic business and marketing plans to achieve corporate and brand volume, profit and share objectives.
</p>
<p></p>
<p>ESSENTIAL DUTIES &amp; RESPONSIBILITIES:
</p>
<ul>
<li>Develop business objectives and marketing plans for assigned brand/categories, reflecting overall corporate goals; seamlessly execute marketing plans to deliver against brand, financial and share objectives
</li>
<li>Monitor performance measurements to ensure brand is tracking against goals - recommends and implements corrective actions
</li>
<li>Analyze data to identify insights and translate into business initiatives and/or actionable tasks to drive results
</li>
<li>Lead/participate in development and execution of marketing strategies including brand positioning, communication strategies, advertising and consumer promotion initiatives
</li>
<li>Direct agency partners as required to ensure effective and efficient translation of marketing strategies into activation plans
</li>
<li>Lead/participate in development and execution of product and packaging innovation plans including concept development, feasibility validation, product and packaging R&amp;D, business proposal and approval, project execution, customer sell in and tracking of in-market performance
</li>
<li>Partner with short term and long term innovation, channels and customer business teams to develop and execute innovation plans including concept development, feasibility validation, product and packaging R&amp;D, business proposal and approval, project execution, customer sell in and tracking of in-market performance
</li>
<li>Work hand-in-hand with the sales organization to understand and integrate customer strategies in business and brand plans
</li>
<li>Proactively work with Sales Strategy and Planning partners to ensure forecast accuracy, maximization of opportunities and development of plans to deliver against JBP objectives
</li>
<li>Manage monthly volume forecast for assigned brand/categories, coordinating between demand and supply planning to deliver best-in-class forecast accuracy
</li>
<li>Manage brand budget including MAP spending for assigned product responsibility
</li>
</ul>
<p><br>
REQUIREMENTS:
</p>
<ul>
<li>An MBA or equivalent education/experience is required
</li>
<li>A minimum 2 years of relevant marketing experience is required
</li>
<li>Demonstrated strong analytical and strategic thinking skills are required; strong communication and presentation skills are required; strong collaborator with a demonstrated ability to lead cross functional teams; proven ability to effectively manage multiple projects in a dynamic environment is required
</li>
<li>Experience with Nielsen and/or IRI is preferred
</li>
<li>6-10 trips per year
</li>
</ul>
