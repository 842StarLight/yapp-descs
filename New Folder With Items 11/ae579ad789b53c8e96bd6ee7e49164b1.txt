<h2>Mortgage Loan Originator- Houston, TX</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<ul>
<li>We are looking for an experienced Mortgage Loan Originator in the Houston (77064) area for Highland HomeLoans, an exciting joint venture between PrimeLending and Highland Homes<br>
</li>
<li>We offer outstanding technology, a very wide variety of products, and industry-leading operations and support teams</li>
<li>Over 120 estimated closings in 2018 for this market and more projected for future years!!!</li>
<li>Guaranteed leads... this is a captured account, and serves as a BONUS for loan officers</li>
<li>Even higher commissions paid for non builder-referred loans
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p>Duties:</p>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Market and promote the company’s financial products</li>
<li>Monitor overall customer interaction and interface with all parties involved on each individual loan from application to closing, including but not limited to counseling and pre-qualifying potential home buyers</li>
<li>Collect and analyze information regarding the customer’s income, assets, investments or debts</li>
<li>Evaluate the companies and other loan products and services and determines which financial products best meet the customer’s needs and financial circumstances</li>
<li>Advise the customer regarding the advantages and disadvantages of different loan products</li>
<li>Service existing customers and obtains all necessary support documents along with the appropriate fees and lock in information</li>
<li>Oversee the loan process by monitoring loan status and ensures conformity with terms</li>
<li>Communicate loan status to all interested parties, and obtains loan documentation after closing</li>
<li>Negotiate terms and conditions of loans with customers</li>
<li>Stay abreast of trends and developments in the local real estate market and competitive environment, as well as, changes in rules and regulations pertaining to private and government insured mortgages</li>
<li>Maintain a professional image and adheres to standards consistent with company policies and procedures</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p>Qualifications:</p>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>High school diploma or equivalent</li>
<li>Appropriate licenses, if state required</li>
<li>A minimum of 12 months loan originator experience required for Loan Originator; minimum of 3 years loan originator experience required for Production Manager</li>
<li>Interacts with regional &amp; corporate departments, as well as realtors, sales agents, builders, appraisers, etc.</li>
<li>Demonstrated judgment and decision-making ability</li>
<li>Excellent and effective presentation and communication skills, both verbal and written</li>
<li>Ability to quickly assess and process large amounts of data and use of appropriate AU software technology</li>
<li>Must be active in the community and in those job-related organizations to enhance the network of beneficial contacts</li>
<li>Continue to expand knowledge of issues influencing the industry and the organization</li>
<li>Establishes and maintains relationships with realtors, bank officers, builders and new home sales representatives to develop and retain existing business; and by visiting subdivisions as required</li>
<li>Originators are expected to meet the company’s minimum production standards of 3 funded units per month on an ongoing basis, by taking complete and accurate loan applications, using the company’s product set</li>
</ul>
