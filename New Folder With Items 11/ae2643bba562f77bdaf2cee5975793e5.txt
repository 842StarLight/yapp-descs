<h2>Assistant Project Manager – OEM</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Position Responsibilities:
</p>
<p>The Assistant Project Manager - OE (Original Equipment) is responsible for the on-time and on-customer specification execution of all new OE projects. He/she will work with OE Sales, Design, Engineering, and Operations both in Oakland and HK to create, implement and track the success of a system that helps enable the completion of all OE projects according to our customers’ requirements and timelines. This position reports to the Senior Product Manager.
</p>
<ul>
<li>–Build and maintain a line plan of all new products and projects by customer
</li>
<li>–Create and maintain a schedule of key dates &amp; deliverables by customer and product/project
</li>
<li>–Understand all customer project requirements and making sure that they are defined, documented, and allocated as deliverables within the project schedule
</li>
<li>–Collaborate cross functionally with all groups to track and update status of all project deliverables
</li>
<li>–Create a standard template/checklist of all project needs and deliverables agreed to by all collaborators with greater detail of what’s required than can be communicated in the topline schedule</li>
<li>
–Create and maintain a master file directory of all key docs for each customer/project</li>
</ul>
<p>
Qualifications:
</p>
<ul>
<li>–Bachelor's degree (B.A./B.S.) from four-<i>year</i> college or university or at least four years direct of relevent experience and/or training
</li>
<li>–Effective and precise verbal and written communication skills</li>
<li>
–Strong proficiency with Windows based computers, Microsoft Word, Excel, and PowerPoint</li>
<li>
–PLM software experience is a plus</li>
<li>
–Accurate, organized, and prompt work routines</li>
<li>
–Superior attention to detail</li>
</ul>
