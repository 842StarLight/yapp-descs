<h2>Full Charge Bookkeeper</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>ARTBOOK | D.A.P. is the world's foremost distributor of books on art and visual culture, representing international museums, galleries, and publishers. We are looking for a Full Charge Bookkeeper with strong A/P and Excel experience to join our team of 15 creative, hard-working book and art loving people here in New York City.</p>
<p><b>Your Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Receive all invoices; set up new vendors as needed; match invoices to PO's and receiving documents; code invoices to proper GL account(s) and input into ERP accounting system (Acumen Book Publishing software system).</li>
<li>Maintain vendors in accounting system; code by default GL account; obtain 1099's; respond to inquiries and maintain contact log; reconcile statements; research and correct any discrepancies</li>
<li>Reconcile against receipts and allocate company credit card expenses to GL accounts</li>
<li>Format inventory receipts and adjustments Excel file from warehouse and import data files into ERP accounting system following precise written instructions and resolve discrepancies with company's Operations Director; record consignment inventory versus purchased inventory according to instructions</li>
<li>Format sales and returns Excel data from warehouses and import data files into ERP accounting system following precise instructions, including proofing and resolving inquiries with warehouses</li>
<li>Prepare monthly Consignment Sales Statements by following detailed written instructions (roughly 175 different consignment vendors) to produce statements from ERP accounting system</li>
<li>Review warehouse charges and other expenses and code by Vendor and Type; enter credits against Vendor A/P on a monthly basis.</li>
<li>Record monthly commission earnings for company's 8 commissioned Sales Rep in A/P and make any GL adjustments.</li>
<li>Set up advance and royalty terms in ERP accounting system; prepare semi-annual author royalty statements for approximately 75 authors</li>
<li>Provide management with weekly recommended disbursement list based on AP Aging, payment terms, and communications with staff and vendors; provide rolling forecast of future AP disbursements; process and send out weekly payments by initiating ACH payments, outgoing wires and check runs</li>
<li>Manage payroll; solicit timesheets and enter payroll to Primepay payroll service for part-timers; enter payroll into GL for full-time employees twice monthly from both Primepay and Trinet</li>
<li>Manage relationship with TriNet PEO for employee benefits; enroll and terminate employees from TriNet PEO as well as COBRA; set up and maintain PTO for full-time employees</li>
<li>Handle routine tax matters; collect all needed information and file all vendor 1099’s; prepare sales taxes for six states as needed; pay corporate taxes according to accountant's directions</li>
<li>Perform month-end Accounts Payable closing duties including journal entries, accrual entries, and reconciliations</li>
<li>Assist with audit requests from company management and outside CPA, such as preparing supporting documentation and schedules</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Skills and Experience Required</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Minimum 3 years of Bookkeeping/Accounting experience, with extensive A/P work</li>
<li>Accurate and swift data entry skills with meticulous attention to detail</li>
<li>Advanced MS Excel skills are mandatory for this role. Must be able to easily manipulate large volumes of data and use advanced formulas including Pivot Tables, and V-Lookups. Must be familiar with general process of importing and exporting csv and txt files to and from Excel and external databases into accounting system.</li>
<li>Superior time management skills and the ability to meet weekly, monthly, quarterly and annual deadlines without needing supervision</li>
<li>A strong work ethic, self-discipline and ability to sustain focus on complex projects, and the self-confidence to ask for help when needed</li>
<li>Demonstrated integrity, ability to maintain confidential information, and fiduciary responsibility backed by strong references</li>
<li>Strong analytical, mathematical, troubleshooting, problem solving abilities, and multi-tasking skills</li>
<li>Ability to learn and adapt to company's publishing ERP system (Acumen Book Publishing Software)</li>
<li>Foundational understanding of accounting principles, including General Ledger, Chart of Accounts, Journal Entries, Reconciliations, Trial Balance, and Year-end Closings</li>
<li>Ability to communicate -- both orally and in writing -- in a clear, professional manner to co-workers, clients, management and company's outside accountant</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Skills and Experience -- "A Plus"</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Prior work experience within book publishing, museums, bookstores, galleries, media, design or other creative businesses is a plus.</li>
<li>Experience with a variety of different accounting software packages is a plus.</li>
<li>Experience with international transactions and foreign exchange is a plus.</li>
<li>Familiarity with handling inter-company transactions and reconciliations is a plus.</li>
<li>Experience with inventory reconciliation, consignment inventory and multiple warehouses is a plus.</li>
</ul>

We represent the world’s most respected museums and arts publishers, including the Guggenheim, the MFA Boston, the Museum of Modern Art, the Royal Academy of Arts, Hatje Cantz, Skira, Damiani, and many more. D.A.P. has been the exclusive distributor for such seminal publications as <i>Soul of a Nation: Art in the Age of Black Power, Agnes Martin,</i> and <i>Gerhard Richter: Forty Years of Painting</i>. Learn more about us at www.artbook.com</p>
