<p><b>National Accounts Recruiter</b></p>
<p>Blackstone Technology Group is hiring an experienced National Accounts Recruiter in our Denver office to meet our client’s contract, contract to hire and direct placement needs. Our National Enterprise Account Management practice is a tight knit operation in Denver and a global provider of contingent talent solutions. Our seasoned talent professionals have a successful history of servicing the toughest demands of our staffing clients. Our agile and customized-to-client approach ensures that we meet our client’s specific needs and that we adapt to and fully support our client’s way of doing business.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>The Job:</b></p>
<ul><li>Our Technical Recruiters enjoy an immense amount of autonomy to drive the hiring process of delivering quality IT professionals in a time-sensitive, split-desk staffing environment. Our goal of meeting our client’s contract, contract to hire and direct placement needs. We have a thriving business within large and mid-cap accounts and need help fulfilling well-qualified requirements for clients who are ready to strike.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Blackstone Technology Group is also looking for qualifications from hard-working tenured technical recruiting professionals who are deadline driven, bright, enthusiastic, competitive and creative in navigating the competitive IT and VMS marketplace in getting to the right candidate first. We seek self-managed, driven performers who are results-focused and committed to the hunt of uncovering new candidates while nurturing lasting relationships with your network.</li></ul>
<br>
<div></div>
<div><b>Requirements:</b></div>
<br>
<div></div>
<ul><li>1+ years of quantifiable recruiting experience within a technical talent agency</li></ul>
<ul><li>Good working knowledge of VMS systems and high volume recruiting</li></ul>
<ul><li>Prior experience managing the full-life cycle candidate recruitment process (source, network, screen, recruit, market and close top tier technology talent) within the IT Technology Sector</li></ul>
<ul><li>Ability to assess candidate's technical ability; identify and match candidate's behavioral traits</li></ul>
<ul><li>Strong experience in salary negotiation with formal training a plus</li></ul>
<ul><li>Experience utilizing traditional and non-traditional recruiting methodologies; Bullhorn &amp; LinkedIn Recruiter a plus</li></ul>
<ul><li>Self-starter, motivated, organized and detailed/results oriented</li></ul>
<ul><li>Proven experience meeting and exceeding individual quotas; $500K+ annual gross spread ideal.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Creative and resourceful to provide solutions to both the sales force and clients</li></ul>
<ul><li>Ability to think outside of the box in order to get to the right candidate first</li></ul>
<ul><li>BA / BS from an accredited 4-year university preferred</li></ul>
