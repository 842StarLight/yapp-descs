<p><b><i>Admin. Assistant/Receptionist Purpose</i></b></p>
<p>Our Admin. Assistant provides a full range of front office administrative and clerical support as well as warehouse support. They also provide key support to the CEO and company staff and sets the tone of engagement for visitors and callers. They expertly balance friendly interactions with effective and efficient execution of company standards and role responsibilities. They are resourceful, supportive and assertive. The Admin. Assistant/Receptionist acts as the gatekeeper and enables the CEO to focus on strategy and leadership. To preform this job successfully, an individual must be able to preform each primary responsibility satisfactorily.</p>
<p>The requirements listed below are representative of the knowledge, skill and / or ability required and is not intended to be an exhaustive list of all duties, responsibilities or qualifications associated with this job. This position does not supervise other personnel.</p>
