<h2>Contract Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Location: </b> Beale Air Force Base, California</p>
<p><b>Job title: </b> Contracts Specialists/Acquisition</p>
<p>Experience:</p>
<ul>
<li>Minimum of 2 years of construction contracts relevant experience as required.</li>
<li>Personnel proficient in the use of Procurement Desktop (PD2) or similar programs, and additional commercial software packages such as Microsoft Word, Excel, and PowerPoint.</li>
<li>24 Credit hours in business from an accredited college or university or have held a Defense Acquisition Workforce Improvement Act (DAWIA) Level 1 certification.</li>
<li>Knowledge of Federal Acquisition Regulations</li>
</ul>
<p>Duties:</p>
<p>Employees will independently perform non-personal services in accordance with the performance work statement for contracted contract administrators. Develop negotiation objectives as well as pre-award and post-award contract documents with minimal oversight for government personnel review and approval. Review procurement requests to ensure compliance with procedures and regulations and sufficiency of technical specifications or purchase description and supporting information. Determine the suitability of set-asides, such as small business set asides, in accordance with all applicable laws. Review justifications for other than full and open competition and make recommendations for approving and disapproving choices to limit competition. Develop solicitation documents and other contractual documentation, such as determinations and findings, justifications, solicitation amendments, supporting memoranda.</p>
<p>Employee will review bids or proposals for compliance to solicitation and determine contractor responsibility, which includes making competitive range determinations. More specifically, provide services that include the following contract/acquisition support activities:</p>
<ul>
<li>Pre-award Functions</li>
<li>Price &amp; Cost Analysis</li>
<li>Preparation of Award and Supporting Documentation</li>
<li>Contract Administration</li>
<li>Contract Closeout</li>
<li>Pricing Support</li>
<li>Training</li>
<li>Information Technology</li>
<li>Other Duties as Assigned</li>
</ul>
<p>Job Type: Full-time</p>
<p>Job Type: Full-time</p>
