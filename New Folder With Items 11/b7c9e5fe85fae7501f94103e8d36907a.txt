<h2>Outside Sales Executive</h2>

<br>Define and execute sales pursuit strategies within your assigned territory
<br>Collaborate with Inside Sales, Solution Engineering and Marketing in support of your territory business plan
<br>Effective pipeline management to accurately forecast and close new business
<br>Negotiate contract terms and pricing
<br>Execute the e-Builder sales process and document your activity in our CRM of record Salesforce.com

<br>3-5 years sales experience in a software sales position
<br>Demonstrated history of sales target and quota achievement
<br>Outstanding communication and presentation skills
<br>Ability to interact with C-level executives and to present and discuss technical issues in a polished, well-spoken manner both in person and over the phone.
