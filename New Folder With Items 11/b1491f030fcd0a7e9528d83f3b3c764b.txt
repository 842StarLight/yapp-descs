<b>Position Summary:</b><br>
Founded in 1947 CEB is one of California's oldest and most prestigious legal information and continuing legal education providers. CEB publishes judgments of California case law, practical guidance for attorneys and, CLE all in a variety of practice areas and formats e.g. print, online and, in-person.<br>
<br>
The Marketing Automation Administrator is responsible for managing CEB's marketing automation platform and related systems. This role will work to implement new initiatives, define operational processes and take steps to maximize effectiveness of campaigns. Evaluate and recommend new tools and technologies for streamlining CEB's lead management process, oversee all marketing list pulls, list maintenance, work with our ESP (E-mail service provider) to prepare and send our marketing emails, as well as developing sales reports. The Systems Administrator will be responsible for creating nurture campaign and Marketo or Hubspot landing pages, forms, and email templates. This role will also be responsible for maintaining lead scoring and values.<br>
