<p><b>
Job Summary</b></p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
The Director of Marketing, Vail Resorts Retail &amp; Rental is responsible for setting the strategic growth objectives to deliver on the revenue and volume targets for the company’s ski/snowboard equipment rental and retail business.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
As a key leader on the Vail Resorts Marketing, Sales &amp; Communications (MSC) team, the Director of Marketing helps establish long term strategic plans for the growth and evolution of the rental and retail business in resort and city locations. S/he sets the vision for brand architecture across the portfolio of 50+ retail and rental brands that Vail Resorts Retail (VRR) operates across 250+ locations.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
The Director leads the VRR Marketing Team in delivering advance reservations for ski rental products, as well as driving traffic and sales to physical brick and mortar store locations for the company’s Retail locations, through the creation and execution of strategic marketing plans which leverage paid media, digital and web channels, CRM, email and direct marketing, all in concert with Vail Resorts’ overall marketing practices and approach.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Job Responsibilities</b></p>
<p></p>
<br>
<ul>
<li>
Leads the development and execution of a multi-year growth roadmap, which guides the priorities for the annual and seasonal business &amp; experience planning for the division.
</li>
<li>Leads the development of brand positions and a scalable brand architecture for the company’s portfolio of rental and retail brands</li>
<li>
Serves as a key member on the Resort Marketing Leadership Team, as well as on the VRR Leadership Team, helping to set the company strategies to deliver visitation &amp; revenue targets for Vail Resorts’ collection of world-class mountain resorts</li>
<li>
Drives the strategy, marketing plans and user experience for the equipment rental advanced purchase commitment in collaboration with resort-based teams for all rental products.
</li>
<li>Delivers the traffic and sales required to reach or exceed budgeted sales goals for each of the retail brands/store groups/regions where VRR operates, including locations in Colorado (Denver, Keystone, Breckenridge, Frisco, Silverthorne, Vail, Avon, Beaver Creek, , Aspen, Telluride, Crested Butte, Copper Mountain, Winter Park and Granby), UT (Park City and Kimble Junction), CA/NV (Heavenly/South Lake, Kirkwood, Northstar, Tahoe City, Concord, Corte Madera, Dublin, and Redwood City), MN (Minneapolis), NE (Stowe, Okemo, Mount Sunapee), Stevens Pass and British Colombia (Whistler).</li>
<li>
Fosters and develops meaningful and powerful internal relationships with leaders and teams within VRR (Operations, Merchandising, Finance, IT and HR) and within the teams we work with and support within the broader company
</li>
<li>Establishes a long term vision and annual plan for co-operative marketing programs with key vendor partners in order to support overarching VRR goals and objectives; facilitates sell-in of co-op plans with vendor partners; and reports out co-op plan performance</li>
<li>
Researches, analyzes, and monitors financial, technological, and demographic factors to capitalize on market opportunities and minimize effects of competitive activity
</li>
<li>Owns annual budgeting process for the Marketing Department including budget development, forecasts and spending approvals</li>
<li>
Provides leadership and direction for the VRR Marketing Department; oversees and supervises the recruitment and training of department staff; creates and executes a deeply focused and well planned program to foster the professional and personal growth of the VRR Marketing Team with a focus on emotional intelligence and self-awareness in the service of creating a high performing teams</li>
<li>
Strong learning agility. Enjoys and excels in environments where he/she must tackle and solve new and increasingly complex strategic business challenges and issues—incorporating the newest ideas, technologies, and approaches to business.</li>
<li>
A brave, ambitious, and passionate leader who is comfortable in his/her own skin while also demonstrating a high level of emotional intelligence and self-awareness.</li>
</ul>
