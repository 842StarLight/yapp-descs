<h2>Junior Application Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div><div><div><div><div><div><div><div><div>
<div><div><div>
<p>The Junior Application Analyst role will be responsible for assisting with the ongoing support of our enterprise professional services clients within hospitality. Success in this role requires the ability to learn, grow and better your understanding in all things hospitality technology and hospitality operations.</p>
<p><b>
Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Assist with the configuration, training, installation, and ongoing support for our client’s Point-of-Sale software and hospitality technology needs</li>
<li>Responsible for the execution of client’s needs, requests and requirements based on <i>contracted</i> SLA guidelines</li>
<li>Acquire and integrate industry knowledge related to general trends and emerging technologies</li>
<li>Build relationships with customers to become a trusted advisor, expert and personable resource for each client</li>
<li>
<i>Work</i> collaboratively with all DMC teams and stakeholders to coordinate the company’s efforts to help the customer</li>
<li>Based on customer interactions, identify <i>opportunities</i> for DMC to improve in its ability to service, support, manage and communicate with its customers and communicate those <i>opportunities</i> in an effective and prioritized way</li>
<li>Participate in cross functional teams on process improvement to benefit customers</li>
<li>Act as an escalation point for customers to ensure effective and rapid communication and resolution of problems</li>
<li>Manage and track customer interactions in multiple CRM/Support Ticketing Systems</li>
<li>Practice effective, <i>excellent</i> communication with management, customers and team members</li>
<li>Travel to customer locations can be required based on project scope. Up to 50% travel can be expected</li>
<li>Engage directly with customers in technical or business escalations, and drive issues to resolution. You will be a direct point of contact for escalations and will be responsible for handling crisis situations.</li>
<li>Off-hour evening, holiday and/or weekend <i>work</i> required to support new installations, product upgrades, training, live support, and configuration changes</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p><b>
</b></p>
<p><b>Ideal Candidate:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Understands the standard concepts, best practices, and procedures of Point-of-Sale (POS) software integration and deployment within hospitality. Specific experience with the Agilysys InfoGenesis™ POS, Micros, Aloha or other POS systems a plus</li>
<li>Demonstrates attention to detail and organization skills</li>
<li>Clear and professional written and oral communication skills</li>
<li>Identifies and resolves problems in a timely manner; develops alternative solutions</li>
<li>Demonstrates accuracy and thoroughness; looks for ways to improve and promote quality</li>
<li>Interacts effectively with all levels of management and other employees</li>
<li>Ability to handle a fast-paced environment and challenging workload</li>
<li>
<i>Work</i> well on their own or as a member of a team</li>
<li>Confident, assertive, flexible, team-oriented and professional</li>
<li>Effectively prioritize and escalate customer issues as required</li>
<li>Maintain composure and customer focus while troubleshooting and solving technical issues</li>
<li>An aptitude for acquiring new skills in hospitality technology &amp; mobile solutions</li>
<li>Ability to quickly learn new software and applications</li>
<li>Be punctual, with a positive attitude</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Key Requirements:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>BA/BS degree preferred</li>
<li>1 year of F&amp;B Operations and/or Hospitality Technology Experience Required</li>
<li>Must be proficient in MS Word, Excel, Outlook, and PowerPoint</li>
<li>POS or other hospitality technology experience a plus</li>
</ul>
</div></div></div>
