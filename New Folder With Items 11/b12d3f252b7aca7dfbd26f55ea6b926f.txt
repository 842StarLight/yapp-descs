<h2>Retail Merchandising Display Tech</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>Are you searching for a new career opportunity that incorporates your outgoing personality, tech-savvy aptitude, and sense of accomplishment?</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
BDSmktg values your commitment and in return provides an environment with growth opportunities. We offer flexible schedules, great hourly pay with paid mileage and drive time, monthly business expense reimbursement, bi-weekly paychecks, as well as paid training. A retail or merchandising background is beneficial but not required as we make an investment in your training to ensure your success.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>IDEAL CANDIDATE:</b></p>
<p>
Our team of Retail Merchandising Display Techs represents leading consumer electronic brands in retailers nationwide where they install, maintain, troubleshoot and repair interactive displays and demos. Your ability to problem solve, follow directions and establish rapport with store management will directly impact your success in this position. You will be at the forefront of new product releases which include headphones, phones, speakers, tablets, drones, and other consumer electronic products. Basic tools are needed for constructing and maintaining interactive displays. An Android or Apple mobile device is required for uploading photos and data upon completion of your assignments. Your schedule flexibility should be Monday – Friday with the ability to work early morning to midday.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p>To learn more, please view our YouTube video: Day In the Life of A BDS Retail Merchandising Display Tech</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
PURPOSE:</b></p>
<p>
Our Retail Merchandising Display Tech will be conducting break-fix, installation, and ad-hoc maintenance work, in addition to general merchandising tactics. Responsibilities include installing/building displays, end caps, fixturing, and maintenance of those displays, planogram resets, conducting break-fix repairs, FedEx team leadership, and in-market training for other areas.</p>
<br>
<p></p>

<li>We stimulate, encourage and nurture creativity in an environment that allows employees to think outside-the-box in order to do what’s best for each client.</li>

<br>
</ul>
<p>
</p>
<p><b>ESSENTIAL FUNCTION:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Install and/or build displays, fixturing, and endcaps; involving the setup of security, cabling, wiring, monitors/media players, and audio connectivity</li>
<li>Troubleshoot and resolve issues necessary to ensure displays are fully functional and meet maintenance requirements</li>
<li>Meet Ad-hoc maintenance requirements which include:
<ul>
<li>Store service within 48-72 hours</li>
<li>Travel up to 60 miles with paid Drive Time and Mileage</li>
<li>Independently troubleshoot and enable a fully-functional display given necessary parts</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>Report the status of each store visit accurately to show success in achieving objectives</li>
<li>Perform general merchandising functions including but not limited to reset PLANOGRAMs, POP, and pricing/fact tags</li>
<li>Conduct one-on-one training for other BDS market reps as necessary regarding break-fix work and IC Offline App data entry</li>
<li>Participate actively in weekly conference calls</li>
<li>Complete administrative tasks, including but not limited to scheduling reports within 24-48 hours of program launch and report visits by project end date</li>
<li>Other tasks as requested by management</li>
<br>
</ul>
<div>
</div>
<p><b>EDUCATION AND EXPERIENCE:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>High school diploma or equivalent required</li>
<li>Complete/Pass required Break Fix and ad-hoc paid training requirements as well as one-off project training prior to conducting visits</li>
<li>Experience in building displays, fixturing, monitor cabling/connectivity</li>
<li>Able to read schematics and correctly connect components</li>
<li>Experience using tools required to maintain displays</li>
<li>Experience in troubleshooting and fixing display issues, i.e.…light fixtures, malfunctioning buttons, computer/tablet/Smartphone demo-related issues, software reboots, electronic component connectivity, etc.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p>
</p>
<p><b>SKILLS AND REQUIREMENTS:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Must have good verbal and written communication skills</li>
<li>Detail oriented and highly organized</li>
<li>Ability to independently manage an account base</li>
<li>Display a professional demeanor at all times</li>
<li>Must be able to work Monday through Friday with availability to work in-store from 8 am – 2 pm each week with some occasional weekend work</li>
<li>Must be proficient with using and have either a Smartphone or Tablet which is an Android or Apple product as well as an active data plan, <i>according to required specifications</i>
</li>
<li>Must have a computer with internet/web access and the ability to send and receive email attachments</li>
<li>Reliable transportation required</li>
<li>Must make the commitment to complete the market area’s minimum weekly or special project hours requests</li>
<li>Must meet all pre-and post-employment requirements, which may include but not limited to background check, drug screening, and motor vehicle review</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p>
</p>

While performing the duties of this job, the employee is required to:</p>

<li>Occasionally travel via flight or other modes of transportation</li>
<li>Continuous hand/eye coordination and fine manipulation</li>
<br>
</ul>
