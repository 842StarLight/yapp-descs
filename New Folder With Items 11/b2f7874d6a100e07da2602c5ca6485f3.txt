<p>Evoqua Water Technologies is currently seeking an experienced Project Manager in Waukesha, WI.</p>
<br>
<p><br>
The Project Manager will be responsible for execution of multiple projects with all applicable assigned project resources and to ensure a high level of customer satisfaction. Performs project management activities (Scheduling, Budget, Risk, Change management, Opportunity, and Resource management activities). Performs technical review of system design and documentation. The Project Manager must have the ability to manage the multiple projects utilizing project performance reporting tools, leading project review/ project closeout meetings, status updates and reporting. Phases include managing projects from commercial hand-off through project close out (shipment, start-up or customer acceptance), while driving receivables.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Primary duties include, but are not limited to:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Define, forecast, measure, and report risk, opportunities, revenue, and gross margin performance on all assigned projects.</li>
<li>Report status to Management via monthly project review meetings</li>
<li>Report technical/schedule status to customer</li>
<li>Coordination and lead of technical project team. Provides direction to the internal team who includes engineering, production planning, purchasing, finance and sales.</li>
<li>Able to negotiate with the customer for changes to the project scope and additional work</li>
<li>Central point of contact for the customer of each project, with respect to technical, commercial items/activities and project schedule</li>
<li>Establish baseline schedules within SAP PS Module and drive project resources to deliver on committed schedule baseline in support of the work assigned.</li>
<li>Participate in Make/Buy analysis with Sourcing staff to drive cost reduction and cost control measures.</li>
<li>Lead and manage specific project activities from Bid Handover Phase to project close out.</li>
<li>Accepts, completes, and updates personal developmental planned targets as agreed to annually with the department Manager.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Requirements:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s Degree in Engineering, or advanced degree, or equivalent combination of education and experience</li>
<li>PMP Certification is a plus</li>
<li>Candidate possesses 5-8 years of experience, preferably with minimum 2-4 years’ experience in a Project Manager role</li>
<li>Project Management skills mandatory</li>
<li>Problem solving skills mandatory</li>
<li>Solid communicator both written and oral</li>
<li>Works well with and has demonstrated success with customer</li>
<li>Strong interpersonal skills with team members, peers, etc.</li>
<li>Leadership qualities and role model qualities demonstrated</li>
<li>Takes initiative to proactively identify and mitigate project risks</li>
<li>Functional knowledge of MS Office software; working knowledge of MS Project and SAP</li>
<li>Experience in manufacturing environment preferred</li>
<li>Ability to travel when required</li>
</ul>
