<p><b>Job Description: </b>Product Owner – Messaging</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>The Product Owner is a pivotal role within P97 as they are accountable P97’s Digital Messaging platform. The key responsibility of the Product Owner includes executing business plans related to creation and execution of the messaging features of P97’s product experience and serves as the link between Product Directors (strategy) and the Engineering Department (execution).</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>The Product Owner must have a passion for web and mobile technologies, a strong understanding of modern web and mobile software technology and possess demonstratable knowledge of the Agile product development lifecycle. The Product Owner is also responsible for supporting Sales, Business Development and Professional Services with their domain expertise as well as end customer’s and/or other stakeholders, with an intent to maximize the value of the product through ensuring that the work is done correctly—on specification, on schedule, and on budget.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>
<b>Core Platform Capabilities and Product Planning</b>: The Product Owner will work with the Product Director(s) to help develop a three-year roadmap for core products and support the creation of new product and digital transformation strategies, including product-market fit, product positioning, product architecture, alignment with partner roadmaps and integration strategies. The Product Owner will be responsible for writing user stories that align to the product requirements, including UI/UX, platform architectural requirements, and feedback from engineering, sales, marketing, and network operation teams. The Product owner will be responsible for understanding and documenting customer requirements, minimal viable product, product-market fit, business constraints/technical issues preventing rapid deployment and work with product marketing to help define go-to-market strategies, provide key product and market insights, and serve as the internal and external evangelist for your product offering.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>
<b>Product Features, Capabilities, and Schedule</b>: The Product Owner acts as a strategist who will translate the company’s high-level vision and goals into actionable tasks, set product strategy, prioritize features, build consensus, and coordinate product schedules with the design and engineering teams. The Product Owner will champion efforts to develop product vision, objectives, review acceptance criteria for user stories, and ensures that user stories meet Definition of Ready and are always current (e.g. daily and weekly updates). The Product Owner will also provide functional design, clarifications, and other details on JIRA stories to help the Engineering team build quality product. The Product Owner will play a significant role in quality and works with the Product Director to prepare features and user stories for continuous planning cycles. Product Owner must work very closely with the Scrum Team and Professional Services team and coordinates their activities over the whole lifetime of the project. The Product Owner is responsible for ensuring the agile development team works from a set of verified priorities as well as delivers customer/business value through product features and is the conduit to other business partners for the team.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>
<b>Domain Expertise and Support</b>: The Product Owner will support early adopter programs, minimal viable product launches, funded pilots with strategic customers. This role will heavily engage with the Professional Services team to detail requirements and understand what needs to be delivered to help the P97 Engineering team and is expected to provide guidance to the Engineering team in areas such as compliance with industry standards, and partner/network certifications.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p>This role will coordinate Product Director(s) to facilitate the development of the product capabilities necessary to deliver the agreed roadmap, while developing sponsorship within the sales team, engineering and architect teams to build the products and solutions enabled by the platform(s) for which they are responsible.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Key Responsibilities</b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Act as the “messenger of the market” from the outside-in to the rest of the organization, including validation of product offerings with global customers and align region specific implementation for products</li>
<li>Obsess over the customer, and test P97 experiences, to ensure customer first journey is driving a five-star rated app experience</li>
<li>Win industry awards in the area of customer related experiences</li>
<li>Analyze various solutions options and collaborate with leadership to choose the best options to pursue</li>
<li>Develop and maintain business requirements that makes a compelling case for continued investment into your products</li>
<li>Collaborate with other stakeholders on the creation of positioning products</li>
<li>Manage the entire product line life cycle from sunsetting legacy products to prototyping and testing new features/products and ideas</li>
<li>Drive buy-build-partner analysis, and where necessary, review potential partner relationships for the product</li>
<li>Know why we are winning and losing in the market, and drive changes to our product and processes to create improvement</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Key Success Factors</b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Create and maintain a consistent product roadmap definition that aligns with P97 goals and market requirements</li>
<li>Launch products with the help of other teams</li>
<li>Identify market opportunities and define product vision and strategy</li>
<li>Champion efforts to attain product vision and objectives.</li>
<li>Understand customer needs and gather product requirements in order to develop new products and enhance existing products</li>
<li>Engage and work closely with the design, marketing, and engineering team to help determine the best technical implementation methods as well as a reasonable execution schedule.</li>
<li>Experience with mobile commerce, retail fuel systems, fleet cards, and/or payments and processing is a huge plus!</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Minimum Requirements</b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree in advertising or marketing related field, or equivalent industry experience.</li>
<li>Demonstrated knowledge of mobile technology and messaging, preferably B2B-focused</li>
<li>Experience with mobile marketing and messaging (Urban Airship, Firebase, Branch.io, Parse, etc.)</li>
<li>Pragmatic Marketing Certified (PMC) III or higher is a plus!</li>
<li>Technical certification strongly preferred in a professionally recognized area</li>
<li>5+ years of experience working on scrum/agile projects</li>
<li>5+ years of marketing, development, sales, or product management experience</li>
<li>Tech savvy and passionate about building products</li>
<li>Understanding of internet and marketing platforms and SaaS based software</li>
<li>Metrics driven: Strong analytical and synthesis skills are a must, including the ability to absorb and process large amounts of data into actionable information</li>
<li>5+ year of experience as a product manager/functional consultant leading an IT project</li>
<li>Ability to manage work time to ensure the accomplishment of specific goals.</li>
<li>Strong experience in working across multiple cultures, countries, and working with complex large corporations</li>
<li>International or global/virtual project or program management experience</li>
<li>Technical aptitude and a passion for staying current with new and upcoming technologies to ensure the best possible solutions for clients.</li>
<li>Strong understanding of common standards (ISO, PCI, GDPR, etc.)</li>
<li>Extensive experience developing relationships with customers and internal leaders.</li>
<li>Self-motivated and charismatic leader and peer</li>
<br>
</ul>
