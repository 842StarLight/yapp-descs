<h2>Inside Rental Sales Representative</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Overview
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div>The principal function of the Inside Rental Sales position is to increase store rental revenue and profitability by providing superior customer service. Qualified candidates will be ambitious and motivated self-starters that utilize a strong sense of urgency and ability to multi-task to stay organized in a fast-past environment. Responsibilities include servicing new and existing accounts to achieve rental revenue and additional business unit sales goals while increasing market share and exceeding customer expectations.</div>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<ul>
<li>Coordinate with and support outside sales efforts on orders, bids/quotes, deliveries, and service</li>
<li>
Receive customer orders while negotiating maximum potential rate and quantity, obtaining full details of order and serving as the customer’s in store contact point on rental equipment.</li>
<li>
Responding to customer inquiries, managing customer expectations, resolving customer problems, issues, and concerns.</li>
<li>
Organize and maintain a best in class rental product display and an organized and clean warehouse.</li>
<li>
Dispatch delivery drivers for rental equipment delivery/pick-up and customer equipment delivery/pick-up</li>
<li>
Communicate and coordinate with customers, delivery drivers, service department and outside sales representatives to ensure customer expectations and revenue goals are exceeded.</li>
<li>
Processing rental contracts and invoicing with accuracy and detail including; maintaining/documenting of on/off rental dates, rates, ancillary charges, contract changes, filing of open and closed contracts.</li>
<li>
Monitor fleet availability for reservations and walk-ins including working with service department to ensure equipment availability and increase utilization.</li>
<li>
Professionally cross-sell all company product/services lines and up-sell value-added products/services</li>
<li>
Conduct outgoing calls to generate revenue, new accounts, and maintain existing accounts</li>
<li>
Document details of customer calls in phone log and maintain missed rental log</li>
<li>
Assist in the delivering of, loading/unloading of equipment and orienting customer on equipment</li>
<li>
Other duties/projects as assigned</li>
</ul>
</div></div></div>
