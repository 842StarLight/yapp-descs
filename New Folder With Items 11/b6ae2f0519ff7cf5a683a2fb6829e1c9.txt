<h2>Finance Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div></div>
<div><div>
<div>Responsible for managing all financial forecasting and planning processes of Zimmer Biomet, as well as, supporting the consolidation and reporting process for the division.
</div>
<div>
<h5 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Principal Duties and Responsibilities</b></h5>
•Develop budget instructions and submission templates for use by Functional Management.<br>
<ul>
<li>Develop a budget timeline for data submissions, presentations, etc.</li>
<li>Communicate budget instructions and targets to global Senior Management.</li>
<li>Review initial budget submissions for reasonableness and completeness.</li>
<li>Work with Region personnel (both Finance and other functions) to resolve plan submission issues.</li>
<li>Review monthly forecast submissions with the Regions (via conference calls and in person meetings).
</li>
<li>Analyze forecast versus actuals, plan and prior forecasts.</li>
<li>Work with Regional personnel (both Finance and other functions) to resolve forecast submission issues.</li>
<li>Provide GM with timely consolidated forecast information on a monthly basis.</li>
<li>Assist in the development of necessary financial reports to satisfy the Company’s internal and external reporting requirements,</li>
<li>Oversee all aspects of data gathering, interpreting, processing, consolidating, and reporting financial results.</li>
<li>Stay abreast of changes in reporting requirements and accounting developments and interpret the effect on Zimmer Biomet Spine.</li>
<li>Supports management and provides compliance and accuracy in consistent reporting that is used on a regular management basis across Spine and Group.</li>
<li>Helps to develop and report upon key financial metrics and assists in assessing the current results of the business.</li>
<li>Responsible for developing the financial reporting systems &amp; processes that the business heavily relies upon.</li>
<li>Special financial projects and analysis as assigned.</li>
</ul>
<br>
</div>
<div>
<h5 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Expected Areas of Competence</b></h5>
•Ability to lead a team and work independently in a timely and accurate manner.<br>
<ul>
<li>Strong written and verbal communication skills are required.</li>
<li>Must understand discounted cash flow analysis, ROI, payback, etc.</li>
<li>Need a diverse understanding of accounting areas including cost accounting, distribution, sales &amp; marketing, and R&amp;D. Need detailed knowledge of cost accounting and manufacturing.</li>
<li>Need an excellent understanding of accounting within a global organization. This includes items such as foreign exchange, inter-company mark-ups, inter-company charges and allocations, and tax issues.</li>
<li>Must be able to effectively use standard desktop software (Excel, PowerPoint, databases, etc.) as well as fully integrated ERP systems (examples include SAP, JD Edwards, Oracle).</li>
<li>Must be flexible, able to multi task and meet deadlines.</li>
<li>Must be able to effectively delegate and distribute assignments.</li>
<li>Must be able to effectively communicate with a diverse group including the Global Finance organization, Senior Management outside of the Finance function, and the Executive Management group.</li>
<li>Must be able to identify and take ownership of significant issues as they arise.</li>
<li>Must be able to make concise, effective, and professional presentations to senior management.
</li>
<li>Must be able to quickly analyze issues, determine what other persons or groups are needed to address the issue, and take necessary steps to assemble the group to drive resolution.</li>
<li>Must be willing to take an active role in mentoring and developing staff.</li>
<li>Must understand culture differences within a global company and be able to effectively communicate and work with those globally.</li>
</ul>
</div>
<div>
<h5 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Education/Experience Requirements</b></h5>
•B.S. degree in finance or accounting, MBA is preferred. 6-8 years’ experience in accounting/finance. Experience managing and developing a staff of finance professionals is required, or equivalent combination of education and experience. C.P.A. or C.M.A. is preferred.
</div>
