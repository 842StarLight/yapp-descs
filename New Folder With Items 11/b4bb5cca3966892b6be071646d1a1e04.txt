<h2>Client Service Executive, Infrastructure Services- Media</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">Client Service Executive, Infrastructure Services (Media &amp; Entertainment)
<br><br>
The Opportunity
<br>Cognizant's Infrastructure Services (CIS) organization seeks a top-tier professional and market savvy leader to drive infrastructure services growth in select accounts in North America. This vital Client Services Executive (CSE) role within CIS will generate revenue for the company with this sell to and sell through sales and services model by building well-rounded customer relationships centered on our existing and future Cognizant offerings.
<br><br>
Responsibilities
<br>This leader will own overall CIS revenue quota, customer profitability targets and relationships between CIS and the Media &amp; Entertainment vertical. This role will collaborate with the account managers &amp; other leaders in ensuring profitability and goal attainment for their portfolios of IT outsourcing customers.
<br><br>
The CSE is responsible for developing account management strategies and driving new business throughout the existing portfolios of select accounts; including revising and improving the entire bid management process, as needed.
<br><br>
<b>Key Result Areas:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Responsible for bid strategy, executive communications, executive summary in proposal and pricing to improve opportunity to win business and profitability</li>
<li>Responsible for overall P&amp;L within the account for CIS portfolio, including attaining revenue target, customer profitability, and customer satisfaction</li>
<li>Responsible to ensure CIS meets its delivery contractual requirements and meets/exceeds customer’s expectations.</li>
<li>Define, develop and execute an CIS plan for assigned vertical.</li>
<li>Lead virtual solution/proposal team on customer bids.</li>
<li>Responsible for overall P&amp;L within the accounts, including attaining revenue target, customer profitability, and customer satisfaction</li>
<li>Ensure high levels of client satisfaction through active engagement and relationship management (CSS)</li>
<li>Drives the sales of Next Generation Infrastructure solutions to enterprise customers</li>
<li>Fully establishes CIS CSE role within vertical structure and provides a comprehensive plan to open new CIS business in existing accounts</li>
</ul>
