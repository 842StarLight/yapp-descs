<h2>Production and Scheduling Associate</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>If a company is going to make a difference in today’s world, it’s going to have to think differently. At Organic Valley, our philosophy and decisions are based on the health and welfare of people, animals and the earth. We’re a mission-driven cooperative, owned by family farmers, and we’ve been leaders in organic agriculture from the very beginning. Once pioneers of organic agriculture, we’re now an established leader. Organic is all we do. We are driven by our mission to promote regional farm diversity and economic stability by the means of organic agricultural methods and the sale of certified organic products.<br>
<br>
<b>Summary of Role</b><br>
The Production and Scheduling Associate is responsible for providing clerical and administrative support to the management and staff at the Chaseburg Creamery, while also providing excellent customer service to internal and external customers. This role collaborates with multiple different departments and personnel both within and outside of CROPP on a daily basis.</p>
<p><b>
Essential Duties and Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Enter Chaseburg milk and butter production in to ERP and Production Excel spreadsheets with accuracy.</li>
<li>
Work with Ignition (Program) to look at Live Milk Data and for use with Calculator.</li>
<li>
Enter needed Data into Calculator to use for Entering and Issuing into Shop Orders.</li>
<li>
Enter shuttle truck transfers in ERP and coordinate the movement of product going to and coming from other warehouses and facilities.</li>
<li>
Coordinate and manage the movement of packaging materials and production supplies to keep operations at the creamery functioning smoothly.</li>
<li>
Enter and receive in purchase orders and incoming transfers in ERP. Communicate with outside vendors when a purchase order is entered to ensure production supplies arrive when requested.</li>
<li>
Enter and update information in the DOORS application, daily.</li>
<li>
Maintain organized records according to the legal standards of the dairy and production industries, as well as meet the
</li>
<li>Global Food Safety Standards as reviewed by internal and external auditors.</li>
<li>
Create and maintain Excel spreadsheet for data and tracking purposes.</li>
<li>
Place office and supplies orders and food orders for the employee kitchen and Corner Store.</li>
<li>
Enter Codes or Receipt numbers needed for Invoices from Accounting.</li>
<li>
Work with OV Federal Milk Marketing Dept. for balancing EOM Dairy Inventory and Print out EOM sheets for Recording of Fluid Milk.<br>
