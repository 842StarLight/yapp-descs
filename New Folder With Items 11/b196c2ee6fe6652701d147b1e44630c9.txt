<h2>Marketing Representative - Undergraduate Admissions</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">Major/Essential Functions
<br><br>
Proficient use of the customer relationship management software
<br>Create weekly and monthly marketing reports to be distributed to staff and leadership which includes updates on projects, analytics, and communication campaigns
<br>Coordinates logistics to meet or exceed timelines and identifies barriers that may hinder progress. Works directly with the director of marketing and/or project manager to manage delays.
<br>Assist with development and assessment of the lead generation strategic plan
<br>Manage the recruitment funnel to ensure print, electronic and social communication is delivered in a timely manner
<br>Routinely audit marketing campaigns as new leads enter the recruitment funnel, inventory control, ROI reports, and logistics

<br>Supports the digital marketing manager with the digital communication plan consisting primarily of decision, search and event email communication
