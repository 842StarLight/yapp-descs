<p><b>Territory Sales Manager - Northeast Region</b></p>
<p>
Founded in 1940, Chicago-based Beltone is part of the GN Hearing Care Group, a global leader in intelligent audio solutions with a unique portfolio of medical, professional and consumer audio solutions. Utilizing advanced technology to produce hearing aid instruments sold in the United States, Canada and over 50 countries worldwide, Beltone provides industry-leading products, services and support for its U.S. hearing care network operating in over 1,500 offices in North America. Beltone remains the most trusted brand for quality products and care among its patients and adults aged 50 and older. Visit Beltone at www.beltone.com.</p>
<p>
The primary function of the Territory Sales Manager (TSM) is to meet annual revenue goals for assigned territory. To achieve revenue goals the TSM should partner, consult, plan, and guide (1) all existing dispensers to operate a profitable and growing business, (2) expand locations in a profitable way, and (3) lead new owners to establish a profitable and successful business.</p>
<p><b>
Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Achieving net hearing instrument sales goal on a monthly, quarterly, and annual basis.</li>
<li>Work within assigned Territory as primary dispenser contact to increase Beltone unit sales and market share.</li>
<li>Preparing annual and quarterly territory plans.The quarterly plans are updated monthly and reviewed each month with the Director of Sales.</li>
<li>Building and maintaining customer relationships as a means of growing current customersâ€™ business through guidance in areas which include marketing, financial, product expertise, sales processes and general business operations.</li>
<li>Initiate training strategies to assist the Dispenser, Hearing Care Professional and Patient Care Coordinator to help grow their business.</li>
<li>Successfully introduce new products to customers.</li>
<li>Develop budget benchmarks for advertising allocations, and provide guidance on marketing plans as needed. Work towards helping each dispenser achieve a consistent marketing strategy.</li>
<li>Identify potential new store opening opportunities through market predictions.</li>
<li>For new dispensers, plan and execute a 12 to 18-month ramp-up plan that coordinates internal resources, Sales, Marketing and Training (product and practice management).</li>
<li>Provide clear and concise reports to management, using the appropriate channels, in a timely manner. Provide immediate reporting on problematic issues with solutions.</li>
<li>Work closely with the finance team to determine AR goals and a payment plan strategy for high risk accounts.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Qualifications:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Four-year college degree. Business or Audiology degree preferred</li>
<li>Minimum of three-to-five years of experience in front-line sales and sales management</li>
<li>No direct employees</li>
<li>60-80% travel</li>
<li>Proficient in MS Office programs</li>
<li>Must be able to handle various working environments (office, field, travel)</li>
<li>Convenient access to major airport is desirable for extensive travel</li>
</ul>
