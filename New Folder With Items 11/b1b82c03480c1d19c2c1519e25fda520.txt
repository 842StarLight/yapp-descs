<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
JOB PURPOSE:</b></h3>
<div>
Responsible for managing the day-to-day paid digital marketing efforts, driving campaign execution and optimization for digital performance campaigns in partnership with internal and external partners. Includes managing effective digital advertising plans focused on maximizing new patient acquisition as cost efficiently as possible. Works with CRM to support direct to consumer marketing efforts and works with Media to drive the paid digital marketing efforts across an integrated media plan.</div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b><br>
</b></h3>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
GENERAL DUTIES &amp; RESPONSIBILITIES:</b></h3>
<p></p>
<br>
<ul>
<li>
Consult and partner with key stakeholders on the development of digital marketing campaign strategies and test plans across paid search, retargeting, mobile ads, and digital video in support of a network of affiliated individual practices.</li>
<li>
Manage campaign development across all digital advertising tactics, including the overall search strategy, to ensure campaigns are successfully implemented, drive traffic to our website and the individual practice websites.</li>
<li>
Provide guidance and direction to third party agencies in the development of digital marketing plans to ensure optimization of and adherence to our budget and campaign performance across our digital marketing channels.</li>
<li>
Leverage digital technologies and best practices to drive campaign performance optimization.</li>
<li>
Track and analyze conversion rates of campaigns, prepare dashboards for marketing leadership highlighting performance and learnings, budget and ROI measurements.</li>
<li>
Drive the execution and approval of the digital channel strategy, priorities and goals to ensure cross-functional alignment and effective roll out of new initiatives; field questions and inquiries from Field Operations and solicit support from IT, as required, and obtain approvals from Practice Owners.</li>
</ul>
