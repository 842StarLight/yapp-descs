<h2>Sr. Marketing Manager (Demand Generation)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>TeamSupport is growing! We are looking for a hands-on Sr. Marketing Manager (Demand Generation) that can develop and execute campaigns to drive inbound marketing adoption among target prospects. Located in Dallas, TX this is a key role that will play a vital part to our company’s success as we continue to rapidly grow. We are looking for someone who is willing and ready to roll their sleeves up, think outside of the box, and demonstrate a “can do” approach to solving challenges.
</p>
<p>The Senior Demand Generation Manager is a vital position that will design and manage all inbound marketing activities. This position is responsible for getting customers excited about TeamSupport’s product and services. The primary goal of demand generation is to build and nurture key prospects and build a robust sales pipeline.</p>
<p>
Responsibilities:</p>
<ul>
<li>Research and acquire lists and lead sources to improve audience segmentation of target buyer profiles.</li>
<li>Create and execute integrated marketing campaigns to significantly increase inbound marketing results.
</li>
<li>Design compelling content for sales and marketing campaigns, aligning content and messaging with buyer interests.</li>
<li>Tightly monitor and reporting of web traffic, web site engagement, lead conversions and other metrics to ensure marketing qualified leads are moving down the pipeline funnel.</li>
<li>Work with sales and business development teams to ensure messaging and content is align with sales methodologies.</li>
<li>Utilize digital marketing as part of the overall demand generation strategy to increase web traffic.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Requirements</b></p>
<ul>
<li>5 years of B2B demand generation experience, preferable in SaaS-based software.
</li>
<li>Able to demonstrate the ability to execute and measure successful marketing campaigns.</li>
<li>Ability to work closely with sales, business development and operations to improve marketing processes.</li>
<li>Experience working with marketing and sales tech stacks including HubSpot, Salesforce, and ABM platforms.</li>
<li>Analytical mindset with the ability to track and report on campaign performance.</li>
<li>Creative mindset to drive new campaigns and call-to-actions.</li>
<li>Excellent marketing and communication skills.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Benefits</b></p>
<ul>
<li>100% Employer paid medical/dental coverage!</li>
<li>Vision, VTL, STD, LTD, 401K with company match.</li>
<li>Flexible time off policy.</li>
<li>Work on a highly sought after product.</li>
<li>Join a great, highly productive and fast paced team that will listen to you.</li>
<li>Be a name, not a number.</li>
<li>Flexible time off policy including vacation and holidays.</li>
</ul>
