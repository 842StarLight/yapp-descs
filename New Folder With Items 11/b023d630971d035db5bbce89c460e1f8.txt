<h2>Account Executive</h2>

<p><b>Who We Are: </b></p>
<p>Merlin is on a mission to revolutionize the way hourly talent finds work. We believe that everyone deserves a job they love and employers deserve to find the right employees for their businesses. We won’t rest until the whole world is happily employed!</p>
<p>Today, finding a job in the hourly talent market (2/3rds of the economy in the U.S.) is excruciatingly hard for both employers and candidates. 80% of positions are placed by word of mouth or dropping off a resume in person. When job needs change for businesses it can take months to fill a position with the right people.</p>
<p>Our groundbreaking mobile product is taking on that problem head-on. We help people in these industries find jobs faster and more effectively than any other platform out there, while also building fairness and transparency into the process.</p>
