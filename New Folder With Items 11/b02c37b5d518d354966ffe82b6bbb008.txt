<h2>Customer Account Specialist (Waconia, MN)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">This role will be the customer’s advocate and ensure high levels of retention for acpi. Partner with sales agencies to collaboratively address customer’s challenges, training needs, and opportunities. As the single point of contact, they will manage the customer’s complete order lifecycle from need recognition to referral, supporting a customer base of approximately $15-$18M in annual sales. Responsible for understanding and anticipating the needs of the customer and building a long term post-sales relationship.
<br><br>
RESPONSIBILITIES
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Communicate with customers via phone/email/chat and build the relationship through high levels of service. Responsible for listening for themes and communicate feedback trends to leadership and sales agencies.</li>
<li>Manage individual customer order activity, analyze account information by producing customer specific reports and determine the best treatment strategy necessary.</li>
<li>Deliver an exceptional end to end order entry experience, focusing on order accuracy and being responsive to escalation concerns that are generated from our customers.</li>
<li>Act as a product consultant, articulating technical product specifications, features and benefits and making recommendations based on customer needs both internally and externally.</li>
<li>Advocate for the customers success by anticipating challenges, removing barriers, and working cross-functionally to drive timely and accurate job completion by communicating schedules with designers, installers and end customers.</li>
<li>Provide technical understanding, support and promote 20/20, Pro-Kitchens and other design software; Decade Order Entry Tips &amp; Tricks; Customer Ordering Portals (Mercury and myacpicabinets.com).</li>
<li>Sustain process standards including accuracy and flexibility in an ever-changing environment in order to provide the highest service level.</li>
</ul>
EXPERIENCE REQUIREMENTS
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>2 year Associates Degree desired; 4 year College degree preferred.</li>
<li>3+ years’ work related experience in account management or customer service environment desired</li>
<li>Cabinet or related industry experience preferred</li>
</ul>
SKILLS REQUIREMENTS
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Strong Collaboration Skills</li>
<li>Coaching and mentoring skills</li>
<li>Proficient organizational skills enabling end-to-end account management to ensure profitability, as well as on-time complete orders.</li>
<li>Strong communication skills, including the ability to clearly, concisely communicate; both in writing as well as verbally while listening to interpret customer needs</li>
<li>Technical aptitude of product, industry, and related technologies</li>
<li>Ability to manage multiple opportunities simultaneously</li>
<li>Process improvement minded with a customer focus mindset</li>
<li>MSOffice Suite Proficient</li>
</ul>
