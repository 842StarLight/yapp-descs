<h2>Solutions Engineer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">As a Solutions Engineer at App Annie you will help drive the world's leading brands' mobile transformation. In conjunction with our world-class enterprise sales team, you will capitalize on App Annie's unique market position through your deep product knowledge and technical expertise. Additionally, through consultation with stakeholders throughout the client enterprise, you will work closely with our Field Sales team to ensure executive alignment on technical implementation through effective storytelling and product demonstration.
<br><br>
How will you be doing this?
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Partnering with Field Sales team to provide product consultation and technical expertise to fit client needs</li>
<li>Participating with the Revenue team to formulate qualification techniques, sales strategies and technical approaches for prospects</li>
<li>Evaluate clients' Data &amp; technology stacks for 1st and 3rd party integrations through Technical Discovery calls with relevant stakeholders, and recommend implementation options</li>
<li>Lead in advanced methodology, accuracy, and technology discussions with clients</li>
<li>Communicating effectively with customer stakeholders' across the enterprise through sales decks, proof-of-concepts, product demonstrations, and technical requirements of RFP process</li>
<li>Creating and maintaining a library of scripts and documentation that can be used across clients</li>
<li>Conveying customer feedback to Product Management team to ensure continuous product-market fit</li>
</ul>
What will you bring to the team?
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>5+ years in technical sales capacity</li>
<li>A Bachelor's degree in a technical field i.e. computer science, engineering or a related field</li>
<li>Experience in delivering client-focused solutions based on customer needs</li>
<li>A demonstrated ability to explain advanced technical concepts to stakeholders with a varying level of technical understanding</li>
<li>Previous project work with data APIs and JSON output as well as one of the following programming languages: Python, or Javascript (R, SQL, SPSS bonus)</li>
<li>Experience with enterprise customer SSO deployment</li>
<li>Excellent listening and presentation skills</li>
<li>Prior experience with mobile apps industry (preferred)</li>
</ul>
