<p>
With more than $2 billion in revenues, CDK Global is a leading global provider of integrated information technology and digital marketing solutions to the automotive retail and adjacent industries. Focused on enabling end-to-end automotive commerce, CDK provides solutions to dealers in more than 100 countries around the world, serving approximately 28,000 retail locations and most automotive manufacturers. CDK Global solutions automate and integrate critical processes from pre-sale targeted advertising to the sale, financing, insurance, parts supply, repair and maintenance of vehicles, with an increasing focus on utilizing data analytics and predictive intelligence.</p>

<p>
We’re large enough to make a difference but small enough for your voice to be heard. This means that we are an organization where every person matters. You can make an impact on the success of our business and that of our customers regardless of what career you decide to pursue.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
From data scientists to sales and client service experts, we’re hiring to support your growth and ours - Green light your career.</p>
<p>
As the liaison between the client and CDK Global, the <b>Associate Account Sales Executive</b> creates and maintains business relationships and increases incremental revenue to large CDK clients through face-to-face sales activities. Works in conjunction with channel partners to increase sales opportunities in CDK Strategic Products to grow CDK’s share of wallet in existing client base. Achieves annual assigned sales plan, working under guidance of the Director of Sales.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Position Responsibilities &amp; Essential functions
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Under guidance provided from Director of Sales, set clear, measurable performance goals to consistently achieve a sales quota:<ul>
<li>
Identify new opportunities with existing customers</li>
<li>
Grow and maintain existing accounts</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>
Generate significant revenue and profit growth utilizing a disciplined and process-oriented approach.</li>
<li>
Under the direction of Director of Sales, develop an overall sales plan and forecast short and long-term sales results</li>
<li>
Develop comprehensive understanding of specific CDK products and their functionality.</li>
<li>
Work with internal teams through the sales and implementation process.</li>
<li>
Gain an understanding of the industry segment and keep abreast of competitor developments in the market and/or territory.</li>
<li>
 Focus on consultative relationship with the client by developing an understanding of their unique business needs and working with the Director of Sales to bring forth integrated solutions.</li>
<li>
Escalate customer concerns/issues for a timely and appropriate resolution for the customer and company.</li>
<li>
Utilize Salesforce.com for maintaining client information and forecasting, leveraging the 9 steps of the “Triangle”.</li>
</ul>
