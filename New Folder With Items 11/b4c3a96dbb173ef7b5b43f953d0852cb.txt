<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Job Overview and Responsibilities</b></h3>
</div></div>
<div>
<div>Responsible for performing backup and support functions within distribution, import/export and customer forwarders to ensure continuity. In addition to performing specific core functions such as inventory allocations, this position will provide full backup capabilities to the Inventory Specialist, and will perform additional function related specific duties as necessary or as assigned. Performing audits and maintaining critical data will also be part of this role, as well as maintaining and updating functional procedures when appropriate or as assigned.</div>
<div>
</div>
<div><ul>
<li>
Provide backup support to maintain paperwork requirements and communications throughout the order to cash process</li>
<li>
Perform order allocation duties which support our order fulfillment and transportation performance.</li>
<li>
Provide ongoing support to the inventory accuracy efforts at the site, performing counts, driving cycle counts, supporting physical inventories and reporting data versus metrics.</li>
<li>
Establish and maintain improved filing system and audit for adherence.</li>
<li>
Provide support for sample shipment processing</li>
<li>
Adhere to all supply chain related ISO procedures and any applicable cross functional processes to ensure procedural excellence and alignment.</li>
<li>
Manage or participate in special projects as required; travel may be required</li>
<li>
Build and maintain good relationships with carriers, forwarders, and internal customers</li>
<li>
Perform all necessary training and ongoing monitoring of back up support to ensure stability.</li>
<li>
Learn back up responsibilities for allocating product to orders.</li>
<li>
Performing routine inventory transactions, processing of shop orders.</li>
<li>
Learn BAAN systems/ process and procedures related to each function, and obtain required regulatory training.</li>
</ul></div>
