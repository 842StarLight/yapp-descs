Order Replenishment Merchandiser<br>
<br>
Responsible for replenishing, and merchandising products at all designated accounts.<br>
<br>
Position Summary<br>
<br>
<ul><li>Designate location, build and maintain product displays and equipment placements in compliance with company standards.</li></ul>
<br>
<ul><li>Maintain appropriate product levels in beverage sections and throughout accounts including shelf allocation and cold equipment.</li></ul>
<br>
<ul><li>Manage backstock inventory levels and trade breakage in assigned accounts.</li></ul>
<br>
<ul><li>Meet customer service expectations through daily communication, problem resolution, and appropriate follow up</li></ul>
<br>
<ul><li>Maintains professional, team relationship with co-workers, customers, and shoppers.</li></ul>
<br>
<ul><li>Maintain appropriate inventory levels, company assets and point of sale, ensuring account meets Company merchandising standards.</li></ul>
<br>
<ul><li>Determine stores' product needs, place and transmit appropriate order in conjunction with existing geographic sales routes</li></ul>
<br>
<ul><li>Conduct store surveys.</li></ul>
<br>
<ul><li>Communicate account activities to appropriate parties.</li></ul>
<br>
<ul><li>Maintain Merchandising standards while rotating products each time in designated accounts.</li></ul>
<br>
<ul><li>Transport, replace and maintain Point of Sale advertising as appropriate for account.</li></ul>
<br>
<br>
<br>

<br>

<br>
<ul><li>Strong attention to detail and follow-up skills</li></ul>
<br>
<ul><li>Excellent planning and organization skills</li></ul>
<br>
<ul><li>Proficient computer application skills</li></ul>

<ul><li>1+ years CPG / Beverage / Grocery experience preferred<br>
