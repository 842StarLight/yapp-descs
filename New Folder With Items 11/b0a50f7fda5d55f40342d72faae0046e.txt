<h2>AP/AR Coordinator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Riley Power Inc., a division of Babcock Power Inc., is looking to hire an AP/AR Coordinator to join their Marlborough, MA Accounts Payable department. The team is responsible for up to eleven (11) legal entities; therefore the AP/AR Coordinator role is required to understand intercompany and multi-currency transactions, relocation, and sales tax rules.</p>
Essential Responsibilities:
<ul>
<li>Process vendor invoices for timely and accurate payment while ensuring approval policies, purchasing procedures and fraud prevention policies are followed.</li>
<li>Audit and process employee expense reports in electronic expense reporting system in compliance with the company travel policy.</li>
<li>Research and resolve. vendor invoice discrepancies and review statements.</li>
<li>Provide courteous and timely responses to vendor inquires and internal department requests.</li>
<li>Process and audit weekly payments including checks, wires and ACH payment methods</li>
<li>Interact and assist internal and external customers with inquiries, problem resolution and other informational requests while maintaining a positive relationship with all vendors.
</li>
<li>Expedite special payment requests and follow through on vendor issues.</li>
<li>Assist with Auditor requests relating to AP transactions</li>
<li>Take an active role with recommending and implementing process improvements and new strategies.</li>
</ul>Job Requirements / Skills, Knowledge and Abilities:
<ul>
<li>Experience: Two years of Accounting or related experience required.</li>
<li>Ability to work in a fast paced environment, with the ability to multitask and meet deadlines.</li>
<li>Must be a team oriented, problem solver.</li>
<li>Strong communications skills, both verbal and written, are essential.</li>
<li>Proven technical accounting aptitude and skills are required.</li>
<li>Strong attention to detail and solid analytical skills a must.</li>
<li>Proficiency with computerized accounting software (Deltek Costpoint preferred, not required) as well as an electronic expense reporting system and invoice scanning system.</li>
<li>Ability to collaborate with internal and external teams.</li>
</ul>
