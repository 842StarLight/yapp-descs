<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
What we're looking for:</b></h2>
<p></p>
<p><b>
What we’re looking for</b></p>
<p>
You are a Networking Sales Specialist with 5+ years of direct sales experience and extensive knowledge of Networking Solution selling. You are an enthusiastic, aggressive individual who enjoys working in a dynamic, fast-paced environment to join our Field Sales team as a Networking Sales Specialist mostly in Phoenix and some Los Angles Metro market.</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Position Overview</b></p>
<p>
In this role you will be responsible for developing and growing the market in your defined territory. You will be an essential element of our sales organization by planning, building, enabling and accelerating the sales engine for our application Networking product sets (Citrix ADC, Citrix SD WAN, Secured Unified Gateway, Management &amp; Analytics, and security solutions).</p>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>
Acting as the key field representative to develop and drive sales pipeline for our Networking Solutions.</li>
<li>
Managing forecast, pipeline, and plan for achieving quarterly/annual quota</li>
<li>
Driving Citrix’s Networking infrastructure solutions into leading Enterprise businesses</li>
<li>
Providing regular communication with Sales and other teams to augment efforts and share support</li>
<li>
Maintaining knowledge of competitors in accounts to strategically position Citrix Networking solutions better</li>
<li>
Use specialty expertise to grow new opportunities</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>
 Education &amp; Experience Required:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Bachelor’s degree or equivalent experience preferred</li>
<li>
5+ years of experience in direct sales</li>
<li>
Previous experience in selling Networking solutions</li>
<li>
Track record of meeting/exceeding quota</li>
<li>
Hands-on experience in closing complex deals in new markets</li>
<li>
Extensive executive contacts within territory</li>
<li>
Travel as required</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p>
Keywords: Networking, ADC, Routing and Switching, Network security, account manager, account executive, sales, SDN, SD-WAN, WAN Optimization</p>
