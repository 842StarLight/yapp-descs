<h2>Digital Campaign Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">Townsquare is looking for a Digital Campaign Manager [DCM]. This role acts as the technical hub that connects sales and our centralized buying team. This is a collaborative role that assists sales in campaign structure, buying in campaign launching and acts as the liaison between our client services team and our local markets. This position reports to the Regional Director of Campaign Management and is ultimately responsible for the design, implementation, optimization and reporting of digital campaigns. Success in this role comes in the form of accelerated sales and reduced campaign cancellations.
<br><br>
Responsibilities
<br><br>
<b>Strategy:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Coach &amp; develop sales professionals on our product capabilities</li>
<li>Oversee technical approval of proposals being built by our sales strategists</li>
<li>Give insight and suggestions sales for ongoing campaigns based on feedback from our buyers</li>
</ul>
<b>Campaign Pre-Launch:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Coaches sales on crafting quality messaging for various tactic (social, SEM, programmatic, etc.)</li>
<li>Assigns targeting segments based on experience and overall tactic mix</li>
<li>Delegates to sales tasks to be completed in order to fully prepare a new client campaign for launch (creative, pixels, KPIs)</li>
<li>Responsible for compiling all elements of the insertion order and scheduling campaign to be trafficked.</li>
</ul>
<b>Campaign Post-Launch:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Primary point of contact for local markets you support to our centralized buying, client services and analytics teams.</li>
<li>Support local market as client optimizations, revisions, upsells and renewals come in from our post sale teams.</li>
<li>Continually refine and improve continuity between all work groups.</li>
</ul>
<b>Qualifications:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Manages time; focuses on urgent and important tasks; avoids procrastination; follows up; shows strong attention to detail; meets deadlines</li>
<li>Exceptional Communicator. Experience taking the lead in interdepartmental projects.</li>
<li>Familiar with demand side platforms (DSP), Asana, Facebook Business Manager, Google Ads, Google Analytics or demonstrates ability to learn new systems quickly</li>
<li>Experience with digital ad messaging best practices (does not actually build the ads)</li>
<li>Actively listens; clearly and effectively conveys information; uses professional telephone skills; demonstrates effective business writing skills; shows excellent grasp of grammar</li>
<li>Exhibits good interpersonal skills; collaborates with others; maintains composure when faced with difficult situations and personalities</li>
<li>3+ years' experience in digital advertising</li>
<li>4 year college degree or comparable work experience required</li>
</ul>
