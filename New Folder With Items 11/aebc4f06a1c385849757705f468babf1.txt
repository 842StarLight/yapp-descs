<h2>Motorcycle Sales Associate</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>We are currently looking for a responsible and motivated MOTORCYCLE SALESPERSON to help support our Sales Department due to the increased volume of sales. The ideal candidate must have a strong desire to succeed and a healthy love for all things Motorsports. Applicants must have exceptional customer service and previous sales experience.</p>
<p>***Bi-lingual in English and Spanish is a plus but not mandatory.</p>
<p>This position is Full-Time 40 hours a week Tuesday through Saturday 9:00am to 6:00pm with an hour lunch each day but can also be part time. Starting pay for the ideal applicant is $18.00 per hour, plus commission. Commission is based off each sales deals margin of profit and warranty sales.</p>
<p>Responsibilities include:</p>
<ul><li>Spending time with customers to determine their needs and discuss vehicle options.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Commit to becoming an auto sales expert and gain in-depth knowledge of all vehicles and technology.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Complete quotes and explain financing options.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Follow up with prospective customers and return email / voicemail.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Support on-line customers.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Follow up with existing customers to confirm their satisfaction and generate leads.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Help complete sales transactions ensuring all paperwork is complete and correct for every sales deal.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Help complete DMV paperwork for every sales deal.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Run credit applications, communicate with multiple financial bank institutions, and explain all financial options to customers including interest rates, terms, and monthly payments.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Complete finance contracts, gather all documents necessary for funding and send them to the financial bank institutions and then track and audit funding received.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Keep aware of the manufacturer's current products, programs and promotional novelties and/or those of the banking institutions.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Help sell and handle warranty contracts to customers.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Pay off units to flooring companies when needed.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Help warranty register sold units.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Cooperates in vehicle exchanges between dealers.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Monitor and maintain inventory.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Approves transactions, at the request of his/her supervisor.</li></ul>
<ul><li>On request, helps the sales manager perform administrative tasks (advertising, inventory...).</li></ul>
<ul><li>Participates in planning and managing the display of vehicles in the show room and parking area.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Assist the Sales Manager in planning and implementing strategies to attract customers.</li></ul>
<p>Requirements Include:</p>
<ul><li>A motorcycle enthusiast. I.e., dirt bikes, ATVs, sidexsides, or sport/cruiser bikes.</li></ul>
<p>- Prior experience working with customers and providing an exceptional experience.</p>
<ul><li>Sales including inside sales, outside sales, or retail sales is a plus.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Steady work history and the commitment to starting a new career.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Outgoing personality with expertise at developing relationships (i.e., a "people person")</li></ul>
<ul><li>Basic MS Office knowledge; computer software and internet proficiency.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Excellent appearance, verbal/written communication, strong negotiation and presentation skills.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Must possess the ability to ask for the sale and follow through.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Valid U.S. Driver's license.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Finance and Insurance experience or at least a familiarity with financial and customer service principles a plus.</li></ul>
