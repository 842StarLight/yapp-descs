<h2>Mgr-Member Service Center</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>This position is responsible for managing the day-to-day operations of the Member Service Center and delivering outstanding service to our Members while also delivering outstanding internal service to fellow employees. The Manager of the Member Service Center is directly responsible for the Member experience through the Credit Union's alternate delivery channels, which includes phone, web and e-mail. This may also include consumer loan production through a team of Member Service Specialists, Underwriters and third-party business partners.
