<h2>Sr Product Development Mgr, Absence Mgmt</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>

</div>
<div>
<b>
Description</b><div>
</div>
<div><b>
Position Objective</b></div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>As our Senior Product Development Manager, Absence Management, you will serve as our strategic product lead, demonstrating accountability for product scope and the success of the product by ensuring that the development teams are building the right things in the right order to optimize business value and customer satisfaction. You will effectively drive product direction within Guardian &amp; ReedGroup’s product portfolio working across organizational lines to create a positive outcome for the program.</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div><b>Your Responsibilities</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Develop products by identifying potential products; conducting market research; generating product requirements; determining specifications, production timetables, pricing, and time-integrated plans for product introduction, and developing marketing strategies.</li>
<li>
Determine customers' needs and desires by specifying the research needed to obtain market information.</li>
<li>
Recommend the nature and scope of present and future product lines by reviewing product specifications and requirements and appraising new product ideas and/or product or packaging changes.</li>
<li>
Review epics, features and backlog to ensure all requirements are captured.</li>
<li>
Manage backlog grooming and prioritization based on customer needs.</li>
<li>
Accountable for a successful launch for MVP and future product and program deliverables.
</li>
<li>Assess market competition by comparing the company's product to competitors' products.</li>
<li>
Provide source data for product line communications by defining product marketing communication objectives.</li>
<li>
Bring new products to market by analyzing proposed product requirements and product development programs, preparing return-on-investment analyses and establishing time schedules with engineering and manufacturing.</li>
<li>
Serve as the voice of the market and point of contact for sales and customers.</li>
<li>
Focus and strong emphasis on customer experience within every customer deliverable.</li>
<li>
Maintain and ensure 100% visibility of current product prioritization and future plans (road maps and backlog).</li>
<li>
Provide product/marketplace direction throughout the project life cycle.</li>
<li>
Accountable for marketing collateral and documentation for current and ongoing functionality and appropriate distribution.</li>
<li>
Accountable for and participate in user acceptance testing to test product features and functionality.</li>
<li>
Ensure legal and regulatory compliance on all offerings.</li>
<li>
Mentor, train, and guide the organization’s teams as they learn, and then adopt, best practices and lessons learned from product.</li>
<li>
Maintain professional and technical knowledge by attending educational workshops, reviewing professional publications, establishing personal networks and participating in professional societies.</li>
<li>
Support our cross functional team as a resource and advocate the usage of their support.</li>
</ul>
<div><b>Reporting Relationships</b></div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>As our Senior Product Development Manager, Absence Management, you will report to our Assistant Vice President, Absence Product Development for Guardian &amp; ReedGroup, who reports to the Chief Revenue Officer at ReedGroup.</div>
<div>

<b>
Qualifications</b><div>
</div>
<div>
<div><b>
Your Functional Skills</b></div>
<ul>
<li>You are comfortable with ambiguity in strategic direction. Priorities will change as current uncertainties become new standards and shape the market, and you will balance between proactive and reactive strategy and execution.</li>
<li>
You work well in a collaborative and diverse team environment, understanding and employing the approaches and standard operating procedures of all functional areas across Guardian and ReedGroup.
</li>
<li>The success of the Absence Strategy team depends heavily upon building strong partnerships with all areas of the organization. You will work with and influence peer groups and individuals with specific functional expertise, building consensus on many issues through persuasive presentation of well-developed, rational business cases, complete with supporting information, impact analysis, and success metrics. A key challenge and opportunity lies in the ability to influence and manage people over whom you have no direct authority.</li>
</ul>
<div><b>Your Qualifications</b></div>
<ul>
<li>BA/BS or equivalent professional work experience.</li>
<li>
7+ years product management experience, preferably working on COTS (Commercial Off the Shelf) products and an understanding of the product management life cycle.</li>
<li>
Experience in the following areas is a plus: product management, people management, understanding the customer, product development, requirements analysis, pricing, planning, competitive analysis, sales planning, inventory control, financial planning and strategy.</li>
<li>
Experience working in a SAFe Agile software development environment.</li>
<li>
Strategic and creative thinker with the ability to think outside the box and translate strategies into actions.</li>
<li>
Excellent problem-solving skills.</li>
<li>
Team player with proven ability to foster and manage working relationships within a matrix environment.</li>
<li>
Ability to juggle multiple priorities.</li>
<li>
Excellent oral and written communication skills.</li>
<li>
Strong influencing and presentation skills.</li>
<li>
Strong process management skills and experience with Agile toolset (JIRA, Confluence, Rally, VersionOne).</li>
</ul>
