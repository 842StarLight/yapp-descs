<div><b><i>Customer Call Center Representative - Evenings - FT &amp; PT</i></b></div>
<div></div>
<div><b><i>FT Evening shift position is Monday - Thursday 12:00 pm - 8:00 pm; Friday 9:00 am - 5:00 pm</i></b></div>
<div></div>
<div><b><i>PT position hours - Monday - Thursday 4:00 pm - 8:00 pm; Friday 1:00 pm - 5:00 pm.</i></b></div>
<div></div>
<div><b>No Cold Calling, No Telemarketing</b></div>
<div></div>
<div>
<b><i>Ohio Basement Systems, </i></b>a regional leader in the foundation repair industry, is seeking positive, energetic individuals to join its team of Customer Service Representatives. <b>Fast paced, small office, working with a team of 6 other members. The role of the Customer Call Center Representative is to schedule sales and maintenance appointments for our salespeople and service technicians.</b>
</div>
<div></div>
<div><b>Job Duties</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Proactively represent our company primarily through telephone contact by answering calls, setting service and sales appointment, targeting inactive accounts in order to generate new sales opportunities, and following up with customers on various issues.</li>
<li>Keep customer records and database current</li>
<li>Call customers after the sales appointment and/or production installation to check on quality and satisfaction.</li>
<li>Provide customers with a remarkable experience.</li>
</ul>
<div><b>Job Requirements</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Demonstrable experience in telephone sales or related field; excellent interpersonal skills and organization skills</li>
<li>Customer service focused - understands the need, solves problems, and responds in a timely and efficient manner</li>
<li>Self-motivated and energetic</li>
<li>Ability to make a large number of calls to prospective clients per day and even with rejection by the customer, have the persistence and positive attitude to continue in these efforts every day.</li>
<li>Proficiency in Microsoft Office computer applications and ability to navigate internal software programs; proficient in Google Maps</li>
<li>Competitive wages and the opportunity for <b>bonuses</b>
</li>
<li>401k (for the FT position)</li>
<li>Health Insurance, dental, vision, life insurance and much more (for the FT position)</li>
<li>PTO (for the FT position)</li>
</ul>
</div>
