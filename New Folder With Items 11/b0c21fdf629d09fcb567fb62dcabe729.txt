<h2>Payroll and Accounts Payable Accountant</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>JOB TITLE: </b> Payroll and Accounts Payable Accountant</p>
<p><b>POSITION STATUS: </b> 20 hours per week. Salaried, non-exempt, year-long position.</p>
<p><b>REPORTS TO: </b> COO/CFO.</p>
<p><b>SALARY: </b> Commensurate with experience</p>
<p><b>ABOUT FASPS: </b> The French American School of Puget Sound (FASPS) is accredited by the French government and the Northwest Association of Independent Schools (NWAIS) and offers a rigorous bilingual program in French and English to students from preschool (starting at age 2) through eighth grade.</p>
<ul>
<li>OUR MISSION: We challenge students to excel academically and thrive in French, American and international cultures.</li>
<li>OUR VISION: To inspire the next generation of global citizens to learn, understand, and act wisely in a multicultural world.</li>
<li>OUR CORE VALUES: The core values that guide our work and shape our students’ educational experience are Excellence, Integrity, Cultural Agility, and Community.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>POSITION SUMMARY: </b> We are looking for an additional member of the Business Office team who can bring a strong professional background to handle accounts payable and payroll responsibilities.</p>
<p><b>RESPONSIBILITIES: </b></p>
<p>Accounts Payable</p>
<ul>
<li>Review and enter invoices, including employee expense reimbursements.</li>
<li>Review and code A/P invoices for a weekly check run</li>
<li>Coordinate check signing based on signed authority and send out checks in timely manner</li>
<li>Reviews and verifies all purchase orders for coding, documentation, and sales tax postings.</li>
<li>Insure that Accounts Payable internal controls and GAAP are being followed.</li>
<li>Processes vendor setup, maintain vendor records,</li>
<li>Verify W-9's are on file and process 1099’s</li>
<li>Calculate USE tax for the State</li>
<li>Fields accounts payable inquiries, maintains accounts payable records, and performs role specific filing and copying.</li>
<li>Continuously improve payment processes</li>
</ul>
<p>Payroll</p>
<ul>
<li>Prepare and transmit monthly payroll for approximately 90 employees</li>
<li>Process complex timesheets with multiple allocations and pay rates.</li>
<li>Research and resolve payroll and timesheet related issues.</li>
<li>Post and reconcile payroll transactions in the general ledger, including employee taxes and benefits.</li>
<li>Prepare payroll reports required by Federal, State and other agencies.</li>
<li>Compile payroll data such as garnishments, vacation time, insurance and 403(b) deductions.</li>
<li>Contact various supervisors for any missed times.</li>
<li>Compile internal management reports from payroll system software.</li>
<li>Completes ad hoc payroll related items such as, stop payments, check replacements, reimbursements, rush and termination checks.</li>
<li>Generates necessary payroll reports, handles routine payroll questions/issues, assists with maintaining employee payroll records and setup.</li>
<li>Process accurate and timely year-end reporting when necessary (W-2, W-2c, etc.)</li>
</ul>
<p>Other</p>
<ul>
<li>Conduct appropriate reviews and reconciliations to ensure data integrity.</li>
<li>Assists with monthly closings and accounting requests.</li>
<li>Assist in annual audit and other regularly required audits.</li>
<li>Perform accounting and clerical functions to support Business Office</li>
</ul>
<p><b>REQUIREMENTS / QUALIFICATIONS: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>A minimum of 3 years of general accounting, accounts payable and payroll experience</li>
<li>Bachelor's degree in accounting or related field of study</li>
</ul>
<p><b>REQUIRED SKILLS: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Strong computer skills and thorough understanding of accounting and financial principles</li>
<li>Demonstrated proficiency in MS Office applications (Word, Excel, Outlook)</li>
<li>Detail-oriented and highly organized, with a high degree of personal integrity and mature judgment.</li>
<li>Demonstrated data management skills, including the ability to enter data accurately, produce reports, and create Excel spreadsheets.</li>
<li>Ability to work on multiple and concurrent tasks with constant interruptions in a busy office environment.</li>
<li>Excellent time management skills and the ability to prioritize multiple tasks in order to meet deadlines.</li>
<li>Ability to work independently with minimal supervision.</li>
<li>Ability to effectively communicate with all levels of management, staff, parents, and vendors</li>
<li>Respect the confidentiality of private information relating to all members of the school community.</li>
<li>Acceptance and respect for cultural diversity in all its forms, including ethnicity, sexual orientation, abilities, and religious backgrounds.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>PREFERRED SKILLS: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Experience with QuickBooks accounting system and ADP payroll</li>
<li>Requires excellent organizational, analytical, and problem-solving skills as well as excellent verbal and written communication skills.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>PHYSICAL REQUIREMENTS AND WORK ENVIRONMENT: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Ability to lift up to 20 pounds.</li>
<li>Ability to work at a desk and computer for extended periods of time.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>BENEFITS AND COMPENSATION: </b> Competitive compensation commensurate with experience, paid vacation and personal days. As a part time position this position does not include other benefits.</p>
<p><b>SCHEDULE: </b> Work hours are 20 hours per week, schedule to be determined in conjunction with the Business Office team.</p>
