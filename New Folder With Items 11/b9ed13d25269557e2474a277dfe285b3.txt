Job Description
<br>---------------
<br><br>
Vlocity, Inc. is recruiting a Senior Demo Engineer - Vlocity Tools to help build Vlocity product demonstration capabilities for Vlocity Solution Consultants. The Senior Demo Engineer - Vlocity Tools will be responsible for building an array of capabilities to deliver repeatable, reliable and reusable demo to our Solution Consultants.
<br><br>
This role will be part of the Demo Engineering Team. We work within the broader Vlocity Solution Consulting organization and our goal is to provide a trusted single source of truth regarding the latest demo environments – so that our Solution Consultants can confidently demo the latest, stable product capabilities to customers. This role will report to the VP Demo Engineering, and will be part of Vlocity's first dedicated Demo Engineering team – helping Solution Consultants deliver faster and more accurate demos – directly contributing to the hyper-growth of our company.
<br><br>
Prior experience working with Salesforce.com and Vlocity is preferred, along with an industry expertise in our target verticals: Communications, Media, Utilities, Health Insurance, Insurance &amp; Public Sector.
<br><br>
.Responsibilities
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Collaborate with product teams (Communications, Media, Utilities, Health Insurance, Insurance &amp; Public Sector) and Solution Consultants to understand demo requirements</li>
<li>Ensure demo requirements are accurately represented in the Demo Engineering org</li>
<li>Take initiative to ensure that the Demo Engineering org is pro-actively kept up to date; apply automation where possible; reuse existing assets where appropriate – build new assets where required</li>
<li>Bias to making those around you successful and are comfortable working as a team player</li>
<li>Contribute to building a positive, can-do, high-performance culture</li>
</ul>
Experience
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>5+ years of enterprise consulting experience spanning large organizations and enterprise platforms such as Vlocity, Salesforce.com, Oracle, Siebel, Microsoft, Amdocs, Netcracker, etc.</li>
<li>Proven ability to analyze, and design business processes via technology and integration</li>
<li>Experienced at guiding colleagues toward best practices in building a repeatable, reliable, and reusable demo capabilities for our Solution Consultants – that drive faster time-to-value (faster demo capabilities, faster close of business, etc.)</li>
<li>Excellent communication skills, both written and verbal</li>
<li>Industry domain experience in Communications, Media, Utilities, Health Insurance, Insurance or Public Sector is preferred, but not required</li>
<li>Experience in small, agile sales teams and must enjoy the 'small company' challenge</li>
<li>Experience configuring and demonstrating the Salesforce.com Sales or Service Cloud Apps, or a similar leading CRM application is preferred</li>
<li>Experience and desire to work within a fast-paced, iterative sales and development environment</li>
</ul>
Technical Skills
