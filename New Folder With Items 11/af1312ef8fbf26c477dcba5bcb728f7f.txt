<h2>Client Partnerships Associate</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div></div>
<div>
<div>
<div>Do you want to work in a fun, smart, and energetic environment where you will be challenged, rewarded AND inspired? Come work with top performers at CJ Affiliate to make a real difference in how top brands run their businesses. We are the world’s leading affiliate marketing network - we transform digital marketing with the help of big data and business intelligence. We help advertisers drive more sales and expand their reach … and we want to hear from you!<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<div>
Client Partnerships Associates play a key role in support of CJ's advertisers. As part of an account team, the Client Partnerships Associate is responsible for providing tactical support in implementing digital marketing strategies and campaigns.</div>
<br>
<div>
</div>
<div><b>Do these things interest you? You will:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Help advertisers maximize the effectiveness and efficiency of their affiliate spend by building and interpreting reports that capture program performance</li>
<li>Build creative newsletters to inform publishers of updates, brand guidelines, performance objectives, etc.</li>
<li>Identify, recruit and review new publishers who can provide additional reach and revenue growth</li>
<li>Work with advertisers to troubleshoot complex data transfer issues, build new program terms, as well as ensure details are accurate and up-to-date</li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<div><b>What we look for:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>You have a Bachelor's Degree</li>
<li>You are passionate about providing excellent service to internal and external customers</li>
<li>You are driven to succeed</li>
<li>You are a clear and effective communicator</li>
<li>You are able to work independently and collaborate with your team to solve complex issues</li>
<li>You are proficient in Microsoft Office, especially Excel</li>
<li>You have strong analytical skills</li>
</ul>
