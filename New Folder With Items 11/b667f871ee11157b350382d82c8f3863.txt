<h2>International Account Coordinator (Family Focused Culture)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Summary</b>: The International Account Coordinator provides the Manager of International Sales with a high level of support and assists in all activities related to the planning, development and execution of CCA and B’s international sales functions. He/she supports the Manager of International Sales in meeting customer’s needs daily. The International Account Coordinator engages in activities to support and identify new international partners through the IMVSP process, entering PO’s and shipping information, managing the ship calendar, samples and administrative functions. This position works closely with the Manager of International Sales and other departments across the company, especially Retail Marketing, to execute international sales strategies in line with company vision, values and objectives.</p>
<p>
The International Coordinator has excellent communication and organization skills and communicates with a variety of internal stakeholders while creating a positive in-office partnership with the Retail Marketing Manager as well as all internal stakeholders. In addition, he/she has regular contact with international partners and distributors while working with a high level of autonomy.</p>
<p><b>
Essential Duties and Responsibilities</b>: “Essential functions” are primary job duties that incumbents must be able to perform unassisted or with some reasonable accommodation made by the employer.</p>
<ul>
<li>
Enter/update forecast and sales projections in Microsoft Navision (NAV) per market and product;</li>
<li>
Enter distributors PO’s in NAV and liaise with distributors and Director of Logistics to ensure they are processed;</li>
<li>
Use internal systems to set up customers, enter countries of sale and complete auto-generated tasks assigned to International Sales;</li>
<li>
Work closely with Supply Chain to create the international shipment calendar and maintain distributors’ order/shipping expectations;</li>
<li>
Use available databases to contact potential distributors in markets identified in International Strategic development plan;</li>
<li>
Send out samples to all external sales partners for international shows, pitches, retailer needs;</li>
<li>
Respond to distributors inquiries promptly to provide our partners a high level of support. Liaise with internal departments to ensure accuracy of the information provided to distributors;</li>
<li>
Assists International Account Manager in various projects: International Seminar, yearly market business plans, distributor/market timelines per quarter;</li>
<li>
Collaborate closely with International Account Manager on updating and managing international product assortment per market;</li>
<li>
Coordinate with Travel Coordinator and distributors in regards to the International Account Manager’s travel plans and general schedule;</li>
<li>
Create a positive in-office partnership with retail marketing, consumer marketing, supply chain, product development, legal, IT, accounting, and other internal stakeholders;</li>
<li>
Represent the International Account Manager in meetings when the Account Manager is traveling;</li>
<li>
Collect and manage POS reports from each market; Analyze data with International Account Manager throughout Q4</li>
<li>
Work with International Account Manager on special projects to develop and implement processes and procedures to improve operational efficiencies;</li>
<li>
Notify the International Account Manager of any potential distributor issues that arise;</li>
<li>
Collaborates with other departments including but not limited to operations, sales, customer compliance, legal and new product development; and</li>
<li>
Protect company assets through the proper management of asset files and adherence to company policy with regard to information security and management.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Knowledge, Skills and Abilities Required</b>:</p>
<ul>
<li>
One to two years of administrative experience required; International Sales/account management experience preferred;</li>
<li>
Bi-lingual in French, Spanish, German, Mandarin, Japanese or Italian preferred;</li>
<li>
Intermediate to advance proficiency in all Microsoft Office software; Experience with Microsoft Navision is preferred;</li>
<li>
Excellent oral and written communication skills; adept at building strong relationships;</li>
<li>
Ability to work well with distributors at all levels as well as colleagues throughout the organization;</li>
<li>
High degree of business acumen;</li>
<li>
Team oriented, strong team building skills, ability to self-identify and resolve conflicts as needed;</li>
<li>
Capable of working with a high degree of autonomy;</li>
<li>
High sense of urgency, ability to handle competing priorities in a deadline-driven, dynamic environment;</li>
<li>
Demonstrates strong willingness to learn with a high level of flexibility and adaptability;</li>
<li>
Ability to utilize active listening skills and follow directions;</li>
<li>
Ability to compile and analyze data; and</li>
<li>
Demonstrates initiative and strong work ethic.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Education</b>:</p>
<ul><li>
Bachelor’s degree in Business preferred, International Business, Logistics, Marketing or a related field required. Minimum 1-2 years in supporting Account Management.</li></ul>
<p><b>
Working Conditions</b>:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Normal office environment with little exposure to excessive noise, dust, temperature and the like.</li>
<li>
Position requires sitting for extended periods of time, standing, visual acumen, and manual dexterity for working with computer key boards.</li>
<li>
Position requires occasional after-hours and weekend work to meet project requirements.</li>
<li>
Position requires ability to travel up to 10% of the time to trade show and event destinations and key accounts both within the continental United States and internationally.</li>
</ul>
