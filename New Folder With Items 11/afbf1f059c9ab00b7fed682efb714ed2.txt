Your Challenge
<br><br>
This position reports directly to our Project Services Manager, and requires a self-starter with proven skills in desktop publishing for a highly technical audience. Primary duty is to research, write, publish, and maintain service and operation manuals for custom automation systems. May also create or edit service bulletins or related information for existing systems in the field. This position requires strong computer skills, the ability to read and understand engineering-level documents and machine drawings, and a faultless command of the English language.
<br><br>
Your Responsibilities
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Work closely with our design and engineering team to research and document the operation and service requirements for custom-built automation machinery</li>
<li>Work within our Microsoft Word template to integrate text, images, and engineering drawings into professional-quality documents</li>
<li>Verify accuracy, completeness, and formatting of documents for both internal and external audiences</li>
<li>Research and create safety documentation</li>
<li>Maintain Microsoft Word documentation templates to client and DWFritz formats</li>
<li>Assist sales and marketing departments with creating and editing technical brochures</li>
<li>Photography and video experience a plus</li>
<li>Experience with SolidWorks CAD software a plus</li>
</ul>
Your Qualifications
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Proficient with Microsoft Windows-based PC’s and related Office applications</li>
<li>Demonstrated skills in MS Word, Excel, and computer graphics software</li>
<li>Proficient with Adobe Creative Suite (or equivalent)</li>
<li>Strong understanding of Microsoft Word as used in desktop publishing</li>
<li>Ability to edit digital images as needed for integration into MSWord documents</li>
<li>Ability to maintain confidentiality</li>
<li>Read, speak, and understand English language</li>
<li>Author comprehensive technical procedures in audience-appropriate language</li>
<li>Ability to work under pressure and manage multiple project deadlines</li>
<li>Physical capabilities include using a computer workstation for extended periods</li>
</ul>
