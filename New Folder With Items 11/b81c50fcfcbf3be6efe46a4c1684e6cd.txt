<h2>Solar Sales Engineer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p>The Solar Sales Engineer is responsible for the electrical design of all commercial and utility scale systems. We are looking for an individual who can manage the day to day tasks as well as continue to expand the department’s capabilities as the company grows. The Engineer is responsible for ensuring that the system designs meet our customers’ needs in the most cost effective way through a thorough understanding of equipment available on the market and deep understanding of system design principles. The Engineer will set electrical design standards and work with a team of engineers taking solar project designs from preliminary analysis through full construction drawing as well as supporting permitting, construction, and project close-out processes.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
Duties and Responsibilities</p>
<ul>
<li>Perform generation analysis and system optimization with solar design software</li>
<li>Create PV Solar System designs and professional quality renderings for inclusion in proposals</li>
<li>Perform detailed cost-benefit analysis of available products, installation options and new technologies</li>
<li>Audit sites as needed for PV solar installation suitability, cost considerations, environmental limitations, structural limitations, etc.</li>
<li>Contribute professional quality proposal content for technically complex, multi-site PV solar installations</li>
<li>Work on assignments requiring considerable judgment and initiative. Understand implications of work and make recommendations for solutions</li>
<li>Build productive internal and external working relationships</li>
<li>Assist with cost estimation and competitive pricing for commercial PV solar projects</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p>
</p>
<p>Competencies and Qualifications</p>
<ul>
<li>1+ years solar design experience</li>
<li>1+ years of Commercial, Enterprise or Government sales engineering/designing or technical sales experience preferred</li>
<li>General understanding of construction</li>
<li>Proficient in AutoCAD, Sketchup or similar image manipulation program</li>
<li>Knowledge and correct usage of the National Electric Code</li>
<li>Excellent communication skills and the ability to interact in a team environment</li>
<li>Passion and know-how around new energy, energy technology and projects is a must.</li>
<li>BA/BS degree (preferably in engineering)</li>
<li>NABCEP PV Installation Professional (preferred)</li>
<br>
</ul>
