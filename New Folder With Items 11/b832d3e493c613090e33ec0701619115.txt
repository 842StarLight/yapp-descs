<p><b>About Bentley Systems</b></p>
<p>
Bentley Systems is the leading global provider of software solutions to engineers, architects, geospatial professionals, constructors, and owner-operators for the design, construction, and operations of infrastructure. Bentley’s MicroStation-based engineering and BIM applications, and its digital twin cloud services, advance the project delivery (ProjectWise) and the asset performance (AssetWise) of transportation and other public works, utilities, industrial and resources plants, and commercial and institutional facilities.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
Bentley Systems employs more than 3,500 colleagues, generates annual revenues of $700 million in 170 countries, and has invested more than $1 billion in research, development, and acquisitions since 2014. From inception in 1984, the company has remained majority-owned by its five founding Bentley brothers. Bentley shares transact by invitation on the NASDAQ Private Market.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Day-to-Day Responsibilities:</b></p>
<p>
A Senior Success Manager will partner with our accounts to deliver predictable outcomes and experiences for their clients and predictable growth for their business by:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Delivering and communicating ROI for our accounts business’s, throughout the customer lifecycle</li>
<li>
Being the trusted advisor for the customer on use-case and product functionality</li>
<li>
Quarterbacking experiences by various cross-functional teams at Bentley, on behalf of the customer</li>
<li>
Identify opportunities for customers to act as Bentley advocates (e.g. testimonials, case studies)</li>
<li>
Collaborate closely with Sales to support pilot customers, renewals, and expansion opportunities</li>
<li>
Marshall resources across the Bentley organization as needed to support accounts needs</li>
<li>
Represent the voice of the customer to inform our sales process and product roadmap</li>
<li>
Maintain a cadence of communicating with accounts about their adoption trends, sentiment, and mining opportunities for deeper engagement by providing a valid point of view.</li>
<li>
Host Monthly and Quarterly business reviews. Agree schedule for Executive Business Reviews</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
What You Bring To The Team:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
BA/BS (MBA/Master preferred)</li>
<li>
Ideally 4+ years of experience in Management Consulting, Customer Success, Account Management, Business Development, or another client-facing role</li>
<li>
Experience in working with complex, multi-divisional, multi-geographical customers</li>
<li>
Strong leadership skills</li>
<li>
Impressive executive presence and communication abilities</li>
<li>
Ability to create structure in ambiguous situations and design effective processes</li>
<li>
Bias for action</li>
<li>
Passion for technology and for being a part of a fast-growing SaaS company</li>
<li>
Experience working with cross-functional teams (e.g. Sales, Product, Marketing, Services)</li>
</ul>
