<p>JOB DESCRIPTION</p>
<p><b>Globalstar is hiring a Project Manager to join our team in the Greater New Orleans, LA Area!</b></p>
<p>This individual will have a minimum three years (3) in a PMP role and Bachelor’s degree in Engineering, Business Administration, Operations or related field of study. PMP certification preferred. The ideal candidate will be responsible with planning, managing and tracking full life-cycle, high complexity product development project. The project manager coordinates project proposals to determine time frame, procedures for accomplishing project, and allotment of available resources to various phases of the project. Ability to multi-task is essential. Ability to effectively manage time and prioritize tasks is a must. This individual will have good organizational, analytical, and numerical skills. This individual will have excellent communication and interpersonal skills. They must work with other department team members to ensure departmental deadlines are met and objectives are achieved</p>

</p>

<li>Identify major risks, major expenses, and resource estimates</li>
<li>Support outsourcing (manufacturing and design) strategy and discussions</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>Lead project kickoffs
<ul>

</li>
<li>Lead project kickoffs</li>
