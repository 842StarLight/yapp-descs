<h2>Project Account Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div>Sabre is currently recruiting for a full-time Project Account Manager in our Warrington, PA office to support project managers from the early stages of cost/price proposal development to the execution of successfully awarded contracts. The candidate will perform multiple program financial functions such as budgeting, auditing, and forecasting funding expenditures across multiple projects and contract types. This will involve tracking activities on multiple funding lines within one contract and/or several separate contracts and supporting multiple project managers in managing, tracking, monitoring, and reporting financial data as required. This may include preparing data calls for subcontractors, preparing pricing spreadsheets, conducting market research, developing methodology documentation, compiling backup data, and preparing compliant packages for contract submission.</div>
<div></div>

 <b>10% - New Sales</b>

<div><b>
50% - Project Management
</b></div>

<li>Working with technical team to make sure the statement of work is in line with customer’s needs</li>
<li>

Responsible for all aspects of the development and implementation of assigned projects and provides a single point of contact for those projects</li>

Develops detailed work plans, schedules, project estimates, resource plans, and status reports</li>

Conducts project meetings and is responsible for project tracking and analysis</li>

Manages the integration of vendor/partner tasks and tracks and reviews vendor/partner deliverables</li>
