<li>
Use the power of social and in-person networking to reach CEOs, business owners and HR managers to determine which voluntary benefits programs suit their employees best</li>
<li>
Research and develop new business opportunities by seeking out clients in the surrounding market area</li>
<li>
Provide employers with information as well as presentations that effectively communicate Aflac's ability to enhance benefit packages</li>
<li>
Enroll clients into Aflac products • Make a difference by connecting with existing policyholders to provide service and support</li>
<li>
Build and maintain client relationships by working the Aflac Way</li>
</ul>
