<h2>Inside Sales Representative - Identifix</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>The Role
</b></p>
<p>We are looking for an experienced Account Executive who ranks in the top 25% among their peers. This is someone who has a highly competitive and entrepreneurial mindset that is wired with a team-first attitude, has no problem rolling up their sleeves to execute their missions, and can modulate between leading and following as needed.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>What You’ll Do
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Achieve sales objectives by acquiring new client business through regular proactive consultative sales contact. Responsible for full sales cycle including prospect, question/develop, propose, negotiate and close.
</li>
<li>Increase revenue in mechanical vertical through new client acquisitions, with primary sales focus on competitive takeaways.
</li>
<li>Develop relationships to maintain sales pipeline of opportunities focused on small to medium sized (SMB) clients.
</li>
<li>Learn and retain strong knowledge of Identifix product features, benefits, intended use, value proposition, and competitive position.
</li>
<li>Develop and maintain knowledge of automotive software industry including trends and potential impact on clients. Apply product and industry knowledge to drive sales and serve customer needs.
</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Accurately complete order documentation and/or contracts for customer service.
</li>
<li>Consistent and complete documentation of sales activities for each activity in our CRM tool (Salesforce.com)
</li>
<li>Effectively manage email and voicemail to ensure prompt response to internal and external customers.
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>What You’ll Bring
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Pipeline management with consistent follow-up
</li>
<li>Demonstrated learning aptitude – ability to absorb and apply extensive industry and product knowledge to customers
</li>
<li>Willing to be adaptable to the business needs, when the business dictates the requirement or change
</li>
<li>Goal oriented, focused on achieving/exceeding expected results while exercising initiative
</li>
<li>Confident yet coachable with feedback from trainers and managers
</li>
<li>Strong customer focus—dedicated to meeting the expectations and requirements of customers and acts with customer in mind
</li>
<li>Ability to manage multiple functions at the same time, while being able to deliver in a fast-paced environment with tight deadlines
</li>
<li>Hunter mentality and the ability to be resilient
</li>
<li>Technically proficient
</li>
<li>Outbound cold calling phone sales experience strongly preferred
</li>
<li>SalesForce.com experience preferred
</li>
<li>SaaS platform sales with short sales cycle (1-2 weeks) preferred
</li>
<li>Ability to sit for multiple hours at a time and to work multiple hours a day at a computer keyboard
</li>
<li>Ability to remain alert and focused during the workday
</li>
<li>All other related and/or additional responsibilities that may be required or assigned
</li>
</ul>
