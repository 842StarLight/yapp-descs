<h2>Implementation Associate</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div></div>
<div>
<div>We are building a new kind of software to help clinical research teams advance cures. Why does this matter? Today cumbersome document and data management in clinical trials impedes progress and keeps new medicines from getting to market; the Florence suite of solutions fixes this and we need you to help us scale our business.</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div><b>
What you’ll bring to the team</b></div>
<div>
At Florence we’re passionate about making it easy for our customers to contribute to research and find cures. As Implementation Associate you ensure our customers get up and running as efficiently as possible and create raving fans. You will:</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<ul>
<li>
<b>
Partner </b>with customers to understand their internal dynamics and goals</li>
<li>
<b>
Collaborate </b>with Florence team members in Customer Success, Sales, Account Management, Development, and Product to understand implementation scope</li>
<li>
<b>
Work with Implementation Manager to customize </b>configuration, training, and validation to match the customer’s unique workflows while ensuring compliance</li>
<li>
<b>
Drive </b>user adoption and engagement to ensure long-term success</li>
<li>
<b>
Overcome </b>barriers introduced by customer’s existing software systems to ensure interoperability and process improvement</li>
</ul>
