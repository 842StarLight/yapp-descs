<h2>Account Executive EAP</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>How You'll Make a Difference:</b></p>
<p>
Acts as Employee Assistance Program (EAP) liaison and advisor to leadership of assigned client customers to facilitate all EAP initiatives. Plans, develops, and implements EAP services for all levels of employees of assigned client customers. Responsible for the development and integration of EAP services to support the strategic business goals and objectives of assigned client customers.</p>
<br>
<p>
Establishes and maintains strong working relationships with client companies and responds to their EAP needs in a timely, professional manner. Facilitates communication among employees and management of client companies. May guide managers and employees in problem solving and conflict resolution.</p>
<p>
Works with clinical services staff to ensure seamless service that is congruent with the EAP service model purchased by client customers.</p>
<p>
Confers with management, supervisors and/or employees of client customers to gain knowledge of their culture and work situations in order to determine best possible delivery of EAP services.</p>
<p>
Consults with management of client customers on employee performance, organizational problems, and leadership matters and recommends appropriate solutions and improvement strategies.</p>
<p>
Collaborates with leadership and Account Representatives to meet annual revenue goals by retaining and selling additional services to current client companies. Supports EAP revenue goals by collaborating with the leadership and Account Representatives to increase market share with identified populations.</p>
<p>
Provides advice and counsel to managers and supervisors regarding Human Resource and EAP practices and policies (FMLA, ADA, Harassment, Drug Free Workplace, DOT Regulations, etc.).</p>
<p>
Coordinates and delivers on-site services to client companies in managing workplace trauma or critical events.</p>
<p>
Consults with client companies to determine appropriate training topics. Develops and delivers training to all levels of employees of client customers.</p>
<p>
Selects and/or develops teaching aids such as training handbooks, multimedia visual aids, and reference works as well as appropriate instructional methods for various groups.</p>
<p>
Provides coaching for client companies to resolve supervisory referrals based on the uniqueness of each situation and specific, desired outcomes. Guides managers through the steps in the supervisory referral process. Documents the information received according to established protocols.</p>
