<p><b>Automotive Finance &amp; Insurance Manager
</b></p>
<p><b>Description
</b></p>
<p>Hubert Vester is seeking an <b>Automotive Finance and Insurance Manager </b>to join our winning team. If you are experienced in giving exceptional customer service, have the drive to earn unlimited income, and want to build a career as an Automotive Finance &amp; Insurance Manager working with exciting new products, then we look forward to talking with you.
</p>
<p><b>SUMMARY
</b></p>
<p>Automotive Finance Manager / Automotive F&amp;I Managers help our customers arrange the financing of their purchases and also present them with additional products that enhance their vehicle and ownership experience
</p>
<p><b>Responsibilities
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Structures deals for maximum profitability and collectability
</li>
<li>Fully proficient with title laws and registration process
</li>
<li>Maintains proficiency and certifications as required for the position
</li>
<li>Accurately desks deals, submits deals to lenders for approval, makes credit decisions, and effectively closes deals
</li>
<li>Ensures all deals are fully compliant with local, state, and federal guidelines
</li>
<li>Prepares paperwork, contracts, and delivers deals
</li>
<li>Prepares all deal packages with complete and proper documentation for lenders and follows any other process that may be in place in their respective department
</li>
<li>Accurately audits team deals post-sale
</li>
<li>Ensures the expeditious funding of all contracts
</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Qualifications
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>College degree preferred or equivalent experience
</li>
<li>Three years automotive or finance sales experience
</li>
<li>Knowledge of dealership finance and insurance procedures
</li>
<li>Professional personal appearance and excellent communication skills
</li>
<li>Basic Microsoft Office knowledge; computer software and internet proficiency
</li>
<li>Excellent verbal and written communication, strong negotiation and presentation skills
</li>
<li>Must possess the ability to close a deal
</li>
<li>Valid driver's license
</li>
<li>At least one year of automotive finance required
</li>
</ul>
