<h2>Strategic Partner Manager</h2>

<p>About us:</p>
<p>
Crawford Technologies is an award-winning, worldwide leader in print-stream conversions, document re-engineering, high-volume document workflow, document accessibility and archiving software solutions. For over 20+ years, Crawford Technologies has expanded its solution offerings in Customer Communications Management, Enterprise Output Management, Enterprise Content Management, and Document Accessibility markets. CrawfordTech is dedicated to helping organizations improve their customer communications delivery systems so people can receive their documents in their format and channel of preference.</p>
<p>
Crawford Technologies is on the forefront of technology. As a North American Deloitte Technology FAST 500 company, and listed for the past seven years as a Branham 300 Top ICT Companies in Canada, CrawfordTech is an exciting high growth company with a lot of opportunity.
</p>
<p>Crawford Technologies has a fun, diverse and fast-paced culture with an inherent drive to make an impact with an eye for innovation in technology. We have the best, brightest talent and our employees make the difference, and we have the awards and recognition to demonstrate it.</p>
<p>
WORK FROM HOME within the US!</p>
<p>
POSITION DESCRIPTION:</p>
<p>
In this role you will be responsible for managing and cultivating Crawford Technologies most strategic relationships. You will be the primary point of contact for both our internal and external stakeholders. This position requires high levels of strategic thinking, creativity and leadership skills. Your primary role is to meet or exceed targeted revenue goals through existing and new strategic partners.</p>
<p>
MAJOR DUTIES and RESPONSIBILITES:</p>
<ul>
<li>Meet or exceed partner sales quotas within assigned territory through the sale of solutions, services and software that address customer business requirements.</li>
<li>Self-starter and creative problem-solver with demonstrated ability to work independently and productively with minimal oversight.</li>
<li>Manage relationships with partners to build the brand and the business.</li>
<li>Develop new business opportunities with existing partners, independently managing the negotiation process from exploration, discussion, contract, execution, and launch.</li>
<li>Be a liaise with partners to solve issues, communicate needs and create synergy.</li>
<li>Analyze and report on partner initiatives, forecast for strategic changes and report on key metrics.</li>
<li>Grow net new partner revenue through joint business, pipeline and forecast planning and execution.</li>
<li>Manage executive-level contacts with key partners, including CEO/COO level meetings.</li>
<li>Identify, develop and manage partner relationships with third parties to resell, refer and team for Crawford Tech software solutions and services.
</li>
<li>Follow established RMA Sales Process (Results, Method, Action).</li>
<li>Conduct Business to Business lead generation and prospecting.
</li>
<li>Exercise strong business development with demonstrated ability to identify new partner opportunities and convert them into partners.</li>
<li>Display ability to negotiate and close complex deals.</li>
<li>Utilize strong business acumen and analytical skills.</li>
<li>Provide on-time and accurate/realistic revenue forecasts in Salesforce.</li>
<li>Provide sales leadership within the territory and company.</li>
<li>Communicate with Technical and Professional Services personnel and other departments as necessary, provide them with direction, support and leadership throughout the sales cycle.</li>
<li>Follow-up on referrals, new leads and advise team/management on status weekly and accurately keying information into Salesforce.</li>
<li>Track account activities in Salesforce in a timely manner.</li>
<li>Provide feedback to Product Marketing and Product Management regarding customer requirements, potential new features, effective messaging and product positioning.</li>
<li>Develop and maintain existing and new product knowledge and sales materials.</li>
<li>Prepare proposals, presentations, and sales contracts using current Crawford Tech templates.</li>
<li>Travel as required.</li>
<li>Utilize Salesforce to its full capacity with accuracy.</li>
<li>Other duties as assigned/required.</li>
</ul>

<li>5+ years of partner management or related selling experience.</li>
<li>Experience with Solution Integrators, Enterprise Output Management and Content Management solution providers.
</li>
<li>Experience in building and maintaining customer relationships required.</li>
<li>Demonstrated ability and track record of achieving goals, objectives and targets.</li>
<li>Proficient computer skills in Microsoft Office, Outlook, and online research tools.
</li>
<li>Experience with Salesforce.com.</li>
<li>Excellent telephone skills.
</li>
<li>Must possess a strong work ethic and the ability to work independently.</li>
</ul>

</li>
