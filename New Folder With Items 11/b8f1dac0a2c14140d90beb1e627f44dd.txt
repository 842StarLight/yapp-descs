Tech Ops at Policygenius...
<br><br>
Policygenius is looking for a self-driven, adaptable, and quick-witted IT Support Engineer to join our Technical Operations function! We're looking for someone to support our growing team by working directly with our 200+ employees (and counting!) across multiple teams in NYC. The ideal candidate should be excited about creating a seamless employee experience while delivering exceptional technical support.
<br><br>
Reporting into our Senior Manager of Technical Operations, you'll be working on various projects and continue building your technical skills as we scale.
<br><br>
You will...
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Own hardware and software system access for employee onboarding and offboarding (including Slack, Google groups, and general user administration).</li>
<li>Troubleshoot setup, connectivity, functionality, and update issues for Macs and PCs. Administer workstation hardening, encryption, anti-virus installation and configuration.</li>
<li>Maintain conference room hardware (including projectors, computing equipment, audio/visual, telephony)</li>
<li>Collaborate with members of the People team for internal event functions.</li>
<li>Tackle and address multiple tickets concurrently.</li>
<li>Act as the first point of escalation for resolving all internal IT software, hardware, and wireless network connectivity issues.</li>
</ul>
You have...
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>2-3+ years of professional experience in IT, including hands-on experience with help desk support, service desk management and system administration.</li>
<li>Strong technical knowledge of MacOS, Windows, and a wide variety of SaaS solutions (e.g. G-Suite, JAMF, Okta, Slack, Keeper, Atlassian, etc).</li>
<li>Knowledge of Active directory, DHCP, DNS, and Radius services</li>
<li>Familiarity with scripting languages (e.g. Powershell, Bash, etc)</li>
<li>Proven, stellar written communication, verbal communication, and interpersonal skills--across all levels. You're just as comfortable supporting a Jr. Sales Associate as you are our CEO.</li>
<li>Outstanding project management, change management, and analytical problem-solving skills. You should have exceptional attention to detail and be able to juggle several tasks at once.</li>
<li>Tenacity, the ability to tackle problems head on, and a self-starter mindset.</li>
<li>A sense of urgency and high level of proven productivity in a fast-paced, ever-changing environment.</li>
</ul>
