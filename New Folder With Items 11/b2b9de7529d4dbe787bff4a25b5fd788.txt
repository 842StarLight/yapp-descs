<h2>Senior Accountant</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Punch Studio is a leading Home, Gift &amp; Stationery product supplier for thousands of specialty retailers worldwide. We create high quality, beautifully designed and giftable products that bring delight to people's everyday lives and homes. We are a privately held company headquartered in Culver City, CA looking for passionate leaders to join our growing, vibrant team!</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>Under the general supervision of the CFO, the Senior Accountant is responsible for the following:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Conducting monthly and quarterly account reconciliations and review to ensure accurate reporting and ledger maintenance.
</li>
<li>Helping to coordinate month-end close process, including preparation and review of journal entries and assistance with financial statements.</li>
<li>Preparing monthly inter-company transactions.</li>
<li>Assisting with daily cash management and forecasting.</li>
<li>Assisting with Payroll function and bi-weekly payroll processing. Includes collecting and reviewing timesheets for needed adjustments, reviewing external processor payroll registers for accuracy, processing new hires and changes in pay and tax status.
</li>
<li>Preparing high-level monthly reporting on payroll by department with fluctuation analysis.</li>
<li>Preparing schedules and support for year-end independent audit.</li>
<li>Calculating and report quarterly sales and use tax liability.</li>
<li>Assisting with Accounts Payable and/or serve as back-up for department, as needed.</li>
<li>Reviewing accounting and other processes for improvement in efficiencies and internal controls.</li>
<li>Preparing ad hoc financial reports, and other analysis, as needed.</li>
<li>Other related duties as required.</li>
</ul>
