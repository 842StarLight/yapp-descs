<p>Oracle America, Inc. currently has Reston, VA &amp; Chicago, IL based openings for the <b>Enterprise Sales Representative</b><b> </b>position reporting to the Oracle University global business unit. Utilizing a programmatic approach, the Enterprise Sales Representative is responsible to build and sell complete education services programs inclusive of product-based learning subscriptions, IP solutions, change management services and traditional instructor lead training events to the Oracle customer base through face to face and virtual meetings.</p>

<b>
Detailed Description and Job Requirements</b><div>
</div>
The primary focus of a Telesales Representative is to sell solutions / services to existing Oracle customers and to prospect for new customers in the assigned territory or vertical.<br>
<br>
As a Telesales Sales Representative you will sell products and services to an assigned geographic or national account base territory to achieve assigned objectives. Designs and develops account penetration strategies, and closes business . Responsible for understanding Oracle*s product offerings and competitive issues. Identifies new business opportunities by designing and implementing territory campaigns with management assistance. Liaise with customer contacts for the purpose of managing the customer relationship, identifying new opportunities, and selling Oracle products and services. Acts as a role model within the sales team including wider sales teams including field sales, support and education sales and sales consulting.<br>
<br>
Job duties are varied and complex utilizing independent judgment. May have project lead role. Requires limited intervention from his/her manager, is able to manage complex deals, using judgement where deals fall outside of existing guidelines. 8 years of relevant experience. Ability to communicate and influence effectively over the telephone with high level decision makers. Ability to work independently. Typically bachelor degree in Business or Computer Science or equivalent.<br>
