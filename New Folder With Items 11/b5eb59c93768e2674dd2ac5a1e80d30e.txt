<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Your daily responsibilities include
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<ul>
<li>Adheres to departmental and property policies and procedures regarding guest service standards.</li>
<li>
Responsible for serving as the “face” of the company to our guests by assisting Executive/Sales Host staff and casino floor staff in delivering comps, guest communications, and introductions.</li>
<li>
Assists VIP Guests with various questions regarding their player status, offers, and benefits.</li>
<li>
Assists in the administering of promotional, entertainment, and special events, including the creation of events, running of registration, communication to interdepartmental parties, event needs, etc.</li>
<li>
Maintains accurate head counts in the VIP Lounge through management of the VIP Lounge desk and Lounge Swipe program.</li>
<li>
Assists internal and external customers with event and promotional logistics along with utilizing customer service and problem-solving skills to understand and meet the needs of preferred customers.</li>
<li>
Maintains cleanliness of VIP area by contacting other departments as needed, as well as vacuuming, dusting, watering flower displays, etc.</li>
<li>
Assists VIP Lounge staff as needed (bussing, setting tables, restocking supplies, etc.); serves as support during busy season/weekends.</li>
<li>
Complies with Ameristar Casino Resort and Spa’s standards, guidelines, and objectives and maintains administrative processes to ensure proper planning and efficient operation of department functions.</li>
<li>
Performs all other related and compatible duties as assigned.</li>
<li>
Maintains strict confidentiality in all company matters.</li>
</ul>
</div></div></div>
