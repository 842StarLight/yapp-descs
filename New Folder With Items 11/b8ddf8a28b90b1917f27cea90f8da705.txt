<h2>Associate Principal/Business Technology</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>ZS</b> is a professional services firm that works side by side with companies to help develop and deliver products that drive customer value and company results. From R&amp;D to portfolio strategy, customer insights, marketing and sales strategy, operations and technology, we leverage our deep industry expertise and leading-edge analytics to create solutions that work in the real world. Our most valuable asset is our people—a fact that’s reflected in our values-driven organization in which new perspectives are integral and new ideas are celebrated. ZSers are passionately committed to helping companies and their customers thrive in industries ranging from healthcare and life sciences, to high-tech, financial services, travel and transportation, and beyond.</p>

<br>
<p><b>
ZS's Business Technology group </b>focuses on conceptualizing, designing and implementing a wide spectrum of technology solutions to improve commercial effectiveness. Offering targeted support from strategy and roadmap definition to full implementation and on-going operations, our solutions include enterprise information management, reporting, analytics, CRM/SFA and business process automation enablement. Our team possesses deep expertise in leading analytics, business intelligence, CRM and system integration technologies, which we apply in a pragmatic fashion to create and deliver optimal architecture and implementation solutions.</p>
<p><b>
ASSOCIATE PRINCIPAL - BUSINESS TECHNOLOGY</b></p>
<p>
This role will have responsibility to develop services and offerings, lead business development work with clients to deliver professional services, and manage client engagements. The concentration will be on developing and delivering strategic relations with CIO and direct reports, Strategic Sourcing and to develop Technology Solutions to transform the organization. The person in this role will be responsible for building the life sciences practice by generating new business opportunities and building trusted advisory relationships with key stake holders in the pharmaceutical and medical device clients. They will need to fully understand the company and its service lines and actively assess/present ways to serve clients by developing solutions and offerings relevant to their client base. The goal is to develop and maintain long-term client relationships and networks and to develop and leverage relationships with team members across all the practices to serve client needs. The successful candidate will work closely with ZS leadership grow the practice by identifying and building new customer relationships and building trusted advisory relationships with executives at the company's largest pharmaceutical and medical device accounts.</p>

Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Lead large scale client business development and delivery engagements in the Life Sciences Industry - Pharmaceutical and/or Medical Devices sector;</li>
<li>
Consistently deliver quality client services and drive high-quality work products within expected timeframes and on budget;</li>
<li>
Monitor progress, manage risk and ensure key stakeholders are kept informed about progress and expected outcomes;</li>
<li>
Stay abreast of current business and industry trends relevant to the client's business;</li>
<li>
Effectively lead and motivate teams with diverse skills and backgrounds;</li>
<li>
Provide constructive on-the-job feedback/coaching to team members;</li>
<li>
Foster an innovative and inclusive team-oriented work environment;</li>
<li>
Play an active role in counseling and mentoring junior consultants within the organization;</li>
<li>
Demonstrate deep life sciences industry sector knowledge with a functional expertise across operations;</li>
<li>
Demonstrate ability to quickly assimilate new knowledge;</li>
<li>
Possess in depth business acumen;</li>
<li>
Remain current on new developments in advisory services capabilities and industry knowledge.</li>
