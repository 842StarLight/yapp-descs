<h2>Receptionist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Receptionist / Middletown Branch</p>
<p>Transcend Credit Union has an opportunity for a Receptionist at our new Middletown Branch!</p>
<p>This position will perform Receptionist duties, as well as teller transactions.</p>
<p>The successful candidate will present a polished, professional image regarding conduct and appearance, have a solid work history, and be able to pass a criminal background and credit check.</p>
<p><b>Specific Duties: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Deliver high-quality, consistent service to both external and internal members that fulfill our service promises.</li>
<li>Respectful, Resourceful, Attentive, Responsive, Accurate, and Appreciative.</li>
<li>Continuously review and abide by operating procedures and internal controls in order to protect member accounts, cash, investments and assets from loss</li>
<li>Report any suspected questionable conduct involving financial improprieties, violation of operating procedure, company policies, misuse of Credit Union’s assets, or other fraudulent, dishonest, or illegal conduct detrimental to the interests of the Credit Union.</li>
<li>Identify the financial needs of the members and suggest an appropriate Credit Union solution. Cross-sell appropriate products and services to benefit the member and achieve sales goals set forth by the Credit Union.</li>
<li>Receive and announce visitors and guests to the credit union in a prompt and professional manner. Coordinate follow through so visitors and guests are met promptly.</li>
<li>Answer telephone, disseminate information or resolve questions, and/or route calls to appropriate individual. Put calls on hold and coordinate follow through so caller is handled as promptly as possible and/or put into voice mail or take message at caller’s request.</li>
<li>Process and distribute all mail, interoffice correspondence and deliveries. Research and resolve questions on distribution.</li>
<li>Open new accounts and service existing accounts. Set up new account files and provide members with all necessary membership information.</li>
<li>Maintain an up-to-date and comprehensive knowledge of all credit union products and services. Maintain an up-to date and comprehensive knowledge on all related policies and procedures, rules and regulations, including robbery procedures.</li>
<li>Perform Greeter role serving and directing members and guests as well as taking primary responsibility for phone calls not handled by Call Center.</li>
<li>Perform routine member transactions, including deposits, withdrawals, cash advances, loan payments, transfers, and check cashing.</li>
<li>Participate in Call Center call queue as needed.</li>
<li>Other duties as assigned.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Position Requirements: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>High School Diploma required.</li>
<li>Two years clerical experience preferred.</li>
<li>Maintain a consistently positive, helpful attitude with internal and external members. Act with a sense of urgency to resolve issues and provide outstanding service. Must be service driven.</li>
<li>Ability to abide by Transcend's Service Mission and Promises.</li>
<li>Present a polished, professional image regarding conduct and appearance.</li>
<li>Communication and interpersonal skills to effectively work with members and co-workers.</li>
<li>Ability to proficiently use a personal computer and applicable software employed by the Credit Union.</li>
<li>Ability to handle multiple and/or changing priorities simultaneously.</li>
<li>Ability to cross-sell Credit Union products and services effectively and accurately.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Physical Demands: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>The physical demands described here are representative of those that must be met by an employee to successfully perform the essential functions of this job.</li>
<li>The employee is required primarily to sit, and occasionally stand, stoop, kneel, or crouch.</li>
<li>The employee shall have the ability to occasionally lift boxed coin (approx. 25 pounds).</li>
</ul>
