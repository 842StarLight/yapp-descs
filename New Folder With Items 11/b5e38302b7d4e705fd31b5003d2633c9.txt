<h2>Territory Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>
<div>Corvus is creating a safer, more productive world through technology-enabled commercial insurance. Utilizing sensors, wearables, IoT and other emerging technologies, Corvus combines data and digital tools with insurance expertise to create relevant commercial insurance products and reduce the total cost of risk. Delivering its solutions worldwide, Corvus is reinventing the commercial insurance buying experience making it accessible and transparent for brokers and buyers. A leader in commercial insurance innovation, Corvus is headquartered in Boston, Massachusetts.</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
Corvus is looking for two or three Territory Managers to scale our initial product launch around the US. In this role, you will own and drive revenue by leading your team to acquire new business in key markets. You will also execute and implement our sales process and methodology, achieve your quota each month, and uncover strategies to coach and develop your team.</div>

CAPABILITIES</div>

A successful candidate will be extremely comfortable with technology and have high emotional intelligence. That includes the ability to communicate new technology solutions to intermediaries at large commercial insurance brokers as well as end customers who may find some technology to be a challenge.</div>
