<h2>Dispatcher/ Customer Service</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>BWS Plumbing, Heating and Air Conditioning is a family owned and operated family business specializing in Residential/Commercial Plumbing &amp; HVAC/R service in the Twin Cities. BWS has been serving the Twin Cities for over 50 years. Our customers love us and keep recommending our services.</p>
<ul>
<li><b>Are you good at analyzing information and priorities, and then quickly coordinating your work?</b></li>
<li><b>Do you like people? Are you a customer service or dispatch professional that is able to display empathy, while still being assertive?</b></li>
</ul>
<p><b>SUMMARY DESCRIPTION: </b></p>
<p>Maximize revenue opportunities by matching the appropriate service call to each technician by opportunity levels. Monitor and continually adjust our scheduling board to maximize our capacity. Efficiently schedule parts as they arrive to meet the customers' needs and generate revenue.</p>
<p><b>DUTIES &amp; RESPONSIBILITIES: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Support multiple Field Technicians</li>
<li>Match the best suited job to the most appropriate Service Technician, considering several factors, such as specialty expertise, opportunity level, and geography to maximize our revenue potential</li>
<li>Work effectively with the other CSR's, Service Manager, Field Supervisors, to ensure customer service and company objectives are met</li>
<li>Dispatch to Service Technicians timely with accurate information for incoming customer orders, order status, and information regarding assignments</li>
<li>Address customer concerns and ensure a timely resolution to their problem</li>
<li>Build relationships with Service Technicians to garner trust</li>
<li>Process customer orders/changes according to established policies and procedures</li>
<li>Respond to customer inquiries via telephone, emails and chats partnering with all departments to meet and exceed customer service expectations</li>
<li>Follow scripts provided to overcome objections and capture appointments</li>
<li>Provide timely feedback to company regarding service failures or customer concerns</li>
<li>Maximize capacity of the scheduling board by scheduling part installs, moving calls up when running ahead of schedule, etc.</li>
<li>Analyze work orders to correct inaccurate information so it can be dispatched properly</li>
<li>Constantly evaluate the Dispatch board to catch potential issues</li>
<li>Proactively move up calls to find appropriate call for the technician</li>
<li>Initiate post-service follow-up with customers in order to monitor our performance, ensure customer satisfaction and enter any additional leads into our Service Management software</li>
<li>Support administrative staff and service department personnel with various projects and issues</li>
<li>Perform other related duties, as assigned by the Service Manager</li>
</ul>

<li>Strong customer orientation with ability to display empathy while being assertive</li>
<li>Time Management: ability to organize and manage multiple tasks efficiently</li>
<li>Strong computer skills</li>
<li>Focus and excellent attention to detail</li>
<li>Exceptional verbal and written communication skills in English language</li>
<li>Ability to learn quickly, think proactively, recognize &amp; establish priorities, and use independent judgment</li>

</ul>
