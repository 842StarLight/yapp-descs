<div><b>Job Description</b></div>
<div><b>
Candidate should have experience articulating solutions to stakeholders as this role involves discussions at various levels with client business users. Responsible for gap analysis of functional flows, provide solution design, perform demo for the Gaps solution from standard solution sets as well customized solutions. Can independently solution business requirements and recommend industry best practices. Should be able to lead cross functional discussions to analyze the requirements and brainstorm the solutions.</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Review, analyze and evaluate business systems and user needs. Recommends, develops and executes plans for additional software module implementations based on business requirements Act as functional advisor and manager of technology resources
</li>
<li>Prepare design documentation and work with application delivery resources as necessary to support business requirements
</li>
<li>Prepare and present updates on projects to executive management
</li>
<li>Adept at determining requirements by interviewing client personnel, reviewing documentation and facilitating working sessions.
</li>
<li>Adept at creating business process models based on the requirements
</li>
<li>Adept at mapping industry trends and best practices to the client's unique requirements and processes to determine applicable fit.
</li>
<li>Capable of defining, and re-engineering business processes.
</li>
<li>Able to present solutions described above to senior client management via whiteboard and PowerPoint.
</li>
<li>Knowledgeable about the Oracle applications functionality, tools, templates and method and able to setup the system to meet the desired business process and requirements.
</li>
<li>Adept at working with clients to develop testing scenarios and test scripts and leading teams in unit, integration and acceptance testing.
</li>
</ul>
