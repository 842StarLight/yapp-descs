<h2>Bid Response Analyst</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Overview
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div>
<b>JAGGAER</b> is the world's leading provider of comprehensive source-to-pay solutions. Some of the largest Commercial, Manufacturing and Life Sciences companies in the world trust JAGGAER with billions of dollars of annual spend. Additionally, JAGGAER is the leading procure-to-pay provider in the Higher Education and Government sectors. JAGGAER eProcurement and strategic sourcing customers across the globe have gained access to the best suppliers, with the best terms, on our scalable, customizable, user-friendly platform. Our SaaS-based, source-to-settle solution provides unparalleled visibility, insights and recommendations to procurement leaders and suppliers. The result is a fluid supply chain driven by powerful spend analysis, comprehensive contract management and efficient accounts payable solutions</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
The <b>Bid Response Analyst</b> will own the end-to-end bid management cycle (RFI/RFQ/RFP) for complex, enterprise SaaS deals. The Bid Response Analyst will collaborate closely with field Sales, Product Marketing, Legal, Finance, and other management teams. As part of the Sales Operations team, the Bid Response Analyst will be responsible for developing, managing and continuously improving our response processes, metrics, content and communications. This includes planning and assigning team resources, ensuring consistent, accurate and timely messaging, evaluating content effectiveness, streamlining and driving efficiencies, and increasing JAGGAER win-rates and RFx success.
</div>
</div></div></div>

<div><div><div>

<li>Capture Bid Response Requirements</li>
<li>
Breakdown work structures among team including their own</li>
<ul>
<li>
Sales</li>
<li>
Bid Response</li>
</ul>
<li>
