<h2>Billing Supervisor</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>Are you motivated to participate in a dynamic, multi-tasking environment? Do you want to become part of a company that invests in its employees? Are you seeking a position where you can use your skills while continuing to be challenged and learn? Then we encourage you to dive deeper into this opportunity.</p>
<p>
NeoGenomics is looking for a Billing Supervisor who wants to continue to learn in order to allow our company to grow.This position reports to the Billing Manager and is responsible for all day-to-day activities associated with getting claims entered into the Company’s billing system and invoiced to clients, government, and third party payers. This position will be responsible for recommending process and procedural recommendations to improve operational effectiveness and financial outcomes. The Billing Supervisor is also responsible for helping to ensure that metrics established to measure quality, production and efficiency are strictly followed.</p>

<p>
As an employer, we promise to provide you with a purpose driven mission in which you have the opportunity to save lives by improving patient care through the exceptional work you perform. Together, we will become the world's leading cancer reference laboratory.</p>
<p><b>
Core Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Assist the Billing Manager with supervision of staff and daily operations of the Billing and Collections business functions under the direction of the Billing Manager and/or Director of Finance.</li>
<li>
Ensures that claims are billed on a timely basis and are accurate and complete</li>
<li>
Monitors postings and all billing to ensure compliance with established company policies and healthcare regulations</li>
<li>
Works with collection staff, commercial insurance, Medicare, Medicaid, HMO’s and clients to collect all balances that are due</li>
<li>
Works with hospitals to ensure that their bills are accurate and complete</li>
<li>
Collaborates with the IT department to design, improve and implement effective processes</li>
<li>
Manages actions related to delinquent accounts and minimizes write-offs</li>
<li>
Assists with developing operational and volume metrics and analyzes them on a monthly basis. Makes appropriate staffing recommendations based on volume</li>
<li>
Recommends and implements process improvements</li>
<li>
Reviews receivables aging and takes actions to drive down Days Sales Outstanding (DSO’s)</li>
<li>
Works with the Finance Department at month-end and helps to analyze and understand trends</li>
<li>
Maintains up-to-date expertise and knowledge of compliance, CPT code changes, healthcare billing laws, rules, regulations, and developments necessary for the organization to make informed business decisions</li>
<li>
Ensures procedural requirements are followed and ethical standards for the follow-up of overdue accounts are maintained</li>
<li>
Communicates immediately to the Billing Manger and Director of Finanace regarding any compliance related problems or unresolved processes</li>
<li>
Ensures that employees are trained and compliant with SOP’s and competency checklists</li>
<li>
Assists the Billing manager with personnel management activities for the department; selects and hires personnel; conducts or guides supervisor in selection interviews, hiring, motivating and training staff</li>
<li>
Assists the Billing Manager with performance appraisals, documenting deficiencies, developing plans for improvement and counseling employees</li>
<li>
Works with the Billing Manager to develop department objectives and performance goals; assists with monitoring work performance to determine staff resources utilization; assists with reviewing monthly work statistics and budget reports; assists with establishing work performance standards and adjusts staffing levels as needed</li>
<li>
Ensures that a safe working environment is maintained and that safe work practices are employed; ensures staff participation in all safety, compliance and other mandatory training</li>
</ul>

<li>
Achieves all performance goals or objectives (individual or departmental) as established by the Billing Manager and/or Director of Finance</li>
<li>
Effectively schedules and manages resources to meet department goals</li>
<li>
Communicates effectively with all levels of staff</li>
<li>
Maintains thorough knowledge and shows demonstrated support of NeoGenomics Laboratories core values, safety, compliance, and Company policies and procedures</li>
</ul>
<p><br>
<b>Qualification Requirements:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
5+ years prior Medical Billing/Revenue Cycle experience, with laboratory billing preferred</li>
<li>
Education: Associate’s degree required (Bachelor’s preferred) in Business or a related field</li>
<li>
Knowledge of Business processes, accounting principles, billing practices, medical terms, CPT and ICD10 coding</li>
<li>
Ability to interact with IT and the laboratory departments to drive improvements</li>
<li>
Creative problem solving and analytical skills</li>
<li>
Proven ability to drive resolution of billing issues with customers</li>
<li>
Understanding of internal controls &amp; cash management</li>
<li>
Experience working with Commercial Insurance, Medicare, Medicaid and HMO’s</li>
<li>
Ability to make sound decisions under stressful situations</li>
<li>
Demonstrated success in leading team members to reach goals and objectives</li>
<li>
Good computer skills including working knowledge of MS Office and medical billing software. (experience with Telcor/XIFIN a plus)</li>
<li>
Strong organizational skills and attention to detail</li>
<li>
Ability to work independently and within a team environment</li>
<li>
Must be able to work in a fast paced, multi-tasking environment and maintain production and quality standards</li>
<li>
Must be able to work in a biohazard environment and comply with safety policies and standards outlined in the Safety Manual</li>
</ul>
