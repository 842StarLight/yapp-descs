<p><b>Saitech Inc</b> is <i>always</i> <i>looking</i> for enthusiastic, energetic, self-starters, motivated individuals to <i>join</i> our growing team. We are <i>looking</i> forward to <i>hiring</i>, training, grooming, growing people to their maximum potential, giving them an environment where they can achieve their goals . </div>
<div>
<p>
Successful Account Reps are goal-oriented, highly motivated, and effective communicators.<br>
A Saitech Inc: Information Technology Hardware &amp; Software Solution Provider’s daily tasks include:</p>
<div>
<ul>
<li>Providing cost effective solutions and cost savings. Educating customers about the unique products and value added services that Saitech Inc <i>offers</i>.</li>
<li>Developing new business <i>with</i> networking and sales calls and emails to customers worldwide. Use your knowledge, network and <i>experience</i> to grow new business <i>with</i> OEM / CM customers worldwide.</li>
<li>Studying RFQ and RFP’s, quoting and negotiating price <i>with</i> customers. Negotiate price through PM <i>with</i> manufacturers, and cooperate <i>with</i> purchasing and marketing department.</li>
<li>Calling existing customers and establish new accounts by planning and organizing daily <i>work</i> schedule on existing or potential sales outlets.</li>
<li>Mailing, emailing company brochures to Universities, Government and corporate clients and document the feedback from them upon receiving.</li>
<li>Working <i>with</i> Supplier Diversity Managers in Corporate, Govt, Educational institutions and registering and promoting the company as a Certified Small Minority MBE supplier of IT products and services.</li>
<li>Constructive feedback needed to improve after reaching <i>out</i> to existing or new customers <i>based</i> on the last conversation <i>with</i> them to drive sales.</li>
<li>Keep management informed by submitting activity and results reports, such as daily call reports, weekly <i>work</i> plans.</li>
<li>
<i>Job</i> requires multi-tasking and positive interaction and assistance <i>with</i> different departments like accounting, purchasing, shipping, return merchandize authorization.</li>
</ul>
</div>
<p>The Account Executive will focus on new account penetration, account growth, account and relationship development. Focus on developing and expanding new accounts. Must have the ability to learn, <i>apply</i> and present sales principles and techniques within a short time frame.</p>
<p><b>
Minimum </b><b><i>Job</i></b><b> Requirements</b></p>
<div>
<div><ul>
<li>Strong selling skills <i>with</i> 1-2 years <i>experience</i> in PC hardware</li>
<li>Excellent relationship skills <i>with</i> ability to develop and maintain key contacts</li>
<li>Intermediate systems knowledge and capability to navigate through applications</li>
<li>General understanding of products and basic business development skills</li>
<li>Ability to multi-task and prioritize in fast paced environment</li>
<li>Demonstrated business communication skills, including but not limited to verbal and written communication skills</li>
<li>Possesses strong organizational and time management skills</li>
<li>High level of professionalism and business maturity</li>
</ul></div>
