<h2>IDeaS Sr. Product Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div></div>
<div><div><div><div>
<div>The Sr. Product Manager will be responsible for the product planning and execution throughout the product lifecycle, including: gathering and prioritizing product and customer requirements, defining the product vision, and working closely with development, sales, marketing and support to ensure revenue and customer satisfaction goals are met. Collaborates with the development team to drive product development in line with the three-year product roadmap. Ensures that the product supports the company’s overall strategy and goals.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
<b>
Major Responsibilities:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Identifies opportunities and develops business plans including recommended actions for emerging technology and business issues that present opportunities or threats to IDeaS.</li>
<li>Identifies and develops business plan for new market opportunities within the company’s distinctive competence.</li>
<li>Works on complex issues where analysis of situations or data requires an in-depth evaluation of variable factors. Exercises judgment in selecting methods, techniques and evaluation criteria for obtaining results.</li>
</ul>
</div>
<ul>
<li>Networks with key contacts outside own area of expertise.</li>
<li>Defines product requirements based on customer, prospect and evaluator inputs, market direction and field contribution; generates Market Requirements Document (MRD).</li>
<li>Drives product development in R&amp;D based on requirements management and product lifecycle.</li>
<li>Demonstrates excellent product knowledge with external audiences, such as press, thought leaders, analysts, speaking engagements at trade shows and user groups.</li>
<li>Participates heavily in the development and execution of the product vision and roadmap.</li>
<li>Defines user personas and use cases.</li>
<li>Creates executive briefing documents to drive decision-making process.</li>
<li>Ensures knowledge transfer of product functionality to marketing &amp; sales.</li>
<li>Delivers technical, fact-oriented content for pricing, positioning and collateral.</li>
<li>Demonstrates good judgment in selecting methods and techniques for obtaining solutions.</li>
<li>May manage OEM/Technical provider relationships.</li>
<li>Run beta and pilot programs with early-stage products and samples</li>
<li>Identifies opportunities for emerging technology and business issues that present opportunities or threats to the company.</li>
<li>Performs other duties, as assigned.</li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
<b>
Requirements:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree in a business Marketing, Engineering, Computer Science or quantitative field (Operations Research, Industrial Engineering or similar.)</li>
<li>8+ years’ experience in Product Management, Marketing, Sales, Consulting or related function in the technology industry.</li>
<li>5+ years’ experience working with data integration in a hospitality/ travel context e.g. Hotel Technology Next Generation, Oracle APIs, Oracle OXI, REST communication and other XML-based exchanges common in a hospitality and travel context.</li>
<li>3+ years forecasting, optimization or pricing technology experience in a revenue management related technology within the hospitality, airline and/ or travel industry</li>
<li>Demonstrably strong ability to translate business requirements to analytical needs to act as the liaison between SAS analytics and software development at IDeaS.</li>
<li>Ability to understand product features and describe associated customer value</li>
<li>Ability to map market requirements into product features</li>
<li>Proven ability to influence cross-functional teams without formal authority</li>
<li>Full understanding of the product management skills; ability to resolve a wide range of issues in creative ways</li>
<li>Skilled in effective communication, influencing and negotiation</li>
<li>Organizational skills for managing commitments, schedules, and deadlines</li>
</ul>
</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
<b>
Preferences:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Master’s degree in a business marketing, engineering, computer science or quantitative field (operations research, industrial engineering or similar.)</li>
<li>Product management experience specific to the hospitality or travel industry at a Fortune 500 company</li>
</ul>
</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
<b>
Other:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Work is typically performed in an office environment, using simple, easy muscular movements, involving only the handling of light materials, tools, or equipment in easy work positions, and with no special speed requirements or long stretches of exertion.</li>
<li>Vision requirements for this job include close vision and the ability to adjust focus.</li>
<li>Ability to travel up to 50% depending on location</li>
</ul>
</div>
