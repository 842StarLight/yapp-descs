<h2>Agricultural Laboratory Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div></div>
<div>
<ul>
<li>Perform laboratory testing on agricultural samples.</li>
<li>Oversee all activities in the Agricultural Lab.</li>
<li>Build new business and improve existing business to exceed Revenue and Profit expectations.</li>
<li>Report results to clients in a timely and accurate manner.</li>
<li>Organize, motivate and lead the laboratory to provide the highest quality of service to customers and maximize profit.</li>
<li>Build and proactively manage trust relationships with customers.</li>
<li>Assist clients in any way possible in problem solving when applicable.</li>
<li>Be proactive and open minded in expanding laboratory capabilities when profitability makes it feasible.</li>
</ul>
REPORTING LINE<br>
Laboratory Director<br>
<br>
SPECIFIC RESPONSIBILITIES<br>
<ul>
<li>Perform laboratory tests in accordance with approved test methods or procedures.</li>
<li>Conduct required testing on samples such as moisture, protein, toxins, falling number, kheldahl, dockage or other tests on liquids and grains.</li>
<li>Train and develop employees to carry out laboratory testing, method development and compliance with QA/QC requirement.</li>
<li>Make sure all relevant raw data or test information to be accurately recorded in the provided raw data or log book.</li>
<li>Adhere to group HSE safety procedures.</li>
<li>Perform any other tasks as assigned by Lab Director and/or Trade Services Manager.</li>
<li>Supervise and organize the team’s activities, including planning, coordinating lab work, visits, reports and results.</li>
<li>Maintain and manage price lists.</li>
<li>Liaise with Business &amp; Operations managers, as well as Group sanctioned superusers to ensure Systems are fully deployed and utilized</li>
<li>Ensure correct procedures are adhered to, including ISO related procedures</li>
<li>Coach and motivate team to meet agreed objectives, deliver outstanding services and provide extra-mile support</li>
<li>Take full accountability for the service delivery to clients</li>
<li>Participate in the annual budget process and regularly monitor expenditures</li>
<li>Advise and support the preparation of quotations</li>
<li>Ensure communication and coordination with other teams (e.g. sales and operations) to facilitate the successful handover and execution of services</li>
<li>Proactively keep in touch with the client base to strengthen relationships and ensure retention</li>
<li>Ensure client feedback is gathered and acted upon</li>
<li>Manage timely trouble shooting and follow up to the customer</li>
<li>Identify business opportunities and support team members to materialize new deals</li>
<li>Participate in sales forecasts</li>
<li>Forward to the Sales Team business opportunities beyond sphere of influence</li>
<li>Network with key players and maintain an understanding of market developments to ensure SGS becomes the services provider of choice</li>
<li>Gather market intelligence and inform team as appropriate</li>
<li>Be abreast of SGS new products and services and propose them to customers as appropriate</li>
<li>Whenever required carry out tasks belonging to a Customer Service Executives (see corresponding Job Description)</li>
<li>At all times, adopt a safe behavior by exercising due regard for the health and safety of SGS employees and clients, in line with SGS policies and procedures</li>
<li>At all times, comply with SGS Code of Integrity and Professional Conduct</li>
</ul>
<br>
<div>PROFILE<br>
<ul>
<li>Degree in Science or in the relevant technical subject</li>
<li>Minimum of 5 years of technical experience in a Agricultural Lab or similar</li>
<li>Minimum of 3 years of Management or Business Development experience with a successful track record</li>
<li>High level of written and verbal English</li>
</ul>
REQUIRED SKILLS &amp; QUALIFICATIONS:<br>
<ul>
<li>Ability to lead and motivate a Laboratory Team towards shared objectives</li>
<li>Ability to coach team members on CS related techniques</li>
<li>Able to handle multiple tasks concurrently and prioritize</li>
<li>Strong customer orientation and service oriented mindset</li>
<li>Ability to interact with all levels of management, consultants and clients</li>
<li>Technical skills or understanding in the related field</li>
<li>Capacity to use computer systems</li>
<li>Capacity to be trained to use Group sanctioned systems</li>
<li>Assertiveness</li>
<li>High degree of Initiative</li>
<li>Excellent negotiation and persuasion skills</li>
<li>Strong interpersonal and communication skills</li>
<li>Ability to build trust relationships</li>
<li>Team player</li>
<li>Ability to synthesize and communicate information</li>
<li>Strong organizational skills</li>
<li>Enthusiasm and perseverance</li>
<li>Quick and pro-active</li>
<li>Flexibility</li>
<li>Excellent interpersonal and communication skills with the ability to establish and maintain effective working relationships with subordinates, peers, management, external customers and the public.</li>
<li>Proven ability to manage and coordinate multiple projects in a fast paced, highly professional environment.</li>
<li>Ability to exercise discretion and independent judgment when necessary.</li>
<li>Candidates must be proficient in using various type of computer software (Word, Excel, Outlook, Internet, web based programs, etc).</li>
<li>Must possess and maintain a valid driver’s license as travel to various client sites is required.</li>
<li>Proven time management skills and a strong attention to detail.</li>
<li>Demonstrates good verbal and written communication skills including grammar and composition.</li>
<li>Positive team spirit.</li>
<li>Must be able to lift and carry 25 pounds.</li>
</ul>
<br>
HEALTH &amp; SAFETY GUIDELINES:<br>
<br>
<ul>
<li>Demonstrates strong commitment to SGS HSE Policies, Procedures, and Work Instructions by participating in safety meetings, completing required training, intervening in unsafe situations, refusing unsafe work, and complying fully with all applicable laws and regulations related to HSE.</li>
<li>Responds appropriately to emergency situations and assists other staff members in maintaining readiness to respond to emergencies within the workplace.</li>
<li>In accordance with the SGS HSE Management System requirements, disposes of, or directs the disposal of, waste generated as a part of daily work performed in a safe manner and in compliance with all waste transportation and disposal regulations and requirements.</li>
<li>Reports all incidents (actual incidents and near misses) in accordance with the SGS Incident Reporting requirements.</li>
<li>Uses all equipment (including safety equipment) in the manner intended and reports any damaged / lost equipment to supervisor.</li>
<li>Maintain a safe and tidy worksite.</li>
<li>Maintains awareness of hazards and proposes controls to supervisor, HSE Representatives or relevant client / site contact.</li>
<li>Actively participates in incident investigations and risk assessments as deemed necessary by SGS management.</li>
</ul>
