<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Please review the following job description:
</b></h3>
<p></p>Support agents by servicing accounts. Be familiar with company guidelines and underwriting. Be knowledgeable in coverages. Maintain rapport with clients and company underwriters and to develop existing accounts.
<p></p>
<p><b>Essential Duties and Responsibilities:
</b><br>
Following is a summary of the essential functions for this job. Other duties may be performed, both major and minor, which are not mentioned below. Specific activities may change from time to time.
<br>
1.Receive phone calls and walk-in clients.
<br>
2.Answer questions, process and resolve customer service requests and problems.
<br>
3.Order and process new business, renewals and changes, including updating the computer system.
<br>
4.Assist in completing applications and marketing.
<br>
5.Maintain proper documentation on coverages and exceptions.
<br>
6.Maintain customer files on both paper and computer, according to procedure. Keep manuals up-to-date.
<br>
7.Obtain expiration dates for policies not written by us to follow up and quote. Monitor all expirations and be sure all renewals are processed. Refer other insurance lines to appropriate departments.
<br>
8.Review entire account needs when first written.
<br>
9.Review accounts at renewal.
<br>
10.Prepare proposals and client presentations as required.
<br>
11.Due to changing business conditions, management may request that additional duties or functions be assigned to this position.
<br>
12.Responsible for some in-house training and mentoring of other Client Service Agents
</p>
<p>Commercial Property and Casualty Only: Take, report and monitor claims where applicable. Contact clients for collection of premiums as necessary. Invoice premiums.
</p>
