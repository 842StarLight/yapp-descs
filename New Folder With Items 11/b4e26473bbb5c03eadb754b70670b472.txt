<h2>Digital Marketing Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>The Digital Marketing Manager is a multifaceted position responsible for collaborating with the digital marketing team to build brand affinity and fan engagement through content creation and management of web, social, and email marketing. The ideal candidate will have prior professional experience maintaining a social media presence for a brand and have a passion for facilitating a world-class fan experience in the online sphere. They also must have exceptional writing and communication skills, with an understanding of racing series and their fan bases, or the eagerness to learn.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Essential Functions:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Work alongside digital marketing team to plan and execute all digital marketing for Circuit of The Americas with an emphasis on COTA and Austin360 Amphitheater brands, including social media, email, website content/SEO, and digital advertising campaigns</li>
<li>Measure and report performance of digital marketing campaigns</li>
<li>Identify trends and insights and optimize strategy accordingly</li>
<li>Bring original and innovative content to all digital strategy</li>
<li>Maintain and grow the brand's social media presence through real-time engagement/outreach and through impeccable customer service</li>
<li>Assist in maintaining and growing the brand's email database</li>
<li>Collaborate with team to optimize user experience online</li>
<li>Collaborate with agencies and vendor partners</li>
<li>Consistently bring fresh, practical ideas that drive sales and fan interest</li>
<li>Collaborate with team to create and maintain editorial calendar for digital marketing initiatives</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Knowledge, Skills, and Abilities
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Demonstrated excellence maintaining a social media presence for a major brand Strong research and analytical skills Knowledge of best practices on major social media platforms and how to strategically utilize those platforms</li>
<li>Knowledge of best practices in email marketing Excellent communication skills a must</li>
<li>Impeccable grammar and copywriting ability</li>
<li>Excellent editorial judgment and a strong creative sensibility Must be able to handle multiple projects and tight deadlines</li>
<li>Ability to work closely and communicate on a daily basis with teammates to share workload and collaborate on projects</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Requirements
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor's degree in journalism, communications, or marketing</li>
<li>1+ years of experience in social media/digital marketing</li>
<li>1+ year of database marketing</li>
<li>Ability to work evenings and weekends for events as needed</li>
<li>Thrives in a collaborative, fast-paced event environment</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Preferred (but not required):</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Appreciation for and knowledge of motorsports and music</li>
<li>Experience with the event industry</li>
<li>Eye for great design</li>
<li>Photography skills</li>
<li>Photoshop/Illustrator proficient</li>
<li>Bilingual English/Spanish</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Physical Demands:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Ability to stand, sit, use hands to handle or feel, and reach with hands/arms</li>
<li>Ability to lift and/or move up to 25 pounds</li>
<li>Specific vision abilities required by this job include close vision and distance vision</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Work Environment:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Open floor environment adjacent to an active racetrack with a moderate level of noise</li>
<br>
</ul>
