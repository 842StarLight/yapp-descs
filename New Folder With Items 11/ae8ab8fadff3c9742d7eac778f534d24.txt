<h2>RETAIL SPECIALIST NF4</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div><div><div>
<div><div><div><div>
<div><p>Marine Corps Community Services (MCCS) is looking for the best and brightest to join our Team! MCCS is a comprehensive program that supports and enhances the quality of life for Marines, their families, and others in the Marine Corps Community. We offer a team oriented environment comprised of military personnel, civilian employees, contractors and volunteers who keep the organization functioning smoothly and effectively. </div></div></div></div>
<div><div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Major Duties</b></h2>
<div>
<div></div>
<div>
<p>Serves as primary contact to ensure operational effectiveness and efficiency of several programs within Business Operations to include Retail Workforce Scheduling, Retail Point of Sale, , SharePoint and Task Management. Key responsibilities include: researching and identifying industry standards, trends, user requirements and recommending new system enhancements to improve operations. Monitors all aspects of systems’ performance, communicating with commands,stores, and Headquarter’ teams.. Works in a cross-functional environment in support of Business Operations. Serves as liaison between Business Operations and the Informational Technology Branches to solve problems and implement new systems. May train systems’ users and serves as the functional expert on systems’ operations and procedures. Provides status reports to supervisor to support program management.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
Supports management of systems by conducting analysis of historical data to determine appropriate recommendations. Formulates analysis and makes recommendations to management regarding trends derived from forecasts and historical data. Prepares forecasts and analyzes trends in sales, general business conditions and other related areas. Assists in the development of business policies and other retail related studies. Actively participates in product training as necessary.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
Serves as gatekeeper responsible for maintaining and monitoring the system and management tools for the task management system and database infrastructure. Plays a key role in monitoring the communication within the system. Maintains all communication between the task owner and the tasked individual. Responsible for all actions involved in developing new processes and reports using the application reporting module for designated business units.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
Develops best practices, rules of engagement, and innovative initiatives that adhere to established policy and procedure by the United States Marine Corps and Marine Corps Community Services. Conducts procedural inspections and reviews for compliance with MCCS policies and regulations. Conducts and organizes portions of analytical studies and economic analyses to ensure functional needs are met for programs and services. Makes recommendations on corrective actions and conducts follow up inspections. Reports to management on the effectiveness of deployed systems based on appropriate reporting. Provides, creates and monitors detailed analysis documents to trend scheduling, sales performance and results. Plans, develops and conducts analysis for current and projected programs to achieve goals and objectives.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
May represent the organization by attending conferences and meetings. Serves as subject matter expert on assigned systems and presents findings and system errors. Prepares supporting documentation for presentations and proposals. May prepare correspondence, briefs, action documents, routing sheets, point papers and slide presentations for use by management officials.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
May work a fluctuating work schedule to complete work assignments outside of the typical work week and work hours. May travel to complete work assignments, and conduct or attend presentations, conferences and meetings.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p>Provides World Class Customer Service with an emphasis on courtesy. Assists customers and communicates positively in a friendly manner. Takes action to solve problems quickly. Alerts the higher-level supervisor or proper point of contact for help when problems arise. Adheres to safety regulations and standards. Promptly reports any observed workplace hazards, and any injury, occupational illness, and/or property damage resulting from workplace mishaps to the immediate supervisor. Adheres to established standards of actively supporting the principles of the EEO program and prevention of sexual harassment.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
This is a white-collar position where occasional lifting up to 20 lbs may be required.</p>

<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Qualifications</b></h2>
<div>
<div></div>
<div>
<p><b>BACHELOR’ DEGREE </b>in Information Technology, Business or a related field appropriate to the work of the position and three years of related experience; <b>OR</b> an appropriate combination of education and experience that demonstrates possession of knowledge and skill equivalent to that gained in the above, <b>OR</b> appropriate experience that demonstrates that the applicant has acquired the knowledge, skills, and abilities equivalent to that gained in the above. Must have Microsoft SharePoint Developer experience.</p>
<p>
Experience in retail store operations, including knowledge of retail software systems preferable. Knowledge of functional support for software relating to point of sale, work force and task management tools. Demonstrated knowledge of and skill to analyze and evaluate efficiency and effectiveness of retail software systems and other functional program operations to meet goals and objectives. Exceptional communication and presentation skills. Must have excellent troubleshooting and performance analysis skills. Demonstrated ability to resolve complex issues that lead to thoughtful recommendations and action plans. Excellent writing, editing and communication skills. Must possess solid organizational and detail-orientated expertise and the ability to handle numerous projects at one time.</p>
