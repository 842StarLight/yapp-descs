<div><div>The <b>Program Operations Specialist</b> will manage all aspects of the AirCare Membership program including outreach, marketing sales, and public relations. They will take primary responsibility to develop short and long-term strategic goals for AirCare Membership retention and prospective clientele. They will perform research and analysis to provide management with AirCare Membership data and recommendations for use in reporting and communicating results and trends. This position will also identify, recruit and work with direct solicitations of potential members and/or provide training and support for team leaders to make these solicitations.
<br>
<br>
The Program Operations Specialist will be required to travel to other offices and markets to represent Airlift Northwest. This position will ensure that organizational requests for memberships are handled effectively and efficiently. This position will handle day to day operation of the AirCare Membership program and provide training to classified staff who provide. Incumbent will be working with various software programs such as RescueNet, Zoll Analytics, Zoll Online, Tableau, Microsoft Office Suite products, and potentially converting to a new membership system.
<br>
<br>
The Program Operations Specialist is required to have a strong understanding of operational program management and database maintenance. This individual will build and develop rapport with Airlift Northwest Members and outside organizations and businesses. The Program Operations Specialist will be required to collaborate with the ALNW Marketing and Outreach teams to ensure growth of new business and persistency of existing business along with meeting increasingly aggressive sales goals. Along with the required knowledge, this position will also be expected to coordinate, and distribute marketing, public relations and outreach material unique to the membership program. Additional duties include the development and the coordination of sales proposals and pertinent sales reporting. The Program Operations Specialist will be responsible for creating and managing the AirCare Membership annual budget. This position will drive market research and competitive intelligence while enforcing brand consistency through, marketing collateral, public relations, website, etc. This position will be required to coordinate and participate in internal and external meetings and industry events.
<br>
<br>
The Program Operations Specialist will have a thorough knowledge of various programs used by ALNW in the process of day to day business. Audit daily data entry in the AirCare Membership system to ensure accuracy, completeness and consistency. Communicate data administration concepts to management and staff via oral and written reports and presentations. Provide interaction with development and end user personnel to perform volume analysis and other pertinent data required to develop and maintain integrated databases and reports. Possess strong understanding of data analysis techniques and data mining of ALNW's pertinent data. Work closely with IT Department to troubleshoot and resolve AirCare Membership database problems. Assist with AirCare Membership database performance tuning and optimization. Work closely with Outreach Coordinators, Chief Flight Nurse, Regional Managers, Safety Coordinator, Senior and Executive Management Leadership Team, on projects initiated by them, seeing the project through by developing and managing the project. Plan, implement and execute projects according to project plan. Develop forms, record or document project activities and report progress to appropriate parties. Based on the analysis outcome, present complete and concise recommendations to management. Establish and maintain relationships with third parties or vendors. Maintain technical knowledge by attending training, educational workshops, establishing personal networks, attending committee meetings. This position will participate in the continuous quality improvement of ALNW operations
<br>
<br>
ALNW is a UW Medicine entity which provides essential air medical transportation to patients throughout the WAMI region. This position brings unique knowledge in support of ALNW's mission and vision to integrate with the UW Medicine's current and future strategic mission.
