<h2>Part-Time Center Associate</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Apply to be considered in joining The UPS Store Leestown Road team as we conquer the customer service world! We are a team of hard working professionals who get the job done right in a fast paced working environment! Our days are spent making customers happy and are looking for another candidate who has the skills and attitude to help grow our team! The Part-Time Center Associate delivers world-class customer service to all retail customers by receiving and processing packages for courier shipment and operating copiers, fax machines, binding equipment, laminating machines, and point-of-sale devices. The Associate expertly advises our valued customers by providing accurate information on our array of products, services, and best-value options and exhibits confidence by knowing best practices related to the industry.<br>
<br>
The ideal candidate has previous retail sales experience, strong computer and internet skills, a high school diploma or GED, a friendly and genuinely helpful demeanor, and a professional appearance. He or she must be a fast learner who makes an effort to learn all aspects of the business in the shortest possible time. This position does require availability to work some Saturdays during each month and also closing shifts throughout the week, but they're not too late, as we close at 8:00PM Monday through Friday and are open 9-3 on Saturdays.</p>
