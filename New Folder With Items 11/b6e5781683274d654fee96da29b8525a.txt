<p><b>Chantilly, VA - Preserve at Westfields:</b></p>
<p><b>POSITION OVERVIEW</b></p>
<p>The Leasing Associate is responsible for leasing, marketing, and maintaining positive resident relations of multi-family residential apartments. As the front line of the community office, Leasing Associates will provide customer service to current residents, potential residents, business partners and neighborhood partners.</p>
<p><b>LOCATION</b></p>
<p>Chantilly, VA</p>
<p><b>POSITION AUTHORITY</b></p>
<p>The Leasing Associate does not have direct authority over any team member.</p>
<p><b>ESSENTIAL FUNCTIONS</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Represent the company in a professional manner at all the times. Exemplary customer service, attitude, and professionalism are paramount.</li>
<li>Greet prospects and qualify by covering all criteria. Record all prospect visits and initial.</li>
<li>Demonstrate community and apartment/model and apply product knowledge to clients needs by communicating the features and benefits.</li>
<li>Assist prospect in completing application and secure deposit.</li>
<li>Update leasing software, process applications for approvals, and submit processed applications to the Community Manager for approval. Follow up with applicant regarding status.</li>
<li>Maintain follow up communication via email and telephone.</li>
<li>Ensure apartment is ready for resident to move-in.</li>
<li>Prepare lease and assist resident with lease execution. Orient new residents to community.</li>
<li>Inspect models and available market ready, communicate related service needs to Property Manager.</li>
<li>Assist in monitoring renewals and lease expiration dates.
</li>
<li>Distribute and follow-up on renewal notices.</li>
<li>Monitor advertising effectiveness and update all online marketing outlets. Gather information about market competition in the area and file.</li>
<li>Participate in outreach marketing activities on a regular basis to obtain prospective residents.</li>
<li>Assist in placing, removing/updating banners, balloons, bandit signs, flags, etc.. Maintenance of signs for appearance and visibility is a daily activity.</li>
<li>Conduct market surveys and shop competitive communities.</li>
<li>Consistently implement policies of the community.</li>
<li>Complete appropriate application processing paperwork and input all prospect, applicant, and resident information into leasing software system accurately and on a timely basis.</li>
<li>Maintain current resident files.</li>
<li>Receive all telephone calls and in-person visits.</li>
<li>Patiently and empathetically listen to resident requests, concerns and comments.</li>
<li>Quickly complete maintenance Service Request and inform the maintenance team. Answer questions for residents about community, repairs, rent, rules, etc.</li>
<li>Maintain open communication with all community team members.</li>
<li>Contribute to cleanliness and curb appeal of the community on continuing basis.</li>
<li>Assist in planning resident functions. Attend functions and participate as host for any functions as directed by the Community Manager.</li>
</ul>
