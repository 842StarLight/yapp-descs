<h2>National Accounts Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>Plantation Patterns, a division of NBGHome, is a manufacturer and distributor of beautiful outdoor cushions and umbrellas in the United States. We are currently seeking a<b> National Accounts Manager</b> to be based in our <b>Birmingham, AL</b> office. This is a full-time salary exempt position reporting to the Vice President of Sales.</p>
<p><b>
Summary</b></p>
<p>
This position is responsible for growing sales and developing customer relationships to meet specific goals at assigned accounts as established by the VP of Sales. This position acts as a primary point of contact for our customer base and also analyzes relevant market and Company sales data in order to make strategic decisions regarding products, budgets, and forecasts.</p>
<p><b>
Essential Duties and Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Develops, manages and leads sales objectives, strategies and initiatives to achieve profitable sales growth, increased market share and a sustainable industry position with their accounts.</li>
<li>Meets targeted monthly and annual growth of gross sales and gross profit dollars as established by the VP of Sales.</li>
<li>Makes professional, accurate, and timely sales presentations to customers. Fosters and develops on-going relationships with customers. Develops ways to be in constant contact with accounts through monthly (on average) in person meetings and weekly product updates. Takes care of all customer follow up as needed.</li>
<li>Performs market research in order to prepare budgets and support forecasting customer growth, trends, and business opportunities. Maintains up-to-date understanding of industry trends, competitive activity, and technical developments that effect target markets and customers.</li>
<li>Maintains and develops relationships with key personnel at accounts. Effectively communicates updates and changes to product, pricing, and account initiatives.</li>
<li>Works cohesively with Marketing, Sourcing and Engineering teams to clearly outline customer needs and requirements. Provides consistent feedback to Teams regarding customer relationships, results of sales development activities, new accounts, and market trends.</li>
<li>Establishes and maintains contacts in industry that lead to sales. Travels to and attends trade shows as required. Maintains customer profiles and other related data.</li>
<li>Shops competition and reviews trend magazines. Brings samples and ideas to Product Development to reverse engineer.</li>
<li>Challenges Marketing team to design domestic and import products at sellable price points.</li>
<li>Performs other related duties as required.</li>
</ul>
