<h2>Customer Service Representative</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<b>Description:</b><br>
The Customer Service Representative is a member of the CMS Call Center responsible for providing outstanding support to our customers as well as to our field service and sales teams. The ideal candidate will have experience working in a call center and using enterprise software to enter customer parts orders and/or service dispatches.
<br><br>
CMS Imaging, Inc. is the premier healthcare solutions provider specializing in the sales and service of diagnostic medical imaging equipment. With Service Engineers through the southeast, CMS continues to grow and expand. Founded in 1987 in Charleston, SC as an independent service organization, CMS Imaging has expanded its product line to include MRI, CT, Ultrasound, Digital X-Ray, and Advanced Fluoroscopic systems.
<br><br>
The CMS Imaging, Inc. Customer Service Representatives support our customers throughout the southeast by providing superior and world class service and achieving excellence one customer at a time.
<br><br>
<b>Requirements:</b><br>
The ideal candidate will have experience working in a call center and using enterprise software to enter customer parts orders and/or service dispatches.
