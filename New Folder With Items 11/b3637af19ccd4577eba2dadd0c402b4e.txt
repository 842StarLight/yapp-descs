<h2>Regional Safety Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>We are looking for an Regional Safety Manager to join our Seattle, WA office!</b></p>
<p>ACCO Engineered Systems has been annually recognized by a national industry magazine as one of the top-10 specialty contractors in the United States, and the leader in its mechanical contracting field for the Western United States. We have a diverse environment of skilled professionals - field construction workers, engineering, project managers, service technicians, and supporting staff - who collaborate together and make ACCO an engaging place to work</p>
<p>The Area Safety Manager ensures that the Company complies with all applicable federal, state, and local Health, Safety, and Environment (HSE) regulations. Under the direction of the Regional Safety Director, this position is responsible for the development, implementation, and auditing of safety policy across a wide spectrum of levels. This individual will directly interact with field employees, Company Management and customers. The Area Safety Manager will provide oversight management of all HSE activities in their area, provide leadership, coaching, and mentoring to all Site Safety personnel, provide all levels of the area management team with the services and technical advice needed for the proper administration of the Company’s HSE and Injury/Illness Prevention Programs. This includes supporting the company’s established network for the communication of HSE matters, managing HSE programs at job sites, supporting other Area Safety Managers and developing and maintaining a strong HSE culture within the area.</p>

<p><b>ROUTINE DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
<p>Shall ensure that all ACCO employees comply with all of ACCO’s, General Contractor and OSHA safety policies, procedures, and standards.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Shall conduct at least one comprehensive site inspect of the whole project for safety compliance. Ensure copies are distributed to the customer.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Shall notify the customer immediately of non-compliance safety hazards verbally and in writing.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Shall ensure chemical storage and material waste disposal, are followed within ACCO policies and procedures.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Shall assist foremen in completing PTP (pre-task plans) for their crew’s safe operations. With copies distributed to the customer as required.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Shall ensure all project have adequate supplies of personal protective equipment for employees.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Shall ensure foremen are monitoring daily inspection for motor vehicles and mechanized equipment.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Shall assist foremen in the inspection of tools, materials, and supplies.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Shall assist project managers with the project’s policies and procedures as they relate to safety and environmental issues such as SWPPPS Programs and hazardous materials.</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p><b>WEEKLY</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
<p>Shall ensure weekly tailgate meetings and weekly safety inspections are being completed and that foremen turn in all appropriate paperwork. Ensure foremen distributed copies to the general contractor.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Attend weekly coordination meeting with Regional Safety Director.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Submit site inspections, training records and other safety documentation to the Regional Safety Director.</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p><b>MONTHLY</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
<p>Shall inspect fire extinguishers monthly and documented appropriately.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Shall assist foremen and crews in monthly inspections of Harness/Lanyard/Rigging and document findings.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Shall perform at least once every six months an all hands crisis management drill (emergency evacuation drills).</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p><b>AS NEEDED</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
<p>Assist foremen in the safety and health orientation process for all new employees.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Schedule, conduct, and document additional safety meetings due to unsafe acts or conditions on site.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Shall assist foremen in completing PTP (pre-task plans) for high hazard activities, (crane lifts, trenching, confined space entries, etc.) Ensure foremen distribute copies to the general contractor.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Shall perform, assist, arrange and document employee training in a scissor lift, forklift, grade-all, boom lift, powder actuated tools, hazard communication, heat stress awareness, personal protection equipment, fall protection, scaffolding usage and procedures, ladders usage and procedures, CPR/First Aid and Bloodborne Pathogens. Ensure copies of training are distributed to the customer as required.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Shall assist foremen in updating the site’s MSDS book with new chemicals brought on to the site. Ensure new MSDS’s are distributed to the customer.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Shall investigate independently all near misses, injuries and mobile equipment accidents on site and submit a report to the Regional Safety Director.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Shall assist foremen in writing all reports of near misses, injuries and mobile equipment accidents on site. Ensure copies of reports are distributed to the general contractor.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Shall report all near misses, injuries and property damage incidents within 8 hours of the incident to ACCO Corporate Office. This must also include an initial report of the incident. Ensure reporting and copies of the incident are distributed to the customer within their required time frame.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Ensure all required Federal, State, and ACCO posting are in place on job site along with Site Medical Plan, Heat Illness Plan, Evacuation Plan and all other posting required by law.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Shall assist site safety coordinators when requested.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Shall assume the role of site safety coordinator when site safety coordinator is on vacation or sick from their assigned projects as required.</p>
</li>
</ul>
