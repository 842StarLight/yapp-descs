<div><b>
Description</b></div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div>The Price Analytics &amp; Planning Manager has ownership for establishing pricing strategies, action plan, analytics to maximize price realization for ASCO and ASCO Numatics brands in the Americas region as well as Global Industrial Automation. This position will develop and present pricing recommendations to Sales and Marketing and establish annual pricing plan and related initiatives. The Price Analytics &amp; Planning Manager develops price analytical reports (customer/channel/products) and enforcement of sales department price related policies, identify and eliminate price leakage through process improvement, create/improve price management tools and track performance of key pricing metrics. This role is also responsible for all corporate planning and reporting activities (Board Meetings, Financial Reviews, Strategy Reviews, etc...)</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div><b>Responsibilities</b></div>
<div>
1. Price Management &amp; Analysis</div>
<div>
<ul>
<li>Owner of Americas regional pricing for ASCO &amp; ASCO Numatics brands and Industrial Automation Global pricing lead.</li>
<li>
Determine methodology to analyze historical pricing information to determine the best price points for the business. This activity is synchronized with Sales and Product Marketing.</li>
<li>
Leverage information from net price management tool and historical sales figures by channel (direct, distribution, e-business), segment, type of customers (OEM, End Users, Contractors…), ABC Customer segmentation.</li>
<li>
Identify the needs to help the sales force get price and develop/implement tools with the IT team: net price management tool and pricing analytics software.</li>
<li>
Works with sales to optimize price realization and gross profit. Organize regular meeting with sales directors, customer service to define actions, processes and track execution and results.</li>
<li>
Mine data, analyze and interpret results using standard statistical tools and techniques. Provide insight on pricing, identify actions.</li>
<li>
Coordinate with Americas data manager/analysts. Use data analysis techniques to identify meaningful relationships, or trends from complex data sets.</li>
<li>
Proactively engage the Product Managers to develop the overall product series pricing strategy by providing recommendations on how to optimize price realization through strategic pricing actions (new products, obsolescence, mid-year stealth increase…). Power and Risk for product portfolio. List Price increase by product line.</li>
<li>
Identify and track key pricing metrics to improve visibility of results.</li>
<li>
Pricing program execution, review and reporting - update Price milestones.</li>
<li>
Training for sales: Best Practices &amp; Market Pricing Conditions</li>
<li>
Reporting: price exhibit 128 and exhibit 261</li>
<li>
Organize and lead Pricing Governance meeting and Pricing Sales Force review</li>
<li>
Communicate with other regions (EMEA, AP) and leverage best practices</li>
</ul>
