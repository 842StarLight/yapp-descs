<p>About NCR Corporation: NCR Corporation (NYSE: NCR) is a global technology company leading how the world connects, interacts and transacts with business. NCR's assisted- and self-service solutions and comprehensive support services address the needs of retail, financial, travel, healthcare, hospitality, entertainment, gaming and public sector organizations in more than 100 countries. NCR (www.ncr.com) is headquartered in Duluth, Georgia, USA.</p>
<p>NCR Local PA Office specializes in Restaurant Point of Sale Systems. We are offering a great opportunity in a fast paced environment for a highly motivated and organized team player.</p>
<p>Primary responsibilities include: Education of equipment (hardware and software), staging of equipment, installing equipment, training customers on hardware and software, troubleshooting equipment and addressing all questions, concerns and service issues. Requirements include: Minimum 1 year experience designing and implementing Windows networks. Some restaurant experience preferred, but not required. We are willing to train the right candidates on all software we currently sell. Qualified candidates must be able to work independently and enjoy working with others. Travel is required for on-site training and service calls. Some evening hours may be required as well as participating in a rotating week night/weekend on-call schedule.</p>
<p>We offer competitive salaries, reimbursements for travel, and benefits after the first 90 days of employment. We encourage creative thinking so you won't find a micro-manager here.</p>
<p>BASIC QUALIFICATIONS: Bachelor's degree highly preferred. Alternatively, High School Diploma with significant, relevant experience considered. Exceptional communication and presentation skills, both verbal and written Highly motivated and driven to succeed Strong relationship-building skills Excellent problem-solving skills Objectivity and ability to make sound decisions Aptitude to learn quickly and perform well under pressure Ability to work independently yet within company guidelines High level of integrity, confidentiality and professional maturity Ability to travel within local or regional territory 50% - 100% of time.</p>
