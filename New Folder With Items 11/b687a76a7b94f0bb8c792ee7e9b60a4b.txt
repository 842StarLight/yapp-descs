<div><b>#CDC</b></div>
<div>Coors Distributing Company (CDC) is one of the nation’s leading beer distributors based out of Denver, CO. We are the critical link between our brewery suppliers that produce the beer and the retail outlets where they are sold, as well as the restaurants and bars where they are consumed. We were founded in 1971 and became a MillerCoors LLC in 2008 as part of the joint venture between Miller Brewing Company and Coors Brewing Company, and we remain the only company owned distributor for MillerCoors. Major brand acquisitions occurred in January of 2010 making Coors Distributing Company one of the top 25 largest beer distributors in the country with over 30 suppliers, roughly 14 million cases per year, and nearly 400 employees.</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div><b>The Headlines:</b></div>
<div>In the role of <b>Inventory Manager</b> working in <b>Denver, CO</b> you will be part of the Inventory department in Operations. This person will be responsible for monitoring the flow and control of all items inventoried and stored within the secured areas of the product warehouse to ensure accuracy. The supervisor maintains accountability for inventory accuracy and control, to include product, dunnage, pallets, empty kegs, and glassware; also maintains responsibility for processing all breakage and repackaging of product.</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div>This position reports to <b>Sr. Operations Manager</b> and works closely with a variety of internal and external partners, including vendors and suppliers and Warehouse Operations.</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div><b>The Responsibilities:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
<div>Direct, and as necessary, develop and evolve all inventory-related processes and procedures.</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Ensure variances are promptly researched/corrections made following established procedures.</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Confirm all bi-annual inventories are scheduled and completed; coordinate supplier’s audits and inventories required by the brewery.</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Perform analysis to determine proper product storage placement in the bulk warehouse; responsible for auditing storage practices and procedures, and working with the warehouse receiving team to ensure proper processes are being followed.</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Proactively manage on-hand inventories, with emphasis on understanding potential overages, out-of-stocks, date issues or shortages that need attention.</div>
</li>
</ul>
<ul><li>
<div>Collaborate with accounting personnel to research and adjust discrepancies between physical count and book inventory; responsible for audit, reconciling inventory balance, and systematically improve inventory controls accuracy, with the objective of reducing write-offs.</div>
<br>
</li></ul>
<div></div>
