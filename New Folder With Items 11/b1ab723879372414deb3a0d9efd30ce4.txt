<h2>Wireless Field Engineer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<div>Ann Arbor, MI</div>
<div><div>
<p><b>
Summary</b></p>
<p>
Builds relationships with clients; works with clients on tasks defined by Technical Service Manager</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Essential Duties and Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Work as part of a team on large scale wireless site surveys</li>
<li>Collect and analyze daily survey data in the field, ensuring that the RF survey requirements are being met</li>
<li>Author full site survey reports <i>based</i> on survey data using Visio and Microsoft Word &amp; Adobe Acrobat</li>
<li>Interact with customers IT teams daily – requires strong communication skills</li>
<li>Monitor ongoing progress and make recommendations for improvement in efficiency.</li>
<li>Communicates with Sales Support Representatives and Operations team to check on progress of existing orders and projects.</li>
<li>Track time involvement in project for billable hours.</li>
<li>Works with Technical Services Manager to create strategic plans for training.</li>
<li>Creates documentation for Customer and follows up with Customer and Sales Team for turnover of documentation</li>
<li>Demonstrates and actively promotes an understanding and commitment to the mission of CTC Tech through performing behaviors consistent with the organization’s values.</li>
<li>Supports and conducts self in a manner consistent with client service expectations.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p>
</p>
<p><b>Qualifications:</b></p>
<p>
To perform this <i>job</i> successfully, an individual must be able to perform each essential duty satisfactorily. The requirements listed below are representative of the knowledge, skill, and/or ability required. Reasonable accommodations may be made to enable individuals with disabilities to perform the essential functions.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Education/</b><b><i>Experience</i></b><b>/Technical Requirements/Certifications</b></p>
<p><b>
Education:</b></p>
<p>
Bachelor’s degree in computer science, business, management disciplines, or related field.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b><i>
Experience</i></b><b> / Technical Requirements:</b></p>
<p>
Must have: <i>Experience</i> with Site Survey software Ekahau or AirMagnet</p>
<ul>
<li>Understanding requirements for WiFi site surveys and documentation</li>
<li>Understanding of VoWiFi, <i>Location</i> Tracking</li>
<li>Understanding of WLC and Access point models WLAN Controller configuration and tuning</li>
<li>Meraki AP configuration and tuning</li>
<li>Strong documentation skills using Microsoft Visio, Word, and Excel</li>
<li>Routing Protocols – OSPF, EIGRP, BGP L2 / L3</li>
<li>Architecture and Design Spanning Tree Protocol VSS, HSRP, VRRP, GLBP</li>
</ul>
<p>May consider an equivalent combination of education and <i>experience</i>.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Other Skills and Abilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Cisco Route/Switch/Wireless certifications a plus</li>
<li>Scissor and/or Boom Lift <i>Experience</i>
</li>
<li>Excellent communication skills – oral and written presentation abilities.</li>
<li>Strong client interaction skills.</li>
<li>Possess high degree of self-motivation and drive.</li>
<li>Self-starter with excellent organizational, administrative and interpersonal skills.</li>
<li>Works well with team members</li>
<li>Capacity to flourish in competitive environment.</li>
<li>Resilient with ability to cope with rejection.</li>
<li>Leads with confident and determined approach.</li>
<br>
</ul>
