<h2>Solutions Engineer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p>The Solutions Engineer serves as the advocate for the client by developing applications for consumer electronics manufactures who utilize the client company technology. Responsibilities include interacting with potential and existing customers to understand needs and propose solutions based on existing technology or help to define requirements for new product development by working with the sales team to quote.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Essential Job Functions - Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Manage the technical proposal process and ensure all information and recommendations regarding customer requirements are accurate, defined, and detailed.</li>
<li>Respond to inquiries via phone and email from customers and the sales team.</li>
<li>Oversee technical management of customer visits and product demonstrations.</li>
<li>Develop long-term relationships with customers through managing and interpreting product requirements.</li>
<li>Provide technical assistance and product education for potential customers.</li>
<li>Manage proof of concept testing, including designing and executing experiments, writing test procedures, supervising resources, and providing timely reporting on test completion.</li>
<li>Collaborate with team members to estimate engineering resources needed to deliver defined solutions.</li>
<li>Create proposals outlining systems requirements and specifications.</li>
<li>Transfer quoted sales solutions to the engineering group and oversee the development and production process to ensure specifications are met.</li>
<li>Attend sales forecasting and strategy meetings.</li>
<li>Maintain expert level knowledge of company products and industry standards in order to present at marketing events and training the sales team.</li>
<br>
</ul>
