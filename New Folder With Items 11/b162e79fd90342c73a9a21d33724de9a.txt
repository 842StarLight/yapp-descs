<p><b>What will you contribute?
</b><br>
<br>
An excellent opportunity for an experienced product manager to join Finastra’s Financial Messaging group based in Baden, Switzerland as a Senior Product Manager taking special responsibility for Swiss Market Infrastructures and Swiss customer requirements for both established and innovation product offerings.
</p>
<p>You will report to the head of product for Finastra’s Financial Messaging and Services business unit and work closely with colleagues in the product and R&amp;D teams in addition to other stakeholders: customers, sales/pre-sales, service bureau operations, project delivery and executive management.
<br>
<br>
<b>Responsibilities &amp; Deliverables:
</b><br>
<br>
Your deliverables will include, but are not limited to, the following:
</p>
<p>· Build and maintain a strong practical knowledge of the product functionality and deployment architecture.
</p>
<p>· Representing the customer, interfacing and engaging with customers and other stakeholders.
</p>
<p>· Backlog management and iteration planning: maintaining a well-groomed Product Backlog according to business value for SIC, EuroSIC and SECOM.
</p>
<p>· Act as Product Owner for Swiss market functionality in one or more Agile Scrum teams: provide vision and direction to the R&amp;D team and actively work to mitigate impediments that might otherwise impact successful team completion of release and sprint goals.
</p>
<p>· Contribute to innovation planning, with focus on the needs of the Swiss market
</p>
<p>· Produce / contribute to and maintain product collateral – product release notes, user guides, product fliers, product overviews, product presales presentations, roadmap communications.
</p>
<p>· Scrum and resource planning with R&amp;D
</p>
<p>· Contribute to roadmap planning across all Financial Messaging products.
</p>
<p>· Contribute as a product SME to Sales and Account Management activities.<br>
</p>
<p><br>
<b>Required &amp; Desired Experience:
</b></p>
<p>· Fluent in German and English for written and verbal communication
</p>
<p>· Experienced with products services providing connectivity to some or all of the following market infrastructures: SWIFT, SIC, EuroSIC, SECOM, Fed, CHIPS
</p>
<p>· Experience with Agile development and Scrum/Kanban processes
</p>
<p>· Recent strong experience in a Product Owner / Manager role. Minimum 5 years’ experience.
</p>
<p>· Experience capturing functional requirements in the form User Stories and Epics.
</p>
<p>· Experience with hosted and/or multi-tenant software delivered SaaS or Cloud that utilise service orientated architecture and that present published API's
</p>
<p>· Experience with enterprise-grade software products that can perform, scale, and integrate in a payments or financial messaging enterprise ecosystem.
</p>
