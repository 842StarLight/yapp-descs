<b>About the Opportunity</b><p></p>
<p>
A law firm in New Jersey is currently seeking an experienced Attorney to join their staff as their new Senior Life Sciences Attorney. In this role, the Senior Life Sciences Attorney will be responsible for handling an array of health insurance and managed care related issues on behalf of pharmacy &amp; Healthcare clients, including defending pharmacy benefit manager (PBM) and insurance audits, investigations and network terminations. Apply today!</p>

Job Description</b><p></p>
<p>
The Senior Life Sciences Attorney will be responsible for:</p>
<ul>
<li>Handling an interesting and diverse docket of pharmacy and life sciences regulatory issues</li>
<li>Assessing operational structure and processes of pharmacies and healthcare / life sciences companies</li>
<li>Analyzing and structuring arrangements among Life Sciences stakeholders</li>
<li>Drafting policies and procedures regarding marketing activities related to pharmacies and drug products</li>
<li>Reviewing payor, sale, software licensing, and central fill and shared services agreements</li>
<li>Assisting pharmacies in obtaining state licensure and assisting in the representation of clients before the state licensing boards</li>
<li>Handling other duties, as needed</li>
</ul>
