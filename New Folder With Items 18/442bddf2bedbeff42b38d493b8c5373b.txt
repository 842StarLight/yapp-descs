<h2>Sr. Director, IT</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Mood Media Corporation is a leading in-store media specialist that uses a mix of music, visual and scent media to help its clients communicate with consumers with a view to driving incremental sales at the point-of-purchase. Operating through its two principal divisions; In-Store Media and Retail Point-of-Purchase; Mood Media Corporation works with more than 800 retail chains in more than 40 countries throughout North America, Europe, Asia and Australia.</p>
<p>
SR. DIRECTOR, IT</p>
<p>
The Senior Director Information Technology role will direct the planning and implementation of strategic and tactical enterprise IT initiatives across the company focusing on cost effectiveness, efficiency, service, and data quality. They will act as a critical leader and driver of enterprise transformation, partnering with the GVP of IT in achieving goals and objectives. This role will identify new technologies, as appropriate; to further strengthen Mood's competitive position while continuing to improve customer and employee experiences. This role will be responsible for developing and managing IT infrastructure and projects across the company as well as managing the IT Helpdesk and back office deliverables to the Company. The Senior Director of Information Technology will report to the Global VP of Information Technology.</p>
<p><b>
Essential Job Functions and Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Oversee the technological infrastructure (including networks and website best practices) to ensure optimal performance</li>
<li>Responsible for overseeing the creation and administration of continuity plans to execute in the event of a critical outage, disaster, or cyber security incident that will keep the company functional in the event of a crisis</li>
<li>Monitor changes or advancements in technology to discover ways to improve service delivery, both internally and externally</li>
<li>Design and customize technological systems and platforms to improve business performance</li>
<li>Drive and be actively engaged in vendor contract negotiations around new assets such as computers, laptops, software, licenses, etc.</li>
<li>Stay abreast and advise senior leadership of technology risks, issues, solutions, compliance and regulatory mandates</li>
<li>Ensure proper training and development including but not limited to; security awareness and IT policy creation</li>
<li>Direct and organize IT-related projects</li>
<li>Responsible for the enterprise-wide applications (i.e., SAP, SharePoint, etc. ) including prioritizing and executing enhancements and upgrades</li>
<li>Manages budget and controls expenses effectively for assigned areas of responsibility</li>
<li>Ability to convert business strategy to technology execution</li>
<li>Experience with server hosting, cloud computing, and cyber security</li>
<li>Create vision and objectives for core operational capabilities that enable and enhance the delivery of key performance objectives</li>
<li>Create vision and objectives for transformation of IT processes, capabilities and operating model in support of business transformation vision to achieve the status of premier technology organization</li>
<li>Deliver safe, secure and efficient solutions for IT asset management, central user authority system and integration, automated onboarding and offboarding processes across all departments, laptop hardware and software management, antivirus software solutions.</li>
<li>Ensure email, messaging and productivity tools (G-suite) are robust and centrally managed for security, stability and overall governed for compliance purposes</li>
<li>Direct Management of North American Helpdesk staff and function</li>
<li>Oversight North American office spaces and new hire build-outs throughout the estate</li>
<li>Oversight and management of Helpdesk management tools</li>
<li>Manage technology obsolescence and vendor risks</li>
<li>Manage and enhance risk management strategy, policies, processes, and controls to mitigate organization exposure</li>
<li>Attract, develop, and retain top talent to continually improve IT capabilities necessary to increase performance of the organization</li>
<li>Define and maintain an IT core competency strategy and plan to guide talent investments and focus</li>
<li>Define and execute forward-looking plans to develop relevant new technology skills and capabilities</li>
<li>Ensure that disaster recovery and business continuity plans are in place and tested</li>
<li>Oversee identity and access policies</li>
<li>Maintain a current understanding of the IT threat landscape for the industry</li>
<li>Translate that knowledge to identification of risks and actionable plans to protect the business</li>
<li>Ensure cyber security policies and procedures are communicated to all personnel and that compliance is enforced</li>
<li>Supervisory Responsibilites</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Knowledge/Skills/Abilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Analyze, select and implement suitable technology for company projects and initiatives</li>
<li>Expert level knowledge and understanding of technology across various domains</li>
<li>Successful history of working in and leading teams to meet business needs</li>
<li>Must have strong problem solving and decision-making skills and good business acumen</li>
<li>Ability to connect business and technology strategy</li>
<li>Strategic decision-making</li>
<li>Success building and mentoring a team that thrives</li>
<li>Driving results by aligning to thoughtful performance measures</li>
<li>Success building, cultivating, and sustaining partnerships</li>
<li>Excellent communication skills (written and verbal)</li>
<li>Strong vendor/partner contracting and negotiation skills required</li>
<li>Work with cross-functional teams internally and externally to explain, understand, manage, and implement complex partnerships and projects</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Qualifications:</b></p>
<ul>

<li>Minimum of 10+ years of relevant IT experience</li>
<li>5 + years leadership/management experience</li>
</ul>
