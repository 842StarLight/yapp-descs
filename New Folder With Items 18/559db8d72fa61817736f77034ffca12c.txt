<b>Position Summary:</b><br>
Founded in 1947 CEB is one of California's oldest and most prestigious legal information and continuing legal education providers. CEB publishes judgments of California case law, practical guidance for attorneys and, CLE all in a variety of practice areas and formats e.g. print, online and, in-person.<br>
<br>
As Product Manager, Primary Law &amp; Secondary Sources, you will drive the vision, product strategy, and execution for CEB's core digital legal research products that serve the California legal community. CEB is an award-winning provider of California-focused practice guides, and other secondary source content. Our staff content development attorneys work with recognized experts throughout the state to create, publish and deliver digital access to more than 120 regularly updated practice guides in substantive areas including business law, civil litigation and torts, criminal law, employment law &amp; workers' compensation, trusts and estates, family law, and real property.<br>
<br>
You will collaborate with a cross-functional team, including staff content-development attorneys, marketing, sales and development to execute on a roadmap that is a balanced portfolio of big bets, tactical changes, and core refinements on the product &amp; feature portfolio for CEB's new primary law and secondary source products. Your market and customer research, through direct interaction with practicing attorneys across the state, especially those who are solo practitioners or working at small firms, will inform how we can grow and improve our products and share of market.<br>
