<p><b>Overview</b></p>
<p>RJ Health is a data analytics company providing Payors, PBMs, Providers, and Service organizations with an independent resource for HCPCS-coded drug data for determining the appropriate price of professionally-administered specialty drugs. RJ Health maintains proprietary pricing and clinical coding data and underlying methodology that is licensed by over 70% of mid and major US Health Plans in determining the appropriate reimbursement rate for Physician-administered specialty drugs. Our clients rely on RJ Health to be the independent arbiter of Medical claim reimbursement rates, as well as acceptable dosage and diagnosis criteria for both FDA-approved and Compendia uses.</p>
<p>RJ Health is looking to welcome a qualified <b>Outbound Communication Executive</b> to our successful sales team! This essential position in Inside Sales needs to be filled by an applicant who is ambitious, confident, organized and who has an “never quit” mindset.</p>
<p>Our accomplished, modern organization offers a <i>generous base pay, c</i>ompetitive incentives, <i>hands on training and the opportunity to develop a professional career in an exciting work environment</i>. We want you to grow with us!</p>
<p><b><i>Job Responsibilities: </i></b></p>
<ul>
<li>Outreach via phone and email to set meetings, conduct introductory calls and develop/qualify leads from past clients &amp; prospective clients for Enterprise Sales Team</li>
<li>Complete outbound (email/phone) tasks assigned by Marketing Automation tool generated from outreach/promotional campaigns</li>
<li>Meet daily, weekly and monthly key performance indicators</li>
<li>Utilize prospecting tools like LinkedIn Sales Navigator, Dux Soup, Google Search and other sources to build target lists</li>
<li>Update and maintain daily activity with key information in Salesforce (CRM/Tracking software)</li>
<li>Participate in Sales and Marketing meetings to provide feedback to help refine messaging and account level targets</li>
<li>Assists Marketing in the development and implementation of promotional campaigns targeting new customers and specific business segments</li>
</ul>
