<h2>Senior Release Engineer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<div>
<div>As a leading worldwide provider of navigation devices and wearable technology, our focus is on developing, designing and supporting superior products. Our advanced technology promotes performance, safety and ease of use in every market we serve. That makes us an engineering and technology company with a keen focus on recruiting talent in those fields. But the same vertical integration business model that keeps all design and manufacturing processes in-house also gives us the incentive to hire top performers from different backgrounds, including operations, finance and accounting, information technology, sales, marketing and communications.</div>
<div>
<br>
Associates get the royal treatment at Garmin. Regardless if you’re a newbie or a veteran, engineers dream it, design it and use it. Here, you will be surrounded by others who share the same passion about what they do every day. We offer a relaxed team environment (no dress code!) where advancement is found from sharing knowledge, not hiding it! While we promise you a lot of responsibility, you also will have the opportunity to have ownership over projects…oh, and we promise you there will be some fun mixed in there, too!</div>
<div>
<br>
We are looking for a full-time Senior Release Engineer for our headquarters in the greater Kansas City area. In this role, you will ensure the integrity of Garmin's production environment is maintained and that all is released into the environment is well-organized, communicated, and managed. You will provide mentoring and training to less experienced Release Engineers. Other essential functions include:</div>
<div></div>
<ul>
<li>
<br>
Coordinate and oversee basic to complex releases into Garmin's production environment</li>
<li>
Coordinate and oversee real-time monitoring to ensure stability of Garmin software systems</li>
<li>
Provide coordination and oversight of the production environment following a release to ensure issues are addressed in a timely manner</li>
<li>
Drive process improvements throughout the project cycle and release process, encouraging standardization, consistency, repeatability, and predictability</li>
<li>
Create and improve processes and procedures for development teams to utilize when releasing applications, ensuring the integrity of environments</li>
<li>
Proactively investigate issues to eliminate recurrence</li>
<li>
Produce and analyze performance and problem reports on a regular basis to assist engineers with troubleshooting and software improvement</li>
<li>
Build and maintain tools of all levels of complexity to automate the generation of reports for real-time and stored data</li>
<li>
Build and maintain tools of all levels of complexity to automate the processes that introduce change to the environment.</li>
<li>
Proactively perform complex gating activities to ensure successful deployments and environment availability</li>
<li>
Perform and mentor others with complex problem resolution and debugging (including code-level debugging)</li>
<li>
Establish and provide training on processes and procedures for development teams to utilize when releasing applications into production, ensuring the integrity of the production environment</li>
<li>
Communicate release information effectively to team members and release stakeholders</li>
<li>
Work with Software Engineers to correlate complex production issues to recent releases</li>
<li>
May be responsible for planning own technical work and mentoring other release engineers (Release Engineer 1 &amp; 2)</li>
<li>
Effectively balance quality, quantity and complexity in work output</li>
<li>
Independently determine general approach and specific solutions to technical problems</li>
<li>
Provides reliable solution to a wide range of difficult problems using sound problem solving techniques</li>
<li>
Apply good judgment in setting schedules and risk taking</li>
<li>
Exemplify Garmin’s Mission Statement, Vision, Values and Quality Policy and proactively works to improve Garmin’s image and culture</li>
<li>
Review product and/or application information including manuals and brochures for technical accuracy</li>
<li>
Participate in product and/or application definition activities including feature analysis and system tradeoffs</li>
<li>
Participate as needed with on-call rotation and support of local and remote locations</li>
<li>
Perform other duties as necessary</li>
</ul>
</div>
<p></p>
<div>
<b>
Qualifications</b><div>
</div>
<div>
Qualified candidates possess Bachelor of Science in Electrical Engineering, Software Engineering or Computer Engineering AND a minimum of 5 years relevant experience performing a substantially similar Applications Engineering role OR an equivalent combination of education and relevant experience. Other requirements include:</div>
<div></div>
<ul>
<li>
<br>
Excellent academics (cumulative GPA greater than or equal to 3.0 as a general rule)</li>
<li>
Demonstrate advanced proficiency in supporting Linux and/or Windows based server installations based on environment</li>
<li>
Demonstrate advanced skill in Linux and/or Windows based scripting</li>
<li>
Previous work experience using version control system such as GIT / Subversion.</li>
<li>
Demonstrate proficient advanced knowledge of analysis, design and configuration of complex multi-tiered server applications</li>
<li>
Demonstrated knowledge of SDLC, Quality Standards, and Best Practices</li>
<li>
Demonstrated strong and effective verbal, written, and interpersonal communication skills</li>
<li>
Must be team-oriented, possess a positive attitude and work well with others</li>
<li>
Demonstrate proficient project management, organizational and time management skills with a proven track record in meeting timelines and customer expectations</li>
<li>
Demonstrate effective discretion, judgment and decision making abilities</li>
<li>
Demonstrate advanced problem solving and debugging skills</li>
<li>
Desirable Qualifications Include:</li>
<li>
Experience with build and deployment automation</li>
<li>
Experience with automated testing tools and testing procedures</li>
<li>
Experience with application appropriate language such as Java /J2EE or C#</li>
</ul>
