<h2>Director of Enterprise Demand Planning</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>USANA Health Sciences is seeking a detail oriented and skilled leader to join our supply chain and forecasting teams as a director of enterprise demand planning. In this position, you will manage a team of professionals to efficiently and accurately forecast, produce and distribute products for a multinational nutritional company.</p>
<p>As USANA’s Director of Enterprise Demand Planning, you will oversee forecasting, demand planning, inventory management, and mitigate distribution costs. Your ability to effectively lead your team will be instrumental to your success. In this position, you will manage change control over a dynamic product offering, direct operational efforts for new product and market launches and act as USANA’s expert for importing and exporting goods. As you develop in this position, your ability to coordinate with various teams across USANA will be instrumental to your success.</p>
<p>A successful individual in this position will exemplify USANA’s core values and will be highly motivated to create positive changes.</p>
<p><b>Essential Functions</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Oversee supply chain and materials management activities, including Demand Planning and Inventory Management</li>
<li>Maintain ERP item attributes to ensure accurate supply planning and inventory management parameters within the corporate supply chain applications</li>
<li>Identify and implement a performance dashboard of KPIs necessary to clearly identify functional performance and opportunities for improvement</li>
<li>Perform strategic assessment of company and customer freight carriers, costs, and load and lanes optimization</li>
<li>Align freight management and transportation with customer requirements to ensure high service levels and cost minimization</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Leads and executes development of demand forecasts for inline and near launch products for all global Usana markets, including development of forecast models and methodology, statistical trending, development and alignment of forecast assumptions and dissemination of performance and forecast tracking data and metrics</li>
<li>Collects, analyzes and synthesizes market information from diverse sources to provide insight on Brand and Market Performance and a cohesive and clear manner</li>
<li>Monitors competitive landscape and proactively identifies and models events which may have an impact on overall sales performance and incorporates into forecasts as appropriate</li>
<li>Participate in promotional post-mortems and be accountable for the incorporation of findings into guidelines.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Qualifications/Knowledge, Skills, and Abilities Requirements</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree or displayed abilities through experience and certifications; MBA or advanced degree a plus</li>
<li>10+ years relevant work experience, 5 years in a leadership or management position</li>
<li>Certifications preferred: APICS CPIM, Certified Supply Chain Professional (CSCP), Certified Professional in Supply Management (CPSM), SCPro from the Council of Supply Chain Management Professionals (CSCMP), Global Business Management Certificate</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Excellent working knowledge of Oracle ERP planning modules or other material planning, MRP, software required</li>
<li>Intimately familiar with the international shipping and domestic freight distribution</li>
</ul>
