<h2>Buyer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Description:
</p>
<p>As part of a small, passionate and accomplished team of experts, you will perform day to day tactical purchasing functions for various spaceflight systems. You will share in the team’s impact on all aspects of supplier communications, product pricing, and quality, and build schedule. This position will directly impact the history of space exploration and will require your dedicated commitment and detailed attention towards safe and repeatable spaceflight.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Responsibilities:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Perform day to day procurement activities supporting internal production and manufacturing, specifically through the purchase of indirect items that are consumed on the production floor and support services.
</li>
<li>Coordinates purchasing the material and services requirements for internal production needs
</li>
<li>Works with production floor managers and buyers to select suppliers for bid and award to achieve company objectives
</li>
<li>Prepares purchase orders in Costpoint and Coupa software
</li>
<li>Ensures supplier's documentation such as terms and conditions and NDAs are ready prior to engagement
</li>
<li>Manages orders with suppliers, monitors backlog and daily order status report, adjusts schedules as needed, manage/modify POs with overdue shipments and open orders
</li>
<li>Works with logistics team to ensure timely and economical deliveries of all materials to production location
</li>
<li>Ensure that all requirements for purchasing, storage, and transportation of materials are adhered to
</li>
<li>Monitors suppliers’ performance and prepares reports and other records
</li>
<li>Resolves discrepancies related to purchasing, delivery, receiving, accounts payable invoices, and payment to the supplier
</li>
<li>Expedites materials for shortage management
</li>
<li>Coordinate returns to vendor for discrepant material from receiving inspection
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>

</li>
<li>Strong skills in Microsoft Outlook and Windows programs (Excel, Word, Powerpoint)
</li>
<li>Ability to work effectively in cross-functional teams, and independently
</li>
<li>Strong written and verbal communication skills
</li>

</li>
