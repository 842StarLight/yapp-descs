Norcross, Georgia</p>
<p>Continuing to invest in our future, we have an opening for a results-driven <b>VP, Marketing </b>to play a critical role on our Surgical team.</p>
<p>
As the <b>VP, Marketing</b>, you’ll use your expertise to develop and implement comprehensive business plans and strategies; and drive tactical execution, ensuring that our business objectives and financial metrics are achieved. You'll also have the opportunity to manage, mentor, and motivate a high performing marketing team. This leadership role is responsible for managing a marketing budget, ensuring alignment with our P&amp;L commitments.
</p>
<p><b>Your role:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Review and approve packaging, displays, promotions, digital content, and advertising campaigns.</li>
<li>Work closely with the VP/GM, Sales Leadership, and cross-functional partners to create and execute synchronized sales and marketing plans.
</li>
<li>Conduct market research as necessary to capture Voice of Customer and support key programs &amp; initiatives.
</li>
<li>Take an active role as a key stakeholder in the new product development process, ensuring the company’s product portfolio will meet market needs and deliver both top and bottom line growth.
</li>
<li>Evaluate business trends and financial results, and initiates changes or additions in overall marketing strategies and policies to ensure achievement of established business plans.</li>
<br>
</ul>

About Mölnlycke:</b></p>
<p>Mölnlycke is a world-leading medical solutions company. We design and supply solutions to enhance performance at every point of care – from the hospital to the home.
</p>
<p>We specialize in:</p>
<ul>
<li>Wound management, including dressings such as Mepilex® and Mepitel®</li>
<li>Preventing pressure ulcers: with Mepilex® Border used prophylactically and devices to help turn and re-position patients</li>
<li>Surgical solutions: including HiBi® antiseptics and Biogel® surgical gloves</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p>Mölnlycke was founded in 1849. Nowadays our solutions are available in around 100 countries; we’re the number one global provider of advanced wound care and single-use surgical products; and we’re Europe's largest provider of customized trays. Our headquarters are in Gothenburg, Sweden and we have about 7,500 employees around the world.</p>
