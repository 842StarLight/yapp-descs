<h2>Director of Sales and Marketing</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>ProMaxima is a commercial fitness equipment manufacturing company located in SW Houston. We are seeking a motivated and dynamic individual to manage all sales and marketing efforts working directly for the owner of the company. ProMaxima is an established privately held company that is committed to building a quality product at an affordable price and providing excellent customer service to our customers located throughout the United States.</p>
<p>The director of sales and marketing is responsible for planning and implementing sales, marketing and product development programs, both short and long range, targeted toward existing customers and new markets.</p>
<p>Requirements and Responsibilities Include:</p>
<ul>
<li>Review and evaluate the company’s current sales and marketing strategies and initiatives</li>
<li>Review and evaluate the adequacy and structure of the existing sales team</li>
<li>Train existing sales force to develop more highly functioning and productive sales people</li>
<li>Lead and motivate sales staff to achieve weekly, monthly and yearly sales goals</li>
<li>Accomplish marketing and sales objectives by planning, developing, implementing, and evaluating advertising</li>
<li>Represent company at trade shows, associations and meetings to promote products</li>
<li>Build brand awareness of the company and its products and direct social media efforts</li>
<li>Design and implementing a strategic sales plan to that expands the company's customer base and grows its presence with existing core customer markets.</li>
</ul>
<p>Skill and Abilities:</p>
<ul>
<li>Experience in the fitness equipment industry or related fields a major plus</li>
<li>A proven track record of success</li>
<li>Take ownership for their performance and for their team’s performance</li>
<li>Results oriented</li>
<li>Strong knowledge of sales, management and motivational techniques</li>
<li>Ability to recruit and train a sales and support team</li>
<li>Outstanding organizational, leadership, negotiation and time management skills</li>
<li>Self-motivated and with a strong attention to detail</li>
<li>Able to work with a flexible schedule which may include weekends as needed</li>
</ul>
<p>Job Type: Full-time</p>
<p>Benefits:</p>
<ul>
<li>Health insurance</li>
<li>Dental insurance</li>
<li>Vision insurance</li>
<li>Paid time off</li>
</ul>
</div>
