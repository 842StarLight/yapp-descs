<p><b>Purpose:
</b></p>
<p>~ Responsible for all Regional Distribution activities. This includes Vehicle Distribution, Logistics, Regional Fleet sales and Company Cars. The Regional Distribution Manager job is the most critical of all Regional staff positions due to the massive impact this individual has on HMA’s daily business operations.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Major Responsibilities:
</b></p>
<p>~ Develop and order the optimal product mix for maximum turn-rate.
</p>
<p>~ Monitor and control the allocation process to assure timely and equitable distribution.
</p>
<p>~ Coordinate all activities of port operations to ensure vehicles are expediently shipped.
</p>
<p>~ Coordinate all Regional Fleet and Service Loaner activities.
</p>
<p>~ Assist Regional Sales Manager with other activities, i.e., auto shows, sales training, HDAA activities.
</p>
<p>~ Supervise and direct activities of department analysts.
</p>
<p>~ Maintain monthly / yearly department expenses within established guidelines.
</p>
<p>~ Mentor and train DSMs.
</p>
<p>~ Regional source for QMF/SQL data generation.
</p>
<p>~ Regional contact to Director of Sales Operations for all inventory, allocation and product planning issues.
</p>
<p>~ Perform all other assigned duties (duties that are ancillary to this position and/or complement the essential duties) as required.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Authority:
</b></p>
<p>~ Responsible for approving Time Off Requests for regional distribution section.
</p>
<p>~ Responsible for approving Equipment/Software requests for regional distribution section.
</p>
<p>~ Responsible for completing the Performance Goals/Evaluations for regional distribution section.<br>
</p>
<p></p>

</p>
