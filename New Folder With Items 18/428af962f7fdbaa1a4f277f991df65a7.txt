<h2>Manager of Client Strategy</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p><b>What you’ll be doing...</b>
</p>
<p>As a <b>Manager of Client Strategy</b>, you will be responsible for winning, keeping and growing customers in a new market. You will be working with a cross-section of mid-market accounts - from drone service providers to corporate customers, helping them manage and scale their drone operations.
</p>
<p>You will be passionate about technology, drones, aviation, or all of the above. You’re a driven self-starter with a high level of personal integrity, who can collaborate across teams.</p>
<p>
This role is responsible for driving the adoption of Skyward, A Verizon Companies, platforms and services in the areas of Drone Operations Management. This position represents a key element of the organization which will deploy, evolve, and support the cutting-edge technology vision over the coming years.</p>
<p><b>
Key Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Responsible for driving revenue by supporting the sales of enterprise SaaS solutions to mid-market accounts.</li>
<li>
Independently capable of negotiating agreements with business stakeholders, procurement, senior management, and executives.</li>
<li>
Working closely with the Verizon sales teams to support actionable territory and account-specific growth plans and executing.</li>
<li>
Consistently meeting quarterly sales targets.</li>
<li>
Support the sales cycle from hello through negotiation and close.</li>
<li>
Prospecting and qualifying a combination of outbound and inbound prospects.</li>
<li>
Performing customer-focused product demos using web tools and in-person to prospects and customers.</li>
<li>
Developing proposals and quotes.</li>
<li>
Consistent pipeline, forecast and sales cycle management.</li>
<li>
Teaming with Customer Success to ensure customer satisfaction and business value.</li>
<li>
Providing input to Marketing, Product and leadership on market and customer trends.</li>
</ul>
