<h2>Marketing Director - Campaigns and Growth Programs</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div></div>
<div>
<div>
<div>Industry-leading tech company seeks experienced B2B Campaign and Growth Marketer. Be part of a team that is transforming cloud and IT services. Drive meaningful business through data analytics and creative campaigns. Work with the world’s biggest tech brands including AWS, Microsoft, Google...</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
Working on a global stage, the exciting role will ensure Megaport customers and partners get the most out of our platform. You will be an expert on the customer and their buying journey and help Technical Decision Makers get connected to the services they need to power their cloud-based IT solutions. As an absolute whiz at pulling insights from a broad set of data, the Marketing Director for Campaigns and Growth Programs will identify opportunities to cross-sell, up-sell, and play matchmaker between buyers and sellers of data services, including cloud, data networking, managed services, and telephony among others.
</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>A fundamental part of this role is to develop campaigns, including nurtures, communications programs, and digital and field engagement packages that resonate with customers and partners and drive increased utilization of Megaport services.
</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>Campaigns will run the gamut from digital assets and automated nurtures to field marketing and telephone scripts. Ultimately, the MD - CGP will incorporate creative thinking and novel tactics to execute campaigns that actually HELP our customers and partners achieve their business goals. This role reports directly to the Chief Marketing Officer based in San Francisco and will work cross functionally with a global team of outstanding people in Megaport’s digital and brand marketing, product, sales, and data and business analytics teams and third party vendors.
</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>As a critical function, you’ll have visibility with the executive leadership and will significantly contribute to the way Megaport, a global leader in cloud networking, is transforming the IT landscape. The MD - CGP will be measured on direct revenue impact. This role will work closely with the various Regional / Global sales teams, the Internal Sales Team, and partners that “sell with” Megaport, around the World.</div>
</div>
<div><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>What You'll Be Doing</b></h3>
<div><ul>
<li>
Work with internal data teams and utilize industry data sources to identify technology, vertical, partner, and customer trends that map to up-sell and cross-sell opportunities for Megaport services</li>
<li>
Analyze and size the revenue opportunity for driving uptake of services to support customer use cases: E.G - connecting to multi-cloud, redundant access architectures, accessing digital supply chain service providers in Finance, Digital Media, and Retail.</li>
<li>
Develop global, regional, and local campaigns and engagement programs designed to increase uptake of Megaport services</li>
<li>
Support the creation of assets and deliverables including social, product and solution collateral, EDM, sales scripting, blog copy, and special content among many others.</li>
<li>
Support Megaport and partner account management and sales across a variety of up-sell and cross-sell scenarios.</li>
<li>
Support the evolution and optimization of our product and services to align with the customer journey</li>
<li>
Own measurement and reporting of campaigns and programs, with metrics on success criteria</li>
<li>
Own continuous refinement and improvement of campaigns and programs</li>
<li>
Drive requirements for systems, services, and processes to support scaling and execution of campaigns and growth programmes</li>
<li>
Work with product team to align product and offering, with a focus on special campaigns or promotional events</li>
<li>
Create and influence the development of highly repeatable and bespoke partner marketing programs to drive maximum acquisition opportunities and oversee partner driven marketing campaigns across all stages of the campaign development process (i.e., concept, creative development, execution, measurement).</li>
<li>
Work with appropriate partners, including field marketing and alliance partners, in campaign execution and response management</li>
<li>
Own and manage campaign budgetsIdentify and report back on customer feedback, as this relates to product gaps, service aspects when compared to the market and new locations or markets for Megaport</li>
</ul></div>
</div></div>

<div>
Megaport has transformed the way IT gets connected. We're global leaders in Network as a Service (NaaS), changing the way businesses reach the cloud. We're also a leading partner to Amazon, Microsoft, Google, IBM, Oracle, NYSE, and many other leading technology companies. Staff enjoy an environment that is collaborative, supportive, and fun.</div>
