<h2>Content Quality Technician</h2>

It is focused on providing multi-platform marketing solutions to reach audiences most effectively and efficiently. and tape continuity.<br>

<b>
Core Responsibilities:</b><br>
<ul><li>Monitors the audio and video on commercials/avails to ensure</li></ul>
broadcasting quality.<br>
<ul>
<li>Maintains tape library and data base files</li>
<li>Troubleshoots the Wide Area Network on issues of connectivity.</li>
<li>Distributes schedules to all markets.</li>
<li>Dubs tapes for clients, Account Executives, Marketing, etc.</li>
<li>Modifies traffic instruction on insertion equipment for live events to</li>
</ul>
ensure successful billing.<br>
<ul><li>Creates cue tone reports to determine the best day-part configurations</li></ul>
for all networks.<br>
<ul><li>Communicates regularly with sales and traffic departments to make sure</li></ul>
tape is received and aired.<br>
<ul>
<li>Responds immediately to down time in systems.</li>
<li>Regular, consistent and punctual attendance. Must be able to work</li>
</ul>
nights and weekends, variable schedule(s) and overtime as necessary.<br>
<ul><li>Other duties and responsibilities as assigned.</li></ul>
