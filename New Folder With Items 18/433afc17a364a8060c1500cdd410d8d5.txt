Position Description
</div>
<div>
<div>Responsible for the ongoing execution and improvement of sales key position in the North American Sales Compensation team and is responsible for incentive commission processing, reporting and case management for the sales organization. Works independently and cross-functionally within Sales Operations, Human Resources, Finance and Payroll teams. The North American Sales Compensation team is the leader for sales commission polices and processes for all sales teams for US and Canada.<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<ul>
<li>Manage the monthly commission process for our sales teams and other commissionable team members. This includes understanding and applying the commission plan consistently, ensuring commissions calculations comply with the plan and managing exception requests versus policy. Provide expertise, support and training to the various leaders these plans.</li>
<li>Resolve case inquiries and be a trusted advisor to our sales team. Frequent communication with sales leadership. Process corrections and adjustments as they arise.</li>
<li>Provide recommendations to sales leadership and the internal comp management team regarding exceptions requests and case escalations.</li>
<li>Be the trusted partner for all upstream and downstream business functions. Provide backup support within the team for the following areas: comp metrics and reporting, accounting schedules, SOX support, Commission checklists and commission administration.</li>
<li>Document business processes and create desktop procedures for monthly/quarterly close, commission accounting tasks, and audits.</li>
<li>Execute assigned projects as needed and recommend and deliver process improvements.</li>
<li>Sales Operations Programs: Responsible for planning, organizing and tracking activities required to support and improve commissions process. Primary emphasis may include one or some of the following: goal setting, sales compensation, sales programs ROI and sustainment, and other support as necessary.</li>
<li>Process Improvement &amp; Documentation: Identifies opportunities for improvement and coordinates the implementation of process improvements to support key initiatives and programs. Provides input to the ongoing enhancement of tools to support processes. Documents new business processes.</li>
<li>Field Interface: Facilitates and engages in regular communication between Sales Operations and the field. Demonstrates problem solving and strategic thinking to influence field action and adoption.</li>
<li>Cross-functional Collaboration: Effectively partners and collaborates with key stakeholders at all levels, influencing across various functions and departments; Develops and coordinates communication both within the team and across other teams/functions as needed.</li>
</ul>
