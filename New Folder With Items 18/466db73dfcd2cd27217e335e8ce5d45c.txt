What This Position Is All About:<br>
Under direction from the General Manager, the Marketing Director is responsible for creating and executing a strategic local marketing program, maximizing exposure for Saks Fifth Avenue in the local marketplace, and effectively being a liaison between their individual Saks Fifth Avenue store and the Corporate Marketing Team. The Marketing Director will operate with clearly defined return on investment goals for sales and client acquisition and development.<br>
<br>
<b>Who You Are:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>You get things done by engaging in high level teamwork and flexing your interpersonal skills</li>
<li>
You are a respected and proven thought leader with a quantifiable track record of success in delivering results within a large complex organization.</li>
<li>
A natural problem-solver, who is also intuitively analytical and creative</li>
</ul>
<b>
You Also Have:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Minimum 1 year retail experience/3 years fragrance experience, preferably in a customer focused, cosmetics environment.</li>
<li>
Previously demonstrated successful sales experience, exceeding quota.</li>
<li>
Proficient in Microsoft Office. Previous experience using online platforms preferred.</li>
<li>
Willingness to travel at least quarterly to attend vendor and in-store product training.</li>
<li>
Ability to learn and utilize online clienteling tools and software proprietary to Saks Fifth Avenue.</li>
<li>
Apply professional make-up and conduct treatment consultation</li>
</ul>
<b>
As The Online Beauty Specialist, You Will:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Model all Saks Fifth Avenue Principles (Greet, Relate, Exceed, Attend and Thank)</li>
<li>
Focus on achieving and exceeding individual and vendor line sales goals and event sales goals.</li>
<li>
Effectively utilizes Clienteling Systems and Online Platforms to drive vendor sales. Communicates daily with vendor to grow and develop online business.</li>
<li>
Proactively initiates dialogue with customers browsing online. Provides customers with beauty advice and offers product expertise.</li>
<li>
Delivers personalized beauty counter service to customers shopping online. Provides customers with guidance on skincare regimens, makeup application and product recommendations.</li>
<li>
Partners with corporate marketing team to generate additional customer purchases by following up on recent customer transactions. Offers additional product suggestions to customers and send samples directly to customers.</li>
<li>
Consistently maintains a high level of customer service by creating and developing excellent customer relationships as the brand expert and representative.</li>
<li>
Participates in vendor and in store product trainings; stays current on beauty products, trends and techniques.</li>
<li>
Maintain a positive image as a liaison between Saks Fifth Avenue and designated vendor.</li>
<li>
Utilizes Saks Fifth Avenue's online clienteling resource tool to record and maintain client information and preferences to drive your business.</li>
<li>
Focus all efforts on providing an exceptional customer experience from approach, to application, to closing the sale and following up with the customer.</li>
<li>
Demonstrates consistent adherence to company standards and procedures.</li>
</ul>
