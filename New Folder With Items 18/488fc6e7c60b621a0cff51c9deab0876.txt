<h4 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>THE JOB:</b></h4>
<p>As an Inside Sales Executive, you will be responsible for identifying, sourcing, Global 2500 companies in the corporate communications function. Using strong consultative selling skills, you will balance sourcing your own pipeline as well as warm leads.</p>
<br>
<p>
</p>
<p>An Inside Sales Executive is responsible for successfully selling the value of the firm’s membership, measurement, and advisory programs along with their proprietary SAAS platform. During the sales process, you will guide corporate communication executives on the value of their image and how that impacts the company’s profits and losses. Selling is done primarily over the phone with a targeted territory and set of accounts.</p>

<p>This role is an inside sales role working in our downtown Boston office.</p>

<br>

</p>

<li>Become an expert at presenting the value and impact that the firm’s services can provide an organization</li>
