<h2>Sales Lead/Assistant Manager - Retail</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Sales Leads/Assistant Manager will help inspire all women in their style adventures &amp; drive business goals. You must have a fabulous personal sense of style and super strong, positive communication skills, self-starter. A passion for fashion and a love for making connections with everyone is a must! At leela &amp; lavender, our Sales Lead/Assistant Manager works as a team with our Boutique Store Manager to create a unique shop experience.</p>
<p><b>Who you are: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Friendly, fun and has a passion for fashion and helping all women discover their best self</li>
<li>Driven, genuine + positive personality</li>
<li>Very outgoing, super observant, can engage in a conversation with anyone and a motivated self-starter!</li>
<li>You can provide out of this world customer service and an amazing in-store experience</li>
<li>Has a distinct personal style and an eye for detail</li>
<li>Experience in sales, marketing, networking and/or advertising is a plus</li>
<li>Has excellent interpersonal and communication skills - you can talk to anyone</li>
<li>Ability to work well with other team members + take direction</li>
<li>Excitement for + enjoyment of being creative &amp; working at a boutique</li>
<li>Is creative, innovative and well organized</li>
<li>Problem-solving, communication, customer service, analytical and time-management skills</li>
<li>Prior POS experience and computer skills a plus</li>
</ul>
<p><b>RESPONSIBILITIES: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Demonstrate strong leadership abilities.</li>
<li>Builds &amp; maintains a strong customer following thru personal styling + client ling &amp; wardrobing.</li>
<li>Be a brand ambassador for Leela.</li>
<li>Conduct high quality customer service by going above and beyond to provide our customers with a welcoming, helpful and fun styling/shopping experience.</li>
<li>Be knowledgeable + inform customers about product lines, upcoming events, sales, new merchandise, styling program etc. Our leads have in-depth knowledge of our brand, the lines we carry.</li>
<li>Manage inventory by unpacking + prepping new freight and organizing back stock. Restock merchandise on the sales floor.</li>
<li>Work as a team with the store manager to create visual displays to highlight new merchandise, training style catalysts &amp; running day to day operations.</li>
<li>Assist the store manager in coordination of style catalyst communication and responsibilities.</li>
<li>Assist store manager in evaluating store performance &amp; create action plans of ways to continue to grow and improve.</li>
<li>Act as shift manager when the store manager is not present.</li>
</ul>
<p>Job Type: Part-time</p>
<p>Experience:</p>
<ul><li>relevant: 1 year (Preferred)</li></ul>
<p>Additional Compensation:</p>
<ul><li>Store Discounts</li></ul>
<p>Work Location:</p>
<ul><li>One location</li></ul>
<p>Benefits:</p>
<ul><li>Flexible schedule</li></ul>
