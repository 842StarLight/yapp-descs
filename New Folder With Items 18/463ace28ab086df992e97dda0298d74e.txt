<p>Elekta is looking for a Product Manager to join our team!
</p>
<p>As a Product Manager you are responsible for spanning a worldwide company to bring both long term and short term needs together from multiple business units to drive product planning and development. This involves interacting with many different business lines within the organization to maintain a forward view of product potential within Elekta’s line of software products. Multiple opportunities exist for highly organized, motivated professionals dedicated to making an impact in cancer care technology.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Responsibilities
</b></p>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Drive development priorities to meet established business objectives.
</li>
<li>Assume responsibility for release planning, product specification and requirements, general support of development activities, regulatory compliance, and participate in rollout activities.
</li>
<li>Ensure that staff stays abreast of the challenges faced by Elekta’s current and prospective customers.
</li>
<li>Provide in-depth knowledge of competitive products as well as emerging products, services, and technologies being developed to address these challenges.
</li>
<li>Support product promotion and sales efforts and handle escalated product issues and provide service training and support.
</li>
</ul>

<p><b>Qualifications
</b></p>
<p>• Bachelor’s degree in Oncology related field, nursing or healthcare.
</p>
<p>• Experience in product management, health informatics preferred. Business administration background preferred.
</p>
<p>• Combined 5 years of experience in Oncology department.
</p>
<p>• Ability to visualize long-term organizational strategy and motivate others to see the same.
</p>
<p>• Knowledge of full clinical workflows in Oncology care
</p>
<p>• Team player on both departmental and management teams.
</p>
<p>• Ability to effectively interact with members of other departments.
</p>
<p>• Ability to effectively interact with customers (via phone and face-to-face).
</p>
<p>• Good organizational skills.
</p>
<p>• Good working knowledge/understanding of all Elekta products and services as well as the general healthcare market.
</p>
<p>• Must have excellent communication skills and be able to speak, read, and write English to deliver compelling presentations to senior-level management.
</p>
<p>• Travel 25 – 50%, domestic and international.
</p>
