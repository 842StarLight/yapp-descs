<h2>Manager, Customer Education</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p>Numerator is a market intelligence firm that brings together omnichannel marketing, merchandising and sales data to make pursuing new possibilities simple for brand, retail and agency clients. Owned by Vista Equity Partners, Numerator is the only company in the marketplace to connect omnichannel purchase data (powered by the InfoScout OmniPanel) and comprehensive path data to deliver an unmatched view of the consumer shopping and purchase experience.</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Job Description</b></p>
<div>
The Customer Education Manager is a critical position within the Customer Success Department. This individual owns the education process, including creating educational content, course curriculum, and best practices, for new and existing customers and individual users. This individual may also assist with onboarding of new customers and re-onboardings of current customers. The Customer Education Manager manages and creates Customer Help Center documentation and leads webinar, virtual, and in-person client user trainings.<br>
<br>
Responsibilities:<br>
<br>
<ul>
<li>Develop educational content for use with onboarding, the Customer Help Center, and on-going customer education</li>
<li>Create learning progressions to support a Customer’s journey from beginning to more advanced platform use based on key business questions</li>
<li>Confirm and refine with initial use cases and lead platform acceleration/expansion of user base</li>
<li>Collaborate with Client Directors, Sales Directors, and Engagement Managers to build and execute components of the Account Plan and Customer Success Plan</li>
<li>Refine playbooks and strategies to ensure key power users and business owners are properly onboarded when contact turn occurs mid-cycle or during a renewal</li>
<li>Provide feedback to Product Team to help improve platform offerings</li>
<li>Continually create or refine processes and internal documentation</li>
</ul>
<br>
<br>
</div>
<p><b>Skills &amp; Requirements</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s or Master’s degree with at least 3 years of relevant experience</li>
<li>Desired CPG experience in Brand Marketing, Market Research, Category Management, and/or Shopper &amp; Consumer Insights roles, or experience as a service provider to these functions</li>
<li>Desired experience with corporate training or leading educational/guidance conversations</li>
<li>Working knowledge of enterprise software applications and services designed for the retail and consumer goods industries is highly valued (e.g., Nielsen, IRI, NPD, Dunnhumby)</li>
<li>Working knowledge of Business Intelligence software such as Microstrategy, Tableau, QlikView, or Business Objects</li>
<li>Expertise with one or more of Numerator’s products is advantageous</li>
<li>Skilled at developing relationships with clients to become a trusted and value-added business partner</li>
<li>Excellent oral and written communication skills</li>
<li>Strong organizational and project management skills</li>
<li>Strong presentation skills, particularly with training customers in a technological area</li>
<li>Ability to easily walk people through an entire process, step by step, and create a story to increase understanding of the data</li>
<li>Proven analytic skills: conceptualizing, analyzing, deriving insights from data, and designing an execution plan to achieve success on our platform</li>
<li>Ability to travel up to 25% to new clients in order to train all new users onsite</li>
</ul>
