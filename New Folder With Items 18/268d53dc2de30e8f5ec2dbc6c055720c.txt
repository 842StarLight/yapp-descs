<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Scope
</b></h3>
<p>As the <b>Account Manager – US Foods &amp; Dot Foods </b>, you will directly manage the day to day relationship at US Foods and Dot foods, and support in identifying and supporting new and existing accounts in the US. Reporting to the Director of US Foodservice Sales. Your primary role is to work with the US Sales Team to manage key aspects of the business relationship with US Foods and Dot Foods. The Account Manager will need to subscribe to a collaborative approach and be comfortable in a matrix organization.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Responsibilities
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Use market and product knowledge to source and develop incremental business through active prospecting, referrals and leads with new and existing Foodservice customers.
</li>
<li>Assist in the development of the annual business plan in support of annual goals and objectives;
</li>
<li>Develop and establish key business relationships taking the business to the next level and aiming at driving profitable volumes;
</li>
<li>Development of customer presentations and proposals including new product proposals and business reviews;
</li>
<li>Effectively manage and reconcile all rebates, allowances, deductions and receivables with the applicable trading partners;
</li>
<li>Collaborate internally with Sales, Customer Service, Marketing, Finance, Operations, Product Development functions to best maintain existing sales and win new customer business;
</li>
<li>Maintain a thorough working knowledge of all Weston baked goods and programs, and a complete awareness and understanding of the competition with regards to markets, products and pricing
</li>
<li>Assist in the development of budgets and sales strategies;
</li>
