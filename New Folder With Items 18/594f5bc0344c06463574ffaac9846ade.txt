<h2>Mentor Recruiter, IDD</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<i>Everybody needs a job but only extraordinary people work here.</i><br>
<br>
Our team is fun, creative, and dedicated to making a difference every day in the lives of the people we serve. The heart of what we do is support people with Intellectual and Developmental Disabilities of all ages.<br>
<br>
As a Mentor Recruiter with California MENTOR Family Home Agency, you’ll be one of those extraordinary people. We seek a passionate, proven recruiter who can successfully identify families in the Sacramento area to become adult foster care providers, whom we call “Mentors.” Bring your creative ideas and your passion for success; we’ll train you on the rest. We’re the leader across California in matching adults with developmental disabilities with amazing families who want to make a difference.<br>
<br>
Do you have experience working in sales or recruitment and you’re seeking more meaning in your work life? Leverage your skills to make an impact in people’s lives. Build and enhance an active network of family homes through the strategic recruitment of qualified Mentors and guide them through the certification process. Join our dedicated, dynamic and fast growing team today!<br>
<b>Job Description:</b><div><b>
Job Responsibilities:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
Develop and coordinate targeted marketing, including advertising and hosting information sessions</li>
<li>
Represent the company at community events, promotional presentations, and other events to recruit Mentors</li>
<li>
Support applicants throughout certification process, providing guidance and managing timelines</li>
<li>
Review applications, medical examinations and references. Conduct evaluations and assessments</li>
<li>
Create and design new campaigns as needed to continuously attract new Mentors; measure success of campaign effectiveness</li>
<li>
Implement and fully leverage recruiting and retention tracking tools and strategies</li>
</ul>
