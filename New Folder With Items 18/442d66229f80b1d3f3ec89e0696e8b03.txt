<h2>Client Success Representative</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>What is UltraLinq?</p>
<p>UltraLinq Healthcare Solutions, Inc. is a SaaS cloud-based medical image solution. We give our clients the ability to read, store, report and archive anytime and from anywhere with an internet connection. Our team solely consists of passionate, motivated problem-solvers dedicated to providing exemplary service to our customers as we improve the healthcare experience for healthcare providers and professionals. We are a progressive meritocracy lead by a shared leadership team.</p>
<p>About the Role:</p>
<p>We are currently seeking a Client Success Representative to join our New York office. (We will also consider a remote team member for the right candidate.) This is a great role for someone who is energized by building relationships with clients, loves to cross and up-sell, and is motivated to reduce client churn.</p>
<p>As a Client Success Representative, you'll:</p>
<ul>
<li>Cultivate strong, proactive relationships with your book of clients</li>
<li>
Proactively identify churn risks and increase client retention</li>
<li>
Meet quotas to actively cross and up-sell UltraLinq products</li>
<li>
Actively engage two sub-branded products and transition them to UltraLinq brand</li>
<li>
Aid with existing-client market research efforts, including our NPS survey</li>
<li>
Aid with existing-client marketing efforts, including creating engagement campaigns</li>
<li>
Work cross-functionally to provide sales support with referrals and marketing efforts</li>
<li>
Salesforce database management of all client information and details</li>
<li>
Opportunity for up to 5-10% yearly travel to attend and exhibit at industry trade shows</li>
</ul>
<p></p>

<p>UltraLinq would be a great fit for you if you're:</p>
<ul>

<li>
Great at chatting up clients and energized by building loyal relationships</li>
<li>
A strong written and verbal communicator</li>
<li>
Excellent with organization and project management</li>
<li>
Poised within in a deadline-driven environment</li>
<li>
Detailed oriented</li>

A self-starter who works well independently and within a small team</li>

Thrive in a role with a balance between creative and methodical projects</li>

<p></p>
<p>Working for UltraLinq:</p>
<p>UltraLinq values personal and professional balance and growth that's demonstrated through our offered benefits. Some of which include health insurance offerings, 401K offerings, pre-tax commuter benefits, a flexible Vacation policy, and a Vacation Incentive. We offer a competitive base salary and commission structure for our Sales roles.</p>
<p>UltraLinq is a mature SaaS company with a startup mentality. Personally, we're a passionate group of people who genuinely care about making a difference in healthcare. We provide a casual, diverse and energetic atmosphere with highly supportive co-workers. We value enthusiasm, collaboration, learning, and an overall commitment to our clients and growing business.</p>
