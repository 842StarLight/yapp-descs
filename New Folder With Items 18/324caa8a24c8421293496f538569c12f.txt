<h2>IT Dental Equipment Technician</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>The entry-level Digital Radiography Technician is a member of the Capital Dental Equipment (CDE) team, serves as a 'technician in training' for radiological/imaging equipment recertified by Capital Dental Equipment. This includes assisting in the certification, un-installation, and re-installation of previously used dental imaging equipment for resale to new end-purchasers. This involves technical skill with troubleshooting electronic equipment as well as interacting professionally with high-value customers in addition to assisting with completing the certification process as well as the maintenance and coordination of preventative maintenance tasks for previously-installed systems.</p>
<p>The Digital Radiography Technician I also manages part inventory levels for certification of equipment as well as ensuring a safe working environment and he/she maintains documentation of all customer service events including system performance tests and ongoing maintenance procedures performed on such dental radiological/imaging equipment technology types as (1) Panoramic, (2) Dual Panoramic and Ceph Systems, and (3) Cone Beam Computed Tomography (CBCT) imaging systems. The Digital Radiography Technician I also provides technical support for customers and related company 'Pano' activities as well as assisting in the re-certification process to ensure that all quality standards and deadlines are met.</p>
<p><b>Experience Level</b></p>
<ul><li>2 to 5 years' experience in electronics, troubleshooting, and/or manufacturing</li></ul>
<p><b>Manages or Supervises Others</b></p>
<ul><li>No</li></ul>
<p><b>Travel Required</b></p>
<ul><li>Potential travel up to 50% to customer locations and field events.</li></ul>
<p><b>Essential Duties</b></p>
<ul>
<li>As related to the description above, the Digital Radiography Technician I:</li>
<li>Provides outstanding customer service and technical support to customers of CDE.</li>
<li>Assists senior technicians in verifying the proper performance of Dental Imaging Systems and performs equipment calibrations per manufacturer specifications.</li>
<li>Troubleshoots/Calibrates/Repairs Dental Imaging Systems (incl. onsite at customer's location).</li>
<li>Sources parts needed for various Dental Imaging Systems via Technical Service Manuals and prices necessary components, supplies, materials, and tools as-required.</li>
<li>Verifies firmware or software configurations and updates firmware or software as-needed.</li>
<li>Reconditions and/or refurbishes equipment to meet manufacture's specifications and ensures the quality of reconditioned and/or refurbished Dental Imaging Systems meets Reseller standards.</li>
<li>Repackages equipment per manufacturer's specifications and coordinates efficient outbound shipping with sales team and customer.</li>
<li>Provides technical support to internal customers and other divisions of the company which utilize Digital Imaging Systems to provide mobile dental services in the regional area.</li>
<li>Communicates status of inventory and re-certification processes to company team members and management.</li>
<li>Assists in maintaining the CDE warehouse and manufacturing area with regards to organization and cleanliness.</li>
<li>Performs other duties as assigned.</li>
</ul>
