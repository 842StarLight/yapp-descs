<h2>Commercial Lines Account Manager I</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Why Choose RISQ Consulting?</p>
<p>
RISQ Consulting is the product of two long-standing Alaskan businesses with a desire to provide Alaskans holistic solutions for employee benefits, property and casualty, and workers compensation, surety bonds, employer services, and individual &amp; family insurance.</p>
<p>
Our teams formed RISQ Consulting to become the largest insurance brokerage and consulting firm in Alaska. Our team of Alaskans brings you over 750 years of combined industry experience and expertise.</p>
<p>
Our culture is strong. We are a collaborative company of entrepreneurial, innovative, and talented people who believe in our future. We out think and out work the competition. We look outside our walls and are energized by our fast-paced trajectory.</p>
<p>
We are adding a Commercial Lines Account Manager to our growing team. This position will be responsible for performing insurance functions necessary to support the agency processing claims, premiums, refunds, billings, file maintenance, policy changes, audits, new business &amp; renewals, midterm policy changes.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Essential Duties and Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Process claims, premiums, refunds, billing</li>
<li>
Assist Account Executive or lead Account Manager in follow up on client billing questions &amp; collection issues (accounts receivable).</li>
<li>
Review quotes obtained from markets to formulate proposal.</li>
<li>
Complete all Accord applications and enter data into database.</li>
<li>
Initiate marketing of new &amp; renewal business with input from Account Executive or lead Account Manager.</li>
<li>
Manage the services, insurance renewal and marketing calendar for 35-60 accounts, typically small accounts, or a book revenue of $120,000 to $250,000.</li>
<li>
Assists Producer, Account Executive or other Account Managers on the team with client servicing needs on other accounts.</li>
<li>
Research and compile answers to client policy and coverage questions.</li>
<li>
Maintain an in-depth knowledge of insurance coverages.</li>
<li>
Communicate orally and in writing with co-workers, clients and insurance companies regarding insurance programs.</li>
</ul>
<p>
This description is not meant to be all-inclusive and may be modified from time to time at the discretion of management.</p>
