<div><b>Essential Duties &amp; Responsibilities:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Verifies all information related to orders is accurate; identifies and corrects discrepancies.</li>
<li>Completes all documentation in an accurate and efficient manner and processes information regarding cash balances, deliveries, sales orders, account balances in compliance within corporate guidelines.</li>
<li>Provides customer service support by answering phones, managing counter inquiries, processing payments, solving customer complaints/questions.</li>
<li>Completes and processes credit applications, payments and financing paperwork.</li>
<li>Communicates with internal and external personnel in a professional and timely manner.</li>
<li>Maintains accurate files and processes in order to maximize productivity.</li>
<li>Performs clerical support for store staff as needed.</li>
<li>Other duties as assigned.</li>
</ul>
