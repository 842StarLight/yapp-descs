<h2>Digital Marketing Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>DIGITAL AND MARKETING AGENCIES - DO NOT APPLY</b><br><b>(Employee position only)</b></p>
<p><b>Position Overview</b></p>
<p>Collaborate with the owner in this family business on social media, graphic design (digital and print), website (content creation and SEO), jewelry photography, and interacting with external vendors/partners. Ideal position for someone who thrives on variation in their duties, can juggle multiple projects and deadlines, and wants an active part in marketing decisions. This position is both analytical and creative.</p>
<p>PT or FT, 25-40 hours per week. Salary listed is based on FT, and is DOE.</p>
<p><b>About Fox Fine Jewelry</b><br>Voted “Best place to work in Ventura County” in 2017, Fox Fine Jewelry is an independent, family owned store that specializes in on-premises custom jewelry and repairs. Fox Fine Jewelry was named “America’s Coolest Jeweler” in 2015, the most prestigious jewelry award in the nation, and has been voted #1 in the county annually since 2010. Owned by gemologist and jeweler George Fox and his wife Debbie, the company is dedicated to the community, actively supporting numerous charities and hosting art shows. The owners and staff are dedicated to working as a team in a positive atmosphere. As evidenced by the longevity, employees are a part of the Fox Fine Jewelry family, including the resident dog!</p>
<p><b>Key Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Use SEO strategies and create content to increase the company's search engine results</li>
<li>Set measurable SEO goals, monitor and track with Google Analytics</li>
<li>Develop and implement marketing campaigns across digital and traditional platforms</li>
<li>Build strategy for social media, create and maintain calendar, generate content, moderate comments, use analytics to adjust strategies</li>
<li>Create, test, and analyze emails through Mailchimp</li>
<li>Create graphics and videos for social media, website, signage, youtube, print, and other</li>
<li>Photograph jewelry in our lightbox, edit photos, and upload</li>
<li>Art Shows: communicate with artists and write artist biographies, market events through website, postcards and press releases</li>
<li>Edit website, including content creation (pages, events, and blogs)</li>
<li>Update online listings on third party websites</li>
<li>Other projects as needed</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Core Qualifications</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree in marketing, communications or business</li>
<li>2+ years experience in digital marketing and social media for business applications, including Instagram and Facebook analytics and Advertising</li>
<li>Experience with Google Analytics</li>
<li>Proven track record of increasing social media followers and engagement</li>
<li>Experience in graphic design (Photoshop or other design software) preferred</li>
<li>Mid level product or macro photography skills preferred</li>
<li>Ability to juggle multiple projects and deadlines with careful attention to detail</li>
<li>Excellent written and verbal communication skills</li>
<li>Ability to work independently and collaboratively with the owner</li>
<li>Excellent computer skills</li>
<li>Ability to lift 20 pounds</li>
</ul>
