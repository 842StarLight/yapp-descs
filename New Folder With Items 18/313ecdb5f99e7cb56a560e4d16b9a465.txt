<h2>Salesforce Administrator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>
<div>This role is part Salesforce administrator, part sales operations analyst and part technical project manager. You will be responsible for the executing on the day-to-day configuration, support, maintenance and improvement of our CRM platform. By optimizing Salesforce to be more sophisticated and robust, you will play an integral role in helping the sales team and greater HelloSign maintain cleaner metrics, reporting, analytics and business intelligence.</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
If you’re a certified Salesforce administrator looking to join a fast-paced team within a growing company, this is a fantastic opportunity to establish process, leverage data insights and be a strategic partner.</div>
</div>
<div><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>What you will do:</b></h3>
<div><ul>
<li>
Salesforce Admin: collaborate in Salesforce architecture discussions and design, data cleanliness and integrity, building sales methodology and process into Salesforce, testing and deploying new processes</li>
<li>
Handle all basic administrative functions including user account maintenance, reports and dashboards, workflows and other routine tasks</li>
<li>
Coordinate the evaluation, scope and completion of new development requests</li>
<li>
Optimize and operate the sales tech stack(Salesforce, Outreach, Yesware, Drift, ZoomInfo, Distribution Engine, etc.)</li>
<li>
Close collaborator with the marketing operations team(lead flow from Marketo into Salesforce)</li>
<li>
Sales reporting and analytics through all stages of the sales funnel from response to lead to pipeline and Closed Won business</li>
</ul></div>
</div></div>
<div><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Who you are:</b></h3>
<div><ul>
<li>
Certified Salesforce Administrator(ADM 201)</li>
<li>
1-3 years working experience on a sales team as a SFDC admin</li>
<li>
Exceptionally strong analytical, quantitative and problem solving skills</li>
<li>
Ability to create reporting and derive insights out of data</li>
<li>
Strong communication skills both written and oral. Ability to articulate complex subjects in understandable terms to both technical and non-technical stakeholders</li>
<li>
Excellent organizational skills and attention to detail; ability to manage multiple priorities and tasks simultaneously</li>
<li>
Proven ability to design and implement new processes and facilitate user adoption</li>
<li>
Strong understanding of the platform, with the ability to build custom apps and objects, formula fields, workflows, custom views, and other content of intermediate complexity</li>
<li>
Bachelor’s degree</li>
</ul></div>
