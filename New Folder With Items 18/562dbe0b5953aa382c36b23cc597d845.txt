<h2>Inside Sales</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<b>Job Summary:</b><br>
DR Power Equipment’s South Burlington Vermont office is seeking a New Dealer Recruiter to join our B2B Sales Team in our beautiful new location overlooking Vermont’s Green Mountains. You will be joining us at an exciting time in our history as we grow into new markets and create innovative new products. If you’re looking to be a part of a dynamic and hard-working team; a career at DR Power may be right for you.
<br><br>
Our B2B Sales Team explores new outdoor power equipment retailer opportunities. From researching prospective retailers and initiating contact to writing initial orders, the person in this role is an expert at building and maintaining relationships with new and existing customers.
<br><br>
<b>Essential Duties and Responsibilities:</b><br>
Proactively sells DR® power equipment and related accessories to approved DR® Dealers and B2B customers. Works to upgrade orders by suggestive selling (through both inbound and outbound telephone contacts) including quantities, model selection, parts and accessories, and possible financing options.
<br>Using the market data available to develop strategies for establishing new business relationships, looking at key or underperforming markets (working in conjunction with Territory Managers) as well as reviewing competitive product lines carried in support of targeting markets.
<br><br>
Collaborates as needed with Marketing, Sales, Credit, Tech, etc., as well as external allies in the development and implementation of new business strategies and plans.
<br>Develops and participates in forecasting of new business sales demands.
<br>Maintains in-depth knowledge and understanding of DR® product lines and application, as well as the Dealer Channel and corporate Go-To-Market strategy (seasonal discount schedules, advertising promotions, distribution strategies and mailing schedules) to effectively present and respond to prospect’s questions and instill customer confidence.
<br>Continually works to identify and develop ‘new’ business-to-business opportunities with recommendations for set-up or decline.
<br>Participates in sessions to train dealers/distributors (as needed) on the benefits of selling the DR product line in order to improve sales and marketing techniques including training webinars, dealer events, trade shows, and out of town travel as necessary.
<br>Ensures that all customers, both internal and external are treated with respect and excellence in keeping with the Country Home Products tradition.
<br><br>
Qualifications, Knowledge, Skills, Abilities and Physical demands: Minimal Qualifications:
<br>An Associate’s degree along with 3 - 5 years experience in business-to-business sales, or equivalent combination of education and experience.
<br><br>
<b>Preferred Qualifications:</b> A high level of professionalism and the ability to maintain confidential information. Experience with outdoor power equipment and an ability to learn our products and their applications in various regions.
<br><b>Knowledge:</b> Proficiency with MS Office Software (Word, and Excel).
<br><b>Skills:</b> Ability to effectively and clearly communicate with customers and co-workers, both verbal and written. Ability to use/learn to use inventory control systems, and Google Apps. Ability to gather information and make decisions based on the criteria of the department.
