<h2>Manager, Business Systems Analysis</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div><div><div>
<div>Responsible for leading a team of business analyst professionals who own the requirements, elicitation, effort-estimate scoping, design, analysis and functional specification development activities for complex projects or programs. Working closely with clients, team members and other stakeholders, actively supports project and change management lifecycle. This position is required to understand, translate, and document complex business requirements to enable design, development, and implementation of effective and efficient technology solutions.</div>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Job Description
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<ul>
<li>Plan, direct, and coordinate the business-analysis aspects of an information technology project, including on-going production support and training</li>
<li>
Manage, direct and coordinate the daily activities of the business analyst team</li>
<li>
Review and approve business analysis related documentation including: requirements, current and future state processes and workflows, report specifications and others</li>
<li>
Review and approve test plans and test cases, and perform system testing</li>
<li>
Plan and execute user acceptance testing and obtain user sign-off</li>
<li>
Develop and maintain solid partnerships with project managers, business and IT leads, and other key stakeholders</li>
<li>
Manage and train business systems analyst in all aspects of the project lifecycle from initiation to closing, to ensure that the technical solution meets business needs</li>
<li>
Develop and motivate staff to maximize full potential and ensure the mission readiness of all team members</li>
<li>
Support the initiation and planning of the project, including facilitating the definition of objectives, scope, and deliverables, ensuring the project is set up for success</li>
<li>
Collaborate with business and information technology stakeholders facilitating the selection of solution alternatives for implementation</li>
<li>
Suggest and implement recommendations on efficiency improvements where applicable</li>
<li>
Assist business stakeholders in operational readiness activities, including impact analysis and documentation of process changes</li>
<li>
Perform other related duties as required and assigned</li>
<li>
Demonstrate behaviors which are aligned with the organization’s desired culture and values</li>
</ul>
</div></div></div>

</b></h2>
