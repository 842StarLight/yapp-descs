<h2>Membership Consultant</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>The "Best One Hour Workout in the Country," Orangetheory Fitness is a trendsetting fitness organization with over 1000 locations open in 20 Countries and over 200+ new fitness studios under development. We are currently seeking qualified <b>MEMBERSHIP CONSULTANTS </b>at the Attleboro, MA studio.</p>
<p>We are seeking performance based Sales Associates who can connect with our members. An outgoing personality is necessary, as this is a very high-energy atmosphere! The studio is extremely team oriented with growth opportunities for each individual team member.</p>
<p>The goal for this position is to share and promote the experience and many benefits of the Orangetheory Fitness workout. Your position would include but is not limited to maintaining the highest level of customer service for our members, exceeding sales goals, and support of fellow team members. Most importantly candidates will have a true passion for fitness and a commitment to creating positive experiences for everyone around them.</p>
<p>This role assists in running all “front of house” fitness studio operations, with a strong focus on premium customer service as well as meeting sales expectations. Ideal candidate has strong customer service skills and basic sales experience. The Membership Consultant is responsible for meeting and exceeding all sales goals, including membership sales and renewals, retail and concession sales, maximizing workout traffic, and maintaining premium customer service levels.</p>
<p>http://www.orangetheoryfitness.com/</p>
