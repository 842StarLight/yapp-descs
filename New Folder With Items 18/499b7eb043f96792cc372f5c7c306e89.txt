<h2>Maintenance Mechanic</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Northfield, a CRH company, is a division within Oldcastle APG. APG is North America’s leading manufacturer and supplier of concrete masonry, dry mix, and hardscape products. With over $2B in sales, APG operates across 36 states and 5 Canadian provinces through a network of over 195 operating locations and more than 6,500 employees.</p>
<p>Job Summary</p>
<p>This full-time position is responsible for the overall maintenance and repair of production equipment, mobile equipment, and the facility. The ideal candidate will have thorough knowledge and experience of pneumatic, hydraulic, electrical, and mechanical systems in a manufacturing environment.</p>
<p>Essential Duties and Responsibilities</p>
<ul>
<li>Maintains production and quality by ensuring operation of machinery and mechanical equipment</li>
<li>Perform preventative maintenance on the equipment as needed and overhaul the equipment when necessary</li>
<li>Following diagrams, sketches, operations manuals, manufacturer's instructions and engineering specifications</li>
<li>Troubleshoot and determine appropriate repairs, replace defective parts as needed, and maintain machinery and mechanical equipment</li>
<li>Removes defective parts by dismantling devices, using hoists, cranes, and hand and power tools and examining form and texture of parts</li>
<li>Maintains continuity among work teams by documenting and communicating actions, irregularities and continuing needs</li>
<li>Maintains safe and clean working environment by complying with procedures, rules, and regulations</li>
<li>Other duties and responsibilities related to the nature of the job may be assigned on a temporary or permanent basis as needed</li>
</ul>
<p>Requirements</p>
<ul>
<li>At least 3 years experience in Maintenance in an industrial plant</li>
<li>Ability to work with the production department to ensure timely repair of equipment is essential</li>
<li>Strong mechanical and electrical aptitude</li>
<li>Basic understanding of pneumatic and hydraulic systems</li>
<li>Ability to understand and work from mechanical and electrical drawings/prints</li>
<li>Experience with PLC systems and frequency drives</li>
<li>Some welding and pipe fitting experience necessary</li>
</ul>
<p>Preferences</p>
<ul>
<li>Knowledge of maintenance programs is a plus</li>
<li>Universal maintenance experience (well rounded)</li>
<li>Strong on motor control and variable frequency drives (VFD's)</li>
<li>Knowledge of control circuit wiring 24VDC is a plus</li>
</ul>
