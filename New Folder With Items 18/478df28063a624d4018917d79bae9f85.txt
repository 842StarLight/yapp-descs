FBC NY is a creative marketing &amp; advertising agency. Born in 1873, we established our legacy by doing two things uniquely well: storytelling and precision. Reborn in March 2016 with the intense focus of a start-up, we emerged stronger than ever, taking the best of both worlds and creating ideas today for the world of tomorrow. We’re talking about equity-building, media-agnostic thinking that aggregates to a greater, purpose-driven story that can withstand the winds of change, while converting leads into sales and ROI overnight.</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
FCB NY is looking for a SVP of Talent and Culture. This is both a strategic and hands-on role that provides support for the full Human Resources life cycle. The role is critical in leading and executing integrated and aligned strategies for talent acquisition, talent management, total rewards, learning and organization development, HR technology, as well as providing great internal HR support, fully engaging a diverse workforce and enhancing the agency culture.</div>
<div>
This position reports to the President of the FCB NY and has a matrixed relationship into the Global Chief Talent Officer for FCB Global.</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
Specific Responsibilities Include:</div>
<div><ul>
<li>
Aligning FCB NY’s Talent strategy to the agency’s business strategy and goals, to create a compelling strategy and plan to compete in the marketplace based on Talent and Culture.</li>
<li>
Utilizing knowledge of the business, industry and external market factors, to provide insight into the talent market with respect to competitors and the industry trends.</li>
<li>
Lead change with respect to rapid and complex industry, societal and work related changes.</li>
<li>
Success in creating and driving effective integrated talent management, development, retention and reward programs.</li>
<li>
Provide coaching, counseling and mentoring to department leaders, offering feedback and advice on employee and organizational issues, reinforcing an accountable, performance driven culture while maintaining a creative driven agency.</li>
<li>
Contribute to a shift in mindset and meaningful change to ensure a true culture of inclusion; assist with implementation of effective Culture and Inclusion programs; work collaboratively/ in partnership with staff, global team, and external partners, demonstrating a strong understanding and innovative approach to diversity and inclusion initiatives, with a deep understanding of the success to the business, creative and the culture.</li>
<li>
Being an enthusiastic team player with a strong drive to create a positive work environment.</li>
<li>
Have a hands-on approach with the ability to successfully monitor the pulse of the staff to ensure a high level of employee engagement as well as create and execute an Employee Engagement strategy using the Engagement Survey and action plans.</li>
<li>
Diplomatically navigate and leverage resources and stakeholders to provide mutually-beneficial solutions to problems, conflicts and/or employee relations issues.</li>
<li>
Work collaboratively with finance and other departments to determine talent/resource needs and allocations.</li>
<li>
Coaching and mentoring at all levels; coach employees to identify development opportunities for themselves and their staff; contribute to employee performance and behavior improvement through feedback, counsel and training.</li>
<li>
Proficiency in translating data and analytics into actionable insights, solid understanding and curiosity for emerging analytics techniques and how they translate into actionable insights.</li>
</ul></div>
