<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>About the Company</b></h2>
<div>
Validity is a leading provider of data integrity and compliance solutions that tens of thousands of organizations worldwide rely on to trust their data. For more than a decade, Validity's products - DemandTools, DupeBlocker, PeopleImport and BriteVerify - have received exceptionally high ratings on peer review sites like G2Crowd, TrustRadius and the Salesforce AppExchange. That's because Validity empowers business and IT leaders to save time and make better decisions that drive more leads, close more deals, and confidently plan for continued growth. Validity is headquartered in Boston's Back Bay and has offices in Tampa and London.</div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
About the Role</b></h2>
<div>
Validity is looking for Account Executives - Expansion to join our high performing sales team. As an Account Executive - Expansion, you will be responsible for selling Validity's Software as a Service products to Validity Customers across all industries and responsible for customer retention within your assigned territory. Works closely with Customer Success and Technical support resources to increase customer satisfaction resulting in bookings and revenue. The Account Executive - Expansion is an internal/territory-based position working in our Tampa, FL office.</div>
