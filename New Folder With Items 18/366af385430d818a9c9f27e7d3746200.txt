<p><b>Company Description</b></p>
<p>
Our client is the number one outdoor advertising specialist in the world, as well as the market leader in Europe and in Asia Pacific. It is one of only two companies worldwide to focus exclusively on outdoor advertising and develop activities in all three segments: street furniture, billboard and transport advertising.</p>
<p>
People's lifestyles are changing. Americans are increasingly spending more time outside of their homes and traditional media channels like television, radio, newspapers, and magazines are no longer sufficient to reach them. Our client reaches millions of Americans everyday while they travel by car and when they walk and shop in city downtowns. Our client offers advertising space on street furniture like bus shelter advertising, newsstand kiosks, and City Information Panels in New York, Los Angeles, Chicago, Boston and San Francisco</p>
<p><b>
Position Summary</b></p>
<p>
The National Account Executive is responsible for managing and growing an existing list of billing. He/She must open new doors and generate revenue from new clients as well. The ideal candidate must be capable of working in a fast paced and deadline-oriented environment, handling a variety of daily tasks focusing on prospecting and developing new customer relationships as well as managing existing relationships and business, persuasively communicating customized solution based strategies to meet individual client's needs, utilizing the company's portfolio of out of home assets, and collaborating with internal departments including operations, marketing, and finance, to ensure customer satisfaction.</p>
