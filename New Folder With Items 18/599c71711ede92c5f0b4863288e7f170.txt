<h2>Account Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>SR Education Group’s mission is to help students make informed decisions about their education by providing transparent, data-driven, and user-focused products. We embrace a culture of growth and experimentation. Our small and agile team allows all members to exercise great responsibility and freedom, allowing each person to have a measurable impact on our business and a unique opportunity to be exposed to all areas of an online marketing company. We reward results with a profit sharing program on top of a competitive salary, and encourage self-development through benefits like our tuition reimbursement program.</p>
<p><b>Only applications submitted through the following link will be considered (no direct Indeed apps will be reviewed): </b></p>
<p><i><b>https://careers.sreducationgroup.org/o/account-manager/c/new</b></i></p>
<p>We have a network of over 200 partner colleges and agencies that we provide lead generation services for. We are also at an exciting growth point, as we’ve expanded our portfolio to serve new audiences within the education space and have expanded our client base. As such, we’re looking for a dedicated account manager to join our team with exceptional customer service skills and experience analyzing metrics to drive insights and meet goals.</p>
<p>Our account managers support and manage our network of partner schools and agencies. They negotiate terms and costs per lead, work with our legal team to finalize contracts, input and manage all partner data for our products, handle reporting discrepancies with partners, and manage our accounts receivable. They also manage and mentor our account specialists, who help support many of the needs of this department.</p>
<p><b>Primary Responsibilities: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Cultivate strategic relationships with leading accredited universities and agencies<b>,</b>including negotiating costs per lead and resolving complex partner requirements</li>
<li>Own all facets of the account management process for medium to large accounts, including managing accounts receivable and diagnosing issues related to failed form submissions</li>
<li>Make changes to partner accounts and respond to emails from partners in a timely and professional manner</li>
<li>Responsible for renewal and growth within the existing client base</li>
<li>Training, mentoring and management of account specialists</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Requirements: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor's degree</li>
<li>Minimum 1-2 years of experience in account management</li>
<li>Exceptional attention to detail, organizational skills, and analytical skills</li>
<li>Proven ability to communicate clearly and concisely, verbally and in writing</li>
<li>Ability to multitask, delegate, and work independently</li>
<li>Proficiency with Excel, and experience with data analysis and creating metric reports</li>
<li>Understanding of HTML and/or SQL a plus</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Title</b>: Account Manager</p>
<p><b>Position Type</b>: Full-time</p>
<p><b>Pay</b>: $60,000-$75,000 DOE</p>
<p><b>Benefits: </b>Equity sharing; profit- and performance-based bonuses; health, dental, and vision insurance; retirement contributions; competitive &amp; flexible vacation policy (up to 6 weeks with seniority); tuition reimbursement program, charitable matching program</p>
<p>Our waterfront office is located in downtown, historic Kirkland. We keep our kitchen fully stocked and have ping pong and pool tables, a growing board game collection, and a troop of quirky office dogs. We hope you’ll consider joining us and being a part of our team at SR Education Group.</p>
