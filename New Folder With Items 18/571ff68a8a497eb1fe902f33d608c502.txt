<p><b>Job Description
</b></p>
<p><b>Responsibilities/Duties:
 </b></p>
<p>Mechanical Assembly
</p>
<p>o Works closely with mechanical engineer to develop/improve upon machine<br>
functionality
</p>
<p>o Assembly of machines within project schedules
</p>
<p>o Assembles engineer’s concept into reality<br>
o Reporting status to project manager on machine development, and escalating
</p>
<p>risks and issues<br>
o Interface with all project team (i.e. Software Engineer, Mechanical Engineer,
</p>
<p>Electrical Engineer, Assemblers, Project Manager…)<br>
o Analyzes full requirements of system involved and checks and tests system
</p>
<p>components.
</p>
<p>o Build tooling fixtures (on the fly where necessary)
</p>
<p>o Adheres to Inventory &amp; Floor Stock procedures
 </p>
<p>Customer Service<br>
o Answers support phone line as well as any voicemail throughout each business
</p>
<p>day<br>
o Responds to customer service calls (on-site, or on-line; during the day and after
</p>
<p>hours)<br>
o Ensures timely response to customers (i.e. Adhering to Customer Service SLAs
</p>
<p>“service level agreements”)
 </p>
<p>Troubleshooting
<br>
o Risk Mitigation - anticipate problems before they occur
</p>
<p>o Troubleshoots and debugs (i.e. Diagnoses trouble or defect, determines<br>
corrective action and repairs system)
</p>
<p>o Performs preventative maintenance and diagnostics on automation system and<br>
components. Ensures that automation system is properly maintained and<br>
operating correctly.
</p>
<p>o Completes service repairs, replacements, upgrades, adjustments and calibration<br>
on automation systems and components following issued tasking, maintenance,<br>
troubleshooting and installation instructions.
</p>
<p>o Performs system checkout and assists in automation systems startups for service<br>
installed jobs. May coordinate electrical installation with electrical contractor at<br>
job site as required.
</p>
<p>o If improper operation is due to another system or component, discusses<br>
problem with customer and/or office and advises of the best course of action.
</p>
<p>o Knowledge of the software to be able to make programming changes and<br>
adjustments where necessary (e.g. Vision Cognex Insight, Maple Systems<br>
HMP/PLC Allen Bradley, IAI Controller)
 </p>
</div></div>
<p></p>
<div><div>
<p>Documentation<br>
o Read all spec sheets on machine to be fully knowledgeable; have a working
</p>
<p>knowledge of how machine works<br>
o Completes and submits written service orders (for non-warranty as well as
</p>
<p>warranty products). Documentation to include such items as Issue, Resolution,<br>
Date/Time Customer Initiated Service Ticket, Date/Time ticket closed/resolved,
</p>
<p>o Documents Quality Control, Test Plan &amp; Acceptance Tests<br>
o Documents Product Change Notifications and submits to Project Manager
<br>
o Mechanical BOM &amp; Schematic redlines (and project closeout audit)
 </p>
<p>Acceptance Tests<br>
o Work in conjunction with engineers throughout assembly process to ensure
</p>
<p>thorough knowledge and familiarity with machine<br>
o Testing of all aspects of machine<br>
o Coordinate with customer to ensure we have samples for testing of the machine<br>
o Coordinates and documents Acceptance Tests (based on customer
</p>
<p>requirements) and ensures signoff internally and final signoff by customer
 </p>
<p>Shipping, Installation &amp; Training
</p>
<p>o Prepares machine for shipment to customer site
</p>
<p>o Installs machine at customer site<br>
o Trains the customer (e.g. process engineer) on all aspects of the machine<br>
o Delivers technical support, coaching and direction to end users and field labor
</p>
<p>when applicable
 </p>
<p>General<br>
o Provides weekly status updates to Project Manager on assigned projects
<br>
o Escalates to Operations Project Manager and/or Customer Service Manager
<br>
o Completes and submits bi-weekly timesheets (includes project time tracking)<br>
o Comply with the company safety procedures. Set an example of company
</p>
<p>culture with integrity and professional competence.
<br>
o Adhere to company policies &amp; procedures<br>
o Must be able and willing to travel and work at customer sites<br>
o Involvement in Lessons Learned for all assigned projects
</p>
<p>o Continuous improvement in standardization of product line<br>
o Involved in the recruitment of new staff (e.g. interviews prospective persons)<br>
o Help Train/Orient/”Bring Up to Speed” new employees.<br>
</p>
<p><br>
<b>Work Experience, Education, Qualifications and Skills:
 </b></p>
<p>5+ years experience as a mechanical assembly and/or field service technician preferable<br>
in the automation industry
 </p>
<p>Working engineering and programming background preferred. Skilled in programming,<br>
job start-up and checkout, and troubleshooting.
 </p>
<p>Ability to read/understand SolidWorks mechanical drawings and schematics and do<br>
redlines
 </p>
</div></div>
<p></p>
<div><div>
<p>High precision, close tolerance proficiency
 </p>
<p>Ability to use all hand tools
 </p>
<p>Familiarity with machine shop equipment (e.g. mill machine, drill press, lathe…)
 </p>
<p>Must be able to lift at least 50 lbs.
 </p>
<p>Basic electronics knowledge
 </p>
<p>Very strong analytical and troubleshooting skills of mechanical systems
 </p>
<p>Aware of design tools and techniques
 </p>
<p>Proficient in standard and metric systems
 </p>
<p>MRP/ERP experience a plus
 </p>
<p>Proficient in MS Office 2010 (Excel, Word, Outlook) and general computer literacy
 </p>
<p>Excellent written and verbal communication and customer relation skills
 </p>
<p>Must be a self-starter, team player, work well with others and have a positive attitude</p>
