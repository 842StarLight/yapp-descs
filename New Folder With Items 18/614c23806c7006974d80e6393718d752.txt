<h2>Enterprise Account Executive</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p><b>Primary Purpose and Function</b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>The achievement of Branch sales goals through the development of new Enterprise Security Sales and key account management in the assigned market area. The EAE will work in accordance with the organization's policies and procedures, keeping in mind the overall core values of Securadyne Systems.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Essential Functions and Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Exceed sales goals as established by the organization</li>
<li>Identify &amp; develop new enterprise level customer prospects</li>
<li>Utilize consultative sales techniques in customer development situations</li>
<li>Preparation and presentation of company offerings to end-user customers</li>
<li>Aggressively grow service and maintenance agreements as part of solution selling process</li>
<li>Determine hosted, managed, monitored sales opportunities with new customer prospects</li>
<li>Preparation of timely and accurate solution proposals</li>
<li>Maintain quality assurance by following organization standards</li>
<li>Assist with other sales projects as assigned by General Manager</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Knowledge and Skills</b></p>
<ul>
<li>High School Diploma required; Associates Degree preferred</li>
<li>5+ years relevant experience in the electronic security industry or enterprise level sales experience</li>
<li>The ability to work in a dynamic team environment with an expanding customer base</li>
<li>Organized and analytical decision making capabilities</li>
<li>Highly-motivated and customer service oriented</li>
<li>Must be able to manage multiple tasks, manage deadlines and have excellent follow-up skills both internally and externally</li>
<li>Proficient with MS Office Suite (Word, Excel, Outlook, PowerPoint, Project and SharePoint).</li>
<li>Commitment to professional, ethical, and compliant business practices</li>
<li>Must be professional and polished in appearance and speech</li>
<li>Good verbal and written communication skills</li>
<li>Ability to establish and maintain effective working relationships with both internal and external customers</li>
<li>Ability to conduct site walks and attend onsite customer meetings</li>
</ul>
