<p>Integrated Practice Solutions, Inc. (dba ChiroTouch), a fast-growing and leading healthcare software company, is looking for a Senior Director of Marketing to lead corporate marketing, product marketing, marketing programs, social media, partners and website. This person must build strong relationships with the Leadership Team and align closely with Sales and Products/Technology departments. This person will also lead a team of marketing professionals to achieve department and individual objectives, as well as support the key objectives of related departments including Sales, Development and Support.</p>

<li>
Develop marketing plans and proposals and manage budgets to ensure very positive ROI.</li>
<li>
Identify and implement innovations in lead generation, especially via digital marketing techniques</li>
<li>
Monitor trends in the health care industry and chiropractic segment</li>
<li>
Supervise and improve ChiroTouch's market visibility and brand through a comprehensive marketing mix, and address the following areas:<ul>
<li>
Industry conference participation</li>
<li>
Marketing materials</li>
<li>
White papers</li>
<li>
Industry trade public relations</li>
<li>
Business social media marketing including blogs</li>
<li>
Chirotouch website enhancement and maintenance</li>
<li>
Lead generation</li>
<li>
Speaking opportunities</li>
<li>
Public relations</li>
<li>
Advertising</li>
<li>
Chiropractic college programs</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>
Develop, refine, measure and optimize replicable marketing processes designed to exceed revenue targets</li>
<li>
Manage outside marketing communications agencies as required to support above objectives</li>
<li>
Demonstrate excellent presentation and writing skills</li>
<li>
Comfortable speaking and presenting at trade conferences</li>
<li>
Monitor competitors by gathering current marketplace information on pricing, products, programs, marketing communications, etc.</li>
<li>
Supports internal communication to help brand ChiroTouch as a best place to work</li>

Bachelor's Degree in Marketing or related required; MBA preferred</li>
<li>
Experience with Salesforce or other similar CRM tools</li>
