<p>
OpsRamp product marketing drives go-to-market strategy by identifying repeatable sales plays and delivering supporting integrated marketing campaigns. This team develops product messaging and positioning, campaigns, events, and sales enablement. We measure our success by our ability to accelerate deals to closure through these marketing strategies.<br>

<p></p>
<p>OpsRamp's mission is to accelerate digital transformation and the adoption of new digital infrastructure that will be required to monitor and manage the increasingly complex hybrid infrastructure. Our easy to use and easy to deploy SaaS platform delivers a modern IT operations management platform that will drive efficiency, productivity and align technical teams to business outcomes though shard visibility, intelligence and greater automation.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p>As a Manager on the OpsRamp Product Marketing team, you will own one or more technology-led sales plays targeted at IT buyers, including the CIO, VP IT Infrastructure and Operations, and DevOps practitioners. You will define a strategic platform narrative through the lens of customer stories, deep product content, and thought leadership that links business initiatives to technology projects and ultimately IT operations management use cases. You will be responsible for defining the go-to-market, content and end-to-end integrated marketing campaign strategy. Solutions Consulting and Sales teams will be one of your most important and strategic internal customers. You will also work closely with Product Management teams.</p>
