<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Position Summary:</b></h2>
<p>
Responsible for day-to-day execution of social media campaigns including daily community listening, engagement/conversion, and monitor issue escalation/response as well as organic search (SEO).</p>
<p>
Role model and demonstrate the Company's core values of respect, honesty, integrity, diversity, inclusion and safety of self and others. Fred Meyer also requires that all associates perform all tasks in a safe manner consistent with corporate policies and state and federal laws.</p>
<p><br>
</p>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Essential Job Functions:</b></h2>
<ul>
<li>Model "Customer 1st" behavior; deliver and encourage other associates to deliver excellent customer service
</li>
<li>Perform ongoing keyword discovery, expansion and optimization
</li>
<li>Implement SEO recommendations
</li>
<li>Establish a link building strategy
</li>
<li>Work closely with the eCommerce production team to optimize copy and landing pages for SEO
</li>
<li>Work with Marketing team to drive SEO in content creation and content programming
</li>
<li>Provide copywriting and content as needed
</li>
<li>Establish Social Media Strategy for Facebook, Twitter, Pinterest, Google+, YouTube and Instagram
</li>
<li>Manage Social Media channels on a daily basis. Create and post engaging content daily.
</li>
<li>Support brand loyalty and encourage user generated content via active community participation and conversation; manage online discussions by listening and responding to users in a timely and authentic manner
</li>
<li>Escalate customer issues as needed and provide feedback on community happenings
</li>
<li>Manage and adhere to email and online storefront editorial calendar to meet deadlines
</li>
<li>Manage and execute on campaigns and paid social advertising
</li>
<li>Monitor and track social media activities and provide reports that measure key performance indicators
</li>
<li>Establish content marketing strategy for the blog and write regular content
</li>
<li>Maintain a high-level understanding of latest trends in social media
</li>
<li>Explore and determine relevance of new social media channels
</li>
<li>Must be able to perform the essential functions of this position with or without reasonable accommodation
</li>
</ul>
