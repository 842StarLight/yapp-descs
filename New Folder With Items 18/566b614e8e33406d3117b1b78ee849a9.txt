<p></p>
<p>The Business Development Manager (BDM) will find, qualify, develop and win new sales opportunities to aggressively grow the revenue and profitability of Tekni-Plex’s medical product lines. The BDM is exceptionally skilled at developing win-win partnerships with medical device manufacturers and their suppliers by understanding and helping solve some of their most important business issues. <i>BDMs are specifically responsible for identifying and penetrating prospect accounts and supporting Key and Regional Account Managers</i>.</p>
<p><b>ESSENTIAL DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES</b></p>
 <p>Develop relationships and new business with prospective customers
</p>
<ul>
<li>Target a portfolio of Prospect Accounts based in Ideal Customer Profiles, determined in collaboration with Strategic Marketing and Product Marketing.</li>
<li>Co-ordinate and prioritize prospecting activities with Inside Sales</li>
<li>Partner with Key/Regional Account Managers in their accounts on new Opportunities within the BDM’s Therapeutics or Application areas of expertise.</li>
<li>Develop trusted-advisor relationships with personnel within the target customers - in all functional groups, at all levels including senior executives - with particular emphasis on R&amp;D, design and process engineers.</li>
<li>Liaise between prospects/customers and cross-functional internal teams to ensure the timely and successful delivery of Tekni’s solutions according to customer needs and criteria.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>EDUCATION and EXPERIENCE:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Expert knowledge in one of the following areas is required:
<ul>
<li>Medical grade polyvinyl chloride (PVC) and thermoplastic elastomer (TPE) compounds</li>
<li>Tubing manufactured from PVC, TPE, polyolefins, silicone, thermoplastic polyurethanes (TPUs), nylons, etc.
</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>Bachelor’s degree or equivalent experience is required</li>
<li>Seven or more years of experience in an outside sales and/or outside technical/engineering position with a demonstrated track record of successful business development.</li>
<li>Knowledge of the plastics industry and plastic materials.</li>
<li>Direct involvement with medical device applications / design with a demonstrated expertise in one or more therapeutics or application areas, as noted above.</li>
<li>Ability to travel 60% of the time.</li>
<li>Ability to effectively use Customer Relationship Management Software.</li>
<li>Exposure to Sales Management Operations Systems is preferred</li>
<br>
</ul>
