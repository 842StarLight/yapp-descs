Description:</b><br>
<div>Aramark Uniform Services (AUS) provides uniforms and related products to more than 400,000 customers nationwide, in virtually every industry. From designing and manufacturing to laundering and delivering, AUS works with local and national clients to create and maintain a total uniform solution that promotes teamwork and establishes a professional identity. AUS operates from over 200 locations nationwide.</div>

<b>
Responsibilities</b><b>:</b>
</div>
<ul>
<li>
Within assigned districts, support the efforts to maintain satisfactory targets for cost controls and growth. Proactively follow up on accounts in jeopardy and lost accounts.</li>
<li>
Perform daily route check-in by reviewing and approving invoices, deposits and other necessary documents for accuracy. Conduct weekly route observations within assigned districts.</li>
<li>
Execute prescribed action plan and truck audits when directed/as necessary.</li>
<li>
Support the efforts to drive company Action Plans and hold the route organization accountable for executing against those plans.</li>
<li>
Provide outstanding customer service and maintain a continuous focus on customer service. Make direct service calls and resolve service concerns.</li>
<li>
Maintain close coordination with production department to resolve shortages and other production related opportunities.</li>
<li>
Through implementation and execution of company safety initiatives, create an environment where safety is a primary concern.</li>
<li>
Partner with the sales organization to identify areas of growth.</li>
<li>
Periodically fill in on routes to cover for sick days, vacation, etc.</li>
<li>
Achieving defined milestones to progress to DM position, ideally within 9-12 months.</li>
<li>
Perform other duties as required or assigned by management.</li>
</ul>
