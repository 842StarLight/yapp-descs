<h2>Order Processing Associate</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>We are currently seeking a <b>Order Processing Associate</b> for our Customer Service Department. The successful candidate is responsible for providing exceptional service to the customers, and possess the ability to pay attention to detail when processing orders. The main purpose of this position is to aid the production, purchasing, warehousing and transportation departments, while maintaining customer satisfaction.</p>
<p>
Responsibilities:</p>
<ul>
<li>Ensure customer and salesperson calls are handled promptly, courteously, effectively and efficiently</li>
<li>Key product orders into system with speed and accuracy</li>
<li>Process order acknowledgements for customers</li>
<li>Process invoices to customers</li>
<li>Make changes to existing orders</li>
<li>Work closely with production, purchasing, warehousing and transportation departments to verify product availability, shipping needs and to ensure customer issues are resolved</li>
<li>Work closely with Key Account personnel to verify customer contact information, pricing and product needs and to ensure other customer issues are resolved</li>
<li>Update and maintain reference data utilized by the department; i.e., national customer list, mailing labels, etc.</li>
<li>Back-up S&amp;D Sales Order Processors as necessary</li>
<li>Completes other duties as assigned by management.</li>
</ul>
</div>
