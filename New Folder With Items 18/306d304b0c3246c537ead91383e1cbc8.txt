<div><b>
SENIOR REVENUE ACCOUNTANT</b></div>
<div>
The Senior Revenue Accountant plays a critical role in positioning SAI Global as a leader of best-in-class accounting practices and processes. The Senior Revenue Accountant manages multiple revenue functions as well as provides meaningful insight into accounting treatment for complex contracts. This position requires a person with impeccable attention to detail who is comfortable managing a variety of functions while providing a high level of value to our partners across the business.</div>
</div>
<div><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Key Responsibilities:</b></h3>
<div><ul>
<li>
<i>
Review customer contracts for new and add-on license and/or SaaS sales by completing deal analysis sheet: </i>Document contract key data points; Perform revenue recognition analysis in accordance with company policy and current authoritative guidance; Outline of any manual adjustments to systematic revenue recognition; Follow-up with Contracts, Sales, and Professional Service Teams on any open items related to deal completion; Coordination of related order to invoice processing and delivery</li>
<li>
<i>
Deferral monitoring and communication: </i>Own monthly and quarterly revenue close process including monitoring, review, and analysis of revenue activity including generating journal entries; Reconcile and review Deferred Revenue and prepare required adjusting entries</li>
<li>
<i>
Sales support: </i>Maintain new bookings report outlining each sale by element; Key contact for Contracts, Sales, and Professional Services Teams for guidance on revenue related questions during contract negotiations; Process credit memo requests related to new sales and maintain supporting documentation/approvals</li>
<li>
<i>
Technical accounting: </i>Assist with analysis and adoption of IFRS15; Perform technical accounting research and prepare documentation around complex revenue recognition accounting issues</li>
<li>
<i>
Controls: </i>Develop, maintain and update internal control and process documentation for quote to cash; Prepare supporting documentation for audit and tax requests as needed; Ensure the integrity of key processes by understanding systems, flow of transactions, internal controls and recommending efficiency and effectiveness improvements; Maintain controls around the revenue process to ensure accurate revenue reporting</li>
<li>
<i>
Analysis: </i>Create detailed reporting as needed for management purposes; Analyze customer revenues and trends on a monthly basis
</li>
<li>
<i>Commission Control: </i>Manage accounting related to incremental costs of obtaining contracts, including variable sales compensation</li>
<li><i>
Perform other duties and projects as assigned</i></li>
</ul></div>
</div></div>
<div><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Qualifications and Experience:</b></h3>
<div><ul>
<li>
Accounting degree from an accredited four year college or university; CPA preferred
</li>
<li>5+ years of relevant accounting experience in industry or public accounting
</li>
<li>Accounting experience in a software company especially as it relates to revenue (license, SaaS, support &amp; Maintenance professional services)</li>
<li>
Strong knowledge of IFRS, especially in the area of revenue recognition</li>
<li>
Excellent organizational skills and detail oriented with the ability to handle multiple, competing tasks simultaneously</li>
<li>
Experience with an Accounting ERP system, experience with Epic is a plus</li>
<li>
Excellent communication skills and the ability to develop effective working relationships within and across departments</li>
<li>
Strong Excel skills and computer skills required</li>
<li>
Hands on, be part of a growing team that helps the company develop processes and procedures as we scale, willingness to get into the detail</li>
<li>
Ability to work well in a fast-paced environment, prioritize, work under pressure, and meet deadlines</li>
<li>
Strong analytical skills and leadership qualities</li>
</ul></div>
