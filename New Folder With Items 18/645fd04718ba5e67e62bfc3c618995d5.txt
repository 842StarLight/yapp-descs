<h2>Customer Service Representative</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Position Description
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div><b>At a Glance</b></div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
<b>
Legrand </b>has an exciting opportunity for a <b>Customer Service Representative</b> to join the <b>Legrand AV</b> team in <b>Vandalia, OH</b>. The basic purpose of this position is to field inbound interactions by providing customer care technical support services for connectivity products, via e-mail, chats and phone calls.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div><b>
What Will You Do?</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
Identify and solve customer connectivity issues or needs on inbound requests from Legrand customers.</li>
<li>
Answer phone calls, chats, emails and all other inbound customer inquiries in a timely and effective manner striving to provide a first contact resolution.</li>
<li>
Log and update customer information in CRM to maintain data integrity.</li>
<li>
Participate in continual training and development (internal processes &amp; software, product and application knowledge, sales process and outcomes, and industry based training)</li>
<li>
Maintain levels of productivity as outlined by management.</li>
<li>
Accomplish other assignments or projects assigned by management</li>
</ul>
<div><b>
Skills/Knowledge/Abilities</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
Proficient with Microsoft Office applications to include Word, Excel, and Outlook.</li>
<li>
Must possess excellent verbal and written communication skills.</li>
<li>
Ability to type a minimum 30 WPM</li>
<li>
Demonstrated ability to multitask using technology</li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<br>
</div></div></div>

</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div><b>Required Skills</b></div>

<div><b>
Preferred Skills</b></div>
