<h2>Finance Manager, Cloud Management Business Unit</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Business Summary</b></p>
<p>
VMware is a global leader in cloud infrastructure and business mobility. We accelerate customers’ digital transformation journey by enabling enterprises to master a software-defined approach to business and IT. With VMware solutions, organizations are creating exceptional experiences by mobilizing everything, responding faster to opportunities with modern data and apps hosted across hybrid clouds, and safeguarding customer trust with a defense-in-depth approach to cybersecurity.</p>
<p></p>
<p>
The Global Business Unit (GBU) Finance team’s overall mission is to provide trusted and insightful financial advice to enable the executive leadership teams to make effective decisions. GBU finance serves as a trusted strategic and operational finance partner to business unit leadership and is critical to ensuring strategic and financial success of the business units.</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Job Role and Responsibility</b></p>
<p>
VMware’s GBU Finance team is seeking a world-class Finance Manager to be a key member of the Cloud Management Business Unit (CMBU) finance team. In this role you will serve as a strategic and operating finance partner to the business unit, fully embedded within the business unit and a key partner to the leadership team. You will own a BU P&amp;L and help the business meet its strategic and financial goals.</p>
<p></p>
<p>
Core Responsibilities</p>
<ul>
<li>
Own the bookings/revenue forecast and partner with the business to drive top line results</li>
<li>
Partner with BU and Finance leadership on short and long term financial planning</li>
<li>
Provide financial modeling, analytics and strategic advice in support of pricing decisions</li>
<li>
Support R&amp;D investment decision making to optimize risk and return trade offs</li>
<li>
Develop financial models and analyses to support strategic initiatives</li>
<li>
Lead business partnership and financial oversight of expense management</li>
<li>
Prepare quarterly business reviews to ensure execution against the operating plan is on track</li>
<li>
Participate in all M&amp;A activities for CMBU including target identification, business case development, financial modeling, due diligence, valuation and post-acquisition integration</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Required Skills</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Bachelor’s degree in business, finance, economics or a related field</li>
<li>
5 - 8 years of experience in finance/accounting with a strong record of career progression</li>
<li>
Demonstrated results providing strategic and financial business partnership to business leaders</li>
<li>
Strong financial modeling and analytical skills</li>
<li>
Proven ability to work cross functionally and drive results across a large global organization</li>
<li>
Strong interpersonal skills, including written and oral communication skills, and ability to work and effectively communicate with senior-level business partners</li>
<li>
Understanding of accounting and compliance requirements</li>
<li>
Tech industry experience</li>
<li>
Experience with Excel, PowerPoint and Enterprise Financial Planning Systems</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Preferred Skills</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
MBA</li>
<li>
Experience in investment banking, management consulting, corporate development or corporate strategy is a strong plus</li>
<li>
Experience working in enterprise software or SaaS</li>
</ul>
