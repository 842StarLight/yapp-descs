<h2>Customer Success Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>Job Description<br>
<br>
<p>The Customer Success Manager’s primary responsibilities are to take ownership of a portfolio of Conga customers, commit to their success, and relentlessly work to remove barriers to adoption both within their organization and cross-functionally within Conga.</p>
<p><b>
Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Lead, drive, and manage change within large, complex, enterprise organizations</li>
<li>
Become a trusted advisor and advocate for customers’ executive sponsor(s)</li>
<li>
Oversee implementations and ensure seamless transition from pre- to post-sales</li>
<li>
Remove adoption roadblocks by performing light system configuration</li>
<li>
Ensure high levels of impact and client satisfaction with the services rendered</li>
<li>
Up to 25% travel could be required</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Who you are:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
A change agent that can analyze multiple problems then develop and drive solutions</li>
<li>
You thrive working in the unknown, taking initiative and designing process when it doesn’t yet exist.</li>
<li>
You possess an understanding of the people, process, and technology realities that are part of transformation efforts, and approaches and tools to mitigate risks</li>
<li>
You see the big picture, but still roll up your sleeves and dig into the system to execute against your customer's’ business goals</li>
<li>
Impressive executive presence and communication abilities</li>
<li>
Passion for technology and for being a part of a fast-growing SaaS company</li>
</ul>
</div>
<br>
<div>Qualifications<br>
<br>
<p><b>Knowledge, Skills and Experience:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
3+ years’ experience in SaaS, process consulting, change management, project management strategic account management and executive engagement is a must</li>
<li>
Experience with technology stack and customization of: Salesforce, CLM Solutions, Smartsheet, Slack, Google Apps, Microsoft Office, etc. Salesforce Admin or Development certifications a plus</li>
<li>
BA/BS (MBA/Master preferred)</li>
</ul>
<br>
Additional Information<br>
<br>
<p>All your information will be kept confidential according to EEO guidelines.</p>
