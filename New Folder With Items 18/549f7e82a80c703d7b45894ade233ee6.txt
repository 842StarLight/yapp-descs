<h2>Account Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>The Opportunity</b></p>
<p>
Masabi is the world’s leading provider of mobile and full fare collection solutions for the public transport industry. With deployments to over 60 clients across 4 continents, Masabi’s team has been delivering innovation to millions of riders of public transportation for over ten years.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
The company is on a mission to build a multi-modal cloud-based fare engine that delivers true Mobility as a Service. As a first step, it has announced key partnerships utilizing its Software Development Kit to integrate public transit ticketing with rideshare (Uber) and journey planning (Transit) applications.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
Come join us on this exciting ride as we build the transit technology ecosystem of tomorrow.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
The Role</b></p>
<p>
As an Account Manager, you will act as the interface between our clients and Masabi, cultivating strong relationships and helping them maximise the benefits of Masabi’s ticketing technology.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
You will act as a consulting partner to leading transportation and transit technology partners and present the Masabi vision for full fare collection strategies and Mobility as a Service.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
You will build strong stakeholder relationships within agencies to gain access to decision makers and influencers who have the vision for full-fare collection strategies and can assist their understanding of the Masabi vision, and assist in developing the upgrade and migration path for Masabi’s future product strategy.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
You will negotiate commercial and legal terms that cover our contracts and contract extensions, upsells or revised business strategies and assist clients by developing marketing and launch plans.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
You will coordinate and collaborate with internal stakeholders (Product, Engineering, Marketing, R&amp;D and Services) to represent the customer voice and viewpoint on product quality, functionality, technology and direction.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
You will gain a deep understanding of our products and industry and will support your accounts with customer education, workshops, presentations and training.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Required skills and experience:</b></p>
<p></p>
<br>
<ul>
<li>
Strong customer advocacy and stakeholder management</li>
<li>
Commercially astute with proven negotiation skills; both external and internal</li>
<li>
Ability to handle complex customer relationship and building strong client relationships</li>
<li>
Experienced in account planning in a SaaS model and translation into financial plans and pipeline development</li>
<li>
Interest in transit, MaaS and smart-city initiatives</li>
<li>
Ability to present technical concepts and product strategies throughout all levels of an organization</li>
<li>
Demonstrated track record of account retention and upsell</li>
<li>
Strong communication and presentation skills; in-person, written</li>
<li>
Willingness to travel up to 30%</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
What you will learn:</b></p>
<p>
You will work with experienced Account and Project Managers who are delivering critical transit services to some of the world’s best known cities. You will learn much about internal stakeholder management as you coordinate between Project managers, Product Managers, Finance and Engineering. You will learn how to evangelize product and industry trends for smart cities and Masabi's global fare collection platform to leaders in the transportation field.</p>
