<h2>Sales Director - Advertising/Media Solutions team (Brands)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Numerator is looking for a top performing sales professional to join us. As a Sales Director, you will have the opportunity to work with some of the world’s most iconic brands to help bring to life their advertising, marketing, and pricing strategies through Numerator’s rich portfolio of solutions and insights. This role will be in charge of building out new relationships. Over the last five years, by leveraging the rise in smartphones and putting user experience first, we have successfully built the largest purchase panel in the world. Our data pipeline processes over 300k receipts per day, including purchases from both brick &amp; mortar and online.</p>
<p>
We are looking for a highly motivated salesperson with a passion for media, to join our team of media ninjas. In this role, <b>you will be selling a suite of media related planning and analytic tools</b>. The ideal candidate will be a 'Hunter' with proven experience opening new accounts and building c-suite relationships. Our ideal candidate will have <b>worked with (or sold to) brand marketing teams;</b> focus on media teams (Media Marketing Director type roles) a plus. The Sales Director will meet our customer retention, expansion and revenue growth objectives within our Advertising and Media Solutions team. We're looking for a true sales hunter with knowledge and experience within the CPG industry, who also understands the marketing and advertising world. Additionally, you will manage and maintain relationships with clients as you expand sales throughout your region.</p>
<p><b>
Responsibilities</b><b> include:</b>
</p>
<p>Create strategic account plan to meet sales quota.</p>
<p>
Identify appropriate brand leads/partners and book qualified meetings.</p>
<p>
Be able to demo a suite of media solutions, including our <b>Ad Intelligence software, Audience Targeting and Campaign Measurement solutions.</b></p>
<p>
Work with counterparts to solution sell to additional areas of current clients.</p>
<p>
Close business in a timely manner, utilizing salesforce throughout the sales process.
</p>
<p>Some level of domestic travel is required, but generally within the North East region.</p>
<ul>
<li>Hunter with enterprise SaaS software sales experience, with particular emphasis on building new relationships and opening new accounts</li>
<li>Proven track record of delivering high-quality results in a timely manner, including consecutively exceeding past sales quotas</li>
<li>Deep understanding of multiple facets of retailer, agency and consumer products with prior experience selling into marketing, shopper or consumer insights</li>
<li>Deep understanding of the sales process with the ability to engage senior level executives in strategic meetings to ensure top down alignment regarding strategy and objectives</li>
<li>Skilled at building and managing customer relationships, including stakeholder management</li>
<li>Strong oral, written, and presentation skills</li>
<li>Understanding of household panel data (NPD, Nielsen and/or IRI) is a plus</li>
<li>Microsoft Office expertise (Powerpoint, Excel, Word)<br>
 <br>
</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Experience Required</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s or Master’s Degree (preferred) with 5+ years relevant experience</li>
<li>Good sense of humor; we work hard, but like to have fun along the way.</li>
<li>Media experience, either as part of a brand team, or a seller to CPG clients.</li>
<li>A minimum of 5 years of software sales experience, selling into consumer products and retailers, in the functional areas of Brand Marketing, Market Research, Category Management, and/or Shopper &amp; Consumer Insights</li>
<li>Working knowledge of NPD, Nielsen or IRI data/solutions is a plus</li>
<li>Functional knowledge of Salesforce.com and its use for updating accounts, opportunities, and deal stages</li>
<br>
</ul>
