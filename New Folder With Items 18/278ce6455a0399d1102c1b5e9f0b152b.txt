<p>In the Director of Business Development role you will:</p>
<ul>
<li>Establish and maintain continuing favorable relationships through outreach with social service departments, hospitals, physician offices and other marketing and referral sources.</li>
<li>Be the driving force with achieving occupancy goals as budgeted throughout startup and stabilization.</li>

<li>Initiate and coordinate the visit of influential individuals or groups and other community members or groups to the community for planned visits, tours, meetings, presentations, etc.</li>

<li>Build personal relationships with the professionals as well as those seniors and their families looking to make a move.</li>
