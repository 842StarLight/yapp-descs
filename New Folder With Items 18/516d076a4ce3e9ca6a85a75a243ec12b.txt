<h2>ASSOCIATE RETAIL & VISITOR SERVICES</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p>Retail and Visitor Services Associate at El Museo del Barrio acts as ambassador of the institution. The Museum seeks an outgoing, reliable person to join our Retail &amp; Visitor Services department team. This person will be responsible for welcoming and engaging visitors to the Museum, providing general information. The Associate person will be performing duties at the gift shop, coat check and reception area.</p>
<p><b>
Gift shop / Coat-check:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Basic knowledge in credit card and cash transactions required</li>
<li>Provides excellent customer service to visitors, engaging and interacting with them</li>
<li>Uses knowledge of products and exhibitions to provide complete and accurate information to the Museum visitors, promoting our current programs, discounts, events</li>
<li>Sell tickets to visitors in a professional and courteous manner while also providing customer service regarding general customer inquiries</li>
<li>Receive and unpack, incoming merchandise as needed.</li>
<li>Prepares merchandise for sale, labeling,organizing and restocking under supervision</li>
<li>Persuades our customers to increase sells merchandise, admissions, memberships and tickets for events to reach monthly revenue goals</li>
<li>Maintains appearance of displays impeccable and shelves organized by sections with an emphasis on cleanliness and attention to detail.</li>
<li>Operate the Museum’s front desk and provide positive customer service experiences to all Museum guests, assisting them with information of the current exhibitions, programs and events.</li>
<li>Actively promote and sell the Museum memberships to reach monthly revenue goals</li>
<li>Assist guests receiving and tagging carefully and responsibly their belongings as yours</li>
<li>Performs other related duties as required</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Reception:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Receives visitors at the front desk by greeting, directing and announcing appropriately</li>
<li>Answers screening and forwarding incoming phone calls</li>
<li>Receives, sort and distribute daily mail/deliveries</li>
<li>Maintains office security bycontrolling access via the reception desk</li>
<li>Ensures reception area is tidy and presentable, with all necessary stationery and material</li>
<li>Updates conference room calendars daily</li>
<li>Forwards off-site emails to the corresponding person/staff in a professional manner</li>
<li>Notifies personnel of the departments of visitor arrival</li>
<li>Performs other related duties as required.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Qualifications:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>High school education required. At least 1 year experience in a retail operation and or visitor services, preferably a museum store</li>
<li>Have a basic knowledge of customer service best practices and possess excellent communication skills Enjoy working with the public, meeting new people, and working with volunteers</li>
<li>Show proficiency using technology/computer software (iPad, Microsoft Word, Excel, PowerPoint)</li>
<li>The person must be able to work a flexible schedule including weekends and holidays</li>
<li>May occasionally have to move cartons weighing up to 30 pounds.</li>
<li>Bilingual a must (English &amp; Spanish).</li>
<li>Basic cash register knowledge is required. Learn and use the museum retail system.</li>
<li>Telephone Skills, Verbal Communication</li>
</ul>
<p>To apply, please email resume and cover letter to jobs@elmuseo.org with “Associate Retail &amp; Visitor Services” in the subject line. No phone calls, please.</p>
