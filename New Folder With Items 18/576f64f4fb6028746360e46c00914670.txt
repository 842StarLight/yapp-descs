<h2>Bi-Lingual Assistant Community Office Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Bi-Lingual-Assistant Community Office Manager</b></p>
<p><b>General Responsibilities/Job Summary</b></p>
<p>The individual in this role will serve as the point person/translator for <i>Spanish</i> speaking customers and prospective customers.</p>
<p>The Assistant Community Office Manager may report to a Community Office Manager and coordinates the efficient daily operation of a full-service branch office. Ensures that customer service is provided including opening new accounts, completing loan applications, and answering routing customer inquiries. Participates in sales development activities to grow the enterprise and assist with achieving assigned branch goals. Assists with the coaching and development of branch staff along with providing input with regards to personnel issues including hiring, evaluations, and terminations.</p>
<ul>
<li>Work with the Community Office Manager to set goals, determine needs, and develop action plans to enhance sales and service.</li>
<li>Develop new branch business to increase deposit growth.</li>
<li>Proactively identifies and solicits cross sales opportunities. Refer business to bank partners.</li>
<li>Motivate and encourage staff to meet goals.</li>
<li>Review and Prepare Reports. Be able to analyze data related to the efficient operation of a branch office.</li>
<li>Follow all security measures, policies, and procedures related to the operation of the branch.</li>
<li>Performs other duties as assigned.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Minimum Qualifications</b></p>
<p><b>Education/Training: </b></p>
<ul><li>BS or BA degree or equivalent knowledge and/or banking experience.</li></ul>
<p><b>Knowledge/Skills/Abilities: </b></p>
<ul>
<li><b>Ability to communicate fluently in both Spanish and English is required</b></li>
<li>Excellent oral and written communication</li>
<li>Sound judgment</li>
<li>Strong interpersonal skills including the ability to work with all levels of employees and types of customers</li>
<li>PC proficiency</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Experience: </b></p>
<ul><li>At least 2 years of experience in related banking position with at least 1 year experience in related supervisory or management position.</li></ul>
<p><b>Required Certification/Licenses: </b></p>
<ul><li>NMLS License; Valid Driver’s License</li></ul>
<p><b>Travel Requirements: </b></p>
<ul><li>Localized travel within the bank’s footprint</li></ul>
<p><b>Physical and Mental Job Requirements</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Typically sits for sustained periods of time at a desk or in a vehicle.</li>
<li>Requires close visual acuity involved with preparing and analyzing bank documents offerings and reports, viewing a computer terminal and extensive reading.</li>
<li>Ability to receive and deliver detailed information through oral communication</li>
<li>Ability to lift at least 25lbs</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Working Conditions</b></p>
