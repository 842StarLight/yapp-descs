<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>About the job</b></h2>
<p>
As a Technical Account Manager, you are responsible for providing technical solutions and managing relationships with Google's largest and most strategic online publishers. As a liaison between clients and our technical staff, you flawlessly lead deployments, implementations and integrations of a variety of our product lines (such as Search, Mobile, Video and Commerce). From monitoring product performance to debugging and troubleshooting product code, you are the resident expert that addresses partner needs. You are a technical expert with an entrepreneurial drive and passion for client management.</p>
<p>The Android Partner Engineering team at Google works closely with the Android device ecosystem to extend the boundaries of what can be achieved with the Android platform. The team is deeply engaged with OEMs, carriers, chipset vendors, and ODMs across the globe and works in a fast-paced technology driven environment. Our aim is to make Android the platform of choice across multiple consumer and enterprise domains while ensuring that these devices remain secure and updated.<br>
<br>
As a Technical Account Manager, you will work on solving hard problems that the Android device ecosystem faces. You'll define and streamline processes for partner engagements, lead technical projects, and optimize implementations and integrations to increase business and drive new business opportunities. In this role, you'll take a lead on code and architecture reviews for new features from external stakeholders and gather and analyze data to help Google and its partners make informed decisions. Additionally, you will define the technical scope of projects with external partners and oversee the end-to-end execution.</p>
<p>
Whether working with next-gen chipset manufacturers or emerging market telcos, Google's Android Partner Engineering team helps solve some of the toughest mobile technology problems. We work with teams throughout Google to develop solutions for strategic partners around the globe. We maintain crucial relationships with and build technical solutions for our long-term partners who are making Android into a world-changing platform. But we're just getting started.</p>

Responsibilities</b></h2>
