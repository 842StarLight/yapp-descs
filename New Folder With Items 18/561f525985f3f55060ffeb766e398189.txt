<h2>Principal Cost Account Project Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p>Northrop Grumman Mission Systems is seeking a Control Account Manager (CAM) to support the Planning Decision Aid System (PDAS) program located in Landover MD. PDAS is a 100+ FTE full lifecycle development enterprise in operations, engineering, data management, and information assurance.<br>
As a CAM, the individual will work with a technical team and Task Order leadership to gather necessary information for cost control and forecasting the Task Orders (TO) financial data and delivering monthly program reports. The CAM will update EVM tools and produce required data products, review monthly EV data and provide detailed cost account status, and support program requirements for the EV battle rhythm. The Task Orders employ Agile Development methodologies and the CAM must be able to integrate that approach with the EVM requirements.<br>
</p>
<p><br>
Primary duties include: participate in CAM status calls, prepare status for/attend weekly Task Order status meeting, provide/enter weekly IMS status, support stakeholder meetings, review Actuals Hours reports for time charging accuracy and alignment with IMS status. On a monthly basis: calculate &amp; enter ETCs into the financial system, analyze EV metrics and generate VARs as required, and attend program VAR meetings. Support the development of the IPMR CDRL deliverable. As required identify/request IMS forecast and baseline changes via schedule change requests and "what if" schedules, and plan technical work, including scope, cost &amp; schedule, with direction from Task Order leadership. Participate in Integrated Baseline Reviews as required and maintain Work Authorization Documents (WADs) for each Control Account, ensuring the WADs are always up-to-date.<br>
</p>
<p><br>
The selected Team Member will use their skills and learning to achieve and maintain EV integrity across the Task Order, alert leadership to trends that may negatively impact TO, as well as perform other duties as assigned.<br>
</p>
