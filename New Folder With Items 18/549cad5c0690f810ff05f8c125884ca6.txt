<h2>Partner Development Representative</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p><b>YOU'LL BE RESPONSIBLE FOR</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Recruit, enable and manage MSP and Resale partners in assigned territory. Effectively articulate the requirements, expectations, and benefits of the Arctic Wolf Channel Program</li>
<li>Building strong relationships with channel partners to generate revenue and opportunities</li>
<li>Develop a cohesive business plan for the partners and execute effectively against that plan</li>
<li>Team with channel partners to build pipeline and close deals (joint call out days, spiff, campaigns, etc.).</li>
<li>Initiating lead generation programs with partners</li>
<li>Achieve against a set of pre agreed sales targets and KPI's on a monthly/quarterly/annual basis</li>
<li>Ensure consistency in terms of the company’s channel sales strategy &amp; execution</li>
<li>Liaise between inside/field sales teams (and CAMs) to support identification and recruitment (where needed) of partners with experience in both Resale and MSP</li>
<li>Building strong working relationships with sales, customer success and channel marketing</li>
<li>Field and route inbound partnership requests appropriately (support new on-boarding processes)</li>
<li>Provides impeccable support for assigned CAM teams and partners</li>
<li>Capturing of accurate and complete partner and customer data in SFDC</li>
<li>Outbound Calls:
<ul>
<li>Work with assigned partners to generate prospective customers through channel campaigns and channel promotions and account mapping activities</li>
<li>Promotes up-sell and new sales opportunities within partner’s existing and competitive accounts</li>
<li>Provide marketing/sales collateral to partners with prospective clients</li>
<li>Managing, administering and delivering certification training for partner sales and partner systems engineers</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>Inbound Calls:
<ul>
<li>Respond to partner requests and ensure proper field and inside sales alignment in partner end user opportunities</li>
<li>Track/monitor /support partner pipeline</li>
<li>Provide marketing/sales collateral and respond to channel inquiries regarding product, channel programs and channel promotions.</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>Work with other Arctic Wolf sales resources to support identified opportunities</li>
<li>Consistently articulate Arctic Wolf value proposition to assigned partners</li>
</ul>
<p><b>SKILLS AND REQUIREMENTS</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s Degree</li>
<li>Valid Passport</li>
<li>Proven experience of managing channel partners in a technology environment</li>
<li>Experience in facilitating onboarding and enablement activities for channel partners</li>
<li>Knowledge of software/services channel ecosystems</li>
<li>Familiarity with CRM and forecasting systems</li>
<li>Strong Negotiation skills</li>
<li>Strong Presentation skills</li>
<li>Strong Communication and Interpersonal skills</li>
<li>Excellent telephone communication skills</li>
<li>Ability to thrive in a team selling environment</li>

</li>
