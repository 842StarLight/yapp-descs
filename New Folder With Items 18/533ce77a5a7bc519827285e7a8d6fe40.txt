<h2>Sales Planner</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Haymarket Media, Inc. is seeking a <b>Sales Planner</b> to join our <b>Medical Communications Media Strategy</b> team. This position is based in our <b>New York, NY</b> office.</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Job Overview:</b></p>
<p>The Sales Planning team is crucial component of Haymarket Medical's success. It is a fast paced environment and a fantastic learning opportunity to grow within a rapid growing industry. The Sales Planner will be working with the sales team to develop proposals and presentations for our clients' strategic imperative.</p>
<p></p>
<p>As the primary contact for all pre-sale activity, this role will provide guidance on media plans that best align Haymarket's products and their value proposition to our client objectives to maximize revenue. This tole will also be working with our award winning editors, in-house marketing group, and our knowledgeable ad operations teams.</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Responsibilities:</b></p>
<p>Proposals:</p>
<ul>
<li>Partner with the sales team to understand client goals and objective to brainstorm strategic solutions</li>
<li>Own the RFP process for our medical advertisers by creating strategic proposals in response to advertiser requests, including the development and maintenance of: media kits, sales presentations, print and digital media plans</li>
<li>Uncover and address client needs while highlighting the value and capabilities of Haymarket products</li>
<li>Maintain in-depth knowledge of all advertising products, inventory, and pricing</li>
<li>Drive the pricing and negotiation strategy at opportunity level</li>
<li>Apply data/research to support Haymarket's value proposition in client-facing recommendations</li>
<li>Track RFPs to closed/sold opportunities</li>
<li>Develop knowledge of internal processes as they relate to AdOps, Marketing, and Editorial to provide succinct, comprehensive proposals with attainable goals</li>
<li>Work with internal teams to build custom audiences tailored for each client's needs under the guidance of manager</li>
<li>Carefully evaluate all RFP materials to accurately interpret client's objectives</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p>Sales Marketing:</p>
<ul>
<li>Develop general presentation across Haymarket Medical Brands</li>
<li>Provide Sales &amp; Media Strategists with Toolkits to hold key offerings across our digital and print portfolios</li>
<li>Packaging of ad sales products and promotions - provide pricing model and rate cards for products</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p>Competitive Intelligence:</p>
<ul>
<li>Research and present competitive intelligence to the Sales Organization with guidance from amanger</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Skills and Requirements:</b></p>
<p>Qualifications include:</p>
<ul>
<li>0-2 years of industry experience or related work</li>
<li>Strong communication skills and a logical thinker</li>
<li>Superior attention to detail and organiztion</li>
<li>Advanced Excel and PowerPoint skills</li>
<li>Knowledgeable of the latest digital advertising trends and online media</li>
<li>Ability to adapt to a fast paced environment</li>
<li>Bachelor's degree in related field</li>
</ul>
