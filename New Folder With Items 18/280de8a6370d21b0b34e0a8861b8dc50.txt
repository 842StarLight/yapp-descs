<h2>Client Service Representative</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Client Service Representative I – CSR I </b></p>
<p><b>Job Summary</b></p>
<p>This is an entry level position responsible for processing all release of information (ROI) specifically medical record requests in a timely and efficient manner ensuring accuracy and providing customers with the highest quality product and customer service. Associate must at all times safeguard and protect the patient’s right to privacy by ensuring that only authorized individuals have access to the patient’s medical information and that all releases of information are in compliance with the request, authorization, company policy and HIPAA regulations.</p>
<p><b>Qualifications and Skills: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>A High School Diploma or GED is required.</li>
<li>Must be able to communicate effectively in the English language.</li>
<li>Administrative experience in an office setting; previous release of information, medical records, or other related experience in a healthcare environment is preferred.</li>
<li>Proven customer service experience and/or training.</li>
<li>Ability to effectively use computer software and technology as required by the member facility including Microsoft Word and Excel</li>
<li>Ability to understand and become knowledgeable of Release of Information standards, policies &amp; procedures and HIPAA regulations and to complete work in compliance of these and other standards.</li>
<li>Ability to read and comprehend simple, healthcare terminology</li>
<li>Effective verbal and written communication skills.</li>
<li>Effective organizational skills a must</li>
<li>Ability to use fax, copier, microfilm machine, and multi-line phone system and other required work tools</li>
</ul>
