<div><b>Position Summary:</b></div>
<div>The Deal Desk Coordinator will focus on the accurate and timely processing of customer requests for Perforce software from various communication methods. The Position is responsible for receiving customer requests, customer setup, order entry, processing in Salesforce and fulfillment. In addition, Position may have post sales activities and maintaining the customer database. This position is a cross functional role that directly interacts with our Sales, Sales Operations, Finance, and Customer Support teams.</div>
<div></div>
<div><b>Responsibilities:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Follow up on inbound customer requests, not limited to email, phone and case management.</li>
<li>Works with various teams (deal desk, support, legal, sales, finance, PS) to ensure all requirements are met for order entry</li>
<li>Escalate issues to Sales and Sales Operations Teams as necessary and assist with revisions.</li>
<li>Ensure sales requests are processed in a timely manner with a strong focus during period-end deadlines.</li>
<li>Fulfill Order Requests, not limited to the following:
<ul>
<li>Order Entry and Processing</li>
<li>License Generation and Delivery</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>Maintain accuracy of customer database within Salesforce</li>
<li>Regularly provide detailed information to other departments such as order status, revenue recognition dates, pricing, etc.</li>
<li>Manages Quote to Order Processing Lifecycle for single-user</li>
<li>Assists Sales Operations Administrators with Licensing Requests</li>
<li>Month-end close responsibilities and other duties as assigned</li>
</ul>
<div><b>Requirements:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Related experience in order processing and invoicing, preferably in a software environment</li>
<li>Salesforce.com and NetSuite experience a plus</li>
<li>Associates Degree in Business, Accounting or related program is preferred</li>
<li>Proficiency in Microsoft Word, Excel and Salesforce is a plus</li>
<li>Demonstrated ability to communicate effectively</li>
<li>Must be detailed and organized</li>
<li>Must enjoy a fast-paced environment with the ability to be flexible and look for areas of improvement</li>
</ul>
