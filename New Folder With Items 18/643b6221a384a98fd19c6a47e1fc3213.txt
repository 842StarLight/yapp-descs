<b>
Job Summary</b>
</div>
<div>
<div>The Manager of Global Compensation will be responsible helping to develop and implement compensation philosophy and strategy for Black and Veatch. The ideal candidate will bring a global and innovative approach to compensation programs for all professionals worldwide.<br>
<br>
The Company is seeking top-flight individual who will report to the Vice President of Global Total Rewards and serve as a key member of the Human Resources team. The successful candidate will help drive business results and provide leadership and vision around strategic program development. The goal is to help attract, motivate and retain key talent for Black and Veatch. This individual will serve as an expert resource in building and implementing scalable infrastructure and processes and designing best practice rewards programs which encompass enterprise-wide compensation plans.</div>
</div>
</div>
<div>
<div>
<b>
Key Responsibilities</b>
</div>
<div><ul>
<li>Assess long-term business needs and assist with the design and implementation of compensation and rewards initiatives that will proactively support the Company’s long-term business strategy and future business requirements.
</li>
<li>Provide leadership and daily management of the compensation strategy, design, implementation and administration of programs for the global organization.
</li>
<li>Provide thorough evaluations and analyses on an ongoing basis ensuring compensation and reward programs are competitive and designed to meet the evolving needs of our global population.
</li>
<li>Work with the Vice President of Global Total Rewards regarding the Company’s annual executive compensation review process and the preparation of analyses and materials for the Compensation &amp; Development Committee of the Board of Directors.
</li>
<li>Work with the Vice President of Global Total Rewards in the review, analysis, design and implementation of the Company’s long-term and annual stock and cash-based incentive compensation programs; ensuring that the programs are competitive, are aligned with the Company’s strategic business strategy, objectives and performance and drive executive and employee engagement and performance.</li>
<li>Manage the delivery and communications of long-term, annual and variable compensation programs globally.</li>
<li>Provide subject matter expertise and guidance to line managers, HR Business Partners and other professionals regarding compensation strategy, administration, procedures/policies, and communications.
</li>
<li>Manage the global compensation team in delivering high levels of responsiveness and execution of compensation programs and processes.</li>
<li>Influence and manage domestic and international compensation benchmark processes including market pricing, base compensation salary structures, annual incentives and long-term incentive plans.</li>
<li>Assist in the design, implementation and administration of sales incentive plans that support business strategies and follow enterprise-wide design objectives.</li>
<li>Ensure, consistent, and unified global compensation program - benchmarking protocols; a systematic and consistent linkage between compensation and performance; developing and rolling out consistent global communications about the Company’s compensation philosophy and programs.</li>
</ul></div>
</div>
<div>
<div>
<b>Minimum Qualifications</b>
</div>
<div><ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree required.</li>
<li>A minimum of 8 years of experience in the development and management of compensation programs and implementing HRMS systems within a global business environment.
</li>
<li>Must have a strong track record of delivering results and operating as an internal consultant across all levels of management.</li>
<li>Must have strong skills in strategy, organizational capability, change management, business partnering and employee communications.</li>
<li>Specific and relevant experience with all elements of domestic and international broad-based compensation programs is required.
</li>
<li>Proven track record aligning compensation programs with a progressive environment and designing and delivering new programs.</li>
<li>Highly motivated and willing to roll up his/her sleeves.</li>
<li>Strong project management experience and effective organizational skills.</li>
</ul></div>
</div>
<div>
<div>
<b>Preferred Qualifications</b>
</div>
<div><ul>
<li>MBA or a CCP would be of significant value.</li>
<li>Persuasive and self-confident, with excellent communications skills and executive presence to interact effectively with senior management, the HR Leadership team and HRBP’s.</li>
<li>Familiarity with executive compensation and stock and cash-based long-term incentive programs a plus.</li>
<li>Enthusiastic; a capacity to overcome barriers and make impactful change</li>
<li>Confident and results driven leader; strategic with a consultative orientation, strong people and process/project management capability.</li>
<li>Superior intellect; with a combination of business acumen, analytical skills, conceptual ability and judgment.</li>
<li>Outstanding communicator; an individual who possesses superior written/oral communication skills, as well as the ability to present his/her views in a clear and compelling manner.</li>
<li>Strong interpersonal skills; someone who can forge close relationships, influence decisions, and who will be sought out for their opinions and counsel.</li>
<li>Must possess a business viewpoint and think and communicate strategically.
</li>
<li>Must have a strong customer focus and be able to effectively and quickly build relationships and establish trust, respect, competence, and confidence.</li>
<li>Must be proactive and have the ability to operate at a fast pace with clients and ever-changing business needs.</li>
<li>Strong drive, work ethic, and a sense of urgency.</li>
<li>Tenacity and ability to build from scratch are imperative.</li>
<li>Successful candidate must be flexible and willing to travel globally as required.</li>
</ul></div>
