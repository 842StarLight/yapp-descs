<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>WHAT YOU WILL DO:</b></h2>
<p>The Director of Internal Logistics leads a team of associates to create, develop and manage strategy that will ensure accurate, timely and efficient material flow through the business. In the areas of Materials Planning, Inventory Control and Internal Logistics (receiving, shipping and internal handling), this leader owns key metrics of material transaction accuracy, material availability, stock accuracy, obsolescence risk, on-time shipping, logistics total cost and inventory turns. In the area of Logistics Engineering, this leader is responsible for developing, implementing and training of a sustainable logistics system.<br>
<br>
<b>Managerial Functions:</b><br>
</p>
<ul>
<li>
Creates and implements development plans as appropriate for staff</li>
<li>
Holds subordinates accountable and addresses poor performance</li>
<li>
Conducts performance reviews in a timely fashion and gives clear and honest feedback.</li>
<li>
Demonstrates responsibility for budget and effective utilization of resources – plans and manages resources to maximize results</li>
</ul>
<b>
Internal Logistics:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Manage internal logistics team members and ensure that procedures, processes and training systems are in place for them to be successful</li>
<li>
Holding people accountable to follow the implemented processes</li>
<li>
Manage the receiving, shipping and internal material handling supervisors in order to on time and sustainable deliver parts to the assembly lines</li>
</ul>
<b>
Inventory Control:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Develop strategy and systems for inventory management and cycle counting to ensure optimal accuracy</li>
<li>
Responsible for the accuracy of raw and WIP inventory</li>
<li>
Resolve stock and missing parts issues as soon as they come up</li>
<li>
Apply root cause analysis for all stock and missing parts issues</li>
</ul>
<b>
Logistics Engineering:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Implements best practices to ensure an uninterrupted flow of material in a safe, efficient and cost effective manner using internal and external storage locations</li>
<li>
Develop an internal logistics strategy including procedures/processes for the storage and movement of manufactured and purchased components</li>
<li>
Participate in global bench marking activities to create best in class processes and procedures</li>
</ul>
<b>Materials Planning:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Maintain high component material availability while also meeting inventory velocity goals</li>
<li>
Collaborate with purchasing to identify key suppliers to focus on lead time reduction, stock programs and other improvements</li>
<li>
In partnership with Production Management, utilize and improve master scheduling processes to balance and stabilize resources in the plant while also meeting the needs of the customer</li>
<li>
Maintain and improve transaction accuracy and integrity in all material-related transactions. Drive the use of root cause analysis for system and process failures</li>
<li>
Improve, create and implement processes that improve departmental efficiency while maintaining system accuracy</li>
<li>
Develop robust communication and tracking systems that effectively and proactively inform production and other departments of issues and changes</li>
<li>
Establish and implement ERP(SAP) systems and best practices related to all aspects of material management including planning, warehousing and internal logistics</li>
<li>
Implement and manage a best in class excess and obsolete inventory system that minimizes potential loss to the organization</li>
<li>
Eliminate material delivery issues through use of root cause analysis</li>
</ul>
<b>
Lean and continuous improvement:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Drives lean deployment to ensure continuous improvement</li>
<li>
Coaches and mentors team regarding lean concepts to develop a culture of waste elimination</li>
<li>
Leads Kaizens to target inefficiencies and optimize processes</li>
<li>
Develop KPIs to ensure achievement of all plant goals</li>
</ul>
