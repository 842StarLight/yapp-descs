<h2>Real Estate Analyst</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>The Real Estate Analyst is responsible for the analysis of all real estate financial, accounting, and web based software and database systems. Under the direction of the Executive Director of Real Estate, this person analyzes and prepares all real estate department and asset management reports; monitors, updates and analyzes budgets for real estate holdings and department; audits and exports all financial transactions for upload into University accounting system; and monitors, prepares, and processes all accounting and financial support for University real estate assets and transactions. This position is responsible for system updates, installation and training for all real estate systems including Yardi asset management system, REO website, and the mortgage office loan servicing software system.</p>
<p><b>
Duties</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Oversee and ensure timely processing all Real Estate Property and Department Accounts Payable activity, which includes property taxes, utilities, gardening, repairs/maintenance, pool/spa, security, homeowner association dues, insurance, supplies, cash management, employee reimbursements, etc.</li>
<li>Prepare and analyze reporting as required for real estate, receivable, and payable holdings; audit property transactions for accuracy. Prepare reporting as required for tenants, borrowers; prepare and analyze financial statements for REO department; produce and analyze performance reports all properties, providing periodic and year-end and Board financial reports; provide Tenant Aging Reports; prepare Yardi bank reconciliation reporting; provide monthly Yardi reports on current properties.</li>
<li>Process all journal entries and update systems and accounts as required for acquisition and sale transaction closings; maintain and update chart of accounts for all systems; ensure that system is updated with all property data information.</li>
<li>Conduct all system upgrades and updates as required for listings, properties, tenants, and borrowers (MLS, Yardi, The Mortgage Office, Website), sporadically updating information into MLS (Multiple Listing Service); process all technological updates and assistance point-of-contact for systems; perform regular duties and updates of Yardi; extract information from the Pepperdine People soft system online for various reports.</li>
<li>Oversee and ensure timely processing of all Real Estate Tenant receipts; credit to proper account in Yardi software; analyze data and prepare appropriate aging reports; ensure system is updated with all tenant information.</li>
<li>Conduct staff system training as required to ensure full and accurate utilization of all systems by staff.</li>
<li>Analyze and implement systems and process automation as needed and/or required.</li>
<li>Maintain and update all REO department website information as needed.</li>
<li>Develop budgets for real estate assets and REO department. Obtain and analyze variances and submit revisions.</li>
<li>Post monthly commissions, rents, and close Yardi system. Analyze tenants for lease escalations and recharges.</li>
<li>Oversee and ensure timely completion of credit card account reconciliations.</li>
<li>Monitor real estate bank account balances and submit bank cash transfer requests as necessary.</li>
<li>Process year end IRS 1098 and 1099 statements for borrowers and vendors.</li>
<li>Perform other duties as assigned.</li>
<li>Uphold University mission through work performed.</li>
</ul>
