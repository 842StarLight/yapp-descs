<p><b>Job Summary</b></p>
<div>SWC Technology Partners, a BDO USA Company, is a growing IT Solutions Provider. We believe that technology drives innovation, but people are what makes it a success. We are committed to creating a work culture where our employees are happy, empowered and excited to come to work every day. Our commitment to each other is why SWC is a recognized leader for our culture, employee satisfaction and career growth opportunities. Our dynamic team is made up of problem-solvers driven by a desire to help customers succeed. We’re looking for people who share that same drive and who are looking to combine teamwork with technology to produce amazing results.<br>
<br>
The Engagement Manager (EM) is responsible for acquiring new clients by identifying technology needs that add value to their business. The EM has a focus on new business development and account management of new and existing accounts.<br>
<br>
The successful candidate will have a proven track record in Business to Business (B2B) IT sales engagements midsize business organizations (average 200 – 2000 users). This role is a mix of business development, account management and understanding how technology solutions can address the customer’s business needs. This role is responsible for building strong, successful partnerships with our customers.</div>
</div>
