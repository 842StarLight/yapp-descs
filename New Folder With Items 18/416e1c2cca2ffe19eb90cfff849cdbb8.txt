<p>Do you enjoy recruiting in an environment where hiring top talent is a companywide priority? We are seeking an experienced full-time, in-house Corporate Recruiter. <p>
<b>What You Will Be Doing</b></p>

<li>Identify niche skill candidates based on job specifications</li>

<li>Determine appropriate recruiting sources for advertising and posting positions and promote the company image to candidates and external service providers</li>
<li>Apply thorough knowledge of full lifecycle recruiting to fill positions quickly</li>
<li>Serve as the first point of contact for candidates at all levels</li>
<li>Conduct telephone screens to further qualify applicants for all open requisitions</li>
<li>Ensure timely communications with all candidates and hiring managers</li>
<li>Network to develop candidate flow</li>
<li>Other duties as assigned to support the Human Resources/Operations team</li>
</ul>
<p><b>What You Need for this Position</b></p>
<ul>
<li>At least 2 years of experience in a fast-paced, full life cycle recruiting environment</li>
<li>Bachelor's degree preferred</li>
<li>Ideal candidate comes from a corporate recruiting position with a fast growth company, or has an agency background in permanent placements</li>
<li>Candidates should be highly skilled in a variety of internet recruiting techniques</li>
<li>Experience recruiting and building rapport with C-level executives</li>
<li>Proficient working with application tracking systems such as Taleo, Cornerstone or Hirebridge.</li>
<li>Strong candidate qualification and interview skills required</li>
<li>The ability to prioritize, manage time well, and work closely with hiring managers to quickly address needs and adjust focus as needed</li>
<li>Quick learner, with a positive attitude and ability to work well within a team</li>
<li>Ability to treat sensitive and confidential information with appropriate discretion</li>
<li>Ability to contribute ideas for creative recruiting techniques</li>
<li>Ability to offer recommendations to Hiring Leaders</li>
</ul>
