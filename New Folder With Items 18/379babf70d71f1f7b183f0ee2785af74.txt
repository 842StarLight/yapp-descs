<p><b>JOB TITLE: Marketing Research, Data and Analytics Leader</b><br>

<p><b><i>
About the role:
</i></b></p>
<p>We are looking for a Marketing Research, Data and Analytics Leader to manage and lead a dynamic marketing research and data and analytics team.</p>
<p>
In this role, you should have a background in marketing research (qualitative and quantitative) and data and business analysis. You should be analytical, have business acumen and problem-solving aptitude, and excellent leadership qualities.</p>
<p>
Ultimately, you will enhance our marketing research, analytics and reporting ecosystem to help us make better decisions.</p>
<p></p>
<p><b><i>
What’s in it for you?
</i></b></p>
<p>An opportunity to be a central part in a rapidly expanding business in a world class company. A chance to test and apply every skill you have and develop new ones. A chance to build a solution that has never been built before for a market comprised of some of the most mission driven and kind professionals in the world while improving the outcomes of millions of pets. A career in a world class and innovative organization.</p>
