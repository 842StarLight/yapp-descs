<h2>Digital Product Manager II</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>As a part of TIAA’s Financial Solutions business, TIAA Bank, a division of TIAA, FSB, provides full-service, nationwide banking and lending services to consumer, commercial and institutional clients through a variety of channels, including online, its Florida-based financial centers and at other business offices throughout the country.<div>
<br>
<div><b>
PRIMARY CHARACTERISTICS</b></div>
<div>
The Digital Product Manager II is responsible for managing strategic process and solution enhancements for a specific set of features from conception to completion. The position has responsibility related to client experience for researching, selecting, scoping and planning new solution developments, as well as enhancing existing solutions or technologies they support. This position will need to be able to effectively enact change by organizing strategic and tactical initiatives supported by internal and industry trends while collaborating with key partners to ensure organizational goals are met. The ability to help define both short term and long term strategy and lead tactical projects for implementation is essential. A background and understanding of digital financial solutions is also valuable.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
</div>
<div><b>
ESSENTIAL FUNCTIONS</b></div>
<div><ul>
<li><div>
Manage overall planning process for new features and initiatives for designated products, solutions, and business lines</div></li>
<li><div>
Serves as lead contact and product knowledge expert for solutions supported by the role - identifying and assessing strategic partners, defining and constructing requirements and working closely with other functional managers through project completion and providing strategic assessments of evolving technologies with focus on next-generation initiatives</div></li>
<li><div>
Assist in developing strategies to sustain and grow TIAA Bank’s competitive position</div></li>
<li><div>
Use written and verbal communication skills to develop fact based industry research documentation, business cases, use cases, or presentations as part of the digital growth, implementation or management review processes</div></li>
<li><div>
Identify, compile and understand data related to the solution’s effectiveness</div></li>
<li><div>
Develop pro formas, charts, reports or competitive analysis pertaining to solution development within the digital channel</div></li>
<li><div>
Lead research of product and process improvements that will maximize performance, reduce expense, generate income, or reduce risks associated with the suite of solutions managed</div></li>
<li><div>
Act as a sponsor to solution-related Marketing or Web requirements; provide effective communication of the requirements and ensure accuracy of execution through effective quality assurance</div></li>
<li><div>
Collaborate with cross functional partners to ensure business functions and process management protocols are followed for timely and effective delivery of stated requirements</div></li>
<li><div>
Demonstrate significant knowledge of solution requirements when working with business areas such as sales, IT, marketing, risk, legal, products, compliance, and operations to evaluate, scope, and implement initiatives</div></li>
<li><div>
Ensure transparency of management communication and consensus on priorities and initiatives.</div></li>
</ul></div>
