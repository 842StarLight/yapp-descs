<h2>Manager Trainee</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b><i>First Peoples Community Federal Credit Union</i></b></p>
<p><b>POSITION DESCRIPTION</b></p>
<p><b>POSITION PURPOSE</b></p>
<p>This position will prepare you to be an Assistant Branch Manager. In this position you will learn to direct and participate in the day-to-day operations of a branch, ensuring that it meets its production and service level goals.</p>
<p><b>ESSENTIAL FUNCTIONS AND BASIC DUTIES</b></p>
<ul>
<li>In charge of directing and monitoring staff to ensure that they are providing the highest level of service to our membership.</li>
<li>Involved in business development and community activities.</li>
<li>Takes ownership of the branch. This includes resolving member complaints and problems, handling emergency situations, and ensuring branch activities are in compliance with established Credit Union policies and procedures.</li>
<li>Responsible for communicating branch needs to the branch manager.</li>
<li>Assists members with the opening and closing of all account and loan types.</li>
<li>Provides members routine information concerning their account. Directs member to appropriate department for more complex or specialized service needs.</li>
<li>Maintains accurate member and employee records. This includes account files, signature cards, scanned records, time cards, reviews, etc.</li>
<li>Takes an active role in learning and understanding our members’ needs and to fulfill those needs by cross-selling the appropriate credit union products and services.</li>
<li>Responsible for scheduling of branch staff.</li>
<li><i>Performs other duties as assigned.</i></li>
</ul>
<p><b>PERFORMANCE MEASUREMENTS &amp; EXPECTATIONS</b></p>
<ul>
<li><i>Job duties are efficiently, accurately, and effectively performed in accordance with established policies and standards.</i></li>
<li><i>Security procedures are understood and adhered to by all Service Representatives.</i></li>
<li><i>Transaction drawers are to be balanced within fifteen minutes after the department has shutdown or within fifteen minutes of serving their last member.</i></li>
<li><i>Good business relations exist with members. Members' problems or questions are courteously and promptly resolved.</i></li>
<li><i>To establish a good working relationship through teamwork and coordination with co-workers and management, both within and external to the department.</i></li>
<li><i>To insure that reports and records are accurate and completed in a timely manner.</i></li>
<li><i>The Credit Union’s professional reputation is maintained and conveyed.</i></li>
<li>Serves as the role model for providing excellent service in all areas of the branch.</li>
<li>Responsible for building a strong team environment.</li>
<li>To maintain and develop a qualified staff to meet the needs of members and the branch’s future growth plans.</li>
<li>To meet or exceed sales and service level goals.</li>
<li>To complete performance reviews on staff.</li>
<li><i>Comply with the Information Security Policy and the Business Ethics and Conduct Code</i></li>
</ul>
<p><b>QUALIFICATIONS</b></p>
<p><b>Education/Certification: </b>High school graduate or equivalent.</p>
<p><b>Required Knowledge: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>A thorough understanding and knowledge of teller, member services and loan policies and procedures.</li>
<li>Understanding of Credit Union operations.</li>
<li>Understanding of the credit unions products and services.</li>
<li>Understanding of Credit Union philosophy.</li>
<li>Knowledge of basic accounting.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Experience Required: </b></p>
<ul><li>Six months to two years of experience as a financial service representative at a full-service financial institution. (preferred)</li></ul>
<p><b>Skills/Abilities: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Strong leadership skills</li>
<li>Excellent communication skills.</li>
<li>Good interpersonal skills.</li>
<li>Teambuilding skills.</li>
<li>Ability to delegate and multi-task.</li>
<li>Attention to detail.</li>
<li>Solid math skills.</li>
<li>Ability to operate related computer software, and other business equipment including 10-key, money counters, and telephone.</li>
</ul>
