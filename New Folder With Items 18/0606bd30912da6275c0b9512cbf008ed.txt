<h2>Technical Program Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><ul>
<p>The Advanced Concepts division of Solute is an organization of world class technologists that builds cutting edge technology in cutting edge Software, Cyber Security, Data Analytics, Cloud Computing, Mobility, and Advanced Networking. We foster an open, innovative work environment and collaborate daily on building the latest and greatest technology solutions on the planet..
</p>
<p>Solute Advanced Concepts is looking for a Sales Engineer to join our division, conducting relationship management, building new sales pipelines, and providing technical expertise, guidance, architecture, and support in many different technologies directly to government customers. Applicants with an <b>ACTIVE SECRET CLEARANCE</b> are strongly preferred and must possess excellent communication skills in addition to impressive technical acumen.</p>

Requirements</b></p>
<li>
<b>
ACTIVE SECRET CLEARANCE</b><br>
</li>

Managing the Agile process with Sprint planning, Sprint retrospectives, scrums, and backlog grooming</li>
<li>
Creating and delivering contractual deliverables (CDRL)</li>
<li>
Background in STEM (preferably Software Development)</li>
<li>
Knowledgeable in the effective management of a matrix team</li>
<li>
The ability to lead a project team to success and manage cost, schedule, and risk for multiple efforts</li>
<li>
Experience presenting to and communicating with government and defense contractor customers</li>
<li>
Ability to design and suggest innovative solutions to address prospective challenges</li>
<li>
Able to function effectively in a DoD environment</li>
<li>
Strong communication skills to collaborate effectively with team members and customers</li>
<li>
Willingness to participate in training/certification to acquire and maintain knowledge necessary to be effective in the role</li>
<li>
Experience in setting and achieving mid and long-term goals</li>
