<h2>ANALYST II, BSC - CASE, MDC</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<div><div>
<ul>
<li>Support the Category Leadership in executing Merchandise Planning functions to produce Best in Class planograms for our retail and wholesale partners.</li>
<li>
Develop and analyze merchandise assortments that deliver overall category growth through a combination of internal merchandising tools coupled with retailer specified guidelines.
</li>
<li>Analyze retail and market level data to develop planogram schematics utilizing JDA Space Planning software platform.</li>
<li>
Create relevant product assortments and optimal space planning that drive sales across the business.</li>
<li>
Assist with administrative responsibilities prior to retail project sets such as data cleansing, planogram management, planogram retrieval, reviewing retail and category management guidelines, etc.</li>
<li>
Structured space planning software and company overview training program provides basic industry and specific role knowledge prior to executing live merchandising planograms.</li>
<li>
Certified JDA Space Planning training available for space planning software provides industry recognition and elevates ability to work within the software.
</li>
<li>Certified Category Management web based training available to assist with industry, analytical and category management skills.</li>
<li>
Learn CPG industry specific sales processes and merchandise strategies through collaborative projects.</li>
</ul>
<br>
<br>
</div></div>

<div>
<b>Job Qualifications</b><div>
</div>
<ul>
<li>
Undergraduate or Graduate in the field of Business, Marketing, Merchandising, Retail Management, or other equivalent degree or equivalent work experience
</li>
<li>Experience in business, marketing, or related field a plus.</li>
<li>
Proficiency with Microsoft Office, Outlook, Word, and Excel. Access is a plus.</li>
<li>
Excellent analytic skills</li>
<li>
Ability to collaborate with a wide range of professionals, strong oral and written communication skills, good judgment, time-management skills, and creativity.</li>
<li>
Excellent organizational skills/ability to prioritize</li>
<li>
CPG industry or alcoholic beverage sales experience is a plus.</li>
<li>
Flexible and capable of adapting to rapidly changing situations</li>
<li>
Ability to follow established protocol and work independently or within a team</li>
<li>
Ability to relocate is preferred.</li>
</ul>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div><b>WORKING CONDITIONS:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>A Merchandising Specialist may work as a member of a team or individually to complete projects.
</li>
<li>A considerable amount of time is spent working at a computer to analyze and design merchandising schematics.
</li>
<li>Minor overnight travel may be required throughout the year, but is not anticipated.</li>
<li>
The required attire is considered smart casual with jeans acceptable.</li>
<li>
Overtime may be required during peak times or in cases of project deadlines.</li>
</ul>
