What We Offer:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
A family environment with a commitment to honest values</li>
<li>
Full time day hours during the week between 7:00am and 5:00pm. Enjoy nights and weekends off.</li>
<li>

<p><b>
The Position:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Greet and assist all customers and potential customers in a professional and courteous manner – are stores are always open house ready!</li>
<li>
Help identify customer's needs and provide them with knowledgeable assistance to ensure they select the correct tool or equipment to serve their needs</li>
<li>
Answer customer questions over the phone and in person and process quotes and customer orders in our computer system</li>
<li>
Work with sales staff to fulfill customer requirements</li>
<li>
Stay up to date and informed on new and current products and services offered at Total Tool</li>
<li>
Assist in maintaining a safe work environment by adhering to safety standards</li>
<li>
Rolling with the punches when it comes to taking on new and exciting challenges to BEST serve our customers</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
The Requirements:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Minimum of one year of previous customer service experience</li>
<li>
Strong computer skills and ability to learn new software</li>
<li>
Ability to communicate effectively with customers</li>
<li>
Ability to work in a team environment and respect your co-workers</li>
<li>
Possess an attention to detail</li>
<li>
Ability to lift up to 50 lbs</li>
<li>
Ability to stand on a hard surface for extended periods of times</li>
</ul>
