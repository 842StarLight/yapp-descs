<h2>Field Applications Engineer 4</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<ul>
<li>Provide technical support to customers relative to Lattice products throughout the design process</li>
<li>
Make &amp; give custom sales presentations and provide creative, thorough and practical problem resolution</li>
<li>
Provide system level guidance to customers implementing designs with Lattice products</li>
<li>
Act as an expert in hardware, software and ISP technology with a thorough understanding of board and device challenges</li>
<li>
Conduct technical training of Distributor personnel</li>
<li>
Assist in the training of Distributor Field Applications Engineers (DFAEs)</li>
<li>
Manage DFAEs in the territory and track Design Win performance</li>
<li>
Provide technical back up for DFAE issues and escalations</li>
<li>
Effectively position Lattice products against the competition</li>
<li>
Provide detailed system architectural solution proposals to customers</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Required Skills</b></p>
<p>
Expert knowledge of Verilog or VHDL for FPGAs. Expert knowledge of timing closure considerations in FPGAs. Expert knowledge of synthesis and simulation tools. Extensive knowledge of high-speed signaling and designing for signal integrity. Extensive understanding of competitive PLD, FPGA, and ASIC design alternatives.</p>
<p>
Expertise in some or all of the following technologies with an understanding of both design and debug of these protocols at the FPGA and board level:</p>
<ul>
<li>
High speed generic DDR interfaces</li>
<li>
Memory DDRx interfaces</li>
<li>
High Speed SERDES</li>
<li>
PCI Express, SGMII, GbE, XAUI</li>
<li>
MIPI DSI/CSI-2</li>
<li>
HDMI/DVI or SMPTE video</li>
<li>
JTAG, SPI &amp; I2C</li>
</ul>
<p>
Self motivated and a driven performer. Strong written and verbal communications skills and the ability to work with multiple groups and in a matrix management structure. Must be detail oriented with strong customer support skills. Travel required. <b>Position is based in Seattle.</b></p>
<p></p>
