<p><b>Office Administrator &amp; Bookkeeping</b></p>
<p><b>Fully Organic LLC </b>is one of the fastest growing manufacturers and producers of all-natural dog chews and treats. We are looking for the right fit for our highly dynamic and adaptive team.</p>
<p>Essential Duties and Responsibilities include the following (Other duties may be assigned):</p>
<p>Coordinating external company communications with potential and existing clients, vendors, and service providers.</p>
<p>Entering daily transactions into QuickBooks such as (checks, invoices, bills into our clients’).</p>
<p>Contacting clients and vendors regarding Accounting matters (reconciliations, revisions).</p>
<p>Reviewing inventories, Account receivables, Accounts Payables daily, weekly, and monthly for accurate reporting.</p>
<p>Finding and ordering office and warehouse supplies and materials.</p>
<p>Organizing and filing documents and overall office organization.</p>
<p>Reconciling financial reports, such as balance sheet and profit and loss statements, monthly reporting, and inventory reports in QuickBooks.</p>
<p>Assisting/preparing monthly management reports such as (cash flow statements, PL statements, and monthly balance sheets) in Excel format.</p>
<p>Assist company CPA with tax preparation of monthly sales, and Federal Tax obligations among others.</p>
