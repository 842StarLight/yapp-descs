<h2>Account Manager - Construction Supply (SEA)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Steeler is a family owned and operated steel stud manufacturer since 1974.</p>
<p>Steeler is looking for a motivated, self-starting individual with the ability and willingness to hit the ground running and be the friendly face our customers need. The ideal candidate must be familiar with the construction industry as your 2 primary roles will be developing new accounts and growing current client business.</p>
<p><b>Candidates must be familiar with the construction industry preferably in commercial building but not required. </b></p>
<p>As an outside salesperson you will have the support of the branch to assist you with quotes and taking of orders.</p>
<p>Key Job Responsibilities</p>
<ul>

<li>Generating Job quoting opportunities</li>
<li>Continually enhancing personal knowledge of available products</li>
<li>Achieving sales plans and goals</li>
</ul>
<p>Qualifications</p>

<li>Self motivated, enthusiastic and looking to build a long term career</li>
<li>Excellent customer service skills and passionate about the job</li>
<li>Strong verbal and written communication skills.</li>
</ul>
