<li>Negotiation Skills</li>
<li>Leadership</li>
<li>Oral and Written Communication</li>
<li>Sales and customer relations</li>
<li>Organization and planning</li>

<p><b>Education/Experience:</b></p>
<ul>

<li>Previous dispatching and construction experience a plus</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Compensation:</b> Position includes base salary, benefits and performance bonuses</p>
