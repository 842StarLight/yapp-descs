WHAT WE'RE LOOKING FOR
<br><br>
We are looking for a hands-on Accounts Receivable Analyst to join our Finance team. This role has the opportunity to be an integral piece of a growing company and gain experience in the area of accounts receivable, cash collections, sales tax, and revenue recognition. This role will report to the Accounts Receivable Manager.
<br><br>
WHAT YOU'LL DO
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Assist in monthly billing process by ensuring timely and accurate invoicing to customers</li>
<li>Process invoices and sales orders on a regular basis</li>
<li>Perform customer credit checks</li>
<li>Monitor collections regularly, focusing on lowering DSO and bad debt</li>
<li>Ensure accuracy of sales tax charged to customers and assist in regular sales tax filings</li>
<li>Assist in establishing controls and procedures around the billing cycle</li>
<li>Work cross functionally with sales operations to ensure proper invoicing to customers</li>
<li>Perform analytical analysis over KPIs related to billing cycle</li>
</ul>
