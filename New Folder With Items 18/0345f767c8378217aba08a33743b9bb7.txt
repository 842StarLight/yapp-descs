<h2>Business Development Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p>We currently have an opening for a Business Development Manager supporting our North American Kimberly-Clark Home Professional Sales Team. This role is based in Rodgers, Arkansas and will work out of our KC Sales Office – reporting to the Sales Director, KC Home Professional. Retailer accounts supported by this role will primarily be in the Mass and Club channels.
<br>
<br>
<b>KEY RESPONSIBILITIES
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Deliver profitable growth for Kimberly-Clark by building and executing joint business plans with our retail partners
</li>
<li>Implement Kimberly-Clark brand strategies and tactics with the customer by reaching out to the key decision makers
</li>
<li>Manage customer relationships, business planning, and trade management utilizing cross-functional resources and data sources
</li>
<li>Develop and lead the creation of sales presentations / decks for joint business planning, category / line reviews, new distribution pitches, and promo planning
</li>
<li>Lead event execution – includes forecasting, promotional planning, coordinating / attending trade shows, and tracking sales performance
</li>
<li>Expand assortment – leveraging product innovation, identifying category needs / gaps, and providing Kimberly-Clark solutions to drive growth with retail partners
</li>
<li>Track spending – includes sales agency commissions, advertising and promotional funds, and budget management
</li>
<li>Recommend and implement process improvements, to increase efficiency within the team. Also, enhance our customer relationships by supporting retailer led initiatives
</li>
<li>Provide a commitment to quality performance through personal example by adopting a customer-based approach to business support
</li>
<li>Demonstrate a source of business knowledge and problem-solving capabilities (leverage insights, identify risks / opportunities, and recommend action-based solutions) supported by analytics
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Qualifications:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree required
</li>
<li>A proven track record of high performance
</li>
<li>Minimum of 5 years of relevant work experience
</li>
<li>Experience in the CPG industry with a focus on Club / Mass retailers (preferred)
</li>
<li>Ability to develop joint business plans, manage trade / marketing budgets, and forecast
</li>
<li>Solid knowledge of PC applications (Windows, Word, Excel, PowerPoint) is required.
</li>
<li>Ability to work collaboratively in a matrix organization
</li>
<li>Sound business judgment, problem-solving skills, and analytical expertise
</li>
<li>Demonstrates Strong interpersonal, communication and presentation skills
</li>
</ul>
