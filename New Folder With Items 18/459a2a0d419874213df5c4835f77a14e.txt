<h2>General Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>A national leader in luxury fitness is looking for amazing talent to join our team and seeking a motivated General Manager to lead our studio to success! This position will be based in the SW Waterfront in the Portland area.</p>
<p><b>
POSITION:</b> The General Manager will oversee all studio functionality from sales to studio operations.</p>
<p><b>
REQUIREMENTS:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
2+ years of fitness sales or relevant sales experience preferred</li>
<li>
Confident in generating personal sales and supervising Sales Reps</li>
<li>
Ability to manage and drive multiple revenue streams including memberships and retail</li>
<li>
Previous management or supervisory experience preferred</li>
<li>
Must have excellent communication and strong interpersonal skills in person and over the phone</li>
<li>
Must be solution-based and results oriented, competitive spirit</li>
<li>
Ability to recognize areas of improvement and make changes using good judgement</li>
<li>
An affinity and passion for fitness</li>
<li>
Solid writing and grammar skills</li>
<li>
Highly organized, proficient in data management, ability to prioritize and meet deadlines</li>
<li>
Professional, punctual, reliable and neat and organized</li>
<li>
Strong attention to detail and accuracy</li>
<li>
Trustworthy and ability to handle confidential information</li>
<li>
Ability to work harmoniously with co-workers, clients and the general public</li>
<li>
Proficiency with computers and Studio software</li>
<li>
College Degree Preferred</li>
</ul>
<ul><li>This is a full-time position. Candidate must be able to work a flexible schedule with nights and weekend availability.</li></ul>
<p><b>
DUTIES</b>:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Lead generation including Grass Roots Marketing and networking</li>
<li>
Implement sales process to schedule prospects into introductory classes</li>
<li>
Membership and retail sales</li>
<li>
Manage staff schedule</li>
<li>
Supervise Sales Representatives</li>
<li>
Hire/Manage all instructors at the studio</li>
<li>
Independently make decisions related to high level customer service</li>
<li>
Maintain cleanliness and organization of the studio</li>
<li>
Enforce studio policies and procedures</li>
<li>
Schedule and participate in networking/community events and studio promotions</li>
<li>
Strategically manage marketing campaigns to generate leads for the studio</li>
<li>
Any other duties as assigned</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
COMPENSATION &amp; PERKS:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
This position offers a very competitive base rate plus commissions and bonus if all goals are met</li>
<li>
Complimentary Fitness Membership while employed</li>
<li>
Employee Retail Discounts</li>
<li>
Comprehensive Benefit Package</li>
</ul>
