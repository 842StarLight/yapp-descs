<h2>Partner Success Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div><div><div>
<div>
<div>
<p>The Partner Success Manager collaborates with Channel Management, Channel Sales and Distributor Services Management within their region to ensure that they achieve the goals of the Channel Lead segment of the Rockwell Automation’s Customer Success Program. Responsible for inspiring Distributors implement an effective Annual Recurring Revenue Strategy that leads to high &amp; on-time renewal rates and expansion of annuity based contracts and subscriptions.</p>
<br>
Key Responsibilities:<ul>
<li>
Works with Channel Sales Management Team and Distributor Management to ensure local Channel Partners have strategic plan, enabling staff and policies to renew &amp; expand Annual Contracts on-time. Additionally, inspire Channel Partners develop an effective Annual Recurring Revenue Strategy.</li>
<li>
Responsible for ensuring participants of channel segment of Customer Success program are successful per Adoption, Expansion, Renewal and Expansion metrics/goals outlined by the program.</li>
<li>
Responsible to ensure data and workflow information from Customer Success Platform (Gainsight) is available to Distributors within their area of responsibility to help execute Onboarding, Adopt, Expand and Renewal motions relevant to each account in their respective portfolios.</li>
<li>
Helps Distributors translate available customer data contained within Customer Success Platform and other sources into value delivered to customer by engaging in Services contracts and software subscriptions.</li>
<li>
Works with Channel Sales Team and Distributor Management to ensure Distributor personnel Communicates value delivered to customer on a periodic cadence using QBR methodology and other means of communication/cadences.</li>
<li>
Responsible for supporting programs that help Distributor Management &amp; personnel understand customer data and insights to recommend changes or additions to existing contract and subscription scope that can improve the outcomes a customer can obtain. Use such recommendations to initiate an Expand Motion in conjunction with Sales.</li>
<li>
Foster a teaming environment with Sales, Delivery and Distributor Management team members in order to help &amp; inspire Distributors be successful with their ARR strategy.</li>
<li>
Collaborate with team members to ensure an optimal customer experience.</li>
<li>
Provide feedback to the portfolio team to ensure customer feedback is built into service offerings.</li>
<li>
Collaborate with stakeholders (Distributor Services Program Manager, Marketing, Enablement, etc.) in the development and execution of programs supportive of a successful deployment of channel segment of the Customer Success charter.</li>
</ul>
#LI-AP1<br>
This position can be located in either Dallas, Atlanta, Charlotte, Nashville, or remote within these locations.<br>
