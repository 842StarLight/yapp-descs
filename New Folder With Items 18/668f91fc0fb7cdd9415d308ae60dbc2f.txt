<p><b>Company Summary</b></p>
<p>
At Crown Castle, we work around the country to build and maintain the infrastructure behind the world’s most revolutionary technologies. Our portfolio of communications infrastructure (towers, small cells, fiber) connects cities and communities to essential data, technology, and wireless service—bringing information, ideas and innovations to the people and businesses that need them. As the nation’s largest provider of communications infrastructure and with more than 5,000 employees across the country, we’ve been building the connections that people depend on for 25 years. Crown Castle is an S&amp;P 500 company and one of the largest Real Estate Investment Trusts in the United States with an enterprise value of approximately $70 billion.</p>

<p><b>
Position</b> <b>Title:</b> – SD Lead Project Manager -Fiber-6E</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Position</b> <b>Summary</b></p>
<p>
The Lead Project Manager will oversee multiple service delivery projects from sales order initiation to customer project completion. The Lead Project Manager will manage the operational and tactical aspects of the delivery to ensure that the customer’s initiatives are completed on time and within specifications</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Essential Job Functions</b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Coordinate and lead customer meetings including kick-off and status update calls.</li>
<li>Manage the activities of internal operations and engineering groups to ensure proper planning, communications and implementation of solutions.</li>
<li>Ability to understand and engage in project design discussions and be able to communicate the details of these projects at both technical and non-technical levels</li>
<li>Communicate customer requirements to other cross-functional technical teams and project stakeholders to achieve project results.</li>
<li>Be responsible for the overall budget of projects validating that the quoted budget will support the project and managing potential risks that could have the project run over budget.</li>
<li>Communicate project status including dependencies to customer in order to set and meet expectations.</li>
<li>Develop a relationship with the client and serve as primary point of contact for any discussions regarding the project status and deliverables with the customer as well as with Crown Castle Fiber management.</li>
<li>Perform critical path analysis for projects to calculate, identify and manage aspects of the deployment and mitigate risk.</li>
<li>Ability to produce and interpret complex network designs</li>
<li>Resolve and/or escalate issues in a timely fashion.</li>
<li>Provide guidance and support to team members who need assistance resolving issues related to installations to ensure delivery milestones complete.</li>
<li>Assume management responsibilities when needed as a designee.</li>
<li>Effectively communicates customer escalations to management and contribute to resolution</li>
<li>Develop and coach SD PMs, SD ASSOC PMs, and/or PM Coordinators on matters of process, systems, etc. Identify training gaps and create a career path for team members.</li>
<br>
</ul>
