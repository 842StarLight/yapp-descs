<h2>Business Developer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Snapt is a fast-growing and vibrant tech start-up, offering an amazing opportunity for a business developer to become part of tech history. This will be a challenging role with key KPIs and is perfect for a driven and goal orientated person. You will become part of an amazing channel sales team who is focused on global channel growth, working with large multi-national technology companies to fast track sales.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>
What we are offering:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Join a company that is an exciting blend of a vibrant start-up environment and professional operations.</li>
<li>
Play a significant role in developing business for one of the most vibrant tech start-ups in the world.</li>
<li>
Enjoy flexible structures, flat hierarchies and motivated colleagues.</li>
<li>
Remuneration is competitive, and is negotiable depending on experience.</li>
<li>
Learn unique and valuable skills from experts in a USD 6 billion industry.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>What you'll be doing:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Work collaboratively to develop and execute the sales/business development plan while working with key internal stakeholders,</li>
<li>
Generate leads through the various channels and cold call prospective leads,</li>
<li>
Score and qualify leads via research, phone and email reach outs in collaboration with marketing and other team members.</li>
<li>
Schedule meetings and demos with the prospective leads and opportunities.</li>
<li>
Understand the needs of prospective opportunity and be able to respond effectively with a plan on how to meet these,</li>
<li>
Work strategically - carrying out necessary planning in order to achieve goals,</li>
<li>
Have a good understanding of the businesses' product and be able to advise others about it,</li>
<li>
Update and manage all prospective opportunities on CRM,</li>
<li>
Daily reporting on activity and progress,</li>
<li>
Achieve sales targets as determined by management,</li>
<li>
Attend seminars, conferences and events where appropriate,</li>
<li>
Keep abreast of trends and changes in the business and IT world,</li>
<li>
Perform any other duties and projects assigned by management.</li>
</ul>

5 Years experience in the art of business development.</li>
