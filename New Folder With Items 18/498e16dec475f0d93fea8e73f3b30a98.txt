<h2>Global Solutions Consultant</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>The Solutions Consultant for Telco Global Accounts is the presales technical strategy leader and primary interface to CTO, technologist, architecture, development, and product decision-makers for our first-tier service provider and carrier customers.</p>
<p>
In this capacity you will coordinate the efforts of supporting product and BU specialist teams working in the account, and lead the development, presentation, and positioning of multi-product solutions for customers in the vertical industry.</p>
<p>
Responsibilities:</p>
<ul>
<li>
You will present VMware vision, strategy and product roadmaps to executives and technical management and illustrate relevant Service Provider use cases for VMware products</li>
<li>
You will drive the adoption and usage of VMware products within strategic Service Provider accounts</li>
<li>
You will provide technical leadership and business pre-sales support for strategic opportunities involving the VMware core K8s, NFV, SDDC, SD-WAN, SDN, SDS, IoT, security, and mobile / device client solution sets</li>
<li>
You will act as the advocate in driving the customer agenda engaging with internal entities at VMware, including leadership, product, engineering, marketing and R&amp;D groups</li>
<li>
You will maintain deep technical and business knowledge of industry directions, trends, relevant standards, trade associations, industry consortia, etc.</li>
<li>
You will maintain working knowledge of applicable competitor products and offerings</li>
<li>
You will actively participate in regular professional development, training, and certification activities that are relevant to your job role</li>
<li>
You will provide consultative support and collaboration on factory driven development programs, working with product, marketing, support or training organizations representing the voice and point of view of the customer</li>
<li>
You take action to secure and maintain positive satisfaction ratings from your direct customer contacts</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p>
Expectations:</p>
<ul>
<li>
You will have 8-10 years of experience in the industry with a major portion selling to or working directly for companies in the communications, media and entertainment sector(s) in a technical, engineering, or development capacity</li>
<li>
You have pertinent professional credentials in the field, such as undergraduate and / or graduate degrees or equivalent, professional certifications, etc.</li>
<li>
You will have direct knowledge of and exposure to the technologies, architectures, and products used throughout the networks and operational environment of communications and media service providers</li>
<li>
You are an enthusiastic self-starter with a friendly demeanor, and the desire / ability to work effectively within a team comprised of a range of personality types, backgrounds, and skill sets</li>
<li>
Excellent communication skills, both written and oral are required along with excellent presentation skills and strong customer facing and relationship building skills. The ability to present extemporaneously is desirable</li>
<li>
You are self-driven and must be effective in working both independently and in a team setting</li>
<li>
You have strong listening and question based selling skills with the ability to uncover business challenges and develop solutions to tackle those challenges</li>
<li>
You can demonstrate a history of embracing the EPIC2 values of VMware (https://www.vmware.com/radius/vmwares-culture-built-epic2-values/ ) and are prepared to lead by example in upholding the positive culture and workplace environment that has made VMware a destination employer</li>
</ul>
