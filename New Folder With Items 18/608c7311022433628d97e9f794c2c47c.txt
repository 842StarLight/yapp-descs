<h2>Sales Engineering Manager - West</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">We are looking for a seasoned Sales Engineering Leader in the Bay Area who has the ability and energy to lead from the front. Your team will anchor all the technical aspects of the sales process—starting with qualification and solution design and moving on to proving the solution through presentations, custom demonstrations, and POCs / pilots.
<br><br>
The Sales Engineering Manager will need to be a hands-on technology leader both inside the company as well as with customers. You will have several direct reports and will be expected to collaboratively manage the team in concert with the Sales team to ensure alignment of objectives and goals and take ownership of outcomes.
<br><br>
<b>Key Objectives:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Lead, grow, and mentor a team of Senior Sales Engineers aligning the team with Sales Leadership and product and go-to-market strategy</li>
<li>Own and lead technical sales process, being accountable for driving sales (net new opportunities, up-sells, and cross-sells) within your portfolio of accounts - in partnership with your sales engineering team and account executives</li>
<li>Understand the value chain from data to user to action to value - across multiple use cases - and effectively articulate search-driven applications and technology strategies to your account base</li>
<li>Develop processes and best practices that dramatically increase the productivity and effectiveness of your team</li>
<li>Provide input into account strategies and territory plans, helping to shape the company's go-to-market efforts</li>
<li>Provide feedback to Product and Engineering, acting as an advocate for your account base</li>
</ul>
<b>Experience and Skills:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Previous experience with search, data analytics, Hadoop, Spark, and / or BI is required</li>
<li>Demonstrated experience in a pre-sales engineering position (typically 10+ years) and a pre-sales engineering management position (typically 2+ years) is required</li>
<li>Entrepreneurial mindset: self-starting, motivated, proven ability to take ownership of your area of responsibility</li>
<li>Excellent presentation skills: able to engage both technical and business stakeholders - from developers to executives - and deliver effective technical presentations and product demos</li>
<li>Proven ability to effectively prioritize and manage team responsibilities</li>
<li>Knowledge of Solr is a major plus</li>
<li>Experience at a search company is a plus</li>
<li>Knowledge of SQL, relational databases, ETL, and distributed systems</li>
<li>Development experience with Java is a plus</li>
<li>General knowledge of machine learning concepts and algorithms</li>
<li>Comfort working with both Linux and Windows Server</li>
<li>Ability to travel within the West region will be required – assumed to be 50%</li>
</ul>
<b>Company Description:</b><br>
Lucidworks builds AI-powered search and discovery applications for some of the world's largest brands. Fusion, Lucidworks' advanced development platform, provides the enterprise-grade capabilities needed to design, develop and deploy intelligent search apps at any scale. Reddit, Red Hat, Moody's, Commvault and the U.S. Census are just of few of the companies that rely on Lucidworks every day to power their consumer-facing and enterprise search apps. Lucidworks' investors include Top Tier Capital Partners, Shasta Ventures, Granite Ventures, Silver Lake Waterman, and Walden International.</div>
