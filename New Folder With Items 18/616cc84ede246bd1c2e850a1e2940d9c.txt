<h2>Quality Control Home Preservation Officer-Tigard</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>MAJOR JOB RESPONSIBILITIES</p>
<p>
Performs monthly servicing and loss mitigation quality control reviews for all investors and agencies. Reviews include compliance to investor, agency and bank policy and procedures as well as regulatory and state laws. Loss Mitigation review includes collections, delinquency, forbearance, modification, short sale, bankruptcy, litigation, and foreclosure. Servicing reviews include escrow analysis, ARM reset, paid note and early termination as well as review of assumptions and other related reviews as assigned. Keeps up to date on regulation, investor and agency changes.</p>
<p>
Performs quality control reviews for investor reporting and custodial accounting for all investors and agencies. Reviews include compliance to investor, agency and bank policy and procedures as well as regulatory and state laws. Creates and uses monitoring test spreadsheets and implements and performs monthly testing for all investors and agencies.</p>
<p>
Assists in Preparation reports for distribution to management and other Bank associates. Researches and analyzes a variety of reporting and/or accounting issues related to investor and agency custodial accounting and investor reporting. May interpret reports and records for managers.</p>
<p>
Demonstrates compliance with all bank regulations for assigned job function and applies to designated job responsibilities. Keeps up to date on regulation, investor and agency changes. Follows all Bank policies and procedures, compliance regulations, and completes all required annual required or job-specific training. May be asked to coach, mentor, or train others and teach coursework as subject matter expert. Responsible for other duties as assigned. Actively learns, demonstrates, and fosters the Umpqua corporate culture in all actions and words. Takes personal initiative and is a positive example for others to emulate. Embraces our vision to become "The World's Greatest Bank."</p>
<p>
Requirements:</p>
<ul>
<li>A minimum of one years of relevant mortgage loss mitigation and servicing experience.</li>
<li>Good analytical and problem solving skills.</li>
<li>Experience analyzing borrower income and credit reports.</li>
<li>Knowledge of third party investors, including Freddie Mac and Fannie Mae, FHA, VA and GNMA.</li>
<li>Excellent written and verbal communication.</li>
<li>Ability to work effectively with individuals and groups across the company to manage internal and external customer relationships.</li>
<li>Proficiency with personal computers and related software packages such as Word and Excel, and other business machines.</li>
<li>Requires a bachelor’s degree in business, finance, or equivalent; and familiarity with collections.</li>
<li>Ability to train and present to small and large audiences or has the interest in learning to train and present.</li>
</ul>
