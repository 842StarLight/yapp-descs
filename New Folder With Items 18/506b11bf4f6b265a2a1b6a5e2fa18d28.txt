<h2>Energy Engineer - Solar & Energy Storage</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>At McKinstry, we’re proud to be a collective group of innovators and problem-solvers, builders and engineers, mentors and students. We strive to create a safe and engaging work environment that empowers you to do the work you are passionate about.</p>
<p>
We believe deeply that energy and facility services, engineering and construction are revolutionizing the built environment – and if you’re driven by our mission to transform ideas into meaningful relationships and together, build a thriving planet, McKinstry may be the place for you. Serving our community together is one of the ways we at McKinstry put our shared purpose into action.</p>
<p>
Total collaboration and a diverse, employee-centric work experience are what we can provide you. Integrated delivery and true consultative partnership is what you will help us provide for our clients. Our culture is what differentiates us and because of this, we seek exceptional talent that believes what we believe.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Here's where you come in:</b></p>
<p>
We are currently seeking an<b> Energy Engineer - Solar &amp; Energy Storage </b>to join our team as a member of our growing Energy and Technical Services National Platform division.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
You're great at:</b></p>
<p><i>
Communication</i></p>
<ul>
<li>Demonstrates regional subject matter leadership in Solar and Energy Storage by staying on the forefront of the technology and working closely with others to deploy knowledge to other departments within the company such as project development, project management, sales and estimating, etc.</li>
<li>Act as a steward of McKinstry culture; communicate and influence policies and procedures.</li>
<li>Ability to communicate information and ideas clearly and concisely, in writing and verbally required.</li>
<li>Communicate with local utility representatives to assist in the collection of utility rebates and incentives.</li>
<li>Perform other duties as assigned.</li>
</ul>
<p><i>Analysis</i></p>
<ul>
<li>Quantify renewable energy measures primarily focused on and including on-site and off-site solar, battery energy storage.</li>
<li>Perform energy and savings calculations using modeling software, spreadsheets, and utility calculation tools.</li>
<li>Generate Solar and Energy storage conceptual designs that meet client needs including layouts, designs, and written scope narratives.</li>
<li>Support other Energy Engineers and Project Energy Engineers in determining the right technical approach for evaluating complex renewable energy solutions.</li>
</ul>
<p><i>Project Management</i></p>
<ul>
<li>Develop strategies, goals, and objectives specific to project execution</li>
<li>Coordinate with energy and design teams on solar and energy storage feasibility, basis of design and scope of work development.</li>
<li>Prepare Project Risk Reviews for Energy Calculations prior to presenting ROM or Final savings.</li>
<li>May support performance assurance team with energy analysis in Measurement and Verification reports.</li>
<li>Support construction and commissioning teams during/after construction to solve issues related to facility improvement measures performance.</li>
<li>Work closely with the Performance Assurance Specialist in developing the Measurement and Verification Process and Protocols to be used for performance validation.</li>
<li>Establish Key Performance Indicators and key factors to be used in the Measurement and Verification Phase (in conjunction with the Performance Assurance Specialist.</li>
<li>Demonstrate proficiency in writing, reviewing, and editing technical documentation.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p>
</p>
<p><b>What we would like to see from you:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>(2-6+) years in solar and/or energy storage commercial industry required.</li>
<li>Bachelor’s Degree in engineering, or related field required.</li>
<li>Master’s degree in Renewable Energy and/or Energy specialization a plus.</li>
<li>Understanding of calculation methods including but not limited to, Helioscope, PVSyst, Energy Toolbase, CAD, PVWatts, NREL System Advisor Mode, shade calculations, soiling calculations, snow soiling calculations, battery storage modeling platforms required.</li>
<li>Working knowledge of estimating preliminary budgets for Solar and Energy Storage projects.</li>
<li>Maintain a good understanding of pertinent building and energy codes including National Electric Code, International Fire Code, International Building Code.</li>
<li>Understanding of Energy Services Performance Contracting required.</li>
<li>Understanding of Power Purchase Agreements required.</li>
<li>Maintain a good understanding of incentive programs and processes.</li>
<li>NABCEP registration preferred.</li>
<li>LEED AP registration a plus.</li>
<li>EIT professional designation a plus.</li>
<li>Certified Energy Manger (CEM) certification a plus.</li>
<li>Advanced knowledge of Microsoft Office Suite - Word, Excel, Outlook, and Visio required; working knowledge of SharePoint preferred.</li>
<br>
</ul>
