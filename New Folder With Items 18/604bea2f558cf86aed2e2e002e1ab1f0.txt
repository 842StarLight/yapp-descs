<h2>Sales Executive</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>
<p>Sonic Boom Wellness is a fast-growing, B2B, software-as-a-service (SAAS) corporate wellbeing company specializing in improving employees’ daily health habits. At the intersection of health care, wellness, and technology, we have developed a proprietary wellbeing platform sold to clients nationwide. Our industry has consolidated recently, leaving the door wide open for us to capture market share, expand our distribution channels, and grow rapidly.</p>
<p>
Our Sales Executive will be – well, SELLING the BOOM! The right candidate will display courage, intellectual curiosity, and creativity. There will be occasional travel onsite, but most presentations are done by webinar, and this position is based in our Carlsbad location. Culture is everything to us at the Boom, so be prepared to make us say WOW!</p>
<p>
Position Description</p>
<p>
The Sales Executive is responsible for all sales processes in an assigned territory, from lead generation through the close. The person who fills this role will implement specifically-designed marketing strategies and ask tough questions to uncover the most valuable and relevant opportunities. They are directly responsible for expanding Sonic Boom’s visibility and utilization within a designated set of consulting/brokerage firms, and maintaining relationships with key offices while developing relationships with mutual clients. They are also tasked with direct prospecting, qualifying leads, delivering high-energy demo presentations, and addressing all follow-up requests as a knowledgeable expert and professional representative of Sonic Boom. All of this is done with a strong sense of integrity and an effort to align prospects’ needs with Sonic Boom solutions.</p>
<p>
Core Responsibilities</p>
<ul>
<li>Consultatively sell Sonic Boom’s services and make wellness recommendations to prospects and clients including incentive structuring, program funding, program integrations, and more</li>
<li>Be responsible for the full sales cycles, including prospecting, qualification, needs assessment, presenting, and closing</li>
<li>Meet or exceed revenue targets</li>
<li>Demonstrate product knowledge in all areas of the program, allowing for consistently effective sales presentations</li>
<li>Engage in telephone and email strategies to build strong relationships with brokers &amp; consultants on a national scale</li>
<li>Further develop our database of qualified leads through referrals, cold calls, networking, and extensive research</li>
<li>Develop a complete understanding of pricing and proposal models</li>
<li>Prepare for presentations by researching prospective clients, preparing qualifying questions, and preparing for counter responses to predictable objections</li>
<li>Maintain accurate record of all sales and prospecting activities including sales calls, presentations, closed sales, and follow-up activities, mainly through the use of CRM</li>
<li>Respond to inbound sales email inquiries</li>
<li>Occasional on-site visits for face-to-face presentations</li>
<li>Stay current with changes in the health care benefits industry</li>
<li>Assist in the transition from sales to implementation</li>
</ul>
<p>Required Skills / Experience</p>
<ul>
<li>At least one year of Insurance/Benefit or Wellness sales industry experience and experience selling to any of the distribution channels (brokers/consultants, direct-to-employer, health plans/healthcare, TPAs, resellers) is REQUIRED</li>
<li>Comfort selling to committees/groups of buyers with various amounts of influence in the purchasing decision</li>
<li>OUTSTANDING listening and questioning skills – our sales style is inquisitive, not pushy or overbearing</li>
<li>Impeccable written and verbal communication – in addition to making calls, drafting e-mails, and developing long-term relationships, you will regularly perform live demos and present to executive-level decision makers</li>
<li>Empathy – ability to build strong relationships with brokers and consultants and “walk in their shoes” to understand implicit client needs</li>
<li>Ability to work independently when needed AND as a member of collaborative team</li>
<li>ORGANIZATION – our sales cycle is extensive and requires multiple levels of proactive outreach and follow-ups. You must be capable of managing a wide range of relationships and tasks, and never let leads slip through the cracks</li>
<li>CREATIVITY – think outside-the-box!</li>
<li>Resourcefulness – the ability to take a project and run with minimal direction, seeking answers and solutions independently whenever possible</li>
<li>Fast-moving, entrepreneurial mentality – we put the pedal to the metal!</li>
<li>Attention to detail – if we didn’t emphasize it enough, organization and INCREDIBLE attention to detail is a must … especially with formal quotes and contracts</li>
<li>Outgoing personality – You have to enjoy – truly enjoy – making solid connections and interacting with people in all mediums (via e-mail, phone, and face-to-face)</li>
<li>Experience with CRM tools (specifically Nutshell is a plus!)</li>
<li>Above-average skills in the Microsoft Office Suite – Word, Excel, PPT, and Outlook</li>
</ul>
<p>Hours</p>
<p>
This is a full-time position, with working hours Monday through Friday. Our ideal candidate is local to Carlsbad, CA or located in the Midwest region.</p>
<p>
Compensation, Benefits, and Perks!</p>

Compensation will include a base salary, commission and is dependent on experience. Our benefits package includes health insurance, HSA, 401(k), PTO, and paid holidays. We also provide our employees with some fun perks (think free fruit, happy hours, and company sponsored events)!</p>
