<h2>Director, Global Services</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Overview
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div>The Director, Global Services is responsible for setting, leading, and managing the strategy, performance and results for multiple or one complex area of the Global Services department.</div>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<ul>
<li>Oversee and plan the strategic vision, organizational structure, operating policies and procedures and management practices to ensure department delivers services that earn customer loyalty and deliver operational excellence</li>
<li>
Ensures appropriate strategy and resource plans are set to meet the forecasted workforce needs, with the goal of optimizing resource billable utilization rates across teams</li>
<li>
Regularly interact and collaborate with senior management and/or executives of marketing, sales, and development areas to continue the evolvement of solution sets that help achieve strong reputation in the market</li>
<li>
Ensure teams adherence to the usage of company standards, tools and guidelines; enforcing procedures and standards to meet established audit and compliance standards</li>
<li>
Oversee the business development activities that generate services engagement opportunities for respected area of the business</li>
<li>
Establish and assure leadership adherence to budgets, schedules, work plans, and performance requirements</li>
<li>
Manage competing demands through negotiation and compromise to ensure that the strategic needs of the organization are met</li>
<li>
Leverage existing business relationships through frequent communication, including formal and informal meetings and customer visits</li>
<li>
Serve as an escalation point for issues; provide guidance and resolve issues as necessary</li>
<li>
Oversee the direction on the quality metrics; track performance against established metrics and ensure management is holding employees accountable for achievement</li>
<li>
Oversee the management of performance and compensation management for direct reports; provide coaching and development opportunities</li>
<li>
Establish and monitor required processes for the accurate forecasting of revenue to leadership on a regular basis, and maintain forecasting accuracy within each quarter.</li>
<li>
Serve as the leader and expert for the vertical/business unit representing the interests of the vertical/business unit and fully understand its marketplace, opportunities, best practices and terminology.</li>
</ul>
