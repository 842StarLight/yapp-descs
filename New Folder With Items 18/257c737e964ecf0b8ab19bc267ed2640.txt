<h2>Senior Product Manager, Discoverability</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">InVision is the digital product design platform used to make the world's best customer experiences. We provide design tools and educational resources for teams to navigate every stage of the product design process, from ideation to development. Today, more than 5 million people use InVision to create a repeatable and streamlined design workflow; rapidly design and prototype products before writing code, and collaborate across their entire organization. That includes 100% of the Fortune 100, and organizations like Airbnb, Amazon, HBO, Netflix, Slack, Starbucks and Uber, who are now able to design better products, faster.
<br><br>
Our team is in search of a Senior Product Manager, Discoverability to help us change the way digital products are designed.
<br><br>
<b>About the Team:</b><br>
The Discoverability squad's charter is to enable teams to efficiently organize and manage design work and to seamlessly navigate InVision. At InVision, we know that time is precious for our users. Users want to find the InVision content they are looking for in the most efficient and expedient manner possible. The Discoverability squad owns a large and important product surface area and operates with the goals of improving our user's experience and speed when navigating and managing their InVision content.
<br><br>
<b>What you'll do:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>You'll be responsible for defining a product strategy and KPIs, gaining alignment from company leaders and stakeholders, and prioritizing a roadmap that helps your team achieve your business goals</li>
<li>You will articulate user problems and product needs, iterating with a core team of design and engineering experts as well as stakeholders in research, data, support, sales, and marketing and other company stakeholders</li>
<li>You'll become an expert in the pathways that lead to successful user onboarding, and create product experiences that showcase the power of InVision to new users</li>
<li>You'll help define hypothesis for product enhancements and run experiments and A/B tests to validate contributions towards user and business outcomes</li>
<li>You will coordinate across teams to maintain product and strategy alignment, eliminating surprises and finding creative ways to make the "whole greater than the sum of its parts"</li>
</ul>
<b>What you'll bring:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>5+ years as a Product Manager at a software company</li>
<li>Familiarity with design and development team workflows</li>
<li>Data-driven approach to product development and growth</li>
<li>Comfortable engaging with end-users, conducting user research, and intelligently integrating findings into your work</li>
<li>The ability to dive into big projects and manage them from start to finish</li>
<li>Ability to thrive in a variable and demanding startup environment</li>
</ul>
