<h2>Assistant HVAC Service Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>An HVAC service company is offering an immediate, full-time position for an Assistant Service Manager. This opportunity involves managing and training service technicians and supporting office staff within a full-service commercial HVAC service company. You will support, train and instruct service technicians, you will assist with service, sales and service technician dispatching, and perform other duties as required. Workdays are Monday through Friday with telephone rotation for on-call telephone duties after hours and on weekends/holidays.<br>
<br>
</div>
<div><b>Job Requirements:</b></div>
<ul><li>
Qualified candidates must have a minimum of 5 years of field experience, must have HVAC and/or refrigeration service management experience, must be highly organized with strong communication skills, and must have strong office skills, such as proficiency in using a computer and knowledge in basic software including Microsoft Office and Outlook. Phone skills are a must and candidates must be able to work well as a team within the office.</li></ul>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div><b>
This is your chance to land a career position with an industry leader.</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
Responsibilities include direct customer contact, problem resolution, scheduling, dispatching, coding, billing and tracking information. Maintaining spreadsheets and tracking systems, files, and technician timesheets.</li>
<li>
Schedules and dispatches all technicians for planned and emergency service work, including scheduled maintenance, warranty and start-up work.</li>
<li>
Develops plans to ensure that employees are meeting customer needs and contractual obligations, service agreements and completion of other service work.</li>
<li>
Handles technician and customer inquiries and routes questions and problems of a technical nature appropriately.</li>
<li>
Coordinates warranty administration process.</li>
<li>
Coordinates the start-up of newly commissioned equipment and tracks all Jobsite work-in-progress.</li>
<li>
Expedites all parts orders for technicians and end-user customers.</li>
<li>
Coordinates equipment delivery and maintains tracking system.</li>
<li>
Communicates effectively with customers regarding scheduling, progress and follow-up.</li>
<li>
Tracking technician hours worked so that correct wages are paid.</li>
<li>
All other assignments as requested or needed.</li>
<li>
This job description is subject to change upon discretion of department supervisor.</li>
</ul>
