<p>JOB SUMMARY:</p>
<p>
Polaris Industries, Inc. is currently looking for an Applications Manager within their Information Systems (IS) department located in Plymouth, MN. This is an exciting opportunity for the right candidate who will be responsible for working with IS and business users to lead the Dynamics 365 Finance and Operations (D365FO / AX) team on a journey of digital transformation at Polaris. The primary focus for the next two years will be to upgrade International AX2009 system to Dynamics 365 Finance and Operations and assisting with an ERP conversion for a US based legacy ERP to D365FO. The Applications Manager will manage a small local team located in Plymouth and will provide work direction for a global matrixed team. The Applications Manager will be responsible for a team that will oversee the analysis, design, development, configuration, implementation and support for AX2009, AX2012 and D365FO.</p>
