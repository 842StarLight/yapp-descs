<h2>Account Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Primary Purpose and Function</b><br>
The Enterprise Account Manager (EAM) will be responsible for maintaining and managing Securadyne’s presence in certain accounts as well as driving sales activities in an assigned market area / accounts. The EAM will be primarily responsible for managing an existing book of business ($2.5M+) and serving as the primary account interface for this elite customer portfolio. The EAM is expected to mine existing accounts for new business as well as well as establish new customer relationships. The EAM will work in accordance with the organization's policies and procedures, keeping in mind the overall core values of Securadyne Systems.<br>
</p>
<div>
<b>Essential Functions and Responsibilities</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Exceed sales goals as established by the organization</li>
<li>Manage existing enterprise level customers</li>
<li>Identify &amp; develop new enterprise level customer prospects</li>
<li>Utilize consultative sales techniques in customer development situations</li>
<li>Preparation and presentation of company offerings to end-user customers</li>
<li>Communicate with Project Managers on a consistent basis as required to ensure timely and consistent delivery of work to dedicated customer base</li>
<li>Aggressively grow service and maintenance agreements as part of solution selling process</li>
<li>Determine hosted, managed and monitored sales opportunities within new and existing customers alike</li>
<li>Preparation of timely and accurate solution proposals</li>
<li>Maintain quality assurance by following organization standards</li>
<li>Assist with other sales projects as assigned by General Manager</li>
</ul>
<br>
<b>Knowledge and Skills</b><br>
<ul>
<li>High School Diploma required; Associates Degree or Bachelor’s Degree strongly preferred</li>
<li>8+ years of overall professional experience required with prior experience in enterprise level sales desired</li>
<li>5+ years relevant experience in the electronic security industry required</li>
<li>Proficiency in using and learning software packages associated with sales quoting</li>
<li>The ability to work in a dynamic team environment with an expanding customer base</li>
<li>Must be able to manage multiple tasks, manage deadlines and have excellent follow-up skills</li>
<li>Ability to establish and maintain effective working relationships with internal and external customers</li>
<li>Highly-motivated and customer service oriented</li>
<li>Good verbal and written communication skills</li>
<li>Proficient with MS Office Suite</li>
<li>Organized and analytical decision making capabilities</li>
<li>Commitment to professional, ethical, and compliant business practices</li>
<li>Must be professional and polished in appearance and speech</li>
</ul>
</div>
