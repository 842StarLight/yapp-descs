<h2>Service Manager (Press Division), Rolling Meadows, IL</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>Komatsu is an indispensable partner to the construction, mining, industrial and forestry industries that maximizes value for customers through innovative solutions. With a full line of products supported by our advanced IoT technologies, regional distribution channels and a global service network, we help customers safely and sustainably optimize their operations. Our Komatsu, P&amp;H, Joy, Montabert, Modular Mining Systems, Hensley Industries, NTC and Gigaphoton equipment and services are used to extract fundamental minerals and develop modern infrastructure.
</p>
<div>
<div>
<div><b>Job Overview
</b></div>
<div><p>This role is to oversee, manage and coordinate Service and Parts organization staff to ensure business goals and customer expectations are successfully met. The position’s responsibility includes processing sales orders daily while supplying technical support to customers.
</p></div>
</div>
<div>
<div><b>Key Job Responsibilities
</b></div>
<div><ul>
<li>Manage Field Service Technicians (FST) and support staff work schedule including billable and warranty service, equipment installations, retrofits &amp; upgrades, and preventative service jobs. Manage the responsibilities of PTD service group by maintaining effective communication, allocating resources efficiently, and mitigating on going customer issues. Insure timely monthly invoicing and reports as required.
</li>
<li>Provide phone and email technical support for all customers and service staff.
</li>
<li>In-house sales promotion - recommend parts, accessories, and Service work necessary to maintain safe and reliable operation of equipment.
</li>
<li>To manage all subcontractor activities which include, service activities, in house repairs and misc. assembly work. Coordinate support with Project Manager.
</li>
<li>Perform other duties as assigned.
</li>
</ul></div>
</div>
<div>
<div><b>Qualifications/Requirements
</b></div>
<div><ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree or Equivalent Education/Experience required.
</li>
<li>Skilled in all aspects of machine tools (electronic, electrical, mechanical, pneumatics, hydraulics, and computers)
</li>
<li>Solid understanding of PC usage and ability to learn various software/applications. Experienced with Microsoft Office programs Word-Excel-Access required, Experience with AutoCAD or related CAD systems preferred.
</li>
<li>Ability to read and write technical documents blueprints and schematics (electrical and mechanical).
</li>
<li>Ability to motivate, organize, and achieve max output from subordinates. Ability to multitask and prioritize the duties of the staff.
</li>
<li>Knowledge of facility management and maintenance.
</li>
<li>Ability to communicate effectively with all levels of professionals and management.
</li>
</ul></div>
