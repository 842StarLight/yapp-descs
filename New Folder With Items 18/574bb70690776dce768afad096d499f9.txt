<h2>Front Desk Administrative Assistant - Bilingual Mandarin</h2>

<p><b>Company Description:</b></p>
<p>
Dahua Technology USA Inc. is a subsidiary of Dahua Technology, the world’s second-largest manufacturer of video surveillance equipment by market share, according to a 2018 IHS report. Dahua Technology USA brings high-value, total security solutions to the North American market by focusing on integrity and personal relationships to enhance the customer experience. Dahua Technology’s surveillance solutions, from award-winning cameras to video management software, deliver unparalleled quality, reliability, and stability. Dahua Technology USA provides sales and support throughout the U.S. and Canada.</p>
<p><b>
Job Summary:</b></p>
<p>
Under direct supervision, this position is primarily responsible for routine clerical work, including receiving and directing visitors, word processing, creating spreadsheets and presentations, and filing. Extensive software skills, Internet research abilities and strong communication skills are required.</p>
<p><b>
Key Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Assist in setting up office location, connecting with vendors for drop off/pick up, or installation.</li>
<li>
Assists in preparing and processing correspondence, reports, and other documents.</li>
<li>
Answer phones and greets clients warmly.</li>
<li>
Answers inquiries about the company.</li>
<li>
Handle travel arrangements and expense reports for staff.</li>
<li>
Schedules appointments and maintains calendars.</li>
<li>
Processes incoming and outgoing mail and packages.</li>
<li>
Sends and distributes FAX messages.</li>
<li>
Monitors and orders supplies.</li>
<li>
Runs errands as needed.</li>
<li>
Performs other related duties as assigned or requested.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Requirements:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
High School Diploma or equivalent and 2 years’ experience.</li>
<li>
General office experience &amp; proficiency in the following: data entry, typing/word processing, filing, &amp; basic math skills for assigned business tasks. Needs to operate varied office equipment.</li>
<li>
Bilingual Mandarin preferred</li>
<li>
Excellent time management skills and ability to multi-task and prioritize work.</li>
<li>
Attention to detail and problem solving skills.</li>
<li>
Excellent written and verbal communication skills.</li>
<li>
Proficiency in MS Office</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Compensation</b></p>
<ul><li>
Dahua is offering an attractive base salary, depending on experience.</li></ul>
