<h2>Assistant Store Manager - Pennywise Thrift Shop - Institutional Advancement</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p><b>PRIMARY FUNCTION:</b></p>
<p>
The Assistant Store Manager will be responsible for directing staff with the overall goal of increased sales, and cost management. The position will also assist in store opening, closing, reporting key data, and act as the manager on duty (MOD) in the absence of the manager. The expectation is that the position will provide leadership-by-example for customer service on the sales floor, process donated goods and perform general store duties.</p>
<p><b>
ESSENTIAL FUNCTIONS:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Manage staff scheduling, call outs, and ETO (vacations)</li>
<li>
Manage staff metrics such as cleaning, customer service and production
</li>
<li>Oversee cash drawer, deposits, and financial reconciliation</li>
<li>
Report Key Performance Indicators (KPIs) to manager
</li>
<li>Responsible for data collection on sales, donations, production and consignment (aligned with manager expectations)</li>
<li>
Work collaboratively with the manager to institute activities designed around increasing sales/donations, foot traffic, and exposure of the store</li>
<li>
Direct staff with the goal of increasing sales, manage costs, overall store appeal, cleanliness and freshness of goods</li>
<li>
Interacts with co-workers, visitors, and other staff consistent with Jefferson’s core values.
</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Other Duties:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Expected to help with customer service, cashier, cleaning, donation/consignment intake, and production
</li>
<li>Market and merchandise goods for floor, window and wall displays</li>
<li>
Assist with merchandise handling including moving items to sales floor, repricing or removing old items and cleaning and organizing displays</li>
<li>
Guided by Jefferson’s mission, organizational culture, values, and vision for the future, provide inspirational fundraising leadership through teamwork, collaboration, and loyalty.
</li>
</ul>
<p><b>EDUCATIONAL/TRAINING REQUIREMENTS</b>:</p>
<p>
High School Diplomas</p>
<p><b>
EXPERIENCE REQUIREMENTS</b>:</p>
<p>
2 years retail experience, preferably in a thrift shop environment.
</p>
