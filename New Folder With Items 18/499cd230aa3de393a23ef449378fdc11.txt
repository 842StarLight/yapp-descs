<h2>Electrical Project Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<ul>
<li>Electrical Project Managers oversee electrical services jobs involved with the inspecting, testing, troubleshooting, performing start-up and collection of data of low, medium and high voltage (to 500kV) electrical systems, and moderately complex relay calibration and power metering.</li>
<li>Involved in the pre-planning, walk-through and working with sales department on estimating and quoting jobs.</li>
<li>Identify and assign individual job assignments of crew members.</li>
<li>Interface with the customer throughout jobs and attend customer meetings as requested or needed to provide job updates, progress status, and resolve issues.</li>
<li>Identify opportunities for expanded job scope and discuss with customer and Account Manager.</li>
<li>Communicate with the assigned Account Manager on any additional billing.</li>
<li>Monitor job budget by reviewing each technicians’ job time and material costs.</li>
<li>Conduct a final job walk with customer before energizing, discuss job scope and ensure start-up goes well.</li>
<li>Complete, compile and review reports for accuracy and provide to Production Manager.</li>
<li>On some jobs, may be required to work as a Technician on some job tasks performing various electrical testing &amp; maintenance services.</li>
<li>Rotate emergency on-call duty and if called respond to customer emergencies in a timely manner.</li>
</ul>
<h4 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Qualifications</b></h4>
<ul>
<li>High school diploma or GED required; prefer an Associate’s degree from an accredited 2 year technical college in Electrical Power, or similar experience and training from the military (Army Prime Power, Air Force Electrical Power Production, or NAVY Seabee)</li>
<li>5 or more years of experience project managing electrical maintenance shutdowns/turnarounds or switchgear commissioning</li>
<li>Prefer experience with most of the following or similar: Doble software, automated CT set, Power DB, RTS or Protest relay testing, oil testing, infrared scanning, automatic transfer switching, performing start-up on switchgear up through 500kV class, VFD (variable frequency drives)</li>
<li>NETA Certification a plus, but not required</li>
<li>Must have general PC skills including email, internet, data entry and Microsoft Office; experience with PowerDB a plus.</li>
<li>OSHA 30 hour, MSHA, NFPA 70-E, CPR/First Aid/BBP/AED training a plus.</li>
<li>Must be able to work safely and adhere to all electrical safety procedures.</li>
</ul>
