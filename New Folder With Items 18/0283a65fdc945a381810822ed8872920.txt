<p><b>Position</b></p>
<p></p>
<p>We’re seeking a digital marketing specialist to join our all-star team. This position is ideal for a creative, meticulous writer in search of the perfect opportunity to gain valuable exposure to all things ecommerce. If you can envision yourself developing an obsession with driving sales through the art of crafting the perfect story, then this may be your new home.</p>
<p></p>
<p>The ideal candidate will be an exceptional writer and must possess an appreciation for and willingness to understand digital marketing and how to effectively reach today's consumer online. This writing-intensive position will focus on content creation for our ecommerce websites and developing creative integrated marketing campaigns. They will work closely with brands such as Carhartt, Under Armour and Nike to build compelling customer-facing promotions. This person will serve as a key contributor in increasing revenue through multi-channel marketing, including email marketing, display advertising, retargeting and paid search.</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Duties</b></p>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Write attention-grabbing product descriptions, website content, email marketing blasts and blog articles on deadline</li>
<li>Generate and pitch creative, effective marketing campaigns</li>
<li>Collaborate with in-house designers to build marketing assets</li>
<li>Maintain Facebook, Twitter, Google+ and YouTube accounts</li>
<li>Understand and create content that aligns with our customer’s interests and needs</li>
<li>Conceptualize and execute compelling incentives and offers</li>
<li>Gather and report data on campaign performance</li>
<li>Increase traffic to drive sales through promotions, email, social, search engine marketing, and content marketing strategies</li>
<li>Assist with paid search and display advertising campaigns, including Google Adwords, BingAds and Facebook</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Qualifications</b></p>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor's Degree in Journalism, English, Marketing or Communications</li>
<li>Passion for internet marketing and strong understanding of how to communicate effectively through the use of photos, video, and text online</li>
<li>Outstanding writing, research and editing skills. You will be asked to provide writing samples and complete a writing test</li>
<li>Exceptional attention to detail and ability to multitask in a deadline-driven atmosphere</li>
<li>Desire to work in a fast-moving environment with moving parts and unforeseen requests</li>
<li>Strong organizational, project management, problem solving and time management skills</li>
<li>Familiarity with AP Style, WordPress and basic HTML (photos, text and links)</li>
<li>Proficiency in Microsoft Excel</li>
</ul>
