<b>About SuperLumin Networks</b>
</div>
<div>
<br>
SuperLumin provides sophisticated Web and Enterprise Content Delivery Network (eCDN) solutions. We are part of the STRATACACHE family of companies which focuses on digital signage, media displays and marketing solutions. Our network technology increases network performance, reduces congestion by decreasing the overall volume of content moving over the network. Our technologies identify frequently requested data and stores or caches it locally. Caching eliminates the need to repeatedly transmit the same content over and over again from the web server through the cloud to the client improving web content delivery and performance.<br>
