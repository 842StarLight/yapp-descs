<h2>Manager, Consumer Relationship</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p>SpartanNash is a value-added wholesale grocery distributor — supplying more than 2,100 independent grocery retail locations throughout the United States — as well as a grocery retailer, with more than 155 corporate supermarkets in nine states. We also serve the military community through our MDV division; in addition to offering premier fresh produce distribution and fresh food processing through Caito Foods and third-party logistics through BRT.</p>
<p><b>
At SpartanNash, relationships matter,</b> and our Corporate team takes that sentiment to heart in the way we work as one totally aligned team, support and respect each other and share the highest standards of excellence. We’re always looking for the next great idea or a new way to approach a challenge, and we collaborate and innovate to achieve our goals.</p>
<p>
A day in the life of the Manager, Consumer Relationship is never the same but, in this role, you will be responsible to manage and/or develop the consumer engagement strategies, tactics and metrics to ensure growth in key consumer segments by overseeing the execution of consumer and loyalty and e-commerce programs and communications while working closely with external vendors and internal cross-functional departments. Also responsible to act as a key resource for marketing data management and monitoring promotional and consumer relationship management (CRM) trends.<br>
<br>
<b>What You’ll Do:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Manage and provide support for the consumer marketing programs (i.e., customer relationship management, loyalty programs, e-commerce, shopper marketing) processes and systems. Work with marketing and merchandising to manage internal and external communication platforms and to streamline and ensure the successful implementation and execution of consumer marketing programs.</li>
<li>Collaborate with vendors, merchants, and internal departments to prepare and deliver consumer marketing solutions to increase consumer engagement, enhance the customer experience and to ensure an understanding of customer needs; provide recommendations, insight and support to consumer marketing strategies.</li>
<li>Participate in the development of, and manage assigned consumer and loyalty marketing strategies, programs, processes and deliverables; work cross functionally with internal departments, external vendors and/or customers to develop the calendar timelines/deliverables and communications, manage such through execution of the programs.</li>
<li>Develop and manage loyalty and consumer marketing activities for retail banners and ensure successful implementation of promotional activities, store events, trade shows, etc. and provide support to company-owned retail locations regarding the Company’s loyalty initiatives.</li>
<li>Provide project leadership to support loyalty marketing, e-commerce, consumer marketing and shopper marketing strategies, programs, activities and initiatives, including key projects and support of distribution loyalty marketing efforts.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Sound Like You? Here’s what y</b><b>ou’ll need:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor's Degree (Required) Marketing, Business Administration, or related field or equivalent combination of education and/or experience.</li>
<li>Five years in Marketing/Consumer Marketing, Merchandising or related area.</li>
<li>2 years supervisory/management experience.</li>
</ul>

We are not able to sponsor work visas for this position.</p>
