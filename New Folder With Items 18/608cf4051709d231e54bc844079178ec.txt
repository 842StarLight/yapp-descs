<p>
As a Product Marketing Manager for SumTotal Systems, you will concept, write and develop product messaging used in various marketing and sales tools across SumTotal’s portfolio of learning and talent solutions. This is a great opportunity for a driven marketing professional who thrives in a highly dynamic, flexible work environment. This opportunity will allow the right candidate to take their next career step with a market leader in the HR Tech industry.</p>
<p>
This opportunity is based in Boston or Nashua, NH and offers a competitive base, bonus, and benefits package. Additional information about the role can be found below.</p>
<p>
Key Skills That Will Guarantee Success in this Role:</p>
<ul>
<li>Strong writing skills</li>
<li>Comfort and ability to manage workload in a fast-paced/agile environment</li>
<li>Ability to own projects from start to finish</li>
<li>Competency in messaging/positioning (software) products
</li>
</ul>
<p>Duties &amp; Responsibilities:</p>
<ul>
<li>Develop core product messaging for SumTotal’s software solutions..</li>
<li>Author and develop content including thought-leadership whitepapers, website content, sales collateral and other tools
</li>
<li>Own the go-to-market process of select SumTotal products.</li>
<li>Ensure messaging resonates with the customers and prospects to help drive sales goals.</li>
<li>Ensure consistency of the messaging throughout the entire company</li>
<li>Help drive new product introduction and launch activities across the organization
</li>
<li>Work in conjunction with AR/PR to gain analyst and influencer mindshare</li>
<li>Develop a deep knowledge and understanding of the market, target audiences and competition.</li>
<li>Develop a strong partnership with Sales to ensure that product marketing is enabling the sales team to be successful.</li>
<li>Evangelize in the market by attending and speaking at high-visibility events and forums.
</li>
</ul>
<p>Skills &amp; Requirements:</p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree or equivalent degree with work experience.</li>
<li>3 to 5 years of experience in project management, product management or product marketing in software/HR Tech industry.</li>
<li>High level of professionalism with strong written and verbal communication skills to both internal employees and external customers.</li>
<li>Proven track record of driving revenue through integrated marketing programs.</li>
</ul>
<p>Preferred Qualifications:</p>
<ul>
<li>Experience within a technology company working with sales, marketing, and services in a distributed work environment
</li>
<li>Experience analyzing market research within the HR/HCM software space.</li>
<li>Product Marketing or industry certifications.</li>
</ul>
