<h2>Assistant Corporate Controller</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Position Summary:</b><br>
<br>
The Assistant Controller will play a key role in managing the overall accounting operations of the Company and will be the point person for the Company’s day-to-day accounting operations. This position will be hands on, interact and coordinate with colleagues in FP&amp;A, Billing Operations, and the various Business Units to ensure proper accounting treatment, execution of accounting functions and reporting of results. The Assistant Controller will play a key leadership role in the Finance organization around change management, process improvement, scaling accounting infrastructures and operations, and building and developing the team of professionals. This position will report into the Corporate Controller.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Primary Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Directs and oversees certain accounting operations, including all general ledger responsibilities, disbursements and accounts payables, fixed assets, and other functions</li>
<li>
Manage the monthly/quarterly close process and the preparation of monthly flux analysis for balance sheet and income statement ensuring that all results are properly posted and fully analyzed in a timely manner and reported to executive management per defined timelines</li>
<li>
Manage the monthly and quarterly close process including financial statement reviews with international locations</li>
<li>
Manage the general ledger accounting and consolidation processes, including journal entries, reconciliations, closing, statements, analysis and schedules. Oversee intra-company and inter-company accounting and consolidation.</li>
<li>
Ownership and management of policies and processes for areas impacting financial reporting and related processes</li>
<li>
Coordinate the planning and execution of quarterly and annual audits with our external auditors</li>
<li>
Support the Corporate Controller with the preparation of quarterly board and management presentations</li>
<li>
Develop and maintain working relationships across Finance, Legal and other organizations.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Qualifications and Experience:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
A mix of industry and public accounting experience is required</li>
<li>
Minimum 10+ years of experience with taking on progressively higher responsibilities. Experience as a people manager and thought leader is required</li>
<li>
Solid knowledge of GAAP (Generally Accepted Accounting Principles) and regulations</li>
<li>
Proficient at use of MS Office (especially excel) and accounting software (NetSuite) with working knowledge of accounting and financial processes (budgeting, financial closure, reporting etc.)</li>
<li>
Strong organizational, verbal and written communication skills and interpersonal skills are essential</li>
<li>
Attention to detail and problem-solving ability with a drive for continuously improvement</li>
<li>
Experience with NetSuite required</li>
<li>
BSc/BA in accounting, finance or relevant field is preferred</li>
<li>
Professional certification (e.g. CPA) is required</li>
</ul>
