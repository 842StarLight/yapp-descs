<h2>Commercial Account Executive, San Francisco</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">Come be a founding member on the Commercial Sales team here at InfluxData We're looking for a rockstar who's ready generating pipeline for our business with small &amp; mid-sized organizations and close some deals!
<br><br>
We want a go-getter who is ready to roll up their sleeves and take full control of their sales career. You enjoy creating new relationships and nurturing old ones. You're all about working hard, supporting one another, ideate nonstop, and excited to have a lot of fun while doing it. At InfluxData, no loss takes us down, and no win goes uncelebrated.
<br><br>
<b>What you'll be doing:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Building a sustainable pipeline and manage the entire sales lifecycle</li>
<li>Understanding the competitive landscape, &amp; positioning InfluxData to win</li>
<li>Building strong relationships and establish trust, both internally and with prospects/customers</li>
<li>Building the pipeline through various methods, including cold calling, research, etc.</li>
<li>Forecasting and managing your sales activity and pipeline to meet revenue targets and company goals</li>
<li>Meeting or exceeding monthly targets and quarterly quota</li>
</ul>
<b>What we're looking for:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>+2 years (SDR/BDR) selling data solutions, SaaS, or PaaS with another +1 year(s) in closing (AE)</li>
<li>Superior written and verbal communication skills, able to talk to all levels at an organization</li>
<li>Demonstrable history of meeting or exceeding quota</li>
<li>Strong prospecting, qualifying, closing and managing skills</li>
<li>Excellent negotiation skills</li>
</ul>
<b>It's a plus if you have:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Experience selling open source/SaaS</li>
<li>Experience in NoSQL data technologies or other modern data platforms</li>
<li>Experience selling enterprise management solutions ( Systems Management, Cloud Management, log analytics, APM, Network Monitoring)</li>
</ul>
InfluxData ( https://www.influxdata.com/ ) is the creator of InfluxDB, the leading open source time series database. We are a Series D-funded startup, backed by Sapphire Ventures, Norwest Venture Partners, Mayfield Fund, Trinity Ventures, and Battery Ventures, and a Y Combinator success story. Headquartered in San Francisco, InfluxData's workforce is distributed throughout the U.S. and across Europe. The company was recently named one of the 50 Best Workplaces for Innovators by Fast Company.
<br><br>
Our technology is purpose-built to handle the massive volumes of time-stamped data produced by IoT devices, applications, networks, containers and computers. We are on a mission to help developers and organizations, such as Cisco, IBM, PayPal, and Tesla, store and analyze real-time data, empowering them to build transformative monitoring, analytics, and IoT applications quicker and to scale.
