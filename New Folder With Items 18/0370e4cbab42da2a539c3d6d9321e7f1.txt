<p>Job Summary<br>Service Advisor/Writer, Aug 2019<br>The Lester Glenn Auto Group, one of the largest private dealership groups in the country is in immediate need for experienced Service Advisor/Writer to fill our Full Time position at our Buick/GM Dealership in Ocean County, NJ. A successful proven track record in CSI is required for this position. Our business is constantly increasing and as a result, we need to add additional advisors so we can maintain the level of quality our customers have come to expect.<br>Primary Duties &amp; Responsibilities will include:</p>
<ul>
<li>Greets, establishes, and maintains a positive rapport with customers and potential customers.</li>
<li>Assists customers in determining needs and promotes our products accordingly.</li>
<li>Estimates cost of repair and prepares itemized service order, listing costs of parts and labor.</li>
<li>Initiates service orders, secures customer’s signature, and closes when completed.</li>
<li>Develops a keen understanding of all of our products and services.</li>
<li>Understands effective service sales processes and actively seeks sales opportunities.</li>
<li>Maintains good communication with customer and follows up after work is completed to ensure satisfaction.</li>
<li>Understands customer’s needs and is committed to exceed customer expectations every day.</li>
<li>Complies with all company policies and procedures.</li>
</ul>
<p>Responsibilities and Duties<br>Requirements/Qualifications:</p>
<ul>
<li>Must possess excellent interpersonal and customer service skills.</li>
<li>Previous Service Writer/Service Advisor experience required. Previous GM epxer pref. Work experience in the vehicle service industry also required.</li>
<li>Working knowledge of computer business software to include Microsoft Word, Excel Outlook or similar business software applications.</li>
<li>Requires strong organizational skills to include the ability to effectively manage multiple projects, prioritize tasks and complete assignments within expected time frames.</li>
<li>Must be dependable, friendly, enthusiastic and self motivated</li>
<li>Organized, multi-tasked, with the ability to work effectively as part of a team or on individually-assigned tasks in a fast-paced, dynamic environment where superior time-management and prioritization skills are essential and required.</li>
<li>Must be flexible to work Saturdays and must achieve customer service index goals.</li>
</ul>
