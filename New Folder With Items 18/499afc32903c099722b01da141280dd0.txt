<p><b>Position Summary</b></p>
<p>The position of Billing Clerk will work under supervision and responsibilities include supporting and performing administrative and billing functions. Will provide customer service, general administrative, billing and data entry support including; but not limited to, ensure all billing is processed accurately and timely; as well as, aiding in any necessary collection efforts</p>
<p><b>Essential Duties</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Answer and direct calls</li>
<li>Compile all required documents related to billing and process invoices in an accurate and timely manner</li>
<li>Ensure all customer contract and special requirements are met before submitting for invoice
</li>
<li>Send invoices to clients through multiple channels including emails and customer portals</li>
<li>Prepare billing summary and able to discuss billing status, such as unprocessed invoices and aged receivable waiting on cash receipts</li>
<li>Work with the corporate accounting group to resolve any invoice issues</li>
<li>Respond to customer inquiries, maintain good customer relations</li>
<li>Resolve disputes in a timely manner, including communication with clients, sales and operations</li>
<li>Calculate, prepare and issue documents related to accounts such as rental agreements, invoices, account statements and other correspondences as requested</li>
<li>Assist with AP
</li>
<li>Perform related clerical duties, such as word processing, maintaining filing and record systems</li>
<li>Perform basic collection and follow-up activities, including misapplied cash transactions</li>
<li>Advise customers/sales team on corporate billing policies and procedures</li>
<li>Work closely with main office Accounting Department members to maintain the integrity of all financial operations</li>
<li>Perform other duties, as assigned.</li>
</ul>
