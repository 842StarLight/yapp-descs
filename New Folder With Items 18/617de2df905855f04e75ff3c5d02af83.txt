<h2>Demand Planning Analyst</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>We have an exciting opportunity for a Demand Planning Analyst at our Knoxville, TN location. The ideal candidate is a high energy, personable, self-motivated individual who is able to think quickly on his/her feet, solve problems independently, and proactively seek out work that needs to be done without waiting for it to arrive. This position offers growth potential for the right motivated candidate who is eager to learn and grow with the company.</p>
<p><b>Summary</b><br>The Demand Planning Analyst will work within various departments of the company to perform revenue forecasting, inventory level alignment, material planning, and procurement forecasting.</p>
<p><b>Essential Functions</b><br>Utilizing forecast and historical data to create and run weekly/monthly demand planning needs<br>Analysis and dissection of complex data and extraction of key information<br>Interpretation of data to identify and develop trends and their implications<br>Initiate, develop and undertake analyses or projects to improve inventory metrics<br>Analyze data to identify trends and suggest strategies to ensure proper inventory levels are achieved to ensure company goals are met – to service the customer<br>Create effective cross-functional working relationships with all internal departments and individuals: Finance, Logistics, Inventory, Supply Chain, and Sales<br>Based upon calculated data, issue blanket purchase order requests to suppliers<br>Reasonable accommodations may be made to enable individuals with disabilities to perform the essential functions.</p>
<p><b>Required Education and Experience: </b><br>Bachelor’s Degree Required<br>Three (3) + years of relevant experience preferred</p>
<p><b>Competencies</b>:<br>Exceptional integrity and honesty. The confidence and tact to communicate effectively and persuasively, with customers, consumers, and all levels of internal staff.<br>Resourceful, pro-active mind set with the ability to multi-task, problem solve, and prioritize with minimal supervision.<br>Self-motivated and flexible individual, but also a team player with excellent organizational, planning, time management skills, and possess the ability for follow-up.<br>Ability to work in a team environment managing multiple projects, while concurrently able to manage personal projects from inception to completion in a timely manner.<br>Rigorous attention to detail while utilizing strong written and oral communication skills.<br>Ready and excited to be a key player in the growth of our business and company</p>
<p><b>Work Environment: </b><br>This job operates in an office within a warehouse environment. This role routinely uses standard office equipment such as computers, phones, photocopiers, filing cabinets.</p>
<p><b>Supervisory Responsibility: </b><br>This position has no supervisory responsibilities.</p>
<p><b>Physical Demands: </b><br>The physical demands described here are representative of those that must be met by an employee to successfully perform the essential functions of this job.<br>While performing the duties of this job, the employee is regularly required to talk or hear. The employee frequently is required to sit, use hands and fingers, and reach with arms.</p>
<p><b>Position Type/Expected Hours of Work: </b><br>This is a full-time position, with work days Monday through Friday, with general hours from 8am – 5pm.</p>
<p><b>Travel: </b><br>Regular travel is not anticipated for this position, however, limited travel may be occasionally requested.</p>
<p><b>Additional Eligibility Qualifications</b><br>Must pass Microsoft skills test.<br>Advance skills in Microsoft Excel Required<br>Strong working knowledge of MS Office Word and Outlook<br>Experience with Crystal Reports and Microsoft Dynamics Great Plains a plus<br>Adapt in the use of common cloud based business software tools such as Google Docs, Basecamp, Drobox, etc.</p>
