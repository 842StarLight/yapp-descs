<h2>Advertising Sales Representative</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Job Summary</b></p>
<p>This is an outside sales role focusing on the Victoria, Goliad, Refugio, and Cuero markets. It allows the account executive to be in the field selling business to business. With this outside sales role you are able to meet with business owners and managers face to face with a process and a plan that works for them. Who doesn't love a win-win proposition?</p>
<p><b>Company</b></p>
<p>We are a family-owned group of community newspapers and websites serving the South Texas market in Bee, Karnes, Live Oak, McMullen, San Patricio, Refugio and Goliad Counties. Our sales team members sell both print and digital advertising targeting wonderful communities overlooked by metropolitan media. It's a fun and rewarding job with competitive pay, incentive packages, group health, 401K plan and more. If you are reasonably smart, poised, organized, dependable and motivated, you can join a supportive team and excel at this people-oriented career. Good base with no cap to commissions, so you control your own pay.</p>
<p><b>Experience Needed</b></p>
<p>Outside sales experience is advantageous, but not required if you have the right attributes -- organized, poised, smart, motivated. If you live in Beeville, Victoria, Goliad, Refugio region and would like to find out more about these opportunities to start a new career in sales please contact us ASAP!</p>
