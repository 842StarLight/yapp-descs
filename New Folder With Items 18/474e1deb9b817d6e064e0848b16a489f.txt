<h2>Customer Success Manager - West</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div><b>Customer Success Manager</b></div>
<div></div>
<div>RichRelevance is the global leader in Experience Personalization, driving digital growth and brand loyalty for more than 200 of the world’s largest B2C and B2B brands and retailers. The company leverages advanced AI technologies to bridge the experience gap between marketing and commerce to help digital marketing leaders stage memorable experiences that speak to individuals – at scale, in real time, and across the customer lifecycle. Headquartered in San Francisco, RichRelevance serves clients in 42 countries from 9 offices around the globe.</div>
<div></div>
<div>As a Customer Success Manager (CSM), you will manage assigned customer relationships post-sale and ensure that our customers enjoy a positive experience with RichRelevance and remain committed to our solutions.</div>
<div></div>
<div>To be successful you must:</div>
<ul>
<li>Develop deep, trusted relationships with key influencers and decision-makers at your assigned accounts to build loyalty and satisfaction, and cultivate long-term, referenceable engagement with our solutions.</li>
<li>Secure the renewal of customers’ existing licenses and service agreements and propose, negotiate and sell additional personalization solutions that align with their strategy and needs.</li>
<li>Convey RichRelevance’s value proposition throughout the client organization on its own merits and versus competitor (or no) solutions.</li>
<li>Gain visibility into the customer’s vision, understand their strategic objectives and trajectory and their key success metrics; use your insights to identify further opportunities for partnership and facilitate collaboration between customer and RichRelevance teams.</li>
<li>Agree on key success metrics, monitor and ensure that our customers optimize their use of our solutions in a way that builds our strategic impact and drives deeper product utilization.</li>
<li>Lead onsite business reviews with key stakeholders in the client organization; internally, create status reports, churn and upsell forecasts, and other presentations and reports as needed.</li>
<li>Anticipate customer personalization needs and proactively engage the right parties on both sides from scoping through launch.</li>
<li>Project-manage cross-functional interventions and escalations to resolve product or service challenges; ensure that appropriate resources are dedicated, aligned and collaborate effectively (both customer and RichRelevance) to meet/exceed project expectations.</li>
<li>detailed deployment timelines, and project success criteria.</li>
<li>Educate the client on how to be successful during all phases of relationship with tools and processes.</li>
<li>Share feedback internally on evolving customer needs and market pressures that could impact our product and service development; partner with Services, Product and other internal teams to identify and prioritize new capabilities that could add value to the customer and RichRelevance and collaborate on launching new offerings.</li>
</ul>
<div><b>Qualifications Needed to be Successful:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree in a related field such as business, marketing or technology (or the equivalent in combined education and experience) required; commitment to continual learning preferred.</li>
<li>5 or more years of relevant customer success, account management or related experience in a fast paced, innovative technology company.</li>
<li>Demonstrated proficiency representing complex SaaS solutions in complex, multi-level customer environments required.</li>
<li>Experience in ecommerce ecosystem, online consumer analytics (e.g., analysis of online transactions, avg. order value, conversion rates) and Web-based reporting (e.g., MS Access, Cognos, Crystal Reports) strongly preferred.</li>
<li>Proven track record of meeting and exceeding key metrics (churn, upsell quotas, etc.) and growing the assigned book of business.</li>
<li>Consultative and a strong communicator; able to navigate complex, dynamic organizational structures and build exceptional relationships with key stakeholders and influencers.</li>
<li>Demonstrated success managing mid-cap to top-tier ($5B+) accounts and client relationships up to the VP/C-level.</li>
<li>Demonstrated success managing all aspects of the customer lifecycle from contract execution through service integration to ongoing relationship management and strategic support.</li>
<li>Experience with defining, documenting and adhering to professional services best practices.</li>
<li>Demonstrated proficiency delivering customer-facing presentations up to the C-level.</li>
<li>Think creatively, sell strategically and communicate efficiently internally and externally.</li>
<li>Ability to work cross-functionally and with a wide range of employees with varying skill sets, creating advocates at the execution- through C-level (on both sides).</li>
<li>Diligent, reliable, and extremely detail oriented with strong project management abilities; able to multi-task, prioritize issues/escalations, and manage multiple customer initiatives simultaneously.</li>
<li>Strong business acumen, excellent analytical skills, proficient using CRMs (Salesforce preferred) and highly skilled at using Microsoft Office (Excel, PowerPoint, Word)</li>
<li>Travel required, estimated at 20-30%, depending on account assignment; predominantly domestic.</li>
</ul>
