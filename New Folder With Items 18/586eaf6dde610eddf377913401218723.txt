<h2>Self Storage Facility Manager in El Dorado Hills</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>We are seeking to fill a <b>Self-Storage Facility Manager (full-time/permanent)</b> position with one of the leading providers of self-storage in the Northern California area. This position contributes to facility profitability by developing and maintaining mutually beneficial business relationships and being committed to customer satisfaction. This Manager pays close attention to detail and has general sales, marketing, and computer skills.</p>
<p><b>Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Responsible for the sales, marketing, occupancy, and delinquency management of the facility</li>
<li>Provides outstanding customer service including assessing and resolving customer problems and professionally handling telephone and walk-in inquiries</li>
<li>Shows, rents and cleans self-storage units as well as actively selling and maintaining supplies of moving and packing materials</li>
<li>General management of the office including but not limited to opening and closing the office on time each day, keeping and balancing petty cash, preparing daily, reports as required by management.</li>
<li>Keeping the facility rented at optimum levels by effectively utilizing advertising, promotional and customer relation skills as approved by management</li>
<li>Maintains all required tenant documentation in a neat and orderly manner as directed by management</li>
<li>Controls delinquencies through telephone contact and letters on a scheduled basis including sending out monthly invoices</li>
<li>Prepares units for auction as prescribed by law, and company procedures</li>
<li>Cleans space(s) as vacated, hallways, and grounds as needed. Clean office and restrooms daily</li>
<li>Maintains a neat, clean, safe and secure facility including minor maintenance and daily lock checks as well as visual inspection daily</li>
<li>Effectively communicates with management and fellow associates</li>
<li>Other functional duties as assigned</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Work Skills</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Basic Computer Skills are required</li>
<li>Good organizational and customer service skills</li>
</ul>
