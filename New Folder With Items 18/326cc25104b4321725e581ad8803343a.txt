<p><b>Sweet Career Opportunity at IT'SUGAR!</b></p>
<p><b>Who we are:</b></p>
<p><b>IT’SUGAR </b>was founded to create an environment that fosters the greatest feeling of happiness and humor; that allows you to smile and laugh out loud without judgement. Expanding rapidly, with over 100 locations nationwide, <b>IT’SUGAR </b>aspires to a future where everyone has access to the pure joy that comes from indulging in a world with fewer rules and more sugar.</p>
<p>There are three major things that differentiate <b>IT’SUGAR </b>from other candy retailers. One is the product – an unconventional twist on traditional candy store goods that focus on the humorous and outlandish. Second is the ambiance – full of bright colors, loud music, and lighthearted rebellion. Third is the people – passionate, optimistic, and energetic.</p>
<p>We are seeking a <b>Nights and Weekend PT IT Help Desk Support</b> candidates for our home office in <b>Deerfield Beach, FL!</b></p>
<p><b>Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Promptly respond to Store and Corporate service tickets, emails, and phone calls indicating a user support request, using a root cause analysis approach including assigning ticket severity and updating service work notes</li>
<li>Troubleshooting tier 1 and tier 2 technical issues including, but not limited to, passwords management, troubleshooting network connectivity issues, and Point of Sale Retail components, POS, scanners, printers, scales, payment devices</li>
<li>Establishing a high level of personal credibility and building strong relationships</li>
<li>Maintaining ownership of service tickets through the full life cycle to resolution</li>
<li>Ensuring the security standards of all configurations made on client systems are followed</li>
<li>Providing updates, status, and completion information to management to support a team environment</li>
<li>Installation, configuration, and troubleshooting of Microsoft desktop operating systems and applications, Microsoft server software, such as Server 2012, Active Directory, Group Policy, Exchange/Office 365, SQL Server</li>
<li>Background in business network troubleshooting, implementation, and VPN configuration</li>
<li>Install, test and configure new workstations, peripheral equipment and software</li>
<li>Imaging software and the use of such for mass deployment of Desktop / POS pc based systems.</li>
<li>Help maintain accurate inventory of all POS equipment, corporate hardware and software licenses</li>
<li>Ability to resolve basic technical problems with LAN or WAN related</li>
<li>Manage PC setup and deployment for new employees using standard hardware, images and software</li>
<li>Willingness to wear many hats and take on many different tasks throughout the day</li>
<li>Patience to solve technical problems remotely with end users of varying technical aptitudes</li>
<li>Other job duties as deemed necessary to maintain a stable IT department and fluid operations of the company may be included at any time.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Qualifications:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>High School Diploma, GED or equivalent and a minimum</li>
<li>3-5 years of experience in systems installation, maintenance and upgrades required.</li>
<li>Apple, Windows, SQL and Network certifications</li>
<li>E CompTIA Network + CompTIA A+</li>
<li>Experience with Win 10 rollouts and MDM solutions</li>
<li>Verifone equipment experience</li>
<li>Organized individual with the ability to prioritize and multi-task daily work, projects, and requests.</li>
<li>Detail oriented with strong documentation and communication skills</li>
<li>Experience using formal Help Desk ticketing systems – implementation and on boarding experience with Help Desk ticketing systems a huge plus.</li>
<li>Very strong desktop support using various technologies preferred</li>
<li>Individual that likes to “roll up their sleeves” to find and resolve permanent solutions to ongoing issues.</li>
<li>Must be able to lift 50-70lbs as pertained to Servers and Computer equipment.</li>
<li>Previous retail experience a big plus.</li>
</ul>
