<h2>Category Buyer - Central Network Retail Group (CNRG) Birmingham, AL</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>We are looking for outstanding candidates to Category Buyers at Central Network Retail Group (CNRG). Candidate must have 2 years or more in industry retail. Experience with hardware is a must for this position.</p>
<p>
The Category Buyer position is comprised of multiple roles with the ultimate responsibility being support of store operations through planning, selecting and purchasing quantities of goods and merchandise that are sold within CNRG locations. They have company wide responsibilities of vendor collaboration and source new and review existing goods to ensure all CNRG Brands remain competitive. The Category Manager manages product categories sourcing and building relations with new suppliers. Identifying new products and programs through attending national and international shows as well as monitoring market changes, competitive pricing and sales trend analysis is part of the Category Manager's responsibility. Additionally, the Category Manager will participate in strategic business initiatives, data management and contribute to the company's overall growth.</p>

<div><p><b>
Essential Duties &amp; Responsibilities</b></p></div>
<h1 class="jobSectionHeader"><b><br>
</b></h1>
<p><b>
Merchandise Management</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Select and merchandise products and lines consistent with company strategy for the maximization of sales and profit.</li>
<li>
Collaborate with vendor partners and negotiate the best possible total costs.</li>
<li>
Establish retail pricing and maintain gross profit levels consistent with company objectives.</li>
<li>
Recommend merchandise for promotions and advertising.</li>
<li>
Responsibility for sales growth, gross margin, turn rate and GMROI.</li>
<li>
Select merchandise in accordance with good customer safety standards, quality consistent with our desired image and from vendors with a high reputation for integrity and reliability.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Planning</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Establish operational and merchandise-based objectives in conjunction with the marketing team and take appropriate steps to see that these are achieved.</li>
<li>
Quarterly review of your merchandise categories and presentation of a summary of your progress plans for the future and objectives for the year.</li>
<li>
Plan, schedule and execute buys to support advertising program.</li>
<li>
Anticipate merchandise trends and communicate direction or recommendation to team.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Control</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Maintain gross profit objectives of the company.</li>
<li>
Maintain inventory turnover of the company.</li>
<li>
Maintain GMROI with company objectives.</li>
<li>
Maintain current, competitive lines.</li>
<li>
Approve new vendors and new products for respective areas of responsibility.</li>
<li>
Review and approve rebuys of regular stocked goods.</li>
<li>
Keep current on market trends, competition, prices, and changes in consumer buying habits.</li>
<li>
Visit Brands and stores on a regular basis and provide feedback.</li>
<li>
Monitor store replenishment, in-house fill rates, lead times and external vendor fill rates.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Communication</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Communicate merchandise news to team and Operations.</li>
<li>
Communicate effectively and cooperatively with vendor community, peers, subordinates, supervisors, other departments and store employees.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>

Self-Development</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Continually improve job knowledge and skills by reading, studying or attending seminars related to the merchandising and marketing fields.</li>
<li>
Stay abreast of advances in technology related to all aspect of role and</li>

Attend trade shows.</li>
