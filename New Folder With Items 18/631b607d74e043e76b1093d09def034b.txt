<h2>Business Operations Specialist Raleigh NC • Business Operations Department • Full-time</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>CompostNow is looking for a stellar individual to join our Raleigh-based team and play a key role in working with our internal systems, analyzing company business data and completing special projects within our Business Operations department.</p>
<p>Our mission at CompostNow is to grow the composting movement by making it easy for our members to divert waste from landfills and enrich soils. By hauling food scraps and delivering compost, we do the dirty work for them and ensure success. To build the movement, it is imperative that our systems, data and company metrics continue to scale and adapt to the growth of our Operational footprint.</p>
<br>
<div>
<p><b>Position Overview</b></p>
<p>You’ll be part of a small, but quickly growing, team in a startup environment and be able to get involved in many aspects of growing a social enterprise. The core of this Entry-level position is to help support (and enhance) the functionality of our Business Operations department, as well as our Growth department. This will involve working closely with members of both departments to assist in daily functional tasks, as well as one-off business needs and longer term project initiatives.</p>
<p><b>Qualifications &amp; Skills</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Extremely organized, with a fine tuned attention to detail</li>
<li>Strong work ethic</li>
<li>Proactive problem solver</li>
<li>Interested in, or already knows, APIs / SQL</li>
<li>Extremely proficient in Excel (Google Sheets)</li>
<li>Ability to navigate and use web-based administrative tools and apps such as Google Drive, project management tools, and CRM’s (tech savvy)</li>
<li>Ability to multitask and handle multiple requests at once, while prioritizing tasks and executing efficiently</li>
<li>Ability to communicate with team members in a clear, precise and friendly manner, while completing assigned tasks independently</li>
<li>Ability to professionally represent the CompostNow brand in fast-paced environment</li>
<li>Willingness to ask questions when unsure, being able to work with multiple stakeholders</li>
<li>Customer Service experience a plus</li>
</ul>

<ul>

<li>Creating and sending member invoices in a timely and accurate manner</li>
<li>Responding to member inquiries regarding invoices</li>
<li>Managing new &amp; changing billing information for our members</li>
<li>General data entry</li>
<li>Connecting 3rd party apps to help improve &amp; automate business processes</li>
<li>Cleaning up raw data to sync across multiple 3rd party apps and systems</li>
<li>Assisting in and helping to manage various transactional processes
<ul>
<li>Compostable Packaging reordering &amp; inventory levels</li>

<li>Using CompostNow internal systems to create delivery instructions for our drivers</li>
</ul>
</li>

<li>Learn, understand, and help maintain Growth Department KPI’s</li>

<li>Analysis of existing data to draw conclusions and help forecast outcomes of potential business changes</li>
</ul>
