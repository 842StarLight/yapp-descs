<h2>Material Program Management Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>The MPM Specialist role supports the Air Warfare product line in Tucson, AZ. This role has a strong emphasis on managing program financials, combined with managing daily tasks and the relationships with suppliers/customers. With our company focus on cash, profit, bookings, and sales, managing the program financials to high expectations in this position is top priority. The successful MPM candidate will work extensively with internal customers including PMO, Quality, Engineering, Operations and Finance. Successful candidate will have Finance and Supply Chain experience and exhibit strong leadership skills with the ability to influence and communicate effectively across the organization. This role will also be asked to contribute to enterprise initiatives, both in the program and within Supply Chain.
<br>
<br>
U.S. Citizenship status is required as this position will need a U.S. Security Clearance within 1 year of start date.<br>
<br>

Identifying and mitigating program risks; support weekly supplier meetings</li>

Resolving Supply Chain issues for the program</li>

Security Clearance: This position requires the successful issuance, transfer or maintenance of a Secret security clearance. Non-US citizens may not be eligible to obtain a security clearance. The Defense Industrial Security Clearance Office (DISCO), an agency of the Department of Defense, handles and adjudicates the security clearance process. Security clearance factors include, but are not limited to, allegiance to the US, foreign influence, foreign preference, criminal conduct, security violations and drug involvement. Additional detail regarding security clearance factors can be obtained by accessing the DISCO website at http://www.dss.mil/psmo-i/indus_psmo-i_interim.html<br>
