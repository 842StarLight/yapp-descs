<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Job Description</b></h2>
<p>
The Professional Solutions Consultant (PSC) will be accountable for all project related activities associated with the support and delivery of a client solution on behalf of the Commercial Services business unit. The position is responsible for complex and custom customer projects in the areas of products, services, data, systems, tools, processes and procedures. The three primary functions of the role are to ensure the operational objectives of quality, time service and customer satisfaction are met. The successful PSC candidate will have a solid work history preferably in the business-to-business data provider space. The PSC will represent the Operations department in a leadership role while fitting into the project team environment, and will be responsible for client management, project scheduling, quality assurance, project delivery and post-delivery support. The candidate will have a proven ability to consult with assigned customer base on “outside-the-box” processing and/or products. They will demonstrate the desire to look for and uncover opportunities to improve customers’ programs. The ideal candidate will be an active leader in a collaborative team environment, and passionate about the quality of our work, our clients, and our culture.</p>
