<div>
The Senior Oracle Applications Developer’s role is to partner with customers, functional business analysts and other team members in driving appropriate design and implementation of technical solutions to meet desired business requirements. Design, develop, test, analyze, and maintain the Oracle E-Business Suite Applications within the application in support of the business function. The developer will also research, design, document, and modify software specifications throughout the production life cycle in accordance with the business goals and organizational architectural standards.</div>

<div><b>Essential Functions<br>
</b></div>
<div></div>
<div><b>Strategy &amp; Planning</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Collaborate with business analysts, business users and other developers in conceptualization and development of new technical objects within the Oracle applications environment.</li>
<li>
Analyze and assess existing procedures to allow the application to respond to business changes.</li>
<li>
Evaluate requirements from functional specifications and create efficient technical designs.</li>
<li>
Analyze and plan Oracle release upgrades.</li>
</ul>
<div><b>Acquisition &amp; Deployment</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Develop complex technical objects in accordance with the technical development standards and best practices.</li>
<li>
Mitigate significant risks associated with projects, which have a high technical complexity and/or involve significant challenges to the business.</li>
<li>
Create and resolve Service Requests in collaboration with Oracle Support.</li>
<li>
Provide generalized production technical support.</li>
<li>
Gather business requirements and recommend industry best practices and work with functional knowledge holders.</li>
</ul>
<div><b>Operational Management</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Design, develop, deploy, test and maintain technical objects that exist in Oracle applications production environment, e.g., reports, interfaces, conversions and custom applications extensions.</li>
<li>
Create process flows, high level functional and detailed technical design specifications from business requirements.</li>
<li>
Provide customer support for business critical application problems and issues.</li>
<li>
Assist with testing and deployment of Release Update Pack and other module upgrades to keep application release level current.</li>
<li>
Proactively work through service requests with vendor support as needed.</li>
<li>
Coordinate work efforts with the DBA and middleware teams for application change controls and system performance issues.</li>
<li>
Provide support to System Administrators relating to custom responsibilities, security profiles, menus, and user account provisioning.</li>
<li>
Partner with business analysts and application super users to successfully complete projects.</li>
</ul>
