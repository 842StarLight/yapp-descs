<b>Your Role and Responsibilities</b><br>
The Hybrid Cloud Services Sales Line Representative is a position in the IBM GTS organization responsible for sales of IBM’s Multi-Cloud and Hybrid-Cloud solutions portfolio, Managed Services for Client's Server, Storage, and Cloud Platforms, Data Center Transformation, IT Outsourcing, and Selected Business Partner Offerings among other capabilities.<br>
<br>
<b>What you can expect in the role:
</b><ul>
<li>This role requires Client interfacing and engagement while selling the Hybrid Cloud Services portfolio directly with Computer Services Industry Market clients in the IBM Enterprise and Commercial space</li>
<li>
Develop, Own, and Drive Hybrid Cloud Opportunities across the assigned territory to closure</li>
<li>
Lead IBM sales support team to cost, solution, and price opportunity for your clients.</li>
<li>
Be responsible to write statements of work and negotiate statements of work as the opportunity owner with your clients.</li>
<li>
Provide accurate and timely forecast, funnel management, target/objective updates to deliver on territory plans</li>
<li>
Sell services solutions across Public Clouds, Private Clouds, Containers, Mainframe, I-series, and P-Series.</li>
<li>
Be a Dealmaker leveraging IBM’s Brand and Partner Ecosystem to provide value to clients while delivering sales growth into IBM’s GTS Industrial Market Enterprise and Commercial territory
</li>
</ul>
<br>
<b>What you bring to the role:</b><ul>
<li>
Leverage existing experience to develop new opportunities leveraging IBM Hybrid Cloud Portfolio.
</li>
<li>Superb time management, judgment, decision-making and problem-solving skills</li>
<li>
Thrives as both an individual contributor and in a highly-collaborative team environment</li>
<li>
Expert in high-impact communication and presentation skills</li>
<li>
Drive results and overachieve sales targets for business solutions</li>
<li>
Proficient in sales &amp; closing, business development, financial selling</li>
<li>
Significant knowledge of IT Industry, cloud, server, storage, and operating systems</li>
<li>
Significant expertise/industry knowledge selling into Computer Services Industry marketplace</li>
</ul>
The Hybrid Cloud Services Service Line Representative (SLR) must possess the following Skills: sales/closing skills, business development skills, financial selling skills, significant IT Industry knowledge, large and complex deal skills, significant knowledge of cloud , server, storage, and operating systems, deep understanding of managed services, an ability to work in a matrixed environment, outstanding oral, written, and presentation communication skills, and an ability to team and function in team selling environments as well as sell alone and without assistance.<br>

<li>
At least 5 years of experience in Solution/Managed Services Sales</li>

At least 3 years of experience in Consultative Sales strategies and methodologies</li>

At least 5 years of experience in Executive Relationship Management</li>
