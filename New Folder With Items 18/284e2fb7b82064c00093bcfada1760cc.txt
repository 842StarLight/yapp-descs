<b>Where you fit:</b><br>
Springbrook is currently seeking a Business Development Executive to focus on Small/Medium Government customers. This position can be located ideally on the West Coast. The SMG Business Development Executive will be responsible for selling Springbrook's subscription and perpetual license offerings to small and mid-size local government agencies throughout the United States.
