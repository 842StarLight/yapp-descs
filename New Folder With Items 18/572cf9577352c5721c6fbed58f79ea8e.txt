<h2>Bookkeeper</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>The ideal candidate will be able to work independently and problem solve; not shying away from challenges that arise with new billing systems or application requirements of customers. This candidate will be able to work alone, must be well organized, flexible, and enjoy the challenges of supporting team members. This person will be able to complete complex billing tasks in a timely manner with limited supervision.</p>
<p>Must have expert level written and verbal communication skills, and pay great attention to detail while providing exceptional customer service. They must also be extremely trustworthy in the handling of confidential information. Experience in the QuickBooks &amp; Construction billing is not required but is highly preferred. Compensation based on experience.</p>
<p>Job Responsibilities include but are not limited to:</p>
<p><b>Invoice Entry and Delivery</b></p>
<p><b><i>Project Billing</i></b></p>
<ul>
<li>Maintain list of projects with billing dates</li>
<li>Pull projects for billing according to billing dates</li>
<li>Create invoice in the books for each draw directly from project estimate</li>
<li>Complete pay application paperwork</li>
<li>Submit application documents to customers (email, fax, mail, online systems)</li>
</ul>
<p><b><i>Sales Billing</i></b></p>
<ul>
<li>Receive sales order documentation from sales department</li>
<li>Flip sales order to invoice</li>
<li>Double-check all information on newly created invoice against sales documents</li>
<li>Make copies of invoices</li>
<li>Submit all required paperwork to customers (mail, fax, email, online systems)</li>
</ul>
<p><b><i>Service Billing</i></b></p>
<ul>
<li>Receive approved work order from Operations Manager</li>
<li>Create invoice in the books from work order documentation</li>
<li>Service Agreement and Alarm Monitoring Company</li>
<li>Print master spreadsheet with billing schedule and amounts</li>
<li>Create invoices from information contained in master spreadsheet</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Filing</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Assure security of project files and safekeeping</li>
<li>Know where project files are at all times; maintain the project file checkout log</li>
<li>Pull project files for billing</li>
<li>Pull according to billing cycles, or as otherwise directed by management</li>
<li>Keep a record of which files are pulled for billing to ensure that no files are missed in a given period, and that all files are returned</li>
<li>Keep a record of projects that are billed 100% so that they are not pulled during standard billing cycles</li>
<li>Help maintain project file accuracy and organization</li>
<li>Verify that all documents for filing have been accurately coded</li>
<li>Make sure that all documents are file in appropriate sections of the project files</li>
</ul>

</ul>
