<p><b>About You</b></p>
<p>
You are an expert full-stack Javascript developer, who will be working on our React/React Native apps and our API, written in Node.js and Typescript, backed by MySQL and Redis, and deployed to AWS. You are hands-on, and desire to be involved in all aspects of the product lifecycle from the initial idea through prototyping and shipping the final product. You want to be part of a team where everyone feels comfortable to voice their thoughts and ideas. You are interested in enterprise cloud, big data, and have an appetite for compliance and data governance. You love to write clean, fast, compatible, easy-to-use, and testable Javascript to realize ideas, and collaborate with Product Designers, Product Managers, and Software Engineers to deliver compelling products.</p>
<p>
</p>
<p><b>Skills &amp; Qualifications</b></p>
<ul>
<li>3+ years of real product experience - you have shipped products used by thousands of users</li>
<li>Experience with our tech stack - Typescript, MySQL, Docker, AWS, HTML/CSS/JavaScript, Sass &amp; LESS, React.js, Node.js, SQL, Java, Kotlin, Python, Spring</li>
<li>High degree of fluency in JavaScript - you are a strong coder and know how to write clean, effective code at scale (React/Redux)</li>
<li>Testing with Jest and Enzyme (or similar)</li>
<li>Experience with automatic building/deployment services like Jenkins, Fastlane etc.</li>
<li>Writing standards-compliant, accessible markup and styling using CSS preprocessors or css-in-js</li>
<li>Using, designing and building RESTful APIs</li>
<li>Experience building connectors</li>
<li>Familiarity with design patterns, data structures, and algorithms</li>
<li>The desire and ability to learn quickly</li>
<li>You want to work on software that is changing the world and you're passionate (and obsessed) about creating intuitive, scalable products</li>
<li>Ability to work effectively in teams of technical and non-technical individuals (sales, subject matter experts in legal and compliance).</li>
</ul>
