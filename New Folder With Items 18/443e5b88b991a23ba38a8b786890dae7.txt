<h2>Regional Account Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<div><div>
<p>Manages the security services and related operations in the United States, provided on an assigned account including client service and problem resolution, service enhancement and expansion, new business development, operational effectiveness, preparation of post orders, staffing, scheduling, supervision and training. Reports to the National Account Manager for the assigned account.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>ESSENTIAL FUNCTIONS</b></p>
<ul>
<li>The functions listed describe the business purpose of this job or position. Specific duties or tasks may vary and be documented separately. An associate might or might not be required to perform all functions listed. Additional duties may be assigned, and functions may be modified, according to business necessity.</li>
<li>All assigned duties or tasks are deemed to be part of the essential functions, unless such duties or tasks are unrelated to the functions listed, in which case they are deemed to be other (non-essential) functions.</li>
<li>Associates are held accountable for successful job performance. Job performance standards may be documented separately, and may include functions, objectives, duties or tasks not specifically listed herein.</li>
<li>In performing functions, duties or tasks, associates are required to know and follow safe work practices, and to be aware of company policies and procedures related to job safety, including safety rules and regulations. Associates are required to notify superiors upon becoming aware of unsafe working conditions.</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>All functions, duties or tasks are to be carried out in an honest, ethical and professional manner, and to be performed in conformance with applicable company policies and procedures. In the event of uncertainty or lack of knowledge of company policies and procedures, associates are required to request clarification or explanations from superiors or authorized company representatives.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p></p>
<p>Directs or completes tasks associated with these areas of responsibilities:</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>1. Serves as a key point of and initiates any necessary corrective action in a timely manner.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>2.Assumes full P &amp; L responsibilityalong with the National Account Manager and Branch Managers; analyzes operational and financial indicators to continuously improve account performance.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>3.Coordinates activity with and acts as liaison for the National Account Manager to provide highest level of service for an assigned account; implements programs and initiatives as directed by National Account Manager.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>4.Meets regularly with client representatives for status updates; addresses any actual or potential problems; assists line management in negotiation of contract; provides support during client start-up; supports security planning, assessments and surveys; reviews and updates post orders.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>5.Oversees and coordinates with Site management, and participates in the recruitment, selection, orientation, training, development and retention of high caliber staff; acts to ensure that each staff member is treated with dignity and respect; plans, assigns, and directs work; coaches employees and carries out disciplinary actions, as necessary.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>6.Assists National Account Manager in development and administration of budget in relation to assigned account.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>7.Maintains a positive, professional environment in full compliance with applicable laws, regulations, policies and procedures; acts to ensure that staff members understand and comply with applicable laws, regulations, policies and procedures.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>8.Within scope of the client contract and assigned duties, authorizes appropriate expenditures including equipment, supplies, and vehicles; acts to ensure that vehicles are properly maintained and administers driver training; acts to ensure that there is an adequate inventory of uniforms, radios, and other supplies and equipment; maintains and submits payroll records and other associate and business information.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>9.Prepares and coordinates staffing schedules for account, in collaboration with line management as necessary; acts to ensure that scheduling is handled effectively to meet client requirements while controlling labor costs; reviews Security Officer site reports to verify post orders and client directives have been satisfactorily followed; personally inspects all posts as part the evaluation of security staff.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>10.Provides input to company initiatives; promptly assists in the resolution of legal, financial, human resources, and administrative issues.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>11.Performs tasks and duties of a similar nature and scope as required for assigned account.</p>
</div></div>

<p><b>WORKING CONDITIONS (Physical/Mental Demands)</b></p>
<p>With or without reasonable accommodation, requires the physical and mental capacity to perform effectively all essential functions. In addition to other demands, the demands of the job include:</p>
<ul>
<li>Maintaining composure in dealing with authorities, executives, clients, staff, and the public, occasionally under conditions of urgency and in pressure situations.</li>
<li>Must undergo and meet company standards for background and reference checks, controlled substance testing, and behavioral selection survey.</li>
<li>Ability to handle multiple tasks concurrently.</li>
<li>Handling and being exposed to sensitive and confidential information.</li>
<li>May require regular use of vehicle and frequent travel in the performance of duties.</li>
<li>Regular talking and hearing.</li>
<li>Frequent lifting and/or moving up to 10 pounds and occasional lifting and/or moving up to 25 pounds.</li>
<li>Walking, reaching with hands and arms, stooping, kneeling, crouching and crawling.</li>
<li>Close vision, distance vision, and ability to adjust focus.</li>
<li>Conducting oral presentations and group meetings.</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Directing, motivating, training, coaching, and disciplining staff in a positive manner.</li>
<li>Reading and analyzing reports and financial data, including related computer usage.</li>
<li>Responding on an on-call basis to emergencies and incidents at all hours.</li>
<br>
</ul>
