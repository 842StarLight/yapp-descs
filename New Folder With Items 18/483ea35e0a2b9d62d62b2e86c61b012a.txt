<h2>Part-Time Center Associate</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>The Part-Time Center Associate delivers world-class customer service to all retail customers by receiving and processing packages for courier shipment and operating copiers, fax machines, binding equipment, laminating machines, and point-of-sale devices. The Associate expertly advises our valued customers by providing accurate information on our array of products, services, and best-value options and exhibits confidence by knowing best practices related to the industry.<br>
<br>
The ideal candidate has previous retail sales experience, strong computer and internet skills, a high school diploma or GED, a friendly and genuinely helpful demeanor, and a professional appearance. He or she must be a fast learner who makes an effort to learn all aspects of the business in the shortest possible time.<br>
</p>
<b>
RESPONSIBILITIES</b><ul>
<li>
Delivers outstanding customer service to walk-in customers and telephone inquiries</li>
<li>
Continuously practices good listening skills with customers, UPS Store team members, and leadership</li>
<li>
Takes ownership of the customer's shipping needs and offers viable solutions</li>
<li>
Takes action to learn all product and service offerings, alternative solutions, and industry trends</li>
<li>
Operates all equipment, software, and devices in an expert fashion and is willing to teach others
</li>
<li>Maintains a clean and safe working environment</li>
</ul>
<br>
<b>
QUALIFICATIONS</b><ul>
<li>
High school diploma or GED required</li>
<li>
Retail/customer service experience</li>
<li>
Good communication and people skills</li>
<li>
Outstanding phone skills</li>
<li>
Strong verbal and written communication skills, including spelling and math</li>
<li>
Prompt, reliable, and responsible</li>
<li>
Able to lift 40+ pounds</li>
<li>
Strong computer skills, including Microsoft Office.</li>
<li>
Store is open Mon thru Fri 10:30AM - 7:00PM and Sat 9AM - 4PM.</li>
<li>
Knowledge and application of printing concepts helpful.</li>
<li>
Available to work 8AM - 12PM or 3PM - 7PM and be available some Sat from 9AM - 4PM.</li>
</ul>
