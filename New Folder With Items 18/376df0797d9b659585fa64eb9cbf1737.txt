<h2>Leasing Consultant</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Hills Properties is seeking a passionate and dedicate Leasing Professional to join our team at our luxury apartment community!</p>
<p>
Established in 1958 by Murray Guttman, Hills Properties is a family owned company headquartered in Cincinnati, Ohio. Today, Hills is celebrating 60 years of excellence and excited to be a growing company within our industry. We recognize we would not be celebrating our achievements if it weren't for our talented and devoted teams!</p>
<p>
This is a full-time, hourly position. Some weekends required.</p>
<p><b>
In this role, you will be excited by the fast paced environment and supported by a small and passionate team. Your duties will include:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Greeting, assisting, and leasing luxury apartments to all prospective residents who enter the community</li>
<li>Ensuring appearance of the model apartment and target apartments are acceptable for showing</li>
<li>Responsibility for processing paperwork for application approval as well as renewal paperwork</li>
<li>Creating all lease documents and facilitates getting all parties to sign where required</li>
<li>Assisting Property Manager in ensuring all property marketing is current, accurate, relevant and at its greatest effect</li>
</ul>
<p><b>You Will Make an Impact by:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Going above an beyond for our residents - striving to uphold HILLS' high standards of customer service</li>
<li>Being dedicated to HILLS Mission and Values - exceeding goals and maintaining a positive relationship with your team, residents, vendors, and community</li>
<li>Being committed to your team - maintaining enthusiasm and not being afraid to go beyond your job title to assist team members</li>
</ul>
<p><b>What You Will Bring to Us:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Minimum of 6 months experience in customer service field, sales or property management</li>
<li>Must have good computer skills and be able to navigate software</li>
<li>Have good verbal and written communication</li>
<li>Be able to provide great customer service to our residents</li>
<li>Possess great attention to detail</li>
</ul>
