<h2>Major Gifts Officer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div><div><div><div>
<div><div><ul>
<li>Develops a comprehensive fundraising plan for assigned School in conjunction with the Director, Academic Development.</li>
<li>Executes strategies for identification, cultivation, solicitation, and stewarding of major donors and key volunteers for assigned School.</li>
<li>Manages an ongoing portfolio of 75-125 prospects and donors.</li>
<li>Conducts prospect research and identification leading to the cultivation and solicitation of individual major gifts.</li>
<li>Develops and maintains a current database of major gift prospects while executing strategies to ensure that newly identified prospects are contacted in a timely manner.</li>
<li>Drafts and customizes correspondence, proposals, and other written materials for assigned prospects.</li>
<li>Communicates progress toward development goals with the School Dean and the Director, Academic Development.</li>
<li>Provides leadership and support for volunteers.</li>
<li>Encourages qualified prospects to consider the benefits of including JSU in their estate plans.</li>
<li>Monitors a timely system of gift acknowledgment, including the mailing of receipts, the advising of appropriate University officials, and reporting to donors for memorials and similar projects.</li>
<li>Inputs pertinent information into the donor database in a timely manner. Travels nationally as well as attends evening and weekend activities.</li>
<li>Serves on committees as assigned.</li>
<li>Performs other duties as assigned.</li>
</ul></div></div>
<br>
<div><div>
<p><b>Required Minimum Qualifications:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s Degree in marketing, public relations, communications or a related field, and</li>
<li>At least three (3) years’ experience in fundraising, may include individual solicitation for campaigns, non-profits, or foundations, or</li>
<li>Experience in high-end sales, marketing, public relations, higher education admissions, or financial planning</li>
<li>Valid driver’s license and reliable transportation required. (Travel reimbursement at state rates.)</li>
</ul>
</div></div>
<br>
<div><div>
<p><b>Preferred Qualifications:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Working knowledge of a donor database or customer relationship management software</li>
</ul>
