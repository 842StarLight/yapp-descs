<h2>Control House Operator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div><div>
<div>
<div>
<b>Job Summary</b>
</div>
<div>
<p>The employee holding this job must perform the work activities in a correct, accurate and timely fashion due to the fact that every single work activity in our line of business is crucial for the whole operation.</p>
</div>
</div>
<div>
<div>
<b>
Job Responsibilities</b>
</div>
<div><ul>
<li>Weighs and records weight of customer vehicles and generates and maintains shipping records</li>
<li>Uses computer hardware and software to obtain customer vehicle weights, record those weights and produce scale tickets for customers.</li>
<li>Operate scales and ensure customer vehicles are loaded according to Company Policy.</li>
<li>Receive telephone requests for price quotations, order changes, adjustments and cancellations directly from assigned and non-assigned customers. Achieve and maintain rapport with customers and work to give them the best possible service.</li>
<li>Responsible for maintaining good customer relations. Generally, deals with representatives and customers concerning matters that require some tact and resourcefulness. Coordinates with other personnel including production and sales departments.</li>
<li>Maintains and coordinates shipping activity.</li>
<li>Provides professional administrative support to the Area Manager, Plant Supervisor and other team members.</li>
<li>Maintains good housekeeping in assigned area.</li>
<li>Replace or assist other workers as needed or directed.</li>
<li>Perform other tasks as assigned by the Supervisor or Leadman.</li>
</ul></div>
</div>
<div>
<div>
<b>Qualifications</b>
</div>
<div>
<ul>
<li>High school preferred</li>
<li>Bilingual a plus</li>
<br>
</ul>
</div>
</div>
<p></p>
<div>
<div>
<b>Knowledge, Skills, and Abilities</b>
</div>
<div><ul>
<li>Ability to carry out simple oral and written instructions and deal with simple problems.</li>
<li>Ability to perform basic computer functions and operate computer software</li>
<li>Basic mathematical skills, including basic arithmetic functions and common units of measurements.</li>
<li>Ability to read English sufficiently to be able to read information contained in manuals, regulations, and reports, including Safety issues and related matters.</li>
<li>Ability to speak English and communicate with others via a two-way radio.</li>
<li>Basic English language skills for completion of various paperwork, such as pre-shift inspection forms.</li>
<li>High level of attentiveness and physical coordination to operate scale control house and fill in for ground personnel.</li>
</ul></div>
</div>
<div>
<div>
<b>Working Conditions</b>
</div>
<div><ul>
<li>Continuous exposure to extreme heat, extreme cold, extreme noise, and working outdoors.</li>
<li>Must wear protective equipment while at the location</li>
</ul></div>
</div>
<div>
<div>
<b>Physical Requirements</b>
</div>
<div><ul>
<li>Requires walking, sitting, lifting, pushing, pulling, and climbing to a significant degree</li>
<li>Heavy Work-Exerting up to 100 pounds of force occasionally, and/or up to 50 pounds or force frequently, and/or up to 20 pounds of force constantly to move objects</li>
<li>While performing the duties of this job, the employee is regularly required to talk and hear, in order to communicate to employees/visitors as well as function safely around heavy rolling equipment</li>
<li>Must be able to perform all job functions which include, but may not be limited to, pushing, turning, and/or pulling of controls</li>
<li>Must pass DOT physical to include back x-ray, hearing test and drug/alcohol screen</li>
</ul></div>
