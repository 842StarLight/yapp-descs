<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Summary
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div><b>Client Finance Manager</b></div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
The Client Finance Manager will partner with Senior Management on all financial and operational matters, as well as oversee and lead during Budget, Forecast and Month End close, track spend against budgets, manage accounts payables and accounts receivables for the Company, coordinate new vendor setup, and lead or assist various other strategic or ad hoc finance related initiatives. As part of the Operations Team, you will be accountable for understanding the Agency’s business in order to ensure that all entries, reports and transactions are coded to the general ledger with accuracy, attention to detail and in a timely manner in accordance with corporate fiscal schedules, vendor due date and management requests, recommend and implement improvements to processes, systems and procedures. The Client Finance Manager is expected to partner with Advantage Accounting Controller and accounting staff to ensure all transactions are accurately recorded. The Finance &amp; Operations Manager is responsible for handling financial information with the utmost confidentiality.</div>

<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div>The Company is one of North America’s leading sales and marketing agencies specializing in outsourced sales, merchandising, category management and marketing services to manufacturers, suppliers and producers of food products and consumer packaged goods. The Company services a variety of trade channels including grocery, mass merchandise, specialty, convenience, drug, dollar, club, hardware, consumer electronics and home centers. We bridge the gap between manufacturers and retailers, providing consumers access to the best products available in the marketplace today.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
<b>
Position Summary:</b><br>
</div>
<div></div>
<div>The Client Finance Manager functions as a leader of shopper marketing fiscal accountability and stewardship across multiple programs, categories and retail channels. The Client Finance Manager serves as the face of the Agency to assigned client financial contact(s) and works across internal teams to ensure excellence in budgeting, financial tracking and reconciliation.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
A successful Client Finance Manager must practice effective written and oral communication skills with clients, customers, agency partners and third-party vendors. Strong attention to detail and a passion for financial rigor are a must.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div><b>
Essential Job Duties and Responsibilities:</b></div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div><b>
Act as cross-functional lead for finance process, working across levels within agency and client teams</b></div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
o Partner with Account Service team to manage budget tracking across 10+ retailers and 200+ programs<br>
o Collaborate with internal company accounting teams for resource tracking, estimates for incremental work, etc.<br>

<div></div>
<br>
<div><b>
Develop strategic recommendations and reports</b></div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
o Work with client team VP to build and implement finance process<br>

o Provide suggestions for budget shifts based on trends</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div><b>
Lead strategic evaluation and analysis of vendor costs/invoices as well as real-time reconciliation</b></div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
o Interface with account service team for comprehensive tracking of all vendor estimates<br>
o Validate all vendor invoices to ensure accuracy in billing, collaborating with clients on revisions, as needed<br>
o Manage reconciliation process, ensuring timely invoicing and developing reports as needed</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div><b>
Manage budget changes and period close processes, work closely with client’s finance and shopper marketing leads</b></div>
