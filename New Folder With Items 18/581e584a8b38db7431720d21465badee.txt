<h2>Marketing Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Join the team shaping the future of healthcare! MedBridge is looking for a highly motivated and self-directed <b>Marketing Specialist</b> with a proven track record of successfully developing and executing against marketing campaigns and initiatives. You will be an integral part of deploying successful marketing campaigns from brand awareness to lead generation and product promotion.</p>
<div>
<p>
The<b> Marketing Specialist </b>is responsible for creating, implementing, tracking, and optimizing our digital and traditional marketing campaigns across various marketing channels for current and emerging markets. You should be well-versed in specialized marketing concepts, principles, and tactics. This position is an individual contributor, reporting to the VP of Marketing.</p>
<p>
In this role, you'll make a big impact by helping produce campaigns that directly impact the lives of healthcare professionals and patients around the world. If you want to partner with healthcare professionals to revolutionize healthcare, improve patient outcomes, and create an unparalleled educational ecosystem, then we want to work with you.</p>
<div><b>
In this role, you will:</b></div>
<ul>
<li><div>
Work cross-departmentally (product management, development, marketing, account management, and sales) to assist in project management of go-to-market strategy and launch plans.</div></li>
<li><div>
Collaborate with the Enterprise Lead Gen Manager to execute setting-based campaigns that will drive lead generation and move future clients through the sales funnel.</div></li>
<li><div>
Assist in developing compelling audience-specific messaging and resources (case studies, testimonials, white papers, press releases, product videos, demos, and how-to guides, for example).</div></li>
<li><div>
Manage marketing campaigns that span multiple channels, including SEO, digital advertising, email, social, sponsorships, affiliates, and event marketing.</div></li>
<li><div>
Ensure accuracy and timeliness of content in marketing communications through rigorous quality control, proofing, and data checking.</div></li>
<li><div>
Conduct competitor and market research to become an expert and establish trends and buyer personas.</div></li>
<li><div>
Assist with analyses of marketing data, including campaign results, conversion rates, and online traffic in order to improve future marketing strategies and campaigns.</div></li>
<li><div>
Develop detailed project plans and workback schedules to monitor and track progress and ensure all projects are delivered on time as well as within scope and budget.</div></li>
<li><div>
Assist in the definition of project scope and objectives, involving all relevant stakeholders and ensuring technical feasibility.</div></li>
<li><div>
Coordinate internal resources and third parties/vendors for the flawless execution of projects.</div></li>
</ul>
<br>
