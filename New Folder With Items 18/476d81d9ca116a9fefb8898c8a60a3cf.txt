<h2>Vice President of Sales</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">Company
<br>EverlyWell provides consumer-initiated at-home laboratory testing with easy-to-read and actionable results. We are at the forefront of personalized health, transforming the $25B lab testing industry. We’re here to empower people to take control of their health by making lab testing easy to access, understand, and afford.
<br><br>
Role Summary
<br>Reporting directly to the CEO, the Vice President of Sales will be responsible for bolstering and executing the Company’s enterprise-level partnership strategy and driving channel development including implementation and account management. Specific verticals of focus include health providers/payers, self-insured employers, and other enterprises with testing needs.
<br><br>
<b>What you’ll do:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Cultivate a pipeline of opportunities and manage progression/deal flow, with keen emphasis on large strategic partners. Must have clear, proven ability to close deal, exceed sales goals, and operationalize partnerships</li>
<li>Collaborate with leaders in product, operations, engineering and marketing to scope partnership implementations, define opportunities, and launch profitable new revenue streams</li>
<li>Lead business development and manage all partnership activities, including all steps in the partnerships &amp; sales lifecycle, from prospecting to launch to account management</li>
<li>Scale sales, implementation, and account management organizations; hire a team and implement a management &amp; incentive structure that will meet or exceed the company’s sales goals</li>
<li>Make well-reasoned decisions and manage the day-to-day sales operations and performance</li>
<li>Must be willing to roll up sleeves and execute in a scrappy environment, while also making leadership decisions and significantly contributing to the company’s overall strategy, growth objectives, and culture</li>
</ul>
<b>Your Education and Experience:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Executive level leadership capabilities with significant management and team development experience</li>
<li>Top 25 MBA with a proven track record of leading sales organizations</li>
<li>Deep healthcare expertise and network</li>
<li>Demonstrable competency in enterprise sales processes, contracting, partnerships and closing deals</li>
<li>Resilient with ability to work in a fast-paced environment with changing priorities</li>
<li>Ability to set sales goals and plans and motivate a team to meet them</li>
<li>Demonstrated interpersonal and verbal communication skills with ability to build team culture</li>
<li>Analytical/data-driven coupled with creative problem solving</li>
<li>Deep understanding of the sales and implementation cycle, matching deals to company capabilities and ensuring profitable partnerships</li>
<li>Ability to represent the company with external constituents as the scientific business lead and member of the executive team</li>
<li>Must be willing to travel 75%.</li>
</ul>
