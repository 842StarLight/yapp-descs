<h2>Advertising Executive</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>Advertising Executives are responsible prospecting and closing new advertising clients which offer a product or service in which CPA Fuel can provide leads or sales for via our affiliate network. We are looking for someone with at least one <i>year</i> <i>experience</i> as an account executive or account manager with an ad network and a long list of advertiser contacts.<br>
<br>
<b>Duties Include:
</b><ul>
<li>Develop relationships with other businesses in our industry <i>by</i> cold calling or contacting leads
</li>
<li>Grow existing client relationships
</li>
<li>Use the internet to research and understand both the competition and current client price trends
</li>
<li>Negotiate prices and contracts with advertisers</li>
<li>Assist in the collection of past-due accounts</li>
<li>Travel to tradeshows and client locations as needed (2-3 times per <i>year</i>)</li>
</ul>
<b>Please send resumes to: </b><b><i>jobs</i></b><b>@cpafuel.com</b> <b>Education/</b><b><i>Experience</i></b><b>/Skills:
</b><ul>
<li>Bachelor's degree and <i>experience</i> in online marketing preferred</li>
<li>Adaptable, self-motivated, passionate, creative team player</li>
<li>
<i>Excellent</i> written and verbal communication skills</li>
<li>Outgoing and willing to take initiatives in finding new business</li>
<li>Strong sales and negotiation skills</li>
</ul>
</div>
