<h2>Tax Accountant</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Job Summary:</b></p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
Prepares and completes tax returns and reports involved with property, sales, and other tax liabilities. Responsible for meeting all required deadlines. Maintains current knowledge of all tax laws affecting the company. Reviews proposals for tax implications and recommends actions that meet the business plans while managing the tax liability. Assists in tax audits. May be involved with tax implications of mergers and acquisitions.</p>
<div>
</div>
<ul>
<li>Gathering data and preparing Sales and Use Tax returns.</li>
<li>Gathering data and preparing Canada GST/HST/PST/QST filings.</li>
<li>Gathering data and determining appropriate use tax accruals.</li>
<li>Maintaining and tracking exemption certificates.</li>
<li>Maintain and update Vertex sales and use software.</li>
<li>Prepare balance sheet reconciliations for responsible accounts.</li>
<li>Prepare data necessary for property tax filings.</li>
<li>Prepare data necessary for unclaimed property tax filings.</li>
<li>Gather data and prepare business license filings.</li>
<li>Gather data and prepare gross receipts type tax filings.</li>
<li>Prepare and assist with other miscellaneous type tax filings and calculations as assigned by supervisor.
</li>
<li>Conduct Tax research and document conclusions under the direction of supervisor.</li>
<li>Other duties and projects that may be assigned by supervisor.</li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<div>
<div>
<b>Experience: </b>This position requires 0-1 years of previous related professional experience</div>
<div>Experience in Oracle A/R or A/P preferred</div>
Experience using Vertex a plus<br>
<b>Education: </b>Bachelor's degree in related field or equivalent experience.<br>
