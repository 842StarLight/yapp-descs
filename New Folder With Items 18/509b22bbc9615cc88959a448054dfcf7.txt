<h2>Senior Accountant</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><i>HIMG seeks a Senior Accountant to work closely with the CEO to support accounting functions within the organization. The Senior Accountant will be required to: </i></p>
<ul>
<li>Lead monthly financial close activities, posting all journal entries for multiple corporate entities;</li>
<li>Preparation of monthly, quarterly, and annual reports and analysis;</li>
<li>Compile information necessary for the annual operating budget and assist Senior Management in its preparation</li>
<li>Assist in preparation of quarterly physician compensation model;</li>
<li>Conduct or coordinate audits of company accounts and financial transactions to ensure compliance with state and federal requirements and statutes, including Generally Accepted Accounting Principles (GAAP);</li>
<li>Analyze financial information to produce forecasts, budgets, reports, etc to assist in business decisions;</li>
<li>Review operational department performance and profitability and give recommendations for improvement to maximize profits;</li>
<li>Responsible for tax filings and related correspondence, including B&amp;O, property, sales and use, and others as necessary;</li>
<li>Assist in Accounts Payable functions including entering invoices, ensuring proper authorization, and performing check runs;</li>
<li>Provide coverage for Payroll Clerk in his / her absence;</li>
<li>Responsible for asset tracking and capitalization software;</li>
<li>Identify complex problems and use tools and information necessary to evaluate options and implement solutions;</li>
<li>Other activities and responsibilities as assigned by the CFO/CEO while maintaining the strictest confidentiality.</li>
</ul>
<p>This position requires a minimum of a bachelor’s degree in Accounting or Finance and three (3) years’ experience. Some management level and healthcare-related experience preferred.</p>
<p>Must demonstrate strong computer skills with advanced knowledge in Microsoft Excel as well as familiarity with other Microsoft Office programs and an electronic accounting software package. Must have the ability to communicate effectively through written and oral communications with staff, management, and physicians.</p>
