<div>Renewal by Andersen is looking to add a Canvasser Lead to our team in Utica</div>

We believe that we can make the world a better place by living up to the promise that everyone benefits from their association with Andersen. <div>
<b>PURPOSE: </b>The purpose of this position is to assist the Canvassing Manager to cost effectively drive non-media lead generation and local awareness through the local canvassing program.</div>
<div></div>
<div><b>RESPONSIBILITIES:
</b></div>
<ul>
<li><div>Assists in the daily management of canvassing team with a hands-on approach, provides motivation and coaching to help team reach daily, weekly and monthly goals.
</div></li>
<li><div>Assists in recruiting, and training of canvass team.
</div></li>
<li><div>Instructs, demonstrates, observes and coaches team to generate leads using approved company methodology.
</div></li>
<li><div>Monitors team members daily to ensure they are following scripts and guidelines.
</div></li>
<li><div>Sets the pace for the canvassing team’s efforts and is the living example of their conduct and behavior.
</div></li>
<li><div>Assists in coordinating canvass logistics with mapping and tracking, permit logistics, script and flyer maintenance, organizing field lead sheets, verifying / validating leads gathered during each shift.
</div></li>
<li><div>Provides timely tracking and entering of marketing data into corporate database.</div></li>
<li><div>Provides information to supervisors, co-workers, and subordinates by telephone, in written form, e-mail, or in person on neighborhoods and cities where Renewal by Andersen’s products and services can be demonstrated with the intent of generating qualified leads and appointments.</div></li>
<li><div>Coordinates w/ Corporate Marketing to assist with local implementation and tracking of other corporate marketing initiatives.</div></li>
<li><div>Performs other related duties, as assigned
</div></li>
<li><div>Executes projects based on business need as assigned</div></li>
</ul>

</div></li>

</div></li>
<li><div>Ability to think strategically, with strong project management skills, including attention to detail
</div></li>
<li><div>Strong verbal and written communication skills
</div></li>
<li><div>Ability to self-manage resources (time and budget) to achieve objectives
</div></li>
<li><div>Ability to analyze effectiveness of canvassing program and canvassing team
</div></li>
<li><div>Ability to understand the role of marketing communications, sales promotion and advertising
</div></li>
<li><div>Ability to demonstrate strong leadership skills
</div></li>
<li><div>Demonstrated analytic skills
</div></li>
<li><div>Demonstrated ability in Microsoft Office programs, specifically Excel</div></li>
</ul>
