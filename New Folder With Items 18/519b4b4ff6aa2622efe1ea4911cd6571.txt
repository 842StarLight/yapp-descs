<h2>Commission Auditor</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div><div><div>
<div>
<div>The Commission Auditor is responsible for the auditing of completed transactions for the Central Processing Center. The Auditor handles both transactions closed in the field and by commission processors across many Local Operating Companies (LOCs). The Auditor will also be expected to support the branch offices. Reviewing both deals and internal forms (CSAs, Voids, Deleted Deal requests) submitted by the OA and working to resolve any issues with their request before submitting.<br>
<br>
Supervision: Supervision will be provided within Central Processing Services.<br>
<br>
Essential Job Duties<br>
<br>
Auditing of closed transactions:<ul>
<li>
Complete through audits from both centralized and de-centralized LOCs.</li>
<li>
Review each of the main audit points and make sure internal systems are matching closing documentation such as the settlement statement, commission checks, purchase agreement, etc.</li>
<li>
Identify issues including missing documentation or and communicate back to the commissions team or branch office location.</li>
<li>
Identify any ongoing issue trends and bring possible solutions to management.</li>
<li>
Meet or exceed expected number of transactions audited daily.</li>
</ul>
Support for the Branch offices and team members:<br>
<ul>
<li>
Provide feedback to management regarding LOC specific issues.</li>
<li>
Provide feedback to the branch office regarding incorrect deal/form submissions and work to resolve.</li>
<li>
Provide prompt response times to all e-mail and phone call inquiries.</li>
<li>
Be able to calculate and explain commission payouts to a branch office staff member.</li>
<li>
Assist team members in auditing of deals by daily deadline as needed.</li>
<li>
Provide outstanding customer service including a positive attitude and constant willingness to assist both branch office staff and team members.</li>
</ul>
<br>
Other Duties:<br>
<ul><li>
Additional duties as required by business need.</li></ul>
<br>
Qualifications:<br>
<ul>
<li>
High School Diploma required. Associates or Bachelor’s degree preferred.</li>
<li>
Ability to learn new software programs on an as needed basis, skillful in using the following programs including, but not limited to: Microsoft office programs such as Outlook, Word, and Excel, and Adobe programs such as Adobe Acrobat.</li>
<li>
Creative problem-solving skills.
</li>
<li>Ability to communicate with different audiences to convey message successfully and to change gear quickly to support different customers.</li>
<li>
Ability to multi-task, prioritize and be flexible with changing business needs in a team environment as well as manage deadlines and time constraints.</li>
<li>
Outstanding organizational skills; emphasis on attention to detail.</li>
<li>
Customer focused, delivery oriented, good team member, values honesty and integrity.</li>
<li>
Solid math and clerical skills (typing, data entry).</li>
<li>
Real Estate/Mortgage industry experience a plus.</li>
</ul>
Position Type and Expected Hours of Work:
<br>
<ul>
<li>There is not expected travel in this position.</li>
<li>
Scheduled hours are based on business need.</li>
</ul>
Work Environment:
<br>
<ul><li>This job operates in a professional office environment. This role routinely uses standard office equipment such as computers, phones, photocopiers, filing cabinets and fax machines.
</li></ul>Physical Demands:
<br>
<ul><li>This is largely a sedentary role; however, the ability, with or without accommodation, to move around the office occasionally for filing is required. This would require the ability to lift files, open filing cabinets and bend or stand as necessary. The employee must occasionally lift office products and supplies weighing up to 20 pounds. Must be able to physically, with or without accommodation, operate a computer, phone and/or copy machine. Must possess the ability, with or without accommodation, to express oneself, communicate with others, listen and exchange information.</li></ul>
