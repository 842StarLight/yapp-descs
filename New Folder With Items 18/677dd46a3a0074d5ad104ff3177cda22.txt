<p></p>
<p>A Merchandiser – Off Premise (OFP) is responsible for ensuring assigned customer accounts are consumer ready; this entails stocking the store shelves and cold boxes/coolers with fresh products, building displays to draw attention to our products, and providing exceptional service to our retail clients. This position is part of the Off Premise Merchandising department or Off Premise Sales team.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>WORK TASKS
</b></p>
<p>A Merchandiser OFP can be expected to perform the following tasks in this role as a routine part of their job:
</p>
<p></p>
<p>Maintains fresh products by stocking and rotating, organizing, and refilling back stocks, cold boxes/coolers, shelves, beer caves and displays adhering to all quality and rotation policies.
</p>
<ul>
<li>Ensures service levels and compliance rates for each assigned account are met or exceeded.
</li>
<li>Works with Coaches/Leadership to record and track OOS in each assigned account.
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p>Builds displays and executes placement of signage and other merchandising items.
</p>
<p></p>
<p>Ensures all Andrews SKUs are priced appropriately and meet standards of visibility.
</p>
<p></p>
<p>Keeps backroom and inventory areas clean and organized.Notifies manager of any product breakage in an account.
</p>
<p></p>
<p>Completes and documents retail call with signature and provides feedback to Account Manager, District Sales Manager (DSM) and/or Key Account Manager (KAM) to ensure appropriate inventory levels and exceptional customer service in all assigned accounts.
</p>
<p></p>
<p>Walks the account for merchandising opportunities and pairing with Andrews’ portfolio.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><i>This list is not all inclusive of other tasks and subtasks that may be necessary in performing this role, but rather an overview of the role’s primary work tasks.
</i></p>
