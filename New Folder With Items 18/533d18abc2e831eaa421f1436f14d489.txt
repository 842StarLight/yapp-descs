<h2>Account Executive - Sales Hunter</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<b>Description:</b><br>
We are looking for a driven Inside Sales Account Executive to join our team – could that be you?
<br><br>
Our Account Executives are responsible for increasing the Bestpass customer base through the acquisition of new business to new and existing accounts. This is a full-time, inside telephone sales role with a base salary plus commission.
<br><br>
What does it take to be successful?
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Identify revenue opportunities through outbound cold calls, emails and relationship building</li>
<li>Own the entire sales process from prospecting to completion of the sale</li>
<li>Research and contact prospects to identify key contacts, qualify and drive leads through the sales pipeline</li>
<li>Utilize a consultative sales approach to explain the value of our service</li>
<li>Be knowledgeable on Bestpass services as well as customer needs and requirements.</li>
</ul>
<b>Requirements:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>2+ years’ experience of inside sales B2B experience</li>
<li>Proven track record of successful phone sales</li>
<li>Excellent verbal communication skills; strong listening and presentation skills</li>
<li>Ability to multi-task, prioritize and manage time effectively</li>
<li>Persistence</li>
<li>Ability to work independently as well as in team environment</li>
<li>Ability to travel +/- 10%</li>
<li>Associate’s degree; Bachelor’s preferred</li>
</ul>
