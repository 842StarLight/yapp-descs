<h2>Associate Civil Engineer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Overview:</b></h3>
<p>
The Associate Civil Engineer is responsible for reviewing photovoltaic solar system designs for structural integrity and code-compliance, and providing structural analysis for a high volume of residential solar projects. Additionally, the Associate Civil Engineer will design and draft repairs/structural upgrades as needed. This position requires excellent communication skills, both verbal and written, to work across many departments from sales to installation.
</p>
<p>This role can be based out of either our Denver, CO office or our Irvine, CA office.
</p>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities</b></h3>
<ul>
<li>
Review and analyze structural integrity of residential rooftop solar designs.</li>
<li>
Required to consistently handle high production volumes efficiently while maintaining quality.</li>
<li>
Research engineering requirements for potential new markets.</li>
<li>
Provide Structural Engineering support for Site Technicians, Designers, Permit Coordinators, and Installers.</li>
<li>
Provide onsite field inspections and Structural Observations Reports when requested.</li>
<li>
Works with PE Team in taking direction and executing on special projects as needed (Engineering SOP, New Product Research, etc.).</li>
<li>
Additional responsibilities as needed.</li>
</ul>
