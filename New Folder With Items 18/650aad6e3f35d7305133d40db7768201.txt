<p><b><i>The Big Task</i></b></p>
<p>As HVAC Install Manager you will build and lead an ideal install department with the best in the business working with you.</p>
<p><b><i>Key Sub Tasks
</i></b></p>
<ul>
<li>Coordinate, manage, and inspire an HVAC install team (installers &amp; office coordinators) while promoting safety, efficiency, and high-quality workmanship.</li>
<li>Oversee multiple projects at once from sale to final inspection in an organized manner.
</li>
<li>Evaluate and determine materials and staff needed for each project, controlling operational expenses.</li>
<li>Remain up to date on codes, new products, and proper installation methods.</li>
<li>Provide administrative, technical direction and supervision to in-office staff in completing work assignments.</li>
<li>Help develop annual budgets, control expenses and meet or exceed plan.</li>
<li>Ensure sufficient staff levels to meet ongoing customer demand and seasonal spikes.</li>
<li>Train, develop and hold your team accountable to exceptional customer satisfaction and performance standards.</li>
<li>Bring more energy than you take, because you will set the tone every day for the entire department.</li>
</ul>
<p><b><i>Desired Skills and Experience</i></b></p>
<ul>
<li>Minimum 5-10 years industry experience</li>
<li>Project management experience</li>
<li>Proficiency to make independent decisions to manage and resolve obstacles</li>
<li>Ability to work independently and complete projects in a timely manner</li>
<li>Possess the ability to inspire both personal and professional growth in our team members</li>
<li>Ability to drive team performance to achieve all business goals and objectives and promote unparalleled customer satisfaction</li>
<li>Be detail oriented and a highly organized multi-tasker with good presentation skills and the ability to convey confident and decisive messages in a fast-paced and goal-driven environment.</li>
<li>Be proficient using a computer and Microsoft Office applications (Excel, Word &amp; PowerPoint).</li>
<li>Exceptional follow-through capabilities</li>
<li>High sense of urgency to complete the job correctly and on time</li>
</ul>
