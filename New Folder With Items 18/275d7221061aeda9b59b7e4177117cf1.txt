<p></p>
<p>Analog Devices, Inc. is seeking an Optical Sensors Applications Engineer that will help accelerate the optical sensor strategy of the Molecular Sensing Group. ADI is a leader in the exciting emerging area of integrated optical sensors. These are internally designed, specialized optical sensors combined with state of the art sensor signal conditioning electronics to produce highly differentiated light measurements. The optical sensors group is targeting fast growing applications in Medical, Automotive, Industrial, and Consumer markets including vital signs monitoring, gas and aerosol sensing, touch-less controls, object detection and tracking, and many more. We need a skilled and enthusiastic engineer to join this emerging team and help us build this technology and product portfolio.</p>
<p>As part of a cross functional team, the optical sensors applications engineer provides a critical link between the external customer base and the internal product design and development teams. Additionally, the applications engineer will play a pivotal role in ensuring customer satisfaction through technical engagement, critical problem solving, and rapid response to support requests.<br>
</p>
