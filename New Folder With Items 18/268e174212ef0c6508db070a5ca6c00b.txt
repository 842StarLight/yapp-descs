<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Job Brief</b></h2>

<p>The Enterprise Account Executive is responsible for sales to enterprise level commercial customers, including large business customers of both internet &amp; telephone services. This includes proactively identifying new customer sales opportunities, defining customer needs, preparing proposals, and closing deals.</p>
<p><b>
Responsibilities include:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Conduct proactive sales activities, including cold-calling and knocking on doors, proactive needs assessment, applications development, proposal presentation, order negotiation and post-sales service requirements</li>
<li>Negotiate to secure contracts with telecommunications decision makers in order to achieve revenue growth and retention.</li>
<li>Manage installation projects with various teams to ensure on-time delivery, successful turn-up and customer satisfaction</li>
<li>Respond to requests from customers for information, support, assistance, joint proposals, pricing, etc.</li>
<li>Respond to demand sales requests</li>
<li>Supports others within the sales/service team to achieve customer satisfaction</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Required Qualifications:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Demonstrated success in telecommunications and internet sales to end-user customers, including strategic and large business customers and government accounts using relationship management and system sales concepts</li>
<li>Ability to sell to C level executives within an organization</li>
<li>Experience in systems selling, consultative sales techniques, customer needs analysis, sales opportunity development, and service improvement planning.</li>
<li>Technical skills related to network and transmission design and local access services</li>
<li>Product knowledge of both switched and dedicated services, as well as associated end-user and carrier applications</li>
<li>Operational understanding of telecommunications ordering, provisioning, and billing processes</li>
<li>Working knowledge of general marketing principle tools and processes</li>
<li>Skills necessary for decision making and maintaining customer retention</li>
<li>Strong interpersonal skills</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Preferred Qualifications:</b></p>
<ul>

<li>Exceptional presentation, negotiation and closing skills</li>

</ul>
