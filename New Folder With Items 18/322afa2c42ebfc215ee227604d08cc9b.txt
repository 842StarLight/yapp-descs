<div>
<b>Mosaic</b> Sales Solutions is an event marketing agency that delivers brand experiences that bring our clients' products directly to the people who buy them. Advertising Age recently included us in their <i>Best Places to Work in Marketing and Media</i> list, and we are about to expand our team!</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
“Store of the Future” is a first of its kind, experiential store that leverages unique innovative merchandising and selling technologies to enhance the consumer’s retail experience. The Store of the Future will support multiple exciting brands/retailers, initiate new personalized and discovery-oriented shopping experiences (such as Magic Mirror try on), and test cutting edge store automation technology (e.g., checkout/mobile payment solutions) to revolutionize the way retailers think about the shopping experience.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
We are currently seeking a Sales Associate to work at the Mall of America location.<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<div>The successful candidate will be responsible for:</div>
<ul><li> Greeting customers with a friendly and helpful attitude by providing a welcome script set forth by Store of the Future and answering consumer questions.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Maintaining a clean and organized sales floor.
</li></ul>
<ul><li> Maintaining product knowledge and keeping up to date with assortments in-store and on-line.
</li></ul>
<ul><li> Managing the fitting rooms</li></ul>
<ul><li> Answering customers questions, replenishing stock and troubleshooting basic technology issues in the event of malfunction or failure</li></ul>
<ul><li> Assisting with checking out candidates at stationary and/or mobile POS</li></ul>
<ul><li> Supporting customers in how to use key technologies in the store</li></ul>
</div>
<p></p>
<div>
<b>
Qualifications</b><div>
</div>
<ul><li> 1 -3 years of previous retail experience, preferably in one that utilized technology to enhance an exciting consumer environment.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Ability to communicate effectively with customers and the Store of the Future team
</li></ul>
<ul><li> Must have a professional, customer service focused and friendly attitude.
</li></ul>
<ul><li> Strong interpersonal and communication skills</li></ul>

<ul><li> Comfort with and willingness to learn about brands and technology</li></ul>
<ul><li> Demonstrated dedication and effort to understanding the brands and core tenets of those brands</li></ul>

<ul><li> Comfortability/interest in learning about new technologies and willingness to support customers on how to use them</li></ul>
<ul><li> Comfortable with change and have an interest in ongoing learning/training</li></ul>
<ul><li> Ability to work a flexible schedule to meet the needs of the business, including days, evenings, weekends and holidays.</li></ul>
