<h2>Purchasing Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>PRIMARY OUTCOMES FOR THE POSITION</p>
<p>
As Purchasing Manager, you will be directly responsible for planning the purchasing, tracking, product mix, volume levels and merchandising of inventory. Manage the vendor accountability and the purchasing staff. Establish and document formal programs with vendors in each product category with a concentration in commodities. Provide analysis and lead systemization, implementation and use of inventory management systems, automating the replenishment system wherever possible. Help develop strategies and tactics for improving operation efficiencies, improving service levels and maintaining inventory levels and operation costs. Work with the Marketing Department on external advertising and promotions of select product categories. The Purchasing Manager reports to the Company President.</p>
</div>

<p><b>KEY RESPONSIBILITIES</b></p>
<p><b>
Management Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Assume responsibility to lead, direct, train, mentor and manage Purchasing Team</li>
<li>Coordinate communication with Accounting, Sales, Location Management and all other impacted departments</li>
<li>Establish a regular physical presence in all locations, working with branch management to maximize profits through product selection, promotion and merchandising</li>
<li>Ensure compliance with established procedures and maintain effective internal control over the physical product flow and accounting of inventory, receiving, shipping, and other inventory areas</li>
<li>Research and monitor competitive pricing, communicate market pricing, devise pricing strategies which optimize company profitability</li>
<li>Collaborate with Marketing and Sales on promotion of products</li>
<li>Follow all applicable OSHA and MCHC safety regulations relevant to position</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Supplier Engagement</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Involved in vendor reviews, selection and negotiation process</li>
<li>Track and ensure compliance to quantity initiatives and rebate programs with all suppliers</li>
<li>Build relationships with key suppliers through attendance of trade events, including Massachusetts Retail Lumber Dealers Association (MRLDA) events, International Builders Show (IBS), Orgill, and other vendor engagement opportunities.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Inventory Management</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Automate the replenishment system wherever possible</li>
<li>Responsible for product reviews, including which items to obsolete and new product programs to introduce. Will have responsibility related to the implementation of such programs and related committees</li>
<li>Ensure adequate inventory of product in accord with inventory cycles and forecasts</li>
<li>Works with warehousing management to establish the plan for the storage of all inventory product using frequency specifications and replacement calculations to determine fill rate</li>
<li>Oversee the planning, scheduling and general management of all inbound product</li>
<li>Develop and implement inventory control procedures</li>
<li>Devise and implement methods to sell or dispose of dead and slow-moving inventory</li>
</ul>
<p>
Analyze inventory loss, determine causes, develop and implement counteractive measures</p>
