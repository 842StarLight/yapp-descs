<h2>Contract Administrator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>POSITION SUMMARY</b></p>
<p>W.W. Gay Fire Protection, a Division of Sciens Building Solutions, is searching for an experienced Contract Administrator to review, edit, negotiate, finalize and execute contracts for the Sciens organization in Florida. The ideal candidate must a have solid background working with both standard commercial agreements and standard government agreements and a strong understanding of the contract process and contract structure. This person needs to be able to review and revise standard form agreements, which requires the ability to write and communicate clearly and precisely. They should also be a team player and handle administrative aspects of contracts management.</p>
<p><b>DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Conducting initial review of all incoming contract requests and correspondence, to identify any terms that vary from corporate standards to ensure accuracy and compliance and to ensure contracts fully capture scope of work, price, and other attribute.</li>
<li>Proposing modifications to contracts.</li>
<li>Assist in developing/improving contracts-related processes and procedures to scale with company’s growth.</li>
<li>Acting as a liaison between internal and external customers to efficiently drive the contract review and approval process.</li>
<li>Engaging and collaborating with internal stakeholders in the sales, project management, accounting, administrative, and other internal departments as required.</li>
<li>Filling out business or government forms.</li>
<li>Preparation and submitting contract documents, including certificate of insurance, bonds, safety documents, etc.</li>
<li>Creating status report regarding contract process on projects.</li>
<li>Coordinating signatures on all contracts.</li>
<li>Ensuring customer contracts and information are fully compliant with company policies and procedures and conducting basic due diligence on customer's business information.</li>
<li>Supporting resolution of contract disputes by researching miscellaneous legal issues.</li>
<li>Complying with all company policies and procedures; abides by company quality policy and core values.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>REQUIREMENTS</b></p>
<ul>
<li>3-5 years prior experience in a contract administration role or a related field.</li>
<li>Contract administration/management certification preferred (i.e., CCCM, CCFM, CPCM)</li>
<li>Possesses superior attention to details.</li>
<li>Experience in customer services a plus</li>
<li>Strong communication skills.</li>
<li>Proactive and willing to take initiative.</li>
<li>Strong time-management skills.</li>
<li>Knowledge of investigation techniques.</li>
<li>Well-developed negotiating skills.</li>
<li>Strong MS Office skills – MS Word, Excel, Outlook.</li>
<li>Experience using DocuSign, EchoSign, Adobe Sign, or other e-sign software.</li>
<li>Highly motivated, positive, professional, friendly and team-oriented attitude.</li>
<li>Willing to work without administrative support.</li>
<li>Enjoys working with contracts.</li>
<li>Some travel required.</li>
</ul>
<p>Job Type: Full-time</p>
</div>
