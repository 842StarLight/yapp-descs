<h2>Delivery Lead</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">Our Story
<br><br>
We started MedMen with a simple vision; cannabis as a consumer product. It is a simple idea with profound consequences for how marijuana is cultivated, produced and marketed. Quality standards matter, best practices matter, brand reputation matters. Today, MedMen is the most dominant cannabis enterprise in the emerging legal marijuana industry.
<br><br>
Job Summary
<br><br>
The Delivery Lead reports to the Co-Manager of Delivery or Co-Manager of Operations and is responsible for day to day oversight of the logistics and process of delivery.
<br><br>
The Delivery Lead is charged with maximizing routes through Onfleet, with the ultimate goal of efficiency, accuracy and timeliness of deliveries and proactively solves delivery-related problems. Also, the Delivery Lead oversees delivery payment and inventory management.
<br><br>
Essential Functions
<br><br>
Reasonable accommodations may be made to enable individuals with disabilities to perform the essential functions.
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Manage Onfleet navigation system to assign deliveries to drivers and optimize routes</li>
<li>Select a collection of delivery orders (tasks) in the Onfleet dashboard and assign it to a driver</li>
<li>Follow maximum retail delivery amount per driver</li>
<li>Ensure all orders are within daily compliance limits</li>
<li>Create a route as soon as delivery orders are ready and there is an available car and driver</li>
<li>Notify drivers when an order is assigned</li>
<li>Communicate delivery issues to customers within Onfleet</li>
<li>Ensure delivery address is within regulations and to a physical address</li>
<li>Work with operation associates to increase efficiency, accuracy and timeliness of orders to be prepared and packaged for MedMen delivery customers</li>
<li>Check all cannabis orders against the pick receipt with the driver before placing in the storage box for the delivery run</li>
<li>Complete Delivery Cash Reconciliation form before and after each delivery run and report any discrepancies</li>
<li>Ensure drivers complete Delivery Driver Checklist before going on any delivery run</li>
<li>Complete till bag for drivers</li>
<li>Ensure scheduled drivers have the Onfleet app toggled to "On Duty" at all times during their scheduled shift</li>
<li>Reconcile returned product after each delivery back into inventory</li>
<li>Manage all cash and pick receipts</li>
<li>Report any deviation from Onfleet navigation except for BCC approved fuel or vehicle repair stops</li>
<li>Maintain Delivery documents at the store</li>
</ul>
