<h2>Development Engineer I</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>
<p>Development Engineer I</p>
</div>
<div>Essential Duties and Responsibilities include the following. Other duties may be assigned.</div>
<ul><li>Develops solutions to routine technical problems of limited scope.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Under supervision, gives limited advice and analyzes information within a specific function for others within the organization.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Under the close guidance of experienced engineers, evaluates, analyzes, and makes recommendations on products, designs, and cost improvements to improve the competitive position of the company.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Procures materials needed to construct prototypes and participates in the design engineering of new models.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Supports the planning and development of test programs for prototypes.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Analyzes test data and reports to determine if designs meet functional and performance specifications.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Assists in analyzing, evaluating, sourcing, and coordination of the procurement of new materials to support model changes.</li></ul>
<ul><li>With direction, analyzes, evaluates, and recommends cost improvement ideas through product, vendor, and design changes.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Performs technical analysis of competitive products.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Provides support to sales and marketing on product presentations and customer literature.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Uses computer-assisted engineering and design software and equipment to perform engineering and design tasks.</li></ul>
<div></div>
<div>Qualifications: To perform this job successfully, an individual must be able to perform each essential duty satisfactorily. The requirements listed below are representative of the knowledge, skill, and/or ability required. Reasonable accommodations may be made to enable individuals with disabilities to perform the essential functions.</div>
<ul><li>Bachelor's degree (Technical, Scientific, Engineering)</li></ul>
<ul><li>0-2 years of related experience or an equivalent combination of education and experience.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Requires detailed knowledge of engineering, preferably in the HVAC industry.</li></ul>
<ul><li>PC skills including Microsoft office applications. Basic skills with CAD applications, a plus.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Strong organizational, interpersonal and communications skills.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Ability to read and interpret documents such as technical literature and safety standards, operating and maintenance instructions, and engineering drawings.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Ability to write routine reports and correspondence.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Ability to speak effectively before cross functional groups of employees within the organization.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Knowledge of Word Processing software; Spreadsheet software; Design software and Internet software, and the ability to learn and apply Development software specific to the company.</li></ul>
