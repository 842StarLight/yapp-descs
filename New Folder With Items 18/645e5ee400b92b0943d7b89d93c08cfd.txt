<h2>Research Associate</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">Digimarc is the inventor of Digimarc Discover® software and Digimarc Barcode for the automatic identification of virtually any object. <br><br>
We are a global, values-based organization empowering talented engineers and scientists, along with a wide-range of sales, marketing and operations professionals to manage a publicly-traded company consistently ranked as one of Oregon’s best places to work. Will you join us? We are Digimarc: The Barcode of Everything™.

Digimarc is seeking an Associate Research Engineer with strong skills in experimental design, data analysis, and technical insight to answer challenging problems at the heart of Digimarc’s technology. As part of a small, multi-disciplinary research team working in an Agile environment, this individual will need to balance strong quality assurance capabilities with research and data analysis experience.
<br><br>
<b>QUALIFICATIONS &amp; EXPERIENCE:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>BS or MSc in Mathematics, Physics, Computer Science, Electrical Engineering, or equivalent experience</li>
<li>Experience in Research, Data Analyst, or Quality Assurance role</li>
<li>Knowledge of signal processing, image processing or color science is preferred</li>
<li>MATLAB skills (or equivalent prototyping language)</li>
<li>Development experience in C/C++ programming and source code control</li>
<li>Possesses strong technical curiosity and diverse technical interests/skills</li>
<li>Effectively communicate technical information, verbal and written</li>
<li>Familiar with statistical data analysis</li>
<li>Experience in test design, data analysis and presentation</li>
<li>Is self-motivated, collaborative, and able to prioritize tasks</li>
<li>Alignment with the core Digimarc values of Innovation, Caring, Loyalty, Integrity, Commitment, and Knowledge in a fun, fast-paced, multi-disciplined team</li>
</ul>
