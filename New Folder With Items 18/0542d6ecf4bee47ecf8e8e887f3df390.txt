<h2>Cost Accountant</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<div>
<p><b>Summary:
</b></p>
<p>Applies principles of cost accounting to conduct analyses, which provide detailed cost information to the Controller and Upper Management.</p>
<p><b>
Essential Duties and Responsibilities</b> include the following. Other duties may be assigned.</p>
<ul>
<li>
Prepare, analyze, and report weekly, monthly, and quarterly gross margin analysis by customer type. Assist in monthend closing, prepare journal entries as necessary, analyze inventory evaluation, inventory roll forward.
</li>
<li>Plan, study, and collect data to determine cost of business activity such as raw material, purchases, inventory, and labor.
</li>
<li>Coordinate with various departments within the company including IT, accounts payable, accounts receivable, receiving, shipping, production, purchasing, and other.
</li>
<li>Analyze cost accounting data and assist with cycle count/physical inventories.
</li>
<li>Prepare detailed account analysis and reconcile sales, cost and inventory, and liability accounts by customer type and by division. Review and analyze inventory and margin reports, conduct research and perform analytical studies in regards to cost analyses and profitability.
</li>
<li>Analyzes manufacturing costs and prepares periodic reports comparing standard costs to actual production costs; provide various reports to management.</li>
<li>
Make routine entries in the accounting system; maintain Cost Accounting System; maintain internal control documentation and test internal controls</li>
<li>
Support and assist internal department reporting requirements such as audit schedules, etc.; Work on special projects as required.
</li>
<li>Ability to work overtime
</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Supervisory Responsibilities
</b></p>
<p>This job has no supervisory responsibilities.</p>
