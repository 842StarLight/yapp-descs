<h2>Network Copier Installer/Trainer and Delivery Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>The Delivery Specialist / Network Copier Installer/Trainer is responsible for delivering, installing, connecting and training clients on network copiers. You are responsible for providing quality services and solutions to our clients while maintaining a high level of client satisfaction.</p>
<p><b> Main Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul> <li>Review daily delivery schedule, confirm scheduling calls have been made in advance to client</li>
 <li>Deliver equipment to client ensuring all established quality assurance standards are meet</li>
 <li>Assist with other product set-ups as necessary</li>
 <li>Pick up appropriate copier equipment and supplies to return ensuring all paperwork is completely filled out and turned in to the proper personnel</li>
 <li>Obtain client signatures on required paperwork and turn into designated representatives in a timely manner</li>
 <li>Train and remain up-to-date on install requirements as copier makes and models change</li>
 <li>Conduct weekly maintenance and cleaning of area</li>
 <li>Frequent travel to client sites to perform network installation and training sessions ensuring compliance with set guidelines and procedures</li>
 <li>Complete the connectivity of equipment by installing printer software on to workstations/servers and install/configure scanning software per the Statement of Work.</li>
 <li>Provide customize training to the end users of the new product</li>
 <li>Troubleshoot any issues that arise during network installation and training utilizing all resources available to ensure complete customer satisfaction</li>
 <li>Test all functions and applications prior to leaving client to ensure newly installed equipment operates to clients expectation</li>
 <li>Maintain scheduled appointments to insure efficiency and profitability for Marco and our clients through timely arrivals or notifying schedule and client of scheduling changes/conflicts</li>
 <li>Complete proper paperwork with customer and save into SharePoint Database.</li>
<li>Communicate to sales representative’s post-installation information.</li>
<li>Organize information to create effective training &amp; installation tools.</li>
<li>Research and keep up-to-date with new and existing products supported by the DS Installation Team.</li>
<li>Assist sales representatives with pre-sales questions and assist on demonstrations for potential clients when requested.</li>
<li>Rebuild, reinstall OS, and update various print controllers.</li>
<li>Assist internal customers with copier questions when requested.</li>
<li>Keep DDS Install Calendar updated with away from office appointments.</li>
<li>Perform back-up helpdesk duties when requested.</li>
<li>Maintain 70% billable percentage</li>
<li>Accurately maintain documentation and comply with service administrative procedures in a timely basis to include, collecting client signatures and time entry process</li>
<li>Perform all work in a safe manner including but not limited to loading/unloading delivery vehicles, lifting equipment, etc to protect yourself and your coworkers. Use good judgment and give constant attention to the work being done</li>
</ul> <p><b>Required Qualifications:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Associate’s degree (AA) or equivalent from two-year college or technical school and three or more years related experience with a good basic understanding of networks</li>
<li>Strong understanding of TCP/IP, DNS, SMTP, and other network protocols.</li>
<li>Proficiency with business collaboration tools such as MS Office applications, Outlook and company and client specific programs</li>
<li>Superior communication skills in working with technical and non-technical people and the ability to develop and maintain collaborative relations among all levels of an organization</li>
<li>Balance team and individual responsibilities; Exhibit objectivity and openness to others' views; Give and welcome feedback; Contribute to building a positive team spirit; Put success of team above own interests; Able to build morale and group commitments to goals and objectives; Support everyone's efforts to succeed</li>
<li>Cost Conscious - Conserve organizational resources</li>
<li>Treat people with respect; Work with integrity and ethically; Uphold organizational values</li>
<li>Follow policies and procedures; Complete administrative tasks correctly and on time; Support organization's goals and values</li>
<li>Demonstrate accuracy and thoroughness; Look for ways to improve and promote quality; Apply feedback to improve performance; Monitor own work to ensure quality</li>
<li>Meet productivity standards; Complete work in timely manner; Strive to increase productivity; Work quickly</li>
<li>Observe safety and security procedures; Report potentially unsafe conditions; Use equipment and materials properly</li>
<li>Adapt to changes in the work environment; Change approach or method to best fit the situation; Able to deal with frequent change, delays, or unexpected events</li>
</ul> <p><b>Preferred Qualifications:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Experience with networking and printing with Macintosh computers</li>
<li>Previous training experience</li>
</ul>
