<h2>Project Accountant</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>Are you an exceptional accountant known for not missing a detail? Do you thrive on conducting analysis using data and proven methods? As a Project Accountant, you will be contributing to the financial success of a company and reporting directly to the Controller.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b><i>DUTIES, TASKS, AND RESPONSIBILITIES</i></b></p>
<ul>
<li>Conduct ongoing analysis of process constraints – target costing projects, margin analysis and tracing costs back to underlying activities.</li>
<li>Monitor cost effective data accumulation systems to provide costing information to management.</li>
<li>Create and review controls for data compilation and reporting.</li>
<li>Report on margins by product, job, and division.</li>
<li>Report on variances and their causes.</li>
<li>Reconcile sales, cost and inventory accounts.</li>
<li>Work on special projects as required.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Position-specific Competencies</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Strong critical thinking skills</li>
<li>Adept at reviewing detailed information and able to meet deadlines</li>
<li>Extremely organized</li>
<li>Careful attention to detail</li>
<li>Maintain confidentiality while handling files and records</li>
<li>Ability to travel limitedly to Job sites</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b><i>MINIMUM QUALIFICATIONS</i></b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s Degree in Accounting</li>
<li>Proficient with Microsoft Office applications</li>
<li>Effective written and verbal communication</li>
<li>
<i>Preferred</i>: Three years accounting experience in Manufacturing or Construction – with an emphasis on costing activities</li>
<li>
<i>Preferred:</i> experience with Sage 300RE Timberline software</li>
<br>
</ul>

</ul>
