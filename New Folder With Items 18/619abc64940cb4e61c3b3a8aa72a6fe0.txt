<h2>Jr. Marketing Analyst</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>General Summary of Position: </b></p>
<p>The Jr. Analyst, Circulation &amp; Planning (Jr. Marketing Analyst) is responsible for direct mail marketing analysis, demand planning, catalog circulation project coordination and budget record-keeping. A successful candidate will possess superior attention to detail, be driven to accomplish tasks timely, and multi-task in a fast-paced environment.</p>
<p><b>Requirements: </b></p>
<ul>
<li><b>Required Education &amp; Experience</b></li>
<li>Bachelors Degree</li>
<li>Marketing or related field preferred</li>
<li>2+ years of previous work experience producing reports, analyzing data and coordinating projects</li>
<li><b>Specific Skill Requirements</b></li>
<li>Excel and pivot table skills</li>
<li>Demonstrated ability to work independently, multi-task, and meet deadlines</li>
<li>Intermediate math skills</li>
<li>Ability to coordinate with vendors and team members alike</li>
<li>Excellent oral and written communication skills as well as ability to communicate effectively across multiple levels of the organization</li>
<li>Catalog circulation experience preferred</li>
<li>Retail merchandising industry experience a plus</li>
<li>Budget record-keeping experience a plus</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Essential Job Functions: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Create, develop, maintain and analyze new or existing sales analysis reports</li>
<li>Coordinate with vendors and the marketing team for catalog mailing projects</li>
<li>Help coordinate team schedule to ensure that deadlines are met and tasks are moving forward</li>
<li>Update, report on and reconcile Advertising costs</li>
<li>Write and maintain process related documents for direct marketing</li>
<li>Monitor activities for process improvements</li>
<li>Special projects assigned by the Director of Marketing</li>
</ul>

</ul>
