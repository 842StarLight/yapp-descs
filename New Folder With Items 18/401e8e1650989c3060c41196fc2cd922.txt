<h2>Insurance Submission (Marketing) Specialist – Private Client Team</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div></div>
<div>
<div><div>
<p>Brown &amp; Brown of Southwest Florida’s Naples office is growing and seeking an exceptional individual with proven discipline, drive, and commitment to join our team as <b>Marketing Associate for our Private Client Division. </b>The hired individual will assist in the prospecting of new accounts as well as the retention and renewal of existing accounts, by supporting the sales team as requested, and soliciting rounding opportunities. The Marketing Associate will also support the team in developing risk potential, analyzing customer insurance needs, and providing high quality of service to assigned accounts, with a focus on excellent customer service and the profitability of the business to the agency.</p>
<p><b>
Essential Duties &amp; Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Assist in the review of company issued new and renewal policies, endorsements and audits for accuracy rating, typing, endorsements forms and coverages</li>
<li>Develop a full working knowledge of AMS 360, Image Right, and other company software</li>
<li>Responsible for the marketing of assigned accounts, obtaining and evaluating quotations from our preferred carriers, and determining and recommending markets.</li>
<li>Maintain knowledge of carrier underwriting criteria, deviations, plans, and marketing policy.</li>
<li>Learn and become proficient with various carriers online rating, and appetite guidelines.</li>
<li>Prepare complete and accurate submissions.</li>
<li>Prepare and/or modify quotations when required.</li>
<li>Prepare policy orders to the successful carrier, binding and invoicing as appropriate.</li>
<li>Where requested, check carrier issued policies for accuracy. Inform carrier and client of forthcoming corrections.</li>
<li>Maintain a suspense system to follow up on outstanding orders, correspondence, reports and follow up on overdue and suspense items.</li>
<li>Review client exposures and be able to advise on coverage options; be a subject matter expert</li>
<li>Be familiar with and follow agency E&amp;O guidelines</li>
<li>Perform all other duties as assigned</li>
</ul>
</div></div>
<div>
