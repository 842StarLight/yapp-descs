<h2>General Manager - Park East</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Aspen Heights strives to provide the best experience in student living across the country. We want to hire and develop the best talent to help deliver a truly above &amp; beyond customer experience and we’ll stop at nothing in our quest to lead the industry.<br>
<br>
<b>Job Description:</b><br>
The General Manager (GM) is the CEO of the property. The GM is responsible for creating the best end-to-end living experience in the industry through “World Class Customer Service”.<br>
<br>
<b>Responsibilities include:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Team Management – Responsible for the hiring, training, coaching, motivating and management of on-site team.</li>
<li>
Marketing - Collaborate with marketing to develop and oversee the marketing plan for the property.</li>
<li>
Finance – Responsibilities include, but not limited to, budget, operating expenses and payroll.</li>
<li>
Relationship Management – Develop professional, service-based relationships with our clients, residents and vendors.</li>
</ul>
<p><br>
<b>
Key Characteristics</b><b> for a successful General Manager:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Collaborative – Ability to work closely with team members to achieve common goals.</li>
<li>
Coaching and Mentoring – Ability to effectively coach and mentor teams.</li>
<li>
Resilient- Able to be flexible and bend with changing circumstances.</li>
<li>
Self Starter - Ability to execute and deliver results with minimal supervision.</li>
</ul>
<div><p><br>
<b>
Company Values:</b></p></div>
<ul>
<li>
Connection - Committed to collaboration, play, healthy relationships.</li>
<li>
Creativity - Committed to continuous learning, imagination, curiosity.</li>
<li>
Performance - Committed to excellence, accountability, and responsibility.</li>
<li>
Leadership - Committed to self-awareness, integrity, and kindness.</li>
</ul>
<p><br>
<b>
Required/Preferred Qualifications:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Bachelor Degree preferred.</li>
<li>
3+ years of management experience.</li>
<li>
Preferred industry experience in sales, customer service, restaurant or hospitality.</li>
<li>
Experience in reporting financials and managing professional budgets.</li>
<li>
Proficient in Microsoft Office applications and technical software.</li>
</ul>
