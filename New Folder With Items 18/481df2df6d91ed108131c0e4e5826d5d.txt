YOUR ROLE
<br><br>
As a Sales Director-Payers, you will be responsible for the scaling of Innovaccer's platform to U.S. healthcare payers. Reporting to a Regional Vice President (RVP) of Sales under Innovaccer's COO, you will work closely with top executives at leading payers within a region of the U.S. to introduce and expand the adoption of Innovaccer's offerings. Innovaccer is focused on growth across the entire payer spectrum, including national, Blues, regional, and government health plans, as well as specialty plans, local payers, and payer-led integrated networks. To accomplish this, you will have the opportunity to grow an enterprise sales team, and work closely with dedicated inside sales, sales engineering, and marketing teams. Furthermore, you will partner cross-functionally across the company (e.g., payer solutions product lead, customer success leadership) to align on and execute against an enterprise sales strategy.
