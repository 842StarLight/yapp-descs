<h2>Sales Coordinator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Ewald Consulting's Sales Department currently serves multiple professional and trade associations with tasks including, but not limited to: annual sponsorship sales; advertising sales; conference sponsorship/exhibitor sales; and membership sales. As Ewald Consulting's sales department continues to grow, additional support is needed to help expand the offerings we provide to our clients</p>
<p><b>
Roles and Responsibilities</b></p>
<p>
The Sales Coordinator uses a CRM, multiple database systems, and collaboration with other segments of our internal team and client leaders to maximize revenue and growth for our clients. Main duties include working with the Sales department on clients that have sales opportunities written into their contracts. The scope of sales services varies by client.</p>
<p>
The responsibilities for each area of focus include and are not limited to the following:</p>
<ul>
<li>Conduct prospect development, specific to each client</li>
<li>Demonstrate consultative selling skills and an understanding of clients</li>
<li>Execute tactics that increase sales volume, including outreach via email and phone calls</li>
<li>Learn and execute the company's sales process</li>
<li>Proactively and accurately track sales development</li>
<li>Meet and exceed both internal and external sales goals</li>
<li>Provide regular reports on outreach efforts</li>
<li>Attend client events on an as-needed basis</li>
<li>Other duties as assigned</li>
</ul>
