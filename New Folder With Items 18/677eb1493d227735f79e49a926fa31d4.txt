<h2>Director – Regional Solutions (LAC)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>Job Description<br>
<br>
<p>Overall responsibility for Solutions within the LAC region. The person in role will be expected to be an active member of LAC CyberSource regional team. The role will have a hard reporting line to Head of Regional Solutions and a dotted line to the CyberSource LAC business lead. In the short term this will be an individual contributor role.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Responsibilities:</b></p>
<p><b>
Solution Strategy and Design</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Drive efforts to evolve overall CyberSource solution strategy/prioritization within the region and to establish business sponsorship and define execution criteria</li>
<li>
Lead creation/design of new specific solutions that generate significant revenue contribution for the region and overall organization</li>
<li>
Manage the ongoing solution prioritization process for new, proposed initiatives to create fact based assessments of the projects to provide recommendations for Executive Leadership</li>
<li>
Develop and maintain regional solutions roadmap informed by the solution prioritization process and underlying product capability builds and specific project efforts</li>
<li>
Conduct regional competitive and regulatory intelligence activities to evaluate competitors’ activities and implications to inform senior executive decision-making</li>
<li>
Engage and coordinate cross-functional teams to understand the opportunity, review the health of the overall business, identify opportunities for growth, address issues to develop and structure an overall solution value proposition and initial proposal/definition</li>
<li>
Create and update financial models and business cases to define business rationale and financial impact of investment of proposed solutions</li>
<li>
Thought leadership: You will continuously challenge the status quo and you will be relentless in your focus to initiate and drive change</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Commercialization</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Maintain close relationships with key merchant and acquirer business contacts and end-users to proactively solicit feedback on features, functionality, services and future requirements.</li>
<li>
Author content on closed customer and public forums.</li>
<li>
Lead regional commercialization for new solutions across functional disciplines to ensure a successful launch</li>
<li>
Participate in the commercialization process for new launches including further solution design, pricing, packaging, sales training and develop criteria/operational processes for piloting new launches where appropriate</li>
<li>
Maintain commercialization plans that drive CYBS and Visa's competitive advantage and revenue growth agenda for core and emerging products (including ecommerce, mobile and alternative payments) across the region</li>
</ul>
</div>

<p></p>
<p><b>
QUALIFICATIONS</b></p>

<p><b>
Preferred Qualifications</b></p>

Motivation, drive and a self-starting attitude</li>
