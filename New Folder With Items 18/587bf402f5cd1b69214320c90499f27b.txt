<h2>Social Media Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Red Jacket Resorts is seeking a talented expert in social media to grow our company’s social networks, curate content, increase engagement and gain new followers.</p>
<p>This individual will create new social marketing campaigns, build brand recognition, and manage all published social media content. Additional tasks include; utilizing web analytics to track campaign progress, researching industry trends, maintaining an active social media presence and replying to customer inquiries.</p>
<p>The social media manager will assist with marketing and collaborate with PR and sales teams to align company messages, promotions, and goals.</p>
<p><b>Social Media Manager Duties and Responsibilities</b></p>
<p>· Develop and implement social media brand strategies, campaigns, and plans to build brand/company awareness</p>
<p>· Oversee day-to-day management of campaigns and ensure brand consistency</p>
<p>· Create, maintain, and grow new and existing social networks, including Twitter, LinkedIn, Pinterest, YouTube, Instagram, Google+, Facebook, and others</p>
<p>· Manage company blog and editorial calendar that aligns with social updates</p>
<p>· Monitor social media progress using web analytic tools</p>
<p>· Incorporate optimization strategies, analyze data, and research best ways to increase traffic</p>
<p>· Review success of campaigns and develop ways to improve</p>
<p>· Plan paid social media advertising strategies and budgets</p>
<p>· Drive engagement with social media influencers</p>
<p>· Resolve customer issues through social media</p>
<p>· Create engaging written and visual content</p>
<p>· Research new media platforms, trends, and industry opportunities</p>
<p>· Provide feedback from social media trends and research</p>
<p>· Write effective, concise copy for multiple platforms, websites, and social networks</p>
<p><b>Social Media Manager Requirements and Qualifications</b></p>
<p>· Excellent verbal and written communication skills</p>
<p>· Strong photography skills including composition, balance, color, creativity and design</p>
<p>· Experience in marketing or related field</p>
<p>· Two years of social media management experience preferred</p>
<p>· Experience with Hootsuite, Sprout Social, or HubSpot a plus</p>
<p>· Knowledge of Photoshop, Illustrator, and Google Analytics</p>
<p>· Knowledge of Search Engine Optimization (SEO) best practices</p>
<p>· Experience with technologies and best practices for production, and creative design across multiple platforms</p>
<p>· Experience identifying and creating campaigns for target audience</p>
<p>· Excellent analytical and time-management skills</p>
<p>· Strong project management skills with ability to supervise multiple projects</p>
