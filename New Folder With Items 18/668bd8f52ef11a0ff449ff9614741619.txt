<p><b>Application Specialist
</b><br>
<br>
Anritsu is looking for an Application Specialist to join our team. The Application Specialist provides support to the sales team by performing a number of administrative tasks, including the generation of sales quotes. This person will be the main point for the Regional Sales Managers. In order to be successful at this roll the ideal candidate should be organized, flexible, and tech savvy. This person will need to balance multiple projects and prioritize them.<br>
</p>
