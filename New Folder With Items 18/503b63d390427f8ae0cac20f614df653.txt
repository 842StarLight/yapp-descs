<h2>Business Intelligence Analyst - Marketing</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">BI Analyst - Marketing
<br><br>
<b>Department:</b> Marketing / eCommerce
<br><br>
<b>Reports To:</b> Senior Director, E-Commerce
<br><br>
<b>General Summary:</b><br>
Do you have a passion for data/analytics and a talent for providing information and recommendations that drive results? The Business Intelligence Analyst will manage performance analytics such as transaction trends (basket analysis, SKUs) to transform data into clear and actionable insights that drive decisions and results. The Analyst will also monitor and analyze marketing campaigns, measure those result against industry benchmarks and make recommendations to improve performance. The Analyst will collect, organize, analyze and disseminate large amounts of data to a variety of business leaders in an easy to understand format. The Analyst will also establish metrics and dashboards and educate business owners how to leverage data to improve results.
<br><br>
Position Responsibilities
<br><br>
Collaborate with business owners to understand goals, challenges, process and data sources
<br>Utilize multiple data sources to build standard reports and utilize ad-hoc queries to provide analytics and business intelligence to drive data-driven decisions and increase sales productivity
<br>Monitor and analyze campaign performance to inform future marketing strategies, which include reporting at high levels (monthly and seasonal roll-ups) as well as granular levels (segment, etc.).
<br>Build and deploy reporting and performance dashboards showing sales metrics and data trends identifying opportunities and risks to the business, along with recommendations. Ensure that existing reports and dashboards are well understood and effectively utilized
<br>Pre and post analysis of sales promotions, campaigns and marketing program.
<br>Develop and manage custom campaign performance databases and dashboards, which includes the bringing together datasets from different systems for unified reporting
<br>Verify accuracy of data and develop reporting standards and best practices to ensure data standardization and consistency.
<br>Forecasting, projecting-to-complete and recalculating results in-season, in order to properly project trends, end-of-season and end-of-year results and outcomes.
<br>Participate in annual budget preparation
<br>Partner with internal stakeholders on reports and dashboards for enhancements and modifications
<br><br>
<b>Position Requirements:</b><br>
Minimum 2-4 years of experience required in a retail/eCommerce organization in a business analysis role, working with progressively more complex analytics, supporting a sales and/or marketing function
<br>Strong analytical skills with hands-on experience collecting, organizing and analyzing large amounts of data and presenting results to stakeholders in a clear and concise manner
<br>Experience with marketing metrics and strategy with knowledge of advertising measurement tools and technology
<br>Understanding of media planning and media math (reach, frequency, audience composition, lift, etc.)
<br>An analytical, creative, and innovative approach to problem solving
<br>Knowledge of and experience with database concepts and data models
<br>Ability to teach business users how to interpret results and use dashboards to improve performance. Must be able to communicate technical topics to non-technical audiences
<br>Expert Excel skills; experience using formulas Pivot Tables, charts and graphs to organize and interpret data
<br>Working knowledge of MircoStrategy and Tableau
<br>Experience writing and executing SQL queries is a plus
<br>Ability to successfully manage multiple projects
<br>BS in Mathematics, Economics, Computer Science, Information Management, Statistics or Marketing or equivalent experience
