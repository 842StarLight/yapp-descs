<div><b><i>
JOB SUMMARY</i></b></div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
To provide leadership, technical supervision, training and guidance to workers in assigned area(s) in accordance with the instruction of and with authority delegated by the Director of Exterminating, leads special projects, team training, and work load distribution; troubleshoots and assists staff, performs services as needed, collections, and other functions deemed necessary.</div>

<div><b><i>
CHARACTERISTIC JOB TASKS, DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES</i></b></div>
<ul>
<li>
Supervision and coordinating activities of workers in a Residential &amp; Termite Branch.</li>
<li>
Interprets company policies to workers and enforces safety regulations.</li>
<li>
Analyzes and resolves work problems, or assist workers in solving work problems.</li>
<li>
As part of the team, ensure a safe and healthy work environment for staff, and our customers by monitoring/checking/inspecting, responding immediately to hazardous conditions; ensuring equipment and physical resources are properly maintained; identifying and arranging for necessary repairs/replacement of equipment/structural failures.</li>
<li>
Initiates or suggests plans to motivate workers to achieve work goals.</li>
<li>
Will train new workers.</li>
<li>
When supervising workers engaged in several services, is required to possess general knowledge of the activities involved.</li>
<li>
Facilitate and lead team meetings.</li>
<li>
Continuously update workers on changing practices and current information within the company.</li>
<li>
Communicating job responsibilities and expectations with staff and measuring performance outcomes.</li>
<li>
Manages production and sales goals.
</li>
<li>Serves as a resource person for staff;</li>
<li>
Job specific/system training for new employees.<br>
</li>
</ul>
