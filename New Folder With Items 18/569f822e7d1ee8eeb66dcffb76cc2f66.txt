<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities<br>
</b></h2>
<div></div>
<div><div><div>
<div>You enjoy meeting and speaking with people and have a talent for presenting information to consumers that convinces them to purchase your company’s products and services. You like the challenge of visiting with a cold lead and turning that lead into a customer. Does this sound interesting? We have a great opportunity for you.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
As a Direct Sales Representative, you will have opportunities for ongoing development in the company and a great team supporting you in the sales processes. You will receive training on our products and their content, packaging, and pricing to convey to your customers. You will also respond to historical billing queries, follow-up on leads, prepare for sales visits, and document sales. The more you sell, the greater your rewards will be. We offer a competitive base salary, as well as a commission and training bonus.</div>
