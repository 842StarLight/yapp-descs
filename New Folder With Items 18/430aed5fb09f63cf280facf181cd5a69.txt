<p><b>Water Treatment Technical Sales Support-Miami, Florida</b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>Technical Service Support Technicians are responsible for maintaining the existing customer base in Miami and the surrounding area, and for performing water safety tests (e.g. Legionella) and water treatment tests at those existing customers. With the freedom of working from your home, you can work and train in a territory alongside veteran Territory Managers. Live in or very near Miami or West Palm Beach.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>As a Technical Service, Sales Support Technician, you will receive an industry competitive salary, expenses, benefits, and a $625 per month car allowance. In addition, ample technical support is provided along with continuous training. Paperwork is kept to a minimum. Compensation is based upon experience and the ability to succeed. $50,000 to $65,000 first year base for a qualified candidate.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>We hire with a focus that favors the committed employee both in terms of overall compensation and growth opportunities. We have designed our compensation package accordingly. Benefits include a corporate 401k, health, dental, vision, and life insurance.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Job Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul><li> Solve customers' water problems.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Collect Legionella samples and gather other key data (i.e. disinfectant residuals, fixture type, etc.)</li></ul>
<ul><li> Exhibit good knowledge of and use of Excel.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Learn and understand the use of plumbing and domestic water distribution systems.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Learn waterborne pathogens and the conditions that promote their growth.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Track, handle, and ship samples using Chain of Custody forms.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Enter data into various trending platforms.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Perform other water treatment service &amp; testing on applicable systems (cooling towers, softeners, boilers, etc.)</li></ul>
<ul><li> Treat industrial boilers and cooling towers to keep them energy efficient and environmentally friendly.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Perform detailed water testing &amp; analyses at industrial sites, food processing plants, hospitals, commercial buildings, and data centers.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Help sell Garratt-Callahan's proprietary chemical blends, equipment, &amp; monitoring technology.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Help sell corrosion &amp; scale inhibitors, coagulants, flocculants, biocides, bio-augmentation, feed &amp; monitoring equipment, water management plans, reverse osmosis products.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Keep our existing customers happy.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Help bring on new industrial customers.</li></ul>
<p><b>About Garratt-Callahan:</b></p>
<p>Garratt-Callahan is a US based private 115 year old, nationwide water treatment chemical company. Garratt-Callahan www.garrattcallahan.com treats industrial boilers and cooling towers to keep them energy efficient and environmentally friendly with one of the most advanced product lines in the industry. We are registered to ISO 9001:2015 and sell an industry leading line of “green solutions, G-C GreenTech. We are headquartered in Burlingame, California.</p>
<p>G-C is family owned, profitable, and expanding. We have five chemical plants in the USA, sales people in every state, and operations in Asia, the Pacific, the Caribbean, and Europe.</p>
