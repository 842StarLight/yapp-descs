<h2>Solutions Architect - CSP</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>NVIDIA is searching for an extraordinary Sr. Solutions Architect as key part of our Cloud Provider team. Primary responsibilities will be to lead hardware/software customer technical engagements around NVIDIA products and technologies deployed in our partners cloud datacenters; including some of the hottest technology areas like computer vision, robotics and digital personal assistants.
</p>
<p></p>
<p>You should enjoy working in a dynamic environment, sometimes taking the lead and sometimes supporting colleagues with diverse skillsets. Will be working cross functionally and across organizations to build a positive experience for cloud partners and improve NVIDIA's future products. Excellent interpersonal skills, the ability to work independently and passion for keeping technically current are critical. Love using your systems architecture skills to collaborate with partners to build state-of-the-art AI applications and services and putting together custom solutions/prototypes that demonstrate the value of NVIDIA technologies. Experience with cloud, virtualization and remote visualization is a plus, as well as the ability to quickly learn new technologies and skills.
</p>
<p></p>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"></h2>
<p><b>What you’ll be doing:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li><b>First and primary point of contact for architectural advice around hardware and software technologies.
</b></li>
<li><b>Work closely with the entire customer team, including sales and program management, as well as applications engineering.
</b></li>
<li><b>Shepherd critical customer issues and provide timely advance warning of critical issues that need attention.
</b></li>
<li><b>Technical authority on all NVIDIA products, directly supporting Sales Account Managers, and working closely with the team to secure design wins, and to lead the support of the product through the design-in phase all the way to product end of life.
</b></li>
<li><b>Become a trusted advisor on technology to your customer and establish relationships to facilitate rapid resolution of issues and promote the highest levels of customer delight.
</b></li>
</ul>
