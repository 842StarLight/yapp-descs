<h2>Sr. Staff Engineer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>FogHorn Systems, Inc. seeks a Sr. Staff Engineer – Responsible for the development of system-level software modules to implement in Foghorn’s Edge Intelligence Stack products.</p>
<p>
Specific duties include:</p>
<ul>
<li>Designing, developing, and integrating software modules worked on with the rest of FogHorn’s product offering;</li>
<li>Modifying existing software to correct errors, adapting it to new container modules, compilers, microprocessor platforms, virtual machines, or to upgrade API interfaces;</li>
<li>Performing unit, integration and performance testing of the modules worked on, for latency and throughput measurements and improvements;</li>
<li>Providing input and contributing towards development and review of product documentation;</li>
<li>Consulting with Product Managers, Pre-Sales Engineers, and prospective customers on project status, technical issues such as software design and/or implementation;</li>
<li>Co-ordinating the installation of the product for internal engineering purposes or external customer requirements;</li>
<li>Providing a regular report or correspondence concerning project specifications, design and implementation status; and</li>
<li>Overseeing and mentoring junior developers on projects and providing training.</li>
</ul>
<p>Worksite: Sunnyvale, CA. This position requires a Master’s degree or the equivalent in Computer Science or a related field and three (3) years of experience. Will also accept a Bachelor’s degree or the equivalent in Computer Science or a related field and five (5) years of experience in lieu of a Master’s degree and 3 years of experience.</p>
<p>
To apply, submit resume to jobs@foghorn.io.</p>
