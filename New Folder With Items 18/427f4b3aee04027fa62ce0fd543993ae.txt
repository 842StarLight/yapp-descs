<h2>Director, Product Manager (EDC)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Bioclinica is the trusted leader in clinical trial management innovation and therapeutic expertise. We employ cutting edge technology to provide our customers with industry leading solutions creating clarity in the clinical trial process. Currently we are seeking a Director, Product Management.
</p>
<p></p>
<p>The Director, Product Management is responsible for providing leadership, structure and vision within Bioclinica's Electronic Data Capture (EDC) business segment. This individual must be experienced with EDC systems and have Project Management experience within a CRO or pharmaceutical research organization.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Primary Responsibilities
</b></p>
<p></p>
<p>Responsible for providing leadership, structure and vision while managing the Electronic Data Capture (EDC) business segment to meet established operational and financial goals and objectives:
</p>
<ul>
<li>Responsible for product roadmaps, direction, investment in EDC products to ensure industry leadership, while also understanding cross-product prioritization
</li>
<li>Creating and maintaining the commercial business case that supports the product road maps
</li>
<li>Ensuring global EDC service delivery
</li>
<li>Managing staff across the US, Europe and India
</li>
<li>Establishing and managing operational plans, objectives, priorities and activities
</li>
<li>Ensuring internal processes for the EDC team is defined
</li>
<li>Identifying/addressing opportunities for efficiency and quality within the EDC business
</li>
<li>Acting as Sales Overlay in support of Business Development and where SME support is needed
</li>
<li>Recommending strategies to the VP, Product Management
</li>
<li>Keeping Sr. Mgmt. advised of current issues and working with same to optimize processes
</li>
<li>Working with other product management functions to improve the overall community and best practices
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p>Responsible for directing/coordinating day-to-day activities of assigned departments in order to promote effective execution, delivery and communication:
</p>
<ul>
<li>Meeting financial objectives, especially revenue targets and reporting departmental results
</li>
<li>Communicating departmental updates to appropriate personnel throughout the company
</li>
<li>Participating in financial planning/analysis, reporting, contract review, process improvement
</li>
<li>Developing and managing the franchise budget
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p>Provide direction, structure, coaching and support to department managers and other employees:
</p>
<ul>
<li>Managing personnel to assure compliance to customer and internal requirements
</li>
<li>Coaching, counseling, disciplining and developing key staff
</li>
<li>Conducting performance reviews, and approving timesheets and time off for direct reports
</li>
<li>Coordinating and conducting interviews; facilitating hiring decisions
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Secondary Responsibilities
</b></p>
<p></p>
<p>Maintains Quality Service and Departmental Standards by:
</p>
<ul>
<li>Reading, understanding and adhering to organizational Standard Operating Procedures (“SOP”)
</li>
<li>Assisting in establishing and enforcing departmental standards
</li>
<li>Reviewing and updating company SOPs related to product and service delivery
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p>Contributes to team effort by:
</p>
<ul>
<li>Representing Bioclinica’s EDC business at industry conferences and events
</li>
<li>Working with internal staff to resolve issues
</li>
<li>Exploring new opportunities to add value to organization and departmental processes
</li>
<li>Helping others to achieve results
</li>
<li>Performing other duties as assigned
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p>Maintains Technical and Industry Knowledge by:
</p>
<ul><li>Attending and participating in applicable company-sponsored training
</li></ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Qualifications
</b></p>
<p></p>
<p>Education:
</p>
<ul><li>Bachelor’s Degree in the sciences, or equivalent work experience, required
</li></ul>
<p></p>
<p>Experience:
</p>
<ul>
<li>12 years clinical trials experience within a CRO or pharmaceutical research organization
</li>
<li>5 years management experience in clinical development of investigational medications
</li>
<li>5 years experience with EDC systems
</li>
<li>5 years personnel management experience
</li>
<li>Working knowledge of the software development life cycle
</li>
<li>Working knowledge of GCH, ICH guidelines and FDA regulations
</li>
<li>Working knowledge of FDA Guidance Documents: Computerized Systems Used in Clinical Trials, General Principles of Software Validation
</li>
<li>Working knowledge of 21 C.F.R. Part 11: Electronic Records; Electronic Signatures – Scope and Application
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p>Additional skill set:
</p>
<ul>
<li>Strong interpersonal and communication skills, both verbal and written
</li>
<li>High degree of personal and business integrity
</li>
<li>Ability to successfully balance multiple projects and timelines
</li>
<li>Ability to work in group setting and independently; ability to adjust to changing priorities
</li>
<li>Team builder with strong interpersonal, communication and mentoring abilities
</li>
<li>Strong organizational and leadership skills
</li>
<li>Goal oriented
</li>
<li>Ability to maintain professional and positive attitude
</li>
</ul>
