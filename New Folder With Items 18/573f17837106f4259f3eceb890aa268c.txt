<p>
Position Overview</p>
<p>
Reporting to the Customer Service Manager, the Customer Service Representative will serve as a liaison for all matters pertaining to LABORIE products, including: order processing, answering product questions, providing updates on orders and assisting with complaints, orders, errors, account questions, product returns and other customer queries.</p>
<p>
Responsibilities</p>
<ul>
<li>Professionally handle incoming requests from customers and ensure that issues are resolved both promptly and thoroughly.</li>
<li>Manage incoming calls and customer inquiries.</li>
<li>Identify and provide accurate part number and cost information for disposable/equipment items.</li>
<li>Review/edit capital equipment and disposable orders from customers and sales representatives, received via fax, phone or e-mail to ensure customer receives accurate pricing and product(s).</li>
<li>Accurately and promptly enter capital equipment and disposable orders into our ERP system for processing.</li>
<li>Distribute processed orders to appropriate personnel (production, warehouse, clinical consultants, course coordinator and/or technical coordinator).</li>
<li>Resolve customer complaints, assessing customer needs to provide appropriate solutions to achieve customer satisfaction and following up to ensure resolution.</li>
<li>Process product returns when necessary.</li>
<li>Retain appropriate records of customer complaints following company procedures.</li>
<li>Direct advanced product questions to the appropriate personnel for identification and following up (if required) with customers to ensure question is answered and appropriate product/service is obtained.</li>
<li>Provide tracking information, when requested, for disposable and equipment orders.</li>
<li>Provide information on back orders and updating customers when product is received.</li>
<li>Perform additional add-on tasks as requested by manager.</li>
</ul>
<p>Requirements</p>
<ul>
<li>Highly developed sense of integrity and commitment to customer satisfaction.</li>
<li>Ability to communicate clearly and professionally, both verbally and in writing.</li>
<li>Ability to handle complaints and unpleasant customers in a professional manner.</li>
<li>Pleasant, patient and friendly attitude.</li>
<li>Strong decision making and analytical abilities.</li>
<li>Accurate and detail oriented.</li>
<li>Willingness to work occasional overtime when needed.</li>
<li>Possess a strong work ethic and team player mentality.</li>
<li>Efficient computer skills: Basic Microsoft Office, Internet navigation, e-mail composition, typing skills and ability to learn order processing software.</li>
<li>At least 1-2 years of college preferred or 2+ years of experience in a customer service or help desk capacity required.</li>
</ul>
