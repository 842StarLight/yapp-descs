<h2>Automotive Technician - Chapman Nissan - Philadelphia, Pennsylvania, US</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">Hiring Immediately, Automotive Technicians. All skill levels needed.
<br><br>
We are seeking Automotive Technicians to add to our growing service department. Our sales are on the rise and we need energetic people that love cars to help us keep up with increased traffic. Mechanics with Independent Service Station experience as with Midas, Monroe, Goodyear, etc please apply. Start your career with the Chapman Auto Stores today!
<br><br>
REQUIREMENTS
<br><br>
Previous automotive experience is REQUIRED for this position. Qualified candidates will need a valid driver's license, high school diploma or equivalent degree, and the ability to work a flexible schedule. Candidates with ASE certification, inspection license, and advanced training are highly desired. We are looking for someone who works hard, communicates clearly and is interested in a real career with long term potential, not just a job.
<br><br>
BENEFITS
<br><br>
Generous compensation based on experience with optional flat rate
<br>Available 4 day work week schedules
<br>Health with choice of medical plans starting at $84.50 per month
<br>Dental and Vision insurance
<br>401K retirement investment plan with company match
<br>Paid vacation and holiday time
<br>Life insurance &amp; Short-term and long-term disability insurance
<br>Generous employee discounts on vehicles purchases, parts, and service
<br>Company provided uniforms
<br><br>
RESPONSIBILITIES
<br><br>
Perform work as outlined on repair order with efficiency and accuracy in accordance with dealership and factory standards
<br>Diagnose cause of any malfunction and perform repair as authorized.
<br>Communicate with the Parts Department to obtain needed parts.
<br>Communicate needed repairs to Service Advisors to obtain approvals
<br>Participate in and complete training as assigned by the manufacturer and management
<br>Road-test vehicles to ensure the repair has addressed the customer complaint
<br>Administer safety and housekeeping policies and procedures, keeping spills cleaned up as they happen.
