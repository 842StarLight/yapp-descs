<h2>Shift Leader/Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>We are looking for a Shift Leader/ Manager to ensure every customer is receiving a consistent product and competent service in a clean environment. A successful manager will ensure efficient restaurant operation, as well as maintain high production, productivity, quality, and customer-service standards.</p>
<p>RESPONSIBILITIES<br>- Oversees production of food according to sanitation and quality standards<br>- Manages and mentors staff, including, managing issues of discipline/termination, ensuring ongoing training/education, etc.<br>- Manages supplies and ensures orderly work areas<br>- Ensures that work areas are clean and that equipment, tools, and supplies are properly stored<br>- Ensures compliance with all applicable federal and state laws and all company policies<br>- Addresses customer concerns and issues<br>- Prepares deposits and maintains a cash management system<br>- Ensures compliance with cash control procedures<br>- Operates the complete POS system and other required software</p>
<p>QUALIFICATIONS<br>- Point-of-sale (cash register) and credit or debit card processing skills<br>- Excellent communication skills, verbal and written<br>- Ability to lead a team by supervising and managing staff</p>
<p>Job Types: Full-time, Part-time</p>
<p>Salary: $12.00 to $14.00 /hour</p>
</div>
