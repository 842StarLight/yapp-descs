<h2>Event Production Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>SUMMARY OF DUTIES</p>
<p>Primary responsibility for successful assessment, planning and execution of client events; authority to act as a business “owner.” Works closely with event production team, staffing team, warehouse team and external vendors to prioritize, organize and order necessary components, includes up selling clients. Accountable from start to finish, including point of contact with prospective clients, research of industry trends/new ideas, reviewing post-event feedback surveys and making procedural improvements; responsible for this key line of business.</p>
<p>QUALIFICATIONS</p>
<ul>
<li>Demonstrated successful event planning and production experience</li>
<li>Strong customer service skills to understand the clients needs and budgets</li>
<li>Effective communication skills, verbally and in writing, with clients, employees, and vendors</li>
<li>Must be able to communicate confidently and independently to clients and vendors</li>
<li>Must be able to successfully work in a fast paced, constantly changing environment</li>
<li>Working knowledge of computers and databases for tracking proposal elements and invoicing</li>
<li>Must be able to gain access to military bases, requiring drug and background checks</li>
<li>Valid driver’s license</li>
</ul>
<p>DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES</p>
<ul>
<li>Cultivates new prospects through phone calls, e-mails and meetings, including promotions such as complimentary tastings</li>
<li>Prepares proposals, and tracks data through the catering software program in a timely manner</li>
<li>Schedules and processes contracts and payments from clients, following sales SOP</li>
<li>Reviews proposals and connects with clients to discuss the timeline/elements of the event</li>
<li>Continuous research of industry best practices to deliver WOW to clients</li>
<li>Enters data through the catering software program, adjusts proposal for final invoices</li>
<li>Submits orders for staff, supplies and vendors to meet proposal requirements</li>
<li>Provides regular updates to the client and EP team at meetings, through e-mails and discussions</li>
<li>Proactively problem solves event details to the satisfaction of the client and GEH standards</li>
<li>Requires development of new practices or updates to existing SOPS to improve policy and procedures</li>
<li>Handles all invoices and receipts for each event, and is responsible for timely payment collections from clients</li>
<li>Provides feedback of staff performance and makes recommendations for coaching up to and including terminations</li>
<li>Performs miscellaneous tasks such as site visits, client/operational meetings and supervising events as needed for the success of the event</li>
</ul>
<p>PHYSICAL REQUIREMENTS OF THE JOB</p>
<ul>
<li>Frequent walking and standing at events and pre-event inspection visits</li>
<li>Moderate lifting, an average of 50 pounds</li>
<li>Moderate sitting at computer and using the phone</li>
<li>Performs work at offices and events, both indoors and outdoors</li>
<li>Variable hours of work, occasional event days can be as long as 20 hours</li>
</ul>
<p>Job Type: Full-time</p>
<p>Experience:</p>
<ul><li>relevant: 1 year (Preferred)</li></ul>
