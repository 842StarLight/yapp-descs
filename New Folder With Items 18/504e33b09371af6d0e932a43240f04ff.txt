<h2>Patient Care Coordinator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>The Patient Care Coordinator (PCC) / Front Office Associate is the first point of contact and demonstrates professionalism by using a patient centered approach of building trust, meeting needs and delivering solutions through recognizing the needs and opportunities that exists.  This position screens the customer to determine how we can best help them, responds to questions regarding advertising promotions, learn to recognize a potential “opportunity” and facilitate smooth patient flow and services to the customer.  They support the clinical staff by scheduling appointments and handling administrative processing and record- keeping. Support the mission of the company by demonstrating excellent customer care. This position is responsible for tasks needing completion daily, weekly and monthly, as well as identifying the ongoing and varied needs of the patients.</p>
<p> </p>
<p>Essential Duties and Responsibilities:</p>
<p>         Provide the first customer contact for patients who call or come in to schedule an appointment or inquire about our services.  Screen calls, recognizing opportunities and facilitating excellent service by providing the information necessary to secure an appointment, sell a product or answer questions.  Schedule and confirm appointments.</p>
<p>         Initiate appointments with customers in a professional manner to generate appointments.</p>
<p>         Schedule and register patients according to company guidelines.  Coordinate provider’s schedule and ensure the smooth and efficient flow of patient care while in the office.</p>
<p>Check patients in and out, collect and record payments, posting of charges, entering invoices, payments and insurance contract information. </p>
<p>Verify patient information, billing/insurance data, request third party prior authorization as needed, complete appropriate documentation and maintain patient charts to include the filing of all patient records. </p>
<p>Work with insurance agencies to facilitate authorizations and benefit verification.</p>
<p>Maintain all daily, weekly and monthly reports and tracking documents as directed. </p>
<p>Respond to company requests for billing, patient and accounting information. </p>
<p>Responsible for a variety of administrative tasks to include typing, filing, inventory, mail and verifying the status of and checking in hearing aids and repairs.</p>
<p>Responsible to open and close the office on a daily basis and ensure the reception and waiting area is well maintained.</p>
<p>  </p>
<p>Education and Experience Requirements:</p>
<p>A minimum of 2 years office management experience in a customer driven industry.</p>
<p>Front desk medical/sales field experience and/or knowledge of procedures desirable.</p>
<p> </p>
<p>Required Skills:</p>
<p>Experience with word processing and database software.</p>
<p>Excellent interpersonal skills that allow effective working relationships with a diverse, patient, colleague, and vendor population.</p>
<p>Excellent oral and written communication skills, strong customer service orientation, detail oriented &amp; excellent organizational skills.</p>
<p>Basic understanding of accounting procedures and good math aptitude.</p>
<p>Ability to manage multiple tasks within strict deadlines.</p>
