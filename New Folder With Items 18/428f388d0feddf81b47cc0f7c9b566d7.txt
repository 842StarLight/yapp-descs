<h2>Programmer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">Cinderella, Inc. is a family owned, large and growing wholesale distributor to the pool and spa industry. From warehouses in Saginaw and Grand Rapids, Michigan and Indianapolis, Indiana, Cinderella distributes swimming pool and spa products around the country. At Cinderella, our people, products and solutions make us who we are as a company.
<br><br>
Cinderella is in search of a Programmer to join our outstanding team and provide hands-on and strategic services to support day to day operations and contribute to our continued growth.
<br><br>
<b>Position Summary:</b><br>
The Programmer will manage work with the current programming resources to:
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Develop, maintain and document code for the company’s e-commerce, reporting and pricing systems;</li>
<li>Website development and maintenance;</li>
<li>Programmatic interaction with company servers, databases and SQL reporting;</li>
<li>Data import and export with varied customer ordering systems;</li>
<li>Programming analysis and design;</li>
<li>Assist with the maintenance of the company’s e-commerce fulfillment business;</li>
<li>Maintain and enhance the code to meet changing customer and industry needs;</li>
<li>Assist with the design and development of company sales catalogs;</li>
<li>Assist with layout and design of sales brochures and other graphic design needs;</li>
<li>Assist management with implementation of company strategy;</li>
<li>Interact with management, operations, customer service, purchasing, accounting, sales, and other functional teams to ensure our systems are fulfilling departmental needs;</li>
<li>Other assignments as directed by company management.</li>
</ul>
<b>Minimum Qualifications and Abilities:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science, Mathematics or Information Systems strongly preferred.</li>
<li>5 + years of experience</li>
<li>Experience with a Distribution organization preferred</li>
<li>Experience in partnering with an executive team • High-level written and oral communication skills</li>
<li>Exceptional organizational ability and ability to meet deadlines As the company grows and transitions these duties are subject to adjust accordingly</li>
</ul>
