<p><b>
Position Summary</b></p>
<p>
The Vice President, Data &amp; Analytics is responsible for driving Ascend’s technology strategy for Analytics. This includes enabling compelling insights across the enterprise by providing data collection, aggregation, and storage across our learning applications and various business processes. This role also includes evolving Ascend’s analytics competency for analytics, enabling descriptive, diagnostic, predictive, and prescriptive analytics capabilities and solutions to align to business unit strategies. This leader will manage a technical organization with expertise in data and technology and will partner with product leaders across Ascend to deliver results. The Vice President, Data &amp; Analytics ensures that the company and our customers have the necessary data and information for ongoing daily operations and strategic planning and deliverables.</p>
<p><b>
Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Provide executive leadership for the Data and Analytics team, ensuring that the team can meet the analytics needs of the businesses. This includes both customer-facing analytics products as well as internal capabilities.</li>
<li>Lead the Analytics organization’s effort to instrument and assemble data from multiple sources across Ascend Learning’s business units. This includes sales, product, content authoring, and customer repositories in order to create integrated views that can be used to drive decision making.</li>
<li>Identify opportunities to develop insights, forecasts, statistical models, segmentation schemes, and data driven analyses to drive better learner outcomes and marketing/merchandising efforts around delivering customer value.</li>
<li>Assure data model consistency to the extent possible and continue driving towards creating a self-service model to allow internal users to engage with the analytics platform and drive insights that will allow Ascend to differentiate in its markets.</li>
<li>Set challenging and realistic goals for the organization that reflects an exciting vision of insights leading to successful outcomes for our students and educators.</li>
<li>Drive the adoption of new technologies that will enable further insights to be derived from data as well as automating the collection and correlation of data.</li>
</ul>
