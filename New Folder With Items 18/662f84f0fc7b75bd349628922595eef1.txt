<h2>Customer Service Representative</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p><b>Hours/Status</b>: 8:00 am - 5:00 pm Monday through Friday<br>
</p>
<p><b>Job Summary</b>: Process orders from The Graphic Edge customers, Edge 2 stores and sales force, verifying quantities, design, and desired completion date. Assist customers with the art approval process including noting art changes and art approvals.</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Essential Duties and Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Receive incoming calls, e-mails, faxes, and mail from customers and/or the sales force for order requests, and accurately process pertinent information into a production order.</li>
<li>Enter order data into the software systems accurately to ensure proper art design and production requirements are met.</li>
<li>Initiate and receive phone, fax, or emails from customers requesting art design changes and/or approvals. Properly process said changes and/or approvals to move the production order along.</li>
<li>Initiate communication with customers and/or sales staff to clarify order information to ensure accurate order production.</li>
<li>Work within a team of customer service representatives that are responsible for a number of sales territories. Coordinate the work activities within the team to ensure order entry, web quotes, art changes and approvals are performed in a timely manner.</li>
<li>Work with the other teams within the customer service department to ensure order entry, art changes and approvals are performed in a timely manner.</li>
<li>Work and communicate with other departments to ensure steady production flow.</li>
<li>Maintains cleanliness of work areas and equipment.</li>
<li>Performs job consistently within established performance standards.
</li>
<li>Is punctual, observes prescribed work break/meal periods and has an acceptable overall attendance record.</li>
<li>Other duties as assigned or dictated by the needs of the business.</li>
<li>Perform and complete daily production reports. (Team 4) Other teams assist as the business or staffing needs dictate.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>

</ul>
