<p><b>Overview &amp; Responsibilities</b></p>
<p><b>GENERAL ACCOUNTABILITIES</b></p>
<p>The Product Designer plays an integral role in working with our pre-need client families, via phone and email, who have purchased a bronze marker at one of our cemeteries. The designer is responsible for concept design/layout of memorials to support the Company’s marker storage initiative. This role works to consistently uphold established standards, maintainaccuracy/integrityand ensure that all artwork and image files are properly maintained</p>
<p><b>RESPONSIBILITIES</b></p>
<p>Customer Service 50%</p>
<ul>
<li>Conducts phone calls to existing pre-need client families to review their memorial selection and gather the details needed for the memorial design to be manufactured and stored with our supplier, Matthews.</li>
</ul>
<p>Product Development 50%</p>
<ul>
<li><p>Uses our internal point-of-sale system (HMIS) and our supplier, Matthews’, eVantage system to research, design and submit bronze marker designs for client family approval then for submission for manufacturing and storage.</p></li>
<li><p>
Meets the weekly design-in-process goals set forth for the Product Design team.</p></li>
<li><p>
Catalogs, updates and maintains digital library of all projects within established filing system.</p></li>
<li><p>
Provides the highest level of customer service to both internal and external customers.</p></li>
</ul>

<p><b>SCI Overview</b></p>
<p><b>Who we are. What we do.</b></p>
<p>We’re more than North America’s largest provider of funeral, cremation and cemetery services. We are mothers, fathers, sisters, brothers, sons and daughters who are devoted to the communities where we live and work. We are more than 20,000 dedicated individuals who provide caring assistance to families in need, honor veterans and public servants and deliver lifesaving programs to help keep children and seniors safe.</p>
<p>We operate under the umbrella of the Dignity Memorial network of 1,800 funeral homes and cemeteries. Dignity Memorial providers care for more than 300,000 families each year and understand the importance of thoughtful, personalized arrangements. We believe creating meaningful ways to pay tribute to a loved one begins with compassion and is shaped by the understanding that each life is truly unique. For us, there is no greater responsibility than honoring and preserving the story of one’s life.</p>
