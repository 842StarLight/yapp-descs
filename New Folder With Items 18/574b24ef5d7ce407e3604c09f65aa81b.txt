<h2>Retail Salesperson</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Chattanooga Sewing Machines</b> is currently seeking an experienced <b><i>Salesperson</i></b> to join our team in Chattanooga, TN</p>
<p>
We are a family owned and operated company. We run a successful sewing machine sales and service business with two locations in Tennessee and Georgia. Our original store is located in Chattanooga, TN and we have added a second location in Calhoun, GA Thanks to our wonderful customers, we expanded the original building to over 6,500 square feet, adding and event center, added a separate area for fabric and vinyl, and we work daily on updating our online shopping site.</p>
<p><b>
Summary</b></p>
<p>
Receive requests for quotations, orders and lead times from existing and potential customers by performing the following duties.</p>
<p><b>
Essential Duties and Responsibilities</b> include the following. Other duties may be assigned.</p>
<p>
*Offers price quotes in response to walk in, phone, or email inquiries. Prepares quote forms as needed *Calls and follow up with customer leads and explain type of service or product offered. *Informs current and prospective customers of current promotions and new or upgraded products. *Quotes prices and encourages customers to buy. *Checks customer credit and confirms full payment before approving any sale. *Maintains files of active orders and posts activities to ordering systems, including any notes *Prepares and/or monitors "paperwork" flow. *Secures and places orders with others for filling. *Enters names, addresses, purchases, and reactions of prospects into computer database system. *Follows up with customers to assure satisfaction, respond to queries, acquire about further sales, and solve or refer problems. *Prepares report on sales activities.
</p>
