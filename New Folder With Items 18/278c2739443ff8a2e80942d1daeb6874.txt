<h2>Property Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Position Summary</b></h3>
<p>To work in accordance with established policies and procedures while managing the day to day operations of the community. The Property Manager is responsible for overseeing and enhancing the value of the property, developing and motivating the staff, maintaining positive resident relations and performing marketing as required.</p>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader">
<b>Responsibilities</b><b> (</b><b>Other duties may be assigned.)</b>
</h3>
<ul><li>Directly supervises leasing and service staff and monitors daily leasing activity</li></ul>
<ul><li>Responsible for scheduling, hiring, counseling, training, and other personnel related activities directly related to on-site staff</li></ul>
<ul><li>Evaluate and review the performance of leasing personnel and report to supervisors with concerns, compliments and/or areas requiring improvement</li></ul>
<ul><li>Maintains the physical condition of property by seeing that appropriate maintenance is routinely performed on the property</li></ul>
<ul><li>Sends appropriate correspondence to residents for lease termination, violation of lease terms, lease renewals, lease extensions, etc.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Assist in the creation of marketing strategies</li></ul>
<ul><li>Adheres to Social Media policy including posting to Social Media sites as required</li></ul>
<ul><li>Help create up beat/positive atmosphere</li></ul>
<ul><li>Submit monthly objectives such as marketing and outreach, miscellaneous functions, event planning, outreach results, and training strategy and results, etc.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Submit monthly personnel schedules, in advance, to regional staff</li></ul>
<ul><li>Responsible for showing (leasing) the model and vacant apartment homes to potential residents as needed. In addition, this includes recording accurate marketing and demographic information from residents calling and visiting the Leasing Center on Guest Cards and in the computer. Additionally, responsible for monitoring the follow-up response activity with potential residents</li></ul>
<ul><li>Performs inspections of “make-ready” apartment homes to ensure company quality standards are met or exceeded in a timely fashion</li></ul>
<ul><li>Responsible for ensuring all paperwork necessary to qualify applicants who are interested in leasing an apartment home is complete, accurate and processed timely</li></ul>
<ul><li>Responsible for ensuring accuracy and consistency of move-in inspections and all related paperwork, including providing notary services as needed</li></ul>
<ul><li>Responsible for monitoring, reviewing, documenting and recapping all resident service requests, lease renewal, delinquency and any other miscellaneous requests, using the “warm call” program.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Responsible for performing and reviewing move-out inspections and all paperwork necessary upon a resident’s move-out</li></ul>
<ul><li>Maintains and transmits payroll records timely and accurately</li></ul>
<ul><li>Perform written evaluations of all property employee and provide recommendations for compensation adjustments and/or promotions</li></ul>
<ul><li>Responsible for learning the entire community layout including all floorplan styles and amenities</li></ul>
<ul><li>Maintains a thorough understanding of competitive market by conducting monthly competitive surveys and periodic visits to competitive properties</li></ul>
<ul><li>Responsible for exploring and documenting information regarding the property’s market area, the competition, area amenities, and area service on a monthly basis</li></ul>
<ul><li>Update Regional Manager weekly on status of miscellaneous functions, i.e., event planning, outreach results, training plans and results, etc.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Responsible for handling and resolving resident and vendor issues</li></ul>
<ul><li>Conducts weekly and quarterly property inspections and submits a detailed report of condition of property to the Regional Property Manager. Coordinates with the maintenance service team on both preventative and ongoing maintenance</li></ul>
<ul><li>Responsible for completing weekly, monthly and quarterly reports to owners and the corporate office</li></ul>
<ul><li>Keeps accurate records of income and expenditures to ensure keeping within budget to achieve maximum NOI</li></ul>
<ul><li>Approves and codes appropriate invoices using proper general ledger categories</li></ul>
<ul><li>Keeps accurate records of apartment maintenance and inventory</li></ul>
<ul><li>Maintains accurate resident files</li></ul>
<ul><li>Responsible for evaluating the services of contractors at the community</li></ul>
<ul><li>Responsible for preparation and presentation of delinquent rent accounts in county Rent Court</li></ul>
<ul><li>Monitors pool activity and gatehouse security</li></ul>
<ul><li>Responsible for completing other miscellaneous projects assigned by the Regional Property Manager</li></ul>
<ul><li>Flexibility in work schedule as determined by regional management may be required periodically to insure adequate staffing in leasing office</li></ul>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Qualifications</b></h3>
<p>To perform this job successfully, an individual must be able to perform each essential duty satisfactorily. The requirements listed below are representative of the knowledge, skill, and/or ability required. It is important to be able to handle multiple tasks. Reasonable accommodations may be made to enable individuals with disabilities to perform the essential functions.</p>
<ul><li>Strong commitment to company goals and values</li></ul>
<ul><li>Excellent communication skills, both verbal and written</li></ul>
<ul><li>Customer-centered</li></ul>
<ul><li>Friendly and positive attitude</li></ul>
<ul><li>Ability to maintain confidentiality</li></ul>
<ul><li>Dependable, organized team player</li></ul>
<ul><li>Ability to direct, coach and motivate others</li></ul>
<ul><li>Must have valid driver’s license and dependable vehicle</li></ul>
<ul><li>Thorough knowledge of Fair Housing Laws</li></ul>
<ul><li>Must be able to analyze monthly Income Statement to prepare variance reports</li></ul>
<p><b>

<p><b>

<p><b>
Flexibility:</b> Must be able to manage changes and work demands</p>
<p><b>
Dependability:</b> Must be a reliable team player who can take initiative</p>
<p><b>

<p><b>

Ability to write reports, business correspondence and procedure manuals. Ability to effectively present information and respond to questions from groups of managers, clients, customers and the general public.</p>
