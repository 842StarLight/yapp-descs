<h2>Intern, Brand Experience, Project Manager (Summer 2020)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div><b>Summary/ Objective</b></div>
<div>
The Project Manager manages key client projects. Project management responsibilities include the coordination and completion of projects on time within budget and within scope. Oversee all aspects of projects. Coordinate with product owners to set deadlines, assign responsibilities and monitor and report progress of project. Prepare reports for upper management regarding status of project.</div>
<div>
The successful candidate will work directly with internal clients to ensure deliverables fall within the applicable scope and budget. He or she will coordinate with other departments to ensure all aspects of each project are compatible, on time and communicated among the product team.<br>
<br>
<b>Essential Functions</b>
</div>
<ul>
<li><i>Reasonable accommodations may be made to enable individuals with disabilities to perform the essential functions.</i></li>
<li>Manage relationships between Product Owner, Leadership and Product Team</li>
<li>Perform Change Control processes</li>
<li>Track Project Performance, focused on successful completion of long- and short-term goals</li>
<li>Develop Project Plans that are shared and agreed to by Product Team and meet company goals and priorities</li>
<li>Coordinate meetings with Business analysts and Product Team to gather and document all requirements</li>
</ul>
<div>
<br>
<b>Required Skills/Abilities</b>

<li>Strong Organization Skills and attention to detail while multitasking</li>
</ul>
