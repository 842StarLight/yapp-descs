<div>
<div>
<p>iFLY is an organization that has PASSION for EXCELLENCE and INNOVATION. We live and breathe our values in everything we do and in every decision we make. As a member of iFLY's team, you aren't an employee, you are a TEAM MEMBER. You will be expected to contribute your talents and energies to make what is already great about iFLY, even better and available to more people. In return, we commit to work with you to make your career at iFLY rewarding,</p>
<p>
iFLY is seeking a Lead Instructor. The main duties of a Lead Flight Instructor is to ensure the safe, efficient operation of the wind tunnel and uphold the customer service provided by the instructor staff. Day to day responsibilities will include, but are not limited to: standard Flight Instructor duties, ensure customer’s safety while flying, provide excellent customer service, up-selling and sales, coaching, manage the wind-tunnel and flight deck, clean and maintain the facility, organize training for Instructor staff, and conduct the daily run-up inspection.</p>
<p>
This is one of those rare opportunities for you to join an already successful company with a passion for innovation, but where you will have the latitude to be a valuable member of the Tunnel Team.</p>
<p><b>
What you’ll do:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Wind tunnel safety practices for instructing and training</li>
<li>Report to the GM about the Instructor staff and the safe operation of the wind tunnel</li>
<li>Report to the appropriate IBA Examiner about the development of the Instructor staff and safety</li>
<li>Manage incident reports and distribute to the appropriate personnel</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Day-to-Day responsibilities include:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Create development plans with the management team for the Instructor staff</li>
<li>Responsible for writing and/or approving the schedule for the Instructor staff in partnership with the GM</li>
<li>Manage the efficiency and flow of the flight deck</li>
<li>Uphold all of iFLY's policies and procedures with the Instructor team</li>
<li>Standard Flight Instructor responsibilities</li>
</ul>
<p><br>
<b>Required Skills</b><b>/Who You Are</b><b>:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Demonstrate and aptitude for leadership and management</li>
<li>Top performer in up-sales</li>
<li>Excellent safety record</li>
<li>An IBA-rated Trainer</li>
<li>Embodies the iFLY culture</li>
<li>Must meet all Flight Instructor qualifications and currency requirements</li>
<li>Strong team player</li>
<li>Demonstrated commitment and dedication</li>
<li>Technically capable of operating the tunnel and it’s systems</li>
<li>A self-starter, detail-orientated and able to organize and prioritize</li>
<li>Tact, discretion, and good judgment are required to maintain positive relationships with co-workers and customers</li>
<li>Excellent oral and written communication skills</li>
<li>Basic computer skills</li>
<li>High-school diploma or equivalent</li>
<li>IBA Level IV Flight Instructor</li>
<li>IBA Level II or higher Trainer</li>
</ul>
<ul><li>If hired, the company will provide advanced and safety training on a regular basis</li></ul>
