<h2>Content Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>France Media Inc., an Atlanta-based multi-media publisher and conference producer, is seeking a content manager to coordinate digital content for our advertisers and to assist with marketing our conferences. The content manager will work with editorial and sales groups to execute advertiser campaigns, including sponsored content and sponsored email blasts. This position will also assist the conference division staff with marketing efforts, such as writing press releases, updating websites, and posting social media links.</p>
<p>The successful candidate will possess a bachelor's degree or higher in journalism, public relations, advertising, or marketing. Applicants must have strong writing and editing skills, as well as solid organizational habits. Pluses include experience with Wordpress, Photoshop, basic HTML knowledge and understanding of the commercial real estate industry.</p>
<p>Less than 10 percent travel may be required for the position. We offer full benefits, including health and dental insurance, and 401(k) program. Salary is commensurate with experience.</p>
<p>This is an office-based position, based in the Buckhead area of Atlanta. To apply, please send your cover letter, resume and salary requirements through the indeed link below. No phone calls regarding open positions are accepted.</p>
