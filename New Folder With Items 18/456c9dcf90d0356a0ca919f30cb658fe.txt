<p><b>Primary Responsibilities
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Promotes the use and sale of HeinzSeed tomato varieties throughout North America
</li>
<li>Manages dealer network throughout region or direct sales
</li>
<li>Co-ordinates the forecasting and ordering of seed from assigned territory.
</li>
<li>Co-ordinates variety trialing and evaluation with dealers.
</li>
<li>Co-ordinates export-import procedures with customer service in California.
</li>
<li>Promotes and markets HeinzSeed varieties to Dealers by planting commercial sales trials and strip trials for harvest in all growing areas of the region. Seed samples are also provided to Dealers for their own experimental trials.
</li>
<li>Provides Dealers with marketing information, grower guidelines and seed samples.
</li>
<li>Visits Dealers/Customers and personally informs them of the advantages of using HeinzSeed varieties.
</li>
<li>Conducts demonstration and production trials for growers and processors during season.
</li>
<li>Forecasts regional sales 2 years in advance for production, annually and quarterly for budgeting.
</li>
<li>Obtains sales orders from Dealers.
</li>
<li>Recommends price change annually.
</li>
<li>Reports regional market share annually.
</li>
<li>Determines the best cultural practices for the new varieties.
</li>
<li>Recommends and develops regional advertising copy in cooperation with HeinzSeed dealer.
</li>
<li>Coordinates visitor programs in territory.
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Qualifications
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Minimum B.S. in Agronomy or related field, M.S. preferred;
</li>
<li>5 Year sales experience in agriculture related field.
</li>
<li>Good English speaking and writing capabilities, multi-lingual preferred;
</li>
<li>Experience in scientific research and/or experimental design;
</li>
<li>Willingness to travel extensively, not averse to rugged conditions.
</li>
<li>Computer: competent with Microsoft Office
</li>
<li>Analysis: correlation, root cause and effect.
</li>
<li>Organization: set and comply with milestones and deadlines
</li>
<li>Communication: clear, concise and organized writing and speaking skills
</li>
<li>Familiarity with Microsoft Dynamics 365
</li>
<li>Able to work independently, acting with sense of urgency and as an owner
</li>
</ul>
