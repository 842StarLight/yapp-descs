<h2>Assistant Buyer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<div>SKILLS</div>
<ul>
<li>Multitask / time management</li>
<li>Proficient in Microsoft Excel, Word, Outlook</li>
<li>Attention to detail</li>
<li>Good communication skills</li>
<li>Organizational skills</li>
<li>Team oriented</li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<div>JOB DESCRIPTION ITEMS</div>
<div></div>
<ul>
<li>Place and maintain Purchase Orders via ERP system</li>
<li>Tracking Purchase Orders and ensuring timely delivery</li>
<li>Setting up delivery appointments to warehouses</li>
<li>Placing intercompany transfers from warehouses across U.S. and Canada</li>
<li>Maintain good vendor relations</li>
<li>Checking invoices, packing lists, bill of ladings, and other shipping documents for accuracies</li>
<li>Communicating and confirming information with vendors domestically and overseas</li>
<li>Communicating with warehouse personnel across U.S. and Canada about product stock issues including but not limited to backorders, warranty issues, and substitutions.</li>
<li>Supplying support and assistance to import team, including any additionally assigned tasks</li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<div>HELPFUL KNOWLEDGE
</div>
<ul>
<li>Purchasing / Purchasing assistant or support (1 year preferred)</li>
<li>Understanding of Supply Chain Procedures</li>
<li>ERP software knowledge</li>
<li>Forecasting / Sales Analysis</li>
<li>Import Purchasing / international shipping</li>
<li>Trailer parts knowledge a plus</li>
<li>Maintains a positive work atmosphere by acting and communicating in a manner so that you work effectively and cooperatively with coworkers, customers, vendors, and leadership team</li>
<li>Participate in process improvement and problem solving utilizing continuous improvement and rational thinking skills</li>
<li>Must be self-motivated to perform simultaneous tasks without close continuous supervision</li>
</ul>
<div>OTHER INFO</div>
<ul>
<li>May be asked to work overtime (&lt;5% per fiscal year)</li>
<li>May be asked to travel if needed for company business (&lt;2% per fiscal year)</li>
<li>Will be required to sit long periods of time (&gt;60% per day)</li>
<li>Will be required to help count during physical yearly inventories (local only)</li>
</ul>
