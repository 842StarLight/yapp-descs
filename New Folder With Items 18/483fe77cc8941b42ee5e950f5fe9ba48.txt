<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Mission</b></h3>
<p>The Business Development professional will be an integral partner responsible for implementing key strategies for growing construction business in the commercial and industrial sectors. This position is responsible for acquiring new clients, maintaining existing relationships and promoting the brand of S. M. Wilson in both the commercial and industrial markets as well as the local community. The Business Development professional will help position the company to be selected by our target clients and evaluate business opportunities to enhance our financial success.</p>
