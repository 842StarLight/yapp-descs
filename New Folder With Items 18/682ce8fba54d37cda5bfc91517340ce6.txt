The world's leading private security organization, G4S, has an immediate job opportunity for a Recruiting Manager. As a Recruiting Manager, you will be responsible for the process of recruiting non-exempt personnel at the field office level. In addition, ensures that field office interviewing, advertising and selection processes comply with company’s EEO and Affirmative Action policies and applicable federal, state, and local laws.
<br><br>
The Recruiting Manager will actively recruit, host job fairs, open houses, hiring events, and connect with outreach organizations. Primary responsibilities of include actively recruiting to fill critical staffing needs of G4S Secure Solutions USA throughout the designated area(s) by proactively sourcing, screening and qualifying mid-level managers, security supervisors, security personnel and other positions as assigned.
<br><br>
G4S is a security provider for the United States government, fortune 500 companies, nuclear power plants, oil and gas companies, airport, ports, banks, hospitals, factories, warehouses, commercial facilities, residential communities and much more.

Main Responsibilities
<br>Specific Duties and Essential Functions
<br><br>
Screening and interviewing mid-level management, supervisors and security personnel job applicants
<br>Sourcing and recruiting mid-level management, supervisors and security personnel job applicants
<br>Track and maintain necessary data for compliance with OFCCP and EEO candidate data requirements
<br>Track and maintain necessary data to support candidate recommendations, summaries and debriefs
<br>Assist in creation of new job descriptions and advertisement requests
<br>Manage the Applicant Tracking System for designated areas as assigned
<br>Periodically assist with personnel onboarding, to include new hire orientation
<br>Actively seek out, organize and participate in job fairs, career expo, hiring events etc. and document all Outreach Efforts to comply with AAP reporting
<br>Develop and maintain outreach relationships and contacts with Veterans and Minority Organizations and Agencies
<br>Actively engage with Operations to identify staffing needs
<br>Oversee the recruiting of security personnel for start-ups for various local and national clients
<br>Approving purchase of recruiting advertisements (online and in print) and outreach programs within budgetary restrictions
<br>Approve methods for implementing Employee Referral/Bonus Program (if applicable)
<br>Ensure that field office advertising, interviewing, hiring and selection processes are in compliance with the company’s EEO and Affirmative Action policies, and applicable federal, state and local laws
<br>Direct supervision of Recruiting Specialist roles
