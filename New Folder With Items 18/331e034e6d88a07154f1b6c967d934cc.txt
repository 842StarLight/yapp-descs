<h2>Solutions Engineer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">We are seeking a Solutions Engineer with a strong executive presence and who thrives in a fast-paced, dynamic environment to cover our West Coast territory. Our Solutions Engineers handle product demonstrations and pre-sales support for the worldwide sales organization and reports directly to the Vice President of Solutions Engineering. The ideal candidate will possess a solid technical understanding of multiple Enterprise Resource Management (ERP) systems, be able to speak to and understand financial reporting challenges faced by companies of all sizes and scopes today, be able to write SQL based queries and reports, and have advanced Microsoft Excel skills. Also, this role has the capacity to work remotely.
<br><br>
What will you do?
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Use knowledge of Reporting solutions to assess prospect requirements</li>
<li>Work with Account Executives (Sales) to articulate how insightsoftware solutions solve specific business problems</li>
<li>Deliver online and in person product demonstrations</li>
<li>Travel for on-site presentations and conferences</li>
<li>Proof of Concept installations and product training as part of sales process</li>
<li>Assist clients in installing and deploying insightsoftware solutions when needed</li>
<li>Build relationships to become a trusted solution advisor</li>
</ul>
How will you do it?
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Drive &amp; Discipline – ability to focus and work hard to achieve the right results</li>
<li>Integrity – high ethical standards and doing the right thing even when others aren't looking</li>
<li>Mental agility – a keen intellect and comfort with complexity; enjoy tackling new challenges and solving problems</li>
<li>Detail-orientation – be thorough and consistently deliver high quality work</li>
</ul>
Are you a fit?
<br><br>
<b>Minimum qualifications:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>CPA or Bachelor's Degree in Finance, Accounting, Computer Science, Information Technology or related discipline</li>
<li>3+ years Accounting or Finance Business Analyst or relevant industry experience</li>
<li>Understand financial and month-end reporting requirements</li>
<li>Understanding of Enterprise Resource Planning from a functional perspective</li>
<li>Familiarity with advanced Excel capabilities and functions</li>
<li>Strong written and oral communication skills</li>
<li>Ability to work independently as well as in a team environment and be able to communicate effectively with co-workers and customers who have varying levels of functional and technical expertise</li>
</ul>
