Job Summary</b>
</div>
<div>
<p>We are seeking an experienced <b>Principal Scientist 3 (In-Vivo Pharmacokinetics) </b>for our <b>Discovery </b>site located in <b>Worcester, MA.</b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>We are seeking a highly skilled, passionate and energetic In-Vivo PK Scientist to provide scientific leadership for all technical aspects of studies. The qualified candidate will work closely with our clients and our established teams in the design, conduct, analysis, interpretation and reporting of a wide variety of in-vivo PK studies in a fast paced and dynamic environment. Responsibilities of this position include extensive interaction and communication with client scientists in the discovery research area of PK screening, lead optimization and candidate selection.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>The following are minimum requirement related to the <b>Principal Scientist 3</b> postion.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor's degree (B.S./B.A.) or equivalent in a scientific related discipline. Related Master's degree (M.S./M.A.) or PhD/DVM preferred.</li>
<li>Minimum 9 years related experience in the contract research, academic or pharmaceutical industry.</li>
<li>An equivalent combination of education and experience may be accepted as a satisfactory substitute for the specific education and experience listed above.</li>
<li>Understand of regulatory requirements of study types assigned, as well as Testing Facility SOPs and the Good Laboratory Practices (GLPs), as appropriate. Demonstrate leadership skills as they pertain to intra- and interdepartmental initiatives, Sponsor interactions, and business development.</li>
<li>Effective written and verbal communication skills. Ability to handle multiple projects, prioritize work and meet deadlines.</li>
<li>Proficiency in the use of standard software including Microsoft® Excel, Word, Powerpoint, etc. and with standard laboratory calculations.</li>
<li>Proficiency with NCA using Phoenix WinNonlin software.</li>
</ul>
