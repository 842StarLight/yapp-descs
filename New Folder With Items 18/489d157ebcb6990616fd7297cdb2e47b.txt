<div>The Service Specialist Lead – Payroll Systems, will be responsible for the delivery and improvement of payroll systems, and requires the use of specialized knowledge and skills, including payroll, systems management configuration and integration. Primary responsibilities include systems IT project leadership, Configuration, Design, Documentation, Planning and Testing. This role requires a combination of process and technology expertise. This role will report directly to the Global Service Owner.<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<div>Responsibilities:</div>
<ul>
<li>Lead Design and Implementation for Projects and Improvement utilizing your specialized payroll systems knowledge.
</li>
<li>Follow J&amp;J and industry standard policies and procedures (e.g. SDLC, Agile, IAPP, etc.).
</li>
<li>Participate in the detailed design of payroll technology solutions.</li>
<li>
Drive positive partnerships with other IT organizations on requirements and design.</li>
<li>
Provide creative options to meet requirements.</li>
<li>
Build and maintain valuable application documentation.</li>
<li>
Lead projects/improvements in an Agile manner.</li>
<li>
Lead change control for software changes.</li>
<li>
Partner with Testing, SQA, CSV and ITRA services to ensure requirements are met.</li>
<li>
Aid in the coordination of release planning.</li>
<li>
Engage with IT and business partners to ensure accurate testing is planned appropriately for solution.</li>
<li>
Ensure the appropriate support model is in place post-production.</li>
</ul>
