<p>Development &amp; Marketing Associate</p>
<p><b>POSITION OVERVIEW </b></p>
<p>Reporting to the Director of Development, Development &amp; Marketing Associate will be a key member of the organization’s team helping to drive fundraising and communication strategy and growth as it begins its second quarter century of impact.</p>
<p><b>Development </b></p>
<ul><li>Work closely with the Director of Development to develop and implement an annual comprehensive fundraising strategy that includes prospects, individual donors, corporations, foundations, and events.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Develop and implement all aspects of direct donor solicitations and communications for various channels (print, social media, web, mail, email, video).</li></ul>
<ul><li>Develop and support strategies and activities to ensure proper acknowledgement, recognition, and stewardship of donors.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Assist Director of Development with communicating with members of the Board of Directors around key meetings, cultivation efforts and other fundraising priorities.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Remain current on best practices for fundraising efforts, guidelines and procedures used in the nonprofit sector to guide and inform the program’s development strategic goals.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Perform other related duties as needed and directed by Director of Development.</li></ul>
<p><b>Marketing &amp; Communications </b></p>
<ul><li>With Director of Development and Programming leads, develop year-long content for various marketing efforts and platforms. o Maintain RFL website (wordpress) o Maintain and manage communications calendar o Create, implement, manage social media plan</li></ul>
<ul><li>Manage production of online and collateral materials/publications (annual report, newsletters, blog, event programs).</li></ul>
<p><b>Events </b></p>
<ul><li>Support signature fundraising event Banned in Boston to ensure successful guest experience and development goals met.</li></ul>
<p>This includes, liaison with Event Planning firm, Guest Registration, Implementation of special event software</p>
<ul><li>Attend and support evening events as needed.</li></ul>
<p>This position requires passion for creative youth development. Rehearsal for Life is an equal opportunity employer.</p>
<p>Position status: Exempt, full-time<br>Hours: 8:30am – 4:30pm</p>
<p>Job Location: Jamaica Plain, Massachusetts, United States</p>
