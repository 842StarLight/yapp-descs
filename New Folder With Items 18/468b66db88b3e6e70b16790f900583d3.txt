<p><b>
Executive Search Coordinator Opportunity</b></p>
<p>
HARTZ Search has an opening for a professional who is driven, possesses strong internal motivation, excellent communication skills, a strong work ethic, flexibility, self-confidence, detail-oriented with a high sense of urgency and multi-tasking abilities. We are seeking a dynamic and proven professional with a sales industry or recruitment background. This position offers a tremendous growth opportunity with our company through training and ingenuity.</p>
<p>
Initially, the Executive Search Coordinator will be expected to perform recruitment operations including – sourcing, screening resumes and applications, processing candidates throughout the search process, providing a point of contact for candidates generated and maintaining detailed candidate and client records.</p>
<p>
HARTZ Search is an industry leader in Executive &amp; Interim Leadership recruitment. We are a boutique firm, growing quickly and in search of fun, hard-working, zealous providers of customer service - focused on search coordination duties and administrative assistant duties.</p>
<p><b>
The Executive Search Coordinator should possess the following attributes:</b>
</p>

<li>
Competitive, goal-oriented, confident</li>
<li>
Task-Oriented balanced with sociable</li>
<li>
Work well in a team environment and strategize with teammates to accomplish weekly goals.</li>
<li>
Be a highly motivated, self-starter, go-getter that possesses an entrepreneurial spirit.</li>
<li>
Present a polished and professional image</li>
<li>
Sense of humor</li>

Don't be a jerk!</li>

Qualifications:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
We seek someone with strong verbal and written communication skills, and</li>
<li>
Superior drive and motivation.</li>

Associates Degree or bachelor’s degree preferred, Undecided Welcomed as well</li>
<li>
Knowledge of the healthcare recruiting industry and/or experience in recruiting or sales is preferred.</li>
<li>
