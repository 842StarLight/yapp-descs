<p><b>Essential Duties and Responsibilities (Other duties may be assigned.)</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Responsible for the accurate and timely preparation of vouchers for all appropriately signed vendor invoices properly submitted for payment.</li>
<li>Ensures that invoices are properly prepared and paid within the terms of payment required by the vendor.</li>
<li>Ensures that invoices presented for payment are properly signed by the cognizant cost center manager, and that appropriate guidance is annotated so that general ledger codes, if not already noted on the invoice, can be assigned.</li>
<li>Ensures that invoices not signed or otherwise properly prepared for processing are returned to the cognizant cost center manager expeditious for processing and resubmission.</li>
<li>Employee expense reports are received with appropriate approvals. Enters into AP system with general ledger codes. Expense reports must be entered and sent to payroll per schedule.</li>
<li>Responsible for the maintenance of merchandise costing. Vendor invoices are compared to product master and maintenance performed when necessary.</li>
<li>Responsible for the accurate and timely mailing of vendor payments.</li>
<li>Responsible for maintaining vendor files in an accurate, timely and orderly manner.</li>
<li>Responds to vendor inquiries regarding payment schedules; refers inquiries from “sensitive” vendor accounts to the A/P Manager or Controller as specifically directed by the A/P Manager.</li>
</ul>
