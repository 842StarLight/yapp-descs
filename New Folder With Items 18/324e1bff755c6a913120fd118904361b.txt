<h2>Associate Director, Branded Partnerships</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>Job Description<br>
<br>
<p>The Producer, Branded Partnerships will help drive the creative process of advertiser programs and campaigns from beginning to end: responding to RFPs, researching, brainstorming, formulating content strategy, and working with various internal and external teams to produce content and programs that resonate with our audiences and achieves clients’ goals/kpis. They will recognize which content — from written articles to social media campaigns to long-form video and everything between — or program elements will be the most effective to deliver on advertiser kpis.They will also provide leadership to the team, spearhead innovations across platforms and expertlynavigate client communications.</p>
<p>
A background as a producer (campaigns or branded content) is required, along with a positive track record of developing impactful programs, writing and ideating compelling RFPs/writing proposals, skillfully and profitably producting and executing campaigns at either a publisher or a creative agency is required, along with a drive to monetize partnership combinations and content and satisfying advertiser objectives.</p>
<p><b>
External Focus:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Ability to craft creative and monetizable concepts for advertisers across digital, print, social, mobile, events, and experiential.</li>
<li>Develop relationships with existing, and new clients, and with their ad agencies</li>
<li>Develop unique strategies (short and long term) to meet sales goals and build viable customer base in designated categories and/or territories.</li>
<li>Adapt to changes in industries by benchmarking, (assisting in) innovating our capabilities and improving our processes.</li>
<li>Have context to our pricing structures when communicating with clients</li>
<li>Create value ‘on-the-fly’ in client meetings</li>
<li>Stay acutely aware of program details throughout the value creation/execution process</li>
<li>Adept in pivoting programs while assisting the team in re-pitching the revised elements successfully to clients and agencies.</li>
</ul>
<ul><li>
Understand our brands/platforms and how combinations can provide value to advertisers</li></ul>
<ul>

</ul>
