<h2>Business Development Assistant</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Brooks Applied Labs (BAL) a growing analytical chemistry laboratory in Bothell, Washington specializing in trace metals analysis and elemental speciation, is looking for an outgoing, customer-service oriented individual with exceptional organizational and communication skills to join our team as a Business Development (BD) Assistant. The BD Assistant supports inside sales by providing quotations and information to potential clients and ensures the efficient operations of the BD Team by providing stellar administrative support and customer service.</p>
<p>This is the ideal role for someone with laboratory experience who would like to apply their knowledge of analytical chemistry in a customer-facing role. The successful candidate is a self-starter with a science background, a strong commitment to customer service, an ability to identify and implement process efficiencies, and the skills to communicate professionally and effectively with internal and external customers.</p>
<p><b>Job responsibilities include, but are not limited to: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Setting up new client folders and initiating draft quotes for incoming requests</li>
<li>Managing records in Customer Relationship Management software (CRM), including data entry of quotations and customer information into the CRM</li>
<li>Generating a variety of reports from that CRM that are used throughout the organization</li>
<li>Fielding web and phone information and quotation requests</li>
<li>Generation of quotations and informative emails for potential clients</li>
<li>Following-up with potential clients by email and phone</li>
<li>Assisting BD Team on proposal development</li>
<li>Working with Graphic Designer on marketing-related tasks</li>
<li>Pro-actively seek out ways to improve process efficiency in support of the BD Team</li>
<li>Some infrequent travel may be required to attend national trade shows as an exhibitor for BALâ€™s display booth</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Required Qualifications &amp; Competencies: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Minimum Associates Degree in a physical science or related field with 2 years of laboratory coursework, or equivalent professional experience in a laboratory setting.</li>
<li>Proven ability to provide outstanding customer service to internal and external clients</li>
<li>Aptitude and enthusiasm related to sales and marketing</li>
<li>Able to speak confidently and professionally with clients over the phone, email, and in-person.</li>
<li>Exceptional attention to detail, the ability to work under pressure, and prioritize multiple tasks to ensure deadlines are met.</li>
<li>Proficient in Microsoft Office products (Word, Excel, PowerPoint, Outlook, etc.)</li>
<li>Positive attitude and ability to work with teams and independently</li>
<li>Must be legally able to work in the U.S.</li>
</ul>
<p>Preference give to candidates with:</p>
<ul>
<li>Experience with Microsoft Dynamics or other CRM software</li>
<li>BA/BS degree in a physical science</li>
<li>Experience working in an analytical laboratory and/or with methods for metals analysis</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Location, Hours &amp; Compensation: </b></p>
<p>Job duties are expected to be performed at our office in Bothell, WA. Relocation expenses are not reimbursable. Compensation for this entry-level business development position is dependent on sales and laboratory experience, with a range of $16 - $20/hour. This is a full-time (Monday-Friday, 40 hours per week), hourly (non-exempt; eligible for overtime) position with full benefits including medical/dental/vision insurance, generous PTO, 401k matching, profit-sharing, and significant opportunities for growth. Please see our website (<i>www.brooksapplied.com</i>) for more information.</p>
