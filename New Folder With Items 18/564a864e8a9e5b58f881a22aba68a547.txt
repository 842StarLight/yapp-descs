The Opportunity
<br><br>
As we continue our expansion into new markets as well as further deepen our footprint in existing markets, One Medical is hiring a talented enterprise sales professional. Reporting to our Enterprise Sales leader, this individual will have a consistent track record of building relationships and closing deals with employer groups of 500+ ranging in size from $50,000 up to $500,000 or more. Experience leveraging existing relationships and building new ones, both internally and externally, will be paramount to success, and this individual should demonstrate a high degree of ownership and accountability.
<br><br>
The ideal candidate is a high achiever who has a thirst for perfecting his/her craft. In this role, you will have exposure to and work closely with senior leadership at One Medical as well as multiple levels of leadership with potential prospects and clients. With a continuous drive for results and a strong sense of accountability, this individual values hard work to achieve sales targets and personal goals. S/he will be resourceful, leveraging internal structures, processes and relationships to achieve the common goal of growth as One Medical continues to evolve. The successful candidate is motivated by and thrives in an autonomous environment, taking ownership of the sales process - from prospecting to process to closing of deals.
<br><br>

<ul>
