<h2>Software Outside Sales Representative</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Quest Software is a worldwide provider of information technology services and business solutions to a broad range of clients. We are currently looking for a Outside Regional Account Manager for the Foglight for Databases tool. This position can be based anywhere in California and works remote.</p>
<p>Quest Software is an award-winning IT management software provider offering a broad selection of solutions that solve some of the most common and most challenging IT problems. Foglight bolsters Quest’s competitive position in delivering premier database performance tools.</p>
<p>This is a field sales position responsible for selling Quest’s Foglight for Databases solutions to Key Regional Accounts. Individuals in this role should possess a depth of Enterprise Software and Best of Breed sales experience within the Fortune 1000 enabling them to comfortably discuss business and technology issues with low, mid and high-level IT and business managers within the marketplace.</p>
<p>Responsibilities</p>
<ul>
<li>Prospecting, finding, developing and closing opportunities through the entire Sales Cycle</li>
<li>Developing and maintaining relationships with key decision-makers</li>
<li>Creating and implementing account strategy</li>
<li>Recognizing customer business problems and driving / influencing resources to address opportunities</li>
<li>Obtaining customer commitment and delivering a total enterprise solution</li>
<li>Maintaining current revenue and focusing on identifying new opportunities to increase market share and revenue growth</li>
<li>Coordinating and communicating with various internal resources in Pre-sales, Contracts and Post-Sales</li>
<li>Working with Channel Partners and other Quest resources to increase opportunity size and expedite closure</li>
<li>Strong written and oral communication skills, including cold calls, proposal preparation presentation and selling skills: Opening, probing, supporting, closing and overcome customer indifference</li>
<li>Knowledge of solution and value-based selling techniques</li>
<li>Ability to sell to high-level executives using articulate value propositions</li>
<li>Strong teamwork skills are required to successfully work in a highly-matrixed environment</li>
<li>Up to 50% of travel.</li>
</ul>
