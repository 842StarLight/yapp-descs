<h2>Technical Representative (Commercial/Industrial Roofing)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>CentiMark Corporation, the National leader in the commercial/industrial roofing industry, has an exceptional opportunity for a full time <b>Roofing Technical Representative </b>to support sales reps in our <b>Westland, MI</b> office.</p>
<p><b>Summary of Job Description:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Perform project take-offs from Architect/Construction Spec Drawings.</li>
<li>Prepare project submittals for specified contract projects.</li>
<li>Travel to customer's locations and inspect roofs.</li>
<li>Communicate with customers and check roofs for problem areas.</li>
<li>Measures roofs and estimate the amount of roofing material and labor needed to re-roof.</li>
<li>Will work closely with the sales force to provide them with accurate information for the creation of proposals for customers.</li>
<li>Inspect roofs for the service department and assist the operations department in quoting small repair jobs.</li>
<li>Will need to travel out of town occasionally.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Job Requirements:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Organizational, Analytical, Problem Solving and People Skills.</li>
<li>Understanding of new construction and specification driven bidding processes.</li>
<li>Ability to read/interpret architectural drawings and perform project take offs.</li>
<li>Ability to compile and prepare project submittals.</li>
<li>Ability to track project change orders.</li>
<li>Able to complete project closeout documentation as required.</li>
<li>Computer skills (proficient in MS Word, Excel and Bid Estimating Software)</li>
<li>General Construction, Roofing and/or Sales Experience (1 year)</li>
<li>A Valid driver's license in good standing</li>
<li>Able to climb up &amp; down ladders to minimum heights of 25 feet</li>
<li>Experience with: THERMOPLASTIC, BUR, METAL, SHINGLES, TILE, SPF FOAM and COATINGS but will train the right individual.</li>
</ul>
