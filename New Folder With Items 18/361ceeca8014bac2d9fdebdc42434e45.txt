<h2>Assistant IT Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>The Assistant IT Manager is responsible for the overall planning, organizing and execution of all IT related functions. This position ensures the maintenance and continuity of computer services for computer users throughout the hotel property through planning and project coordination.</p>
<p>Duties and Responsibilities:</p>
<ul>
<li>Manage the day to day activities of the Information Technology function, plan and organize work</li>
<li>IT support and maintenance of systems, systems configuration, hardware maintenance, troubleshooting and problem resolution</li>
<li>Ensures cleanliness of all data equipment and computer equipment</li>
<li>Assist employees with PC problems and requests, remote and local</li>
<li>Assist the senior management in ongoing assessment and upgrading of IT systems</li>
<li>Keep systems current with software upgrades, added functionality and programs</li>
<li>Provide analytical support including status report generation for management</li>
<li>Analyzes business needs presented by the user community and/or guests and recommends technical solutions</li>
<li>Continually keep informed of approved systems and technology that may contribute to improve hotel efficiency, including, but not limited to: cabling systems, personal computer and software, electronic key systems, Accounting systems, and other systems</li>
<li>Maintain an up-to-date inventory of all computer hardware</li>
<li>Manage the configuration reports, job control languages, program files and data files on the computer system(s) to ensure maximum operating efficiency</li>
<li>Monitor the performance of the software and maintain a log book of performance report e.g., through systems measurement facility/utility, error and integrity check reports, system malfunctions and solutions</li>
<li>Install and test corporate approved program changes to the hotel computer system(s)</li>
<li>Understand all standard and customized features and functions of the hotel front office systems, point of sale systems, and other systems, including but not limited to system security, system reports, manager functions, system utilities, and user functions</li>
<li>Designate selected individuals for each system to train so that these individuals can train other users</li>
<li>Establish documents, test, and communicate appropriate emergency procedures to follow when the hotel computer system(s) are inoperable</li>
<li>Responsible for overseeing and handling network configuration, Servers, Workstations, Networking equipment, PBX, email accounts, and also support for the underlying server/desktop infrastructure</li>
<li>Ensure that all computer media saves and back-ups are completed, documented and stored per JP Allen Inc. specifications</li>
<li>Control all user IDs, passwords, and security parameters</li>
<li>Promote teamwork and quality service through daily communication and coordination with other shifts and departmental management</li>
<li>Interact with outside contacts:</li>
<li>Guests – to ensure their total satisfaction</li>
<li>Vendors – to arrange services</li>
<li>Regulatory agencies – regarding safety and emergency matters</li>
<li>Other contacts as needed (professional organizations, community groups)</li>
<li>Perform other duties as assigned</li>
</ul>
<p>Minimum Qualifications:</p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science, Business Administration or a related field and a minimum of five (5) years of systems experience</li>
<li>An equivalent combination of education and experience may be accepted</li>
<li>Working knowledge of local area networks and Microsoft Windows</li>
<li>Excellent communication, reading and writing skills for various customer interactions and reporting</li>
<li>May be required to work nights, weekends, and/or holidays</li>
<li>Experience working in a hospitality environment</li>
<li>Courteous and professional guest interaction is a must</li>
</ul>
<p>Preferred Qualifications:</p>
<ul>
<li>Bilingual (English/Spanish)</li>
<li>Mathematical skills, including basic math, budgeting, profit/loss concepts, percentages, and variances</li>
</ul>
<p>Physical Requirements:</p>
<ul>
<li>Ability to move and lift large, bulky and/or heavy objects up to 50 pounds</li>
<li>Ability to perform tasks that may require prolonged standing, sitting, repetitive bending, and other physical activities</li>
</ul>
