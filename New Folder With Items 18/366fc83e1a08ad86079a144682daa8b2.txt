<h2>Ad Operations/Yield Manager/Account Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">

<div>Kika is looking for an Ad Operations/Account Manager to join the Media/Advertising team. The Account Manager is responsible for helping to scale the media/advertising team through direct and indirect revenue channels, ensuring delivery and performance of advertising campaigns and 3rd party monetization channels. As a part of this small and growing team, the Ad Operations Manager will have the opportunity to be rewarded for growth and revenue performance. The scope includes, but not limited to, reporting, analytics, sales process, and cross-functional initiative support. This is an individual contributor role.</div>
