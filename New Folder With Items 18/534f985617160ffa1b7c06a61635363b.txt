<p><b>About the Role
</b></p>
<p>Intermedia is looking for a contract eLearning designer/developer to assist our Sales &amp; Channel Readiness Team. The Sales &amp; Channel Readiness Team is responsible for training and enabling both our internal Sales Teams as well as our growing Partner community. You’ll create effective product-specific eLearning content and experiences for our audience.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b><i>As an eLearning designer and developer, you’ll:
</i></b></p>
<ul>
<li>Build courses using Articulate 360 (primarily Rise 360) under our design direction Develop actionable and measurable learning objectives for the courses you create
</li>
<li>Quickly learn the assigned product(s) and identify the appropriate level of detail required for our Sales and Partner audiences
</li>
<li>Work efficiently, effectively and amiably with subject matter experts across the organization
</li>
<li>Work both independently and collaboratively
</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Required:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>3+ years of experience in instructional design and/or development for eLearning
</li>
<li>Demonstrated understanding of adult learning principles
</li>
<li>Examples of engaging eLearning you’ve built
</li>
<li>Ability to self-direct and work independently effectively
</li>
<li>Ability to communicate effectively (written and verbal) and to build relationships at all levels in an organization
</li>
<li>Acute attention to detail
</li>
<li>Ability to work well with SMEs to quickly learn new information, then use it to turn out beautiful, effective courses
</li>
<li>A positive attitude and a passion for learning
</li>
<li>Ability to adapt according to changing business needs
</li>
<li>Expertise with Articulate applications, especially Rise 360 and Storyline 360
</li>
<li>Experience with Camtasia or other video editing software
</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Preferred:</b>
</p>
<ul>
<li>Tech industry experience (especially telecom, software, SaaS)
</li>
<li>Sales and/or Sales Training experience</li>
</ul>
