<h2>Customer Advocacy Program Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<div><b>Customer Success Advocacy Manager
</b></div>
<div><b>Location: Hoboken, NJ<br>
</b></div>
<div></div>
<p>The Customer Success Advocacy Manager (CSAM) is at the heart of Wiley’s customer centric culture. This role is responsible for identifying, recruiting, and mobilizing advocates of Wiley’s digital products. The CSAM will lead and manage customer engagement and communications programs that help marketing, sales, and product management meet business objectives through customer advocacy initiatives.
</p>
<p>Successful candidates will have experience in building customer nurture, community, and reference programs. They will also have a passion for identifying key customers that can serve as a marketing channel through case studies, testimonials, speaking opportunities, and more. You will enjoy this role if you like working with fast-paced teams, leading cross-functionally, and having autonomy to drive your initiatives.
</p>
<p>You will be a part of our team which includes Customer Education, Product Implementation, and Community Management. This role reports directly to the VP of Customer Success.
</p>
<p><b>What You'll Do:
</b></p>
<p>Build and execute a comprehensive customer engagement and advocacy strategy to achieve adoption, retention, sales, marketing, and product development goals.
</p>
<p>Work to develop Wiley’s customer reference program, including library of customer success stories, videos, and blogs.
</p>
<p>Work closely with our sales &amp; marketing team to identify customer reference prospects. Liaise with customers to develop content.
</p>
<p>Lead the strategy for continued growth and engagement on our online customer communities, powered by Influitive
</p>
<p>Develop and execute a Customer Advisory Board program to inform Wiley’s pricing &amp; product strategy
</p>
<p>Organize and manage the strategy for two annual Customer Town Halls
</p>
<p><b>What You Will Need:
</b></p>
<p>5+ years in either a marketing or customer success function.
</p>
<p>Excellent written and verbal communication skills.
</p>
<p>Natural inclination to think creatively and apply design thinking.
</p>
<p>Ability to effectively influence both internal and external stakeholders across all levels of the organization.
</p>
<p>Ability to manage multiple projects simultaneously and navigate unforeseen obstacles gracefully.
</p>
<p>Results oriented with experience using qualitative and quantitative metrics with a focus on revenue, conversion, sales, and other marketing goals.
</p>
<p>Deep curiosity, excellent listening skills, empathy, and ability to get things done in a collaborative team environment.
</p>
<p>Experience using Salesforce dashboards and reports.
</p>
<p>A passion for Wiley’s mission of helping teachers teach and students learn.
</p>
<p><b>Desirable </b>:
</p>
<p>MBA, customer advocacy certification, customer experience certification
</p>
<p>2+ years higher education sales or marketing experience
</p>
<p>Experience with Customer Advocacy Management software
</p>

Which means you can look forward to a robust health and wellness package, competitive salary and incentive program, generous paid time off, staff discounts, an employee assistance program.
</p>
