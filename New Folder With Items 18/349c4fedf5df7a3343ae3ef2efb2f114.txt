<h2>Salesforce Developer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Position Description</b></p>
<p>
Key activities for the role include but are not limited to: For various projects and enhancements, execute all configuration / code work to implement custom application development, assist with developing related Salesforce reporting and analytics requirements, and deploy to production the changes/ enhancements.</p>
<p>
The position requires detailed knowledge of and experience shaping the SFDC environment to achieve business objectives.</p>
<p><b>
Job Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Comprehensive SFDC knowledge
</li>
<li>Work with SFDC team, business units and IT teams (as needed), to build solutions in salesforce.com that support business requirements and processes for continuous improvement.</li>
<li>Configuration and development including but not limited to, Apex code and supporting test classes, Custom Objects, Workflow Rules, Custom Fields, Validation Rules, and Visual Force Pages.</li>
<li>Perform administration tasks as needed related to SFDC, including but not limited to:
<ul>
<li>User management (activation, deactivation, password support)</li>
<li>Security (roles, profiles, sharing roles, global security settings)</li>
<li>Configuration (field set-up, page layouts, record types, assignment rules)</li>
<li>Workflow and approval management</li>
<li>Communication templates
</li>
<li>Analytics (reports, dashboards)</li>
<li>AppExchange (apps and plug-ins)</li>
<li>Data management exercises to assure (data loads, transfer, cleansing)</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>Provide user support to troubleshoot individual issues and logging support issues to identify trends and topics requiring escalation and feature / enhancements.</li>
<li>Work with IT to supports ongoing release management and rollout with new configuration</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Qualifications</b></p>
<ul>
<li>A minimum of 5-7 years of progressive responsibility in Sales Force Automation on the SFDC platform, including advanced workflows and complex configurations (i.e. non-coding) tasks</li>
<li>Experience in designing and developing processes in a highly agile environment
</li>
<li>Working knowledge of Visualforce, Apex, and Informatica Cloud or other ETL tools</li>
<li>Detailed knowledge of Salesforce.com data modeling</li>
<li>Experience using Salesforce.com data tools (Data Loader)</li>
<li>Demand Tools experience and knowledge a plus</li>
<li>Strong communication skills: oral, written, and presentation, plus the ability to communicate with a wide range of people and behavior styles.</li>
<li>Ability to learn quickly and operate in a fast paced environment</li>
<li>A self-starter, confident in his/her abilities, self-motivated and able to work effectively with little supervision.
</li>
<li>A results-oriented individual who can make a difference; a person who takes initiative, makes things happen, accepts accountability, &amp; has a "can do" attitude; sense of urgency.
</li>
</ul>
