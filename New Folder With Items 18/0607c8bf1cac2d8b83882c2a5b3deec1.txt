<h2>Global Pricing Analyst</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div><div><div>
<div>
<div>At Fossil, we dare to dream, disrupt, and deliver in a better way. Our goal is simple - bring innovation, style, and connectivity to an industry ripe for change. Fossil is on a mission to revamp the way fashion accessories are done. We are committed to creating great watches, jewelry, handbags, small leather goods and wearables by investing in technology and long-term value creation. With our diverse portfolio of proprietary and licensed brands, along with department stores, specialty stores, eCommerce websites, and company-owned and operated retail stores, we are building a leading fashion- and tech-forward accessories company. Are you in?</div>
</div>
<div>
<p><b>Make an Impact</b></p>
<div>As a Pricing Analyst, you will be a critical member of the Fossil Pricing team whose objective is to drive financial performance (sales and margin) across all categories and brands through a best in class pricing management.<br>
<br>
In this role, you will use analysis of historic, industry, market, consumer and competitive data to drive pricing decisions and influence pricing strategies.<br>
<br>
Your specific responsibilities will include:<ul>
<li>
Analyzing and providing insights from ongoing pricing performance to achieve business goals</li>
<li>
Analyzing and providing insights from global and regional pricing trends, consumer trends, and competitive landscapes</li>
<li>
Analyzing and testing new and innovative pricing initiatives to generate competitive advantage</li>
<li>
Developing analytical capabilities of pricing performance using tools such as Tableau</li>
<li>
Creating management reporting as needed to assist in understanding of pricing performance</li>
<li>
Creating analysis and measurements to quantify the results of actions as it relates to price performance</li>
<li>
Conducting ad-hoc analyses to support key merchandising decisions/tests</li>
<li>
Developing best practices for analytical processes to support merchandising decisions</li>
</ul>
<br>
</div>
</div>

<div>
<p>Aligning with Fossil’s core values, you are someone who:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Develops strong partnerships across our organization</li>
<li>
Brings a positive, service-oriented approach to work</li>
<li>
Is driven by creating unique and efficient solutions for business challenges</li>
<li>
Can articulate thoughts clearly as well as listen to and considers others’ ideas</li>
<li>
Is passionate about their contribution to Fossil</li>
<li>
Has a great time at work and encourages others to do the same</li>
</ul>
<p>
Required Skills and Experience:</p>
<ul>
<li>
You have completed a degree in Business Administration</li>
<li>
You have 2-5 years of experience that includes Pricing, Trade Spend, Promotion Optimization, Category Management, Insights, Consulting, or related functions</li>
<li>
You have deep expertise in retail and/or CPG pricing. Proficiency with retail pricing optimization software packages a plus</li>
<li>
You have financial knowledge, including modeling, sensitivity analysis and end-to-end revenue recognition</li>
<li>
Your analytical, quantitative and problem-solving skills are outstanding</li>
<li>
You have deep expertise in Excel and Tableau. SQL or other query writing experience needed</li>
<li>
Data management experience is highly preferred</li>
<li>
You are driven and self-motivated</li>
</ul>
