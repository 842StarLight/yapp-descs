<h2>Full Time Receptionist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">We are seeking a Full Time Receptionist with excellent customer service skills and a winning attitude. This individual will receive calls into the dealership, determine the nature of the callers business, and direct the caller to their destination. This position will also be responsible for basic accounting skills and knowledge of routine accounting functions in both accounts receivable and accounts payable.

Responsibilities
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Answer phones and direct consumer to the proper department and follow up in a timely manner</li>
<li>Handle customer complaints diplomatically and refer complaining customers to the appropriate individual for resolution</li>
<li>Operate switchboard telephone system</li>
<li>Communicate with callers in a professional, friendly and efficient manner, striving to minimize the amount of time customers spend on hold</li>
<li>Assist the managers with various clerical duties as needed</li>
<li>Be friendly, professional, courteous and efficient when working with all customers and employees</li>
<li>Familiarize yourself with department structures &amp; internal procedures for assisting customers efficiently</li>
<li>Saturdays are required</li>
</ul>
