<p><b>About Blackbaud:
</b></p>
<p></p>
<p>At Blackbaud, we’re here to help good take over. We lead uniquely at the intersection point of technology and social good, providing cloud software, services, expertise, and data intelligence that empowers and connects people to drive impact for social good. We serve the entire social good community, which includes nonprofits, higher education institutions, K-12 schools, healthcare organizations, faith communities, arts and cultural organizations, foundations, companies, and the individual change agents who support them. With millions of users in 100+ countries, our vision is to power an Ecosystem of Good® that builds a better world. Our customers are passionate about making the world a better place, and we’re inspired by the opportunity to help them raise resources, deliver programs, manage operations, and understand their outcomes.
</p>
<p></p>
<p>Responsible for leading the product management of Data Intelligence capabilities delivered in Blackbaud vertical cloud solutions. This role will be responsible for leading product development for these capabilities, which will include incorporating Blackbaud’s research, benchmarks, usage data and discovery process. They will manage and improve the process of bringing engineering, user experience, and market insight to help deliver capabilities that will give customers better outcomes, regardless of the vertical. This position will hire, coach and develop product managers who bring a broad business perspective, but also are comfortable with the emerging technologies and application of Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, and Continuous Deployment. Since these product managers sit outside the primary product management organization, this person will also need to proactively seek alignment and efficient, high quality delivery of the DI capaiblities into the vertical cloud solutions.
</p>
<p></p>
<p>Success in this role will be measured in:
</p>
<ul>
<li>Increases in product utilization by role
</li>
<li>Improvements in customer outcomes as measured by organizational benchmarks
</li>
<li>Win rates in adding new customers where DI product capabilities are winning new logos
</li>
<li>Increasing the number of verticals, and geographic markets where DI product capabilities are active
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Role Responsibilities
</b></p>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Maintain product management coverage on DI capabilities
</li>
<li>Hire and develop product managers who bring strong business judgement and technical acumen
</li>
<li>Coordinate with RDO capabilities product management and vertical product management in maintenance of an integrated set of priorities
</li>
<li>Leverage insight about industry issues for customers which allow Blackbaud to help them digitally transform into more effective organizations
</li>
<li>Present to industry groups, customers, and partners about applied Data Intelligence
</li>
<li>Lead a high performing organization that is adapting to SaaS and an environment of continuous deployment
</li>
<li>Cultivate and deliver outcome-based evidence of customer performance improvement from DI capabilities that helps our sales team win more customers and expand revenue from existing relationships
</li>
<li>Manage 4-5 direct reports
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Education and Experience
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>8 years relevant professional experience required
</li>
<li>2-3 years or more managerial experience preferred
</li>
<li>Bachelor’s degree or equivalent experience required. MBA preferred.
</li>
<li>Customer domain knowledge preferred. Strong leadership experience required. Effective project management skills required.
</li>
<li>Effective presentation skills, effective writing skills, and experience with customers - preferred
</li>
</ul>
