<h2>Sr. Manager - Marketing & Communications - 14001</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div><div>
<p>Job Family Descriptor
</p>
<p>This group of positions is responsible for the marketing and corporate communications activities which include developing and driving a marketing and communication strategy, developing digital marketing plans (cross selling, op-selling etc.) to support business strategies, developing a roadmap for analyst relations and sharing of marketing insights with top mgmt. for presenting in various forums; Define and evolve partner enablement program. Conduct product marketing activities include advertising/ brand marketing, sales promotion, communication messaging, PR exhibits and trade exhibitions to create strong brand perception of products and service for the product/business. Manage internal marketing media like intranet and extranet; Develop all necessary marketing collateral like presentations, product slicks and Webex for new products and services across all geographies; Manage and maintain relationships with external marketing and event agencies, Develop and assess efficacy of marketing material with relevant business teams. Prepare annual communication plans aligned with Corporate strategy and identify brand positioning across product portfolio. Deliver the communication strategy including aspects such as key messaging, communication channels and target audience. Develop and repackage the BU/product specific communication to maintain consistency with the overall company. Develop the messaging and media presentation. Develop and deliver a coherent media and analyst relations strategy.
</p>
<p></p>
<p>Level Descriptor
</p>
<p></p>
<p>The purpose of the Corporate Communications function within Tata Communications is to promote and protect our corporate reputation as a source of competitive advantage, so that our capabilities and ‘DRIVE’ company culture are recognized as defining assets by our key stakeholders and influencers, globally.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p>We do this by articulating and sharing our story with the right people, in the right way, everywhere, every day.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p>As a Senior Manager within the Corporate Communications team, you will create, manage and execute high impact campaigns, content and news for the business, spanning both internal and external stakeholders:<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>
<b>Corporate stakeholders </b>– establishing thought leadership to influence company perception (PR and influencer relations)
</li>
<li>
<b>Employee stakeholders </b>– connecting employees with our business strategy and laying a foundation of employee ambassadors (internal communications)
</li>
<li>
<b>Customer and partner stakeholders </b>– build reputation to condition the market for sales to win (internal communications, PR and influencer relations)<br>
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p>Tata Communications has around 10,000 employees globally, and the Corporate Communications team is an independent function that represents all parts of the business. We are a close-knit team of around a dozen collaborative, committed and creative individuals, spread across the US, UK, India and Singapore. We leverage our diverse set of skills to support the growth of Tata Communications, with a can-do attitude to every project we embark on – while never forgetting to have fun along the way.
</p>
<p></p>
<p>Purpose - Broad objective of the role
</p>
<p></p>
<p>Brand consideration, awareness and advocacy globally are key focus areas for Tata Communications. You will be responsible for proactive storytelling and thought leadership in the Americas (with a focus on the US market). You will collaborate with internal stakeholders, Corporate Communications colleagues and our agency partners to ensure that Tata Communications’ overall business objectives translate into meaningful communication campaigns that are effectively executed internally and externally in the region.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p>Working closely with the Corporate Communications leadership team - who will define the global strategy and messaging - and colleagues in other regions, you will be responsible for managing a regional PR agency to support you with external campaigns, within delivery and budget agreements, and with tight integration across other business functions.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p>A part of your role will include supporting global campaign development, project management and content creation, curation and management where you will work with the wider team and agency network – taking an editorial style approach to content.
</p>
<p>Finally, at Tata Communications, we are firm believers that brand perception begins on the inside. In this role, you will work with our senior leadership to develop programs that connect our US employees with the company’s global growth strategy; foster Tata Communications as a great place to work in the Americas region; and help employees share the Tata Communications story internally and externally.
</p>
