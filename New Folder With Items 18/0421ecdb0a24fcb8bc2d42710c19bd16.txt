<h2>Senior Sales Development Representative</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Sanguine is accelerating personalized medicine research by empowering patients with their healthcare data, and making it as easy as possible for them to participate in the medical research process. We are helping thousands of researchers at 20 of the top 40 global pharmaceutical and biotechnology companies increase the speed and quality at which they are conducting their research. We’re growing quickly and need the best team members to join us.</p>
<p>We are looking for a strategic Senior Sales Development Representative responsible for generating interest in our research services and data, developing relationships with ideal customers, and qualifying new business opportunities. Using a consultative approach, the Senior Sales Development Representative will also offer helpful resources and insight that will inform the buyer’s decision-making process.</p>
<p>As we increase our commitment to become more client focused and provide unique, concierge service to potential customers, the role of Senior SDR will be vital to our success. This position is part of the Sales team and serves as a critical liaison between Demand Generation and Account Executives.</p>
<p><b>What You’ll Do: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Developing cold calling stamina and becoming a hunter; prospecting key decision makers and leads</li>
<li>Qualify leads through prospecting: cold emails, cold calling, and networking (social outreach and live events)</li>
<li>Maintaining active engagement with leads until they are ready</li>
<li>Partnering with our experienced Account Executives to help fill sales pipelines</li>
<li>Crushing monthly quotas and exceeding expectations</li>
<li>Answer prospects’ questions and offer content, including new product developments, based on their current needs or pain points</li>
<li>Work closely with the Lead Nurturing, New Business, and Brand Awareness teams to nurture marketing leads into sales opportunities</li>
<li>Coordinate calls and meetings between qualified leads and Sales team</li>
<li>Establish and incorporate best practices for scalable processes for SDR hires</li>
<li>Fearlessness - willing to hop on the phone with new people every single day and explain [redacted] value proposition as it relates to each individual you speak with.</li>
<li>The courage to challenge the status quo when logic and reason require it. See something broken? Fix it.</li>
</ul>
