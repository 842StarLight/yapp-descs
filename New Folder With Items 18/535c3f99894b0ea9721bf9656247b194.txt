<h2>Marketing Support Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p>This is a full-time position located at our Operations Center in Millersburg, Ohio.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Summary: </b>To provide detail-oriented, operational and advertising support for the Marketing Department in such a way that drives CSB’s commitment to Enduring Greatness and preserves the bank’s positive reputation of being a trusted community partner.<br>
<br>
<b>Essential Duties and Responsibilities</b> include the following. Other duties may be assigned.</p>
<ul>
<li>Create a wide variety of communication pieces that exhibit professionalism, accuracy and communicate CSB’s brand standards in an effective way; pieces may include but are not limited to customer correspondence, CSB Connect articles, press releases, print advertisements, and exterior signage.</li>
<li>Assist the Marketing Department with content creation in digital channels such as our website and social media platforms.</li>
<li>Promote new employees through ordering business cards, promotional materials, creating press releases, updating appropriate web content, etc.</li>
<li>Ensure marketing pieces for exiting employees are generated in a timely and professional manner.</li>
<li>Coordinate ordering and delivery of marketing materials for print, radio, web, ATM, online, and mobile banking, social media, billboards, cinema, etc.</li>
<li>Manage projects with vendors to ensure timeliness and quality standards are met in a cost effective manner.</li>
<li>Work in conjunction with the Compliance Department to ensure marketing materials adhere to applicable regulations and guidelines.</li>
<li>Utilize available analytics software to monitor and report users, trends, and conversion rates.</li>
<li>Assist in the review and analysis of the budget; track expenses, research and create related budgetary reports for the Marketing Committee, Senior Management, and the Board of Directors.</li>
<li>Processes Marketing Department invoices and ensures payments are completed by the Finance Department.</li>
<li>Provides administrative support to the Marketing Department</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Education and/or Experience</b></p>
<p>An Associates degree in a business related field required; Bachelor’s degree preferred and/or three or more years of managing projects in a professional setting.<br>
<br>
<b>Required Skills</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Proficiency in setting up and interpreting website metrics using Google Analytics</li>
<li>Proficiency in website Content Management Systems (CMS) and intermediate HTML coding</li>
<li>Advanced proficiency in Microsoft Office applications</li>
<li>Time management, organizational skills, and the ability to manage multiple projects through to completion on or before stated deadlines</li>
<li>Above average communication skills, both oral and written</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p>The Commercial &amp; Savings Bank is an Equal Opportunity Employer.</p>
