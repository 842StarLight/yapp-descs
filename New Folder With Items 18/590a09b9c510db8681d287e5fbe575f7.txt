<h2>HR Business Partner</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>Job Description<br>
<br>
<p>SmartRecruiters is working to build one of the most innovative and successful companies in the world - one that fundamentally changes how recruiting is done.</p>
<p>
As our dedicated HRBP, we are looking for a game-changing individual who will be a key driver of our company's success. This person will partner with all US-based teams to ensure they are highly engaged and are enabled to achieve their results.</p>
<p>
You will:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Advise and support teams on all People-related issues to share insights and drive action</li>
<li>
Be the go-to person for our US employees for any HR matters</li>
<li>
Support and act as the expert on payroll/benefits matters in coordination with our Payroll Manager (based in Europe) and our vendors</li>
<li>
Act as a true business partner, mostly for the Sales, Marketing, and Product teams based in the US, by sharing your knowledge and expertise</li>
<li>
Contribute to initiatives designed to drive operational efficiency in a fast-growing environment</li>
<li>
Develop strong relationships with team-members and external partners</li>
<li>
Build and maintain the company culture as we scale</li>
</ul>
</div>

<div>Qualifications<br>
<br>
<p>The most important qualification bar none is that you are Double IQ person (high EQ and high IQ) who loves to get #$#$ done and is thrilled to work for a company who is going to disrupt an entire industry. We are a progressive people ops team and are looking for people who are interested in the constructive disruption of traditional HR.</p>
<p><b>
More Specific Qualifications include:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Solid understanding of HR practices, policies, and employment law across the US (with a deep knowledge of California employment law)</li>
<li>
A successful first hands-on experience (in a tech startup ideally)</li>
<li>
Ability to perform in a fast-paced environment</li>
<li>
Excellent written and oral communication skills a MUST.</li>
<li>
Good sense of humor.</li>
</ul>
<p>
The ideal candidate will have a dynamic personality, the ability to build relationships and good communication skills. If you are hard-working, exceptionally dedicated, upbeat and enjoy working with great people, SmartRecruiters is the company for you!</p>
