<h2>Assistant Municipal Underwriter</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div><div><div><div>We are seeking a talented and motivated individual to join our Municipal Underwriting desk as an Assistant Municipal Underwriter. The successful candidate will support all functions of the institutional municipal underwriting desk including but not limited to primary negotiated offerings, primary competitive offerings, and operational tasks with respect to the desks day-to-day functions. The candidate will play an integral role in communicating with Baird’s Fixed Income Institutional Sales force as well as Baird’s Public Finance Department with respect to new Municipal Bond issues managed by Baird. The successful candidate should be passionate about the Fixed Income marketplace.<br>
<br>
As an Assistant Municipal Underwriter, you will:
<ul>
<li>Work closely with Institutional Municipal Underwriters to help ensure the best execution of new Municipal Bond issues. This includes: assistance with computation of competitive bids for new municipal bond underwritings, distribution of pricing/pertinent materials for both competitive and negotiated underwritings, assisting in communications between Baird’s Institutional Underwriting desk and external Institutional Municipal bond dealers.
</li>
<li>Supports the Negotiated and Competitive Underwriters with respect to their day-to-day operations. This includes clerical, recordkeeping, and new issue settlement duties.
</li>
<li>Works alongside our compliance group to ensure the Underwriting desk’s day-to-day operations are compliant with all procedures Baird’s compliance department has put in place.</li>
<li>
Assists Underwriters in communicating with Fixed Income personnel, including Institutional traders and salespeople with regard to new issues managed by Baird.
</li>
<li>Communicates with Baird’s Public Finance department with respect to new Negotiated offerings both before and after a new issue has been brought to market by the Underwriting desk.
</li>
<li>Perform other duties as assigned.
</li>
</ul>
