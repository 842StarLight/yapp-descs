<h2>Warehouse Associate</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Overview
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div>MRC Global serves the oil and gas industry across the upstream, midstream and downstream sectors as well as the chemical and gas distribution market sectors worldwide. We have an immediate opening for a <b>Warehouse Associate.</b>
</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
<b>
Job Purpose: </b>Responsible for processing orders received by mail, telephone, or personally from customer or company employees, and entering into SIMS. Orders may include company-wide range of products, such as pipe, valves, fittings, and electrical products.</div>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<p>Individual must be able to perform the essential duties with or without reasonable accommodation.</p>
<ul>
<li>
Unload material, tools, and equipment from vendor trucks; inspecting material for minimum MRC Global quality requirements;</li>
<li>
Stock and transfer material, tools, and equipment to appropriate shelf, bin, rack, or pipe bundle by a predetermined sequence, including bind location, size, type, style, and length;</li>
<li>
Mark material with correct identifying information;</li>
<li>
Transfer material to and from the shops for modification purposes;</li>
<li>
Assemble customer orders from stock and package orders in boxes and pallets as per customer requirements;</li>
<li>
Pull orders for delivery and stock-to-stock transfer; load onto correct truck or transfer to packing area using a forklift, overhead crane, and heavy equipment;
</li>
<li>Prepare documentation for UPS, LTL, SH, or Air Shipments;</li>
<li>
Adhere to required safety policies, guidelines and training;
</li>
<li>Maintain warehouse cleanliness in compliance with OSHA and customer safety policies;</li>
<li>
Perform other duties and projects as assigned;</li>
<li>
Take reasonable care for the safety and health of yourself and others; report workplace hazards, injuries, or illness immediately.</li>
</ul>
</div></div></div>
