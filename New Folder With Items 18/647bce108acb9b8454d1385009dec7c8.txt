<p><b>JOB DESCRIPTION</b></p>
<p><b>Job Title: </b>Content Writer</p>
<p><b>Department: </b> Sales &amp; Marketing</p>
<p><b>Reports To: </b> Creative Manager</p>
<p><b>Summary</b>: The Creative Content Writer engages in the ideation and creation of compelling content including website copy, blog posts, social media content, and product copy. A Content Writer is responsible for adding value by actively engaging customers while maintaining a cohesive brand voice. Additional tasks handled by a Content Writer may include analyzing marketing metrics and collaborating across departments in order to execute content brand initiatives.</p>
<p>Duties and Responsibilities<br>*<br>Write new SEO-friendly copy for all brand webpages with as much focus on data-driven results as creating quality messaging/content<br>*<br>Optimize &amp; implement copy on all brand websites (page titles, meta descriptions, H1s, alts, anchor text, etc.)<br>*<br>Blog post ideation, writing, and posting<br>*<br>Engages in ideation and creation of digital assets such as:<br>*<br>Infographics<br>*<br>Pinterest pins<br>*<br>Instagram and Facebook posts<br>*<br>Digital ad campaigns<br>*<br>YouTube videos<br>*<br>Write engaging promotional copy including, but not limited to: emails, landing pages, social media posts, product descriptions, titles, and headlines<br>*<br>Collaborate frequently with team members to strategize on copy and campaign goals<br>*<br>Actively work to increase email open rates and click-through rates via A/B testing and optimization of subject lines, intro copy, etc.<br>*<br>Create and manage customer facing help center content<br>*<br>Proofread and edit content produced by other members of the team<br>*<br>Analyze content marketing metrics and makes changes as needed</p>
<p><b>Competencies</b><br>*<br>Excellent verbal and written communication skills<br>*<br>Superior writing skills with attention to grammar and punctuation<br>*<br>A powerful storyteller that thrives on conveying the personality of a brand while balancing the technical needs of an SEO program<br>*<br>A working knowledge of SEO and how it applies to content marketing<br>*<br>Functional HTML knowledge: ability to do light modification of existing templates using basic HTML markup<br>*<br>Creativity &amp; resourcefulness<br>*<br>Well-organized with extreme attention to detail<br>*<br>A self-starter with strong communication skills and the ability to work under minimum supervision<br>*<br>Openness to constructive feedback and copy edits from multiple channels</p>
