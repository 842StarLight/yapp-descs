ABOUT US
<br><br>
Xnor.ai brings state-of-the-art artificial intelligence to the edge. Xnor's platform allows companies to run complex deep learning algorithms, formerly restricted to the cloud, locally on a range of devices including mobile phones, drones, and wearables. This new, highly scalable approach ensures complete privacy of data, eliminates the need for connectivity, and significantly reduces memory load and power demands, all without compromising accuracy or performance. Xnor is a venture-funded startup, founded on award-winning research conducted at the University of Washington and the Allen Institute for Artificial Intelligence. Xnor's industry-leading technology is used by global corporations in aerospace, automotive, retail and supply chain, photography, and consumer electronics.
<br><br>
THE ROLE
<br><br>
Xnor is seeking a Product Manager to turn more of our incredible technology into scalable products that can be easily deployed in high-value use-cases for our customers and partners. If you are collaborative, a self starter, and want to be part of the AI revolution, this is the opportunity for you. Xnor has traction in many problem spaces, applications, and markets, including: consumer, retail, and industrial. We're seeking an experienced Product Manager with a proven background in technologies related to IoT, Edge computing, or machine learning. Ideally, you have a bachelor's in electrical engineering or computer science, experience in a customer-facing role, and depth in industries of interest to Xnor. You will be responsible for the entire product lifecycle from conception through launch, channel development, and scale-up. You will also work closely with Engineering, Marketing, and Sales.
