<h2>Associate Product Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">The Associate Product Manager will support management of product life-cycle activities for various categories of personal protective equipment (PPE) under the guidance of the Senior Product Manager. The Associate Product Manager will work closely with product managers to develop and execute short and long term strategic growth plans in key categories that meet company objectives. This position will collaborate closely with cross-functional teams including sales, marketing communications, supply chain and manufacturing to bring new products to market. Key responsibilities include competitive surveillance, market/industry trend research, financial analysis and sales team support.
<br><br>
Responsibilities
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Monitor industry trends to determine potential growth opportunities</li>
<li>Conduct competitive product assortment audits to identify product line gaps</li>
<li>Prepare product category financial analysis reports and recommend pricing strategies to drive profitability</li>
<li>Manage end-user product concept wear testing process</li>
<li>Present new product recommendations to upper management</li>
<li>Provide ongoing support of the sales team through answering product questions, developing support tools and assisting with product training</li>
<li>Support new product launches by outlining key feature and benefits, competitive advantages and customer needs for marketing communications</li>
</ul>
Requirements
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>BS or BA in Advertising, Marketing, Business Administration, or related field required.</li>
<li>At least 2 years of relevant work experience including internships.</li>
<li>Ability to work effectively across functions</li>
<li>Persuasive written and verbal communications skills</li>
<li>Excellent research and analytical skills</li>
<li>Exceptional attention to detail</li>
<li>Proficient in Microsoft Excel, PowerPoint and other applications in the Microsoft Office Suite</li>
</ul>
Magid offers a competitive salary along with comprehensive benefits plans to include generous profit sharing and 401K plans, medical, dental and life insurance, and more.
