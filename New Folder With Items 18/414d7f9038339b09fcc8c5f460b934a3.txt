<h2>Concessions Supervisor</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Summary</b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
SMG, the leader in privately managed public assembly facilities, has an excellent and immediate opening for Concessions Supervisor for SMG/SAVOR Jacksonville. Responsible for assisting the food and beverage manager to oversee all concession functions.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Essential Duties and Responsibilities</b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Train, schedule, and supervise concessions food and beverage staff</li>
<li>Assist in settling concessions event paperwork per event</li>
<li>Maintains correct stock levels utilizing prior sales history</li>
<li>Assists supervisor with day to day operations</li>
<li>Monitor compliance with health and fire regulations regarding food preparation and serving</li>
<li>Works with other supervisors on distribution of all products to stands and bars</li>
<li>Ensures all POS systems are in proper working condition prior to the event</li>
<li>Understanding of Quest POS system BOH/FOH operations</li>
<li>Ability to operate and finalize events in Quest POS system</li>
<li>Accurately reviews quality product before serving to customer</li>
<li>Ensure health codes and liquor laws are followed</li>
<li>Establish and maintain positive client relationships</li>
<li>Other duties may be assigned</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p>
</p>
<p><b>Qualifications<br>
</b></p>
<p></p>
<p>To perform this job successfully, an individual must be able to perform each essential duty satisfactorily. The requirements listed below are representative of the knowledge, skill, and/or ability required. Reasonable accommodations may be made to enable individuals with disabilities to perform the essential functions.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Must have attention to detail</li>
<li>Must have good oral and written communication skills</li>
<li>Must have ability to add, subtract, multiple and divide</li>
<li>Must have strong customer service skills</li>
<li>Must be able to adapt to changes in the work environment, managing competing demands, frequent changes, delays or unexpected events</li>
<li>Must have strong interpersonal skills</li>
<li>Must have good organizational skills</li>
<li>Must have basic working knowledge of cash register and cash handling procedures</li>
<li>Must be compliant with health codes</li>
<li>Must complete Alcohol TEAM Compliance Training</li>
<li>Must be able to pass a background check to include a credit history report and meet corporate minimum requirements</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p>
</p>
<p><b>Education and/or Experience<br>
</b></p>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Four year college degree preferred</li>
<li>Two years of customer service experience</li>
<li>Understanding of survey based measures (Guest Experience)</li>
<li>At least two years of experience in Quest POS software</li>
<li>Must have two years of management experience in food and beverage</li>
<li>Must have Serve Safe certification (Mangers Certification)</li>
<li>Must be Excel and Microsoft proficient</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p>
</p>
