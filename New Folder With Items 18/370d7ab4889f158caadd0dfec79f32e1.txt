<p><b>Department Marketing Statement</b>
</p>Founded in 1917, Parker Hannifin Corporation is a $14.3 billion, global company. With annual sales of $14.3 billion in fiscal year 2019, Parker Hannifin is the world's leading diversified manufacturer of motion and control technologies and systems, providing precision-engineered solutions for a wide variety of mobile, industrial and aerospace markets. The company has operations in 50 countries around the world. Parker has increased its annual dividends paid to shareholders for 63 consecutive fiscal years, among the top five longest-running dividend-increase records in the S&amp;P 500 index. Parker’s engineering expertise and broad range of core technologies uniquely positions the company to solve some of the world’s greatest engineering challenges. By partnering with customers, Parker improves their productivity and profitability and seeks new ways to solve humanity's biggest challenges.<p><b>
Department Marketing Statement</b>
</p>The Hydraulic Valve Division is part of the Motion Systems Group of Parker Hannifin Corporation and consists of three locations: Elyria, OH, Hicksville, OH and Forest City, NC. The principal business function of the Division is the manufacturing of Hydraulic Valves.<p><b>
Essential Functions</b>
</p>Under supervision by design engineering, this position will be responsible for support of engineering operations, product qualification testing, and miscellaneous experimental investigations. It is a hands-on position that allows exposure to a variety of products, test techniques, design software, and fabrication. Interfaces with lab technicians, engineers, quality, and supply chain staff to expedite testing and assist in the product development cycle. Some interaction with customers and vendors may be required.<p><b>
Major Duties</b>
</p>1. Provide support to design engineering department 2. Work in R&amp;D lab as needed 3. Perform design and assembly of test fixtures 4. Create ECRs/ECOs/helpdesk tickets to change engineering documents (drawings, specifications, etc.) 5. Perform electro-hydraulic testing of development and production products 6. Interface with suppliers to quote prototype parts as needed 7. Analyze and post-process test data 8. Support value stream and Winovation projects to expedite products to market Any other duties as assigned.<p><b>
Qualifications</b>
</p>Must have a High School Diploma or GED equivalent. Willing to do hands on work in a lab or shop environment at least 35% of the time. Working towards degree in Electrical Engineering. Well organized and able to work with minimal supervision. Basic electrical proficiency (analog and digital circuits) Demonstrated verbal and written communication skills. Experience with 2D or 3D CAD software, data acquisition preferred. Experience with LabVIEW and C programming preferred<p><b>
Pay, Benefits, Work Schedule</b>
</p>Pay based on incoming year in college and major area of study Internship/co-op</div>
