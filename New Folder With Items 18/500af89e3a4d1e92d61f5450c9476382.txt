<h2>MadLab Technologies Graphics Student (2019-2020)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div><div><div><div>
<div>
<p><b>(Purpose):</b></p>
<ul>
<li>MadLab Technologies is a part of the Wildcat Shop. Our department produces course packs each term for instructors, as well as provides services for students, faculty and staff such as copies, lamination, printing, poster mounting, binding, engraving, sublimation, scanning.
</li>
<li>Our student assistants help cover our department during the week, some weekends and evenings as needed.</li>
<li>The MadLab Graphics Student requires extensive graphics skills. Experience with programs such as Adobe Photoshop, Adobe Illustrator, Acrobat Reader Pro, Adobe InDesign, CorelDraw, and/or other graphics programs preferred.</li>
</ul>
</div>
</div></div></div></div>
<div><div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Job Duties</b></h2>
<div><div>
<div>
<p><b>(Essential Functions):</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Design and produce graphics for store advertisements and signage. Create and manipulate files for engraving projects.</li>
<li>Assist in performance of all MadLab Technologies sales, service, and production duties. Primary duties involve customer service and delivery of materials to instructors/professors, printing graphics projects for students, binding, lamination, filing, communication with instructors, desk copy fulfillment and tracking, database maintenance, graphics creation, laser engraving, sublimation and heatpress operation, and many other duties.</li>
</ul>
</div>
</div></div>
</div></div>

<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Pay &amp; Work Schedule</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<p><b>Pay:</b><b> </b>$12.00 per hour.</p>
<p><b>
Schedule/Hours:</b><b> </b>up to 19 hours per week during the hours listed above.</p>
<p><b>
Working Conditions:</b><b> </b>This position involves heavy lifting, carrying, and walking. It is a high traffic, often high-volume department. Student have constant contact with a broad and diverse demographic.</p>
<p>
This position is an excellent stepping-stone to employment after graduation. Applicants utilize and improve skills and gain experience desirable in a wide variety of careers. It is not an understatement to say that successful student employees in this department learn and utilize skills that are highly desirable in any future job market.<br>
<br>
Our department functions as a tight team, and reliability is a requirement for employment. The hours we cover are from 8 am to 7 pm Monday through Thursday, 8 am to 5 pm on Friday, and 2 pm to 5 pm on Sunday. We may soon implement Saturday hours as well during the academic year. We can only promise to work around class schedules during those hours, not sports or clubs. We also have about three weeks that require coverage until 7 or 8 p.m. and 10 to 5 on weekends. This is always at the start of the quarters. We also require coverage during all term breaks, including summer. We try to spread the coverage over all student schedules so that each one does get <i>some</i> time off, but everyone is asked to work part of each break. Because of the work we do, there are sometimes non-toxic odors in the area. We try to minimize this with air purifiers, but there are the occasional odors.</p>
<p><b>
Benefits:</b> All Student and Temporary/Hourly positions are eligible to accrue paid sick leave. This leave will accrue at one (1) hour of paid sick leave for every 40 hours of work.</p>
