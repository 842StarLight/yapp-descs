<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>About the role</b></h2>
<div>
As a part of our technology organization, you will have the opportunity to build next generation solutions that will transform the way our customers interact with our family of iconic brands. Our team employs a DevOps model, allowing our product teams to have full ownership of design, build and operate with immense scale. Stores account for the lion’s share of GAP’s revenues of $16B, and as a Technical Lead (iOS Mobile) you will lead a team of mobile software engineers focused on developing Gap’s mobile Point-Of-Sale solution being rolled out to phones and tablets across 1000+ Gap Inc. store. The successful candidate will be technical (hands-on), capable of working as a senior contributor, and will provide front-line engineering leadership.<br>
<br>
<ul>
<li>Lead a team of high-performing iOS engineers to build an architecturally sustainable codebase; participate in code reviews and proof of concepts</li>
<li>Set technical direction while ensuring they align with the goals of the larger organization</li>
<li>Help implement effective engineering processes and policies that emphasize quality</li>
<li>Lead the team in its DevOps journey; instill a culture of constant analysis of business and systems metrics to improve system performance and user experience</li>
<li>Collaborate with Product Owner, operational teams and other engineering managers to provide guidance on complexity and delivery estimates</li>
<li>Guide team in analysis and resolution of production issues and defects; Facilitate Root Cause Analysis discussions</li>
<li>Monitor and manage team performance; coach team members; drive adoption via mentoring</li>
<li>Participate in hiring activities including screening, and interviewing
</li>
</ul>
</div>
