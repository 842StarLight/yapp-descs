<h2>Technical Service Representative</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>POSITION TITLE: Technical Service Representative<br>REPORTS TO: Technical Service Manager</p>
<p>MISSION: To provide technical support to our customers (internal &amp; external).</p>
<p>REQUIREMENTS:<br>Experience:  * At least 2 years of customer service.</p>
<p>Education:  * High school (minimum).</p>
<p>Skills:</p>
<ul><li>Must have excellent communication skills (verbal &amp; written)</li></ul>
<ul><li>Must have good organizational skills</li></ul>
<ul>
<li>Knowledge of Microsoft office software (Outlook, Word, Excel, Access)</li>
<li>Basic knowledge of automotive and electrical systems a plus</li>
</ul>
<p>DUTIES &amp; RESPONSIBILITIES:</p>
<ul>
<li>Respond promptly and courteously to customer request telephone, email, or other methods</li>
<li>Alter manager to all feedback regarding quality or safety</li>
<li>Keep manager aware of product trends</li>
<li>Maintain records about inquiries received</li>
<li>Forward information about the requests to the appropriate sales personnel</li>
<li>Pursue additional training as necessary in order to maintain and improve technical ability</li>
<li>Assist other department personnel in the development of technical literature</li>
<li>Participate and assist in department activities as needed</li>
<li>Produce a monthly report which summarizes activities</li>
<li>Minimal travel required.</li>
<li>Other tasks as assigned by Technical Service Manager.</li>
</ul>
<p>Job Type: Full-time</p>
<p>Additional Compensation:</p>
<ul><li>Bonuses</li></ul>
<p>Work Location:</p>
<ul><li>One location</li></ul>
</div>
