<h2>MSP Account Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Overview
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<p>SolarWinds MSP is the global leading provider of complete IT management and Automation solutions for Managed Service Providers (MSPs). SolarWinds MSP has an award-winning and industry’s #1 RMM and MSP Service Automation Platform. SolarWinds MSP has a proven track record of helping MSPs standardize and automate the setup and delivery of IT services in order to achieve true scalability. Thousands of MSPs use SolarWinds MSP solutions to deliver scalable, flexible, profitable managed services to over 100,000 SMBs worldwide.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
The SaaS Account Manager is proactive and consultative in phone sales and responsible for selling SolarWinds MSP software solutions to current customers in an assigned territory. This person will work collaboratively with the Sales Leadership Team and cross-functional team members to develop and manage customer accounts.</p>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<ul>
<li>Develop and apply a thorough understanding of SolarWinds MSP products and sales methodology and processes to retire assigned quota via growth of assigned territory through prospecting, ownership of a sales pipeline, and collaboration with Sales Leadership.</li>
<li>
Demonstrate advanced ability for multi-product sales, attaching other SolarWinds MSP products to current RMM customers.</li>
<li>
Cultivate and leverage relationships with key partners to drive sales outside of core territory calling duties.</li>
<li>
Well versed in SFDC and ensures accurate and timely forecasts as well as opportunity updates for management visibility in the sales pipeline.</li>
<li>
Complete and thorough ability to perform product presentations to customers focusing on selling and positioning SolarWinds MSP solutions.</li>
</ul>
</div></div></div>
