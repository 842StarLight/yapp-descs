<h2>Branch Manager - ROOFING DISTRIBUTORSHIP</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Branch Manager / Managing Partner</b></p>
<p>We are a prominent roofing supply distributorship located in Northern California and are looking for a successful and seasoned leader to become our next Branch Manager. Our unique entrepreneurial opportunity allows you the flexibility to manage and build your own business within the region while receiving state-of-the-art support and oversight.</p>
<p>You know best how to service your customers, and as a member of our team, you know that means a commitment to excellence through quality people, customer service, respect and integrity, and quality products. We have very high standards – in the Branch Manager role you’ll always have these commitments in mind while you:</p>
<ul>
<li>Manage the day-to-day operations of the branch, providing leadership to all employees;</li>
<li>Instill a culture of safety through training, repetition and being a role model for all employees;</li>
<li>Drive the profitability and financial success of the branch through planning and implementing strategies for growth;</li>
<li>Build relationships with customers and ensure their continued satisfaction;</li>
<li>Track and ensure your inventory is accurate and sufficient to meet the needs of the customers;</li>
<li>Collaborate with and/or coach inside and outside sales representatives in the development of new key accounts;</li>
<li>Investigate and provide insightful solutions to customer issues;</li>
<li>Ensure the organization and maintenance of the warehouse, yard and store layout exemplifies maximum efficiency, merchandising and safe operations;</li>
<li>Maintain accurate and complete personnel records and supervise the timely, correct reporting of time and work records to the payroll department;</li>
<li>Conduct respectful disciplinary actions as needed, following all Company policies and procedures in providing adequate documentation of all such actions;</li>
<li>Schedule personnel for optimum customer service;</li>
<li>Other duties related to the role, as required.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Qualifications: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Proven history of superior ethics and integrity;</li>
<li>5 years prior management experience (preferably in the building material distribution industry);</li>
<li>Able to develop and lead a diverse team;</li>
<li>Demonstrated excellent financial prowess, strategic planning and successful execution of growth plans within the roofing supply industry or similar;</li>
<li>Must be computer literate and able to learn new software programs with ease;</li>
<li>Proficiency in Microsoft Outlook, Word, Excel and the Internet;</li>
<li>Must be able to read, speak and write the English language to communicate efficiently through writing and spoken word with vendors, customers and employees;</li>
<li>Experience in Outside Sales within the roofing supply industry or similar (highly preferred);</li>
<li>Able to cultivate long-term customer relationships;</li>
<li>Must be able to set and achieve goals;</li>
<li>Must be able to pass the required tests for a Safety Sensitive position;</li>
<li>Must ensure Company safety policies and DOT requirements are followed;</li>
<li>Must have experience in the building material distribution industry, particularly selling residential and non-residential roofing products, with demonstrated success;</li>
<li>Must be dependable and have a current state issued driver’s license with a satisfactory driving record;</li>
<li>Must be legally entitled to work in the United States;</li>
<li>Bachelor’s degree in Marketing or Business, preferred.</li>
</ul>

</ul>
