<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>About the job</b></h2>
<p>
The Business Strategy &amp; Operations organization provides business critical insights using analytics, ensures cross functional alignment of goals and execution, and helps teams drive strategic partnerships and new initiatives forward. We stay focused on aligning the highest-level company priorities with effective day-to-day operations, and help evolve early stage ideas into future-growth initiatives.</p>
<p>
The GCS Product and Solutions Activation (PSA) team is responsible for commercializing performance and brand solutions for SMBs. We are the product and business experts behind the thousands of Google Customer Solutions (GCS) Googlers and advertisers. We’re responsible for driving sustainable revenue growth and aligning product adoption with customer goals across GCS channels.
</p>
<p>As the Global Product Lead for Apps, your focus will be be threefold. 1) Work with Product/ Engineering to build high performance mobile app promo products that are easy to use by our SMB app developer customers. 2) Work with Marketing and the regional teams to educate our sales teams and our advertisers that Google has app solutions that are just as simple and efficient as web products. 3) Work with global AppDev leadership and Sales Operations teams to help meet customers’ needs.</p>
<p>You will work closely with multiple sales teams and test new approaches focusing on how to apply cutting edge features to help advertisers achieve their goals, gain valuable global experience, work directly with teams in other regions to manage programs, share discoveries, and uncover new insights that can help all mobile app businesses grow around the world.
</p>
<p>
When our millions of advertisers and publishers are happy, so are we! Our Google Customer Solutions (GCS) team of entrepreneurial, enthusiastic and client-focused members are the "human face" of Google, helping entrepreneurs both individually and broadly build their online presence and grow their businesses. We are dedicated to growing the unique needs of advertising companies. Our teams of strategists, analysts, advisers and support specialists collaborate closely to spot and analyze customer needs and trends. In collaboration, we create and implement business plans broadly for all types of businesses.</p>

Responsibilities</b></h2>
