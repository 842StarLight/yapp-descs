<h2>Marketing Coordinator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>We are seeking a Marketing Coordinator to join our team. The ideal candidate will have at least 2 years experience in digital marketing including email and social media marketing, very strong English writing and editing skills, and the ability to juggle multiple priorities at one time.</p>
<p>Responsibilities:</p>
<ul>
<li>Execute marketing strategies developed by the leadership team</li>
<li>Write and edit blog posts, web copy, and other marketing content</li>
<li>Together with the Marketing Manager, develop and create email and online marketing campaigns</li>
<li>Develop and create social media posts and social media marketing plans</li>
<li>Assist with webinars and other events as needed</li>
<li>Help analyze web traffic, advertising results, and other data</li>
<li>Help create marketing materials such as flyers</li>
</ul>
<p>Qualifications:</p>
<ul>
<li>2+ years' experience with email and social media marketing</li>
<li>Strong English writing and editing skills</li>
<li>Experience with inbound marketing software such as HubSpot and email marketing programs such as Constant Contact</li>
<li>Strong knowledge of online marketing campaign best practices</li>
<li>Experience executing multifaceted campaigns (email, online ads, direct mail, etc.)</li>
<li>Strong attention to detail and quality</li>
<li>Desire and ability to learn and use new skills quickly</li>
<li>Well organized and able to use time efficiently</li>
<li>Ability to work independently without a lot of supervision</li>
<li>Familiarity with international travel and a global mindset</li>
<li>Experience with Photoshop and InDesign a plus</li>
</ul>
<p>Job Type: Full-time</p>
<p>Additional Compensation:</p>
<ul><li>Bonuses</li></ul>

<p>Benefits:</p>
<ul>
<li>Health insurance</li>

<li>Paid time off</li>
</ul>
