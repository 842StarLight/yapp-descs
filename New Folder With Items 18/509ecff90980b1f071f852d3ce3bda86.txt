<h2>Program Development Manager</h2>

<p>PDI helps convenience retailers and petroleum wholesalers thrive through digital transformation and enterprise software that enables them to grow topline revenue, optimize operations and unify their business across the entire value chain. Over 1,500 customers in more than 200,000 locations worldwide count on our leading ERP, logistics, fuel pricing and marketing cloud solutions to provide insights that increase volume, margin and customer loyalty. For more than 35 years, our comprehensive suite of solutions and unmatched expertise have helped customers reimagine their enterprise and deliver exceptional customer experiences. Backed by the investment of TA Associates, Genstar, and Insight, PDI is embarking upon an aggressive expansion strategy through internal investment in development as well as intensive M&amp;A activity. The result will be a broader, more powerful, global company that serves customers across the entire supply chain.</p>
<p>
The Program Development Manager is responsible for program optimization for various assigned Fuel Rewardsâ„¢ clients. The goal of this position is to become an invaluable loyalty advisor to our clients to deliver strategy, technology, analytics, and services. Our clients will rely on the Program Development Manager to develop and support new opportunities to promote the brand in the marketplace. This position will support the long-term vision of the Fuel Rewards strategy within the assigned accounts. Responsibilities include strengthening relationships with the retail partners, driving sales and growing the program, visiting store/site locations with specific customers.
