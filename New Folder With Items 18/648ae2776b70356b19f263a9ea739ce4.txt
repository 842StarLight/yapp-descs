<h2>Strategic Account Executive - Financial Services (Boston)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>STRATEGIC ACCOUNT EXECUTIVE</b></p>
<p><b>Who We Are</b></p>
<p>Crunchy Data is a leading provider of trusted open source PostgreSQL and enterprise PostgreSQL technology, support and training. Crunchy Data offers Crunchy Certified PostgreSQL, the most advanced pure open source RDBMS on the market. Crunchy Data is a leading provider of Cloud Native PostgreSQL – providing open source, cloud agnostic PostgreSQL-as-a-Service solutions.</p>
<p><b>Description</b></p>
<p>Crunchy's Strategic Account Executives drive awareness and are responsible for generating new business for the fastest growing Relational Database Management System in the world, PostgreSQL. The Strategic Account Executive focuses on formulating and executing a sales strategy within an assigned territory, resulting in revenue growth from existing support subscription customers and net new customer acquisition.</p>
<p><b>Primary Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Prospect, identify, qualify and develop sales pipeline</li>
<li>Meet or exceed quarterly and annual bookings objectives</li>
<li>Build strong and effective relationships with existing customers and net new targets to foster on-going credibility and growth opportunities</li>
<li>Act as "quarterback" with Pre-Sales and Technical Account Manager resources</li>
<li>Manage and forecast sales pursuits in SFDC</li>
<li>Participate in industry trade shows, using a strategic approach to monetize these events through customer &amp; prospect meetings</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Required Qualifications</b></p>
<ul>
<li>5+ years successful experience selling enterprise technology in competitive markets</li>
<li>Demonstrated success within the financial services vertical</li>
<li>Desire to work in a small, fast-paced and growing company where personal accountability and work ethic are highly critical</li>
<li>Ability to interact and add value with both customer executives and IT personnel</li>
<li>Goal oriented, with a track record of over-achievement (President's Club, Winner's Circle types of attainment)</li>
<li>Disciplined – skilled in managing time and company resources</li>
<li>Knowledge and experience with Open Source platforms, i.e. Postgres, Kubernetes, Red Hat Solution stack, and/or Pivotal</li>
<li>Must be able to travel up to 50% of the time</li>
<li>Desired Qualifications</li>
<li>Bachelor Degree from an accredited college or university</li>
<br>
</ul>
