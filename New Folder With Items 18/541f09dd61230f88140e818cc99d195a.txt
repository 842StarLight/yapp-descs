<h2>Principal PM Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>Intelligent Edge is a big part of our strategy to be the #1 cloud provider to organizations and people around the globe. Microsoft Azure is at the forefront of delivering on this Intelligent Edge vision by providing offerings that scales all the way from microcontrollers to cloud at the edge. Our team is working on building an exciting new offering to our Intelligent Edge suite and looking for a seasoned PM to join us in this v1.0 effort.
</div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities</b></h2>
<div>
You will be responsible for understanding customer requirements across different verticals, analyzing and identifying market opportunity, translating those into product features and building a product plan for delivering a compelling v1.0 offering. This role requires you to partner and collaborate with teams across Azure.<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<div><b>Qualifications</b></div>
<div>
You think strategically and analytically about our customer's business and technical challenges, and come up with innovative ideas to solve these challenges. You have keen sense of ownership, drive, and entrepreneurship, and, most importantly, you know how to earn trust and work cross-organizationally to deliver results. You work well in an unstructured environment and are able to adapt quickly to change while making sure we are staying focused on delivering on our customer success goals. You have a passion to lead from the front and influence others to follow with data and customer advocacy.</div>
