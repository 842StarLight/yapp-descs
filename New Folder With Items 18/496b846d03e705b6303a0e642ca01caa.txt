<h2>Director of Product, Consumer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Overview
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div>Paciolan is the #1 primary ticketing company in college athletics, and the #2 largest primary ticketing provider in the US including performing arts, arenas, and professional sports. As a leader in ticketing, fundraising, marketing, analytics, and technology solutions, we power more than 500 live entertainment organizations, selling over 120 million tickets a year.<br>
<br>
While we are the market leader in many areas, we will never act too big or move too slow as a company. At Paciolan, we come to work ready to innovate and succeed, and we always have fun doing it. You may not recognize our name, because we white-label our consumer channels, but we create great products at scale not just once a year Black Friday, but every day our events go on sale!<br>
<br>
As we continue to grow, we need an outstanding, true product leader to come join us to lead our e-commerce space. You will own and drive the creative vision and strategy for our next generation of products in this area. You will manage and mentor a team of product professionals while guiding our company towards innovative e-commerce solutions dominated by a mobile-first / IoT mindset.<br>
</div>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<p><b>What you'll do</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Strategize and build the roadmap for a modern, innovative platform of products in our e-commerce space including responsive, mobile and Internet of Things (IoT) solutions.</li>
<li>
Collaborate with small, lean agile teams to execute the overall strategy.</li>
<li>
Communicate with internal and external business units to evangelize the vision and gather information, including competitive analysis to ensure we are building products that our fans will consider to be the best in the industry.</li>
<li>
Own and deliver product using industry best practices and product management techniques to emphasize an Inception thru Implementation approach leveraging data and metrics to drive decisions.</li>
<li>
Own products through Alpha, Beta and GA phases of implementation, A/B testing and continue to gather data around adoption, usage and sale conversion.</li>
<li>
Manage, mentor and grow product managers within our organization, and help us recruit.</li>
</ul>
</div></div></div>
