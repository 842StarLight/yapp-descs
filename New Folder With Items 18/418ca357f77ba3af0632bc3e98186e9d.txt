<h2>Director of Infrastructure & Operations</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Johnstone Supply, Inc., the nation s largest HVACR distribution Cooperative, has a great opportunity for a Strategic Accounts Director to join our team. Johnstone has been in business for over 60 years and has grown in to an industry leader with 428 store locations and over $2.5 billion in annual sales. We work with some of the largest companies in the world, including Daikin/Goodman, Honeywell, Emerson, Johnson Controls, and Fujitsu, to provide our customers with world class products, technical expertise, and quality service.</p>
<p>
As a member of the Information Technology team and reporting to the Chief Information Officer, the Director of Infrastructure &amp; Operations is responsible for leading the planning, implementation, execution, and on-going maintenance of IT Infrastructure &amp; Operations services including; compute, storage, connect, and end user support. Additionally, this position is responsible for setting the I&amp;O service strategy and technical roadmap to ensure its integration with the overall IT and enterprise strategic plans, working with IT leadership on the service portfolio and governance required to prioritize projects, resources, and budget.</p>
<p><b>
Specific responsibilities include:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Provides strategic direction and oversight for the design, development, operation and support of IT systems that fulfill the needs of the business, including the full life cycle of technical architecture, infrastructure engineering, infrastructure operations, and IT service support</li>
<li>Act as a trusted advisor and develop a clear understanding of business needs. Ensures cost-effective delivery of IT services to meet those needs, and respond with the agility required to address changing business priorities</li>
<li>Develop &amp; execute a multi-year roadmap to increase our reliability &amp; agility while reducing our complexity &amp; maintenance burden. Need to consider cloud, managed services and automation</li>
<li>Recruit, develop, and lead the I&amp;O team to deliver on our technology and business objectives with excellence. Ensuring the highest levels of infrastructure availability for our business partners and customers</li>
<li>Develop and I&amp;O talent strategy that forecasts future skill and competency needs to acquire, develop, or source. Balance between growing the agility required to achieve digital business objectives and ensuring that the core IT functions are reliable, stable and efficient.</li>
<li>Direct, develop, and provide leadership to the Infrastructure &amp; Operations (I&amp;O) team, establish priorities, set goals, monitor progress, and coordinate efforts with other groups in Information Technology</li>
<li>Plan and staff the Service Desk function to assist employees on service issues and requests in accordance with agreed upon service levels</li>
<li>Lead end user computing to provide employees the utmost reliability, productivity, and mobility</li>
<li>Protect our customers, our employees, and our brand by incorporating security and compliance in all decisions; ensure security policy and procedure requirements are met</li>
<li>Direct the development and execution of an I&amp;O sourcing strategy and provides leadership for strategic vendor and partner relationship management</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Minimum qualifications:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor s or Master s Degree in computer science, information systems, business administration or related field, or equivalent work experience</li>
<li>15 or more years of experience in IT and business/industry</li>
<li>5 -7 years of leadership responsibilities</li>
<li>Preferably 5 or more years of experience in large, cross-functional teams; influencing key stakeholders effectively across the organization regarding innovative changes</li>
<li>Demonstrated experience in strategic planning, organization design and development</li>
<li>Preferably proven experience or demonstrated capability in leading IT transformational initiatives in complex and dynamic environments</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p>Johnstone offers a competitive wage; medical, dental and vision insurance, 401(k), generous Paid Time Off; convenient location from I-205 &amp; Airport Way; and a great work environment.</p>
