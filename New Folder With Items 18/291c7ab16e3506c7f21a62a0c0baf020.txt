<p>
Luxottica is a global leader in the design, manufacture and distribution of fashion, luxury and sports eyewear. Our wholesale network covers more than 150 countries and our retail presence consists of over 7,200 retail stores across the globe.</p>
<p>
In North America, our wholesale business is the home to global brands like Ray-Ban, Oakley, and many of the top fashion house brands. Our leading retail brands include; LensCrafters, Sunglass Hut, Pearle Vision, Target Optical and Sears Optical. We are also home to EyeMed, the fastest growing vision care company in the United States.</p>
<p>
Supporting over 4,500 retail stores in North America, a career in our retail headquarters allows us to stay in touch with the end customer and use their ever-changing behaviors and preferences to shape our offerings of the best eyewear and services.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
GENERAL FUNCTION
</b></p>
<p>The Sr. Java Developer will be working on the Point-of-Sale team responsible for the software solutions in support of Luxottica Retail’s brands such as LensCrafters and Sunglass Hut. The position will develop, modify, or direct others in updating the software utilizing Agile or Phased Waterfall methodology. The position will be responsible for specific development tasks using design best practices, analytical skills, proposing solutions to requirements, building designs according to the Architecture, and implementing various technologies. Consult with customers on projects or technical issues. Make recommendations on new or modifications of systems.</p>
<p></p>
<br>

<ul>
<li>
Own the technical design and development of cross-functional, multi-platform application systems.</li>

Responsible for overall solution design quality</li>
