<h2>Technology Sales Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div><div>
<p><b>POSITION PROFILE</b></p>
<p>
The Technology Specialist executes the core equipment and basic services strategy inside assigned accounts working with Client Executive in Strategic and Enterprise Accounts.</p>
<p><b>
JOB DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Works with Client Executive to execute equipment and basic services strategy within an assigned account.</li>
<li>Independently drives customer engagements, meetings, and develops opportunities using SMEs and other skilled assets to position, propose and close deals.</li>
<li>Efficiently qualifies opportunities and develops proposals that meet/exceed customer requirements.</li>
<li>Craft and position the Ricoh portfolio</li>
<li>Assess the fit and affordability with the customer</li>
<li>Understands and leads the pricing, proposal and negotiations.</li>
<li>Propose, sell and deliver profitable CORE equipment and basic services.</li>
<li>Works collaboratively with matrix teams to provide a quality deliverable to the client.</li>
<li>Uses collaboration to build trust, confidence and influence with client.</li>
<li>Clearly articulates Ricoh's position in the industry and value to the client.</li>
<li>Performs other duties as assigned.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>QUALIFICATIONS (Education, Experience, and Certifications)</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor degree or equivalent experience in related field.</li>
<li>5-10 years experience selling into F5000 companies.</li>
<li>Proven consultative selling skills.</li>
</ul>
<p>Ricoh is an EEO/Affirmative Action Employer - Minorities/Women/Protected Veterans/Disabled.</p>
