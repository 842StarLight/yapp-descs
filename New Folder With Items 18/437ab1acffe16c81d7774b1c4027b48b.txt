<p><b>
POSITION SUMMARY</b><br>
<b><i>
</i></b><br>
View, Inc. is seeking an Account Associate to support our presence in Florida. The Account Associate is a customer facing team member in a defined territory, responsible for supporting the sales activities of the Account Executives. The Account Associate acts as a multiplier for their team in supporting the sales efforts from lead generation through collection.</p>
<p><b>
ESSENTIAL POSITION RESPONSIBILITIES</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Support in developing and executing territory-based account plans.</li>
<li>
Support in developing an opportunity pipeline to meet quarterly and annual quotas.</li>
<li>
Qualifies and prioritizes accounts and opportunities, and then actively works to engage them through warm network introductions.</li>
<li>
Confidence to engage with C-Level Executives on complex enterprise sales with long sales cycles.</li>
<li>
Works with internal technical and support teams to produce and present custom benefits analyses, budgets, quotes, etc.</li>
<li>
Knows and understands their territory as a market. Canvasses the entire territory based upon priority</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>
DESIRED SKILLS AND EXPERIENCE</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Proven track record of excellence, academics, sports, arts, etc.</li>
<li>
Commitment to continuous improvement and learning.</li>
<li>
Account sales experience selling to large accounts.</li>
<li>
Experience selling new product categories.</li>
<li>
Desire to build long term relationships with customers.</li>
<li>
Passion and drive to closes deals.</li>
<li>
Ability to handle and prioritize multiple tasks and situations that require urgent attention.</li>
<li>
Excellent communication skills – verbal and written.</li>
<li>
Technology savvy.</li>
</ul>
