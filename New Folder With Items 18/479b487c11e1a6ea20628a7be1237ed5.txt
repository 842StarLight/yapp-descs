<h2>Watson Health - Business Analyst - Claims Investigator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>IBM’s Industry Consultants are essential to our clients as they work to reinvent themselves for the future. Join our highly visible and highly skilled team of global consultants as we strive to accelerate sales, deliver client engagements, and build IBM’s market eminence. <br>
<b>Location:</b> You will be working on-site at the client's downtown location in Des Moines.<br>
<br>
Review and monitor health claims for the identification of aberrancies and possible provider fraud, waste and abuse. Develop provider education, demand letters, and self-audit letters and tracking the disposition of all cases in the case tracking tool/database and ensure follow up and appropriate disposition of each case for IBM Watson Health’s Program Integrity team supporting the Iowa Medicaid Enterprise. Document key milestones in provider investigations and assist in identifying opportunities for policy alignment and cost savings for the IBM Watson Health Program Integrity team that supports Iowa Medicaid.
<ul>
<li>Reviews claims data and reports to identify aberrant providers for possible audits.</li>
<li>
Monitors the progress of each case within the case tracking system/database and ensures that appropriate actions are taken.</li>
<li>
Works collaboratively with the other members of the IBM Watson Health Program Integrity team for the Iowa Medicaid Enterprise to determine the status and final disposition of each case.</li>
<li>
Prepares demand letters and other notifications for providers and ensures that the customer/appropriate staff person has approved all letters before they are sent out where necessary.
</li>
<li>Responds to provider inquiries regarding the status of an investigation or audit and ensures prompt and provider-friendly approach.</li>
<li>
Coordinates the appeals process for providers as needed with the Team’s Audit subcontractor.</li>
<li>
Assists with notifications, tracking, collection and reporting of overpayment recoveries, including working within the Medicaid claims system to process claims adjustments reflecting overpayments recovered/changes in payment initiated.
</li>
<li>Works with team members to develop appropriate educational materials for providers.</li>
<li>
Assists in developing policies, procedures, processes and workflows to support the Audit and Case Disposition Process.</li>
<li>
Provides input into the configuration, testing, implementation and ongoing operations of the Case tracking system/database.</li>
<li>
Coordinates activities and case dispositions with other functional teams as needed.</li>
<li>
Strong initiative and adept at ability to plan and prioritize work efficiently, multi-task, and work well under pressure with minimal direction.</li>
</ul>
<br>
<ul>

3+ years coding experience required.
</b></li>

<li>
3+ years experience in customer service, administrative support and/or data entry within the healthcare, insurance or government sector</li>
</ul>

<ul>

Prior experience working collaboratively with health care providers is strongly preferred.</li>
<li>
Experience with health care claims and/or health care fraud/program integrity</li>
<li>
Knowledge of health care claims and/or health care fraud/program integrity</li>
<li>
Additional coding credentials such as RHIT, CCS, or CCS-P highly preferred.</li>

Medical record review experience and/or clinical background such as RN or LPN</li>
