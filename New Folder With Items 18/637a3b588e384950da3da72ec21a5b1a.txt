<div><b>What You’ll Do</b></div>
<div>This position is focused on driving revenue through managing FellowshipOne billing and accounts receivable processes. This position is responsible for all tasks related to Billing for FellowshipOne products – creating and processing monthly invoices, receivables, credits and account reconciliation. This position is customer facing with daily interaction to provide excellent customer service to ensure understanding of invoices and timely payments.</div>
<ul>
<li>Process sales orders for new and existing clients to set up or modify client invoicing as a result of new sales, upgrades, and/or renewals</li>
<li>Manual invoicing for online giving transaction fees</li>
<li>Process cancellations for departing clients</li>
<li>Respond to customer billing inquiries using excellent verbal and written communication skills</li>
<li>Resolve outstanding accounts by examining customer payment plans and payment history to ensure all accounts are brought up to date</li>
<li>Draft and send monthly invoices for various FellowshipOne products and services</li>
<li>Interact with and serve as a business partner to internal customers, including sales, customer success, legal and accounting</li>
<li>Oversight of the billing specialist functions related to payment processing and updating client information</li>
<li>Produce accurate revenue reports for FellowshipOne book of business</li>
</ul>
