<h2>Inside Partner Account Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Inside Partner Account Manager</b></p>
<p>
Sitecore is the global leader in experience management software that combines content management, commerce, and customer insights. The Sitecore Experience CloudTM empowers marketers to deliver personalized content in real time and at scale across every channel—before, during, and after a sale. More than 5,200 brands––including American Express, Carnival Cruise Lines, Kimberly-Clark, and L’Oréal––have trusted Sitecore to deliver the personalized interactions that delight audiences, build loyalty, and drive revenue.</p>
<p>
Sitecore is experiencing spectacular growth all over the world. To build on Sitecore’s success and growth, we’re looking for a skilled Inside Partner Account Manager to work as part of our Global Partner Programs team.</p>
<p><b>
Position Summary:</b></p>
<p>
Sitecore is seeking an Inside Partner Account Manager to service and nurture an ecosystem of system integrators, consultancies, digital agencies and other solution providers who recommend and influence the selection of marketing technology to Sitecore’s clients and prospective clients. Candidates for these roles should be highly self-motivated, draw energy from building and maintaining personal relationships, and have passion for ensuring nothing less than an exceptional experience for their portfolio of accounts.</p>
<p><b>
Responsibilities:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Provide outstanding customer service and sales support to Affiliates and Partners.</li>
<li>
Serve as primary account manager for Affiliates and those Partners without assigned Partner Management in the field.</li>
<li>
Drive inbound and outbound calls, email and Web presentations to establish and maintain relationships with existing and new accounts.</li>
<li>
Train partners on program benefits with emphasis on driving active participation in training, certification and other enablement programs and activities.</li>
<li>
Generate, qualify and route sales leads from Partner and Affiliates.</li>
<li>
Create and update activity and pipeline reports.</li>
<li>
Identify capable and engaged affiliates and grow them into Solution Partner candidates.</li>
<li>
Recommend and drive internal process improvements needed to ensure high levels of Partner and Customer satisfaction.</li>
<li>
Collaborate with a variety of internal groups to ensure an exceptional experience for Affiliates and Partners.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Job Qualifications:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Highly self-motivated individual who thrives in a Sales, Telesales or Channel Sales Support environment.</li>
<li>
Naturally inquisitive, with a desire to want to understand the partners business to help drive growth of their practice within their business model.</li>
<li>
Proficiency in Microsoft Excel, Dynamics and/or Power BI a plus.</li>
<li>
Ability to work independently within a geographically distributed team.</li>
<li>
Exceptional interpersonal, verbal, written and presentation skills.</li>
<li>
Strong business and technical acumen.</li>
<li>
Capable of building and maintaining a large number of personal business relationships.</li>
</ul>
