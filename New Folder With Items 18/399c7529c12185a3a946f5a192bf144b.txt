<p><b>Position Description</b></p>
<p>One of Concur’s key competitive advantages is our ability to work with industry-leading partners to expand the scope and value of the client’s implementation of Concur’s employee spend management platform. Concur has created long-standing and mutually beneficial partnerships, generating revenue for both Concur and the partner and increasing the value to our joint customers. The SAP Concur Alliance Manager will be responsible for ensuring the ongoing success and value creation of these partnerships.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>What You Will Be Doing</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Ensuring that partner and Concur together is better for our mutual customers and our respective organizations</li>
<li>Developing and executing business plans and strategies with the partner to drive bi-lateral pipeline and revenue. This includes working with the partner’s alliance and marketing resources as well as Concur’s field engagement and channel marketing organizations to identify and execute strategic initiatives focused on bi-lateral pipeline generation and revenue production.</li>
<li>Launching new initiatives, including product launches, marketing campaigns and go-to-market strategies within the existing partnerships.</li>
<li>Resolving operational issues that involve the partner to satisfaction of the Concur customer.</li>
<li>Collaborating with Concur’s product, R&amp;D and marketing organizations to design and execute joint innovation projects with the partner.
</li>
<li>Establishing C-level/Executive relationships within Concur and the partner to ensure strategic alignment.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Position Requirements</b></p>
<p><b>Mandatory Skills</b></p>
<p>BA/BS degree or equivalent practical experience</p>
<ul>
<li>Minimum of five years of client and/or alliance management experience
</li>
<li>Ability to collaborate with multiple levels within Concur and our partners to develop and communicate business plans</li>
<li>Ability to lead by persuasion and influence rather than title or org chart placement</li>
<li>Strong business acumen and executive communication skills</li>
<li>Ability to prioritize and manage multiple priorities in a proactive, responsive and productive manner</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Preferred Qualifications</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Variety of experiences working with and for cloud-based software companies</li>
<li>Demonstrated track record of working effectively cross-functionally internally and externally</li>
<li>Familiarity with emerging channel partnership strategies, technologies and trends</li>
<li>Prior experience working regionally and nationally in support of an alliance partnership</li>
<br>
</ul>
