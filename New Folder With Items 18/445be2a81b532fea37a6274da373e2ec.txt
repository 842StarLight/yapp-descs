<h2>Customer Service Representative</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div><div>Trividia Health, Inc.<br>
Customer Service Representative I<br>
JD #735<br>
<br>
The customer service representative’s primary responsibility is to process customer’s orders, customer inquiries and participate in general department duties.<br>
<br>
ESSENTIAL DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES include the following. Other duties may be<br>
assigned.<br>
<br>
<ul>
<li>Daily order taking and order input (EDI, E-mail, Manual Entry), returns processing and credits as per QSI procedure QSI-03-00.</li>
<li>General department duties (i.e. filing, copying, faxing, etc.).</li>
<li>Provide daily, weekly, and monthly system reports from ERP System.</li>
<li>Order and shipment tracking via Internet.</li>
<li>Review, research and troubleshoot orders for accuracy; handle complaints as needed.</li>
<li>Good knowledge of company products and ability to provide customers with accurate information.</li>
<li>Work closely with sales, marketing, and clinical support to resolve customer issues.</li>
<li>Working knowledge of integrated enterprise systems (QAD, SAP, JDD preferred).</li>
<li>Intermediate experience with MS Office Products - Word and Excel (other application a plus).</li>
<li>Strong knowledge of the supply chain/business cycles.</li>
<li>Detail oriented with numbers and documentation.</li>
<li>Ability to approach problems with diplomacy and tact.</li>
<li>Ability to use good judgment and when necessary be assertive.</li>
<li>Ability to work in high volume, fast-pace environment with speed and accuracy.</li>
<li>Ability to interact in team environment or independently.</li>
<li>Clear, concise, professional written and verbal communication.</li>
</ul>
<br>
QUALIFICATIONS<br>
To perform this job successfully, an individual must be able to perform each<br>
essential duty satisfactorily. The requirements listed below are representative of the knowledge, skill,<br>
and/or ability required. Reasonable accommodations may be made to enable individuals with disabilities<br>
to perform the essential functions.<br>
<br>
EDUCATION and/or EXPERIENCE<br>
<ul>
<li>Minimum high school diploma or general education degree (GED);</li>
<li>3-5 years related experience</li>
<li>Bi-Lingual (English/Spanish) is preferred</li>
</ul>
<br>
LANGUAGE SKILLS<br>
Ability to read and comprehend simple instructions, short correspondence, and memos. Ability to write<br>
simple correspondence. Ability to effectively present information in one-on-one and small group<br>
situations to customers, clients, and other employees of the organization. Bilingual (English/Spanish)<br>
preferred.<br>
<br>
PHYSICAL DEMANDS<br>
The physical demands described here are representative of those that must be<br>
met by an employee to successfully perform the essential functions of this job. Reasonable<br>
accommodations may be made to enable individuals with disabilities to perform the essential functions.<br>
While performing the duties of this job, the employee is regularly required to sit and talk or hear. The<br>
employee frequently is required to use hands to handle or feel. The employee is occasionally required to<br>
walk and must frequently lift and/or move up to 10 pounds.<br>
