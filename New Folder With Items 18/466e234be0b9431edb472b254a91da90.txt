<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>SUMMARY</b></h2>
<p>The product manager is primarily responsible for defining market requirements, packaging those requirements into product releases, and managing the execution of those releases. Your success will be measured by revenue, expanded customer implementation and competitive win rates. You must understand the market needs of application teams, specifically product managers and developers, who need to embed analytics into their mission critical applications.</p>
<p>This position involves close interaction with development leads, product architects, and key customers. A strong technical background is required. You will serve as the internal evangelist for your product offering. You will write development stories, review schedules and ensure high quality on-time delivery of product.</p>
<p>Job duties include gathering requirements from existing and potential customers as well as recent evaluators, writing market requirement and product requirement documents, developing product backlogs, and monitoring the implementation of each product delivery.</p>

<p><b>ESSENTIAL DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES </b>include the following. Other duties may be assigned.</p>
<ul>
<li>Evaluate market requirements for new products including market research to determine key market trends and competitive threats.</li>
<li>Create market requirements and product requirements documents along with development stories to define product needs and capabilities.
</li>
<li>Work with product marketing, marketing, and sales teams to author including presentations, brochures, white papers, web site content, blogs, and emails for lead generation and sales execution.</li>
<li>Work with engineering teams to ensure product development tracks to schedule and specification.</li>
<li>Conduct technology assessments</li>
<li>Analyze the competitive landscape</li>
<li>Maintain the product portfolio roadmap</li>
<li>Monitor and incorporate industry innovations</li>
<li>Define user personas for individual products</li>
<li>Write product requirements and use scenarios</li>
<li>Maintain a status dashboard for all portfolio products</li>
<br>
</ul>
