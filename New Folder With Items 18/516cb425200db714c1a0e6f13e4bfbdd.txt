<h2>Assistant Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<b>Description:</b><br>
<b>Title:</b> ASSISTANT MANAGER
<br><b>Reports to:</b> General Manager
<br><b>Job Summary:</b> Manages the enterprise (the Company's Jimmy John's restaurant) to ensure outstanding customer service and high quality products are delivered in order to achieve restaurant profitability.
<br><br>
<b>Duties and Responsibilities:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Manages a staff of approximately 3 to 50 employees, in excess of 80 hours of labor each week</li>
<li>Assigns, oversees and evaluates work</li>
<li>Recommends promotion, transfer, or termination of employees based on performance</li>
<li>Provides on-the-job training for new employees</li>
<li>Delegates and is responsible for the ordering, receipt, storage and issuing of all food, labor, equipment, cleaning, and paper supplies for the unit to ensure a minimum loss from waste or theft</li>
<li>Supervises preparation, sales, and service of food</li>
<li>Forecasts food items</li>
<li>Estimates what amount of each food item will be consumed per shift in order to avoid customer threats and set the following shift up for success</li>
<li>Ensures that every customer receives world-class customer service</li>
<li>Routes deliveries and serves drivers to maximize delivery business and speed within the four walls of the restaurant</li>
<li>Completes and oversees daily food preparation (opening procedures, meat and vegetable slicing, bread production)</li>
<li>Completes and oversees Closing Procedures</li>
<li>Executes systems and procedures with 100% integrity and completeness</li>
<li>Completes daily and weekly paperwork</li>
<li>Responsible for 100% of the cash drawers at all times during the shift</li>
<li>Receives and stores product</li>
<li>Audits previous shift's systems and procedures for 100% integrity and completeness</li>
<li>Completes preventive maintenance and upkeep on store's equipment and supplies</li>
<li>Performs other related duties as requested</li>
<li>Displays a sense of urgency in all tasks</li>
</ul>
.
<br><br>
<b>Requirements:</b><br>
Knowledge, Skills, and Abilities
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Must be able to read and write and communicate in English</li>
<li>Position requires bending, reaching, stooping, climbing, repetitive motions, cognitive skills, standing and moving about the unit the entire workday</li>
<li>Must be able to lift 50 pounds</li>
<li>Must be at least 18 years of age, have a valid driver's license, reliable transportation, current automobile insurance and a clean driving record</li>
</ul>
<b>Special Requirements:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Willing to work evenings, holidays, weekends as business dictates</li>
<li>Ability to establish priorities, work independently and proceed with objectives with minimal supervision</li>
<li>Ability to handle and resolve customer threats and issues</li>
<li>Ability to handle and resolve employee issues</li>
<li>Skills to use a personal computer and various software packages (Word/Excel)</li>
<li>Ability to handle stress and a high-volume enterprise</li>
</ul>
