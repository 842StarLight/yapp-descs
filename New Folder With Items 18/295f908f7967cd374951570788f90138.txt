<h2>Commercial Intelligence Analyst</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<b>Description</b><br>
<div><p><b>Overview:</b></p></div>
<div>
Partners with management team, sales associates and divisional marketing force to promote key supplier initiatives. Demonstrates ability to proactively evaluate existing strategies, track key deliverables, and recommend changes when appropriate. Supports critical Sales and Marketing initiatives with quantitative / qualitative analysis. Provides reporting, analytics, and support to front line sales and executive management.</div>
<div><b>
Duties and Responsibilities:</b></div>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>
Track, review, verify, analyze and report accurate results to Commercial leadership, field team, and/or suppliers in a timely manner</li>
<li>
Provide Sales and Marketing stakeholders with a data-driven roadmap for sales execution</li>
<li>
Run standard and ad hoc reports for business and supplier needs</li>
<li>
Identify potential brand risks and opportunities</li>
<li>
Cross-compare customer selling opportunities with sales performance and brand challenges/initiatives</li>
<li>
Recap performance and volume goals</li>
<li>
Evaluate past program success/failed trends; use trend analysis to advise stakeholders on proposed programs</li>
<li>
Provide accurate insights on live program performance and evaluate ROI on key initiatives</li>
<li>
Follow established reporting and documentation standards to ensure uniformity throughout the organization</li>
<li>
Developperformancemanagementinitiativesincludingcompensation/incentivedesign,developmentof KPIs,etc.</li>
<li>
Analyze data available to anticipate potential issues and proactively model potential solutions</li>
<li>
Administer sales systems including: creating and/or running reports, creating and/or cascading goals, account and territory management, staff training, etc.</li>
<li>
Prepare commercial analysis that recaps business performance by trade channel and sales inputs</li>
<li>
Organize and maintain account segmentation data within Information Technology system; review and update quarterly</li>
<li>
Update master records to define customer account segmentation</li>
<li>
Classify accounts based on volume and/or revenues</li>
<li>
Track competitive pricing activity based on sales input</li>
<li>
Analyze and rank teams and sales representatives that fail to deliver minimal standards</li>
<li>
Perform maintenance of data dimensions and routine updating of tracking tools to promote accuracy and accountability</li>
<li>
Perform other duties as assigned</li>
</ul>
