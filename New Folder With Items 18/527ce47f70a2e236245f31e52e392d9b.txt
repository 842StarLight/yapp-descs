Description Summary
<br><br>
We are looking for an outgoing, self-motivated and multitalented professional to manage our relations with China-focused external organizations and grow our business with Chinese enterprises operating in the Americas.
<br><br>
China Telecom (Americas) Corporation (CTA) is an active member of the Chinese business community in the US. We partner closely with a number of China-focused external organizations, such as chambers of commerce, trade groups and media outlets, in order to better connect with our stakeholders and customers, promote international economic cooperation and make a positive social impact in our local communities.
<br><br>
The Corporate Relations Specialist will build and manage the relations between us and these external organizations to achieve CTA?s corporate communications and marketing objectives.
<br><br>
You will play a leadership role in planning and executing marketing campaigns aimed at Chinese enterprises operating the US. You will also be an integral part of the Corporate Communications Team, fighting to protect and promote our public image.
<br><br>
You must have strong background and passion in event marketing, event organization, networking, media relations and corporate writing. You need to be fluent in both Chinese and English. Leadership, budget management, and creative design are also valuable skillsets for this position.
