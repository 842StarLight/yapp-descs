<h2>Landscaping Operations Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p>As Operations Manager, you will provide leadership to all areas within the company including sales, operations, customer service, safety, financial stability and administrative functions. Exemplifying our code of values, you show respect and courtesy to all customers and employees.</p>
<p>
You are a proactive leader and self-starter who can interact with the public and our employees. You have a strong work ethic and are able to manage time to effectively meet deadlines. You have experience in landscaping and at least two years of supervisory experience, as well as proven communication skills with supervisors, employees, and customers.</p>
<p><b>
Specific Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Coach, train, and mentor staff to ensure they are consistently delivering high quality service</li>
<li>Maintain a clean and well-stocked office and shop</li>
<li>Proactive customer relations, including mitigating damages and liability issues</li>
<li>Secure and maintain telecommunications system</li>
<li>Ensure all employees receive the required education and training necessary to perform their jobs and grow in their career</li>
<li>Improve upon current processes to ensure quality, profitability, and future growth</li>
<li>Capable of taking over complete responsibilities when the owner or Production Manager/Team Leader is out of office</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Job Requirements:</b></p>
<ul>
<li><b>Background in landscape industry 5 years</b></li>
<li><b>At least 5 years supervisory experience</b></li>
<li><b>Valid Driver's License</b></li>
<li><b>Strong written and verbal communication skills</b></li>
<li><b>Computer literate, with working knowledge of work processing, business software and spreadsheet applications</b></li>
<li><b>Wall design and paver installation a must</b></li>
</ul>
<p><b>Benefits:</b> Benefits package varies by location</p>

We are actively interviewing for this position - Apply today and our hiring manager will follow up!</b></p>
