<h2>Implementation Consultant, Technical Publications</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>Job Description<br>
<br>
<p><b>Summary</b></p>
<p>
EAC seeks an Implementation Consultant to deliver technical publication solutions to its customers.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
The Implementation Consultant’s role is to implement solutions that support customer needs and achieve customer goals. The Implementation Consultant works with the project team to understand customer goals and requirements, understand the approach for executing the project, and implementing a successful solution. Someone in this role works with project teams to ensure alignment and success for customers. The Implementation Consultant provides technical leadership and guidance to customers and team members as a subject matter expert.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
EAC Product Development Solutions (EAC) is a complete source for product development. We provide a full suite of product development software, and offer consulting and training services to support every phase of product development. EAC is ready to offer our brightest ideas to ensure you enjoy the best solutions in the industry.</p>
<p><b>
Job functions</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Understand customer goals and requirements and provide technical leadership.</li>
<li>
Implement customer projects and solutions.</li>
<li>
Implement data migration procedures, tools, and strategies.</li>
<li>
Provide support to sales and business development teams.</li>
</ul>
<p>
Provide technical leadership to customers.</p>
</div>
