<h2>Digital Marketing Internship - Paid</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Directive is searching for an energetic and passionate student looking to learn and engage with an industry leading agency. Interns will work directly with the sales and marketing team to attract new business. They will also play a key role in marketing/sales meetings to learn and interact with the strategic-logistical processes of a digital marketing agency. Takeaways will include hands-on, industry experiencing for skills like prospecting, sequencing, segmentation, follow up, direct mail, account based marketing, SEO, PPC, and content.</p>
<p><b>
Requirements:</b></p>
<p>
Ideal candidates will have strong writing experience, certifications by Google, previous internships/experience, and are adept in the use of software tools. Must be available on weekdays for three to four days a week.</p>
<p><b>
Tasks:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Interns will be responsible for the following tasks in a hierarchal order:</li>
<li>
Completing assigned reading and prepaid learning modules</li>
<li>
Working with sales to send creative direct mail to target accounts</li>
<li>
Crafting innovative account based marketing campaigns</li>
<li>
Prospecting &amp; following accounts via social media profiles</li>
<li>
Assisting the sales team in identifying new opportunities</li>
<li>
Participating in sales related office tasks like addressing direct mail letters, proofreading proposals, sitting in on sales calls, etc.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Learning Opportunities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Exclusive inside access to marketing and sales meetings</li>
<li>
Sitting in on sales calls with CEO and Account Execs</li>
<li>
Access to prepaid learning modules such as Distilled.edu, etc</li>
<li>
Exposure to account based sales outreach</li>
<li>
Guidance on social media prospecting and promotion</li>
<li>
Vital experience learning how to connect long term projects with daily inputs</li>
<li>
Meetings with mentor to discuss learnings and career goals</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Compensation:</b></p>
<p>
Interns are not guaranteed a full-time job offer at the completion of their internship. Compensation for their assistance and time will come in the form of a $500 a month stipend + industry experience and hands-on learning model to better engage with new opportunities. Opportunities for full time employment is our goal and is evaluated on a case by case basis.</p>
<p><b>
About Directive:</b></p>
<p>
Directive is a digital marketing agency specializing in comprehensive search campaigns for industry leading B2B firms. We were founded on the principle of learn, engage, create. True innovation comes from combining a diligent and disciplined foundation with passionate minds always looking towards "what's next." We are a tight-knit group of like-minded innovators that are passionate about bettering our business and bettering ourselves. We hope that you can join us to start learning, engaging, and creating with the Directive team!</p>
