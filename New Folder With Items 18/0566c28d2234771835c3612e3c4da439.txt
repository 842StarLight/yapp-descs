<h2>Center Associate - 30+ hours/week</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>A Center Associate provides excellent customer service to all retail customers. He/She receives and processes packages for shipment, expertly packs items when needed, operates copiers, fax machines, binding equipment, laminating machines, and point-of-sale devices and assists customers with their printing and copying needs. The Associate will receive formal training/learn best practices in order to provide customers with the best products and value possible.</p>
<p>The ideal Center Associate has retail sales experience, strong computer, internet and math skills, a high school diploma, a friendly, helpful attitude, and a professional appearance. He/she must be a fast learner who makes an effort to learn all aspects of the business in the shortest possible time and enjoy working as part of a team.</p>
<p>Store hours are M - F 8:00 a.m. - 6:30 p.m., and Saturdays 9:00 a.m. - 4:00 p.m. Shifts generally will be 4-7 hours long, depending on the day. Total of approximately 30 hours per week, although there is quite a bit of flexibility. Applicants must be able to work at least two Saturdays per month.</p>
<p>RESPONSIBILITIES**</p>
<ul>
<li>Delivers outstanding customer service to walk-in customers and telephone inquiries</li>
<li>Practices good listening skills with customers, The UPS Store team members, and leadership</li>
<li>Takes ownership of any shipping problems and offers viable solutions</li>
<li>Takes action to learn all product and service offerings, alternative solutions, and industry trends</li>
<li>Operates all equipment, software, and devices in an expert fashion and is willing to teach others</li>
<li>Maintains a clean, organized, and safe working environment</li>
<li>Performs other duties as required</li>
</ul>
<p><b>QUALIFICATIONS</b>*</p>
<ul>
<li>Neat, clean, and professional appearance</li>
<li>Non-smoking environment</li>
<li>High school diploma</li>
<li>Valid, unrestricted driver's license</li>
<li>Able to lift over 40 pounds</li>
<li>Strong verbal and written communication skills, including spelling and math</li>
<li>Prompt, reliable, and responsible</li>
<li>Willing/able to work a 25+ / less than 40 hour-per-week for a 5 day work week</li>
<li>Have the ability to obtain Notary Public</li>
<li>Retail/customer service experience a plus</li>
</ul>
