<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>FOX 5 KVVU a Meredith Corporation TV Station

<p></p>
<p><b>I. Job Summary </b>| <i>Major goals and objectives.
</i></p>
<p>FOX5 KVVU is looking for a dynamic, motivated Design Director to join our award-winning creative team. Daily responsibilities include leading and managing a team of graphic artists while designing and creating motion, still graphics and print for use in News, Local Programming, Promotions, Sales, and various other station projects.
</p>
<p></p>
<p>Strong emphasis on working with real-time on-air graphics systems, such as Chyron Lyric and Prime. Required skills include an advanced working knowledge of After Effects, Photoshop, Illustrator and Cinema 4D. Strong leadership and interpersonal skills a must.
</p>
