<h2>Client Success Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>Job Title: Client Success Manager</p>
<p>Department: Sales</p>
<p>Reports to: Vice President, Client Success</p>
<p></p>
<p>Job Summary:</p>
<p>The Client Success Manager is primarily responsible for the relationship, retention and growth of Calero’s existing enterprise customer base. The mission is to gain a deep understanding of our customers’ business needs in order to maximize value and delight our customers.</p>
<p></p>
<p>Duties and Responsibilities of the job:</p>
<ul>
<li>Meet quota-based revenue retention and revenue growth targets within assigned customer portfolio of customers as defined by the individual compensation plan</li>
<li>Engage the channel partner team, client’s project sponsors, client executive leaders and solution managers to establish relationships, monitor success and value from the client’s perspective and plan for account growth</li>
<li>Provide the client’s perspective and advocate to Calero’s internal stakeholders</li>
<li>Understand client’s business strategy and environment related to Calero offerings to identify strengths, weaknesses, opportunities and threats</li>
<li>Monitor, analyze and report on the specific qualitative and quantitative customer health information to identify areas of risk, improvement and growth</li>
<li>Plan, negotiate and secure contract renewals directly with clients and through channel partners when applicable</li>
<li>Account planning and execution including identifying the decision maker/influencer hierarchy and client marketing map related to Calero principal solution offerings</li>
<li>On-going collaboration with Calero business unit delivery teams focused on shared goals related to customer health, retention and growth</li>
<li>Effectively resolve client expectations that are unrealistic or contrary to contractual agreements</li>
<li>Prepare business case, pricing, proposal, presentation and renewal documents</li>
<li>Create and refine client engagement tools and processes with the Success team</li>
<li>Coach clients on best practice utilization of Calero solutions</li>
<li>Timely entry and maintenance of client, opportunity and operational data into Calero systems of record</li>
</ul>
<p>Education:</p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelors’ degree in a relevant field</li>
</ul>
<p>Experience and Training:</p>
<ul>
<li>5-10 years of Account Management or Sales experience with Fortune 1000 clients for solutions related to Software, IT, SaaS and/or Managed Services</li>
<li>Proven track record of meeting quota, driving retention and creating growth within assigned base of customers</li>
<li>Experience selling solutions where annual recurring revenue per client was greater than $100,000</li>
<li>Experience/training in large account management best practices</li>
<li>Previous experience in one or more of Calero’s principal solutions is a plus</li>
<li>Highly skilled in leading calls and meetings to create consultative dialogue that is open, candid and results-oriented</li>
<li>Self-confidence to be able to lead when appropriate and the ability to work well within the team</li>
<li>Consultative and structured problem-solving skills</li>
<li>Experience developing and presenting an effective business case and proposals</li>
<li>Exceptional organizational, presentation and communication skills, both verbal and written</li>
</ul>
<p>Technical Knowledge:</p>
<ul>
<li>Microsoft Office Suite (Excel, Word, PowerPoint)</li>
</ul>
<p>Contact with Others</p>
<ul>
<li>Senior Management and all staff in each Calero location</li>
</ul>
<p>Confidentiality:</p>
<p>This position will be exposed to extremely confidential information and discretion is paramount. A confidentiality, non-compete, and non-solicitation agreement will be required.</p>
<p></p>
<p>Management Responsibility</p>
<p>This position is seen as an individual contributor with no direct supervisory responsibilities. Minimum 25% travel is required.</p>
