<p><b>OVERVIEW:</b></p>
<p>
The Director of Omnichannel Marketing will have the critical role of defining, championing, and deploying new channels between IEWC and our customers, while also innovating current channels through technologyand process improvements. In doing so, this will create a 360° view of our customer that ensures a seamless, Omnichannel experience that simplifies and accelerates how our customers do business with us in the way they prefer. Through diligent technology discovery and voice-of-customer (VOC) research, communication, and project management skills, this individual will proactively act as a champion for innovative technologies to exceed customer expectations to grow customer loyalty and share-of-wallet.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
KEY RESPONSIBILITIES</b></p>
<p><b>
Omnichannel Technology Roadmap</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Perform technology discovery and research to identify additional channels of connectivity with our customers and suppliers alike. Technologies would include (but not limited to):
<ul>
<li>Web / eCommerce</li>
<li>Vendor Managed Inventory (VMI) technologies (barcode, RFID, vending, etc.)</li>
<li>Mobile App</li>
<li>EDI</li>
<li>Supplier and Customer Portals</li>
<li>Punch-Out solutions for procurement software platforms</li>
<li>Technologies to drive direct sales channel efficiency, speed, and consistency
<ul>
<li>Customer Relationship Management (CRM) platforms</li>
<li>Service/Case Management</li>
<li>Configure Price Quote (CPQ) technologies</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>Work closely with commercial teams to get first-hand experiential (VOC) insights into how they procure and consume wire, cable, and wire management products today, with a focus on discovering unmet needs and customer delighters for IEWC to address</li>
<li>Lead planning with internal IT resources and 3rd party development firms to translate business requirements into technical requirements to create project plans, budgets, and timelines</li>
<li>Participate in the annual strategic planning and operating plan processes, helping to maximize the investment dollars in our Omnichannel marketing technologies</li>
<br>
</ul>
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Initiative Leadership</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Based on the Omnichannel Technology roadmap, serve as the business’ initiative leader (or direct and mentor members of your team leading initiatives) to execute on projects and programs to bring the roadmap to fruition</li>
<li>Create and maintain business case / ROI analyses, as well as project budgets and timelines</li>
<li>Serve as strategic lead in relationship management of 3rd party firms executing on marketing technology development for IEWC, driving partnership and accountability</li>
<li>Along with team, lead the “go live” planning and execution to successfully deploy, train, and equip our commercial and marketing team with tools and messaging for a successful launch</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p>
</p>
<p><b>Team Leadership</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Manage team of marketing technology professionals, empowering the individuals to perform at a high level and develop professionally</li>
<li>Work with team members to set roadmaps and project plans for existing channels and technologies (website, CRM, etc.)</li>
<li>Set annual and quarterly goals with quantifiable metrics and deadlines</li>
<li>Create a strong team culture that excels at collaborating with other departments and executes to achieve IEWC’s objectives and vision</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p>
</p>
<p><b>QUALIFICATIONS:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>7 or more years of related professional experience</li>
<li>A Bachelor's degree in Engineering, Computer Science, Management Information Systems (MIS), or business field required</li>
<li>2 or more years of experience managing a team of marketing or technology professionals required</li>
<li>A valid driver’s license.</li>
<li>Ability to work in a Windows environment and standard MS Office applications</li>
<li>Strong experience with MS Excel is required</li>
<li>Experience with SAP and Salesforce.com (or equivalent CRM) strongly preferred</li>
<li>Experience with web and SEO technologies strongly preferred</li>
<li>Strong technical background and experience related to web, CRM, BI, and marketing automation technologies</li>
<li>Demonstrated verbal and written communication skills, focused on internal and external customers, including executive level</li>
<li>Excellent interpersonal skills and experienced at dealing with users at all levels, individually and in groups</li>
<li>Demonstrated project management experience and skills</li>
<li>Experience and passion for being a 'change agent' within a team and the broader organization</li>
<li>Experience with training delivery and material creation</li>
<li>Training and/or professional experience in Continuous Improvement disciplines preferred</li>
<li>Strong analytical and problem-solving skills</li>
<li>Highly self-motivated and directed</li>
<li>Passion for leadership and developing others</li>
<li>Up to 10% travel required</li>
<br>
</ul>
