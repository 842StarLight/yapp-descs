<p><b>Business Development Sales Representative</b><br><b>$40k-$50k + Uncapped Monthly Commission + Unlimited PTO, Generous Benefits, 401k</b></p>
<p>Workzone is a privately owned cloud-based project management tool based in suburban Philadelphia. Founded in 2000, Workzone was among the first SaaS project management tools on the market.</p>
<p>We are looking for a Business Development Representative to qualify sales opportunities and open new doors for our inside sales team. This entry level position lays the foundation for a successful high tech sales or marketing career. There is a path for career growth within Workzone for the right candidate, who is willing to learn and work toward that goal. This team member should have charisma that allows them to build rapport quickly and easily. Our superstar team possesses grit, stamina, and resiliency that allows them to complete a high number of daily calls. Every individual maintains a positive attitude, is highly motivated, and driven to exceed their goals.</p>
<p>What you will do:</p>
<ul>
<li>Schedule discovery calls with qualified prospects, and set appointments and online demos for Account Executives.</li>
<li>Follow up with inbound leads - qualify leads and opportunities.</li>
<li>Achieve designated monthly quota for attended booked calls</li>
<li>Make a high volume of daily emails and phone calls to decision makers.</li>
<li>Use and maintain BDR prospects and lead data in CRM.</li>
<li>Stay current on industry trends and maintain extensive knowledge of competitor product offerings.</li>
<li>Create and refine prospecting and selling strategies and test key hypotheses in collaboration with the marketing team.</li>
<li>Maintain strong relationships with marketing and sales teams through clear communication and follow-up.</li>
</ul>
<p>What you will need to have:</p>
<ul>
<li>BA, BS degree or comparable experience</li>
<li>0-2 years experience in B2B cold calling or comparable experience (IT or SaaS a plus)</li>
<li>Goal driven mindset</li>
<li>Open to coaching and feedback</li>
<li>Excellent verbal and written communication skills</li>
<li>Highly motivated by asking probing questions, gathering information, and converting leads</li>
<li>Organizational skills to balance and prioritize a high volume of tasks and objectives on a daily basis.</li>
<li>Organized, detail oriented skill set and understand the need to track everything you do.</li>
<li>Natural inclination to pick up the phone and talk to anyone, about anything, at anytime.</li>
</ul>
