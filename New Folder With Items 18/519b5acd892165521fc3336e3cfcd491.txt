<h2>Accounting Assistant (Part-time)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>On The Go Hosiery</b> is a national Consumer Products Company seeking a Part Time <b>Accounting Assistant </b>to support our wholesale operations. We are a National Leading supplier of Women's and Men's Hosiery, Socks, and Legwear Products to grocery, drugstore, and mass retailers.</p>
<p><b><i>This is a perfect position for someone who needs to work around a school bus schedule or has similar work-life balance challenges. Must be OUTSTANDING at Microsoft Excel - this is used throughout all aspects of the job.</i></b></p>
<p><b><i>Work Hours are flexible, ~ 9:30 – 2:30.</i></b></p>
<p>We go to market with a DSD Store-Direct Merchandising Strategy that generates RESULTS! Our team of Sales and Merchandising Reps interact with store level &amp; district level management in key retailers to maximize sales with our Retail Partners..</p>
<p>We pride ourselves on achieving the highest standards of integrity, value and quality. Our success comes from our quality products and values-driven people.</p>
<p>If you are genuinely a nice person, act professionally, and are REALLY good at accounting, then congratulations – you found your next job.</p>
<p><b><i>THE DETAILS: </i></b></p>
<p>On The Go Hosiery, a leading manufacturer &amp; distributor of hosiery products to the food, drug, and mass trades, is seeking a Part-Time Accounting Assistant (20-25 hours/week).</p>
<p><b>RESPONSIBILITIES: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Prepare monthly bank reconciliations on Excel.</li>
<li>Prepare and Post Bi-Monthly Payroll entries using Payroll Service Reports and Excel template.</li>
<li>Reconcile company lines of credit and loan accounts to meet payment obligations while minimizing interest expenses.</li>
<li>Prepare and transmit weekly sales reports on Excel.</li>
<li>Reviewing and entering purchase orders and other A/P work. Reviewing entries for accuracy, and posting and preparing reports.</li>
<li>Reconciling multiple credit card and similar accounts on a monthly basis, scrutinizing charges for appropriateness and accuracy.</li>
<li>Processing batch-level invoice files ON-TIME on a weekly basis. This involves importing the data from the route scheduling system into MAS90 , reviewing summary reports and detail paperwork for consistency &amp; accuracy, entering missed documents and making necessary adjustments, and posting the invoices into MAS90.</li>
<li>Preparing and transmiting electronic and paper statements to customers for timely remittance. Processing Customer payments against open invoices for accurate A/R posting and reporting.</li>
<li>Reviewing and posting Inventory transactions ON-TIME on a weekly basis. This involves importing the data from the route scheduling system into MAS90, reviewing summary reports and detail paperwork for consistency &amp; accuracy, entering missed documents and making necessary adjustments, and posting the inventory transactions into MAS90.</li>
<li>Fulfilling web orders.</li>
<li>Other duties as assigned</li>
</ul>

<ul>
<li>
<b><i>VERY STRONG </i></b><b>Microsoft</b> <b>Excel skills</b> – much of this job is done in Excel. <b>YOU MUST COMPLETE THE EXCEL ASSESSMENT TO BE CONSIDERED FOR THIS POSITION. IT WILL BE EMAILED TO YOUR INDEED EMAIL ON FILE AS SOON AS YOU APPLY THROUGH THE INDEED SYSTEM FOR THIS POSITION.</b> Also feel free to email us directly with any Excel work samples that you feel highlight your strengths with this software. Email to hr @ onthegohosiery.com, subject line “Excel Samples”</li>
<li>Proven ability to multi-task with strong attention to detail, providing accurate, timely accounting work in a fast-paced small business environment</li>
<li>Working knowledge of accounting processes: cash management, accounts receivable/payable, on-line banking and payroll/benefit services, general ledger, inventory processes</li>
<li>Able to handle confidential files and work well both independently and as part of a team</li>
<li>Degree in accounting or business with at least two years experience</li>
<li>Experience with MAS90 or similar accounting system, preferred</li>
</ul>
