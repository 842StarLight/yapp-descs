<h2>CADD DRAFTER</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>CADD Drafter will create and update drawings and documents used for the design, production, sales, and use of products. Will provide documentation of work performed and be required to do any and all tasks deemed necessary by his or her supervisor for the safe and efficient operation of the company.</p>
<p>MINIMUM QUALIFICATIONS</p>
<p>This position requires the following minimum qualifications: accredited Associate degree in Mechanical Drafting Technology or equivalent; driver’s license; ability to communicate verbally and in writing effectively to the extent required by the position, including the ability to explain technical concepts to non-technically oriented people; ability to effectively and cooperatively work with other people both in and outside the company; familiar with standard mechanical drafting practices including orthographic, isometric drawing methods and Geometric Dimensioning and Tolerancing; proficient in the usage of 3D parametric modeling software “SolidWorks;” familiar with Arabic and Metric measurements systems; general computer skills using: Windows; MS Word; MS Excel; MS Outlook; Windows File Explorer; Windows Internet Explorer and other software as assigned and trained; general computer hardware abilities with disc drives, CD’s, DVD’s, and pointing devices; acquainted with manufacturing machinery and tools and their usage, including mills, lathes, saws, welding, soldering, wrenches, etc.; familiar with mechanical principles including levers, gears, friction, bending, stress, etc.; familiar with common measuring tools including calipers, radius gauges, rules, etc.; ability to maintain established records and files; ability to use various database software including; Visual Manufacturing ERP software, Access databases; excellent attention to detail; must be able to imagine three dimensional objects and visualize the effect of rotating them; and must be able to meet the working conditions and physical demands identified below.</p>
