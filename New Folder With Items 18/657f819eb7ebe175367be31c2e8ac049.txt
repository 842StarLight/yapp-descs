<h2>Production Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>PURPOSE / NATURE OF POSITION</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Responsible for achieving targeted production volumes by managing line operations consistent with the Company’s safety, quality, cost, high performance, training and development standards.</li>
<li>Responsible for providing appropriate leadership and support to the operation teams.</li>
<li>Responsible for promoting teamwork and development, so that the location, team and individual goals are fulfilled.</li>
<li>Responsible for using a constructive and fair coaching style to promote teamwork and fairness throughout the site operations.</li>
<li>Responsible for cost management, product quality, maintenance of the assets and continuous improvement.</li>
<li>Responsible for development and improvement of processes for new products and for improvement of manufacturing performance and maintenance of systems to ensure process control.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>RESPONSIBILITIES</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Maintain compliance for all Health, Safety and Environmental policies and procedures within the Company.</li>
<li>Ensure a safe and positive work environment free of harassment and hostilities.</li>
<li>Enhance best practices or work methods for continuous improvement.</li>
<li>Support Company Strategy and goals both long term and short term.</li>
<li>Provide leadership and guidance to all operation personnel working with the Operation Team Leaders.</li>
<li>Troubleshoots complex production and quality problems.</li>
<li>Ensure the line operates as per production plan.</li>
<li>Communicate problems and issues to appropriate resources in a timely manner.</li>
<li>Initiate appropriate action for non-compliance to rules, policies and expected performance.</li>
<li>Responsible for all team metrics and reporting.</li>
<li>Identify employee development needs.</li>
<li>Assist in the training of operational and maintenance personnel as required.</li>
<li>Promote and support the “high performance” culture within the site.</li>
<li>Provide input to New Product Requests, work with Technical Services to develop and commercialize new products as required.</li>
<li>Lead process improvements affecting safety, yield, productivity, quality and recycling including planning and execution.</li>
<li>Establish and verify process capability.</li>
<li>Establish equipment calibration requirements.</li>
<li>Provide Customer Technical Support.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>CORE CAPABILITIES</b></p>
<ul>
<li>BS Engineering Degree with a minimum of 10 years manufacturing experience.</li>
<li>Six Sigma Black Belt or Green Belt preferred.</li>
<li>Understanding of nonwovens products and process desired.</li>
<li>Understanding and working knowledge of statistical techniques.</li>
<li>Excellent computer skills and familiarity with most common software packages.</li>
<li>Able to build and lead cross-functional teams.</li>
<li>Experience with high volume operations.</li>
<li>Polished presentation, business acumen and facilitation skills.</li>
<li>Flexible, innovative and comfortable in setting own priorities.</li>
<li>Understanding of Quality Assurance/Improvement tools and techniques and Quality Management Systems.</li>
<li>Understanding of Health, Safety and Environmental Management Systems.</li>
<li>Project Management skills desired.</li>
<li>Start-up Projects experience highly desired.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>PRIMARY WORKING RELATIONSHIPS</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Reports to Plant Manager.</li>
<li>Shift Managers, Shift Leads and production associates are the direct reports.</li>
<li>Member of Plant Leadership Team.</li>
<li>Provides customer support, working closely with R&amp;D, Technical Services and Sales &amp; Marketing.</li>
<li>Customer interface.</li>
<li>Critical Material Supplier interface.</li>
<li>Interfaces with other Fitesa resources.</li>
</ul>
