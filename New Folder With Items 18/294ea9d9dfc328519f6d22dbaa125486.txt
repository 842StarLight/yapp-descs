<h2>Brand Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Overview: Responsible for driving the success of SmithFoods brands in the marketplace. Will specifically lead SMITH’S and evolving licensed brands efforts while overseeing the management of Ruggles®, Ajoyo™ and emerging brands. The position will take the lead on marketing analysis and strategies, keeping current on emerging trends and issues. This position will interface with sales, operations and finance to assure that strategies are feasible and will achieve overall company goals. This position will also work closely outside company vendors.</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Essential Duties and Responsibilities:
</b></p>
<ul><li> Product management: responsible for planning, managing and coordinating the brand portfolio through product development, pricing, promotion and positioning; creating and managing marketing programs and business plans to achieve revenue, profit and market share goals</li></ul>
<ul><li> Identify new product opportunities: work with product development team to develop innovative, consumer-driven products</li></ul>
<ul><li> Collaborate with sales team to develop and implement high impact, innovative, effective trade marketing programs to support distribution expansion and increase consumer trials and buy rates</li></ul>
<ul><li> Manage all marketing functions pertaining to assigned brand portfolios, including promotion, research, packaging, promotion, analysis and P&amp;L</li></ul>
<ul><li> Conduct brand, segment, and category analysis to evaluate performance against business objectives, identify competitive threats, and highlight product strengths/weaknesses</li></ul>
<ul><li> Assist in development of brand strategies across all functions, including advertising, research and consumer promotions</li></ul>
<ul><li> Develop effective consumer promotions to build awareness, generate trial and drive volume/distribution. Conduct post-promotion analysis to measure effectiveness</li></ul>
<ul><li> Direct internal cross-functional teams to develop and implement strategies</li></ul>
<p>Additional Duties and Responsibilities:</p>
<ul><li> Additional duties as assigned by supervisor</li></ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Skills and Abilities:
</b></p>
<ul><li> Strategic planning</li></ul>
<ul><li> Brand building experience</li></ul>
<ul><li> Experience in consumer marketing to drive engagement using traditional, digital, social advertising</li></ul>
<ul><li> Strong project and process management skills</li></ul>
<ul><li> Knowledge of consumer research techniques</li></ul>
<ul><li> Knowledge of regulatory related to packaging claims</li></ul>
<ul><li> Strong analytic skills</li></ul>
<ul><li> Syndicated data experience (IRI, Nielsen, Mintel, Consumer Shopper insights)</li></ul>
<ul><li> Excellent verbal and written communications skills</li></ul>
<ul><li> Excellent organizational skills and attention to detail</li></ul>
<ul><li> Proficiency with MS Office</li></ul>
