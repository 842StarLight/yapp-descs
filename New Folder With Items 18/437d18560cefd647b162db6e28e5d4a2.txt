<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities</b></h3>
<div>
<p>Working as part of the global Corporate Communications team, the Senior Manager of GlobalAnalyst Relations is a strategic addition to Veeam’s worldwide marketing organization. The role will be responsible for cultivating Veeam’s relationships with analyst firms across the globe. The successful candidate will craft and own the company’s dialogue among analyst firms that influence customers and prospects globally, ensuring that Veeam is recognized as <i>the </i>market leader in Cloud Data Management.</p>
<ul>
<li>
Execute and help define a global analyst relations strategy, including goals and objectives, guiding principles, measurement mechanisms, templates and forms</li>
<li>
Work with diverse teams to generate programs that are specifically tailored to analyst communities; coach the global team to think of analysts as core to Veeam’s GTM audience for every activity and strategic communication</li>
<li>
Build communication tools to consistently touch the analyst community</li>
<li>
Serve as a strategic point of contact for analyst relations activities, including suggestions for potential initiatives to drive awareness and understanding within tier one and two firms globally</li>
<li>
Support the analyst relations team to cultivate ongoing relationships with firms such as Gartner and IDC, identify new firms to engage, and ensure that Veeam is top-of-mind for all reports about Cloud Data Management (thought leadership)</li>
<li>
Orchestrate world class briefings, strategy days and analyst events and drive Inquiry programs</li>
<li>
Work closely with Product and Solutions Marketing to understand priorities, test messaging and position our solutions for multiple segments including SMB, Enterprise and vertical markets</li>
<li>
Work with Veeam’s leadership team to develop entries and submissions for core analyst reports (such as Gartner Magic Quadrants)</li>
<li>
Arrange and drive strategic analyst engagements that will support sales/business planning</li>
<li>
Disseminate market intelligence to the leadership team and wider Veeam stakeholders</li>
<li>
Special projects as assigned</li>
</ul>
