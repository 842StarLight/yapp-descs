<div><b>
The Role</b></div>
<div>
The product marketing team ensures that Medallia is at the forefront of Experience Management, that our solutions are differentiated, and we have a winning go to market strategy. Reporting to the VP of Industry &amp; Solutions Marketing, you will drive the vision, messaging, and go-to-market strategy for the Hospitality vertical working with Medallia’s product management, sales, marketing, and services teams.</div>
</div>
<div><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities</b></h3>
<div><ul>
<li>
Deliver creative and compelling messaging that clearly articulates and reinforces Medallia’s unique brand and value proposition specifically for Hospitality and it’s associated buyer personas and use cases.</li>
<li>
Work with the broader marketing team to build and execute an industry growth plan to drive awareness and demand for Medallia in Hospitality.</li>
<li>
Partner with product management to distill key functionality and benefits into core product marketing messages and assets, including engaging product demonstrations for stage, in videos and customer, partner, and prospect engagements.</li>
<li>
Drive the creation of thought leadership content such as blog posts, webinars, and white papers that evangelize our message.</li>
<li>
Develop market/competitive positioning and rationale to demonstrate how Medallia works and excels for your vertical. Partner with sales to differentiate against competition and accelerate opportunities.</li>
<li>
Develop effective sales plays and training materials, making it easy for sales to understand our industry narrative, product offerings and key positioning.</li>
<li>
Create content and work with campaign and field marketing teams to drive field marketing, online events, and digital campaigns to drive pipeline and accelerate opportunities.</li>
<li>
Communicate customer requirements and provide input to product management on product enhancements and future product direction.</li>
<li>
Engage industry analysts to communicate Medallia’s strategy, roadmap, unique value proposition and customer success.</li>
<li>
Be an excellent communicator who can engage internal leaders and teams across functions and who can engage external stakeholders.</li>
</ul></div>
</div></div>
<div><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Minimum Qualifications</b></h3>
<div><ul>
<li>
Minimum of 5 years proven product marketing experience, or related, in tech environment.</li>
<li>
Track record of marketing technical innovations to users and buyers.</li>
<li>
Experience creating product narratives and positioning products in compelling ways.</li>
<li>
Marketing portfolio with examples showing your writing and visual communication style.</li>
<li>
Strong interpersonal and teamwork skills; ability to influence without authority, including senior management.</li>
<li>
Analytical and critical thinking mindset; previous experience interpreting and summarizing data with actionable insights.</li>
<li>
Strong written and oral communication skills; proven ability to present compelling narratives in public settings and to internal audiences.</li>
<li>
Strong sales and customer orientation with a passion for software products.</li>
<li>
Bachelor's degree or equivalent relevant practical experience.</li>
</ul></div>
</div></div>
<div><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Preferred Qualifications</b></h3>
<div><ul>
<li>
Experience in the Hospitality industry.</li>
<li>
Minimum of 2 years enterprise software industry experience (CRM, Marketing Automation, BI, etc.)</li>
<li>
MBA or other equivalent advanced degree.</li>
</ul></div>
