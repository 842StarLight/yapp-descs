<h2>Dispatcher</h2>

<p>Summit Express is a 23 year old transportation and logistics company that continues to grow with a Company fleet having average age less than 3 years - - we offer health insurance, competitive pay based on experience, 401K.</p>
<p>As Dispatcher, you will be responsible for managing a board of drivers.</p>
<p><b>QUALIFICATIONS: </b></p>
<p>1-Current employment at least 4 years</p>
<p>2-Prior Dispatch Experience</p>
<p>3-Understanding of DOT regulations</p>
<p>4-Highly organized</p>
<p>5-Ability to communicate directly with drivers and other team members</p>
<p>6-Initiative to discover and solve problems</p>
<p>7-Strong work ethic, high integrity</p>
<p>8-Past sales experience helpful</p>
<p>9-Capable of working in a fast-paced environment</p>
<p>10-Capable of prioritizing and meeting deadlines</p>
<p>11-PC skills (MS Office)</p>
<p>12-Minimum HS Diploma/GED</p>
<p><b>COMPENSATION: </b> Based on qualifications</p>
<p>*BENEFITS:<br>*Health Insurance; dental and vision insurance; 8 paid Holidays; Vacation; 401K plan; Life insurance, AD&amp;D</p>
<p><b>DUTIES- OVERVIEW: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Dispatch drivers with load information and ensure Company software is updated with correct data for each load and other related data entry requirements.</li>
<li>Manage drivers throughout duration of load to ensure on-time pick-up and delivery of shipments</li>
<li>Retain drivers by developing good rapport and continued communication with a professional and courteous telephone demeanor</li>
<li>Oversee all driver board pay to ensure accurate payroll for driver</li>
<li>Track and monitor driver miles and provide info to planners</li>
<li>Coordinate driver “time off” schedules with supervisor and planner</li>
<li>Assist to secure backhaul loads for drivers</li>
<li>Provide quality customer service to all internal and external customers</li>
<li>Read your Electronic mail daily and reply in a timely manner.</li>
<li>Assist team when other Dispatchers are away from office, which could increase driver board by 10-20</li>
<li>Plus other related duties</li>
</ul>
