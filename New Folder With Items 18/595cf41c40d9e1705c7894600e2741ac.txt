<p>Revenue Management Responsibilities</p>
<p>
Support hotel's yield efforts, via analysis and reporting that ensure that all segments and distribution channels are mined for revenue opportunities based on budget, forecast, pace and market demand.<br>
Maximize revenue potential and ensure proactive and accurate maintenance and management of all internal and external systems/sites including brand systems and external websites<br>
Support Corporate Director of Revenue in compiling and communicating all documents, reports and presentation required ownership, hotel and regional leadership reporting.<br>
Curate information, conduct audits, reporting and analytics to present opportunities maximizing the value of each asset<br>
Lead select project planning and success measurement within an agile environment.<br>
Marketing Responsibilities<br>
Assist in driving integrated marketing tactics and brand identities for our assets through innovative and collaborative hands-on efforts<br>
Activation on key channels includes, but not limited to, digital/print/social/PR and CRM to drive the most profitable business and demonstrate set return on ad spend goals.<br>
Other responsibilities include collaborating with the sales, digital and revenue strategy leaders to ensure convergence and alignment are in place at all levels of the organization.<br>
Support all brand positioning, messaging and retailing on designated hotel(s) and restaurant(s) core marketing and activation channels; managing content, photography and digital assets.<br>
Assist with all efforts to drive positive .com profitability through the management and execution of all aspects of SEO, SEM, digital marketing, social media, email/CRM, and brand reputation management while ensuring activities are aligned with each assetâ€™s vision and overall corporate strategies.<br>
Provide ongoing content and tool management, taking initiative to provide insights.</p>
<p>
Business Intelligence Responsibilities:</p>
<p>
Assist in the management and maintenance of M3 and other BI tools including report building and data analysis<br>
Develop well-organized and insightful reports, dashboards and other forms of data visualizations to support business awareness and decision-making.<br>
Proactively interface with Highgate operating disciplines to gather and validate reporting and analysis requirements including data elements and report owners.<br>
Develop support documentation and training for system users and administrators.<br>
Ensuring the accurate delivery of data.<br>
Qualifications</p>
<p>
Bachelor's degree or equivalent in Hotel Management or a related field. Familiarization with hotel revenue management concepts and tools preferred.<br>
Lead key partner expectations and collaborate across the enterprise to deliver projects on deadline.<br>
Demonstrated aptitude for problem solving and problem identification.<br>
Ability to collect, analyze and interpret revenue performance data<br>
Must effectively manage and adhere to strict daily/weekly/monthly deadlines.<br>
Extensive proficiency in G-Suite and Microsoft Office experience required with ability to manipulate essential data<br>
Knowledge of reservations system and revenue management data.<br>
Attention to detail required.<br>
Ability to operate in a team environment.<br>
Excellent written and verbal communication skills<br>
Excellent interpersonal skills and demonstrated maturity.<br>
Ability to manage multiple tasks and responsibilities at once.<br>
60% Revenue Management Focus, 25% Marketing Focus, 15% Business Intelligence</p>
