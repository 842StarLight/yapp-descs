<b>ABOUT US</b><br>
Founded in San Francisco in 2010, Off the Grid is a leading mobile food innovator changing where and how food is consumed and created by transforming underutilized and temporary spaces into community gathering places. Through public markets, corporate dining services and catering options, Off the Grid brings communities together around food while increasing economic opportunity for mobile food entrepreneurs. Acclaimed for reinvigorating the Bay Area street food scene through 60 public events that feed more than 100,000 people weekly, Off the Grid also offers a suite of B2B products and services, including Off the Grid Catering, Off the Grid at Work, and Cubert, a modular mobile kitchen.<br>
<br>
<b>POSITION SUMMARY</b><br>
Off the Grid’s Field Account Executive will drive acquisition of new clients for Off the Grid’s B2B business lines, OTG Catering and OTG at Work, our corporate dining offering.<br>
<br>
As a Field Sales Account Executive, your role is responsible for but not limited to handling high level leads, writing proposals, performing in person pitches, developing venue partnerships, managing client relationships and closing deals, all while working successfully with internal partners in pursuit of company and team goals. This is primarily an Outside Sales role where you are expected to be able to successfully steer business opportunities through the sales funnel from initial lead to deal closure while helping your clients execute events that express our core values. A successful candidate will be results-oriented, resourceful, and focused on attaining quarterly and annual benchmarks.<br>
<br>
You will be responsible for the full sales life cycle of our clients, building trust and rapport while identifying opportunities to elevate our service and customer experience. This will include prospecting, discovery, pitch presentations, negotiations and solutions design. You will be responsible for the implementation and the initial stages of account management of any new clients you acquire. You handle pressure with efficiency and grace, changing gears quickly in order to respond to client requests. You have a can-do attitude and look at each opportunity to take to the next level.<br>
<br>
<b>HOW THIS ROLE RELATES TO THE SUCCESS OF OTG</b><br>
You will drive growth and profitability for Off the Grid by bringing in key strategic events and clients, monitoring and pushing forward every stage of the pipeline, understanding the key business levers and effectively optimizing and executing them. You will effectively communicate learnings from your meetings and client relationships to provide recommendations cross-functionally within Sales, Marketing, Product, Production, and Operations to drive improvements to our business that will enable us to close larger deals and continue growing our book of business.<br>
<br>
<br>
<b>KEY RESPONSIBILITIES:</b><br>
This position will be responsible for, but not limited to the following:</div>

<br>
<b>Outside Sales (Approx. 70% of time)</b>
</div>
<ul>

<li>Client hosting (may be required outside of normal business hours</li>
