<h2>SEO Account Coordinator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>3Q Digital is Silicon Valley's agency of record, and the way we work reflects our roots: we're relentless, restless, and constantly striving to innovate and drive growth for our clients.</p>
<p>
We offer full marketing services including SEM, SEO, social advertising, display, mobile advertising, analytics, and business strategy. We work with the fastest-growing B2C, B2B, ecommerce, and lead gen clients in the U.S. We're smart and fast-paced, and we value great client service—which is where you come in</p>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
About You</b></h3>
<p>
Our Account Coordinators are SEO experts in the making, responsible for keeping on top of trends, developments, and best practices and putting them to optimal use for each client they support: brand-name companies who demand – and recognize – excellence.</p>
<p>
 You will be tasked with helping to grow our clients’ SEO program—taking what we’ve already built and making it work better and scale even more effectively. As an SEO Account Coordinator, you’ll be responsible for the following key areas: 1) auditing &amp; identifying areas of improvement and expansion opportunities 2) execution of the established SEO strategy 3) support your clients and your team by answering client emails and participating in weekly calls.</p>
<p><br>
<br>
<b>You’ll be responsible for:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Reporting: develop and maintain reporting for all clients on core SEO KPIs, including traffic, conversions (e.g. sales, leads, phone calls), organic impressions, CTR, and rankings.</li>
<li>
Technical Auditing: have working knowledge of how to crawl websites to identify crawl errors, URL treatment &amp; duplication issues, crawler accessibility and readability, and site speed.</li>
<li>
On-Page &amp; Content Auditing: be able to audit &amp; understand keyword strategy as well as identify optimization opportunities in meta data, on-page content, and structured data.</li>
<li>
Optimizations: be able to complete Meta Data &amp; Content Optimization projects</li>
<li>
Project Planning &amp; Roadmap Development: Create and maintain project plans for client set.</li>
<li>
Development of all core deliverables, including reporting, strategy assessment, auditing, keyword research and mapping, and content optimization projects.</li>
<li>
Client management: acting as the face of 3Q by participating in and helping facilitate client calls and meetings</li>
<li>
Providing consistent and well-structured follow up on status of SEO recommendations for all owned clients</li>
<li>
Manage &amp; analyze ongoing reporting to identify optimization opportunities for each of our clients</li>
</ul>
<p>
 <b>You’ll need to have:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
A minimum of 1-2 years of SEO-specific experience: proven prior work experience presenting findings and making recommendations for account optimizations to internal or external clients a must</li>
<li>
Working knowledge of SEO best practices</li>
<li>
Ability to think creatively and strategically and must be comfortable with identifying and solving difficult website problems</li>
<li>
The ability to audit client sites: know what to look for and know how to identify and resolve issues/errors</li>
<li>
Strong analytical and reporting skills and be proficient in Powerpoint, Excel, and Word</li>
<li>
Strong written and verbal communication skills</li>
<li>
Strong time and project management skills as well as high attention to detail</li>
<li>
A Bachelor’s Degree or equivalent years of experience required</li>
</ul>
<p>
 <b>Additional things that will impress us:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
A Google Analytics certification (will be required within 1 year of employment)</li>
<li>
Tableau or Data Studio experience</li>
<li>
Working knowledge of HTML, CSS, and Javascript development and constraints</li>
<li>
Experience using SEO-related tools like Screaming Frog, DeepCrawl, BrightEdge, Searchmetrics, SEMrush, and AHREFs.</li>
<li>
Familiarity with Google Search Console.</li>
<li>
Eager to roll up your sleeves and be hands on, learn, and grow in the SEO space</li>
<li>
You are entrepreneurial spirited and a self-starter; able to work both independently and in a team environment</li>
</ul>
