<h2>Project Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div><div><div>
<div>
<div>
<p>Responsible for successfully planning, controlling and managing project deliverables to ensure that customer needs are met. Manage the efforts of team members and third party vendors. Manage the integration and/or development and delivery of deliverables directly to the customer.</p>
<p>
ESSENTIAL FUNCTIONS:</p>
<p>
Functional<br>
</p>
<ul>
<li><p>
May participate in development of business team strategic plan.</p></li>
<li><p>
Performs all aspects of project management functions for customer projects, including commercial responsibility and minimal technical.</p></li>
<li><p>
Manages execution of assigned projects within margin targets.</p></li>
<li><p>
Manages and reports all financial aspects of assigned projects.</p></li>
<li><p>
May apply complex and advanced project management and scheduling techniques.</p></li>
<li><p>
The ability to manage a portfolio of projects and being organized is a must.</p></li>
</ul>
<p>
Leadership</p>
<ul>
<li><p>
Leads efforts of project personnel on complex projects.</p></li>
<li><p>
Coaches and helps develop less-experienced engineers assigned to projects.</p></li>
<li><p>
Leads multiple project teams typically consisting of engineering, manufacturing, testing and quality teams.</p></li>
<li><p>
Coordinates activities with support teams and other capabilities.</p></li>
<li><p>
May lead internal project process improvement efforts.</p></li>
</ul>
<p>
Interpersonal<br>
</p>
<ul>
<li><p>
Responsible for resource scheduling to meet project plan requirements.</p></li>
<li><p>
Negotiates change orders with customers.</p></li>
<li><p>
Serves as the primary interface with customer, sales and other Rockwell Automation entities for assigned projects.</p></li>
<li><p>
Supports an environment conducive to successful team interaction.</p></li>
<li><p>
Must have excellent negotiation skills.</p></li>
</ul>
<p>
Business<br>
</p>
<ul>
<li><p>
Responsible for the plan, budget, structure, schedule and staffing for projects which are of significant and strategic importance to the customer and corporation.</p></li>
<li><p>
Must perform monthly project forecasting, be financially responsible for a portfolio of projects, track costs and discuss revenue recognition during monthly meetings with finance and management.</p></li>
<li><p>
Acts as Rockwell Automation representative for assigned projects.</p></li>
<li><p>
Complete responsibility for successful outcome of projects. Manages a portfolio of projects with a total value between $5M to $10M.</p></li>
<li><p>
Responsible for managing subcontracts that include labor and/or fixed price deliverables.</p></li>
<li><p>
Responsible for meeting project schedule, margin, and customer satisfaction targets.</p></li>
<li><p>
Builds partnerships at the business management level of key customers.</p></li>
<li><p>
Responsible for following all applicable corporate, company, governmental and regulatory policies and procedures as appropriate for this position.</p></li>
</ul>

<p><b>Qualifications</b></p>
<div>
<ul>
<li><p>Bachelor's Degree</p></li>
<li><p>
Legal authorization to work in the US is required. We will not sponsor individuals for employment visas, now or in the future, for this job opening.</p></li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<ul><li><p>
Preferred Qualifications:</p></li></ul>
<ul>
<li><p>
BS in Engineering discipline or equivalent knowledge; further education in business administration, operations, or project management is a plus.</p></li>
<li><p>
Minimum five years experience in a systems, software, process automation or application engineering capacity with four thousand five hundred hours of documented project management experience.</p></li>
<li><p>
Project Management Professional (PMP) certification is desirable</p></li>
<li><p>
Sixty hours of formal PMI based Project Management training is desirable.</p></li>
<li><p>
Extensive experience with direct customer contact.</p></li>
<li><p>
Typically possesses industry, application or technology knowledge relevant to assigned business or geography.</p></li>
<li><p>
Must be able to work in a stressful environment where constructive feedback from others is encouraged</p></li>
<li><p>
Must have excellent verbal and written communication skills.</p></li>
<li><p>
Must have excellent conflict resolution and people interface skills.</p></li>
<li><p>
Must perform duties in the office as part of a project team.</p></li>
<li><p>
Possess critical competencies as defined in the Project Manager Competency Profile.</p></li>
</ul>
