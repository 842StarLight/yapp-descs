<h2>FOH/Servers</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>-FOH/Servers</p>
<p>Green corner is a thriving family-owned Mediterranean restaurant. We are looking for someone who is friendly, loves people, has an enthusiastic attitude, and is ready to learn. We will provide you with the training necessary to succeed. We set high standards regarding food preparation, cleaning and sanitation, teamwork and customer service, to ensure that the Green Corner customer experience is always the best it can be.</p>
<p>Due to the nature of the job, team members are usually required to be on their feet working while clocked in, unless on break. If they are not busy, they are expected to take on tasks they see that need to get done, and pitch in to help their teammates (examples include deep cleaning, wrapping silver wear, etc.). Team members are responsible for delivering great and friendly guest experiences.</p>
<p>Customer service and restaurant experience preferred, but not necessary.</p>
<p>We offer both full time and part-time positions.</p>
<p><b>Requirements: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Arrive to scheduled shifts on time</li>
<li>Good customer service skills</li>
<li>Ability to multitask</li>
<li>Be able to follow given tasks</li>
<li>Respond positively to any feedback and show passion for learning</li>
<li>Follow all safety, food safety and sanitation guidelines</li>
<li>Adhere to uniform standards</li>
<li>Must be able to read and have basic math skills</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Duties: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Use POS register system to place orders</li>
<li>Serve food with enthusiasm in a fast-paced environment</li>
<li>Wrap silver wear</li>
<li>Cleaning duties around the store</li>
<li>Performs any other job-related duties as directed by immediate supervisor</li>
</ul>
