<div>
<b>D.R. Horton, Inc., the largest homebuilder in the U.S.</b>, was founded in 1978 and is a publicly traded company on the New York Stock Exchange. It is engaged in the construction and sale of high quality homes designed principally for the entry-level and first time move-up markets. The Company also provides mortgage financing and title services for homebuyers through its mortgage and title subsidiaries. Please visit our website at www.drhorton.com for more information.<br>
<br>
D.R. Horton, Inc. is currently looking for a <b>Programmer-Analyst II</b><b> </b>for their Corporate Information Technology Department. The right candidate will customize Empower GUI’s and documents based on general specifications provided by Business Analyst or self written specifications after BA consultations.</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div><b>
Essential Duties and Responsibilities</b></div>
<ul>
<li><div>Design, implement and maintain all Empower custom forms and business rules</div></li>
<li><div>Troubleshooting and defining Empower functionality and finding ways to implement solutions within existing Empower parameters without having to rely on FNIS software to update and enhance product wherever possible
</div></li>
<li><div>Assist on a regular basis the document design staff with document scripting and troubleshooting</div></li>
<li><div>When document design staff is not available to be primary document specialist
</div></li>
<li><div>With Business Analyst approval, provide user setups, and design and implement all Empower level loan/user securities</div></li>
<li><div>Carries out activities as designated by a Project Manager or Team Leader</div></li>
<li><div>Performs such activities as necessary to tune and maintain proprietary software to meet the needs of DHIFS</div></li>
<li><div>Under general supervision, maintains and modifies existing applications programs.
</div></li>
<li><div>Writes new programs of moderate to complex difficulty and scope, working with basic applications systems’ designs and specifications and utilizing standard procedures and techniques in accordance with DHIFS standards</div></li>
<li><div>Develops internal programming specifications.
</div></li>
<li><div>Can prepare programs requiring a wide variety and extensive number of internal processing actions</div></li>
<li><div>May conduct code walk- throughs</div></li>
<li><div>May make recommendations to management regarding programming standards and techniques</div></li>
<li><div>May perform data analysis, systems requirements definition and external design tasks</div></li>
<li><div>Participants in certain aspects of user consulting, systems analysis, general systems design, vendor contracts and detail systems design</div></li>
<li><div>Perform other duties as assigned</div></li>
</ul>

<b>
Qualifications</b><div>
</div>
<div><b>
Education and/or Experience</b></div>
<ul>
<li><div>Fifth year college or university program certificate; or two to four years related experience and/or training; or equivalent combination of education and experience.</div></li>
<li><div>BS or BA degree in computer science, business administration, or related field is preferred</div></li>
<li><div>3 -4 years experience as a programmer in a hardware/software environment similar to DHIFS
</div></li>
<li><div>Ability to do programming debugging, data analysis and limited system analysis</div></li>
<li><div>Good planning, problem solving, analytical, people and communication skills required</div></li>
<li><div>Previous experience working with “packaged/proprietary” software essential</div></li>
<li><div>Customer service orientation a must</div></li>
</ul>
