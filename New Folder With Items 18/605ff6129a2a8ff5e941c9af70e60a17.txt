<b>POSITION:</b><br>
The ideal lead instructor candidate will have a passion for Pilates and helping others (staff and members) improve their lives through health and fitness. If you are a professional Pilates instructor who is energetic, friendly, willing to learn, build relationships with potential and existing clients, sell classes and lead dynamic apparatus focused workout routines (we use Balanced Body reformers, EXO chairs, TRX, spring boards, ballet barre and more), youâ€™ll be a great addition to our team. We can typically offer blocked scheduling, the ability to teach multiple level classes, club membership, continuing education, employee status (not independent contractor), room for growth and a supportive &amp; fun environment!

The lead instructor is also responsible for setting the tone at the studio, ensuring each class is taught to studio standars, mentoring other instructors, conducting workshops and outside events to promot and lift up the entire community.
<br><br>
<b>REQUIREMENTS:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Current full apparatus Pilates Certification with 450 hours of experience or equivalent</li>
<li>Experience teaching group classes</li>
<li>Ability to demonstrate effective group reformer instruction</li>
<li>Energetic, positive and motivational teaching style</li>
<li>Capable of using a contemporary approach to classical exercises</li>
<li>Punctual, reliable and dedicated</li>
<li>Desire to build a successful business in an entrepreneurial environment</li>
<li>Focus on customer service, including professional and effective communication skills</li>
<li>Experience with club management software and/or the ability to learn new systems (e.g. Club Ready)</li>
<li>Must be comfortable selling teacher training and promoting it within our studios</li>
<li>Must maintain an average class teaching schedule of 5 classes or more per week</li>
<li>Must be able to give and recieve feedback in a positive and thoughtful manner</li>
<li>Must have schedule flexibility to fill in where needed</li>
</ul>
<b>RESPONSIBILITIES:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Providing Pilates group reformer classes pursuant to Club Pilates standards</li>
<li>Instructing clients (up to 12 clients per class) using proper form and safe methods on a variety of equipment including, but not limited to, Reformer, Springboard, Exo-Chair, hand weights, etc. to ensure a safe, yet creative experience</li>
<li>Ability to lead workshops and events</li>
<li>Conducting private, semi-private, demo or other additional sessions as needed</li>
<li>Visually inspecting the equipment prior to each class to ensure it is in good working order and safe to use</li>
<li>Maintaining a clean and orderly workout environment</li>
<li>Building studio revenue by promoting retail and membership sales &amp; services</li>
<li>Being a motivating and inspiring force to promote our motto: Do Pilates. Do Life.</li>
</ul>
