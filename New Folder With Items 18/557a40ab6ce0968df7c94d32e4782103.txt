<p><b>Qualifications: </b></p>
<p>The ideal District Manager is a self-motivated, detail-oriented person who is comfortable leading a team to success. A District Manager would be responsible for supervising all day-to-day drug testing processes and conducting annual/quarterly/weekly department meetings. Overall staffing would be at the responsibility of the manager in order to maintain a productive and efficient team (around 8-14 employees), this includes but is not limited to interviewing and developing current employees. He or she would be required to prioritize tasks in order of importance while still being available to change daily plans at any moment<b>. </b></p>
<p>*<br>People on our team are passionate about serving both the client and their fellow coworkers. We all are excited to be on a team that’s winning while also looking for ways to contribute to the team win ourselves. Excellence is expected and may mean giving more effort than usual when called upon, but we know the others on our team would do the same for us.<br>*<br>*<br>Benefits are available for all full-time employees; including: medical, dental, vision, 401(k), other supplemental insurance packages, holidays &amp; vacation time. Additional benefits include annual raises and monthly bonuses based on performance.<br>*<br>*<br>A valid driver’s license, outstanding driving record, background check, valid fingerprinting screen, and negative drug screen are all required. Applicant must be the age of 21 or older for automobile insurance requirements. Previous experience in management is required. Experience in case management or drug testing is preferred but not expected.<br>*<br>*<br><b>Why Work for Tomo Drug Testing?: </b></p>
