<h2>Principal Land Surveyor</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Henkels &amp; McCoy, Inc. (H&amp;M) is a leading utility construction firm providing critical infrastructure for the Power, Communications, Oil &amp; Gas Pipeline, and Gas Distribution markets throughout North America. Founded in 1923, H&amp;M brings yesterday’s experience to the evolving infrastructure needs of today. We consistently rank in the top ten of Engineering News-Record’s Specialty Contractors and have been recognized with an E.I. DuPont Safety Excellence Award for our safety performance. H&amp;M is also a founding member of the ET&amp;D Partnership, a collaborative industry partnership working to reduce fatalities, injuries, and illnesses in the construction industry.</p>

<ul><li>At least 5 years of surveying experience required.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Technical knowledge of Mapping and Field Surveying required.</li></ul>

<ul><li>Experience recording Survey, Corner Records, Legal Description and Plats required.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Experience with Autocad 2000-Autocad 2009 Land Development Desktop, Civil3D 2012, AutoDesk Map, Microstation J, V8 a required.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Proficient with survey equipment and software, TGO (Trimble Geomatic Office) Trimble S6 Robotic Total Station, R8 and R10 GPS Systems and hand held data collectors required.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Knowledge of Microsoft Office and Excel required.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Proficiency in technical math, which includes Algebra, Geometry, Trigonometry, and Applied Physics required.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Ability to read Utility maps preferred.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Professional Surveyor License in California required.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Must have a valid Drivers License.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Must be able to lift 50 lbs. or more</li></ul>
