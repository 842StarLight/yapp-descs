<h2>Development Associate</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>New England Biolabs (NEB) is seeking an Applications Development Associate to work with an experienced scientific staff on in-house development projects concerning production of oligonucleotides. The individual in this position will also assume responsibilities associated with the production of a diverse line of products. This position is in our Beverly, MA facility.<br>
<br>
</p>
<p><b>Primary Responsibilities:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Assume product management responsibilities for several products and/ or kit components in support of catalog sales.
</li>
<li>Routine synthesis and purification of DNA and RNA oligonucleotides, including synthesis of DNA utilizing a multi 96/384 plate format.
</li>
<li>Purification of oligonucleotides and nucleoside triphosphates utilizing methods such as SPE, PAGE, HPLC, FC and FPLC.
</li>
<li>Quantify oligonucleotides for the generation of libraries using liquid handling systems.
</li>
<li>Maintain detailed documentation of working protocols, vialing records, and substrate inventory, using tracking software, and LIMS where appropriate.
</li>
<li>Assist with the production of existing affinity chromatography-based products along with development of novel derivatized solid-phase supports.
</li>
<li>Assist in the development of products and processes supporting NGS technologies.
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Required Qualifications and Experience:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>B.A/B.S. degree in Chemistry, Biology or related field.
</li>
<li>Minimum 5 yearsâ€™ experience in the life science industry.
</li>
<li>Demonstrated ability to work under conditions necessary for ISO 9001 and ISO 13485 compliance.
</li>
<li>Good hands at the bench and an ability to perform careful and consistent synthesis runs and experimental procedures.
</li>
<li>Demonstrate significant skills in production while adhering to safe laboratory practices and environmental safeguards.
</li>
<li>Able to manage timelines productively and bring projects to completion.
</li>
<li>Maintain a strong work ethic, emphasizing both efficiency and quality of work.
</li>
<li>Be capable of self-management within a team framework.
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Preferred Qualifications and Experience:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Working knowledge of phosphoramidite chemistry and experience with high-throughput DNA synthesis platforms desired.
</li>
<li>Experience with liquid handling systems and purification of oligos in plate format desired.
</li>
<li>Familiarity with LIMS as applied to oligo synthesis and NGS is advantageous.
</li>
<li>Knowledge of Agilent Technologies1200 series HPLC systems and available column technology along with the Agilent 6120 single-quad LCMS (or equivalent) is beneficial.
</li>
</ul>
