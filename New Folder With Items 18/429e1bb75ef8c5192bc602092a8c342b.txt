<h2>Marketing Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>For more than 78 years, the USO—a private, nonprofit organization—has served the men and women of the U.S. military, and their families, throughout their time in uniform – from the moment they join, through their deployments and as they transition back to their communities.</p>
<p>
Today’s USO continuously adapts to the needs of our men and women in uniform and their families, so they can focus on their very important mission. We operate USO centers at or near military installations across the United States and throughout the world, including in combat zones, and even un-staffed USO service sites in places too dangerous for anyone but combat troops to occupy.</p>
<p>
The Marketing Specialist, is responsible for supporting the successful execution of all marketing strategies and campaigns, from concept through evaluation.</p>
<div>
<p><b>Principal Duties and Responsibilities (*Essential Duties)</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Support the work of Marketing and Communications Directors.*</li>
<li>Collaborate on campaign concept development, support project management and track campaign metrics.*</li>
<li>Support RFP process for AOR; prep, track and conduct follow up for AOR meetings/action items/deliverables.*</li>
<li>Support PSA distribution plans and track metrics.*</li>
<li>Help identify, evaluate and execute on sponsorship, advertising and award opportunities.*</li>
<li>Provide support to the Marketing &amp; Communications team with OneUSO efforts and crisis communications management/responses.*</li>
<li>Field inquiries from regional field offices.*</li>
<li>Support marketing content and collateral development and drafting and review of PR/comms materials and plans; Develop and manage tracking process for Corporate Partner activations/marketing plans.*</li>
<li>Serve as marketing POC for corporate partner activations – Three Star and below.*</li>
<li>Take lead on editorial calendar, content development and distribution of '3 Things.'</li>
<li>Other duties as assigned.</li>
</ul>
<div>
<div>
<p><b>Job Specifications</b><b> - </b>minimum Knowledge, Skills, Abilities and Behaviors necessary to perform the job successfully. Equivalent combination of education and experience is acceptable.</p>
</div>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s Degree in Marketing, Communications or related field.
</li>
<li>1-3 years work experience in a web marketing or communications support role. Relevant experience in a not-for-profit, military, multicultural and/or global organization preferred.</li>
<li>General knowledge of web publishing technologies, digital marketing tools, online media applications, user experience best practices, website analytics platforms and social media channels and interest communities.</li>
<li>Strong interpersonal, customer service and problem-solving skills. Ability to interact with USO Leadership, the general public and military audiences at a variety of levels with integrity and professionalism.</li>
<li>Proficiency using computers and electronics equipment. General knowledge of various software, applications, and programs including but not limited to Microsoft Office Suite.
</li>
<li>Demonstrated ability to exercise sound judgment related to controversial and/or culturally sensitive subjects. Ability to handle confidential information with tact and poise.</li>
<li>Must show initiative, self-motivation, and attention to detail with the ability to manage multiple projects with competing priorities. Ability to quickly and easily adapt to changing organizational needs.</li>
<li>Ability to achieve desired results while working collaboratively in a team environment. Willingness and ability to work non-standard hours as needed.</li>
<li>Ability to perform basic math and follow proper cash/donation handling and reporting procedures.
</li>
<li>Ability to obtain and maintain proper credentials necessary to access USO Center locations and facilities including but not limited to security, credit and/or background screening, SOFA status, valid driver’s license.</li>
<li>Must be a strong advocate of the USO’s mission.</li>
</ul>

<li>Resume and cover letter are required for full consideration.</li>
</ul>
