<h2>Inventory Manager, Ad Sales</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<div><b>Summary</b></div>
<div>
Managesadvertisement inventory activities that analyze, identify and allocateavailable commercial inventory, either for television or digitalplatforms. Monitors, reports, dispensesand, recaptures advertising units to ensure the highest use of availableinventory. Ensures the creation anddistribution of revenue reports by department and by sales person.<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<div><b>Essential Dutiesand Responsibilities</b></div>
<ul>
<li> Workclosely with VP, Pricing &amp; Planning to strategically maximize revenuethrough inventory usage.</li>
<li> Generate,track and distribute assigned sales reports.</li>
<li> ManageNational, DR &amp; ADU Inventory/Avails.</li>
<li> Participatein the creation and maintenance of upfront and scatter ratecards.</li>
<li> Coordinatewith Sales Assistants &amp; Planners to ensure all sales administrations,deadline, policies and procedures are met.
</li>
<li> Maintaintraffic and sales systems.
</li>
<li> Helpmaintain stewardship, import and track internal estimates, and compilestewardship reports.</li>
<li> OverseeMSA posting process.
</li>
<li> AssistDR team with rate analysis, daypart allocation and inventory maximization.</li>
</ul>
