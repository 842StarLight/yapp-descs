<p>Job Description:</p>
<p>The Digital Marketing &amp; Social Media Supervisor needs to have a strong grasp of current marketing tools and strategies to be able to lead integrated digital marketing campaigns from concept to execution. Digital Supervisor is responsible for maintaining our social media presence across all digital channels. Optimize digital strategy based on analysis, trends and analytics. Measuring and reporting on the performance of all digital marketing campaigns, website and our digital channels. Optimize Hy Cite’s web pages for relevant and optimal messaging and working with other team members leading in the generation of content.</p>
<p>Duties &amp; responsibilities:</p>
<p>1. Plan and execute all digital marketing initiatives, including email, grow our social media channels, SEO/SEM, and monitor effectiveness of advertising campaigns. Collaborate with vendors and</p>
<p>2. Measure and report performance of all digital marketing campaigns and assess against goals (ROI and KPIs). Utilize strong analytical ability to evaluate end-to-end customer experience across multiple channels and customer touch points.</p>
<p>3. Collaborates with and leads other team members in the generation of content.</p>
<p>4. Identify trends and insights. Optimize performance based on insights</p>
