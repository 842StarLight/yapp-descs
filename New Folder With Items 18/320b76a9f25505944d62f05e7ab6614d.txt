<h2>Senior Manager, Corporate & Brand Marketing</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>
<div><b>About the Role</b></div>
<div>
FiscalNote is looking for a Corporate Marketing &amp; Brand Manager to join our growing Marketing team. The ideal candidate is proactive and experienced in the areas of corporate marketing, brand management, event marketing, and communication strategies. You will play a critical role in the development and execution of the organization’s internal and external corporate marketing and communications strategies in support of our corporate mission and goals. You will also establish and maintain consistency of brand across all channels working closely across the organization, while managing 1 direct report. The position reports into the VP of Marketing.</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div><b>
You Will:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Develop corporate messaging and implement communication strategies to build and deliver a positive, consistent story among key external stakeholders, corporate and business unit messages targeted to key external audiences</li>
<li>Oversee PR agency relationship to provide ongoing strategies, updates, and visibility of internal initiatives and goals</li>
<li>Own the FiscalNote brand and develop assets for use across the entire organization including monitoring all messaging and imagery used externally for consistency and accuracy</li>
<li>Develop FiscalNote social media strategy and best practices to share with internal stakeholders</li>
<li>Oversee event strategy with a goal of demonstrating ROI</li>
<li>Manage, coach, and develop event marketing associate</li>
<li>Collaborate with executive team leadership to develop marketing and communication strategies and tactics including:</li>
<li>Development of corporate marketing and PR strategies and tactics that support differing vertical goals</li>
<li>Deployment of an executive communications platform: identify topics for each of the key executives to ‘own’ and secure relevant, high-impact speaking opportunities for them to demonstrate their expertise and insights.</li>
<li>Support senior leaders with speaking engagements to ensure consistent external messaging and positioning.</li>
<li>Operational oversight and leadership of event sponsorship and/or participation opportunities that highlight FiscalNote and CQ Roll Call’s brand</li>
<li>Proactive outreach to key partners and stakeholders (when relevant)</li>
<li>Content strategy and deployment (white papers, blogs, social, etc.) to support corporate story.</li>
<li>Ongoing management of a crisis communication plan, ensuring FiscalNote is poised to address any potential issues that may develop</li>
<li>Establish and maintain external relationships including thought leaders, key customers and agencies</li>
<li>Maintain expert knowledge of competitive landscape and news</li>
</ul>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div><b>
You Have:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s Degree or equivalent work experience</li>
<li>5 years in marketing, brand management, or related experience</li>
<li>Excellent written and oral communications skills</li>
<li>Strong interpersonal skills to develop relationships across the entire organization</li>
<li>Hands-on experience managing brand, PR, and social marketing initiatives</li>
<li>Led, motivated, and mentored direct reports to ensure they are set up to succeed</li>
<li>Experience with marketing tools, such as Hootsuite (preferred)</li>
<li>Proven project management skills and the ability to manage multiple deadlines</li>
</ul>
