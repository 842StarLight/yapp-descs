<div>
We are looking for a dynamic product manager to lead growth initiatives for our Assess and Protect offerings. These initiatives include:</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<ul>
<li>Contrast Community Edition – a freemium version of our SaaS services targeting the developer and DevOps community.</li>
<li>Software Composition Analysis (SCA) offering – focused on capturing the market for open source security use cases.</li>
</ul>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
We need an energetic, talented, and data-driven individual who can drive strategic services and marketing campaigns with a targeted focus on user acquisition, engagement, retention and conversion.</div>
</div>
<div><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities</b></h3>
<div><ul>
<li>
Own the business and roadmap for our new Software Composition Analysis (SCA) offering, driving the product vision, strategy, and execution for the service across SaaS and on-prem deployment models.</li>
<li>
Own the strategy and roadmap for Contrast Community edition focused on the Developer and DevOps personas. Drive new features and marketing campaigns to grow the freemium user base and convert users to our paid Assess and Protect offerings.</li>
<li>
Inform product decisions with quantitative analysis based on user behavior analytics and experimentation (A/B testing, ...)</li>
<li>
Drive and own the product requirements process while working collaboratively with key stakeholders to prioritize and validate technology, features, product direction and strategy.</li>
<li>
Influence the Product Development and R&amp;D teams while helping them better understand what to build and why.</li>
<li>
Collaborate with customers, sales, engineering and designers on fresh new concepts</li>
<li>
Establish clear metrics and KPIs to continuously evaluate business performance and feature usability with a direct feedback loop into the product strategy.</li>
<li>
Develop a deep understanding of the market landscape and identify key areas of competitive differentiation and market disruption.</li>
<li>
Work side-by-side with product marketing on positioning and marketing campaigns.</li>
</ul></div>
</div></div>

<div><ul>
