<h2>Account Manager - 65396BR</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<b>Description:</b><br>
bswift is currently seeking an experienced Account Manager to join our direct Client Services Team.<br>
<br>
The Account Manager (AM) is directly responsible for helping bswift meet and exceed client expectations by supporting consumers on our best-in-class HR software and services.<br>
<br>
Located in downtown Chicago, bswift offers software and services that streamline benefits, HR and payroll administration for employers and public and private exchanges nationwide. bswift’s state-of-the-art cloud-based technology and outsourcing solutions significantly reduce administrative costs and time-consuming paperwork, making life easier for administrators and millions of consumers who enroll in benefits with bswift.65396<br>
<br>
<b>Fundamental Components:</b><br>
<b>Responsibilities:</b><br>
<br>
The Account Manager (AM) will serve as the primary point of contact to our direct clients, internal team members and partners by helping implement and maintain client web sites. This individual will:<br>
<ul>
<li>Help manage and provide technical guidance through the implementation of new client Web sites</li>
<li>
Continually update sites as requirements change each year or as often as needed (this includes benefit class matrix, requirements document, rates, permissions, field options, site text and any open items on questions log)</li>
<li>
Test client sites for quality assurance; track and monitor the resolution of issues</li>
<li>
Prepare and conduct technical/product presentations, demonstrations and trainings (such as an open enrollment demo for new/changing plans, site text, and release functionality changes, etc.)</li>
<li>
Provide resolution of client issues by responding to questions and concerns about the use of bswift products; ability to leverage research to find resolution</li>
<li>
Maintain thorough understanding of all internal systems to respond to general and specialized client requests</li>
<li>
Triage client tickets and/or questions (email and voicemail) within 24 hours; maintaining ownership from initial customer contact to call resolution which includes supporting escalations if appropriate and providing customer updates</li>
<li>
Look for and help drive continuous process improvements/efficiencies by maintaining a thorough understanding of all internal systems</li>
<li>
Develop and implement fulfillment procedures</li>
<li>
Lead and create agendas for client meetings (when CRM is not available)</li>
<li>
Coach and mentor other team members; share new ideas and customer needs with bswift teams throughout all phases of product life cycle</li>
<li>
Manage workflow in the setup and maintenance of carrier and payroll feeds</li>
<li>
Assist in sales and marketing activities as needed</li>
<li>
Provide open communication to bswift customers in a pleasant, professional manner with a can-do attitude</li>
<li>
Responsible for special projects as assigned</li>
</ul>
