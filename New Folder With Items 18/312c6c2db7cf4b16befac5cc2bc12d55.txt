<br>Product Leadership - USA New York, New York
<br><br>
Nielsen pioneered the measurement of media and consumer behavior, and we’re on a mission to transform our products and technology to help the next generation of content creators, digital publishers and media platforms compete for consumer and advertiser attention. Through cutting edge technology and industry leading data science, we’re enabling publishers to monetize their media while empowering advertisers with the intelligence they need to efficiently reach consumers and optimize their marketing investments. Backed by decades of analytics R&amp;D, proprietary data assets, and a drive to reinvent our industry, Nielsen is on a journey to apply the rigor and expertise that made us successful in Television and Radio measurement to Digital media.
<br><br>
Nielsen’s Digital Product team is looking for a skilled professional to join as VP of Product Commercialization, supporting Nielsen’s Digital Measurement product family. In this role, you will be responsible for transforming how product management, commercial leadership, and our clients partner to bring our products to market, including:
<br>Driving culture change in how our teams bring products to market, stewarding the transition from ad hoc / service based tactics to an always-on / SaaS go to market strategy
<br>Building and disseminating expertise in our products to upskilling our commercial organization
<br>Representing and advocating for our product strategy with industry forums, clients and strategic partners
<br><br>
Responsibilities and Objectives
<br>Manage, develop, and empower a team of product specialists to
<br>Live, breath, and evangelize agile product development practices across our product, technology, and data science teams
<br>Define and execute the strategy for digital and cross-platform measurement currency, both near and long-term, in the US and Globally
<br>Own the roadmap and take personal accountability for executing against our Operating Plan and P&amp;L
<br>Collaborate cross functionally with engineers, data scientists, vendors, product marketing, and business development to plan, develop, and deliver products
<br>Conduct user research and collaborate with our commercial leadership to ensure our strategy remains relevant and orients us for growth
<br>Develop and manage towards OKRs that track performance and align team incentives to our strategy
<br>Regularly review the competitive landscape and user feedback to improve product features and discover M&amp;A opportunities
<br><br>
Qualifications
<br>Experience building, managing, and upskilling a team of product managers in a fast-paced, entrepreneurial context
<br>6+ years in technical product management roles with extensive experience practicing, applying, and coaching others on Agile development principles
<br>Ability to bring simplicity and structure to ambiguous problems with multiple external and internal stakeholders
<br>Experience in marketing / media preferred, including domain expertise in any of the following areas:
<br>Audience measurement and Targeting (e.g. reach and frequency measurement, audience segmentation, programmatic activation)
<br>Digital / Cross-Platform advertiser and activation (e.g. orchestrating digital ad buys, executing digital / cross-platform campaigns, digital ad operations)
<br>Marketing effectiveness measurement (e.g. attribution measurement, marketing mix modeling, sales lift measurement)
<br>Strategic and tactical media planning (e.g. budget allocation, building cross platform or channel-specific media plans)
<br><br>
<b>Preferred but not required:</b> Technical knowledge of cross-platform media / behavior measurement technologies and data, including any of the following:
<br>Media panels
<br>Advertising technology (e.g. JS / pixel tags; data management platforms)
<br>“Big data” media measurement sources (e.g. set top box, Smart TV data)
<br>Data matching methods (e.g. cross-device data; online-to-offline Onboarding; PII matching and management)
<br>Familiarity with Big Data technologies, public cloud product architecture, and general software engineering practices
<br>Familiarity with statistical modeling / machine learning techniques
<br>Medium travel required
