<h2>Product Manager – EloView SaaS Platform</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<b>Responsibilities:</b>
<br>
<div>Job Purpose:
<br>
<br>
As a leading global supplier of touchscreen solutions, it is our business to know touch. In fact, we pioneered the touchscreen over 40 years ago and haven’t stopped since. Every 21 seconds, a new Elo touchscreen is installed somewhere in the world. Our product portfolio includes a broad selection of interactive touchscreen products including: interactive displays ranging from 10- to 70-inches, all-in-one touchscreen computers, touchscreen monitors and OEM touch screens and controllers. Consumers can find the company’s touchscreen solutions in gaming machines, interactive kiosks, hospitality systems, point-of-sale terminals, wayfinder displays, interactive retail displays and transportation applications—just to name a few! Visit us at www.elotouch.com
<br>
<br>
The Product Manager reports to the VP of Product Management, Interactive Signage and Android Platforms, and will be responsible for owning the EloView SaaS offering from Elo. EloView, coupled with Elo’s commercial Android devices is the highest growth area for Elo and therefore this is a strategic position with significant executive visibility.
<br>
<br>
This position has a significant “solution engineering” role in addition to software product management. It will allow product managers or experienced engineers and marketers a fantastic development path to leadership roles in products, strategy and management.
<br>
<br>
Responsibilities:
<br>
<ul>
<li>Define, drive, and deliver industry leading features and reliability for the EloView platform
</li>
<li>Collaborate to define customer requirements for current and future features based on market research, competitive analysis, understanding of the market, ‘Voice of the Customer’, sales and support departments
</li>
<li>Develop hardware + software solutions based on company strategy and customer inputs
</li>
<li>Develop long-term vision and short-term road map for EloView
</li>
<li>Oversee and improve business processes surrounding EloView (e.g., quote-to-cash, contracts etc.)
</li>
<li>Prepare and manage product pricing, prepare any product quotes, update and maintain product pricing through the product life cycle.
</li>
<li>Interact with the Executive team on strategic direction, opportunities in new markets and ensuring the product supports the company's overall strategy and goals.
</li>
<li>Deliver excellent written documents describing updates, product features and benefits and customer presentations.
</li>
<li>Act as a key product/ marketing point of contact Sales as needed to support enterprise customers and opportunities.</li>
</ul>
</div>
