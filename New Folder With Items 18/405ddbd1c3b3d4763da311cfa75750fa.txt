<h2>Senior Systems Engineer - Data Protection (Enteprise Pre-Sales)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>The business is looking for a people-focused technical sales leader who provides technical direction and business guidance to the regional sales team. As a Sr. Sales Engineer, you will be accountable for regional revenue goals by driving innovative technical programs and overseeing day-to-day account-level activities.<br>
<br>
The successful candidate must be a highly technical professional possessing in-depth knowledge of the back-up recovery industry who should be able to demonstrate technical leadership and subject matter expertise on various data protection products.<br>
<br>
<b>GENERAL SUMMARY</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Provides technical leadership and direction to customers and internal staff in the development of fully integrated technology solutions in support of pre-sales activities in the assigned market.</li>
<li>
Assists in the analysis, design and development of fully integrated technology solutions.</li>
<li>
Acts as technical expert and consultant to develop and propose solutions that meet the technology and business requirements of assigned customers.</li>
<li>
Technical emphasis is on hardware capabilities, software requirements and systems integration.</li>
<li>
Makes technical and sales presentations to customer's technical staff and senior management. Understands competitive technology and business applications within the assigned market.</li>
<li>
Conducts research, answers questions and removes objections that arise in a sales campaign.</li>
<li>
Serves as a trusted technology adviser to customers and serves as an internal resource on technical issues or specific business applications within an assigned market segment.</li>
<li>
Makes technical and sales presentations to technical staff and top management.<br>
</li>
</ul>
<p><i>Applicable markets: Enterprise, Commercial, Partner, Specialist/Practice</i></p>
<p></p>
<p><b>
PRINCIPAL DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Works with Sales Team to develop and implement specific account penetration strategies, produce account specific product and service and sales plans.</li>
<li>
Maintains knowledge of competitive solutions to effectively address and dispel customer objections to solutions, and train the account team.</li>
<li>
Anticipates technology and/or market trends and provides guidance on their application to external customers and feedback of business requirements back into engineering and marketing organizations.</li>
<li>
Successfully builds relationships with the account team, partners and customers in support of sales team objectives and engages and leverages corporate resources, abilities, budgets and personnel as appropriate.</li>
<li>
Leads technical sales calls</li>
<li>
Configures and documents software, hardware and service solutions to meet customer and sales objectives.</li>
<li>
Qualifies sales opportunities in the terms of customer technical requirements, competition, decision making process and funding.</li>
<li>
Presents and markets the design and value of proposed solution and business case to customers, prospects and management.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Desired Skills &amp; Experience:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
5-10+ yrs of enterprise sales engineering management experience, be a self-motivated leader, highly energetic, with a strong hands-on, “can do” approach.</li>
<li>
<b>
Deep expertise in back-up recovery products/solutions required</b>, either from the Dell EMC Portfolio (Data Domain, Avamar, Networker, Receoverpoint, etc) or competitor product lines.</li>
<li>
Knowledge of distributed computing, virtual memory subsystem and scale-out storage architecture is a plus.</li>
<li>
Demonstrated strong written, oral and presentation skills with the ability to discuss highly technical concepts to a variety of audiences, including executive level technical decision-makers.</li>
<li>
Demonstrated ability to develop and execute strategic initiatives.</li>
<li>
A high level of business acumen and experience bringing technology solutions to solve business challenges.</li>
</ul>
