<h2>Digital Content Creator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Regional position for Univision Arizona Local Media –that will support Account Executives in their revenue-driving marketing and digital promotions and efforts. The Digital Content Creator will be responsible for media campaigns that support our local brands, initiatives, platforms, and programming, utilizing all available promotional inventories with an emphasis on Digital and Social Media strategies and TV segments as needed for Spanish language medium Univision Arizona. In addition, this person will create strategies to grow social media through creative promotions and organic sales campaigns in Spanish.
</p>
<p></p>
<p>JOB RESPONSIBILITIES:
</p>
<ul>
<li>Station and News branding, contesting promotions, creation of original content, and B2C marketing pieces across all platforms, with an emphasis on social media engagement with the community
</li>
<li>Create impactful and successful revenue-driven cross platform campaigns that increase visibility, and awareness while staying true to Univision’s core mission in the ever-changing digital space.
</li>
<li>Manage strategic AE client needs analysis brainstorming session and identify, develop, and implement social media marketing strategies for media partners and sales initiatives.
</li>
<li>Review budgets for social media ads campaigns and foster client campaigns through ideation and talent endorsed cost-effective and turn-key social media promotions.
</li>
<li>Coordinate logistics for digital production and local social media sales promotions.
</li>
<li>Adhere and commit to deadlines and contribute to corporate goals, including attending all station events.
</li>
<li>Work closely with corporate content team and promotion team to maintain awareness of company initiatives
</li>
<li>Produce, edit, and oversee digital and social media marketing operations, including online micro sites and contests across Spanish language markets.
</li>
<li>Coordinate between departments in developing social campaigns.
</li>
<li>Responsible for managing recaps and all online promotional elements.
</li>
<li>Other duties as deemed necessary
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p>REQUIRED SKILLS &amp; EXPERIENCE:
</p>
<ul>
<li>Excellent Spanish/English writing and grammar skills.
</li>
<li>Bachelor’s degree or equivalent work experience in lieu of education
</li>
<li>Practical knowledge of multimedia production as well as social media platforms and trends.
</li>
<li>Strong organizational and analytical skills as well as detail oriented.
</li>
<li>Ability to work both independently and in a team environment.
</li>
<li>Must be able to drive and have a valid Arizona driver’s license with a good driving record.
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p>DESIRED SKILLS &amp; EXPERIENCE:
</p>
<ul>
<li>Experience building digital sales campaigns a plus.
</li>
<li>Minimum 1-year prior marketing, sales or similar experience preferred.
</li>
<li>Knowledge Wyng/Offerpop or other micro site sweepstakes software.
</li>
<li>Experience with video production &amp; digital media company advertising
</li>
<li>Proficiency in Power Point, Final Cut, and Adobe Creative Suite
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p>ELIGIBILITY REQUIREMENTS:
</p>
<ul>
<li>Must be willing to work from Office in Phoenix, AZ
</li>
<li>Must be willing to submit to a background investigation
</li>
<li>Must have unrestricted authorization to work in the United States
</li>
</ul>
