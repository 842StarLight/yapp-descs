<h2>Customer Marketing Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Job Summary</b></p>
<p>We’re looking for an accomplished marketing professional to help support our customer marketing efforts and communicate the uniqueness of our products. In this hands-on role, you’ll work with the Product and Content Teams to own the customer journey and enhance the customer experience. Working with the Client Success team, you’ll develop compelling product positioning and messaging programs and provide sales enablement. You’ll evangelize our products to our customers through social media, email, collateral, and at events. Since #MeToo and our recent coverage in the Wall Street Journal, Bloomberg, CNN and NPR, the demand for our training has soared with more than 1,700 customers including brands such as Hilton, Panera Bread, LA Fitness, Cedars-Sinai and the US Congress to name a few. This is a fantastic opportunity to join a hyper-growth company with an award-winning product and an awesome team.</p>
<p><b>Key Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul><li>Become an expert in Traliant solutions and deliver the tools our customers need to comply with changing regulations and culture. Together with the Director of Product Marketing, educate both internal and external stakeholders about our product features and their benefits.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Create and communicate the core value proposition and messaging about our solutions to our customers and customize sales tools that support the selling process, including, decks, case studies, email templates, sales sheets, thought leadership, etc.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Attend trade shows and events to support lead generation and customer engagement.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Manage webinars to educate prospects, customers, and partners on trending issues and Traliant solutions.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Work with Content Marketing and Product Marketing in assisting with daily social media posts.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Work with Product and Content Teams to own the customer journey and enhance the customer experience.</li></ul>
<p><b>Preferred Skills and Experience</b></p>
<ul><li>Prior experience in a customer marketing role required.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Strong written communication skills, with the ability to creatively describe and position the uniqueness of our products.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Strong verbal and presentation skills.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Results-oriented, organized and decisive with the initiative to prioritize projects and adjust as needed.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Experience with Adobe Creative Suite preferred but not required.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Previous experience in a SaaS business, eLearning, GRC, or Human Resources a plus.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Bachelor’s degree or equivalent experience.</li></ul>
<p><b>Location</b></p>
<p>Remote</p>
<p><b>About Traliant</b></p>
<p>Traliant was founded by industry veterans from some of the world’s most successful compliance training companies to meet the challenge of transforming compliance training from boring to brilliant. Backed by Martinson Ventures, Traliant delivers modern, bite-sized training on an eLearning platform that enables fast and easy customization.<br>*</p>
