<div><b>
How You Will Contribute</b></div>
<div>
The <b>Staff Accountant </b>will perform various accounting functions within the Accounting Department with the primary responsibility of maintaining accurate property general ledgers.</div>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div><b>Things You Will Do
</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Close assigned property ledgers within a scheduled time frame – complete thorough review of all revenue and expense items (Monthly)</li>
<li>
Prepare standard accrual entries for various expense accounts (Monthly)</li>
<li>
Complete account/bank reconciliations and analysis for various asset and liability accounts (Monthly)</li>
<li>
Communicate with on-site, accounts payable, IT and system support personnel (as needed)</li>
<li>
Assist in filling audit requests for external auditors</li>
<li>
Research actual to budget variances through analytical and substantive methods (as needed)</li>
<li>
Preparation of owner reporting for owned portfolios including Balance Sheet, Income statement and other reports as required (Monthly)</li>
<li>
Participate in special projects (as needed)</li>
<li>
Assist in Cash Management</li>
<li>
Prepare requisite credit applications for CAO signature</li>
<li>
Prepare Lender Compliance for distribution</li>
<li>
Prepare HUD reporting</li>
</ul>
</div></div></div>

<div><div><div>
<div><b>Things You Need</b></div>

<li>

1-2 years of experience in accounting or related field</li>

<ul><li>
Excellent communication skills</li></ul>
<ul><li>
Excellent organizational and analytical skills</li></ul>
<ul><li>
Detail-oriented</li></ul>
<ul><li>
Team player</li></ul>
<ul><li>
Able to review invoices or other documents and determine proper accounting treatment</li></ul>
