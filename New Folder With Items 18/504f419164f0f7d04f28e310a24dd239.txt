<p><b>Systems Integration Engineer AA</b></p>
<p>
The System Integration Engineer (SIE) primary role is to install and support the various interfaces that allows Omnicell’s products and services to intercommunicate with a healthcare facility’s information system. Typical interfaces include receiving patient data, receiving medication order data, sending billing data, sending purchase order information, confirming receipt of an order, and exchanging information necessary for efficient management of inventory.
</p>
<p>Working independent or as part of a larger project team, the SIE is the subject matter expert responsible for delivering Omnicell’s interface integration design and deployment strategy. Using their technical and creative ability, SIE’s deliver interface solutions to overcome common integration problems encountered during the installation of Omnicell products within a healthcare environment. The Omnicell SIE excels in solving integration problems with customers, while at the same time, advocates for Omnicell implementation best practices. SIE’s traditionally serve as valued consultants during Omnicell sales and support calls with customers.</p>
<p><b>
Responsibilities:
</b></p>
<p><i>Installation/Upgrades</i></p>
<ul>
<li>Lead interface installation and conversion projects as the technical expert to ensure customers, colleagues, and business partners understand interface functions, design, specifications, implementation strategy, and support requirements</li>
<li>Collaborate with project teams in formulating a project plan and scope of work document to design, deploy, test, and bring live Omnicell interfaces</li>
<li>Produce and properly archive requirements, specification, design model, data flow, testing, disaster recovery, and acceptance documentation for system interfaces</li>
<li>Coordinate with Technical Implementation Managers in scheduling projects, and resolve any escalations from various sources to keep projects moving</li>
<li>Act as spokesperson for technical escalations that need to go to other departments, while in turn implementing any provided solutions</li>
<li>Implement customized Heath Level (HL7) interfaces using XSLT, XML, and Omnicell’s Interface Engine (OIS) to integrate all of Omnicell’s products into the customer’s information systems environment</li>
<li>Use well developed problem solving and troubleshooting skills to find and resolve interface issues that arise during testing or troubleshooting</li>
</ul>
<p><i>Interface Support</i></p>
<ul>
<li>Support the Omnicell Help Desk 24/7 with emergency service ticket escalations requiring advanced interface engineering support</li>
<li>Submit unique customer configurations to the Omnicell help desk for entry into Omnicell’s service ticket system</li>
</ul>
<p><i>Consulting/Education</i></p>
<ul>
<li>Train others to support all existing interfaces and newly developed interfaces</li>
<li>Work closely with other SIEs and Omnicell management to develop and maintain interface policy and procedures</li>
</ul>
<p><i>Marginal Functions</i></p>
<ul>
<li>Support external validations/Beta projects</li>
<li>Work closely with Omnicell’s Training, Marketing, and Engineering Departments as content expert to devise new company products and supporting materials</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Required Knowledge and Skills:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Working knowledge of scripting and Object Oriented Programming (OOP) techniques and mechanisms</li>
<li>Understanding of inter-system communication methods (ex. HTTP(S)/TLS, TCP/IP, FTP(S), RS-232, network drive mapping, and firewalls)</li>
<li>Strong troubleshooting skills and strong customer communication skills (written and verbal)Good understanding of server architecture</li>
<li>Flexible work schedule to communicate with U.S. based customers on a regular basis.</li>
<li>A creative, innovative, problem-solver with a passion for quality</li>
<li>Proficient in MS Office applications</li>
<li>Familiarity with Windows Server / SQL 2008 or later.</li>
</ul>
