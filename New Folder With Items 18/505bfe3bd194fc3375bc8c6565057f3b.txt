<h2>Head of Channel Sales and Alliances</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">Synaptic AP is seeking a seasoned sales executive who will help drive the continued development of a best-in-class channels sales organization. This individual must be a creative, hands-on executive with proven leadership skills and a legacy of driving channels effectiveness. This individual will work collaboratively with the leadership of other internal functional organizations to coordinate all sales activities and programs necessary to drive a synergistic, high-performing, team-oriented sales culture. This position will report to the Senior Vice President of Sales.
<br><br>
Who are you?
<br><br>
So, who are you? You are detail and process-oriented, consultative, and always up to evaluate and test new ideas. You understand B2B sales challenges and complex Salesforce relationships with companies in diverse industries. You "get" cloud-based technologies and how to build successful sales plays and processes that communicate the value of SaaS offerings. Previous sales experience buys you credibility with tenured sales professionals and senior management.
<br><br>
What you will do
<br><br>
This candidate will drive the business strategic planning function for all Channels including the development of key business processes, metrics and analytics. This person will facilitate the weekly Channels Sales forecast process. Organizes competitive market analysis where appropriate to support channels strategies and channels sales planning cycle.
<br><br>
Responsibilities
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Works with executive and cross functional leadership to establish sales goals to support and meet Synaptic's sales, revenue and strategic growth goals.</li>
<li>Ensures that a robust management operating system exists, including key business metrics and trends that support the business unit decision-making process.</li>
<li>Develops, aligns and implements regional business plans in collaboration with sales leadership and the territory Account Executives.</li>
<li>Primary administrator of all sales support systems inside of Salesforce; drives implementation and adoption of Salesforce system and reporting systems as it relates to channel activity.</li>
<li>Traveling to onsite events to support successful execution of channel messaging and pipeline development.</li>
<li>Manage the partner relationship with Salesforce. Including increasing our status and working through contracts and certifications.</li>
<li>Assist in onboarding of new AEs as it relates to their channel territory plan and exposure to the Salesforce ecosystem.</li>
<li>Work with SF Industry teams to create buyer profiles and define elements of a successful sales cycle for Synaptic AP products, industries, and buyer types.</li>
<li>Monitor and communicate Salesforce trends and buyer behavior.</li>
<li>Source new Salesforce meetings, pipeline development for the AEs through message boards, social channels, open questions and conversations on boards, managing and engaging in Synaptic AP's chatter group and getting tied into relevant industry chatter groups.</li>
<li>Identify and grow new partnerships with other complimentary organizations including global systems integrators and ISVs.</li>
</ul>
Requirements for the position
<br><br>
To be successful in this role, you MUST have all of the following:
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>2-3 years of experience working within the Salesforce Ecosystem in any sales or alliance capacity.</li>
<li>Interest in career growth in either the alliance or sales fields.</li>
<li>Full life-cycle consultative sales experience, from first call to close, including technical demonstrations and independently leading needs analysis workshops with prospects.</li>
<li>History of consistently achieving or exceeding sales KPIs.</li>
<li>Experience with sales methodologies strongly desired.</li>
<li>Excitement around building and owning a critical business function.</li>
</ul>
Location
<br><br>
This role is remote and the location preference is in the vicinity of a Salesforce Office.
