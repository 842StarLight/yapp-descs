<h2>District Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Position Summary: </b></p>
<p>Responsible for directing and supervising activities involved in operating a group of stores in an assigned area to maximize sales and profitability.<br><b>Essential Job Functions: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Travel to store locations and lead store management in the planning, implementation and execution of merchandising and operations initiatives</li>
<li>Oversee that all stores are providing the customer service that meets or exceeds the company standards and customer expectations</li>
<li>Maintain high standards of store image ensuring that all stores are clean, well stocked and ready for business</li>
<li>Oversee that all assigned stores operate within the established inventory levels, salary budgets (and other control lines), and gross profit margins to achieve maximum profitability</li>
<li>Accountable for building store sales and gross profits through implementation of corporate merchandising policies, procedures and programs</li>
<li>Support and enforce all company policies, and local, state and federal laws and regulations</li>
<li>Assure that personnel processes (hiring, training, associate evaluations, employee discipline and termination actions) are handled in a timely manner, and in accordance to company policy</li>
<li>Maintain working relationships with associates to support the open door policy</li>
<li>Serve as a proactive leader of the operations team, evaluating and implementing new concepts in the stores</li>
<li>Coach and develop associates for growth and promotion to maintain a high quality staff</li>
<li>Partner with Human Resources to recruit, hire, train, develop and evaluate store management and store associates</li>
<li>Supervise and coach direct reports in the performance of their duties; completes performance reviews and provides feedback and development opportunities to direct reports</li>
<li>Must be able to perform the essential functions of this position with or without reasonable accommodation</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Minimum Position Qualifications: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>2+ years’ experience as a Store Manager in the convenience store industry and/or has successfully completed the training program</li>
<li>Strong leadership and analytical skills</li>
<li>Highly organized and proficient at multi-tasking</li>
<li>Excellent interpersonal skills</li>
<li>Proficient in Microsoft Office, PDI (Professional Data Solutions Inc.), Radiant and Core-tec computer software programs</li>
<li>Ability to travel independently up to 75%</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Desired Qualifications &amp; Education: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Associate’s or Bachelor’s degree</li>
<li>2+ years’ experience working as a multi-unit supervisor in a retail setting</li>
</ul>
<p>Job Type: Full-time</p>
