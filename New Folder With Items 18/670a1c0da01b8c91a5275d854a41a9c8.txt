<p>

<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
In this role, you will be part of our production team performing a variety of duties, including:</b></p>
<p></p>
<br>
<ul>
<li>
Assisting machine operators</li>
<li>
Maintenance, yard work, warehouse, production, utilities, and material handling</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Additional responsibilities include:</b></p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
General Maintenance<br>
</b></p>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Clean-up machinery and conveyors, maintain the surrounding area and keep equipment free from debris during downtime</li>
<li>
Clean floors and pick up reusable scrap for salvage, transport trash and debris away from production areas</li>
<li>
Responsible for keeping pallets neat and orderly</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Utility / Production Equipment</b></p>
<p></p>
<br>
<ul>
<li>
Assist lead operator in preparing for shift production, work closely with the lead operator and become knowledgeable of the mechanics and operation of the machines</li>
<li>
May be required to work with conveyors, ramps, elevators, etc. as assigned</li>
<li>
Operate machinery or equipment as trained and instructed following safety guidelines at all times</li>
<li>
May be required to operate equipment such as hoppers, bagging and sealer machines, forklift, palletizers, hydraulic equipment, powered tractors or vehicles, among others</li>
<li>
Operate a variety of hand tools</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Yard / Warehouse<br>
</b></p>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Assist in the loading/unloading of trucks</li>
<li>
Assist in completing shipping/receiving documents, verify loads leaving the premises</li>
<li>
Package and label products properly</li>
<li>
Maintain, verify, and count product inventory</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>

Requirements / Education / Experience</b></p>
<p></p>
<br>

Requires pre-employment drug screening/background test</li>

<li>
Ability to follow company safety procedures and attend weekly safety meetings</li>
<li>
Must be able to demonstrate lock out tag out procedures and keep key lock at all times</li>
<li>
Must have the ability to lift and/or move up to 50 lbs. from ground level</li>
<li>
Must be able to stand for extended periods of time</li>
<li>
Able to pass internal certification training</li>
<li>
Reasonable accommodations may be made to enable qualified individuals with disabilities to perform the essential duties of the job</li>
</ul>
