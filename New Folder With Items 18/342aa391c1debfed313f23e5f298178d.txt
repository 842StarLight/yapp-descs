Who are we?</b></p>
<p>
Live Nation Entertainment is the world’s leading live entertainment company comprised of global market leaders: Ticketmaster, Live Nation Concerts, Live Nation Media &amp; Sponsorship and Artist Nation Management. Ticketmaster is the global event ticketing leader with nearly 455 million tickets sold, over 145,000 events ticketed and more than 12,000 clients in 21 countries. Artist Nation Management is one of the world’s leading artist management companies, representing approximately 280 artists. These businesses power Live Nation Media &amp; Sponsorship to deliver music marketing programs across 750 brands enabling advertisers to tap into the 59 million fans Live Nation Entertainment delivers annually through its live event and digital platforms. For additional information, visit www.livenationentertainment.com.</p>

Who are you?</b></p>
<p>
Passionate and motivated. Driven, with an entrepreneurial spirit. Resourceful, innovative, forward thinking and committed. At Live Nation Entertainment, our people embrace these qualities, so if this sounds like you then please read on!</p>

<p><b>
The Role:</b></p>
<ul><li>
Assist Restaurant Staff to ensure that all kitchen orders are delivered in prompt, courteous and efficient manner</li></ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Perform table maintenance duties according HOB service standards</li>
<li>
Perform Opening/Running/Closing duties according to HOB Service Standards</li>
<li>
Respond to guest inquiries and requests in a courteous and polite manner</li>
<li>
Maintain line of sight/atmosphere control by circulating through work area throughout shift</li>
</ul>
<ul><li>
Keep floor neat, clean and free of food, trash and other obstacles throughout shift</li></ul>
<ul>
<li>
Clean all spills immediately, use floor signs to caution guests and staff</li>
<li>
Put all trash and recyclable products in proper receptacles</li>
</ul>
<ul><li>
Clean and stock line to par throughout shift</li></ul>
<ul>
<li>
Keep current on all standard operating procedures</li>
<li>
Stay informed of current menu items and descriptions</li>
<li>
Follow private party service guidelines to ensure event success</li>
<li>
Assist Other Employees as needed</li>
<li>
Follow all safety standards</li>
<li>
Required to occasionally work Special Events that are scheduled throughout the venue</li>
</ul>
