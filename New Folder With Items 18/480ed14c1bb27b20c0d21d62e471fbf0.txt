<h2>Care Specialist Full Time</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<b>Description:</b><br>
<b>SUPERVISION (RECEIVED BY AND/OR GIVEN TO):</b><br>
Directly responsible to the Purchasing Manager and HME billing manager
<br>This position does not have any supervisory status
<br><br>
The intent of this job description is to provide a summary of the major duties and responsibilities of this position and shall not be considered as a detailed description of all the work requirements that may be inherent in the position. Responsibilities of this position focus heavily on sales and customer service, including verifying valid insurance coverage, processing customer orders from various media for medical equipment and supplies, and collecting any customer payment due. Competent understanding of medical billing policy and medical equipment is necessary for this position.
<br><br>
<b>JOB DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>The primary function and focus of the position is providing customer service in the DME department and on the retail floor.</li>
<li>Greet customers entering the store or department.</li>
<li>Answer and route incoming calls on a complex digital telephone system.</li>
<li>Collect initial customer intake orders various sources (fax, telephone, in-person) and triage to the correct area of workflow for processing.</li>
<li>Ability to set accurate customer expectations and achieve a high level of customer satisfaction.</li>
<li>Execute workflow procedures to accurately guide order tasks through all steps to completion.</li>
<li>Educate patients on products available within our company in order to increase customer knowledge and increase company sales.</li>
<li>Educate patients on basic insurance coverage guidelines and limits as it pertains to products available within our company in order to increase customer knowledge.</li>
<li>Direct customers in locating products/services within our company.</li>
<li>Assist customers picking up orders per company policy.</li>
<li>Process customer sales through our POS system.</li>
<li>Triage customer concerns/questions to the correct individual for resolution.</li>
<li>Ability to identify customer service issues and complaints and forward to appropriate level of management.</li>
</ul>
<b>OTHER FUNCTIONS:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Organize and file completed documents (prescriptions, delivery sheets, etc) per company policy.</li>
<li>General light cleaning of retail areas including but not limited to dusting shelves, vacuuming the floor, wiping/cleaning the counters.</li>
<li>Other duties as assigned.</li>
</ul>
<b>MOST COMPLEX DUTY:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Balancing customer service tasks (waiting on customers at pick up, answering the phone, etc) with non-customer service tasks.</li>
<li>Ability to multitask and address incoming orders from various sources (electronic, phone, fax, in-person).</li>
<li>Interacting with customers to determine their needs and to communicate their insurance requirements. .</li>
</ul>

<b>QUALIFICATIONS (MINIMUM):</b><br>
<b>ADDITIONAL REQUIREMENTS:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Ability and willingness to be trained on internal systems required for the position including but not limited to the phone system, Point-of-Sale System, and any applicable software or hardware required for completion of assigned tasks.</li>
<li>Ability and willingness to be trained on company policy and procedures.</li>
<li>Ability and willingness to exercise tact, initiative and good judgment in dealing with people.</li>
<li>Ability to multi-task</li>
<li>Comprehensive knowledge of medical equipment and supplies or willingness to be trained</li>
<li>Comprehensive knowledge of medical insurance coverage or willingness to be trained</li>
</ul>
<b>PHYSICAL REQUIREMENTS:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Good physical and mental health.</li>
<li>Ability to stand and walk for up to eight hours per day and up to sixty consecutive minutes.</li>
<li>Occasionally lifting, carrying, pushing and pulling objects weighing up to 35 pounds.</li>
<li>Occasionally stooping and bending.</li>
<li>Rarely crouching and reaching above shoulder level.</li>
</ul>
<b>SENSORY REQUIREMENTS:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Hearing as it relates to normal conversation, high and low frequency.</li>
<li>Seeing as it relates to general vision, near vision, peripheral vision, and visual monotony.</li>
</ul>
<b>POTENTIAL ON-THE-JOB RISKS:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Lifting and moving of medical equipment during the sales process</li>
<li>Potential exposure to blood-borne pathogens via medical supplies and equipment contaminated with bodily fluids</li>
<li>Potential exposure to chemicals used in the cleaning/disinfection of medical equipment</li>
</ul>
<b>SPECIFIC PROTECTIVE EQUIPMENT AVAILABLE:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Hand sanitizer</li>
<li>Examination gloves, eye protection, and mask provided and encouraged use when necessary.</li>
</ul>
<b>COMMUNICATIONS:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Ability to communicate effectively, orally and in writing</li>
<li>EDUCATION: None</li>
<li>AGE: Employee must be at least 16 years of age at time of application.</li>
<li>TRAINING AND EXPERIENCE: Any previous work experience in a pharmacy, home medical equipment supplier, or busy retail environment dealing with the public would be considered a plus.</li>
</ul>
