<h2>Strategic Account Representative</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>QAD is seeking a <b>Midwest based, Japanese speaking Strategic Account Representative who </b>is passionate about knowing their customers’ world better than their customers know it themselves. We are challenging you to teach, tailor and control. To push the customer’s thinking and teach them something new. We are looking for Account Representatives who are fluent in Japanese and have a proven track record of sales success in <b>Large Enterprise</b> for one of our dedicated segments: <b>Automotive &amp; Industrial. </b></p>
<p>You will positively impact QAD business metrics including (but not limited to) business growth attainment, shorter business cycles, bigger business sizes, new account acquisition, etc. through knowledge, team-work and programmatic engagement.</p>
<p>Our Sales Representatives work with Sales Management, Regional Marketing and Vertical Marketing Leadership to define and achieve strategic goals. This will involve working with field marketing to build campaigns, tailoring business assets and tools to the needs of the market segment. You will also provide sales teams the support they need for customers and work with the wider Sales Success team to implement programs that drive further growth.</p>
<p>This role can be remote on the condition you easily travel to customer locations.</p>
<p><b>What you’ll do: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Prospect, identify, develop, negotiate and, ultimately, close new enterprise accounts by uncovering opportunities from both new leads and existing active customers</li>
<li>Develop and manage pipeline to meet or exceed target</li>
<li>Assist in the sales process from qualifying to closing</li>
<li>Maintain laser focus on target and named account acquisition</li>
<li>Ensure that metrics are at all times updated with the most recent accurate forecasts, latest activities and next steps</li>
<li>Understand the business landscape and be able to build differentiators into the recommendations</li>
<li>Present effectively to external customers across all levels from marketing staff to C-suite executives</li>
<li>Thrive working in a fast pace and intense sales environment</li>
<li>Effectively organize various internal and external teams to work together to drive deals through complex sales cycles</li>
</ul>
<p><b>What you’ll need</b></p>
<ul>
<li>A self-motivated overachiever attitude that enjoys developing and closing opportunities by leveraging relationships and challenging the status quo.</li>
<li>2+ years of on-quota, B2B SaaS sales experience</li>
<li>Deep understanding of marketing and customer acquisition processes</li>
<li>Experience selling into in one of our dedicated segments: Automotive, Consumer Products, Food &amp; Beverage, High Tech, Industrial and Life Sciences.</li>
<li>Stellar written and verbal communication skills, particularly the ability to communicate clear, persuasive proposals, emails and presentations.</li>
<li>Enjoy working in a collaboratively competitive environment. Driving continuous improvement in the team and wider sales function while achieving your own goals.</li>
<li>Demonstrates QAD core values - Partnership with Our Customers, Commitment to Excellence, Innovation, Global Teamwork, Integrity, and Respect for the Individual</li>
<li>High integrity, credibility, and business ethics</li>
<li>A desire for extensive travel</li>
<li>Fluent in Japanese language required</li>
<li>Bachelor’s degree preferred</li>
</ul>
<p><b>What we offer: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Your health and well being are important to us at QAD. We provide programs that help you strike a healthy work life balance.</li>
<li>Opportunity to join a growing business, launching into its next phase of expansion and transformation.</li>
<li>Collaborative culture of smart and hard-working people who support one another to get the job done.</li>
<li>An atmosphere of growth and opportunity, where idea-sharing is always prioritized over level or hierarchy.</li>
<li>Competitive compensation packages based on experience and desired skill set</li>
</ul>
<p><b>About QAD: </b></p>
<p>Imagine a future where business processes are operating at peak efficiency and perfectly aligned strategic goals. At QAD, we provide solutions and services for customers in the automotive, consumer products, food and beverage, high technology, industrial and life sciences industries so they can better align daily operations with their strategic goals. We are committed to helping our customers achieve this vision that we call the Effective Enterprise. Join us in building the Effective Enterprise, apply today!</p>
