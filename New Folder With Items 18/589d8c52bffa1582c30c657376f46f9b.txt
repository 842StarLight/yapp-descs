<p>
<b>Responsibilities</b>:</p>
<p>
Individual contributor or supervisor of small teams, responsible for assignments within own area.</p>
<p>
Works with a greater degree of independence, conferring with manager on unusual or complex assignments.</p>
<p>
Responsible for implementing good process safety practices in own area and within project teams.</p>
<p><b>
Engineering duties:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Completes assignments which involve the application of a variety of diverse engineering methods.</li>
<li>Identifies and investigates issues that present unusual conditions; solutions may require development of new or improved products, processes, or techniques.</li>
<li>Assesses feasibility and soundness of proposed engineering tests, products, and equipment.</li>
<li>Coordinates implementation of new or revised engineering solutions.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Process Improvement duties:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Executes and leads performance improvement activities which may employ various process improvement methodologies (e.g., Lean, Six Sigma, BPI, KAM, etc.).</li>
<li>Facilitates the implementation of applicable BEST/CLASS criteria.</li>
<li>Develops key performance indicators (KPIs) and management dashboards based on strategic objectives and performance improvement goals.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Project Management duties</b>:</p>
<ul>
<li>Effectively manages small to mid-sized projects to ensure project completion on-time, on-budget, and on-quality; resolves issues and conflicts; coordinates project work with other operating units; regularly reports project status</li>
</ul>
<p><br>
<b>Qualifications:</b></p>
<p><b>
Technical Competencies</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Ability to independently research, evaluate, and and/or test engineering techniques</li>
<li>Ability to evaluate and advise on project viability</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Leadership Competencies</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Plans &amp; Aligns</li>
<li>Collaborates</li>
<li>Ensures Accountability</li>
<li>Drives Results</li>
<li>Optimizes Work processes</li>
<li>Manages Complexity</li>
<li>Listens</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Education</b></p>
<ul>
<li>B.S. in Chemical Engineering is required</li>
<li>M.S. or Ph.D. in Engineering or MBA is preferred</li>
<li>Certification in a process improvement methodology (such as Six Sigma Green Belt, or Lean) or a P.E. certification is preferred.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Experience</b></p>
<ul>
<li>3 years of engineering experience is required, preferably in a manufacturing facility.</li>
</ul>
