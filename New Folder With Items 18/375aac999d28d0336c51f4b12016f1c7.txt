<h2>Senior Accountant</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Description</b></h3>
<p></p>
<p>Seismic, the world’s leading provider of sales enablement tools, is on the hunt for a for a passionate and talented Senior Accountant. Seismic has been doubling revenue year-over-year for the past five years and just raised $100 million. Our Finance organization is growing with the goal of being prepared for a potential future IPO.</p>
<p>
 You are right for this role if you are passionate about adding value to a fast pace organization through the delivery of timely, complete and accurate financial information. You are forward looking and possess the experience to build the foundation for Seismic to scale to a multi-billion dollar company. Ideally, you have a mix of Big Four and industry experience and a bachelor’s degree.</p>
<p><b>
 Responsibilities</b>:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Maintain general ledger by preparing and recording asset, liability, and expense entries of multiple subsidiaries</li>
<li>
Record, track, and reconcile multi-currency intercompany transactions</li>
<li>
Assist in management of month-end and quarter-end close</li>
<li>
Perform variance analyses and commentary for monthly financial statements</li>
<li>
Prepare schedules and provide information for year-end audit and tax returns</li>
<li>
Assist with ad hoc accounting projects in preparation for Big 4 audit</li>
<li>
Document business processes in conjunction with accounting policies that pertain to period end close work</li>
<li>
Other duties as assigned</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
 Qualifications:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
3+ years’ experience in an accounting role</li>
<li>
Must have strong knowledge of accounting practices and GAAP</li>
<li>
Must be proficient with Excel and have working knowledge of accounting software; Netsuite a plus</li>
<li>
Strong written and verbal communication skills</li>
<li>
Detail oriented with excellent organizational skills</li>
<li>
Demonstrated ability to work with minimal supervision in a fast-paced accounting environment</li>
<li>
BS/BA in Accounting or equivalent</li>
<li>
Big 4/public experience a plus</li>
</ul>
