<h1 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Title: Analyst- Marketing and Demand Generation</b></h1>
<h1 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Reports Directly To: Director, Marketing</b></h1>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<h1 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Who Are We Looking For?</b></h1>
<div>
The Analyst- Marketing and Demand Generation owns optimization and delivery of demand generation for marketing campaigns, programs and tactics that contribute to the Minnesota Wild’s opportunities, pipeline and revenue goals. This position will lead the development, implementation and optimization of funnel-building lead nurturing strategies, lead scoring, and conversion metrics.</div>
<h1 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
</b></h1>
<h1 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Responsibilities</b></h1>
<div>
<b>Demand Generation</b>
</div>
<ul>
<li>In partnership with additional members of the marketing team, develop and execute demand generation vision and strategy using industry best practices to target both B2C and B2B leads.</li>
<li>Responsible for demand generation, lifecycle analytics and reporting inclusive of testing, measurement, and optimization of all channels (SEO, SEM, paid social, digital advertising, and marketing automation) to drive results.</li>
<li>In partnership with channel owners, help plan and execute marketing campaigns which generate leads and retention of customers.</li>
<li>Own funnel optimization via consistent testing and optimization.</li>
<li>Responsible for reporting and communicating marketing impact inclusive of demand generation goals, metrics for success, lead forecasting, and conversion rates.</li>
<li>Oversee audience segmentation and develop lead nurturing campaigns based on target buyer personas and real-time analytics.</li>
<li>Proactively review best practices, research trends and competitive activity; recommend and implement scalable changes to the marketing automation platform.</li>
</ul>
<div>
<br>
<b>Collaboration</b>
</div>
<ul>
<li>Collaborate with various team members to deliver the marketing mix of digital tactics including but not limited to: paid/organic social and search, display, content development and email to drive quality, funnel volume.</li>
<li>Closely collaborate with the Sr. Marketing Manager on the planning, delivery and performance measurements of demand generation campaigns including single game ticket purchases, season tickets members, and more.</li>
<li>Work with key channel leaders to propose, develop, test, and execute multi-channel acquisition programs.</li>
<li>Approach challenges and help align activities to sales team goals.</li>
</ul>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<h1 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Qualifications</b></h1>
<ul>
<li>Minimum 4 years of demand generation and analytics strongly preferred</li>
<li>Experience analyzing customer data required</li>
<li>Prior experience working on professional sports events preferred, but not required.</li>
<li>Demonstrated knowledge of marketing campaign execution and strong reporting and tracking</li>
<li>Expert in lead scoring, nurturing programs, list management, cleansing, segmentation and targeting</li>
<li>SQL and database knowledge</li>
<li>Ability to translate overall business and marketing strategy into a precise and effective demand generation strategy</li>
<li>Meticulous attention to detail</li>
<li>Ability to lead and communicate effectiveness of marketing campaigns and provide recommendations for pipeline growth</li>
<li>Strong verbal and written communication and interpersonal skills</li>
<li>Proven collaborator who achieves results through others</li>
<li>Ability to prioritize and manage multiple, parallel projects within cross-functional teams</li>
<li>Computer proficiency in the use of Microsoft office suite to include Outlook, Word, Excel and PowerPoint</li>
<li>Knowledge of various marketing software systems- specifically Eloqua, Tableau, KORE Dynamics 365, and marketing attribution software strongly preferred</li>
</ul>
