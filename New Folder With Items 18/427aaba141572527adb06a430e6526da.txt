<h2>Sr. Product Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">About CLARA
<br>CLARA analytics ("CLARA") drives change in the commercial insurance markets with easy to use artificial intelligence (AI) based solutions that dramatically reduce claims costs by anticipating the needs of claimants and helping align the best resources to meet those needs. Leading examples of our solutions include CLARA providers, an award-winning provider scoring engine that helps rapidly connect injured workers with top performing doctors, and CLARA claim, an early warning system that helps frontline claims teams efficiently manage claims, reduce escalations and understand the drivers of complexity. CLARA’s customers include a broad spectrum from the top 25 insurance carriers to small, self-insured organizations.
<br><br>
This is a chance to get in early with a rapidly growing Silicon Valley company, and to participate in developing the next generation of truly game-changing products in the Insurance Industry. Job title and compensation will be adjusted as appropriate to meet the experience level of the right candidate.

Job Description
<br>We are looking for a self-driven, proven Senior Product Manager who is passionate about creating great products while solving really complex customer problems through the use of AI and Machine Learning technology. We are a leading software provider in the fast growing Insurtech industry. We improve insurance claims outcomes for patients and carriers in commercial insurance through easy-to-use AI.
<br><br>
Key Responsibilities
<br><br>
§ Be the product owner of the Clara Providers product line
<br>§ Be the quarter-back and define what we do and when we do - define and document the product features and roadmap for Clara Providers
<br>§ Work with the technology, data and client facing teams to define/deliver on the product roadmap
<br>§ Develop a working relationship and establish an open dialog with the client community. Collect and evaluate product requirements through task analysis, business process analysis, storyboards,
<br>surveys, workshops, and usability studies
<br>§ Present roadmap updates to customers on a periodic basis
