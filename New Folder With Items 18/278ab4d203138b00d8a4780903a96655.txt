<h2>Thermal Systems Sales Engineer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>This position is responsible for various aspects of Thermal System activities including: Sales &amp; Marketing, Engineering, R&amp;D, Production, Commissioning, Finance, HR, Legal, Quality, Safety aspects. Develop new products and applications for the systems group.
</p>
<ul>
<li>Establish and continue to evolve the strategic direction of the department.</li>
<li>Roll-out of sales and marketing activities, including the P&amp;L responsibility
</li>
<li>Manage the project execution to satisfy financial objectives and customer requirements.</li>
<li>Guide all Engineering activities, including R&amp;D projects and the product development</li>
<li>Responsible for production planning, order processing, manufacturing and commissioning</li>
<li>Ensure an active and ongoing commitment to quality, safety and continuous improvement</li>
<li>Management of supplier selection and supplier inspection</li>
<li>Act as a leader and manager for the individuals and teams responsible for the execution of the strategy. Build, train and coach the team. Establish and follow-up the personal resource planning</li>
<li>Mentor, and coach direct reports to improve quality and overall output. Ensure employee training and development.
</li>
<li>Promote and ensure positive relations within the organization, with customers, vendors, and the Works Council.</li>
<li>Establish cash planning, preparation and adherence to budgets and follow-up of the cashflow, including invoice approvals.</li>
<li>Developing the Capital Expenditure plans to support company objectives for growth and revenue.</li>
<li>Management of all legal aspects of contracts, project execution, commissioning and warranty
</li>
<li>Be the advocate for the customer within the Production side of the business</li>
<li>Establish and manage a key account plan for global customers</li>
<li>Work with other group heads to ensure channel partners are trained and aware of systems opportunities</li>
<li>Analyze profitability of projects after completion and ensure compliance is followed in achieving the predicted margins.</li>
<li>Develop new applications for existing systems and as the need arises develop new solutions for existing customer base to continually grow the Systems product capabilities</li>
<li>Have a core sales plan for Dealcoholizing and evaporation systems that is down to customer level.</li>
 <li>Report out on a 30 and 90 day project outlook every month</li>
</ul>
<div>
<b>Requirements</b>:</div>
<div>
BS Mechanical Engineering or MBA with strong technical understanding</div>
<div>
 10 years of experience in a similar role working with engineering products.</div>
<div>
 Heat transfer background required</div>
</div>
