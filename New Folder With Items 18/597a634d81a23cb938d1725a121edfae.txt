<h2>Mgr III Material Program Mgmt</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>The Material Program Manager (MPM) is a strong leader with demonstrated project management skills. You must be a self-starter, capable of developing and achieving organizational and program objectives, and must be able to successfully interface with all levels of internal management.<br>
<br>
You will be responsible for developing and executing Supply Chain strategies and plans for capture/proposal efforts and program execution. The MPM will serve as the liaison between the Program Executive, Program Managers, IPTs and the Supply Chain organization and will be responsible for the development, coordination and execution of all program material strategies, supply chain objectives, and enterprise objectives. You serve as the leader for all program supply chain activities, and will advise the Business Unit SCM Director and Sr. Manager as well as the Program Management Office leadership team on material strategies and plans. You may also be required to direct other MPMs on the supported program activities. You lead the front end of the business SCM activities including early Supply Chain Management involvement and early supplier involvement that is focused on Enterprise Subcontract Process Excellence (ESPX) and other strategic sourcing initiatives.<br>
<br>
The position requires an individual who will be able to exert influence and have an effect on the overall objectives and long-range goals of the program and the program IPT team. You are the single point of contact to management and to internal customers, including Program Office, Quality, Engineering, Operations, Finance, and Contracts. You will represent the Supply Chain organization on policies, strategies and objectives, oversee all Supply Chain activities needed to meet program requirements, and ensure a strong link within and among various Supply Chain disciplines, including Sourcing, Logistics, and Production Control. You are the acknowledged expert on all Supply Chain issues and their impact on the enterprise programs.<br>
<br>
<b>Other MPM duties:</b><ul>
<li>
Develop and support Supply Chain proposal activities including task descriptions and bases of estimates that support price to win (PTW) strategies.</li>
<li>
Develop and execute material program plans (MPP). Establish Material Sales Forecasts for assigned programs.</li>
<li>
Execute make/buy/where process.</li>
<li>
Develop and execute risk/opportunity plans to meet program objectives.</li>
<li>
Balance strategic thinking with tactical application of strategies to programs.</li>
<li>
Work closely with Engineering, Quality and Operations to create common schedule based on Material Requirements Planning (MRP).</li>
<li>
Collaborate with Supply Chain disciplines for support as needed.</li>
<li>
Monitor performance metrics and proactively act to address program and functional issues.</li>
<li>
Strong Earned Value Management and Financial management skills, including Material EACs and Material Forecasting.</li>
</ul>
