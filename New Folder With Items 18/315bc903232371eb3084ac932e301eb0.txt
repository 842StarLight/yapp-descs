<h2>Project Manager, IAS Business Consulting</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>The Project Manager will play a critical role on our Consulting Enablement &amp; Effectiveness team within our Business Consulting department, which is responsible for client growth and retention. They will plan, execute and measure the success of our key consulting programs, as well as develop ongoing growth and development opportunities for our team members. This role will provide the candidate with an exceptionally broad view of LPL’s business and serve as critical support for senior management.</p>
<p><b>
Specific responsibilities include:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Enhancing our client retention and growth by leading the development and delivery of consulting campaigns.</li>
<li>
Increasing the skill and competencies of our Business Consultants through planning and execution of our ongoing training plan.
</li>
<li>Increase the effectiveness of our Business Consulting campaigns through the creation of sales or educational materials used throughout the consultative sales process. Examples include fact sheets, newsletters, emails and sales scripts.</li>
<li>
Augment the management reporting and key performance indicators (KPIs) used to monitor the success of our consulting engagements through oversight of and enhancements to our Client Relationship Management (CRM) software and other management tools.</li>
<li>
Improving awareness and responsiveness to client relationship issues through oversight of our escalation process.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
