<h2>Self Storage Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Ready to be </b><b><i>Personal?</i></b> If you are self-motivated, outgoing, and have a passion for helping people, we want to hear from you!</p>
<p>We are Personal Mini Storage, a self storage management company that started in 1982 as a single location in Orlando. We now provide 3,000,000+ rentable square feet of storage and RV/boat space across 44 central Florida locations and continue to grow in the area. Come grow with us!</p>
<p>We're looking for a <b>Store Manager</b> that shows up fully each day to join our team!</p>
<p>As a Store Manager, you'll have responsibilities of managing a high performing storage facility in<b> Leesburg,</b><b> FL</b>.</p>
<p><b>Here's how you'd make an impact and be successful:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Store Manager's manage the day to day operations of a storage facility and provide a personal experience and excellent customer service to customers. Customer service is our #1 priority.</li>
<li>
To help achieve facility goals, you'll utilize your strong sales and marketing abilities to rent storage units, U-Haul trucks, up-sell boxes and moving supplies, and increase brand awareness in the local community.<ul>
<li>
You'll learn how to use our self storage sales best practices to convert storage and truck rental prospects to customers.</li>
<li>
You'll need to have a friendly, positive attitude and strong sales presence over the phone and in-person.</li>
<li>
Create relationships with nearby area businesses and implement marketing strategies.</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>
Moving can be very stressful for customers. Our job is to make it a bit easier for them by identifying our customer's needs, confidently create a <i>PERSONAL</i>ized solution by asking the right questions (eg. Do they need storage + rental truck + boxes?) and deliver an exceptional experience.</li>
<li>
Manage daily payment flow (cash, check, and credit card balances) and making timely, error-free bank deposits.</li>
<li>
Maintaining a clean, professional appearance inside the office and outside on the facility property. Managers spend a sufficient amount of time outside ensuring the property is clean and 'customer-ready' by sweeping units/aisles, performing lock-checks on units, and performing other general maintenance tasks. (light duty / light lifting)</li>
<li>
Maintaining low accounts receivables by performing collection calls to delinquent tenants over the phone, keeping up-to-date notes on accounts, and by sending email notices and SMS/text message reminders through our software.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Here's the passion and skills you'd want to have:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
<b>Excellent verbal and communication skills</b> are key as you are the face and front line of the business. "You" will be the reason many customers choice to store their belongings with us vs. the competition.</li>
<li>
<b>
Outgoing, engaging personality </b>that takes a proactive role when handling customer requests/issues and daily tasks such as: calling new leads to reserve units, managing daily unit inventory, and replying back to existing customers in a timely manner.<br>
</li>
<li>
<b>Strong, general computer skills and competency</b> is needed to navigate Windows operating systems, internet browsers, and other applications. You'll learn how to efficiently navigate and operate various software platforms used by the company to perform daily operations tasks.</li>
<li>
<b>
Past job experiences in a customer-focused position</b> such as in retail, sales associate, self storage, hotel industry, restaurant, and other industries. A minimum of 2 years of experience in retail sales, retail store, management, sales associate of retail jobs, or in customer service roles.</li>
<li><b>
Reliable transportation, valid driver's license, and willing to travel from time to time for corporate meetings.</b></li>
</ul>
