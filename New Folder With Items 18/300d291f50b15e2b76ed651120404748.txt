<p>OXO is hiring a <b>Go-To-Market Project Manager </b>for our New York City Office.
</p>

</b></p>

When we have an idea, we’re going to make it happen, even if it hasn’t been done before—especially if it hasn’t been done before. </p>
<p></p>

With a guest speaker series covering topics like service robots and crafting hard-to-write letters, group limoncello making, book and bike clubs, volunteering opportunities, luau-themed happy hours, summer outings to theme parks, and an annual ski trip—we like coming to work every day and think you should, too.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>What will I be doing?
</b></p>
<p></p>
<p>The Go-To-Market Project Manager will be assigned to 1-2 OXO Product Development Team(s) and will have the following areas of responsibility –
</p>
<ul>
<li>
<b>Schedule Management </b>– Drive product development project schedules from kickoff to shipping, collaborating closely with internal Product Development, Sales and Marketing teams, as well as internal and external Design teams. Ensure schedule status is effectively communicated across key teams.
</li>
<li>
<b>Effective Cross-Function Communication </b>– Work closely with Product Development, Sales, Brand, Supply/Demand teams to ensure they have a thorough understanding of schedule and cross-functional deliverable status. Conversely, build knowledge of cross-functional team needs, consolidate feedback and relay to product teams. Coordinate project prioritization and successful launch strategies working with all parties.
</li>
<li>
<b>Successful Product Launch Strategies </b>– Working with Go-to-Market Project Management Team, act as a facilitator of cross-functional communication and drive alignment/resolution on launch related questions and issues that arise. Continually build on existing product launch strategies and implement new strategies where possible.
</li>
<li>
<b>Development Process Insights </b>– Working with Go-to-Market Project Management team, establish an in-depth understanding of product development schedules, identifying common bottlenecks and potential tripping points, and build reference tools to aid in project planning and schedule management.
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>What are the requirements for consideration?
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s Degree or other 4-year Degree from an accredited college or university in a relevant field.
</li>
<li>1+ years of prior experience in project/account management preferable.
</li>
<li>Proficiency in Microsoft Office.
</li>
<li>Understanding of industrial design, product development, consumer product manufacturing a plus.
</li>
<li>Understanding of Smartsheet or other project management software, as well as project management methods a plus.
</li>
<li>Self-motivated, able to effectively manage large number of projects and meet tight deadlines.
</li>
<li>Extremely organized, possesses an eye for detail, able to think ahead and anticipate next steps.
</li>
<li>Excellent verbal and written communication skills.
</li>
<li>Applicants must be authorized to work in the United States on a Full-Time basis.
</li>
</ul>
