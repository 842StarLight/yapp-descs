<p><b>
Job Summary:
</b></p>
<p>The Engraver manages the operation of a customized mobile engraving machine for the purpose of etching personalized computer developed logos or messages on individual spirit bottles. The Engraver provides a high level of quality work while engaging with key account personnel, distributor sales reps/managers, trade influencers, and consumers with the objective of building strong relationships and maximizing sales.</p>
