RnD Interactive is currently seeking an experienced Automotive Sales Professional with a proven history of success selling Internet Advertising and Marketing solutions to car dealers to open a new sales territory in Illinois.<br>
<br>
</div>
<div>Job Overview</div>
<div>As an Account Manager, you will have direct sales responsibility for RnD Interactive's complete suite of Lead and Traffic generating products for Franchised and Independent car dealers within an assigned sales territory. Within the territory, the Account Manager will have primary responsibility for prospecting and contracting of new customers, handling escalations that are a risk to the retention of the client, and upselling existing clients to more valuable packages of Marketing Services. A successful candidate will possess a well-developed network of dealership contacts.
<br>
<br>
The Account Manager will interact with clients within all levels of the dealership organization as well as the majority of our internal departments. The Account Manager must be adept at building relationships, developing successful sales plans, creating compelling presentations and possess a true passion for follow-up.
</div>
<div><b>The job requires extensive travel within the sales territory.</b></div>
<div>Responsibilities:
</div>
<ul>
<li>Identifying and contacting prospective new customers within the sales territory using phone, in-person visits, and other methods that prove effective</li>
<li>Present Marketing Solutions to prospective new clients via phone / web presentation and in person</li>
<li>Closing sales on a regular basis, focusing on sales of certain types of packages when directed by Sales Leaders</li>
<li>Maintaining relationships with the existing customer base in your territory</li>
<li>Up-selling customers to a higher level package of services</li>
<li>Traveling into your territory for in person meetings</li>
<li>In person sales at major conventions and important meetings of clients, groups, or partners</li>
<li>Other tasks as assigned</li>
</ul>
<div>Skills and Experience:
</div>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor's degree is preferred</li>
<li>Minimum of three years Sales experience in the Automotive Industry</li>
<li>Demonstrated knowledge and understanding of Internet Marketing Solutions and their use within the Automotive industry is strongly preferred</li>
<li>Excellent communication skills (English)</li>
<li>Familiarity with Web based technologies (with on the job training provided for proprietary technologies and applications)</li>
<li>Proven sales ability, critical thinking skills, and familiarity with common office and communications programs</li>
</ul>
