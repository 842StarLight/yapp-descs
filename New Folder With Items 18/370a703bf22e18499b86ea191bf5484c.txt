<h2>Mortgage Loan Originator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div><div><div><div>
<div>
<div><b>Primary Responsibilities</b></div> <div>Under general guidance of the VP of Residential Mortgage Sales and in accordance with established policies and procedures, the incumbent is responsible for the solicitation and origination of mortgage loan products in designated sales markets on behalf of the Bank. Establishes effective referral networks and professional relationships to meet assigned loan production objectives.</div>
</div>
<br>
<div>
<div><b>Tasks Performed</b></div> <div>
<b>Key Accountabilities</b><br>
1. Model behaviors consistent with A+ Merits to foster an effective culture.<br>
2. Originate Residential and equity products to meet portfolio and secondary market requirements<br>
3. Maintaining current working knowledge of industry current events and changes impacting future mortgage activity.<br>
4. Continuously updates working knowledge of competitive products, procedures and processes to ensure superior customer experience and satisfaction.<br>
<b>Success Measures</b><br>
1. Ability to work cohesively with internal support staff to close loans within regulatory guidelines and while exceeding borrower service expectations.<br>
2. Establishes a network of centers of influence to provide referrals to meet or exceed gross loan volume.<br>
3. Total comprehension of all mortgage products offered by NHMB and a working knowledge of other mortgage products available within the mortgage industry.</div>
</div>
<br>
<div>
<div><b>Essential Functions</b></div>
<div>
<div>1. Adheres to the NHMB Values: A+ Merits - Accountability, Mutuality, Excellence, Respect, Integrity, Teamwork and Stewardship.</div>
<div>
2. Provides loan origination service to existing and potential customers via the phone or in person appointments; identifies and presents various loan program options that will meet their needs; and qualifies potential customers by analyzing tax returns, income, asset statements, credit reports and other necessary documents.
</div>
<div>3. Meets established loan production objectives by originating Bank mortgage loan products in accordance with established bank procedures and underwriting guidelines as well as compliance with applicable state and federal regulations.</div>
<div>
4. Ensures complete and thorough documentation; monitors application status of originated loans throughout the processing and closing steps. Actively assists with resolving customer documentation or other application issues to minimize processing delays and to meet customer service expectations.</div>
<div>
5. Provides optimum customer availability to take mortgage loan applications, provide financing options and products and conduct pre-qualification of clients. Provides in-branch walk in coverage for the branch locations as directed based on business need.
</div>
<div>6. Actively participates in sales, training and development, or other business development opportunities such as realtor meetings, open houses, etc. to inform agents of the latest developments in the market and bank programs. Distributes rate sheets and other marketing materials to realtors and other referral sources.</div>
<div>
7. Promotes and obtains Bank deposit accounts and makes appropriate referrals to other Bank departments and Affiliates as appropriate.</div>
<div>
8. Satisfactorily completes all required compliance training and complies with all regulations which apply within the scope of the position.</div>
<div>
9. Performs additional duties as requested.</div>
</div>
</div>
<br>
<div>
<div><b>Minimum Physical Requirements</b></div> <div>Physical surroundings vary as the person does not work solely in the bank but mostly travels to different off-site locations. Minimal physical effort required; normally seated with the freedom of movement on a regular basis; handling of materials, supplies. Work includes evenings and weekends, and driving your own insured vehicle to meet with realtors, clients, builders, etc.</div>
</div>
<br>
<div>
<div><b>Desired Education</b></div> <div>College</div>
</div>
<br>
<div>
<div><b>Desired Degree</b></div> <div>Associates</div>
</div>
<br>
<div>
<div><b>Desired Major</b></div> <div>Business, Finance, Marketing</div>
</div>
<br>
<div>
<div><b>Desired Job Experience</b></div> <div>Three to five years banking experience with a strong knowledge of residential mortgage lending products, regulations and systems.</div>
</div>
<br>
<div>
<div><b>Certifications Required</b></div> <div>NMLS registration or ability to become registered.
</div>
</div>
<br>
<div>
<div><b>Other Qualifications</b></div> <div>Excellent verbal and written communication skills; strong computer skills; self-directed with experience working with diverse clientele base; ability to foster effective referral networks to meet production objectives; working knowledge of mortgage lending/servicing software applications; demonstrated sales qualifying, presentation and closing skills; visual and manual dexterity to operate general office equipment, such as a computer, copier/printer.</div>
