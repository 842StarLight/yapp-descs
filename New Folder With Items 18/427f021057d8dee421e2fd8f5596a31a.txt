<p><b>JobSummary</b></p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
Manage branding and strategic initiatives for key global markets,working in partnership with franchise partners, cross-functional Global teams(Operations, New Market Development, Training, Culinary, Supply Chain, Finance)and with US Brand Strategy team. This critical role promotes and safeguards theconnective framework of the Chili's brand in each respective market, drivingtraffic, relevance and positioning for growth.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<div><b>
Your Key Job Functions</b></div>
<ul>
<li><p>
Develop marketing strategies that leverage andreinforce brand strengths to drive traffic, grow short-term sales and long-termbrand value in the responsible regions.</p></li>
<li><p>
Provide on-going marketing consultation toGlobal Franchise Partners via financial/data analysis and strategic insightstranslated into successful marketing programs.</p></li>
<li><p>
Prioritize projects and manage deliverablesthat are aligned with achieving global KPIs.</p></li>
<li><p>
Ensure brand marketing strategies are executedin-line with Chili's brand equities.</p></li>
<li><p>
Manage third-party vendors/agency partners forcreative and media development.</p></li>
<li><p>
Responsible for managing co-op marketingbudgets (where available) and Marketing Budget.
</p></li>
</ul>
<p><b>What YouBring to the Team</b></p>
<ul>
<li><p>
Bachelor's in Business Administration,Management or Marketing; MBA highly preferred.</p></li>
<li><p>
5-7 years of consumer facing, progressive brandmarketing experience within the Restaurant, Casual Dining category; backgroundin CPG and/or franchisee network preferred.</p></li>
<li><p>
Knowledge of international markets - eitherfrom work experience or from residing in region.</p></li>
<li><p>
Strong Communicator: Excellent written andverbal communication skills in English, second language a plus. Ability toconfidently present objectives and desired outcomes, in a clear and concisemanner, to leadership and cross-functional teams. Consistently usescommunication / presentation skills to influence outcomes, without directauthority, to get things done in a timely fashion.</p></li>
<li><p>
Strategic and Creative Thinking: Thinkholistically, strategically to problem-solve and drive towards resolutioncreatively; able to write focused briefs, evaluate creative against big picturestrategies.</p></li>
<li><p>
Business and Financial Acumen: Stronganalytical and measurement/tracking skills. Be able to identify actionableinsights, develop strategies and translate them into effective marketingprograms. Skill includes understanding and experience withquantitative/qualitative research.</p></li>
<li><p>
Marketing Acumen: Experience leading strategiccommunication channels (e.g., digital/social, menu/print, etc.), with theability to interpret and modify media plans. Robust experience in selection andmanagement of external supplier partners is key - Research, Advertising,Creative, PR, Digital, Social Media and activation based agencies.</p></li>
<li><p>
Project and Process Management: Manage timeand budget effectively, including balancing multiple priorities to meetdeadlines. Ability to identify or implement systems and process improvements.</p></li>
<li><p>
Cross-functional Collaboration and Leadership:Demonstrated leadership skills with the ability to effectively build relationships,collaborate and influence across the business with a variety of stakeholders,cultural backgrounds, expertise &amp; experiences.</p></li>
<li><p>
Self-starter with High Energy Level: Abilityto work independently and equally skilled to collaborate in a team, rallyingthe group to deliver results. Strong attention to detail.</p></li>

Arabic or Mandarin language skills a plus</div></li>
