<h2>Product Marketing Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div><div>
<p><b>The Opportunity:</b>
<br>
We’re looking for a Marketing Manager, Partnerships to help us shape the future of education. With nearly 20,000 organizational customers and millions of student users, Blackboard is the world's leading education technology company with the industry's most comprehensive student-success solutions. Our mission is to partner with the global education community to enable student and institutional success by leveraging innovative technologies and services.
<br>
<br>
This role works in our Global Field Marketing organization, responsible for creating and implementing effective program strategies that are aligned with the go-to-market goals and objectives for our products and services solutions. Our Blackboard Partner Program is leveraging Blackboard’s vast partner ecosystem to enable our clients to provide a richer, more engaging educational experience for their learners, no matter what their unique needs may be, and to differentiate Blackboard.
<br>
<br>
As Marketing Manager, Partnerships, you will report to the Sr. Director, Partnerships Operations, and be responsible for crafting, launching, and driving adoption of Blackboard partner solutions in the Higher Education space. You will be a key contributor in delivering client needs definition, messaging, and the orchestration of marketing programs to create awareness, drive lead generation and adoption, and enable Sales for a portfolio of partner solutions. You will translate solution positioning into client-facing and sales-facing assets and help build effective campaign strategies. You will be instrumental in content creation and campaign execution. You’ll develop an understanding of Blackboard products, prospect and client needs, market opportunities, and competitive threats and positioning, and be able to apply this knowledge to build excitement that drives Blackboard Partners through engagement journeys.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>Additionally, you will work cross-functionally with key members of Blackboard’s Strategy &amp; Partnerships, Product Management, Product Development, Sales, and Marketing teams. You’ll cultivate effective internal relationships that can contribute to identifying new solutions while also optimizing our marketing practices to meet the needs of our Partner Program. You will guide and work with cross-functional teams, communicate both internally and externally, and work with Blackboard Partners.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>Specific responsibilities include:</p>
<ul>
<li>Supporting the development, management, and execution of Partnerships marketing programs, focusing on creating demand and retention for Blackboard’s Partner services and developing further innovative ideas to drive Blackboard’s messaging to the client ecosystem</li>
<li>
Overseeing and executing the launch planning activities for new partnerships</li>
<li>
Working with partners and cross-functionally within Blackboard to develop comprehensive operational plans and successful program execution</li>
<li>
Becoming fluent in the marketplace dynamics, Partner segment needs, roles, and personas and developing and helping to execute scalable marketing activities based on this Partner-centric and segmented approach</li>
<li>
Generating reports and analysis to support marketing key performance indicators such as lead generation, lead follow-up, and lead conversion</li>
<li>
Creating and managing campaigns on behalf of Partners into the Blackboard installed customer base, leveraging all relevant elements of the marketing mix including PR, social media, events, online marketing, direct marketing, and field programs</li>
<li>
Managing and developing the creation of effective sales tools and assets, including sales training materials</li>
<li>
Defining and reporting on key program metrics to stakeholders and executive leadership</li>
<li>
Building and maintaining working relationships with our premier Partners and representing their marketing interests within the Blackboard organization</li>
<li>
Coordinating with numerous groups including Strategy &amp; Partnerships, Field and Product Marketing, Corporate Marketing, Product Management, and Client Success</li>
</ul>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>The Candidate:</b></p>
<p><i>Required skills:</i></p>
<ul>
<li>3+ years of experience in B2B and/or B2G software marketing</li>
<li>
A deep understanding of customer segmentation and experience using data and analytics to inform and influence decision-making</li>
<li>
Outstanding writing, presentation, storytelling, and organizational skills</li>
<li>
Proficiency with developing integrated, multi-touch marketing plans</li>
<li>
Ability to drive demand for a large Partner ecosystem, leveraging a wide variety of techniques including content marketing, drip campaigns, digital/web, etc.</li>
<li>
Outstanding project management and coordination skills and ability to track multiple simultaneous projects and deliverables</li>
<li>
Ability to excel in a fast-paced, cross-functional environment</li>
<li>
Self-motivated, innovative, collaborative, multi-tasker, creative, and analytical</li>
</ul>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><i>Preferred skills:</i></p>
<ul>
<li>Familiarity with Salesforce and with principles of content marketing and marketing automation</li>
<li>
A bachelor’s degree in Marketing or Business</li>
<li>
Education industry experience</li>
</ul>
