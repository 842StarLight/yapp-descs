<div><b>Software Engineering Manager</b></div>
<div><b>Reston, VA</b></div>
<div></div>
<div>Cirium is looking for a Software Engineering Manager based in the Reston, VA office to cultivate and inspire an agile software engineering team. This position reports to the Sr. Director of Software Engineering and serves as the glue between the engineering teams and individuals from functional groups across Cirium including: Engineering, DevOps / SREs, Product Management, Data Science, Support, Sales, Marketing and Finance, and also interfacing with key external partners on data and support issues.</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div>The Software Engineering Manager provides leadership, direction, vision and management for Data Acquisition, Data Processing and Data Delivery for ticketing data, DOT traffic and financial data, as well as transforming schedules data for input into QSI models and other downstream products. The position collaborates with Product Managers and others to lead the teams through a variety of efforts such as research, design, estimation, implementation, test and delivery, covering all of the teams’ software products, features, fixes and internal tools. The position also works to build bench strength by developing Principal Engineers and future team leaders. Additional responsibilities may include overseeing team budgets, identifying and supporting talent, defining resourcing requirements and allocations, plus recruiting of engineers.</div>

<div>A valid passport and the ability to travel occasionally within the United States, Canada and the United Kingdom is required.</div>
