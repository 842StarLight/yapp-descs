<div><div><div><div>
<p>Scientific Games (SG) is a global leader focused on delivering an ever-expanding portfolio of lottery and gaming products and services to the world's government-regulated and government-sponsored entities. Scientific Games' global reach is made possible through the expertise, skill and dedicated efforts of its 8,000+ employees who serve lottery and gaming customers in more than 100+ countries on 6 continents.<br>
<br>
The Marketing Operations Project Manager will play a key role in managing the Marketing Department’s roles and responsibilities in projects in the areas of Product Marketing and Insights, including business intelligence, marketing information systems and primary research. The duties will often require working closely with internal and external customers, in conjunction with Insights’ SMEs, to ensure that all deliverables are in sync with overall objectives of the larger organization. This role will manage projects of varying size and complexity through the entire project lifecycle.<br>
<br>
1. Coordinate Marketing and Insights’ resources and third parties/vendors for the flawless execution of projects.<br>
2. Process new vendors/contractors and update existing vendors including all required Compliance and Procurement forms.<br>
3. Assist in defining project scope and objectives.<br>
4. Monitor and track projects’ progress for internal and external stakeholders, including many primary research projects.<br>
5. Enter, submit for approval, and receive into Oracle all project-related purchase requests and invoices.<br>
6. Report on resource availability, performance, risks and escalate to management as needed.<br>
7. Provides a single point of contact for projects and ensures projects are on time, in budget, and within scope.<br>
8. Maintain comprehensive project documentation.<br>
9. Coordinate and document marketing requirements for bid responses, including maintenance of the procurement responsibility and asset database.<br>
10. Establish a proactive process with the sales team for tracking marketing contract deliverables upon signed contract.<br>
11. Recommends/takes action to direct the analysis of and solution to problems.
</p>
