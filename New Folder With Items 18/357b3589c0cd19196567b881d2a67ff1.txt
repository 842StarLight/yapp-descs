<p>
In this position you will support the organization by providing day-to-day administrative and operations tasks. Position requires an organized, detail-oriented, proactive person who enjoys providing support to an outgoing team of individuals. Our company has a history of developing long-lasting relationships with our clients, and our Administrative Assistant is key to the development and maintenance of these important relationships.</p>
<p><b>
Duties Include:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Answer phones and assist with customer requests</li>
<li>Process sales orders for supply requests</li>
<li>Assist with general filing and mail distribution</li>
<li>Assist with data entry of invoices and payables</li>
<li>Ensure invoices are distributed in a timely manner</li>
<li>Maintain electronic customer and vendor files</li>
<li>Collect billing/print volume data i.e. meter reads</li>
<li>Work with service to resolve any print volume discrepancies</li>
<li>Support the maintenance and organization of office related services</li>
<li>Enter and track manufacturer rebate programs</li>
<li>Assist marketing with blog postings, social media, collateral materials, etc.</li>
<li>Book corporate travel for DTS employees</li>
<li>Help to plan and execute company events</li>
<li>Backup internal admin staff as needed</li>
<li>Utilize multiple software systems to perform essential duties</li>
</ul>
