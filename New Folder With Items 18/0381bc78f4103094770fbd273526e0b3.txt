<div><b>A Day in the Life</b></div>
<div>The Sr Market Manager, Program Management is a member of the Global Marketing Innovative Business Modeling team and will report directly to the Global Marketing leader. This strategic and operational role leads and manages a wide array of complex and difficult programs, most importantly, the development, and launch of the Patient Monitoring Global Marketing Strategic Planning and Execution Process, directing optimal alignment of resources, rollout to the global regional marketing teams, and ongoing governance and accountability to the plan and results, globally.<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>The Sr Market Manager, Program Management is responsible for several key initiatives including being the PM global marketing core team member of the Integrated Business Planning (IBP) MITG initiative, establishing a standardized digital marketing capability for internal communication programs, managing the ongoing regional communication and strategic alignment governance, society and conference strategy, Key Opinion Leaders (KOL) management in partnership with medical affairs and supporting the needs of the Patient Monitoring strategic planning processes.</div>
The Sr Market Manager will work collaboratively across the business with functional partners including Global Marketing, Regional Marketing, R&amp;D, Finance, Medical/Clinical Affairs, Regulatory Affairs, Legal, Research and Development, and Information Technology.<div>
