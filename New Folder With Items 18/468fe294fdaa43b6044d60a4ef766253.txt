<b>BDC Representative - Job Description:</b><br>
<br>
The BD Representative handles a high volume of phone calls, e-mail, live chat and text/sms correspondence from internet and traditional advertising.<br>
</p>

</li>
<li>
<div>Handles all Internet sales inquiries personally</div>
