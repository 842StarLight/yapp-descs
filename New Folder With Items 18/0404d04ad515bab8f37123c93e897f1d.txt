<p><b>Position Summary:
</b></p>
<p>The <b>Client Manager </b>is responsible for the relationship with clients and serves as the main point of contact. The Client Manager manages all aspects of the energy management and procurement program, such as procurement and risk management recommendations, reporting, energy budgets etc. for those clients. This position is based out of our <b>Louisville, Kentucky </b>office.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Position responsibilities:
</b></p>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Manage the relationship and serve as the primary point of contact for an assigned group of industrial clients
</li>
<li>Develop long term relationships with persons in the clients’ organizations
</li>
<li>Lead the industrial clients in the Client Manager’s portfolio to making cost effective and sustainable decisions related to their energy costs and usage so as to strategically position each company
</li>
<li>Assist the clients with purchase transactions
</li>
<li>Report out on the clients energy program using Schneider Electric’s proprietary software Struxtureware Resource Advisor and advanced visualization functionality
</li>
<li>Lead, manage, complete, and delegate tasks specific to the clients’ scope of work to ensure each client is served with excellence and sees the value being delivered.
</li>
<li>Manage the client contract, including contract renewal, and sales of additional services.
</li>
<li>Coordinates with other departments for sale and delivery of further services
</li>
</ul>

<p><b>Essential skills and qualifications:
