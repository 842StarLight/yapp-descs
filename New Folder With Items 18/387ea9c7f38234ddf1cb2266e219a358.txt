<p><b>About the Position</b></p>
<p>The Accounting Manager is responsible for overseeing the day-to-day accounting processes in coordination and support of the CFO, and plays a key role in developing and implementing financial procedures for the financial health of the company. The Accounting Manager will be a key person on the team to take the Parker Clay brand to the highest levels of performance, establishing it as a premium high-end leather goods company while creating sustainable impact through the financial operations of the company.</p>
