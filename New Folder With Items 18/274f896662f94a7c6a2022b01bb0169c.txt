<h2>Marketing Program Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">Projectline is looking to add a Program Manager to work with one of our largest tech industry clients! Do you want to love where you work? Are you a positive, tenacious and organized person? Would you describe yourself as motivated and a can-do problem solver, with a knack for solving technical issues? If so, this is an excellent opportunity to help our large technology client drive global adoption of one of their core products across their enterprise customer base.
<br><br>
This contract position will work with our clients own field sales and marketing teams to drive increased adoption and usage of their software productivity suite with their end users. This will require the development of subject matter expertise in our clients business and their software solutions, as well as relationship development to build trust over time. The right consultant will bring their problem-solving skills with the ability to sort out high-level technical issues and engage the right team members to resolve issues. This consultant will also possess the innate desire to dive deep into the details to own execution and results.
<br><br>
<b>Responsibilities:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>You will support customer relationships and communication, maintaining a high level of transparency and accountability.</li>
<li>You will align with key partners on needs, timing and goals for content, and ensure that partners can access content and receive notice for updates.</li>
<li>You will customize content and create new assets clearly and concisely based on program needs and optimizations.</li>
<li>You will build and maintain work-back schedules to handle project deadlines and ensure timely delivery while maintaining high quality of program deliverables.</li>
<li>You will collaborate with internal and external teams for program planning and execution.</li>
<li>You will engage various internal partners to help clear technical blockers</li>
<li>You will use various systems and tools to plan, execute, and report on the program.</li>
<li>You will utilize data to drive program innovation, actively making recommendations to key partners.</li>
<li>You will lead overall program Rhythms of Business (ROB), internal comms, and documentation across several channels, including SharePoint and other project management tools.</li>
<li>You will work collaboratively with internal teams to define, detail and implement process workflows.</li>
<li>You will identify and drive content optimizations to improve the efficiency of marketing campaign execution and delivery.</li>
<li>Build and handle extensive project documentation on processes, workflows and communication standards.</li>
</ul>
<b>Qualifications and requirements:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>5+ years of experience in Program Management</li>
<li>Experience working with teams to resolve technical issues required</li>
<li>Bachelors Degree or equivalent professional training in marketing, operations, or related business experience.</li>
<li>2+ years of experience interacting with sales in a fast-paced environment</li>
<li>Highly proficient in PowerPoint (required); additional design tool experience a plus</li>
<li>Proficient in MS Office, with solid Excel capabilities</li>
<li>Strong presentation skills</li>
<li>Previous Microsoft experience in a consulting role or employee preferred but not required</li>
</ul>
