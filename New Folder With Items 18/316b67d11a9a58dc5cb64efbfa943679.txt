<h2>Pricing Analyst</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div><div><div>
<div><div>PRICE ENTRY AND ANALYSIS<br>
<ul>
<li>
Under the guidance of the Manager, Pricing &amp; Product Supply, input order pricing adjustments, maintain customer master data, produce monthly, quarterly and yearly reporting.</li>
<li>
Lead or participate in business and financial analyses for sales, production, supply chain, strategy and projects.</li>
</ul>
<br>
BUSINESS ANALYTICS - MARGIN CONTROL<br>
<ul>
<li>
Analyze and monitor the supply costs, the production costs, determine and follow up the product profitability as well as customer profitability.</li>
<li>
This function is run in coordination and with strong relationship with sales, the manufacturing sites, supply organization, and business organization.</li>
<li>
Propose corrective actions in case of margin drift.</li>
</ul>
<br>
PRICE LIST CREATION, MIGRATION &amp; MAINTENANCE<br>
<ul>
<li>
Prepare the relevant data necessary to create updated customer price lists. Lead or participate in migration of updated customer price lists. Perform quality analysis and maintenance of price lists.</li>
<li>
Under the supervision of the Manager, Pricing and Product Supply, provide analysis and other input for TOTAL Distributor Programs including incentives and rebates.</li>
<li>
The incumbent must have the necessary general knowledge of effective pricing and controlling techniques and systems, and also must be capable of acquiring the specific knowledge and insight regarding the company’s commercial and logistical operations, marketing efforts and information systems in order to meet the challenges of the duties mentioned above.
</li>
</ul>
</div></div>
<div>
<p><b>Context and environment</b></p>
<br>
<div>
<br>
The successful candidate should be self-motivated, analytical, results focused, relationship oriented, resolute, possess good judgment, and enjoy challenges. The ideal candidate should possess:<br>
<ul>
<li>
Strong figures skill and understanding of the key pricing drivers.
</li>
<li>Will interact with all facets of the organization including Business Unit Managers, Sales &amp; Marketing Managers, Production &amp; Logistics Managers, as well as the other Finance staff &amp; Managers.</li>
<li>
Develop, implement and maintain customer price lists based on market competition and positioning.
</li>
<li>Produce reporting on sales &amp; margin capture and distributor programs; contribute to other integral projects as deemed necessary.</li>
</ul>
<br>
