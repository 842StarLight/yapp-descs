<h2>IT Operations Support Analyst</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>Sealed Air partners with customers to solve their most critical packaging challenges with innovative solutions that leave our world, environment, and communities better than we found them. Our portfolio of widely recognized brands includes Cryovac® food packaging and Bubble Wrap® protective packaging which respectively enable a safer, more efficient food supply chain and protect valuable goods shipped around the world. Sealed Air generated $4.7 billion in sales in 2018 and has approximately 15,500 employees who serve customers in 123 countries. To learn more, visit www.sealedair.com.</div>

<b>
Summary</b>
</div>
<div>
<div>As an IT&amp;D Operations Support Analyst, you will work in a team that manages our global core IT platforms. The primary role of the Analyst is to run, maintain, and support our global IT Infrastructure system such as Windows Servers, Cisco Networks , Data Center activities etc. The continuous change and fast paced environment of our team requires this person to be eager to learn, innovative, ensure best practices are implemented, and results-driven.</div>
</div>
</div>
<div>
<div>
<b>
Job Description</b>
</div>
<div><ul>
<li>Strong experience and knowledge in working with enterprise platforms such as VMWare ESX, MS Windows Server, Cisco Routers and Switches</li>
<li>Experience with various technologies such as backup, storage, and Hyper-Converged Infrastructure</li>
<li>Minimum 2 years support experience in an operations team environment</li>
<li>Strong troubleshooting skills in Network and Windows Server environments</li>
<li>Understanding of general IT Sysadmin tasks</li>
<li>Ability to lead and contribute to the development, maintenance, and usage of supported platforms</li>
<li>Ability to work directly with higher level teams to debug problems and solutions</li>
</ul></div>
</div>
<div>
<div>
<b>Qualifications</b>
</div>
<div><ul>
<li>Day to day administration of Sealed Air’s various Enterprise Infrastructure environments</li>
<li>Collaborating with L3 teams to understand strategies and roadmap items for Sealed Air’s global Infrastructure</li>
<li>Collaborate with Sealed Air’s Security and Operation teams to ensure our Infrastructure environments are secure</li>
<li>Review and assist management and other departments with capacity planning</li>
<li>Create and maintain documentation related to supporting SEE IT Infrastructure</li>
<li>Train our Command Center to do day-to-day support and monitoring</li>
<li>Ensure the infrastructure is continuously up, operational, and stays stable</li>
<li>Work with others on the team to help overcome and solve difficult problems</li>
<li>Present to management areas of opportunity and improvements to ensure optimal benefits of environment</li>
<li>Other duties as assigned</li>
