<b>General Purpose:</b>
<br>
<br>
This position is responsible for managing the sales operations of the west region, while effectively increasing revenue. They are responsible for developing and implementing sales strategies including training, pipeline, and forecasting. Partner closely with internal and external strategic partners to achieve corporate forecast and revenue goals.
<br>
<br>
<b>Essential Duties and Responsibilities:</b>
<ul>
<li>Oversight of the sales team operations; responsible for the assessment of sales strategies, processes, training, lead generation, and all related pipelining activity</li>
<li>
Review, adjust and manage objectives and goals for the team based on key metrics that will ensure business strategy and revenue objectives are met</li>
<li>
Periodically review individual assignments, goals/quotas and ensure the compensation plan drives desired behaviors and outputs</li>
<li>
Evaluate people, processes and systems regularly making necessary adjustments to improve individual and overall team performance</li>
<li>
Work closely with other managers and teams to ensure a cohesive strategy is executed and overall corporate goals are achieved</li>
<li>
Team leadership responsibilities which include:
<ul>
<li>Assist in hiring and on-boarding new team members</li>
<li>
Ensuring new team members are properly and continuously trained</li>
<li>
Coaching low performers for improvement</li>
</ul>
</li>
</ul>
<b>Education, Experience and Skills:</b>
<ul>
<li>In-depth knowledge of Salesforce.com</li>
<li>
Track record of sales operations in a high growth environment</li>
<li>
Meaningful experience in software company, high tech experience desired; software preferred especially SAAS</li>
<li>
Strength in direct operations and roles of responsibility for customer experience</li>
<li>
Good recruiter, track record of hiring, training, coaching and leading a team</li>
<li>
Proactive leader able to anticipate issues and take actions to avoid them</li>
<li>
A minimum of 7 years of experience in a senior-level sales position in the finance/treasury industry</li>
<li>
Ability to motivate teams to produce results with integrity within tight timeframes and simultaneously manage several projects</li>
<li>
Experience in strategic planning and execution</li>
<li>
Knowledge of treasury management</li>

Knowledge of structuring sales quota goals and revenue expectation</li>
<li>
Willingness to work a flexible schedule; travel required</li>
<li>
Excellent organizational and interpersonal skills required</li>
</ul>
