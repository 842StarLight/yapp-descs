<div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>About the job</b></h2>
<p>
Google Cloud teams work with schools, companies, and government agencies to make them more productive, mobile, and collaborative. On this team, you will drive Google Cloud business relationships by analyzing partner business performance, identifying methods of increasing partner and Google returns, pitching ideas to and identifying opportunities within partners, and coordinating across Google teams to deliver.</p>
<p>As part of the Google Cloud team, the Go-to-Market (GTM) Partner team is shaping the future of how technology is used in the workplace. As a Partner Sales Manager, you will be the lead for all partner sales activities in your Vertical Named Account territory, focusing on growing the practices of partners who specialize in the Google Cloud businesses (e.g., Google Cloud Platform, Machine Learning, Maps, and G Suite).
</p>
<p>In this role, you will create and maintain a Sales strategy within named accounts and vertical markets to align external partners with our Sales strategy. Additionally, you will create and manage executive relationships with existing and new partners, developing close relationships with our Business team, and offering strategic advice, thought leadership, and day-to-day management in order to grow the business.</p>
<p>
Google Cloud helps millions of employees and organizations empower their employees, serve their customers, and build what’s next for their business — all with technology built in the cloud. Our products are engineered for security, reliability and scalability, running the full stack from infrastructure to applications to devices and hardware. And our teams are dedicated to helping our customers and developers see the benefits of our technology come to life.</p>

<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Responsibilities</b></h2>
<ul>
<li>Advocate for Google Cloud’s partner ecosystem and keep Sales teams and leadership apprised of the solutions and skills across our partners.</li>
<li>Define customer-centric and industry-specific use cases for Google to target via service partners. Build an ecosystem of service partners for vertical market and named account sales strategy.</li>
<li>Shape Google’s approach to partnership strategy with stakeholders in Partner Programs, Product Management, Engineering, Business, and Marketing. Support engagement with strategic global and regional partners across Vertical Named Accounts.</li>
<li>Align to the vertical named account Americas Sales territories and be act as a guide to identify the right partner and right fit for all engagements.</li>
<li>Ensure goals are met by aligning, managing, and holding partners accountable in engagements. Manage partners success and increase Google Cloud Platform (GCP) engagement.</li>
</ul>
