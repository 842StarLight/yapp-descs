<div>
<div>
Job Summary: Serves as Lead Client Services Technician, with responsibility for, and providing tasking oversight for, providing procurement, configuration, implementation, troubleshooting, and overall support for end user devices and other related equipment. Devices include computing and mobile devices of varying operating systems, peripherals, point-of-sale/service devices, etc.<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>Duties and Responsibilities: Incumbents of this position must be U.S. Citizens.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<ul>
<li>Leads and performs the full spectrum of end user computer (ie PC desktop/laptop, MacBook, iMac) and mobile (ie Blackberry, iPhone, tablet) support to include procurement, configuration, implementation, troubleshooting, and overall support for end user devices and other related equipment.</li>
<li>Performs Lead duties in the review of open trouble tickets, project tasking, supervisor input, and daily workload and assigns appropriate tasks to subordinate team. Communicates with supervisor on task status, new tasking, or issues.</li>
<li>Leads the installation of end user device software to include operating system, and works closely with Client Services Engineering staff and management when issues with the image process, software installation or application support needs arise</li>
<li>Leads the integration of hardware, software and end-user applications.</li>
<li>Leads system integration of hardware and/or software.</li>
</ul>
<div>
 - Serves as primary customer service liaison to NEXCOM end-users. - Respond to Level II Helpdesk requests.</div>
<div></div>
<ul><li>
<br>
Leads analytical, technical, and administrative support in the design, installation, and maintenance of new and existing end user computer and mobile systems, including network and e-mail operations.</li></ul>
<div></div>
<ul><li>
<br>
Responsible for collecting complete IT requirements for NEX initiatives including, but not limited to, numbers of end user computers / mobile devices and placement, number of Point of Service registers and placement, number of printers and placement.</li></ul>
<div></div>
<ul><li>
<br>
Coordinates with responsible personnel and other NEXCOM functional codes to ensure and verify that all required infrastructure is in place and tested prior to turnover to user.</li></ul>
<div></div>
<ul><li>
<br>
Troubleshoots end user computer / mobile device problems and determines appropriate course of action to return to operational use. Rectifies problems through appropriate adjustment to equipment and/or configuration. Refers problems beyond his/her ability to manufacturer's representative for resolution. Follows up with users and vendors to ensure problem is resolved.</li></ul>
<div></div>
<div>
<br>
 - Ensures site survey information and documentation is provided to vendors after reconciliation. - Tests new applications utilizing application analysis tools.</div>
<div></div>
<ul><li>
<br>
Leads end user device software training for NEXCOM associates.</li></ul>
<div></div>
<ul><li>
<br>
Prepares purchase requests for software and hardware utilizing automated procurement system, and provides central point to manage assigned equipment purchases, and logistics tracking.</li></ul>
<div></div>
<ul>
<li>
<br>
Ensure/implement the rigorous application of Information Security/Information Assurance policies, principles, and practices in the delivery of Systems, Applications and/or Services (Hardware &amp; Software).</li>
<li>Responsible for IT asset management, reporting and recording all IT assets for all completed IT assignments.</li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
Perform other related duties as assigned. Must possess valid driver's license to support off site facilities. Required to respond to calls 24x7x365 (On-Call).</div>
