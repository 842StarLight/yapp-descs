<h2>Product Implementation Analyst III</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<div>
<p>JOB PURPOSE: This position is responsible for managing the implementation of new or renewing accounts, new legislation, and products across the enterprise. Works with cross functional HCSC teams/internal stakeholders to lead the implementation. Responsible for communications and implementation artifacts that describe progress, issues, and risks surrounding the implementation. Responsible for managing implementation project activities of moderate to high complexity. Gathering, organizing, analyzing, and interpreting marketing, legislative, financial, and operating data. Keeps informed of best practices and strategic trends in product lifecycle management, sales and account implementation, and legislative developments. Works to cultivate partnerships with other HCSC business areas.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
REQUIRED JOB QUALIFICATIONS:</p>
<ul><li>Bachelor Degree with 3 years marketing, marketing support, health insurance, strategic consulting, project management or product management experience OR Technical Certification or College Courses with 4 years marketing, marketing support, health insurance, strategic consulting, project management or product management experience OR 5 years marketing, marketing support, health insurance, strategic consulting, project management or product management experience.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Knowledge of national account contract specifications.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Knowledge of corporate marketing practices, product lines, and claims systems.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Programming and report generation skills.</li></ul>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
PREFERRED JOB QUALIFICATIONS:</p>
<ul><li>Experience in marketing, marketing support, project management or product &amp; account operations.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Knowledge of health care regulatory and legislative process.</li></ul>
