 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">

<div><div><div><div>Harvard Business Publishing (HBP) is an idea-driven company with a commitment to improving the practice of management. We're a wholly owned subsidiary of Harvard University serving customers across three primary markets: educational institutions, corporations, and individual managers.<br>
HBP partners with corporate clients to understand their strategic learning objectives and to build customized solutions. Our portfolio includes Harvard ManageMentor, an online program for fostering management skills; the Leadership Direct program for emerging leaders; and the Case In Point collection for middle and senior-level managers.: harvardbusiness.org<br>
<br>
<b>The Job:</b><br>
HBP is actively looking for a Marketing Director of Demand Generation to support the overall growth objectives for Corporate Learning (CL). This role will be responsible for developing and leading our global and regional lead generation programs, and partnering on retention and enrichment efforts. The CL Market Unit operates against a targeted named account strategy focused against Global 2000 companies that are committed to Learning and Development. CL requires the demand generation role to balance broader top of the funnel lead generation with one to one account-based marketing approaches that will be relevant to senior leadership in L&amp;D roles.<br>
<br>
<b>Responsibilities:</b>
<ul>
<li>Take the lead on CL’s targeting, customer segmentation and tiering efforts, working with our internal Analytics team and collaborating with 3rd parties to leverage predictive tools to enhance lead generation efforts and align with Sales to define and prioritize the right accounts to jointly pursue.</li>
<li>
Responsible for establishing, tracking and optimizing the lead to revenue process with Sales and establishing key marketing and sales operations, processes, and workflows. Work with sales leadership to develop lead management within Salesforce to enable tracking pipeline activities from MQL to close.
</li>
<li>Plan and execute end-to-end lead generation programs, which include, but are not limited to webinars, emails, nurtures, events etc.to drive enterprise MQLs and sales pipeline, while closely monitoring conversion rates and optimizing campaigns.</li>
<li>
Demonstrate expertise in targeting, campaigns development and messaging to deliver pipeline and extend relationships at C-level, VP or enterprise networking audiences in existing or new logos.
</li>
<li>Own CL’s marketing automation process and marketing technology stack. Have a strong command of Eloqua or other leading marketing automation software and linkages to Salesforce to develop lead to revenue metrics and monitoring.</li>
<li>
Test and scale one-to-one personalized account-based marketing plans and initiatives alongside the sales team that allow HBP CL to target and influence relationships and purchasing decisions.</li>
<li>
Manage agency and contractor relationships, expectations, and budgets. Leverage agencies where necessary to ensure quality execution and performance for multiple key campaign development and outbound programs.</li>
<li>
Strong project management skills and ability to manage projects on budget with a controlled set of resources.</li>
<li>
Lead bi-weekly, monthly and quarterly reporting against KPIs, recommend and refine execution and pipeline progression. Utilize industry-leading demand generation best practices to execute, track, evaluate and report the impact of marketing.</li>
</ul>

<ul>
<li>8+ years’ experience creating and managing go-to-market, campaigns or demand generation in a medium to large size SAAS and Technology companies, with experience in consultative selling organizations</li>
<li>
Bachelor’s Degree in business or marketing preferred; MBA a plus</li>
<li>
B2B experience preferred</li>
<li>
Proven ability to work effectively in a matrix management environment, building and leading strong direct and virtual teams.</li>
<li>
Results-driven, Self-motivated, organized, proactive, collaborative.</li>
<li>
Knowledge of marketing automation and CRM system – experience with Eloqua or similar leading marketing automation tools</li>
<li>
Experience and demonstrated knowledge working with Account Based Marketing agencies and vendors to build a world-class program preferred</li>
<li>
Ability to travel up to 25% of the time.
</li>
</ul>
