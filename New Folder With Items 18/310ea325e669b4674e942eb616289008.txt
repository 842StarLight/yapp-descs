<div>Jekyll Island is dedicated to maintaining the delicate balance between nature and humankind through conservation, education, and preservation. Surrounded by salt marshes, expansive oceanfront, and ancient maritime forests, it is situated within the chain of the Golden Isles, and just a few miles from St. Simons Island, Sea Island and Brunswick. As a State Park, Jekyll Island is a coastal haven where nature and humans peacefully co-exist.</div>
<div></div>
<div><b>Job summary:</b></div>
<div>Under general supervision, the Assistant Golf Professional assists the professional staff in daily operations of the golf facility by providing a quality member and guest experience. Responsibilities include opening and closing the pro shop, completing various reporting functions, and arranging individual, group, and tournament tee times. The Assistant Golf Professional will provide golf lessons to individuals and groups as requested.</div>
<div></div>
<div><b>Job duties:</b></div>
<div>
<ul>
<li>Performs opening and closing of pro shop, operates and reconciles Point of Sale System, prepares and makes deposits, completes daily reports, and provides basic pro shop security and cleanliness.</li>
<li>Provides general information to customers relating to tee times, upcoming tournaments, etc.</li>
<li>Works with professional staff, and outside staff to ensure a quality member and guest experience on golf course and driving range.</li>
<li>Assists professional staff in tournament preparation and operations.</li>
<li>Instructs individual and group lessons as requested.</li>
<li>Assists merchandise manager in daily golf shop operations.</li>
<li>Assists ladies’ and men’s golf associations with home and away outings and tournaments. Serves as point of contact for Jekyll Island Men’s Golf Association.</li>
<li>Oversees junior golf programming. To include, summer camps, PGA Junior League, and coordination with First Tee of The Golden Isles.</li>
<li>Performs club repair as necessary.</li>
<li>Assists with the preparation of work schedules outside staff.</li>
<li>Promotes and participates in training activities to optimize customer service skills of self and staff.</li>
<li>Performs other duties as assigned.</li>
</ul>
<div><b>Qualifications:</b></div>
<div>
<ul>
<li>High school diploma or equivalent required.</li>
<li>College Degree Preferred</li>
<li>Application as an Associate PGA Professional Golf Management (PGA PGM) Program when employed.</li>
<li>Must progress through the PGA PGM Program following the acceptable progress guidelines of the PGA.</li>
<li>Minimum of one year golf course operations experience (or related);</li>
<li>In addition to PGA requirement, may consider any equivalent combination of education, training, and experience that provides the requisite knowledge, skills and abilities for this position.</li>
<li>Must possess basic computer knowledge and ability to operate Point of Sale Systems and Tournament Software.</li>
<li>Flexible availability required, to include weekends, holidays and early evenings.</li>
<li>Regular and predictable attendance and punctuality is required.</li>
</ul>
<div><b>Physical requirements:</b></div>
<div><ul>
<li>Exert up to 20 pounds of force occasionally and negligible amount of force constantly to move objects.</li>
<li>More than 50% of the typical work day will be spent standing, walking and twisting as well as repetitive motions of the wrists, hands and fingers.</li>
<li>Between 25% and 50% of the typical work day may be spent climbing stairs, pushing and pulling.</li>
<li>Less than 25% of the typical day is spent balancing, stooping and/or crouching, kneeling, reaching.</li>
<li>Sensory abilities required are talking and hearing and communicating.</li>
<li>Visual abilities to perform tasks required are preparing and analyzing data and figures, transcribing, use of the computer, expansive reading</li>
</ul></div>
<div><b>Working conditions:</b></div>
<div><ul>
<li>Work is conducted within an inside, office setting and outside with exposure to extreme heat, high humidity and occasionally adverse weather conditions as well occasional extreme cold.</li>
<li>Will be exposed to hazards such as moving mechanical parts and moving vehicles.</li>
<li>May be subject to atmospheric conditions such as fumes, oils, odors, dusts, mists, or poor ventilation.</li>
<li>May be subject to close quarters, such as narrow aisles or passageways/hallways.</li>
<li>Noise levels are generally considered normal.</li>
</ul></div>
