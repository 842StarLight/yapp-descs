<p><b>Essential Duties and Responsibilities (Other duties may be assigned.)</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Performs functions to obtain sales, secure new business accounts, and service the needs of existing sales accounts within assigned territory.</li>
<li>Evaluate current account sales, trends, and spending to ensure proper execution in non-traditional channel.</li>
<li>Performs merchandising activities in accounts that would include stocking shelves, product rotation, proper pricing, stocking cold boxes, vending machines, and building displays.</li>
<li>Under general direction, solicits business by personally visiting clients, attempting to interest prospective buyers, and explaining the merit of product and its application.</li>
<li>Analyze the overall non-traditional business and solicit new business while maintaining and growing existing business. Familiarizes established accounts with new products and developments.</li>
<li>Write orders subject to approval by company and ensure compliance on all contract agreements and obligations.</li>
<li>Prepare and submit regular reports on activities, sales volume, and expenses.</li>
<li>Keeps information on competitive products, promotional matters, sales techniques, pricing, and marketing policies. Informs company of competitive activity and conditions that would affect company interests.</li>
<li>Prepares price quotations, delivery dates, and service obligations, in addition to, investigate and resolves customer complaints in accordance with company policy.</li>
<li>Work with sales representatives in accounts to develop positioned supplier relationships and strengthen market presence.</li>
</ul>
