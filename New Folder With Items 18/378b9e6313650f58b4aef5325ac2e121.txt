<h2>Spectrum Store Greeter (Part-Time)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<b>JOB SUMMARY</b><br>
The <b>Spectrum Store Greeter</b> will actively and consistently support all efforts to enhance the customer experience within the <b>Spectrum </b>Retail Store environment by being the first point of contact for customers, providing a welcoming presence as customers enter the premises and displaying a positive attitude.<br>
<br>
<b>MAJOR DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES OF THE POSITION</b><ul>
<li>
Responsible for providing prompt and courteous customer service within the <b>Spectrum </b>Retail Store, while supporting other Store employees and Management</li>
<li>
Greet all customers in a friendly, professional and timely manner to ensure every customer feels welcomed and appreciated</li>
<li>
Manage customer traffic to afford customers the opportunity to browse the store without losing their service position</li>
<li>
Direct customers to <b>Spectrum Retail Store Associates</b><b>and Specialists </b>as availability and needs dictate</li>
<li>
Promote all <b>Charter</b> products and services with each customer interaction. Educate and engage customers through product demonstrations as appropriate and respond to customer questions as needed</li>
<li>
Provide support to Store Associates with sales related administrative duties, including assisting with inventory counts, maintaining store appearance and work stations</li>
<li>
Consistently demonstrate adherence to all Company and Store policies and procedures, while maintaining the confidentiality of customer and business data at all times</li>
<li>
Actively engage in training programs and demonstrate associated skills and competencies</li>
<li>
Perform other duties as may be requested by management</li>
</ul>
<b>
REQUIRED QUALIFICATIONS</b><ul>
<li>
Ability to read, write and speak the English language to communicate with other employees and customers in a clear, straight-forward, and professional manner</li>
<li>
Detail oriented and a good problem solver</li>
<li>
Possess a self-motivated, competitive spirit and desire to provide a good sales and customer service experience</li>
<li>
Positive and professional demeanor</li>
<li>
Flexibility to work in other locations as the needs of the business dictate</li>
<li>
Basic math skills</li>
<li>
Knowledge and ability to use computer and software applications</li>
<li>
Ability to multi-task in a fast-paced team environment</li>
</ul>
<b>
PREFERRED QUALIFICATIONS</b><br>
Current knowledge of latest technology and devices<br>
<br>
<b>Education</b><br>
High School Diploma or equivalent required<br>
<br>
<b>Related Work Experience</b><br>
Sales experience: 1 yr.<br>
Customer Service experience: 3+ yrs.<br>
<br>
<b>WORKING CONDITIONS</b><ul>
<li>
Retail environment</li>
<li>
Exposure to moderate noise level</li>
<li>
Work scheduled shift hours and overtime as needed</li>
<li>
Ability to handle a physically demanding job, often times lifting up to 35 lbs.</li>
<li>
Ability to stand for prolonged periods of time</li>
<li>
Required to wear a uniform</li>
</ul>
