<h2>Account Executive - Security & Monitoring Solutions</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>SEA is a global leader in security, monitoring and automation software and is looking for an Account Executive to sell and market SEA’s industry leading automation &amp; security software products to enterprises in North America. SEA’s customers include 85% of the Fortune 500 and leaders in all industries including, financial services, retail, healthcare, banking, technology, manufacturing, and telecom.</p>
<p>SEA is looking for an account executive with at least 4 years of selling experience, and at least 2 years of experience selling security (technology) software or services to companies. As a SEA Account Executive, you will be responsible for selling new accounts and up-selling existing accounts for SEA’s security solutions. SEA will provide you with full training on our products and services. The Account Executive will be responsible for coordinating and managing the sale from start to close and will work directly with SEA pre-sales and support engineers to deliver solutions to secure corporate IT environments, monitor system up-time and meet audit and compliance requirements.</p>
<p>This is a great opportunity to join a team of extremely successful, seasoned sales professionals and deliver world class software and services.</p>
<p>This position is based in Garden City, New York.</p>
<p><b>Responsibilities Include: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Identifying and prospecting to target companies in North America</li>
<li>Managing the entire sales cycle from product introduction to close</li>
<li>Using social media to influence prospects and target companies about SEA solutions.</li>
<li>Being knowledgeable about SEA products, security solutions and services and being able to drive a consultative, value based sales approach.</li>
<li>Working with SEA pre-sales and technical support engineers to deliver software solutions</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Required Skills: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>4-7 years prior sales or customer facing experience in a B2B sales environment.</li>
<li>At least 2 years of prior experience sales experience in a security related technology field. The field does not have to be related to SEA’s technologies.</li>
<li>Knowledge of security technology needs, regulations, and compliance requirements.</li>
<li>Experience building relationships through a consultative sales approach.</li>
<li>Excellent verbal and non-verbal communication skills</li>
<li>Team player with a positive attitude, initiative and personal accountability</li>
<li>Comfortable on the Phone</li>
<li>Demonstrate a high-level of self-motivation, commitment and activity to attain corporate and personal goals as well as the ability to excel in professional environments.</li>
<li>Must be trainable</li>
<li>Basic computer proficiency and have the ability to understand technology.</li>
</ul>
<p>Salary + sales commissions + Benefits.</p>
