<h2>Outside Sales Representative</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>Job DescriptionEdit</div>
<br>
<div><div>A Sales Representative is an entry-level position. At minimum, they are responsible for securing and retaining profitable business for the company. The individual typically works under direct supervision and expected to work long hours, and subject to working weekends and holidays. This role is also responsible for running the day-to-day marketing activities of the Company, including outside public relations and advertising initiatives in support of the marketing programs.
<br>
<br>
Major Responsibilities – Sales
<ul>
<li>Increase profitable sales and rental revenue in the assigned territory through scheduled and cold sales calls, email, direct mailings, and various marketing methods used to promote the Sterett brand.</li>
<li>Offer a full inventory of equipment and services to new and current customers including the sale and rental of equipment.</li>
<li>Coordinating with company personnel to ensure customer satisfaction through timely and accurate execution of paperwork, quotes, and various correspondences.</li>
<li>Daily input of account information into iCraneTrax.</li>
<li>Consistent and professional follow up and communication through email, phone, and personal interaction either at customer offices or jobsites.</li>
<li>Assist in collecting on past due accounts if necessary.</li>
<li>Perform at level 3 (Meets Requirements) or better on all performance evaluation.</li>
</ul>Major Responsibilities – Marketing
<ul>
<li>Develop sales material, case studies, presentations, and other customer facing materials for both digital and print. Write and edit copy to ensure effective messaging. Distribute and control corporate branding standards.</li>
<li>Provide in-house marketing services to design and develop appropriate advertising content for placement in a variety of client facing channels.</li>
<li>Develop internal marketing communications plan (newsletter, sales training, market news, success stories.</li>
<li>Work with sales, human resources and the outside public relations agency to manage web site content and digital collateral.</li>
</ul>
<b>Key Objectives</b>
<ul>
<li>Employee will be honest and open with management at all times.</li>
<li>Maintain excellent communication skills both verbal and written in company communication and customer interaction.</li>
<li>Maintain thorough customer records, quotes, industry and territory information and be able to report back to management periodically.</li>
<li>Exhibit an attitude that is focused on developing, nurturing and maintaining long term relationships.</li>
<li>Recognize the team concept within the company and work efficiently through other team members to achieve the common company goals.</li>
<li>Maintain vehicle in appearance and operation while maintaining a clean driving record.</li>
<li>Protect and safeguard all company property, i.e. computers, phones, air cards, fuel cards, etc.</li>
<li>Employee is expected to conduct him/herself in a professional and courteous manner at all times. This includes company functions both onsite and offsite</li>
</ul>
<b>Knowledge, Skills, and Experience</b>
<br>
<br>
<b><i>Essential:</i></b>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree in Marketing, Journalism, Communications, or related field</li>
<li>Minimum of 2 -5 years of sales of equipment rentals</li>
<li>Thorough understanding of all marketing functions;</li>
<li>Must have strong oral, presentation and written communication skills;</li>
<li>Must be able to pass a drug test, background check, and fit for duty test.</li>
</ul>
<b><i>Desired:</i></b>
<ul><li>Experience working in like industry</li></ul>
