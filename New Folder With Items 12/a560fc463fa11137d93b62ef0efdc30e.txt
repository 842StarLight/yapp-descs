<h2>Senior Datacenter Practice Director</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>
<b>JOB SUMMARY:</b><br>
The Sr. Practice Director works independently with little or no direct supervision under the guidance of the Sr. VP of Technology Solutions Group to strategically plan and deliver the growth and expansion of the attach rate of professional services. The Sr. Director utilizes independent judgment and discretion in selection, development, and coordination of methods within a broad framework of general policies. The Sr. Practice Director manages engagements and collaborates with all of Connection's sales subsidiaries in support of growing and expanding the attach rate, as well as increasing the size, scope and margins of the product only deal. As a leader, the Sr. Director manages a large technical team in terms of assignments and organizational alignment, sets performance objectives, creates development plans, and measures results. Utilizes coaching and motivational techniques to ensure satisfactory results and ensure continuous improvement of the assigned team. Builds relationships with and serves as a point of contact for customers, partners, and vendors for any opportunities or concerns. The Sr. Director personally acquires and maintains relevant certifications, and tracks the post-sales technical certifications and accreditation of the practice team in support of vendor program requirements, services enablement, and engineering competency.<br>
