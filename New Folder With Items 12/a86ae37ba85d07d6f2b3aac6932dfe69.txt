We are looking to hire a Request For Proposal (RFP) Coordinator to support the sales, solution consulting, and customer success teams in fulfilling inbound requests for information and documentation associated with competitive bids (e.g. RFI’s &amp; RFP’s), IT Security Questionnaires, Vendor Surveys and other requests from clients and prospective clients. This position can sit in either our Franklin, MA, Indianapolis, IN or Austin, TX offices.
<br><br>
Triage inbound requests, determine level of effort, critical path dependencies, resource requirements and priority in the queue.
<br>Create and manage folders, create and communicate response plan, assignments and timelines to request owner and response team.
<br>Works closely with the following cross functional teams to ensure that all responses, documents and knowledge base records incorporate and are compliant with:
<br><br>
Marketing’s branding and competitive positioning statements.
<br>IT Risk &amp; Compliance security policies and procedures.
<br>Human Resources and Legal policies and procedures.
<br>Technical Subject Matter Expert’s inputs, policies and procedures.
<br><br>
Organize and facilitate cross-function meetings to ensure timeliness in responses and proposals.
<br>Use workflow, knowledge base tools, and document libraries to create and package the response.
<br>Review response package and ensure accuracy / quality / adherence to guidelines.
<br>Submit completed responses to prospects and clients using email, online portals and file sharing tools.
<br><br>
Manage the competitive bid post mortem process, generate detailed win/loss analysis and distribute to executive team.
<br>Additional reporting / analysis as needed.
<br>Complete required training and compliance courses.
<br><br>

Bachelor’s Degree.
<br>2+ years of relevant experience.
<br>Proficient in Microsoft Office Suite (Word, PowerPoint, Excel) and Adobe Acrobat.
<br>Extremely organized and detailed oriented.
<br>Demonstrated capability to write clear, logical, and persuasive documents; both technical and non-technical in nature.
<br>Ability to manage, prioritize and execute on multiple objectives and projects.
<br>Ability to self-manage and self-teach while demonstrating an openness and willingness to learn, take direction and be flexible.
<br><br>
<b>Desired:</b><br>
Experience with Call Center operations.
<br>Experience supporting sales organizations.
<br>Basic knowledge of SaaS based software, artificial intelligence, information technology, and business operations .
<br>Experience working with G-Suite, Salesforce.com, RFP360, File Sharing Tools, Slack, Ariba or other online portals.</div>
