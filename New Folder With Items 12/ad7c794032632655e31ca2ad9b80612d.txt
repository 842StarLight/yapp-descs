<h2>PR Account Executive</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Crawford Strategy is a full-service marketing and public relations agency in Greenville, South Carolina. Teammembers should have a positive attitude, a strong sense of self-motivation with a desire to achieve and exceed expectations. Crawford’s core values of quality, responsibility, flexibility, respect and positivity are essential to both the Company’s and the team member’s success.</p>
<p>
QUALITY – Commitment to high standards and price of ownership.<br>
RESPONSIBILITY – Consistently reliable and accountable for work product.<br>
FLEXIBILITY – Open to new things and ideas – adventurous.<br>
RESPECT – Esteem and appreciation for each other, our clients and our community.<br>
ATTITUDE – Positive and supportive attitude.</p>
<p>
The PR Account Executive is responsible for execution of PR efforts in the day-to-day workflow of client accounts while developing the strategic skills required to advance to a more senior PR position. The PR Account Executive is responsible for providing updates on those efforts to both the Account Services team and the Manager of Public Relations, and in many cases the client. The PR Account Executive may lead efforts for a client or support a more senior PR team member on a larger account.</p>
