<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Job Summary:</b></h3>
<p>
As the foundation of our Sales teams and the link between Marketing and Sales you will be responsible for managing HighRadius’ global inbound leads. The successful Financial Technology Adviser (FTA) will be able to profile accounts, refine our message and build highly customized messages to penetrate new accounts with the goal of setting up qualified meetings for HighRadius teams.</p>
<p>
This role combines huge opportunities for growth into other teams within our HighRadius fast growing structure and top-notch sales and marketing training!</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Speak with active inbound leads and follow-up as required</li>
<li>Respond to and qualify incoming web and phone inquiries regarding HighRadius products</li>
<li>Manage prospects with the ability to articulate value proposition, navigate objections, and foster relationships</li>
<li>Work closely with the entire sales organization to accelerate the sales cycle and to extend our reach into target accounts</li>
<li>Collaborate with marketing team to ensure that inbound marketing campaigns are integrated with other marketing efforts ensuring a seamless user experience for customers</li>
<li>Develop and maintain marketing KPI reporting for all lead generation sources, ensuring comprehensive lead tracking through the sales funnel</li>
<li>Collaborate with sales development team to solicit feedback on lead quality and quantity and adjust inbound marketing efforts as necessary</li>
<li>Primary responsibility for following up on marketing leads and sales inquiries to qualify future opportunities.</li>
<li>Executing a high volume of phone calls, emails and follow-ups to develop pipeline opportunities account teams</li>
<li>Successful team members will learn about our client’s business financial supply chain (FSCM) processes and challenges; the HighRadius solutions, and; the HighRadius sales methodology for promotion to the Account Executive role.</li>
</ul>
