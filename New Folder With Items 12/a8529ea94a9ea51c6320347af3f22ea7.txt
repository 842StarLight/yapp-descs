<h2>Purchaser/Router</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Summary: </b> Review daily sales orders for short-ordered products and order inventory required for next-day’s business. Maintain inventory for low-par inventory items. Review next-day stops and route fleet vehicles for delivery within customer delivery windows. Participate in the warehouse management team – working to improve all warehouse operations, identifying efficiencies and creating solutions for problems.</p>
<p><b>Schedule: </b>**OVERNIGHT POSITION** - Sunday-Friday, 8:00pm-6:00am.</p>
<p><b>Key Responsibilities: </b></p>
<p><i>Shorts Management</i></p>
<ul>
<li>Review daily sales orders and identify short-ordered inventory items.</li>
<li>Order required product with appropriate vendors for delivery before next-day business. Create and send Purchase Orders for all ordered items.</li>
<li>Monitor low-par items and maintain correct inventory.</li>
</ul>
<p><i>Routing</i></p>
<ul>
<li>Identify all stops for next-day delivery.</li>
<li>Route stops (using routing software) for maximum efficiency and to meet all customer requests, specifically required delivery windows.</li>
</ul>
<p><i>Communication</i></p>
<ul>
<li>Communicate with all other members of the warehouse management team to ensure smooth daily operations.</li>
<li>Provide feedback to all supervised employees on a regular basis.</li>
<li>Review any issues or concerns with the Warehouse Manager.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Required Skills: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>
<i>Multitasking: </i> handle many concurrent, detailed-oriented tasks</li>
<li>
<i>Flexibility: </i>the ability to “go with the flow” as things change day-to-day while always focusing on accomplishing the task at hand</li>
<li>
<i>Teamwork: </i> work with your team to accomplish daily tasks and collaborate with other warehouse managers to improve all warehouse operations</li>
<li>
<i>Independence: </i> the ability to work on your own to accomplish each night’s tasks</li>
<li>
<i>Computer Skills: </i> good working knowledge of Microsoft Office and willingness to learn and master Edible Software (DelBene Produce’s inventory management and accounting software)</li>
</ul>
<p>Job Type: Full-time</p>
<p>Education:</p>
<ul><li>High school or equivalent (Required)</li></ul>
<p>Work Location:</p>
<ul>
<li>One location</li>
<li>On the road</li>
</ul>
<p>Paid Training:</p>
<ul><li>Yes</li></ul>
