<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>The Position
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div>The Appraisal Department Admin supports the Appraisal Department with the day-to-day execution of tasks required for the successful functioning/performance of the deptpartment. This position adheres to company, investor, industry and regulatory standards, and reports to the Appraisal Department Manager.</div>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<ul>
<li>Monitor incoming appraisal orders</li>
<li>
Check and confirm email notifications for borrower payments for appraisal orders</li>
<li>
Provide appraisal payment confirmations to loan staff</li>
<li>
Monitor system email messages for open and pending appraisal orders</li>
<li>
Perform follow up on open/overdue appraisal assignments</li>
<li>
Assist in responding to internal and external email appraisal inquiries</li>
<li>
Aid with any invoicing issues necessary for sales offices/appraisers</li>
<li>
Perform other duties and assist with special projects as needed</li>
</ul>
</div></div></div>
