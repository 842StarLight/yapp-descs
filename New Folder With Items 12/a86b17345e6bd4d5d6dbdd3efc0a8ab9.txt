<h2>Assistant Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p><b>Position Summary:</b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
Assists with all retail and production operations to achieve maximum sales potential. Perform all management tasks in the absence of the manager. Assists manager in planning and training. Presents a professional image to set an example for staff. Plays an active role on management team. Duties include, but are not limited to:</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Conducts regular staff meetings that improve communication with staff, such as daily tool box talks, monthly department meetings, and weekly management team meetings.</li>
<li>Develops a superior workforce (through the use of coaching conversations, development plans, and by taking action with any staff that are failing to meet expectations, as per HR direction)</li>
<li>Reviews production results on a daily, weekly, and monthly basis</li>
<li>Monitor staff productivity to established standards/goals and take appropriate actions to correct deficiencies.</li>
<li>The primary focus for Assistant Manager is in Production, however, must also be knowledgeable and proficient in Retail operations.</li>
<li>Assists manager in the consistent and accurate use of staffing model to schedule staff and to forecast, plan and track salary expenditures</li>
<li>Assists manager in the timely and accurate reporting (paperwork and electronic “forms”) including those related to HR, Safety/Asset Protection and Accounting</li>
<li>Responsible for store performance and meets budgets in the following areas: Revenue, payroll, controllable expenses</li>
<br>
</ul>
<br>
Required Skills<br>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Sensitive to guest needs and wishes</li>
<li>Ability to work effectively with people with disabilities or other special needs preferred</li>
<li>Must have computer experience and be competent in internet use and a variety of computer software applications.</li>
<li>Prefer profit and loss, sales, or cost management accountability experience.</li>
<li>Must have strong interpersonal, communication, monetary, organizational and decision making skills.</li>
<li>Must be able to read, write and speak the English language in order to communicate with staff and guests.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p>
</p>
<p><b>Physical Requirements</b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Ability to move independently or with reasonable accommodation within the facility and community.</li>
<li>Applicant must be physically and mentally able to perform all requirements of the job.</li>
<li>Able to lift and carry 20-100 pounds with team lifting or mechanical assistance.</li>
<li>Able to stand, stoop, bend, or carry for extended periods.</li>
<li>Able to go up and down steps.</li>
<li>Able to stand and walk entire shift.</li>
<li>Tolerance of extreme changes in temperature and humidity.</li>
<li>Must be able to work flexible hours, evenings, and weekends or longer shifts if needed.</li>
<li>May be required to work more than 40 hours per week.</li>
<li>Occasional overnight travel and weekend work required if applicable.</li>
<li>Must have reliable transportation, valid driver’s license and proof of personal vehicle insurance.</li>
<li>Applicant must pass background and DMV check.</li>
<li>Wage $15.00</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p>
</p>
<br>
<br>
Required Experience<br>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>0-2 years retail or comparable management experience</li>
<br>
</ul>
