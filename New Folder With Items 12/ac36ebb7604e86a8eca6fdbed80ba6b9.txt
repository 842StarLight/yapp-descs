<h2>Field Service Technician Production Equipment (Digital Service Engineer)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Overview
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>Works on Production equipment, including Canon and Oce industry-leading products. Provides operational, maintenance and networking support for customers through your extensive product and customer skills knowledge. Works closely with Field Service Managers, Branch Service Managers and other engineers to provide the high level technical support with seamless technical expertise of Canon Solutions America supported products in accordance with the current Service and Parts Standards.
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities
</b></h2>
<div><div>
<ul><li>Diagnoses mechanical, software, network and system failures, using established procedures.</li></ul>
<br>
<ul><li>Services and Repairs designated equipment, in an assigned territory, to Canon standards and specifications.</li></ul>
<br>
<ul><li>Prepares reports for analysis of product failure trends and service ability issues ensuring accurate information and record-keeping.</li></ul>
<br>
<ul><li>Complete working knowledge and aptitude of multiple product groups. This includes all aspects of troubleshooting and diagnostics. This level is the final comprehensive tier to include work flow analysis, customer relations and ultimately customer satisfaction and retention.</li></ul>
<br>
<ul><li>Meets customer demands by providing efficient, responsive and accurate flow of service information.</li></ul>
<br>
<ul><li>Maintains all technical information and Canon property assigned and provides direction to less senior engineers in same.</li></ul>
<br>
<ul><li>Provides the solutions of escalated technical and / or customer service related problem areas for any territory requested.
</li></ul>
</div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Qualifications
</b></h2>
<div><div>
<ul><li>Typically requires general education and/or vocational training plus 1 to 2 years of related experience.</li></ul>
<br>
<ul><li>Associates degree in electronics (or related area), technical school, or military experience a plus; high school diploma or equivalent.</li></ul>
<br>
<ul><li>Possess a basic understanding of internet environments.</li></ul>
<br>
<ul><li>May require some travel for training purposes; valid driver's license and acceptable driving record necessary.</li></ul>
<br>
<ul><li>Must be able to work in a 24x7 environment and support on-call rotations.</li></ul>
<br>
<ul><li>Must be able to lift 50 lbs and be in a mobile activity more than 50% of the time (walking, standing, stooping/kneeling).
</li></ul>
</div></div>
