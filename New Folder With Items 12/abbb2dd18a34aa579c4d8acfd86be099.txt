<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Job Requisition:</b></h2>
ServiceNow Senior Developer<p></p>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Job Description:</b></h2>
<p>
The ServiceNow Senior Developer will work closely with the Solutions Architect and Project Manager to support Leidos’ functional and business requirements for client engagements. The Senior Developer will define requirements, recommend improvements, and determine timing for implementation of system updates and deployment of new functionality. The Senior Developer will be adept at working in a matrix environment and collaborating with team members in a fast paced, growing ServiceNow practice. The successful candidate will also be skilled at the following:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Proactively researching and suggesting enhancements to existing engagements</li>
<li>
Detailed expert knowledge of JavaScript and ServiceNow-related JavaScript APIs</li>
<li>
Provide pre-sales technical guidance and support to proposal generation for new projects</li>
<li>
Ensuring the security architecture is consistent with federal standards and industry best practice</li>
<li>
Collaborating with the Solutions Architect to support ServiceNow out of box and customized solutions</li>
<li>
Synthesis with ITIL compliance and industry best practice process design</li>
<li>
Communication and coordination within a matrix organization to ensure on-time delivery of project requirements</li>
<li>
Working with multiple projects simultaneously and completing project activities in a way that minimizes project risks and issues in a client services organization.
</li>
<li>Completing timely reporting, KPIs, dashboards, project plans, and project documentation from a solutions architecture perspective</li>
<li>
Supporting and delivering training initiatives for end users</li>
<li>
Must have or be able to obtain a Public Trust Clearance.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
