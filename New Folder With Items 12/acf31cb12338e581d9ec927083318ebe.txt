<div><b>Opportunity</b></div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
</div>
<div>
Treasury Services is a leading provider of core cash management services to the healthcare segment. As we continue to expand our healthcare business, we have identified the need to provide more dedicated industry and solution support for large, strategic sales opportunities within CIB, along with focused attention for proof of concepts and new healthcare solutions launched across the firm.<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<div>This individual will work with Healthcare Payment and Product Executives in the support of strategic sales and product initiatives, as well as directly support sales efforts as needed to advance the objectives of the healthcare business. This includes, but is not limited to, providing sales support for the CIB healthcare sales team, in addition to providing support more broadly for strategic complex healthcare opportunities across the firm. As new solutions are piloted or launched, this individual will be responsible for supporting and monitoring early adopters, providing feedback, and assisting with training and messaging as needed to support the new solutions and expanded markets.<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<div>As necessary and as indicated through strategic growth initiatives, this Lead position will build out a strong team knowledgeable in the healthcare industry and with specific solutions supported by the team. The ISS team will serve as knowledgeable subject matter experts and contribute in meaningful ways to the growth of J.P. Morgan’s focused healthcare treasury business.<br>
</div>
<div></div>
