<h2>Staff Accountant</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Capstone Restaurant Group</b> is one of the largest franchisees of the Hardee’s restaurant system. We operate nearly 300 locations throughout the Midwest and Eastern United States. We are in search of a talented Staff Accountant to join our team based in Boulder, Colorado.</p>
<p>To learn more about Capstone Restaurant Group, go to https://capstonerestaurants.com/ . To learn more about the Hardee's and Carl’s Jr. Franchise, go to https://www.hardees.com/ or https://www.carlsjr.com.</p>
<p><b>SUMMARY OF POSITION: </b> The Staff Accountant is an integral part of the Accounting team. This position is responsible for critical accounting functions for multiple locations.</p>
<p><b>RESPONSIBILITIES: </b></p>
<p>· Analyze and prepare month end profit and loss reports</p>
<p>· Daily sales preparation and cash verification</p>
<p>· Balance general ledger accounts</p>
<p>· Reconcile Bank Accounts</p>
<p>· Monthly bonus calculation</p>
<p>· Sales, payroll, and monthly recurring journal entry preparation and posting</p>
<p>· American Express Expense Report Management.</p>
<p><b>OTHER DUTIES: </b> As assigned by management.</p>
<p><b>WHAT WE CAN OFFER: </b></p>
<p>· Competitive salary</p>
<p>· Health, Dental, Life, Disability and Vision Insurance</p>
<p>· Paid vacation</p>
<p>· Family atmosphere with a great work/life balance</p>
<p><b>EXPERIENCE: </b></p>
<p>· Associates Degree in Accounting/Finance preferred, but not required</p>
<p>· Working knowledge of Microsoft Office &amp; intermediate to advanced skills in Excel</p>
<p>· Excellent oral and written communication skills to effectively maintain customer service levels with the field</p>
<p>· Must have a positive attitude in a changing environment</p>
<p>· Familiarity with Great Plains Software a plus</p>
<p>· Demonstrated commitment to Accuracy</p>
<p><b>KNOWLEDGE, SKILLS, AND ABILITY: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Strong interpersonal and communications skills (written and verbal)</li>
<li>Ability to multi-task and support remote locations in the quick serve restaurant business</li>
<li>Demonstrated ability to build and maintain effective working relationships with internal customers and external partners</li>
<li>Proficiency with basic Computer skills (Word, Excel, Power Point, etc.)</li>
<li>Represents themselves with the highest level of integrity and professionalism. Maintains a Service-focused approach, and a caring and sincere attitude at all times.</li>
<li>Experience within the restaurant or related industry preferred</li>
<li>Ability to meet deadlines under pressure</li>
<li>Ability to learn new things quickly</li>
</ul>
