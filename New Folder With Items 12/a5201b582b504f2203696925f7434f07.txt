<h2>Associate Systems Engineer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Our Mission</b></p>
<p>
At Palo Alto Networks® everything starts and ends with our mission:</p>
<p>
Being the cybersecurity partner of choice, protecting our digital way of life.</p>
<p>
We have the vision of a world where each day is safer and more secure than the one before. These aren’t easy goals to accomplish – but we’re not here for easy. We’re here for better. We are a company built on the foundation of challenging and disrupting the way things are done, and we’re looking for innovators who are as committed to shaping the future of cybersecurity as we are.</p>
<p><b>
Your Career</b></p>
<p>
As an Associate Systems Engineer you will join an elite cohort of recent technical college graduates for a two-year intense immersion into cybersecurity, Palo Alto Networks technologies, automation, and on-the-job learning in 2-3 Systems Engineering teams. The program includes formal technical instruction with hands-on lab work, independent projects and technical study, and professional skills development.</p>
<p>
While you are quickly ramping on Palo Alto Networks technology you will be assigned to an SE team and will work alongside senior SEs completing automation projects and other critical work that supports customers in the presales process. In year two of the program, you will work with sales and channel partners on customer engagements and establish relationships with customers with the goal of helping them detect and prevent advanced cyberattacks and breaches.</p>
<p>
Upon completion of year two, you will have proven technical skills and professional skills and will be qualified to take on a field SE position in any number of cities across the US based on business needs. Our SEs are among the technically best-in-class in the cybersecurity industry, with a deep technical understanding of cybersecurity products, integrations, and the critical cyberthreats facing our potential customer’s environments.</p>
<p><b>
Your Impact</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Establish yourself as a trusted team member to SE leadership and colleagues and contribute to sales wins throughout the program</li>
<li>
Architect and propose solutions which address the identified cybersecurity problems in each customer’s unique environment</li>
<li>
Ensure ongoing customer happiness, support, and adoption of cybersecurity solutions</li>
<li>
Understand and effectively differentiate against our top competitors</li>
<li>
Identify and document specific problems with prospective and current customers which can be solved through the deployment of integrated solutions</li>
<li>
Act as the customer advocate for any issues that require technical assistance and follow up with the customer until the issue is resolved</li>
<li>
Continuous self-improvement and learning to maintain technical leadership of applicable technologies (data center, SDN, public cloud, security, networking, etc.)</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Your Experience</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
BS in Computer Science, technical cybersecurity or networking program</li>
<li>
Experience with systems installation, UNIX/Linux, and Windows-based systems</li>
<li>
Scripting (Ruby, Python preferred) and JavaScript programming experience</li>
<li>
Experience with cloud networking services such as AWS, Azure, GCP</li>
<li>
Understand and effectively present complex technical concepts to technical and non-technical audiences</li>
<li>
Strong analytical skills to evaluate complex problems and a systematic approach to create solutions</li>
<li>
Mature and effective time-management skills</li>
<li>
Knowledge and desire to learn of modern network threats and malware, network forensics, automation tools and technologies, and endpoint security technologies</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
The Team</b></p>

You won’t find someone at Palo Alto Networks that isn’t committed to your success – with everyone pitching in to assist when it comes to solutions selling, learning, and development. As a member of our systems engineering team, you are motivated by a solutions-focused sales environment and find fulfillment in working with clients to resolve incredible complex cyberthreats.</p>
