<h2>Technical Account Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<div>
<div>WalkMe is looking for a Technical Account Manager who will be a part of the team that scopes, designs, develops and deploys solutions for our customers using the WalkMe product.</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
The Technical Account Manager is a point of escalation for all technical matters that arise in the Professional Services department. This person becomes a trusted advisor to the customer by building a strong relationship through an in-depth understanding of customer goals and needs. The Technical Account Manager has a comprehensive view of the customer's product and support requests, understands customers’ business priorities and provides guidance on our products.</div>
</div>
<div><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>POSITION DETAILS:</b></h3>
<div><ul>
<li>
This role requires a natural excitement and ability to learn technologies and explain technical issues to a variety of audiences, including enterprise architects, developers and management.</li>
<li>
The candidate is expected to be a self-starter, results-oriented leader with solid technical skills, strong commercial skills, a super strong commitment to customer success and passion for providing world-class on-going service to customers.</li>
<li>
You must also enjoy collaborating with customers, Professional Services, Engineering, Customer Success Managers, and the Product Department.</li>
</ul></div>
</div></div>
<div><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>POSITION DELIVERABLES:</b></h3>
<div><ul>
<li>
Sync and coordinate between the customer, the Customer Success Manager and the R&amp;D Department on customer’s requests</li>
<li>
Convey business oriented requests to the R&amp;D Department</li>
<li>
Manage end to end technical solutions to customer requests: analyze, plan, approve, deliver and maintain</li>
<li>
Be the technical point of contact for the Success and Sales departments</li>
<li>
Continuously drive towards improving customer service and increasing customer efficiency</li>
<li>
Work closely with the Product department to recommend on product ideas based on analysis of customers’ requests</li>
<li>
Work closely with the Professional Services teams to be able to suggest creative out-of-the-box solutions to customers' needs</li>
</ul></div>
</div></div>
