<p><b>Summary of Key Responsibilities</b></p>
<p><b>Position Summary: </b></p>
<p>The IT Operations Professional is responsible for effective provisioning, installation, configuration, operation, and maintenance of building systems hardware and related infrastructure at assigned facility. This individual ensures that system hardware, and related procedures adhere to Smart City and building organizational values, enabling administrative staff, volunteers, and partners of the facility.</p>
<p>The IT Operations Professional position also requires voice and data network experience in wired and wireless networks and skills in installation and programming for complex services. This position will provision internet services, networking applications and infrastructure connectivity for all clients in the facility, including planning and coordinating use of resources at site and installing, repairing, monitoring, and programming all data/telephony products and services.</p>
<p><b>Essential Functions: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Develop and maintain installation and configuration procedures. Contributes to and maintains system standards. Research and recommend innovative, and where possible, automated approaches for system administration tasks. Identify approaches that leverage our resources and provide economies of scale.</li>
<li>Perform daily system monitoring verifying the integrity and availability of all hardware; systems and key processes; reviewing system and application logs; and verifying completion of scheduled jobs such as backups. Perform regular performance monitoring to identify any possible service issues.</li>
<li>Add new equipment, as acquired, and provide connectivity and configuration of said equipment. Provide daily help desk support.</li>
<li>Performs on-site installations and all functional client level voice and data services for permanent tenants and events. Possesses and applies in depth knowledge of the products and services at the assigned facility, including: voice(PBX/VoIP) and data network (wired/wireless) products, testing/troubleshooting procedures to the floor level, basic edge switch configurations (VLAN setup and ports assignments) and PBX/VoIP programming for set installations of assigned facility. Knowledgeable and utilizes standard testing procedures and all facility test tools and company proprietary software with high levels of proficiency</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Other Functions: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Attend pre-conference meetings.</li>
<li>Provide technical sales assistance to the overall sale of our products while assisting the Management team with technical expertise.</li>
<li>Support all levels of management by handling other assigned duties.</li>
<li>Responsible for maintaining data/voice infrastructure and equipment.</li>
<li>Demonstrated ability to multi-task between simultaneous computer applications.</li>
<li>Must pass an in-house Aesop and a PC Literacy Test annually.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Minimum Job Requirements: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>
<b>Education: </b>A high school diploma or equivalent is required. BS/BA or equivalent experience preferred.</li>
<li>
<b>Experience: </b>2 years in hardwire/wireless network support environment.</li>
<li>
<b>Specific Skills: Desirable: </b>Proficient in MS Office Suite 2010 and 2013. Must be able to provide technical assistance and support for incoming queries and issues related to computer systems, and hardware. Respond to support request either in person or over the phone. Good listener and clearly communicates in English.</li>
<li>
<b>Specialized Knowledge, Licenses, etc. </b>This team member will have good working knowledge of physical data networks, both fiber and CAT5/6, proper terminations, familiarity with Internet and IP networks, Cisco router IP configuration and troubleshooting tasks, ability to use Telnet, Traceroute, Ping, properly configure/troubleshoot TCP/IP configurations on multiple PC operating platforms, build and test straight and crossover cables both Ethernet and T-1, troubleshoot physical connectivity for both fiber and CAT5/6 backbones and build networks. Must be able to work with minimal supervision, possess good customer service skills and be able to exercise independent judgment.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Working Conditions: </b>A split between office environment as well as work in high places pulling cable and in small dusty areas installing cabling.</p>
