<h2>SOC Operator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">Job Overview
<br><br>
Act as the front-line in triaging, escalating, and communicating incidents that originate from production systems. Proactively monitor incident and ticket trends to feed into a Problem Management program to improve the stability of our systems. Work with Ops Engineering, Development, and Business Teams to solve issues that occur within these 24x7 environments.
<br><br>

The product and engineering teams at Marchex work on some of the hardest problems at the intersection of business and technology: we work with our customers across many service industries to better use digital analytics of phone calls to map out intent and drive data analysis from first contact to a closing sale.

<br><br>
Outline of Duties and Responsibilities
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Monitor an event console for real-time incident events.</li>
<li>Track incidents using a ticketing system and document all steps taken for future review.</li>
<li>Manage customer impacting outage events, while maintaining communication to all key stakeholders and providing accurate timeline management.</li>
<li>Manage a high-volume centralized email inbox</li>
<li>Follow clearly documented resolution procedures for incidents and requests.</li>
<li>Maintain Service Level Agreement (SLA) compliance for Time to Engage, Time to Escalate, and Time to Fix within the Service Operations Center.</li>
<li>When unable to resolve issues at the Tier 1 level, escalate incidents to on-call engineers following clearly defined standard operating procedures (SOP).</li>
<li>Provide clear and detailed verbal/written communications to both internal and external parties.</li>
<li>Assist in maintaining systems and product documentation to ensure the highest level of knowledge within the SOC.</li>
</ul>
Experience, Skills and Qualifications
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Experienced Linux server based environments.</li>
<li>Ability to communicate effectively at all organization levels and at different levels of technical knowledge.</li>
<li>Proven ability to work effectively both independently or as a team member with peers and/or senior technical staff.</li>
<li>BS degree or Military technology experience or equivalent experience is a plus.</li>
<li>One or more of the following certifications highly desired: LPI, MCITP, Server+, Network+, CCENT, CCNA, ITIL.</li>
<li>1+ years working within a 24x7x365 service/network operations center role is a plus.</li>
<li>Experience with the ITIL or MOF based guidelines for service operations is a plus.</li>
<li>Familiarity with fundamental networking/distributed computing environment concepts such as DNS, IP networking, routing, etc is a plus.</li>
<li>Familiarity with fundamental concepts of relational database systems and corresponding query languages is a plus.</li>
<li>Experience with shell scripting and automating tasks is a plus.</li>
</ul>
