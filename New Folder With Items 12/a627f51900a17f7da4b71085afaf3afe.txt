<b>Responsibilities</b>:
<ul>
<li>Calculate and process monthly and quarterly commission payments, whether with an Excel-based commissions model or through the Compensation System (Xactly Incent)</li>
<li>
Prepare monthly commission reports, accruals, and payroll workbooks for Accounting</li>
<li>
Track, research, and resolve commission calculation-related inquiries and exceptions including disputes and appeals; interact with the Sales organization to address inquiries regarding territory alignment, bookings allocation, and commissions</li>

<li>
Assist with monthly and quarterly analyses to measure performance, including variance to budget, correlation between result and payouts, and detail by individual contributor and by manager</li>
<li>
Distribute, track, and report on the status of Plan acceptance to ensure compliance and timeliness</li>
<li>
Provide world-class customer service to our Sales organization and internal customers, serving as the "go-to" resource for commission-related questions</li>
<li>
Perform audits to ensure data integrity between Xactly and the plan documents</li>
<li>
Administer Xactly system set-up and monthly updates (new hires, exits, quota/rate changes, etc.), and ensure that rules align with current comp plans</li>
<li>
Support audits</li>
