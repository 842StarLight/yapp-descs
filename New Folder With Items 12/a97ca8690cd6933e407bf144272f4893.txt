<h2>Pharmacist in Charge - Store 1421</h2>

<b>POSITION OVERVIEW:</b><br>
<br>
This position reports directly to the Healthcare Manager. The PIC provides prescription product and appropriate service and consultation to every customer, including appropriate clinical services. The Pharmacist-in-Charge manages and supervises the pharmacy staff including: staff pharmacists, pharmacy interns/externs, pharmacy technicians, and pharmacy associates. The PIC also ensures that that the pharmacy is in compliance with any and all federal and state laws, rules and regulation and to ensure FREDâ€™S policies and procedures are followed by all pharmacy team members.<br>
<br>
<b>DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES:</b>
</div>
<ul>
<li>
<div>Drive pharmacy sales results by ensuring pharmacy team members provide a consistent high quality customer/patient experience.</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Responsible for the operations and oversight of all matters occurring within the pharmacy.</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Ensures that all pharmacy and employee licenses, certificates and paperwork are up-to- date and kept in employee personnel files.</div>
</li>
<li>

As part of this, the PIC has the authority to hire, discipline, evaluate, counsel, guide, train, direct, supervise, promote and terminate pharmacy team members as needed.</div>

<li>

<li>

<li>

</li>
<li>
<div>Promote safe work practices by all team members and provide a safe environment for team members and customers/patients.</div>
</li>
<li>

</li>
<li>
<div>Solicit and observe customer feedback to understand customer needs and the needs of the local community.</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Handle and process employee and customer complaints and grievances and use independent judgment and discretion to ensure customer satisfaction.</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Planning and directing the objectives, procedures and activities for the pharmacy.</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Takes all steps required to fill prescriptions and operate a pharmacy in compliance with all laws, regulations, and company policies and procedures.</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Assists with and respond in a courteous and professional manner to questions from customers, physicians, and vendors.</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Offers advice and professional consultation; acts as a role model to the pharmacy team; efficiently handles customer complaints; and directs pharmacy operations to keep wait times to a minimum.</div>

<li>

</li>
<li>

<div><b>EXPERIENCE/REQUIREMENTS:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
<div>A graduate of an accredited School of Pharmacy with a Bachelor of Science or PharmD degree</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Must maintain a valid pharmacy license in the state in which you are practicing.</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Successful completion of APHA approved Immunization Training Course</div>

<li>
