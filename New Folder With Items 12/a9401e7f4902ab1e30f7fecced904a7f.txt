<h2>Student Employment - Box Office Associate</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>Position Type: Student Department: Freed Center Operations Job Summary:
<p>The Box Office Associate is responsible for providing patrons with superior experience while selling tickets, subscriptions, and memberships to the public over the phone and in-person leading to customer satisfaction and retention. The Box Office Associate is expected to have a high degree of professional behavior and accuracy.</p>
Principal Responsibilities
<ul>
<li>Communicate arts information for events including making recommendations for additional events (i.e., suggestive selling)</li>
<li>Accurately and thoroughly record sales and patron data using the customer relationship management software</li>
<li>Develop relationship/rapport with patrons and constituents including alumni, parents, current students, faculty, staff, and friends of the University</li>
<li>Accurately balance daily accounting sessions including check, cash, receivables and credit card sales</li>
<li>Manage routine patron problems and questions</li>
<li>Provide administrative support including, but not limited to, correspondence and research</li>
<li>Maintain a thorough knowledge of policy and software</li>
<li>Organize and maintain an orderly work space</li>
<li>Complete other duties as assigned</li>
</ul>
Required Skills:
<ul>
<li>Basic math/banking skills are essential; must be able to calculate figures and amounts and apply basic algebra skills</li>
<li>Exceptional computer skills including the ability to learn and utilize new software</li>
<li>Ability to handle cash responsibly and accurately</li>
<li>Ability to read, analyze, and interpret documents and to effectively present information to various audiences</li>
<li>Demonstrated strong customer service skills</li>
<li>Excellent verbal and written communication skills</li>
<li>Punctuality</li>
<li>Ability to represent the University and the Freed Center in a positive manner</li>
<li>An interest in the performing arts is preferred</li>
</ul>
Minimum Qualifications:
<ul>
<li>Must be enrolled as an ONU student</li>
<li>Good academic standing</li>
</ul>
