<h2>Customer Software Support Representative</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<div><div><div>
<p>Priority Dispatch is looking for a knowledgeable <b>Customer Software Support Representative</b> to work with their support team. This individual will work with Support, Sales, and Research &amp; Development to troubleshoot, install and provide general support of various software products. This dynamic individual will also perform some internal helpdesk functions such as providing expertise, assistance, and coordination necessary to install computer software products. We are looking for an engaging individual that will provide a high level of Customer Service to our clients.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Supports Priority Dispatch System software for emergency communication centers</li>
<li>Investigate user problems to identify the source and determine possible solutions</li>
<li>Work with clients installing and troubleshooting PDC's software</li>
<li>On-call support for after-hour and holiday periods assigned by management</li>
<li>Ability to instruct individuals with various levels of computer skills</li>
<li>Follow up with clients in a timely manner</li>
<li>Maintains confidentiality regarding information being processed, stored or accessed by the end-users on the network</li>
<li>Assist internal groups with software upgrades, questions, etc.</li>
<li>Conduct testing on new release software when necessary and report results to developers</li>
<li>Report client feedback to Quality Assurance</li>
<li>Travels to client sites for installations, configurations, and training</li>
</ul>
</div></div></div>
<div>Qualifications</div>
<div><div>
<div>
<ul>
<li>This position requires a high level of interpersonal skills, with the ability to communicate technical information to nontechnical personnel in both verbal and written formats</li>
<li>The ideal candidate must have organizational skills and the ability to establish priorities</li>
<li>Ability to rotate work hour shifts between the hours of 6:30 a.m. to 5 p.m. Monday through Friday</li>
<li>Must have prior Customer Service phone and email experience supporting Tier 1 and Tier 2 technical and software support type questions</li>
<li>Ability to learn quickly and keep up with a fast pace environment</li>
<li>SQL knowledge preferred</li>
<br>
</ul>

PDC offers multi-agency emergency dispatching ProQA® software, as well as a card-set version, AQUA® quality improvement software, training, consulting, and Academy accreditation support.</p>
