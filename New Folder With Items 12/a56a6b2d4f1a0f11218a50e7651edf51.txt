<h2>CSR - Customer Service Representative</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>The Coeur d'Alene Window Company is looking to hire a Customer Service Representative.</b></p>
<p>PRIMARY FUNCTIONS</p>
<ul>
<li>Completes quotes and enters orders as requested or required by dealers and/or contractors</li>
<li>Provides product, delivery, and other information requested by Coeur d’Alene Window customers</li>
<li>Supports the efforts of sales team members</li>
<li>Communicates customer concerns directly to the window plant general manager and other team members</li>
<li>Communicates current lead times to customers</li>
<li>Ensures that sales team members and customers are kept up to date with product, service, and pricing changes as needed</li>
<li>Communicates quality concerns to manufacturing and service without haste</li>
</ul>
<p>SKILLS REQUIRED</p>
<ul>
<li>Ability to communicate verbally in a professional manner</li>
<li>Ability, after training, to understand Coeur d’Alene Window’s proprietary manufacturing and quoting software</li>
<li>Knowledge and competence in Microsoft Word and Excel</li>
</ul>
<p>ADDITIONAL DESIRED SKILLS</p>
<ul>
<li>Knowledge and competence in AutoCAD</li>
<li>Ability to read blueprints</li>
<li>Prior experience in window manufacturing, construction, or customer service</li>
</ul>
<p>EDUCATION REQUIRED</p>
<ul><li>High School Diploma or equivalent</li></ul>
<p><i>Our company offers a competitive package including vacation, sick, paid holidays, 401(k), health, dental and vision insurance and voluntary STD, LTD, and life insurance benefits.</i></p>
<p>Job Type: Full-time</p>
</div>
