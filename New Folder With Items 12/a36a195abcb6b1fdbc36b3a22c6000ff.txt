<h2>Social Media Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Come be our new Social Media Manager</b></p>
<p>Icon Foods is a leading manufacturer of natural sweetening solutions, geared for food manufacturers and consumers. Begun more than 15 years ago, specializing in stevia, the company has grown over time to include a comprehensive portfolio of healthy and natural sweetening solutions perfect for consumers looking to get sugar out of their diets and for manufacturers looking to replace unhealthy ingredients in their products. The burgeoning demand for manufacturers to replace unhealthy sweeteners in their recipes has uniquely positioned us as the go-to solution for today's market.</p>
<p>As we approach 2020 we are putting in place a major initiative to expand our marketing and social media reach. Overall you will be taking on the following challenges:</p>
<ul><li>Development and implementation of the on-line Brand strategy.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Developing the on-line marketing strategy for new and existing products.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Brochures, whitepapers, spec sheets and press releases</li></ul>
<ul><li>Overseeing implementation of the digital Marketing strategy - including campaigns, events, digital marketing, and PR.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Working closely with the company's Sales team; enabling them to meet their commercial objectives by providing them with appropriate tools, materials and presentations.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Ensuring that the marketing objectives are implemented.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Work closely with product management team to define on-line marketing materials and programs.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Manage social media presence and direct programs to improve social media reputation and recognition.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Developing and delivering on-line marketing and communications strategies for the organization.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Undertake continuous analysis of competitive environment and consumer trends.</li></ul>
<p>A key component of our 2020 initiative will include implementing a social media strategy aimed at: Facebook, Twitter, IG, Pinterest and Linked In. This will include:</p>
<ul><li>Generating pertinent content</li></ul>
<ul><li>Creating and disseminating on-line newsletters using MailChimp</li></ul>
<ul><li>Initiating ads on appropriate platforms</li></ul>
<ul><li>Building follows and engagement</li></ul>
<ul><li>Engaging with our community on line, as a central voice of the brand</li></ul>
<p>You will also be key in compiling and reaching out to key influencers in our market, helping to build brand ambassadors, corporate sponsorships and expanding our affiliates program.</p>
<p>If you have experience or interest in the field of nutrition and wellness, this could be an especially good fit for you. Some of the key constituents we will connect with include consumers and industry professionals dealing with:</p>
<ul><li>Diabetes</li></ul>
<ul><li>Weight Management</li></ul>
<ul><li>Fitness</li></ul>
<ul><li>Food and recipe development</li></ul>
<ul><li>Specialized diets, such as paleo and keto</li></ul>

This is a rare opportunity to join a strong, stable team in an accelerated growth period. In addition to your genuinely positive attitude, we are looking for the following skills and experience:</p>
<ul><li>Are a budding wordsmith! Must be very comfortable writing at a professional level.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Expressiveness, personality and professional tone a must.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Great communicator, both spoken and written.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Comfortable with spreadsheets &amp; learning new software.</li></ul>
