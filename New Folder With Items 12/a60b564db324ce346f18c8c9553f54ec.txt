<p>
How you will spend your time as our next Services Sales Manager</p>
<p>
As a Services Sales Manager at Qlik you work closely with the Qlik License Sales teams, Qlik Consulting counterparts, customers and partners to ensure the overall success of the sale and implementation of QlikView and Qlik Sense solutions. You are expected to significantly contribute to the overall success of the customer relationship.</p>
<p>
You will leverage the Qlik sales model to maximize revenue growth and increase local market share. You will be leading the Sales process for our Consulting Services team. In this role you will effectively present and scope Qlik Consulting Implementation offerings and capabilities, to new prospects and existing customers. You will be responsible for managing the Services Sales lifecycle from proposal through final signature of SOW.</p>
<p>
We are looking for a self-motivated team player who excels at cultivating strategic relationships while developing new business. The right candidate will have strong business acumen and a successful track record of selling to C–level executives, including the ability to translate client needs into appropriate Qlik solutions. Strong sales experience in software &amp; services environment with a proven track record of success is a must</p>
<ul>
<li>
Creating Consulting opportunities, managing pursuits and closing deals.</li>
<li>
Successfully achieving or over-achieving quotas to generate Consulting Services sales.</li>
<li>
Qualifying and developing value-add propositions for Enterprise clients and prospects.
</li>
<li>Manage the customer relationship(s) from initial discussions to close of deal.
</li>
<li>Develop a strong revenue backlog, account planning, account development and lead generation</li>
<li>
Present implementation offerings to existing customers and new prospects</li>
<li>
Forecasting for future accounts and opportunities</li>
<li>
Work collaboratively with the Consulting Director for resource planning to ensure successful knowledge transfer and onboarding of new customers</li>
<li>
Updates CRM tools (Salesforce) with documentation from the sales cycle</li>
<li>
Maintain and creates new tools and templates for usage in working with new prospects, such as service offerings and SOW templates</li>
</ul>
