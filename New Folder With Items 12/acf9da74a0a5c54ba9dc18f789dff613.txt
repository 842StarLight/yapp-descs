<h2>Business Development Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">WE HELP ONLINE COMMUNITIES THRIVE
<br><br>
Spot.IM's mission is to create exceptional technology that empowers media publishers to develop meaningful and monetizable online communities for their readers within their owned-and-operated spaces. Started in 2012, our software platform helps the world's biggest online publishers form independent communities around their distinct content, quickly bolstering their overall engagement and social interactions. We connect millions of unique users a month across leading media publishers including News Corp, Verizon Media, CBS, Fox News, Hearst, Refinery 29 and more. Spot.IM is VC-backed by Insight Ventures, one of the world's most trusted and successful institutional investors, and we are focused on growing our standout global team and product stack.
<br><br>
We look for self-starters; those with a founder mentality. We ship every day. We embrace bold ideas and encourage experimentation.
<br><br>
<b>BD Manager - What You'll Do:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Cultivate and expand professional relationships with Tier-2 and Tier-3 media publishers across the globe in order to sell Spot.IM's community engagement software platform and drive the partner's revenue base</li>
<li>Own the full sales life-cycle for your prospective deals from initial call, to product demo, through deal signing. We encourage our team to meet their prospective partners in person and to bring those partners to our office as well!</li>
<li>Define target markets/verticals, partner with sales operations on lead generation processes and pipelines, and document interactions and follow-ups in Salesforce</li>
<li>Partner with internal Product, Partner Management, and Monetization teams to develop and introduce new revenue opportunities into the market and pitch white-labeled solutions to prospective partners</li>
<li>Help to co-define Business Development team processes, goals and KPI's as our growing startup continues to scale</li>
</ul>
<b>The Skills and Experience You Bring:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Demonstrated SaaS publisher sales experience and a proven track record of achieving annual revenue targets</li>
<li>Ability to navigate complex sales organizations and build world-class business relationships with senior executives in order to execute partner deals</li>
<li>A thorough knowledge of digital media, advertising technologies and SaaS business models</li>
<li>Solutions selling mindset and approach to relationship building - we're here to solve major industry problems with technical solutions</li>
<li>Entrepreneurial, self-motivated, goal and target-oriented executor combined with a team-first mentality - our Business Development team regularly works together as a team to close deals with each other!</li>
</ul>
