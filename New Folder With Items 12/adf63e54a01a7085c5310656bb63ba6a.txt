<h2>Area Sales Director (ASD)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<b>Locations:</b> Southeast, North Central, New England, South Central, or West
<br><b>Contact:</b> hr@packetviper.com
<br>Role Overview
<br>The PacketViper Area Sales Director (ASD) is an individual contributor responsible for driving company revenue
<br>and growth in the defined geography while collaborating with other company resources. The ASD is goal oriented
<br>and will succeed by implementing a process oriented strategic territory plan. They will take pride on being a
<br>subject matter expert who understands the security market and customer challenges and initiates sales activities
<br>based on solving customer problems. The ASD will ensure an appropriate volume of these activities are always
<br>ongoing to ensure their territory drives an increasing, predictable revenue stream. Successful candidates
<br>understand how to navigate large complex sales and are challenger type sellers familiar with a variety of sales
<br>process disciplines.
<br>Responsibilities
<br><ul>
<li>High level sales planning and accurate forecasting of business</li>
<li>Developing a sales funnel of sufficiently qualified opportunities to ensure revenue goals are achieved</li>
<li>Manage multiple concurrent sales cycles from qualified lead (SQL) to close for enterprise size deals</li>
<li>Regular business interactions with both customers and channel partners</li>
<li>Engage a process-oriented approach to generate demand, develop and expand the territory</li>
<li>Ongoing rapport building and probing of key prospects and thought leaders in the territory</li>
<li>Bring to bear all cross functional resources from PacketViper to support the revenue targets in the defined</li>
</ul>
territory
<br><ul>
<li>Develop and maintain a fundamental understanding of security related industry knowledge</li>
<li>Stay current on PacketViper product information, sales strategies and positioning</li>
<li>Timely and accurate CRM data entry and records management</li>
<li>Travel as necessary</li>
</ul>
Requirements
<br><ul>
<li>Reside within the defined territory</li>
<li>3+ years of documented, successful cybersecurity related sales experience (products or services) with a</li>
</ul>
proven track record of negotiating large enterprise software deals
<br><ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree preferred</li>
<li>Excellent time management skills, attention to detail and ability to work with high levels of autonomy.</li>
<li>Excellent interpersonal, spoken, written and presentation communication skills</li>
<li>Process oriented sales approach and excellent probing/discovery skills</li>
<li>Experience working with channel partners</li>
<li>Ability to thrive in fast-paced, rapidly changing environment that requires urgency to capitalize on</li>
</ul>
opportunities
