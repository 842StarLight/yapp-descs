POSITION SUMMARY</div>
<div><div>
<div>
<p>KTGY is seeking a part-time Office Services Associate to support our Irvine office. The facilities studio is a tight knit group responsible for ensuring each office can work efficiently. You will be responsible for actively responding to onsite demands and must be able to exercise effective time management skills. If you would describe yourself as someone who is organized and works hard without forgetting to have fun, send us your qualifications!</p>
<p>This position is responsible for maintaining cleanliness and order within the office. Primary functions include maintaining office inventory, supply pick up, and general maintenance.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p>Minimum qualifications:</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Minimum of 1 year experience working in an office environment</li>
<li>High school diploma or equivalent</li>
<li>Ability to display positive attitude about the work to be done, co-workers, clients, management, and firm policies</li>
<li>Self-motivated with the initiative to be proactive and take prompt action to accomplish objectives</li>
<li>Accurate and detail oriented</li>
<li>Strong oral and written communication skills</li>
<li>Basic understanding and proficiency using the following software:
<ul>
<li>MS Office</li>
</ul>
</li>
</ul>
