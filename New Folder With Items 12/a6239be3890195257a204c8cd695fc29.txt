<h2>Restaurant Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Restaurant professionals, are you excited to work with a company that will reward your hard work and dedication? Are you looking for a company that provides education and opportunities for career growth and personal development? At TravelCenters (TA/Petro/TA-Express), we provide you with the tools and training you need to be successful and grow within our restaurant group which operates more than 650 restaurants and food outlets.</p>
<p>We are looking for an experienced Assistant General Manager to join our team!</p>
<p><b>In this role, you can expect: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Provide visible leadership to management and employees while generating a restaurant atmosphere focused on great hospitality where the expectations of the guests are exceeded.</li>
<li>Embody a professional demeanor and a high level of character, honesty and integrity in conducting the business affairs of the restaurant. Inspire all team members to do the same</li>
<li>Develop a trusting atmosphere that is conducive to receiving feedback from team members and guests; coach, discipline, and/or terminate the staff according to company policy</li>
<li>With General Manager support, recruit, hire, train and retain highly motivated employees according to company guidelines and maintain a culture where employees feel respected and recognized for their achievements</li>
<li>Respond to guest concerns, both written and verbal, with the ability to creatively solve problems and develop robust solutions. Personally contacts guests as appropriate</li>
<li>Assist in achieving financial objectives through effective management of people, product, service and facility processes, including diligently monitoring sales, margins and expenses vs. budget</li>
<li>Adhere to safety standard, comply with standards of operation and follow company guidelines on vendor relationships</li>
<li>Stay alert to competitive practices that could have an impact on business; offer recommendations when appropriate.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>What we’d like to see: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>A leader who sets the example when working alongside team members</li>
<li>High School Diploma (or GED), Associate or Bachelor Degree preferred in Hospitality Management, Business Administration, or other related major</li>
<li>Supervisory experience in a casual dining restaurant</li>
<li>Exhibit excellent verbal and written communication skills.</li>
<li>Ability to work flexible hours including nights, weekends and some holidays</li>
<li>Proficient in Microsoft Word, POS software and email etiquette; Able to run and understand various operational reports</li>
<li>Strong influencing and collaboration skills</li>
<li>Meet the requirements for the needed physical tasks with or without accommodation</li>
</ul>

<li>Opportunity for growth and advancement</li>

</ul>
