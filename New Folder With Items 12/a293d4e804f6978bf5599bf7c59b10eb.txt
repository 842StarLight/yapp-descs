<h2>IT Support Technician</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">This position serves dual functions of Information Systems (IS) and Media Production support duties. Your 'customers' are two groups: Administrative staff which consists of the business side of the operation (management/sales) and the creative staff who produce our on-air and online products.
<br><br>
Essential Duties and Responsibilities
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Ensure compliance with all Company Engineering and Information Technology practices.</li>
<li>Interface with Vice President of Engineering and Director of Information Technology on a regular basis.</li>
<li>Talking to customers to get details of faults, working out the reasons for a fault and explaining these to the customer.</li>
<li>Logging of problems and their solutions for future reference.</li>
<li>Perform ongoing performance tuning, hardware upgrades, and resource optimization.</li>
<li>Perform daily system monitoring, verifying the integrity and availability of all hardware, server resources, systems and key processes, reviewing system and application logs, and verifying completion of scheduled jobs such as backups.</li>
<li>Monitor daily backup operations, ensuring all required file systems and system data are successfully backed up to the appropriate media, recovery disks are created, and media is recycled and sent off site as necessary.</li>
<li>Installation, maintenance and repair of PC, servers, printers, software, LAN systems.</li>
<li>Audio and Video Web streaming systems maintenance.</li>
<li>Telephone and voicemail systems maintenance and updating.</li>
<li>Training and helping creative staff with studio equipment and computer issues.</li>
<li>After hours and weekend work as required.</li>
<li>Other duties as necessary.</li>
</ul>
Qualifications
<br><br>
To perform this job successfully, an individual must be able to perform each essential duty satisfactorily. The requirements listed below are representative of the knowledge, skill, and/or ability required. Reasonable accommodations may be made to enable individuals with disabilities to perform the essential functions.
<br><br>
<b>Education/Experience:</b><br>
AA/AS degree or certification in Electronics, or equivalent electronics and/or IT training/experience. Will consider previous work experience in lieu of degree.
<br><br>
<b>Language Ability:</b><br>
Ability to read and interpret documents such as safety rules, operating and maintenance instructions, and procedure manuals. Ability to write routine reports and correspondence. Ability to speak effectively before groups of customers or employees of organization.
<br><br>
<b>Reasoning Ability:</b><br>
Ability to solve practical problems and deal with a variety of concrete variables in situations where only limited standardization exists. Ability to interpret a variety of instructions furnished in written, oral, diagram, or schedule form.
<br><br>
<b>Key Competencies:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>A high aptitude in technology and the ability to grasp complex concepts quickly is required.</li>
<li>Must have solid working knowledge of PC hardware, be familiar with Microsoft Windows (all versions), Microsoft Office, Networking concepts and computer hardware, wireless and handheld devices.</li>
<li>Possess IT networking skills; knowledge of TCP/IP, UDP and local area networking.</li>
<li>Demonstrates the ability and desire to learn new technologies quickly.</li>
<li>Candidate must be a self-starter capable of independent work, study, problem analysis and problem solving; thrives on technical challenges and takes pride in solving them.</li>
<li>Customer service orientation with attention to detail, Ability to train others</li>
<li>Radio Broadcast tech support experience is desirable.</li>
</ul>
<b>Certificates and Licenses:</b><br>
SBE or computer related certification desired.
<br><br>
Physical Demands
<br><br>
The physical demands described here are representative of those that must be met by an employee to successfully perform the essential functions of this job. Reasonable accommodations may be made to enable individuals with disabilities to perform the essential functions.
<br><br>
The employee must frequently lift and/or move up to 10 pounds and occasionally lift and/or move up to 50 pounds. While performing the duties of this job, the employee is frequently required to stand; walk; sit; use hands to finger, handle, or feel; reach with hands and arms and talk or hear. The employee is occasionally required to climb or balance and stoop, kneel, crouch, or crawl. The worker is required to have close visual acuity to perform an activity such as: preparing and analyzing data and figures; transcribing; viewing a computer terminal; expansive reading; visual inspection involving small defects, small parts and/or operation of machines (including inspection); using measurement devices; and/or assembly of fabrication of parts at distances close to the eyes.
<br><br>
This job specification should not be construed to imply that these requirements are the exclusive standards of the position. Incumbent will follow any other instructions, and perform any other related duties, as may be required by the supervisor.
