<h2>Machine Operator - Day Shift</h2>

<p><b>Brady Corporation Overview</b></p>
<p>
Brady Corporation is an international manufacturer and marketer of complete solutions that identify and protect people, products and places. Brady's products help customers increase safety, security, productivity and performance and include high-performance labels, signs, safety devices, printing systems and software. Founded in 1914, the Company has a diverse customer base in electronics, telecommunications, manufacturing, electrical, construction, medical, aerospace and a variety of other industries. Brady is headquartered in Milwaukee, Wisconsin and as of July 31, 2018, employed approximately 6,200 people in its worldwide businesses. Brady's fiscal 2018 sales were approximately $1.17 billion. Brady stock trades on the New York Stock Exchange under the symbol BRC. More information is available at www.bradycorp.com.</p>
<p>
This opening is for a 12 hour day-shift positions.</p>
<p><b>
Position Summary</b></p>
<p>
The machine operator will be responsible for operating equipment in the areas of coating, compounding, and/or converting. This position is responsible for the production of valuable, defect-free products that meet or exceed our customers' expectations. The machine operator will be successful in this position by executing to standard work and adhering to all company policies.</p>
<p>
 <b>Essential Duties and Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Perform production equipment set up, clean up, safe operation and operator level maintenance of assigned equipment</li>
<li>Weigh, catalyze and mix coatings (coating department)</li>
<li>Ensure converting machines are running properly to cut product to desired widths. Read measurement devices such as rulers or calipers to ensure correct product specifications and tolerances are adhered to. (converting department)</li>
<li>Evaluate and tag out defects as necessary</li>
<li>Prepare pump and filtration systems for production process</li>
<li>Operate material handling equipment</li>
<li>Follow detailed written product specifications</li>
<li>Perform in-process inspection</li>
<li>Perform operator level quality testing to maintain product quality</li>
<li>Make on the fly process adjustments to maintain product quality</li>
<li>Perform roll changes using material handling equipment and web threading</li>
<li>Participate actively in gain sharing and business goals</li>
<li>Comprehend and adhere to equipment and material specification</li>
<li>Maintain quality standards while meeting daily and weekly production goals</li>
<li>Communicate production misses and quality issues to appropriate leaders and departments</li>
<li>Participate in continuous improvement initiatives</li>
<li>Identify gaps and process improvements proactively</li>
<li>Cross train in other departments and equipment</li>
<li>Support Lean Daily Management and Key Performance Indicator tracking</li>
<li>Work with hazardous materials utilizing the proper personal protective equipment</li>
<li>Adhere to Brady's attendance, safety and other company policies</li>
<li>Maintain accurate paperwork, labeling, packaging and material transactions.</li>
<li>Record production data for entry in the ERP system in an accurate and timely manner.</li>
<li>Complete general housekeeping duties</li>
<li>Maintain safe working conditions in the department</li>
<li>Performs other tasks as assigned. Responsibilities include, but are not necessarily limited to, the tasks listed on this job description.</li>
</ul> <p><b>Required Knowledge, Skills &amp; Abilities</b></p>

<li>Ability to troubleshoot and communicate process issues</li>
<li>Effective verbal and written communication skills</li>
<li>Technical and analytical skill set</li>

<li>Detail oriented, strong work ethic and a positive can do attitude</li>
<li>Desire to learn</li>

<li>Quality conscious and attention to detail</li>
<li>Ability to maintain standards of production, both in quantity and quality.</li>
<li>Willingness and ability to be cross-trained on other machines and in other departments</li>
</ul> <p><b>Desired Knowledge, Skills &amp; Abilities</b></p>
