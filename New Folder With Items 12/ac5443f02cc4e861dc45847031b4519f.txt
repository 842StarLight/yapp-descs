The world's leading private security organization, G4S, has an immediate job opportunity for a Senior Marketing Campaign Manager. The Senior Marketing Campaign Manager is responsible for developing marketing campaigns that span all business units to further promote our Integrated Security Solutions strategy.
<br><br>
This is a senior individual contributor role used to unite a cross-functional sales and marketing team in order to develop compelling and integrated marketing campaigns. The Senior Marketing Campaign Manager will have deep customer and target market knowledge, will have an understanding of business strategy, and will have a good knowledge of company product.
<br><br>
G4S is a security provider for the United States government, fortune 500 companies, nuclear power plants, oil and gas companies, airport, ports, banks, hospitals, factories, warehouses, commercial facilities, residential communities and much more.

Main Responsibilities
<br>Specific Duties and Essential Functions
<br><br>
Develop integrated marketing campaigns with the aim of acquiring and retaining customers by working with the marketing and sales teams,
<br>Manage the implementation, tracking and measurement of marketing campaigns
<br>Write, edit and proofread copy for promotional materials within marketing campaigns
<br>Use social media and other available channels to drive campaign engagement
<br>Ensure that the organization’s brand and identity is adhered to in campaigns and in all communication channels
<br>Deliver regular reports/dashboards of campaign results, including web analysis and evaluation of KPIs
<br>Maintain regular measurement of the ROI of campaigns through the use of Salesforce
<br>Collect and use data to inform new campaigns and the evaluation of existing campaigns
<br>Keep abreast of current trends in marketing
<br><br>
