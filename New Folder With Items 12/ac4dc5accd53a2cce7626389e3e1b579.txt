<h2>Associate Proposal Writer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>Sharecare is the digital health company that helps people manage all their health in one place. The Sharecare platform provides each person - no matter where they are in their health journey - with a comprehensive and personalized health profile, where they can dynamically and easily connect to the information, evidence-based programs and health professionals they need to live their healthiest, happiest and most productive life. With award-winning and innovative frictionless technologies, scientifically validated clinical protocols and best-in-class coaching tools, Sharecare helps providers, employers and health plans effectively scale outcomes-based health and wellness solutions across their entire populations. We are always looking for people that value the opportunity to work hard, have fun on the job, and make a difference in the lives of others through their work every day!</p>
<p><b>
Job Summary</b><br>
Interested in serving an essential role in driving Sharecare's bottom line revenue? Have a passion for supporting individuals in improving their health and well-being? Looking to use your project management and writing skills in an environment that's fast-paced, collaborative, and on the cutting edge of population health?</p>
<p>
Sharecare is looking for an Associate Proposal Writer to join our team, supporting the successful submission of critical proposal projects. As an Associate Proposal Writer for Sharecare, you will be responsible for developing, managing, maintaining, and building our content to support proposal and sales support requests. You will work collaboratively with a diverse set of key stakeholders to troubleshoot and resolve any issues associated with keeping our content competitive, current, and in alignment with Sharecare's brand values. This role holds great value in strategically influencing Sharecare's voice to the marketplace through the RFP process.</p>
<div>
<b>
Essential Functions</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Maintain considerable product and operational knowledge for developing clear and concise responses</li>
<li>Liaison with the team lead and sales throughout the proposal process from kick-off to win</li>
<li>Gather proposal content by leveraging proposal software (i.e. Loopio) to research content and clearly describe products and services to relay value</li>
<li>Seek insight as needed through additional company resources and obtain approval on new and revised content</li>
<li>Ensure quality assurance by adhering to standard processes and project timelines</li>
<li>Combine input from various sources to communicate key messages with a single voice that flows and provides a logical progression through the subject matter</li>
<li>Support knowledgebase manager with setting up and maintaining boilerplate response language, content library records, and library attachments</li>
<li>Improves responses by following and evaluating proposal writing standards for readability and maintaining consistency with company positioning/tone</li>
<li>Utilize functional and technical content to create custom proposals with a persuasive sales approach</li>
<li>Conduct RFP/RFI analysis to support qualifying opportunities and developing win strategies</li>
<li>Accept ownership for new and different responsibilities as business priorities shift, exploring and building opportunities to add value</li>
<li>Facilitate printing and shipping of proposals, if necessary</li>
</ul>
</div>
</div>

<li>Knowledge of well-being and employee benefit platforms</li>
