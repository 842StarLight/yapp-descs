<b>About The Medical Center of Aurora</b><br>
<b>The Medical Center of Aurora, the first community hospital in the Denver Metro area to receive two-time Magnet designation for nursing excellence by the American Nurses Credentialing Center (ANCC), is a 346-bed acute care hospital located in Aurora, Colorado. The Medical Center of Aurora is comprised of six campuses in Aurora and Centennial, Colo., including the Main Campus, located at Interstate 225 and Mississippi, the North Campus Behavioral Health and Wellness Center, Centennial Medical Plaza, Saddle Rock ER, Spalding Rehabilitation Hospital, Spalding Rehabilitation at P/SL, and a medical office building at Green Valley Ranch. The Main Campus facility is a Level II Trauma Center with Primary Stroke Certification and Chest Pain Center accreditation, serving the eastern metro area and I-70 corridor. The Medical Center of Aurora received ‘A’ grades from The Leapfrog Group (Fall 2015, Fall 2016, Spring 2017), was named Colorado’s only 2016 Leapfrog Top General Hospital, was recognized as the #4 hospital in the Denver metro area and the #4 hospital in Colorado by U.S. News &amp; World Report (2014-2015, 2017-2018), and was one of only nine hospitals in the country to receive the Lantern Award for Excellence in Emergency Room Care by the Emergency Nurses Association (2013).</b><br>
<br>
This position supports the mission of The Medical Center of Aurora and Centennial Medical Plaza: above all else, we are committed to the care and improvement of human life.
<br>
<br>
Under the supervision of the Executive Team, the Director of Business Development is responsible for integrating the vision of the corporation with internal organizational information, market research and environmental analyses in order to formulate with senior management, a workable, long-term strategic direction for The Medical Center of Aurora’s Centennial Medical Campus.<br>
<ul>
<li>Directs the collection and analysis of data for the strategic planning purposes and other plans developed to meet Centennial hospital strategic initiatives in collaboration with the VP of Business Development.</li>
<li>Responsible for developing and implementing a business plan aligned with the strategic objectives identified for the hospital as determined by the CEO and executive team.</li>
<li>Leads the Strategic Planning process including regular progress updates. Focus will be on developing tracking and reporting mechanisms to insure that strategic objectives are measurable. Strategic planning, business development, sale of services is directed at increasing awareness and use of our facility, products, services and physicians.</li>
<li>Leads the implementation of new business opportunities as determined through market research, proformas and the strategic planning process.</li>
<li>Responsibility and oversight for the implementation of the strategic plan across all service lines.</li>
<li>Cultivates relationships with physicians by developing &amp; conducting needs assessment survey—design, letters, operationalize, and implementation of succession planning protocol.</li>
<li>Works closely with PSG adult employed practices to develop and oversee growth opportunities at the Centennial Campus.</li>
<li>Responsible for annual community needs analysis and physician recruitment plan in collaboration with the VP of Business Development.</li>
<li>Develops and oversees primary care growth plan for TMCA adult services including establishment of new PCP clinics and relationships with urgent care centers.</li>
<li>Engages staff, physicians and community in regard to Business development initiatives.</li>
<li>Responsible for campus planning including medical office buildings and tenants.</li>
<li>Works closely with TMCA physicians and practice administrators with medical office building planning and issue resolution.</li>
<li>Collaborates directly with physicians to discuss growth opportunities related to TMCA strategic plan.</li>
<li>Oversees all business case submissions to Sr. management and HealthOne.</li>
</ul>
