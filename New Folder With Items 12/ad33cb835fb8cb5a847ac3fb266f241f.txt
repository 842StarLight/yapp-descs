<h2>Front End Developer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Position Description</b></p>
<p>
Ally is seeking an innovative and seasoned Mid-Level Front-End Developer to help build the next generation of our award-winning customer facing experiences used by millions of customers. The ideal candidate should be passionate about front-end technology, great user experience and have extensive experience developing dynamic, responsive web applications. Candidates should have strong HTML5, CSS3 and JavaScript (ES6) skills and experience with enterprise-scale content management systems that enable rapid authoring and delivery of experiences to end users.
</p>
<p><b>Job Responsibilities</b></p>
<p>
As a Mid-Level Front-End Developer, you will help your team on its journey to create engaging sales and marketing experiences across numerous channels. This is a role where your technical skills, ability to communicate effectively, and drive to implement the strategic vision of content managed experiences come together to enable value delivery.</p>
<p>
You will:</p>
<ul>
<li>Participate with a small development team in an Agile Scrum format</li>
<li>Engage with business partners, UX design and authoring teams, and content platform teams to create world-class customer experiences for the digital channel</li>
<li>Develop new features, components, and unit tests for various portions of an experience</li>
<li>Participate in peer code reviews, unit testing, and automation</li>
<li>Actively participate in business requirement reviews, feature and story grooming, test plans and test cases, and performance testing plans</li>
<li>Effectively communicate technical solutions and recommendations to senior leaders</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Qualifications</b></p>
<p><b>
Required Technical Skills</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Expert level experience with browser-based technology, user interface development, responsive web design and engineering cross-browser, cross-device compliant code</li>
<li>Strong working experience with HTML5 and web template engines - Markdown, Handlebars, etc.</li>
<li>Strong working experience with CSS3 and CSS Preprocessors - SASS, LESS, etc.</li>
<li>Strong working experience with JavaScript/ES6</li>
<li>Experience with writing high-quality, semantic, testable, maintainable, and well-documented code</li>
<li>Experience utilizing componentized content authoring and management platforms</li>
<li>Experience creating highly-performant, optimized websites</li>
<li>Experience consuming REST API using asynchronous methods
</li>
<li>Experience with version control (Git/Bitbucket) and branching strategies</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Preferred Technical Skills</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Experience with MVC JavaScript frameworks - React, Vue, Angular, Ember, etc.</li>
<li>Experience with 508 Compliance/Web Accessibility</li>
<li>Experience with command-line utilities, build tools - Webpack, Grunt, Gulp</li>
<li>Experience with NodeJS and server-side JavaScript development
</li>
<li>Enterprise exposure in defect analysis and resolution
</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Behavioral &amp; Communication Skills</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Excellent communication skills at all levels, from team members to senior management</li>
<li>Ability to exchange constructive and respectful feedback, collaborating with other groups on standards/best practices</li>
<li>Must be able to communicate ideas and best practices to more junior developers (the why and the how)</li>
<li>Enthusiasm and a desire to be part of an innovative team</li>
<li>A commitment to quality, and a desire to improve<br>
</li>
</ul>
