<h2>Accounting Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>
<b>Accounting Manager</b><br>
<br>
<br>
Neon One was founded with the mission of becoming the most comprehensive and authentic technology ecosystem for nonprofits. The team has worked for and with nonprofits for decades and is dedicated to transforming the way nonprofit organizations’ missions are supported with software and services built for results. The growing ecosystem, comprised of Neon One companies as well as 30+ technology and service partners, is dedicated to helping nonprofit organizations solve their end-to-end technology needs and grow their missions.<br>
<br>
<br>
We’re a passionate team of dedicated and experienced nonprofit professionals who are constantly working to make the world a better place. We are hiring for an <b>Accounting Manager</b>. This role will report to the Corporate Controller. The Accounting team is currently responsible for the accounting and consolidation of four companies.<br>
<br>
<br>
<b>Responsibilities:</b>
</div>
<ul>
<li>Oversee the monthly close process for a number of key functional areas such as revenue, accruals, fixed assets, intercompany, and equity for four companies</li>
<li>Prepare and review monthly financial statement variance analyses for each company and understand month-over-month and quarter-over-quarter fluxes. Be able to explain activity to management</li>
<li>Play a key role in additional M&amp;A integration and transition of accounting activities for newly acquired companies post acquisition</li>
<li>Responsible for revenue recognition analysis over new streams of revenue and products. Work closely with the Salesforce team to ensure information is flowing properly between Salesforce and NetSuite</li>
<li>Assist in development and implementation of new or enhanced processes in order to improve workflow efficiencies of the Accounting team</li>
<li>Assist with all audit and tax requests</li>
<li>Communicate effectively with non-accounting teams and work together to form best-practice processes. Collaborate with other departments including Sales, Product and Engineering.</li>
<li>Specific projects and other ad hoc assignments as required</li>
<li>Assist with coaching and mentoring of team</li>
<br>
</ul>
<div>
<br>
<br>
<br>
<b>We’re excited about you if you have:</b>
</div>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s or higher degree in Accounting/Finance</li>
<li>5-8 years of accounting experience</li>
<li>3+ years experience with strong focus on month end close and accounting operations</li>
<li>Solid understanding of accounting ERP systems, preferably NetSuite</li>
<li>Sound understanding of US GAAP and month end close processes</li>
<li>Public accounting and/or public company experience is preferred</li>
<li>CPA is a plus</li>
<li>Comfort in a high-growth, PE backed business environment</li>
<li>A passion for nonprofits</li>
<li>You're seeking to be a part of a collaborative but driven Accounting department</li>
<li>You're a details person with a penchant for preparing meticulously accurate work</li>
<li>You're curious, enthusiastic, adaptable, and see challenges as an opportunity to grow</li>
<li>You thrive in an often-times ambiguous environment</li>
</ul>
</div></div>
