<h2>Senior IT Business Systems Analyst (CRM)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div><div>
<b>Genetech</b> is looking for a <b>Senior IT Business Systems Analyst (CRM)</b> in <b>South San Francisco, CA</b>. It is a 6 to 36 months contract on W2.<br>
<br>
The Senior IT Business Systems Analyst (Sr. BSA) will be responsible for working closely with partners across the Genentech Commercial organization supporting the Patient Engagement Portfolio. At a high-level, Sr. BSA will need to understand strategic drivers and tactical needs to help deliver a solution that meets the business need with the necessary stability, usability, and scalability.<br>
<br>
<b>IT BSA Duties</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Facilitate workshops - story-boarding workshops, translate story-boarding artifacts into an electronic format and provide input in design sessions</li>
<li>Communicate keeping IT and business partners apprised of developments and requirements, key metrics</li>
<li>Analyze and document current and future business process models, specifications, business rules, etc</li>
<li>Analyze and document data requirements (from a business needs perspective)</li>
<li>Use Agile constructs and tools to create user personas, user stories, epics, and other artifacts and iterate on Minimum Viable Product (MVP) solutions</li>
<li>Plan and manage user acceptance test (UAT), create UAT scripts and facilitate UAT; report out on UAT progress and outcomes</li>
<li>Collaborate with business partners, IT Project Managers, User Experience Designers, Solution Architects, Developers &amp; Software Quality Assurance on scope, solutions, constraints, and risks</li>
<li>Mentor the teams on UX best practices and oversee the creation of delightful prototypes &amp; dashboards</li>
<li>Perform knowledge transfer to training and communication teams</li>
<li>Support user deployment activities as needed</li>
<li>Lead business stakeholders and other business systems analysts thru the business analysis lifecycle</li>
<li>Work closely with Project Manager, Technical Lead, Developers, Software Quality Assurance Lead to accomplish a successful project delivery, focused on Minimum Viable Product (MVP)</li>
</ul>
<br>
<b>Qualifications</b>:<br>
<ul>
<li>6+ experience analyzing business requirements and support information systems projects</li>
<li>Proven leadership abilities and emotional intelligence</li>
<li>Demonstrated success handling initiatives of high complexity and risk</li>
<li>Salesforce.com models experience - specifically around Workforce planning, digital Omni-Channel, such as text, chat, email</li>
<li>Implementation of Health Care related Customer Relationship Management (CRM) systems</li>
<li>Vendor Assessment (help develop assessment criteria, distill assessment and report out final analysis to the team and stakeholders )</li>
<li>Strong skills in critical thinking and analysis</li>
<li>Pharma, Life sciences industry, pharma sales data, and commercial business and process knowledge preferred</li>
<li>Support business partner on the development of Organizational Change Management (OCM) deliverables</li>
<li>Extensive Agile Methodology experience a strong +</li>
</ul>
<br>
<b>Education</b>:<br>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor's degree or equivalent experience in business administration, systems design, or equal field</li>
<li>Post-graduate work in a related field preferred</li>
</ul>
