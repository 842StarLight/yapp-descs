<h2>Regional Service Line Manager - Neuroscience / Oncology / Cardiovascular</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<b>Position Overview:</b><br>
<div>The <b>Sutter Medical Foundation (SMF) Regional Service Line Manager</b> provides overall proactive management and day-to-day operational support of the activities and resources of the clinics within Sutter Medical Foundation. The incumbent operationalizes marketing and business plans to ensure service growth and develops an effective team to provide cost effective, quality services which result in patient, payer, employer, physician and employee satisfaction. The Regional Service Line Manager provides support in planning, organizing, staffing and controlling the operational functions related to the service line in all facilities throughout SMF. The incumbent ensures optimal provision and coordination of services and resources across the assigned area and implements various cost savings components to operations and standardizes processes where appropriate. Provides direction for all of the applicable SMF Service Line.</div>
