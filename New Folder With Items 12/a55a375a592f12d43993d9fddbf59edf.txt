We’re in it to win it. Today we’re the #1 global provider of fleet management solutions for both enterprise and small/medium businesses. <p>
As a top 20 SaaS leader, we know our talent is the most important component to our success. We hire top talent and empower them to do their best work. As a division of Verizon, we combine the fun and excitement of a start-up environment with the resources, operational excellence, and brand recognition of an established tech giant.</p>
<p>
Responsibilities:</p>
<p>
Develop the marketing technology strategy and roadmap to support customer and marketing goals including growth, loyalty and revenue. The position leads a team that manages the design, development, integration, optimization, and maintenance of platforms to support critical marketing functions. These include, but are not limited to, campaign automation and management, master data management, segmentation and CRM platforms. Oversee implementation of marketing technology initiatives by working closely with internal customers, development teams and vendors. Actively partner with end users, technical teams and vendors to drive accountability for on time delivery. Manage implementation process of marketing automation systems, guiding cross-departmental teams through training, integration, and program build out / roll out to sales teams. Develop, manage and execute best practices within marketing automation systems data flow efficiency and speed-to-lead. Possess a strong understanding of business needs and marketing strategy to proactively identify new functionality and capabilities that will be useful for marketing teams to meet their objectives. Lead a team of professionals as well as effectively influence others within cross functional teams, some at senior level positions within the company. The ideal candidate should have the experience and ability to comfortably work with both marketing and technical teams and be able to think strategically and act tactically to drive the necessary actions required in order to meet the strategic goals of the business.</p>
