<h2>General Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>QUALIFICATIONS:</b></p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
Strong communicator and connector who can rally the team, resolve conflicts and continually motivate and develop staff</p>
<p>
Experience with employee scheduling and cash handling</p>
<p>
Strategic minded, business development driven</p>
<p>
Demonstrated leadership in safety in a customer-centric environment</p>
<p>
Proven track record in budgeting, P&amp;L, labor management</p>
<p>
Focused on continuous improvement of the customer experience</p>
<p>
Friendly, empathetic and consistent personality</p>
<p>
Ability to work nights, weekends and holidays</p>
<p>
Organizational, follow-up, and multitasking skills are a must</p>
<p>
Eagerness to train, coach and develop fellow managers and part-time crew</p>
<p>
Experience managing a facility with more than $1 million in annual revenue is preferred</p>
<p>
Two years minimum of management experience required</p>
<p>
A bachelors degree in business management, hospitality or food service management is preferred</p>
<p>
ServSafe Food Handlers Certificate, First Aid and CPR Certification (or willingness to obtain)</p>
<p>
Above all this position requires a fun fueled spirit and a dedicated team player</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
JOB RESPONSIBILITIES AND NECESSARY SKILLS:</b></p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Strategic Financial Management: </b>Responsible for short and long-term profitability of Amp Up. Active involvement in sales strategy. Delivers value through strong financial management and drives revenue, profitability and market share. Reviews and authorizes budgets on an ongoing basis.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Customer Service Hero: </b>Ensure positive guest experiences and utilize guest feedback for continuous improvement. Ensure all safety rules are communicated. Serve as a brand ambassador to ensure the high standards of Amp Up are delivered in every touch point of the customer experience. Ensure every crew member wears a smile on duty. Ultimately responsible for guest reviews and responsiveness the General Manager will be empowered to make things right.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Leadership: </b>Be visible, proactive and a personally involved leader. Strive to unify the employees of Amp Up and create a team atmosphere that attracts and retains top talent. Select, train and manage the talent pool to optimize labor between departments and ensure cross training and internal development. Develop team solutions and promote ideas for continuous improvement. Opening and closing the facility as the primary Manager on Duty, and performing the duties of fellow managers or crew members when necessary. Maintaining Amp Up policies and procedures in all aspects of the business.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Relationship Builder:</b> Display proactive communications with the owners of Amp Up, including regular evaluations of performance against objectives. Identify and build strategic partnerships with charitable and for-profit organizations to engage Amp Up in the broader community. Manage the companys reputation online and in the St. Louis region.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Business Development: </b>Set marketing and sales goals and manage the advertising budget. Work closely with local media outlets and marketing representatives. Develop, coordinate and track all sales and marketing strategies. Drive group sales and corporate events. Develop and maintain relationships with key influencers such as schools, churches, corporations, nonprofits. Maintain and protect customer and email data and oversee digital media strategies. Identify, research and grow our business and increase community engagement.</p>
