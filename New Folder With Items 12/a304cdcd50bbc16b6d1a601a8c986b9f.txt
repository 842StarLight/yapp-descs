<h2>Marketing Coordinator for Orthopedic Medical Device Company</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Summary: The marketing coordinator will interface with Sales and Marketing, Research and Development and Operations to support sales and marketing initiatives in addition to developing and deploying marketing programs ranging from social media campaigns to trade show planning and coordination.</p>
<p>Job Duties:</p>
<p>Assist the Sales and Marketing Manager with the development of, but not limited too, ads (print and online), sales aids, trade show materials, surgical technique guides, promotional materials, patient education materials and product packaging.</p>
<p>Assist in product launch planning and execution.</p>
<p>Manage the company website, online advertising and social media presence.</p>
<p>Plan and coordinate meetings and trade shows.</p>
<p>Support the development of advertising collateral for traditional and online media with 3rd party vendors.</p>
<p>Manage the development of product packaging and supporting material for regulatory compliance.</p>
<p>Assist the sales team via marketing collateral and in-office support when needed.</p>
<p>Qualifications:</p>
<p>BA/BS in a related field.</p>
<p>1-2 years of experience in medical device marketing or communication preferred.</p>
<p>Excellent written and verbal skills.</p>
<p>Strong knowledge of online marketing ranging from website development to social media management.</p>
<p>Strong organizational management.</p>
<p>Working knowledge of graphics and video editing software.</p>
<p>Demonstrated track record of successful marketing projects.</p>
<p>Ability to understand and communicate technical details related to surgery and surgical products.</p>
<p>Comfortable in surgical environment.</p>
