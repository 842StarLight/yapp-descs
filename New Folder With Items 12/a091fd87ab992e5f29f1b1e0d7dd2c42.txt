<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Company Description</b></h3>
<p>
Hamilton Company is a global enterprise headquartered in Reno, Nevada with manufacturing facilities in Reno, Boston and Bonaduz, Switzerland. Branch sales offices are located in France, Germany, and the United Kingdom.</p>
<p>
We are the worldwide leader in the design and manufacture of manual, semi-automated and robotic products for precision fluid measuring. For over 56 years, Hamilton has been satisfying customer needs by combining quality materials with skilled workmanship, ensuring the highest level of performance of every precision fluid measuring device we manufacture.</p>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Job Description</b></h3>
<p>
Hamilton Company is seeking a Field Service Engineer to join our Robotics team, supporting, repairing and maintaining robotic instrumentation. The ideal candidate will work in the field and support areas within the San Francisco Bay area.</p>
<p>
As a Field Service Engineer with Hamilton Company you will be tasked with providing high quality customer support through instrument installation, maintenance and troubleshooting.</p>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Essential Job Functions</b></h3>
<ul>
<li>
Responsible for new equipment installation.</li>
<li>
Perform preventative maintenance.</li>
<li>
Troubleshoot and repair Hamilton equipment.</li>
<li>
Report problems and perform root cause analysis on instrument failures.</li>
</ul>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Knowledge, Skills, and Abilities</b></h3>
<ul>
<li>
A high desire for quality.</li>
<li>
Demonstrated experience working in the field as well as corporate setting.</li>
<li>
Ability to train non-technical personnel on highly technical material.</li>
<li>
Ability and willingness to work in a safety conscious manner necessary due to potential hazardous chemicals and biologicals.</li>
<li>
Ability to learn new robotic operating systems.</li>
</ul>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Education and Experience</b></h3>
<ul>
<li>
Must have a Bachelor's degree in Biomedical Engineering, Electronic Technology or comparable.</li>
<li>
Minimum of 2 years Field Service experience preferably in the biotech / life science field.</li>
<li>
Ability to lift, carry, push, pull or otherwise move approximately 60 lbs.</li>
</ul>
