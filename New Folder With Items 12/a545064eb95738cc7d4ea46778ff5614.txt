<b>Description:</b><br>
Manager of Call Center Operations
<br><br>
For nearly 80 years, Beltone has been “Helping the World Hear Better” and has achieved the highest customer care satisfaction rating of over 95%. A global leader in hearing healthcare, Beltone is currently seeking dynamic and motivated Sales and Customer Service Professionals to join our team and launch a rewarding new career.
<br><br>
Beltone is seeking a skilled salesperson to join the team as the Manager of Call Center Operations in our growing call center. Our business spans seven states and provides the highest levels of service, product integrity, and talent. We’re looking for a Manager to oversee our telemarketing call center. The successful applicant will be responsible for directing representatives’ workflow and workload as well as handling complaints, concerns, and questions from patients. We’re looking for a highly organized professional with management experience who can ensure the most efficient operations of our call center.
