<h2>Senior Manager, FP&A</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p>At Avon, beauty is more than skin deep. We are inspired by our community of passionate women; yesterday, today, and tomorrow. The Avon Nation is all about celebrating each other and our achievements in business and in life, inviting others to join us, and committing to be a force for good.</p>
<p><b>
About the Role</b></p>
<p>
The Senior Manager of FP&amp;A will be responsible for the financial leadership of our US Sales team, supporting the development of revenue projections and management of Sales-related expenses including commissions, incentives, recognition and other costs related to sales drivers. You will be an important contributor who partners with Sales leadership, including the President of Sales.</p>
<p>
You will address the complex nature of the direct selling business and will promote coordination among other Avon teams - both operationally and within Finance – while providing proper analysis and information for decision-making.</p>
<p>
This position reports to the Chief Financial Officer.</p>
<p><b>
Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Provide financial guidance to Sales leadership and Executive Leadership Team (e.g., CEO, CFO, VPs and Department Heads)</li>
<li>
Meet with Sales senior management and dedicated teams to stay abreast of all operations, proposals, risks, and opportunities</li>
<li>
Consult business partners and our leadership team on the use of resources and human capital</li>
<li>
Develop Monthly Forecast, Annual Budget and Long-term plan for the Sales team</li>
<li>
Engage with senior management on reporting and forecasting activities and financial analysis of business proposals</li>
<li>
Oversee activity of Senior Analysts; provide appropriate training, review work, and advise on how best to approach assignments</li>
<li>
Identify opportunities and risks in the daily tracking of expenses</li>
<li>
Month-end close responsibilities which include the review of results and recording of journal entries</li>
<li>
Work with peers and superiors in Finance in relation to division-wide activities and personnel matters</li>
<li>
Build financial models to support forecasting and budgeting</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Qualifications</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
7+ years’ related experience preferably supporting the sales team of a direct selling business</li>
<li>
Bachelor’s Degree from an accredited college or university</li>
<li>
Experience managing direct reports and partnering with senior management</li>
<li>
History leading the process of organizing and controlling the budgeting and internal reporting</li>
<li>
Experience supporting the development of revenue and sales related expense projections</li>
<li>
Proficient in Microsoft Excel (can maintain complex spreadsheets)</li>
<li>
You have used JD Enterprise and Hyperion at a prior employer</li>
</ul>
