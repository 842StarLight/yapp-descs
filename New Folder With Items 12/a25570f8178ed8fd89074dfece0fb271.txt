<h2>Customer Service Representative</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Job Summary</b><br><b>Job Description: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Respond a modest to high amount of support inquiries from or to nonprofit customers as well as to support agents from other collaborating teams; transfer and escalate inquiries to the appropriate support agent(s)/team(s) if needed.</li>
<li>Striving to achieve first call resolution and maintain the quality of service.</li>
<li>Using listening skills to understand the needs and challenges of the customer before offering them the best possible solution to create a positive experience.</li>
<li>Remaining calm and professional while dealing with emotional customers and providing them with the best solutions to resolve their issues.</li>
<li>Joining CSS team to provide escalated support; build a positive relationship of teamwork, trust, and excellence.</li>
<li>Understanding and using the required software, reports, tools, and metrics.</li>
<li>Participating in training and striving to increase and improve existing skills.</li>
<li>Adhering to all company policies and procedures.</li>
</ul>
