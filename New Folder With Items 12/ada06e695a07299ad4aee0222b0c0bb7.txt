Position Overview
<br><br>
NewsCred is seeking an all-star Business Development Manager to lead a team of BDRs who position our solutions to the various markets we serve and deliver highly qualified leads to our sales team. The BDR Manager will need to be self-motivated with a great attitude, a fantastic work ethic, and the desire to build a growth-focused team. The ideal candidate must display excellent communication and relationship-building skills, as well as the drive to turn every conversation into a sale.
<br><br>
The BDR Manager will be responsible for hiring, onboarding, ongoing training and ensuring the BDR team is successful. This role collaborates closely with sales and marketing to maximize the creation of highly qualified sales opportunities. The candidate must be a strong coach &amp; leader, extremely well organized and have a passion for continually improving the team.
<br><br>
What You'll Do
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Manage, hire, and scale a world-class BDR team to overachieve their quota</li>

<li>Influence a repeatable scalable process for generating pipeline as the company scales</li>
<li>Coach and empower your team as they grow their careers. Create and implement training and a clear path of success and promotion for team members</li>
<li>Create and deliver accurate analysis on individual/team results and maintain reporting to accurately track and manage BDR sales metrics</li>
