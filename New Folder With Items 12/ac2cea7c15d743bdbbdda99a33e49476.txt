<h2>Apartment Leasing Professional</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p>Advenir Living (www.advenirliving.com) has several Leasing Professional positions open in the Denver area. If you're looking for a new career, we have it! We offer apartment discounts, 401(k) match, quarterly bonuses, full medical/dental/health, opportunity for advancement and a friendly, fast paced working environment. Previous customer service, sales, or other customer-focused experience preferred. We offer a hourly wage and commission for leases and renewals in addition to great benefits.</p>
<p><b>
Job Summary:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Responsible for leasing, marketing and maintaining positive resident relations for the community.</li>
<li>
Provide reliable and friendly service to our customers by performing all responsibilities with accuracy, completeness, and timeliness. Our customers include residents, Advenir associates, vendors and investors.</li>
<li>
Answer incoming phone calls and emails timely, entering data into our property management software system and maintaining lease files with all required documentation.</li>
<li>
Ensure curb appeal, leasing office, clubhouse, amenities and vacant apartments are clean and present a warm and welcoming image of the property.</li>
<li>
Completing all additional tasks, projects, and responsibilities as assigned.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Job Requirements:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Ability to work a flexible schedule, including weekends. Understand that your scheduled work days may vary according to staffing and property needs.</li>
<li>
Ensure a professional image as dictated by the associate dress code guidelines.</li>
<li>
Friendly and outgoing, with the ability to work in a fast paced and busy office setting.</li>
<li>
Organized, detailed and computer literate.</li>
<li>
Communicate effectively using oral and written communication.</li>
<li>
Ability to perform the functions of the position, which may include but are not limited to: ability to walk property, including up to 4 flights of stairs, complete physical inspections, deliver resident communications, show apartments and drive a vehicle.</li>
<li>
Complete and pass a background and drug test. We do test for marijuana and we are a drug-free workplace.</li>
<li>
Have a valid, active driver's license and reliable transportation.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Advenir is an equal opportunity employer and is a drug free workplace.</b></p>
