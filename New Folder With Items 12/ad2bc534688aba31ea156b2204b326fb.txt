<p><b>Marketing Senior Data Analyst / Associate Job Description</b></p>
<p>
Be an integral part of the marketing team of a groundbreaking e-commerce company taking the $5.1B rug market to the next level!</p>
<p><b>
About Ruggable</b></p>
<p>
Ruggable is a Los Angeles-based, venture-backed e-commerce company revolutionizing the market for residential rugs. Our patented 2-Piece Rug System allows you to remove the top layer of your rug and wash it in your home washing machine, offering an affordable, stylish and convenient solution for young families, pet owners, and busy individuals. We're a small, entrepreneurial team with big ambitions. We have a strong brand, established traction, and amazing advisors fueling our steady growth. This is an opportunity to get in at the ground floor of a quickly growing e-commerce startup at a very exciting time.</p>
<p><b>
The Marketing Team</b></p>
<p>
The Ruggable Marketing Team is collaborative and dynamic with clear growth targets in mind. Working closely with the Design, Operations, and Executive teams, the team aims to exceed revenue targets, elevate our brand status and broaden our customer appeal. We learn fast to adapt to the ever-changing e-commerce landscape.</p>
<p><b>
Job Summary</b></p>
<p>
We are currently seeking a Marketing Data Senior Analyst and/or Associate to drive marketing reporting needs and projects. This role needs a detail oriented, analytical, strategic and creative thinker who is driven by data, is goal-oriented, and has a passion for working in a fast-paced environment. This candidate will be analyzing data and providing guidance to the marketing team with actionable insights to help optimize campaigns.</p>
<p>
In this role, you will have the opportunity to help determine measurable KPI metrics, build custom reports, and collaborate with different team members. The right candidate will be excited about meeting aggressive goals, and staying on top of a quickly evolving ad landscape.</p>
<p><b>
Responsibilities and Duties</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Run ongoing and ad-hoc analysis and reports to support the marketing team</li>
<li>
Give insight into performance trends, working closely with media teams to analyze the impact of marketing optimizations, promotions, campaigns, and macro factors</li>
<li>
Provide actionable recommendations and conclusions from data to shape media strategy and investments</li>
<li>
Build and maintain automated reporting dashboards that stakeholders in the marketing team will use on a daily basis</li>
<li>
Help develop frameworks, models, tools, and processes to ensure data influences decisions at all levels within different marketing channels</li>
<li>
Collaborate with marketing leadership to develop strategy, goals and KPIs through the entire marketing funnel from awareness to purchase</li>
<li>
Collaborate with data and consumer insights teams to drive actionable decisions that improve the performance of our marketing campaigns</li>
<li>
Present data and findings to cross functional team members</li>
<li>
Lead and inspire an analytical driven approach to other members of the marketing team</li>
<li>
Audit existing analytics and tagging implementation across online media channels</li>
<li>
Seek out opportunities to mentor junior team members on analytics and best practices</li>
<li>
Perform ad-hoc analysis as necessary</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Qualifications and Skills</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Proven track record of turning raw data into meaningful analysis, presenting to business stakeholders, and driving value for the business</li>
<li>
Advanced use of Google Analytics, Shopify, Google Ads, Facebook Ads Manager, and other media or data platforms preferred</li>
<li>
Advanced ability in analyzing marketing channel performance with expertise in evaluating channel mix and attribution models</li>
<li>
Advanced knowledge of Microsoft Excel and Google Sheets.</li>
<li>
Analytical with strong quantitative skills.</li>
<li>
2+ years of hands on experience working on an analytics driven role within an organization</li>
<li>
Team-player, hard-working, organized, and goal-oriented.</li>
<li>
Excitement for start-ups and building from the ground up</li>
<li>
Experience with Tableau or similar business intelligence/data visualization tools is a plus</li>
<li>
Knowledge of SQL with relational databases including data extraction and manipulation is a plus</li>
<li>
Bachelor's Degree or equivalent work experience</li>
</ul>
