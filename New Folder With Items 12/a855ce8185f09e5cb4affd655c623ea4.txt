<h2>Jr. Designer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Premier Packaging</b> provides innovative packaging solutions through a combination of curiosity, drive, and a culture that embraces an entrepreneurial spirit. We are responsive to our customers’ needs, responsible to one another and the environment, and improve our processes to deliver quality products. We ask ‘Why’ so we can deliver the ‘How’.</p>
<p>
The <b>Junior Designer</b> position reports to the Design Lab Supervisor. This position is responsible for creating designs, drawings and prototype samples utilizing ArtiosCad software and Kongsberg cutting table. Satisfies customer’s packaging needs by designing functional corrugated and protective foam packaging; and incorporates other materials (i.e. outsourced plastic display components, snap-rivets, blister or thermoformed items, chipboard, etc.) as relevant and necessary. Designs according to GMP and machinery limitations of the internal plant, and external outsource vendors and suppliers. This position will be responsible for the direct design support for our Ohio Valley Division.</p>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
ESSENTIAL FUNCTIONS</b></h2>
<ul>
<li>Acts as a supportive interface between sales, customer service, and production</li>
<li>Initiates and/or participates in project management and process improvement meetings as necessary</li>
<li>Creates designs, drawings and prototype samples utilizing ArtiosCad software and Kongsberg cutting table</li>
<li>Works closely with Design Technician to gain an understanding of best Design/Assembly practices</li>
<li>Designs according to GMP and machinery limitations of internal plant, and external outsource vendors, and suppliers</li>
<li>Possesses or develops competency in company's management system software – ASI and/or Epicor</li>
<li>Possesses or develops general graphics knowledge as relating to structural packaging design</li>
<li>Uses drop tester and vibration table as needed to validate designs</li>
<li>Maintains orderliness and cleanliness of design areas</li>
<li>Processes new orders (NOCs) and change orders according to procedure</li>
<li>Orders and/or checks in cutting dies and printing plates in absence of tooling coordinator</li>
<li>Performs other duties as assigned by department manager</li>
<li>Takes direction from Design Lab Supervisor with regards to training/processes</li>
<br>
</ul>
