<h2>Marketing Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Primary Responsibilities
</p>
<ul>
<li>Supporting sales and lead generation efforts.</li>
<li>Creating promotions with advertising.</li>
<li>Understanding and developing budgets, including expenditures, research and development.</li>
<li>Compiling lists describing organization's offerings.</li>
<li>Developing and managing advertising campaigns.</li>
<li>Organizing company conferences, trade shows, and major events.</li>
<li>Building brand awareness and positioning.</li>
<li>Evaluating and maintaining a marketing strategy.</li>
<li>Directing, planning and coordinating marketing efforts.</li>
<li>Communicating the marketing plan.</li>
<li>Researching demand for the organization's products and services.</li>
<li>Evaluating competitors.</li>
<li>Handling social media, public relation efforts, and content marketing.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p>
</p>Secondary Responsibilities
<ul>
<li>Obtains market share by developing marketing plans and programs for each product and directing promotional support.</li>
<li>Influences present and future products by determining and evaluating current and future market trends.</li>
<li>Develops new uses for existing products by analyzing statistics regarding market development; acquiring and analyzing data; and consulting with internal and external sources.</li>
</ul>
</div></div>
