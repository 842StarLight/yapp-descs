<h2>Legal Assistant</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>
<p><b>Duties &amp; Responsibilities:</b></p>
<p>
Residential real estate closings</p>
<p>
Perform all administrative tasks relating to sales of newly constructed homes from contract preparation through closing including:</p>
<ul>
<li>Coordinate with other parties (buyer, seller, Title Company, opposing counsel, lender).</li>
<li>Take comments about purchase agreement and revise as necessary.</li>
<li>Arrange for execution of purchase agreement.</li>
<li>Review purchase agreements for critical dates and contingencies and calendar same</li>
<li>Prepare all documents</li>
<li>Conduct closings</li>
<li>Monitor and maintain various status reports on progress of house construction and sales.</li>
</ul>
<p>Tenant evictions and collections</p>
<ul>
<li>Coordinate with Collections Manager all administrative tasks relating to the summary eviction of residential tenants in default and collection of outstanding accounts from delinquent tenants.</li>
<li>Monitor and maintain tracking and status reporting for all tenant legal matters.</li>
</ul>
<p>Additional Responsibilities:</p>
<p>
Maintain checking and escrow accounts (accounting department to reconcile statements)</p>
<p>
Prepare monthly expense reports and biweekly time sheets.</p>
<p>
Reconcile and code receipts for monthly credit card statements</p>
<p>
Perform other related duties as required and assigned.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Knowledge &amp; Skills:</b></p>
<p>
Must be self-starter with the ability to maintain continual attention to detail and ability to follow through on projects to completion.</p>
<p>
Must have high level of interpersonal skills to tactfully and diplomatically interact with buyers, sellers, attorneys, lenders, and municipal offices.</p>
<p>
Must have excellent command of the English language for written correspondence.</p>
<p>
Must be proficient in use of general office equipment and software applications including Microsoft Word, Excel, and Outlook.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Education/Work Experience:</b></p>
<p>
Associates Degree in related field or Paralegal certification.</p>
<p>
Minimum of 3 years of prior experience as a legal secretary/paralegal for a real estate company or real estate services of law firm.</p>
