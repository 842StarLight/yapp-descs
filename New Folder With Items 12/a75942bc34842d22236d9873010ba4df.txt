<h2>Project Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>Visions Inc provides marketing and print solutions to clients ranging from non-profit to Fortune 500 accounts. We are just the right size for you to find your fit and personal passion with a team of dependable, innovative thinkers who are developing their careers within a diverse, inclusive empowering environment.<br>
<br>
</div>
<div>In this position you will serves as primary customer liaison on all production jobs and backs up sales executives to meet customer requirements. Enter job specifications into system to generate detailed job tickets utilizing knowledge of production process, machine capabilities and industry terminology. Monitors job throughout production stages to meets target ship dates. Problem solves and addresses customer complaints or dissatisfaction.</div>
<div>
</div>

Duties and Responsibilities:</b></div>

<li>
Understand basic production process, machine capabilities and industry terminology</li>
<li>
Basic specialized software functionality, quotes, jobs, die look up &amp; inventory</li>
<li>
Understand workflow throughout office and production areas</li>
<li>
Understand &amp; communicate customer information</li>
