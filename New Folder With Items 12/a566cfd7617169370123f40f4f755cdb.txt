<p><b>Job Description - Brand Ambassador (Thousand Oaks &amp; surrounding areas)</b></p>
<p><b>Rockwood Shutters, Blinds and Shades </b>has an immediate opening for a <b>Brand Ambassador</b>. The successful candidate will be responsible for managing the creation and development of store support for our service provider, as well as social media and online positioning for Rockwood. This full-time job comes with excellent pay and benefits.</p>
<p><b>About Our Company</b></p>
<p><b>Rockwood </b>is a Texas-based manufacturer of premium interior plantation shutters, custom-designed to each client’s specifications. We are proud to be an exemplary Service Provider for <b>The Home Depot</b>, the world’s largest home improvement specialty retailer. In addition to our high-quality plantation shutters, we offer the latest trends in blinds and shades, featuring the amazing <b>Kirsch by Hunter Douglas </b>brand. As a leader in our industry, we have developed a refined and proven sales and service model that complements our beautifully crafted window covering products, and we stand behind everything we do. We have an extraordinary company culture and our work environment is a major factor in our success.</p>
<p><b>Your Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Weekly visits providing store support and motivation to our 18 Home Depot Store partners</li>
<li>Engage with store associates and department leads bringing awareness of the Rockwood program and lead generating</li>
<li>Contribute to social media accounts to posting visits</li>
<li>Advise sales reps on content marketing campaigns and product positioning logistics</li>
<li>Measure and report lead to sales strategies based on store metrics and scorecards, and make necessary adjustments</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Candidate Qualities and Job Requirements</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Experience in marketing or advertising</li>
<li>Excellent communication and problem-solving skills</li>
<li>Active presence on social media a plus</li>
<li>Ability to learn product details quickly and translate what you’ve learned to content marketing strategies</li>
<li>Familiarity with social media metric tracking preferred</li>
<li>Leads to sales tracking</li>
<li>Superior organization and planning skills</li>
<li>Outstanding interpersonal skills and unwavering dedication to customer service</li>
<li>High standard of integrity and strong work ethic</li>

</ul>
