<h2>Account Executive</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div></div>
<div><div>
<div>
<div>Hylink works with industries of all shapes and sizes. We service direct clients in North America, and China, which makes up most of our core business. We also partner with key media and creative agencies which is paramount to our success.</div>
<div>
 As an Account Executive, you will provide the client with analytical and sales power required to drive advertiser, acquisition and overall business growth of your portfolio. You'll collaborate closely with senior Hylink team members, identify sales and business development opportunities, and share Hylink’s client resources to internal teams. You will evangelize new Hylink products and technologies throughout the agency, and develop and manage strong relationships with internal agency influencers and decision-makers, driving scalable product adoption and growth across the broader portfolio of accounts.
 </div>
<div>Reporting directly to senior agency staff, you will learn the skills necessary to succeed in advertising, as well as understand emerging business models as they happen. Your knowledge of online media combined with your communication skills and analytical abilities shapes how new and existing business grows. You will be involved with a team of fast-paced, multitasking high-achievers. Covering training across programmatic, digital, industry analysis, and client service, you will develop influential, relationship-building skills, learn to provide Hylink-caliber client service, and fully grasp research and market analysis.
 </div>
<div>You will anticipate how decisions are made, and persistently explore and uncover the business needs of Hylink's key partners and objectives. Your responsibilities include, but are not limited to:</div>
</div>
<div><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Responsibilities</b></h3>
<div><ul>
<li>
Coordinate day-to-day as well as long-term schedules of key agency management.</li>
<li>
Execute self-service platform campaign buys with training (eg. Google, Facebook, etc.).</li>
<li>
Conduct competitive, industry, or client research for presentations, pitches, or industry analysis.</li>
<li>
Develop briefs, client reports, and key agency documentation in a timely manner.</li>
<li>
Assist various senior staff in execution of internal agency projects.</li>
<li>
Develop client-facing relationship and servicing skills to better provide efficient deployment to our partners and clients.</li>
<li>
Help manage daily business of account(s) with responsibility for ensuring that work is completed on-time and on-budget.</li>
<li>
Support the execution of strategy, provide brand stewardship and maintain strong client satisfaction on assigned brand(s).</li>
</ul></div>
</div></div>
<div><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Preferred Qualifications</b></h3>
<div><ul>
<li>
3-4 years experience in an agency, tech startup, marketing, integrated marketing communications, or media sales role.
</li>
<li>Graduate of a Bachelor’s, Master’s, or Associate’s degree program focused on Business, Communication, Marketing, and Market Research fields.</li>
<li>
Ability to travel up to 10% of the time.</li>
<li>
A deep understanding of the industry's issues, a vision for its growth, and a commitment to advance Hylink's forward-looking strategies within the marketplace.</li>
<li>
Balanced team player dedicated to client service.</li>
<li>
Strong presentation, analytical and problem solving skills.</li>
<li>
Demonstrated ability to lead and manage multiple projects with a broad range of internal and external constituents simultaneously.</li>
<li>
Demonstrated ability to think strategically about complex issues and develop recommendations and action plans.</li>
</ul></div>
