<h2>Application Specialist (for paint not IT)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>(This is </b><b>NOT</b><b> an IT or IS job in the computer field)</b><br>
<br>
<b>Title: </b>Application Specialist I</p>
<p><b>
Reports to:</b> Product Director</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Position Objective:</b></p>
<p>
The Application Specialist will work with and support both the Product Director &amp; V.P. of Sales in the delivery of their objectives, plus the Application Specialist drives preference for Wagner/Titan's technical products by providing product and application expertise, specification, support and training. The Application Specialists primary focus is supporting the technical commercialization of all Home &amp; Professional Products. The Application Specialist will fill this role by working closely with PS&amp;P personnel, Sales Managers, Business Development Specialists, Marketing Team, Distributors, OEM's, Integrators, Material Suppliers and End Users.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Responsibilities:</b></p>
<p><b>
% of time</b><b> </b><b>Priority Order</b><b>
</b></p>
<p>20% 1. In conjunction with Account Management and Channel Marketing, the incumbent works closely with coatings manufacturers to make recommendations on ideal product application tools for their individual coating.</p>
<p>
20% 2. Supports Account Management group as it relates to joint ventures with key coatings manufactures and retailers. Supports account management with key customer demonstrations.</p>
<p>
15% 3. Works closely with end-users and distributors to recognize new application opportunities. I.e. new sprayers and accessories, new technologies, and new product niches</p>
<p>
15% 4. Works with Product Management on the coordination and execution of all field-testing for assigned product categories</p>
<p>
15% 5. Evaluates competitive product in various application scenarios so that we have a database of competitive benchmarking.
</p>
<p>10% 6. Assists Product Management in the creation and execution of sales and customer training programs. Supports Product Management in development activities as needed</p>
<p>
5% 7. Manages the scheduling and cleaning of the demo lab</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Relationship to Others:</b></p>
<p>
Work in a team manner throughout the organization to accomplish category goals and objectives. Play an active and participative role in the implementation of category plans for their respective categories throughout the organization, with special attention to new product development and the Product Creation Process. This will require strong interaction with Account Management, Channel Marketing, Marketing Communications, Purchasing, Manufacturing, Quality, and Finance.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Dimensions of Position:</b></p>
<p>
Work in direct communications with consumers, retail customers, sales organization, and vendors. The Application Specialist will work with confidential information, including company financials and costing data.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Knowledge, Skills, and Abilities:</b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Education:</b></p>
<p>
Bachelor's degree in business administration, management, marketing, engineering, operations, or related field is preferred. Associate level degree in business or related field is required. Formal leadership and/or management training is a strong plus.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Experience</b></p>
<p>
Three to five years of professional painting experience is preferred. One to two years of marketing/product management/sales within a consumer durables or related business is required. Some experience working with big box DIY retailers, specialty hardware, paint channel outlets, and/or contractors preferred. Knowledge of the fluid handling industry.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Skills</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Must have strong initiative, energy, and positive attitude.</li>
</ul>
<ul><li>Willingness to travel up to 15-20 % of the time</li></ul>
