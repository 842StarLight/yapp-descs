<p><b>Community Leader</b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
About us - The Maverick Oath:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
I will take care of every Maverick.</li>
<li>I will take care of every Prospective and Current Resident. Here at Maverick Residential, our core philosophy is People First. Mavericks are ambitious, innovative, and lead passionately. If you are looking for a place to build your career and make a genuine impact on people's lives, welcome home! At Maverick Residential, we challenge our teams to focus on customer service by utilizing technology to its fullest potential. The Community Leader is the gate keeper to community success!</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>
What you'll do at Maverick:</b> At Maverick Residential, we challenge our teams to focus on customer service by utilizing technology to its fullest potential.</p>
<ul>
<li>
Drive optimal P&amp;L performance of community through managing daily operations directly or indirectly.</li>
<li>Be knowledgeable of provisions in standard lease documents and maintain a working knowledge of resident rules and regulations.</li>
<li>Oversee all day to day property management functions in assigned community, including but not limited to collecting rents, marketing, showings, application gathering and review, lease renewals and general resident management.
</li>
<li>Lead property management team in maintaining a high degree of customer service and competency level in all resident interactions, whether over the phone or email, through formal written communication or in person.</li>
<li>Reviews and analyzes monthly performance; completes meaningful variance reports; initiates annual budgets and forecasts.</li>
<li>Identifies potential market challenges; educates self, staff and Vice President Operations about competitors and conditions in the marketplace; creates effective marketing plans.</li>
<li>Maintain community appearance and ensure repairs are noted and completed on timely basis. This requires regular community inspections and tours.</li>
<li>Ability to manage a team towards long- and short-term goals, ensuring the organization is successful in its endeavors.</li>
<li>Supervises entire on-site staff Provide support to staff to encourage team work and lead as an example in creating a harmonious environment.</li>
<li>Ensure efficiency of staff through ongoing training, instruction, counseling and leadership.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Job Requirements</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Ability to work in a fast-paced environment.</li>
<li>Hold a minimum education of high school diploma/GED.</li>
<li>Physically inspects community when on grounds, picks up litter and reports any service needs to maintenance staff. Inspects Move ins, move-outs and vacancies.
</li>
<li>Ability to utilize technology for marketing, advertising, and sales-related circumstances, including using social media, reputation management, Google and other search engines, and navigating the internet and websites.
</li>
<li>Using computers and tablets to organize resident files on a shared cloud environment and comfort with mobile optimized platforms.</li>
<li>Rise to the challenge of working on a lease incentive structure based on performance. This is in addition to your base pay!</li>
<li>Effectively convey ideas, images and goals to a diverse group of personalities.</li>
<li>Must possess a positive attitude and the ability to smile under all circumstances.</li>
<li>Proven effective Supervisor of people, ability to clearly communicate to team member the goals of the organization.</li>
<li>Ability to work flexible hours which may include weekends and evenings to meet resident needs.
</li>
<li>Strong skills with MS office products including Excel, Entrata Property Management software experience a plus.</li>
</ul>
