<h2>Mid-Shift PIC</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>MID-SHIFT PIC: $9.00/ hour starting wage, no bonus</b></p>
<p><b>
5-28 hours per week</b></p>
<p><b>
Reports to: Assistant, GM, Area Manager</b></p>
<p><b>
Job Summary: </b>Bridges time between shifts so the GM and Assistant can keep their hours to 47 hours per week. Is not responsible for running entire shifts, but must be able to maintain shift operations and complete shift paperwork like a PIC as needed Mid-shift PIC schedules will be discussed on a person-to-person basis, as the requirements of each location will differ.</p>
<p><b>
Duties and Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Manages a staff of approximately 3 to 15 employees. Assigns, oversees and evaluates work. Recommends promotion, transfer, or termination of employees based on performance.</li>
<li>
Provides on-the-job training for new employees.</li>
<li>
Delegates and is responsible for the organization, receipt, storage and issuing of food, labor, equipment, cleaning, and paper supplies for the unit to ensure a minimum loss from waste or theft.</li>
<li>
Assists in the supervision of the preparation, sales, and service of food.</li>
<li>
Forecasts food items. Estimates what amount of each food item will be consumed per shift.</li>
<li>
Supervises food preparation and service operations while on duty. Assists inshoppers during rush periods to ensure the maintenance of restaurant efficiency.</li>
<li>
Assists inshoppers in greeting customers, taking orders, using the cash register, assembling order and checking for completeness and correctness.</li>
<li>
Ensure that every customer receives world-class customer service.</li>
<li>
Route deliveries and serve drivers to maximize delivery business and speed.</li>
<li>
Completes daily food preparation (meat and vegetable slicing, bread production) as needed.</li>
<li>
Executes systems and procedures with 100% integrity and completeness.</li>
<li>
Completes daily and weekly paperwork as needed.</li>
<li>
Responsible for 100% of the cash drawers at all times during the shift.</li>
<li>
Receives and stores product as needed.</li>
<li>
Audits previous shift’s systems and procedures for 100% integrity and completeness as needed.</li>
<li>
Completes preventive maintenance and upkeep on store’s equipment and supplies.</li>
<li>
Performs other related duties as required.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Knowledge, Skills, and Abilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Must be able to read and write and communicate in English.</li>
<li>
Ability to establish priorities, work independently, and proceed with objectives with minimal supervision.</li>
<li>
Ability to handle and resolve customer threats and issues.</li>
<li>
Ability to handle and resolve employee issues.</li>
<li>
Skills to use a personal computer and various software packages (Word/Excel).</li>
<li>
Ability to handle stress and high-volume operations.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Special Requirements: </b>Willing to work holidays and weekends as business dictates. Position requires bending, standing, and walking the entire workday. Must be able to lift 50 pounds. Must be at least 18 years of age.</p>
