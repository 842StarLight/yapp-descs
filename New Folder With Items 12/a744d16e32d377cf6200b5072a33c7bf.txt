<h2>Machine Operator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<b>Company Overview:</b><br>
Brady Corporation is an international manufacturer and marketer of complete solutions that identify and protect people, products and places. Brady's products help customers increase safety, security, productivity and performance and include high-performance labels, signs, safety devices, printing systems and software. Founded in 1914, the Company has a diverse customer base in electronics, telecommunications, manufacturing, electrical, construction, medical, aerospace and a variety of other industries. Brady is headquartered in Milwaukee, Wisconsin and as of July 31, 2018, employed approximately 6,200 people in its worldwide businesses. Brady's fiscal 2018 sales were approximately $1.17 billion. Brady stock trades on the New York Stock Exchange under the symbol BRC. More information is available at www.bradycorp.com.
<br><br>
The Machine Operator will be responsible for operating equipment in the areas of coating, compounding, and/or converting. This position is responsible for the production of valuable, defect-free products that meet or exceed our customers' expectations. The Machine Operator will be successful in this position by executing to standard work and adhering to all company policies.
<br><br>
<b>Essential Duties and Responsibilities:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Perform production equipment set up, clean up &amp; operation</li>
<li>Weigh, catalyze &amp; mix coatings</li>
<li>Evaluate and Tag Out Defects as necessary</li>
<li>Prepare pump &amp; filtration systems for production process</li>
<li>Operate material handling equipment</li>
<li>Follow detailed written product specifications</li>
<li>Perform in-process inspection</li>
<li>Perform operator level Quality testing to maintain product quality</li>
<li>Set-up, cleanup and operator level maintenance of assigned machines and equipment</li>
<li>Make on the fly process adjustments to maintain product quality</li>
<li>Perform roll changes using material handling equipment and web threading</li>
<li>Participate actively in Gain Sharing &amp; Business Goals</li>
<li>Comprehend &amp; adhere to equipment &amp; material specification</li>
<li>Maintain quality standards while meeting daily &amp; weekly production goals</li>
<li>Communicate production misses &amp; quality issues to appropriate leaders &amp; departments</li>
<li>Participate in continuous improvement initiatives</li>
<li>Identify gaps &amp; process improvements proactively</li>
<li>Cross train in other departments &amp; equipment</li>
<li>Support Lean Daily Management &amp; Key Performance Indicator tracking</li>
<li>Work with hazardous materials utilizing the proper personal protective equipment</li>
<li>Adhere to Brady's Attendance, Safety &amp; other Company policies</li>
<li>Maintain accurate paperwork, labeling, packaging and material transactions.</li>
<li>Record production data for entry in the ERP system in an accurate and timely manner.</li>
<li>Complete general housekeeping duties</li>
<li>Maintain safe working conditions in the department</li>
<li>All other duties as assigned</li>
</ul>
