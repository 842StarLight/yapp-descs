<div><b>Description</b></div>
<div>
Design and develop electronic devices and systems, in conjunction with embedded software development, by performing the following duties. Work under the direction of a project team lead, Project Manager, and senior technical staff.</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div><b>
Responsibilities:</b></div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
<b>
Essential Duties and Responsibilities</b> include the following. Other duties may be assigned.</div>
<div></div>
<div>
<ul>
<li>
<br>
Supply technical leadership for junior technical staff for new product development and support of existing products.</li>
<li>
Conduct technical research to support investigations related to specification and concept validation and to establish the scope of work involved for implementation.</li>
<li>
Perform technical research to identify and evaluate possible circuit concepts and detailed hardware design for assigned circuits.</li>
<li>
Design, prototype, and/or test new hardware circuits.</li>
<li>
Work with the compliance engineer to verify the product design addresses all relevant standards.</li>
<li>
Implement and/or direct circuit board layout</li>
<li>
Verify that assigned hardware circuits work properly, satisfy applicable requirements and coincide with embedded software.</li>
<li>
Support test personnel performing system test. May create system test specifications.</li>
<li>
Provide input for technical manuals and user documentation as required.</li>
<li>
Work with the Product Test and Product Service Center to identify and/or correct reported discrepancies in assigned hardware.</li>
<li>
Conduct audits to verify component selection for obsolete components or second-sourcing.</li>
<li>
Participate in the investigation for new technology and new technical tools.</li>
</ul>
