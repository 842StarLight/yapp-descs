<p>
The Inventory Analyst I ensures the optimal level of inventory while maintaining required service levels for assigned customer accounts and/or commodity item groups. Special attention must be paid to improving turns, identifying inventory levels that exceed demand and minimizing 120 Day inventory.</p>
<p><b><i>
Responsibilities:</i></b></p>
<ul>
<li>Inventory management - medium to high level of difficulty</li>
<li>Analyzes inventory levels, prepares forecasts and makes purchases to support demand for assigned locations and vendors</li>
<li>Processes forecast change requests</li>
<li>Manages turns and 120 Day inventory reduction efforts</li>
<li>Review and follow up on daily, weekly and month-end inventory reports</li>
<li>PO processing</li>
<li>Software replenishment maintenance</li>
<li>Report generation</li>
<li>Assists in line reviews and cost reduction efforts with Supply Chain</li>
<li>Identifies and resolves supplier issues</li>
<li>Participates in conference calls and meetings as determined</li>
<li>Inventory expert (processes, issues, ‘one-off’ information)</li>
<li>Enter vendor returns; inventory write-offs and destructions</li>
<li>Follow up on open purchase orders and back-orders, expedite as needed, update ETA in system</li>
<li>Manages inventory levels to meet budget guidelines</li>
<li>Responsible for system maintenance on usages and buy multiples</li>
<li>Communicates and coordinates usage changes and sales opportunities with supported branches</li>
<li>Schedules inbound freight</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Requirements</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s Degree in Business Administration or similar field is desired</li>
<li>Two or more years working in a distribution warehousing or purchasing environment with hands-on knowledge of inventory management principles and systems</li>
<li>Communication, both written and verbal</li>
<li>Analytical, with critical thinking and problem-solving skills</li>
<li>Leadership</li>
<li>Project and priority management (Organization)</li>
<li>Knowledge of contemporary inventory management concepts, practices and procedures</li>
<li>PC proficiency including Word and Advanced Excel experience</li>
<li>Report generation (Subjective)</li>
<li>Service-driven; detail-oriented</li>
</ul>
