<p><b>Customer Service Representative</b></p>
<p>Perrin Manufacturing is looking to hire a Customer Service Representative to support our outside sales team, dealer and OEM customer network.</p>
<p>The Customer Service Representative is a member of the sales team and is responsible for supporting the team as they service existing, and pursue new relationships, with over the road trucking dealerships, trucking fleets and manufacturers of off-road, heavy duty equipment.</p>
<p><b>About Perrin Manufacturing</b></p>
<p>Perrin Manufacturing is comprised of three divisions, Perrin Manufacturing, PowerCube APU (Tridako Energy) and Dynasys APU. We design, test and build HVAC and auxiliary power systems for over the road, off-road and heavy-duty vehicles providing cost-effective heating and cooling allowing operators to efficiently work in comfort.</p>
<p><b>Perrin Manufacturing</b> designs, tests and builds HVAC systems for off-road, heavy duty vehicles providing the heating and cooling needed for operators to work in comfort. These climate control systems are in earth movers, skid steers, tree mulchers, broom sweepers, tractors, sprayers, road pavers, loaders, road graders and more.</p>
<p><b>PowerCube APU</b> is a leading-edge auxiliary power unit, approved by the EPA SmartWay Clean Diesel Program to reduce emissions. It gives the driver fuel savings and reduced engine wear.</p>
<p><b>Dynasys APU</b> is dedicated to manufacturing and distributing energy-efficient auxiliary power systems for the transportation industry that reduce emission and the magnitude of climate change.</p>
<p>All brands are designed, manufactured and tested at Perrin’s manufacturing facility in Alliance, NE.</p>
<p>The customer service representative position will be located at the company’s headquarters in Alliance, NE.</p>
<p><b>Responsibilities</b></p>
<p>· Act as a point of contact for existing and potential customers</p>
<p>· Present our products and services to prospective customers</p>
<p>· Identify customer needs and recommend product solutions</p>
<p>· Work closely with outside sales, production and other internal teams to meet sales quotas and maintain customer satisfaction expectations</p>
<p>· Answer customer questions about features, pricing and additional services</p>
<p>· Cross-sell products, when appropriate</p>
<p>· Collaborate with sales representatives to share best practices and support a cohesive sales approach</p>
<p><b>Requirements</b></p>
<p>· 3+ Years of Proven Sales and Customer Service Success</p>
<p>· Experience in the trucking or heavy-duty equipment manufacturing industry a plus</p>
<p>· Experience with CRM software</p>
<p>· Excellent written and verbal communication skills</p>
<p>· Resilient and reliable with a proven track record of excellent customer service</p>
<p>· High School Degree required; Bachelor’s Degree preferred</p>
