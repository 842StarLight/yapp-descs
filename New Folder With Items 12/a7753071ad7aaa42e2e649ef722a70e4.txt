<p><b>ESSENTIAL DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES</b><br>

<ul>
<li>Assists in identifying, targeting, and establishing potential marketing and sponsorship opportunities for the Surprise Stadium

<li>Assists in event promotions, events, and programs

</li>
<li>Assists with the development of in-game presentations
</li>
<li>Coordinates pre-game events, including national anthem, first pitch, and special recognitions
</li>
<li>Participates in sales/promotional and team events outside normal business hours
</li>
<li>Prospects new customers and qualifying leads provided
</li>
<li>Generates weekly sales efforts reports
</li>
<li>Performs cold calling to area businesses, local groups/organizations, and individuals
</li>
<li>Performs the essential functions as described in the physical demands and work environment sections below
</li>
<li>Maintains regular attendance and punctuality
</li>
<li>Performs other duties of a similar nature or level</li>
