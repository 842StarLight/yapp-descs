<h2>Senior Satellite Bandwith Technician</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>EchoStar Corporation (NASDAQ: SATS) headquartered in Englewood, CO, with additional locations world-wide, is a premier global provider of satellite solutions and technology services. Together with its major subsidiaries—EchoStar Satellite Services and Hughes Network Systems— EchoStar offers employees the opportunity to explore the boundaries of what’s possible in the fields of engineering and the business functions transforming this engineering into revenue.
</p>
<p></p>
<p>EchoStar is looking for a Satellite Bandwidth Technician. This position is available in Englewood CO or Cheyenne, WY.<br>
<br>
</p>
<p><b>Responsibilities:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Manage satellite capacity to insure maximum utilization efficiency while avoiding interference issues.
</li>
<li>Support sales team with technical support on proposals.
</li>
<li>Support prospective and current clients with network design.
</li>
<li>Identify best options available for prospective clients for orbital slot, bandwidth capacity and hardware, based on link budget goals.
</li>
<li>Prepare link analysis and transmission plans, as needed.
</li>
<li>Ensure clients are not exceeding contracted segment amounts or coordination limits
</li>
<li>Provide weekly satellite usage reports to sales and finance teams.
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Basic Requirements:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>High School graduate or GED
</li>
<li>At least two years of RF engineering experience
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Preferred Qualifications:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor's degree (B. S.) in electrical engineering or related field
</li>
<li>Good communication skills which shall include preparing written reports, and presentations.
</li>
<li>Comfortable presenting information and responding to questions from management, customers, and general public.
</li>
<li>Ability to work with mathematical concepts such as probability, calculus.
</li>
<li>Strong software and programming skills.
</li>
</ul>
