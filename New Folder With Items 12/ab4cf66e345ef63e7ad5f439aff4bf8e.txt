<h2>Director, Clinical Operations</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p>Hospital IQ is seeking a Director, Clinical Operations to own, manage, and further provide clinical expertise to a team of professionals who are committed to helping clients achieve enduring impact from their investment in our platform. This Director will work with HIQ sales and clients pre and post installation of HIQ applications to ensure that the solutions are being integrated into the day-to-day clinical environment and are attaining continuous impact.</p>
<p>
This role requires strong clinical knowledge specifically around optimizing care delivery including care management, utilization management, bed management, workforce management, patient flow, and procedural performance management. It will require an individual who can scope out initial and ongoing efforts, identify unmet client needs and influence the client to mobilize the resources necessary to address these needs. The role will require a leader who can lead a team of like-minded client success professionals to understand the clinical process and translate into the technical workflow of the software. In addition, the Director, Clinical Operations will be an integral member of the Operations team, reporting to the VP, Client Operations and interacting with all aspects of the company including sales, product management, data science, engineering and marketing.</p>
<p>
Responsibilities:</p>
<ul>
<li>Participate in the service delivery of solutions to new Hospital IQ clients as a subject matter expert, providing thought leadership, evaluating gaps between clinical practices and best practice, identifying use cases, crafting and delivering executive updates, and providing support to the deployment team.</li>
<li>Work with hospital/client executives to make organizational and process changes necessary to sustain impact in their investment in HIQ, as well as to hold their teams accountable to ongoing improvement.</li>
<li>Structure and execute regular (monthly or quarterly) business reviews with clients, operating as an executive account manager both internally and externally.</li>
<li>Support clinical leaders in preparing and presenting the client impact achieved by their investment in HIQ as well as surfacing additional actions that can be taken to increase impact.</li>
<li>Partner with other members of the Operations team in the maturation of existing delivery processes and the creation of new service offerings.</li>
<li>Work with various internal teams to prepare proposals for deploying HIQ solutions in the hospital/health system.</li>
<li>Develop and prepare consulting proposals for specific improvement projects that will help the client achieve additional impact.</li>
<li>Collaborate with product management in the identification and prioritization of new features to better support our clients.</li>
<li>Participate in the review of market-facing collateral not limited to data sheet reviews, presentations, web content, blogs, etc.</li>
<li>Participate in webinars, trade shows, and other industry events to show thought leadership under the HIQ name.</li>
<li>Assist and support the sales team with pre-sales activities as needed, such as providing clinical subject matter expertise for software demonstrations, statement of works, discussion of HIQâ€™s client success protocols, client impact statements, etc.</li>
</ul>
<p>Minimum Qualifications:</p>
<ul>
<li>Registered nurse with management and administrative experience.</li>
<li>4+ years in a previous client success and/or professional services leadership (consulting) role.</li>
<li>Experience in building teams of like-minded professionals.</li>
<li>Experience in deploying operational process changes with specific focus on change management and sustainability.</li>
<li>Excellent organizational, project management, time management and communication skills.</li>
<li>Technically proficient and strong analytical background with experience in Excel and PowerPoint.</li>
<li>Experience presenting to executive-level audience.</li>
<li>Ability to self-manage and self-motivate within a remote work environment.</li>
</ul>
<p>Preferred Qualifications:</p>
<ul>
<li>Experience deploying complex IT solutions or managing the implementation of large projects.</li>
<li>Consulting experience.</li>
</ul>
<p>Work Environment:</p>
<ul>
<li>Office environment and remote options available.</li>
<li>This role requires moderate travel (30-40%).</li>
</ul>
