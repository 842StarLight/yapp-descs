<p>At Southern Management, people come first, and our Leasing Agents live this principle every day. Our extraordinary Leasing Teams help people find their perfect apartment-homes.</p>
<p>
Leasing Agents are Brand Ambassadors who communicate with hundreds of people-networking, marketing, responding to email, taking phone calls, answering questions, conducting apartment tours and more.</p>
<p>
As a Leasing Agent at Southern, a typical day could include:</p>
<ul>
<li>Joining a Service Technician to walk through available apartments to insure each is ready for tour</li>
<li>Calling the people who came by yesterday to answer their questions about lease terms</li>
<li>Completing a new lease and conducting a move-in orientation</li>
<li>Working on a marketing survey with the Property Manager</li>
<li>Meeting with a vendor to discuss an advertising partnership</li>
<li>Planning the upcoming resident pool party</li>
</ul>
