<h2>Home Builder Finance - Senior Relationship Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>JOB PURPOSE</p>
<p>
Serves as the account manager for new and existing commercial real estate clients. Establishes and maintains a "trusted advisor" relationship with clients based on knowledgeable advice that is aligned to the clients' business objectives and lifecycle. Develops and grows new business and builds/maintains strong relationships with prospects. Drives financial performance through profitable revenue and growth, and quality credit.</p>
<p>
ESSENTIAL DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES</p>
<ul>
<li>Responsible for credit and deposit needs for commercial banking and commercial real estate investment customers involved in ownership and/or operation of businesses or who invest in or develop real estate.</li>
<li>Initiates and develops new business through outside business development activities.</li>
<li>Achieves annual growth goals for loans, deposits and fees to ensure the bank meets its overall financial targets and conforms to credit terms.</li>
<li>Interviews applicants to gain information regarding their business, desire, ability and earning potential so that decisions regarding approval can be made based on acceptable risk levels.</li>
<li>Provides credit, deposit and other banking services in conjunction with client management activities.</li>
<li>Develops and analyzes financial background data for new or renewed loans.</li>
<li>Makes decisions on loans and terms within established lending limits, or refers these issues to next appropriate level of authority.</li>
<li>Follows-up on current loans to ensure compliance with internal procedures and other required terms.</li>
<li>Maintains ongoing contact with client to be kept current on significant changes in financial condition or operations.</li>
<li>Provides financial advice and counsel to existing and potential customers within appropriate legal constraints.</li>
<li>Studies industrial, commercial, economic and financial situations relating to new or existing business.</li>
<li>Makes recommendations on financial and organizational structure and other matters; visits plant or company sites as required.</li>
<li>Ensures the sale of additional financial services.</li>
<li>Analyzes credit to determine the ability of customers and prospective customers to qualify for ensuing borrowing requirements.</li>
<li>Oversees the timely completion of required documents.</li>
<li>Assists internal and external customers, including introducing them to other products/services and other personnel when necessary.</li>
<li>Demonstrates compliance with all bank regulations for assigned job function and applies to designated job responsibilities - knowledge may be gained through coursework and on-the-job training. Keeps up to date on regulation changes.</li>
<li>Follows all Bank policies and procedures, compliance regulations, and completes all required annual or job-specific training.</li>
<li>Maintain a working knowledge of Bank's written policies and procedures regarding Bank Secrecy Act, Regulation CC, Regulation E, Bank Security and other regulations as applicable to this job description.</li>
<li>May be asked to coach, mentor, or train others and teach coursework as subject matter expert.</li>
<li>Actively learns, demonstrates, and fosters the Umpqua corporate culture in all actions and words.</li>
<li>Takes personal initiative and is a positive example for others to emulate.</li>
<li>Embraces our vision to become "The World's Greatest Bank."</li>
<li>May perform other duties as assigned.</li>
</ul>
<p>SUPERVISORY RESPONSIBILITIES</p>
<p>
None</p>
<p>
REQUIRED KNOWLEDGE, SKILLS AND ABILITIES</p>
<ul>
<li>Requires a Master's degree or equivalent, and significant experience successfully managing the most complex and valuable customers.</li>
<li>6-10 years of relevant commercial lending experience.</li>
<li>Excellent relationship management and business development abilities, analytical and problem solving skills, sales skills, and credit and credit quality skills.</li>
<li>Advanced knowledge of commercial C&amp;I bank products and services that result in successfully capitalizing on all opportunities to cross-sell the bank's products.</li>
<li>Proven track record of success and skills in developing new clients and business, including prospecting, sourcing and networking.</li>
<li>Excellent credit analysis and commercial underwriting skills, including accounting, financial statement and cash flow analysis experience.</li>
<li>Advanced knowledge of commercial lending policies, procedures, practices and documentation.</li>
<li>Excellent written and verbal communication and presentation skills.</li>
<li>Ability to work effectively with individuals and groups across the company to manage customer relationships.</li>
<li>Proficiency with personal computers and related software packages such as Word and Excel.</li>
<li>Ability to train and present to small and large audiences or has the interest in learning to train and present.</li>
</ul>
<p>PHYSICAL AND ENVIRONMENTAL DEMANDS</p>
<ul>
<li>Work involves sitting, occasional walking, and lifting up to 10 pounds.</li>
<li>Requires close visual acuity.</li>
<li>Normal office environment and exposure to weather when visiting customer sites.</li>
<li>Frequent local travel.</li>
</ul>
