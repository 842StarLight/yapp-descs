<div><b>Responsibilities</b></div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>Lead technical aspects of software architecture implementation projects. Direct and organize key technical architectural aspects of brand websites while leading a core architecture implementation team. Analyze user needs and software requirements and determine system performance standards. Direct the continued development of platforms during the implementation processes. Oversee the development of software architecture on Java-based ecommerce. Direct meetings regarding optimizing integration of architecture in order to increase speed, efficiency and improve overall quality of ecommerce web site delivery. Communicate with clients to quantify, analyze and formalize requirements for ecommerce software projects and develop implementation approach. Monitor and troubleshoot existing production ecommerce websites. Introduce and propose new features or improvements based on latest industry trends and technology standards. Monitor and ensure software compliance with industry security standards. Work with sales department on discovery, estimation and architectural stages of ecommerce projects. Offer technical assistance and expertise and improve internal engineering efficiency. Facilitate education and technical learning within project teams. Optimize communication between engineering teams, clients, and third-party representatives.</div>
