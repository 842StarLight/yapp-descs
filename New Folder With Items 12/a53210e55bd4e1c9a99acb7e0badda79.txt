<p><b>Full-Time Marketing Assistant</b></p>
<p>Zynex is seeking the best <b>Marketing Assistant </b>in the state to join our team. If you are looking to join an amazing company that is passionate about making a difference in our patient’s lives, then Zynex is the place for you. Zynex has since 1996 been a leading provider of pain management and rehabilitation devices without side effects and striving to reduce the use of opioids.</p>

<ul>
<li>Competitive &amp; above-market base pay</li>
<li>Health, Dental &amp; Vision Insurance Benefits</li>
<li>Additional $50/mo. health benefit for gym membership or other health-related expense</li>
<li>401K available + company contribution</li>
<li>8 Paid Holidays + 15/ days Paid Time Off (PTO)</li>

<br>

<p><b>Essential Job Duties and Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Build positive relationships with Zynex sales representatives</li>
<li>Customize marketing materials to meet the needs of each individual sales rep</li>
<li>Respond promptly to marketing requests from sales reps and internal staff</li>
<li>Print &amp; package professional marketing materials for sales reps across the country</li>
<li>Offer innovative ideas about marketing materials or products that could be added to help sales reps be more successful</li>
<li>Ensure exceptional packaging &amp; presentation of all company materials to be shipped</li>
<li>Create PowerPoint presentations for on-line use and internal staff training needs</li>
<li>Attend trade-show to show case our products</li>
</ul>

<li>1-2 years of experience working in an office environment</li>
<li>Marketing experience a plus</li>
<li>Adobe Creative Cloud experience a plus</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Education:</b></p>

</ul>
<p><b>Physical and Environmental Requirements:</b></p>
<ul>
