<h2>Finance Director</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p>Telegraph Chrysler looking for a finance leader.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>POSITION SUMMARY: </b>Responsible for the entire F&amp;I department profit, compliance, lender/vendor relations, etc. and oversees the production and performance of the individual F&amp;I Managers in his/her dealership. The Director may or may not be a producer (i.e. contracting customers). Also responsible for protecting the financial welfare of the dealership by adhering to company compliance guidelines.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Manage overall production in accordance with company and/or dealership standards related to reserve income, F&amp;I product penetrations/income, and Per-Vehicle-Retail (PVR) averages for retail (new and used) and lease customers. This includes establishing monthly F&amp;I objectives (in conjunction with sales department forecasting) and clearly communicating those objectives to the dealership F&amp;I team.
</li>
<li>Review, understand and train on company compliance guidelines in the following areas:
<ul>
<li>Equal Credit Opportunity Act (ECOA)
</li>
<li>Privacy Notices
</li>
<li>Identity Theft Protection Program (ITPP) – Red Flag Compliance
</li>
<li>Office of Foreign Asset Control (OFAC)
</li>
<li>IRS 8300/FinCEN
</li>
<li>Adverse Action Notices
</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>Manage deal and cash flow in accordance with company Contracts-In-Transit (CIT) guidelines.
</li>
<li>Ensure all F&amp;I Managers acquire and maintain necessary insurance licenses in accordance with state requirements.
</li>
<li>Educate F&amp;I Managers on the features and benefits of all F&amp;I products and the correct method of selling and disclosing those products to customers.
</li>
<li>Instruct F&amp;I Managers on the proper presentation and disclosure of all documents related to the purchase of a vehicle, including F&amp;I products, finance/lease interest rates and loan terms.
</li>
<li>Review and understand the Ken Garff Preferred Lender Program and manage lender production based on the parameters outlined in that program.
</li>
<li>Effectively communicate and train on the specific program requirements for each of the Preferred Lenders.
</li>
<li>Assist in the structuring of deals (when necessary) and submitting customer and vehicle information to lending sources for approval.
</li>
<li>Work directly with lending sources to increase the probability of deal approvals and the maximization of per deal profit.
</li>
<li>Ensure F&amp;I Managers are collecting and safeguarding all money and fees required to satisfy loan requirements.
</li>
<li>Monitor F&amp;I Managers adherence to company guidelines related to the video recording of vehicle transactions (100% of the time) and completion of at least one F&amp;I training module per week.
</li>
<li>Conduct weekly one-on-one meetings with each F&amp;I Manager.
</li>
<li>Participate in dealership and company required compliance training, per company guidelines.
</li>
<li>Attend and participate in manager meetings when instructed (including daily “Save-A-Deal” meetings).
</li>
<li>Other duties as assigned
</li>
</ul>
