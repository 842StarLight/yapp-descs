<p></p>
<p><b>
Skills:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Ability to work in a fast-paced, real time environment</li>
<li>
Communication – excellent written, verbal and e-mail skills</li>
<li>
Excellent analytic skills with the ability to use key tools to consistently provide in-depth analysis on performance metrics</li>
<li>
Strong organizational skills to ensure critical timelines are met</li>
<li>
Familiar with digital marketing experience in display ads, digital video, social media or other core digital areas preferred</li>

Strong project management skills with ability to create executive level presentations and communicate with large groups</li>
<li>
Experience with Microsoft Office (excel expertise required), Google Analytics, AdWords, Bing AdCenter, tag management, and attribution modeling required</li>
<li>
Ability to lead a small team by providing strategic direction, prioritizing tasks at hand, and providing strong leadership skills</li>
<li>
Collaboration across functions, matrix marketing environment</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Responsibilities:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Supports the Sr. Director of Media in the execution of a strategic paid search and affiliate marketing annual plans that integrates with organizational marketing initiatives</li>
<li>
Consistently reviews web analytics provided by Google Analytics, DoubleClick for Search, marketing mix modeling and digital attribution tools, and agency reporting platforms to identify trends and provide optimization</li>
<li>
Provides strategic direction to paid search and affiliate marketing teams and current agencies to ensure established goals are achieved while continuing to test and broaden program exposure</li>
<li>
Responsible for the management of multi-million dollar budgets (reforecast, accruals, and invoices), creating test scenarios, and implement marketing mix modeling</li>
<li>
Partner with the category merchandisers, the SEO team, the analytics team, merchants, and marketing to ensure key sales drivers have the proper coverage and marketing messages</li>
<li>
Optimization of the current Google Shopping and other product feeds</li>
<li>
Partner with key stakeholders to integrate an attribution strategy into paid search and affiliate marketing’s strategies, with the end goal of driving enterprise ROAS</li>
<li>
Forecast/reforecast traffic performance of channels based on budgets, updates to strategy, planned testing and attribution tool influences</li>
<li>
Create 3-5 year plans for channels and testing roadmaps</li>
<li>
Perform tests that have omni-channel focus and inform on the influence of the ads on store traffic</li>
<li>
Provide support and direction for team members and assist with career development</li>
<li>
Work on creation of new co-op programs</li>
<li>
Required to learn company policies, procedures, and safety rules</li>
<li>
Duties may change and associates may be required to perform other duties as assigned</li>
</ul>
