<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities and Qualifications
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div>The primary responsibility of the Service Manager is to oversee the service department and drive operational success; technicians, service advisors, service call center, lot attendants, detailers and shuttle driver, etc.</div>
<ul>
<li>
Manage employee schedules and pay plans</li>
<li>
Forecast goals and objectives for the department and strive to meet them</li>
<li>
Work with the Parts Manager to ensure a timely turnaround of parts needed for internal jobs</li>
<li>
Work with shop foreman to ensure customers vehicles are serviced properly and in a timely manner</li>
<li>
Attend manager meetings and conduct service department meetings</li>
<li>
Prepare and administer an annual operating budget for the service department</li>
<li>
Maintain Asburys specified service customer pay gross profit growth</li>
<li>
Maintain technician proficiency as specified by Asbury standards</li>
<li>
Partner with technical schools to recruit technicians</li>
<li>
Partner with OEM and other third party vendors</li>
<li>
Handle and resolve customer complaints</li>
<li>
Motivate, train, develop and reward employees</li>
<li>
Communicate with customers about maintenance and service work needed effectively</li>
<li>
Continue to maintain Asbury's value, quality, and integrity</li>
<li>
Provide and maintain a safe work place and environment for employees and customers</li>
<li>
Other duties to be determined by management</li>
<li>
Strong customer service skills are a must</li>
<li>
Must be able to manage a team, motivate and develop</li>
<li>
Experience with CDK or other automotive software required</li>
<li>
Strong technical aptitude is required</li>
<li>
Proven track record to grow the fixed operation business through excellent CSI, advertising, managing utilization and proficiency of the shop</li>
<li>
Must have 3+ years as an automotive Service Writer/Service Assistant Manager</li>
<li>
Previous automotive Service Management experience is required</li>
<li>
Must be able to manage in a fast paced work environment</li>
<li>
Must be able to pass pre-employment screens (background &amp; drug test)</li>
</ul>
