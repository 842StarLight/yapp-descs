<h2>Public Sector Labor & Trust Sales Executive</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<div>The Senior Sales Executive for UnitedHealth Care is responsible for broker / consultant and direct sales to Public Sector customers (state governments, municipalities, school districts, public funded hospitals and higher education and tribal nations) and Labor/Trust customers (Multi-Employer Taft-Hartley Funds, and Unions). The principle focus is the sale of new business to sophisticated customers with an emphasis on fully insured, self-funded ASO, and shared network administration business, and an indirect responsibility for the retention of existing accounts. This is a high level individual contributor sales role tasked with new business procurement in the Public Sector Labor &amp; Trust market.</div>
<br>
