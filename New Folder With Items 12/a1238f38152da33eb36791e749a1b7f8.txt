<p>Sony Electronics Professional Solutions Division is looking for dynamic, driven business builders who love to win as much as they love technology, care about our customers’ success as much as our growth and excel at communicating our product vision. The SaaS Sales Account Representative is responsible for qualifying leads, demonstrating our product and helping to close new deals. If you have the ambition to join a thriving SaaS business and drive sales, deliver results and help change the way professional content is created and managed, we want to speak with you.
</p>
<p></p>
<p>Key responsibilities of the role are as follows:
</p>
<ul>
<li>Proactively identify, contact and cultivate new business opportunities using a combination of calling, emailing, social media and industry events
</li>
<li>Respond to and qualify incoming web and phone inquiries
</li>
<li>Provide product demonstrations, understand customer workflows and explain how our product can meet their requirements
</li>
<li>Create customer specific presentations and collaborate with our Marketing teams to maximize the effectiveness of our demand generation strategies
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p>Qualifications
</p>
<ul>
<li>1+ years of quota-carrying technical solution sales or business development experience (preferably in SaaS)
</li>
<li>Experience working with content collaboration and digital asset management technologies
</li>
<li>Experience working in production or post production is a plus
</li>
<li>Proven record of meeting and exceeding monthly qualified opportunity targets
</li>
<li>Experience selling technical solutions to creative and technical professionals, preferably within the media technology space
</li>
<li>Experience in the creative arts such as film or music creation, or a strong interest in working in the video creation industry.
</li>
<li>Excellent communication skills including listening, writing, speaking and cold-calling
</li>
<li>Self-driven, motivated and results oriented with new business sales or “hunter” mentality
</li>
<li>Diligent follow through, no lead left behind
</li>
<li>Some travel (20% or less) – may grow over time
</li>
</ul>
