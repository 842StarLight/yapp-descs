<p><b><i>Expectations and Tasks</i></b></p>
<p>The primary purpose of HANA Delivery Execution is to help our customers adopt SAP HANA technology and related software applications, realizing and maintain their business competitive advantages through successful implementation of most advanced technology in enterprise business software industry.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>To accomplish this objective the Data Sr. Consultant acts as the driving force supporting Go To Market and Delivery execution teams focused on Data Management solutions. The Data Sr. Consultant will partner with the client to analyze and define enterprise data management strategy, data movement, data governance strategies, and objectives to scope project and deliverables for the same. In addition, the Data Sr. Consultant will be active in both practice and overall skill enablement elements of SAP Consulting. The Digital Transformation members will provide transformational leadership in targeted and knowledge areas across HANA technologies.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Drive Data Management solutions to support SAP Leonardo and SAP S/4HANA implementations</li>
<li>Drive Data Strategies including Data Migration, Data Movement, and Data Governance</li>
<li>Design customer specific data management strategy, solution, and implementation roadmap</li>
<li>Be actively involved in the preparation, conceptualization, realization and Go Live of customer implementation projects</li>
<li>Conduct use case, and technical information gathering workshops</li>
<li>Analyze and map clients’ business requirements, processes and objectives; develop necessary product modifications to satisfy clients’ needs.</li>
<li>Drive data strategy involving cloud systems (i.e. S/4HANA, BW/4HANA, SuccessFactors, Ariba, Concur, Hybris, …)</li>
<li>Support project scoping and effort estimation activities</li>
<li>Deliver proof of concepts (PoC) and proof of values (PoV)</li>
<li>Practice Development</li>
<li>Knowledge management - identification, codification, cleansing, and mass re-use of IP</li>
<li>Community development (i.e. formal mentoring program)</li>
<li>Supports Consulting Services Sales and License Sales</li>
<li>Support of Service Select package offerings (development/positioning/rollout)</li>
<li>Provide specialty consulting services (i.e. roadmap development; assessments, solution (Q/A) reviews, landscape design, technology optimization etc.)</li>
<li>Maintaining a high degree of knowledge &amp; understanding of SAP products, SAP sales tools/processes by ongoing investment in professional &amp; personal development.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b><i>Position Requirements</i></b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>In addition to a Bachelor Degree in Business Administration, Engineering, Computer Science or related discipline, the successful candidate will possess:</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>7+ years SAP consulting experience with at least 5 years in Data Management, Data Movement, and Data Governance</li>
<li>Strong knowledge of data provisioning and data migration solutions</li>
<li>Experience with physical, logical, and conceptual data modeling</li>
<li>Experience with SAP Data Services, Legacy System Migration Workbench (LSMW), Master Data Management, Master Data Governance
</li>
<li>Experience with SAP Customer Vendor Integration</li>
<li>Experience leading Data Management related initiatives (system integration, data warehouse build, data mart build, or similar)</li>
<li>Experience implementing and supporting Operational Data Stores, Data Warehouses, and Data Marts and Data integration platforms.</li>
<br>
</ul>
