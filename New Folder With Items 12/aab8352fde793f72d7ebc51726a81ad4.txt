<p><b>
What You’ll Get to Do</b></p>
<p>
Work on a collaborative, fast-paced, culture-oriented in-house marketing team servicing a rapidly growing Top 5 real estate brand in the US and its partners. Present creative design solutions for our clients (both in-house and external) and produce a wide variety of print and web materials.</p>
<p><b>
A Look at Typical Daily Tasks</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Creating web and social media graphics</li>
<li>Developing layouts for emails, flyers, newsletters, announcements, office displays, ads, brochures, exhibits, presentations, landing pages, etc.</li>
<li>Creating animated GIFs for social media and email</li>
<li>Designing infographics</li>
<li>Creating design templates for agents and brokers</li>
<li>Creating franchise logos and logo variations</li>
<li>Photo editing</li>
<li>Proofing and editing artwork</li>
<li>Designing and creating emails in Hubspot</li>
<li>Custom requests, and other duties as assigned</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Education</b></p>
<p>
BA/BS or equivalent in graphic design, visual communication, or related field.</p>
<p><b>
What We’re Looking For</b></p>
<ul>
<li>At least 3-5 years of graphic design experience</li>
<li>Both print and web design experience</li>
<li>Keen eye for detail</li>
<li>Ability to analyze design issues and present thoughtful solutions</li>
<li>Ability to generate design concepts with minimal direction</li>
<li>Ability to effectively work with constructive criticism from Art Director, as well as feedback from multiple stakeholders</li>
<li>Excellent application of typography, color, form and space in layout design</li>
<li>Ability to apply a grid to layout design</li>
<li>Proficiency in Adobe Creative Suite (current version), especially INDD, AI, PS, Acrobat on Mac platform</li>
<li>Basic knowledge of HTML and CSS</li>
<li>Ability to proof work for spelling, punctuation, capitalization,basic grammar, etc</li>
<li>Knowledge of web graphic formats and requirements</li>
<li>Knowledge of social media apps and graphic formats</li>
<li>Knowledge of print production processes</li>
</ul>
<p><b>You’ll Score Brownie Points for the Following</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Advanced web coding/HTML/CSS skills</li>
<li>Experience with Hubspot (online inbound marketing and sales software)</li>
<li>Experience with WordPress</li>
<li>Photography experience/skill</li>
<li>Animation or video editing experience (After Effects, Final Cut, Premiere Pro)</li>
<li>Agency or in-house job experience</li>
<li>Have real estate knowledge/experience</li>
</ul>
