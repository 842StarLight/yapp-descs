<h2>Client Coordinator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Job Position: Client Coordinator</b></p>
<p>Job Description:</p>
<p>The Client Coordinator at Stonebrook Day Spa is involved with every step of the client’s spa experience. We have an amazing team that we consider family and would like to add someone who is absolutely passionate about what we do and can portray that passion in every way possible towards our future clients and patients. Sales and service, spa, health or medical experience required.</p>
<p>Stonebrook Day Spa describes it’s culture as:</p>
<ul>
<li>Detail-oriented -- quality and precision-focused</li>
<li>Innovative -- innovative and risk-taking</li>
<li>People-oriented -- supportive and fairness-focused</li>
<li>Team-oriented -- cooperative and collaborative</li>
<li>Stable -- traditional, stable, strong processes</li>
<li>Outcome-oriented -- results-focused with strong performance culture</li>
<li>Aggressive -- competitive and growth-oriented</li>
</ul>
<p>In this role you will be working the front desk greeting guests, answering multi-line phones, scheduling appointments, checking clients in and out, collecting money, managing inventory, light housekeeping, assisting therapists, nail techs and our facial team in the flow of the spa and any other duties as assigned. This is a very busy position. We need someone with a great attitude who can deliver a positive guest impression and oversee the coordination of the spa. Strong knowledge of sales and interpersonal skills is needed.</p>
<p>Candidate must be proficient in the following front desk duties:</p>
<ul>
<li>Answering calls and email inquiries</li>
<li>Scheduling appointments</li>
<li>Educating potential clients</li>
<li>Retail sale and knowledge of our products and services</li>
<li>Checking clients in and out</li>
<li>Collecting balances</li>
<li>Posting payments</li>
<li>Monitoring inventory</li>
<li>Keep notes on clients accounts and managing records</li>
<li>Manage flow of spa &amp; guests – assisting service providers in staying on-time</li>
<li>Ensure cleanliness in the spa</li>
</ul>
<p>Knowledge:</p>
<p>· Microsoft Office</p>
