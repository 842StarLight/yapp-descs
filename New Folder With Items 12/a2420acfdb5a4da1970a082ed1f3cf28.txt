<h2>Seasonal Supervisor</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<b>Description:</b><br>
<b>Summary:</b><br>
ProService Boise is looking for a customer-oriented individual to join our growing team. This individual must have a commitment to creating a remarkable experience for our customers across multiple brands.The Account Supervisor will be responsible for day-to-day supervision of our customer care associates in Meridian, Idaho while also delivering customer service and sales support for online brands. As an ambassador for our brands, the Account Supervisor will play a key role in shaping the brand experience through direct customer interaction via phone, and providing support and guidance for customer care agents.
<br><br>
<b>JOB DUTIES &amp; RESPONSIBILITIES:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Supervising a team of 10-25 customer service representatives</li>
<li>Keeping management well informed of office happenings</li>
<li>Provide support to team and providing real time coaching to customer care agents to ensure customers are delighted</li>
<li>Making customer care decision as needed</li>
<li>Handling initial and secondary customer escalations from customer service representatives and senior reps.</li>
<li>Guiding customers through our product selection and helping them make a purchase decision that meets their needs</li>
<li>Offering customers additional products to complement their purchases</li>
<li>Assisting customers with questions regarding our products, our policies, or their order</li>
<li>Maintaining detailed customer notes</li>
<li>Other duties as assigned by management.</li>
</ul>
<b>Requirements:</b><br>
<b>SKILLS AND EXPERIENCE REQUIRED:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>At least 2 years of experience in supervisory/team lead position, including coaching and mentoring experience</li>
<li>At least 3 years in “direct-to-consumer” based environment</li>
<li>Minimum of 1 year in a call center environment</li>
<li>Ability to effectively coach and mentor fellow team members</li>
<li>Strong ability to multi-task and to establish appropriate priorities</li>
<li>Excellent professional communication skills, both written and verbal</li>
<li>Strong attention to detail</li>
<li>Ability to work independently with little supervision</li>
<li>Strong customer service skills</li>
<li>Soft sales skills – the ability to converse with the customer to understand their needs and desires</li>
<li>Ability to type 25 WPM</li>
<li>Ability to maintain composure under stressful situations; has a great attitude at all times</li>
<li>Ability to learn how to use online applications and software</li>
</ul>
</div>
