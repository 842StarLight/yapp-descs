<h2>Account Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>We are the leading provider of safety and security solutions in the Mid-Atlantic region, serving healthcare, education, manufacturing, hospitality/property management and construction markets. As the largest partner of Motorola Solutions Two-way Radio in the area, we also offer Call Boxes, Instant Notification Systems, Access Control and Video Solutions, Security Analytics Software and In-Building Wireless Systems.</p>
<p>We are currently aligning and expanding our sales force in Baltimore/Washington/Northern Virginia due to increased demand from customers and expansion of the portfolio of products and services we offer. <li>Conversion of prospects from legacy and/or competing technologies to the latest digital solutions</li>
<li>Working an assigned territory requiring both prospecting for new customers and developing existing customers</li>
<li>Mix of long and short cycle selling</li>
</ul>

<li><b>2 years of B2B sales experience</b></li>
<li><b>verifiable track record of ethical sales success</b></li>

<li><b>Experience using a CRM software</b></li>
</ul>
