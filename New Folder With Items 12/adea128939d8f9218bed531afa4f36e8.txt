<h2>Sales Director</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>The <b>Director, Sales</b> position is responsible for the sale of the Periscope's products in a specified region or major geographical area and provides ongoing support to product distribution channel.</p>
<p><b>
Duties and Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Develops and increases sales revenue to meet assigned targets within the defined territory</li>
<li>
Responsible for managing the complete sales cycle from opportunity generation through contract negotiation and execution</li>
<li>
Works with key partners, including lobbysists, to develop and qualify new sales opportunities</li>
<li>
Collaborates with and provides guidance to Business Development Rep who supports this role through nurturing leads and performing product demos</li>
<li>
Manages territory pipeline and revenue forecast within Salesforce, ensuring information is kept up to date and accurate</li>
<li>
Assists with the planning of sales exhibits and presentations, and attends trade shows as requested</li>
<li>
Participates in education and training conferences on selling and marketing programs.</li>
<li>
Works with marketing department to develop new ideas for outreach campaigns.</li>
<li>
Coordinates and assists in leading sales meetings to include site selection and agenda preparation.</li>
<li>
Works with the Sales Operarions Manager to direct and help manage the proposal process RFx to ensure succesfull submittal of responses.</li>
<li>
Keeps informed of new products, services and other general information of interest to customers.</li>
<li>
Checks on competitive activity and develops new methods of attaining Account Executives and assisting Account Executives in attaining new accounts.</li>
<li>
Performs other job-related duties and responsibilities as may be assigned from time to time.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Qualifications</b></p>
<p>
Required</p>
<ul>
<li>
Bachelor's degree</li>
<li>
5 plus years of successful sales experience with an enterprise software organization</li>
<li>
Experience selling into the public sector</li>
</ul>
<p>
Preferred</p>
<ul>
<li>
Advanced training in recent sales techniques</li>
<li>
Strong knowledge of procurement processes and operations within the public sector</li>
</ul>
</div></div>
