<b>SUMMARY OF JOB DESCRIPTION</b><br>
<b>
</b><br>
The position is an individual contributor, reporting within the Instrument’s Sales Team.<br>
The position is responsible for driving profitable sales growth helping to strengthen the<br>
value our customers realize from their partnership with Mesa Laboratories. This<br>
individual uses strong fundamental sales process and CRM to qualify inbound leads and<br>
develop new opportunities for either direct selling or escalating to the appropriate sales<br>
representatives when appropriate. This position requires the ability to manage multiple<br>
product segments and ongoing understanding and support of customers’ requirements.<br>
This position requires effective collaboration with technical and support team members<br>
and the ability to building relationships between customer and Mesa stakeholders. In<br>
addition, all team members will pursue continuous improvement in process expertise,<br>
selling skills, and application knowledge.<br>
<br>
