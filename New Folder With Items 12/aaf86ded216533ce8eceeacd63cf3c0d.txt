<h2>UM-Analyst, Decision Sciences</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div><div>
<p>We are looking for you - dynamic, best-in-class talent - to join the UM team as an Analyst, Decision Sciences. In this role you will assist in the analytics process for our top clients and help take data management to the next level. As an Analyst, Decision Sciences you will create, innovate, and guide the analytics process. This opportunity will kick-start your career in media analytics and provide a platform to partner with the world’s leading consumer brands.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Essential Functions</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Assist in all types of measurement and reporting &amp; analysis for digital campaigns, including display, video, mobile, search, social</li>
<li>Manipulate multiple sources of data to analyze the state our clients’ businesses across multiple dimensions (sales, brand equity, search interest, website visits, social media, advertising activity)</li>
<li>Produce regular and ad hoc campaign reports, drawing out insights and optimizations related to media objectives, clearly communicating results to internal &amp; external stakeholders</li>
<li>Develop and maintain large data sets, used in reporting and benchmarking</li>
<li>Data quality &amp; hygiene (including extraction, transformation &amp; loading), identify inconsistencies and gaps in data to ensure consistency and accuracy</li>
<li>Streamlining data and analytics processes, automating tasks and identifying opportunities to improve workflow</li>
<li>Train other teams on analytics tools &amp; processes</li>
<li>Ad hoc client and team requests</li>
<li>Compile and assess competitive advertising media intelligence</li>
<li>Apply substantial amounts of independent critical thinking, and from the analyses, uncover media and marketing communications opportunities</li>
<li>Participate in brainstorming sessions with communication planners, creative assets, media planners, buyers, clients and other partner agencies</li>
<li>Manage syndicated research tools in support of agency and client stakeholders</li>
<li>Contribute to the department’s overall capabilities and understanding of media and communications channels</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Qualifications</b></p>
<p>Education:</p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree required</li>
</ul>
<p>Work Experience:</p>
<ul>
<li>1-2+ years of experience of digital media analytics experience or related Media/Advertising experience in a quantitative role (agency experience STRONGLY preferred)</li>
</ul>
<p>Skills:</p>
<ul>
<li>Must enjoy working with data and manipulating numbers to develop new marketing insights</li>
<li>Experience analyzing digital media campaign performance</li>
<li>Working knowledge of digital media and analytics/measurement tools including: Digital Ad Servers (e.g. DCM, Atlas, Sizmek), Website Analytics (e.g. Google Analytics, Omniture, Web Trends), AdWords, Syndicated Consumer (e.g. Simmons, MRI), Syndicated Sales (e.g. IRI, Nielsen), Media Consumption (e.g. N-Power, Arbitron, Comscore), Paid Media Monitoring (e.g. Kantar, AdViews), Social Media Monitoring (e.g. NetBase, Sysomos), 1st Party (e.g. CRM), 3rd Party (e.g. Blue Kai)</li>
<li>Strong Excel experience, using Excel for data compilation, analysis, visualization, automation</li>
<li>Experience with analytic software such as RDBMS (e.g. SQL Server), Data Visualization (e.g. Tableau) a plus</li>
<li>Possess sound written, oral, and organizational skills</li>
</ul>
