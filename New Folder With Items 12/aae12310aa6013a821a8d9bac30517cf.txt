<h2>Sr. Digital Marketing and Customer Experience Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>The Sr. Digital Marketing and Customer Experience Specialist is dedicated to providing expertise in all areas of marketing with an emphasis on Digital Marketing &amp; Communications and Customer Experience in order to support the Company’s mission to be the Provider Of Choice for our customers in the United States.<br>
<br>
The Sr. Digital Marketing and Customer Experience Specialist plans and executes projects primarily for the Volvo Financial Services (VFS) USA organization and ensures all activities create brand awareness, promote an integrated full-service solution and value proposition. The selected candidate will report to the Manager, Commercial Marketing for Volvo Financial Services USA.<br>
<br>
Working within the Marketing team, the Sr. Digital Marketing and Customer Experience Specialist has the opportunity to work in and/or support all aspects of commercial marketing such as Digital Marketing &amp; Communications, Dealer and Customer Communications, Media Relations, Merchandising, Event management, Dealer and Sales Incentives, as well as other Marketing areas.<br>
<br>
<b>Key Responsibilities:</b><br>
<b>Digital Marketing &amp; Communications</b> -<br>
</p>
<ul>
<li><p>
Manage and maintain all web and digital marketing communication initiatives, including but not limited to External Websites, Portals, Social Media, Dealer and OEM Newsletters and Program Announcements.</p></li>
<li><p>
Maintain marketing database segmentation to ensure any communications delivered are relevant to the audience receiving them.</p></li>
<li><p>
Analyze websites individual performance with an eye towards continuous improvement, and sharing insights across the team. Including lead generation focusing on customer acquisition and retention.</p></li>
<li><p>
Build and maintain digital marketing plan.</p></li>
<li><p>
Support global strategies to drive traffic to VFS related channels using a range of market visibility techniques.</p></li>
<li><p>
Track conversion rates and make improvements to channels, contents and approach.</p></li>
<li><p>
Deploy and manage dealer customizable digital communication templates and promotional materials to effectively convey VFS marketing &amp; communications strategies and brand image from our dealers to their customers.</p></li>
<li><p>
Initiate and utilize market research, competitive analysis, and qualified metrics to develop effective strategies, communication plans &amp; materials.</p></li>
<li><p>
Utilize VFS CRM to streamline and enhance dealer and customer communications</p></li>
</ul>
<p><br>
<br>
<b>Customer Experience –
</b></p>
<ul>
<li><p>Assess and analyse the present customer satisfaction position by collecting and compiling data from different measurement surveys and tools.</p></li>
<li><p>
Work cross-functionally across the organization to define appropriate and concrete customer satisfaction KPI’s throughout the customer journey.</p></li>
<li><p>
Ensure we have the right measurements, surveys, tools, KPI’s and systems in place in order to measure our customer satisfaction position vs. the competition throughout the customer journey as well as throughout the entire lifecycle of our customers.</p></li>
</ul>
<p><br>
<br>
<b>Event Management –
</b></p>
<ul>
<li><p>Plan, coordinate and manage tradeshows, OEM Events and other key meetings in alignment of the overall Volvo Group and VFS Strategy. Including event details such as travel logistics, staffing levels, food/beverage, entertainment, registration, agendas, promotion and display, incentives, giveaways and production needs.</p></li>
<li><p>
Coordinate marketing strategy for events including collaboration with OEM and VFS personnel, and creation of marketing materials and promotional offers.</p></li>
<li><p>
Set, measure and report on event project budgets and plans and KPI’s including lead generation.</p></li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Other -
</b></p>
<ul><li><p>Plan and execute on special projects as assigned by Manager.</p></li></ul>

<li><p>

Seizes opportunities and has a proactive approach. Ability to coordinate and organize multiple transactions and deadlines. Proactive / Self-starter.</p></li>
<li><p>

<li><p>
Learns quickly when facing new problems and is a versatile learner open to change and quickly grasps the essence of ideas. Self-directed with the ability to adjust in a dynamic environment.</p></li>

Excellent interpersonal skills and the ability to work cross-functionally and within a team. Works well with employees at all levels of the Company’s and has the ability to function and build relationships in a highly matrixed environment.</p></li>
