<h2>Enterprise Account Executive</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div></div>
<div>
<p><b>Job Summary:</b></p>
<p>
Event Farm is looking for an Enterprise Account Executive to join our growing team. Your main responsibilities are securing new customers and negotiating/closing deals. Event Farm is an experiential marketing platform. We combine our expertise and suite of event technology—including attendee texting, wearable tech, and cutting-edge websites—to make innovative events possible.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Responsibilities and Duties:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Pursue a list of targeted Key Accounts; develop relationships with new prospects in an effort to meet and exceed revenue expectations. Present products and services to new corporate accounts.</li>
<li>
Accurately forecast sales opportunities via pipeline report, effectively manage personal revenue pipeline to maximize all new business opportunities; monitor and report sales activity within the system.</li>
<li>
Work with Customer Success and Professional Services teams to ensure complete customer satisfaction with the Event Farm product.</li>
<li>
Establish and maintain relationships with key decision-makers for accounts that will provide new business.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Qualifications:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Seeking 3+ years software sales experience.</li>
<li>
Experiential Marketing, Event Management software or experience in one or more of the following areas is highly preferred: SaaS, mobile apps, and analytics.</li>
<li>
Organized, detail-oriented, and able to multi-task in a dynamic, fast-changing environment.</li>
<li>
Ability to integrate knowledge across disciplines including cold calling, consultative/solution selling, product demonstrations, closing contracts, operation/process flow, product function, etc.</li>
<li>
Bachelor's degree.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Company Overview:</b></p>
<p>
Event Farm is an experiential marketing platform: invitations, guest registration, and digital activations designed specifically to promote brands and engage targeted audiences at events. For more information, please visit www.eventfarm.com.</p>
