<h2>Senior Product Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">As a Senior Product Manager you will have the opportunity to work with nearly all functional groups at HostGator (an Endurance International company). You will be a key member of the product management team, acting as the subject matter expert and representing the voice of the customer.
<br><br>
<b>What you'll do:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Lead cross functional initiatives to identify business problems and validate proposed solutions through customer and market research</li>
<li>Write clear use cases/requirements that articulate desired product functionality</li>
<li>Understand strategic and competitive position and deliver products that are recognized as best in the industry</li>
<li>Work collaboratively with Engineering, User and Customer Experience to develop simple, intuitive and easy to use product features</li>
<li>Develop product roadmap and get buy in from key stakeholders at all levels of the organization including Sales, Marketing, Engineering, Operations and Executive Management</li>
<li>Maintain prioritized product backlog with input from Customers, Partners, Sales and Support</li>
<li>Create customer and business key performance indicators that you will use to drive priorities and communicate results</li>
<li>Monitor innovative third party technology offerings and recommend solutions to extend product functionality</li>
<li>Mentor more junior product managers.</li>
</ul>
<b>Who you are:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Minimum of 8+ years of software Product Management experience</li>
<li>Management of products in SaaS delivery model is required</li>
<li>Expertise researching, defining and documenting complex business processes and requirements</li>
<li>Exceptional analytical, verbal, and written communication skills</li>
<li>Successful track record of collaboration with key stakeholders</li>
<li>Demonstrated ability to work at a strategic level, but also able to get into the details on requirements</li>
<li>Bachelor's degree or equivalent work experience</li>
</ul>
