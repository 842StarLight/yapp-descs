<h2>Fast Casual Assistant General Manager - Mansfield</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>We are searching for a Fast-Casual Restaurant Assistant General Manager with high volume alcohol sales and is passionate about delivering the highest quality service to make our customer’s experience unparalleled . The right candidate will have high energy, are driven and have high customer service standards. We are focused on delivering excellence in everything that we do and take immense pride in being part of a winning team.<br>
<br>
Pay is $44,000 per year plus bonus and benefits<br>
<br>
Job Description<br>
<br>
<ul>
<li>Support all areas of operations for the Front of the House including human resources, profitability, and sales growth</li>
<li>Help manage all areas of operation for the Back of the House including human resources, company’s food &amp; bar specifications and standardized recipes, cleanliness, storage and safety issues.</li>
<li>Control wastage and spoilage.</li>
<li>Independently manage entire operation of restaurant during scheduled shifts, which includes daily decision-making, staff support, guest interaction, scheduling, and planning, while upholding standards, product quality, and cleanliness</li>
<li>Manage staff throughout the shift including when employees can check out for the day</li>
<li>Provide coaching, development and discipline to all staff</li>
<li>Ensure all employees adhere to the company’s policies and guidelines</li>
<li>Perform line checks throughout shifts</li>
<li>Ensuring that restaurant is properly staffed to par, at all times using the company’s recruiting and hiring practices for hourly employees</li>
<li>Manage and schedule training as required</li>
<li>Help prepare daily, weekly and monthly reports which may include cashier’s reports, daily sales reports, weekly labor reports end of the month reports, maintenance reports, etc.</li>
<li>Ensure that proper cash handling procedures are followed with all cash-handling employees, from cashiers to supervisory staff</li>
<li>Balance cash on hand with end of day reports, petty cash, cashier drawers and in-house bank</li>
<li>Manage all check-in orders ensuring proper specs and quality are adhered to</li>
<li>Identify operational opportunities; create and implement plans to address these opportunities</li>
<li>Responsible for ensuring that all financial (invoices, reporting) and personnel/payroll related administrative duties are completed accurately, on time and in accordance with company policies and procedures</li>
</ul>
