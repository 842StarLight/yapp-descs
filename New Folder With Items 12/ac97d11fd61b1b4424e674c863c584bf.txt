<h2>VENDOR ACCOUNTS RECEIVABLE CLERK</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<b>Position Summary Description:</b><br>
Maintains vendor rebate programs utilizing Trackmax software. Upkeeps vendor contact information file. Processes and distributes all related vendor rebate billings, as well as miscellaneous claims. Responsible for timely collections of payments from vendors, posts payments to vendors’ accounts, and prepares journal entries from Trackmax transactions.
<br><br>
<b>Essential Duties and Responsibilities:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Maintain vendor rebate programs utilizing Trackmax software.</li>
<li>Processes and distributes all vendor rebate billings and all miscellaneous claims billings.</li>
<li>Enter cash receipts into Trackmax and update vendor receivable ledgers.</li>
<li>Reconcile specific general ledger accounts and research and resolve discrepancies between Trackmax calculated sales rebates and bid file allowances.</li>
<li>Prepare journal entries from Trackmax transactions.</li>
<li>Scan and file check stubs and bank receipts.</li>
<li>Research and resolve differences between vendor billings and payments.</li>
<li>Answer accounts receivable and vendor receivable phone inquiries and follow ups.</li>
<li>Correspond with vendors as necessary in order to update accounts and ensure timely collection of monies owed to Y. Hata.</li>
<li>Uses the Company’s Mission/Vision Statements and Core Values as their guiding principles.</li>
</ul>
<b>Education/Training and Experience:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Associate’s Degree in Business Administration, Accounting or related field.</li>
<li>Two years of experience in Accounting, preferably in Accounts Receivable.</li>
<li>Must be able to perform basic to moderately difficult mathematical functions involving addition, subtraction, multiplication, division, averaging and percentages.</li>
<li>Must be able to demonstrate proficiency will all Microsoft Office applications</li>
</ul>
