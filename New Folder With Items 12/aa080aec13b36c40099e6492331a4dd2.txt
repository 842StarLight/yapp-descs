<p><b>
GENERAL FUNCTION</b>: The Business Banking Client Manager will assist BBRMs with loan applications, CRM and iris updates, preparing and completing relationship reviews, assisting with documentation of various products and services, ensure new deposit funding, assist BBRMs with COI development, drive new solutions and cross sell by engaging partners, with a strong focus on treasury management, assist with various sales related portfolio management responsibilities, coordinate prospecting, blitz and promo activities for the RMs, ensure strong level of customer contact, and drive new ways to consistently enhance the customer experience.</p>
<p></p>
<p>
Responsible and accountable for risk by openly exchanging ideas and opinions, elevating concerns, and personally following policies and procedures as defined. Accountable for always doing the right thing for customers and colleagues, and ensures that actions and behaviors drive a positive customer experience. While operating within the Bank's risk appetite, achieves results by consistently identifying, assessing, managing, monitoring, and reporting risks of all types.</p>
<ul>
<li>
Assist the BBRM team in providing an excellent customer experience by acting as a secondary officer on all BBRM assigned relationships</li>
<li>
Assist BBRMs with calling efforts, blitzes and campaigns by developing calling strategies, assisting with potential targets, and looking for new ways to add meaningful touches to clients and prospects.</li>
<li>
Work with BBRMs on developing COI strategies.</li>
<li>
Review new deposit reports to ensure deposit account funding is as expected, and if not coordinate solutions to potential problems the customer is facing.</li>
<li>
Review calling activity and pipeline reporting with BBRMs to ensure all current and relevant information is captured.<br>
</li>
</ul>

ESSENTIAL DUTIES &amp; RESPONSIBILITIES:</b></p>
