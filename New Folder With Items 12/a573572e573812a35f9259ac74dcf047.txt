<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>ABOUT UPCLEAR</b></h3>
<p>
UpClear is a technology SaaS company with a global customer base that is changing how Consumer Packaged Goods (CPG) companies manage their Trade Promotions.</p>
<p>
UpClear’s BluePlanner software is a best-in-class platform for Revenue Management, covering Sales Planning, Trade Promotion Management (TPM), Analytics, and Optimization (TPO). BluePlanner has been acknowledged by the Consumer Goods Technology publication as one of the leading solutions for Trade Promotion Management. UpClear is growing quickly and has been recognized for the sixth year in a row (2014 to 2019) as one of the fastest growing private companies by Inc 5000.</p>
<p>
UpClear serves clients in 25+ countries across Americas, Europe, and Asia-Pacific from its primary offices in New York (US), London (UK), Paris (France), and Hong Kong (China), and branch in Sydney (Australia).</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader">
<b>
ABOUT THE ROLE</b><br>
</h3>
<p>UpClear is rapidly developing data analytics capabilities for the Consumer Goods industry, involving more and more data sources, and increased data point granularity. Efficient data integration is essential to breaking down information silos and ensuring our continuous success.</p>
<p>
The Platform Integration Engineer is responsible for further developing data integration architecture and capabilities for the UpClear SaaS platform. They will work with UpClear technical team as well as third party data providers and clients’ data teams.</p>
<p>
Ideal candidates have several years of experience working on data integration, including a good understanding of data processing, cleansing, and integration techniques.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
RESPONSIBILITIES</b></h3>
<ul>
<li>Support development of existing integration points and define the strategy for future integration solutions</li>
<li>Work with technical teams to consolidate customer processes</li>
<li>Work with client success teams to translate customer / business needs into technical procedures</li>
<li>Design and improve custom data-integration procedures for individual customers</li>
<li>Enhance and maintain our business intelligence platform to deliver improved analytics for end users</li>
<li>Provide support and streamlining of data consolidation processes</li>
</ul>
