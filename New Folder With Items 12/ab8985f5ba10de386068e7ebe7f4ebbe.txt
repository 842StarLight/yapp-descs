<h2>Administrative Assistant</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>R<b>ROCHE BOBOIS</b>, world leader in the high-end furniture industry is looking for a Sales Administrative assistant within its Upper East Side Flagship showroom.</p>
<p>His/ Her role will be to assist the sales staff in all aspects of the store business, from customer service to administrative tasks, through the assistance in the merchandising of the showroom and the processing of the purchase orders with our workshops. He/ She will be benefiting from an intensive period of training on Internal administrative procedures, sales techniques, corporate process and Roche Bobois collection knowledge.</p>
<p>The main responsibilities will be:</p>
<p><b><i>Administrative tasks: </i></b></p>
<ul>
<li>To work on the administrative reporting (Traffic, leads..) to the US headquarter;</li>
<li>To coordinate the after sales administrative process with the NY headquarter;</li>
<li>To prepare purchase orders to Roche Bobois factories;</li>
<li>To follow up the production process with factories;</li>
<li>To be the contact of local vendors;</li>
<li>To review customer files and the corresponding administrative documents.</li>
</ul>
<p><b><i>Merchandising: </i></b></p>
<ul>
<li>To prepare and update the store tagging (Prices, special promotions...) under the store manager supervision;</li>
<li>To assist the sales staff in updating the merchandising of the showroom, especially when new collections are implemented in the store;</li>
<li>To coordinate logistics and showroom transfers with our New Jersey Distribution center.</li>
</ul>
<p><b><i>Sales and Customers service: </i></b></p>
<ul>
<li>To welcome and greet customers when needed;</li>
<li>To assist the sales staff in providing/ presenting sales tools (tear sheets, sample books..) to the customers;</li>
<li>To prepare quotes when needed, under the sales staff supervision;</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Requirements: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Motivation to work in a sales environment;</li>
<li>Bachelor degree or equivalent;</li>
<li>Interest in the furniture business;</li>
<li>CAD and/ or 20/20 software knowledge a plus;</li>
</ul>
<p>Job Type: Full-time</p>
<p>Experience:</p>
<ul>
<li>customer service: 1 year (Preferred)</li>
<li>Administrative Assistant: 1 year (Preferred)</li>
</ul>
<p>Education:</p>
<ul><li>Bachelor's (Preferred)</li></ul>
<p>Benefits:</p>
<ul>
<li>Health insurance</li>
<li>Dental insurance</li>
<li>Retirement plan</li>
<li>Paid time off</li>
</ul>
<p>Schedule:</p>
<ul><li>Monday to Friday</li></ul>
