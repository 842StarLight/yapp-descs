<h2>IT Technical Alignment Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Summary</b></p>
<p>The Technical Alignment Manager (TAM) is responsible for engaging with current Rouse Consulting Group’s clients to prepare and deliver strategic technology reviews. The goal of the meeting is to clearly identify and communicate technical risks as well as the remediations so that the clients are able to make educated decisions that is best for their organization. Successful reviews culminate with clients being more in line with our policies which leads to less reactive work and a clear perspective of service being delivered.</p>
<p><b>Required Traits: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Customer-focused</li>
<li>Confident</li>
<li>Professional</li>
<li>Interpersonal</li>
<li>Good listener</li>
<li>Multi-tasker</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Roles &amp; Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Develop strategic relationships with RCG clients to schedule, prepare, and deliver technology reviews on a weekly basis</li>
<li>Engage with the network administrators or sales department with proper review follow ups tasks</li>
<li>Use review results to build fiscal budgetary and technical roadmaps for RCG clients</li>
<li>Assist with maintaining the standards and best practices library (add, remove or changes of standards)</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Knowledge, Skills and Abilities Required: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree from four-year college or university preferred</li>
<li>3-5 years professional experience in consulting or technical analysis</li>
<li>Excellent verbal, written, interpersonal and presentation skills with the ability to communicate effectively with a wide variety of audiences</li>
<li>Ability to present complex solutions clearly to nontechnical C level executives</li>
<li>Superior work ethic and high energy level</li>
<li>Organization and time-management skills</li>
<li>Moderate to high level of understanding of technology solutions preferred</li>
</ul>
