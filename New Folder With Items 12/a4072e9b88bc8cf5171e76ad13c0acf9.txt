<h2>Sales Operations Specialist - Enterprise Cybersecurity Software</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>At XYPRO, our mission is to protect our customers' data as if it was our own. The role of XYPRO Sales Operations is to make sure our international sales team is running as efficiently and productively as possible, so our customers receive the attention they deserve, as XYPRO’s strategic partners in the protection of their customers’ data.</p>
<p>
As our sales team’s number one supporter, you will own the creation and maintenance of the end-to-end tracking of sales processes and conversion rates. The information you manage will be used to inform sales reps and leadership on historical results, current performance, and expected future results.</p>
<p><b>
Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>

</li>
<li>
<p>Oversee contracts administration and liaise with Finance Team for Revenue Recognition</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Train new sales team members</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Serve as point of contact for sales team members for questions and logistics</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Develop daily, weekly, monthly and quarterly reports, Key Performance Indicators &amp; analysis of the effectiveness of campaigns like win/loss rates, pipeline growth, activity, quota attainment, etc.</p>
</li>

</li>
