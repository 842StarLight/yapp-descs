<p><b>Collections Manager – Raleigh, NC</b></p>
<p></p>
<p><b><i>What makes you successful in this role?</i></b></p>
<p>As a Collections Manager, you will be responsible for leading the finance department in collecting over-due payments from our customers. You will also assist in generating invoices for new sales that have been initiated from the Sales team.</p>
<p><b><i>Organizational Role:</i></b></p>
<p>You will report directly to our Director of Operations and our Financial Controller.</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Your responsibilities will include:</b></p>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Adding new customer general &amp; billing information into Zoura, as well as maintaining existing accounts.</li>
<li>Contacting customers for collection of past due accounts via phone calls, e-mail and letter correspondence.</li>
<li>Collaborating with the Sales and Customer Service departments to resolve customer disputes.</li>
<li>Actively monitoring the Accounts Receivable (A/R) aging for past due accounts and customers at risk of collection.</li>
<li>Working with third party debt collectors once debt reaches 120 days past due.
</li>
<li>Performing special projects as assigned.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Requirements</b></p>
<p><b><i>
What would we like for you to have?</i></b></p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Required:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>HS Diploma/GED, AA, or BS/BA Degree.</li>
<li>2+ years of experience in Billing, Customer Service, and Collections.</li>
<li>Demonstrated ability to gather and interpret data, compile information and prepare reports.</li>
<li>Demonstrated ability to interact professionally and effectively, with internal and external customers, both in person and over the phone.
</li>
<li>Ability to handle multiple tasks simultaneously while paying attention to detail.
</li>
<li>Proficient in MS Office Suite, including Outlook, Excel and Word.</li>
<li>Strong verbal and written communication skills.
</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Preferred:</b></p>
