<p>The Senior Analyst Payer Sales is responsible for planning and strategizing to retain and expand current customer relationships, client engagement activities, industry-specific relationship management activities, and direct business-to-business sales.</p>
<p></p>
<p>
Job Responsibilities</p>
<ul>
<li>
Planning and strategizing to retain and expand current customer relationships, ensuring high quality customer service and issue resolution in order to retain current clients</li>
<li>
Responsible for: client engagement activities including identifying client business requirements and preparing bids, proposals, quotations, and presentations; industry-specific relationship management activities, including brokering, financial planning, asset management, advertising account management, etc.</li>
<li>
Involved in selling directly to the end consumer/business purchaser through primarily field (i.e., face-to-face) interactions with customers, including: New sales to new customers as well as cross/up/repeat sales to existing customers; and assessing customer needs and suggest appropriate products, services, and/or solutions</li>
<li>
Other responsibilities as judgment or necessity dictate</li>
</ul>
