KPMG is currently seeking a Manager, Cloud Solution Advisor to join our Global Information Technology organization.</p>
<p>
Responsibilities:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Establish relationships with key technical and non-technical decision makers to help drive long-term cloud adoption to resolve business problems and develop impactful solutions</li>
<li>
Lead the team to produce compelling and workable solutions, including working directly with potential customers where needed. Act as the technical design specialist creating and / or approving solutions designs requested by the sales team</li>
<li>
Drive and deliver strategy through leading technology decisions and proving expertise solutions &amp; offerings</li>
<li>
Quantifies the needs of the business to achieve value outcomes, champions required methodology, captures/reuses IP and shares recommended practices</li>
<li>
Create up to date overview of customer needs/ opportunities and overall planning</li>
<li>
Support gathering of customer requirements and needs (regarding Cloud Services)</li>
</ul>
<p>
Qualifications:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Minimum of five years’ experience with architecture, design, implementation, and/or support of complex application architectures as well as a minimum of<br>
two years’ experience in public cloud services (Microsoft Azure required)</li>
<li>
Bachelor’s degree from an accredited college/university or equivalent work experience)</li>
<li>
Proven track record of building technical and business relationships with senior executives and growing wallet share in large or highly strategic accounts</li>
<li>
Excellent negotiation skills, with the ability to persuade and influence
</li>
<li>Experience with on-site, offshore, third party and remote team members</li>
<li>
Ability to understand ITIL processes, SOW’s, Affidavits, Agreements, SLA’s and KPI’s, cost of service and Risk and security</li>
</ul>
