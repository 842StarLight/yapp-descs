<h2>Business Development Representative</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Salary.com provides software and data to organizations who want their compensation strategies to be fair, equitable, and market competitive. We’re recognized by leading industry analysts as having the most innovative software platform in our space, and we grew new business bookings by 34% last year. We value training, continuous feedback, career development, and internal advancement as our keys to success. With this momentum, we are actively expanding our Business Development Representative (BDR) team as we look to meet our growth targets in 2019.</p>
<p>As a BDR here, you will be responsible for identifying and qualifying new business opportunities. You will get support from your fellow BDRs, sales leadership, marketing, and our team of certified compensation professionals. This job presents tremendous opportunity for candidates looking to start their career in sales as it offers high growth potential with more than 40% of our BDRs being promoted last year.</p>
<p>We want to train you so that you’re not only prepared for your BDR responsibilities, but you’re also ready to move into to your next career opportunity with us. We are committed to cultivating our future leaders and have built our BDR program for you to achieve multiple levels of promotion with increasing responsibilities and compensation.</p>
<p><b>What Salary.com BDRs do: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Conduct phone, email, and social media activities to potential prospects</li>
<li>Use sales intelligence tools to identify new companies and individuals</li>
<li>Qualify marketing generated leads to fully understand prospects’ needs</li>
<li>Design and execute territory plans with their respective Account Executive (AE)</li>
<li>Grow and expand their knowledge of the compensation management space</li>
<li>Actively participate in team meetings and weekly trainings</li>
</ul>
<p><b>What qualifications Salary.com BDRs have: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>0-2 years experience (training provided)</li>
<li>A bachelor’s degree or equivalent work experience</li>
<li>Strong written and verbal communications skills</li>
<li>A willingness to implement feedback and coaching to improve conversion rates</li>
<li>The ability to multi-task while working in fast-paced environment</li>
<li>Above average organizational and time-management skills</li>
<li>An optimistic outlook and a great sense of humor</li>
<li>A desire to meet and exceed measurable performance goals</li>
<li>A working functionality with LinkedIn, Twitter, and other social media tools</li>
</ul>
<p><b>What Perks Salary.com BDRs receive: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Medical, dental, and vision coverage</li>
<li>Company outings</li>
<li>Monthly sales contests</li>
<li>Guaranteed first year compensation of $50,000</li>
<li>10 paid holidays</li>
<li>Cell phone reimbursement</li>
<li>401k with generous company match</li>
<li>Base salary and uncapped commission compensation structure</li>
<li>Free snacks, coffee (iced too!), and beer &amp; wine</li>
</ul>
<p>Continuous training on product, industry and selling.</p>
