<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
MISSION:</b></h3>
<p>
ClickBank’s mission is to inspire, support and grow thousands of existing and new businesses worldwide to market and sell products and services that are potentially life-changing.</p>
<p>
ClickBank’s platform helps vendors around the world market and sell digital and physical products.</p>
<p>
We’re a team of full stack product-development engineers. We execute our mission by continuously and consistently improving the ClickBank experience with new product offerings and enhancements to our platform.<br>

<p><b>
SUMMARY:</b></p>
<p>
This person will work directly with the development teams to ensure high quality code across the Click Bank platform. This position includes designing, building, and maintaining the tests and test cluster written in Java and running in AWS.<br>
</p>
<p><b>
DAY to DAY:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Design, develop, and maintain new automated tests.</li>
<li>Work directly with other QA and Software Development teams to determine areas of the platform which should be automated.</li>
<li>Create infrastructure &amp; code to regularly and reliably test our products.
</li>
<li>Write well documented and maintainable automated testing code.</li>
<br>
</ul>
