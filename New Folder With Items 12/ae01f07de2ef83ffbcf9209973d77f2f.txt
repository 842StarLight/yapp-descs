<h2>Logistics Coordinator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>The Logistics Coordinator is responsible for reviewing upcoming orders and ensuring the Las Vegas branch has the assets necessary to fulfill upcoming demands. The Logistics Coordinator works closely with the Asset Management team in the corporate office to facilitate planning and movement of assets into the Las Vegas branch. The Logistics Coordinator is responsible for requesting equipment from other branches nationwide, as well as responding to equipment requests from the other branches, and scheduling transfers of such equipment.</p>
<p><b>Responsibilities: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Work with Asset Management on equipment planning for upcoming orders.</li>
<li>Coordinate with Warehouse Manager on inventory management and accuracy.</li>
<li>Communicate clearly with Sales Team on equipment availability and allocation for upcoming orders.</li>
<li>Run daily reports to identify equipment shortages and confirm inbound equipment.</li>
<li>Maintain accurate records of equipment allocation for large projects.</li>
<li>Schedule equipment allocation/transfers to other branches on shipping calendar.</li>
<li>Review Rental Agreements for equipment accuracy.</li>
<li>Review all shipping records to ensure shipping accuracy.</li>
<li>Ensure accurate and timely receiving of Purchase orders.</li>
<li>Other duties and responsibilities as may be assigned by the Operations Manager.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Qualifications: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>A minimum of 3 years' experience in warehouse inventory control and/or asset allocation.</li>
<li>The ideal candidate will be highly organized, possess excellent communication and customer service skills, be detail-oriented, and have strong work ethic with demonstrated ability to handle multiple tasks under minimal supervision.</li>
<li>Capable of reading and interpreting inventory data.</li>
<li>Outstanding written and oral communication skills.</li>
<li>Strong knowledge of Microsoft Excel.</li>
<li>Computer-savvy with a working knowledge of logistics software (ERP).</li>
<li>Problem solver and resourceful, with a can-do attitude.</li>
</ul>
