<p><b>Summary of Responsibilities</b></p>
<p>The MRO Buyer position is responsible for developing, maintaining andinvestigating potential new suppliers as well as current suppliers for new andexisting programs. The Buyer will create Purchase Orders, Request forQuotations and assist/work with various departments.</p>
<p><b>MRO Buyer Responsibilities: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Responsible for buying of materials, operating supplies, manufacturing equipment and services.</li>
<li>Procurement/Development of sources of supply.</li>
<li>Able to maintain a positive contribution towards quality, customer satisfaction and company profitability.</li>
<li>Maintain vendor file, purchase price, price fluctuations and quality records on purchased items.</li>
<li>Resolve material claims with vendors.</li>
<li>Work on projects, reports and contribute various studies as required.</li>
<li>Train other employees as required.</li>
<li>Insourcing, outsourcing, and resourcing parts as required.</li>
<li>Monitor back order report and min/max levels to ensure appropriate amount of inventory available.</li>
<li>Customs issues including importation and exportation of related items.</li>
<li>Negotiate pricing, terms and conditions and contracts.</li>
<li>Maintain relations with current vendors, conduct vendor visits when required, and develop new sources of supply.</li>
<li>Responsible for coordinating pre-production requirements to meet customer demands.</li>
<li>Responsible for resolving shortages and issues with suppliers and products.</li>
<li>Implement and maintain all MRO items in the MRP System</li>
<li>Perform other duties as required within the Purchasing Department.</li>
<li>Expedite operations to meet unforeseen conditions</li>
<li>Lead by example with 5S, wearing of PPE, safety and housekeeping practices</li>
<li>Promote and facilitate Open Door process, effective communication and Fair Treatment</li>
<li>Participate in Continual Improvement activities</li>
<li>Adhere to customer requirements, Quality Operating System(QOS) and company policies</li>
<li>Adhere to and support the Environmental Management System per ISO-14001 requirements and Federal Regulations</li>
<li>Understand your responsibility for health, safety and environmental in the workplace.</li>
<li>Cooperate with OSHA/State OSHA inspectors.</li>
<li>Wear the appropriate personal protective equipment.</li>
<li>Follow all safety rules.</li>
<li>Report unsafe conditions immediately.</li>
<li>Report injuries, accidents, illnesses, near misses, property damage immediately.</li>
</ul>
