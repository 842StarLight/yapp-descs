<h2>Solutions Architect</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Codal is a user experience design and development agency with a focus on blending an Agile process with the latest technologies. Our clientele has ranged from small business to the Fortune 100, but our philosophy has always remained the same: to empower brand visibility and deliver the most elegant web and mobile solutions possible.</p>
<p><b>
Roles &amp; Responsibilities</b></p>
<p>
Codal is searching for a highly skilled enterprise architect to act as an expert consultant and thought leader for Codal's marquee clients. With veritable experience in architecting large-scale systems with enterprise technology, our ideal candidate has a broad level of skills in architecting solutions including mobile, web, integration middleware, database &amp; data warehouse, front-end technology &amp; framework, security, and cloud infrastructure.<br>
<br>
Candidates must have proven experience in large-scale application architecture, as well as a firm understanding of cloud-native, microservices-based systems. Other responsibilities include:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Guiding engineers on proper development practices, patterns, and techniques for enterprise technologies</li>
<li>
Assisting in pre-sales work by detailing requirements needed to successful realize an enterprise client's vision</li>
<li>
Consulting clients in the strategic direction of business solutions</li>
<li>
Leveraging new and emerging technologies to fulfill project requirements and business objectives</li>
<li>
Advising on building technical roadmaps, defining modern enterprise architecture and guiding teams to deliver it successfully</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Requirements / Must Haves</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
5+ years of verifiable experience in enterprise architecture</li>
<li>
A complete and total mastery of how enterprise companies work and how to address their needs.</li>
<li>
Proficiency in back-end technologies and frameworks</li>
<li>
Excellent written / verbal communication skills</li>
<li>
Ability to operate in dynamic, fast-paced environment</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Why Work For Codal</b></p>
<p>
As a Codal employee, you're a member of a dedicated &amp; driven organization, composed of industry visionaries and auteurs. We take the utmost pride in our work and are truly passionate about the services we provide. As a world-class agency, we offer all of the benefits of an enterprise company, in a friendlier, tight-knit office community</p>
