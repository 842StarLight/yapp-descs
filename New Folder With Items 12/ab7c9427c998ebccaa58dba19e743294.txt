<h2>Manager, Core Business Operations, Core Industry Solutions</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<div>Deloitte Consulting LLP seeks a Manager, Core Business Operations, Core Industry Solutions in Chicago, IL and various unanticipated Deloitte office locations and client sites nationally.</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
<br>
<b>Work You’ll Do</b>
</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>Direct and coordinate activities of project personnel by managing day-to-day interactions and assigning duties and responsibilities to project personnel. Develop and deliver components of client engagements that identify, design, and implement creative business solutions for information technology projects with large companies. Responsibilities include monitoring and tracking project milestones and deliverables, and directing/coordinating the identification of business requirements, functional and process design, prototyping, testing, training, and defining support procedures. Perform the role of counselor and coach, and actively participate in recruitment activities and selection of project personnel. Provide leadership and support for delivery teams and staff in local offices by monitoring performance of project team members and providing and documenting performance feedback. Synthesizing overall operations strategy by analyzing a wide variety of business solutions. Selecting the most relevant tools/techniques to meet specific client requirements such as business case, pricing, or complex financial analysis and interpreting and reporting on outputs. Participating in setting engagement objectives and scope, developing work plans for components of engagements, coordinating activities between workstreams and identifying changes in scope. Performing risk assessments to identify and mitigate risks in process and technology, and developing response strategies. Working on and performing solution design, implementation, and maintenance of analytic solutions utilizing ARIS business process modelling, iRise simulation and CaliberRm. Analyzing current state workflows for registration, scheduling, order entry, order sets, structured clinical documentation, and claims, and converting them into future state in alignment with EMR products. Supporting end-to-end analysis, development, management, and delivery utilizing SharePoint, team foundation server, MS Visio, HP Quality Center, Jira, SQL, and .NET. Managing, designing, developing, testing and implementing custom developed applications in .Net, SQL, and Java environments across entire lifecycles of software development. Providing architecture, design, and development of SOA and other integration platform solutions to allow information sharing and management between disparate business partners, processes, and systems, including the support of large project initiatives including integrating disparate technical components to enable seamless end-to-end business processes. Aiding in the creation of test strategy, test plans, scripting for unit, system, integration and UAT phases for electronic medical record product launches using Agile and SDLC delivery methodologies. Designing test scripts, implementing automated testing, and performing SOA quality assurance using Java and .NET. Managing testing initiatives using Jira, HP Quality Center, and HP application lifecycle management.</div>
