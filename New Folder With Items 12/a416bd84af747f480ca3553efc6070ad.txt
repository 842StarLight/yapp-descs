<h2>Agile Technical Project Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">Are you interested in learning and growing each day? Join us, and unleash your potential while delivering value-centric solutions for one of the world's most recognized brands in the entertainment Industry. As our team builds and supports new applications at a rapid pace, we are looking for an exceptional PRS Tower Agile Technical Project Manager who will own and drive multiple critical Product Resort Sales (PRS) tower initiatives; which scan across multiple cross-segment Technology and Business teams.
<br><br>
The Sr. Program &amp; Project Manager is responsible for delivering complex large-scale E commerce software development across multiple disciplines within an assigned portfolio. This role blends program and project management and delivery, coaching and development of team members, and financial and business acumen. The Sr. Program &amp; Project Manager will oversee, on an annual basis, a combination of one or more programs with multiple project tracks, and multiple medium to large-scale digital projects across web and mobile applications. Programs may also include tracks outside of software delivery. The Sr. Program &amp; Project Manager ensures on-time and on-schedule delivery working closely with Product, Technology, and Design.
<br><br>
<b>Responsibilities:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Facilitate scope definition, cross-functional estimates and high-level planning workshops</li>
<li>Quantify cost factors to deliver program and project deliverables including resources, program and project oversight, objective risk and related contingency</li>
<li>Document key assumptions and dependencies established through estimation activities</li>
<li>Prepare and present high-level plan with estimates for executives and cross-functional leaders based on estimate outcomes</li>
</ul>
<b>Program and Project Planning:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Create full-scale master program and project schedules and communication plans consisting of multiple projects or components</li>
<li>Ensure proactive scheduling of key deliverables, milestones and deliverable tasks</li>
<li>Establish program and project financial forecasts including resource plans and other expense plans</li>
<li>Develop the program and project plans and schedules with cross discipline functional leads</li>
<li>Ensure necessary steps and dependencies to deliver programs and projects and their components are established</li>
<li>Validate that scope can be completed within timeline and budget</li>
<li>Estimate resource needs with help from functional leads</li>
</ul>
<b>Program and Project Delivery:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Confidently lead and drive cross-functional projects, components of large programs, and full programs through full delivery life-cycle ensuring project is delivered on-time and on-budget</li>
<li>Manage resource assignments, budgets, scheduling, and scope prioritization, and change control</li>
<li>Work with cross discipline team members to validate program and project assumptions and review individual program and project plans with senior leadership</li>
<li>Ensure proper documentation of scope of work with help from cross discipline functional leads</li>
<li>Serve as primary liaison among multiple teams, including Product Management, Design, Technology, vendors and other key functional delivery teams (when appropriate)</li>
<li>Communicate regular status updates to program and project executive leadership team on delivery progress, plan updates and budget spend and forecast</li>
<li>Monitor adherence to master schedule and milestone commitments</li>
<li>Analyze labor demand vs. capacity labor to ensure efficient resource assignments</li>
<li>Escalate key issues and risks to ensure timely resolution and mitigation</li>
<li>Prevent scope creep and facilitate change requests to ensure impacts to schedule or budget are approved and communicated</li>
<li>Use metrics to drive performance, efficiency and forecast improvements</li>
<li>Ensure proper program and project closure including retroactive/lessons learned, transition and hand-off to production operations support, and final financial reporting</li>
</ul>
<b>Supervise Delivery Project Managers:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>May provide leadership and coaching for a small team of project managers (2 to 5 team members)</li>
<li>Assist in identifying and analyzing project management staffing needs and team assignments</li>
<li>Assist in selection, on-boarding and on-going training for project management staff</li>
</ul>
<b>Preferred Qualifications:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Direct management and accountability for single project budgets of $5-10M in labor</li>
<li>Program accountability for program budgets of $11M+</li>
<li>7-10 years of relevant work experience in program and project management with an online retailer, ideally in eCommerce, marketing, merchandising, or operations. Consulting experience is a plus</li>
<li>Strong leader and decision maker with at least 4 years of experience in leading large, multidisciplinary digital teams (preferably websites, mobile web, and apps)</li>
<li>2-5 years Agile delivery experience</li>
<li>Strong experience gathering and developing requirements from eCommerce solutions</li>
<li>1-2 years mobile application (iOS, Android) delivery experience</li>
<li>Minimum 3 years of client management experience in successfully delivering internet solutions, preferably in a global client-service organization</li>
<li>Experience in a matrixed responsibility organization working with multiple Product Managers per effort</li>
<li>Possesses extensive experience in the basics of program and project management (estimation, program and project planning, resource planning, issue management, risk management, change management, communication planning), and coaching more junior team members in the PM practice</li>
<li>Experienced in multiple full lifecycle software deliveries from business development to final software acceptance</li>
<li>Led teams with multiple discipline representation (including product management/strategy, technology, design, SEO, and analytics)</li>
<li>Proven accomplishment as a driver of multiple programs and projects</li>
<li>Proven track record managing individual program and project budgets and budget roll-up of multiple programs and projects</li>
<li>Demonstrated analysis/assessment capabilities</li>
<li>Able to confidently lead teams to a successful delivery approach; ability to motivate, influence, and drive progress</li>
<li>Able to work both collaboratively and independently while maintaining a high level of productivity</li>
<li>Able to work under tight timetables and high pressure situations</li>
<li>Thrive in a fast-paced environment and able to juggle, shift gears, and maintain a sense of humor; deliver under extreme deadlines and intense business environment</li>
<li>Must possess excellent business communication, writing, and interpersonal skills</li>
<li>Proactive and solution-oriented when managing issues</li>
<li>Comfortable when communicating with executive level (Directors, VPs, SVPs) in presentations, working sessions, and 1-on-1 interactions</li>
<li>Practiced in negotiating with others in ways that result in win/win agreements and outcomes</li>
</ul>
