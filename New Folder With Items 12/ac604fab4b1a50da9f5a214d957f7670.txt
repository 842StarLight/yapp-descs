<h2>Teamcenter Applications Engineer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p><b>About the Opportunity</b></p>
<p>
Saratech is seeking an Applications Engineer for our Software Business Unit. Candidates must be comfortable with customer interaction and be willing to work in a variety of disciplines and across the full range of industries that Saratech supports.</p>
<p><b>
Responsibilities &amp; Objectives</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Provide pre-sales and post-sales support using the Teamcenter product suite;</li>
<li>Provide customer Teamcenter training on the software/products, product support, trouble-shooting and customer support;</li>
<li>Perform maintenance of Siemens PLM software specifically Teamcenter;</li>
<li>Work closely with and guide development towards the completion of work as defined or as assigned by management;</li>
<li>Remain up to date with newer improvements of Siemens software and its use;</li>
<li>Serve as a liaison between customer and sales</li>
<li>Travel to customer sites (as needed) for purposes of demonstrating of the software that will address the customer requirements</li>
<li>Answer technical questions from customers who require product assistance via the Company’s support line;</li>
<li>Perform analysis of customer’s problems and requirements to identify solutions, business propositions and quotations/proposals;</li>
<li>Prepare technical notes and presentations for use in Company’s marketing presentations, webinars and Company’s participation in conferences;</li>
<li>Respond to sales team and customer inquiries on a priority basis and with a sense of urgency.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Knowledge, skills and abilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>2-5 years of experience in engineering and/or manufacturing industries;</li>
<li>Comfortable using and deploying the Siemens PLM Teamcenter product suite of software</li>
<li>4 year degree in a technical field or equivalent;</li>
<li>Advanced organizational, interpersonal, and presentational skills;</li>
<li>Advanced written and verbal communication skills;</li>
<li>General computer skills in Microsoft Word, Excel, and PowerPoint.</li>
<li>Ability to travel up to 75%;</li>
<li>Effectively work as a member of a team and willingness to learn and adapt;</li>
<li>Ability to work remotely under general guidance;</li>
<li>Comfortable in front of an audience</li>
<li>Possess the necessary engineering background and experience to support sales activities and to support customers in the application, integration and operation of Teamcenter PLM Software;</li>
<li>Flexibility to cover working with teams in other time zones.</li>
</ul>
