<div>Goldbelt Hawk, LLC is a small, but growing 8(a) certified Small Disadvantaged Business located in Newport News, Virginia with a satellite office in Alexandria, VA. We provide diversified support services to Federal customers. Goldbelt Hawk has an immediate need for a Field Service Representative.</div>
<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<div>The Marine Sierra Hotel Aviation Readiness Program (M-SHARP), is an automated, government-owned database and software program that provides real time forecasting, scheduling, execution, and reporting of accurate data and information.</div>
<div></div>
<div>
<b>Job Duties:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Installs, updates and maintains M-SHARP software for local end users.</li>
<li>Provides local end user support as required.</li>
<li>Plans, develops and conducts end user training on the M-SHARP Application</li>
<li>Analyzes process and re-engineering, with an understanding of technical problems and solutions as they relate to the current and future business environment.</li>
<li>Identifies, documents and communicates common user or software issues to developers as required.</li>
<li>Creates process change by integrating new processes with existing ones and communicating these changes to impacted Business Systems teams.</li>
<li>Recommends and facilitates quality improvement efforts.</li>
<li>Manages daily tasking and priorities, participates in scheduled team meetings, and assesses requirements for software and database development needs.</li>
<li>Reports activity weekly and monthly.</li>
</ul>
</div>
<div></div>
<div>
<b>Required Skills:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Strong written and oral communication skills</li>
<li>Strong organizational and time management skills</li>
<li>Ability to communicate effectively with senior military personnel, government civilian staff and technical experts</li>
<li>Familiarity with the software development life cycle</li>
<li>Working knowledge of MS Office Suite</li>
</ul>
</div>
<div></div>
<div>

Bachelor's degree or 4-6 years of experience in the field or in a related area.</div>

</ul>
