<p>New Member Enrollment Outreach Specialist (overnights/weekends)</p>
<p>Are you looking for a new and exciting position working in healthcare? AbsoluteCARE,an Ambulatory Intensive Care Unit, is seeking a creative problem solver who is highly motivated and organized with an extensive knowledge of Baltimore and local hospitals. The <b>New Member Outreach</b> Specialist Hospital Overnight Worker will be responsible for outreaching to potential AbsoluteCARE members while in Emergency Department or inpatient unit to inform them of AbsoluteCARE and the benefits offered, helping them elect to sign up to become a member of the center. This is an overnight position, requiring working non-traditional hours that might include weekends. Hours might include 11pm-7am4pm-midnight or midnight-8am.</p>
<p><b>Duties and Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Work with the Director of New Member Enrollment to execute creative outreach strategies that will:
<ul>
<li>Locate and engage potential members, who might be hard to reach or locate, either by visiting Emergency Departments, or inpatient units</li>
<li>Attract potential members to sign up for the AbsoluteCARE program</li>
<li>Effectively explain the benefits of signing up for the AbsoluteCARE program</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>Outreach to families and/or caregivers</li>
<li>Communicating and working closely with hospital staff</li>
<li>Monitor CRISP hospital alerts and visit potential members in Emergency Departments or inpatient units</li>
<li>Schedule potential members for a tour or first appointment of the center</li>
<li>Report and enter daily outreach activities and update member’s /potential member’s addresses and phone numbers as needed.</li>
<li>Establish trust and build meaningful relationships with members/potential members</li>
<li>Promote AbsoluteCARE medical center as the “Go-To” resource for our patients members instead of using the emergency room or other fragmented higher cost medical resources.</li>
<li>Instill and promote AbsoluteCARE’s 5-star customer service philosophy in the medical center and in the community</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Qualifications:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Ability to work overnights and weekends</li>
<li>The ability to work independently</li>
<li>Bachelor’s Degree in healthcare related field preferred</li>
<li>Extensive knowledge of Baltimore hospitals and local resources</li>
<li>Prior experience working in a hospital setting a huge plus.</li>
<li>Prior experience working with CRISP preferred.</li>
<li>Ability to meet potential new members in Emergency Departments and inpatient units</li>
<li>Personal and professional passion to help improve healthcare delivery</li>
<li>Demonstrated respect for and experience with culturally diverse communities</li>
<li>Ability to build and maintain relationships with a diverse population</li>
<li>Strategic thinker: ability to analyze, develop and implement non-traditional solutions with results, understanding patient population and financial constraints</li>
<li>Ability to multi-task, highly organized, and great communication skills</li>
<li>Excels in developing real-world solutions and is quick to resolve and overcome obstacles</li>
<li>Creative and enthusiastic</li>
<li>Computer proficient in Microsoft Office, using emails, entering documentation in CRM, with the ability to learn new computer software</li>
<li>Team and customer service oriented</li>
<li>Previous Sales Experience Highly Preferred</li>
</ul>
<p><b>PHYSICAL DEMANDS</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Daily travel to local regionswithin Baltimore, using personal vehicle</li>
<li>Frequent walking required</li>
</ul>
