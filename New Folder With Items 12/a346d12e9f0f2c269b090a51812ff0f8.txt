<h2>Senior Network Engineer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>FirstLight Fiber is seeking an experienced Senior Network Engineer to join our technical leadership team.
</p>
<p><b>SUMMARY:</b>
</p>
<p>The Senior Network Engineer will provide Layer 2 and 3 expertise, designing the framework to strategically expand our network, while delivering packetized services to carrier and enterprise customers.
</p>
<p><b>ESSENTIAL DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES:</b>
</p>
<ul><li> Analyze and assess structure and behavior of expanding network systems including interior and exterior routing protocols.
</li></ul>
<ul><li> Author written network Engineering Design Packages including detailed implementation project plans and procedures.
</li></ul>
<ul><li> Collaborate with other operations departments to design approved infrastructure.
</li></ul>
<ul><li> Manage contractors and vendors during implementation phase.
</li></ul>
<ul><li> Provide technical and costing expertise to sales engineering teams during complex network developments.
</li></ul>
<ul><li> Continuously identify, review, and recommend potential opportunities for cost-savings, service quality improvement, and operational efficiency.
</li></ul>
<ul><li> Develop operational strategies such as Service Migration (grooms). End of Life (EOL) management, expansion, and vulnerability mitigation processes.
</li></ul>
<ul><li> Develop Technology roadmaps.
</li></ul>
<ul><li> Serve as the escalation contact during complex outages requiring Layer 3 analysis.
</li></ul>
<ul><li> Develop and assist in developing Reasons for Outages (RFO) and corrective action plans.
</li></ul>
<ul><li> Perform special projects and other duties as assigned.
</li></ul>
<p><b>QUALIFICATIONS, KNOWLEDGE, and SKILLS</b><b>:</b>
</p>
<ul><li> Minimum of five (5) years relevant experience in a Service Provider environment utilizing ISIS, OSPF, BGP, and MPLS.
</li></ul>
<ul><li> Experience architecting large scale IP networks and supporting system design as either the lead engineer or as part of a team.
</li></ul>
<ul><li> Experience with design and implementation of large scale/regionalized Route Reflectors.
</li></ul>
<ul><li> Experience with Carrier Ethernet design/deployment, focused on MEF compliant architectures.
</li></ul>
<ul><li> Experience with equipment and vendors such as Adtran NetVanta, Ciena CES, Cyan Z-Series, Telco Systems, Accedian, and Juniper MX Series.
</li></ul>
<ul><li> Thorough knowledge of Engineering Design Package (EDP) creation and execution.
</li></ul>
<ul><li> Proven ability to manage contractors and vendors.
</li></ul>
<ul><li> Functional working knowledge of Linux/Unix OS variants.
</li></ul>
