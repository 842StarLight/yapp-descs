<h2>Advertising Account Executive</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Successful and growing leader in the field of Guide Book &amp; Directory Publishing is seeking an additional Account Executive to sell &amp; market direct mail and online advertising.</p>
<p>Guide Book Publishing, founded in 1991, is an industry leader in publications serving the communications needs of non-profit organizations. GBP is a dynamic growth orientated company. Our customer base is national. The position will be headquartered in the Cleveland metro area with approximately a fourth of the time requiring travel.</p>
<p>Ideal candidate will have the following characteristics:</p>
<ul>
<li>Outside Sales Experience - Preferably at least three to five years of successful sales experience</li>
<li>Communication Skills - Ability to work the telephone to transfer leads into appointments and then into sales</li>
<li>Proactive – High energy level and can work independently within a team concept</li>
<li>Organized – Well organized to keep track of multiple clients at different stages in the sales process</li>
<li>Self-Starter – Ability to seek out new leads for clients with the self-confidence to overcome objections</li>
<li>Professional, honest and ethical</li>
</ul>
<p>Compensation Program:</p>
<ul>
<li>Base Salary (or Draw) and Lucrative Commission program</li>
<li>Bonus Program</li>
<li>Health &amp; Life Insurance</li>
<li>401K program with company match</li>
<li>Travel Expenses</li>
</ul>
<p>Job Duties (include but not limited to):</p>
<ul>
<li>Locate and contact potential clients to offer advertising services</li>
<li>Prepare and deliver sales presentations to new and existing clients</li>
<li>Process all correspondence and paperwork related to accounts</li>
</ul>
