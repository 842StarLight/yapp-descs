<h2>Sales Research, Analyst</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Overview
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div>The Sales Research Analyst is responsible for supporting Sales organization. You are a self-starter and will distill down data from multiple sources and extract meaningful insights that highlights ION as a highly effective vehicle for advertisers. You should have extensive research knowledge to translate metrics into effective sales positioning.</div>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<ul>
<li>Utilize quantitative and qualitative data to create thoughtful and innovative audience analyses in order to best position ION’s story in the marketplace</li>
<li>
Manage and prioritize requests from various sides of the Ad sales organization, including quick turnaround ad hoc requests</li>
<li>
Collaborate with Analytics team maintain clean datasets, provided either internally or licensed from vendors</li>
<li>
Use an extensive range of research tools and databases to identify key metrics, trends and insights that successfully position ION
</li>
<li>Apply a curiosity around media, television, and consumer engagement with media content to prepare data summaries that speak to ION’s wide and engaged television audience</li>
<li>
Strike the balance between informed, deep insights and summaries that are concise and coherent</li>
</ul>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Qualifications
</b></h2>
<div><div><div><ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree in a quantitative discipline preferred</li>
<li>
3-8 years’ work experience in media or advertising analytics, i.e. TV network, advertising agency or client experience positioning data for ad sales teams and clients; ability to "tell a story" with data</li>
<li>
Hands-on experience with television measurement tools and consumer data sources (Nielsen Media Research, GfK MRI, Simmons, Kantar, Catalina, iSpot etc.)
</li>
<li>Ability to interpret data to create a compelling narrative
</li>
<li>Excellent verbal, written, data visualization and presentation skills</li>
<li>
Expert knowledge of Microsoft Excel and PowerPoint, as well as excellent written and verbal communication skills</li>
<li>
Must have unrestricted work authorization to work in the United States</li>
</ul></div></div></div>
