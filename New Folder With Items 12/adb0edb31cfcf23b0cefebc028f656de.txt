<h2>Staff Product Engr</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div><div>
<div><div>
<div>Participate in all aspects of new product introduction (NPI) and development from planning to production ramp for IOT products.
</div>
<div>Good knowledge and experience of RF test program and ATE hardware maintenance; qual hardware design and burn_in patterns debugging; product characterization; qualification; yield improvement; FA/RMA activities; safe launch for production ramp.
</div>
<div>Full ownership of product characterization deliverables from design phase to production including post-silicon debug and ATE characterization of IOT products.</div>
<div>Work with Design and Test engineers on correlation of ATE and system bench environments</div>
</div></div>
<div>
<div>
<b>
Skills</b>
</div>
<div>
<div>Interact with appropriate functional teams (design, technology, manufacturing, assembly, quality, reliability, marketing, sales, etc.) to execute NPI goals.</div>
<div>Some customer interface may be required.</div>
<div>Good understanding of semiconductor technology and wireless product operation.</div>
<div>Solid understanding of the ASIC design and experience on RF devices</div>
<div>Working knowledge of fab process and technologies</div>
<div>Solid understanding of the effect of fab process parameters on device performance and yield.</div>
<div>Strong understanding of common failure modes / mechanism and screening techniques. FA experience is highly desired.</div>
<div>Strong problem solving techniques and debugging skills</div>
<div>Advanced statistical data analysis skill; proficient with statistical analysis tools and concepts</div>
<div>Automatic Test Equipment (ATE) experience, Teradyne uFLEX, and Advantest 93K preferred</div>
<div>Software skills: script writing (perl); excel macros and visual basic; C (C+) desired</div>
<div>Experience using evaluation boards (EVB) and software tools is preferred.</div>
<div>Good written and verbal communication skills</div>
<div>Ability to work independently or with a team to solve problems</div>
<div>BS or MS in Electrical or Computer Engineering with 5+ years direct experience in semiconductor industry</div>
</div>
