<h2>Call Center Agent</h2>

<p><b>POSITION SUMMARY: </b></p>
<p>The Inbound Appointment Setter will manage leads, handle customer service calls, schedule appointments, and engage in inside sales activities to achieve Circle L’s solar program sales goals. This position requires extensive customer communication and relationship management via phone and online platforms.</p>
<p><b>Title: </b>Inbound Appointment Setter</p>
<p><b>Hours: Monday-Friday 3:30 pm - 7:30 pm Saturday 10:00 am - 6:00 pm</b></p>
<p><b>Location: Work from home with a reliable internet connection/ Must be located in Ft. Worth, TX</b></p>
<p><b>Salary: </b>$10/hr + Bonus for appointments set/closed</p>
<p><b>REQUIREMENTS</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Excellent customer service skills</li>
<li>Prior sales experience a plus (inside/telesales, retail, customer service, solar)</li>
<li>Proven success and track record of meeting and exceeding sales performance metrics; creating delightful customer and employee experiences</li>
<li>Excellent written and verbal communication skills, outstanding presentation and organizational skills</li>
<li>Proficiency using mainstream PC applications (Word, Excel, PowerPoint, etc.)</li>
<li>Experience using CRM and lead management software solutions, Zoho preferred</li>
<li>Rock-solid time management skills</li>
<li>Ability to overcome and handle concerns</li>
<li>Team player – Ability to work in a collaborative environment</li>
<li>Ability to adjust quickly to changing priorities and conditions</li>
<li>Follow through – lives up to verbal and written commitments</li>
</ul>
<p><b>PRIMARY RESPONSIBILITIES: </b></p>
