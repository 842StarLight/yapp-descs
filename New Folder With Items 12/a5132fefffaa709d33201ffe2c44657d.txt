<div>
Hillrom is a $2.8B leading worldwide manufacturer and provider of medical technologies and related services for the health care industry, including patient support systems, safe mobility and handling solutions, non-invasive therapeutic products for a variety of acute and chronic medical conditions, medical equipment rentals, surgical products and information technology solutions. Hillrom's comprehensive product and service offerings are used by health care providers across the health care continuum and around the world in hospitals, extended care facilities and home care settings to enhance the safety and quality of patient care.<div>
</div>

<div>
<b>
Description</b><div>
</div>
<div>
On the high-growth Care Communications team at Hill-Rom, the Care Communications Strategic Account Executive will grow current and future revenue at the nation's leading integrated healthcare systems and leverage our clinical IT and software applications portfolio to enhance outcomes for patients and their caregivers. As a Strategic Accounts Executive, you will have an opportunity to establish and maintain multilevel relationships with Hill-Rom’s strategic accounts for care communications solutions. This unique role includes identifying sales potential, negotiating and closing opportunities, delivering clinical and operational outcomes. You will collaborate in a matrixed sales organization, leveraging communication of shared customer strategies. Hillrom’s portfolio includes high-value clinical applications that can help our customers drive improvements in patient and caregiver communication, clinical outcomes, and clinical workflow. You will partner with cross-portfolio sales executives to target key accounts and close IT and software solution sales that aim to protect patients and improve caregiver and patient satisfaction. This is a field-based role with geographical flexibility.</div>

ESSENTIAL DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES: – Other duties may be assigned:</div>
<div><ul>
<li>
Use health economics data to develop territory business plans to meet/exceed assigned sales goals</li>
<li>
Own all relationship with IDNs / strategic accounts for Care Communications’ products, including increasing adoption, ensuring retention, expansion</li>
<li>
Establish a trusted/strategic advisor relationship with corporate IDN and health system leaders to maximize credibility with customers and partners</li>
<li>
Partner with IDN leaders to establish critical goals or other key performance indicators and aid the client in achieving their goals</li>
<li>
Consistently deliver on orders target for assigned accounts</li>
<li>
Gain deep understanding of IDN budget processes and processes for approval of capital</li>
<li>
Proactively source and drive new business by capturing previously untapped customers as well as growing existing installed base for Hillrom Care Communications solutions, including clinical communication applications, nurse call/communication, real time locating, patient safety applications, connectivity, and other new products and solutions as they are introduced.</li>
<li>
Deliver effective presentations to promote the value of Hillrom solutions as drivers for clinical outcomes and with technical insight that provides customers with confidence and assurance that Hillrom solutions complement their IT environment</li>
<li>
Interact effectively with all roles within the C-Suite via strong executive presence, and the tenacity and health economics’ savvy to gain access to this audience for meetings that drive the business forward.</li>
<li>
Coordinate quoting and deliver proposals to customers that align with their needs, budget, and processes while managing discounts to protect margins for the business</li>
<li>
Understand customer’s buying processes well and effectively navigate the customers’ contracting process in partnership with Hillrom legal and business resources</li>
<li>
Effectively forecast opportunities in salesforce.com, inclusive of capturing next steps in the process, sales stages, probabilities, close dates and deal sizes. Opportunities should be managed closely and funnels updated weekly.</li>
<li>
Maintain and grow industry and product knowledge by staying up-to-date on healthcare trends, technology and economics.</li>
<li>
Fully understand care communications’ operational and service process and be able to articulate processes to customers throughout the sales process</li>
<li>
Effectively negotiate and close deals, interfacing with key buying influencers, such as clinical users and IT, as well as department heads and the C-suite</li>
