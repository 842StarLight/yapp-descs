<h2>Paralegal (ZCCO)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Safran </b>is an international high-technology group, operating in the aircraft propulsion and equipment, space and defense markets. Safran has a global presence, with more than 58,000 employees and sales of 16.5 billion euros in 2017. Working alone or in partnership, Safran holds world or European leadership positions in its core markets. Safran is listed on the Euronext Paris stock exchange, and is part of the CAC 40 and Euro Stoxx 50 indices.
</p>
<p>In February 2018, Safran took control of Zodiac Aerospace, significantly expanding its aircraft equipment activities. Together with Zodiac Aerospace, Safran has more than 91,000 employees and would have around EUR 21 billion in adjusted revenue (pro forma 2016).
</p>
<p><b>Safran </b>is ranked among the Top 100 Global Innovators by Thomson Reuters and is featured on the "Happy at work" rankings. The Group places fourth on the Universum ranking for the favorite companies of newly-qualified engineers in France.
</p>
<p><b>Safran Cabin </b>is the world’s leading supplier of cabin interiors for regional, narrow and wide-body, business and military aircraft.
<br>
The company's product range includes integrated cabin interiors, overhead bins, galleys, lavatories, crew rests and cargo containers.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p>The Paralegal is responsible for providing legal assistance and support to Safran Cabin internal Legal department and Safran Cabin’ related business divisions and affiliates. This position requires the use of judgment and initiative in arranging job details in order to achieve objectives. This position reports directly to the Head of Legal of Safran Cabin and is located in Huntington Beach (CA-USA).
</p>
<p>This position interacts with Safran Cabin attorneys, staff and senior management on a routine basis and occasionally represents the Legal Department in various tactical activities. An integral member of the Legal Department team, the Paralegal will be expected to assist in various tasks designed to improve the department’s effectiveness while reducing costs. The Paralegal must also have the highest client management skills, ensuring that internal clients are aware of the status of matters while managing often competing expectations with diplomacy and understanding.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Duties and Responsibilities:
</b></p>
<p>Preparing and filing of:
</p>
<ul>
<li>Ability to assist on the implementation of the Group policies and procedures sponsored by Safran Group Legal Department and Group Trade compliance rules and procedures;
</li>
<li>Prepares, maintains, and distributes Trade compliance information as requested and needed;
</li>
<li>Assist with contract negotiation, drafting and redlining including preparation of non-disclosure agreements;
</li>
<li>Create, maintain and administer contract database;
</li>
<li>Work closely with subsidiaries and affiliates relating to corporate, insurance and trade compliance issues with ability to troubleshoot issues in a timely manner;
</li>
<li>Create templates and work instructions for Legal Department staff and internal customers who require Legal Department support;
</li>
<li>Create and maintain databases, trackers, calendars of legal tasks, projects and information;
</li>
<li>Additional duties and responsibilities, as assigned, in support of the various functions of the Legal department and organization.<br>
 </li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Essential Skills:
</b></p>
<ul><li>Attention to detail, conscientiousness, keen observation, consistency, reliability and ability to work independently.
</li></ul>
<ul>
<li>Ability to perform high quality work in a deadline-based environment.
</li>
<li>Excellent organization skills, and oral and written communication skills; as well as the capacity to be able to work under pressure to meet deadlines.
</li>
<li>Ability to interface effectively with attorneys and staff and others internally and externally to develop and maintain good working relationships.
</li>
<li>Ability to work with minimal supervision, set priorities, be self-motivated, meet routine deadlines, and resolve basic to intermediate problems with an understanding of the business and legal environments.
</li>
<li>Being viewed by company attorneys as an important asset.
</li>
<li>Ability to be a productive and cooperative team member.
</li>
</ul>
<ul><li>Ability to interact effectively and in a professional and supportive manner with persons in all job responsibilities and from all backgrounds.<br>
</li></ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Specific Qualifications:<br>
</b></p>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Four to six years of applicable work experience.
</li>
<li>Paralegal certification required.
</li>
<li>Experience at a multinational company preferred.
</li>
<li>Proficient in Microsoft Office suite, including MS Word, Excel and PowerPoint.
</li>
<li>Experience in database, document and software management (e.g., Concur and Contract Logix).
</li>
<li>Legally authorized to work in the US without company sponsorship.<br>
</li>
</ul>
