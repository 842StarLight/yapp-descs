<b>Overview</b><b>

<div><div><div>
<div>If you are a motivated individual with a technical background and driven to succeed, we have a great sales opportunity for you in our <b>Denver, CO</b> market!</div>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
The <b>Account Executive - Hosting</b> is responsible for selling solutions and services to the key executives and staff at target companies. Focus of these sales efforts will be exclusive to Hosting Solutions (Colocation, Cloud, Managed Services, Application Management, Advanced / Professional Services, and Implementation Services). Although this position may also identify hardware resale opportunities, pursuit of resale opportunities would occur in partnership with a Hardware Account Executive.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
The <b>Account Executive - Hosting </b>also pursues new clients within the geographic territory and/or account lists as outlined by leadership. Contributions expected include support of the business unit and effective collaboration within the organization to achieve organizational goals. General responsibilities include, but are not limited to:</p>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader">
<b>Responsibilities</b><b>
</b>
</h2>
<div><div><div>
<ul>
<li>Partnering to develop successful strategic sales pursuit plans of targeted accounts</li>
<li>
Pursuing new customer relationships to achieve projected revenue growth, conducting cold calls and professional networking.</li>
<li>
Developing and maintaining relationships with customers, partners, and coworkers.</li>
<li>
Gathering prospect requirements and participating in technical solution designs.</li>
<li>
Developing professional presentations, correspondence, and responding to RFPs.</li>
