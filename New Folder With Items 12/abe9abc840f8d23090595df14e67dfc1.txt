Product Development Engineer<p></p>
The product development engineer is responsible for the coordination of designing new and existing products. This position is responsible for managing a product development process that fulfills Niagara’s or customer goals while collaborating with different internal departments for prototyping, testing procedures, manufacturing capabilities/ specifications, supply chain and marketing.<p><b>
Detailed Description</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Collaborate with the Sales team and customers on new product concept along with the following:<ul>
<li>
Identify the needs and goals for new product, from function to aesthetics.</li>
<li>
Evaluate ROI and manage budget costs for product development projects.</li>
<li>
Work with R&amp;D team on product concept, prototyping, and design.</li>
<li>
Work with packaging team to test and develop specifications and requirements for prototype/ new products</li>
<li>
Manage the scope on new product rollouts and work with vendors and the end customer to identify any packaging requirements and solutions for any issues raised by the customers.</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>
Manage the product development process in Oracle Agile while collaborating with key stakeholders to track progress on all new products, flavors and packages. Continue to innovate, improve and optimize this process for the product development department and R&amp;D components.</li>
<li>
Research and benchmark competitive products, comparing design and performance data.</li>
<li>
Creating prototype designs, case mock-ups, and renderings for customer related presentations and projects.</li>
<li>
Develop and manage relationships with customers and sales team members to participate in customer visits and customer related inquires for department.</li>
<li>
Assist in project planning and provide design/ technical guidance in project evaluation.</li>
<li>
Lead, participate in or support the following product development activities:<ul>
<li>
Concept, planning, design and execution stages of major new products or product enhancements</li>
<li>
Risk assessment</li>
<li>
Prototyping and testing concept designs and initial engineering builds</li>
<li>
Pilot Production</li>
<li>
Design reviews</li>
<li>
Design iterations</li>
<li>
Design verification testing</li>
<li>
Product validation and usability testing</li>
<li>
Pre-production</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>
Please note this job description is not designed to contain a comprehensive list of activities, duties or responsibilities that are required of the employee for this job. Duties, responsibilities and activities may change at any time with or without prior notice.</li>
</ul>
