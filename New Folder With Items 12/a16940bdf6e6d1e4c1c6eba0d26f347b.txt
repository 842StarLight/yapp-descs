<div></div>
<br>
<div>Join our team now at Siemens Healthineers as Director of Surgery Market Development.</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div></div>
<div><b>Position Overview
</b></div>
<div></div>
<div>The Director of Surgery Market Development will be responsible for developing a zone-specific surgery growth strategy such that we sustainably grow our surgery market share for both mobile and fixed c-arms.<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>The role will support in KOL engagements, improve our approach to reference sites, support in gaining internal mindshare through engagements with the sales organization, education and direct customer engagement.<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>This role will serve to help grow our sales success in close cooperation with the Advanced Therapies (AT) Area Vice President, our Zone Sales Management team and our KOL Surgery Lead, to increase our visibility and relevance in surgery. The role will also work closely with the marketing team to gain a deep understanding of our strategic direction for Surgery to implement within the zone.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
This role will drive select strategic engagements and sales opportunities, while also driving the broader Siemens U.S. Surgery strategy. This position will also engage with current and future key opinion leaders and industry partners to increase our success both in the zone and nationally, as a strong solution partner.<br>
</div>
<div>
</div>
<div><b>Responsibilities
</b></div>
<div></div>
<ul><li><b><i>SU Market Development:</i></b></li></ul>
<div>Develop the SU market in the zone through an understanding of the business conduct in the zone, market segmentation, customer segmentation, and tie to the resource and financial investment plan to grow the market</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<ul><li>
<b><i>Customer Relationship Management:</i></b>
</li></ul>
<div>Engage with our local zone and regional management and sales teams to identify key customers where Siemens success is critical, and act as primary owner of our engagement and sales strategy. Maintain and build close relationships with important accounts and key opinion leaders, and help to develop strong regional reference sites. Identify critical customer decisions, trends, and assess business implications for Siemens Surgery Business Line. Target top surgery projects and tactical strategies to win and grow share. Work with Director of Professional Development to develop regional Centers of Excellence and develop peer to peer training events.</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<ul><li>
<b><i>Siemens and Industry Partner Engagement:</i></b>
</li></ul>
<div>Serve as primary regional contact for our industry partners and grow regional/zone based relationships to maximize impact of these partnerships for our customers and our success. Develop with our partners peer to peer training events to highlight an imaging for outcomes procedure based approach. Explore opportunities to pilot initiatives within key IDNs and/or surgery customers that will enhance Siemens value proposition and solutions portfolio. Attend key trade shows and industry events. Structure and implement zone based clinical and procedural ongoing quarterly training events for AE’s, RVPs, PSEs. AVPs, consisting of web-based surgery conferences, one on one field rides, and regional meetings.<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<ul><li>
<b><i>Business Management:</i></b>
</li></ul>
<div>Assess the strategic position and customer perception of Siemens surgery solutions in US customer segments (example – IDNs, academic hospitals, OBLs…). Assist Business Line Vice President in finalization and execution of a Surgery Strategic Plan. Help to drive increase in market share, customer satisfaction, and market knowledge in Surgery. Track and report regional market and sales data to gauge success of our strategy.</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div></div>
