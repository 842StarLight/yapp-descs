<h2>Direct Sales Manager</h2>

<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Overview
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>

<br>
<div><b>
GENERAL PURPOSE:</b></div>
<div>
Sales Manager is responsible for meeting revenue objectives generated from recruiting, hiring and managing a team of Account Executives/Senior Account Executives who sell TPx services to their defined user base of small/medium size businesses.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div><b>
SUPERVISION RECEIVED:</b></div>
<div>
Works under the supervision of the Director of Sales for the field office.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div><b>
SUPERVISION EXERCISED:</b></div>
<div>
Directly supervises a team of sales professionals.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div><b>
ESSENTIAL DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
Required to maintain high levels of professionalism as a representative of TPx to the business community and employees.</li>
<li>
Will hire, lead, and oversee a team of Sales Professionals, which is segmented to work effectively with small to mid-sized enterprise accounts</li>
<li>
Must have a proven track record recruiting and developing Sales talent resulting in high performance against quantifiable business targets</li>
<li>
Develops strategic territory coverage plans and tactical activity standards and reporting procedures.</li>
<li>
Responsible for fostering high individual and team morale</li>
<li>
Ability to drive personal or company vehicle is an essential function of this position in accordance with company policy.</li>
</ul>
<div><b>
DESIRED MINIMUM QUALIFICATIONS:</b></div>
<div>
Education and Experience:</div>
<ul>
<li>
Graduation from an accredited four‑year college or university with a degree in business administration or a related field, and</li>
<li>
Four (4) years of experience in Direct Sales for a telecommunications company, or</li>
<li>
Any equivalent combination of education and experience.</li>
<li>
Valid Drivers’ License, Clean DMV</li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<br>
<div><b>
NECESSARY KNOWLEDGE, SKILLS AND ABILITIES:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
4+Telecommunications industry background and knowledge (local telephone preferred)</li>
<li>
3+ years of direct sales management</li>
<li>
Strong team management/leadership capabilities are required.</li>
<li>
Excellent oral, written, time management and presentation skills.</li>
<li>
Must be capable of balancing between fulfilling customers needs and meeting company expectation and goals.</li>
<li>
Knowledge of computer and word processing systems and programs (i.e. MS Office, Excel)</li>
<li>
6 months with TPx as a sales representative preferred with 100% year to date booked sales revenue.</li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<br>
<div><b>
SPECIAL REQUIREMENTS:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
Will be required to drive for customer prospecting and appointments (52% of job).</li>
<li>
Must have a clean DMV with a maximum of 3 points on driving record</li>
<li>
No DUIs within the past 3 years</li>
</ul>
<div><b>
TOOLS AND EQUIPMENT USED:</b></div>
<div>
Personal computer, including word-processing, database and spreadsheet programs; calculator, telephone, copy and fax machine.</div>
