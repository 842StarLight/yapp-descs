<h2>Product Marketing Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Position Overview</b><br>
As a Product Marketing Manager, you will play a critical role in positioning, launching, and evangelizing the Validity products at a global level. You will have responsibility for product messaging, positioning, content and collateral strategy, pricing, product launches, as well as orchestrating and managing our go-to-market strategy. This position requires a diverse breadth and depth of marketing knowledge and technical knowledge. You will generate market insights that lead to increasing market share and sales velocity across a competitive landscape.</p>
<p>
The Product Marketing Manager will have close interaction with product managers as well as sales enablement, business development, sales, brand, marketing, and training teams.</p>
<p><b>
Company Overview</b></p>
<p>
Validity was formed to bring confidence to customers who rely on data every day to run their business. As a global industry leader in data quality, our role is to ensure our customers have the tools they need to tackle the complexities of data administration and regain certainty with the integrity of their data assets.</p>
<p>
Our Trust Platform is comprised of software services and solutions that help customers know where they stand with their data and enable an ongoing assessment and toolset to correct data issues. For over a decade, tens of thousands of administrators in over 20 countries across the world have trusted our products to regain integrity with their CRM data.</p>

Essential Position Duties and Responsibilities</b></p>
<p>
Market Analysis, Positioning &amp; Messaging</p>
<ul>
<li>
Define the market opportunity for products and bundles.</li>
<li>
Perform market validation and segmentation exercises.</li>
<li>
Continually monitor the pulse of the global market. Analyze competitors' products/activities, determine differentiation, and create competitive advantage.</li>
<li>
Define and execute the market development strategy to build new categories, foster brand association, and increase market awareness and education internationally.</li>
<li>
Drive product naming, pricing, and positioning through qualitative and quantitative research.</li>
<li>
Build positioning and messaging for owned products in concert with positioning and messaging for other lines of business.</li>
<li>
Collaborate across the wider marketing team on key messages for research and thought leadership, lead generation, customer acquisition, partner development, events, and more.</li>
<li>
Build content for relevant content for buyers to consume in a variety of formats, including web pages, video, online events, and industry conferences, in collaboration with available internal resources.</li>
</ul>
<p>
Product Strategy &amp; Launch</p>
<ul>

Develop vision for global product strategy, working with the product teams to create value propositions that address regional market needs, depending on business environment, competition, and target audience.</li>
<li>

<li>
Work cross-functionally to build comprehensive go-to-market product/service plans.</li>
<li>
Manage messaging, pricing, and go-to-market strategy for acquisitions and additions to our product and bundle portfolio.</li>
