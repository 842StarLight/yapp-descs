<h2>Insurance Advisor - NC - September</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Who We Are:</b></h2>
<p>As a sales agent with SelectQuote Auto &amp; Home, you will speak to people who have responded to a variety of marketing campaigns, conduct an extensive needs analysis with these customers, and match them with the Auto and Home products that meet their needs. In this unique full time opportunity, our agents work in a fast-paced environment where performance is rewarded. You're helping families every day by ensuring they have the right amount of coverage without overpaying for it. If you're interested in joining a rapidly growing company with a winning environment, then apply today!</p>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>What We Offer:</b></h2>
<ul>
<li>Unbiased comparison shopping - We represent over 18 A-rated carriers that allow agents the flexibility of unbiased comparison shopping, to ensure we match customers with the products that meet their needs. We offer Auto &amp; Home insurance products, and an assortment of ancillary products including identity protection.</li>
<li>
All leads are provided – No cold calling or prospecting.</li>
<li>
High earning potential – Base plus commission with a 1st year average annual income of $70k. Top agents make well over $100k annually. Our agents have the ability to be their own CEO &amp; employees can take charge of their career.</li>
<li>
Growth opportunities – We offer work-from-home opportunities and leadership development training. We promote from within for those employees seeking career advancement between all three divisions, regardless of tenure.</li>
<li>
No prior insurance sales experience required – We provide highly compensated formal sales and insurance training to prepare you for success in this role.</li>
<li>
Full benefits – Including health, life, dental, vision, 401(k) + company match, paid time off, etc.</li>
<li>
Licensing - We cover the licensing and continuing education costs to get you licensed in multiple states and appointed with multiple insurance companies.</li>
</ul>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>What You Need To Be Successful:</b></h2>
<ul>
<li>Coachability &amp; willingness to learn - You'll go through an extensive training program to prep you about Auto &amp; Home sales and SelectQuote so you need to be willing to absorb this information and put it to use once you're on the phone, as well as be open to constructive criticism about how to improve your process.</li>
<li>
Competitive personality &amp; an internal motivation - You need to have a drive to overcome customer objections and compete with yourself and other agents to succeed and hit new goals.</li>
<li>
Time efficiency &amp; technology skills - Be efficient with yours and your customer's time. Learn when to move on from a call and when to continue the discussion. Be able to multitask and manipulate multiple screens including our software system, quote engine, and carrier applications while continuing a conversation with the customer.</li>
<li>
Positivity - It's important to be positive in this role and not let your previous call affect your current or future calls. The next call can be the one that gets you a sale!</li>
<li>
Flexibility - As business needs change and to accommodate our customers you may be asked to adjust your schedule accordingly. This may include working evenings and a few hours on Saturday on occasion.</li>
</ul>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Training and Experience</b></h2>
<ul>
<li>High school diploma or the equivalent is required, Bachelor's degree preferred</li>
<li>
Personal Lines or Property &amp; Casualty resident insurance license. If you're not already licensed, you must become licensed by a given deadline (licensing costs will be reimbursed after successfully completing our training program)</li>
</ul>
