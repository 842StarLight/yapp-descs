<h2>Graphic Designer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Bison Designs is an Outdoor Accessories company that has been in business for over 30 years. Located in Longmont, Colorado Bison Designs creates and manufactures products for the everyday outdoor customer. Bison is looking for a full-time, in-house Graphic Designer to join the team.</p>
<p>The Graphic Designer will design and prepare digital assets for marketing, including product campaigns, website, email support, digital advertising, photography, assist with product design and development. This person will work closely with the Sales Manager and CEO and play an essential role in the creative look and feel of marketing efforts supporting the brand.</p>
<p><b>Graphic Designer Responsibilities: </b></p>
<p>· Design and prepare graphic assets for digital and print including website, social media, email, trade show materials and advertising.</p>
<p>· Work with in house team to create product launches and brand campaigns</p>
<p>· Collaborate with third party social media coordinator to provide relevant content</p>
<p>· Assist with product design and packaging design as needed</p>
<p>· Photograph and edit product images</p>
<p>· Maintain File System Management</p>
<p>· Collaborate with team members to understand marketing strategy in service to project objectives</p>
<p>The Graphic Design position is a 40 hour a week position at Bison’s location in Longmont, Colorado. This is an entry level position that requires being on site M-F, 8:30am – 5:00pm. At Bison, we ensure our employees are taken care of with a strong compensation and benefits package including healthcare, 401(k) with company match, PTO, and of course, product!</p>
<p><b>Graphic Designer Qualification/Skills: </b></p>
<p>· Strong graphic design skills</p>
<p>· Proficient in Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator and InDesign</p>
<p>· Proficient in Microsoft Office</p>
<p>· Knowledge of MailChimp Email Marketing</p>
<p>· Layout skills</p>
<p>· Analytical skills</p>
<p>· Creativity</p>
<p>· Flexibility</p>
<p>· Attention to detail</p>
<p>· Deadline-oriented</p>
<p>· Time-management skills</p>
<p><b>Education and Experience Requirements: </b></p>
<p>· Bachelor’s degree in graphic design, marketing, or equivalent experience</p>
