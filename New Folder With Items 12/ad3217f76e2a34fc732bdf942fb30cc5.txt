<div><b>Work you’ll do/Responsibilities:</b></div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
</div>
<div>
</div>
<p>As a SAP Managed Services Manager, you will be responsible for coordinating teams of consultants to deliver across multiple SAP client outsourcing engagements with excellence. You will lead and drive sales of SAP Operate engagements. This position requires 40% sales and 60% delivery.<br>
</p>
<div>
</div>
<p>Managers are expected to contribute to the firm’s growth and development in a variety of ways, responsibilities will include:<br>
</p>
<div>
 </div>
<ul><li><p>Engagement Management: managing engagement risk, project economics including planning and budgeting, managing accounts receivable, defines deliverable content, ensures buy-in of proposed solutions from top management levels at the client<br>
</p></li></ul>
<div>
 </div>
<ul>
<li><p>Business Development: develops and maintains contact with top decision makers at key clients, organizes and leads pursuit teams, participating and leading aspects of the proposal development process, drives proposal pricing strategy.</p></li>
<li><p>
 Delivery Management: work directly with clients to ensure the quality delivery of services, enhance customer satisfaction, define and advance performance metrics (KPIs), resolving escalated conflicts, provide feedback to team, drive continuous improvement opportunities,<br>
</p></li>
</ul>
<div>
 </div>
<ul>
<li><p>Practice Development &amp; Eminence: development of solutions and methodologies, development of "thoughtware" and "point-of-view" documents, public speaking and publication in industry periodicals.</p></li>
<li><p>
 People Development: performs role of counselor and coach, provides input and guidance into the firms staffing process, and actively participates in staff recruitment and retention activities.<br>
</p></li>
</ul>

<b>The Team</b><b>: SAP Enterprise Managed Services</b>
</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
</div>
<div>Our SAP Enterprise Managed Services practice focuses on design, implementation, and on-going operations and maintenance of SAP applications and technology. Professionals have hands-on access and exposure to the latest technologies and thought leaders.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
</div>
<div>Deloitte’s Managed Services act as an extension to client’s organizations by bringing deep capabilities in advising, maintaining and enhancing technologies to serve alongside client teams to deliver insights across flexible economic models that align cost to consumption, delivery to realized outcomes and engagement accountability to a truly collaborative partnership.<br>
</div>
<div></div>

</div>
<ul>
<li><div>
Programmatic approach to and improved focus on optimization and innovation, not just on management and maintenance</div></li>
<li><p>
 Adoption of leading automation technologies to improve service delivery and efficiency</p></li>
<li><p>
 Better cost efficiency and increased speed to market for application development</p></li>
<li><p>
 Improved focus on delivering business initiatives and outcomes, rather than IT staffing<br>
</p></li>
</ul>

<div><b>Qualifications</b></div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
</div>
<div>
<b>Minimum required:</b><br>
</div>
<ul>
<li><div>
Minimum of 6 years of consulting or relevant industry experience</div></li>
<li><div>
Functional/technical proficiency with SAP - SAP ECC or S/4 HANA</div></li>
<li><div>
Strong domain knowledge and experience in at least one SAP functional area<br>
</div></li>
<li><div>ITIL Framework understanding and knowledge, ITIL V3 Foundations Certification or higher preferred</div></li>
<li><div>
3-5 years' experience as a senior engagement manager or equivalent</div></li>
<li><div>
Experience collaborating and developing client relationships, developing new opportunities with clients, and identifying engagement follow-on opportunities for continuous improvement</div></li>
<li><div>
Project scoping, estimating and planning experience</div></li>
<li><div>
Experience supporting the sales cycle, including but not limited to responding to Requests for Proposals, presenting at Orals, contributing to the creation of Statements of Work</div></li>
<li><div>
Excellent Interpersonal and networking skills; along with strong communication skills both verbal and written</div></li>
<li><div>
Experience across multiple industries</div></li>
<li><div>
Bachelor’s degree or equivalent required; Advanced degree preferred</div></li>
<li><div>
Able to travel 80% - 100%<br>
</div></li>
</ul>
