<h2>Marketing Operations Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Skedulo, a fast-growing SaaS company based in the FiDi neighborhood in San Francisco, is looking for a Marketing Operations Manager who can own the metrics and measure the impact marketing makes across the organization.</p>
<p>Skedulo is the platform for intelligent mobile workforce management. Our solution helps enterprises intelligently manage, schedule, dispatch, and track resources in the field, whether they are full-time, part-time, or contract employees. Skedulo is a flexible solution with many use cases that vary by industry and geography. This is a unique opportunity to join an exciting, rapidly growing company with a very ambitious marketing and growth strategy.</p>
<p>
At Skedulo, you will own the process and analytics of our demand generation programs across Skedulo’s Mobile Workforce Management solution, including campaign effectiveness and reporting, tech stack ownership, and continual process improvements within demand marketing and greater marketing teams.
</p>
<p>The person who excels in this role will be an expert in marketing analytics, marketing programs and lead generation methodologies across the top, middle, and bottom funnel. You will engage directly with our global marketing, sales, and business development teams to understand campaign objectives and offer continual feedback to maximize effectiveness through data insights and optimization.
</p>
<p>Skedulo is simply a great place to work. Our people are positive, supportive, and talented. We have big visions and ambitions, and we work together to realize them. We are at a stage of fast growth, which means you’ll need to stay focused, collaborative, and strategic as needs and headcount multiply. You will have the opportunity to develop professionally and be coached by people who care about your growth and want you to succeed.</p>
<p>
If you are a self-starter obsessed with metrics, eager to showcase your marketing acumen and learn some new skills along the way, this position is for you. Send us your resume and a short cover letter telling us a bit about yourself and why this job is interesting to you. We look forward to hearing from you!</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
FUNCTIONAL RESPONSIBILITIES</b></p>
<p><b>
OWN THE METRICS</b> - You will own campaign effectiveness across all marketing programs. Your key role in working with the marketing team is to show what channels are converting to pipeline and which are not.</p>
<ul>
<li>Work closely with Marketing, BDR and Sales Teams to regularly refresh target accounts for maximum data and pipeline coverage</li>
<li>Build prospect lists from in-house and third-party data sources to meet company objectives for Target Account engagement and Pipeline creation.</li>
<li>Manage strategy and data governance for leads/contacts and any marketing campaign related data and activities in marketing automation and CRM systems</li>
<li>Adjust dynamic lead scoring to increase the accuracy of our prospects and ensure BDR teams are achieving conversation rate expectations and pipeline goals.</li>
<li>Work with marketing team to regularly help develop and deploy nurture programs, digital display advertising, direct mail, and other demand tactics. This includes continual improvement and optimization of all marketing campaigns.</li>
<li>Oversee marketing technology stack operations and work closely with the marketing team on the overall plan for utilization and regular health checks on performance and effectiveness. Regularly interface with Sales Operation team on cross-departmental projects and issues.</li>
<li>Assist with building reports and dashboards to provide visibility in marketing campaign results and measure campaign effectiveness, including channel metrics, opportunity stages, pipeline contribution, and ROI. Provide campaign effectiveness feedback weekly, monthly, and quarterly</li>
<li>Lead technical aspects of marketing and sales system integrations including the marketing automation platform (Marketo and Engagio), analytics (Google Analytics) and SFDC as it related to target accounts and overall marketing health and measurements.</li>
<li>Monitor and maintain data quality for leads, contacts, and marketing campaign-related data and activities within the marketing databases.</li>
<li>Oversee day-to-day marketing budget, regularly report on investment and ROI to marketing team and leadership.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Requirements</b></p>
<ul>
<li>3+ years of B2B SaaS marketing experience either in demand generation or marketing disciplines within a corporate marketing team.</li>
<li>Heavy experience with Marketing Automation Platforms, with specific Marketo experience preferred.</li>
<li>Passion for ROI, connecting data across multiple systems to tell the full story of marketing results.</li>
<li>Familiar with Salesforce.com - including reports, data import/export, dashboard creation, etc.</li>
<li>Proficient in deploying emails, including A/B testing and campaign creation</li>
<li>HTML/CSS, Wordpress experience highly desirable.</li>
<li>Proficient in Excel</li>
<li>Drive. This is a great company for people who love to love their work.
</li>
<li>Focus and judgment. We are a growing company. You can always learn and do more stuff.
</li>
<li>A positive and collaborative attitude in the workplace.
</li>
<li>Bachelor’s degree in business-related disciplines such as Marketing, Economics, or Finance- or commensurate experience.</li>
<li>Proficient in Excel</li>
</ul>
