<h2>SDR Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p><b>SDR MANAGER
</b></p>
<p><b>JOB LOCATION: Frisco
</b></p>
<p><b>Position Summary:
</b></p>
<p>Are you interested in working for the hottest company in one of the world’s most innovative industries?
</p>
<p>GTT is disrupting the telecom/networking industry with its breakout growth, and was recently named to the inaugural "Future 50" - Fortune Magazine's list of the fifty public companies best positioned for even greater success in the coming years. We are looking for a decisive and results-oriented manager who can motivate, mentor and guide our inaugural inside sales team of Sales Development reps and contribute to our regional market strategies.
</p>
<p></p>
<p>This is a highly visible position in one of our largest global offices, and critical to the company’s long-term growth strategy.
</p>
<p></p>
<p>The ideal candidate will recruit and manage a diverse team of ten entry-level Sales Development Representatives. Key responsibilities include implementing business-to-business outbound lead generation campaigns, training reps on how to understand and leverage technology, and most importantly developing talent to be promoted to quota-bearing sales roles after 12-to-15 months in the program.
</p>
<p></p>
<p>The candidate we select will know what it takes to develop high-performing sales talent. They will create an environment that awards success and sets reps on an accelerated track to a career in enterprise sales at GTT.
</p>
<p></p>
<p>This is an exciting time at GTT. We have recently launched our industry-leading SD-WAN service and we are moving fast. If you are someone who enjoys rolling up their sleeves, diving in and building a team of great sales people, apply today.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Essential Duties and Responsibilities:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Hire, train and assimilate new Sales Development Representatives into GTT’s SDR Program
</li>
<li>Work jointly with regional sales leaders to build their pipeline and achieve territory goals
</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Ensure inside sales processes are executed consistently
</li>
<li>Monitor and hold reps accountable to activity-based KPIs
</li>
<li>Motivate your team to meet and exceed quotas and objectives
</li>
<li>Coach and deliver structured training on sales skills, product knowledge and market dynamics
</li>
<li>Assess the capabilities of each team member and work to fully develop them for a successful career at GTT
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Qualifications:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>B2B inside sales experience and 3+ years managing an inside sales development team
</li>
<li>Ideal candidate has built and / or scaled an inside team to support field sales goals
</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Knows how to leverage sales automation and tools to streamline efforts
</li>
<li>Has experience implementing outbound B2B phone-based campaigns
</li>
<li>Understands and has succeeded at prospecting, discovery and building a strong pipeline
</li>
<li>Has successfully sold into IT departments or has Telecom or Cloud Networking experience
</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>The ability to communicate with all levels of the organization
</li>
<li>Bachelor’s degree
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>To apply:
</b></p>
<p>If interested, submit a resume and cover letter addressing the above points. We look forward to hearing from you.
</p>
<p>GTT is headquartered in the Washington, DC metro area with offices all over the globe. We have built highly successful sales development teams in our Chicago, New York, and Southern California offices, and are recruiting to form a fourth team in Austin.
</p>
