<h2>Technical Recruiter</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Summary: </b>HR Job Recruiter is responsible for being the first point of contact for potential job candidates. Along with proactively contacting competitive candidates and build a talent pipeline for future hiring needs the requirements of this position are wanting a person who has a self-driven spirit and are competitive in spirit. We bring deep expertise in critical transformation technologies: API-first design, cloud platforms, data analytics and integrated security. Attracting talent and across the country to advance Federal projects by establishing client relationships and maintaining high satisfaction with the solutions we provide.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Required Education:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelors Degree</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Required Years of Experience:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>3+ years of combined HR/Recruiting experience</li>
<li>1+ years of HR/Recruiting experience working in the IT field</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Work with the business, HR, and Compensation to determine appropriate salary ranges and negotiate compensation with candidates</li>
<li>Interviews, screens and evaluates external and internal candidates for job openings to identify applicants who fully meet the position requirements of posted requisitions and refers them to the hiring manager for consideration.</li>
<li>Makes employment recommendations based on information developed through prescreens, interviews, reference checks, applications, and resumes, insuring the compensation offer is competitive, internally equitable, and affordable.</li>
<li>Coordinates and actively participates in the selection of the final candidates.</li>
<li>Recommend ideas and strategies related to recruitment that will contribute to the long-range growth of the company, implementing any new processes and fine-tuning standard processes for recruiting that fits within Liberty's mission to deliver the highest quality results to the customer.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Minimum Qualifications:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Strong desire to work with people</li>
<li>Prior recruiting or sales experience</li>
<li>Strong collaborative communication skills and an ability to work with members at all levels of experience and role in the company.</li>
<li>Ability to work with higher management</li>
<li>The candidate must be able to work collaboratively in a team environment.</li>
<li>Strong problem solving and critical thinking skills</li>
<li>Strong attention to detail</li>
<li>Ability to prioritize and multitask</li>
<li>Strong self-starter. Must be investigative by nature and willing to challenge the status quo.</li>
<li>The candidate must be able to work in a team environment and be proficient in MS Office applications, including Word and Excel.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Preferred Qualifications:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Experience in IT and software development environments, Federal contracting, or Professional consulting firms.</li>
<li>Microsoft SharePoint</li>
<li>2+ years of client-facing responsibilities working directly with hiring teams to build effective sourcing, assessment, and closing approaches with an ability to manage customer/partner expectations</li>
<li>Experience with Brightmove Applicant Tracking System or equivalent ATS</li>
<li>Preferred experience with college recruiting</li>
</ul>
</div></div>
