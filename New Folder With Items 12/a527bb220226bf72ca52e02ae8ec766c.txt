<h2>Registered Operations Leader</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>Manage operational structures/processes that support the business objectives. Responsible for leadership of branch staff, direct practice support for one or more advisors, and provide financial management/reporting and related tasks. Work directly with both employee advisors and the branch office leader and use the client relationship model and turnkey tools to assist advisors in serving clients.<br>
<br>
<b>Responsibilities</b><br>
<br>
<ul>
<li>Lead/Manage activities that support business results, revenue growth, client experience &amp; efficiencies in practice. Align/manage resources to achieve results for Organic: HVCA, GDC &amp; Net Flows, Plans, Retention &amp; DCM. Support producer growth strategy through design/execution of effective systems &amp; structure. Execute on a support team strategy around client acquisition via an effective structure, tracking &amp; impact. partner with BM to ensure Advisor business plans are completed/filed and marketing events are entered in Contact Mgr. Seminars are tracked &amp; reported.</li>
<li>Assist advisors/BM in servicing clients including preparing financial plans, conduct investment research &amp; complete trades and transactions. Prepare &amp; summarize client meetings by scheduling &amp; confirming meetings, entering data into Contact Manager, creating agenda &amp; summary of meeting, escorting clients to advisors &amp; providing follow-up. Prepare &amp; ensure new business paperwork processes successfully (prepare forms, obtain signatures, etc.). Provide general administrative duties (answering phones, processing reports, prepare correspondence, et-up files,etc.).</li>
<li>Lead/Manage foundational functions. Operational support (determine &amp; realign leadership &amp; staff needs). Create, manage, track &amp; analyze reports &amp; branch vitals (occupancy &amp; future needs). Review advisor exp reports, Branch P&amp;L, budgeting &amp; problem solving, reporting/correcting errors, identify &amp; implement exp reduction opps. Partner with RRS to ensure compliance support is established &amp; assess &amp; close training needs. Lead real estate &amp; facilities, Conduct Pride of Place surveys. Ensure Single Office Policy implemented, annual service pkg &amp; Customer Solutions Center utilization.</li>
<li>Human resource management. Staff all non-sales position openings, on-board, train &amp; performance management. Provide, lead &amp; participate in leadership, coaching, mentoring &amp; performance management for directs. partner with Sr Operations Leader &amp; RDO to determine staffing needs; request, hire and allocate staff. Conduct 1:1s with staff. Execute on staff development program and host monthly meetings with team.</li>
</ul>
<br>
<b>
Required Qualifications</b><br>
<br>
<ul>
<li>Bachelors degree or equivalent.</li>
<li>5 - 7 Relevant Experience Required.</li>
<li>Series 7 or ability to obtain within 150 days.</li>
<li>State securities agent registration (S63 or S66) or ability to obtain within 150 days.</li>
<li>State IAR registration (S65 or S66) or ability to obtain within 150 days.</li>
<li>Life, Accident and Health licenses &amp; Variable Contracts or ability to obtain within 150 days.</li>
<li>Previous successful supervisory experience including knowledge of HR policies and labor laws.</li>
<li>Excellent written and verbal communication skills; demonstrated ability to motivate team members to delivery results.</li>
<li>Ability to manage multiple priorities in a fast paced environment with little or no supervision and strong organizational/time management skills</li>
<li>Able to communicate with all levels within the organization.</li>
<li>Detail orientated, strong math, and analytical skills. Good organization and time management skills.</li>
<li>Process oriented and can work with a team. Proficient with standard business software applications.</li>
</ul>
</div></div>
