<h2>Traffic Coordinator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">The Traffic Coordinator is responsible for managing all aspects of in-house print advertising projects, pre turn in through final release to pre-media in support of Belk Sales Promotion print and advertising campaigns.
<br><br>
<b>Essential Functions:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Provides assistance for all Sales Promotion Project Campaigns.</li>
<li>Delivers/Creates Workload breakdown report – assigning graphic designers, production artists and copy writers to print media.</li>
<li>Serves as key point person for assigned merchant groups.</li>
<li>Prioritize creative work order (layout), image placements and production projects daily.</li>
<li>Manages multiple projects that are ready to be designed, or ready for edits.</li>
<li>Prioritizes, tracks, handles and maintains records of the elements and time schedules of multiple projects.</li>
<li>Traffics flow at all stages of assigned projects. Communicates status to all relevant internal/external parties.</li>
<li>Mange marketing offers for all Sales Promotion Project Campaigns.</li>
<li>Creates Re-Cap Report for internal creative team.</li>
<li>Final check against annotation report.</li>
<li>Adheres to all project deadlines.</li>
<li>Attends meetings to discuss, requirements and core messages for advertising copy strategic focus and brand guidelines.</li>
<li>Provide clear historical documentation for all past projects.</li>
<li>Prioritizes, tracks, handles and maintains records of the elements and time schedules of multiple projects.</li>
<li>Maintains all creative project and design files, illustration library, signing library as needed.</li>
<li>Adheres to all Quality Assurance Process and Procedures.</li>
<li>Compiles and distributes Recap/Revises/Reshoot Lists as well as Match Merch Lists.</li>
<li>Obtains model/final proof approvals as well as vendor approvals.</li>
<li>Responsible for keeping track of the number and types of changes per print job and for the life of each print project.</li>
<li>Delivers and collects all hard proofs to and from external parties.</li>
<li>Creates Project Planning Guides and other related documents/reports as needed and communicates to relevant internal parties and external vendors.</li>
<li>Oversee that all production details, vendor schedules and specifications are met as communicated by Print Manager.</li>
<li>Assist the Project Manager with schedules from sample turn-ins through distribution and keep department schedule</li>
<li>updated on “as needed” basis.</li>
<li>Assist Project Manager when needed and keep direct and open lines of communication for all production</li>
<li>specifications and schedule needs.</li>
<li>Keep Creative Team informed for any vendor quality or scheduling issues, or specification changes.</li>
</ul>
