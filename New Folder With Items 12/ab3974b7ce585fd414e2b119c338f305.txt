<b>Primary Duties:</b><br>
Working independently, under the general supervision of the Director of University Public Engagement (UPE), the Marketing and Communications Coordinator develops, coordinates, and facilitates the implementation of marketing campaigns, branding, and public and campus messaging for University Public Engagement (UPE) with a primary focus on Chico Performances and Commencement. Incumbent develops marketing and public relations strategies and materials to successfully promote UPE/Chico Performances with a goal of selling tickets and creating a positive public image in support of the mission and business model of Chico Performances. Incumbent serves as the Production Coordinator of the annual Arts &amp; Ideas campus arts and culture brochure/ Chico Performances season brochure. Incumbent develops positive working relationships with external and internal stakeholders, such as patrons, artists, artists' representatives, community media outlets, corporate sponsors, university staff and faculty and works collaboratively with the UPE management team, to achieve marketing, sales, and engagement goals for the department.<br>

<b>Required Education:</b><p>
This position requires current knowledge of the fundamentals of marketing and marketing research, public affairs and/or media relations as well as strong written and verbal communications skills with a solid foundation in grammar, spelling, and the composition of various communication vehicles. These qualifications and those listed above would typically be obtained through equivalent to a Bachelor’s degree from an accredited institution of higher education including relevant coursework in the field.<br>
AND<br>
Three years directly related professional experience in organizational communications, media, marketing, or public relations.</p>
