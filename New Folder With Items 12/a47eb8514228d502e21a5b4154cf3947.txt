<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Job Summary</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Under general supervision, the Marketing Specialist provides expert consultation and advice in a specialized area of marketing, and plans and executes marketing projects within their specialization, including working with stakeholders to set project goals, timelines, and campaign strategies.</li>
<li>In addition, the Specialist must set project priorities, identify and assign required resources, manage a budget, and direct the work of external vendors to ultimately deliver within timing and budget constraints.</li>
<li>Incumbents specialize in a specific marketing discipline, which may include creative services (advertising), branding, divisional marketing, regional marketing, events, technical marketing, or digital marketing.</li>
<li>The Marketing Specialist is expected to demonstrate expertise in their field, and is directly accountable for a wide range of concurrently running projects or marketing campaigns.</li>
<li>Specialists apply their expertise in developing marketing strategies for business units, geographies, and Product Service Lines (PSLs) through project work and expert advice.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Qualifications</b></p>
<p>Requires a high school diploma and a minimum of 5 years of experience in Marketing or Communications, which includes high-level project management experience. An associate or undergraduate degree in marketing or communications, and experience in Halliburton is preferred.</p>
<br>
