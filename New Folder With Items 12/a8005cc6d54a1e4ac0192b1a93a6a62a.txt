<h2>Support Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Key Objectives</p>
<ul>
<li>Successfully resolve support tickets to our customers' satisfaction by conducting support sessions though email, phone and web meetings</li>
<li>Help team members in resolving support tickets and troubleshooting</li>
<li>Identify and highlight best practices in support and contribute to a knowledgebase of articles</li>
<li>Proactively assist new customers and partners in deploying and using the ClickDimensions solution</li>
</ul>
<p>Technical Duties</p>
<p>
To carry out his/her duties the Support Specialist will preferably have experience with:</p>
<ul>
<li>Microsoft CRM online and OnPrem deployments</li>
<li>Microsoft CRM configuration, security and application usage</li>
<li>Microsoft CRM error troubleshooting and plugin registration</li>
<li>HTML and email template design/modification</li>
<li>Simple JavaScript (e.g. web form field validation)</li>
<li>ClickDimensions Application deployment/installation (i.e. follow a detailed installation procedure and troubleshoot/resolve issues as they arise)</li>
<li>Solution and networking concepts at a high level including web services, deployment types (e.g. online vs. on premise), Windows Servers, Active Directory, connections, firewall, etc.</li>
<li>SaaS based applications and support experience strongly preferred</li>
</ul>
<p>Qualifications</p>
<p>
Direct experience with Microsoft Dynamics CRM is preferred.</p>

Interpersonal skills are a key element of the support role. The Support Specialist will be required:</p>

<li>To develop strong relationships with clients</li>
<li>To interact productively with ClickDimensions sales people and development team</li>
