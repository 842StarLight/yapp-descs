<b>Job Summary:</b> Leads the Information Security function in Shentel to ensure enterprise information assets and data are protected. The CISO will direct the strategy, operations and budget for the Information Security program. The CISO will have responsibility for the program that encompasses the provider network as well as internal applications and network.
<br><br>
<b>Qualification Requirements:</b><br>
<b>a. Education:</b> Bachelor's degree with a concentration in network and application security or equivalent experience.
<br><b>b. Experience Level:</b> 10+ years security administration with at least 5 years in a management position.
<br><br>
<b>c. Special Requirements:</b><br>
<b>d. Skills Summary:</b> Active Directory, Windows and AS/400 security, Checkpoint, Sophos, Sharepoint, Policy Management, SOX Controls, and device management.
<br>e. Check Point Security certification preferred.
<br><b>f. Licenses or Certifications:</b> CISSP certification preferred.
<br><br>
<b>Knowledge of:</b><br>
<b>a. Technology/Business Skills:</b> Active Directory, application and role-based security, SOX controls and testing, cybersecurity fundamentals, intrusion detection, mobile device management and data encryption.
<br><b>b. Security Knowledge/Tools:</b> NIST Framework, Incidence Response Planning, PCI compliance requirements, SIEM/logging tools.
<br><b>c. Skills:</b> Management skills: Team building, project management and risk management skills.
