DESCRIPTION</div>
<div><div>
<div>
<p>BearCom is the largest value-added distributor of wireless, voice, data and video solutions that boost operating efficiency and increase safety. The company is currently in a high-growth phase and building a team of people who share core values of positivity, exceptional work ethic and a commitment to working hard in order to get the job done. Through partnerships with Motorola, JVC Kenwood, Avigilon, and the like, BearCom services the mission-critical communications needs of its sophisticated customer base. With nearly forty years in the industry and a reputation for technical expertise and the ability to offer customized solutions, BearCom delivers a variety of communication applications across a diverse mix of over 40 end markets in the US and Canada. BearCom's ability to create tailored solutions for its extensive base of over 20,000 active customers allows them to enjoy relationships with the Fortune 100 to small municipalities. BearComâ€™s headquarters and distribution center are in Dallas, TX and the company is currently partnered with the industry renowned Private Equity sponsor Bertram Funds out of San Mateo, California.</p>
<p></p>
<p>As a Field Technician you will be responsible for meeting customer expectations, some of which are quality, performance, timeliness, and cost-effectiveness. Wireless communications systems include: complex radio systems for public safety and enterprise customers. The ideal candidate must have excellent communication skills, be motivated, customer service oriented, possess a willingness to learn, and be reliable. The Field Technician will interface directly with the end customer therefore, a professional attitude and appearance are vital.</p>
<p><b>RESPONSIBILITIES</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Install, maintain and provide technical support and service of wireless communications equipment such as: UHF/VHF and 800/900 MHz analog and digital systems. Operating mobile, portable radio equipment, dispatch consoles, and microwave systems, using both conventional and trunked radio technologies.</li>
<li>Diagnose, troubleshoot, repair, and resolve wireless communication equipment problems with customers in a positive and efficient manner.</li>
<li>Repair customer equipment by replacing defective modules or components through standard troubleshooting procedures and practices.</li>
<li>Ability to diagnosis and repair public address systems and BDA systems.</li>
<li>Recommend appropriate hardware and peripheral equipment that meets customer requirements.</li>
<li>Provide hands-on and oversight of communications equipment and peripheral hardware installation such as base stations, repeaters, mobile devices, portable devices, wireless broadband devices, and CCTV equipment. Analyze and comprehend diagnostic test results in order to repair and program radios and wireless equipment.</li>
<li>Deliver and execute customer Functional Acceptance Test Plans (FATP), perform and document results of site surveys and assessments per defined customer requirements in pre-sales delivery and post installation.</li>
<li>Work with customers to prepare quotes, bids, and schedules for completing installation and repair tasks.</li>
<li>Make minor modifications to electronic circuitry per customer requests and/or deploy factory software/firmware updates.</li>
<li>Install coaxial and fiber-optic cabling, connectors, antennas, and couplers in accordance with customer requirements and industry standards.</li>
<li>Must be team-focused with a passion to meet customer requirements and company goals.</li>
</ul>
</div>
</div></div>
<div>QUALIFICATIONS</div>
<div><div>
<div>
<ul>
<li>2+ years Service Technician experience in diagnosing, repairing, programming, and installing Motorola, Kenwood, ICOM, Vertex equipment in the 2-way radio wireless and /or communication field.</li>
<li>Knowledge and understanding of 2-way radio theory.</li>
<li>Ability to interpret schematics, blueprints, and diagrams.</li>
<li>Knowledge of battery backup systems, base stations, repeaters, multicouplers, and duplexers, TCP/IP principles and NIC programming.</li>
<li>Proficiency in operating various test equipment including but not limited to, spectrum analyzers, system analyzers, watt meters, TDRs, FDRs, and multi-meters.</li>
<li>CET Certification a plus. Journeyman level must be attained within 6 months of employment.</li>
<li>2 year Associate degree in Electronics Technology or equivalent work experience.</li>
<li>Knowledge of audio, RF, digital, and microprocessor electronic circuits found in communications equipment at the component level.</li>
<li>Background checks are part of the hiring process which may also include a Motor Vehicle Check.</li>
</ul>
<p>The following experience is a plus.</p>
<ul>
<li>R56 Certified Installer a plus.</li>
<li>Motorola Astro 4x-7x training providing support for various system operations a plus.</li>
<li>Certification in various Astro 25 products (Quantar, GTR8000, MCC5500/7500, etc..) a plus.</li>
<li>Certification in various MotoTRBO products and systems (Connect+, Capacity+, Linked Capacity+, IP Site Connect, Capacity Max etc.) a plus.</li>
<li>Knowledge of audio, RF, digital, and microprocessor electronic circuits found in communications equipment at the component level.</li>
<li>Security Clearance a plus.</li>
</ul>
