<b>
Corporate Overview</b><br>
</div>
<div></div>
<div>QuinStreet makes the digital media channel affordable at scale for marketing clients. We match high-intent consumers in one of the nation’s largest media networks to the right solution and the right client.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
Our industry leading segmentation &amp; matching technology platform allows marketing clients to target and reach “research &amp; compare” customer prospects, segment by segment. Our databases and optimization algorithms are built from almost 20 years and billions of dollars of online media experience.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
We believe in the direct measurability of digital media. We believe in performance marketing. (We pioneered it.) And we believe in the advantages of technology. We bring all of this together to deliver truly great results for our clients in the world’s biggest channel.</div>
<p></p>
<br>
<div><b>
Job Category<br>
</b></div>
<div>
</div>
<div>The Senior Client Strategy &amp; Development Representative will be responsible for growth and profitability within assigned geographical area and/or vertical market to develop new vendor accounts in the B2B Technology vertical.</div>
<p></p>
<br>
<div><b>
Responsibilities</b></div>
<div></div>
<br>
<ul>
<li> Increase sales profits and achieve sales quotas by sourcing, soliciting, and opening new accounts; as well as servicing current vendors</li>
<li> Meet and exceed quota targets</li>
<li> Research, follow-up on leads, and track opportunities to continually feed sales pipeline with qualified leads</li>
<li> Build relationships from the ground up</li>
<li> Plan, monitor, and adjust activities to ensure success</li>
<li> Effectively and professionally represent QuinStreet and its products</li>
<li> Working with account management post sale (in some cases) to ensure a productive long term relationship</li>
<li> Utilizing a consultative sales approach, which requires significant time and effort to educate prospects about QuinStreet’s value proposition</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<div><b>
General Requirements</b></div>
<div></div>
<br>
<ul>
<li> Bachelors Degree in business related field</li>
<li> A minimum of 3 years of experience in B2B technology sales (performance based online media, advertising, etc.) preferred</li>
<li> Strong phone sales, prospecting, and closing skills</li>
<li> Consultative sales experience</li>
<li> Knowledge of online media business models, and common user experiences a strong plus</li>
<li> Knowledge of 2-3 product areas such as storage, networking, security, enterprise software, servers, cloud solutions, mobility solutions for businesses, etc.</li>
<li> Must have excellent communication, time management, and organizational skills</li>
<li> A team player who is self motivated and driven to succeed</li>
<li> Strong focus on generating results</li>
<li> Confident and energetic; professional and team orientated sales approach; Entrepreneurial mindset a must</li>
<li> Strategic thinker</li>
<li> Knowledge of online media business models, and common user experiences a strong plus</li>
<li> Experience with the internet, and proficiency with software such as MS Word, Excel, Power Point, MS Outlook, and Salesforce.com</li>
<li> Must have a “Hunter” mentality</li>
</ul>
