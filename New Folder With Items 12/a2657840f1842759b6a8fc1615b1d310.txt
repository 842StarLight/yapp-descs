<h2>Senior Network Consulting Engineer 19-02-154</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Position Summary: </b></p>
<p>The Senior Network Engineer role will develop, implement, and support network services and solutions for consulting clients. Build a customer-focused relationship with client to identify business challenges and develop specifications and requirements to arrive at the best solution.</p>
<p><b>Roles and Responsibilities/ Essential Functions: </b></p>
<ul><li>Work as a member of the HBS networking team that provides consultative and proactive project support to HBS’s account base.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Collaborate across multiple internal teams to ensure successful delivery of results based on scopeof work.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Contribute to product selection and research, configuration standards and best practices and educate our sales teams on these products and services so that they can sell them.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Provide support, maintenance, and administration for customer networks.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Ability to work in a team atmosphere between regional and virtual practices. At all times maintaining a professional and respectful demeanor.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Provide time estimates and project scopes for networking related projects.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Maintain current vendor/industry specific certifications and stay current on new products and solutions.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Minimum of 1,450 hours billed per fiscal year prorated based on start date. These charge hour requirements will be balanced against professional development and on the job training.</li></ul>
