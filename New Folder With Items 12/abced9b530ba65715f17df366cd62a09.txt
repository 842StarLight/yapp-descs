<p><b>ABOUT LOANPAL</b></p>
<p>
We are the world-positive lender. Our mission is to connect billions of dollars of capital to millions of homeowners that want to convert their outdated houses into modern, smart, energy-efficient homes. By unlocking access to numerous products that help people achieve a more sustainable lifestyle, we are revolutionizing the $350 billion+ home improvement industry. As such, we have become the financial ecosystem for mission-driven businesses who need fast and frictionless lending to empower their customers, and we help consumers save money with our modern lending technology.</p>
<p>
As part of our world-positive initiative, we are also the official sponsor of GivePower – a foundation that uses solar energy and storage technologies to deliver the most essential community services to regions of the world that need it most.</p>
<p>
If you have an unstoppable desire to make a meaningful impact on our planet and help mission-driven businesses and consumers achieve a more sustainable future, join us.</p>
<p><b>
The Role</b><br>
<br>
Loanpal seeks a Group Product Manager to lead our Product Management team. The ideal candidate is experienced in financial operations AND a product evangelist with a passion for creating elegant software solutions in the financial technology space. We are a fast growing, technology-first consumer finance company backed by a strong-performing mortgage business. Launched in January 2018, we are the largest financier of solar residential installations in the US, and with an exciting product roadmap ahead, the prospects are excellent. Do you want to accelerate the adoption of residential solar and other home energy efficiency technologies? Are you passionate about financial services software and operations? We’re looking forward to meeting you!</p>
<p>
We are looking for candidates with outstanding verbal &amp; written communication skills, extreme curiosity, and the commitment to build outstanding software solutions for our internal and external customers. The successful candidate will be an experienced recruiter and team builder, an agile expert, and passionate about influencing high-growth product organizations. He or she will be a ninja-level expert at ensuring solid operational support WHILE driving and executing to an innovative roadmap for our residential lending business. This is a position with explicit potential for rapid promotion and expanding scope of responsibilities.</p>
<p><b>
Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Develop a deep understanding of our Investor needs pre-and post-sale; collaborate with Capital markets to define and distribute appropriate reports, documentation, AND build the speediest possible automation of elegant Investor self-service tools to access these.</li>
<li>
Recruit outstanding Analytical thinkers with high standards, great UX instincts, workflow design capability, and a passion for explaining complex concepts to non-technical users to form a world-class management group.</li>
<li>
Lead by example in best practice Agile adoption to help the cross-functional development team deliver consistent, high-quality, highly predictable releases of new features and business value.</li>
<li>
Lead change management plans &amp; adoption of new functionality in order to assure maximum value realized from our development efforts.</li>
<li>
Work with executive team and stakeholders to design and deliver delightful software user experiences.</li>
<li>
Breakdown complex solutions into viable roadmaps and tractable delivery plans</li>
<li>
Prioritize and represent automation needs to our development squad clearly and concisely in Agile ceremonies and through outstanding (yet concise!) requirements documentation (User stories, Agile breakdown of implementation plans, Acceptance Criteria, etc.)</li>
<li>
Interact effectively with business leaders and executives to translate “1-liner emails” and “ad hoc” report requests into actionable business needs, requirements, and viable, sustainable solutions.</li>
<li>
Primary responsibility for backlog grooming, initiative and feature prioritization; in collaboration with the team’s Scrum Master(s), leadership of sprint planning</li>
<li>
User Acceptance Testing of new features and functionality prior to delivery to the business</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Qualifications:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
7-10 years professional experience in financial services operations and software</li>
<li>
Demonstrated ability to recruit and coach a product management team</li>
<li>
Outstanding written &amp; verbal communication skills, ability to breakdown complex projects into logical actionable parts</li>
<li>
Strong competency (&amp;/or FAST learning!) of the Atlassian suite of products (JIRA, Confluence)</li>
<li>
Software development experience in agile development environments</li>
<li>
Experience eliciting functional requirements and documenting clear, concise written requirements</li>
<li>
Demonstrated ability to interact with internal and external software customers and Executives</li>
</ul>

 Benefits and Perks:</b></p>

Casual dress</li>
<li>
Benefit program with discounts &amp; rewards to theme parks, restaurants and other exciting venues</li>
<li>
Comprehensive medical, dental, vision, 401k benefits</li>
</ul>
