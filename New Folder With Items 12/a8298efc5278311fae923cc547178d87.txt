<h2>Technology Program Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p><b>What you’ll be doing...</b>
</p>
<div>As a Technology Program Manager, you’ll be asked to perform many functions while helping our sales and service users achieve their objectives through homegrown Verizon technology platforms. Specifically, you will be required to manage software development projects simultaneously from inception to completion. You will be responsible for managing all phases of the software development lifecycle including working with the Business Stakeholders to identify requirements and complete user stories. Furthermore, you will be responsible for driving sub projects to ensure they are delivered within committed timeframes and to the satisfaction of internal stakeholders. Your responsibilities will include but not limited to, managing the programs, conducting requirements interview, documentation of business requirements / user stories, consultation on solution options and presentation, UAT planning and script development, UAT execution, production shakeout, and pre and post go-live planning and support.</div>
<div>
Responsibilities:
</div>
<ul>
<li>Manage technology platform’s program</li>
<li>
Conduct requirements sessions</li>
<li>
Define user stories
</li>
<li>Track user stories and associated tasks to ensure developed as designed</li>
<li>
Communicate progress of deliverables to the business</li>
<li>
User acceptance testing</li>
<li>
Ensure successful release implementation</li>
<li>
Communicate the new/changed functionality to the user base</li>
<li>
Develop training and help documentation</li>
<li>
Single resource for Tier II ticket support</li>
</ul>
<div>
This position can be located in any valid Central or Eastern Time Zone Verizon location or work remotely within a reasonable commute distance to a valid Verizon location.</div>
