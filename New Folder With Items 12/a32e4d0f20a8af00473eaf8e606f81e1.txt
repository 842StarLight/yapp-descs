<h2>Sourcing Recruiter</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div><div>As a Sourcer on our Recruiting team, you will be responsible for developing a pipeline of qualified Account Executives to join Scout RFP. The main focus of this role will be finding and attracting the 'best of the best' in the Sales industry utilizing a variety of sourcing methods. Most important to your success will be strong sourcing techniques to build robust candidate pipelines, as well as outreaches and follow-ups using out Lever Nurture tool.</div></div>
<div><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>What you will do:</b></h3>
<div><ul>
<li>
Utilize external and internal sourcing methods
</li>
<li>Become the expert on where and how to attract top Sales talent
</li>
<li>Input, update, maintain, and leverage recruitment data and records using our applicant tracking systems</li>
<li>
Participate in Stand-up meetings and build new sourcing and recruitment strategies with recruiters and hiring managers
</li>
<li>Review resumes</li>
<li>
Demonstrate excellent time management and project management skills to optimize results to meet target metrics</li>
</ul></div>
</div></div>
<div><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>What we're looking for:
</b></h3>
<div><ul>
<li>1+ years of sourcing talent</li>
<li>
Experience building talent pipelines</li>
<li>
Hands on experience working with Applicant Tracking System (ATS)</li>
<li>
A good eye for talent and strong intuition gauging a candidate's abilities</li>
<li>
Ability to collaborate, multitask and adapt to a fast-paced changing work environment</li>
<li>
Excellent verbal and written communication skills
</li>
<li>Hands on experience working in an ATS</li>
</ul></div>
</div></div>
