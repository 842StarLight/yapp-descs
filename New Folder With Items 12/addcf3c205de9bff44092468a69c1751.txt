<h1 class="jobSectionHeader">
<b><i>Account Executive</i></b><b><i> (Outside Sales)</i></b>
</h1>
<p><b>Company Overview</b></p>
<p>The Radio One story began simply with a dream and one radio station. Thirty-five years, 16 markets, and 50+ stations later, we are an urban media empire, spanning multiple media platforms.</p>
<p>We are in your ears with Reach Media/Radio One, on your televisions with TV One, and may even be the name behind your favorite website, with our digital platform Interactive One.</p>
<p>Information is power, and our mission here at Radio One, Inc. is simple – to be the most trusted media source in the African-American community. At Radio One, we pride ourselves on hiring people who are dedicated and continue to challenge the norm, while embracing a corporate culture built on creativity and passion. We are our employees’ biggest fans, and it is our daily mission to attract, develop, and retain the brightest stars in the business.</p>
<p></p>
<p>Radio One is looking for the best and brightest Account Executives to join our Sales Team!</p>
<p><b> KEY RESPONSIBILITIES:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Business to business sales, and superior management of both large and small accounts</li>
<li>Prospecting, cold calling, and development of new sales opportunities</li>
<li>Creation of client-focused sales plans to meet/exceed goals, and ensuring client needs are met</li>
<li>Participation in team/market efforts, meetings and events</li>
<li>Assisting with collection efforts from clients</li>
<li>Creation of client-focused sales plans to meet/exceed goals in conjunction with Target and Key Account development</li>
</ul> <p></p>
<p><b>CRITICAL COMPETENCIES FOR SUCCESS:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Highly motivated and creative individual who is looking for ways to innovate</li>
<li>Committed to building and developing new relationships</li>
<li>Maintaining and building strong relationships with new and existing clients</li>
</ul> <p></p>
<p><b>EDUCATION/QUALIFICATIONS:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>3-5 years business to business sales experience, with experience in media preferable</li>
<li>Professional demeanor, and excellent verbal and written communication and customer service skills</li>
<li>Successful track record working with major accounts</li>
<li>Web sales, and CRM experience is a plus</li>
<li>Knowledge in the following industries, radio, digital, marketing, advertising, a plus</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>BENEFITS</b></p>
<ul>
<li>6 Month Pay Guarantee</li>
<li>Medical/Dental/Vision/Life Insurance Plan Options</li>
<li>401k</li>
<li>Paid Vacation Time and a Competitive Holiday Schedule</li>
<li>Pre-Tax Transit Benefit (DC metro area only) and Flexible Spending Accounts</li>
</ul>
