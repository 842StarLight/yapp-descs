<h2>Advertising Sales Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Advertising Sales Manager</b></p>
<p>
BringFido is the world's leading pet travel and lifestyle brand. Our website and mobile apps connect millions of pet owners around the globe with information about more than 250,000 places to stay, play and dine with Fido. Our team of pet travel experts is growing, and we’re on the hunt for talented dog lovers who have a passion for making the world a pet-friendlier place!</p>
<p><b>
Job Description:</b></p>
<p>
We’re looking for a seasoned Advertising Sales Manager to oversee advertising sales at BringFido. This is a great opportunity for a talented dog lover who enjoys working independently, selling for a growing company in a fun industry, and working directly with the executive team to create compelling campaign opportunities for our advertising partners.</p>
<p>
The position is based in Greenville, SC (our company headquarters), but remote candidates will be considered. If your dog has a say in the matter, he’ll definitely want you to commute to the office. Unlimited toys, treats and belly rubs – need we say more?</p>
<p><b>
Duties Include:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Creating attractive advertising packages for prospective partners, including a mix of display, native, email, and social media campaigns.</li>
<li>Prospecting for new business opportunities at brand and agency levels, both domestically and internationally</li>
<li>Cultivating relationships with existing advertising partners</li>
<li>Creating RFPs and proposals for individual advertising partners</li>
<li>Selling advertising campaigns to brands, agencies and programmatic platforms</li>
<li>Creating reports and regularly communicating performance results with advertisers</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Qualifications:</b></p>
<p>
Candidates should have a bachelor's degree in business, marketing and/or a related field, at least 3 years of advertising sales experience, strong project management and negotiation skills, an active network of client/agency relationships, a track record of meeting and exceeding annual sales goals of $500k+, and a passion for our brand mission. Ideal candidate will be a dog-loving sales superstar with experience in building and selling ad campaigns within the travel/hospitality and/or pet industries.</p>
<p><b>
What We Offer:</b></p>
<p>
As a full-time employee, our Advertising Sales Manager will enjoy the following company benefits:</p>
<ul>
<li>Competitive comp with uncapped commission structure</li>
<li>PTO and paid holidays</li>
<li>Access to medical, dental, vision, disability and other insurance-related benefits</li>
<li>Access to a 401(k) retirement plan with company match</li>
<li>Fun work environment with lots of yappy hours, company events, and epic howliday parties
</li>
<li>Travel stipend</li>
<li>Remote opportunity available for the right candidate</li>
</ul>
<p>You don't have to have a dog to apply, but you must love dogs, no exceptions! Please submit your resume, along with a cover letter explaining why this is the ideal position for you.</p>
