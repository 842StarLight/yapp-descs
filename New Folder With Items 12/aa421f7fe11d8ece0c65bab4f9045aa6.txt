<p><b><i>Senior Lifestyle Specialist - Float</i></b></p>
<p><b><i>
Bring your heart to work! Caring people make the difference at American Senior Communities!</i></b></p>
<p><b><i>
Compassion, Accountability, Relationships and Excellence</i></b> are the core values for American Senior Communities. These words not only form an acronym for C.A.R.E., they are our guiding principles and create the framework for all our relationships with customers, team members and community at large.</p>
<p>
American Senior Communities has proudly served our residents and their families for over 19 years.</p>
<p>
Our Senior Living Division offers residents more than just a place to rest their head. We offer a new way of life, by providing opportunities for our residents to explore their passions. We are committed to nurturing the physical, emotional, mental, social and spiritual well-being of our residents through whole-person centered care.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b><i>
What will you be doing and how will you make a difference at American Senior Communities?</i></b></p>
<ul>
<li>Is responsible for achieving maximum occupancy in the community by touring prospects and marketing the community.</li>
<li>Markets externally by making person-to-person sales calls as goals indicate and documents conversation details in software database.</li>
<li>May assist in scheduling clinical assessments for determining whether or not inquiry is suitable for community.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p><b><i>
</i></b></p>
<p><b><i>Requirements</i></b></p>
<ul>
<li>Demonstrates C.A.R.E. values to our residents, family members, customers and staff.</li>
<li>Previous healthcare industry experience strongly desired.</li>
<li>Must be willing to work flexible hours, including some evenings and weekends as sales / marketing responsibilities dictate. Will work every other weekend on an as-needed-basis.</li>
<li>Must be able to travel 100% of the time</li>
</ul>
<p><b><i>Our commitment to our team members:</i></b></p>

<li>PayActive – Have immediate access to up to 50% of your pay</li>
<li>Medical/Dental/Vision insurance available - Plans starting as low as $20 a week</li>
<li>401(k)</li>
