<h2>Full Time Marketing Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<b>SUMARY:</b><br>
SMG, the leader in privately managed public assembly facilities has an excellent and immediate opening for a Marketing Manager at SMG-OKC. This position oversees marketing activities for Chesapeake Energy Arena and Cox Convention Center, including promotional campaigns, advertising, social media, and interactive marketing.
<br><br>
<b>MAJOR RESPONSIBILITIES:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Creative Campaigns and Promotions</li>
<li>Coordinates and implements promotional campaigns for event and company exposure.</li>
<li>Leads brainstorming efforts to produce creative promotions.</li>
<li>Develops strategies to boost ticket sales and generate additional event promotion.</li>
<li>Establishes and maintains relationships with community influencers and partners.</li>
<li>Manages and directs grassroots and viral efforts.</li>
</ul>
*
<br><br>
Plans and conducts market research to identify promotional opportunities.
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Digital Marketing</li>
<li>Oversees social media platforms and strategies.</li>
<li>Builds content calendar and runs special promotions across digital platforms.</li>
<li>Creates and manages eblast campaigns and list management.</li>
<li>Updates and manages website.</li>
<li>Advertising/Media Buying</li>
<li>Coordinates and places advertising campaigns for a variety of clients including concerts, family events, consumer shows, comedy shows, and other events as required.</li>
<li>Prepares advertising budgets for prospective shows.</li>
<li>Prepares invoicing for event settlements.</li>
<li>Negotiates best rates and trade agreements with local media outlets</li>
<li>Offers assistance to promoters looking to place advertising for upcoming events.</li>
</ul>
*
<br><br>
Supports Box Office team with email communications, group sales collateral, and special offer campaigns as needed.
<br>*
<br><br>
Assist Director of Marketing with regional and national public relations campaigns along with coordinating media and press during events.
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Assists Director of Marketing as needed with marketing campaigns for the facility.</li>
</ul>
*
<br><br>
Assists with the management, development, production, and distribution of promotional and collateral materials to support sales and marketing programs.
<br>*
<br><br>
Drives data and analytic results for higher exposure and conversion.
<br>*
<br><br>
Provides post-event reports, analysis, and regular status reports on marketing programs.
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Maintains, at all times, high standards, positive attitude, and professional appearance.</li>
</ul>
Supervisory Responsibilities
<br><br>
This position has limited supervisory responsibilities for employees or interns in the Marketing Department. Carries out supervisory responsibilities in accordance with SMG's policies and applicable laws.
