<h2>Business Development Associate</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Konnectclub (www.konnectclub.com) is a social platform for events and group activities. We are a start-up with our home-base in the Seattle area.</p>
<p>We are seeking a dynamic, highly self-motivated individual to develop and expand our business partner base in the Seattle area. We work with local restaurants, bars, bowling alley, activity venues, gaming centers, concert venues and many other types of businesses wherever someone can organize a group event. The role of a Business Development Associate involves reaching out to potential business partners and on-boarding the partner on our SaaS platform.</p>
<p>Do note that this is an independent Part-Time position. You will be expected to manage your own schedule, take charge of your goals, proactively remove blockers, balance your priorities and feel comfortable switching contexts. This requires an individual who is a confident, aggressive self-starter driven to succeed.</p>
<p>Responsibilities &amp; Duties</p>
<p>Pick up business leads from the Marketing team or from your own research<br>Initiate communication with business partners via email or phone<br>Continue subsequent follow-ups until the final agreement is in place<br>Set up ticket offers/accounts for the business partner on the Konnectclub SaaS portal<br>Provide on-boarding help and subsequent account management support to the partner<br>IMPORTANT: This is an Independent Contractor Part-Time position (15-20 Hrs per week) with the flexibility to create your own schedule and work from home.
