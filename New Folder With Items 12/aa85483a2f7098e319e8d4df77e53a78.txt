Your self-motivation will drive results and accountability within our organization and create a lasting impression. We work as a collaborative team to create solutions through problem solving and strategy while remaining focused on our mission to protect and promote the L3 brand and evolving our success.

<p></p>
<div>As a <b>Staff Accountant</b> you will be a key contributor to our financial reporting and internal control processes within our Security and Detection Systems (SDS) division, which is part of Commercial Aviation Solutions (CAS) sector. You will analyze transactions in accordance with generally accepted accounting principles (GAAP), prepare journal entries and reconcile balance sheet accounts in support of the financial reporting process. You will also prepare ad hoc analysis and undertake project based assignments at the request of management. To be successful in this role you will be a highly analytical self-starter and collaborative problem solver who is accustomed to the cadence of deadline driven public company.</div>
<p></p>
<p><b>
You will demonstrate success by:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Supporting the attainment of monthly, quarterly and annual financial closing and reporting deadlines.
</li>
<li>Ensuring compliance with US GAAP and with corporate policies.</li>
<li>
Assisting with the preparation of internal reporting and analysis as needed and potentially filings.</li>
<li>
Preparing and/or assisting with management reports.</li>
<li>
Meeting all corporate policies and complying with internal SOX control objectives.</li>
<li>
Developing continuous improvement efforts to ensure optimization.
</li>
<li>Assessing and ensuring the proper execution of financial controls.
</li>
<li>Demonstrating a strong desire for increased responsibility and professional growth.</li>
<li>
Providing relevant reporting and analysis to multiple stakeholders.</li>
