<h2>Assistant Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">Our Assistant Manager's role is to actively support their team in all facets of the business. Their mission will be to foster teamwork and create a positive work environment. We are looking for fun, hardworking people who are willing to go the extra mile for a customer or fellow team member. Great attitude, smile and positive energy are the basic foundation for this position.
<br><br>
If you have prior experience in restaurant management, general hospitality management, or at a quick serve restaurant, please apply for this wonderful opportunity.
<br><br>
Requirements/Responsibilities
<br>Our Assistant Manager is responsible for helping with all operational aspects of the CREAM store that includes but is not limited to: P&amp;L, staffing, customer service and POS/inventory management.
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Outgoing, fun and friendly personality with focus on customer service &amp; satisfaction</li>
<li>Help create and maintain a positive team environment and high staff moral</li>
<li>Help with P&amp;L and financial reporting (sales, food and labor costs, store-wide)</li>
<li>Proven leadership skills in accessing and developing talent</li>
<li>Mastery of front and back end restaurant software (POS, Online Ordering)</li>
<li>Assist corporate to ensure the success of company and region-wide roll outs and initiatives, as well as ongoing company policies and procedures</li>
<li>2+ years in a management or supervisory capacity within the restaurant industry</li>
<li>ServSafe Manager Certified</li>
<li>Must be able to commit to working weekends and most major holidays.</li>
</ul>
<b>Physical Requirements:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Must be able to stand for long periods of time</li>
<li>Climbing, reaching, walking, sitting, grasping, repetitive motions all coupled with a great deal of smiling</li>
<li>Able to bend and lift up to 50 lbs.</li>
<li>The physical demands and work environment characteristics described here are representative of those that must be met by an employee to successfully perform the essential functions of this job. Reasonable accommodations may be made to enable individuals with disabilities to perform the essential functions.</li>
<li>Competitive Hourly +tips</li>
<li>Non-exempt</li>
<li>Performance bonus</li>
</ul>
