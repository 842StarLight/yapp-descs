Description</b></div>
<div>
<div>IT Edge &amp; Integrated (ITEI) Offering Management requires capability beyond that typically applied in technical offering management. For success, the Offering Manager role requires:</div>
<div><ul>
<li>
Manages the product lifecycle management function for the Offering globally, effectively determining and influencing resource allocation among stages of identification, growth, harvest, and divestiture.</li>
<li>
Competitive assessment, development influence, and optimization management of not only the Offering, but also the Vertiv components and non-Vertiv compliments (hardware, software, services).</li>
<li>
Identification of emerging or existing market opportunities rooted in technology applications and spanning multiple Vertiv product, software, and service domains, plus adjacent non-Vertiv categories pertinent to that opportunity, often in areas outside the traditional deployment locations for Vertiv products, and utilizing different deployment processes in different form factors.</li>
<li>
Execution and guidance of primary and secondary market research, including direct interfacing with Fortune 100 customers at executive levels.</li>
<li>
Supports Director, Offering Management in comprehensive market requirements development for Global Offerings across product, software, and services, specialized for prioritized vertical and application segments, rooted in value drivers that are business outcome-based but rely on enabling technologies and capabilities.</li>
<li>
Speed of optimized execution and efficiency of communication required to enable engineering, operations, marketing, sales, and IT to successfully achieve the prime market position for Vertiv in the target segments.</li>
<li>
Effective understanding, leverage, and guidance of the evolving Configure-Price-Quote and ERP tool suite.</li>
<li>
Achievement of positioning as a ‘customer’ of the applicable hardware, software, and services Offering Management entities within Vertiv.</li>
<li>
Achievement of positioning as a ‘consultative partner’ of End Users and Channel Partners relevant to the target segments.</li>
</ul></div>
