Job Description<p></p>
<p><b>
The Role:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
To support the production manager by providing agreed hospitality and superior customer service to visiting artists / performers / clients and crews.</li>
<li>
Confidentiality is very important</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Essential Functions:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Anticipate and respond to guest needs</li>
<li>
Respond to guest concerns/complaints regarding food or service and correct errors or resolve complaints with a friendly, positive attitude</li>
<li>
Organize and prioritize service per appropriate service sequence</li>
<li>
Take menu, beverage and bar orders from artists and communicate orders to kitchen and/or bar through micros terminal</li>
<li>
Check food orders prior to serving to ensure that they are correct, properly presented, and that special requests have been met</li>
<li>
Deliver food and beverages to artist</li>
<li>
Perform opening/running/closing duties according to Van Buren policy</li>
<li>
Maintain line of sight/atmosphere control by circulating through work area throughout shift</li>
<li>
Have the ability to memorize menus and daily specials</li>
<li>
Remove dirty plates, glasses, etc., from green rooms once artist has finished</li>
<li>
Clean spills on tables or around work area</li>
<li>
Maintain Van Buren safety and sanitation standards</li>
<li>
Have knowledge of all music, promotional or special events in the house</li>
<li>
Provide production coordinator with proper documents to enable catering costs to be calculated and flashed accurately.</li>
<li>
Communicate any last minute menu changes or dietary restrictions to the kitchen staff for execution.</li>
<li>
Ensure dressing rooms are set up with agreed rider and in-house items upon bands arrival.</li>
<li>
Ensure all traces of previous occupants are removed and room is clean.</li>
<li>
Responsible for counting in and out merchandise with touring artist representative</li>
<li>
Responsible for accurate settlement of all house/artist split and taxes with tour merchandise representative</li>
<li>
Responsible for all associated paperwork</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Job Requirements:
</b></p>
<p></p>
<p><b><i>Required:
 </i></b></p>
<ul>
<li>2-5 years high volume experience in restaurant operations</li>
<li>
Skill in guest relations</li>
<li>
Wine &amp; Liquor knowledge</li>
<li>
High School Diploma</li>
<li>
Transportation in order to retrieve anything the artist may require that we do not carry on site.</li>
<li>
Basic Excel knowledge and Office Suite</li>
</ul>
<p><b><i>
Preferred:</i></b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Point of Sales knowledge, preferably MICROS</li>
<li>
Responsible Alcohol Awareness Training Certification or Equivalent</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Physical Demands/Working Environment:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Working environment is fast-paced</li>
<li>
Position requires extended periods of prolonged standing and working on your feet</li>
<li>
Must be able to lift or move up to 25 lbs using proper lifting techniques</li>
</ul>
