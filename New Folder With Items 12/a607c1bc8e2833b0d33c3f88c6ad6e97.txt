<h2>VP, Enterprise Sales</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>The VP, Enterprise Sales is responsible for generating revenue through direct sales to enterprise organizations. The VP will be required to establish and maintain high-level professional relationships with key decision-makers, implement sales and marketing strategies, and drive revenues to achieve sales objectives. Additional responsibilities include appropriate coordination between sales, marketing, contract administration, and operations.</p>
<p>
If you are very organized, self-directed and enjoy selling, this is a fantastic opportunity.</p>
<p><b>
As our VP of Enterprise Sales, you will:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Assist the organization in building and establishing a “Best in Class” Sales &amp; Marketing Organization to effectively identify, target, and generate a consistent and continuous revenue stream from qualified long-term care clients through effective and systematic, sales execution</li>
<li>Establish and maintain relationships with key area healthcare executives and professionals in the market</li>
<li>Provide software demonstrations to prospective clients</li>
<li>Attend and coordinate several industry Trade Shows</li>
<li>Participate in professional societies and activities related to the health care industry, as appropriate</li>
<li>Develop and implement market-specific strategic sales and marketing plan to achieve objectives for areas of responsibility</li>
<li>Prepare and submit required management reports reflecting progress and accomplishments</li>
<li>Identify both new business opportunities and new customer call points within the market served for additional revenue opportunity</li>
<li>Build, maintain and update CRM database information, as required</li>
<li>Coordinates joint sales &amp; marketing efforts as appropriate</li>
<li>Champion the concept, principles, and services of MatrixCare</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p>
</p>
<p><b>Skills and Experience needed for the VP of Enterprise Sales position:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>A Bachelor’s Degree. A combination of education and experience will be accepted</li>
<li>5 + years of experience in software sales is required</li>
<li>Experience in software sales to the long-term care industry and/or a high-level consultative healthcare product and/or service sales experience is preferred</li>
<li>A demonstrated record of accomplishments in healthcare-related sales</li>
<li>Strong high-level communication, negotiation, and interpersonal skills</li>
<li>Effective sales management and organizational skills</li>
<li>Self-motivated and ability to work with minimal supervision</li>
<li>Results oriented</li>
<li>A proven team player</li>
<li>Excellent written, verbal, and presentation skills</li>
<li>Computer literate in MS Windows, MS Word, MS Excel and MS PowerPoint</li>
<br>
</ul>
