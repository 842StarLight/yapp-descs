<p>The <b>Customer Relationship Manager</b> is responsible for the initial set up and training of new customers as well as building a lifetime relationship to include: ongoing support, rolling out new features and the coaching of customers to ensure maximum value is received. This is not a sales position and we provide the training needed to succeed!

<li>Coordinates all promotional materials provided by LPi.</li>
<li>Responds in a timely manner to all support inquiries and provides general support, tips &amp; tricks, best practices, training for new functionality and general assistance as needed.</li>
<li>Shares customerâ€™s feedback and ideas on enhancement and functionality ideas to improve the usage of our product.</li>

<p><b>CANDIDATE REQUIREMENTS:</b></p>
<ul>

<li>Strong critical thinking skills to provide training for new and existing WeShare customers</li>
<li>Excellent verbal and written communication skills</li>
<li>Strong attention to detail, creative thinking and result oriented skills are required
</li>
