<h2>Outlet Operations - Banquets Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Summary: </b> Ensure proper training and supervision of all room service, restaurant and banquets employees to deliver prompt, courteous service in a manner that adheres to Hotel ZaZa Food and Beverage standards and company policies and procedures. Responsibilities include but are not limited to the following:</p>
<p><b>Essential Functions: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Interview, hire, train, and discipline, employees according to Hotel ZaZa policies, procedures, service and operating standards.</li>
<li>Monitor all Restaurant/Room Service deliveries and inventories, as well as banquets and events. This position will float between both departments - Outlets and Banquets</li>
<li>Maintain an adequate supply of both equipment and food items to ensure speed and quality of service.</li>
<li>Complete accurate daily inventories with accompanying requisition orders to keep all pars stable and Room Service employees equipped to deliver orders promptly and properly. (same for Restaurant)</li>
<li>Maintain adequately stocked liquor back-up cabinet (if applicable).</li>
<li>Complete daily inventories and attach to daily orders to keep liquor pars stable.</li>
<li>Maintain control of both payroll and food expenditures for Outlets.</li>
<li>Plan carefully for weekly coverage, being careful of needless overtime and insuring proper coverage, keeping holidays, leaves of absence and vacations in mind when granting time off for any employee.</li>
<li>Keep inventories at established par levels, being sure that unnecessary food waste is not permitted, checking all food order pick-ups against requisitions.</li>
<li>Train order takers/cashiers so that they are competent in both phone conduct and cashiering procedures.</li>
<li>Give order takers exact instructions on cashiering procedures: Check writing, figure balancing, reconciliation, cash control forms</li>
<li>Give detailed menu information, proper phone etiquette and written scenarios to the cashiers/cashiers.</li>
<li>Check reconciliation sheets on a daily basis and calls must be monitored periodically.</li>
<li>Maintain a good working relationship with other departments and outlets.</li>
<li>Offer help to other outlets and departments when available or needed.</li>
<li>Encourage courtesy of all Outlet/Room Service staff toward all other employees.</li>
<li>Know emergency procedures and work to prevent accidents.</li>
<li>Be familiar with and adhere to all liquor laws. (TABC certification) Prohibit the sale/service of alcohol to intoxicated guests. Be familiar with and enforce all policies related to liquor liability laws. Specifically: Prohibiting sale of liquor to intoxicated guests. Prohibiting sale of liquor to minors.</li>
<li>Attend/conduct all designated staff meetings.</li>
<li>Follow and execute proper grooming guidelines for self and staff (uniform standards), wear Z-pin.</li>
<li>Ensure staff has completed required Z certification for New Hires (30 days) and recertification as required.</li>
<li>Perform any other duties assigned by management.</li>
<li>Cross training in other food and beverage outlets as directed by Director of Outlet Operations.</li>
<li>Constantly perform positive up-selling techniques with service staff.</li>
<li>Expedite table turnover and table resetting.</li>
<li>Implement sales promotions and merchandising programs.</li>
<li>Know and adhere to all company policies and procedures.</li>

</ul>
