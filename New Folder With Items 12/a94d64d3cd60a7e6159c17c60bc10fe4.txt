<h2>STORE MANAGER - BEVERLY</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Responsible for directing the daily operations of a single store. Operates in accordance with prescribed policies and practices. Ensures compliance with standards for customer relations, food costs, safety, sanitation, and product preparation and merchandising. May participate in image-building activities within local community. As highest management position within unit, is accountable for the unit and its operations at all times whether physically present or not.</p>
<p><b>Job Specifications</b>: Knowledge, skills and abilities normally required for competent performance in the job.</p>
<ul>
<li>2+ years in a food service/retail environment</li>
<li>Strong interpersonal and leadership skills</li>
<li>Judgment, tact, and diplomacy to effectively resolve conflicts</li>
<li>Intermediate PC skills - ability to use basic word processing and spreadsheet software for internal communications and data analysis.</li>
</ul>
