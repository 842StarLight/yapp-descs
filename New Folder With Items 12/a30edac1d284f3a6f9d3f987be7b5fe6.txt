<h1 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Purpose and Objectives</b></h1>
<p>The Global Senior Sales Operations Business Partner is a member of the Global SAP CX Operations team.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>In this role, you will provide ongoing support to the Global SAP CX Line of Business leadership teams for all operational aspects such as driving the GTM and sales budget process, deploying the Franchise model, supporting the regular forecast cadence and providing ongoing and ad-hoc reports and analysis.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>Strategic thinking, senior-level communication skills, attention to detail and flawless execution are fundamental for this role.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>The Global Sales Operations Business Partner will be a self-starter with solid sales/program management and/or operations experience, enabling you to take on assignments at a senior level, with a proven ability to work in international cross-functional teams and to deliver high quality within deadlines.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Expecations and Tasks</b></p>
<p><b>I) Strategic skills &amp; E2E Governance</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Provide Sales operational thought leadership; act as trusted advisor to the Sales management teams</li>
<li>Drive GTM and budget planning for respective Business Area and define operational business requirements. Align with GTM, Total Rewards, Controlling and Compensation teams to ensure consistent deployment of compensation and quota credit framework. Work with Sales management teams on consistent GTM execution; monitor progress.<br>
<b>II) Focus on Sales Productivity</b>
</li>
<li>Provide end-to-end operational support for the regional management teams, ensuring transparency on demand generation, pipeline, forecast, top deals, actuals and other KPIs</li>
<li>Support the weekly bookings forecast and quarterly end race cadence – consolidate management calls, prepare forecast sheet and provide analysis and insight on key trends, risks and deviations.</li>
<li>Prepare regular ad-hoc reports and analysis – translating data into insights and decisions.<br>
<b>III) Drive Sales Excellence
</b>
</li>
<li>Secure sponsorship and commitment from regional management teams to ensure consistent deployment of Sales Planning, Demand Management, Deal Execution, Forecasting and Performance Management in the Business Area. Drive transformation and coach the teams to reduce manual reports.</li>
<li>Align with and leverage global/regional Operations and Controlling teams.</li>
<li>Ensure consistent high data quality in all relevant systems</li>
</ul>
<h1 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Education and qualifications / skills and competencies</b></h1>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s University degree or comparable experience; MBA preferred</li>
<li>Minimum of 5 years’ experience in Sales Operations or Program Management in an international environment</li>
<li>Thorough understanding of Cloud business model and processes</li>
<li>Change agent: Proactively drives operational efficiencies / effectiveness; coaches others</li>
<li>Strategic thinker with strong problem solving and hands-on analytical skills: Problem identification, data collection, thorough analysis, solution development, action planning and execution</li>
<li>Organizational savvy – appropriately assertive, yet cool and respectful. Overcomes barriers within own organization, peer organization and virtual organizations</li>
<li>Results Driven – confident, thrives on hard work and consistent challenge, and is quick on his/her feet</li>
<li>Team Oriented – builds strong relationships at every level of the organization and is a strong motivator who knows how to influence and get others to consistently do their best work.</li>
<li>Fast-Paced – thrives in a demanding, quick turnaround environment where change and ambiguity are daily realities</li>
<li>Proven track record of successful project management and delivery within an international matrix environment</li>
<li>C-level communication skills</li>
<li>Fluent English language skills; other languages are a plus</li>
<li>Expert level knowledge of SAP’s internal reporting platforms (GCO reporting tools, BWP/BI) , CRM and Microsoft Excel</li>
</ul>
