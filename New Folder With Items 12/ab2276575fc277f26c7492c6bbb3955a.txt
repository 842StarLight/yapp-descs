<h2>Manufacturing Team Supervisor</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>This position is primarily responsible to meet production, quality goals, and objectives. Provide complete, timely, and informative communication to all shifts. Ensure that Production Control schedule is followed and contribute to all teams reaching monthly sales and quality goals.</p>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Responsibilities:</b></h2>
<ul>
<li>Provides leadership to the team on all shifts and interrelates well with all areas of the company.</li>
<li>Effectively resolve all problems that arise or seek assistance in a timely manner.</li>
<li>Understands company policies and procedures and ensures the team follows them.</li>
<li>Assist in maintaining schedules of machines to best meet customer requirements.</li>
<li>Performs duties in a manner to promote good team and company morale.</li>
<li>Demonstrates responsibility for Company efficiency, quality, pre-production planning, process control, corrective actions, cleanliness and safety, and continually strives for process improvements.</li>
<li>Continually mentor co-workers to encourage individual growth within company.</li>
<li>Develop a complete knowledge of management software as it pertains to the shop by supporting Production Control and tool crib. Printing routers, IPâ€™s, and blue prints when needed.</li>
<li>Handle employee safety and building issue needs in a timely manner.</li>
<li>Responsible for all employee and building issues including security. Contacting Stellar Management and / or Building services as needed.</li>
<li>Responsible for recommending disciplinary action to Manager. Handling employee discipline in a professional manner as per company guidelines</li>
<li>Input into performance reviews as needed.</li>
<li>Responsible for weekly and monthly platinum machine cleanout and control of platinum inventory.</li>
<li>Work with Manufacturing Coordinator to ensure that daily, weekly and monthly priorities are achieved.</li>
<li>Other duties as assigned.</li>
</ul>
<p>An individual in this position must be able to successfully perform the essential duties and responsibilities listed above. Reasonable accommodations may be made to enable individuals with disabilities to perform the essential functions of this position.</p>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Qualifications:</b></h2>
<ul>
<li>A minimum 5 yrs. supervisory experience</li>
<li>Mechanical medical machining background a preferred</li>
<li>Team membership and leader training</li>
<li>Read ISO procedures that apply to this position</li>
<li>Strong Blue print reading skills</li>
<li>Strong problem solving skills</li>
</ul>
