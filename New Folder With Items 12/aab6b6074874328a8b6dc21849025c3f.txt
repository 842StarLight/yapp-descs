<h2>Corporate Travel Account Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Tangerine Travel is hiring for a Corporate Account Manager in our Bothell, WA offices. If you have travel industry experience in account management or customer service, we would enjoy speaking to you further and telling you about our exciting opportunity and benefits!</p>
<p><b>
POSITION SUMMARY:</b></p>
<p>
Under moderate supervision, the Account Manager is responsible for the on-going satisfaction and partnership of assigned corporate customers. This position will serve as the partner and advocate for corporate accounts to ensure the highest levels of on-going retention and satisfaction, as well as identifying upsell opportunities and opportunities for increased utilization and adoption. In addition, the Account Manager will develop qualified references within their client base. Account Managers will develop and maintain strong relationships, with both internal partners and clients. This is a client facing position with a high volume account base. Expected to attend and lead onsite client visits and training. Moderate travel may be required.</p>
<p><b>
 </b><b>ESSENTIAL DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES:</b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
· Develop and maintain strong relationships within assigned client base to ensure maximum satisfaction and retention levels.</p>
<p>
· Conduct/attend/lead client and vendor meetings, trainings and reviews.</p>
<p>
· Maintain a high level of knowledge of Tangerine services and benefits.</p>
<p>
· Identify and execute business strategy for assigned client base. Work cross-functionally to execute on individual clients needs and goals. Identify opportunities for adding greater value.</p>
<p>
· Knowledge and ability to update and work within CRM (SalesForce), Sabre GDS and other office applications to maintain client information.</p>
<p>
· Recognize opportunities for additional travel related services by proactively contacting clients on a scheduled basis and developing strong relationships with Tangerine’s Technology team to strategically manage clients to leverage upsell opportunities.</p>
<p>
· Responsible for new client implementation functions and management tasks. Work directly with new clients and internal departments to onboard successfully.</p>
<p>
· Identify and implement specific strategies to increase utilization and adoption for overall client portfolio performance.</p>
<p>
· Develop qualified references by establishing credibility and responsiveness with assigned clients. Ensure clients receive appropriate acknowledgement for providing references.</p>
<p>
· Classify, prioritize and resolve client issues/concerns; coordinate with the appropriate internal departments to provide response and/or solutions.</p>
<p>
· Identify and implement improvements to processes, documents, tools, reports, etc. to benefit team and internal/external clients.</p>
<p>
· Participate on cross-functional teams to discuss accounts and look for trends or commonalities. Ensure the best interests of both clients and Tangerine Travel are being fairly and objectively represented.</p>
<p>
· Identify and execute business strategies that contribute to the success of the key metrics of the position – retention, upselling, increased utilization/adoption, contractual compliance and opportunities for expansion.</p>
<p>
· Attend and participate in weekly department meetings to communicate progress, key client updates, weekly accomplishments and issues. Hold department meetings when necessary.</p>
<p>
· Other duties/special projects as assigned.</p>
<p>
· Be aware of, and comply with, all corporate policies.</p>
