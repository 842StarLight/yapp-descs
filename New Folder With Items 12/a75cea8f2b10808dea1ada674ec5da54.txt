<h2>Referral Partner Operations Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>The Referral Partner Operations Manager supports the referral partner team’s key initiatives and goals of driving referral partner-initiated opportunities; partner-led service implementations; clear and concise reporting on KPIs, OKRs and meaningful results; team presentations to leadership and cross-functional internal teams; forecasting for the team; partner portal operations focused on referral partners’ needs; interactions with the partner success team to reduce PPO and improve partner support experiences; and ensuring referral sales growth and partner success.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
The successful candidate will work closely with the multiple functional groups involved in delivering, managing, and measuring referral partner program benefits to ensure they effectively contribute to our program goals.</p>
<p><b>
Primary Responsibilities</b><b>: </b>The primary responsibilities of this position include managing referral partners’ adherence to the referral partner program’s requirements and provisioning partner benefits while establishing, communicating, and managing processes and projects related to the Avalara Referral Partner Program. The ability to measure impact, make adjustments and provide recommendations for change will distinguish the candidate.</p>
<p><b>
Job Duties &amp; Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Maintains visibility of our Referral Partner Programs<ul>
<li>
Track partner attainment of program requirements</li>
<li>
Measure success, identify issues, and drive remediation plans – track partner performance to various KPIs and OKRs</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>
Creation and Launch of new Partner Certification Programs (PSP)</li>
<li>
Interface with Sales Operations and be primarily responsible for various referral partner-centric reporting issues</li>
<li>
Support the overall Referral Partner Experience (Onboarding/messaging/lifecycle), supporting the vision and policies established by the Partner Management Team</li>
<li>
Be the voice of the referral partner (and the Referral Partner Team) for internal systems operations and work with functional teams to support the launch of tools, systems, and policies<ul>
<li>
Partner Portal (rollout, adoption, usage, and reporting)</li>
<li>
Reporting and BI/Data Warehouse</li>
<li>
CRM and Order Management</li>
<li>
Sales/Partner Rules of Engagement</li>
<li>
Partner/Client Product Support and go-live engagement</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>
Work cross-functionally across the company, including Accounting/Finance, Sales Operations, Partner Support related to our mutual customers, direct partner support, New Sales, Partner Marketing, other marketing teams, and Alliance teams in maintain existing referral partner programs and creating new programs<ul><li>
Articulate referral partner needs and engage cross-functional teams to address opportunities – (partner centric, not sales centric)</li></ul>
</li>
<li>
Assist with and coordinate for the team all referral partner Avalara CRUSH sponsorships</li>
<li>
Help drive partner attendance at Avalara CRUSH</li>
<li>
Collaborate with Marketing teams on outbound partner campaigns and communications, SPIFFS and events</li>
<li>
Work collaboratively with the Accounting/Finance Dept. on any referral partner commission issues</li>
<li>
Support the initiatives of the Partner Management team</li>
<li>
Collaborate with the Partner Marketing team with referral partner channel communications – newsletter, partner-only quarterly webcast etc.</li>
<li>
Support new referral partner recruitment efforts – create partner program materials for recruitment (PPT deck, program documentation, printed collateral)</li>
<li>
Serve as the liaison with the Partner Marketing and Events team for events where referral partners and their customers are present</li>
<li>
Overall liaison with PMM team re: Strategic Alliance team needs</li>
<li>
Support Referral Partner Team operations</li>
<li>
Coordinate and support referral partner advisory board meetings</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>

<b>
About Avalara</b><br>
<br>
Avalara helps businesses of all sizes achieve compliance with transaction taxes, including sales and use, VAT, excise, communications, and other tax types. The company delivers comprehensive, automated, cloud-based solutions designed to be fast, accurate, and easy to use. The Avalara Compliance Cloud® platform helps customers manage complicated and burdensome tax compliance obligations imposed by state, local, and other taxing authorities throughout the world.<br>
<br>
Avalara offers more than 600 pre-built connectors into leading accounting, ERP, ecommerce and other business applications, making the integration of tax and compliance solutions easy for customers. Each year, the company processes billions of indirect tax transactions for customers and users, files more than a million tax returns, and manages millions of tax exemption certificates and other compliance documents.<br>
<br>
Headquartered in Seattle, Avalara has offices across the U.S. and overseas in the U.K., Belgium, Brazil, and India. More information at www.avalara.com<br>
