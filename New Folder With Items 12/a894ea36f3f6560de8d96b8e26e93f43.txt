<h2>Sales Supervisor</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div><div>
<p>The <b>Sales Supervisor </b>will support and assist the General Manager with day-to-day duties that contributes to profitable sales growth in the Window, Mattress, and Omni Channel businesses by leading and engaging associates, promoting a selling culture, and supervising the fulfillment process to achieve the sales plan.</p>
<p><b>
Primary Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Customer Service &amp; Sales<ul>
<li>
Approaches customers in a friendly manner to engage, determine needs, and help customers make decisions about desired product or service</li>
<li>
Informs customers of key product attributes to generate interest and to build clientele</li>
<li>
Provides coaching and feedback to selling team to achieve sales and care plan objectives</li>
<li>
Greets and assists customers in finding products and partners with other team members when additional help is needed</li>
<li>
Models for and holds team accountable for outstanding customer service; demonstrates WORTH behaviors consistently</li>
<li>
Supports the Manager on Duty program</li>
<li>
Directs customer service activities throughout the Window, Mattress and Omni Channel businesses</li>
<li>
Resolves customer issues and concerns in a professional manner</li>
<li>
Partner with Specialty Associates when customers require a deeper level of product information or to assist with sales needs</li>
<li>
Actively and enthusiastically engages customers to support the company’s Instant Credit Application Process (iCAP)</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>
Enterprise Fulfullment<ul>
<li>
Responsible for the completion of all Ship from Store &amp; Buy Online Pick Up in Store orders within prescribed time periods</li>
<li>
Achieves productivity targets by reviewing available reports and metrics to identifying opportunity items and reduce shrink</li>
<li>
Partners with Operations Supervisor to resolve operational concerns that impact Enterprise Fulfillment</li>
<li>
Responsible for ordering all Enterprise Fulfillment supplies in accordance with recommendations, ensuring fiscal responsibility to company</li>
<li>
Conducts audits of Enterprise Fulfillment orders to ensure accuracy of orders, drive customer experience and minimize shrink</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>
Team Development<ul>
<li>
Responsible for the completion of Specialty associates training on product knowledge and care/protection plans</li>
<li>
Consistently provides ongoing feedback and coaching to associates</li>
<li>
Manages and administers Productivity Standards Program processes to include recognition and coaching</li>
<li>
Maintains notes / observations of each associate’s strengths and opportunities</li>
<li>
Takes action on performance and policy issues within the team</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>
General Operations<ul>
<li>
Oversees the planning and execution of special events for Window and Mattress departments</li>
<li>
Manages or assists with inventory processes (cycle counts, system uploads, stock ledger, etc.) within specialty businesses</li>
<li>
Manages the SET processes by planning floor moves and graphics placement, and executing visual detailing based on company guidance</li>
<li>
Identifies sell-through opportunities and remerchandise the selling floor to increase rate of sale in specialty businesses</li>
<li>
Oversees and maintains sales floor recovery processes to ensure a clean and organized store environment</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>
Performance Standards<ul>
<li>
Supports company shrink and safety initiatives</li>
<li>
Consistently meets established performance standards for the role, including (but not limited to) the company’s iCAP program, product and service sales, customer service, profit, productivity, and attendance</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>
Jcp.com<ul>
<li>
Responsible of the completion of order binning in a timely manner</li>
<li>
Responsible for the jcp.com Return Shipment Processes and ensures that all items are returned to Direct Logistic Centers in a timely manner, in accordance with documented policies and procedures and in the proper condition</li>
<li>
Responsible for stockroom standards for all Omnichannel processes to ensure efficient execution and a safe work environment</li>
<li>
Reviews available jcp.com reports and metrics to identify opportunity items</li>
</ul>
</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Core Competencies &amp; Accomplishments:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Thinks Critically – Develops sound conclusions based on analysis of data and deep understanding of the business/ customer; understands complex issues and makes sound decisions based on wisdom and experience; anticipates implications of decisions; finds solutions with minimal guidanceYears of Experience</li>
<li>
Manages Execution – Organizes, plans and manages projects/processes with simplicity and efficiency; partners with key stakeholders to align interests and remove roadblocks; consistently achieves successful outcomes within important deadlinesSoft Skills</li>
<li>
Drives Performance – Establishes challenging standards; inspires and holds self and others accountable to achieve results; provides feedback; gives visibility and recognition to others for mastering tasks and successful contributions</li>
<li>
Strategic Vision – Sets a purpose and outlines a future path for the organization; provides clear expectations and shares rationale behind decisions that are made; identifies strategies and objectives</li>
</ul>
