<h2>Financial Analyst I</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div><div>
<div>
<b>Responsibilities:</b><br>
This role is a key asset in the FP&amp;A organization. The Financial Analyst joins a team where there are limitless opportunities to work on a diverse set of projects including: annual operating budgets, revenue and billing models, short and long term forecasts, project profitability analysis, cost-drivers, capacity planning, compensation and benefits planning, operating plan variances analysis, purchasing/sourcing analysis and special projects. They provide ongoing financial consulting and strategic support to key internal and external stakeholders and supports the office of the CFO in communicating business metrics via preparation of board presentations, financial diligence materials, executive dashboards, industry/peer group comparisons, modeling of emerging product profitability/ ROI, and other internal projects. At Kasasa we pursue excellence in our drive toward accuracy, timeliness, business acumen, and intellectual curiosity, culminating in a dynamic, continuous learning environment with great growth potential inside and throughout our organization.</div>
<div></div>
<div><b>Job duties:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Utilize budgeting, forecasting, and modeling tools to support the FP&amp;A deliverables</li>
<li>Trend analysis/KPI/benchmarking including revenues, cost of sales, expenses, strategic and capital planning, product profitability, bookings/contract backlog</li>
<li>Monitor performance indicators, highlighting trends and analyzing causality;</li>
<li>Perform monthly P&amp;L and cash flow variance analysis</li>
<li>Perform monthly Budget vs. Actual variances for select business units</li>
<li>Develop financial models and analyses to support strategic initiatives</li>
<li>Analyze complex financial information, from a variety of systems, to provide accurate and timely recommendations to management for decision making purposes; synthesize data to distill the message and key assumptions, identifying relevant and non-relevant data</li>
<li>Support cross-functional business leaders with accurate data they can use to run their business</li>
<li>Prepare presentations for internal and external parties</li>
<li>Analyze and QA financial and business operations data</li>
<li>Assist in process improvements and strategies to automate and streamline FP&amp;A processes</li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<div><b>Job Requirements</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s Degree in Accounting or Finance</li>
<li>1-3 years experience, preferably in technology or services;</li>
<li>Private Equity reporting a plus</li>
<li>Advanced MS Excel and PowerPoint</li>
<li>Experience with Business Intelligence tools a plus</li>
<li>Strong analytical/modeling skills with high standards of precision</li>
<li>Ability to articulate with excellent verbal and written communication skills</li>
<li>Passion for creative problem solving</li>
<li>Highly driven and self-motivated with a passion for making an impact</li>
<li>Ability to partner and influence business leaders</li>
<li>Knowledge of US GAAP and/or public accounting experience/ Licensed CPA a plus</li>
</ul>
