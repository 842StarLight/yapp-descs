<h2>Pro Shop Management</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">This exciting internship will focus on Golf Operations at Wildflower Golf Course and is geared toward a college student pursuing a degree in Golf Enterprise Management. The student will report directly to the General Manager of the Golf Course. This internship includes responsibility of staff supervision while acting as manager on duty, completing front desk tasks which include but not limited to, checking in golfers, answer the telephone, and face-to-face interaction with customers. The Intern will also assist with facilitating tournaments and weekly league events. Finally, the Intern will assist in managing the pro shop inventory with includes merchandising, running promotions and meeting sales goals. This internship will provide an emphasis on the following skills: marketing &amp; merchandising, organizing &amp; running tournaments, building guest relations and supervising staff.
