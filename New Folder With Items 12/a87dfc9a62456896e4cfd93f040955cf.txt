<p>
Our Marketing Technology/API team has a Business Analyst internship opportunity available for Summer 2020. At the end of the program, the interns will have a better understanding of the IT function within Designer Brands.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p>The BA Intern requires a candidate that is eager to learn about information technology processes that encompass collection and management of corporate application generated data as well as support of analytical users that rely on corporate application data.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
Common Tools Used by Team Members: MS OneNote, MS Excel, MS Outlook, MS Word, Google DataStudio, Azure DevOps, Unicenter Service Desk</p>
<p>
Common Discipline Focuses: Agile software development methodologies, software &amp; user support methodologies</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
Candidates must be an individual that thrives in an analytic, collaborative and detail oriented environment. Role responsibilities include understanding software development lifecycle, gathering business requirements for associated project, building process flows to aid development and business alignment, writing story cards with ADO as well as knowledge of Designer Brands business acumen. A Business Analyst must bridge the gap between the business and IT partners through facilitation, mediation, documentation and analytics. A Business Analyst drives software development vision through the use of industry standard requirements collection methods combined with use of Designer Brands SDLC requirements and design templates. Role includes direct user training, tool support and communication that support user adoption of technology solutions.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p>Tools Frequently Used: MS PowerPoint, MS Visio, MS Excel, Azure DevOps</p>
<p>
Discipline Focuses: Requirements collection, user management, training and user support documentation creation and coordination
