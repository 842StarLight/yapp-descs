<h2>Microsoft Dynamics 365 Finance and Operations (D365F&O)/AX Developer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>Mobile Instrument Service and Repair, Inc., among the nation's leaders in repair and maintenance services for reusable surgical equipment, is seeking a <b>Microsoft Dynamics 365 Finance and Operations (D365F&amp;O)/AX Developer </b>for our <b>National Headquarters</b>. Our primary goal is to consistently provide a high level of support and technical experience to the team to maintain clients, generate revenue, and grow the client base. The ideal candidate will be professional, organized, a fast learner, have a positive attitude and be ready to get to work. You will demonstrate a concern for customer service and attention to detail.<br>
<br>
<b>POSITION DESCRIPTION:</b><br>
The primary responsibilities of this position will be to provide Dynamics 365 Finance and Operations/AX design, development, documentation, and support for customizations to existing features and new features while upholding X++ coding standards and methodologies. Working with Consulting professionals, AX Consultants, Project Managers, Sales Executives and others. The ability to scope out work, manage and in some cases delegate and oversee other developers work. Quality control and testing.<br>
<b>POSITION REQUIREMENTS:</b><br>
In addition to the necessary skills and experience to perform the responsibilities outlined above, there are a number of traits that a successful candidate will possess.<br>
<b>Responsibilities:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Technical design, develop and testing of core Dynamics 365/AX granules and add-on’s</li>
<li>Work with internal stakeholders to understand the technical requirements and scope out modifications and custom development</li>
<li>Convert functional requirements to technical specifications and design documents</li>
<li>Make technical recommendations for installation, modifications and custom development</li>
<li>Ensure development best practices are adhered to by other team members</li>
<li>Communicate effectively in all mediums and to all levels within the organization</li>
</ul>
<b>Technical Requirements:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>BS in Computer Science or related field</li>
<li>Experience in at least two D365 or AX 2012 implementations</li>
<li>Minimum 3 years of experience in software programming and development in Microsoft Dynamics 365/AX X++</li>
<li>Computer Science Degree</li>
<li>Experience with Dynamics AX Jobs and Service modules</li>
<li>Experience participating in Work Scoping &amp; Estimation</li>
<li>Excellent communication, planning and organization skills</li>
<li>Ability to establish priorities and work independently</li>
<li>Must be proficient developing and coding in</li>
<li>Dynamics 365F&amp;O/AX 2012 (for ERP)</li>
<li>X++</li>
<li>Microsoft Visual Studio C#.Net and ASP.Net</li>
<li>Microsoft SQL Server Reporting Services</li>
<li>XML and Dynamics Application Integration Framework</li>
<li>Enterprise Portal on Microsoft SharePoint</li>
<li>The ability to travel between 20-40%</li>
</ul>
