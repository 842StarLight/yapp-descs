<p><b>Essential Job Functions</b>
</p>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Effectively test media and complete test reports</li>
<li>Prepare and print marketing media samples for field sales</li>
<li>Performs color quality checks on all lab equipment and print shop equipment</li>
<li>Manage and maintain all equipment in the test lab and website</li>
<li>Coordinate sales samples on the web to print site with print shop</li>
<li>Primary interface to third party solution vendors based on assigned products</li>
<li>Participate in trade shows, meetings and seminars affecting the perception, influence and sales of these products, including third party vendor participation</li>
<li>Visit customers as required</li>
<li>Engage in learning activities required to maintain current knowledge of assigned products and essential business practices</li>
<li>Travel as required</li>

<p></p>

<li>Solid product knowledge including software and print solutions, applications, and software utilities in a graphic arts workflow</li>
<li>Excellent written/verbal communication skills</li>
<li>Understand graphic arts print standards, ISO, GRACol, SWOP, G7</li>
