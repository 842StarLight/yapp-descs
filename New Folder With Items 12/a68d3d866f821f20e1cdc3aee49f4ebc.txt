<h2>Inside Sales Representative</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p><b>Our client</b> is looking for passionate, ambitious, and motivated professionals who will be responsible for driving new business to the front of the sales funnel by educating potential clients.<br>
</p>
<p>The successful candidate needs to be able to think quickly as their prospecting efforts will require the ability to tailor conversations to the many role players and various industries they will come in contact with.<br>
</p>
<p><b>What You’ll Do</b></p>
<ul>
<li>You will be responsible for initiating relationships with prospective customers</li>
<li>You will be in contact with Sales managers to strategize a new approach</li>
<li>You will help design a Sales pipeline, create and improve the Sales process</li>
<li>You will utilize CRM software to track and measure all sales activities</li>
</ul>
<p><b>What We Are Looking For:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>You need to have strong interpersonal skills and be creative in solving problems</li>
<li>You need to have excellent verbal communication skills and professional phone manners</li>
<li>You’re a go-getter, well-organized and passionate with entrepreneurship</li>
<li>You've worked with B2B and/or SaaS previously, will be an advantage</li>
</ul>
</div>
