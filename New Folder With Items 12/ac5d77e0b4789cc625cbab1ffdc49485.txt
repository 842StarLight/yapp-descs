<h2>Business Systems Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>JOB SUMMARY</b></h3>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
The Business Systems Manager is accountable for service delivery for aligned clients. A typical client alignment consists of a group of clients with various degrees of size and complexity who may be in implementation or in ongoing mode. The Business Systems Manager is accountable for the successful execution of all tasks that support benefits administration delivery for implementation and ongoing clients both internally and externally to clients. The Business Systems Manager may also directly manage aligned Business Systems Analysts. The Business Systems Manager’s role requires an in-depth systems/technology background, and health and welfare domain knowledge.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
ESSENTIAL DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES</b></p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
Consistently demonstrates core Empyrean values: attitude, aptitude, and ownership</p>
<p>
Ownership of continuous gross margin improvement through configuration, out of scope project support, automation, process improvement, quality assurance, and support of co-worker growth</p>
<p>
In partnership with the Client Service Manager, drives accountability for team performance and owns client dashboard, including client satisfaction, client profitability, change management tickets, case management, and data checks for aligned clients</p>
<p>
Ensures that compliance regulations are met across aligned clients, including SOC1</p>
<p>
Coordinates with Implementation Managers and Client Service Managers on project management and resource allocation for aligned clients. Ensures resources complete assigned tasks in a quality manner</p>
<p>
Responsible for implementation configuration management</p>
<p>
Accountable for BSA development across book of business through direct coaching and mentoring</p>
<p>
Follows and enforces Empyrean’s data security policies</p>
<p>
Is aware of all planned provision migrations across assigned teams, and manages and reinforces team adherence to provision migration protocols</p>
<p>
Is aware of all planned base platform releases and works with aligned teams to understand the content, assess impact, and execute testing, as required</p>
<p>
Provides guidance and supports issue research and resolution and system configuration</p>
<p>
Demonstrates in-depth knowledge and understanding of current and new system functionality; is able to effectively convey to and consult with clients regarding changes and decisions that may impact them; escalates gaps in functionality as appropriate</p>
<p>
Demonstrates in-depth knowledge and understanding of client requirements and statements of work for aligned clients</p>
<p>
Provides quality oversight and guidance and ensures adherence to Empyrean best practices and standards</p>
<p>
Works with the Enterprise Information Technology and the Application Development teams to develop solutions and resolve issues</p>
<p>
Records and submits time accurately and timely against assigned project codes</p>
<p>
Perform other duties as assigned</p>
<p><b>
EDUCATION AND EXPERIENCE REQUIREMENTS</b></p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
Education: BA/BS degree.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
Experience: 3+ years benefits administration, Empyrean technology platform experience, and project management experience. Direct external client consulting experience preferred. Strong communication and analytical skills required.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
Or, any equivalent combination of education, experience, and training that provides the required knowledge, skills, and abilities.</p>
