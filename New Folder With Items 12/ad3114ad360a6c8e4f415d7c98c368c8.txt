<h2>Hardware Development Engineer IV</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p><b>TransCore (TRN), a subsidiary of Roper Technologies, Inc. (NYSE: ROP) is seeking a full-time Hardware Development Engineer IV to join our team in Round Rock, Texas.</b></p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Job Summary:</b> Hardware Development Engineer IV will support project design and documentation needs from proposals and concept drawings through installation drawings and as-built drawings. Position will also support product line including manufacturing support, trouble shooting, design, documentation, and ECO requirements. The platform is a large-scale commercial electro-mechanical sensor array.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Essential Duties and Responsibilities </b>include the following. Other duties may be assigned.</p>
<ul>
<li>
Creates hardware design solutions including mechanical and electrical aspects</li>
<li>
Supports product line, generates ECOs/ECRs, designs improvements, identifies part/assembly alternatives, and documents products</li>
<li>
Supports projects during sales, installation and maintenance; communicates product capabilities/requirements/specifications</li>
<li>
Guides nationwide teams on product fit to sites (bills of materials, layouts, wire schedules, conduit fill, etc.)</li>
<li>
Improves product line using 3D models, ANSI/ASME drafting standards, cable design, and simple circuits (possible PCB/PWB layout)</li>
<li>
Performs Engineering calculations using Excel, including thermal analysis, power consumptions, network bandwidth, etc.</li>
<li>
Grows as necessary in skills and expertise</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Education</b><b>:</b> Bachelor's degree in an Engineering field preferred</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Required Skills &amp; Experience:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Minimum five years of work experience</li>
<li>
AutoCAD proficiency with some knowledge of drafting standards</li>
<li>
Basic knowledge of circuits and programming</li>
<li>
Mechanical aptitude</li>
<li>
Excellent communication skills both written and verbal</li>
<li>
Excellent organizational skills, including documentation of projects and decisions</li>
<li>
Aptitude and familiarity with MS Office (Excel, Outlook, Word)</li>
<li>
Understanding of Engineering Standards, especially ECO systems.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Desired Skills:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Experience with firmware / assembler</li>
<li>
Circuit design experience</li>
<li>
CADsoft Eagle or other PCB design software</li>
<li>
Linux experience</li>
<li>
Sheet metal fabrication background</li>
<li>
Knowledge of ANSI / ASME Drafting standards</li>
<li>
Understanding of NEMA / IP-rated enclosures</li>
<li>
Hands-on troubleshooting of electrical and mechanical assemblies</li>
<li>
Program Management background or skills</li>
</ul>
