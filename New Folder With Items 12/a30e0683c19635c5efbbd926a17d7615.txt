<h2>Automated Lighting and Window Treatments Designer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Job Description:</p>
<p>Join our technology integration firm in Rockville, Maryland! We are looking for an energic full-time Automated Lighting and Window Treatments Designer to enhance our Design Division. The Designer will work with internal staff as well as custom home builders, architects, interior designers and the clients to complete amazing projects. The Designer will be responsible for each stage of the projects they are involved in. The stages include sales, system design, project management, reconciliation, billing, and maintaining the client relationship. This employee is expected to arrive at the office on-time every day at 8 am and conclude their day at around 5 pm. Due to the demands of specific projects, these hours will fluctuate.</p>
<p>Knowledge of window treatments, lighting design, electrical principals, outdoor lighting, and Lutron systems is preferred. On the job training, will be provided. Pay will be commensurate with experience and skills. Total compensation range includes salary and commission.</p>
