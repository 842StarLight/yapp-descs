<p><b>Sales Enablement Manager</b></p>
<p>
Revel is a next gen healthcare technology company that is challenging convention by reimagining what's possible. We believe the way healthcare traditionally communicates with people is broken. So we're reinventing it. We're replacing stone age practices with friendly conversations powered by smart technology and AI. We're putting data to work for healthcare like every other industry on the planet. Because we can do better. Join us.</p>
<p>
If you're a change maker who wants to make a difference in healthcare, read on.</p>
<p><b>
About the Role</b></p>
<p>
As Sales Enablement Manager, you'll be an integral part of how Revel identifies new relationships, manages them throughout their lifecycle, and looks for ways to make it easier for our prospective clients to get started working with us. You'll work closely with our Marketing, Product, Sales, Client Success and Implementation teams to optimize the way we interact with our current and future clients. We are looking for someone who has exceptional communication skills and a knowledge of the healthcare industry.
</p>
<p><b>You Will</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Ensure the Sales team is equipped with the right information and tools to support our prospective clients and partners questions</li>
<li>
Collaborate with cross functional teams to deliver new and updated insights to the market</li>
<li>
Ensure team members are consistently using tools and resources</li>
<li>
Support with CRM reporting</li>
<li>
Manage external vendor relationships that assist with sales tool development</li>
<li>
Assist in the full commercialization of new products and offerings</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
You Are</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Customer focused and have the values and practices that align customer needs and satisfaction as a primary considerations in all business decisions.</li>
<li>
Self-directed and bring new ideas to the team</li>
<li>
Able to manage multiple projects or tasks, making effective judgments as to prioritizing and time allocation.</li>
<li>
Able to Collaborate with multiple internal teams to complete projects</li>
<li>
Flexible and adaptable to changing environments
</li>
<li>Organized and detail oriented</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
You Have</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
5+ years of healthcare industry experience</li>
<li>
Bachelor's degree in Business or related degree (preferred)</li>
<li>
Working knowledge of a SaaS company product/program cycle</li>
</ul>
