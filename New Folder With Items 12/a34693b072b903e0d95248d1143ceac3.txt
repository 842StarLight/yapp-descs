<h2>Digital Marketing PPC Campaign Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>LiveHelpNow’s mission is to make real customer relationships possible by providing the best customer service software platform for support teams. Even though we are a 15-year-old company we still run it as a startup, so it is a lot of fun to work here.</p>
<p>We are looking for a highly competent and proficient Digital Marketing PPC campaign hero! The right candidate will be experienced and able to achieve results for our businesses in regards to Pay-Per-Click, Brand Awareness Campaigns, SEO, SEM, and Social Media Campaigns. <ul><li>Provide reporting and analytical data as to the effectiveness of each campaign</li></ul>
