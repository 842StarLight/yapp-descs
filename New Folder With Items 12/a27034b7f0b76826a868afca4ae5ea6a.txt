<p><b>BRANCH MANAGER – POWELL, OHIO</b></p>
<p>The Middlefield Banking Company is a full service FDIC-insured commercial bank and LPL Financial® brokerage headquartered in Middlefield, Ohio. We have 15 branches serving Beachwood, Chardon, Cortland, Dublin, Garrettsville, Mantua, Middlefield, Newbury, Powell, Orwell, Solon, Sunbury, Twinsburg and Westerville, Ohio. The Bank also operates a Loan Production Office in Mentor, Ohio.</p>
<p>Dating back to 1901, The Middlefield Banking Company has been a leader in delivering quality personal financial service to its communities. We are continually recognized as one of the most profitable community banks in the U.S. If you are you seeking an opportunity to learn more or have a desire to start or advance your career in the banking industry, the Middlefield Banking Company is looking for enthusiastic team members to join our team!</p>
<p>We are seeking a Branch Manager for our Powell, Ohio location.</p>
<p>The qualified individual will have excellent leadership, lending and sales experience. You will be responsible for overseeing all branch operations while supervising the work of others. In this position you will:</p>
<ul>
<li>Work directly with customers to build relationships and provide products and services to satisfy their needs.</li>
<li>Actively promote Bank products and service</li>
<li>Conduct business development calls.</li>
<li>Responsible for mortgage, consumer and small commercial loan portfolio</li>
<li>Manage the branch budget to meet branch revenue and expense objectives.</li>
<li>Responsible for the acquisition, retention and expansion of customers through proactively making outside business calls.</li>
<li>Maintain high visibility in the community through participation and attendance at functions and community organizations.</li>
<li>Effectively coach and lead your team to deliver/drive business to your branch.</li>
</ul>
<p>To be considered you will need:</p>
<ul>
<li>A strong business acumen with attention to operational policies and procedures combined with a strong sales ability and persuasiveness.</li>
<li>High School Diploma and some college preferred.</li>
<li>Minimum of 5 years related management experience in financial services to include sales, lending and leadership.</li>
<li>Outstanding business development calling skills</li>
<li>Excellent verbal and written communication skills and proficient in bank information systems and software.</li>
</ul>
