<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"></h2>
<p><b>Overview</b></p>
<b>
</b><div><div><div>
<div>Assists the Int’l Marketing Director in all aspects of brand management, business analysis and marketing programs that support strategic business objectives inclusive of the development and introduction of new &amp; existing products into International markets. Works closely with US Marketing team.</div>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"></h2>
<p><b>Responsibilities</b></p>
<b>
</b><div><div><div>
<ul>
<li>Organizes regular updates with International markets (conference calls, emails, weekly updates) and communicate key decisions and assignments</li>
<li>
Communicates and coordinates with various departments (Marketing, Forecasting &amp; Planning, Purchasing, R&amp;D, etc) to complete projects.</li>
<li>
Assist in development, implementation and management of Country and Category Marketing Plans (including brand positioning, pricing, promotion and advertising) for new and existing products.</li>
<li>
Helps in the implementation of Marketing Plans (including promotion and advertising) for new and existing products</li>
<li>
Assists each market with Product Project Plans and calendars for approved product concepts</li>
<li>
Works with markets to create Product Project Plans for new products, restaged products and discontinued products. Presents for review and approval.</li>
<li>
Reviews and gets approval for annual forecasts &amp; pricing.</li>
<li>
Reviews artwork for consistency of brand and product messaging and routes artwork for approval</li>
<li>
Provides or helps develop messaging for products and product categories</li>
<li>
Works with purchasing, R&amp;D, and the international markets to complete product registration requirements</li>
<li>
Manages and reduces potential scrap costs resulting from product, packaging and formula changes</li>
<li>
Organizes and manages cross-functional project teams for assigned countries/categories for the following activities: product development, consumer research, manufacturing, forecasting &amp; purchasing, quality, inventory management, distribution, sales and marketing.</li>
</ul>
