<p><b>
Job Description</b><br>
</p>
<p>
As Content and Copywriter Manager, you’ll be responsible for creating compelling copy that educates, influences, inspires and attracts prospective clients and maximizes SEO value. You will be writing for all of our various communication channels: email, display advertisements, one-sheets, sales collateral, white papers, blog posts, landing pages, social media, presentations, and more. You’ll be on the forefront of creating our voice and an important piece of an amazing team of marketers.<br>
</p>
<p><b>
Responsibilities:</b><br>
</p>
<ul>
<li> Ability to work independently as well as part of a team</li>
<li> Write great content that maximizes SEO value</li>
<li> Work well with the Digital Marketing Manager and the rest of the marketing department to create amazing content</li>
<li> Contribute ideas for content to be used in digital and social channels</li>
<li> Aid in the construction of the overall content strategy and messaging</li>
<li> Deliver projects on schedule, on budget and on time</li>
<li> Managing several projects at the same time</li>
<li> Create polished and well-written creative materials for presentations</li>
<li> Understand creative direction and ask insightful questions to produce high impact content</li>
<li> Analytics and reporting to measure the effectiveness of our content</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Requirements</b><br>
</p>
<p>
3+ years’ experience in the following:<br>
</p>
<ul>
<li> Writing content for SaaS, Tech or digital commerce companies</li>
<li> Writing search and display ads for various channels, presentations and decks for external use in webinars, training, educational, engagement, as well as writing email content and thought leadership pieces</li>
<li> Experience with Google Ads, LinkedIn, Facebook Business Manager, WordPress, Pardot, Salesforce</li>
<li> Basic HTML and CSS Knowledge</li>
<li> Social Media Experience</li>
<li> Portfolio and Examples Required</li>
<li> Local candidates in the Los Angeles area only</li>
</ul>

Bachelor's Degree Preferred</div>
