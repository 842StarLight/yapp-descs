<h2>Sr Manager Supply Planning/S&OP</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p><b>About Dexcom:
</b></p>
<p>Founded in 1999, Dexcom, Inc. provides continuous glucose monitoring technology to help patients and their clinicians better manage diabetes. Since our inception, we have focused on better outcomes for patients, caregivers, and clinicians by delivering solutions for people with diabetes - while empowering our community to take control of diabetes<b>.</b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Summary:
</b></p>
<p>Are you a supply chain leader that thrives in a highly collaborative, high growth company? Do you enjoy building new tools, processes, and team members to become a best in class organization? Dexcom is looking for a Sr. Manager of Supply Planning to help us continue our journey with S&amp;OP while also building out a team of Master Planners and Analysts that will enable us to scale the business in the most efficient way possible, all while continuing to meet customer experience requirements.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Essential Duties and Responsibilities:</b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>This position is responsible for leading a variety of supply chain planning functions that contribute to commercial growth and proper inventory management. You will cover many different areas but focus on S&amp;OP, data analysis, master supply/demand planning, distribution planning, system optimization, product launch and retirement execution, and talent development.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Education and Experience Requirements:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Typically requires a Bachelor’s degree with 13+ years of industry experience. 5-8 years of previous people management experience.</li>
<li>Leads Supply Review and Pre-S&amp;OP meetings with key stakeholders to drive optimal Master Production Schedule that balances resources, inventory, and financials</li>
<li>Work cross functionally with Sales, Estimating, Quality, Manufacturing, Engineering, and Design to ensure best cost manufacturing strategy is being achieved.</li>
<li>Organize a central tower function where all areas are orchestrating in similar fashion focused on inventory goals, E&amp;O reduction, and revenue growth</li>
<li>Accountable for non-revenue demand planning, including warranty, R&amp;D, Engineering requirements</li>
<li>Provide development and learning opportunities that support staff growth and career development</li>
<li>Work closely with Master Data Management and Manufacturing to ensure BOMs are creating in time to support master planning/scheduling activities</li>
<li>Lead and Support standardization and best practices</li>
<li>Develops and maintains metrics to track, analyze, and improve performance and business contributions</li>
<li>Engage with each manufacturing site and partner organization to understand their supply chain and procurement capabilities, gaps, needs, and limitations</li>
<li>Own MRP accuracy and continue to improve the performance and adoption as the organization grows</li>
<li>Partner with members of the commercial teams in forecasting short / long-term drug requirements, manage inventory levels, and track supply chain costs for the Company</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Preferred Qualifications:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Experience with global sourcing and supply base management • Knowledge of electronics manufacturing, logistics, warehousing, and transportation</li>
<li>Advanced Excel user
</li>
<li>Knowledge of SQL, Tableau, and other business intelligence tools
</li>
<li>Oracle experience a plus</li>
<li>Advanced Planning software (Anaplan, Kinaxis, JDA, etc.) a plus</li>
<li>Certifications and/or degrees in Supply Chain preferred<br>
</li>
</ul>
