<h2>Global Business Analyst</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Based in Cincinnati, Ohio and with offices and support teams around the globe, Hydro Systems is the world’s largest independent manufacturer of chemical injecting, proportioning, dispensing and medicating equipment. With strategic partnerships, innovative solutions and more than 50 years of experience, Hydro Systems is committed to enriching the lives of its customers by creating a cleaner, more sustainable world. Its products serve numerous dosing and dispensing applications within cleaning, commercial laundry, warewash, food service, industrial, irrigation, horticulture, dairy, animal health, water treatment and other industries. Hydro Systems ensures dosing is always accurate, safe and cost effective, helping customers clean with confidence. For more information, visit http://hydrosystemsco.com/.</p>
<p><b>
Position Summary:</b></p>
<p>
The Business Analyst will be responsible for timely business reporting, improve turnaround time on new business quotations, and aid in the maximization of profit. In addition, this role will improve business intelligence and trends on products, customers and markets. This role will strive to assist sales in eliminating bottlenecks and other impediments to increase speed through the channel.</p>
<p><b>
Essential Duties and Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Serve as a liaison between Information Technology/Business Intelligence team, Finance team, and business units.</li>
<li>
Translate business objectives of the stakeholders to system/ functional requirements and develop solutions to business issues.</li>
<li>
Identify business trends utilizing real data, create analysis reports, and follow up on results</li>
<li>
Implement a comprehensive management plan for each managed project and keep all interested parties updated in project progress
</li>
<li>Determine, document, and serve as a gatekeeper for the for the business processes, data rules and requirements, and reporting standards within the organization</li>
<li>
Provide sales reports to Business Management team and manipulate data to show profitability as well as track and report significant variances relative to Business Plan.</li>
<li>
Create analyses of sales and profit scenarios by market, product, and/or customer.</li>
<li>
Generate and distribute Oracle P&amp;L / Sales reports.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Qualifications/Requirements:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>

<li>
2 years of applicable experience
</li>
<li>Systematic gathering, analyzing, and reporting of data</li>
<li>
Ability to understand and manage complexity</li>
<li>
Preferred experience with international business entities (Europe, Asia)</li>
<li>
Experience with Oracle is a plus</li>
<li>
Experience with BI tools (e.g., Cognos, Tableau) is a plus</li>
</ul>
