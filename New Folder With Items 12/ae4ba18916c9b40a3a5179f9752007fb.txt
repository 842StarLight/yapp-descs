<h2>Task (Temporary) - Store 1040</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>Under direct supervision, responsible for stocking merchandise, while following all company policies and procedures. The Task (Temporary) employee may also be assigned cashier responsibilities depending on the staffing needs of a particular store.<br>
<br>
<b>Use your skills, experience &amp; talents to be part of something BEAUTIFUL!</b> As a Task (Temporary) employee you will perform the following essential functions…<br>
<br>
<ul>
<li>Assist the Merchandise Manager with daily store replenishment, merchandise flexing process, store cleanliness standards including trash removal, physical inventory preparations, and merchandising and visual standards.</li>
<li>Execute load processing to productivity goals.</li>
<li>Execute out-of-stock, cycle count, return-to-vendor, return-to-distribution center, product break-ups, and destroy-in-field processes.</li>
<li>Set up advertising displays or arranges merchandise on counters or tables to promote sales and promotional events. Ensures compliance with established merchandising and housekeeping standards.</li>
<li>Adhere to all company policies, standards, and procedures.</li>
<li>Other duties as assigned.</li>
</ul>
</div>
<div>
<b>Experience we are looking for…</b><br>
<br>
<ul>
<li>High school diploma, no relevant work experience or equivalent combination of education and relevant work experience.</li>
<li>Demonstrates significant competency in sales, products and service.</li>
<li>Good communication skills.</li>
<li>Ability to troubleshoot.</li>
<li>Ability to work independently and as part of a team.</li>
<li>Ability to prioritize guest needs over task execution.</li>
<li>Ability to build and maintain strong customer relationships.</li>
</ul>
<br>
<b>
Other</b><br>
<br>
<ul><li>On a regular basis, requires the ability to stand, bend and lift up to 50lbs. merchandise</li></ul>
</div>
