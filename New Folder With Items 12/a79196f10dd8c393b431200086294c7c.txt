About Business Development
<br><br>
Do you love connecting with prospective clients and evangelizing a product? Are you motivated by working towards a target? Do you feel rewarded when you meet or exceed your goals?
<br><br>
Our Business Development Team is the first point of contact for many of our future customers and provides significant value to our national sales team by driving interest in our platform. Business Development Associates conduct research about decision makers inside target organizations and connect with those leaders to engage them in conversation about the value of our product. Experience in business development is highly valuable as one grows a career in cyber security sales and is broadly applicable across any number of disciplines.
<br><br>
SecurityScorecard welcomes recent grads starting a career and professionals who desire to pivot their careers towards cyber security sales to join our Talent Community.
<br><br>
