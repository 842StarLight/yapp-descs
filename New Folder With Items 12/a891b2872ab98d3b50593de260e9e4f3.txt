<b>PRIMARY PURPOSE OF POSITION</b>
<br>
This position has overall responsibility for the financial and business operations and execution of projects within the Utility of the Future (UoF) organization. Leads a team of analysts, engineers, consultants and project managers that perform high-level analysis, prepare comprehensive reports and manage high profile projects with broad internal (Legal, Regulatory, Finance and Operations) stakeholders and externally facing partners. Develops business cases and other general financial and operational analysis for current and future UoF Initiatives.

<br>

<b>PRIMARY DUTIES AND ACCOUNTABILITIES
</b>
</div>
<ul>
<li>Develop, own and maintain business cases and models for current and future initiatives. Obtain buy in and support from internal stakeholder groups including but not limited to regulatory, finance and operations.
</li>
<li>Lead and manage high profile cross-organizational projects. Will have responsibility for managing the contractual relationships established with the various project partners and the on-time delivery of project deliverables.
</li>
<li>Develop tracking and reporting mechanisms to validate business case assumptions during pilot projects and conduct analysis that inform decisions and strategies for broader scale deployment.
</li>
<li>Develop and enforce change management associated with full scale implementation of Emerging Technologies.
</li>
<li>Lead and oversee the activities including establishing strategy, goals and managing communication, activities and relationships with internal and external stakeholders.
</li>
<li>Manage the process of developing and updating short-term (annual budget) and long-term financial plans (LRP) for the department. Provide periodic updates as needed to Finance, Investment Strategy and other stakeholders.
</li>
<li>Prepare periodic and as needed status reports on the projects and initiatives being executed.
</li>
<li>Responsible for monthly handling day to day issues regarding the budget and finances for the department.
</li>
<li>Participate in industry leading activities including writing articles and participating in working groups or task forces.
</li>
</ul>
