<h2>Plant Manager - Maurice, IA</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p>Job ID: 154031</p>
<p>
 Siouxland Concrete Company, A CRH Company, is based in South Sioux City, Neb., and delivers ready mix concrete and related construction materials throughout the Siouxland region.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Job Summary</b></p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
Supervises and coordinates activities of workers engaged in manufacturing concrete products and delivering ready mix concrete by performing the following duties.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Essential Duties and Responsibilities</b></p>
<p></p>
<br>
<ul>
<li>
Quotes prices and receives orders for company products such as ready mix concrete, pipes, utility vaults, or septic tanks.</li>
<li>
Computes amount of water to be added to each mix of concrete.</li>
<li>
Records quantity of sand, cement, gravel, and water required for each mix on loading ticket for use by Concrete Batch Plant Operator.</li>
<li>
Prepares invoices, delivery tickets, and related shipping documents and assigns drivers for scheduled deliveries.</li>
<li>
Maintains payroll and driver records.</li>
<li>
Directs production workers to cast concrete products according to customer specifications.</li>
<li>
Confers with plant production and yard personnel to establish delivery schedules according to factors such as truck capacities, distances to delivery site, and unloading time.</li>
<li>
Communicates with supervisor, on issues effecting the business and personnel, safety, maintenance, upcoming jobs, competition, and pricing.</li>
<li>
Supervises the processing of delivery tickets, and purchase orders.</li>
<li>
Oversees and maintains appropriate inventory levels.</li>
<li>
Receives cash payments for shipments from drivers and prepares reports of cash and charge sales.</li>
<li>
Works with Safety Department to ensure compliance with City, State and Federal regulations.</li>
<li>
Addresses customer relations’ issues.</li>
<li>
Ensures that equipment and facilities are properly maintained.</li>
<li>
Communicates with the Human Resources Department on all issues affecting employee hiring, promotion, discipline and termination.</li>
<li>
Works with the Quality Control Department to ensure products meet specifications.</li>
<li>
Other duties as assigned.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
