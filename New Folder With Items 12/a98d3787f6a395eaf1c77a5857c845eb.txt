<p>
Oldcastle APG, a CRH company, is a leading supplier of products for North America’s building and landscaping markets. Its products include concrete masonry and hardscapes, packaged cement mixes, packaged lawn and garden products and composite decking products that are sold through a variety of channels. Oldcastle APG is the innovator behind many of the industry’s well-known brands including Belgard Hardscapes, Echelon masonry products, Sakrete bagged dry-mixes, Anchor Wall Systems, MoistureShield composite decking, among others. With over 195 operating locations and 6,500 employees, Oldcastle APG operates across 36 states and 5 Canadian provinces. Oldcastle APG is a U.S. subsidiary of CRH plc., a leading global diversified building materials group.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Job Summary</b></p>
<p><br>
We are seeking an entry level Data Analyst responsible for reviewing and/or summarizing data with the goal of <b>extracting meaningful information or developing data-based conclusions for APG Leadership</b><b>. </b>The Data Analyst will analyze or provide historical, current, and predictive views of data stored in sales, production or financial databases. The successful candidate will turn data into information, information into insight and insight into business decisions.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<ul>
<li>
Interpreting data, analyzing results using statistical techniques</li>
<li>
Developing and implementing data analyses, data collection systems and other strategies that optimize statistical efficiency and quality</li>
<li>
Partner with the Analytics Architect to develop and implement databases, data collection systems, data analytics and other strategies that optimize statistical efficiency and quality</li>
<li>
Acquiring data from primary or secondary data sources</li>
<li>
Identifying, analyzing, and interpreting trends or patterns in complex data sets</li>
<li>
Filtering and “cleaning” data</li>
<li>
Partnering with leadership to prioritize business and information needs</li>
<li>
Locating and defining new process improvement opportunities</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Experience and Qualifications</b></p>
<p></p>
<br>
<ul>
<li>
BS in Mathematics, Economics, Computer Science, Information Management or Statistics</li>
<li>
Proven working experience as a data analyst or business data analyst</li>
<li>
Technical experience regarding data models, database design development, data mining and segmentation techniques</li>
<li>
Strong knowledge of and experience with reporting packages, databases (SQL etc), programming (XML, Javascript, or ETL frameworks)</li>
<li>
Knowledge of statistics and experience using statistical packages for analyzing datasets (R, Python)</li>
<li>
Strong analytical skills with the ability to collect, organize, analyze, and disseminate significant amounts of information with attention to detail and accuracy</li>
</ul>
