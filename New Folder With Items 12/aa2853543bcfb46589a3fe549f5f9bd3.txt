<h2>Internal Communications Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<div>
<p>Connecting people with each other and the right information is mission-critical. Intrado, formerly West develops innovative cloud-based technology to make it easier, more effective and more efficient to make the right connections. Our solutions put people in sync with each other and the right information, so they gain the insight needed to reach better decisions on the issues that matter most. We do it with a laser focus on reliability.</p>
<p>Intrado is a leading provider of technology-driven, communication services, serving Fortune 1000 companies and other clients in a variety of industries, including telecommunications, retail, financial services, public safety, technology and healthcare. For more than 30 years, we have been leading the way in hosted and cloud-based solutions.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
Intrado is looking to hire an <b>Internal Communications Manager</b> in Islandia.
</p>
<p>Intrado is looking for a passionate and strategic manager to support internal communications and engagement efforts with a focus on informing employees, providing meaningful connections, and enhancing our culture. You’ll create, shape and implement programs and related communications that will support company-wide and business unit initiatives, keep employees up-to-date about company news and events and connected to Intrado culture.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p>Our <b>Internal Communications Manager </b>will be a skilled communicator, preferably with experience managing internal and employee communication programs. We need a storyteller - a skilled communications professional who can drive cohesion across all initiatives and create compelling, relevant connections between our employees, our strategy and our business. This is an individual contributor position.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p>The ideal candidate has experience and demonstrated success in managing B2B marketing organizations in executing programs that utilize a broad mix of communication vehicles. Top-notch communication skills, a self-starter mentality, the capacity for both strategic thinking and tactical know-how, and the motivation to drive projects with a sense of urgency and thoughtfulness are absolute must-haves.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>Team interfaces are vitally important, and the ideal candidate will establish a good working relationship with project team members and department contacts in order to maintain and continuously strive to improve the level of overall service being provided and ensure the quality of all written communications by reviewing issues and content prior to distribution.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Requirements</b>:</p>
<ul>
<li>Passion for effective storytelling</li>
<li>Excellent written and verbal communications skills</li>
<li>Keen ability to gather feedback via survey writing</li>
<li>Experience managing a corporate Intranet and automated email programs</li>
<li>Ability to work with all levels of employees, including executives and other senior leaders</li>
<li>Strong project management skills with strong attention to detail</li>
<li>Outstanding relationship-building and customer service skills</li>
<li>Ability to exercise judgment, particularly in narrow time-frames</li>
<li>Emotional intelligence and authenticity</li>
<li>Willingness to take on projects and tasks as assigned/as needed</li>
<li>All-in approach to work</li>
<li>Good analytical abilities and basic P&amp;L/financial understanding</li>
</ul>
</div>
<p></p>
<div>Qualifications<div>
</div>
<div><b>
Education</b></div>
<ul><li>
Bachelor's degree from an accredited college or university with major course work in communications, journalism, marketing or related field required</li></ul>
<div><b>Experience</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
Minimum 5 years’ experience marketing communications experience required for a global company</li>
<li>
Intermediate knowledge of Word and Excel (inclusive of excel charting), PowerPoint and Outlook required</li>
<li>
Writing and presentation samples will be required prior to interview</li>
</ul>
