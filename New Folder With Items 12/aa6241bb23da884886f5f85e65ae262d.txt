<h2>Account Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Account Manager</p>
<p>
Location: Columbia, MD, or potential to work remotely</p>
<p>
Personify, Inc. provides associations, non-profits, and event planners and organizers with powerful SaaS solutions that help engage constituents, attendees, increase and accelerate booth sales and drive value to event participants. We are looking for a high-energy individual to join us in the role of Account Manager, working as an important part of our Sales team within the Events portfolio. This exceptional individual will have the opportunity to act as a client advocate and help our clients improve their event sales and marketing processes by fitting them with the right technology solution. If you are a people person with a background in sales, marketing or management…this job could be for you. Prior experience or knowledge of Personify A2Z Events and the events and association industry is a plus, but not required.</p>
<p>
General description of the position:</p>
<div>
Directly related to client/project:<br>
<ul>
<li>Build strong relationships with clients during their event cycle to understand their needs and establish the value of Personify.</li>
<li>Assess upgrades and enhancements to their current Personify solution set and offer them the value set that is best for their challenges and needs.</li>
<li>Work collaboratively with cross functional teams to ensure client’s success</li>
<li>Conduct face to face and virtual meetings with Key Clients</li>
<li>Develop and maintain knowledge of Personify’s Event solutions and services</li>
<li>Consult clients with best practices utilizing their Personify Event solution</li>
<li>Maintain a strong renewal percentage with a key focus on upgrading and enhancing the Client’s solution set</li>
<li>Become proficient in Salesforce to manage your pipeline of renewals and upsells</li>
<li>Participate in recurring client outreach via phone calls, emails or other forms of communication</li>
<li>Attend industry events to foster relationships with clients and potential clients</li>
<li>Understand, learn, and utilize SPI sales methodology to showcase the value of Personify Events solutions.</li>
<li>Other projects as assigned.</li>
</ul>
</div>
<div>
Other:<br>
<ul>
<li>Participate in weekly pipeline reviews to assess status of client’s solution set and work on acquiring new upsell leads from current client list</li>
<li>Participate in project kick-off calls with Project Managers and clients as needed</li>
<li>In office, part-telecommunication, or full telecommunication depending on candidate’s location.</li>
<li>Some travel is required (20 – 30%)</li>
</ul>
Administrative:<br>
<ul>
<li>Maintain accurate records of all email and phone communication for every client for future reference.</li>
<li>Update client and potential client information in Salesforce and client database systems.</li>
</ul>
</div>
<p>
Preferred Background/Experience:</p>
<div>
Education:<br>
<ul><li>Bachelor’s degree preferred</li></ul>
</div>
<div>
Experience:<br>
<ul>
<li>Two or more years of experience with sales or account management skill set</li>
<li>Strong aptitude to increase and maintain solid pipeline</li>
</ul>
<br>
Skills:<br>
<ul>
<li>In-depth knowledge of the event industry is preferred</li>
<li>Experience using Salesforce is desirable</li>
<li>Involvement in event industry organizations is a plus! (IAEE, PCMA, MPI, AMPS, ASAE, etc.)</li>
<li>Knowledge of Personify Events products or event floorplan software is a plus</li>
<li>Strong written and verbal communication skills</li>
<li>Personable, relationship builder who can be assertive and persistent</li>
<li>Must be self-motivated and a team player</li>
<li>Strong organization skills and attention to detail necessary</li>
</ul>
</div>
