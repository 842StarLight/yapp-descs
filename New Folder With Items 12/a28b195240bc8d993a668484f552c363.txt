<h2>Membership Coordinator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Overview
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div>The Salvation Army Ray &amp; Joan Kroc Corps Community Center is seeking a full-time Membership Coordinator to spearhead the acquisition of new memberships and maintain existing memberships at the Salvation Army Boston Kroc Center.</div>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<ul>
<li>Provide direction and oversight to Customer Service Associate and Membership Service Leads as related to recruitment of new members and sales of membership to walk-ins, center guests, and phone inquiries regarding center membership.</li>
<li>
Oversee all aspects of training of all Customer Service Associates and Membership Service Leads on job related functions via regular staff meetings and/or training sessions.</li>
<li>
Provide on-going feedback to staff and conduct initial and annual performance evaluations</li>
<li>
Achieve or maintain membership targets through a variety of sales techniques including conducting thorough tours, developing follow-up processes with membership inquiries, active pursuit of member referrals, development of corporate and civic relationships, medical and general community contacts.</li>
<li>
Assist in the development of and to work within department’s operating budget.</li>
<li>
Coordinate internal and external communication and implement recruitment strategies to acquire memberships in collaboration with Kroc staff; this will include (but not limited to) direct mail, membership mailings, print advertising, advertising in appropriate publications, and website information.</li>
<li>
Communicate center services, fees and hours or operation to prospective members and members, Kroc staff, and the community.</li>
<li>
Responsible for all aspects of the work shift, including delegation of duties and tasks, as well as direction and control of daily workload for all staff under the supervision of the Membership Coordinator.
</li>
<li>Perform public speaking engagements for community groups, employers, area corporations and others to create positive public relations for the center and client.
</li>
<li>Participate in operational oversight as related to departments integral to center membership growth and attend management meetings, as scheduled.</li>
<li>
Develop and implement a plan to integrate Kroc Center information sharing and membership with other campus-wide initiatives, including (but not limited to) center space rentals, partnerships, and outside groups that utilize Kroc facilities.</li>
<li>
Promote, sell, and support each product line or service provided at the Kroc Center, including membership, programs and corps functions.
</li>
<li>Represent the center by attending off-site events such as health fairs and corporate events with the intent of sharing center information and procuring memberships.</li>
<li>
Plan, organize, coordinate, promote and facilitate special events. This includes but is not limited to: Walk for Dudley, Miracle on Dudley, Boston Shines, Multi-Cultural Festival, and Seasonal Events.</li>
<li>
Provide assistant with schedule coordination for room reservations</li>
<li>
Provide reports as needed using the Center’s software system (s), including but not limited to CCMS.</li>
<li>
Must be willing and able to work weekends and evenings as may be required in performance of duties or as requested to support the ongoing operations of the Kroc Center.
</li>
<li>Represent The Salvation Army’s spiritual ministry and social mission in the community at large.</li>
<li>
Perform other assignment/duties as directed.</li>
</ul>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Qualifications
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<ul><li>Bachelor’s degree in sales and marketing or related field</li></ul>
<ul>
<li>
Minimum of three (3) years experience in sales field.
</li>
<li>Two-Three (2-3) years’ experience in management field</li>
<li>
Minimum one (1) year customer service experience (excluding retail or food sales).</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>
Excellent sales, marketing, research and tracking skills required.</li>
<li>
Must have strong customer service and interpersonal communication skills.</li>
<li>
Must be able to read, write and communicate in English</li>
<li>
Valid Driver’s License and the ability to pass The Salvation Army’s Motor Vehicle Request.</li>
<li>
Hospitality management skills a plus.</li>
<li>
Must be flexible with work schedule.</li>
<li>
Must be proficient in Net Simplicity, CCMS, Microsoft Word, Excel, Access, Lotus Notes, and MS Publisher.</li>
</ul>
