<h2>Marketing Coordinator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Master Service Companies, a regional leader in the waterproofing, foundation repair and concrete repair industry, is looking for a full-time Marketing Coordinator to help our growing Indianapolis team.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>Our purpose at MSC is to redefine the construction industry by providing our customers with a remarkable experience. We care deeply for and strive to build a long-term relationship with each customer. At MSC we also redefine for our employees. We want our employees to know that they are the most valuable to us and experience success in a way they never imagined possible.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Marketing Coordinator</b></p>
<p>Master Service Companies – Indianapolis, IN</p>
<p><b>The Opportunity:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Assist the Marketing Manager with a range of projects and campaigns as needed</li>
<li>Write articles, blogs, case studies, and promotional material for the website</li>
<li>Creating compelling content for marketing materials as needed</li>
<li>Develop content and calendar for all social media posts</li>
<li>Manage all aspects of local home shows and events throughout year, including but not limited to, booth materials, lead goals, budget, and ideas to improve efficiency and conversion.</li>
<li>Oversee all external site listings and respond to reviews on external sites</li>
<li>Research and pursue award opportunities, including writing and submitting entries</li>
<li>Achieve organization goals by accepting ownership for accomplishing new and different requests; exploring opportunities to add value to job accomplishments.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Job Requirements:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>~1 year of intern/professional experience in Communication or Marketing</li>
<li>Bachelor’s Degree in Communications, English, Marketing, or related field</li>
<li>Superior writing skills including use of proper grammar, spelling, and punctuation</li>
<li>Strong organization, planning, and time management skills</li>
<li>Experience in show or event management is a plus</li>
<li>Exceptional problem-solving skills</li>
<li>Exhibit a sense of urgency and ownership of projects through to completion</li>
<li>Effective communication and interpersonal skills</li>
<li>High proficiency with software packages such as Microsoft Office, Constant Contact, etc.</li>
<li>Ability to work independently and in a team environment</li>
</ul>
