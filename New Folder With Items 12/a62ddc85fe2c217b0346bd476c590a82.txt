<h2>National Account Manager, West</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<b>Division Information</b><br>
<p>Spectrum Brands – The Pet Care business unit of Spectrum Brands is based in St. Louis, Missouri with distribution, manufacturing and sales offices throughout the US and globe. Owners of dogs, cats, birds, small animals, fish and reptiles rely on our brands for exceptional value and trusted results. Our industry-leading brands include Tetra®, Instant Ocean®, Marineland®, 8-in-1®, Dingo®, FURminator®, Nature’s Miracle®, GloFish®, DreamBone® and SmartBones®.</p>
<br>
<b>
Job Summary</b><br>
<p>The National Account Manager will be managing all activities related to sales and relationship growth with existing and prospect accounts, including strategy development, presentation creation, analysis, forecasting and tactical preparation related to calls and events.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
The accounts in this region include – Albertsons, Safeway, R.C. Taylor, WinCo Foods, Save Mart Supermarkets, Stater Bros. Markets, Bi-Mart, Vons, Raley’s, Unified Grocers, Northern Sales, Combined Sales, C&amp;S Wholesale Grocers West</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<b>
Primary Duties &amp; Responsibilities</b><br>
<p></p>
<p><i>
Procedures-70%</i></p>
<ul>
<li>Developing strategies that will maximize the Spectrum Brands SKU listing.</li>
<li>Identify opportunities from within the existing Spectrum Brands line that will achieve the "best practice" scenario and recommend additions.</li>
<li>Lead development of annual quotas and profit plan</li>
<li>Lead sales action plans; monitor actual results vs. plan throughout the year; make adjustments when appropriate.</li>
<li>Become the expert on assigned accounts. Understand the retailer’s needs, strategies, and objectives. Use this expertise to build the Global Pet Care business within each individual account.</li>
<li>Learn and utilize Category Management and Fact-Based Selling concepts in every account.</li>
<li>Manage Broker partnerships to effectively complete all administrative forms, programs, resets, or projects necessary.</li>
<li>Work with consumers, store personnel and marketing to identify and evaluate new product ideas</li>
<li>Target specific prospect accounts for conversion, execute sales action plans, understand account needs, develop relationships and assist in securing new business.</li>
<li>Build and deliver retailer specific presentations for line reviews, programs, mid-season updates, etc.</li>
<li>Build and closely manage relationships with customers at the buyer level and beyond to create or enhance the partnership</li>
<li>Provide the team lead with regular summaries of total POS performance vs. prior year same period.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p>
</p>
<p><i>Promotion-25%</i></p>
<ul>
<li>Develop the annual promotional plan for each respective account to maximize sales opportunities by having the right product, in the right quantity, at the right time.</li>
<li>Use data analysis (AC Nielsen, IRI, Comp market data, Store Level POS, etc.) to assist in the coordination of promotional plans &amp; display allocation suggestions. Integrate national promotional plans (FSI’s, IRC’s, etc.) into each account’s promotional plan.</li>
<li>Recommend and assist development of unique promotional ideas.</li>
<li>Evaluate the performance of all promotions including those from our competitors.</li>
<li>Accurately and timely forecast with Forecasting/Demand Planning to guarantee on-time delivery.</li>
<li>Develop and coordinate the plan for product delivery and merchandising. (Warehouse delivery, direct to store, cross-dock, retail service, etc.)</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p>
</p>
<p><i>Pricing-5%</i></p>
<ul>
<li>Analyze retailer GM by SKU and retain a running average weighted margin. Evaluate against industry standards, customer goals, and competition. Recommend pricing and programs that will achieve the customer goals and maximize the Spectrum Brands AGM.</li>
<li>Develop/manage account-specific promotional plan, coordinating with finance, trade marketing, marketing and sales management to align with brand strategies and advertising plans as appropriate.</li>
<li>Manage P&amp;L by recommending opportunities for increased penetration, based on knowledge of customers and territory; Assist in accurate and timely forecasting and work with Forecasting/Demand Planning to guarantee on-time delivery</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p>
</p>
<br>
<b>Education and Experience Profile</b><br>
<p><i>An equivalent combination of:</i></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree in Business or related discipline preferred but not required</li>
<li>5 years in retail field sales and direct account responsibility</li>
<li>A minimum of 2-4 years’ work experience in consumer products industry is preferred</li>
<li>Experience working with syndicated data or category management preferred</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p>
</p>

<li>Strong customer focus</li>
<li>Teamwork approach to accomplishing goals</li>

</ul>
