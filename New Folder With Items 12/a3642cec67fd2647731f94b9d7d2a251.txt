<div>Content Marketing Manager – Data Platform</div>
<div></div>
<div>Location: San Francisco Bay area</div>
<div>Term: Full-time</div>
<div>Website: www.intertrust.com</div>
<div></div>
<div><b>WHO WE ARE:</b></div>
<div></div>
<div>Intertrust’s mission is to make every internet transaction trustworthy. Our family of integrated media, security, and data management products allow small businesses and leading global corporations alike, to expand their reach by delivering rights-managed services, secure apps, and private data sets for various verticals including energy, entertainment, fintech and IoT. We have a legacy of invention and hold fundamental patents in the areas of computer security and digital trust. Intertrust is headquartered in Silicon Valley, with offices in San Francisco, Sunnyvale, London, Tokyo, Beijing, Mumbai, Riga, and Tallinn.</div>
<div></div>
<div><b>WHO WE’RE LOOKING FOR:</b></div>
<div></div>
<div>We are seeking a thoughtful, creative and tech-savvy Content Marketing Manager to support our data platform technology and products.</div>
<div></div>
<div>In this role, you will develop marketing materials from the ground up, spanning a range of short- and long-form content—from blog posts and email campaigns, to solution briefs and white papers. The Content Marketing Manager reports directly to the Global Head of Content but will work closely with product marketing and key stakeholders across the organization to create scalable, engaging campaigns that support sales, demand and the brand.</div>
<div></div>
<div>As the Content Marketing Manager, you will deftly handle incoming content requests as well as develop your own strategic content plans. You should thrive on creativity, execution, and accountability.</div>
<div></div>
<div><b>HOW YOU WILL MAKE A DIFFERENCE:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Work closely with marketing teams to formulate content strategies across all channels, including web/blogs, social media, video, and email marketing</li>
<li>Help define messaging and branding for a product and then do everything that promotion requires (including content, web, invitations, ads) to bring that product into the world</li>
<li>Maintain schedule of quality content that engages our audiences, as you measure performance and optimize accordingly</li>
<li>Explore and experiment with unique ideas to help promote Intertrust and its products</li>
<li>Manage the full lifecycle of content development, from idea generation and implementation to analysis and refinement</li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<div><b>WHAT YOU SHOULD HAVE IN YOUR TOOLKIT:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>6+ years of experience in marketing writing with an agency or in-house team working for software companies</li>
<li>Excellent story teller, comfortable condensing complicated ideas into concise. readable copy that influences and engages</li>
<li>Comfortable working with product managers and sales teams to refine messaging frameworks and playbooks</li>
<li>Self-starter who identifies opportunities, comes up with great ideas, and moves projects over the goal line</li>
<li>Background and understanding of data rights management—especially related to energy, IoT, healthcare or automotive—a strong plus</li>
<li>Background in building ecosystems or marketplaces, another plus</li>
</ul>

<li>The opportunity to actively shape the direction of a leading global corporation and to regularly engage with company leadership</li>
</ul>
