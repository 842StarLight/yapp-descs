<b>VALUE ADVISOR</b><br>
SAP’s Value Advisory Practice helps companies transform their organizations by collaboratively developing plans and justifications for technology investment decisions, aligning solutions and business outcomes and experiences to strategic imperatives, building business cases, and developing value roadmaps.<br>
<b>ROLE DESCRIPTION</b><br>
The role of a Value Advisor requires a combination of management consulting expertise, executive relationship management, strong thought leadership and strategic expert consultation to shape the most strategic sales cycles and customer co-innovation and transformation programs, drive pipeline growth and quality through demand generation strategies, and contribute to building and maintaining SAP’s leadership position in the industry. The role is designed for a senior expert that is comfortable to lead, self-driven, hungry to learn, always looking to improve, and adaptable to changing priorities.<br>
<b>Responsibilities:</b><br>
<b>Business Development</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Strategy &amp; Industry Thought Leadership</li>
<li>Develop industry and LoB thought leadership and content and represent SAP externally and internally on a local level on industry/LoB sectors, innovation and value management related topics</li>
<li>Combine SAP go-to-market strategies with industry perspective to develop and execute sales plays and programs designed to build high-quality pipeline</li>
<li>Build and maintain a customer network and link customers together to help build and secure pipeline</li>
<li>Develop customer references in an industry to showcase SAP’s customer successes and educate the ecosystem on these references</li>
</ul>
<b>Strategic Account Business Development</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Partner with field sales teams to identify revenue opportunities, create account strategies, and execute sales plays</li>
<li>Lead account strategy sessions internally and with customers based on customer’s business priorities, industry trends and SAP sales plays</li>
<li>Align with customer C-level executives to uncover business challenges that can be solved by SAP</li>
</ul>
<b>Vision-to-Value engagements with priority customers</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Develop and validate high-quality business plans which clearly articulate our IVE strategy, GTM activities and key execution steps</li>
<li>Design customer engagements and align with team members</li>
<li>Position and lead SAP’s engagements with the customer with mutually agreed upon outcomes to build the case-for-change enabled by SAP solutions leading to deal closure</li>
<li>Act as a liaison between several teams within SAP to ensure that most important business drivers relevant to our customers are the point of focus in sales engagements</li>
<li>Use techniques such as design thinking, interviews and process benchmarking to envision potential new business models with customers, find new ways of conducting work more effectively and discover value opportunities</li>
<li>Hypothesize, calculate, validate and socialize the value potential of SAP powered business transformation while collaborating with internal SAP experts</li>
<li>Oversee engagement delivery and provide quality assurance. Work with the account team to ensure storytelling, compelling industry and value messages are build in the deliverables</li>
<li>Act as trusted advisor to the customers</li>
<li>Assist customer executives build and deliver board-level justification documents (case for change) for approval of multi-million dollar software led transformation projects</li>
</ul>
<b>Value Realization</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Monitor the progress selected customers are making in their SAP-enable transformations</li>
<li>Document value realized for reference purposes through customer interviews and assessments</li>
</ul>
<b>Practice Development</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Develop thought leadership delivered through content creation and presentations, business benchmarking, best practice assessments, customer success studies, social and traditional media interviews, etc.</li>
<li>Synthesize insights from best in class customers and our knowledge of business / industry into whitepapers, publications and other market relevant thought leadership artefacts</li>
<li>Develop industry and LoB relevant field content as well as enabling the field sales teams on its context</li>
</ul>
</div>
<p>The requirements of the role are similar in many ways to Management Consulting, however with significantly less travel (25% vs. 80%) and more multi-tasking on multiple customer engagements in different stages. The successful candidate will be comfortable in a fast-paced, sales results oriented, work environment and be able to develop new expertise in different industries, lines-of-business and solution areas as required.<br>
Given the dynamics of our enterprise software sales environment we are often called upon to execute our services by SAP top management within complex customer contexts and sales cycles.</p>
