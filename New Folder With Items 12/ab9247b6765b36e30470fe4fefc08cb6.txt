<div>The Business Development Operations role is a rotational role, having an opportunity to be exposed to all areas of the business by supporting operations across sales and marketing. You will play an integral role in selling and marketing, Cerebri AI’s solutions for enterprise by interacting with the respective teams and business stakeholders. Target account research, market research, planning, working with customers, analyzing business needs and requirements are all part of the business development operations role. This role is meant for someone who wants a broad exposure to the enterprise AI and software business and is willing to be creative and resourceful to getting the job done. You will be part of a focused team working in fast paced environment and must keep up with adjustments to responsibilities and priorities. Cerebri AI is proactive with hiring a diverse and inclusive team. Cerebri AI encourages people of all backgrounds, sex, ethnicity, diverse life experiences, immigrants, refugees, disabilities and veterans to apply.<br>
</div>
