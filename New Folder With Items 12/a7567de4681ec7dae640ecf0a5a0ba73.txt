<h2>Commercial Lender – FQHC| National Healthcare Lending</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<b>Job Description:</b><br>
<p><b>Summary:</b> Responsible for commercial loan business development in the National Healthcare Lending Business Unit, specifically to Federally Qualified Health Centers (FQHC) and not-for-profit community health centers.
</p>
<p></p>
<p>This is an officer level position. This position has the potential to develop and lead a team to support business development.
</p>
<p></p>
<h1 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Essential Duties and Responsibilities</b></h1>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li><p>
Manages an existing or develop a new commercial loan customer base and develops new business from referral sources, designated marketing efforts, and through cold calling efforts. Properly structures and successfully negotiates loan transactions with customers and prospects within accepted risk tolerance levels.
</p></li>
<li><p>Effectively cross-sells all bank loan, deposit, treasury management and electronic products.
</p></li>
<li><p>Manages daily overdrafts, provides quality customer service and answer customer inquiries or directs/refers them to appropriate BankFinancial personnel/third-party vendors.
</p></li>
<li><p>Manages loan renewals, collection efforts on all past due loans and loans in default in a timely manner and maintain a high level of asset quality within a designated portfolio.
</p></li>
<li><p>Maintains a satisfactory level of familiarity with target markets and customers.
</p></li>
<li><p>Reports to Management issues, new developments, market conditions as needed.
</p></li>
<li><p>Receives, previews, and conducts initial viability analysis on loan applications and related documents. Ensures that applications are complete as to core requirements and all other initial items have been received or requested prior to submission to underwriting.
</p></li>
<li><p>Reviews and presents loan presentations to supervisor, provide review of loan documents, and provide final review of all other related loan documentation and closing items.
</p></li>
<li><p>Follows-up with the customer or designated representative whenever the customer/representative provides an inadequate response, insufficient information, or does not respond in a timely manner to BankFinancial personnel through the underwriting and closing process as well as ongoing portfolio management.
</p></li>
<li><p>Maintains database software pipeline to record calling and sales efforts.
</p></li>
<li><p>Ensures electronic loan files are properly documented and maintained.
</p></li>
<li><p>Prepares management reports as needed.
</p></li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><i>BankFinancial is an Equal Opportunity Employer (minorities/females/veterans/individuals with disabilities/sexual orientation/gender identity)</i></p>
<p><br>
Required Experience:<br>
</p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s Degree (B.S.) from four-year college or university; three years related credit experience or equivalent combination of education and experience
</li>
<li>At least three years of relevant commercial credit experience that includes lending to FQHCs and/or not-for-profit community health centers
</li>
<li>Credit analysis experience
</li>
<li>Loan documentation skills
</li>
<li>Organizational skills
</li>
<li>Time management skills
</li>
<li>Negotiation skills</li>
</ul>
