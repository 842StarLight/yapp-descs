<h2>GL Accountant</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div><div>
<p><b>Summary</b></p>
<p>
The GL Accountant will perform accounting work involving compilation, consolidation, analysis, and review of financial data in assigned areas. Serves as a subject matter expert and provides guidance to team members as needed.</p>
<p>
The GL Accountant works closely with the Controller/Asst Controller to help promote our organization and add to the growth of the business.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Essential Job Functions</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Prepare balance sheet reconciliations by analyzing general ledger activity and propose appropriate adjusting journal entries</li>
<li>
Prepare sales and use tax returns</li>
<li>
Assist in monthly inventory compilation and review</li>
<li>
Prepare special financial analyses as requested by operations and/or finance management.</li>
<li>
Perform month-end financial close responsibilities (i.e. generate monthly standard journal entries, generate appropriate accruals, research variances)</li>
<li>
Generate monthly and daily journal entries</li>
<li>
Post all sales and reconcile to POS</li>
<li>
Perform weekly tender reconciliations</li>
<li>
Support accounts payable including credit card and out-of-pocket expense report tracking, reconciliation, and processing</li>
<li>
Support accounting period closing activities (monthly, quarterly, and yearly)</li>
<li>
Prepare and post adjusting balance sheet entries</li>
<li>
Work with external auditors</li>
<li>
Running daily, weekly and monthly reports to be distributed to all department heads</li>
<li>
Support annual financial audit</li>
<li>
Support the preparation of financial statements</li>
<li>
Maintain accuracy in records, data entry, and related files/systems</li>
<li>
Perform additional duties as assigned by the Controller/Asst Controller</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
