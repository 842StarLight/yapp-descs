<h2>Finisher</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>To perform tasks in support of the Made to Stock and Finishing PEI area; and to assist in ensuring that the product is manufactured, inspected, packaged and shipped in accordance with established specifications. To ensure that Hose Master and customer standards are archived.<br>
</p>
<br>
<b>RESPONSIBILITIES</b><ul>
<li>
Inspect assemblies to prints / specifications</li>
<li>
Inspect hose and identify leaks</li>
<li>
Use all types of measuring equipment</li>
<li>
Read and understand prints, Sales Orders and Job Orders</li>
<li>
Testing assemblies – air and HMS (Helium Mass Spec)</li>
<li>
Buff and/or Paint</li>
<li>
Assemble swivels</li>
<li>
Cover Hoses</li>
<li>
Maintain inventory</li>
<li>
Tag assemblies</li>
<li>
Package assemblies</li>
<li>
Complete paper work for certifications
</li>
<li>Enter Sales Order shipments into courier software programs such as UPS, FEDEX, DHL, USPS, etc.</li>
<li>
Ready packages for shipment, attaching labels, certifications etc. as sales order requires</li>
<li>
Knows and adheres to standards of quality and accuracy required</li>
<li>
Ability to react to change productively and handle other essential tasks as assigned</li>
</ul>
<br>
<b>
QUALIFICATIONS</b><ul>
<li>
High school diploma or equivalent</li>
<li>
Ability to understand and follow English instructions</li>
<li>
Manufacturing experience preferred, but not required</li>
<li>
Knowledge of hose and fittings</li>
<li>
Knowledge of using measuring instruments</li>
<li>
Ability to read and understand detailed work instructions and production
</li>
<li>Basic Computer skills – ability to use office software and courier software such as but not limited to, UPS, FEDEX, DHL, USPS</li>
<li>
Must be able to stand for long periods of time</li>
<li>
Must be able to lift and carry 50 pounds</li>
<li>
Must be able to work a minimum of 40 hours per week</li>
<li>
Ability to use hand and power tools</li>
<li>
Good hearing and vision for hose testing</li>
<li>
Must be able to bend, squat, stoop, twist and kneel</li>
</ul>
