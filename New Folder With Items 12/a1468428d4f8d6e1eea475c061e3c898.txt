<h2>Programmatic Account Executive</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">The ideal candidate is a self-starter, comfortable in prospecting new business, enjoys providing great service with new and existing clients and is comfortable speaking about programmatic advertising.
<br><br>
Candidates must be creative thinkers and passionate about storytelling, technology, media, and selling. Success will be highly dependent upon the candidate’s willingness to navigate prospective accounts, understand marketing strategies, and position Insider Inc’s programmatic products on their behalf.
<br><br>
<b>Responsibilities:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Grow advertising revenues by creating, maintaining, and sustaining relationships with media agencies, and clients directly</li>
<li>Coordinate across departments to create, execute and manage programmatic campaigns.</li>
<li>Achieve revenue goals</li>
<li>Learn and master multiple SSP UIs.</li>
<li>Manage, optimize and grow PMP deals</li>
<li>Convert Open Exchange advertisers to PMP or PD advertisers</li>
<li>Attend weekly and monthly sales and client service meetings as required</li>
<li>Represent Insider Inc. at events and serve as a brand ambassador</li>
<li>Manage activity using salesforce.com [http://salesforce.com/]</li>
</ul>
<b>Qualifications:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>1-3 years of Digital media experience, including mobile, digital video, social, programmatic, and data</li>
<li>Must have the ability to obtain and conduct face-to-face meetings with decision makers</li>
<li>Excellent written and oral communication skills</li>
<li>Great attitude and interest in learning new skills</li>
<li>Attention to detail</li>
<li>Need to be self-motivated</li>
<li>Possess a strong business acumen</li>
<li>Ability to thrive in an innovative environment</li>
<li>Drive, determination, consistency, grit and passion are always a plus!</li>
</ul>
</div>
