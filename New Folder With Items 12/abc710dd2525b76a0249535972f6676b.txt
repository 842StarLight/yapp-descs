<h2>Technical Support Engineer I (Linux Proficiency)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div><b>Your Role:</b></div>
<p>
Tenable is looking for motivated individuals to join our Technical Support Team. The Technical Support Engineer (TSE) is the first contact for all customers when they require technical assistance. In a time when the next Spectre and Meltdown may be announced to the public at any moment, TSEs have what it takes to thrive in a fast-paced, high stakes environment. TSEs are experts on all Tenable products in our goal to minimize Cyber Exposure, but you’ll also have experience in several technologies and a strong interest to learn more.</p>
<p><b>
Your Opportunity:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Build an in-depth knowledge of the entire Tenable product suite</li>
<li>
Resolve customer issues; help them understand their vulnerability scan results, their compliance with industry standards, and how Tenable can help them close their Cyber Exposure gap</li>
<li>
Diagnose, research, reproduce, and report defects, working closely with Research &amp; Development</li>
<li>
Engage customers in a variety of mediums, including email, phone, live chat, and screen sharing sessions</li>
<li>
Help maintain the Tenable knowledgebase, writing articles based on your first-hand experience helping customers mitigate the vulnerabilities that can impact all of our day-to-day lives</li>
<li>
Work closely and build relationships with the rest of the Tenable team, including Sales, Customer Advocacy, and Operations</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
What you'll need:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Bachelor’s degree in a technical field (or equivalent experience)</li>
<li>
1+ Years Customer Support / Helpdesk Experience</li>
<li>
Excellent verbal and written communication skills</li>
<li>
Robust problem-solving skills, the ability to learn from doing, personal accountability, and a positive and professional attitude</li>
<li>
In depth log analysis skills</li>
<li>
Experience and knowledge in the following areas:</li>
<ul>
<li>
Network administration, Firewall Configuration, and strong knowledge of TCP/IP</li>
<li>
Linux/Unix administration and system configuration</li>
<li>
Windows and/or Mac OS administration and system configuration</li>
<li>
Either professionally or in an academic setting, using Nessus and/or other vulnerability management or cyber security tools</li>
</ul>
</ul>
<b>
And ideally:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Log analysis using Splunk or another SIEM product</li>
<li>
Database technologies and SQL knowledge (Oracle, MySQL, MSSQL, etc.)</li>
<li>
Virtualization technology (VMWare, Hyper-V, Amazon AWS, Microsoft Azure, Docker, etc.)</li>
<li>
Basic programming knowledge</li>
<li>
Scripting in Bash, Python, Powershell, etc.</li>
<li>
Experience in Industry Security Standards (DISA, HIPAA, CIS, ISO 27001, etc)</li>
<li>
Certificates and CA’s</li>
<li>
Wireshark and pcaps</li>
<li>
Bilingual candidates are desirable but not required (Spanish, German, French, Mandarin, Japanese)</li>
</ul>
