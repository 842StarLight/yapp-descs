<p>Description</p>
</div>
<div>
<div>
<p>The Regional Operations Manager has full responsibility for their designated region of EDPR North America’s Operations and Maintenance (O&amp;M). You will define and build an efficient O&amp;M organization to meet the company’s growth targets and to ensure that the operating fleet maintains high availability and efficiency performance.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Main accountabilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Oversee selection, productivity, and professional development of all Operations personnel, including site managers, turbine specialists, and high-voltage specialists</li>
<li>Ensure optimum efficiency and maximum generation availability of all the company’s current and future wind farms</li>
<li>Ensure that contracts with service providers (e.g., turbine, high-voltage, balance-of-plant) are effectively managed on a day-to-day basis</li>
<li>Responsible for operational excellence programs, supplier management programs, the best practices manual, and the SOP manual</li>
<li>Manage site-level transitions from warranty to post-warranty service</li>
<li>Ensure that operating expenses stay within the approved budget</li>
<li>Define Key Performance Indicators (KPIs) for Operations personnel</li>
<li>Responsible for properly aligning key managers’ compensation with metrics and goals</li>
<li>Define and implement operational/metric programs to reach the continual company goal of 97% availability</li>
<li>Ensure that field personnel complete all daily activities safely and in compliance with industry and corporate standards</li>
<li>Demonstrate behaviors consistent with the Company’s values, Code of Ethics and Business Conduct.</li>
<li>Additional duties as required</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p><b>
</b></p>
<p><b>Direct reports: </b>6 – 9 direct reports; 60 – 80 indirect reports</p>
<p><b>
Travel: </b>As needed</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Minimum qualifications:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree in Engineering or an equivalent combination of technical training and experience</li>
<li>12 – 15 years of progressively responsible operational experience, including 5 years of managerial experience</li>
<li>Considerable knowledge of Six Sigma and other quality management processes</li>
<li>Experience managing a geographically diverse manufacturing operation with minimum staff of 75 (distributed power production preferred)</li>
<li>Experience with both direct and contract manufacturing preferred</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p>
</p>
<p><b>Behavioral requirements:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Strong written and verbal communication along with presentation skills</li>
<li>Strong organizational and high attention to detail</li>
<li>Strong quantitative and analytical skills including use of PC software such as Excel to perform analyses</li>
<li>Demonstrated ability to lead cross-functional, multi-departmental teams</li>
<li>Self-motivated and able to work effectively with minimal direction in both structured and unstructured work environments</li>
<li>Ability to lead, delegate, and set goals and targets</li>
<li>Ability to maintain and enhance customer relationships</li>
<li>Ability to lead and support complex negotiations</li>
<li>Ability to multi-task and prioritize effectively in a fast-paced, dynamic environment</li>
<li>Ability to foster and help create a collaborative working environment</li>
<li>Proven leadership skills</li>
<br>
</ul>

</b></p>

<b>Speech/Reading:</b> Ability to speak, read, and write English proficiently and deliver a variety of instructions furnished in written, verbal, diagram, or schedule form</li>
<li>
<b>Lifting:</b> Ability to lift items weighing up to 10 pounds</li>
<li>
