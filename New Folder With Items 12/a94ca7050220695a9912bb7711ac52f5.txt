<p>POSITION SUMMARY
</p>
<p>The Enterprise Account Executive is a ‘hunter’ position, prospecting to identify and develop new customer relationships to meet or exceed the stated sales goals of the company. The territory for the Texas based Account Executive includes: Texas, Louisiana, Oklahoma, and Arkansas. Focused on the Enterprise segment (defined as 201+ employees), solutions revolve around Managed Print Services, Hardware Maintenance Services, Managed IT, and IT related projects.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p>KEY DUTIES/RESPONSIBILITIES
</p>
<p><b>Customer Related
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Make necessary contact and communication (written and oral) to ensure customer satisfaction and maximize customer retention
</li>
<li>Perform large account status meetings as required in a thorough and timely manner
</li>
<li>Work with customer to sign up add-on maintenance and maintain accurate lists of equipment under service
</li>
<li>Forward new product opportunities to management for evaluation
</li>
<li>Request sales leads via customer referrals
</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Prospect Related
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Initiate contact via telephone, written correspondence, and/or personal visits to begin the sales process
</li>
<li>Utilize SPIN Selling techniques during face to face sales calls to identify the customer needs
</li>
<li>Prepare thorough, professional, and timely proposals to qualified prospects
</li>
<li>Follow up as required to move prospects through the sales process
</li>
<li>Close the sale
</li>
<li>Request additional sales leads via referral
</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Business Partner Related
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Maintain regular contact with business partners in a specified geographic area
</li>
<li>Work with the appropriate business partner coordinator to foster additional business and maximize our business partner relationships
</li>
<li>Identify and forward qualified dealer leads to the appropriate dealer coordinator
</li>
</ul>
<p><b>General
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Update and maintain our sales database according to our established procedures
</li>
<li>Complete prescribed sales forecasts, travel schedules, large account reports, new equipment approvals, and other designated activities in a timely and thorough manner
</li>
<li>Participate in sales training and sales meetings as necessary
</li>
<li>Demonstrate good judgment pertaining to expense control
</li>
<li>Use good judgment and care to maintain company assets in your possession. These assets may include: vehicles, computers, price books, as well as other company provides resources
</li>
<li>Attend trade shows and conferences as necessary
</li>
<li>Meet all attendance requirements.
</li>
<li>Work with all facets of the organization to help meet our defined corporate objectives.<br>
</li>
</ul>
