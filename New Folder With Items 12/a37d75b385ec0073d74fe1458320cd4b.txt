<h2>VP, North America E-Commerce</h2>

<div>
<p>America’s oldest and most iconic clothier and retailer, Brooks Brothers started in 1818 as a gentlemen’s furnishings and haberdashery shop in New York City. For more than 200 years, we have defined the standard of dress in the United States, evolving into a global lifestyle brand that offers complete apparel and accessories collections for men, women and children. In 1915 our present ten-story flagship store opened at 346 Madison Avenue near New York’s most prominent social organizations, including the Harvard and Yale Clubs and the New York Yacht Club. Renowned the world over for innovation, quality, timeless style and value, Brooks Brothers proudly crafts many items in our own factories in the USA.<br>
 Brooks Brothers has an established history of building long-term relationships with our Customers and our Associates. With more than 250 stores throughout the United States and Canada, and more than 650 locations in 42 countries worldwide, we continue to grow with a respect for our heritage and a strong vision for the future.</p>
<p>
The diversity of our operations has recently created an opportunity for a new VP, North America E-commerce in our New York offices.</p>
<br>
<p>
<br>
The VP, North America E- commerce will drive customer acquisition strategies via the marketing funnel and digital marketing services. You will lead a growing marketing team of experts focused on awareness, acquisition, activation, retention and revenue through paid and owned marketing channels. In this role, you will help with strategy, optimizations and overall management of all major media channels. You will manage strategic and creative development of digital marketing programs, media planning, and implementation of our digital advertising and direct response initiatives for our DTC business. You’re the right person for the job if you’re curious &amp; optimistic, consumer-first, equally analytical and entrepreneurial, and if you are scrappy, flexible and solutions-oriented in a fast-paced environment.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
</div>

<p><b>Key Accountabilities:</b></p>
<p>
⁻ Daily analysis of online sales, trends and web KPIs to optimize site performance and create actionable insights for implementation<br>

⁻ Develop traffic acquisition &amp; search strategy (SEM, SEO, affiliation, paid social, display, Programmatic) and retention programs (email campaign, remarketing) in collaboration with HQ Digital Marketing Teams<br>

⁻ Propose and optimize customer-centric operations and new services to increase sales performance and reduce contact center inbound call volumes<br>

⁻ Develop wholistic customer engagement strategy, partnering with Stores, Brand Marketing and CRM teams, with consistent online and offline strategy with a 360° leveraging all touchpoints (website, social media, in-store clienteling, point of sale, brand ambassadors, etc)</p>
