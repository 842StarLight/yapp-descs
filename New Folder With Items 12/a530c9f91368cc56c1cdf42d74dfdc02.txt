<h2>Chief Marketing Officer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">LogicGate is a venture-backed, Chicago-based technology company on a mission.
<br><br>
We believe easy-to-use, flexible technology can alter the trajectory of an organization and improve the lives of its employees. It’s this very spirit that drives us to build and deliver a SaaS platform that brings unprecedented clarity, collaboration, and accountability to our clients’ governance, risk, and compliance (GRC) processes. Join our fast-growing, passionate team of innovators as we continue to disrupt the multi-billion-dollar GRC industry.

We work with mid-market organizations and large enterprises in all verticals, especially Financial Services, Technology / Software and Healthcare, with a focus on their GRC activities. This is a rare opportunity to play a key role in building a category-leading SaaS business in a multi-billion dollar industry, at a company who has already established industry leading results.

<ul>

<li>Partner with Sales Team to define, coordinate, and improve marketing programs for demand creation and lead generation</li>
<li>Refine lead-tracking and management process</li>
<li>Manage all aspects of marketing planning, budgeting, KPIs / metrics, and reporting</li>
<li>Implement and leverage the latest marketing tools and technologies</li>
<li>Manage external agencies and relationship with marketing services</li>
<li>Develop and maintain long-term relationship with industry thought-leaders and research analysts</li>
<li>Develop and execute on a best in class SEO/SEM strategy</li>
</ul>
