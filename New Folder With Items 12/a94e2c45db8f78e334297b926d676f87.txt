<div><b>About us:</b></div>
<div>
Grand Rounds is a new kind of healthcare company. Founded in 2011, the company is on a mission to raise the standard of healthcare for everyone, everywhere. The Grand Rounds team goes above and beyond to connect and guide people to the highest quality healthcare available for themselves and their loved ones. Grand Rounds creates products and services that give people the best possible healthcare experience. Named a 2019 Best Place to Work by Glassdoor and Rock Health’s 2018 Fastest Growing Company, Grand Rounds works with inspiring employers and doctors to empower them to be the change agents we need to make our shared vision a reality.
</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div><b>The Role</b></div>
<div><b>
</b></div>
<div>
Our Large Enterprise Sales Directors help us further our mission by selling the Grand Rounds’ suite of products to enterprise customers (5,000 - 25,000 employee population), so these large employers can offer a world class healthcare experience to their employees and employees’ family members. As an Enterprise Sales Executive, your role will be to identify, pursue and close business with self-funded employers, within your territory, having 5000+ employees. Through a consultative approach, you will work both directly with senior Compensation &amp; Benefits / Total Rewards leadership as well as with their trusted advisors and consultants.</div>
</div>
<div><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>During your first 30 days, you will:</b></h3>
<div><ul>
<li>
Onboard with the Grand Rounds team in San Francisco, and engage in internal learning</li>
<li>
Quickly develop an understanding of our products and become highly knowledgeable with our offerings</li>
<li>
Partner with appropriate Grand Rounds Market Lead to come up to speed quickly on respective market dynamics, targets and opportunities</li>
<li>
Begin developing Year 1 Sales plan</li>
<li>
Tap your strong network of buyers or influencers to identify major sales opportunities</li>
<li>
Ride along with Market Lead on Sales calls for learning purposes</li>
</ul></div>
</div></div>
