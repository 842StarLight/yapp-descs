<div>
<div>Position Overview
</div>
<div><p>This position is responsible for performing routine and novel analyses of compensation-related information from internal and external sources. Will assist in the development and administration of variable compensation programs (e.g., incentive awards, sales commissions, base pay, stock award analyses, and payment and award distribution, etc.). In addition, incumbent will audit jobs for content and responsibilities, activities, duties and requirements to assess appropriate benchmark pay level for the position. Will develop and/or participate in compensation surveys to collect and analyze competitive pay information for positions throughout the organization.
</p></div>
</div>
<div>
<div>Responsibilities
</div>
<div><ul>
<li>Conduct analysis of jobs, salaries, and incentives in order to evaluate internal equity, external competitiveness, and legal compliance of the organization’s pay practices.
</li>
<li>Lead the submission of internal compensation data to survey companies and perform periodic reviews of compensation market data to develop market reference points.
</li>
<li>Propose changes to salary structures based on market movement and cost analysis for the US and additional countries as necessary.
</li>
<li>Supports the review and development of job hierarchies and pay structures for base and variable pay.
</li>
<li>Perform relative job positioning and pay comparisons for M&amp;A events.
</li>
<li>Design, implementation and administration of compensation programs including: base pay structures, short-term incentive programs, and equity. Support the annual merit and equity grant nomination process.
</li>
<li>Calculate, analyze and distribute corporate incentive bonuses and sales commission payments.
</li>
<li>Assists in the development of compensation training programs (base and variable) and communications at all employee levels
</li>
<li>Leverage relationships with HR business partners/leaders to understand business issues and propose resolutions related to compensation. Train business partners/managers on compensation programs and processes.
</li>
<li>Act as a resource to Business Partners and Managers on compensation policy and practice.
</li>
<li>Assists Manager with special ad hoc compensation analysis and special projects.
</li>
</ul></div>
</div>
