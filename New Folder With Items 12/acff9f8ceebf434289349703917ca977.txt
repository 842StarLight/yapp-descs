<p><b>Job Title:</b></p>
<p><b>Accounts Receivable Specialist – Cash Application</b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Key Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul><li> Download customer payment information from banks.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Apply cash receipts against customer invoices.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Research improperly documented payments for appropriate customer and invoice applications.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Process misc credits and debits against customer invoices.</li></ul>
<ul><li> File departmental documents.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Prepare and update monthly status reports on various topics (such as unassigned cash and bank fees &amp; charges)</li></ul>
<ul><li> Prepare and respond to customer calls and requests related to cash receipts, account statements, proof of delivery and cash application.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Prepare requests to refund customer as appropriate.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Address invoices requiring special handling.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Create manual invoices for progress billed customers.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Process credit card payments, billings, and receipt issuance.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Release cash holds.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Follow up on consignment orders to assure collection and correct cash application.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Cover customer credit department.</li></ul>
<p>o New customer setup including credit checks.</p>
<p>o Release cash holds.</p>
<p>o Receive returned invoices – distribute to required sales team for resolution and chart reason for return to initiate corrective action.</p>
<ul><li> Various other clerical and administrative functions as assigned by Accounting Manager.</li></ul>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Requirements:</b></p>
<ul><li> Strong organizational, communication and computer skills.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Self-motivated and be able to drive processes with minimal external help.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Knowledgeable in the use or Oracle or similar software.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Knowledgeable in the use of Microsoft Word, Excel and Outlook.</li></ul>
