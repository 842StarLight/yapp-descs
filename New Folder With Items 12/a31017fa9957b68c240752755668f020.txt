<p><b>You are.</b></p>

<p></p>
<p>Employee-owners have made Cenlar one of the nation’s largest mortgage subservicers. We have achieved success by empowering people with company ownership, real programs that provide avenues for advancement, and a great atmosphere that makes everyone look forward to the workday. Get your share of our success by considering the opportunity to join our team as a Satisfaction Research Specialist.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
<div>Recover missing recording information utilizing public records, online resources, and established contacts.</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Interact effectively with clients, title companies, attorneys, mortgagors, and county recorders to obtain necessary information for satisfaction completion.</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Update tracking system with all research information and results.</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Prepare and send correspondence to inform appropriate individuals of satisfaction status.</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Act swiftly in resolving all inquiries in order to avoid penalty situations.</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Maintain ongoing communication with callers, keeping them informed of satisfaction status progress.</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Keep abreast of private label clients in order to prepare proper logo correspondence.</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Complete penalty research and follow up with attorneys in a timely fashion.</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Complete uniform commercial code satisfaction requirements</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Develop rapport with county clerks’ offices in order to obtain mortgage recording information that is not accessible online.</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Prepare necessary documents to facilitate a pre-foreclosure sales satisfaction.</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Follow standard procedures when informing the Veterans Administration of certificate cancellation.</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Follow state requirements and policies and procedures when completing notifications and releases to various entities, e.g. the Texas Land Board, Virgin Island releases.</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Assist in the continuing documentation of work policies and procedures.</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Complete all task items within the standard timeline.</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Other duties and projects as assigned.</div>
<br>
</li>
</ul>
