<h2>Real Estate Administrative Assistant</h2>

<p>About Us</p>
<p>
Block &amp; Company, Inc., is a full-service commercial real estate organization in Kansas City and throughout the Midwest region. We pride ourselves on excellent customer service in commercial real estate and have been in business since 1946. We are committed to redefining innovation in the real estate arena and always apply our business philosophy of integrity and teamwork to achieve organizational success.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
Overview</p>
<p>
Block &amp; Co. Inc. is currently seeking a Real Estate Administrative Assistant to join our team. This position will be responsible for supporting and performing administrative functions on behalf of the Operations team.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
Essential Functions &amp; Responsibilities</p>
<ul>
<li>
Performs general administrative duties such as filing, typing, conducting research, and scheduling appointments.</li>
<li>
Monitors key dates related to Purchase and Sale Agreements/Contracts, Managed Leases and notifying clients and agents accordingly.</li>
<li>
Manages and reviews calendar entries, meetings, and details related to daily schedules for you and your brokers.</li>
<li>
Prepares leases, sales agreements, listing agreements, letters of intent, proposals, correspondence and other contract documents.</li>
<li>
Prepares listing paperwork and other documents, within the KCRAR platform for both the Missouri and Kansas state sides.</li>
<li>
Coordinates with lenders, insurance agents, escrow and title companies.</li>
<li>
Creates listing and marketing materials.</li>
<li>
Answers inquiries from clients and agents and provides clerical support to agents during lease and sale closings.</li>
<li>
Performs general clerical duties to include answering phones, scanning, filing, and mailing documents for the department and agent.</li>
<li>
Processes new commercial real estate property listings and monitors listing inquiries.
</li>
<li>Schedules appointments for commercial real estate viewings on behalf of agents.</li>
<li>
Provides technical support in administrating Block &amp; Company's CRM database.
</li>
<li>Generates status reports on all commercial property listings and provides to agents.</li>
<li>
Other duties as assigned.</li>
</ul>
<p>
Supervisory Responsibilities</p>
<p>
This position has no supervisory responsibility.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
Placement Criteria</p>
<p>
High School Degree or GED required. A minimum of 3 years of administrative experience working in commercial real estate or professional services environment preferred.</p>
<ul>
<li>
Ability to prioritize workflow, organize diverse material and handle multiple, competing and changing priorities.</li>
<li>
Capacity to perform duties effectively without supervision and within established timelines.</li>
<li>
Ability to interact and communicate effectively, and provide exceptional service, both internally and externally.</li>
<li>
Proficient computer skills including Microsoft Office.</li>
<li>
Display adaptability in learning the use of company specific software and comprehension of fundamental computer practices including database management.</li>
<li>
Exhibit exemplary customer service skills, with external customers, vendors, visitors, co-workers, and management staff.
</li>
<li>Excellent written and verbal communication.
</li>
<li>Exercise confidentiality concerning business affairs.
</li>
<li>Exhibit a willingness to learn, problem solve and support the team.<br>
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p>Physical Demands</p>
<p>
Ability to bend, stoop, reach, stand, move from one area of the building to another on a regular basis, sit and use a computer for a long period of time. Manual and physical dexterity needed to operate a computer keyboard and handle paper documents. Sufficient near vision acuity to read information appearing on computer display screen, in hand-written forms, and printed on paper. Adequate hearing and verbal abilities to communicate effectively in person and by telephone. Ability to lift and carry items weighing up to 15 pounds.</p>
