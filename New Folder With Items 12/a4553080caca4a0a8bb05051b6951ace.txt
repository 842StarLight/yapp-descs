<p><b>General Summary and Purpose:</b></p>
<p>
The Western Region Business Development Director is a member of the sales team. This position is field-based, working from a home office. This is a relationship and consultative selling-based position, with success defined by how well the team member leverages contacts and scientific data to accelerate the evaluation, validation, and adoption of our proprietary biopreservation media products in the acquisition, storage, manufacture, distribution and administration of cell and gene therapies.</p>
<p><b>
Duties and Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Extensive travel to develop, cultivate and maintain relationships with prospective and current customers.</li>
<li>Attendance and presentations at regional and national trade shows and scientific conferences.</li>
<li>Leveraging relationships to introduce members of our scientific team to key decision makers.</li>
<li>Driving and managing product evaluations from initial qualification to adoption and execution of supply agreements.</li>
<li>Regular weekly reporting of field activities and follow up.</li>
</ul>
