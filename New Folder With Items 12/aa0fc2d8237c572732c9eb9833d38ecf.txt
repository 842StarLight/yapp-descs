<h2>General Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<b>Position Title:</b> General Manager
<br><br>
<b>Department:</b> Entire Restaurant Accountable To: Corporate Team
<br><br>
The General Manager is responsible for all daily operations in the entire restaurant. They must demonstrate strong communication skills and leadership abilities, as well as maintain a safe and sanitary work environment.. The General Manager is responsible for the development of new and current team members, while upholding all service standards and ensuring all food and beverage is served according to standards.
<br><br>
In addition, they must coach team member’s performance – remaining friendly and flexible—resulting in the best guest experience possible.
<br><br>
Specifically, the General Manager ensures consistent execution of both FOH and BOH systems and profitability by providing and ensuring quality training, coaching and follow-through. This position is ultimately accountable for store profitability, labor cost controls, service standards, systems, organization, team member engagement and the guest experience.
<br>The General Manager reports directly to the Regional Operations Manager.
<br><br>
<b>Owning Your Program:</b><br>
 Step 1 is to understand that you are responsible for ensuring that everything is done on time and accurately.
<br><ul>
<li>This doesn’t mean that you have to do everything. What this means is that you have to ensure that everything is done.</li>
</ul>
 Step 2 is knowing what those things are
<br> Step 3 is determining how those things will get done. Delegation
<br> Step 4 is follow up or accountability. Inspecting what you expect
<br> Step 5 is teaching others to do the tasks you are delegating.
<br>Key Performance Elements/Essential Functions
<br> Strong understanding of all FOH and BOH systems and operations; must demonstrate the ability to run effective shifts in all aspects of the restaurant.
<br> Provides direction, coaching, and leadership for all staff including safety and sanitation, and company policies and procedures.
<br> Responsible for establishing and appropriately communicating goals to the Corporate Team on a regular basis.
<br> Communicates effectively with the Management Team.
<br> Ensures the Management Team’s continued development and focus on creating and maintaining a strong guest first focus.
<br> Effectively maintains the restaurant’s facility, both exterior and interior, to Twigs’ standards (clean, neat, and organized) and ensures sufficient supply levels of tools available for all employees to execute their responsibilities.
<br> Ensures completion and posting of the FOH schedule is done, effectively maintaining labor goals and guest satisfaction. The schedule should be planned using forecasted sales and labor budget, while taking team member availabilities, requests and vacations into consideration.
<br> Leads the staff in a positive manner as to achieve our desired result; being 100% guest satisfaction.
<br> Ensures 100% effectiveness of both FOH and BOH systems and standards.
<br> Ensures execution of company standards for each Guest from the front doors to the table.
<br> Ensures all marketing materials are current throughout the restaurant.
<br> Ensures that dress code standards are 100%.
<br> Communicates effectively with vendors.
<br> Attracts patrons by developing local store marketing, advertising, public and community relations programs; evaluating program results; identifying and tracking changing demands.
<br> Hiring of all FOH positions, maintaining appropriate staffing par levels.
<br> Assists in hiring of all BOH positions, maintaining appropriate staffing par levels.
<br> Completes all Team Members reviews once a year.
<br> Holds regular meetings, including workshops, recognizing what we do well and what we can improve (safety, cost controls, quality and execution).
<br> Weekly and monthly inventories.
<br> Responsible for meeting the restaurant’s financial goals, including sales growth (by providing exceptional guest service 100% of the time), labor, hiring, developing and retaining the very best team members.
<br> Implements and manages plans that result in improved financial (sales and cost centers) performance regarding all operations.
<br> Manage restaurant P&amp;L to optimize manageable profit, control COGS, Labor, and Controllables.
<br> Maintains a strong FOH sales focus, coaching team members throughout and after the shift.
<br> The ability to meet financial goals, including sales targets and well as budgeting and cost control measures.
<br> Timely completion of all financial obligations and reports. Including payroll, payables, receivable etc.
<br> Responsible for the management staff and their duties.
<br> Arrange for R&amp;M of equipment and other services.
<br> Other duties as necessary for successful and efficient operations
<br><br>
Other
<br>Regular attendance in conformance with the standards, which may be established and or altered by Twigs Bistro and Martini Bar from time to time, is essential to the successful performance of this position. Employees with irregular attendance will be subject to disciplinary action, up to and including termination of employment.
<br><br>
Due to the cyclical nature of the hospitality industry, employees may be required to work varying schedules to reflect the business needs of the restaurant.
<br><br>
Upon employment, all employees are required to fully comply with the Twigs Bistro and Martini Bar rules and regulations for the safe and efficient operation of our properties. Employees who violate the Twigs Bistro and Martini Bar rules and regulations will be subject to disciplinary action, up to and including termination of employment.
<br><br>
Qualification Standards
<br><br>
<b>Education:</b><br>
Any combination of education and experience equivalent that provides the required knowledge, skills, and abilities. High school education is preferred.
<br><br>
<b>Experience:</b><br>
Minimum three (3) years full service restaurant experience required. Prior supervisory experience preferred.
<br><br>
<b>Licenses or Certificates:</b><br>
Ability to obtain any government required licenses or certificates. For example: Local health department certification and state liquor control board certificate. CPR certification and/or first aid training preferred.
<br><br>
<b>Grooming:</b><br>
All employees must maintain a neat, clean, and well groomed appearance (specific standards available in our employee training manual)
