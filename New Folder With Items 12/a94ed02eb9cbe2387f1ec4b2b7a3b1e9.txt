<p><b>Lot Attendant</b></p>
<p>The lot attendant is responsible for the overall management of our new and used inventory in our lots.</p>
<p><b>Responsibilities include: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Maintaining and organizing inventory</li>
<li>Working with sales to stock in and move vehicles, making room for new inventory on the lot.</li>
<li>Managing for all new and used inventory accessories such as books, keys and codes.</li>
<li>Taking pictures of vehicles on the lot and advertising them on website-Keep dealership lot clean and organized</li>
<li>Working in various weather climates/conditions outside.</li>
<li>Other responsibilities may include moving vehicles for snow removal.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Skills &amp; Qualifications: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Valid driver's license and clear driving record.</li>
<li>Organizational skills and ability to multi-task and meet deadlines.</li>
<li>Ability to work outside in warm/cold weather</li>
<li>Ability to do some heavy lifting (up to 40lbs)</li>
</ul>
