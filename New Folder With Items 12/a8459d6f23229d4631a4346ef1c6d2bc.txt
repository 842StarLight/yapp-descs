<b>Position Responsibilities</b><ul>
<li>
Design, develop and implement automated testing and tooling solutions with a primary focus on automated functional and regression testing for projects and applications.</li>
<li>
Create, extend and maintain test automation libraries and frameworks.</li>
<li>
Provide test automation estimates and timelines for assigned projects.</li>
<li>
Create and maintain data sets to be used in test automation activities.</li>
<li>
Coordinate and manage automated testing efforts across concurrent projects.</li>
<li>
Ensure that test results are documented, evaluated and disseminated; collaborate on troubleshooting issues.</li>
<li>
Incorporate test automation into CI/CD process.</li>
<li>
Communicate and report on status of test automation activities.</li>
<li>
Collaborate with QA manager and team to develop, implement and improve innovative QA capabilities, approaches and services.
</li>
<li>Help bring awareness to the test automation function throughout IT and the business, communicating applicable testing practices and expectations.</li>
</ul>
