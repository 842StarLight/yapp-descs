<h2>Analyst II - Inventory</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<b>Carpenter's Latrobe Specialty Metals operations, based in Latrobe, Pa.,</b> manufacture high-performance specialty alloys serving aerospace, energy and other demanding markets. Carpenter acquired Latrobe in 2012 to increase capacity to meet strong customer demand and to broaden the company's product mix. Latrobe employs more than 1,000 dedicated production, maintenance and salaried staff and continues a century-long legacy of leadership in metals technology. Latrobe is part of the corporation's Specialty Alloys Operations division.<br>
<br>
<b>Analyst II - Inventory
</b><br>
<br>
<b>PRIMARY DUTIES</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Evaluate and execute planning system recommended order management transactions.</li>
<li>
Manage volume, turns and safety stock levels to optimize inventory utilization in support of Corporate objectives.
</li>
<li>Provide material alternatives to customers through the application of slow moving and non-standard inventory.</li>
<li>
Evaluate and manage forecasts for semi-finished and finished materials.</li>
<li>
Consolidate and verify order requirements and make assignment switches in conjunction with technical personnel.
</li>
<li>Monitor and evaluate planning systems to identify and resolve action messages. Analyze off quality/dormant inventory/over production and returned material.
</li>
<li>Develop procedures in concert with Manufacturing, Sales, and Technical to reduce surplus materials.
</li>
<li>Represents inventory group on cross-functional projects and manages Inventory-specific subsets of these projects.</li>
<li>
Perform all other duties and special projects as assigned.</li>
</ul>
<br>
<b>
REQUIREMENTS</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Two year college degree or demonstrated equivalent experience as determined by department management.</li>
<li>
Minimum three years of experience in a production, scheduling, and planning environment, manufacturing or technical process environment preferred.</li>
<li>
SAP knowledge, a plus</li>
<li>
Knowledge of problem solving techniques.</li>
<li>
Knowledge of statistical methodologies and techniques.</li>
<li>
Knowledge of various computer software packages for word processing, statistical analysis etc.</li>
<li>
Ability to use appropriate technical knowledge to solve material application problems.</li>
<li>
Ability to operate a computer workstation.</li>
<li>
Ability to organize and prioritize tasks and make appropriate decisions.</li>
<li>
Ability to manage larger, more complex projects.</li>
<li>
Ability to coordinate activities across multiple disciplines.</li>
<li>
Ability to provide proactive advice and direction to customers pertaining to material alternatives.</li>
<li>
Ability to utilize and analyze data and follow a process.</li>
<li>
Ability to work in a production oriented environment.</li>
<li>
Verbal and written communication skills.</li>
</ul>
