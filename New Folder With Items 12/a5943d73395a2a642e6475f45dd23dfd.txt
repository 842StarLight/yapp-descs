<h2>Inside Channel Account Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>About the Position
</b></p>
<p>The National Inside Channel Account Manager (ICAM) responsibilities include working within the sales team to promote and sell network security products within a select group of National Accounts. The ICAM is responsible for supporting assigned WatchGuard partners and their customers. The ICAM will be responsible for: quoting, training, follow-up, event support, and the forecasting of their assigned partners. The ICAM will report to the Director of National Partners and will work closely with the National Channel Account Manager (NCAM) to assure territory quota attainment, in addition to joining with the sales and field marketing teams in the territory. Success in this position will be measured by: meeting successful metrics, sales conversions, and account growth with the assigned partners. The ICAM will keep up-to-date knowledge of the industry and the competitive posture of the company, as well as prepare activity and forecast reports as requested.</p>
<p><b>
Position Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Strive towards the quota attainment and growth/development of National Partners</li>
<li>Assist National Partner’s Sales Teams with: product information, opportunity management, and customer calls</li>
<li>Partner with National Channel Account Managers to generate new business and increase current business through the specified partners</li>
<li>Support partners efforts of selling WatchGuard including: training, event support, and lead generation</li>
<li>Create and perform sales presentations to match WatchGuard with audience needs</li>
<li>Assist with management of National Partner pipeline of sales opportunities</li>
<li>Conduct user discovery calls and follow up</li>
<li>Be knowledgeable of all WatchGuard products, services, and internal sales tools</li>
<li>Other duties and projects assigned</li>
</ul>
