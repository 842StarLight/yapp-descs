<h2>Supply Chain Analyst Buyer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>The Materials Management Department is seeking a<b> Supply Chain Analyst Buyer </b>for their team.</p>
<p>
Our <b>Supply Chain Analyst</b> <b>Buyer </b>position functions as an integral member of the Materials Management team to provide data analysis to assist all functions of the company’s supply chain.</p>
<ul>
<li>
Supports contract negotiations by providing data analytics, monitoring contract compliance, analyzing contract utilizations and formulating recommendations.</li>
<li>
Perform advanced level buying tasks to support Request for proposal.</li>
<li>
Ensures accuracy of reported information and the date integrity of supply chain systems.</li>
<li>
Places orders with vendors and follows up.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p>
Preferred Experience and Credentials:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Bachelor’s Degree in related field</li>
<li>
Minimum of 3-5 years Pricing analysis of finished goods comparing like and different Unified Ordering Model (UOMs ), experience with report writing software, experience using and inventory control system and maintaining master files, purchasing, and inventory management.</li>
<li>
Pharmacy Tech license/experience preferred</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Schedule: 40 hours a week, with specific days and times to be determined.</b></p>
