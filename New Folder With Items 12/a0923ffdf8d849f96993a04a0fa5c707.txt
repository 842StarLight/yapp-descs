<h2>Restaurant Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Bavarian Bistro and Bar seeks a talented, detail-oriented, and hardworking Manager to join our established and dynamic team in Leavenworth. The ideal candidate has a strong background in hospitality management and customer service. Experience with scheduling 30+ employees, bar management, and managing multiple styles of service are a plus. Must have people skills and have full weekend availability. This is a Full-Time job that requires a minimum 40-hour work week, and strives to provide a great work-life balance.</p>
<p><b>Position Duties and Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Manage and oversee the entire restaurant operation</li>
<li>Deliver superior guest service with an eye for detail and care for people</li>
<li>Plan new and update existing menus</li>
<li>Plan and develop the overall restaurant marketing strategy</li>
<li>Participate at local food events</li>
<li>Respond efficiently to customer questions and complaints</li>
<li>Organize and supervise shifts</li>
<li>Manage and lead staff</li>
<li>Hire new employees</li>
<li>Train and evaluate staff performance</li>
<li>Estimate forecast requirements and maintain food and beverage inventory</li>
<li>Manage restaurant supplies and control costs to minimize waste</li>
<li>Oversee bar operations</li>
<li>Nurture a positive working environment with an “Open Door” policy</li>
<li>Monitor operations and initiate corrective actions among staff</li>
<li>Implement strategies to improve productivity and sales</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Position Requirements and Preferred Qualifications</b></p>
<ul>
<li>2+ years of experience as a Restaurant Manager or similar role</li>
<li>2+ years of experience customer service management</li>
<li>Healthy food, wine, and spirits knowledge</li>
<li>Computer literacy, especially in Excel, Word, and other design programs a plus</li>
<li>Familiarity with restaurant management software and POS systems</li>
<li>Strong leadership, motivational and people skills</li>
<li>Good financial management skills</li>
<li>Time management skills</li>
<li>Critical thinking and problem-solving skills</li>
<li>Team player</li>
<li>Strong interpersonal and communication skills</li>
</ul>
<p>Salary is $50,000+ per year plus paid time off and bi-yearly bonus structure, with a sixty-day review. Please reply with a resume for consideration.</p>
