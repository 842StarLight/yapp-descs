Job Summary: Loss Prevention / Security is responsible for protecting H-E-B assets by preventing theft.<br>
<br>
Essential Functions / Process Responsibilities include the following; other duties may be assigned as necessary<br>
<ul>
<li>Detects and resolves external theft by identifying, investigating, and resolving theft incidents</li>
<li>Conducts surveillance, detains, and interviews persons apprehended in shoplifting incidents</li>
<li>Recovers assets and / or makes safe apprehensions</li>
<li>Responsible for writing and documenting the case reports for theft incidents and weekly activity reports</li>
<li>Educates store Partners and management on Loss Prevention-related matters to improve theft awareness and maintain a safe store environment</li>
<li>Conducts inspections of physical security standards</li>
<li>Learns advanced interviewing techniques</li>
</ul>
<br>
Preferred Education and Experience<br>
<ul><li>High school diploma or equivalent</li></ul>
- 1 - 2 years of experience in investigative auditing and / or retail Loss Prevention<br>
<ul>
<li>Proficiency in MS Office and computer software programs / applications required by the job</li>
<li>General knowledge of video systems</li>
<li>Working knowledge and experience in investigative techniques, including good interviewing skills</li>
<li>Must have a valid driver's license</li>
</ul>
Preferred Key Competencies<br>
<ul>
<li>Able to multi-task with attention to detail</li>
<li>Excellent written and verbal communication skills</li>
<li>Bilingual language skills a plus</li>
<li>Able to work with highly confidential information</li>
<li>Able to confront individuals in a calm and diplomatic manner</li>
<li>Able to work in a team environment</li>
<li>Moderate PC skills, including MS Office software (Word, Excel, PowerPoint, Access, Outlook) and computer software programs / applications required by the job</li>
</ul>
Physical and Other Requirements<br>
<ul>
<li>Function in a fast-paced, retail, office environment</li>
<li>Travel by car or airplane with overnight stays</li>
<li>Lift up to 20 lbs in a regular basis</li>
<li>Work extended hours</li>
<li>Work rotating schedules</li>
<li>Must have reliable source of transportation</li>
<li>Able to access and move around all areas of the store quickly</li>
<li>Able to make theft apprehensions</li>
<li>Able to conduct visual surveillance of the store</li>
<li>Must have a valid driver's license</li>
</ul>
