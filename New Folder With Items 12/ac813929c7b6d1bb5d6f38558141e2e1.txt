<h2>Director of Digital Marketing</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>The director of digital marketing is charged with building, supporting, and optimizing the organization’s digital marketing programs by driving its digital narrative, enhancing its image, improving digital communication with clients, and realizing business growth through effective campaigns in all applicable markets. He/she works closely with all departments to ensure consistency through all marketing channels and achieve business growth objectives.</p>
<p>
Read the entire job description here.</p>
<p><b>
ESSENTIAL DUTIES &amp; RESPONSIBILITIES:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Plan and execute all digital marketing efforts, including SEO/SEM; websites; blogs; email; social media, and display advertising campaigns.</li>
<li>
Create and manage electronic mailing lists and marketing database.</li>
<li>
Design, build, and maintain the company’s social media presence across all digital channels.</li>
<li>
Measure and report performance of all marketing efforts and assess against goals (ROI and KPIs).
</li>
<li>Create and manage an annual department budget that optimizes spending and performance based on identified trends and insights.</li>
<li>
Plan, execute, and measure experiments and conversions tests.</li>
<li>
Evaluate end-to-end customer experience across multiple channels and customer touch points.</li>
<li>
Identify, engage, and collaborate with agencies and other vendors or partners as needed.</li>
<li>
Negotiate sponsorship agreements as appropriate.</li>
<li>
Conduct (or cause to be conducted) regular competitive research on other products and companies in the industry to inform strategic planning and provide ideas for unique product positioning.</li>
<li>
Inform internal teams about customer behaviors and buying preferences/patterns. Collaborate to increase client satisfaction through cohesive strategies.</li>
<li>
Conduct (or cause to be conducted) regular buyer journey research, instrument conversion points, and help to optimize website, marketing touch points, and sales approach accordingly.</li>
<li>
In concert with the leadership, work to position the company as the informed opinion leader and the go-to people in the field of Christian secondary online learning.</li>
<li>
Evaluate emerging technologies and to provide thought leadership and perspective for adoption where appropriate.</li>
<li>
Management and oversight of all marketing personnel and functions.</li>
</ul>
