<h2>Territory Manager I-Columbia</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>Millennium Health is a leading health solutions company that delivers timely, accurate, clinically actionable information to inform the right treatment decisions for each patient at the right time. Due to our continued success, we are looking to hire a <b>Territory Manager (territory sales)</b> in the Columbia market.<b> </b>This is a great opportunity for a seasoned medical professional with 5+ years of documented medical sales success. This position is responsible for building sample volumes in the assigned territory through ongoing account management and development of new customer accounts. This is a 100% remote position and will require a home office. Essential office equipment will be provided by the company. The position will require extensive travel by car as well as some air travel. A safe driving record is mandatory.</p>
<br>
Required Skills<br>
<p>Required skills include:</p>
<ul>
<li>Meet and exceed territory volume growth objectives by developing new accounts and increasing business in existing accounts</li>
<li>Committed to aggressive volume growth curve</li>
<li>Demonstrate strategic business acumen by providing examples of tracking sales progress and decline</li>
<li>Strong closing skills</li>
<li>Effective cold calling skills</li>
<li>Excellent communication and interpersonal skills</li>
<li>Polished presentation skills</li>
<li>Proven ability to develop and maintain relationships with key physicians and office staff</li>
<li>Organizational skills and prior experience managing a large geographic territory</li>
<li>Deliver sales goals while respecting expense budgets</li>
<li>Utilize savvy business prospecting techniques, networking and other lead generation tools to establish new customers</li>
<li>Develop and manage strong sustainable relationships with customer base</li>
<li>Provide regular updates to Regional Manager on prospects, pipeline and monthly volume forecasts</li>
<li>Forge a strong partnership with the Customer Support Specialist for the territory and work as a team to achieve seamless interaction with customers from sign up to long term support and business growth</li>
<li>Attend local and national conferences and tradeshows as assigned, to represent Millennium’s products and services, generate leads and interact with customers</li>
<li>Arrange and host peer-to-peer educational programs, providing physicians with a forum to learn about Millennium’s services</li>
<li>Adhere to all HIPAA confidentiality requirements</li>
<li>Manage Laboratory Service Assistants following company policy</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p>
</p>

Required Experience<br>
<ul>
<li>Demonstrated track record of continued progressive sales growth
</li>

<li>High energy level and ability to develop sales rapidly</li>
<li>Uncompromising ethics and integrity</li>

<li>Understanding of insurance and reimbursement issues</li>

<li>Able to maintain a home office</li>
<li>Clean driving record</li>
<li>Ability to travel overnight within the territory and to attend designated conferences, company meetings</li>
<br>
</ul>
