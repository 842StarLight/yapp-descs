<h2>Business Development Representative</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>SportsEngine is currently seeking bright, motivated sales professionals to join our fast paced, high growth sales team as a Business Development Representative in our Minneapolis office. If you have a passion for sports technology, excellent communication skills and an enthusiastic drive to succeed we would love to talk to you about our Business Development Representative position. This position requires working out of our Minneapolis office and is not open to remote employees.</p>
<p>
Each day, hundreds of thousands of volunteer coaches, directors, and managers positively impact the lives of millions of athletes through Sports. In addition to teaching them about their sports, they also impact their lives by nurturing in them lifelong values of working hard, being honorable, and treating each other like family.</p>
<p>
At SportsEngine, our mission is to help make these volunteer's lives easier by building and supporting world-class technology solutions to help them spend less time running their organization and more time putting smiles on the faces of children. To us, they are heroes.</p>
<p>
We're looking for people who not only agree with our mission, but have a passion for craftsmanship. We're one of Minnesota's Top 100 Companies to Work For, provide only the best facilities and equipment, offer competitive pay, great benefits, and the opportunity to go home each night knowing you truly made a difference in the lives of heroes.</p>
<p>
Join us.
