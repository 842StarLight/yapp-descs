<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
About You</b></h2>
<p>
Our Account Directors are responsible for cultivating and maintaining successful client relationships along with managing a team of direct reports.</p>
<p>
As an Account Director, you will be responsible for managing your client portfolios to acceptable revenue and margin goals. In addition, you will be responsible for leading, developing, and mentoring your account teams, from production-level employees up to Account Leads.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
You’ll be responsible for:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Cultivating and maintaining successful client relationships</li>
<li>
Driving strategy and improving client performance</li>
<li>
Cross channel/service project management</li>
<li>
Providing feedback and strategic guidance to your account teams</li>
<li>
Leading, mentoring and managing team members</li>
<li>
Promoting our core values and driving a positive work environment/culture</li>
<li>
Ensuring your client portfolios meet and exceed acceptable revenue and margin goals</li>
<li>
Building sales audits and presenting findings to potential clients</li>
<li>
Onboarding new clients</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
You’ll need to have:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
A Bachelor’s Degree or equivalent experience</li>
<li>
A minimum of 5 years’ paid search &amp; social experience (spanning all core AdWords &amp; Facebook products) and a minimum 3 years of experience in a client-facing digital marketing role</li>
<li>
Proven prior work experience managing a team of 3+ direct reports / team members and demonstrated ability to mentor and develop direct reports into more senior roles</li>
<li>
A deep understanding of AdWords/Facebook Ads Manager and be skilled at effectively communicating paid search &amp; social goals, targets, and tactics to clients and can get into the weeds when necessary.</li>
<li>
The ability to audit an account and present actionable insights to a client</li>
<li>
The ability to provide feedback and strategic guidance to all paid media channels (search, social, shopping, display, mobile, native and video)</li>
<li>
To hold your own talking growth strategy, SEO, CRO, analytics and measurement with marketing managers and C-Level executives alike</li>
<li>
Experience acting as an escalation point for clients, evaluating team services levels and proposing improvements</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Additional things that will impress us:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
The ability to identify upsell opportunities across all agency offerings and execute on those opportunities to grow revenue on existing clients</li>
<li>
You are skilled at effectively communicating/teaching optimization tasks to multiple team members at once and have past experience managing managers</li>
<li>
Successful track record of crafting and participating in sales pitches and audits</li>
<li>
Proven prior work experience managing a P&amp;L and hitting margin targets</li>
</ul>
