About This Role:<br>
Under general supervision, ensures and continually improves the quality and reliability of landscape irrigation products and/or lighting products.<br>
<br>
Demonstrates behavior that is consistent with the company’s values of Customer Satisfaction, Innovation, Family, and Social Responsibility.<br>
<br>
Essential Job Functions/Tasks:<br>
1. New product development team member. Ensures the reliability of new products by developing, managing, and executing comprehensive qualification plans. Assists the development team with robustness designing through the active participation in Failure Modes and Effects Analyses (FMEA) and by developing accelerated component level test methods.
<br>
<br>
2. Continually develops and improves in-house testing. Ensures test methods are aligned with test intent such as functional testing, environmental testing, and reliability testing. Reviews in-house testing and compares results to the field test results. Reports similarities and discrepancies to the Product Manager, Engineering Team, and Quality Assurance Team.<br>
<br>
3. Continually ensures understanding of product performance in the field. Retrieves product performance information from Contractors, Product Managers, Regional Sales Managers, the Customer Service Team, and the Technical Services Team. Analyzes product failures based on geography, environmental conditions, and product usage. Analyzes field failures, returned product, and customer complaints. Compares warranty guarantees to product issues and reports all correlations. Generates monthly reports.<br>
<br>
4. Conducts head-to-head testing of Hunter products against "best-in-class" by product line (Rotors, Sprays, Valves, and Controllers).<br>
<br>
5. Coordinates Hunter product installation with Contractors throughout various geographical regions for the purpose of performing Beta Field tests. Travels to Contractor sites, conducts installation, and trains Contractors on installation and operating procedures.
<br>
<br>
Other Job Functions:<br>
Continually expands knowledge of reliability engineering methods and applies methods in a practical manner.<br>
<br>
