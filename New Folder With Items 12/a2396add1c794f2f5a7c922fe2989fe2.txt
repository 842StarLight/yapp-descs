<h2>Leasing Consultant - Arlo Apts (Lease Up - Nov 2019)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Arlo Apartments is currently seeking a <b>Leasing Consultant </b>with at least 1-3 years’ experience in the field to join our team. This candidate will have <b>Lease Up experience </b>for our brand new 244-unit class A mid-rise in Malvern, PA. <b>This position will begin in November 2019.</b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>Job responsibilities for the position include:</p>
<ul>
<li>Touring prospective residents throughout each building and leasing apartments</li>
<li>Answering leasing calls and responding to online inquiries</li>
<li>Processing applications, preparation of lease paperwork, and weekly / monthly reports</li>
<li>Resolving resident issues or concerns</li>
<li>Coordination and follow up of maintenance requests for residents</li>
<li>Marketing of the community thru online advertisements, corporate outreach, and social media.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p>Successful candidates should possess the following job requirements:</p>
<ul>
<li>
<b>1 to 3 years’ experience</b> in Property Management</li>
<li>Experience with mid rise lease up preferred</li>
<li>Knowledge of Yardi Software</li>
<li>Working knowledge of LRO is a plus</li>
<li>Excellent customer service and communication skills</li>
<li><b>Working weekends may be necessary.</b></li>
<li><b>Working during inclement weather is also necessary.</b></li>
<br>
</ul>
