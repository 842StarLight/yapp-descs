<h2>Analyst - Sr Compensation</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p><b>General Summary</b></p>
<p>The Senior Compensation Analyst will be a key partner in the administration of the compensation plan. This role requires working with a variety of key partners across the business, including Field Operations teams, Human Resources (HR), Payroll, Finance, Sales and our Business Analyst team to manage and administer incentive / commission eligible representatives, including initial plan set up, change management and payouts. The role will also help support and drive key compensation initiatives and processes across the business and will be a key player in gained efficiencies within the HR organization.</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Essential Job Functions</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Maintains a safe, secure and healthy environment by adhering to Company/Customer safety standards and practices and to legal regulations, alerting others regarding potential hazards or concern</li>
<li>Performs all duties as assigned and adheres to TEAM’s Core Values</li>
<li>Administers various human resource plans and procedures for all organization personnel</li>
<li>Support the day to day administration of the sales compensation and/or commission eligible employees including:</li>
<ul>
<li>Validating HR data</li>
<li>Initial plan set up</li>
<li>Budget targets / quota and territory changes</li>
<li>Coordination with Payroll for quarterly payouts</li>
<li>Participate in periodic plan performance audits with respective regional leadership</li>
</ul>
<li>Ensures all compensation methodologies, policies, procedures and governance are followed; including, but not limited to, partnership with Compliance on job descriptions and job evaluations</li>
<li>Assist with the distribution of individual compensation plans, managing data collection and validation from data sources, and reporting on attainment to target</li>
<li>Assess and analyze data to identify reporting issues, gaps in processes or data, reconcile variances, and drive timely resolution</li>
<li>Assist in the development and management of Incentive and Compensation Plan documents, including assurances that all policies are maintained</li>
<li>Support overall compensation philosophy and corresponding analysis, as relevant and necessary</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Job Qualifications</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Prefer at least 4+ years direct compensation system management, finance or HR compensation experience</li>
<li>Advanced skills in Excel</li>
<li>Experience analyzing data to review for anomalies and trends</li>
<li>Motivated self-starter, proactive and action-oriented, requiring limited supervision</li>
<li>Strong quantitative, analytical and organizational skills</li>
<li>Highly organized, have multi-tasking skills, and efficient in ambiguous situations</li>
<li>Bachelor’s degree required, preferably in business, finance, accounting, or Computer Science</li>
<li>Experience with ADP platforms including, Enterprise and Employee Self Service is a plus</li>
<li>Experience with Cornerstone is a plus</li>
<li>Travel requirement 0% - 25%</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Work Conditions</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Position is located at the corporate office</li>
<li>Work is conducted in a semi-private office/cubicle setting</li>
<li>Days and hours of work are Monday through Friday 8:00 am to 5:00 pm. Some flexibility in hours is allowed, but the employee must be available during the core work hours of 9:30 am to 3:30 pm</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Physical and Mental Requirements</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Ability to lift and carry 25 pounds</li>
<li>Sufficient clarity of speech and hearing or other communication capabilities, with or without reasonable accommodation, which permits employee to communicate effectively</li>
<li>Ability to sit for prolonged periods of time with or without reasonable accommodation</li>
<li>Sufficient manual dexterity with or without reasonable accommodation, which permit the employee to perform routine office duties</li>
<li>Sufficient personal mobility and physical reflexes, with or without reasonable accommodations to perform office duties and travel when necessary to off-site locations</li>
</ul>
