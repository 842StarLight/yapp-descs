<h2>Accounting Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>Position Summary / Purpose</p>
<p>
The Accounting Manager is responsible for maintaining the company’s financial operation including but not limited to the accounting procedures, budgeting, cash flow, internal controls, and maintaining accounting software. The Accounting Manager also has the responsibility for compiling and maintaining the employee’s files and payroll.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
Principle Duties and Responsibilities</p>
<ul>
<li>Accounts Receivable – Recording payments in SAGE Accounting software, preparing customer invoices and processing change orders and supplements.
</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Accounts Payable – Entering all invoices and credit card charges into SAGE Accounting software, weekly payments to vendors, reconciling credit card statements (for both Swartz Contracting &amp; Emergency Services and PSCS companies).</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Payroll – Inputting daily time sheets, process payroll weekly, paying monthly, quarterly and semi-annual taxes. Also, does the payroll for Baty Rd.</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Opening New Jobs in SAGE.</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Syncing SAGE with DASH.</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Preparation and distribution of Financial Reporting for both companies.</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Bank Reconciliations for both companies.</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Maintain all personnel files.</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Track attendance, personal days and vacation days.</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Process and distribute weekly reports.</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Prepare monthly and annual sales reports.</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Preparation of BN financial reporting.</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Compile fixed asset and annual information for accountant.</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Assists with answering telephones, as needed.</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Flexibility of schedule when in CAT mode.</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Ability to organize, efficiently document and routinely update information; ability to apply procedures to work problems and situations.</li>
</ul> <ul><li>Establish and maintain a positive and professional relationship with co-workers, client, and customers; cultural sensitivity and ability to relate well and effectively in diverse workplace and with a diverse client population.
</li></ul>
<ul>
<li>Familiarity with current Windows operating systems (as applicable) and proficiency in required software, including current supporting versions of Windows applications such as Word, Excel, etc. and also SAGE Accounting Software</li>
</ul>
<p>Performance Competencies</p>
<ul>
<li>Oral Communication – Speaks clearly. Shows respect when addressing customers and superiors. Able to effectively respond to excited customers. Able to think on his/her feet.</li>
</ul>
<ul><li>Planning &amp; Organizing – Plans and organizes work activities considering the nature of the job at hand. Focuses on key priorities. Pays attention to details.
</li></ul>
<ul>
<li>Technical – Is appropriately trained and effectively performs all accounting work within the company.</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Quality – the individual demonstrates accuracy and thoroughness and monitors his / her own work to ensure quality.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p>
</p>
<p>Qualifications – Knowledge, Skills and Abilities</p>
<ul>
<li>High School Graduate with at least 2 years accounting experience and preferably some college accounting courses.</li>
<li>Must have excellent verbal and written communication skills and be detail oriented.</li>
<li>Construction background preferred.</li>
<li>Strong analytical skills and considerable skills in problem solving, conflict resolution and dealing with confidential information.</li>
<li>Self-motivated, responsible, and accountable. Identifies own learning needs and seeks appropriate steps to continue self-development.</li>
<li>Organizational and time management skills with the ability to prioritize a variety of tasks/projects.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p>
</p>
<p>Compensation and Benefits</p>
<ul>
<li>An Accounting Manager’s pay rate shall be based on experience and ability. Salary wage is to be paid weekly.</li>
</ul>
