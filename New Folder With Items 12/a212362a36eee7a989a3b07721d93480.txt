<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>About the job</b></h2>
<p>
Our team is the connective tissue sitting between GCAS (Global Client and Agency Solutions) clients. Global Marketing Partners and the ABAT Global Service Org who is responsible for driving business-defining insights, ad effectiveness and innovation support to drive business results globally. As a Customer Strategy Partner, you will develop and package strategies for GCAS clients and agency partners and serving as their strategy lead throughout the year ensuring ABAT is a key partner in driving measurable value. You also serves as the voice of the clients and GCAS verticals back to ABAT, playing a key role in defining ABAT’s vertical thought leadership strategy and editorial calendar. You will partner with your Cluster Team to identify their client’s business challenges and develop a strategic SVA support model leveraging ABAT’s global offerings to unlock growth in priority markets. You will also serve as a Vertical Lead within ABAT ensuring that our service offerings and thought leadership are addressing the opportunities within your respective GCAS vertical.
</p>
<p>Our Global Clients &amp; Agency Solutions team helps serve some of the world's largest brands. Our dedicated teams of marketing and advertising specialists use their unique skill sets to help shape how companies grow their businesses in the digital age. We aspire to advise our clients on all aspects of their online business strategy. Using Google's broad range of products, we help our clients connect instantly and seamlessly with their audiences.</p>
