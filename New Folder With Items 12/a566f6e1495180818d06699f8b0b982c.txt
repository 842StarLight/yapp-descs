PRIMARY PURPOSE OF POSITION<br>
Individual will be responsible for the coordination and execution of audit activities, including business risk assessment activities, design and operational effectiveness testing, and evaluation of control failures while serving as a liaison to business partners to ensure that the company’s risk management, governance and internal control processes are operating effectively. Engagement work will include assurance, compliance and advisory projects over information technology (IT) service delivery processes, IT infrastructure, cyber security, system implementations and process transformations.<br>
<br>
Individual will support other business initiatives and will be responsible for expressing opinions and providing insight on the adequacy and effectiveness of the control environment while identifying and communicating continuous improvement, control optimization and risk mitigation opportunities to business partners. Individual will also participate in enablement activities, working collaboratively with business partners to provide value-add risk and control expertise to help the business achieve their objectives.<br>
