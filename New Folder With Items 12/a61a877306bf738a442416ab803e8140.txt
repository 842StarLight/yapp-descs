<h2>Client Relationship Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Network Computer Solutions (NCS) empowers organizations by delivering the latest IT solutions. Our approach to business is simple; keeping our customers satisfied and prepared to interact with the rapid evolution of information technology. Our mission, to deliver high-performance solutions that are efficient and cost effective for our customers. Their success is our success.</p>
<p>NCS is seeking a highly capable, hardworking Client Relationship Manager. The Client Relationship Manager will work with clients directly. Responsibilities will include maintaining and expanding business development efforts to extend NCS’s reach. The successful candidate will play a key role in increasing income and revenue by managing and negotiating with clients, generating leads, qualifying prospects and managing sales of products and services. As a sales representative, or sales agent, they will also research sales competition.</p>
<p><b>Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Obtain a thorough knowledge of the company and its offering</li>
<li>Evaluate customers skills, needs and build productive long-lasting relationships</li>
<li>Attend meeting, sales events, and trainings to keep abreast of the latest developments</li>
<li>Report and provide feedback to management using financial statistical data</li>
<li>Maintain and expand client database within your assigned territory</li>
<li>Perform prospect outreach – speak with key decision makers to schedule meetings and consultations</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Requirements</b></p>
<ul>
<li>BA/BS degree or equivalent, High school degree; BS degree in Marketing or related field would be a plus</li>
<li>Computer use competency; Technical expertise would be a plus</li>
<li>Strong communication, negotiation and interpersonal skills</li>
<li>Self-motivated and driven</li>
<li>You are highly organized and possess excellent time management skills; you are able to meet deadlines and juggle multiple tasks at once in a fast-paced environment.</li>
<li>Ability to work within a team and independently</li>
</ul>
<p>We are an equal opportunity employer and value diversity. All employment is decided on the basis of qualifications, merit and business need.</p>

<p>Experience:</p>
<ul><li>Sales: 3 years (Preferred)</li></ul>
