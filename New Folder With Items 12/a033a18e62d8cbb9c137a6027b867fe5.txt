<h2>Shift Leader/Retail Sales Associate/Customer Service</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Asheville Emporium is seeking a personable and motivated individual to join our team! <b>Please note that this position is primarily evening and weekend hours.</b></p>
<p>Asheville Emporium's mission is "to celebrate creativity and connection." We represent 50+ artisans ranging from local to global and have partnered with more than 2 dozen nonprofits to date. We are a small team of dedicated individuals who enjoy learning and bettering this enterprise together. Heidi Swann-our founder-recently received "Small Business Leader of the Year Award for 2018" from the Asheville Chamber of Commerce. On a typical day at the Emporium, we have 100-700 visitors with an average mix of ⅓ locals and the others are folks from everywhere who are visiting Asheville.</p>
<p>Position Outline</p>
<ul>
<li>greet customers as they enter</li>
<li>engage through conversation and honest recommendations with customers to further sales</li>
<li>maintain facility in a clean and organized manner</li>
<li>process sales transactions accurately and efficiently</li>
<li>provide concise area information to guests</li>
<li>process incoming merchandise and clothing, restock as needed</li>
<li>key-holder open/closing duties</li>
</ul>
<p>Job Requirements:</p>
<ul>
<li>friendly, self motivated, honest and conscientious</li>
<li>1 year retail sales experience</li>
<li>excellent communication skills and ability to interact with public</li>
<li>knowledge of Asheville's cultural highlights and amenities</li>
<li>ability to work a flexible schedule that included some evenings and weekends.</li>
<li>functional computer and software literacy</li>
<li>reliable transportation</li>
<li>ability to lift 35 lbs</li>
<li>ability to adapt to seasonal scheduling changes and needs of the Emporium</li>
<li>excellent references</li>
</ul>
<p>Are you are seeking a part-time position working in an energetic, enjoyable work environment and are dedicated to excellence in your professional life? Then, we’d love to have you apply to join our team!</p>
