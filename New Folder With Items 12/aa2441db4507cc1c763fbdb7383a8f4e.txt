<p><b>
Position Summary
</b></p>
<p>Researches, writes, edits, and produces training materials for classroom and/or computer-based delivery. Applies a working knowledge of CDK products, process and/or skill sets and a strong understanding of dealership operations and our Industry to all designs. Has a strong understanding of how to use the software used for learning design and development, and our development standards.</p>
<p><b>
Position Responsibilities &amp; Essential functions</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Analyze training needs to acquire information needed to plan for instructional deliverables.</li>
<li>
Facilitate meetings, conduct research and conduct interviews to understand learner needs.</li>
<li>
Share work with subject matter experts, business stakeholders and peers for regular review and feedback.</li>
<li>
Develop learning deliverables using tools such as Adobe Captivate, Storyline, Camtasia, MadCap Flare, and the Microsoft Office suite.</li>
<li>
Maintain project documentation and utilize standard processes and templates.</li>
<li>
Collaborate and consult with other team members.</li>
<li>
Mentor Jr. Level Learning Experience Designers sharing best practices and opportunities for innovation as well as providing feedback on designs.</li>
<li>
Participate in quality improvement and/or organization wide initiatives.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Qualifications &amp; Job Requirements</b></p>
<ul><li>
5-7 years’ experience or a Master’s Degree in Adult Education or related field.</li></ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
PREFERRED ATTRIBUTES &amp; Qualifications</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Working knowledge of the learning design process and learning development software.</li>
<li>
Working knowledge of dealership operations and the automotive industry</li>
<li>
Strong knowledge of adult learning theory and learning best practices</li>
<li>
Advanced knowledge of assigned products, process and audience</li>
<li>
Advanced communication skills, both written and verbal</li>
<li>
Ability to solve problems (pivoting when needed) in a creative yet organized manner</li>
<li>
Strong technical background or aptitude</li>
</ul>
