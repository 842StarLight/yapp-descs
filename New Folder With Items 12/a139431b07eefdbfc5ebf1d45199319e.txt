The world's leading private security organization, G4S, has an immediate job opportunity for a Corporate Counsel - Corporate Paralegal. As a Corporate Counsel - Corporate Paralegal, you will work on a wide variety of both sophisticated and routine legal matters involving contracts, bid solicitations, proposals and other miscellaneous agreements and related documents. This individual will be responsible for drafting, reviewing, negotiating and counseling business managers on a broad range of commercial transactions, including service contracts, bid solicitations, requests for proposals, license agreements, non-disclosure or confidentiality agreements, and consulting agreements. Additional responsibilities will include maintaining documents in various forms, including but not limited to software tools used by G4S to maintain and report on Contracts. Word processing, filing and maintenance of contract files, and other general administrative duties related to contracts are required
