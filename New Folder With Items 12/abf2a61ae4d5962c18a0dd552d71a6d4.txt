<h2>Lead Product Management Analyst - Auto Product Line</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">

<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Purpose of Job</b></h2>
We are currently seeking talented Lead Product Management Analyst - California Auto Product Line for our San Antonio, TX (WestRidge South), Chesapeake, VA, Phoenix, AZ, Tampa, FL, Colorado Springs, CO facility or Remote.<br>
<br>
Accountable for analyzing the profitability, competitiveness and sales performance for the product life cycle, with a deliberate focus on state and local market results. Incumbents support (Executive) Directors of State Management and partner with other Centers of Excellence (COEs) to compile, analyze and interpret data to make strategic and tactical recommendations. Responsible for defining the business questions and providing insights to improve business performance.<p></p>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Job Requirements</b></h2>
<ul>
<li>
Leads the evaluation of profit, competitive, and sales performance as compared to plan and/or key metrics</li>
<li>
Leads the interaction with a team of cross-functional experts (e.g., Pricing, Underwriting, Claims, etc) working in agile methods to ensure objectives are met</li>
<li>
Analyzes and evaluates product performance metrics to form recommendations regarding market opportunities and product enhancements</li>
<li>
Leverages resources to research internal/external and member information to support formulation of product strategy</li>
<li>
Responsible for maintaining thorough level of knowledge of business performance across state(s) (e.g. trends, results, etc.)</li>
<li>
Partners with Government &amp; Industry Relations, Attorney, and Compliance Advisor to manage relationship with local regulatory agencies and industry organizations</li>
<li>
Meets regularly with key partners such as Pricing, Underwriting, Claims, P&amp;C Counsel, Military Affairs, Affinity, Experience and Marketing to discuss financial results and formulate state strategy</li>
<li>
May be responsible for the state level plan, including products, rate and revenue.</li>
<li>
Influence key stakeholders such as Underwriting, Claims, P&amp;C Counsel, Military Affairs, Affinity, Experience and Marketing to execute on the USAA mission</li>
<li>
Partners with Director to lead business reviews to align knowledge of organization around business performance</li>
</ul>
<p></p>

Minimum Requirements</b></p>

<li>
Bachelor's degree OR four additional years of related experience beyond the minimum required may be substituted in lieu of a degree</li>
<li>
8 or more years relevant business support and/or general analysis experience</li>
</ul>
