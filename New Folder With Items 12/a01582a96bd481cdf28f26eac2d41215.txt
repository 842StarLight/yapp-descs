<h2>Marketing Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader">
<b>Local Unit &amp; Position Description</b><b>
</b>
</h2>
<div><div><div>
<div>DNV GL – Business Assurance Marketing group is seeking a Marketing Specialist who can bring a combination of creativity, enthusiasm and energy to our Marketing Team. The selected candidate will be expected to be creative and efficient while planning, managing and executing projects related to marketing campaigns. This position is based out of our Katy, TX office.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div><b>
Tasks May Include</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
Create and manage content calendar
</li>
<li>Collaborate with writers and designers to produce content
</li>
<li>Create project budgets and timelines</li>
<li>
Create and send e-marketing emails to current and prospective customers</li>
<li>
Develop and maintain external vendor relationships</li>
<li>
Coordinates, promotes and assists with events, tradeshows and webinars</li>
<li>
Assist in content life cycle maintenance, quality testing, support and contribution to our websites</li>
<li>
Hold an active role in the lead generation and nurturing process that generates qualified leads for sales which includes meeting deadlines, follow up emails, phone calls and tracking for each campaign</li>
</ul>
