<h2>Senior Financial Reporting & Business Analyst</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>The <b>Senior Financial Reporting &amp; Business Analyst</b> will be responsible for the management of our data warehouse as well as the integrity of all underlying data within or connected to the main database(s). The analyst will produce the required internal and external reports for the Sales, Operations, &amp; Finance departments. This role reports to the Director of Finance; the ideal candidate will have strong report building skills, acute analytical skills, and a working understanding of business systems.</p>
<p><b>Essential Duties and Responsibilities</b> include the following. Other duties may be assigned.</p>
<ul>
<li>Produce sales and operations reports for our customers on a daily, monthly and quarterly basis and set up the processes and procedures that ensure complete, accurate, timely reporting using a replicable process that over time seeks to approach automation. This will involve working closely with the sales and operations leaders to understand their needs.</li>
<li>Assist the Director of Finance in developing and maintaining the data warehouse that allows disparate sources of information to be brought together under one reporting framework.</li>
<li>Development of financial reports, dashboards, scorecards and other internal &amp; external reporting needs.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Job Requirements: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree, Finance or Business preferred</li>
<li>5 – 8 years of experience</li>
<li>Strong analytical experience and business acumen</li>
<li>Strong experience with business systems, data warehousing and business intelligence platforms</li>
<li>Advanced Excel knowledge and skills including knowledge of excel add-ins including fuzzy look-ups and building data connections within Excel</li>
<li>Experience with Microsoft SQL Server Management Studio, SQL Server Reporting Services, and SQL Server Integration Services. Solid and demonstrable report writing, query and scripting experience</li>
<li>Attention to detail</li>
<li>Excellent verbal, written, and communication skills</li>
<li>Possess strong problem-solving skills and the ability to identify and implement process improvements</li>
<li>A-player</li>
<li>High-achiever</li>
<li>Self-starter, self-motivated</li>
</ul>
