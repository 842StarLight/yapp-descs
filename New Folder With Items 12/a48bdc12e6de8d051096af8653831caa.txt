<h2>Business Developer - Public|Corporate</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Overview
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div>HGA is an award winning architectural, engineering and planning firm with an excellent opportunity in our Santa Monica office for an experienced business developer to collaborate with our Principal whose focus is in the Public|Corporate market sector with marketplace development locally and regionally. This includes assisting with strategic planning, identification of front-end contacts, networks and similar initiatives. Candidates must offer experience as it relates to successful strategic planning and network development.</div>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div><b>Most of your time will be spent:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
Working with Practice Group Leader and Principals to develop a list of potential targeted clients and project opportunities that support the market-driven strategy of the Market Sector.</li>
<li>
Building and maintaining relationships through a consultative and solutions-based approach with decision makers at the organizations identified as targeted clients.</li>
<li>
Researching leads and making cold or warm calls to potential clients, evaluating and targeting prospects based on evaluation criteria.</li>
<li>
Developing and maintaining strong business network contacts to keep abreast of industry, client and competitor activity.</li>
<li>
Possessing a deep understanding of current business trends (across sectors) within your geographic region.</li>
<li>
Deepening the understanding of clients, their business and their needs to prepositioning through strong relationships.</li>
<li>
Meeting with Principals on a regular basis to strategize, track and inform collaborative efforts in client contact activities and prepositioning.</li>
<li>
Consulting with Principals to inform pursuit and interview strategy as part of “go/no-go” decisions.</li>
<li>
Assisting the Practice Group Leader in budgeting and monitoring the overall marketing budget. Work with marketing department to report KPIs.</li>
<li>
Collaborating with marketing team to develop marketing collateral for client engagement.</li>
</ul>
<div><b>
There will also be times you will:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
Identify and represent HGA at key industry associations and events and coordinate participation of other key HGA personnel, plus follow-up on requests.</li>
<li>
Work closely with marketing team to address communication strategy and media plan.</li>
<li>
Coach and mentor project teams on business development strategy and skills.</li>
<li>
Update CRM data (in Deltek Vision) database on a timely basis and build reports.</li>
<li>
Perform other secondary duties and responsibilities as required.</li>
</ul>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Qualifications
</b></h2>
<div><div><div><div><div><div>
<div><b>The Business Developer we are looking for will have:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
Previous experience in sales or business development, with the ability to strengthen and further deepen our existing client relationships.</li>
<li>
Experience in a design-related field preferred.</li>
<li>
An interest to work with a dynamic team.</li>
<li>
Outstanding communication skills that are adaptive to a wide range of personalities.</li>
</ul>
<div>
<b>
Qualifications</b>:</div>
<ul>
<li>
BS/BA in Marketing, Communications, Architecture, Engineering or related field.</li>
<li>
Intermediate skills with Microsoft Office Products – Word, Excel, PowerPoint.</li>
<li>
Skills in Deltek Vision and/or other CRM/real estate software, is helpful. If not proficient, has a strong acumen to learn new technologies and software.</li>
<li>
Skills with Adobe Creative Suite or other publisher-type software is helpful.</li>
<li>
This position requires frequent early morning, evening and some weekend event attendance outside of normal business hours.</li>
<li>
This position may require frequent air and over-the-road travel.</li>
</ul>
