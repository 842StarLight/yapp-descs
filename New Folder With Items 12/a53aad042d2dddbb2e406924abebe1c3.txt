<p><b>Summary: </b>This Payroll position has with specific oversight of The Rinks and Great Park ICE Payroll. The Payroll A ccountant is responsible for performing all activities necessary to process payroll for multiple entities. This position involves auditing payroll and time records, processing voluntary and involuntary deductions, and documenting and updating procedures. This position is also responsible for updating procedures, preparing accounting G/L related entries, account reconciliations, and preparing miscellaneous reports for management, etc. This position resides with the Accounting Department at Great Park ICE.
</p>
<div></div>
<p><b><i>Examples of Essential Duties/Responsibilities:
</i></b></p>
<p><b>PAYROLL
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Accurate and timely completion of bi-weekly payroll processing for multiple entities.
</li>
<li>Balance and audit payroll data before and after processing to ensure completeness
</li>
<li>Audit all payroll and time records for accuracy and ensure compliance with federal, state and local regulations including auditing meal breaks and time entry adjustments
</li>
<li>Sort and distribute payroll checks to various sites as needed
</li>
<li>Process garnishment orders to include accurate calculations, notification, system input and compliance.
</li>
<li>Ensure accurate and timely funding of 401(k) submissions to John Hancock
</li>
<li>Process periodic, quarterly, annual tax filing integrations and reconciliations between Workday and ADP Smart Compliance
</li>
<li>Responsible for validating the data integrity between the ABI and Workday integration
</li>
<li>Data input and processing for onsite / manual checks, voids, stop payments, direct deposit reversals, and other payroll adjustments
</li>
<li>Research, analyze, and resolve payroll discrepancies and inquiries
</li>
<li>Work closely with Human Resources to ensure accurate data flow to payroll module
</li>
<li>Collaboration with other team members and management to continuously improve the bi-weekly payroll process to ensure consistency and accuracy
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>ACCOUNTING
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Post labor distribution by G/L classification, per department, event code, and event type into G/L
</li>
<li>Ensure cash reconciliation of payroll posting on a bi-weekly basis.
</li>
<li>Posting of G/L entries in Accounting software (Great Plains)
</li>
<li>Partner with Human Resources, Controller, and other Managers with labor reports as required/requested
</li>
<li>Maintain balance sheet account reconciliations for compensation and benefits-related G/L accounts
</li>
<li>Provide audit support for various annual audits
</li>
<li>Prepare monthly reports and journal entries as needed
</li>
<li>Reconcile Prepaid accounts and various liability accounts and prepare journal entries
</li>
<li>Prepare fixed assets and journal entries
</li>
<li>Provide support for monthly financials as necessary
</li>
<li>Great Park Ice Daily Sales Reconciliation
</li>
<li>Between the Rinks Restaurant Daily Sales Reconciliation
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>REPORTING
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Weekly Bank Account reconciliation for Great Park Ice (operation &amp; restaurant)
</li>
<li>Weekly Journal entries for Great Park Ice for both operation and restaurant
</li>
<li>Troubleshooting and reconciliation of daily revenue for Great Park Ice
</li>
<li>Weekly review of Accounts Receivable Aging &amp; reconcile Accounts Receivable to General Ledger
</li>
<li>Provide support for monthly financials as necessary
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
