<p>Leasing Agent Responsibilities<br>Include:</p>
<p>Presenting properties and provided amenities in a positive light to prospective tenants<br>Advertising available properties using a variety of media and promoting materials<br>Job brief<br>We are looking for capable Leasing Agent to present and lease properties to prospective lessors. <p>Requirements<br>Up to date with property market status<br>Excellent communication and negotiation skills<br>Persuasive with marketing and sales skills<br>Customer service orientation</p>
