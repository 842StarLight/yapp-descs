<p><b>Technical Architect - Full Stack</b></p>
<p><b>
J.C. Penney Company, Inc.</b></p>
<p><b>
Plano, Texas</b></p>
<p>
We are looking for an experienced <b>Full Stack Technical Architect</b> to join our team. In this role, your main responsibilities will include E2E design &amp; development of new systems/features for Customer Experience - FindMore and Point of Sale teams using agile best practices, leveraging Java and/or Microsoft .NET technologies, Spring Framework and relational databases along with JavaScript Frameworks such as ReactJS, Redux, Node and npm.
</p>
<p><b>Primary Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Drive the technical architecture of Stores Checkout that includes in Store items as well as .com ordering.
</li>
<li>Drive technology roadmap of the Product and be comfortable handling conflicts amicably with Product when it comes to balancing technical backlog versus function features.</li>
<li>
Lead the team’s technical direction by exploring and implementing the best in class technologies and advocate the use of best tools, libraries, and processes.</li>
<li>
Work directly with other JCPenney IT associates as well as JCPenney business partners to define contracts, dependencies and communicate technical capabilities</li>
<li>
Collaborate with Product Managers, Designers, DevOps, Front &amp; Back-end developers to deliver best user experience.</li>
<li>
Troubleshoot pre and post-production functional and non-functional issue</li>
<li>
Stay up to date on the latest industry technology trends and techniques</li>
<li>
Propose new ideas and best practices to improve performance and experience of the site</li>
<li>
Continuously test your software to fail-fast and fail-forward to ensure the best user experience.</li>
<li>
Communicate effectively with remote and distributed teams.</li>
<li>
Take pride in owning what you build, support it in production and continuously monitor and evolve it to improve performance, scalability and ease-of-use.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Core Competencies &amp; Accomplishments:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Strong development experience in Java and/or .NET technologies, Spring Framework and relation databases (preferably PostgreSQL, Oracle, SQL Server).</li>
<li>
Prior experience leading Product/Portfolio level architecture.</li>
<li>
Prior experience with DevOps and Automation.</li>
<li>
Work experience in Agile Scrum methodologies</li>
<li>
Micro service architecture experience and knowledge of GIT for version control.</li>
<li>
Experience building applications using Adaptive/Responsive Design Principles and leveraging various applications layers to enhance performance</li>
<li>
Familiarity with Data Structures, Algorithms, and general Computer Science Fundamentals</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Minimum Requirements:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Bachelors of Science Degree in Computer Information Systems, or equivalent work experience</li>
<li>
10+ years of experience with Java and/or .NET technologies and relation databases.</li>
<li>
5 plus years of experience with leading strong engineering teams.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Desired Experience:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Containers
</li>
<li>RESTful API</li>
<li>
Cloud/AWS</li>
</ul>
