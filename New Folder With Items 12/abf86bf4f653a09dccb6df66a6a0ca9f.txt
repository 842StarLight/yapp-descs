<h2>IT Sales Consultant</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>Are you looking for a company where you can learn, grow, and have the potential to earn $60,000+ in your first year with the realistic opportunity of $85K - $100K+ as you meet and exceed your sales targets? Do you want to utilize your current sales/customer service skills, that we'll enhance with ongoing training, to go to the next level in a professional B2B sales career? Take the chance to turn your ambition and competitive spirit into a rewarding sales career with Access Systems. As we continue to grow, we are looking for highly motivated, success-oriented people to join the team as sales consultants.
</p>
<p>In this role, you will be responsible for consulting with current and potential clients and selling them our IT products and services within an assigned geographic territory. You'll have technical engineering assistance, so you don't need to be an IT wizard in this role. You'll simply need to have a general knowledge of technology and the ability to learn and think on your feet. You need to be able to communicate to businesses, in layman's terms, what we offer and how it will benefit them in order to convince them to partner with Access Systems for their technology needs. If this sounds like the kind of career move you've been wanting to make, we want to talk with you.
</p>
<p>Apply today and join one of <b>Iowa's Top Workplaces</b> for the fourth year in a row!</p>
