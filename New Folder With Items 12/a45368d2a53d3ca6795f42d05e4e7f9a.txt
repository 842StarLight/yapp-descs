<p>Pharma Field Service Engineer Raleigh North Carolina</p>
<p>Reports to Sales Manager (Indirectly to Operations Service Manager)</p>
<p>Job Purpose</p>
<p>The Field Service Engineer position installs, diagnoses, repairs, and calibrates the refrigeration and electrical systems on our environmental test chambers. The position travels to troubleshoot equipment at customer sites. Travel can be up to 50% overnight Stays of annually scheduled work time. The position also serves as a customer service support person that provides customer based solutions and is a sales liaison working with the sales division of the company for both service and chamber needs.</p>
<p>Duties and Responsibilities</p>
<ul><li> Diagnoses, repairs and calibrates complex cascade refrigeration, single stage refrigeration systems, electrical systems and controls on our environmental test chambers</li></ul>
<ul><li> Installation and maintenance of environmental test chambers</li></ul>
<ul><li> Usage of hand and power tools, refrigeration gauges and evacuators</li></ul>
<ul><li> Usage of datalogger and software for mapping of environmental test chambers</li></ul>
<ul><li> Prepare commissioning and mapping documentation for the Life Science industry</li></ul>
<ul><li> Supervision of field labor/subcontractors on Life Science projects</li></ul>
<ul><li> Supports Life Science Project Coordinator on installations of projects</li></ul>
<ul><li> Completion of daily reports, daily site task forms, subcontractor time sheets, filed material requisitions, and maintain copies</li></ul>
<ul><li> Maintain accurate as-built drawings during installations</li></ul>
<ul><li> Completes all required development training requirements including but not limited to Career Path Training with goal to achieve highest level of expected skills for position</li></ul>
<ul><li> Supports the Key and Strategic Accounts to achieve budgeted/planned performance, grow sales, grow territory, brand awareness and increase market share</li></ul>
<ul><li> Supports service sales goals of company and becomes liaison for large and small business opportunities by supporting customer needs</li></ul>
<ul><li> Participates in various teams and initiatives within the company</li></ul>
<ul><li> Provides the company input on customer contractual requirements</li></ul>
<ul><li> Provides the company input on product development requirements</li></ul>
<ul><li> Supports the company’s protection of intellectual property</li></ul>
<ul><li> Demonstrated ability of professional and public communication skills as it relates to building relationships with customers</li></ul>
<ul><li> Multi-dimensional problem solving skills</li></ul>
<ul><li> Ability to use and perform in Microsoft office (Word, Excel, Powerpoint)</li></ul>
<ul><li> Management of multiple customer locations and ability to work with mid to upper level customer management</li></ul>

<ul><li> Possession of High School Diploma or GED required</li></ul>
<ul><li> HVAC program certification, associate’s degree and or equivalent work experience in specified field required</li></ul>
<ul><li> Two year minimum experience in the Life Science Industry</li></ul>
<ul><li> Valid driver’s license with good driving record</li></ul>
<ul><li> Ability to possess major credit card to cover travel expenses to be reimbursed</li></ul>
<ul><li> CFC Type II or Universal EPA certification required</li></ul>
<ul><li> Self-starter-ability to work independently with little or no supervision</li></ul>
<ul><li> Thorough understanding of mechanical and electrical operations preferred</li></ul>
<ul><li> Ability to read blue prints (mechanical and electrical) and identify components</li></ul>
<ul><li> Ability to read and understand Life Science technical submittals</li></ul>
<ul><li> Ability and willingness to travel (local, national and international as required)</li></ul>
<ul><li> Good verbal and written communication skills</li></ul>
<ul><li> Ability to represent company well</li></ul>
<ul><li> Basic computer skills</li></ul>
<ul><li> Fundamental understanding in air, water, refrigeration piping, compression system, electricity and electrical installation preferred</li></ul>

<ul><li> Understand and execute cGMP compliance on documentation</li></ul>

<p>Working Conditions Mostly standing work. Frequently lifting up to 25 lbs, occasionally lifting up to 100 lbs. Occasionally carrying up to 100 lbs. Detailed work with hands; fine work, simple grasping and pushing and pulling. Position requires frequent bending, squatting, climbing, crawling, reaching, and twisting with occasional kneeling. Significant portions of travel time require extended sedentary periods. Subject to weather and adverse driving conditions when outside.</p>
