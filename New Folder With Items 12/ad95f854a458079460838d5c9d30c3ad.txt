<p>
Oldcastle Infrastructure, a CRH company, is the leading manufacturer of precast concrete, polymer concrete, and plastic products in the United States. Our products range from utility vaults, reinforced concrete pipe, catch basins, drainage and septic tanks, to retaining walls, storm shelters, wall panels, concrete barriers, a variety of prestressed concrete products and more. With more than 80 locations nationwide and 3,000 plus employees, Oldcastle Infrastructure is committed to upholding core values of reliability, quality, and service in cutting edge ways.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Job Summary</b></p>
<p></p>
<br>
<ul>
<li>
The Estimator is the fulcrum to OI’s business to ensure there is a counterbalance to the customer needs and OI’s profitability through accurate summation of all project costs through analyzing drawings and technical documents to create an accurate proposal for the customer.</li>
<li>
The Estimator is responsible for ALL allocated inbound non-transactional RFQs. They review drawings, technical documents and other associated documentation to complete take-offs, pricing and ultimately a quote through partnering with other key OI functional areas such as sales, strategic account managers, production, engineering and transportation in a timely manner.</li>
<li>
Estimator's are individuals that exhibit organizational discipline and meticulous attention to detail while managing multiple RFQs.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Key Responsibilities</b></p>
<p></p>
<br>
<ul>
<li>
Complete take-offs and quotes for all RFQ’s brought in by the O.S.R.</li>
<li>
Send quote to customer before due date, ensuring O.S.R. is copied on all correspondence.</li>
<li>
Communicate directly with customer regarding quantity verification/confirmation and quote structure</li>
<li>
Work with O.S.R. on proposal strategy, basis-of-design changes, or value engineering options</li>
<li>
Work with vendors to prepare bids and write proposals.</li>
<li>
Manage bids that range in size and complexity simultaneously.</li>
<li>
Review and analyze drawings, specifications, proposals, and other documentation to prepare, cost and labor estimates for products, projects or services.</li>
<li>
Utilize market-based price list provided by sales manager.</li>
<li>
Assist in development of department improvement initiatives such as historical data, tool and template development and closing out win/loss jobs in ERP</li>
<li>
Work with O.S.R to ensure all OI products are being considered – “Own the Jobsite”</li>
<li>
Work with OSR to understand and complete an entire bid package properly</li>
</ul>
