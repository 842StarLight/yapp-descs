<h2>Business Event Sales Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>Summary: The Business Event Sales Manager provides leadership and manages the work of a sales team comprised of inside and outside specialists and account managers. The Sales Manager plays a critical role in establishing sales strategy, goals, tactics, evaluating performance of the team, and ensuring maximization of revenue generation and customer satisfaction.<br>
<br>
Essential Duties and Responsibilities include the following. Other duties may be assigned.
<br>
<ul>
<li>Works closely with the CBO Director to develop and carry out the sales function related to business event sales, including but not limited to the sales cycle, methodology and metrics.</li>
<li>Directly manages a sales team and works closely with the sales team in developing and managing business accounts.</li>
<li>Develop products and pricing in collaboration with the Operations Manager to fulfill product and service requirements while maintaining margin.</li>
<li>Analyze and evaluate the effectiveness of sales methodology, cost, and results.</li>
<li>Develop and maintain a sales budget.</li>
<li>Develop sales tools</li>
<li>Establish short-term and long-term goals, objectives, and policies.</li>
<li>Participate in the planning and development of marketing and communication materials</li>
<li>Represent Regal and promote the company at tradeshows and meetings.</li>
<li>Supervise the preparation, issuance, and delivery of sales materials, exhibits, and promotion programs.</li>
<li>Recommend policies and procedures to enhance operations</li>
<li>Recruit, hire, train, supervise and evaluate sales team.</li>
<li>Work with other departments to develop sales, marketing, and operational best practices</li>
<li>Carry out other special assignments and duties as required</li>
<li>Regular and consistent attendance</li>
</ul>
</div>
