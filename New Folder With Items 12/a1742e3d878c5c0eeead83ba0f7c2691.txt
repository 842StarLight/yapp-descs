<h2>Customer Service Rep with Screen printing or Embroidery industry experience</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>We are currently looking for a Customer Service Representative to join our team.**</p>
<p>The Customer Service Representative position is responsible for managing and overseeing the production of the job through the entire process that involve embroidered and screen printed and branded merchandise sales along with general customer service needs. This includes quality control as well as the sustainment and development of relationships with customers. Timely and frequent contact with customers, sales and production make this a critical position for monitoring and improving quality and performance.</p>
<p>Key Tasks: **</p>
<ul>
<li>Responsible for working directly with customers, sales and production to ensure job is completed correctly. Including working on scheduling projects, estimating printing and obtaining prepress proof approvals and imaging data signoffs on all projects</li>
<li>Meet and follow up with walk-in customers, answer phone calls and reply to emails from customers about products, services, and pricing</li>
<li>Ensuring excellent relationships between production, vendors, sales and customers requiring strong organizational work habits, good judgment, excellent communication skills, and follow-through on time-sensitive activities.</li>
<li>Quote and enter orders via QuickBooks, Production Management Software (shopworx, ASI Smartbooks) experience preferred for all product operations in electronic management system as well as work with purchasing to order raw materials; scheduling any outside services, work with department managers to schedule and track projects through the production process.</li>
<li>Work with shipping department to ensure projects are completed on time and shipped/routed as required, including working with sales to produce final invoices for all projects.</li>
<li>Personally act as the representative on behalf of the customer in all activities. Including reviewing proofs with a customer and notifying the customer of all scheduling changes.</li>
<li>Serves both Sales Reps and Customers as primary contact and source of information on particular jobs.</li>
<li>Services the customer by solving problems, gathering and transmitting information as needed and demanding the highest quality product.</li>
</ul>
<p>Requirements: **</p>
<ul>
<li>Experience in embroidery, screen printing , promotional &amp; print is preferred. Relevant sales experience of tailored and catered products &amp; services is also welcomed (marketing, advertisement, ad, web)</li>
<li>Must be able to appropriately communicate and interact with customers and production team on a day to day basis</li>
<li>Good judgment, an eye for quality, and knowledge of printing are necessary</li>
<li>Must be able to read and interpret customer specifications</li>
</ul>
<p>Job Type: Full-time</p>
