<h2>RVP of Sales - East</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>
<div><b>Role</b></div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
We are looking for a Regional VP of Sales who will be able scale with this fast growing organization. You will lead our efforts to build our culture to attract and retain the best employees and customers. As a member of the executive team, you will be responsible for employee engagement, performance management, leadership development, staffing and much more.</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
The person successful in this role will have a passion for building and maintaining high performing organizations, be a “hands on” generalist who is not only naturally data driven, and a strategic thinker with a doer attitude understanding the value and importance of company culture and happy employees. They will thrive in the fast-pace environment of a startup, and is able to execute on large initiatives, while wearing multiple hats.</div>
</div>
<div><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Your Daily Adventures Will Include</b></h3>
<div><ul>
<li>
Support direct reports (7-12 Account Executives) by participating and leading in client and prospect meetings or engaging other corporate resources as required. #1 job is helping reps close business</li>
<li>
Directly support enterprise sellers and manage a frontline sales leader in the corporate segment</li>
<li>
Ongoing development of sales team which includes recruiting, hiring and training new reps on the Outreach sales process</li>
<li>
Conduct weekly forecast meetings. Coaches direct reports re: strategies to drive deal closure</li>
<li>
Reports on sales activity and forecast to senior leadership</li>
<li>
Serve as an active member of the leadership team to build strong, collaborative relationships throughout the Sales, Marketing, Legal, Support, Pre-Sales, and Success organizations</li>
<li>
Collaborate with sales enablement, product and technology business leaders to ensure optimal support of sales activity</li>
<li>
Assess the needs of our customers and gather user feedback to improve upon existing solutions and design and iterate sales process and operations</li>
<li>
Align with our corporate values</li>
</ul></div>
</div></div>
<div><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Basic Qualifications
</b></h3>
<div><ul>
<li>8+ years of outstanding results leading SaaS sales organizations delivering new customer growth</li>
<li>
Experience in building sales organizational structure, process and measurement focus</li>
<li>
Results-driven coach, mentor and role model who manages and develops best-in-class talent and drives group results through teamwork and individual performance improvement</li>
<li>
Proven ability to drive process improvements while leveraging strong problem solving and analytical skills</li>
<li>
Demonstrated, proven success in lead generation, prospecting, contract negotiation and closing customers</li>
<li>
Proven ability to build positive relationships and influence. Strong communication and interpersonal skills; ability to be personable yet persistent</li>
<li>
Experience leveraging Salesforce.com</li>
<li>
Proven record of success in a high volume transaction sales environment. Experience selling to the enterprise</li>
</ul></div>
