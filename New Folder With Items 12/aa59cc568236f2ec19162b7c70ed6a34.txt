<div><b>
Solutions Marketing Manager – Finance</b></div>
<div>
As part of a massive investment in marketing to support our market-leading growth, we are looking for a dynamic, creative, and results-oriented Solutions Marketing Manager. Our ideal candidate is someone who understands solution marketing as ranging from solutions that are based on market needs served by messaging, packaging, and pricing; to solutions that are based on market needs served by product integrations. We’re looking for candidates with backgrounds that demonstrate excellent written communication skills, technical awareness, and a track record of successfully matching technologies with use cases and industries to create solutions.</div>
<div>
</div>
<div><b>
Responsibilities Include:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Gathering input from market research, sales teams, customer interactions, and knowledge of product roadmap to identify sufficiently large, specific, targeted use cases and/or industries where Mendix is differentiated.</li>
<li>Creating the messaging and positioning that maps the value of Mendix for this specific use case and/or industry. This may include messaging briefs, persona development, competitive positioning, and other source documents.</li>
<li>Collaborating with demand generation, lead conversion, PR, and AR stakeholders. Act as SME for Campaigns around these solution areas, and deliver demos, content, and assets for successful execution.</li>
<li>Partnering with Sales Enablement to educate sales teams on solutions offerings.</li>
<li>Connecting with Product Management to inform the long-term product roadmap as is necessary to continue delivering high-quality solutions content and programs.</li>
</ul>
<div><b>
</b></div>
<div><b>
Ideal background:</b></div>
<div>
Demonstrated success marketing a software product or solution designed specifically for customers in the Finance/Banking industry.</div>
<div>
Understanding of current trends in FinTech, such as the impact of AI, mobile payments, biometrics, and digital-only banks and their impact on traditional companies and processes.</div>
<div>
5-10 years of relevant professional experience.</div>
<div>
Comfort working across geographically dispersed teams.</div>
<div>
Familiarity with technologies and processes central to application development, such as agile application development methodologies, application lifecycle management, DevOps, modern application frameworks, and the IaaS/PaaS technology space.</div>
<div>
Success in marketing complex products or solutions, such as those that span different companies’ product lines, those that span several industries with identified pain points, or those that offer a horizontal platform that is applicable to numerous use cases.</div>
<div>
Experience interacting with external customers to gather market and pain point data and information.</div>
<div>
Strong focus on delivering the best possible outcomes to our customers.</div>
