We are looking for a Regional Field Services Manager to help build our territory for Property Preservation and Inspection. Come be a part of a stimulating business venture where you can expand your skill set!
<br><br>
WHAT YOU GET TO DO
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Evaluate inspections performed for properties. Determine and recommend repairs to be performed for each property</li>
<li>Prepare and interpret bid and work item requests made by vendor, customer, and internal team members to ensure bid and work item consistency with intent of requestor</li>
<li>Evaluate bids for property repairs, verifying that bids are appropriate for work to be performed, determining repair activity to be performed, and the vendor to perform such repair</li>
<li>Evaluate completed work submitted by vendors to ensure work quality, consistency against work requested, and turn-around-time for work performed, inspecting properties on a random basis to verify vendor performance</li>
<li>Provide input as requested on upgrades or enhancements to the automated property tracking and accounting system software in order to improve functionality and utility</li>
</ul>
WHAT ABOUT YOU? You have...
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Experience building relationships with local code enforcement, municipalities and utility companies</li>
<li>Experience developing portfolio and report changes to local codes and laws that pertain to business division</li>
<li>Experience identifying local property preservation vendors for Vendor Management Department, participating in the budgeting and goal setting processes</li>
<li>Experience attending public hearings as needed to settle lien and enforcement claims</li>
</ul>
