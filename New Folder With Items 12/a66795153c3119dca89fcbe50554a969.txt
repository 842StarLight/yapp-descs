<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div>We're looking for successful outside sales professionals with an appetite for building new business. If you have a media sales background, we want to talk to you. We offer a competitive base salary with monthly uncapped commissions. Our advertising product suite allows businesses to achieve their marketing objectives by designing custom campaigns that deliver the highest-value and return on their investment. As a new business development executive, your primary mission is to focus on developing new customers. In this business-to-business sales role, you will drive the relationship and sale from start to finish. You will identify opportunities, present to decision makers and close new business. Responsibilities include:</div>
<ul>
<li>
Generate and identify new sales opportunities using multiple outbound channels and strategies</li>
<li>
Develop a deep understanding of prospects objectives and challenges and deliver solutions against these elements</li>
<li>
Conduct a consistently high volume of booked product demonstrations or discovery meetings with fully qualified prospects</li>
<li>
Build and manage an active pipeline through the sales cycle to deal close</li>
<li>
Internally partner with management, marketing, sales operations and delivery teams to collaborate and drive business</li>
<li>
Deliver against monthly sales targets and pipeline commitments</li>
<li>
Track all your activity in our CRM and related tools</li>
<li>
Continually develop digital advertising industry knowledge</li>
</ul>
</div></div></div>
