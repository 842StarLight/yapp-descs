We are seeking an Inventory Planner for Bluestem Brands to be located at our office in Eden Prairie, MN.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
What You'll Do:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Collaborate with buying team to manage the monthly forecast and open to buy process for a family of business</li>
<li>Manage system alerts to ensure accurate product level forecasts</li>
<li>Optimize replenishment settings to balance inventory flow while delivering expected in stock results</li>
<li>Drive effective product life cycle management through recommending appropriate marketing and promotional action</li>
<li>Identify and implement strategies to deliver and exceed inventory goals of in stocks, turn, weeks of supply while driving sales</li>
<li>Partner with supply chain and fulfillment teams to reconcile issues</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p>
</p>
<p><b>What You'll Need:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree required</li>
<li>2-5 years relevant work experience</li>
<li>Excellent analytical skills</li>
<li>Strong Excel skills</li>
<li>Ability to collaborate and effective communication skills</li>
<li>Attention to detail with an eye on the big picture</li>
<li>Experience with Oracle Retail Planning software a plus (AP/IP, MFP, RDF)</li>
<li>Experience in an eCommerce retail environment</li>
<br>
</ul>
