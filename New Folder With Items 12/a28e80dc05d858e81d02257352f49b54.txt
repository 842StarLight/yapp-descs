<p><b>National Director of Special Events</b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>U.S.VETS is the nation’s largest veteran services nonprofit, providing housing, counseling, career development and comprehensive support to veterans and their families. With 30 residential and service sites across the country and national homeless prevention programs, U.S.VETS ensures that more than 5,500 veterans have a place to call home every night, and a network of resources and support to help them achieve self-sufficiency.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>The National Director of Special Events reports directly to the Vice President, Development &amp; Communications, and is responsible for national and regional special event and revenue goals. The Director is charged with increasing special event visibility, entertainment and corporate engagement, and sponsorship platforms nationally. Activities include direct responsibility for U.S.VETS National SALUTE Gala, and supervision of site development staff executing regional and third party events, capital groundbreaking and grand openings, and other such activities. This role is highly collaborative and works closely with key national staff, regional Executive Directors and Development Managers, contractors and stakeholders to conceptualize, plan and execute successful events that engage constituents, meet revenue goals and serve as key acquisition tools for new annual giving.</b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>PRINCIPAL RESPONSIBILITIES:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Oversee annual event fundraising goal of $1,150,000 nationally, including both regional and national events.</li>
<li>Directly execute production and sales for national U.S.VETS Salute Gala, with $500K+ revenue annually.
</li>
<li>Manage Salute Gala including event design, timeline, logistics, venue, vendors, program and participants, including honorees, presenters and talent, and all revenue/expense related deliverables.</li>
<li>Responsible for annual Salute Gala revenue goals including sponsorship, table, ticket, ad, auction and appeal.</li>
<li>Lead national event strategy; develop processes and resources to support site Executive Directors and Development Managers in meeting revenue and cost of fundraising goals, improving process, increasing constituent engagement.</li>
<li>Partner with National Communications Manager to produce print/digital event collateral and correspondence, develop mailing lists, coordinate dissemination; support sites to ensure quality, branded event communications.</li>
<li>Coordinate with national communications and site development staff to produce event webpages, social media, eblasts and other advance marketing and on-site deliverables.</li>
<li>Manage gift administration associated with Salute Gala including management of online purchasing process, limited data entry, gift acknowledgment, pledge collection, and other related activities.</li>
<li>Manage event budgets including expenditures, check requests, deposits, invoicing; ensure timely reporting and debriefs on event revenue/expense for national, site and third-party events.</li>
<li>Negotiate national and site event and vendor contracts; ensure review and policy adherence of site contracts.</li>
<li>Identify and solicit in-kind donations, partnerships and advertising from local and national vendors.</li>
<li>Partner with individual and corporate philanthropy teams to ensure effective stewardship of event participants and volunteer leadership; manage event volunteers, support site event committee management.</li>
<li>Other duties as assigned.</li>
</ul> <p>Please send cover letter and resume to: lkapgan@usvetsinc.org Resumes without cover letters will not be considered.</p>
