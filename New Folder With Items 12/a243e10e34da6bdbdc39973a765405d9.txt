<b>Job Title: Account Executive, Marketing Cloud - HLS
</b></p>
<p></p>
<p>The Marketing Cloud HLS AE sells the full Marketing Cloud portfolio into the Healthcare Life Sciences vertical made up of the following segments:
</p>
<ul>
<li>Healthcare Providers
</li>
<li>Healthcare Payers
</li>
<li>Medical Device
</li>
<li>Pharma
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>The role:<br>
</b></p>
<p><br>
The Healthcare Life Sciences Account Executive creates, identifies and closes sales for the Marketing Cloud within a specific geographical region or set of defined accounts. As the individual who represents the Marketing Cloud and drives the revenue, this person must be a self-starting closer who can create a large pipeline of business within a short period of time and work with existing clients as well as new logo opportunities.
<br>
<br>
We look for business athletes who are aggressive team players - hungry, nimble and intelligent - with the ability and willingness to close a mix of complex enterprise and more transactional deals.
<br>
<br>
<b>Responsibilities
</b><br>
<br>
• Create and drive revenue within a specified region or list of defined accounts
<br>
• Generate business opportunities through professional networking and cold-calling
<br>
• Drive brand awareness, campaigns, and lead generation via networking, associations, etc.
<br>
• Meet and exceed all quarterly and annual sales quotas
<br>
• Own the sales cycle - from lead generation to closure
<br>
• Develop strategic territory business plan
<br>
• Maintain account and opportunity forecasting within our internal SFA system
<br>
• Generate leads from trade shows and regional networking events
<br>
• Ensure 100% customer satisfaction and retention
<br>
<br>
<b>We are looking for the following attributes:
</b><br>
<br>
• A proven sales hunter and closer.
</p>
<ul><li>5+ years of outside enterprise software sales experience
</li></ul>
<p>• Strong preference for Digital Marketing sales experience
</p>
<p>• Strong preference for Healthcare Life Sciences acumen
<br>
• Proven track record of sales excellence
<br>
• Knowledge of territory/accounts assigned preferred
<br>
• Be able to work independently &amp; as part of a team in a fast pace, rapid change environment
<br>
• Superior professional presence and business acumen
<br>
• Experience selling at the "C" level – CMO is a plus
</p>
