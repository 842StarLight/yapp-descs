<h2>New Business Associate</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">The New Business Associate is responsible for assisting in the execution of sales and product development strategies to launch and grow Alternative Investment Exchange (AIX), a start-up financial technology subsidiary of FS Investments. The Associate will work closely with the leadership team and the client-facing teams of AIX on business development efforts.
<br><br>
<b>The successful candidate is expected to:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Have a keen interest in technology.</li>
<li>Portray an understanding of the capital markets, the securities industry and alternative investments.</li>
<li>Learn, understand and professionally represent all AIX solutions across multiple distribution channels, keeping up-to-date with competing strategies and products.</li>
<li>Utilize the Consultative Selling Approach to help position AIX, coordinating the logistics involved in supporting the management of prospects through every aspect of running an enterprise sales process and building a pipeline of prospects.</li>
<li>Identify and help pursue leads generated from extensive research, referrals, ad campaigns and canvassing.</li>
<li>Exercise deliberate and thoughtful planning of scheduling, calendar management and multi-tasking of all sales calls, demonstrations, projects, and campaigns.</li>
<li>Leverage and utilize Salesforce at an expert level and maintain databases for accurate and current buy-side, sell-side and intermediary information.</li>
<li>Utilize technology and automation to orchestrate the build out of an enterprise sales process.</li>
</ul>
<b>Qualifications:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor's degree required.</li>
<li>3+ years of financial services experience, with preferred exposure to a sales support and/or portfolio reporting.</li>
<li>Knowledge of financial services distribution channels is required, including RIAs, Broker/Dealers and wire houses as well as investment sponsors.</li>
<li>Experience in Microsoft Office (Outlook, Excel, Word, and PowerPoint), Salesforce, and Webinar skills required.</li>
<li>Ability to create trust, credibility and rapport in-person and over the phone.</li>
<li>Strong communication skills and professional etiquette.</li>
<li>Strong organizational skills and the ability to manage multiple competing projects.</li>
<li>High standards for accuracy and work quality.</li>
</ul>
<b>About AIX:</b><br>
Alternative Investment Exchange LLC (AIX), a subsidiary of FS Investments, is headquartered in Philadelphia, PA.
<br><br>
The AIX platform is designed to improve the investment process and ownership experience for non-traded alternative investments.
<br><br>
AIX was built by a team with deep experience in servicing alternatives throughout the full lifecycle, and in supporting legal, regulatory and operational aspects of retail alternative accounts across multiple asset classes, structures and product types. We believe that establishing an industry standard supported by world-class technology will create value for the entire asset management industry.</div>
