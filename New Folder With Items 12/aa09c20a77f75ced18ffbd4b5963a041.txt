<h2>HR Coordinator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<div><div><div>
<div><i>Great opportunity to join the BSI Group America HR team during an exciting time as we implement Workday as part of our global HR Transformation. This role reports into the Sr. HRBP/Head of Americas HR supporting HRIS (currently SAP) and transition to Workday. This role also involves a variety of HR responsibilities coordination with the HR team.</i></div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div><b>
Essential Responsibilities</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
Ensures employee records and files follow current Human Resources policies and guidelines.</li>
<li>
Responsible for managing employment verification process (E-Verify)</li>
<li>
Identifies opportunities for improving Human Resources processes through collaboration with HR &amp; Finance leaders prior to implementing information system changes</li>
<li>
Develops and runs Ad-hoc reports on a routine basis for HR and business leaders utilizing SAP queries, ADP, Workday, and Microsoft excel</li>
<li>
Prepares data and reports for US &amp; Canada government compliance reporting such as EEO-1, Affirmative Action Plan and Canada Pension reporting</li>
<li>
Manages data integrity &amp; confidentiality in company-wide HR System (SAP HR, Web Benefits Design, ADP HR, various vendor sites). Routinely performs audit of data accuracy and consistency<ul><li>
Maintains quality and consistency of HRIS database information specifically the Organization Management (OM) and Personnel Development/Management (PA) SAP modules</li></ul>
</li>
<li>
Updates and maintains the US &amp; Canada HR Intranet site to streamline information distribution and availability
</li>
<li>Coordinates HRIS upgrades, changes, customization and tests, implements, maintains the HRIS database</li>
<li>
Works with Rewards Specialist to coordinate annual open enrollment, including info sessions and answers employee benefit questions via phone, email or chat</li>
<li>
Liaises among IT, Human Resources, Recruitment, Payroll and Finance in support of Human Resources Operations</li>
<li>
Maintains the Service Award database and provides milestones information to Human Resources Business Partners</li>
<li>
Supports the HR team with monthly induction/orientation and onboarding programs</li>
</ul>
