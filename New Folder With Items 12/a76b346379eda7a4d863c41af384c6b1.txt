<h2>Product Marketing Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>Dell provides the technology that transforms the way we all work and live. But we are more than a technology company — we are a people company. We inspire, challenge and respect every one of our over 100,000 employees. We also provide them with unparalleled growth and development opportunities. We can’t wait for you to discover this for yourself as a <b>Product Marketing Manager</b> on our<b> ISG Marketing Strategy &amp; Operations </b>team in <b>Round Rock, TX, Hopkinton, MA or, Seattle, WA.</b><br>
<br>
As a global business with game-changing products, there’s always scope for Dell to grow sales and expand market share. So a highly skilled Product Marketing team is essential. Our team has real vision. They develop and implement strategic approaches to solutions and technology marketing that put our products and services at the forefront of our customers’ minds. Always ready to adjust strategies in response to market change and competition, the team works closely with sales, product partners and vendors. Crucially, their remit goes beyond the customer value proposition to making sure product development is informed by customer insights.<br>
</p>
<p><b>
Responsibilities:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Supports marketing efforts for flexible consumption offers across our Infrastructure, infrastructure-as-a-Service (IaaS) and high value workload solutions</li>
<li>
Develops marketing content, positioning, messaging, sales training modules and marketing materials for offer</li>
<li>
Manages product launches, core positioning, messaging, sales training, competitive battlecards and marketing programs for assigned offer</li>
<li>
Develops value proposition and core messaging works cross functionally to integrate it into related offers and campaign materials, where appropriate</li>
<li>
Drives and represents Dell EMC presence at key events and tradeshows</li>
<li>
Drafts and Participate in blogger updates to drive Dell EMC’s thought leadership, share of voice and influence industry/trade coverage and improve competitive awareness</li>
<li>
Assists with defining programmatic elements of offer like sales and partner incentives works to integrate these into sales training as appropriate</li>
<li>
Assesses market requirements, benchmarks competition to understand market and help shape a favorable point of view on our offers</li>
<li>
Collaborate with a diverse set of teams across Dell EMC, including professional services, partner/channel, and financial services to create an end-to-end marketing message and to develop effective strategies and programs to meet functional and cross-functional goals</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Requirements:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
2-5 years of experience in product marketing / product management for infrastructure products (server, storage, solutions)</li>
<li>
Experience developing marketing content like infographics, blogs, white papers, website content and social media copy for variety of stakeholders - sales, customers, partners, demand gen, press, analysts</li>
<li>
Demonstrated ability to create compelling yet simple messaging from complicated technical concepts</li>
<li>
Experience of creating enablement for and working closely with enterprise sales teams</li>
<li>
Ability to work collaboratively and influence across multiple functions and teams within the company</li>
<li>
Bachelor's degree required; MBA or advanced technical degree is a plus (not required)</li>
</ul>
<p><br>
<b>
Desirable Requirements
</b></p>
<ul><li>Subject market expertise and product marketing experience in flexible consumption models like metered usage, leasing etc. and cloud models such as Infrastructure-as-a-Service (IaaS), Containers-as-a-Service (CaaS), Platform-as-a-Service (PaaS) etc.</li></ul>
<p></p>

We offer highly competitive salaries, bonus programs, world-class benefits, and unparalleled growth and development opportunities — all to create a compelling and rewarding work environment.<br>
