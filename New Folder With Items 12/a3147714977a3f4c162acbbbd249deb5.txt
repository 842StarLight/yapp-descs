<h2>Professional Development Coordinator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div></div>
<div>
<p>Cambium Learning® Group is an award-winning educational technology solutions leader dedicated to helping all students reach their potential through individualized and differentiated instruction. Using a research-based, personalized approach, Cambium Learning Group delivers SaaS resources and instructional products that engage students and support teachers in fun, positive, safe and scalable environments. These solutions are provided through Learning A-Z® (online differentiated instruction for elementary school reading, writing and science), ExploreLearning® (online interactive math and science simulations and a math fact fluency solution), Voyager Sopris Learning® (blended solutions that accelerate struggling learners to achieve in literacy and math and professional development for teachers), and VKidz Learning (online comprehensive homeschool education and programs for literacy and science). We believe that every student has unlimited potential, that teachers matter, and that data, instruction, and practice are the keys to success in the classroom and beyond.</p>
<p>The Professional Development Coordinator will provide administrative and coordination assistance to ExploreLearning’s professional development team.</p>
<b>Essential Job Functions:</b>
<p>The administrative coordinator will be responsible for:<br>
Workshop Fulfillment</p>
<ul>
<li>Communicating with Area Implementation Managers on training needs</li>
<li>Developing positive working relationships with implementation site contacts</li>
<li>Scheduling the fulfillment of assigned PD events with consideration for: evaluation of event needs, identifying a qualified instructor, and after-event follow-up with site contact</li>
<li>Maintaining a department calendar in collaboration with the team of PD Coordinators</li>
<li>Maintaining department procedural documentation, in collaboration with the team of PD Coordinators</li>
<li>Assigning Certified Instructors (CIs) and preparing event assignment paperwork</li>
<li>Creating credentials reports and assigning them to CIs for trainings</li>
<li>Posting pre- and post-correspondence related to workshop events</li>
<li>Exporting workshop evaluation reports</li>
</ul>
<p>Supporting Customer Relations</p>
<ul>
<li>Providing administrative support to Implementation Managers including, but not limited to, preparing data and sending correspondence to client on behalf of Implementation Manager</li>
<li>Maintaining professional development records for each customer</li>
<li>Communicating with Sales and Customer Service Departments to facilitate a unified customer experience</li>
</ul>
<p>Certified Instructors, Implementation Coordinators, and Consultants</p>
<ul>
<li>Assisting, as requested, with the CI on-boarding process</li>
<li>Maintaining the PD staff and CI database</li>
<li>Helping support CIs when they are on assignment</li>
<li>Maintaining communication with all CIs</li>
<li>Providing workshop feedback to CIs</li>
</ul>
<b>Additional Job Functions</b>
<p>Other tasks as the need arises and according to the schedule and talents of the administrative assistant.</p>
<b>Required Qualifications:</b>
<ul>
<li>Previous experience as an administrative assistant or coordinator</li>
<li>Excellent computer skills: Microsoft Office Suite, Google Drive</li>
<li>Highly organized and/or possess excellent project management skills</li>
<li>Excellent oral and written communication skills</li>
<li>Team player</li>
<li>Ability to fulfill assigned tasks with minimal supervision</li>
<li>Excellent interpersonal and customer relations skills</li>
</ul>
<b>Preferred Skills and Abilities:</b>
<ul>
<li>Associates or Bachelor’s degree</li>
<li>Previous experience working in the K-12 education field</li>
<li>Previous experience working with SalesForce</li>
</ul>
