<div><b>Position Overview</b></div>
<div>
KPA has an immediate opening for enthusiastic sales professionals with a desire to continue to grow in their success. The Account Executive is a strategic member of the sales team and is responsible for hunting, identifying, nurturing and closing new software sales opportunities.</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
The role is focused on selling KPA’s EH&amp;S and Insurance software portfolio into the SMB, and enterprise sized business market. KPA software serves as the central hub of activity for growing compliance, safety, and insurance departments seeking an adaptable, quick-to-implement solution featuring built-in best practices that accelerate business processes for compliance, insurance, and risk professionals.</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
This is a challenging, exciting role that will test your sales skills, strategic thinking capabilities, and ability to remain focused and follow through on plans.</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div><b><i>
More specifically, you will:</i></b></div>
<ul>
<li>Hunt within a defined geographic territory to identify new prospects</li>
<li>Build a pipeline that is 3x annual quota target</li>
<li>Meet monthly and quarterly sales bookings targets by effectively managing the full sales-cycle, including contract generation and other deliverables required to close new business</li>
<li>Actively engage with client executives at all levels to facilitate deal closure</li>
<li>Prioritize opportunities and coordinate your internal team to provide the best client experience</li>
<li>Develop and execute a comprehensive territory plan</li>
<li>Use Salesforce.com to continuously update and maintain active deals for pipeline management, daily activity and sales forecasting</li>
<li>Rapidly become proficient in the value proposition of KPA’s offering and demonstrate the ability to deliver that message in person, via webinar and by phone</li>
<li>Actively participate in team meetings, share best practices and maintain a positive, team player attitude – motivating others as necessary</li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
</div>
<div><b>
Requirements</b></div>
<ul>
<li>5+ years of experience in B2B software sales, preferably with SaaS experience</li>
<li>Consistent over-achievement in past quota carrying experiences</li>
<li>Aggressive “hunter” mentality and adept in using creative efforts in business development process</li>
<li>Highly effective communication skills that convey professionalism in all written, verbal, and virtual meetings</li>
<li>Proficient working with Salesforce.com, MS Office suite including Outlook, Word, Excel and PowerPoint</li>
<li>Ability to learn quickly and be a self-starter</li>
<li>Proven ability to work in a goal-driven environment</li>
<li>Strong business ethics</li>
<li>Positive attitude and a team player</li>
</ul>
