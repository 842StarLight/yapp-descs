<h2>Sr Manager Corporate Strategy</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Job Location: Hershey, PA</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><br>
<br>
<b>Summary</b>:<br>
</p>
<p>Strategy is a critical function at the Hershey Company, responsible for shaping enterprise-wide, business unit and functional strategies. Our Strategy team acts as a critical advisor to the Global Leadership Team by framing key choices, providing a well-reasoned point of view, and ensuring choices are made and effectively translated into action.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>As a member of the team, the Senior Manager, Strategy is both a general management position and an individual contributor role that has responsibility for developing short and long-term strategies to drive sales, share, and profitability across Hershey. The job requires conceptual and strategic thinking, business acumen, judgement and leadership as well as a highly developed set of analytical, financial modeling, and project management skills.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>The Senior Manager of Strategy will support Enterprise Strategy initiatives, particularly focusing on the three-year Strategic Planning cycle to ensure the company is achieving the optimal equilibrium that balances financial obligations and strategic aspirations, short-term imperatives and long-term goals, and the strengthening of competencies with the building of capabilities, all grounded in the realities of our complex and evolving external environment.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>The Senior Manager will gain exposure across the company and will join a driven, creative, and enthusiastic team focused on positioning Hershey for its next wave of growth.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Major Duties and Responsibilities</b>:</p>
<ul>
<li>Support development and implementation of 3-year strategic plan for the enterprise.</li>
<li>Provide thought-leadership and test plans for coherence and comprehensiveness</li>
<li>Ensure plans satisfy financial and strategic aspirations and deliver shareholder value</li>
<li>Reconcile plans across regional and functional imperatives</li>
<li>Ensure clarity and commitment to the plans across levels in the organization</li>
<li>Proactively support and lead other enterprise strategy initiatives including but not limited to the following for the office of the CGO and CEO:</li>
<li>Enterprise strategy scenario development</li>
<li>Category and market entry strategies</li>
<li>Mergers, acquisitions, and divestments</li>
<li>Capability, cost, and risk optimization</li>
<li>Customer and competitive analyses</li>
<li>External landscape assessments</li>
<li>Mergers &amp; Acquisition</li>
<li>Lead category Priority/Analysis</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Conduct Pre-Due Diligence Retail Analysis and strategy work</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Minimum knowledge, skills and abilities required to successfully perform major duties/responsibilities</b>:</p>
<ul>
<li>Ability to develop clear and concise senior level communications.</li>
<li>Ability to synthesize large sets of data from multiple sources, formulate/validate hypotheses and make business recommendations.</li>
 <li>Ability to analyze business processes and to recommend and implement process improvements.</li>
<li>Comfort level in facilitating and leading projects that are cross functional in scope.</li>
<li>Strong understanding of financial metrics and P&amp;L fundamentals.</li>
<li>Ability to develop working relationships across functional areas of the organization.</li>
<li>Proven Quantitative and Analytical skills.</li>
<li>Fluency with Excel and PowerPoint.</li>
<li>Orientation towards strategic thinking.</li>
<li>Strong desire for personal and professional development.</li>
<li>Ability to multi-task in a fast-paced environment.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Minimum Education and Experience Requirements</b>:</p>
<ul>
<li>Minimum 8 years progressive professional experience</li>
<li>Experience in roles requiring balanced profile across strategy, finance, and operations</li>
<li>International business experience preferred:</li>
<li>BS/BA degree required. MBA Preferred.</li>
<br>
</ul>
