<h2>Fulfillment Specialist (Englewood, NJ)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div></div>
<div>
<div>
<p>This team member evaluates the fulfillment needs of the sales force and operates independently to ensure those needs are met. This role works across multiple departments and sales areas to ensure on time and accurate order fulfillment. They will serve as the main point of contact for internal field sales teams for all aspects of order fulfillment.</p>
<p><b>Essential Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Maintains direct contact with sales team throughout the entire surgery transaction to ensure that fulfillment is met on the front end and that ordered items are appropriately assigned to the next transaction</li>
<li>Serves as liaison between sales and internal teams to support production planning and assists in developing logistics plans and procedures</li>
<li>Assigns budget dollars to the scheduling of third-party logistics</li>
<li>Establishes best practices and drives process efficiencies to increase revenue within their assigned geography</li>
<li>Supports sales team by developing and maintaining positive customer relations with internal and external clients/customers, which can substantially affect service and/or product revenue(s)</li>
<li>Understands quality and regulatory policies to ensure proper complaint handling and reporting</li>
<li>Resolves unexpected scheduling and shipping issues by changing shipping and distribution schedules to ensure proper order fulfillment</li>
<li>Organizes and implement procedures related to the flow of materials, products, services and associated systems information</li>
<li>Duties involve but are not limited to; update supply chain to ensure proper product planning, inventory control by ensuring proper inventory velocity, awareness of critical parts availability, proper material handling, shipment of products, and third-party logistics</li>
<li>Applies principles, concepts, practices, and standards of a professional field to complete a variety of on-going assignments and projects</li>
<li>Normally receives general instructions on routine work, detailed instructions on new projects or assignments</li>
<li>Works on problems of moderate scope; exercises judgment and independently identifies next steps</li>
<li>Analyzes quantitative information and applies professional judgment to resolve a variety of moderately complex problems</li>
<li>Builds productive internal/external relationships; actively represents an area in inter-departmental projects</li>
<li>Impacts team effectiveness through the quality of own work and potentially the work of others</li>
</ul>
</div>
<div><b>Basic Qualifications</b></div>
<div><ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree or equivalent experience</li>
<li>2-4 years in order processing, account management, operations or customer support</li>
</ul></div>
<div><b>Preferred Qualifications</b></div>
<div><ul>
<li>Strong aptitude for working with numbers and reports</li>
<li>Demonstrated ability to interface with salespeople, all levels of management and experience working across multiple functional groups required</li>
<li>Excellent verbal and written communication skills</li>
<li>Excellent interpersonal skills</li>
<li>Must be proficient in Microsoft Office including Word, Excel, and PowerPoint</li>
<li>Experience in SAP</li>
<li>Requires a broad knowledge of the organization’s services and products</li>
<li>Requires in-depth knowledge of the sales cycle</li>
<li>Must be very detail and process-oriented</li>
<li>Strong analytical and problem-solving skills</li>
<li>Excellent time management skills, service-oriented, and self-directed</li>
</ul></div>
