<h2>Suppression Territory Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<div>
<div><b>What you will do</b></div>
<div></div>
<div>You will be responsible for suppression operations and sales of a geographic area of Johnson Controls. You will have P&amp;L responsibility for Suppression in Regional geographic area. You will drive standardized field and sales processes in an enterprising way. You will be responsible for setting financial goals &amp; creating and managing budgets. You will drive continuous growth through partnering with Area leadership structure and functional teams.</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div><b>How you will do it</b></div>
<div></div>
<ul>
<li>Build, hire, coach, develop and align a high-performing team.</li>
<li>
Be committed to developing the skills and talents of people.</li>
<li>
Review and monitor field / sales performance, and refine strategies as required in discussion within the area.</li>
<li>
Reallocate resources to improve overall results as needed.</li>
<li>
Contribute to the development of marketing, customer retention, advertising, pricing, and distribution strategies for the area.</li>
<li>
Support the day-to-day activities of Territory Suppression leadership in order to implement the organization’s overall field &amp; sales strategy for an assigned region</li>
<li>
Ensure the field &amp; sales resources receives coaching and training on the technical aspects of the organization’s products and services; on marketing campaigns and sales promotions; and on delivery sales techniques, procedures, and standards that will help them achieve their targets.</li>
<li>
Ensure Delivery of field performance and sales channel(s) strategic initiatives are executed in accordance with enterprise goals and area budgets.</li>
<li>
Manage performance against plan for the assigned direct reports.</li>
<li>
Directs subordinates to complete assignments using established guidelines, procedures, and policies.</li>
<li>
Performs other related duties as assigned</li>
</ul>
<br>
</div>
<p></p>
<div>
<b>Qualifications</b><div>
</div>
<div><b>
What we look for</b></div>
<div></div>
<ul>
<li>Strong suppression background with experience in the day to day operations of the suppression business.</li>
<li>
Requires good interpersonal skills, along with effective writing, speaking and presentation skills.</li>
<li>
Demonstrated management/leadership abilities to include training, team building, presentation and negotiation skills, branch administration and effective interaction with customer/company management</li>
<li>
Managing in an ever changing environment. Skills include leadership, planning, developing policies, delegation, and tracking performance against measurable goals.</li>
<li>
Strong project management background. Experience overseeing all aspects of the project, including planning, resource allocation, and budget.</li>
<li>
Capable of staffing, structuring, and maintaining a productive team of skilled employees.</li>
<li>
Growth-oriented leader with proven track record of success</li>
<li>
Knowledge of applicable local, state and federal codes required.</li>
<li>
Computer fluency in Windows, Excel, and other Microsoft products</li>
</ul>
