<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Overview
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<p><b>Business Learning Advisor: CompuCom</b></p>
<p>
Position Overview: A Business Learning Advisor (BLA) is a trusted “go-to” learning specialist and guide who partners with business leaders and HR professionals to deliver performance results using a deep knowledge of learning theory and practice. The BLA for CompuCom will evaluate business needs and people capabilities and work with internal subject matter experts to create, administer, and deliver training programs.</p>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<p><b>Roles/Responsibilities:</b></p>
<p>
Partner with HR Business Partners and Business Leaders to assess and solve for business needs through interviews, learning needs analyses, and culture surveys.</p>
<p>
Look at total CompuCom organization and align their business priorities and people capabilities to the greater Office Depot strategy.</p>
<p>
Align and integrate development solutions to strategic business objectives and ensuring a 70/20/10 approach to deliverables.</p>
<p>
Consult and activate learning plans using evidence-based, researched modalities leveraging subject matter experts within CompuCom or team resources to design and create training programs and learning materials. Facilitate as needed.</p>
<p>
Determine training solutions, methods, and materials, and choose those that best fit CompuCom business needs and requirements—this could include evaluating 3rd-party training resources and reviewing <i>current</i> internal programs to make edits for effectiveness.</p>
<p>
Perform administrative tasks such as monitoring costs, scheduling classes, setting up systems and equipment, and coordinating enrollment</p>
<p>
Monitor instructors, guide employees through software-based instruction, or facilitate informal or collaborative learning programs.</p>
</div></div></div>
