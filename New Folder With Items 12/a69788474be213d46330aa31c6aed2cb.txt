<h2>Executive Assistant (#11E-19)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>ESSENTIAL JOB FUNCTIONS</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Facilitate and perform administrative functions in support of meetings of Company President as assigned. Support activities include timely collection and preparation of draft agendas; prioritizing items in consultation with Company President to finalize agenda; dissemination of meeting materials; coordination of meeting records, evaluations and follow-up minutes</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Schedule, arrange and coordinate meetings, conference calls, conference rooms, catering, appointments, interviews and manage calendar for Company President</li>
<li>Draft documents, letters, internal memos, emails, PowerPoint presentations and other items of communication as directed by the President</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Reviews, sorts, and distributes incoming correspondence, including faxes. Reads and routes incoming mail; files correspondence and other records.</li>
<li>Prepares outgoing mail</li>
<li>Assists in the preparation of required documents for domestic and international statutory requirements, e.g., Board meetings, audit letters, customer mailings</li>
<li>Manages distributor liability insurance certificates and requests</li>
<li>Answers telephone, provides information to callers or routes call to appropriate individual, and places outgoing calls</li>
<li>Greets visitors, ascertains nature of business, and conducts visitors to employer or appropriate person.</li>
<li>Maintains Company patent, trademark, and legal records and facilitates electronic filing of applications and renewals; supports Patent Attorney will all legal related duties</li>
<li>Maintains docket with status of all cases, schedules of payment of annuities, reviews and maintains schedule of or renewals one year in advance. Will also obtain supporting documents</li>
<li>Interacts and communicates with outside legal firms. Will handle legal correspondence/invoices</li>
<li>Assists Company President with projects as needed; ensures project and task deadlines are tracked and met</li>
<li>Acts as custodian of corporate documents and records.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p>
</p>
<ul>
<li>Enters and maintains data into Management Operating System (MOS) dashboard/document</li>
<li>Maintains cashbook</li>
<li>Participates in sales support activities and needs such as, sales meetings, mailings, etc.</li>
<li>Serves as notary public for company-related documents</li>
<li>Travels between various company locations</li>
<li>Exhibits effective communication skills by demonstrating tact, diplomacy and discretion when presenting information and responding to questions from all levels of the organization.</li>
<li>Ensures strict confidentiality of all information as required.</li>
<li>Utilizes judgement and initiative to problem solve issues related to administrative functions and implement effective solutions, on behalf of the Company President</li>
<li>Excellent organizational skills with an ability to think proactively and prioritize work</li>
<li>Maintain reliable and consistent attendance, including being punctual, and dependable in order to meet the needs of the department and the organization.</li>
<li>Execute each essential duty satisfactorily in order to perform job successfully.</li>
<li>Follows all safety procedures required in work area, wears PPE as needed, attends all safety meetings, and reports safety issues regarding equipment or unsafe/hazardous conditions.</li>
<li>Performs effectively as a team member, able to work well with others, open to receive and give feedback, and treats everyone with respect.</li>
<li>Takes ownership of own work and behavior, accepts accountability for own actions, encourages solutions, and communicates status of work/projects.</li>
<li>Follow all department quality standards/criteria. Raise concerns and issues to immediate manager.</li>
<li>Able to understand and demonstrate Cambro company culture, display company core values (Safety, Quality, Respect, and Service).</li>
<li>Understands department’s key performance indicators and contributes to achieve these goals both individually and as a team.</li>
<li>Other duties as needed or required.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>ADDITIONAL RESPONSIBILITIES</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Must be able to work overtime as needed, remain flexible and open to possible schedule changes in order to meet business needs.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>REQUIRED QUALIFICATIONS</b></p>
<p>
The requirements listed below are representative of the knowledge, skill, and/or ability required. Reasonable accommodations may be made to enable individuals with disabilities to perform the essential functions.</p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor's degree (B.A.) from four-year college or university; or two to three years’ related experience and/or training; or equivalent combination of education and experience required.</li>
<li>Eight or more years’ experience in supporting a top-level executive or team of executives required.</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Excellent organizational and time management skills required.</li>
<li>Ability to work in a team management system.</li>
<li>Ability to work under pressure, multitask and prioritize daily workload.</li>
<li>Ability to interact with staff at all levels of the organization.</li>
<li>Skill in operating standard office equipment.</li>
<li>Exceptional computer knowledge including familiarity with the Internet, MS Word, Excel, Outlook and Power Point.</li>
<li>Full comprehension of office management systems and procedures. Ability to follow oral and written instructions.</li>
<li>Ability to make arithmetical computations.</li>
<li>Ability to establish and maintain effective working relationships and maintain a professional demeanor in all situations.</li>
<li>Ability to withhold confidential information.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>PREFERRED QUALIFICATIONS
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Five or more years of U.S. and foreign patent secretary / paralegal experience preferred.</li>
<li>Experience with Concur or other travel-related software preferred.</li>
<li>Ability and willingness to become a California Public Notary</li>
</ul>
