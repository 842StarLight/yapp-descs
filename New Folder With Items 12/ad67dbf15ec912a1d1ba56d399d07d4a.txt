<p><b>About Etheric Networks</b></p>
<p>
Etheric Networks is in its 16th year of business in the Bay Area. We are an Internet Service Provider with the largest coverage area of any local ISP, with a network of fixed-wireless towers throughout the Bay Area, from SF to Walnut Creek in the North down to Hollister and Salinas in the South. In addition, Etheric Networks operates its own dark fiber ring, allowing us to deliver the lowest latency and highest capacity internet access of any Bay Area Operator. Etheric is planning to expand to many more California cities and counties in the near future.</p>
<p><b>
About Etheric Networks</b></p>
<p>
We are seeking a Marketing &amp; Public Relations Manager/Analyst who is enthusiastic, extroverted and capable. You will analyze market data to determine the best methods of getting the word out to the Bay Area Businesses and Residential users most likely to benefit from our unique differentiators. Namely: Low latency precision-engineered internet access, delivered to your building more rapidly than our competitors, with very high quality 7 x 24 support. Locally owned and operated, dedicated to the users’ Internet privacy, not blocked, surveilled or otherwise interfered with. As a result of these principles, our latency is much lower than the cable or telephone companies internet services. Additionally, Etheric’s contracts are simple and friendly, paving the way for smooth customer acceptance.</p>
<p>
The Marketing &amp; Public Relations Manager will manage outreach to our current customers, ensuring they are kept informed of our latest technological breakthroughs and undertakings, as well as to show our care and appreciation for the people who make our network practice possible.</p>
<p><b>
The primary responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Lead Generation</li>
<li>Branding</li>
<li>Working to ensure sales converts marketing campaign leads efficiently</li>
<li>Analyzing marketing effectiveness and constantly improving upon it</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Technical Aspects:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Develop Marketing and Advertising campaigns. Video, Text, Audio, and Graphics.</li>
<li>Improve our products by adding features and analyzing pricing.</li>
<li>Conceive, set up, and execute tests using experimental design methods</li>
<li>Measure the effectiveness of all communications and other direct marketing channels</li>
<li>Make data-driven recommendations and decisions that optimize channel performance</li>
<li>Work with vendors and internal teams to ensure data integrity</li>
<li>Develop and maintain business requirements for new automated marketing reports</li>
<li>Create, produce and publish videos/webinars/decks for marketing, sales, and training for target end-users</li>
<li>Manage email and direct mail campaign deployment</li>
<li>Develop business logic to create lists for email and direct mail campaigns</li>
</ul>
<p>In addition to using your analytical expertise, you’ll be responsible for Campaign Management from idea to execution of video, email, direct mail, and other direct marketing campaigns – writing creative briefs, and proofreading content. This position will not only employ your analytical skills, but it will also allow you to use your creative mind while executing each step in the process from concept design to campaign analysis.</p>
<p>
To be successful in this role you must be a strategic analytical thinker who is able to do independent analysis and provide recommendations to improve the bottom line as well as have an interest in the creative side of marketing. The ideal candidate must be technically proficient in manipulating databases and reports, have strong experience and a keen eye for completing projects independently while meeting deadlines.</p>
