<h2>Real Estate Marketing Coordinator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Are you ready to take your marketing career to the next level? We have an exciting opportunity for a Marketing Coordinator to join our growing real estate team.</p>
<p>
The ideal candidate will have a growth mindset, and a well-developed capacity for coordinating and implementing marketing projects with responsibilities in advertising, brand stewardship, special events management and social media. They will have command of trends in social media and print marketing, enjoy being creative, and understand how to build and convert a digital audience into adoring clients.</p>
<p>
How’s your grammar? In addition to marketing, events, and social media related work, this position will also write, proof and edit copy. Exceptional attention to detail is a “must have” for the right candidate. They will also share a role in maintaining collateral and marketing materials inventory; maintaining databases; and preparing reports.</p>
<p>
We are a hardworking, tight knit, welcoming team; all working toward a common goal of being the top real estate group, while providing outstanding service to our clients. If you are looking for an opportunity with growth potential, have a great work ethic and a passion for leading digital and traditional marketing in a very fast-paced entrepreneurial environment, we want to hear from you.</p>
