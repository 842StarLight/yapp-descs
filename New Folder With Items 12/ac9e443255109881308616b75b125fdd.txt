<h2>Enterprise Sales Development Representative</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<b>Description:</b><br>
<b>WHO WE ARE HIRING:</b><br>
As an Enterprise Sales Development Representative you will be responsible for generating and a high-quality pipeline of new business for our Enterprise Account Executives. BirdEye is a high growth Silicon Valley SaaS company and a leader in the small business category that is rapidly expanding into the large enterprise space. As a member of this team you will be in a high stakes, high visibility position, helping to develop and scale our go-to-market strategy along-side senior leadership.
<br><br>
Sitting at the top of our sales funnel, you will have the opportunity to create a stellar first impression with prospective customers and communicate how our platform could drive valuable outcomes for their business. By using a comprehensive sales technology suite, you will identify, research, prospect, and qualify new leads to pass to our Account Executive team.
<br><br>
You will be a part of a collaborative sales culture dedicated to innovation and the development of its team members, while maintaining a fun and rewarding atmosphere. As a valuable contributor to the SDR team, you will have growth opportunities into more senior sales positions and even leadership.
<br><br>
<b>Requirements:</b><br>
<b>WHO YOU ARE:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>2+ years experience in customer service, sales, or business development, in any industry</li>
<li>You have a bachelor’s degree or equivalent practical experience</li>
<li>Relentless drive to learn, achieve your goals, and be promoted</li>
<li>Excellent written &amp; verbal communication skills</li>
<li>Highly organized and able to adapt to changing priorities</li>
<li>Exceptional listening skills combined with the ability to synthesize information</li>
<li>Experience working with Salesforce or any other CRM, a plus</li>
<li>Sharp business acumen and experience engaging with senior-level decision-makers</li>
<li>Experience in using LinkedIn and other social media channels</li>
<li>Outstanding relationship building skills with a high degree of responsiveness and integrity</li>
</ul>
<b>WHAT YOU’LL DO:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Create a lasting and positive first impression of BirdEye for our prospects</li>
<li>Become an expert at articulating our value proposition to anyone who asks (or who doesn’t ask)</li>
<li>Create successful cold calling and email campaigns targeting new prospects</li>
<li>Partner with our account executive team to help generate meetings, build sales pipeline and drive deals</li>
<li>Discover new leads through internet research and social media monitoring/messaging, using tools such as LinkedIn Navigator</li>
<li>Manage prospecting status, data integrity, and forecasting in Salesforce</li>
<li>Identifying champions and key stakeholders within target accounts</li>
<li>Meet all key performance metrics and goals on a weekly and monthly basis</li>
</ul>

<ul>
<li>A competitive salary</li>
<li>A rich benefits package including medical, dental, and vision</li>
<li>401(k) plan</li>
<li>Stocked kitchen complete with coffee, snacks, and Friday lunch catered</li>
<li>Flexible work hours</li>
<li>All of the opportunities inherent in a fast-growing organization!</li>
</ul>
<b>WHO WE ARE:</b><br>
BirdEye is the leading customer experience and business reputation platform, reimagining the way reviews are used to better understand customers, improve operations, and increase revenues. More than 50,000 businesses across all industries trust BirdEye to accelerate their growth through improved customer experience and review management. BirdEye’s all-in-one software includes review monitoring, review generation, review marketing, customer surveys, social listening, social publishing, social engagement, ticketing for customer support, listings, webchat, customer surveys, actionable insights, and competitive benchmarking. With BirdEye, companies can put customers at the core of every business decision, turning feedback into insights, insights into action, and customer happiness into revenue.
<br><br>
Founded in 2012, BirdEye is headquartered in Silicon Valley and led by alumni from Google, Amazon and Yahoo. In 2019, BirdEye was ranked #60 in the “100 Best Software Companies” in the world by G2 Crowd. BirdEye is backed by Salesforce founder Marc Benioff, Yahoo co-founder Jerry Yang, Trinity Ventures and World Innovation Lab.
