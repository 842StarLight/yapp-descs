<h2>Network Northeast Regional Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>This Associate Manager will report to the Data Voice and Video (DV2) East, Program Direct, and Classified Network Manager. This manager is responsible for the daily operations, service delivery and management of Unclassified Network Data staff supporting locations in the Northeastern region.<br>
<br>
This position manages the planning, implementation, testing, documentation, and maintenance of enterprise-wide solutions to total system or subsystems using internally created and/or off the shelf products. Responsible for coordinating employee recruitment, selection and training, performance assessment, work assignments, salary, and coaching. Directs the analyses and identification of all or part of a company's existing or new peripheral, network, and telecommunications systems requirements, taking into consideration the special technology needs. Oversees the establishment of functional and technical specifications and standards, solves hardware/software interface problems, defines input/output parameters, and ensures integration of the entire system or subsystem.<br>
<br>
This position can be located at the Stratford or Moorestown Facilities; however, other locations in the Northeast will be considered.<br>
<br>
This position will require some travel within the Region.<br>
