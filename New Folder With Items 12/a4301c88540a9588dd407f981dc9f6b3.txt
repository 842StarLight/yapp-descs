<p>
Crown Health Care Laundry Services is the Industrial Laundry leader in the Southeast United States with locations in Georgia, South Carolina, Mississippi, Florida and Alabama. Due to tremendous growth, we are adding a Service Representative to our greater Tampa market team to help us service our hospital/health care customers in that region. If you excel at Customer Service and are able to strengthen current relationships, this may be a great job for you. The territory will include cities North, South, and West of Tampa.</p>
<p><b>
Some of the areas that will be covered by the Service Representative are: Brooksville, FL, Citrus, FL, Englewood, FL, and Lakeland, FL.</b></p>
<p><b>
Our Territory Customer Service Reps work at assigned customer locations (hospitals) to establish our marketplace presence, generate increased customer relations and resolve customer issues. They will interact with client personnel at various levels to help manage linen inventory, educate the client on linen management and any other related duties.</b></p>
<p>
Other duties include handling customer requests or issues, or communicate with appropriate individual to ensure that customer requests are consistently met in timely manner, maintaining exceptional records regarding service visits and issues, and identifying opportunities for improved service. Our Reps will work with all levels of the organization and must have exceptional professional presence. While you will spend the majority of your time at customer locations, you home base will be in Lakeland, FL.</p>
