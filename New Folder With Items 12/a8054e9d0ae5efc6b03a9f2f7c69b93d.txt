<h2>Account Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Are You a Great Communicator? Organized? Confident?</b></p>
<p>Do you love talking (and more importantly- listening) to people?</p>
<p>Are you very well organized and super attentive to details?</p>
<p>Are you confident talking to people you don’t know, both in person and over the phone?</p>
<p>If all this sounds like you, keep reading.</p>
<p><b>About us: </b></p>
<p>Victory Performance and Physical Therapy is a boutique performance and recovery studio in Culver City. We are hell-bent to put the “<i>care</i>” back into “<i>healthcare</i>”. We help people that are passionate about their health avoid surgery, stay off pain-meds, and keep active and mobile. We are not your average physical therapy clinic. We offer an over the top experience that people are not used to in healthcare.</p>
<p>We are growing and are looking for people just as passionate as we are about helping others get out of pain naturally, without using dangerous and addictive pain-meds or surgery.</p>
<p><b>About the role: </b></p>
<p>This is a full-time salaried (+bonuses) position. You will be responsible for growing the revenue of the business by:</p>
<ul>
<li>holding 15-20 minute phone conversations with inquiring clients determining whether these potential clients are the right fit for our practice</li>
<li>following up with potential clients that may not be ready to schedule at first contact</li>
<li>confidently overcoming objections</li>
<li>keeping clear and up-to-date records that allow us to analyze our marketing systems as well as your success in the role</li>
<li>scheduling new and current clients</li>
<li>you will be the liaison between inquiring leads and the next step in the potential client’s journey with us.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Traits of success for this role: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>
<i>Organized</i>: You keep lists, can multi-task, and understand how to prioritize what’s most important</li>
<li>
<i>Excellent Communication Skills</i>: You are great at talking, but even better at listening. You know how to make people open up to you.</li>
<li>
<i>Motivated</i>: You understand that the high standards you hold represent your own values. You will do everything you can so that goals are met.</li>
<li>
<i>Goal Oriented</i>: You set goals that push you to work your best, and you don’t give up until you are 100% satisfied with the work.</li>
<li>
<i>Fun</i>: You like to laugh, try new things, explore, and be around people that value the same</li>
<li>
<i>Problem Solver</i>: You love finding streamlined ways to make repetitive tasks more effective and efficient</li>
<li>
<i>Flexible</i>: You are not a 9-5’er and you are not someone who gets “stuck in their ways”</li>
<li>
<i>Learner</i>: You are coachable and are committed to self-improvement through learning on and off the clock</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Benefits: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Be part of a dynamic team that love to learn and have fun</li>
<li>Work in a supportive environment where personal development is always a top priority</li>
<li>Make a difference; we’re part of the solution to the opioid epidemic!</li>
<li>Starting salary year one 45-70k (including bonuses) + PTO + Medical + Dental</li>
</ul>
