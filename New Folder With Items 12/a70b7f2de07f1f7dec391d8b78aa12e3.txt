<h2>Staff Accountant</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>I. Job Overview and General Purpose</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Responsible for performing general ledger account and bank statement reconciliation, preparing journal entries and financial statements, creating and maintaining periodic reporting, maintaining fixed asset system, and assisting the Accounting Manager.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>II. Essential Duties and Responsibilities</b></h2>
<ul>
<li>Support the treasury function by preparing monthly bank reconciliations; preparing quarterly Collateral Schedules with supporting documentation; monitoring positive pay functions; issuing stop-payment on checks; and maintaining petty cash.</li>
<li>Support the accounting function by maintaining current capitalization and depreciation records within our fixed asset database; assisting with journal entries and maintaining journal entry records; monitoring personal use of company vehicles; oversight of the E-Z pass program and Corporate Lodging program; processing credit applications and credit reference activities.</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Support the finance function by carrying out Surety Bonding processes; oversight of the monthly Corporate credit card processes and procedures; processing Accounts Payable and related functions; various other tasks associated with AP/AR.</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Supports taxation and statutory functions by processing multi-state tax payments associated with real estate, property, sales and use, and business and occupational taxes; maintains multi-state business and contractor licenses; prepares annual pre-qualification and sub-contractor qualifications; prepares multi-state quarterly and semi-annual fuel tax refunds.</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Assist with annual audits by independent auditors and those by various state, federal and policy related audits</li>
<li>Serves as a role model of excellent customer service by responding constructively to the needs of all those he/she interacts with on a daily basis on behalf of the organization.</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Assure the security and confidentiality of all business records and employee personally identifiable information.</li>
<li>Demonstrates effective communication and problem-solving skills.</li>
<li>Other duties and responsibilities as assigned.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>III. Qualifications</b></p>
<p>

Ability to read, analyze, and interpret general business publications, relevant technical practices, and governmental instruction and regulations. Ability to write routine business correspondence. <p>
Ability to effectively manage and prioritize work between multiple departments while working under stringent time constraints.</p>

<p>
