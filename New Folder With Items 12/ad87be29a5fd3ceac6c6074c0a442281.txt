<b>JOB DESCRIPTION SUMMARY</b><br>
WPI is a leading provider of graduate education. Our students can opt for the traditional classroom setting, participate in a cohort at an onsite corporation location, or select our innovative online delivery platform. These options enable the working professional to incorporate WPI's acclaimed graduate education programs into their busy life.<br>
<br>
The Student Enrollment Specialist is responsible for enrolling part-time graduate students into WPI programs.<br>
<br>
<b>JOB DESCRIPTION</b><br>
<br>
<b>Responsibilities:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Accountable for recruiting new students into graduate programs. Emphasis will be on finding new leads and converting into graduate students.</li>
<li>
Responsible for developing and implementing measurable strategies/tactics for student enrollment.
</li>
<li>Work within a team of Sales, Marketing, Development and Operations to effectively build and execute a plan for achieving successful enrollment goals in graduate programs.</li>
<li>
Proactively promote educational solutions to achieve sales revenue goals.</li>
<li>
Responsible for attaining budget related revenue goals for sales by growing/retaining existing key accounts and support developing new accounts.</li>
<li>
Aggressively review account activities in pursuit of new students and utilize active listening skill to identify up-selling opportunities.</li>
<li>
Assist in developing written documents when necessary, including follow-up letters and internal communications.</li>
<li>
Coordinate mailings and email marketing with the marketing group to existing clients to generate corporate leads and the utilization of social networking tools.</li>
<li>
Work closely with the Institutional Engagement team to efficiently and effectively target, explore, qualify and transition new accounts.</li>
<li>
Special projects and other duties as assigned.</li>
</ul>
<br>
<b>
Requirements:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor's degree required.
</li>
<li>Two years + experience working in a sales environment, solution selling a plus.</li>
<li>
Strong persuasive skills and presentation skills with the ability to positively influence and motivate others.
</li>
<li>A dynamic, outgoing personality.</li>
<li>
Strong customer service orientation, creativity and problem solving skills.</li>
<li>
Ability to identify and articulate opportunities to increase existing accounts.</li>
<li>
Business development abilities.</li>
<li>
Excellent written, oral communication and listening skills.</li>
<li>
Strong multi-tasking, organizational, business and functional skills and knowledge.</li>
<li>
Ability to work independently and as part of a team.</li>
<li>
Experience in selling educational solutions and/or enrollment management preferred.</li>
<li>
Microsoft Office products and computer skills required, as well as familiarity with Salesforce or similar CRM tool.</li>
<li>
Ability to work evening hours and travel beyond New England required, possible overnights.</li>
<li>
Valid driver's license.</li>
<li>
Ability to lift up to 45lbs.</li>
<li>
Must be authorized to work in the United States.</li>
</ul>
