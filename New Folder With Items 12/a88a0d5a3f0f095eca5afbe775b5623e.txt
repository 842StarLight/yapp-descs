<h2>Small Business Sales Representative</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">Job Summary
<br>This position will be assigned an account base that will consist of small business accounts with an MRC of $1K and under in all Segra Enterprise Markets. The SMB Sales Representative will contribute to the success of the Enterprise Sales Team by adding new services, upgrading existing services, renewing and maintaining existing accounts. This position will also review opportunities for growth of higher level services. When the opportunity for growth exists, the representative will leverage the account to the appropriate Sales Director to pursue a Sales Opportunity. The SMB Sales Representative will also be responsible for marketing campaigns within their assigned market, as well as handling mailers and cold calling lists of prospects provided by our Marketing Team. This position will receive a compensation for transferring a lead to the outside sales team as well. The successful candidate enjoys interacting with customers over the phone or in person and has experience selling products and services via outbound calling.
<br><br>
Qualifications
<br><ul>
<li>Education: Bachelors preferred</li>
<li>Experience: Experience with telecom or technology sales preferred;</li>
<li>Key Competencies: Proven ability to sell products or services via phone required. Ability to navigate efficiently through multiple systems and databases.</li>
</ul>
We are proud to be an EEO/AA employer M/F/D/V. We maintain a drug-free workplace and perform pre-employment substance abuse testing.</div>
