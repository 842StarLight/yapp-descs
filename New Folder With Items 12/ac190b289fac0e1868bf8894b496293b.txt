<h2>Enterprise Sales Director</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>WHO WE ARE HIRING:</b></p>
<p>As the <b>Enterprise Sales Director</b>, you’ll manage a team and help them generate demand, interest, and excitement for BirdEye while creating a steady pipeline of business to arm the Enterprise Sales team. In this hands on role, you will have direct impact on growing BirdEye’s up market presence in the enterprise space.
</p>
<p>We are looking for a hands-on leader who develops and implements a comprehensive strategy that maximizes needs to drive revenue through managing direct sales. Masterful in client engagement, you will impact how your customers do business in an ever-changing environment.
</p>
<p><b>Requirements</b></p>
<p><b>
WHO YOU ARE:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>8+ years of solution sales experience selling to large, complex enterprise organizations within the Software (SaaS) space</li>
<li>5+ years managing and leading a sales team, team of Enterprise Account Executives
</li>
<li>Proven ability to create high performing teams and lead them to success</li>
<li>Demonstrated track record of consistent over achievement of quotas, targets and key metrics</li>
<li>Ability to build trust with multiple stakeholders at large national organizations and navigate large organizations with ease</li>
<li>Excellent communication, presentation, and organization skills with impeccable follow-through</li>
<li>Ability to operate effectively in a fast-paced, team environment</li>
<li>Consultative selling experience</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>WHAT YOU’LL DO:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Identification and research, to formalize a go to market strategy and create qualified Enterprise target accounts</li>
<li>Build and lead operational scale and excellence across the AE team</li>
<li>Develop and manage strong relationships within our Enterprise market segment, looking for opportunities to connect with organization’s key stakeholders and develop additional revenue</li>
<li>Forecast sales activity and revenue achievement, while creating satisfied and referenceable customers</li>
<li>Should have the vision and aptitude to grow the sales team, with a flexibility to work with the changing sales process and structure</li>
<li>Accurate reporting on sales activity and forecasting to senior sales management</li>
<li>Assists in the development of short, medium, and long-term plans to achieve strategic objectives</li>
<li>Regularly interacts across functional areas with senior management or executives to ensure objectives are met</li>
<li>Ability to influence thinking or gain acceptance of others in sensitive situations</li>
</ul>

<p><b>Our Company:</b></p>

BirdEye converts all feedback into actionable insights so businesses can engage in every step of the customer journey, establish a positive online presence, and benchmark performance across locations and against competitors.
</p>
