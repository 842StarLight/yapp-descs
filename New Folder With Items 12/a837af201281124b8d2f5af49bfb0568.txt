<h2>Spa Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Scents of Serenity Organic Spa is Richmond's only truly organic spa. Our philosophies are rooted in a holistic and organic approach to wellness, and we offer our clients a beautiful and peaceful atmosphere where they experience extraordinary service.</p>
<p>We are seeking a full-time Guest Services Manager who is truly passionate about wellness, holistic approaches, and customer care. The perfect candidate is a highly motivated individual with compassion and poise, and who is looking for long-term employment within a growing company.</p>
<p>Responsibilities include:</p>
<p>-Reports actively to Spa Manager and Owner<br>-Upholds a pleasant and supportive working environment for all SOS staff by leading by example<br>-Strong knowledge of spa services and products<br>-Provides feedback and coaching in a respectful manner to help all staff grow within their positions.<br>-Actively participates in daily operations by making reservations, checking clients in and out, various cleaning responsibilities, and follow up that staff members have completed their daily chores. May be assigned to fill in at call center, front desk, or spa attendant position, as needed.<br>-Supports staff by helping in any areas that may need assistance<br>-Ensures that appointments are scheduled in the most effective manner.<br>-Participates in social media and other marketing opportunities<br>-Creates inventive ideas to promote sales growth, and strives to exceed projected sales goals.<br>-Create healthy work relationship with all staff members, while maintaining managerial boundaries<br>-Ability to troubleshoot equipment in treatment rooms and scheduling software<br>-Assist Spa Manager with inventory maintenance and reports when there is a shortage or outage of supplies that may need to be ordered.<br>-Addresses any client complaints promptly and politely<br>-Ability to make executive decisions that benefit all parties included in the absence of the Owner or Spa Manager.</p>
<p>Desired Qualities:<br>-Minimum 2 years management experience, preferably in a spa or hospitality setting<br>-Excellent communication skills-both written and oral<br>-Impeccable phone etiquette<br>-Computer savvy. Knowledge of Mindbody software considered a plus<br>-Enthusiastic, go-getter attitude<br>-Positive self-esteem and professional image<br>-Strong team player, but also has the ability to work independently with little supervision.<br>-Attention to detail and strong organizational skills<br>-Ability to multitask in a fast paced environment<br>-Ability to improvise and problem solve<br>-Time management skills</p>
<p>This is an excellent opportunity for a Guest Services Manager who shares the same philosophies as Scents of Serenity Organic Spa, and who wants to provide outstanding customer service in a beautiful, peaceful, and positive working environment.</p>
<p>If this sounds like you, please submit a cover letter, resume, and salary request to info @ scentsofserenityspa.com</p>
