<p><b>ABOUT WALMART</b></p>
<p>
This Is That Place where opportunity knocks. Our Walmart eCommerce team is obsessed with finding ways to help our customers live better, raising the bar for an entire industry along the way. You’ll play a crucial role in shaping the future of shopping, working with crazy talented people in an environment that values kindness and transparency. With the energy of a startup and the scale of the Fortune One, it’s the ultimate innovator’s playground. Are you hungry to own your career and catapult ideas into action? Read on.</p>
<p><b>
ABOUT THE ROLE</b></p>
<p>
The Site Merchandiser is responsible for leading the strategy, innovation and development of our customer experience. The successful candidate will demonstrate a winning attitude, be flexible, action and results oriented, analytical and self-starting. They will partner with cross-functional teams in business and technology to drive efficiencies with automation and optimize the customer's shopping experience. They will be passionate about fashion and creating the best-in-class online fashion destination.</p>
<p><b>
WHAT YOU’LL DO</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Execute merchandising strategies online to contribute to overall business goals</li>
<li>Maximize placement of products and digital assets to drive sales, margin and traffic</li>
<li>Continually analyze, execute, and provide improvements to the customer experience as it relates to navigation and product page content; provide reporting to stakeholders on an ongoing basis</li>
<li>Work cross functionally to launch site stories and ensure the customer experience is clear and drives high conversion</li>
<li>Provide recommendations related to site improvements including features, functionality and capabilities to enhance the consumer experience</li>
<li>Demonstrates up-to-date expertise in Site Operations and applies this to the development, execution, and improvement of action plans</li>
<li>Review site daily with customer-first mindset and make site updates as needed</li>
<li>Research industry trends and provide competitive analysis to identify opportunities</li>
<li>Models compliance with company policies and procedures and supports company mission, values, and standards of ethics and integrity</li>
<li>Provides and supports the implementation of business solutions</li>
<li>Resolves complex back-end and customer facing content issues related to site assets, navigation and item data</li>
<li>Shares knowledge of systems and processes</li>
</ul>
<p><b>WHAT YOU SHOULD HAVE</b></p>
<p><b>
Minimum Qualifications:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>2+ years of experience within retail and/or e-commerce merchandising; Fashion industry experience preferred</li>
<li>Omniture, or other analytical experience</li>
<li>Expert in Microsoft Excel</li>
<li>Bachelor’s degree in business or related field, or equivalent experience</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Preferred Qualifications:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Highly organized and exceptional attention to detail and accuracy</li>
<li>Willingness to take on responsibilities outside primary job description when necessary</li>
<li>Ability to quickly learn new processes and systems</li>
<li>Ability to adapt in a fast-pace environment</li>
<li>Strong communication skills</li>
<li>Customer centric mindset</li>
<li>Proven ability to function well in a collaborative environment</li>
<li>Self-motivated, resourceful with a creative mindset</li>
</ul>
<p>This is that place where your passions meet purpose. Join our family and build a career you’re proud of. We’re committed to supporting and developing our workforce–further strengthening our “people-led, tech-empowered” enterprise.</p>
