<h2>Call Center Representative</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">Assists with incoming and outbound offered inquiries and/or requests regarding products and/or services and has sales ability to convert calls to qualified appointments.
<br><br>
Assists customers by providing information while providing efficient and courteous service.
<br><br>
Assists with investigating and resolving routine problems, and recognizes when to escalate customer issues.
<br><br>
Assists in scheduling of appointments with in and out of network providers.
<br><br>
Performs simple, routine work within area of specialization.
<br><br>
Selects appropriate processes from clearly documented rules, past practices, or instruction.
<br><br>
Seeks advice and guidance on non-routine or problem areas from manager.
<br><br>
Basic Microsoft Office Skills (Word, Excel, PowerPoint, Outlook) - Willingness to learn and use new computer programs/applications
<br><br>
Ability to work quickly, accurately and independently in a fast-paced environment
<br><br>
Basic or advanced written communication skills - Ability to communicate detailed or technical information clearly, accurately and concisely - Experience interacting with customers/clients
<br><br>
Experience or demonstrated ability to hit productivity and KPI goals in a consistent manner - Familiarity or knowledge of customer service techniques with a drive to be productive - Ability/willingness to learn HearUSA software and scripting programs - Excellent/strong skills required in, inbound and outbound tele-services with a senior target audience - Ability to represent HearUSA as a professional, courteous, knowledgeable extension of the company brand.
<br><br>
Travel not required for position</div>
