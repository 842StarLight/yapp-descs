<h2>Parts Counterperson</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Kendall Ford of Meridian has immediate openings for a <b>Parts Counterperson.</b></p>
<p>
This is a full time position offering excellent compensation and benefits. Kendall provides a fun and dynamic workplace with many opportunities to grow as an individual as well as a Kendall team member. Come join the Kendall family! We offer Health, Vision and Dental Benefits; company sponsored Life Insurance as well as paid time off.</p>
<p>
This is an opportunity to join an established company and grow your career in the automotive industry.</p>
<p>
 This position will include selling, ordering and locating parts for clients and technicians along with maintaining inventory. You will be working closely with technicians, sales staff and most importantly our customers. The ability to make recommendations and prioritize items in order of importance for our customers is key.</p>
<p>
Required schedule will be Tuesday - Saturday 8:00 - 5:30 with Sunday and Mondays off.</p>
<p>
Experience in automotive parts, inventory control software and a working knowledge of electronic parts cataloging is required. Experience with brand specific parts catalog is preferred. A valid drivers license is required along with a driving record acceptable for company insurance.</p>
