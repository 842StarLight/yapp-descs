<p><b>Account Manager</b></p>
<p>
Headquartered just outside of Atlanta, Brightree’s team of more than 600 employees is thinking, creating, and challenging ourselves to better serve our patients and customers. Each day is a new day for ground-breaking ideas and unparalleled opportunity. Ours is a culture focused on what we can accomplish today, and where it can lead us tomorrow.</p>
<p>
We have vision. We’re a team of thinkers and innovators who constantly challenge ourselves to do everything better than the day before, so that people around the world can breathe easier.</p>
<p>
Our Software as a Service (SaaS) business is an exciting new area of growth focused on the full continuum of out-of-hospital care settings. The team is building a global portfolio of connected software and services designed to drive more choices and better outcomes for patients and providers. We are looking for individuals with a strategic mindset, business acumen, an entrepreneurial outlook, strong technical capabilities and a “get it done” attitude to help fulfill our vision as the leader in out of hospital solutions.</p>

<p><b><br>
Account Manager</b></p>
<p>
Location: <b>Remote</b></p>
<p><b>
Who we are looking for:</b><br>
Brightree is looking for an Account Manager for the Home Health and Hospice business<br>
<br>
<b>Let' talk about your responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
<b>
 </b>Help customers converting from HealthCare First EMR to Brightree EMR software platform.
</li>
<li>Assist our customers in thorough successful use of our best of breed home health and hospice software solutions.</li>
<li>
Meet with customers regularly to review and improve customer processes surrounding our technology products.</li>
<li>
Keep customers informed of upcoming product changes.</li>
<li>
Participate with the customer sales team to identify customer needs to support the sale of ancillary modules to customers.</li>
<li>
Meet with customers in group settings to share new customer initiatives.</li>
<li>
Travel – 20%</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Let's talk about your qualifications:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
<b>
 </b>Clinical or revenue cycle experience in a home health or hospice organization.</li>
<li>
A strong desire to assist customers in utilizing technology to improve their business performance.</li>
<li>
Self-starter, results driven, highly motivated, high energy, with a proven track record of customer relationships.</li>
<li>
Ability to analyze and assess existing client needs and requirements through a consultative process</li>
<li>
Ability to independently craft a sales value proposition for each customer.</li>
<li>
Ability to work in a team environment that is fast-paced, dynamic work environment that values integrity.</li>
<li>
Confident, aggressive, and highly organized.</li>
<li>
Excellent verbal and written communication skills.</li>
<li>
The ability to balance multiple, high priorities issues simultaneously.<br>
</li>
</ul>
<p><b>About your experience:</b></p>

 </b>A college degree is highly preferred</li>

Account management experience within the post-acute care sector</li>
