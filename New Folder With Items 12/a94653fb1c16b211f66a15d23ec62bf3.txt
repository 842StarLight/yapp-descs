<h2>Senior Full Stack Software Engineer</h2>

<p>The PaySimple engineering team is looking for great engineers to join us in building a SaaS platform that provides eCommerce, payments, and invoicing solutions for small businesses.</p>
<p><b>
</b><b>You</b></p>
<p>
You love to build great products, and view software as just one of the tools in your toolbox. You also can put the customer’s hat on to see your product through their eyes. You have a bag of tricks for SaaS development, and you prefer to run your own code instead of throwing it over the wall. You have used relational databases and NoSQL (so do we), and are comfortable with both.</p>
<p><b>

<p>
PaySimple is an early-stage growth company building a top-flight engineering team. We are technology agnostic and believe in using the best tool for the job. We use Agile to both deliver quality code and to continually improve how we do things.
