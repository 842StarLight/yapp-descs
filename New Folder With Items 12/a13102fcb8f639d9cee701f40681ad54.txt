<p><b>Job Responsibilities</b></p>
</div>
</div>
<br>
</div></div>
<p>This position is an active supporter of FirstService Residential philosophies and culture to staff, clients, and community that advances the FirstService Residential brand in terms of image, reputation, differentiation, and professionalism. Reporting to the Manager of Strategy &amp; Operations, the System Administrator's responsibility is to design, implement, maintain and support end users, business systems and technical solutions to improve company work-flow, efficiency and effectiveness. These responsibilities involve analyzing business needs and opportunities, developing and executing system enhancements and new technology installations as well as providing technical support and training.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<div>
<div>
<div><div>
<p><b>Essential Duties &amp; Responsibilities</b></p>
<br>
</div></div>
</div>
</div>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Assist in the implementation, maintenance and support of the vendor platforms used to support sales and property operations including but not limited to; Zen Desk, Qualtrics, Microsoft Dynamics and SharePoint</li>
<li>Serve as subject matter expert for 3rd party technology solutions as assigned (Zen Desk, Qualtrics, Microsoft Dynamics and SharePoint)</li>
<li>Work collaboratively to troubleshoot, research and escalate issues as necessary, proactively keeping end-users aware of status and taking responsibility for ensuring that outstanding issues are addressed in a timely manner</li>
<li>Assist internal customer by providing end user support and respond to help desk support tickets in a timely and effective manner</li>
<li>Support the structure of vendor systems, user credentials, maintenance and access/security guidelines</li>
<li>Manage customer experience data integrity and hierarchy changes</li>
<li>Monitor custom system solutions and work closely with vendors to ensure proper execution and timely resolution of bugs and enhancement requests</li>
<li>Monitor ticket assignments and workflows and troubleshoot routing errors</li>
<li>Manage the daily maintenance of assigned applications and troubleshoot errors and outages</li>
<li>Assist with the development and implementation of customer and associate experience survey and research initiatives</li>
<li>Maintain dashboard configurations, testing and reporting in all assigned applications</li>
<li>Support national team, regions and markets by providing custom queries and reporting as required or requested</li>
<li>Monitor the activities of our 3rd party outbound vendor and report on productivity and efficiency opportunities</li>
<li>Educate/train end users to become self-sufficient on the technology for the platforms you support</li>
<li>Maintain knowledge base, video tutorials and training materials</li>
<br>
</ul>
