<h2>Sr. Account Executive</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Overview
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<p>SMG is looking to add an experienced, top-performing Sr. Account Executive to play an integral part in accelerating SMG’s growth through initiating, developing, and closing new client partnerships.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
The Sr. Account Executive will support pipeline creation for assigned industries through effective outbound prospecting with a focus on generating qualified leads for sales discovery and conversion to pipeline. The role requires targeted research, calling and emailing to identify and qualify buyers in healthcare, multi-unit restaurant, retail, and other relevant industries. It also entails travel to relevant industry events to generate interest in SMG solutions.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
This position acts as a CEO of their own vertical—knowing their industry target inside and out—and uses that drive to know more and do better. You should be a fierce competitor, but also a team player who recognizes and appreciates success in others.</p>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<ul>
<li>Have daily activity responsibility against individual lead qualification and discovery meeting setting goals.</li>
<li>
Collaborate with sales leaders to identify named accounts for outbound prospecting.</li>
<li>
Obtain business intelligence on target organizations utilizing various methods that include cold calling, warm lead follow-up and various research methods including third party data providers.</li>
<li>
Develop an understanding of the target organization's structure, buyers, pain points, key buying influences, program needs and timing, competitor incumbents and more.</li>
<li>
Initiate the sales process through cold outbound prospecting.</li>
<li>
Secure qualified discovery meetings for assigned industries and sales teams, handing off to VP Sales for opportunity development and closure with the objective of converting the opportunity to bookings.</li>
<li>
Document in CRM the details of qualified leads to clearly communicate client profile, buyers and current needs and timing.</li>
<li>
Maintain data integrity in the CRM through ongoing data cleanup, management and enrichment.</li>
<li>
Follow demand waterfall and lead lifecycle processes defined by Vice President of Strategic Operations.</li>
<li>
Conduct regular sales alignment meetings to review progress against named account strategies and tactics.</li>
<li>
Lead initial discovery conversations and present initial capabilities as required to qualify the opportunity</li>
<li>
Travel to relevant industry conferences and tradeshows to represent SMG</li>
<li>
Work closely with the Marketing team to create individualized campaigns such as ABM and on other support needed for outbound campaigns.</li>
<li>
Continue to grow and increase sales skills and knowledge of SMG offerings and assigned industries.</li>
</ul>
</div></div></div>

</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<ul>

<li>
4-7 years of business development or outside B2B sales experience, preferably at a technology company, with proven ability to deliver consistently strong and measurable results</li>
<li>
Accountability for sourcing pipeline and bookings (not just leads)</li>
<li>
Comfortable performing against a variable compensation model</li>
<li>
Strong and professional phone presence with willingness to make dozens of calls a day</li>
<li>
Experience with CRM and marketing automation systems to generate, capture, nurture, track and report on leads, conversions and ROI</li>
<li>
A collaborative team player</li>
<li>
Proficient in Microsoft Office product suite</li>
</ul>
