<b>
PURPOSE AND OBJECTIVES</b></p>
<p>
This position is responsible for accurate and proficient collection and maintenance of accounts including those in bankruptcy, and coordination of special projects related to credit management. Reports to Sr. Manager, Global Accounts Receivable. We are looking for an individual who executes operational (including transactional) tasks independently and deals with customers' queries. Gains a good knowledge of standard processes and related transactions. Maintains good relationship with external and internal customers.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
EXPECTATIONS AND TASKS</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Identify, research and collect on past due receivables, to achieve team goals</li>
<li>
Verify pre-petition and post petition
</li>
<li>Work with internal and external legal counsels as the bankruptcy case moves through court system.
</li>
<li>Work with Customer, Sales, Legal to resolve contract disputes</li>
<li>
Ensure timely and accurate maintenance of accounts</li>
<li>
Investigate and resolve credits on customer accounts</li>
<li>
Assist with distribution of monthly invoices</li>
<li>
Responsible for accurate creation and timely distribution of required reporting to management</li>
<li>
Partner with internal business groups to resolve outstanding cash collection matters</li>
<li>
Special projects as necessary to accomplish finance team's objectives<br>
</li>
</ul>
