<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities
</b></h2>
<div>
<div>The Senior HR Application Analyst will drive application support and administration via hands-on application maintenance, management, and issue resolution. You will provide Tier 2-3 support to employees and managers, ensuring that business technology programs and processes are administered seamlessly. HR technology solutions include but are not limited to core HR, payroll, time and attendance, compensation, benefits, talent acquisition, onboarding, performance management, learning management and business continuity.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<ul>
<li>
Implement HR processes and technology solutions that drive efficiency, reduce the cost of operations and mitigate operational risk across HR globally.</li>
<li>
Deliver exemplary customer service and solutions by troubleshooting all incoming inquiries and resolve or escalate as required; maintain ownership of an issue from start to finish to ensure resolution.</li>
<li>
Provide functional and technical system support including: superuser system administration, process implementation, troubleshooting, user acceptance testing, reference guide creation, and reporting.</li>
<li>
Support the technical design and development of interfaces to internal HR systems and other third party application including end to end integration testing with business users.</li>
<li>
Analyze issues, identify failures, and provide break-fixes related to existing HR system interfaces.</li>
<li>
Oversee successful system updates, enhancements, and maintenance releases:</li>
<ul>
<li>
Develop and document requirements together with business users and obtain signoff.</li>
<li>
Create and execute integration and regression test scripts; coordinate User Acceptance Testing with business users.</li>
</ul>
<li>
Identify, assess impact, and provide recommendation for resolution of issues related to HR business processes, procedural and system changes; drive automation and system enhancements.</li>
<li>
Escalate process change requests, system enhancements or any other major resource requests.</li>
<li>
Review HR systems user access requests and approve based on established security requirements/policy.</li>
<li>
Create and maintain security matrix/documentation.</li>
<li>
Maintain knowledge of technology modules; make recommendations on enhancements to continually improve the effectiveness and efficiency of processes.</li>
<li>
Maintain a strong focus on metrics and data trends to understand and align with business needs</li>
<li>
Perform regular HRIS data audits to ensure system accuracy and to determine areas in need of additional training/resources.</li>
<li>
Write and maintain a variety of reports or queries utilizing Cognos reporting and SQL tools. Develop and enhance standard reports to address ongoing HR and business needs.</li>
</ul>
</div>
