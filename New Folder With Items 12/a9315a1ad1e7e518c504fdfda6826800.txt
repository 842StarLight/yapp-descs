We are looking for a Technical Lead who has strong innovative product development skills to build data platform as a Product within digital ecosystem for medical devices. Digital foundation includes a platform led approach with re-usable components for assets across the J&amp;J MD portfolio – enabling collaboration between product teams while reducing effort in creating new products. Experience in handling a variety of structured and unstructured data sources in the healthcare field, the adeptness to collaborate closely and influence others in an ambiguous and fast-paced environment, creativity to invent, customize and work in a multidisciplinary environment to drive business solutions and exposure to new technologies in data and analytics.<br>
<br>
<b>Position Summary:</b><br>
<br>
The primary responsibility of the Data Management Technical Lead is to collaborate with internal and external partners across the organization to develop sustainable solutions that support data-driven decision-making with hands-on expertise to build Data and Analytics services. As an integral part of Digital Surgery Data Management team, you will architect, design, build, and support data management capabilities for a next generation cloud data and analytics platform integrating device/robot and clinical health data from various hospitals.<br>
<br>
<b>Primary responsibilities include:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Develop Data Management capabilities integrating various Hospital Information Systems datasets like EMR/EHRs, RIS, Patient Case Schedules, Lab results and Financial information like Claims, Payments into a cloud data platform to enable analytics consumption.</li>
<li>
Apply domain expertise with Hospital Integration Systems and industry interoperability standards such as HL7 V2, V3 and CDA, HL7 FHIR, PACS DICOM to implement a cloud data platform solution.</li>
<li>
Apply subject matter knowledge of Compliance and data privacy frameworks like HiTRUST, GDPR, CCPA to build a compliant and certified cloud data platform solution</li>
<li>
Act as data domain guide in the topics of Hospital health data integration and data management, data persistence across operational and analytical data layers, data veracity and data analytics.</li>
<li>
Customize and extend FHIR Resource model, FHIR Server APIs to address health data acquisition, processing, persistency and consumption.</li>
<li>
Ensure data accuracy and completeness while producing high quality data management software with scalable data services to address business needs.</li>
<li>
Develop and evolve microservices/APIs for data ingestion, data transformation, persistence and access.</li>
<li>
Define user stories/enablers/tasks/tests to address solution Functional and Non-Functional requirements.</li>
<li>
Programmatic implementation of groomed user stories and enablers with expected functionality in agile delivery cycles.</li>
<li>
Analyze and adopt new tools and solution patterns to build high performing and scalable solutions.</li>
<li>
Proactively find opportunities and technologies providing access, processing, visualizations, exploration and analysis of data.</li>
<li>
Support and contribute to creation of relevant documentation, knowledge exchange sessions with various partner to articulate the solution design and implementation roadmaps.</li>
</ul>
