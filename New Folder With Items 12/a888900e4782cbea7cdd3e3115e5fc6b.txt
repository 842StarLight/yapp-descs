<div><b>About the Company:</b></div>

</div>
<div>
<br>
Named to the Forbes 2018 list of America's Best Midsize Employers, this achievement recognizes ITT's focus and commitment to fostering a healthy, high-performing culture and creating a work-place defined by innovation and continuous improvement. ITT was also awarded the 2018 Manufacturing Leadership Award which distinguishes ITT for its visionary leadership, commitment to employees and workplace culture, and ability to innovatively solve problems for customers.</div>

<b>
ITT operates through three Value Centers (business</b><b> units):</b>
</div>
<div>
<b>Industrial Process</b> designs and manufactures pumps, valves, monitoring and control systems, water treatment and aftermarket services for the chemical, oil and gas, mining and other industrial process markets, as well as global service capabilities;
</div>
<div>
<b>Motion Technologies</b> designs and manufactures brake pads, shock absorbers and sealing solutions for the automotive and rail markets;
</div>

<b>
Job Summary/Overview</b><b>:</b><br>
The Director, Global Export/Import Compliance is primarily responsible for ensuring the global export and import health of the enterprise through the implementation of strategies that provides direction and regulatory guidance to all ITT Value Centers (VC) ensuring compliance with the ITT Code of Conduct, policies, procedures and U.S. and international export and import law.<br>
<b>Essential Functions of the Role:</b><br>
<ul><li>Determines and communicates appropriate management accountability and responsibility for</li></ul>
the Global Export/Import Compliance function. Interfaces regularly with VC and corporate leadership. Primary management accountability is focused on value creation and preservation.<br>
<br>
<ul>
<li>Develops and implements export and import strategy, in partnership with key internal functions like finance, tax and supply chain, across the enterprise and provides regulatory guidance to all VCs.</li>
<li> Directs Export/Import Compliance based on best industry practice ‘right sized’ for ITT at all VCs relative to:
</li>
</ul>
</div>
<ul>
<li> operational processes and excellence</li>
<li>export licensing/classification</li>
<li> export/import compliance (offsets, export and import documentation, Foreign/Free Trade Zones,</li>
</ul>
Free Trade Agreements, etc.) with a specific focus on duties
<ul><li>export/import violation identification/disclosure</li></ul>
<div>
Specifically, the Director, Global Export/Import Compliance:<br>
</div>
<div>
<br>
<ul>
<li>Designs and oversees the implementation of Global Export/Import Compliance strategies, including software, and evaluates and monitors their effectiveness.</li>
<li> Develops and manages effective relationships with key internal functions including IT, Finance, Logistics, Tax and others in pursuit of strategies that optimize work flow, quickly integrate acquisitions, better manage cash flow, and ultimately mitigates VC costs.</li>
<li> Designs and monitors compliance with commercial and defense export license processes and provides direction and guidance as appropriate.</li>
<li> Serves as lead subject matter expert on M&amp;A due diligence of export and import matters.</li>
<li> Designs and directs the implementation of periodic internal audits to review, test, and evaluate the implementation of trade directives ensuring each VC’s import/export programs are effective, robust and in compliance with relevant laws/regulations.</li>
</ul>
</div>
<ul>
<li> Develops a LEAN and focused export/import team and provides for periodic training and on-going counsel to VC staff.</li>
<li> Develops strategies and coordinates offset programs for all of the VCs.</li>
<li> Maintains expert-level knowledge of all applicable legislation and regulations.
</li>
<li> Reviews and updates all policies and provides direction for compliance with other laws and regulations affecting international trade, including (for example) anti-boycott regulations.</li>
<li> Serves as a primary advisor for ITT on export/import policy negotiations.</li>
<li>Works with regulators as appropriate and represents ITT and serves as a primary interface with government agencies on export/import compliance issues.<br>
</li>
</ul>
