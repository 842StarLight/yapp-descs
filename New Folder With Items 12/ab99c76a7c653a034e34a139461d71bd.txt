<h2>Retail Assistant Store Manager (Full-time) - San Francisco - CA</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p><b>Retail Assistant Store Manager
</b></p>
<p>EQ3 is an imaginative leader in creative furniture and accessory design. We are seeking an extraordinary individual for a <b>full-time position</b> as <b>Retail Assistant Store Manager </b>to be part of our high-energy Retail team in our <b>EQ3</b> <b>Location</b> in<b> San Francisco, California</b>. This individual will provide the professional management impetus behind all aspects of the operation of EQ3 on behalf of the Store Manager.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Skills and abilities required...</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
3 years of Retail Management experience</li>
<li>Creative and persuasive communication sales style</li>
<li>Excellent organizational skills and able to multi-task</li>
<li>Demonstrated talent for effective use of computerized systems such as Microsoft office.</li>
<li>Self-motivated, able to work well with little or no supervision, and a strong positive attitude</li>
<li>Ability to communicate effectively in French and English (both oral and written)</li>
<li>Willing to learn all the aspects of the Management position such as review income statements and create action plans at the store level to meet monthly budgets and support the team to achieve sales targets</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Duties include...</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Full support for managing and controlling the entire Store's inventory as well as ensuring that the store's budgeted inventory goal is not exceeded</li>
<li>Partner with the Store Manager to take responsibility to network locally and create excitement via events, public relations and local advertising.</li>
<li>Identifies key strategic issues and opportunities affecting the business and translates them into achievable action plans</li>
<li>Measures his/her own performance results as well as the results of Sales Associates against strategic business targets</li>
<li>Help control payroll expenses within monthly budgets</li>
<li>Achieves budgeted sales goals</li>
<li>Reduces turnover-hire costs for qualified Sales Associates</li>
<li>Provides suggestions for new strategies of advertising or marketing EQ3 products</li>
<li>Delivers organized, timely and effective administrative responsibilities</li>
<li>Understands importance of and delivers workplace safety initiatives</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
