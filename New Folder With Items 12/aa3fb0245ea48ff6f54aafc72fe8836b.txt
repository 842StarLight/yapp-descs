<p><b>Strategic Growth Account Executive – Consulting &amp; Agency</b></p>
<p>
The Growth Account Executive is responsible for new business development in the consultancy and marketing agency space.</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Responsibilities:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Responsible for delivering sales revenue from our consulting and agency segment in order to meet individual/team quotas and the organization's business objectives</li>
<li>
Operates as the primary point of contact to the partner (for the purpose of the sale)</li>
<li>
Responsible for executing the sales process, either alone or through the assembly of a sales and support team</li>
<li>
Ensures the sales process progresses in a timely fashion and chooses when to terminate the sales process</li>
<li>
Ensure coordinated management of assigned strategic partnerships within and across Business Units</li>
<li>
Ensure effective governance and management of the relationship with assigned partner(s) (across all individual products, verticals, and BUs) - balance competing priorities; spot and capitalize on synergies</li>
<li>
Act as champion for assigned partner(s) within Equifax - ensure sustainable success of the partnership(s) by ensuring value to the partner(s)</li>
<li>
Balance a focus on day-to-day operations with a strategic focus on long-term opportunities and risks (working closely with BU and vertical leaders, sales leaders, product managers, and marketing managers responsible for the day-to-day operations of products developed and/or sold through assigned partnership(s)</li>
<li>
Constantly search out, uncover, and ensure evaluation of innovation and new business opportunities with assigned partner(s) - and ensure that adequate resources are assigned to pursue those opportunities</li>
<li>
Help ensure alignment between account plans for assigned partnerships, Equifax strategy (enterprise, BU, vertical, and channel strategies), partner strategy, and other Equifax products and third party relationships</li>
<li>
In concert with the Enterprise Alliance Executive, lead the negotiation of new contracts with assigned partners</li>
<li>
Support the development of alliance tools, processes, and procedures and ensure that they are continuously updated to be in line with industry best practices</li>
<li>
Coach and support individuals within the BUs who are managing the interface with partners (on an ad hoc basis)</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Requirements</b><b>:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
7+ years of experience in client facing, strategic sales / partnership roles</li>
<li>
Experience with consultancies or marketing agencies required</li>
<li>
Experience selling data and insights driven solutions highly preferred</li>
<li>
Experience in Fraud, Identity Verification, Marketing and Debt Management industries highly preferred</li>
<li>
Bachelor’s degree in marketing or similar field required</li>
</ul>
