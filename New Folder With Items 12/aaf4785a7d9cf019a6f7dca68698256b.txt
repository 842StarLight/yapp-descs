<h2>Manager of Business Development IoT</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p><b>What you’ll be doing...</b>
</p>
<p>Verizon’s Global IoT Product and Solutions team identifies winning strategic opportunities and leads the development of a technology partner ecosystem to generate new revenue streams and profitability. The team leads business development activities to cultivate external opportunities with technology partners, determining greatest fit and benefit to Verizon. Some of those partners would include the likes of AWS, Microsoft, Google, Dell, Samsung and Apple. To do so, the team is required to lead partnership management and gain alignment with internal and external executive leaders with go to market efforts being executed. As a key function, the team will drive and generate market assessments and recommendations across an ecosystem of technology partners through joint commercial agreements, acquisitions or ventures to ultimately exceed P&amp;L forecasts. The objective will be to drive new commercial partnerships within Verizon’s current IoT product portfolio but also establishing new partnerships where 5G MEC can be leveraged and monetized as a technology enabler for Verizon’s next generation product portfolio.</p>
<p>
You have in-depth knowledge of Verizon’s IoT product portfolio, and the telecommunication and technology sectors position across the IoT stack</p>
<p>
You have a deep understanding of the digital environment that is being impacted by leaders in the CSP space</p>
<p>
You are a self-starter, creative and innovative thinker. You are professionally polished with sound business acumen, proven cross-functional program management skills, and a passion for organizing complex information.</p>
<p>
You can ideate, develop and execute on comprehensive sales and go to market strategies, defining solutions, target customer segments, target accounts, and routes to market.<br>
You have experience in collaborating with global fortune 500 customers and a diverse set of stakeholders, generating tangible results across multiple groups and business units</p>
