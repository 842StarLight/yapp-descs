<div><b>The work:</b></div>
<div>The Accenture Salesforce Integrated Platform Team (IPT) is seeking a Manager with experience transforming Quote to Cash (QTC) for enterprise clients. The ideal candidate will work closely with our clients, technology vendors and internal delivery teams in delivering robust and scalable solutions for our clients. As the QTC Consultant, the candidate will be accountable for managing the successful delivery of solution enhancements, upgrades and the implementation of new tools as required to meet the needs of stakeholders. The candidate should be able to identify clients’ high-level business needs and lead the creation of business value cases using industry experience and leading best practices. The candidate should have strong project management skills and experience, with a strong knowledge of service and delivery management principles and a proven track record of delivering results and be ready to build those air miles with frequent weekly travel: Monday-Thursday as needed.</div>
