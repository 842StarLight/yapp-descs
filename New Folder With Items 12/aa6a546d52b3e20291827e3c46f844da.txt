<h2>Solution Operations Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div></div>
<div>
<div><div><div>Cerner is growing and adding to our Behavioral Health organization, looking for a Solution Operations Manager! As a Solution Operations Manager, your main responsibility will be to promote client satisfaction through achievement of maximum value, adoption and efficiency from Cerner solutions. Additional responsibilities include, managing relationships with key stakeholders across the client base for assigned solutions or venues; consulting with clients in navigating Cerner, advocate for their needs, and channel feedback appropriately; identifying and tracking client value statements and return on investment; identifying reference clients and managing coordination of reference activity for solution area; executing strategies to increase client solution adoption and alignment to best practices; delivering client-facing communication, including email, newsletters, client council calls, workshops, and industry events; and measuring and report on client key performance indicators (KPIs) for the solution or venue.<br>
