<div>
<b>Position Title:</b> Digital Marketing Specialist<br>
<br>
<b>Position Reports To: </b>Marketing Communications Manager<br>
<br>
<b>FLSA Status: </b>Exempt<br>
<br>
<b>POSITION SUMMARY:</b>
</div>
<div>The Digital Marketing Specialist understands, analyses and reports on the digital customer’s journey and works cross-functionally to execute targeted eCommerce related strategies. With the goal of driving website traffic, this position cultivates leads, and turns site visitors into buying customers. The Digital Marketing Specialist also monitors and updates the North American corporate website and develops content for social media channels. With content creation being an important part of the role, creativity and versatility are key to this position.</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div></div>
<div>
<b>ESSENTIAL FUNCTIONS:</b><br>
<b>Essential duties and responsibilities may include, but are not limited to, the following:</b>
</div>
<ul>
<li>Create content for websites, social media and digital initiatives</li>
<li>Create 3D product and application renderings for marketing and sales use</li>
<li>Regularly monitor site traffic and usage patterns (ex. using Google analytics)</li>
<li>Identify opportunities to increase website traffic, session duration, conversion rate and average order value</li>
<li>Project management of website design projects (timelines, budgets, scopes)</li>
<li>Develop and maintain a keen understanding of internal eCommerce, technical and SEO/SEM capabilities</li>
<li>Lead and support software implementation changes to identify possible technical and/or UX implications</li>
<li>Create and distribute e-mail marketing campaigns and regular communication to customers</li>
<li>Create, design, and structure online marketing initiatives</li>
<li>Development and execution of annual digital marketing plan</li>
<li>Shoot, edit and create company videos</li>
<li>Communicate, monitor and measure performance of digital agency partners</li>
<li>Develop, implement content and maintain social media sites as well as related advertising</li>
</ul>
<div><b>MINIMUM QUALIFICATIONS:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree in Marketing, Website Design, Graphic Design, Art or other related field. Two (2) or more years of marketing experience in a professional environment preferred.</li>
</ul>
<div>Knowledge and proficiency of:</div>
<ul>
<li>Website design, development (specifically Shopify)</li>
<li>3D Software, 3D Modeling and animation</li>
<li>Online marketing tactics, google analytics, SEO, SEM and social media</li>
<li>Adobe programs (i.e. Acrobat, Photoshop, Illustrator, InDesign)</li>
<li>CMS, html, coding and database driven websites</li>
<li>Photography and video editing</li>
<li>CRM systems (such as Microsoft CRM) and marketing automation tools</li>
<li>Basic knowledge of the development, management and execution of a successful digital marketing campaign</li>
<li>Proficient in the use of Microsoft Office Suite (specifically Excel, PowerPoint and Word)</li>
<li>Excellent organizational skills and verbal and communication skills</li>
</ul>
<div>
<br>
<b>Ability to:</b>
</div>
<ul>
<li>Interact effectively with people</li>
<li>Work in a demanding, deadline-driven environment with a customer service focus</li>
<li>Multi-task successfully while working independently or within a team environment</li>
<li>Travel 10% for content creation, trade shows, corporate meetings &amp; various other events</li>
<li>Follow ISO14001/9001 processes. IATF/TS16949 as required.</li>
