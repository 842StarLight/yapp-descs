<h2>Director of Sales</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<h1 class="jobSectionHeader">JOB DESCRIPTION
</h1>
<div>
<p>The <b>Director of Sales</b> is a senior level leader who will report to the station General Manager and who will be responsible for all sales related revenue streams including spot television, digital, mobile, and other non-traditional streams from which to grow revenue. Primary responsibilities include leading a team of approximately 24 in the achievement of revenue goals; effective yield management; accurate forecasting; department budget creation and management; goal setting for the sales organization (including various revenue streams), setting strategic direction with a clear execution plan. The right individual for this position must get up to speed quickly and as a result, must be able to convey and demonstrate strong leadership and communication skills, creativity in revenue generation, flawless execution in previous positions, experience developing processes and SOPs for sales departments.</p>
<p><b>The Director of Sales will:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Communicate effectively with the General Manager on all sales related activities</li>
<li>Drive revenue generation and create demand on multiple WVEC Media platforms.</li>
<li>Grow the station’s revenue share on WVEC-TV, 13newNow.com and other digital platforms</li>
<li>Control and price inventory to meet and exceed budget goals.</li>
<li>Forecast and budget the TV spot and digital revenue of 13newNow on a monthly and quarterly basis</li>
<li>Develop and implement sales projects that create spot demand and generate incremental revenue</li>
<li>Annually increase digital, enterprise and business development revenue for 13newNow</li>
<li>Recruit, develop, and supervise the Sales Department</li>
<li>Determine the strategic direction of the department with the ability to sustain that strategic direction throughout the year</li>
<li>Collaborate with others on ratings estimates and projections</li>
<li>Interface with TeleRep on all matters related to national revenue</li>
<li>Work with various department heads of 13newNow on relevant activities</li>
<li>Provide top level station management with an accurate picture of 13newNow revenue conditions at all times.</li>
<li>Represent 13newNow in external sales and marketing related industry functions</li>
<li>Travel on a regular basis and attend sales related functions after business hours</li>
<li>Take full advantage of 13newNow consultants and training protocols</li>
</ul>
</div>
</div>

<h1 class="jobSectionHeader">JOB REQUIREMENTS
</h1>
<div>
<p><b>Requirements:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Five or more years of television sales management. The ideal candidate should have experience in digital media</li>
<li>Candidates must be proficient in strategic and tactical thinking; have strong analytical, leadership, planning and budgeting skills; and possess the creative abilities to generate revenue outside of traditional sources</li>
<li>Must be able to work under pressure and prioritize goals for the department and the station</li>
<li>Must be able to manage a large staff with various skill sets with the ability to work with other TEGNA entities and build consensus</li>
<li>Proficient with Microsoft Office Suite, NSI, Wide Orbit, and ComScore (formerly Rentrak)</li>
<br>
</ul>
