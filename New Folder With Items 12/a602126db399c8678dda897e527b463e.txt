<h2>Technical Support Agent</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>ESET Technical Support Agent provides technical product support to all ESET customers, partners, resellers, and internal personnel. Technical Support Agents are expected to stay current with changes and trends to service our customers with excellent technical troubleshooting ability, exceptional problem solving skills, and outstanding customer service skills.
<br>
Individuals must have a passion for problem solving, have excellent verbal and written communication skills and be comfortable working with all business type customers. This role is also expected to identify potential cross-sell opportunities and present them to higher tier support or management when appropriate after working with customers.
<p>Job description</p>
<p><b>
DUTIES and RESPONSIBILITIES:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Answer and address all incoming online chat support requests and emails from ESET customers. In high-volume situations or when not otherwise tasked, OSAs are expected to assist other teams when needed and address queued calls and/or voicemails.</li>
<li>
Performs ESET Professional Service engagements for business type customers. Delivers preliminary documentation and collaborates with TS Management as well as Sales to deliver paid services including but not limited to ESET Deployment and Upgrade Service and ESET Enterprise Inspector deployment.</li>
<li>
Actively identifies and communicates cross-sell opportunities as appropriate for customer environments. Properly documents and prepares these opportunities for higher tier support or management.</li>
<li>
Take ownership of each issue assigned and effectively attempts their best to bring issue to resolution or escalation. Using product information, client tools, client specific reference materials, and other methods to resolve complex issues within defined ESET SLAs.</li>
<li>
Assist in creating and updating solutions for our Knowledgebase for training and information purposes.</li>
<li>
Assist with identifying, troubleshooting and properly documenting malware cases.</li>
<li>
Keeps precise records of all customer interactions, recording details of inquiries, complaints, troubleshooting, comments, as well as any actions taken.</li>
<li>
Helps improve product reliability by asking the customer probing and effective questions to gather information in determining source of problems and customer needs.</li>
<li>
Maintains a favorable image of ESET when interfacing with outside sources.</li>
<li>
Accurately records and submits timesheets and paid time off requests.</li>
<li>
Contributes to BPCC resource site by documenting findings from escalated cases. Generally informs TS team training efforts by sharing knowledge in team meetings and assisting less senior agents via chat, email and other mediums.</li>
<li>
Performs other duties and responsibilities not specifically outlined herein, as requested by the management team.</li>
<li>
Provides support to resellers and end users in selecting optimal business solutions for their environment.</li>
<li>
Ensure the successful onboarding of new, recently renewed, or upgraded partners, partner customers and direct customers.</li>
<li>
Ensure customer needs are met by ensuring customer awareness of relevant ESET products and services when appropriate.</li>
<li>
Effectively collect and submit relevant data to appropriate groups for analysis. Based on findings, follow up with customers regarding ticket statuses in a timely manner.</li>
<li>
Communicate escalated and trending issues to other Technical Support Teams.</li>
<li>
Test and replicate reported issues in test environment then report/document findings and results.</li>
</ul>
