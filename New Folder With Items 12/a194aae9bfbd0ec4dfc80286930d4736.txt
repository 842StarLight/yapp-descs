This Position
<br><br>
The Solution Architect partners with the Enterprise field sales team to discover and understand customer business challenges, complex processes, and architectural landscapes in order to build world-class integrated solutions leveraging DocuSign's platform and suite of products. Through engaging stories and custom-built demonstrations, the SE illustrates the value and benefits of modernizing a prospect's System of Agreement via the DocuSign offering.
<br><br>
This position reports to the Director of Solution Architecture for Enterprise sales. We are seeking to staff 2 roles and can be flexible on location.
<br><br>
Responsibilities
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Collaborate with the Enterprise field sales team to cultivate and secure net-new business within an assigned territory and targeted accounts</li>
<li>Ability to uncover, comprehend, and document complex business challenges, processes, and enterprise architecture</li>
<li>Engage in meaningful business and technical conversations, sharing industry insights with key stakeholders from all levels and business units within large enterprise organizations</li>
<li>Establish technical credibility and build confidence in recommended solutions leveraging your product expertise, custom product demonstrations, technical Q&amp;A, business process diagrams, and product roadmap discussions</li>
<li>Articulate selected recommendations and the business value they provide to diverse audiences</li>
<li>Work collaboratively with sales partners and other internal teams to provide a world class buying experience</li>
</ul>

<li>Ability to travel 40% or more as needed</li>
</ul>
