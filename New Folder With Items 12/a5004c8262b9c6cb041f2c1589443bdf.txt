<h2>Member Relations Director</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Job summary:</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<p>Oversees member communications, services and referral programs in order to increase and foster private club member satisfaction and retention</p>
</div></div></div>
<div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Essential Duties and Responsibilities:</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<ul>
<li>Works with department heads to identify, complete and implement annual Club calendar of Member events to meet needs and desires of members and guests driving member usage and retention.</li>
<li>Works closely with General Manager to develop and implement Quarterly Retention Plan focused on developing member retention and growth through targeted programming and events focused on membersâ€™ demographics</li>
<li>Oversees member communications, such as email blasts, newsletters; updates web-site, and social media; maintains promotional collateral, member media boards, displays and any other member communication tools</li>
<li>Partners with Membership Director on new member orientations, new member mixers, and other new member connect processes designed to acclimate and connect new members within the first year</li>
<li>Functions as the club representative on various member committees</li>
<li>Works with club food &amp; beverage department to create, maintain function sheets and event files for each member event, tradition and other promotions to include but not limited to entertainment contracts, budgets, and event floor plans</li>
<li>Assists with resignation calls and related activities whose objective is to save outgoing members</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p>
</p>
