<p><b>
Responsibilities include, but are not limited to:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Maintain master data and parameters to best utilize planning and production systems.</li>
<li>Develop production plans to meet customer demand, inventory stock level and consistent production pace</li>
<li>Monitor and plan the usage of key raw materials by utilizing planning and execution systems and by working with the procurement and manufacturing departments.</li>
<li>Work with customer facing departments, to ensure that production schedules meet both manufacturing and customer requirements and properly communicate availability dates.</li>
<li>Daily, weekly, monthly, review of existing and future production planning and preparations.</li>
<li>Formulate weekly, monthly production plans and associated manufacturing information.</li>
<li>Manage production capacity and strive to achieve plant goal of on time delivery.</li>
<li>Participate in the weekly production planning and scheduling meetings.</li>
<li>Provide monthly report on key metrics.</li>
</ul>
