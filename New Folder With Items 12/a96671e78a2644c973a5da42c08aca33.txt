<b>About the role:</b><br>
We are looking for an SEM/PPC expert with hands-on experience developing strategies, creating, testing, analyzing, and optimizing campaigns. Channels include paid search, shopping, display, retargeting, contextual, and social advertising.

You have comprehensive and authoritative knowledge of Google Ads, Microsoft Advertising, and Google Analytics. You dream about conversion rate optimization and know how to drive down CPA while maximizing ROAS. You're comfortable working in a multi-client agency, have excellent budget management skills, and are comfortable talking with clients as needed.
