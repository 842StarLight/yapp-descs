<h2>Online Marketing Associate</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div><div>
<p>TechTarget’s Online Marketing Team – responsible for content marketing strategy and execution across the organization – is seeking a detail-oriented marketing professional to help generate maximum engagement with our products and for our customers. This entry-level role affords the right candidate an invaluable opportunity to familiarize oneself with the many markets and customers we serve, understand core content marketing principles, and serve as a resource supporting the entire Online Marketing Team.</p>
<h4 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Essential Job Functions:</b></h4>
<ul>
<li>Become familiar with a broad array of technical markets, unique audience segments, and copy writing best practices – with the goal of applying that knowledge to improve customer outcomes;</li>
<li>Execute on all marketing activities that feed our email engine to get maximum response for our products and customers;</li>
<li>Develop and maintain a documentation library of both (i) content marketing best practices, and (ii) market- and audience-specific analyses and trends;</li>
<li>Establish internal best practices for project management and campaign execution and/or follow/improve current practices for process efficiencies</li>
<li>Analyze the performance data of email campaigns to develop copywriting best practices and audience insights (i.e. click-thru rates, conversion rates, open rates);</li>
<li>Write brief marketing email promotions and abstracts about sponsored content (white papers, reports, infographics, webcasts, etc.) to maximize lead capture for lead generation campaigns on a daily basis;</li>
<li>Assist the entire Content Marketing team with a variety of marketing projects ranging from assessment of customers’ content and campaign optimization to email set-up and data aggregation; and,</li>
<li>Contribute to a marketing team that specializes in information technology trends, insights and education.</li>
</ul>
<h4 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Knowledge, skills and abilities required:</b></h4>
<ul>
<li>0-3 years marketing experience;</li>
<li>Bachelor's degree;</li>
<li>Comfortable leveraging Excel for project tracking and data manipulation</li>
<li>Ability to multi-task and excellent time management skills;</li>
<li>Ability to work independently and be self-motivated</li>
<li>Strong organizational skills and attention to detail;</li>
<li>Proven written and verbal communication skills;</li>
<li>Exceptional direct response email marketing copy writing ability; and,</li>
<li>Demonstrated success working in team environment.</li>
<br>
</ul>
