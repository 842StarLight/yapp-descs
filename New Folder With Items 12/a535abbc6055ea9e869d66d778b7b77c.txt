<p><b>Who we are:</b></p>

<p>

<p><b>
Position:</b></p>
<p>
The role of the Community Care Specialist is to provide support and advice to customers with regards to ClientBooks software (mobile and web). You must have an understanding of business processes around bookkeeping/accounting to advise customers on the use of ClientBooks.</p>
<p>
The role will be collaborating with the Product, Sales, Billing, and Fulfilment teams. As awareness of ClientBooks grows, so will the number of queries. The role requires a professional, friendly and effective manner at all times.</p>
<p>
The Community Care Specialist takes responsibility for resolving any technical or data issues in the software application, liaising with other teams to provide appropriate information, and analyzing the resolution to be implemented.</p>
<p><b>
Who you are:</b></p>
<p>
A positive and bright-minded individual dedicated to enhancing customer experience and reducing overall technical obstacles for users and employees alike. Compelled to be the best at what you do, you seek answers to questions, you are a top-notch communicator that adheres to a consultative approach of listening first, and are comfortable presenting information by phone and in writing. You are a problem solver, analytical thinker and collaborative individual that works effectively to transfer knowledge to others and has no problem taking direction.</p>
<p><b>
Core Duties &amp; Responsibilities would include:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Effective resolution and prioritization of customer support requests related to ClientBooks Software including, but not limited to bank feed issues, product inquiries, product feedback, etc.</li>
<li>
Respond to the customer by email and/or phone within our targeted initial response times, to either resolve the issue or request further information/clarification regarding the issue.</li>
<li>
Maintain a high quality of written communication in an agreed style and format.</li>
<li>
Application of technical knowledge and experience to support and assist with ticket resolution</li>
<li>
Where necessary, consult with other teams for their input in diagnosing and providing the customer with a resolution to the issue</li>
<li>
Where necessary, engage with third-party software vendors to ensure issues are being worked on and resolved</li>
<li>
Where appropriate, escalating feedback received from customers to ensure continuous improvement of the ClientBooks application and Community Care service.</li>
<li>
Staying up-to-date on all product releases and future roadmap</li>
<li>
Collaborate with Data Analyst on reporting relaying support tickets</li>
<li>
Contribute to departmental initiatives and identification of opportunities for process improvement</li>
<li>
Analyzing and looking through application logs to help resolve product bugs/issues</li>
<li>
Improve and document the technical processes around application support</li>
</ul>

Demonstrated ability dealing with escalated customer issues</li>
<li>
Strong information-gathering and troubleshooting skills</li>
<li>
Proficient with Microsoft, Excel and Google Suite</li>
<li>
Tech-savvy</li>
<li>
Website debugging experience preferred</li>
</ul>
