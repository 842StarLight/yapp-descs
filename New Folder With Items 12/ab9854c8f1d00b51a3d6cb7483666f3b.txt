<h2>Application Engineer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">Are you looking for a rewarding Engineering Job? If so, Agri-Cover, Inc., an aftermarket automotive manufacturer is looking for an Application Engineer in our Jamestown, ND location. Visit their website and apply online at www.agricover.com/careers/
<br><br>
Summary
<br><br>
Agri-Cover utilizes a configurator to convert customer input into manufacturing standard part number configurations, configured on-the-fly part number configurations, and BOM's (Bill of Material), and MO's (Manufacturing Orders) for production. This position would maintain the configurator (Experlogix) logic, interface, database, as well as creation of new features, logic, and interfaces as directed.
<br><br>
Essential Duties and Responsibilities include the following. Other duties may be assigned.
<br><br>
Responsible for the overall operation and maintenance of the Configurator (Experlogix).
<br><br>
Collaborating with others that may be involved with specific areas of the configurator logic, configurator interacting with other software, and the user interface.
<br><br>
Communicate and collaborate engineering and IT related information effectively with other engineering, manufacturing, IT and office personnel.
<br><br>
Responsible for collaborating with configurator suppliers including Engineering, Manufacturing, etc.
<br><br>
Collaborating with configurator customers including Customer Service, Sales, etc.
<br><br>
Ensuring proper data and logic are created and maintained for correct configuration inputs and outputs utilizing a user-friendly interface.
<br><br>
Writing engineering/IT communication that conveys technical information in an understandable non-technical fashion.
<br><br>
Maintain product knowledge related to PN (Part Number) BOM structure, Routing, and MO logic in a Design/Manufacturing environment.
<br><br>
Designing, developing and implementing programs and applications - specifically related to Experlogix.
<br><br>
Analyze customer needs and create innovative solutions to common problems.
<br><br>
Prototype and test solutions.
<br><br>
Debug, test, code improvements and re-design tasks in the configurator (Experlogix).
<br><br>
Provide customers with ongoing technical support.
<br><br>
Respond to issues and provide updates.
<br><br>
Acquire and analyze customer needs and problems.
<br><br>
Make recommendations to the Engineering, Manufacturing, IT and Customer Service teams to satisfy needs and develop solutions.
