<h2>Marketing Outreach Coordinator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>POSITION SUMMARY:</b></p>
<p>
The initial objective of this entry level position is to build stronger relationships between our surgery centers and local community resources. This individual will be expected to do extensive direct selling and awareness-building with targeted physicians (and their office staff), with the expectation of increasing referrals from them to the center over time. Additionally, this person will also be required to perform outreach to relevant businesses, organizations and institutions as well as any pre-approved activities (such as health fairs or race events) within the local community. This position will be expected, over time, to generate a positive quantifiable impact on center volume. This position represents the local marketing efforts for the practice as well as the center.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
ESSENTIAL RESPONSIBILITIES:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Conduct face-to-face sales presentations on center(s) and physician capabilities to referring physicians.</li>
<li>Act as primary relationship-builder between centers and the referring community.</li>
<li>Analyze existing referral trends to develop targeting priorities and sales strategies.</li>
<li>Capture and track performance data to determine impact of outreach/strategies.</li>
<li>Develop presentations and materials that can be used to increase interaction with center personnel.</li>
<li>Identify opportunities to tie in other AmSurg marketing programs (e.g. ethnic segment marketing).</li>
<li>Coordinate activities with other marketing personnel to maximize effort and outcomes.</li>
<li>Regular and reliable attendance is required.</li>
</ul>
