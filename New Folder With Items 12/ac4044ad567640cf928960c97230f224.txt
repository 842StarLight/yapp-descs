<p><b>The opportunity:</b>
</p>
<p>The role Systems Reliability Engineer is to build solutions to enhance availability, performance and stability of OpenText services as well as automating away repetitive work.<br>
<br>
You'll be working on non-production and production environments, monitoring, data collection and configuration management, as well as disaster recovery planning, capacity engineering, reliability improvement initiatives and platform automation.<br>
<br>
Your mission will be to use cutting edge technology for monitoring and maintaining the day to day operations of the entire production infrastructure for OpenText Discovery on our AWS platform.</p>
<p><b>You are great at:
</b></p>
<p>Provide attention to incidents according to Service Level Agreements.<br>
<br>
Take ownership and accountability for the incident resolution process.<br>
<br>
Act as a technical liaison with other teams to evaluate and report bugs.<br>
<br>
Establish and maintain a good relationship with team members, Product Development, Customer Service and Sales.<br>
<br>
Act as backup for other team members when necessary.<br>
<br>
May requires rotating shift work.<br>
<br>
On-call rotation is required, as 7x24x365 support is required.</p>
<p><b>What it takes:
</b></p>
<p>The ability to understand and maintain Scripting software<br>
<br>
Deep understanding of Windows systems<br>
<br>
Good working knowledge of Linux<br>
<br>
Hands on experience with cloud infrastructure; AWS a plus - Min 4 years<br>
<br>
Experience with AWS Web Services EC2, RDS, VPC, IAM, Route53, S3, and other AWS Services<br>
<br>
Strong working knowledge of Cloud operational best practices.<br>
<br>
Experience with installing and configuring Apache and Tomcat.<br>
<br>
Deep expertise in Monitoring distributed systems application architectures<br>
<br>
Exposure to &amp; maintenance of configuration management tools at scale</p>
