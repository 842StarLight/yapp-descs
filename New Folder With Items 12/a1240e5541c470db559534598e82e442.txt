<h2>Associate Marketing Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Our Associate Marketing Managers are valued team members in the marketing department of our firm. This position is considered entry level to start during training which includes a comprehensive overview of basic marketing strategy, direct advertising, and promotional sales techniques.</p>
<p>
Responsibilities and Duties:</p>
<ul>
<li>
<p>Train with current account managers in our office on full-cycle sales with a focus on branding and client products and services</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Using a consultative marketing approach</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Assist with membership enrollments and follow up with prospects via phone</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Maintain a high degree of product and service expertise</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Promote and enhance company reputation, providing professional, effective assistance</p>
<br>
</li>
</ul>
<p>
</p>
<p>Qualifications and Skills:</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>
<p>There's no one "right" profile. If you're a superstar now, or one in the making, let's talk</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>High level of motivation to achieve personal goals and improve earning potential</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Strong communication skills, with the ability to engage and persuade</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Prior experience in marketing, sales and promotions is preferable but this is considered a junior role where training is provided</p>
</li>
<li><p>Degree or internship in a related area of marketing, business management or communications is considered an asset to the role</p></li>
</ul>
</div></div>
