<p><b><i>Job Summary
</i></b></p>
<p>The Customer Planning Manager is responsible for leading all customer planning initiatives supporting <b>Campbell Snacks Growth Channels teams (PF Trade Systems)</b> with a fact-based planning approach to drive highly effective forecasting and optimization of trade marketing funds. This role will facilitate the translation of key brand strategies to customer execution and work with their respective teams to ensure the delivery of net sales targets within provided trade budgets.</p>
<p></p>
<p><b><i>Essential Job Duties</i></b></p>
<ul>
<li>Manages and facilitates annual and monthly planning process for the Campbell’s Snacks sales organization.</li>
<li>Responsible for the delivery of Gross and Net Sales targets for assigned geography.</li>
<li>Coordinates with cross functional leaders, Sales Strategy, Category Management and Customer Teams to deliver against brand strategies.</li>
<li>Partner with field sales to ensure plans deliver against Merchandising, Assortment, Pricing and Shelving (MAPS) standards.</li>
<li>Develops customer level distribution and merchandising plans with supporting insights and measurable execution plans.</li>
<li>Monitors brand trends at key account level for performance mediation.</li>
<li>Conducts retailer event post performance analysis.</li>
<li>Power user of T-Pro (trade promotion optimization software) and IRI to build effective consumption based analysis and optimize promotional performance.</li>
<li>May perform other duties as required</li>
</ul>
<p><b><i>Scope of Responsibility
</i></b></p>
<p>This position provides analytical support and guidance to assigned accounts. Work is performed independently with a moderate to high level of autonomy. No supervisory responsibility. Responsible for planning support over $240MM Gross Sales and over $26MM Promotional trade spending. Will be expected to collaborate with counterparts in customer planning and the IBP team, including learning new e4 trade system as SL systems are migrated.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b><i>Knowledge and Skill Requirements</i></b></p>
<ul>
<li>Experience managing strategic/tactical trade promotion plans and conducting post performance analysis</li>
<li>Strong knowledge and use of syndicated data resources (IRI &amp; AC Nielsen).</li>
<li>Demonstrated ability to influence across the organization</li>
<li>Financial acumen, P&amp;L management exposure</li>
<li>Written and verbal communication skills</li>
<li>Problem solving skills</li>
<li>Proficient with all Microsoft applications</li>
</ul>
<p><b><i>Education</i></b> <b><i>and Experience</i></b></p>
<p>
Bachelor’s Degree and a minimum of 5 years industry experience, including trade management, customer planning, customer/field account management.</p>
