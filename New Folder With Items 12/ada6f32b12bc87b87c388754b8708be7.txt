<p>
Tilcon Connecticut Inc., a CRH company, has played a vital role in building infrastructure around our state, serving the market from 23 locations throughout Connecticut. As a leading supplier of quality crushed stone, hot mix asphalt, and ready mix concrete, Tilcon Connecticut has supported the construction of superior roads, buildings and bridges. The most respected name in the construction industry, from the quarry to the road, Tilcon Connecticut does it all!</p>

<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Position Overview</b></p>
<p>
Provides support to the operating company by partnering with operations across the business in coordinating and performing accounting functions.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Key Responsibilities (Essential Duties and Functions)</b></p>
<p></p>
<br>
<ul>
<li>
Completing monthly accounting closings.</li>
<li>
Assist with parent company reporting.</li>
<li>
Maintaining/monitoring accounting records.</li>
<li>
Cash management and bank reconciliations.</li>
<li>
Completion of various accounting projects and financial analyses.</li>
<li>
Liaising with the company’s regional operations.</li>
<li>
Implement and follow accounting standards and controls as developed by or in conjunction with the Controller.</li>
<li>
Processing of purchase orders, invoices and payment to vendors.</li>
<li>
Reconciliation of local bank accounts and funding local accounts to corporate office.</li>
<li>
Month End Closings: Journal entries, A/P accruals, inventory adjustments, review P&amp;L’s – compare to budget and investigate variances, income recognition.</li>
<li>
Review status of current jobs and post monthly income, sales and use taxes – ensure appropriate taxes are paid when necessary; provide Corporate with monthly calculation of tax payable.</li>
<li>
Inventory Management of aggregates, liquid asphalt, fuels, etc.</li>
<li>
Equipment: accurate accounting of all mobile equipment, monitor fuel accounting systems, IFTA tax reporting, administer and manage vehicle leases – preparation of vehicle purchasing and proper follow-up to get vehicle into the fleet.</li>
<li>
CAPEX; assist in preparation of capex proposals, monitor capex vs. budget</li>
<li>
Assist with preparation of annual budgets, analyze actual costs vs. budget.</li>
<li>
Ability to perform multi-tasks in a fast pace environment.</li>
<li>
Ability to manage time effectively and set priorities.</li>
<li>
Adhere to all state and federal regulations, if applicable, as set forth by the US Department of Transportation (DOT), Occupational Safety and Health Administration (OSHA), Mine Safety and Health Administration (MSHA), or any other regulatory agency.</li>
<li>
Interface with operations, project managers and senior management on all aspects of accounting.</li>
<li>
Respond to inquiries from Controller, Vice President and CFO.</li>
</ul>

<p><b>
Qualifications
</b></p>
<p>To perform this job successfully, an individual must be able to perform each essential duty satisfactorily.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Education/Experience</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
BS or BA in Accounting is required</li>
<li>
At least 2 to 4 years of accounting experience</li>
<li>
Construction accounting software experience preferred but not required.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Work Requirements</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Individual must possess a working knowledge and understanding of Generally Accepted Accounting Principles, Practices and Procedures on manual and automated systems including P&amp;L’s and relevant balance sheet accounts.</li>
<li>
Must demonstrate strong numerical skills and the ability to analyze data and draw conclusions.</li>
<li>
Attention to detail and presentation of the work product to a high standard is required.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Knowledge/Skill Requirements</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Individual must possess a questioning mind and the ability to think independently.</li>
<li>
Ability to challenge situations in a constructive manner and present reasonable alternatives.</li>
<li>
Must possess superior problem-solving skills: ability to identify and investigate potential problems with respect to accounting and internal control issues.</li>
<li>
Self-directed and proactive, work independently with broad guidelines.</li>
<li>
Build teamwork and cooperation among individuals and departments and partner regularly with operations in a consultative manner.</li>
<li>
Demonstrate effective communication skills by listening effectively, transmitting accurate information and actively seeking feedback.</li>
<li>
Demonstrate excellent organizational skills with the ability to prioritize and work effectively on multiple tasks in a fast-paced, demanding environment.</li>
<li>
Be dependable/reliable and follow through on commitments, producing timely work and pitching in to get the job completed with quality and precision.</li>
<li>
Be attentive to detail and monitoring adherence to company standards.</li>
<li>
Demonstrate a proficiency with MS Excel, Windows and Word.</li>
<li>
Understand and perform a variety of accounting tasks.</li>
<li>
Assist with development, implementation, and documentation of internal controls.</li>
</ul>
