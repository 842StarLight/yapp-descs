<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Responsibilities</b></h2>
<div><b>
Customer Obsession</b></div>
<ul>
<li><div>
Help customers discover, buy, use, and love Microsoft technology solutions and services that meets and exceeds their individual expectations</div></li>
<li><div>
Illuminate product offerings and cross platform ecosystem integration through in-store experiences and learning engagements that build on increased utilization with devices customers own and love</div></li>
</ul>
<div><b>
Adaptability</b></div>
<ul>
<li><div>
Effectively respond to changing in-store environment, circumstances, and situations through customer engagements</div></li>
<li><div>
Have a level of intuitiveness to thrive in a dynamic and fast paced retail environment</div></li>
</ul>
<div><b>
Drive for Results</b></div>
<ul>
<li><div>
Tenaciously pursues positive outcomes by using effective approaches to solve problems</div></li>
<li><div>
Takes responsibility in delivering on commitments and seeks positive results that benefit customers, both externally and internally</div></li>
</ul>
<div><b>
Influencing for Impact</b></div>
<ul>
<li><div>
Continuously maintain product knowledge by learning through others along with self-educating, showcasing product and service offerings building on our #WeWinAsATeam culture</div></li>
<li><div>
Possesses the ability to persuade positive business outcomes through in-store interactions with customers and team mates</div></li>
</ul>
<div><b>
Collaboration</b></div>
<ul>
<li><div>
Feel empowered to engage with peers, store leaders, corporate and field team members in the development of innovative ways to make a larger impact in our customers' lives</div></li>
<li><div>
Combines resources and joins efforts with teammates to achieve store and company goals</div></li>
</ul>
