<p><b>Position - Rebar Estimator</b></p>
<p><b>Summary</b></p>
<p>Trowbridge Steel is a rebar division of the Salit Group of Companies. Salit is an integrated network of companies that deliver steel solutions in North America.<br>Now in our fifth generation, we have been family-owned and operated since 1905.</p>
<p>Our team is passionate about building strong relationships with our customers, suppliers and industry<br>partners by delivering outstanding service and value.</p>
<p>We have operations in Ontario, Quebec, Western Canada and the US. Through our steel service centers<br>and rebar fabrication division, we offer a wide range of steel solutions.</p>
<p>We are currently seeking an estimator with the opportunity to grow into lead estimator!</p>
<p><b>Position Functions</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Prepare timely estimates of the quantities of fabricated reinforcing bar, wire mesh, accessories and related products required for bidding projects.</li>
<li>Build relationships with customers and provide sales support</li>
<li>Perform take-offs from customer-provided drawings to determine bid quantities.</li>
<li>Communicate effectively and professionally with customers and branch teammates regarding all aspects of the estimating and bidding process.</li>
<li>Maintain an ongoing bid calendar, including tracking for addenda and updates, to ensure accurate estimating and to keep management aware of bid schedules and upcoming work.</li>
<li>Ability to read and interpret blueprints for estimating reinforcing bar and affiliated materials for concrete reinforcement</li>
<li>Must adhere to Trowbridge Steel’s safety and quality programs and standards.</li>
<li>Demonstrate conduct consistent with Trowbridge Steel’s company values.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Working Conditions</b></p>
<p>The successful incumbent will be working 40+ hours per week in an office setting.</p>
<p>Occasional travel to various customer locations will be required.</p>
<p><b>Physical Requirements</b></p>
