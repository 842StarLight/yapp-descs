<b>Title:</b> Construction Sales Representative
<br><b>Location:</b> New York - Remote
<br><b>Department:</b> Precision Tools
<br><b>Reports to:</b> World Wide Sales Manager
<br><br>
The Construction Sales Representative will be responsible for developing sales opportunities with a unique interior positioning solution that is designed to provide interior contractors with improved productivity and accuracy for layout of interior walls, ceilings and fixtures. You will be expected to identify new opportunities, develop and maintain a sales pipeline, provide technical sales support and meet sales targets. The Construction Sales Representative will report to the Americaâ€™s Sales Manager for the Precision Tools business.
<br><br>
Ideally you will have knowledge of construction applications, particularly with interior construction of walls, ceilings and HVAC. You will need to demonstrate an ability to create new opportunities, prospect and close sales. You will require strong verbal and written communication skills, needs analysis, positioning, business justification and presentation skills. Demonstrated ability to create and articulate solutions that meet customer needs. Ability to travel is required.
<br><br>
<b>Expectations :</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Gain sufficient product knowledge to be able to demonstrate the benefits of the solution to potential clients. Be able to take a CAD file and enter it into the system and show an end user how the product works and develop an effective ROI statement.</li>
<li>Identify potential customers and build a sales pipeline that supports or exceeds the sales targets.</li>
<li>Prospect for new opportunities and record progress in Salesforce.</li>
<li>Create a business plan for the region and build a backwards integrated sales plan with key milestones to measure success factors.</li>
<li>Expand the business plan to factor in lessons learned from initial activity.</li>
<li>Provide feedback to Sales Manager and Product Management for improvement of go-to-market plan.</li>
<li>Develop relationships working in partnership with Business Development manager with third party vendors of drywall materials.</li>
<li>Meet or exceed sales targets.</li>
</ul>
<b>Required Skills/Qualifications:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>BS or equivalent work experience, ideally in either sales of construction products/materials or construction experience particularly in interior construction</li>
<li>Experience with developing sales pipeline, prospecting and a proven track record in sales.</li>
<li>Ability to work with CAD files</li>
<li>Strong interpersonal, written and verbal skills</li>
<li>Ability to travel. 60% - majority of travel in New York State.</li>
<li>Be flexible, able to work under pressure with minimal supervision and meet deadlines</li>
<li>Work ethically and within company guidelines</li>
</ul>
