Intelligence Surveillance &amp; Reconnaissance (ISR) Segment is one of three L3 business segments, and provides a broad range of aerospace systems, security and detection systems, and pilot training.<br>

<div>
<b>About L3 ISR Segment:</b>
</div>
<div>L3’s ISR Systems Segment brings together a broad collection of some 30+ unique L3 divisions across 10 business areas to offer a diverse portfolio of innovative sensor and intelligence technologies across the maritime, land, airborne, space, cyber and warfighter domains. Comprised of 15,000 employees in 5 countries including in 16 states in 40+ locations, we are a global partner of choice delivering innovative capabilities to gather, share and protect intelligence for a more secure world.<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<div><b>Position Description:
</b></div>
<div>The Business Development group within the Reconnaissance Missions Systems (RMS) Sector is currently seeking a detailed-oriented, highly self-motivated Business Development professional with a proven record of successfully bringing products and systems from conceptualization to market, for our Europe/NATO Intelligence, Surveillance, and Reconnaissance (ISR) pursuit’s team. This Senior BD Manager position will serve as a leader for the Sector’s NATO and European airborne ISR growth campaign. The Senior BD Manager will be a key member of the business development team focused on identifying and winning new airborne ISR-related business within the international Intelligence Community (IC) and associated European Minister of Defense (MoD) customers.<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<div>This individual is directly responsible for establishing and maintaining a pipeline of new business opportunities from lead generation, opportunity identification, maturation and shaping through capture and proposal submission that will deliver significant top and bottom line growth for L3 Technologies RMS Sector. A successful candidate should BD or capture experience in the international market, preferably Europe/NATO. The ideal candidate should have a fundamental understanding of ELINT and COMINT sensor and enterprise processing, exploitation and dissemination (PED) technologies. Must have the ability to work with the technical teams to develop, plan, manage and deliver complex solutions for the multi domain environment. The successful candidate should be an agile, innovative thinker and executor able to move from a strategic level to the implementation level in interactions with multiple audiences. This person shall be an excellent communicator with the ability to clearly communicate and present information across all levels of the organization both internally and externally.<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<div>Duties include, but are not limited to, development and implementation of country business plans, identification and implementation of marketing activities, and adherence to compliance, regulatory requirements and strong business ethics.<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<div><b>Key Responsibilities:
</b></div>
<ul>
<li><div>Develops and implements regional business plans to consistently achieve and exceed sales targets for the European and NATO Region.

</div></li>
<li><div>Collaborate with counterparts from the other regions, corporate assets both regionally and globally and the extended teams in L3 ISR Segment to achieve goals and growth objectives.
</div></li>
<li><div>Lead with vision and radiate a “can do” team spirit. Apply conflict resolution where required, facilitate change and solve problems in the best interest of the company.
</div></li>
<li><div>Responsible for providing guidance to marketing team to prioritize country and regional market needs. Ensure professional effective launching of extension products.
</div></li>
<li><div>Develops key relationships with industry/market thought leaders, organizations, and institutions across the region.
</div></li>
<li><div>Assist with the efforts required for any regional events and/or marketing initiative
</div></li>
<li><div>Work with the indirect teams (CDR’s) to ensure successful pursuit compliance with all US Government and Corporate guidelines.
</div></li>
<li><div>Ensure accurate sales forecasts each month/quarter as well as other information as required for executive leadership.
</div></li>
<li><div>Promote L3 ISR capabilities, applications data sheets and technical information to target customers in the specific market.

<li><div>Responsible for keeping the International Business Development teams informed of all sales related activity, market trends, highlights and competitor activity within the region.
</div></li>
