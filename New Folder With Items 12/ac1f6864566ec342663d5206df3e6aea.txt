<div>
<h4 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities</b></h4>
<ul>
<li>Manage purchasing, planning activities and select suppliers for best cost, quality, delivery and service.
</li>
<li>Ensure materials are purchased to quoted pricing, manage suppliers to meet company production targets, schedules and cost targets.</li>
<li>Manage Purchasing Bonded Inventory programs, shared MRP Forecast programs.
</li>
<li>Perform other work as assigned by the manager.</li>
</ul>
</div>
<div>
<h4 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Work Experience</b></h4>
<ul>
<li>Experience in Electronics Contract Manufacturing is preferred.
</li>
<li>Strong computer skills, including experience using ERP software packages, Word and PowerPoint.</li>
<li>Must have advanced knowledge in Excel: Pivot Tables, V Lookup, and Macro Formulas.</li>
<li>Strong organizational skills with the ability to perform and prioritize multiple tasks seamlessly with excellent attention to detail.</li>
<li>Excellent communication skills.</li>
</ul>
</div>
<div>

Knowledge of:
<ul>
<li>Basic purchasing procedures, quoting procedures, quality management concepts.</li>
<li>Electronic components, such as resistors, capacitors, diodes, PCBs, etc.</li>
<li>Supply chain management skills, inventory control principles, MRP/ERP concepts.</li>
</ul>
</div>
<div>
