<h2>Dealer Technical Support Manager - Region 3</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div><div><div><div>
<div>
<p><b>Position Specifics:</b></p>
<p><b>Department: </b>Service</p>
<p><b>Reports to:</b> Corporate Service Manager</p>
<p><b>Manages:</b> Field Service Technicians and Dealer Technical Support Specialists</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Purpose:</b></p>
<p>To increase customer satisfaction and loyalty by delivering an exceptional customer support experience. Provide technical support to customers via phone calls, text messages, emails, and field support for products and services offered through the dealership. May occasionally be required to provide technical support to service personnel.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Manage Field Service Technicians and customer call center
</li>
<li>Manage Work Orders, generation through completion, for work within determined region utilizing available technology</li>
<li>Provide Technical Support to customers, accurately diagnose problems utilizing Service Advisor Remote, Remote Display Access, DTAC/CCMS, and other John Deere resources to provide solutions</li>
<li>Take responsibility to ensure customer interactions always result in an effective and timely solution, follow-up with customers after repairs have been made</li>
<li>Manage the Expert Alert program and Machine Health Dashboard tool for the region, provide JDLink support, evaluate JDLink Alerts. Create Work Orders based on data received or communicate information to responsible Service Departments as required</li>
<li>Manages recruiting, staffing, and employee development activities for employees reporting to this position. Including time and attendance, performance reviews, etc.</li>
<li>Work additional hours during seasonally busy times, some weekend shifts as required</li>
<li>Support management in the pursuit of corporate policies, plans, goals, and longterm objectives</li>
<li>Foster a great place to work environment, encouraging accountability, open communication, teamwork, and a commitment to serving the customer</li>
<li>Maintain a positive and professional working relationship with peers, management, and support resources, with a constant commitment to teamwork and exemplary customer service</li>
<li>Ensure that companys reputation and image in the community is consistent with company Core Values</li>
<li>Participate in ongoing training to continually develop technical knowledge and skill sets</li>
<li>Develop and deliver training to customers and employees during off-season periods</li>
<li>Support and participate in companys marketing plan for demonstrations and clinics.</li>
<li>Follow all safety rules and regulations in performing work assignments</li>
<li>Complete all reports and forms required in conjunction with work assignments</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
</div>
<p></p>
<div>
<p><b>Experience, Education, Skills and Knowledge:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>3+ years experience in Service Department operations</li>
<li>Demonstrated outstanding customer service and communication skills</li>
<li>Proven experience solving problems effectively with best possible outcome for all involved</li>
<li>Experience using computer software such as Microsoft Office, Dealer Business System and internet-based systems</li>
<li>Flexibility to travel when required</li>
<li>Knowledge of Deere systems:
<ul>
<li>Service ADMIN Portal</li>
<li>Machine Dashboard</li>
<li>Expert Alerts</li>
<li>John Deere Customer Experience Portal</li>
<li>Remote Display Access</li>
<li>DTAC/CCMS</li>
<li>Parts ADVISOR</li>
<li>Service ADVISOR Remote
</li>
<li>JDLink</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>Ability to write and speak effectively to individuals and groups</li>
<li>Familiar with John Deere and competitive products</li>
<li>Ability to analyze and interpret internal reports</li>
<li>Ability to work extended hours and weekends as needed</li>
<li>High School Diploma or equivalent experience</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>The Dealer Technical Support Manager for Region 3 will service the Crowley, Welsh, Lake Charles, Eunice, and Opelousas locations.</b></p>
