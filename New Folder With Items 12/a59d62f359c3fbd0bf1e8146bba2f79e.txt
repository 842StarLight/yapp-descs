<b>OVERVIEW:</b><br>
The Denver Nuggets are seeking a highly motivated and energetic professional to join the Ticket Sales leadership team as a Director of Membership Services. The Director of Membership Services is responsible for strategizing and executing Season Ticket Member retention plans, leading a team of Membership Services Executives and a Membership Services Manager, and providing superior customer service to all Season Ticket Members.<br>
<br>
<b>MINIMUM POSITION RESPONSIBILITIES:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Utilize a solid knowledge of the sales process to increase retention rates &amp; generate new ticket sales through referrals and the existing fan base.</li>
<li>Provide leadership and guidance to Membership Services Manager and a team of Membership Services Executives, each of whom have a dedicated account base to retain.</li>
<li>Train Membership Services Executives on world-class customer service techniques.</li>
<li>Provide service and attention to detail for clients year-round.</li>
<li>Develop and execute a detailed events and touchpoint strategy in order to create strong relationships between Season Ticket Members, the Denver Nuggets brand and their Service Executive.</li>
<li>Provide and serve as the primary communication outlet on all matters related to Denver Nuggets Season Ticket Members, responsible for an action plan to have direct one-on-one communication with Season Ticket Members, working in conjunction with all departments in setting a standard level of care with Nuggets fans.</li>
<li>Develop and execute numerous in-season and off-season sales &amp; service touch point events.</li>
<li>Maintain customer database and facilitate communication of renewal campaigns.</li>
<li>Provide and update reporting for VP of Ticket Sales and Service through ticketing system and Microsoft CRM.</li>
</ul>
<br>
