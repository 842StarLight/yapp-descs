<h2>Inside Sales (B-2-B Appointment Setter) – (Part-Time/Full-Time)</h2>

<p><b>SaveOn a Direct mail/Digital advertising company located in Troy, MI is growing, and seeking great individuals to join our winning team!</b></p>
<p>We are seeking <b>Inside Sales/Appointment Setters</b> (Full-time or Part-Time) in Troy, MI<b> </b>(Willing to Train)!!!! <b>This is not a remote position.</b></p>
<p>Our Inside Sales/Appointment Setter will drive growth in overall revenue and appointments on behalf of new customer acquisitions. They will drive revenue from new prospects, and using the resources provided to schedule and procures new sales opportunities.</p>
<p><b>We Offer our Inside Sales/Appointment Setter:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Guaranteed salary + Commission</li>
<li>Medical, Dental and Vision healthcare programs</li>
<li>Short-term, long-term and life insurance plans and 401k plan</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Duties and Corresponding Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Incoming Calls – this includes taking incoming calls from prospective clients or customers and they are setting appointments for a sales representative to meet with each individual.</li>
<li>Outgoing Calls – making outgoing calls are being done by most appointment setters by way of lists made by people who express interest through websites, e – mail, or direct mail coupons or through automated dialing systems.</li>
<li>Data Entry – Encoding or entry of data is an additional duty of an appointment setter as he or she lists any appointments he or she has made throughout the day.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Requirements of the Inside Sales/Appointment Setter:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Excellent communication skills</li>
<li>Strong listening and probing skills to identify business needs, desires and opportunities</li>
<li>Passion and perseverance for new business</li>
<li>Career and goal oriented</li>
<li>Computer literate - internet navigation, Outlook, and Word</li>
<li>Strong Data Entry Skill</li>
<li>Strong time management and organization skills</li>
<li>Ability to think on your feet and communicate well</li>
</ul>
