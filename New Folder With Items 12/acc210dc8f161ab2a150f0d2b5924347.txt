<h2>Customer Service Support 4</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Job Title</b></h3>
Customer Service Support 4<p></p>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Job Description</b></h3>
<p><b>
In this role, you have the opportunity to</b></p>
<p>
be our initial communications link between our customer base (internal and external) and our sales team. You will coordinate the entry of product orders for our customers and the tracking &amp; communication of price discrepancies.</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>
You are responsible for</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Utilizing a variety of communication methods, processing all orders through the SAP system</li>
<li>
Answering phone calls and questions in professional manner to ensure the highest level of customer satisfaction</li>
<li>
Complete product shipments/returns
</li>
<li>Tracking price discrepancies received from order entry on the pricing compliance program spreadsheet</li>
<li>
Assuring timely processing of sales teammate requests each day</li>
<li>
Processing return requests, for credit or exchange, and replacement shipments</li>
<li>
Assuring timely processing of customer orders each day</li>
<li>
Verifying pricing, shipping address, and item numbers on each order</li>
<li>
Verifying each request via the same method it was received</li>
<li>
Answering phone calls and questions in professional manner to ensure the highest level of customer satisfaction and directing calls to appropriate departments in a professional and timely manner</li>
<li>
Assisting customer support as needed</li>
<li>
Coordinating efforts throughout the sales order cycle to insure product is shipped accurately and timely</li>
<li>
Maintaining necessary files and procedures insuring highest level of compliance to FDA/TUV and Sarbanes-Oxley guidelines</li>
<li>
Processing product returns (RMA’s)</li>
<li>
Having a high attention to detail while performing administrative duties and following all company SOPs</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>
You are a part of</b></p>
<p>
our highly influential customer service team who can greatly impact the way we support and deliver our strategic objectives to both our internal customers and external customers.</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>
To succeed in this role, you should have the following skills and experience</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Requires high school diploma or equivalent and 1+ years of experience in customer service</li>
<li>
Highly proficient in Microsoft Office applications, phone skills, and Excel</li>
<li>
High attention to detail, strong organizational skills, computer proficiency, superb communication skills, excellent customer service experience</li>
<li>
Demonstrated time management, problem solving skills and the ability to work well with others in a professional environment</li>
<li>
SAP software experience a plus</li>
</ul>
