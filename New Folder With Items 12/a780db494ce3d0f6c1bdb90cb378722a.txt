<h2>Office Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Position Summary</b>: Provide uncompromised service, support, and administration to internal and external Service Center customers. The Office Manager aides the Service Center Manager with overseeing the day-to-day activities within the branch. This individual must be able to effectively communicate and motivate team members to achieve service center goals.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Essential Functions</b>:</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<ul>
<li>
Comply with state and federal regulations, company policies, procedures, and guidelines</li>
<li>
Oversee and manage administrative staff and functions</li>
<li>
Provide initial and ongoing training to administrative staff</li>
<li>
Confer with customers by telephone, email, and mail</li>
<li>
Act as point of contact for escalated customer resolution</li>
<li>
Respond to answering service messages</li>
<li>
Audit all data entry into client management software system for quality assurance</li>
<li>
Persuade customers to pay on account balance through proactive outbound collection efforts via telephone, email, and mail</li>
<li>
Configure custom billing options as necessary</li>
<li>
Invoice services rendered</li>
<li>
Manage 3rd party collection process</li>
<li>
Process daily deposit, including posting payments, balancing, handling cash, checks, money orders, and web payments</li>
<li>
Enter new services into client management software system</li>
<li>
Schedule appointments and new sales for Technicians and Sales</li>
<li>
Maintain and update customer account information</li>
<li>
Support other office administrative staff in the completion of their duties when needed</li>
<li>
Attend and complete training and meetings as required</li>
<li>
Perform other duties as required by management</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Knowledge, Skills &amp; Abilities</b>:</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<ul>
<li>
Customer Service - Knowledge of principles and processes for providing customer service. This includes active listening, assessing customer needs, and meeting quality standards for services.</li>
<li>
Compliance &amp; Safety - Knowledge of relevant regulation, procedures, and documentation to ensure effective, compliant, and safe treatment and services.</li>
<li>
English Language - Ability to write, speak, and communicate in English and ability to read and understand technical product labels.</li>
<li>
Active Listening - Giving full attention to what other people are saying, taking time to understand the points being made, asking questions as appropriate, and not interrupting at inappropriate times.</li>
<li>
Active Learning - Understanding the implications of new information for both current and future problem-solving and decision-making.</li>
<li>
Time Management - Managing one's own time and the time of others. Ability to work under pressure and meet deadlines</li>
<li>
Computers - Ability to transact and interact with multiple software systems with accuracy and attention to detail.</li>
<li>
Persuasion - Persuading others to change their minds or behavior.</li>
<li>
Mathematics - ability to calculate balances, debits, and credits &amp; use a calculator</li>
<li>
Social Perceptiveness - Being aware of others' reactions and understanding why they react as they do. Demonstrates empathy and discretion.</li>
<li>
Administration and Management - Knowledge of business and management principles</li>
<li>
Instructing - Teaching others how to do something</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>

Minimum Qualifications</b>:</p>
<p></p>
<br>

<li>

Working knowledge of Microsoft Office Suite</li>
<li>
Compliance with Drug-free Workplace Policy</li>
