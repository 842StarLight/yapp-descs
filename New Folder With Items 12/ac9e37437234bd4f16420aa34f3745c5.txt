<h2>Warehouse / Parts Room Supervisor</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p>Atlantic Design, Inc is an established leader in the design of custom equipment for the blasting and coating industry. We are currently seeking a <b>Warehouse / Parts Room Supervisor </b>to add to our team for our growing company in the Baltimore area. This is an excellent opportunity for career minded individuals. This is a full-time position with benefits.</p>
<p><b>
Some Specific Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Supervise the Parts Room Helpers and Technicians: delegate daily continuous and special parts room tasks as appropriate, monitor that they are being completed accurately, thoroughly, and in a timely manner. Recruit, train and evaluate performance of assigned Helpers and Technicians.</li>
<li>
Daily receiving tasks include: Ensure that all newly received shipments are received into Fishbowl our inventory software and that packing lists are signed and turned into the office. Any discrepancies between purchase order and received items are brought to the attention of purchasing. Assess all received items for damage: test anything electric, asses motor freight delivered items for damage while the shipper is still on the premises to note on the BOL before they leave, check delivered fab parts against drawings to make sure nothing was missed or made improperly. Assess any customer-returned items before receiving in inventory software and arrange with purchasing if any malfunctioned item needs to be returned to original vendor.</li>
<li>
Daily shipping tasks include: Monitor open sales orders in inventory software in tandem with open pick tickets. Inform sales of any problems with issued sales orders and get with purchasing if anything needs to be ordered or changed to a drop ship. Ensure any orders that are scheduled to go out are packed up with packing list and accurate shipping label before last UPS pickup.</li>
<li>
When orders need to go out motor freight, make sure items are properly packaged, weight and dims entered into inventory software.
</li>
<li>Assist management in establishing Parts Room standards and procedures and advise on budget related needs.</li>
</ul>
<p>
<b>Expectations / Qualifications:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
High School diploma or GED.</li>
<li>
Minimum four to five years' experience in warehousing, shipping / receiving, inventory control or materials management in a manufacturing environment.</li>
<li>
Minimum three years of supervising / management experience.</li>
<li>
Experience with inventory software a plus.</li>
<li>
Forklift experience.</li>
<li>
Must be able to lift up to 50 lbs, and load / unload equipment.</li>
<li>
Must be willing to work overtime.</li>
<li>
Dependable with strong attention to detail.</li>
<li>
Excellent leadership and communication skills, and ability to hold staff accountable.</li>
</ul>
<p>
Atlantic Design offers a competitive salary and generous benefits package in addition to providing you with the opportunity to be a part of a dynamic and growing company. If you are interested and feel you are qualified for this position, then please apply!</p>
