<h2>Technical Support Account Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p><b>You will have -</b><br>
College Degree (Computer Science or related engineering field preferred but not required)<br>
5-7 years of experience in a Client Management/Relationship role<br>
Experience working in a Supporting role in the Wealth management industry, ideally for a Fintech company servicing banks, brokerage firms and/or Advisor firms.<br>
Working knowledge of Capital markets, security identification and asset classification<br>
<br>
<b>You will definitely possess these technical skills –</b><br>
Working knowledge of current web technology standards (RESTful Services, JavaScript)<br>
Understand basic SQL concepts and networking concepts<br>
Strong analytical and problem-solving skills<br>
Hands on experience in debugging and testing applications<br>
Exposure and hands on experience with various Customer Relationship Management applications</p>
<p><b>
Bird’s eye view of your role – </b>Yodlee, with its rapidly growing customer and user base, is seeking a skilled, driven, and innovative Technical Support Account Manager to support customers using our Wealth solutions. You will act as a client evangelist within Yodlee, across all levels, to ensure customers are successful with the adoption and successful usage of Wealth products. In this role, you will work cross-functionally with sales, consulting services, operations, technical specialists, product development and the product teams to ensure the customer's needs are well met, complex escalations are being addressed professionally and support processes are meeting service level agreements. You will proactively identify and enhance areas for service delivery while recommending changes to operating procedures. This role will allow you the ability to supervise Yodlee's release management and change management processes for your assigned customers.</p>
<p><b>
What will you bring –</b><br>
Excellent written and spoken communication skills<br>
Strong analytical and problem-solving skills<br>
Flexibility to occasionally work outside standard business hours<br>
Ability to prioritize and handle multiple client/partners, tasks and projects concurrently<br>
Ability to build and maintain relationships with our client base and internal departments</p>
<p><b>
What your performance objectives will be –
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Contribute to ensure successful and referable Wealth solution customers.</li>
<li>
Work directly with engineering and product teams to contribute and enhance product delivery.</li>
<li>
Manage a diverse and complex scope of support issues directly related to the customer, support and implementation of Yodlee products and solutions.</li>
<li>
Work closely with various internal groups to ensure timely resolution of open issues.</li>
<li>
Track all open problems relating to major customers, be ready to provide insightful reporting to customers and internal stakeholders.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
How will your lofty goals be translated into specific actions / short term goals –
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>By the end of the first 30 days, you will understand basic concept of our technology, platform and products. You would have also learnt how to use all internal tools intended to assist you in helping our customers.</li>
<li>
By the end of the first quarter, you should have fully mastered all the key concepts of Yodlee’s Wealth solutions technology as well as how our customers are using it. You should also have started to fully support assigned customers.</li>
<li>
By the end of first six months, you will have successfully supported all assigned customer and you have been a key link between our customers and our Product/Engineering teams.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
What’s in it for you? (EVP – Employee Value Proposition) –
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Be the a critical and highly visible Principal Technical Support Engineer fully dedicated to key Fortune 500 customers.</li>
<li>
Hold a key role within the organization as you will be directly responsible for the success of these Tier1 customers.</li>
<li>
Be a part of a fun, leading, innovating fintech company that specializes in personal finance management and the data aggregation space.</li>
</ul>
