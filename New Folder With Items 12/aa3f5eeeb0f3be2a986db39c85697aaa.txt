<div><div><div>
<div>Hertz Car Sales is one of the fastest growing dealer groups in the country. If you are tired of working for someone who isn’t working for you…Join our team….The Financial &amp; Insurance Manager will interact with customers and build customer loyalty by assisting them with financing options and products for the continued care of their vehicle purchase.</div>

<div><b>
Successful Candidates Will:</b></div>

<li><div>

<li><div>
Establish and maintain good working relationships with lenders and insurance product sources</div></li>
<li><div>
Sell finance and insurance products to customers and process payment</div></li>
<li><div>
Provide outstanding customer service that is memorable</div></li>

Train Sales Consultants on how to properly respond to F&amp;I questions</div></li>
<li><div>

<li><div>
Acquire and maintain proper licenses associated with the selling of all products and services as required by federal, state and local government agencies</div></li>
<li><div>

<li><div>
Follow all Legal Standards such as, Red Flags, OFAC and Safe-Guards rules</div></li>
<li><div>
Achieve product penetration targets.</div></li>
<li><div>
Use proven Menu training aids provided by our trainers to present the products to our clients.</div></li>
