<h2>Service Technician</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Position Summary</b></p>
<p>
We are looking for a fast learner who is interested in being trained to be a Service Technician. The Copier Technician is responsible for repairing and maintaining customer equipment in a prompt and professional manner and promoting a positive company image. You will be responsible for the installation, care, and maintenance of all integrated digital equipment and interface devices, including printers, engineering products, digital duplicators, copiers, multifunction and facsimile equipment.</p>
<p><b>
Duties and Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Accurately diagnose, repair, and maintain equipment and accessories</li>
<li>
Install and configure software pertaining to system products</li>
<li>
Provide key operator support and training</li>
<li>
Explain repairs completed to customers and how the customer can prevent it from re-occurring</li>
<li>
Perform scheduled preventive maintenance routines, adhering to factory specification on all customer equipment
</li>
<li>Reinforce operator training issues with customers</li>
<li>
Assist sales department with installation of new or trial equipment to ensure proper placement and operation</li>
<li>
Call dispatch promptly when contacted or when finished with an appointment to receive the next assignment
</li>
<li>Maintain proper inventory of parts through accurate record-keeping in personal log and work order</li>
<li>
Record completed work and parts used on the work order and on the machine's recording</li>
<li>
Updated preventative maintenance list</li>
<li>
Document expenses and trip logs</li>
<li>
Clean and maintain tools and supplies</li>
</ul>
