<h2>Logistics Coordinator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Whittlesey Landscape Supplies &amp; Recycling is looking for a reliable Logistics Coordinator to assist Dispatcher to create trucking routes that reduces costs and meet customer scheduling needs.</p>
<p>The Logistics Coordinator will learn to use the latest in routing software to establish daily trucking routes that make required stops outlined in customer contracts.</p>
<p>The Logistics Coordinator will need to be able to adjust to last minute changes, work with drivers to assist to schedule emergency pick-up or delivery for new and established clients when necessary.</p>
<p>Responsibilities:</p>
<ul>
<li>Assist to develop delivery routes and maintain contact with drivers throughout the day.</li>
<li>Assist to collaborate with management to modify daily routes to accommodate the needs of customers.</li>
<li>Assist as a communication liaison via phone, text, email and answer incoming phone calls from drivers on the road.</li>
<li>Assist to work with customers/vendors to resolve issues surrounding logistics and freight.</li>
<li>Cultivate strong relationships with drivers, customers and vendors to reduce the stress that comes with logistical issues and last-minute schedule changes.</li>
<li>Assist Dispatcher to monitor any sudden route changes or road construction issues and work with drivers to establish new routes for timely deliveries.</li>
<li>Assist to quote freight for sales team.</li>
<li>Work closely with internal departments to move/stock material to and from our five (5) facilities.</li>
<li>Must be computer literate and have strong communication skills. Flexibility and ability to take on multiple tasks at once.</li>
<li>Must be efficient in using Excel and other programs for information gathering and reporting.</li>
<li>Preferred basic knowledge of 18-Wheeler trucking logistics, Department of Transportation (DOT) rules and regulations and construction/aggregate industries.</li>
</ul>
