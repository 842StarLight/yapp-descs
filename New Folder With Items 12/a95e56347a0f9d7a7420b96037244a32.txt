<h2>Mortgage Loan Originator/Loan Officer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Alpine Mortgage, a growing mortgage lender, has the following opportunities available:</p>
<p><b>MORTGAGE LOAN ORIGINATOR/LOAN OFFICER OPPORTUNITIES</b></p>
<p>As a mortgage originator/loan officer, you will have access to a full range of residential loan products that will allow you to meet the needs of every customer that you speak with. We offer a variety of conventional, jumbo, FHA/VA and other non-conventional loan products at very competitive rates and credit scores that can go down to the 500s. We are a growing company that is committed to creating a fun, successful, and productive work environment. We provide the best training in the industry and free exclusive leads delivered in real time through our lead management system to help our loan originators become top producers. No cold calling is necessary. Inside and outside loan originator positions are available.</p>
<p>Candidates should have at least 1-2 years of mortgage sales experience or general sales experience. The ideal candidate will posses a positive, upbeat attitude who can communicate well with his or her potential clients.</p>
<p><b>BRANCH MANAGER/TEAM LEADER OPPORTUNITIES</b></p>
<p>As a Branch Manager for Alpine Mortgage Services, you will train and manage a team of new and experienced loan officers. Duties will include coaching and training your team on company programs and procedures, ensuring all loans meet regulatory guidelines, and setting goals and tracking individual and team performance.</p>
<p>Candidates should have at least 5 year experience in mortgage loan origination, strong communication skills and be able to manage multiple priorities in a fast-paced environment. The ideal candidate will also be familiar with loan origination and automated underwriting software and have experience in leading and coaching a sales team.</p>
