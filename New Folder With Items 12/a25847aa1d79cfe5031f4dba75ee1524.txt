<h2>Account Director, Ad Sales</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">Account Director, Ad Sales, East Coast
<br><br>
Townsquare Media is looking for an experienced and energetic ad sales director, who is a passionate music fan to sell across XXL, Taste of Country, Loudwire and other music sites within the portfolio. We are looking for someone with past brand partnerships, focused in the millennial + hip-hop space, spanning digital, branded content, social &amp; editorial. Reporting to the VP, Head of Sales, this person will be based in New York City and responsible for their assigned accounts. They will be able to sell across our entire portfolio of properties, including events and radio, however digital sales will be the primary focus and a majority of their revenue. Below is a more thorough list of responsibilities and requirements for this position.
<br><br>
<b>Responsibilities:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Deliver and exceed revenue goals set by the company</li>
<li>Cover assigned territories and accounts Identify and close major brand advertisers</li>
<li>Entertain and manage agency and client contacts</li>
<li>Negotiate with accounts on all details of campaigns</li>
<li>Set up weekly meetings, presentations and lunch + learns to highlight our company's opportunities and offerings</li>
<li>Work closely with account managers to oversee all aspects of booked advertising campaigns thru completion</li>
<li>Travel as necessary throughout territory or for other required business trips</li>
<li>Maintain weekly pending reports for accurate accounting of all business</li>
<li>Work with sales development team on presentation materials and proposals</li>
</ul>
Skill Set and Abilities
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>6-8+ years in online ad sales or digital agency with demonstrated success with large national brand campaigns and sponsorships</li>
<li>Thorough understanding of online media business including platforms, ad products, general pricing strategy, knowledge of online ad inventory and all other aspects of running a digital media company.</li>
<li>Strong relationships at agencies and/or clients across various categories including of cellular, apparel, auto, liquor, beverages, banking and insurance.</li>
<li>Proven ability to be self-motivating and work independently, as well as operate in fast-paced, entrepreneurial environment</li>
<li>Experience putting together materials for RFPs, including strategy, position, data and creative concepts.</li>
<li>Sold digital media, sponsorships, branded content, editorial, pre-roll and social across a publisher platform.</li>
<li>Proven ability to prospect and close new accounts</li>
<li>Demonstrated ability to create and present promottonal concepts &amp; branded entertainment opportunities that enhance clients marketing efforts</li>
</ul>
