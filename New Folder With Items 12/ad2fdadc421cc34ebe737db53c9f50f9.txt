<h2>Graphic Designer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>
<p>CORE FUNCTIONS</p>
<ul>
<li>
<b>Creative:</b> Help establish creative approach, messaging or theme that communicates an intentional story or otherwise personifies soul of the client’s brand.</li>
<li>
<b>Graphic Design:</b> Design traditional marketing communications pieces including sales literature, POP materials and advertising elements.</li>
<li>
<b>Digital Design:</b> Design digital elements including website interfaces, digital display ads and digital signage messaging.</li>
<li>
<b>Graphics Animation:</b> Bring your designs to life with animated graphics for signage, video integration and websites.</li>
</ul>
</div>
<div>
<p><b>
REQUIREMENTS AND QUALIFICATIONS:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Solid verbal and written communication skills</li>
<li>Strong organization, planning, prioritization and time management skills</li>
<li>Strong written/oral communication and proofing skills</li>
<li>Professional and welcoming demeanor in working with both clients and co-workers</li>
<li>Flexibility to seamlessly adjust to changing projects, priorities and dates</li>
<li>Expertise with Adobe Creative Suite</li>
<li>A bachelor’s degree in Graphic Design, Visual Communications or related field</li>
<li>Proficiency with Adobe Creative suite, Office, Google Docs, etc.</li>
</ul>
<p><br>
<i>REPORTS TO: Creative Director</i></p>

FSLA STATUS: Full-time/Exempt</i></p>

HOURS PER WEEK: 40</i></p>

May require lifting up to 40 pounds. Requires normal range of hearing and vision.</i></p>
