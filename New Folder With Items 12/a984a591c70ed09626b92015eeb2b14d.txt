<h2>Technician A</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p>SUMMARY:Performs highly skilled tasks in the diagnosis of malfunctions, repairs, preventive maintenance, adjustments, and overhauls.</p>
<br>
 <p></p>
<p><b>About Western Branch Diesel</b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>Western Branch Diesel is a distributor and service provider for Detroit Diesel, John Deere, MTU, MTU Onsite Energy, Volvo Penta, WheelTime, and Allison Transmissions. Headquartered in Portsmouth, Virginia, Western Branch Diesel operates out of 11 branches strategically located throughout Virginia, West Virginia, Ohio, Kentucky, and North Carolina. These branches provide sales and service to the construction and industrial, marine, mining and quarry, oil/gas, on-highway, and power-generation markets. The company’s 300+ team members include over 120 highly trained service technicians. For more company information, please visit our website www.westernbranchdiesel.com</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>Essential Job Functions</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<ul><li>Expert level knowledge of all tools and equipment required to diagnose and repair vehicles and/or equipment.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Ability to learn new activities and procedures relating to the position (i.e. new technology on new equipment).</li></ul>
<ul><li>General knowledge on warranty periods and guideline for replacement components.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Computers and electronics knowledge of various diagnostic tools such as laptop computer, multi-meter, scanners and computer hardware and software, including applications and programming.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Actively maintains current knowledge of products in order to associate symptoms or failures to a specific technical bulletin or other published information.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Must be able to work special/emergency hours as required.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Commit to delivering the highest level of customer service and quality.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Strive for completion on time, within estimate, done right the first time, and as per customer expectations on all assignments.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Attention to detail and cleanliness in completing repairs on customer vehicles and equipment.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Provides high level support and technical assistance to other members of the maintenance staff on complex problems as needed.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Ability to identify and communicate “up sell” opportunities increasing the average ticket amount.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Must maintain an average minimum labor efficiency of 80%</li></ul>
<ul><li>Must attend and successfully complete all job related educational courses &amp; updates.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Must have completed all WBT &amp; ILT for at least one major product line.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Ability to follow oral and written instructions/directions.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Must possess a complete set of tools and tool box.</li></ul>
