<h2>Fluid Engineer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>The Fluid Engineer is responsible for innovating and designing the systems and products sold by Precision Valve Automation - PVA. This position ensures that the product is designed correctly, and within customer requirements, budget, and timeframe.</p>
<p><b>Job Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Review sales orders and interpret engineering needs to deliver an on-time, complete, and accurate design and documentation package.</li>
<li>Facilitates project kick-off meeting.</li>
<li>Coordinates with other departments to develop project schedule.</li>
<li>Answers questions, manages expectations and acceptance criterion, and fosters customer relationships throughout the design process.</li>
<li>Monitors project to ensure that goals and customer expectations are met, and product is developed on schedule.</li>
<li>Designs and develops machine documents and submits models to CAD group to create diagrams and generate full bill for parts.</li>
<li>Partners with purchasing and internal shops to order parts for on time delivery.</li>
<li>Organizes and facilitates Engineering Cross Review to assess and ensure design meets the needs of the customer and company.</li>
<li>Interprets safety codes so that equipment meets specifications on every design.</li>
<li>Conducts meetings to gain customer approval of all specifications prior to manufacturing.</li>
<li>Creates test run-off document, post run-off punch list, and conducts customer run-off acceptance meeting.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Skills:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Customer centric, committed to supporting the internal and external customer.</li>
<li>Strong verbal communication and listening skills.</li>
<li>Self-starter who works independently and in a fast-paced team environment.</li>
<li>Maintains a calm, tactful demeanor when dealing with difficult situations.</li>
<li>Manages multiple projects and timelines with a sense of urgency and follow through.</li>
<li>Well organized, analytical, and detail oriented.</li>
<li>Strong computer and excel skills.</li>
<li>Knowledge of industrial robots, machinery, electrical and fluid mechanics, electronics, and</li>
<li>modeling/drawing software. required.</li>
<li>Minimum three (3)+ years of custom engineering and design required.</li>

</ul>
