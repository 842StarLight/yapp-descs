<h2>Associate Account Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<div><div><div>
<div><b><i>Trend Micro: Securing Your Connected World</i></b></div>
<div>
Trend Micro is the world’s largest independent security software company and strives to make the world a safer place by protecting digital information. They offer solutions for consumers, businesses and governments that provide layered security to protect information on mobile devices, endpoints, gateways, servers and the cloud. Our client enables the smart protection of information, with innovative technology that is simple to deploy and manage, and security that fits an evolving ecosystem. Their solutions are supported by over 5000 employees in over 50 countries and powered by the world’s most advanced cloud-based, global threat intelligence network.<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<div>A <b>Tele-Sales Account Manager</b> is responsible for selling products and services in assigned territories, paired up with several Enterprise Regional Account Managers. The person in this role will work together with sales management and marketing to coordinate and plan sales activities within their assigned territory. They will be responsible for outbound calling to new or prospective customers and driving new meetings, product demonstrations and helping to arrange product proof of concepts. They will qualify and follow up with sales leads and have a thorough knowledge of our client’s products and services. They will be responsible for keeping up-to-date with industry knowledge as well as the competitive landscape.</div>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Duties &amp; Responsibilities
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<ul>
<li>Teamed with outside Regional Account Managers, they will help develop business to new prospective organizations and on new sales to existing customers</li>
<li>
Selling is primarily through outbound calling efforts to organizations of over 1000 employees as coordinated with their outside Regional Account Managers</li>
<li>
Product focus is all of our client’s security product offerings, unless otherwise noted
</li>
<li>They will work intensively to increase and improve product awareness within their assigned territory – through channel partners and through direct interaction with prospects and customers</li>
<li>
They will be required to create and refine a revenue pipeline and to accurately forecast their revenue outlook to sales management using the CRM platform Salesforce.com</li>
<li>
Candidates must have a proven track record in telesales and ideally can demonstrate their ability to comprehend new technology and to develop strategies to get meetings created to increase sales</li>
<li>
Candidates must have the ability to engage over the phone to identify customers’ pains and challenges then effectively present how our client’s solutions can address their needs. It is also critical to convey, internally and externally, how customers use the client’s products and solutions</li>
</ul>
</div></div></div>
