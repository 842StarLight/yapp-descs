<h2>Enterprise Sales Director</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div><div>
<div>
<p><b>Primary Purpose:</b></p>
<p>Responsible for selling Quotit SAAS Software and Agent Cubed CRM Software to Enterprise companies. Develop trusting/productive relationships with prospects and clients. Initiate development of action plans, hand off and communication of implementation, and work collaboratively to make informed decisions with all resources and key contacts.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Essential Duties and Responsibilities:</b></p>
<p>Following is a summary of the essential functions for this job. Other duties may be performed, both major and minor, which are not mentioned below. Specific activities may change from time to time.</p>
<ul>
<li>Perform prospecting through telemarketing, email campaigns, networking, conferences and tradeshows</li>
<li>Proactively seek to identify business opportunities</li>
<li>Develop and establish positive working relationships with prospective enterprise entities, agencies and carriers in order to meet or exceed sales goals</li>
<li>Become knowledgeable with the Agent Cubed, Quotit, and other Company software and gain the ability to provide a high-level demonstration on how the SAAS works</li>
<li>Provide summary to determine if our SAAS is a good fit for the prospective organization</li>
<li>Follow-up on prospecting attempts and determine how we can offer a tailored solution to help obtain their business goals</li>
<li>Maintain comprehensive reporting and documentation on the sales CRM</li>
<li>Work to ensure timeliness of all reporting requests</li>
<li>Promptly respond to and manage inquiries for RFI’s and RFP’s</li>
<li>Communicate and effectively hand off to the operations team to seamlessly and effectively help with the installation and implementation process</li>
<li>Develop strategies to increase Enterprise and Health Plan sales</li>
<li>Responsible for effective communication and coordination within internal team</li>
<li>Facilitate communication between functional leaders and subject matter experts</li>
<li>Serve as a liaison between clients and the internal operations staff</li>
<li>Collaborate with Marketing Department for Marketing opportunities</li>
<li>Develop and maintain positive working relationships with key contacts and team members</li>
<li>Attend all appropriate and needed training</li>
<li>Work collaboratively with Enterprise Companies and Health Plans to support and build sales and marketing opportunities</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Minimum Skills and Competencies:</b></p>
<p>The requirements listed below are representative of the knowledge, skill and/or ability required. Reasonable accommodations may be made to enable individuals with disabilities to perform the essential functions.</p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor Degree or in-lieu of degree equivalent education, training and work-related experience</li>
<li>10+ years related business experience</li>
<li>Provide strong leadership and present interpersonal and consultative skills needed to effectively sell to a multi-tied executive team</li>
<li>Must possess effective verbal and written communication skills</li>
<li>Ability to work independently and assess situations quickly and make logical and appropriate decisions</li>
<li>Ability to multi-task and effectively work in a fast paced environment; must meet deadlines</li>
<li>Self-motivated, requiring little or no supervision; flexible, team player, able to motivate others</li>
<li>Ability to develop corrective strategies and assist in presenting</li>
<li>Proficient in Microsoft Office (Word, Excel, Outlook, PowerPoint)</li>
<li>Develop and maintain effective business relationships with clients and functional management</li>
<li>Mature and professional in attitude and appearance</li>
<li>Effective organization and time management skills with the ability to work under pressure and adhere to project deadlines</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Desired Skills:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Management and health care benefits experience</li>
</ul>
