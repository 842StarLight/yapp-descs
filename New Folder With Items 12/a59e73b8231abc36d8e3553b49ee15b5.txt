<p><b>IT Recruiter</b> needed for a full-time, staff opportunity with Yoh located in <b>Sacramento, CA</b></p>
<p><b>The Big Picture – Top Skills You Should Possess:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>IT Recruiting Experience</li>
<li>Staffing Industry Experience</li>
<li>High Volume Recruiting</li>
</ul>
<p><b>What You’ll Be Doing:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Utilize creative sourcing techniques and network with active consultants.</li>
<li>Maintain relationships with candidates and customers to build knowledge of needs and opportunities.</li>
<li>Determine applicant qualifications by interviewing applicants; analyzing responses; verifying references; comparing qualifications to job requirements.</li>
<li>Evaluate applicants by discussing job requirements and applicant qualifications with business development managers; interviewing applicants on a consistent set of qualifications.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>What’s In It For You?</b> Candidates that are hired will receive a competitive base salary, an aggressive commission plan, 401k plus company match, a comprehensive medical/dental/vision plan, and paid time off.</p>
<p><b>What You Need to Bring to the Table:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>High Volume recruiting experience, preferably in an IT staffing environment.</li>
<li>Proven success in placing IT professionals.</li>
<li>Experience in utilizing traditional and non-traditional recruiting techniques.</li>
<li>Knowledge of state and federal laws as they pertain to hiring.</li>
<li>Strong computer skills including MS Word, Excel, Outlook and Internet recruiting tools.</li>
<li>Great attitude and team player.</li>
<li>Successful completion of background screening process.</li>
<li>A Bachelor’s Degree or relevant experience in a similar recruiting role.</li>
</ul>
<p>Must be able to perform the following physical and mental tasks: • Visual Acuity, balancing, stooping, reaching, walking, manual dexterity, grasping, feeling, hearing, talking.• Capacity to think, concentrate and focus for long periods of time.• Ability to read complex documents in the English language.• Capacity to reason and make sound decisions.• Ability to write complex documents in the English language.• Capacity to express thoughts orally.</p>
