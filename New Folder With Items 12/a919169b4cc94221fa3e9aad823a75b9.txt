<p>The MatrixCare R&amp;D team is seeking a <b>Software Engineering QA </b>I<b>ntern</b> to join our Quality Assurance team in Bloomington, MN. In this role, you will be part of an interdisciplinary team that includes fellow Software Engineering QA , Scrum Masters, Business Analysts, Developers, and Product Managers throughout all phases of the development cycle. You'll be a part of the Scrum team and participate in all testing activities related to assigned web application. The candidate will also get an opportunity to participate in multiple automation research and testing activities.<br>

<b>As our Software Engineering QA Intern, you will:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Develop software based on completed designs and requirements.</li>
<li>
Participate in debugging and implementation of test automation framework, infrastructures, and tools that will be utilized across multiple test and development teams under guidance of lead.</li>
<li>
Perform manual functional testing and write Test automation scripts/Code.</li>
<li>
Document Test results and support the efficient resolution of problems and capture defect data.</li>
</ul>
<ul><li>
Collaborate with a cross-functional team including Development, QA, Implementation, Documentation, Support and Sales</li></ul>
