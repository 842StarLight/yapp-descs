<h2>Proposal Writer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div><div><div>
<div>
<div>Job Description<br>
<br>
This position supports the Fluor Government Group (FGG) in winning new work by writing the technical volumes for U.S. Government proposals, creating compelling value propositions, developing effective proposal materials, and implementing good business administration and marketing practices. The proposal writer is responsible for understanding the technical aspects required in a Request for Proposal (RFP), interpreting those proposal requirements, and preparing a final response that is clearly expressed to critical technical and non-technical decision makers. This position collaborates with FGG business lines to promote marketing strategies and incorporate those into sales and marketing materials. This position typically has business line intermediate coordination responsibilities and indirect reporting relationships with proposal managers and direct reporting relationship with the Marketing Director.<br>
<br>
<ul>
<li>Serve as a competent writer and capable editor with the ability to work to strict deadlines</li>
<li>Effectively work multiple tasks requiring a variety of writing components.</li>
<li>Understand the technical information required in client Request for Information (RFIs) and RFPs including key elements of the "full spectrum life cycle" service offerings from conceptual design, detailed engineering, procurement, and construction to start-up, commissioning, operations and maintenance, program management, consulting, and decommissioning.</li>
<li>Work with FGG proposal teams to understand the current situation (market position, competition, current capabilities, culture, and client drivers) to effectively create the content to promote Fluor’s integrated solutions differentiators</li>
<li>Interview subject matter experts, conduct additional research on technical topics, and transform those interviews and research into clear and strong proposal text.</li>
<li>Provide excellent communications skills and express thoughts clearly in writing.</li>
<li>Understand commonly used proposal concepts, practices, and technical writing procedures.</li>
<li>Organize well, adhere to schedules, and handle pressure while multi-tasking.</li>
<li>Possess experience in understanding of the federal procurement process and Federal Acquisition Regulations (FAR).</li>
<li>Help individual business line and proposal teams develop strategic messaging for client submittals that will enable Fluor to be the successful bidder</li>
<li>Conduct information gathering and market research activities by using internal company resources and databases, networking, Internet, or subscription sources to support marketing initiatives, expand industry and client knowledge, prepare responses to various client and corporate requests, and support other internal reporting needs</li>
<li>Suggest continuous improvement to current work processes, proposal management plans, and overall business activities, as appropriate</li>
<li>Other duties as assigned</li>
</ul>
</div>
</div>
