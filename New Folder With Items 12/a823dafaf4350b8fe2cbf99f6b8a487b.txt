<h2>Brokerage Administrator/Marketing Coordinator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>
<div>
<b>Marcus &amp; Millichap</b>, the nation’s largest real estate investment brokerage firm, is looking for a <b>Brokerage Administrator/Marketing Coordinator </b>to provide professional support to the <b>San Francisco</b> office. Candidates must be extremely dependable, organized, detail oriented, flexible, creative, and able to work independently. Additionally, the candidate must have exceptional follow-through, have the ability to work in a fast-paced environment, be highly motivated, possess exceptional PC skills, as well as written/verbal communication skills. The ability to multitask under pressure is a must.</div>
<div>
</div>
<div><b>
Must be proficient in InDesign and Photoshop</b></div>
</div>
<div><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Job Responsibilities include but are not limited to:</b></h3>
<div><ul>
<li>
Process new listings, this may include: proofing and editing according to Marcus &amp; Millichap guidelines, executing OOJ compliance procedures</li>
<li>
Create and maintain confidential files relating to employment records and sales transactions files</li>
<li>
Assist Operations Manager in the processing of closings and under contracts</li>
<li>
Utilize postage machine and other shipping methods</li>
<li>
Create and maintain office marketing material and sales aids using InDesign</li>
<li>
Generate, copy, scan and bind documents for use by management and agents</li>
<li>
Marketing support, including the coordination of events, contests, advertising, press releases, and public relations</li>
<li>
Back-up phones/front office support
</li>
<li>Assist with daily miscellaneous duties such as scanning, binding, supply stocking, and maintaining office cleanliness</li>
</ul></div>
</div></div>
<div><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Preference will be given to applicants who possess the following:</b></h3>
<div><ul>
<li>
Proficient in Adobe Suite.</li>
<li>
Fanatic attention to deadlines and scheduling of multiple projects</li>
<li>
Experience in the real estate industry or executive support</li>
<li>
Excellent computer skills including proficiency in Microsoft Applications</li>
<li>
Ability to prioritize tasks and work efficiently under pressure</li>
<li>
Must be versatile and flexible in making last minute adjustments to work requests</li>
<li>
Great interpersonal skills</li>
</ul></div>
