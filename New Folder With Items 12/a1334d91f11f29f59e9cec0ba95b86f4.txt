<h2>Global Demand Planner</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>Microsoft is looking for a motivated &amp; inspiring leader to join its World Wide Product Line of Business team, a highly influential group within the Devices Supply Chain organization, responsible for driving end to end supply chain strategy &amp; performance to enable achievement of the financial &amp; overall category targets for Microsoft devices such as Xbox, Surface &amp; products of the future.</div>
<p>
We are looking for a seasoned professional with experience in Demand Planning to manage the Global Demand planning process for the Retail segment. This position will primarily engage with a global team of demand planners, segment category managers, Sales &amp; Operations Planning (S&amp;OP) and Supply Chain Management teams. Success in this position requires proven business results in Global demand planning with experience in Sales &amp; Operations (S&amp;OP) and product transition (NPI/EOL) processes. The ideal candidate will also have strong cross-group collaboration and strong data analytical skills with the ability to lead and influence others within a matrix environment.</p>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Responsibilities</b></h2>
<div><b>
Key Responsibilities:
</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
<b>Global Demand planning:</b> Coordinate the consolidation of WW unconstrained demand process with the regional sales teams, lead the consensus demand process with Sales and Marketing teams and negotiate the Supportable demand plan with Supply Chain and category managers. Document demand planning assumptions and track any gaps to performance to drive actions to close gap.</li>
<li>
<b>
Global Demand shaping:</b> Apply product roadmap (NPI/EOL), price/promo assumptions to country and area demand plans. Leverage demand/supply recon and sell down plans (EOL) to shape the Global demand signal.</li>
<li>
<b>
Execution and Performance Management: </b>Review trends, ingest demand drivers, use of statistical forecast models, and evaluate forecast results.
</li>
<li>
<b>Stakeholder Management:</b> Develop and maintain productive relationships with Sales &amp; Operations, Supply Chain, Marketing and Segment teams. Point person to Communicate to the regional planning teams and channel leaders.</li>
<li>
<b>
Functional Leadership:</b> Lead the demand planning function globally for Retail. Leverage industry standards and best practices to develop and implement a demand planning roadmap to improve functional maturity.</li>
<li>
<b>
Lead close loop supportability communication </b>with the Stores segment teams by working closely with supply planning teams on supply outlooks and commits from manufacturing.</li>
</ul>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Qualifications</b></h2>
<div><b>
Qualifications/Experience:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
10+ years’ experience in Global demand planning, forecasting or Sales &amp; Operations related functions (required).</li>
<li>
Proven record of leading across functions (demand planning, sales, supply chain and marketing) and geographies within a matrix environment.</li>
<li>
Experience with in CPG or Retail is a plus</li>
<li>
Ability to think strategically and execute tactically with a hands-on spirit</li>
<li>
Advanced conceptual, analytical, and problem-solving abilities</li>
<li>
Experience in leveraging Machine Learning models is considered a plus</li>
<li>
Leadership of complex cross functional projects, with proven ability to meet challenging organizational objectives
</li>
<li>Ability to partner at senior levels to design and implement programs &amp; solutions</li>
<li>
Knowledge of business systems and information technology for managing supply chain</li>
<li>
Good business judgment, exceptional communication skills and ability to resolve conflicts diplomatically</li>
<li>
Bachelor’s degree in business, supply chain, operations management, or engineering. MBA preferred</li>
</ul>
