<h2>Employer Support Services Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Employer Support Services Manager
</b></p>
<p></p>
<p>The Employer Support Services Manager is responsible for leading a Service Delivery team that provides services and operational assistance via phone and/or email communications to Employers/Clients that utilize WEX Health products and services. This leader will manage a team that needs to be equipped to answer questions on benefits, services, import files and various Heath care accounts while maintaining accuracy and high-quality customer service. The Employer Support Services Manager is responsible for leading the team to high levels of efficiency, quality, and growth while maintaining a positive work environment.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Duties and Responsibilities:
</b></p>
<p><b>Team Management and Development </b>– Responsible for leading a variety of teams that are servicing Employers/Clients supported by the BPO. This includes but is not limited to recruiting/interviewing high quality candidates, setting individual goals/commitments and completing evaluations with each team member in accordance with the company practices. Demonstrate ability to coach, mentor, performance manage, develop future leaders and foster a positive work environment encouraging career growth within the organization. In addition, understanding attrition drivers and addressing those issues.
</p>
<p><b>Employer/Client Facing </b>– Ability to participate in pre-sales engagements, service onboarding, and management of escalated situations/calls on urgent situations. This includes follow up on quality issues or executive escalations and being able to assist in the handling of high priority service requests. Load balance activities and one-point contacts to deliver outstanding levels of service to Employer/Client groups.
</p>
<p><b>Product and Healthcare Knowledge </b>– Ability to understand the WEX Health product family and Healthcare Industry. This includes understanding, or ability to learn, the WEX Health Cloud platform, Debit Card functionality, and related integration tools used to service Employers/Clients. Deliver content to internal and external audiences to improve knowledge, product satisfaction and overall efficiency.
</p>
<p><b>Customer Service Quality and Metrics – </b>Must manage the team to meet and exceed Service Level Agreements related to all core BPO Service functions. Including and understanding of contractual obligations related to Service Delivery and the allocation of resources across queues, partners, and schedules to meet agreed upon Service Level and Contractual agreements.
</p>
<p><b>Operational Efficiency </b>– Understand detailed operational procedures related to the Employer/Client Call Center. This includes ownership of the systems, tools and processes necessary to service the Employer/Client contacts. Ability to manage these areas within P/L targets defined in conjunction with WEX Health Finance. This will also include forecasting of future volumes to make staffing decisions to achieve service level agreements.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Qualifications
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Minimum of a Bachelor’s Degree in Business or Management or equivalent work experience
</li>
<li>Previous customer service team management experience required (5+ years preferred)
</li>
<li>Healthcare or Benefits experience or related field preferred
</li>
<li>Experience working with software and tools on a day to day basis (Office, Outlook, Web)
</li>
<li>Must have excellent written, verbal, and communication skills
</li>
<li>Ability to manage multiple priorities and initiatives while managing a large team
</li>
<li>May have a need for occasional travel
</li>
</ul>
