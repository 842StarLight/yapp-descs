<h2>Product Manager, Ad Platform</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Overview and Responsibilities
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div>The Product Manager, Ad Platforms plays a meaningful role in ensuring that Viacom has the needed technology and product offering to support its global digital advertising businesses. You will play a key strategic, consultative and operational function to ensure that Viacom is able to monetize its digital products via a scalable suite of high quality ad offerings.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<ul>
<li>
You lead agile product development for advertising specific platform capabilities.</li>
<li>
You act as lynchpin in assessing and resolving technical, product and operational issues.</li>
<li>
Develop deep partnership with program management office to handle project and program budget, resource allocation and delivery</li>
<li>
Identify and drive delivery of new platform capabilities that improve the ad product offering, drive increased revenue yield or improve operational efficiency</li>
<li>
Facilitate communication across the organization on product specs, feature sets current projects and upcoming priorities</li>
<li>
Manage critical third party application partnerships, evaluate new partnership opportunities</li>
<li>
Maintain deep familiarity with digital ad tech ecosystem in order to act as a subject matter expert internally, understand and participate in the development of industry standards</li>
</ul>
<div>
The team runs the technology that powers the ad supported video experiences across all devices all brands globally. This includes the following:</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<ul>
<li>
Custom video ad integrations on all platforms including custom ad reporting and advanced analytics.</li>
<li>
HTML5 player for web, Chromecast, Xbox</li>
<li>
iOS native player for iOS mobile, tablet, and TVOS</li>
<li>
Android native player for Android mobile, tablet, Android TV, Amazon FireTV</li>
</ul>
<div>
Proprietary server-side ad insertion (SSAI) platform which facilitates digital ad insertion on all platforms. Includes the following features:</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<ul>
<li>
Direct integration with Viacom’s CMS and global content workflow to build HD HLS streams for all content</li>
<li>
Server-side ad fetching and transcoding with audio level normalization.</li>
<li>
Dynamic, Frame accurate ad insertion</li>
<li>
Custom big data analytics and alerts to track key KPIs on ad delivery</li>
</ul>
</div></div></div>
