<h2>Accounts Receivable Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>LEAP leads the world in the development and sale of cloud based legal practice management software for small law firms under a Software as a Service (SaaS) model.
</p>
<p>We employ more than 300 people, have offices in the USA, Australia and the UK with more than 13,000 law firms using our software worldwide. LEAP will provide you with the best training, equipment, mentoring and support in order for you to be successful.</p>
<p>
We are seeking an Accounts Receivable Specialist to join our highly motivated, high velocity and growing team in Jersey City. LEAP provides its customers with convenient financing options to pay for LEAP service. The Accounts Receivable Specialist is responsible to successfully execute finance agreements between LEAP’s customers and external lending business partners.</p>
<p><b>
Duties may include but are not limited to:</b></p>
<ul><li>
Central contact for LEAP's customers and funding partners</li></ul>
<ul><li>
Attend weekly meetings with the partners to run through the pipeline and resolve roadblocks
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Attend weekly meetings with internal stakeholders to provide status reports and resolve roadblocks</li></ul>
<ul><li>
Responsibility for collection of past-due accounts</li></ul>
<ul><li>
Responsibility for the creation of leased invoices in Zuora</li></ul>
<ul><li>
Maintenance of the funding system in Salesforce.com</li></ul>
<ul><li>
Sell the benefits of funding to reluctant clients</li></ul>
<ul><li>
Assist our funding partners in gathering the data needed to get credit approval</li></ul>
<ul>
<li>Responsibility for meeting LEAP's funding goals</li>
<li>Ad-hoc reporting and funding analysis on funding activity</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Requirements</b></p>
<p>
Candidates should possess the following qualifications and experience:<br>
</p>
<ul>
<li>At least one year of experience with customer service preferably in the financial sector
</li>
<li>An Associate degree and/or a Bachelor’s degree is a Plus
</li>
<li>Customer service experience in the financial industry</li>
<li>Knowledge and understanding of simple lending/borrowing practices</li>
<li>Collection experience is a plus
</li>
<li>Excellent computer skills (MS Office Excel and Salesforce.com)</li>
<li>Accurate and detailed orientated</li>
<li>Must be a self starter</li>
</ul>
<ul><li>Organizational and time-management skills</li></ul>
<ul><li>
Strong communication skills with a problem-solving attitude</li></ul>
