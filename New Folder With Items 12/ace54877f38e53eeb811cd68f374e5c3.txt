<h2>Chief Marketing Officer, Employer & Individual</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>This position is responsible for setting the strategic direction for E&amp;I marketing including structure, staffing, capabilities and investments, as well as strong coordination with internal business segments and external partners. As the leader of the marketing organization, the CMO is responsible for the quality and timely deliveryof the strategic and creative work product. The overarching goal is to support revenue growth through acquisition of plan sponsors, and the onboarding and engagement of members, delivering on cost control through health engagement and better use of the health system, as well as improved member satisfaction as measured through NPS. The CMO also leads development of team protocols, programs and processes, digital and analog marketing activities, market research, advertising, promotion, product positioning, market-level program management, distribution support and agency management.<div>
<br>
</div>
<div><b>
Primary Responsibilities:</b></div>
<div><ul>
<li>
Create and lead overall commercial business marketing vision around B2B and B2C as it pertains to developing optimal strategies and programs that facilitate consumer access, improve health outcomes and meet or exceed affordability targets</li>
<li>
Collaborate with internal business partners (e.g., Product, Digital, Clinical, Quality, Operations, Communications, Optum, Rally) on marketing strategy for all product &amp; service offerings through the commercial business</li>
<li>
Assist internal business partners with value proposition definition, strategy development, creative development, execution and measurement of effective means to attract, onboard and engage business and members</li>
<li>
Define and manage metrics designed to determine effectiveness of marketing initiatives</li>
<li>
Lead a marketing function that is well-equipped to manage multiple and diverse requests through effective processes, aligned business partnerships and highly engaged and capable talent</li>
<li>
Develop and manage market strategy for third party strategic partnerships that align to and enhance national position</li>
</ul></div>
