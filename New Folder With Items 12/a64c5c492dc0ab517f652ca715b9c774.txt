<b>You will be employed by HiTouch Business Services</b> in the position of HiTouch First Contact Resolutions Specialist. HiTouch is a newly acquired Staples subsidiary that operates as a single source for virtually everything a business needs to operate - office products, supplies and equipment, contract furniture, coffee/beverage/break room supplies, promotional products, printing and forms, and total workspace environments. It is comprised of three distinct yet fully integrated operating brands that individually provide best in class solutions and services in their respective lines. These brands are formidable competitors in the vast business services sector and are among the nation’s fastest growing competitive business products and service providers. Combined, they create a resource with unprecedented product and service offerings and unparalleled single-source capabilities making HiTouch a game-changing partner in today’s business environment.<br>

<b>Position Summary:</b>
</div>
<br>
<div>
<br>
First Call Resolution Specialists are responsible for interacting with our customers via phone, email and chat. We complete order entry, returns, research, website assistance and problem resolution in a single contact. FCR Specialists act as liaisons between multiple departments in order to obtain accurate and complete problem resolution on the very first contact with our company.</div>
<div></div>
<ul>
<li>
Answering customers' inquiries to learn about and address their needs, complaints, or other issues with products or services.</li>
<li>
Focus on first call resolution by responding efficiently and accurately to customers, explaining possible solutions, and ensuring that they feel supported and valued</li>
<li>
Utilizing software, databases, scripts, and tools appropriately.</li>
<li>
Understanding and striving to meet or exceed customer service metrics while providing excellent consistent customer service.</li>
<li>
Making sales or recommendations for products or services that may better suit client needs.</li>
<li>
Taking part in training and other learning opportunities to expand knowledge of company and position.</li>
<li>
Adhering to all company policies and procedures</li>
<li>
All other duties as assigned</li>
</ul>
