About the Position
<br><br>
We're looking for a Content Marketer to join our growing Marketing department, reporting to the Senior Content Marketing Manager. The ideal candidate is an organized, creative, good natured ass-kicker with exceptionally strong writing skills, a background in content ideation and creation, and strong project management experience.
<br><br>
Key Responsibilities of a Content Marketer at Showpad
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Work with Senior Content Marketing Manager to develop content strategy aligned with quarterly and annual goals</li>
<li>Develop and execute on integrated marketing campaigns that are aligned with Demand Generation goals</li>
<li>Project manage from concept to design delivery</li>
<li>Collaborate with Senior Content Marketing Manager and Design team to plan and develop content including eBooks, whitepapers, infographics, checklists, guides, templates, blog posts, reports, videos, case studies, etc.</li>
<li>Help build Showpad Style Guide that will serve as a resource to ensure consistency across every content touchpoint</li>
<li>Edit, proofread, and improve freelance writers' content</li>
<li>Liaise with content writers to ensure messaging, tone, and brand consistency</li>
<li>Optimize content according to SEO</li>
<li>Manage content distribution to online channels and social media platforms to increase web traffic</li>
<li>Work with the Senior Content Marketing Manager to develop and drive editorial calendar</li>
<li>Stay up-to-date with developments and generate new ideas to draw audience's attention</li>
</ul>
Skills and Qualifications we are looking for
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>3-4 years experience in Content Marketing</li>
<li>Experience working in the B2B SaaS space</li>
<li>Familiarity with GSuite, Wordpress, SproutSocial, and Slack preferred but not required</li>
<li>Basic technical knowledge of HTML and web publishing</li>
<li>Knowledge of SEO and web traffic metrics</li>
<li>Familiarity with social media</li>
<li>Experience driving original research projects</li>
<li>Excellent writing skills</li>
<li>Attention to detail</li>
<li>Good organizational and time-management skills</li>
<li>Ability to produce high-quality work in a fast-paced, deadline-driven team environment</li>
<li>Enough confidence to share your ideas, participate in business conversations, and take (or give) constructive criticism</li>
<li>Bachelor's degree in Journalism, Marketing, or relevant field</li>
</ul>
