<div><div><p>Are you a new or recent graduate, eager to start or grow your sales career with a Fortune 200 Company? Launch your sales career with the CenturyLink Sales Academy Program! The Sales Academy is seeking driven and highly motivated individuals to join our January 2020 College Connect program. Participate in an immersive CenturyLink Enterprise Sales Development program to learn the technical and professional fundamentals to facilitate success. Learn through classroom, virtual instructor-led (VILT), and On-the-Job Training (OJT) of CenturyLink’s full product and solution portfolio. Sales, Business and Technical training courses will increase overall knowledge of CenturyLink solutions and go-to-market strategies. The possibilities are endless to grow your career with a Fortune 200 Company!</p></div></div>

Job Description</b>
</div>
<div>
<div><b>About Our Sales Academy Program Experience:
</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Temporary relocation to our Broomfield, CO office for our classroom based – 12 week training program.</li>
<li>
Learn technical and professional fundamentals for success from proven sales leaders.</li>
<li>
Understand CenturyLink’s full product and solutions portfolio to increase overall knowledge for go-to market strategies.</li>
<li>
Network with a group of other sales trainees, sales leaders and business executives.</li>
<li>
Transition into an Account Manager role in a major U.S. market territory Arlington, VA after completion of the Sale Academy training program.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<div><b>
Sound Interesting? Specifics for our Account Manager Role:
</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Generate potential sales leads by using lead-generation and research tools during the first 3 month. Proactively qualifies leads and sales prospects by understanding business profile, buying process, requirements and timeline expectations.</li>
<li>
Formulate solutions and present products and solutions persuasively and effectively while linking benefits to the client’s business objectives and needs.</li>
<li>
Produce new sales from prospects and current Enterprise customers via telephone and other methods. Must have a sense of urgency and respond to inquiries within established timeframes.</li>
<li>
Successful performance must be clearly demonstrated by achievement of monthly quota.</li>
</ul>

Part of an entrepreneurial team, responsible for managing a designated existing customer base.

</li>
<li>Continued mentoring and growth/development through, Instructor-led, web-based and hands-on training - all designed to help you gain new experiences
</li>
