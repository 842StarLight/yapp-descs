<h2>Paid Media Advertising Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div><div><div><div>
<p>Force Marketing is looking for a talented and ambitious Paid Media Specialist to join our Paid Media Team at the headquarters in Atlanta. The Paid Media Specialist will report directly to the Paid Media Team Manager. The specialist will be responsible for developing, implementing, launching and monitoring campaigns for various clients in the automotive industry. The ideal candidate will be an ambitious, organized team player that is comfortable working in a fast-paced environment.</p>
<p><b>
Job Duties &amp; Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Effectively manage and optimize campaigns within budget on major platforms including Facebook, Google and Trade Desk</li>
<li>Monitor and report performance of pay-per-click advertising campaigns</li>
<li>Work with the onboarding and client success team to effectively launch campaigns on time</li>
<li>Manage campaign budget pacing on a month-to-month basis for accounts</li>
<li>Proactively communicate with team members and manage account expectations</li>
<li>Develop client and market search analysis reports and recommendations</li>
<li>Assist team members with questions, tasks, and trainings as needed</li>
<li>Demonstrate enthusiasm and passion for growing skills and facing new challenges</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Skills &amp; Qualification Requirements</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Experience building campaigns in any of the following platforms:
<ul>
<li>Facebook Ads Manager</li>
<li>Google Ads</li>
<li>Trade Desk or other programmatic DSPs</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>Strong understanding across the pillars of traffic, conversion, retention and value maximization</li>
<li>Familiarity with analyzing data in Google Analytics</li>
<li>Excellent prioritization skills and proactivity in completing daily tasks</li>
<li>Ability to work independently as well as with a team</li>
<li>Strong digital ad operations technical knowledge</li>
<li>Keen attention to detail</li>
<li>Excellent verbal and written communication skills</li>
<li>A positive, go-getter attitude</li>
<li>Comfortable in a fast-paced environment</li>
<li>Experience with dynamic ads is a plus</li>
</ul>
