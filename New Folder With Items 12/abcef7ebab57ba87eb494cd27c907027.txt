<b>
Your Career</b><br>
<br>
</div>
<div>Palo Alto Networks is looking for an experienced finance professional to join the team as a<b> </b>Finance Business Manager, Strategic Finance. We’re looking for a self-starter with strong analytical skills, an effective communicator, and a leader passionate for driving change in a dynamic fast-paced environment – all-in-one.<br>
<br>
</div>
<div>This role will be responsible for driving key strategic finance deliverables, which includes managing and continually improving corporate financial models, providing business/financial insights &amp; analytics, and collaborating with stakeholders across the organization to establish Company alignment. This is an individual contributor role.</div>
<div>
<br>
<b>
Your Impact</b>
</div>
<div><ul>
<li>
Contribute to our consolidated strategic and financial planning processes (e.g. Forecast, Budget, Long Range Plan) and be responsible for portions of underlying integrated financial forecast models, systems, and tools</li>
<li>
Collaborate with key executives and functional leads across our Company to establish alignment, while providing analytical rigor in evaluating strategic initiatives and decision processes</li>
<li>
Develop and manage scenario models and in-depth analyses utilized for internal investment and operational decision-making</li>
<li>
Manage key deliverables and assist management in preparing external communications, including quarterly Board of Directors’ updates</li>
<li>
Drive and provide support for ad-hoc strategic and financial analyses</li>
</ul></div>
<div><b>
Your Experience</b></div>
<div><ul>
<li>
Bachelor degree in Economics/Finance, MBA from top tier institution or equivalent experience preferred</li>
<li>
5-8 years related experience showing strong career progression; management consulting/investment banking/private equity experience a plus</li>
<li>
Excellent analytical and financial modeling skills, with an understanding of software, appliances and/or internet industry business models</li>
<li>
Capable of seamlessly moving from the big picture to the details and back</li>
<li>
Strong team player with effective communication skills and high EQ</li>
<li>
Comfortable working with high-level guidance and independently building a working action plan</li>
<li>
Experience with and/or a willingness to master various financial systems, including SAP, BPC, Tableau, and other BI tools</li>
<li>
Possess intellectual curiosity and willingness and ability to adapt to the rapid business and organizational demands that result from a high-growth environment</li>
</ul></div>
