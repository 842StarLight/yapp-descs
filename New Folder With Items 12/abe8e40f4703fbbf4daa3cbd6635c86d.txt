<h2>Corporate Communications Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div><b>About the role:</b></div>
<div>
As the Corporate Communications Manager for TQL, you will be responsible for creating high impact, targeted content and communications for internal and external audiences. You will master the elements of TQL’s competitive advantage and create highly effective content using the TQL voice to showcase our brand across multiple marketing channels. You will also work directly with senior executives to write speeches, scripts for videos and other outreach needs.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div><b>
What’s in it for you:</b></div>
<div></div>
<ul>
<li><div>
Competitive compensation and benefit package</div></li>
<li><div>
Own communications for a privately held, $3.6 billion organization</div></li>
<li><div>
Directly support the initiatives that continuously make us a Top Workplace nationwide</div></li>
<li><div>
Work hard, play hard – enjoy happy hours, team outings, company-sponsored volunteer events</div></li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<br>
<div><b>
Your job:</b></div>
<div></div>
<ul>
<li><div>
Know the required voice and tone for executives, customers, carriers and employees</div></li>
<li><div>
Deliver well written, on-target and high-impact communications in an extremely fast paced environment</div></li>
<li><div>
Drive strategic communication initiatives that increase company visibility and industry expertise, while promoting and protecting TQL’s brand and reputation</div></li>
<li><div>
Proactively manage media, industry, and community responses, stories, and reports regarding TQL</div></li>
<li><div>
Manage freelance and agency relationships to ensure delivery of quality content and brand recognition</div></li>
<li><div>
Take on the ownership of web and blog content, press releases, external marketing campaign content, advertising, industry-facing content, major internal communications, executive-level speeches and content, public outreach, scripts and videos</div></li>
<li><div>
Support others in the Marketing department with development and proofing of content</div></li>
<li><div>
Assist other departments with developing, auditing, and proofing content for strategic and on-tone messaging</div></li>
<li><div>
Supervise the team and processes responsible for workplace and industry awards</div></li>
<li><div>
Oversee the internal videographer as it relates to scripts, scheduling and quality of video output</div></li>
<li><div>
Identify key industry and media targets to establish and build relationships and serve as a company spokesperson and contact</div></li>
<li><div>
Be obsessed with promoting and building TQL’s brand</div></li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<br>
<div><b>
You need the following:</b></div>
<div></div>
<ul>
<li><div>
Bachelor’s degree in Communications, Marketing, English, Public Relations or related</div></li>
<li><div>
7+ years of corporate communications experience</div></li>
<li><div>
Clear writing and editing skills – recognized for high impact work</div></li>
<li><div>
Extensive PR and media relations experience</div></li>
<li><div>
Excellent communication and interpersonal skills with all levels of organizations</div></li>
<li><div>
Comfortable working in a fast paced, sales organization with ever-changing business needs</div></li>
<li><div>
Proven ability to deliver planned and unplanned projects on extremely tight deadlines</div></li>
<li><div>
Thick-skin and the ability to work with a wide-range of personalities</div></li>
<li><div>
Excellent project management skills and the ability to lead others to achieve results</div></li>
<li><div>
Ability to maintain a positive attitude while handling high volumes of work and continuous changing of priorities</div></li>
<li><div>
Self-motivated and highly career-driven with a passion for branding and corporate communications</div></li>
</ul>
