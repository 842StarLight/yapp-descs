<h2>B2B Marketing Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Summary/Objective: </b></p>
<p>The role will have experience in developing strategic marketing programs for a dealer audience, as well as a history of discovering and analyzing dealer’s motivations in order to execute innovative outreach initiatives. The B2B Marketing Manager is responsible for aligning all marketing activity with the sales team, helping to generate revenue by launching marketing tactics that focus on customer acquisition, account retention and increased penetration and usage of Tire Profiles products and services. This role is also responsible for leading aspects of the end-to-end dealer customer lifecycle, including dealer satisfaction.</p>
<p><b>Essential Functions </b></p>
<p>Reasonable accommodations may be made to enable individuals with disabilities to perform the essential functions.</p>
<p>1. Responsible for increasing eBay Motors’ market share across key dealer segments.</p>
<p>2. Manages the dealer segmentation strategy, including research &amp; insights, identification, and nurture activities.</p>
<p>3. Responsible for the full-funnel marketing plan, including acquisition, retention, and win-back programs.</p>
<p>4. Translates customer profiles into actionable engagement and advocacy programs.</p>
<p>5. Develop a deep understanding of the eBay dealer customer journey and, working in partnership with the sales leadership team, delivers a robust strategy, complete with measurable tactics.</p>
<p>6. Builds brand value and reputation with key partners</p>
<p>7. Portion of time to be spent in face-to-face interactions with dealerships across the country.</p>
<p>8. Serves as the marketing point of contact for issues management.</p>
<p>9. Provides input into other marketing and product plans and objectives, based on understanding the customer and market demands.</p>
<p><b>Competencies </b></p>
<p>1. Strong analytic abilities centered on consumer and customer intelligence and data.</p>
<p>2. Strong communication skills with keen ability to balance multiple priorities and deliver timely results.</p>
<p>3. Seeks and provides honest, transparent feedback</p>
<p>4. Excellent facilitation, written and verbal communication skills with adaptable approach.</p>
<p>5. Employs strong organizational skills.</p>
<p>6. Thoroughness.</p>
<p>7. Professionalism</p>
<p>8. Business Acumen</p>
<p>9. Team Player</p>
<p><b>Education &amp; Required Experience </b></p>
<p>1. Bachelor’s degree in Marketing or advertising</p>
<p>2. 6-10 years of related work experience</p>
<p>3. Outstanding knowledge of the automotive industry in the USA</p>
<p>4. Experience in designing and/or planning integrated marketing plans</p>
<p>5. Ability to clearly and consistently articulate vision, creativity and enthusiasm to internal stakeholders and external customers</p>
<p>6. Proven capability of thriving in a fast-paced, ambiguous marketing environment</p>
<p>7. Experience managing multiple external agency partners with efficiency</p>
<p>8. Experience with marketing CRM tools</p>
<p>9. Ability to work independently with minimal supervision</p>
<p><b>Supervisory Responsibility </b></p>
<p>No</p>
<p><b>Travel </b></p>
<p>Up to 25%</p>
<p><b>Position Type/Expected Hours of Work </b></p>
<p>This is a full-time position. Days and hours of work are Monday through Friday, 8:00 a.m. to 5 p.m. CST. Some evenings and weekends required. This position does have the opportunity to work remotely.</p>
