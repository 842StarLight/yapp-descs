<h2>Group Business Development Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">Sonoma County Tourism is the official destination stewardship organization for California's Sonoma County. SCT is a private, non-profit marketing and sales organization dedicated to promoting overnight stays and creating a sustainable hospitality economy in Sonoma County, California.
<br>Summary
<br>The Group Business Development Manager (GBD Manager) has the overall responsibility to grow the destinations client base by through targeted outreach across various channels, including new account discovery, Tradeshow participation, monthly and quarterly communications and client events. The results of these efforts will be reflected in increased group visitation into the county. In addition to the client outreach, the GBD Manager will be responsible for continuing to convey value to our hotel partners by providing customized creative content, strategic prospect reports and partnership opportunities. This position oversees a geographic territory and will be responsible for developing an account base within the defined area.
<br><br>
This position is located in Santa Rosa, CA.
<br><br>
Essential Duties &amp; Responsibilities
<br><br>
<b>Duties may include but are not limited to the following:</b><br>
Group Room Lead Generation
<br><ul>
<li>Responsible for meeting semiannual/annual lead generation goals as established</li>
<li>Actively prospect assigned geographic territory via phone solicitation, Knowland, LinkedIn and other resources to generate qualified leads the destination</li>
<li>Create and execute SMART plans monthly designed to increase lead generation</li>
<li>Provide detailed and timely communications to our partners and clients via Simpleview, Cvent and email</li>
<li>Maintain CRM database (Simpleview) to include logging of all activities including but not limited to email traces, partner referrals, client notes and incentives</li>
</ul>
Client Communication
<br><ul>
<li>Create and execute annual communications plan to reach broad base of new and existing clients monthly / quarterly</li>
<li>Identify and execute strategic partnerships with meeting planner organizations such as MPI, Northstar, CalSAE etc to gain greater exposure within the industry via advertising opportunities</li>
<li>Work with assigned partner hotels to identify need periods and special promotions to incorporate into communication plan</li>
<li>Track and analyze results based on communications sent and adjust accordingly to maximize reach</li>
</ul>
Tradeshows, Sales Missions &amp; Industry Relations
<br><ul>
<li>Identify and attend key industry tradeshows and sales missions in assigned markets</li>
<li>Manage tradeshow registration, submissions, appointment requests, shipping and travel</li>
<li>Execute tradeshow follow-up including distributing leads to Sonoma County partners and future correspondence with client</li>
<li>Work collaboratively with industry colleagues e.g. MPI, CalSAE and DI</li>
</ul>
FAM (Familiarization) Tours / Site Inspections and Client Events
<br><ul>
<li>Identify and invite qualified meeting planners to attend Sonoma County FAM/Client events</li>
<li>Provide support to the GBD Specialist as needed to aid execution of events</li>
<li>Work with Clients and Partners to organize and execute Site Inspections in the destination for active leads</li>
</ul>
Partner Relations
<br><ul>
<li>Work closely and collaborate with assigned hotel partners to create customize communication plan</li>
<li>Identify need periods and special promotional opportunities for partners</li>
<li>Provide targeted account lists / lead for hotel partners through utilization of LinkedIn, Knowland and prospecting efforts from sales calls and tradeshows</li>
<li>Measurably promote Sonoma County Tourism partner services to planners of potential and existing client base</li>
<li>Keep up to date with Sonoma County product and experiences, staying abreast of ‘what’s new’</li>
<li>Manage and conduct coordinated Partner Drop In’s with local businesses to discuss business opportunities and to continue to develop long term partnerships</li>
</ul>
Administrative Duties
<br><ul>
<li>Submit amenity requests to the Sales &amp; Services Coordinator for fulfillment</li>
<li>Create new and update existing sales flyers and marketing materials</li>
<li>Maintain activity log in Simpleview for tradeshow appointments, trainings, networking, partner referrals and partner drop ins</li>
<li>Respond to RFP’s from clients through Cvent</li>
</ul>
Required Education and/or Experience
<br><ul>
<li>Minimum of three years’ experience with a convention/visitors/tourism bureau, hotel, or hospitality industry-related business</li>
<li>Bachelor's degree (B. A.) from four-year college or university with academic coursework in a relevant discipline (i.e., hospitality, tourism, international marketing, business administration or other closely related fields of study) preferred</li>
</ul>

<li>Experience working with a CRM database (Simpleview experience a bonus)</li>
<li>Displays an aptitude for identifying and learning new software programs</li>

<li>Ability to work effectively with a cross-section of people, nationalities and time zones</li>
<li>Ability to perform consistently in a fast-paced, multi-tasked and deadline-driven environment</li>
</ul>
