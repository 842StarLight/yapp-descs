<h2>Automotive Technician - Don Davis Ford - Arlington, Texas, US</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">Hiring Immediately, Automotive Technician. Great compensation and benefits. All skill levels needed.
<br><br>
We are seeking Automotive Technicians to add to our growing service department. Our sales are on the rise and we need energetic people that love cars to help us keep up with increased traffic. Mechanics with Independent Service Station experience as with Midas, Monroe, Goodyear, etc please apply. Don Davis is one of the largest privately owned dealerships in the Dallas Fort Worth area, providing excellent service for 40 years and we are still growing.
<br><br>
REQUIREMENTS
<br><br>
Previous automotive experience is REQUIRED for this position. Qualified candidates will need a valid driver's license, high school diploma or equivalent degree, and the ability to work a flexible schedule. Candidates with ASE certification, inspection license, and advanced training are highly desired. We are looking for someone who works hard, communicates clearly and is interested in a real career with long term potential, not just a job.
<br><br>
BENEFITS
<br><br>
Generous compensation based on experience
<br>Benefits available – Health, Dental, 401k, Paid Time Off
<br>Positive and family friendly atmosphere.
<br>Short and long term disability
<br>Paid training and advancement opportunities
<br><br>
RESPONSIBILITIES
<br><br>
Perform work as outlined on repair order with efficiency and accuracy in accordance with dealership and factory standards
<br>Diagnose cause of any malfunction and perform repair as authorized.
<br>Communicate with the Parts Department to obtain needed parts.
<br>Communicate needed repairs to Service Advisors to obtain approvals
<br>Participate in and complete training as assigned by the manufacturer and management
<br>Road-test vehicles to ensure the repair has addressed the customer complaint
<br>Administer safety and housekeeping policies and procedures, keeping spills cleaned up as they happen.
