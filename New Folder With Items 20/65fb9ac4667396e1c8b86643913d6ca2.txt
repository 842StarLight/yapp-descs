<h2>Global Operations Planning and Control Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<div><div><div>
<p>As a key member of the Supply Chain team, the Global Operations Planning and Control Manager is responsible for managing Biamp's global production planning and control capability and its execution to ensure demand and supply across all operational sites are balanced and key operational metrics are achieved. This person manages the planning and scheduling teams across all sites, responsible for aligning demands, capacities, and inventories as well as synchronizing the timely execution of all production work orders.
</p>
<p><b>Essential Functions:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Leads the efforts to assess, define, and execute a global multi-site planning and logistics strategy, to optimize service levels to customer demand and business goals against inventories and production utilization.</li>
<li>This person shall be responsible for the complete redesign, implementation, and management of Biamp's future global Sales and Operations Planning (S&amp;OP) process across the whole of the company and coordinate and consolidate the work of the company's Planning resources across Biamp's Production and key supply chain sites which currently include; Tigard Oregon, Waltham Massachusetts, Chester Pennsylvania, and Shenzhen China.</li>
<li>Responsible for the analysis and interpretation across all operational sites of sales forecast and shipment histories, order backlog, Manufacturing inputs, and material supply to establish, adjust, and control the ERP/MRP master schedule operations plans.</li>
<li>Manages the site teams responsible for ensuring product inventory levels against targets, accurate and executable production schedules are met for each product group, and that Biamp customer order lead-times and on-time service levels are met.</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Manages the site teams responsible for the correctness of Demand Planning and Scheduling data within Biamp's ERP System.
</li>
<li>As Biamp engages in mergers and acquisitions, this role will need to work closely with the newly merged or acquired companies to consolidate and integrate the S&amp;OP, inventory management and production planning process of these entities into Biamp.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Secondary Functions:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>When necessary, working with the extended teams to identify and resolve potential and actual delivery issues and, where appropriate, drive assessment and solutions to avoid future issues.</li>
<li>Plays a central role in identifying and leading continuous improvement activities associated with Operations Planning and Scheduling.</li>
</ul>
</div></div></div>
<div>Job Qualifications</div>
<div><div>
<div>
<ul>
<li>Bachelors degree in a related field or equivalent experience.
</li>
<li>Minimum of five (5) years of experience in master scheduling.
</li>
<li>Demonstrated ability to:
<ul>
<li>Identify, confront, and clearly communicate existing challenges, mitigation strategies, and objectives in writing and verbally, leveraging sophisticated manufacturing supply chain techniques, tools, processes, and procedures to secure operational and customer results.
</li>
<li>Identify trends and take anticipatory actions to minimize surprises and operational challenges, and manage business risk.
</li>
<li>Facilitate large cross functional strategy development and alignment.</li>
<li>Manage large, cross functional, complex projects incorporating technological, geographic, and human challenges.</li>
<li>Build and nurture, positive, collaborative and lasting relationships with peers across the company organizations.</li>
<li>Develop and deliver key strategic presentations and messages to the Executive and Senior Management.
</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>Supply planning or supply chain certifications (CPIM, APICS, etc.) preferred or a solid understanding of the scheduling and planning process.
</li>
<li>Experience with Microsoft Dynamics AX preferred.</li>
<li>Demonstrated extensive experience and mastery in forecasting, material and resource planning, and material supply chains.</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Advanced knowledge of Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP) systems and material planning functions. Experience in Microsoft Dynamics AX preferred.</li>
<li>Strong analytical, organizational, communications, critical thinking, and decision-making skills.
</li>
<li>Working knowledge of and experience with lean manufacturing principals.
</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Working knowledge of Microsoft Office applications.</li>
<li>Ability to travel 10% on-going and as high as 25% at times.
</li>
</ul>
