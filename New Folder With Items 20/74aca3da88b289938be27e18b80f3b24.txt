<h2>Recruiter</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>Uses independent judgment to maintain the continuous resume flow for the recruiting and selection of highly qualified employees for assigned areas.</p>
<ul>
<li>Position dedicated to SurePayroll - recruiting for IT, Sales, Service and Corporate Roles</li>
<li>Position must reside in SurePayroll - Glenview, IL location</li>
<li>Develops and administers sourcing strategies for identifying and recruiting potential new hires.</li>
<li>Ensures all positions are internally and externally posted and routed accordingly to provide our clients with a pool of qualified candidates.</li>
<li>Acts as a liaison to all sourcing contacts. Places advertising with vendors to ensure compliance with federal and state guidelines and company policies. Participates in all recruiting programs.</li>
<li>Maintains regular contact and relationships with all sources, including temporary employment agencies, placement offices, and outreach recruiting programs to attract a pool of qualified candidates for current and future open positions.</li>
<li>Uses effective and professional communication skills in order to deliver and gather information, projects a positive company image, and to attract candidates.</li>
<li>Conducts screening of resumes and initial phone or in person interviews. Refers candidates for the next level of interviews.</li>
<li>Conducts and trains other personnel on the process of screening, testing, and interviewing in a professional and effective manner within the process outlined for the company.</li>
<li>Conducts reference checks, communicates status of selection process to applicants and extends employment offers to candidates to ensure company standards are met.</li>
<li>Conducts new hire orientation, new supervisor on-boarding calls and re-recruiting calls to ensure the recruiting process is complete.</li>
<li>Interacts with hiring managers and candidates regarding status of openings to ensure recruiting process is being followed.</li>
<li>Responds to inquires from associates, field recruiting, and applicants, pertaining to staffing activities and career opportunities.</li>
<li>Works closely with Rochester/Corporate Hiring Managers in order to determine interviewing effectiveness and discuss staffing issues in each organization.</li>
<li>Participates in special projects and may act as project lead as needed.</li>
</ul>
</div>
<br>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor's Degree - Preferred</li>
<li>3 years of experience in Recruiting.</li>
</ul>
</div>
