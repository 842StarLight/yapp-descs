<h2>Product Manager / Director - Hardware</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">About Clover
<br><br>
Clover was founded to deliver more to the small and medium-sized businesses that desperately need easy-to-use software and beautifully designed hardware. Our open-source Point of Sale system fosters a seamless merchant-customer interaction, creates an exceptional customer experience, and allows merchants to run their businesses at maximum efficiency.
<br><br>
What team and role will you be joining?
<br><br>
Clover's product team is responsible for moving new products and enhancements through the execution lifecycle. The team is focused on defining product strategy and positioning to drive the product's shape and implementation.
<br><br>
As a Hardware Product Manager / Director on the Core Framework product team, you will be responsible for identifying new product opportunities, collaborating with Clover's hardware and engineering squads, having comprehensive market knowledge of competitors as well as Clovers customers and the needs of those customers by vertical. You will partner with product marketing, design and content teams to position and to take new hardware offerings to market and educate sales.
<br><br>
You will be responsible for market research, prioritizing enhancements, and partnering with international product teams to reshape how our users access Clover products and services.
<br><br>
You are an expert at cross-functional alignment and will work closely with subject matter experts, other product managers, designers, engineers, and operations to understand to implement your roadmap. You will drive the end-to-end management each effort and be responsible for communicating progress and key milestones to various stakeholders.
<br><br>
Small businesses make up the majority of Clover's portfolio. You will engage closely with merchants across multiple verticals and countries to ensure their specific needs are considered. You can interpret complex industry standards and write detailed user stories and requirements. You're a good listener and "empathy" is one of the qualities you use to describe your personality.
<br><br>
You enjoy working in a fast-paced office, you understand that constant attention to detail leads to success and you're rarely satisfied with the status quo. You find that storytelling comes naturally.
<br><br>
<b>Responsibilities:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Overall product responsibility for Clover's hardware portfolio</li>
<li>Identify new product opportunities, collaborate with Clover's hardware and engineering squads</li>
<li>Drive market analysis of competition</li>
<li>Define merchant needs by vertical</li>
<li>Partner with product marketing, design and content teams to position and to take new hardware offerings to market and educate sales.</li>
<li>Influence packaging and unboxing of hardware</li>
<li>Coordinate with user research teams to validate product concepts and refine designs</li>
<li>Communicate requirements with various stakeholders</li>
<li>Be the Product Owner of a dedicated scrum team for execution</li>
<li>Collaborate with design and content team on release notes and help content</li>
<li>Coordinate releases with release management team</li>
<li>Assist in building business cases (including opportunity sizing and financial analysis) to define, evaluate and prioritize competing opportunities to inform Clover's roadmap</li>
<li>Communicate rationale, prioritization and development status with product, engineering and other cross-functional stakeholders</li>
<li>Analyze user feedback and data to optimize the user experience</li>
<li>Evangelize Clover's values and products across the team, Fiserv, and sales channels</li>
<li>Be part of an agile cross-functional product, engineering and QE team to create a best-in-class products and product experiences</li>
<li>Deep understanding of how to create accessible products for a dynamic user base</li>
</ul>
<b>Qualifications:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>5-7 + years of product management experience. Mobile, Android, Cloud based POS experience preferred</li>
<li>BA/BS degree required. Technical degree preferred.</li>
<li>Comfort in a fast-paced and dynamic environment</li>
<li>Experience delivering products / features which have a high degree of cross-functional complexity and integration.</li>
<li>Strong presentation and training skills</li>
<li>Comfort presenting to internal and external stakeholders</li>
<li>Proven track record of using data to influence requirements, design and implementation</li>
<li>Strong project management skills, including the ability to think and lead complex projects simultaneously</li>
<li>A track record of delivering successful consumer or small business products</li>
</ul>
