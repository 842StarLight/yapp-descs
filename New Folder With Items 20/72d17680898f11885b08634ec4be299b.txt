<h2>Administrative Assistant - II</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Description: </b></p>
<ul><li>Provides administrative and clerical support to an individual manager, department or group of managers.</li></ul>
<ul><li>May spend a significant amount of time coordinating functional administrative activities that support clinical trials, medical information, sales, etc.</li></ul>
<ul><li>In addition to the Administrative Assistant responsibilities provide administrative and clerical support to the department's interview process, which includes creating, and distributing</li></ul>
<ul><li>agendas, collecting assessment forms and scheduling and making travel arrangements. Organizes and schedules extra-department meetings.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Ensures computer equipment, and all new hire materials are arranged for the persons first day at Client.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Tracks and verifies data using a variety of software to conduct data entry and sorting of information.</li></ul>
<ul><li>May track the department budget as directed by the immediate manager. Conducts internet searches as directed.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Creates or may assist in the creation of presentation materials and draft technical documents as needed.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Manipulates Excel spreadsheets into different sorts and formats and works with Word, Power Point documents as directed by immediate manager.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Gathers, collects, records, tracks and verifies relatively complex data and information from multiple sources.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Liaises with external agencies or organizations to coordinate activities that are important to the function of the department.</li></ul>
<p>Job Type: Contract</p>
<p>Salary: $24.00 /hour</p>
</div>
