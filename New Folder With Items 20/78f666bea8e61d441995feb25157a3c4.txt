<br>Maintains an internal audit system

<br>Facilitates and manages customer audits.
<br>Manages calibration system.
<br>Provides development and training of management in quality system and quality improvement tools.
<br>Completes GMP training and tracks competency.
<br>Provides technical assistance to production department.
<br>Actively participates as a member of corporate quality team.
<br>Plans and completes supplier, subcontractor and internal quality audits.
<br>Locates, evaluates, purchases and implements technology to promote quality objectives.

<br>Provides technical assistance to Sales and Customer Service.
<br>Coordinates and directs customer incident reviews; manages the CAPA system.
<br>Provides supervision to others through motivation, direction, review or feedback of assigned tasks related to quality systems and tools.
<br>Performs other job duties as assigned.
<br>Coordinates testing and measurement program.
<br>Determines inspection and release criteria.

Completes specification review and interpretation to shop floor.
