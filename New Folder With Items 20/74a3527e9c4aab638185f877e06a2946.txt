<h2>Product Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<div>You can make a salary. Or you can make a difference. Or you can work as a Product Manager at Herman Miller and make both.<br>
<br>
<b>About this Opportunity</b><br>
As a Product Manager, you'll serve as product champion for a specified product line(s) globally by developing, implementing, and managing product management activities to provide optimum sales and profits. You will compile and evaluate research on assigned product line(s) to establish product management strategies. You'll be responsible for product direction, pricing, profit plans, and product development to manage the product line(s) life cycle. You will manage your assigned product line(s) to approved performance metrics. You may also lead product business teams and the development of product strategy for select products.<br>
<br>
<b>What You'll Do</b><br>
You'll have opportunities to speak up, solve problems, lead others, and be an owner every day as you...<div>
<ul>
<li>
Define market opportunities determined by an analytical assessment. You will translate the market need into a product specific criteria and support the product development and commercialization process with various product management tactics.</li>
<li>
Develop, drive, and implement individual product plans and strategy.</li>
<li>
Direct ongoing analysis and lead the pricing and discounting strategy to provide greatest financial performance.</li>
<li>
Provide direction, make recommendations, and make decisions in support of data management system functionality and usage.</li>
<li>
Create necessary product sales support and training information, including product feature summaries, competitive evaluations, and pricing comparisons.</li>
<li>
Define analytical tools and observe research to provide insights and observations for decision-making.</li>
<li>
Field general questions, including competitive inquires, and make decisions or recommendations to resolve.</li>
<li>
Own the effort to resolve quality issues and evaluate and drive changes that affect P&amp;L.</li>
<li>
Manage the product life cycle of the product lines from identification, introduction, through growth and maturity, to decline and discontinuation, and provide input on product simplification/discontinuation for product lines.</li>
<li>
Act as a mentor to help develop other Product colleagues.</li>
<li>
Define product criteria for a new product, and support product management deliverables through the product development and commercialization process for local or global new products.</li>
<li>
Perform additional responsibilities as requested to achieve business objectives.</li>
</ul>
