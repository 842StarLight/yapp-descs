Overview
<br>--------
<br><br>
The Professional Services team delivers a wide range of projects for both clients and business partners. The Delivery Director will build and maintain a solid working relationship with coworkers and clients, from pre-sales efforts to project delivery. The Delivery Director will work with the broader team of Engagement Managers to solidify engagement related best practices,and ensure delivery quality across their team's client accounts. The ability to mentor a team of Lead Engagement Managers, and Engagement Managers to manage customer expectations and establish long-term partnerships by delivering complex projects effectively, on time, on budget, and according to agreed-upon scope, is critical to the Delivery Director's role.
<br><br>
Our Delivery Directors are responsible for the growth and development of their team of 4-8 Lead Engagement Managers, and Engagement Managers. This includes helping more junior Engagement Managers navigate difficult situations, serving as the primary escalation point for any issues they may encounter, and managing the career growth path of each member of their team.
<br><br>
What you'll be doing
<br>--------------------
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Oversee and mentor a team of Engagement Managers and Lead Engagement Managers that are responsible for the tactical execution of Onica projects</li>
<li>Manage and appropriately address project escalations pertaining to budget, scope, performance, etc.</li>
<li>Grow the capabilities of the Delivery &amp; Engagement practice through continued development of Onica's Engagement Delivery Framework (EDF) and maturation of Onica's consulting and transformation capabilities</li>
<li>Hands-on project management, including initiation, planning, and execution of projects and account-related activities, facilitating resolution of issues and roadblocks</li>
<li>Drive key decisions across projects, manage customer expectations, present and interface with project sponsors, and communicate trade-offs and risks</li>
<li>Effectively leverage Agile principles, coaching and training technical project and customer team members on EDF</li>
<li>Contribute to Onica's delivery lead sales efforts by establishing relationships with key customers, understanding Onica's value proposition and offerings, and aligning them with customer needs to grow the business</li>
</ul>
