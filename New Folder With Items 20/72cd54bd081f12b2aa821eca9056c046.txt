<h2>Solution Architect - Oracle Cloud Financials</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p>The Solution Architect will be primarily focused architecting functional solutions for client Oracle EBS/Cloud projects. It will involve providing expertise and leadership responsibilities and heavy focus in the design and red-design of GL Chart Accounts as well as clearly articulating solutions as part of the discussions with stakeholders at various levels with client business users. Candidate must have worked as a lead consultant for Finance Suite.</p>
<p><b>
Essential Job Functions</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Responsible for gap analysis of functional flows, provide solution designs, and perform demos for the Gaps solution from standard solution sets as well customized solutions</li>
<li>Has a very good understanding of implementation life cycles</li>
<li>Act in a functional lead capacity, responsible for implementing finance modules</li>
<li>Review, analyze and evaluate business system and user needs. Recommend, develop and execute plans for additional software module implementations based on business requirements</li>
<li>Act as functional advisor and manager of technology resources</li>
<li>Prepare design documentation and work with application delivery resources as necessary to support business requirements</li>
<li>Prepare and present updates on projects to executive management</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Knowledge, Skills &amp; Experience Requirements</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Must have worked as a lead consultant for Finance suite</li>
<li>Able to independently identify solution business requirements and recommend industry best practices</li>
<li>Able to lead cross functional discussions to analyze the requirements and brainstorm the solutions</li>
<li>Successful Consultants will have the following background:
<ul>
<li>10 years’ experience working with Oracle EBS Financials; professional who has transitioned to Oracle Cloud Financials, coming from the Oracle EBS space.</li>
<li>Conduct pre-sales activities and leading EBS/Cloud Financial project assessments</li>
<li>Experience in solution architecture and writing functional specifications, functional solution design for technical development</li>
<li>Business process analysis and redesign experience, strong business analysis skills, process mapping, business process redesign and implementation</li>
<li>Possess strong leadership skills</li>
</ul>
</li>
</ul>
<ul><li>Application experience
<ul>
<li>Oracle EBS General Ledger (GL) experience: Experience with 2 or more complete cradle to grave implementations of the General Ledger (GL) module, with responsibility for designing the solution, Chart of Account design and/or redesign, setting up and documenting the setup of those modules.</li>
<li>Experience with Multi-Org setup (Set of books, legal entities, operating units)</li>
<li>Foreign currency</li>
</ul>
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Proficiency/knowledge in multiple business processes:
<ul>
<li>General Ledger (GL)</li>
<li>Order-to-Cash (OTC)</li>
<li>Procure-to-Pay (PTP)</li>
<li>Fixed Asset Management (FA)</li>
<li>Budgeting</li>
<li>Cash Management</li>
</ul>
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Business Skills required
<ul>
<li>Systems implementation skills: requirements/process analysis, conceptual and detailed design, configuration, testing, training, change management and support</li>
<li>Consulting skills: self-motivated, team oriented, positive attitude, with a can-do, jump-right-in attitude</li>
<li>Excellent written and verbal communication including presentation skills</li>
<li>Ability to work independently and manage multiple task assignments</li>
<li>Tool knowledge: MS Project, MS Visio, MS PowerPoint, Excel, Word</li>
<li>Strong problem solving and troubleshooting skills with the ability to exercise mature judgment</li>
<li>Strong business background, working knowledge of generally accepted accounting principles</li>
<li>Effectively produces deliverables</li>
<li>Ability to identify, assess and mitigate relevant risks during project execution</li>
<li>Organized and detailed oriented</li>
</ul>
</li></ul>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree in Business or Accounting with a focus on Finance/Accounting</li>
<li>Chartered Accountant or CPA highly preferred</li>
