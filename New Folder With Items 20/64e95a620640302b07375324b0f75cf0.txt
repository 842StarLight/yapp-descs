<h2>Senior Financial Analyst - Calabasas Bakery</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div><b><i>Position Overview:</i></b></div>
You may know us for our delicious cheesecakes…and for being recognized by Fortune Magazine as one of the “100 Best Companies to Work For®” 6 years in a row! What you may <i>not </i>know is at The Cheesecake Factory Bakery, our staff members are our highest quality ingredient.
<div></div>
<br>
<div>Reporting to the Plant Controller, you will work with a team of 13 staff members at The Cheesecake Factory Bakery in Calabasas, CA. As the Senior Financial Analyst, you are a key individual contributor that will play an integral part in understanding and maximizing financial and operational efficiencies. You will be developing, preparing and analyzing data and reports to help the company understand trends and impacts. You will also play a key role in facilitating ongoing improvements to our reporting and forecasting processes and tools for a bakery with more than 700 total staff members that create delicious cheesecakes and memorable experiences.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div><b><i>You’ll thrive in this position if you are:</i></b></div>
<div>
<ul>
<li>
<b>A gold medal analyst: </b>you enjoy breaking down numerical data and all other sources of information. You systematically probe for additional information to gain a greater understanding of the problem.</li>
<li>
<b>A communicator: </b>you’re capable of translating complex business requirements to a non-technical audience in a clear and concise manner.</li>
<li>
<b>Excel savvy: </b>you’re able to take a rough dataset and use Microsoft Excel to turn it into something actionable.</li>
<li>
<b>Self-starting:</b> you have an understanding of current project needs and when it’s time to execute on crucial tasks.</li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<br>
<b><i>Here’s more of what you’ll get to do:</i></b>
</div>
<div><ul>
<li>Prepare and review key financial reporting schedules for management on a daily, weekly, and period basis.</li>
<li>Assist with the period end close process and reporting.</li>
<li>Prepare complex financial trend and data analysis such as sales trends, financial and expense performance, return on investment and other business investment opportunities.</li>
<li>Enable enhanced analysis through greater and more efficient access to numerous data sources.</li>
<li>Assist with planning process for annual and long-term budgets and strategy, including set up of application and working with cost center managers for submissions.</li>
<li>Facilitate ongoing improvements to reporting processes and infrastructure.</li>
<li>Perform variance analysis and summarize key findings.</li>
<li>Prepare customer pricing analysis and recommendations for current and new products.</li>
<li>Perform Ad-hoc reporting, special projects and other department support as needed.</li>
</ul></div>
<div></div>
<br>
<p><b>Qualifications</b></p>
<br>
<b><i>
What we require:
</i></b><div>
<ul>
<li>3+ years of related experience in Financial Planning and Analysis and/or Accounting.</li>
<li>Strong knowledge of finance and accounting principles.</li>
<li>Strong ability to read and interpret financial statements.</li>
<li>Exceptionally strong computer skills, particularly with spreadsheet modeling (Excel).</li>
<li>Proficiency in other Microsoft Office Applications (Word, PowerPoint, Outlook).</li>
<li>Exposure to Enterprise Resource Planning systems and software.</li>
<li>Strong written and verbal communication skills.</li>
<li>Detail oriented and accurate.</li>
<li>Ability to work independently and as a team member.</li>
<li>Bachelor’s Degree in Accounting, Finance, Economics or related field.</li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<br>
<b><i>What we prefer:</i></b>
</div>
<div>
<ul>
<li>Manufacturing industry experience.</li>
<br>
</ul>
