<p>Job Summary:</p>
<p><b>
Who are we?</b></p>
<p>
Live Nation Entertainment is the world’s leading live entertainment company, comprised of global market leaders: Ticketmaster, Live Nation Concerts, and Live Nation Media &amp; Sponsorship. Ticketmaster is the global leader in event ticketing with over 500 million tickets sold annually and more than 12,000 clients worldwide. Live Nation Concerts is the largest provider of live entertainment in the world promoting more than 30,000 shows and 95+ festivals annually for nearly 4,000 artists in over 40 countries. These businesses allow Live Nation Media &amp; Sponsorship to create strategic music marketing programs that connect over 900 brands with the 86 million fans that attend Live Nation Entertainment events each year. For additional information, visit www.livenationentertainment.com.</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Who are you?</b></p>
<p>
Passionate and motivated. Driven, with an entrepreneurial spirit. Resourceful, innovative, forward thinking and committed. At Live Nation Entertainment, our people embrace these qualities, so if this sounds like you then please read on!</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>
The Role:</b></p>
<p>
The US Concerts Finance/Accounting Department is in search of a highly motivated individual to join its Accounts Payable team. We are looking for an individual who is both passionate about music and possesses a strong professional work ethic, is eager and quick to learn.</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Receive &amp; process A/P invoices</li>
<li>
Perform A/P processing tasks, such as date-stamping of invoices, filing, reviewing and securing authorization for invoice payments, checking invoices for sales and use tax requirements, proper code classifications, providing G/L coding for invoices, and entering invoices into Oracle and Captiva</li>
<li>
Preparing paperwork and securing the proper W-9 documentation for new vendor requests</li>
<li>
Answering inquiries from vendors concerning open A/P items</li>
<li>
Following up and resolving open A/P credit items with vendors</li>
<li>
Initiating payments for items such as rent and taxes</li>
<li>
Inputting show checks used during the day/night of the show into Oracle</li>
<li>
Inputting weekly wire payments</li>
<li>
Review outstanding show checks that have not cleared the bank within 90 days for possible void and re-issue</li>
<li>
Complete monthly bank reconciliations in accounting software</li>
<li>
Other duties as assigned on an “as needed” basis</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Qualifications</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Bachelor's degree in Accounting or Finance, preferred or equivalent work experience</li>
<li>
Zero to three years comparable work experience</li>
<li>
Must be flexible, self-starter, change-oriented, self-motivated team player.</li>
<li>
Strong spreadsheet/computer skills (Excel)</li>
<li>
Oracle experience a plus</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p>
Applicants for employment in the U.S. must possess work authorization which does not require sponsorship by the employer for a visa.</p>
