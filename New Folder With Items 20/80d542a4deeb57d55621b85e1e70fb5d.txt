<h2>Director of Marketing</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Epic Sciences, Inc. is developing novel diagnostics to personalize and advance the treatment and management of cancer. Epic’s mission is to enable the rapid and non-invasive detection of genetic and molecular changes in cancer throughout a patient’s journey. The company was founded on a powerful platform to identify and characterize rare cells, including circulating tumor cells (CTCs). Our technology helps match patients to targeted therapies and monitor for drug resistance, so that the best treatment path can be chosen at every clinical decision point. Today, we partner with leading pharmaceutical companies and major cancer centers around the world. Epic’s goal is to commercialize our technology to increase the success rate of cancer drugs in clinical trials and improve patient outcomes by providing physicians real-time information to guide treatment choices.
</p>
<h1 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Director of Marketing
</b></h1>
<p>Reports To: Chief Commercial Officer<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>SUMMARY:<br>
</b></p>
<p></p>
<p>The Director of Marketing is an integral member of the Commercial team and supports the company’s rapidly growing commercial and services business. The successful candidate will be familiar with the life science market, with an emphasis in Pharma, Biotech and Physician sales; and be able to work both on a strategic and tactical level to support revenue growth. The position will report to the Chief Commercial Officer and oversee a team that will work to develop product positioning, global marketing strategy and then implement marketing programs and tactics to support the strategy. The successful result of this will consist of creating a strategic approach made up of focused messaging and positioning, targeted and clear sales support materials which will not only bring clarity and value to the end users, but also empower and train Epic staff (account managers, business development and internal staff) to have robust interactions with target customers.<br>
</p>
<p><b>ESSENTIAL FUNCTIONS:<br>
</b></p>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Responsible for developing business and marketing plans for assigned area. Develop all marketing deliverables for product commercialization which includes leading development of user documents and labeling required for development, initial launch, and global launch.
</li>

May be involved in translating global strategy into local marketing programs or translating local marketing programs into segment specific initiatives.
</li>
<li>Translate the above strategy into tactics in the form of sales/corporate development support, collateral development and leadership presentation materials
</li>
<li>Essential to the success of this is to liaise effectively between business and technical teams (PMs)

<li>Ability to work through and leverage distribution partners in promoting of Epic’s core product
</li>
<li>Provide guidance to maximize resources and impact of marketing activities (i.e. advertising, web content, conference participation and content, abstracts, podium presentation content and speakers).
</li>
<li>Manage a team to:

<li>Develop Brand strategies, both print and web-based collateral, webinars, and newsletters
</li>
<li>Develop tradeshow, advertising, and promotional programs
</li>
<li>Set strategic direction and then use internal as well as external resources to develop marketing programs
</li>
<li>Measure and quantify effectiveness of marketing programs
</li>
<li>Gather competitive information as part of a positioning strategy
</li>
<li>Prepare and execute on press releases and general mar com duties
</li>
<li>Develops go-to-market strategy, in collaboration with in-region Platform Product Marketing; gathers understanding of customer needs
</li>
</ul>
<li>Collaborate with external agencies on development of marketing materials for launch, brand initiatives and any necessary collateral
</li>
<li>Oversees and ensures alignment of content with initiative objectives
</li>
