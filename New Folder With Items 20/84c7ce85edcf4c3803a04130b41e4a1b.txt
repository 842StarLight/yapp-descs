<h2>Marketing Analytics Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>About NCR
</b></p>
<p>NCR Corporation (NYSE: NCR) is a leading software- and services-led enterprise provider in the financial, retail, hospitality, telecom and technology industries. NCR is headquartered in Atlanta, Ga., with 34,000 employees and does business in 180 countries. NCR is a trademark of NCR Corporation in the United States and other countries.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>POSITION </b>: Marketing Analytics Specialist
</p>
<p><b>LOCATION </b>: Atlanta, GA
</p>
<p></p>
<p>The Marketing Analytics Specialist provides insight to business leaders, using data to deepen campaign performance knowledge and improve the effectiveness of campaign strategies. This role studies customer behavior to campaigns and analyzes trends across channels – merging transactional, behavioral, engagement, and segmentation data to improve the effectiveness of consumer interactions, brand events and business initiatives. In addition, the Marketing Analytics Specialist develops marketing insights to quantify success or identify potential marketing or strategic opportunities.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>MAJOR DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Direct and coordinate insight and campaign development process
</li>
<li>Leads, plans, and executes project planning with multiple internal teams and organizations
</li>
<li>Responsible for marketing &amp; campaign optimization
</li>
<li>Manages several brand, industry, product, and communication channel focused projects concurrently
</li>
<li>Manage expectations and SLA’s to ensure all timelines are appropriately defined and achieved to ensure brand success
</li>
<li>Designs and implements sound analytic approaches and testing methodologies to address marketing and business questions
</li>
<li>Forecast results, devising strategic course corrections to meet or exceed business results
</li>
<li>Conduct hands-on analysis of customer and campaign data using analytical and statistical tools such as SPSS to uncover insights and provide program recommendations.
</li>
<li>Develops and executes well-structured marketing measurement models to calculate campaign budgeting and ROI.
</li>
<li>Analyzes website consumer behavior and customer experience data to drive corporate brand strategy and messaging.
</li>
<li>Fulfills ad hoc marketing research and data analysis requests.
</li>
<li>Drives increased effectiveness of retention and acquisition campaigns recommending targeting tactics and predictive models
</li>
<li>Summarizes findings in written, verbal, and data visualization reports to key stakeholders.
</li>
<li>Builds, manages, and maintains reporting and dashboards to track KPIs, pipeline and revenue contribution for business unit programs and campaigns.
</li>
<li>Works with team to summarize and present results of campaign analysis focusing on insights and recommendations to affect CRM strategies and tactics
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>BASIC QUALIFICATIONS
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s Degree
</li>
<li>2-5 years of related work experience in Marketing or Business Analytics
</li>
<li>Experience in creating processes for ensuring accuracy, efficiency, and prioritization
</li>
<li>Experience building, managing, and maintaining reporting and dashboards to track KPIs, pipeline and revenue contribution for business unit programs and campaigns.
</li>
<li>Strong communication skills, with ability to explain complex models and data-sets to business stakeholders
</li>
<li>Ability to explain to stakeholder’s technical challenges in campaign execution and manage expectations and resolution of issues when they arise
<br>
</li>
</ul>
<p><b>PREFERRED QUALIFICATIONS
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Graduate degree
</li>
<li>Knowledge of capabilities of CRM (Salesforce) and Marketing Automation Platforms (Oracle Eloqua), Website and Digital (Google &amp; Adobe Analytics) etc.
</li>
<li>Experience working with large data-set and statistical analysis software (SPSS, R, Tableau, Minitab)
</li>
</ul>
