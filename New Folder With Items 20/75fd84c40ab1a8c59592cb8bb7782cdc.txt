<p>"Builders and Fixers, Wanted!"</p>
<p><b>Ephesoft</b> is immediately seeking a VP of Global Sales to be based in our Irvine, Ca. HQ. Qualified candidates from other areas within the region may be considered. Travel is required.</p>
<p><b>Company Description: </b></p>
<p>Ephesoft, Inc. develops technology that makes meaning out of unstructured data for decision-makers worldwide. Using supervised machine learning and a focus on efficiency and reliability, Ephesoft has crafted the next generation of enterprise content capture and data discovery solutions. Organizations use this power to automate any document-based business processes, improving accuracy, increasing productivity and reducing costs. Ephesoft is headquartered in Irvine, Calif., with regional offices throughout the US, EMEA and Asia Pacific. The company is undergoing rapid growth and has customers in over 50 countries. To learn more, visit www.ephesoft.com</p>

<p><b>Job Description: </b></p>
<p>We are looking for a dynamic leader to join our rapidly expanding software company, challenging the status quo in multiple industries. We want Builders and Fixers on our team, and this role is a pivotal component to our continued growth.</p>
<p>Reporting to the Chief Executive Officer, the VP of Global Sales will be responsible for driving success by leading our Global teams to new heights and maximizing our market potential. This person will manage a mix of Regional VPs, some individual contributor enterprise sales reps, channel reps, inside sales, etc. As a member of the Senior Leadership Team, this individual will take command of the sales motion globally..</p>
<p>This is a hands-on role which will require the successful individual to demonstrate field sales leadership, a solid understanding of the sales process and sales management, and expertise in leveraging/driving partner relationships. You must be comfortable engaging on deals and in front of customers/partners at the senior leadership level. We also need someone who moves fast and is comfortable building a high-growth &amp; high-performance culture.</p>
<p>We believe in earning the right to believe in our success, working as a team, and “Owning It”. In this role, we are looking for a high energy, positive leader who can motivate and lead a team through change. As a leader and not just a manager, the VP of Global Sales will influence outcomes across the board with departments that may not be part of his/her direct reporting structure such as Customer Support and Professional Services.</p>
<p><b>Requirements / Qualifications: </b></p>
<p>To succeed in this role, we are looking for a strong leader who has the energy, skills, mindset, and passion to propel our North American sales organization to the forefront of our industry. To be successful, the right candidate will need the following attributes/skills:</p>
<ul><li>10+ years in B2B Enterprise SaaS sales. Strong preference for direct experience with ERP, BI, and BPM/workflow tools.</li></ul>
<ul><li>5-8+ years in Senior Sales Management (VP level)</li></ul>
<ul><li>Experience leading teams comprised of Field Sales, Inside Sales, and Channel Sales Managers</li></ul>
<ul><li>Background in direct selling roles and 5+ years enterprise sales success as an individual contributor</li></ul>
<ul><li>A high degree of expertise and comfort in selling via partners, while managing the concurrent dynamics of both a channel and a direct sales strategy</li></ul>
<ul><li>A strong degree of demonstrated comfort &amp; proficiency with all elements of sales management including planning, forecasting, pipeline management, prospecting, sales strategy/execution</li></ul>
<ul><li>Enthusiastic approach to training and development of the sales team; must be an excellent coach and mentor</li></ul>
<ul><li>Ability to recruit and hire high-caliber enterprise sales professionals</li></ul>
<ul><li>Proven ability at managing remote teams of 30+ employees.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Operational and execution focus - ensuring that we execute flawlessly on our major initiatives and predictably deliver what we promise. The ability to prioritize and maintain focus is essential</li></ul>
