<h2>Media Operations Analyst</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div></div>
<div><div>
<div>
<div>As a Media Operations Analyst, you are responsible for analyzing data, thinking creatively and dreaming up innovative strategies to make sure our clients reach the right target audience in the most effective way possible. Armed with critical analysis, media planners devise or recommend strategies for using certain media effectively to attract and retain customers, increase brand recognition, and maintain customer satisfaction and loyalty. Media Analysts need to work closely with their clients, understand their wants, needs and objectives and then make the important decisions about what media channels should be used for specific campaigns. These choices have an impact on what the creative team produces and vice versa.</div>
<div>
You will evangelize new Hylink products and technologies throughout the agency, and develop and manage strong relationships with internal agency influencers and decision-makers, driving scalable product adoption and growth across the broader portfolio of accounts. Reporting directly to senior agency staff, you will learn the skills necessary to succeed in advertising, as well as understand emerging business models as they happen. Your knowledge of online media combined with your communication skills and analytical abilities shapes how new and existing business grows. You will be involved with a team of fast-paced, multitasking high-achievers. Covering training across programmatic, digital, industry analysis, and client service, you will develop influential, relationship-building skills, learn to provide Hylink-caliber client service, and fully grasp research and market analysis. You will anticipate how decisions are made, and persistently explore and uncover the business needs of Hylink's key partners and objectives.
</div>
</div>
<div><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities</b></h3>
<div><ul>
<li>
Coordinate day-to-day as well as long-term schedules of key agency management.</li>
<li>
Execute self-service platform campaign buys (eg. DV360, Facebook, PMP, etc.).</li>
<li>
You will collect and analyze information about different digital media channels, with regards to consumer behavior, circulation, audience trends and the impact of different methods.</li>
<li>
Research and coordinate with team to determine the best media outlet mix for our campaigns.</li>
<li>
Optimize ad campaigns according to geographic exposure, frequency, time spans and more</li>
<li>
Develop briefs, client reports, and key agency documentation under guidance of senior staff.</li>
<li>
Assist various senior staff in execution of internal agency projects.</li>
<li>
Assist account services team to manage daily business of account(s) with responsibility for ensuring that work is completed on-time and on-budget.</li>
<li>
Support the execution of strategy, provide brand stewardship and maintain strong client satisfaction on assigned brand(s).</li>
</ul></div>
</div></div>
<div><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Minimum Qualifications</b></h3>
<div><ul>
<li>
2-3 years experience in an agency, tech startup, marketing, integrated marketing communications, or media sales role.</li>
<li>
Graduate of a Bachelor’s, Master’s, or Associate’s degree program focused on Business, Communication, Marketing, and Market Research fields.</li>
<li>
Proven experience as a media planner/buyer ; experience in programmatic is preferred</li>
</ul></div>
</div></div>
<div><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Preferred Qualifications</b></h3>

Familiarity with campaign evaluation metrics and media buying.</li>
<li>
Ability to travel up to 10% of the time.</li>
<li>
A deep understanding of the industry's issues, a vision for its growth, and a commitment to advance Hylink's forward-looking strategies within the marketplace.</li>
<li>
Strong presentation, analytical and problem solving skills.</li>
