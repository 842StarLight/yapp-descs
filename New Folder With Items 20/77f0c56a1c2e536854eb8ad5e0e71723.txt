<h2>National Strategic Channel Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Overview</b></h2>
A strategic CM is responsible for the overall relationship between the strategic partner(s) and LogMeIn (Jive/UCC), and acts as an advocate of the partner. This person would establish deep relationships with key strategic partner stakeholders, and in collaboration with the local channel team(s) provides and manages to jointly developed business objectives, and engages in strategic sales opportunities, to drive profitable growth for LMI and our partner(s). A successful candidate will possess a strong business acumen, leadership experience and large partner management experience.
<br>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities</b></h2>
<ul>
<li>Enable and develop assigned partner accounts in order to achieve assigned sales quota goals.</li>
<li>Establish and manage executive, regional, local level relationships with assigned partners to insure strategic and tactical alignment and successful execution of an annual business plan.</li>
<li>Develop annual business plans with assigned partners and conduct quarterly business reviews to insure execution to plan.</li>
<li>Interact with Jive channel team to achieve street level alignment and help support the of close of opportunities with partner.</li>
<li>Work closely with Jive marketing to execute business development initiatives.</li>
<li>Insure all assigned partners are fully trained and enabled including produce, sales, and tools.</li>
<li>Develop and maintain understanding of individuals partner business model, plans and solutions and effectively communicate within the Jive organization.</li>
<li>Potentially recruit, enable and develop new partners in order to drive new revenue growth.</li>
<li>20% - 30% travel.</li>
</ul>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Qualifications</b></h2>
<ul>
<li>10+ years of sales channel management experience in hi-tech Unifidied Communications, SaaS, UCaaS, SW, Collaboration (WebEx, Blue Jeans, Zoom), and or Telecom</li>
<li>Worked for or with large technology value-added-resellers</li>
<li>Successful track record with managing large strategic partners</li>
<li>National channel management experience preferred</li>
</ul>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>About</b></h2>
<br>
LogMeIn simplifies how people connect with each other and the world around them to drive meaningful interactions, deepen relationships, and create better outcomes for individuals and businesses. One of the world’s top 10 public SaaS companies, and a market leader in communication &amp; conferencing, identity &amp; access, and customer engagement &amp; support solutions, LogMeIn has millions of customers spanning virtually every country across the globe. LogMeIn is headquartered in Boston with additional locations across North America, Europe, Middle East, Asia and Australia.
