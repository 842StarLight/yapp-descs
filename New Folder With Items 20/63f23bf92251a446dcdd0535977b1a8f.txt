<h2>Rollover Specialist - TeleSales Outbound/Inbound (FINRA Series 6 Licensed)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>We are recruiting for a Rollover Specialist, requiring a FINRA Series 6 license, in our outbound/inbound Telemarketing sales department, in Boca Raton, Florida. This opportunity offers base salary plus annual bonus earning potential, an exceptional employee benefits package that starts on the very first day of employment, and is an onsite position (no remote work).</p>
<p>This is a telephone sales position, retaining assets of terminated participants by contacting and informing them of features and benefits of Mutual of America Rollover IRA; providing inbound support for IRA Rollovers and annuity exchange requests; and contacting target customers to cross-sell financial products. Possible cold calling is required.</p>
<p>ESSENTIAL JOB FUNCTIONS:</p>
<ul>
<li>Outbound Telesales: Follow-up on leads developed via advertising programs; contact participants/contract owners identified through marketing programs; provide overview of Company services and products; assist individuals in opening individual annuity products; rolling funds into Mutual of America's IRA; contact beneficiaries (spouse and non spouse) to retain assets within a Mutual of America products. Sales tasks include outlining product features and benefits; respond to buyer's objections. Involve Regional Office sales staff as required. Update Company's Contact Management System (CMS) as required. Collect information and communicate with Regional Offices and other Marketing units. Possible cold calling required.</li>
<li>Inbound Telesales: Assist individuals calling the 800-Line relative to Rollover IRA and non-qualified annuity exchanges; participate in marketing and asset retention programs; assist customers with information about various Company products and services. Ensure CMS is updated as required.</li>
<li>Follow up with external financial service provider to ensure that rollover/exchange requirements are completed in a timely manner; communicate with applicant regarding the process.</li>
<li>Assist Telemarketing management in other duties as assisgned, including inbound 800-Line call handling.</li>
</ul>
<p>QUALIFICATIONS:</p>
<ul>
<li>FINRA Series 6 License required.</li>
<li>FINRA Series 63 license (or must be obtained)</li>
<li>Life, Health and Variable Insurance license (or must be obtained).</li>
<li>College degree.</li>
<li>Three years outbound and inbound Telesales experience in the Insurance and/or financial service industry. Cold calling required.</li>
<li>Must be able to work onsite office hours, rotating 8-hour shifts between 9:00 a.m. and 8:00 p.m., Monday through Friday. (no remote work)</li>
<li>Must be able to work the evening's shift until 8:00 p.m. approximately 3 days per week.</li>
<li>Must be able to work occasional Saturdays.</li>
<li>Bilingual (English/Spanish) is a plus.</li>
</ul>
