<p>Job Description</p>
<p></p>
<p>Are you passionate about delivery, enabling and empowering teams to succeed and looking to make a difference in the emerging space of the Internet of Things? If so, please read on!</p>
<p>
IoT is the next computer revolution, offering exciting opportunities to transform society and business using internet-connected devices. Arm’s Internet Services Group (ISG) is creating device software and cloud services to enable these opportunities to become reality.</p>
<p>
Within ISG, the Devices group is a fast-growing SaaS business, developing and proliferating the industry-defining IoT Platform, Pelion, underpinned by the leading IoT platform OS, Mbed. We’re helping industry leaders harness IoT in new ways, gain insight into their data, and unlock new business possibilities.</p>
<p>
The devices group has an opening for a fixed term contracting position as a <b>Business Operations Manager </b>to track, monitor and plan the internal operations of our business and aid in the communication efforts between our leadership team, our employees and our sister business units. If you have relevant experience this could be a great opportunity to try a change in career direction or develop new skills. If you think you could be that person, we’d love to hear from you!</p>
<p><b>
About</b> <b>the</b> <b>role</b></p>
<p>
In this role, you will coordinate with multiple functions and teams, on behalf of the entire BU, to communicate and track objectives, risks, budget, headcount, and critical metrics. You will be dependent on other activities as well as being depended upon, and will need to successfully handle transactions in both directions.</p>
<p>
It is meaningful that you perform as a great team member, providing quality and reliable information and being self-organized with tracking, recording, monitoring and reporting. Your stakeholders will rely on the data you provide and key business decisions will be taken from it. You’ll need to be enthusiastic and flexible in applying different techniques to succeed. You should be an excellent communicator with the passion and resilience to get things done!</p>
<p>
You will work across geographical and cultural boundaries, with the possibility of travel to other Arm sites as necessary.</p>
<p><b>
What</b> <b>will</b> <b>I</b> <b>be</b> <b>accountable</b> <b>for?</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Supporting the VP Product and Operations to monitor annual objectives for the group, tracking and reporting on these on a quarterly basis</li>
<li>
Collect, monitor and report on risks for the BU</li>
<li>
Work with our finance partner to define the two-year plan and ten-year plan, tracking, monitoring and reporting against this</li>
<li>
Track and report against the defined critical metrics for the BU</li>
<li>
Manage the headcount needs of the business, alongside the hiring managers and provide key pieces of analysis to support hiring decisions</li>
<li>
Provide operational support for the hiring process and assist in onboarding communications</li>
<li>
Coordinate and host weekly leadership meetings and follow up on corresponding action items</li>
<li>
Plan and execute group all-hands, town halls, and group communication blasts</li>
<li>
Serve on ISG communications committee to ensure messaging is supported and consistent.</li>
</ul>
