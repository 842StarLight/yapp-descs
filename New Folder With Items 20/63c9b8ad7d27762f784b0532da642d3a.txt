<h2>Product Analyst-PowerLine</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>Product Analyst-PowerLine – Spartanburg, S.C.</p>
<p>What You’ll Be Doing:</p>
<ul>
<li>Serves as a liaison between the PM team and marketing, sales and client services as it relates to product enhancement or features.</li>
<li>Serves as subject matter expert for assigned PowerLine product or services.</li>
<li>Gathers and documents functional, system, and data requirements for assigned product features.</li>
<li>Develops functional specification within VersionOne for assigned features.</li>
<li>Responsible for grooming functional specifications with PM, Dev and QA to verify accuracy and product consistency.</li>
<li>Assists with product design, documentation, and development of various documentation on PowerLine products and services.</li>
<li>Assesses available technologies and recommends solutions to PM team.</li>
<li>Ensure on-going compliance with industry standards such as NCPDP and other applicable standards organizations.</li>
</ul>
<p><i>Specialized Knowledge and Skills:</i></p>
<ul>
<li>Knowledge of software development methodologies, design, and implementation.</li>
<li>Specialized knowledge of claims adjudication (switching) and/or ePrescribing back end processes and standards.</li>
<li>Analytical and design skills at multiple product levels.</li>
<li>Must possess research and analysis, project planning, and implementation.</li>
</ul>
<p>Who Are We Looking For:</p>
<ul>
<li>Someone who is detail oriented and is dedicated to a high standard of quality.</li>
<li>Effective and professional communication with both technical and non-technical personnel in both written and verbal forms.</li>
<li>Good understanding of business issues and how they impact overall project plans.</li>
<li>Good time management, decision making, organization, presentation, and problem resolutions skills.</li>
<li>Good communication, human relations and interpersonal skills.</li>
<li>Outstanding intuitive problem solver.</li>
</ul>
<p>What Education and Experience You’ll Need to Have:</p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree in computer science, software engineering, business, or mathematics.</li>
<li>Typically requires a minimum of three years’ experience in product development and/or product support.</li>
<li>Relevant experience plus an AS degree may substitute for bachelor’s degree</li>
</ul>

</ul>
