----------------------
<br>Why we're hiring
<br>----------------------
<br><br>
Alloy is rapidly growing and seeking a Product Manager to develop and communicate product ideas that will substantially improve the experience of our customers. This role is an opportunity to lead on a newly formed team focused on bringing ideas to life by prioritizing, managing and shipping new Alloy product features &amp; enhancements.
<br><br>
--------------------------------
<br>What you'll be doing
<br>--------------------------------
<br><br>
<b>As the Product Manager, you'll:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Work with our CEO and technical leaders on a coherent product strategy and own Alloy's product roadmap</li>
<li>Break that strategy into phases, prioritize and drive backlogs for on-time releases working alongside the development team</li>
<li>Work at all levels of the release process: Design, Engineering, QA, Customer Success, and Marketing</li>
<li>Be the voice of the customer: attain a deep understanding of their needs and wants</li>
<li>Build something great!</li>
</ul>
---------------------------
<br>Who we're looking for
<br>---------------------------
<br><br>
Reporting to the CTO, Alloy is looking for a project-focused product manager with proven success of shipping outstanding customer experiences.
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>5+ years of experience building great software products.</li>
<li>Familiar with customer onboarding, know-your-customer, identity management, enterprise account management or similar feature sets</li>
<li>A track record of owning product initiatives from requirements to fully deployed functionality</li>
<li>Worked with internal project stakeholders from marketing, sales, operations, customer success, and executive management to translate customer needs into a product</li>
</ul>
We are a lean team, so your impact will be felt immediately. If this all sounds like a good fit for you, why not join us?
