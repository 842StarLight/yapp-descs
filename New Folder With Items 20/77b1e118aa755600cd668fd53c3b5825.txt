<p><b>THORNE </b>Software Development Engineer II</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
We are looking for top of market talent to help grow and innovate our company. If you are someone who embraces challenge, fosters innovation, and desires to make a difference in the world, then let’s talk.</p>
<p>
At Thorne we make products that matter - ones that make people's lives better. Each day begins with a mission to help others discover and achieve their best health. We count on our team members to challenge and push the boundaries to make that happen. As a self-motivated Software Development Engineer II, you’ll be joining a team of more than 350 passionate individuals committed to our cause of providing superior health solutions.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
The Software Development Engineer II is responsible for developing, testing, and implementing various ERP and web-based initiatives. Projects will include modifying existing ERP and Ecommerce code bases, designing and developing new business critical applications, and driving organizational efficiency through the use of technology. The Software Development Engineer II will also be responsible for defining project objectives, defining scope of work, overseeing development and testing, coordinating efforts of team members and managing 3rd party contractors in order to deliver projects according to plan. This is an Exempt position located in our Madison, WI office.</p>
<p>
A day in the life of a Software Development Engineer II at Thorne requires:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Responsibility for the overall management, security, support, and feature development of the company’s ERP and Ecommerce code bases.</li>
<li>
Develop fault tolerant, high performance integrations with internal and third party systems.</li>
<li>
Assist in the development, testing, and implementation of enhancements to the company’s web properties.</li>
<li>
Assist in the development, testing, and implementation of enhancements to the company’s ERP system.</li>
<li>
Develop, document, and maintain business reporting solutions.</li>
<li>
Understand business needs, translate into technical requirements, and develop appropriate solutions.</li>
<li>
Provide technical leadership and mentoring to Thorne’s development staff.</li>
<li>
Participate in an on-call rotation providing technical support for Thorne’s software assets.</li>
<li>
Effectively communicate to executives and business owners as well as system users.</li>
<li>
Quickly respond to user requests for immediate issues while also being able to drive projects to completion.</li>
<li>
Create implementation and migration plans as well as back-out and contingency plans for all projects.</li>
<li>
Stay current on technology trends and new software solutions.</li>
</ul>
