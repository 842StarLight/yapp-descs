<h2>VP of Sponsorship</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>New York Public Radio is committed to continuing to build online, mobile and other emerging platforms to extend its content beyond terrestrial broadcasting and meet the rapidly changing ways that audiences consume and engage with news, information, music and culture content. The VP of Sponsorship will lead New York Public Radio’s revenue strategy, building the organization’s ability to deliver customized and off-the-shelf offerings to sponsor and underwriting prospects across multiple media platforms. He or she will be a “revenue evangelist” for the organization, working collaboratively with key constituents internally and externally to drive future growth in a manner that is consistent with New York Public Radio’s overall mission.</p>
<p>
This executive will assume the corporate underwriting revenue leadership position at NYPR, directing the local, national, and digital underwriting managers and staff responsible for radio, digital media, a growing array of online and mobile products, and The Jerome L. Greene Space.</p>
<p>
This role is designed to both enhance the commitments to NYPR from current sponsors and significantly propel forward the organization’s ability to prepare, position, and deliver on an expanding agenda of ‘radio plus’ digital ad products and live event sponsor opportunities as NYRP’s on-air listening audience, members, and new audiences increasingly engage with its brands and programs ‘off-air.’</p>
<p>
Specific Duties Include:</p>

Develop and establish near term and long-range corporate underwriting revenue strategy and planning in context of the overall New York Public Radio mission and service priorities.</li></ul>

<p></p>
<br>

THE PERSON</b></p>

Hands-on knowledge of the marketing tools and practices that drive digital advertising environment, including ad targeting; measurement, pricing models; mobile and social media applications; online audio and video ad products and trends.</li>

Solid knowledge of both local spot media and national network media models.</li>
