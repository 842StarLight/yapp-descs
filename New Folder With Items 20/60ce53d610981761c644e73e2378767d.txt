<h2>Product Marketing Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><ul>
<div>
<p>Roche Diagnostics has created a new business charged with building a modern software stack that will change the way patients are cared for. Our challenge is to bring together complex diagnostic and treatment data from medical devices and IT systems within a hospital and intelligently process and present this data to doctors and care-givers to improve patient care.</p>
<p></p>
<p>
The Product Marketing Manager that will have a direct hand in driving the growth of a new decision support software product. This is an exceptional opportunity to help define the product marketing strategy, messaging, competitive positioning, and go-to market strategy for an emerging and transformative category - be a driving force at the forefront of the next evolution of personalized healthcare! Under the direction of the Product Director, this role will work closely with the Product Managers and other cross-functional teams (Customer Success, Roche regional affiliates, and Support) to effectively translate complex product features into easily understood messaging, content, and tools to our key audiences.</p>
<p></p>
<p>
Responsible for maintaining and continuously improving the quality system and achieving quality objectives through daily actions.</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>
The Opportunity:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Own NPI activities</li>
<li>
Create and manage the implementation of product level annual marketing plan for each customer segment including but not limited to strategies for promotion, management of key opinion leaders and conference strategies.</li>
<li>
Create promotional materials to help the uptake of the product. Collaborate with the regional affiliates to develop the sales tools that support the selling process of the assigned product.</li>
<li>
Responsible for creating and developing innovative product level marketing campaigns that drive awareness and demand</li>
<li>
In collaboration with the Product Director and Product Manager, contribute to product positioning and develop messaging for different personas/segmentation</li>
<li>
Build and implement the strategy for new product launches, collaborating with sales affiliates and other cross-functional teams to ensure well-coordinated execution.</li>
<li>
Act as a product SME to the regional affiliates and evangelize the value proposition of the products including product training, event support, and key customer demos, etc. Support commercial teams as needed.</li>
<li>
Responsible for content creation for the assigned product to support demand generation and content marketing campaigns with expertly crafted content that speaks to each target vertical and persona.</li>
<li>
Analyze the effectiveness of all marketing programs and expenditures and make recommendations to improve effectiveness and efficiency.</li>
<li>
Work with the Digital Marketing to define and execute social media marketing strategy.</li>
<li>
Research the market trends, customer insights, barriers and competitive information to identify customer and purchaser needs for target segments and persona development.</li>
<li>
Stay up to date on the competitive landscape and be the go to expert on how to win for the assigned product</li>
<li>
Advise Product Director and Product Manager on best practices of product marketing and launch.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Who you are:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor's degree in Marketing, Business, or related discipline required.</li>
<li>
5+ years B2B in product marketing</li>
<li>
Proficient in Microsoft Office suite, Google Suite, and Adobe Creative Suite</li>
<li>
Excellent listening, verbal and written communication/presentation skills</li>
<li>
Experience developing the positioning and messaging for bringing new products to market that serve multiple use cases and personas</li>
<li>
Expert at creating visually compelling, engaging presentations that distill complicated concepts into succinct stories.</li>
<li>
Experience developing and validating positioning, category creation, messaging, and competitive segmentation.</li>
<li>
Proven partner who can create a trusted relationship/partnership with sales and customers.</li>
<li>
Proven ability to deliver content including presentations, datasheets, white papers, blog posts, web content, etc.</li>
<li>
Experience managing third party vendors, including evaluation, contracting, budgeting, scheduling and project management.</li>
<li>
Self-motivated with a true passion for product marketing and marketplace platforms and a strong work ethic</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Preferred:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
MBA preferred.</li>
<li>
Experience in healthcare, medical device, MedTech, or SaaS product industry strongly preferred.</li>
<li>
Experience in oncology marketing preferred.</li>
<li>
Previous experience in a commercial role preferred.</li>
</ul>
