<h2>Manager, Customer Success</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<div>Real Customer Success comes from the heart. The foundation of our culture is Customer First.<br>
<br>
In this role you will be responsible for the leadership and management of a regional team of customer success managers (CSMs). The Manager, CSM will oversee a team of CSMs in a defined industry and be responsible for some pre-sales activity, post-sale account management and customer relationship development. You will play an active role in defining strategies that maximize our customers' return on investment and accelerate adoption of current and future Autodesk solutions.<br>
<br>
<b>Responsibilities</b><br>
People Leadership<br>
<ul>
<li>Leads a team of CSMs in the Americas to ensure success for some of our largest Named Accounts</li>
<li>
Articulates a clear role and direction for the team</li>
<li>
Demonstrates a "can-do" spirit, a sense of optimism, ownership and commitment</li>
<li>
Provides useful real-time feedback and coaching to the team</li>
<li>
Ensures the availability and development of the talent needed to meet current and future organizational goals</li>
<li>
Establishes and nurtures positive relationships with internal Delivery, Support, Product and Sales teams</li>
</ul>
<br>
Results Leadership<br>
<ul>
<li>Takes on tough challenges quickly and directly</li>
<li>
Helps the team prioritize what is most important</li>
<li>
Builds success plans in collaboration with Sales and Solutions Executives to align customer and Autodesk objectives</li>
<li>
Ensures the team delivers services to customers according to published SLAs and initiates service improvement plans as required</li>
<li>
Supports sales organization in positioning Customer Success offerings and provides weekly Customer Success forecast</li>
</ul>
<br>
Thought Leadership<br>
<ul>
<li>Champions new ideas, technologies and initiatives in the market</li>
<li>
Creates an environment that supports innovation and encourages the team to come up with opportunities for improving the customer experience</li>
</ul>
<br>
<b>
Minimum Qualifications</b><br>
<ul>
<li>5+ years of managing people and leading customer-facing organizations</li>
<li>
Ability to influence through persuasion, negotiation, and consensus building</li>
<li>
Deep understanding of value drivers in SaaS business models</li>
<li>
Excellent communication and presentation skills</li>
<li>
Prior consulting and customer success experience preferred</li>
</ul>
<br>
<b>
The Ideal Candidate</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Strong empathy for customers AND passion for revenue and growth</li>
<li>
Technical Aptitude: Ability to learn Autodesk’s product portfolio capabilities</li>
<li>
You’re driven: No one needs to push you to excel; it’s just who you are</li>
<li>
Enthusiastic and creative leader with the ability to inspire others</li>
<li>
A growth mindset that supports continuous learning and improvement</li>
</ul>
<br>
