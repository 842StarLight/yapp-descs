<p><b>
ABOUT INTERSTATE RESTORATION
</b></p>
<p>Interstate Restoration is a national emergency response restoration and general contractor specializing in the return of compromised property to their pre-loss condition. We work with our clients to restore their properties. Our team restores commercial, multifamily, hospitality, and educational properties from water damage, flood, fire, smoke, and natural disasters. We honor our commitments, enforce high ethical standards, and treat all stakeholders with respect. Our projects are done right, on time, and on budget.</p>
<p>
We ensure that we are providing world-class products and services through the best people in the industry. In order to attract and retain the highest-caliber staff, we offer competitive compensation, a robust benefits package, and a fun work environment. We work hard and play hard because life is too short to not enjoy what we do.</p>
<p><b>
A DAY IN THE LIFE AS A PROJECT DIRECTOR</b></p>
<p>
As a Project Director, you estimate construction related projects using Xactimate and other professional estimating software. Your solid understanding of the true costs of construction increases the accuracy of margins/line items within Xactimate. Your positive, friendly disposition comes through in all your interactions and is how you are able to foster and maintain strong relationships with insurance adjusters, property managers, property owners, industry related associations, and other potential business sources.</p>
<p>
You actively market Interstate Restoration through associations, meetings, local functions, and trade shows. You also develop and execute new marketing strategies for company growth and provide projection reports on individual loss sales and growth opportunities. You are proud of the important role you play in the success of Interstate Restoration.</p>
<p><b>
QUALIFICATIONS</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Proficient in large loss and commercial claims</li>
<li>
Proficient in Xactimate or other loss estimating software</li>
<li>
Experience in mold/asbestos abatement</li>
<li>
Experience in the drying and restoration industry including structural drying</li>
<li>
Experience reading blueprints/architectural plans</li>
</ul>
