<h2>Sales Support and Data Analyst</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p><b>Kingsley Associates</b> seeks a Sales Support and Data Analyst to join our team in Atlanta. The Sales Support and Data Analyst manages the proposal building and contracting process. To complete this process, the Sales Support and Data Analyst facilitates timely internal communications with other Kingsley Team members and also interfaces with clients and prospects. The Sales Support and Data Analyst creates and ensures the accuracy of reporting related to the business development pipeline primarily driven from Salesforce. Secondarily, this role supports the marketing function. The ideal candidate for Sales Support and Data Analyst will be detail-oriented, a proactive problem-solver, a strong communicator, and have analytical capabilities.</p>
<p>
Kingsley Associates’ work environment is fast-paced, dynamic, and interactive, with each team member playing a critical role in expanding the company. We are dedicated to providing a collaborative, team-first environment that also fosters individual development.</p>
<p><b>
Benefits We Offer:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Competitive base salary with bonus potential</li>
<li>Close-knit work environment focused on growth, performance, and accountability</li>
<li>Medical, dental, and vision benefits</li>
<li>Generous PTO policy and 10 company-paid holidays</li>
<li>401(k) plan with company contribution</li>
<li>Training focused on aligning individual development and company growth</li>
</ul>
<p><b>What You Will Be Doing:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Create proposals and facilitate client contract renewals ensuring accuracy of information and expediting communication within Kingsley, with clients/prospects, and with legal counsel</li>
<li>Create and update sales presentations and supporting materials for business development proposals</li>
<li>Manage the accuracy and collection of all client and prospect information within Salesforce</li>
<li>Oversee business development reporting</li>
<li>Assist in preparing for select industry conferences</li>
<li>Answer sales inquiries (phone, web, and email) by gathering appropriate information and route to the appropriate sales executives</li>
<li>Assist in implementing marketing initiatives</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Qualifications:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Four-year bachelor’s degree in relevant field of study</li>
<li>2-3 years of sales and/or marketing experience, preferably B2B</li>
<li>Intense attention to detail</li>
<li>Advanced proficiency with MS Word, MS PowerPoint</li>
<li>Experience with Salesforce</li>
<li>Powerful communication skills, both written and verbal</li>
<li>Proven ability to quickly and accurately respond to requests from both internal and external audiences</li>
<li>High-energy and dynamic personality with the ability to continuously drive Kingsley products</li>
<li>Proven problem-solving capabilities</li>
<li>Strong commitment to client service</li>
<li>Self-motivation/willingness to take initiative</li>
<li>Experience with Adobe Creative Cloud (Illustrator / Photoshop), Prezi, or comparable tools are a plus</li>
</ul>
