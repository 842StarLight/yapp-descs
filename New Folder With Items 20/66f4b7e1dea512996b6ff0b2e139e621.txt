<h2>Director of Financial Planning and Analysis</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>The Director of Financial Planning and Analysis provides leadership and support to both the University’s treasury-related operations as well as financial forecasting, annual budgeting process, operating/capital plan variance analysis and special projects, as assigned. They will provide financial consulting and strategic support for optimal decision making including preparing financial presentations, industry/peer group comparisons, new business opportunities, and other projects, as assigned. This position reports to the Associate Vice President of Finance.</p>
Duties and Responsibilities
<ul>
<li>Analyze current and past trends in key performance indicators including all areas of revenue, cost of sales, expenses and capital expenditures</li>
<li>Ad-Hoc reporting and analysis</li>
<li>Develop financial models and analyses to support strategic initiatives</li>
<li>Analyze complex financial information and reports to provide accurate and timely financial recommendations for decision making purposes</li>
<li>Supporting Departments heads with in-depth analysis</li>
<li>Serve as administrator of the University’s Planning Systems</li>
<li>Guide and manage the planning and annual budget process, as assigned.</li>
<li>Support treasury functions including: cash management and reporting/analysis, investment management/analysis and reporting, and debt compliance.</li>
<li>Provide oversight to daily banking transaction, ensuring they are performed timely and in adherence to internal controls.
</li>
<li>Work cross-functionally throughout the organization, ensuring integrated financial planning and analysis, awareness of issues, and promoting shared best practices.</li>
<li>Administer lease financing and reporting/analysis/compliance.</li>
<li>Coordinate claims and assist with risk management plans and initiatives</li>
<li>Participate in annual insurance policy renewal, ensuring adequate coverage and alignment to industry best practices</li>
<li>Identify, develop and facilitate processes and improvements in planning, analysis and reporting. This includes leveraging information technology to automate, streamline, and integrate processes.</li>
<li>Work with multiple systems, i.e., Dynamics, Management Reporter, Budget Maestro, CAMS, Sage, and Powerfaids for use in information retrieval and analyses.</li>
</ul>
Education and Experience
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s Degree in Accounting or Finance required.</li>
<li>MBA or related Advanced Degree or Certification (CPA, CMA, etc…) required</li>
<li>Minimum of 5 years’ experience in accounting or finance and financial/business planning and analysis required.</li>
<li>Previous experience in higher education a plus.</li>
<li>Nonprofit experience is desirable.</li>
</ul>
<p>
REQUIRED SKILLS AND ABILITIES:</p>
<ul>
<li>Knowledge in accounting and financial analysis</li>
<li>Knowledge of internal controls</li>
<li>Strong analytical ability</li>
<li>Demonstrated problem solving skills</li>
<li>Strong leadership and supervisory skills</li>
<li>Articulate with excellent verbal and written communication skills</li>
<li>Ability to think creatively, highly driven and self-motivated</li>
<li>Highest standards of accuracy and precision; highly organized</li>
<li>Ability to work independently and as part of a team</li>
<li>Proven ability to multi-task, plan and execute competing priorities in a complex organizational environment</li>
<li>Must have high integrity and ability to discreetly handle confidential information.</li>
<li>Advanced MS Office skills (i.e. Excel, Word, etc.)</li>
</ul>
