<h2>Finance Data Analyst</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div><div><div>
<div>
<div>Finance Data Analyst<br>
</div>
<div>San Francisco, CA</div>
<div></div>
<div>Role Summary:</div>
<div></div>
<div>Sendoso is the world’s first Sending Platform, located in San Francisco, California. Our fully integrated solution enables organizations to easily source, store, ship, and measure ROI for everything they ever need to send: direct mail, physical gifts, plants, perishables, company swag, handwritten notes, gift cards, and anything else. By marrying technology with logistics, we help businesses rise above the noise.</div>
<div></div>
<div>Sendoso is looking for a Finance Product Specialist to help with the enhancement of our fulfilment revenue stream processing and accounting. This Finance Product Specialist will be working closely with the Controller and Product Manager on reconciling and auditing the large volume of logistics transactional data at the sender, customer, and consolidated level. This role will also provide feedback for the product team to improve the user interface and back end design of the system. The ideal candidate has a experiencing creating or enhancing reconciliation processes for areas with large amounts of data, such as revenue or tax transactions. We are looking for someone with a strong foundation in excel, Sequel and/or other reconciliation systems, such as Blackline.</div>
<div></div>
<div>This Finance Product Specialists must have a willingness to dive into the details and gain a deep understanding of our Sendoso system. The Finance Product Specialists will play a vital role in the success of a fast-growing company and must possess a great attitude a passion for Sendoso.</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div></div>
<div>Responsibilities:</div>
<ul>
<li>Gain a deep understanding of the Sendoso system and the fulfilment revenue stream</li>
<li>Develop reconciliation processes to reconcile customer accounts</li>
<li>Implement automation tools within the finance organization to reconcile transactional level data</li>
<li>Provide feedback and report issues to the product team on a timely basis</li>
<li>Develop key performance metrics for measuring the improvements to customer accounts</li>
<li>Assist with finance issues raised related to customer accounts</li>
<li>Monitor daily reconciliation reports designed by engineers to identify potential issues</li>
<li>Investigate negative customer accounts</li>
<br>
</ul>
<div></div>
<div>
<br>
Skills and Experience</div>
<ul>
<li>2+ year’s experience</li>
<li>Accounting or finance background preferred</li>
<li>Startup experience preferred</li>
<li>Ability to thrive in fast-paced environment</li>
<li>Experience manipulating large amounts of data</li>
<li>Sequel experience preferred</li>
<li>Advanced Excel skills a must</li>
<li>We believe backgrounds in areas such as Data Science, Treasury, Sales Audit, Sales &amp; Use Tax, or similar areas would be useful</li>
<li>Detail oriented and able to effectively organize and manage multiple responsibilities.</li>
<li>Excellent analytical and communication skills (verbal and written).</li>
<li>Able to work both independently and collaboratively with a team.</li>
<li>Confidence, good judgment, energy and personality to work in a dynamic environment across all levels of management and across functions.</li>
</ul>
