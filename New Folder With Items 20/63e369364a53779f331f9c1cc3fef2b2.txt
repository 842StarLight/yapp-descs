<p><b>About The Position</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Together with our expertise and your commitment to excellence, we can assist our customers with the implementation of our flagship ECM product, <b>IntelliChief</b>, by:</li>
<li>Performing on-site and remote software installations and upgrades</li>
<li>Configuring and customizing <b>IntelliChief</b> to fit their needs</li>
<li>Providing detailed implementation documentation and roadmaps</li>
<li>Proactively training administrators and end users on <b>IntelliChief</b> deployment</li>
<li>Conducting validation tests of <b>IntelliChief</b> deployment against solution design</li>
<li>Conducting the right research, analyzing and resolving customer-reported issues during implementation and post-implementation while creating a trusting relationship with our customers.</li>
<li>Diagnosing, assisting, collaborating, coordinating and troubleshooting customer issues with ease and knowledge</li>
<li>Effectively communicate best practices usage of <b>IntelliChief</b> will set you apart from the rest</li>
<li>Abllty to work independently as well as collaboratively to achieve established company goals</li>
</ul>

<ul>
<li>PS Engineers are to have 7+ years of experience, preferably in an ECM or related field</li>
<li>Ideal candidates will possess experience having deployed ECM in Accounts Payable, Purchase-to-Pay or Order-to-Cash, working knowledge and experience implementing Capture/OCR and a strong understanding of best practices in Accounts Payable and Sales Order Processing</li>
<li>Qualified candidates must have a demonstrated commitment to high professional standards with integrity, credibility and a strong commitment to excellence and the company’s mission</li>
<li>A Bachelor’s degree in a technical discipline is required and an advanced degree or relevant certification is preferred</li>
<li>All candidates must have excellent oral and written communication skills with superior analytical/problem solving skills, showing a natural ability to logically solve complex problems in a structured manner</li>
<li>Technical skills and knowledge with Windows, TCP/IP networking and SQL scripting/query development moves candidates to the top of the list</li>
<li>Training provided to highly motivated self-starters with positive, can-do attitudes who possess a strong thirst for knowledge</li>
</ul>
