<h2>Inside Sales Manager (Team Manager)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>
<div>8x8, Inc. (NYSE:EGHT) communication solutions help businesses transform their customer and employee experience. With one system of engagement for cloud voice, video, collaboration and contact center and one system of intelligence on one cloud communications platform, businesses can now communicate faster and smarter to exceed the speed of customer expectations. For additional information, visit www.8x8.com, or follow 8x8 on LinkedIn, Twitter, and Facebook.</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
8x8 is growing its SMB sales organization and is seeking a highly respected, effective and motivated Sales Manager.<i> This role is a " people manager " role, experience managing or leading a team within the last 5 years is required.</i> <b>Please highlight this experience on your resume or in the question box below.</b>
</div>
<div>
</div>
<div>
The selected candidate will have a track record in building successful SMB business, on-boarding, enabling and making SMB team members productive leading to significant pipeline growth.
</div>
<div></div>
<div>Understanding and establishing SMB networks within the region is a must. Significant emphasis on growing our SMB business, recruiting desirable SMB Partners and developing business plans to ensure continued growth plans are met.
</div>
<div>Working within 8x8’s network of SMB resources to sell cloud communications to Small to Midsize customers forms part of the overall success plan.</div>
<div>
</div>
<div>
The role interacts closely and manages a team of Sales Account Managers and efforts must be coordinated.</div>
<div>
A key requirement is for proficiency in maximizing revenue growth within the Reseller and Service provider communicating. Experience within the communications (UC, Collaboration &amp; CC), Cloud and Software space are desirable attributes.</div>
<div>
</div>
<div>
The position requires a person who is used to meeting revenue quotas on a quarterly basis, and preferably has worked for a US based organization in the past.
</div>
</div>
<div><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities</b></h3>
<div><ul>
<li>
No Remote. Our office is located in downtown Minneapolis MN.</li>
<li>
Pipeline creation – business planning and campaigns</li>
<li>
Identifies industry events to utilize for recruiting efforts in region</li>
<li>
Develop business plans for sales and activities and reviewed quarterly</li>
<li>
Manages recruitment process of prospects</li>
<li>
Negotiates customer contracts</li>
<li>
Develops business plans with customers to ensure appropriate resources for successfully meeting sales goals.</li>
<li>
Coordinates introduction of appropriate 8x8 departments to new customers</li>
<li>
Coordinates certification process with new partners and insures timely completion</li>
<li>
Ensures necessary 8x8 resources are available and known to customers in region</li>
<li>
Monitors progress of new customers for a period no less than 12 months</li>
<li>
Evaluate customers performance in the region and provide assistance to customers that are under-performing
</li>
<li>Responsible for retention efforts across the sales floor</li>
<li>
Knowledge of Cloud, Call Centre, CRM, PABX or IVRS related applications</li>
<li>
Sound presentation skills
</li>
<li>Capable of representing the company at the most senior levels</li>
<li>
Excellent written and spoken English and local language</li>
</ul></div>
</div></div>
<div><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Requirements</b></h3>
<div><ul>
<li>
3 years SMB management or SMB development experience; previous experience managing a team is required.</li>
<li>
Knowledge of 8x8 product line advantageous, though not a requirement</li>
<li>
Highly organized</li>
<li>
Self-motivated
</li>
<li>Excellent presentation skills</li>
<li>
Excellent verbal and written communication skills
</li>
<li>Ability to coordinate efforts of multiple teams and team members to achieve common goal</li>
<li>
Quick learner</li>
<li>
Selling Unified Communications as a Service (UCaaS), SaaS and Cloud Contact Center Solutions is a plus</li>
<li>
Demonstrated ability to accurately manage a multi-channel pipeline and forecast in Salesforce.com</li>
<li>
Collaborative, solutions, consultative selling</li>
</ul></div>
