<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<ul>
<li>Coordinates all activities related to procurement of a commodity beginning with intent to purchase through delivery.</li>
<li>
Analyzes the requirements of the commodity, including preliminary specifications, preferred supplier, and date commodity is needed.</li>
<li>
Solicits and evaluates proposals for the requested commodity. Understands the different levels of complexity in various quote packages and can evaluate proposal responses accordingly.</li>
<li>
Investigates and/or interviews potential suppliers to determine if they meet the specified requirements.</li>
<li>
Evaluates the most appropriate supplier to be used for various purchased items and advises team members and/or management on the appropriate supplier to be used.</li>
<li>
Negotiates the lowest possible cost for the commodity balanced against the optimum quality and schedule needs. Cost control and reduction is a key metric of this role.</li>
<li>
Implements purchase contracts (ie Purchase orders, etc) that comply with company and government regulations.</li>
<li>
Assures that the commodity is delivered on schedule, at the negotiated price, and meets the quality standards of the company. Drives On Time delivery above 95% overall from all key suppliers.</li>
<li>
Maintains timely control of orders, amendments, shipping notices, and other documents to assure accurate retention of records and compliance with all department procedures and goals.</li>
</ul>
