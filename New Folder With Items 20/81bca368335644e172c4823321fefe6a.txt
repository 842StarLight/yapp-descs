JOB DESCRIPTION
<br>---------------
<br><br>
Vlocity is looking for a Collections Specialist who will be responsible for accounts receivable collections including contacting customers for collections, tracking and resolving billing issues, reporting on receivables performance, ensure cash applications are performed timely, and perform credit analysis and analysis of results. The candidate must have demonstrated ability to follow a collections process, and adhere to internal controls. The candidate will exhibit the highest professional standards and ethical principles. Other valued attributes that we are seeking are a strong work ethic, capacity to get things done independently, and consistent performance with a sense of urgency and commitment.
<br><br>
RESPONSIBILITIES
<br>----------------
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Perform accounts receivable collections activity.</li>
<li>Drive to closure all past due items while achieving quarterly departmental and company DSO, Cash Collections, Aging Over 60, Unapplied Cash and audit goals.</li>
<li>Contact customers to obtain payment commitment, or resolve issues preventing payment, and take appropriate action.</li>
<li>Partner with order management, revenue accounting, credit, sales and operations teams to research and resolve customer inquiries and/or disputes.</li>
<li>Maintain record of collection and dispute activity or call history log.</li>
<li>Manage adherence to the company collections escalations process and participate, as appropriate.</li>
<li>Manage assessment of past due receivables for bad debt reserve. Prepare quarterly bad debt reserve analysis and account reconciliations of the reserve account.</li>
<li>Manage weekly, monthly and quarter-end management reporting for receivables aging and key metrics.</li>
<li>Continually assess unapplied cash to ensure timely posting within company guidelines and SLA.</li>
<li>Manage and support business needs aligned to internal and external audit.</li>
<li>Assess customer creditworthiness by performing credit evaluations using Dun &amp; Bradstreet reports, bank &amp; trade references, purchase history, and financial statement analysis.</li>
<li>Establish credit limits in accordance with Company Credit Policy.</li>
<li>Document credit review to ensure compliance with Credit Policy, internal and external audit requirements, and SOX compliance.</li>
<li>Prepare credit file packages for audit purposes.</li>
<li>Contribute to efforts to enhance policies and procedures that streamline, credit, and revenue operations processes.</li>
<li>Assist with the audit requests and reviews as needed.</li>
<li>Perform other special projects and analyses as directed by management.</li>
</ul>
QUALIFICATIONS
<br>--------------
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>BA/BS degree or equivalent combination of education and relevant work experience.</li>
<li>Minimum of 5 years of enterprise collections experience.</li>
<li>Unwavering integrity.</li>
<li>Basic accounting skills to perform analysis and reconciliation of bad debt reserve accounts.</li>
<li>Proficiency with Netsuite and Microsoft Office products (Excel, Word and PowerPoint)</li>
<li>Must have the ability to execute multiple tasks and adhere to strict deadlines in a fast-paced, changing environment.</li>
<li>Strong communication and interpersonal skills, including the ability to interact effectively with customers and company personnel.</li>
<li>Thorough understanding of the invoice to collect process</li>
</ul>
