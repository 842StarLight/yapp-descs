<h2>Customer Service Administrator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Does the idea of working in a fast-paced environment invigorate you? Do others say you have the ability to remain calm and calm others in the midst of stressful situations? Do you enjoy a collaborative team environment?</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
We are looking for a fast-learner who is eager to serve others and finds joy in diagnosing and conquering challenges that arise in a phone-centric customer service setting. Our Customer Service Administrators are the public’s first point of contact, serving as the front-line heroes of the company. They are responsible for quickly discerning a customer’s needs and directing calls and information accordingly. Success in this role requires a heart of service and the ability to endure the day-to-day challenges inherent in serving customers with a wide-range of expectations.</p>
<p>
Basic Function: The Customer Service Administrator is ABI’s front line team member who answers and directs incoming calls, in addition to completing administrative duties.</p>
<p>
Primary Activities &amp; Responsibilities:</p>
<ul>
<li>
<p>Answer &amp; Route Phone Calls:</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Professionally and enthusiastically answer phone calls and route them appropriately throughout the business, serving as an integral intermediary between the public and the ABI team.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Answer multiple incoming calls almost simultaneously</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Create cases for Customer Support Team</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Utilize ABI’s CRM (business software) to discern caller’s context and document all pertinent activity.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Identify and properly escalate priority issues while seeking to reassure and emotionally deescalate customers.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Act as a gatekeeper, providing a kind and professional filter to streamline or divert calls in a manner consistent with ABI brand story and core values.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Emotionally engage, empathize with, and serve callers, assisting with their needs even if not ABI related.</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p>Troubleshoot Low-Level Customer Support Issues:</p>
<ul>
<li>
<p>Collect payment and finalize outstanding customer quotes.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Advise customers on basic troubleshooting concerns.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Troubleshoot Customer Online Account issues</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p>Additional Activities &amp; Responsibilities:</p>
<ul>
<li>
<p>Create &amp; Maintain Customer Records in Database:</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Create new and update existing customer records with accurate contact and request follow-up details, in accordance with ABI’s rotation assignment policy. This “lead entry” function serves as a backup role to the primary lead entry staff member, but also enters marketing lists data as it becomes available.</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p>Confirm Sales Orders From Vendors:</p>
<ul>
<li>
<p>Maintain vigilant communication with vendors to ensure accuracy and timely processing of Sales Orders.</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p>Manage Office &amp; Facility Supplies:</p>
<ul>
<li>
<p>Receive and document staff requests for supplies required.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Monitor stock office supplies inventory and communicate needs with purchasing agent.</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p>Manage Company Mailings</p>
<ul>
<li>
<p>Process Catalogue Requests</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Ensure catalogues and mailings are properly labeled, posted, and routed correctly.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Manage company postage machine and supplies.</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p>Projects and other duties as assigned</p>
<br>
<p>
</p>
<p>Ideal Candidate:</p>
<ul>
<li>
<p>Experience in an administrative, receptionist, or call center role.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Emotionally mature, maintaining a positive demeanor in the midst of high stress situations.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Situationally intelligent, knowing how and when to bounce between work and play</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Ability to prioritize and juggle multiple tasks throughout a shift</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Possess and live out a passion for serving and caring for others</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p>Basic Requirements:</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>
<p>Must have the desire to serve others</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Must have unwavering integrity and positivity</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Must have the ability to stand, sit, and see a screen eight hours of the work day</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Must be self-motivated and able to work alone and in collaboration.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Must have excellent organizational and communication skills.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Must be fast-paced thinker and typist with the ability to simultaneously juggle several calls between internal team members.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Must be willing to participate in ongoing education</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Must be proficient utilizing computers, mobile devices, and core office applications.</p>
</li>
<li><p>Must be agreeable to and able to pass random drug testing.</p></li>
</ul>
