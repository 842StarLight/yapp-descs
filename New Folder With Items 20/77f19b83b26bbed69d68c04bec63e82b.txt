<h2>Oracle Business Consultant for Digitized Business Processes</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Your way of thinking is process- and solution-oriented? You are communicative, devoted, and a socially competent team player, but an equally strong lone fighter? You work proactively, possess analytical skills, rapid comprehension and a high quality standard? You like to accept technical responsibility and are able to visualize complex issues? <b>In this case, we’re looking forward to have you in our team</b>.</p>
<p>Together with our customers, PROMATIS-consultants design intelligent business processes and indicator systems. <b>Our special feature: </b>Our consultants consistently count on easily comprehensible graphical business process models and provide already predefined best practice solutions. With the products of global market leader Oracle® and in close collaboration with our customers, we realize <b>innovative digitization and AI solutions</b>.</p>
<h4 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Your remit in our customer projects</b></h4>
<ul>
<li>Implementation of workshops for business process analysis and conception of digitization solutions</li>
<li>Planning, implementation and monitoring of organizational change management measures</li>
<li>Design and implementation of business requirements by configuration and adaptation of Oracle enterprise software (Oracle Cloud Applications, NetSuite ERP, Oracle E-Business Suite)</li>
<li>Development of solution concepts and technical specification of customer-specific adaptations (customization)</li>
<li>Execution of user training courses and user coaching as well as supporting tasks during prototyping and test phases</li>
<li>Support of the customer during the commissioning of the system</li>
</ul>
<h4 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Your profile</b></h4>
<ul>
<li>Studies in industrial engineering, business informatics, information management, business administration with IT affinity, computer science with business administration affinity or lateral entrants with practical business experience</li>
<li>Knowledge of business administration in at least one of the following areas:<br>
Finance and Accounting, Enterprise Performance Management, SCM/Logistics, Production, Customer Experience (Sales, Marketing, Service), Human Resources Management (HCM)</li>
<li>Experience in the practical use of business application software is an advantage</li>
<li>You have a quick grasp, work independently, are solution and customer oriented and are a responsible team player</li>
<li>You have strong communication skills, abstraction and structuring skills as well as a good command of German and English</li>
<li>Mobility in project work at home and abroad</li>
<li>Your goal is the responsible use of modern digital technologies and processes to create sustainable, humane user experiences.</li>
</ul>
