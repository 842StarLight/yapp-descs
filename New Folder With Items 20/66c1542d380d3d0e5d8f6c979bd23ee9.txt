<h2>Leasing Consultant</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>The <b>Leasing Consultant </b>is the property’s sales representative whose primary duties are to greet prospects, to present professionally the features and benefits of their assigned community and properly secure lease agreement from qualified persons.</p>
<p>
A <b>Leasing Consultant </b>is very service oriented and strives to make current residents feel welcome and comfortable in their community.</p>
<p><b><i>
Responsibilities:</i></b></p>
<p>
Conducts all business in accordance with company policies and procedures, Fair Housing, Americans with Disabilities Act, Fair Credit Reporting Act, and all other laws pertaining to apartments.</p>
<p><i>
Marketing and Leasing</i></p>
<ul>
<li> Maintains a professional, yet friendly, atmosphere in the leasing office and other areas where prospective residents meet.</li>
<li> Inspects models and “market ready” vacancies daily to ensure cleanliness.</li>
<li> Answers incoming phone calls and handles each call accordingly, whether it is a prospect call, irate resident, service request, etc. Transfers calls to assistant manager or manager when appropriate.</li>
<li> Greets prospective residents, qualifies, determines needs and preferences, professionally presents community and specific apartments while communicating features and benefits.</li>
<li> Maintains awareness of local market conditions and trends. Contributes ideas to the manager for marketing property and for improving resident satisfaction.</li>
<li> Monitor advertising effectiveness Gather information about the market competition in the area.
</li>
<li> Participate in outreach marketing activities on a regular basis to obtain prospective residents.
</li>
<li> Advise residents of referral concessions (if permitted).
</li>
<li> Conduct market surveys and shop competitive communities.<br>
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><i>Administrative</i></p>
<ul>
<li> Correctly completes all lease applications, assists with application verification and notifies prospective residents of results. Types miscellaneous resident communication as needed.</li>
<li> Completes all lease paperwork including related addenda and accepts rents and deposits.</li>
<li> Completes Guest Card information form on ALL prospects, sends thank-you notes and performs follow-up.</li>
<li> Maintain current resident files.
</li>
<li> Physically inspects property when on grounds, picks up litter and reports any service needs to maintenance staff.</li>
<li> Inspects move-outs and vacancies.</li>
<li> Inventories office supplies on periodic basis. Reports needed items to manager.</li>
<li> Organizes and files appropriate reports, leases and paperwork.</li>
<li> Attends company meetings when requested.</li>
<li> Assists manager and assistant manager in preparation of daily and weekly reports, resident communications, move-out inventory, market surveys, etc.</li>
<li> Assist in monitoring renewals. Distribute and follow-up on renewal notices.
</li>
<li> Update availability report, process applications for approvals (i.e. credit check, rental history, etc). Submit processed applications to the Property Manger for approval. Follow up with applicant regarding status.
</li>
<li> Distribute all company or community-issued notices.<br>
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><i>General</i></p>
<ul>
<li> Performs any additional duties assigned by manager or assistant manager.</li>
<li> Welcomes guests and handles incoming phone calls from prospective new residents and completes appropriate paperwork.</li>
<li> Maintains awareness of market/industry conditions and trends via trade publications, professional organizations, etc.</li>
<li> Ensure apartment is ready for resident to move-in on agreed date.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b><i>
Qualifications</i></b></p>
<ul>
<li> Position requires at least 1 years of experience in <i>on-site </i>multi-family property management.</li>
<li> Type at least 35 wpm.</li>
<li> Knowledge of Microsoft Office Suite, Gmail, OneSite, Real Page, and Velocity rental systems.</li>
<li> Two years’ experience in previous relevant customer service field.<br>
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b><i>Skills and Abilities Required</i></b></p>
<ul>
<li> Ability to effectively manage competing demands on time and attention.</li>
<li> Strong problem-solving skills.</li>
<li> Exhibits honesty and integrity in business dealings and decision making.</li>
<li> Strong work ethic.</li>
<li> Possesses perseverance to see the job through completion.</li>
<li> Well-developed interpersonal skills.</li>
<li> Takes instructions from supervisors.</li>
<li> Interacts with co-workers, supervisors, guests and the public in a professional and pleasant manner.</li>
<li> Able to apply principles of logical thinking to define problems and initiate valid responses.</li>
<li> Ability to make effective presentations to prospective tenants.</li>
<li> Ability to travel and, if necessary, attend out of town meetings or conferences which require overnight travel.</li>
<li> Ability to work a flexible schedule, including evenings and weekends.</li>
<li> Neat, clean, professional at all times throughout the workday and/or whenever present at the community.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b><i>
Education and Training</i></b></p>
<ul>
<li> High School diploma or equivalent required.</li>
<li> Ability to read, write, and speak English.</li>
<li> Must have valid driver’s license and automobile insurance.</li>
<li> National Apartment Leasing Professional (NALP) preferred, not required.</li>
<li> Fair Housing Certification, willingness to obtain prior to interacting with prospective residents.<br>
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b><i>Physical Requirements</i></b></p>
<p>
Ability to work a minimum of 40 hours per week. Must be available evenings, nights, weekends for staffing needs and emergencies.</p>
<ul>
<li> Be able to stand and walk or sit alternatively depending on specific needs of the day. Estimate 60% of time is spent on feet and 40% sitting at a desk.</li>
<li> Frequent need to see small detail and view computer screens.</li>
<li> Ability to work in a fast-paced environment, meet deadlines and multi-task.</li>
<li> Have occasional need to perform the following physical activities:</li>
<li> Bend/Stoop/Squat</li>
<li> Climb Stairs</li>
</ul>
<p>
</p>
<ul><li> Reach above shoulder</li></ul>
