<h2>Marketing Content Writer and Editor</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Job Summary:</b></p>
<p>
The Marketing Content Writer and Editor is a member of the Strategic Engagement team, reporting to the Director, Communications and Media Relations. The position will serve as a writing resource for colleagues throughout the organization up to and including the president and chief executive officer. The incumbent will possess the ability to understand and give voice to priorities and initiatives within both the AccessLex Center for Education and Financial Capability and the AccessLex Center for Legal Education Excellence.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Responsibilities and Duties:</b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Write, proofread and edit all materials, publications, emails, social content, ad copy, etc. as needed;</li>
<li>Write, proofread and edit all press releases, blog posts, articles, CEO communications as needed;</li>
<li>Write, proofread and edit all research reports, policy position papers, academic and bar success newsletters and other materials as needed;</li>
<li>Produce engaging email copy to be sent to schools, students and other invested stakeholders;</li>
<li>Create and implement communication plans to increase market visibility;</li>
<li>Contribute to the creation of advertising materials, including relationship building emails, meetings and events campaigns and other sales and marketing resources</li>
<li>Make recommendations for improvements, edits, changes, updates to design and copy where appropriate, and oversee implementation of approved recommendations;</li>
<li>Ensure consistency and brand protection in all marketing and communications material, both online and in print;</li>
<li>Develop and refine marketing goals, promotional activities and branding in consultation with the Strategic Engagement team;</li>
<li>Work closely with the Director, Marketing Campaigns and Promotions to coordinate the design and distribution of promotional material through online and offline channels;</li>
<li>Play an integral role on the annual Conference core team; materials development, copywriting (Conference packet, collateral/direct mail, presentations, correspondence, etc.) and on-site content development such as talking points, speeches, etc.;</li>
<li>Campaign and project management as necessary; and</li>
<li>Other duties as assigned.</li>
<br>
</ul>
