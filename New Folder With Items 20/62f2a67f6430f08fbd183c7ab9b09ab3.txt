<h2>Senior Product Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>At White Ops, we’re all hackers. This doesn’t mean you have to hold a key to the internet (although one of our founders does!), but it does require approaching problems in unconventional ways. We are attacking criminal botnets through the ad fraud that finances their operations. Our proprietary technology detects and prevents sophisticated online fraud. By reducing the incentives for crime, we are making the internet a better place. Our mighty team of humans have several things in common:</p>
<p>
We are hackers.<br>
We create tau for ourselves and each other.<br>
We think big and move fast.<br>
We practice humility.<br>
We “pwn it”.</p>
<p><b>
What we do:</b></p>
<p>
We keep the internet safe by fighting organized cybercrime. Our products accurately detect and prevent even the most sophisticated bots and our customers include some of the web's largest and most forward-thinking companies.
</p>
<p>Our team of Product professionals are entrepreneurial, curious, and driven. We are passionate about our mission of keeping the internet safe by fighting organized crime.
</p>
<p>We support and challenge one another and regularly hold team learnings, product reviews and progress updates to fly faster. We strive to develop a highly collaborative team that works well with all the other teams to meet our clients’ needs.</p>
<p><b>
What you'll do:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>White Ops is the leader in global bot detection and mitigation. You will work with our growing product management team to continue building best in class fraud detection and mitigation products that protect the integrity of the Internet.</li>
<li>Build new products, from conception to launch. Build business case, define requirements, work closely with engineering to execute the roadmap, work closely with sales and marketing to define and execute the go to market plan.</li>
<li>Be the expert on your products or features and the key go-to person for sales.</li>
<li>Work closely with our R&amp;D team to create a process to turn well defined requirements and priorities into features that delight our customers.</li>
<li>Build a kick-ass user experience for our customers</li>
<li>Regularly meet with customers and industry influencers to evangelize our products and gather input on our strategy and roadmap.</li>
<li>Inspect and adapt to continually improve execution</li>
<li>Drive key industry partnerships and lead initiatives collaboratively that bring fundamental changes to protect the internet</li>
<li>Do whatever is necessary; general manager / co-founder / owner mentality</li>
</ul> <p><b>Who you are:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Obsessively passionate about the product. Dream about it. Be able to clearly articulate what a winning product looks like.</li>
<li>4+ years building successful SaaS products in a product management role</li>
<li>Passion for the White Ops mission of verifying the humanity of every online transaction.</li>
<li>Self-motivated and ability to motivate others. Lead through influence.</li>
<li>Take a customer focused approach to building products. Passion for great user experience, from demo to implementation and ongoing customer success.</li>
<li>Ability to build trust and empathy with internal stakeholders (engineering, sales, customer success) and with customers and industry influencers.</li>
<li>High competence with Agile product management and processes</li>
<li>Excellent communicator</li>
<li>Highly collaborative</li>
<li>Entrepreneurial drive and demonstrated ability to achieve stretch goals in an innovative and fast-paced environment</li>
<li>Strong analytical ability to heavily utilize data insights in decision making. Metrics driven.</li>
</ul>

<p>
