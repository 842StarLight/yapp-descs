<h2>Senior Manager / Director of Engineering</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">As an engineering leader, you will be responsible for managing one or more scrum teams to focus on key feature developments in our core SaaS product.
<br><br>
You'll work closely with product management, design, and executive team to develop features that align with the product roadmap, and the overall vision of the company.
<br><br>
<b>Responsibilities:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Build and manage a highly efficient and motivated team of engineers</li>
<li>Work with Product Management and Design team to develop engineering solution that best satisfies customer needs</li>
<li>Develop efficient engineering delivery process and manage the delivery</li>
<li>Prepare and present recommendations, reports, and findings from experimental data all the way up to the executive level.</li>
<li>Provide technical guidance and mentorship to other engineers.</li>
</ul>
Skills and Qualifications
<br><br>
<ul>

<li>Experience in web application performance tuning</li>
<li>Experience managing a senior team fo engineers</li>
<li>Experience maintaining large and growing code base in a fast-moving environment is a plus.</li>
<li>Interest in staying current with new and evolving cloud technologies.</li>
<li>5+ years engineering management experience</li>
<li>8+ years software engineering experience</li>
</ul>
