<h2>Donor Recruiter</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>LifeSouth Community Blood Centers is currently seeking an individual to join our team as a <b>Donor Recruiter</b> in <b>McDonough, GA</b>. This position is responsible for planning and scheduling blood drives including publicity, donor recognition and education.<br>
<br>
<b>Responsibilities include, but are not limited to:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Manage assigned blood drive accounts</li>
<li>Work to establish new accounts as directed by management</li>
<li>Achieve monthly, quarterly and annual collection goals</li>
<li>Schedule blood drives and determine accurate projections for each drive</li>
<li>Recruit on-site as necessary by actively encouraging donations from potential donors in order to meet daily projections</li>
<li>Prepare and deliver promotional materials while coordinating with Corporate Marketing &amp; Recruitment staff as needed</li>
<li>Develop rapport with blood drive chairperson of assigned donor group</li>
<li>Effectively coordinate blood drives and handle concerns as needed</li>
</ul>
<br>
<b>
Minimum Qualifications:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>High school diploma or GED</li>
<li>Must be able to work a varied schedule including nights, weekends and holidays</li>
<li>Valid driver's license. Must also meet and maintain LifeSouth driver's eligibility requirements</li>
<li>Must have consistent and reliable transportation</li>
<li>Exceptional communication skills</li>
<li>Strong interpersonal and team building skills</li>
<li>Positive and outgoing attitude</li>
<li>Innovative problem solving ability</li>
<li>Ability to follow moderately complex oral and written instructions</li>
<li>Proficient in a variety of computer software applications</li>
</ul>
<br>
<b>
The ideal candidate will possess:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Associate's or Bachelor's degree</li>
<li>Two years of sales or marketing experience</li>
</ul>
<br>
This is a full time position. Starting salary range is $17.50 - $19.25 an hour. Background check and drug test required. Equal Opportunity/Affirmative Action Employer/DFWP/Tobacco Free.<br>
