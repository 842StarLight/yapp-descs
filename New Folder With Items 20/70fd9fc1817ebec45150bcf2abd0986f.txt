<p></p>
<p>
Position Summary</p>
<p>
The Trustee Team Lead plays an important role in the organization by performing a variety of activities related to the company’s foreclosure functions. The role is responsible for ensuring foreclosure checklists and processes are up-to-date with regulatory and compliance updates. The team lead will ensure all processes are carefully followed and monitored. This role serves as a lead support for the Trustee Administration team.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
Essential Functions</p>
<ul>
<li>Update Policies and Procedures to comply with Regulatory and Compliance updates.</li>
<li>Work with Compliance to ensure all letter/notice templates are compliant with current regulations.</li>
<li>Provide End of Month statistics based on active portfolio and timelines.</li>
<li>Oversight of trustee team members (Up to 5)</li>
<li>Receive and prepare foreclosure referrals, including, calculating fees and expenses and preparing documents to initiate foreclosure action.</li>
<li>Prepare out of State/Attorney foreclosure documents using Microsoft Word templates and keeping templates up-to-date with updates provided by Foreclosure Attorney per state.</li>
<li>Receive 10/30 day letters from Title Companies and prepare mailings(using the Pitney Bowes mailing system) for new foreclosure loans.</li>
<li>Prepare Publication orders, schedule sale dates, prepare Notice of Sale documents, addendums, obtain legal descriptions and plat maps from blitz, prepare order forms to sale company, and prepare any additional mailings (using Pitney Bowes mailing system).</li>
<li>Process sale Postponement request and send required postponement mailings.</li>
<li>Prepare and mail cancellation documents to Title Company.</li>
<li>Prepare certified mailings for documents that require proof of delivery.</li>
<li>Approve vendor invoices and checks.</li>
<li>Assist with preparation of trustee invoices.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p>
</p>
<p>Qualifications</p>
<ul>
<li>College degree preferred, along with one year mortgage Loan Servicing and/or Trustee work experience required</li>
<li>General knowledge of overall mortgage lending processes, documents, and terminology.</li>
<li>Prior work experience as Foreclosure Trustee and/or Trustee Assistant with knowledge of Trustee processes, documents and terminology.</li>
<li>Prior work experience in consumer relations/customer service positions requiring excellent verbal and written communication skills is preferred.</li>
<li>Highly organized and detail oriented; ability to work in a fast-paced, deadline-driven environment required.</li>
<li>Basic math skills required.</li>
<li>Proficiency with data entry, Microsoft Word, Excel, Google Sheets and Outlook required.</li>
<li>Familiar with basic internet searches.</li>
<li>10-key by touch strongly preferred.</li>
<li>Familiarity with document retention software; Blitzdoc preferred.</li>

</ul>
