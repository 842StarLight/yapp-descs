<h2>Graphic Designer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Duties and Responsibilities </b>include the following. Other duties may be assigned.</p>
<ul>
<li>Create concepts to illustrate vision for design including selecting colors, artwork, photography, animation, style of type and other visual elements</li>
<li>Works cross functionally with various team members across different department</li>
<li>Able to create high-caliber, innovative visual communication materials</li>
<li>Develop overall layout and production design of catalogs, posters, brochures, icons, sales materials, websites, banner ads, animation, infographics, advertising (print and digital), sales collateral, trade show materials, e-newsletters, emails, web pages/portals, interactive media and multimedia projects</li>
<li>Aptitude to conceptualize and create compelling and progressive brand stories through imagery and typography</li>
<li>Ensure all creative work adheres to the corporate standards and builds the brand</li>
<li>Brings innovative design concepts to the Adam brand portfolio</li>
<li>Follows brand guidelines for each brand and ensures consistency of visual voice Able to take direction from written or spoken ideas and convert them seamlessly into designs</li>
<li>Experience with user-centered design principles, typography, and color, including digital design best practices</li>
<li>Ability to successfully organize, prioritize and manage multiple projects</li>
<li>Excels in collaborative work and thinking with the ability to absorb and apply design critique from others</li>
<li>Comfortable working with a small team and willing to take on new challenges/opportunities as they arise</li>
<li>Maintains schedules to ensure deadlines are met</li>
<li>Collaborates with team on developing campaign concepts</li>
<li>Follows existing processes and systems for file management and archiving</li>
</ul>

<ul>
<li>Bachelor's degree in Graphic Design</li>
<li>1+ years of relevant experience with a creative agency or an in-house creative department</li>
<li>Expert user of design tools including Photoshop, Illustrator, InDesign and other Adobe products</li>
<li>Understanding of print production</li>
<li>Strong visual literacy and understanding of typography, illustration, color theory, wire framing and photo manipulation</li>
<li>Familiar with JavaScript, Flash, iMovie, Final Cut Express, Director, Premiere, Live Motion a plus</li>
<li>Knowledge of HTML, CSS and Microsoft Office products (PowerPoint &amp; Word)</li>
<li>Excellent communication skills, both written and verbal</li>
<li>A problem-solving mentality, and can unlock ways to express complex ideas through intuitive design</li>
</ul>
