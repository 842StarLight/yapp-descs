<h2>Sales Agent</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>We are currently looking for a Sales Agent who would be responsible for showing and selling mobile homes to prospective residents as well as greeting visitors/potential residents, providing swift and courteous customer service for current residents. The ideal candidate will be excellent with customer service and in communication, have strong organizational and follow-up skills, and strong attention to details. The ideal candidate must be able to adapt to changing schedules. Evenings and weekends may be required for showings and based on the company’s needs. Must be willing and able to use personal vehicle to visit homes for showings and for marketing needs.</p>
<p><b>Responsibilities Include but are not limited to;</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Contact and follow up with all leads</li>
<li>Manages all sales activities within the community</li>
<li>Attend mandatory sales calls and meetings</li>
<li>Maintain regular contact with leads and referrals</li>
<li>Marketing/Advertising of units – this may require travel to different locations within close proximity to the property – Manhattan and Junction City</li>
<li>Showing of units to prospects</li>
<li>Pre-screen prospects based on approval criteria</li>
<li>Complete and submit required sales paperwork for financing</li>
<li>Assist prospect through application process</li>
<li>Ensuring files are complete based on company procedure</li>
<li>Ensuring company sales goals are met for assigned communities</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Requirements: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Prior leasing/sales experience</li>
<li>Strong customer service skills</li>
<li>Strong communication skills</li>
<li>Strong closing skills</li>
<li>Ability to work evenings or weekends, based on company’s needs</li>
<li>Vehicle for marketing purposes</li>
<li>Bilingual preferred</li>
</ul>
<p>Job Type: Full-time or Part-time</p>
<p>Experience:</p>
<ul>
<li>Home or auto sales: 2 years (Preferred) – but, willing to train</li>
<li>Customer Service: 2 years</li>
</ul>
<p>License:</p>
<ul><li>Driver's License (Required)</li></ul>
<p>Job Types: Full-time, Part-time</p>
