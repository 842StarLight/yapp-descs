<p><b>
Position Overview</b></p>
<p>
The Diagnostics Sales Specialist will drive value expansion goals and economic profitability by conducting thorough analyses, customer business reviews, and identifying improvement opportunities within customer segments. The Diagnostics Sales Specialist drives to support the Sales Ambassadors to improve economic profitability &amp; increase revenue.</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Position Location</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
This is a remote-based position covering the following states: Texas, New Mexico, and Arizona.</li>
<li>
Qualified candidates must currently reside in, or near, one of the following cities: Houston, TX or San Antonio, TX or Austin, TX or Phoenix, AZ.</li>
<li>
Relocation assistance is not authorized for this position.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Position Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Analyze, develop and achieve value expansion goals through creation of account-specific plans.</li>
<li>
Review contract compliance (volume, pricing, utilization, efficiency, Free of Charge).</li>
<li>
Review and assure Abbott adherence to customer KPIs, and identify improvement opportunities.</li>
<li>
Prepare and participate in customer business reviews.</li>
<li>
Support and drive Ambassadors in commercial sales and value expansion.</li>
<li>
Support Ambassadors in sales process for renewals.</li>
<li>
Provide strategic pricing support.</li>
<li>
Analyze and optimize instrument utilization.</li>
<li>
Manage lifecycle of instruments and assays.</li>
<li>
Cost Per Reportable management.</li>
<li>
Collaborate with Ambassador and other matrix functions to ensure successful achievement of goals.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Key Position Deliverables</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
The Diagnostics Sales Specialist interacts with many levels within the Customer (Lab Managers, Clinicians, Procurement and Finance, potentially C-Suite).</li>
<li>
Key internal stakeholder interactions include: Customer Experience District Manager, Abbott Ambassadors, Marketing, Sales Specialist team, Hematology, Informatics, Lab Solution Design), Enterprise Accounts team, Finance, Contracts &amp; Pricing, Contract Managers</li>
<li>
Key performance metrics: District sales, margin, and plan; Positive Economic Profitability momentum ; Growth rate achievement ; Customer contract compliance, and Customer Net Promoter Score growth.</li>
</ul>
