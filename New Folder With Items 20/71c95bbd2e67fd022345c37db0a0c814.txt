<h2>Retail Part time Assistant Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>Job Description<br>
<br>
<p>Assist the store management in leading assigned location to achieve COMP or New Store Sales through consistent operational excellence, discipline, and a committed team who execute flawlessly. Is obsessive about our product, people and our customers. The role of a 2nd Assistant Manager is to assist the Store Manager in fulfilling and executing the company mission statement on a daily basis.</p>
</div>
<br>
<div>Qualifications<br>
<br>
<p>SALES</p>
<ul>
<li>Expected to assist the store manager in meeting or exceeding assigned sales plans and target metric objectives within the assigned store.</li>
<li>Maintains a commitment to the company’s sales processes, values and business code of ethics.</li>
<li>Ability to achieve results when assigned a MOD (Manager-on-Duty) segment. Work with employees to ensure goals are achieved.</li>
<li>Responsible for properly and accurately receiving merchandise, totaling bills, accepting payment, and making change for customers in our retail stores. Teaches others to do the same.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
PRODUCT</p>
<ul>
<li>Ensures consistent execution of company’s marketing and visual presentation.</li>
<li>Sets up advertising displays or arranges merchandise on counters or tables to promote sales.</li>
<li>Stamps, marks, or tags price on merchandise. Teaches others current directives.</li>
<li>Stocks shelves, counters, or tables with merchandise. Sets up displays according to company directives. Teaches others to do the same.</li>
<li>Ability to create excitement and show passion for the product by communicating relevant information about fitness and fashion to customers. Able to answers questions about product features and benefits. Teaches others to do the same.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
PEOPLE</p>
<ul>
<li>Train staff on how to drive sales to fullest potential through continued development on product knowledge, customer service standards and visual presentation.</li>
<li>Assists store manager by recruiting top talent to support sales and growth of company.</li>
<li>Partners with store manager to hire, develop and retain top talent, as well as, address employee relations issues when directed to by senior management.</li>
<li>Ensures all HR paperwork and training are current and kept in folders for associates per retention guidelines.</li>
<li>Ability to complete new hire and other human resources paperwork timely and accurately.</li>
<li>Ability to identify performance challenges and report concerns to senior management to correct issues.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
OPERATIONS</p>
<ul>
<li>Demonstrates the ability to consistently follow and also ensure others follow The Promise and all company customer service standards.</li>
<li>Ensures compliance to policies and procedures at all times. Reports concerns to senior management.</li>
<li>Confer with management to ensure that banking deposits are made daily; responsible for delivering to the bank, at a specified location, with or without armored car service, as directed by the store manager.</li>
<li>Ensures that the store is secured at closing (i.e. doors locked, safe locked, alarms are set).</li>
<li>Ability to remove and record cash in register at end of shift/closing and prepare bank deposits properly.</li>
<li>Works with store manager to develop solutions to decrease losses and shrink.</li>
<li>Maintains proper inventory controls, facilitate inventory transaction and bulk counts as directed by senior management .</li>
<li>Ensures price integrity and general pricing of product on the floors is reflective of current markdowns and markups per company directives.</li>
<li>Ensures all sales are rung correctly and that all associates are trained on how to ring a transaction.</li>
<li>Delegates tasks to other hourly employees to ensure the store is properly recovered for the next day’s business.</li>
<li>Willing and able to clean shelves, counters, tables and overall store. Teaches standards to others.</li>
<li>Ensures inventory is checked in, stocked correctly on shelves and displayed within 24 hours of receipt per operational standards.</li>
<li>Ensures Elite goals are met for the store.</li>
<li>Keeps daily record of store sales, scheduling, damages, mismates, deposit log, employee discounts, new hire paperwork, paid outs and visitor log.</li>
<li>Responsible for carrying out area of responsibilities (AOR) tasks, assignments and execution under the supervision of the Store Manager.</li>
<li>All employees are required to use ladders as an essential function of this job, as that is part of the daily duties to maintain the store’s daily operations and in order to service customers. Therefore, dress code and safety policies must be followed. This is also outlined under the PHYSICAL DEMANDS section of the job description.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
LEADERSHIP</p>
<ul>
<li>Demonstrates the Skechers Key Behaviors for each Core Competency: Results Driven, Customer and Product Focus, People, Visual/Marketing Excellence, Strategic Thought, Setting Direction, Planning, Communication, Leadership Courage, Innovation, Adaptability, Creates Skechers environment, Skechers Savvy, and Ethics and Integrity.</li>
<li>Is a role model by presenting a professional image in appearance, words and action.</li>
<li>Demonstrates excellent personal salesmanship and teach employees selling skills.</li>
<li>Assumes responsibility and displays the ability to take charge (in absence of senior management).</li>
<li>Follows the store manager's direction to ensure associates contribute to the company objectives.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
Additional Information<br>
<br>
<p>All your information will be kept confidential according to EEO guidelines.</p>
