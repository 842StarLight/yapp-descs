<p><b>
About the team</b></p>
<p>
The SAP Ariba Customer Marketing team is looking for a creative, highly motivated, team player with a growth mindset who is interested in developing a career in events marketing. This internship is based in Palo Alto, CA. In this role, you will report to the Global Customer Events Marketing Lead and work on projects such as; data analysis, demand generation and audience acquisition, digital marketing, internal communications, event planning and management, and content creation. In addition, you’ll have the opportunity to support both solution and campaign launch activity for SAP Ariba Live and virtual supplier success events. This internship is a great opportunity to develop a strong foundation in events marketing and gain experience working at a world leading global B2B technology company</p>
<p><b><br>
</b><br>
