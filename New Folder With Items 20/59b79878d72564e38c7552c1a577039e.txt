<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Job Posting Title</b></h2>
Senior IT Business Analyst, Digital Commerce<p></p>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
The Challenge</b></h2>
As the Senior IT Business Analyst for Digital Commerce, the candidate will be responsible for maintaining and enhancing the e-commerce platform to support our house of brands. This individual will drive commercial growth by scoping and executing projects to enhance the consumer experience, understand and support the technology landscape to ensure platform stability and scale, and collaborate with partners in Sales, Marketing, and other areas of the business to build a shared vision and roadmap for digital commerce.<p></p>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
What you will Do</b></h2>
<p><b>
Agile Project Management</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Work closely with e-commerce business team to develop the e-commerce strategy and articulates the roadmap to the IT development team.</li>
<li>
Responsible for working with clients to identify and document user stories, prioritizing new features, communicate frequent updates to all stakeholders, and oversee system launches.</li>
<li>
Lead sprint retrospective meetings for continuous improvement.</li>
<li>
Manage scope changes by negotiating with business stakeholders and development team, including clear communication on the impact of scope change to other priorities, timeline, or cost.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>
System Ownership</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Drive core development principles by articulating business requirements in terms of stability, reliability, performance, speed, flexibility, and capacity.</li>
<li>
Identify and assess potential for new technologies to drive improvements to the user experience with the goal to boost sales and customer satisfaction.</li>
<li>
Collaborate with the development team, business clients, and IT Strategic Architecture Council to assess outsourcing vs. in-house capability to identify and onboard suppliers as needed for new technologies or development work.</li>
<li>
Define, track, and report on e-commerce metrics that align with the business strategy.</li>
<li>
Drive compliance with industry requirements or best practices such as PCI compliance and PII data protection.</li>
<li>
Ensure appropriate support for e-commerce systems.</li>
</ul>
