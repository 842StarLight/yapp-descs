<h2>Associate Director, Dupixent US Respiratory Marketing - Nasal Polyps and Disease State Education</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Position Overview/Key Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Lead the Respiratory US Dupilumab HCP marketing materials for unbranded Disease State Education (DSE) and support Nasal Polyps (NP) launch</li>
<li>
Competencies and expectations associated with this role include but are not limited to the following:<ul>
<li>
Unbranded DSE strategy development – evolving existing campaigns and development of future campaigns based on multiple launches</li>
<li>
Nasal Polyp support and execution</li>
<li>
Unbranded professional and non-personal promotion campaign lead across Respiratory<ul>
<li>
Development of the non-personal strategy and message execution across digital, media and journals</li>
<li>
Continuous assessment for alignment with Dupilumab franchise strategy and opportunities to leverage franchise synergies across Respiratory DSE and NP</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>
Close collaboration with alliance partners</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>
Unbranded DSE lead for the Joint Review Committee (JRC); oversee all JRC meetings and coordinate launch of all materials</li>
<li>
Manage advertising agency partner and other vendors to ensure proper execution of objectives</li>
<li>
Support the development and management of the Dupilumab marketing budgets across unbranded activities and Nasal Polyps</li>
<li>
Work effectively within the established corporate and industry compliance guidelines</li>
</ul>

Experience Qualifications:</b></p>

<li>
A comprehensive understanding of the key dynamics driving the US health care environment</li>
<li>
Ability to quickly understand and communicate all clinical data / publications to support business objectives</li>
<li>
Biologic product launch experience</li>
<li>
Understanding of market research, creative development and experience with the regulatory review/approval process</li>
<li>
Understanding of the multi-channel marketing space with preferred launch experience and/or respiratory market experience</li>
<li>
Ability to interact widely and effectively within the company, with co-promote partner, with Medical, and across multiple vendors</li>
<li>
Prioritizing resource allocation, deploying resources strategically and monitoring budget to ensure execution and efficient use of resources</li>
</ul>
