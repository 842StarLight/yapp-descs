<h2>Enterprise Services Director</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>AutoRABIT is a hyper-growth SaaS software company providing a Continuous Delivery &amp; DevOps platform for SaaS technologies like Salesfore.com &amp; MuleSoft. We are located in US, India, Australia. AutoRABIT helps Salesforce.com developers to automate their daily tasks to be more productive and increase the release velocity for their development team. AutoRABIT is specialized in Continuous Integration/Continuous Delivery (CI/CD) for SaaS platforms such as Salesforce, Mulesoft, ServiceNow, SAP, Adobe, etc. AutoRABIT framework covers the entire DevOps cycle, and is highly scalable making it a favorite by large enterprises. The framework is complemented by 4 products with strategic innovation and expansion of product offerings.<br>
<br>
<b>Overview:</b><br>
<br>
As the <b>Director of Enterprise Services</b>, you’ll be managing the professional services divisions in AutoRABIT. We’re looking for a leader who can solve problems and manage multiple moving pieces including customers onboarding, adoption and engagement to help our customers maximize their value while engaging with the AutoRABIT platform.</p>
