<h2>Manufacturing Maintenance Coordinator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>This position is responsible for maintenance activities in ICP Building Solutions Group's Norton, OH Manufacturing facility. This direct hands-on position involves completing assigned Preventive Maintenance items, documenting the same in MQ1 system, equipment troubleshooting, and repair as required by management, and actively advising management on issues that could lead to reduced efficiencies and poor equipment reliability. This position is accountable to assist the Supervisor, Maintenance &amp; Plant Engineering to determine the best manner to deploy our limited resources, and to actively pursue ways to improve our operational efficiency. The Maintenance Coordinator will proactively schedule any required external vendors for pre-authorized maintenance or engineering work in the plant or offices. This position requires internal and external communication within the company, and with vendors. Ability to learn and use specialized software to document Preventive Maintenance issues is mandatory.</p>
<p><b>
Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Focus on safety, cost management, and business growth in all interactions and directives.</li>
<li>
Supports ICP BSG safety directives and takes personal responsibility for the safety of all employees.</li>
<li>
Ensure that assigned equipment is operating such that a satisfactory product is being produced and shipped.</li>
<li>
Proactively troubleshoot equipment problems and repair any equipment that is causing poor efficiency or reliability.</li>
<li>
Report preventive maintenance procedures, parameters, and metrics through email and MQ1.</li>
<li>
Responsible for implementation, documentation, recordkeeping, and reporting of preventive maintenance completion rate.</li>
<li>
Accountable for accurate recordkeeping, organization, and maintenance of all related maintenance and equipment documents, records, reports, etc. in hard copy and/or electronic format per company standards.</li>
<li>
Perform non-routine tasks related to plant engineering, and maintenance development and evaluation, as deemed necessary by management.</li>
<li>
Authority to stop production and/or place products on stop if quality, safety, regulatory, specification, or customer criteria are not met.</li>
<li>
Other duties may be assigned, possibly outside of normal position scope.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Requirements:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
&gt;4 years' experience in Plant Maintenance.</li>
<li>
Experience with Aerosol Production equipment and/or the manufacturing of Polyurethane systems is preferred.</li>
<li>
Strong human relations skills and excellent communication skills, both written and verbal, with the ability to influence others and drive change for continuous improvement efforts.</li>
<li>
Excellent problem-solving skills and ability to analyze data for sound decision making.</li>
<li>
Knowledge and proficiency of computer software and hardware necessary to perform duties and aptitude to learn new software as needed.</li>
<li>
Ability to work in chemical manufacturing environment involving isocyanates, solvents, and other hazardous materials requiring fitness for respirator use and physical ability to handle containers and equipment necessary to perform duties.</li>
<li>
Must be able to multi-task effectively, close the loop, and follow specific instruction (work instructions, procedures, etc.).</li>
<li>
Ability to travel infrequently, as deemed necessary by management.</li>
</ul>
