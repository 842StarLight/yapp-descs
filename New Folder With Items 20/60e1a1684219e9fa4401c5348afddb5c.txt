<div>
<b>BOK Financial (BOKF),</b> Headquartered in Tulsa, Oklahoma, BOK Financial Corporation (NASDAQ: BOKF) is a top 25 U.S.-based financial services holding company with operations in ten states – Oklahoma, Texas, Arkansas, Arizona, Colorado, Kansas/Missouri, New Mexico, Nebraska and Wisconsin. The company began more than 100 years ago in Tulsa and has successfully diversified into a variety of industries, businesses and geographies. .</div>
<div>
The <b>Mortgage Servicing Training and Research Manager</b> is responsible for new hire and continued education for Mortgage Servicing personnel, as well as managing review and resolution of loan level research requests. Responsible for training personnel on new and existing processes/ procedures; ensuring the accuracy of the Loan Servicing Portfolio, and providing superior customer experience. Responsibilities also include designing, scheduling and conducting training programs that meet the needs and areas of risk within the Servicing Group, in additional to managing the continuous updating of training curriculum(s), job aids and other reference materials utilized within Mortgage Servicing. Managing loan level research includes but is not limited to the following: loan level research related to new loan boarding, initiated by customer complaints, and credit dispute review.</div>
<div><b>
PRINCIPAL DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Understand and examine company policies, HUD, FNMA, FHLMC, VA, GNMA and other investor/insurer and third party servicing requirements. Working knowledge of loss mitigation requirements to include HAMP, HAFA, modifications, short sales, deeds in lieu and other programs as dictated by the investors. Create training curriculum surrounding the process metrics, definition and monitoring of thresholds and critical breakpoints. Analyze workflow and operations to seek out efficiencies that can be gained with additional training opportunities. Understanding governing rules and regulations surrounding the Servicing Portfolio and implications of processes internally and externally. Analyze recent audit findings and create additional training curriculum(s) to mitigate findings needing correction and provide pro-active plans for future continued training. Provides Mortgage Servicing staff with training and instruction in the following applications: MSP, MSP interfaces, Synergy, All Regs, Enterprise Complaint Portal and Genesys phone systems. Provides training on relevant forms and procedures.</li>
<li>Develop, design, and present Mortgage Servicing training curriculum, teaching aids, instructor guides, and media materials for courses. Facilitates training with external vendors. Ensure training material stays current with Regulatory changes and Policy changes. Plans and implements the training schedule, including room set-up and necessary training materials. Develop alternate training methods such as computer-based training and outside vendor assisted courses.</li>
<li>Develops and maintains Servicing SharePoint site, including online manuals, materials, quick reference guides etc. Creates and maintains Servicing courses and class schedules in SharePoint site.</li>
<li>Manages research team to ensure timely review and processing of research items based upon defined service levels.<br>
</li>
</ul>
<div><b>KNOWLEDGE, SKILLS and ABILITIES:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Broad knowledge of applicable Mortgage systems including MSP and interfaces</li>
<li>Comprehensive knowledge of the Mortgage Servicing business and Mortgage Servicing Regulations</li>
<li>Strong skills in development of training curriculum</li>
<li>Broad knowledge of the daily operating procedures of Mortgage Servicing.</li>
<li>Superb PC and software skills in order to design and implement materials, ability to research by internet use, website setup a plus; prefer MS Office</li>
<li>Excellent interpersonal skills including oral and written communication skills</li>
<li>Excellent group facilitation strategies and presentation skills</li>
</ul>
<div>This level of knowledge is normally acquired through completion of a Bachelor’s Degree and 5 years experience in Mortgage lending <b>or</b> a combination of 8 years of training experience and/or experience in a Mortgage lending department. Or 11+ years of equivalent work related experience.</div>
