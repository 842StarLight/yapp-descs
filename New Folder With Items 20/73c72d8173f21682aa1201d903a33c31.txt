<h2>General Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p>Responsible to meet expectations in the areas of Profit Management, Guest Services, Marketing and Sales Management, Human Resources Management, Asset Management, and Safety and Security Management as noted below:</p>
<div>
<b>
Guest Services</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Maintains guest service as the driving philosophy of the hotel.</li>
<li>Personally demonstrates a commitment to guest service by responding to guest needs.</li>
<li>Is committed to making every guest a satisfied guest.</li>
<li>Ensures all hotel staff, including new-hires, know all components/features of our guest service guarantee and are trained to meet service standards; develops added-value customer service programs.</li>
<li>Empowers hotel staff to deliver guest service by encouraging and rewarding responsive guest</li>
</ul>
assistance.<br>
<ul><li>Ensures hotel standards contribute to the delivery of consistent guest service.</li></ul>
</div>
<div>
<b>
Marketing and Sales Management</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Can reposition the hotel as needed, upon management's approval.</li>
<li>Develops and implements marketing and sales plans based on demand segments and to</li>
</ul>
maximize revpar.<br>
<ul><li>Knows why competitors are successful in each demand segment and directly markets/sells</li></ul>
against them.<br>
<ul>
<li>Ensures that marketing and sales plans are appropriate for each sales period, including special plans to maximize room revenue during forecasted low occupancy.</li>
<li>Coordinates advertising, public relations, and promotional programs.</li>
<li>Knows key accounts and actively sells through sales calls, property tours, etc.</li>
</ul>
</div>
<div>
<b>
Human Resources Management</b><br>
<ul><li>Manages human resources functions including recruiting, selection, orientation, training,</li></ul>
performance planning and evaluation, pay and reward programs to maintain a qualified workforce.<br>
<ul>
<li>Maintains a positive, cooperative work environment between staff and management.</li>
<li>Emphasizes associate selection, training and development as a way of doing business.</li>
<li>Ensures all hotel associates know hotel objectives.</li>
<li>Ensures personnel files are accurate and comply with both local and Federal laws and</li>
</ul>
regulations.<br>
<ul>
<li>Administers personnel policies fairly and consistently.</li>
<li>Resolves associate grievances in a fair and timely way.</li>
<li>Helps to develop management talent by acting as a mentor for direct reports.</li>
<li>Ensures training objective and development plans are completed.</li>
<li>Monitors and maintains acceptable turnover levels.</li>
<li>Performs all performance appraisals as required for direct reports.</li>
</ul>
</div>
<div>
<b>
Asset Management</b><br>
<ul><li>Maintains physical product standards by managing preventive maintenance programs and by</li></ul>
scheduling deep-cleaning activities.<br>
<ul>
<li>Ensures associate involvement in preventive maintenance programs.</li>
<li>Protects the interests of the hotel during capital projects.</li>
<li>Coordinates capital projects within budget, on time, and with minimal disruption to the guests.</li>
<li>Has acceptable property quality audits.</li>
<li>Personally conducts a weekly property audit with the hotel's maintenance worker and Executive Housekeeper.</li>
<li>Periodically inspects rooms, building exterior, parking lot, etc.</li>
</ul>
</div>
<div>
<b>
Safety and Security Management</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Knows local health and safety codes and regulations that apply to the hotel.</li>
<li>Understands and implements Right to Know laws.</li>
<li>Recognizes and corrects potential safety hazards such as broken doors or railings, fire hazards, etc.</li>
<li>Recognizes and corrects potential security problems such as locking doors after hours, etc.</li>
<li>Understands and follows policies and procedures for the hotel's key control system and ensures</li>
</ul>
others are trained in same.<br>
<ul><li>Uses ongoing safety training to minimize worker's compensation claims.</li></ul>
</div>
<div>
<b>
Profit Management</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Meets or exceeds budgeted profit and margin for hotel.</li>
<li>Accurately forecasts revenues/expenses.</li>
<li>Prepares annual budget that accurately reflects the hotel's business plan.</li>
<li>Anticipates revenue/cost problems and manages the timing of discretionary expenditures to</li>
</ul>
stabilize cash flow.<br>
<ul><li>Analyzes financial and operating information on ongoing basis to adjust business plans, labor</li></ul>
requirements and operating costs.<br>
<ul><li>Ensures hotel staff is trained in financial control procedures for cash vouchers, inventories and</li></ul>
receivables, and that these procedures are regularly followed.<br>
<ul>
<li>Knows the hotel's demand segments and sources of business for each.</li>
<li>Knows the principal competition for each demand segment and can take advantage of the</li>
</ul>
hotel's relative strengths against each.<br>
<ul>
<li>Identifies major revenues and expense opportunities and possible problems.</li>
<li>Accurately forecasts occupancy changes based on the changing market conditions (e.g.</li>
</ul>
increased competition).<br>
<ul><li>Translates business plans into action and manages those actions toward the achievement of</li></ul>
revenue and cost objectives.</div>
<p><b>
Experience/Education</b><br>
College degree in Hotel Administration preferred; 2-3 years hotel management experience or equivalent combination of education and experience in hotel of similar size and stature.</p>
<p>
This job description in no way states or implies that these are the only duties to be performed</p>
</div>
