<b>
What you'll do:</b>
</div>
<div>Our Account Executives are highly motivated sales professionals with a proven track record of success. Account Executives are experienced working with minimal direction to develop and maintain long-term client relationships while helping to grow revenues and profits. The Account Executive will be responsible for the entire sales life cycle, with support from internal lead development representatives. This position requires effective customer service and sales skills to traverse the process of building and managing a sales pipeline.</div>
<ul>
<li>
Identify and qualify prospects that support alignment between sales and marketing strategies which drive new clients and new revenue streams.</li>
<li>
Qualify and convert both sales sourced leads and identified opportunities through provided leads that result in closeable business.</li>
<li>
Develop customer acquisition and account penetration strategies and lead them through execution.</li>
<li>
Summarize the right solution(s) and offer it to prospect in an organized and comprehensive format.</li>
<li>
Create clear, concise synopsis of the key messages into a creative presentation (usually PowerPoint) and present it to engage and share key information.</li>
<li>
Continuously develop improved relations with clients and internal departments through frequent discussion and interaction.</li>
<li>
Successfully complete discovery calls with prospects/clients to determine solution alignment that will result in an effective, well planned campaign that delivers ROI.</li>
<li>
Negotiate contracts, with the help of our General Council.
</li>
<li>Ensure client's needs are met while achieving maximum profitability for Televerde.</li>
</ul>
<div><b>
What you'll need to have:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
Bachelor's degree or relevant experience required.</li>
<li>
Minimum 3+ years B2B direct marketing sales experience, with lead development into mid-size and enterprise accounts.</li>
<li>
Experience in developing sales plans and accurate sales forecasts is required.</li>
<li>
Experience within the Technology market sector is helpful.</li>
</ul>
