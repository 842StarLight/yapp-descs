<h2>Account Executive</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Who we are looking for...</b></p>
<p>You are a digital marketing and technology sales executive and a superior communicator. You can give outstanding presentations to demonstrate the value of our partnership and approach. You have a background in digital media\marketing\technology.</p>
<p>You are a thought leader, or on your way to becoming one, and possess a strong desire to collaborate with clients, reinventing the way they do business. You enjoy working closely with clients while educating and guiding them on their digital journey. You like to listen, learn and excel. You are curious, with an ability to understand new technology solution, and bring the spirit of innovation to your clients.</p>
<p>Your clients trust your judgment and expertise to help them solve their most pressing business problems. You will consistently strive to find the right solution to positively impact the business, improve business processes, or reduce costs and risk. You understand and are comfortable with complex sales cycles.</p>
<p>Responsibilities Include:</p>
<p>· Business development activities including professional communications via phone and email.</p>
<p>· Prospecting for new opportunities with new and existing clients.</p>
<p>· Continuously build and maintain a pipeline of sales activity through prospecting and a variety of methods.</p>
<p>· Proposal writing and client communications.</p>
<p>· Ability to identify digital project opportunities across a broad range of technical solutions.</p>
<p>· Establish trusted relationships with clients.</p>
<p>· Develop a trusted advisor relationship with multiple stakeholders across the client organization.</p>
<p>· Leverage industry knowledge and internal subject matter experts to develop solutions.</p>
<p>· Penetrate new organizations/accounts, develop relationships and capture new business.</p>
<p>· Listen to your prospects and customers.</p>
<p>In addition, the ideal candidate will possess:</p>
<p>· A background in digital marketing.</p>
<p>· Digital agency experience.</p>
<p>· Experience in competitive analysis.</p>
<p>· An understanding of or experience with the AWS Cloud environment.</p>
<p>· Ability to think on your feet and speak with authority on Valtira’s offerings.</p>

<p><b>What We Do...</b></p>
<p>We are a software and services company based out of Minneapolis, Minnesota. We build technically sophisticated and highly customizable websites and applications, leveraging both our own products and custom-built software. We focus on fulfilling our stated purpose: <i>“Do Work That Matters”.</i> Our clients vary in size and industry and have long-term relationships with us. This is a chance to become a key member of the team directly responsible for making a real difference to the companies (and the people) with whom we work. We work across industries on a wide variety of solutions. We care about our clients and our employees. We nurture these relationships to create compelling work that delivers game-changing results.</p>
<p>We have a flexible office, space with open areas and private rooms which allows for casual conversation or headphones – whatever you are feeling. This also contributes to a collaborative environment where sharing is encouraged. We are very tech friendly and the dress code is relaxed. While we are headquartered in Minneapolis, working remotely is acceptable on an as-needed basis.</p>
