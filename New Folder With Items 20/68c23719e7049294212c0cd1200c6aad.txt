<p><b>GENERAL DESCRIPTION – PURPOSE OF ROLE:</b></p>
<p>
Blue Buffalo is looking for an individual to mature an existing BI function. As a Business Intelligence Developer you will be engaging directly with business teams consisting of product development, marketing, sales, customer service etc. and developing elegant reporting solutions to help them fully understand their data, derive valuable insights and drive business change. Individual is responsible for the design, development and programing of systems to consolidate and analyze data from multiple internal sources and to integrate unstructured, diverse data sources to generate actionable solutions.</p>
</div></div>

<ul><li>Provide insights and own reporting solutions, data validation, and presenting data in a format that allows the team</li></ul>
