<h2>Austin 02 - Receiving Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div><div>Asset protection to reduce Shrink and maximize contribution through the 100% execution of product organization and processes.<br>
<br>
<br>
<br>
<br>
Accountabilities of this position include:<br>
<br>
Receives and ensures accuracy of all incoming loads (HEB and DSD)<br>
<br>
Oversee wareroom activity and ensure SOP compliance<br>
<br>
Maintain solid Partner and vendor relations<br>
<br>
Make decisions based on established guidelines and standards<br>
<br>
Resolve issues by involving the appropriate individuals as needed<br>
<br>
Demonstrate knowledge of vendor/partner sales floor conditions and communicate to ensure compliance<br>
<br>
Ensure communication of vendor stock conditions/levels to appropriate parties<br>
<br>
Ensure that incoming invoices and outgoing credits are accurate and complete.<br>
<br>
Communicate below standard wareroom, cooler, and freezer conditions to top store management.<br>
<br>
Researches unusual billing issues with the appropriate party in order to resolve<br>
<br>
Ensures quality of incoming products by physically inspecting packaging and verifying code dates.<br>
<br>
Utilizes online tools to ensure that vendors are giving consistent and timely credit on DSD merchandise<br>
<br>
Maintains paperwork file system for all departments.<br>
<br>
Maintains a return goods station for all damaged product.<br>
<br>
Maintains safety guidelines<br>
<br>

<br>
Computer skills: Microsoft Office Software including Word, Excel, and HEB systems<br>
<br>
Wareroom Manager Certification<br>
<br>
Receiving Controller Certification<br>
<br>
Basics of Safety<br>
<br>
Company Orientation<br>
<br>
Power jack training<br>
<br>
Physical Requirements:<br>
<br>
Lift loads up to 60+ lbs, bending, kneeling, standing, reaching, and squatting.</div></div>
