<p><b>COMPANY DESCRIPTION</b></p>
<p>SAP is the global market leader for business software and related services, and SAP National Security Services Inc.™ (SAP NS2™) is an independent U.S. subsidiary, offering SAP solutions with specialized levels of security and support to meet the requirements of U.S. national security and critical infrastructure customers.
</p>
<p>Must be a U.S. Person; NS2 does not offer Visa sponsorship for this role</p>
<p>All internals must have manager’s approval to transfer</p>
<p><b>POSITION DESCRIPTION</b></p>
<p>SAP National Security Services, SAP NS2, is seeking a Senior Level Account Executive to join its Intel IT Sector. He/she will be responsible for the sale of the complete range of SAP software and cloud solutions, including SAP’s industry leading platform for Big Data, Machine Learning and Predictive Analytics to Intel agencies in the DC, MD, VA area. Territory will include installed base clients as well as new client development.
</p>
<p>Specifically, he/she will be selling in the INTEL agency environments.</p>
<p>Duties include:</p>
<ul>
<li>Closing business to exceed monthly, quarterly; and annual revenue objectives;</li>
<li>Creating, enhancing, and managing business relationships to foster business opportunities;</li>
<li>Proactively and creatively understanding client needs and identifying solutions to requirements; actively creating business opportunities in conjunction with partner;</li>
<li>Strong usage of CRM and forecasting tools to within 95%+ accuracy on an on-going basis; and
</li>
<li>Working with SAP NS2 sales and support organizations including Marketing, Pre-sales, and Professional Services to build and execute overall account strategy</li>
<li>High motivation and activity level</li>
<li>Understanding of SAP NS2 products and value proposition</li>
<li>Internal management of cross functional resources</li>
</ul>
<p><b>EXPECTATIONS AND TASKS</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Serving as primary client liaison within the INTEL territory;</li>
<li>Managing and growing installed base and new client relationships;</li>
<li>Creating, enhancing, and managing business relationships to leverage additional opportunities;</li>
<li>Identifying business opportunities and mapping appropriate solutions to client requirements; and</li>
<li>Driving opportunities in conjunction with partners and support team to meet and exceed monthly, quarterly, and annual revenue objectives.</li>
<li>Matrix manage cross functional organization that includes: SE, BD, Support and Professional Services resources.
</li>
</ul>
<p><b>WORK EXPERIENCE</b></p>
<ul>
<li>5-7 years of demonstrated history as an individual contributor selling enterprise software solutions to senior level decision makers with Federal DOD agencies, specifically, in the INTEL agency environment.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>EDUCATION AND QUALIFICATION / SKILLS AND COMPETENCIES</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Active TS/SCI Clearance highly desired;</li>
<li>Federal government intelligence sector experience required;</li>
<li>Bachelor's degree in Business, Marketing, or other relevant area of study;</li>
<li>Strong usage of metrics and ability to accurately forecast on an on-going basis;</li>
<li>Demonstrated excellent communication and presentation skills; and</li>
<li>Exuded confidence and leadership in the execution of the job function; solutions to senior level decision makers with Federal DOD agencies, specifically, in the INTEL agency environment.</li>
<li>Expected travel 20%<br>
</li>
