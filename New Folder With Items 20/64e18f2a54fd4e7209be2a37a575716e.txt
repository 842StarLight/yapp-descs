<p><b>Guest Service Agent
</b></p>
<p>The Lied Lodge &amp; Conference Center is looking for a decisive thinker with strong verbal and written communication skills to fill a Guest Service Agent role on our team. The ideal candidate has excellent interpersonal skills and thrives on serving others, has high attention to detail and is committed to providing outstanding service to guests.</p>
<p>
A natural setting, rustic ambience and exceptional service make Lied Lodge &amp; Conference Center unique to our guests. Lied Lodge &amp; Conference Center is regionally recognized and operated by the Arbor Day Foundation, offers 140 guest rooms, over 14,000 square feet of meeting space, and hosts a diverse audience of business and leisure guests 365 days a year.</p>
<p>
The Foundation offers an excellent working environment, salary commensurate with experience and the chance to make a difference. If this position speaks to your passion and your proficiency, please apply with a cover letter and resume to the Arbor Day Foundation’s careers page at:</p>
<p>
https://www.arborday.org/careers/
</p>
<p><b>Essential Functions of the position:</b></p>
<div>
<ul>
<li>High degree of professionalism and a commitment to high-quality work.</li>
<li>Greet and welcome all guests approaching the Front Desk.</li>
<li>Provide excellent guest service in person, over the phone, and via email.</li>
<li>Communicate with all departments to ensure a seamless Guest experience.</li>
<li>Must be effective in handling problems, including anticipating, preventing, identifying and solving problems as necessary</li>
<li>Answer inquires pertaining to hotel services, guest registration, travel directions, and make recommendations.</li>
<li>Arrange tours, taxis, or shuttles for Guests</li>
<li>Record, sort and distribute incoming faxes and packages.</li>
<li>Manage luggage storage and distribution</li>
<li>All other duties as assigned.</li>
</ul>
</div>
<p><b>Knowledge, Education, Skills, and Abilities:</b></p>
<div>
<ul>
<li>Exceptional communication and organizational skills with superior attention to detail.</li>
<li>Demonstrated time management, multitasking and creative problem-solving skills.</li>
<li>Passion for the Foundation’s mission of tree planting and environmental stewardship.</li>
<li>Detail-oriented with strong organizational, time management and planning skills</li>
<li>Professional, self-motivated person with an optimistic outlook &amp; attitude</li>
<li>Ability to work independently and within a team.</li>
<li>Ability to accept constructive feedback</li>
<li>Excellent customer service and communication skills</li>
<li>Proficient in MS Word and Excel</li>
<li>Ability to learn the OPERA system</li>
</ul>
</div>
<p><b>Physical Requirements:</b></p>
<div>
<ul>
<li>Flexible and long hours sometimes required</li>
<li>Ability to stand for extended period of time</li>
<li>Light physical activity throughout shift (moving luggage, sorting packages, etc.</li>
<li>Clear vision and hearing – Guest Communication</li>
<br>
</ul>
</div>
<p></p>

<div>
<ul>

<li>Experience with basic office tasks</li>
<li>Basic computer &amp; multi-line phone system skills</li>
</ul>
</div>
<p><b>Preferred qualifications:</b></p>

<li>Prior experience working with a team in an open-office environment</li>
</ul>
</div>

</ul>
</div>
