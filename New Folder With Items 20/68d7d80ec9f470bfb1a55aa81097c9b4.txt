<b>Description:</b><br>
About eHealth Technologies
<br><br>
Founded in 2006, eHealth Technologies is the leading provider of medical record retrieval and organization services and image-enabled Health Information Exchanges (HIEs). With customers across the country, eHealth Technologies works with prominent HIEs and the nation’s top-ranked hospitals, including 16 of the 20 US News &amp; World Report Honor Roll Hospitals for 2018-2019. The company’s eHealth Connect® solution enhances patient and physician satisfaction by streamlining referrals and intelligently aggregating patients’ clinical records. This assures physicians have the right healthcare information to care for their patients, right when they need it. As a critical adjunct to HIEs, eHealth Connect® Image Exchange enables the automated access to all types of medical images in the context of the aggregated patient record, in full diagnostic quality, and supports the ability of providers to meet Meaningful Use Stage 2 requirements.
<br><br>
Position Description
<br><br>
The account manager fulfills a very important role within the company with the ultimate responsibility of maintaining and developing long term, mutually beneficial relationships with a portfolio of current clients. Acting as the primary liaison between our customers and internal teams that include operations, sales, marketing, and customer success, the account manager’s role is to maximize client satisfaction and ongoing business revenue and value.
<br><br>
<b>Primary Responsibilities:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Develops and maintains account plans to generate sales among named accounts, including upselling, cross-selling, and opportunities to grow/expand business within accounts.</li>
<li>Operates as the main point of contact to key stakeholders within assigned customers</li>
<li>Develops and maintains long-term relationships with accounts</li>
<li>Focuses on customer satisfaction and retention and makes sure clients receive requested products and services in a timely fashion</li>
<li>Communicates client needs and demands to internal team members</li>
<li>Forecasts and tracks client account metrics</li>
<li>Manage projects within client relationships, working to carry out client goals while meeting company goals</li>
<li>Coordinate with staff members working on the same account to ensure consistent service</li>
<li>Collaborates with enterprise and inside sales and marketing team to reach prospective clients</li>
<li>Service multiple clients concurrently, often meeting deadlines</li>
<li>Keep records of client interactions and transactions</li>
<li>Leadership of the contract renewal process with their assigned accounts</li>
<li>Ownership of the executive relationships at named accounts</li>
</ul>
<b>Activities:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Provide overall view of account status and suggestions for continuous improvement in the Company’s products and processes to support world-class customer satisfaction.</li>
<li>Create and maintain strategic plan to drive increase in business at customer sites: Promoting usage; Adding capabilities not being utilized such as indexing and integrations; Additional clinical departments that may be able to utilize company services.</li>
<li>Monitor assigned accounts for volume trends, produce reports/maintain dashboards, and initiate actions for sites that are decreasing in volume beyond standard volatility.</li>
<li>Manage and hold regular Account Review meetings with account stakeholders, track all actions of the meetings to conclusion.</li>
<li>Work with Executive Sponsors and Regional Directors who may own other strategic accounts to ensure there is regular contact with all strategic accounts.</li>
<li>Working with set accounts to manage and solve any issues of dissatisfaction in collaboration with appropriate organizations in the company.</li>
<li>Solicit customer feedback from all sources- primarily through surveys, Sales, Operations, and direct interaction with customers and communicate such within the company</li>
<li>Work in concert with other company organizations to ensure high customer satisfaction</li>
<li>Coordinate with Marketing team to develop tools/messaging to support sales and customer support activity that follows existing brand standards.</li>
<li>Assist in pre-launch account activity for new clients within named territory, by working with Sales Executives, Operation’s Regional Directors, Marketing, and Customer Success</li>
<li>Assist in post-launch account activity, by working with Operation’s Regional Directors, Sales Executives, Marketing, and Customer Success Manage and update tool(s) used to communicate account status to all stakeholders (e.g., Salesforce.com, Trello, etc.)</li>
<li>Work with other organizations to identify new or enhanced features that can be incorporated into the client portal and potentially other new technology offerings that can have value within the customer base.</li>
</ul>
