<div><b>WHAT WE'VE GOT GOING ON</b></div>
<div>
The Director of Alliance Sales Engineering will provide direct leadership to our Global Alliance Sales Engineering team. In this role, you will own all aspects of building and managing a scalable, world-class team of Sales Engineers, providing leadership and guidance in SaaS technology, sales and partner strategy, relationship building, solution development and trial execution / conversion.</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
The Alliance Sales Engineering team within Looker serves as a primary point of technical contact to drive the successful introduction, adoption and broad understanding and usage of Looker’s products within the Looker alliances ecosystem. Activities of the team include product demos, technical qualification, identification of customer/partner business / data analysis use cases, trial development and delivery, definition of appropriate business and technical solutions, customer/partner management, account development, technology evangelizing, and business consulting. Key metrics for the team currently include partner influenced and assisted deals.</div>
