<h2>Business Development Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>
<div>At Getaround, we’re building a global platform to enable a future where all cars are shared. There are more than 1 billion cars in the world, and these cars are parked, on average, more than 95% of the time. With instant and keyless carsharing via our patented hardware and software platform and our industry-leading mobile apps, we’re helping communities around the world share these underutilized resources to reduce their vehicle footprint while increasing mobility solutions.</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
Getaround is seeking a resourceful, self-motivated sales or business development professional eager to seek out and close high-value customers. You’ll be responsible for growing our car supply by leaps-and bounds by finding and developing relationships with fleet owners. You are highly personable, a compelling spokesperson, and driven above all by closing deals. You’re passionate about delivering a superior experience for the customer, love the fast-pace of a growing startup, and thrive in ambiguity. You’ll be writing the playbook for this role as you go.</div>
</div>
<div><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>What you'll be doing</b></h3>
<div><ul>
<li>
Grow Fleet Owner community through outreach by email, phone, virtual meetings, and in-person presentations</li>
<li>
Travel to current and launching markets to find and sell new business</li>
<li>
Serve as an Getaround product expert and evangelize the benefits of car sharing, including our patented software and hardware technology</li>
<li>
Conduct market research to determine where and when the best business opportunities will present themselves</li>
<li>
Formulate effective and on-brand messaging tailored to fleet owners</li>
<li>
Maximize new car owner confidence by providing an informative and transparent ramp up experience</li>
<li>
Partner with Marketing Managers to offer insight into the effectiveness of developing fleet owner leads</li>
<li>
Partner with Operations Coordinators to ensure a timely and responsive car onboarding experience</li>
<li>
Partner with General Managers to develop new business strategies</li>
</ul></div>
</div></div>
<div><div>

<div><ul>

Proven track record with progressive experience in developing new business</li>
<li>
Independent operator with a high degree of motivation to hit ambitious goals</li>
<li>
Familiarity with using data and process to increase sales velocity</li>
<li>
Superior presentation skills communicating the Getaround value proposition for fleet owners</li>
<li>
Excellent verbal and written communication skills with both internal and external stakeholders</li>
<li>
Ability to operate in a fast-paced environment, stay organized, and prioritize responsibilities</li>
<li>
Readiness to close the deal, whenever and wherever the opportunity exists</li>
</ul></div>
