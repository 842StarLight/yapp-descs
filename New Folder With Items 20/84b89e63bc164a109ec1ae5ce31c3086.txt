<h2>Operations & Transaction Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Are you ready to join a dynamic, collaborative, fun and giving culture that works to succeed every day?</p>
<p>Good Company Real Estate is seeking a highly talented person to fill a critical role with our tight-knit team! The Operations &amp; Transaction Manager is the hub of our business. This position is responsible not only for providing 5-star customer service to our clients, vendor-partners and team members but also organizing and managing the company’s systems and processes.</p>
<p><b>You may be a fit if you: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Love moving at a fast pace and are excited to be a catalyst for big growth.</li>
<li>Thrive on organizing and planning and can think at least one step ahead.</li>
<li>Enjoy building and implementing systems.</li>
<li>Are a great communicator with the ability to be polite, friendly and professional.</li>
<li>Are always looking at solutions rather than problems.</li>
<li>Are flexible with your time when necessary.</li>
<li>Have an extensive understanding of the Arizona Real Estate contract.</li>
<li>You love the real estate industry and have experience as a broker or transaction manager.</li>
<li>Are coachable and thrive on accountability.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Position Responsibilities: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Manage listing and closing processes</li>
<li>Write and negotiate contracts</li>
<li>Manage all contract timelines</li>
<li>Write and negotiate addendums and BINSRs</li>
<li>Oversee repair process</li>
<li>Manage closing and post-closing process</li>
<li>Maintain consistent, extraordinary client communication</li>
<li>Keep the entire team updated on transaction progress and advise team members when they need to become involved</li>
<li>Obtain showing feedback from agents and provide updates to sellers</li>
<li>Oversee files at all times (10-20 at a time)</li>
<li>Manage team operations and administrative functions</li>
<li>Maintain database of clients, cross-sale agents, and vendors</li>
<li>Manage and create marketing campaigns</li>
<li>Manage a portfolio of investment properties (10-20 at a time)</li>
<li>Manage wholesale purchases and sales</li>
<li>Track profit &amp; loss and transaction numbers for retail business and wholesale business</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Qualifications</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Excellent and effective written and verbal communication skills</li>
<li>Enthusiastic, high energy, self-starting</li>
<li>Ability to focus on tasks, meet deadlines and handle last-minute details</li>
<li>Team-player who thrives on being part of a tight-knit team</li>
<li>Has an AZ Real Estate Sale (Broker’s license a plus)</li>
<li>Technologically savvy</li>
<li>Can-do attitude</li>
<li>Problem Solver/Creator of Solutions</li>
<li>Excellent organization and project management skills</li>
<li>Ability to work independently</li>
<li>Proactive</li>
</ul>

<p><b>How you know this position is a fit: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>You have an orderly process for all of your to-dos</li>
<li>You find fulfillment in helping people</li>
<li>People stare in wonder as you describe your insane, or “orderly” filing system</li>
<li>You are a master communicator, able to diffuse almost any situation</li>
<li>You are able to handle last-minute emergencies calmly and with a smile</li>
</ul>
<p><b>How you know this position is NOT a fit: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>You prefer solitude or position with little or no social interaction</li>
<li>You are uncomfortable having difficult conversations</li>
<li>You stress out when something doesn’t go as planned</li>
<li>You don’t like to check your work email or voicemail in the evenings or on weekends</li>
<li>Frequently need to borrow a pen during meetings</li>
<li>You are inflexible</li>
<li>You have ever said “that’s not my job”</li>
</ul>
