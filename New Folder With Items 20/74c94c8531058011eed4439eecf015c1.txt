<h2>Field Project Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><ul>
<div>
<p>Roche Diagnostics is seeking an experienced Field Project Manager to lead our external Customer implementations. Responsible for developing, creating, documenting and facilitating highly professional project plans and implementing Roche products and services within our Customer facilities.</p>
<p></p>
<p>
Characteristics of responsible projects include high complexity, medium to high risk and long term duration. Directs and coordinates activities to ensure project progresses on schedule and within prescribed guidelines and objectives while meeting all other specified project performance parameters. Serves as a trusted consultant, liaison and advisor regarding all facets of the implementation project plan.</p>
<p></p>
<p>
This is a field based position within the northeast corridor with preferred location being near Philadelphia to Boston</p>
<p></p>
<p>
The Opportunity:
</p>
<ul>
<li>Designs, gains agreement, communicates and leads the execution of professional project strategies and plans for external facing large diagnostic system Customer implementations which conform with Roche Diagnostics policies and procedures.</li>
<li>Identifies, acquires, forms, leads and wins commitment of internal Roche Diagnostics and external Customer stakeholders/resources in assembling a project team.</li>
<li>Collaborates with project stakeholders and teams to outline work plan and to assign duties, responsibilities and accountabilities.</li>
<li>Champions the program/project through business processes.</li>
<li>Conducts pre-purchase order, pre-implementation meetings, implementation meetings and post-project review meetings for Roche Diagnostics customers and internal stakeholders.</li>
<li>Recommends methods and develop policies and processes to improve area operations, processes, efficiency and service to both internal and external customers that will provide long term direction for Roche Diagnostics Centralized Diagnostics/Tissue Diagnostics organizational strategies.</li>
<li>As a member of the field project management team, will be expected to coach and mentor peers.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p>
Who you are:
</p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree in life science or technical degree related to product line or equivalent experience</li>
<li>5 years experience in an IVD laboratory or in sales, marketing or services in the IVD industry</li>
<li>5 years project management experience, preferably with end user customers</li>
<li>Strong communication, influence, leadership, conflict management and negotiation skills</li>
<li>Extensive knowledge of internal systems and business procedures</li>
<li>Knowledge of key competitors</li>
<li>Knowledge of customer needs and features/benefits to meet those needs</li>
<li>Training in program/project team leadership/change management</li>
<li>Proficiency in specific product areas (understanding of automated diagnostic system hardware, software and reagents – clinical chemistry, immunology, automation and robotics, molecular diagnostics and point of care)</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p>
Preferred:</p>
<ul>
<li>PMP certification</li>
<li>Experience in cloud-based project management and other cloud-based software</li>
</ul>
