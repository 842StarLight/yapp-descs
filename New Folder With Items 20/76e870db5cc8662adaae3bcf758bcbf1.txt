<b>Company Overview:</b><br>
PrecisionLender is modernizing commercial banking. Our sales and negotiation solution empowers bankers with actionable, in-the-moment insights and coaching, so they win better deals and build strong, more profitable relationships. Andi®, PrecisionLender's virtual insights analyst, augments banker strengths and intelligence with the latest technology and data, delivering the best recommendations at exactly the right time. More than 12,000+ bankers at 200+ banks – ranging from $1B to $1T in assets – use our solution.
<br><br>
<b>What You'll Do Here:</b><br>
As a Data Implementation Manager on the Client Success team, it's your responsibility to work with our newest clients to get them up to speed using our commercial loan pricing and profitability solution. This position will be extensively involved in implementing PrecisionLender's Relationship Awareness (RA) module for new clients, as well as maintaining RA installations for existing customers. In this role, you'll:
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Work with our newest clients, on a daily basis, to configure, test and validate the integration of data from the customer's core processing system with PrecisionLender.</li>
<li>Understand client concerns and address them appropriately, knowing when to seek out help from others.</li>
<li>Independently manage medium-sized projects to completion under general supervision.</li>
<li>Communicate client issues and requests to our developers and create appropriate solutions with them.</li>
<li>Ensure that the RA onboarding process is completed in a timely manner consistent with client expectations.</li>
<li>Independently manage client queue to perform RA maintenance and be responsible for decisions on queue priority.</li>
<li>Identify and implement ways to improve the integration processes in order to improve efficiency and effectiveness over time.</li>
</ul>
<b>Responsibilities:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>High willingness to gain product &amp; financial knowledge to effectively serve our clients.</li>
<li>Key member of the Client Success Team working closely with our clients as well as our sales, operations, and product development teams.</li>
<li>Translate client data appropriately into the RA platform to ensure data is being used appropriately in calculations.</li>
<li>Participates in client Kick-Off meetings to set expectations, answer questions and communicate process and data requirements for new customers.</li>
<li>Manages subsequent client calls independent of other team members.</li>
<li>Develop good working relationships with clients including individuals from IT, Lending, Credit and/or Operations.</li>
<li>Work closely with client counterparts to identify unique business requirements and then map, test, validate and maintain the IRM interface.</li>
<li>Organize and maintain all client data files used in the integration process.</li>
<li>Create the mapping configuration file used by PrecisionLender to execute scheduled imports from the client data files provided using Excel and MS Visual Studio.</li>
<li>Design, implement, support and monitor exception reporting systems for prompt resolution to any problems in processing, including cleanup of daily errors for all RA installations.</li>
<li>Keep internal project boards and Salesforce client records updated to reflect the current status and key communications with each client.</li>
<li>Perform business and data analysis against the growing client database to assist management in identifying insightful and actionable data.</li>
<li>Special projects and analysis.</li>
</ul>
