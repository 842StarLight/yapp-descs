<h2>Store Leader</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>since our launch in 1993 with a collection of six essential handbags, kate spade new york has always stood for optimistic femininity. today, our brand is a global life and style house with handbags, ready-to-wear, jewelry, footwear, gifts, home décor and more. polished ease, thoughtful details and a modern, sophisticated use of color—kate spade new york’s founding principles define a unique style synonymous with joy. under the vision of creative director nicola glass, we continue to celebrate confident women with a youthful spirit.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>kate spade new york is part of the tapestry house of brands.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Essential Duties &amp; Responsibilities:
</b></p>
<p><b>Client &amp; Service Expert:
 </b></p>
<p>Oversee the development of clientele by leading by example in the ability to build and maintain relationships with clients, produce strong results from proactive outreach and the development of a personal business trade.
 </p>
<p>Development of business driving initiatives that build a repeat business or that attracts a new customer.
 </p>
<p>Ensure each associate is actively utilizing their client book in order to generate increased sales through monthly client book reviews and evaluation of their outreach.
 </p>
<p>Ensure team is compliant with all clientele standards and thank you note policy.
 </p>
<p>Ensure all associates complete the sales training program.
 </p>
<p>Model and supervise the selling environment, provide consistent coaching on sales training process in order to ensure the highest level of customer service and sales.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Leadership Presence/Steward of Talent:
 </b></p>
<p>Achieve financial success through improvement of measurable statistics that positively impact the store performance.
 </p>
<p>Hire, train and motivate a qualified sales team that effectively represents and communicates the kate spade new york brand to our customers.
 </p>
<p>Provide specific development for the assistant manager and supervisor by assigning accountabilities and training with consistent follow-up and feedback.
 </p>
<p>Perform annual performance evaluations with quarterly review of employee goals.
 </p>
<p>Network and create positive relationships in the community that result in business generation, awareness of the kate spade new york brand and builds a bench strength of qualified candidates to fill key open positions.
 </p>
<p>Attend, on average, two annual company management meetings.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Building Brand Equity:
 </b></p>
<p>Understand and able to communicate the kate spade new york brand aesthetic, brand philosophy and lifestyle to the sales team and customer.
 </p>
<p>Ensure visual merchandising directives are implemented efficiently as per direction and visual standards are maintained at all times.
 </p>
<p>Analyze and react to business trends regarding assortment, communicating sell-through, stock levels, opportunities to increase sales to DL.
 </p>
<p>Communicate to DL customer feedback and quality issues in order to improve customer service and sales.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Operational Excellence:
 </b></p>
<p>Perform and supervise store opening and closing procedures including counting register funds, completing bank deposits, opening and closing registers and securing facility.
 </p>
<p>Conduct regular store meetings to ensure accurate and consistent communication with employees.<br>
 </p>
<p></p>
<p>Maintain payroll budget and weekly hours to ensure appropriate sales floor coverage by reviewing store schedule and adjusting as necessary to meet the needs of the business.
 </p>
<p>Supervise operational responsibilities to ensure the timely and accurate processing of all markdowns, transfers, damages, shipping and receiving in order to maintain the integrity of the inventory.
 </p>
<p>Oversee loss prevention policies and procedure to be 100% compliant.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Qualifications:
</b></p>
<p><b>Education and Experience Required:
 </b></p>
<p>Minimum 3 years management experience in luxury goods or a comparable retail environment
 </p>
<p>College degree preferred
 </p>
<p>Prior luxury goods experience preferred<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Skills and Abilities Required:
 </b></p>
<p>Professional sales development and exceptional interpersonal skills
 </p>
<p>Strong leadership qualities and the ability to communicate effectively with all levels within the organization and our client base
 </p>
<p>Ability to analyze selling reports, identify business trends and react quickly to the needs of the business
 </p>
<p>Comfortable in making decisions and mediating conflict within a team-environment
 </p>
<p>Proficient in windows-based software such as excel, word and outlook<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Physical Requirements:
 </b></p>
<p>Available to work store schedule, as needed, including evenings and weekends
 </p>
<p>Standing for extended periods of time
 </p>
<p>Able to safely lift boxes up to 40 pounds
 </p>
<p>Comfortable climbing ladders<br>
</p>
