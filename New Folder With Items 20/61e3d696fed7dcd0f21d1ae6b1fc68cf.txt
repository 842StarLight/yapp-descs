<h2>Customer Marketing Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div><div>
<div><b>Job Summary</b></div>
<br>
<div>The Customer Marketing Manager is responsible for developing and maintaining customer communication and community development initiatives using various customer success and customer relationship management techniques. The Customer Marketing Manager will drive the strategy and manage the execution of Paycor customer community activities, such as in-person and virtual events. This role will partner with cross-functional areas of the organization to ensure continued brand awareness and product education are prevalent throughout the customer relationship lifecycle. He or She will focus on increased client engagement through events, community development and proactive communication with the customer base.</div>
<br>
<div><b>Essential Duties and Responsibilities</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Develop proactive communication strategies that nurture the customer relationship throughout the lifecycle</li>
<li>Establish and maintain the vision for the customer communities: online and in person</li>
<li>Educate other customer-facing functions (e.g. sales, product, customer success, client services) on the available programs and deployment of community activities</li>
<li>Serve as primary point for managing customer communication and engagement to establish coordination of message and outreach frequency</li>
<li>Identify strategies for embedding customer engagement into cross-functional areas, including customer success, services</li>
<li>Identify potential influencers and advocates from community activity and create scalable outreach to cultivate these individuals</li>
<li>Conduct periodic assessments of the current state of customer engagement activity to identify critical gaps in and develop a plan to address them</li>
<li>Communicate with external teams, clients and vendors regarding project deliverables and details.</li>
<li>Communicate creative needs by developing a brief and sharing it with marketing services so they can develop supporting materials such as emails, websites, videos, graphics, etc.</li>
</ul>
<div>
<b>Requirements</b>
</div>
<br>
<div>To perform this job successfully, an individual must be able to perform each essential duty satisfactorily. The requirements are representative of the knowledge, skill, and/or ability required.</div>
<ul>
<li>3+ years of customer engagement marketing</li>
<li>4-year business or marketing-related degree</li>
<li>Significant experience in B2B SaaS organizations, preferable in the Human Capital Management space</li>
<li>Proven complex project management experience</li>
<li>Demonstrated experience working cross-functionally and with clients, including with stakeholders at all levels</li>
<li>Experience with marketing automation, web analytics and email marketing solutions</li>
<li>Experience with Salesforce.com and Marketo preferred</li>
</ul>
