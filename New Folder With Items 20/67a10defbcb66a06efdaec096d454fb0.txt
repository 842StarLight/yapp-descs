<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
About the role</b></h2>
<p>
Zillow is looking for an experienced Salesforce Architect to join our team. This is a hands-on role where you will be required to write code for both classic and lightning development frameworks. As a Salesforce Architect, you should be a champion for solid system architecture and best practices. You should have a working knowledge of all the various Salesforce objects and how they interact for an efficient sales process. You should also know when to use out-of-the-box Salesforce offerings and when to use custom code.</p>
<ul>
<li>
You have experience architecting and developing complex technical solutions on the Salesforce platform using VisualForce, Apex and Javascript.</li>
<li>
You have a deep understanding of Salesforce architecture in terms of the data model, integration and design patterns.</li>
<li>
You have experience writing scalable, reusable code with well-written unit tests.</li>
<li>
You’re comfortable working with external systems and building integrations with Salesforce.</li>
<li>
Collaborate with sales teams, software engineers and data engineers through the entire software development process to deliver tools and features</li>
<li>
You have experience with large-scale system refactoring and data migrations.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Who you are</b></h2>
<ul>
<li>
7-10 years of experience developing in Salesforce.</li>
<li>
Prior experience with Lightning implementations and building Lightning components.</li>
<li>
Salesforce Architect certification or can get your certification.</li>
<li>
AWS experience a plus.</li>
<li>
Hands-on development experience with Apex, VisualForce and Lightning components</li>
<li>
2+ years’ experience leading a development team.</li>
<li>
Comprehensive understanding of Salesforce.com architecture and the various APIs.</li>
<li>
You have strong JavaScript skills and are comfortable working in modern front-end frameworks, such as React/Redux.</li>
<li>
Experience working in other programming languages, such as Python, Java, GoLang, etc.</li>
<li>
Comfortable working in an RDBMS</li>
<li>
Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science/related field OR equivalent experience</li>
</ul>
