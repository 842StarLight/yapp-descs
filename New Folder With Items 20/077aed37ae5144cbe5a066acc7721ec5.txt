<h2>Customer Service Representative</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Job Summary</b></p>
<p>Under the supervision of the Supply Chain Manager, working in coordination with the Sales, Operations, and Finance teams, this person will be responsible for daily customer service inquires, invoicing, accounts receivable oversight and daily order fulfillment processing . As a liaison between all company departments, this position will play a vital role in the success of Coffee Manufactory.</p>
<p><b>Activities &amp; Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul><li>Invoicing creation, issuance, and oversight</li></ul>
<ul>
<li>Assist with development, implementation, and day-to-day supervision of e-commerce and wholesale customer web portal.</li>
<li>New account creation into wholesale portal and activate new customers</li>
<li>Basic day-to-day customer service inquiries</li>
<li>Daily shipping label creation and customer tracking communications</li>
<li>Customer outreach and support between sales and production departments</li>
<li>Customer support in regard to accounts receivable</li>
<li>‪<b>Batch daily production reports for roasting and production staff</b>
</li>
<li>
<b>Maintains and develops a deep knowledge of coffee, tea and specialty food and beverages</b> to be communicated to customers on a need be basis.</li>
<li>Liaison between sales department, operations team and customers</li>
<li>Facilitate changes made to orders</li>
<li>Record customer service KPI’s and communicate to Supply Chain Manager</li>
<li>Stay up to date on current offerings and inventory</li>
<li>Assist in maintaining office filing and storage system</li>
<li>Purchase office supplies as needed</li>
<li>Assist Supply Chain Manager with administrative duties as requested</li>
<li>End of day file report to ensure all orders were fulfilled and processed</li>
</ul>

<ul>

<li>Be 18 years of age or older</li>
<li>Minimum two-years customer service specializing in order fulfillment and logistics</li>
<li>Minimum one-year specialty coffee experience</li>

<p><b>Coffee Manufactory Story: </b></p>
<p>We are a team of coffee explorers that have been around the industry for years. Coffee has taken us all across the globe and introduced us amazing people along the way.</p>
<p>Our immense focus on sourcing impacts and sets a standard for every part of our business.</p>
<p>We put the producer first and set prices directly at the producer level with the help of our sourcing partners. By buying directly we are able to build supply chains and help nurture long-term producer sustainability.</p>
<p>We believe that through key sourcing partnerships coffee quality will reach its highest potential. In order to ensure this, we are working on toward a more sustainable future for our producers.</p>
<p><b>Coffee Manufactory Philosophy: </b></p>
<p>We want to bring customers closer to farms, farms closer to roasters, roasters closer to baristas, and back again. Back to the roots of simple beans, great farming friendships, and precise roasting. Back to being good. Balanced. Clean.</p>
<p>Great connections made over great coffee. On every table, in every hand, everywhere. This is coffee. It’s a tool to get up. But it’s also a tool to sit down, and connect. Because over coffee, everyone has a story to tell.</p>
