<p><b>Responsibilities will include the following: </b></p>
<p>We are looking to hire an HVAC Estimator. Responsibilities will also include but not limited to reading construction drawings and cut sheets of HVAC units and determining total costs of projects and preparing bids. The position will also include choosing the appropriate materials, verifying prices, checking contractors, and calculating profits on a given projects. The HVAC estimator will also utilize effective communication skills to communicate with project managers, clients, customers, and other associates. In addition, meeting with contractors, builders, owners, and different coordinating engineers on the jobs to bid. Creating additions or changes on projects currently under contract as needed. Prepare the billing of contract jobs.</p>
