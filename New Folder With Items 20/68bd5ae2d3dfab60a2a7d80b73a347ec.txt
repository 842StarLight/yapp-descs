<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Overview

<div><div><div>Responsible for retrieving and processing equipment orders on a timely basis and for working with the various departments for order completion. Communicates with sales on the order status.
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities

<div><div><ul>

<li>Works with incentive compensation to identity discrepancies.
</li>
</ul></div></div>
