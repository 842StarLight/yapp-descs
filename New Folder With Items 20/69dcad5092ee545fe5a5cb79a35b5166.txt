<h2>Welcome Center Attendant-Chapel Hill-PT</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>
<p><b>POSITION SUMMARY:</b><br>
Under the supervision of the Membership Experience Director, is responsible for administering both point of sale transactions and transformational work done at the Welcome Center. This includes customer service, membership, registrations, and financial transactions for the Chapel Hill Carrboro YMCA. He/she will be responsible for performing those functions in accordance with the stated mission, values, principles, and practices established by the administrative staff, governing committees and Board of Directors of the YMCA of the Triangle Area.</p>
<p><b>
RESPONSIBILITIES:</b><br>
Responsibilities include but are not limited to:</p>
<ul>
<li>
To enthusiastically engage and greet each member/guests that come in the YMCA</li>
<li>
Build and maintain excellent and positive relationships with all staff, members, and guests by serving their needs and concerns while taking the initiative to provide exceptional customer service</li>
<li>
Provide assistance as required to members, program participants, the public, and staff.</li>
<li>
Properly use Personify software to complete sales orders that may include but are not limited to youth programs, memberships, and cash program registrations</li>
<li>
Understanding of Personify’ s financial functionality necessary to communicate with customers regarding their financial transactions</li>
<li>
Be accountable for revenue generated in Personify each shift including batch reconciliation</li>
<li>
The ability to promote the YMCA, its programs and their correlating staff team members
</li>
<li>Be aware of financial assistance offerings including handling financial assistance applications and transactions confidentially
</li>
<li>Attend trainings and meetings as required that include and not limited to First Aid/CPR, and Listen First</li>
<li>
Work closely with branch leaders to ensure the efficiency of Welcome Center policies and procedures are being met
</li>
<li>Answer telephone, providing information and routing calls according to procedure</li>
<li>
Work a variety of weekday, evening, and weekend shifts</li>
<li>
Preferred to be able to work a minimum weekly standard of 16-20 hours</li>
<li>
Attend meetings and Personify trainings necessary for maintaining/increasing job knowledge</li>
<li>
Monitor the flow of the Welcome Center to ensure all members are being served in a quality manner
</li>
<li>Provide tours to potential new members and uses <i>Listen First</i> skills before and during the tour. Follows branch protocols for prospective members and sales leads.
</li>
<li>Understand all Program offerings, pricing, business policies and procedures, and registration methods necessary to complete a customer transaction in the Association database</li>
<li>
Ensures the delivery of a high-quality Y Connect Program – focusing on connecting members to Y communities and facilitating a YMCA Experience utilizing the full VOICE of the YMCA.</li>
<li>
Have a basic working knowledge of the equipment on the wellness floor to support members when necessary.</li>
<li>
Other responsibilities as deemed necessary by supervisor</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
YMCA COMPETENCIES:</b><br>
<b>Mission Advancement:</b><b> </b>Accepts and demonstrates the Y’s values. Demonstrates a desire to serve others and fulfill community needs. Recruits volunteers and builds effective, supportive working relationships with them. Supports fundraising.
</p>
<p><b>Collaboration</b>: Works effectively with people of different backgrounds, abilities, opinions and perceptions. Builds rapport and relates well to others. Seeks first to understand the other person’s point of view and remains calm in challenging situations. Listens for understanding and meaning; speaks and writes effectively. Takes initiative to assist in developing others.
</p>
<p><b>Operational Effectiveness:</b> Makes sound judgments, and transfers learning from one situation to another. Embraces new approaches and discovers ideas to create a better member experience. Establishes goals, clarifies tasks, plans work and actively participates in meetings. Follows budgeting policies and procedures, and reports all financial irregularities immediately. Strives to meet or exceed goals and deliver a high value experience for members.
</p>
<p><b>Personal Growth:</b> Pursues self-development that enhances job performance. Demonstrates an openness to change and seeks opportunities in the change process. Accurately assesses personal feelings, strengths, and limitations and how they impact relationships. Has the functional and technical knowledge and skills required to perform well. Uses best practices and demonstrates up to date knowledge and skills in technology.</p>

<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Minimum Requirements</b></h2>
</div>
<div>
<p><b>QUALIFICATIONS:</b><br>
The position requires an understanding of and commitment to the total effort of the YMCA and the Christian Principles upon which it is founded. The ideal candidate:</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<ul>
<li>
Must be professional, genuine, nurturing, welcoming, courteous, friendly, and relate well to customers.</li>
<li>
Must be highly motivated, an effective problem solver and have a positive attitude.
</li>
<li>Strong personnel leadership skills supported by excellent communication skills and adaptability in a fast-paced environment</li>
<li>
Must have the ability to work independently, as well as cooperatively with others.</li>
<li>
Must have excellent written and verbal communication and customer service skills.
</li>
<li>Must have strong computer skills and be proficient in Microsoft Word, Excel, and Outlook.</li>
<li>
Have an understanding/familiarity with basic financial principles.
</li>
<li>Flexibility and ability to work weekday, evening, and weekend hours, as required.
</li>
<li>Strong organizational skills and the ability to perform in a fast-paced environment while meeting deadlines are essential.</li>
<li>
Maintain excellent relationships with all staff and be an effective liaison with professional directors and other staff striving to serve everyone.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
ESSENTIAL REQUIREMENTS:</b><br>
Requires corrected vision and hearing to normal range, or special accommodations made of sufficient nature for completion of assigned tasks. Ability to communicate clearly through speech and understanding of the English language. Ability to walk, stand, kneel, stoop and manual dexterity. Ability to lift a maximum of 40 pounds. Ability to adapt to change quickly, work under stressful conditions on a regular basis, and have responsibility for the health and safety of others.
</p>
