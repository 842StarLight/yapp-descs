<h2>Account Executive</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Position Summary:</b></p>
<p>
The Account Executive is primarily responsible for driving new account sales growth; they are experienced in selling to business (Legal Ops), Finance and technical buyers. Key skills and traits are being creative, energetic and a self-starter. In addition, they understand sales process, how to sell innovation and disruption through needs analysis and challenger/solution selling. The Account Executive can drive sales opportunities forward, minimize decision cycles and achieve on target Quota. Finally, they have in-depth knowledge of Legal Market needs, product value, key proof points and are viewed by prospects as a trusted advisor and guide through the buying process.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
This role will focus on clients located in the Midwest / South Central region of the U.S.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Responsibilities/ Essential Duties:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Own and achieve sales Quota in a manner that aligns with company values, objectives and practices</li>
<li>
Manage end to end sales cycle for prospects, keeping CRM up to date and accurate based on company guidelines and sales milestones process</li>
<li>
Responsible for the development and performance of all sales activities for assigned Accounts and\or territory</li>
<li>
Develops a business plan and sales strategy for the market that ensures attainment of company sales goals and profitability.</li>
<li>
Provides timely, accurate, competitive pricing on all completed prospect applications submitted for pricing and approval, while striving to maintain maximum profit margin.</li>
<li>
Creates and conducts proposals, presentations and RFP responses.</li>
<li>
Conducts contract negotiations related to business terms, ensuring alignment of contract to company guidelines and practices.</li>
<li>
Assists in the development and implementation of marketing plans as needed.</li>
<li>
Works closely with product and marketing groups to provide insights into market needs and emerging demands, to help inform market and product strategy and tactical planning</li>
</ul>
