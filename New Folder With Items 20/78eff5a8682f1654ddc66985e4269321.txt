<h2>National Sales Manager - West Region</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Job Title
</b></p>National Sales Manager - West Region
<p></p>
<p><b>Job Description
</b></p>
<p>Meredith Corporation's Local Media Group is seeking a West Region National Sales Manager who will be responsible for achieving national revenue and share goals for a regional group of Meredith television stations. The position will be based in either Portland, OR or Las Vegas, NV.
</p>
<p></p>
<p>This position collaborates with the national rep firm, Chief Revenue Officer and General Sales Managers to maintain and grow national revenue and share for assigned stations. The National Sales Manager interacts and utilizes all sales resources including research, traffic, web/mobile platforms, and integrated marketing as appropriate. Must possess strong leadership skills, highest level of customer service and sales skills with a proven successful track record of negotiating and selling all marketing platforms to national agencies.
</p>
<div></div>
<p><b>Essential Job Functions
</b></p>
<p><b>40% - </b>Works closely with station sales management and rep firm to generate revenue for stations while growing revenue and share utilizing special projects/programs and all digital platforms.Evaluates along with station sales management the input of the Traffic Department functions related to ordering, booking, airing, billing and collection of all national business.Works directly with station sales management and rep firm to develop sales strategies to maximize revenue and share for all national accounts.
</p>
<p><b>35% - </b>Performs training and sales negotiations at major National rep firm offices. Maintain close watch on competitive developments in the marketplace including the quality of competing National seller, pricing, and inventory. Works closely with NSA and rep firm to resolve preempts and posting for national agencies and clients. Maintain close contact with important National agencies and major National advertisers. Develop and maintain strong relationships with any local contacts associated with the National advertiser base.
</p>
<p><b>15% - </b>Supervise and direct the National Sales Assistants.Help with goal setting for all National sales personnel and for the total National sales effort.
</p>
<p><b>10% - </b>Works closely with the station sales management to price inventory. Helps to prepare and present quarterly Entitlement reports and development of station’s strategic sales plan.
</p>
<div></div>
<p><b>Minimum Qualifications and Job Requirements </b>| <i>All must be met to be considered.
</i></p>
<p>Education - College degree in Business Administration, Communications, or any related fields; or related experience.
</p>
<p>Experience - Minimum five years’ experience in Media Sales management preferred. National Sales experience also preferred. Previous experience working for a broadcast television advertising rep firm helpful but not required.
</p>
<p></p>
<p>Specific Knowledge, Skills and Abilities:
</p>
<ul>
<li>Must have technical proficiency with ratings and strong presentation skills.
</li>
<li>Knowledge of Microsoft Word, Excel, and Power Point needed.
</li>
<li>Knowledge of Wide Orbit Traffic and Media Sales, Medialine, and all other Cox Reps National Sales systems preferred.
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p>% Travel Required <i>(Approximate) </i>: 20% or as required
