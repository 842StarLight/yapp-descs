<h2>Principal Consultant – AWS Professional Level</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>47Lining, a Hitachi Vantara Company, helps customers build, operate and manage breathtaking “Data Machines” for their data-driven businesses.</p>
<p>
At 47Lining, we believe in building high performance teams who are passionate about what they do. We enjoy learning from each other and our clients, and our spirit of collaboration permeates everything we do.</p>
<p><b>
Job Summary:</b></p>
<p>
We are seeking experienced Principal Consultants to lead and manage industrial process optimization engagements. Engagements focus on optimization of customer industrial processes through application of IoT, edge computing and backhaul, cloud-native data lake, agile analytics, and data ops in Amazon Web Services. The ideal contributor will have a strong hands-on engineering background coupled with professional services delivery experience and a track record for partnering closely with customer teams to deliver successful engagements on-time and in-budget.</p>
<p><b>
Business Skills:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Vision to partner with customers on roadmap and liaise with sales and delivery teams to distill customer needs to actionable proposals and statements of work</li>
<li>Ability to identify and articulate sources of customer value from data &amp; advanced analytics. Ability to create roadmaps and decompose phases and steps to realize value.</li>
<li>Strong complex process, workflow, and business system requirements capture, decomposition, solutions integration, and cross-team management capabilities.</li>
<li>Ability to write high-level agile stories based on customer needs and break them down into actionable technical tasks for contributors</li>
<li>Experience managing and supporting technical contributors</li>
<li>Ability to work closely with customer teams and communicate effectively about scope, schedule, resources trade-offs</li>
<li>Experience creating deliverables that articulate business value of technical work</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Technical Skills:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Strong background in commercial-grade software development with Java, Python, SQL</li>
<li>Hands-on experience developing advanced analytics in SQL, R, Spark or similar tools</li>
<li>Experience with AWS architectures, development, and deployment. AWS Certified Solutions Architect Professional desired. AWS Certified Solutions Architect Associate required.</li>
<li>Strong experience with middle-tier web services systems decomposition, definition, and development (REST APIs)</li>
<li>Ability to work across all layers of an application, from back-end databases through UI</li>
<li>Proficient with SQL databases and knowledge of standard methodologies</li>
<li>Experience with enterprise-scale data warehouse technologies (Teradata, Oracle, SQL Data Warehouse, Vertica, Redshift)</li>
<li>Good working knowledge of Big Data technologies such as Hadoop, Hive, Spark</li>
<li>Experience managing applications in AWS and familiarity with core services including EC2, S3, RDS, etc.</li>
<li>Experience with deployment automation tools like Puppet, Chef, and Ansible</li>
<li>Exposure to networking &amp; load balancing solutions</li>
<li>Ability to work with development teams to deliver high-quality</li>
<li>Agile / Scrum development methodology</li>
<li>Technical system decomposition, architecture, design, and specification</li>
<li>Excellent documentation habits</li>
</ul>

</li>
