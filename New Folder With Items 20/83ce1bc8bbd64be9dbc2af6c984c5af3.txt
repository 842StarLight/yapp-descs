<h2>Commercial Contracts Administrator 2</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>General Summary:</p>
<p>Serves as the primary customer contact for integration and international transactions. Responsible for quotes, and working extensively with the Sales or bid capture managers. Builds and maintains customer relationships through ongoing communications on order status and order inquiry resolution. Performs order entry functions for integration and international sales and US Government contracts. Additionally, assists Export Compliance Manager with license requirements and documents. Follows all Astronics Test Systems? policies and procedures.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>Principal Duties and Responsibilities:</p>
<ul>
<li>Ensures proper quoting and order entry for new purchase orders for integration, international and US Government purchases</li>
<li>In coordination with the Export Manager, administers export licensing procedures and requirements</li>
<li>Ensures company compliance with US Export Regulations/requirements, creates export documents and coordinates the licensing requirements for international shipments. May assist Shipping department in the coordination with carriers/freight forwarders and customs officials regarding international product distribution.</li>
<li>Performs ongoing updates to customer database covering new orders, scheduled shipment dates and tracking numbers for order fulfillment</li>
<li>Manages the quoting process for customers</li>
<li>Maintains files and documentation on quoting and order activities</li>
<li>Monitors and maintains customer?s portals and certifications</li>
<li>Monitors product delivery schedules and communicates with customers regarding changes to customers purchase orders, such as delivery date, out of scope requirements, quantity and or pricing changes</li>
<li>Ensures that all quotes are issued to customers in accordance Astronics Test Systems' policies</li>
</ul>
<p><br>
Job Specifications:<br>
Typically 5 or more years' related work experience with customer service, contracts administration, knowledgeable in EAR/ITAR Regulations, export documentation, or an equivalent combination of education and experience. A Bachelor's degree in a related field is preferred. Must have competencies in basic Microsoft office software including Excel and Word. Must be a US Citizen.</p>
