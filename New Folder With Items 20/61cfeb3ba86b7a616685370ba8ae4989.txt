<h2>Enterprise Sales Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Our team is looking for a talented, motivated, and high-performing <b>Enterprise Sales Specialists </b>to join our team. Qualified candidates can reside in either the <b>Nashville, Huntsville or Birmingham </b>area. The Enterprise Sales Specialists is a customer facing position that articulates the Neopost offering and identifies potential areas of impact we can make within an organization. This role works alongside assigned sales teams actively participating to discover &amp; diagnose GAPS that Neopost solutions can potentially impact. Additionally, the ESA will create and document a business case that contains qualified &amp; measured business impact, customer specific workflows, while providing compelling reasons for a customer to move forward with the sales opportunity</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
As an Enterprise Sales Specialists, you will enjoy a highly competitive salary, commission and bonus plan. As well, Neopost provides many fun and engaging incentives thru out the year culminating with an annual all expenses paid, annual sales performance trip to extraordinary locations. Neopost also provides matching 401k program, benefits plan, travel reimbursement and sales education and professional development programs. Neopost also provides excellent opportunities for advancement and professional growth.</p>
