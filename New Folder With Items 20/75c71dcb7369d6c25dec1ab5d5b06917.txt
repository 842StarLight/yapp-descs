<h2>Digital Marketing Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p><b>In this role, you will </b>spearhead the keyword research, strategy, and execution of SEO campaigns to grow the online presence of the GOJO and PURELL brands, with an emphasis on in-depth data analysis and web content optimization. You will collaborate with sales leaders and marketing teams across the enterprise as you create and implement recommendations to optimize user flow and experience.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Essential Job Duties
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Develop SEO strategies that drive traffic and ROI for our company and select customers
</li>
<li>Analyze search engine results pages (SERP) and organic traffic patterns to provide recommendations for content creation and website optimizations
</li>
<li>Regularly analyze data using third party tools to understand user behavior and provide insight into the topics that drive interest and engagement
</li>
<li>Develop ongoing plan to monitor and measure site performance, while reporting on campaign results
</li>
<li>Track progress against key competitors
</li>
<li>Work within the web team to optimize website, drive organic traffic, and increase conversions based on data
</li>
<li>Align available resources to enterprise and business unit strategic priorities
</li>
<li>Identifies Key Performance Indicators for website content and digital campaigns
</li>
<li>Provides data reporting via reports, dashboards/visualizations
</li>
<li>Collaborate and create connections between GOJO sales and marketing participants
</li>
<li>Monitor industry trends<br>
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Education and Experience Required
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelors' degree in Data Science, Marketing, or Business preferred
</li>
<li>2-4 years of experience in SEO, data analytics and digital marketing strategy preferred</li>
</ul>
