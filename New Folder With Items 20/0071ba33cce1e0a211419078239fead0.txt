<h2>Certified Financial Planner</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p>Case Central, LLC, is seeking an individual holding the Certified Financial Planner certification. This position entails servicing existing clients; this is a salaried position there are no direct sales involved.</p>
<p><b>
Specific responsibilities include but are not limited to:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Service insurance agents, investment advisors, registered representatives, and tax professionals by providing financial planning support.</li>
<li>Handle day-to-day communication with agents, advisors, reps, and internal sales team to coordinate product recommendations, portfolio management and development of financial plans.</li>
<li>Investment analysis utilizing various software tools, mainly Morningstar and in-house software (stocks, bonds, funds, ETFs, etc.).</li>
<li>Assist investment team with various projects.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Job requirements include but are not limited to:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Experience with annuities, insurance, securities, investment advisory products and services and basic tax rules.</li>
<li>Experience in developing and implementing financial plans.</li>
<li>Participate in the implementation of systems and processes.</li>
<li>Work to anticipate potential problems and compliance issues, provide problem resolution, and escalate items to management as applicable.</li>
<li>Ability to multi-task, manage competing priorities and operate efficiently in high-volume entrepreneurial environment.</li>
<li>Ability to collaborate and interact with multiple business units.</li>
<li>Ability to clearly and simply communicate complex topics to a variety of internal and external customers.</li>
<li>Provide a high level of professionalism with internal and external clients via face-to-face, phone, and e-mail.</li>
<li>Must be well organized and detail oriented</li>
<li>Proficient in Microsoft Office Suite</li>
</ul>
