<h2>Assistant Manager (Multi Site, Floater)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Assistant Store Manager: </b><i>Please review details below</i></p>
<p><b><i>Company Overview: </i></b></p>
<p>Prime Group is a real estate owner-operator focused on acquiring and adding value to a growing portfolio of self-storage facilities located throughout the United States. The company combines an off-market approach to acquisitions with institutional management methods. Headquartered in Saratoga Springs, New York, Prime Group owns and manages over $1.6 billion of self-storage facilities. Prime Group is the nation’s largest private owner of self-storage in the United States, with a portfolio of approximately 220 self-storage facilities located in 24 states.</p>
<p><b><i>Position Overview: This position will cover multiple sites, as a Assistant Manager / Floater.</i></b></p>
<p>As the Assistant Manager with Prime Storage Group, you will be responsible for assisting in the day to day operation of one or more self-storage facilities, including renting units, selling merchandise, renting trucks, interacting with customers, and maintaining the property overall. Our employees are the first line representative to the customer. The Assistant Manager must have the ability to work face-to-face with customers and build excellent relationships while enhancing your customer service and sales skills and experience.</p>
<p><b><i>Job Responsibilities: </i></b></p>
<ul>
<li>Renting self-storage units, parking space, and other related products;</li>
<li>Converting telephone and walk-in inquiries into rentals;</li>
<li>Maintain Account Receivables through the pursuit of collections, posting of payments and processing, and delivery of daily bank deposits accurately and timely;</li>
<li>Upsell moving products and assist in unit sizing decisions;</li>
<li>Greeting customers promptly, enthusiastically and professionally;</li>
<li>Knowing and presenting the features and benefits of the property at all opportunities;</li>
<li>Resolves customer issues in a timely and effective manner;</li>
<li>Ensures that company standards of cleanliness and appearance are met;</li>
<li>Inspect the property, including performing lock checks;</li>
<li>Showing customers our self-storage units;</li>
<li>Perform other duties as assigned.</li>
</ul>
<p><b><i>Qualifications: </i></b></p>
<ul>
<li>High school diploma or GED, required;</li>
<li>A valid state Driver’s License;</li>
<li>2+ years of customer service experience;</li>
<li>Able to work with minimal supervision;</li>
<li>A professional, positive, team-oriented attitude;</li>
<li>Be proficient with a computer (including Microsoft Office) and be willing to learn a proprietary software that manages rentals, payments, and occupancy reports;</li>
<li>Excellent organizational skills;</li>
</ul>
<p><b><i>Requirements: </i></b></p>
<ul>
<li>Willing to work a flexible schedule, including weekends;</li>
<li>Ability to travel to multiple facilities;</li>
<li>Must be able to perform the physical requirements of the job, including daily walks of the property, cleaning the units, public areas and the property, opening and closing the storage doors (up to 50lbs), and similar light activities as needed;</li>
<li>Ability to stand for extended periods of time;</li>
<li>Ability to work in both indoor and outdoor, and during varying weather conditions;</li>
<li>Manual hand dexterity required to operate POS, copiers and complete customer paperwork;</li>
<li>Must have a valid Driver’s license;</li>
<li>Must be willing to complete pre-employment background check;</li>
</ul>
<p><b><i>What We Offer: </i></b></p>
<ul>
<li>Competitive rate of pay plus an excellent bonus structure;</li>
<li>Health insurance, dental, life, vision, short-term disability, and long-term disability insurance programs also available;</li>
<li>Investment opportunities through our 401(k) Plan;</li>
<li>Paid time off and paid holidays.</li>
</ul>
<p>Job Type: Full-time</p>
<p>Salary: $12.00 to $14.00 /hour</p>
