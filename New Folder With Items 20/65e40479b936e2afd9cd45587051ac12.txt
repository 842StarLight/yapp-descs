<h2>Product Manager, Managed Account Infrastructure</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Your Opportunity
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div>This position is in the Managed Account, Alternative Investment, and Structured Product Solutions group, within the Asset Management Client Solutions organization. Our team supports the separately managed account, alternative investment (hedge and feeder funds, Reg D private offerings and advisor controlled funds) and structured product (structured CDs, notes and buffered notes) platforms at Schwab with the goal of maintaining Schwab’s leadership position and to continue to add value to Clients, third-party product providers and Schwab’s bottom line.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
As an individual contributor Product Manager you will be working in a team oriented environment that helps support the continued growth of our multi-billion dollar managed account, alternative investment, and structured product platforms that we offer to Schwab’s retail and institutional customers. Specifically, this position will focus on working with our existing colleagues and business partners to understand their needs as they relate to supporting and enhancing our existing products and platforms and identifying opportunities to improve our offering by creating efficiencies and scale to position us for future growth.</div>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>What you’re good at
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<ul>
<li>Supporting a Morningstar hosted reporting solution that is used to produce 450+ unique pieces of client materials each quarter.</li>
<li>
Supporting systems management functions including: data management, defining business requirements to make improvements, and other necessary documentation, testing, and post-production support for end users.</li>
<li>
Utilizing Workfront.com cloud based workflow software to manage asset manager related Schwab content available on internal and external websites to ensure accuracy and for performing annual certification.</li>
<li>
Supporting ad hoc and regular monthly management reporting by using analytical skills to identify trends to support strategic decision-making.</li>
<li>
Exercising excellent independent decision-making and problem solving skills.</li>
<li>
Developing creative approaches to supporting products and services that lend themselves to scale, efficiency and profitability.</li>
<li>
Build and maintain a strong network of internal and external business partners to address challenges, opportunities and solutions</li>
<li>
Cross-train on other team functions as needed. Responsibilities may vary based on department need and priorities.</li>
</ul>
