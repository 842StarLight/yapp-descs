<b>Main Responsibilities:</b><br>
Review customer specifications to determine the appropriate Rotork controls product for their application.
<br>Generate submittal documentation including WD’s, product drawings, specification deviations and clarifications
<br>Engineer nonstandard solutions to meet unique customer requirements.
<br>Design adaptions to couple valve and actuator.
<br>Generate BOM’s, assembly instructions, test instructions and user manuals for nonstandard products.
<br>Implement product design changes and modifications. Non-standard product requests (NSPR)
<br>Implement company Engineering Changes as pertains to local product manufacturing to maintain alignment with design authority.
<br>Provide technical support for electric valve actuators including sales service and production.
<br>Provide support on the use of software utilities for configuration and diagnostics of valve actuator products.
<br>Coordinate/develop technical documentation and training materials as needed
<br><br>
<b>Candidate Specification:</b><br>
<b>Requirements:</b><br>
Four year Mechanical engineering degree preferred
<br>Experience with installation and commissioning of electronic industrial equipment
<br>Ability to use electronic test equipment
<br>Ability to read wiring schematics/diagrams
<br>Understanding of motor controls and electro mechanical machines.
<br>PC literate, Working knowledge of Microsoft Office applications, Word, Excel, Access, PowerPoint etc.
<br>Good communication and organization skills
<br>Limited travel required (estimated 5-10% max) both national and international
<br><br>
<b>Experience/knowledge in the following areas desirable:</b><br>
Experience with third party certification agencies such as UL/TUV/CSA/FM
<br>Experience in industrial automation
<br>Generation of basic electrical schematics
<br>Mechanical design
<br>Electrical motor controls
