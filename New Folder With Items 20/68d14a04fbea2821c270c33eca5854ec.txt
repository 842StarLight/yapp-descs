<b>Job Overview:</b><br>
As an Ivalua Account Executive you will be focused on customer acquisition within your assigned territory of named Federal agency accounts. You will have a proven track record of exceeding quota in SaaS/Cloud application sales in Procurement or Supply Chain Domains. An Ivalua AE should be a motivated and tenacious self-starter who is comfortable selling to senior level executives in Procurement, Finance, and IT.
<br><br>
<b>Responsibilities:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Prospect, forecast, resource allocation and planning of new business deals</li>
<li>Develop and execute on a strategic territory plan to deliver maximum revenue potential</li>
<li>Evangelize the Ivalua vision within key Fortune 1,000 accounts in the Eastern US Territory</li>
<li>Working in collaboration with solution consulting, management, and ecosystem business partners, sell Ivalua's value proposition to senior level executives</li>
<li>Forecast sales activity and revenue achievement in CRM tool, while creating and maintaining satisfied customers</li>
<li>Achieve quarterly sales objectives and forecast sales activity accurately</li>
<li>Develop add-on business with existing accounts</li>
<li>Champion you customer's implementations to ensure great success</li>
</ul>
<b>What Ivalua is looking for:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>5 to 10 years of experience in Solution Software Sales</li>
<li>Bachelor's degree from a reputable institution</li>
<li>Procurement or Supply Chain Domain knowledge – must be able to have credible business conversations with Procurement and Financial executives</li>
<li>Verifiable track record of consistent overachievement of quota's and revenue goals</li>
<li>Experience in managing complex sales-cycles and demonstrated ownership of territory and account management</li>
<li>Outstanding communication (written and oral) negotiation, presentation, and solution selling skills to manage complex organizations</li>
<li>Demonstrates the ability to work in an environment where growth and change is the only constant</li>
<li>Proficient in Microsoft Word, PowerPoint, and Excel</li>
<li>Ideal Background – Procurement or Supply Chain consulting presales and moved to a quota-carrying sales role</li>
</ul>
