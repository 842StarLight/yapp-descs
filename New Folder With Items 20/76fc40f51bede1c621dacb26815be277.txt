<p>Description</p>

<div>
<div>
<p>Milliman, Inc. is recognized as one of the top firms providing professional services to the insurance industry. Milliman IntelliScript is a specialty software-as-a-service (SaaS) practice of Milliman that provides valuable real-time data and underwriting decision support tools, primarily to health and life insurers. With deep understanding and expertise in the life and health insurance markets, IntelliScript offers an innovative suite of products to interpret and deliver electronic medical data (such as prescription histories, diagnoses, treatment data, etc.) to help clients make effective underwriting and risk management decisions. IntelliScript has averaged 30% annual sales growth over the last several years.</p>
<p>
As part of Milliman, IntelliScript offers a unique, entrepreneurial, balanced environment, as well as competitive compensation and benefits. We are looking for collaborative people who are ready to be part of our long-term success. Visit our LinkedIn page for a closer look at our company and culture: Milliman IntelliScript on LinkedIn</p>
<br>
<p>
<b>
Opportunity Highlights</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Responsibility for real projects with proven technology</li>
<li>Innovation and ideas are always welcome at IntelliScript, and success is highly visible</li>
<li>Enjoy autonomy to work independently with strong trust and communication among the team</li>
<li>Milliman demonstrates work-life balance through a variety of perks and programs, as well as opportunities for training and networking.</li>
</ul>
<p><br>
<b>Expectations</b></p>
<p>
Offering tremendous ROI, the Milliman IntelliScript Irix™ rules engine provides our clients with intelligent analysis of prescription histories and other medical data. Irix enables life and health insurance underwriters to make better decisions by generating comprehensive, customized reports that reduce the time needed for risk analysis.</p>
<p>
As part of the Medical Data (Dx) team, the Technical Specialist will be responsible for supporting team processes at macro and micro levels. This is the perfect role for a person who enjoys collaboration as well as opportunity to work autonomously.</p>
<br>
<p>
<b>
Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Implement Medical Data client study documentation
<ul>
<li>Create Rule Results spreadsheets with study details and stats</li>
<li>Enter client decisions / other table changes and validate tables</li>
<li>Recommend changes to Logic Maps (Visio) based on changes to rules</li>
<li>Create and maintain documentation of processes and procedures</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>Research and develop Medical Data specifications for system changes</li>
<li>Business analysis and documentation to fulfill internal and external requests, i.e. research and answer client questions relating to rules</li>
<li>Help us to continue to automate whenever possible, such as creating/maintaining macros to automate documentation or other processes</li>
<li>Use Microsoft SQL</li>
</ul>
<p>Create and run reports on rules and applicant decisions</p>
<ul>
<li>Analyze rules and impact of changes</li>
<li>Identify trends in data</li>
<li>Investigate codes and incidence for future bucketing</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Develop in-depth knowledge of our industry and business
<ul>
<li>Understand IntelliScript business (Product demo, study, rules)</li>
<li>Learn underwriting process and concepts by attending courses and conferences</li>
<li>Use SQL to answer business questions</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>Learn how to use InRule (rules engine software), in order to:
<ul>
<li>Define and document Medical Data rule changes</li>
<li>Review changes implemented by others</li>
<li>Run/write SQL queries to find test cases</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>Proactively move issues to resolution</li>
<li>Explore new product opportunities, research relevant technical implementation issues</li>
<li>Use macros in Excel and Visio to ensure that client-requested changes to rule decisions are consistent and sensible</li>
</ul>
