<h2>Order Administrator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>LEWA-Nikkiso America, Inc. is seeking an Order Administrator to support internal and external customers through scheduling and processing orders.</p>
<p><b>Responsibilities: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Processes LNA, Inc. pump &amp; system orders in a timely manner and clarifies all purchase order requirements with the appropriate teams to avoid errors.</li>
<li>Inputs data for orders into appropriate software.</li>
<li>Creates, updates, and distributes a production schedule for all purchase orders.</li>
<li>Processes basic document submittals to external customers</li>
<li>Maintains key customer information, interfacing across the Company to resolve issues and ensure on-time delivery of the order, shipment, dispatch time, carrier, and route.</li>
<li>Maintains technical knowledge by attending Company training sessions and suggests continuous improvement techniques.</li>
<li>Contributes to team effort by communicating sales and service issues with appropriate employees and participates in required meetings.</li>
<li>Cross trains with coworkers to share knowledge and skills.</li>
<li>Maintains an updated log of approved customer terms &amp; conditions.</li>
</ul>
<ul><li>Actively demonstrates the LEWA values of inclusion, ownership, respect, integrity, collaboration, and continuous improvement.</li></ul>
<p><b>Required Experience &amp; Knowledge: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Associates degree and three to five years experience in a customer service role, or equivalent work experience</li>
<li>Strong attention to technical and commercial details</li>
<li>Excellent computer skills with MS Office experience and high level of proficiency in MS Excel, Project and Outlook</li>
<li>Strong organizational skills with the ability to multitask</li>
<li>Excellent written and verbal communication skills</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Preferred Skills and Experience: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>SAP Business One experience</li>
<li>Experience in a manufacturing environment</li>
</ul>
