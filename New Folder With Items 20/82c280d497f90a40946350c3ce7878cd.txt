<p><b>Job Description Summary:
</b></p>Final Mile Services specializes in professional and cost effective final mile deliveries throughout the United States. With over 100 distribution centers nationwide and 1 million square feet of warehouse space, we are able to provide commercial, business to business and home delivery services. Our solutions range from jobs site deliveries for construction and remodeling projects to retail and home deliveries including appliances, furniture, mattresses and many other products. Our network is growing and we are flexible to fit every client’s need.
<p></p>
<p><b>Job Description:
</b></p>
<p>As an Operations Supervisor, you will be responsible for managing the daily operations of an account, which includes maintaining revenue generation, safety, equipment maintenance, payroll and billing, and ensuring Customer Value Delivery. The environment is challenging, fast-paced, and very rewarding
</p>
<p>Qualifications:
</p>
<ul><li>Four-year college degree from an accredited educational institution with a preferred GPA of 2.70 or higher, or
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Minimum of 3 years continuous employment with J.B. Hunt demonstrating management potential, or
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Minimum of 3 years recent supervisory experience outside of J.B. Hunt, or
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Military Non-Commissioned Officer with at least 3 years of supervisory experience (i.e., minimum E-4).
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Preference will be given to candidates who are willing to relocate for future opportunities within the company.
</li></ul>
<p></p>
<p>Responsibilities:
</p>
<ul><li>Dispatch and supervise a group of drivers
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Maintain performance metrics and participate in customer meetings
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Monitor and train drivers to promote company safety culture
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Route or pre-plan delivery of freight to ensure on-time service
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Involvement in the financial analysis as it relates to the specific profit-and-loss statements
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Monitor compliance with safety and Department of Transportation regulations
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Coordinate with maintenance support personnel to manage service of all account equipment
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Reduce driver turnover by utilizing JB Hunt’s Driver Dedicated program
</li></ul>
