<b>
Job Description</b>
</div>
<div>
<div>Offer Management’s job is to increase profitable revenue and support Sales by:<br>
<ul>
<li>Performing detailed, customer-specific pricing analyses to formulate highly competitive and profitable service offerings and to help coordinate final customer contracts to support these offerings.</li>
<li>Develop and evaluate complex pricing strategies and models to ensure long-term profitable growth.</li>
<li>Providing consultations for unique product pricing, special pricing and approvals.</li>
<li>Act as Sales’ trusted advisor for offer definition and deal negotiation in support of accelerating sales productivity.</li>
<li>Develop persuasive Bid (RFP/RFQ/RFI) responses aimed at major accounts that focus on the customer's needs and why CenturyLink is the best choice for meeting those needs.</li>
<li>Navigate and negotiate complex contract requests.</li>
</ul>
