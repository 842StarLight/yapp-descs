<b>Who we are:</b><br>
DoubleVerify is the leader in digital performance solutions, improving the impression quality and audience impact of digital advertising. Built on best practices, DoubleVerify solutions create value for media buyers and sellers by bringing transparency and accountability to the market, ensuring ad viewability, brand safety, fraud protection, accurate impression delivery and audience quality across campaigns to drive performance. Learn more at doubleverify.com ( http://doubleverify.com/ ).

About the job
<br><br>
As the Product Manager, Advertiser &amp; Agency Standards, you will contribute to the growing Exposure &amp; Engagement team by managing the implementation of viewability measurement standards. The ideal candidate will possess an in-depth understanding of the advertising ecosystem and will be rolling out products and services to capitalize on new opportunities. This position offers an exciting opportunity to impact a product used by a majority of our clients, driving many ongoing strategic initiatives within the organization.
<br><br>
What you'll do
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Research, groom, and manage: viewability, exposure and engagement measurement product requirements.</li>
<li>Work closely with the measurements engineering team and other product teams to execute the product vision and roadmap.</li>
<li>Represent DV in front of advertiser and agency clients to build and implement new solutions that maximize value for our clients.</li>
<li>Keep abreast of the evolving platform/browser capabilities and restrictions to ensure that DV's measurement services are comprehensively available throughout the major areas of the online advertising ecosystem</li>
<li>Become a subject matter expert for your product line; support client service, sales and business development teams to drive the success of your products and revenue for DoubleVerify.</li>
<li>Lead product acceptance efforts to ensure accurate, high-quality, on-time product enhancements.</li>
<li>Analyze and document the performance of new product rollouts and enhancements for internal and external stakeholders.</li>
</ul>

<li>Bachelor's degree or higher. Computer Science or related technical fields (STEM) is a plus.</li>
