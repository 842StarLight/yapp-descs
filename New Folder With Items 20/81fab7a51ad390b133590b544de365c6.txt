<h2>Account Executive</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Well established digital marketing agency for the real estate industry is seeking to add an inside sales rep to its sales acquisition team. Headquartered in Sorrento Valley, the company provides an industry-leading digital marketing and social media marketing solutions to real estate professionals across the nation.</p>
<p>We are seeking to hire an inside sales rep with at least 6 months of inside sales experience. Current Sales Development Reps (SDR) who are seeking to close their own deals are welcome to apply. Comprehensive paid training provided - career growth opportunities available</p>
<p><b>Account Executive Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Inside Sales Rep will contact sales prospects nationwide via outbound phone calls and responding to inbound lead sources</li>
<li>Schedule online webinar presentations to educate customers on product offerings and services</li>
<li>Follow up with prospects via telephone or email in a timely manner</li>
<li>Maintain sales metrics with goal of exceeding quotas</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Account Executive Requirements</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Prior B2B inside sales preferred</li>
<li>Current SDR reps seeking to close their own deals a big plus</li>
<li>Four-year college degree</li>
<li>Strong desire to begin a career and not seeking a "job"</li>
<li>Friendly open personality willing to learn and desire to grow</li>
</ul>

Competitive, health and dental insurance, generous vacation, a 401(k) plan with company match, and other great benefits.</p>
