<p><i><b>Entry Level Diesel Mechanics welcome to apply.</b></i></p>
<p>Founded in 1965, <b>Rush Enterprises, Inc</b>. owns and operates the largest network of commercial vehicle dealerships in the United States, representing truck and bus manufacturers including Peterbilt, International, Hino, Isuzu, Kalmar, Ford, Mitsubishi Fuso, Blue Bird, Elkhart, Collins and IC Bus. The company's dealerships are strategically located in high traffic areas on or near major highways in 20 states throughout the United States. These one-stop centers offer an integrated approach to meeting customer needs - from sales of new and used vehicles and equipment to aftermarket parts, service and body shop operations plus a wide array of financial services, including financing, insurance, leasing and rental. Rush Enterprises' operations also provide vehicle up-fitting, custom modifications, chrome accessories and tires.</p>
<p><b>Skillset: </b> Peterbilt, PACCAR, CAT, Cummins ISB ISL ISX, Eaton &amp; Allison Transmissions. Experience using Davie4, Cummins Insite, CAT ET, Allison DOC, RoadRanger software. Quickly and efficiently diagnose engine performance complaints, DEF &amp; Aftertreatment Emission System components. Diagnose and repair drivability complaints: Brakes, S-Cams, Air Brake systems, Air Compressors, PTO, Leveling Valves, Drivetrain, and Alignment repairs. Ability to fully document steps followed to diagnose symptoms and make repairs using SAP dealer management software.</p>
