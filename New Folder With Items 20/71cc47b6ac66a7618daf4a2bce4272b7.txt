Oil States is currently looking for qualified candidates for the position of Master Control Systems Coordinator.
<br>
<br>
POSITION SUMMARY:<br>
Responsible for efficiently compiling and controlling all manufacturing, vendor and customer documentation generated for product lines. Develop and maintain the Arlington facility's Master Control processes and systems.<br>
<br>
ESSENTIAL DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES:<br>
<ul>
<li>Review departmental transactions of documentation in accordance with Document Control Guidelines</li>
<li>Maintain and update Master Control users, workgroups, routes, security access, vaults and document types.</li>
<li>Verify info card fields, launch approval routes, monitor approver sign off and transfer files upon approval.</li>
<li>Communicate with all levels of management regarding the creation/replacing/updating of documentation. Generate document usage histories and reports.</li>
<li>Interact with Engineering and Project Management in the release of documentation.</li>
<li>Interact with customer document control departments.</li>
<li>Coordinate and consult with other OSI divisional Document Control departments.</li>
<li>Support QA and represent Document Control during audits (ISO, API, Customer, etc.)</li>
<li>Submit documentation internally, as well as requests from customers and vendors.
</li>
<li>Data entry of project files.</li>
<li>Assist in reviewing contract data during the Sales and proposal process, as it relates to Document Control requirements.</li>
<li>Attend Project, Contract Review, Production meetings, etc. when asked, and or required.</li>
<li>Train personnel on Master Control processes, revisions to the processes, or upgrades to the system.</li>
<li>Periodically audit Master Control Processes to insure they are being utilized to its most efficient extent, and personnel are following the process in the order it was designed to function.</li>
</ul>
<br>
OTHER DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES:<br>
<ul>
<li>Understand and comply with ISO process requirements in regard to document control. Update procedures as necessary.</li>
<li>Understand and comply with Corporate Policies, Procedures and Standards in regards to gathering, maintaining and sharing information from and with outside parties.</li>
<li>Attend corporate sponsored Document Control trainings and facilitate divisional trainings.</li>
<li>Provide standard support and troubleshooting for users in regard to Master Control applications and Document Control procedures.</li>
<li>Miscellaneous office duties.</li>
<li>Travel when required to attend Master Control functions.</li>
<li>Contribute to the success of meeting Document Control Goals, objectives, and monitor the process measurements in order to achieve the OSI overall Goals and Objectives.</li>
<li>Lead others in a team atmosphere.</li>
<li>Perform other duties assigned by supervisor.</li>
</ul>
<br>
QUALIFICATION REQUIREMENTS:<br>
<ul>
<li>High School Diploma or equivalent</li>
<li>At least one year of experience in a similar or related function.</li>
<li>Experience working in a document control system/database preferred.</li>
<li>Experience training large and small groups preferred.</li>
<li>Good understanding of quality operation and office procedures.</li>
<li>Must be self-motivated, able to work with teams and individually.</li>
<li>Must be detail oriented and able to sustain long, sometimes monotonous data processing tasks.</li>
<li>Strong working knowledge of office equipment, computers, and software (Microsoft Office related products). AutoCad, Acrobat experience helpful.</li>
<li>Understanding or working knowledge of Network server systems.</li>
<li>Understanding of Design and Manufacturing documentation process.</li>
<li>Good verbal and written communication skills.</li>
<li>Ability to prioritize and manage multiple tasks, and follow detailed instructions in an efficient manner under minimal supervision.</li>
<li>Basic mathematical skills.</li>
</ul>
