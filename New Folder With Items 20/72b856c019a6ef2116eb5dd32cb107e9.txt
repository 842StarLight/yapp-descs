<h2>Account Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Overview
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div>EXL Service (NASDAQ: EXLS) is a global end-to-end Insurance Administration provider with Life, Annuity and Health operations, headquartered in New York. EXL was incorporated in 1999 and has since grown to be one of the leading insurance service provider companies with its 17,500 employees including 7000+ talented insurance domain experts and 300+ FLMI, CLU, CPCU, ChFC, RHU, REBC, Series 6 and Series 26 licensed professionals, 40+ service delivery centers across US, India, The Philippines, Malaysia, Czech Republic, Romania and Bulgaria.</div>
<div>
The LifePRO division of EXL is a team dedicated to the development and implementation of the EXL LifePRO insurance administration system for Life, Health and Annuity Business. LifePRO streamlines policy administration for insurance companies through features such as a flexible product engine, real-time customer service and extensive agent compensation support.</div>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<p><b>Job Responsibilities:</b></p>
<p>
The Account Manager is responsible for leading account planning and support with the LifePRO clients. The account manager is the lead point of contact for all client matters. They anticipate the client's needs, work within the company to ensure deadlines for client are met, and help the client succeed.The ultimate goal is to drive service quality, assure client satisfaction and retention, and to establish and maintain a consistent opportunity pipeline.<br>
</p>
<ul>
<li>
Operating as the lead point of contact for any and all matters specific to the assigned account. Building and maintaining strong, long-lasting client relationships</li>
<li>
Generate and manage leads that result in revenue producing opportunities for the company; Play a major role in negotiating the contracts to maximize profits</li>
<li>
Coordinates the involvement of company personnel, including support, service, and management resources, in order to meet account performance objectives and client expectations</li>
<li>
Proactively assesses, clarifies, and validates customer needs on an ongoing basis</li>
<li>
Leads solution development efforts that best address customer needs, while coordinating the involvement of all necessary company personnel</li>
<li>
Maintain the source of truth for information about the assigned accounts</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Accountabilities and Performance Measures:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Completes strategic customer account plans that meet company standards.</li>
<li>
Maintain high customer satisfaction ratings that meet company standards and develop remediation plans and execute upon those plans when required</li>
<li>
Maintain a viable opportunity pipeline</li>
<li>
Have a key role in the annual conference</li>
<li>
Oversees contract requests and matters as well as client invoicing</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Organizational Alignment:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Reports to the Account Management Lead</li>
<li>
Enlists the support of sales specialists, implementation resources, service resources, subject matter experts, and other sales and management resources as needed</li>
<li>
Closely coordinates company executive involvement with client management</li>
<li>
Works closely with the application SMEs to provide responses to client inquiries and requests ultimately ensuring customer satisfaction and problem resolution</li>
</ul>
</div></div></div>
