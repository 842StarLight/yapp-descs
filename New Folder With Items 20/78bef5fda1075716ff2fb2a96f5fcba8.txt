<div>The Oracle Application Technical Lead is responsible for the leading design and development of solutions for Oracle EBS and integrated tools/technologies. This role will be responsible for strategic and tactical advice and partner with customers, functional business analysts and other team members in driving appropriate design and implementation of technical solutions to meet desired business requirements. The incumbent will posses knowledge in the Oracle e-Business suite, Oracle toolset and Application framework. This role will provide proactive guidance for solution design with internal and external systems to facilitate maximum utilization of the Oracle Applications for business success and recommends solutions that enable the organization to achieve its goal.<br>
<br>
An effective Technical Lead will need to have a broad range of technical and functional knowledge, with good blend of problem solving &amp; communication skills; and will deliver technical solutions across multiple projects, as well as consultative technical and functional leadership across the organization, from strategic decision making to project planning to governance and oversight. Lead the projects through all phases of SDLC by working with the business users, application developers and infrastructure teams. Engage key business and technology stakeholders across the enterprise; be highly collaborative; drive communication to the business, functional, and software delivery teams; and apply analytical and problem-solving skills to help identify and solve system problems.<br>
<br>
<b>Essential Functions</b><br>
<br>
<b>Strategy &amp; Planning</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Ensure that applications meet business requirements and systems goals, fulfill end-user requirements, and identify and resolve systems issues</li>
<li>Review and analyze existing applications effectiveness and efficiency, and then develop strategies for improving or leveraging these systems</li>
<li>Cultivate and disseminate knowledge of application-usage best practices, standard processes and local variations due to customer, legal or business mandates</li>
<li>Proactively seek opportunities for support reduction through effective training, root cause analyses and process refinement</li>
<li>Strives to keep knowledge and expertise current with new releases, technology advances and analyzes potential opportunities and risks in adopting upcoming versions of software</li>
<li>Actively engage business management to ensure business needs are met</li>
<li>Determine staffing and skills requirements.</li>
<li>Drive change.</li>
</ul>
<br>
<b>
Acquisition &amp; Deployment</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Evaluate, install, configure, and participate in the deployment new applications, systems software, products, and/or enhancements to existing applications throughout the enterprise</li>
<li>Collaborate with analysts, designers, and system owners in the testing of new software programs and applications.</li>
<li>Analyze documentation and technical specifications of any new application under deployment or consideration to determine its intended functionality.</li>
<li>Ensure that any new software integration into company systems meets functional requirements, system compliance, and interface specifications.</li>
<li>Participate and facilitate in the design, development, and deployment of application enhancements and upgrades.</li>
<li>Coordinate feasibility studies for software and system products under consideration for purchase, and give advice based on findings.</li>
<li>Develop, enhance and deploy user training content, procedures, troubleshooting guides</li>
<li>Contribute to pre-testing phase of development by evaluating proposals to identify potential problem areas and make the appropriate recommendations.</li>
<li>Liaise with company’s software suppliers for prompt rectification of any problems or emergencies.</li>
</ul>
<br>
<b>
Operational Management</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Provide operational guidelines to ensure projects are completed within the appropriate framework.</li>
<li>Provide regular, clear, and consistent communication (written and oral) on the status of projects, issues, and deliverables to representatives from the customer/vendor.</li>
<li>Develop and maintain understanding of key processes, schedules, cycles, profiles, etc. for the technical systems in use by business.</li>
<li>Develop solutions to leverage ERP application functionality and suggest process improvements.</li>
<li>Liaise with business users, analysts, and software engineers to assist with quality assurance, program logic, and data processing</li>
<li>Work with vendor technical support to facilitate analysis and resolution to technical issues.</li>
<li>Understand and modify Application Configuration as required (including system setups, flex-fields, workflow builder, custom library, profile options etc.).</li>
<li>Guides technical team in the development of reports, conversions, interfaces and extensions for Oracle Applications.</li>
<li>Plan &amp; lead testing efforts for a deployment, including creating test plans, test cases, test scripts and performs functional &amp; technical testing.</li>
<li>Work with existing systems to track and manage requests and issues.</li>
<li>Provide leadership to ensure appropriate reviews for design, code reviews, and standards are adhered to.</li>
<li>Conduct activities like staffing, performance and resource management, and strategic direction of the team.</li>
</ul>
<br>

Incidental Functions</b><br>

<li>Maintain current knowledge of the technology workspace in terms of current state, future possibilities, forthcoming technologies, and their relevance and impact.</li>
<li>Develop &amp; provide training and documentation for supported applications.</li>
<li>Assist application development with technology design considerations.</li>
<li>Mentor other team members as needed.</li>
</ul>
