<h2>Staff Accountant</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Staff Accountant</b></p>
<p><b>Francis S. Infurchia &amp; Company, LLC</b></p>
<p><b>Do you want to pursue a career in Public Accounting?</b> We are currently hiring full-time entry level <b>accounting majors</b> to become members of our firm. We are seeking highly focused, dedicated professionals who work well independently as well as with a team and are ready to start their <b>accounting career</b> in a rapidly growing, successful accounting firm. We are able to offer valuable on the job training due to the experience of our Partners, seasoned CPA’s and accountants which makes our firm a great starting place for accounting majors entering the workforce. We offer a rewarding work environment with a focus on a work-life balance.</p>
<p><b>Essential functions: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Perform various accounting and tax assignments under the direction of the firm’s Accounting Managers and Partners</li>
<li>Prepare trial balances and deliverable financial statements for closely held companies in Quickbooks and Peachtree accounting software, either for financial reporting purposes or tax returns</li>
<li>Participate and conduct all phases of financial statement audits and reviews for privately held companies and non-profit organizations</li>
<li>Assist with forensic accounting and business valuation engagements as needed</li>
<li>Prepare state and federal tax returns for businesses, individuals, partnerships, estates, trusts and non-profits including extensions, along with required quarterly estimated payments</li>
<li>File sales tax, payroll tax, highway/ fuel use and various other state, federal and local monthly, quarterly and annual tax forms</li>
<li>Prepare local personal property tax reports and income and expense reports</li>
<li>Provide support to managers and partners for special projects and audits</li>
<li>Collaborate with staff members and accountants to obtain information for completing tax returns</li>
</ul>
<p>*</p>
<ul>
<li><b>Competencies: </b></li>
<li>Take ownership of projects and tasks</li>
<li>Ability to manage multiple tasks and meet time constraints and deadlines</li>
<li>Ability to communicate with clients, staff and partners in a professional manner</li>
<li>Demonstrate excellent written and oral communication skills</li>
<li>Flexibility with schedule during tax season</li>
</ul>
<p>*</p>
<ul>
<li><b>Position Requirements</b></li>
<li><b>Bachelor’s Degree in accounting (recent or near graduates welcome)</b></li>
<li>Computer skills, including Outlook, Excel and Adobe Acrobat</li>
<li>Knowledge of Quickbooks, Peachtree and tax preparation software a plus</li>
<li>Must be authorized to work in the US</li>
</ul>
