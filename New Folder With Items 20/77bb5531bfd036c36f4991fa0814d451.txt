<h2>Purchasing Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Overview</b></p>
<p>This position requires general Purchasing knowledge, as well as professional experience in dealing with the public. The Purchasing Specialist will assist other members of the Purchasing Department, while also being responsible for performing daily Reception duties.</p>
<p><b>Reporting Relationship</b><br>The position will report to the Procurement Manager.</p>
<p><b>Compensation</b><br>The objective of the compensation structure is to attract an individual who is detail oriented and is interested in a challenging opportunity with a growing company.</p>
<ul><li>Base Salary — Commensurate with experience and earnings history.</li></ul>
<p><b>Competencies</b><br>The person we are seeking will:</p>
<ul>
<li>Have excellent verbal and written communication skills; a professional yet easygoing writing style and an excellent command of grammar and punctuation</li>
<li>Must be accurate with data entry</li>
<li>Good planning and organizational skills</li>
<li>Ability to multitask and prioritize work</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Style</b><br>The Ideal Candidate will:</p>
<ul>
<li>Be detail oriented</li>
<li>Possess the ability to positively interact with the general public, both in person and via the phone</li>
<li>Have the flexibility to adapt to business conditions to achieve corporate goals and meet customer demands</li>
<li>Work effectively in a demanding, deadline-driven environment</li>
<li>Be able to communicate clearly with other departments within the organization</li>
<li>Work successfully as a team player</li>
<li>Possess strong time management, multi-tasking and organizational skills</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Objectives &amp; Responsibilities</b></p>
<p>The company’s objectives are to maintain historical growth rates and profitability increases, despite market conditions. For the company to achieve these objectives, the Purchasing Specialist assures inventory items are ordered and received as scheduled and that vendor invoices are entered promptly and accurately.</p>
<p>The primary responsibilities of the Purchasing Specialist are:</p>
<ul>
<li>Greet all visitors and guests entering the building and assign guest badges to all non-employees</li>
<li>Answer any general phone calls and transfer the callers to the appropriate person/extension</li>
<li>Input and manage changes to BOMs and Work Orders</li>
<li>Maintain overall knowledge of entire Purchasing cycle and process</li>
<li>Order and expedite purchased materials as assigned by Procurement Manager</li>
<li>Furnish lead time information for Sales and Production</li>
<li>Take responsibility of the internal process for corrective action for defective or unacceptable goods received</li>
<li>Follow up with vendor related to returns until resolved with replacement items or credit memo</li>
<li>Maintain open purchase orders and purchasing files, as well as proper approvals</li>
<li>Help resolve internal receiving issues for prompt resolution</li>
<li>Attend planning meetings to provide updates to Production with regard to meeting customer required dates or prompt notification of any material delays</li>
<li>Perform all other duties as required</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Previous Experience</b></p>
<p>The ideal candidate will have experience in a Purchasing environment or has experience with the Order/Receiving Process. Preferably, they will have experience greeting or communicating with customers. Additionally, they should possess:</p>
<ul>
<li>Associate’s degree or equivalent combination of education and experience preferred</li>
<li>A polite, professional demeanor</li>
<li>2-5 years data entry/office experience, purchasing preferred</li>
<li>PC skills in MS Office (Outlook, Excel, Word)</li>
<li>Purchasing experience is desirable</li>
<li>Flexibility to perform various tasks, as well as a quick learning aptitude</li>
<li>Strong oral and written communication skills</li>
<li>Familiarity with SBT or MRP type software is a plus</li>
</ul>
<p>Job Type: Full-time</p>
<p>Work Location:</p>
<ul><li>One location</li></ul>
<p>Benefits:</p>
<ul>
<li>Health insurance</li>
<li>Dental insurance</li>
<li>Vision insurance</li>
<li>Paid time off</li>
<li>Retirement plan</li>
</ul>
