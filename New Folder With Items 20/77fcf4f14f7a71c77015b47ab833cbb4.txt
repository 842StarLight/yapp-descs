<h2>SEO/Analytics Consultant</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<div>We are looking for an exceptional <b>SEO, Google Ads </b><b>and</b><b> Digital Analytics Consultant </b>to help deliver excellent sales support, marketing support and customer service. Your knowledge, passion and ability to deliver impactful and valuable solutions will be key to ensuring that Siteimprove Employees, Customers and prospective Customers understand: SEO best practices, Google Ads, Digital Analytics implementation and the knowledge and tools at their disposal to meet their Digital Presence Optimization (DPO) needs. As part of a diverse team, you will operate in a fast-paced dynamic environment united in one common mission – to deliver experiences that will attract new Customers and keep our Customers coming back for more! With the challenges you will face in this exciting role, there will be plenty of opportunities to grow both personally and professionally.
</div>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Participate in high value Sales and Customer interactions as ‘the expert’ where SEO, Google Ads and Digital Analytics is a strong focus</li>
<li>
Collaborates regularly scheduled workshops and trainings for internal sales and marketing teams. Reviews recorded sales and other calls to look for opportunities. Works as a partner with the Sales team</li>
<li>
Create and deliver SEO, Google Ads, Digital Analytics focused blog posts and other marketing content for both internal and external audiences</li>
<li>
Educates and raises the base SEO, Google Ads, Digital Analytics knowledge of the sales team</li>
<li>
Maintain on a continuous basis expert level knowledge on the topic of SEO, Google Ads and Digital Analytics.</li>
<li>
Attends meetings, conferences, tradeshows and other company functions necessary to perform duties, to include international travel (up to 30 travel days per year)</li>
<li>
Performs other related duties as assigned</li>
</ul>
