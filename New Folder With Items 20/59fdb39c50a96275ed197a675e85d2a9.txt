<h2>Director of Revenue</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>The Director of Revenue Management oversees revenue for our company. As a key member of the leadership team, this position works closely with the Corporate VP of Revenue Management, General Manager, Director of Sales and Marketing and the Executive Committee.</p>
<p><b>DUTIES &amp; FUNCTIONS</b></p>
<p>· Analyze short and long-term forecasting, analyze trends in group inventories &amp; rates to include occupancy, goals, product line and strategic sales goals.</p>
<p>· Analyze and monitor marketing channels, key and other account production. Ensure that all major channels of distribution effectively support the hotel and maintain rate parity.</p>
<p>· Update all channels of distribution as needed with rate or inventory restrictions.</p>
<p>· Work with each department to develop property-specific strategies to ensure growth.</p>
<p>· Establish appropriate goals and benchmarks in order to effectively measure performance.</p>
<p>· Explore and provide variations in market mix which would stimulate additional revenue without increasing costs.</p>
<p>· Responsible for the maintenance of any software programs which contribute to the management of revenue.</p>
<p>· Assist with implementation of Revenue Management basics and standards.</p>
<p>· Foster a culture of Revenue Management throughout all departments of the hotel.</p>
<p>· Develop and implement succession planning for Revenue Management talent.</p>
<p><b>QUALIFICATION STANDARDS</b></p>
<p>Skills</p>
<p>Knowledge of hospitality sales, marketing and other revenue management functions to</p>
<p>include research, planning, market positioning, market segments, distribution channels, sales management, reservations and yield management.</p>
<p>· Excellent oral and written communication skills.</p>
<p>· Excellent organization skills.</p>
<p>· Proficient in Microsoft Word, Excel and PowerPoint.</p>
<p>· Proficient in property management and revenue management systems use and development.</p>
<p>· Proficient at statistical and competitive analysis.</p>
<p>· Individual is high energy individual with a positive attitude is a must. Guest service and team oriented.</p>
<p>· Strong interpersonal skills and ability to clearly communicate with team members and senior leadership.</p>
<p>Abilities</p>
<p>· Ability to effectively analyze and use operational, financial, and marketing data to maintain and improve market share/penetration goals.</p>
<p>· Ability to move quickly and adjust strategies based on trends, market demand and property needs.</p>
<p>· Understanding of our operations and can associate with their needs through the revenue department.</p>
<p>· Ability to work well under deadline pressure.</p>
<p>· Ability to develop and deliver effective presentations.</p>
<p>· Ability to quickly adapt to a constantly changing market.</p>
<p>· Independent hotel background is preferred.</p>
<p>Qualifications</p>
<p>· Bachelor’s degree required, preferably in business or analytics.</p>
<p>· Minimum of 5 years’ experience in hotels/resorts with minimum of 2 years in Revenue Management.</p>
<p>· Previous hospitality management experience required.</p>
