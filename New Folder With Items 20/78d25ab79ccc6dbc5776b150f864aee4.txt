<p><b>About The Company: </b></p>
<p>Haven Management Services LLC., is a hotel management company based in San Marcos, Texas. We are a company of proven team leaders with an uncompromising commitment to quality of service, a forward-thinking attitude along with a strong positive outlook. We operate and manage full and limited-service hotels throughout the state of Texas.</p>
<p><b>Your Opportunity: </b></p>
<p>Haven Management Services is seeking a motivated sales and marketing intern with a passion for sales, marketing, and social media to play an integral role in our organization. This is an exciting and rewarding opportunity for anyone interested in or pursuing a career in the hotel/hospitality industry with challenging work in a result-driven atmosphere. As a result, the ideal candidate will have gained a broad perspective in various aspects of sales and marketing techniques within the hotel and hospitality industry and will be rewarded with the knowledge to bring to their future careers.</p>
<p><b>Projects Include: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Creating hotel print ads and other marketing materials for the sales team to distribute on sales visits.</li>
<li>Going to different segments in the market (Medical, Apartments, Outlets, etc) to distribute marketing materials such as Hotel print ads, business cards and vouchers for Spring’s Bistro + Bar.</li>
<li>Maintaining the company sales funnel and entering data such as notes on each segment visit and company/group information.</li>
<li>Responsible for collecting company business cards and returning them to the Regional Director of Sales (RDOS).</li>
<li>Attending networking events such as trade shows and conventions.</li>
<li>Performing a SWOT Analysis of the hotels’ competitive set and leveraging on how we can outperform the comp set through sales, marketing, and social media.</li>
<li>Prospecting via Facebook/Instagram/LinkedIn and other channels.</li>
<li>Maintaining a hotel website and blog.</li>
<li>Opportunity to help kick start a restaurant and new convention center.</li>
<li>Ad-Hoc projects when they arise.</li>
</ul>
