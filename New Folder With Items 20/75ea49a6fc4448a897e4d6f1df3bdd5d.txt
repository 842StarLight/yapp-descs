<div>MERCHANDISING ASSISTANT - Natick, MA</div>
<p></p>NIC+ZOE is looking for a Merchandising Assistant to join the team. In this role, you will manage key tools and reports that drive success for the Merchandising team. The ideal candidate has a natural desire for product along with strong analytical skills.
<br>
<br>
LINE DEVELOPMENT
<br>
<ul>
<li>Support Merchandising Team to create tools for Design Kickoff</li>
<li>Create and maintain design templates throughout the development process</li>
<li>Manage data for Merchandising Plans</li>
<li>Compile team information to create end of season recaps</li>
<li>Create visual guides for Buy Meetings</li>
<li>Conduct ongoing market research and competitive benchmarking</li>
<li>Provide strong organization support for the Merchandising Department</li>
</ul>
<br>
MERCHANDISING SUPPORT FOR SALES
<ul>
<li>Manage deadlines, file prep and communicate with printers to create order forms and workbooks</li>
<li>Create special size order forms</li>
<li>Create sales tools for reps, including but not limited to Style Guides and Trend Guides</li>
<li>Partner with Sales Assistant to create and assemble Rep Binders, ship sales samples and maintain templates based on received orders</li>
<li>Identify special requests in Market Recaps and flow up to Merchandising Manager. Take necessary steps to create templates based on special requests</li>
Manage Style Master to ensure all Missy, Petite and Womens styles are passed to data entry in a timely manner so that orders may be entered
<li>Ensure Product Managers/Tech/Design are clear on all Petite/Plus size styles through templates and style masters</li>
<li>Partner with Canadian distributors to confirm pricing and provide sales tools</li>
<li>Partner with Sales Assistant to prep for Natick sales appointments</li>
<li>Provide product swaps to customer service and sales team for cancelled styles</li>
</ul>
<br>
SKILL SET REQUIREMENTS
<br>
<li>B.A. or B.S.</li>
<li>Work / internship experience in fashion/apparel</li>
<ul>
<li>Ability to multi-task and prioritize, creative problem solving, organization, strong verbal and written communication skills, MS Office</li>
<li>Experience with InDesign is a plus but not required</li>
<li>Travel may be required</li>
</ul>

<div>SENIOR DESIGNER - WOMEN'S WOVEN CUT + SEW - Natick, MA</div>
<p></p>The Senior Designer will create and execute on the vision of the Chief Creative Officer, ensuring that the product is consistent with market trends while maintaining the brand identity and supports overall business strategy. Candidates will have a passion for the NIC+ZOE brand and customer with a strong eye for trend and color. The ideal candidate will have strong communication and organization skills with the ability to direct and develop junior designers.
<br>
The Designer will be an integral member of the design and development function. The Senior Designer will be working in a fast paced environment with the support of a design team to execute all design details.
<br>
Primary responsibilities include but are not limited to:
<br>
<br>
CORE RESPONSIBILITIES
<br>
<ul>
<li>Ability to manage the design and development process from concept through final proto/sample, including but not limited to flat sketch, initial spec, and production fit approval.</li>
<li>Presentation of concepts and ideas throughout the design and development process</li>
<li>Responsible for product design process, including market research, identifying new fabrications, design, sketch, spec’ing and fit</li>
<li>Interpret the vision of the Chief Creative Officer, ensuring the brand esthetic is in keeping with our customer lifestyle needs and at the center of all creative work.</li>
<li>Possess strong knowledge of garment construction and conduct fit sessions to help solve all problems that may occur during proto samples stage.</li>
<li>Build strong relationships with merchandising team, understanding seasonal framework.</li>
<li>Collaborate with creative teams to form seasonal design direction and inspiration stories.</li>
<li>Participate in all fittings, partner closely with tech designers to ensure product fit is as intended.</li>
</ul>
<br>
LEADERSHIP
<ul>
<li>Motivate, inspire, energize and lead a team of Design Assistants.</li>
<li>Identify growth, development and learning opportunities in team members, provide coaching and genuine real time feedback.</li>
<li>Inspire creative collaboration across the design team.</li>
<li>Drive and manage creative team to deliver projects on time and on budget.</li>
<li>Ensure creative output is consistently brand appropriate; continually evaluate resources and accountabilities within the creative team.</li>
</ul>
<br>
SKILL SET REQUIREMENTS
<br>
<ul>
<li>4 year college degree in Apparel Design</li>
<li>8+ years of design experience with cut + sew
</li>
<li>History of contributing innovations within a collaborative environment</li>
<li>Knowledge of textiles and garment construction and finishes</li>
<li>Experience with manufacturing product in multiple countries simultaneously with multiple agents.</li>
<li>Strong computer knowledge in PDM, Photoshop/Illustrator</li>
<li>Ability to design around concept direction</li>
<li>Strong sense of style, silhouette and color</li>
<li>Strong organizational, time management, communication and presentation skills</li>
<li>Ability to be flexible and adaptable in a fast-paced environment</li>
<li>Well organized, problem solver with a positive can do attitude</li>
<li>Ability to motivate, lead and work well cross functionally</li>
<li>Ability to travel both domestically + internationally</li>
</ul>
