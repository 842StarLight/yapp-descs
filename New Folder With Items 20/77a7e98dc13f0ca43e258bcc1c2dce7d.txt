<h2>Hourly Trader ERTC 07.22.19</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Hourly Trader</b></p>
<p><b>Raleigh, NC</b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>This position will be responsible for the management of generation and native load requirements of any participating member or client. This position will be accountable for generation and load scheduling, demand side management, transmission purchases, energy transactions, market interaction, hourly sales of member’s generation or<br>
contract resources and purchasing as required.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>REQUIRED QUALIFICATIONS</b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree in business or a related field or an Associate’s degree with applicable power trading experience from an institution accredited by an accrediting agency recognized by the US Department of Education.</li>
<li>Previous business experience preferred to include price negotiation, data analysis and customer service.</li>
<li>Demonstrated proficiency in computers, specifically Microsoft Excel.</li>
<li>Competence with mathematics and technical concepts.</li>
<li>High degree of analytical, attention to detail and organization skills.</li>
<li>Effective written and oral communication skills as well as computer skills.</li>
<li>Application and use of critical thinking skills.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>RESPONSIBILITIES</b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Candidate will be required to work rotating shift to provide 24-hour coverage of the business as well as participation in daily trading floor operations.</li>
<li>Gain understanding of industry practices and entities, as well as Market Structure of ISO/RTO’s, as applicable.</li>
<li>Responsible for filling native load requirements for participating members.</li>
<li>Manage market of generation or contract resources for participating Members and Clients.</li>
<li>Manages market exposure with regards to open book positions.</li>
<li>Handle market and/or reliability communications between Reliability Entities and Generation Plants or Member Operators.</li>
<li>Accurately input data into ACES’ software systems.</li>
<li>Optimize basic generation by utilizing economic decision making abilities.</li>
<li>Coordination of Demand Side Management activities.</li>
<li>Assists in the gathering of power marketing intelligence.</li>
<li>Participates in marketing functions while interfacing with the term trading and scheduling desks.</li>
<li>Monitors market conditions and analyzes transactions to help identify and execute energy trades.</li>
<li>Manages optimization opportunities of client portfolios.</li>
<li>Interface with member and client representatives and associated entities when necessary.</li>
<li>Obtain NERC and/or appropriate market certification and/or CEH.</li>
<li>Will adhere to all ACES corporate policies and comply with all ACES regulatory requirements, including but not limited to NERC, FERC, and relevant state regulations, as applicable to this position.</li>
<li>Any additional responsibilities assigned by management including potential travel.</li>
</ul>
