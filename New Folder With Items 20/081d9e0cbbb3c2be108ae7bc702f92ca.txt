<b>Overview</b><br>
<br>
<div>SITECH Chesapeake, part of the Alban CAT family and authorized dealer for Trimble Heavy &amp; Civil Construction products and services, is currently accepting applications for a Software Support Specialist.</div>
<br>
<b>
Responsibilities</b><br>
<br>
<div>The Software Support Specialist is responsible for providing technical support and training to customers and SITECH Sales Team on all Trimble Software and Survey products. Reviews customer designs and generates 3-D models to demonstrate the benefits of Trimble Software. Provides end user training to customers, building relationships and making recommendations based on their current and future needs. Keeps Sales Team informed of product upgrades and enhancements. Collaborates with Sales Team to understand customer needs and promote Sales activities.<br>
</div>
<br>
<br>
<b>Qualifications</b><br>
<br>
<div>Minimum of three years of construction / survey experience or Bachelor’s degree in Civil Engineering or CAD, and knowledge of construction layout. Experience with 3-D modeling, CAD/AutoCAD and Takeoff preferred. Must possess ability to train customers on operation of technologies and recommend technologies. Must be able to communicate effectively and interact professionally with customers at all levels within the organization. Must be able to make effective and persuasive presentations. Must have a valid driver’s license and be willing to travel.</div>
