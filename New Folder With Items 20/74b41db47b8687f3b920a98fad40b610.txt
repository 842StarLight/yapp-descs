<h2>Mechanical Engineer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>HCI Energy is an industrial power solutions company that integrates renewable energy with traditional power generation, providing better reliability in a more cost effective and environmentally friendly system. We are currently looking for a <b>Mechanical Engineer</b> to join our team.</p>
<p><b>Responsibilities</b>:</p>
<p>1. Provide conceptual and detailed designs via renderings, 3D models, mechanical drawings. Uses tolerance analysis and stress calculations for design analysis</p>
<p>2. Create and maintain engineering documents. Comply with document control processes (ECO, ECN, and BOM)</p>
<p>3. Participate and support efforts in creating Bill of Materials, installation instructions, and other support documents</p>
<p>4. Evaluate technical risks through DFMEAâ€™s and ensures timely completion of required actions</p>
<p>5. Maintain relationships with vendors and initiates part orders for prototypes</p>
<p>6. Obtain prototypes and develop written test protocols to verify or evaluate design performance. Create reports and presentations related to design activities</p>
<p>7. Design and develop new test methods to quantify product/component performance capabilities</p>
<p>8. Ensure products are fully tested and conform to functional specifications and/or customer requirements</p>
<p>9. Specifies and qualifies mechanical and electro-mechanical components.</p>
<p>10. During the course of product redesign projects, establishes, updates and maintain product specifications, including all meaningful details pertinent to product manufacturability requirements, regulatory considerations, and marketing/sales considerations.</p>
<p><b>Experience</b>:</p>
<ul>
<li>Minimum 3 years of experience in mechanical design and analysis</li>
<li>Experience with 3D solid modeling tools like SolidWorks or Catia for design conceptualization and realization</li>
<li>Knowledge about FEA techniques to estimate stress, strain, deformation and modal response, etc of structures using any FEA software</li>
<li>Demonstrated ability to derive conclusions from analysis and make practical recommendations</li>
<li>Adept in FBD (free body diagrams), strength of materials calculations, and tolerance analysis</li>
<li>Strong technical writing, communication, and presentation skill</li>
<li>Knowledge of power industry standards and best practices (e.g. NERC, IBC)</li>
<li>Possession of, or ability to acquire a Professional Engineering license</li>
<li>Working familiarity value analysis / value engineering tools</li>
</ul>
