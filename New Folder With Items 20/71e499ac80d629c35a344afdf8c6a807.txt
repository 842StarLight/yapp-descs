<h2>Local Sales Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>It's fun to work in a company where people truly believe in what they are doing!
</p>
<p>KOIN 6 TV/Portland/s CW, a Nexstar Media Group Inc. owned CBS and CW duopoly in Portland, Oregon has an immediate opening for an experienced Local Sales Manager. Nexstar is the largest media company in the country with 197 television stations, web sites and full suite of digital solutions. The ideal candidate will be passionate, persistent and enjoys the sales process. Be a part of the expanding world of broadcast and digital advertising!
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>JOB SUMMARY: </b>The Local Sales Manager is responsible for leading, teaching, and motivating the sales team along with growing local broadcast and digital revenue.
</p>
<p><b>Principal Duties &amp; Responsibilities </b><b>:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Train, develop and motivate the local sales staff.
</li>
<li>Work with sales management team to develop new and non-traditional revenue streams, sales packages and promotions while overseeing implementation.
</li>
<li>Passion for new business development with a focus on zero share accounts
</li>
<li>Manage and direct account executives to achieve local sales goals.
</li>
<li>Plan and direct staffing, training and performance evaluation to develop staff.
</li>
<li>Prepare and submit sales and financial reports as directed.
</li>
<li>Direct and coordinate activities involving all sales.
</li>
<li>Manage and confer with potential clients and customers.
</li>
<li>Work with sales management team on inventory management and pricing.
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Specialized Knowledge/Skills/Abilities:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Strong leadership &amp; team-building skills.
</li>
<li>Strong analytical, problem solving, communications, writing, negotiation, &amp; presentation skills.
</li>
<li>Must be well organized, display a professional demeanor, exude high energy and show attention to detail.
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Education/Experience </b><b>: </b>A Bachelor’s degree with a minimum of three (3) years of sales management experience including local and digital sales. Must have strong record of understanding process development and management.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Training/Equipment </b><b>: </b>Strong MS Office skills including Word, Advanced Excel, and PowerPoint are required. Nielsen, Strata and Wide Orbit a plus.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Work Environment/Mental/Physical Requirements </b>:
</p>
<p>High stress environment with deadline pressures. Travel and long work hours may be required. A valid driver’s license, state mandated automobile insurance and an acceptable driving record are also required.
</p>
<p></p>

</p>
