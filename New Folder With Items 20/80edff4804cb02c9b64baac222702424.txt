<h2>Strategic Accounts Hunter - Cloud Authorization</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div></div>
<div>
<div>The strategic accounts cloud authentication solutions sales hunter will proactive seek out and develop new business opportunities which have the potential to drive growth and profitability within the Entrust Datacard software market. Carrying the quota and responsibility for all of North America for Entrust Datacard’s authentication cloud solutions, this high level sales specialist will overlay the regional field sales teams to drive growth through net new logo and expansion sales in the company’s top echelon of customers and prospects. This is a unique sales opportunity to develop strategies and sales tactics to generate pipeline for the company’s cloud authentication solution, and play a key role in the growth and expansion of our top market. Responsible for regional accelerated growth plan to penetrate strategic accounts, the strategic accounts cloud authentication sales person will perform the accountabilities detailed below and other duties as assigned.</div>
