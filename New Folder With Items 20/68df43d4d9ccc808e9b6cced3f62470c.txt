<h2>Accounts Receivable Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p><b>Summary/Objective</b></p>
<p>The Accounts Receivable Specialist will support the accounting team by processing and managing client accounts in accordance with contractual terms. This position will manage daily processed sales, customer deposits, collections and perform reconciliations. This role will be responsible for providing financial, clerical and administrative services to complete efficient, timely and accurate payment processing on accounts. The Accounts Receivable Specialist will have the opportunity to identify and suggest enhancements to existing processes which result in a leaner, more efficient and effective process.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Essential Functions</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Maintain up-to-date billing system.</li>
<li>Generate and send out invoices weekly/daily to clients.</li>
<li>Research customer registration problems and resolve by correcting databases as needed.</li>
<li>Respond to accounting and contract inquires timely.</li>
<li>Responsible for collections of outstanding accounts receivable and reducing client billing inquiries. Follow up, collect and allocate payments.</li>
<li>Complete billing, collection, and reporting activities according to specific deadlines.</li>
<li>Research and resolve payment discrepancies.</li>
<li>Prepare bank deposits and process credit card payments timely.</li>
<li>Investigate and resolve client inquires.</li>
<li>Perform reconciliations on accounts.</li>
<li>Assist with month-end closing.</li>
<li>Seek out and communicate opportunities to continuously improve, standardize the company’s A/R and collections process.</li>
<li>Complete special projects as assigned by management.</li>
<br>
</ul>
