<h2>General Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<div>Wen-Robb, LLC, is a franchisee of The Wendy's Company. We currently operate Wendy's Restaurants in Griffin, Fayetteville, Peachtree City, &amp; Barnesville, GA. We would like to take this opportunity to thank you for your interest in our organization. We have a rich heritage, traditions, quality standards, and values that we believe are critical to our success.</div>
<div></div>
<div>Company mission statement "To Serve The Customer Beyond Their Expectations! Each Time! Every Time!"</div>
<div></div>
<div>Wendy’s fast food restaurants are exceptionally clean and well-run facilities that are predictable in terms of guest and restaurant team expectations. The Wendy’s General Manager manages the operations and staff of a Wendy’s restaurant, including the execution of all company policies, procedures, programs, and systems while promoting ethical business practices and ensuring compliance with all federal, state, and local laws. The General Manager is a confident decision-maker, highly practiced in guest contact, employee management, business and financial management, people motivation, and communications.<br>
<br>
The General Manager supports the enterprise by creating and maintaining an "Employer of Choice" environment and by providing leadership, direction, training, and development to subordinate managers and team members alike.</div>
<div></div>
<div>All employees in a management or supervisory position within Wendy's Restaurants must possess a Serve-Safe Certification within 60 days of their hire date.</div>
<br>
<p></p>
<b>
RESPONSIBILITIES</b><ul>
<li>
Work with leadership team to meet budgeted sales goals by ensuring expedient and friendly guest service</li>
<li>
Manages food, labor, paper costs, and other controllable expenses</li>
<li>
Provides proper training for crew members through established systems and follow through to ensure compliance with company standards</li>
<li>
Trains, monitors, and reinforces food safety procedures</li>
<li>
Ensures food safety procedures are executed according to company policies and health/sanitation regulations</li>
<li>
Executes company policies and procedures for the control of cash, property, product, and equipment</li>
<li>
Monitors inventory levels through ordering to ensure product availability</li>
<li>
Manages and maintains safe working conditions</li>
<li>
Manages crew member employees in a manner that maximizes crew retention</li>
<li>
Interviews and recommends crew members for hiring</li>
<li>
Anticipates and identifies problems and initiates appropriate corrective action</li>
<li>
Maintains fast and accurate service and positive guest relations and ensures products are consistent with company quality standards</li>
</ul>
<br>
<b>
QUALIFICATIONS</b><ul>
<li>
Two years of restaurant experience at a minimum</li>
<li>
College degree or equivalent experience in operations</li>
<li>
Management/Supervisory/Mentor experience desired</li>
<li>
Guest-friendly demeanor</li>
<li>
Fluent English-speaking and writing skills</li>
<li>
Able to lift 35 pounds to waist level</li>
<li>
ServSafe Certified</li>
<li>
Exhibit a sense of urgency</li>
<li>
Willing to assume around-the-clock responsibility for restaurant operations</li>
<li>
Willing to work normal schedule of 50+ hours per week (all shifts)</li>
</ul>
