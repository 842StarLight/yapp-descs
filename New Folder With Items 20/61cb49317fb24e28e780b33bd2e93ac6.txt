<p><b>CORE SERVICES </b>: Workers Compensation Specialists Professional &amp; General Liability Insurance Personal Home, Auto and Valuables Insurance Risk Management Consulting Commercial Insurance Employee Benefits Employee Benefit and Human Resources Consulting Independent Medical Examinations Occupational Health Networks Hiring Protocols Injury Management / Return to Work.

<p><b>JOB SUMMARY:
</b></p>
<p>The Personal Lines Assistant I will support the Personal Lines department in providing clerical assistance related to processing, customer service and sales.
</p>
<p><b>ESSENTIAL DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITES:
</b></p>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Pull new business, renewal, endorsement and cancellation declarations from the company websites as needed.
</li>
<li>Process non-pay and reinstatements.
</li>
<li>Scan and attach completed Personal Lines applications to imaging.
</li>
<li>Complete CARCO inspections, notify insurance company and update Agency Management System.
</li>
<li>Ensure Manufacturers Statement of Origin (MSO) and Bill of Sale (BOS) are received, notify insurance company and update Agency Management System.
</li>
<li>Contact dealerships when MSO and BOS are not received.
</li>
<li>Upload electronic checks and sweep payments to company website.
</li>
<li>Print and forward duplicate ID cards or declaration pages to insured.
</li>
<li>Review and refer cancelled policy information to Account Managers per agency guidelines.
</li>
<li>Contact insured for completion of pending Progressive paperless sign-ups.
</li>
<li>Participate in seminars and classes for skills and knowledge development.
</li>
<li>Perform other duties as required.
</li>
<li>Maintain proficient knowledge of, and demonstrate ongoing compliance with all laws and regulations applicable to this position, ensure ongoing adherence to pertinent policies, procedures, and internal controls, and meet all job-specific training requirements in a timely fashion.
</li>
</ul>
