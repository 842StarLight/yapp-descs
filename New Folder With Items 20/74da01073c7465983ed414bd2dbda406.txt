<p>What You’ll Be Doing
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Summary
</b></p>
<p>The ideal candidate for this Director of Product Management- Titling role is one who has successfully directed the transformation of legacy product portfolios. This candidate will have demonstrated abilities to build and deliver new products through the standard product management framework and ensure portfolio provides ROI and a strong value proposition. This person is ready to set the direction of an entire portfolio and participate with senior business leaders on platform level strategy.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Key Responsibilities
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Lead strategic product management efforts for our growing Electronic Titling and Registration Portfolio including; implementation, positioning market analysis, roadmap planning, and prioritization.
</li>
<li>Collaborate with global and cross-vertical product teams to help shape the strategy and direction around the accurate research and market expansion of titling solutions across markets and verticals.
</li>
<li>Understand market requirements for the EVR business and translate to strategic portfolio roadmaps. Collaborate with product development to find efficiencies in quality and delivery portfolio through the software development life cycle.
</li>
<li>Execute strong financial, profitability, ROI and customer value results – from business case to delivery, ensure that products are commercially successful and operationally effective.
</li>
<li>Partner with Product Marketing to equip sales and internal teams with compelling marketing tools and strategies to support positioning of existing products, new product and feature launches, value proposition and targeted sales campaigns. Product determines value proposition and provides competitive information. Ultimately Sales is responsible for setting the price.]
</li>
<li>Create and support delivery of white papers, discussion papers, client case studies; maintain competitor and industry awareness to drive product evolution.
</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Other Responsibilities
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Remediate customer issues and escalations to ensure fast and accurate resolution.
</li>
<li>Qualify and requalify team to be able to train internal and external users on product/solution; develop training and sales tools.
</li>
<li>Other duties as necessary to ensure ongoing and on time delivery of portfolio with expected scope and quality to customers.
</li>
</ul>
