<h2>Customer Service Representative</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<b>Position Purpose:</b> To ensure friendly and knowledgeable service support for customersReports To: Director of Customer ExperienceExperience: 2+ years work experience, with 1 year in customer service
<br>Tasks
<br><ul>
<li>Constantly remains a friendly, professional demeanor with customers and coworkers.</li>
<li>Remains readily available to customers, develops a genuine understanding of their needs and assists them properly.</li>
<li>Answer telephones, relay messages, and route calls to appropriate team member.</li>
<li>Use routing software to schedule start-up and recurring services.</li>
<li>Maintain accurate records of important customer comments and interactions.</li>
<li>Make calls to customers to check satisfaction.</li>
<li>Answer basic questions from customers and prospects about company services.</li>
<li>Develops standard customer proposals and close a sale over the phone and/or pass the sale to the appropriate salesperson.</li>
<li>Sell company services to in-bound callers when needed.</li>
<li>Make calls to customers to collect past due amounts.</li>
<li>Manage the on-hold customers as directed.</li>
<li>Processing payments.</li>
<li>Post production of services to customer accounts.</li>
<li>Prepare customer mailings and perform other administrative tasks.</li>
<li>Willingly help others to complete tasks.</li>
</ul>
Minimum Qualifications
<br><ul>
<li>High school graduate or equivalent</li>
<li>Previous customer service experience is required</li>
<li>Proficient in Microsoft Office and basic computer skills</li>
<li>Effective verbal and written communication skills is required</li>
<li>Ability to add, subtract, multiply and divide all units of measure</li>
</ul>
</div>
