 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">

<div><div><div>
<div>
<div><div><div><div><p>Accounting &amp; Reporting Systems Manager - GROWMARK, Inc. - Bloomington, IL
</p></div></div></div></div>
<div><div><div><div>
<div>GROWMARK is a regional cooperative with annual sales of $8.5 billion providing agronomy, energy, facility planning, and logistics products and services, as well as grain marketing and risk management services in more than 40 states and Ontario, Canada. GROWMARK is ranked fourth on the NCB Co-op 100 listing of the nation’s 100 highest revenue-earning cooperative businesses. GROWMARK owns the FS trademark, which is used by affiliated member cooperatives. GROWMARK is large enough to offer solid career opportunities, but small enough to provide a family-oriented, welcoming environment and great benefits.</div>
<div>See what it's like to work at GROWMARK: https://youtu.be/CSVMnKCySsY</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div><b>
PURPOSE AND SUMMARY STATEMENT</b></div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div>Responsible for management of the General Ledger system for GROWMARK and certain retail/subsidiary business units and the reporting of GROWMARK consolidated financial results.<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<div><b>SUPERVISION AND LEADERSHP</b></div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div>Supervises, leads, and develops the Staff Accountant, Accounting Associate, Senior General Ledger System Control Specialist, and General Ledger Specialists.</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div><b>ESSENTIAL JOB FUNCTIONS</b></div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div>Manages the preparation of monthly and quarterly financial statements for GROWMARK and its retail/subsidiary business units.<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<div>Responsible for the preparation of quarterly consolidated financial reports and special bank group financial statements.<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<div>Manages and utilizes the SAP/BPC General Ledger/Reporting software tools including security management, skill cross training and support in increasing the effective use of the software tools.</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div>Responsible for the administration of the SAP/BPC systems including report writing, interface management, and chart of accounts management for GROWMARK and consolidated entities.</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div>Oversees the fixed asset accounting and general &amp; administrative overhead processing cycles. Ensures the related corporate policies are followed.</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div>Provides guidance to subsidiary and retail division accounting staff regarding information required for consolidated financial statements.</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div>Manages the integration of financial information from subsidiaries and retail divisions for preparation of consolidated financial reports.</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div>Manages and documents the various consolidation processes required to prepare GROWMARK consolidated financial statements including identification of all eliminating entries.</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div><b>OTHER JOB FUNCTIONS</b></div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div>Provides recommendations relating to approved accounting policies and technology enhancements, and maintains strong internal controls over financial reporting to ensure compliance with GAAP.<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<div>Assists in coordination of the annual audit, including compiling information from the different companies of the consolidated group and developing certain GAAP consolidated footnotes.</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div>Follows GROWMARK’s Code of Conduct and Corporate Compliance Program, Environmental Health and Safety, OSHA and DOT policies and procedures, as they apply.</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div>Performs all other duties as assigned.</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div><b>REQUIREMENTS</b></div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div>Normally requires a college degree in accounting, finance, business administration, or the equivalent thereof, and 5-7 years of related work experience to demonstrate the ability to provide general ledger and fixed asset accounting leadership to the GROWMARK System.</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div>Prefer a CPA or MBA degree to demonstrate advanced accounting/business knowledge</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div>Demonstrates essential abilities including business knowledge, collaboration, communication, customer focus, decision making and skill development.</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div>Understanding and experience with accounting-related areas including overhead, general ledger, fixed assets, financial statements, systems and software, security management, technical support, internal controls, audit strategy, policies and procedures, and compliance along with general ledger planning, program execution, budgeting, and communications.</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div>Proven supervisory and leadership skills including staffing, coaching, and performance evaluation and the ability to achieve results through others.</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div>Proven professional communication, interpersonal, persuasion, and customer service skills.</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div>Demonstrated planning, organization, problem-solving, and project management skills.</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div>Proven team building skills and the ability to work independently and in a team-oriented environment.<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<div>Functional experience with SAP BPC or other EPM software package preferred.<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<div>Must have and maintain a valid driver’s license and satisfactory driving record.<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<div>Ability and willingness to participate in required training that may include education on GROWMARK’s policies and procedures and additional training as it relates to the requirements of the position.
</div>
