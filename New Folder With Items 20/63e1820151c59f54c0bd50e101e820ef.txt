<p><b>Director, Product Marketing</b></p>
<p>
A content technology platform, Nativo helps advertisers create meaningful brand connections with consumers and empowers publishers with breakthrough monetization solutions. We're preparing the world for the age of content, where digital advertising is becomes a seamless, authentic, and helpful consumer experience.</p>
<p>
We invite you to join us for an exciting and rewarding experience as a Director of Product Marketing. This lead role will lead our Product Marketing team and set the direction for our go-to-market processes. This individual will know how to grasp and translate technical capabilities into benefits, and is the important nexus between product development and external audiences. You will play a critical role in informing the creation of outbound marketing, identifying market need/opportunity, identifying buyer personas, and last but not least, identifying product positioning.</p>
<p><b>
What you'll do:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Own the go-to-market process for your products</li>
<li>
Market intelligence – be the expert on the supply chain and associated dynamics, be the expert on your competition, and position your products relative to that backdrop</li>
<li>
Develop product positioning and messaging that differentiates your products in the market</li>
<li>
Inform Sales enablement – communicate the value proposition of the products to the sales team and develop the sales tools that support the selling process of your products</li>
<li>
Product launch: plan the launch of new products and releases and manage the cross-functional implementation of the plan</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
What you need:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
5+ years of software product marketing experience with at least 2 years' experience in a field facing role</li>
<li>
Must have B2B Marketing, SaaS, and/or ad tech background</li>
<li>
Bachelor's in business or marketing</li>
<li>
Very strong written communication skills</li>
<li>
Very strong project management skills</li>
<li>
Advertising technology knowledge and practical experience</li>
<li>
Excellent people and management skills to interact with staff, colleagues and cross-functional teams, and third parties.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
What We Offer:</b></p>
<p>
At Nativo, you make an immediate impact. Nativo is made up of smart, talented and driven people looking for other potential team members with the same attitude of innovation and excellence. We offer a competitive compensation and benefits package that includes stock options, health coverage, employer-matched 401k, cutting edge work, and the opportunity to join a rapidly growing startup with a proven product.</p>
