<h2>Asst Mgr & Team Member - SuperClean Carwash</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Responsible for maintaining customer service as per company standard, by ensuring customer satisfaction and handling of customer request.</p>
<p>Essential Duties and Responsibilities (Other Duties may be assigned to meet business needs):</p>
<ul><li>Maintain awareness of services provided by Super Clean Car Wash</li></ul>
<ul><li>Explains services provided by Super Clean Car Wash to customers</li></ul>
<ul><li>Sells additional services by recognizing opportunities and benefits to customers</li></ul>
<ul><li>Accurately and efficiently complete sales transactions and maintain proper cash accountabilities at POS registers</li></ul>
<ul><li>Stocking and maintaining of merchandise</li></ul>
<ul><li>General housekeeping and light maintenance responsibilities (driveways, restrooms, and car wash)</li></ul>
<ul><li>Explain procedure to customers, ensuring employee or customer safety is not jeopardized</li></ul>
<ul><li>Follow exact car loading and "prepping" procedures</li></ul>
<ul><li>May hold advertising signs or perform other marketing related functions</li></ul>
<p>Qualification/Requirements</p>
<ul><li>Smile and maintain a clean appearance as per the dress and grooming standards</li></ul>
<ul><li>Ability to communicate effectively</li></ul>
<ul><li>Ability to read, count, and write accurately</li></ul>
<ul><li>Be punctual, responsible and accountable</li></ul>
<ul><li>Ability to work various hours, weekends, and holidays</li></ul>
<ul><li>High school diploma or equivalent preferred</li></ul>
<p>Work Environment And Physical Demands</p>
<p>Person will work outside in a loud environment due to car wash equipment. The person will be in constant motion and will have physical activities, such as lifting (0-50 lbs.), bending, reaching and stooping.</p>
<p>Job Type: Part-time</p>
<p>Experience:</p>
<ul><li>customer service: 1 year (Preferred)</li></ul>
<p>Work authorization:</p>
<ul><li>United States (Required)</li></ul>
