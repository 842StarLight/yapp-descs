<h2>Strategic Account Executive</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">The Strategic Account Executive is a key position within our salesforce. Reporting to our Chief Growth Officer, the SAE has a high level of visibility in the organization and will manage the strategic account growth for our largest targeted accounts by effectively developing client relationships and executing the sales process. This role is a critical piece to building the relationship with significant clients, ensuring customer success and identifying our biggest and best opportunities.
<br><br>
<b>Key Activities:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Set strategy and vision for building and maintaining key strategic accounts</li>
<li>Achieve assigned strategic account objectives</li>
<li>Establish strong long-term client relationships</li>
<li>Suggest solutions and innovative ideas to meet client needs</li>
<li>Monitor Sales Performance Metrics</li>
<li>Proactively work to generate new qualified prospects</li>
<li>Sell to prospect executives, including CEO, COO, CNO, CMO and CFO</li>
<li>Collaborate as appropriate, with other WellSky sales personnel on joint sales efforts</li>
<li>Build and maintain a sales pipeline</li>
</ul>
Requirements
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor's Degree</li>
<li>3+ years' experience in selling Enterprise solutions and services in health care.</li>
<li>Experience in negotiating and meeting client requirements</li>
<li>Strong Communication Skills</li>
<li>Ability to lead and motivate</li>
<li>Travel (up to 80%)</li>
</ul>
<b>Preferred Requirements:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Knowledge of the Post-Acute Care environment (Home Health, Skilled Nursing, Long-Term Care, Rehab, other).</li>
</ul>
About WellSky
<br><br>
WellSky is a technology company advancing human wellness worldwide. Our software and professional services address the continuum of health and social care — helping businesses, organizations, and communities solve tough challenges, improve collaboration for growth, and achieve better outcomes through predictive insights that only WellSky solutions can provide. A portfolio company of TPG Capital, WellSky serves more than 10,000 customer sites around the world — including the largest hospital systems, blood banks and labs, home health and hospice franchises, government agencies, and human services organizations. Informed by more than 30 years of providing software and expertise, WellSky anticipates providers' needs and innovates relentlessly to help people thrive. Our purpose is to empower care heroes with technology for good, so that together, we can realize care's potential and maintain a healthy, flourishing world.
