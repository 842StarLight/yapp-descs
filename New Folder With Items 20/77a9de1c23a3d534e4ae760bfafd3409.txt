<p>The Implementation Project Manager is a member of a team responsible for oversight of all Implementation activities related to installation and deployment of Amadeus Hospitality products and services from post-sale, through transition to Support. Responsibilities are performed in a fast paced environment and project work may range from managing standard repeatable services to custom software work.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p>This role is based in our Portsmouth, NH office and is not remote.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Essential Duties and Responsibilities
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Primary responsibility is management of software installation and associated services.
</li>
<li>Conduct project hand-off with sales and customer kick-off calls.
</li>
<li>Understand customer’s business needs and establish timeline.
</li>
<li>Conduct contract and documentation review as needed including SOW, DOU.
</li>
<li>Secure resources and communicate deliverables.
</li>
<li>Review and approve all information/documentation that is sent to our customer base and is the transition point for all incoming and outgoing hand offs within the organization.
</li>
<li>Responsible for effective and efficient delivery of all services at the highest quality.
</li>
<li>Responsible for forecasting accurate project revenue streams.
</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Primary point of contact for customer and Amadeus Hospitality’s resources pertaining to the implementation.
</li>
<li>Monitor project, manage scope changes and execute change orders.
</li>
<li>Responsible for accurately tracking and approving project tasks and associated revenue.
</li>
<li>Manage all first-level project or customer issues and problem escalation.
</li>
<li>Conducts project post-mortems and implements change when necessary.
</li>
<li>May participate in client facing meetings and lead project planning sessions to develop project strategy – a strong understanding of the customer business needs and goals is required.
</li>
<li>Other duties may be assigned.
</li>
<li>Travel is not required.<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Education and/or Experience
</b></p>
</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>BS Degree is Business or equivalent
</li>
<li>2-5 years customer-facing project implementation management experience in a software implementation role is required
</li>
<li>Experience managing multiple projects of varying complexity with simultaneous timelines
</li>
<li>Very strong organization and time management skills
</li>
<li>Exceptional written and verbal communication skills
</li>
<li>Salesforce experience desired
</li>
<li>Hospitality Background a plus
</li>
<li>Project Management Certification a plus<br>
</li>
</ul>
