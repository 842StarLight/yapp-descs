<h2>Brand Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div><div>SUMMARY:<br>
The Brand Manager is an integral member of the ecommerce team who manages the day to day operations, marketing, strategy, execution, analysis, growth and development of e-commerce site(s) within the National Tobacco portfolio. This brand manager reports to, and provides ongoing support to, the Vice President, Marketing.<br>
<br>
A Brand Manager is forward thinking, goal orientated, passionate about building and driving a healthy ecommerce ecosystem. Brand managers live and breathe their brands and enable collaboration with the marketing team as they formulate and execute large-scale ecommerce plans to ultimately achieve goals.<br>
A brand manager is energetic, creative and highly organized and able to prioritize tasks while staying committed to delivering quality work and meeting revenue goals &amp; deadlines.<br>
<br>
ESSENTIAL FUNCTIONS:<br>
<ul>
<li>Defining and driving key brand strategies across multiple marketing channels while optimizing revenue programs and pursue new customers/channels in an effort to increase online traffic and achieve established performance goals.</li>
<li>Ongoing research of new or competing products, and optimizing how we are found and how we present our products. Management of SEO, Email, Social, Paid &amp; Affiliate campaigns working with cross-functional teams and agencies.</li>
<li>Grow and nurture the customer database, engage idle customer list, and constantly optimizing profiling tools to enhance the customer’s journey.</li>
<li>Ensure weekly promotions are executed properly and gather performance data, prep analysis and synthesize daily, weekly, monthly sales performance to achieve KPIs</li>
<li>Other duties as necessary.</li>
</ul>
<br>
REQUIREMENTS:<br>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s Degree in marketing, business administration, e-commerce, public relations, advertising or a related field with/or 3-5 years of relevant marketing/ecommerce required.</li>
<li>Expertly skilled in MS Excel, Word, PowerPoint, Google Analytics, ecommerce platforms, Email marketing platforms, affiliate platforms, content management, and a variety of Social Media functions. Working knowledge of Adobe suite preferred</li>
<li>Experience with Magento, Jira, Post Affiliate Pro, Shopify and/or other related platforms is required.</li>
<li>Capable of sitting or standing for extended periods of time as needed for computer analytic work required as well as ability to lift and carry up to 30lbs of multimedia equipment or other materials as needed.</li>
<li>Experience working within regulated industries and working knowledge of FDA regulations pertaining to the promotion of tobacco related products preferred.</li>
<li>Organized, punctual, detail oriented, keen eye for design, proficient writer, creative, working knowledge of HTML &amp; CSS, and understanding of retail processes.</li>
<li>Responsible for creation, communication execution, reporting of all promotions and events for the brand.</li>
<li>Monitors growth of all digital channels and compiles reports, and partners with teams to optimize those funnels.</li>
</ul>
<br>
COMPETENCY INFORMATION:<br>
<ul>
<li>Ability to assume and maintain a position of brand leader within the organization with a willingness and ability to step into any of the other brands as needs arise.</li>
<li>Must be able to work in a PC and/or Mac based environment.</li>
<li>Advanced HTML Knowledge and knowledge of Social Media: Instagram, YouTube, Facebook, Google analytics and social metrics.</li>
<li>Previous experience with e-cigarettes, CBD and/or vaping products preferred.</li>
</ul>
