<h2>Staff Accountant - Dallas, TX</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Hillwood is currently seeking a Staff Accountant to join our Communities Group that is responsible for the development of master planned communities. The Staff Accountant will focus on project accounting, budgeting, cash forecasts and reporting.</p>
<p><b>
Responsibilities:</b></p>
<p><b>
Project Accounting:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Prepare monthly journal entries for assigned projects (accruals, COS, sales, etc.)</li>
<li>Projects will include development accounting and partnership accounting</li>
<li>Be the point person for the project and development managers for assigned projects.</li>
<li>Communicate and coordinate with other Hillwood personnel on all matters relating to the projects.</li>
<li>Prepare financial statements and reports as required for lenders, partners and internal Hillwood divisions</li>
<li>Prepare account reconciliations</li>
<li>Coordinate and complete annual audits</li>
<li>Review builder and vendor contracts to ensure terms are adhered to.</li>
<li>Review note and loan agreements and ensure compliance with all terms.</li>
<li>Prepare monthly loan draw packages and submit to lenders</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Budgeting &amp; Cash Forecasting:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Prepare annual budgets for projects, coordinate with Project and Development managers</li>
<li>Prepare/review budgets for Communities overhead, such as payroll, marketing and office/administrative costs</li>
<li>Prepare cash forecasts for assigned projects throughout the year, to be used internally or for outside parties.</li>
<li>Prepare variance analysis comparing actuals to budget or forecast</li>
<li>Anticipate cash needs, prepare capital calls for Partnerships.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Reporting:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Responsible for all aspects of reporting for assigned projects which includes reporting to internal Hillwood divisions, lenders, joint venture partners and districts.</li>
<li>Work with staff accountant and accounting assistant on weekly/monthly internal reporting.</li>
<li>Review weekly check selection, review coding of invoices</li>
<li>Write reports to automate and expedite reporting in either excel or software</li>
<li>Maintain fixed asset inventory</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Qualifications and Key Competencies</b>:</p>
<ul>
<li>1-2 years accounting experience, CPA or CPA eligible (Real Estate a plus)
</li>
<li>Accounting software experience (JDE preferred but not required);</li>
<li>Strong knowledge of accounts payable, accounts receivable, reconciliations, cash and accrual accounting methods, partnership accounting, development accounting and contracts.</li>
<li>Proficient in Excel, knowledgeable in Word &amp; PowerPoint</li>
<li>Strong communication and interpersonal skills, team player, great attitude</li>
<li>Strong organizational skills and detail oriented</li>
<li>Ability to work independently</li>
<li>Ability to work in a fast-paced environment and to multi-task</li>
</ul>
