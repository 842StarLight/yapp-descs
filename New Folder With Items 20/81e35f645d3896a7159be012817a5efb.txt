<h2>Operations Trainee</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p><b>Duties &amp; Responsibilities:</b></p>
<h5 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Inventory &amp; Purchasing Management</b></h5>
<ul><li>
Maintain appropriate inventory levels in the yard, control shrinkage &amp; damaged inventory, oversee monthly cycle counting &amp; close out procedures, and negotiate pricing, terms, conditions &amp; program details with vendors</li></ul>
<p><b>
Safety &amp; Warehouse Operations</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Responsible for training and enforcing that all yard employees adhere to company safety, driving, and machinery policies</li>
<li>
Organize and host weekly safety meetings with appropriate yard personnel</li>
<li>
Perform weekly and monthly GMS management safety reviews</li>
</ul>
<h5 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Dispatch &amp; Delivery</b></h5>
<ul>
<li>
Maintain open and clear lines of communication with customers at all times, informing them of any issues related to their order &amp; delivery if they should arise</li>
<li>
Monitor the delivery of materials from other yards to coordinate with agreed upon “Drop Dead” time</li>
</ul>
<h5 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Special projects may include:</b></h5>
<ul>
<li>
Case pick reduction</li>
<li>
Warehouse layout changes</li>
<li>
Implementing new software</li>
<li>
Reducing overweight trucks</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Requirements:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Bachelor’s degree preferred or equivalent work experience</li>
<li>
Effective communication skills, both written and verbal</li>
<li>
Internship or related work experience in a customer facing or sales role preferred</li>
<li>
Results oriented, attention to detail and good time management skills</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Company benefits:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
401k and Medical, Dental, Vision, Disability &amp; Life Insurance</li>
<li>
Paid holidays &amp; vacation days</li>
</ul>
