Position Purpose (Summary)
<br><br>
<b>The function of this position is to:</b><br>
This is a 120-day training position, after which the employee may be offered the opportunity to apply for a HVAC Installer or Service Technician position. There is no promise that a position shall be available or that the trainee will be offer employment after the 120-day training period. At the company’s discretion, it may alter the training period as it sees fit.
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Be willing participate following, helping, and assisting his assigned mentor learning and preparing for becoming a tech.</li>
<li>Perform other tasks as requested, including running parts, maintenance, janitorial, and clerical.</li>
<li>Eventually provide installation of warm air furnaces, air conditioners, duct distribution systems, water heaters, and related piping and electrical.</li>
<li>Assist consumers by providing information on options for improved operations of the HVAC equipment.</li>
<li>Can identify and report all issues that arise that would keep the equipment from functioning properly.</li>
<li>Communicate to the consumer on the proper operation and owner’s maintenance of the installed equipment. Answer related questions as to operation, features, and functions to the homeowner.</li>
<li>Perform required documentation for each call, recording recommendations, repairs, accurate readings and observations. Have client sign necessary paper work and collect payments as directed.</li>
</ul>
