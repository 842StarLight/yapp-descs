<h2>Customs Brokerage - Import Specialist - Fort Myers</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>AFC International, LLC </b>is seeking an <b>Import Specialist </b>to join our Customs Brokerage team</p>
<p><b>
Summary of Key Responsibilities</b></p>
<p><b>
Responsibilities and essential job functions include, but are not limited to the following:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Tracks inbound freight and prioritizes documents to ensure timely entry filing</li>
<li>
Prepares and submits timely and compliant customs entries using CargoWise One</li>
<li>
Makes corrections and updates to entries as necessary</li>
<li>
Prepares and executes billings for entry related services</li>
<li>
Determines tariffs and duty payments, tracks shipments and uses parts tables and the HTS to classify shipments</li>
<li>
Ensures all government required forms and information are compliant and provided timely</li>
<li>
Resolves both internal and external customer issues timely, accurately and professionally</li>
<li>
Works closely with other departments, including sales, to develop and sell new business</li>
<li>
Works with transportation agents, CBP and others to resolve issues as they pertain to entries</li>
<li>
Stays up-to-date on all customs procedural and regulatory changes</li>
<li>
Performs additional duties as assigned by management</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Knowledge, Skills, and Abilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
The ability to communicate clearly and persuasively via email, verbally, and in presentations, tailoring your messaging to the appropriate level of audience</li>
<li>
Strong organizational skills with ability to manage deadlines and prioritize workload, and make adjustments to meet business needs</li>
<li>
Ability to work in a blended role of operations and sales in an office environment</li>
<li>
Manages complex transactional or emotional customer situations promptly and professionally meeting commitments for service and assistance</li>
<li>
Ability to communicate complex concepts in easy-to-understand terminology</li>
<li>
Have a keen eye and display excellent attention to detail</li>
<li>
Ability to read, write, and speak English fluently</li>
<li>
Ability to travel to meet the demands of training, staffing, and customer expectations</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Qualifications</b></p>
<p><b>
Summary of Experience and Education</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Three (3) plus years of relevant customs brokerage experience processing varied import entry air and ocean transactions. An Associate or Bachelor's degree in a related field of study is a plus</li>
<li>
Proficient in Microsoft Office Excel, Outlook and Word</li>
<li>
Proficiency in Cargowise One Software System is a plus</li>
<li>
Customs brokerage license or Certified Customs Specialist (CCS) is a plus</li>
</ul>
