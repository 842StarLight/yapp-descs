POSITION OVERVIEW
<br><br>
Lytics is growing and we’re hiring additional Senior Account Executives in multiple US locations to join our team during a critical and exciting time in the fast-growing “Martech” and enterprise software sector. Our Senior AE's are responsible for driving and closing new logo &amp; incremental business with enterprise-level clients. You are a strategic and consultative salesperson who understands the importance of finding the right solution for your clients. You are laser-focused throughout all stages of the sales cycle and are experienced in selling a highly complex and technical SaaS solution to enterprise-level clients. You have a passion for data, analytics, technology and digital transformation.
<br><br>
<b>Responsibilities:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Drive new business and incremental revenue across your assigned territory</li>
<li>Create thoughtful and strategic sales plans to deliver maximum revenue potential</li>
<li>Manage, prospect, qualify, forecast and generate pipeline and sales activity in Salesforce in order to hit revenue targets and quotas</li>
<li>Champion Lytics' platform and product offerings for prospective clients through product demonstrations, trade-shows, market events in close collaboration with our Solutions Engineering team</li>
<li>Lead strategy with pricing, new business, expanding existing relationships</li>
<li>Deliver to achieve against monthly and quarterly quotas</li>
<li>Partner with Solutions Engineering, Product Marketing, Sales Enablement, Sales Development, Professional Services and Customer Success to execute a seamless experience for our new and existing clients</li>
</ul>
<b>Qualifications:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>BS/BA degree required or equivalent combination of education/experience</li>
<li>Experience selling a complex SaaS platform, added bonus if you’ve had exposure to MarTech</li>
<li>Proven track record of consistent overachievement of quotas and revenue goals within large enterprise accounts</li>
<li>Consistently and successfully managing sales process with complex technology, deal and pricing structures</li>
<li>Consultative and solutions-oriented sales style with proven ability to effectively identify and sell to C-level executives, leveraging value and insight selling best practices</li>
<li>Strong business acumen and ability to respond positively and strategically to challenges or objections</li>
<li>Ability to work in a fast-paced, evolving environment</li>

</ul>
