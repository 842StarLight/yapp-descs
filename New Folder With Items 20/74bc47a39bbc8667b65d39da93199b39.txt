<h2>Store Communication Coordinator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p>Job Title: Store Communication Coordinator</p>
<p>
Reports to: Senior Manager of Operations</p>
<p>
Department: Store Operations</p>
<p>
Exempt Status: Exempt</p>
<p>
Position Overview:</p>
<p>
The Store Communication Coordinator is responsible is to be a liaison between the stores in the field and the corporate office (Store Support Center). They serve as the primary point of contact between the field and all departments within the Store Support Center, to ensure consistent communication across the company.</p>
<p>
Primary Responsibilities:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Evaluate and properly address store questions and issues within 24 hours (excluding weekends).</li>
<li>
Identify and address potential issues proactively.</li>
<li>
Develop strong partnerships with field management and other departments.</li>
<li>
Assess issues to properly communicate with appropriate corresponding business partners.</li>
<li>
Produce daily email communication to the field and Support Center.</li>
<li>
Propose and implement initiatives to improve operational processes to increase efficiency and consistency among stores.</li>
<li>
Maintain and track any store-related initiatives and analyze effectiveness.</li>
<li>
Partner with Information Technology to register test Point-Of-Sale updates and troubleshoot.</li>
<li>
Compile and process data requested by stores or business partners.</li>
<li>
Update weekly, monthly, quarterly and yearly reports and documents.</li>
<li>
Complete any additional responsibilities and/or duties as assigned.</li>
</ul>
<p>
Qualifications:</p>
<ul>
<li>
High School diploma or equivalent required.</li>
<li>
College education or similar experience preferred.</li>
<li>
Excellent interpersonal, listening, and communication skills - both written and verbal.</li>
<li>
Familiarity with Microsoft products such as Word, Excel, Outlook, etc.</li>
<li>
Maintaining strong planning, prioritization, and organizational skills while juggling multiple priorities.</li>
<li>
Ability to work effectively in a professional environment while exercising sound judgment.</li>
<li>
Flexibility to work both independently and as part of a team.</li>
<li>
A minimum of one year of retail experience, preferably at a management level.</li>
</ul>
<p>
Physical Requirements:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Physical ability to sit for extended periods, working at a computer.</li>
<li>
Ability to see, hear, and speak regularly.</li>
<li>
Ability to operate all equipment necessary to perform the job.</li>
<li>
Ability to work in a constant state of alertness and safe manner.</li>
<li>
Willing and able to travel on a minimal basis.</li>
</ul>
