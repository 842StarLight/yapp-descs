<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<ul>
<li>Draft, review, negotiate, and administer commercial technology agreements related to the provision of software and services, including, licensing, SaaS, and maintenance agreements</li>
<li>
Negotiate contracts in conjunction with business and technology personnel</li>
<li>
Add value throughout the sales process and post-signature contract management, including review and negotiation of project proposals, contracts, and amendments, familiarizing delivery teams on exceptional provisions in negotiated contracts, and advising project managers with respect to contract terms</li>
<li>
Provide legal support and guidance to supervisory management and other business executives</li>
<li>
Ensure compliance with corporate and divisional policies, and acceptable risk standards</li>
<li>
Liaise with internal stakeholders to promote sound business decisions</li>
<li>
Assist in making updates to corporate legal strategy</li>
<li>
Advise management of contractual rights and obligations and assist on requests for analysis of contracts in connection with other business transactions</li>
<li>
Actively identify and drive improvements to contract risk management and contracting efficiency</li>
<li>
Train sales and delivery teams to enhance their working knowledge of contract risk management issues and mitigation paths over time</li>
<li>
Research and guide internal stakeholders regarding new and updated legislation and regulations related to software licensing and data privacy and security</li>
</ul>
</div></div></div>
