<h2>Meeting and Event Planning Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p><b>Section 1: Position Summary</b></p>
<p>
The Meeting and Event Planning Specialist will help in the development and advancement of Ascensus’ B2B event marketing strategy for multi-channel events and sponsorships. This position will serve as the lead on multiple medium- to large-scale conferences, C-level meetings, and trade shows annually, and at times, simultaneously.</p>
<p></p>
<p>
He or she will manage all event-related communications, including invitations, marketing ads/collateral, agendas, attendee travel information, executive briefing documents, and event management software.</p>
<p></p>
<p>
The ideal candidate has financial services experience and is a quick learner who can think strategically and operate effectively in a fast-paced, deadline-driven environment. This is a client-facing role, which will work directly alongside supporting members of the marketing, sales, and business development teams to deliver unforgettable experiences to our key partners, advisors, associates, and clients.</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Section 2: Job Functions, Essential Duties and Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Work with Ascensus’ marketing, sales, and business development teams to identify and execute on events that align with business objectives, demonstrate positive ROI, and provide attendees with an integrated, consistent experience across the Ascensus brand.</li>
<li>
Source venues, recommend and engage catering, and coordinate production of required marketing materials and signage to execute high-quality events.</li>
<li>
Collaborate with various departments to effectively promote events to target audiences.</li>
<li>
Prepare pre-event communications for attendees and run of show document for internal stakeholders.</li>
<li>
Act as the direct point person for key stakeholders and vendors during the planning, on-site, and post-event phases and provide full-service logistical support throughout the event.</li>
<li>
Maintain an annual event calendar and implement a system that anticipates long-term planning and effective event management for the organization’s meetings, conferences, trade shows, and events.</li>
<li>
Oversee inventory of giveaways in anticipation of upcoming events and conferences, as well as research and recommend new items that are cost-effective and tie back to the corporate brand.</li>
<li>
Maintain and manage the vendor relationship for the Ascensus brand store, making branded premium items available for purchase by Ascensus leaders, employees, key partners, and clients.</li>
<li>
Proactively prepare backup plans and solve potential and immediate issues relating to the successful execution of event programs.</li>
<li>
Conduct post-event surveys of attendees to enable process improvement, and provide post-event analysis, budget recaps, and participant feedback, incorporating learning into future plans.</li>
<li>
Support internal events as required, such as key client meetings, executive retreats, etc.</li>
<li>
Take a consultative approach and use effective communication skills to present event strategies and secure buy-in from key leaders;</li>
<li>
Manage collaboration across internal team members and external vendor partners to ensure successful execution of all key events; and</li>
<li>
Represent marketing at key meetings to provide status updates, planning decisions, innovative solutions, and brand governance.</li>
<li>
Responsible for protecting, securing, and proper handling of all confidential data held by Ascensus to ensure against unauthorized access, improper transmission, and/or unapproved disclosure of information that could result in harm to Ascensus or our clients.</li>
<li>
Our I-Client service philosophy and our Core Values of People Matter, Quality First and Integrity Always® should be visible in your actions on a day to day basis showing your support of our organizational culture.</li>
<li>
Assist with other tasks and projects as assigned</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Supervision</b></p>
<ul><li>
N/A</li></ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Section 3: Experience, Skills, Knowledge Requirements
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Experience with planning various event types, including C-level executive conferences; investor and client meetings; networking receptions; associate appreciation initiatives; and industry tradeshows and conferences.</li>
<li>
Proven ability to take direction and translate organizational goals into effective, engaging events that deliver an impactful attendee experience.</li>
<li>
Experience minimizing financial risk and maximizing ROI during the vendor sourcing and contract negotiation phases.</li>
<li>
Ability to foster professional relationships that result in consistent and courteous communication with clients and colleagues.</li>
<li>
Excellent organizational, planning, and project management skills.</li>
<li>
Independent and proactive thinker adept at setting goals, prioritizing, and making decisions.</li>
<li>
Exceptional written and verbal communication skills.</li>
<li>
Experience working with Microsoft Office Product Suite, including Word, Excel, PowerPoint.</li>
<li>
Experience working with and/or developing custom branded destination pages in leading event management platforms (such as Cvent) preferred.</li>
<li>
Ability to work a flexible schedule with occasional evening/or weekends with approximately 20% travel.</li>
<li>
Bachelor’s Degree in business, marketing, or a related field.</li>
<li>
A minimum of 5 years of event management experience in a fast-paced corporate environment required.</li>
<li>
Financial services industry experience preferred</li>
</ul>
