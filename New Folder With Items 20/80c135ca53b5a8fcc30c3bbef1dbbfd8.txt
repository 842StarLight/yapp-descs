<h2>Senior Agile Coach</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div><b>Job Overview:</b></div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>The Senior Agile Coach will arm the organization with the right knowledge, tools and training to use agile to its full potential. The Senior Agile Coach helps individuals, teams and leaders adopt and improve agile mindset, methods and practices. The Senior Coach seeks to understand where the teams are in their agile journey and employ the right approach to take them to the desired maturity. The coach will leverage knowledge and experience in fundamentals of frameworks and methodologies, such as: Agile and /or Scrum, scaled agile frameworks (i.e. SAFe, LESS etc.), Design Thinking, Lean / Six Sigma / Kanban, Lean Start Up, Product Development, Organization Development / Behavior, Business process design/management. Effective coaches know that one size does not fit all and will employ teaching, mentoring, facilitation, counseling, partnering, modeling, reflective observation and other techniques as required by the team’s circumstances. The coach helps teams apply agile and lean thinking to the specific environment and impediments they face. The ideal candidate will have large body of work in implementing agile projects at the enterprise level and will be able share that experience, provide feedback and advice. Senior Agile Coach seeks to improve collaboration, innovation, and will introduce techniques that promote adaptability and flexibility in the face of unpredictable circumstances. The Senior coach will use data (productivity, team health, and value delivery metrics) skillfully to asses and guide the organization to better agility. The Senior Agile Coach will use techniques that increase collaboration, predictability, transparency and promote a culture of experimentation and innovation. The Senior Coach will embody the agile principles and lead by example. Ideal person should demonstrate and value honesty, fairness, openness, listening, is respectful to all team members, and has excellent communication skills. Perform other duties as assigned.</div>

<div><b>Essential Functions:</b></div>

</div></li>
<li><div>Employs teaching, mentoring, and professional coaching techniques as appropriate for the circumstances and people to increase the agile knowledge, mindset and execution of agile principles of the coaches.

<li><div>Exposes our teams to the latest innovative thinking and ideas from the Agile community to increase agility.
</div></li>
</ul>
<ul><li>Draws from multiple frameworks and developmental models to provide a customized coaching approach to team / individual. Incorporates strong and fast feedback loops in the coaching practice to improve both the agility of the coaches and the coaching itself.</li></ul>
