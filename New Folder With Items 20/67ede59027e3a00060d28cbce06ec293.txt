<p>At Workiva we create best-in-class, next-generation collaborative solutions for enterprise productivity, risk, and compliance. We pride ourselves on bringing the consumer level user experience to business users. We love our customers, and they love us back. <p>

Our technology stack primarily consists of Python, Java, and Go on the backend and Javascript, HTML, Dart, and CSS on the front-end. We have plenty of opportunity for new skills, experiences, and tools. We promote openness through open source contributions (github.com/workiva).</p>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
What You’ll Do:</b></h3>
<ul>
<li>
Participate in a rotation of infrastructure software development and operations work.</li>
<li>
Architect, implement, and support infrastructure tooling and services in AWS and Google.</li>
<li>
Develop standards and procedures to maintain consistent practices for administration of production applications and systems.</li>
<li>
Collaborate and consult with development teams and support engineers to resolve critical issues and assist in design decisions and best practices regarding cloud technologies.</li>
<li>
Work with cloud vendors and external technical support on upgrades, problem resolution, and design issues.</li>
<li>
Monitor, maintain, and enhance systems to ensure optimal performance and availability of cloud infrastructure and applications.</li>
</ul>
