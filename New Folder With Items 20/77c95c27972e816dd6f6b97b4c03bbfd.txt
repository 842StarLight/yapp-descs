<h2>Director, Sales Enablement</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">Because you belong at Twilio
<br><br>
The Who, What, Where and Why
<br><br>
Who?
<br><br>
The Twilio Sales Enablement organization's mission statement is to "help reps look like heroes to customers and prospects" and we are looking for a leader to drive toward this outcome. The successful Director, Global Sales Enablement at Twilio will be a proven enablement leader with experience serving scaled enterprise sales organizations, but hasn't lost their "do what it takes" attitude and "builder" spirit. They will have deep domain expertise on sales training modalities and sales methodologies (and mentoring new career reps into enterprise reps), but will know when to break the mould and design something custom. The best candidates will thrive in a fast-paced, high-growth environment, will have built high-performance enablement programs from scratch, and will also have supported a global, scaled sales enterprise sales team of 500+ in their career.
<br><br>
Our perfect Director, Sales Enablement will set the bar for cross-functional collaboration, best practice implementation, and operational cadence. They will be incredibly focused on improving sales productivity and customer outcomes by delivering a best-in-class enablement machine. This person will have a strong track record of scalably accelerating sales knowledge and comprehension for global sales teams, but will also have a coach's mentality—being willing to go 1:1 where necessary to provide guidance or remediation of varying technical depths to help individuals achieve their goals.
<br><br>
Additionally, our perfect candidate will be focused on increasing impact over time through the professional development of 6 superstar enablement professionals, plus additional to-be-hired team members.
<br><br>
What?
<br><br>
This role will focus primarily on four major workstreams—enablement strategy, sales tools and methodology, training processes and systems, and impact metrics and operational excellence for the team.
<br><br>
You'll drive enablement strategy
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Develop and direct successful execution of enablement strategies for all major regions and sales team segments, with focus on developing best-in-class Enterprise selling programs</li>
<li>Work across leadership layers to discover requirements, build consensus, and proactively take feedback from our key internal stakeholders</li>
<li>Ensure AEs, partners, and other customer-facing field representatives are equipped with the content, tools, training, and resources to differentiate and sell Twilio's value to customers</li>
<li>Leverage Product Marketing for content and share field input with these teams in order to optimize messaging and product direction</li>
</ul>
You'll help develop sales tools, methodology, and selling programs
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Work in partnership with Sales Leadership, Product Marketing, Market Strategy/Sales Intelligence to document and create tools for Twilio's most "transformational" solutions across products, markets, and industries</li>
<li>Make selling at Twilio easier, more repeatable and procedural, and less time-consuming by delivering a best-in-class sales playbook and sales plays library</li>
<li>Direct development of sales enablement tools and enablement materials that support the sales force, tailored for their unique region or sales segment</li>
<li>Support the global roll out of a custom Twilio selling methodology and related sales tools and support structures</li>
</ul>
You'll drive training processes and systems
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Create and facilitate a highly engaging (and fun!) learning environment utilizing including technologies as well as case studies, simulations, role plays, and other interactive methods</li>
<li>Improve time to productivity with thoughtful, comprehensive sales training programs, including new hire onboarding and continuous improvement sales training programs</li>
<li>Partner with GTM Leadership to find and fill knowledge gaps across the organization</li>
<li>Conduct ongoing needs assessments and roll out targeted programs</li>
</ul>
You'll deliver impact metrics and operational best practices
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Develop and create accountability to a global sales enablement calendar, inclusive of major "team owned" milestones (SKO, Summer SKO), training requirements, sales team events (QBRs, etc), and other programs with operational cadence</li>
<li>Measure and report on the effectiveness of sales enablement investments and major programs to executive leadership</li>
<li>Create alignment between sales enablement efforts and key internal and external events, such as new product releases, campaigns, trade shows, and corporate initiatives</li>
</ul>

<br><br>
Twilio is transforming the way the largest companies in the world communicate by empowering developers to build any customer experience they can imagine on a scalable, trusted and flexible platform. The product marketing team at Twilio is responsible for defining the next chapter of customer engagement through world-class messaging and positioning, storytelling and customer insights.
<br><br>
Twilio is truly unique; we are a company committed to your growth, your learning, your development and your entire employee experience. We only win when our employees succeed and we're dedicated to helping you develop your strengths. We invest in weeks dedicated to tackling hard problems and creating your own ideas. We have a cultural foundation built on diversity, inclusion and innovation and we want you and your ideas to thrive at Twilio. Come join us.
