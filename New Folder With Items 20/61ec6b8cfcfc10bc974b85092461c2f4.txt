<h2>East Coast Regional Director</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<b>Who we are:</b><br>
Unite Us is reinventing the delivery of health and human services. We connect service providers on a common platform, enabling scalable, accountable and measurable delivery of wraparound care. Our technology provides the collaborative infrastructure for these communities. We care deeply about the work we do and the communities our software benefits. We're looking for people to join our team who share that passion for our mission to reinvent Health &amp; Human Services and aspire to make a lasting difference for future generations. No matter how large our team grows, we will always be family. Unite Us prides itself on offering a competitive salary, full benefits, and the opportunity to change the world. Come to Unite Us and together we can build healthier communities for everyone.
<br><br>
<b>Description:</b><br>
Unite Us is seeking a Regional Director to support company efforts for one of the top integrated healthcare systems in the country. This person should have both program management and team leadership experience, and a passion for public health. The Regional Director will be responsible for communication, implementation, launch, roll-out, and growth of either the east or west coast region while supporting localized teams. The Regional Director will work hand-in-hand with the National Director and National Deputy Director to ensure consistent communication and feedback is shared between service regions and headquarters; ensuring all goals are met and the on-the-ground team has guidance and feels successful. Success in this role will require performance management to drive both client and company results, industry expertise and acumen, and the ability to thrive in a high growth and fast paced company environment.
<br><br>
<b>What You'll Do:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Project Strategy &amp; Oversight</li>
<li>Collaborate with National Director, National Deputy Director and National Project Manager to create and coordinate implementation of a detailed rollout plan that is executed at the local level and meets contracted deadlines. Adapt the project plan as necessary to align with changing project priorities and requirements &amp; communicate changes to your team.</li>
<li>Partner with local Senior Account Managers, Project Managers, and/or Community Engagement Managers to implement marketing activities and events</li>
<li>Leadership and Support</li>
<li>Lead regional account teams consisting of 3-10 team members &amp; create a collaborative team culture that promotes excellence.</li>
<li>Serve as a manager and strategic and operational advisor to local Senior Account Managers on the launch plan implementation.</li>
<li>Track progress on local implementation and provide progress reports to Unite Us &amp; client Leadership teams.</li>
<li>Ensure that best practices and lessons learned are shared and applied across local project teams</li>
<li>Manage and support the professional development of Unite Us team members and assist with the recruiting and training of new staff</li>
<li>Lead regular team meetings to foster team discussion and sharing; keep the team informed of process changes and best practices</li>
<li>Track &amp; report on Key Performance Indicators for network success based on Unite Us best practices and client priorities</li>
<li>Project Planning and Management</li>
<li>Set attainable launch goals and KPIs for regional project teams</li>
<li>Work with other members of the project-specific leadership team to integrate specific reporting requirements of key stakeholders into software configuration and data plan</li>
<li>Identify project risks and problems at the regional and enterprise level &amp; coordinate resources and solve problems</li>
<li>Identify product improvements and work internally with the Unite Product and Engineering teams towards solutions</li>
<li>Collaborate cross-team with Sales, Marketing, and Product to streamline workflows and create a seamless customer experience</li>
<li>Client Management</li>
<li>Build and maintain strong, long-lasting customer relationships with key stakeholders and executive sponsors by becoming a valuable resource on project progress</li>
<li>Report on project progress to key stakeholders</li>
<li>Collect customer feedback on a regular basis and share relevant product/service feedback</li>
</ul>
<b>What's Required:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Refined leadership skills - you are committed to setting your team up for success and you expect great results</li>
<li>Demonstrated success managing complex projects and implementations with confidence</li>
<li>Self-starter motivation with strong organizational and time management skills – you make things happen!</li>
<li>Analytical ability to leverage data to drive decisions and recommendations</li>
<li>Demonstrated GSD "Get Stuff Done" attitude and results</li>
<li>Emotional intelligence- you speak with candor and authenticity and you know your audience</li>
<li>Both professionalism and determination- you build trust with clients by consistently overdelivering</li>
<li>Excellent communication and presentation skills – you present and influence credibly and effectively at all levels of the organization, including executive and C-level</li>
<li>Outstanding interpersonal skills and ability to communicate clearly and effectively (both written and verbal)</li>
<li>Proven ability to manage multiple projects at a time while paying strict attention to detail</li>
<li>The ability to thrive in a variable and demanding startup environment - you are solutions oriented and highly adaptable</li>
<li>A passion for healthcare or human services, and a familiarity with/ knowledge of the health and human services landscape</li>
</ul>
Education and Experience
