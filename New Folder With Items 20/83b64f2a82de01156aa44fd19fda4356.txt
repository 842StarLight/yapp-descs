<h2>Director, Consumer Digital Privacy & Compliance</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>The Integrity &amp; Compliance team is seeking a leader for our Consumer Digital Privacy &amp; Compliance team. As the Director, you will lead complex, multi-disciplinary compliance team focused on digital. You will oversee the digital compliance frame work for the following key pillars: Privacy Assessments, Digital Disclosures, Advertising Technology and Accessibility. You will foster and develop tools and processes to ensure compliance with privacy policies and standards, such as data mapping and DPIA’s, and assist business units to develop and efficiently utilize tools and best practices to adhere for ongoing compliance. This role requires a special combination of privacy expertise, technical experience, organizational skills, and the ability to influence. Part of this role will also be to help iterate and improve privacy processes and infrastructure.<br>
<br>
This role will partner with multiple business units within the Consumer Digital team and across the enterprise, including Corporate Privacy, Comm Co Privacy, Legal, Corporate Compliance, Public Policy, Marketing, Channels and External Affairs.<br>
<br>
Responsibilities<br>
<ul>
<li>Manage AT&amp;T’s digital privacy projects and activities</li>
<li>
Facilitate privacy discussions and build collaborative relationships between different functions at AT&amp;T-DE including Sales, Services, Operations, and outside DE i.e. Legal, Corporate Compliance, Public Policy, Marketing and Home Solutions</li>
<li>
Lead internal process improvement projects, including the development and implementation of internal tools</li>
<li>
Lead Privacy Impact Analysis creation and reviews for DE business units</li>
<li>
Assist in building out marketing preferences and compliance Do-Not’s such as do-not-contact and CAN-SPAM opt outs to non-compliant practices</li>
<li>
Conduct refresher training sessions and workshops on complying with laws like the Telephone Consumer Protection Act and the CAN Spam Act</li>
<li>
Create and manage consumer privacy education campaigns</li>
<li>
Lead creation and maintenance AT&amp;T Digital Privacy, Accessibility and Compliance strategies, policies and practices in collaboration with critical business partners.</li>
<li>
Develop, support and lead a team of knowledgeable Privacy and Accessibility experts supporting continuous building of best-in-class Monitor developments in US privacy requirements and best practices.</li>
<li>
Identify privacy-related risks for all areas of digital marketing, sales and service as well as AdTech and Tag management and establishes operational practices and procedures to reduce them.</li>
<li>
Work closely with the Data Protection &amp; Privacy Operations team to support compliance monitoring and enforcement capabilities and activities</li>
<li>
Set team strategy for monitoring and understanding developments in relevant US accessibility standards at Federal and State levels, W3C Web Content Accessibility Guidelines, and Corporate Accessibility Technology Office (CATO) Accessibility Standards.</li>
</ul>
