<h2>Order Entry/Customer Service</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Job Description</p>
<p><b>Job Summary: </b></p>
<p>Carries out all customer service and office duties such as responding to inquiries and processing orders in a prompt, courteous and effective manner. Other responsibilities include data entry, phone duties, processing credits, invoicing and other clerical duties as directed.</p>
<p><b>Essential Functions: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Receives orders and processes by delegating to the proper departments.</li>
<li>Assisting in the management of incoming orders. This includes providing out of stock, backorders, tracking numbers, processing credit cards, and providing other general information to the customer upon request related to PO’s.</li>
<li>Work with inventory management to follow up on ETA’s for fill-in’s to communicate back-in-stock dates to customers and the sales team.</li>
<li>Answer phone calls and maintain professional and courteous relationships from sales reps &amp; customers.</li>
<li>Responding to all inquiries in a timely manner.</li>
<li>Checks inventory for stock availability.</li>
<li>Monitoring shipping requests with the plant manager, expediting orders as necessary.</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Handle returned merchandise and issue call tags in an efficient manner and ensure proper credit is provided to the customer.</li>
<li>Report customer feedback to management concerning quality</li>
<li>Communicates to customer inquires regarding order processing and any order changes.</li>
<li>Assist in scheduling various repairmen or service men to correct a problem.</li>
<li>Example: plumbing service, or alarm service calls, building issues. etc.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Required Experience</b></p>
<p>*</p>
<p>- Expert level knowledge/experience ERP software such as “Sage 100”</p>
<p>- Strong computer skills; knowledge of Microsoft Excel</p>
<p>- Bachelor's degree in accounting, business administration, communications, management, or related field</p>
<p>- Minimum of 3 years of related work experience</p>
<p>- General understanding of Accounts Payable/Receivable</p>
<p>- Knowledge in the apparel or textile industry</p>
<p>- Experience in creating shipments for UPS, FedEx, USPS</p>
<p>- Strong interpersonal, verbal and written communication skills</p>
<p>- High level of self-motivation and creative problem solving ability</p>
<p>- Strong decision making ability and attention to detail</p>
<p>- Excellent time management skills, multitasking, and organizational skills</p>
<p>- Desire to work closely with a team in a cooperative setting</p>
<p>- Confident, mature, and positive attitude with external and internal communications</p>
<p>- Commitment to a high performance culture</p>
