<h2>Marketing Coordinator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div><div>Summary<br>
The marketing coordinator will be participating in Alterra’s advertising campaigns. The main responsibilities include being the main concierge for Alterra’s Salesforce, conducting market research, producing promotional materials, utilizing social media and webinar systems and analyzing sales data.<br>
<br>
Essential Duties and Responsibilities<br>
<ul>
<li>Conduct research to analyze customers’ behavior (e.g. purchasing habits, trends and preferences)</li>
<li>Design and implement successful marketing campaigns</li>
<li>Set up tracking systems for online marketing activities</li>
<li>Track progress with Marketing Managers and/or Marketing Specialists</li>
<li>Identify and analyze competitors</li>
<li>Prepare reports by collecting and analyzing sales data</li>
<li>Collaborate with the design department to produce promotional materials</li>
<li>Setup advertising accounts (payment set-up)</li>
<li>Organize promotional activities for new products/services</li>
<li>Prepare (monthly, quarterly and annual) forecasts</li>
</ul>
<br>
Disclaimer: Job description is not a complete list of duties. The Marketing Coordinator may and/or will be required to perform other functions as asked by management. The Marketing Coordinator must be self-sufficient and show the initiative to self-educate in industry products and program guidelines.<br>
<br>
Supervisory Responsibilities<br>
This position has no supervisory responsibilities.<br>
