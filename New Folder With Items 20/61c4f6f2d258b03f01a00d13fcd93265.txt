<div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Overview</b></h2>
<div>
Come lead as a Senior BSA Manager for Intuit’s evolution of the enterprise billing systems. We're looking for a dynamic, intuitive, positive and creative individual with the ability to manage and work with a talented testing and reporting team to analyze, design, implement and support Intuit’s next generation of enterprise billing platforms.<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<div>You’ll join a company whose culture, from individual contributor to executive staff to the CEO, is characterized by the terms Lean, Agile, and Customer Focus. And you’ll be a significant part of accelerating growth for the diversity of Intuit functional and business units.</div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Responsibilities</b></h2>
<ul>
<li>
Lead and grow a high performing Testing and Reporting team to deliver at scale with operational excellence built in.
</li>
<li>Create an environment where world class BSAs can do the best work of their lives. In this role you will help to bring out the best ideas from the best BSAs by empowering them and leading by example.</li>
<li>
Instil and drive best practices with the team to maximize excellence and productivity</li>
<li>
Employ strong business and technical acumen to guide the team (customer obsession, strategic thinking, asking the right questions, sweating the details).</li>
<li>
Create shared vision, build and maintain strong cross-functional relationships with partners and stakeholders.</li>
<li>
Partner with Product Management to understand requirements, prioritize tasks, and incorporate new features into existing products</li>
<li>
Build a culture of Innovation, technical and operational excellence on the team.</li>
<li>
Develop business cases for taking on new work and conduct resource estimation to justify spend for new capabilities.</li>
<li>
Lead through influence: Actively participate in discussions with senior business leaders making convincing arguments that inform decisions. Develop concise and compelling written communications that build the case for change and articulate solutions.
</li>
<li>Problem solving and analytics: Understand business performance through dashboards and data that you help create or gather. Use data to recommend creative and innovative solutions. Frame issues in a compelling way and drive resolution to them, providing focused quantitative or qualitative analytical and research support as input into decision making.
</li>
<li>Rigorous execution: Lead or co-lead strategic initiatives and discrete projects. Develop clear action plans. Suggest relevant success metrics and targets. Manage timeline and process.</li>
<li>
Cross-functional leadership and capability building: Build and maintain strong relationships inside and outside the organization to ensure strong collaboration, raise the profile of the business and its key projects, and capitalize on emerging opportunities. Help foster a culture of continuous reinvention and customer obsession.</li>
</ul>

BS/MS in Computer Science, Finance or related field.</li>

8+ years experience as an established leader of software testing and or reporting development teams
</li>

</li>
<li>Demonstrated experience leading positive change, empowering people, cultivating product technology visions and innovative solutions, and fostering effective teams.</li>
<li>
Organizational competencies and project management skills to keep projects, processes, and the entire team on track in a distributed global ecosystem.</li>
<li>
Excellent interpersonal communication and presentation skills with expertise in distilling complicated topics to a broader audience.</li>
<li>
Experience with Agile software development.</li>
<li>
A passion for building a great working environment where the best want to come work and stay</li>
<li>
Implementation experience at large organizations is required.</li>
<li>
Exceptional collaboration and relationship-building skills; leads and influences across boundaries and considered a trusted partner to peers and executives alike.</li>
<li>
Strong written and verbal communication skills.
</li>
<li>Able to think both strategically and tactically - can devise and execute an action plan (strong process and project management skills).
</li>
<li>Highly self-motivated, able to manage multiple projects with little day to day direction. Able to both lead and support.</li>
<li>
Exceptional analytical problem solving ability.</li>
<li>
High level of professionalism, passion, and sense of urgency</li>
</ul>
