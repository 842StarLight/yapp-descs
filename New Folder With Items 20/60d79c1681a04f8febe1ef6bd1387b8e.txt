<h2>Senior Director, Services Portfolio Product Management</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>We are looking for a leader to build Product Management function for entire Nutanix services portfolio including consulting, education, residents and technical services. This role will be part of our dynamic and growing Customer Success leadership team driving expansion and adoption of our full-stack enterprise cloud products across our top customers worldwide.</p>
<p><b>
Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Define and develop services portfolio strategy and roadmap. Translate customer needs into services that complement products to achieve outcomes.</li>
<li>
Create services offers aligned to solution focus areas in Private Cloud, End User Computing, Databases, Business Critical Applications, Analytics, ROBO and Cloud-Native Applications.</li>
<li>
Manage the product lifecycle for services spanning consulting, education, technical account management, and residents. Drive packaging and pricing decisions.</li>
<li>
Deliver simplified offers to sales teams and partner with them on customer engagements.</li>
<li>
Collaborate with product teams to drive customer outcomes with services and delivery teams to drive continuous improvements.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Requirements:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
BS/MS in Engineering or related fields. MBA preferred.</li>
<li>
10+ years of product management or business development experience preferably with services.</li>
<li>
Domain experience with hybrid cloud, enterprise infrastructure, data center transformation, application delivery</li>
<li>
Must have business acumen and ability to build business cases, communicate effectively, influence others, and measure success</li>
<li>
Ability to be self-motivated towards the achievement and measurement of established goals</li>
<li>
Aptitude to develop and maintain satisfactory working relationships with customers, sellers/SEs, engineering, and cross-functional teams.</li>
<li>
Attention to detail in executing complex projects.</li>
</ul>
