<h2>Sr. Production Coordinator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Please review the job details below.
</b></h2>
<p></p>Maxar is seeking a Production Coordinator who will monitor the status of imagery acquisition and production processing, provide production feasibility status, generate reports on production volumetric data and provide performance status to internal/external customers as needed. You will also support shared tasks from Senior and Lead Production Coordinators for execution and monitoring of production scenarios. In this role, you will utilize a combination of proprietary and commercial-off-the-shelf software suites to perform work required.
<p></p>
<p><b>Responsibilities
</b></p>
<ul>
<li><p>Evaluation and selection of source material required for imagery based projects
</p></li>
<li><p>Report generation on production volumes
</p></li>
<li><p>Order and acquisition tracking and troubleshooting
</p></li>
<li><p>Development and maintenance of GIS systems related to inventory management
</p></li>
<li><p>Production planning and feasibility support for imagery and elevation products
</p></li>
<li><p>Performance monitoring and metric creation
</p></li>
<li><p>Support cross platform production planning and material selection requirements for projects and products
</p></li>
<li><p>Perform basic order entry processes in support of product fulfillment
</p></li>
<li><p>Identify data required for product generation to include commercial imagery source, ground control, elevation data and other sources.
</p></li>
<li><p>Provide interdepartmental interfaces between operations, customer service, sales and marketing for production status and order processing.
</p></li>
</ul>
<p></p>
