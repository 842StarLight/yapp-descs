The Role
<br><br>
The Sales Operations Analyst is responsible for helping us align and improve the way Sales and Marketing work together across the company, with an emphasis on leveraging our CRM (Salesforce) and Marketing Automation Platform (Marketo). Additional responsibilities include supporting sales processes, creating bookings reports for Executives and providing insights to Sales leadership as well as owning our Sales Reporting tools. This will be a highly visible role that requires someone who can build strong cross-departmental relationships, understand business requirements, and effectively deliver results to our internal stakeholders.
<br><br>
Responsibilities
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Support the development of sales and operations strategy, understand priorities and execute operational plans.</li>
<li>Support revenue generating functions by driving full-funnel performance by interfacing the demand generation, sales development and sales teams and providing regular insights and recommendations.</li>
<li>Develop and maintain top-funnel analytics reports and dashboards to provide actionable insights that support data-driven decision-making for the sales and executive leadership teams.</li>
<li>Oversee the development, and ongoing management of Marketo platform to ensure leads flow through to Salesforce correctly</li>
<li>Apply quantitative analysis and data visualization to tell the story behind the numbers.</li>
<li>Analyze sales performance on a daily, monthly and quarterly basis to provide status updates and insights to key leadership.</li>
<li>Assist in understanding process bottlenecks and inconsistencies to implement data enrichment processes to improve sales team's performance.</li>
<li>Apply data cleansing techniques to improve the quality and accuracy of contact and account databases.</li>
<li>Standardize and maintain quality of multi-directional exchange of information across CRM, Marketing Automation, ERP and other applicable systems.</li>
</ul>
Competencies
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Experience with high-volume, direct sales models (SaaS).</li>
<li>Previous experience in Sales Operations and/or Sales Support preferred.</li>
<li>Exhibits strong quantitative analysis, critical thinking skills and presentation skills.</li>
<li>Experience analyzing and interpreting large data sets.</li>
<li>Good understanding of full revenue cycle (quote/bookings to cash).</li>
<li>Familiarity with business analysis and process flow mapping.</li>
<li>Comfortable working as part of a team, consistently a key contributor.</li>
</ul>
