<h2>Accountant</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>Stribling Equipment, LLC and Empire Truck Sales, LLC are two of the most respected dealers in the Southeast. With the quality and reliability of great brands like John Deere with Stribling and Freightliner/Western Star with Empire, we are well positioned for continued growth as we continue to develop our markets. Due to our continued growth, we are looking for an Accountant to join our team in Jackson, Mississippi.
</p>
<p><b>Position Summary:</b></p>
<p>Applies the principles of accounting to record financial transactions, reconcile account balances, and analyze financial transactions. Creates and interprets financial statements and reports. Communicates key operational and financial results.</p>
<p><b>Primary Functions &amp; Responsibilities (including, but not limited to):</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Maintain department financial statements and report on their results</li>
<li>Reconciles account balances monthly and resolve differences</li>
<li>Prepare and monitor debt reports</li>
<li>Maintains fixed assets records and fixed asset depreciation schedules in accordance with generally accepted accounting principals</li>
<li>Makes recommendations to management for problem resolution</li>
<li>Reviews purchases for compliance with sales and use tax requirements</li>
<li>Initiates and processes journal entries into various accounts</li>
<li>Performs additional duties as assigned</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Qualifications:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree from an accredited university in Accounting or closely related equivalent required</li>
<li>CPA preferred but not required</li>
<li>Prior dealership experience preferred but not required</li>
<li>Experience with Excel and other business software applications</li>
<li>Excellent organizational skills and ability to work well with others</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Skills/Abilities:</b></p>
<p>Strong knowledge of computer and software systems (Microsoft Office)</p>
