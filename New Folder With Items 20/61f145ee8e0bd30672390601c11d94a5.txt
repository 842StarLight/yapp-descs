<div>About Us</div>
<div>
Sea-Bird Scientific was formed in 2010 by the union of Sea-Bird Electronics (Bellevue, WA), WET Labs (Philomath, OR) and Satlantic (Halifax, NS) (wholly owned subsidiaries of Danaher Corporation (NYSE: DHR)). Through the combination of the Sea-Bird Electronics, WET Labs and Satlantic product lines, Sea-Bird Scientific is the world’s leading provider of advanced technology for oceanographic research. The Sea-Bird Scientific group of company’s design, manufacture and market oceanographic instrumentation and systems that enable the world’s leading scientists to push the boundaries of ocean knowledge. Sea-Bird Scientific works with a global network of international representatives and partners to serve customers in more than 30 countries around the world. Our dedicated team is comprised of individuals with diverse backgrounds and interests with a common goal of serving the world’s oceanographic community with leading edge technology and solutions. We offer an entrepreneurial environment that is team-centered, customer-driven, quality-focused, and growth-oriented. Working at one of the Sea-Bird Scientific Company’s locations gives you access to a robust career development process and challenging "stretch" opportunities.<div>

Description</div>
<div>
<div><b>
Purpose of Position:</b></div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
As the Ocean Science Director, you make scientists successful. You have a passion for accelerating the understanding of natural waters. As a co-practitioner with the scientific community, and as a leader of a team of scientists, you form a critical bridge. You represent the realization of the leading edge of science and understand the importance of bringing people along in the adventure.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
Sea-Bird Scientific’s strength is the fusion of science and technology. You are an advocate for the advancement of science and work cross functionally to develop an early technology roadmap – balancing what could be done with what should be done in light of strategy and the most valuable, unsolved customer problems.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
This position can be based out of one of the Sea-Bird Scientific sites in Bellevue, WA or Philomath, OR and may have direct reports in both sites.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div><b>
Essential Functions:</b></div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
People &amp; Talent Management</div>
<div></div>
<ul>
<li><div>
Develop and mentor direct reports and team members to reach their next career goals and to increase understanding of customer expectations and operating environments; further strengthening their ability to be co-practitioners to the community we serve</div></li>
<li><div>
Build and develop a highly-engaged, high-performing, growth-oriented organization</div></li>
<li><div>
Ensure the team is doing the rights things (focusing on the critical few) and doing things right (applying applicable processes from our DBS toolkit to reduce friction)</div></li>
<li><div>
Motivate associates to perform at their highest levels</div></li>
<li><div>
Work closely with internal functional teams across a wide breadth of the organization including marketing, sales, service, support, product management, science, technology and operations</div></li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<div>
Co-Practitioner</div>
<div></div>
<ul>
<li><div>
Work directly with our customers to provide data quality that exceeds requirements for understanding climate level change in our oceans; at times this may include traveling to customer sites / ocean going vessels</div></li>
<li><div>
Work with scientific customers to improve their measurements, contributing to their success by diagnosing issues and if something isn’t right, acting on behalf of Sea-Bird to make it so. This may involve prescribing changes in characterization/calibration, measurement methodology, data correction, or as appropriate driving cross-functional teams to undertake changes in instrument design</div></li>
<li><div>
Custodian of maintaining strong cross-functional relationships with key members of the scientific community by helping map customers to appropriate contacts at Sea-Bird and planned collaboration opportunities in partnership with Sales, Product Management and Service</div></li>
<li><div>
Partner with Sales and Product Management to drive scientific partnerships in China</div></li>
<li><div>
Grow the global impact of Sea-Bird Scientific University</div></li>
<li><div>
Act as a liaison, communicating customer needs to appropriate departments</div></li>
<li><div>
Further ocean science and promote understanding of Sea-Bird’s solutions by attending conferences, speaking engagements, writing papers, developing posters, etc.</div></li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<div>
Innovation &amp; Growth</div>
<div></div>
<ul>
<li><div>
Lead our scientists and CTO, providing support, focus and development</div></li>
<li><div>
Own the development of the early technology roadmap; create process muscle and clarity regarding what early technology is being developed and why</div></li>
<li><div>
Lead team of scientists who will participate directly on cross cross-functional product development teams, ensuring impactful contributions</div></li>
<li><div>
Drive improvement for Sea-Bird’s calibration capabilities and other Sea-Bird differentiators</div></li>
<li><div>
Deepen our calibration capabilities, continually improving and applying to new technologies</div></li>
<li><div>
Partner with Engineering, Product Management and Sales, consulting on science and providing VOC as needed</div></li>
</ul>

Other duties as assigned.</div>
