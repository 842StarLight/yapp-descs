<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>About the job</b></h2>
<p>
The Google Cloud Platform team helps customers transform and evolve their business through the use of Google’s global network, web-scale data centers and software infrastructure. As part of an entrepreneurial team in this rapidly growing business, you will help shape the future of businesses of all sizes use technology to connect with customers, employees and partners.</p>
<p>Google Cloud Professional Services exists to bring specialist expertise around Google Cloud products, services, and the supporting practices which make their adoption successful. We engage directly with our flagship customers and key Google partners to deliver, design, build, and advise our customers on the best way to transition to Google Cloud.<br>
<br>
As a Digital Transformation Executive, you will specialize in the program management, governance, and orchestration of large scale cloud transformations. This will involve supporting both our customers and internal teams from pre-sales through to delivery and execution of the joint Google Cloud/Customer transformation program. You will have experience of planning and executing the governance of cloud adoption, IT transformation or cloud migrations. While not expected to be a technical expert, you should possess a good understanding of the fundamentals of both Enterprise IT and cloud technologies.</p>
<p>
Google Cloud helps millions of employees and organizations empower their employees, serve their customers, and build what’s next for their business — all with technology built in the cloud. Our products are engineered for security, reliability and scalability, running the full stack from infrastructure to applications to devices and hardware. And our teams are dedicated to helping our customers and developers see the benefits of our technology come to life.</p>
