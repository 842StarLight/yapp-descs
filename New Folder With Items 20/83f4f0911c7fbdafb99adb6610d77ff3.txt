<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Job Details
</b></h2>
<p>We are looking for a visionary and innovative leader for our Salesforce Industries, Retail &amp; Consumer Goods (RCG) team. The ideal candidate is an industry veteran with deep knowledge and relationships in retail and consumer goods. This executive will lead/drive our enterprise positioning and solution strategy for the Retail &amp; Consumer Goods (RCG) industries. This is a senior leader with a successful track record building, leading, and developing an enterprise software go-to-market strategy for retail and consumer goods companies. The role is a mix of thought leadership, strategy, business development, sales execution, account planning and partner engagement. This executive is a natural evangelist who has proven leadership experience to drive customer engagement; an extensive executive-level network across the retail and consumer goods industries; developed deep partnerships with System Integrators and ISVs, and driven industry thought leadership around digital transformation.
<br>
<br>
Responsibilities
</p>
<ul>
<li>Develop and help execute an RCG product, solution and go-to-market strategy including sales, sales enablement, marketing, alliances, and our internal and external communities.
</li>
<li>Collaborate with product management teams to deliver industry-specific capabilities in future product roadmaps and drive RCG product vision into the market.
</li>
<li>Define and support awareness and pipeline building RCG marketing initiatives including conferences, trade shows, and events.
</li>
<li>Engage with top customers and prospects to provide direct deal support, industry expertise and strategy in key sales opportunities globally.
</li>
<li>Drive blueprints, solution and sales play creation to help educate and enable sales, presales and customer success teams.
</li>
<li>Define and create RCG collateral and thought leadership pieces including white papers, datasheets, and social media posts.
</li>
<li>Act as a company spokesperson &amp; RCG domain expert with analyst firms, industry trade press, trade organizations, industry influencers and deliver high profile presentations to large scale audiences.
</li>
<li>Collaborate with partner teams to identify, recruit and enable industry-specific system integrator and independent solution vendor partnerships including personally cultivating key partner relationships.
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p>Experience/Skills Required:
</p>
<ul>
<li>Minimum 15+ years combined Sales, Leadership, Product Marketing, Sales Enablement, Operations and/or similar functions in Fortune 500 enterprise software vendor, top tier professional services firm, or iconic and respected brand retailer.
</li>
<li>Deep retail and consumer goods industry expertise.
</li>
<li>Experience with deploying or using Salesforce with a passionate vision for how we can change the industry in the Age of the Customer.
</li>
<li>Proven track record of building and managing high performing teams.
</li>
<li>Strong executive presence and an ability to quickly establish credibility with senior leadership.
</li>
<li>Exceptional written, oral communication, and presentation skills.
</li>
<li>Self-starter comfortable working in a start-up paced software company.
</li>
<li>Strong customer orientation and results-driven leader.
</li>
<li>Strong understanding of Salesforce products.
</li>
<li>Exceptional problem solving and analytical skills; demonstrated ability to structure complex problems, develop hypotheses, and present well-supported solutions.
</li>
</ul>
