<h4 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>What You’ll Do</b></h4>
<ul>
<li>Play the Business/System Analyst or senior business analyst role</li>
<li>Work closely with client to ensure requirements are captured, scope is controlled and expectations are met</li>
<li>Work closely with team members to synthesize analysis into key features and develop actionable recommendations</li>
<li>Participate or facilitate workshops, stakeholder interviews, solution review meeting etc</li>
<li>Read and understand project documentation; creates and maintain high-quality business analysis artifacts, including specifications, requirement documents, user story, wireframes etc</li>
<li>Establish and maintain professional and consultative relationships with clients; communicate with business stakeholders throughout project lifecycle</li>
<li>Maintain expertise and knowledge of business domain, technologies and solutions</li>
<li>Participate in and support sales activities</li>
</ul>
<h4 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>What You Have</b></h4>
<ul>
<li>Minimum of 5 year’s business analysis experience in software development or enterprise software products</li>
<li>Knowledge of software development life cycle. Be able to follow a defined software development process</li>
<li>Experience working with Agile and other software methodologies; Strong knowledge of Agile practices</li>
<li>Experience in requirement management, including eliciting, analyzing, validating and formalization/regulation of requirements in written format. Ability to create and maintain specification documentation</li>
<li>Experience in modelling and analysis of business processes (e.g. UML, Modelling and Design skills)</li>
<li>Knowledge of Front Office products and technologies</li>
<li>Proficiency in BA techniques, tools or business areas</li>
<li>Strong analytical, critical thinking and problem-solving skills</li>
<li>Good understanding of one or multiple business domain areas and business processes. Demonstrate ability to learn and systematize business knowledge</li>
<li>Ability to adapt quickly to new processes and standards, with a strong commitment to providing exceptional customer service</li>
<li>Good client management skills; strong ability to interface and build relationships with client stakeholders</li>
<li>Ability to participate in phone conferences; be interviewed by customers; be involved in customer workshops and presentations; visit customers without supervision</li>
<li>Good interpersonal and strong communication skills to effectively communicate with stakeholders and team members</li>
<li>Read and write the Customer's Communication Language and have advanced speaking ability</li>
<li>Ability to work collaboratively in a multi-IT-discipline and multi-cultural environment</li>
<li>Ability to collaborate with distributed teams and client stakeholders</li>
<li>Excellent writing and communication skills in English and Chinese; able to write high-quality unambiguous texts</li>
<li>Basic IT knowledge to understand and communicate with software engineers</li>
<li>Able to self-manage, work without supervision, and check his/her own work thoroughly</li>
<li>Demonstrate a sense of responsibility for the quality of the deliverables created</li>
</ul>
