<h2>Pharmacy Technician/Front Clerk</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>We are an independent retail pharmacy that offers high touch personalized service. We are focused on integrating traditional pharmacy with a holistic approach to health. We offer an array of natural and organic products from reputable brands. Our priority is to provide exceptional customer service.</p>
<p>We are looking for a motivated, reliable Pharmacy Technician and/or Front Clerk to join our team! We are looking for a service oriented person who is a team player and is looking to learn and grow with our business.</p>
<p><b>Pharmacy Technician Responsibilities: </b></p>
<p>Process and fill prescription using computer software as directed by pharmacist.</p>
<p>Pull, package, and label product for patients</p>
<p>Answer and make daily calls to coordinate patient deliveries</p>
<p>Handle calls from patients and doctors</p>
<p>Complete insurance benefit verification, initiate prior authorizations and correct claim discrepancies</p>
<p>Other pharmacy related tasks</p>
<p><b>Pharmacy Front Clerk Responsibilities: </b></p>
<p>Greet and assist patients and customers</p>
<p>Manage inventory, orders and restock the front OTC shelves</p>
<p>Check order that are received and price daily orders</p>
<p>Communicate with doctor’s office staff and clients</p>
<p>Work counter and register; take prescriptions and ring up sales for customers</p>
<p>Assist pharmacist with tasks</p>
<p><b>Qualifications: </b></p>
<p>Strong customer service skills</p>
<p>Excellent written and verbal communication skills</p>
<p>Strong attention to detail</p>
<p>Self-motivated, positive attitude and has an interest in natural products</p>
<p>Team player</p>
<p>Reliable and always on time</p>
<p>Knowledge of Micro Merchant Prime Rx a plus</p>
<p>Bi-Lingual: Spanish or Russian Speaking preferred but not required</p>
<p>Compensation based on skills and experience</p>
