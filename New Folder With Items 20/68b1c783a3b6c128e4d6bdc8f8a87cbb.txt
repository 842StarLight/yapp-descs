With more than $2 billion in revenues, CDK Global is a leading global provider of integrated information technology and digital marketing solutions to the automotive retail and adjacent industries. Focused on enabling end-to-end automotive commerce, CDK provides solutions to dealers in more than 100 countries around the world, serving approximately 28,000 retail locations and most automotive manufacturers. CDK Global solutions automate and integrate critical processes from pre-sale targeted advertising to the sale, financing, insurance, parts supply, repair and maintenance of vehicles, with an increasing focus on utilizing data analytics and predictive intelligence.</p>

<div>
In this role, you will be responsible for managing a team of client-facing email marketing consultants who deliver email marketing to our customers. The team of Specialists is responsible for managing account relationships to include strategic understanding of customer's business needs and how this drives their digital marketing; creating and executing email; analyzing results and conducting ongoing optimization and coordinating across teams to support reporting and drive ongoing performance improvement.<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<div><b>KEY RESPONSIBILITIES:<br>
</b></div>
<div></div>
<ul>
<li>Manage a team to deliver on defined performance targets. Continue to refine these targets as product offerings evolve.
</li>
<li>Manage day-to-day activities of the team including scheduling, communicating process, policies, performance, quality expectations, technical and product updates, as well as department and company information.</li>
<li>
Provide management and professional guidance to team members through regular one-on-one meetings, regular team meetings, monthly quality reviews, quarterly and annual performance goals and reviews, documenting and delivering timely performance and behavior feedback while taking appropriate disciplinary actions as required.</li>
<li>
Responsible for determining staffing needs including hiring, disciplinary actions and terminations.
</li>
<li>Assist with email marketing performance reviews, Adhoc reports, projects and ongoing optimization around operations and marketing delivery.</li>
<li>
Responsible for participating in launch projects and being the representative and spokesperson for the email Marketing Services functions.</li>
<li>
Ensure cross-teaming within the Digital Marketing Services group, all of Services, Sales, and the Advertising COE to support high-level delivery to our customers.</li>
<li>
Train and guide employees on all aspects of email marketing execution, best practices, optimization and troubleshooting.</li>
<li>
Demonstrate Leadership traits.</li>
<li>
Attend and represent team in customer meetings and marketing events as needed.</li>
<li>
Conduct, review, and respond to customer feedback and prepare action plans to address identified issues. Improve customer relations, which are reflected in customer service index (CSI) and net promoter score (NPS).</li>
</ul>
