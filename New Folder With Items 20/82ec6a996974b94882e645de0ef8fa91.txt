<h2>Business Development Representative</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>The BDR is responsible for proactively responding to marketing generated leads and lead/contact management. This individual will have a direct impact on OnShift’s success by providing qualified leads into the sales pipeline. Working with Marketing, this position will participate in strategic and industry relevant calling campaigns based on a geographic territory. The main goal of the BDR is to respond quickly to newly created leads through industry trade shows, white papers, case studies and other marketing initiatives and cultivate opportunities.</p>
<p><b>
WHO WE ARE</b></p>
<p>
Software is one of the most interesting industries to work in today and healthcare is the place to be in Cleveland in 2019 - combining those, as we do, is what makes our business so compelling. We’re growing fast, we love what we do and we do it with passion.<br>
<br>
OnShift is a B2B software company headquartered in the theater district of Cleveland, Ohio. We provide cloud-based human capital management software and proactive services to solve everyday workforce challenges in healthcare around hiring, scheduling and employee engagement. OnShift has secured a range of awards highlighting our technology innovation and business growth. Notable awards include: OHTec Tech Company of the Year (2014, 2019), OHTec Best Healthcare Tech Product (2017), Deloitte Technology Fast 500 winner (2015-2018) and Weatherhead 100 winner (2015-2018).</p>
<p>
As an organization, we value people who are dedicated and innovative and we reward them with challenging work, competitive pay, solid benefits, equity participation, career growth and personal development.</p>
<p><b>
A DAY IN THE LIFE</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Ability to develop strong rapport over the phone, exhibits excellent interpersonal skills and service abilities</li>
<li>Has a strong telephone manner for successful cold calling</li>
<li>Interest in making a large volume of outbound calls per day</li>
<li>Use of strong prospecting, selling, and influencing skills to identify qualified/quantified opportunities.</li>
<li>Collaborates closely with the Marketing Team to follow up active opportunities</li>
<li>Maintain complete, accurate, and up-to-date account, lead/contact and opportunity data in SalesForce database</li>
<li>Works effectively in a fast-paced and rapidly evolving corporate sales environment.</li>
<li>Stay abreast of market trends and relevant industry news in the Long Term Care industry.</li>
<li>Learn established sales methodologies (we use CCS - Customer Centric Selling)</li>
</ul>
