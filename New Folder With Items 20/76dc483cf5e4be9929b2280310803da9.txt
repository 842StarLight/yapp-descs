<h2>I & C Engineer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>About NECI:</b></p>
<p>NECI, an Emerson Impact Partner, is the leading Digital Automation solutions provider transforming manufacturing, lab operations, process development and process control within a range of industries in New England. NECI relentlessly seeks to drive outcomes that change the game for our clients and is seeking team members to join in our mission.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Position Summary:</b>
</p>
<p>I&amp;C Engineers are responsible for working closely with NECI customers to support a wide range of work scopes focused on instrumentation &amp; electrical design across various DCS &amp; PLC platforms. Some working knowledge of the design and specification of Emerson products and computer networking equipment preferred.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Principal Duties and Responsibilities:</b>
</p>
<ul>
<li>Technical I&amp;C site lead with ownership of customer site relationships (satisfaction, on time delivery, service and quality).
</li>
<li>Support specification of instrumentation, control system equipment and misc. materials based on defined customer needs for assigned<br>
projects and proposals.
</li>
<li>Manage and provide direction to site vendors to maintain project schedule.
</li>
<li>Create and review OEM/vendor submittals for adherence to customer standards and defined controls guidelines.
</li>
<li>Execute pre-FAT/FAT on OEM or customer supplied equipment.
</li>
<li>Execute site startup activities (loop checks/commissioning).
</li>
<li>Generate and manage loop check/commissioning documentation.
</li>
<li>Generate and manage project punchlists to completion.
</li>
<li>Capable of troubleshooting traditional analog and discrete signals as well as other communication types (HART, Fieldbus, Profibus, DeviceNet, AS-I,<br>
Ethernet I/P, Modbus).
</li>
<li>Capable of utilizing various handheld testing devices to support control loop testing and/or troubleshooting of wiring and connectivity issues.
</li>
<li>Utilize DCS/PLC platform software to support troubleshooting (e.g. DeltaV Diagnostics, AMS Device Manager)
</li>
<li>Support I&amp;C engineering (FEED) studies based on customer requests.
</li>
<li>Work with sales team to lead proposal efforts by assisting with I&amp;C estimates, site walkdowns, scope write-ups and preliminary BOM<br>
generation.
</li>
<li>Provide feedback to the organization of site opportunities.
</li>
<li>Responsible for providing uncompromising quality to all work processes in designated area of responsibility.
</li>
<li>Has the authority to stop those work processes at any time it is believed that quality or safety is being compromised.
</li>
<li>Performs other duties as assigned; responsibilities and activities may change or be assigned at any time with or without notice.
</li>
<li>Physical demands: Lifting up to 50 lbs.
</li>
<li>Travel: 90% travel within the New Hampshire, Vermont and Maine areas.
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Minimum qualifications:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s Degree in related engineering field or equivalent OTJ experience.
</li>
<li>Minimum of 3 years’ experience in engineering or related field.
</li>
<li>Ability to perform as part of a team.
</li>
<li>Appropriate and professional demeanor and appearance.
</li>
<li>Ability to manage multiple tasks while maintaining project commitments.
</li>
<li>Solid written, organization and communication skills.
</li>
<li>Ability to work in a fast paced environment, with multiple and changing priorities while maintaining strong attention to detail and a high level of accuracy.
</li>
<li>Knowledge of the basic concepts of process control theory.
</li>
<li>Ability to interpret P&amp;ID’s, electrical schematics, loop drawings and general equipment arrangement drawings.
</li>
<li>Understanding of DCS/PLC physical layer and control hardware (DeltaV specific preferred)
</li>
<li>Knowledge of Microsoft Products. (Excel, Word, Access, Project)
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Preferred qualifications:</b>
</p>
<ul>
<li>DCS/PLC programming preferred
</li>
<li>Some understanding of DeltaV software physical layer configuration
</li>
<li>Experience working with cGMP or in a validated environment
</li>
<li>Understanding of hazardous area and intrinsic safety design
</li>
<li>Working knowledge of AutoCAD software
</li>
</ul>
