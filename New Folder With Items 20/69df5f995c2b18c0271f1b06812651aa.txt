<h2>Solutions Architect</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<div><b>Solutions Architect</b></div>
<div></div>
<div>DornerWorks, a leader in embedded engineering design, is seeking an experienced <b>Solutions Architect</b> to join our talented team. The Solutions Architect will work closely with our Business Development Team to provide innovation and excellence to our customers in a way that results in consistent and growing work with that customer.</div>
<div>
<br>
This individual will have a system and architectural understanding that will enable them to meet with customer managers and engineers to explore direction, recommend solutions, and consider DornerWorks fit for leading project solutions. This individual will have the business savvy to balance meeting customer’s needs, helping the customer see the breadth and depth of capability that DornerWorks can offer, and ensuring profitability for DornerWorks.</div>
<div>
<br>
If you are someone who has experience in systems, software and hardware engineering, along with strong customer skills, this is the opportunity you have been seeking.</div>
<div></div>
<div><b>What you would be doing:</b></div>
<div></div>
<div>Business Development</div>
<ul>
<li>Support our Business Development Team with impromptu and planned customer sales efforts.</li>
<li>Identify new business opportunities.</li>
</ul>
<div>Systems Architecture/Design</div>
<ul>
<li>Identify/Design approach for new customer initiatives.</li>
<li>Research and exploration to entice customer collaboration.</li>
</ul>
<div>Project Execution</div>
<ul>
<li>Assist engineering teams by ensuring technical and tools ramp-up of the projects associated with the customer(s) you are aligned with.</li>
<li>Direct technical oversight/tasking of the team in conjunction with a DornerWorks project manager.</li>
</ul>
<div>Technical Expertise</div>
<ul>
<li>Serve as a resource to niche groups on architect projects.</li>
</ul>
<div><b>What we need from you:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>BS in Electrical Engineer (BSEE), Computer Engineering (BSCE), or Computer Science (CS) from an ABET accredited institution.</li>
<li>8 plus years’ experience performing engineering with increasing level of technical expertise; significant experience at the architect level.</li>
<li>Prior experience leading or mentoring project teams.</li>
<li>Demonstrated direct customer experience in creating electronic solutions.</li>
<li>Experience with product development cycle, including project deliverables timing, workflow, and gate reviews (from requirements development through test).</li>
<li>Knowledge in developing embedded electronics and software and the architecture needed to meet the customer needs.</li>
<li>Ability to develop and maintain effective external relationships with customers.</li>
<li>Experience with financial concepts, budget, profit margins, and bill rates.</li>
<li>Ability to undertake overnight travel 25 to 50% of the time.</li>
<li>Unrestricted right to work in the US without requiring sponsorship.</li>
</ul>
<div>
<br>
<b>What we offer you:</b>
</div>
<ul>
<li>Competitive compensation and benefits.</li>
<li>A healthy work/life balance that is not just a catchy phrase; it is one of our core values.</li>
<li>Compressed work week, with the opportunity to take every other Friday off and flexible hours.</li>
<li>A collaborative team where you can work with other outstanding technical minds.</li>
</ul>
