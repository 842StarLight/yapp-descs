<b>Duties and Responsibilities:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>
<b> </b><b>Develop new school business via telephone and mass communication such as email and social media to introduce Rebel Athletic and identify appropriate buyers.</b>
</li>
<li>
<b> </b><b>Follow up on leads and conduct research to identify potential prospects.</b>
</li>
<li>
<b> </b><b>Identify key buying influencers within these prospects to determine budget and timeline.</b>
</li>
<li>
<b> </b><b>Build and cultivate prospect relationships by initiating communications and conducting follow-up communications in order to move opportunities through the sales funnel.</b>
</li>
