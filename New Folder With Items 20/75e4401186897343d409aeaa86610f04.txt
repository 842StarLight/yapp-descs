<h2>Product Manager, Automation Solutions</h2>

<div><b>
Position Summary:</b></div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
The Professional Solutions division is looking for an experienced and innovative Product Manager to perform the definition, development, and lifecycle management of our suite of control and automation products. This includes both monetized hardware and software based products, as well as non-monetized partner program product resources. This position involves working closely with the Product Management Director to develop portfolio strategies in line with the long-term strategic vision for this segment. As a product manager for HARMAN Professional Solutions, you will drive cross-functional teams to bring new products and new features to market that will be used every day by AV professionals all over the world. You will work closely with our research and development teams to deconstruct problems into actionable items, assist in creating an execution plan, and then will work to keep teams on track.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
<b>
Job Responsibilities:</b><b><br>
</b>
</div>
<div></div>
<div>Essential responsibilities include:</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<ul>
<li>
Determines customers' needs and desires through a combination of direct customer interaction and by specifying the research needed to obtain market information.</li>
<li>
Recommends the nature and scope of present and future product lines by reviewing product specifications and requirements; appraising new product ideas and/or product or packaging changes.</li>
<li>
Assesses market competition by comparing the company's product to competitors' products.</li>
<li>
Define detailed user stories, acceptance criteria, product specifications, and feature requirements</li>
<li>
Lead development of wireframes and designs that articulate the product vision</li>
<li>
Brings new products to market by analyzing proposed product requirements and product development programs; preparing return-on-investment analyses; establishing time schedules with engineering and manufacturing.</li>
<li>
Determines product pricing by utilizing market research data; reviewing production and sales costs, and anticipating volume by consolidating input from regional sales organizations</li>
<li>
Obtains product market share by working with regional sales directors to develop product sales strategies.</li>
<li>
Supports regional sales directors in the product messaging and launch of new products including train-the-trainer activities.</li>
<li>
Maintains professional and technical knowledge by attending educational workshops; reviewing professional publications; establishing personal networks; participating in professional societies.</li>
<li>
Contributes to team effort by accomplishing related results as needed</li>
</ul>
