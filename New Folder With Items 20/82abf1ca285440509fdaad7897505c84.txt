<h2>Human Resources/Office Assistant (Entry-Level)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>We are looking for an entry-level <b>Human Resources/Office Assistant</b>. This Human Resources Assistant will have access to outstanding mentors and opportunities for growth. Recent college graduates are encouraged to apply.</p>
<p><b>Responsibilities of a Human Resources/Office Assistant: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Process, verify, and maintain personnel related documentation, including staffing, recruitment, training, grievances, performance evaluations, classifications, and employee leaves of absence</li>
<li>Request information from law enforcement officials, previous employers, and other references to determine applicants' employment acceptability</li>
<li>Process and review employment applications to evaluate qualifications or eligibility of applicants</li>
<li>Arrange for advertising or posting of job vacancies, and notify eligible workers of position availability</li>
<li>Explain company personnel policies, benefits, and procedures to employees or job applicants</li>
<li>Record data for each employee, including such information as addresses, weekly earnings, absences, amount of sales or production, supervisory reports on performance, and dates of and reasons for terminations</li>
<li>Provide assistance in administering employee benefit programs and worker's compensation plans</li>
<li>Interview job applicants to obtain and verify information used to screen and evaluate them</li>
<li>Inform job applicants of their acceptance or rejection of employment</li>
<li>Perform day-to-day administrative tasks such as maintaining information files and processing paperwork</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Transferable Skills Needed for a Human Resources/Office Assistant: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>
<b>Social perceptiveness -</b>be aware of other people's reactions and understand why they react the way they do. Maintain a positive, friendly composure even in difficult situations</li>
<li>
<b>Processing Information -</b>Compile, code, categorize, calculate, or verify information to ensure that all data is correctly filed and processed</li>
<li>
<b>Integrity -</b>adhere to a strong moral and ethical code in compliance with organizational principles</li>
<li>
<b>Independence -</b>develop one's own way of doing things, guiding oneself with little or no supervision, and depend on oneself to get things done</li>
<li>
<b>Personnel and human resources expertise</b>- knowledge of principles and procedures for personnel recruitment, selection, training, compensation and benefits, labor relations and negotiation, and personnel information systems</li>
</ul>
