<h2>Senior Writer, Demand Generation</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>Cvent is an exciting, fast-growing tech company that provides industry-leading software to meeting and event professionals around the world. We are looking for a Content Marketing Lead to join our Hospitality Cloud marketing team!</p>
<p>
We are looking for a talented content creator to strategize, write, edit and produce content for distribution across demand generation channels. This is an exciting opportunity for the right candidate to help expand Cvent’s brand awareness and drive quality leads for our sales team.</p>
<p>
This position reports to the Senior Manager, Content Marketing on the Hospitality Cloud, and aligns closely with the Marketing Manager, Demand Generation. The role requires a high level of creativity, analytical thinking, attention to detail, and project management skills. The ideal candidate will have a good understanding of multi-channel content marketing and a track record of delivering high-quality content as part of demand generation marketing campaigns for the Hospitality Cloud. B2B marketing experience is required, ideally in the hospitality, events, enterprise software or marketing technology sectors.</p>
<p>
<b>What You’ll Be Doing:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Create and optimize content on company websites to drive and grow traffic, build brand awareness, solicit prospect interest and engagement, and generate demand for Cvent hospitality solutions</li>
<li>Assist in developing a content strategy to support marketing campaigns with the intention of driving highly qualified lead generation for the hospitality cloud.</li>
<li>Be a creative thinker and a relentless marketer who understands the B2B technology space and can act quickly to take advantage of marketing opportunities – but also plan thoroughly for what’s next.</li>
<li>Communicate with internal stakeholders to ensure the needs and goals of lead generation marketing projects are met and build meaningful relationships within the organization.</li>
<li>Apply your storytelling, writing, and visual skills to create marketing that matters. Ensure content is appealing, informative and satisfying to the visitor/reader.</li>
<li>Create eBooks, infographics, promotional copy for emails, webinars and such, and additional forms of content designed to create engagement across the demand gen funnel.</li>
<li>Measure, track, and report on content metrics to evaluate campaign results and effectiveness across lead gen channels.</li>
<li>Help to maintain content inventories and calendars (eBooks, etc.); assess existing content and identify new content types; analyze existing content to ensure it conveys a consistent tone and credible message to our primary personas and is on-brand and consistent in terms of style, quality and tone.</li>
<li>Develop briefs and reports analyzing industry research conducted or sponsored by Cvent that is of interest to Cvent customers and prospects.</li>
<li>Be a creative thinker and a relentless marketer who understands the B2B technology space and can act quickly to take advantage of marketing opportunities – but also plan thoroughly for what’s next.</li>
<li>Communicate with internal stakeholders to ensure the needs and goals of marketing projects are met and build meaningful relationships within the organization.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>What You Need For This Position:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>3-5 years B2B marketing experience, preferably in technology or software industry, ideally in marketing tech, hospitality or events sectors. Prior experience in lead generation focused initiatives preferred.</li>
<li>Creative thinker, team player, detail-oriented, process-driven, self-motivated and effective communicator</li>
<li>Experience of working in a multi-national, matrixed organization to develop marketing campaigns in partnership with multiple product, sales &amp; marketing departments</li>
<li>Strong knowledge of online &amp; digital marketing techniques for inbound marketing, and of developing outbound e-marketing nurture campaigns</li>
<li>Education: BA/BS degree in Marketing or related field</li>
</ul>
