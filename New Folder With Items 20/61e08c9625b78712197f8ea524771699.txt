<h2>Sales Development Representative</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Interested in helping to create a new-SaaS market within the Creative Advertising Tech space? Interested in broadening your SaaS sales experience at the leading Creative Mgmt. Platform? If so, read on. Celtra is hiring a Sales Development Representative to continue building awareness, educating the market, and generating leads across Fortune 1000 Brands and global Media Partners.</p>
<p>
The Sales Development Representative will partner with our global sales and marketing teams and be expected to deliver qualified leads to senior sales team members. A smart, creative, problem-solving candidate will thrive in this role as they deliver an aligned message that generates leads as a means to acquire new logos into our customer base.</p>
<p>
Responsibilities:</p>
<ul>
<li>Prospect, educate, qualify, and develop target accounts and inbound leads to create sales-ready leads and opportunities</li>
<li>Research accounts, identify key players, generate interest and develop messaging that will garner interest</li>
<li>Engage with prospective customers to develop new business relationships, understand the digital advertising challenges they are trying to address and determine the solution(s) that will be of value</li>
<li>Handoff opportunities to sr. sales team members, educating as necessary about opportunities</li>
<li>Successfully handle and overcome prospect objections</li>
<li>Be highly coachable and committed to building an understanding of the market, developing your sales skills, and learning our products and process</li>
<li>Ensure efficient lead management by updating lead scoring and prospect interaction in SFDC</li>
<li>Partner with sales and marketing team to generate a robust pipeline of qualified sales opportunities</li>
</ul>
<p>Qualifications:</p>
<ul>
<li>1-3 years within a Sales/Business Development Representative role (MarTech and/or AdTech company preferred)</li>
<li>Self-starter with a track record of successful, measurable lead follow-up and sales development at multiple executive levels within an organization</li>
<li>Strong opportunity qualification, objection handling, and time management ability</li>
<li>Trained on call structuring, conversation control, and pitching</li>
<li>Ability to work in a high-energy sales team environment</li>
<li>Positive and energetic phone skills, excellent listening skills, strong writing skills</li>
<li>Proficient with standard corporate tools (email, voicemail, MS Office)</li>
<li>Sales training and salesforce.com experience a plus</li>
<li>4-year college degree or equivalent experience required</li>
</ul>
