<h2>Bookkeeper</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>The Part-Time Bookkeeper will assist in preparation for Board meetings, and plan and prepare for annual audits. Some administrative tasks, such as filing of financial documents, are associated. The successful candidate will be a self-starter who has the ability to work independently, as well as part of a team, and is driven to succeed.</p>
<p>
Key Duties and Responsibilities:</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>
<p>Bookkeeping Duties include:</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Weekly Expense Coding/Categorization of all accounts in Quickbooks Online</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Ensure a class is assigned to every expense</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Entering in checks/cash as received</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Depositing all checks within 72 hours of receipt</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Ensure every expense has a receipt attached to it</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Manage PO’s from employees</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Manage Accounts Receivable and Accounts Payable</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Prepare monthly budget reports</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Approve payroll hours biweekly</p>
<br>
</li>
</ul>
<p>
</p>
<ul><li>
<p>Deliver weekly financial statements (Profit and Loss, Balance Sheet &amp; others as requested)</p>
<br>
</li></ul>
<p>
</p>
<ul><li>
<p>Manage Company Credit Card System “Divvy”</p>
<br>
</li></ul>
<p>
</p>
<ul><li>
<p>Manage Expensify</p>
<br>
</li></ul>
<p>
</p>
<ul><li>
<p>Assist in the entering of budgets into Quickbooks Online</p>
<br>
</li></ul>
<p>
</p>
<ul><li>
<p>Batch log check/cash deposits into Kindful CRM software</p>
<br>
</li></ul>
<p>
</p>
<ul><li>
<p>Match all QBO deposits to the Sales Receipts</p>
<br>
</li></ul>
<p>
</p>
<ul><li>
<p>Assist with the company’s HR system; BambooHR</p>
<br>
</li></ul>
<p>
</p>
<ul><li>
<p>Prepare reimbursements weekly</p>
<br>
</li></ul>
<p>
</p>
<ul><li>
<p>Performs clerical and other related duties as required</p>
<br>
</li></ul>
<p>
</p>
<ul><li>
<p>Assists with annual financial audit<br>
