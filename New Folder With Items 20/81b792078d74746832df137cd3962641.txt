<h2>Seasonal Visitor Center Staff</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>At Hilmar Cheese Company we improve lives around the world. Committed to innovation and sustainability, Hilmar Cheese Company, and our division, Hilmar Ingredients, serves customers in more than 50 countries. State-of-the art production facilities in California and Texas convert an abundance of high-quality milk into a variety of nutritious cheeses, whey protein and lactose.</p>
<div>
<div>
<div>
<b>
A Typical Day</b>
</div>
<div>
<p>The Visitor Center Staff (Seasonal) is responsible for greeting customers and provide excellent service. Conduct the hands-on daily visitor center retail, café and catering operations. Work as a team to keep the visitor center and all work areas clean and presentable.</p>
</div>
</div>
<div>
<div>
<b>
Job Responsibilities</b>
</div>
<div><ul>
<li>Provide excellent and friendly customer service</li>
<li>Ability to handle customer questions and concerns or properly direct the question</li>
<li>Provide proper serving etiquette during and event</li>
<li>Demonstrate basic numeric understanding to open and close cash register, accurately count money, process a sale, collect money and provide correct change</li>
<li>Maintain a clean, well-organized and safe work environment</li>
<li>Understand first in, first out rotation to ensure freshness of products</li>
<li>Proper phone etiquette</li>
<li>Excellent time management skills</li>
<li>Handle multiple tasks</li>
<li>Exemplify good attitude</li>
<li>Ability to perform operations in a consistent repeatable fashion</li>
<li>Must be able to follow written and verbal direction</li>
<li>Must be able to be an effective part of a team</li>
<li>Responsible for constant improvement of customer service</li>
<li>Have availability of 20 hours per week</li>
<li>Annual completion of OSHA required safety training.</li>
<li>Safe and proper use of the equipment</li>
<li>Knowledgeable of menu items and proper register buttons</li>
<li>Provide guest direction for self-guided tours</li>
<li>Keeps current about Hilmar Cheese Company, Hilmar Ingredients and visitor center catering, tours, cafe and retail information in order to answer customer questions</li>
<li>Active participation and ownership in your training and development program and associated activities, classes and events, which will be held before, during, after shifts and during scheduled days off.</li>
<li>Help coach and train teammates and participate in on the job, buddy training.</li>
<li>Place high priority on food safety and sanitation by understanding and executing HCC’s food safety and sanitation policies and procedures.</li>
<li>Hold self and others accountable to HCC’s food safety &amp; sanitation guidelines and policies by communicating any food safety incident, observation, or opportunity to your leadership team or teammates.</li>
<li>Participate in food safety and sanitation root cause analysis and improvement activities when required.</li>
<li>Prioritize safety above all other job duties by having a safety first approach at all times.</li>
<li>Report any safety issues, observations, opportunities to your teams and follow up.</li>
<li>Participate in departmental safety teams.</li>
<li>Promote Company Culture</li>
<li>Achieve common business goals through cooperative alignment with all departments.</li>
</ul></div>
</div>

<b>What You Will Need</b>
</div>
<div><ul>
