<h2>Product Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Come join a multi-dimension team at LISNR as an Associate Product Manager. We are revolutionizing user experience for all proximity use cases. Like how Tesla unlocks your car when you approach it, or when your school marks your attendance in class when you walk in ? Then you will love working for LISNR.</p>
<p>
You will work alongside experienced leaders from PayPal, Sony, P&amp;G and researchers at the cutting-edge of sound. You will drive innovations to make user experiences in retail, payments and mobility so delightful that end users can't imagine what they would do without it. APMs have a seat at the table from a product's conception to its launch. They're encouraged to push the boundaries of traditional markets in order to bring alive magical experiences in never-been-done-before ways.</p>
<p>
We are seeking contributors with a demonstrated history of defining, leading, influencing and delivering in technical capacities at school, universities, open-source or any technical platform like GitHub or App Stores. If you are an engineer and have always wanted to switch over to defining products, then this is your dream job.</p>
<p><b>
Responsibilities:</b></p>
<p></p>
<br>
<ul>
<li>
End-to-end ownership of the LISNR SDK, service, infrastructure and user experiences globally, from setting the strategy, delivering the product, and bringing it to market.</li>
<li>
Develop and evangelize a strong point of view on proximity retail and payment best practices for delivering core product functionality.</li>
<li>
Passionate to create the best in class platform services.</li>
<li>
Work with partners who leverage your products (SDK/API and ) to build amazing proximity experiences.</li>
<li>
Work cross-functionally (with engineering, sales, marketing, and other teams / leaders) and develop strong relationships that will allow you to work more efficiently and build trust with key partner teams.</li>
<li>
Run lean experiments to learn to develop &amp; evangelize a strong point of view on how to improve LISNR Level 2 KPIs.</li>
<li>
Lead a cross-functional team (including data, engineering, design, marketing, etc.) and influence key partners across teams to develop strong relationships that will allow you to work more efficiently and build trust.</li>
<li>
Use your keen business acumen to set a strategy, influence to gain wide acceptance of that strategy, and then ensure that priorities and tests ladder to business targets.</li>
<li>
Drive data architecture and instrumentation strategy to enable partners with key proximity insights.</li>
<li>
Partner across the org to deliver business results in a product ecosystem environment while communicating and influencing effectively.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Success in this role requires the following:</b></p>
<p></p>
<br>
<ul>
<li>
Passion for building amazing software SDK/APIs.</li>
<li>
Strong technical knowledge.</li>
<li>
Identifying new opportunities based on research, industry trends and best practices, driving innovation.</li>
<li>
Quantifying the opportunities by continually assessing the needs of the customer, market trends, competitive opportunities and threats.</li>
<li>
Creating and managing worldwide product roadmaps, prioritization and constraints to ensure consistent execution of the product lifecycle process from concept to launch.</li>
<li>
Defining the product and customer experience with high quality business requirements and user stories and manage scope of each product launch to deliver on time and on budget.</li>
<li>
Readiness for leading and developing a team of Product Managers, setting a consolidated vision, and collaboration with excellence across all functions.</li>
<li>
Helps teams move forward swiftly through rigor, speed and transparency in decision-making and removing roadblocks.</li>
<li>
Ability to identify opportunities in the organization around you (process, people, etc) and drive change to improve.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Qualifications:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
5+ years of Product Management experience in a top tech or payments company.</li>
<li>
Proven experience working in an Agile environment in a Product Owner role.</li>
<li>
You have a great eye for product and you refuse to deliver anything less than world-class.</li>
<li>
Demonstrated success in creating innovative and user-friendly consumer or SMB products.</li>
<li>
A passion to seek a deep understanding of customer needs and turn that insight into a compelling vision and drivers to create solutions that solve important jobs for Large and Small businesses.</li>
<li>
Strong background working on solutions for small businesses.</li>
<li>
Strong execution and ability to drive work to completion.</li>
<li>
CS degree or strong engineering/programming background.</li>
<li>
Previous experience working on a developer-focused product (nice to have)</li>
<li>
Past experience in Fintech, Digital Signal Processing, Consumer Products or developer platforms is a strong bonus</li>
</ul>
