<h2>Common Grounds & Hospitality Coordinator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<b>SUMMARY:</b><br>
Prepares and service food items, specialty coffee and other beverages with exceptional customer service to the residents, staff, family members and guests at St. John’s United. Supervises, trains, schedules and directs volunteer baristas and provides greeter functions for organizations at coffee entrances.
<br><br>
<b>ESSENTIAL JOB FUNCTION: Related Responsibilities:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Greets guests and customers, take orders, mixes and serves hot and cold beverages such as coffee, espresso drinks, blended coffees or teas</li>
<li>Works to maintain exceptional customer relations and timely delivery of all orders</li>
<li>Stock and maintain inventories of products or organizational resources</li>
<li>Tracks and records inventory, consumer and retail sales</li>
<li>Resolve customer complaints or problems</li>
<li>Manage and process customer payments with accuracy</li>
<li>Ensure schedule coverage</li>
<li>Ensure that sanitation levels are at an acceptable level and all food is being handled and prepared safely according to HACCP guidelines.</li>
<li>Review financial statements, sales and activity reports, or other performance data to measure productivity or goal achievement and to identify areas needing cost reduction.</li>
<li>Direct and coordinate activities concerned with the production, pricing, sales, or distribution of products.</li>
<li>Monitor vendors to ensure that they efficiently and effectively provide needed goods or services within budgetary limits.</li>
<li>Manage profit and loss within a budget to create sustainability.</li>
<li>May perform other duties and responsibilities as assigned</li>
</ul>
<b>ESSENTIAL JOB FUNCTION: Greeter Responsibilities:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Greets all guests and residents</li>
<li>Assists guests with way finding and basic organization information.</li>
<li>Creates a welcoming atmosphere by socializing with residents and</li>
</ul>
<b>ESSENTIAL JOB FUNCTION: Volunteer Responsibilities:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Assists with the recruiting, engaging, interviewing, screening, and placing of volunteers.</li>
<li>Supervises day-to-day work activities of volunteers by delegating authority, assigning and prioritizing activities and monitoring operating standards.</li>
<li>Trains new volunteers and identifies and trains volunteer shift leaders to assist in the training and managing of volunteers.</li>
<li>Manages by providing positive and constructive feedback to volunteers in order to reward, coach, correct and motivate.</li>
<li>Generates and maintains volunteer schedule.</li>
<li>Utilizes volunteer software to schedule, track, and communicate with volunteers.</li>
<li>Participates in annual volunteer recognition events and provides ongoing recognition to volunteers.</li>
</ul>
<b>ESSENTIAL JOB FUNCTION: Kitchen sanitation and safety:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Maintains clean work areas, utensils, and equipment</li>
<li>Practices appropriate sanitation and safety techniques in accordance with Serv Safe</li>
<li>Obtains Serv Safe Certification</li>
<li>Practices appropriate knife and equipment safety at all times</li>
<li>Performs daily and weekly cleaning duties as assigned</li>
<li>Labels all foods appropriately</li>
<li>Protects equipment, utensils, food, and other items of inventory from contamination, spoilage, and pilferage</li>
<li>Reports accidents and injuries in a timely manner</li>
</ul>
<b>ESSENTIAL JOB FUNCTION: Safety:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Obeys safety rules of St. John’s United/Mission Ridge/Vista</li>
<li>Identifies, reports, and/or corrects obvious safety hazards</li>
<li>Attends mandatory safety in-services</li>
</ul>
<b>ESSENTIAL JOB FUNCTION: Mission/Customer Service:</b><br>
Supports the growth and actively engages in the Eden Alternative philosophy, including a work environment with plants and animals.
<br><br>
Follows the St. John’s Guidelines for Customer Service
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Let the Mission be your guide</li>
<li>Make eye contact and smile</li>
<li>Greet and welcome each and every person</li>
<li>Seek out personal contact</li>
<li>Provide immediate service-listen and act</li>
<li>Use appropriate body language at all times</li>
<li>Be gracious to each and every person</li>
</ul>
<b>ESSENTIAL JOB FUNCTION: Employee Responsibilities:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Follows policy and procedures on annual TB testing.</li>
<li>Follows policy and procedures on annual influenza vaccinations.</li>
<li>Attends all mandatory in-services</li>
<li>Works under the general supervision of the Social Service Department</li>
<li>Has daily contact with the potential residents and their families</li>
<li>Has daily contact with nursing home direct care staff to communicate pertinent resident admission information</li>
</ul>
<b>REQUIREMENTS:</b><br>
<b>Minimum Requirements:</b> High School Diploma or GED required. Two years’ experience in managing a coffee shop or equivalent food and beverage establishment.
<br><br>
<b>Knowledge, skills, and abilities required:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Knowledge of food and beverage preparation, sanitation</li>
<li>Knowledge of day to day operations of a coffee and barista establishment</li>
<li>Proven face to face customer service experience</li>
<li>Retail supervision and management experience</li>
<li>Ability to understand written and verbal communication Ability to communicate and interact effectively in a professional manner</li>
<li>Knowledge of scheduling staff</li>
<li>Skill in organization, coordinating, and time management</li>
<li>Ability to understand and follow safety policies and procedures</li>
<li>Ability to respond to management and staff level volunteer needs in a timely and professional manner.</li>
<li>Ability to analyze data and financial records to inform operational decisions and improve efficiency.</li>
<li>Must have an entrepreneurial spirit to fulfill the vision of successful operation.</li>
<li>Ability to communicate and articulate the Mission of St. John’s.</li>
<li>Interacts effectively and in a professional manner</li>
<li>Performs job tasks following established procedures and reports problems</li>
</ul>
<b>PHYSICAL DEMANDS:</b><br>
Requires moderate physical work more than 70% of the time, including lifting up to 25 pounds regularly and up to 50 pounds occasionally, standing, carrying, lifting, walking, and bending for prolonged periods of time.
<br><br>
<b>WORKING CONDITIONS:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Work involves some exposure to extreme temperatures, dirt, fumes, smoke, unpleasant odors, and/or loud noises.</li>
<li>Work involves minimal exposure to hazards, bloodborne pathogens, or physical risks, which require following basic safety precautions.</li>
<li>Normal health care and food service environment.</li>
<li>Work involves exposure to plants and animals within the environment.</li>
</ul>
