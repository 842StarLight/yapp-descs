<p><b>About AccuLynx</b></p>
<p>
AccuLynx is a fast-growing SaaS provider of CRM/project management software for roofing contractors. With more than 10 years in the business and impressive year-over-year revenue growth, we have quickly established ourselves as the leading software product in this multi-billion dollar industry.</p>
<p><b>
Position Overview</b></p>
<p>
We are looking to add a <b>Content Marketing Specialist </b>to our growing marketing team. This position will be responsible for producing high-quality marketing assets used to drive lead generation and advance our position in the roofing software marketplace.</p>
<p>
 The Content Marketing Specialist will act as our lead copywriter, crafting engaging content for both online and offline channels, ranging from promotional to educational to highly technical. This role requires a proven track record of producing high performing marketing content in a B2B SaaS environment.</p>
<p>
 The role is based in our downtown Chicago office and will report to the Director of Marketing.</p>
