<p><b>Job Description:
</b></p>Responsible for providing assistance to Manager, Contracts section, in the advertisement, preparation, submission, and maintenance of contractual agreements and related processes.
<p><b>-
</b></p>
<p><b>Job Details:
</b></p>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities
</b></h2>
<ul>
<li>Ensures sufficient familiarly with contract and state procurement requirements and University processes to perform job duties and provide administrative and technical support to PMCS staff.
</li>
<li>Prepares documents and coordinates distribution and quality control with all stakeholders documents for project solicitation and request for proposals, addendums, contracts, notices to proceed, change orders, pay applications, and close-out.
</li>
<li>Prepares contracts for execution. Reviews contract documents for quality control and compliance to state procurement regulations and PMCS processes.
</li>
<li>Updates Electronic State Business Daily, ESBD, and PMCS websites with project solicitation and contract award information. Performs database entry and generates reports.
</li>
<li>Schedules pre-proposal conferences, bid openings, and contractor selection committee meetings. Prepares required documents for, and assists manager on facilitating, pre-proposal conferences, bid openings, contractor selection committees, and periodic meetings with contractors and professional service providers.
</li>
<li>Schedules and coordinates multi-task projects in an evolving and deadline-driven environment in order to maintain timely completion of contract functions. Proficiently utilizes computer applications Windows PC, Word, Excel, Outlook, Define, FAMIS, Filemaker Pro database, and updates website using Contribute.
</li>
<li>Provides technical and contracting support for PMCS staff. Works independently as well as a team member with other contracting staff, project managers, HUB coordinator and other professionals on PMCS projects.
</li>
<li>Other related functions as assigned.
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Required Qualifications
</b></h2>
<p>High school graduation or GED and five years administrative experience. Experience with construction project contract or business contract processing. Experience in a deadline-driven environment involving multiple projects and duties. Five years of experience in Microsoft Office software. Advanced Word skills including merging, formatting styles, templates and Excel linked workbooks. Experience with database and report generation.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><i>Relevant education and experience may be substituted as appropriate.
</i></p>
<p></p>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Preferred Qualifications
</b></h2>
<p>Bachelor's degree in contract management, business administration, accounting, or a related field. More than five years administrative experience. Two years of experience in the preparation of contracts and contract solicitation process. Experience in State of Texas procurement process. Experience in construction industry, architectural, or engineering processes. Successful completion of the Campus Planning and Facilities Management Leader Development program.
</p>
