<b>Introduction</b><br>
The Channels Specialist position is a sales leader and operations role focused on the successful transition of Business Partner OEM/ESA/ASL/xSP contracts in relation to executing on the contractual obligations of a recent IBM divestiture.<br>
<br>
<b>Your Role and Responsibilities</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Assist with monitoring and responding to correspondence received in project functional mailboxes.
</li>
<li>Update channel FAQs regarding the divestiture when applicable - VAD, VAR, BP, and extended IBM ecosystem.
</li>
<li>Prior IBM software Channel/Ecosystem sales and operations experience is necessary
</li>
<li>A strong understanding of IBM's software contracting especially with Business Partners is preferred.
</li>
<li>This position reports directly to the Global Director for GTM and Client Success for this divestiture in the Cognitive Applications BU</li>
</ul>

Required Professional and Technical Expertise</b><br>
