<h2>AHF Products - Strategic Account Executive, Texas/TOLA</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>AHF Products is an independent wood flooring company established following American Industrial Partners’ (AIP) acquisition of the Armstrong Flooring wood division on December 31, 2018. AHF Products is a leader in hardwood flooring products boasting an impressive portfolio of leading wood flooring brands, including Bruce®, Armstrong Flooring®, HomerWood® and Capella® and serves both the residential and commercial markets.</p>
<p>
AHF Products is a leader in hardwood flooring products boasting an impressive portfolio of leading wood flooring brands, including Bruce®, Robbins®, Hartco®, HomerWood®, Simplicity®, Capella®, LM Flooring® and Versini® and serves both the residential and commercial markets. “We Create Beauty That Lasts For Generations”</p>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
Headquartered in Mountville, Pennsylvania with manufacturing operations across the United States, AHF Products employs over 1,700 dedicated team members.</div>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
We are currently seeking experienced, aggressive, high-energy, self-motivated sales professionals with proven track records of selling building product related solutions to develop new commercial and large contractor customers. In this commissioned – based role you will have direct impact on our growth and expansion plans, along with an equally significant impact to expand your own earning potential. You will be a key factor in taking our vision to the next level, leading our organization into new and undeveloped market segments and business opportunities.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
The ideal candidate will be located within the state of Texas. The region includes the TOLA areas- Texas, Oklahoma, Louisiana &amp; Arkansas, as well as Colorado.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Job Objective:
</b></p>
<ul><li>Responsible for uncovering new multi-million dollar opportunities for an assigned geographic region, cultivating new business opportunities through research, customer prospecting and closing the deal.</li></ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Essential Duties and Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Works with upper level management to establish direct sales, strategic territory plan, and additional market development within targeted geography and product verticals.</li>
<li>
Creates and implements Territory Development Plan.</li>
<li>
Creates indispensable, long-term, solution-focused consultative partnerships with customers, shaping the requirements and solutions required to meet their needs and advance our value proposition.</li>
<li>
Responsible for delivering profitable growth and driving sales to achieve and exceed budgeted targets with direct customers for Robbins branded flooring products.</li>
<li>
Sells AHF products and services by developing and interacting with new prospects at the enterprise level. Creates and builds influential selling relationships with key personnel.</li>
<li>
Understands the customer’s business including its organization, financial objectives, strategy, market position, as well as specific AHF related production capabilities, etc.</li>
<li>
Assesses the competitive environment for both the client (forces and factors facing their business and industry), as well as AHF Products in the particular selling situation.</li>
<li>
Assesses competitor’s strategy and neutralizes with a competitive counter strategy.</li>
<li>
Conducts strategic sales/manufacturing needs analysis individually or with a team.</li>
<li>
Communicates the impact of today’s actions on future opportunities.</li>
<li>
Develops business plans to maximize profitability.</li>
<li>
Interacts with AHF Operations and corporate staff to develop proposals, pricing and credit terms, with the goal of negotiating terms and long-term contracts with prospects.</li>
<li>
Manages relationships with newly landed clients, always looking for opportunities to grow business.</li>
<li>
Performs other duties as assigned or requested.</li>
<li>
Manages expense at or below budgeted levels.</li>
<li>
Keeps abreast of technologies related to business objectives and industry specific trends. Contributes to the development of the company technology platform and best practices, sharing information and technical knowledge with others.</li>
<li>
Develops and maintains relationships throughout customers' organization, buyers, upper management, operations, logistics, store planning, advertising, store management, etc.</li>
<li>
Analyzes areas of opportunity; then develops tactics and implementation plan to deliver the results</li>
<li>
Effectively manages their assigned sales promotion, advertising, and expense budgets</li>
<li>
Responsible for coordination and communication between new customers and AHF support personnel in matters of Customer Financial Services, Advertising, Logistics, Information Systems, and Operations (Customer Service Center, Order Department, and Billing)</li>
<li>
Provides sales forecasts for anticipated volume to close, by category or key products to drive our manufacturing plan and inventories;</li>
<li>
Develops a broad and rich understanding of our products and program offerings in AHF.</li>
<li>
Develops an understanding of the numerous competitors' products and offers. This knowledge ranges from international to US manufactured products depending on the category and size of the competitor.</li>
</ul>
