<b>What You'll Do:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Be a leader within the Customer Success team, partnering with the Global Director of Client Relations to develop global processes and strategies to be implemented on a regional level.</li>
<li>This is a player/coach role where you will manage your own book of brand accounts while managing and mentoring the LA team of Customer Success Managers and dotted-line overseeing LA Product Support team members. Essentially, you will be responsible and accountable for the Los Angeles region's customer experience.</li>
<li>Create strategy for and motivate the team to deliver expected KPIs, ensuring high unit and revenue retention for the region.</li>
<li>Onboard, train and develop trusted advisor relationships with strategic and high-value accounts to continually drive incremental value and return on the customer's investment.</li>
<li>Serve as a resource for the region for client escalations and higher-level issues relating to adoption/usage, product, pricing, accounts receivable, integrations and more.</li>
<li>Strategically expand our revenue in accounts through cross-sell and upsell opportunities, and support team in their efforts.</li>
<li>Train and manage individual team member's performance, ensuring Customer Success team members consistently master and execute CS process and strategy, leveraging all necessary tools/technology (JOOR platform, Salesforce, Jira, etc.).</li>
<li>Create strong partnerships with Sales and Marketing counterparts to ensure a seamless client experience from prospect to ongoing client.</li>
<li>Be an advocate for regional client needs by communicating to senior leadership and working cross-functionally with our Product, QA and Data teams to help move projects forward.</li>
</ul>
<b>What We're Looking For:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor's degree</li>
<li>7+ required years of experience managing accounts or owning a book of business</li>
<li>5+ years of relevant work experience in SaaS, B2B or Account Management preferred</li>
<li>Experience managing and coaching a team</li>
<li>Strong negotiation and sales skills</li>
<li>Experience collaborating with cross-functional teams</li>
</ul>
