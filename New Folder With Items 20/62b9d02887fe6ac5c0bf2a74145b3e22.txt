<h2>Executive - FP&A Management</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><ul>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Role Summary:</b></h2>
The Executive - FP&amp;A Management leader will provide financial leadership to GE Digital FP&amp;A. This role will help lead the business through both short &amp; long-term strategic financial planning &amp; analysis by partnering with leaders across functions (including Engineering, Product Management, Sales, Marketing, Operations, Finance, Legal and Channels) to develop robust, dynamic, &amp; scalable models that can be quickly adapted &amp; updated based on strategic direction of the organization.<br>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Essential Responsibilities:</b></h2>
<p>
This role will partner closely with GE Digital leadership and the Finance organization to integrate and develop processes that meet the business’ needs across the global organization, manage complex issues within Finance, and support investor relations needs.<br>
In this role you will:<br>
</p>
<li>
Develop a deep understanding of all critical aspects of the business model including the infrastructure, platform, and applications as well as the go-to market strategies.<br>
</li>
<li>Continuously benchmark various aspects of the business vs. key competitors.<br>
</li>
<li>Keep a close pulse on industry trends and changes in technologies, competitive landscape and markets.<br>
</li>
<li>Provide coordination for the FP&amp;A team, overseeing global revenue and departmental operating expense forecasting and business reporting for the business CEO/CFO/COO.<br>
</li>
<li>Set a vision for executive level metrics and systems support that ensures we can track, measure and optimize our business, at scale, across our product, sales and finance teams.<br>
</li>
<li>Drive a unified, comprehensive process for forecasting and reporting that ensures product line profitability and sales productivity impacts are aligned and incorporated into our executive reporting and operational dashboards.<br>
</li>
<li>Drive enhanced visibility into business model dynamics (e.g. product maturity curves) &amp; share financial insights with management.<br>
</li>
<li>Translate and interpret our financial results into operational recommendations to improve and accelerate the business.<br>
</li>
<li>Partner with our Business Operations team to drive an efficient, effective and comprehensive process for annual budget planning that links to company strategic objectives and evolves our understanding of budget accountability as we enhance our matrix operating model.<br>
</li>
<li>Provide financial modeling, financial analysis and valuation support for acquisitions and other ad-hoc financial projects. Supervise and manage the performance of the corporate finance team and ensure cross-department collaboration to best optimize and accelerate the business.<br>
</li>
<li>Assist FP&amp;A Leader with management of Investor Relations needs including FAQs, competitive benchmarking, business model analysis, financial outlook, and more.<br>
