 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p><b>Head of Sales, Beam</b></p>
<p>
Nutanix is looking for a seasoned Technical Sales Leader who will essentially be running their own business within the larger Nutanix infrastructure. This is a unique opportunity to embrace your entrepreneurial spirit and essentially be a mini-CEO inside Nutanix. In this role, you will be responsible for managing and developing a sales team while also selling Nutanix’s Products and Solutions through Channel Partners and interacting directly with worldwide customers.</p>
<p>
Nutanix Beam is a multi-cloud optimization service delivered as part of the Nutanix Enterprise Cloud OS. Beam provides organizations with deep visibility and rich analytics detailing cloud consumption patterns, along with one-click cost optimization across their cloud environments. Beam provides you deep visibility into your multi-cloud environment and ability to optimize cloud with one-click. Beam identifies underutilized and unused cloud services and provides convenient single click remediation suggestions, empowering cloud operators to realize cost savings immediately. With Beam, you can set policies that continuously maintain high levels of cloud cost efficiency. More on Nutanix Beam can be found here: https://www.nutanix.com/products/beam/</p>
<p>
Essential to success in this role is a keen ability to lead a successful worldwide sales team and develop new accounts to penetrate new divisions and organizations within your assigned accounts. Nutanix provides standout support and tools from the Engineering, Marketing, and Product Development departments for you to leverage to exceed sales performance goals. In this position, you will be working very closely with the co-founders and SVPs and have a large amount of influence on sales team decisions and initiatives.</p>
<p><b>
Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
At the crux of this role, you will be responsible for driving revenue.</li>
<li>
You will help coordinate sales efforts around the world and will help close deals with the team.</li>
<li>
You will both lead an existing cross-geographical team that will consist of a combination of sales and technical sales. Initially this team will be 8-10 people with the opportunity to build and grow.</li>
<li>
In this role, you will work closely with Marketing to build pipeline; enable the field teams on the product and be an evangelist for the product</li>
<li>
You will provide feedback to the product management team on specific product needs.</li>
<li>
Use relationship management techniques to develop selling opportunities within partner organizations; penetrate new divisions and organizations within assigned partner account; develop new selling relationships within assigned partner account; develop new direct selling opportunities.</li>
<li>
Schedule and attend sales call appointments with a prospect in partner organization. Nutanix Channel Partner Representatives may also participate in the sales call to help qualify the opportunity.</li>
<li>
Utilizing a consultative approach, discuss business issues with prospect and develop a formal quote, a written sales proposal or a formal sales presentation addressing their business needs.</li>
<li>
Respond to RFP's and follow up with prospects.</li>
<li>
Develop an account plan to sell to customers based on their needs in the business.</li>
<li>
Build and strengthen the business relationship with current accounts and new prospects.</li>
<li>
Recommend marketing strategies.</li>
<li>
Provide status information to your Manager including forecast/pipeline information.</li>
<li>
Provide or facilitate training opportunities for your accounts.</li>
<li>
Identify Nutanix customer references that can be utilized when reference selling.</li>
<li>
Provide product feedback back to engineering to improve Nutanix complete block solutions</li>
</ul>
<p><br>
<b>
Skills</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
You will have a strong technical sales background to succeed in this role.</li>
<li>
Strong verbal and written communications skills including presentation skills.</li>
<li>
Consistent track record in people &amp; sales management</li>
<li>
Ability to work collaboratively with employees within the sales function and across functions including Marketing, Sales Operations, System Engineering, and Product Development.</li>
<li>
You will need to understand and work with the Channel team and the Field teams around the world</li>
<li>
Experience with target account selling, solution selling, and/or consultative sales techniques</li>
<li>
An aptitude for understanding how technology products and solutions tackle business problems as well as the competitive landscape.</li>
<li>
Ability to communicate with senior managers about their business challenges and Nutanix data management storage solutions.</li>
<li>
Experience using SFDC and other CRM software.</li>
<li>
Track record of exceeding assigned sales quotas in contiguous, multiple years.</li>
</ul>

Qualifications and Experience</b></p>
<ul>

15+ years of sales experience preferred.</li>
<li>
Experience that demonstrates a strong level of expertise in technical specifications required to sell Nutanix products and services is required.</li>
<li>
Bachelor’s Degree or equivalent experience</li>
<li>
Strong desire to travel up to 30% of the time</li>
</ul>
