ABOUT US:</b></div>

<b>Who We Are:</b> The Texas Military Department (TMD) is commanded by The Adjutant General of Texas, the state's senior military official appointed by the governor, and is comprised of the Texas Military Department (State &amp; Federal Civilian Employees), the Texas Army National Guard (TXARNG), the Texas Air National Guard (TXANG) and the Texas State Guard (TXSG).</div>

<div><b>
BRIEF POSITION SUMMARY</b></div>
<div>Performs routine (journey-level) marketing work for the Texas ChalleNGe Academy (TCA). Administers the TCA program application process, schedules and conducts program presentations, maintains contact information, disseminates marketing information and works with the screening coordinator to ensure completion of application documents. Provides communities with TCA program publicity information and participates in civic events. The TCA Recruiter works in an environment external to the TCA program facility and may be co-located with National Guard units or with the home program training facility. The TCA Recruiter reports directly to the Marketing Coordinator and works under moderate supervision with limited latitude for the use of initiative and independent judgment.</div>
<br>
<div></div>

<div><b>ESSENTIAL JOB FUNCTIONS AND RESPONSIBILITIES</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Handles the TCA program application process from applicant prospecting to preparation for acceptance.</li>
<li>
Identifies and annually recruits 200 qualified statewide high school aged youth.</li>
<li>
Recruits qualified TCA program candidates according to Master Youth Programs Cooperative Agreement acceptance standards; using computer databases, networking, internet recruiting resources, cold calls, media and TCA program graduate referrals.</li>
<li>
Provides applicants with information on program facilities, operations, benefits and job or career opportunities.</li>
<li>
Provides potential applicants and their families with TCA program information to include training, education and job skills opportunities.</li>
<li>
Reviews and evaluates applicant qualifications for eligibility.</li>
<li>
Provide applicants with application material and forwards completed applications to the screening coordinator.</li>
<li>
Contacts applicants to inform them of acceptance possibilities, consideration and selection.</li>
<li>
Recruits applicants for acceptance by arranging program presentations.</li>
<li>
Gathers and maintains current contact information/records.</li>
<li>
Establishes point of contacts for independent school districts and the juvenile justice system.</li>
<li>
Networks and identifies communities and/or other civic organizations to promote TCA program.</li>
<li>
Develops and maintains contact information on database system.</li>
<li>
Assists with developing and implementing the TCA recruiting campaign.</li>
<li>
Arranges presentations and prepares travel arrangements as necessary.</li>
<li>
Promotes and markets the TCA program through public speaking engagements.</li>
<li>
Coordinates TCA recruiting campaign with community organizations and the local media.</li>
<li>
Works with the TCA Marketing Specialist to organize, prepare and disseminate recruiting materials and/or presentations.</li>
<li>
Addresses civic and social groups and attends conferences to disseminate TCA information concerning acceptance criterion, opportunities and class cycles.</li>
<li>
Performs related work as assigned.</li>
<li>
Maintains current knowledge of the Master Youth Programs Cooperative Agreement as it pertains to applicant qualifications.</li>
<li>
Projects annual recruitment expenditures for budgetary consideration and control.</li>
</ul>
