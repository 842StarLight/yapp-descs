<h2>Sales Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Job Summary:</p>
<p><b>
Unity in Diversity
</b></p>
<p>Our team members are as diverse as our music venues. With its owned/operated/managed venues, Live Nation Clubs and Theaters, a division of Live Nation Entertainment, is wide-ranging, including the legendary Fillmore venues and the intimate House of Blues clubs throughout the United States. Live Nation Clubs and Theaters is the country’s preeminent group of intimate music venues, featuring superior sound and lighting technology in one-of-a-kind custom-designed environments aimed to bring fans and artists together in unparalleled musical environments.</p>
<p></p>
<p>
Our mission is to create a profitable and principled global entertainment company. To celebrate the diversity and brotherhood of world culture. To promote racial and spiritual harmony through love, peace, truth, righteousness and non-violence. Our venues bring fans as close to artists as possible and our aim is to create teams that are just as close.</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>
The Role:</b></p>
<p>
A leadership position grounded in a firm commitment to providing a superior entertainment experience to our guests and guidance and development for the Premium Experiences sales team. As the Premium Experiences Sales Manager, you are counted on to generate revenue through new and exciting Premium Experience revenue streams while always attracting new customers and retaining existing ones. You are someone who can be counted on to grow and develop a strong Premium Experiences team and is committed to the growth and development in Philadelphia. You are a senior sales expert committed to enhancing the guest experience, growing the Premium Experiences program and creating leaders in our organization.</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>
What You’ll Do:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Sell Premium Seating to new customers and responsible for renewing contracts for existing customers. Budget requirements are set for the Manager, monthly.</li>
<li>Develops and executes Business Plan for the Premium Experiences Program including: pricing, advertising/marketing, expense outline, etc.</li>
<li>Develop and maintain client relationships in keeping with standards of a Premium Experience.</li>
<li>Act as venue liaison with local Convention and Visitor’s Bureau, Chambers of Commerce, Meeting Planners Associations, and like type organizations.</li>
<li>Develop and manage operating expense budget and adhere to budget parameters.</li>
<li>Participate in events, promotions, client entertainment and other activities as required.</li>
<li>Responsible for facilitating on-site Premium Experiences program operations and client entertainment at most concerts/events.</li>
<li>Assist in negotiation with internal and external vendors pertaining to the Premium Experiences Program under the direction of the General Manager.</li>
<li>Responsible for collection of accounts receivable for Premium Experiences Program.</li>
<li>Secure promotional and advertising materials for department including: brochures, website information page, e-cards, etc.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>
In addition to the performance of essential job functions, the Sales Manager will be evaluated on the following qualities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Appearance</li>
<li>Punctuality and Attendance</li>
<li>Attitude and Teamwork</li>
<li>Job Skills / Productivity</li>
<li>Communication (Written and Verbal)</li>
<li>Adherence to Company Policies</li>
<li>Working Relationships</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>
What You’ll Need:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>College degree and 3+ years of experience in a sales role</li>
<li>Strong commitment to customer service</li>
<li>Strong commitment to empowering leaders in the organization</li>
<li>High level of attention to detail</li>
<li>Independent working style and desire to own and drive business performance improvements</li>
<li>Ability to clearly communicate and work effectively with all levels and functions of HOBE organization</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Preferred:</b></p>
<ul><li>Prior experience in Hospitality &amp; or Entertainment Industry</li></ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Our Benefits:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Competitive compensation and bonus plans</li>
<li>Professional career development</li>
<li>Additional benefits</li>
</ul>
