From high-speed broadband and ultra-HD video, to digital advertising solutions, local news and voice offerings, we are making connections possible around the country - to businesses, to consumers, to communities, to schools, to hospitals, and everyone in between.<br>

<div>The primary function of the Account Manager is to provide the Altice Business Sales Channels (SMB &amp; AE) back office support on all aspects of the Sales process by providing daily, weekly, and monthly sales orders updates and productivity reports. Responsibilities extend to the management of order/contract status along with the order flow in Salesforce.com (SFDC). Interaction with Direct Sales, Agent Sales, Carrier Sales, Sales Management, Marketing, Product, IT, and Field Service, Construction and Business Planning.<br>
The Account Manager is involved in all stages of the pre-sale and post-sale customer experience.<br>
Sale &amp; Sales Operations pre-sale process:<br>
<ul>
<li>Liaison between the SMB AE and business to identify and correct obstacles regarding the customer’s location or system configuration and advises accordingly</li>
<li>Assists in processing orders into the appropriate business system</li>
<li>Communicates with the Account Executive, Customer, Field Service, OV Operations, and TSG to resolve any post order / pre-install issues on orders</li>
<li>Responsible for overseeing the processing of all other services purchased with sales</li>
<li>Support administrative functions of lead generation and administration</li>
<li>Provide administrative support for the Referral Partner program, including but not limited to Lead distribution, vendor qualification forms, payment confirmation etc.</li>
</ul>
Post-sale:<br>
Point of contact for customer inquiries involving: quality of order issues, billing, disconnects and contract related concerns<br>
Duties and Responsabilités :<br>
1. Report in to local Sales Manager &amp; Director<br>
2. Support the SMB AE’s (who directly report into local Sales Manager)<br>
<ul>
<li>Serviceability checks, surveys, construction assistance</li>
<li>Sales order issues – not dones, etc.</li>
<li>Process small move-add-change orders</li>
</ul>
3. Compensation- Determined by combination of Base salary and Commissions<br>
<ul>
<li>Commission Plan- Ability to earn commissions based on both Team and Individual SMB AE Performance metrics</li>
<li>Base Salary</li>
</ul>
4. Interact effectively as a team member within a Commercial Team Organization to support achievement of sales objectives and deliver Altice’s quality of Service<br>
5. Partner with Sales, Service and Support personnel to strategically support Nationwide Accounts<br>
6. Make accurate and timely descisions based on customer needs and business requirements<br>
7. Develop, manintain and manage customer relationships from the operational to executive levels throughout the organization<br>
8. Ability to contribute to the attainment of revenue objectives by managing orders from the sales stage through to accurate billing and revenue recognition<br>
9. Project Management of the activities of multiple departments as Contracts, Pricing, Order Entry, Provisioing, Design, Installation and Billing on behalf of Customer to install new service, or to coordinate moves, adds or changes to existing service.<br>
10. Understands all Optimum Business telecommunication products and services, including new and varied technological features, product equipment functionality, existing programming content, pricing, packaging and channel lineup.<br>
11. Keep up to date on all competitor’s products, services, pricing and promotions.<br>
12. Attend and participate in all sales meetings and workshops scheduled by management.<br>

15. Responsible for meeting Company standards pertaining to quantity and quality of work performed as well as performing these duties in compliance with all Company guidelines, values, procedures and/or policies.<br>
16. Provides quality internal and external customer service surrounding the Company values<br>

18. Negotiates and prepares complete and accurate documentation for Commercial Service Agreements based on established strategic, financial, legal and operational criteria.<br>
