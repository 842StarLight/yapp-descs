<h2>Regional Trainer - Northwest</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p>We are focused on becoming the preeminent and most effective digital PK-12 STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering, and Math) resource used by teachers, students, and parents as measured by adoption, usage, and results.</p>
<p>Do you love to <b>teach</b>! Are you making a difference? We are looking for a Regional STEM Coach to join a talented team dedicated to developing and supporting thousands of teachers in STEM education. If you can engage a room of adult learners and impact others, this position may be for you.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>JOB DESCRIPTION</b></p>
<p>The Regional STEM Coach develops, coordinates, and delivers high-quality professional development on STEM topics, products, and programs to educators in a designated region. Other responsibilities include participating in sales presentations, supporting pre-purchase trials and pilots, and consulting with current/prospective customers as a STEM content and product implementation expert. Regional STEM Coaches recruit, train, and support a cohort of independent consultants who deliver professional services on the Regional STEM Coach’s behalf, especially in regions with a large customer base. This full-time position reports to the Vice President and Managers of Professional Development.</p>
<h1 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>ESSENTIAL DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES:</b></h1>
<p>? Personally deliver training, coaching, and support services in person and via webinar</p>
<p>? Consult with district leadership to identify professional development needs/opportunities</p>
<p>? Produce and deliver customized professional learning in response to customer needs</p>
<p>? Recruit, prepare, and support contract trainers to support regional demand for services</p>
<p>? Coordinate scheduling, staffing, and materials for all face-to-face training in designated region</p>
<p>? Participate in sales presentations as a product/content expert</p>
<p>? Train and support potential customers during pre-purchase trials and pilots</p>
<p>? Represent Accelerate Learning by presenting at regional/state conferences</p>
<p>? Inform Account Managers on relevant account details including opportunities and issues</p>
<p>? Collaborate and contribute to the greater good of the entire Professional Development team</p>
<h1 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>COMPETENCIES:</b></h1>
<p>? Strong understanding of best practices for educator professional development</p>
<p>? Expertise in STEM curriculum, content, and instructional strategies</p>
<p>? Passion for excellent customer service and high standards for quality</p>
<p>? Excellent written and verbal communication skills</p>
<p>? Effective communication with customers and colleagues one-on-one and in large groups</p>
<p>? Ability to connect, engage, and energize a room full training participants</p>
<p>? Solid organization and planning skills, including prioritizing multiple tasks to meet deadlines</p>
<p>? Must be able to collaborate in a virtual team environment</p>
<p>? Solutions-oriented approach to problem-solving</p>
<p>? Ability to learn new information quickly</p>
<p>? Positive attitude toward change with an ability to flex and adapt</p>
<p>? Absolute personal integrity and credibility</p>
<p>? Regional travel up to 60% of the time with possibility for national travel (depending on need)</p>
<h1 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>COMPUTER SKILLS:</b></h1>
<p>? Desire to learn new technology/software/apps</p>
<p>? Virtual meeting tools such as Zoom</p>
<p>? Microsoft Office: PowerPoint, Excel, and Word</p>
<p>? G-Suite: Gmail, Calendar, Drive, Docs, Sheets, Slides</p>
<p>? Mac operating system/computers</p>
<p>? Technology troubleshooting skills (eg. connecting to projectors, speakers, etc.)</p>
<h1 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>EDUCATION AND/OR EXPERIENCE:</b></h1>
<p>? Bachelor’s degree or equivalent</p>
<p>? 5+ years of experience in K-12 education/administration</p>
<p>? Experience providing professional development to educators</p>
<p>? Experience writing professional development content/materials preferred</p>
<p>? Certified teacher preferred</p>
<p>? Science, Mathematics, Technology, and/or Engineering teaching experience preferred</p>
<p>? Experience teaching with STEMscopes curriculum highly desired</p>
<p>? Public speaking/platform experience preferred</p>
<h1 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>PHYSICAL DEMANDS:</b></h1>
<p>Physical demands of the position will include mobility throughout the day; lifting, reaching, and carrying materials associated with the preparation, distribution, demonstration, use and maintenance of various materials and equipment for training events and showcases. Occasionally, more rigorous activity may be associated with field experiences or other program activities. While performing the duties of this job, the employee may occasionally be required during evenings and weekend hours, especially during periods of high demand such as back-to-school season. Must be able to travel for conferences, events, showcases, and trainings.</p>

<h1 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>ABOUT US:</b></h1>

Our core brand, STEMscopes, is the most widely used PreK-12 science curriculum in Texas and is currently in use with over 4 million students across all 50 states.</p>

Students flourish when teachers deeply understand the content, best teaching practices, and the use of available instructional resources. As a Regional STEM Coach, you will be joining a talented team dedicated to developing and supporting thousands of teachers in STEM education.</p>
