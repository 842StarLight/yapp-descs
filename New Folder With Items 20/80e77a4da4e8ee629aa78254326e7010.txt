<h2>Applications Engineer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>An Application Engineer provides technical support and guidance to the Global Sales team. He/she assists the sales team in understanding customer applications and technical/business requirements and proposing cost-effective solutions. He/she travels with Sales or independently to meet with existing and prospective customers. Key market verticals for Comtech include cellular backhaul, premium enterprise, mobility, satellite operators, and Government communications.<br>
<br>
<b>Essential Duties and Responsibilities</b><br>
<br>
Provide technical sales assistance to Sales.<br>
<br>
Prepare technical responses to Request for Proposals (RFPs), Request for Information (RFIs), Request for Quotes (RFQs). Generate proposals for customers, clearly articulating the benefits of a Comtech EF Data solution.<br>
<br>
Design solutions to meet customer business and technical requirements.<br>
<br>
Create Bill of Materials and assist Sales with the generation of Sales quotations.<br>
<br>
Maintain detailed knowledge of competitor offerings and qualitatively and quantitatively articulate the benefits of Comtech EF Data's solutions versus competing alternatives.<br>
<br>
Ability to develop business cases.<br>
<br>
Attend customer meetings and industry events / trade shows to evaluate and define technical solutions and facilitate sales (international and domestic travel required).<br>
<br>
Assist with product demonstrations at customer site.<br>
<br>
Maintain SE lab in Tempe, AZ.<br>
<br>
<b>Education and/or Experience</b></p>
<br>
<p><br>
Bachelor's degree in Electrical Engineering, Computer Science, Computer Engineering, Networking, Telecommunications or related work experience is required.<br>
<br>
Minimum five (5) years of experience in technical pre-sales in networking, telecom or satellite communications industry.<br>
<br>
Excellent knowledge of IP networking including LAN/WAN architectures. Cisco (CCNA, CCNP, CCIE) and other relevant training and certifications are highly desirable.<br>
<br>
Knowledge of 2G/3G/4G LTE backhaul is highly desirable. Experience working with major MNO vendor equipment is highly desirable.<br>
<br>
Knowledge of satellite communication technologies including different access technologies is required. Familiarity with industry standards such as DVB-S2X, DVB-RCS2 is desirable.<br>
<br>
Ability to perform link analysis is desirable.<br>
<br>
Excellent oral and written communication skills required.<br>
<br>
Detail-oriented with aptitude for thoroughness, follow through, and creativity.<br>
<br>
Must be able to travel as needed.<br>
<br>
US Citizenship required.<br>
<br>
Security Clearance preferred.<br>
<br>
<b>Position Location</b><br>
<br>
One position in Tempe, AZ, USA.<br>
<br>
<b>About Us</b><br>
<br>
Comtech EF Data Corp. is a leading supplier of communications equipment with a focus on satellite bandwidth efficiency and link optimization. The high-performance satellite communications ground equipment is deployed globally to support mission-critical and demanding applications for government, mobile backhaul, premium enterprise and mobility. Service providers, satellite operators, governments and commercial users wanting to optimize communications, increase throughput and delight customers, are leveraging the performance and flexibility of the Comtech brand. The solutions are facilitating fixed and mobile networks in 160+ countries and across every ocean. For more information, visit www.comtechefdata.com.
