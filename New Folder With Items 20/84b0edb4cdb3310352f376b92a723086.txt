<h2>Event Marketing Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><i>Dictyon, Language Solutions is looking to hire an event marketing manager to be responsible for spearheading Dictyon’s event marketing strategy, including but not limited to: organizing cultural immersion events, including field trips and travel, representing Dictyon at industry events, and implementing guerilla marketing campaigns. This individual should have a successful track record of organizing cultural events, familiar with the international community, languages and world cultures, attendance at trade shows and proving ROI through event marketing strategies and tactics.</i></p>
<p><b>Job Summary: </b><br>Help manage and oversee the majority of Dictyon's corporate and marketing events which includes planning, developing, executing and measuring results.</p>
<p><b>Major Responsibilities/Activities: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Manage the day-to-day planning and execution of corporate and client facing events, industry conferences, and trade shows, including pre- and post-event communications and attendee management</li>
<li>Negotiate and execute contracts for both large and small scale events</li>
<li>Collaborate across departments to contribute to meeting design, agenda development, brand building, and event communications and marketing</li>
<li>Partner with the management and sales team to develop strategies leveraging existing content and access to industry communities to promote events and tradeshows</li>
<li>Communications: provide consistent and clear communication of events program to all stakeholders, including internal briefing documents for specified events and conferences.</li>
<li>Work alongside the Marketing team to design, document, and execute innovative multi-channel marketing programs for Dictyon's events.</li>
<li>Event Systems Administrator: responsible for effective provisioning, configuration, operation, and maintenance of event systems.</li>
<li>Vendor Management: Collaborate with promotional &amp; print vendors and maintain the best relationship possible with those vendors.</li>
<li>Budget Management: You will oversee, manage, and wrap-up event budgets with internal teams and vendors</li>
<li>Provide ongoing marketing support and material development for all event initiatives</li>
<li>Manage and execute events starting with the RFP process, through vendor contracting, planning, registration and on-site execution</li>
<li>Source new venues / locations and sponsors for upcoming events</li>
<li>Assist in evaluating ROI for all events</li>
<li>Work as a shared resource to the sales force, in support of their event needs</li>
<li>Develop an event planning process with a strategic planning of events calendar</li>
<li>Plan, coordinate and execute events</li>
</ul>
