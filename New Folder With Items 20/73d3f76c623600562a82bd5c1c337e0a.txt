<h2>Travel Expert, Part-Time</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Welcome to the Travel Corporation! With over 10,000 employees across 35 offices across the world and over 70 years of experience, we aim to spread the love of travel. Our philosophy is simple: To create enriching experiences for our travelers by combining an unbeatable mix of exceptional service and quality at great value.</p>
<p>We are seeking vacation planners, city explorers and travel experts. We would like to welcome you on board as a <b>Customer Service Representative</b>- <b>Reservation Agent </b>to support our Family of Brands and begin your career in travel.</p>
<p>We are excited to now offer this role as both full-time and part-time. If you are looking for flexible scheduling, and a great workplace, apply today!</p>
<p><b> THIS ROLE INVOLVES:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
<b>Sales: </b>You will answer incoming calls and match our product with customer needs. You will maximize opportunities to up-sell and participate in call center initiatives for increased sales.</li>
<li>
<b>Exploration: </b>You will develop a quest for knowledge to understand the brand you represent.</li>
<li>
<b>Problem Resolution: </b>You will identify, research, and resolve issues to customer satisfaction.</li>
<li>
<b>Collaboration: </b>You will partner with your manager and teammates to meet your individual goals and contribute to the overall sales goals for the brand.</li>
<li>
<b>Taking initiative: </b>You will be proactive in generating business by following up on incoming leads from our sales and marketing campaigns.</li>
<li>
<b>Nurturing: </b>You will build, develop and cultivate relationships with repeat clients, travel partners and colleagues.</li>
</ul>

<li>
<b>Attentive to details: </b>Providing quality is important to you. You know that being accurate and thorough contributes to positive customer experiences.</li>
<li>
<b>Enthusiastic: </b>You have an infectiously positive personality.</li>
<li>
<b>A planner: </b>You are eager to research each destination so you can navigate the city like a local.</li>
