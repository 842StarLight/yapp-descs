<h2>Sr. Director, System Test and Customer Adoption</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>ISBU has been an innovative start-up within VMware that past two years, growing at a rocket ship pace. ISBU’s mission is to create secure, agile, and consistent hybrid clouds – which then enables enterprises to run a spectrum of workloads, from mission-critical workflows to apps leveraging containers, ML/AI, Blockchain, Big Data, FaaS, HPC infrastructure or other technologies in both private and public environments. To achieve this mission, ISBU delivers a full stack hybrid cloud infrastructure, VMware Cloud Foundation (VCF), which has become VMware’s de facto solution in both the private and public clouds. For more information: https://www.vmware.com/products/cloud-foundation.html</p>
<p></p>
<p>
We are looking for an ambitious, entrepreneurial leader with a strong combination of business and technical skills to lead a unique role to maintain and enhance the phenomenal customer experience of VCF. There are two primary responsibilities in this role:</p>
<p>
(a) owning and driving Customer adoption and ensuring great Day 0 and Day N experience for VCF, and</p>
<p>
 (b) being responsible for the end-to-end quality of our full stack software solutions.</p>
<p></p>
<p>
Through your efforts, you will directly impact customer adoption and customer experience for VCF, and bring the learnings back to improve our product development and system testing.</p>
<p></p>
<p>
In the first portion of the role, you will own strategic customer success at scale, ensuring a great customer experience through successful deployments, and helps customers maximize ongoing value from our solution. Approaching 1,000+ customers worldwide by the end of the year, the Customer Adoption team represents a significant opportunity to deepen and expand VMware’s overall customer relationships and significantly contribute to the sales objectives. This role is highly visible and will work closely with executives and leaders from Sales, Product Management, Engineering, Technical Support, Professional Services, Partners, Operations. The leader will create and implement systems and processes to operationalize these practices at scale.</p>
<p>
For the second portion, you will own the overall efforts of end-to-end System Testing and DevOps/Serviceability Tools. You will lead a dynamic, global, System Test engineering team with the responsibility to ensure that we deliver high-quality solutions to meet our customers’ needs. You will also develop and maintain innovative tools to improve customer experience when using our product, and engineering and DevOps experience with our CI/CD pipeline. As the leader of this team, you will be responsible for the overall quality of VCF, communication, teamwork, and people management of our System Test Engineering teams.
</p>
<p></p>
