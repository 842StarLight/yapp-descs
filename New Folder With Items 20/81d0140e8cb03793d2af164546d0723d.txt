Requisition Summary
</div>
<div><p>The District Parts Manager is responsible for managing the parts sales of their assigned district of Kenworth dealers. Coordinate and promote day-to-day heavy-duty and medium-duty parts sales program through dealer visits and marketing programs.</p></div>
</div>
<p></p>
<br>
<div>
<div>
Job Functions / Responsibilities
</div>
<div><ul>
<li>Drive wholesale and retail sales growth through aggressive dealer, customer and fleet visits in coordination with key dealership personnel, promoting part sales, PACCAR Parts programs, systems and marketing plans.</li>
<li>
Develop a close working relationship with dealer parts and service teams to achieve parts sales goals and objectives, while maintaining dealer program participation goals and network consistency.</li>
<li>
Work with Dealer Principal and dealership senior management to serve as consultant to dealers' parts and service business growth and departmental profitability.</li>
<li>
Train dealer personnel on key initiatives, programs and systems.</li>
<li>
Assist dealer personnel in identifying and developing key parts and service accounts.</li>
<li>
Act as liaison between dealer, PACCAR Parts Division, Kenworth Division and other PACCAR Divisions in resolving heavy-duty and medium-duty parts related issues as needed.</li>
<li>
65% travel required.</li>
<li>
Other projects as assigned.</li>
</ul></div>
