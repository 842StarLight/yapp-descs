<h2>Funding Coordinator - Administrative - Houston, TX</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<b>We need dedicated people passionate about helping those in need! No experience necessary!</b><br>
<br>
Travis Medical is a growing home medical equipment company with branch locations in Texas, Florida and Oklahoma. We are a private, family-owned business that cares about providing quality service to our communities, and we are looking for compassionate individuals to assist us with administrative duties required to provide medical equipment to customers. Our office is located at: <b>1332 Upland Drive. Houston, TX 77043</b><br>
<br>
This position is full-time and requires someone who is dedicated and punctual, who can balance compassion and kindness with purpose and professionalism. The ideal candidate is a quick learner who can retain knowledge and training. The candidate should also demonstrate initiative and be detail oriented.<br>
Responsibilities of the position include:
<ul>
<li>Provide quality, compassionate service to all internal and external clients</li>
<li>Complete insurance eligibility verification checks, create quotes from initial assessment documents, ensure accuracy of reimbursement &amp; profitability calculations and generate delivery tickets utilizing industry-related software</li>
<li>Medical Coding and processing of orders</li>
<li>Coordinate between physician's offices, referral sources and funding sources to ensure that all necessary documentation is on file and corroborates patient's need for equipment</li>
<li>Routinely follow up on submitted requests for prior authorization</li>
<li>Research and review coverage policies of funding sources</li>
<li>Assist with administrative tasks such as scanning, filing, data entry, reporting, etc.</li>
</ul>Our ideal candidate will meet the following professional requirements:
<ul>
<li>High School Diploma with some continuing education</li>
<li>Proficiency with Word, Excel and Outlook</li>
<li>Excellent verbal and written communication skills</li>
<li>Detail-oriented and dedicated</li>
<li>Great attitude and a positive demeanor</li>
<li>Stable work history</li>
</ul>The normal work schedule is Monday-Friday, 8:30am - 5:00pm. We offer medical, dental, vision and life insurance, paid time off, paid holidays and a team-oriented work environment.<br>

We will train the right individual, so this is a great way to get your foot into the medical industry with no previous experience!
