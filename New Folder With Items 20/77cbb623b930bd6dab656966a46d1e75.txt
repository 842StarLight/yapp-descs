<h2>Regional Credit Analyst</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p><b>ACCOUNTABILITIES</b></p>
<p>
Contacts delinquent customers by phone regarding collection of monetary payment due to TTI by utilizing the Overdue Customer Listing report and prioritizing the calls based on severity of delinquent balances.</p>
<p>
Provides accounts receivable information to customers by reconciling their accounts and looking up account information on the company system, ensuring that the customer is given the most accurate account status. Utilizes fax and e-mail to send account statements to the customer.</p>
<p>
Verifies and investigates information provided on new account credit applications by utilizing bank and trade references, along with D &amp; B credit reports to establish credit limits within authority up to $50,000. Makes recommendations to Regional Credit Manager for approvals of amounts over authority levels.</p>
<p>
Performs new account set-ups on the company database by utilizing the information provided on the approved credit applications, ensuring timely and accurate processing.</p>
<p>
Evaluates orders being held for credit reasons by reviewing daily reports, or from a special request by a customer via e-mail or telephone. Will execute an order release once payment has been secured or credit has been reviewed and approved within guidelines to justify an order release.</p>
<p>
Make recommendations to RCM to improve the level of write-offs/purges/and/or reserves by utilizing branch/region reports for assigned accounts.</p>
<p>
Provides troubleshooting assistance to regional sales force by analyzing customer account information, evaluating customer balances, billing discrepancies, and orders being held, in an effort to assist the sales person win a bankable order.</p>
<p>
Provides support to the regional credit department by preparing and maintaining business correspondence, creating customer files, printing statements and duplicate invoices, providing credit reference information, and providing proof of deliveries and tracers to customer.</p>
<p>
Participates actively in TTI’s Total Quality program by following established policies and procedures and identifying the areas of process improvement.</p>
<p>
Completes other projects as assigned</p>
<p></p>

EDUCATION &amp; EXPERIENCE</b></p>
