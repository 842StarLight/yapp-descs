<h2>Support Technician, Facilities</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div></div>
<div>
<div><div>The Support Technician – Facilities is responsible all job duties associated with supply processing, inventory maintenance, shipping and receiving, cylinder supply, facilities maintenance and grounds functions.
</div></div>
<br>
<br>
<div></div>
<div><div>Detailed Description</div></div>
<br>
<br>
<div></div>
<div><div>
Duties of the Support Technician – Facilities include the following:<br>
<br>
<li>Receiving and sanitizing used supplies from production areas;</li>
<br>
<li>Preparing clean supplies for distribution and filling containers as requested;</li>
<br>
<li>Loading, unloading, running, and performing maintenance on cage/rack washers;</li>
<br>
<li>Receiving, categorizing, inventorying, and distributing supplies;</li>
<br>
<li>Driving delivery vehicles and making deliveries to customer facilities;</li>
<br>
<li>Self motivator; comfortable with working independently for hours at a time;</li>
<br>
<li>Interacting with and receiving service from vendors;</li>
<br>
<li>Processing in-house and contract laundry;</li>
<br>
<li>Processing, documenting, and packing surplus animal sales;</li>
<br>
<li>Performing basic maintenance and janitorial duties;</li>
<br>
<li>Tearing down, repairing, and reprocessing isolators;</li>
<br>
<li>Inspecting and performing maintenance on containers and cylinders;</li>
<br>
<li>Running autoclave and maintaining boiler systems when necessary;</li>
<br>
<li>Assisting with the completion of special projects;</li>
<br>
<li>Entering and retrieving data within a computer system, experience with Oracle helpful;</li>
<br>
<li>Completing necessary log books and documentation.
</li>
</div></div>
<br>
<br>
<div></div>
<div><div>Job Requirements</div></div>
<br>
<br>
<div></div>
<div><div>
Candidates must have a High School Diploma or GED and at least three months’ work experience or related training. Valid driver's license required. Mechanical aptitude helpful. Candidates must have a demonstrated ability to follow standard operating procedures and instructions, as well as an ability to communicate clearly, verbally and in writing. Candidates should have a demonstrated ability to solve problems. Regular physical exertion is required, such as climbing, crouching, crawling, and/or lifting up to 50 lbs.</div></div>
