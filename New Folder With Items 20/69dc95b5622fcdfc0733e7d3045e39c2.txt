<h2>Spa Concierge</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Primary Purpose of Position</b></p>
<p>The Spa Concierge Level 1 is the primary point of contact for all guests during their visit in the Spa and assist them with whatever needs they may have.</p>
<p><b>Essential Duties and Responsibilities</b>
</p>
<ul>
<li>Answer phones in a prompt manner, answer guests' questions, and book appointments accurately/efficiently (maximizing revenue)</li>
<li>Schedule appointments while adhering to LHW standards</li>
<li>Check guests into appointments, offering an orientation to the Spa</li>
<li>Check guests out of appointments and ensure guest satisfaction with treatments; handle cash, credit card, and room transactions accurately</li>
<li>Facilitate communication between guests and service providers</li>
<li>Obtain a thorough understanding of Spa products and services in order to ensure effective and efficient recommendations to guests</li>
<li>Maintain a clean Spa environment and ensure all areas are fully stocked</li>
<li>Coordinate with various departments to meet guests' needs, including: reservations, room service, front desk, engineering and housekeeping</li>
<li>Participate in regularly scheduled training sessions with Lead Spa Concierge and/or Spa Director</li>
<li>Opening and Closing of the Spa area in an effective and efficient manner using daily checklists</li>
<li>Sell retail to guests and close sales for aestheticians/therapists
</li>
<li>Participate in performing monthly retail inventory</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Education/Experience</b></p>
<ul>
<li>High School Diploma</li>
<li>Resort and/or spa experience preferred</li>
<li>Experience with computer software systems, including but not limited to: Spa Soft, Microsoft Office (particularly Outlook), Springer Miller Systems, HotSOS</li>
<li>Based on performance this position can lead to a higher classification in the department; performance is determined based on the results from regular inspections, following LHW standards, quality and quantity of work.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Special Skills/Equipment</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Effective communicator with the ability to work in a team-environment</li>
<li>Proficient in English language (oral and written)</li>
<li>Customer service and sales experience</li>
<li>Ability to work in a fast-paced environment and adapt to any new changes in policies or procedures with minimal issues</li>
<li>Self-initiator with well-rounded problem solving skills</li>
<li>High attention to detail</li>
<li>Maintain a high level of guest service and exceed all guests' expectations when possible</li>
<li>Knowledge of Hotel policies and procedures, outlets and specials</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Essential Physical Requirements</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Full-time: Must work 36-40 hours per week, on a varying schedule ranging from 7am until 8pm</li>
<li>Part-time: Work up to 30 hours per week, on a varying schedule ranging from 7am until 8pm</li>
<li>Must be able to work 10 hour shifts</li>
<li>Ability to lift 20 lbs</li>
<li>Must be able to stand for 8-10 hours during shift</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Employee Conduct</b></p>
<ul><li>It is the responsibility of every employee to contribute to a positive work environment through cooperative and professional interactions with co-workers, guests and vendors.</li></ul>
