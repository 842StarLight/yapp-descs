<div>DevOps / Network Engineer<br>

<div>We are looking for a talented engineer with Network experience and DevOps skills to join the NetFortris team. This position's primary focus is environment automation and improvement, while also participating in builds/deployments and legacy environment upkeep.<br>

<div>Contribute to configuration management (Puppet and SaltStack).</div>
<div>Contribute to procedures for packaging, deploying and automating Netfortris software.</div>
<div>Participation in Release management.</div>
<div>Fixing environment issues that QA or Developers report.</div>
<div>Maintaining load balancers (HAProxy)</div>
<div>Scripting (Python, Bash)</div>
<div>Setup, Configure, Troubleshoot Linux (Networking, VLAN, Network Bridge)</div>
<div></div>
<div><b>Skills &amp; Requirements</b></div>
<div>5+ years of Network deployment and administration experience</div>
<div>2+ years of solid experience as DevOps Engineer and scripting in Bash, Python and / or Perl</div>
<div>2+ years of networking experience (LAN,VLAN,TCP/IP, Layer 2, Layer 3)</div>
<div>Have solid practical skills with Linux, Git and Configuration Frameworks like Puppet or SaltStack</div>
<div>Strong ability to troubleshoot complex issues ranging from system resources to application stack traces</div>
<div>Comfortable with Agile methodologies and working closely within small teams</div>
<div>Self-motivated / self-starter</div>
<div>Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science or related degree, or equivalent industry experience</div>
