<h2>Digital Analyst</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">As a Digital Analyst for Graybar in St. Louis, MO, you will work with digital business teams, operations teams, e-commerce developers and Graybar customers to optimize digital solutions that support Graybar customers and are aligned with business strategies. You will produce metrics, dashboards, and deliver cross-channel performance reports with actionable insights.
<br><br>
<b>In this role you will:</b><br>
Drive the optimization of overall digital customer experience through daily analysis of web analytics and Key Performance Indicators (KPIs).
<br>Create analytics, KPI reports, and dashboards to be used by operations, merchandising and digital marketing teams.
<br>Assist in improving effectiveness and efficiency of digital operations processes and procedures by performing consistent data, inventory, pricing and revenue analysis.
<br>Ensure data accuracy by investigating data anomalies to determine if there are any technical concerns.
<br>Collaborate with digital business, information technology and development teams to address any digital or omnichannel disputes and ensure correction.
<br>Collaborate with business teams and customers to understand any user experience optimizations, data needs or platform enhancements.
<br><br>
<b>What you bring to the table:</b><br>
1 year of relevant experience in data analytics
<br><b>Bachelor's degree required:</b> math, business, accounting, marketing or other closely aligned field
<br>Experience working with Alteryx, Tableau, Qlik, IBM Digital Analytics or Google Analytics and ForeSee
<br>Basic technical understanding of web basics, including Hyper Text Markup Language (HTML) and Uniform Resource Locator (URL) structures
<br>Advanced level Excel skills including creation of pivot tables
<br>Proven ability to make sound decisions based on a mix of analysis, experience and judgment
<br>Ability to work effectively with people across all levels of the organization
<br>Ability to statistically describe and analyze business financial, sales, and operating results
