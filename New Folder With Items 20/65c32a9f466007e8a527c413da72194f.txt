<h2>Learning and Development Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Job Title: </b>Learning &amp; Development Manager</p>
<p><b>Reports to: </b>Director of HR</p>
<p><b>Job Class: </b> Exempt</p>
<p><b>Position Summary: </b></p>
<p>The successful candidate will be responsible for creating, implementing, and administering online training and webinars with supporting training materials. This person will determine training needs and requirements for each group by collaborating with management and employees; create, develop and oversee training program design including online training, printed manuals, group sessions, training videos and existing program enhancements; manage job code curriculum and work with management to review and update as needed; schedule training sessions, organize information technology and other equipment; and manage course enrollment through the LMS Master Control.</p>
<p><b>Duties and Responsibilities: </b></p>
<p>Responsibilities include, but are not limited to:</p>
<ul>
<li>Design and deploy online training</li>
<li>Directly manage corporate training staff</li>
<li>Design in-person curriculum for corporate training staff to administer in the retail setting</li>
<li>Work with compliance department to keep all curriculum up to date with current regulations</li>
<li>Manage the company’s Learning Management System (LMS)</li>
<li>Report, analyze and disseminate findings of employee participation data in LMS</li>
<li>Administer and troubleshoot live webinar training</li>
<li>Research and evaluate new technologies and industry trends relating to training and development</li>
<li>Update education programs, handbooks, and materials annually to reflect changes in the industry and compliance environment</li>
<li>Develop digital and print materials for training and education seminars</li>
<li>Oversee participant registrations and manage billing for training</li>
<li>Facilitate website product sales through promotion efforts, order fulfillment, shipping, and billing</li>
<li>Perform administrative duties, including maintaining department mailing list; additional administrative duties as needed</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Qualifications: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Must possess excellent knowledge of general compliance and training requirements.</li>
<li>Minimum of 5-7 years of training experience, preferably within a retail or cannabis environment.</li>
<li>Knowledge and experience with ERP Systems a plus.</li>
<li>Knowledge and experience with MS Excel, Word, and Outlook required.</li>
<li>Learning Management System (LMS) experience required.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Skills and Attitudes: </b></p>
<p>Must be motivated, hard-working, and passionate. This position requires someone who is a strong leader with the ability to handle multiple tasks and responsibilities.</p>
<ul>
<li>Must have ability to:</li>
<li>Perform all job functions with attention to detail, speed and accuracy;</li>
<li>Prioritize and organize;</li>
<li>Be a clear thinker, remain calm, and resolve problems using good judgment; and</li>
<li>Lead by example and ensure that team executes the game plan laid out for them.</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Excellent organizational and time-management skills</li>
<li>Excellent communication skills, both verbal and written</li>
<li>Attention to detail and accuracy of work</li>
<li>High integrity and openness combined with commitment to continuous improvement</li>
<li>Ability to plan, organize, and coordinate activities across multiple locations and properties simultaneously</li>
<li>Ability to work under minimal supervision</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Education: </b></p>
<p>Must have one or more of the following:</p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree (B.A.) from a four-year college or university</li>
<li>Four years’ relevant experience and/or training</li>
<li>Equivalent combination of education and experience</li>
</ul>
