<p>Clearlink is hiring a Vice President of Business Development to spearhead our business growth strategy. In this role, you will lead the research and evaluation of partnerships and acquisitions, and build the relationships that make these transformative business decisions possible. You’ll leverage analysis and acumen to establish aggressive expansion into new verticals, and deliver Clearlink’s differentiated suite of services to new partners.</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Breaking Down Your Day – to – Day</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Research market data to provide meaningful, accurate, and informative data analysis for business development efforts</li>
<li>Create high value business proposals that engage a variety of industries based on Clearlink’s suite of services</li>
<li>Build relationships with future Clearlink clients through a deep understanding of their business objectives</li>
<li>Foster an active pipeline of forecast sales and accurate, detailed reporting</li>
<li>Identify and develop relationships with key decision makers in prospect organizations</li>
<li>Collaborate with Clearlink executives to to set revenue goals and continuous industry expansion</li>
</ul>
<p><b>What You Need</b></p>
<ul>
<li>5+ years of enterprise level sales experience</li>
<li>Demonstrated presentation, reporting, and CRM skills</li>
<li>Stellar written and verbal communication skills, especially when connecting with the executive level internally and externally</li>
<li>Ruthless personal accountability and focus on continuous improvement</li>
<li>Excellent self-management and intrinsic motivation</li>
<li>Ability to thrive in a fast-paced, ever evolving professional environment</li>
<li>Complex decision making and data analysis experience</li>
<li>Strong technical knowledge and IT fluency</li>
<li>Command of marketing services and call center industry solutions preferred</li>
<li>Digital marketing experience preferred</li>
</ul>
