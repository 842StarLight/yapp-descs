What You’ll Get to Do:</b></p>

Deliver Hardware and Software technical presales demonstrations with potential clients and partners onsite to decision makers</li>
<li>
Work alongside the sales team in the East Coast region to develop training opportunities</li>
<li>
Act a product expert, conduct customer software and hardware trainings via web based sessions</li>

Deliver onsite technical training courses for integrator groups on the Panasonic Surveillance products</li>

Develop an understanding of all PSSNA products and maintain that understanding for training and demo purposes.</li>
