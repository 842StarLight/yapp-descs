<h2>Director of Technical Operations (Electronic Security Solutions) - Sterling, VA</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">The Director of Technical Operations, Electronic Security Solutions provides management and leadership of personnel and process across the ESS Technical Operations Group.
<br><br>
What part will you play?
<br><br>
The ultimate responsibility of the position is to provide management of all personnel within the group while achieving positive P&amp;L results. This individual will report directly to the Senior Director of Technical Operations.
<br><br>
What will you be doing?
<br><br>
The job responsibilities of the Director of Technical Operations include the following:
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Manages Cross Regional/Cross Divisional work as it comes into ESS.</li>
<li>Collaborates with other Directors of Technical Operations throughout the territory and organization.</li>
<li>Management of the entire Project Management and Engineering teams including, Hiring/Firing, Mentoring and monitoring performance.</li>
<li>Provide support, direction and leadership for the entire team.</li>
<li>Manages entire department to meet budgeted yearly financial goals.</li>
<li>Provides sales support to ESS and Cross Divisional sales as required.</li>
<li>Manages and ensures projects are completed within the established profit targets.</li>
<li>Coordinates, assigns, and prioritizes the activities of all departmental personnel.</li>
<li>Coordinates activities with Service to ensure all systems requiring support are successfully concluded.</li>
<li>Maintains and measures employee’s performance against performance and productivity standards.</li>
<li>Maintains systems of quality control to ensure technical standards are maintained.</li>
<li>Approves timesheets and expense reports after reviewing for accuracy.</li>
<li>Ensures training objectives are meet for all direct and in-direct reports.</li>
<li>Holds regular meetings with the internal team to improve process by setting standards that create efficiency and profitability. This includes but not limited to monthly and weekly department meetings and weekly project meetings</li>
<li>Visit jobsites to inspect workmanship and meet with clients to ensure client satisfaction is being met.</li>
<li>Maintain a master schedule of all project timelines and assigned resources</li>
<li>Participate in internal project meetings to ensure proper hand off from Sales to PM and project team</li>
<li>Maintain a master project status spreadsheet that includes financial standing and forecasted profits.</li>
<li>Perform ongoing project financial reviews with project managers</li>
</ul>
What do we require from you?
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>5+ years of electronic security experience required.</li>
<li>Bachelor's degree in business or finance or equivalent work experience</li>
<li>Ability to manage and mentor personnel to meet department goals and financial budget</li>
<li>Ability to generate and maintain profit and loss statement and work in progress schedule</li>
<li>Ability to trouble shoot and solve technical, personnel or other project related issues.</li>
<li>Proficiency with Microsoft Office, Microsoft project, Adobe Acrobat, and AutoCAD viewing or</li>
<li>markup utilities.</li>
<li>Ability to generate timely, concise, and effective business correspondence.</li>
<li>Ability to interact with employees, customers and colleagues in a professional and courteous manner.</li>
<li>Effective time management and attention to detail.</li>
</ul>
