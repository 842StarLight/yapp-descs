<p>ABOUT OUR COMPANY<br>
Mesa Laboratories, Inc. (Mesa) is a global technology innovator committed to solving some of the most critical
</p>
<p>quality control and analytical challenges in the pharmaceutical, healthcare, industrial safety, environmental, food
</p>
<p>and beverage industries. Mesa offers products and services through four divisions (Sterilization and Disinfection
</p>
<p>Control, Instruments, Cold Chain Monitoring and Cold Chain Packaging), which help our customers ensure product
</p>
<p>integrity, increase patient and worker safety, and improve quality of life.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b><i>Today, each of Mesa’s products and services play a role in ensuring the safety and efficacy of the products we
</i></b></p>
<p><b><i>use every day. No matter if it’s the environment, products or people, Mesa is committed to
</i></b></p>
<p><b><i>Protecting the VulnerableTM.
</i></b></p>
<p></p>
<p>Founded in 1982, Mesa went public in 1984. After 10 years of more than 15% growth per year, the company now
</p>
<p>has revenue approaching $100M and a market cap of approximately $750M. Our growth is attributed to the 350+
</p>
<p>dedicated employees who follow our customer-focused, lean-based value system, called <i>The Mesa Way!</i>. The
</p>
<p>Mesa Way! is based on four pillars that drive our work every day:
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Measure what Matters</b>: We take the customer’s perspective, our “True North”, both to measure what matters
</p>
<p>most to customers and to set absolute standards for performance. We manage to leading indicators, which drive
</p>
<p>us to proactively avoid problems.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Empower Teams</b>: We move decision making as close to the customer as possible and provide the structure and
</p>
<p>real time communication forum to align the whole organization behind surpassing customer expectations.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Steadily Improve</b>: We leverage a common and proven set of lean-based tools to prioritize our biggest
</p>
<p>opportunities, address those opportunities at root cause, and quickly implement change.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Always Learn</b>: We ensure that improvements are sustained, enabling us to raise performance expectations and
</p>
<p>repeat the cycle of improvement. Equally, this cycle strengthens the Mesa team by providing endless learning
</p>
<p>opportunities for our employees and helps us to become an employer of choice in our communities.
</p>
<p></p>
<p>Mesa Labs is located in the Denver area. Learn on what Denver has to offer here.
</p>

<p><b>Essential Duties </b>Core duties and responsibilities include, but are not limited to, the following:<br>
• Model personal Lean Philosophy 5S behaviors in creating a continuously improving,
</p>
<p>clean, well-organized and self-maintaining work environment. Work closely with<br>
team members and others to motivate and improve 5S knowledge and<br>
implementation.
</p>
<ul><li>Follow a continuous improvement approach for team accountability for achieving</li></ul>
high performance SQDCM (Safety, Quality, Delivery, Cost, &amp; Morale) targets<br>
through a visual work environment.
<ul><li>Comply with company ethics, code of conduct, policies, and best practices, with a</li></ul>
commitment to safety in the workplace, valuing of diversity, and promotion of a<br>
harassment-free environment.
<ul><li>Maintain and continuously improve NetSuite OneWorld implementation,</li></ul>
customizations, and associated third party products
<ul><li>Develop, test and deploy customizations, custom objects, third party</li></ul>
tools/applications and new NetSuite functionality based on evolving business needs
<ul><li>Design, implement, and maintain configurations and customizations of NetSuite to</li></ul>
meet evolving needs
<ul><li>Design, implement, and maintain 3rd party software connections to NetSuite to</li></ul>
support various business needs
<ul><li>Support end user requests for new saved searches, reports, KPI’s, and dashboards,</li></ul>
and maintain proper system access for user roles
<ul><li>Provide internal, ongoing system/technical support to NetSuite users, including</li></ul>
training
<ul><li>Monitor end-user usage of systems and track performance
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Provide front line support with regards to all aspects of NetSuite and data integrity
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Develop, document and implement policies, procedures and guidelines to ensure</li></ul>
data integrity, change protocols, customization, usage etc.
<ul><li>Identify, evaluate and recommend other key technologies required to support and</li></ul>
improve the business process centered on the NetSuite platform
<ul><li>Responsible for the change management and release process of NetSuite
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Provide a quality and timely communication regarding system status
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Maintain up-to-date knowledge of NetSuite functionality, customization and</li></ul>

<ul><li>Perform other duties as assigned by management.<b>
