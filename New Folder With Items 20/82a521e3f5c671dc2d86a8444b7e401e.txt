<h2>VP, Division Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<div>
<p>Jobs Description</p>
</div>
<p>Position Summary:</p>
<p>Manage mortgage division and supervise personnel and their workflow, ensuring high morale, excellent customer service and maximum volume and profits.
</p>
<p>Primary Duties and Responsibilities:</p>
<div><ul>
<li>Manage and develop branch staff.</li>
<li>
Manage day-to-day operations including originating, FHA/VA/CONV loans following Eagle roadmap.</li>
<li>
Develop branch business.</li>
<li>
Actively participate in company’s strategic planning. Set goals and objectives which are measurable, achievable and revolve around our customer service standards.</li>
<li>
Interview, test and hire new branch associates.</li>
<li>
Conduct new hire orientation, performance planning and reviews, disciplinary actions and terminations.</li>
<li>
Provide coaching and promote a professional team effort.</li>
<li>
Provide support for problem solving. Create an interface with lenders, division escrow coordinators, design centers and sales agents and disperse necessary information to staff.</li>
<li>
Handle issues with borrowers, sales consultants and team members.</li>
<li>
Function as liaison between Homebuilder and Mortgage Division.</li>
<li>
Lead and direct processing and Home Loan Advisors in the implementation of new programs and systems. Delegate and review workflow.</li>
<li>
Oversee and facilitate the origination of mortgage loan transactions for Division's homebuyers.</li>
<li>
Perform pre-qualifications on prospective homebuyers.</li>
<li>
Take mortgage loan applications for homebuyers that have signed a purchase contract.</li>
<li>
Communicate loan status updates to all necessary parties.</li>
<li>
Maintain daily and monthly logs and reports for Mortgage Division. Provide monthly reporting to mortgage division’s corporate office.</li>
<li>
Work with Mortgage Division in monitoring lender programs and requirements.</li>
<li>
Communicate with Senior Management on training needs and issues.</li>
<li>
Attend homebuilding division sales meetings.</li>
<li>
Represent Mortgage Division at builder grand openings and preview as requested.</li>
<li>
Attend status meetings and special functions.</li>
<li>
Responsible for regulatory compliance and quality control.</li>
<li>
Provide training to sales associates in residential lending. Be available to assist Home Loan Advisors with training, sales meetings and openings.</li>
</ul></div>
<p>Education and Experience Requirements:</p>
<div><ul>
<li>Minimum 1-3 years as a branch manager</li>
<li>
Minimum 4 - 5 years of application and processing experience or equivalent mortgage background</li>
<li>
Strong computer background with 4 to 5 years experience in mortgage software</li>
<li>
Current knowledge of closing and title requirements</li>
<li>
Current knowledge of loan processing and automated underwriting systems</li>
<li>
Current knowledge of FHLMC/FNMA/FHA/VA and industry underwriting guidelines</li>
<li>
Current knowledge of self employed borrowers, partnership and corporation tax return analysis</li>
<li>
Strong computer background with 2 to 3 years experience in mortgage software and working knowledge of Word for Windows and Excel</li>
<li>
Strong mortgage operations background</li>
<li>
Valid driver's license and transportation</li>
</ul></div>
<p>Contacts:</p>
<p>Regular interaction with homebuilding sales associates, prospective home buyers, lender representatives and underwriters, escrow and title personnel, credit bureaus and appraisers. Also deals with homebuilding division management as well as mortgage division staff.</p>
<p>Additional Requirements:</p>
<div><ul>
<li>Must have professional attitude and represent company in a positive manner</li>
<li>
Provide outstanding customer service, work well under pressure and be a team player</li>
<li>
Must have excellent oral and written communication skills</li>
<li>
Must have excellent team-building and motivational abilities</li>
<li>
Must have excellent organization and PC skills</li>
<li>
Understand and follow posted work rules and procedures</li>
<li>
Accept constructive criticism</li>
<li>
PHYSICAL REQUIREMENTS:</li>
<li>
Requires the ability to operate a personal computer, fax machine, copier, climb stairs, bend, stoop, reach, lift, move, and carry materials and supplies weighing 20 pounds or less. Finger dexterity is required to operate a computer keyboard.</li>
</ul></div>
