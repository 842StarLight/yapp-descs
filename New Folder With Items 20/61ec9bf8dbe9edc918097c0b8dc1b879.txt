<h2>Product Marketing Manager, Brand Advertising</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">Description
<br><br>
Roku is seeking a technical Product Marketing Manager (PMM) to support its growing suite of advertising products for Television marketers (Agencies, Brands). The PMM will develop and implement go-to-market strategies and define the positioning and messaging for our product portfolio and data-driven solutions within the Roku Ad Platform.
<br><br>
The ideal candidate for this role will be a smart, gritty self-starter who can partner cross-functionally to drive innovation, solicit and implement feedback to establish clear go-to-market and data strategies. The successful candidate will display a balance of hard and soft skills, including the ability to respond quickly to changing business needs, possess a strong understanding of the linear, OTT and digital video across both the supply and demand sides of the advertising technology ecosystem.
<br><br>
The Role and Responsibilities
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Develop Go-To-Market launch plans including positioning, messaging, packaging reportable metrics, KPIs and pricing</li>
<li>Deeply understand the market trends, the OTT/Linear TV ecosystems, and competition to inform product development strategy</li>
<li>Collaborate with Engineering, Data Science, Operations, Sales, Sales Development as well as external Marketers and Customers</li>
<li>Together with the Product team, educate and train internal and external customers and prospects on our product features and their benefits</li>
<li>Drive feature prioritization, process improvements, and product adoption with an eye on key KPIs</li>
</ul>
<b>Required Experience/Qualifications:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>7-10 years of experience in advertising technology, product management and/or technical marketing</li>
<li>Strong analytical skills and experience with data driven product design and decision making</li>
<li>Strong data base querying skills and data visualization (i.e. SQL, Tableau, Looker)</li>
<li>Data-driven and an entrepreneurial self-starter who deals well with ambiguity</li>
<li>Ability to distill complex technologies and product features into succinct value propositions</li>
<li>Strong technical knowledge of the programmatic advertising ecosystem and an ability to influence engineering decisions</li>
<li>Understand Roku's competitive position in the advertising industry</li>
<li>B.S. or M.S. in Computer Science or Engineering preferred, MBA a plus</li>
<li>Tenured experience in programmatic ad technologies and protocols specific to OTT video and cross-device ad formats</li>
<li>Located in New York City</li>
</ul>
