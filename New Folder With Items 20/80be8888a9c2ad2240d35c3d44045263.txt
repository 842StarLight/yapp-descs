<p><b>SUMMARY</b><br>
SMG, the leader in privately managed public assembly facilities, has an excellent and immediate opening for a Staff Accountant. The Staff Account will perform the daily activities of the accounting function at the facility and as the Accounting Manager or Finance Director by performing the following duties:</p>
<p><b>
ESSENTIAL DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES </b>include the following. Other duties may be assigned.</p>
<ul>
<li>Assists Accounting Manager in preparation of financial statements.</li>
<li>Enters all A/P and A/R into the accounting software and reconciles the sub ledgers to the A/R and A/P control accounts.</li>
<li>Performs a daily reconciliation of all cash receipts and disbursements.</li>
<li>Performs monthly closing of sub ledgers.</li>
<li>Performs regular audits of Change Funds and Physical Inventory.</li>
<li>Inputs journal entries.</li>
<li>Calculates Cost of Goods Sold for the Food and Beverage Department.</li>
<li>Prepares and submits cost allocations.</li>
<li>Performs account analysis on general ledger accounts.</li>
<li>Performs any other functions delegated by accounting manager.</li>
<li>Maintains and audits box office ticket sales reports, and reconciles them to the proper liability accounts.</li>
<li>Maintains and audits parking sales.</li>
<br>
</ul>
