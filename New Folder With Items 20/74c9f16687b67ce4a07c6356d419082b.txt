<h2>Senior Salesforce Admin/Developer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">--------
<br>Overview
<br>--------
<br><br>
A Salesforce Administrator at DiscoverOrg is responsible for ensuring maximum efficiency and capitalization on full features and benefits of the Salesforce system deployed to various user groups.
<br><br>
----------------
<br>Responsibilities
<br>----------------
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Work with sales and marketing leadership to develop and implement complex solutions to business problems</li>
<li>Develop workflows, process builders, custom objects, validation rule and other advanced functionality with SalesForce</li>
<li>Be able to take complex requirements and find novel solutions to implement them with limited guidance</li>
<li>Understanding of the SAAS Model and how it is implemented and executed in SalesforceHigh proficiency in performing mass uploads and updates of data as required</li>
<li>Create and maintain reports and dashboards</li>
<li>Proactively seek out and identify issues and improvements</li>
<li>Proactively gather feedback from users</li>
<li>Communicate system changes to the users in advance so they understand the change and how to use it prior to implementation</li>
<li>Manage all processes that impact / relate to Salesforce.com</li>
<li>Provide sales and financial data to company executives.</li>
<li>Design, Create and maintain user roles, profiles and hierarchies.</li>
<li>Create and administer training to existing or new users/groups.</li>
</ul>
----------------------
<br>Minimum Qualifications
<br>----------------------
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>3 years of Salesforce administration experience</li>
<li>Experience in Sales Reporting and analysis</li>
<li>Previous experience with reporting, workflows and enhanced functions</li>
<li>Hard working. We run fast.</li>
<li>Ability to work independently, as well as part of a team</li>
<li>Bachelors degree in Business, Economics, Computer Science OR equivalent education/experience</li>
<li>Strong problem-solving skills, data and software applications.</li>
</ul>
------------------------
<br>Preferred Qualifications
<br>------------------------
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Experience with CPQ for Salesforce</li>
<li>Experience with DocusSign</li>
<li>Experience with SalesForce Einstein</li>
<li>Experience with Outreach</li>
<li>Experience working with international teams</li>
<li>Experience with Data Science and Statistics</li>
</ul>
------------------
<br>Abilities Required
<br>------------------
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Demonstrated ability to learn new technologies quickly and independently</li>
<li>Strong technical, organizational and interpersonal skills</li>
<li>Strong written and verbal communication skills</li>
</ul>
