<p><br>
<b>Job Summary:</b></p>
<ul><li> Full and ultimate responsibility for business development, growth, planning and sales performance of the Home Improvement business.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Responsible for all aspects of sales management.
</li></ul>
<ul><li> Develop budget and manage financial performance to achieve sales and P&amp;L objectives.
</li></ul>
<ul><li> Provide leadership. Develop the sales team, recognizing the good and poor performers. Mentor and coach those who exhibit the basic qualifications and encourage the drive and spirit to achieve maximum potential in themselves and in their team. Encourage team to set personnel stretch goals for their business and career and help guide them to meet these goals.
</li></ul>
<ul><li> Instill the values, commitment and culture of the company.
</li></ul>
<ul><li> Drive process improvements to improve sales effectiveness, efficiency and productivity.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Develop short and long term goals by business line and sales representative.<br>
</li></ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Primary Duties and Responsibilities: (Essential Job Function)</b></p>
<ul><li> Directly oversee, manage and drive growth and P&amp;L performance of the HI sales force.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Coordinate and communicate closely with the GM E&amp;CC Sales.
</li></ul>
<ul><li> Development and management of the sales process and value proposition.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Develop and manage prospecting and acquisition of new business.
</li></ul>
<ul><li> Understand the competitive environment and modify approach to the customer’s needs while working in conjunction with marketing, service and installation to grow the home improvement business.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Continuously review the business, provide a fresh outlook on the day-to-day working operations as well as the current direction of the business lines.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Prioritize, organize and handle details in the day-to-day activities and manage multiple projects, tasks and business lines simultaneously.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Establish and enforce team spirit with-in the team, to work with all departments in order to meet the company’s overall objectives.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Establish oneself as a leader, who lives the values and sets the standard for others to follow.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Produce and submit accurate reports and updates monthly as requested.</li></ul>
<ul><li> Provide continuous and timely feedback to upper management, keeping them abreast of day-to-day issues that are important to the business, customer or the employee. Provide a level of information that allows upper management a grass root feel from all perspectives.<br>
</li></ul>
