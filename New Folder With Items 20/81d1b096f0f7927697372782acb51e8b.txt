<div>
Interested in becoming a Senior Manager of Tax Accounting at Aaron's? The Senior Tax Accounting Manager is responsible for managing the tax accounting and related financial reporting/internal control processes. The ideal candidate will apply knowledge of ASC 740 Income Taxes, U.S. GAAP, and applicable tax laws and regulations to ensure that the Company's financial statements are correct with respect to taxes. This role will also assist with income tax compliance and audit defense. The duties entail preparation and review of financial reporting disclosures related to taxes in the Company’s financial filings for accuracy and completeness. This role will also coordinate the identification, tracking, and reporting of uncertain tax positions under ASC740-10 as well as implement tax processes and controls with the objective of achieving improved accuracy and operational efficiencies.</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div><b>
Duties and Responsibilities:</b></div>
<div>
<ul>
<li>
Manage internal audit procedures and controls related to tax accounting including. meeting with audit team members and resolving outstanding audit issues.</li>
<li>
Assist with company tax compliance efforts with outside provider to meet filing deadlines.</li>
<li>
Manage external audit defense as needed.</li>
<li>
Prepare and review forecasts of income tax expense and cash taxes to support internal and external reporting functions.</li>
<li>
Lead tax process improvement initiatives including automation efforts to enhance process effectiveness and efficiencies.</li>
</ul>
<div><b>
Required Skills and Competencies:
</b></div>
<div>
<ul>
<li>Accounting knowledge and ability to apply GAAP.</li>
<li>
Technical expertise in federal and state income tax.
</li>
<li>Strong interpersonal skills, including the ability to communicate clearly and effectively with peers and all levels of management.</li>
<li>
Strong mathematical and data analytical skills required, including use of spreadsheets, ERP systems, and tax research &amp; compliance software.</li>
<li>
Negotiation skills.</li>
<li>
Technical writing skills using multiple visual elements.</li>
<li>
Able to develop corrective action plans.</li>
<li>
Identify conflicts and able to provide feedback and recommendations.</li>
<li>
Microsoft Office Suite proficiency.
</li>
</ul>
<b>Required Education, Experience and Certifications:</b>
</div>
<div>
<ul>
<li>
Bachelor’s degree in Accounting or Finance.</li>
<li>
Master’s degree in Taxation or Accounting preferred.</li>
<li>
Certified Public Accountant (CPA) preferred.</li>
<li>
Must have at least 5 to 9 years of experience working with ASC 740.</li>
</ul>
