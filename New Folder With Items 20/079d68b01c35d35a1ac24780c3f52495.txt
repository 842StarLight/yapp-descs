<h2>Bookkeeper (3 month - part-time, temporary position)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<b>Description:</b><br>
<b>Goal:</b><br>
The goal of the Accounting Representative is to ensure the smooth running of Comforts accounting department in partnership with the CFO. This can include, but is not limited to accounting processes, such as AP/AR and other bookkeeping tasks.
<br><br>
<b>Our Aim:</b><br>
To encourage creativity and growth for our employees and providing the proper tools and training for our staff in a safe and supportive environment.
<br><br>
<b>Who You Will Work With:</b><br>
The Accounting Representative will work closely with the heads of each department, as well as the employees and the owner. This position reports to, and takes day-to-day direction from, the CFO.
<br><br>
<b>Essential Duties and Responsibilities:</b><br>
The essential functions include, but are not limited to the following:
<br><br>
<b>A/P &amp; A/R:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Pull invoices (when requested)</li>
<li>Check run</li>
<li>Bill Pay</li>
<li>Net 15s</li>
<li>PG&amp;E AutoPay</li>
<li>Rent &amp; Storage payments</li>
<li>Donations (when requested)</li>
</ul>
<b>Bookkeeping:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Worker’s comp payments - through Hartford</li>
<li>Sales tax, W-2’s, 1099’s, etc.</li>
<li>Insurance policy renewals</li>
<li>Recording manual checks (e.g. payroll &amp; operating)</li>
<li>Recording Auto &amp; Bill Pays</li>
<li>Credit cards reconciliation</li>
<li>Entering in interest into QuickBooks, etc.</li>
<li>Bank reconciliations</li>
<li>Managing the office assistant to (1-2 days/week):</li>
<li>Enter invoices</li>
<li>Admin misc. (e.g. birthday lists, email, filing, etc.)</li>
<li>Daily deposits.</li>
</ul>
<b>Requirements:</b><br>
A four-year degree in hospitality, business administration, or a related field is preferred. Professional certifications can also be helpful but not required. To be successful in this type of position, having previous work experience in all areas of a restaurant is helpful. Strong analytical and problem-solving skills are essential.
<br><br>
Must be self-directive, demonstrating common sense and negotiation skills. The ability to handle stress well and to confront a variety of problems throughout the day.
<br><br>
Required to be familiar with various computer software packages, including Microsoft Word, PowerPoint, Outlook and Excel, Google Business software suite, QuickBooks and Toast POS System. Strong written communication skills and be able to interact with many different individuals in a professional manner.</div>
