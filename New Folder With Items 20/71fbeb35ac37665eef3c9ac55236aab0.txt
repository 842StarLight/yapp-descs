<b>Salesforce.com System Analyst/Administrator</b><br>
<br>
This is a technical position that will be dedicated to the support of our In-House Brokerage group where rapid growth is driving an expansion of its sales team and the need for an experienced Salesforce.com (SFDC) Systems Analyst/Administrator. The successful candidate will need to work collaboratively with the Business and Technical team to drive implementation and automation of processes and workflow. Candidates must possess the ability to understand business requirements and processes and build workable solutions in SFDC. This includes technical design, internal/external systems integration, configuration, maintenance, administration, and data management.<br>
<br>
Candidates must be able to work directly with the system/data, work collaboratively on multiple technical projects or portions at one time, as well as provide solutions that are highly usable, scalable, extensible, and maintainable on the Force.com platform. They will be passionate about their work, self-motivated, creative, detail oriented and have excellent problem solving abilities. They will deal with ambiguous problems, taking responsibility for finding solutions, guide and assist the user group on the development, and deployment of related software critical to business operations.<br>
<br>
The ideal candidate for this role is a strategic thinker, comfortable with sharing and accepting ideas. You have a passion to deliver solid process and technology solutions and to fulfill critical business objectives. You enjoy working in a fast-paced, self-directed, diverse, and customer service-oriented environment. This position will report directly to the Salesforce Program Manager.<br>
<br>
<b>Key Responsibilities</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Seek out ways to improve processes, productivity and capability of the Salesforce.com environment in a fast passed rapidly expanding environment.</li>
<li>
Works closely with internal IT team on system integration requirements between internal/proprietary legacy systems and Salesforce, as well as system integration requirements with external vendor systems and data.</li>
<li>
Works closely with business unit managers to form effective alliances on projects, operational decisions, scheduling requirements, conflicts, and vendor contract clarification.</li>
<li>
Support execution of our marketing strategy and roadmap to support long-term and short-term business requirements</li>
<li>
Design solutions that leverage existing system investments and provide scalability to support company's future growth.</li>
<li>
Proactively identify and implement operational improvements, enhancements, and system customizations that meet business requirements.</li>
<li>
Define, communicate and execute a data quality process to manage data health including standardization, completeness, and validity that drive key business decisions.</li>
<li>
Participate in the research on new and emerging technologies that can be leveraged through existing CRM investments.</li>
</ul>
<br>
