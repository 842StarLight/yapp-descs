<h2>HITSS III Contract - IT Security Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><p>Job Type Full Time Responsibilities Monitors, evaluates, and maintains systems and procedures to protect data systems, databases, and network systems from unauthorized users.<br>
Identifies potential threats and responds to reported security violations.<br>
Determines causes of security violations and recommends corrective actions.<br>
Researches, recommends, and implements changes to procedures and systems to enhance security.<br>
Assists in communicating security procedures to users.<br>
Establishes and satisfies system- wide information security requirements based upon the analysis of user, policy, regulatory, and resource demands.<br>
Applies expertise to Government and commercial common user systems, as well as to dedicated special purpose systems requiring specialized security features and procedures.<br>
Examples could include classified intelligence and command and control-related networks.<br>
Requirements Education:<br>
BS/ BA or Equivalent</p></div>
