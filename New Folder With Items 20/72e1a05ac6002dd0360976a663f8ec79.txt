<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Job Description</b></h2>
<div><div>
<div>
<p>Through comprehensive wealth planning and holistic investment management, the Wealth Advisor is responsible for developing and growing client relationships involving affluent individuals and their families. This individual should have the ability to effectively work within a full wealth team when servicing clients in order to meet their total financial objectives and provide an exceptional client experience. The Wealth Advisor will also be required to have a strong grasp of Wealth Management strategies, products, and services to identify opportunities and coordinate meetings with subject matter experts. This individual will need to utilize internal and external networks to closely team with Bank Partners to enhance account relationships, identify referral opportunities, retain existing business, and promote Bremer's image and foster client and community relations.
</p>
<p>This role will also be responsible for day to day administration of individual and fiduciary accounts, processing of account-related administrative activities in an accurate and timely manner, ensuring that investment strategies are fully executed, and maintaining compliance with trust documents.</p>
<p><b>
 Qualifications</b></p>
<p>
A combination of education and experience which enables the incumbent to be successful is generally acquired through:</p>
<ul>
<li><p>The attainment of a four year degree in finance, accounting, or other related field.</p></li>
<li><p>MBA, CTFA, CPA, or JD is a plus.</p></li>
<li><p>At least five (5) years of financial services sales experience.</p></li>
<li><p>Excellent interpersonal, sales, and communication skills.</p></li>
<li><p>Demonstrated ability to solve complex problems that involve several concrete and abstract variables applied to both standardized and unique situations.</p></li>
<li><p>Ability to evaluate financial needs of current and prospective customers in order to ascertain potential financial service needs.</p></li>
<li><p>Demonstrated ability to effectively present information and respond to questions from groups of managers, clients, and customers.</p></li>
<li><p>Ability to respond to common inquiries or concerns from customers.</p></li>
<li><p>Demonstrated ability to interact constructively and effectively with all constituencies.</p></li>
<li><p>Operate independently in sometimes ambiguous circumstances and conditions.</p></li>
</ul>

<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Shared Expectations</b></h2>
<div><div>
<div>
<p><b>Personal Leadership:</b> Model Bremer values in all interactions; communicate clearly, honestly and with authenticity; identify and respect style differences in others and be willing to adapt; read and respond appropriately to individuals and/or situations; enhance and maintain robust relationships within and outside the organization; be accountable and demonstrate credibility and follow through on commitments; and build self-awareness by continually seeking feedback from others.</p>
<p><b>
Thought Leadership:</b> Use holistic approach when problem solving and decision making; proactively seek diverse perspectives to develop synergetic solutions; promote engagement and empowerment; translate complex concepts and challenges into tasks and activities that are quick and easy to achieve; use authority appropriately and acknowledge the contributions of others.</p>
<p><b>
Team Leadership:</b> Lead strategically with rigor around communicating the what and why behind decisions; demonstrate transparency, honesty and courage to earn a reputation as an authentic leader; communicate a vision and engage team in achieving that vision; drive dedication to serve and promote the client intimacy model; clearly communicate and hold employees to performance expectations providing clear and timely feedback and holding all employees accountable; build high performing teams through engagement and accountability; delegate and draw on others knowledge and expertise; commit to coaching and developing team and next generation of leaders by taking an enterprise point of view.</p>
<p><b>
Results Leadership:</b> Prioritize strategically by organizing resources, time and people to achieve key goals and objectives; act with a sense of urgency, proactively address critical concerns, work quickly to get things done; lead change effectively; demonstrate knowledge skill and abilities while demonstrating operational excellence; deliver consistent results.</p>
