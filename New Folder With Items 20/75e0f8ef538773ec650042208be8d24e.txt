Are you motivated by leading conversations, driving your own schedule, getting things over the finish line and have a competitive desire to win?<br>
<br>
Valpak provides integrated print and digital advertising solutions led by our iconic “Blue Envelope” as well as mobile and online platforms. Join a culture known for encouragement, collaboration, having fun - <i>and winning! </i>Valpak is at the heart of communities across North America, helping people save, businesses grow, and neighborhoods thrive.<br>
<br>
<b>We Offer:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Base salary, plus uncapped commission</li>
<li>Sales bonuses, contests and incentives</li>
<li>Tools and customer data at your fingertips, including Salesforce.com, leads and prospecting database, tablets, best practice playbooks, a guided sales application and more…</li>
<li>Comprehensive benefits package</li>
</ul>
<br>
<b>
Responsibilities</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Generate new business in assigned area through effective networking, referral generation, and local prospecting, with a demonstrated passion and curiosity about local businesses, for what they do and what they need in order to grow and thrive</li>
<li>Identify key decision makers by face-to-face and phone call prospecting, as well as use of email, LinkedIn and other contemporary methods within a defined territory to grow and nurture</li>
<li>Provide exceptional ongoing account management to your existing clients to ensure their programs are successful, to develop trusted relationships and to renew and upgrade their programs</li>
<li>Work with clients and Valpak designers on high-impact ad design, including effective offers</li>
<li>Be a product expert and develop compelling and effective marketing solutions from Valpak’s robust and market-leading suite of products, including direct mail, Valpak.com, Web sites, SEM and more</li>
<li>Meet and exceed established sales goals - and exceed your own personal earnings goals</li>
<li>Use Salesforce.com to effectively manage your business and participate in Valpak’s customer management ecosystem</li>
<li>Execute required sales administrative functions to initiate orders and accurately publish ad campaigns</li>
<li>Love what you do, have fun and be a positive contributor to your local Valpak team</li>
</ul>
<br>
<b>
Qualifications</b><br>
<ul>
<li>2+ years of proven success in business development and outside sales</li>
<li>Experience in print and digital advertising sales preferred</li>
<li>College degree is preferred, but not required</li>
</ul>
<br>
<b>
Knowledge, Skills and Abilities</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Must be a results-oriented self-starter and have a record of success working in a goal-oriented, highly accountable environment</li>
<li>Strong verbal and written communication and presentation skills, with emphasis on engagement and persuasion</li>
<li>Curiosity and a true passion for talking face-to-face with local businesses, stimulating interest on their end and helping develop effective client solutions.</li>
<li>Well-developed contemporary productivity and technological skills (Internet, Word, Excel, PowerPoint, CRM, Mobile and Social)</li>
<li>Ability to manage deadlines and to operate with a sense of urgency</li>
</ul>
