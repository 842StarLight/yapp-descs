<h2>Software Sales Recruiter</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>The Software Sales Recruiter will be primarily responsible for identifying and hiring Software Enterprise Sales professionals for our global SaaS company.</p>
<p>*Job Responsibilities<br>*</p>
<ul>
<li>Perform detailed job intake sessions with hiring managers in order to construct the ideal candidate profile.</li>
<li>Implement creative and targeted search strategies that help the Recruiting team become more successful in pipelining both active and passive candidates.</li>
<li>Generate interest in passive candidates to move forward in the process and conduct phone screens.</li>
<li>Phone screen candidates across all job disciplines and provide actionable feedback to hiring managers.</li>
<li>Possess an eye for talent for qualifying and engaging candidates for skill, as well as cultural fit.</li>
<li>Ensure candidates are moving through the hiring process in a timely manner.</li>
<li>Facilitate the job requisition approval and job offer approval processes.</li>
<li>Capture and document all search activity in our applicant tracking system and all other relevant data points for various scheduled and ad hoc reports.</li>
<li>Create a positive experience for our hiring managers and candidates, regardless of the outcome.</li>
</ul>
<p>*Skills &amp; Requirements<br>*</p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor's degree</li>
<li>At least 3 years of software/SaaS enterprise sales recruitment experience</li>
<li>Additional agency/search firm recruiting experience desirable</li>
<li>Proficiency with G Suite (Gmail, Docs, Sheets, Hangouts, Drive, etc.) and Microsoft Word, Excel, and PowerPoint</li>
<li>Boolean, LinkedIn, and alternative search technique skills</li>
<li>Excellent interpersonal skills and the ability to manage multiple tasks simultaneously</li>
<li>Ability to work in a fast-paced environment with changing priorities</li>
</ul>
