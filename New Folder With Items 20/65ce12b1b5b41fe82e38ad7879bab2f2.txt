<h2>Senior Manager, Ticketing Solutions, TD Garden</h2>

Please visit www.tdgarden.com to learn more.</p>

The Opportunity<p>
The Manager of Ticketing Solutions will be responsible for leveraging current and future technologies to develop services and solutions that allow for ticketed products to be properly inventoried, accounted for and optimized.</p>
<p>
The qualified candiate will vet, administer and operationalize solutions that integrate guest access throughout all areas of the customer journey including parking, turnstiles, elevators and exclusively ticketed and private membership areas.</p>
<p>
The Manager will support sales, strategy and marketing teams to develop and support ticket-based guest amenities like parking, added value and potentially loyalty.</p>
<p>
The Manager will manage all ticketing technology solutions in an effort to deliver customer based data with ongoing BI efforts.</p>
<p>
The qualified candiate will develop ticketing technology solutions to be operationalized and have a fully integrated service and support plan.</p>
<p>
This is a role that will work collaboratively with IT and Business Solutions teams to administer and deliver departmental goals across the organization.</p>
Responsibilities<ul>
<li><p>
Responsible for administration of Ticketmaster Host and Archtics ticketing systems and online resources (TMOne, AccountManager Tools, AccountManager CMS); Identifies needs and manages systems, maintenance, updates and product enhancement/releases; effectively communicate system updates/changes to appropriate channels as needed.</p></li>
<li><p>
Responsible for all ticketing related hardware and technology assets including, but not limited to, turnstile/suite access control, onsite servers (ZipPark, TM Integration), hosted servers, and current and future Ticketmaster hardware and software (Presence, SalesDeck, KONE).</p></li>
<li><p>
Assume intermediary role for Delaware North Ticketing, Technology, Business Intelligence and Strategy, Operations between Ticketmaster and all third party integrations for matters related to technology, products, APIs, hardware, software, and development needs.</p></li>
<li><p>
Investigate industry leading technologies and explore applicability and implementation at Delaware North, Sportservice, and Boston Bruins with appropriate departments.</p></li>
<li><p>
Collaborate with stakeholders to manage priorities on all technology initiatives.</p></li>
<li><p>
Assist with planning and developing technological solutions that extend across ticketing, CRM, mobile, access management (Boundary Device, KONE), IOMedia, KORE, POS (Retail and F&amp;B), Wi-Fi, Givex, parking (ZipPark, Skidata) and other current and future technologies.</p></li>
<li><p>
Work with departments to identify, research, develop and implement opportunities to expand the Delaware North and Boston Bruins footprint into existing and future opportunities.
</p></li>
<li><p>Direct and support all technological Service License Agreements (SLA’s) for Delaware North and Boston Bruins and between external and third-party vendors; Collaborate and support DNC-Boston HelpDesk/ISET/IT/Control Room on matters related to technology and future data gathering enhancements.
</p></li>
<li><p>Collaborate with all departments to develop innovative ways to execute plans and ensure that goals and procedures are accomplished.</p></li>
<li><p>
Partner with and support Business Solutions team in effectively identifying, researching, and implementing business needs across web, CRM, ticketing, mobile, data warehousing, analytics and reporting (CrystalReports, SAS).</p></li>
<li><p>
Spearhead JIRA knowledgebase for real-time issue reporting and maintain library of present and past issues with their respective outcomes/solutions. Ensure clear communication with applicable stakeholders to explain strategy and track outstanding items requiring resolution.</p></li>
<li>
All other duties as assigned.</li>
</ul>
