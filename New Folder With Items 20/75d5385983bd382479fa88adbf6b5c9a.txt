A day in your life as a Lock Box Specialist:</div>
<div>
Under the direction of the Manager, the Specialist is responsible for the pricing of residential loans, new product programming set-up, testing and coding the loans for sale to Investors or held in Portfolio, and supporting the Mortgage Banking Division’s (MBD’s) daily operations. If applicable or appropriate, assists in business unit’s compliance with applicable laws, regulations, regulatory requirements and Bank policies and procedures, including but not limited to those related to Fair Banking, Anti-Money Laundering laws and regulations, Bank Secrecy Act and USA PATRIOT Act.</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
We’ll give you these important responsibilities…</div>
<div><ul>
<li><div>
Validates and confirms pricing for lock-in requests, project pricing, extensions, off-sheet pricing changes, pricing waivers and all changes to the locked loans. Updates pricing for float loans for disclosure purposes. Works with LOS Vendor on products, testing, pricing, Investor and Bank product and pricing changes.</div></li>
<li><div>
Codes loans for sale to an Investor or held in Portfolio. Uses working knowledge of the Bank’s loan operations, servicing, and LOS system. Inputs trade commitments performed by the trader into the Secondary Marketing Risk Management system. Provides backup and assists with Trader functions, such as preparing the daily pricing export from FNMA, assisting with off-sheet pricing, preparing delivery schedules and weekly reports uploaded in Sharepoint for MBD.</div></li>
<li><div>
Prepares and publishes daily mortgage rate sheets for Retail (SOH, Guam) and Wholesale channels. Updates website mortgage rates for Bank to consumer, boh.com, google and mobile banking pages. Updates WSJ daily Libor index for rate sheets and LOS.</div></li>
<li><div>
Using working knowledge of the Bank’s mortgage products, corresponds with bank loan officers and brokers on pricing requests. Assists LO’s and Brokers, Operations, QC/GFE review, PPS and all Bank personnel with training on locking, set-up, eligibility, LQ system, Encompass and EPPS and pricing validations. Provides backup for QC/System programming for testing loan scenarios for eligibility and price adjustments. Responsible for training Lock Specialist I employees.</div></li>
</ul></div>
