<div><div><div>
<div><b>Who We Are</b></div>
<div>

If this sounds like a team you would like to join, apply today.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div><b>
How You Will Contribute</b></div>
<div>
The <b>Assistant Community Manager</b> serves as the financial backbone of the community. In addition to providing support to residents, future residents, visitors and team members, their primary responsibility is managing the community finances. They record rent payments, security deposits and application fees and deposit all monies collected daily. In the Community Manager’s absence, The Assistant Manager will assume all responsibilities associated with the community.</div>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div><b>Things You Will Do</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
Empower team members to take on new challenges and bring new ideas to the forefront</li>
<li>
Maintain accurate resident records, including rent and lease renewals and terminations</li>
<li>
Issue appropriate notices, such as late payments, eviction notices and returned check memos</li>
<li>
Record monies collected and deposit</li>
<li>
Prepare weekly and monthly reports</li>
<li>
Work with the Leasing Professional to secure lease renewals from residents</li>
<li>
Proofread lease paperwork</li>
<li>
Process move-ins and move-outs</li>
<li>
Provide outstanding customer service to residents and future residents</li>
<li>
Take pride in the apartment community; visually inspect and address areas of concern, keep community clean and report service needs to maintenance team members</li>
<li>
Think safety first and ensure that unsafe conditions are corrected promptly</li>
<li>
Maintain an organized environment even during busy times</li>
<li>
Answer incoming phone calls and handle accordingly</li>
<li>
Build rapport with residents and future residents</li>
<li>
Update online advertising</li>
<li>
Know your market by being abreast of competition and surrounding area</li>
<li>
Work closely with the Community Manager to ensure seamless community management in the Community Manager’s absence</li>
<li>
Communicate effectively with residents, future residents, visitors, vendors and team members while presenting a positive, professional image</li>
<li>
Perform additional duties as assigned by the Community Manager</li>
</ul>
</div></div></div>
