<div>Virgin Pulse, founded as part of Sir Richard Branson’s famed Virgin Group, helps organizations build employee health and wellbeing into the DNA of their corporate cultures. As the only company to deliver a powerful, mobile-first digital platform infused with live services, including coaching and biometric screenings, Virgin Pulse’s takes a high-tech-meets-high-touch-approach to engage employees in improving across all aspects of their health and wellbeing, every day – from prevention and building a healthy lifestyle to condition and disease management to condition reversal, all while engaging users daily in building and sustaining healthy habits and behaviors. A global leader in health and wellbeing, Virgin Pulse is committed to helping change lives and businesses around the world for good so that people and organizations can thrive, together. Today, more than 3100 organizations across the globe are using Virgin Pulse’s solutions to improve health, employee wellbeing and engagement, reduce costs and create strong workplace cultures.<br>

<p><b>Who you are.</b></p>
<div>
You are a creative, resourceful <b>corporate </b><b>recruiter with strengths in recruitment of Sales, Product and Software Engineering (ideally) professionals. </b>You know how to actively source, identify and pursue top talent to support rapid growth in multiple markets in the U.S. You seek an exciting opportunity when you can be an integral part in the building process of one of one of the world's most successful digital health engagement companies! This onsite role will be exciting, challenging, and multifaceted as it interacts with all levels and work streams.<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<div>Once chosen you will demonstrate that you have a proven ability to be highly effective in managing your time and work on ongoing project deliverables. If you are technically savvy, have a keen sense for top talent, know how to source, screen and manage software engineering and other candidates; relate to hiring managers, and can creatively source when the requisition requires. Most of all, you are motivated by excellence and seek to be part of a great team!</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div><b>
In the role of Corporate Recruiter you will wear many hats but your attention will be crucial in the following:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
Become a partner to hiring managers; work with hiring managers and teams to identify their hiring needs and guide them through the interview process</li>
<li>
Partner with hiring managers to write clear and concise job descriptions that follow our ideal message/template</li>
<li>
Work within our internal ATS - JobVite (Changing to iCIMS in Q4)</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>
Utilize knowledge of multiple recruiting sources and advanced sourcing techniques to identify top talent</li>
<li>
Source passive candidates through networking, internet searches, social media, email, LinkedIn, telephone, etc.</li>
<li>
Create a pipeline of “bench” candidates for future opportunities</li>
<li>
Maintain accurate and thorough documentation within the company's ATS</li>
<li>
Adequately screen candidates for availability, interest level, salary range, relocation needs, etc.</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>
Provide a professional and welcoming atmosphere when sourcing and interviewing candidates</li>
<li>
Assist if needed in scheduling of interviews in the ATS and Outlook , for hiring managers.
</li>
<li>Will schedule your own phone and virtual screens.</li>
<li>
Maintain a high level of confidentiality</li>
</ul>
