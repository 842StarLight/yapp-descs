<h2>Sr. Design Engineer - Lund</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>Lund, a Truck Hero company is a leading manufacturer of premium automotive and aftermarket accessories located in Lawrenceville, Georgia. We have the leading market share in several marquee product lines and have a long-standing, powerful blue-chip customer relationships. We have broad distribution networks that allows us to efficiently serve all sales channels. You can find out more about us on our website at www.lundinternational.com.</p>
<p>
Due to current needs and to support continued future growth in our business, Lund has an immediate opening for an experienced Sr. Design Engineer at our Lawrenceville, Georgia facility. We have an outstanding culture, energetic management team and innovative work environment. Some of the responsibilities of this role will include the follow:</p>
<ul>
<li>Conceptualize, design and develop new products and product extensions to compliment and support current product lines. Manage projects and resources appropriately.</li>
<li>Design and develop products to effectively support existing and product offerings.</li>
<li>Explore new and refined product ideas and methods of advancement for each product design.</li>
<li>Successfully drive research applications into technical plans.</li>
<li>Examine total cost of each project, while the effectiveness and safety of each design remains a high priority.</li>
<li>Participate in activities to verify the accuracy of designs, while documenting each process.</li>
<li>Construct all bills of material for the designs and maintain accurate records.</li>
<li>Troubleshoot designs and identify modifications for improvements.</li>
<li>Communicate and present project initiatives, updates and results.</li>
<li>Maintains product and process data base by writing computer programs; entering data.</li>
<li>Completes design and development projects by training and guiding technicians.</li>
<li>Maintains professional and technical knowledge by attending educational workshops; reviewing professional publications; establishing personal networks; participating in professional societies.</li>
<li>Contributes to team effort by accomplishing related results as needed.</li>
</ul>
</div>

<div>
<p><b>Requirements:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor degree.</li>
<li>5+ years’ related work experience in manufacturing and process improvement environment.</li>
<li>5+ years’ CATIA v.5 or NX.</li>
<li>Project Management skills and excellent communication skills.</li>
<li>Experience dealing with complexity, informing others, hardware specifications, manufacturing methods and procedures, manufacturing quality, product development, presenting technical information, CAD.</li>
<li>Support and lead projects with a multi-disciplinary matrix organization.</li>
<li>Knowledge of database software, MSOffice including MSOutlook and customer based portals.</li>
<li>Available for occasional travel and different shifts based on phase of project.</li>
</ul>
