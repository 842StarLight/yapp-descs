<h2>Social Media & Marketing INTERNSHIP</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>This UNPAID internship will include a wide range of social media, administrative, promotional, and community outreach duties for the Program Marketing Departments. The intern will gain experience in the areas of social media, web design, graphic design, online advertising (facebook &amp; google), content writing, product photography, market research, data collection, organization, and promotion of community events. Time and responsibilities will be split between multiple projects.</p>
<p>Some of the projects we are currently working on include; Green Attic Insulation, Deluxe Wine &amp; Spirits (liquor distributor), EcoBuild + (construction) and many more.</p>
<p>General Information</p>
<ul>
<li>Internships available from October-January</li>
<li>Hours may vary: We prefer interns be available at least 3 days per week. 6-8 hours per day. <b>However, you may work remotely at least 50% of the time.</b>
</li>
<li>We will work with schools for student interns receiving credit, but receiving credit is not required.</li>
</ul>
<p>Requirements</p>
<ul>
<li>Eligible candidates must be at least 18 years old.</li>
<li>Students of any major are encouraged to apply, but an interest in social media platforms, sales, marketing, event planning, and promotions would be recommended. Candidate should be social media savvy and innovative.</li>
<li>They should have excellent content organizing, research and communication skills.</li>
<li>They should be able to demonstrate an expert understanding of social media applications (Facebook, Twitter, etc.) as well as an awareness and experience with PR and social media business trends.</li>
<li>Intern must provide his or her own transportation.</li>
</ul>
<p>Responsibilities</p>
<ul><li>Weekly social media networking (Facebook, Instagram, Youtube, Twitter, etc.)</li></ul>
<ul>
<li>Assisting with ongoing marketing campaigns.</li>
<li>Assisting in content writing</li>
<li>Maintaining an awareness of the company's projects, initiatives, and long-term goals.</li>
</ul>
<p>Please attach a cover letter expressing your qualifications, experience, and interest in the internship.</p>
