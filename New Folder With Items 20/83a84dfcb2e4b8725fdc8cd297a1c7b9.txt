Our Story
<br><br>
We started MedMen with a simple vision; cannabis as a consumer product. It is a simple idea with profound consequences for how marijuana is cultivated, produced and marketed. Quality standards matter, best practices matter, brand reputation matters. Today, MedMen is the most dominant cannabis enterprise in the emerging legal marijuana industry.
<br><br>
Job Summary
<br><br>
The Hospitality Associate/Delivery Driver is responsible for creating a world class "showtime" customer experience for each and every customer as well as providing a delivery service for online customers. This position is accountable for executing initiatives that achieve all hospitality goals at MedMen.
<br><br>
This position is responsible for complying with all policies and procedures, compliance regulations, and all other operational objectives of the business. This position is also responsible for executing visual standards, presentation requirements, meeting customer needs and product performance.
<br><br>
Essential Functions
<br><br>
Reasonable accommodations may be made to enable individuals with disabilities to perform the essential functions.
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Meet/exceed personal sales volume and KPI goals.</li>
<li>Meet/exceed Hospitality and Marketing objectives.</li>
<li>Meet/exceed delivery targets and be detailed to ensure accuracy of items being delivered.</li>
<li>Assist in building a great company and business that disrupts the traditional retail model and embrace change.</li>
<li>Provide first class delivery service that drives new business and contributes to online profitability.</li>

<li>Practice safe driving and cash handling.</li>

<li>Drive repeat business through strong local grass roots networking, customer data capture and our loyalty program.</li>
<li>Execute high customer experience standards, to include a "showtime" culture and industry leading customer engagement through strong sales techniques and product knowledge.</li>
</ul>
