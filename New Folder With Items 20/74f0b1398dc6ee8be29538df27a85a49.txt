<h2>Senior Business Development Associate - Financial Institutions Division</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Description</b></h2>
<br>
<div><b>
Job Summary:<br>
</b></div>
<div>
<br>
Under limited supervision uses specialized knowledge and skills obtained through experience, training and/or certification to support a team in recruiting advisors to Raymond James Financial Services Financial Institutions Division partners. Extensive contact and collaboration with internal and external stakeholders is required to establish and maintain relationships that lead to recruiting new advisors. Acts in a professional sales and marketing capacity to help meet firm goals.</div>
<div>
<b><br>
</b><br>
<b>Essential Duties and Responsibilities:<br>
</b>
</div>
<div>
<br>
<ul>
<li>Collaborate and communicate with external recruiters and PCG divisions for cross-referral opportunities of FID clients.</li>
<li>Manage and communicate the FID opportunity pipeline with internal, external and PCG recruiting resources.</li>
<li>Coordinates Drip Campaigns.</li>
<li>Sources, solicits and develops new affiliations by responding to incoming calls and inquiries, sending out materials and may visit prospects to meet specific recruiting goals.</li>
<li>Maintain FID recruiting checklist and resources.</li>
<li>Assists with the creation of business development strategies, and with maintaining and expanding referral sources including the coordination of target marketing campaigns.</li>
<li>Maintains a prospect database; generates reports as needed.</li>
<li>Establishes relationships with prospective advisors, provides consultation and education regarding financial institutions division opportunities.</li>
<li>Proactively calls existing prospects to educate them on RJFS products and services.</li>
<li>Responds to incoming calls and inquiries including research, follow-up, sending out materials and obtaining background information.</li>
<li>Gathers information and data on prospects to be used in the sales process.</li>
<li>Participates in conference calls with prospects.</li>
<li>Visit with prospective advisors to address issues, interests and concerns.</li>
<li>May participate in visits to with divisional teams to discuss recruiting opportunities with investment program management.</li>
<li>May participate in visits to home office by prospects to address issues, interests and concerns.</li>
<li>Coordinates with other functional areas to ensure a professional recruiting process.</li>
<li>Assists division management with the creation of proformas for prospects.</li>
<li>Serve as backup to Division Director and Assistant Division Director in recruiting activities.</li>
</ul>
</div>
