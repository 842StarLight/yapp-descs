<h2>Gunsmith</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Position Title: </b> Full-time Gunsmith</p>
<p><b>Reporting To: </b> Warranty Service Manager</p>
<p><b>Position Summary: </b></p>
<p>Traditions Performance Firearms is currently searching for a self-motivated individual with experience in both firearms technology and customer service to join our team. Previous experience in firearms production is a plus.</p>
<p><b>Position Qualifications: </b></p>
<p>· Graduate of a resident trade school program with a 1-year Certificate or a 2-years Associates Degree of in Gunsmithing or Firearms Technology. Note: 2-3 years of experience working in a gunsmithing setting will be considered in-lieu of education.</p>
<p>· Familiarity with usage of hand tools with relation to working on firearms.</p>
<p>· Must be familiar with primary measuring tools for standard gunsmithing work (Calipers, micrometers, bore/pin gauges, torque wrenches, sight adjustment tooling, headspace gauges, etc.)</p>
<p>· Time management and organizational skills are a must</p>
<p>· Proficiency with MS Office programs (Word, Excel, etc.)</p>
<p>· Previous knowledge of SAGE software (MAS) is a plus for the position</p>
<p>· Knowledge/Experience in cycle of operations, reparations, and customization experience in any or all of the below firearms platforms is needed in order to perform in this role:</p>
<p>· Knowledge of sighting systems and sight system principles is needed for this role</p>
<p>o Open sights, sight radius establishment, Minute-of-Angle, Exterior ballistics, etc.</p>
<p>o Sighting optics, various scope reticle platforms, and industry standards/trends for hunting industry optics.</p>
<p>· Knowledge of ballistics, propellants, and projectiles in both cartridge and blackpowder firearms.</p>
<p>· Experience in the American Hunting &amp; Shooting Industry is a plus for this position.</p>
<p><b>Principle Duties: </b></p>
<p>· Repairing and performing work on customer returns and in-house products as needed.</p>
<p>· Answering warranty and technical inquiries for our customer base via phone or email</p>
<p>· Processing customer sales and service charges for customers in the context of a non-warranty scenario.</p>
<p>· Research, troubleshoot, and help resolve customer issues with their firearms or optics.</p>
<p>· Assisting customers in the Traditions retail store as needed.</p>
<p>· Testing firearms for function, accuracy, and mechanical repeatability at the shooting range.</p>
<p>· Assisting the QC team with Quality Control inspections and maintain BATFE/FFL compliance with serialized cartridge firearms through proper acquisition, handling, and disposition procedures pertaining to this position.</p>
<p>Job Type: Full-time</p>
<p>Work Location:</p>
<ul><li>One location</li></ul>
