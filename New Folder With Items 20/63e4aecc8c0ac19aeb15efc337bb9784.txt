<h2>Production Assistant</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Production Assistant</b></p>
<p>The purpose of this position is to support the manufacturing process through various data entry processes, to include: Manufacturing Order receipts, Updating Production Schedule, Producing “Hit List”, various reporting and processing as it relates to Inventory Management as well as maintaining time and attendance for production staff.</p>
<p><b>ESSENTIAL FUNCTIONS</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Verifying labor data collection on manufacturing orders and recording time in Great Plains including validation against labor standards.</li>
<li>Verifying component quantity and build quantity of manufacturing orders, and recording the receipt of manufacturing order through Great Plains.</li>
<li>Entry and verification of Purchase Order Receipts.</li>
<li>Updating our Finished Goods shortage list (Hit List) daily.</li>
<li>Processing Sales Order Returns, Bin Transfers in Great Plains.</li>
<li>Printing Manufacturing Orders for daily production release from Great Plains.</li>
<li>Printing Sample Sales Order Pick Lists including documentation as required by regulations.</li>
<li>Generating Weekly Stock Count Variance Reports for Production and Warehouse Management.</li>
<li>Producing and distributing reports on soon to expire inventory to management (and Sales for finished good items).</li>
<li>Weekly maintenance of production and warehouse staff attendance, clock in/ out times in web-based time and attendance software.</li>
<li>Documenting personal paid time off/ sick time in web-based time and attendance software.</li>
<li>Weekly production and warehouse attendance reporting to department managers.</li>
<li>Review and submit bi-weekly time sheets to Human Resources for payroll.</li>
<li>Train as backup for entering Stock Count Quantities in GP.</li>
<li>Perform other duties as assigned, if qualified, to meet business demands.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>KNOWLEDGE, SKILLS &amp; ABILITIES</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Must possess the ability to effectively maintain communication across functional departments.</li>
<li>Must be self-motivated, dependable and flexible to keep up with demands.</li>
<li>Must be accurate and thorough during all stages of data entry.</li>
<li>Must be able to self-check work for accuracy.</li>
<li>Must be computer literate – previous experience with account software packages a plus.</li>
<li>Must be able to interact well with a variety of personalities within a production/manufacturing environment.</li>
<li>Must be able to work under pressure and meet production deadlines.</li>
<li>Must display initiative and independent judgment.</li>
</ul>

</ul>
