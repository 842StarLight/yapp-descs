<h2>Division Sales Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">Job Description
<br><br>
<b>JOB SUMMARY:</b><br>
Oversee and
<br>manage the development and performance of all sales activities in the division in Indiana and Michigan.
<br>Staff and direct a sales team and provide leadership towards the achievement of
<br>maximum profitability and growth in line with company vision and values.
<br>Establish plans and strategies to expand the customer base in the marketing
<br>area and contribute to the development of training and educational programs for
<br>clients and Account Representatives.
<br><br>
<b>CORE JOB RESPONSIBILITIES:</b><br>
Develop
<br>business plans and sales strategies for the market. Initiate and coordinate
<br>development of action plans to penetrate new markets.
<br>Provide
<br>timely, accurate, competitive pricing on all completed prospect applications
<br>submitted for pricing and approval, while striving to maintain maximum profit
<br>margin.
<br>Develop
<br>and implement marketing plans as needed.
<br>Maintain
<br>records of all pricings, sales, and activity reports submitted by Account
<br>Representatives.
<br>Create
<br>and conduct proposal presentations and RFP responses. Assist Account
<br>Representatives in preparation of proposals and presentations.
<br>Conduct
<br>one-on-one review with all Account Representatives to build more effective
<br>communications, to understand training and development needs, and to provide
<br>insight for the improvement of Account Executive’s sales and activity
<br>performance.
<br>Develop
<br>and mentor staff through on-boarding, open communication, training and
<br>development opportunities and performance management processes; build and
<br>maintain employee morale and motivation; ensure the team is appropriately
<br>staffed with required competencies. Job Qualifications
