<h2>Large Account Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<div><div><div>
<div>We have an exciting opportunity available for Large Account Manager. In this role, you'll market, design, underwrite, price and service Large Accounts. This is accomplished with interactions and the input and support of the field personnel, other corporate departments, and our independant agency plant. May assist in mentoring and training more junior Large Account Managers.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
Level (or grade) will be determined based on candidate's experience.</div>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<ul>
<li>Researches, designs, markets, underwrites, prices and coordinates the issuance of new Large Accounts in the assigned territory.</li>
<li>
Promotes the development and production of profitable business and ensures the effective marketing of the company’s services and products.</li>
<li>
Develops new business opportunities by understanding the customer’s business and identifying additional product and service offerings to improve efficiency.</li>
<li>
Assists field and agency personnel in the sale or survey process of Large Accounts. Ensures effective utilization of field force including safety management and claims to provide assistance and exceptional service to agents, insureds and other internal staff members.</li>
<li>
Provides training and technical support to field personnel and agency plant on the Large Accounts products and tools and services to write and manage such accounts.</li>
<li>
Assists in strategic planning and implementation of short and long-term goals for Large Accounts that support Selective's business strategies. Recognizes trends and patterns in financial data and draws conclusions to make sound business decisions and recommendations to positively impact those trends and patterns.</li>
<li>
Represents the Large Accounts unit and Selective through agent visits, account visits, and participation at association trade shows, committee meetings, and other organization meetings/events.</li>
<li>
Maintains information on competitive intelligence, industry trends and consumer needs to ensure competitive advantages with Large Accounts.</li>
<li>
Works with the AMS and Territory Manager to recommend the appointment of new agents and the termination of undesirable agents, if applicable.</li>
<li>
Must be able to drive an automobile to travel within territory. Car travel represents approximately 20% of employee’s time.</li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<br>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Qualifications
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div>College degree preferred.<br>
5+ years underwriting experience preferred.<br>
3+ years field U/W experience preferred<br>
3+ years Large Accounts UW experience preferred.</div>
<div>
Must have alternative market knowledge.<br>
Must be results oriented with a proven track record in establishing, prioritizing and achieving business goals. This role is for a seasoned AMS with proven track record.<br>
Demonstrated superior communication, negotiation &amp; presentation skills<br>
Must be a high achiever with superior organizational skills who is self-motivated, pro-active, flexible, and able to work independently, from a remote location.<br>
Must be able to maximize technology to ensure high productivity (Must be proficient with Microsoft Word, Excel, PowerPoint and all internal business related software).<br>
Must have valid state-issued driver’s license in good standing and be able to drive an automobile.</div>
