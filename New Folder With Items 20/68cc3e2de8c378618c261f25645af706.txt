<p><b>About Automation GT<br>
</b></p>
<p><br>
Automation GT is currently looking to hire a hands-on Engineering Manager to lead our team of Mechanical,<br>
Controls, Electrical and Software Engineers. The incumbent will possess technical leadership, system<br>
engineering expertise, assume responsibility for all engineering projects and guide our team to ensure on-time<br>
delivery. This position reports to the President and CEO.
<br>
<br>
Founded in 2002 in Southern California, Automation GT is an innovative company focused on the design and<br>
construction of custom automated machinery and systems. We employ professionals with a range of skills and<br>
qualifications, which allows us to approach every aspect of machine design, engineering, assembly, customer<br>
service, marketing and sales on an in-house basis.
<br>
<br>
We provide an ideal work environment for engineers looking to grow their skillsets, flourish in a community with<br>
opportunities for independent creativity, as well as collaboration with talented engineers and technicians<br>
trained in diverse fields including software integration, mechanical and electrical engineering and robotics.
<br>
<br>
At Automation GT, you will work with a team of forward-thinking individuals that are well-versed on industry<br>
standards and best practices to identify creative solutions for a diverse set of design and engineering challenges.<br>
You will be able to put your skills towards the creation of machines that have a positive impact on the lives of<br>
many people. We have done extensive work in the design and manufacturing of automation machinery for the<br>
biotechnology, life science, pharmaceutical and medical device industries. However, we do not limit ourselves<br>
to any particular project type, and are interested in all applications of advanced automation technology.
<br>
<br>
At Automation GT, we hold three core values as the heart of our company culture:
 <br>
</p>
<p>We are committed to making decisions based on data and the scientific method.
 </p>
<p>We are committed to prioritizing customer satisfaction in all that we do, which often involves<br>
thinking ahead and anticipating what they will need.
 </p>
<p>We are committed to ensuring the job satisfaction of our employees and will work with you to<br>
ensure that you are finding plenty of opportunities to grow and find fulfillment.
<br>
</p>
<p>We offer staff members a compelling package, including a competitive salary, superior benefits, an outstanding<br>
work environment and opportunities for career advancement. Through these comprehensive offerings, every<br>
individual has the chance to achieve significant growth, both financially and professionally.<br>
</p>

<p><b>Job Description<br>
</b></p>
<p><br>
<b>Responsibilities:</b><br>
<b>
 </b></p>
<p>Leads a team of mechanical, electrical, controls and software engineers with a hands-on involvement to<br>
develop advanced technology solutions in automation
 </p>
<p>Develops, deploys, monitors and measures internal design standards within all engineering sub groups
 </p>
<p>Works closely with Application Engineers to develop concepts and support sales engineering as needed
 </p>
<p>Manage internal design reviews and participate in design reviews with customer<br>
 Ensures final output of machine designs are functional, cost-effective and meet customer requirements
 </p>
<p>Prepares, communicates and monitors individual workloads to optimize and adhere to project schedules<br>
as well as capacity management
 </p>
<p>Develops department best practices and standard operating procedures to include integration and<br>
hand-off of designs to the Purchasing and Operations departments
 </p>
<p>Provides technical expertise on strategic initiatives and presents key industry trends to senior<br>
management to include creating and deploying new and unique approaches in automation
 </p>
<p>Provides periodic insights and reports to senior management to ensure continued alignment with<br>
department and company goals
 </p>
<p>Leads the Engineering team to deploy product strategies
 </p>
<p>Monitors vendor products and new technologies
 </p>
<p>Promotes good communication and engagement amongst internal and external teams to ensure<br>
customer expectations are met
 </p>
<p>Continuously collaborates with internal and external teams to achieve shared objectives
 </p>
<p>Inspires, mentors, grows and develops engineering talent while providing vision and direction for the<br>
team<br>
