<h2>Legal Coordinator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>SS&amp;C Eze (formerly Eze Software) is seeking a Contracts Administrator to join the SS&amp;C Eze Legal Team in its Boston office. SS&amp;C Eze is a leading global provider of best-in-breed software solutions and technology services designed to maximize investment and operational alpha for the entire institutional investment process and community. Our vision: to lead a re-imagined investment process by creating a completely open, seamless, and fluid investment ecosystem.
</p>
<p>The Contracts Administrator position offers a high level of exposure with all of SS&amp;C Eze’s business units. If you have a keen attention to detail, an appropriate sense of urgency in your work, strong organizational skills, top-notch client service habits and excellent multi-tasking abilities, then this is the position for you!
</p>
<p>Job Functions:
</p>
<ul>
<li>Primary liaison between the legal team and the global sales team, regional consultants and prospective clients.
</li>
<li>Facilitate the preparation, delivery, negotiation, and processing of client and vendor contracts and ancillary documentation.
</li>
<li>Maintain and update contract templates.
</li>
<li>Participate in contract negotiations with prospective clients.
</li>
<li>Review vendor agreements.
</li>
<li>Initiate and execute legal team projects, including optimizing contract processing workflows.
</li>
<li>Analyze, interpret and communicate contract terms both internally and externally.
</li>
<li>Provide training and direction to internal teams related to contracts, compliance, market data, and commercial process matters.
</li>
<li>Partner with other internal teams to manage client-related data in internal systems.
</li>
<li>Report directly to and support SS&amp;C Eze attorneys.
</li>
<li>Train, coordinate and supervise legal intern(s), as required.
</li>
</ul>
<p>Qualifications:
</p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree with a strong academic record.
</li>
<li>Genuine interest in the legal field and/or preferably 2 plus years’ experience in contract administration and/or paralegal experience in a law firm or corporate legal department.
</li>
<li>Self-starter, strong organizational skills, professional discretion, follow through, and ability to multi-task a must.
</li>
<li>Ability to work well in a fast-paced environment with tight deadlines.
</li>
<li>Excellent verbal and written communication skills.
</li>
<li>Commitment to maintaining and improving internal processes.
</li>
<li>Strong analytical and problem-solving skills with excellent attention to detail.
</li>
<li>Strong individual plus team player.
</li>
<li>Highly proficient with all Microsoft Office applications (Outlook, Word, Excel, Sharepoint, etc.)
</li>
</ul>
<ul><li>Prior experience with Salesforce, DocuSign, Adobe Professional, Onbase and/or Smartsheet is desirable but not required; ability and desire to learn and adapt to various work management and database applications is essential.
</li></ul>
