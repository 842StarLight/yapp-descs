<h2>Territory Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>The position of Territory Manager is a vital role in developing both new and existing dealers within an assigned territory. This individual informs, inspires, educates, and engages our Authorized Dealer’s/Prospects/Industry Professionals via a unique array of sales, marketing and training programs in order to accomplish our annual sales goals.</p>
<p><b>
Position Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Manage Existing Dealers to use the Sales Tools and Programs available to them to increase Sales Volumes in the assigned Territory which includes Indiana, Western Ohio, Illinois, and St. Louis.</li>
<li>Create monthly business review meetings with key business partners to ensure that they are on track to hit the mutually agreed upon goals</li>
<li>Increase Branding through consistent Territory Marketing and Dealer Displays</li>
<li>Maintain brand display excellence</li>
<li>On a consistent basis, contact existing customers to inform, train, and educate them on all new and current Miele products and related accessories.</li>
<li>Contact potential new customers by telephone, email or in person to arrange a personal visit to open up New Business through Traditional Distribution and through the Trade Partner Program</li>
<li>Participate in trade shows, sales promotions, and dealer promotions to foster customer relations.</li>
<li>Participate in the overall experience at our Experience Centers</li>
<li>Focus on sales quota, new business development &amp; maintaining customer relationships</li>
<li>Supports and implements marketing plans, promotions and new product launches</li>
<li>Other duties as assigned</li>
</ul>

<li>Proficiency with MS Office programs is required</li>
<li>Must have excellent verbal and written communication skills, as well as demonstrated presentation skills</li>

</ul>
