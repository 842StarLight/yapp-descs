<p>Job Description:</p>
<p>We need an individual who is passionate about print and digital advertising, who can work in a fast-paced environment, who's excited about writing and creating proposals that make our advertisers want to work with WSJ</p>
<p>The Marketing Coordinator will work to develop RFP responses and proactive opportunities within vertical-specific categories.</p>
<p>This person will be the main point of contact for vertical-specific RFPs, and will work closely with the Sales Reps to develop winning proposal ideas and proactive opportunities for specific category clients.</p>
<p>Responsibilities</p>
<ul>
<li>Leading and developing RFP responses and internal brainstorms in regards to pre-sales opportunities</li>
<li>Creating and developing proposal decks and marketing materials with a focus on creative storytelling</li>
<li>Collaborating with multiple internal teams, including Sales, Media Planning, Research, Ad Product, etc. to ensure ideas are uniquely WSJ and executable, as well as meet the objectives of our advertisers</li>
<li>Stay up-to-date with new product and feature launches</li>
<li>Provide support, where necessary, to the wider team</li>
</ul>
<p>Skills &amp; Experience</p>
<ul>
<li>1-2 years of relevant experience in a sales marketing function - publisher-side experience a plus</li>
<li>Basic knowledge of the sales and creative development process, as well as content marketing and emerging digital and social platforms</li>
<li>Knowledge of basic Research metrics with experience in translating and shaping data into compelling stories aligning with a brand’s target market</li>
<li>Strong organizational, project management, writing, storytelling and presentation skills</li>
<li>Ability to manage multiple projects simultaneously and successfully operate within a large organization, effectively communicating with executives, interdepartmental teams and external clients</li>
<li>Has superior proposal writing skills that translate into clear and compelling marketing solutions and client proposals</li>
<li>Strong attention to detail</li>
<li>A positive, can-do attitude, team player</li>
<li>Commitment to excellence. No job too big, no job too small</li>
</ul>
