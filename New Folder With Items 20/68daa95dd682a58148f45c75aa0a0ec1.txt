<h2>Customer Service Representative</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Do you enjoy working with customers over the phone, value relationships with customers, and have a commitment to provide legendary experience for them? Customer Service Representatives first and foremost enjoy working with people whether that is the members of the team or customers. They enjoy working on a computer and talking to customers over the phone. They have the ability to communicate effectively, defuse difficult situations, interpret customer concerns and decide on the best course of action to resolve pending issues while upholding company and vendor policies; maintain excellent customer relations to improve customer loyalty and promote positive word-of-mouth advertising; ensure satisfaction by following up with customers and verifying that all issues have been resolved and can follow policies and procedures.
</p>
<p><b>Where you’ll spend your time:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Provides customers with answers to inquiries</li>
<li>Resolve customers problems or concerns by following company policy and using good judgment</li>
<li>Confer with customers by telephone, and email to provide information about products, product availability, shipping charges, tracking questions and/or other customer inquiries
</li>
<li>Take or enter orders, cancel orders, track orders, or obtain details of complaints
</li>
<li>Keep records of customer interactions or transactions, recording details of inquiries, complaints, or comments, as well as actions taken
</li>
<li>Process orders received through the phone, email, fax, or the internet
</li>
<li>Work with internal departments and external business partners to resolve customer issues
</li>
<li>Enters accurate order notes that communicate actions and results</li>
<li>Demonstrates good judgment, tact, and diplomacy in dealing with customers, both external and internal</li>
<li>Other duties and responsibilities as needed</li>
</ul>
<p><b>You have / Are:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>High School Diploma or equivalent</li>
<li>Customer Focused
<ul>
<li>Demonstrated through 1 to 2 years customer service
</li>
<li>Look for ways to add value beyond the customer's immediate requests</li>
<li>Addresses the unidentified, underlying and long-term needs and anticipates upcoming needs and concerns</li>
<li>Enhance the customers experience through following processes</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>Communication – Listing and communicating openly, honestly, and respectfully with different audiences, promoting dialogue and building consensus and fosters two-way communication.
<ul>
<li>Listens/pays attention actively and objectively.</li>
<li>Presents information and facts in a logical manner, using appropriate phrasing and vocabulary.</li>
<li>Communicates with others honestly, respectfully, and sensitively</li>
<li>Recalls others' main points, taking them into account in own communication.</li>
<li>Checks own understanding of others' communication (e.g., paraphrases, asks questions)</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>Basic to intermediate proficiency with MS Office programs to include Word and Excel</li>
<li>Achievement Oriented – you focus your efforts on achieving high-quality results</li>
<li>Passion for Teamwork - Work with others to help them achieve their personal and the organizational goals. You celebrate your wins with others, celebrate others wins and success, and realize when the company falls short, you fall short and look for ways to succeed</li>
<li>Exemplify Integrity - you treat others fairly, honestly, and respectfully, furthering the integrity of the organization and its relationships of trust within your own teams, customers, and the broader community.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>We have:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>A team of enthusiasts who have a drive for customer service and sales. Not an enthusiast yet? That’s ok you probably will become one</li>
<li>A growing team that has the desire and drive to continue to grow the company and watch employees grow with it</li>
<li>Excellent Benefits including medical, dental, vision, 401(k) with a company match, paid Holidays, Paid Time off Benefits, discounts on products and more</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Your work environment:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Office environment must be able to work at a desk and requires extended periods of sitting/standing</li>
<li>Requires repetitive movement and requires repeating the same physical or mental activities
</li>
<li>Requires speaking / reading English</li>
<li>Requires ability to talk and speak through the telephone</li>
<br>
</ul>
