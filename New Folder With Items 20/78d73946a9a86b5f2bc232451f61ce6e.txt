<h2>Collection Analyst - RMG - Dallas</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<b>Job Description:</b><br>
<h1 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>PURPOSE:</b></h1>
<p>
To identify and resolve any issues that would delay or prevent the receipt of payment from customers, along with minimizing the number of days receivable invoices are outstanding.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>JOB RELATIONSHIPS</b></p>
<ul>
<li><p>
Reports to: Controller, Accounting &amp; Finance
</p></li>
<li><p>Positions Supervised: NA
</p></li>
<li><p>Interrelationships: Internal: All levels of employees at RMG Networks. External: Clients: Consultants: Vendors.
</p></li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>JOB RESPONSIBILITIES</b></p>
<p></p>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Primary Responsibilities</b></h2>
<ul>
<li>
Review accounts receivable aging reports, identify past due invoices, research receivables history and issues in order to expedite timely payment of receivables by customers. This will include any potentially misapplied payments or credits, billing or PO problems. While taking a perspective of "owning" the receivables, work with applicable stakeholders to resolve issues in order to be paid timely.</li>
<li>
Contact RMG Networks customers via phone calls and/or email when receivables fall past due including documentation of all collection activities, customer responses, and related correspondence in Great Plains.</li>
<li>
Process the daily bank deposits, copy the related documentation, and apply payments to invoices including scanning information to customers’ accounts in Great Plains.</li>
<li>
Develop and execute a collections plan assuring that large and/or past due receivable balances are collected within terms or a reasonable timeframe.</li>
<li>
Place customers on credit hold when necessary in order to reduce risk to the company including communicating with Sales, Warehouse and Sales Order Processing.</li>
<li>
Process approved returns/credits and debits in the Great Plains accounting system including scanning related documents and information.</li>
<li>
Establish good working relationships with the RMG Networks sales staff and keep them aware of issues relating to the collection of receivables and enlist their help collecting as necessary.</li>
<li>
Prepare weekly reporting on AR Aging and collection efforts.</li>
<li>
Update a rolling 3 week cash forecast for expected cash receipts from customers.</li>
<li>
Use the various internal computer software including Sales Force, Great Plains and the Maintenance Search Engine.</li>
<li>
Submit invoices on customers’ on line payment systems when deemed appropriate.</li>
<li>
File customer invoices and pulling copies when needed</li>
<li>
Perform other duties as assigned</li>
</ul>
