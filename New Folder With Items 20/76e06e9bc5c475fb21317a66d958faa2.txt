<div>Operations Metrics Specialist Responsibilities:<br>
<br>
The Analyst will develop detailed reporting and analysis focused on operational capacity, operational efficiencies, process outputs, and sales planning. A successful analyst will bring strong technical skill to this role, including an ability to understand data structures, develop algorithms and problem solve with complex models.<br>
<ul>
<li>
Provide analytical support, capacity planning, and operational metrics for multiple internal customers.<ul><li>
Internal Customers: Licensing &amp; Commissions, New Business, Front End, Inforce Servicing, Information Technology, Underwriting, Product Development &amp; Business Leadership.</li></ul>
</li>
<li>
Develop and publish key operational and individual metrics. Identify trends in data and advise course correcting actions based on findings.</li>
<li>
Understand Complex Business Processes &amp; Workflows.</li>
<li>
Effectively communicate with various levels of leadership and frontline employees.</li>
<li>
Support the development of the annual expense budget and program spend plan.</li>
<li>
Engage with internal customers to provide comprehensive analysis including identification of trends, gaps and opportunity areas within the business.</li>
<li>
Provide cost benefit analysis for proposed business development opportunities and investments.</li>
<li>
Partner with operations functions to manage operational capacity and FTE's required based on incoming work &amp; trends.</li>
<li>
Partner with distribution to continually develop and optimize the sales forecast.</li>
</ul>
<br>
Factors for Success:<br>
<br>
Ability to understand complex data structures &amp; algorithms required.<br>
Experience working within SQL, databases, or Business Intelligence/ Business Objects software preferred<br>
Experience in Tableau preferred<br>
Advanced M.S. Excel knowledge preferred.<br>
Insurance Industry knowledge preferred.<br>
Operational capacity modeling experience preferred.<br>
Business Objects or other database querying tool experience preferred.</div>
