<h2>Sales Account Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><i>We are looking for a Sales Account Specialist to join the team in our Columbus office. Our Business Development team is the engine that will continue to power NextChapter into the future and beyond.</i></p>
<p><b>Our Story</b></p>
<p>Launched in 2016, NextChapter is a modern bankruptcy software that is truly moving the legal industry forward. NextChapter's cloud-based application provides legal professionals with the tools needed to prepare, manage and file bankruptcy cases. As the only true web-based bankruptcy software on the cloud, NextChapter offers flexibility and overall improves the lives of legal professionals across the country. Growing quickly, we employ nearly twenty individuals, eight of which work out of our Columbus office. Our CEO has recently been recognized as an innovator and thought-leader in the legal industry, winning awards such as Fastcase 50 and ABA's Women of Legal Tech. Our welcoming and encouraging culture makes NextChapter the place to work in Columbus. We're selling the cloud-based pioneer solution, for bankruptcy case preparation, management, and filing. We help attorneys make the move to the cloud and transform the way they manage their bankruptcy practice.</p>
<p><b>Are you the perfect fit?</b></p>
<p>NextChapter wants a hustler, a go-getter, someone who is highly motivated and produces results. Are you someone who thrives in a rapid-growth, high-velocity environment and embodies a professional, entrepreneurial spirit? We value someone who is motivated and organized while possessing strongly developed communication and interpersonal skills. We desire a dedicated professional, eager to take the sale from the initial conversation to the close; helping grow NextChapter's client base. To make the impact that we envision, we will work with someone who can hop on the phone and contact hundreds of current and prospective customers per week. Our team is comprised of experienced leaders, legal professionals, and entrepreneurs. If you want to learn from the best and build the foundation for a long sales career, NextChapter is the place for you.</p>
<p><b>A day in the life of a Business Development Specialist: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Access tens of thousands of qualified leads from our active and engaged user base</li>
<li>Qualify leads through phone and email prospecting on warm and cold leads</li>
<li>Conduct conversations, demos, and walk-throughs with key decision-makers in law firms</li>
<li>Responsible for the full sales cycle from beginning to end</li>
<li>Creating awareness of, boosting interest in, and assisting in sales meetings and presentations of NextChapter's product</li>
<li>Build rapport with current customers to up-sell products</li>
<li>Follow-up on marketing campaigns (direct mail, webinars, trade shows, etc.)</li>
<li>Maintain call notes, contacts, accounts and opportunities in Salesforce</li>
<li>Manage and track leads, tasks, reports, and dashboards in Salesforce</li>
</ul>

<li>A measurable track record of accomplishments and sales results</li>
<li>Analytical, data-driven mindset towards the sales pipeline</li>
<li>A focus centered on collaborative company growth</li>
<li>A continuous growth mindset</li>
<li>An infectious, positive attitude</li>
<li>A team mentality - if one wins, we all win</li>
</ul>
