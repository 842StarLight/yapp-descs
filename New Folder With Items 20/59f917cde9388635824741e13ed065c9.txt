<p><b>POSITION SUMMARY:
</b></p>
<p>To maintain and improve TAK networks and software tools, with a focus on security and improvement in the corporate use of software tools.</p>
<p><b>ESSENTIAL FUNCTIONS OF THE JOB INCLUDE, BUT ARE NOT LIMITED TO:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Manage all TAK networks in concert with the local area manager.
<ul>
<li>Migrate each network to a fixed set of common topologies based upon network demands.</li>
<li>Install and support LANs, WANs, network segments, internet, and intranet systems.</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>Fill the role of administrator for Office 365:
<ul>
<li>User and software setup, maintenance, training, licensing and security.</li>
<li>TAK wide responsibility shared with local area assets.</li>
<li>Encourage training of staff to maximize utility of these tools.</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>Monitor system security:
<ul>
<li>Deploy consistent security packages appropriate to the specific location.</li>
<li>FortiGate security appliances and Bitdefender desktops.</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>Hardware
<ul>
<li>Develop and maintain the new desktop preinstallation image.</li>
<li>Aid in the deployment of properly configured workstations.</li>
<li>Install and support printer, workstations, mobile devices and anything properly attached to the network.</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>Maintain flexibility to support the business with automation.</li>
<li>Travel to other TAK sites to inventory, install and configure a single TAK infrastructure and toolset.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>PREFERRED CANDIDATE WILL POSSESS:</b>
</p>
<ul>
<li>Formal computer education or direct equivalent experience.</li>
<li>Desired: 3 years’ experience as systems analyst or administrator.</li>
<li>General knowledge of networking and networking technologies is preferred.</li>
<li>Ability to execute detailed plans, repeatedly, with consistent positive outcomes.</li>
<li>Proficient in Microsoft Word, Excel and Access.</li>
<li>Strong analytical skills.</li>
<li>A demonstrated ability to positively deal with the oddities of software, hardware and humans.</li>
</ul>
