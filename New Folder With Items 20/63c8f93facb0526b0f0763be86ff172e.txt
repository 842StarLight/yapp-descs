<h2>Advisor, Supervision - Annuity Review</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Description</b></h2>
<br>
<p><b>
Job Summary:</b><br>
Under limited supervision, uses specialized knowledge and skills obtained through experience, specialized training or certification to administer and analyze various reviews to detect trends, trading patterns, and possible rules violations to ensure adherence to securities industry regulations and limit firm liability. Approves day-to-day trading activity. Some guidance is provided to perform varied work that is somewhat difficult in nature and that requires evaluation, originality and ingenuity to make moderately complex decisions. Resolves or recommends solutions to complex problems. Leads defined work or projects of moderate scope and complexity.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<div>
<b>
Essential Duties and Responsibilities:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Conducts compliance and suitability reviews, and monitors trading activities, through the use of various reports and systems.</li>
<li>Pro-actively monitors and ensures that trading activities in clients’ accounts meet the objectives of those accounts. Reports findings and manages concerns in a professional manner.</li>
<li>Interfaces with sales management and compliance associates to inform them of any supervisory concerns that may arise with branch office managers/FAs and works together with them to address these concerns as well as to resolve any conflicts that may arise related to client accounts.</li>
<li>Monitors and keeps up to date with regulations regarding trading activity in client accounts and applies them accordingly.</li>
<li>Assists in researching Supervision issues.</li>
<li>Assists in developing training programs, including maintaining training records and coordinating training with functions.</li>
<li>Prepares and delivers written and oral presentations to business units.</li>
<li>May participate in the formation of policies related to compliance.</li>
<li>Performs other duties and responsibilities as assigned.</li>
</ul>
</div>
<br>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Qualifications</b></h2>
<br>
<div>
<b>
Knowledge, Skills, and Abilities:</b><br>
<b>Knowledge of:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Investments and trading, securities industry regulations, and client suitability (all at a level consistent with the requirement of Series 24 licensing). Familiarity/facility with RJ and its systems is preferable.</li>
<li>Company’s working structure, policies, mission, and strategies.</li>
<li>General office practices, procedures, and methods.</li>
<li>Suitability and other compliance issues involved with branch manager/FA trading activity.</li>
<li>Knowledge of annuities and insurance products including general understand ing o the f broker dealer interface with insurance carriiers.</li>
<li>Ability to build rapport with branch associates and uses effective communication skills to deliver outcomes of suitability determinations both positive and negative.</li>
</ul>
</div>

</ul>
