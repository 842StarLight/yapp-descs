<p><b>
Job Summary:</b></p>
<p>
As a member of the West Division Sales Compensation team, this position is responsible for analysis, reporting and process management to support sales employee effectiveness. This person will work cross-functionally to identify opportunities while helping improve systems, tools, and processes across a diverse group of sales channels. As the Senior Analyst on the team, this person will lead projects, coordinate members of the team, and act as resource for colleagues.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
This position will infer trends from a variety of data sources, gather insights from sales leadership, and evaluate strategic positions, while confidently delivering their interpretation through professional communications. Sales Compensation Plan effectiveness will be analyzed and recommendations presented to executives. Performance evaluation of sales goals and recommendations will be evaluated, while managing processes involving sales leadership and accuracy improvements. Visual reporting will range from internal team processes to sales employee trends, and will require data and metric management. This established role engages with all levels of the organization, oversees projects with high visibility, and provides strategic insights into our growing organization.</p>

<p><b>
Primary Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Partners with Sales Channel teams to ensure all employees are receiving the correct compensation per the terms of the plan. Works with leaders to identify situations where employees are in unique pay situations.</li>
<li>Leads in the development and execution of sales tools, compensation platforms, performance management tools, and sales goal setting</li>
<li>Validates with Sales Compensation team that all elements related to compensation including employee changes, compensation engine changes, and compensation questions are complete, accurate, and on time.</li>
<li>Creates visualizations and interactive/action oriented reporting in systems such as Tableau</li>
<li>Leads Analysis projects evaluating sales practices and opportunities</li>
<li>Performs ad-hoc analysis</li>
<li>Write queries to extract, assemble, and prepare large data. Helps manage and automate data systems.</li>
<li>Initiates communication of findings via oral, written, and visual delivery to all levels of management</li>
<li>Works independently in own area of knowledge.</li>
</ul>
