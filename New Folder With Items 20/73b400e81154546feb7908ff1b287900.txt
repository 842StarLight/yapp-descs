<h2>Assistant Controller ("Asistente de Controlador")/ Property Controller (Controlador de Propiedad)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">The Assistant Controller should possess an Accounting degree and have working knowledge of the General Ledger and Financial Statements. The position will require heavy account reconciliation, managing general ledger month end entries, managing full cycle accounts payable, participation in monthly inventories, and daily interaction with hotel operators.
<br><br>
ESSENTIAL DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES include the following. Other duties may be assigned.
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Prepare and Post Month End journal entries as assigned.</li>
<li>Preparation of monthly financial statements.</li>
<li>Monthly reconciliation of balance sheets and bank accounts.</li>
<li>Involvement and supervision of month-end inventory counts.</li>
<li>Oversee and manage accounts payable staff.</li>
<li>Work with hotel operators to prepare semi-annual budgets.</li>
<li>Attend weekly hotel operational meetings.</li>
<li>Prepare and calculate monthly tax and rent payments.</li>
</ul>
<b>Computer Skills:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Proficient working in Excel, Word, Power Point, and Outlook.</li>
<li>Prior experience using accounting software to prepare financial statements.</li>
</ul>
Supervisory Responsibilities
<br><br>
Directly supervises individuals throughout the Accounting Department. Carries out supervisory responsibilities in accordance with the organization's policies and applicable laws. Responsibilities include interviewing, hiring, and training employees; planning, assigning, and managing work expectations; appraising performances; rewarding and disciplining employees; addressing and resolving problems.
<br><br>
Service professionals with a friendly, enthusiastic, can-do attitude is what we are looking for! Candidates must be able to maintain a friendly, positive attitude and a professional demeanor at all times. They must be dedicated to providing a memorable experience to each of our guests. They must be committed to the pursuit of excellence in service, a consistent product of high quality and value, and an entrepreneurial, sales-focused culture.
<br><br>
<b>Qualified candidates should possess:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Accounting degree from a four-year college; or five to ten years' related experience and/or training, preferably in the hospitality industry; or equivalent combination of education and experience.</li>
<li>Working knowledge of the General Ledger and Financial Statements.</li>
<li>Prior experience with reconciliation, managing full-cycle accounts payable, monthly inventories, and daily interaction with hotel operators.</li>
</ul>
</div>
