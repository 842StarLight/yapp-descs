<h2>Director Channel & Alliances North America</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>Job Description<br>
<br>
<p>As Director of Channel Sales Central you are responsible for increasing sales and pipeline through a network of existing partners and alliances. In order to further increase Nexthink’s presence in the region, the company is also looking at expanding its partner base with a special focus on solution-oriented partners that sell into enterprise accounts. As a result, the most important KPI is pipeline development via partners. To achieve this, the Director of sales will manage the relationship between Nexthink and existing partners and recruit new partners with the goal of increasing market coverage in the region. In this role, he/she will work with cross-functional teams from Nexthink’s Field Sales, Solution Consulting and Field Marketing as they will be key resources for you to leverage to attain and exceed performance goals.<br>
<br>
<b>Day-to-Day</b><br>
<br>
As Director of Channel Sales Central, you will grow the channel business in your region through strategic partner planning, enablement, and driving sales acceleration and pipeline generation activities.<br>
</p>
<ul>
<li>
Work closely with sales leadership on development and execution of a sales strategy for the Central region</li>
<li>
Demonstrate leadership and initiative to grow our Channel business across the Central Region.</li>
<li>
Create and maintain executive relationships with key partners in the region, as well as key sales people that work for the partners.</li>
<li>
Create and maintain executive relationships with the sales teams of key alliances in the region.</li>
<li>
Participate in strategic planning sessions with channel partners and sales management team on a quarterly and annual basis.</li>
<li>
Plan and direct channel activities including implementing forecasts, marketing program development, relationship development, customer satisfaction and collateral material development and distribution.</li>
<li>
Create sales team alignment between partners, alliances and account teams</li>
<li>
Establish short-term and long-term goals and quotas in line with corporate objectives.</li>
<li>
Achieve quarterly and annual pipeline targets.</li>
<li>
Identify needed resources for supporting sales strategy and develop a proposed budget for management review.</li>
<li>
Identify customer references that can be utilized when reference selling.</li>
<li>
Develop quarterly funding spend budgets based on partner revenue targets</li>
<li>
Develop a budget to support the enablement, marketing and promotion of the partner business plans</li>
<li>
Be the internal champion at Nexthink for the central region partners and alliances</li>
<li>
Seniority Level: Director</li>
<li>
Function: Marketing</li>
</ul>
</div>
