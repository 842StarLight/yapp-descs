<h2>Accountant</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">Bracco Diagnostics Inc. is a subsidiary of Bracco Imaging which is part of the overall Bracco Group. The Company specializes in the clinical development, marketing and sales of diagnostic contrast imaging agents. Bracco Diagnostics Inc. primarily markets its products to hospitals throughout the United States. Bracco Diagnostics Inc. is committed to the discovery, development, manufacturing and marketing of imaging agents and solutions aimed at providing a better use and management in terms of diagnostic efficacy, patient safety and cost effectiveness.
<br><br>
<b>TITLE:</b> Accountant
<br><b>DEPT:</b> Accounting
<br><b>LOCATION:</b> Monroe, NJ
<br><br>
<b>SUMMARY:</b><br>
The Staff Accountant will serve an integral role in the monthly close process, performing journal entries and completing reconciliations and analyses as part of the North America Finance Shared Services group. The Staff Accountant will work closely with other finance team members and North America legal entities to ensure accuracy and completeness of the companies’ books and records.
<br><br>
<b>ESSENTIAL DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES: include the following:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Prepare and analyze various account reconciliations and resolve related issues and reconciling items</li>
<li>Perform a search for prepaid expenses and unrecorded liabilities</li>
<li>Develop &amp; maintain recurring journal entries within our financial system</li>
<li>Provide support during the Interim &amp; Annual external financial audit</li>
<li>Assist in the Financial Statement preparation &amp; reporting</li>
<li>Assist in the Balance Sheet annual budgeting and forecast process</li>
<li>Maintain shared services performance levels</li>
<li>Prepare intercompany balance confirmations report on a quarterly basis</li>
<li>Prepare monthly, quarterly and annual government reports</li>
<li>Perform financial SAP system user acceptance tests for upgrades and enhancements, as needed</li>
<li>Participate in adhoc projects (e.g. to streamline, automate and simplify various G/L activities)</li>
<li>Provide general assistance in other day-to-day responsibilities</li>
<li>Prepare and maintain Standard Operating Procedures for Finance processes</li>
</ul>
<b>CORE COMPETENCIES:</b> To perform this job successfully, an individual must be able to perform each essential duty satisfactorily.
