<h2>Software Engineer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<b>OVERVIEW</b><p>
If you believe in making a difference, then Sentry is the company for you! As a member of a multi-billion-dollar organization, you will have the opportunity to make a direct, and immediate impact on our business and customers. Do you enjoy building new and innovative solutions to old, outdated problems? How about working on fast-paced teams with the passion for driving change and continuous improvement? You’ll have the opportunity to apply the power of your imagination to drive our business forward. Sentry is setting itself apart from the old insurance platforms of yesterday, and we want you to come along for the cutting-edge vision of tomorrow.</p>
<p>
This position may be filled as a Software Developer or Software Engineer depending on the related experience and qualifications of the selected candidate.<br>
</p>
<b>
WHAT YOU'LL DO</b><div>
As a developer on the Personal Lines Agile Development team, you’ll:<br>
<ul>
<li>Deliver innovative solutions to real-world problems</li>
<li>Work collaboratively with others across the organization, including architecture, project management, infrastructure, and other application development teams</li>
<li>Full stack development on a variety of applications</li>
<li>Maintain quality through code reviews, applying programming and security standards, and develop automated tests</li>
<li>Continuously improve by expanding business domain knowledge, collaborating with associates, participating in training and development opportunities, maintaining personal networks, and involvement in professional</li>
<li>Participate in an agile environment, including team and program level ceremonies</li>
<li>Coordinate software deployment and releases in a continuous delivery pipeline</li>
</ul>
</div>
<b>
WHAT IT TAKES</b><div>
 Minimum Qualifications<br>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s Degree in Computer Science, Web Development, or equivalent work experience in a related field.</li>
<li>Excellent communication and organizational skills.</li>
<li>Ability to work both independently and in a team environment.</li>
<li>Experience with an object-oriented programming language, such as Java, C#, or C++.</li>
</ul>
<br>
Preferred Qualifications<br>
<ul>
<li>2+ years of related work experience</li>
<li>Development experience with Guidewire PolicyCenter or BillingCenter</li>
<li>Experience with Spring (MVC, JDBC, Batch), Hibernate, SOAP/REST Webservices, BitBucket, Git, Jenkins, Gradle, SQL Server, HTML, CSS</li>
<li>Experience with automated unit and integration testing</li>
<li>Knowledge of professional software engineering practices for the full software development life cycle (SDLC), including coding standards, code reviews, source control management, build processes, testing, and operations.</li>
<li>Understanding insurance and how to apply appropriate solutions to meet business needs.
</li>
</ul>
</div>
