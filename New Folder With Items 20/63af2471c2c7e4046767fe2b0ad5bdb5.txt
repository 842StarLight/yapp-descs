<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Overview
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<p>Berkshire Hathaway GUARD Insurance Companies, an A+ Superior rated company, is rapidly expanding our footprint throughout the US. We are currently searching for a Customer Service Representative Trainee.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
The Customer Service Representative is responsible for communicating, over the phone, with insureds, agency personnel, claimants, providers, vendors, and any other callers with questions related to GUARD Insurance Group’s products and services and New Claims Intake. Some of the highlights of working in our call center are:</p>
<ul>
<li>
No Sales!! All calls are service based</li>
<li>
No weekends</li>
<li>
We’re closed all major holidays</li>
<li>
Excellent working hours (we operate between the hours of 8am and 7:30pm M-F)</li>
<li>
Generous amount of vacation and sick time</li>
<li>
401k with company match and profit sharing</li>
<li>
Tuition reimbursement</li>
<li>
A competitive healthcare package</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<p>Responsibilities:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Answer customers’ questions and if unable to answer their questions, direct them to the appropriate person</li>
<li>
Taking New Claims calls and entering the information using proper spelling, grammar, and punctuation</li>
<li>
Check voice mail, Customer Service Requests, and Policy Service Center Requests at scheduled times</li>
<li>
Utilize the Department Procedure Manual as appropriate</li>
<li>
Utilize GUARD Insurance Group System Software, Internet, and Agency Service Center to answer caller requests by accessing the appropriate information from the system</li>
</ul>
