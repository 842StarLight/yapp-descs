<h2>Sr. Staff Integration Management Specialist, LSG IT IMO</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<b>Job Description</b><br>
<p><b>What You Will Be A Part Of:</b><br>
<br>
Provide global project management to lead cross-functional projects with diverse internal teams in support of the LSG Integration Management Office (“LSG IMO”). Responsibilities include:</p>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>
Providing global advisory support for cross-functional/regional M&amp;A transactions and inter-company integrations</li>
<li>
Developing detailed integration project plans, including strategic and tactical considerations, timelines, key milestones, as well as appropriate business cases (revenue and/or cost synergies, cost-to-achieve/budget), including managing resource availability and allocation.</li>
<li>
Leading project status / cross-functional alignment review meetings, and monitoring interdependencies through appropriate recurring and one-time mechanisms</li>
<li>
Drive project deliverables and schedule ensuring while driving resolutions to any mitigation risks/issues that may arise</li>
<li>
Developing and leading the execution of change management plans, including functional training and internal/external communications</li>
<li>
Maintaining project plans, including managing electronic data rooms</li>
<li>
Coordinate multiple projects simultaneously and the ability to prioritize projects and to conduct presentations of complex situations.</li>
<li>
Provide general guidance to other employees and must have a demonstrated ability to influence others</li>
<li>
Preparing presentations for steering committees, executive staff, and board of directors meetings</li>
<li>
Functioning as a liaison between the LSG IMO and various integration teams to ensure the business needs have been met</li>
<li>
Able to understand the strategic discussions, work as a resource for certain project if required, but also able to be very tactical and execution oriented</li>
<li>
Working as a team member on special projects as needed</li>
<li>
Influencing stakeholders in highly matrixed organization</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
How You Will Get Here:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Strong understanding of our internal LSG operating mechanisms, ERP, WEB &amp; CRM functionality along with Operational Supply Chain required.</li>
<li>
Must be quantitative and detail-oriented.</li>
<li>
Solid organization, analytical, critical thinking, and creative problem-solving skills.</li>
<li>
Effective oral and written communication and presentation skills.</li>
<li>
Strong interpersonal and teamwork skills a must. Must be able to interface with varied audiences including senior leadership, and to work across multiple functional areas (Operations, Product Management, R&amp;D, Finance, Sales, etc.).</li>
<li>
Prior integration experience required.</li>
<li>
Strong understanding of formal project management methodologies.
</li>
<li>Able to learn quickly and take initiative with virtual teams. Uncompromising integrity.</li>
<li>
Ability and willingness to take on various assignments in a fast-paced organization. Conflict resolution and problem-solving skills.</li>
<li>
Life sciences, pharma, or Biotech background is strongly preferred. Ability to work in a very matrixed environment required. Experience managing complex global cross-functional projects required.<b> </b>Solid proficiency in Microsoft Excel, PowerPoint and Word.</li>
</ul>
<p><br>
Experience/Education:<br>
<br>
MBA preferred, BA or BS required.<br>
Minimum four to six years’ experience with at least two to four years in a lead role for successful completion of complex project/program development and delivery.</p>
