Requisition Summary
</div>
<div><p>Supports export sales, developing, and facilitating export process. Processes customer orders and performs in-house administrative functions related to export order processing, order fulfillment and invoicing for assigned accounts. Responds to incoming customer telephone, SupportLink and e-mail inquiries.
