<h2>Mechanical Design Intern</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">Work in concert with Engineering Team, Manufacturing, and Vendors to develop and maintain product and system designs for air pollution control technology. This role will require the extensive use of two- and three-dimensional CAD software to create mechanical models, simulations and drawings. From this design work, this position will prepare bills of materials, technical specifications, and costs for the assigned projects.
<br><br>
Responsibilities
<br>Under a Product Manager’s supervision, create and update CAD drawings, schematics, technical specifications, BOMs, and cost details for new and existing product and system designs.
<br>Prepare preliminary concept drawings &amp; pipe layouts, technical specifications, and cost and lead time information to support the solutions sales process.
<br>Support Solution Engineers in the creation and modification of approval design drawings for specific end users.
<br>Participate in engineering team activities and take ownership of specific tasks. The specific goals are to reduce product cost, address performance issues, and standardize/modularize the current product offering.
<br><br>
Qualifications
<br>In 2nd Year of Mechanical Design Program or 2nd to 3rd Year of BSME Program
<br>Experience in an industrial environment a plus
<br>AutoCAD and Autodesk Inventor proficiency required
<br>Microsoft Office proficiency required
<br>Knowledge of engineering standards in drawing practice, dimensioning and tolerances, drawing symbols, welding practice, etc.
