<p><b>ESSENTIAL DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES
</b></p>
<p>To be effective, an individual must be able to perform each job duty successfully.
</p>
<ul>
<li>Seeks new business while maintaining existing relationships with dealerships through Dealers marketing.
</li>
<li>Negotiates with dealer finance managers.
</li>
<li>Collects any funds owed to the credit union by the dealership per the CUDL dealer agreement.
</li>
<li>Handles and resolves disputes with member, dealers, and branches.
</li>
<li>perfects liens on all contracts purchased through the Indirect Lending Platform.
</li>
<li>Works with the local branches making marketing calls to the dealerships in close proximity to the branch.
</li>
<li>Performs other duties as assigned.
</li>
</ul>
