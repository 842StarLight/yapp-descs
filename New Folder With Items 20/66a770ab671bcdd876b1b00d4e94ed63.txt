<h2>Sugarloaf Day Groups Service Representative</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Overview
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<p>The Sugarloaf Sales Department is seeking to hire a part-time representative in our Day Groups division for this coming winter season. A Day Groups Service Representative is the link between the Sales Department and Day Groups Lift Tickets, Rentals, and other needs of the group for their day. The Day Groups Service Rep is there to ensure proper execution of the distribution of lift tickets, rentals, and other ski related requests and questions. The Day Groups Service Representative coordinates with the sales team and the sales service manager to ensure quality service. This is a part-time winter position with a 20 hour work week that may include weekends and holidays.</p>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<ul>
<li>Coordinate all service aspects related to Day Groups and related events booked through the sales office.</li>
<li>
Responsible for proactively supporting the sales efforts.</li>
<li>
Work with short lead times while maintaining efficient and effective inter-department communications and deadlines.</li>
<li>
Provide detail oriented documentation of groups with established Point of Sale and Customer Management software programs.</li>
</ul>
</div></div></div>

</b></h2>
<div><div><div><ul>

<li>

Be available for weekend shifts and some holidays</li>

Resort or customer service experience preferred and shown proficiency with computer programs required.</li>
