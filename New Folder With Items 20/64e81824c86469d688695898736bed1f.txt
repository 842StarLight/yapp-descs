We have succeeded, with over 4 million clinicians around the world using Imprivata for secure and convenient access. Now we are taking on new and exciting challenges in healthcare. We’re looking for a skilled professional that can help propel us into new areas of growth.</p>

<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Job Summary</b></h3>
<p>
We are looking for a passionate <b>Product Manager</b> to join our Identity Governance product team in Lexington, MA. This role focuses on strategic product innovations that help our customers realize the total ROI with the Identity Governance solution by enabling customers to deploy more features and capabilities, faster. The position requires a technical understanding of enterprise software, software-as-a-service, customer IT environments, and rapidly evolving security and compliance technologies. The ideal candidate will have an established track record of successfully growing new and existing products.</p>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Responsibilities</b></h3>
<ul>
<li>
Manage customer feedback and monitor deployment success, driving product innovation to improve the speed and scale of deployments.</li>
<li>
Define market-driven product requirements based on customer feedback and usage data.</li>
<li>
Work closely with product managers and product line leads to assess common needs and growth drivers across the business.</li>
<li>
Conduct primary and secondary research to validate new product concepts.</li>
<li>
Prioritize product features to meet strategic product and business goals.</li>
<li>
Produce personas, scenarios, and requirements and identify those necessary to reach a minimum viable product.</li>
<li>
Drive design activities that include rapid prototyping and feedback cycles.</li>
<li>
Participate in user experience testing and product feedback sessions to capture key feedback that feeds product development.</li>
<li>
Clearly communicate product ideas, requirements and business plans to all levels of the organization.</li>
<li>
Work with customers and partners as well as dive into the technical issues with developers.</li>
<li>
Create business models and identify target market, competitive landscape and key differentiators for the product.</li>
<li>
Support Sales, Services and Marketing teams as the product expert.</li>
<li>
Coordinate cross-functional team activities during the product release cycle.</li>
</ul>
