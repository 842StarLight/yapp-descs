<h2>MARKETING ACCOUNT MANAGEMENT</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>We are growing our Marketing Client Services teams and looking for career-minded individuals with the desire to succeed in a high performing, team oriented environment. Our clients are major companies in healthcare, financial, consumer products, publishing, energy, retail and travel industries. There are no sales requirements with this position.</p>
<p>What you will be doing:</p>
<ul><li>Primary or secondary point of contact for assigned client's program(s)</li></ul>
<ul><li>Accountable for maintaining positive, productive relationships with your client</li></ul>
<ul><li>Responsible for day-to-day service delivery for your client program(s)</li></ul>
<ul><li>Implementing, coordinating, and managing the set-up of new client programs with our corporate and contact center staff</li></ul>
<ul><li>Providing contact center staff with program specifications and goals</li></ul>
<ul><li>Generating, analyzing, and delivering regular client program reports</li></ul>
<ul><li>Monitoring and analyzing program performance</li></ul>
<ul><li>Monitoring live agent phone calls</li></ul>
<ul><li>Representing clients internally and addressing ongoing service needs</li></ul>
<ul><li>Proactively identifying and pursuing resolution of issues affecting client programs</li></ul>
<ul><li>Fulfilling established performance levels and continuously making recommendations for improvements</li></ul>
<ul><li>Occasionally traveling to clients headquarters and/or call centers</li></ul>
<ul><li>Presenting or assisting in the presentation of program results and summaries to internal staff/clients</li></ul>
<p>What you will bring:</p>

<ul><li>Enjoy problem solving and learning</li></ul>
<ul><li>Have superior organization/time management skills</li></ul>
