Qualifications</b>
</p>
<div>Education and/or Experience Qualifications</div>
<div>
<ul>
<li>
Bachelor degree in business, computer science, a related technical degree or equivalent</li>
<li>
Five years’ experience delivering complex technical solutions including planning, development, implementation and support</li>
</ul>
Other Required Qualifications<br>
<ul>
<li>Minimum of two years consulting experience</li>
<li>
Minimum of one year experience in programming or analytical work</li>
<li>
Fluent with Design and Implementation of Cisco networking technologies, including ACI, VXLAN, UCS, advanced switching, routing, and firewall technologies</li>
<li>
Knowledge of Cisco network equipment including: Cisco Nexus 9000 / 7000 / 5000 / 2000 series of Data Center switches running both NX-OS and ACI code, Cisco Catalyst 9000 / 6500 / 4500 / 3850 / 3750 series of Campus switches, Cisco UCS B and C-Series compute, and ACI APIC Controllers.
</li>
<li>Knowledge of network/internet Protocols/Services such as OSPF, EIGRP, BGP, VXLAN, LACP, DNS, TCP/IP, and VPN.</li>
<li>
Knowledge of Layer 2 &amp; 3 Switching
</li>
<li>Ability to manage and deploy network changes, including firewalls and access lists</li>
<li>
Knowledge of VMware solutions including vSphere, vCenter, and NSX a plus</li>
<li>
Knowledge of automation and programmability using Ansible and Python a plus.</li>
<li>
Knowledge of Storage Area Networking (fiber channel switches) a plus
</li>
<li>Knowledge of security issues and implications to network solutions a plus
</li>
<li>CCNP or significant progress towards it required</li>
<li>
Ability to work weekends and/or off hours as necessary to meet clients' needs
</li>
<li>Ability to effectively communicate with clients
</li>
<li>Strong written and verbal communication skills with the ability to effectively interact with all stakeholders</li>
<li>
Proven ability to maintain and create technical documentation for the enterprise-level customers</li>
<li>
Strong analytical skills with the ability to demonstrate solid attention to detail and a penchant for accuracy</li>
<li>
History of balancing competing priorities with the ability to adapt to the changing needs of the business while meeting deadlines</li>
<li>
Attention to detail on inventory and logistics is important</li>
<li>
Physically able to push, pull or lift up to 50 pounds of equipment. The team lift system must be utilized for loads weighing more than 50 pounds</li>
<li>
Ability and willingness to travel up to 50% or as needed to other CDW locations or client sites
</li>
