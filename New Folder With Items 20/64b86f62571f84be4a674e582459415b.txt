<br><br>
This position
<br><br>
The Senior Account Manager (AM) is a high impact position combining equal parts relationship management and client satisfaction. The Senior AM will work with existing DocuSign customers, managing an average book of 50 accounts ($10M+ book in annual contract value) to develop and maintain extraordinary relationships to ensure their satisfaction, retention and expansion. The Senior AM is accountable for customer retention, proactive client management, forecast accuracy, and driving adoption/usage with customers and prospects in collaboration with internal teams and the broader DocuSign partner ecosystem.
<br><br>
This position reports to the Director, Commercial Account Management.
<br><br>
Responsibilities
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Develop and leverage customer relationships to improve customer retention</li>
<li>Uncover additional opportunities within the Commercial team's most strategic customers</li>
<li>Manage renewal process and negotiate multi-variable contracts.</li>
<li>Drive usage and adoption and consult customers to increase successful adoption of the Docusign Platform</li>
<li>Facilitate cross functional support to achieve successful product implementation.</li>
<li>Develop and execute strategic territory plans to achieve metrics.</li>
<li>Become an expert within the specific vertical/geographical accounts managed.</li>
<li>Foster strong working relationship with DocuSign Account Executives to support and grow current customer base.</li>
<li>Travel to foster relationships with customers in territory</li>
</ul>
