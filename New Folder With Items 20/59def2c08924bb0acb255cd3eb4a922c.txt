<h2>Bookkeeping Accountant</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">The bookkeeping position entails responsibilities for our in house bookkeeping clients and special bookkeeping projects. The employee is responsible for retrieving, compiling, entering, reconciling bookkeeping data into the accounting software. Including contacting clients who are behind to alert them to supply their bookkeeping paperwork. Producing Financial Statements, new business chart of accounts set up, researching coding classifications.
<br><br>
Assist with payroll processing functions and payroll quarterly and year end tax returns. Assist with quarterly, monthly and annual sales tax return filings.
<br><br>
This position is also responsible for front desk back up and support other accountants as needed. Including but not limited to answering phones, assisting with tax season client pick up and drop offs, relaying phone messages to and from clients. As well as other projects assigned by management.
<br><br>
Must have experience with Quickbooks accounting software, Microsoft Office, and be comfortable with creating and editing pdf's and excel spreadsheets.
<br><br>
The employee should be able to work independently and as part of a team environment.
