<h2>Software Developer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Hub Promotional Group (HPG), a limited liability company with 7 promotional products manufacturing suppliers across North America is seeking a full time ERP System Developer. Candidates with <b>NetSuite</b> <b>or any ERP system</b> experience highly desired and encouraged to apply.</p>
<p><b>Position Summary: </b> The ERP System Developer (ESD) will be a part of a team currently supporting <b>NetSuite</b> for 4 subsidiaries within HPG. This role will be responsible for the development of coding based on the design of a solution that meets enhancement requests that are requested by various departments that are using the system. He\she will need to be able to develop and configure NetSuite based on the architecture\design of the solution using any or a combination of custom coding/programming and configuration capabilities within NetSuite’s such as custom records\fields, custom lists and or workflows. The ESD will need to understand department’s business needs and translate them to system requirements, write technical specification documents, ensure test cases are mapped to requirements document and verify all system functionality is tested. The ESD will work with the requesting department’s business process owner to determine a design that will meet their needs and as a result will need to explain in general terms of inherent issues or challenges or pros and cons of one design solution vs another.</p>
<p>This role may also be a project team member for the onboarding or migration of a newly acquired promotional products manufacturing entity or an existing subsidiary that will be migrated into HPG’s NetSuite system. During a project of this nature, travel maybe necessary at times up to 35%. This role will work with users, department managers, members of IT and consultants and will report to HPG’s Enterprise Software Manager. This is an onsite position and can be located in either Salt Lake City, UT or Braintree, MA.</p>

<p>· Pro-actively communicate, collaborate and build relationships with business users, IT development and support teams</p>
<p>· Manage project assignment to successful completion with limited direction and within assigned deadlines</p>
<p>· Provide reliable and informative project status to supervisors and business users</p>
<p>· Develop and monitor project plans to track and project results</p>
<p>· Pro-actively identify, document and resolve issues</p>
<p>· Lead cross functional work groups to identify and document detailed requirements, work flow, information sources and distribution paths, and system specifications</p>
<p>· Evaluate user requirements and implement working solutions through system design, analysis, testing, training, and documentation</p>
<p>· Apply technical and business knowledge in the analysis of user requirements.</p>
<p>· Determine short- and long-term solutions to resolve pressing businesses process and technology issues</p>
<p>· Perform post deployment evaluation of solution to determine necessary adjustments and enhancement to solution</p>
<p>· Liaison with business users and development to resolve issues that arise during development</p>
<p>· Liaison with development and support teams to transfer/communicate business requirements</p>
<p>· Influence the decision-making process by effectively presenting concepts and project overviews to vendors, technologist, and management</p>
<p>· Deliver documented business requirements\system design\workflow with in enough detail for IT team to support and provide training to end users</p>
<p>· Prepare recommendations and propose IT solutions</p>
<p>· Ability to read, digest and communicate complex subject matter to all levels and to influence personnel to accomplish the business purpose necessary</p>

<p>· Other duties as assigned</p>
