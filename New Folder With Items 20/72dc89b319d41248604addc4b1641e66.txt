<p>
Acumatica is seeking a Director of Product Marketing!</p>
<p><b>
Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Go-to-Market Strategy: develop and execute go-to-market strategies and plans by product, industry, theme, and/or audience.</li>
<li>
Proven experience in planning and leading product launches.</li>
<li>
Analyze current market position and recommend ways to boost market share, considering customer segmentation, industry segmentation, product bundling and product TCO/ROI.</li>
<li>
Collaborate with Product Strategy and Product Management to ensure marketing plans support product roadmap and releases.</li>
<li>
Positioning and Messaging: create compelling and differentiated content, including customer stories via presentations and video, infographics, social media copy, whitepapers, web content, brochures, surveys, contributed articles, sales kits that can be leveraged globally.
</li>
<li>Lead creation of playbooks that identify/define strategic and targeted opportunities, while enabling sales and marketing alignment for closed-loop execution.
</li>
<li>Demand Generation: Coordinate with various marketing execution departments including field marketing, corporate communications, brand and advertising to ensure successful and targeted execution of various demand generation activities including events, web seminars, direct mail, nurturing campaigns, and sponsorships.
</li>
<li>Analyze competitive product and messaging to create competitive strategies to win.</li>
<li>
Employ SEO techniques on developing content for our webpages.</li>
</ul>

WordPress and web technology skills a plus.<br>
</li>
