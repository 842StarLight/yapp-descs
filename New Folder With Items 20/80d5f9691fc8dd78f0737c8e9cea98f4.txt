<div><b>Summary:</b></div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<p>Trade Strategy Associate Manger (TSAM) will work closely with cross functional teams in the development of category leadership stories focusing on driving overall category growth. Leveraging syndicated data, shopper and consumer insights, category and industry trends, the TSAM will develop comprehensive category strategies focused around shopper centricity, enabling greater sell-thru and market share gains. This information will be leveraged among the business unit, Sales and the Regional Category Management teams to ensure cohesive messaging and execution. This position works as a strategic thought leader, imparting knowledge of the category to drive profitable business results. Duties and responsibilities include the following:</p>
<div><b>Responsibilities:</b></div>
<div>Extensive Category Understanding</div>
<ul>
<li>Act as subject matter expert in assigned category and provide thought leadership to internal constituents as well as external customers</li>
<li>
Serve as the expert on the use of syndicated and household panel data to assist Category Managers in the development of strategies and tactics</li>
<li>
Develop supporting category narratives for select Customer meetings as the credible Category expert</li>
<li>
Identify and disseminate Category Management best practices</li>
</ul>
<div>Reporting and Data Analytics</div>
<ul>
<li>Provide input into category definition and product hierarchy and ensure consistency across data providers</li>
<li>
Understand category purchase drivers, trip missions, shopper need states, etc. and liaise with Brand Development, CMI and Sales Planning on executional strategies</li>
<li>
Provide strategic category direction based on syndicated and HH panel data</li>
</ul>
<div>Multi-channel Experience</div>
<ul>
<li>Provide insight and perspective of the various channel dynamics on assigned categories</li>
<li>
Monitor channel shifting and the associated trends reflected in the marketplace</li>
<li>
Form perspective on multi-channel purchasing behaviors (e.g. site to store, store to site, etc.)</li>
<li>
Understand how online purchase behaviors differ from those in-store and how we can capitalize on those differences</li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<div>Shopper Understanding</div>
<ul>
<li>Ability to synthesize large amounts of data (e.g. Shopper Research, POS, loyalty card information, registry, etc.) into actionable insights</li>
<li>
Use knowledge of shopper and consumer to recommend optimal category assortments, flows, adjacencies, cross merch opportunities, etc.</li>
<li>
Build in-store category innovation into all category leadership discussions (e.g. aisle reinvention, store of the future, disruptive merchandising strategies, etc.)</li>
</ul>
<div>Manage Innovation Pipeline</div>
<ul>
<li>Partner with Brand Development to manage pipeline of innovation, bringing both a customer and consumer perspective to the development process</li>
<li>
Use knowledge of customer and consumer to recommend optimal assortments and channel exclusives</li>
<li>
Build in-store and .com requirements for a successful go-to-market into launch development plans</li>
<li>
Understand impacts of innovation to the division P&amp;L</li>
</ul>
