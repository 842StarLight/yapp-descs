<h2>Demand Planner- Charlotte, NC</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<div>
<div>The Demand Planner is responsible for successfully developing and recommending a multiple-input demand plan through the management of collaborative relationships with the sales and marketing teams. Recommends a plan that delivers upon key revenue and operating income targets. Has ultimate responsibility for the ongoing maintenance and continuous improvement of both the aggregate and detailed demand plan for a specific equity within the Snyder’s-Lance business. This role will work closely with Sales and Operations Planning functions by providing demand level insights to drive production, inventory deployment, and customer service decisions.
</div>
<div></div>
<div><b><i>Essential Job Duties
</i></b></div>
<ul>
<li>Work with the sales and marketing team to ensure that they’ve reached consensus upon a demand plan based solely on what they’re planning to sell. Communicate with each collaborating sales and marketing person regarding future promotional activity and any other notable movement in sales. Monitor Salesperson’s communications, ask questions and enter promotional activities into the demand planning system.
</li>
<li>Update and maintain the demand plan with future demand.
</li>
<li>Become trained and fluent with the Demand Planning software including the variations and methods that can be used to produce an accurate statistical forecast
</li>
<li>Analyze new items and determine the impact to the demand plan. This would encompass any product life cycle management, including product phase outs.
</li>
<li>Collaborate as needed with product supply managers to navigate changes via demand control events.
</li>
<li>Development and management of customer specific demand.
</li>
<li>Monitor the flow of customer specific demand plans through the planning process and enterprise software tools and provide item specific input into the monthly S&amp;OP process.
</li>
<li>Leverage demand exception tools and planning skills to proactively identify and resolve demand control issues.
</li>
<li>Measure, monitor and report key performance indicators
</li>
<li>Prepare, understand, and present monthly plans.
</li>
<li>Lead collaborative process improvement efforts that directly impact the demand plan.</li>
</ul>
<div><b><i>Knowledge and Skill Requirements
</i></b></div>
<div>Understanding of go to market strategies and sales strategies. Strong analytical, problem solving and reasoning skills. Excellent knowledge of statistical modeling techniques. Ability to interface with various organizational levels throughout multiple departments. Excellent oral and written communication skills. Exposure to Sales &amp; Operations Planning and other internal and external collaborative processes. Understanding of warehouse and transportation operations. This positions requires the skills to communicate and collaborate with others in ways that lead to consensus on a decision. Prior experience with consumer-packaged goods (CPG) suppliers to mass-merchant retailers. Ability to work independently on various projects simultaneously.
</div>
<div></div>
<div><b><i>Education and Experience</i></b></div>
<div>BA/BS Degree in a related area preferred with 5-10 years of direct experience; or an equivalent combination of education and experience.</div>
<div></div>
<div><b><i>Travel Required
</i></b></div>
<div>Travel required 0 – 15% of the time.</div>
