<h2>Inside Sales Associate - Federal Accounts</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>The Sales Support Associate is responsible for assisting Account Managers in the day to day fulfillment of orders and resolving customer service issues on assigned accounts. Provide a support resource for the day to day transactions to allow the Account Managers an opportunity to network through their existing accounts to expand their book of business. Minimize problem order situations by proactively following up on order processing and customer service issues for resolution. Attend various training seminars to gain knowledge on product sets Connection - Public Sector sells. Making outbound calls to customers to help find opportunities where Connection can be of assistance to them, as well as generating leads for Account Managers to follow up on.<br>
<br>
<b>Qualifications:</b>
</div>
<ul>
<li>
H.S. Degree or equivalent required.</li>
<li>
Ability to develop rapport and maintain strong working relationships with internal and external customers</li>
<li>
Ability to articulate ideas in a clear, concise manner</li>
<li>
Ability to multi-task and work in a multi-windows environment</li>
<li>
Effective written and verbal communication skills</li>
</ul>
