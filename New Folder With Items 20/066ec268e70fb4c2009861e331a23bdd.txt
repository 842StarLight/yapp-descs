<p>Responsibilities include:</p>
<ul>
<li>Repairs and maintains all components in light, medium, and heavy-duty trucks.</li>
<li>Operate various manufacturer computer programs to run diagnostic tests.</li>
<li>Diagnoses trouble codes, disassemble engines and other truck components and examines parts for defects and excessive wear.</li>
<li>Reconditions and replaces truck parts and components using, hand tools, pneumatic tools and precision measuring instruments.</li>
