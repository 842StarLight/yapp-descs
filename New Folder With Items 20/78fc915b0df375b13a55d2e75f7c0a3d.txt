<h2>Senior Digital Analyst</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<div>Cox Automotive is seeking a <b>Senior Digital Analyst</b> to develop narratives and insights on behalf of automotive manufacturers as it relates to the success of their sponsored advertising products. This person plays a key role in enabling the sales teams to go to market by providing data-driven solutions and thoughtful recommendations to support revenue driving initiatives.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
Day to day, the <b>Senior Digital Analyst</b> will:</div>
<ul>
<li><div>
Analyze digital media performance to explore and incorporate new data sources</div></li>
<li><div>
Connect insights by bringing client data to life through stories/narratives
</div></li>
<li><div>Creates queries and statistical analysis (SQL, R, Studio etc.) for a variety of internal customers and partner</div></li>
<li><div>
Develop client-ready presentations, dynamic dashboards, and reports that provide key insights on KPI performance, utilizing visualization and storytelling techniques and best practices</div></li>
<li><div>
Present directly to clients and internal stakeholders to highlight optimization opportunities and inform the creation of new client product solutions to meet individual client’s specific goals and measurements</div></li>
<li><div>
Manage the development of unique innovative analyses and methodologies</div></li>
<li><div>
Leverage the latest state-of-the-art data mining tools and modeling techniques
</div></li>
<li><div>Inform sales strategies by proactively providing quantifiable recommendations to drive growth</div></li>
<li><div>
Transform analysis into product/industry case studies and best practices to enhance knowledge of internal teams and individuals within the organization
</div></li>
<li><div>Lead the development and creation of new KPI’s/Reports/Dashboards by leveraging existing and incorporating the use of new datasets and analytics platforms</div></li>
<li><div>
Manage the extraction, analysis and interpretation of site traffic using sophisticated data methods, exploration, analysis, and visualization tools.</div></li>
<li><div>
Act as a liaison to Product, Operations, Sales Operations, Analytics, and other internal partners throughout the organization</div></li>
</ul>
<h4 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Qualifications:</b></h4>
<div><b>
Required:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
Bachelor's degree or an equivalent combination of education and work-related experience.</li>
<li>
5+ years of related analytics experience</li>
<li>
Expertise with digital analytics tools and business intelligence systems (e.g. Adobe Analytics Suite, Moat, DFP, Microstrategy, Tableau, Amazon Web Services)</li>
<li>
Advanced Excel skills, including custom formula development, functions, pivot tables, and visualization techniques</li>
<li>
Proficiency using SQL, Python, or equivalent to capture and manipulate complex datasets</li>
<li>
Strong analytical skills, including an understanding of cause and effect and basic statistical methods available to enhance findings</li>
<li>
Must be able to understand, analyze data, and synthesize large volumes of data into a coherent narrative or story to answer non-standard/complex questions</li>
<li>
Leverage mathematical and statistical principles to inform and validate analyses</li>
<li>
Strong communication skills, including experience in clearly and effectively presenting to internal stakeholders</li>
<li>
Strong organizational skills and the ability to effectively multi-task and prioritize in a deadline-driven atmosphere</li>
<li>
Experience leading moderate to large analytics projects involving cross-functional teams.</li>
<li>
Ability to solve problems and deal with a variety of concrete variables in situations where only limited standardization exists.</li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<br>
<div><b>
Preferred:</b></div>
<ul><li>
Experience in an automotive, advertising and/or internet environment</li></ul>
