<h2>Senior Revenue & Royalty Accountant</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>Do you dream of what cars of the future will look like when you combine them with connectivity, a smartphone, and cloud services? Can you imagine uniting those dreams with a company that has the skills and relationships to make that a reality? If so, Telenav wants you!</p>
<p>
At Telenav, we believe the car is at the beginning of a massive innovation wave that mirrors what happened on the smartphone several years ago. Building on our long history of mobile and in-car navigation software and services, we are on a mission to make people’s lives less stressful, more productive and more fun when they’re on the go.<br>
<br>
We’re looking for a highly motivated Senior Revenue &amp; Royalty Accountant to join our growing Finance team.<br>
<br>
<b>Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Prepare and maintain line of business revenue models</li>
<li>
Drive monthly close for line of business revenue and cost of revenue accounting to ensure proper recognition for revenue and cost of revenue</li>
<li>
Prepare balance sheets account reconciliations</li>
<li>
Prepare flux analysis for month-end close management meeting</li>
<li>
Maintain and update internal Sarbanes-Oxley documentation for revenue and royalty recognition and participate in SOX testing</li>
<li>
Collaborate with the sales organization, the engineering team and MIS teams to ensure that all revenue and royalty transactions are booked in accordance with Company’s revenue and royalty recognition policies</li>
<li>
Perform contract review, understand revenue recognition implications, identify potential issues and propose accounting treatment.</li>
<li>
Prepare and reconcile billings for Advertising, Mobile Nav and Auto</li>
<li>
Research revenue accounting guidance as needed and prepare accounting memos to document appropriate accounting treatment</li>
<li>
Prepare schedules in support of internal and external audits</li>
<li>
Prepare disclosure for Earnings Release and SEC filings (i.e. 10Q/10K)</li>
<li>
Participate in ERP system implementation including setup of revenue module, data validation, data reconciliation and testing transactions</li>
<li>
Identify opportunities for process improvements including automation where appropriate</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Desired Skills &amp; Experience:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
5+ years progressive experience, with combination of public accounting and industry preferred</li>
<li>
Revenue experience and strong knowledge of accounting concepts; knowledge of ASC 606 is a plus</li>
<li>
Must be excel savvy</li>
<li>
Experience with Netsuite a plus</li>
<li>
Strong attention to detail, well organized and able to work effectively with all levels</li>
<li>
Ability to take ownership of assigned responsibilities and work under minimum guidance.</li>
<li>
Excellent verbal and written communication skills required</li>
<li>
Bachelor's degree in Accounting or Finance</li>
<li>
CPA preferred.</li>
</ul>
