<p>Philosophy</p>
<p>HONOR is determined to change the payments industry one merchant at a time by providing a whole new merchant experience. We believe that truly great solutions are technically driven and not an after-thought. We don’t offer bait and switch pricing models. We don’t make short term decisions for temporary gains. We don’t chase quarterly earnings. We place trust in our partners and merchants as we are asking for them to place trust in us. We are dedicated to constantly building a brand that matches our name.</p>
<p>What we do</p>
<p>HONOR was founded to help technology platforms (eCommerce, Software companies, CRMs, ERPs and ISVs)improve their clients cash flow by accepting credit cards. We work to solve the complex world of payments with elegant, scalable, secure solutions paired with transparent pricing.</p>

We greatly value the support our employees provide, and you’ll be areal member of our team, not a coffee runner.</p>
