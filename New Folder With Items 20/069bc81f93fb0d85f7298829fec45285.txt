Submit your resume and interest below; generic and templated responses will be trashed. Optional: candidates may submit a short video to tell their story in 3 minutes or less via a Vimeo or YouTube link.<br>
<br>
The Account Executive will be focused on selling against Chive Media Group &amp; Atmosphere platform(s), with the ability to sell everything under the CMG hood including theCHIVE.com as part of a 360 package.<br>
<br>
Atmosphere is the first-ever content programming platform designed specifically for viewership outside the home. With 70% of our waking hours spent out of home, up 50% over the last two decades, Atmosphere created a unique video strategy that entertains audiences where they are spending their time. With family-friendly channels like CHIVE TV, Happy TV, Motiv8 TV, and more, brands are quickly recognizing that this innovative platform is an extremely impactful and cost-effective way to reach an audience.<br>
<br>
We are looking for a hustler, go-getter, and someone who will evangelize our brand to agencies and clients directly on the East Coast. <b>We’re not looking for an order taker</b>. You should be self-motivated, ambitious, a fast learner, and eager to make a real contribution. <b>Don’t even bother applying if this isn’t you.</b><br>
<br>
This position will be based in New York City and will report to the VP of Sales.<br>
