<h2>Director, Market Sales</h2>

<p><b>ABOUT US</b></p>
<p>
The We Company’s mission is to elevate the world’s consciousness. Our global platform reimagines and positively impacts how we work, live and grow through three distinct business lines: WeWork’s mission is to create a world where people work to make a life, not just a living; WeLive’s mission is to build a world where no one feels alone; WeGrow’s mission is to unleash every human’s superpowers. Through design, technology, and hospitality we are creating a community that helps people live life with purpose and have a meaningful impact in the world. The We Company began 2019 with more than 400,000 members. In less than ten years, we’ve built a global network of 400 locations across 100 cities and nearly 30 countries, and we’re just getting started.</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>
THE ROLE</b></p>
<p>
WeWork is seeking a Director to lead the Market sales segment in the Midwest &amp; Texas region. Our Sales Director will build, lead, and motivate a team to consistently exceed sales targets, contribute to the company culture, and develop cross-departmental relationships to increase new member base and building occupancy.</p>
<p></p>
<p>
The Market Sales segment represents an incredible opportunity for WeWork. This portion of the business is designed to house an entrepreneur with a million dollar idea to a fast growing start-up (1-49 employee companies). To date we have thousands of members ranging from 1 desk to 50 desk offices.</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>
RESPONSIBILITIES</b></p>
<ul>
<li><p>
Hire, train, motivate and lead sales managers and reps both in market and remotely</p></li>
<li><p>
Accurately forecast and achieve/exceed monthly &amp; quarterly sales targets and important metrics for the region</p></li>
<li><p>
Leverage the new member development team to create top of funnel opportunities</p></li>
<li><p>
Collaborate cross-functionally to define and execute on sales and marketing strategy and budget for the region</p></li>
<li><p>
Provide innovative solutions to hit/exceed team goals that are data-driven and scalable</p></li>
<li><p>
Work cross-functionally with Medium-Large Business &amp; Enterprise sales leadership to customize offerings and contractual agreements for larger members</p></li>
<li><p>
Communicate regularly with the US, Canada &amp; Israel &amp; Global sales teams to share best practices</p></li>
<li><p>
Work closely with Growth Operations to constantly analyze results and identify opportunities to improve systems, processes and programs</p></li>
<li><p>
Provide Real Estate team recommendations for current and future expansion based on market demand</p></li>
<li><p>
Partner with the regional GM on managing the overall health and growth of the region</p></li>
</ul>
