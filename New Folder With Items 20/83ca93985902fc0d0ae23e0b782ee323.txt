<h2>Senior Manager, Marketing</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">The Senior Manager Marketing will develop, lead and be accountable for all aspects of Marketing for the Industrial Group across the Americas. The position will direct and deliver on defined targets, while providing strategic direction on commercial issues. The Senior Manager of Americas Marketing will be charged with improving the top line performance of Fluke Corp, improving coordination and communication across the organization, and developing new and optimizing existing processes.
<br><br>
The Senior Manager Marketing will serve as a member of the Americas Sales Leadership team and work closely with the VP Sales to support company initiatives and to develop and implement strategic direction. We are looking for a candidate who will have the runway to be a successor to Vice President and/or General Manager roles within Fortive.
<br><br>
<b>Core Responsibilities:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Develop and implement strategic Marketing plans and forecasts in partnership with Channel Management, Field Sales, Global Marketing Organization, and Business Unit owners.</li>
<li>Directly Lead teams of Channel Marketers, Marcom, and Marketing Logistics associates across The Americas as primary owner of localized marketing in the Americas.</li>
<li>Lead matrixed teams of digital marketing, and NPI campaigns associates across The Americas as primary owner of all marketing actions in the Americas.</li>
<li>Build and maintain strong relationships with key opinion leaders, key distribution leaders, and key marketing leaders across the industry relevant to the Fluke business.</li>
<li>Coordinate and lead programs to drive new product launches to success and broad market penetration</li>
<li>Evaluate performance to objectives and work with Channel Marketing Managers, Marcom Mangers, and Marketing logistics personnel to ensure proper and effective countermeasures are in place to meet Fluke’s Americas marketing targets.</li>
<li>Develop and manage KPIs, campaign funnels, and other reports as necessary to effectively manage performance to monthly, quarterly and annual targets.</li>
<li>Evangelizing effective deployment of Fortive Business System through the Marketing organization.</li>
</ul>
<b>Operational Responsibilities:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Drive sales activities to achieve or exceed business objectives, sales growth, and profitability targets in North America. Other business targets will include revenue, market share, unit sales, and effectiveness of supporting processes to drive these metrics. Partner with Fluke leadership in other functions such as Finance, Legal, Operations, and Marketing to ensure 100% compliance in all aspects of business conducted internally or externally.</li>
<li>Ensure the use of FBS and other tools for direct planning, monitoring, problem solving and reporting of results against plans and budgets.</li>
<li>Ensure that the operating objectives and standards of performance are not only understood, but also owned by the management team and other associates.</li>
</ul>
<b>Strategic Responsibilities:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Assess geographical markets to deploy market specific growth priorities in all North American regions.</li>
<li>In conjunction with the Senior Leadership team, develop a vision and strategic plan for the NA sales organization based on past success, current competitive environment, and future market trends.</li>
<li>Advise the Business Development and Open Innovation functions of local market opportunities and facilitate opportunities in the local market.</li>
<li>Contribute to senior management’s knowledge of product/market status through the regular submission of field intelligence and sales reports.</li>
</ul>
<b>People Development Responsibilities:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Identify and grow field sales talent.</li>
<li>Define roles and responsibilities, set performance and development goals, motivate and coach team of direct reports, implement training strategies and sales plans that motivate team execution.</li>
<li>Give feedback to all team members on a regular basis and develop talent within the team to ensure effective succession planning and career development.</li>
</ul>
<b>Experience:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree with a concentration in a marketing related field preferred and 10+ years' experience in Marketing and Marketing leadership, preferably in test and measurement. 5+ years of proven leadership and management of a marketing team. MBA a plus!</li>
<li>Experience in digital marketing and marketing automation.</li>
<li>Experience developing and managing matrixed teams and structures.</li>
<li>Experience with marketing through channel partners, and the design of joint marketing ventures.</li>
<li>Proven strategic planning and business leadership capabilities.</li>
<li>Proven ability to identify, position, develop, lead and retain talented people who are focused on rapid speed of implementation and flawless execution of the strategy and tactics.</li>
<li>Proven ability to develop functional metrics and drive them into the organization so that his/her team effectively and efficiently understands, adopts, and executes them to achieve the results.</li>
<li>Experience driving change, integration and results.</li>
<li>Experience in continuous improvement and lean manufacturing methodologies and principles, especially policy deployment.</li>
<li>Understanding of business financial analysis.</li>
<li>Strong organizational and planning skills.</li>
<li>Effective listening and strong collaboration skills.</li>
<li>High level of organizational savvy.</li>
<li>Ability to drive execution of strategies.</li>
<li>This person should also be creative, objective and possess a high level of integrity.</li>
</ul>
<b>Other Qualifications:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Frequent travel of up to 40%.</li>
<li>All external hiring is contingent upon the successful completion of a pre-employment drug screen and a criminal background check.</li>
</ul>
