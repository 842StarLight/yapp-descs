<h2>Senior Logistics Analyst</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Atkore International Group Inc. is a leading manufacturer of Electrical Raceway products, primarily for the non-residential construction and renovation markets, and Mechanical Products &amp; Solutions for the construction and industrial markets. Our mission is to be the customer’s first choice for electrical raceway and mechanical products and solutions, by providing unmatched quality, delivery and value based on sustainable excellence in strategy, people and processes.</p>
<p>
Our business is comprised of two reporting segments, Electrical Raceway and Mechanical Products &amp; Solutions, with total annual revenue of $1.6 B. Electrical Raceway has 2 business units, Cable Solutions and Conduit &amp; Fittings, totaling just over $1B in annual revenue. Mechanical Products is a stand-alone business unit with about $600M in annual revenue.</p>
<p>
Our global locations include 58 manufacturing and distribution centers in the US, Canada, the United Kingdom, China, Australia, and New Zealand with over 3,600 employees.</p>
<p>
We are currently searching for a Senior Logistics Analyst to be based out of Harvey, Illinois. Harvey located approximately 30 miles south of Downtown Chicago. Reporting to the Director Distribution, Operations and Logistics, this position provides logistics support primarily by assisting in the diagnostics of distribution and transportation activities. Presenting the results of the analysis in various formats to enable the logistics team to reach conclusions and recommendations to reduce costs and improve service.</p>
<p>
The ideal candidate will have a bachelor's degree and minimum of 5 years' transportation and distribution experience. Experience in lean manufacturing or business system based company is a plus.</p>
<p>
Additional responsibilities include, but are not limited to:</p>
<ul>
<li>Develop Standard Work (SW) and document processes.</li>
<li>Establish and monitor KPIs and in-depth “root cause” variance analysis.</li>
<li>Collect, maintain, and manage a data set to continually analyze Supply Chain impacts across all Supply Chain modes on an ongoing and long term basis.</li>
<li>Review freight payment data for compliance, accuracy and perform follow ups as necessary.</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Confer with distribution management and logistics teams to determine ways to optimize service levels, maintain supply-chain efficiency or minimize cost.</li>
<li>Work across functional lines with departments such as Demand Panning, Customer Service, Regional Distribution Centers and Corporate Supply Chain.</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Lead data collection and analysis to draw business decisions and root cause analysis to solve operational problems</li>
<li>Develop and lead transportation cost, carrier performance, routing guide compliance.</li>
<li>Help, support and lead dedicated /fixed run delivery and zone shipping concept for selective products or sales divisions</li>
<li>Support and lead DC network optimization, freight cost optimization for Atkore facilities in North America including running RFP’s for carrier bids.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Qualifications Include:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree required preferable in the field supply chain or logistics.</li>
<li>Minimum of 5 years’ transportation and distribution experience.</li>
<li>Experience working with freight payment data</li>
<li>Experience with collecting and analyzing numerical and statistical data</li>
<li>Strong distribution, transportation and network analysis background.</li>
<li>Demonstrated ability to analyze data, draw conclusions and make recommendations.</li>
<li>Must be advanced with Excel and proficient with PowerPoint and Word.</li>
<li>Able to query data from SQL views and create simple join statements.</li>
<li>Display analysis in Tableau</li>
<li>Ability to train and communicate across all levels of the organization.</li>
<li>JDE experience a plus.</li>
<li>TMS experience a plus.</li>
</ul>
