<h2>Front Desk Agent</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>The Delton Grand Resort &amp; Spa is looking for an energetic, friendly individual to assist at the front desk. Our luxury condo property caters to a wide variety of individuals from all over the world, so we are looking for a highly adaptable individual who can guide and assist a wide customer base.</p>
<p>The successful candidate will have strong sales ability, good organizational skills, and the ability to learn and operate various types of software used in the position. The front desk aspect of the role will require you to be able to close a sale and guide the guests through their stay when they arrive on property. A person with strong self-discipline, able to cooperate in a team environment, plus stay cool under pressure are all qualities we are looking for in this candidate.</p>
<p>All front office staff must have night and weekend availability. Will consider full or part time candidates.</p>
<p>We look forward to hearing from you!</p>
