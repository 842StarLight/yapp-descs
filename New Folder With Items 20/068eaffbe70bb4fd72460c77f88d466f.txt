<h2>Practice Management Specialist - Orthodontics</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>General Summary:</b></p>
<p>
Responsible for working with existing customers, new business owners and prospect DPMS users in an assigned territory to drive business via telephone solicitation of DPMS software, add-on modules and related services. Serve as a subject matter expert for multiple DPMS solutions and perform detailed product demonstrations as part of the sales cycle. While following established strategies, work closely with both management and other areas of the sales organization to achieve and surpass established profitable annual sales goals.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Essential Duties &amp; Responsibilities</b><b>:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Ability to prospect (primarily via telephone) and close business in assigned DPMS product portfolio with a time-sensitive sales cycle</li>
<li>Develop a deep understanding of assigned products with an overall knowledge of all aspects of Carestream Dental's solutions/products</li>
<li>Responsible for maintaining current customer base by placing outbound calls to uncover/analyze customer needs and provide web-based product demonstrations for assigned DPMS products and services offered as add-ons to DPMS product portfolio</li>
<li>Respond to incoming customer calls to obtain orders for various software solutions or services and take a consultative selling approach to not only answer their questions, but look for opportunities to sell them additional products based on their needs</li>
<li>Expand software user base by prospecting for and closing new business for assigned DPMS products</li>
<li>Achieve and surpass established profitable annual sales goals.</li>
<li>Consistently keep a full demo schedule each week and maintain target daily outbound call metrics</li>
<li>Play a critical role in managing all assigned customer accounts and routinely help triage their needs to the correct department or individual who can assist them</li>
<li>Regularly help create and engage in assigned outbound call campaigns to proactively penetrate untapped customer market and seek out new business; this is particularly important with the launch of new DPMS offerings</li>
<li>Serve as the subject matter expert for DPMS sales for all employees and customers/prospects in the assigned territory</li>
<li>Prepare and/or assist others in preparing complex pricing quotes requiring detailed knowledge of company products</li>
<li>Manage and maintain a list of references and competitive DPMS product offerings</li>
<li>Occasionally work trade shows and user conferences within an assigned territory</li>
<li>Perform special duties as directed by management</li>
</ul>
