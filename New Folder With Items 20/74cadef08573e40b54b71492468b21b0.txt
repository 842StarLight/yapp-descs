<h2>Sales Executive</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>
<p><b>Salary:</b> 50-60K + commission. 100K+/yr opportunity with strong performance.</p>
<p><b>Location:</b> Austin, TX</p>
<div>
<p>Looking for an experienced hunter with a killer instinct, never say die attitude and desire to be part of something special. Must be able to represent a complex technology product and sell solutions.
</p>
<p>This is a new business development position and needs a team player who can think and sell outside the box. The primary role is to execute direct sales for purpose of new client acquisition. He/she must develop sales strategies to successfully meet and exceed sales targets and build a profitable book of business.
</p>
<p>Propago is a young SaaS technology startup but the core platform has been proven for over 15 years. Formed by carving out a technology platform from a larger organization gives us a unique perspective on the market and a proven track record. Propago is a Marketing Asset Management platform that streamlines the entire marketing supply chain.
</p>
<ul>
<b>Responsibilities:
</b><li>Create and execute multi-faceted prospecting plan to create and close new client opportunities that meet or exceed monthly acquisition objectives.
</li>
<li>Execute cold calls and outbound email outreach, develop strategic alliances, and effectively utilize networking groups to create new business opportunities.
</li>
<li>Create and execute multi-faceted prospecting plan to create and close new client opportunities that meet or exceed monthly acquisition objectives.
</li>
<li>Develop, along with management, sales plans for achieving revenue targets.
</li>
<li>Input and manage all sales activities and opportunities through Salesforce.com.
</li>
<li>Partner with management and other staff to communicate sales problems and solutions, geographical and industry business observations, and customer/prospect feedback.
</li>
<li>Participate in weekly sales meetings, which will include sales pipeline forecasting and management.
</li>
<li>Positively represent the company to clients and provide excellent customer service.
</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<b>Qualifications:
</b><li>Strong ability to build rapport and network effectively.
</li>
<li>5-10 years of direct sales experience.
</li>
<li>Bachelor's degree.
</li>
<li>Strong "hunter" skills, strong closing skills, ability to communicate and close at the C-level, consultative questioning skills and ability to sell value, strong problem solving skills, good negotiating and conflict resolution skills, and excellent interpersonal skills.

<li>A proven track record of consistently meeting and/or over-performing quota, as well as a proven ability to develop a new book of business from scratch, is required.

<li>Ability to sell in a highly competitive space.

<li>Strong Microsoft Office &amp; CRM skills are required.
</li>
<li>Must be goal-oriented and highly customer focused.
</li>

</li>
