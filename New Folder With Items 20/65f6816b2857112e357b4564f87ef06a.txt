<h2>Data and Systems Analyst</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>We believe work is not a place, but rather a thing you do. Our technology revolves around this core philosophy. We are relentlessly committed to helping people work and play from anywhere, on any device. Innovation, creativity and a passion for ever-improving performance drive our company and our people forward. We empower the original mobile device: YOU!</p>
<p></p>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
What we're looking for:</b></h2>
<p></p>
<p>
This entry-level role works under the close direction of senior team members and focuses on learning and demonstrating knowledge of established concepts in reviewing, analyzing, and evaluating business processes and systems aimed at improving Sales and Marketing processes and performance. Contributes to Marketing Demand Generation processes by entering, scrubbing and validating leads before import into our systems. Creates and runs reports, develops basic applications, automates processes, and maintains data system configuration and architecture. Performs basic systems testing, evaluations, and troubleshooting.</p>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>
Works with Marketing Managers and Vendors to receive lead information, cleanse and enter data, and upload into Enterprise Marketing tools.</li>
<li>
Create data visualizations and dashboards to present information under close direction from senior team members. Conduct data analysis and prepare findings.</li>
<li>
Automates manual processes to improve quality, efficiencies, and performance</li>
<li>
Develop and configure basic applications with guidance from senior team members</li>
<li>
Identify data discrepancies, escalating issues to senior team members when necessary.</li>
<li>
Raise visibility for findings related to improving Marketing, Sales and Business Operations, escalating issues when appropriate.</li>
<li>
Participate in issue resolution for production issues under close direction from senior team members.</li>
<li>
Maintain data quality by evaluating and improving business processes that capture information.</li>
<li>
Resolve system issues that have been assigned, prioritizing as necessary, and escalate challenges when appropriate.</li>
<li>
Perform User Acceptance Testing by following test scripts, plans, and procedures. Document test results in a standardized format.</li>
<li>
Execute project tasks as assigned.</li>
</ul>

<ul><li>LI-LS17, Fort Lauderdale, FL</li></ul>
