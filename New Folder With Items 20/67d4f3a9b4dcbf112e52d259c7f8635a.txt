Job Description</div>
<div>
We’re looking for someone with a passion for media and marketing partnerships, to lead account services for government campaigns and contracts. This role is a critical internal liaison to ensure client goals are understood and brought to life across multiple media platforms working through internal teams and systems to bring campaigns flawlessly to life! The right candidate for this role loves managing multiple people, projects, tasks – works quickly and accurately and owns using their role is the internal conductor to ensure client goals are met and exceeded while keeping internal teams well aware of campaign status through frequent and detailed communication.</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
This position will closely with Government Partnerships Manager &amp; Specialist to understand client goals, as well as all departments of Entercom’s Marketing Solutions team, including: Media Strategy &amp; Planning, Research, Product, Editorial, Digital, Live Events, Activation and Design to lead campaign implementation for government clients across the country. Campaign Activation Manager will work collaboratively with and through internal teams to ensure we implement campaigns that routinely over deliver against client expectations.</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
Key Responsibilities</div>
<div><ul>
<li>
Proactively oversee client campaign activation – across all media channels – working with internal teams and external vendors to ensure flawless campaign execution and achievement of client goals</li>
<li>
Interface continually with Campaign Strategist, Government Partnerships Manager &amp; Specialist to understand client needs and campaign goals</li>
<li>
Internal liaison for all departments to ensure successful placement and implementation of client deliverables</li>
<li>
Act as media representative for all post-sale correspondence – internally and externally – including key point of contact for all campaign insertion orders</li>
<li>
Maintain post sale deliverables including pulling pre/post logs, taking screen shots, and managing traffic instructions</li>
<li>
Diligently and accurately communicate all invoicing and billing requirements to internal Accounting team – ensuring proper order entry, financial reporting and ease of billing reconciliation and reporting to clients</li>
<li>
Actively participate in brainstorming &amp; optimization sessions to aid with client campaign success</li>
<li>
Actively guide the optimization of all media and campaign elements continually throughout the life of the campaign</li>
<li>
Provide consistent and clear communication with management and all campaign stakeholders on the status of campaigns</li>
</ul></div>
