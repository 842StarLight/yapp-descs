<h2>Director, Creative Services</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b><i>If you’re passionate about becoming a Nationwide associate and believe you have the potential to be something great, let’s talk.
</i></b></p>
<p></p>
<p><b><i>Number 57 on the Fortune Magazine 100 Best Places to Work.
</i></b></p>Summary: Directs the conceptual approach, production, development and implementation of multiple communications and creative strategies for internal and external communications in support of business goals and initiatives. Directs all creative service activities including content, graphics, photography, video and live events, along with other new electronic and multimedia opportunities. Responsible for setting a department vision and guiding managers and staff toward successfully reaching that vision.
<p></p>
<p><b>Job Description
</b></p>
<p><b>Role Summary
</b></p>
<p>The Creative Director will lead a creative team supporting various business lines throughout Nationwide. They will direct creative strategy, idea generation and execution of sales generating campaigns and content in support of Nationwide business objectives.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Key responsibilities
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Responsible for the overall holistic creative output produced by the team, including content and design. This includes creative strategy, idea generation and execution of individual tactics. Work is primarily external facing advertising, sales collateral and campaign and content development.
</li>
<li>Consults with project leaders and partners to ensure creative work delivers on business objectives and provides measurable results. Ensures clients are getting best-in-class creative work that is an accurate expression of the Nationwide brand.
</li>
<li>Articulates team vision and guides associates toward successfully reaching that vision. Establishes a creative culture where an open exchange of ideas is valued. Works to assure team is current on industry trends and technology.
</li>
<li>Responsible for performance management, salary planning, training and development, process and organizational planning and recruiting and retaining staff.
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Reporting Relationships: </b>Reports to AVP level. Responsible for 2 or more professional level direct reports. May be responsible for matrix reporting relationships and virtual teams.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Typical skills and experiences
</b></p>
<p><b>Education </b>: Degree in Marketing, Advertising, Communication, Fine Arts or Graphics Arts.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>License: </b>Series 6 &amp; 26 licenses (Required)
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Experience </b>: 10 or more years related experience in progressively responsible creative positions; including five years of creative management experience.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Skills </b>: Creative idea generation, technical understanding of creative and content development, ability to coach and guide creatives with award winning level of creative output. Strong leadership and organizational management. Relationship management. Strong written and verbal communications. Command skills to engage with business partners and team.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Core Duties and Responsibilities continued:
</b></p>
<p></p>
<p>1. Directs consultation to senior management and project leaders on internal and external creative service issues and programs to ensure that creative service plans support the business unit and/or company strategic initiatives and objectives. Responsible for the overall holistic creative output looking at the totality of the communications including the design and content.<br>
</p>
<p><br>
2. Directs the planning, development and implementation of complex, strategic creative services programs. Responsible for the development and consultation to senior management on strategic and tactical creative service plans for the organization and/or specified business unit(s).Recommends best practices based on metrics (what worked, what didn't work, how to improve).<br>
</p>
<p><br>
3. Directs a multi-functional creative services staff; provides direction on production methods; develops and implements workflow processes; and, adheres to established time standards while maintaining quality. Directs cross-functional teams to develop creative communications solutions.<br>
</p>
<p><br>
4. Directs the production of multiple communications solutions for internal/external audiences such as: web based products and productions, presentations and other audio/visual programs; the production of video and electronic and print media productions; the planning and production of photography for multiple uses, web design; graphic design; live events.<br>
</p>
<p><br>
5. Interacts with senior leadership presenting creative proposals. Directs cross-functional teams to develop communications. Consults with managers, editors, and writers to resolve graphic and audio-visual problems. Responsible for effective use of social media as extension of creative strategy.<br>
</p>
<p><br>
6. Reviews current technology in fields of web development and graphic design, photography, video and live events; identifies improved methods, materials, and equipment, and equipment that may benefit department productivity.<br>
</p>
<p><br>
7. Responsible for multiple production project budgets and responsible for adherence to the overall department budget.<br>
</p>
<p><br>
8. Directs and negotiates all hiring and coordination of outside resources including contractors, freelancers, photographers, audio/visual vendors, special events coordinators.<br>
</p>
<p><br>
9. Directs associate management including: performance management, salary planning and administration, training and development, workflow and organizational planning, hiring and placement, and disciplinary actions.Performs other duties as assigned.<br>
<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Skills/Competencies: </b>Customer-focused orientation and excellent verbal and written communication skills for the creation and delivery of communication projects and interaction with management and creative service teams. Must have demonstrated proficiency with personal computers and related software. Demonstrates an ongoing commitment to education, training, and development activities. Must have the ability to set priorities, manage time, organize work and meet deadlines. Must have the ability to articulate a vision and drive the team to successful and measurable results. Must be comfortable directing large cross-functions teams toward a common goal. Must be able to research, plan and develop creative strategies, projects and programs. Must have the ability to influence and direct creative service teams that are not direct reports and to influence executive leaders.Other criteria, including leadership skills, competencies and experiences may take precedence.Staffing exceptions to the above must be approved by the hiring manager’s leader and HR Business Partner.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Values: </b>Regularly and consistently demonstrates the Nationwide Values and Guiding Behaviors.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Job Conditions:
</b></p>
<p><b>Overtime Eligibility: </b>Not Eligible (Exempt).
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Working Conditions: </b>Production environment. Moderate travel. Extended/nonstandard hours
</p>
