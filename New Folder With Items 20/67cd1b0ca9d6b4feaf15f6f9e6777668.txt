<h2>Principal Architect - Unified Communication and Collaboration</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p><b>What you’ll be doing...</b>
</p>
<p>As an Advanced Communications Principal Architect in Verizon Business Group (VBG) within the Advanced Technology Group (ATG), you will be responsible for acting as the technical presales expert, primarily focused on engaging with clients, contributing technical elements to Product Management, supporting the ATG Solution Architect team in complex solutions and driving sales opportunities to closure by providing technical design and validation.</p>
<ul>
<li>
Responsible for articulating and designing solutions for the latest technology and market trends and relating them to Verizon Unified Communications and Collaboration product features. Your goal is to ensure that our internal and external clients are equipped with the latest reference design architectures, and are following best practices</li>
<li>
Design and/or implement UC technology for a range of enterprise customers, and would like to further enhance their skills, tenure and experience while sharing their passion for technology</li>
<li>
Serve as pre-sales engineering and technical expert in Verizon’s IP Telephony, Unified Communications and Collaborations solutions portfolio, including associated hardware, software, licensing, professional services, and network architecture</li>
<li>
Work with the Solution Architects and account teams as the lead UC subject matter expert to lead customer discussions, develop solutions and recommend Verizon products and services to meet customer’s business requirements</li>
<li>
Create and deliver customer facing materials, both formal and informal at various levels, including leading detailed technical discussions as well as delivering business-focused C-Level presentations</li>
<li>
Work with CISCO Webex teams &amp; Jabber- designing solutions, encompassing Cisco collaboration suite for meetings, messaging and calling. Cisco Webex teams Board for Room and Admin functionality encompassing security, compliance and analytics solution review. Leading to design validation for provisioning, management and Professional Services support</li>
<li>
Solid understanding of network design best practices convergence (QoS/CoS), routing (BGP, EIGRP) and switching (VLAN trunking, Ethernet) with focus on resiliency, availability, and latency</li>
<li>
Knowledge of all aspects of IP Telephony and UC&amp;C solutions:<ul>
<li>
Station- and/or trunk –side SIP designs including a detailed understanding of SIP Messaging and Call Flows</li>
<li>
Experience designing and engineering technical solutions containing IP-PBX components</li>
<li>
Session Border Control design</li>
<li>
Determine customer requirements, and designing hosted UC solutions leveraging cloud based architectures, such as: Unified Communications as a Service (UCCaaS)</li>
<li>
Video (HD) and Telepresence design</li>
<li>
Webex, Microsoft, Avaya and Zoom collaboration suite.</li>
</ul>
</li>
</ul>
