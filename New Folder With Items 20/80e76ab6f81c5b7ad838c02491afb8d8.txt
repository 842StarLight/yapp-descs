<p>Our client is a rapidly growing, well-funded software company in Chicago’s West Loop area.</p>
<p>
The environment is fast-paced, yet casual and friendly for those ready to roll up their sleeves and get the job done. If you are looking for a chance to begin a career in the software industry and join an expanding company, I encourage you to apply.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
The base salary is $40,000 and $50,000 total. </b><b>There is NO COLD CALLING.</b></p>
<p><b>
Product Support Specialist Job Description:</b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
Our Product Support Specialists are critical to the retention of our growing customer base. This job entails: (1) Reactive dealership problem resolution (with definitive escalation procedures), (2) Proactive system upgrade training and account management calls to current customers, and (3) Support of field teams (Sales and Account Managers) for research, system information, and customer relationship management.</p>
<p><b>
Specific Duties and Responsibilities</b>:</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Professionally engage all dealership end users of the performance management system to maximize their usage and loyalty.</li>
<li>Maintain and enhance visibility, rapport and relationship within the Dealer partners Action all inbound help desk calls and e-mails, provide updates to customer during remediation and follow up after solution in place</li>
<li>Promote fullest utilization of business tools</li>
<li>Problem solve and document account problem resolution</li>
<li>Document trends, FAQ’s, and related troubleshooting</li>
<li>Provide entrepreneurial balance through <b>strong work ethic</b>, teamwork, accountability, excellent interdepartmental communication and a “Whatever it takes” winning attitude</li>
<li>Available on phone call rotations during after-hours, which you can do from your home (One day per week including one weekend every six weeks)</li>
<li>Self-manage customer case queue</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Qualifications:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree required</li>
<li>Strong and demonstrated work ethic – willingness to go above and beyond</li>
<li>Excellent communication, presentation, and customer service skills</li>
<li>Proficient personal computing expertise</li>
<li>Proven ability and comfort with learning new technologies quickly</li>
<li>Local Chicago candidates only- no relocation will be provided</li>
<li>1-2 years of professional work experience preferred</li>
</ul>
