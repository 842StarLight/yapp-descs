<b>Position Title:</b> Wealth Advisor 2
<br><b>Reports To (Title):</b> Director of Wealth Management Department: Five Star Investment Services
<br><b>FLSA Status:</b> Exempt
<br><b>Purpose:</b> The Wealth Advisor 2 is responsible for working with a diverse clientele within assigned territory including individuals, business owners, professionals and families in providing a broad range of products and services to assist with attaining financial objectives. The Wealth Advisor 2 is also responsible for collaborating with internal business partners in the referral process to meet additional client needs.
<br><br>
Scope and Direction
<br><br>
<b>Area of Responsibility:</b> Sales for Five Star Investment Services

<br><br>
Degree of Supervision Given (None, Minimal, Moderate or Extensive): None
<br><b>To (Title(s)):</b> N/A
<br><br>
<b>Essential Functions:</b> The major duties of the job that constitute the fundamental tasks and or responsibilities of the position. Employees must be able to perform the essential functions of the position with or without reasonable accommodations.
<br>1. Meets or exceeds established production goals by consulting with clients to determine needs and suggest investment strategies to meet those needs. Thoroughly explains the investment products available, disclosing the risks and costs of the various options. Focuses on investment balance, diversification and asset allocation.
<br><br>
2. Responsible for accurate and timely completion of client documentation necessary to open accounts and complete purchases.
<br><br>
3. Works directly with LPL Financial on issues and complaints related to client accounts.
<br>4. Collaborates with internal bank business partners by cross-selling appropriate bank products and receiving/submitting referrals as appropriate.
<br><br>
5. Mentors and assists Wealth Consultants and other Wealth Advisors with client recommendations and required documentation.
<br><br>
6. Provides training and assistance to Retail Branch employees and Lenders on investment products and services to obtain referrals.
<br>7. Consistently complies with the Financial Industry Regulatory Authority (FINRA) and compliance guidelines while maintaining current knowledge of industry through attendance at investment meetings, seminars and trainings.
<br>8. Demonstrates the standards and principles of the Five Star Bank Experience in every interaction with internal and external customers and associates. Incorporates the high performance behaviors of teamwork, leading by example and service in every facet of work.
<br>9. This job description is not exhaustive. The Wealth Advisor 2 may be required to perform other duties as assigned.
