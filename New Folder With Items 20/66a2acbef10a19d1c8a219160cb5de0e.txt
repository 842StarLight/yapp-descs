<h2>Retirement Plan Account Executive</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p>The Retirement Plan Account Executive is expected to contribute to the growth of the financial services department by supporting the Sales and Service efforts. The Account Executive will have extensive contact with the client as an extension of the producer. Responsible for acting as quarterback for the team.
 </p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Duties and Responsibilities:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Develops relationships beyond the Producer’s relationship with the key decision makers
</li>
<li>Directs marketing/RFP/plan implementation/education efforts
</li>
<li>Assist in developing, implementing and monitoring of on-going service model
</li>
<li>Presents renewal and/or new proposals to clients
</li>
<li>Schedules and leads client meetings and attends open enrollments if necessary
</li>
<li>Coordinates and directs efforts of team members
</li>
<li>Strategic planning with producer regarding plan design, servicing and education strategies
</li>
<li>Meets with clients on a quarterly or on an” as needed” basis
</li>
<li>Rounds accounts, especially voluntary and employee benefits
</li>
<li>Makes sure the client is aware of resources that HUB offers (minimum annually)
</li>
<li>Must follow Maritime/HUB Standards
</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Key Competencies:
</b></p>
<p>To perform the job successfully, an individual should demonstrate the following competencies:
</p>
<ul>
<li>Team Work – Supports everyone's efforts to succeed.
</li>
<li>Written/Oral Communication - Writes and speaks clearly and informatively; Edits work for spelling and grammar; Varies writing and speaking style to meet needs; Presents numerical data effectively; Able to read and interpret written information. Ability to effectively communicate complex concepts in an easy-to-understand manner.
</li>
<li>Quality Management – Demonstrates attention to detail.
</li>
<li>Project Management – Ability to lead a team and adhere to deadlines.
</li>
<li>Self-Starter- Ability to work independently and complete work efficiently and effectively.
</li>
</ul>

</b></p>
<p></p>

<p><b>Experience and Education
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree preferred.
</li>
<li>At least 5-10 years of related experience working as a Relationship Manager (or similar position) in the retirement plan arena (equivalent combination of education and experience is acceptable).
</li>
<li>Series 6/63 Securities License required (Series 7 and 66 preferred)
</li>
<li>L&amp;H License required
</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Physical Requirements:
</b></p>
<p></p>
<p>The physical demands described here are representative of those that must be met by an employee to successfully perform the essential functions of this job. Reasonable accommodations may be made to enable individuals with disabilities to perform the essential functions. While performing the duties of this Job, the employee is regularly required to talk or hear. The employee is frequently required to stand; walk; sit and use hands to finger, handle, or feel. The employee must occasionally lift and/or move up to 25 pounds. Specific vision abilities required by this job include Close vision and Distance vision. Some travel required by car to meet with clients and other outside events.

</li>
