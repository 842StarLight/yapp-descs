<h2>2020 - Winter - Tax Associate - State and Local</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>At RSM, associates work with large and small companies in various industries. They develop strong working relationships with clients built on understanding their businesses and challenges. Associates work on multiple team engagements each year, including several pieces of any particular assignment - not just one part. Working in a mutually respectful team environment helps our associates perform at their best and integrate their career with their personal life.</div>
<p><b>
Examples of the candidate’s responsibility include:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Integrating state and local tax services with existing clients</li>
<li>
Completing tax planning and research tasks</li>
<li>
Developing an understanding of state and local tax regulations and accounting pronouncements</li>
<li>
Reviewing client’s current tax processes to identify inefficiencies and provide enhancements to improve the clients’ procedures and reduce tax exposure</li>
<li>
Working with the client’s information technology personnel to obtain data of historical purchasing and sales to analyze for reporting requirements, audit procedures, and review for potential refund or credits</li>
<li>
Assisting the client with evaluating sales/use tax software systems that meet their compliance needs and support the implementation process</li>
</ul>
<div><b>
Basic Qualifications:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
Minimum B.A. or B.S. degree or equivalent from an accredited university</li>
<li>
Accounting major</li>
<li>
Travel to assigned client locations is required. Must have access to and ability to arrange for use of reliable modes of transportation to those locations</li>
<li>
Minimum 3.0 GPA preferred</li>
<li>
Meeting education requirement for CPA certification in the state where you are applying for employment</li>
</ul>
<div><b>
Preferred Qualifications:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
Working toward the successful completion of the CPA exam - tax associates have the opportunity to obtain the audit hours required for full certification</li>
<li>
Excellent written and verbal communication skills</li>
<li>
Strong computer skills, including proficiency in Microsoft Excel</li>
<li>
Ability to work effectively on a team</li>
<li>
Ability to work and multitask in a fast-paced environment</li>
</ul>
