<h2>Account Executive</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p><b>Who are we?</b></p>
<p>
Elite Transit Solutions, LLC. has developed an automation focused software to virtually eliminate second guessing and human error for our customers as we provide cost efficient transportation solutions. Our processes are not only innovative and industry changing, but they allow our team members to use their talents to focus on providing strategy driven solutions to our customer and carrier network, while fulfilling themselves with career growth.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Job brief:</b></p>
<p>
Account Executives (AE) are responsible for winning new customers via strategic sales prospecting and nurturing existing customers to meet sales quotas. An AE is directly responsible for identifying business opportunities while maintaining current customer relationships. The position requires skills needed to execute the entire sales process and deliver outstanding customer service. An AE is expected to be a reliable professional who balances a results-driven sales focus with a priority of maintaining an outstanding customer experience.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Roles and Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Achieve assigned sales goals for revenue and profitability</li>
<li>Achieve goals for new customer wins</li>
<li>Increase sales to existing customers</li>
<li>Retain existing customers by utilizing proactive approach to customer needs with appropriate solutions.</li>
<li>Seek win-win relationships to grow sales and resolve issues</li>
<li>Maintain accurate sales records, sales reports and customer information in the sales database</li>
<li>Develop and remain current on industry and market trends with objective of industry expertise</li>
<li>Develop and remain current on prospecting techniques, sale skills, and best in class customer service.</li>
<li>Demonstrate professionalism in the workplace</li>
<li>Contribute to the management of day to day operations by striving to ensure customers have the following experience with our service:</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p>
</p>
<p>The Right Product · In the Right Condition · At the Right Place · For the Right Price<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Skills and Requirements:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>3-5 years sales experience</li>
<li>Experience or working knowledge of the logistics market is a plus</li>
<li>Excellent interpersonal skills</li>
<li>Excellent communication skills - written and verbal</li>
<li>Driven to achieve outstanding results</li>
<li>Strong desire to serve customer needs</li>
<li>Demonstrable business acumen and a deep understanding of business sales process</li>
<li>4-year degree from accredited university</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p>
</p>
<p>Roles are managed through specific KPI’s based upon business requirements as deemed appropriate.</p>
