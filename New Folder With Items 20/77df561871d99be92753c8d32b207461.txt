<h2>Telesales Representative</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Overview</b></p>
<p>MDSL are world leaders in Technology Expense Management and partner with enterprises to provide clarity, control, compliance, and cost savings across their global technology estates.</p>
<p>We are seeking individuals with a background in telemarketing and/or telesales to uncover target contacts within MDSL prospective accounts, nurture relationships, and deliver qualified opportunities to our regional sales managers. These individuals should be friendly, well-spoken, and have a knack for navigating enterprise hierarchies to cultivate interest in MDSL software and services. Qualified candidates will understand how to keep a pulse on top of funnel marketing leads until being ready to convert into qualified selling opportunities.</p>
<p><b>Duties</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Research accounts, identify key players and generate interest</li>
<li>Source new sales opportunities through inbound lead follow-up and outbound cold calls and emails</li>
<li>Build rapport with key stakeholders at prospective accounts and maintain consistent communication</li>
<li>Understand prospect needs and requirements so MDSL is recognized as a viable option</li>
<li>Route qualified opportunities to the appropriate sales executives for further development and closure</li>
<li>Maintain and expand your database of prospects within your assigned territory</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Qualifications</b></p>
<ul>
<li>2+ years of business development experience</li>
<li>Experience building relationships within enterprise-sized organizations</li>
<li>Comfortable making 100+ outbound calls daily and taking inbound calls</li>
<li>Ability to work independently and think creatively to drive new opportunities</li>
<li>Technologically curious, within the tools we use and the tools we sell</li>
<li>Possess strong communication and presentation skills</li>
<li>Organize and manage/maintain projects from conception through execution</li>
<li>Ability to work with cross-functional groups across the organization to deliver key project requirements</li>
<li>Experience with various CRMs, MATs, and dialer technologies</li>
<li>Understanding of Sales and Marketing alignment to execute a consistent message</li>
<li>Strong understanding of B2B enterprise sales</li>
<li>Telecommunications experience a plus</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Attributes</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Great written and verbal communication</li>
<li>Ability to learn</li>
<li>Technologically savvy</li>
<li>Self-motivated and can work independently</li>
</ul>
