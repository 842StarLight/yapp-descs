<h2>Event Sales Leader</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">GateHouse Live
<br>Event Sales Leader
<br>Summary
<br>Lead day-to-day sales objectives for strategies and execution. Develop processes and materials that provides efficiency to local markets. Extensive travel required.
<br>Responsibilities
<br><ul>
<li>Sell and execute events in conjunction with current year's business plan and budget;</li>
<li>Manage and provide leadership to local markets on each event to ensure that staffs are fully supported to accomplish tasks required for successful events;</li>
<li>Partner closely with executives and cross-functional teams to facilitate trust, communication and collaboration;</li>
<li>Ensure implementation of event objectives;</li>
<li>Develop sales packages, contracts, presentations and lead lists;</li>
<li>Sell and support event sponsorships;</li>
<li>Collaborate with local Marketing Director to ensure all sponsorship obligations are met;</li>
<li>Work with Brand Manager to create sales materials including flyers and PowerPoints;</li>
<li>Launch event sales initiatives to local sales staffs;</li>
<li>Train local sales staffs on event sales;</li>
<li>Monitor goal tracking in local markets for event sales;</li>
<li>Manage CRM tool;</li>
<li>Make in-market blitz calls as needed;</li>
<li>Develop commissions plans for events;</li>
<li>Develop incentive programs for event sales;</li>
<li>Manage event set-up and execution;</li>
<li>Develop systems and processes to ensure consistent, efficient and effective communication;</li>
<li>Research and monitor trends in newspaper and events industry;</li>
<li>Extensive travel required.</li>
</ul>
Skills and Experience
<br><ul>
<li>5+ years of experience in sales, ideally event sales;</li>
<li>Bachelor's Degree in marketing, public relations or related;</li>
<li>Strong communication skills, attention to detail, and ability to prioritize;</li>
<li>Proficiency in MS Office;</li>
<li>Excellent time management skills;</li>
<li>Strong understanding of marketing principles and advertising strategy;</li>
<li>Strong business expertise, with a high bar set on quality;</li>
<li>Able to think both strategically and operationally.</li>
</ul>
