<b>
Position Objective</b>
</div>
<div>
<div>The Healthcare IT Services Consultant (ITSC) role is market-based sales and business development position. An ITSC may cover a single large market or multiple smaller markets within the region. The primary responsibilities of the role are to help grow All Covered Revenue Cycle Management Services sales through presenting and closing:</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<ul><li>Recurring professional, managed Revenue Cycle Management Solutions which are part of the Healthcare services portfolio to Healthcare customers</li></ul>
<div></div>
<br>
<ul><li>Strategic and complex RCM project to prospects and existing Healthcare clients.</li></ul>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
These sales are consultative to our prospects and customers, and collaborative internally. ITSC’s rely on operations, delivery, client management, executive and other sales resources throughout various stages of an opportunity’s progression.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
ITSC’s may work closely with the local Konica Minolta Business Solutions sales team and their customers to cross-sell our Healthcare specific services for the mutual benefit of each division.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
Additionally, ITSC’s develop their territories through social media, business development initiatives and networking activities such as mixers, tradeshows, client events, Better Business Bureau and Chamber of Commerce initiatives, industry associations events, etc. as well as by soliciting references from existing All Covered customers.</div>
</div>
</div>

<b>
Essential Job Functions</b>
</div>
<div><ul>
<li>Apply an in-depth understanding of All Covered Revenue Cycle Management (RCM) services, programs, and sales methodology to address complex and strategic RCM Services opportunities.</li>
<li>
Work with Solution Architects, Strategic Account Managers, engineering project resources, sales and management resources to achieve and exceed quota.</li>
<li>
Work with Konica Minolta branch offices, as assigned, to cross-sell IT Services to their client and prospect base.</li>
<li>
Provide thorough and accurate insight into the financial results and forecast of sales of strategic and complex ACC, project services.</li>
<li>
With support from marketing &amp; channel strategy resources, present special programs to prospects and All Covered Care clients.</li>
<li>
Develop a thorough understanding of how All Covered’s Healthcare Practice’s RCM solutions and services create value for customers.</li>
<li>
Drive All Covered’s RCM Services in assigned area of responsibility to grow market share with; recurring monthly services and project services.</li>
<li>
Maintain consistent interaction &amp; communication with team members and clients.</li>
<li>
Work closely with Managers, Solutions Architects, and Client Account Managers to create and sell appropriate solutions to prospects.</li>
</ul></div>
