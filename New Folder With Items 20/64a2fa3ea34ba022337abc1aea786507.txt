<h2>Leasing Consultant</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Seeking someone who is motivated, hardworking, dependable and has the desire to be part of an AMAZING TEAM!</p>
<p>FUNCTIONS OF THIS JOB INCLUDE:</p>
<ul>
<li>General office duties.</li>
<li>Assist in lease transactions by typing leases, gathering applicant history and credit approvals.</li>
<li>Provide general clerical assistance to the community office (i.e., answer telephone, writing work orders, maintaining green files).</li>
<li>Community Leasing.</li>
<li>Greet prospects and determine needs and wants. Complete guest card.</li>
<li>Tour property by showing model or ready vacant apartments. Have prospect complete all application paperwork and secure deposit and application fee. Update Weekly Activity Status Report.</li>
<li>Obtain required information to process applications for approval (i.e., credit check, rental history and employment verification).</li>
<li>Once approved by Property Manager, prepare lease and applicable addendum. Insure apartment is ready for move-in on agreed date.</li>
<li>Follow up on prospects unable to close (i.e., phone calls, thank you notes and e-mails) attempt to close sale again. If not viable prospect for our community, work to send the prospect to a sister property to meet their needs without losing them to a competitor.</li>
<li>Secure new resident signature(s) on appropriate paperwork prior to move-in.</li>
<li>Review and orient new residents to the communityâ€™s specific rules, regulations and policies.</li>
<li>Monitor Renewals. Distribute and follow-up on renewals on a daily basis until finalized.</li>
<li>Write and distribute resident notices as needed.</li>
<li>Resident Relations.</li>
<li>Residents may call or visit in person and will require the Leasing Professional to listen respectfully to their requests, complaints and comments.</li>
<li>Respond accordingly to rectify the situation after discussing with the Property Manager when required. Ensure consistency with Residents on all matters.</li>
<li>Enforce Community Policies.</li>
<li>Community Marketing.</li>
<li>Update all web-sites with current availability and pricing.</li>
<li>Place multiple daily ads on Craigslist.com, Facebook and other online advertising mediums.</li>
<li>Distribute resident flyers promoting Resident Referral Fees &amp; various community events or promotions.</li>
<li>Assist in placing, removing and updating all banners, balloons, bandit signs and flags around the property and other locations where outward marketing is performed.</li>
<li>Distribute newsletters, flyers and pamphlets.</li>
<li>Shop competitors monthly. Keep updated market surveys.</li>
<li>Suggest specials and/or pricing modifications based on market surveys to stay competitive.</li>
<li>Attendance is an essential job function.</li>
</ul>
