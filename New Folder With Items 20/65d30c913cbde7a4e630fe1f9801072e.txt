<h2>Major Account Executive II - Enterprise Accounts East</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>What We'll Bring:
</b></p>At TransUnion, we have a welcoming and energetic environment that encourages collaboration and innovation. We are consistently exploring new technologies and tools to be agile. This environment gives our people the opportunity to hone current skills and build new capabilities, while discovering their genius.
<br>
<br>
Come be a part of our team – you’ll work with great people, pioneering products and cutting-edge technology.
<br>
<br>
Preferred Location: Philadelphia, PA/Wilmington, DE
<p></p>
<p><b>What You'll Bring:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Experience in customer relationship management and sales experience in financial services or credit industry.
</li>
<li>Significant credit risk or marketing analytic operations experience.
</li>
<li>Effective communication skills to all levels of the buying organization including C-level executives.
</li>
<li>A 4-year college degree required, preferably with a business focus.
</li>
<li>Ability to travel 50% of the time.
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>We’d Love to See:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Analytic experience and demonstrated strong acumen in applying analytics to solve client business issues and challenges.
</li>
<li>Demonstrated presentation skills.
</li>
<li>Knowledge of fraud, ID Management, collections and credit decisioning tools used in the financial services industry.
</li>
<li>Demonstrated ability to lead external client planning process to uncover critical business needs requiring appropriate solution implementation to solve for the issue.
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Impact You'll Make:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>In the first 90 days, you’ll focus on acquiring deep understanding of TransUnion, the solutions, the clients and industry as a whole.
</li>
<li>Beyond 90 days, you’ll begin to be fully engaged with clients assessing, leading and facilitating all business development associates and subject matter experts to identify, sell, close and manage existing projects in a collaborative manner.
</li>
<li>You will be responsible for growing and protecting revenue in our current portfolio of business solutions deployed within the specific territory of Enterprise Accounts as well as building out a pipeline for additional revenue opportunities with these clients.
</li>
</ul>
