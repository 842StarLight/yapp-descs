<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>What you’ll be doing:</b></h3>
<ul>
<li>Prepares and submits bi-weekly payroll</li>
<li>Compiles and enters payroll data such as hours worked, taxes, insurance, and employee identification numbers from time sheets and other records</li>
<li>Reviews wages computed and corrects errors to ensure accuracy of payroll</li>
<li>Inputs changes, which may include changes to employees or employee statuses, exemptions or withholdings, or bank information.</li>
<li>Reconciles payroll-related general ledger and prepares reports or statements summarizing payroll-related accounts</li>
<li>Prepares periodic reports of earnings, taxes and deductions</li>
<li>Answers employee questions related to payroll and resolves issues</li>
<li>Verifies new employee set up</li>
<li>Provide administration of employee wage garnishments/assignments and handling</li>
<li>Helps to ensure payroll practices meet government compliance</li>
<li>Process 401(k) elections, set up deductions and monitor reports to ensure proper deductions are taken and reported</li>
<li>Sets up and maintains all benefit payroll deductions</li>
<li>Prepare year end payroll tax summaries, including W2s, and approves for distribution</li>
<li>Prepares and files quarterly payroll tax reports for federal and multiple states</li>
<li>Comply with local, state and federal payroll regulations and respond to questions and special requests from regulatory agencies</li>
<li>Assists with special projects, as necessary</li>
<li>Continuously conduct payroll analyses to ensure accuracy</li>
<li>Ensures the accurate processing of wage and other compensation payments to employees.</li>
<li>Keeps Accounting Manager informed on all significant matters affecting financial operations, accounting, payroll, and data processing activities</li>
<li>Maintains financial security by following internal payroll and accounting controls</li>
</ul>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>KNOWLEDGE, SKILLS AND ABILITIES:</b></h3>
<ul>
<li>Ability to read and interpret documents such as financial reports, safety rules, operating and maintenance instructions, and procedure manuals</li>
<li>Ability to calculate figures and amounts such as tax, discounts, interest, commissions, proportions, percentages, area, circumference, and volume</li>
<li>Detail oriented, organized, and efficient</li>
<li>Ability to multi-task, establish priorities</li>
<li>Strong software skills, especially Microsoft Word and Excel</li>
<li>Excellent writing, editing, and communication skills</li>
<li>Tactful in their dealings with people using good customer service and interpersonal skills, communicating effectively with clients and managers</li>
<li>Ability to establish and maintain professional working relationships</li>
<li>Knowledge of Sales and Use Tax is preferred</li>
</ul>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>What you’ll bring to the table:</b></h3>
<ul>
<li>Associate degree in Accounting, HR or another related field</li>
<li>Minimum of two years in a payroll administration role</li>
<li>Proficient computer skills</li>
</ul>
