<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Company Overview
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div>Nuance Communications, Inc. is the pioneer and leader in conversational AI innovations that bring intelligence to everyday work and life. The company delivers solutions that understand, analyze and respond to human language, amplifying human intelligence. With decades of domain and artificial intelligence expertise, Nuance works with thousands of organizations – in healthcare, telecommunications, automotive, financial services, retail, and more – to create stronger relationships and better experiences for their customers.<br>
<br>
Join our <b>Enterprise</b> team…great customer service starts here. We design virtual assistants for intelligent and effortless customer service helping customers find the information they need using whatever channel they prefer.</div>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Job Summary
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div>Summary: Innovation is a critical element to our Digital differentiation, and this product manager will ensure that take advantage of internal and external solutions that are driving value in Intelligent Engagement. Also key to the Nuance strategy is to deliver more of our solutions via partners, and this person will identify and nurture critical partnerships, then ensure that we have the necessary APIs, SDKs, packages, and infrastructure to grow revenue through the partner channel. This role requires working closely with cross-functional teams to understand market needs and opportunities, manage product requirements, author user stories, and support new product introductions with internal and external stakeholders.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
Responsibilities:</div>
<ul>
<li>
As a thought leader, drive a multi-year packaging and partnership solution strategy that supports continued growth and evolution of Nuance’s digital Intelligent Engagement solutions</li>
<li>
Conduct market, customer, and competitive research to inform strategic direction</li>
<li>
Define overall product growth strategy and product roadmap, supported by a business plan</li>
<li>
Expand the productization of innovation in our portfolio and across channels</li>
<li>
Lead customer and internal steering meetings to inform and vet the product strategy and roadmap</li>
<li>
Conduct build versus buy analysis</li>
<li>
Define release goals, features, user stories, and acceptance criteria</li>
<li>
Support the R&amp;D team throughout the Agile software development process</li>
<li>
Actively assist in promoting and selling system to customers, partners, press, and analysts</li>
<li>
Monitor KPIs of product success and take corrective action as needed</li>
<li>
Ensure compliance with data privacy and security</li>
</ul>
<div>

Number of Years of Work Experience: 8+</div>
