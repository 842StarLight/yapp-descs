<p></p>
<br>
<div>
The Senior Accountant’s primary focuses are internal reporting &amp; analysis, the month-end close cycle, and general accounting as it relates to payroll, inventory, accounts payable and certain accounts receivable balances. They will ideally bring any technical accounting to include GL Management, ASC-606: Revenue Recognition, ASC-842: Lease Accounting, Financial Statements and MD&amp;A and Balance Sheet and Key Control Account Reconciliations. This person must provide exceptional customer service to internal departments, vendors, and external customers.<br>
</div>

<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<ul>
<li>
<b>Reporting:</b><ul>
<li>
(a) Prepare any monthly internal / external financial reporting with regards to payroll, inventory, accounts payable and certain accounts receivable balances;</li>
<li>
(b) Lead the monthly Cost Center review process;</li>
<li>
(c) Maintain monthly agings on certain balance sheet accounts</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>
<b>
Journal Entries:</b> Prepare assigned journal entries accurately and on-time; provide appropriate documentation to support the journal entry being made.</li>
<li>
<b>
Reconciliations:</b> Prepare assigned general ledger reconciliations accurately and on-time; resolve reconciling items the next month at the latest. Maintain documentation to support the reconciliation.</li>
<li>
<b>
Systems:</b><ul>
<li>
(a) Reconcile the inventory sub ledger system to the general ledger system every month;</li>
<li>
(b) Adjust inventory costs as approved within the sub ledger system;</li>
<li>
(c) maintain sales/ cost of sales / inventory mapping table between sub ledger system and GL system;</li>
<li>
(d) Post sub ledger sales/ cost of sales / inventory transactions from the sub ledger system to the GL system and explain / correct for any reconciling differences.</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>
<b>
Vendor Entitlements:</b><ul>
<li>
(a) Audit the vendor entitlements schedule for existence and accuracy before preparing the vendor entitlement journal entries for the month;</li>
<li>
(b) Compare vendor entitlement information provided to other sources of data for validation;</li>
<li>
(c) Ensure that the company’s policies around revenue recognition are being followed.</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>
<b>
Inventory Returns to Vendor:</b> Work with the AP team to resolve any return to vendor issues.</li>
<li>
<b>
Risks and Uncertainty:</b> Identify and report on any inventory at risk and / or certain accounts receivable balances at risk.</li>
<li>
<b>
Cross-Training:</b> May be cross-trained, or asked to cross-train others, in areas of responsibility between the two companies in order to support coverage for absences and provide succession for career development and enterprise growth.
</li>
<li>Assist with special projects and additional duties as assigned.</li>
</ul>
</div></div></div>
