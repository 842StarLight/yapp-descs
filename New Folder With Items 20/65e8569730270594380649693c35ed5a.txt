<p><b>Purpose:
</b></p>
<p>~ The Manager is responsible for planning and overseeing the development and delivery of various projects in the areas of Customer Engagement, Dealer, and Field Satisfaction projects.
</p>
<p>~This position is responsible for collaborating and influencing across HMA business units and affiliates to improve our existing Customer Engagement, Parts, Dealer and Field applications and build new applications.
</p>
<p>~ Act as liaison between HMA and AutoEver resolving system concerns and implementing system enhancements.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Major Responsibilities:
</b></p>
<p>~ Works with HMA business divisions, HAEA, and other partners to ideate, evaluate priority, evaluate development cost, and secure funding for the development and delivery of various IT and process changes to support the business.
</p>
<p>~ Collect high-level business requirements from the business units to identify business need and assist in solution development to accomplish defined objectives.
</p>
<p>~ Lead projects through ideation, pre-planning, funding and testing to ensure functionality and compliance with specifications. Track and oversee Business projects and working with our IT partner to deliver enhancements on time.
</p>
<p>~ Capable of independently leading small and large projects in the capacity of a Project Manager. Develop project plans and track project according to plan. Mitigate variances from the plan to achieve target dates.
</p>
<p>~ Assist in strategy development for Business Capability enhancement.
</p>
<p>~ Plan, direct, research and facilitate the computer-related activities and technologies business areas spanning across all business units (Such as Sales, Service, Parts, Marketing, etc.)
</p>
<p>~ Manage contractors to capture business requirements and develop test scripts for user acceptance test.
</p>
<p>~ Work independently and manage multiple priorities. Assist Sr. Mgr. to focus on tasks that needs intervention.
</p>
<p>~ Partner with business owners, PM’s and third parties to manager multiple projects, monitors project progress by tracking activity; resolving problems; publishing progress reports; recommending actions.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Authority:
</b></p>
<p>~ Vendor selection of software applications.
</p>
<p>~ Approve System enhancements and modifications.
<br>
~ Expense report and invoice approval.
</p>
<p>~ This position has common manager-level authority as defined by HMA HR / Finance policy.
</p>
<p>~ Employment decision-making abilities in designated areas of responsibility<br>
