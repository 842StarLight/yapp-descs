<p><b>Lundell Manufacturing Corporation</b></p>
<p><b>Customer Service Representative Job Description</b></p>
<p><b>POSITION DESCRIPTION: </b></p>
<p>As an OEM Customer Service Rep you will work directly with our customers’ procurement teams to support their buying initiatives and production builds. This position is responsible for processing orders, requests for quotations, and collaborating with other departments to exceed our customer’s expectation for their production schedules. You will report directly to the Director of Sales. This is a process driven role and demands timely and accurate communication with both internal departments and external customers.</p>
<p><b>POSITION REQUIREMENTS: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Proficient in Word, Excel, Outlook, 10-key and CRM Software</li>
<li>Proven track record of being able to prioritize multiple customer requests in a timely manner</li>
<li>Provide a world-class service focused experience for customers</li>
<li>High School diploma (or GED) + a min of 3 years of professional customer service experience</li>
<li>Must agree to and sign Lundell Manufacturing’s non-compete agreement</li>
</ul>
<p><b>PREFERRED EXPERIENCE: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>5 + years of OEM customer service experience</li>
<li>Foam/Flexible material knowledge/experience</li>
<li>Custom Fabrication/Custom Job Shop experience</li>
<li>Bachelor’s and/or Associates degree</li>
</ul>
<p><b>BENEFITS: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Medical, dental, life insurance and STD and LTD coverage</li>
<li>Flexible Spending Account and wellness program</li>
<li>401(k) Program</li>
<li>Paid holidays and paid time off</li>
<li>Competitive base salary plus incentive plan opportunity</li>
</ul>
<p><b>ABOUT LUNDELL MANUFACTURING: </b><br>Lundell Manufacturing Corporation is a family-owned custom fabricator of a wide range of flexible materials catered to the international OEM market. Lundell provides technical sales and engineering support in the design stages with our OEM customers providing tailor-made solutions for their applications. Applications include, but are not limited to: acoustical &amp; thermal composites, seals, filtration, gasketing and vibration damping. Our fabricating capabilities have positioned us to be a leader in our industry and achieve preferred supplier status with fortune 500 companies.</p>
