<h1 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Service Coordinator Job Description</b></h1>
<p><b>Objective</b>:</p>
<p>The <b>Service Coordinator </b>is expected to perform a variety of duties in the coordination of <b>scheduling service for clients</b>. The Service Coordinator is responsible for scheduling clients and CAREGiversSM in order to provide the highest quality service to clients with emphasis on creating extraordinary relationships. <b>This is a critical position</b> that involves the following:<br>
1. Creating and maintaining accurate client and CAREGiver schedules in our scheduling system<br>
2. Reacting quickly to schedule changes<br>
3. Frequently communicating with clients and CAREGivers<br>
4. Resolving critical issues immediately</p>
<p><b>Primary Responsibilities</b><b>:</b><b>
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Reflect the values of MD Services, Inc. (d.b.a. an independently owned and operated Home Instead Senior Care franchise).</li>
<li>Answer incoming calls in a friendly, professional and knowledgeable manner.</li>
<li>Create and maintain accurate client and CAREGiver schedules in our scheduling system, with an emphasis on creating high quality matches and the development of extraordinary relationships.</li>
<li>Resolve all open schedules in a timely manner, ensuring our clients receive the care they are expecting</li>
<li>Monitor, mediate, and log all client and CAREGiver activity utilizing our scheduling system.</li>
<li>Follow up with all client and CAREGiver issues to ensure their problems are resolved.
</li>
<li>Enter and maintain accurate client and CAREGiver records in the scheduling system.</li>
<li>Increase client loyalty to Home Instead Senior Care by utilizing the consultative sales process to better meet our current client needs.</li>
<li>Follow up and communicate CAREGiver and client issues to ensure problems are resolved timely</li>
<li>Recognize and capture opportunities to increase service hours to enhance and/or increase quality care.
</li>
<li>Field new client inquiries over the phone in a knowledgeable manner, enter the information into the software system and work with team to communicate and prepare for the Care Consultation.</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Demonstrate open and effective communication with the franchise owner, colleagues, CAREGivers, clients and family members.</li>
<li>Work at least 1 week per month 'on-call'. This entails answering calls after hours and on the weekend, and resolving any scheduling issues, client issues and service inquiries that arise</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Secondary Responsibilities</b><b>:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Participate as needed in all CAREGiver meetings</li>
<li>Conduct Service Inquires and Care Consultations following the consultative sales process.</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Perform any and all other functions and responsibilities deemed necessary</li>
<li>Maintain regular attendance at the office to execute job responsibilities</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Education/Experience Requirements</b><b>:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>High school graduation or the equivalent. Associate or Bachelor's degree preferred</li>
<li>One year of related business experience or an equivalent combination of education and work experience may be considered</li>
<li>1-3 years of Home Care experience preferred</li>
<li>Experience with scheduling systems is required</li>
<li>Must possess a valid driver’s license</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Supervisory Responsibilities:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>None</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Knowledge, Skills and Abilities:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Must have an understanding of and uphold the policies and procedures established by MD Services, Inc., (d.b.a. an independently owned and operated Home Instead Senior Care franchise)
</li>
<li>Must be able to work in a fast paced environment, and deal with sudden changes and critical issues that can arise at any time</li>
<li>Must demonstrate excellent oral and written communication skills and the ability to listen effectively.</li>
<li>Must have the ability to work independently, maintain confidentiality of information and meet deadlines
</li>
<li>Must demonstrate effective interpersonal skills as well as sound judgment and good decision-making skills</li>
<li>Must demonstrate discretion, integrity and fair-mindedness consistent with company standards, practices, policies and procedures</li>
<li>Must have the ability to organize and prioritize daily, quarterly, monthly and yearly work
</li>
<li>Must have the ability to establish good working relationships with the franchise owner, office colleagues, clients and CAREGivers</li>
<li>Must have the ability to sit at a desk and listen effectively for long periods of time on the telephone</li>
<li>Must present a professional appearance and demeanor</li>
<li>Must have the ability to operate office equipment and office technology</li>
<li>Must be patient and congenial on the telephone, resolving issues with CAREGivers in a timely fashion</li>
<li>Must have computer skills and be proficient in Word and Excel
</li>
<li>Must have the availability to work evenings and weekends as required (see on-call respnsibilities)</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Must have the ability to perform duties in a professional office setting</li>
<li>Must demonstrate knowledge of the senior care industry</li>
<br>
</ul>
