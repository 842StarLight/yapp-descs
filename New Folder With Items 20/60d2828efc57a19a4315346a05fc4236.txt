<h2>Coordinator, SR- Mktg</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p>The Sr. Marketing coordinator reporting into the Sr. Marketing Specialist is responsible for executing print media marketing to support the sales objectives of the organization.</p>
<p>
The Sr. Marketing coordinator is required to work closely with the Sales, Creative, Web, and Brand Marketing teams to assist in developing, managing and executing the marketing strategy.</p>
<p><b>
Essential Job Functions</b>:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Support the market positioning for multiple media programs ranging from catalogs and quarterly posters to sales job and direct mail collateral across B2B, B2C and internal platforms.</li>
<li>
Execute B2B, B2C promotional strategies for catalogs and additional printed materials.</li>
<li>
Pursue, execute and optimize print marketing opportunities to attract, acquire and retain customers and grow revenue profitability across platforms.</li>
<li>
Work in close collaboration with/on cross-functional teams in creative, sales, web, ecommerce and customer service areas to evolve marketing opportunities.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Key Responsibilities and Accountabilities</b>:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Identify, develop, manage, and support specific print media programs, promotions, products, and market segments</li>
<li>
Effectively merchandise and market our footwear line via multi-channel marketing media including: catalogs, direct marketing materials and customer sales presentations</li>
<li>
Assist with crafting, testing delivering marketing programs</li>
<li>
Analyze performance metrics to measure the effectiveness/ROI of marketing programs, from segment level, up to media and market views</li>
<li>
Budget maintenance and invoice reconciliation</li>
<li>
Promotional key code setup and management</li>
<li>
Proofing all printed materials</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Knowledge, Skills and Abilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Possess good computer skills, and knowledge of computer software (Proficient in Microsoft Office Suite, Adobe Creative Suite).</li>
<li>
Possess proofreading skills and high attention to details.</li>
<li>
Possess excellent interpersonal and organizational skills.</li>
<li>
Ability to prioritize work and coordinate multiple projects with minimal direction.</li>
<li>
Ability to meet deadlines, respond urgent matters accordingly and prioritize tasks to meet deadline.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Experience</b></p>
<p>
Marketing: 1-2 years</p>
<p><b>
Education</b></p>
<p><b>
Bachelors degree in Marketing or equivalent</b></p>
