<h2>Online Marketing/Demand Gen Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<b>Location:</b> Saratoga, CA, or New Brunswick, NJ
<br><br>
Import.io is on a mission to enable the world's data.
<br><br>
Effective use of data can provide incredible efficiency and value to almost any business, from the smallest independent company to the largest multi-national. But more than that, it can also advance education, academic research, charitable work, investigative journalism, and many other efforts that help to make our world a better place.
<br><br>
Import.io delivers a SaaS or managed service web data extraction solution that enables users to convert the mass of data on web sites into structured, machine readable data with no coding required. Our highly efficient and scalable platform allows customers to process 1,000s of URLs concurrently or on a schedule and gain access to millions of rows of data that they use for hundreds of different use cases. Most common uses include building training/testing sets for machine learning models, doing retail price monitoring analysis, lead generation activities, application development, market research, academic research, tracking store locations, etc.
<br><br>
<b>The Role:</b><br>
We are looking for a Digital Marketing Demand Gen Manager to join our Marketing team! The Digital Marketing Demand Gen Manager is responsible for examining and understanding the SaaS and managed services offered by the organization, and then helping to develop and refine the way the company generates demand through online advertising, email, and social media. The manager will have significant experience and proven skills in ABM, lead generation, persona development, inbound marketing, content marketing, lead follow up, sales funnels, online advertising platforms and marketing automation systems. The manager will be responsible for building online advertising demand gen programs to support ABM. The manager will analyze response data and then continue to refine and adjust online marketing/demand generation approaches to maximize return and help the company grow market share. Your role is to work with the larger marketing organization to promote go-to-market strategies while continually leveraging outreach approaches that generates interest in and offers education of the web data extraction market.
<br><br>
The online marketing specialist will collaborate with outside SEO and PPC firm, internal web development team and product marketing to form and execute on demand gen and online advertising approaches through email, advertising and social campaigns.
<br><br>
This role is a linchpin to ensure lead flow continues to grow at an exponential rate.
