<h2>Sales Executive</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">Does building a pipeline and closing deals get you excited? Are you obsessed with winning big?
<br><br>
If you love being part of something at the very earliest stages, influencing its growth and evolution, and see huge opportunities in selling disruptive SaaS solutions to the largest asset class in the world, read on.
<br><br>
About Remarkably
<br><br>
Remarkably is a venture-funded portfolio performance platform for multifamily real estate. We are driven to help the nation's top owners, investors, developers, and managers reach their leasing, occupancy, and revenue goals with portfolio-grade marketing operations and analytics software. Our platform enables customers to achieve significant and measurable results at all stages of the real estate lifecycle, from ground-up developments to repositioned properties.
<br><br>
<b>Why you'll want to work here:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Real Estate is the largest asset class in the world. U.S Multifamily is a $3.3T industry yet today, most multifamily real estate marketing management is done with spreadsheets, emails, and phone calls. These inefficient workflows require too many people, produce too many mistakes, leave too many dollars on the table, and ultimately degrade portfolio and asset value.</li>
<li>Remarkably is experiencing explosive growth backed by some of the top Venture Capital in the country as well as an elite list of angels and advisors.</li>
</ul>
<b>What you'll enjoy:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Direct access to senior management: you will work right alongside the CEO, who has been running sales since day one.</li>
<li>Mentorship program to quickly acclimate you to Remarkably and set you on a path to success</li>
<li>Competitive base salary and structured commission plan, commensurate with experience</li>
<li>Full benefits package including medical, dental, and vision</li>
<li>Self-managed Paid Time Off (PTO) policy to ensure a solid work-life balance</li>
</ul>
<b>What you'll do:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Build sales pipeline and be comfortable with all facets of the sales process, including but not limited to: outbound lead development, inbound/outbound lead nurture, appointment setting, pitching, and closing. As a member of the inaugural sales team you'll wear a lot of hats!</li>
<li>Become an expert in portfolio performance software who can clearly articulate and demonstrate our value proposition, creating excitement and enthusiasm among prospects</li>
<li>Attend industry events to meet with prospective and current clients and further awareness of the latest trends in multifamily real estate</li>
<li>Be exposed to some of the best Venture Capital sales &amp; marketing minds in the world</li>
<li>Consistently blow your monthly and quarterly quotas out of the water, including required criteria for sales funnel targets of both volume and conversion (e.g. Inquiries, Marketing Qualified Leads, Sales Qualified Leads, Contract Submissions, Contract Close)</li>
<li>Become adept at selling complex solutions to large and small organizations; adaptability is key</li>
</ul>
<b>What you won't do:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Feel like a number - as a Remarkably Sales Executive, you will be a member of our first sales pod, reporting directly to the co-founder and CEO. This role is a critical driver to company growth, moving us from establishing product market fit into significant customer acquisition.</li>
<li>Get bored – Remarkably is an incredibly fast-growing and exciting company with a hungry team and ambitious goals</li>
<li>Hit the ceiling – we are huge proponents for aligning incentives: your success is our success. The inaugural team of Sales Executives will have unprecedented access and opportunity</li>
</ul>
<b>What you bring:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor's Degree or equivalent (focus on Marketing, Real Estate, or Technology preferred)</li>
<li>High-energy personality with an infectiously positive attitude</li>
<li>Hard-working style with 3-5+ years of past quota-carrying B2B sales experience as part of a global or national B2B tech, SaaS or enterprise software business</li>
<li>Ambition, motivation, and sales-mindedness</li>
<li>Ability to excel in a dynamic, results-driven environment</li>
<li>Problem-solving and critical-thinking approach with fantastic communication, negotiation, and interpersonal skills</li>
<li>Keen attitude toward working cross-organizationally with senior leaders and customers</li>
</ul>
