<h2>Business Development Executive</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div></div>
<div>
<p><b>Primary job responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Plan sales programs to achieve key business objectives</li>
<li>Collaborate with peers from the Sales and Marketing teams to help your team run programs and campaigns to increase opportunities.</li>
<li>Using marketing and business knowledge to identify potential decision makers.</li>
<li>Contacting potential clients by telephone, to establish new contacts and their requirements</li>
<li>Scheduling and attending client meetings, in order to ensure client’s commitment.</li>
<li>Developing business at new clients in middle east utilizing a consultative approach to identify and evaluate client needs, and opportunities for our client to help develop and implement solutions.</li>
<li>Researching organizations and individuals online (especially on social media) to identify new leads and potential new markets.</li>
<li>Researching the needs of other companies and learning who makes decisions about purchasing.</li>
<li>Planning and overseeing new marketing initiatives.</li>
<li>Preparing PowerPoint presentations and sales displays.</li>
<li>Contacting clients to inform them about new developments in the company’s products.</li>
<li>Developing quotes and proposals.</li>
<li>Negotiating and renegotiating by phone, email, and in person.</li>
<li>Experience in mobile telecommunication and cloud services</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Qualification:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Experience in mobile telecommunication and cloud services</li>
<li>+3 years of industry experience, especially technology-related sales or business development experience</li>
<li>Experience selling Telecom and cloud services.</li>
<li>Experience selling to large government and corporate clients</li>
<li>Experience at a leading management consulting, investment banking, or private equity firm
</li>
<li>Superb English-language written and oral communication skills</li>
<li>Excellent interpersonal and negotiation skills</li>
<li>Above-average ability to use the MS Office suite (Word, Excel, PowerPoint); email software such as Outlook; and sales tracking software (CRM, Sales force etc.).
</li>
<li>Excellent attention to detail, follow-up, and organization skill.</li>
<li>Able to partner with clients to understand their organizational needs and recommend solutions that add value to their business.</li>
</ul>
