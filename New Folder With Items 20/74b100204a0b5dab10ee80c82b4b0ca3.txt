Job Description
<br>As a Customer Success Manager for Mid-Market, you are responsible for creating and maintaining successful partnerships with our Mid-Market level customers and ensuring they realize value from our platform and services. This position works cross-functionally with our Sales, Product, Marketing and Professional Services teams to deliver value, drive adoption, and develop relationships, and ensure a world-class customer experience.
<br><br>
<b>Responsibilities:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Oversee customer adoption and expansion throughout the customer lifecycle</li>
<li>Conduct onsite and remote monthly updates and quarterly partnership reviews</li>
<li>Become a trusted advisor and advocate to your customers, and develop executive level relationships</li>
<li>Ensure value through strategic discussions, capturing success criteria, building Success Plans, and delivering business outcomes</li>
<li>Drive customer retention, deliver a best in class customer experience, and make fans of our customers</li>
</ul>
<b>Qualifications:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree</li>
<li>3 - 5 years customer facing experience</li>
<li>Exceptional communication, problem-solving and relationship development/management skills</li>
<li>Motivated, independent and organized team player</li>
<li>Broad understanding of digital marketing, product development, SAAS, and the Customer Experience landscape</li>
</ul>
