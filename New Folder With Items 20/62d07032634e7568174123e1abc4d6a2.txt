<h2>Pricing Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Overview
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<p>Bowlero Corporation, the world's largest Bowling and Entertainment corporation, is looking for a <b>Pricing Manager</b> based out of our corporate office in New York City.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
If you are a driven, motivated professional seeking a challenging and rewarding position with a fun company and great work environment, apply today.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
SUMMARY: </b>The Pricing Manager participates in the development of pricing strategies, policies, procedures, and processes; and implements pricing strategies, business plans, and forecasts for a company’s products or services in line with corporate strategy. The Pricing Manager also will develop, maintain, score and leverage a database of pricing information.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
ESSENTIAL DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES:</b> Primary duties and responsibilities include, but are not limited to the following.</p>
<ul>
<li>
Develop and maintain a database of pricing data.</li>
<li>
Understand what our utilization is and what our traffic looks like (revenue, traffic and units sold).</li>
<li>
Build and react to pricing elasticity curves.</li>
<li>
Perform regression testing to isolate, and test changes in, key variables.</li>
<li>
Create and maintain analyses to provide each bowling center location a unique score (collects all key attributes of a center) which will inform go-forward pricing strategy.</li>
<li>
Assess the correlation of data points as price changes go into effect</li>
<li>
Helps company identify target markets, working with Sales, Operations, and Marketing to develop effective pricing strategies and market evaluations, keeping in line with corporate strategy.</li>
<li>
Reviews customer trends to help determine the feasibility of new products or services.</li>
<li>
Determines optimal pricing for the new products by evaluating and considering production and other internal costs to establish the price point to make a profit and considering competition, regulations, and market constraints.</li>
<li>
Sets base prices and develop discount programs, assist in creating promotional campaigns, compare prices set by competitors and measure customer satisfaction based on the cost of a company’s product or service.</li>
<li>
Develops price elasticity curves by evaluating trends to determine what consumers would buy a certain product or utilize a specific service.
</li>
<li>Assist marketing and sales departments in targeting consumers to generate revenue for the company.</li>
<li>
Works with various departments, displaying strong leadership and project management capabilities, along with a good sense of finance or accounting principles.</li>
<li>
Coordinates with Sales, Operations, and Marketing personnel to build pricing models.</li>
<li>
Ability to work in a team oriented environment and build strong interdepartmental connections across the organization.</li>
<li>
Ability to work extended workdays to support business needs.</li>
<li>
Other duties may be assigned</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
SUPERVISORY RESPONSIBILITIES:</b> N/A</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
QUALIFICATIONS: </b>The Pricing Manager has a proven track record of solid math and statistical skills, is highly organized with the ability to track and prioritize multiple projects. The Pricing Manager demonstrates the aptitude and desire to quickly adapt to changing business objectives and to quickly learn and apply quantitative techniques and analytics technologies.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
EDUCATION AND/OR EXPERIENCE:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
BA/BS minimum, MBA or equivalent preferred</li>
<li>
Advanced Proficiency in Microsoft Office Suite, including Excel</li>
<li>
3 years of SQL and RDBMS experience (SQL Server, Oracle, MySQL, etc.)</li>
<li>
Preferred experience with a visualization software (ex. Tableau, Spotfire, Miscrosoft Power BI)</li>
<li>
5-10 years pricing experience with demonstrated progressive growth</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
WORK ENVIRONMENT /PHYSICAL DEMANDS: </b>General office environment where you will walk, bend and stand for periods of time.</p>

<p><b>
ABOUT BOWLERO CORPORATION:</b></p>
<p>
Bowlero Corporation is the largest owner and operator of bowling entertainment venues in the world with over 290 total centers across four brands. Bowlero combines bowling, dining, and nightlife into an experience that’s is packed with amusing throwbacks and ready to amaze. Bowlero is a blacklight bowling, retro-inspired hangout powered by interactive arcade games, signature cocktails, an outrageous menu of Oversized Shareables, and... nostalgia. Forget everything you thought bowling could be—and experience the kind of epic fun that only happens when you go Bowlero.</p>
<p>
As a brief timeline of Bowlero history:
</p>
<ul>
<li>Bowlero started as a single bowling alley established in Greenwich Village, New York in 1938.
</li>
<li>In the late 1990’s and early 2000’s Bowlmor Lanes revolutionized the sport with the concept of high-end, event-centric bowling.
</li>
<li>In 2013, Bowlero Corporation was founded as Bowlmor AMF following the successful acquisition of AMF Bowling Worldwide by Bowlmor Lanes.
</li>
<li>In 2014, Bowlmor AMF acquired the Brunswick Corporation’s bowling center business and solidified its reputation as the worldwide leader in bowling entertainment.
</li>
<li>In 2018, Bowlmor AMF officially became Bowlero Corporation, beginning a new chapter and signaling a new direction in the company’s continued growth.</li>
</ul>
