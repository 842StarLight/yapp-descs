Professional Services at Talend is not just a P&amp;L; it’s a strategic part of our company that helps drive our growth through the success of our customer base. Talend is looking for a <b>VP of global Professional Services</b> to establish Talend as a transformational leader in cloud data management.</div>
</div>
<div><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>The VP of Professional Services will be responsible to:</b></h3>
<div><ul>
<li>
Establish the methodologies used by Talend, our Customers, and our Partners to successfully deploy Talend technologies;</li>
<li>
Refine and roll-out a new project delivery framework across PS and other customer success functions;</li>
<li>
Define, roll-out, and deliver new outcome-based services for customers globally;</li>
<li>
Manage a global team to revenue and margin targets, including direct and subcontracted resources;</li>
<li>
Partner with sales to enable a global sales organization on the value of services, empower the sales organization to set up the Services sale, and provide the technical expertise that makes Services an obvious choice for our customers;</li>
<li>
Implement and manage according to a cadence of accountability around operational and quality metrics;</li>
<li>
Manage enablement of new technologies across the Professional Services organization;</li>
<li>
Continue to develop our Strategic Services offerings;</li>
<li>
Foster a learning culture: hold the team and colleagues accountable in a mutually respectful way and drive continuous improvement;</li>
<li>
Win as a team – and as a company;</li>
<li>
Use Professional Services as a way to understand health/risk in a customer and to drive success either directly with the Services team or in a collaborative fashion by raising risk/opportunity and getting other parts of the organization involved;</li>
<li>
Provide visibility to the Product, R&amp;D, Sales, and Customer Success organizations into customer engagements and activity;</li>
<li>
Support the development of Customer Success subscription offerings that will complement, and in some cases, drive, our Professional Services offerings.</li>
</ul></div>
