Benefitfocus is experiencing exciting growth and we are looking for top selling Account Executives to join our Employer Sales division. If you are a highly motivated self-started, with a proven track record in gaining new accounts, Benefitfocus provides the opportunity to showcase your talents and grow your income!<br>
<br>
<b>Job Description</b>:<br>
<br>
Our Employer Sales Account Executive will have the assigned quota responsibility to use consultative selling skills to identify and secure new large (1000 employee and above) accounts within a defined geography. The Account Executive will utilize Challenger skills to differentiate the complete suite of Benefitfocus products designed for large client accounts- to include the Benefitfocus Marketplace, Core and Advanced Analytics, the Benefit Service Center, Benefitstore and video. This skilled individual will manage and sell to HR, VP and C-level Executives as well as key influencers within each account and will use SF.com as the system of record. Our Account Executives have a home based office and are supported in sales by an Inside Sales team, Solution Engineers, strategic partners and our Marketing team.<br>
<br>
<b>Responsibilities:</b><br>
<br>
<ul>
<li>Consistent track record in meeting and exceeding sales quotas.</li>
<li>Proven experience in proactively identifying and closing new account opportunities in an assigned market.</li>
<li>Ability to manage sales through forecasting, account resource allocation, account strategy and ecosystem partnerships.</li>
<li>Experience in cultivating relationships with existing accounts at the highest strategic level for additional sales.</li>
<li>Knowledge of our industry and large market expertise.</li>
<li>Exceptional verbal and written skills.</li>
<li>Ability to negotiate pricing and contractual agreements to close million dollar sales.</li>
<li>Desire to become part of our extraordinary culture of respect, providing anticipatory service and celebrating together!</li>
</ul>
