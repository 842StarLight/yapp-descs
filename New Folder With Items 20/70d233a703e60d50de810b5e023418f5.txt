Due to its continued growth the Berman Auto Group is looking for a highly qualified General Manager to take charge of an already successful dealership and build it into one of the best in the Midwest.
<br><br>
The Berman organization believes that its strength, continued success, future growth and profitability depend on building trusting relationships with our employees, customers and industry partners.
<br><br>
Individual and organizational pride are critical elements of our future. Our people make all the difference. This belief fosters a commitment to provide opportunities for growth and new challenges for our employees so they may achieve professional development and personal fulfillment. Working with our local communities, we help and support those people and organizations whose fulfillment enhances their quality of life as it does our own.

We will continually strive to define, improve and operate by setting the highest standards in the retail automobile industry.

<b>General Manager:</b><br>
We are in growth mode and looking for an experienced and motivating General Manager to join us in supporting our dealership staff and customers while assisting us in building and representing our brand.
<br><br>
<b>Responsibilities:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Responsible for profitability in all departments in the dealership</li>
<li>Develop and execute a strategic plan to achieve monthly and annual forecasts for all departments</li>
<li>Hire, mentor and monitor the performance of all department heads</li>
<li>Maintain superior customer service (CSI) through dealership culture</li>
<li>Review KPIs daily for every department and make necessary adjustments to increase production</li>
<li>Assist in developing an advertising and marketing plan</li>
<li>Work directly with our employees and customers to develop relationships and help bridge disconnects in the sales process</li>
<li>Build rapport with customers to build a base of referrals to establish customer network</li>
<li>Provide training and support to the all staff</li>
<li>Assist sales managers in desking deals and closing more customers</li>
</ul>
