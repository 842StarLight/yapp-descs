<br>ROLE SUMMARY
<br>We are currently seeking an Account Executive for our team in Alpharetta, GA office to work with events in our Retail portfolio, including the Internet Retailer Conference &amp; Exhibition (IRCE). The Account Executive will work with sales manager to strategically sell exhibit space for designated territory and for implementing aspects of the sales campaign. Additional responsibilities include selling sponsorships and various advertising opportunities to customer base to generate added revenue, as well as uncovering new prospects and product categories.
