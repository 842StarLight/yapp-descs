<p><b>
Technical Consultant, Wireless &amp; Mobility</b></p>
<p>
The Wireless and Mobility Engineer's role is to implement the WLAN project for the overall logical and technical WiFi network architecture, including all wired/wireless and core components. This candidate will take the project, as designed in the SOW, and be able to successfully implement the hardware and/or software required. It is important the individual will be a team player, working with Architects and other Engineers from the team, and be able to handle working in very diverse client environments.<br>
</p>
<p><b>
What you'll do:</b><br>
</p>
<ul>
<li>
Must be able to deliver successful implementations as described in SOW's within the set timeline</li>
<li>
Will perform tasks as a post-sale implementation engineer</li>
<li>
Collaborate with end users and senior management, both internally and externally, to define business requirements for complex systems implementation for all infrastructure plans</li>
<li>
Provide architectural consulting expertise to clients and make recommendation to clients on implementation plans and be able to identify other areas that may need to be addressed</li>
<li>
Document and develop in-depth knowledge of company's existing WiFi network architecture/infrastructure, and technology portfolio</li>
<li>
Attend vendor training classes and obtain new certifications and/or keep certifications current</li>
<li>
Learn from Wireless and other System Architects and Engineers</li>
<li>
Conduct research on emerging technologies in support of infrastructure development efforts and recommend technologies that will increase cost effectiveness and infrastructure flexibility</li>
<li>
Interact with project team members for a successful implementation of the architectural design</li>
<li>
Define and document the system architecture</li>
<li>
Recommend, document, advocate and enforce industry best practices to the client</li>
<li>
Identify, research, select and introduce appropriate new technologies and techniques to current architecture</li>
<li>
Understand and communicate cost-benefit analysis and associated risks relating to system architecture integrity, sustainability and manageability</li>
<li>
Support other engineers and clients with questions by participating in problem resolution activities</li>
</ul>
