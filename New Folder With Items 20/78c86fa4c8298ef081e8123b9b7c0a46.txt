<p><b>
Where do you fit?</b></p>
<p><b>
SugarCRM is seeking an experienced Regional Sales Director to help grow our customer base in North America.</b><br>
<br>
The role of the Regional Sales Director (RSD) is to manage a territory to grow revenue through our channel and direct sales efforts. This is a great role for an experienced seller who knows how to work a territory to build a book of business and create long lasting relationships. The RSD will be responsible for directing all pre- and post-sales activities with the goal of obtaining high margin, long-term business and increasing market share. This is a closer’s dream role. CRM experience is a plus. Additionally a preferred candidate will have a proven track record of exceeding quota, experience selling a SaaS solution, selling to the C-level, working large enterprises and carrying a quota in excess of $2 million. This is a high profile role within the organization, with direct access to company executives, and input on product development. The role reports directly to the Vice President of Sales, Enterprise Accounts.</p>
<p><b>
 Impact you will make in this role:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Meet and exceed individual sales goals, quota and activity</li>
<li>
Partner with our channel to drive joint opportunities to close</li>
<li>
Manage enterprise level sales opportunities at Fortune 1000s</li>
<li>
Cultivate sales opportunities with high profile accounts and partnerships</li>
<li>
Develop and execute plans to drive customer awareness of Sugar’s solutions</li>
<li>
Document and manage your sales pipeline in our CRM application</li>
<li>
Coordinate and manage a team selling process</li>
<li>
Responsible for managing the customer relationship, including participating in solving payment disputes</li>
</ul>

 Expertise you will bring in:</b></p>

A proven ability to manage to a defined forecast with stated quarterly objectives</li>
