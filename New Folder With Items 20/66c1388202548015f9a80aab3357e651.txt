<p><b>Position Summary:</b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>Samsung SDS America is looking for an industry leader who has had success in selling Managed Mobility Services (MMS) and Telecom Expense Management (TEM) to large enterprise customers. This role will be working cross functionally with our global leaders in the areas of mobile device hardware, infrastructure, integration management services, operations, and enterprise application support and maintenance to incorporate MMS into complete solution offerings.</p>
<p>The successful candidate will work with enterprise clients, alliance partners and sales teams within Samsung affiliated companies to develop B2B solution opportunities and to ensure customer success and revenue growth. We are looking for a business expert with consultative sales experience, and the ability to bring expertise in the areas of strategic planning, solution development, technical business enablement and process management.</p>
<p>He/she will also be responsible for the management of sales support materials and building relationships with clients. The right candidate for this position possesses a sales-focused mindset with a proven ability to aggressively develop opportunity pipeline.</p>
<p><b>The role is</b> <b>based in our office in Herndon, VA, but can be open for remote.</b></p>
<p><b>Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Work with the SDSA Managed Mobility Services (MMS) lead.</li>
<li>Support MMS sales based on customer requests regarding product, service, delivery, etc. through communication with HQ.</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Generate new sales opportunities while fostering a trusting relationship with clients and delivering business value.</li>
<li>Work cross functionally across engineering, operations, and internal and external business partner teams to deliver highly scalable and reliable customer experience services.</li>
<li>Act as a liaison between company and client/partner for quality assurance.</li>
<li>Ability to generate new solution sales by utilizing the full breath of SDSA’s enterprise offerings.</li>
</ul>
</div></div>
