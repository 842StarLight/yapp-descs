<h2>Sr. Managing Data/BI Architect</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Washington DC, NYC or Boston</p>
<p>We are looking for a Consulting Managing Data Architect &amp; Big Data with practical, hands-on knowledge of modern data architectures and tools such as data warehousing, ETL/ELT, analytics, and the Microsoft Azure cloud platform. You should be driven to provide quality solutions to challenging problems. You will work closely with client stakeholders and an award-winning team of engineers, architects and thought leaders to design, build and implement next-generation solutions in advanced analytics, Big Data, BI and the cloud. As a Managing Architect you will be responsible for industry research &amp; development, rapid prototyping, and outreach to the technology community. You will collaborate with sales and marketing, and delivery in the creation of Insight Digital Innovation offerings, providing pre-sales collateral in the form of whitepapers, presentations, blog articles, demos and videos.</p>
<p>What you’ll do at Insight:</p>
<ul>
<li>Design and build enterprise-grade data solutions for a variety of external clients</li>
<li>Design and code solutions to tough data challenges and provide feedback on others’ work</li>
<li>Work directly with client stakeholders to develop technical solutions for business cases</li>
<li>Aggressively grow your skillset and expertise to meet the emerging needs of our clients</li>
<li>Drive business decisions and press customers for requirements and to challenge current technology concepts</li>
<li>Lead teams of developers and be familiar with standard project management processes (scrum, Kanban, etc.) including forecasting of effort and project financials</li>
<li>Manage and mentor Data Engineers and Data Architects</li>
<li>Participate in pre-sales activities – scoping and estimating projects, and authoring of SOWs and Change Orders</li>
</ul>
<p>What you’ll need to join Insight:</p>
<ul>
<li>10+ years of hands-on experience working with data tools within the Microsoft data platform with an demonstrated understanding of most of the following technologies:</li>
<li>SQL Server</li>
<li>Analysis Services and DAX</li>
<li>Reporting Services</li>
<li>Integration Services</li>
<li>2+ years of experience in three or more of the following technologies:</li>
<li>Self-Service BI Tools: Power BI, Tableau, Qlik</li>
<li>Azure: Azure SQL Database, Azure Data Warehouse and Azure Data Factory</li>
<li>Azure Big Data Technologies: Azure Data Lake and Azure Data Lake Analytics</li>
<li>Big Data Technologies: Hadoop or HDInsight, Hive, Pig, Python, Spark, Oozie, or any of the other tools with the Hadoop ecosystem</li>
<li>Predictive Analytics: R, Azure Machine Learning</li>
<li>Scripting: PowerShell, Azure Automation</li>
<li>Development Languages: .NET, Java, Python, Scala</li>
<li>Experience designing, building, and evolving enterprise grade software</li>
<li>Passion and experience managing and mentoring employees with a proven track record of growing talent from within an organization</li>
<li>Knowledge of cloud and hybrid-cloud implementations including IaaS, PaaS and SaaS</li>
<li>Strong analytical and reasoning skills that result in clear technical execution</li>
<li>Skill at translating requirements into clean, efficient and quality code</li>
<li>Proven ability to prioritize, self-direct and execute at velocity</li>
<li>Passion to deliver craftsman-quality work both individually and as part of a team</li>
<li>Solid communication skills with both technical and non-technical stakeholders</li>
<li>Desire to learn new skills and grow competencies</li>
<li>Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science or related discipline</li>
</ul>
