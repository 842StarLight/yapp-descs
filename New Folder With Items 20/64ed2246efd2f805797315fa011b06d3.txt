<p>GENERAL DESCRIPTION</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
The Senior Site Construction Manager will be responsible for managing and overseeing the construction of wind-powered and solar generation projects to ensure the fulfillment of project requirements in terms of schedule, cost, and quality.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Main accountabilities:</b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Support the completion of preliminary engineering specifications of construction and supply contracts for assigned projects and review and provide constructability feedback</li>
<li>Support the development of Project Construction Execution Plan to accomplish project goals and provide leadership on the project teams</li>
<li>Participate in the supplier qualification process and in selecting qualified suppliers and contractors in the RFP process</li>
<li>Assist in hiring competent field staff to ensure adequate resources are in place for effective and timely execution of the project</li>
<li>Manage the project construction schedules and budgets and coordinate the same with other team participants including the Project Construction Manager</li>
<li>Coordinate all the internal and external relations necessary to assure the development and delivery of the project site</li>
<li>Manage contractors to ensure project construction activities and the execution of work are performed to ensure that quality, schedule, cost, safety, environmental, and landowner requirements are met</li>
<li>Supervise field staff and maintain current knowledge of the overall project, leading the resolution of differences when required and reviewing plans and procedures to identify and effect changes when necessary</li>
<li>Maintain familiarity with contracts and documents in order to ensure that work is executed in accordance with EDPR’s expectations and that the work fulfills contractual requirements</li>
<li>Develop effective relationships with contractors, suppliers, engineers, consultants, local officials, and permitting officials to ensure successful, timely completion of deliverables that support the project goals</li>
<li>Develop effective and supportive relationships with colleagues including those with responsibilities in other areas of the company such as development, operations, tax, accounting, finance, electric sale, insurance, legal, etc.</li>
<li>Ability to mentor and oversee a more junior construction manager remotely</li>
<li>Additional duties as required</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p><b>
</b></p>
<p><b>Direct reports: </b>4 -7</p>
<p><b>
Travel</b>: 0 – 20%</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
Minimum qualifications:</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree in a related field or an equivalent amount of relevant experience</li>
<li>10+ years in construction, energy project execution, an environmental or engineering field, or a directly related field</li>
<li>Proficient knowledge of Microsoft Office software</li>
<li>Experience with power plant construction, wind turbine installation and maintenance preferred</li>
<li>Wind farm and solar construction experience preferred</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p>
</p>
<p><b>Behavioral requirements:</b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Ability to operate a motor vehicle and possession of a valid driver’s license</li>
<li>Ability to work around low, medium, and high voltage equipment</li>
<li>Ability to solve practical problems and deal with a variety of concrete variables in situations where only limited standardization exists</li>
<li>Ability and willingness to travel occasionally to other development, construction or operational sites within the US, Mexico and Canada as needed</li>
<li>Ability and willingness to work overtime on holidays and weekends and to work on short notice as needed due to on-call scheduling</li>
<li>Ability and willingness to respond to site emergencies 24 hours a day, 7 days a week as needed</li>
<li>Ability to quickly understand the basics of a variety of subjects relating to wind energy, including wind studies, environmental and cultural studies, transmission issues, public relations, public policy, sales, and civil and electrical engineering</li>
<br>
</ul>
