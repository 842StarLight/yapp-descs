<h2>Director, Business Applications Development</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>Director, Business Applications Development<br>
<br>
<p>The Director of Business Applications Development is a key member of the Information Technology Leadership Team. This leadership role is responsible for planning, implementing, and managing business solutions for the enterprise. The Director understands the processes performed by functional business areas and is proficient at leading IT transformation to support business process re-engineering and IT deployment initiatives.</p>
<div>
<br>
The Director will work with Product Management and business stakeholders to identify the proper technology solutions to meet or exceed business objectives. This Director plays a critical role in standardizing Business Application delivery and support throughout the organization by developing partnerships with the business unit leadership to identifying and deploying common solutions to business challenges while ensuring each business unit’s needs are met.<br>
<br>
Essential Duties/Responsibilities<br>
<br>
<ul>
<li>Create vision and strategic direction for business application development and delivery.</li>
<li>Collaborate with Product Management, Research and Development and business leadership to ensure that business needs are fully understood by identifying the optimal solution to deliver on business needs.</li>
<li>Effectively manage IT professionals setting goals and priorities, establishing delivery processes and ensuring employee development that maximize individual effectiveness and engagement.</li>
<li>Collaborate with business partners to ensure that business application administrators are adequately trained to develop and foster both technology and business acumen within their respective portfolios.</li>
<li>Ensure that application administrators and developers work collaboratively with business and third-party partners to meet strategic objectives, regulatory compliance, risk management requirements, and to ensure that industry leading best practices are leveraged effectively.</li>
<li>Engage stakeholders to ensure that software releases and application configuration changes are tested, qualified and implemented successfully.</li>
<li>Promote collaboration with other technology teams including Research and Development, Infrastructure, Support and Information Security to deliver end to end value for the IT customer.</li>
<li>Ensure compliance with all company policies and procedures, and IT standards as well as state, federal, and regulatory requirements, including but not limited to HIPAA, FDA and SOX requirements.</li>
<li>Meet financial objectives by forecasting requirements, participating within the annual budget and strategic planning processes, and ensuring judicious use of company funds.</li>
<li>Enhance department and organization reputation by accepting ownership for accomplishing new and different requests; exploring opportunities to improve IT service delivery.</li>
<li>This job description in no way states or implies that these are the only duties to be performed by the employee(s) incumbent in this position. Employee(s) will be required to follow any other job-related instructions and to perform any other job-related duties requested by any person authorized to give instructions or assignments.</li>
</ul>
<br>
Education: Bachelor’s degree required. Master’s degree preferred.<br>
<br>
Relevant Work Experience:<br>
o 10+ years of IT solution deliver experience with 5+ years leadership/management experience.<br>
o Demonstrated experience improving technology delivery and business processes.<br>
o Software development and / or systems integration experience required.<br>
o Demonstrated ability to create strategies and execute on those strategies.<br>
o Experience managing multiple projects and resource management.<br>
<br>
Required Knowledge, Skills, and Abilities<br>
<br>
<ul><li>Knowledge/Abilities –Technical Skills: Familiarity with enterprise architecture and the ability to learn the inner workings of complex systems.</li></ul>
