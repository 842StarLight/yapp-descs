<h2>Senior Ecommerce Account Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>DESCRIPTION</p>
<p><b>Voted Sun Sentinel Top Workplace and ranked INC 5000’s 29th fastest growing company, ShipMonk is America’s fastest-growing fulfillment center. We believe in working smart, playing hard, and innovating everything. From our Forbes 30 Under 30 CEO to our newest hires, everyone on our team is valued and recognized for their contributions. Want to help us shape the future of eCommerce? Join ShipMonk, and do meaningful work you enjoy with people who inspire you.</b></p>
<p>Our Sr. Customer Service Account Managers (called Sr. Happiness Engineers) are responsible for providing training, support and problem resolution to ShipMonk customers. They coordinate special projects on behalf of our customers and work closely with the Warehouse Manager and warehouse staff to ensure requests are done on-time and in a professional manner.</p>
<p><b>Responsibilities and Duties</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Prioritize and respond promptly to customer tickets via Freshdesk and resolve issues in a timely, professional and efficient manner.</li>
<li>Walk customers through the onboarding process and review guidelines to set expectations for our services.</li>
<li>Train customers on the ShipMonk software, shipping methods, and procedures.</li>
<li>Deal directly with customers either by telephone, electronically or face to face and keep records updated.</li>
<li>Provide feedback on the efficiency of the customer service process.</li>
<li>Communicate and coordinate with internal departments.</li>
</ul>
<p>REQUIREMENTS</p>
<ul>
<li>Exceptional interpersonal and relationship-building skills required</li>
<li>Must possess superior problem-solving skills</li>
<li>Demonstrate strong verbal and written communication skills</li>
<li>Strong organizational skills and the ability to multi-task</li>
<li>Freshdesk or comparable support software experience a PLUS</li>
<li>Tech-savvy</li>
<li>Ability to learn, understand, prioritize and communicate complex processes</li>
<li>Able to deal with ambiguity &amp; strong stress management</li>
<li>5 years Problem Resolution Experience (Preferred)</li>

</ul>
