<h2>Treasury Management Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<div><div>
<p>The position of Treasury Management Specialist will provide overall support and administration<br>
for Treasury Management products, online banking and mobile banking for Seattle Bank retail,<br>
commercial and private banking business lines with the underlying objective of enhancing sales<br>
and service effectiveness.
</p>
<p><b>Essential Duties
</b></p>
<ul><li>Administer Treasury Management Program due diligence, site visits and annual</li></ul>
reviews. Evaluate business financial statements, credit history, business operating<br>
activities, and banking history. Work with senior/executive management on accounts<br>
requiring escalated credit review. Track and ensure that annual reviews and site visits<br>
for active Treasury Management customers are conducted in accordance with policy.
<ul><li>Maintain Treasury Management client files ensuring all proper paperwork and</li></ul>
documentation are completed and current, including authorized signers and limits.
<ul><li>Install and train clients on the usage of Remote Deposit Capture service and equipment.<br>
</li></ul>
