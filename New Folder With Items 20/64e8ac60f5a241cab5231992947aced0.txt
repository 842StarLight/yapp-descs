<h2>Sales Operations Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>We believe that phone calls should be secure, engaging, free from fraud and robocalls. Hiya is building the modern trust and identity layer for the phone call experience. We serve more than 100M users worldwide directly through our apps and in partnership with mobile device makers like Samsung and mobile carriers like AT&amp;T. We also serve businesses with a rapidly maturing SaaS platform designed to help call center operators, marketers and others who rely on real time connections to amplify trust in their brand to reach more customers.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
We are looking for a Sales Operations Specialist with solid Salesforce Admin skills<b> </b>to join our team in Seattle<b>. </b>We'd like you to have experience with supporting an enterprise sales team, a background in technology and a passion for data and how it can help businesses be more efficient in customer interactions is a definite must-have in this role. We are seeking a Sales Operations Specialist who can provide strategic consultation and expertise across the sales organization for continuing growth and development.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
In this role you will play a pivotal part in shaping our rapidly growing organization. This individual must be a thought leader and be able to work cross-functionally with many teams to develop an operational cadence that will provide visibility into the business and ensure maximum revenue growth. This position requires an analytical mind with the ability to tell a “story” with data. This is a highly visible role that will partner with senior sales leadership, finance, OEM partners, marketing and other executives across Hiya to drive efficiency and operational excellence. In this role you will need mad Salesforce Admin skills to succeed and the ability to build out all sales operations processes from the ground up. We are currently selling into six countries and with your vital help we will successfully sell into twenty countries and ramp from there!</p>
