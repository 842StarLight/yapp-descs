<h2>Demand Planner</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<div><b>Position Overview</b></div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div>Based at our Pennsauken, NJ USA HQ,<i> </i>in order to further strengthen our national Supply Chain team we are currently seeking an:</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div><b>Demand Planner</b></div>
<div>Pennsauken, NJ Headquarters</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div>
<i>Reporting directly to the US Planning Manager</i><i>, </i><i>the Demand Planner will be responsible for overseeing the forecasting and collaborative demand planning processes for their assigned sales regions</i><i> within Puratos USA.</i><i> Ultimately, this individual is responsible for ensuring the appropriate demand planning strategy is in place to support the commercial, service level, and inventory objectives, which benefit the company’s overall objectives.</i>
</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div><b>Key Accountabilities</b></div>
<br>
<div></div>
<ul>
<li>Facilitate the monthly Demand Validation meetings as part of the overall Sales &amp; Operations Planning Process (S&amp;OP)</li>
<li>Initiate collaborative planning programs with strategic accounts</li>
<li>Drive continuous improvement of the planning process to align with the financial budget and the needs of the business</li>
<li>Actively support the S&amp;OP planning process by organizing relevant data, updating KPI's and participating in the monthly meetings by representing the demand planning function.</li>
<li>Monitor and maintain statistical forecasts by collecting and analyzing data
</li>
<li>Error analysis to improve forecasts</li>
<li>Review demand drivers, sales, and trends with Sales and Marketing</li>
<li>Reach consensus for operational demand forecast with Sales and Marketing</li>
<li>Work with warehouses and customer service to ensure effective inventory management
</li>
<li>Manage and maintain forecasting software</li>
<li>Be a key participant in establishing forecast requirements for commercialization of new items, existing item extensions, and product discontinuations</li>
<li>In collaboration with supply planning, and production planning, maintain accuracy of material level master data</li>
<li>In partnership with cross-functional business partners, facilitate a review of make-to-order (MTO) and make-to-stock (MTS) items.
</li>
<li>Be an active participant in all regional sales meetings, for those assigned regions</li>
<li>Become knowledgeable and an advocate for the customer relationship management (CRM) system, and encourage that all new business opportunities and risks are identified in the system, and that updated forecast are in place<br>
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Profile</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s Degree or 5 years work experience in a supply chain planning, procurement, or financial planning function</li>
<li>3+ years of analytical experience required</li>
<li>Able to demonstrate and provide examples of Excel experience including dealing with multi tab spreadsheets, record sets with a minimum of 5,000 rows, and pivot tables</li>
<li>Knowledge and understanding of material requirements planning (MRP). Experience with an enterprise resource planning (ERP) or planning system strongly preferred (SAP, JDA/Manugistics, Oracle/Netsuite, Microsoft Dynamics, Logitlity, DemandWorks, DemandSolutions, John Galt, etc)</li>
<li>Experience with statistical tools and forecasting is a plus
</li>
<li>Experience building or maintaining databases and using SQL is a plus</li>
<li>APICS CPIM (Certified in Production and Inventory Management) or ISM (Institute of Supply Management) CPM (Certified Purchasing Manager) or CPSM (Certified Professional of Supply Management) certification</li>
</ul>
