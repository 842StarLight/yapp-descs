<h2>HubSpot Marketing Strategist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>As a top HubSpot partner agency, LyntonWeb works with some of the best and brightest companies and organizations looking to grow with the HubSpot stack. The marketing strategist role serves as the face and the personality of our company. The marketing strategist will form lasting, strategic partnerships and warm relationships with our clients and their team. The marketing strategist embraces all facets of the HubSpot Growth Stack and prescribes the software, processes, and services to help clients achieve their goals.</p>
<p>
As a Hubspot Marketing Strategist...</p>
<p><b>
You are:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
A customer advocate</li>
<li>
Adaptable to change and methodical in your approach</li>
<li>
Ambitious, goal-oriented for growth</li>
<li>
Creative and technical</li>
<li>
An analytical thinker</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
You will:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Serve as the client's single point of contact at the company and the client's evangelist within our team.</li>
<li>
Conduct meetings with the client including pre-sales, discovery and strategy, project status, monthly or quarterly account reviews, and the inevitable "last-minute firefighting".</li>
<li>
Collaborate with and manage internal resources responsible for HubSpot implementation, website development, and integration.</li>
<li>
Manage expectations within internal and external stakeholders.</li>
<li>
Present budgets and proposals to the client for ongoing or one-time services.</li>
<li>
Consult with agency clients to develop a marketing strategy and growth plan using the HubSpot platform, including but not limited to, inbound marketing, website, and marketing stack integrations.</li>
<li>
Oversee the implementation of new customers and assume responsibility for a successful onboarding.</li>
</ul>
<p>
Bonus Points:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Inbound Marketing experience/certification</li>
<li>
Experience working with 1 or more of the following CRMs: Microsoft Dynamics, NetSuite,<br>
Salesforce, SugarCRM at project level</li>
<li>
HubSpot Experience</li>
<li>
Experience working remotely</li>
<li>
Fluency in other languages</li>
</ul>
