<h2>Director of Marketing</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<b>Description:</b><br>
Summary/Objective
<br>The Director of Marketing will develop, manage and implement comprehensive strategies designed to build and strengthen multiple JV partnership brands as well as the brand of its corporate parent, Premier Health. The person in this role will report to the VP, Strategy and Development will work closely with that individual, as well as the marketing team, to design and implement comprehensive marketing strategies across all services lines and corporate endeavors. Key responsibilities include owning, developing, and overseeing the companyâ€™s brand strategy, messaging platform, sales and marketing collateral.
<br><br>
Essential Functions
<br><ul>
<li>Provides leadership and oversight of marketing/advertising, image branding, public relations, communications, and reputation management functions.</li>
<li>Develops branded marketing campaigns for new and existing initiatives as needed, including comprehensive marketing plans, logos, taglines, branded print collateral, promotional items, and original marketing copy.</li>
<li>Works with internal teams to evaluate data to determine marketing and service line strategies, and community involvement/sponsorships.</li>
<li>Conducts market research, performs evaluations, identifies specific business launch approaches and prepares presentations.</li>
<li>Analyzes, organizes, and improves existing public relations programs and efforts; identifies and initiates new public relations opportunities.</li>
<li>Participates in developing and executing strategies for growth into new markets through exploration, sourcing and originating new joint-venture relationship opportunities with health systems. Includes in-person meetings, information gathering sessions, and negotiating sessions.</li>
<li>Leads identification of merger/acquisition opportunities and potential diversification initiatives.</li>
<li>Leads efforts to enhance relationships with employer groups (in coordination with TOM) and managed care organizations.</li>
<li>Develops and leads implementation of customer loyalty strategies.</li>
<li>Collaborates with owners, executive management team, and other managers in identifying, developing, assessing, and executing business plan components and strategies.</li>
<li>Participates in department quality improvement activities, staff educational programs, attends departmental meetings, and demonstrates and maintains clinical competency in the delivery of patient care services in accordance with Company guidelines.</li>
<li>Prepares summaries and status reports and leads presentations on selected project reports and updates.</li>
<li>Performs additional duties as assigned.</li>
</ul>
.
