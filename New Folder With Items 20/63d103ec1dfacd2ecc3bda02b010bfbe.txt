GoSite is hiring a Senior Account Executive, Enterprise to join a new and exciting multi-location and franchise sales team. This role will function as a full cycle sales executive for approximately the first 90 days, with the responsibilities to pursue leads, qualify prospects, conduct product demonstrations and build relationships with key decisions makers to generate new opportunities. Upon hitting key first quarter targets, business development representatives will be hired to support this role in prospecting, qualifying and appointment setting. This role will then remain focused on the product demonstrations, strategic negotiations and successful acquisition of key accounts. The Senior Account Executive, Enterprise will be responsible for providing their team of three BDR's with the training, mentorship and guidance to be successful in their roles to support the overall success of the team. The Senior Account Executive, Enterprise will be expected to consistently meet and exceed their sales targets each quarter as well as work closely with the Enterprise Customer Success Specialist team to consistently grow and expand existing account relationships.
<br><br>

<li>Experience partnering with cross functional teams to expand, grow and renew accounts year over year</li>
