Highlights:</b>

As a driven and client-centric representative, you focus on highlighting the ways our fiber-based solutions simplify efforts and improve efficiency for local enterprise organizations. As an Enterprise Account Representative, you are passionate about proactively pursuing new accounts while supporting existing clients. You have a developed ability for identifying roadblocks and overcoming obstacles to increase business. Through your expertise and understanding of client needs, you outline uniquely beneficial combinations of our products. <div><b>
What you will do:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
Be an impactful member of the sales team through the achievement of monthly revenue goals and the promotion of marketing campaigns.</li>
<li>
Consult with prospective clients and develop tailored product solutions for their complex business needs using effective telephone-based sales techniques.</li>
<li>
Expand business and client base using a marketing-generated lead list, qualify new prospects through the submission of a return on investment (ROI) analysis and determine serviceability by requesting site surveys.</li>
<li>
Improve sales by building long-term client relationships mindful of opportunities for upsell potential, incremental sales and cross-selling.</li>
<li>
Enhance client confidence in our products by providing competitor knowledge, a side-by-side product comparison and coordinating efforts with other departments to resolve issues.</li>
<li>
Develop key sales skills by attending sales meetings and participating in training sessions.</li>
<li>
Perform additional duties related to the position as assigned.</li>
</ul>
