<h2>Clinical Technical Service Representative</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Polymedco Inc. is a leading manufacturer, marketer, and distributer in the clinical laboratory marketplace. Polymedco supplies clinical diagnostic test kits and devices that are specialized in hematology and cancer. We are a world leader in non-invasive colorectal cancer screening technology, providing the new standard of care with the OC FIT-CHEK® product line.</p>
<p>Since 1980, Polymedco has evolved into a highly skilled and successful marketing, sales, and manufacturing organization.</p>
<p>Combined with an experienced team of field representatives and strong technical service personnel, Polymedco provides a high level of customer, technical and reimbursement support.</p>
<p>Polymedco is located in northern Westchester County in the town of Cortlandt Manor, New York.</p>
<p>We are currently looking for a Clinical Technical Service Representative to join our team!</p>
<p>The Clinical Technical Service Representative will provide customers with on-site installation of and training on medical diagnostic equipment, reagents and testing kits. In addition, they will provide phone and field technical support and training for new hires/Sales Representatives, as well as perform laboratory testing on Polymedco products.</p>
<p>This is a great opportunity for independent, outgoing, motivated, initiative taking Lab Technicians or Technologists seeking to get out of the laboratory and work directly with customers and Sales Representatives.</p>
<p><b>Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Have excellent working knowledge of the systems including hardware, software, and reagents</li>
<li>Answer and return phone calls from customers and sales representatives to provide technical assistance with laboratory operations and product related inquiries/issues, as defined in all relevant departmental SOP’s.</li>
<li>Perform customer cold calls and maintain proper documentation</li>
<li>Travel up to 25% to 50% of time to customer sites to perform analyzer installation and analyzer and product training, as defined in all relevant SOP’s.</li>
<li>Working Hours are rotating 8-5 or 9-6 as needed. After hours on call required as needed.</li>
<li>Initiate, maintain and complete complaint tickets. Perform customer site assessments, necessary complaint and installation follow-up activities and maintain proper documentation.</li>
<li>Assist with new hire and sales representative training on products and equipment.</li>
<li>Assist in laboratory activities including, but not limited to, proficiency testing, product qualification studies, troubleshooting studies, customer correlation and various special studies.</li>
<li>Assist in special projects as requested and other related duties as necessary.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Qualifications</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Minimum of Associates Degree in Medical Technology plus working experience in a clinical laboratory or other clinically related field.</li>
<li>Prior supervisory experience a plus</li>
<li>Have excellent time management and organizational skills.</li>
<li>Must be flexible to multitask, be organized and perform different tasks at the same time.</li>
<li>Have good voice, diction and customer handling skills</li>
<li>Working knowledge of Microsoft Word, Excel, Power Point, Access and Internet Explorer.</li>
</ul>
