<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Overview</b></h2>

Patterson Companies, a Star Tribune Top 50 Company, is seeking a qualified leader for our Senior Manager Security Operations &amp; Engineering role. This role will be located at our corporate headquarters in Mendota Heights, MN and will report to the Director of Information Security.</div>

The Senior Security Manager provides leadership, as well as strategic and operational direction to a diverse team, potentially including analysts, engineers and architects. This role requires proven successful leadership experience and relevant technical expertise to help deliver successful results of the information security program.</div>

(Nasdaq: PDCO) is a value-added distributor focused on providing best-in-class products, services, technology and experiences to dental and animal health markets in North America and the U.K. Additional offerings include: software and equipment sales and services, practice design and management solutions, patient and staff education, regulatory compliance, and technology support and expertise from the Patterson Technology Center.</p>
