<div><b>
POSITION PURPOSE:</b></div>
<div>
The Route Planner II is responsible for handling the day to day routing system maintenance of all regional customers. The position will analyze past and current routing to develop efficient and cost-effective solutions, facilitate customer reroutes and work with the IBL team to recognize potential backhaul opportunities utilizing RoadNet Transportation Suite.</div>
<div>
</div>
<div><b>
Job Responsibilities:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
Maintain customer profiles in RoadNet, Trimble, CoolTrax (UBS, WBS)</li>
<li>
Routes new customers/special deliveries; communicate information to customer care and local DC</li>
<li>
Performs post route analysis and compares RoadNet plan to actual, using the results as the basis for change to routing or customer profiles in RoadNet</li>
<li>
Verify all new accounts and determine best routing solution.</li>
<li>
Track and analyze results and provide savings opportunities to Centralized Routing Supervisor</li>
<li>
Work closely with Sales and Warehouse departments to determine delivery feasibility</li>
<li>
Create relationship with IBL coordinators, develop initiatives to identify and maximize backhaul opportunities</li>
<li>
Routing, database maintenance, and analysis/reporting as geographically assigned</li>
<li>
Update map data including: Rush hour patterns, Historical travel speed %, Road speeds, construction.</li>
<li>
Ensure effective two-way communication with all Distribution Centers to improve accuracy of the database through field and driver input</li>
<li>
All other duties as assigned</li>
</ul>
