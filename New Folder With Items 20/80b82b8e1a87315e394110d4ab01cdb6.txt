<h2>Sr Digital Product Consultant</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Job Description:</b></p>
<p></p>
<p>
Bank of America’s consumer cash-back rewards program, BankAmeriDeals, is seeking a Senior Digital Product Consultant to help advance the development of innovative and rewarding customer experiences and partnerships. The successful candidate will have the opportunity to create a strategic roadmap and drive execution of these initiatives through partnerships with brands, retailers, fintechs, and digital wallets; while providing leadership and management over the team's operational and business controls process.</p>
<p></p>
<p>
The ideal candidate will be a forward thinking leader with a proven track record of successfully bringing next generation products, services and technologies to market. The role requires an individual with a strong combination of strategic thinking, detailed tactical planning, and project management expertise.</p>
<p></p>
<p>
Candidates should have a solid understanding of banking products and services, digital strategies [payments, affiliate partnerships, mobile sales, digital advertising] and be proactive in their approach, with an ability to own and manage multiple priorities simultaneously. Familiarity with agile development methodology is ideal.</p>
<p></p>
<p>
As a Senior Digital Product Consultant, the successful candidate will have the opportunity to serve as a primary conduit for and between Bank of America’s Digital business, product, marketing, operations, technology, internal counsel/control, and line of business partners; and will have accountability for establishing/maintaining strong relationships and building consensus with peers and partners across the organization.</p>
<p></p>
<p>
Responsibilities:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Manage BankAmeriDeals business operations, processes, and risk controls.</li>
<li>
Drives quality assurance program standard adherence through the identification, measurement, monitoring, and mitigation plan development regarding business processes.</li>
<li>
Ensure that processes are appropriately documented, risks are assessed, and metrics are developed and monitored to ensure appropriate visibility is provided to senior executives regarding over health as well as potential issues and problems.</li>
<li><p>
Actively monitor effectiveness of the control environment via process and control performance, system impacts, regulatory impacts, issue alignment, and independent testing/monitoring</p></li>
<li><p>
Provide visibility to control performance failures and potential issues to leadership, and direct development of activities to improve</p></li>
<li>
Partner with external vendors, digital wallets, and retailers to identify integration opportunities that facilitate the development of new digital experiences and partnerships within BankAmeriDeals</li>
<li>
Coordinate and lead cross line-of-business partner alignment to drive initiative strategy, planning, stakeholder approvals and execution pathing.</li>
<li>
Establish and maintain clear execution routines for initiative continuity and regular management briefing/status reporting.</li>
<li>
Collaborate with executive management to shape the Bank of America Digital and partnership roadmap.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p>
Required Skills:</p>
<ul>
<li>
5 or more years of Business Control, Compliance and/or Risk experience</li>
<li><p>
2 or more years of experience in Digital product execution and delivery, with demonstrated excellence in product and project management.</p></li>
<li><p>
Ability to succinctly convey information to executive leaders and stakeholders</p></li>
<li>
Proven ability to bring new products/services to market.</li>
<li>
Exceptional expertise with Microsoft Power Point and Office suite.</li>
<li>
Excellent communication [verbal, written, presentation] and networking skills; able to drive consensus.</li>
<li>
Strong interest and knowledge of emerging technology trends and applications.</li>
<li>
Comfortable working in a fast-paced, highly collaborative, customer-driven environment.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>

Shift:</b></p>
1st shift (United States of America)<p></p>
