<h2>Automotive mechanic / Auto technician</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Looking for an automotive mechanic to join our family in the Renton area. Our business model to build customer satisfaction through honest, loyal, genuine customer service with high quality automotive repairs.</p>
<p>Our business goals are to create positive results for our customers, teammates and our company. We strive to have all our customers and employees to have an enjoyable experience. Ideal applicant takes pride in their work and understands the quality of work our customers expect. We are searching for team members with the following skills and attributes to join our family.</p>
<p>Duties: Communicate a positive attitude to all customers and teammates. Adhering to company policies and procedures to ensure a safe and environmentally responsible work place. Perform inspections, diagnose and perform mechanical adjustments, repairs and parts replacements for company, fleet and consignment vehicles and mechanical equipment.</p>
<p>Key Responsibilities: Inspect vehicles for damage, missing parts, mechanical problems and vandalism. Diagnose problems and estimate repair costs according to appropriate guidelines. Order repair parts as instructed and perform designated repairs (i.e. replace brake pads, rotors, radiators, gaskets, water pump, hoses, plugs, wires, distributor cap, switches, mirrors, knobs, tires, etc.) Perform routine vehicle maintenance on personal and company vehicles and trucks (i.e. mechanical work on brakes, fuel pumps, alternators, air conditioning, oil changes, tire balancing and rotation, replacing batteries, etc. must have your own tools and a good knowledge of general auto repair. Must be able to change engines, do head gaskets, heater cores, change transmissions as well as diagnostics on all makes of cars and trucks. Must be able to meet or beat book time with low come back rate.</p>
<p>We are looking for good all around mechanics. We are a very busy shop. Rate of pay depends on experience and tools. We offer paid vacations. ASE certificates a plus but not required, welding and exhaust experience a plus also.</p>
