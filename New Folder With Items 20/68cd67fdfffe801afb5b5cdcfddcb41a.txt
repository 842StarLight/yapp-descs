<p><b>Role and Responsibilities
</b></p>
<p></p>
<p>As the Sr. Manager, Key Accounts, you will be responsible for:
</p>
<ul>
<li>Foster strong relationships with key accounts at the C-Suite to operational contacts in order to successfully position Samsung's mobile solutions.
</li>
<li>Revenue generation from mobile solutions including hardware, services and security in Strategic key accounts within a geographic area or market.
</li>
<li>Perform long range planning to increase product placement, customer satisfaction and sales growth for long term results and achievement of assigned sales targets
</li>
<li>Ensure sales plans and key account strategies are implemented.
</li>
<li>Higher autonomy to direct some level of reasoning and logical analyses in order to form solutions to problems.
</li>
<li>Manage communications, sales promotion development, and training as required to stimulate and facilitate the sale of Samsung's mobile products.
</li>
<li>Driving moderately complex collaboration across functional teams, partners, strategic alliances and headquarters
</li>
<li>Understanding customer requirements and how Samsung Mobile solutions align to solve problems and deliver value.
</li>
<li>Maintain working knowledge of Hand held Mobile products, Tablets, wearables, Solutions and Services into strategic corporate end user accounts.
</li>
<li>Follow and understand business market trends.
</li>
<li>Work independently and travel up to 70% of the time.
</li>
</ul>
