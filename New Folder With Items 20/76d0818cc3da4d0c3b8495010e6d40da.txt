<h2>Financial Services Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p>Responsible for coordinating the sale of finance and insurance programs to customers. The Finance Manager works with lenders and financial institutions to provide financial services to dealership customers.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Supervisory Responsibilities: </b>This job has no direct supervisory responsibilities.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Essential Duties and Responsibilities </b><b>include the following:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Sells financing and insurance to customers.
</li>
<li>Responsible for the attainment and gross production of financial services’ goals and objectives.
</li>
<li>Reviews customer credit applications.
</li>
<li>Presents extended service contracts and other owner protection programs available to customers.
</li>
<li>Assesses profitability to dealership of financing arrangements
</li>
<li>Utilizes the menu selling process.
</li>
<li>Ensures necessary documentation for each deal is complete by utilizing a deal checklist.
</li>
<li>Works closely with sales team, maintains effective employee relations
</li>
<li>Maintains AFIP Certification.
</li>
<li>Acts as liaison between the customer and the lending institution.
</li>
<li>Ensures adherence to F&amp;I reporting requirements of company.
</li>
<li>Attends weekly manager meetings.
</li>
<li>Maintains CSI at or above Company standards
</li>
<li>Maintains an organized, clean and safe work area
</li>
<li>Participates in required training
</li>
<li>Records all hours worked accurately in company timekeeping system
</li>
<li>Follows Safeguards rules and regulations.
</li>
<li>Demonstrates the Company’s Core Values
</li>
<li>Complies with Company policies and procedures
</li>
<li>Observes all Federal, State, Local and Company safety rules and regulations in the performance of duties.
</li>
<li>Other duties as assigned
</li>
</ul>
