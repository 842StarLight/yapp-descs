<h2>Surveillance Tech Support</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>
<b>Job Description</b><br>
<br>
</div>
<div>We are looking for skilled, detail oriented Technician to join our team. If you are a tech savvy, with experience in troubleshooting, possess good verbal and written communications skills, this position is right for you.<br>
<br>
</div>
<div><b>Responsibilities:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
Provide troubleshoot and CCTV technical Support to customers through phone and remote devices</li>
<li>
Identify, document, and report any quality problems to production development Manager</li>
<li>
Perform Product inspection, insure product meet the quality requirements</li>
<li>
Accurately enters information on production and quality control into the computer system</li>
<li>
Test and troubleshoot returned products, provide solutions for the sales team or clients</li>
<li>
Guide customers with RMA process, policies; follow up with RMA status</li>
<li>
Maintain network and phone service stability, solve any technical issues that might disrupt business operations</li>
</ul>
<div>
<br>
<b>
Qualifications:</b>
</div>
<ul>
<li>
Experience in troubleshooting and installing CCTV systems is preferred</li>
<li>
Knowledge of wired and wireless networking, including access points, managed switches, and enterprise-grade routers</li>
<li>
Excellent customer service skill</li>
<li>
Good English oral and written communication skills</li>
<li>
Good record keeping and project management skill</li>
<li>
Multi-tasking, detail orientated, able to work effectively</li>
<li>
Detail oriented, responsible and self-motivated</li>
</ul>
