<h2>Hospitality Finance Analyst</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>About NCR
</b></p>
<p>NCR Corporation (NYSE: NCR) is a leading software- and services-led enterprise provider in the financial, retail, hospitality, telecom and technology industries. NCR is headquartered in Atlanta, Ga., with 34,000 employees and does business in 180 countries. NCR is a trademark of NCR Corporation in the United States and other countries.
</p>
<p></p>
<p>This is an opportunity to join the Hospitality Finance team and be responsible for various aspects of analysis, reporting, forecasting, planning, and special projects. This position will support senior management across several functional areas including by not limited to Sales, Operations, Product Development and Marketing. In collaboration with the functional team leaders, this position will analyze market issues, develop strategy, pricing decisions, product development and partnerships.
</p>
<p></p>
<p>The Hospitality business generates over $800M of revenues per year and is a strategic priority for the growth of NCR. This position will report to the Hospitality manager priority responsible for Software and Strategic Growth Platforms.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>KEY AREAS OF RESPONSIBILITY:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Assist in monthly forecasting and annual strategic planning
</li>
<li>Develop financial models for new products and solutions and analyze financial impact of partnerships and M&amp;A opportunities
</li>
<li>Responsible for developing a suite of business metrics and reports to support business needs aligned to Dashboards &amp; KPI’s from relevant systems
</li>
<li>Maintain the rollout schedule of pre-defined reports and generate and customize reports in response to specific business requests
</li>
<li>Consistently look at process to identify opportunities for improved accuracy and efficiencies
</li>
<li>Work in a collaborative and globally diverse environment as a team player and perform multiple tasks simultaneously
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>BASIC QUALIFICATIONS:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s Degree in Accounting, Finance, or other related Business discipline
</li>
<li>Self-starter with strong teaming skills
</li>
<li>Ability to quickly learn new processes, systems, tools
</li>
<li>Strong oral/written communication and presentation skills
</li>
<li>Excellent time management and ability to work under pressure
</li>
<li>Demonstrate a level of responsibility for taking ownership of projects
</li>
<li>Experience with Microsoft Office and collaboration tools (e.g. OneDrive, SharePoint, Teams)
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>PREFERRED QUALIFICATIONS:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Experience with Microsoft Office, Business Objects, SQL, Essbase (or comparable tools)
</li>
<li>Proficient in database management, Oracle, EDW and Hyperion Finance Management systems
</li>
<li>Ability to adopt next generation business analytics and planning tools (e.g. Anaplan, Tableau)
</li>
</ul>
