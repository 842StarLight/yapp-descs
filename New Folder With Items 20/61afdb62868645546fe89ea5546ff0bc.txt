<h2>Mid-Market Sales Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div><b>Job Brief:</b></div>
<div>
We are looking for a talented Sales Leader to join our fast growing Klaviyo Sales organization. The Mid-Market Sales Manager will be responsible for leading a team of 4+ Account Executives that sell into the Mid-Market ($20m-$500m in revenue). This includes hiring, training, forecast management, performance analysis, and day-to-day coaching/talent development. This team will do a mix of inbound, outbound, and agency referred deals. We're looking for someone that has successfully scaled a Mid-Market team in a growing SaaS company and has experience selling to marketers.
