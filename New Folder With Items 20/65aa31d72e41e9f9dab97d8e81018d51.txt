<b>About Hardware Express:</b><br>
We are a local business with three generations of family working together alongside our fellow employees, serving the communities in which we live. We began in 2001, new to the hardware industry, with two stores totaling 12,000 square feet; one in Redding and one in Anderson. We added a third 6,000 square foot store in Shasta Lake that same year. Since then, we have tripled our square footage of retail space in the Greater Redding market and expanded into Oregon. We own and operate a 20,000 square foot store in Coos Bay near the beautiful southern Oregon coast.

At our Ace Hardware stores, we are dedicated to being people oriented. We have many responsibilities, but the most important responsibility is to provide the very best customer service possible.

<b>Job Summary:</b><br>
Greets, listens and helps every customer available to obtain merchandise desired. Also, receives merchandise, totals bill, accepts payment, and makes change for customers in retail store by performing the following duties.
<br>The Sales Associate will assist in the receiving, stocking, pricing of all merchandise and help maintain a clean and orderly merchandise presentation and overall store cleanliness.
<br><br>
Essential Duties and Responsibilities include the following. Other duties may be assigned.
<br><ul>
<li>Stocks shelves, counters, or tables with merchandise.</li>
<li>Sets up advertising displays or arranges merchandise on counters or tables to promote sales.</li>
<li>Stamps, marks, or tags price on merchandise.</li>
<li>Obtains merchandise requested by customer or receives merchandise selected by customer.</li>
<li>Answers customer's questions concerning location, price, and use of merchandise.</li>
<li>Totals price and tax on merchandise (including any discounts) purchased by customer to determine bill.</li>
<li>Accepts payment and makes change.</li>
<li>Wraps or bags merchandise for customers.</li>
<li>Cleans shelves, counters, or tables.</li>
<li>Assemble items for sale</li>
<li>Stocks rental/ service center shelves, counters, or tables with merchandise.</li>
<li>Sets up advertising displays on rental floor and service area in order to promote sales.</li>
<li>Stamps, marks, or tags price on rental merchandise.</li>
<li>Obtains rental merchandise requested by customer or receives rental merchandise selected by customer.</li>
<li>Answers customer's questions concerning location, price, and use of rental merchandise.</li>
<li>Totals rent, fees, and deposits on rentals by customer to determine bill.</li>
<li>Accepts payment and makes change.</li>
<li>Assists customers in obtaining rental equipment and informs customers about the use of the equipment.</li>
<li>Keeps service area &amp; warehouse workbench clean, neat &amp; stocked.</li>
<li>Keeps rental inventory stocked.</li>
<li>Maintains and cleans rental equipment.</li>
<li>Be available to learn to use: forklift, pallet jack, power saws, or be a possible company driver.</li>
</ul>
