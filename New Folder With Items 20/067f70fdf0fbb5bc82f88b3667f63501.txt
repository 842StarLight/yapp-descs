<h2>Associate Strategy Planner (Freelance), MRM//McCann</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div></div>
<div>
<div>MRM // McCann is seeking an Associate Strategic Planner (ASP). This is a rare opportunity to break into the strategic planning team of a top global agency.</div>
<div></div>
<div>
<b>You:</b> Inside of you lives a curiosity that always drives you to ask <i>“why?”</i> and occasionally sends you down a deep Wikipedia-rabbit-hole. You believe equally in the power of a truly great creative idea and the value of sound data. You’re a persuasive writer. You love a good story and you read—more than just on your phone. You know how to analyze and synthesize information to get to the big “aha.”</div>
<div></div>
<div>You’re comfortable both in the limelight and behind the scenes. You want to be part of an agency that rewards loyalty, promotes from within, and appreciates people’s individual quirks.</div>
<div></div>
<div>Most importantly: you want to learn to be an exceptional Planner and to help produce award-winning work that matters.</div>
<div></div>
<div>
<b>Us:</b> We’re MRM // McCann, part of the global McCann Worldgroup network. Our Strategic Planning team members develop deep understanding of target audiences and help translate those insights into effective creative work that motivates action.</div>
<div></div>
<div>We’ve got clients you’ve actually heard of and, as an ASP, you’d have the opportunity to touch many of them. We’ve designed our ASP role to support several account teams at once. This gives you the chance to:</div>
<ul><li>Learn about various client industries</li></ul>
<ul><li>Work directly with several different Strategy Directors, Creative teams and Account Directors</li></ul>
<ul><li>Gain experience in many areas of the business, including: research, audience insight development, brand planning, comms planning, advertising, marketing, digital strategy and UX, social, RM, sales and event support</li></ul>
<div></div>
<div>We want you to challenge us and our thinking. And we intend to challenge the hell out of you.</div>
<div></div>
<div><b>RESPONSIBILITIES</b></div>
<div></div>
<div><b>Research Assistance, Audience Championing + Storytelling</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Be a scrappy researcher intent on helping your Strategic Planning team members more deeply understand our clients’ businesses, target audiences, and competitors</li>
<li>Help distill complex research and data into simple, compelling and insightful narratives</li>
<li>Learn to develop discussion guides, research briefs, audience personas, customer journeys, competitive audits, messaging architectures, and agency POVs</li>
<li>Play the role of champion for the target audience, ensuring that agency work is as impactful for him or her as possible</li>
<li>Learn to craft beautiful, tightly defined creative briefs and partner with Creative team members to help produce extraordinary work</li>
<li>Other duties as assigned</li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<div><b>QUALIFICATIONS</b></div>
<div></div>
<div>Currently Pursuing a BA or BS in marketing, advertising, public relations, journalism, sociology, psychology, or related field</div>
<div></div>
<div>Skills</div>
<div></div>
<div>Exceptional ability to get things done</div>
<div>Exceptional collaboration skills</div>
<div>Solid storyteller with excellent written, verbal, and presentation skills</div>
<div>Able to take guidance and feedback from multiple sources, process it, and put it into practice</div>
<div>Some experience using secondary or syndicated research tools preferred</div>
<div>Bonus points for any candidates experienced in assisting in the design or facilitation of primary research methods, in either a professional or academic setting</div>
