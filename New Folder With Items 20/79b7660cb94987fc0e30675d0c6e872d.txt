<p><b>
The Work You'll Do:</b></p>
<p>
The Implementation Services supervisor is responsible for the Professional Services team for the Business-to-Government Reporting product line for Spanish Speaking Americas. The Supervisor is responsible for ensuring that all implementations and change requests are responded to and completed in a timely fashion while delivering an outstanding customer service experience. This role is also responsible for aligning departmental goals with company objectives and strategic anchors.<br>
 </p>
<p></p>
<p>A key role for the Implementation Services Supervisor is to be a champion of customer concerns and ensure details are professionally and adequately managed by the implementation team. This supervisor must ensure to bridge communications between customer opportunities for product, sales, and support services. Implementation Services are the base for a solid relationship with the customer business and IT team and this manager should execute competent plans to promote customer success while maintaining scope.<br>
</p>
