<h2>Project Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>We are looking for an experienced electronic security Project Manager to join a growing Building Controls Integrator in Maryland. In the past decade this company has experienced significant growth, and is well positioned to continue that trend in the Maryland, DC and Northern, VA markets.</p>
<p>
Compensation commensurate with experience, in the $110,000 range, plus bonus, company provided vehicle, and exceptional benefits. Selected candidate will have Software House experience (certification desired) and be willing to travel occasionally out of state.
</p>
<p>Job responsibilities include:</p>
<ul>
<li>Coordinate, plan and schedule details of projects implementation and financial plan.</li>
<li>Track and manage project plan and schedule</li>
<li>Plan, schedule and coordinate the activities of electrical, network and engineering subcontractors in a rapidly changing project environment.</li>
<li>Drive Processes with knowledge to labor laws, construction contracts, construction administration, and be familiar with construction dispute resolution processes.</li>
<li>Manage contract documents written scope of work to insure customer satisfaction.</li>
<li>Anticipate, recognize, and negotiate changes from documentation scope of work to insure customer satisfaction.</li>
<li>Responsible for written and verbal communications, along with keeping a very organized work and documentation flow process.</li>
<li>Provide written costs estimates, supporting narrative justifications, and proposals as necessary</li>
<li>Manage multiple projects simultaneously.
</li>
</ul>
<p>Job Requirements:</p>
<p>
Five or more years of successful Electronic Security or Life Safety Project/Construction management experience.
</p>
<p>Verifiable track record of consistently meeting budget and timeline objectives</p>
<p>
Ability to provide spec review, revenue forecasting, final technical review, definition and adherence to work process, manpower scheduling, sales support, estimate review, and customer satisfaction.</p>
<p>
Valid driver's license and ability to pass drug screen and background check.</p>
<p><b>
Company provides above average compensation and outstanding benefits in a team oriented work environment. Career opportunities are numerous with this growing company.</b></p>
</div>
