<div><b>Advertising Sales Representatives:</b></div>
<div></div>
<div>Our Advertising Sales Representatives are responsible for securing new business while maintaining lasting relationships with current business client accounts. Exemplary interpersonal skills, a high ability to influence others and strong listening skills are all key to success in this position.<br>
