<h2>Senior Business Analyst</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Creative Group, a Direct Travel company, designs global programs and experiences that reward excellence and inspire people to reach their full potential – we call it Thrivability. Creative Group is a full-service performance improvement company specializing in meetings and events, group incentive travel, employee engagement and recognition, sales incentives and channel loyalty programs. Creative Group serves a client roster of Fortune 500 companies in a broad range of industries. Our employees reflect our Vision and Core Beliefs in everything we do.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p>The primary duties of this position are the monthly reporting of financial results, planning and forecasting and secondary duties include ad-hoc analysis. This individual will develop financial models through benchmarking and process analysis and will be responsible for financial planning, analysis, and projections.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Essential Functions:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Prepares the monthly/quarterly financial presentation of Creative Group’s consolidated financial performance for monthly meeting with senior leaders. Includes commentary and description of key performance drivers. Compares anticipated and actual results and identifies areas of improvement.
</li>
<li>Performs regular, weekly, monthly and quarterly ad-hoc reporting and analysis.
</li>
<li>Provides senior leaders with monthly dashboards with variance key metrics.
</li>
<li>Forecasts models for revenue changes and expenditure increases/decreases.
</li>
<li>Supports and drives the annual and quarterly budgeting process and quarterly forecasts.
</li>
<li>Reviews departmental actual expenses versus budget.
</li>
<li>Reviews accounting transactions for data accuracy.
</li>
<li>Interprets and analyzes program and client account performance through the lens of pricing, job costs, and gross margins.
</li>
<li>Translates accounting results into financial analysis.
</li>
<li>Communicates regularly with senior management as needed.
</li>
<li>Supports operational projects by performing a variety of analysis.
</li>
<li>Identifies reliable conclusions through data analysis to help implement more effective operational strategies.
</li>
<li>Builds successful partnerships with key internal clients and cross functional teams.
</li>
<li>Performs other duties as assigned.<br>
</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Minimum Requirements/Qualifications:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s Degree in Accounting or Finance.
</li>
<li>A CFA or CPA certification is preferred.
</li>
<li>Strong knowledge or exposure to Accounting.
</li>
<li>Knowledge of forecasting models and cost accounting required.
</li>
<li>Minimum 5 years of Accounting or Finance related experience.
</li>
<li>Well adept in Microsoft Excel and Office Products. SQL is a plus.
</li>
<li>Familiarity with financial data bases and experience with Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP) System.
</li>
<li>Up to date knowledge of accounting laws and regulations.
</li>
<li>Sales Force knowledge is a plus.
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Non-Negotiable Hiring Criteria:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Must possess excellent attention to detail and analytical skills to reconcile, analyze general ledger entries, make appropriate recommendations to improve the process, to analyze income and balance sheet accounts, and prepare various analytical reports.
</li>
<li>Must possess good interpersonal skills to deal effectively with different levels of management within the organization and to interact well with financial personnel from other entities.
</li>
<li>Must possess very good organizational and planning skills to be able to handle a variety of responsibilities concurrently, to assist in closing the books in a timely manner, on a monthly basis.
</li>
<li>Must possess strong written communication skills to prepare presentations, and to be able to present financial data using detailed reports and charts.
</li>
<li>Must be able to demonstrate strategic thinking skills to be able to generate bold, creative ideas to improve company’s performance.
</li>
<li>Must demonstrate financial modeling, problem solving, and quantitative skills.
</li>
<li>Must be able to maintain confidentiality in handling sensitive financial information.<br>
<br>
</li>
</ul>
<p>Direct Travel is an EOE/AA/Veteran/People with Disabilities employer.</p>
