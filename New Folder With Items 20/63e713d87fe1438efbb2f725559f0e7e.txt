<h2>Medical Practice Manager (Center Manager)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>The Practice Manager (Center Manager) is responsible for the overall performance of the operational, financial and people-related center level goals. The incumbent in this role partners closely with the center’s clinical leadership to ensure patient care, service and clinical metrics meet and exceed expectations. He/She is an individual that, first and foremost, has a strong desire to serve others. The Center Manager must be equally comfortable greeting patients in the waiting room, engaging with PCPs to further the ChenMed Way, leading a diverse staff, growing a business and owning a profit and loss budget. This service-oriented individual will have a keen understanding of how serving others results in staff that are better developed and more capable, and patients that are more engaged in their health, thereby yielding better patient outcomes.</p>
<p><b>ESSENTIAL DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES</b>:</p>
<ul>
<li>Owns and operates an average budget in excess of $25M.</li>
<li>Leads people, coordinates and inspires the team and achieves results under challenging circumstances.</li>
<li>Works comfortably with financial statements, and financial concepts, in a service organization.</li>
<li>Provides extraordinary customer service to all internal and external customers (<i>including patients and other Chen Medical team members</i>).</li>
<li>Monitors office communication and performance to ensure that front desk, referrals and care promoter teams operate in an efficient, accurate and customer-focused manner.</li>
<li>Collaborates with team leaders to conduct performance evaluations of team members and complete any required disciplinary actions.</li>
<li>Monitors the number of patients and wait times to ensure proper flow of patients from check-in through appointment to check-out.</li>
<li>Addresses and resolves all customer-service or team member issues.</li>
<li>Ensures accuracy of all incoming and outgoing information including supplies, invoices, billing requests and patient records.</li>
<li>Works in partnership with the Sales and Marketing COE in developing strategies to increase and retain membership.</li>
<li>Applies a bias towards frugality and creativity when it comes to problem solving for operational challenges, seeking to improve people, process and systems resolutions before increasing costs and complexity.</li>
<li>Utilizes a keen sense of business acumen that assists with understanding our business, appreciating the various operating levers to utilize for success and driving significant new patient growth in their center.</li>
<li>Demonstrates an astute sense of ownership; the successful Center Manager will drive success of the center from all dimensions – people, finance, service, growth and outcomes, as if they owned the organization.</li>
<li>Maintains an awareness of the competitive healthcare environment; actively drive sales and ensures that ChenMed et.al’s level of service exceeds all other healthcare providers.</li>
<li>Performs other duties as assigned and modified at manager’s discretion.</li>
</ul>
<p><b><i>Other responsibilities may include: </i></b></p>
<ul>
<li>Conducts tours for prospective patients and ensures New Patient paperwork is completed and in chart.</li>
<li>Works with PCPs to assist with scheduling meetings with patients and/or writing letters to/for patients.</li>
<li>Reviews reports to ensure target metrics are achieved and processes are being followed.</li>
<li>Ensures co-pays are compliantly collected and cash is reconciled and deposited.</li>
<li>Conducts walk-through of the Center to ensure a clean and safe environment, including spot checks of back office for expired medications, proper biohazard waste and medicine disposal, etc.</li>
<li>Conducts trainings and refreshers regarding Medicare and HIPAA compliance, ensures annual OSHA trainings occur and employees have all required certifications current in their files.</li>
<li>Monitors transportation and housekeeping activities.</li>
<li>Monitors and/or alters team member work schedules, including approval of overtime or vacations.</li>
<li>Collaborates with the Leadership Team and Administrators in relation to strategic business planning.</li>
</ul>
<p><i><b>Competencies: </b></i></p>
<ul>
<li>Drives Results: Consistently achieves results, even under tough circumstances.</li>
<li>Communicates Effectively: Developing and delivering multi-mode communications that convey a clear understanding of the unique needs of different audiences.</li>
<li>Builds Effective Teams: Building strong identity teams that apply their diverse skills and perspectives to achieve common goals.</li>
<li>Situational Adaptability: Effectively building formal and informal relationship networks inside and outside the organization.</li>
<li>Customer Focus: Building strong customer-centric relationships and delivering customer-centric solutions.</li>
<li>Demonstrates Self-awareness: Using a combination of feedback and reflection to gain productive insight into personal strengths and weaknesses.</li>
<li>Business Insight: Applying knowledge of business and the marketplace to advance the organization's goals.</li>
<li>Financial Acumen: Interpreting and applying understanding of key financial indicators to make better business decisions.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>KNOWLEDGE, SKILLS AND ABILITIES: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>The experience, skills and abilities commensurate with managing an entity that exceeds $25M in revenue.</li>
<li>Ability to learn with an openness and curiosity about the ChenMed Way and willingness to adapt to our innovative approach to improving health outcomes.</li>
<li>Strong track record of taking on new assignments and quickly mastering new ways of accomplishing goals.</li>
<li>Exceptional written, verbal and interpersonal communication skills.</li>
<li>Ability to effectively communicate with employees, patients and other individuals in a professional and courteous manner.</li>
<li>Exceptionally detail-oriented with a high degree of objectivity and analytical skills to ensure accuracy of reports and data.</li>
<li>Ability to manage multiple projects and processes and work effectively with other team members.</li>
<li>Proficient in Microsoft Office Suite products including Excel, Word, PowerPoint and Outlook, plus a variety of other word-processing, spreadsheet, database, e-mail and presentation software.</li>
<li>Ability and willingness to travel locally, regionally and nationwide up to 20% of the time.</li>
<li>Spoken and written fluency in English.</li>
<li>This position requires use and exercise of independent judgment.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>EDUCATION AND EXPERIENCE CRITERIA: </b></p>
<ul><li>High school diploma with a minimum of 12 years’ related internal and/or external work experience in same or similar service industry, preferable healthcare or hospitality; <b>OR</b>
</li></ul>
<ul>
<li>Associate degree with a minimum of 10 years’ related internal and/or external work experience in same or similar service industry, preferable healthcare or hospitality; <b>OR</b>
</li>
<li>Bachelor’s degree in Business Administration, Hospitality, Healthcare Administration or a related discipline with a minimum of 7 years’ related internal and/or external work experience in same or similar service industry, preferable healthcare or hospitality.</li>
<li>
<b>FOR EXTERNAL CANDIDATES</b> the above plus: A minimum of 5 years’ progressive supervisory/managerial experience required. A minimum of 3 years’ Income Statement management experience required.</li>
<li>Master’s degree in Business Administration, Healthcare Administration or related discipline preferred.</li>
</ul>
