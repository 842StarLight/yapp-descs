<p>
Onsite Technical Management
</p>
<p>The Onsite Technical Manager builds relationships within and account to become a support services trusted advisor focused on the total customer experience and satisfaction. OTM leverages various Dell Technologies resources to ensure incident resolution, drive proactive activities promoting system health and customer satisfaction. In addition, provides detailed customer reporting using a cross section of services tools and applications. The OTMin this role is responsible for ensuring contractual service/support requirements are understood and managed to the support service requirement of the customer. The OTM will also ensures effective communication and coordination of problem-solving efforts between support teams, account teams, field service personnel and customers. The OTM primarily performs support service activities spanning three major workflow categories: installation/diagnosis/repair, escalation support and preventative maintenance as entitled by warranty level.</p>
<p>
Key Responsibilities:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Installation: Performs scheduled hardware platform implementations, installations or upgrades including pre-installation site readiness, rack and stack of relatively short duration installations, or configurations. Coordinates with delivery teams (local or VSD) as needed. May be assigned to work as part of a larger project delivery team.</li>
<li>
Preventative and Corrective Maintenance: Performs scheduled, pro-active and reactive maintenance of Dell Technologies equipment and software at customer locations, diagnosing, managing and correcting hardware/software problems that are complex in nature. The OTM will provide suggestions and strategies of upgrades for all Dell Technology firmware and software suites. Additionally, day-to-day site operations tasks such as hardware moves, equipment decommissioning and customer security compliances.</li>
<li>
Assist customer and sales team with design, definition, and implementation of complex system requirements for mission support and analyze existing systems according to customer requirements and mission needs. Support the technical and design direction for a proposed IT solution with ability to link solution sets to mission outcome.</li>
<li>
Poses deep technical acumen and understanding of the pan Dell Technologies portfolio to include storage, compute, networking, applications, cybersecurity, converged and hyper-converged systems, and edge-core-cloud synergies. Understand the advantages and disadvantages associated with various architectures (including on- versus off premise solutions), especially as these relate to mission, expense, and maintenance.</li>
<li>
Perform critical functions of assisting customer, sales team, and systems integrator with the fielding of system into its mission environment and the subsequent sustainment and maintenance of it.</li>
<li>
Perform diagnostic and hardware repair service as required. Diagnose any system malfunction to isolate problems to Dell, other OEM vendors or software.</li>
<li>
Perform firmware updates on a variety of platforms and deliver software updates (Application, Firmware, BIOS, Driver, and Diagnostics).</li>
<li>
Serve as single point of warranty support; provide customer account management on a 24 x 7 x 365 basis, including resource coordination, customer management, technical resolution management, and communications at various levels during warranty services engagements.</li>
</ul>
