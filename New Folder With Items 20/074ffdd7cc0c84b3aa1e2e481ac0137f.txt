<h2>Placement Assistance Technician</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Ohio Valley Goodwill is seeking a reliable, people-oriented individual to be responsible for Outreach and Recruitment activities for potential PAC clients. Provides job leads for unemployed veterans and/or family members and other disadvantaged groups.</p>
<p>
REQUIREMENTS:</p>
<p>
High School diploma required, Associate’s Degree preferred; will accept appropriate work experience in lieu of Associate degree. Minimum of one year experience working with disadvantaged populations; minimum two years’ outside commission sales or placement experience. Must be able to communicate effectively with veterans, homeless personas and ex-offenders; must have knowledge of local social service agencies and their services; must communicate effectively with private business and industry representatives; knowledge of computers and Microsoft software including databases required.</p>
<p>
Must have independent means of transportation for extensive local travel and auto insurance. Must transport participants as needed. Pay is commensurate with work experience and education. If interested please complete application and attach resume. Incomplete applications will not be considered for the position.</p>
<p>
EEO Employer/Vet/Disabled</p>
