<h2>Talent Development Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<h1 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Job Description
</b></h1>
<p></p>
<p>Individual in this position is responsible for leading the development and execution of key organizational development process and solutions . Partners with Talent Partners and leaders on the development and implementation of key strategies, programs, and processes for creating and sustaining a high performing organization. Owns the learning &amp; organizational development framework , ensuring it’s fit for purpose across business needs and delivering solutions at the enterprise and individual levels. Additionally, this position function as the change management steward for the company, understanding how projects impact the end user and developing communications, training, and engagement tools to support the user experience.
</p>
<p></p>
<p>This person will create and implement a career development program, which integrates career paths, competencies, skills and various tools across the talent spectrum – from hiring, onboarding, learning &amp; development, performance and succession. Collaborating with leaders, SME’s and HR professionals, this person will create solutions to grow talent across the Enterprise.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>PRINCIPAL FUNCTIONAL RESPONSIBILITIES:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Collaborate with talent partners to develop and implement employee development programs, including career development, mentoring, leadership development
</li>
<li>Conducts needs assessment for training and staff development to achieve the goals and objectives of the organization
</li>
<li>Develop in-house content for various training programs
</li>
<li>Manage learning and development programs to engage all levels of staff including professional development and management training
</li>
<li>Promote employee adoption of self-development via LinkedIn Learning and coaching
</li>
<li>Manage and leverage learning management system to drive standardized training and track employee training
</li>
<li>Manage new employee orientation and onboarding approaches, providing consultation and support for managers, talent partners, and People Services Partners to engage and retain new talent
</li>
<li>Evaluate and improve the effectiveness of learning and development programs
</li>
<li>Works collaboratively with departments and teams to ensure effective change management present and executed in all organizational changes
</li>
<li>Develop change management communications for projects, and develop mediums to gather stakeholder feedback
</li>
<li>Own the employee engagement program, inclusive of engagement surveys, initiatives, and liaison to employee action groups
<br>
</li>
</ul>
<p><b>ESSENTIAL QUALIFICATIONS &amp; SKILLS:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>BA/BS in Human Resources, organizational development or related field
</li>
<li>Minimum 7 years of training and development experience, working with adult learning methods and design principles to create effective training programs
</li>
<li>Experience involving the implementation of organizational change initiatives
</li>
<li>Experience utilizing and management Learning platforms (e.g. – LMS, LinkedIn Learning, etc.)
</li>
<li>Strong written and verbal communication skills
<br>
</li>
</ul>
<p><b>PREFERRED QUALIFICATIONS &amp; SKILLS:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Familiarity with project management techniques and skills
</li>
<li>Knowledge and experience supporting corporate and manufacturing environments
</li>
<li>Savvy with innovative technologies and software applications
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>COMPETENCIES:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Developing Talent – displays a passion for developing employees, consistently raise expectations to support growth.
</li>
<li>Organizational skills – strong planning, organization, and problem-solving skills.
</li>
<li>Collaboration – Leverage internal/external relationships and resources to get better outcomes; actively remove barriers to honest collaboration; accelerate our progress by sharing best practices and learning
</li>
<li>Strategic Thinking – intently knowing the business and it’s employees to identify what’s needed to meet future demands
</li>
</ul>
