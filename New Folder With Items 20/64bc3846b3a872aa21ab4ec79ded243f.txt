<h2>Environmental Emergency Response Supervisor</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Environmental Management Specialists, Inc. (EMS) is a professional environmental contractor specializing in environmental cleanup and site remediation, emergency spill response, waste and industrial services, demolition, tank removal and installation and environmental construction. We provide these services with our experienced and highly qualified project managers, technicians and equipment operators to a wide variety of customers, including environmental consultants, energy companies, industrial facilities, commercial property owners/developers, general contractors, public entities and other environmental service companies.</p>
<p><b>
Supervisor</b></p>
<p>
The qualified candidate will have experience in the Emergency Response, Remediation or Industrial Services industry. Experience working on Hazardous Waste Spills preferred.</p>
<p><b>
Responsibilities and Requirements:</b></p>
<p>
The Supervisor will be responsible for running and leading crews on a day-to-day basis providing industrial services, hazardous waste emergency responses and remediation projects.</p>
<ul>
<li>Support EMS Safety Culture, Purpose and Core Values</li>
<li>Manage crews on site</li>
<li>Ensure 24/7 coverage</li>
<li>Provide training, coaching, mentoring, and direction to crews</li>
<li>Implement program for staff performance management and development</li>
<li>Work with Environmental Health &amp; Safety to maintain training records for crews</li>
<li>Maintain EMS policies</li>
<li>Work with Human Resources to address employee relations issues in a timely manner</li>
<li>Interpret and ensure self and subordinate employees are in compliance with EMS and client policies and procedures</li>
<li>Provide leadership and manage the projects, make decisions, solve problems, develop job procedures, complete all required paperwork associated with project and conduct all necessary meetings to perform the work in a safe and efficient manner
</li>
<li>Identify essential functions and knowledge, skills and abilities required to assign work tasks to be completed safely, in a timely manner and in compliance with EMS and Client policies and procedures</li>
<li>Manage employees and crew performance, train or provide adequate training for employees to perform safely, coach, counsel and motivate employees, investigate complaints or performance concerns, implement disciplinary action as needed in compliance with EMS policies and procedures</li>
<li>Manage and ensure effective employee/labor relations, create an ethical, non-discriminatory and safe work environment, establish effective communication lines/methods, identify and solve employee problems, manage conflict, respond to grievances</li>
<li>Initiate and assign appropriate work</li>
<li>Establish and maintain a clear line of communication with Client Representative at all times, keep client rep informed throughout the work shift, present, explain all paperwork and obtain authorization by signature on any work performed for each shift worked at shift end</li>
</ul>
<p>Supervisors also assist Branch Manager and Response Manager with targeted activities, including maintain the ER trailer and support equipment to be ready at a moment’s notice. Support implementation of ER Program Quality Initiatives Support scheduled Environmental Services work, when appropriate and receive scheduled assignments from them or Dispatch.</p>
<ul>
<li>Coordinate with other EMS locations to ensure projects will be covered appropriately</li>
<li>Work with EMS clients to ensure all projects are completed on time and within budget</li>
<li>Work with ER teams across EMS to share best practices and focus on client satisfaction</li>
<li>Communicate with Sales &amp; Marketing to maintain ongoing project opportunities and adjust to market trends</li>
<li>Serve as operational point of contact for selected clients to maintain consistent correspondence channel Ensure the safekeeping of EMS equipment, vehicles, supplies, staff / company confidential information Increase personal knowledge and expertise in business, appropriate technology, etc.</li>
<li>Assist achievement of the profitability goals/objectives of the division/branch and organization</li>
<li>Other duties as assigned by management</li>
</ul> <p><b>Experience, Education, Certification:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Work experience in the Environmental Services industry</li>
<li>Self-motivated, organized, and able to prioritize tasks and exercises independent judgment</li>
<li>Strong problem solving skills</li>
<li>Excellent written and verbal communication skills</li>
<li>Intermediate working knowledge of Microsoft Office software (Word, Excel, Outlook, etc.)</li>
<li>OSHA HAZWOPER 40 hour certification</li>
<li>OSHA 10 hour, and CPR/First Aid (preferred)</li>
<li>Valid driving license (clean driving record) – prefer Class B or A CDL license</li>
<li>DOT medical card, preferred</li>
<li>Familiar with physical and chemical hazards typically encountered in the industry</li>
<li>Heavy Equipment Operation (excavator, backhoe, skid steer, etc.) experience preferred</li>
<br>
</ul>
