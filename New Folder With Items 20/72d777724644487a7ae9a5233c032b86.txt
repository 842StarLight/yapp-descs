Location:</b> Pittsburgh, PA (and various client sites nationwide)</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Purpose and Objective:</b> SAP America, Inc. seeks a Business Processes Consultant for our Pittsburgh, PA office location (and various client sites nationwide) to provide application and process specific solution consulting (for a specific application and/or process area: creation of business blueprint, Implementation based on a blueprint, creation of test cases, test scheduling and execution, key user training, go live support, and post go live support).</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Expectations and Tasks:</b> Support in escalated projects. Performance of feasibility studies / solution reviews. Support of pre-sales activities. Taking over responsibility from time to time as a sub project team leader. Detailed planning of process implementation. Define detailed blueprint for development requirements. Industry /corporate process implementation across all related applications (SAP/non-SAP, OnPremise and Cloud)). Develop and Assure quality of process models. Demonstrating basic knowledge of modeling standards and tools. Drive deployment of SAP Model Companies for specific area of business. 70% travel required to client sites nationwide.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Education and Qualifications/Skills and Competencies:</b> Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science, Engineering, Business Administration, or a related field of study and 5 years’ experience or alternatively a Master's degree and 2 years’ experience.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Work Experience:</b> Experience must include 2 years of experience in BW on HANA and BW/4HANA – Business Warehouse on HANA Application including modeling Business Warehouse system to extract, transform, and load the required data into BW system and model BW objects for Operational, Analytical, and Managerial reporting and conversion and optimization of LSA to LSA++ models and feasibility check of existing system landscape to move to HANA database; SAP HANA and HANA Modeling including SAP HANA design, development, integration, testing and deployment; SAP ABAP, ABAP Technical to deliver objects in SAP to support transactional business requirements in ECC, S/4 HANA and BW on HANA; SAP Landscape Transformation; Business Objects(BOBJ) – Business Object Web intelligence, Universe and Lumira.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Travel:</b> 70% travel to client sites nationwide required.</p>
