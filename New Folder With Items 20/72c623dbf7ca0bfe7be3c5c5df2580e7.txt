<h2>Account Executive</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><i>If you are looking to jumpstart or accelerate your career in the tech industry, Finicity is hiring its future leaders into our sales organization. This is a chance to join one of the hottest tech companies in Utah, grow your skills, and be rewarded for the value you deliver.</i></p>
<p><b>
About Finicity</b><b>:</b></p>
<p>
Finicity is an open banking platform that enables people to make better financial decisions by powering fintech apps, online lenders, and banks. You have mostly likely used one of the solutions we power: budgeting apps, accounting software, robo-advisors, and online lenders. The company has raised over $100M, has a new world class office space, and is growing rapidly. Finicity’s products are best in class and heavily in demand, leading to an incredible opportunity for a talented and motivated sales team member to thrive.</p>
<p><b>
Position Summary</b><b>:</b></p>
<p>
We are looking for a talented and competitive Account Executive that thrives in a consultative sales environment. As a fast growing fintech, this role is fundamental in achieving our ambitious customer acquisition and revenue growth objectives. The inside sales team spends most of its time talking with prospects, searching for potential customers, and working within the team to help build, refine, and test our sales process. Each rep has sales goals and quarterly quotas. Finicity offers a unique opportunity for those that want to make their mark in building a sales organization and contributing significantly to a growing company.</p>
<p><b>
About You</b><b>:</b></p>
<p>
You are ambitious, outgoing, competitive, and always looking to improve. You are a sales athlete. You are creative in finding ways to reach and connect with potential customers. You must have the ability to understand and communicate technical products. You must enjoy working with prospects, discovering business opportunities, and know how to close the deal. The ideal candidate is a college graduate, has a track record of business/academic success, and has meaningful technical/consultative sales or lending experience. You also better like having fun!</p>
<p><b>
Qualifications</b><b>:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Proven experience</li>
<li>Track record of over-achieving quota</li>
<li>Strong phone presence</li>
<li>Proficient with corporate productivity and web presentation tools</li>
<li>Experience working with Salesforce.com or similar CRM</li>
<li>Excellent verbal and written communications skills</li>
<li>Strong listening (can’t stress this enough) and presentation skills</li>
<li>Ability to multi-task, prioritize, and manage time effectively</li>
</ul>
<p><b>What You Can Expect</b><b>:</b></p>
<p>
From day one, you will have the opportunity to contribute, handle responsibility, and grow with an unlimited ceiling.</p>
<ul>
<li>Mentored (~3 months) – Initially, you will be paired with a successful sales consultant to learn the products and sales process.</li>
<li>Supervised (3-6 months) – When ready, you will begin conducting outreach and setting up meetings with prospects, culminating in your first sales.</li>
<li>Production – You will have full sales responsibility for a product area or geographic region, be actively pitching Finicity products, and closing sales. You will be rewarded for your production.</li>
<li>Leadership – Successful sales consultants will be asked to mentor new sales consultants and potentially take on other leadership roles.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Salary &amp; Benefits</b><b>:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Competitive Base + Commissions</li>
<li>Health Insurance</li>
<li>Retirement Benefits</li>
<li>Flexible Vacation</li>
<li>Gym Facilities</li>
</ul>
