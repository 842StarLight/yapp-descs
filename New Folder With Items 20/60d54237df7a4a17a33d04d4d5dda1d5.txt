<h2>Marketing Representative (Central/Southern Ohio)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>If you seek a truly outstanding employment experience, Utica National is the place for you. We are always looking for conscientious, customer-oriented people - whether you're a seasoned insurance professional or just starting out. We offer a supportive and friendly work environment that features a variety of career paths.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p>We offer challenging assignments, advancement opportunities, full benefits packages, competitive salaries, and a great work environment. We're big enough to offer the opportunities you need to be successful, but not so big that you'll get lost in the crowd.
</p>
<p>Responsible for the development of profitable, sustainable growth in assigned territory.
</p>
<p><b>DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Drives the Personal Lines agency relationship by marketing and selling company products and services of the territory while working closely with an underwriting team.
</li>
<li>Excellent communications, interpersonal skills and sales skills required to build relationships with agency staff
</li>
<li>Problem solving and analytical skills to interpret data within the agency or territory to provide conclusions and recommendations.
</li>
<li>Strong training skills with products, procedures and agency systems.
</li>
<li>Develops agency plant to drive profitable growth of new and renewal business opportunities within the assigned territory.
</li>
<li>Implements Founders Personal Lines Sales Process with agencies. (diagnostics, sales strategy, adoption, execution and evaluation) to increase agency production and/or retention.
</li>
<li>Utilizes sales/marketing skills to effectively drive market penetration and grow new business opportunities within territory.
</li>
<li>Ability to work with comparative raters, agency management and internal systems (reports, quoting, product manuals and underwriting).
</li>
<li>Identifies, selects, and on-boards new agency appointments to meet new business production expectations.
</li>
<li>Maintain file of current agency/broker prospects.
</li>
<li>Cultivate and develops new appointments.
</li>
<li>Agent training for products, procedures, and company compensation programs.
</li>
<li>Monitors market conditions for changes in competitiveness, identifies new markets and adapt selling skills to create a value proposition and increase sales
</li>
<li>Collaborates with other channels to maximize Utica National franchise value with agents.
</li>
<li>Involvement in regional marketing planning, including both premium and expense budgeting.
</li>
<li>Additional personal development such as the Utica National Leadership Skills Course, Toastmasters, Dale Carnegie, etc.
</li>
<li>Perform all other tasks as assigned by Supervisor.
</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Skills, Knowledge &amp; Abilities
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Strong technical expertise, sophistication, and independent judgment to manage business relationships with distributors and individual business units.
</li>
<li>Solid insurance industry knowledge as it applies to business development and determining business opportunity.
</li>
<li>Strong verbal, written and presentation skills with the ability to effectively interact with internal and external business partners.
</li>
<li>Uses creativity in resolving issues and problems.
</li>
<li>Results-oriented with the ability to develop tactical strategies and business plans which support organizational vision and goals.
</li>
<li>Knowledge of Microsoft Office Suite as well as other business-related software.
</li>
<li>Some travel.
</li>
<li>Ability to work independently and remotely in the assigned territory to achieve results with limited supervision.
</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Education:
</b></p>
<ul><li>Four-year college degree in general studies/liberal arts or business administration or equivalent business experience.
</li></ul>
<p><b>Experience </b>:
</p>
<ul><li>3-5 years as a marketing representative with a proven mastery of the position consistently meeting or exceeding premium, appointment collection and profitability goals.<br>
</li></ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>*This position will be based in Central/Southern Ohio.<br>
</b></p>
