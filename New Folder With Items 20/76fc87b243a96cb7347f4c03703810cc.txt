<h2>Office Marketing Assistant</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>The New Glarus Bakery and its subsidiary businesses are seeking an Office Marketing Assistant who will assist the owner and other staff with customer service duties, office management, marketing, and other business administration tasks.</p>
<p>The Office Marketing Assistant will work at least 25 hours per week, up to 40 hours per week. Some weekend and holiday availability is required.</p>
<p>Duties include, but aren’t limited to Store merchandising and decorating, drafting information advertising, social media management, website management, process and procedures development, mail order coordination, sales data entry, assisting with accounting, and assisting in product deliveries and material pick-up. The Office Marketing Assistant will also be responsible for assisting in the front of the store, providing quality customer service and suggestive sell through understanding customer needs and requirements. The Office Marketing Assistant is expected to comply with health and safety policies, to communicate with both customers and colleagues professionally and empathetically, and to promote efficiency, company standards and commitment to quality.</p>
<p>Qualified applicants will have:</p>
<ul>
<li>At least two years of experience working in a customer service position, dealing with the public</li>
<li>Experience with general office procedures and tasks, including mail distribution, data entry, customer eservice, inventory management, product shipping, and filing</li>
<li>Knowledge of marketing concepts; experience developing social media content, website content and/or advertising a plus</li>
<li>Focus on health and safety compliance</li>
<li>Strong written and verbal communication skills; ability to adjust communication style appropriately to meet the needs of the customer</li>
<li>Demonstrated computer and software skills; proficiency with Microsoft Office Suite and databases</li>
<li>Able to be customer-focused and quality-driven with strong customer service skills and focus</li>
<li>Able to work in a fast-paced environment with minimal supervision</li>
<li>Able to effectively work both independently and cooperatively as part of a team</li>
<li>Able to drive as part of the job; must possess a valid driver’s license</li>
<li>Willingness to assist wherever needed</li>
</ul>
<p>To apply, please submit a resume, or stop in the New Glarus Bakery to complete an application at 534 1st Street, New Glarus. Resumes and applications will be accepted through August 15.</p>
<p>Job Types: Full-time, Part-time</p>
<p>Salary: $10.00 to $13.00 /hour</p>
<p>Experience:</p>
<ul><li>customer service: 1 year (Preferred)</li></ul>
