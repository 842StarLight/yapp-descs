<h2>Sr. Salesforce Developer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<div>
<div>
<div>
<b>Position Summary:</b>
</div>
<div>
<p>As a Senior Salesforce Developer at Irvine Company, you will be a member of the team responsible for the configuration, development, and on-going support of our Salesforce platform. This is an opportunity to design and deliver new customizations, integrations and data migrations in a dynamic and growing organization.</p>
<div>
Your responsibilities will include identifying system needs and defining changes and customizations to be made to the Salesforce Sales Cloud and Service Cloud application. An excellent understanding of Salesforce platform and best practice methods to customize is essential.</div>
</div>
</div>
<div>
<div>
<b>
Job Duties:</b>
</div>
<div><ul>
<li>Implement Salesforce solutions that adhere to platform best practices, and perform peer code reviews. Typical Salesforce implementations include custom platform development (Apex, Apex Triggers, Lightning Components / Lightning Web Components), integrations with back office systems (often through the use of middleware tools) and complex data migrations. Experience in one or more of these aspects of a Salesforce project is a must.</li>
<li>
Perform system development, integrations and customization to support User Stories that represent end user requirements</li>
<li>
Work with senior members of the team to analyze business requirements, translating those requirements into customized solutions using the Salesforce platform.</li>
<li>
Create and/or update Requirement, Process, Functional, and Technical documentation.</li>
<li>
Familiar with best practices for SFDC sandbox environments and release management to ensure quality of releases.</li>
<li>
Lead development workstreams, from design through testing and deployment, often working with junior members of the team both offshore and onshore.</li>
<li>
With project leadership, assist in the estimation of work effort, determination of required resources, and set a realistic schedule for development.</li>
<li>
Troubleshoot and resolve user issues for SFDC by performing extensive root cause analysis to ensure non re-occurrence of defects and direct them to responsible groups.</li>
<li>
Research Salesforce.com capabilities as needed to suit business requirements, and provide gap analysis</li>
<li>
Understand capabilities that exist in the AppExchnage.</li>
</ul></div>
</div>
<div>
