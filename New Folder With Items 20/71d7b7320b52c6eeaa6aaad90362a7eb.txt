<div>
Our <b>Community Development Specialist (CDS),</b> will cultivate partnerships and alliances within the local community that will promote brand awareness of the Microsoft Stores. The CDS must be able to construct a robust schedule of events both during and after store hours, so that Microsoft Retail brings the technology experience and excitement of our products directly to our local community. In partnership with Store leadership and Microsoft Retail Stores marketing, this role will build and sustain a viable community presence and events for local affiliations.</div>

<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Responsibilities</b></h2>
<div><b>
Customer Obsession</b></div>
<ul>
<li><div>
Build relationships with local community groups such as schools, non-profit organizations, and small to medium size businesses (SMB)</div></li>
<li><div>
Evangelize product offerings and cross platform ecosystem integration through in-store events and learning engagements that build on increased utilization with devices customers own and love</div></li>
</ul>
<div><b>
Adaptability</b></div>
<ul>
<li><div>
Effectively respond to changing in-store environment, circumstances, and situations</div></li>
<li><div>
Ability to thrive in a dynamic and fast paced retail environment</div></li>
<li><div>
Proactively respond and engage in efforts and activities that promote diversity and inclusion of local community</div></li>
<li><div>
Respond to changing in-store needs by maintaining and evangelizing an inclusive greater Microsoft mindset</div></li>
</ul>
<div><b>
Drive for Results</b></div>
<ul>
<li><div>
Tenaciously pursues positive outcomes by using effective approaches to drive in-store usage and engagement</div></li>
<li><div>
Takes responsibility in delivering on commitments and seeks positive results that benefit the local community and our customers.</div></li>
<li><div>
Maintain a consistent operational workflow of day to day activities in community outreach and connection that delivers both in effectiveness and efficiencies in-store</div></li>
</ul>
<div><b>
Influencing for Impact</b></div>
<ul>
<li><div>
Continuously maintain product knowledge by learning through others along with self-educating, showcasing product and service offerings building on our #WeWinAsATeam culture</div></li>
<li><div>
Foster relationships locally through unique events that drive brand awareness in our community</div></li>
</ul>
<div><b>
Collaboration</b></div>
<ul>
<li><div>
Feel empowered to engage with peers, store leaders, corporate and field team members in the development of innovative ways to make a larger impact within the local communities</div></li>
<li><div>
Combines resources and joins efforts with teammates to achieve store and company goals</div></li>
<li><div>
Use established partnerships in the community to create unique events that drive in-store traffic and reoccurring theater utilization</div></li>
