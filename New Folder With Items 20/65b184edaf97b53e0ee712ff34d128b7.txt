<h2>Sales Operations Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Description</p>
<div>
<i>Position at The HON Company LLC</i>
</div>
<p></p>
<p><b>The HON Company</b>, the largest operating company of HNI Corporation and North America’s leader in providing workplace furniture solutions, is in search of a Sales Operations Specialist to join our team in Muscatine, IA!</p>
<p><i>
Some of the essential aspects of the position include:</i><br>
</p>
<ul>
<li>
Execute the dealer/customer account set-up, including onboarding and customer account maintenance and acquistions. This entails implementing the onboarding methodology and process for dealerships and dealer sales representatives</li>
<li>
Implement salesforce.com processes and assist sales members with execution and reporting. Create and deliver content to field sales including standard work, best practices, etc</li>
<li>
Participate in activities focused on driving process improvement across the sales organization that enables sales force productivity and efficiency.</li>
<li>
Deliver key sales metrics, via reports, dashboards, and salesforce.com for field sales.</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>
Work cross-functionally to help resolve issues that arise for field sales members</li>
<li>
Manage the execution and assignment of requests coming into the Sales Operations Department e-mail in box.</li>
</ul>
<p><i>
What it takes:</i></p>
<p>
Individuals considered for this role will have a Bachelor’s Degree in Business Administration, Marketing, or a related field as well as strong written and verbal communication skills. Proficiency in Word, Excel, and PowerPoint required.</p>
<p>
Strong candidates will have knowledge of HON sales channels and experience with Salesforce.com and HNI business systems, including Oracle and BI Reporting, preferred.<br>
<br>
Apply today!</p>
