Job Description
<br>---------------
<br><br>
Vlocity puts the customer first in everything we do - and our Delivery Managers are critical to the success of this approach.
<br><br>
You will engage early with the customer, often during the sales cycle, and help them (and their system integrator partners) shape a plan to deliver the solution that will transform their business.
<br><br>
You'll use your knowledge of implementation best practices and the Vlocity product set to formulate a plan that will deliver value quickly - and act as a platform for future innovation - and you'll stay with the customer as they execute it.
<br><br>
Throughout the program, you'll act as the customer advocate within Vlocity - reporting progress, identifying, articulating and mitigating risk, and coordinating activities and resources.
<br><br>
You'll bring a wealth of Vlocity resources and tools to enhance the program team and to ensure customer success.
<br><br>
This role will require travel to customer locations.
<br><br>
Required Qualifications
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>You'll have 5+ years' experience of leading deployment teams, ideally using Agile development techniques, through full project lifecycles.</li>
<li>You'll have a proven track record of successful delivery of customer projects, preferably enterprise CRM implementations.</li>
<li>Ideally, you will have extensive experience of at least one of our industry sectors - telecommunications, media, insurance, health, utilities, or public sector.</li>
<li>You'll be a self-starter, adept at picking up new skills and technologies, and eager to break new ground.</li>
<li>You'll have excellent communication skills, equally comfortable with crafting emails as leading a white-board discussion with customers, or delivering an executive presentation.</li>
<li>You'll have the vision to help us take our company to the next level.</li>
</ul>
Technical Skills
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Project Management techniques and methodologies (e.g Agile)</li>
<li>Project Management tools (e.g. JIRA)</li>
<li>A working knowledge of the Salesforce platform and applications</li>
</ul>
Desired Certifications / Qualifications
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Bachelors or Masters degree in Computer Science, Software Engineering, Business or a related field</li>
<li>Certified Scrum Master</li>
<li>PMP/PMI</li>
<li>Salesforce Administrator</li>
</ul>
