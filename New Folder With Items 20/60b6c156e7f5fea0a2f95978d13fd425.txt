<p><b>
Role Purpose</b><br>
This position is responsible for administering and supporting sales commissions and providing a supporting role in ensuring these commissions are processed and reported accurately and timely. Critical components of this job include customer contract and sales compensation review and proper reporting. This role will have frequent interaction with other business groups and all levels of management. This position reports to the Senior Accounting Manager responsible for Global Commissions.<br>
<b>Responsibilities</b>:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Analyze sales performance results, prepare commission statements, presentations, and recommendations for sales and/or finance management.</li>
<li>
Research and resolve commissions and accounting issues.</li>
<li>
Work directly with Commissions Senior Manager as well as partner with key stakeholders in cross-functional teams to coordinate efforts in researching, addressing, and resolving compensation disputes.</li>
<li>
Support audit requirements related to compensation payouts and provide assistance related to testing of sales compensation processes.</li>
<li>
Identify process and/or system improvement opportunities, recommend solutions, obtain necessary approval and assist with implementation.</li>
<li>
Perform special projects as requested.</li>
<li>
Prioritize and solve problems with little direct supervision.</li>
<li>
Be aware of, and comply with, all corporate policies.</li>
</ul>
