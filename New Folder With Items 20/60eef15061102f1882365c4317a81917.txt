About the Team:</p>
<p>
Rally Health’s Senior Business Solutions Team is responsible for acting as a Salesforce expert and business consultant to teams within Rally Health, Optum, and UnitedHealthcare.</p>
<p>
Your day to day:</p>
<p>
This role will primarily be engaged with developing solutions on the Salesforce Health Cloud platform utilizing the Health Cloud Managed package in conjunction with Field Service Lightning. In this capacity, the Senior Business Solutions Manager will be expected to guide partners/stakeholders towards the appropriate, scalable, and highest quality solutions; manage the delivery of those solutions; and ensure appropriate post-launch planning and execution including, but not limited to: live site support, documentation of processes and best practices, support for any handoffs, and potential risk identification and mediation.</p>
<p>
Core Responsibilities:</p>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li><p><br>
Learn business stakeholder processes end-to-end and establishing structures by which to maintain that knowledge as processes evolve.</p></li>
<li><div>
Consult with product owners (internal and external to Rally Health) as well as any other relevant stakeholders to define the scope of any Salesforce solutions.</div></li>
<li><div>
Provide guidance on best practices for solutions based on expertise in the Salesforce platform with specific expertise in Service Cloud, Health Cloud, and Field Service Lightning.</div></li>
<li><div>
Write and distribute all business documentation for each stakeholder based on stakeholder’s unique needs; this may include the scheduling of status update meetings, drafting project plans, providing weekly status updates, etc.</div></li>
<li><div>
Establish governance process(es) that allows for the efficient evaluation, prioritization, and intake of any requested work.</div></li>
<li><p>
If necessary, write stakeholder-facing documentation that supplements technical and functional documentation.</p></li>
</ul>

Particular detail to communication, problem-solving, and cross-group collaboration skills.</div></li>

Keen eye on identifying business issues and developing risk mitigation strategies.</div></li>
