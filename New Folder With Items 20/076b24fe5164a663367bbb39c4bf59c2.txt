<h2>Director of Product Management</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>FM:Systems, a leading provider of Integrated Workplace Management Systems (IWMS) and the top performer in space management and workplace services software, is seeking a Director of Product Management to manage our product line and have accountability over the success of the product portfolio. You will own all aspects of the product, including the product platform vision and roadmap. You will lead a team to drive platform strategy, innovation and evolution to build a best-in-class offering.</p>
<p>
At FM:Systems, we have 3 guiding values around what we believe and how we behave: Do the right thing, act with urgency and cultivate a culture of excellence &amp; accountability. We aim to offer our clients an exceptional experience with every interaction, foster innovation and invest in our people. If this sounds like a company you would like to work for, apply today!</p>
<p><b>
In this role you will have the opportunity to:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Establish the platform value proposition and vision, strategy and go-to-market (GTM) plan as basis for a strategic forecast</li>
<li>
Understand and manage our market position - why we win, why we lose and utilize this to inform our strategy</li>
<li>
Influence the GTM approach and ensure that FM:Systems has a competitive market offer, balancing priorities across end-using clients, internal and external development teams</li>
<li>
Product Lifecycle Management – Engage closely with the engineering team to bring product to market in a reasonable timeframe and end of life products when appropriate</li>
<li>
Communicate the platform strategy and gain support among departmental teams</li>
<li>
Integrate usability studies into platform requirements to deliver brilliant user experience and developer experience, making sure the products meet and exceeds market demand</li>
<li>
Lead a team of product managers to produce product requirements, prioritize new features, build consensus, and coordinate product schedules with the engineering team</li>
<li>
Ensure consistent delivery of user- centric experiences developed with measurable goals, grounded in needs, supported by data, in an iterative fashion</li>
<li>
Work collaboratively throughout the development process between a variety of internal teams including Marketing, Sales, Customer Support, and the Senior Leadership Team</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
The successful candidate for this role will have the following skills and experience:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Proven track record of launching and growing software products in the Enterprise and/or SaaS space</li>
<li>
Exceptional leadership and communication skills with the proven ability to manage cross functional teams through influence</li>
<li>
10+ years of Product Management experience
</li>
<li>Deep understanding of Enterprise Cloud/SaaS software platform technologies and methods</li>
<li>
Bachelor’s degree or greater
</li>
<li>Externally focused with very strong determination to win in the market</li>
<li>
Self-confident with the ability to challenge and be challenged</li>
<li>
Open, candid and persuasive in your style, engendering trust and confidence internally, with customers and other industry and market contacts</li>
<li>
Highly effective negotiator</li>
<li>
Credible with customers, analysts – to work closely with the VP of Strategy as external face of the product</li>
</ul>
