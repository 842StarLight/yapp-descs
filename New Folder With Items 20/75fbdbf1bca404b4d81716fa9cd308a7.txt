<p>E-commerce Marketing Manager</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
About HH Global</p>
<p>
We are a global marketing services group specializing in the execution of print, digital and retail media. Providing services to brands and retailers, both directly and via their nominated agencies, HH Global delivers a seamlessly integrated Customer Brand Experience and a greater measurable return on marketing investment (ROMI).</p>
<p>
With offices across Europe, the US, Latin America, the Middle East and Asia-Pacific, HH Global delivers for some of the most successful and challenging brands in the world and is a market leader in the coordinated, leveraged, management of global marketing supply chains to unify and synchronize all customer touch points across digital, print and retail media.</p>
<p>
The HH Global proposition includes: Marketing Technology; Media Asset Management; Creative Production; Artwork Management; Trans creation and Adaptation; Marketing Procurement; Production Management and Marketing Fulfilment.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Description</b></p>
<p>
The Ecommerce Marketing Manager will be the hub between our marketing agency and partner sales team to execute site marketing, search &amp; strategy to hit the revenue targets across the entire Nest, Pixel &amp; Create portfolios. This role will support the end to end project management of marketing initiatives across Amazon &amp; walmart.com, facilitate promo creative &amp; optimization strategy and budget operations across partners.</p>
<p><b>
Job Responsibilities</b> :</p>
<ul>
<li>Support Partner Marketing Lead with product launch and promotional campaign execution via end to end project management of timelines, creative deliverables and advertising media. Includes cross functional partnerships with sales, product, finance and our ecommerce agency.
<ul>
<li>For Search: product catalog management + SEO + score card management of share of voice &amp; keyword competition</li>
<li>For Advertising &amp; Media: design, target and optimize AMS and DSP campaigns + manage budget allocation to achieve ROAS goals + measure conversion funnel metrics and profitability</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>Develop weekly campaign reports (status summary) + post campaign reporting. Work with Retail Analytics team to plan and track ROI of marketing executions to assess effectiveness. Channeling sales, traffic &amp; industry data on spend optimization.</li>
<li>Support budget forecasting &amp; execution, including SOWs, POs, invoices, and flagging any budget risks. Responsible for building and implementing project management infrastructure and tools to complement the above.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Skill/Experience/Education</b></p>
<p>
Mandatory</p>
<ul>
<li>Minimum Qualification: BA/BS Degree - Marketing Field/Business Administration 2-4 years of experience in digital or Ecommerce marketing, experience with Amazon and/or Walmart.com is a must.
</li>
<li>Strong working knowledge of marketplace models: 1p vs 3p listings, buy box, etc.</li>
<li>Content management experience on major e-tailer websites via Amazon Vendor Central and/or Walmart Supplier Center</li>
<li>Experience with Amazon Brand Store and comparable tools with other e-tailers</li>
<li>Manage content syndication via Syndigo/WebCollage, Sellpoints, etc.</li>
<li>Experience troubleshooting and resolving issues related to shared product listings, partner data feeds, vendor catalogs and self-service ticketing platforms</li>
<li>Experience managing multiple accounts and initiatives/projects across multiple product lines simultaneously
</li>
<li>Budget/billing reconciliation experience
</li>

</ul>
