<p><b>
PURPOSE AND OBJECTIVES</b></p>
<p>The objective of this internship is the support of the SAP HANA Enterprise Cloud Global Go-to Market team for the Premium Supplier program. There will also be opportunities to work with our HEC Launch Advisor program. This program works with customers who are transitioning to the cloud as part of their digital transformation journey.</p>
<p>We encourage knowledge expansion concerning the SAP portfolio, mainly Cloud and especially SAP HEC This internship also offers insights into our Go-to-Market strategies, global supplier management, and global demand generation programs with our premium suppliers.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>Furthermore, knowledge in the fields of sales enablement, marketing, and deal support can be acquired and directly applied.</p>
<p>By Supporting a global team, learning opportunities concerning communication and cross functional team occurs as well.</p>
<p>This internship is a unique way to acquire portfolio knowledge, get in touch with the most recent innovations and gain insights into the internal process of SAP.</p>
<br>
