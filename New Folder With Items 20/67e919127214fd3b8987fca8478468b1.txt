<h2>Manager, Commercial Markets Consulting (Priority Health)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">Leads a team of analysts and is responsible for developing and producing best-in-class employer-facing analytics and reporting for the Commercial Markets division.This team develops reports, proactively monitors group performance, and provides recommendations. Partners with Sales, brokers, and employer groups to train them on the reporting tools as well as discuss current and future reporting needs. Proven leader who can drive change and bring creative/innovative ideas to the organization. Responsible for the development and mentoring of staff.
<br><br>
<b>Skills:</b><br>
Proficiency with SQL, SAS, R, JMP, Tableau, or other reporting tools.
<br>Demonstrated knowledge of the healthcare industry and a working knowledge of informatics reporting systems, relational databases, data extraction methods, and analytics and statistics.
<br>Leadership abilities, including team and peer mentoring, cross-departmental problem solving and the ability to communicate complex findings to multiple audiences.
<br>Excellent written/verbal communications and interpersonal skills with the ability to effectively present information to the executive leaders and organizational/regulatory committees within the organization.
<br>Demonstrate the capability to develop and maintain effective relationships with all departments and employees in a team-oriented environment.
<br>Ability to make high-quality, independent decisions, with strong problem-solving and critical thinking skills that are presented in a professional manner. Time management and organizational skills.
<br><br>
<b>Roles and Responsibilities (For This Position):</b><br>
Creates and executes a plan to conduct appropriate analytics, taking into account corporate strategies and regulatory and accreditation requirements.
<br>o Fosters relationships with internal departments and external clients/agents to provide actionable analytics.
<br>o Ensures relevancy of measures and analysis for a wide variety of customers across the company and for levels of reporting from member-level to executives.
<br>o Conducts analysis that provides descriptive, relational, or predictive information.
<br>Leads the delivery of timely and accurate reports, actionable analytics, collaborative conversations, and the development of new dashboards and reports.
<br>o Establishes and maintains policies and procedures that ensures accurate data reporting.
<br>o Develops data collection and evaluation methodologies including, but not limited to, design, requirements, and usage.
<br>o Develops and implements methodology to support data completeness and accuracy.
<br>Leads team in producing best-in-class Commercial Markets external facing client reporting. Collaborates with external clients and internal stakeholders to ensure Priority Health delivers actionable analytics while adhering to corporate guidelines.
<br>Leads team in interpreting internal or external business issues and recommends best practices. In addition, leads team to solve complex problems and takes an outside/in perspective to identify innovative solutions.
<br>Conducts analysis that provides descriptive, relational, or predictive information. Continuously improves information and analysis for descriptions, measurement, and operational support.
<br>Evaluates and plans the adoption of reporting and analytic technologies and techniques to improve data reporting. Leads the purchase or development and maintenance of informatics tools or data systems by which analytics are conducted.
<br>Fosters relationships with IS, Informatics, and other units conducting analytics to maximize the use of data, data tools, and analytical methods.
<br>Has an in-depth knowledge of what analytics clients want/expect and the need to make meaningful business decisions.
<br>Actively participates in safety initiatives and risk mitigation measures where appropriate and completes all position and unit safety-related competencies and requirements on a timely basis.
<br>Performs other duties as assigned.
<br><br>

Education - Bachelor's Degree in in health care, engineering, mathematics, computer science, information technology, business administration, biostatistics, social or nature sciences or related field
<br>Experience - 7 years of experience typically gained through skills/knowledge/abilities in healthcare quality, analytics, patient experience, or informatics
<br><br>
<b>Preferred Qualifications:</b><br>
Education - Master's Degree in in health care, engineering, mathematics, computer science, information technology, business administration, biostatistics, social or nature sciences or related field
<br><b>Experience:</b> 2-5 years of management or supervisory experience preferably in a health insurance or hospital setting.
