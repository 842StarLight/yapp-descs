<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Senior Commercial Insurance Account Manager - New Orleans
</b></h2>
<p><b>TITLE: </b>Senior Commercial Account Manager
</p>
<p><b>JOB TYPE: </b>FLSA Exempt
</p>
<p><b>ESSENTIAL DUTIES &amp; RESPONSIBILTIES:</b></p>
<p><b>
Documentation</b></p>
<p>
Manage the Client Insurance Program, utilizing agency management system. Generally manages larger and more complex accounts than the Account Manager. Duties include those that are done directly (higher level) or accomplished through other resources (i.e. assigned Analysts or external resources) and not limited to:</p>
<div><ul>
<li>Acts as a main point of contact with the Producer</li>
<li>
Initiate correspondence with the marketplace</li>
<li>
Function as a main contact with vendors to ensure proper processing of requests necessary to effectively maintain the client’s insurance program</li>
<li>
Maintain positive client relationship and have the ability to analyze situations, make proactive decisions and manage the follow-through.</li>
<li>
Demonstrate a strong consultative ability to instruct and direct the client
</li>
<li>Manage upcoming deadlines and events, informing and updating the Producer, as needed.</li>
<li>
Manage Accounts Receivables</li>
<li>
Manage Direct Billing with respect to Cancellations</li>
<li>
Responsible for ensuring the following are accomplished (via delegation to Analysts or external resources):<ul>
<li>
Create Acord applications, including Supplementals, as needed, for submissions to Marketing Department and/or carriers.</li>
<li>
Create and complete COPE spreadsheets, as needed.</li>
<li>
Prepare Loss Summaries</li>
<li>
Prepare proposals for renewal terms</li>
<li>
Issue Acord Insurance Binders for renewal terms</li>
<li>
Initiate Premium Finance Requests are
</li>
<li>Prepare Schedules of Insurance for delivery to client</li>
<li>
Prepare policies for delivery to client</li>
<li>
Process Policy Audits and Monthly Reports</li>
<li>
Identify and resolve Accounting discrepancies</li>
<li>
Create requests for changes to client’s coverages</li>
<li>
Process mail and phone requests on daily basis</li>
<li>
Prepare Acord applications</li>
</ul>
</li>
</ul></div>
<ul>
<li>Prepare documents for renewal terms, including Insurance Proposals, Acord Insurance Binders and Certificates of Insurance</li>
<li>
Adhere to document retention software documentation guidelines</li>
<li>
Manage Perform Quality check and control on insurance policies in their entirety</li>
<li>
Where appropriate, make recommendations for additional products/services as well as expanding broker/consultant services as appropriate to meet client needs</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Teamwork</b></p>
<p>Function as team member of the Client Services team to ensure superior service to all MMA Clients. These activities include but are not limited to:</p>
<ul>
<li>Delegate and manage tasks given to the Analyst, as well as outsourcing teams, as appropriate.</li>
<li>
Ensure tasks from both Analyst and outsourcing team are completed as directed</li>
<li>
Ensure Analyst is performing all tasks as outlined in the MMA Analyst I or Analyst II Job Description</li>
<li>
Provide direction and guidance to other members when appropriate</li>
<li>
Treat clients, prospects, and company employees with courtesy and respect.</li>
<li>
Contribute to a team approach toward meeting goals and providing excellent client service.</li>
<li>
Coordinate and monitor usage and quality of outsourcing teams</li>
<li>
Seen as a role model and begins to mentor both Account Managers and Analysts, support their training and as a part of their training and development</li>
<li>
Build and maintain vendor relationships</li>
<li>
Negotiate with carriers on cost and program design</li>
<li>
Assist in plan recommendations for the client</li>
<li>
Coordinate, Present and Attend Client Meetings including though not limited to:</li>
<li>
Schedule/Participate in Mid-year and Renewal Meetings, including coordinatingmaterials used in meetings (MINIMUM OF 12 visits per year with demonstrated meeting purpose (i.e., pre or post renewal meeting, claims/safety/loss control meeting,gathering information, delivering policies, collecting payments ….etc.), with or without Producer as necessary</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Technical Expertise</b></p>
<p>Demonstrate the technical expertise to assist in maintaining the Client Insurance Program.These duties include but are not limited to:</p>
<ul>
<li>Analyze insurance quotes and make recommendations</li>
<li>
Comprehend and interpret Insurance Contract requirements for Certificates</li>
<li>
Communicate with insurance carriers as needed</li>
<li>
Communicate with clients as needed</li>
<li>
Client facing meeting as required, either in-house or offsite</li>
<li>
Stay up to date on carrier plans and websites.</li>
<li>
Become proficient using premium analysis software.</li>
<li>
Utilize carrier online rating programs as necessary.</li>
<li>
Maintain knowledge of policy forms and endorsements with ability to explain detailed coverage provisions to the client and Analyst
</li>
<li>Design insurance program for client based on defined needs</li>
<li>
Utilize agency management system to monitor pre-defined activities (policies past expiration, aged A/R, SIC codes, etc.)</li>
<li>
Identify and address any coverage gaps with producer, carrier and client</li>
<li>
Become subject matter expert and resource in area of interest or technical capability.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>REQUIREMENTS:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Licensed: Louisiana P&amp;C Agent</li>
<li>
Education:Bachelor’s Degree, preferred</li>
<li>
(Internal) 5-7 years of Account Manager experience</li>
<li>
(External) Minimum of 7-10 years of midmarket Commercial Account Manager experience</li>
<li>
CIC &amp; CRM, or CPCU required</li>
<li>
If no designation, then 10-15 years of experience as an Account Manager</li>
<li>
Demonstrated ability to meet and exceed objectives through effective delegation and account management</li>
<li>
Demonstrate ability to multi-task and prioritize work</li>
<li>
Willingness to work to meet client needs beyond scheduled hours.</li>
<li>
Proficient at Word, Excel, and current internet technologies.</li>
<li>
Demonstrate proactive work style - Does not have to be asked or reminded of tasks.
</li>
<li>Ability to learn, analyze and comprehend commercial lines of insurance.</li>
<li>
Demonstrate ability to service clients effectively through problem solving, appropriate confidentiality, diplomacy, sensitivity and tactfulness</li>
<li>
Demonstrate track record of interacting with, meeting with and partnering with assigned clients to achieve mutually desired outcomes via routine client contact by phone, e-mail and periodic meetings and planning sessions.</li>
<li>
Demonstrated experience in client facing roles, i.e., primary presenter of Client new business presentations and renewal presentations, strategy meetings, claims reviews, mid-year reviews, etc.</li>
<li>
Ability to interact with, support, collaborate with and direct employees in a variety of subordinate, peer and superior employee roles</li>
<li>
Ability to speak professionally and comfortably in front of others</li>
<li>
Willingness to work to meet client needs beyond scheduled hours by watching e-mail activity and answering important e-mails and calls after hours and on weekends, when necessary</li>
<li>Maintain and exhibit a positive, professional attitude in the performance of your job, treat clients, prospects, and agency employees with courtesy and respect, ability to maintain composure at all times, conform to the organizational chart on all personnel matters and contribute to a company team approach toward meeting agency goals and providing excellent client services.</li>
<li>Represent the agency in a competent, professional manner, appropriate business attire for all client events or meetings unless otherwise advised by the client.</li>
<li>Ability to travel to meet client service needs when necessary</li>
</ul>
