<h2>Senior Product Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Tuthill Transfer Systems located in Ft. Wayne, IN manufactures Fill-Rite® and Sotera Systems® fuel and chemical transfer pumps. These products have long stood as the industry standard for the fast and safe transfer of a wide range of fuels and chemicals. You can easily spot the Fill-Rite red pump on pick-up trucks across the nation and in retail locations serving agriculture and recreational markets. With today’s innovative technology, Fill-Rite now offers a Fuel Management System that combines hardware, a mobile application, and a web portal to give customers real-time control over their fuel.</p>

<h1 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Summary of Position</b></h1>
</div>
<p>Over-all responsibility for life-cycle management of existing products &amp; services in the Tuthill Transfer Systems portfolio. Mentors Product Managers as they work to research, understand, and articulate market needs and trends. Works with Product Managers to ensure that each product line has a strong strategy and a supporting tactical plan that is executed effectively for product launches and day to day product improvements. Orchestrates tactical planning and practical problem solving across many teams including Product Management, Development, Engineering, Operations, Tactical Planning, Customer Service, Sales and MarCom. Manages and builds the product portfolio and Product Management team.</p>
