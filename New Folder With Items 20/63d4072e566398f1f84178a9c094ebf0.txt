<p>
The Role!</p>
<p>
As a Zoës Kitchen Assistant Manager, you'll manage both the front and back of house operations and therefore, prior kitchen experience is a huge plus. You will assist the General Manager in hiring and training of team members and as well as with the finances and scheduling of your restaurant.</p>
<p>
Responsibilities:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Providing leadership, guidance, and training to hourly team members</li>
<li>
Assisting with the recruiting, interviewing and training of new team members</li>
<li>
Growing grow sales and increase profits through exceptional guest service, word of mouth advertising and marketing</li>
<li>
Embracing and promoting the Zoës Kitchen culture</li>
<li>
Making the guests feel welcome and ensuring that your team members provide the best possible service</li>
<li>
As an AGM, your suggestions and recommendations as to hiring, firing, promotions or similar managerial changes will be given particular weight.</li>
</ul>
