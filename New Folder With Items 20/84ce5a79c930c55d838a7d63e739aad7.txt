<h2>Offering Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<b>Introduction</b><br>
Ready to re-imagine the product management role? At IBM, as an Offering Manager, you are responsible for the full lifecycle of an offering: from validating need to determining the business model and defining the solution requirements. Going beyond the traditional focus of timetables and budgets, you use your keen insights into what customers want in their own business plan - and work across disciplines to build the best solutions to a problem. If being the CEO of your offering excites you, consider an offering management career at IBM.<br>
<br>
<b>Your Role and Responsibilities</b><br>
The IBM B2B Collaboration team under Watson Supply Chain is a market leading solution for enterprises enabling a frictionless network to simplify B2B connectivity, supply chain collaboration and decision making for your business — on premises, hybrid-cloud or cloud-based.<br>
<br>
The B2B Collaboration Offering Team is looking to add an experienced OM with skills and experience to evaluate new opportunities and authoring a visionary roadmap for the B2B collaboration offerings. The role will focus on enabling the portfolio of offerings to support modern deployments over Hybrid cloud platforms.<br>
This role will be responsible for guiding development and business teams to prioritize business outcomes that drive product revenue growth, increased customer satisfaction and broader market penetration to lead and maintain market leadership of the B2B collaboration offerings.<br>
<br>
You would be a lead Offering Manager for one or more products within B2B collaboration suite of offerings and will also oversee and own number of cross-product topics such as containerization, application of AI technologies, micro-services integration for enabling BI and analytical capabilities etc.<br>
<br>
Some of the specific areas of responsibility will include:<br>
<br>
Work with Sales, Marketing and team of Offering managers to align the offering roadmaps and for consistent and timely delivery of key capabilities around common priorities such as cloud and analytics<br>
<br>
Work with IPP development teams to drive the product roadmap and backlog, identify enhancements and advocate for prioritization to ensure end-to-end business outcomes are achieved<br>
<br>
Engage customers to communicate the future plans and work with them to understand their current requirements to be prioritized on the roadmap<br>
<br>
Guide the development of the complex IBM B2B collaboration based on the advanced knowledge on cloud and analytics e.g. Containerization, ICP etc.<br>
<br>
Work with stakeholders to identify small chunks of incremental business value that can be continuously delivered through a prioritized backlog utilizing Agile methodologies (backlog grooming, scrum, showcase, hills/epics, features/user stories, acceptance criteria, story pointing)<br>
<br>
Build and present business-cases to IBM executives for new investments in form of new releases, new offerings etc. Understanding of OMOM process expected<br>
