<p><b>What will you contribute?
</b></p>
<p>We are incredibly proud of our long Canadian history (over 100 years!) as a dedicated service provider to our Canadian banks and other financial institutions as well as to their clients! We provide best-in-class customer service for all things CHEQUE including ordering, printing and designing custom logos and artwork. As a Bilingual Customer Support Representative, Cheques you will join a dynamic team that provides support and customized solutions to financial institutions and their customers on cheques, cheque products and accessories.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Responsibilities &amp; Deliverables:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Customer Focus : first point of contact responding to a high volume of inbound inquiries; supported by scripted responses and an internal knowledge management system; gains insights into customer needs, challenges and risks
</li>
<li>Communicates Effectively – speaks clearly, attentively listens, provides timely and helpful information
</li>
<li>Tech Savvy : ability to learn new tools, technologies and systems building awareness of client policies &amp; programs; capable of navigating screens while type and talk exact verbatim
</li>
<li>Drives Results : works towards exceeding goals and delivering high quality work; required to meet standard Key Performance Indicators
</li>
<li>Escalate issues following established escalation processes to address customer requirements
</li>
<li>Maintain discretion and confidentiality in handling sensitive information
</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Requirements:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Mandatory verbal and written Bilingual skill set in French and English. (Quebecoise French highly preferred)
</li>
<li>Love talking to people and providing solutions; will acquire skills to perform repeatable transactions and services
</li>
<li>Must be comfortable in a structured environment
</li>
<li>Meets benchmark requirement for candidate screening including online cognitive aptitude assessment and ability to complete background checks including Credit, Criminal, and Public Safety Verification.
</li>
</ul>
