<b>Job Responsibilities :</b><br>
Overall management of the day-to-day store operations at their assigned location
<br>Regularly direct the work of their subordinates providing guidance to ensure that all operational goals/initiatives are met.
<br>Oversee the Food Service Leaders in stores with Food Service to ensure that they meet all of the overall functions of being a profitable food service business
<br>Provide superior and quality customer service through consistent interaction. Serve as a role model to store personnel.
<br>Assist customers and responds to customer's complaints or inquiries
<br>The Store Leader is responsible for accurately completing daily reports and reviewing daily close reports.
<br>Responsible for accuracy and integrity of inventory values in regards to shrink
<br>Set and adjust employee pay records, and hours worked
<br>Responsible for daily bank deposits, ordering and picking up change orders and ATM funds, loading safe, ATM, etc.
<br>Determine what is needed for merchandise, supplies, equipment, food service, and places weekly orders.
<br>Scan in orders/vendors and build relationships with the vendors at their assigned location
<br>Hire, train, coach and discipline all store personnel including the completion of documentation
<br>Plan, prepare, and post store personnel work schedules. Responsible for daily timecard edits and verify the accuracy of hours worked and entering vacation hours for store personnel.
<br>Supervision of all employees, Associate Store Leaders, Food Service Store Leaders, and shifts, by assigning and verifying completion of job duties.
<br>Facilitate coverage of shifts when employees call in or don't show up
<br>Ensure compliance of store personnel with policies and procedures, including established security, sales, and record keeping procedures and practices
<br>Responsible for merchandising the store according to company guidelines
<br>Timely execution of marketing promotions and price changes
<br>Execute company standards for mystery shop programs
<br>Manage their assigned store¿s labor budgets and adjust employees pay/time and the hours worked
<br>Maintain fuel pricing compliance standards to ensure competitive street pricing. Duties include changing fuel price sign and the registers.
