Comments<div><div>
The Supply Analyst will ensure parts availability to support OE accessory requirements. They are responsible for ensuring we minimize impacts to customers on critical orders by using sound and standardized expediting processes as well as critical thinking skills to help resolve customer impacting backorder situations. This person may attend meetings and frequently communicate with OE’s and vendors linking the relationship with MPA. This person will monitor and train vendors on AMOS Supplier relationship system (SRS). They will also be responsible for developing and improving standard operating procedures (SOP’s) as well as training employees.<br>
<br>
Ensuring parts and accessories are expedited for critical low inventory at the Ports or PDC situations requires strong communication, a customer centric and proactive mindset, excellent organizational skills, great follow up, and strong critical thinking. These responsibilities have a direct impact on MPA customer service (customer satisfaction ratings, fill rate, and back order lines), expenses (freight, warehousing, vehicle buy back expense, and vehicle rental expense), income (Accy/Parts sales), and therefore have an impact on company profitability.</div></div>

Comments<div><ul>
This person will be supporting the KPI’s: OE system and facing fill rates, months of supply, along with meeting PIO/DIO sales objectives.<br>
Daily<br>
<li>Update SAP/Pipeline workbook</li>
<li>Review daily dealer backorders and shortages</li>
<li>Expedite orders from PDC for port shortages</li>
<li>Review port install errors on AMOS</li>
Weekly<br>
<li>Plan PIO for Port Installed Accessories</li>
<li>Place dealer orders for DIO and accy spare parts</li>
<li>Place PDC to Port transfers (Every Port has a scheduled order day per week)</li>

<li>Send shortage/overage claims to supplying PDC/Port</li>
Monthly<br>
<li>Send monthly inventory update sheet to ports</li>
<li>After it’s received, review it and send back with variances and comments</li>
<li>Send all Port overages and shortages claims to PDC’s for review</li>
<li>Send invoice request to Dan Hecker for 3PL claims by port</li>
<li>Work with Forecasting and Ordering from supplier to PDC</li>
<li>Separate quantities based on PIO demand to ports and Dealer demand to PDC’s</li>
Yearly<br>
<li>In charge of Port Inventory Audits</li>
