<h2>Senior Account Executive - Partnerships</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">ReviewTrackers empowers businesses to understand their customers better through advanced review and customer feedback technology. At ReviewTrackers, we cultivate an inclusive, dynamic, fun, and communicative team environment. ReviewTrackers is currently looking for a passionate, self-motivated, and collaborative Senior Account Executive - Partnerships to join our Partnership Sales Team . The Senior Account Executive - Partnerships is responsible for cultivating new agency and technology partnership opportunities. You will do this by building a strategic plan to grow our marketing agency/technology partnerships, cultivating existing technology partners, and establishing clear measures of success. Come join us as we continue to change the way businesses interact with their customers!
<br><br>
<b>What you will be working on:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Managing a complex sales cycle by selling both horizontally and vertically within mid-market and enterprise clients.</li>
<li>Building a consultative partnering relationship with our clients that follows the strategetic sales process.</li>
<li>The development of industry partner networks and strategic partnerships - specifically within marketing agencies.</li>
<li>Leading product training for the sales teams to support strong product knowledge &amp; adoption.</li>
<li>Track and report on partnerships' impact on new business acquisition and sustaining long-term business relationships.</li>
<li>You will ask, schedule, and host meetings with C-level executives and Partner Teams to develop a robust pipeline by making cold calls and building relationships with prospects.</li>
</ul>
