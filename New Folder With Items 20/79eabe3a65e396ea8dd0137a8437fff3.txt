<h2>Product Solutions Lead, Visa Direct Payouts (Flexible location)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>Job Description<br>
<br>
<p>The Visa Direct Payouts Product team is responsible for leading Visa’s efforts to extend payouts “beyond the card”. Building on Visa Direct’s market leading platform, the Payouts service will enable corporates, governments and banks unprecedented options to send payments to consumers and small business around the world.</p>
<p>
We are seeking a Product Solutions Lead who can help drive critical growth initiatives, both globally and regionally, in a complex, matrixed environment. The ideal candidate will be a structured, analytical thinker with excellent communication skills and a strong interest in how products are developed and delivered. S/he enjoys variety and collaborating with people. S/he is thoughtful, curious and analytical with a desire to make an impact. S/he is client-centric and oriented around finding solutions to complex problems.</p>
<p><b>
Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Identify, develop and deploy solutions for Visa Direct Payouts products</li>
<li>
Consult with clients and partners globally to design solutions that accelerate adoption of Visa Direct Payouts products</li>
<li>
Support segment leads for Global Cross Border, ERP and Treasury Management to sell Visa Direct Payouts products to banks, remittance platforms and corporates</li>
<li>
Provide business and technical solutions for new use-cases penetrating the target markets, by conducting market/client research, reviewing ecosystem readiness, collaboration with regional, global, and cross-border product and commercialization teams</li>
<li>
Enable solutions by working with cross-functional stakeholders including Legal, Finance, Client Support (CS), Risk, Compliance, AML, and other product teams across Visa</li>
<li>
Be a Visa Direct product SME and support Payouts client implementations</li>
<li>
Monitor business enhancement releases and other product related changes and be the focal point of contact for regional, global, and cross-border teams for Visa Direct Payouts</li>
<li>
Responsible for all pricing implementations ; coordinate with Finance Ops, Interchange, and Billing teams as needed</li>
<li>
Support PPGS, Visa Direct tokenization, 3DS and any gateway related solutions for the product team</li>
<li>
Liaison between Cybersource, product and other cross-functional teams in arriving at holistic solutions</li>
<li>
Represent voice of the client within the product roadmap and work closely with product development teams to ensure readiness in implementing new solutions</li>
<li>
Manage marquee customer deployments that have high level of strategic importance, execution and technical complexity, in close coordination with regional commercialization and sales teams</li>
<li>
Represent Visa Direct Payouts within market-facing engagements am at key conferences or industry events</li>
</ul>
</div>
