<p><b>Job Title: Key Account Manager (Military Embedded Computers)</b></p>
<p><b>
Location: </b>Remote within the United States</p>
<p><b>
About this position:</b></p>
<p>
We currently have an opening for a highly motivated and seasoned Key Account Manager focused on servicing Advantech’s existing and future Military/Aerospace clients with embedded computing product and supply chain service needs. The ideal candidate will be dynamic, hungry and technical, exhibiting the ability to hunt and drive new opportunities. The candidate will be required to identify and review engineering specifications, coordinate and propose product solutions, negotiate sales and service contracts while interfacing with OEM/Military/Government customers.
</p>
<p>This role is a hunter role and being customer centric and service minded is key. We want someone with a passion for understanding our customers’ business initiatives and servicing those needs with the various embedded solutions Advantech offers. The ideal individual will be able to understand, find a match for and articulate Advantech’s core competencies and value proposition to key OEM clients. A successful Key Account Manager will have the ability to sell at the VP/C-Level across all disciplines with focus on Engineering, Product &amp; Operations.</p>
<p><b>
The Key Account Manager is responsible for:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Developing and executing on strategic sales objectives</li>
<li>Seek out new OEM/Military/Government accounts through prospecting and customer visits
</li>
<li>Create a target list of accounts for rugged embedded products</li>
<li>Promote Advantech rugged products and participate in tradeshows to generate new leads</li>
<li>Research to understand business and solution objectives the customer is trying to achieve</li>
<li>Strategically position Advantech’s value proposition to key accounts</li>
<li>Serve as a strategic business resource to the OEMs</li>
<li>Develop customer relationships and manage key accounts</li>
<li>Negotiate various types of contracts</li>
<li>Preparation of OEM proposals
</li>
<li>Manage daily sales operations and working with cross functional teams</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p><b>
</b></p>
<p><b>Qualifications:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Prior Pre or Post sales experience with Military/Government customers</li>
<li>BS degree in business, engineering or similar fields preferred</li>
<li>At least 7 years of experience selling industrial computers to OEM accounts and system integrators, <i>Military market accounts preferred</i>
</li>
<li>Experience in rugged open standard COTS board level products</li>
<li>Excellent communication and negotiation skills</li>
<li>Successful track record with credible cold calling and follow-up with key decision makers</li>
<li>Demonstrated ability in the following areas: pre-call planning, opportunity qualification and objection handling, call structure and control, time and territory management</li>
<li>Professional and coachable with polished verbal, written and presentation skills</li>
<li>Extremely proactive, highly organized, with proven ability multi-task in a challenging environment</li>
<li>Ability to adapt to a fast paced environment and diverse culture</li>
<li>Self-starter, team player, positive attitude, passion for creativity and the ability perform independently with little minimal instruction</li>
<li>Ability to work from your home office with limited supervision</li>
<li>Customer-centric, consultative and a passion for excellence in sales</li>
<br>
</ul>
