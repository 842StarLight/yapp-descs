<h2>Business Analyst with Adobe Experience Manager (AEM) experience</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div></div>
<div>
<div>
<div>Business Analysts enable the transformation and performance improvement of an enterprise, with respect to people, process and technology, by providing relevant capabilities, processes and tools. Business Analysts structure, analyze and visualize an enterprise’s current state, design and functional gaps. They visualize the future state, design and guide the change process from current to future state. The role of the Business Analyst is to define business functions, processes and requirements for client marketing and commerce systems. Candidates should have experience working with clients to elicit functional requirements, define user experience needs and effectively communicating those requirements to the technical team.</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div><b>
Key Responsibilities:</b></div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<ul>
<li>Gather business requirements from business users and help them flesh out those requirements to a detailed level that all can easily understand.</li>
<li>Document the business requirements in a way that effectively conveys the required functionality to the customer and to the technical staff.</li>
<li>Ensure that the technical staff thoroughly and accurately understands the business requirements and interprets those requirements correctly into technical specifications.</li>
<li>Track progress on delivery of specific features to the customer and address any business issues that arise during the development process.</li>
</ul>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div><b>
Key Skills and Experience:</b></div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<ul>
<li>5+ years of relevant professional experience and a Bachelor’s Degree in a relevant area or equivalent experience</li>
<li>Ability to understand complex business problems and offer meaningful solutions</li>
<li>Experience with web marketing and commerce systems, Adobe Marketing Cloud and Adobe Experience Manager (AEM) preferred</li>
<li>Ability to apply consulting skills to solve a client business problem</li>
<li>Able to communicate and present complex issues with assurance and confidence</li>
<li>Demonstrates the use of consulting skills including questioning, listening, ideas development, permission and rapport, and influencing</li>
<li>Strong written and oral communications skills</li>
<li>Adobe Certified Expert (ACE) Business Practitioner a plus</li>
</ul>
<div>
</div>
<div>
Candidates should be flexible / willing to work across this delivery landscape which includes and not limited to Agile Applications Development, Support and Deployment.</div>
<div>
</div>
<div>
Applicants for employment in the US must have valid work authorization that does not now and/or will not in the future require sponsorship of a visa for employment authorization in the US by Capgemini.</div>
