<h2>Marketing Account Coordinator/Marketing Account Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div></div>
<div><div><div><div><div><div>
<div><div><p>The Lukens Company (TLC) is an award winning full-service direct response agency that is seeking an Marketing Account Coordinator/Marketing Account Manager to join our non-profit division in our Arlington, VA office. The non-profit division partners with a variety of museums, animal welfare organizations, social welfare organizations, aquariums, zoos, faith-based missions, and other clients staffed by awesome people looking to make a difference in the world. The Marketing Account Coordinator/Marketing Account Manager will play a key role within a client services team, helping to keep marketing campaigns on track, brainstorm new ideas, analyze results, and stay up to date with clients’ missions, messages, and goals.
</p></div></div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>WHAT YOU'LL BE DOING</b></h3>
<div><div><ul>
<li>Developing a deep understanding of your client’s organization and structure, knowing the client’s product/services and staying aware of client news and changes</li>
<li>Attending client meetings and establishing strong relationships with assigned accounts</li>
<li>Consulting with clients about specific campaign requirements and objectives while working with the Account Director to develop and execute strategies in pursuit of program goals</li>
<li>Keeping internal and external teams up to date on campaign progress and results</li>
<li>Facilitating across various internal departments (creative, production, data analytics, etc.) to ensure deadlines are met and campaigns execute successfully</li>
<li>Proactively managing campaign schedules and project deadlines</li>
<li>Overseeing campaign package development and execution including art, imagery, proofs, data counts &amp; dumps, setups, insertions and inventory</li>
<li>Reviewing and analyzing results to guide client recommendations</li>
<li>Communicating and monitoring proper billing procedures</li>
</ul></div></div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>WHAT WE'D LIKE TO SEE</b></h3>
<div><div><ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree</li>
<li>1 – 5 years of experience in marketing/advertising, fundraising, or project management (fundraising background preferred, but not required)</li>
<li>Ability to write and edit industry-related documents (strategy proposals, results analyses, etc.)</li>
<li>Ability to plan, organize, and audit</li>
<li>Strong customer services skills and the capability to establish and maintain goodwill with clients</li>
<li>Self-starter with demonstrated capability to mange multiple projects and think on your feet</li>
<li>Results-oriented “team first” attitude</li>
<li>Deadline and detail-oriented</li>
<li>Adept at problem solving and resolving conflicts</li>
<li>Excellent verbal and written communication skills, giving you the ability to present information, respond, and follow-up on questions and inquiries from clients and/or other managers with clarity</li>
<li>Understanding of the basics in computer software and office equipment</li>
<li>Passion for our mission and making the world a healthier, happier, safer place for ourselves, people around the world, and the next generation</li>
<li>Experience working with non-profit organizations (strong bonus, and if you are passionate about arts and cultural organizations or faith-based organizations, even better!)</li>
</ul></div></div>

<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>ABOUT US</b></h3>
<div><div>
<p>The Lukens Company (TLC) is an award winning full-service direct response agency that has been providing marketing and fundraising services to political and non-profit clients for over 30 years. We innovatively use digital, data, and technology to support the fundraising and marketing efforts of political, non-profit, cultural, and advocacy organizations. We are dedicated to our mission of innovating ideas, inspiring actions, and getting results for our clients and are passionate about making the world a healthier, happier, and safer place for ourselves, people around the world, and the next generation. <b>Join us!</b></p>
