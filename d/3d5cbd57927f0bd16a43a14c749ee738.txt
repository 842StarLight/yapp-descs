<h2>Deputy Product Manager (Mobile Productivity Apps)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<div><b>Job Summary:
</b></div>
<div></div>
<div>In our globalizing economy the availability of skills and expertise is becoming more and more distributed. Within the Mobile Productivity Apps team, we enable EY people to meet the increasing pace of business by delivering productivity apps and services that empower them to work together where they want and how they want. There is no need for specific experience with mobile apps. Product or Project Management experience in the IT mobile space is ideal.</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div>The <b>Product Manager for Mobile Productivity apps</b> is the end to end responsible for our out of the box Microsoft (Outlook, OneDrive, Word, Excel, PowerPoint, Company Portal, etc.) and other 3rd party mobile apps (Adobe Reader, etc.). At a high level the Product Manager will be responsible for:</div>
<ul>
<li><div>Managing the vendor(s) and internal teams that support the product to provide optimal service</div></li>
<li><div>
Monitoring and driving adoption of the product</div></li>
<li><div>
Driving marketing and sales of the product with EY’s internal customers (the business)</div></li>
<li><div>
Delivery of the service through the optimum cost/performance support model</div></li>
<li><div>
Planning and road mapping the future of the service</div></li>
<li><div>
Work with the vendors for continuous improvement of the product functionality and UX</div></li>
<li><div>
Directing the delivery of product enhancements as well as the global deployment of the product.</div></li>
<li><div>
Ensuring security and risk compliance for the product</div></li>
<li><div>
Direct product innovation and industrialization.</div></li>
</ul>
</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
<div><b>Essential Functions of the Job</b></div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div>The Product manager will:
</div>
<ul>
<li><div>Direct the metrics on the total adoption to show business value.
</div></li>
<li><div>Partner with other Product Managers within EUTX to align product roadmaps and service and optimize overall user experience.
</div></li>
<li><div>Directs the product strategy and long term roadmap utilizing resources in the Architecture and Experience team.</div></li>
<li><div>
Proactively researches and evaluates feasibility of any new technologies that may improve mobile productivity.
</div></li>
<li><div>Direct first of a kind projects utilizing the GDS service delivery team.</div></li>
<li><div>
Direct pilots utilizing the GDS service delivery team as well as the Deployment organization.</div></li>
<li><div>
Direct reporting of product utilization and continuously drives for improved adoption of the technologies.</div></li>
<li><div>
Partner with the Deployment team to prepare business change material and product industrialization.
</div></li>
<li><div>Proactively partners with the Area Relationship Management team and engages with local business executives to market, deploy and expand the Services and decommission legacy products.</div></li>
<li><div>
Plan, budget, initiate and sponsor the investment portfolio to deliver new product features and deploy the product. Ultimately accountable for the timely delivery of the investments within the approved budget.</div></li>
<li><div>
Direct and oversees monthly service portfolio review meetings with stakeholders to manage the investment budget and scope management of projects.</div></li>
<li><div>
Oversee the team’s activities to meet deliverable dates for next generation services in accordance with best practices in the industry.</div></li>
</ul>
<br>
<div></div>

<br>

</div></li>

</div></li>

Utilizes all aspects of Emotional Intelligence to navigate the interactions and complexities of the business environment and drive leadership performance in oneself and in others.
</div></li>
<li><div>Brings proven expertise of product management
</div></li>
<li><div>Proven experience in Service Delivery
</div></li>

</div></li>

<div>Passionate, committed and determined</div>
