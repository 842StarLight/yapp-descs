<h2>Trade Groups Coordinator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>THE DEPARTMENT
</b></p>
<p>The Group Sales &amp; Service department is responsible for increasing travel agent sales by integrating sales, service and marketing strategies to establish travel agent relationships. Group Sales &amp; Service is responsible for the sales, service, relationship development, coordination, growth and responsible for identifying and targeting new distribution channel opportunities, and incorporating those new channels into GVI by turning them into viable, profitable business units.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>THE ROLE
</b></p>
<p>The expectations for the role of the Groups Coordinator are to support the Groups team with administrative details, think creatively, find solutions and be flexible in a rapidly changing environment, and to provide excellent, knowledgeable, and prompt customer service.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<h1 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>THE POSITION
</b></h1>
<p>The Groups Coordinator is responsible for the following:
</p>
<ul><li>In conjunction with the Groups Account Manager, coordinating customer payments and invoicing
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Manage incoming quotes and assigning those to the correct file
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Check mail and run payments to accounting
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Creating/updating both physical and Siebel files
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Managing passengers information in Enterprise, and Siebel
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Checking Work Requests and filter to correct Group Account Manager
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Data entry for incoming passenger info. i.e. TSA, bed configuration, Passport information
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Follow up on reporting for: TBA, Past Final, Pending Inventory, and Money for air, etc.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Work with accounts to collect and confirm TSA, passport and other flight information</li></ul>
<ul><li>Make outbound calls for follow up
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Additional projects as determined by Management
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Being a subject matter expert on Direct Groups process and policies
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Support the Specialty Markets Coordinator with Direct Groups related tasks
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Contributing to a team effort by accomplishing related results or projects as needed<br>
</li></ul>
