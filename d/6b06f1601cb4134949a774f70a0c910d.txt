<h2>Customer Success Associate</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>The Position</b></p>
<p>
Do you dream about creating a more sustainable future? At Uplight, we are motivating energy users and providers to accelerate the clean energy ecosystem. Working with over 75 of the world’s leading electric and gas utilities, Uplight provides an end-to-end customer energy experience. Uplight delivers personalized experiences that customers have now come to expect–improving satisfaction, increasing revenue, reducing the cost to serve, and contributing to carbon reduction goals. We are in the process of obtaining our B Corp certification, enabling us to put our values into action by not only making decisions for the benefit of our shareholders, but also for our customers, environment, employees, and community.</p>
<p>
We are seeking a Customer Success Associate to join our team and help us achieve our ambitious goals for our business and the planet.</p>
<p>
 <b>What you get to do:</b></p>
<p>
As a Customer Success Associate, you will be a critical part of Uplight Customer Success organization, responsible for supporting day-to-day activities of Uplight team of Customer Success Managers and Technical Project Managers. The Associate position represents a growth opportunity within Uplight’s organization, with exposure to both internal and external activities. Examples of internal projects include metrics reporting, customer support, issue coordination and resolution, Salesforce.com administration, and market analysis. Examples of external activities include project management, SaaS application configuration, data analysis and troubleshooting, customer training, onsite meeting support, trade show support, and PowerPoint material preparation and delivery.</p>
<p>
An important aspect of the Associate role will entail working collaboratively in a cross functional environment with members of various Uplight teams, including Engineering, Product, Operations, Regulatory, Sales, Marketing and other teams, to deliver measurable value for our clients.</p>
<p>
An ideal candidate will have an interest in energy, utilities, and Software-as-a-Service (Saas) products. You must also be comfortable working in a fast-paced, dynamic environment on a range of different tasks associated with making our customers successful.</p>
<p>
 <b>What you bring to Uplight:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
At least 2 years of experience in business systems analysis, project management, or customer success, preferably at a SaaS or technology company</li>
<li>
Background and interest in exploring new technologies</li>
<li>
Interest in working in a fast-paced multi-disciplinary environment</li>
<li>
Outstanding written and oral communication skills</li>
<li>
Natural affinity for working across departments to drive cross-functional work streams to completion and an independent thinker who proactively identifies issues, interdependencies, and solutions</li>
<li>
You care about energy, its impact on our clients, their customers, and the world</li>
<li>
Willingness to travel up to 20% of the time</li>
</ul>
<p>
 <b>Bonus Points:</b></p>

Experience in software or working with public utilities.</li></ul>
