<h2>Property Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Job Summary: Responsible for insuring the efficient operation of the property in consultation with the Regional Property Manager.</p>
<p>Essential Duties And Responsibilities:</p>
<ul><li>Responsible for interviewing and hiring all property employees in consultation with the Regional Property Manager.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Be familiar with and operate the property and personnel within Fair Housing Guidelines.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Thorough knowledge of lease terms, specifications and all community policies.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Responsible for insuring that all personnel respond to resident requests or complaints in a timely, efficient and courteous manner.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Responsible for job counseling, employee disciplinary notices, and performance evaluations for employees on site in consultation with the Regional Property Manager.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Operate the property within the financial guidelines, i.e., the budget, established by the Regional Property Manager and Owner.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Responsible for adhering to Company policy on delinquent rents and other non-compliant lease violations. Process evictions in accordance with Federal, State, and local laws.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Responsible for all unit inspections including annual inspections, unit move-ins, and move-outs. Preparation of all security dispositions including any damages.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Responsible for the leasing of the property; insuring through supervision of the office and leasing personnel that all sales techniques and methods required by the management company and fair housing standards are being used effectively and in a professional manner. Thorough knowledge of application procedure, screening process, and policies regarding rentals. Fully informed of current rental rates, sizes, locations and all amenities of the property.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Responsible for lease renewal and recertification's procedures.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Responsible for the proper maintenance of all resident and property files.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Bilingual Experience</li></ul>
<ul><li>Must be COS Certified</li></ul>
<ul><li>One Site Software Experience</li></ul>
<ul><li>Hands-on experience with recertification process</li></ul>
<ul><li>Experience with REAC &amp; MOR(s)</li></ul>
<ul><li>Tax Credit</li></ul>
<ul><li>Experience in site level Multi Housing Section 8</li></ul>
