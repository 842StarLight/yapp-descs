<h2>Consultant, Client Services</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">Phreesia is looking for a Consultant in Client Services! You will fully engage the customer at all levels (CEO, physicians, IT, training, billing, finance, office managers, and receptionists) to design and implement a Phreesia solution which maximizes value for the customer. Our mission is to create a better more engaging healthcare experience by helping healthcare providers better understand their patients. As a Client Services Consultant, you will contribute to our mission by configuring and implementing our solution to best fit the needs of our clients. These customers include specialty groups, regional multi-specialty practices, and hospitals. The position interacts with customers virtually and/or on-site. Our Client Services team allows practices to better understand their patients by configuring and implementing our solution to best fit the needs of our clients.

<ul>

<b>What You'll Do:</b><br>
As a Client Services Consultant, you will provide expert consulting services in the areas of workflow analysis, application training and application build activities, go-live support, and post go-live optimization.
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Support sales team during contract negotiations.</li>
<li>Maintain understanding of how the application is used and configured, including the ability to provide an application overview</li>
<li>Lead Phreesia implementation projects, serving as the primary customer contact</li>
<li>Develop and manage project plan, utilizing project management software</li>
<li>Identify resource needs and develop a plan to utilize resources to meet project scope</li>
<li>Maintain complete understanding of contract deliverables and manage project scope to ensure project is delivered on time and within budget</li>
<li>Provide industry and application consulting and offer recommendations</li>
<li>Perform gap analysis to identify implementation issues and consult on options to address issues</li>
<li>Collaborate with Phreesia teams including Support, Account Management, Product Management and Sales to address customer issues</li>
<li>Lead go-live activities, observing results, refining and driving continuous delivery improvement</li>
<li>Manage implementation issues, escalating when appropriate.</li>
<li>Communicate effectively at all levels</li>
</ul>
