<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Job description</b></h2>
<div>
<p>Safran Electronics &amp; Defense is a world leader in optronics, avionics, electronics and critical software for civil and defense applications. The company's products are deployed worldwide on more than 1,000 ships, 25,000 armored vehicles and 10,000 aircraft.</p>
<p>
The Director of Supply Chain for Safran Electronics &amp; Defense, Avionics USA will be joining a dynamic and innovative leadership team. They will be well positioned to create and execute the supply chain strategy to support the organization's short-term and long-term business and financial goals. This role will be based in Irvine, CA and responsible for more than $100M annual spend across four domestic and international locations.</p>
<p>
A key aspect of the role is to define and deploy supply chain strategies which will drive continuous improvement in our costs, quality, and deliveries. These include supply chain processes, make vs. buy and vertical integration strategies, selection of key strategic partners, and associated contracts/long term agreements.</p>

<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Complementary description</b></h2>
<div>
<p>This role partners with the leadership teams, operations leaders, and others across Safran to develop continuous improvement plans consistent with each business unit's strategies in pursuit of meeting or exceeding the business plan expectations. This role ensures that the deployment of these supply chain strategic initiatives and supply chain tools are aligned with the critical needs of the businesses and that the deployment supports attainment of business objectives while driving continuous improvement. The role provides subject matter expertise into the creation of the organization's business strategy as well as supplemental operations strategies.</p>
<p>
This position also focuses on building organizational capability and skill, developing talent within Safran Electronic &amp; Defense's business. This includes building talent pipelines and succession plans for all levels within the function. This role owns the standardized processes and standard work for all supply chain roles. The functional scope of this role includes the entire extended Supply Chain, both internal and external. The role supports Demand Planning, Supply synchronization, Master Scheduling, Plan for every Part, Production Planning, Inventory Management and Shipping / Distribution of the Finished Product.</p>
