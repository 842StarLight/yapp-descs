<h2>SR Commercial Lender</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p><b>About the Role</b>
</p>
<div>
<b>These positions will assume an established portfolio while growing customer base and portfolio.</b><br>
</div>
<div></div>
<div>
Summary</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
In conformance with established policies and procedures, manage a portfolio of commercial loans, retain and develop commercial and deposit banking relationships, and deliver exceptional service to our clients.<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<div><b>DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES:</b></div>
<div></div>
<br>
<ul>
<li>
Participates in developing lending and deposit business for the company. Calls on clients to expand existing business; calls on prospects seeking opportunities to expand the company’s client base.</li>
<li>
Takes applications for commercial loan business. Reviews customer financial condition and assesses the customer’s ability to re-pay the loan. Makes underwriting decisions within approval authority. Completes or directs completion of all loan documents and closes loans.</li>
<li>
Manage assigned loan portfolio for retention, growth, and credit quality to include:</li>
<ul>
<li>
Analyzing and underwriting requests for new loans and loan renewals
</li>
<li>Ensuring complete and accurate loan file documentation</li>
<li>
Collection of past due accounts</li>
<li>
Timely renewal of matured loans</li>
<li>
Prompt identification and notification of the Chief Credit Officer of potential credit issues within the assigned credit portfolio</li>
</ul>
<li>
Embrace a growth and excellence-oriented atmosphere in support of bank-wide initiatives while maintaining bank’s customer service standards.</li>
</ul>
