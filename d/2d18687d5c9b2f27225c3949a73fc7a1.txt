<br>
<div>Artificial intelligence (AI) is experiencing a renaissance and is rapidly growing in importance for customers of every size, across every industry, in almost every conceivable application.<br>
Customers are realizing AI through machine learning (ML) for everything from managing retail staffing to sophisticated demand planning models. Decisions like content recommendation and moderation, even automating the throttle control adjustments on container ships to improve efficiency and accuracy are happening through ML. Machine learning is accelerating Drug discovery, and health-care is starting to move from reactive to proactive. The customer experience is transforming whether it is conversational interfaces, smart biometric authentication, or even real-time predictions in our favorite sporting events. AI is even helping to rescue children from human traffickers. The impact of AI through ML is profound and we are just at the start of this journey.<br>
<br>
Machine Learning is essential for companies to adopt, but executives struggle to understand the best way to implement it for success. With this in mind, the Amazon Web Services (AWS) AI/ML marketing team is seeking a talented, experienced, and self-directed Sr. Product Marketing Manager as a product marketer to join a fast growing team that is responsible for driving the adoption of machine learning on the AWS platform by customers around the world. This is your opportunity to join the AWS marketing team at the forefront of that transformation. More Machine Learning happens on AWS than anywhere else, and if you have the drive and ambition, you can join a team looking to put AI to work for customers around the globe.<br>
<br>
Preference for this position to sit in Seattle, WA, but open to considering these additional cities only for the role: Palo Alto, CA.<br>
