<p><b>COMPANY DESCRIPTION</b></p>
<p>
Westwood Holdings Group, Inc., provides investment management services to institutional investors, private wealth clients and financial intermediaries and manages $15.4 billion in assets as of June 30, 2019. The firm manages a range of investments strategies including U.S. equities, Global and Emerging Markets equities, Global Convertible securities, Multi-Asset and Master Limited Partnerships. Westwood benefits from significant, broad-based employee ownership and trades on the New York Stock Exchange under the symbol “WHG.” Based in Dallas, TX, Westwood also maintains offices in Toronto, Canada, Boston, MA and Houston, TX.</p>
<p><b>
SUMMARY</b></p>
<p>
Supports Westwood Wealth Advisors with client and prospect engagements by completing comprehensive financial plans and other client-requested financial analysis. Financial planning subject areas include financial statement preparation, risk management and review, employee benefits planning, investment planning, income tax planning, retirement planning, and estate planning. This position does not have sales requirements or require federal or state licensing.</p>
<p><b>
ESSENTIAL DUTIES/RESPONSIBILITIES</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Builds relationships with internal Wealth Advisors and assists in identifying candidates for financial planning</li>
<li>
Prepare financial planning analysis, including but not limited to gathering client data, analyzing and evaluating the client’s financial status, and developing the financial planning recommendations. Analysis may include preparation of comprehensive financial plans, annual updates, and client-requested financial planning analysis.</li>
<li>
Lead the financial planning process from client discovery meeting though plan presentation</li>
<li>
Other duties and responsibilities as assigned.</li>
</ul>
