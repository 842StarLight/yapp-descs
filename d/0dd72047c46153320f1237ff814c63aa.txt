<p><b>Field Installation Manager</b></p>
<p>SmartRent, one of the nation’s most exciting and rapidly growing technology companies is hiring! We are looking for intuitive, driven individuals with project management experience in construction and/or tech, to lead large, multifamily smart home conversion projects. We are exploding with growth and are already in most major markets around the country so we are looking for individuals who are excited to travel. Field Installation Managers typically fly out to any given city for 5 days each week. Field Installation Managers interact daily with property managers, leasing agents, maintenance technicians, and residents. Your responsibilities will include communicating with Property Management staff on a recurring basis, technical support, field operations, training, quality assurance, and managing contractors on the ground. We pride ourselves in our dedication to going above and beyond for our client base. We measure our team's success in terms of how proactive we are, how responsive we are, and how many expressions of gratitude we get from our clients. You’ll play a crucial role in ensuring projects go as smoothly as possible and all contractors are representing the SmartRent brand in the highest and best way possible.</p>
<p>We offer a great career path with truly unlimited growth. This position is for the ambitious individual who wants to learn and acquire a number of high valued technical skills. This candidate will work closely with the entire operations team, product team, and engineers who are willing to coach, train and support you in adding all of the required skills, and then some.</p>
