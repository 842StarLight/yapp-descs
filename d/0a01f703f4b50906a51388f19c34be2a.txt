<h2>Sr. Technical Trainer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<b>Careers that Change Lives</b><div>
<p>
The Minimally Invasive Therapies Group strives to enable earlier diagnosis, better treatment, faster complication-free recovery, and enhanced patient outcomes through less invasive surgical solutions.</p>
The Sr Technical Trainer role will focus on develops concept-to-classroom training programs and provide product technical training to biomedical engineers, distributors and internal Medtronic employees.<br>
<br>
<b>A Day in the Life</b>
</div>
<div>
<div>
Develops concept-to-classroom training programs and provides product technical training to biomedical engineers, distributors and internal Medtronic employees.</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<ul>
<li>Collaborate with cross functional groups (Engineering, Product Marketing, Sales, Services, Legal, Quality, and Regulatory) to create technical training programs, curricula, course materials and measurements that support business unit strategies and are compliant with regulatory, quality systems, and Business Conduct Standards.</li>
<li>
Ensure timely updates to the course curricula to reflect changes in product design and market demands.</li>
<li>
Develop comprehensive training manuals, technical documents and course material for new and existing products.</li>
<li>
Deliver training material in a professional manner to both internal employees and customers.
</li>
<li>Develop training and instructional strategies and objectives.</li>
<li>
Drive innovative training methods including on-line modules and video tutorials.</li>
<li>
Assist with all aspects of Product Support Engineering.</li>
<li>
Plan, organize, lead and complete complex projects.</li>
<li>
Identifies alternative operational and servicing solutions or approaches.</li>
<li>
Partners with other facets of the business.</li>
<li>
Prepare Instructor Guides and train-the-trainer program on new/revised curricula and materials, on a global basis.
</li>
<li>Stay abreast of training and development industry research, (e.g. learning theory, motivation theory), and new methods and techniques.</li>
<li>
Work with administrative staff to develop agendas, certificates, tests and to schedule requests to attend training courses.</li>
<li>
Demonstrate training effectiveness through systematic program evaluation and continuous improvement strategy.</li>
<li>
Provide timely evaluation and feedback to trainees and their managers/supervisors.</li>
<li>
Partner with Field Service Engineers (including job shadow) to understand manufacturing process to develop and maintain technical knowledge, system intricacies and troubleshooting skills.</li>
<li>
Manage all hardware, software, classroom and lab equipment logistics required to deliver service training either in classroom or on site at a customer location (domestic or international).</li>
<li>
Other duties as assigned with or without accommodation.</li>
<li>
Normal office conditions exist as well as extended periods of standing and walking in a class room.
</li>
<li>Occasional domestic and international travel to meet business needs.</li>
<li>
Travel Requirement: Less than 10%</li>
</ul>

4+ years of technical training experience with medical products or an advanced degree and minimum 2 years related technical training experience with medical products</li>
