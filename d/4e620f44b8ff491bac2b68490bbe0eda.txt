<h2>Retail Merchandiser/Buyer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Summary</b></p>
<p>The (retail) Buyer is responsible for the procurement of all cannabis-infused, cannabis-extracted, and non-cannabis items for Skinny Pineapple’s retail locations. This position requires sales analysis and reporting to ensure that proper inventory levels are maintained, and sales are maximized.</p>
<p><b>Responsibilities</b></p>
<p><i>Procurement</i></p>
<ul>
<li>Manage stock level plan and predict needed levels for upcoming promotions</li>
<li>Make purchasing decisions in accordance with company procedures and MED/city regulations</li>
<li>Follow proper channels to obtain purchase authorization according to company policy</li>
<li>Review purchase requisitions for accuracy and completion</li>
<li>Maintain an efficient and accurate system for monitoring all open purchase orders</li>
<li>Approve all incoming deliveries as properly completed</li>
<li>Investigate and cultivate new sources for supplies and products</li>
<li>Solve problems with orders in an efficient and timely manner</li>
<li>Review and establish trigger points for automatic reorders</li>
<li>Maintain relationships with existing suppliers</li>
<li>Secure product samples, photos, and descriptions for departments</li>
<li>Collaborate with department managers to clarify purchase requisitions, identify departmental needs, and refine specifications for future purchase orders</li>
</ul>
<p><i>Analyzation</i></p>
<ul>
<li>Create budgets and forecast sales</li>
<li>Analyze and track trends in customer sales</li>
<li>Evaluate sales history to establish future fulfillment needs</li>
<li>Regularly review performance indicators (sales and discounts)</li>
<li>Analyze orders on an annual basis to assist as a negotiating tool for obtaining best price</li>
<li>Provide weekly overview on promotional sales to Retail Store Manager</li>
</ul>
<p><i>Market Research</i></p>
<ul>
<li>Attend industry-related tradeshows</li>
<li>Present new ranges to senior managers</li>
<li>Research suppliers and negotiate lowest costs</li>
<li>Investigate other retailers and maintain competitive retail pricing</li>
<li>Maintain open lines of communication for feedback from retail staff</li>
</ul>
<p><i>Inventory Control</i></p>
<ul>
<li>Maintain inventory levels with awareness of available storage space</li>
<li>Ensure inventory is properly distributed</li>
<li>Create new products in BioTrack and continually update product information for accurate reporting</li>
<li>Attend all EOM inventory sessions and assist in any resulting investigations</li>
</ul>
<p><i>Compliance</i></p>
<ul>
<li>Maintain awareness of changing compliance regulations</li>
<li>Ensure all vendor licenses are received in a timely manner and kept up to date</li>
<li>Confirm all new suppliers and products meet compliance standards before placing initial orders</li>
<li>Communicate with suppliers regarding any non-compliant, missing, or damaged items</li>
<li>Manage visual merchandising</li>
<li>Participate in promotional planning meetings</li>
<li>Educate retail staff on new items and technologies</li>
<li>Other duties as assigned</li>
</ul>
