<h2>Senior Sales Executive - Higher Education</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">Every day, our software is used by millions of people around the world, and we are looking for individuals who share in our excitement and passion for transforming the way the world learns. At D2L, we believe that learning should be accessible and engaging. Our goal is to create easy, flexible, and smart software that ignites the desire to learn in everyone. To do this, we need to give talented, enthusiastic, and passionate people opportunities to create, develop, and collaborate on projects that revolutionize the learning environment.
<br><br>
<b>JOB SUMMARY:</b><br>
As a D2L Senior Sales Executive you will be responsible for meeting and exceeding sales objectives for an assigned territory by promoting and selling the D2L product suite through professional sales techniques. We are looking for a highly knowledgeable and capable senior sales individual with a proven track record of selling high-value complex solutions to the Education industry. Candidates must possess an in depth knowledge of the Higher Education industry, K-12 is an added bonus. The Sales Executive will spend the majority of his/her time in field developing and cultivating prospects, moving them through the sales process and closing new business.
<br><br>
HOW WILL I MAKE AN IMPACT?
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Meeting and exceeding set sales quotas for defined area/region</li>
<li>Making prospecting an integral part of your regular routine ensuring new prospects are being added to the sales funnel on an ongoing basis</li>
<li>Managing a complex, enterprise solution sale with a 6 to 12 month purchasing cycle. Moving the sale through the entire sales process actively engaging other D2L resources as necessary to ensure success</li>
<li>Taking an active role in the RFP process</li>
<li>Continually learning about new products and improving your selling skills. The SSE will be required to attend training events throughout the year and will be expected to participate in self-paced tutorial learning when appropriate</li>
<li>Being well informed about current industry trends and being able to talk intelligently about the education industry in the assigned area/region</li>
<li>Becoming familiar with all D2L Partner relationships and how they relate to D2L sales</li>
<li>Attending and participating in sales meetings, product seminars and trade shows</li>
<li>Preparing written presentations, reports and price quotations</li>
<li>Assisting in contract negotiations</li>
<li>Building and managing a quantifiable 12 month sales pipeline</li>
<li>Effectively and efficiently deploy D2L resources at appropriate stages in the sales cycle to advance the sales process</li>
</ul>

<ul>
<li>5-7+ years' sales experience in the eLearning, education, and/or complex solution software sales industries</li>
<li>Must have strong understanding of software sales cycles and dealing with top decision makers within education</li>
<li>Knowledge of eLearning/education industry preferred</li>
<li>Successful achievement of 1M+ quotas</li>
<li>Ability to manage a pipeline of 50+ accounts at any given time</li>
<li>Ability to work in a team environment</li>
<li>Must possess strong leadership, motivational, and presentation skills</li>
<li>Proven success prospecting, building a pipeline, moving opportunities through the sales cycle; proposing, presenting and discussing solutions with C-level and other decision-makers</li>
<li>Ability to craft a solution with appropriate products and services that meets business goals based on client discussions</li>
<li>Working knowledge of web and database technology</li>
<li>This position requires the SSE to work out of home as base of operations</li>
