<p>About Atlantic Home Loans</p>
<p>Atlantic Home Loans is a 20 yr old Mortgage Bank currently serving 21 markets around the country and growing. We have been ranked as one of the Top 100 mortgage banks in the country on multiple occasions and have received a list of awards an achievements in the industry lending to our position and longevity in the mortgage industry. Atlantic Home Loans has one of the fastest-growing marketing technology platforms made available to its Loan Officers and prides itself on its industry-leading operations team that works towards industry-leading close times. We're working to bring on the best Loan Officers in the industry who would take advantage of our marketing and technology collateral and expertise.</p>
<p>Responsibilities:</p>
<p>Increases mortgage loan portfolio by developing business contacts; attracting mortgage customers; completing mortgage loan processing and closing; supervising staff.</p>
<p>Accountabilities:</p>
<ul>
<li>Accomplishes mortgage loan human resource objectives by selecting, orienting, training, assigning, scheduling, coaching, counseling, and disciplining employees; communicating job expectations; planning, monitoring, appraising job contributions; recommending compensation actions; adhering to policies and procedures.</li>
<li>Meets mortgage loan operational standards by contributing mortgage loan information to strategic plans and reviews; implementing production, productivity, quality, and customer-service standards; resolving problems; identifying mortgage loan system improvements.</li>
<li>Meets mortgage loan financial standards by providing annual budget information; monitoring expenditures; identifying variances; implementing corrective actions.</li>
<li>Attracts new mortgage loan applications by developing relationships within the community, specifically with the real estate community; making sales calls to prospective customers.</li>
<li>Approves mortgage loans by examining the application and supporting documentation; estimating credit-worthiness; calculating repayment risk.</li>
<li>Competes for mortgage loans by monitoring collection, verification, and preparation of mortgage loan documentation; scheduling and completing mortgage loan closing.</li>
<li>Protects bank's image by keeping mortgage loan information confidential.</li>
<li>Updates job knowledge by participating in educational opportunities; reading professional publications; maintaining personal networks; participating in professional organizations.</li>
<li>Accomplishes bank mission by completing related results as needed.</li>
</ul>
