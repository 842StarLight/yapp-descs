<h2>Senior Writer, Customer Marketing</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>Cvent is an exciting, fast-growing tech company that provides industry-leading software to meeting and event professionals around the world. We are looking for a Content Marketing Lead to join our Hospitality Cloud marketing team!</p>
<p>
We are looking for a talented content creator to strategize, write, edit and produce content for Cvent Hospitality Cloud customer marketing channels. This is an exciting opportunity for the right candidate to help expand and strengthen Cvent’s role as a partner to hotels, destinations and special venues by expanding product utilization, drive advocacy for Cvent’s brand and provide support to protect renewals by influencing upsell/cross sells with our users.</p>
<p>
This position reports to the Senior Manager, Content Marketing on the Hospitality Cloud, and aligns closely with the Director of Customer Marketing. The role requires a high level of creativity, analytical thinking, attention to detail, and project management skills. The ideal candidate will have a good understanding of multi-channel content marketing and a track record of delivering high-quality content as part of a customer marketing programming designed to focus on onboarding, adoption and advocacy for the Hospitality Cloud. B2B marketing experience is required, ideally in the hospitality, events, enterprise software or marketing technology sectors.</p>
<p>
<b>What You’ll Be Doing:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Collaborate with the senior manager, and customer marketing team on the Hospitality Cloud to develop content strategy to support customer campaigns (paid and organic) targeted to users</li>
<li>Assist in developing a content strategy to support marketing campaigns targeted to of our marketing solutions and digital software/tools with the intention of protecting renewals</li>
<li>Be a creative thinker and a relentless marketer who understands the B2B technology space and can act quickly to take advantage of marketing opportunities – but also plan thoroughly for what’s next</li>
<li>Communicate with internal stakeholders to ensure the needs and goals of customer marketing projects are met and build meaningful relationships within the organization</li>
<li>Apply your storytelling, writing, and visual skills to create marketing content that matters. Ensure content is appealing, informative and satisfying to the visitor</li>
<li>Publish customer success stories and case studies in print, online and via video across the Cvent Hospitality Cloud product portfolio for use in all marketing programs and sales enablement</li>
<li>Develop briefs for focus groups and reports analyzing research conducted or sponsored by Cvent that is of interest to Cvent customers with a focus on the user base</li>
<li>Create eBooks, infographics, promotional copy for emails, webinars and such, and additional forms of content designed to increase user engagement, further influence with advocates and achieve goals of programming</li>
<li>Create and optimize content on Cvent’s community and Cvent Celebrity portal to drive and grow traffic, build advocacy, support training/education and promote cross sell/upsell</li>
<li>Measure, track, and report on content metrics to evaluate campaign results and effectiveness across all appropriate channels</li>
<li>Help to maintain content inventories and calendars (success guides, case studies, training, etc.); assess existing content and identify new content types; analyze existing content to ensure it conveys a consistent tone and credible message to the customer and user audience, is on-brand and consistent in terms of style, quality and tone across the customer lifecycle</li>
<li>Communicate with internal stakeholders to ensure the needs and goals of marketing projects are met</li>
</ul>
<p><b>What You Need For This Position:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>3-5 years B2B marketing experience, preferably in technology or software industry, ideally in marketing tech, hospitality or events sectors. Prior experience in customer marketing focused initiatives preferred</li>
<li>Creative thinker, team player, detail-oriented, process-driven, self-motivated and effective communicator</li>
<li>Experience of working in a multi-national, matrixed organization to develop customer marketing content and campaigns in partnership with multiple product, client services, sales &amp; marketing departments</li>
<li>Strong knowledge of online &amp; digital marketing techniques for customer and advocacy marketing, and of developing content for customer advocacy, cross-sell, upsell and utilization campaigns</li>
<li>Education: BA/BS degree in Marketing, Journalism or related field</li>
</ul>
