<div>
We are looking for an experienced Data Architect to join our amazing team in Cincinnati.</div>
<div>
Insight Digital Innovation is doing cutting edge Cloud, Big Data, AI, DevOps, Mixed Reality, Mobile, Machine Learning &amp; IoT development. We are a Microsoft National Solution Provider and a Microsoft Managed Partner. We recently won the Microsoft Worldwide Partner of the Year for Open Source on Azure AND Worldwide Partner of the Year in Artificial Intelligence.</div>
<div>
The position is full-time, permanent, with great benefits like Flexible Vacation and a 5%+ yearly bonus. No travel required. Extensive training and mentoring. And best of all, Insight has an amazing culture and is a fun place to work.</div>
<div><b>
What Data Architects Do</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
Lead discussions with client leadership explaining architecture options and recommendations</li>
<li>
Understand and evaluate the latest trends in the BI space including Cloud and Big Data technologies</li>
<li>
Design sustainable and optimal database models to support the client's business requirements</li>
<li>
Advise clients on BI strategy, database management, integration and BI tools</li>
<li>
Assist in performance tuning of databases and code</li>
<li>
Mentor development team members in design and development of complex ETL and BI implementations</li>
<li>
Perform architectural assessments of the client's BI solution including an Enterprise Data Warehouse (EDW)</li>
<li>
Work with the sales team to create and deliver client proposals and demonstrations</li>
<li>
Present to client, industry and internal peer groups</li>
</ul>
<div><b>
What We Look For</b></div>
<ul>
<li>
Successful background as an architect and/or technical lead on an Enterprise Data Warehouse, Big Data, or EIM team</li>
<li>
Track record of successfully architecting small, medium and enterprise scale solutions</li>
<li>
Knowledge of Data Warehouse principles and methodologies: Kimbal, Inmon, Data Vault, Data Lake, etc.</li>
<li>
Knowledge and expertise of database modeling techniques: Star, Snowflake, 3NF, etc</li>
<li>
Knowledge of best practices in the database, ETL and/or BI technologies</li>
<li>
Experience with modeling tools like Power designer, ERWIN, etc.</li>
<li>
Experience working with relational or OLAP database: Oracle, SQL Server, DB2, Teradata, Netezza</li>
<li>
Experience with cloud BI suites including the Cortana Intelligence Suite</li>
<li>
Knowledge and experience with ETL tools: SSIS, Informatica, Datastage, SAP Data Integrator</li>
<li>
Knowledge and experience with BI reporting and visualization tools: Power BI, Tableau, MicroStrategy, Business Objects, Reporting Services, Cognos, Qlikview, etc.</li>
<li>
Eagerness to learn new tools and technologies</li>
<li>
Experience building EIM or BI roadmaps to meet business needs</li>
<li>
Strong communication skills</li>
</ul>
