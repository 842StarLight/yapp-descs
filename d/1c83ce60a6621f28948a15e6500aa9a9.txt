<h2>Business Development Representative</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>The Business Development Representative (BDR)role leads our lead generation efforts across our family of companies byengaging potential customers to among other things prospect customer’s businessneeds to match K &amp; L’s service offerings. Success for a BDR includes scheduling qualified prospect calls for the K&amp; L family of companies, as well as identifying and securing businessopportunities.</p>
<p>A stepping stone to a National Sales Executive(NSE) role, the BDR role is the entry point for a sales career at K &amp; L. Aspart of the role’s onboarding plan, the BDR learns the logistics space,including asset based &amp; 3PL transportation management services.</p>
<p><b>RESPONSIBILITIES&amp; OUTCOMES</b></p>
<ul><li>Prospect into multipleorganizations via cold-calling, email, and networking while utilizing variousinformation sources.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Articulate the Company's valueproposition to decision-makers across multiple industries to assess buyinginterest.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Qualify all sales leads based uponspecific lead qualification criteria definitions.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Provide baseline research withintargeted accounts to identify key contacts and critical account informationprior to prospecting touches.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Achieve or exceed monthly goalsfor activities and first meetings.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Partner with National SalesExecutives in order to continuously evaluate the success rates of prospectseffectively transitioning to opportunities.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Learn and demonstrate afundamental understanding of K &amp; L’s suite of services – and efficientlyevaluate the fit of those Services with prospective customers’ needs.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Develop the initial customerservice relationships with prospects.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Contribute to every component ofthe critical functions associated with lead prospecting (forecasting,reporting, customer database maintenance and data entry, correspondence, andcommunications).</li></ul>
<ul><li>Proactively build knowledge in K&amp; L’s services and understand competitive landscape.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Update leads and prospectinteractions in Hubspot.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Publish a daily “hits and misses”report.</li></ul>
<p><b>QUALIFICATIONS</b></p>
<ul><li>Earned a 4 year college degree. Preferably in business or supply chainlogistics degree.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Preferred recent college graduatesand or 1-2 years of sales or business development experience selling B2B servicesor products and utilizing sales productivity technologies (e.g., LinkedIn; CRM;Sales Productivity software).</li></ul>
<ul><li>Is curious and has the desire tounderstand our customers’ problems – and then effectively communicate how K&amp; L can add value.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Has a passion for finding leads atnew accounts.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Demonstrates great listeningskills, as well as humility. K &amp; L won’t be the right solution all the timefor every organization, so we believe in understanding customer needs firstbefore we make our pitch.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Comprehensively understands ourservices.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Has great people skills, includingthe ability to quickly understand the audience and tailor the right message tothem.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Is proficient at building lastingrelationships.</li></ul>
