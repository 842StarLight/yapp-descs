<h2>Seasonal Sales Associate</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>CB2 Sales Associates are at the core of our customers' experience. Friendly, outgoing and naturally driven, you're a fan of everything we sell. That enthusiasm brings a level of excitement to our stores that makes shopping fun and keeps customers coming back. Under the direction and mentorship of the Sales Manager, your day is dedicated to meeting - and exceeding - daily sales goals, becoming an expert on our products, answering customers' questions and keeping the sales floor neat and ready to shop.</div>
<br>
<ul>
<li>
Maximize company sales growth and profitability through creative sales skills and merchandising techniques, customer service and teamwork.</li>
<li>
Acquire a complete understanding of company policies, philosophy and vision.</li>
<li>
Maximize sales and productivity through excellent product knowledge and selling skills.</li>
<li>
Keep current on merchandise in all departments and sell merchandise throughout the entire store.</li>
<li>
Model company standards in customer service by providing gracious, quick and efficient service with a sense of fun to all of our customers.</li>
<li>
Maintain floor coverage; follow posted schedules with regard to counter assignments.</li>
<li>
Maintain all store displays and uprights/shelves, understanding the priorities of stocking, pricing and cleaning.</li>
<li>
Maintain an awareness of current advertising, promotions and catalogues, and the status of merchandise.</li>
<li>
Participate in the implementation and maintenance of seasonal floor plans.</li>
<li>
Learn merchandising techniques with regard to all CB2 merchandise.</li>
<li>
Communicate customer feedback to store management, staff and product managers concerning policies and/or merchandising requests, problems and suggestions.</li>
<li>
Actively participate in weekend meetings.</li>
<li>
Maintain store counter with regard to cleanliness and supplies.</li>
<li>
Assist in preparing the store and stockroom for Inventory and perform assigned duties.</li>
<li>
Maintain awareness and follow the loss prevention, security and safety guidelines.</li>
<li>
Provide excellent service by exhibiting a positive attitude and enthusiasm towards job and company to both internal and external customers in all forms of communication.</li>
</ul>
</div></div>
