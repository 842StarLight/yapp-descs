<h2>Industry Marketing Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<ul>
<li>5+ years of relevant work experience in fields such as product marketing, technical marketing, advertising, technical pre-sales, public relations, communications, or product management.</li>
<li>A Bachelor’s degree in marketing, business, technology, or a relevant field of study such as computer science or journalism.</li>
<li>Demonstrated track record in crafting positioning and marketing content for technical products.</li>

<br>
<div>Companies in every major industry (Manufacturing, Healthcare, Financial Services, Retail, etc.) are constantly building new innovations on Amazon Web Services (AWS). These companies use combinations of many different AWS capabilities such as Artificial Intelligence, IoT, and Data Lakes to solve problems, accelerate growth, and create new experiences for their customers.<br>
We’re looking for a standout marketer who can help us capture those successes and showcase amazing stories that resonate with the highest levels of decision makers and inspire entire industries to do more. This is a highly visible role on the AWS Industry Marketing Team that will partner with the AWS marketing leaders of every major industry to bring customer stories to life.<br>
This is an exciting opportunity to join a new team in one of the fastest growth areas for AWS.<br>
Expect to hit the ground running with the following goals…<br>
CONTENT STRATEGY &amp; COORDINATION<br>
<ul>
<li>Engage with AWS Marketing Leaders across every major industry to understand their priorities</li>
<li>Partner with Marketing, Business Development, and Account Management leaders to identify the most meaningful customer successes in their industry, secure those customers as references, and turn those success stories into impactful content that can be used and reused across a variety of channels.</li>
<li>Implement a mechanism for prioritizing customer references and story content based on the needs of each industry and geography</li>
<li>Maintain and monitor the inventory of customer story content and generate internal reports and communications for stakeholders.</li>
<li>Track and report content and program effectiveness metrics to internal stakeholders, as well as continually monitor, analyze, and tune processes to drive improvements.</li>
<li>Match content distribution strategies and platforms to objectives</li>
<li>Think big about ways to inspire decision makers and demonstrate how AWS can help customers be successful.</li>
</ul>
CONTENT CREATION<br>
<ul>
<li>Raise the bar for new and existing reference content and create the standard bill of material for how customer stories are presented across channels.</li>
<li>Create innovative ways to repurpose, repackage, and refresh existing content.</li>
<li>Integrate and feature priority product categories (e.g. AI/ML) into story content by showing how they are being used in the industry.</li>
</ul>
CONTENT AMPLIFICATION<br>
<ul>
<li>Establish playbook, clear process and best practices for Industry Marketing to deliver integrated marketing content to target audiences</li>
<li>Own and manage communication channels for customer reference material including an industry newsletter and blog.</li>
<li>Develop mechanisms for industry marketing leads to “Plug and play” into customer story distribution channels, in coordination with larger AWS organization</li>
<li>Regularly evaluate content distribution channels (owned, earned, paid)</li>
<li>Work with the Web team to ensure that industry webpages with customer story content meet best practices, are SEO optimized, and are always up to date.</li>
<li>Collaborate with other Product Marketing, Digital Marketing, Brand Marketing and Field Marketing organizations to amplify content and industry messages.</li>
</ul>
This is a unique opportunity to play a highly visible role in an exciting, industry-leading technology business, while we work hard, have fun, and make history together.</div>
