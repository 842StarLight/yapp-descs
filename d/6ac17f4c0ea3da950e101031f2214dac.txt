<h2>Senior Power Systems Engineer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p><b>POWER YOUR FUTURE WITH CE POWER</b> as a Senior Distribution Engineer. You will be responsible for overseeing executing power systems study and design projects from start to finish in compliance with industry and company standard practices and procedures. The Senior Engineer will review job/project specifications and changes, assist with the execution of the project and work with project managers to coordinate project specific resources. Additional responsibilities include providing both technical and sales support as needed to others inside the company.</p>
<p>
As a Senior Distribution Engineer, you will be responsible for:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Demonstrating consistent proficiency through daily updates and related job execution communications.</li>
<li>
Leading interaction with other functions and locations to determine optimum solutions.</li>
<li>
Remaining current on all safety and technical training.</li>
<li>
Working with CAD staff developing and/or updating client electrical drawings including one lines, wiring diagrams, and schematics.</li>
<li>
Reviewing field data and perform, coordination, short circuit analysis, and arc flash studies.</li>
<li>
Providing technical insight with regard to substation systems and electrical power systems.</li>
<li>
Adhering to all company, industry and client safety protocols and standards.</li>
<li>
Assessing project specific resource needs and coordinates assignments with project manager.</li>
<li>
Communicating core technical job requirements.</li>
<li>
Supporting immediate supervisor on planning and organizing workload to ensure the most effective return on assets.</li>
<li>
Coordinating with the Engineering Manager on the master engineering resource schedule as well as forecasting upcoming engineering requirements/needs including non-job related training schedules.</li>
<li>
Managing expenses against project budgets and makes recommendations to reduce expenses.</li>
<li>
Coordinating proper project hand-off between sales and operations/engineering.</li>
<li>
Participating in project kickoff meetings to discuss work scope, project specifications, budgets, schedules, and safety requirements with project team.</li>
<li>
Effectively communicating with other departments to assure activities are properly coordinated.</li>
<li>
Ensuring timely and accurate invoicing for all engineering projects by providing all paperwork in a like manner; these include Service Acknowledgement Forms (SAFs), weekly time keeping, documentation of change orders, test reports, and other documents as required by the project deliverables.</li>
<li>
Consulting and coordinating as necessary to utilize suppliers with the capabilities to meet material and equipment needs for shop and field activities. Responsible for expediting and resolving issues regarding material and equipment availability.</li>
<li>
Overseeing all required documentation and records related to assigned projects on file in the proper location for reference and backup.</li>
<li>
Training and mentoring junior engineers.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
QUALIFICATIONS:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Minimum<b> </b>4-7 years design engineering or primary power distribution and related protection and control schemes.</li>
<li>
Must understand and be able to apply NEC/NFPA 70, ANSI C57, ANSI C37, and NESC in the design of substations and industrial power.
</li>
<li>Must be able to sustained successful performance at the Electrical Engineer level.</li>
<li>
Education: PE recommended, NETA Level II recommended</li>
<li>
Strong interpersonal skills, communication skills, business acumen, management ability.</li>
<li>
Valid Driver’s license and good driving record.</li>
</ul>
