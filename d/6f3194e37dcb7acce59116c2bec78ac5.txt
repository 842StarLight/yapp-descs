<h2>Closing - Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>Company Introduction:<br>
At Grocery Outlet, you’ll find brand-name groceries and farm fresh produce at up to 40-70% off conventional retail prices.<br>
<br>
Overview:<br>
Assists Store Owners/Operators with the daily closing of the store according to store procedures, assists with the pricing and the promotion of saleable products, and supervises and participates in the stocking of store products as it relates to sales, margin, inventory turns, labor and customer service.<br>
<br>
Reports to: Store Owners/Operators<br>
<br>
PHYSICAL DEMANDS:<br>
<br>
<ul><li>Requires frequent standing, walking, kneeling, squatting, bending, reaching, lifting up to 50 lbs</li></ul>
<br>
GENERAL RESPONSIBILITIES::<br>
<br>
Customer Service<br>
<ul>
<li>Knows and adheres to quality standards for customer service as outlined by the Store Owners/Operators</li>
<li>Adheres to all guidelines around product recommendation and information</li>
<li>Assists in any other departments, based on store need</li>
</ul>
<br>
Personal Effectiveness<br>
<ul>
<li>Reports to work for scheduled shifts, on-time and appropriately uniformed</li>
<li>Understands and adheres to store’s policy and procedures</li>
<li>Accepts and offers feedback and suggestions openly and respectfully</li>
<li>Accepts direction willingly and follows through with all delegated tasks</li>
<li>Learns and adapts to new tasks or situations quickly and cooperatively</li>
<li>Maintains job-related confidentiality</li>
<li>Takes initiative to identify, report and resolve problems before they escalate</li>
<li>Provides a positive model for co-workers</li>
</ul>
<br>
Safety:<br>
<ul>
<li>Maintains a safe work environment according to all store procedures and state and/or federal regulations. Safety being the number one priority.</li>
<li>Wears protective clothing as required for position, (if necessary)</li>
</ul>
<br>
POSITION RESPONSIBILITIES::<br>
<br>
<ul>
<li>Operates all necessary computer equipment and software related to operation of store closing</li>
<li>Required to work night shifts</li>
<li>Stocks, rotates, merchandises and faces product according to store procedure</li>
<li>Maintains backroom areas and sales floor according to store cleanliness standards</li>
<li>Maintains temperature controls for deli, meat and frozen as per store and OSHA standards</li>
<li>Operates equipment related to the merchandising such as scales, flat bed trucks, hand trucks and pallet jacks, etc.</li>
<li>Operates all necessary computer equipment and software related to operation of the grocery department</li>
<li>May receive product deliveries, ensuring order and invoice accuracy</li>
<li>May be responsible for opening based on store need</li>
<li>May be required work early morning, weekends and/or open holiday schedules.</li>
<li>Trains other staff, based on store need</li>
<li>Assists in other departments, based on store need</li>
</ul>
</div></div>
