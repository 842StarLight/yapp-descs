<h2>Business Operations Project Manager</h2>

<div>
<p><b>THE CHALLENGE</b></p>
<p>
Eventbrite is a people-first organization. Our goal of building the global platform for live experience relies on the foundation of top talent and strong company culture. Our team is the face of Eventbrite, and we’re charged with finding, attracting, and bringing on the best of the best to drive Eventbrite’s growing business and award winning culture forward. Hiring the best global talent is no easy feat. However, we have a talented team who has an inspiring story and mission. Our perfect candidate is someone who can connect that vision and story with top-notch talent.<br>
</p>
<br>
<p><b>THE TEAM</b></p>
<p>
Revenue Operations’ mission is to enable the Revenue, Service and Finance teams to scale and grow top line growth quickly and efficiently. This is accomplished by maintaining a healthy, compliant, and up-to-date system architecture in addition to delivering technical solutions to meet business strategy that automate processes, drive efficiency and allow scale.</p>
<br>
<p>
The Business Operations team specifically is charged with driving key operational initiatives for the Sales &amp; Services, Marketing, CX, Legal and Finance organizations as well as strategic projects for the company. The goal is to drive growth and scale through analysis and transformation of core business activities, systems, and enhanced data intelligence.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
THE ROLE</b></p>
<p>
The Business Operations Project Manager is an integral member of the Revenue Operations team. This position requires applied knowledge of project management frameworks and tools to optimize the planning and implementation of strategic projects and initiatives. This is a critical role and includes high-level visibility and interaction with stakeholders across the organization globally. The role requires an understanding of the business need for each project and the ability to make ongoing decisions based on what’s best for the organization, even if that means challenging senior stakeholders on critical success factors.</p>
<h4 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
YOU WILL</b></h4>
<ul>
<li>Work closely with our Sales, Customer, Marketing, Legal, Finance, Product &amp; Engineering teams to develop our roadmap of strategic projects and company wide initiatives</li>
<li>Guide our key stakeholders through our project prioritization framework, ensuring alignment on scope, timing, project plan and final deliverables
</li>
<li>Partner extensively with the Systems Administration &amp; Development team to accurately scope and solution design all components required to deliver against project requirements</li>
<li>Drive complex projects from initiation to completion, ensure that key success metrics such as ROI and revenue impact are identified and tracked through the lifecycle of each project</li>
<li>Implement appropriate quality &amp; risk mitigation practices for all projects to ensure that key project partners and internal stakeholders are aligned to the project plan, scope and success metrics</li>
<li>Lead consistent communication for projects and as a representative of Business Ops with customers, key internal/ external stakeholders and senior management</li>
<li>Collaborate with the wider Business Operations team in the continuing improvement and refinement of our project lifecycle framework</li>
<li>Collaboratively own vendor lifecycle management (procurement, renewals, consolidations &amp; depreciations) for the Revenue Operations Tech Stack</li>
<li>Drive efficiency within our expensed software budget</li>
<li>Partner with the broader business to implement or re-design processes and controls that are SOX compliant</li>
</ul>
<h4 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>SKILL SET</b></h4>
<ul>
<li>3-5+ years of prior experience in driving Business Operations and/or Process Improvement projects</li>
<li>SaaS project management experience in an agile environment a plus</li>
<li>Knowledge and experience with SaaS tech stack such as salesforce.com, Gainsight, Marketo, Jira/Confluence, etc.</li>
<li>Experience in cross-functional project management</li>
<li>Problem-solving maven adept at quickly dissecting an issue into its component parts and identifying the root cause or opportunity</li>
<li>Excellent prioritization skills - ability to multi-task and work well under pressure and adjust work accordingly, often against tight deadlines and without continued direct supervision</li>
<li>Ability to interact with senior management, external client organizations and vendors</li>
<li>Strong organizational skills, attention to detail and flexibility to multi-task across projects with varying deadlines</li>
<li>Excellent verbal and written communication skills</li>
<li>High energy, self-starter with bias for action and sense of urgency to deliver results</li>
</ul>
<h4 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>BONUS POINTS</b></h4>
<ul>
<li>Prior experience working in Operations for high growth start-ups</li>
<li>Foundational understanding of the sales pipeline from lead to bookings and very familiar with marketing contribution to this pipeline</li>
<li>Understanding of deep self serve, up-sell and retention frameworks or experience</li>
<li>Experience leading or working on projects adopting agile project management &amp; agile development methodologies</li>
</ul>
