<h2>Marketing Specialist- Ecommerce</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>Tripp Lite is seeking a Marketing Specialist – Ecommerce, to join our Channel Marketing team. This role is an essential part of our Channel Marketing team as well as our Ecommerce team, reporting to our Director of Marketing. You will be working directly with Tripp Lite’s top ecommerce accounts, including Amazon, to manage all aspects of marketing and website management to grow Tripp Lite’s brand, exposure, messaging and ultimately sales. This position is data-centric and will be tasked with growing the brand via e-commerce and digital advertising channels. You’ll collaborate with other members of the Marketing &amp; Ecommerce Team to produce advertising content and marketing materials. This role requires a proactive and detail-oriented individual who is able to own their marketing voice.</div>
<div></div>
<div>Responsibilities include:</div>
<ul>
<li>Management of Amazon and other e-commerce Advertising platforms, including sponsored search, sponsored product, sponsored brands, DSP and more. From strategizing on campaign ideas to launching campaigns, managing the bid process, creating meaningful reporting results and making improvements to campaigns based on results. All while meeting internal success, spend and sales metrics.</li>
<li>Management of Tripp Lite brand presence on account websites, consistent auditing of brand to maximize exposure, and detailed competitive analysis.</li>
<li>Management of all Tripp Lite skus on sites, including optimizing copy, images and more.</li>
<li>New sku launch and setup, including researching keywords, creating custom copy, loading new skus, and following up to ensure proper website launch.</li>
<li>Work with partners to understand their website structure, product categories and listings, product ranking, search criteria and our competitive landscape in order to improve Tripp Lite product positions. Find opportunities to optimize the Tripp Lite brand and implement these ideas to improve Tripp Lite presence.</li>
<li>Execute on our account-based marketing activities, including writing copy and putting creatives together for various campaigns and catalog pages, web spots, emails, banner ads, landing pages, training materials, and more. Includes creation of fresh and effective catalog pages and exclusive email blasts, as well as Tripp Lite brand stores. Proof materials and ensure they are properly executed on.</li>
<li>Creation of fresh, effective and creative content including flyers, newsletters, powerpoints, email blasts, giveaways and more.</li>
<li>Develop effective customized marketing collateral for various promotions, new products, focus products, vertical segments and key initiatives.</li>
<li>Monitor reviews on partner sites and create timely, sincere and effective negative review responses, collaborate and escalate reviews as needed to get the input of additional company stakeholders for most effective review responses. Recognize and escalate issues and trends to management and QA.</li>
<li>Provide effective and constructive feedback and suggestions to Management in regards to marketing initiatives with given account base.</li>
<li>Act as a business partner to the Ecommerce Director</li>
<br>
</ul>
<div></div>
<div>Job Requirements:</div>
<ul>
<li>BS in marketing or similar field</li>
<li>2+ years marketing experience</li>
<li>Highly organized with ability to handle multiple projects independently</li>
<li>Detail-oriented</li>
<li>Self-motivated – proven track record of working well independently and as part of a team</li>
<li>Amazon or digital experience a plus</li>
<li>While not required, bilingual is a plus</li>
</ul>
</div></div>
