<p><b>
Job Summary</b></p>
<p><b>
**This is a 100% remote position for Arizona residents**</b></p>
<p>
The Tier 2 Support Engineer is responsible for assisting clients with installing, configuring and diagnosing issues with our software products, providing both pre-sale and post-sale technical advice and support to prospects and customers. The Tier 2 Support Engineer may have responsibility to securely access, manage, and update cloud servers and services for use by our customers.</p>
<p><b>
Responsibilities and Duties</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Provide Escalation Support for the Level 1s who can't solve the issues</li>
<li>
Work on Projects for Clients - research, design, production, implementation</li>
<li>
Join in on some sales meetings with clients to be a technical adviser and solutions "person"</li>
<li>
Monitors status of open trouble tickets to ensure that service level agreements and timelines are being met</li>
</ul>
