<h2>IT - Senior Business Intelligence Analyst</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>The Senior BI Analyst will create, analyze and publish comprehensive business intelligence for the ecommerce program while identifying opportunities, threats and trends. Responsible for all aspects of eBusiness intelligence including monthly reporting packages to user behavior/feedback to industry/competitor benchmarks.Develop a 3-5 year ecommerce strategic roadmap and assist in identifying commercial opportunities for the program to pursue. Will work cross-functionally with Marketing, IT, Finance, Sales and Merchandising.</p>
<p>
The Senior BI Analyst must be comfortable participating in all phases of the software development lifecycle for multiple projects simultaneously as well as displaying exceptional communication skills to technical and non-technical team members.</p>
<p><b>
Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Create and publish regular reporting to summarize eCommerce orders, revenues, guest behavior, traffic, and other key digital program metrics.</li>
<li>
Monitor, analyze, and report performance of our eCommerce environment using site analytics such as Google Analytics focusing on purchase behavior, traffic drivers, site experience, site functionality, impact of analysis-driven changes, growth opportunities, revenue effects of site enhancements and split tests, product receptivity, marketing campaigns and seasonal business trends.</li>
<li>
Pull data from Data Warehouse(s) and analyze sales, orders, acquisitions and retention.</li>
<li>
Collect, analyze, and report user feedback from a variety of sources including: surveys, usability/split tests, focus groups, clickstream data, etc.</li>
<li>
Analyze guest behavior and recommend site improvements based on factual data, potential ROI, and business trends.</li>
<li>
Perform competitive analysis including feature set comparisons, vendor/platform adoptions, announcements, etc.</li>
<li>
Act as in-house expert and key business owner for eCommerce site analytics tool. Trains and supports individual business users and stakeholders to leverage tools as needed for efficiency.</li>
<li>
Perform a steady stream of ad hoc reports, analysis, and recommendations on request for individual business users.</li>
<li>
Be entrepreneurial and creative in seeking out new ways/technologies that can assist the team in meeting its goals</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Requirements:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Bachelor’s degree in computer science, mathematics, finance, accounting, business, economics, marketing, or related field with strong quantitative focus.</li>
<li>
Expert skills in data analysis software and computer languages (SQL, VBA, R) to analyze and manipulate data and develop/modify reports.</li>
<li>
Expert proficiency in Excel and Access</li>
<li>
Expert with Google Analytics or comparable platform</li>
<li>
Ability to pull data from a Data Warehouse by authoring SQL queries</li>
<li>
Ability to understand raw data/metrics, draw succinct conclusions, and illustrate these conclusions in easy to read report formats for peers and executives</li>
<li>
Knowledge or experience with relational database structures and query techniques</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Full-Time Position:</b> Monday – Friday</p>
