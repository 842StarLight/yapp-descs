<h2>Buyer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>At McKinstry, we’re proud to be a collective group of innovators and problem-solvers, builders and engineers, mentors and students. We strive to create an engaging work environment that empowers you to do the work you are passionate about.</p>
<p>
We believe deeply that energy and facility services, engineering and construction are revolutionizing the built environment – and if you’re driven by our mission to transform ideas into meaningful relationships and together, build a thriving planet, McKinstry may be the place for you. Serving our community together is one of the ways we at McKinstry put our shared purpose into action.</p>
<p>
Total collaboration and a diverse, employee-centric work experience are what we can provide for you. Integrated delivery and true consultative partnership are what you will help us provide for our clients. Our culture is what differentiates us and because of this, we seek exceptional talent that believes what we believe.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Here's where you come in:</b></p>
<p>
We are currently seeking a <b>Buyer</b> to join the team as a member of our growing New Construction Mechanical Western Washington division. Our buyers support both field and project teams by providing access to timely and accurate purchasing information and delivery of materials, tieing materials and services for project-to-project financials.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
You're great at:</b></p>
<p><i>
Purchasing</i></p>
<ul>
<li>Processing purchase requests received via purchasing hotline, email, or face-to-face for materials, services, and renting of third-party equipment while arranging for their timely delivery.</li>
<li>Placing orders with vendors as required.</li>
<li>Pre-qualifying and developing supplier, considering performance history.</li>
<li>Preparing bidder lists, requests for quotation and conducting bid evaluations.</li>
<li>Pricing purchase orders at creation or from documents received from suppliers.</li>
<li>Preparing, issuing, and administering purchase orders, ensuring compliance with contractual terms and regulations; processing and verifying invoices; ensuring supplier performance and compliance in a cost-effective timely manner; maintaining purchase order and purchasing process documentation.</li>
<li>Attaching related documents to purchase orders as established in the departmental SOP.</li>
<li>Prioritizing work as established by departmental SOP.</li>
<li>Suggesting ways to improve efficiency within the department or throughout McKinstry.</li>
</ul>
<p><i>Communication</i></p>
<ul>
<li>Recognizing Field &amp; Project teams as internal customers and engaging with them in a positive, customer-focused manner.</li>
<li>Escalating Equipment Requisitions to Purchasing Agents or Purchasing Manager, using judgment to escalate potential problems in a timely manner.</li>
<li>Owning transactions from creation through completion with thorough follow-up practices.</li>
<li>Embracing change in processes as McKinstry grows.</li>
<li>Performing additional duties as directed by Purchasing Manager.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p>
</p>
<p><b>What we would like to see from you:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Excellent oral and written communication and organizational skills including a high degree of attention to detail required.</li>
<li>Ability to quickly learn new computer software applications and SOP processes required.</li>
<li>Operates office equipment such as telephones, calculators, computers, copy and fax machines required.</li>
<li>Advanced understanding of MS Outlook and Excel Software required.</li>
<li>Bachelor's degree or equivalent work experience required.</li>
<li>Basic understanding of purchasing principals.</li>
<li>Associate degree or equivalent work experience required; B.A./B.S. preferred.</li>
<li>Typing speed of 50wpm or more; 10-key by touch required.</li>
<li>Two (2) years of purchasing experience including soliciting, negotiating, drafting, and administering purchase orders preferred.</li>
<li>Experience in the construction environment preferred.</li>
<br>
</ul>
