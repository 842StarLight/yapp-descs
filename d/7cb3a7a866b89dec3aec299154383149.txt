<h2>Community Manager - Atlantic at Grand Oaks Apartments</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Greystar</b>, the global leader in rental housing, is hiring an experienced <b>Community Manager </b>(Property Manager) to join our team!</p>
<p>Ideal candidates should have a minimum of three years’ experience in the multifamily industry including manager level experience, ideally with luxury Class A properties. Strong verbal and written communication skills are required. Additionally, candidates must be organized and possess strong computer skills. Extensive knowledge of Yardi or Onesite (based on business need) and community budgeting with an exceptional background in financial reporting is also required.</p>
<p>We offer competitive pay and benefits, as well as opportunity for advancement. Visit our website at www.greystar.com . EOE</p>
<p>Job Description<br>The Community Manager manages the day-to-day operations of an assigned property including managing the team members, daily activities, and resources of the property to achieve established budgeted financial and operational goals, and ensures that the operation of the property complies with Company policies and procedures, Fair Housing, Americans with Disabilities Act, Fair Credit Reporting Act, and other laws and regulations governing multi-family housing operations.</p>
<p>Job Requirements</p>
<p>To supervise all phases of on-site operations:</p>
<ul>
<li>Handles emergency situations.</li>
<li>Develops and implements new strategies and programs designed to ensure long-term residency.</li>
<li>Oversees collection of revenues and handles delinquent accounts.</li>
<li>Makes regular inspections of apartment communities.</li>
<li>Handles evictions and landlord liens.</li>
<li>For all on-site personnel, is responsible for training and job performance.</li>
<li>Hires and terminates hourly personnel.</li>
<li>Determines transitions and promotions for on-site personnel.</li>
<li>Adheres to and implements all company policies.</li>
<li>Audits the lease files.</li>
<li>Implements scheduled rent increases.</li>
<li>Oversees payment of invoices.</li>
<li>Assists in budget preparation.</li>
<li>Monitors parking lot for unauthorized vehicles.</li>
<li>Motivates staff.</li>
<li>Informs residents of work progress or delays.</li>
<li>Other duties as assigned.</li>
</ul>
<p>Desired Skills, Experience, and Qualifications</p>
<ul>
<li>A high school diploma or equivalent and professional knowledge of business discipline are required.</li>
<li>3+ years’ experience in multifamily property management, including management level.</li>
<li>Experience with lease-up properties and luxury amenities desired.</li>
<li>Demonstrated ability to read, write, and communicate effectively to comprehend and complete legal documents, sell and explain apartment features, and answer questions about the property’s operation.</li>
<li>Demonstrated proficiency in Internet, word processing, spreadsheet, and database management programs in order to complete required reports and employment documents. Strong proficiency in using property management software (preferably Yardi and/or One Site).</li>
<li>Demonstrated mathematical skills necessary to add, subtract, multiply, and divide numbers, decimals, and fractions, and calculate percentages in order to complete financial records, budgets, and other fiscal reporting information.</li>
<li>Demonstrated management and supervisory skills sufficient to hire, lead, direct, evaluate, and manage subordinate and team members, including maintenance specialists.</li>
</ul>
<p>Education</p>
<p>High School diploma, GED or an employment history that demonstrates the application of property management, sales, marketing, and customer service background sufficient to manage the day-to-day operation of an apartment community, resolve customer complaints and issues, complete financial records, documents, and reports, increase sales revenues, and coordinate the word of a team. Bachelors degree in Property Management, Real Estate, Business, Hospitality, or related field preferred.</p>
