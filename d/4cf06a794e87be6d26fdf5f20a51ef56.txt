<h2>Financial Analyst</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>OBJECTIVE:</b></p>
<p>
We have an exciting opportunity for a highly motivated and experienced individual with strong analytical skills. This role is critical in partnering with not only finance and accounting but also several business leaders. The person will get the opportunity to work directly with the CEO and CFO on various projects and will be a critical part of this new role being developed at ZWILLING. This position is based in our Pleasantville, NY office.</p>
<p><b>
ABOUT US:</b></p>
<p>
For over 285 years, ZWILLING J.A. Henckels has offered premium products that enhance the lives of professional and home chefs worldwide. Since its start as an esteemed cutlery brand, the company has grown to include a coveted collection of cookware, flatware, glassware and kitchen gadgets. Tradition, quality, innovation, and craftsmanship are its ingredients for success.</p>
<p>
Today, ZWILLING J.A. Henckels is an international industry leader that sells products in 100 countries with the help of ten wholly owned subsidiaries. Always looking forward, ZWILLING has recently acquired global heritage brands —Staub (France), Demeyere (Belgium), Ballarini (Italy), and MIYABI (Japan)—to give its customers access to the most exceptional cookware and cutlery.</p>
<p><b>
PRIMARY JOB RESPONSIBILITIES:</b></p>
<p>
The Financial Analyst will work closely with Sales, Finance, Supply Chain, Marketing, Customer Support, and Shipping. The right candidate will possess a unique ability to build interpersonal relationships with department heads to drive operational and financial improvements; while simultaneously being able to analyze large amounts of data.</p>
<p>
The Financial Analyst will be responsible for:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Work with the Sales team to develop and manage a rolling 12-month sales forecast by product and customer.</li>
<li>
Participate in meetings with key customer account managers to understand programs and product mix.</li>
<li>
Analyze sales, cost of goods sold and expenses to help management make decisions.</li>
<li>
Manage and enter data into planning and budgeting software – Adaptive Planning and Cognos TM1.</li>
<li>
Responsible for accurately updating Cognos TM1 in conjunction with U.S. and German counterparts.</li>
<li>
Analyze data for accuracy, identify inconsistencies and initiate resolution.</li>
<li>
Analyze pricing proposals and quarterly follow-up on pricing decisions.</li>
<li>
Collect and report point-of-sale data for key customers to understand sell-through trends.</li>
<li>
Assist in six month rolling cash forecast and keep track of payables, FX contracts and debts schedules.</li>
<li>
Prepare what if analysis and should have some familiarity with modeling.</li>
<li>
Work on special projects for the Executive Management team as needed.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
QUALIFICATIONS:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Bachelor's degree, ideally in Finance, Economics, Statistics, or Mathematics with a minimum of 3-5 years' experience in planning and forecasting.</li>
<li>
Prior experience in a CPG company preferred.</li>
<li>
Advanced Microsoft Excel and PowerPoint skills including SUMIFS, Vlookups, Pivot tables, Data Queries &amp; Data Connections etc. It is critical that the Financial Analyst be able to rollup large product, customer data files dynamically either using Excel or Access.</li>
<li>
Knowledge of Adaptive Insights, QlikView, TM1 Cognos, MS Access is a plus.</li>
<li>
Experience in DC1 or IBS preferred (AS400 based ERP).</li>
<li>
Sound organizational skills with the ability to plan ahead.</li>
<li>
Excellent communication skills</li>
<li>
Highly pro-active, strong work ethic, ability to work under deadlines and to accept complete ownership of assigned tasks, attention to details and a team player.</li>
</ul>
