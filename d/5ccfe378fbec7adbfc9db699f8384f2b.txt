 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">

<p>The SpectrumVoIP Enterprise Account Executive is responsible for pursuing Cloud Hosted VoIP Sales within the Enterprise specific addressable market. Requires a minimum 6 years business to business outside sales experience with proven success. The ideal candidate will have proven track record selling into C-Level executives, closing sales, and exceeding monthly, quarterly, and yearly quotas. The ideal candidate possesses significant experience specifically in selling Cloud Hosted Voice and Unified Communications.</p>
<p>
Individuals must adopt the company’s structured sales process and sales policies. Includes a methodical mutual fund approach to setting appointments with large target prospects. Includes identifying and targeting prospects that will benefit greatest from SpectrumVoIP services having the highest probability of closing. Must possess solid cold calling skills and techniques, engaged networking skills &amp; experience, history of leveraging personal relationships and self-generated prospecting campaigns, all for the purpose of winning Enterprise focused new logo business. This position report directly to the Area Sales Manager.</p>
