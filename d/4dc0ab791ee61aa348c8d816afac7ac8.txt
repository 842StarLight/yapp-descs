<h2>Technical Business Development Lead</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p><b>Description</b></p>
<p>Burns &amp; McDonnell’s Business &amp; Technology Solution’s Global Practice is seeking an experienced Sales Executive to focus on North America clients.The Sales Executive will work with the Burns &amp; McDonnell Enterprise Systems Team offering a variety of services to our clients such as however not limited to:</p>
<ul>
<li>Cloud Solutions</li>
<li>IoT Service Offering</li>
<li>Technology Road Map Services</li>
<li>Middleware Integration Solutions</li>
<li>Data Governance Solutions</li>
<li>Software / Enterprise Assessments</li>
<li>Project Controls Shared Services</li>
<li>Packaged Application Services</li>
</ul>
<p>The selected candidate will join The Enterprise Systems Business Line, which is focused on providing our Clients a variety of Consulting Services, Offerings, Capabilities and Strategies delivering high value to our clients through a variety of leading technologies and practice disciplines.The Sales Executive shall possess strong entrepreneurial, leadership, collaboration, communication and negotiation skills that focus on building sustainable client relationships.</p>
<p>This position is responsible for sales opportunities while aligning the delivery responsibilities to sales agreements.This position is also responsible for leading sales efforts to identify and deliver a variety of solutions to our clients with a strong emphasis on teamwork, customer commitment, industry best practices, and continuous improvement.</p>
<p>Key responsibilities:</p>
<ul>
<li>Identifying, prospecting and developing new client accounts throughout North America.</li>
<li>Delivering sales presentations to prospects, negotiating contracts and closing new business opportunities.</li>
<li>Working closely with the pre-sales technical support team and performing assessments to determine customer requirements.</li>
<li>Ultimately, the sales executive is responsible for selling our solutions and ensuring the highest level of support to our customer base, while achieving revenue targets.</li>
<li>Coordinate closely with Marketing and Client Services Teams to drive and effective sales process that delivers results.Ensure compliance with company and site safety policies.</li>
<li>All other duties as assigned.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Qualifications</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Consulting Services and solution sales experience selling into strategic accounts. Strong business development, technical presentation skills required.</li>
<li>Consistent track record of meeting and exceeding quotas.</li>
<li>Candidates must possess strong business development skills, and the ability to manage complex enterprise sales cycles.</li>
<li>Candidates should be able to present technical concepts and business solutions clearly through demonstrations and proposals.</li>
<li>Candidates must have good organizational skills, prospecting and follow-up skills.</li>
<li>Candidates must be very technology literate with a good understanding of a variety of software, hardware, middleware and cloud technologies.</li>
<li>Candidate must also be knowledgeable in the latest technology trends and be able to provide a point of view on adding corresponding capabilities to the group’s offerings.</li>
<li>Candidates must also be able to show a strong track record of consistently over-achieving multimillion-dollar quotas in the vertical.</li>
<li>Required qualities are creative, self-motivated, problem solver, who can work with little direction in a start-up like environment.</li>
<li>Must be willing to travel and possess the ability to work remotely and independently.</li>
<li>This is a senior role with ability to develop and maintain relationships at the Directorand CXO level.</li>
<li>Ability to develop and maintain relationships with various LOBs within the organization.</li>
<li>Experience with operational systems in Electric Transmission/Distribution/Generation/Telecom (SCADA, ADMS), Municipal Government, Federal Government, Airport and Facilities industries is preferred.</li>
</ul>
