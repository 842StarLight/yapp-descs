<p></p>
<br>
<p>
Lawn &amp; Garden, a CRH company, a division within Oldcastle APG, is one of the largest manufacturers of bagged and bulk decorative mulch, soil, stone, and limestone products in the nation with over 40 production facilities. We take pride in serving a diverse group of customers including independent garden centers, mass retailers, landscapers, contractors, and homeowners.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Job Summary</b></p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
The coordinator is responsible for all aspects of ensuring quality in Growing Media and Mulch products at the plant-level, including, but not limited to: formulation oversight and testing; incoming raw material inspection, testing, and approval; ensuring that all established specifications and processes are followed. The coordinator is also responsible for the plant’s EH&amp;S program to include OSHA, Oldcastle, and EPA requirements.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Essential Duties and Responsibilities</b><br>
</p>
<ul>
<li>
Oversight and testing of product formulations</li>
<li>
Inspection, testing, and approval of incoming raw materials</li>
<li>
Follow all established specifications and processes</li>
<li>
Investigate and resolve customer quality complaints</li>
<li>
Administer and maintain EH&amp;S program</li>
<li>
Routinely inspect/observe plant equipment and activities for safe processes</li>
<li>
Prepare and submit all required reports to Oldcastle or applicable agency</li>
<li>
Keep the plant in compliance with EH&amp;S regulations</li>
<li>
Provide/produce and prepare samples for sales as needed</li>
<li>
Perform daily, weekly, monthly, quarterly, and annual inspections as required by Regulatory Agencies and company</li>
<li>
Other duties and responsibilities may be assigned as needed</li>
</ul>
