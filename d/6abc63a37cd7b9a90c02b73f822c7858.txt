<h2>Regional Sales Director/Quanum</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Regional Sales Director/Quanum - North Region<br>
</p>
<div>
<p>We Provide Solutions. Patients and Physicians rely on our diagnostic testing, information and services to help them make better healthcare decisions. These are often serious decisions with far reaching consequences, and require sensitivity, tact and a clear dedication to service. It’s about providing clarity and hope.</p>
<p>
In Sales you will work for the world leader in the industry, with a career where you can expand your skills and knowledge. You’ll have a role where you can act with professionalism, you can inspire colleagues, and you can care about the work we do and the people we serve.
</p>
<p>At Quest, our Sales Teams are often the public face of our organization. As a result we make every effort to support and develop their skills. Working across a strong customer base, you’ll find you have the flexibility and autonomy to structure your days while having the confidence that comes from promoting a well-recognized and trusted brand. With lots of opportunities for repeat business and referrals, we also offer outstanding support, plus great pay and benefits.</p>
</div>
<div>
Quest Diagnostics is seeking an experienced, highly-motivated Regional Sales Director to drive new sales of the Quanum Solutions suite including EHR, Practice Management, RCM, and Data Exchange. Collaboration with our Commercial counterparts to identify key opportunities in conjunction with a market plan that combines regional marketing initiatives and analysis of current customer demographics will be key drivers of success.</div>
<div>
</div>
<div>
Duties and Responsibilities:</div>
<ul>
<li>
Meet/exceed the assigned quota within the designated region</li>
<li>
Target and close new opportunities through leads, referrals, campaigns and prospecting</li>
<li>
Develop and maintain detailed regional market plan leveraging available internal resources and external partnerships</li>
<li>
Identify new business opportunities by partnering with Commercial colleagues (Physician Sales, Key Account Directors, Health Plan executives) business development and other regional resources</li>
<li>
Facilitate and lead sales proposals for new business and serve as key contact to manage all dialog with Prospect during the negotiation and contracting phase using standard processes, procedures and templates</li>
<li>
Utilize Salesforce.com to manage and document sales activity including pre-call planning, post-call analysis, consistent follow-up and regular call cycles</li>
<li>
Participate with local leadership and in regional market strategy meetings in developing and executing regional market initiatives</li>
<li>
Work with Marketing to develop proactive approaches to competitors’ new products and services</li>
<li>
Advance industry and product knowledge and provide ongoing feedback to continually improve Company’s offering and market position</li>
<li>
Manage time and resources effectively to meet territory sales objectives with required margin thresholds</li>
<li>
Timely reporting (activity and forecasting) in compliance with Company policies</li>
<li>
This role will require at least 50% travel, and is flexible in terms of physical location</li>
</ul>
