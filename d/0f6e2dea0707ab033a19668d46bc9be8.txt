<h2>Aircraft Modification Quality Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>This position will report to the program quality team managing the Greek F16 Mod Program. This position provides administrative and technical leadership for the in-country team of quality professionals including analysts, inspection, supplier quality and quality engineering supporting program component manufacturing, aircraft handling, aircraft induction, modification, and delivery processes. Assigned staff will include a mix of LM Aero Fort Worth or Field Team Operations (FTO) resources that will interact daily with Hellenic Aerospace Industries (HAI), the Greek Air Force and DCMA. Specific technical and quality support will include establishing and/or validating and testing the HAI site quality management system; ensuring compliance with all related standards including AS9110, Program QMP, DCMAI 8210 and other requirements as identified by the contract or country specific standards. Additionally, this role will work with HAI QA personnel for comprehension of LM specs and processes; provide oversight and verify incorporation of TCTOs and/or completion of other work performed. Role will oversee quality acceptance functions and surveillance for work completed by HAI including inspection point planning/placement. Leader will provide training on the various systems supporting the quality system including SQARs, surveys, work flow systems, AR reporting, etc. Leader will participate in Sustainment site planning meetings, coordinate with Fort Worth Q&amp;MS personnel on quality issues and information flow and perform other related duties and assignments as required by the program. Extensive customer interface required. Role is accountable for budget performance of the in-country quality team and is responsible for recruitment, hiring, and performance assessment, skills assessment, skills gap closure and recognition/disciplinary actions. Leader is expected to identify areas of opportunity for improvement across the site with the various stakeholders and drive projects to improve efficiency.<br>
Ability to convert back to individual contributor after the leadership assignment in-country is over.<br>
