<h2>Business Development Associate Intern</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Time Commitment: 10-20 hours/week (part time)
</b></h3>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Starting Wage: Commensurate with experience
</b></h3>
<p><b>General Characteristics</b><br>
The ideal candidate is a person committed to fulfilling the mission of Beacon Organizational Consulting. The position reports directly to the Operations Director. There are many opportunities for advancement and growth within the company in this position, both in hours and pay.</p>
<p><b>
Personal Qualities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Reliable, punctual, driven, ambitious, and professional; strict confidentiality</li>
<li>Proficient in Microsoft Office Suites (Word, Access, Power Point, Excel)</li>
<li>Some experience with accounting software, esp. QuickBooks</li>
<li>Excellent grammar and editing skills necessary; excellent written and verbal communication</li>
<li>Resourceful and comfortable with conducting extensive research; high attention to detail</li>
<li>Self-motivated and able to anticipate needs, discern work priorities, and meet deadlines.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>General Duties</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Providing administrative support and project management support to Beacon’s executive team</li>
<li>Receiving calls and/or greeting visitors</li>
<li>Drafting templated Organization Assessments, Standard Funding Proposals, and other client documents and deliverables</li>
<li>Researching Requests for Proposals and other grant opportunities for clients</li>
<li>Maintaining calendars, schedules, and coordinating appointments</li>
<li>Drafting and editing grants, publications, correspondence, and reports</li>
<li>Maintaining office equipment and ordering office supplies</li>
<li>Assisting in marketing efforts in accordance with Beacon’s Marketing Plan</li>
<li>Maintaining social media pages and website</li>
<li>Working within and updating Beacon’s strategic plan</li>
<li>Producing ads, flyers, mailings brochures, budgets, templates, and other documents</li>
<li>Creating, organizing, and updating binders and files for clients</li>
<li>Generating invoices for clients</li>
<li>Assisting with basic accounting tasks</li>
<li>Flexibility with other tasks and duties as they arise.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Qualifications</b><br>
Degree in progress for business administration, marketing, nonprofit, finance &amp; accounting, communications, human resources, development, leadership, social change, social services, or other relevant field of study.</p>
<p></p>
<p>Please email your cover letter, resume, and 3 references to info@BeaconOrgConsulting.com.<br>
Thank you for your interest!</p>
