<b>Position Responsibilities</b><ul>
<li>
Reporting to our President, you will lead and direct the organization’s overall information technology function. This includes tactical planning, development, evaluation and coordination of the information and technology systems.
</li>
<li>Act as a business partner in advising top management of the latest technology, recommend appropriate action and serve as the technical advisor.</li>
<li>
Ensure that IT aligns with the growth strategy of the business.</li>
<li>
Provide hands-on help to quickly resolve technology issues impacting customers and business users.
</li>
<li>Collaborate with the Executive Team to prioritize technology resources to support business functions and priorities.</li>
<li>
With an outstanding ability to lead, they will inspire and motivate others to action with demonstrated experience in talent development and succession.
</li>
</ul>
<br>
<b>Minimum Requirements</b><ul>
<li>
Master’s degree in information technology or business-related field.</li>
<li>
15+ years’ experience, 5+ in a Senior Executive or CIO position.</li>
<li>
Extensive knowledge of IT systems and technology, application development, Java and WebSphere, Oracle and data warehousing is <b>a plus.</b>
</li>
</ul>
