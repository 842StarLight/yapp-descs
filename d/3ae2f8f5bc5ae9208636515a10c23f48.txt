<h2>Social Media Marketing Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>We are looking for a full-time Social Media Marketing Specialist to help promote our brand on social media and create new content that helps us connect with current and future customers. You’ll work primarily with our CEO to create and help execute our social media and marketing strategy. You will provide fresh and unique on-brand content of our work and promote engagement through social media channels (LinkedIn, Twitter, Facebook, Instagram, email, etc).<br>*<br>Our office is located in West Berkeley, close to 4th Street and the Ashby BART shuttle.*<br>*</p>
<p>*<br>*</p>
<p>WHO ARE WE?</p>
<p>Industrial Logic helped pioneer the Agile movement starting in the late 1990s and today we are a Modern Agile consultancy that helps unlock agility for people and organizations across diverse industries. Our global group of player coaches, trainers and practitioners help you streamline work, manage risk, increase joy and deliver happiness.</p>
<p>WHO ARE YOU?</p>
<p>You are a hard working individual ready to showcase your creativity. You are comfortable using a camera and video equipment and know how to use video editing software like Camtasia, Adobe Premiere Pro, Final Cut Pro and Headliner. It’s also a plus if you have a background in digital design. You also have the ability to:</p>
<ul><li>Create engaging content daily</li></ul>
<ul><li>Learn new technologies and platforms quickly</li></ul>
<ul><li>Think "outside the box"</li></ul>
<ul><li>Act with an entrepreneur’s tenacity</li></ul>
<ul><li>Work independently to execute on projects and stick to deadlines</li></ul>
<ul><li>Regularly monitor Google Analytics, Social Media Management &amp; Social Analytics Platforms for insights to campaign performance, website performance, and other leading indicators.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Expand and help manage our influencer and social community outreach.</li></ul>
<p>WHY YOU?</p>
<p>You’re creative, you love thinking about new and fun ways to make content, you’re naturally keeping a pulse on social media content trends, and you’re a talented designer and creator with lots of enthusiasm and great communication skills. You have an understanding of the tools and resources necessary to make great social content. You value and understand the importance of speed, creating content for specific social platforms, and value understanding the performance of your creative once it’s distributed.</p>
<p>The Ideal Candidate Qualifications:</p>
<ul><li>Must know Photoshop and Adobe Creative Suite</li></ul>
<ul><li>1–3 years marketing, content, and/or social media experience, with a solid understanding of engaging an audience and growing the user base of a product or brand.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Understand photography, typography, design, and best practices in creating for social media platforms.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Strong ability to identify and write clear, engaging, and cohesive messaging with a sharp eye for high-quality, impactful content and imagery.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Understand how to create a good meme.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Be able to execute designs and projects quickly across multiple accounts, both personal and business.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Proactively be able to manage time, be self-sufficient, solution oriented and a self-starter.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Have good interpersonal and communication skills</li></ul>
<ul><li>Strong organizational skills</li></ul>
<ul><li>Demonstrated accuracy and productivity in a changing environment with interruptions.</li></ul>
<p>Bonus Skills:</p>
