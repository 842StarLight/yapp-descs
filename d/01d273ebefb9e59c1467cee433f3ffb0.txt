<div>Job Introduction – Customer Engineer<br>
Computacenter is Europe’s leading independent provider of IT infrastructure services. We enable users and their businesses by providing great service, keeping our promises and taking responsibility.<br>
Role Responsibility<br>
Main Responsibilities:<br>
<ul>
<li>Perform a range of technical work activities either remotely or at customer site to meet business and customer requirements.</li>
<li>Coordinate small teams delivering basic work packages in line with company process to meet business and customer requirements.</li>
<li>Document and reports on work completed to ensure compliance with Company and Customer Procedures.</li>
<li>Escalate issues in line with company processes to ensure customer demands are met and evaluate escalations and action appropriately to ensure customer demands are met.</li>
<li>Provide customer service to internal and external customers to ensure consistent experience.</li>
<li>Ability to adapt quickly to dynamic team environments to maintain consistent effective contribution.</li>
</ul>
The Ideal Candidate<br>
<ul>
<li>Able to follow instructions and procedures</li>
<li>Proven experience in IT Desktop Service environment.</li>
<li>Uses information systems, technology functions and applications in line with IT industry standards as appropriate to the role</li>
<li>Demonstrates an organized approach to work.</li>
<li>Demonstrates customer service abilities</li>
<li>Routine administrative skills</li>
<li>Good interpersonal skills, basic literacy and numeracy skills</li>
<li>Demonstrates awareness of health and safety at work.</li>
</ul>
About the Company<br>
About the Company<br>
We advise customers on their IT strategy; implement the most appropriate technology from a wide range of leading vendors and manage their technology infrastructures on their behalf. At every stage we make our customers’ businesses sharper by removing cost, complexity and barriers to change across their IT infrastructures.<br>
Key facts about Computacenter<br>
<ul>
<li>Computacenter is Europe’s leading independent provider of IT infrastructure services &amp; solutions</li>
<li>Our focus is on corporate and government organizations, typically of from 500 to 10,000 employees, and large enterprises of 10,000 or more employees.</li>
<li>Over 12,000 staff employed across the Group.</li>
<li>Over 70% of Computacenter’s Group revenue comes from services, software, and sales of enterprise products such as servers and connectivity/networking technology</li>
</ul>
