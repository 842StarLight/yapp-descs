<h2>Tax Analyst</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p>This position is responsible for the preparation of assigned tasks for Federal and state tax compliance, provision and special projects.
</p>
<p><b>Key Responsibilities:<br>
</b><br>
<br>
</p>
<p>Tax compliance – 40%
</p>
<ul><li>Prepare book-tax difference calculations as assigned
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Complete Federal pro-forma returns in OneSource Income tax software
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Prepare state tax workpapers for annual returns, including state modifications and apportionment calculations
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Complete state tax returns in OneSource Income tax software
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Prepare quarterly state tax estimates
</li></ul>
<p>Tax provision – 30%
</p>
<ul><li>Assist with quarterly and annual income tax provision, including:
</li></ul>
<p>o Preparation of FIN48 analysis
</p>
<p>o Preparation of quarterly state apportionment
</p>
<p>o Preparation of book-tax difference calculations, both permanent and deferred
</p>
<p>o Tie-out of deferred tax asset/liability balances
</p>
<ul><li>Prepare and record tax related journal entries
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Assist with management testing of internal control procedures
</li></ul>
<p>Non-income state tax returns - 10%
</p>
<ul><li>Prepare miscellaneous non-income tax returns, including gross receipts, sales and use and personal property returns
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Research and document non-income tax issues
</li></ul>
<p>Account reconciliations – 10%
</p>
<ul><li>Analyze and reconcile income tax accounts on a monthly basis
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Assist with resolution of any unreconciled differences
</li></ul>
<p>Complete tax research and other special projects as assigned – 10%<br>
</p>
<b>
Required Qualifications:</b><p></p>
<p>
<b>Education:</b></p>
<ul><li>
<b>
 </b>Bachelor’s Degree – Accounting or related field</li></ul>
<p><b>Experience:
</b></p>
<ul><li>1-2 years of tax experience at a Big 4 or equivalent corporate experience, including experience in tax provision (ASC 740) and tax compliance
</li></ul>
<p><b>Knowledge, Skills, and Abilities:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Strong federal and state compliance and knowledge of tax accounting (ASC 740)
</li>
<li>Experience with Thomson OneSource tax software, including OTP. OIT and OSA
</li>
<li>Strong computer skills with MS office software, especially Microsoft Excel, and tax research software (BNA)
</li>
<li>Ability to effectively prioritize and multi-task while working under tight deadlines
</li>
<li>Excellent written and oral communication skills and interpersonal skills
</li>
<li>Experience with financial institutions
</li>
<li>PeopleSoft (or another general ledger accounting system) experience<br>
</li>
</ul>
