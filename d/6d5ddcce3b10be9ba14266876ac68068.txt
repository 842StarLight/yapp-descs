<div>
Ensure that U.S. Bank delivers best-in-class digital experiences to its customers, maximizes resources, and contributes to the business results and strategic objectives of the organization. Responsible for the full life cycle management of current and future digital channels within the iOS ecosystem (e.g. mobile apps, wearables, Siri etc.). Partners with segment (e.g. consumer, small business etc.) and product leaders (e.g. deposits, mortgage, card, consumer lending) to create digital experiences that meet core business objectives. Aggressively grow core customer iPhone mobile app adoption, increase engagement to multiple times a day, close self-service feature gaps (DIY), and increase mobile sales penetration. This role will perform market research, benchmarking, evaluate technology trends, form strategic business partnerships, translate digital product vision into a roadmap, and execute roadmap to deliver best in class digital experiences. This role will also build the partnership between U.S. Bank and Apple to ensure that design and development takes full advantage of Apple operating systems (iOS, WatchOS etc.).<br>
