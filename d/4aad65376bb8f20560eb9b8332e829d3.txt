<p><b>How you will contribute
</b></p>
<p>Valmont is currently seeking a highly motivated and talented individual for the Intermediate Accounting position in the Global Utility Division. We are looking for a highly driven individual who is accountable for analysis and reporting of Divisional (Bartow site) Utility financial data including sales, inventory valuation, income statements and operational variances reporting to the Regional Controller.
</p>
<p></p>
<ul><li>Maintain Project Tracking Log, detailing and updating customer information based on orders received
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Input customer sales orders into Sage Accounting system
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Create customer invoices in Sage Accounting system on a daily basis based on jobs shipped
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Assist Site Controller with forecasting and budgeting
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Audit financial and operational data for accuracy and completeness
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Report site operational results
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Develop and implement techniques or procedures to provide enhanced data analysis
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Coordinate monthly closing activities with other members of site staff to ensure accurate and timely information
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Research, prepare and reconcile sales and production reports to ensure timely and accurate reporting
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Audit inventory transactions
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Reconcile various General Ledger accounts, including all Balance Sheet accounts
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Assist Internal/External auditors in their annual site audit
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Reconcile Sales backlog and prepare product line reporting
