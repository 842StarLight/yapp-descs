<h2>Regional Marketing Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<b>Job Description</b><br>
<p>The Regional Marketing Specialist is an individual contributor responsible for the planning and execution of regional marketing activities that support the objectives and goals developed in strategic marketing plans.</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Research, plan and execute regional marketing initiatives to support the mission of creating direct contact with the right prospect for our sales consultants. Tactics include mainly local events supported by email.</li>
<li>
Provide marketing and pipeline acceleration programs as well as sales support for assigned regions.</li>
<li>
Manage and monitor leads in our CRM (Salesforce.com) and work with assigned regional sales consultants to keep data clean, accurate and up-to-date.</li>
<li>
Provide timely and accurate communication to key stakeholders, regarding marketing plans and actual tactical marketing activity in assigned sales regions.</li>
<li>
Responsible for optimizing marketing activity for assigned regions based on results and unique market conditions.</li>
<li>
Build strong relationships with field channel partners, especially regional management.</li>
<li>
Build positive relationships with external regional partners and vendors.</li>
<li>
Manage deliverables to project plans and adhere to costs against budget guidelines.</li>
<li>
Drive results in a team environment, leveraging effective partnerships with cross functional teams.</li>
<li>
Provide fact base and insight to guide ongoing regional marketing program development. Generate support and enthusiasm for marketing programs across internal departments and management.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Experience</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
1-3 years of experience in professional marketing</li>
<li>
1+ years of experience providing sales support</li>
<li>
1+ years of marketing project management</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Knowledge/Skills</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Excellent written and verbal communication skills</li>
<li>
Proven attention to detail, problem solving, planning, and customer focus</li>
<li>
Proficient in Microsoft Excel</li>
<li>
Salesforce.com experience preferred</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Education</b></p>
<ul><li>
Bachelor's degree in Marketing, Advertising, Communications, or related field</li></ul>
