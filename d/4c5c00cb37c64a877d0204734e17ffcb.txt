<p><b>
Role Purpose:</b></p>
<ul><li>
The Customer Service Representative will be making phone calls to a list of Healthcare practices regarding specific pharmaceutical products. A scripted dialogue will be provided. Upon completion of the scripted discussion the Customer Service Representative will offer the practice informative brochures or savings cards in many cases. (client dependent) Outbound phone calls will be upwards of 80+ daily and the representative will be responsible for logging all activity in a database.</li></ul>
<p><b>
Principal Accountabilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Produce a high volume of outbound calls to Healthcare practices , delivering a scripted message</li>
<li>
Detailed tracking of all call activity/results</li>
<li>
Works under moderate supervision and typically reports to a Customer Service Supervisor.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Minimum Education &amp; Experience:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
We require candidates to have a minimum Customer Service background of 2 + years.</li>
<li>
Telemarketing experience preferred.</li>
<li>
Bi-lingual (English/Spanish) preferred.</li>
</ul>
