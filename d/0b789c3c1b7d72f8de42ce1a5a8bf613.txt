<h2>F&I Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">We are seeking a F&amp;I Manager to join our winning team.
<br><br>
If you are experienced in giving exceptional customer service, have the drive to earn unlimited income, and want to build a career as an Automotive Finance &amp; Insurance Manager working with exciting new products, then we look forward to talking with you.
<br><br>
SUMMARY
<br><br>
Automotive F&amp;I Managers help our customers arrange the financing of their purchases and also present them with additional products that enhance their vehicle and ownership experience
<br><br>
<b>Responsibilities:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Structures deals for maximum profitability and collectability</li>
<li>Fully proficient with title laws and registration process</li>
<li>Maintains proficiency and certifications as required for the position</li>
<li>Accurately desks deals, submits deals to lenders for approval, makes credit decisions, and effectively closes deals</li>
<li>Ensures all deals are fully compliant with local, state, and federal guidelines</li>
<li>Prepares paperwork, contracts, and delivers deals</li>
<li>Prepares and mails all deal packages with complete and proper documentation to lenders and follows any other process that may be in place in their respective department</li>
<li>Accurately audits team deals post-sale</li>
<li>Ensures the expeditious funding of all contracts</li>
</ul>
<b>Qualifications:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>College degree preferred or equivalent experience</li>
<li>Three years automotive or finance sales experience</li>
<li>Knowledge of dealership finance and insurance procedures</li>
<li>Professional personal appearance and excellent communication skills</li>
<li>Basic Microsoft Office knowledge; computer software and internet proficiency</li>
<li>Excellent verbal and written communication, strong negotiation and presentation skills</li>
<li>Must possess the ability to close a deal</li>
<li>Valid driver's license</li>
<li>At least one year of automotive finance required</li>
</ul>
