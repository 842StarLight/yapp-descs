<p>WORKING TITLE: Lecturer - Marketing
</p>
<p>UW SYSTEM OFFICIAL TITLE: Lecturer</p>
<p>
POSTING DATE: October 10, 2019</p>
<p>
ESSENTIAL JOB FUNCTIONS:</p>
<p>
The Austin E. Cofrin School of Business at the University of Wisconsin-Green Bay seeks applicants for two lecturer position in Marketing specializing in Selling and Sales Management and/or Advertising. Applicants will be considered for both positions.</p>
<p>
This position includes the following responsibilities:</p>
<ul>
<li><p>Teach a normal teaching load of primarily undergraduate courses in Marketing, with an emphasis in Selling and Sales Management and/or Advertising.</p></li>
<li><p>Courses will be taught both face-to-face and online. The specific courses taught may vary from semester to semester, and successful candidates may be required to teach occasional courses at the Marinette, Sheboygan, and Manitowoc Campuses.</p></li>
<li><p>Advise students.</p></li>
<li><p>Perform service and professional engagement activities consistent with the Austin E. Cofrin School of Business expectations for a Lecturer holding Faculty status.</p></li>
<li><p>Contribute to the ongoing programmatic development of the Austin E. Cofrin School of Business and participate fully in AACSB accreditation efforts.</p></li>
</ul>
<p>
In addition, the successful candidate will be expected to display a demonstrated potential for excellence in teaching and a commitment to undergraduate education. Additionally, the candidate should possess communication and interpersonal skills sufficient to work effectively with a diverse array of students and colleagues.</p>
