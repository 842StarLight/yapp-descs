<p><b>Customer Technical Support Manager</b><br>
<b>
</b></p>
<p><b>POSITION SUMMARY</b></p>
<br>
<p><b>
</b><br>
The Technical Customer Support Manager will manage a team of multi-level tiered technical support staff who are responsible for responding to and resolving technical client facing issues, identifying &amp; driving systemic improvements, developing support tools and processes. Management of the team will include measuring the performance of support staff against established benchmarks, ensuring all client interactions are handled in an efficient and quality focused manner, and providing coaching and feedback to improve team performance. The Technical Customer Support Manager will build relationships with the leaders of all cross functional teams, including Product Management, Engineering, QA, Customer Service and 3rd Party providers. In addition, there will be a requirement to in the development of strategic initiatives and will be accountable for the tactical implementation of those initiatives within their teams.</p>
<p><b>
RESPONSIBILITIES</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Drive a self-managing and collaborative team</li>
<li>Set clear goals, delegate tasks, set deadlines, and oversee day-to-day team operations</li>
<li>Monitor team performance and report metrics</li>
<li>Provide mentorship and guidance to foster individual and team growth</li>
<li>Maintain understanding of current incident states and solutions</li>
<li>Monitor, oversee and regulate open customer cases throughout the issue life-cycle to ensure compliance with documented service level agreements.</li>
<li>Identify, highlight, and document best practices through continuous assessment, and recommending process and product recommendations to the leadership team.</li>
<li>Conduct performance reviews for the team, identifying development opportunities and offering coaching and mentoring in areas that require improvement</li>
<li>Provide guidance and serve as an escalation point to the team and serve as liaison with other organizations</li>
<li>Function independently and provide recommend changes to day-to-day operations, standards, policies, and procedures to maintain support capabilities and team morale.</li>
<li>Proactively identify opportunities to improve customer experience</li>
<li>Understand and participate in developing financial targets, budgeting, product, and business planning.</li>
<li>Work closely with other departments (e.g. Product development, sales, professional services) to provide feedback, develop processes, and ensure a great customer experience.</li>
<li>Manage the performance of team against all established tech support benchmarks and metrics</li>
<li>Conduct regularly scheduled team and one-on-one meetings with direct reports to address employee issues, provide updates regarding performance, and give direction regarding company and department policies and procedures</li>
<li>Ability to learn proprietary software in effort to provide technical guidance</li>
<li>Develop and implement tactical plans for achieving strategic initiatives</li>
<li>Proven experience in incident management</li>
<li>Performs other duties and responsibilities as assigned</li>
</ul>

<p><b>
EDUCATION:</b></p>
<ul>

</ul>
<p><b>EXPERIENCE:</b></p>

 <li>Minimum of 5 years’ experience in a high technical support role; minimum of 2 years’ experience managing technical teams/engineering level</li>

<li>Proficient in Microsoft Office Suite</li>
<li>Experience presenting to high-level managers, groups, and one-on-one</li>
