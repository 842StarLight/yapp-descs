<p><b>
General Purpose and Scope of Position</b><b>:</b></p>
<p>
The Planner, as a part of the Supplier Relations and Procurement team, is responsible for overseeing the planning and inventory management functions necessary to optimize business operations for a diverse group of agricultural processing, distribution, and sales outlets.</p>
<p><b>
Key Skills and Abilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>BS in Business, statistics or related discipline, or</li>
<li>5 + years of experience in business process management, planning and/or operations</li>
<li>Superior project management skills</li>
<li>Outstanding communication skills</li>
<li>Strong background in demand planning</li>
<li>Strong market analysis, statistical and quantitative skills</li>
<li>Working knowledge of seasonal retail operations</li>
<li>Know basic product type and crop use (herbicide, fungicide, traits, N, P, K)</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Key Personal Attributes:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Ability to articulate and drive company strategy</li>
<li>Ability to develop and implement efficient procedures and processes</li>
<li>Strong team orientation</li>
<li>Ability to influence with formal and informal authority</li>
<li>Willingness and ability to be a constructive agent of change</li>
<li>Ability to execute across diverse businesses</li>
<li>Ability to plan and organize</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Key Responsibilities and Deliverables:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Lead and drive the sales forecasting process with sales, marketing and strategy</li>
<li>Adjust and refine forecasting software parameters to optimize planning results</li>
<li>Understand and utilize company, supply, and market strategies when developing and executing product supply plans</li>
<li>Coordinate, document and communicate the Purchase Plan (quantity, timing, location); monitor and adjust</li>
<li>Re-balance inventories as needed to meet customer and market needs</li>
<li>Support the correctness of product and organization hierarchy</li>
<li>Collaborate with key stakeholders to efficiently utilize inventory storage capacity</li>
<li>Collaborate with product Strategy Leads to understand, support &amp; maintain Supplier relations and alignment</li>
<li>Communicate/Coordinate product supply allocations in collaboration with Strategy, Sales and Operations Leads</li>
<li>Support rebate collection and allocation to branches</li>
<li>Conduct analysis of data in support of pre-buys and early order programs</li>
<li>Manage participation of supplier return and swap programs</li>
<li>Educate, train and support areas and branches on supply chain processes and systems</li>
</ul>
