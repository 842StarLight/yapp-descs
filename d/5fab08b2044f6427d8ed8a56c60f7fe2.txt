<p><b>Sales Enablement Manager</b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
The Sales Enablement Manager will be responsible for facilitating the creation and support of sales enablement programs and training focused on enhancing sales results across the Workfront sales organization. A large focus of this role will involve supporting the on-demand strategy of sales training in conjunction with the goals of the sales teams.</p>
<p>
The ideal person in this role will have direct experience working in field sales or alongside sales teams in a sales enablement role. This person will have developed on-demand trainings and LMS-style strategies to increase adoption and influence sales behaviors. This person should be an excellent presenter capable of developing and delivering both in-person and on-demand sales presentations. We’re looking for a team-player who brings a "whatever it takes" attitude to succeed and further the Workfront sales department goals.</p>
<p>
This role will reside in the Sales Strategy and Enablement team and will work directly with peers on the enablement team, sales leaders, and subject matter experts to strategize the design and management of on-demand learning solutions for sales and partner organizations. This position will report to the Director of Sales Enablement.</p>
<p><b>
Key Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Use in-person instruction, virtual classroom, on-demand training, and sales leader support to educate and train reps on sales techniques, sales process, product and business acumen, negotiation skills, account management, and more.</li>
<li>Manage the day-to-day administration of the Learning Management System; create trainings from scratch as well as maintain an updated library of relevant content</li>
<li>Maintain a framework for the course library in the LMS that can be deployed globally</li>
<li>Develop and maintain effective reporting systems to keep management engaged and accountable for sales training</li>
<li>Effectively design content and tools to increase our employees’ level of knowledge and confidence in serving our customers and the business</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p>
</p>
<p><b>Skills and Capabilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>2-5 years experience in direct sales or sales-supporting role</li>
<li>BA or BS Degree</li>
<li>Experience with developing on-demand strategies for increased learning</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Must have experience working with sales and SME’s in a SaaS company</li>
<li>Must offer creativity for the deployment of training initiatives linked to business outcomes</li>
<li>Experience in navigating and maintaining organization, development, and deployment of LMS-style training modules</li>
<li>Experience with design software in an LMS content production area a plus</li>
<li>Strong presentation and communication skills; ideally experience collaborating with sales managers and direct sales teams</li>
<li>Self-motivated team player able to prioritize and manage projects in a fast-paced environment</li>
<li>Strong organizational skills; ability to manage multiple cross-functional projects simultaneously</li>
<li>Willing to live the Workfront company values of: finish strong, do great work, obsess over customers, and win together</li>
<li>Pro-active problem-solver who anticipates and addresses problems before they arise and works cross-departmentally to deliver holistic enablement content.</li>
<li>Self-learner who can learn to use new technologies with minimal guidance as the role’s tech-stack shifts to meet evolving needs</li>
</ul>
