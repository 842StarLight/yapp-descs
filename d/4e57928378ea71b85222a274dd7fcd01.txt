<h2>Office Coordinator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p><b>Description</b><br>
Quantum Spatial is seeking an Office Coordinator in our Anchorage, Alaska location responsible for operations and facility management support. The most qualified applicant will be an independent problem solver who possesses strong organization and communication skills with a forward-thinking mentality.<br>
</p>
<p><b>Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Provide standard and routine clerical and administrative support including:</li>
<ul>
<li>Word processing and data entry</li>
<li>Supply inventory control and replenishment</li>
<li>Receiving, sorting, processing of mail/deliveries,</li>
<li>Answering general inquiry phone calls and greeting visitors</li>
<li>Dandling daily requests from staff</li>
</ul>
<li>Track and report office expense budgets and operating costs</li>
<li>Support talent recruitment through scheduling interviews, organizing applications and participating in reviewing resumes</li>
<li>Primary contact for the facilities manager and vendors</li>
<li>Maintain shared calendars</li>
<li>Coordination and preparation of events such as: staff meetings, on-site client meetings, guest speakers, employee events, etc.</li>
<li>Prepare, arrange, coordinate and track business development related events and projects</li>
<li>Technology setup and general troubleshooting</li>
<li>Support employee appreciation and professional development efforts</li>
<li>Regular communication with other QSI offices’ administration staff</li>
<li>Ensure day to day operational business needs are met</li>
<li>Support the historical imagery sales team throughout all phases of order fulfillment</li>
<li>All other reasonable duties as assigned<br>
</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Minimum Qualifications</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Proficiency with Microsoft Office suite, Google suite and office equipment and systems</li>
<li>Familiarity with workforce management software (e.g. Deltek, Salesforce, ADP, etc.)</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Preferred Qualifications
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Prior experience in a customer service related role</li>
<li>Experience with project tracking and management</li>
<li>Experience in hazardous materials shipping</li>
</ul>
