<h2>Enterprise Sales Executive</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p>Would you like to be part of a team that is transforming the healthcare industry by enabling providers to engage their patient-consumers and improve patient health outcomes?</p>
<p>
 InteliChart is looking for an Enterprise Engagement Sales Executive that will report to the VP of Sales and is responsible for the sales of InteliChart’s Healthy OUTCOMES and InteliChart’s patient engagement solutions. This position is a senior sales position expected to drive company growth and revenue. The expectation is to sell Healthy OUTCOMES and Engage in health systems and large group practice markets.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
 The Ideal Candidate</p>
<ul>
<li>The candidate will understand the market needs of patient engagement, population management, and clinical systems market.</li>
<li>Is well-versed and experienced in managing and closing complex sales processes within an enterprise sales environment.</li>
<li>Demonstrates strong organizational skills, ability to work effectively and efficiently with minimal direction.</li>
<li>Thrives in a self-motivated sales environment – is personally driven to execute and attain the organization’s business and financial goals.<br>
Must be a creative, strategic, and tactical thinker – establish and execute sales plans.</li>
<li>Works successfully as part of a team committed to the business and organizational success.</li>
<li>You are expected to operate with integrity and respect.</li>
</ul>
<p>Responsibilities</p>
<ul>
<li>Meeting assigned sales plan and business objectives.</li>
<li>Prospecting and creating an effective sales pipeline to meet and exceed annual sales goals.</li>
<li>Front line leadership that leads to continuous updating of product messaging and positioning.</li>
<li>Close a minimum of two new accounts within the first six months.</li>
<li>Grow professionally on product knowledge and industry trends.</li>
<li>Effectively and professionally present and demonstrate the InteliChart suite of products. Requires knowledge of products and understanding of market value.</li>
<li>Conduct regular business review of activity, trends, challenges with VP Sales.</li>
<li>Effectively manage a sales expense budget.</li>
</ul>
<p>Additional Comments</p>
<p>
Educational Requirements</p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree in business, sales, marketing or equivalent training in business, sales management, or healthcare payer practices.</li>
</ul>
<p>Experience</p>
<ul>
<li>The ideal candidate has seven or more years of selling Health IT solutions with experience selling into the healthcare systems markets. Desire experience with early-stage organizations.</li>
<li>Must have a proven track record of success in Health IT sales over a sustained period.</li>
<li>Understanding of the patient engagement, population health, and value-based care market direction and expectations.</li>
<li>Experience with Salesforce and/or other CRM programs</li>
</ul>
<p>Travel Requirements</p>
<ul>
<li>Geographic territory will require overnight travel as needed. May require as much as 60% travel.</li>
</ul>
<p>Compensation Plan</p>
<ul>
<li>Competitive Salary</li>
<li>Variable compensation plan paid monthly – commissions and bonus incentives</li>
<li>Competitive benefits package – health, dental, retirement</li>
</ul>
<p>Remote position, the candidate may be located nationwide</p>
