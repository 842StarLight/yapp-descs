<h2>Vice President, Retail Stores</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">Our Story
<br><br>
We started MedMen with a simple vision; cannabis as a consumer product. It is a simple idea with profound consequences for how marijuana is cultivated, produced and marketed. Quality standards matter, best practices matter, brand reputation matters. Today, MedMen is the most dominant cannabis enterprise in the emerging legal marijuana industry.
<br><br>
Job Summary
<br><br>
The VP, Retail Stores will contribute to MedMen's success by creating the direction and leadership for MedMen's retail strategy and business planning. This is a key role for the and is an integral part of MedMen's long-term growth strategy. In this critical role, the qualified individual will be responsible for leading all aspects of MedMen's retail store expansion strategy and will focus on delivering consistency in the customer experience as well as an integrated omni-channel customer experience. This role will require strong partnership with key leaders across the company in the areas of operations, hospitality, marketing, visual merchandising, supply chain operations, and technology.
<br><br>
The ideal candidate will possess prior experience in building and executing on a retail store strategy in an industry specific to the Cannabis industry and/or experience with contemporary retail brands.
<br><br>
Essential Functions
<br><br>
Reasonable accommodations may be made to enable individuals with disabilities to perform the essential functions.
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Provide inspirational leadership and direction to direct report team and ensure the continued development and management of a professional and efficient retail organization.</li>
<li>Create a culture of success and ongoing business and goal achievement.</li>
<li>Define optimal sales force structure.</li>
<li>Cultivate strong and transparent working relationships across the organization, ensuring open communication about the measurement of financial, programmatic and giving impact performance against stated milestones and goals.</li>
<li>Lead with compelling strategic vision that is in alignment with the Company's short and long-term goals and strategic plan.</li>
<li>Clearly creating a retail strategy, engaging key stakeholders and executing on a plan that is in alignment with MedMen's long-range plan to drive revenue and increase market share. This will include developing strategic and operational plans for the team, managing execution, measuring and delivering results.</li>
<li>Constantly monitor the competitive landscape and market conditions to identify opportunities, issues, and risks in order to recommend strategies and tactics for current and future store locations.</li>
<li>Work closely with marketing to establish successful support, channel and partner programs.</li>
<li>Provide detailed and accurate sales forecasting</li>
<li>Directly responsible for the strategic and operational plan to support global retail expansion, inclusive of short-term and long-term business and financial goals in support of MedMen's strategic plan for growth.</li>
<li>Expertise in real estate strategies including a deep understanding of how to translate demographic and other data in determining the right locations for new stores.</li>
<li>Partner closely with Compliance team to ensure all regulations by State are adhered to.</li>
<li>Identify opportunities, develop strategies, present recommendations and implement programs that will enhance retail store operational effectiveness and service strategies that differentiate MedMen from competitors by being a best-in-class retailer and community outpost.</li>
<li>Work with VP of Operations to create a scalable and standardized policies and procedures that support the growth of the direct retail business and continued safety of MedMen's customer.</li>
<li>Partner with planning and allocation to identify product opportunities and maximum sales plan</li>
<li>Operate with strong business acumen, drive topline sales and hold accountability for overall profitability for all MedMen's retail stores.</li>
<li>Work with VP of Visual Presentation for product displays and new store fixtures to maximize $ per sq.</li>
<li>Partner with VP of Hospitality to manage customer experience and contribute to a high level of customer satisfaction.</li>
<li>Develop and mentor the next generation of exceptional leadership talent for our growing retail organization.</li>
</ul>
