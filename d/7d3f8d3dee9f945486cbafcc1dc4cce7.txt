<b>Summary:</b><br>
We are looking
<br>for a highly strategic sales leader to join our team as a Sales Manager for the
<br>CDB Business Unit. You will lead, build and manage an established team of Client
<br>Service Executives (CSEs) focused on selling our Digital Business services and
<br>solutions to our clients and new logo prospects. Reporting to the Head of Sales - CDB, you will collaborate
<br>with other senior Cognizant leaders to deliver sales and revenue goals, and ensure
<br>proper account coverage for CDB at key accounts globally.
<br><br>
<b>Responsibilities:</b><br>
Develop
<br>geography, vertical, and account sales plans to ensure sales and revenue goals
<br>achieved
<br>Build
<br>and lead a team of CSEs to execute the sales plans
<br>Ensure
<br>that each sales team is consistently applying the Cognizant Way to Sell (CWTS)
<br>and Client Value Framework (CVF) to shape, qualify, propose, and close sales
<br>opportunities
<br>Ensure
<br>that all sales opportunities support Cognizant’s strategic business objectives,
<br>and are accurately recorded and maintained to ensure accurate monthly, quarterly,
<br>and annual revenue forecasts
<br>Manage
<br>and resolve any internal issues or escalation requests of the pursuit teams or
<br>sales Managers to maximize sales productivity
<br><br>
<b>Required Experience &amp; Skills:</b><br>
5+
<br>years professional sales management experience in information technology sales
<br>(IT Outsourcing) including 3+ years of digital services to both new logo prospects
<br>and existing customers
<br>Familiarity
<br>with Digital, Cloud, Analytics, and Artificial Intelligence technologies and services

<br>short and long-term business requirements
<br>Demonstrated
<br>ability to hire, coach, mentor and retain extraordinary sales talent and teach
<br>what is expected with clients
<br>Successful
<br>track record of achieving sales targets, growing revenue backlog, and growing

<br>services industry
<br>Ability
