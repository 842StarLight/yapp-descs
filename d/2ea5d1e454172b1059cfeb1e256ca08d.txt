<h2>Product Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>TE Connectivity's Product Management Teams manage all aspects of a product, from driving new product development to manufacturing to marketing. They are responsible for overall product strategy, business leadership and capacity plans in order to meet sales and profit targets.<br>
<br>
As a TE Product Specialist- Low and Medium Voltage, a typical day may include:<br>
<br>
<ul>
<li>Provides technical and commercial support to internal (sales, production, and engineering) and external customers (distributors and end users) for Low &amp; Medium Voltage overhead and underground connectors.</li>
<li>Support the integration of RRL products into the C&amp;F portfolio.</li>
<li>Interpret customers' specifications and provide technical product support.</li>
<li>Assist in the development and implementation of product testing, inventory management, product rationalization, cost reduction, marketing, and advertising projects.</li>
<li>Gather market commercial and technical information to justify product line additions or re-positioning, i.e. cable ranges, materials, installation time, application details, price, etc.</li>
<li>Develop specification and sales support materials including but not limited to installation instructions, brochures, catalogs, data sheets, cross references, etc.</li>
<li>Product areas to include MV/LV Overhead and Underground connectors. 25% effort will be applied to the integration of RRL products into the C&amp;F portfolio.</li>
</ul>
<br>
<b>
What your background should look like:</b><br>
<br>
<ul>
<li>B.S in Electrical or Mechanical Engineering, or related technical field is preferred.</li>
<li>3-5 years of experience in product management, marketing, application engineering, and/or sales for cable accessories or cables in the utility industry is preferred.</li>
<li>Experience specifying and/or installing overhead connectors, or underground cable accessories or cables is preferred.</li>
<li>Experience with ground products preferably exothermically welded connections.</li>
<li>Strong hands-on mechanical aptitude skills are a plus.</li>
</ul>
