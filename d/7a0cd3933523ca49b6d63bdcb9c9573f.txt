<h2>Accountant</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Do you want to work for a company whose values include<b> Respect, Integrity, Accountability, Innovation, and Resourcefulness </b>and is family-owned with <b>family-focused principles</b>? Is a <b>good work/ life balance </b>important to you? If you answered <b>YES</b> to these questions, you should consider a career with Conestoga Wood Specialties.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Key Elements of This Position:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Prepare and record recurring journal entries on a monthly basis and monitors those accounts balances</li>
<li>Reconcile corporate accrual and prepaid accounts on a monthly basis</li>
<li>Record and reconcile daily cash transactions in each bank account. Prepare Daily Cash Position report</li>
<li>Maintain schedule of lease and rental expenses and monitors these expenses for year-end auditing and financial reporting; Maintain asset and depreciation records for additions and changes</li>
<li>Assist in corporate budgeting and annual audits</li>
<li>Reviews and reconciles monthly sales and use tax filings for accuracy before payment released</li>
<li>Reviews and reconciles Property tax returns for accuracy before filing</li>
<li>Audits expense reports for compliance to company policy</li>
<li>Other tasks as assigned</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p><b>
</b></p>
<p><b>What We Ask of You:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor Degree in Accounting, Finance or Business Administration</li>
<li>Knowledge of GAAP</li>
<li>1-3 years of work experience in General Ledger Accounting and working with Financial Statements</li>
<li>Proficiency in working with Microsoft Office Software, particularly MS Excel</li>
<li>Deadline-oriented</li>
<li>Ability to maintain confidentiality</li>
<li>Thoroughness</li>
<li>Must be self-directed with the ability to work independently without close supervision</li>
<li>Display capability for handling multiple tasks and adjusting well to changing conditions</li>
<li>Must be capable of communicating effectively, both written and verbal, at all levels of the organization</li>
</ul>
