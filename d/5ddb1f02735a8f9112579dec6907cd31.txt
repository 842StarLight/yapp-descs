<h2>Sales Development Representative</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Welcome! </b>We’re glad you're interested in becoming a part of the Certent team as a Sales Development Representative. Are you an up-and-coming Sales Professional ready to launch your sales career and positively impact the bottom line? Your excellent communication and presentation abilities, along with your interest in Software as a Service (SaaS), finance and sales will guide you to success at Certent. If this sounds like a great match for your skills and strengths, we encourage you to apply.<br>
<br>
<b>What You'll Do:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Identify potential clients through a variety of resources</li>
<li>
Cold call leads to prospect for new business (phone and email)</li>
<li>
Qualify prospects on multiple criteria to determine fit with Certent’s offerings</li>
<li>
Understand the needs of your prospects</li>
<li>
Answer questions and inquiries from potential clients</li>
<li>
Attend and contribute to sales review meetings</li>
<li>
Update Customer Relationship Management system with details of all interactions with prospects in an accurate and timely manner</li>
<li>
Obtain product knowledge through training and mentoring sessions, and consulting with internal departments</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
What You'll Need:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Solid B2B sales ability</li>
<li>
Must be able to deliver results</li>
<li>
Must be self-motivated and have the ability to work with minimal supervision</li>
<li>
Must have good verbal and written communication skills with the ability to create sales proposals and presentations</li>
<li>
Entrepreneurial mind set</li>
<li>
Good organization and time management skills</li>
<li>
General knowledge of computers, Internet Explorer, and Outlook is a must</li>
<li>
Finance or accounting background preferred</li>
<li>
Bachelor’s degree highly desired</li>
</ul>
