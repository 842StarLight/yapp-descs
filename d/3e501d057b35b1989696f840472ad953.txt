<h2>IT Markets Strategist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Join us as we pursue our disruptive new vision to Turn Data Into Doing™. We are a company filled with people who are passionate about our technology and seek to deliver the best experience for our customers. At Splunk, we’re committed to our work, customers, having fun and most importantly to each other’s success.</p>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
The Role:</b></h3>
<p>
Are you a strategic thinker, expert, practitioner and evangelist in all things IT Operations and DevOps? Are you passionate about clients and the disruptive power of modern IT? Do you have deep industry technical IT subject matter expertise? Do you get a thrill from passing your knowledge onto others and in crafting the future of IT? If you answered yes to these questions Splunk might be the place for you!</p>
<p>
This IT Strategist role is a key part of the broader IT Specialization organization that is accountable for owning premium IT business and is responsible for influencing Splunk's IT business.</p>
<p>
Specifically, the IT Strategist organization drives the evolution and adoption of our IT Markets solutions and scaling our organization’s capability to position Splunk and deliver customer value. You will be part of an elite team of IT industry and experts that work cross-functionally with several organizations at Splunk including Marketing, Product Management, Sales, Sales Engineering and Education in helping solve the toughest IT challenges our customers face today and will face in the future.</p>
<p>
You will use your credibility as an IT authority and practitioner to lead and coordinate the IT Markets Specialization organization’s core strategic programs &amp; initiatives, with the goal of speeding up sales cycles, customer adoption, and success and deliver scale across all steps of the LAER cycle.</p>
<p>
Travel up to 50%</p>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Responsibilities:</b></h3>
<p>
You will curate and evolve our IT Market Field Sales Service Catalog. Use your experience and competitive landscape knowledge and the patterns and trends identified from our selling motion to evolve our storytelling and build repeatable services designed to be used by our sales field teams.</p>
<ul>
<li>
Activities to include creation of repeatable Sales or Technical workshops run by Sales Specialists or Splunks field teams, pipeline focused hands on customer events such as “Boss of the NOC”, or scaling adoption services such as Autobahn.</li>
<li>
You will support and be responsible for enablement (development and delivery of content and strategy in partnership with PMM and respective groups), adoption standard practices and renewal technical success requirements. You are a key advisor to Splunks Customer Success teams.</li>
<li>
Align closely with the Sales Engineering Field organization and become their mentor and innovation partner.</li>
<li>
Cultivate and develop our community of technical SMEs pre/post sales (engineering), customer success and partners, with the objective of scaling, product and engagement skills to the field.</li>
<li>
As the senior IT practitioner tier within the company, you will engage directly with customers. This will include broad changes in engagements, ambitious engagements that scale beyond specific point products, large scale deployments with sound practices etc. In this capacity you will serve as the highest level of customer concern for Pre sales (SE SMEs and Architects) and Sales Specialist teams.</li>
<li>
Be an advisor and contribute to the evolution IT Markets Group go to market strategy, platform and offerings. Represent as a key “voice of the field’ and work closely with Product Management to prioritize features, new releases, business processes, information and packaging.</li>
<li>
Work alongside Splunk’s Vertical and Industry teams and collaborate on opportunities to evolve our IT value proposition, information and service catalog for focused vertical customers and buying personas.</li>
<li>
You will need to maintain and constantly evolve your IT expertise to learn and understand new relevant technologies, industry developments and the competitive landscape. The goal is to continually evolve to be ahead of where the Splunk field are at all times.</li>
<li>
You will play a role in Evangelizing Splunk’s IT Markets value worldwide both internally and externally.</li>
</ul>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Qualifications:</b></h3>
<p>
You will have 8+ years of high-tech customer-facing experience in sales engineering, professional services, product management, or consulting. Hands on understanding and experience of Splunk’s IT products and platform preferred</p>
<ul>
<li>
Deep IT/landscape/competitive expertise and consulted with business regarding standard methodologies and monitoring, APM, Cloud, Microservices, DevOps, IT Operations, etc.</li>
<li>
ITIL certification and/or 5+ years of relevant work experience in IT Operations, DevOps, or Application Development</li>
<li>
Strong communicator with solid executive presentation skills and experience in building and delivering enablement to field teams</li>
<li>
Experience of participating in successfully selling complex solutions to the Global 2000 or Fortune 500 Company List</li>
<li>
A passion for customer success</li>
<li>
Familiar with product roadmap planning to incorporate customer requirements into Splunk service offerings.</li>
<li>
Excellent understanding of the IT, applications, and data center operations.</li>
<li>
You bring a range of industry-related contacts and diverse way of thinking about groundbreaking technology.</li>
<li>
High ethics, integrity, and humility and be able to work with other teams.</li>
<li>
Demonstrated leadership of cross-functional teams tasked with full support.</li>
<li>
Ability to build and maintain high credibility with sales teams and strategic customers.</li>
<li>
Formal sales training: Solution-Selling and/or Value Selling training preferred.</li>
<li>
Bachelor's Degree or equivalent experience.</li>
<li>
Located near to one of Splunks office locations: SF Bay area (SF) CA; San Jose, CA; Seattle, WA; Plano, TX; McLean, VA</li>
</ul>
