The Assistant Community Manager is to assist the Manager of Community Operations in effectively managing the community. The Assistant Community Manager is directly responsible for maintaining daily, weekly and monthly reports including accurate reporting and processing of rental payments and deposits received while assisting with the leasing, marketing and resident relations for the community.
<br><br>
Leasing
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Greet prospects and qualify by covering all criteria set forth by Mark-Taylor Residential.</li>
<li>Immediately record all telephone and in person visits to the appropriate management software by actively utilizing leasing tablets.</li>
<li>Inspect models and available "market ready" apartment homes and communicate related service needs to the Manager and/or Service Manager.</li>
<li>Demonstrates community and apartment and/or model by actively touring the community and amenities and apply product knowledge to future residents needs by communicating the features and benefits; close the sale.</li>
<li>Have future resident complete application, process application credit/criminal.Submit processed applications to the Manager of Community Operations for approval.Follow up with future resident regarding status.</li>
<li>Ensure apartment home is ready for resident to move in on agreed date.</li>
<li>Immediately follow up on prospects that did not lease and attempt to close the sale again.If unable to help prospect, refer them to sister communities that meet prospect's needs.</li>
<li>Secure new resident signature(s) on appropriate paperwork prior to move in.</li>
<li>Represent the company in a professional manner at all times.</li>
<li>Shop competitive communities.</li>
</ul>
Administrative
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Maintains awareness of sub-market conditions and trends.Contributes ideas to Manager of Community Operations for the marketing, concessions and improving resident satisfaction.</li>
<li>Accept and record all monies collected and prepare bank deposit slips on an on-going basis. Deposit all monies same day prior to the bank closing.</li>
<li>Ensure that all proper and legal documents are processed for non-payment of rent, non-compliance resident notices etc. and properly served to the resident(s).</li>
<li>Check figures, postings and documents for correct entry and proper codes are utilized.</li>
<li>Prepare and process move out and cancellation paperwork for past residents.</li>
<li>Proof reads all lease paperwork and processes move ins in computer software.</li>
<li>Prepare, review and close financial books for assigned property on a monthly basis.</li>
<li>Prepare and review weekly report and submit to the corporate office in a timely manner.</li>
<li>Provide accounting support to the Manager of Community Operations.</li>
<li>Maintain accurate resident correspondence.Update on a daily basis all rents, deposits and fees received from current and future residents.</li>
<li>Assist in lease transactions by preparing leases, gathering applicant's history and credit approvals.</li>
<li>Assist Manager of Community Operations in applicant approvals and ensure the lease information is accurate.</li>
<li>Assist Manager of Community Operations with market rental rates, specials, lead management on a daily, weekly and monthly basis.Update all software with updated information and check for accuracy.</li>
<li>Assume Manager of Community Operations's duties/authority in absence and in accordance with company and community guidelines.</li>
</ul>
Resident Retention
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Listen to resident(s) requests, concerns and comments and follow up as needed.</li>
<li>Complete Service Requests and inform the service team.</li>
<li>Answer questions for residents about community, repairs, rent, policies etc.Follow up on a timely basis if unable to respond to resident(s) concerns.</li>
<li>Maintain open communication with Manager of Community Operations and Manager of Facilities &amp; Support.</li>
<li>Contribute to cleanliness and curb appeal of the community on a continuing basis.</li>
<li>Assist in planning resident functions.Attend and participate in company and/or resident functions.</li>
<li>Prepare resident lease renewal letters, phone calls and lease renewal spreadsheet in advance of resident's lease end date.</li>
<li>Prepare lease renewal paperwork.</li>
<li>Communicate with Manager on lease renewal rent increases etc. and follow up with resident(s) regarding their lease renewal.</li>
<li>Enforce policies of the community.</li>
</ul>
Job Functions
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Complete all training courses set forth by Mark-Taylor Residential, Fair Housing and Sexual Harassment etc. prior to interacting with current and prospective residents.</li>
<li>Demonstrate strong oral and written communications skills.</li>
<li>Demonstrate an ability to support and contribute to the community team.</li>
<li>Operate telephone, personal/computer keyboard and community software.</li>
<li>Must possess a positive attitude and the ability to conduct yourself in a positive manner under all circumstances.</li>
<li>Participate in training in order to comply with new or existing laws.</li>
<li>Ability to work a flexible schedule, including weekends.</li>
<li>Neat, clean, professional at all times throughout the workday and/or whenever present at the community or Management functions.</li>
<li>Comply with expectations as demonstrated in the employee handbook.</li>
<li>Demonstrate ability to diffuse and respond to customer concerns to avoid escalation of the problem.</li>
<li>Attendance is an imperative job function.</li>
</ul>
