<h2>Specialist Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>The Azure Apps and Infrastructure Specialist Manager is a senior leader within our enterprise sales organization. The Azure Apps and Infrastructure Specialist Manager leads, develops and manages a team of high performing Azure Infrastructure and Azure App Dev Specialists to drive solution opportunity revenue and market share by executing the Migrate and Innovate sales priorities to migrate infrastructure workloads to Azure, modernize existing applications leveraging the Azure platform and services, and develop Cloud Native applications and solutions built using Microsoft Azure, Windows Server, and System Center Server and related technologies.</p>
<p></p>
