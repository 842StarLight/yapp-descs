<h2>Buyer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>This position is responsible for the procurement of materials, equipment and services. This position will issue Requests for Quotation, evaluate Supplier bids, negotiate final price and Terms &amp; Conditions and issue Purchase Orders to Suppliers for assigned commodities with the overall objective of delivering all goods and services at or below budget, meeting quality and schedule requirements. Included is analysis of current market price levels and delivery trends for assigned commodities. This position will also evaluate overall performance of existing Suppliers and develop new qualified, competitive Suppliers.</p>
 Essential Responsibilities:
<ul>
<li>Formulate a procurement strategy for assigned commodities in support of project material, equipment and service purchases, taking into consideration cost, quality and schedule requirements.
</li>
<li>Interface with the Project Team to develop and issue Requests for Quotation. Review and evaluate Supplier bids, negotiate final price and Terms &amp; Conditions including appropriate "flow down" of Customer terms. In conjunction with the Project Buyer and Project Team, select Supplier and issue Purchase Order in conformance with approved requisition.
</li>
<li>Support the Proposal/Estimating Teams with current market pricing through obtaining quotes, using recent historical pricing and/or developing cost standards for assigned commodities. Monitor Suppliers' capacity/shop loading and provide delivery lead times based on current market conditions.
</li>
<li>Negotiate Long Term Supply Agreements for selective commodities with the objective of ensuring delivery and volume price discounts.
</li>
<li>Interface with Suppliers and Engineering as lead in developing Cost Reduction opportunities for assigned commodities.
</li>
<li>Expedite Suppliers and manage recovery plans when Suppliers fall behind schedule
</li>
<li>Responsible for timely processing of Supplier back charges and performance, schedule and warranty issues.
</li>
<li>Evaluate and manage Supplier performance. Continuously search for new qualified, competitive Suppliers. Responsible for management of Supplier Qualification for assigned commodities.
</li>
<li>Travel to Suppliers, Customers and Sister Companies, as required.
</li>
</ul>
