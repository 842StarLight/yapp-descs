<h2>Project Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<h1 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>SUMMARY STATEMENT</b></h1>
<p>The Project Manager is responsible for managing all aspects of major projects.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>QUALIFICATIONS ESSENTIAL TO QUALITY SYSTEM</b></p>
<p>Education equivalent to a four-year degree and/or three to five years experience in a construction related field.</p>
<h1 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>GENERAL QUALIFICATIONS</b></h1>
<ul>
<li><div>In-depth knowledge of architectural process from conception through implementation.</div></li>
<li><div>
Working knowledge of costing, estimating, bid preparation and contracts.</div></li>
<li><div>
Computer literate, including personal computer, project management software and CAD software.</div></li>
<li><div>
Experienced in architectural plans/ blueprint reading, shop drawings and layout design.</div></li>
<li><div>
Strong engineering and mechanical aptitude and ability to understand technical information.</div></li>
<li><div>
Knowledge of working with general contractors and field installations.</div></li>
<li><div>
Strong communication and interpersonal skills.</div></li>
<li><div>
Strong math and metric conversion skills.</div></li>
<li><div>
Able to work in a fast paced environment with interruptions, managing multiple priorities.
</div></li>
<li><div>Proven leadership skills with the ability to interact and influence others in a positive manner.</div></li>
<li><div>
Results-oriented and able to make decisions when required.
</div></li>
<li><div>Organized, detail oriented and able to set priorities.</div></li>
<li><div>
Flexible to continue skill development and cross-training</div></li>
<li><div>
Flexible to travel when necessary, both domestic and international.</div></li>
<li><div>
Bilingual English and Spanish preferred.<br>
</div></li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>METRICS</b></p>
<ul>
<li><div>Timely and accurate completion of essential responsibilities</div></li>
<li><div>
Active involvement in achieving corporate goals</div></li>
</ul>
<p><b>ESSENTIAL RESPONSIBILITIES</b><b>
</b></p>
<ul>
<li><div>Manage major projects from conception to completion, expediting through the system, obtaining necessary information to assist other Team Members in completing their responsibilities to the job.</div></li>
<li><div>
Communicate with architects, contractors, owners, project managers, installers, and distributors regarding execution of the projects.</div></li>
<li><div>
Prepare quotations based on plans and specifications and sales team strategy, using proper pricing, escalation, and products.</div></li>
<li><div>
Propose the best product solutions, based on architectural drawings, specifications, competitive advantages, internal pricing strategies, and internal manufacturing processes, recommending alternates when possible.</div></li>
<li><div>
Issue Product Variation Requests (PVR) to establish the feasibility and price of any special item or condition, making recommendations for standardization</div></li>
<li><div>
Furnish written quotations in sufficient time to meet the bid date and/or within established turn around times, complete with a description of what is included, a list of qualifications, the material product price, escalation, freight estimate, expiration date, alternates and options.</div></li>
<li><div>
Prepare NA Calcs and adjust Partition Studio to match what was sold.</div></li>
<li><div>
Offer competitive information, assistance to customers, and recommendations for standard product alternatives to custom product requests.</div></li>
<li><div>
Read and interpret blueprints and architectural plans, understand specs and contracts and provide technical assistance and develop solutions to differences between architectural design, product application and actual field conditions.</div></li>
<li><div>
Prepare preliminary shop drawings and layout options, including alternative recommendations that will optimize the proposed layout and/or improve partition operation and setup.</div></li>
<li><div>
Create op wall study, including pricing, drawings, etc.</div></li>
<li><div>
Prepare complete shop drawings and enter track orders as required.</div></li>
<li><div>
Prepare pricing for changes from the original contract.</div></li>
<li><div>
Prepare change orders as required when a project scope changes.</div></li>
<li><div>
Evaluate ways to reduce costs and improve profitability.</div></li>
<li><div>
Manage contracts for Hufcor direct contract projects, including submittals, schedules, installation coordination, special requirements, schedule of values and distributor coordination.</div></li>
<li><div>
Develop product material take-offs listing the items needed, when required.</div></li>
<li><div>
Calculate, order and follow up on the receipt of special buyout material.</div></li>
<li><div>
Manage the Securement records and releases, requesting schedule requirements and information in order to process releases in a timely fashion.</div></li>
<li><div>
Develop a project management program to ensure the timely completion of projects, maintaining an updated schedule of project requirements.</div></li>
<li><div>
Coordinate and communicate on scheduling issues, including reserving production space, ship dates, van schedules, lead times and other information requested by customers.</div></li>
<li><div>
Follow up after shipments to ensure that problems are corrected and payment is received on a timely basis.</div></li>
<li><div>
Coordinate with the accounting department, checking the financial stability and implement special payment terms and requirements.</div></li>
<li><div>
Prepare submittals and closeout documents.</div></li>
<li><div>
Cross-train in other responsibilities.</div></li>
<li><div>
Participate in ongoing process improvements.</div></li>
</ul>
<p><b>SECONDARY RESPONSIBILITIES</b></p>
<ul>
<li><div>Provide training support, including Partition Studio training, to other team members and distributors, such as participating in sales schools or individualized training as required.</div></li>
<li><div>
Complete other duties as assigned</div></li>
</ul>
</div></div>
