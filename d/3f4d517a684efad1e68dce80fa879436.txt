<h2>IT Systems Administrator & Security Lead</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">

<div>
<p><b>About Us:</b></p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
We believe in using space to help life on Earth.</b></p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Planet Federal is a subsidiary of Planet Labs Inc. (Planet). Planet designs, builds, and operates the largest constellation of imaging satellites in history. This constellation delivers an unprecedented dataset of empirical information via a revolutionary cloud-based platform to decision-makers in commercial, environmental, and humanitarian sectors. Planet is a space company and data company all rolled into one.</b></p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Planet Federal works with U.S. government customers to use Planet's data and machine learning-powered analytics to develop new technologies, power research, and understand some of our world's toughest challenges.</b></p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
We have a people-centric approach toward culture and community and we are iterating in a way that puts our team members first and prepares our company for growth.</b></p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Join Planet and be a part of our mission to change the way people see the world.</b></p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
The Role:</b></p>
<ul>
<li><b>
Lead all internal IT related functions (what we call CorpEng) at Planet Labs Federal. This includes:</b></li>
<li><b>
Administration of Single-Sign-On (SSO) and provisioning of users to various cloud systems.</b></li>
<li><b>
Acting as the Information System Security Officer (ISSO) and ensuring compliance with all monitoring and auditing requirements.</b></li>
<li><b>
Coordinate with IT Security and Systems Administration at Planet Federal's parent company, Planet Inc, as appropriate.</b></li>
<li><b>
Address security issues by conducting continual monitoring, vulnerability assessments and log analysis.</b></li>
<li><b>
Assist the full Planet Federal team effort to create, improve or automate processes and/or tools.</b></li>
<li><b>
Track new technology and identify areas of improvement in current systems, then work across Planet to develop a roadmap and budget for improvements.</b></li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
The Must Haves:</b></p>
<ul>
<li><b>
Bachelor's degree in computer science, or a related field, or equivalent years of experience.</b></li>
<li><b>
6-8 years of combined IT and security work experience.</b></li>
<li><b>
Experience in building or re-architecting end-to-end IT Systems solutions for small-to-medium size companies.</b></li>
<li><b>
Ability to quickly understand security threats, vulnerabilities, and implement remediation techniques.</b></li>
<li><b>
Team-oriented interpersonal skills, with the ability to interface effectively with a broad range of people and roles (IT and business personnel).</b></li>
<li><b>
Strong verbal &amp; written communication skills.</b></li>
<li><b>
Eligibility for U.S. Security Clearance.</b></li>
<li><b>
Security+ Certification (or equivalent).</b></li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
The Nice-to-Haves:</b></p>
<ul>
<li><b>
Experience working in a Startup environment</b></li>
<li><b>
Practical knowledge of the latest web application security issues and security trends</b></li>
<li><b>
Cloud Security knowledge of AWS and Google Cloud</b></li>
<li><b>
CISSP, OSCP, OSCE and GIAC certifications desired</b></li>
<li><b>
Proactive, organized, analytical, detail-oriented, and persistent</b></li>
</ul>
