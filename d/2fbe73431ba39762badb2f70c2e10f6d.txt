<h2>Director of Business Development, Fullstack Cyber Bootcamp</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Fullstack Academy is the premier technology bootcamp located in New York City and Chicago. Y Combinator-backed, our school has garnered the attention of Forbes, TechCrunch, and Business Insider, among others. The reputation of our school is built on the professional success of each and every one of our students. Students have gone on to promising careers at top-tier companies, like Google, Facebook, Amazon, and Goldman Sachs. In January 2016, we launched the Grace Hopper Program, which is a need-blind software engineering program for women.</p>
<p>
In late 2018, New York City selected Fullstack to build a world-class cybersecurity bootcamp, as part of its $100M Cyber NYC initiative. Fullstack Cyber Bootcamp opened its doors in June 2019 and the curriculum includes advanced red team and blue team training. You can read more about the cyber bootcamp on our website at cyber.fullstackacademy.com and in some of the news coverage it has received.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
ABOUT THE ROLE</p>
<p>
As the Director of Business Development you’ll be both a strategic leader and a market-facing evangelist for the power of Fullstack Cyber Bootcamp to create a steady pipeline of infosec talent for employers in New York City.
</p>
<p>You will build a strategic plan for targeting and nurturing industry-leading hiring partnerships, and help to drive adoption of the market-leading offerings on our Hiring &amp; Training Platform.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
RESPONSIBILITIES:</p>
<p>
In this role, you will:</p>
<ul>
<li>Grow and build a large network of “Hiring Partners” (companies and government agencies in New York City) that will hire graduates from Fullstack’s Cyber Bootcamp, the official cybersecurity bootcamp of New York City</li>
<li>Establish relationships with executives and cybersecurity recruiters at potential hiring partners, evangelize the mission and capabilities of Fullstack Cyber Bootcamp, and get the hiring partners to be active and enthusiastic users of our <i>Hiring &amp; Training Platform
</i>
</li>
<li>Sign up hiring partners across a range of sectors, including financial services, tech, media, healthcare, public safety, infosec and the intelligence community</li>
<li>Design and run a state-of-the-art business development pipeline for securing hiring partners, including funnel management in HubSpot</li>
<li>Encourage hiring partners to participate in Demo Day and Hiring Day, in collaboration with the Career Success team lead
</li>
<li>Encourage hiring partners to submit feedback on our curriculum, and to attend networking events hosted by Fullstack Cyber Bootcamp</li>
<li>Encourage hiring partners to send their cybersecurity staff to our week-long advanced training classes</li>
<li>Implement a feedback loop between employer partners and Fullstack Academy to inform the work of key teams: Product, Academics, Career Success, etc.</li>
<li>Identify and manage partnership KPIs, goals, metrics and other data to drive partnership strategy</li>
<li>Promptly and meticulously communicate with potential partners</li>
<li>Nurture relationships and collaborate with leaders from other academic institutions that are members of the Cyber NYC partnership, including Columbia University, Cornell Tech, NYU, and CUNY.</li>
<li>Represent Fullstack Cyber Bootcamp at infosec conferences, e.g. DefCon</li>
<li>Keep in clear and consistent communication with Fullstack Cyber Bootcamp leadership, ensuring absolute clarity on progress and needs</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>QUALIFICATIONS:</p>
<p>
You are a strong candidate for this role if you have:</p>
<ul>
<li>A passion for cybersecurity</li>
<li>5-10 years of relevant sales and/or business development experience within the cybersecurity industry</li>
<li>Strong track record (quantifiable) in sales and/or business development, ideally selling into the c-suite or executive level</li>
<li>Experience growing a sales function from scratch, entering a new market, or similar entrepreneurial sales experience
</li>
<li>An understanding of the early career hiring needs of organizations when it comes to technical infosec hires</li>
<li>The ability to articulate and evangelize the mission behind Fullstack Cyber Bootcamp and Cyber NYC</li>
<li>Exceptional and engaging communication and presentation skills</li>
<li>An entrepreneurial owner’s mentality - you independently own and drive your partnership network as if you were the owner of Fullstack Cyber Bootcamp</li>
</ul>
<p>You are an exceptional candidate for this role if you also have:</p>
<ul><li>
Deep connections with members of the infosec community in New York City (ideally including relationships at the executive level)</li></ul>
