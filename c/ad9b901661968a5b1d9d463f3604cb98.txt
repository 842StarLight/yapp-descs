<h2>Director, Custom Content</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>The Director, Custom Content leads the planning, development and tactical execution of content marketing services, including custom content development, lead-nurturing campaigns, and native advertising. This role collaborates with advertisers and editors to develop a portfolio of integrated multimedia products with a voice consistent with the ADA’s editorial platforms. This position will drive the strategy and execution of all branded content to support a custom program of print and digital product expansion for both sponsored and earned media.</p>
<p>
This position will manage the development of new content marketing products; collaborate with sales team on new business development opportunities; build consultative programs with innovative content and distribution methods.</p>
<p>
This position will operate within a B2B environment that focuses on dentistry and the dental market. This position will collaborate extensively with technology and service vendors to manage and oversee the development of content for digital, social media, video, print, webinar, audio, and in-person platforms, as well as engage in lead-generation, thought leadership, custom research, and influencer marketing.</p>
<p><b>
Minimum Qualifications:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Master's degree and 8 years of progressive experience in content marketing, managing content creation across print and digital platforms OR Bachelor's degree and 8 years of experience.</li>
<li>Experience managing projects, people and budgets.</li>
<li>Experience with new product development, content development, and editorial management.</li>
<li>Knowledge of print and digital platforms in a healthcare environment.</li>
<li>Excellent oral, written, presentation, planning, leadership, project &amp; time management, peer relationship, and collaboration skills.</li>
<li>Must be proficient in Microsoft Office Suite, Content asset management systems, HTML-based email programs, CRM’s</li>
<li>Must have the ability to:</li>
<ul>
<li>interact with high-level executives, client-customers, and creative personnel</li>
<li>exhibit business acumen</li>
<li>deal with ambiguity/paradox</li>
<li>drive for results</li>
<li>solve complex issues on the fly</li>
</ul>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Must be able to use data to support business decisions</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Highly Desirable Skills:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Experience working within the process channels of an academic, non-profit or healthcare institution;</li>
<li>Knowledge of health care information management</li>
<li>Professional or association-based B2B scientific or dental publishing experience</li>
<li>Ability to work in a complex, member-driven association</li>
</ul>
