About the Candidate
<br>ServiceMax is seeking an Accounting Manager to join our team in Pleasanton, CA. This is a great opportunity to have an impact at a successful high growth cloud software company where your ideas and contributions will be valued. The Accounting Manager position is key within ServiceMax's Accounting department and will closely partner with the Assistant Controller and others within the department and throughout the organization.
<br><br>
<b>The successful candidate will possess:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>a solid understanding of US GAAP and full cycle accounting.</li>
<li>a strong analytical and problem-solving skillset, with the ability to read, analyze, interpret and explain complex financial data.</li>
<li>a strong understanding of business processes and internal controls.</li>
<li>experience with system implementations, upgrades and enhancements.</li>
<li>a positive and infectious customer services attitude.</li>
<li>a strong work ethic, with a do what it takes to get the job done mindset.</li>
<li>a meticulous attention to detail and accuracy, and highly-developed organizational and follow-through skills with the ability to manage competing priorities.</li>
<li>strong leadership, mentoring and team building skills, and excellent interpersonal skills with demonstrated ability to partner in a cross functional capacity.</li>
<li>excellent written and verbal communication skills, including the ability to interact effectively with all levels throughout the company.</li>
</ul>
<b>Job responsibilities:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Coordinate and prepare for monthly close meetings. Maintain close calendar.</li>
<li>Ensure accurate and timely completion of monthly, quarterly, and year end close.</li>
<li>Ensure all transactions are accurately and timely reflected in the financial statements.</li>
<li>Oversee daily transactions, cash and credit card accounts, and general ledger posting.</li>
<li>Assist in the preparation of journal entries and providing supporting documentation for month end close.</li>
<li>Review and approve work of other accounting team members.</li>
<li>Maintain fixed asset system and depreciation schedules.</li>
<li>Prepare month end expense allocation entries.</li>
<li>Ensure timely filing of regulatory tax reporting for local, state and federal (property tax, sales and use tax, etc.).</li>
<li>Provide guidance on handling non-routine transactions and train new and existing staff as needed.</li>
<li>Work with Assistant Controller to manage foreign accounting. Ensure intercompany accounting and consolidations are accurately completed.</li>
<li>Prepare monthly account reconciliations.</li>
<li>Prepare fluctuation analysis with explanations for trended numbers.</li>
<li>Participate in annual audit, providing information and answers to auditor requests and inquiries.</li>
<li>Identify areas for improvement related to accounting, policies, processes and systems and work with others in the department to implement improvements. Document policies and processes.</li>
<li>Participate in strategic initiatives and assist with other projects.</li>
</ul>
<b>Qualifications:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor's degree in Accounting or Finance is required.</li>
<li>Active CPA license, with public accounting firm experience is preferred.</li>
<li>Strong technical accounting background with the ability to research accounting issues as they arise.</li>
<li>Combination of public and private accounting experience. Minimum five years of relevant experience.</li>
<li>Experience with typical business software applications including NetSuite or similar general ledger software.</li>
<li>Demonstrated experience managing direct reports.</li>
</ul>
