<h2>Marketing Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">Access Intelligence, LLC seeks a high energy, innovative and goal oriented Marketing Manager for our offices in Rockville, MD, Norwalk, CT and New York, NY, to drive attendee and paid subscription revenue for a portfolio of products and events. This person should have a proven track record of developing and executing marketing plans such as event attendee revenue generation, utilizing email marketing and direct mail as well as e-marketing tasks such as SEO and SEM campaigns, Google AdWords, social media marketing, and more. As the marketing manager, this candidate will need to adapt and respond in a fast-paced environment, utilize cutting edge technology to drive attendance and engage readers, and analyze metrics to create efficient and profitable campaigns.
<br><br>
<b>PRINCIPAL DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Meet and exceed revenue goals for events by driving attendee registrations.</li>
<li>Develop and execute marketing plans for all products, including special reports, webinars, directories and other product launches as deemed appropriate</li>
<li>Meet and exceed revenue goals for each paid product as assigned</li>
<li>Develop comprehensive list acquisition and marketing partnerships</li>
<li>Copy writing for direct mail, email marketing campaigns, media kits, paid products and other marketing communication efforts as assigned</li>
<li>Develop marketing plans for website optimization and social media efforts</li>
<li>Oversee coordination of all direct mail campaigns, email campaigns, SEM efforts and SEO efforts</li>
<li>Write copy for any sales supporting materials, including prospectus and one sheet flyers</li>
<li>Oversee email coordination, including approving proofs of emails</li>
<li>Oversee public relations campaign, writing press releases when necessary</li>
<li>Report and analyze data; Write post show analysis of event campaign for smaller events</li>
<li>Travel to various events to assist as needed</li>
</ul>
<b>Requirements:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>B.S. or B.A. in marketing, business or related degree and 3 - 5 years of tradeshow marketing experience required.</li>
<li>Functional knowledge of tradeshows and conferences is required.</li>
<li>Excellent writer, strong organizational, time management, project management and communication skills required.</li>
<li>Excellent interpersonal skills and ability to thrive in a fast-paced environment.</li>
<li>Team player, capable of managing projects requiring input from multiple sources and leading the project from start to finish, including managing other team members and third party vendors.</li>
<li>Ability to multitask on more than one event or project at a time.</li>
<li>Proficiency in Excel, Word and Outlook; experience in html, database management software preferred.</li>
</ul>
</div>
