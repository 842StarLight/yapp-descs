<p><b>Title:</b> Aftermarket Systems Engineer
</p>
<p><b>Exempt Status:</b> Exempt
</p>
<p><b>Summary:
</b></p>
<p>Responsible to interact with Hamon Deltak customers as the primary contact to develop creative solutions to operational and mechanical issues with existing equipment utilizing sound engineering principles and practices, sell the Hamon Deltak solution, and provide support through project completion.
</p>
<p></p>

<ul>
<li>Generate aftermarket sales leads and evaluate leads for fit with Hamon Deltakâ€™s Aftermarket product line, capabilities and goals.</li>
