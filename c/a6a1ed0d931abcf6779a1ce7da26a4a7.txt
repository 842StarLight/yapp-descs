<div><b>
Job Overview:</b></div>
<div>
Oversee daily operation of department functions and works closely with senior merchandiser to achieve the goals of the category business plans. Develop annual promotional plan for the category by evaluating items and negotiate deals with vendors; develop, negotiate, and execute regional tactical plans for Category Management programs (pricing, promotion, product, procurement and placement) for the categories assigned.</div>
<div><b>
</b></div>
<div><b>
Job Responsibilities and Accountabilities:</b></div>
<ul>
<li><div>
Responsible for proofing and ensuring accuracy of promotional activity and communication within assigned category</div></li>
<li><div>
Manage vendor relationships in assigned categories. Provide competitive merchandising solutions for retail formats by researching market conditions to determine potential sales of product or service and retail trends. Execute regional advertising plans of assigned categories</div></li>
<li><div>
Resolve all internal and external customer requests and concerns while maintaining a high level of professionalism and service</div></li>
<li><div>
Grow sales by creating weekly ad and supporting existing merchandising programs. Create sales opportunities through category management and market principles with relation to SKU rationalization, merchandising, and niche market applications</div></li>
<li><div>
Participate in the Supervision, training, and development of Category Management team</div></li>
<li><div>
Coordinate with procurement team to help maintain required shrink and spoilage commitments with consideration of lowest cost of goods</div></li>
<li><div>
Review weekly ad forecasts and provide direction to the buyers to determine what inventory levels are needed to meet ad requirements. Conduct post-promotional review and analysis to determine if promotional requirements were met and if there were problem areas, provide information to vendor</div></li>
<li><div>
Review period data to assess sales, service levels and category profitability, make adjustments as needed. Support and implement region and department initiatives as determined by senior merchandisers and director</div></li>
</ul>
