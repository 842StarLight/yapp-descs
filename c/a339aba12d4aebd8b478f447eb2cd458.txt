<div>
Gravy Analytics is disrupting the world of consumer intelligence. We are the only <i>location</i>-<i>based</i> consumer intelligence platform providing real-world information about the places and events that people visit. Our mission is to be the largest and most accurate source of easily useable and trusted <i>location</i> data services that reveal how consumers live their daily lives. Understanding where people go and how they spend their time gives unprecedented insight into consumer lifestyles, life stages, interests and affinities.
</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>Gravy Analytics works with leading companies in marketing, advertising, retail and data services to drive increased sales, deliver market research and improve customer engagement. Our <i>location</i> intelligence powers ad targeting, competitive insights, and customer relationship management, enabling organizations to better communicate with and serve their customers throughout the life cycle. We provide the data that powers our customers' proprietary solutions, as well as our own products and services.</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
Gravy Analytics is big data. We process tens of billions of daily <i>location</i> signals and match them against our curated database of geo-fences, and the <i>place</i> and event data associated with them. We maintain the largest events database in the U.S., aggregating and improving more <i>than</i> a million events each month.
</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>Gravy Analytics is technology. We deploy solutions using Hadoop, Spark, and other parallel processing technologies; we use heuristic, pattern matching, probabilistic and machine-learning algorithms. We use a variety of DBMS types, file types and analytical tools. We use Java, node.js and Python when it fits. We do everything in the Cloud. We select the right technology for the <i>job</i> versus deploying what we already know.
</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>We <i>work</i> fast, iterate, and test along the way to ensure that we bring the right solutions to market.</div>
