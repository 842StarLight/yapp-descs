<h2>Creative Account Strategist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">Teads.tv is seeking a Creative Account Strategist to roll out our video advertising creative and analytics platform to major national clients, as well as advise these clients on best practices. Your primary focus will be supporting our media sales team and building direct relationships with our clients' creative agencies.
<br><br>
<b>Key Responsibilities:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Advise on best practices and creative execution in order to meet our clients' business objectives across their Teads activity</li>
<li>Work alongside our media sales team to prospect and close business across all agency and advertiser accounts</li>
<li>Develop strong, mutually beneficial relationships with clients and their creative agencies</li>
<li>Work closely with internal teams to ensure a united vision when corresponding with clients</li>
<li>Represent Teads in the online marketplace and become an active brand advocate</li>
<li>Work closely with Client Services, Ad Operations, and Sales Planning and our Strategic Accounts Team, to ensure client campaign objectives are communicated internally and that our media plan and creative execution reflect those objectives</li>
<li>Ensure a high level of client satisfaction to generate renewals and increase incremental revenue</li>
</ul>
<b>Requirements:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Outstanding communication and presentation skills</li>
<li>Experience presenting in front of large audiences</li>
<li>Previous technology platform experience a huge plus</li>
<li>Deep relationships with contacts at top-tier creative agencies and advertisers</li>
<li>Understanding of the digital video marketplace</li>
<li>Desire to work in a changing, fast-paced start up</li>
<li>Driven, passionate, and an ultimate team player</li>
<li>BS/BA degree or equivalent work experience</li>
</ul>
