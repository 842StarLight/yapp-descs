<p><b>
POSITION SUMMARY</b></p>
<p>
The Industry Relations Manager is responsible for growing sponsorship, exhibit and advertising revenue for a dedicated portfolio of up to 3 nonprofit clients in varied fields and industries, generating $1M+ in collective gross annual sales. In this role, the Industry Relations Manager cultivates and nurtures existing client relationships while identifying new prospects for support. In addition to developing relationships with new industry partners who would benefit from alignment with AH clients, the Industry Relations Manager is also responsible for creating new viable revenue streams and support models; ensuring their financial stability year over year.=</p>
<p>
The Industry Relations Manager reports directly to the Senior Director of Industry Relations.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
ESSENTIAL DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Effectively manage and balance a portfolio of up to 3 clients</li>
<li>Generate non-dues revenue of $1M+ annually</li>
<ul>
<li>Oversee and manage all interactions with industry supporter companies</li>
<li>Create cross-sale promotions and value driven sponsorship benefits packages</li>
<li>Ability to grow existing sponsorship sales programs in new directions by researching, analyzing and engaging new markets, industry segments/verticals and groups of potential supporters who would find value in being more closely aligned with our client partners, i.e. growing the universe (prospecting and lead generation)</li>
<li>Maintain and manage a robust sales pipeline according to the financial goals that have been set</li>
<li>Demonstrate growth and success in up-sell initiatives</li>
<li>Demonstrate an understanding of competitive landscapes and sales strategies</li>
<li>Ensure sales best practices are being implemented across the board</li>
<li>Maintain a consultative, relationship-based approach that creates long-term partnerships for sustainable revenue generation</li>
<li>Meet and/or exceed quarterly sales targets and stretch goals</li>
<li>Develop and create effective sales tools and prospectus brochures</li>
</ul>
<li>Develop and disseminate monthly sales progress reports and end of year Board reports to AH clients</li>
<li>Maintain CRM business rules as set forth by the Senior Director and Senior Administrator of Industry Relations</li>
<li>Be a role model for the company culture</li>
<li>Work and collaborate effectively with other AH departments and client teams</li>
<li>Provide creative input and become involved in all applicable event related activities; ensuring a positive experience for all sponsors and exhibitors</li>
<br>
</ul>
