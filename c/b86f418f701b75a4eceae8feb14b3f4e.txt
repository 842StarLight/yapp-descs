<h2>Cloud Security Architect (AWS or Azure)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<div>Are you passionate about cyber and security challenges in information technology, associated with threats and vulnerabilities? Are you a leader who is looking for an enriching experience to build your career and brand? If you are interested in a role that offers an opportunity to provide front line support to our clients instead of a “back office programmer” position then Deloitte’s Government &amp; Public Sector (GPS) Cyber Risk team could be the place for you! Join our team of Cyber Risk professionals who collaborate with government agencies, IT professionals, and clients to support cyber security and risk consulting engagements.</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div><b>Work you’ll do</b></div>
<div>As a Manager within our Cyber Risk team, you will:
</div>
<ul><li>Work with government clients to mitigate cyber risk and threats</li></ul>
<ul><li>Organize and deliver services on a cross-section of complex projects</li></ul>
<ul><li>Actively participate in the development of business and vendor relationships
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Participate and lead aspects of the proposal development process</li></ul>
<ul><li>Responsible for project(s) financials including development of financial plans
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Manages day-to-day interactions with clients and internal Deloitte team</li></ul>
<ul><li>Displays both breadth and depth of knowledge regarding functional and technical issues</li></ul>
<ul><li>Displays leadership and business judgment in anticipating client/project needs and developing alternative solutions</li></ul>
<ul><li>Provide counseling/coaching, oversight, and support for delivery teams and staff</li></ul>
<ul><li>Actively participate in staff recruitment and retention activities providing input and guidance into the staffing process</li></ul>
<ul><li>Lead and drive cloud security design, configuration and operationalization of the environments</li></ul>
<ul><li>Provide advisory and solution architect services, strategies, technology and/or process roadmaps</li></ul>

<div><b>Qualifications</b></div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div>Required:</div>
<ul><li>Bachelor’s Degree in Information Technology, Information Systems Security, Cybersecurity, or related field</li></ul>
<ul><li>4+ years of relevant consulting or industry experience</li></ul>
<ul>
<li>2+ years in a technical or functional lead role
</li>
<li>Ability to travel up to 80%</li>
</ul>
<ul><li>Experience mentoring and coaching others</li></ul>
<ul><li>Proven leadership skills demonstrating strong judgment, problem-solving, and decision-making abilities</li></ul>
<ul><li>Experience managing senior-level client relationships</li></ul>
<ul><li>Experience presenting to clients or other decision makers to present and sell ideas to various audiences (technical and non-technical)</li></ul>
<ul><li>Pre-sales, proposal, and RFP experience</li></ul>
<div></div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div>Preferred:</div>
<ul><li>Previous Consulting experience</li></ul>
<ul><li>Understanding of fundamental cloud computing concepts</li></ul>
<ul><li>Experience with Information Assurance concepts and processes within the Federal government</li></ul>
<ul><li>Knowledge of and experience with Federal security regulations, standards, and processes including FISMA, FIPS, NIST, and FedRAMP</li></ul>
<ul><li>Cloud certification</li></ul>
