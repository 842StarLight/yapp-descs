<h2>Marketing Coordinator [Temp]</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<b>Description:</b><br>
Summary
<br>We are looking for a temporary Marketing Coordinator to help us in our overall marketing efforts. The goal is to deliver effective marketing programs that will help our reputation and growth. This position will manage and track all marketing related activities including internal collateral creation, trade shows, and public-facing marketing materials.
<br><br>
This is a temporary position that will operate within the time period specified below as an at-will employee. There is no guarantee of continued employment. Extensions to the contract are permissible if all parties are in agreement and a new contract is signed.
<br><br>
<b>Start Date:</b> ASAP
<br><b>End Date:</b> Approximately 5 Months from Start Date
<br><br>
Reports To
<br>The Marketing Coordinator will report directly to the Director of Marketing Communications.
<br><br>
<b>Responsibilities Include but are not limited to:</b><br>
Duties include the following (other duties may be assigned as required by management):
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Brainstorm and develop ideas for creative marketing campaigns</li>
<li>Assist in outbound or inbound marketing activities by demonstrating expertise in various areas (content development and optimization, advertising, events planning etc.)</li>
<li>Liaise with external vendors to execute promotional events and campaigns</li>
<li>Ability to track and manage multiple projects simultaneously</li>
<li>Plan and coordinate industry trade shows</li>
<li>Collaborate with marketing and other professionals to coordinate brand awareness and marketing efforts</li>
<li>Ability to work independently</li>
<li>Undertake individual tasks of a marketing plan as assigned</li>
</ul>
Highly Desirable
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Knowledge of web development tools</li>
<li>BSc/BA in marketing, communications or equivalent (desired)</li>
<li>Experience with Adobe Softwares, Canva, and Google Suite.</li>
</ul>

Required
<br><br>
<ul>
