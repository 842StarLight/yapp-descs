<h2>Event Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p><b>Why Work at Russell Morin Catering &amp; Events?
</b></p>
<p>We believe that an engaged and inspired company culture will produce the most genuine and motivated staff to take exceptional care of our clients.</p>
<p><b>Position Summary:</b> The Event Specialist will develop and execute strategies to meet or exceed their sales goal. Enhance the client base, client relationships and be a brand ambassador for the organization.</p>
<p><b>Duties and Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul><li>Grow sales by ensuring inquiries and leads are responded to in an enthusiastic and timely manner, new business is sought out through networking and prospecting, prospects are converted to clients, and client’s expectations are met through effective communication, event planning, and service.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Manage individual sales goal development and achieve period sales goal targets.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Support a culture of no client should be a one-time client.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Partner with Operations and Kitchen Departments to ensure events are correctly prepared for and executed. Provide recommendations to enhance processes and create efficiencies.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Partner with Marketing to develop both short and long range plans that support the brand and product for clients and venues.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Adhere to internal event production paperwork deadlines to include production meetings and tastings.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Attend monthly sales meetings.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Work closely with Culinary in the development of quality, creative and timely presentations, tastings and proposals.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Ensure accurate and comprehensive files are maintained with proposals and clients.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Coordinate event production to include but not limited to: proposal writing, menu development, equipment and logistics, and Synergy data management, day of coordination and profitability goals.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Partner with Accounting/Billing to ensure compilation of accurate final billing and the collection of accounts receivable.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Attend and work Russell Morin Catering and Events’ functions as needed.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Maintain an exemplary level of confidentiality when dealing with sensitive issues.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Develop a holistic understanding of RMC&amp;E Core Values, Mission and Vision.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Perform other relevant duties as required.</li></ul>

<p><b>Desired Skills &amp; Experience</b></p>
<ul><li>Associate’s degree in Business Administration, Marketing, Hospitality or related industry. Bachelor’s degree preferred.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Minimum 3 years of progressive experience within the hospitality industry that includes quantifiable success within a sales role.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Proven experience with developing sales goals and objectives.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Comprehensive knowledge of off-site catering and event operations.</li></ul>
<ul><li>General interest in food, menu design and creative event development.</li></ul>
<p><b>Essential Qualifications:</b></p>
<p>To perform this job successfully, an individual must be able to perform each essential duty satisfactory. The requirements listed below are representative of the knowledge, skill, and/or ability required. Reasonable accommodations may be made to enable individuals with disabilities to perform the essential functions.</p>
<ul><li>Intermediate knowledge of financial management disciplines to include but not limited to – gross profitability, budget design and management, financial analysis, and ROI.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Ability to identify and resolve issues and alert executive leadership when needed.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Ability to function independently and intuitively in a fast-paced environment.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Excellent verbal and written communication skills with the ability to prepare and deliver clear, concise reports and presentations that are understandable by the target audience.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Highly organized with a strong attention to detail.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Demonstrated initiative, results oriented and entrepreneurial minded.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Strong project management experience and ability to manage on-going multiple priorities.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Ability to interact with diverse groups and foster a cooperative work environment.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Ability to manage confidential information and maintain its integrity.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Strong computer skills that include proficiency with MS Office and Google applications, Catering and CRM software.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Local travel is required.</li></ul>
