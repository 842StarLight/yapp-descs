<div><b>DESCRIPTION OF DUTIES
</b></div>
</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div><ul>
<li>Accurately translates, reviews, proofreads and edits in-game text and text files for context, grammar and punctuation issues
</li>
<li>Assists coordinators and teams during debug text-check process when assigned
</li>
<li>Interacts with other departments to discuss text bugs and related issues
</li>
<li>May approve or provide advice for context-related bugs in MCAT bug logs

<li>Follows compliance with strict Nintendo confidentiality and hardware/software security policies
</li>
<li>Provides critical analysis and feedback in a clear and concise way
</li>
<li>Mentors associate text specialists and testers regarding bug write-ups and text issues
</li>
<li>Delegates tasks to team members when functioning in a backup role
</li>
<li>Initiates and participates in team-improvement processes</li>
</ul></div>
