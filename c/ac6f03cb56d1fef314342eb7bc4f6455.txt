<h2>Design Build Lead Structural Engineer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>Schuff Steel (a subsidiary of DBM Global), the largest steel fabrication &amp; erection company in the US, is looking to hire a <b>Design Build Lead Structural Engineer</b>. This position offers high-visibility, growth opportunities, and exciting builds (previous clients include Apple, University of Phoenix Stadium, and Facebook).</p>
<p><b>
Our Employee Value Proposition</b></p>
<p>
Critical to the needs of our Engineering Department, the <b>Design Build Lead Structural Engineer</b> is responsible for all aspects of the company's Design Assist Group's engineering and sales efforts. The <b>Design Build Lead Structural Engineer</b> will focus on the continued structuring and operation of the engineering function as a cohesive unit; the prioritizing, conceptualizing and monitoring the day-to-day management of simultaneous projects related to conceptual design, analysis, interfacing and coordinating with sales and estimating persons; delivering optimized designs within time and budget constraints, and continuing to foster innovative solutions to the customer needs.
</p>
<p>The <b>Design Build Lead Structural Engineer</b> is a strong leader who fosters a team environment with a focus on providing solutions to a wide array of technical challenges while promoting a culture that empowers the team to think big. Supports and encourages "out of the box thinking" from the Group's individuals.
</p>
<p>In return, the Company will offer a competitive market based salary and comprehensive health insurance benefits. We are committed to professional development and growing our business. We are looking for a professional with a growth mindset who desires a progressive career move for a long-term period.
</p>
<p><b>Core Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Develop strategic planning for the direction and control of activities.</li>
<li>Formulate long-term objectives and standards of performance for the department.</li>
<li>Motivate staff and provide technical direction and guidance, as well as hands-on guidance and oversight, recognizing the role as that of both manager and support resource/mentor.</li>
<li>Must have a working knowledge of the basics of Erection Engineering for support of our Erection Engineering staff.
</li>
<li>Must be extremely knowledgeable in Seismic Design and Analysis as well as Wind Design.
</li>
<li>Ensure implementation and refinement as required of formal processes to support the engineering process.</li>
<li>Ensure that engineering is focused on meeting and exceeding the requirements of internal and external customers and outside EOR's.
</li>
<li>Most importantly, the final work product must be defendable whether it is the most basic or most innovative solution for a given project.</li>
<li>Work with Sales, Estimating and Executive Management to develop competitive job bids. Must be extremely knowledgeable in Seismic Design and Analysis as well as Wind Design and yet be able to articulate the most complex solutions to problems. Must also attend future team job interviews with GC's, CM's, etc. to assist in selling Design Assist projects.</li>
<li>Support preparation of pricing estimates on new projects as well as change orders on existing contracts.</li>
<li>Exercise good judgment and experience in working with estimating, sales, purchasing, steel fabrication and erection, equipment and transportation costs as required to support estimating and project execution efforts.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Education/Training</b></p>
<ul>
<li>A bachelor's degree in Engineering is required. BS Degree from an accredited college or university in Engineering. A MS in Structural Engineering (SE) is desirable. Advanced education may be a substitute for additional years of the required experience. Must have a SE license in AZ and/or CA or equal.</li>
<li>Must have experience in structural modeling with at least three of the following analytical software RAM, SAP, ETABS, STAAD, RISA and others.</li>
<li>A minimum of ten (10) years related industry structural engineering experience, with at least five (5) years in a management role.
</li>
</ul>
