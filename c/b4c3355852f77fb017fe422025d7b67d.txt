<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<ul>
<li> Delivering solutions to mid-market manufacturing and distribution clients and growing the Chicago market while developing next generation consultants.
</li>
<li> Enjoy being viewed and accountable as the Subject Matter Expert leading the local consulting charge in manufacturing and distribution.
</li>
<li> Earn client’s trust by immersing oneself into the client’s business vision and serving as a knowledgeable advisor who is focused on ensuring client’s success.</li>
<li> Secure a position of strategic advisor/participant in shaping the client’s business vision.</li>
<li> Serve as the “Chief Road-block Remover”, by anticipating potential risks and collaborating with client/internal teams to bring appropriate mitigation strategies into play.</li>
<li> Understand the client needs/cultural dynamics/competency levels and mentor/coach project team to fine-tune the approach to suit the client’s needs and ensure the success of the engagement.</li>
<li> Be accountable for the engagement’s scope, budget, timeline, process, resource plan and quality of delivery to ensure success.</li>
<li> Link the client requirements to standard solutions and service offerings.</li>
<li> Integrate project activities with other work at client sites and identify cross selling opportunities.</li>
<li> Opportunity leadership and/or active participation</li>
<li> Generate, identify, and qualify leads</li>
<li> Under the direction of senior manager or partner complete specified business development initiatives (i.e. marketing initiatives, referrals sources, prospecting etc.)</li>
<li> Participate as a member of pursuit teams for new sales opportunities and marketing activities.</li>
<li> Collaborate with leadership team to ensure continuous process improvement of service delivery in the region.</li>
<li> By way of being, serve as a leader in the organization.</li>
</ul>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Qualifications
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<ul>
<li> Bachelor’s Degree preferably in a related field.</li>
<li> 10+ years of prior experience in a consulting, technology or accounting firm.</li>
<li> Must have held a senior leadership position in mid to larger organizations with consulting as a service focused on a manufacturing and or distribution industry group.</li>
<li> Strong understanding of manufacturing operations and supporting information technology.</li>
<li> Prior experience with Shop Floor Optics preferred.</li>
<li> Experience with technology or consulting delivery in a manufacturing or distribution settings.</li>
<li> Must be a growth minded individual passionate about manufacturing and/or distribution.</li>
<li> Able to communicate a compelling vision and need for change that generates excitement, enthusiasm and commitment to the process.</li>
<li> Proven leader in staff selection and the development of teams.</li>
</ul>
