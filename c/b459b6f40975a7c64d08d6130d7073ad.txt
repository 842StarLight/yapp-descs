<h2>Manager- Sales Application Development</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<h4 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Company Description</b></h4>
<p>Microchip Technology Inc. is a leading provider of embedded control applications. Our product portfolio comprises general purpose and specialized 8-bit, 16-bit, and 32-bit microcontrollers, 32-bit microprocessors, field-programmable gate array (FPGA) products, a broad spectrum of high-performance linear, mixed-signal, power management, thermal management, radio frequency (RF), timing, safety, security, wired connectivity and wireless connectivity devices, as well as serial Electrically Erasable Programmable Read Only Memory (EEPROM), Serial Flash memories, Parallel Flash memories, and serial Static Random Access Memory (SRAM). We also license Flash-IP solutions that are incorporated in a broad range of products.</p>
<h4 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Job Description</b></h4>
<p></p>
<p>::</p>
<p>The Sales Application Development Manager will provide leadership and direction in design, implementation and support of business application systems at Microchip. This position is responsible for collaborating with business stakeholders and team members to deliver new products and functionality to support business processes in a timely fashion. This position is also responsible for recommending strategies for complex technical problems, assisting in application architecture, troubleshooting, and mentoring team members.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Key Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Drive optimal solution design collaborating with architects, application developers and cross-functional teams.</li>
<li>Provide technical direction and oversight on creation of applications from an end-to-end perspective.</li>
<li>Contribute to software design, solution development and recommendations.</li>
<li>Manage applications/development projects, or complex solution delivery initiatives.</li>
<li>Lead the team's technology and design decisions, such as software engineering practices and coding standards.</li>
<li>Assist in set up and manage development standards and tools.</li>
<li>Oversee the creation of documented designs, system specifications, deployment and unit test plans.</li>
<li>Analyze, develop and execute strategies for data and system integrations for acquisition activities.</li>
<li>Build and execute automated unit &amp; regression test cases.</li>
<li>Promote and deploy code using standard release management tools.</li>
<li>Oversee day to day development tasks performed by the product team.</li>
<li>Liaise with global development team members.</li>
<li>Ensure developers adhere to software quality standards and principles.</li>
<li>Seek and recommend opportunities for process improvement and automation.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<h4 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Job Requirements</b></h4>
<p></p>
<p><b>Skills and Experience:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor's Degree in Computer Science, Information Systems, or other related field required. Master’s degree is a plus.</li>
<li>Hands-on coding experience in C#, Java, or .NET Framework.</li>
<li>Minimum two (2) years' experience developing and managing enterprise application solutions.</li>
<li>Excellent knowledge of architectural standards, systems engineering, information risk and security guidelines.</li>
<li>Knowledge working with web services, REST API, JSON.</li>
<li>Advanced database application design and development.</li>
<li>Experience with system integration and release management.</li>
<li>Familiarity with testing process and methodologies.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>

<ul>

<li>Strong organizational, interpersonal and communication skills and ability to independently lead and manage projects.</li>
<li>Ability to lead customer design meetings with senior management, design team, and end users.</li>
<li>Customer relationship skills including the ability to discover true requirements, underlying feature requests, and recommend alternative technical and business approaches</li>
<li>Must be culturally compatible with Microchip’s value based structure and style and must have a good work ethic in a collaborative environment.</li>
