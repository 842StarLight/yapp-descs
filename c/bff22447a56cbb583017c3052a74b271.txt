<h2>Inside Account Executive</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">Founded by a former English teacher, NoRedInk's mission is to help students unlock their writing potential. Our learning engine engages students through an interest-based, adaptive curriculum and provides them with real-time feedback, guided peer reviews, and self-reflection exercises to more effectively improve their skills at an accelerated pace. Our platform also provides educators and school administrators with actionable insights on how they can most meaningfully support the academic progress of their student body. We are one of the fastest-growing ed-tech companies in the country; driven by our steadfast aim to help millions of students strengthen their writing skills and, in turn, their future prospects, we have already spread to 50% of US school districts.
<br><br>
To further advance our mission, we are looking to find a talented Inside Account Executive to join our team. In this role you will work with teachers, principals, and district administrators across the US to build consensus and trust, leveraging your experience selling SaaS products, knack for building grassroots support, and talent for closing deals to help upend the traditional sales model in K-12 schools.
<br><br>
If you want to be a part of a collaborative team and have a key role at a fast-growing company, read on!
<br><br>
<b>What You Will Achieve:</b><br>
<ul>
