<h2>Member Financial Counselor II</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div><h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>POSITION SUMMARY</b></h2></div>
<br>
<div><div><p>
As a successful Member Financial Counselor II, you will make contact with members that have collection and delinquent accounts, making follow-up telephone calls and/or late payment reminders. Determine the reason for the past due condition of loans and develop a plan to bring the loans current. Make recommendations on what actions that should be taken if the loan is in default. Establish and implement effective monitoring systems for bankruptcies and repossessions. Create correspondence for members and create/update various reports for the Members Financial Counseling Department using word processing and spreadsheet software.</p></div></div>
<p></p>
<br>
<div><p><b>
ESSENTIAL FUNCTIONS</b></p></div>
<br>
<div><div><ul>
<li>
Work active collection accounts and contact delinquent accounts either by telephone or by mail correspondence and determine the reason for default on loans</li>
<li>
Take appropriate action to bring the account current, including extension agreements, loan workouts, or setting up payment arrangements</li>
<li>
Determine adequacy of alternative payment arrangements based on credit union policy guidelines Take and maintain detailed written accounts of all communications and/or actions made in attempt to collect on delinquent accounts</li>
<li>
Generate collection notices and right to cures as they are required on delinquent accounts</li>
<li>
Document in the collection notes, payments received and forward payments to the Member Support department for processing</li>
<li>
Prepare loan corrections and account audit reports as needed</li>
<li>
Maintain a basic knowledge of Bankruptcy and Repossession laws and regulations; ensure compliance by attending webinars and seminars</li>
<li>
Maintain and update repossession status and vehicle sales reports</li>
<li>
Monitor and document attorney and agencies collection efforts and progress</li>
<li>
Enhance members self-esteem, cross-sell products and services that will help the member and provide additional financial counseling as needed</li>
<li>
Keep all member and credit union business strictly confidential</li>
<li>
Provide back-up as needed to the Member Service Department</li>
<li>
Perform other duties as assigned</li>
<li>
Ability to work evening and weekend hours as needed</li>
</ul></div></div>
