<b>Job Overview:</b><br>
<br>
Leveraging Oracle’s position as a market leader, we are looking for a Senior Business Development / Outbound Product Manager to provide expertise in all aspects of Internet Intelligence solutions.<br>
<br>
As a member of the Internet Intelligence team within Oracle Cloud Infrastructure, this seasoned PM/Business Development professional will act as a technical specialist, engaging with clients, and with Oracle sales team to understand their business requirements and identifying applications for the Internet Intelligence suite of products.<br>
<br>
You will join an elite team of data scientists and security researchers that analyzes and monitors global Internet security and performance event.<br>
<br>
Oracle’s Internet Intelligence team is dedicated to reporting and covering issues such as country-level connectivity statistics, transit shifts, and security threats that impact the performance of the global internet. We deliver the resulting intelligence to Fortune 50 and public sector customers in the form of derived data products and applications. We help our customers detect cyber actors, analyze traffic flows, identify traffic chokepoints, and understand internet topology. Our solutions enable our customers to secure their edge, protect brand image, gain competitive intelligence, and protect critical infrastructure.<br>
<br>
The successful candidate has the ability to pick up new product technologies very quickly, and has excellent verbal, written, and presentation communication skills. In this role, you will engage with clients, and through presentations, demonstrations and benchmarks, educate them on our products, and provide support to the sales team and customers throughout the sales cycle. This individual should be self-directed and independent, yet also excel as a team player. The ideal candidate will have five years of vendor (sales &amp; marketing) experience in the technology space. A BS degree or equivalent is required.<br>
<br>
<br>
This highly rewarding role will offer the opportunity for you to leverage your knowledge and skills with internet performance/data and work in unison with Oracle’s best field sales executives, solving complex customer problems and translating customer requirements into deliverable technical solutions. A critical role in driving mindshare of Oracle as an industry leader in Internet Intelligence, a true differentiator for Oracle. Oracle’s Internet Intelligence team is dedicated to reporting and covering issues such as country-level connectivity statistics, transit shifts, and security threats that impact the performance of the global internet. We deliver the resulting intelligence to Fortune 50 and public sector customers in the form of derived data products and applications.<br>
