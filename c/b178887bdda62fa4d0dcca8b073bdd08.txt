<h2>Managing Director - Technology Enablement</h2>

<p><b>Managing Director – Technology Enablement</b></p>
<p>
SLALOM | Phoenix</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
Slalom Consulting is currently seeking an entrepreneurial and progressive leader to refine the technology vision and lead technology teams as we race to become a $50M office and the preeminent enterprise technology build partner in Phoenix, AZ.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
Slalom is a purpose-driven consulting firm that helps companies solve business problems and build for the future, with solutions spanning business advisory, customer experience, technology, and analytics. We partner with companies to push the boundaries of what’s possible—together.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
Founded in 2001 and headquartered in Seattle, WA, Slalom has organically grown to over 6,500 employees. We were named one of Fortune’s 100 Best Companies to Work For in 2018 and are regularly recognized by our employees as a best place to work. You can find us in 28 cities across the U.S., U.K., and Canada.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Responsibilities<br>
</b></p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>Accountable for technology P&amp;L (software engineering, next-generation infrastructure, data &amp; analytics) establishing go to market strategies, driving solutions, recruiting, practice management, and operational aspects of our business.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<ul><li>Sets and leads overall strategic mission and vision for the Phoenix Technology Enablement as well as Data &amp; Analytics.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Drives practice direction, offerings, large and complex solutions and collaboration with internal Slalom technology partners (Slalom build, et al)</li></ul>
<ul><li>Oversees staffing, retention, and career management for direct reports; providing meaningful and frequent feedback
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Supports the GM’s management of overall market operation</li></ul>
<ul><li>Works with Marketing Manager to shape and execute on technology marketing strategy to increase brand recognition by attending various industry or functional meetings, forums, publications, sponsor/speak at industry events and/or client workshops etc.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Oversees recruiting process consistent with the office diversity goals and is ultimately responsible for candidate selections</li></ul>
<ul><li>Develops collaborative relationships with other departments (e.g., HR, IT, F&amp;A, Slalom Build, Slalom Field Teams, Office Operations) to ensure support for internal team, consultants and clients</li></ul>
<ul><li>Champions knowledge management across technology practices. Establishes processes and direction for the archiving of client working materials and post project completion marketing/sales collateral</li></ul>
<ul><li>Maintains a strong network in the market place to promote the Slalom brand</li></ul>
<ul><li>Enhances the Company’s culture, and exemplifies our shared values on a day-to-day basis</li></ul>
<ul><li>Champions continuous technical education and growth for all team members.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Actively participates in multi-market technology leadership summits, and other national forums.</li></ul>
<p></p>

Qualifications<br>
</b></p>
<p></p>
<br>
<ul><li>15+ years management/leadership experience</li></ul>
<ul><li>Previous P&amp;L experience for a $15M or greater technology consulting practice</li></ul>
<ul><li>Excellent negotiation, conflict management, problem solving and decision-making skills</li></ul>
<ul><li>Proven track record of client/employee satisfaction</li></ul>
<ul><li>Ability to communicate effectively and tactfully across all levels of the organization</li></ul>
<ul><li>Servant Leader<br>
</li></ul>
