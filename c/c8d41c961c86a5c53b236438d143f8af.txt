<p><b>Position Description:</b></p>
<p>Working as an Account Director, you will have senior responsibility to develop, implement and maintain a plan to grow revenue from new accounts in the Houston metro. The Director of Consulting Services will be responsible for managing the entire lifecycle of the sales, staffing delivery and member management processes for their accounts.</p>
<p><b>Your future duties and responsibilities:</b></p>
<p>Build and maintain strong relationships and business with target new accounts in line with plan. Ensures service delivery excellence (including application of CPMF). Participate in the delivery of client engagements to achieve targeted revenue and profitability</p>
<p>The Director will:</p>
<ul><li>Be accountable for service delivery to our clients</li></ul>
<ul><li>Develop a strong pipeline of opportunities; qualify, pursue and close business within targeted clients in the Houston metro</li></ul>
<ul><li>Lead proposals and evolve solution offerings</li></ul>
<ul><li>Educate clients and partners on CGI offerings, capabilities and opportunities</li></ul>
<ul><li>Lead a team of professionals – both direct reports and via global delivery where applicable</li></ul>
<ul><li>Ensure success of engagements using global delivery and vendor partners</li></ul>
<ul><li>Manage client satisfaction in partnership with account management teams</li></ul>
<ul><li>Apply delivery and customer management best practices</li></ul>
<ul><li>Apply management best practices such as succession planning, member performance reviews, and finance management</li></ul>
<ul><li>Contribute to business strategy and budget planning</li></ul>
<ul><li>Manage profitability of engagements</li></ul>
<p><b>Required qualifications to be successful in this role:</b></p>
<ul><li>10+ years of minimum IT and Consulting Services experience</li></ul>
<ul><li>Results oriented business leader with solid understanding of budgets, forecasting, planning, P&amp;L’s, and complex pricing models</li></ul>
<ul><li>Business development including strategic sales planning</li></ul>
<ul><li>Strategy and business management</li></ul>
<ul><li>Delivery experience</li></ul>
<ul><li>Client engagement management and relationship building</li></ul>
<ul><li>People management</li></ul>
<ul><li>A keen, flexible and aggressive intellect; superior conceptual thinking as well as analytical and problem solving skills</li></ul>
<ul><li>Strong communication skills, both verbal and written, at all levels</li></ul>
<ul><li>Proven record of creating and maintaining business partnerships and relationships</li></ul>
<ul><li>Strong decision making, judgment and analytical skills</li></ul>
<ul><li>Demonstrated ability to effectively collaborate with cross-functional virtual teams</li></ul>
