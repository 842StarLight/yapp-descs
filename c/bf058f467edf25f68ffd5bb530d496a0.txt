<h2>Clinical Account Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Who We Are: </b></p>
<p>What a dynamic and exciting time to be in the Behavioral Health profession. Our society and employers are recognizing more than ever the critical need to support people with behavioral health and work/life challenges. As a result, Ulliance is experiencing rapid growth and is in search of top-notch Clinical Account Managers to join our team of highly engaged staff. We are passionate about helping people and providing exceptional service that makes a difference. Do you have the right stuff; positive energy, highly engaged, have an abundance of intellectual-emotional intelligence, intrinsically cares and are committed to going above and beyond when no one is looking?</p>
<p>Consider joining our mission: To Improve the Lives of the People We Serve!</p>
<p>Ulliance is an International behavioral health and human resources service company headquartered in Troy MI. For nearly 30 years, Ulliance has been a leading provider of Employee Assistance (EAP), Wellness, Training, Organizational-Leadership Development, Career Transition Services, Professional Health Monitoring and Crisis Management across the globe serving nearly one million people.</p>
<p>If you are looking for a “job”, please do not apply. If you would like to be part of a healthy, winning team, are inspired to learn more and to see if Ulliance would be a good fit, we are waiting to meet you.</p>
<p><b>Specifics: </b></p>
<p>The Account Manager serves as the main point of contact with our clients. You will be the face</p>
<p>of Ulliance, and we are looking for someone who can form relationships with busy HR</p>
<p>professionals in a variety of industries- from manufacturing to government. Your goal? To be</p>
<p>their go-to for knowledge and resources when it comes to employee issues ranging from</p>
<p>personal issues, substance abuse to workplace conflict.</p>
<p>For anyone interested in improving the lives of the people they serve, this role offers a great</p>
<p>opportunity to practice a broad spectrum of skills in a supportive environment. The Account</p>
<p>Manager position is ideal for a variety of backgrounds ranging from behavioral sciences to</p>
<p>business. In particular, candidates with the following experience are encouraged to apply:</p>
<ul><li>Client Service: Experience dealing with and keeping clients satisfied in multiple</li></ul>
<p>capacities across a variety of industries.</p>
<ul><li>Behavioral Health: Our employees are ready to provide support when trauma and crisis</li></ul>
<p>strikes. Our Account Managers develop, oversee, and provide critical/traumatic incident</p>
<p>debriefings as well as formal referrals to our client companies.</p>
<ul><li>Marketing: We are constantly looking for new and exciting ways to reach our clients and</li></ul>
<p>end users. You will be developing and maintaining strong promotional plans for each</p>
<p>account.</p>
<ul><li>Communication: Don’t mind the spotlight? You’ll be providing orientation, training and</li></ul>
<p>other on-site support to our client companies. We need someone who can balance that</p>
<p>with a great deal of tact as you will also be working with companies during times of</p>
<p>crisis after critical incidents.</p>
<ul><li>Sales: While this role does not involve cold calling or direct sales, anyone who has had</li></ul>
<p>to soft-sell or upsell services before will be at an advantage.</p>
<p>Finally, we are looking for people with demonstrated independence and a proven track record</p>
<p>of service. Minimally, this would include a Master’s degree in Psychology, Social Work, Human</p>
<p>Resources or Business and at least three to five years full time post-graduate or equivalent</p>
<p>experience. Candidates holding a CEAP, PHR/SPHR or State of Michigan licensure/certification</p>
<p>preferred. As you will be helping our clients tackle challenging people issues, supervisory</p>
<p>experience is preferred. While you will typically work a standard work week, you do need to</p>
<p>have the ability to work flexible hours at times as well as engage in travel within Southeastern</p>
<p>Michigan area and out-of-state travel on occasion.</p>
<p>Objectives:</p>
<p>Curious to know what a day in the life of an Account Manager at Ulliance is like? You don’t need</p>
<p>to wait until you are hired to find out the contributions you will be making.</p>
<p>1. Client retention: Deliver best in class services so that there is no question in our client’s</p>
<p>mind about the value of the EAP at renewal time. You will also prepare and conduct</p>
<p>quarterly, semi-annual and/or annual service utilization reviews.</p>
<p>2. EAP Promotion: We can only help those that know who we arel You will work with</p>
<p>client organizations to promote EAP services through UR lunches, mailings, health fairs</p>
<p>and other marketing campaigns.</p>
<p>3. Management Consultations and Formal Referrals: Provide consultation to our HR</p>
<p>account contacts on problematic management and employee issues as well as critical</p>
<p>incidents.</p>
<p>4. Crisis Management: When tragedy or unexpected violence touches an organization, we</p>
<p>are there. You will advise accounts regarding emergency employee-related issues (e.g.,</p>
<p>an employee threatens to harm a co-worker or is threatening suicide).</p>
<p>5. New Client Implementation: First impressions are everything- so it will be your</p>
<p>responsibility to help our new clients launch EAP services internally without a hitch. This</p>
<p>will include manager and employee orientations to our services as well as promotional</p>
<p>activities.</p>
<p>6. Cross-selling: At Ulliance, we provide answers to all of the challenges our HR clients</p>
<p>experience. You will have the opportunity to generate leads for ancillary services from</p>
<p>our product line that address client pain points.</p>
<p><b>SUPERVISION EXERCISED</b></p>
<p>None.</p>
<p><b>TYPICAL DUTIES AND RESPONSBILITIES</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Orients to new account with Ulliance sales/marketing personnel.</li>
<li>Coordinates account start-ups and new account implementation process. Includes on-site orientations.</li>
<li>Develops, maintains, enhances and retains strong relationships with account contacts.</li>
<li>Develops, maintains and enhances a strong promotional plan with each account.</li>
<li>Provides orientations and other on-site support at client companies.</li>
<li>Provides high quality, informative management consultations</li>
<li>Provides consultation to account contacts on problematic management and employee issues and critical incidents.</li>
<li>Advises accounts regarding emergency employee-related issues (e.g., an employee threatens to harm a co-worker).</li>
<li>Provides oversight and coordination of formal referrals.</li>
<li>Maintains up-to-date clinical records on management consultations and formal referrals.</li>
<li>Provides ongoing consultation and support to HR contact during formal referral processes.</li>
<li>Effectively utilizes assessment tools (service evaluation and risk assessment) to obtain feedback and foster improved customer service.</li>
<li>Maintains appropriate history and files electronically (using the Ulliance standard account management software program) and in hard copy as necessary on account.</li>
<li>Advises the Ulliance Business Office and Management staff of changes in contact personnel, sites, etc.</li>
<li>Prepares and conducts quarterly, semi-annual and/or annual utilization reviews in person or by phone.</li>
<li>Obtains annual renewal of the Ulliance Service Agreement from account, if applicable.</li>
<li>Conducts roundtable webinars with client companies.</li>
<li>Writes newsletter articles.</li>
<li>Proficiently employs the Ulliance established software and hardware.</li>
<li>Other duties as assigned or directed.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>EXPERIENCE, TRAINING, KNOWLEDGE, SKILLS &amp; ABILITIES</b></p>
<p><b>A. Required Minimum Qualifications</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Have a Master’s degree in Psychology, Social Work, or closely related behavioral science discipline field; <b>AND</b>
</li>
<li>Have a least five (5) years full time post-graduate or equivalent experience.</li>
<li>Have a State of Michigan licensure/certification.</li>
<li>Have the ability to engage in travel within Metropolitan Detroit area and out-of-state travel based on Client Company needs.</li>
<li>Possess a valid motor vehicle operator’s license.</li>
<li>Have the Ability to work flexible schedules/hours.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>B. Additional Desirable Qualifications</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Previous leadership/supervisory experience</li>
<li>Previous EAP account management/business-related/human resources experience.</li>
<li>Knowledge of the principles and practices of employee assistance and managerial consultative services.</li>
<li>Effective oral and written communication skills.</li>
<li>The ability to cope with difficult and/or urgent situations requiring immediate decisions.</li>
<li>The ability to establish and maintain effective working relationships with human resources personnel and other business-related staff.</li>
<li>The ability to decipher reports, compile data and present it to client companies.</li>
<li>Public speaking experience.</li>
<li>Experience in providing training and workshops or seminars.</li>
</ul>
