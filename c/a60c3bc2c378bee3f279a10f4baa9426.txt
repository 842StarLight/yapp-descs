<h2>Commissioned Salesperson</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div><p><b>$1000 Full Time Hiring Bonus!!!</b></p></div>
<br><div>
<p><b>SUMMARY:
</b></p>
<p>Sells flooring, furniture, bedding, appliances and electronics while providing excellent customer service by performing the following duties.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>ESSENTIAL DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES:
</b></p>
<p>Greets customer on the sales floor in a personable and friendly manner and determines make, type, and quality of product desired. Always shows respect to the customer, does NOT use aggressive or “High Pressure” sales techniques.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p>Suggests flooring/furniture products that will complement customer's home and other furnishings. Discusses types and quality of products to ensure customer education and satisfaction.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p>Strives to attain sales goals while providing exceptional customer service at all times.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p>Accurately prepares sales slip or sales contract based on customer’s intended purchase.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p>Arranges for delivery of merchandise to customer if needed; or assists with load out of product into customer’s vehicle according to Marden’s safe loading practices. Asks for guidance from Department or Store Management Team as needed.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p>Works with Department or Store Management Team to resolve customer complaints regarding delivery of damaged or incorrect merchandise to ensure customer satisfaction.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p>Unloads product, moves product and sets up displays as directed by the Department or Store Management Team.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p>Assembles product for display/sale as directed by the Department or Store Management Team.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p>Use company internal website “Web Link – Furniture/Flooring Sections” to keep up to date on merchandise updates, and ensure compliance with best operational practices.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p>Keeps current on such activities as product arrivals, advertising, new products and trends and pricing to assure that Marden's maintains a competitive position.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p>Maintains good knowledge and understanding in-stock product.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p>Follows all Marden’s safety policies and strives for clean, safe working conditions in the department; including performing housekeeping tasks as needed to maintain a clean showroom .<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p>Perform other duties assigned by the Department or Store Management Team.</p>
</div>
