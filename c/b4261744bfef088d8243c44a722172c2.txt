<h2>Account Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Primary Purpose:
</b></p>
<p>Directs and coordinates office staff to support Service and Solutions operations for the Rocky Mountain Service and Solutions Division – includes direct oversight of Service Coordinators, Contracts, and Billing functions and indirect oversight of AP, AR, and payroll functions.
</p>
<p><b>Principal Duties and Accountabilities:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Supervises the daily operations of the service coordinators, contracts, and billing staff in the Service Department; includes staffing the department, establishing and communicating work assignments; training/cross-training staff, and managing the performance of the staff.
</li>
<li>Partners with the Service/Rapid Response Operations Manager and Vice President of Operations - Service and Solutions, to establish objective criteria / metrics for measuring department and individual team member effectiveness. Communicates department goals and objectives to subordinates, and assists each individual team member in identifying and achieving individual goals that support department objectives.</li>
<li>Proactively monitors the productivity metrics for the accounting functions / processes to ensure that desired results are achieved; makes recommendations to improve department effectiveness, as necessary.</li>
<li>Tracks invoice postings throughout the month and ensures that monthly goals are met. Identifies obstacles as to why goals are not met and works with the Service/Rapid Response Operations Manager to resolve issues.</li>
<li>Runs monthly reports to ensure that all work orders are processed. Follows up with Billing Administrators regarding any outstanding work orders. Also investigates low-margin work orders and recommends resolutions if required.</li>
<li>Troubleshoots and investigates any accounting system issues / problems. These may be related to the system s functionality, processes, or reports. Provides documentation to, and coordinates with the Information Technology Department, and follows up to resolve issues. Communicates status of issues and resolutions to Service/Rapid Response Operations Manager and division Vice President.</li>
<li>Continuously seeks process improvement through SAMPro reports and technology. Holds quarterly process improvement meetings with appropriate staff.</li>
<li>Establishes and maintains all billing rate fee structures in the accounting system. Follows up with Project Managers / Account Managers when rates are expiring and updates the system accordingly.</li>
<li>Backs up other team members as necessary, by performing billing, contract administration, or other administrative duties to support the Service and Solutions Division. This may include answering telephones, issuing work orders and purchase orders, etc.
</li>
<li>Interfaces with clients and team members regularly to resolve contract or invoice discrepancies.</li>
<li>Assists Collections Administrator or others in the Accounting Department in resolving outstanding invoices.</li>
<li>Reviews Service Department payroll reports for accuracy.</li>
<li>Prepares various daily, weekly, monthly, annual, or job-to-date reports.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Job Scope:
</b></p>
<p>This position is both a supervisory and individual contributor role. The individual in this position will have supervisory responsibility for a small team of Accounting / Billing and Service Coordinator staff within the regional Service Division. Additionally, the individual in this position will perform some of the same duties as the other team members as necessary to maintain department efficiency.
</p>
<p><b>Education:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor s Degree in Business Administration or Accounting preferred; however, equivalent combinations of training and experience will be considered in lieu of a degree.</li>
<li>High School Diploma / GED required.
</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Experience:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Must have minimum of 3 years of experience in accounting-related function, such as Accounts Receivable, Accounts Payable, Billing, etc., 5 years experience preferred.</li>
<li>At least 1 year of supervisory experience is required, preferably in an accounting function.</li>
<li>Must have experience with accounting system software
</li>
<li>Prior experience in a building service industry (such as HVAC) is strongly preferred</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Knowledge, skills, and abilities</b>:
</p>
<ul>
<li>Solid leadership skills – ability to achieve team results through effective motivational techniques, fostering excellent professional relationships, etc.
</li>
<li>Understanding of project accounting and administration / auditing procedures and techniques</li>
<li>Ability to facilitate strong collaboration within a multi-function team environment.</li>
<li>Capable of producing team results in a fast-paced and flexible work environment.
</li>
<li>Must possess the ability to adapt to changing priorities and lead a team under the same circumstances.</li>
<li>Keen attention to detail and proven ability to consistently follow through.</li>
<li>Excellent customer service skills.</li>
<li>Strong communication skills – verbal and written.</li>
<li>Ability to proactively analyze information or results, which are occasionally ambiguous, and resolve problems – at times using creative methods.
</li>
<li>Strong sense of initiative, perseverance, and drive for results.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Physical and/or travel demands:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>This position requires frequent sitting, use of keyboard/monitor, and telephone.
</li>
<li>Some standing required, with very infrequent reaching, stooping, and pulling/lifting required.
</li>
<li>None-rare travel required.</li>
</ul>
