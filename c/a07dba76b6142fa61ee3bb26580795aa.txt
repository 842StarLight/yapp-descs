<h2>Office Clerk Assistant</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Office Clerk Job Responsibilities:</p>
<p>Assists in any office and clerical duties needed.</p>
<ul>
<li>Posts customer payments by recording cash, checks, and credit card transactions.</li>
<li>Posts revenues by verifying and entering transactions form lock box and local deposits.</li>
<li>Updates receivables by totaling unpaid invoices.</li>
<li>Maintains records by microfilming invoices, debits, and credits.</li>
<li>Verifies validity of account discrepancies by obtaining and investigating information from sales, trade promotions, customer service departments, and from customers;</li>
<li>Resolves valid or authorized deductions by entering adjusting entries.</li>
<li>Resolves invalid or unauthorized deductions by following pending deductions procedures.</li>
<li>Resolves collections by examining customer payment plans, payment history, credit line; coordinating contact with collections department.</li>
<li>Summarizes receivables by maintaining invoice accounts; coordinating monthly transfer to accounts receivable account; verifying totals; preparing report.</li>
<li>Protects organization's value by keeping information confidential.</li>
<li>Updates job knowledge by participating in educational opportunities.</li>
<li>Accomplishes accounting and organization mission by completing related results as needed</li>
<li>Completes all sales and billing reports on schedule.</li>
<li>Responsible for any other task assigned by ownership/management of company.</li>
</ul>
<p>Office Clerk Skills and Qualifications:</p>
<p>Organization, Accounting, Data Entry Skills, General Math Skills, Financial Software, Analyzing Information , Attention to Detail, Thoroughness, Reporting Research Results, Verbal Communication</p>
<p>Experience with Microsoft Office including Excel is required.</p>
<p>Job Type: Full-time</p>
<p>Salary: $10.00 to $13.00 /hour</p>

</ul>
