<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Qualifications
</b></h2>
<ul>
<li>Active TS/SCI with Polygraph level security clearance
</li>
<li>BS degree in a computer or system science discipline from an accredited college or university and one (1) to two (2) years of related experience, or any equivalent combination of education, experience, training, and certifications.
</li>
<li>This position will involve detailed knowledge of audio/video technology (audio/video generation, distribution and display) and current ANSI/IEEE format knowledge for both analog and digital systems, and their deployment (broadband cable, NTSC or HDTV baseband, ISDN, etc.) to customer premises.
</li>
<li>This position requires the execution of good engineering practices that detail the overall system architecture, detailed line (with breakdown of signal and test point values) and rack elevation drawings and to/from cable list.
</li>
<li>The employee can make independent decisions that deal with the daily schedule and work to be accomplished. Issues requiring monetary approval have to go up the reporting chain. The prioritization of projects and the daily schedule of the Engineering team is determined by the manager. Only urgent or high profile projects may be directed down to the capacity team.
</li>
<li>The employee has full discretion and independent judgment as it relates to their project. As long as he or she remains within budget and scope the manager of the engineering team has full autonomy as it relates to the team and its resources. Outside of the project framework the employee does not have approving authority for major monetary decisions.
</li>
<li>Communicate with customers, sales staff, or marketing staff to determine customer needs
</li>
<li>Coordinate network operations, maintenance, repairs, or upgrades.
</li>
<li>Design, build, or operate equipment configuration prototypes, including video hardware, software, servers, or server operation systems
</li>
<li>Develop conceptual, logical, or physical A/V network designs.
</li>
<li>Estimate time and materials needed to complete projects.
</li>
<li>Evaluate A/V designs to determine if requirements are met efficiently and effectively.
</li>
<li>Monitor and analyze A/V system performance and data input/output reports to detect problems, identify inefficient use of computer resources, or perform capacity planning.
</li>
<li>Participate in A/V technology upgrade or expansion projects, including installation of hardware and software and integration testing.
</li>
<li>Prepare or monitor project schedules, budgets, or cost control systems.
</li>
<li>Prepare detailed A/V specifications, including diagrams, charts, equipment configurations, and recommended technologies.
</li>
<li>Research and test new or modified hardware or software products to determine performance and interoperability.
</li>
<li>Communicate with vendors to gather information about products, to alert them to future needs, to resolve problems, or to address systems maintenance issues.
</li>
<li>Coordinate network or design activities with designers of associated networks.
</li>
<li>Develop or maintain project-reporting systems.
</li>
<li>Develop network related documentation.
</li>
<li>Explain design specifications to integration teams or install technicians.
</li>
<li>Prepare design presentations and proposals for staff or customers.
