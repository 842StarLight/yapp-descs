<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Role Overview and Responsibilities
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div>
<b>Summary: </b>Analyzes assembly drawings, specifications, proposals, and other documentation to prepare time, cost, and labor estimates or products, projects, or services by performing the following duties. Provide guidance and leadership to direct report employees that will support the strategic vision of the company.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
<b>
Essential Duties and Responsibilities </b>include the following. Other duties may be assigned.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<ul>
<li>
Review quotation package and collectively with engineering, documentation control, and materials develop a log of issues preventing timely and accurate quotations to the customer.</li>
<li>
Ensure proposals are complaint to FAR/DFAR; including certified cost or pricing, small business plans, etc. and support audits, as required.</li>
<li>
As assigned, communicate with assigned customer contact to collect necessary data to complete closure of quotation.</li>
<li>
Overall responsibility for the development and collection of internal manufacturing costs, including but not limited to Materials, Labor and Overhead.
</li>
<li>Support through project management and scheduling all customer’s request for quotation including new and existing customer’s opportunities.</li>
<li>
Ensure quotations are completed with the highest level of accuracy.</li>
<li>
Ensure quotations are delivered to the appropriate sales channel with sufficient time to meet customer due dates.</li>
<li>
Support the development of final customer quotations excluding any decisions related to margin or profit. Business Development Managers, Commercial Director, Program Managers, or VP/GM will provide direction on margin requirements.</li>
<li>
Support supplemental documentation requirements by the customer for cost details including material quotations, excess analysis, labor and overhead breakdowns, labor reviews, and any other documentation required to support the closure of an opportunity.</li>
<li>
Log, Monitor and Track all North American quote opportunities within all TT factories globally. Document quotation status and activity on a weekly basis and report findings to responsible parties. Maintain an ongoing activity log and elevate to management as necessary, to ensure quotations are completed per customer commitment.</li>
<li>
Manage and provide direction/priorities to B&amp;P employees</li>
</ul>
