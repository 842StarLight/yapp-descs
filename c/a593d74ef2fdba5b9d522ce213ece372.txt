<h2>Client Services Manager - Digital Account Management</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Job Title
</b></p>Client Services Manager - Digital Account Management
<p></p>
<p><b>Job Description
</b></p>
<p>The Client Services Manager acts as an internal sales representative responsible for the conceiving, managing and executing online marketing initiatives and management of all advertising programs and associated revenue pre- and post-sale. This includes media plan creation, campaign implementation, optimization, reporting, program retention and renewal, and ensuring prompt and proper resolution of advertiser business issues.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Essential Job Functions
</b></p>
<p><b>20%
</b></p>
<p>Respond to RFPs, develop media plans in line with strategic marketing goals.
</p>
<p><b>50%
</b></p>
<p>Execute and implement advertising campaigns to ensure delivery and performance. Includes taking a leadership role to coordinate efforts between other cross-functional teams as necessary to implement custom elements). Position interfaces with Ad Product and Creative.
</p>
<p><b>10%
</b></p>
<p>Work with Ad Operations to ensure clients’ ads are trafficked, monitored, and optimized.
</p>
<p><b>10%
</b></p>
<p>Maximize campaign performance and company revenue by coordinating reports to further increase success rate and renew/grow current business.
</p>
<p><b>10%
</b></p>
<p>Update contract management system with campaign change orders (campaign delays, optimizations, etc.) and alert team of changes that will affect revenue.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Minimum Qualifications and Job Requirements </b>| <i>All must be met to be considered.
</i></p>
<p>Education:
</p>
<p>4-year degree required; marketing or business degree preferred. Additional digital marketing or sales experience may substitute for education on a year-for-year basis.
</p>
<p>Experience:
 </p>
<p>Minimum of 2 years of marketing, sales and/or account management experience; including one year of digital advertising experience. Project management experience a plus.
</p>
<p>Specific Knowledge, Skills and Abilities:
</p>
<p>· Possess excellent organizational, interpersonal, and influential written and verbal communication skills. Ability to quickly form relationships usually by telephone or email.
</p>
<p>· Ability to collaborate cross-functionally; ability to exercise judgment and flexibility in balancing program requirements, milestones, and keeping people and projects moving on schedule.
</p>
<p>· High attention to detail. Strong analytical, quantitative and strategic problem solving. Excellent grammar, spelling and proofreading skills.
</p>
<p>· Positive and confident disposition when responding to frequent deadlines for multiple customers (internal and external).
</p>
<p>· High degree of proficiency in MS Office products. Ability to use databases. Excellent working knowledge of Excel, Word, PowerPoint, Outlook and the Internet.
</p>
<p>· Demonstrated ability to quickly build positive team relationships.
</p>
<p>· Be able to think strategically and act tactically.
</p>
