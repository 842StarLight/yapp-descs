<h2>Manufacturing Supervisor</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>G.S. PRECISION INC</b></p>
<p><b>
 Manufacturing Supervisor</b></p>
<p>
G.S. Precision Inc, a global leader in the precision machining field has two openings for Manufacturing Supervisor's. One is in our Brattleboro VT plant the other in our Keene NH Plant.</p>
<p>
 Supervises production employees in a manufacturing environment: plans and assigns work, implements and enforces company policies and procedures and recommends improvements in production methods, equipment, operating procedures and working conditions.</p>
<p><b>
Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul><li>Manage the activities of the production floor by following established procedures and approved processes. Supervise all production operations such as machining, processing and assembly.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Plan, schedule, and coordinate departmental activities to fulfill sales orders, meet inventory requirements, and deliver manufactured products in a timely fashion.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Balances quality, productivity, cost, safety and morale to achieve positive results in all areas. Works to continuously improve in all areas.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Ensures effective employee relations. Provides employee coaching and development. Motivates employees and assists with problem solving. Makes employment decisions. Resolves employee issues through problem resolution.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Manages departmental performance measures, including visual controls and provides regular progress reports to manager.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Tracks employee performance, attendance, and time keeping. Conducts performance reviews, discipline, accident investigations, etc. Use technical expertise to train employees and develop most efficient methods of production.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Responsible for staffing the department and planning workload.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Work harmoniously with other departments and functional units in the coordination of work to drive productivity, providing a good example to employees.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Performs other duties as assigned.</li></ul>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Job Specifications:</b></p>
<ul><li>High School Diploma and atleast 5 years Manufacturing Supervisory experience</li></ul>
<ul><li>Knowledge of manufacturing methods, techniques, equipment, production scheduling and operations</li></ul>
<ul><li>Ability to read and interpret drawings and customer specifications</li></ul>
<ul><li>Strong interpersonal skills, communication skills</li></ul>
<ul><li>Attention to detail</li></ul>
<ul><li>Time management, multitasking</li></ul>
<ul><li>Problem solving and analysis skills</li></ul>
<ul><li>Knowledge of OSHA safety requirements</li></ul>
<ul><li>Computer skills, MS Office, Manufacturing software, etc.</li></ul>
<p>
Along with a competitive wage, we offer medical, dental and vision insurance, company paid life and disability insurance, and a 401(k) plan with Company match as well as Paid Holidays and Vacation time. G.S. Precision is a Company that recognizes and rewards those people who contribute to our success.</p>
