<p>
Maxxon Corporation is recognized as the pioneer and leader in floor technologies. Since gypsum first met sand in 1972, Gyp-Crete, now Maxxon Corporation, has provided superior underlayment products for solving virtually any floor problem. Maxxon not only started the industry but continues to lead it by applying years of experience and knowledge in flooring underlayments and acoustical sound technology to design and problem solve with architects and general contractors.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Our Opportunity</b></p>
<p>
Maxxon Corporation has an immediate opening for an experienced Construction Estimator. The Construction Estimator will assist in the preparation of budgets for multifamily, commercial and residential construction projects.
</p>
<p><b>Key Responsibilities:</b></p>
<p></p>
<br>
<ul>
<li>
Download and organize architectural drawings.</li>
<li>
Organize plans in a provided estimating software.</li>
<li>
Navigate architectural drawings and identify relevant assemblies.</li>
<li>
Navigate specifications and identify material divisions.</li>
<li>
Accurately digitize quantities on a computer and input areas of relevance into Excel.</li>
<li>
Take screenshots of details and assemblies.</li>
<li>
Organize Excel forms, pictures from the plans and specifications for pricing by sales team.</li>
<li>
This position will assist in organizing and response to ASI's and RFI's for ongoing projects.</li>
<li>
 Maintain and update project list with contractor information across multiple states.</li>
<li>
Manage all bid documents (bid forms, drawings, addendums, etc.), post documents to a file sharing system, and ensure all relevant project documents are available to satellite companies.</li>
<li>
Review bid invitations, complete forms as necessary and request information as needed. Responsible for having a complete bidding package ready to submit to local sales teams.</li>
<li>
Support writing proposals / cover letters / scopes of work as necessary to assist sales team in completing a proposal. This includes using Word, Excel and Project documents.</li>
<li>
Archive / Removal of documents from online storage.</li>
<li>
Other responsibilities as assigned.</li>
</ul>
