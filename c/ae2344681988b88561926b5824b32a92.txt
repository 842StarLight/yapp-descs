<p><b>Requirements: </b></p>
<p>As an Inbound <b>Customer Service Specialist for our Lane Bryant</b> team, you must be confident, outgoing and self-motivated with a strong work ethic. You must also be a team-oriented person who is open minded and willing to learn and who has the ability to function effectively in a fast-paced work environment. It is also vital for your role as an Inbound Brand Care Specialist that you are articulate with excellent verbal and written communication and interpersonal skills and the ability to easily establish rapport with customers.</p>
<ul>
<li>Respond to customers with knowledge, empathy &amp; a high level of customer care via telephone, email and chat.</li>
<li>Assist the customer in identifying his/her needs, suggesting products that satisfy those needs.</li>
<li>Provide information to customers regarding products, including features and benefits.</li>
<li>Process orders, prepare correspondence and fulfill customer needs to ensure brand satisfaction.</li>
<li>Solve customer issues in a courteous, friendly and professional manner using client approved procedures.</li>
<li>Use decision-support computer software programs to respond to common customer work/service order inquiries and requests.</li>
<li>Position entails a high amount of customer interaction over the phone. Ensure that customers understand product information, and be able to provide additional information to customers as needed</li>
<li>Listen attentively to customer needs and concerns; demonstrate empathy.</li>
<li>Prepare complete and accurate work and update customer file.</li>
<li>Communicate effectively with individuals/teams in the program to ensure high quality and timely expedition of customer requests.</li>
<li>Effectively transfer misdirected customer requests to an appropriate party.</li>
<li>Contribute ideas on ways to resolve problems to better serve the customer and/or improve productivity.</li>
<li>Participate in activities designed to improve customer satisfaction and business performance.</li>
</ul>
