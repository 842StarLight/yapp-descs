<h2>Business Development Representative</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>

We offer all the energy and opportunity of a startup, plus the strength and stability of a revenue-positive industry leader. For professionals in technical, sales, marketing or administrative roles, Certify is the place to bring your talents and truly love what you do for a lifetime.</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
The Business Development Representative is responsible for prospecting, qualifying and generating new Marketing Qualified Leads (MQLs) to support Certify, Inc.’s team of Sales Executives and Managers.</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div><b>
Responsibilities:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Nurture and prospect warm business leads through phone, email and various outreach programs</li>
<li>Research and uncover information on organizations that have shown interest in Certify’s software by uncovering and reporting on key pieces of data</li>
<li>Identify gatekeepers, influencers and decision makers within organizations while determining the level of interest and project timelines</li>
<li>Build and cultivate B2B relationships by initiating communications and following up on inbound leads</li>
<li>Manage data for new and prospective clients, set tasks, record activity and manage lead pipeline within SalesForce.com</li>
<li>Work with Lead Generation Manager, Sales Managers and VP of Sales and Marketing to develop and grow the sales pipeline</li>
</ul>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div><b>
Requirements:</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Required Hours are Monday - Friday, 8:00 am - 5:00 pm</li>
<li>A college degree is preferred but not required</li>
<li>1+ years’ experience in an office, corporate, or B2B sales setting or commensurate experience conducting business via email and phone</li>
<li>Working knowledge of SalesForce and Pardot preferred, but not required</li>
<li>Knowledge of Microsoft Office and mobile internet applications</li>
<li>Experience with telephone sales calls, techniques, and etiquette within a professional B2B market</li>
<li>Strong influencing and negotiation skills</li>
<li>Ability to work independently as well as collaboratively with team members</li>
<li>Ability to simultaneously handle multiple time-sensitive leads</li>
<li>Excellent verbal and written communication skills</li>
</ul>
