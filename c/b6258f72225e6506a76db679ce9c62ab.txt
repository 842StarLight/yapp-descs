<h2>Technical Writer / Illustrator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p>Our Fairbanks Morse team is currently looking for a Technical Writer/Illustrator to join the team out of our Houston service center. From preparing our technical manuals and helping to shape maintenance standards, to creating service information letters and customer information documentation, no day will be the same. You will interpret technical information included in engineering drawings and translate these instructions into clear and concise language that is understandable to our diverse customer base. You will use both 2D and 3D software to prepare technical illustrations and drawings to help complement our written content.<br>
<br>
EnPro Industries companies are known for our focus on a Dual Bottom Line culture where we seek to enable the full release of human possibility through individual development while achieving strong financial returns. We invest in our employees and provide opportunities for them to make a difference while building a culture that reflects our core values of safety, excellence and respect. Each person has the opportunity to truly make an impact as an integral part of a collective.<br>
<br>
<b>Responsibilities of the Technical Writer:</b><br>
</p>
<ul>
<li>Prepare written copy for Technical Manuals to meet contract requirements.</li>
<li>
Work to meet contract commitments as scheduled, including validation activities.</li>
<li>
Develop repair parts lists and illustrations using bills of material and engineering drawings.</li>
<li>
Manage vendors that are providing data development resources.</li>
<li>
Review specifications as they relate to technical manual deliverables and estimate effort required.</li>
<li>
Read specifications and other contract documents and determine the types of technical documentation required.</li>
<li>
Interact with FM Program Management, Engineering, Training, and Sales &amp; Marketing groups and determine the scope and formatting necessary.</li>
<li>
Develop data necessary for the preparation of operation and maintenance (O&amp;M) technical manuals, maintenance standards, service information letters (SILs) and other technical documents.
</li>
<li>Draft text material as required for new, original technical documentation and prepare updates and revisions as necessary to existing or previously written technical documentation.</li>
<li>
Prepare clear technical illustrations and graphic depictions as necessary to support the written text.</li>
<li>
Prepare, organize and deliver preliminary contract deliverable documentation; adjudicate and incorporate customer comments/corrections and prepare final contract deliverable documentation.</li>
</ul>
<br>
<br>
<br>
<b>Required Qualifications of the Technical Writer:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Completed Associate's Degree or equivalent experience in a maintenance related technical writing environment.</li>
<li>
5+ years' technical writing experience; preferably in heavy machinery manufacturing, military maintenance, power generation, or marine application related environment.</li>
<li>
Strong mechanical aptitude with the ability to use both 2D and 3D software.</li>
<li>
Ability to read blueprints, engineering orders, bills of material, and interpret contract specifications.</li>
<li>
Highly skilled with Microsoft Office with exposure to; Oracle, XML Schema/Stylesheet, FrameMaker Arbortext and SQML authoring/document development.<br>
</li>
</ul>
