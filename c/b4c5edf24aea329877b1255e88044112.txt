<p>Actifio, the market leader in Enterprise Data-as-a-Service is looking for our next generation of sales leaders. If you think you have the energy, ambition and drive to succeed, contact us now to be part of the next Actifio Sales team! DON’T WAIT, CONTACT US NOW!!</p>
<p>
Responsibilities/Key Tasks</p>
<ul>
<li>Work closely with Field Sales teams to develop a targeted prospecting strategy for assigned region</li>
<li>Research relevant background on and make 60+ sales calls a day. Send 20+ personalized e-mails to targeted prospects</li>
<li>Learn and develop knowledge of Actifio and IT-related skills by participating and completing sales &amp; technical training</li>
<li>Leverage our state of the art productivity tools including LinkedIn, Salesforce, Outreach, Tech Target, and Google Apps</li>
<li>Run first calls, drive the conversation to uncover customer needs, describe the Actifio value proposition</li>
</ul>

<ul>

<li>Strong motivational and organizational skills</li>
<li>Entrepreneurial approach to business</li>
<li>Exceptional communication skills</li>

</ul>
