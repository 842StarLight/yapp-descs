<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<ul>
<li>Apply the Hazardous Work Permit (HWP) process, Lock Out, Tag Out (LOTO) and energy isolation procedures
</li>
<li>Follow EMOC procedure for all changes to process and operating procedures</li>
<li>
Maintain site security and maintain site orderliness</li>
<li>
Perform safety indoctrinations for contractors</li>
<li>
Utilize Computerized Maintenance Management System (Maximo)
</li>
<li>Perform and document process and product analyzers calibration checks</li>
<li>
Assist shutdowns, start-ups, and production slate changes
</li>
<li>Troubleshoot process problems to identify equipment requiring maintenance</li>
<li>
Perform water treatment tasks</li>
<li>
Document activities and data in plant logbook and SAP system</li>
<li>
Direct and monitor contractors</li>
<li>
Participate in safety committee activities</li>
<li>
Complete all training activities according to schedule and meet annual performance goals</li>
<li>
Accept on call duty and overtime as required</li>
</ul>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Qualifications
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div>
<b>Do you have what it takes?</b><br>
<br>
High School Diploma, certificate in a technical discipline, or recent military training in a technical field is required. In addition, experience in an industrial plant in the operations or maintenance department is preferable. Must be able to do bending, stooping, kneeling, climbing ladders, and lifting activities as well as carry loads up to 50 pounds. A valid driver’s license with good record is required. Basic math, computer proficiency, and ability to work with simple mechanic’s hand tools is necessary.<br>
</div>
<p>
Personal computer proficiency (Internet, word processing, spreadsheets), and other software used by the company. The ideal candidate must have a basic understanding of pneumatically actuated valves and control systems. He/She must have basic knowlege of math skills. He/she must be capable of operating a forklift, overhead crane, and/or man lift.</p>
