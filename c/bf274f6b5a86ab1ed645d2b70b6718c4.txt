<h2>D365 CE - Solution Architect</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Sikich is seeking a <b>Dynamics 365 CE Solution Architect </b>to support our growing Dynamics 365 Enterprise team in creating &amp; developing solutions for new &amp; existing client projects.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
Essential Job Duties:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Architect roadmap of Dynamics 365 CE solutions for various industry verticals and participate in prospect demonstrations/evaluations.</li>
<li>
Formulate project solution designs utilizing industry and product knowledge</li>
<li>
Responsible for functional &amp; technical development phases of full-cycle Dynamics 365 CE implementations, upgrades, enhancements &amp; integrations with other applications, as well as all other supporting tasks.</li>
<li>
Work with Project Manager to formulate implementation plans, risks, mitigation strategies, &amp; timeline requirements.</li>
<li>
Work with consultant team to successfully complete projects on time, providing client with status updates as needed.</li>
<li>
Provide guidance and mentoring to newer team members</li>
<li>
Drive additional sales opportunities with the customer.</li>
<li>
Document design specifications.</li>
<li>
Travel (domestic) up to 50% to customer locations for on-site work. Possible increase depending on project phase.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
Qualifications:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Bachelor’s Degree in Computer Science or related field.</li>
<li>
4+ years’ Dynamics CRM experience in multiple versions (4.0, 2011, 2013, 2015, 2016, 365).</li>
<li>
10+ years’ consulting &amp; technical experience, leading participation in business process design, complex implementations and large software development build scenarios.</li>
<li>
Excellent client relation skills, organization, &amp; prioritization capabilities.</li>
<li>
Dynamics 365 CE certifications a plus.</li>
</ul>
