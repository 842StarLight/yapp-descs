<b>Job Description:</b><br>
Leadspace is seeking a top-performing Sales Development Representative, (Account Based Marketing Specialist). We are looking for someone to help Leadspace stand out among the crowd and reach the right Martech buyers and decision-makers at enterprise companies. Your job is to develop relationships, nurture, and create qualified meetings for Enterprise Account representatives each month, and each quarter to help them build pipeline.
<br><br>
<b>Responsibilities include:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Work with assigned Account Executives to develop go-to-market strategy and build sales pipeline by navigating target accounts with outbound activities.</li>
<li>Take a consultative approach to calls, emails &amp; LinkedIn with prospects.</li>
<li>Consistently achieve daily activity goals in monthly quotas.</li>
<li>Conduct administrative duties in support of your AEs.</li>
<li>Collaborate with the Demand Generation leader and the SDR team to develop and refine processes and strategies.</li>
<li>Learn best practices of Account Based Marketing and how to effectively communicate with different buying personas.</li>
<li>Be a results oriented student of the game that is committed to developing the necessary skill sets of a full cycle salesperson.</li>
</ul>
