 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Senior Field Marketing Specialist – North America</b></p>
<p><b>
Location: Alameda, CA</b></p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
What you’ll do:</b></p>
<p></p>
<br>
<ul>
<li>
Drive regional and/or vertical marketing campaigns, creating and executing programs to increase brand awareness, penetration across the top enterprise accounts and lead generation in North America.</li>
<li>
Develop and deliver high-touch marketing programs that generate quality pipeline, move prospects through the sales cycle and build customer intimacy.</li>
<li>
Support sales teams, in a fast paced environment, engaging in account-based marketing, C-level executive programs and events management.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
 You will work with:</b></p>
<p></p>
<br>
<ul>
<li>
As a member of the global field marketing team, you will partner with marketing, sales, partners and other stakeholders to design and execute regional/vertical specific campaigns.</li>
<li>
You will work with marketing, sales and sales operations teams to measure and optimize ROI from all campaigns.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Responsibilities:<br>
</b></p>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Define marketing plans to create/accelerate opportunities, build executive relationships, and impact revenue growth</li>
<li>
Integrate corporate strategy and vision within field marketing activities</li>
<li>
Create, execute, monitor, report and follow-up on programs to generate a targeted number of leads and inquiries</li>
<li>
Plan and execute target digital programs, e.g., webinars, newsletter and other digital engagement campaigns</li>
<li>
Plan events including trade shows, seminars, partner activities, executive events, etc.</li>
<li>
Travel to events to provide onsite support for the events</li>
<li>
Engage sales to work proactively with marketing to achieve objectives and drive attendance and participation in field activities</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b><i>
Qualifications</i></b></p>
<p></p>
<br>
<ul>
<li>
BA/BS Degree</li>
<li>
5+ years of marketing background including event and field experience</li>
<li>
Demonstrated history of account-based marketing and executive programs for large enterprise customers</li>
<li>
Self-starter that is comfortable balancing many responsibilities</li>
<li>
Ability to develop integrated, multi-touch marketing programs</li>
<li>
Position requires exceptional creativity, innovation, the ability to solve problems and a hands-on approach</li>
<li>
Proven track record of successfully partnering with sales teams and management</li>
<li>
Detail-oriented, intuitive, collaborative, accountable, passionate and driven</li>
<li>
Strong communication and presentation skills, both verbal and written</li>
<li>
Efficient data entry and proof-reading capabilities</li>
<li>
Financial competence to work on budgets, reports and analytics</li>
<li>
Proficiency in Marketo, Salesforce and other aspects of the marketing technology stack.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Desirable Qualifications</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Familiarity with sales and marketing automation systems.</li>
<li>
Sales and/or business development experience.</li>
<li>
Experience working for a technology enterprise.</li>
</ul>
