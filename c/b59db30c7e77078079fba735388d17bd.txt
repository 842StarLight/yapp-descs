<h2>Digital Sales Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>WDSU-TV seeks a proven Digital Sales Manager to provide energized leadership for our digital sales initiatives. The ideal candidate possesses a passion for new media and enjoys staying up-to-date with its evolution, understands digital marketing strategy and thrives in a customer-focused team environment. Required strengths include: the ability to strategically plan, communicate effectively, build and maintain internal and external customer relationships and training.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<div><b>
Job Responsibilities</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Prepare and implement individual advertiser account strategy including revenue goals.
</li>
<li>Regularly meet with local sales team, including group and individual meetings, to ensure their professional development and success with stated goals.</li>
<li>In partnership with the Local Sales Manager, lead weekly sales meeting; prepare and lead quarterly/annual sales planning meeting.
</li>
<li>Consistently evaluate seller performance and provide feedback.
</li>
<li>Act as primary negotiator for the station on all digital business; review key sales proposals in advance to ensure client goals are achieved in a manner consistent with station sales guidelines.</li>
<li>Respond to RFP requests; meet client deadlines.</li>
<li>Meet extensively with clients to develop/strengthen business relationships; drive client retention.</li>
<li>Develop/strengthen business relationships with key station vendors.</li>
<li>Support local sellers in sales presentations to multiple points of contact with client/agency personnel.
</li>
<li>Develop and generate non-traditional revenue and new sales initiatives.</li>
<li>Manage various expense budgets related to digital initiatives.</li>
<li>Work in partnership with the Local Sales Manager and National Sales Manager to achieve stated and strategic goals.
</li>
<li>Provide training to Local and National sellers.</li>
<li>Ensure that all necessary sales presentations are comprehensive and available in advance of the selling window.</li>
<li>Maintain current and accurate revenue forecasts by account.
</li>
<li>Review and approve new insertion orders.</li>
<li>Prepare and present various reports.</li>
<li>Follow and meet all HTV Digital Sales Managers standard operating procedures as outlined.</li>
<li>Maintain current knowledge of HTV advertising regulations.
</li>
<li>Communicate regularly with the General Sales Manager on all aspects of digital business.
</li>
<li>Be agile in responding to dynamic business conditions and station business needs.</li>
</ul>
<div><b>Experience Requirements</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Have a complete understanding of current digital marketing strategies including synergist values with other media.</li>
<li>Understanding of pricing and inventory management principles.</li>
<li>Ability to dissect an advertising RFP; identify key revenue opportunities, and prepare comprehensive and winning response.</li>
<li>Ability to prospect, meet with, and close business with new station clients.</li>
<li>Possess the skills to manage and supervise others.</li>
<li>Related military experience will be considered</li>
</ul>
