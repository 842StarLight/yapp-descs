<h2>Software Development Engineer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>About the Position</b></p>
<p>
We are seeking a software engineer to create software development solutions for our mission critical services and solutions that support WatchGuard’s firewall and security products. At WatchGuard, you will be a member of our agile development team where we are developing new products and solutions for our end users. We believe by leveraging new and emerging technologies we can enable business outcomes and improve performance. If you are looking for a challenge, like to solve problems, and can get stuff done, then we want to meet you.<br>
</p>
<p><b>Position Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Design, develop, debug, and deliver successful software solutions that support WatchGuard’s products</li>
<li>Build maintainable software products individually as well as collaboratively</li>
<li>Develop resilient applications and services that can withstand multiple failures</li>
<li>Build things quickly while maintaining the high level of quality and reliability</li>
<li>Collaborate with other engineers in system design, architectural and code reviews</li>
<li>Partner with cross functional teams in IT Ops, Product, Sales, Technical Support, and QA</li>
<li>Other duties and responsibilities as assigned</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Skills and Qualifications</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Solid object-oriented programming skills with .NET platform: C#, ASP.NET, MVC, WCF</li>
<li>MS SQL database design and development</li>
<li>REST, XML, JSON, and/or web service experience a plus</li>
<li>Agile methodologies, lean, and CI</li>
<li>Team player who has strong technical, analytical, and problem solving skills</li>
<li>AWS/Azure experience a plus</li>
<li>Be innovative and always want to learn more</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Experience</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
<p>3+ years of proven software engineering experience</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>BS in Computer Science or related technical discipline required</p>
<br>
</li>
</ul>
