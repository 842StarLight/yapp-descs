<h2>Sr. Product Manager, PreSales and Cloud Services</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div><div><div>The PreSales and Cloud Services Product Manager (PM) drives the Storage Solutions product roadmap at the cloud layer to meet global growth and profitability goals. Specific growth initiatives include Pre-Sales, regional support (energy markets, tariffs, incentives, regulations), virtual power plants (grid services) and interoperability. Specific profitability initiatives include asset management and settlement (bill reconciliation, financial performance reporting), scaling network operations, reliability and cybersecurity.
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>Strategic: Refine and communicate product vision, strategy and business planning at the cloud layer. Collaborate with Business Development to execute our go-to-market strategy, globally.<br>
<br>
<ul><li>Ideation: Elicit, gather, prioritize, validate and clearly communicate competing requirements by working directly with customers, partners, engineering leads, peer PM’s, Business Analysts, Sales and Sales Engineers, network operators, finance, Directors and Executives.</li></ul>
<br>
<ul><li>Go-to-market: Support business development, sales and channel partners with demos, training, tools, modeling, proposal approval, new customer requirements, marketing collateral, and subject-matter expertise to drive closing new business.</li></ul>
<br>
<ul><li>Road mapping: Plan and prioritize what and when engineering teams will deliver Master Features (milestones) at the cloud layer. Specific engineering teams include PreSales, Cloud, UI and Financial Services. Define, mockup, validate and generate acceptance tests for features in detail-getting them ready for productive software development.</li></ul>
<br>
<ul><li>Deployment: Participate in Agile/Scrum daily stand-ups, release management, kick-offs, retrospectives, commissioning, operations, and related meetings and events.</li></ul>
<br>
<ul><li>Domain expertise: Scour the market and conferences for partner interoperability, competitive analysis, and industry trends and communicate findings with stakeholders.
</li></ul>
</div></div></div>

</b></h2>
<div><div><ul>

<li>5+ years’ experience in Commercial and Industrial, Behind-the-Meter storage, Solar, wholesale energy markets and/or microgrid industry; implementing a wide variety of BtM applications with energy storage for utility applications and understands controls theory and applications. FtM experience a plus. Demand Response experience a plus.</li>
