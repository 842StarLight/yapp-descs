<h2>Account Executive</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>WHBQ FOX 13 - Cox Media Group</b> Television Station in Memphis, TN seeks a motivated and passionate <b>Local Account Executive</b> that’s eager to WIN while representing a top station in our market.<br>
<br>
Memphis, TN is a fun and exciting place to live and work. Warm summers, very mild winters and always something to do. Located on the Mississippi River, we’re famous for the best BBQ in the world, and “Home of the Blues and Birthplace of Rock 'n' Roll”. Elvis Presley, B.B. King and Johnny Cash recorded albums at the legendary Sun Studio, and Presley’s Graceland mansion is a popular attraction for visitors from around the world. Other music landmarks include the Rock 'n' Soul Museum and Stax Museum. In Memphis, you’re never too far for a quick road trip to Nashville, New Orleans, St. Louis, or Dallas.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
Here's what we’re looking for:</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
The Account Executive is responsible for generating media revenues for Cox Media Group Memphis and its entities to meet or exceed predetermined goals. You will also create revenues by working collaboratively with your Local Sales Manager, by making sales presentations and by directly selling to key primary and secondary accounts as assigned. You will service and secure mutually beneficial relationships with various local businesses, agencies, or individuals that have a desire to reach their target audience through various advertising channels and services.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Develop and manage revenue producing relationships with clients and agencies</li>
<li>
Prospect for new business and build a full pipeline of sales prospects</li>
<li>
Analyze client marketing goals and develop needs based solutions to achieve them</li>
<li>
Oversee campaign execution and analysis</li>
<li>
Provide consultation to sales managers, sales teams and clients</li>
<li>
Work collaboratively with internal partners to drive revenue</li>
</ul>

<li>
Excellent presentation skills</li>
<li>
Excellent verbal and written skills</li>

Hard-working, integrity, relationship-builder with a winning attitude</li>

About Cox Media Group</b></p>

<p>
Cox Media Group is an integrated broadcasting, publishing and digital media company. The company’s operations currently include broadcast television stations, radio stations, daily newspapers, and digital sites. Additionally, CMG operates the National Advertising Platform businesses of CoxReps - the country’s biggest television rep firm - Gamut, and Videa. The company also offers a full suite of local and regional advertising services through its Local Solutions and Ideabar businesses. CMG currently operates in more than 20 media markets and reaches approximately 52 million Americans weekly across all platforms.
