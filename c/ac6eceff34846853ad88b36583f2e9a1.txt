<p>Work Management Planner - SME The Subject Matter Expert (SME) is our leading expert in a field of advanced study (i.e. Work Management, Materials Management, Lean etc.) and is responsible for developing our best practices and work processes for client engagement delivery within their knowledge domain related to Electrical Engineering and Substations projects. In addition, the SME must also be knowledgeable in how their field of study impacts, influences, integrates and interrelates with other domains within our Rx, Operational Excellence, and Asset management delivery solutions. The SME is responsible for keeping informed, educated and trained on innovations and developing new concepts within their field that will continually improve our processes to deliver client value.</p>
