<div>
This position is responsible for managing a business portfolio focused on defined business segments, developing and executing integrated marketing and business strategy (including market analysis, product needs, distribution and marketing), creating and implementing the marketing plan, leading a team to achieve strategic objectives and overseeing the portfolio management process and budget.</div>
</div>
<div><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>How you will make an impact:
</b></h3>
<div><ul>
<li>Leads day-to-day management and annual planning for the defined business segments to meet growth objectives in the strategic plan.</li>
<li>
Develops business and integrated marketing plans (e.g. positioning, product needs, distribution channels, acquisition and retention programs) and implements initiatives to achieve aggressive sales and retention goals, by working in partnership with cross-functional teams (such as Product, Market Research, Actuarial, Regulatory, Legal, Operations and IT) to build plans to meet market, and customer needs.</li>
<li>
Provides strategic leadership in identifying new business opportunities and developing tactics for growth, such as product needs, distribution channels, messaging, direct marketing, media and distribution channels.</li>
<li>
Employs a strong consumer-centric, research-based approach to define market demand, identify attractive market segments to target and strategies to meet their needs.</li>
<li>
Leads and/or represents business segment goals in cross-functional initiatives that support segment growth strategies and programs.</li>
<li>
Develops marketing communications strategy including value proposition, positioning, product naming, and messaging.</li>
<li>
Manages implementation for marketing programs and provides direction to both internal and external marketing agencies including the development of creative, campaign strategy, media plans and budgets.</li>
<li>
Leads development and implementation of marketing materials, enrollee communications and marketing web pages.</li>
<li>
Manages marketing agency and related-vendor contracts and relationships.</li>
<li>
Develops and manages portfolio operating plans and budgets through activities including enrollment analysis, marketing metrics analysis, and competitive benchmarking, and day-to-day management such as portfolio maintenance, issue resolution and regulatory implementation.</li>
<li>
Manages and develops staff to achieve department objectives including determining staffing needs, staff training, and setting goals, interviewing, selecting, coaching, and evaluating the performance of all direct reports.</li>
<li>
Provides team leadership to integrate productivity with all cross-functional teams.</li>
<li>
Performs miscellaneous duties as assigned.</li>
</ul></div>
</div></div>
<div><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>What we look for:
</b></h3>
<div><ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree required.</li>
<li>
A minimum of 7 years management experience, including consumer marketing team at a manager level or above.</li>
<li>
Experience in developing integrated, multi-channel business and marketing strategies and plans that grow top and bottom-line business results through customer acquisition and retention.</li>
<li>
Excellent business management skills, annual planning and staff development.</li>
<li>
Excellent consumer marketing skills and track record including competitive analysis, target identification, positioning and marketing program development and implementation.</li>
<li>
Excellent consumer research-based decision-making skills.</li>
<li>
Excellent project and time management skills and multitasking abilities.</li>
<li>
Excellent analytical skills and strategic thinking skills required including forecasting, ROI analyses.</li>
<li>
Excellent oral and written communication skills to author/edit reports, business correspondence, marketing materials, and enrollee materials.</li>
<li>
Excellent oral communications to present information and respond to questions to all levels (including E-Suite) in the organization and in the external marketplace.</li>
<li>
Strong interpersonal skills and the ability to develop and lead staff and cross-functional teams to achieve results.</li>
<li>
Ability to maintain confidentiality to be aware of issues at corporate and divisional level and customer privacy.</li>
</ul></div>
