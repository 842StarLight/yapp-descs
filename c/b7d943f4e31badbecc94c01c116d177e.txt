Otis, a United Technologies company, is the world leader in reliable, efficient and technologically advanced elevators, escalators and people-moving systems. Our revolutionary Gen2® elevators, energy-saving ReGen™ drives and NCE “green” escalator have clearly set the industry standard for innovation, safety and performance. More than 2.4 million Otis elevators and escalators are currently in operation throughout 200 different countries. We are proud to have a global team that continues to rise to the challenges of a fast-moving company. <p><b>
The Eastern Region of Otis Elevator Company is searching for a highly motivated Open Order Superintendent to drive productivity and promote innovative solutions.</b></p>
<p></p>
<p><b>
JOB RESPONSIBILITIES:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Leading the performance of field operations for all elevator repair and upgrades</li>
<li>
Implementing proper field education training</li>
<li>
Efficiently allocating labor resources</li>
<li>
Maintaining project schedules</li>
<li>
Authorizing repair orders</li>
<li>
Ensuring safety and the quality of service, technical support and other assets for our customers</li>
<li>
Identifying and driving efficiency improvements in our open order operations</li>
<li>
Effectively managing sold open order projects, and exceeding open order margin objectives</li>
<li>
Generating field leads and assisting our service sales representatives in selling open order</li>
<li>
Field safety audits, jobsite inspections and developing site/safety logistics plans</li>
</ul>
