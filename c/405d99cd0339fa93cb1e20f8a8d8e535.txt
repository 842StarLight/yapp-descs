<h2>Director of Marketing, Sales, and Communications</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>JOB DESCRIPTION: </b>Director of Marketing, Sales, and Communications</p>
<p><b>Department: </b>Marketing<br><b>Reports to: </b>Managing Director<br><b>Classification: </b>Full Time Exempt with Benefits</p>
<p><b>Summary: </b>The Director of Marketing, Sales, and Communications is a member of the executive leadership team, responsible for developing audiences and generating annual attendance that exceeds 120,000 and revenues over $3M across the Mainstage Season, ACTLab, and Holiday programming. A successful candidate is equal parts data analyst, storyteller, sales director, creative director, and motivating manager. This position creates annual plans for a full team of dedicated staff to execute through print and letter packages, website and digital communications, social media, press and publicity, engagement events, promotions, ticketing services, group sales, outbound telemarketing, and onsite retention activities. The position requires a solid understanding of budgeting and forecasting as well as experience in guiding the entire marketing process from the ground up. The Director of Marketing, Sales and Communications functions in partnership with other departments, supports engagement with members of the board, and is integral to the strategic planning process outlining the theatre’s future goals.</p>
<p><b>Key Responsibilities: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Build a comprehensive budget with revenue and expense goals based on thorough research and trends. Budgets include performance indicator benchmarking and annual cash flow projections.</li>
<li>Write an annual plan for efforts and activities that achieve ACT’s mission and goals over the calendar year. Plans include the full suite of strategies needed to support the Mainstage Season, and ACTLab partnerships, as well as promotional support for artistic, engagement, development, and operations events.</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Plans and activities include internal as well as external strategies to build awareness and enthusiasm for ACT’s programming</li>
<li>ACT is committed to being an accessible and inclusive organization. Plans will include thoughtful strategies to engage and invite people with diverse backgrounds, life experiences, and abilities * Manage sales campaigns to develop audiences through the sale of subscription packages and single tickets. Plans will include retention activities that build loyalty and repeat attendance and attract first-time tryers. The ability to successfully manage multiple campaigns across numerous product lines is essential.</li>
<li>Work with the Data Resources Manager and Ticket Office Manager to develop pricing and inventory management systems that maximize potential and set each performance up for success.</li>
<li>Outline a media plan for all ACT productions, including advertising placement and purchasing, garnering media sponsors, and tracking ROI across platforms.</li>
<li>Ensure that internal communications about ACT's products and official ACT statements are clear.</li>
<li>Oversee strategies that result in a clear and inspiring brand for ACT's mission and vision.</li>
<li>Supervise department staff, including: Senior Marketing Manager, ACTLab Marketing Manager, Press and Promotions Associate, Digital Communications Associate, Graphic Designer, Ticket Office Manager, and the Telemarketing team.</li>
<li>Provide clear and insightful reports for the Finance Committee and Board.</li>
<li>Participate fully in ACT’s multi-year strategic plan, with a commitment to racial equity and financial sustainability as its foundational goals.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Requirements: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Successful track record of experience in marketing, sales, and communications for the performing arts and/or cultural events and managing a $1M+ budget.</li>
<li>Proven leadership ability in creative and brand strategy</li>
<li>Ability to delegate tasks and provide constructive feedback to staff</li>
<li>Experience managing multiple, overlapping events and sales campaigns</li>
<li>Extensive knowledge of data analytics and tracking systems</li>
<li>Excellent writing, communication, and reporting skills</li>
<li>Impeccable attention to detail, excelling in organization and prioritization</li>
<li>Solid understanding of traditional methods of marketing, balanced with enthusiasm for new methods to engage customers and build audiences</li>
<li>Experience cultivating and maintaining press and media relationships</li>
<li>Knowledge of Tessitura ticketing and customer relationship management software a plus</li>
<li>Ability to attend evening and weekend events will be required on a regular basis</li>
<li>A personable and collaborative spirit</li>
</ul>
