<h2>Service Assistant</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>Komatsu is an indispensable partner to the construction, mining, industrial and forestry industries that maximizes value for customers through innovative solutions. With a full line of products supported by our advanced IoT technologies, regional distribution channels and a global service network, we help customers safely and sustainably optimize their operations. Our Komatsu, P&amp;H, Joy, Montabert, Modular Mining Systems, Hensley Industries, NTC and Gigaphoton equipment and services are used to extract fundamental minerals and develop modern infrastructure.
</p>
<p></p>
<div>
<p><b>Job Overview
</b></p>
<p></p>
<p>The main role is to support and assist Service Manager/Field Service Team Leader in coordinating/processing all Service Dept. administrative matters including Service, Sales program preparation, Service report/quote follow-up, warranty claim process, and periodical (weekly, monthly and annually) internal/external reports preparation etc.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p>Also includes direct office support to customers and supporting and assisting Service Technicians while they are out of the office on job locations.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Key Job Responsibilities
</b></p>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Phone work. Assist and support customers calling into the office by answering questions and direct their calls to the appropriate service technician for proper assistance. Calling technicians on a regular basis to discuss and request required paperwork to assist customers. Contacting vendors to setup jobs and verifying costs when vendor invoices are received. Contacting customers with quotes, follow-ups, and invoicing.
</li>
<li>Handle all coding of vendor invoicing received to be applied to the appropriate GL accounts. Handle all filing and reconciliation of monthly costs received from technicians and vendors.
</li>
<li>Handle all invoicing of customers for Service Department work.
</li>
<li>Handle the processing of and providing support in managing various kinds of warranty jobs. Includes communicating / reporting with Komatsu Industries Corp. (KIC) in Japan.
</li>
<li>Assist in preparing reports for tracking of Sales, Profitability, Quotes, etc. periodically.
</li>
<li>Assist performing various administration functions for Service Dept. including “Work hour management”, “Budget management”, “Expense report processing” and other general administration related matters.
</li>
<li>Assist collecting and organizing Market, Customer, Service/Maintenance and Technical (including Service news) information/data.
</li>
<li>Assist in developing &amp; implementing Service Sales promotion activities and Field Service Technician (FST) performance improvement (customer satisfaction improvement) program.
</li>
<li>Provide necessary assistance to FST visiting customers.
</li>
<li>Perform other duties assigned by Komatsu America Industries (KAI) Management.
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Qualifications/Requirements
</b></p>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Associate Degree or Equivalent Education or More than 2 years similar job experience
</li>
<li>The ability to write routine reports and correspondence. The ability to speak effectively before groups of customers or employees of the organization.
</li>
<li>Ability to create/develop new ideas/programs and to introduce them.
</li>
<li>Ability to calculate figures and amounts such as discounts, interest, commissions, proportions, percentages, area, circumference, and volume. The ability to apply concepts such as fractions, percentages, ratios and proportions to practical situations.
</li>
<li>Solid understanding of PC and common Windows applications. Interest and ability to learn other software programs used within KAI and Komatsu America Corp.
</li>
<li>The ability to define problems, collect data, establish facts, and draw valid conclusions. Ability to make an analysis and prepare various reports.
</li>
<li>Strong organizational and time management skills.
</li>
<li>Ability to communicate with various organizations including Customers, Sub-contractors, other department/divisions as well as KIC.
</li>
</ul>
