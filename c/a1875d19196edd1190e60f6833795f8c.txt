<h2>Director, Area Sales (CPD)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">The primary role of the Director of Area Sales, for Copy Print Depot (CPD) Print Services is to manage the day to day activities of sales and customer interaction to drive profitable B2B revenue growth across all Office Depot Selling Channels. This includes providing leadership and direction to the cross-channel CPD sales team, achievement of business plan objectives for the BSD Business Channel (including both quantitative and human capital goals), supporting and acting as a liaison for CPD in all aspects of cross-channel coordination. This position provides directional leadership for all aspects of field-based roll-outs and new products and services. As well, the Director CPD Sales functions as a field-based bridge for all CPD merchandising and marketing campaigns. This position is ultimately responsible for owning top-line and margin performance for CPD in their respective field locations.
<br><br>
1. Ensure effective execution of tactical and operational segments of the sales plans for all products and services relevant to geographical scope.
<br>2. Effectively implement and monitor talent development programs such as compensation, performance appraisals, and professional development. Assist in development, design, and execution of highly effective training programs. Maintain a high level of morale and proficiency among all regional team members.
<br>3. Ensure availability of appropriate resources and effectively allocate resources within the region. Lead regional staffing meetings to review requirements and establish staffing strategy and territory alignment for future year and necessary changes/additions.
<br>4. Monitor and report to other team members and internal management all market intelligence that may positively or negatively impact practices and operations.
<br>5. Maintain effective, on-going communication with executive management and all region members.
<br>6. Ensure the full and complete compliance of all selling activities within the area of responsibility to Business Unit standards and to all federal and state regulations
<br>7. Partner with CPD Field Support Leaders, BSD Sales Directors &amp; RVPs to ensure that CPD selling programs achieve their revenue and IMU targets.
<br>8. Function as Field liaison for strategic vendor partners engaged in enterprise-level sales initiatives with Office Depot CPD
<br>9. Develop successful paradigms for growing Projects, Programs, and Enterprise Solutions for high value CPD customers.
<br><br>
