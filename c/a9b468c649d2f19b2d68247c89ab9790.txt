<div>
The Application Architect will provide technical consulting to management, business users, and technical associates. This role provides operational, tactical, strategic direction and oversight of development. Expected to be a reliable technical professional who can handle all job responsibilities autonomously, while executing at the highest level. This role will interact with engineers, architects, DBA’s, team leads to lead continuous improvement initiatives, and mentor developers. The role will provide technical oversight for projects spanning multiple application teams related to E-Commerce Interconnected Customer Experience initiatives and modernization of The Americas Group (TAG) Point of Sale and Pricing systems.</div>
<div>
<br>
<b>
Essential Functions</b><br>
<br>
<b>Strategy &amp; Planning</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Develop and maintain high level design plan for overall logical and technical IT architecture.</li>
<li>Acquire and interpret business requirements, create functional specification, and determine the most efficient and appropriate technologies.</li>
<li>Develop standards for determining the designs which best meet project requirements.</li>
<li>Assist in development of coding standards.</li>
<li>Develop proofs of concept for technical solutions.</li>
<li>Submit and review technical and process improvement recommendations as a member of the technical leadership team.</li>
<li>Provide technical guidance, design expertise and leadership for web application development and general tool or component development across the organization.</li>
<li>Provide technical guidance, design expertise and leadership for relational database modeling across the organization.</li>
<li>Accountable for the overall application feasibility, system architecture and system integrity of project solution designs.</li>
</ul>
<br>
<b>
Acquisition &amp; Deployment</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Perform complex programming assignments based upon established programming standards, methods, and best practices.</li>
<li>Design application and database components for all sizes of development projects.</li>
<li>Perform analysis of business models, logical specifications, and customer requirements to design and build solutions utilizing appropriate technologies.</li>
<li>Mitigate risks associated with projects which have a high technical complexity or involve significant challenges to the business.</li>
<li>Partner and collaborate with other teams to ensure proper creation, testing, and deployment of designed solutions.</li>
<li>Work with configuration and change management personnel to create and refine processes.</li>
<li>Recommend best practices for development activities including refining the standard local development environment for development staff.</li>
</ul>
<br>
<b>
Operational Management</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Assist in the discovery, investigation and resolution of production problems as required.</li>
<li>Maintain and modify web applications; make approved changes to application documentation, develop detailed programming logic, and implement coding changes.</li>
<li>Mentor and coach developers and Professional Apprentices.</li>
<li>Train and share knowledge with development teams.</li>
<li>Perform informal and formal code reviews to ensure application code meets development standards.</li>
</ul>
<br>
<b>
Incidental Functions</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Research and recommend new technologies, including tools, components, and frameworks.</li>
<li>Provide project task management and reporting as necessary.</li>
<li>Make presentations to management, business users, and peer groups as requested.</li>
<li>Analyze performance of programs and take action to correct deficiencies based on consultation with business and technical teams.</li>
<li>Provide standards-based approach to application solutions.</li>
<li>Assist with various projects as may be required to contribute to the efficiency and effectiveness of the work.</li>
<li>Share technical approaches, recommended tools and subject matter expertise with development teams across the organization.</li>
<li>Avoid duplication of solutions whenever possible by promoting reuse of products, solutions, code and tools from all teams across the organization.</li>
<li>Participate in hiring activities, fulfill affirmative action obligations and ensure compliance with the equal employment opportunity policy.</li>
</ul>
