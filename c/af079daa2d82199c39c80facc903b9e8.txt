<h2>Macy's Annapolis Mall, Annapolis, MD: Jewelry Sales Supervisor</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<div><b>OVERVIEW:</b></div>
<div>
 As a Jewelry Supervisor, you will support driving a best in class selling and service culture that supports our My Macy's initiatives, our Omni Channel Strategies and MAGIC Selling in our Jewelry Complex in partnership with a Jewelry Business Manager, District Director, and VP Store Manager. You will be responsible for supervising all daily selling activities and leading a selling team. Responsibilities include, assisting in the selling process; driving customer service through coaching and monitoring selling behaviors; teaching product knowledge; communicating policies and procedures, event execution, merchandising and other duties as needed.<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<div>

</div>

</div>
<ul><li>Drive and exceed sales goals and execute company initiatives by developing a highly engaged and knowledgeable sales team
</li></ul>

</li></ul>
<ul><li>Review reports, identify deficient areas and partner with Jewelry Business Manager and/or District Director to implement action plans.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Ensure accuracy of promotional presentation; communicate advertising and sales information to Associates.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Strengthen attendance and weekend hours compliance among staff.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Motivate Associates to maximize company programs by reinforcing the benefits including but not limited to Worry No More &amp; Star Rewards.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Ensure usage &amp; proficiency of the tablet, POS/ MPOS systems including but not limited to Search and Send, My Client and More@ Macyâ€™s tablet app.

</li></ul>
<ul><li>Ensure all jewelry policies, procedures, standards and Fine Jewelry operations are understood and followed by associates.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Execute merchandising process and adhere to floor readiness standards.<br>
</li></ul>
<div></div>
<div>Customer
</div>
<ul><li>Coach Associates to consistently deliver effective selling behaviors resulting in high customer engagement.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Review &amp; utilize Associate Selling Actions Metrics (ASAM) to provide recognition; develop and communicate strategies to improve results partnering with leadership.
</li></ul>
