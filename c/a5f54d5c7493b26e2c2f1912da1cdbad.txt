<h2>Customer Service Rep</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p><b>Customer Service Representative</b></p>
<p>We are currently seeking a talented Customer Service Rep to work in our Phoenix showroom. This would be a non-supervisory position but will be a very vital part of our sales team. The Customer Service Rep will be a very diverse role and be handling everything from answering phones, greeting customers, walk-in sales, order processing, sampling, marketing and sales support. The job functions would include but not limited to:</p>
<ul><li>Be familiar with QDI’s product set and be able to describe colors, styles, and highlight its features and benefits to customers.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Have a basic understanding of travertine, limestone, and marble and their differences.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Understand the sales process from start to finish and be able to close sales both face to face and over the phone.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Responsible for greeting and assisting customers that come into the office or contact the Company via phone.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Write up customer orders using the Dancik system.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Refer customers to a Sales Representative when they are onsite and available.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Answer all customer questions about products, prices, availability, product uses, and available options.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Service Company’s existing accounts and obtain new accounts whenever possible through direct referrals, and out-bound or in-bound sales calls.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Be knowledgeable of Company’s payment procedures.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Understand QDI’s inventory. Check stock levels and request reorder of materials as necessary.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Assist in completion of customer files, which includes: Client Information Sheet, Tax Exemption Certificate (Form 5000), Credit Card Authorization, and Credit application if required.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Arrange pick-up schedules with operations and outside freight companies.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Coordinate customer material pick-ups with warehouse personnel.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Verify outgoing product counts prepared by the warehouse personnel utilizing the invoice to ensure accuracy.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Compare disputed merchandise with original requisitions and information from invoices.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Keep record of customer interactions and transactions by recording the details in Smartsheet (the Company’s CRM system).</li></ul>
<ul><li>Make recommendations for improvements in products, packaging, shipping, service, or billing methods and procedures to prevent future problems.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Refer unresolved customer grievances to designated departments for further investigation.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Resolve customer’s service or billing complaints by performing activities such as exchanging merchandise, refunding money and/or adjusting bills after review with management.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Contact customers about billing issues if requested.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Detect discrepancies on records or reports.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Use computers and computer systems (hardware and software) to set-up functions, enter data, or process information.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Observe, receive, and otherwise obtain information from all relevant sources in an effort to provide excellent customer service.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Compile, code, categorize, calculate, tabulate, audit, and verify information or data.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Develop a constructive and cooperative working relationship with other QDI employees and maintain them on an on-going basis.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Develop specific goals and plans to prioritize, organize, and accomplish your work. Once the goals and plans are developed review with your supervisor.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Attend regular CRS training meetings and dedicate yourself to continuous improvement.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Attend industry functions (EX: ASID, NARI) representing QDI while you network with professionals.</li></ul>
<ul><li>On occasion visit accounts not being serviced by Territory Managers to assist with sampling and general customer service.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Be a team player.</li></ul>
<p><b>Successful candidates will have the following:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>3-5 years in a customer service or office manager roles and preferably natural stone industry experience.</li>
<li>Well organized and self-disciplined - Attention to detail a must.</li>
<li>Outstanding customer service skills.</li>
<li>Professional appearance.</li>
<li>Good presentation skills.</li>
<li>Problem-solving skills with the habit of being proactive.</li>
<li>Excellent communication skills both written and verbal.</li>
<li>Team player</li>
<li>Good computer skills including MS Outlook, Word and Excel</li>
<li>Recent college grads encouraged to apply</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Company Benefits:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Competitive Salary</li>
<li>Full health insurance benefits after 90 days</li>
<li>401 K</li>
<li>Employee discounts on all QDI materials</li>
</ul>
<p>This is a great opportunity with a rapidly growing, Inc. 5000 company. We are consistently expanding and like to promote from within. If qualified, please respond to this add and submit your resume for consideration.</p>
