<h2>Sales Associate</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>SCOPE OF RESPONSIBILITIES: (narrative)<br>
Focus on achievement of company objectives by coordinating the efforts of each order from quote through<br>
punch list. Create great lifetime furniture experiences for our customers by using words and taking actions that<br>
are representative of our values (people, connectedness, improvement and imagination).</p>
<div>
ESSENTIAL FUNCTIONS:<br>
<ul><li>1. Be the point of contact for Goodmans internal and external customers, industry partners, prospects</li></ul>
and vendors<br>
<ul><li>2. Use working knowledge of Goodmans Aligned Supplier Partner Dealer Programs as well as additional</li></ul>
primary supplier partners, including products, pricing, terms and conditions to prepare proposals<br>
consistent with best practices<br>
<ul><li>3. Use knowledge of key customer accounts, including user, technical and financial influencers, product</li></ul>
applications, pricing schedules, purchasing processes and critical issues to coordinate project<br>
schedules to meet or exceed customer expectations<br>
<ul>
<li>4. Enhance a salesperson's ability to over-perform beyond annual goal expectations</li>
<li>5. Initiate Project Pre-Planning and Pre-Order Meetings</li>
<li>6. Review furniture specifications and shipping/delivery information to ensure all required fields are</li>
</ul>
complete before releasing to Purchasing<br>
<ul><li>7. Review acknowledgements and resolve purchase order discrepancies with Goodmans team and</li></ul>
vendors<br>
<ul><li>8. Researches, interviews, and negotiates with vendors to obtain desired delivery dates &amp; shipping</li></ul>
preferences<br>
<ul><li>9. Coordinate work plan, schedule and logistics based on overall project parameters, project schedule,</li></ul>
scope, and customers goals, needs and expectations<br>
<ul>
<li>10. Create scheduling requests and job packets for large projects</li>
<li>11. Create work orders for large projects</li>
<li>12. Create job packet for small projects</li>
<li>13. Confirm delivery and installation for large projects with client</li>
<li>14. Assist Sales Completion Team with punch list resolution</li>
<li>15. Document customer communications in customer order and electronic folder path as appropriate</li>
<li>16. Effectively communicate</li>
<li>With internal stakeholders all material project information in a timely manner.</li>
<li>With the internal and external customer concerning all pre-and post-sale activity.</li>
<li>With stakeholders on decisions that affect cost and customer expectations</li>
<li>17. Generate Credit/Debit Memo requests as directed and approved by Sales</li>
<li>18. Assist Marketing in collating bid materials</li>
<li>19. Compile finish and standards books for customers</li>
</ul>
</div>
<p>
GENERAL FUNCTIONS:<br>
1. Create professional and personal relationships with internal and external customers.<br>
2. Honor all commitments.<br>
3. Respond to stakeholder and management requests within 24 hours.<br>
4. Maintain a high proficiency of product, process, design and industry concepts.<br>
5. Connect with community through professional affiliation, community, charitable and/or civic<br>
organizations.<br>
6. Strategize with department, vendors and sales team in the sales process.<br>
7. Organize and prioritize your work (mail, calendar, contacts &amp; tasks) utilizing company information<br>
technology &amp; software.<br>
8. Seek opportunities for personal progress.</p>
