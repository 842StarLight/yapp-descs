<h2>Dynamics 365/AX Finance Solution Consultant</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>Avantiico is composed of a diverse group of highly ambitious, experienced and knowledgeable trusted advisors. We have developed a solid reputation for providing cross-functional expertise and delivering services and solutions for Microsoft Dynamics ERP and CRM, giving our clients confidence in their investment and a long-term competitive advantage.</p>
<p>
In preparation for 2020 projects, we’re growing and looking for a highly skilled Microsoft Dynamics 365/AX Finance Solution Consultant with deep expertise in Finance functionality to join our Delivery and Support team. You will be working with our certified Microsoft experts as well as working with other global leading partners to transform business by connecting people and processes, and by making management and operations easier.</p>
<p>
We recognize that our people are our most important resource. That’s why Avantiico believes in providing our people with professional development, offering diverse and challenging work, and providing work-life balance with less than 35% travel.</p>
<p><b>
RESPONSIBILITIES</b></p>
<p>
Finance Solution Consultant will be responsible for working on customer implementation, upgrade, and support of Microsoft Dynamics® 365 Finance and Operations and AX (D365/AX). Working in partnership with Avantiico’s Delivery &amp; Support team, the Finance Solution Consultant will be responsible for gathering business requirements, fit/gap analysis findings, functional design documents, test scripts, training documents, and progress updates throughout implementation lifecycle. Additionally, this position will assist in pre-sales/marketing activities such as blogs, videos, and webinars.<br>
<br>
You will require extensive business and application expertise in Microsoft Dynamics 365/AX Finance modules including General Ledger, Accounts Payable, Accounts Receivable, Cash and Bank Management, Fixed Assets, Budgeting, and other financial focused areas. Acting as a solution advisor and managing client relations is vital to the role. Additionally, the right candidate will be an individual with strong attention to detail in business process improvements and the ability to see the big picture.<br>
<br>
Avantiico is a high-performance workplace; the Finance Solution Consultant is expected to lead multiple projects with competing deadlines successfully. Avantiico is a leader in the Dynamics ERP and CRM industry and is expected to continue its significant growth, which will provide the right candidate ample opportunity for professional development.<br>
<br>
This is a full-time position based in either San Diego, Irvine, Los Angeles, Temecula, CA. This job will require up to 35% travel or more if working remotely.</p>
<p><br>
<b>SKILLS &amp; EXPERIENCE</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Must have a Bachelor’s degree, preferably in Management, Accounting, Finance, Management Information Systems or Information Technology</li>
<li>
Minimum of six years of relevant experience with focus in finance and accounting</li>
<li>
Minimum of four years of experience in Microsoft Dynamics 365/AX and two full lifecycle implementations</li>
<li>
Deep knowledge of D365/AX Finance module and functional capabilities including General Ledger, Accounts Payable, Accounts Receivable, Cash and Bank Management, Sales Order, Fixed Assets, Projects, and other financial focused areas</li>
<li>
Cross-functional knowledge of two or more D365/AX modules and functional capabilities in Analytics, Supply Chain (Trade &amp; Logistics), Manufacturing, Finance, Professional Services, Service Management, Transportation, Talent, and Retail</li>
<li>
Knowledge of other D365/AX modules and functional areas including Field Service, Sales &amp; Marketing, Project Service Automation, Artificial Intelligence, E-Commerce, Customer Engagement (CRM), and machine learning is a plus</li>
<li>
Has a proven track record of successfully delivering complex, full life-cycle implementations of D365/AX in a Consulting role</li>
<li>
Understanding of Life Cycle Services (LCS), Microsoft Sure Step, and Microsoft trends and specific best practices is a must</li>
<li>
Current certification a plus or the ability to obtain after joining within 90 days of employment</li>
<li>
Possess excellent communication and presentation skills</li>
</ul>
