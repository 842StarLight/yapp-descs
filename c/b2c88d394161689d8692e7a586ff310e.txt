The Systems Engineer is responsible for the design, documentation, configuration and support of integrated audio/visual systems. Additional responsibilities are:
<br><br>
<b>Job Responsibilities:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Conducts technical research on all audio/visual systems</li>
<li>Design, draws and documents audio/visual systems using Microsoft Visio and AutoCAD programs</li>
<li>Ensures that all system designs adhere with Presentation Products standards</li>
<li>Ensures that rack design specifications are communicated to Rack Specialist</li>
<li>Ensures completion of design and other technical product documentation</li>
<li>Provides technical sales support, client support and solutions and provides training for technicians</li>
<li>Seeks continual training and development relating to relevant audio/visual systems</li>
<li>All other duties as assigned by supervisor</li>
</ul>
<b>Minimum Requirements:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>AutoCAD experience REQUIRED ( either practical or theoretical)</li>
<li>Experience performing each of the job responsibilities outlined above in an associated industry preferred</li>
<li>High school diploma or equivalency</li>
<li>College degree in technology or engineering</li>
<li>CTS and CTS-D certifications preferred</li>
<li>PC literacy to include Microsoft Office applications, AutoCAD, Microsoft Visio</li>
<li>Must posses strong organizational, efficiency and detail-oriented skill sets</li>
<li>Professional presentation and demeanor</li>
</ul>
