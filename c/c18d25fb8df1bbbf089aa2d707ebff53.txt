Component Designer – Cold Formed Steel
<br><br>
Transforms initial rough product designs using BIM (Revit), HSBCAD (AutoCAD Architectural) into working documents by creating highly detailed models. Produce detailed permitting, construction, and shop plans for component (cold formed steel) aspects of residential multifamily structures. Familiar with a variety of the field's concepts, practices, materials, and procedures. Relies on experience and judgment to plan and accomplish goals. Performs a variety of complicated tasks. May direct and lead the work of others. Typically reports to a manager or head of a unit/department. A wide degree of creativity and latitude is expected. Frequent trips to sites and or factory will be required.
<br><br>
Essential Duties &amp; Responsibilities
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Produces designs for cold formed steel. Provides layouts, performs engineering, and takeoff services using ability to understand blueprints and customer requirements for specific projects.</li>
<li>Co-ordinates with sales, estimating, production, engineering and the architects to achieve customer schedules and cost objectives.</li>
<li>Resolves architectural and structural design conflicts.</li>
<li>Ensures project files, engineering seals and records are properly maintained.</li>
<li>Understands loading conditions and utilizes design software packages.</li>
<li>Computes load and grade requirements and material stress factors to determine design specifications based on customer's structural design needs.</li>
<li>Ensures economical and proper use of materials per current inventory levels and material costs.</li>
<li>Demonstrates a strong working knowledge of the capabilities of the factory equipment and produces highly cost effective and production friendly component designs.</li>
<li>Performs job site inspections and resolves customer service issues associated with the job.</li>
<li>Produces materials list (BOM).</li>
</ul>
Required Skills &amp; Experience
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Associate's degree in a technical program plus two (2) years related experience or an equivalent combination of education and experience</li>
<li>Experience in cold formed steel design</li>
<li>3-5 Years of HSBCAD, MiTek, Alpine, Strucsoft, AGA CAD, or Vision REZ design software</li>
<li>2-3 Years preparing shop, construction, and permitting documents in one of the above software platforms</li>
<li>5+ Years of industry experience</li>
<li>Preferred experience with HSBCAD</li>
<li>Detail oriented</li>
<li>Organized &amp; excellent communication skills</li>
<li>Word &amp; Excel</li>
<li>Hands on construction experience is a plus</li>
<li>Demonstrated commitment to diversity, learning and successfully working in multicultural environments</li>
</ul>
Preferred Skills &amp; Experience
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Ability to read blueprints and visualize structural details in three dimensions</li>
<li>Understands code requirements and loading conditions</li>
<li>Understands field requirements and constructability of panelized components</li>
<li>Strong command of mathematical concepts of plane and solid geometry and trigonometry</li>
<li>Expertise in HSBCAD, MiTek, Alpine, Strucsoft, AGA CAD, or Vision REZ design software</li>
<li>Microsoft Office applications</li>
<li>Excellent verbal and written communication skills</li>
<li>Energetic and self-motivated, able to work in a fast-paced environment</li>
</ul>
