<h2>Product Management Lead - Cloud and Machine Learning</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>Job Description<br>
<br>
<p>This is a unique opportunity to join a fast-growing company that is changing the way companies use data to power their business. You will be a key member of the product management team, helping define strategy and vision for our Machine Learning and Cloud Offerings and work with engineering, marketing and sales to execute on that vision. This position is ideal for someone who has broad technology understanding, loves building category-defining products, and is not afraid to roll up their sleeves to make things happen.</p>
<p><b>
Key Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Be a market and product expert. Own the strategy and roadmap for your areas of the Kinetica product. Have a well-informed and articulated point-of-view and drive consensus across the organization to make your vision a reality.</li>
<li>
Be the voice of the customer within the organization.</li>
<li>
Clearly define market and product requirements and work with engineering to turn them into compelling product capabilities.</li>
<li>
Collaborate with sales, sales engineering, marketing, and product marketing to drive the end-to-end product launch process.</li>
</ul>
