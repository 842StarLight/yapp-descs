Overview:<br>
A team lead is responsible for the overall shift operations, sales performance, and execution of brand excellence in a store.<br>
Team leads typically supervise a staff of 1 to 8 team members and carry out management responsibilities consistent with Jamba Juice policies, procedures and legal requirements.<br>
<br>
Reporting Relationship: General Manager<br>
<br>

<li>Current Jamba team member or commensurate rood/retail service experience</li>
<li>Background and/or interest in nutrition is highly desirable</li>
<li>Current High School enrollment or High School graduate</li>
<li>Computer skills including some Microsoft software and register skills</li>
<li>Able to lift 30-40 pounds regularly throughout a four to eight hour shift</li>
<li>Embodies Jamba culture &amp; values will make a good representative to the Jamba Brand (i.e.</li>
