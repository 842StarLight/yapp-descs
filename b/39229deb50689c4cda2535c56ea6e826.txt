<h2>Customer Support Engineer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Why APCON?</b></p>
<p>
APCON, an industry leader in network visibility and security solutions, provides an unparalleled level of confidence to enterprise and midsize businesses seeking network insights for enhanced investigation, threat detection and response. Our customers include Fortune 1000 companies to midsize organizations as well as government and defense agencies. Organizations in over 40 countries depend on APCON solutions.</p>
<p><b>
Purpose of Job</b></p>
<p>
The Customer Support Engineer will be the first point of contact for responding to APCON's customer inquiries. This will require working collaboratively with the APCON team and customers to drive timely and quality technical services and customer support. This individual will report into APCON's Professional Services Department and will have the opportunity to learn about networking technologies.</p>
<p><b>
Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Responsible for providing excellent customer service and support</li>
<li>
Provides prompt troubleshooting and technical support on APCON solutions</li>
<li>
Issues and tracks issue ticketing through task resolution and completion</li>
<li>
Partners with internal teams to resolve issues and escalates customer support issue to appropriate parties when necessary</li>
<li>
Participates in technical customer support initiatives including conducting remote product evaluations, configurations, trainings, and troubleshooting</li>
<li>
Focuses on continuous service improvement based on quality metrics</li>
<li>
Compiles customer feedback and delivers it appropriately within APCON</li>
<li>
Collaborates with internal and external teams to define technical support needs and new feature requests</li>
<li>
Assists with pre-sales activities as needed</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Qualifications and Requirements</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
5+ years of experience as a technical customer support engineer and/or system engineer</li>
<li>
Ability to communicate technical information effectively to technical and non-technical stakeholders</li>
<li>
Knowledge of enterprise IT infrastructures, management, monitoring, and security</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Core Competencies</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Strong interpersonal skills; ability to communicate professionally across the organization</li>
<li>
Excellent organizational, planning, presentation, troubleshooting, and multi-tasking skills</li>
<li>
Ability to maintain a high level of confidentiality and professionalism</li>
<li>
Strong attention to detail and exceptional customer support</li>
<li>
Proficient in Microsoft Office Suite</li>
<li>
Must be able to work independently and as a team member</li>
<li>
Ability to prioritize and be flexible in a fast-paced environment</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Education</b></p>

Degree and/or equivalent certifications/experience</li></ul>
