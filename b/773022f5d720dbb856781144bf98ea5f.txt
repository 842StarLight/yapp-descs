<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
POSITION SUMMARY:</b> The Assistant Fitness Sales Manager is responsible for all aspects of personal training sales. The AFM will be responsible for leading and managing personal training department in absence of the Fitness Manager.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
DUTIES INCLUDE:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Generate personal training sales through selling services to new and existing members</li>
<li>Generate, qualify, and track leads per sales system</li>
<li>Follow up on all prospects by using established sales systems</li>
<li>Enroll new members in services and follow up to ensure customer satisfaction</li>
<li>Maintain orderly and accurate Personal Training System records</li>
<li>Prepare for, attend and actively participate in sales meetings</li>
<li>Assist Fitness Manager administrative and management duties as needed and assigned by Fitness Manager</li>
<li>Perform personal training sessions as needed</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p>
</p>
<p><b>POSITION REQUIREMENTS</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Minimum of 1 year of proven success at the personal trainer level or related industry experience</li>
<li>1 year of related industry or sales experience preferred</li>
<li>High school diploma or General Education Diploma (GED)</li>
<li>Bachelor’s degree in related field preferred</li>
<li>Current accredited personal training certification or degreed physical therapist, physical therapist assistant, or athletic trainer.</li>
<li>Must have current certification from an NCCA accredited PT certification body: AAPTE, ACTION, ACSM, ACE, CI, CSCCA, IFPA, NASM, NCSF, NESTA, NETA, NFPT, NSCA, PMA.</li>
<li>Current CPR and AED certification required</li>
<li>Great personality and passion for fitness necessary</li>
<li>Have a high level of professionalism, honesty, integrity and work ethic</li>
<li>Exhibit a superior sense of customer service</li>
<li>Have working knowledge of computer software programs including MS Word, Excel, and Outlook</li>
<li>Willingness to work flexible hours</li>
<li>Bilingual in Spanish is a plus.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p>
</p>
<p><b>Compensation:</b> Competitive pay, bonus, commission. Benefits available for full time status.</p>
