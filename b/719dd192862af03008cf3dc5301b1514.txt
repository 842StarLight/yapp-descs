<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Job Description:
</b></h2>
<p></p>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"></h3>
<p><b>Pentair currently has an opening for a Field Applications Specialist to join our team. In this role, the Field Applications Specialist will be responsible for interacting with Dealers and Installers to drive profitable new business and expand current relationships. The Field Applications Specialist is expected to sell, provide technical expertise, train and manage the Dealer and Installer customer base.
</b></p>
<p></p>
<p><b>We will:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li><b>Create career growth and rotational opportunities within various areas of the organization
</b></li>
<li><b>Pay competitively
</b></li>
<li><b>Provide on the job training and mentoring in Pentair’s proven best practices
</b></li>
<li><b>Offer health benefits &amp; coaching
</b></li>
<li><b>Support your long term retirement goals through a competitive 401K matching and stock purchase plan
</b></li>
<li><b>Offer employee discounts
</b></li>
<li><b>Protect you with life insurance and other options
</b></li>
<li><b>Tell you more about all the great benefits – just ask!
</b></li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>You will:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li><b>Develop a strong technical understanding of Flow Technologies products across the entire Professional channel/portfolio and how they address customer needs.
</b></li>
<li><b>Direct the analysis of customer specifications to determine proper platform and accessories required with regards to Drives.
</b></li>
<li><b>Meet with customers as needed to convert customer concepts/designs into workable product solutions.
</b></li>
<li><b>Provide expertise and recommend solutions to application and performance problems related to pumps in the field.
</b></li>
<li><b>Build relationships with key Dealers and influence sales of Pentair products and services.
</b></li>
<li><b>Promote Pentair’s products &amp; brands effectively utilizing marketing programs, leveraging field resources and distributor and end-user product trainings.
</b></li>
<li><b>Identify growth opportunities through management and execution of an opportunity funnel or action plan in partnership with the Territory Sales Managers.
</b></li>
<li><b>Hold regular technical presentations, training seminars and provide on-site support.
</b></li>
<li><b>Communicate customer feedback for future product developments.<br>
</b></li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>You should:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li><b>Have earned a Bachelor’s degree (B.S., B.A.).
</b></li>
<li><b>Have 3 or more years of experience in a technical sales/specification role.
</b></li>
<li><b>Possess strong communication skills, both verbal and written.
</b></li>
<li><b>Have the ability to handle multiple priorities, organize, plan, meet difficult deadlines and multi-task in a fast-paced environment.
</b></li>
<li><b>Have strong experience in the application of Water Well pumps and pumping equipment.
</b></li>
<li><b>Possess strong interpersonal, negotiation, follow-up and documentation skills.
</b></li>
<li><b>Have a working knowledge of computer applications including MS Office suite products (Word, Excel, PowerPoint, Outlook) and Customer Relationship Management (CRM) software.
</b></li>
<li><b>Have the ability to make group presentations.
</b></li>
<li><b>Be a high energy self-starter that is goal-oriented and eager to meet and exceed goals and targets.
</b></li>
</ul>
