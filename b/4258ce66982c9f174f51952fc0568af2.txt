<h2>Strategic Account Leader (Miami)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">This position is remote, based in Miami.
<br><br>
Responsibilities
<br>----------------
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Strategic Account Leader (EMEA) will report to the CRO or a regional director over the course of the year</li>
<li>Act as a primary point of contact and the face of GitLab for our prospects.</li>
<li>Contribute to post-mortem analysis on wins/losses.</li>
<li>Communicate lessons learned to the team, including account managers, the marketing team, and the technical team.</li>
<li>Take ownership of your book of business</li>
<li>document the buying criteria</li>
<li>document the buying process</li>
<li>document next steps and owners</li>
<li>ensure pipeline accuracy based on evidence and not hope</li>
<li>Contribute to documenting improvements in our sales handbook ( https://about.gitlab.com/handbook/sales/ ).</li>
<li>Provide account leadership and direction in the pre- and post-sales process</li>
<li>Be the voice of the customer by contributing product ideas to our public issue tracker ( https://gitlab.com/gitlab-org/gitlab-ee/issues ).</li>
</ul>
Requirements
<br>------------
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>A true desire to see customers benefit from the investment they make with you</li>
<li>2+ years of experience with B2B sales</li>
<li>Fluent in Spanish</li>
<li>Interest in GitLab, and open source software</li>
<li>Ability to leverage established relationships and proven sales techniques for success</li>
<li>Effective communicator, strong interpersonal skills</li>
<li>Motivated, driven and results oriented</li>
<li>Excellent negotiation, presentation and closing skills</li>
<li>Preferred experience with Git, Software Development Tools, Application Lifecycle Management</li>
<li>You share our values ( https://about.gitlab.com/handbook/values ), and work in accordance with those values.</li>
</ul>
Hiring Process
<br>--------------
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Selected candidates will be invited to schedule a screening call with our Global Recruiters</li>
<li>Candidates will then be invited to schedule a second interview with Sales Director - EMEA</li>
<li>Next, candidates will be invited to schedule a first interview with Sales Director - US</li>
<li>Candidates will be invited to schedule a third interview with our CRO</li>
<li>Finally, candidates may have an interview with our CEO</li>
</ul>
Compensation
<br>------------
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>You will typically get 50% as base and 50% based on commission. See our market segmentation ( https://about.gitlab.com/handbook/sales/#market-segmentation ) for typical quotas in the U.S. Also see the Sales Compensation Plan ( https://about.gitlab.com/handbook/finance/sales-comp-plan/ ).</li>
</ul>
Remote-MIA</div>
