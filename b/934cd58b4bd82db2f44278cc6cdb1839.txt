<h2>Regional Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Regional Manager</b></p>
 <p><b>Who You Are:</b></p>
<p>The primary objective of the position of Regional Manager is to lead and develop our team of RetailXperts to influence market share for our clients in a tactical environment within their region. The Regional Manager is responsible for the day-to-day operations of a remote Regional Field Team, along with the management of operations/administration, coaching and guiding team members to achieve all of the sales, branding and metrics/goals set forth by the client and CCS. Sometimes involves collaborating with the client as an expert in field marketing to help the client achieve measurable success. Typically has 4 years retail sales or customer service experience with 3-5 years’ experience leading remotes teams in a large territory.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>What You Will Do:</b></p>
<p><b>Talent Management and Development of RetailXperts</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Manages execution of seasonal programs within an assigned region. This involves delivering training, organizing schedule, and hiring staffing</li>
 <li>Manages the team of RetailXperts in assigned region. Acts as main point of contact for day to day concerns or questions, performance, training, removes obstacles and ensure success for team member and program</li>
<li>Travels to Retail Xperts markets to monitor team members performance, build relationship and engagement, and coach for development and success</li>
<li>Ensures that all RetailXperts are trained on reporting processes, in-store communication and retailer expectations, product knowledge, and internal CCS procedures; includes ensuring that new hire orientations and online training modules are completed.</li>
<li>Ensures visits are staffed, scheduled and executed; includes verifying RetailXperts are doing the work they report by executing phone audits and verifying check-in reports to meet metric requirements.</li>
<li>Monitors reporting to ensure same day completion.</li>
<li>Identifies staffing needs, interviews and hires RetailXperts in an assigned territory, and works effectively with internal/external recruiting teams ensuring stores are fully staffed.</li>
<li>Complies and adheres to company policies concerning disciplinary, termination, performance, and conduct issues in communications with Retail Xperts.</li>
<li>Coaches staff as part of regular lines of open communication, and to create learning opportunities through discussion of day-to-day operational issues.</li>
<li>Monitors onboarding process to ensure RetailXperts hiring experience is positive, efficient, and issue free.</li>
<li>Ensures RetailXperts have tools to complete their job from attire to demonstration product(s).</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Client Management</b></p>
 <ul>
<li>Build and maintain long-term trusting relationships with client representatives when opportunities occur.</li>
 <li>Understand Clients KPI’s and how you drive those KPI’s with the accountability of RetailXperts.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Operations, Financial, Reporting</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Communicates effectively to Client Partnership Manager and leadership including summary reports for territory, identification, and timely communication of potential client issues, tracking metrics for performance management, conference calls, and establishing individual and program goals.</li>
 <li>Monitors and maintains regional P&amp;L, includes accountability for expense control, includes ensuring that all expenses are within program guidelines</li>
</ul>
<p><b>What You Will Need:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>High School diploma or GED, 4 year undergraduate degree preferred in business, marketing, or communications</li>
 <li>3-5 years previous management experience required, with experience leading a field management team</li>
 <li>Client/Account Management experience and understanding desirable.</li>
 <li>Experience in/working knowledge of retail sales, retail operations and/or service organizations where client satisfaction is a measurement of success, 4 years preferred.</li>
 <li>Motivational skills to encourage and inspire employees in coaching and development efforts; ability to drive performance of others</li>
 <li>Strong verbal and written communication skills and interpersonal skills, with the ability to build and maintain relationships with stakeholders at all levels</li>
 <li>Process and operationally minded with strong time management and organization skills; ability to effectively manage multiple and conflicting priorities</li>
 <li>Demonstrated collaboration, problem solving, and critical thinking skills</li>
 <li>Ability to work through ambiguity, follow through, and meet deadlines under pressure</li>
 <li>Strong formal presentation skills; ability to present on a variety of work-related topics on a regular basis</li>
 <li>Intermediate to advanced skills in Microsoft Office (Outlook, Word, Excel, PowerPoint) and related computer hardware and software</li>
 <li>Must be available and willing to work on weekends</li>
 <li>Must be able to see, hear, speak and write clearly in English to communicate with colleagues and customers in person and on the phone. Regular walking, standing and sitting. Manual dexterity required for occasional reaching and lifting of small objects and operating office equipment. The ability to lift and carry up to 15 lbs.</li>
 <li>Typical Monday through Friday schedule, 40 hours/week. Travel requirements depends on breadth market – could be 50%-60%.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Who We Are:</b></p>
<p>Fueled by thousands of talented individuals, the Creative Channel Group is a leading marketing agency that specializes in creating inspired shopper experiences by connecting global brands, retailers and consumers at critical moments of recommendation and purchase. Since 1995, Creative Channel has achieved phenomenal growth as both an industry innovator and market leader in creating purchase passion: a powerful, potentially life- long emotional connection, created between a shopper and a brand.</p>
