<h2>Inside Sales Representative</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>GuildQuality is a SaaS company that helps the best homebuilders, remodelers and contractors in North America deliver an exceptional customer experience. Our members represent the “best of the best” in the residential real estate and construction industry. Founded in 2003, GuildQuality is squarely at the intersection of reputation management, real estate and home improvement. We are backed by one of the largest private equity firms in the country and are looking for talented, energetic and motivated people to join our team to continue to build an extraordinary company.</p>
<p><b>
You:</b><br>
You have a relentless drive to improve the lives of small business owners and a background of thriving in a competitive sales environment, persevering through adversity and learning from your experiences. Preferably, a portion of your personal development has come from selling a product in the software industry on a phone-based sales team for 1-3 years.</p>
<p>
You’re looking to take your scrappiness, resilience and sales fundamentals and apply them in a SaaS sales role within a rapidly growing company.</p>
<p><b>
Us:</b><br>
GuildQuality is a growth stage startup company with a proven sales engine. We set our sales reps up with leads and all the support they need to hit their goals and help contractors, homebuilders and remodelers sign up for our SaaS based solution to enhance their customer experience.</p>
<p><b>
Where:</b><br>
GuildQuality is located in Midtown Atlanta, right next to SweetWater Brewery. Our collaborative, open-plan office space is filled with high-energy, talented people who enjoy our weekly lunches, monthly happy hours, ping pong and hanging out with their co-workers.</p>
<p><b>
Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Identify, initiate, and nurture relationships with sales-ready leads both over the phone and via email</li>
<li>Run and manage the entire sales cycle, from initial discovery call to closing the deal</li>
<li>Manage a sizeable pipeline of SMB prospects</li>
<li>Perform product demos using web tools to prospects and customers</li>
<li>Hunt and aggressively prospect new business</li>
<li>Light travel to trade shows and events might be required</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Experience and Skills:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor's Degree</li>
<li>1-3 years of experience in software or service industry sales</li>
<li>History of achievement in the face of adversity</li>
<li>Ability to thrive in a fast-paced, high-growth, rapidly changing environment</li>
<li>Good sales DNA and a hunter mentality</li>
<li>Excellent phone presence and strong writing skills</li>
<li>Proficiency with the Microsoft Office suite</li>
<li>Salesforce and Salesloft experience a plus</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Benefits and Perks:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Competitive salary</li>
<li>Robust medical benefits &amp; 401k</li>
<li>Gym stipend</li>
<li>Results Oriented Work Environment (ROWE), when someone needs time off, they take it, no questions asked</li>
<li>Snacks, lunches and happy hours</li>
<li>Continued investment in your professional development</li>
<li>Open-book company. We share all of our key metrics and financial data.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Our Mission</b><br>
Every day, we strive to increase the prosperity of first-class builders, contractors, and home service providers by helping them grow their businesses while elevating the stature of the industry. We accomplish this by delighting in service, creating great products, and partnering with our members so that we can connect them with authentic customer feedback and generate the highest-quality leads.</p>
