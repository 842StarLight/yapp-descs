<h2>Lead QA / Test Engineer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>The Opportunity</b></p>
<p>
We are currently recruiting a Lead QA / Test Engineer to join the team in our Boston office.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Duties and responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Lead QA/testing team on overall testing activities, approaches and strategies.</li>
<li>
Establish, maintain and oversee all aspects of QA processes and methodologies to ensure effectiveness and alignment to industry best practices.</li>
<li>
Prepare, review and maintain testing documents (test plan, test cases, test reports) to ensure complete test coverage.</li>
<li>
Prepare customer demo presentations for project deliverables.</li>
<li>
Ensure proper execution by the testing team on different types of testing (functional, integration, end-to-end, performance, etc.).</li>
<li>
Maintain test environment. Must be able to work closely with Development Operations and the client to support deployment and testing of parallel releases for all channels and platforms.</li>
<li>
Prioritize testing activities.</li>
<li>
Efficient management of QA resources across multiple projects.</li>
<li>
Provide effort and estimates of testing activities required for the projects or deliverables.</li>
<li>
Provide strategic testing initiatives for projects to introduce efficient techniques and test productivity improvements.</li>
<li>
Manage deliverables to ensure that the testing activities remains within agreed scope, schedule, budget and quality.</li>
<li>
Work with project manager and development team to report and escalate relevant testing issues and challenges that arise during testing activites.</li>
<li>
Work and collaborate with other teams across different offices of the company when needed to achieve organisational objectives.</li>
<li>
Provide support and technical assistance to the customer's test teams.</li>
<li>
Provide support and technical asisstance to support engineer (e.g. recreate issues reported by the customer in production, or in support of customer queries).</li>
<li>
Perform QA team performance review and improvement.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Experience</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
An IT or related bachelor's degree qualification with software testing and QA/test team leading experience.</li>
<li>
Strong knowledge of Agile testing methodologies and best practices.</li>
<li>
Experience with development and testing of complex enterprise or customer-facing applications preferably on Point-of-Sale (POS) or Post Office services background.</li>
<li>
Familiar with bug tracking tools (e.g. Jira), continuous integration (e.g. Bamboo), source control (Perforce) and VMWare.</li>
<li>
Knowledge of xml.</li>
<li>
Familiar with Microsoft operating systems and device literacy across apple and android technologies.</li>
<li>
Testing experiences on webservices, batch/extract file processing, point-of-sale.</li>
<li>
Experience on automation test tools and programming (e.g. Selenium, WinAppDriver, Python) and designing or creating test frameworks.</li>
<li>
Expert knowledge of quality improvement, quality process, quality assurance methodologies and best practices.</li>
<li>
Strong organizational, writing and interpersonal communications with the ability to influence other at all levels.</li>
<li>
Test analysis skills and techniques (e.g. process improvement, identifying problems and requirements definitiion, propose solutions).</li>
</ul>
