Opportunity Overview:</b></div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
As an Account Developer, Digital Agency Services / Account Director / Business Development Executive for Perficient Digital, your primary responsibility will be to drive growth for Perficient Digital through the selling of services across our Digital Agency, Digital Consulting and Digital Marketing portfolios.. You will work with our existing Digital Client Partners and partner with other Business Development Executives to develop client solutions encompassing mobile technologies, web experiences, digital media/SEO and digital strategy both for existing Perficient clients and net-new clients.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
Leveraging your large personal network, you will establish and develop trusted advisor level relationships with Marketing executives across the B2B and B2C space. You will manage the sales process and pursuit strategies that leverage some of the strongest digital strategy, marketing, technical and execution capabilities in the market to craft cutting edge solutions to the challenges our clients and prospects face.</div>
