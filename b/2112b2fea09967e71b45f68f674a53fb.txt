<h2>Director of Business Systems</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Overview:</b></p>
<p>
As the Director of Business Systems, you will leverage your deep financial, business, and technical experience to implement initiatives across organizational boundaries to support our future business growth. This person will also be responsible for rapidly evolving the systems and processes supporting our FP; Accounting, Legal, HR and Sales Ops functions. You'll work closely with business partners to understand business requirements, establish priorities, and then align development resources to ensure predictable, high quality deliveries. The Director of Business Systems will evaluate our current business; financial systems, determine best practice improvements and project manage those improvements with the stakeholder groups to drive towards a best in class business system infrastructure. The Director will also drive the evaluation of potential new applications and solutions against business requirements, ensuring they are aligned to broader architectural and security standards and that they meet tactical and strategic objectives.</p>
<p><b>
Basic Functions:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Lead implementation and configuration of business applications to meet district requirements.</li>
<li>
Maintain current solutions including patching, updates and upgrades.</li>
<li>
Work with users to streamline business processes and systems.</li>
<li>
Stay abreast on technical advances and develop the collective capability of the business systems support team.</li>
<li>
Develop project priorities and timelines for applications updates/improvements.</li>
<li>
Create and maintain a culture of outstanding customer service and end-user experience</li>
<li>
Implement policies, procedures and guidelines to ensure data integrity.</li>
<li>
Develop, manage, measure and report on key service-level metrics, including service level agreements, productivity, and customer satisfaction</li>
<li>
Build and maintain positive relationships with school leadership, campus leadership, and other district departments relating to business applications as well as with other IT teams to ensure that overall IT services and goals are mutually understood and delivered.</li>
<li>
Work collaboratively with other technology teams to design system architectures, standards and policies.</li>
<li>
Supports workforce planning and talent acquisition strategies by effectively hiring and developing diverse talent for the organization</li>
<li>
Adhere to all district policies and procedures</li>
<li>
Maintain high ethical standards</li>
<li>
Perform other tasks and duties as assigned</li>
</ul>
