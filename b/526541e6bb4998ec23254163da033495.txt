<h2>Pardot Marketing Automation Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Position Summary:
</b></p>
<p>Be a part of the critical team that keeps modern marketing humming at Illumina. We are looking for a marketing system professional to oversee marketing automation utilization, administration, maintenance, and support. The Marketing Automation Specialist is the subject matter expert and administrator of the marketing automation platform, defining, developing, deploying, and managing systems, process, and training to drive best practice usage and meet business objectives.
</p>
<p></p>
<p>The Marketing Automation Specialist oversees and executes complex automated marketing programs and supports development and execution of other marketing programs and critical projects that support data management, lead workflow, lead scoring, lead nurturing, and database integrity. You understand how effective campaigns generate interest in our products and services. And how repeated marketing touches will convert prospects to highly qualified marketing leads for a world-class B2B sales force.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Tasks and Responsibilities:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Administer and serve as super-user of marketing automation platform.
</li>
<li>Set up, test, deploy, monitor, track, and report on campaign programs.
</li>
<li>Develop and manage governance policies and processes to ensure best practice usage of marketing automation platform by platform users.
</li>
<li>Document and maintain all marketing automation platform processes and procedures.
</li>
<li>Create documentation outlining campaign set-up and procedures as well as “how-to” guides for other platform team members and configuration personnel.
</li>
<li>Collaborate with marketing teams in development and implementation of key demand creation, automated nurturing, lead scoring, and lead management programs.
</li>
<li>Recommend and implement new platform integrations and workflow processes to increase the scalability and efficiency of the marketing organization.
</li>
<li>Deliver training on new functionality to platform users.
</li>
<li>Develop and test reports in partnership with the marketing analytics group.
</li>
<li>Troubleshoot platform related issues in partnership with headquarters and regional team members.
</li>
<li>Advise on effective use of marketing automation for business goals.
</li>
<li>Advise and participate in annual technology reviews for the upgrade or replacement of existing campaign and lead scoring technology.
</li>
<li>Partner with GIS marketing automation lead on all integrations with other enterprise systems.
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>

</b></p>
<ul><li>Bachelor’s degree required
</li></ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Preferred Experiential Background:
</b></p>
<ul>

</li>
<li>Pardot certification preferred.
</li>
<li>Excellent communication skills.
</li>
<li>Able to work with accuracy and attention to detail required.
</li>
<li>Ability to work independently and as part of a team.
</li>
<li>Demonstrated written and verbal communication and interpersonal skills.
</li>
<li>Self-motivated and know when to seek guidance.
</li>
<li>Comfortable working in a fast-paced environment where strategies or campaigns can quickly shift as business needs warrant.
</li>
<li>Comfortable working with and communicating with a diverse team including corporate marketing, GIS, regional marketing, and outside vendors.
</li>
</ul>
