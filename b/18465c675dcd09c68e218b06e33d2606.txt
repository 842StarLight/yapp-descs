<p><b>Contact Center Loan Agent</b></p>
<p><b>
Position Purpose</b></p>
<p>
This position requires someone who can expertly multi-task in a fast paced environment. The Loan Agent position is a combination of service, sales, and effective communication through our online, phone and email channels. Loan Agents are responsible for being a member advocate and providing superior service to members and potential members of Fort Campbell Federal Credit Union with a commitment to simplifying the loan process in a fast-paced high-volume call center.</p>
<p><b>
Essential Functions and Basic Duties</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Provides courteous, professional, accurate, and personal service and acts as a member advocate regardless of the outcome or decision of the loan application.</li>
<li>Completes the loan application process that includes, but is not limited to the following: thorough and accurate applications; providing a comprehensive picture of member’s credit history; delivering loan approvals, denials, and counter-offer decisions; offering additional loan cross-sell opportunities; completing various loan packets for funding; and closing loans as needed.</li>
<li>Consistently meets or exceeds sales and production goals on a monthly basis.</li>
<li>Proactively promotes and sells ancillary credit union products and services to members and potential members.</li>
<li>Actively engages in follow-up contact through remote channels that include hone, email and chat with members to build and maintain strong relationships.</li>
<li>Responds to all member requests in a timely and professional manner.</li>
<li>Maintains current knowledge of all loan products including loan rules and requirements. Follows all standard operation procedures (SOPs) and state and federal regulations as applicable.</li>
<li>Makes sound decisions in ambiguous situations while minimizing the credit union’s exposure to unnecessary risk.</li>
<li>Communicates and coordinates effectively to create unity with other departments to enhance member experiences in regards to the loan process.</li>
<li>Makes outbound calls to members and potential members using call lists based on marketing promotions.</li>
<li>Inform members of loan application status based on information from the Loan Manager system.</li>
<li>Prepares documents required to successfully process loan applications and book all consumer loan types.</li>
<li>Adheres to assigned work schedule and break periods. Regular attendance and punctuality are required.</li>
<li>Attends and participates in all meetings, trainings and committees as required.</li>
<li>Participates in community and civic events to promote awareness of the Credit Union in the community.</li>
<li>Satisfactorily completes all required compliance training and complies with all regulations which apply within the scope of the position to include Bank Secrecy Act compliance.</li>
<li>Conveys a positive image and ensures that the Credit Union’s professional reputation is maintained both internally and externally.</li>
<li>Ensures that work area is clean, secure and well-maintained.</li>
<li>Performs other duties as assigned by management.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Performance Measurements</b></p>
<p>
Loan functions are efficiently, effectively, and accurately performed in accordance with established policies, standards, and security procedures.</p>
<p>
Good business relations exist with members. Members' problems or questions are courteously and promptly resolved.</p>
<p>
Good working relationships and coordination exist with area personnel and with management. Assistance is provided to other Service Representatives and staff as needed. Supervisors are appropriately informed of area activities.</p>
<p>
Assigned deadlines are met. Required reports and records are accurate, complete, and timely.</p>
<p>
The Credit Union's professional reputation is maintained and conveyed.</p>
<p>
Sales and Service Standards are met.</p>
