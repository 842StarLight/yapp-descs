<h2>Account Manager – Microsoft Dynamics</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>RSM US LLP is looking for a dynamic Client Account Manager to facilitate the growth of our Technology &amp; Management Consulting (TMC) practice in our Boston market.</b></p>
<p><b>
Position Summary:</b></p>
<p>
RSM is looking for a dynamic Client Account Manager who will be responsible for driving revenue with existing Technology &amp; Management Consulting (TMC) clients, focusing on TMC services including business applications, infrastructure, IT managed services and management consulting.</p>
<p>
The Client Account Manager will be responsible for leading all aspects of the sales process for new initiatives, including systematic client targeting, developing opportunity-specific sales strategy, developing of client account expansion plans, selecting pursuit teams and “quarterbacking” the entire sales process.</p>
<p>
This individual will be a growth leader and support the efforts of various firm growth teams through direct prospecting, networking/participation with various industry groups and networking associations. They will also work with technology channel partners like Microsoft and/or NetSuite to identify solutions for existing client accounts.</p>
<p><b>
Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Identify, target and sell RSM’s TMC solutions to current RSM clients; execute the sales plan and process, including the coordination of all necessary resources to best position the firm to secure new business</li>
<li>
Implement team strategy for driving revenue with existing client accounts; work with Partners, Directors and other client servers to prioritize existing firm clients, and create client expansion account plans to include cross-selling of opportunities outside of the TMC services when appropriate</li>
<li>
Achieve stated growth goals through demonstrated mastery of services offerings, account plan execution and sales process best practices implementation</li>
<li>
Work closely with existing account teams to review pipeline, client feedback and status of client projects; communicate with consultants regarding upcoming and ongoing client projects; assist in making resourcing recommendations for upcoming projects</li>
<li>
Work closely with local and national marketing resources to develop effective, targeted go-to-market plans for RSM clients within targeted markets and/or industries</li>
<li>
Develop and maintain a pipeline of client opportunities</li>
<li>
Work closely with National Sales Organization management to provide ongoing, current feedback relative to market opportunities within the firm’s CRM<br>
</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Basic Qualifications:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Bachelor’s degree</li>
<li>
Minimum of 5+ years of professional services sales and/or account management experience, preferably in a consulting firm environment</li>
<li>
Minimum of 2+ years of prior experience selling ERP and/or CRM solutions</li>
<li>
Prior experience working with client accounts to resolve complex client needs and issues</li>
<li>
Prior experience leveraging a CRM tool for report generation and sales tracking</li>
<li>
Prior solution-selling training is a plus, including programs developed by Microsoft and/or NetSuite for their respective channels</li>
<li>
Prior experience in developing relationships with Technology Channel Partners, including Microsoft and/or NetSuite</li>
<li>
Exhibit exceptionally strong communication, presentation, analytical and organizational skills</li>
<li>
Travel is required (local and overnight when appropriate)</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Preferred Qualifications:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Software sales experience with Microsoft Dynamics, Netsuite, or Intacct</li>
<li>
Familiarity with third party solutions to extend these platforms</li>
</ul>
