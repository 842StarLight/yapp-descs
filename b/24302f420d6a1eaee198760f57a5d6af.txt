<h2>Technical Marketing Engineer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Job Summary:</b></p>
<p>
Technical Marketing Engineers at Devo have the impactful role of influencing top-line revenue through product evangelism, sales enablement, and the creation of technical and compelling content - just to name a few. This is an exciting role where technology meets marketing, you will be a highly valued member of the team working to ensure that the sales cycle is as short as possible. In this role you will become a product expert and also remain close to clients. You will work with an energetic cross-functional team to make Devo successful.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Essential Duties and Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Enable the pre-sales team to sell more effectively by creating the compelling technical content they need (e.g. presentations, white papers, demos, etc.).</li>
<li>
Educate and train the sales team to sell more effectively by translating technical product capabilities and mapping them to the Devo marketing message.</li>
<li>
Contribute and build out our competitive intelligence capabilities.</li>
<li>
Assist with ad-hoc requests from sales (e.g. RFIs, questionnaires, and other technical requests).</li>
<li>
Support sales and marketing events (e.g. webinars, tradeshows, regional events, etc.).</li>
<li>
Support product launch activities.</li>
<li>
Identify internal process and capabilities gaps and address them</li>
<li>
Work closely with Product Marketing, Product Management, and the sales team to drive tangible results</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Qualifications Experience:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Bachelor’s degree in a technical field preferred.</li>
<li>
6+ years of professional work experience in a similar or related position.</li>
<li>
Customer and field experience is desirable, but not required.</li>
<li>
Domain expertise and experience with security, IT operations, and log management technologies.</li>
<li>
Understanding of the challenges in Security and Data Operations within the enterprise.</li>
<li>
Demonstrated strong written and verbal communication skills.</li>
<li>
Driven, with an entrepreneurial spirit and sense of urgency. Must be resourceful proactive.</li>
<li>
Highly organized, detail-oriented and possessing a high level of integrity.</li>
<li>
You thrive in a startup culture that is fast moving and changing.</li>
</ul>
