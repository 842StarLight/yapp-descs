<p>
Job Summary:</p>
<p>
Administers, plans and directs operations which provide quality and cost effective food service and nutritional care for its patients, staff and customers.</p>
<p>
Actively promotes a Lean work culture by performing team member duties to encourage consistent use of LEAN principles and processes, including continually seeking work process improvements. Recognizes the necessity of taking ownership of one’s own motivation, morale, performance and professional development. Strives for behavior consistent with being committed to Excela’s missions, vision and values.</p>
