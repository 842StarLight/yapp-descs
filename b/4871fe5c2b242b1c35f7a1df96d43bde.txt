<p><b>
Job Description</b><br>
<br>
Ampere is looking for a talented engineer to work as a Senior Field Applications Engineer (FAE)/ Solutions Engineer and Senior Member of Technical Staff with special emphasis on some of the largest Cloud services provider in the world, with a focus on customers headquartered in the Pacific Northwest of the U.S.. They will also work with the customers to respond to RFPs, optimize workloads to Ampere server architecture and drive new server opportunities through evaluation, proof of concept and production phases.<br>
<br>
This candidate will be accountable to the Server Sales Team and the Field Engineering Director for all aspects of technical engagements required to support sales and account objectives. This position requires the FAE to have a broad technical understanding of server CPUs, platform architecture and the software ecosystem required to evaluate and optimize applications that run at the node, rack and datacenter scale. The position requires specialized knowledge of server, storage and networking software environments including Operating systems (Windows, Linux), Hypervisors, compilers, benchmarks, and targeted applications that may be used to validate server platforms for the webscale and edge markets.</p>
