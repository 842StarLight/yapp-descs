<h2>Marketing Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Celebrate is a nutritional supplement company that specializes in developing products for both surgical and medical weight loss patients. On the surgical side, we have more patient options, more flavors, and more delivery systems than any other bariatric supplement company. Our surgical products contain the highest level of supplementation, always meet the ASMBS Guidelines, and are the most affordable, overall, to patients. Our Medical Weight Loss program, The Path To Success, is a medically supervised weight loss program that focuses on net carbs and places patients in mild ketosis. With our program, patients can expect to experience rapid, safe weight loss while choosing from a 3-stage approach. We provide 4 low-carbohydrate meal plans to support our programs that are all built on evidence-based research.</p>
