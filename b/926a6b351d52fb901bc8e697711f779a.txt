<h2>Morning Stock</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Position Title: Morning Stocker (Part-Time) – Retail Division</b></p>
<p><b>Position Purpose: </b></p>
<p>Morning Stockers are required to work early morning hours starting their daily shifts at <b>5 a.m</b>. and working until business needs dictate to ensure that incoming freight inventory and overstock inventory is brought out to the sales floor as quickly and efficiently as possible. Morning Stockers are required to handle all incoming freight inventory and existing overstock inventory and place the inventory on the sales floor so that it is properly merchandised according to planogram and ready for sale. Morning Stockers will need to have knowledge of product placement, be able to read and interpret planograms and understand SKU use and store policy/procedure regarding the handling of incoming freight inventory and overstock inventory. Morning Stockers must be able to provide guest service when needed. Morning Stockers are required to lift, carry and otherwise handle product and other objects of varying weight in order to carry out their duties in an effective and efficient manner. Morning Stockers have a physically demanding job and must be able to meet those demands on a daily basis.</p>
<p><b>Essential Duties and Responsibilities: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Responsible for keeping their assigned department’s shelves and displays full of product available for sale to Guests.</li>
<li>Morning Stockers must have knowledge about all products for sale in theirdepartment and be able to communicate with all Guests regarding the products offered for sale.</li>
<li>Responsible for following all store policy/procedure and directives.</li>
<li>Must be able to understand and implement store policy/procedure and management directives to ensure full compliance.</li>
<li>Must be able to operate a computer and fully understand all computer software programs utilized in the ordinary course of business.</li>
<li>Each Morning Stocker must maintain regular and predictable attendance.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Physical Requirements: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Lifting Requirement: Exerting up to 50 pounds of force occasionally, and up to 25 pounds of force frequently, and up to 10 pounds of force constantly to move inventory and other objects</li>
<li>Moving, standing, sitting, walking around store to perform administrative functions, and assist Guests and Team Members</li>
<li>Use of arms/hands, legs/feet, climbing stairs/ladders, bending, stooping, reaching, twisting, lifting, pushing, pulling and moving inventory and other objects</li>
<li>Talking, hearing, seeing and listening to effectively communicate and exchange information and interpret data for Guests and Team Members</li>
<li>Physical ability to operate a computer and computer software proficiency is required.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>DISCLAIMER: </b> This job description reflects management’s assignment of essential functions; and nothing in this herein restricts management’s right to assign or reassign duties and responsibilities to this job at any time.</p>
<p>Job Type: Part-time</p>
<p>Hours per week:</p>
<ul><li>20-29</li></ul>
