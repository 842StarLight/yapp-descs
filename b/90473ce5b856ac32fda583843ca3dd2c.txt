<h2>Staff Accountant</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">EyeCare Partners is currently building a tax department and is seeking a Senior Tax Staff Accountant to be a trusted member of the EyeCare Partners team. This position is part of Finance and reports to the Tax Manager. This individual will be responsible for administrative tasks associated with filing sales, use, and property taxes and the associated journal entries for this growing company.
<br><br>
<b>Position Summary:</b> Perform computations and submit payment of sales, use, property, and other miscellaneous taxes for thirteen states and multiple jurisdictions and projected future growth
<br><br>
<b>Responsibilities:</b><br>
Extracting monthly reporting from point of sales system(s) for computation of sales taxes for review by Accounting Manager
<br><br>
Extracting reporting monthly from Accounts Payable for computation of use taxes for review by Accounting Manager
<br><br>
Maintain master listing of locations and jurisdictions assessing property tax and ensure compliance with annual payments
<br><br>
Preparing journal entries for accruals of all sales, use and property tax monthly
<br><br>
Maintain listing of all accounts with states for legal entities and online access to submit payments monthly, quarterly or annually
<br><br>
Assisting with preparing monthly reconciliations for compliance with tax reporting to be reviewed by Senior Tax Accountant
<br><br>
Assist with applications and renewals of state and local business licenses
<br><br>
Preparation of sales and use tax reporting for filing of tax returns by third party
<br><br>
Assist with implementation of tax software for computing sales and use tax
<br><br>
<b>Requirements:</b><br>
Bachelor’s Degree in Accounting or Business
<br><br>
1-3 years’ experience in corporate tax or similar position
<br><br>
Highly skilled in use of Excel spreadsheets and other accounting software. Use of Microsoft Solomon/Dynamics a plus
<br><br>
Excellent communication (written and verbal) skills, customer service and organizational skills.
<br><br>
Excellent work habits, including a willingness to work the hours necessary to complete a task or project, especially when important deadlines cause greater than normal departmental pressures.
<br><br>
<b>Physical Requirements:</b><br>
While performing the duties of this job, the employee is regularly required to talk and hear. The employee is occasionally required to stoop, kneel, or crouch. The employee must be able to lift and/or move up to 10 pounds. Must be able to sit for an extended period of time.
<br><br>
Specific vision abilities required by this job include close vision, distance vision, color vision, peripheral vision, depth perception and ability to adjust focus.
<br><br>
<b>Work Environment:</b><br>
The job operates in a professional office environment. The role routinely uses standard office equipment such as computer, phones, photocopiers, filing cabinets and fax machines.
<br><br>
<b>Position Type/Expected Hours of Work:</b><br>
Some flexibility in hours is allowed, but the employee must be available during the “core” work hours of 8:00 a.m. to 6:00 p.m. Monday – Friday. Must work 40 hours each week to maintain full time status.
