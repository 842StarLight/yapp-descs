<h2>Project/Implementation Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div></div>
<div>
<p><b>Job Summary:</b></p>
<p>
Event Farm (a MemberSuite Company) is looking for an Implementation/Project Manager to manage the implementation of new Customers onto the Event Farm platform, as well as manage projects where an existing Customer is expanding their current program. In this role, you will be responsible for ensuring this pivotal part of the customer lifecycle is seamless and sets the customer up for success. This role will be specifically focused on the suite of attendee engagement applications customers use primarily onsite during an event.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Responsibilities and Duties:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Provide expert knowledge and best practices on the Event Farm implementation process for new Clients and existing Clients adding products/solutions to their current program.</li>
<li>
Oversee Small/Midsized Business (SMB) to Enterprise-level program implementations from the sales handoff to Customer Success and Service Delivery teams.</li>
<li>
Lead client kick-off calls to define objectives, deliverables and timelines.</li>
<li>
Build project plans, manage deliverables, report on the status of milestones and proactively identify project risks.</li>
<li>
Lead weekly project status calls with the support of internal Subject Matter Experts (SMEs).</li>
<li>
Initiate ongoing communications and follow-ups to ensure client program objectives are met.</li>
<li>
Set and reinforce expectations and deadlines with clients and ensure follow<br>
through on any commitments made by the Sales, Customer Success or Service<br>
Delivery teams.</li>
<li>
Work with client and appropriate Event Farm personnel to setup and configure<br>
systems and settings and coordinate training with the appropriate team specialist.</li>
<li>
Coordinate, and in some cases, lead onsite support for customer events that<br>
require technical support from Event Farm</li>
<li>
Ensure and advise customers on production execution to ensure they have the<br>
appropriate hardware, IT needs, and staffing plans to successfully execute their<br>
event and maximize the use of our software solutions.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Qualifications:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Bachelor's degree in Business or a related field and at least 2 years of experience in project management or implementing software for clients in a B2B SaaS environment.</li>
<li>
Must have a strong understanding of familiarity with mobile technologies (hardware and software) including iOS and Android devices, Near Field Communication (NFC), Radio Frequency Identification (RFID), etc.</li>
<li>
Experience working in and/or familiarity with event production onsite.</li>
<li>
Strong project management, organizational and analytical skills.</li>
<li>
Exemplary written and verbal communication skills.</li>
<li>
Strong interpersonal skills and the ability to set clear expectations and build effective relationships with internal teammates and clients.</li>
<li>
Ability to manage multiple implementations with tight deadlines.</li>
<li>
Highly confident presenter, with strong business acumen and dynamic and engaging presence.</li>
<li>
Adaptable attitude, sense of urgency and commitment to service excellence</li>
<li>
Knowledge of project management tools, excellent PowerPoint skills.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Company Overview:</b></p>
<p>
Event Farm is a MemberSuite company that offers an industry-leading event engagement software platform to commercial and non-profit organizations. Currently the company works with many of the Fortune 1000 including Nike, JP Morgan Chase, Levis, Microsoft, Guess, etc. as well as leading upcoming brands in the experiential marketing space including BeautyCon, GOOP, MediaLink, and dozens of agencies. We combine our extensive event expertise and suite of engagement technology—modernized registration tools, mobile check-in, and badge printing, attendee mobile apps, real-time attendee text messaging, wearable tech (NFC), and cutting-edge event websites—to make your innovative events possible.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Location:</b></p>
<p>
This role can be located in any of the following MemberSuite corporate offices: Santa Monica, CA or Atlanta GA but strong preference for Santa Monica.</p>
