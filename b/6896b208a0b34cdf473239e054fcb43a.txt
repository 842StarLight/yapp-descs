<h2>Sales/Marketing Intern (Winter 2020)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Make your mark in Broadcasting and Digital Media. Sinclair Broadcast Group and Sinclair Digital Solutions are dedicated to making Sinclair a communications powerhouse! We are the largest and most diversified television broadcasting company in the country. Sinclair owns and operates, programs or provides services to more television stations than anyone and has affiliations with all major networks. Sinclair Digital group is focused on bringing the most engaging content to web, mobile and over-the-top broadcasting to audiences all over the country! Our success is the result of extraordinary employees and an exemplary management team who believe in a vision and are dedicated ensuring a great future for our employees. Whether you are an industry veteran or a just starting out, you can find it at Sinclair!<b> We are advancing the world of Broadcasting and we want YOU to join our winning team!</b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p>
Get real world experience to give you an edge when you take on the working world. The <b>KOMO </b>Sales and Marketing team in Seattle, Washington has an incredible opportunity for an ambitious intern ready for the challenge! See how our team works with amazing brands – KOMO 4 News, Univision, Seattle Refined, <i>Grey’s Anatomy, The Bachelor, Wheel of Fortune, Jeopardy</i> and more – to deliver the right audience to our advertisers in multi-platform campaigns and events with television, digital and social media. We are looking for an energetic, take initiative and organized student to assist our marketing team. This is a unique opportunity for a college student aiming for a career in media sales, strategic communications, marketing, or public relations to gain experience with a variety of media professionals including sales, design and editorial.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Goals of Internship:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Exposure to day-to-day operations of KOMO media; specifically, Sales, Sponsored Contest, Seattle Refined, and Creative Services.</li>
<li>Opportunity to establish relationships with sales, content producers, marketing and social media professionals.</li>
<li>Understanding of sales/client relationships and how to market and sell media including television and a variety of digital solutions.</li>
<li>Ability to learn promotion and event coordination as it relates to sales and signature community service events.</li>
<li>Gain marketing experience from idea stage to execution and recap of advertising campaigns.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p>
</p>
<p><b>Duties:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Interact with KOMO &amp; KUNS’s Production, Design, Creative Services, and Sales Promotion teams including video, digital and collateral presentation messaging.</li>
<li>Assist in commercial production shoots.</li>
<li>Assist in promotional events which can include being onsite for the event, helping to pack station vehicles for the event or helping with events at KOMO Plaza.</li>
<li>Assist in research projects, prepare sales presentations and recaps of station promotions and events.</li>
<li>Opportunity to work with Marketing Consultants in researching new business leads.</li>
<li>Opportunity to research and co-create content with Seattle Refined team.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p>
</p>
<p><b>Qualifications:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Promotions/marketing or career interests or other interest in the Communications, Public Relations or Sales field.</li>
<li>Responsible, positive attitude, good listener, directed, dependable, self- starter, strong communication, &amp; writing skills.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p>
</p>
<p><b>Essential Functions:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Be organized and professional – you’ll be representing KOMO and your school.</li>
<li>Be able to multi-task and take initiative.</li>
<li>Knowledge of presentation software like PowerPoint, excel and potential to learn editing software including Final Cut Pro, Avid, Edius and/or a similar operating system.</li>
<li>Able to commit approximately up to 20 hours a week.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p>
</p>
<p><b>Requirements:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Minimum 12-16 hours per week with a minimum of 4-hour shift per day.</li>
<li>This is a paid internship and receiving credit toward graduation for hours worked is not required.</li>
<li>Must be available for some weeknight and weekend events which will be discussed well in advance.</li>
<li>Would need to be available to support the So Northwest Women’s Show – February 28-March 1.</li>
<br>
</ul>
