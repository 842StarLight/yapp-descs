<h2>Implementation Project Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div><div>
<div><b>Implementation Project Manager</b></div>
<div></div>
<div>Cincinnati, OH</div>
<div></div>
<div>Job Summary</div>
<div></div>
<div>The Implementation Project Manager is responsible for utilizing best-in-class project management and implementation practices to effectively lead the onboarding experience of new clients with multi-product, complex requirements. He/She serves as the primary owner of the new client experience and is ultimately accountable for the execution of all project tasks and key milestones, ensuring on-time and on-budget delivery to attain the highest possible level of customer satisfaction. The Project Manager documents, coordinates, and monitors progress from kick-off through go-live, and is instrumental in the successful transition of new clients to ongoing support.</div>
<div></div>
<div>Successful Project Managers demonstrate a dedicated focus to clients. More specifically the ability to establish immediate rapport and connection, quickly understand clients’ needs, and tactfully address challenges and concerns while driving the implementation forward. Additionally, they possess demonstrated influencing skills to help clients understand and fully utilize the Paycor software to maximize their business productivity, client satisfaction, and ultimately retention. Their success is measured on successfully transitioning all clients on time.</div>
<div></div>
<div>Essential Duties and Responsibilities</div>
<ul>
<li>Lead and manage the transition of new clients from their existing human capital management software to the Paycor product suite; utilize effective client service and change management practices to ensure a smooth conversion.</li>
<li>Serve as the ultimate owner of project milestones and delivering client satisfaction to positively influence lasting client retention.</li>
<li>Schedule and lead regular project meetings.</li>
<li>Establish a strong working relationship with each client based on clear expectation-setting and mutual trust building.</li>
<li>Ensure clients’ requirements and business objectives are defined clearly that they are well understood and executed by the entire project team.</li>
<li>Act as the primary client contact throughout the client implementation process; monitor implementation progress through Paycor’s project management software.</li>
<li>Maintain an in-depth understanding of all projects including tasks, issues, owners, and risks.</li>
<li>Distribute progress reports as needed. Communicate regularly with all relevant stakeholders.</li>
<li>Raise concerns quickly to stakeholders and escalate when necessary.</li>
<li>Partner with Sales during the Implementation process. Once prospects become assigned clients, meet regularly with applicable Sales associates to understand details from the Sales process and gather context for clients.</li>
<li>Provide timely follow up and communication with Sales regarding status of accounts.</li>
<li>Possess working knowledge of the full Paycor product suite to make connections across services and provide consultation of best practices and system utilization in multiple human capital management disciplines</li>
<li>Work in coordination with non-client facing Implementation team members to ensure accurate database configuration</li>
<li>Maintain accurate project status in Paycor project management software; provide real time updates as needed to project stakeholders; proactively identify and mitigate project risks</li>
<li>Serve as a thought leader for continuous improvement of the Implementation process. Identify broad level enhancements that will continue to foster the delivery of an “amazing client experience” and drive client satisfaction and retention. Provide recommendations for advancing project management processes and/or methodologies.</li>
<li>Transition clients to ongoing Client Services Support resources upon successful implementation</li>
<li>Train and mentor new team members</li>
<li>Other duties as assigned.</li>
</ul>
<div>Requirements</div>
<ul>
<li>Four-year degree in Business Administration, MIS, or similar.</li>
<li>Prior project management experience with multi product implementations strongly preferred.</li>
<li>At least 3 years of experience in a client-facing position; prior implementation, project management or business to business sales experience a plus Knowledge of human capital software preferred Demonstrated proficiency with PC software with emphasis on Microsoft Office Suite and web tools for conferencing</li>
<li>Strong communication and interpersonal skills Demonstrated organizational and time management skills Must be detail oriented with exceptional analytical skills.</li>
<li>Strong client focus and ability to build relationships and rapport.</li>
<li>Flexibility in scheduling to meet client needs with short turnaround.</li>
<li>Travel required with some ability to travel on limited notice.</li>
<li>Ability to work efficiently and effectively on project teams</li>
</ul>
