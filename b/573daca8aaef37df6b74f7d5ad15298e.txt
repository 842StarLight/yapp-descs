<p><b>About Us:<br>
</b></p>
<p></p>
<br>
Barry-Wehmiller is a diversified global supplier of engineering consulting and manufacturing technology for the packaging, corrugating, sheeting and paper-converting industries. By blending people-centric leadership with disciplined operational strategies and purpose-driven growth, Barry-Wehmiller has become a $3 billion organization with nearly 12,000 team members united by a common belief: to use the power of business to build a better world.<br>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>Job Description:
</b></p>
<p>At Barry-Wehmiller Leadership Institute we believe that where we work can be the most powerful force for good in our society today. We are seeking a storyteller that can help us guide clients through our vision leveraging compelling, well thought out copy. We are looking for a team member that can communicate effectively and professionally with potential clients. This role will serve as the go-to writer for marketing content.
</p>
<p></p>
<p>This team member must be open to building the plane and flying it at the same time, able to adapt quickly and collaborate often, and is inspired by the following responsibilities:
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Key Responsibilities:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Assist in maintaining the brand voice for our email marketing to clients, organic content, social media content and paid ad campaigns
</li>
<li>Participates in development of new business for agency by writing agency case studies, content for proposals and creating pitch decks
</li>
<li>Create impactful copy and editorial support that can be used across various platforms such as our website, social media, etc.
</li>
<li>Write marketing/advertising blogs for BW Leadership Institute in a strategic way to drive to digital sales and conversion
</li>
<li>Engage and communicate daily with marketing team to ensure content needs are met
</li>
<li>Edit copy for message clarity, flow, consistency, grammar, brand voice, and tone
</li>
<li>Contribute to content guidelines, develop content patterns
</li>
<li>Create original, breakthrough work
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Required Skills and Details:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>2 to 5 years of experience preferred
</li>
<li>Education: Bachelor’s degree in Marketing, Communications, Journalism or English
</li>
<li>Full time, St. Louis, MO based
</li>
<li>Strong portfolio of copy-heavy online content
</li>
<li>Flawless grammar, spelling and punctuation skills
</li>
<li>Ability to prioritize and manage multiple projects simultaneously
</li>
<li>Outside-the-box thinker with a strong attention to detail
</li>
<li>Proactive self-starter and able to work independently in a fast-paced environment
</li>
<li>The ability to quickly research a topic and produce relevant content while maintaining the clients goals and guidelines
</li>
<li>Invested in creating performance-driven messaging with a desire to understand data behind that performance and iterate on copy based on its performance
</li>
<li>Strong organizational skills and the ability to work on multiple projects concurrently
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>If you are right for this job: </b>You’re always on the hunt for better processes to get things done and can immediately contribute. You have an ability to add value in any circumstance and yet humble enough to learn from any person or situation. You are insatiably curious about people and prefer to listen first. And at the end of the day, you also want to join a team full of people you genuinely enjoy working with and want to make an impact on the world.
</p>
<p></p>
<p>The <b>BW Leadership Institute </b>exists to illuminate potential and ignite human connection. We help organizations create environments where people think of others first; where the business and culture exist not in balance as opposing forces, but in harmony with one feeding the other. We are inspired to transfer the learnings of building culture and a thriving business from Barry-Wehmiller, our parent company, to clients. Some might call us consultants but in less traditional language, we are a diverse team of practitioners with extensive leadership experience from business, industry, military and non-profit roles. We are all committed to building a better world through business. Our clients include the world’s largest airline, an NFL team, the largest wine distributor and a major grocery retailer.<br>
