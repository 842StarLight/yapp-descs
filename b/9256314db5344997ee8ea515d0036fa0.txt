<h2>Order Entry/Process Planning Specialist (Mfg/Production)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<div><div><div>
<p><b>SUMMARY OF JOB</b><br>
Review customer documentation in the form of purchase orders, blueprints, and specifications. Identify customer requirements for use in creating a shop order and process plan for customer hardware.</p>
<p><b>ESSENTIAL ROLES AND RESPONSIBILITIES</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Create a shop order and process plan for customer hardware by thoroughly reviewing customer documentation and entering data into Hohman’s computer system using a software template.</li>
<li>Consult with customers, sales representatives, engineers, and production supervisors as needed to establish processing requirements of customer parts.</li>
<li>Clearly communicate needs to customers; answer questions regarding shop orders and the planning process.</li>
<li>Ensure shop orders are created accurately and in to timely fashion - current target is same day as received.</li>
<li>Visually inspect customers’ parts for conformance to purchase order.</li>
<li>Facilitate the contract review process to ensure compliance of Hohman’s process plan to customer purchase order, blueprint, and specification requirements.</li>
<li>Assist Quality Assurance and Engineering by administering process plans for customer hardware using Hohman’s software.</li>
<li>Tabulate planning-oriented information as required to support Sales, Production, Quality Assurance, and Engineering objectives.
</li>
<li>Drive resolution to outstanding processing, compliance, or billing concerns by working closely with customers and Hohman personnel.</li>
<li>Other related functions and tasks as assigned.</li>
<br>
</ul>

<p><b>EDUCATION, SKILLS &amp; EXPERIENCE</b></p>
<ul>
<li>High school diploma or equivalent.</li>
<li>Minimum 2 years pertinent experience and/or training.</li>
<li>Prior data entry, order entry and/or process planning experience.</li>
<li>Prior experience reading and interpreting blueprint requirements.</li>
<li>Able to type approx. 35 wpm.</li>
<li>Working familiarity with Microsoft Word, Excel, and Outlook applications.</li>
<li>Possess excellent attention to detail and be able to pass a contract review qualification test.</li>
<li>Eager to learn new procedures and accept responsibility for execution of high-quality work in a fast-paced environment.</li>
<li>Excellent written and verbal communication skills</li>
<li>Be part of a high-performing team with a focus on customer service</li>
<li>Take a lead role in effectively addressing open issues to serve customer needs</li>
</ul>
