<h2>Collections Associate</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div></div>
<div>
<div>
<div>Job Summary</div>
<div>
</div>
<p>The Payment Solutions Associate will receive and/or make outbound calls to negotiate settlements or secure payment arrangements with customers who are past due on their credit card accounts, with the purpose of bringing their accounts current.<br>
</p>
<div></div>
<p>Essential Job Functions</p>
<div>
</div>
<div>
<div><div>Make outbound or receive customer calls to negotiate settlements or secure payment arrangements. Provide quality customer service to secure payment arrangements for customers who are between 3-6 months past due on their credit card accounts.</div></div>
<div><div>
Manually dials, skip traces or utilizes automated dialer to communicate with customers and make payment arrangements to meet or exceed established goals to include balances saved, pay-by-phones collected and call quality.</div></div>
<div><div>
Uses computer to enter payment arrangements into care systems.</div></div>
<div><p>
Utilize time-management and organizational skills necessary to effectively handle multiple expectations and thrive in a performance-based call center environment.</p></div>
</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<p>Minimum Qualifications:<br>
</p>
<div></div>
<div>High school diploma or GED</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
Desired Skills:</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
<div>Excellent computer abilities to navigate our care systems, as well as the Windows environment
</div>
<div>Ability to negotiate payment
</div>
<div>Strong problem solving skills</div>
<div>
Basic data-entry and typing skills</div>
<div>
Ability to maintain outstanding attendance at all times</div>
<div>
Schedule flexibility to work 40 hours per week including days, evenings and weekends</div>
<div>
Willingness to submit to and pass E-Verify and background check</div>
<div>
Ability to feel comfortable sitting for most of an 8 hour shift</div>
<div>
Previous collections, call center or sales experience</div>
<div>
Stable work history</div>
<div>
Basic mathematical skills</div>
</div>

Working Conditions/ Physical Requirements</div>
