<h2>North America Commercial Product Marketing Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>The <b>Commercial Product Marketing Manager</b> is an opportunity for an ambitious marketeer to join a dynamic business with high growth ambitions within J2 Global’s cloud services organisation.<br>
</p>
<p>
We are seeking a high-performing, innovative, results-oriented and customer-obsessed <b>Commercial Product Marketing Manager</b> to own the product portfolio P&amp;L, growth strategy, product &amp; initiative roadmaps and work cross matrix to ensure acquisition and retention campaigns meet your revenue targets across the region. Owning your results, you will thrive in rolling up your sleeves and leading by example, intuitively taking a creative approach to getting the most from what you have available to achieve exceptional results.</p>
<p>
Reporting to the Senior Director of Global Voice Marketing, this is an exciting role for the right candidate who enjoys pushing against the status quo. As a key member of the Global Voice Marketing team, this position will shape &amp; drive our B2C and B2B Voice cloud services marketing initiatives across multiple channels and geographies.</p>
<p>
j2 Global®Inc. is a provider of internet services with millions of customers worldwide. Headquartered in Los Angeles California, j2 Global® have developed a clear strategy to deepen the business in US &amp; Canada through aggressive organic and non-organic growth and as <b>Commercial Product Marketing Manager</b>; you will play a crucial role helping the business achieve this.</p>
<p>
This position is located in Los Angeles California, USA.</p>
<p></p>

Role &amp; Responsibilities:</b></p>

<br>
<ul>
<li>
Revenue + P&amp;L</li>
<li>

<li>
Monitor competitive landscapes/positioning and market trends/threats to keep j2 Global® products in a leadership position</li>
<li>
Deeply understand j2 Global’s ecosystem and use this knowledge to inform product messaging, brand differentiation, marketing content and sales training.</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>
Marketing Strategy + Planning<ul>
<li>

<li>

</ul>
</li>
<li>
Product performance + roadmap<ul>

Translate your strategy and messaging into sales enablement tools and collateral incl. training, sales presentations, product one-pagers, webinars, and competitive briefs to support sale</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>
Messaging and positioning<ul><li>
Translate insights from research and data into product positioning, value propositions and messaging that resonates with the target markets including development of the product messaging blueprint that improves awareness of and engagement with our products and features</li></ul>
</li>
<li>
Go To Market<ul><li>
Oversee implementation of the marketing plan, including the generation of campaign briefs; partner with acquisition &amp; retention team to deliver marketing campaigns that build our brand and produce high-quality sales leads.</li></ul>
</li>
<li>
Use insights from continual testing, customer data, and industry best practices to optimise marketing communications to achieve business goals and ensure customer satisfaction.</li>
<li>
Set clear performance goals to achieve quarterly and annual goals through online and offline marketing initiatives.</li>
<li>
Collaborate with a broad range of teams, including international marketing, product, Customer Care, Sales, operations, and creative teams.</li>
</ul>
