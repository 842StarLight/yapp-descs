<p><b>Description</b><br>
</p>
<br>
The Information Analytics team is leading Caterpillar and our industry with the utilization of data analytics, innovation, digital channels and techniques to drive profit, create more efficient revenue growth, unlock new revenue streams and provide more durable competitive advantages for the business through unexpectedly great experiences for our customers.
<div></div>
<div>The Analytics Professional Development Program will immerse you in our business and introduce you to our analytics tools and techniques, enabling you to provide data based insights that drive business decisions. Our smart factories and our integrated dealer network, has provided a wealth of Big Data that is mined and analyzed across various segments such as product development, manufacturing, supply chain, marketing, sales and customer support.<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<div>Analytics Professionals are the data driven, business professionals using quantitative methods such as business simulations, data mining, and advanced statistical techniques to solve problems for internal business partners and external Caterpillar customers.</div>
