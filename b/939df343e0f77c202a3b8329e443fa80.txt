<h2>Commercial Salesman</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Build your career with TruTeam, a TopBuild company. TopBuild Corp. (NYSE: BLD) is the leading installer and distributor of insulation products to the United States construction industry, based on revenue. We provide insulation services nationwide through TruTeam®, which has over 215 branches, and distribute insulation through Service Partners®, which has over 75 branches. TopBuild Home Services’ Environments for Living® program, is committed to helping builders construct high performance homes that are energy efficient, durable, and safe. TopBuild’s corporate headquarters is located in Daytona Beach, FL.</p>
<p><b>
Our Core Values: </b>Safety - Integrity - Diversity - Customer Focus - Continuous Improvement - Innovation - High Performance Teams</p>
<p><b>
SUMMARY</b></p>
<p>
Responsible for developing sales and managing relationships with key commercial contractors for all TruTeam’s insulation and related services.</p>
<p><b>
ESSENTIAL FUNCTIONS</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Capable of identifying, pursuing and developing long-term relationships with prospective customers.</li>
<li>Delivers the company value proposition to customers to influence sales.</li>
<li>Diligently and consistently follows up on all customer proposals to close sales and gain customer insight.</li>
<li>Recommends products, materials and services based on customers’ needs and interests. Provides product knowledge and expertise to current and potential customers.</li>
<li>Meets expectations for proposal volumes and closing sales ratios.</li>
<li>Monitors market conditions, product innovations and competitors’ products, prices and sales.</li>
<li>Works majority of time outside of the office conducting sales calls, providing proposals, generating new business leads, account management.</li>
<li>Communicates the company’s safety program to customers at the point of contact or sale by inspecting job sites to identify any safety concerns.</li>
<li>Manages customer experience from initial sale to project completion. Independence of Action – Results are defined. Partner with Division Manager and Regional Sales Manager or Sales Manager to set goals and determine how to obtain results with some guidelines. Manager provides broad guidance and overall direction.</li>
<li>Any other duties, tasks or responsibilities as assigned.</li>
</ul>
