<h2>Finance Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Overview
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div>Through its people and brands, CNH Industrial delivers power, technology and innovation to farmers, builders and drivers all around the world. Each of its brands, including Case IH, New Holland Agriculture, Case and New Holland Construction, FPT Industrial, Capital, and Parts &amp; Service, is a major international force in its specific sector.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<p>
The Finance Manager role is responsible to oversee all aspects of finance, accounting, and risk control for the CNH Industrial Reman joint venture located in Springfield, Missouri. This position is a key role and works closely with the venture's management team to drive financial strategy and execution.</p>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<p>Specific responsibilities include:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Lead the approval of financial data including P&amp;L, Balance Sheet and KPI reports of business plans, budgets, forecasts and strategic business plan</li>
<li>
Partner with internal operating management in functions such as manufacturing, sales and marketing, human resources, product development, supply chain and quality/warranty to develop financial reports for variances from forecasts and budget, to develop cost/benefit analyses and to provide recommendations to improve sales and/or profit</li>
<li>
With Treasury, manage cash management functions including cash, wires, account funding, interest expense and income, banking agreements and service fees</li>
<li>
Ensure adequate liquidity for working capital, capital expenditures, acquisitions, and general corporate purposes</li>
<li>
Partner with internal and external tax matter experts to prepare federal, state, or local tax returns</li>
<li>
Lead business development activities including researching market conditions and developments within the industry and with competitors, assisting in strategy development, carrying out financial modeling using valuation discounted cash flow analysis, return on investment and payback, assisting acquisition due diligence and identifying synergies and costs savings</li>
<li>
Provide oversight to the general accounting function, which includes inter-company accounting, reconciliations, financial reporting, financial statement management, compliance with internal controls, policies and procedures, technical accounting guidance, system design/validation and internal and external audit</li>
<li>
Prepare financial presentations for the joint venture's Board of Directors, operating reviews, financial institutions and other parties</li>
<li>
Interact with joint venture partners CNH Industrial and SRC and provide financial information and analyses as requested</li>
<li>
Identify and lead process improvement projects to automate, streamline, provide greater efficiencies and accuracy of current finance procedures to maximize the contribution to strategic and business goals</li>
<li>
Set priorities and goals for the department; hire and/or mentor the finance team</li>
<li>
Build relationships cross-functionally to foster collaboration both inside and outside of the finance team</li>
<li>
Instill strong cost control/reporting and planning discipline for SG&amp;A, capital expenditures, and working capital to control business costs</li>
<li>
Assist with the development of the Reman business with CNH Industrial regions and/or CNH Industrial dealers outside North America</li>
<li>
Assist with Information Technology and Communication project implementations</li>
<li>
Assist with special projects as assigned</li>
</ul>
</div></div></div>
