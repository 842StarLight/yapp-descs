<h2>Sales Development Representative (SDR)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">Sales Development Representative
<br><br>
Dito is a leading strategic partner for enterprise IT, app development and cloud services. We are looking for energetic and passionate people who are ready and eager to join the ongoing “cloud shift,” enabling digital transformation for our clients.
<br><br>
The position as all of the positions within Dito are remote “work from home” with occasional travel. As such, we are looking for self-motivated, focused individuals suitable for this style of work culture.
<br><br>
<b>Job Description:</b><br>
The Sales Development Representative (SDR) works closely together with the MDR Team Lead, coordinating with both the Marketing and Sales departments to meet cross-departmental goals. An SDR is focused on lead generation, lead qualification, and qualified handoffs to our Sales team. In this role, you will be engaging in intro and exploratory conversations - both via email and by phone - with business and IT leaders at target accounts.
<br><br>
The core responsibility of an SDR is to create conversations, identify areas for our cloud solutions (i.e., G Suite, Google Cloud Platform, Google Maps Platform, etc.) and professional services (migration and deployments, application development, cloud engineering, etc.) through initial awareness and interest-generating conversations. The goal here is to “tee up” pre-qualified meetings and/or opportunities for our Account Executive and Cloud Engineering teams to carry forward.
<br><br>
Through a programmatic approach to daily outreach and follow-up, the MDR team plays a critically important role in identifying business opportunities, nurturing and qualifying leads into opportunities, driving demand, and filling the early stages of our sales pipeline.
<br><br>
<b>What You’ll Do:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Targeted outbound demand generation</li>
<li>Execute on various outbound lead generation campaigns via email &amp; calling</li>
<li>Respond, engage, and qualify responses</li>
<li>Schedule qualified meetings for Sales team</li>
<li>Help promote various events and follow-up to qualify registrations</li>
<li>Assist Sales-driven campaigns on a requested basis (process TBD)</li>
<li>Lead qualification and handoff process to Sales</li>
<li>Qualification of inbound leads (email, events, web forms, etc.)</li>
<li>Qualification of some partner referred leads (situational; TBD)</li>
<li>Can handle closing smaller Accounts and Opportunities with deal size &lt; $5k</li>
<li>Maintain accurate records, logging interactions, and activities as you engage with prospects and customers</li>
<li>Data cleanup, reporting, and analysis</li>
<li>Be a team player and willing to wear multiple hats to support the needs of a dynamic, rapidly growing company</li>
</ul>
<b>What you need to succeed:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Write and communicate well across a variety of channels: written, phone, in-person, &amp; online</li>
<li>1-3 years of outbound prospecting, sales, or business development experience</li>
<li>Understanding of complex, enterprise sales cycles and solution selling tactics</li>
<li>Proactive problem solver with the ability to translate business ideas into technical solutions</li>
<li>Creative, “growth-hacker” mindset that can identify solutions and adapt quickly to change</li>
<li>Has a good understanding of cross-functional business relationships, with the ability to relate to and communicate at the senior management and executive level</li>
<li>Experience with Hubspot and Salesforce is a big plus (or other sales &amp; marketing automation platforms)</li>
<li>Being an experienced user of G Suite helps</li>
<li>Demonstrate a combination of being both organized and flexible</li>
<li>The ability to build relationships with stakeholders</li>
<li>Occasional travel (&lt; 10%)</li>
</ul>
<b>Job Type:</b> Full-time
