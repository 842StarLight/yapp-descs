<div>Position: Director of Operations – Florida</div>
<p>The successful candidate for this role will lead the growth for our firm in Florida including business development, day to day operations, client interaction, and project management.</p>
<p>You will have the opportunity to work on multiple projects within a short period of time. You will be involved in all aspects of projects and client interaction.</p>
<p>Candidates with an interest in future ownership opportunities are welcome. Entrepreneurial types with a passion for excellence will be a good fit for our company culture.</p>
<div></div>

<p><b><i>Project Management</i></b></p>
<ul><li>Manage our Client’s projects from start to completion</li></ul>

<ul><li>Identify and pursue new business opportunities with existing and potential clients</li></ul>

• Demonstrated ability to perform sales services<br>
• Self-motivated, goal setting, driven personality<br>
• Organizational skills, ability and preference to work on many small to medium sized projects simultaneously; in lieu of, one large project at a time.<br>
• Comfortable performing public presentations<br>
• Ability to communicate effectively internally and externally with all levels of staff<br>
