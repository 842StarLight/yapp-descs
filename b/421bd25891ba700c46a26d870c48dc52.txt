<h2>Project Accounting Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>The Project Accounting Specialist position is fullyresponsible for performing job duties in the functional areas of accounting,customer service, and administrative support for designated businessunits.</p>
<p><b>GeneralJob Duties</b></p>
<p>· Revenuegenerating teams &gt;100M entered, 85% generated by projects. Revenue &lt; 100Mentered, 95% revenue generated by projects.</p>
<p>· Entryof new projects in to D365 and AX2012 including setting up new project budgets,forecasts, on accounts.</p>
<p>· Issuingvendor purchase orders against appropriate project or order.</p>
<p>· Preparingtimely project term billings to optimize cash flow.</p>
<p>· Processingvendor invoices against appropriate order or project.</p>
<p>· Effectivelycommunicate with Project Managers, Business Unit Managers, and/or FieldApplication Engineers during monthly project financial review (PFR).</p>
<p>· Executeall changes identified during PFR in timely matter.</p>
<p>· Researchunbilled orders to optimize billing revenue issues each month.</p>
<p>· Researchproject cost to complete to optimize earned revenue each month.</p>
<p>· Monitorbillable utilization ratios, ensure timecards are entered appropriately onweekly basis.</p>
<p>· Provideassistance to vendors who have questions or problems related to orders theyhave received. If unable to assist,routes the vendor to appropriate persons.</p>
<p>· Maintainfiles for all projects with appropriate documents including job cost tool,customer purchase order, vendor invoice(s), customer invoice(s), in accordancewith standard procedures. (Electronic copies preferred)</p>
<p>· Maintaina continuous line of proactive communication with customers’ account payablecontacts to resolve past dues, clear any open issues.</p>
<p>· Workswith Business Unit Manager to reach NIBT goals.</p>
<p>· Workwith Bastian Credit Specialist to reconcile variances that occur in theapplication of cash.</p>
<p>· Writeoff any uncollectible receivables as directed by Credit Specialist.</p>
<p>· Communicateeffectively with other Bastian Solutions business units.</p>
<p>· Provideyear-end support of auditor requirements, which may include research.</p>
<p>· Providetraining for new team members who will be working in sales and projectmanagement roles as to expectations / requirements for forms and processes.</p>
<p>· Performadditional assignments, as directed.</p>
<p><b>Requirements</b></p>
<p>· Bachelor’sDegree in Accounting or Finance and minimum 5+ years of applicableexperience.</p>
<p>· Intermediateto advanced computer literacy, including experience in word processing,spreadsheets and accounting software. A working knowledge of Microsoft AX orD365 a plus.</p>
<p>· Abilityto work well with others under deadline situations and respond to changes inpriorities.</p>
<p>· Self-motivatedwith ability to work independently and balance workload to complete assignmentswithin time constraints</p>
<p>· Excellentwritten and verbal communication skills and strong organizational skills.</p>
<p>· Abilityto apply analytical and interpretive skills to problem solving.</p>
<p>· Employproblem-solving skills and analysis, and report problems as necessary.</p>
<p>· Generalunderstanding of revenue recognition principles and J Sox compliance.</p>
<p>· Abilityto travel for purpose of training as required</p>

<p>· Communicateeffectively with other Bastian business units.</p>
<p>· Effectivelycommunicate with team members during monthly project financial review (PFR).</p>
<p>· Providetraining for new team members who will be working in sales and projectmanagement roles as to expectations/requirement for forms and processes.</p>
