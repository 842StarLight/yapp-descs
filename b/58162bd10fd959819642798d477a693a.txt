<p><b>Title: Manager, Technology Project and Program Management – Development Manager
</b></p>
<p><b>Req: R0003426
</b></p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Company Overview:
</b></p>
<p></p>
<p>The Wolters Kluwer Global Platform Organization co-creates products and solutions with Wolters Kluwer businesses around the globe to serve professionals in the Legal, Tax, Heath, Finance and Compliance sectors. This wide and growing array of solutions supports business units in building and expanding their online product portfolios. The Global Platform Organization provides global leverage of industry and customer insight, driving technology capabilities for local product innovation.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Position Overview:
</b></p>
<p></p>
<p>We have a Leadership opportunity available! We are looking for self-motivate and passionate development manager to drive the development of our next generation digital sales and marketing (DSM) capabilities across Wolters Kluwer based on Sitecore’s Experience Platform. As part of this initiative we will centralize our global DSM capabilities on a modern, best-in-class platform and leverage this platform to rapidly mature our global DSM capabilities. The role will report into our Global Platforms Organization (GPO), to the Director of Digital Sales &amp; Marketing Solutions and will work closely with the Corporate Strategy team and marketing leads across the enterprise.
</p>
<p></p>
<p>The Development Manager is responsible for delivering solutions for:
</p>
<ul>
<li>Consolidation and transformation of our web ecosystem to the Sitecore Experience Platform to improve discoverability and relevance for our target customers and markets
</li>
<li>Drive overall development and testing processes for DSM solutions using Agile and Test-Driven
</li>
<li>Design and implement the Digital Sales and Marketing Technology Stack based on the Sitecore Development methods of working
</li>
<li>Experience Platform and related services for an integrated web presence that is scalable, resilient, secure and easy to use
</li>
<li>Implement standard web-based customer UX; be a source of best practices
</li>
<li>Drive continual improvement and build a best-in-class, data-driven customer experience
</li>
<li>Assist in planning for web site migration and transformation projects: CMS implementation, web site consolidation, website management and marketing processes e.g. SEO/SEM, and integration with supporting systems
</li>
<li>Effectively communicate progress against objectives to internal teams and senior management
</li>
<li>Support the adoption of best practices, KPIs/SLAs, technology and processes for this effort
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p>The position is responsible for developing the technical architecture for Wolters Kluwer’s Digital Sales &amp; Marketing platform on the Sitecore Experience Platform, designing solutions, and for leading a matrixed team (including staff, consultants, and offshore resources), through the software and cloud services implementation of those solutions. The position will work with business partners to understand and capture business requirements, with business and technical staff to communicate technical architectures and solutions, and with software development staff (designers, testers, programmers, project managers, analysts, system administrators, etc. – both in- and out- of house) to ensure successful delivery of those solutions.
</p>
<p></p>
<p>This position provides project-based management for software development staff in a matrixed organization and will have at least 7 direct reports with budget responsibility. Our successful candidate will demonstrate strong technical and project management skills, execute using Agile and Test Driven Development methods of working and have proven experience with the Sitecore Experience Platform and the .Net MVC framework. Our Software Development Manager will report to the Director of Digital Sales &amp; Marketing Solutions in the GPO.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Essential Duties and Responsibilities:
</b></p>
<p></p>
<ul><li>Analyze business and user requirements, develop system architectures to meet those requirements, and design software solutions to implement the architectures.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Communicate technical solutions to business and technical staff.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Interact with business and technical partners to guide the software development process serving as an interface to the Global Platform Organization DSM development team.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Supervise, assign, and schedule staff and consultants for research and development projects with a minimum of supervision.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Lead an Agile team of 7 to 10 resources consisting of both onshore and offshore contributors.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Manage closure of requirements, issues and defects.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Direct daily developer activities.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Lead technical planning, development, and delivery for DSM solutions.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Champion, collaborate, and drive cloud-based application development, deployments, best practices across multiple development projects.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Lead coordination and communication across Development teams, Project Mangers, Operations team, and QA teams.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Work with project managers and management on prioritization, scope definition, cost &amp; effort estimations, and risk/issue resolutions.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Manage offshore development teams. Direct the activities of offshore team members in conjunction with an onsite liaison. Review work products and take corrective actions.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Ensure the team delivers high quality development deliverables by actively working/collaborating with development and QA resources on approach for design review, code review, and testing.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Responsible for defining and managing best practices and process for code management, software build, and software deployment strategies.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Lead management, execution, and integration of automated testing strategies.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Adhere to existing software development policies and procedures and develop new procedures and standards in software process improvement initiatives.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Participate in the management of budget and planning activities related to software development.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Evaluate, recommend, and implement new technologies for software development. Stay abreast of current trends and technologies and introduce them into the Global Platform Organization development process.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>May occasionally interview and hire staff.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Position may require working some evening, weekend and holiday hours when points in the development, delivery and support cycle require management engagement and support.
</li></ul>
