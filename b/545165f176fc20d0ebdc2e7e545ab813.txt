<h2>Senior Sales Engineer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">TigerGraph is looking for energetic and motivated Sales Engineers to join our Sales Engineering Team. If you're an upbeat personality and enjoy being on the front lines of rapid growth company with your deep understanding of the sales process, cycle, and supporting methodologies in an enterprise and cloud setting, then we want to hear from you.
<br><br>
The Senior Sales Engineer role at TigerGraph is an integral part of the sales team. Having a technical background, the ideal candidate is passionate and comfortable to learn and market new technology in the graph analytics space. Assisting sale team to demo, educate and persuade prospective customers to adopt TigerGraph platform. Sales Engineers are passionate about working with prospects and customers to understand their business and system requirements, identify and refine compelling use cases for which TigerGraph is applicable, showcase relevant product capabilities and deliver proof of concept to close deals.
<br><br>
<b>Responsibilities include:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Master the TigerGraph product line.</li>
<li>Provide informational TigerGraph product presentations and live software demonstrations.</li>
<li>Participate in functional and technical discovery sessions.</li>
<li>Lead white boarding sessions and/or work independently to map requirements to valid use cases that are addressed by TigerGraph product capabilities.</li>
<li>Propose solution architectures and develop prototypes that address customer requirements based on TigerGraph product.</li>
<li>Address competitive differentiation and technical objection-handling as needed.</li>
<li>Facilitate solution knowledge transfer to TigerGraph Consulting and industry partners.</li>
<li>Support local marketing events.</li>
</ul>
<b>Relevant Skills and Experience:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Huge Plus: Prior Graph Database Experience (Neo4j, DataStax Graph, Amazon Neptune, AllegroGraph, AnzoGraph, ArangoDB, JanusGraph, OrientDb, StarDog ), graph algorithms and graph data structure design</li>
<li>Huge Plus: Managed Services Experience, SaaS based Cloud products</li>
<li>Relational or NoSQL database, Big Data, Analytics and Integration technologies and architectures. SQL querying writing experience.</li>
<li>Virtualization and Cloud knowledge. (EC2, Azure)</li>
<li>Knowledgeable in one advanced programming language Java, C++, C, Scala.</li>
<li>Consultative skills including data requirements gathering and data modeling, white boarding and developing/validating proposed solution architectures.</li>
<li>Presentations and software demonstrations for audiences including technical and line-of-business decision makers.</li>
<li>Delivery of informal or other product education to prospective users or project teams.</li>
<li>Soft skills including effective communication, listening and attention to detail.</li>
<li>Ability to work in a fast-paced, sales-focused environment.</li>
<li>Industry vertical experience, e.g. Financial Services, Retail, Government, Security or Telecommunication.</li>
<li>Proficient written and spoken English communication skills.</li>
</ul>
Qualifications
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>5+ years prior software pre-sales, or other client-facing experience.</li>
<li>Bachelor's degree in engineering required.</li>
<li>Ability to work independently, self-directed and remotely in a cross functional organization.</li>
<li>Ability and willingness to travel</li>
</ul>
</div>
