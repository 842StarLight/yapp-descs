 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Customer Order Entry Specialist - Customer Care Representative</b></p>
<p>The primary responsibility of this position is to ensure orders are entered in and processed promptly and accurately while providing outstanding communication with other employees and MOST IMPORTANTLY with our customers to ensure an optimum customer experience. This is a full time position with full benefits for our Simi Valley, CA location. This is a long term hire. This job requires attention to detail. International shipment experience a plus.</p>
<p><b>Duties and Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Receive, review and process incoming purchase orders which includes; reviewing account status, transportation method(s), part number/price accuracy, and payment terms</li>
<li>Coordinate with Production and Inventory Control on inventory stock availability</li>
<li>Email order confirmations; Email invoices and credit notes to representatives and customers,as requested.</li>
<li>Prepare paperwork for International exports and coordinate pick ups from our location</li>
<li>Use FedEx and UPS shipment software to prepare labels for outgoing shipments</li>
<li>Process RMAs and issue credits as needed</li>
<li>Maintain customer files for accuracy: address, terms, contacts, ship-to locations, including resale certificate exemptions</li>
<li>Answer customer emails/phone inquiries and update customers and sales representatives on ship dates/revisions and logistics.</li>
<li>Lend support to Accounting Department by processing and mailing vendor payments</li>
<li>Input equipment data into the Customer Relations Management software, Salesforce</li>
<li>Sort incoming mail and send out outgoing mail</li>
</ul>
