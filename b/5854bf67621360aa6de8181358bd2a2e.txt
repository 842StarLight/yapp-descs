<h2>Business Development Representative- Entry Level</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div><div><div><div><div><div><div><div><div><div>
<div><div>
<div>
<p>Before campaigns begin, you will receive training on the tools we use to facilitate and record the activity and the company's products to understand the value they bring to prospects. For outbound campaigns, you will be armed with the required collateral, scripts, talk track, and appropriate next steps to drive leads down in the funnel. When leads arrive inbound, you will be ready to engage with the proper discovery questions to understand the customer's specific requirements. You will apply your communication skills and curiosity to get conversational engagement underway and will document your progress in Salesforce CRM. As you engage and become a trusted advisor, your goal is to understand the business use cases and technical challenges involved in the prospects IoT or EOS environment.</p>
<p>
Your goal is to secure the commitment of the prospect to the "next step," and to handoff a qualified lead to an outside rep — this<b> </b><b>a lead qualification role, not a selling role.</b></p>
<p>
Ideally, you are a sales contributor today for a successful enterprise software company in the Seattle area and are looking for an opportunity to help build a new business development organization from the ground up.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Responsible for making 30-60+ outbound calls (including follow up) per day as part of outbound campaigns and to inbound leads</li>
<li>Develop leads to the point they are ready to take the "next step" with Bsquare and handoff to an outside team</li>
<li>Nurture prospects who are not prepared to move forward and work with Marketing to develop nurture campaigns</li>
<li>Document all activity in Salesforce CRM – maintain an impeccable history of conversations and insights learned</li>
<li>Work with Marketing and Sales teams to be trained on formal campaigns, goals, and value propositions</li>
<li>Assist in the development of lead nurture programs and custom content strategies</li>
<li>General knowledge of the IT industry, specifically Microsoft, and leading products strongly preferred</li>
<li>Maintain a strong understanding of the organization's products and services and build interest by understanding the needs of the customer and offering appropriate solutions</li>
<li>Build sales/lead pipeline by meeting &amp; exceeding lead generation quotas</li>
<li>Establish account presence and build trust with key contacts</li>
<li>Analyze customer needs in terms of current business priorities and pain points</li>
<li>Develop opportunities with the appropriate Account Executive conveying and sharing all relevant information collected about the prospect until the appropriate hand-over point is reached</li>
</ul>
<p>Bsquare Essential Candidate Traits</p>
<p>
Anyone interviewing for any position at Bsquare Corporation must have the following character traits to be considered a viable candidate for an open position:</p>
<ul>
<li>Curious – people that aren’t happy with the obvious and want to dig into to understand more</li>
<li>Collaborative – people that want to work with people who “aren’t just like them.”</li>
<li>Creative – want people who can think out of the box to solve problems and generate opportunities</li>
<li>Intelligent – want people who are smart enough to know what they don’t know and build a framework for making decisions with imperfect or incomplete information</li>
<li>Disciplined – people that have the individual self-discipline to achieve great results</li>
<li>Experienced – want people that have relevant prior experience though it’s unlikely they’ve done the exact job someplace else</li>
<li>Personable – friendly individuals that you want to spend time with</li>
<li>Passionate – people that are signed up for the Bsquare mission</li>
</ul>
