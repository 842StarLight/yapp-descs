<h2>Accounts Receivable Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Zwanenberg Food Group (USA), Inc. is looking for an Accounts Receivable Specialist to join the team at our Nashville, TN location. We are seeking a motivated self-starter to handle the day to day operations of the AR posting activities. This position will insure the timely invoicing, researching and resolving outstanding issues related to cash posting and supporting the CFO in all AR related functions.</p>
<p><b>RESPONSIBILITIES INCLUDE: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Collecting accounts receivable, as well as credit limit and delinquency assessment.</li>
<li>Dailey entering cash receipts accurately and timely.</li>
<li>Contacting customers for payment</li>
<li>Managing letters of credit, filing timely and properly the paperwork required</li>
<li>Reviewing pre-shipment lists weekly to determine potential credit obstacles to allow shipment, and corrects or communicates issues to sales</li>
<li>Maintaining excel spreadsheet development of ongoing reports</li>
<li>Using timely reporting collection efforts and current status to management</li>
<li>Backing up Accounts Payable and other accounting positions when needed</li>
<li>Any additional items as assigned by manager</li>
</ul>
<p><b>COMPETENCY OR POSITION REQUIREMENTS </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Ability to assess, correctly process and record information into accounting software</li>
<li>Strong communication and time management skills</li>
<li>Logical and deductive reasoning abilities</li>
<li>Microsoft suite, excel, outlook, word</li>
<li>ERP system</li>
<li>EDI Platforms</li>
<li>Accounting</li>
<li>Communication</li>
<li>Detail oriented</li>
</ul>
<p><b>EDUCATION AND/OR EXPERIENCE </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Billing/invoicing processing and postings to GL and EDI platforms</li>
<li>Communication with customers via phone and email while maintaining professional and polite demeanor</li>
<li>Run AR aging and help in collections with Sales staff assisting in providing support documents</li>
<li>Assist purchasing in PO entry and requests to vendors</li>
<li>Assist in inventory</li>
</ul>
