<div>
As the client’s primary account liaison, you will coordinate with C-level marketing executives to plan, measure, and optimize their digital campaigns. To ensure customer success, you will apply consulting and negotiating skills to confirm customers are seeing a return on investment to secure renewals for our largest customers.
</div>What You Will Do:
<ul>
<li>Develop trusted relationships with decision makers in each account, understand their strategic goals, and develop plans to partner on these goals</li>
<li>
Successfully negotiate renewal and up-sell contracts and pricing
</li>
<li>Ensure that customers are leveraging the BrightEdge solution to deliver business value across all levels of their company</li>
<li>
Aggressively prospect new business units while meeting and exceeding revenue and activity goals</li>
<li>
Prepare and execute on quarterly customer success plans</li>
<li>
Understand level of adoption and assess the risk in renewing existing contracts</li>
<li>
Introduce new features and best practices to customers and track adoption</li>
<li>
Develop new materials (presentations, best practices, rollout plans, proposals, etc.)</li>
<li>
Track and report on key metrics for customer success.</li>
<li>
Work with customer success managers to establish strong qualification criteria</li>
<li>
Manage to a monthly, quarterly, and yearly quota</li>
</ul>
