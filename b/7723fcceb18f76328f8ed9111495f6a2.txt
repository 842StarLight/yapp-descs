<h2>Water Resources Engineer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>RK&amp;K is hiring Junior to Mid Level Water Resources Engineers to join our successful and growing Raleigh, NC office.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Essential Functions:</b></p>
<p>As a Water Resources Engineer with RK&amp;K your responsibilities will include design assistance and management of assigned projects, including, but not limited to drainage studies, design, layout and analysis of open and closed storm drainage systems for linear transportation projects, hydraulic flood studies, stormwater management, stream and wetland restoration/mitigation, and sedimentation and erosion control design:</p>
<ul>
<li>Design and analysis of storm drainage features</li>
<li>Design and analysis of Stormwater Control Measures (SCMs)</li>
<li>Design of Sediment and Erosion Control Best Management Practices (BMPs)</li>
<li>Preparation of environmental permit drawings</li>
<li>Preparation of construction plans and specifications</li>
<li>Floodplain analysis utilizing HEC-RAS software</li>
<li>FEMA floodplain analysis and compliance</li>
<li>Use of MicroStation and GEOPAK design software Capable of coordinating simultaneous tasks/projects</li>
<li>Proficient communicating technical information</li>
<li>Able to work closely with other design disciplines to determine most practical solutions,</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Requirements:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Working knowledge of basic hydrologic and hydraulics principals</li>
<li>2-5 years of experience</li>
<li>4-year degree from an ABET accredited civil/environmental engineering program (or related degree)</li>
<li>Successful completion of the Fundamentals of Engineering (FE) exam preferred</li>
<li>Full time engineering, engineering internship or Cooperative Education experience preferred</li>
<li>Ability to learn MicroStation and/or AutoCad; Proficiency with Microsoft Office software</li>
</ul>
<p><b>What we offer:</b></p>
<p>This position affords you the opportunity to fully engage to solve complex problems, provide technical evaluation, and be an integral team member for the design and analysis of water resources projects.</p>
