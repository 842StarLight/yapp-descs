<h2>Senior Product Consultant - Dealer.com</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<div>Please Note: There are two positions available, 1 will be located in the Mission, KS office and 1 will be located in the Burlington VT office</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
Dealer.com Product Consultants are the primary technical resource for the Dealer.com sales force.</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div>You will be responsible for actively driving and managing the technology evaluation stage of the sales process. You'll work in conjunction with the sales team as the key technical adviser and product advocate for our products.</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div>You must be able to articulate technology and our product positioning to both business and technical end users and have the ability to identify all technical issues, as well as concerns of dealerships to assure complete customer satisfaction and understanding through all stages of the sales process. You must be able to establish and maintain strong relationships throughout the sales cycle, in accordance with the sales team needs, as well as, relate your real life experiences, at a dealership.</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div>Responsibilities:</div>
<ul>
<li>Responsible for demonstrating and delivery of product demonstrations</li>
<li>
Responsible for representing the product to customers online, onsite and at Trade shows such as NADA, Digital Dealer, etc.</li>
<li>
Able to respond to functional and technical elements of RFPs</li>
<li>
Able to convey customer requirements to the Sales Team</li>
<li>
Able to travel, as necessary.</li>
</ul>
<br>
<h4 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Qualifications:</b></h4>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<ul>
<li>
HS Diploma or equivalent GED, Bachelor’s Degree a plus</li>
<li>
Ideal candidate must be self-motivated with a proven track record in sales including both website and knowledge of competitive technology. Comfortable in the dynamic atmosphere of a technical organization with a rapidly expanding customer base. Must possess strong presentation skills and be able to communicate professionally to include, written responses to emails, RFPs, and when submitting reports. Organized and analytical, able to eliminate sales obstacles through creative and adaptive approaches. Must be prepared for occasional intermittent travel with short notice.</li>
<li>
1+ years of experience within Dealer.com or similar industry website provider, 2+ years preferred</li>
<li>
1+ years of relevant experience in automotive or other software sales</li>
</ul>
