<h2>Parts Rep</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p>Responds to customers with a sense of urgency, taking ownership of their parts needs, and providing a solution in a timely, accurate manner.</p>
<p></p>
<p>Essential Functions:</p>
<ul>
<li>Makes sure customers are greeted upon arrival to the department and over the phone, using their name as appropriate, waiting on them promptly, and always thanking them for their business.</li>
<li>Asks appropriate questions to determine the best solution to the customers’ parts need. Uses all available resources to find the best solution.</li>
<li>Works to build a solid business relationship with customers and be a resource for questions.</li>
<li>Communicates marketing programs to customers, promoting the R&amp;S value.</li>
<li>Assists Technicians and other Service personnel in retrieving parts, recognizing that shop orders are equally important to the profitability of the Parts department.</li>
<li>Retrieves parts from inventory, transfers in from another store, or orders as needed.</li>
<li>Generates an invoice, accepts payment and accurately processes the transaction.</li>
<li>Coordinates shipments incoming and outbound from the dealership.</li>
<li>Goes through parts orders as they arrive and puts them away or matches them with orders.</li>
<li>Contacts customers and notifies them when orders are in.</li>
<li>Faces, stocks and dusts merchandise on the showroom floor on a daily basis to ensure eye-catching displays.</li>
<li>Creates seasonal merchandising displays to promote sales.</li>
<li>Shares in the responsibility of after-hours calls with other parts department staff.</li>
<li>Refers leads on other sales opportunities to R&amp;S staff as appropriate.</li>
<li>Maintains a clean, neat and safe parts area, observing safety guidelines at all times.</li>
<li>Assists in maintaining an accurate inventory of all parts on hand, participates in annual physical inventory process as requested.</li>
<li>Stays up-to-date on equipment and market trends through attending training, product bulletins and other resources.</li>
</ul>
<p>Qualifications:</p>
<ul>
<li>Knowledge of farm equipment preferred.</li>
<li>Excellent communication skills and ability to work well with people from all levels of the organization, and the general public as well.</li>
<li>Strong attention to detail and organizational skills required.</li>
<li>Ability to use a computer and current software programs required.</li>
<li>Must have a valid driver’s license and insurable driving history.</li>
<li>Riesterer &amp; Schnell is an equal opportunity employer. Women, minorities, people with disabilities and veterans are encouraged to apply.</li>
</ul>
