<h2>VP of Ad Sales</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>The Vice President Advertising Sales directs and controls advertising sales activities for a group of networks, major geographic grouping (e.g., North East, or Mid-Atlantic), major industry, sales channel or daypart. Accountable for directing a team to achieve large revenue targets associated with regions or segments of accountability.</p>
<p><b>Core Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Supervise, mentor and manage team members of Account Executives, Sales Planners and Sales Assistants</li>
<li>Develops and implements sales strategies and tactical plans and programs for regions or segments of accountability</li>
<li>Contacts major accounts and assists on most prominent and complex accounts</li>
<li>Continually evaluates sales performance in assigned regions or segments, and modifies strategies and plans to better results</li>
<li>Approves some policies and major operating procedures</li>
<li>Negotiates complex deals, often at top management level, that affect major aspects of advertising sales division performance</li>
<li>Collaborate with Ad Sales Marketing, Research, content groups to identify outside trends which affect inventory across multiple assets</li>
<li>Accountable for the performance and results of teams focused on similar work</li>
<li>Develops plans, including business, operational and/or organizational priorities</li>
<li>Makes decisions guided by resource availability and short-term functional or brand objectives</li>
<li>May spend up to 20% of time as an individual contributor performing assignments that require a higher level of experience or technical expertise than subordinates</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Education &amp; Experience</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Typically requires BS/BA or MS/MA/MBA or other graduate degree in related discipline and at least 10-15 years of ad sales experience, including a sales management role</li>
<li>Ability to work in a fast-paced environment</li>
<li>Ability to work independently</li>
<li>Strong communication and presentation skills</li>
<li>Understanding of the business to business marketplace</li>
<li>Ability to analyze sales data to help create effective inventory yield management strategies</li>
<li>Knowledge and experience with core ad serving solutions</li>
<li>Work effectively under pressure and meet strict deadlines</li>
<li>Proficient in the use of the Microsoft Office suite of computer software</li>
<li>Attention to detail, accuracy, and strong organizational skills</li>
<li>Strong organizational skills with ability to act independently and responsibly</li>
<li>Capacity to juggle multiple tasks in a fast-paced environment</li>
<li>Excellent written and verbal communication skills</li>
<li>Must be able to work collaboratively with team members</li>
<li>Ability to work independently as well as in cross-functional groups</li>
<li>Employees will also perform other duties and responsibilities as assigned</li>
</ul>
