<h2>Marketing Communications Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>SUMMARY:</b></p>
<p>
The Marketing Communications (Marcom) Specialist's primary responsibilities are supporting the execution of marketing communications tactics. The Specialist will also develop content, support events and provide general Marcom Support as needed..</p>
<p><b>
Essential Functions and Responsibilities:</b></p>
<p><b>
40% - </b>Assist the supervising manager with the execution of marketing campaigns and the production of creative tactics, including direct mail, email, mass media (TV, print, and radio), digital marketing, and sales collateral. This position will support tasks such as:</p>
<ul>
<li>Developing campaign schedules and touch plans for integrated acquisition campaigns and customer loyalty/retention programs.</li>
<li>Working with the creative agency to develop artwork for the marketing tactics.</li>
<li>Obtaining all necessary approvals on marketing tactics from stakeholders, legal, etc.</li>
<li>Working with vendors and internal teams to deploy tactics.</li>
<li>Tracking and analyzing response rates.</li>
<li>Creating and distributing reports to key stakeholders, with recommendations for program optimization.</li>
<li>Managing production of sales collateral including slip sheets, order forms, posters, fliers, signage, videos and testimonials.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>25% - </b>Coordinate event sponsorships and company sales events including the planning, scheduling, set-up, break-down and logistics for events.</p>
<p><b>
20% - </b>Write and curate content to support the education of products and services for use in email marketing, enewsletters, social, website, sales tools, etc.</p>
<p><b>
15% -</b> Other responsibilities may include, but are not limited to, miscellaneous department reporting, graphic design, invoice entry, and tracking industry trends and best practices.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
SUPERVISORY RESPONSIBILITIES:</b></p>
<p>
This position requires no direct supervision of staff.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
COMPETENCIES:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Project Management - Develops project plans; Coordinates multiple projects at once; Communicates changes and progress; Completes projects on time and budget; Manages project team activities.</li>
<li>Strong verbal and writing skills - Ability to communicate effectively in various mediums.</li>
<li>Teamwork - Balances team and individual responsibilities; gives and welcomes feedback; supports everyone's efforts to succeed. Exhibits objectivity and openness to other's views; contributes to building a positive team spirit; able to build morale and group commitments to goals and objectives.</li>
<li>Strong attention to detail - accurate data gathering, insightful analysis, ability to manage logistics and tactical details with flawless execution.</li>
<li>Demonstrates accuracy and thoroughness; looks for ways to improve and promote quality.</li>
<li>Strong Sense of Accountability.</li>
<li>Proven track record in working effectively with cross functional groups, sales organizations, and operations.</li>
<li>Ability to operate in a dynamic environment with minimal supervision.</li>
<li>Understands and applies procedures, regulations, and policies related to areas of specialized expertise.</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p>
</p>
<p><b>QUALIFICATIONS:</b></p>
<p><b>
Education or equivalent</b>:</p>
<p>
Bachelor's degree in Business, Marketing, Advertising or Communications or equivalent of two years' work experience in a related field.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Professional training or knowledge:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Three (3) years of marketing experience in support of consumer or business related campaigns and various collateral development.</li>
<li>Excellent communications skills, both verbal and written.</li>
<li>Proficiency in Photoshop, InDesign, and/or Illustrator and other graphics design software desired.</li>
<li>Demonstrated working knowledge in MS Office (Word, Outlook, Excel &amp; PowerPoint).</li>
<br>
</ul>
