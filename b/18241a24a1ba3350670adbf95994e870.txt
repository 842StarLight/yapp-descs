<p><b>Job Description:
</b></p>Oversee the business/accounting processing functions for the LBJ School of Public Affairs including procurement, business contracts, payments, and receivables. Supervise related business and administrative support staff within the Chief Business Officer’s portfolio ensuring accurate and timely completion of work.
<p><b>-
</b></p>
<p><b>Job Details:
</b></p>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities
</b></h2>
<ul>
<li>Oversee the LBJ School’s business processing to include procurement, business contracts, payments, vouchers, receivables, and other related processes. Ensure funds are disbursed and received according to UT and LBJ rules, processes, and procedures. Audit vouchers, wire requests, reconciliations, and other related documents for quality, validity, and compliance. Serve as designated final approver within delegated limits, obtaining additional approvals as needed. Ensure required documentation is on file and conduct regular record retention reviews. Prepare fund transfers as assigned. Act as primary point of contact with Office of Accounting, Business Contracts, Provost, and other UT offices for these functions.
</li>
<li>Lead the CBO’s Business and Administrative Services (BAS) team to provide high quality, professional services to clients. Service areas include business processing (procurement, payments, receivables, etc.) and designated administrative tasks. Ensure adherence to service level expectations and compliance with policies and procedures. Monitor/assign tickets (work assignments). Evaluate and balance workloads as needed during peak periods. Assess development opportunities and support team member’s professional development plans.
</li>
<li>Maintain an expert level of knowledge of related university and school policies and procedures. Serve as primary internal point of contact for guidance and/or training in related business processes and policies. Collaborate with central offices to ensure accurate and the most current information is available. Assist in developing and continuously improving business processes, procedures, systems, and documentation including desk manuals, cheat sheets, and web pages. Create, implement, and monitor training plans for school employees responsible for related functions. Serve as DocuSign Unit Contact.
</li>
<li>As assigned: coordinate projects, process documents/vouchers; and serve as back-up for CBO and other designated staff including attending meetings on behalf of the CBO.
</li>
<li>Other duties as assigned.
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Required Qualifications
</b></h2>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree in accounting, finance, management, business administration or a related field.
</li>
<li>Five years of management experience in accounting, finance, and/or administration.
</li>
<li>Supervisory experience.
</li>
<li>Experience with UT-Austin business policies and procedures related to procurement and payments.
</li>
<li>Prior experience using university systems including *DEFINE and financial modules in UTDirect (e.g., FRMS).
</li>
<li>Proficient in Microsoft Excel and applications in the Microsoft Office suite. Professional demeanor and demonstrated problem-solving skills.
</li>
<li>Demonstrated ability to manage and motivate personnel to achieve high quality results. Ability to meet deadlines and to successfully identify, organize and complete tasks independently and in a team environment.
</li>
<li>Excellent written, verbal and interpersonal communication skills.
</li>
<li>Experience working with confidential information and providing support to customers using tact, discretion, and diplomacy.
</li>
<li>Relevant education and experience may be substituted as appropriate.
</li>
</ul>
