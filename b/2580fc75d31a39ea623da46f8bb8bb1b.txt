<h2>Technical Principal</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p><b>About ThoughtWorks</b><br>
We are a global software consultancy and a community of passionate, purpose-led individuals. We think disruptively to deliver technology to address our clients' toughest challenges, all while seeking to revolutionize the IT industry and create positive social change.</p>
<p>
As a Principal Consultant at ThoughtWorks, you have total responsibility for technical delivery on a client engagement so you are likely to have a rich breadth of industry experience.</p>
<p><b>
Here’s what you will bring:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Expertise in multiple technologies (including Java, .Net, Ruby/Ruby on Rails/Scala/Clojure etc) across software engineering, security, data interchange, data management etc.</li>
<li>Experience as an architect or leading and inspiring software development team.</li>
<li>Solid understanding of the practical application of agile development methods and leading software development teams in an Agile/Lean/Continuous Delivery environment.</li>
<li>Significant software development and infrastructure architecture experience in designing and architecting enterprise web applications.</li>
<li>Understanding of the issues businesses face and experience working with them to create robust, scalable, elegant, flexible and relevant software solutions that truly transform industries.</li>
<li>Experience working directly with senior IT groups in an advisory role.</li>
<li>Experience working with CTO/COO to create technical strategies.</li>
<li>Any experience building and leading an offshore/outsourcing function would be highly beneficial.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>As a Principal Consultant, here’s what you can do at ThoughtWorks:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Take the needs and challenges of a client and formulate the technical roadmap and technology solution that will support their business strategies and goals.</li>
<li>Provide technical leadership across all areas of the enterprise to ensure delivery of exceptional technical solutions.</li>
<li>Formulate and articulate the technical and political implication of real-world solutions.</li>
<li>Orchestrate the management of technology scope and risks.</li>
<li>Mentor on approach and execution of solutions, coach on technologies and establishing a team-wide comprehension of solution capabilities and direction.</li>
<li>Ensure technical expectations of deliverables are met.</li>
<li>Drive ThoughtWorks’ Thought-Leadership on engineering and architectural practices and standards.</li>
<li>Be an inspiration for innovation to the client.</li>
<li>Become a trusted and valued partner of the client CIO/CTO and team.</li>
<li>Exploit opportunities for networking and relationship building that arise out of delivery activities.</li>
<li>Create and support sales activities.</li>
<li>Maintaining strong expertise and knowledge of current and emerging technologies and products.</li>
<li>Code! We don’t subscribe to the “post-technical” ivory tower leadership style.</li>
</ul>
<div>
<p><i>A few important things to know:</i></p>
<b>Projects are almost exclusively on customer site, so candidates should be flexible and open to extensive travel.</b>
