<h2>E-Commerce Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>eCommerce Manager</b></p>
<p><b>
Job Description</b></p>
<p>
SectorSeven, LLC, a global solutions provider focused on hardware, software, services and related products, has an immediate opening for an E-commerce Manager.</p>
<p><b>
Key Duties:</b></p>
<p>
Primarily responsible for presenting and selling company products and services via e-commerce channels. This includes management of multiple eBay stores, SectorSeven.com, Amazon.com, Walmart.com, and other additional digital marketplace channels.
</p>
<p>Responsibilities include:</p>
<ul>
<li>Day to day hands on management of Sector Seven’s presence &amp; products on e-commerce platforms. Product presentation (listing products and inventory), product marketing strategy , pricing, and adjusting all these as often needed.
</li>
<li>Generate and implement new initiatives to increase D2C sales and profitability.
</li>
<li>Direct technical and transactional support of customers via e-commerce platforms or other tools.</li>
<li>Establishing and monitoring prices for all products in the eCommerce channel.
</li>
<li>Maintain a keen awareness of our competition to study what’s works and what does not.
</li>
<li>Develop and maintain sales materials and current product knowledge.</li>
<li>Develop &amp; lead e-commerce strategy for entry or expansion of e-commerce platforms to reach sales and margin goals.</li>
<li>Prepare action plans and schedules to identify specific targets, and potential sales volumes.</li>
<li>Establish necessary direct relationship with e-commerce retailers to feature SectorSeven products on their platforms. ( I.E weekly daily deal submissions.)</li>
<li>Negotiation of appropriate contracts, fees, and ongoing relationship management with vendors and on-line retailers and other ancillary providers.</li>
<li>Optimization of SectorSeven on-line presence using tactical SEO.</li>
<li>Forecasts across all eCommerce channels, including product launches, promotions, and establishing a steady baseline for unit volume, revenue, and profitability.</li>
<li>Monitor competition by gathering current marketplace information on pricing, products, trends, etc.</li>
<li>Communicate new product and service opportunities, special developments, information, or feedback gathered through field activity to appropriate company staff.</li>
<li>Other duties as assigned.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Skills:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>High enthusiasm towards learning about new wireless products, devices and technology trends.</li>
<li>Energetic and self-motivated. Strong drive for sales and performance management.</li>
<li>Excellent written and verbal communication skills.</li>
<li>Knowledge and experience of developing, implanting and optimizing digital content.</li>
<li>Knowledge and experience using the eBay platform and Amazon.com platform as a seller.</li>
<li>Experience with e-commerce systems, e-commerce retailers, SEO, and social media management.</li>
<li>Demonstrated ability to persuade and influence others; strong negotiation and relationship building skills.</li>
<li>Ability to meet customer needs and close sales.</li>
<li>Strong organization and time management skills; ability to maintain a fluctuating workload and varied job tasks in a fast-paced environment.</li>
<li>Strong analytical and judgment skills.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Qualifications:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>BS or BA in Marketing, Communications, Computer Science or related degree preferred.</li>
<li>Demonstrated experience in wireless sales and marketing – either with a wireless carrier, retailer, or wireless device manufacturer. 2-3 years of experience preferred.</li>
<li>Demonstrated substantial experience running an eBay store – either as an individual or as part of an enterprise.</li>
</ul>
<p>Preferred location for this role is Dallas/Fort Worth area but open to remote options for the perfect candidate.</p>
