<p>
KPMG is currently seeking a Director, Performance Innovation to join our Managed Services organization.</p>
<p>
Responsibilities:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Perform business operating model capabilities with special knowledge in areas such as close/consolidation, planning, budgeting/forecasting, and reporting/analytics</li>
<li>
Lead the design and build business process operating models including those in finance and supply chain</li>
<li>
Define the future of management/financial reporting, key performance measures and other analytics</li>
<li>
Provide technology exposure to leading finance software applications (e.g., Microsoft Dynamics or similar applications)</li>
<li>
Manage and deliver complex Finance and Business Transformations</li>
<li>
Support sales and business development efforts</li>
</ul>
<p>
Qualifications:</p>
<ul>
<li>
A minimum of ten years consulting experience showing progression/promotion</li>
<li>
Bachelor's degree from an accredited college/university; CPA, MBA or other relevant advanced degree is preferred</li>
<li>
Exceptional understanding of Finance and business strategy and operations, project management methodologies, and leading practices</li>
<li>
Progressive experience leading teams, mentoring staff, evaluating performance, and providing performance feedback</li>
<li>
Outstanding verbal and written communication skills including presentation skills and oral presentation skills including presentation and facilitation</li>
<li>
Travel may be up to 50%</li>
<li>
Applicants must be currently authorized to work in the United States without the need for visa sponsorship now or in the future</li>
</ul>
