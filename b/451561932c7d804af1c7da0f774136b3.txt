<h2>Marketing and Technology Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>Weichert Co. is hiring a Marketing and Technology Specialist in the Edison, NJ Sales Office. The Marketing and Technology Specialist is responsible for providing support to Sales Associates to assist in building their individual business through utilization of company systems and resources for their assigned office(s). The position drives the adoption of Weichert's technology platform(s) suite of marketing tools through group and individual meetings and training sessions. In addition, the position prepares marketing materials on behalf of the Sales Associates to help them obtain and promote property listings.The position also provides onsite technical support and other technology related assistance to ensure Sales Associates are aware of and consistently using all Weichert technologies and systems.
<div></div>
<div>Job responsibilities include, but are not limited to, the following:</div>
<div></div>
<div>
<ul>
<li>
<p>Builds relationships with Sales Associates to become a Trusted Advisor partnering with them to support their personal promotion, marketing and advertising efforts.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Educates and trains Sales Associates during the onboarding process and on an as needed basis on all Weichert technologies and systems.</p>
</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>
<p>Conducts training sessions to introduce and/or train Sales Associates on all existing and new company technology initiatives, programs and processes. Acts as the main point of contact for sales office technology and related needs.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Trains, supports, and assists Sales Associate in developing their lead incubation follow-up and conversion on the platform tools and resources available.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Supports the enterprise cross-sell initiative by encouraging utilization of Weichert mortgage, title, insurance and closing services branded as All Under One Roof.</p>
</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>
<p>Promotes, creates, and produces customized listing presentations and marketing materials through Weichert technology platforms and vendor partner systems while adhering to Weichert brand standards.</p>
</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>
<p>Supports office initiatives and recognition programs via social media posts. Monitors and responds to conversations on social media channels when appropriate.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Assists the Sales Manager(s) in creating, promoting and supporting local community and office events by helping to facilitate the event and promoting through social media.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Presents marketing and advertising products and systems to Sales Associates at sales meetings, training sessions, etc as needed.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Partners with Weichert Technology and Marketing Departments to deploy and promote new programs, tools and initiatives and serves as liaison for the technology and marketing departments to the field.</p>
</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>
<p>Remains current on all Weichert technology platforms. Attends regularly scheduled training programs/webinars in these areas.</p>
</li>
<li><p>Performs other duties as assigned.</p></li>
</ul>
</div>
</div>
