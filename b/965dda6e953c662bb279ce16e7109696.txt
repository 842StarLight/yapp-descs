Description</div>
<div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Position Objective
</b></h2>
<div>Perform process-engineering activities (equipment sizing, technology selection, troubleshooting etc.) for the delivery of custom and standard bioprocessing equipment across the Pall Biotechnology catalog. Scope is inclusive of all project phases: quotation, design, fabrication, testing, and customer site acceptance.
</div>
<div>After order placement execute the proposed engineered system solution based on agreed and defined contractual specification together with a defined project team as a process engineer under supervision of an assigned project manager. Work closely with project manager and senior engineers to ensure engineering solutions meet customer requirements and project schedule and budget.
</div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Job Functions</b></h2>
<div>
Deliver process-engineering deliverables under the direction of the project manager or engineer manager in alignment with standard work, including, but not limited to:
</div>
<ul>
<li>Generating all project relevant design documents, P&amp;IDs, GAs, Electrical and Software
</li>
<li>Coordinating vendors and sub-contractors
</li>
<li>Communicating project status internally as well as with customers
</li>
<li>Performing Pre-FAT, FAT and SAT testing and deviation resolution
</li>
<li>Finalizing documentation for turn over packages (TOP)</li>
<li>
To work safely and to align with the company’s health, safety and environmental (HSE) policies and procedures
</li>
</ul>
<div>Provide sales support for assigned preorder projects (generate technical and commercial Quotations for customer specific projects, generate exceptions and clarifications lists, customer presentations, technical clarification meetings). Provide manufacturing costing information on quotations as required.</div>

<div>
Qualifications</div>
<div>
<div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Basic Qualifications</b></h2>
<ul>
<li>
Bachelor's of Engineering (biotechnology, chemical, mechanical or relevant)</li>
<li>
Experience in biotechnology equipment design</li>
<li>
Valid Drivers License
</li>
</ul>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Preferred Qualifications</b></h2>
<ul>
<li>
Ability to review and comment customer URS for biopharmaceutical equipment, and evaluate possible technologies or designs to meet those requirements</li>
<li>
Understanding of filtration separation and purification technologies used in the downstream processing (Direct flow filtration, tangential flow filtration, chromatography)
</li>
<li>Knowledge and understanding of the current industry guidelines for designing, manufacturing, testing, and documenting biopharmaceutical process equipment (ASME BPE, GAMP, cGMP, ISPE, etc.)
</li>
<li>Ability and willingness to work in a multinational and interdisciplinary team with a high degree of travelling
</li>
<li>Ability to communicate effectively on complex technical/engineering issues at a high level, both internally and with the customer</li>
<li>
Strong communication skills</li>
</ul>
