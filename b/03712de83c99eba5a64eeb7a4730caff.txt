<h2>Customer Success Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>
<div><b>Summary</b></div>
<div>
Cosential has been providing comprehensive CRM and Proposal Automation solutions for some of the world's largest Architecture, Engineering, and Construction (AEC) firms for twenty years. Unlike any other solution, Cosential was built by AEC marketing professionals with a deep understanding of your unique pain points and business processes. From key features to implementation and support, Cosential is tailored to AEC's best practices. Voted a 2019 “Best Places to Work”, we have a large addressable market and leading CRM tool that is #1 in the AEC industry. At Cosential, we put culture first and build upon this through teamwork, innovation, and a never give up attitude. Come join our company where you can contribute to an early stage team and help provide the vision for future success.
</div>
<div></div>
<div><b>Your Role:</b></div>
<div>
As a Customer Success Manager, you own the overall relationship with assigned customers and are responsible for product adoption, ensuring retention, driving growth and overall satisfaction. You will be responsible for working with customers to maximize the use of Cosential’s solutions and establish a trusted advisor relationship by working to deliver value of products and services.</div>
<div>
Cosential is looking for a self-motivated, flexible and technology-oriented Customer Success Manager to join our team. The ideal candidate will possess strong consultative selling skills, be a strong negotiator and have experience in identifying customer needs, delivering value and managing relationships.</div>
</div>
<div><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities:</b></h3>
<div><ul>
<li>
Manages customers through consultative selling processes to achieve renewal and expansion revenue targets. Creatively investigates customer’s organizational landscape and builds relationships to expand Cosential’s footprint and drive additional revenue</li>
<li>
Leverages domain and product knowledge to drive product adoption and expose opportunities to expand footprint
</li>
<li>Proactively forecasts and communicates at-risk customers and defines mitigation strategies for securing retention</li>
<li>
Develops long-term plans and prioritizes customer requests to maintain a high degree of confidence and trust
</li>
<li>Coordinates and manages commitments across a team of Cosential resources leveraging subject matter experts to support customer requests. Provides updates and follow ups to customers on outstanding issues, requests and escalations</li>
<li>
Coordinates regular account reviews by gathering and presenting status of customer goals, issues and roadmap requests. Produces reports that include data supporting the value delivered through usage of the product</li>
<li>
Creates advocates by ensuring that customers achieve program goals while maintaining strong relationships</li>
<li>
Develops creative solutions to support complex customer requirements</li>
<li>
Effectively multitasks and manages multiple priorities simultaneously</li>
</ul></div>
</div></div>
<div><div>

<div><ul>
<li>
Bachelors degree or commensurate experience in a Customer Success environment.</li>
<li>
3+ years of demonstrated success in a similarly structured Customer Success or Account Management role, preferably in a technology organization.</li>
<li>
Aptitude for technology knowledge, spreadsheet and CRM Utilization; previous experience in Architecture, Engineering or Construction desirable</li>
<li>
The ability to be flexible and work in a rapidly changing environment is required</li>

Strong creative, multi-tasking, problem solving and organizational skills
</li>
<li>The ability to successfully lead and collaborate cross-functionally with colleagues including Accounting, Technology, Enterprise Sales, Marketing and Administration</li>

</ul></div>
