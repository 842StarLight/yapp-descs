Professional Development Program (PDP)- Various Departments at HQ<p></p>
This program focuses on providing you with the opportunity to grow your professional skills along with the Niagara business. Over the next 24 months, the program will give you the opportunity to experience several different assignments in a variety of departments that will ultimately lead you to a full-time role in one of the following target departments (final placement may not necessarily be limited to these departments as business needs arise):<br>
<br>
<ul>
<li>Asset Management</li>
<li>Finance</li>
<li>Human Resources</li>
<li>Information Technology</li>
<li>International Business</li>
<li>Packaging Development and Design</li>
<li>Procurement</li>
<li>New Product Development</li>
<li>Research and Development (Materials, Technology, and Packaging)</li>
<li>Sales</li>
<li>Supply Chain - Logistics &amp; Operations</li>
<li>Supply Chain Planning</li>
<li>Supply Chain Solutions</li>
<li>Systems Engineering</li>
<li>Technical Services</li>
</ul>
<br>
The goal of the PDP is to transform recent college graduates into high-performing Analysts, Specialists, or Engineers in the business. Additionally, the PDP supports the individual’s growth through exposure to the business, skill development, and networking opportunities. Specifically, you will participate in monthly learning sessions that will cover topics like time management, professional communication, and personal brand!<p></p>
<p><b>
Rotation Plan</b></p>
<p>
During the program, a PDP in assigned a home department and has the opportunity to rotate into 3 additional departments at HQ. We believe it is important to experience the heart of our business and to have the opportunity to get a first-hand glimpse at what makes Niagara’s manufacturing plants the most advanced in the country, therefore, it is required to complete 1 rotation in a manufacturing plant. The department rotations should be 3-5 months each.</p>
