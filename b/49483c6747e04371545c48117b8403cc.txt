<h2>Renewals Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Join the Client Success Team of an exciting software company in Austin, Texas!</b></p>
<p>
CareerPlug provides innovative recruiting and HR software for over 10,000 growing companies. Our applicant tracking and paperless onboarding platforms help companies make better hires to have the right people in place to build a successful business.</p>
<p>
You can feel something special when you walk into our office located in the heart of Austin, TX. You can hear our team collaborating with our clients and one another, see our goals and commitments up on the walls next to our core values, and feel the energy and creativity at our innovation meetings where every team member is contributing their ideas. We believe that people are the heart of our business and are committed to building one of the best places to work in Austin. To us, that means putting care and purpose into our hiring process, providing meaningful development and training opportunities for our team members, and living our core values every day.</p>
<p>
The Renewals Manager partners with both internal and external stakeholders to proactively manage client renewals and increase overall client engagement and satisfaction.</p>
<p><b>
Who are you?</b></p>
<p>
You are a driven, goal-oriented renewals representative who loves the winback. Overcoming objections and proactively helping clients is a challenge that motivates you to bring your best and keep growing every day. You're a natural at building rapport and addressing pain points . . . plus you have experience creating scalable systems to meet client needs. You're looking for a collaborative environment where you can make a daily impact and continue to learn and grow.</p>
<p><b>
Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Contribute to making hiring easier by improving client experience and retention</li>
<li>Proactively manage at-risk clients to prevent cancellations</li>
<li>Manage and overcome client cancellation requests</li>
<li>Educate clients on best practices, focusing on providing value to overcome pain points and full system utilization</li>
<li>Collaborate with team to create an exceptional client experience at scale</li>
<li>Maintain deep understanding of CareerPlug product updates and offerings</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Job Requirements:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>1-3 years experience in renewals, client success or sales; SaaS environments preferred</li>
<li>Client-facing communicator with proven record of developing rapport and connecting with clients via phone/email</li>
<li>Demonstrated experience driving value for clients through product offering</li>
<li>Excellent verbal and written communication skills</li>
<li>Ability to overcome objections and negotiate effectively</li>
<li>Proficient in Salesforce.com or other CRM is a plus</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Compensation: </b>$65,000 - $70,000 including bonus potential. This is a salary + variable compensation role.</p>

</ul>
