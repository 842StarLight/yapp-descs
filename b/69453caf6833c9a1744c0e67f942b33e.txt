<h2>Production and Purchasing Project Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Company: </b></p>
<p>Graphique de France has an exciting new full-time opportunity! We are a leading stationery, gift, and life-style product company located north of Boston.</p>
<p><b>General Overview: </b></p>
<p>This is a multi-faceted role as a Production and Purchasing Project Manager who reports to the Chief Operating Officer.</p>
<p>This role manages the production schedule internally and externally with overseas vendors. It works closely with teams cross-departmentally with Designers, Pre-Production, Licensing and Data to make sure our product is produce correctly across the board. It also works with IT, Sales and Marketing on product data needs.</p>
<p>The Production and Purchasing Project Manager audits the system data regularly through reporting analysis to ensure the integrity of all products in the system. It keeps track of all projects and communicates across the company on all updates and statuses. It ensures our products are represented accurately on the website and all print and digital materials.</p>
<p>This role juggles many priorities at any given time, so a wide degree of creativity and being comfortable in quick decision making is key.</p>
<p>·Work with overseas vendors to ensure projects are produced correctly, on budget, and on time, in partnership with Prepress and Logistics.</p>
<p>·Project manages Product Development, Design, Pre-Press, Licensing, sales and marketing on all projects that come through to ensure processes are in place and being used</p>
<p>·Manages Product Manager in Data Management &amp; Verification</p>
<p>·Quality control management in conjunction with Prepress and Design</p>
<p>·Manage inventory daily and propose replenishment actions</p>
<p>·Product Data analysis for the purposes of performance forecasting and line balance</p>
<p>·Cost analysis to ensure products are going to be produced on budget</p>
<p>·Manage Specification and Cost database for all product</p>
<p>·Create purchase orders and packing specification to send to vendors</p>
<p>· Communicate with Sales and Marketing about schedules and product availability</p>
<p>·Assist in Product SKU creation and entry of specifications and Licensing process</p>
<p>·Manage the calendar grid process; oversees calendar holidays and date verification process for the calendar product line.</p>
<p>·Works with IT and Marketing to ensure web uploads on multiple databases are accurate, on time, and all required information is supplied</p>
<p><b>Requirements: </b></p>
<p>·Bachelor’s Degree in Business or related industry is preferred</p>
<p>·Ability to work independently with little supervision and with other team members</p>
<p>·Excellent written, verbal and interpersonal communication skills</p>
<p>·Strong analytical, problem solving and decision-making skills</p>
<p>·Ability to quickly evaluate incoming work and manage juggling priorities in time critical situations</p>
<p>·Impeccable process and project management skills and proficiency with PM tools such as Basecamp is needed</p>
<p>·Advanced Excel skills; Proficient with MS Office applications including Microsoft Word, and Outlook</p>
<p>·Proven experience with Cost Analysis and Budgeting</p>
<p>·Knowledge of Print Production and/or Manufacturing</p>
<p>·Strong computer skills and ability to learn new programs and softwares</p>
<p>Graphique de France is based in Woburn, MA, 15 miles north of Boston and close to Routes 95 and 93. We enjoy a creative, casual and entrepreneurial atmosphere, with a strong sense of corporate values and morale. Graphique de France offers a competitive salary and a comprehensive benefits package.</p>
