<h2>Community Mortgage Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Overview
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div>Are you Service-Driven and Detail Oriented? Do you thrive in a fast-paced environment? USE’s Real Estate Department is the perfect place for you! As a Community Mortgage Specialist, you’ll have the chance to create an effortless lending experience and help clients achieve their dreams. Become part of a team that is key in delivering extraordinary experiences. Do you have what it takes?</div>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<ul>
<li>Greet and welcome clients to the credit union in a courteous and professional manner.</li>
<li>
Provide an extra-ordinary service experience and adhere to the credit union’s quality service standards.</li>
<li>
Effectively sell first and second mortgage products and services to meet each client’s individual financial needs and goals.</li>
<li>
Originate mortgage loan applications using loan origination systems (LOS), and utilize sales tools to assist clients in choosing the right loan for their personal financial situation.</li>
<li>
Follow-up on all assigned mortgage leads and update the status within the LOS, within established timelines to ensure all service guarantees are met.</li>
<li>
Originate Fixed 2nd Trust Deed and HELOC loan applications using the LOS, when needed.</li>
<li>
Set proper expectations for the loan process, and conduct a warm handoff of loan to processing team to ensure an extra-ordinary service experience.</li>
<li>
Communicate loan status updates to all parties during the mortgage process when needed to ensure on-time loan closing. Ask for referrals and constantly prospect for new clients and business referral partners.</li>
<li>
Develop annual business plan with monthly/quarterly strategies for assigned market area, with VP, Real Estate or Area Lending Manager. Once business plan is approved, you will independently implement the strategies of the plan.</li>
<li>
Initiate proactive calling program to external targeted prospects and proactively manage call list or programs.</li>
<li>
Attend community networking mixers</li>
<li>
Maintain monthly sales log; along with the monthly Status of Report.</li>
<li>
Manage loan pipelines, including timely and proactive follow-up on leads, approved loans, meeting targeted loan closing ratios and other product cross-sell ratios. Update the LOS with member conversations as required by management.</li>
<li>
Maintain the required knowledge of policies and procedures, laws and regulations, as well as other compliance requirements.</li>
<li>
Record all transactions promptly and accurately in compliance with all credit union policies and procedures.</li>
<li>
Properly identify all customers when transacting business and giving information.</li>
<li>
Remain current and knowledgeable of all credit union products and services.</li>
<li>
Cross-sell and refer investment and insurance products.</li>
<li>
Meet loan production volume and all other goals as assigned.</li>
<li>
Respond promptly to customer inquiries, handle and/or resolve client issues/complaints.</li>
<li>
Protect the credit union assets by adhering to safe and sound security practices.</li>
<li>
Work efficiently with all team members to reach monthly and annual loan production goals.</li>
</ul>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Qualifications
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor's degree (B. A.) from four-year College or University; and minimum of 3 years of previous experience in originating first mortgage loans is required; and/or combination of education and experience.</li>
<li>
Knowledge of market area is required.</li>
<li>
Ability to effectively present information in one-on-one and small group situations to customers, clients, and other employees of the organization.</li>
<li>
Ability to use loan origination systems, contact management software, mortgage coach software and Microsoft Office application products, as well as the ability to learn new applications quickly; requires general knowledge of computer networks and database use.</li>
<li>
Valid California driver’s license required.</li>
<li>
All Mortgage Loan Originators (MLO) must be actively registered with the National Mortgage Licensing Service (NMLS). Must renew and pass annually to maintain active status.</li>
<li>
To perform this job, the employee must possess strong interpersonal skills, good phone and e-mail etiquette, a professional presentation, and a high degree of personal initiative.</li>
<li>
Must exhibit the following characteristics: honesty, integrity, stress tolerance, adaptability, patience, initiative, persuasiveness, resilience and enthusiasm. Must be goal driven and results focused.</li>
<li>
Due to the nature of the outside calling functions, the employee must be able to work nights and weekends.</li>
</ul>
