Our technology provides the collaborative infrastructure for these communities. We care deeply about the work we do and the communities our software benefits. Come to Unite Us and together we can build healthier communities for everyone.
<br><br>
<b>Description:</b><br>
Unite Us is looking for an Events Associate to join our growing tech startup that is transforming health and social services. The Events Associate will be working cross-functionally with our Sales, Customer Success, and Marketing teams to support our company's growth. As an Events Associate you will be responsible for managing all company hosted events, annual summit, sponsorships, and conferences. You will report to the Communications Manager but will work cross functionally to coordinate and track specific event KPI's and manage the company event budget.
<br><br>
We are looking for someone who is highly organized, a clear communicator, flexible with the ability to multi-task and manage multiple projects, and is passionate about social issues and transforming our health and human services sector.
<br><br>
<b>What You'll Do:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Coordinate vendors, speakers, exhibitors and other stakeholders for Unite Us hosted regional events and Unite Us Annual Summit.</li>
<li>Work with the Sales and Customer Success teams to identify national and regional conferences to attend and coordinate all registrations, speaking opportunities, and budget.</li>
<li>Coordinate internally within marketing to identify and organize conference materials and necessary communications, collateral, and other marketing assets to support the events.</li>
<li>Work with the Communications Manager to identify strategic sponsorships and speaking opportunities to further promote Unite Us as an industry thought leader.</li>
<li>Manage and track relevant Key Performance Indicators for events and conferences.</li>
</ul>
Education and Experience
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor's Degree from an accredited institution</li>
<li>Excellent writing, communication, and project management skills</li>
<li>1+ years of relevant experience in event planning, sponsorships, or communications.</li>
</ul>
<b>Environmental Job Requirements &amp; Working Conditions:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>This position is based in New York, NY</li>
<li>All prospective employees must pass a background check</li>
</ul>
