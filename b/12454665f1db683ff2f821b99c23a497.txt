<h2>Leasing Director</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Job Description</b></p>
<p>Pomeroy Living Orion Assisted facility is a state of the art senior living community that provides Independent Living, Assisted Living, Memory Care and the soon to open Cottages of Orion accommodations. This unique campus brings together wellness, innovation, expertise, and resources, all provided within a caring connected community</p>
<p>We are currently looking for a Leasing Director to continue our ongoing community awareness of the facility and build the excitement for our state of the art facility within the entire community.</p>
<p><b>The Leasing Director will be responsible for: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Creating community awareness and presence</li>
<li>Sales and Marketing management for the facility</li>
<li>Effective Leadership to build an innovative team approach to sales and marketing, first within the community and eventually ongoing with the entire staff as well</li>
<li>Establish and maintain an effective referral database management system</li>
<li>Excellent communication and follow up with all audiences</li>
</ul>
<p>*<br><b>The successful candidate will possess the following skills and abilities: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>A devotion to our residents which is first and foremost in everything we do</li>
<li>A passion for assisting Seniors to meet their housing and personal wellbeing needs</li>
<li>2-3 years of Sales and Marketing experience</li>
<li>1-2 or more years of experience in Senior Living preferred</li>
<li>Excellent communication skills-verbal, written and electronic</li>
<li>Excellent follow-up ability</li>
<li>A working knowledge of basic office equipment and software</li>
<li>Ability to create an effective sales plan and establish effective and measurable sales and marketing goals</li>
<li>A passion and drive to meet and/or exceed each and every one of these goals</li>
<li>A basic working knowledge of all state and federal requirements</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Benefits: </b></p>
<p>Benefits are competitive and include major medical health insurance, dental &amp; vision coverage, 401(k) program, and paid time off.</p>
<p>Job Type: Full-time</p>
</div>
