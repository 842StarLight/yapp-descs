<h2>Account Manager, Restoration</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>All Dry USA, a 24 hour emergency service company specializing in Water, Fire and Mold Restoration is seeking a highly organized Account Manager, who is looking to work in a fast-paced environment where customer service is the number one priority.<br>
</div>
<div><b>Responsibilities and Duties</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Actively and consistently support all efforts to enhance the customer experience.</li>
<li>Timely and accurately track all job referral data for locations throughout the country, reporting the sources, outcome of incoming leads, outgoing leads, etc.</li>
<li>Maintain records within a CRM system</li>
<li>Represent the company at sponsored events and networking opportunities, acting as a brand ambassador for All Dry USA and educating potential customers on the services we provide.</li>
<li>Build and cultivate prospect sources relationships by initiating communication and conducting follow-up meetings</li>
<li>Manage data for existing and prospective sources within DASH (our restoration management software program), ensuring all communications are logged in and accurate</li>
</ul>
<div><b>Qualifications and Skills</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Must have prior sales or marketing experience, preferably in the restoration or construction industry</li>
<li>Administrative support or clerical experience required</li>
<li>Must have exceptional communication skills</li>
<li>Experience working with Google Suite and Microsoft Office</li>
<li>Must have a strong work ethic</li>
<li>Must have clean driving record and be able to pass a full background check</li>
</ul>
