<h2>DSP Platform Sales (LA or SF)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>As Verizon’s media unit, our brands like Yahoo, TechCrunch and HuffPost help people stay informed and entertained, communicate and transact, while creating new ways for advertisers and partners to connect. With technologies like XR, AI, machine-learning, and 5G, we’re transforming media for tomorrow, too. We're creators and coders, dreamers and doers creating what's next in content, advertising and technology.

<div><b>Responsibilities:
</b></div>
<div><div><div><div>
<ul>
<li>Obtain, develop, and cultivate relationships with key advertiser and agency contacts
</li>
<li>Become an invaluable resource for clients on Verizon Media's capabilities as well as industry and competitive knowledge
</li>
<li>Identify and close strategic opportunities across potential new advertiser and agency partners
</li>
<li>Create unique selling propositions using your knowledge, tenure, and interest in the programmatic landscape including but not limited to DSPs, DMPs, Attribution Modeling, PMPs, Data Onboarding, and Mobile capabilities

<li>Complete regular certifications to maintain the highest level of product expertise
</li>
<li>Identify client feature requests and help prioritize across both product development and engineering

<li>Foster an environment that builds upon Oath’s culture
