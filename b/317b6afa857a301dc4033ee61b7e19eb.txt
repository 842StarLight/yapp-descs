<p><b>
Position: Senior Sales Executive</b></p>
<p><b>
Location: Dallas</b></p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Responsibilities:</b></p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
This position is part of the NTT Global Networks sales team that is responsible for sales development and new customer acquisition. Reporting to the Sales Management Executive, the key responsibilities for this role include:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Prospecting and soliciting new business and drive customer acquisition for NTT Global Networks within the assigned territory.</li>
<li>
Conduct business development activities and other territory-based demand generation to identify and lead the sales process from initial prospect qualification through contract signature.</li>
<li>
Qualify prospects against company criteria for ideal customers and sales</li>
<li>
Understanding and clear articulation of the NTT Global Networks value proposition, key differentiators and competitive positioning to prospects including C level executives</li>
<li>
Adhere to the sales methodology and manage the pipeline in accordance with the criteria required to advance opportunities based on the methodology</li>
<li>
Follow-up qualify and work on all leads that are generated and or assigned from NTT Global Networks demand generation campaigns and outreach programs.</li>
<li>
Provide timely and accurate sales forecasting information and periodically perform pipeline health management to reflect the realistic opportunity workload and value.</li>
<li>
Focus on target attainment and conduct all necessary activities required to meet sales targets</li>
<li>
Work in conjunction with the Solution Engineering team to drive sales progression, relationship development and be the NTT Global Networks point of contact in the positioning of capabilities, solution rationalization and driving opportunities to closure working across all the relevant functions.</li>
<li>
Establish relationships with new customers and secure contracts that achieve assigned sales targets</li>
<li>
Consult with prospect about business challenges and requirements, as well as the range of options and cost benefits of each.</li>
<li>
Maintain a high level of relevant market knowledge in order to have meaningful conversations with prospects.</li>
<li>
Consistently maintains an accurate picture on prospect and opportunity interactions into CRM system</li>
<li>
Provide a forecast pipeline on best case and most likely sales volumes over relevant time periods</li>
<li>
Be a positive representative of the company and its brand in the marketplace</li>
<li>
Conduct all sales activities with the highest degree of professionalism and integrity</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
</b><br>
<b>Skills and Experience</b></p>
<p></p>
<br>
<ul>
<li>
Must have in depth understanding and passion about the network business and previous working background in network technology and/or managed services. Good understanding of one or more of key areas such as SD-WAN, firewall/WAN acceleration, managed LAN services and/or security is required.</li>
<li>
Solid understanding of enterprise IT’s network needs and the transformation that is currently taking place in the enterprise IT landscape</li>
<li>
Prioritize the opportunities to make efficient use of solution engineering resources</li>
<li>
Strong communication/presentation skills as well as analytical skills</li>
<li>
Must have both technical and commercial understanding of the network business</li>
<li>
Demonstrate the passion to succeed, attain sales targets on a consistent basis and achieve top performer status among the peer group</li>
<li>
Self-starter with the ability to work in competitive situations and navigate past the hurdles and objections</li>
</ul>
