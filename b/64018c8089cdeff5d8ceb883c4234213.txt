<h2>General Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<b>AD 1 Global</b> is currently looking for a <b>General Manager</b> for their Tallahassee property, Wyndham Garden. This person will be responsible for managing the property operations on a day to day basis to ensure optimum performance and continual improvement in the five Key Result Areas (guest service, employees, sales/marketing, property appearance, and profit/financial control). This candidate must be self-motivated, determined, hands on and lead-by-example.<br>
As a General Manager, you are responsible for all aspects of the operation including guest and employee satisfaction, financial performance, asset protection, sales and revenue generation, employee relations and delivering a return on investment to the owners.<br>
<b>Some responsibilities include:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Demonstrate and promote a 100% commitment to providing the best possible experience for our guests and employees.</li>
<li>Responsible for maximizing revenues and flow through to GOP to meet or exceed budget</li>
<li>Responsible for preparation of property budget and forecasts</li>
<li>Manage labor standards and property level expenses to achieve maximum flow through to the bottom line profit</li>
<li>Explain and manage financial activities</li>
<li>Participate and monitor monthly inventory of supplies and equipment.</li>
<li>Ensure purchases made are within budget and by approved vendors</li>
<li>Promote 100% guest satisfaction throughout property.</li>
<li>Ensure that all guest related issues are resolved in a manner consistent with the company’s goals and objectives</li>
<li>Inspect and document repairs and cleanliness of property with Eng. Dept. to ensure optimum upkeep and repair, room cleanliness and overall property appearance</li>
</ul>
<br>
<b>
Requirements:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Minimum 5 years’ experience in the capacity of AGM or higher</li>
<li>Ability to speak, read and write fluently in English, bilingual a plus (Spanish)</li>
<li>Ability to organize multiple projects, manage and prioritize</li>
<li>Basic knowledge of computers and software including ability to use email, word, and excel.</li>
<li>Willing to work weekends and holidays when required</li>
</ul>
</div></div>
