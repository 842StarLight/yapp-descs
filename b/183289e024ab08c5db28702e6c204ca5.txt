<div><b>
***Allscripts Laboratory Medicine Demo Specialist*</b></div>
<div><i>
50% Travel, Remote Role</i></div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
The primary purpose of this high-travel role is to deliver world-class laboratory medicine demonstrations to prospective Allscripts clients.</div>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<ul>
<li>Collaborates with the Manager, Sr. Manager and/or Director, Solution Specialists and Sales to define the opportunities and priorities for each demo</li>
<li>
Lead the demonstration process through request, demo preparation, delivery and post demo review stages using established best practices</li>
<li>
Select existing archived scripts to best demonstrate the Allscripts solutions value while avoiding redundant modification</li>
<li>
Contribute to best practice library as new or enhanced workflows are designed and demonstrated</li>
<li>
Contribute to the post demonstration review process to identify best practice, provide feedback and develop enhancements to existing demo system</li>
<li>
Gather competitive intelligence whenever possible during demonstrations and communicate appropriately</li>
<li>
Control expenses to minimize travel and expense costs associated to the demonstration</li>
<li>
Crafts positioning and messaging for presentations developed in collaboration with Sales Team and Marketing</li>
<li>
Develop client relationships pre, during and post demo process</li>
<li>
Application of new technology into the demonstration process</li>
<li>
Prepare and utilize the most current demonstration environment, release and integration availability to meet opportunity needs and requirements</li>
<li>
Participate in the hiring process to ensure talented qualified candidates are selected</li>
<li>
Develop core and specialty demonstration skills and competencies</li>
<li>
Maintain knowledge of clinical practice as advancements are made in area of specialty. This is to include maintaining professional licensure, continuing education, professional organization membership and networking as appropriate</li>
</ul>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Qualifications
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div>Academic and Professional Qualifications:</div>
<ul>
<li>
Bachelors degree desired or equivalent experience</li>
<li>
Licensed medical professional (i.e. MT, CLS) strongly preferred</li>
</ul>
<div>
Experience:</div>
<ul>
<li>Minimum five years’ experience in healthcare environment, with in-depth knowledge of Laboratory Medicine</li>
<li>In-depth knowledge of Hematology, Chemistry, Microbiology, Blood Bank, and Anatomic Pathology</li>
<li>Understanding of current CLIA, CAP, and Joint Commission guidelines pertaining to Laboratory Medicine</li>
<li>Prior experience working with Allscripts Lab, NovoPath, and Blood Bank software (preferred)</li>
<li>Strong ability to communicate with others; excellent interpersonal and customer service skills</li>
<li>Strong oral communication skills with ability to speak and present via WebEx and in person</li>
<li>Able to navigate demo sites, prepare and prep demo systems and customize messaging based on client problems and Allscripts solutions</li>
<li>Proficient in translating client requirements into engaging product demonstrations</li>
<li>Flexibility in working with a variety of audiences from c-suite to end users to IT analysts</li>
<li>Deep understanding of the sales process and the relevance he/she plays re. client engagement and eventual deal closings</li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<br>
