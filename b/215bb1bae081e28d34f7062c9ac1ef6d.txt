<h2>IT Application Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div><div>
<p>The IT Applications Manager is a standalone role, which is responsible for ensuring that the IT applications across the division, are delivered to provide the right capabilities and deliver the right outcomes, cost effectively.
</p>
<p>Located at our Head Office in Chirnside Park and reporting to the IT Manager, the IT Applications Manager’s accountabilities span across planning, development and support for all business applications.<br>
<br>
</p>
<p><b>You will be responsible for:</b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Identify business capability gaps and improvement opportunities. Engage the business, build cooperative relationships and lead business analysis to determine the right solution for delivering the right business outcome</li>
<li>Manage business applications to ensure consistency with applications strategy, architecture and roadmaps</li>
<li>Work with the business to drive effective prioritisation of current and pipeline work. Advise and manage trade-offs between scope, resource, cost and time</li>
<li>Implement business solutions, manage the full development cycle covering both projects and application enhancements</li>
<li>Manage vendor relationships, develop / review service agreements and uphold vendors to respective service levels</li>
<li>Manage data and analytics platform to support business intelligence and reporting needs across the organisation</li>
</ul>
</div></div>

<p>

<ul>
<li>Experience in leading multi-faceted business transformation and continuous improvement projects.
</li>
<li>Ability to understand existing changing business needs and translate into functional requirements</li>
<li>Ability to champion the customer experience and effectively manage stakeholder expectations.
</li>

</li>
