<h2>Technical Lead, (Product Insights & Experiments (PIX))</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>Redfin is revolutionizing the $75 billion real estate industry. We use data, beautiful software, and innovative design to put customers first at every step in the home-buying and selling process. Get ready to dive headfirst into our award-winning website and mobile apps, solving complex business problems in a highly visible, customer-centric way. If you value doing great work in a collaborative environment, join our team!</div>
<div></div>
<div>
Product Insights and Experiments team builds infrastructure and tools for engineers &amp; product managers at Redfin to instrument and analyze product usage. We have a grander vision that includes sharply increasing the number of experiments Redfin runs, more types of experiments (including merchandising experiments), and building systems that allow us to better understand and gain more insights into our users. We are here to build systems that will have a huge impact on Redfin's growth for years to come.</div>
<div></div>
<div><b>
The Role</b></div>
<ul>
<li><div>
Design and architect large scale data pipelines and infrastructure that could directly affect several Engineering teams and contribute to long term strategy.</div></li>
<li><div>
Through research on current trends and exploration provide reliable, performant and elegant solutions to build scalable and maintainable instrumentation and experimentation platforms.</div></li>
<li><div>
Better those around you by driving team efficiency improvements, mentoring teammates to strengthen individual skills and share your learning for building large-scale applications.</div></li>
<li><div>
Be aware of how your decisions impact the team, other developers, and customers. You must care about these impacts and consider them when choosing between options.</div></li>
</ul>
<div></div>
<div><b>
Basic Qualifications</b></div>
<ul>
<li><div>
You are a software developer with 8+ years of experience developing full-stack applications along with 3+ years in a senior-level role.</div></li>
<li><div>
Well versed in Python, Java, and SQL.</div></li>
<li><div>
Experience working with streaming pipelines (like Kafka/SQS) and cloud services (like AWS).</div></li>
</ul>
<div></div>

<li><div>
