<h2>Senior Manager I, Design</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Position Description</b></p>
<br>
<p>
The Senior Manager establishes and drives strategy for product design and execution of our associate experiences within Walmart's enterprise applications. You will join a leadership team with responsibilities that require creativity, technical expertise and business sense. You thrive in a dynamic environment with demanding business partners. Together, we will find solutions to meaningful design challenges at scale.</p>
<br>
<br>
<p>We need a seasoned design leader with a record of shipping user experiences and user interfaces (UX/UI) for business-to-business (B2B) software.</p>
<br>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
What you'll do</b></h3>
<br>
<ul>
<li>
Recruit and develop top talent by mentoring and managing a team of 6-8 designers</li>
<li>
Establish and communicate effective design processes through a deep understanding of user-centered design techniques coupled with practical experience in how a design team should collaborate cross-functionally for maximum effectiveness delivering solutions</li>
<li>
Lead designers through dynamic requirements with complex business needs, including multiple projects with dependencies on other internal teams</li>
<li>
Apply and further develop your experience in developing new approaches to design based on industry standards, including agile and lean product development methodologies</li>
<li>
Deliver a product roadmap with business partners to drive a creative omnichannel associate experience strategy</li>
<li>
Gain strategic alignment within and across the organization</li>
<li>
Identify the need for analysis of customer behavior changes (for example, spikes in sales revenues, traffic, abandonment, engagement)</li>
<li>
Manage and approve prototypes, specification documentation and style guides</li>
<li>
Provide overall direction for design</li>
</ul>
<br>

Minimum Qualifications</b></p>
<br>
<ul><li>
Bachelor Degree and 4 years' design experience OR Masters Degree and 3 years' design experience OR 6 years' design experience</li></ul>
