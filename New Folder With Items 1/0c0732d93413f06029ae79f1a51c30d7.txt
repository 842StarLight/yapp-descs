<h2>Eyewear Consultant</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Quality of vision has a direct impact on quality of life. Here at VisionPoint, we are proud to be able to help our patients see and live better each day. There is an immediate opportunity to become our partner in helping provide the very best patient experience. We are currently seeking an Eyewear Sales Consultant that can utilize their excellent communication skills, sales experience, and passion for both fashion and function to add value to our growing team.</p>
<p><b>Position Summary</b></p>
<p>The Eyewear Sales Consultant assists our valued patients with the entire process of ordering customized eyewear. This involves the analysis of a variety of prescriptions, appropriate frame styling, and any necessary administrative tasks to ensure the products are delivered as requested in timely manner. The Eyewear Sales Consultant assists in all aspects of patient care, including the fitting and adjusting of eyeglasses under the supervision on an Optician or Optometrist to exceed patients' expectations.</p>
<p><b>ESSENTIAL DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Provide patients with world class customer service</li>
<li>Guide and assist patients in the eyewear and frame/lens selection process by using extensive knowledge of product lines and options to educate patient</li>
<li>Converse with patients to analyze and assess patient’s vision problems and needs</li>
<li>Maximize sales by uncovering patients’ needs and finding solutions through different eyewear usages</li>
<li>Demonstrates a team mentality, positivity, and respect in the work place</li>
<li>Provide patients with comprehensive explanation of their insurance benefits</li>
<li>Understand the company’s cost of goods and retail fee schedules.</li>
<li>Educate patient on the adaptation of new prescriptions, usage, and care of lenses</li>
<li>Under the direct supervision of an optometrist or an optician, use technical aids for proper fit and measurements</li>
<li>Under the direct supervision of an optometrist or an optician, use optical equipment to neutralize, verify and make a final inspection of eyewear</li>
<li>Notify patients of order status changes and updates</li>
<li>Utilize multiple sites and software systems to properly execute patient care</li>
<li>Monitor through reporting any potential order issues to ensure a premium patient experience</li>
<li>Clean and merchandise displays daily</li>
<li>Attend all office meetings</li>
<li>Participate in all training opportunities</li>
<li>Other duties, as assigned, including assisting in other areas of the office</li>
</ul>
<p><b>EXPERIENCE AND QUALIFICATIONS</b></p>
<ul><li>1+ year retail experience</li></ul>
<p><b>KNOWLEDGE, SKILLS AND ABILITIES</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Demonstrated success in a retail, office environment addressing a broad spectrum of customer requirements and preferences.</li>
<li>Demonstrated ability to establish positive patient rapport and build relationships to establish repeat customer-trends.</li>
<li>Strong communication skills (oral and written) with the proven ability to manage priorities in a fast paced, entrepreneurial environment.</li>
<li>Ability to multi-task, set priorities and ensure that patient commitments/deadlines are met</li>
</ul>
