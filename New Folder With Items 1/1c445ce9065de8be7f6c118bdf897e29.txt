<b>Position Description</b><br>
<p><b>Job Summary and Scope:
</b></p>
<p>Veritiv is a Fortune 500, business-to-business packaging company. From start-ups to over 80% of the Fortune 500 companies, Veritiv provides unique, scalable packaging and design-driven solutions. The Design Services group consists of design, engineering, testing and project management professionals, partnering together on a global scale to deliver exceptional customer experiences. The Design Services team seeks out talented individuals that have a passion for design, and are looking to build a great career.</p>
<p></p>
<p>
The Packaging Engineering - Designer I is responsible for executing packaging design-related projects to support the company's strategic business goals. These projects center mostly around packaging development. Positive team engagement and motivation is a primary focus of this role. As a Designer I serving our customers, this person will be expected to bring creativity, innovation and ownership to projects. They will collaborate with their functional partners to work through projects from concept to completion.</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Job Responsibilities</b>:</p>
<ul>
<li>Develop design-driven solutions to solve for business opportunities</li>
<li>Stay on top of trends</li>
<li>Stay current with software updates, industry standards and technology develoments</li>
<li>Reflect marketplace and design trends in work</li>
<li>Work with team members and project managers to prioritize projects and workload</li>
<li>Prepare for large upcoming projects and changes in workload</li>
<li>Provide design-related sales support</li>
<li>Work with external customer teams to ensure project success</li>
<li>Ability to work independently as well as on a team</li>
<li>Present work to internal groups and customers</li>
<li>Develop presentations</li>
<li>Align projects to strategy</li>
<li>Design quality solutions on time and within budget</li>
<li>Meet with customers to help sell design capabilities and custom solutions</li>
<li>Interpret customer feedback and offer appropriate solutions</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
