<h2>Front Desk Agent</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<b>SUMMARY:</b> Responsible for greeting guests, taking reservations, providing guest service and
<br>settling guest accounts upon completion of their stay.
<br><br>
<b>What's in it for you:</b><br>
PTO
<br>Paid Vacation after 1 year of service
<br>401K
<br>Medical/Dental/Vision Insurance options
<br>Short and Long Term Disability options
<br>Employer paid life insurance with additional coverage options
<br>Employee Travel Program
<br><br>
<b>ESSENTIAL DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES:</b><br>
Greets, checks in, and assigns rooms to guests
<br>Promptly and effectively deals with guest requests and complaints
<br>Answers phone calls and routes appropriately, takes accurate messages, makes
<br>reservations and uses upselling techniques as directed by General Manager or sales team
<br>Assists sales and marketing efforts as directed by General Manager
<br>Handles confidential information with high integrity
<br>Maintains accurate cash, accounting, and reservation records
<br>Responsible for all cash and credit card transactions so that drawers balance at the end
<br>of each shift
<br>Answers guest inquiries about area attractions, hotel services, directions or reservations
<br>Conducts wake up calls as requested
<br>Records pertinent guest information and issues in log book for opening managers
<br>review
<br>Keeps communal areas and lobby clean
<br>Assists breakfast hosts in replenishing food or supplies and cleaning as directed
<br>Assists with laundry as directed
<br>Follows company policies and procedures
<br>Other duties as assigned by supervisor or management
<br><br>
<b>QUALIFICATIONS :</b><br>
<b>Education/Experience:</b> High School Diploma or GED equivalent. Minimum of threemonths hotel guest service experience or equivalent training and experience.
<br><br>
<b>Skills:</b><br>
Interpersonal skills
<br>Guest service
<br>Innate sense of urgency
<br>Adaptability
<br>Detail oriented
<br>Proficient communication
<br>Ability to multi-task
<br>Advanced experience with personal computers and related software applications
<br>Ability to read, write, and speak the English language
<br><br>
<b>Working Conditions:</b><br>
Will be required to work nights, weekends, and holidays.
<br>Will be required to work in a fast-paced environment.
<br><br>
<b>RELATIONSHIPS:</b><br>
<b>Internal:</b> General Manager, Assistant General Manager, Peers, Sales Manager, Director of
<br>Sales and Field Operations at the corporate level
<br><br>
<b>External:</b> Guests - To provide customer service
<br><br>
