<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Position Summary
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div>Provides the supervision of contractor performance of approved Contractor Connection contractors in geographic district; serves as a liaison between contractors, client and Contractor Connection; assists with implementing expansion of Contractor Connection programs.</div>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities
</b></h2>
<div><div><div><ul>
<li>Provides overall supervision of contractor performance and work product in district.</li>
<li>
Monitors time frames for handling of assignments by each contractor.</li>
<li>
Provides data review, analysis and monthly reporting of individual contractor and district performance results.</li>
<li>
Performs in-person Quality Review Meetings with contractors to review performance measurements and address any areas of concern.</li>
<li>
Assists with resolution of disputes that arise between the insured, the insurer and the contractor; maintains and improves relationships with all team members.</li>
<li>
Facilitates responses to questions any party may have regarding district assignment activity, processes, procedures, etc.</li>
<li>
Assists with locating contractors for participation in Contractor Connection network.</li>
<li>
Performs physical site visits for new contractor applicants to confirm facility requirements, capabilities, etc.</li>
<li>
Schedules contractor staff training sessions for new and existing contractors where required.</li>
<li>
Assists all internal departments where required.</li>
<li>
Maintains overall knowledge of Contractor Connection business model.</li>
<li>
Upholds and projects the public image of the Company.</li>
<li>
Participates in special projects or performs duties in other areas as requested.
</li>
</ul></div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Requirements
</b></h2>
<div><div><div><ul>
<li>Bachelor degree in business administration, management, construction or a related discipline; or an equivalent combination of education and at least 5 years of progressive management experience.
</li>
<li>Previous experience in the construction industry. Insurance property loss adjusting experience helpful.</li>
<li>
Will have frequent interaction with contractors and clients.</li>
<li>
Familiar with claims process.
</li>
<li>Familiar with residential and commercial construction, restoration means and methods.</li>
<li>
Ability to interpret contract terms and conditions.</li>
<li>
Basic construction estimating skills.</li>
<li>
Ability to inspect completed work and determine conformance with contract requirements and standard business practices.</li>
<li>
Ability to analyze and resolve problems while maintaining a spirit of cooperation and teamwork.</li>
<li>
Highly proficient computer skills, (Windows, Microsoft Word, Excel and working knowledge of property estimate software programs).</li>
<li>
Excellent communication skills (written, verbal and listening) and presentation skills.</li>
<li>
Good organizational and interpersonal skills.</li>
<li>
Demonstrated ability to manage multiple projects or contractors concurrently under minimal supervision.</li>
<li>
Tact and diplomacy.</li>
<li>
Strong relationship/leadership/team building skills.</li>
<li>
Strong time management abilities.
</li>
</ul></div></div></div>
