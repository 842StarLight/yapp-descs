<h2>Systems Analyst III</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<div>Grant Thornton is seeking a Senior Manager to join its Financial Management service line and take an active role in engagement execution, project management and key business development activities.<br>
<br>
Grant Thornton Public Sector helps executives and managers at all levels of government maximize their performance and efficiency in the face of ever tightening budgets and increased demand for services. We give clients creative, cost-effective solutions that enhance their acquisition, financial, human capital, information technology, data analytics, and performance management. For more information, visit grantthornton.com/publicsector.<br>
<br>
At Grant Thornton, our professional staff applies traditional, cutting-edge approaches and methods to a variety of analyses. As part of our team, the Senior Manager will utilize various methodologies and models to execute client projects.<br>
<br>
ESSENTIAL DUTIES:<br>
<ul>
<li>Possesses and applies a comprehensive knowledge across key tasks and high impact assignments.</li>
<li>
Plans and leads major work assignments. Evaluates performance results and recommends major changes affecting short-term project growth and success.</li>
<li>
Functions as a technical expert across multiple project assignments.</li>
<li>
Analyzes science, engineering, business, cybersecurity and other data processing problems to implement and improve information systems and architectures.</li>
<li>
Possesses in depth knowledge of the enterprise, logic, systems, programming, and components, and a broad range of knowledge across the organization business, data, technology, applications and organizational views.</li>
<li>
Provides support in the areas of analysis, design, development, implementation, and maintenance of software, systems, and technologies.</li>
<li>
Analyzes user requirements, procedures, and problems to automate business processes or optimize existing information systems.</li>
<li>
Analyzes, designs, evaluates, and plans business architectures and information technology systems architectures in order to optimize the enterprise information technology architecture.</li>
<li>
Provides technical assistance to organization on existing and new technology in all areas of information technology such as platform, operations, network, applications and security.</li>
<li>
Supports customers in the development, implementation, and maintenance of strategy, doctrine, standards, policies, and procedures.</li>
<li>
Supervise, train, and mentor staff throughout the engagement lifecycle and assess performance of staff for engagement reviews.</li>
<li>
Assist partners and senior management on proposals and business development calls.</li>
<li>
Gain comprehensive understanding of client operations, processes, and business objectives and utilize that knowledge on engagements.</li>
<li>
Perform engagement management responsibilities, including performance reviews, task delegation, project scheduling, project financials, quality review and client management.</li>
<li>
Meet or exceed sales targets for new and follow-on work.</li>
<li>
Manage business development activities, such as proposals, capture, account teams, whitepapers, conferences, and/or other thought leadership materials.</li>
<li>
Meet or exceed targeted billing hours (utilization).</li>
<li>
Act as performance manager (coach) for up to four coachees outside of engagement responsibilities.</li>
<li>
Interview campus and/or experienced candidates.</li>
</ul>
