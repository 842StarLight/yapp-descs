Investment Services marketing- Position is focused on establishing dashboard for marketing team metrics; analyzing marketing/sales campaign performance from idea inception through execution, CRM/Salesforce liaison with Business line and ERA. This position will be required to gather data from a variety of sources, aggregate it into meaningful results and provide trend observations and recommendations to the Investment Services marketing &amp; leadership team.</div>
<div>
<br>
Core position responsibilities:</div>
<ul>
<li>
Liaison with business line sales support, enterprise data analytics team, digital marketing and technology and other key partners on CRM/Salesforce</li>
<li>
 Analyze marketing and sales campaign performance, provide statistical reports and suggest improvements as needed
</li>
<li>Support campaign management (MAP) from idea inception through execution and analysis, with focus on digital KPI performance and optimization</li>
<li>
Establish dashboard and best practices for metrics reporting to disseminate to internal departments and executive leadership around marketing and sales metrics for Investment Services
</li>
<li>Conduct closed-loop reporting/ROI reporting to tie marketing’s efforts to sales revenue</li>
<li>
Querying data, analyzing results, developing impactful reports and discussing business implications with the Investment Services marketing leadership team</li>
<li>
Leverage data to tell a business story</li>
<li>
Proactively conduct research to understand our customers’ journeys and stay on top of trends and developments in digital marketing<br>
</li>
</ul>
