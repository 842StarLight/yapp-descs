<p><b>
About the Job</b></p>
<p>
We are currently seeking a full time Digital Marketing Specialist to strategize and implement our digital advertising.
</p>
<p>The candidate will be responsible for the strategy and implementation of our digital advertising. This includes all ads as well as paid social, Google AdWords, content syndication, and banner ads for industry sites. The key to this will be implementing testing strategies and optimizing lead flow. She/He will work closely with the marketing team on demand generation activities which drives the creation of pipeline.</p>
<p><b>
Essential Duties &amp; Responsibilities
</b></p>
<p><b>Primary Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Define and execute advertising strategy across all relevant platforms.</li>
<li>
Measure and optimize performance across platforms.</li>
<li>
Work with team on developing ABM programs.</li>
<li>
Report metrics on performance on a regular basis.</li>
<li>
Define and execute testing plans to continually optimize results.</li>
<li>
Identify new platforms to test and advertise on.</li>
<li>
Assist in optimizing ad copy and format for each platform.</li>
<li>
Work with design team to optimize visual assets for ads.</li>
<li>
Track expenses and budget in an organized fashion</li>
</ul>
