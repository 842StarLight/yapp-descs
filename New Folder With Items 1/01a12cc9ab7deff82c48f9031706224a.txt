<div><b>Job Description:
</b></div>
<div>We seek Business and Systems Integration professionals who possess thorough experience in Project Management specifically using SAP Ariba.</div>
<div>As an SAP Ariba Project Manager your job duties will involve;</div>
<ul>
<li>Ability to develop project plans for large implementations and lead cross-functional teams to deliver successfully to plan.
</li>
<li>Participate in client requirements gathering, solution definition and proposal presentation meetings with sales pursuit team.
</li>
<li>Define, develop, and actively manage implementation schedules and project budgets.
</li>
<li>Manage project scope and utilize appropriate change control mechanisms from inception to closure.
</li>
<li>Assemble capable personnel to form the project team, prepare team for project kickoff, develop a collaborative culture and provide coaching to optimize performance.
</li>
<li>Clearly communicate realistic project delivery commitments and consistently deliver against those commitments.
</li>
<li>Incorporate an appropriate level of quality audits and testing into every project.
</li>
<li>Customer Satisfaction - Develop a clear definition of project success from the client's perspective.
</li>
<li>Strive to achieve a referenceable result from each project. Sales Support - Support late stage sales opportunities to close new business that meets client needs in a manner that is achievable and profitable.</li>
</ul>
</div>

<div><b>Basic Qualifications</b></div>
<div></div>
<ul>
<li>Minimum 10 years of systems integration project management experience on enterprise-level implementations.
</li>
<li>Minimum 3 years of SAP Ariba based project management</li>
<li>
Minimum 3 years of functional knowledge of source to pay, procurement, and catalog management software package being deployed (SAP Ariba).
</li>
<li>Minimum 3 years of experience using project management tools for maintaining scope, schedule and budget. Quality Management
</li>
<li>Bachelor's degree or equivalent work experience (minimum 12 years or 6 years plus an Associate’s Degree)</li>
<li>
PMP Certification</li>
</ul>
