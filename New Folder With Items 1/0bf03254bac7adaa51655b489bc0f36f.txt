<h2>Marketing Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p><b>What you’ll be doing...</b>
</p>
<p>You’ll help us understand markets and how people use our products and services. You will maintain our leadership position by anticipating consumer needs and shaping our future offerings to meet them. Your vision and leadership will help us realize what’s next for Verizon.</p>
<ul>
<li>
Determining what people really want.</li>
<li>
Testing out new ideas.</li>
<li>
Figuring out the upside and downside of the next thing.</li>
<li>
Telling us what the market says.</li>
<li>
Creating the story for our customers.</li>
<li>
Helping sellers tell the story of why this and why Verizon.</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li><p>
Managing a full range of projects for B2B content marketing, by reviewing marketing briefs and project plans—and determining which creative teams will be assigned the work to execute.
</p></li>
<li><p>Work with other Marketing Managers across channels to track and manage project timelines, deliverables and fulfillment.
</p></li>
<li><p>Manage and communicate feedback to our internal creative resources and our advertising Agencies.
</p></li>
<li><p>Responsible for ensuring all work is on brief and follows required legal reviews, product reviews and approvals.
</p></li>
<li><p>Identify and troubleshoot project challenges and come with possible solutions.
</p></li>
<li><p>Provide creative presentation with PowerPoint support for stakeholders and leadership.</p></li>
<li><p>
Manage and organize work—maintain timelines and alignment with Integrated marketing.</p></li>
<li><p>
Stay up-to-date on the latest tech trends and our competition.
</p></li>
<li><p>Identify areas of improvement in process and project management.
</p></li>
</ul>
<p><b>What we’re looking for...</b>
</p>
<p>You are curious about what people need and want. You enjoy digging through data for insight into opportunities for how we can help our consumers work, play and connect in new and better ways.</p>
<p>
You’ll need to have:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Bachelor’s degree in Marketing, Market Research, or four or more years of work experience.</li>
<li>
Six or more years of relevant work experience.</li>
<li>
Experience working with marketing research, research strategy, or consumer research methodologies.</li>
</ul>
<p>
Even better if you have:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Master’s degree – more impressive if you have an MBA.</li>
<li>
Digital experience and an understanding of trends and key business drivers.</li>
<li>
Presented findings and insights targeted to what that audience cares about (up to the executive level).</li>
<li>
Analyzed what’s important from a variety of sources to provide a holistic view of the marketplace, competitive factors, risks, and opportunities.</li>
<li>
Juggled multiple priorities to meet deadlines in a dynamic environment.</li>
<li>
Designed custom research approaches to gain broader or deeper consumer and market insights.</li>
<li>
Translated market knowledge and strategy into compelling stories and tools for the sales team.</li>
</ul>
<p>
In Addition:</p>
<ul><li>
Travel - up to 25%</li></ul>
<p><b>
When you join Verizon...</b>
</p>
<p>You’ll have the power to go beyond – doing the work that’s transforming how people, businesses and things connect with each other. Not only do we provide the fastest and most reliable network for our customers, but we were first to 5G - a quantum leap in connectivity. Our connected solutions are making communities stronger and enabling energy efficiency. Here, you’ll have the ability to make an impact and create positive change. Whether you think in code, words, pictures or numbers, join our team of the best and brightest. We offer great pay, amazing benefits and opportunity to learn and grow in every role. Together we’ll go far.</p>
