<h2>Customer Service Representative - 19199VJ</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Position – Customer Service Representative<br>Position Type – Hourly<br>Location – Norwood, MA<br>Shift – 1st/2nd shift + 1 weekend day (8-4)<br>Duration – Full-time</p>
<p><b>Job Summary</b></p>
<p>Ideal candidate will perform customer service duties supporting the Massachusetts Bay Transit Authority call center project, including but not limited to addressing consumer inquiries (inbound consumer calls and emails) regarding MBTA schedules, fares, payment methods, disability access &amp; route planning. The candidate should be able to update information into the MBTA’s CRM application accurately, while following the SOP and displaying excellent decision making skills regarding escalation of consumer complaints.</p>
<p><b>Job Duties &amp; Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Receives, evaluates and answers incoming customer calls and digital communications in a timely and professional manner</li>
<li>Able to utilize client website to relay information regarding schedules, routes and delays</li>
<li>Accurately record key call and email details into the CRM system</li>
<li>Applies a basic working knowledge of systems, procedures, customers and products to perform assigned functions</li>
<li>Participates in initial and ongoing training to improve knowledge of product, processes and procedures</li>
<li>Display’s excellent communication and active listening skills</li>
<li>Displays excellent written communication skills including the ability to correctly spell consumer names and addresses</li>
<li>Follows standard procedures as set by department</li>
<li>Understand multiple internal areas of business (Sales, Operations, etc) to research, analyze, and resolve potential issues</li>
<li>Able to identify, properly document and resolve or escalate consumer complaints as per client guidelines</li>
<li>Ensures that data transmitted to client particularly for complaints fully and accurately describes the issue to be resolved</li>
<li>Follows all safety and security procedures and notifies supervisor of potential hazards promptly</li>
<li>Performs other duties as the business requires</li>
<li>Able to work assigned schedule between the hours of 6:30 am and 8:00 pm Monday through Sunday</li>
<li>Availability must include some mandatory weekends and holidays</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Experience</b></p>
<ul>
<li>H.S. Diploma or equivalent</li>
<li>Minimum 1 year of progressively responsible Customer Service experience in a call center environment</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Requirements</b></p>
<ul>
<li>H.S. Diploma or equivalent</li>
<li>Minimum 1 year of progressively responsible Customer Service experience in a call center environment</li>
<li>Excellent customer service and interpersonal skills</li>
<li>Unwavering professionalism and executive presence</li>
<li>Ability to multi-task, talk and type at the same time, with above average typing speed</li>
<li>Verifiable, stable work history</li>
<li>Familiarity with MBTA ‘s transit system preferred</li>
</ul>
