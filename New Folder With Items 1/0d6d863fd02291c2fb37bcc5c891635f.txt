<h2>Enterprise Account Executive</h2>

<p><b>Logiwa</b> is a Chicago-based B2B SaaS company with R&amp;D operations overseas. We provide cloud-based warehouse and inventory management (WMS) software for small-to-medium sized “New Age” B2C fulfillment businesses. As we are growing rapidly with double digit month/month growth, we are looking for a highly motivated <b>Enterprise Account Executive.</b></p>
<p>
We are backed by leading North American and European funds including Runway Ventures (NYC),</p>
<p>
Launch Capital(CT), Spider Capital (SF), Otimo Retail (Montreal), Revo Capital (Netherlands).</p>
<p><b>
Job brief</b></p>
<p>
We are looking for a data driven, motivated and ambitious, <b>Enterprise Account Executive</b> to join our team. If you love developing new business opportunities, this job is for you! You’ll be directly responsible for the rapid expansion of our customer base.</p>
<p><b>
Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul><li>Run SaaS Product Demos in order to close new clients</li></ul>
<ul><li>Exceed all sales goals and expectations</li></ul>
<ul><li>Manage the sales cycle from Demo to Close</li></ul>
<ul><li>Assist in finding prospects and leads</li></ul>
<ul><li>Identify new sales opportunities</li></ul>
<ul><li>Build strong client relationships</li></ul>
<ul><li>Identify opportunities to up-sell and cross-sell</li></ul>
<ul><li>Provide customer support when needed</li></ul>
<ul><li>Provide professional after-sales support when needed</li></ul>
<ul><li>Become an expert at the utilization of our CRM (Hubspot)</li></ul>
<ul><li>Work in conjunction with our Sales Development, Marketing and Technical teams to provide the best customer experience</li></ul>
<p><b>
Requirements</b></p>
<ul><li>5+ years of experience as an Account Executive</li></ul>
<ul><li>BS or BA in business administration, sales or marketing</li></ul>
<ul><li>Knowledge of market research, sales and negotiating principles (eg. SPIN Selling)</li></ul>
<ul><li>Outstanding knowledge of MS Office; knowledge of CRM software (eg. Salesforce) is a plus</li></ul>
<ul><li>Excellent communication/presentation skills and ability to build relationships</li></ul>
<ul><li>Experience in the SaaS space is preferred</li></ul>
<ul><li>This position is an Inside Sales role based in our Downtown Chicago office</li></ul>
