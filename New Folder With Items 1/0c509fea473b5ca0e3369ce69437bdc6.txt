<div>
<p>If you have the character or experience of a Top producing sales professional – we may want to talk with you. We are looking for someone with the drive and desire to be the BEST at what they do, and love doing it with passion.</p>
<p>
Whether you’re young or old, we’ll train you on our system for success with our company.</p>
<p><b>
Criteria Needed from You:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Highly motivated, self-disciplined and driven</li>
<li>Some prior sales experience or training in sales</li>
<li>Some prior construction experience or basic construction knowledge</li>
<li>Good grammar, spelling and legible handwriting in order to compose written estimates in the field and computer estimate in the office</li>
<li>Must have knowledge of computers and programs such as Microsoft Excel, Word, etc. Our proposals are generated with Microsoft Excel and imported to customer database software called ACT.</li>
<li>Valid Washington State Driver’s License, must be insurable and must be able to provide driving record for past 3 years</li>
<li>Able to work varying hours from 8 am – 7 pm. If you can’t be flexible in hours to meet our customers’ needs please do not apply.</li>
<li>Able to work varying days from Sun – Sat. If you can’t work weekends to meet our customers’ needs please do not apply.</li>
</ul>

Max Hours: 60</p>

<p>

<p><b>
About Our Company:</b></p>
<p>
Robbins and Company is the LEADER in foundation repair and drainage for Washington State, and you will be trained on our state-of-the-art specialized foundation and drainage products. Our website can be found at www.robbinsfoundationsystems.com and you can search for us on Facebook, Twitter and YouTube. We have been awarded the #1 Foundation Repair Dealer for our region since 2007. We have also been once awarded the #1 National Dealer in total sales.</p>
<p>
We have achieved this goal by consistently providing strong customer service, honesty, and quality to our customers. After 63 years in business in the Seattle Area, we pride ourselves in honesty and integrity and the quality of our products. We also have the best staff in the industry behind the products we offer and install.</p>
<p>
We are expanding our sales staff in Seattle and surrounding areas, so if you think you have similar ethics as our team – let’s talk.</p>
