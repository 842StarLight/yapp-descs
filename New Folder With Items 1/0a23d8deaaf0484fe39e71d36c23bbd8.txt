<h2>Marketing and Inside Sales Coordinator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Position Summary:
</p>
<p>The Marketing &amp; Inside Sales Coordinator is responsible for all marketing activities and generating new business-to-business sales by conducting market research, identifying target accounts and people, prospecting, qualifying leads (phone calls, email, trade shows, website, social media), and securing new business opportunities.
</p>
<p>This person will be engaged in planning and implementing sales and marketing programs targeted at existing and new markets/customers. S/he will develop and implement content across print and online media, manage email marketing campaigns, oversee e-commerce and social media activity, generate and follow-up on sales leads. The Marketing &amp; Inside Sales Coordinator will work closely with Sales Management and Customer Service.
</p>
<p>Essential Functions and Responsibilities:
</p>
<ul>
<li>Develop, implement, and manage communication touch plans (e.g., phone calls, emails, social media posts) for existing customers and targeted prospects.</li>
<li>Partner with sales to create digital content and social media roadmap and engagement strategy that drives brand awareness and grows the business. Develop an activity calendar for each social media platform. Track and report social media analytics.</li>
<li>Create, design, and produce succinct and consistent content for e-commerce, brochures, website, email campaigns, newsletters, presentations, video scripts, webinar scripts, landing pages, and paid ads designed for lead and demand generation purposes, and other sales support materials.</li>
<li>Coordinate public relations and customer relations efforts, including but not limited to, writing and editing press releases, success stories, and e-newsletters.</li>
<li>Determine content and collateral needs for new product or service launches; contribute to project plans to align external collateral due dates with launch.</li>
<li>Monitor competition. Conduct research and review websites for sales and marketing best practices.</li>
<li>Monitor, measure, and report on the effectiveness of marketing initiatives. Take corrective action as required to ensure goals and budgets are met.</li>
<li>Monitor, measure, and implement effective search engine optimization (SEO) and Pay Per Click (PPC) strategies to drive sales opportunities to company website.</li>
<li>Network with trade publications and industry associations to gain access to new opportunities.</li>
<li>Support sales management team by developing presentation materials, creating and issuing monthly sales metrics reports, maintaining sales pipeline spreadsheets, and updating account development plans.</li>
<li>Establish and maintain relationships with advertising vendors.
</li>
<li>Make targeted calls and lead email campaigns to identified target customers.
</li>
<li>Identify, research, and implement methods to increase customer engagement.
</li>
<li>Establish and maintain a consistent corporate image throughout all product lines, promotional materials, and events. Liaise with sales and marketing leadership to raise brand awareness and consistency.</li>
<li>Provide support as required for trade shows.</li>
</ul>
<p>Qualifications Required:</p>
<ul>
<li>Associate s or Bachelor s degree in marketing, communications, public relations, journalism, social media strategy, business or related field.</li>
<li>Minimum of 3 years of marketing, inside sales, business development, or social media related experience in a business-to-business environment.</li>
<li>Highly proficient using Adobe Suite and Microsoft Office Suite – Word, Excel, PowerPoint.</li>
<li>Experience with email marketing software and best practices, social media reporting platforms, and Google Analytics.</li>
<li>Outstanding verbal and written communication skills. Passion for and ability to proofread and make appropriate corrections and recommendations.</li>
<li>Experience and enthusiasm for making phone calls; cold calls and warm calls.</li>
<li>Ability to gather, analyze, and accurately interpret detailed information.</li>
<li>Highly organized, detail-oriented, and efficient.</li>
<li>Demonstrate the ability to actively listen, communicate, present, influence, and motivate.</li>
<li>Strong math and problem-solving skills.</li>
</ul>
<p>Qualifications Desired:
</p>
<ul>
<li>Prior experience with label industry software (LabelTraxx).</li>
</ul>
<p>Success Factors/Job Competencies:
</p>
<ul>
<li>Friendly, positive, personality with the ability to interpret customers needs.
</li>
<li>High integrity and reliability.
</li>
<li>High energy level, drive, and enthusiasm.</li>
<li>Action oriented, results driven, resilient. Comfortable in a metrics driven/accountable environment.</li>
<li>Ability to work well in a team environment.
</li>
<li>Ability to manage and prioritize multiple projects, deadlines, and budgets.</li>
<li>Open-minded personality with motivation to learn and apply learned concepts.</li>
<li>Tenacity – the willingness to do what needs to be done.</li>
<li>Commitment to company values, goals, and objectives.
</li>
</ul>
