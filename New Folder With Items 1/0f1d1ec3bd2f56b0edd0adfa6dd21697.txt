<h2>Sr Sub PMM – M365 Enterprise Commercial</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>We are looking for a strong strategic marketer to drive the Microsoft 365 business for the Enterprise Commercial (EC) segment in the US. If you are passionate about Microsoft’s cloud services business, with strong sales <i>and</i> technical aptitude, analytic skills, and the desire to shape the strategy of a highly visible growth engine for Microsoft…then please read on.</div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Responsibilities</b></h2>
<div>
The Microsoft 365 team in the US M&amp;O is responsible for the business strategy and integrated marketing plans for the Microsoft 365 business. We are looking for an experienced and driven marketing professional to grow Microsoft 365 revenue in the US by successfully driving sales of Microsoft 365 with a focus on Security, Compliance and Teams. This is a highly visible role that is expected to own the M365 go-to-market in the EC accounts in the US and to track and drive insights around the business outcomes. She/He will develop and own recommendations and strategy adjustments to ensure successful outcomes for this business and will represent the business to senior leaders.</div>
<div>
Primary responsibilities include:</div>
<ul><li>
Marketing</li></ul>
<ul>
<li>
Plan &amp; orchestrate US Microsoft 365 EC Marketing strategy &amp; execution synthesizing WW GTM with local channels, market moments, and customer insights.</li>
<li>
Plan and execute Field and Partner readiness.</li>
<li>
Drive seller awareness &amp; usage of marketing campaigns, assets, and the Business Value Program to land strong sales play execution</li>
<li>
Partner with teams across the company to drive Microsoft 365 at key industry moments</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>
Identify solution lighthouse win candidates &amp; secure/develop customer evidence</li>
<li>
Act as the voice of the field by ensuring timely updates/feedback on product marketing and campaign execution.</li>
</ul>
<ul><li>
Business management</li></ul>
<ul>
<li>
Manage the Business Priorities and drive an all-green Microsoft 365 Scorecard</li>
<li>
Manage monthly &amp; quarterly business reporting, review, and correction of errors ROB</li>
</ul>
<ul><li>
Interpret revenue &amp; scorecard results + field learnings and insights.</li></ul>
<ul><li>
Microsoft 365 Evangelism</li></ul>
<ul><li>
Maintain product knowledge and evangelize the product internally and externally to enthusiasts, influential and pre-sales customer</li></ul>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Qualifications</b></h2>
<ul>
<li>
7+ years of experience in Enterprise marketing required with a proven track record of delivering tangible business results. MBA Preferred.</li>
<li>
A passion for technology <i>and</i> sales with the ability to clearly articulate solutions in a business and customer-centric manner; knowledge of Office or EMS value proposition and business preferred.</li>
<li>
Strong analytical skills to analyze business performance, identify market opportunity and develop useful insights from data.</li>
<li>
Ability to view a business holistically end-to-end across Marketing and Sales.</li>
<li>
Proven ability to influence without authority, collaborate effectively, and lead virtual teams</li>
<li>
Exceptionally strong communication &amp; interpersonal skills; experience presenting to senior leaders.</li>
<li>
Ability to handle and thrive in ambiguous business situations.</li>
<li>
This role is based in Bellevue WA area and will require some domestic travel</li>
</ul>
