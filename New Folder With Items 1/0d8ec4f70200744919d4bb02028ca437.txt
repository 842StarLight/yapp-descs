<h2>Dispatcher</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">Waste Connections is looking for a Dispatcher to join our GROWING team in Denver, Colorado.
<br>This person will be responsible for monitoring work flow dispatching our drivers and being the heartbeat of the operation.
<br><br>
Why you need to join us!
<br><b>CULTURE:</b> It’s a Great place to work! We work in an environment where empowered, self-directed All-stars know what they do is important.
<br><b>INTEGRITY:</b> Our definition is “saying what you will do and then doing it!” We keep our promises to our customers and our employees.
<br><br>
<b>DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Work in conjunction with other departments to resolve customer disputes &amp; demonstrate the ability to become proficient in the use of proprietary operational systems.</li>
<li>Analyzing and resolving customer inquiries for missed pick-ups, misplaced containers and property damage.</li>
<li>Assisting driver(s) when a pick up or delivery is in question before servicing.</li>
<li>Scheduling and dispatching work to various employees and departments throughout the day.</li>
<li>Demonstrate effective use of software applications, at a minimum MS Word and Excel proficient.</li>
<li>Delivers services to customers in a manner that promotes goodwill. Interacts with customers daily.</li>
<li>Work in a fast paced, cooperative, high call volume environment. Solicit sale of new or additional services.</li>
</ul>
<b>MINIMUM JOB REQUIREMENTS:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Must be detailed oriented and well organized.</li>
<li>Ability to read, write, comprehend and communicate in English.</li>
<li>2 years of experience in dispatching or logistics preferred.</li>
<li>High School Diploma or GED required.</li>
</ul>
