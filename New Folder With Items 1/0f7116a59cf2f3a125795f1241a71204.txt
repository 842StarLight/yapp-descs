<div><b>Description of Work Unit</b></div>
<p>CSU Online, Division of Continuing Education, is a self-supporting unit that develops and delivers quality programs and educational opportunities in support of the University’s land grant mission. CSU Online provides affordable access to a quality education from wherever students call home around the nation and the world, on a schedule that fits their lives. CSU Online offers 39 degrees, online and at a distance, as well as credit and noncredit courses, certificate programs, and digital badges.</p>
</div>
<br>
<div>
<div><b>Position Summary</b></div>
<p>The Head of Digital Marketing and Analytics leads CSU Online’s digital marketing efforts, as well as the implementation, testing, and reporting structure for web and marketing campaign analytics. By turning customer actions into data, data into insights, and insights into key business decisions, this role provides work direction across the marketing unit, playing a key part in developing and marketing Colorado State University’s online and distance programs to prospective students across the state, nation, and world. This role leads a team responsible for using digital marketing initiatives and data analysis to grow CSU Online product revenue in accordance with Division goals, which directly contributes to CSU Online’s ability to measure and improve marketing strategies and initiatives to meet and exceed enrollment goals and strategic priorities.<br>
This position reports to the Director of Marketing. Location Fort Collins, CO</p>
</div>
<br>
<div>

<li>Strong leadership skills, with demonstrated ability to lead direct reports and cross-functional teams toward meeting project deadlines and objectives</li>
