<h2>Marketing Writer/Project Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>GENERAL RESPONSIBILITIES</b></p>
<p>The Marketing Writer/Project Manager works closely with the Marketing Director on all internal and external marketing and communication efforts for:</p>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Consensus Health: New Jersey’s fastest-growing, independent multi-specialty medical group and management services organization</li>
<li>Partners In Care (PIC): New Jersey’s oldest clinically integrated network and a Consensus Health affiliate</li>
<li>Captify Health: A leader in specialty care support services, particularly in the GI space</li>
</ul>
<p>The Marketing Writer/Project Manager works under the direction of the Marketing Director on a variety of initiatives; market research and budgeting, internal and external communications, advertising, public relations, website management, SEO and SEM, email marketing, social media management, practice/physician marketing, and event planning. The Marketing Writer/Project Manager also works with the Marketing Director to support internal customers; C-Suite, sales, human resources, population health management, operations and other functional areas across the organization. This individual is a hands-on team player who collaborates with other department members to “go the extra mile and get the job done.”</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>QUALIFICATIONS</b></p>
<p>Education/Training: Bachelor’s degree in Communications, Marketing or related field.</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>SKILLS</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Strong emotional quotient and outstanding people skills</li>
<li>Self-motivated and goal-oriented yet willing to take direction as required</li>
<li>Flexible and entrepreneurial; willing to adapt to rapidly changing environment</li>
<li>Strong project management skills, ability to organize and manage multiple projects at one time, as well as work with outside vendors/freelancers as needed.</li>
<li>Collaborative team player with a desire to serve providers, patients and colleagues</li>
<li>Excellent written and oral skills including the ability to develop content in a variety of styles on short notice. Writing/editing may include ad copy, fliers, press releases, blogs, staff communications, job aids, and more.</li>
<li>Knowledge of communication strategy and tactics with demonstrated experience in integrated marketing programs.
</li>
<li>Savvy technological skills including mastery of Word, PowerPoint, Visio, SharePoint, Excel and Outlook. Knowledge of Adobe Creative Suite, Wordpress and Hootsuite extremely helpful.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>EXPERIENCE</b></p>
<p>Minimum of 5-7 years of experience in Marketing &amp; Communications role, with at least 3 years in the healthcare industry. Knowledge of medical group management and population health is idea.</p>
<p></p>
<p>Writing samples required.</p>
