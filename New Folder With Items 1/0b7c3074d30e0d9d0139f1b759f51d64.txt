<h2>WeConnect Enterprise Technician</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>We have a team of highly trained, highly professional technicians with one common goal: to provide our customers with the best customer service experience. We strive to stand apart from our competition. Our sales staff, management &amp; technicians are professionally trained, certified, &amp; knowledgeable. Customer satisfaction is our number one priority. We have locations all over the United States. We are looking for people just like YOU! The Hughesnet Installation Technician is responsible for providing excellent customer service through installing, troubleshooting, maintaining, and upgrading satellite internet systems and equipment. WE WILL TRAIN</b></p>
<p><b>Summary: </b> In this position, our employees will do Installs, tests, and repairs satellite systems and related equipment by performing the following duties.</p>
<p><b>Essential Duties and Responsibilities: </b> Not limited to the following and may include other duties as assigned.</p>
<ul>
<li>Reviews installation specifications, business and/or property permits, manufacturer's instructions, and government ordinances to determine the installation site for the dish.</li>
<li>Visually inspects the installation site to identify obstructions, such as trees or buildings and other items that could distort or block signals from the satellite.</li>
<li>Discusses satellite dish placement and installation requirements with the customer.</li>
<li>Digs hole for the pole to support the satellite dish base, mixes, pours and finishes concrete in the hole for the pole mount.</li>
<li>Digs a trench and lays underground cable to connect a satellite dish to receivers in the customer's building.</li>
<li>Assembles and attaches electronic and structural components of the satellite dish.</li>
<li>Climbs ladder to install satellite dish when the area is inaccessible from the ground like the roof, siding.</li>
<li>Works in limited space areas like attic, basement or crawl spaces.</li>
<li>Runs wiring like coaxial cable, phone line inside the interior wall or the exterior of the building.</li>
<li>Orients satellite dish to direction and altitude of the satellite signal.</li>
<li>Connects satellite system to customer's television and other electronic audio/visual equipment.</li>
<li>Tests installed the system for conformance to specifications and perform upgrades as needed to conform to standards.</li>
<li>Instructs customer in use of equipment.</li>
<li>Replaces or repairs defective parts and equipment.</li>
<li>Places outbound calls to the customers to pre-call, confirm appointments and verify specifications of the work order, communicates with call center employees over the phone to modify, reschedule, close, cancel work orders for installation.</li>
<li>Installation and connection of phone lines or Ethernet connections on all installations.</li>
</ul>
