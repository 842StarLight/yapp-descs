POSITION SUMMARY: Director, Digital Strategy<br>
<br>
The Director, Digital Strategy will help build the most engaging and connective spaces for patients to manage their health.<br>
<br>
The Digitas Health Digital Strategy practice believes that patients are built not born and seek experiences that help them understand their lives post-diagnosis. Through a data-first approach, we develop a digital experience that helps equip patients with the information and education they need to select the right therapy for their needs.<br>
<br>
We’re looking for a Director, Digital Strategy to lead the next evolution of a content marketing practice who:<br>
<ul>
<li>Understands how to build a story and how to amplify it through social channels.
</li>
<li>Works closely with creatives and media partners to help build long term engagement inside a patient community.</li>
<li>Creates clarity for our creative and clients in how to deliver the compelling content and conversation people need, even in the most complex environments.</li>
</ul>
Is a deep social and search channel expert from a communication and media perspective.<br>
Experience managing multiple communities and community managers.<br>
Understands how channels work in a system and how patients move through those channels.<br>
<br>
Detailed Description<br>
We’re seeking a Director, Digital Strategy in our Philadelphia HQ with experience being a high-level advocate and partner to our internal and client teams. This position requires a depth of ability to understand digital and social data to derive insights that help define social and content strategy. Ideal candidate would seamlessly partner with media and client business insight teams to help drive business across a brand’s digital eco-system.<br>
<br>
The Director will be a visionary and able to interpret the overall digital vision into social experiences and will become an expert in client policies and procedures to help in launching of new channels and initiatives.<br>
Director will be able to work with a high degree of autonomy to search in- and outside our tools, develop new methodologies and be seen as a leader. You will bring your perspective and expertise to bear in integrating and leading collaborative work sessions and deliverables. You have a track record of making the projects you touch more dynamic and better off than when you found them.<br>
<br>
Your mix of critical and flexible thinking has developed a history of writing new rules for the ways companies develop innovative, large-scale platforms that haven’t been built before. You have a deep understanding of social platforms and can see beyond the platform pitches and ad products to develop strategies and tactical plans that provide agency to our clients. Then, you can lead our media and regulatory partners to understand how they need to adapt, in detail, to platform changes so brands can furnish compelling content worth sharing.<br>
