<h2>19-20 Office Manager (San Jose Schools)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div></div>
<div>
<div>
<div>Rocketship is hiring for the position of Office Manager (OM) for a Rocketship school. The Office Manager is the first point of contact for parents and students when they come to school, as well as a resource to the school community. The Office Manager should always exhibit professionalism, treat people with respect, and be firm but compassionate in how they address the day-to-day challenges of a rigorous and purposeful school.</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div><b>
Responsibilities Include:</b></div>
</div>
<div><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Essential Functions: Enrollment, Student Information, and Attendance
</b></h3>
<div><ul>
<li>Build strong working relationships with parents and families</li>
<li>
Create and maintain complete and current student cumulative files</li>
<li>
Enter student information into our student information system and keep this information up-to-date throughout the school year as enrollment or information changes</li>
<li>
Manage the Attendance process including contacting absent parents on a daily basis</li>
<li>
Coordinate the school’s truancy process and communicate with parents as needed</li>
<li>
Administer Independent Study process for students with planned absences</li>
<li>
Collect doctor’s notes and excused absence notes from parents for absentee documentation</li>
<li>
Work with current parents to solve problems and prevent student attrition</li>
</ul></div>
</div></div>
<div><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Essential Functions: Health, Safety and Discipline
</b></h3>
<div><ul>
<li>Maintain emergency medical and contact information for all students</li>
<li>
Maintain complete and current immunization records for all students</li>
<li>
Administer basic first aid, distribute medication, maintain injury reports</li>
<li>
Be knowledgeable of all content in school safety binder, including emergency procedures</li>
<li>
Report all injuries and other school incidents to Human Resources</li>
<li>
Supervise students sent to the office for discipline and health reasons</li>
<div>
<br>
</div>
</ul></div>
</div></div>
<div><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Essential Functions: Meals, Finance and Facilities</b></h3>
<div><ul>
<li>
Collect payments from parents</li>
<li>
Manage collection of free/reduced-lunch application forms from all families during the school year</li>
<li>
Collect lunch, uniform sales money from parents and provide receipts for payments</li>
<li>
Assist in managing and reporting day-to-day facilities problems</li>
<li>
Execute responsibilities related to emergency situations as assigned by the Principal</li>
<div>
<br>
</div>
</ul></div>
</div></div>
<div><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Essential Functions: ISE/Special Education
</b></h3>
<div><ul>
<li>Act as intermediary to communicate written or oral information given by parents to the ISE team</li>
<li>
Ensure that all documents delivered or messages received are immediately given to ISE</li>
<li>
Maintain confidentiality regarding ISE students who receive special education services</li>
<li>
Partner with ISE Specialists on a school site to arrange IEP or ISE meetings</li>
<div>
<br>
</div>
</ul></div>
</div></div>
<div><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Essential Functions: Administrative
</b></h3>
<div><ul>
<li>Perform general clerical duties including answering phones, taking and distributing messages, sorting mail, translate documents from English to Spanish and prepare general correspondence</li>
<li>
Facilitate arrangements for school activities, logistics, teacher development, parent development and other activities</li>
<li>
Provide support to the Principal and teachers as necessary</li>
<li>
Use automated telephone system to send out school information as necessary</li>
<li>
Act as an interpreter, when necessary, in whole-school functions and smaller meetings</li>
</ul></div>
</div></div>
<div><div>
<h3 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Qualifications</b></h3>
<div><ul>
<li>
Spanish/English bilingual fluency required, with excellent written and oral communication skills</li>
<li>
Knowledge and cultural competence of the local student and parent community</li>
<li>
Highly developed interpersonal skills using tact, patience, and courtesy</li>
<li>
Effectively prioritize and juggle multiple concurrent projects</li>
<li>
Comfort in a start-up environment and ability to work independently with minimal direction</li>
<li>
Ability to use a variety of software programs and quickly learn new ones</li>
<li>
Managerial skills preferred</li>
</ul></div>
</div></div>
<div>
<div><b>Physical Demands:</b></div>
<div>
The physical demands described here are representative of those that must be met by an employee to successfully perform the essential functions of this position. Reasonable accommodations may be made to enable individuals with disabilities to perform the functions. While performing the duties of this position, the employee is regularly required to talk, hear, and communicate with both adults and children. The employee frequently is required to use hands or fingers, handle or feel objects, tools, or controls. The employee is frequently required to stand; walk; sit; and reach with hands and arms. The employee must occasionally lift and/or move up to 50 pounds and occasionally climb ladders. Specific vision abilities required by this position include close vision, distance vision, and the ability to adjust focus. The noise level in the work environment is moderate to high.</div>

</div>
