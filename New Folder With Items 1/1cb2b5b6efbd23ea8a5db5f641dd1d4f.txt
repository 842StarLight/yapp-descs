<h2>Sr District Sales Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div></div>
<div>
<div>
<div>This position supports the Auto sales wholesale activities for the Honda division.
</div>
<div></div>
<div><b>Responsibilities:
</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Achieve wholesale, retail sales and market penetration objectives as well as other divisional goals
</li>
<li>Conduct in-dealership sales training; improve customer and sales satisfaction levels in assigned areas
</li>
<li>Assist dealers with floor planning, promotions, merchandising, advertising and other sales consultant responsibilities</li>
<li>Build, maintain, and develop dealership accounts and contacts</li>
<li>Review and analyze dealerships financial performance, conduct in-dealership sales training, improve customer and sales satisfaction levels in assigned areas
</li>
</ul>
</div>
<p></p>
<div>
<b>Qualifications</b><div>
</div>
<ul>
<li>A BA/BS degree or equivalent work related experience
</li>
<li>9-12 years of experience in wholesale automotive sales/retail auto sales as well as a demonstrated history of achieving and exceeding sales objectives
</li>
<li>Marketing, advertising, and merchandising experience is a significant plus
</li>
<li>Experience in report writing and large group presentations is essential
</li>
<li>Proficiency in all MS Office applications (Excel, Word, and PowerPoint)
</li>
<li>Position will cover <b>San Fernando Valley</b>
</li>
<li>Position also requires extensive travel and future relocation</li>
</ul>
