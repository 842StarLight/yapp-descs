<h2>Director of Service Technical Operations</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>The Director of Service Technical Operations (DSTO), will manage the Supervisor Area Managers, the Integration team and the field support personnel for patient monitoring and neurology. This position identifies and drives the success strategies that result at a high level of both short term and long-term customer satisfaction. The DSTO owns the client relationship from a technical services perspective post installation. This position ensures that there are key focal contact points for clients in an assigned region covering both sustaining and emergent issues. The ideal candidate will have a strong service background with formal technical training in the medical industry. Additionally, related experience in medical technical IT service would be an added plus. This is a highly technical role that requires the ability to be able to develop deep technical understanding of Nihon Kohden products and their integration with customer IT and customer information systems.</p>
<p></p>
<h1 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>ESSENTIAL FUNCTIONS AND BASIC DUTIES</b></h1>
<ul>
<li>Provide Leadership to the Supervisor Area Managers, the Integration team and the technical field support personnel for patient monitoring and neurology.</li>
<li>Follow up on all customer issues, drive accountability toward resolution.</li>
<li>Implement best practices and retain lessons learned from key site issues.</li>
<li>Develop a relationship with customer key stake holders with regular visits to key / problem sites, attending with the SAMs.</li>
<li>Strategic thinking in order to drive improvements throughout the company.</li>
<li>Lead and Manage the Network Support team to ensure appropriate network support is provided on a timely basis for customers.
</li>
<li>Must have strong interpersonal skills to communicate with cross functional groups like Program Management, Sales, Operations and have the ability to communicate technical solutions / problems to non-technical teams.
</li>
<li>Be able to represent the Nihon Kohden and present NK’s solution to end customers during a pre-sale conversation with the hospital. (critical)</li>
<li>Must be able to handle multiple requests in a calm manner and be able to prioritize work in a fast-paced environment. High organization skills a must.</li>
<li>Calm in the face of pressure, keeping customers and NKA management updated on all issue sin a timely manner. May need to be available to the customer 24x7 during critical periods.</li>
<li>Ability to marshal resources across the company to enable timely support.</li>
<li>Develop Strategies to help the organization achieve strategic goals and objectives.</li>
<li>Responsible for managing the selection and development of people, resources, and budgets necessary to perform the functions of the department.</li>
<li>Create, edit and implement company policies pertaining to the department.</li>
<li>Assign duties and work direction to department personnel.</li>
<li>Monitor work procedures, work schedules, and expedites workflow.</li>
<li>Train appropriate personnel on NKA’s procedures for Network Design and Support.</li>
</ul>

<p><b>EDUCATION/CERTIFICATION:
</b></p>
<p>Bachelor’s degree in a technical field and experience. Master’s preferred.
</p>
<p>Working knowledge of networking, network design, and network troubleshooting</p>
<ul>
<li>Network certification (e.g. Cisco) a plus</li>
</ul>
<p>Working knowledge of Enterprise software development
</p>
<ul>
<li>Solutions Architect certification (e.g. AWS) a plus</li>
</ul>
<p><b>REQUIRED KNOWLEDGE:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Overall medical device general networking, service background. Service Project Management understanding important.</li>
<li>Strong technical knowledge of products and systems</li>
<li>Strong analytical and problem-solving skills</li>
</ul>
<p><b>EXPERIENCE REQUIRED:
</b></p>
<p><b>7+ </b>years of experience as a manager.</p>
<p><b>SKILLS/ABILITIES:</b></p>
<ul>

<li>Strong focus on customer service</li>
<li>Ability to effectively partner with cross-functional teams.</li>
<li>Has provided network and systems support and then managed those who provide these
</li>
<li>Skilled at managing technical teams to meet established production and real-time support goals</li>
<li>Excellent conflict management skills</li>
<li>Ability to work cohesively with various specialties including construction, IT, electrical, and similar</li>
