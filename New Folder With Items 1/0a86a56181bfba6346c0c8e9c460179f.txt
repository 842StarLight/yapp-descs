<p><b>About Us</b></p>
<p>
WorkForce Software is the #1 rated and fastest growing best-of-breed software company in the Workforce Management space.<br>
At WorkForce Software We Make Work Easy for global companies who have better things to do than deal with difficult to use paper timesheets or post-it-note schedules. Our SaaS solutions help large enterprises with complex regulatory and business requirements build schedules, track time, and manage absences efficiently and easily.<br>
The value proposition we communicate to prospective customers is compelling. We improve their top and bottom line, protect them from the risk of breaking wage and labor laws and help them provide a positive work environment with real work life balance for their employees.<br>
Workforce Management (WFM) is the last mile of the Human Capital Management digital transformation journey. Our market continues to grow, evolve and innovate with Gartner predicting over 95% of WFM solutions will be hosted in the cloud by 2023; there has never been a better time to join the industry leading vendor in WFM and help accelerate our success.</p>
