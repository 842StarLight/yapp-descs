<p><b>FINANCIAL AID STUDENT SUPPORT REPRESENTATIVE</b><br>
Ross Education LLC is looking for staff to join our Ross Medical Education Center Team in the role described below. If you have post-secondary for-profit education experience in Admissions, Career Services, Financial Aid or Management, then you know the personal reward of assisting students in changing their lives. If you are new to our industry, but are an experienced professional in administrative, office, banking, sales, customer service or management, you may have transferable skills to move into our industry.</p>
<p><b>Job Summary</b><br>
The Financial Aid Student Support Representative works under the direction of the Campus Director and the Director of the Central Financial Aid Office (CFAO). The Student Support Representative is responsible for providing prospective students with a financial aid (FA) estimate/overview, working closely with Admissions on the process of admitting students, providing loan counseling information, collecting documents, providing required information to the Campus Director and Ross Management and performing related work as required.</p>

You may also qualify for advancement to other positions depending on your credentials. <p><b>Job Duties and Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Provide students with a financial aid estimate/overview and explaining the various forms of financial aid (Title IV grants and loans, Ross Education Payment Plan, etc.)</li>
<li>Support the initial student experience and registration process by exemplifying a positive customer service approach</li>
<li>Follow up with students in the collection of documents as requested</li>
<li>Assist students at FA kiosk/computer as needed for FA processing</li>
<li>Provide entrance and exit loan counseling</li>
<li>Handle some account receivable functions including: accepting registration fee and other limited payments at the campus, distributing receipts, making bank deposits, etc.</li>
<li>Review ledger cards regularly to ensure all funds are received and follow up on delinquent students</li>
<li>Review directives and memos from the CFAO, as well as attend all mandatory training sessions provided by the CFAO</li>
<li>Assist in collection activity for the Ross Payment Plan, and other student payment plans</li>
<li>Follow all Ross Policies and Procedures and be vigilant about compliance</li>
<li>Assist with front desk coverage</li>
<li>Be the designated back-up for the Career Services Student Support Representative</li>
</ul>
