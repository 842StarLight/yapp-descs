<h2>Salesforce Solution Architect</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div><div>
<p>The Senior Professional will be responsible for planning, design, development and implementation of solutions leveraging the Salesforce Force.com platform. This role will also lead and manage project workstreams including resources, tasks and deliverables.</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Additional responsibilities include:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Serves as a trusted advisor and subject matter expert to our EPA clients</li>
<li>
Facilitates functional requirements and design sessions with Customer business stakeholders</li>
<li>
Guides and advises the overall solution design based on customer requirements</li>
<li>
Responsible for matching business process and automation requirements to the capabilities of Salesforce including data model, role hierarchy, sharing and security models</li>
<li>
Provide oversight over solution design documentation for the functional project team members (may be responsible for preparing design documentation)</li>
<li>
Recommend process best practices and functionality approach (including recommendations on make versus buy with Appexchange products)</li>
<li>
May be responsible for execution of configuration activities in Salesforce.com</li>
<li>
Provides project oversight over Business Analyst and configuration resources</li>
<li>
Responsible for coordination with lead Technical resources for integrations, code development and other custom build deliverables.</li>
<li>
Assist with System &amp; Integration Testing (SIT) and Conduct User Acceptance Testing (UAT) for end-to-end solution testing</li>
<li>
May partner with relevant GDIT teams and conceptualize and build enterprise solutions based on the Salesforce platform and products for Go to Market</li>
<li>
Coordinate efforts across multiple initiatives, including staffing</li>
<li>
Build relationships with key client stakeholders to ensure project success</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Additional Qualifications:</b></p>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>
B.S. in Business, Computer Science, Software Engineering, MIS or equivalent work experience</li>
<li>
5+ years of experience in software deployments or implementations in positions of increasing responsibility in the delivery of business consulting, systems development, and systems</li>
<li>
Demonstrated knowledge and experience with Salesforce technologies. Evidence of experience in defining and delivering Salesforce based enterprise applications in a functional or technical role</li>
<li>
Salesforce Certifications (Admin 201, Service Cloud) or the equivalent experience with the ability to attain within 3-6 months</li>
<li>
A good understanding of Requirements Management, Design and Implementation of Salesforce application cloud (Sales/Service/Marketing) and customized solutions, data integration and data migration methodologies</li>
<li>
Detail and quality oriented individual with the ability to rapidly learn and take advantage of new concepts, business models, and technologies; experience with agile development methodologies preferred</li>
<li>
Demonstrate excellent communication and presentation skills</li>
</ul>
