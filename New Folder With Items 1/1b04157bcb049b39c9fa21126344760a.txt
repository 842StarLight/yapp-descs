<div>As the <b>Fiori/SAP UI5 Architect</b>, you will be responsible the management and supervision of the RICE Support Team tasked with the analysis, design, and testing of components of SAP ERP Central Component (ECC), Public Sector Supplier Relationship Management (SRM), Business Warehouse (BW) reporting, and SAP front-end technologies (Fiori/SAPUI5). You will have a range of skills and knowledge of the technologies used and applications supported by Client Service Teams. You will be required to participate in pager/on-call support and reports to your Accenture Supervisor and Client Service Counterpart.</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
<b>Key Responsibilities:</b><br>
</div>
<div><ul>
<li>Implement SAP front-end (Fiori/SAP UI5) and Back-end (SAP Business Suite/NetWeaver Gateway, S/4) integration on SAP HANA Cloud Platform</li>
<li>Design mobile technical solutions for standard and custom SAP Fiori applications</li>
<li>Implement and adopt Agile delivery methodology for UI/UX solutions utilizing Continuous Integration and Continuous Deployment technology (DevOps)</li>
<li>Manage analysis, design, code, configure, and testing lifecycle within emerging SAP technologies such as Suite on HANA, S/4 HANA, front-end Fiori Design/SAP UI5, SAP NetWeaver Gateway/OData, Personas, and associated technical tooling.</li>
<li>Review, Disposition, Assign, Prioritize, and Approve Tickets, Defects, Issues, Risks, Remedial Action Plans, and Process and Solution Improvements.</li>
<li>Coordinate Work-Items with Impacts Sub-Team, Module, and Function Counterparts.</li>
<li>Support Functional and Application Mentoring/Knowledge Transfer.</li>
<li>Support Configuration and Integration Management Mentoring/Knowledge Transfer.</li>
<li>Work with Client, Accenture, and Teaming Partners on and across their assigned Sustainment Support Team.</li>
<li>Plan and execute all required process steps as defined in Accenture Methodologies.</li>
<li>Provide and own SAP application support in assigned areas.</li>
<li>Anticipate, identify, track and resolve issues and risks affecting the SAP applications.</li>
<li>Provide primary support toward installation of application releases into production as directed.</li>
<li>Coordinate and participate in structured peer reviews / walkthroughs.</li>
<li>Meet service commitments for assigned deliverables and proactively provide work status.</li>
<li>Continuously develop industry knowledge and associated skills.</li>
<li>Program-level priority analysis and planning.</li>
<li>Delivery status and SLA reporting to Client Leadership.</li>
</ul></div>
