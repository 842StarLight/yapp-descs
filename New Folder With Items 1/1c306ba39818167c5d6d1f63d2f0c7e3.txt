<p><b>Electronic Banking Specialist - Position Description
</b></p>
<p><b>Job Responsibilities:
</b></p>
<p>Within guidelines and under general supervision, performs multiple functions in support of branches and departments related to electronic banking including, but not limited to, adding new and maintaining existing clients to the Online Banking, Electronic Bill Payment, ACH Origination, and Remote Deposit Capture systems. Processes applications for these services, orders equipment and performs on-site customer set-up and training, and provides customer support for all of these systems via telephone, email and on-site.</p>

<p><b>Essential Duties:
</b></p>
<ul><li>Initiates and maintains appropriate internal procedures to control the various products/services provided to customers under the “Electronic Banking” banner. Ensures client satisfaction through superior knowledge, courtesy, professional appearance, responsiveness, accuracy and communication for customer-based transactions.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Reviews and handles Online Banking questions and concerns from customers, as well as enrolling customers in the program.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Is familiar with NACHA rules and oversees the application, review and approval process for ACH Origination and Remote Deposit Capture customers.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Provides initial set-up and training at customer locations for ACH Origination and RDC products, and answers RDC- and ACH- related questions.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Trains department and branch personnel regarding Electronic Banking products/services in order to increase knowledge and proficiency. Assists in planning for implementation of new product information, regulations, policies and procedures dealing with Electronic Banking systems.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Performs all other duties as directed by Supervisor.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Assures compliance with all Bank policies, procedures and processes, and all applicable state and federal banking laws, rules and regulations; adheres to Bank Secrecy Act (BSA) responsibilities that are specific to the position.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Treats people with respect; keeps commitments; Inspires the trust of others; works ethically and with integrity; upholds organizational values; accepts responsibility for own actions.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Demonstrates knowledge of and adherence to EEO policy; shows respect and sensitivity for cultural differences; educates others on the value of diversity; promotes working environment free of harassment of any type; builds a diverse workforce and supports affirmative action.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Follows policies and procedures; completes administrative tasks correctly and on time; supports the Bank’s Purpose, Values and Vision; supports the bank through outside activities.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Performs the position safely, without endangering the health or safety to themselves or others and is expected to report potentially unsafe conditions. The employee shall comply with occupational safety and health standards and all rules, regulations and orders issued pursuant to the OSHA Act of 1970, which are applicable to one’s own actions and conduct.</li></ul>

<p><b>Desired Skills &amp; Experience:</b></p>
<p>The requirements listed below are representative of the knowledge, skill and/or ability required to perform the position in a satisfactory manner.</p>
<p>• Associate of Arts degree and two years of related experience and/or training; or the equivalent combination of education and experience. Work related experience should consist of a minimum of two years operational experience in a financial institution operations, sales or customer service background. Educational experience, through in-house training sessions, formal school or financial industry related curriculum, should be business or financial industry related. • Intermediate experience, knowledge and training in branch operations activities, terminology and products and services relating to retail and commercial account customers.</p>
<ul><li>Service and sales skills of all bank products.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Basic knowledge of related state and federal banking compliance regulations, and other Bank operational policies.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Basic knowledge of human resource labor laws and policies. Supervisory and leadership skills to provide direction and training.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Intermediate skills in computer terminal and personal computer operation; word processing, spreadsheet and account opening software programs.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Intermediate typing skills to meet production needs of the position.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Basic knowledge of general office practices.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Basic math skills; calculate interest and balance accounts; add, subtract, multiply and divide in all units of measure, using whole numbers, common fractions and decimals; locate routine mathematical errors;.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Effective oral, written and interpersonal communication skills with the ability to apply common sense to carry out instructions, interpret documents, understand procedures, write reports and correspondence, and speak clearly to customers and employees.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Ability to deal with difficult problems involving multiple facets and variables in non-standardized situations.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Effective organizational and time management skills.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Ability to work with general supervision while performing duties.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Current California driver’s license and a vehicle, with proof of appropriate insurance coverage, if required to drive in the course of performing assigned duties and responsibilities.</li></ul>
