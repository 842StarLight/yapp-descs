<p><b>PURPOSE:</b></p>
<p>Create the data analytics strategy and provide comprehensive analysis to execute sales/marketing plans that will increase customer engagement and revenue for all lines of business. Design and enhance data collection, storage and reporting processes to provide detailed understanding of trends and root causes. Develop insights of the customer lifecycle and provide segmentation analysis to effectively target customers. Align with the sales and marketing teams to evaluate program performance and identify contributions to the sales pipeline, to insure an acceptable ROI.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p>This is a key position in capturing and leveraging customer data into actionable and successful programs to drive sales and margin growth. The leader will help create the data analytics strategy, build the team/resources needed and execute the multi-faceted plan.<br>
<br>
</p>
<p><b>ROLES AND RESPONSIBILITIES:</b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Develop the data analytics strategy to drive growth in sales and marketing performance
</li>
<li>Provide customer insights to identify areas of opportunity to reduce churn</li>
<li>Enhance and manage the customer lifecycle campaign strategy
</li>
<li>Create and improve the collection and use of data through customer profiles, customer behavior, demographic information and customer segmentation to improve results of our sales and marketing programs</li>
<li>Coordinate with Sales, Category &amp; Marketing teams to understand the systems and data points applicable to each group. Advise on areas of opportunity</li>
<li>Build and help manage enterprise data standards for optimization of existing and target customer data. Insure best practices for data hygiene are maintained and that the programmatic processes are optimized</li>
<li>Collaborate with IT and Data Management to define and design data warehouse tools (Power BI, Data Cube, etc.) to support marketing/sales needs
</li>
<li>Build the analytics team, tools and processes to manage the data, analyze the results and provide insights</li>
<li>Provide reports, forecasts and trend analysis on customer segments, lifecycles and personas to build targeted marketing campaigns</li>
<li>Provide promotion/campaign reporting on key metrics of performance (segment, trade class, category &amp; market), obtainment of campaign objectives and recommended next steps
</li>
<li>Lead analytics plan on national and local/regional marketing programs (events, digital/online, social, media and email) to determine performance and ROI</li>
<li>Streamline the process of sales leads (new and reactivated customers). Provide reporting on results</li>
<li>Proactively look for opportunities for marketing automation efficiencies using drip or event triggered messaging. Develop and build out targeted testing and implementation strategies</li>
<li>Increase automation of marketing/analytic reports to insure accuracy and output according to schedule</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>PREFERRED QUALIFICATIONS:</b></p>
<ul><li> Education - Bachelor's degree in Marketing, Data Management or related discipline</li></ul>
<ul><li> 6+ years developing and leading a high performance marketing analytics team, preferably with a leading data analytics firm
</li></ul>
<ul><li> Excellent analytical and modeling skills, with the ability to analyze and interpret data from multiple sources, to provide actionable insights and customer/market trends</li></ul>
<ul><li> Experience with campaign management and segmentation tools (Marketo, Eloqua, Exact Target, Marketing Cloud, Watson Campaign Automation).</li></ul>
<ul><li> Results driven with an excellent attention to detail</li></ul>
<ul><li> Ability to track, measure and report on results of various marketing activities (promotions, events, digital/online, social, etc.)
</li></ul>
<ul><li> Able to manage multiple projects and timelines - organizing, prioritizing and scheduling a high workload, in a fast paced environment</li></ul>
<ul><li> Solid understanding of Sales Lifecycle</li></ul>
<ul><li> Strong written and oral communication/presentation skills. Ability to develop reports and effectively present information</li></ul>
<ul><li> Computer Skills – Solid knowledge of Microsoft Office, including Excel (IF statements, Vlookups, VBA basics), Access and Power Point</li></ul>
<ul><li> Must be able to effectively manage intra departmental relationships, and work in assigned teams and other groups as required
</li></ul>
<ul><li> Experience implementing and onboarding software, a plus. This includes business case development, gathering requirements, vendor evaluation and adoption</li></ul>
<ul><li> CRM and Marketing Automation experience, a plus</li></ul>
