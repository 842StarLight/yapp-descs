<h2>HelpDesk Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Join a high-performing, tight-knit team in a fast-growing startup. Our company, Practical Dental Solutions, builds software that helps streamline dental practice operations with well-designed, real-time apps. As a member of the team, you'll have the opportunity to work with all corners of the organization and find projects that align with your personal interests. You may have the opportunity to contribute to sales, content marketing, PR, web design, technical writing, product development, and analytics.</p>
<p><b>WHAT YOU’LL DO</b></p>
<p>· <b>Provide outstanding customer support through phone calls and emails.</b>Customers ask all sorts of questions about things like usability, logic, bugs, and best practices. You need to answer them accurately and efficiently all while making them smile.</p>
<p>· <b>Advocate for customers and exceed their expectations.</b>Sometimes things don’t go as expected for the customer. In those cases, you need to own the situation. Win them over, determine next best steps, and follow through on your promise.</p>
<p>· <b>Get technical.</b>You will have to roll up your sleeves and troubleshoot software issues, so make sure you are comfortable navigating a computer and various IT environments. You will learn when to jump on a scooter to escalate issues.</p>
<p>· <b>Take on projects and create opportunities.</b>Variety is the spice of life, right? Aside from spending a lot of time interacting with customers, you’ll help their accounts run smoothly with some work behind the scenes. In this dynamic work atmosphere, there is always a need for you to take on new tasks and help build our business.</p>
<p><b>YOU’RE GREAT AT: </b></p>
<p>· Communicating verbally and in written form, while wearing headphones</p>
<p>· Solving problems independently and thinking on your feet</p>
<p>· Software Installation / Configuration</p>
<p>· Reading people by listening to their voice</p>
<p>· Staying positive and serving others</p>
<p>· Showing initiative and follow through</p>
<p>· Handling stressful situations</p>
<p>· Organizing and prioritizing competing requests</p>
<p><b>WHAT’S IN IT FOR YOU: </b></p>
<p>· Competitive base salary with enormous opportunity for growth</p>
<p>· Super laid-back environment where hustle goes a long way and work is enjoyable</p>
<p>· Great opportunity to get into a fast-growing company and build something big</p>
<p>· Medical/Dental/Vision/benefits</p>
<p>· Endless supply of fruit, coffee, and other goodies!</p>
<p>· Open office layout with no high cubicles. Casual jeans dress code.</p>
