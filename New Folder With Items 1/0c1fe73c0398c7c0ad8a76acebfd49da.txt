<h2>Product Development Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Pharmacists Mutual has an opening for a Product Development Specialist. This role will Initiate internal reviews and analyzes policy/coverage form, rate and rule revisions in support of Company strategic initiatives. Provides product support for sales opportunities by performing market, competitive and/or financial analysis in the identification or development of new products, expansion of existing businesses, or special products. Partners with Underwriting, Legal, Compliance, Marketing, Claims and Finance to assure product modifications meet requirements of those departments. Assists business units with complex analytical projects, and identifies and addresses key business issues. Prepares presentations for Executive Management of alternative and recommended product changes. Initiates Company implementation of required and selected revisions.
</p>
<p>Product Development Specialist I</p>
<p>
In training and learning:</p>
<ul>
<li>Rate analysis and rate adequacy analysis steps and procedures.
</li>
<li>Review of advisory organization bulletin/circular content/impact.</li>
<li>Components of regulatory filings and state filing requirements.</li>
<li>Preparation and composition of SERFF form, rate, and rule filings.</li>
<li>Data extraction and analysis.
</li>
<li>Works under close supervision of manager or Product Manager / Senior Product Manager.</li>
</ul>
<p>Product Development Specialist II
</p>
<ul>
<li>Performs rate adequacy and rate change analysis using developed rate analysis tools.
</li>
<li>Analyzes advisory organization product and rate revisions/introductions.
</li>
<li>Develops required filing documents.
</li>
<li>Creates and submits regulatory filings for individual line of business revisions and assists with countrywide revision filings.
</li>
<li>Manipulates established data queries and creates simple queries.
</li>
<li>Conducts market analysis.
</li>
<li>Works under intermittent supervision of manager or Product Manager / Senior Product Manager.</li>
</ul>
<p>Senior Product Development Specialist
</p>
<ul>
<li>Performs rate adequacy and rate change analysis sometimes developing and enhancing rate analysis tools.
</li>
<li>Critiques advisory organization product and rate revisions/introductions and recommends appropriate adoption of advisory organization rates and exceptions to them.</li>
<li>Demonstrates critical thinking.</li>
<li>Independently develops required filing documents.
</li>
<li>Independently creates and submits regulatory filings for individual line of business revisions and assists with countrywide revision filings.
</li>
<li>Independently develops data queries.
</li>
<li>Independently conducts market analysis.
</li>
<li>Works with minimal guidance.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Essential Functions</b></p>
<p>
Essential functions may include, but are not limited to the following:<b>
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Reviews advisory organization filings and assesses impact on PhMIC.

<li>Researches industry trends, competitor products and pricing, niche markets, insurance regulations and statutes.
</li>
<li>Demonstrates a working understanding of rate adequacy analysis and policy coverage.
</li>

</li>
<li>Develops Company proprietary form, rate and rule revisions.
</li>
<li>Demonstrates a working knowledge of data extraction, manipulation and analysis.
</li>

</li>
<li>Monitors and assists in testing policy processing system revisions.
</li>
<li>Assists in developing and presenting revision training to internal users.
</li>
<li>Monitors effects of pricing revisions implemented.</li>
</ul>
