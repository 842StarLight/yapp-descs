<h2>Technical Support Specialist - Dental Small Equipment</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Our client, <b>NSK Dental</b>, is an established manufacturer of dental handpieces in the US and Globally. Highly rated for their superior quality and reliability and unsurpassed cost performance, their products sell in over 135 countries around the world. The company and the position is based from Hoffman Estates, IL.</p>
<p><b>ESSENTIAL JOB FUNCTIONS AND RESPONSIBILITIES </b></p>
<p>As the “Technical Support Specialist,” the objective is to provide excellent technical product and customer service support of NSK's dental product portfolio. The main job responsibilities will include but are not limited to:</p>
<ul>
<li>Provide technical support to field techs, sales reps and end-users.</li>
<li>Provide technical training to service technicians and customer service teams.</li>
<li>Inspect and service dental trial products.</li>
<li>Inspect and/or service dental warranties for newly launched products.</li>
<li>Routinely interacts with the sales force and customers and takes ownership to follow problems through to completion</li>
<li>Update the VP of Sales &amp; Marketing on any product, sales force, and customer service issues</li>
<li>Responds to brochure and sales material requests</li>
<li>Prepares ad-hoc reports as needed.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>EXPERIENCE and REQUIRED SKILLS</b></p>
<ul>
<li>5 or more years experience as a field tech.</li>
<li>Understanding product schematics.</li>
<li>Capable of servicing dental hand pieces.</li>
<li>Great communication skills.</li>
<li>Team player.</li>
<li>Ability to train and motivate others.</li>
<li>Candidates must live within reasonable commuting distance to Hoffman Estates, IL.</li>
<li>High energy and self-starter with an ability to work independently.</li>
<li>Outstanding written and oral communication skills.</li>
<li>Ability to build and maintain working relationships with a variety of internal and external individuals.</li>
<li>Experience and knowledge of the medical device industry a plus.</li>
<li>Core Values – Honest, Hard Working, Dependable, Adaptable, Visionary, and Friendly.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>THE POSITION OFFERS</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Base salary - $50,000 - $60,000 based on experience.</li>
<li>Bonus - 7% of base salary.</li>
<li><b>Healthcare benefits paid at 100%!</b></li>
</ul>

<p>Salary: $50,000.00 to $60,000.00 /year</p>
<p>Experience:</p>
<ul><li>dental product field tech: 3 years (Required)</li></ul>
