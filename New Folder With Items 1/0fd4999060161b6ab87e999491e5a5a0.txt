<h2>STORE BOOKKEEPER</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p>This position is accountable for the following: Customer service during shift. Accounting decisions that are consistent with Town Pump standards, policies, and procedures. Assists in planning, leadership, organizing, and follow-ups in the following areas: office procedures, vendor invoicing, computer data entries, and shortage controls. Also responsible for setting the proper example for co-workers in terms of professionalism, attitude, and teamwork in the areas of customer service and store operations.</p>
<p><b>
ESSENTIAL DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES: (Dual responsibilities in some locations)</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Customer service to include handling and screening of telephone calls.</li>
<li>
Effectively prioritize, organize, and follow-up on office tasks to be accomplished.</li>
<li>
Security of all store assets. Control of store cash fund, fuel and inventories, auditing of shift reports.</li>
<li>
Complete transactions for accounts receivables, i.e. counting money, completing deposit slips, banking, closeout, etc.</li>
<li>
Accurate and effective completion of all store paper work: store daily report, store daily purchases, mark-ups/mark-downs, and inventory adjustments.</li>
<li>
Receive merchandize using proper vendor and inventory controls.</li>
<li>
Order office supplies to maintain an effective stock level.</li>
<li>
Process store invoices to include auditing cost and retail price.</li>
<li>
Make computer entries and record sales and purchases in the store’s running book. Balance store records to office reports.</li>
<li>
Post shift statistics to shift analysis.</li>
<li>
Verify time cards for payroll.</li>
<li>
Enter purchases into computer.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
NON-ESSENTIAL DUTIES:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Police the store.</li>
<li>
Clear gas consoles, turns on pumps.</li>
<li>
Dust and stock shelves</li>
<li>
Clean restroom</li>
<li>
Total bill and make transactions with customers.</li>
<li>
Empty island garbage, fill water/cleaning solution buckets on islands.</li>
<li>
Stock cooler</li>
<li>
Other duties as assigned by supervisors.</li>
</ul>

<p><b>
