<h2>BDC Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>OBX CHEVROLET (KITTY HAWK, NORTH CAROLINA)</b><br>
<br>
The Business Development Center Manager is a technically savvy individual who understands technologies and who can communicate in a clear and efficient way with consumers using their preferred method of communication (internet/email, mobile/Smart phones, tablets, etc.).<br>
<br>
He or she must consistently and profitably meet vehicle sales quotas through expert management and leadership of the sales team and by forecasting sales and maintaining proper inventory levels in terms of amount and vehicle types.<br>
<br>
In addition, the BDC Manager hires, trains, and monitors team which handles most follow-up, all internet leads and inbound calls and lead generation.<br>
<br>
The ideal candidate has experience in a large-volume dealership, and some supervisory or management experience. He or she must have a strong understanding of today's technologies (computer, internet, mobile/Smart phone, tablets, etc.), a strong sales aptitude, and a willingness to accept full accountability for sales targets. Excellent communication and interpersonal skills and strong leadership and motivational skills are essential.<br>
</p>
<b>
RESPONSIBILITIES</b><ul>
<li>
Develops viable sales forecasts and associated sales strategies</li>
<li>
Hires, trains, and monitors team member performance</li>
<li>
Establishes sales quotas and manages overall sales goals</li>
<li>
Ensures all sales targets are met or exceeded</li>
<li>
Provides timely and accurate reports on all sales activities as needed</li>
<li>
Monitors and adjusts inventory to optimize customer choice</li>
<li>
Manages the department budget</li>
<li>
Prominently displays web address throughout the dealership, on business cards, in all dealership advertising, and in all other appropriate media</li>
<li>
Helps promote the dealership online via Social Media outlets (Twitter, Facebook, etc.)</li>
<li>
Performs other duties as assigned</li>
</ul>
<br>
<b>

<li>
Two years of progressive and relevant experience</li>
<li>
Experience in supervising and managing staff</li>
<li>
Strong computer/internet, mobile/Smart phone, and Social Media skills</li>
<li>
Experience with website and graphic design programs helpful</li>
<li>
Strong leadership and motivational skills</li>
<li>
Outgoing personality and willing to work with the public on a daily basis</li>
<li>
Highly professional and dependable</li>
</ul>
