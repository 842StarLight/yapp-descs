<h2>SAP SD Solution Architect</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div><div><div>
<div><div><div>
<p>The SAP SD Solution Architect supports the functional business processes for Order to Cash and Procure to Pay utilizing SAP SD/MM/PP Modules. The SAP Solution Architect will work with the business, lead meetings, give guidance to internal and external technical resources and effectively manage business expectations. The Solutions Architected will focus on higher level solution design and bridge the business' needs with the functional opportunities in SAP. This role is the "go-to" Subject Matter Expert for SAP supporting OTC and PTP functional processes and modules including support and improvement of ongoing operations. Delivery related to configurations and development of features is managed by internal Service Delivery lead and executed by external partners.</p>
<p><b>
CORE ACCOUNTABILITIES:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Define and architect SAP to support innovative functional solutions and exceed the expectations of our business partners</li>
<li>Drive significant new business capabilities through identification and deployment of SAP capabilities that enable automation and innovation</li>
<li>Comprehend business strategies, requirements and pain-points / opportunities to develop necessary designs and plans to ensure projects and solutions satisfy those needs</li>
<li>Provide SAP functional expertise as needed to business and support/operations stakeholders as needed</li>
<li>Maintain understanding of how individual work and decisions fit into the broader organizational picture</li>
<li>Participate in resolving cross-functional and cross-system design issues with Integration Team</li>
<li>Influence the IT "to-be" technology footprint, develop roadmap and phased transition approach to enable Cole Haan's future success</li>
<li>Define, develop, maintain, and communicate the future SAP platform strategy to all levels including the Cole Haan IT Leadership and Executive teams</li>
<li>Take ownership of open-ended opportunities and problems and drive them to completion</li>
<li>Highly self-motivated and entrepreneurial spirit</li>
<li>Likes working at a fast pace, is self-directed, and able to manage multiple concurrent competing challenges, issues and priorities</li>
<li>Mentor, educate, and train colleagues as requested</li>
<li>Keep skills updated and remain proactively aware of happenings and current events in the industry</li>
</ul>
</div></div></div>
<div>
<div>
<p><b>Requirements</b></p>
<div><ul>
<li>Bachelor's Degree required in computer science, engineering, finance, business management or a related field. An equivalent combination of education and experience may be considered.</li>
<li>5+ years of progressively increasing IS Leadership and Management experience in a complex global environment</li>
<li>Demonstrated strategic planning experience for both business and systems</li>
<li>Ability to positively influence people at all levels in the organization</li>
<li>Demonstrated experience in managing and mentoring a team of business analysts and contractors on a global scale to deliver complex and reliable solutions to a diverse set of businesses and stakeholders</li>
<li>Demonstrated experience in process mapping, portfolio/project management, training and testing</li>
<li>Understand key business processes and systems employed by the business</li>
<li>Experience and thought leadership around the following major areas in a complex supply chain environment: procurement, outsourced global manufacturing and global logistics processes and systems; Inventory and order management processes and systems</li>
<li>Expertise in SAP R/3 in Sales &amp; Distribution (SD), Material Master (MM) and Production Planning (PP)</li>
<li>Solid understanding on SD &amp; FI-AR integration and experience with SD and FI integration</li>
<li>Demonstrated successful project/program management and process improvement leadership</li>
<li>Experience in Agile software development methodologies is a plus</li>
<li>Experienced in all stages of System Development Lifecycle from business process improvement, requirements gathering, design, construction, testing, implementation and support</li>
<li>Proven skills in integrating enterprise packaged applications is required</li>
<li>Knowledge of the outdoor industry and the Apparel/Footwear industry is a plus</li>
</ul></div>
