<h2>CAD DESIGNER/DRAFTER</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>Job Description<br>
<br>
<p>Macroseal, Inc, a leading manufacturer of Gasket and Fluid Sealing Products, is seeking an experienced, full time CAD Designer/Drafter to join our growing team in Prince George, Virginia. The selected candidate will work collaboratively with members of our manufacturing, engineering, and sales departments to deliver high quality CAD drawings in support of our clients across the USA and internationally.</p>
</div>
<br>
<div>Qualifications<br>
<br>
<p><b><i>Essential Requirements:</i></b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Proficient with 2D-3D modeling software, Autodesk Inventor, and AutoCAD preferred.; as well as Microsoft Excel, Word, and Adobe Acrobat.</li>
<li>
3+ years experience using AutoCAD is required, preferably in an industrial/manufacturing environment.</li>
<li>
An understanding of basic mechanical engineering principles is required</li>
<li>
Knowledge of PC based CNC manufacturing equipment</li>
<li>
Team player with strong problem solving, organizational, time management and communication skills, both verbal and written, due to daily interaction with engineers and factory team members</li>
<li>
Ability to work with limited supervision, positive attitude, and strong work ethic with a strong attention to detail</li>
</ul>
<p><b><i>
Essential Responsibilities:</i></b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Create technical drawings of new or revised parts or assemblies</li>
<li>
Revise existing production drawings with changes indicated by engineers</li>
<li>
Interact daily with other departments to produce parts, assemblies, and drawings</li>
<li>
Manage and maintain the company's exisitng drawing library, ensuring proper documentation and revision levels are accurate and up to date</li>
<li>
Perform field/site work in measuring new or existing equipment and then converting those measurements into usable drawings</li>
<li>
Assassin the design, engineering, and development of new products</li>
</ul>
