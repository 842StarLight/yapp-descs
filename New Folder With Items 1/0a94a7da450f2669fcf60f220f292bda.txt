<b>Current Role &amp; Growth Opportunity:</b><br>
The Contract Specialist is a member of the MedeAnalytics Legal Team and is primarily responsible for drafting, reviewing, negotiating, and finalizing commercial and technology contracts with healthcare clients. This role will interact with internal business partners, external customers, and to provide timely updates and support on various contractual issues.
<br><br>
<b>Essential Duties and Responsibilities:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Drafting, evaluating, negotiating, and finalizing new client contracts, contract renewals or amendments, within established standards.</li>
<li>Serving as the point of contact for internal customers on contractual matters, providing redlined recommendations, and negotiating directly with our external customers</li>
<li>Reviewing and analyzing RFPs to support business growth initiatives.</li>
<li>Maintaining commercial agreements in corporate contract management system.</li>
<li>Provide quantitative analysis for existing commercial contracts for internal stakeholders.</li>
<li>Provide functional internal stakeholders support via phone, e-mail and chat.</li>
<li>Work directly with the internal stakeholders to provide high customer satisfaction.</li>
<li>Communicate effectively with clients, internal stakeholders and colleagues.</li>
<li>Update and maintain existing client contract post-implementation.</li>
</ul>
<b>Essential Skills, Experience and Education:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Minimum of an undergraduate degree, graduate degree preferred</li>
<li>3+ years of experience with reviewing, drafting and negotiating commercial services contracts</li>
<li>Experience working with technology and SaaS commercial agreements</li>
<li>Experience working with other health care commercial agreements (e.g.: business associate agreements)</li>
<li>Experience working with a contract management system (e.g.: Apttus, ContractRoom)</li>
<li>Proficient in redlining within MS Word and data analysis within MS Excel</li>
<li>Strong verbal and written communication skills</li>
<li>Strong customer service and interpersonal communication skills</li>
<li>Ability to build and maintain rapport with all levels of internal and external company contacts</li>
<li>Team player, able to independently handle multiple deadlines</li>
</ul>
