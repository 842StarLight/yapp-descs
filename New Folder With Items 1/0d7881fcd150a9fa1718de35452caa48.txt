<p><b>
Position Summary:</b></p>
<p>
SOCi is looking for a Strategic Account Executive with experience selling into Multi-Location Multifamily Property Management Companies to identify new business opportunities in identified regions of North America.</p>
<p>
The ideal candidate will be a relentless hunter, consistently employing thoughtful sales processes with a consistent track record of sales over-achievement. The primary responsibility of the Account Executive is to drive and grow new business for SOCi. This will require you to serve as a subject matter expert in the industry and our platform. This is a client-facing role requiring deep industry expertise, proven ability and a broad base of industry contacts.</p>
<p><b>

<ul>

<li>

<li>
