<h2>Accounting Assistant/Bookkeeper</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>We are looking for an experienced accounting assistant, staff accountant or bookkeeper to join our accounting staff. Must be organized, great in time management and understands confidentiality. Also must be able to work a full-time schedule Monday-Friday, 8am - 5pm.</p>
<p>Quickbooks experience is preferred and requires Excel proficiency.</p>
<p>Associates in Accounting or 2 years accounting experience minimum.</p>
<p>What you will do:</p>
<ul>
<li>Manage the accounts receivables for several product lines to include review and research of accounts receivable payment discrepancies.</li>
<li>Maintain timely correspondence with customer to resolve disputes, deductions and overpayments to include phone calls to customer regarding past due accounts.</li>
<li>Maintain subsidiary detail spreadsheets to reconcile to internal collections software (Dakcs).</li>
<li>Work proficiently in multiple software packages for different product lines.</li>
<li>Cross train to perform all billing functions from posting payments, making corrections and adjustments to running the final billing for all product lines. To include proofing and ensuring accuracy, printing and preparing to mail.</li>
<li>Cross-train to provide backup as needed to existing accounting staff. Duties may include running daily/weekly/monthly trust reports and posting payments to trust accounts, reconciling complicated payment process of major clients in the utility and medical service provider industries and other corporate accounting to include accounts payable.</li>
</ul>
<p>Additional responsibilities/projects:</p>
<p>· Monthly/quarterly sales tax filings</p>
<p>· Data entry for third party commission payments</p>
<p>· Daily posting of revenue journal entry, bank transfers and customer ACH payments.</p>
<p>· Prepare monthly balance sheet account reconciliations</p>
<p>· Assisting HR manager with variety of tasks</p>
<p>· Special projects as assigned by Controller from time-time.</p>
