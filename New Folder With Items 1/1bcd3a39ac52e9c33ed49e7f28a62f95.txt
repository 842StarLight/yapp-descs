<h2>Senior Implementation Engineer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>**Position can be based out of our offices in Irvine- CA, Brooklyn-NY, or home based anywhere within the US**</b></p>
<p>
As a Senior Implementation Engineer on the Nuxeo Professional Services Team, you’ll get to work with brilliant engineers solving complex business problems through leading technology and advanced open architecture. As an expert in the Nuxeo platform, you will provide technical guidance and leadership to the customer team to design and implement the Nuxeo Platform to meet rigorous business and technology goals. You will collaborate closely with the Nuxeo Implementation Engineers, providing leadership and mentorship to help ensure the highest standards of technical delivery from design through roll-out. Working both at the keyboard coding and shoulder-to-shoulder with customers evaluating and architectural choices you will help bridge the gap between business and technology.</p>
<p>
What will you do?</p>
<ul>
<li>Lead requirements elaboration and architectural design sessions with customers</li>
<li>Identify and assess business, functional, and technical requirements</li>
<li>Recommend solutions to resolve functional and technical requirements using Nuxeo</li>
<li>Develop implementation designs using the Nuxeo Platform</li>
<li>Develop integrations and add-ons to the Nuxeo platform</li>
<li>Configure and customize the Nuxeo platform for customer implementations</li>
<li>Author client deliverables (e.g. specifications, test cases, user stories, and design documents)</li>
<li>Lead the development and delivery of training for customers and teams</li>
<li>Lead technical implementation teams</li>
<li>Mentor and train team members</li>
<li>Relay product feature requests to product management</li>
<li>Must be able to travel 50% of the time</li>
