<div><b>Position Description</b></div>
<div></div>
<div>Emerson is looking for a Project Engineer for control system projects for our Houston, TX location. We provide project management, engineering and design services and supply of control systems hardware and software for control system projects in the oil and gas, refining, and petro-chemical industries</div>
<div></div>
<div><b>Job Responsibilities</b></div>
<ul>
<li><div>As the execution leader you will independently lead a project team in executing control systems projects involving the latest technology available in automation digital control and safety systems.
</div></li>
<li><div>You will work with the customer and provide the technical direction and solution.
</div></li>
<li><div>You will lead the planning and execution of field surveys, generation of project deliverables, checkouts and commissioning of control systems.
</div></li>
<li><div>You will coordinate various engineering, construction, purchasing, scheduling and commissioning activities on projects to ensure the execution and quality of the deliverables.
</div></li>
<li><div>You will support the Project Manager in preparing project execution plan, scope change definition and estimate, technical queries, risk mitigation, project schedule, progress report.<br>
</div></li>
</ul>
</div>
<div></div>

<div></div>
<div><b>Requirements</b></div>
<div></div>
<ul>
<li><div>Bachelor of Science Engineering degree and 13+ years of experience in control system automation engineering projects in projects in the oil and gas, refining, and/or petro-chemical industries.</div></li>
<li><div>Prefer Electrical or Chemical Engineering degree
</div></li>
<li><div>Reasonable knowledge of Oil and Gas, Refining, and Petro-Chemical Industry processes</div></li>
<li><div>Knowledgeable in making application of DCS and control systems like, DeltaV, Honeywell PKS and/or Foxboro systems, 3rd party interfaces, Safety Instrumentation Systems, electrical components and systems, etc.</div></li>
<li><div>Control system automation and field instrument experience and be familiar with the various hardware/ software products and services employed for process control system projects</div></li>
<li><div>Experience in scope definition and execution for front end study and detail implementation</div></li>
<li><div>Experience in field instrumentation specification and signal wiring design</div></li>
<li><div>Supervision and coordination of engineers, designers, technicians, and 3rd party suppliers and contractors</div></li>
<li><div>Experience in coordinate engineering, construction, purchasing and scheduling</div></li>
<li><div>Strong analytical and problem-solving skills along with attention to detail are essential.
</div></li>
<li><div>Must be able to work with a minimum of supervision and be able to provide management oversight</div></li>
<li><div>Can manage large databases for use with automated engineering and design tools</div></li>
<li><div>Experience using and transmitting client data using an electronic data management tool</div></li>
<li><div>Experience in control systems modernization projects
</div></li>
<li><div>Experience in hot cutover planning and commissioning
</div></li>
<li><div>Strong Communication skills
</div></li>
<li><div>Self-Motivated and innovative
</div></li>
<li><div>Track record of successfully managing remote global engineering teams.</div></li>
<li><div>Ability to travel to client locations up to 50% of the time.</div></li>
</ul>
