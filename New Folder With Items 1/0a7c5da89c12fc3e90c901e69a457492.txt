<h2>Program Director</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>CUMULUS MEDIA | Colorado Springs is searching for an experienced On-Air Program Director. Cumulus heritage Top 40 KKMG-FM has a rare opening for a Program Director position. Our new 98.9 Magic FM PD will be focused and driven to keep our #1 rank and lead our local and syndicated talent into a position to grow and succeed. Cumulus has the tools and team in place to help YOU to craft the next chapter for this legendary radio station.</p>
<p>CUMULUS Colorado Springs features 6 unique brands in Southern Colorado. Nash-FM, KCSF-AM, KKFM-FM, 98.9 Magic FM, 92.9 Peak FM and KVOR-AM. These stations reach hundreds of thousands of listeners on a weekly basis.</p>
<p>Key Responsibilities:</p>
<ul><li>Create, implement, and manage the imaging, music, and brand of 98.9 Magic FM.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Coach, direct, develop, and motivate air talent to maximize their performance and appeal</li></ul>
<ul><li>Deliver unique content that builds ratings and grows terrestrial and digital audience in the core demo</li></ul>
<ul><li>Work closely with sales and marketing team to develop relationships contests, promotions and revenue</li></ul>
<ul><li>Ensure operation of station in accordance with FCC regulations and policies</li></ul>
<ul><li>Host on-air programming, both live and tracked. Operate control board, remote broadcasting and other related production equipment as well as music scheduling software</li></ul>
<p>Qualifications:</p>
<ul><li>Minimum 3 years’ of successful programming experience in a Nielsen rated market preferred</li></ul>
<ul><li>Strong social and digital experience</li></ul>
<ul><li>Savvy computer skills and proficiency with research and music scheduling</li></ul>
<ul><li>Ability to multi-task and the discipline to focus on what is the highest and best use of your time</li></ul>
<ul><li>Excellent verbal and written communication skills with air staff, departments and clients</li></ul>
<ul><li>Creative and strategic problem solver</li></ul>
<ul><li>Deep knowledge of music, pop culture and current events and how they relate to the 98.9 Magic FM target demo</li></ul>
<p>What we offer:</p>
<ul><li>Competitive pay</li></ul>
<ul><li>Professional growth and career path</li></ul>
<ul><li>Focused, responsible and collaborative work environment with the ability, to ask “what if” and try innovative solutions</li></ul>
<ul><li>Medical, Dental &amp; Vision Insurance coverage</li></ul>
<ul><li>401K with company match to plan for the long-term</li></ul>
<ul><li>Paid Vacation &amp; Holidays</li></ul>
