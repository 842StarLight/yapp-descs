<h2>Shop Coordinator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p>Alfa Laval is a leading global supplier of products and solutions for heat transfer, separation and fluid handling through our key products – heat exchangers, separators, pumps and valves. We currently play a vital role in areas that are crucial for society, such as energy optimization, environmental protection and food production.</p>
<p>
Alfa Laval’s products are used in the manufacturing of food, chemicals, pharmaceuticals, starch, sugar and ethanol. We are also used in nuclear power, onboard vessels; and in the engineering sector, mining industry and refinery sector as well as treating wastewater and creating a comfortable indoor climate.</p>
<p>
The Shop Coordinator provides overall support for repair and reconditioning work for both our external and internal customers. Key areas include: Provide quotations to our sales force, prioritize and coordinate repairs, overall management / administration of each repair order, continually seek ways to improve repair processes and techniques. The Shop Coordinator contacts customers to discuss repairs, quote repairs, follow up discussions after repairs, and assists in order entry and invoicing as needed.</p>
<p>
Expectations &amp; Responsibilities:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Prioritize and coordinate the overall flow of repair and reconditioning projects with Team Leaders and Management, focusing on improving utilization and throughput</li>
<li>
Maintain production schedules and assist with regularly scheduled production meetings</li>
<li>
Research customer equipment in proprietary systems to confirm accuracy of parts and lead time</li>
<li>
Create job packets for repair and reconditioning projects with applicable drawings, quality plans and checklists, scan sheets, and EHS related documentation</li>
<li>
Create and formalize repair quotes to end customers and internal Sales teams</li>
<li>
Review of shop quotes before pricing and finalization</li>
<li>
Perform tasks within proprietary applications in MRP and Lotus Notes systems (order parts and expedite as needed, create PO’s for outsource operations, occasionally schedule freight, etc.)</li>
<li>
Interact and communicate repair information to shop, end customers, and sales personnel</li>
<li>
Assist Service Center team to meet published baseline measurements and KPI’s</li>
<li>
Manage job costs: Assures selling price and costs are in line to maintain acceptable margins</li>
<li>
Act as primary backup for Order Coordinator as needed</li>
<li>
Maintain applicable inventory reports, records, and proprietary software.</li>
<li>
Coordinate and assist in the completion and documentation of all ACT warranty claims</li>
<li>
Positive participation in meetings, ALPS and improvement initiatives</li>
<li>
Perform other duties as directed by Management</li>
<li>
Travel potential up to 20%</li>
</ul>
<p>
Knowledge, Skills, &amp; Abilities of the Successful Candidate:</p>
<ul>
<li>
High School Diploma or GED equivalent</li>
<li>
Five years hands-on experience with repairing/reconditioning in a manufacturing, job shop or customer service environment desirable</li>
<li>
Ability to read and work from blueprints</li>
<li>
Ability to prioritize work and meet deadlines</li>
<li>
Strong communication and respectful interpersonal skills both written and verbal</li>
<li>
Demonstrate ability to perform effectively within a team environment</li>
<li>
Some understanding of machining and welding techniques</li>
<li>
Ability to work within stressful situations</li>
<li>
Fundamental knowledge of standard word processing software and spreadsheets</li>
<li>
May have occasion to move parts up to 50 lbs</li>
<li>
Main job is in an office environment with approximately 25% of the time in a machine shop &amp; welding environment where there is the possibility of coming into contact with residues of customers’ products.</li>
<li>
Some travel within the USA to visit customers or other Service Centers; meetings; training.</li>
<li>
Occasional requirements to travel outside of the USA to visit customers; training; meetings.</li>
</ul>

EEO/Vet/Disabled Employer</p>
