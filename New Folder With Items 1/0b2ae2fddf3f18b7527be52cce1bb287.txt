<p><b>
SUMMARY:</b></p>
<p>
Under the supervision of the Finance Director, keeps records of financial transactions for the agency by performing the following duties:</p>
<p><b>
ESSENTIAL DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES</b> include the following:</p>
<ul>
<li>Verifies, allocates, and posts details of business transactions to subsidiary accounts in journals or computer files from documents such as sales slips, invoices, receipts, check stubs, and computer print-outs.</li>
<li>Performs Accounts Payable duties from the receipt of a requisition to the preparation of checks for payment.</li>
<li>Payroll duties include the calculation of employee wages from records or time cards, the preparation of payroll reports and vouchers, and the maintenance of payroll files.</li>
<li>Responds to questions from vendors (accts. pay.) and staff (payroll).</li>
<li>Allocates and posts details of business transactions to subsidiary accounts in journals or computer files from documents such as sales slips, invoices, receipts, check stubs, and computer printouts.</li>
<li>Summarizes details in separate ledgers or computer files and transfers data to general ledger.</li>
<li>Reconciles and balances general ledger accounts and bank accounts.</li>
<li>Compiles reports to show statistics such as cash receipts and expenditures.</li>
<li>Analyzes and assists in preparation of Agency budgets.</li>
<li>Maintains accounts receivable records.</li>
<li>Prepares 1099s.</li>
<li>Calculates and prepares OBRA billings.</li>
<li>Tracks Medicaid Inpatient and PHP stays.</li>
<li>Assist in Preparation of financial statements</li>
<li>Assists in preparation of state reports</li>
<li>Assists in annual audit.</li>
<li>Tracks full management stays.</li>
<li>Maintains inventory of equipment.</li>
<li>Other duties as assigned.</li>
