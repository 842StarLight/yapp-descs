<div><b>Position Primary Purpose and General Responsibilities</b></div>
<div>
<p>The VCU Office of Development and Alumni Relations seeks a detail-oriented communications specialist to serve as the point of contact to assigned clients for the project lifecycle of integrated multichannel communications. The Constituent Marketing Specialist will participate as a member of a results-oriented and audience-focused team, working with other members of the Strategic Marketing &amp; Communications department.</p>
<p>
General Duties:</p>
<ul>
<li>Configures all donor and alumni email and direct mail communications in the university’s CRM, to include the development, proofing, testing and deployment of emails and the facilitation of direct mail marketing campaigns.</li>
<li>Builds productive relationships with cross-functional campus partners and internal teammates to facilitate communications goals.</li>
<li>Keeps multiple projects with fast-paced deadlines on track for completion by requested due dates. Reschedules in coordination with the communications calendar when necessary.</li>
<li>Coordinates timing of communications to eliminate over-messaging while satisfying client demand and aligning with resource availability to execute tasks within the requested timeframe. Works with leadership to find alternative solutions when resources are not available.</li>
<li>Encourages compliance with university and department guidelines and policies, industry standards and best practices.</li>
<li>Analyzes and reports ROI, including giving response, email open and click-through rates and other metrics. Provides suggestions for improvement.</li>
<li>Assists in setting strategy for donor and alumni electronic and print communications, including audience segmentation and marketing content for newsletters, event communications, solicitations, special announcements, and annual reports.</li>
</ul>
</div>

<div>
<div><b>Minimum Hiring Standards</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree.</li>
<li>Minimum two years of marketing, communications or fundraising experience, with preference for work in a university or nonprofit setting.</li>
<li>Knowledge and understanding of fundraising and marketing techniques, concepts and channels.</li>
<li>Precise attention to detail and the ability to audit work for accuracy and quality.</li>
<li>Positive, open-minded and flexible.</li>
<li>Ability to work cooperatively in a team environment as well as independently with self-direction. High degree of initiative and independent judgment.</li>
<li>Excellent communication and customer service skills.</li>
<li>Willingness to stay informed of email and direct mail marketing best practices.</li>
<li>The ability to work in a diverse environment.</li>
</ul>
</div>
<br>
<div>
<div><b>Preferred Hiring Standards</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Demonstrated experience working with CRM marketing interfaces (i.e. Blackbaud/Raiser’s Edge, Salesforce, Microsoft Dynamics).</li>
<li>Experience with Blackbaud’s CRM is preferred.</li>
<li>Understanding of segment building and query tools a plus.</li>
<li>Experience working with constituent engagement management software, such as Encompass by iModules.</li>
<li>Proven project management skills, including the ability to manage several projects with varying due dates simultaneously. Experience working with project management tools, such as Jira Service Desk, a plus.</li>
<li>Experience working with colleagues of varying work styles and personalities.</li>
<li>A passion for data. Great analytical skills. Able to define problems, establish facts, draw valid conclusions and provide actionable recommendations. Solutions-oriented.</li>
<li>Ability to thrive in a fast-paced, high-energy environment with multiple stakeholders’ involvement that requires the ability to multitask and implement high-priority initiatives.</li>
<li>Ability to learn and adapt quickly to new software and to work in multiple software platforms on a regular basis.</li>
<li>Knowledge of AP Style a plus.</li>
<li>Experience in development or alumni relations.</li>
</ul>
