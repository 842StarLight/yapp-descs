<h2>Senior Manager - Technical Business Development</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p><b>What you’ll be doing...</b>
</p>
<p>The Senior Manager – Technical Business Development is responsible for leading a team of professionals who create, coordinate, and execute on sales related initiatives to drive sales and revenue growth in the network tower. In this role the Senior Manager will work closely with Verizon leaders, and partners to extend value propositions, direct programs, and accelerate sales cycles.</p>
<p>
You will also:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Clearly articulate Verizon strategy and align team goals and expectations accordingly so team understands personal impact on success of the organization.</li>
<li>
Create safe environment for individual suggestions and collaboration encouraging and motivating team members to share ideas and opportunities try new ways to solve problems, capture market opportunities, and grow revenues.</li>
<li>
Manage Business Development team activity, oversee programs, and prioritize resources for maximizing sales and revenue results.</li>
<li>
Align team with field sales organizations to participate in regular cadence facilitating solution competency and positioning Application Enablement messaging framework in order to maximize sales opportunities.</li>
<li>
Provide program and network tower updates, results, share best practices, uses cases, and solicit feedback on programs.</li>
<li>
Actively participate in sales funnel activity collaboration and forecasts with sales and solution teams. Work with field enablement teams and marketing to continually customize use cases, messaging, targeted personas, playbooks, and sales approach for market segments based on real examples from sales teams.</li>
<li>
Report sales program results, and success action plans to senior leaders on a regular basis highlighting strategic benefits of programs for helping network organization exceed targets</li>
<li>
Drive continuous improvement of sales process providing field sales and customer feedback to product and operations teams in order to make positive changes for accelerating sales and revenue success.</li>
<li>
Collaborate with partner channel team to create joint go to market sales plan for technology partners leveraging collective strength of combined value propositions. Identify and measure milestone metrics as leading indicators of success. Drive joint sales activity leveraging partner and Verizon resources. Key efforts will include coordinating with legal, finance, billing, operations, and other stake holder organizations to address challenges, and drive constant process and sales cycle velocity improvement.</li>
<li>
Create development plans customized to each of team members needs in order to help with professional growth and future promotion opportunities.</li>
<li>
Provide weekly personal feedback and coaching in one on one sessions with team members collaborating with them to solve issues and capture opportunities.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
What we’re looking for...</b>
</p>
<p>You’ll need to have:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Bachelor’s degree in engineering, computer science or four or more years of work experience.</li>
<li>
Six or more years of relevant work experience.</li>
<li>
Six or more years of experience in enterprise technical sales and/or solutions in a Client-facing or IT staff role (including working as a member of a professional services or IT engineering team).</li>
<li>
Five or more years of experience managing senior technical teams.</li>
<li>
Five or more years of experience with key capabilities of similar companies and technologies.</li>
<li>
Experience managing high performance technical and business teams in a fast paced environment.</li>
<li>
Industry relationships with system integrators, service providers, and other industry related firms.</li>
<li>
Strong technical acumen in the SDWAN, next-generation firewall, and unified threat management markets.</li>
<li>
Excellent communication skills, including executive level communications and experience creating proposals, design summaries and presentations.</li>
<li>
Experience and demonstrated success in managing revenue-generating and cost-management initiatives in large organizations.</li>
<li>
Client-facing experience with internal and external audiences at all levels of management.</li>
<li>
Knowledge and comprehension of key solution objectives in regards to business outcomes, value proposition, total cost of ownership, return on investment, process improvement, etc,.</li>
<li>
Ability to quickly learn, understands, and works with new emerging technologies, methodologies and solutions.</li>
</ul>
<p>
Even better if you have:</p>
<ul>
<li>
MA/MS in business or technology.</li>
<li>
Ten or more years of experience in enterprise technical sales and/or solutions in a Client-facing or IT staff role (including working as a member of a professional services or IT engineering team).</li>
<li>
Certifications in relevant industry technologies.</li>
<li>
Experience architecting IT infrastructure for scalable, distributed systems.</li>
<li>
Subject Matter expertise &amp; certifications in Software Defined Networks, Cloud, Security or Unified Communications.</li>
<li>
Advanced design and experience in networking, applications and security.</li>
<li>
Large scale outsourcing/out-tasking experience.</li>
<li>
Willingness to travel.</li>
</ul>
