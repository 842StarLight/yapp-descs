Position Purpose (Summary)
<br><br>
<b>The function of this position is to:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Direct the setup and tear down trade shows with their displays, booths, carpets, and related products and services.</li>
<li>Resolve conflicts and issues that arise between, exhibitor, facility.</li>
<li>Be prepare with a “Plan B” when the situation arises.</li>
<li>Be the “Go To” person as a resource when the unexpected happens.</li>
</ul>
Assistant Foreman/Field Supervisor’s Responsibilities/Duties/Functions/Tasks
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Must be familiar with basic warehouse operations</li>
<li>Event set-up/take-down processes and procedures</li>
<li>Can process paperwork for such tasks.</li>
<li>Be the team leader* Supervising and working with assigned staff to accomplish projects</li>
<li>Maintaining proper records</li>
<li>Reporting their progress and/or completion</li>
<li>Anticipating new tasks or assignments.</li>
<li>Assigns, coordinates and supervises job responsibilities to maximize efficiency and safety.</li>
<li>Establish and maintain good communications with other Managers, Supervisors, staff, clients, exhibitors and other show site personnel.</li>
<li>Transporting equipment and labor to event sites</li>
<li>Moving materials using a variety of Wisconsin Expo‘s company vehicles and equipment.</li>
<li>Driving full-size pickup trucks and /or towing trailers</li>
<li>Operation of powered material handling equipment.</li>
</ul>
