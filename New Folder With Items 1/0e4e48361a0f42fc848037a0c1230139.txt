<h2>Production Planner</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Production Planner- Exempt</b></p>
<div>
<p>EDAC Technologies has long been recognized as a leader in the development and supply of flight critical Aerospace/Defense components and assemblies. Operating out of four state-of-the-art facilities located in Connecticut, EDAC Technologies offers growth and career development opportunities to enrich your talents. We are always seeking to hire talented, hard-working, and committed individuals to work in our E. Windsor location.</p>
<p>
The E. Windsor facility houses EBTEC, and their core business includes Electron Beam welding (EBW) laser cutting (LBC), laser drilling (LDB, laser welding (LBW), abrasive waterjet cutting, plasma coating, and machining. This position is responsible for repairing and maintaining complex machinery and electrical equipment for manufacturing aerospace, power generation, and defense components.</p>
<p>
The primary responsibility of the Production Planner is the realization of the production schedule for our WIP material to meet our customer requirements. Be able to monitor the progress of the production according to the plan and lead projects to improve on-time delivery. Recommend, schedule, maintain inventory stock levels, and follow up work organization improvement projects leading to better efficiency and lead time reductions.</p>
</div>
<p><b>Location: </b>E. Windsor, CT
</p>
<p><b>Reports to: </b>Engineering Manager</p>
<p><b>
Essential Duties &amp; Responsibilities</b></p>
<p><i>
Reasonable accommodations may be made to enable individuals with disabilities to perform.</i></p>
<p>
Division Syteline super-user – working with business to define enhancement.</p>
<p>
Works with users to resolve their queries and issues in existing modules of Syteline.</p>
<p>
Work with customer teams on getting enhancements migrated to the Production environment and validating after the issues are fixed.</p>
<p>
Work with business on reviewing design changes and provide feedback wherever applicable.</p>
<p>
Provide solutions to simplify applications to increase operational excellence.</p>
<p>
Develop User Training documents, plan, coordinate and deliver training sessions.</p>
<p>
Recommend IT process solutions for the improvement of current application functionality.</p>
<p>
Plans and prepares production schedules for manufacture of industrial or commercial products: Draws up master schedule to establish sequence and lead time of each operation to meet shipping dates according to sales forecasts or customer orders.</p>
<p>
Analyzes production specifications and plant capacity data and performs mathematical calculations to determine manufacturing processes, tools, and human capital requirements.</p>
<p>
Plans and schedules workflow for each department and operations according to previously established manufacturing sequences and lead times.</p>
<p>
Confers with department supervisors to determine the status of assigned projects.</p>
<p>
Expedites operations that delay schedules and alters schedules to meet unforeseen conditions.</p>
<p>
Inputs controls, resources and master schedule into Syteline.</p>
<p>
Participate in training as required
</p>
<p>Executes duties as assigned by supervisor</p>
