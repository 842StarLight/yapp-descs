<h2>Senior Enrollment Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<div>iLaw is building an innovative Enrollment Services Team with exciting growth opportunities! We are looking for entrepreneurial and dynamic professionals driven by the mission of making education more accessible to improve lives. Here’s your chance to have an immediate impact and join a mission-driven team that’s growing quickly and partnering with prestigious law schools to bring online innovation to education. We’re looking for smart, articulate, and innovative problems solvers to be the foundation of a great team that’s primed for growth.</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div>
The Senior Enrollment Specialist (SES) will deliver a best-in-class consultative sales and admissions approach that resonates with students and helps them enroll in programs. The SES will also provide leadership and coach team members to delivery best-in-class recruitment activities to achieve the enrollment goals for each of their assigned programs/schools. The SES will recruit and enroll students into iLaw partner graduate programs via phone, social media outreach, webinar/live chat operation, and email. The SES provides outstanding 1:1 consultation with prospective students, and excellent support assisting students with the entire admissions process. Excellent communication skills, the ability to listen and build strong and trusting relationships throughout the entire student’s life-cycle is key.</div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div><b>
Responsibilities include:</b></div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<ul>
<li>Provide a high-touch, concierge level experience with students, while encouraging the pursuit of their educational goals that fit their needs</li>
<li>Meet or exceed stated enrollment goals for assigned partner schools</li>
<li>Quickly become a program subject-matter-expert through continuous process and product knowledge improvement, while representing universities and supporting prospective/continuing students</li>
<li>Execute recruitment measures, ensuring that targeted response times and accuracy guidelines are consistently met.</li>
<li>Provide information to students regarding the availability of classes; enrollment and class status</li>
<li>Perform all functions while complying with all relevant rules and regulations, including the Department of Education Incentive-Based Compensation rules and FERPA (Family Educational Rights and Privacy Act) regulations</li>
<li>Track all recruitment activity on a daily basis and show progress towards recruitment-related goals</li>
<li>Manage projects and drive innovation to help the team succeed and scale</li>
<li>Coach and mentor team members to help improve performance</li>
<li>Participate and lead in staff meetings and training</li>
<li>Share innovative ideas and insights to help the team grow</li>
<li>Help build a culture that’s proactive, positive and fun</li>
<li>Perform other related duties as assigned</li>
</ul>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<div><b>
Qualifications:</b></div>
<div>
<br>
</div>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor's degree preferred or commensurate work experience in admissions</li>
<li>JD and or Graduate degree a plus</li>
<li>Minimum of 3 to 6 year of work experience in sales, student recruitment, preferably in a position focused on graduate higher education recruitment, partnership development, and customer service</li>
<li>Preferred 5 or more years of combined prior experience in inside sales, call center, recruitment, or student enrollment services</li>
<li>Excellent verbal and written communication skills via phone, text, and email.</li>
<li>Ability to be adaptable in a fast-paced dynamic environment</li>
<li>Ability to communicate and collaborate with Law School leaders at the Dean level</li>
<li>Goal-oriented and competitive, setting and achieving, personal and team goals.</li>
<li>Strong expertise with MS Office, specifically Word and Excel, efficient in learning new software.</li>
<li>Experience with social media campaigns to drive results</li>
<li>Ability to connect quickly and authentically with people from a variety of different professional and cultural backgrounds</li>
<li>Strong work ethic and positive attitude, with a willingness to be flexible</li>
<li>Flexibility to be available when students need support and are available to talk (evenings and some weekends)</li>
</ul>
