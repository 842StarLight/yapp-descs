<h2>Business Development Leader</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Local Data Exchange is a start-up working with companies to transform business operations by connecting data sources and making it available for data science, AI, and machine learning models. LDE provides data logistics for intelligent systems. We are passionate about leveraging data for the future of smart business and business automation.</p>
<p>We’re looking for a candidate who is a proven top performer who knows how to successfully approach and close large enterprise sales by leveraging a strong understanding of industry trends and company goals. As a Business Development Leader for Local Data Exchange, this person will evaluate each customer’s business needs and then help craft, articulate, and sell solutions that solve client problems and support their goals. They will help craft the LDE strategy for each market and work closely with our Head of Product on messaging and telling the LDE story.</p>
<p>This person will leverage their experience to build and motivate a strong junior sales team.</p>
<p>This position offers significant growth potential and will be expected to drive revenues against team goals. This role is responsible for building and managing a team and process to support the full sales cycle for large clients. If you are passionate about putting your sales and management skills to good use in an innovative technology startup with accelerating traction, look no further!</p>
<p>Candidates must be located or willing to relocate to San Diego, California. The candidate will report directly to the CEO.</p>
<p>Our team members are given great amounts of responsibility and trust early in their careers. While they work in teams, they often need to "think on their feet," using common sense and flexibility to make quick decisions on their own. They have demanding responsibilities so it is essential that they be energetic. They must know themselves very well and a sense of humor is a plus.</p>
<p>Qualifications:</p>
<ul><li>2+ years experience closing $100k+ deals</li></ul>
<ul><li>2+ years experience managing and building successful teams that can sell, including: hiring, training, and mentoring</li></ul>
<ul><li>Successful history of selling data, analytics, Data Science or AI would be a strong fit for this role, as would a sales professional with experience selling SAAS products to enterprise customers</li></ul>
<ul><li>Experience taking a startup to market and successfully growing sales to $10M.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Experience working for a large enterprise and selling accounts over $1M.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Mastered challenger sale and consultative education approach</li></ul>
<ul><li>Track record of becoming an asset to senior executives by focusing on outcomes and problem solving.</li></ul>
<ul><li>An understanding of the professional motivators of others to inspire a team</li></ul>
<ul><li>You have a deep love for technology and a constant desire to learn new things</li></ul>
<ul><li>You work to your fullest potential in an entrepreneurial environment</li></ul>
<ul><li>You know what it takes to exceed your sales target and are excellent in managing your day to day activities to achieve them</li></ul>
<ul><li>Ability to prioritize and manage multiple needs at once with exceptional organizational skills</li></ul>
<ul><li>BA/BS degree from a top university. BS Degree in fields related to Data Science, Computer Science, Engineering, Management Science, or Math an advantage</li></ul>
<p>Responsibilities:</p>
<ul><li>Close enterprise deals; generate new business through qualified lead building</li></ul>
<ul><li>Compile product/sales pitch decks</li></ul>
<ul><li>Build, lead, and support teams through recruiting, hiring, and development strategies</li></ul>
<ul><li>Support the full sales cycle for large clients</li></ul>
<ul><li>Oversee day-to-day activity of all reps and monitor performance goals while providing ongoing feedback and guidance</li></ul>
<ul><li>Track individual and team metrics for reports to executive leadership on a regular basis</li></ul>
<ul><li>Plan, implement, and optimize strategic sales objectives and policies</li></ul>
<ul><li>Create and maintain workshops focused on challenger sale and consultative education approach, product demonstrations, CRM/sales pipeline management, countering objections, communicating ROI and value propositions, etc.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Inspire and motivate team on a daily basis as well as recognize accomplishments and contributions</li></ul>
<ul><li>Proactively identify business opportunities and work with team to strategize and establish next steps for closing leads</li></ul>
