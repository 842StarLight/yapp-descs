<h2>Inbound Call Specialist 10:30 am - 7:00 pm</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Overview
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>Secures goods and services for all Canon Solutions America retail orders. Work includes reviewing requests, talking to vendors, examining price, suitability, and availability of items or services, and reviewing alternatives to requests to fulfill objectives. Work is performed independently under the general supervision.
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities
</b></h2>
<div><div><ul>
<li>Monitord Supply/Demand of all Resale merchandise and orderd appropriate inventory.</li>
<li>Manages inventory in assigned organizations to minimize purchases and inventory on hand.</li>
<li>Processes Expenditure Purchase Orders for supplies needed by all personnel.</li>
<li>Monitors/analyzes inventory min/max reports to maintain proper re-order points in order to meet inventory level targets.</li>
<li>Works with Order Management and Warehouse staff to ensure orders are using correct inventory item codes.</li>
<li>Communicates to appropriate departments on status of inbound inventory.</li>
<li>Works with Accounting Department to provide appropriate data to close out purchase orders.</li>
<li>Works with Marketing Department to confirm correct processes for non-standard orders submitted to Order Manament Department.</li>
<li>Processes internal requisitions for supplies consumed for office use as well as showroom/demo use.</li>
<li>Provides support and backup for the Inventory Control Department.</li>
<li>Works closely with vendors to ensure timely delivery of all Purchase Orders submitted to them.</li>
<li>Organizes, updates, and retains product information files and purchase order records.
</li>
</ul></div></div>
