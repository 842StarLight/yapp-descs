<h2>Accounting Specialist III</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>How’d you like to work for an organization that truly makes a difference in people’s lives? At Goodwill of North Georgia, the donations we receive are transformed into training that helps people knock down the barriers between them and meaningful work.</p>
<p><b>
What you’ll be doing:
</b></p>
<div><div>
<div>
<div>
<p>As an Accounts Payable Specialist III, you’ll be responsible for processing invoices and expenses in an accurate, timely and efficient manner in addition to maintaining payment documentation and authorization records.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
</div>
</div>
<br>
</div></div>
<ul>
<li>Sort, date stamp, and process all invoices received.</li>
<li>Assign general ledger account coding to vendor invoices.</li>
<li>Verify quantities, price, and payment terms to purchase orders.</li>
<li>Responsible for printing purchase orders and coordinating with supplies when necessary.</li>
<li>Obtain invoice approvals when purchase orders do not exist.</li>
<li>Performs audit of expense reports to company policy.</li>
<li>Troubleshoots issues and works with vendors and internal clients to resolve discrepancies.</li>
<li>Ensures consistent application and compliance with all required accounting policies and controls.</li>
<li>Prepares batch and data entry for review and approval by the department supervisor.</li>
<li>Responsible for verifying and releasing batch totals in the system.</li>
<li>Process and cut check payments and reconcile checks to invoices prior to distribution.</li>
<li>Setup and maintain ACH payments.</li>
<li>Maintain International Organization of Standardization (ISO) nonconformance log.</li>
<li>Compiles, maintains and files sales invoices and other invoices processed for payment to ensure proper record retention.</li>
<li>Maintain backup copies of fixed assets, prepaid and accrued expenses.</li>
<li>Research vendor inquiries for past due invoices.</li>
<li>Serves as second tier contact for escalated accounts payable issues.</li>
<li>Provides training and support to first tier Accounts Payable Specialist.</li>
<li>Perform other duties as assigned by management.</li>
</ul>
<div><div>
<div><div>
<p><b>What we’re looking for:
</b></p>
<div><div>
<div>
<div>
<ul><li>High School Diploma required.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Five to eight (5 - 8) years of experience in Accounts Payable or General Accounting</li></ul>
<ul><li>Proficient in Microsoft Office Suite and accounting software systems.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Should have excellent oral and written communication skills.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Must exhibit a positive attitude and interpersonal skills.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Ability to analyze customer/vendor accounts and resolve problems.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Ability to work independently as well as with a team.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Should be detail oriented and demonstrate initiative.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Should be able to multi-task and adapt to change in a fast-paced environment.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Will need to attain a high-level of customer service and critical thinking skills.</li></ul>
