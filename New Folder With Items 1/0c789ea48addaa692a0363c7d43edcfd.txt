<h2>Regional Coordinator (Internal Wholesaler)</h2>

<p></p>Voya Investment Management (Voya IM) is the asset management business of Voya Financial, a Fortune 500 company with over 6,000 employees seeking to help clients plan, invest and protect their savings. Voya IM manages approximately $220 billion in assets across Fixed Income, Senior Loans, Equities, Multi-Asset Strategies &amp; Solutions, Private Equity, and Real Assets.1 Drawing on over 40 years of experience and the expertise of 250+ investment professionals, Voya IM’s capabilities span traditional products and solutions as well as those that cannot be easily replicated by an index.
<p></p>Voya IM’s award-winning culture is deeply rooted in a client-centric approach to helping investors meet their goals — from insurance companies, corporate and public pension funds, sovereign wealth funds, endowments and foundations, and consultants to intermediaries, and individual investors. Reliability is why our clients hire us and it is why they trust us to navigate the path ahead.
<p></p>
<p>A successful Regional Coordinator will possess very strong product and presentation skills as well as the ability to demonstrate consultative sales skills, such as prospecting, probing and ultimately driving sales through their position on the sales desk.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Key Responsibilities
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Work collaboratively with the Senior Regional Director (External Sales Representative) to maximize territory sales efforts and attain sales goals
</li>
<li>Utilize data segmentation to plan and track targeted Advisors sales efforts
</li>
<li>Manage Financial Advisor relationships via the telephone and “virtual” technology
</li>
<li>Follow up on all sales leads generated
</li>
<li>Take incoming calls from Financial Advisors
</li>
<li>Host webinars, conference calls, roadshows for Financial Advisors and client related events
</li>
<li>Provide competitive analysis, with the ability to have in-depth conversations with Advisors on interpreting the results and positioning Voya products appropriately
</li>
<li>Respond to requests for information, competitive analysis and literature
</li>
<li>Manage the Client Relationship Management (CRM) system for their coverage territory; which includes capturing all advisor level activities, maximize sales targeting and effectiveness and coordinating regional marketing and communications plans
</li>
<li>Ensure all CRM information for their territory is up to date and accurate
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Qualifications
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Must have at least a 4 year degree from an accredited college or university
</li>
<li>Two to five years of customer service/sales experience within the Financial Services industry
</li>
<li>Current Series 7, 63 and 66 licenses strongly preferred. If not currently held, must pass within 60 days of hire
</li>
<li>Candidate should have strong working knowledge of popular sales software and Microsoft Office
</li>
<li>Candidate should be professional, adaptable, collaborative, creative, self-motivated, organized and responsible
</li>
<li>Strong written/verbal communication skills and a strict attention to detail required
</li>
<li>Candidate should demonstrate an upbeat, positive attitude and have an ability to excel in a team environment
</li>
</ul>
