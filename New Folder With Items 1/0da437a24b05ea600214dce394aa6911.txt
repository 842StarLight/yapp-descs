<h2>Account Services Coordinator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p><b>BUILD </b>your career with an organization that is a leader in national advertising focused on local flavor. As part of our team, you will help our clients reach more than 211 million homes each year with the best deals available.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>LIVE </b>your passion through a culture that actively supports community involvement and delivers hope to missing children and their families across the nation through its involvement in the Have You Seen Me? ® Program .<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>CREATE </b>our future by delivering highly relevant, useful and smart content - when and how people need it - to make the best decisions possible in navigating their local community.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Position Summary </b><b>: </b>Under the direction of the Team Leader, the Account Services Coordinator is responsible for delivering quality results and excellent customer service while contributing to the production of Clipper print and digital advertisements and magazines for distribution while meeting time, cost, and quality goals. This position will act as a primary liaison between merchants, sales, and the production staff through ad trafficking and magazine pagination.<br>
<br>
</p>
<p><b>Self-directed work team member characteristics:
</b></p>
<p>These are traits that are not unique to this position but are necessary for all self-directed team members:
</p>
<p>Maintains a high-performance, customer satisfaction-focused attitude. Collaborates and communicates effectively with others to ensure quality results. Monitors and assesses self-performance to make improvements and promote accountability. Looks to enhance the overall effectiveness of the team and involves others in the decision-making process. Pursues continued learning and upgrading skills.<br>
</p>
<p><br>
<b>Key Duties/Responsibilities </b><b>:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Maintain communication with a limited group of sales reps and associated advertisers regarding all aspects of the creative process, including changes and approvals, responding professionally and within a reasonable amount of time. -35%
</li>
<li>Move proofs through the creative process and facilitate the approval of ads by ensuring that all steps are properly completed, working in collaboration with the Production Coordinator. -30%
</li>
<li>Paginate magazines, keeping layouts and associated reporting updated with the most recent changes to content. -15%
</li>
<li>Monitor deadlines with Sales to update allocation changes and ensure timely approvals for all jobs while maintaining communication with team management. -5%
</li>
<li>Maintain business location database of all advertisers to ensure accurate listings on our consumer-facing website, troubleshoot and facilitate resolution of problems when reported. -5%
</li>
<li>Maintain current knowledge of products and Production systems in order to provide related direction and support for customers and sales force as needed. -5%
</li>
<li>Perform functions of the Production Coordinator on an as needed basis, including ad copy entry and review. -5%
</li>
</ul>

<p><b>We are the best local advertising in America with a national reach.<br>

<p></p>
<p>Clipper Magazine is a leading provider of multi-channel, hyper-local advertising and promotional solutions for businesses all over the country. Clipper's distribution includes more than 500 local editions and specialty publications across 28 states, with each issue comprised of money-saving deals and coupons from local merchants as well as great offers from major regional and national businesses. Clipper Magazine's affiliated publications include Mint Magazine ® , Local Flavor ® , REACH Magazine, Maryland Pennysaver, The Flyer, One Brand Marketing and Market Magazine, plus numerous specialty publications. Clipper also operates Total Loyalty Solutions - custom apps and advanced loyalty technology solutions for small and medium sized businesses; Spencer Advertising - a full-service advertising agency; and the online deal and coupon site LocalFlavor.com .<br>
