<h2>Enterprise Software Sales Consultant</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Decisions is a rapidly growing software company (&gt;50% growth/year for the last 3 years) that has just completed a $40MM investment round. We are seeking energetic sales consultants to help continue our successful growth. This is a high activity, highly rewarding and heavily compensated position.</p>
<p>
This position includes excellent, lucrative compensation plan in addition to the base salary. With proven success and demonstrated leadership, comes the opportunity for career advancement within a growing company.</p>
<p>
New team member will be responsible for managing sales opportunities from initial product demo through contract execution.</p>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Responsibilities</b></h2>
<ul>
<li>
Most leads provided by lead development team, but must also be willing to utilize software tools provided to hunt new leads.</li>
<li>
Work within the sales organization “hunting” and prospecting, identifying, qualifying, and developing strategies to win opportunities Utilizing effective methods such as cold-calling.</li>
<li>
Developing and managing sales related projects</li>
<li>
Delivering software demonstrations with sales engineer via web meetings and customer location visits</li>
<li>
Calculating and capturing ROI, responding to RFP/proposal requests, and providing an overall end to end business solution to address the client’s high level business needs.</li>
<li>
The annual quota of $1M in new annual revenue will be achieved by lead generation, consultative solution-based sales abilities, negotiation skills, and diligent follow up and follow through.</li>
</ul>

<li>
20-40% overnight travel to meet with clients or visit headquarters in Chesapeake VA.</li>
<li>
Solution-oriented, consultative sales approach</li>

Proactive, forward looking, and able to maintain a long-term, big-picture view</li>

Must be a team player</li>
