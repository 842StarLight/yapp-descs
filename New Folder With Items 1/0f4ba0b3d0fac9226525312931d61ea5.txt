<p>Description</p>

<p></p>
<div><div>
<p><b>Training/Education</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelors Degree in Marketing, Advertising, Communication or Business required</li>
<li>Valid driver’s license and transportation</li>
<li>Travel required to multiple states</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p>
</p>
<p><b>Experience/Skills
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Minimum of seven years’ experience in the sales/marketing of multifamily properties</li>
<li>Minimum of two years of supervising a team</li>
<li>Demonstrated ability to write, edit, proofread, research and prepare market analysis</li>
<li>Successful track record in CRM, improving lead generation, building and maintaining a cohesive brand, managing campaigns across multiple markets across the US, and ability to build a tech forward, technology driving marking strategy</li>
<li>Management of marketing plans for a portfolio of 40+ properties.</li>
<li>Strong working knowledge of digital marketing strategy, tactics, and best practices.</li>
<li>Track record for designing and executing successful marketing campaigns</li>
<li>Strong computer background in Microsoft Office Suite, Yardi, CRM, Rent Café, Chat Meter, Satisfacts, ILS, Google Suite, Yext</li>
<li>Successful track record with vendor relationships, negotiations and management</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Special Requirements</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Big picture and strategic thinking skills</li>
<li>High degree of professionalism</li>
<li>Transparency, reporting and sharing of information effectively across all departments</li>
<li>Proven record of successful negotiation skills</li>
<li>High degree of experience in budget planning, expense monitoring</li>
<li>Exceptional leadership, management, communication and collaboration skills</li>
<li>Results oriented and solution focused</li>
<li>High degree of accountability and ownership</li>
<li>Excellent judgment and decision making skills</li>
<li>Excellent organizational and time management skills including ability to multitask, prioritize and meet changing deadlines</li>
<li>Excellent internal and external customer service skills</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p><b>
</b></p>
<p><b>Essential Functions</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Develop annual and short-term marketing plans</li>
<li>Define and execute an integrated marketing platform, including but not limited to - digital marketing branding, promotions, social media, website, and online reputation</li>
<li>Assist Operations team and Property Managers with community-level online reputation management by offering oversight, responses and solutions for improved ratings</li>
<li>Research marketing software and new technology for possible game changers based on increased productivity, ease of use, cost effectiveness, or better resident experience</li>
<li>Manage digital marketing touchpoints including website content and messaging, email, social media, Google My Business, Facebook, Instagram, Twitter, etc.</li>
<li>Perform market analysis and manage advertising expenses</li>
<li>Manage marketing vendor relationships</li>
<li>Develop collateral material and signage by working with preferred vendors and ensuring on-time, on-budget, and strategic delivery</li>
<li>Manage marketing-related Help Desk issues</li>
<li>Build reports that track key performance indicators for our communities</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Develops and oversees marketing events, promotions and programs</li>
<li>Reviews shops and surveys of competition to assess competitiveness, pricing, concessions and fee structure and make pricing recommendations</li>
<li>Works with the RVP’s and RPM’s to develop property marketing plans including timetable for marketing/management actions for logo design, complete collateral package, professional/designer selection, lease-up schedule, training staff, establishing preferred employer programs, corporate suites, grand opening, etc., as applicable</li>
<li>Reviews weekly property reports and obtains information on current marketing efforts in order to provide expertise to RVP’s and RPM’s.</li>
<li>Performs marketing audits for properties including analysis of all marketing initiatives, target markets, evaluation of rental personnel, rental levels, critiques of signage, advertising, curb appeal, competition, training needs, etc.</li>
<li>Writes, edits and proofs marketing sales/creative copy and reviews all advertising media to ensure accuracy, effectiveness and compliance with Fair Housing</li>
<li>Manages and reviews property websites for content and analyzes web traffic data</li>
<li>Attends marketing seminars presented by such organizations as the National Apartment Association to ensure that property marketing remains current</li>
</ul>
