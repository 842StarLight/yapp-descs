<p><b>Roles and responsibilities</b></p>
<p>While we prefer you to work from our Manhattan, NYC office, located next to Rockefeller Center, we will hire remote for the right person.</p>
<p>You will be measured on the pipeline inflow you generate and be a key partner with the sales executives.What we need is a driven, creative and can-do Sales Development Representative who will do what it takes to find and convince relevant companies to take meetings with our Sales Execs.</p>
<p><b>Responsibilities include: </b></p>
<p>- Being a lead generation engine supplying the sales team with meetings/discovery calls; mostly cold-calling into target accounts, but also some lead follow-up.</p>
<p>- You'll be required to complete a moderate volume of calls/emails per day – 60-100 depending upon how many meetings you already have booked on your schedule.</p>
<p>- Sales research, building company profiles, finding decision makers, calling into targets and developing the prospect towards a deal.</p>
<p>- Establishing relationships within target organizations to access business opportunities.</p>
<p>- Booking discovery calls and demo meetings for our sales executives.</p>
<p>- Nurturing existing leads to the time when they are ready to engage in evaluation.</p>
<p>- Gather information about leads in the system that are missing details (phone numbers, etc.) from DiscoverOrg or website and update records in CRM. Using Salesforce, Pardot, LinkedIn, other tools to optimize your effectiveness.</p>
<p>- Working closely with sales and SDR team to develop the best script/approach.</p>
<p>- Following-up on email campaigns and other marketing activities.</p>
<p>The existing NYC-based team of SDRs will coach you and energize you to achieve success! You will also work with the North America sales team and marketing team to ensure you bring in highly qualified sales opportunities.</p>
<p>- Demonstrate consistency in meeting quotas and qualifying strong opportunities.</p>
<p>- Be able to present Medius’ value proposition, follow qualification guidelines to BANT (Budget, Authority, Need, Timeline) qualify a prospect to hand off to sales.</p>
<p>- Excellence in articulating ideas and arguments over phone and email.</p>
<p>- Ability to quickly build rapport and connect with strangers in business context.</p>
<p>- Positive attitude with ability to overcome rejection/obstacles and find a way to achieve success.</p>
<p>- Should be comfortable working independently.</p>
<p>- Previous experience (minimum 2 years) with cold calling and lead-gen in a FinTech, SaaS B2B inside sales background. Should be experienced navigating mid-large company hierarchies.</p>
