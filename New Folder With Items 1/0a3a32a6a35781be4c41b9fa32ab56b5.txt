<div>Siemens Smart Infrastructure is currently searching for an experienced account executive to lead business development and sales activities for our IoT Business division. This individual will develop and deliver smart building sales solutions and services targeting existing Siemens customers in addition to growing the customer base. This exciting new role leverages the Enlighted Building IoT platform to lay the foundation of a smart building. In addition to the Enlighted solution, added IoT software applications will be sold to the building owner as a value-added service (SaaS). Collaboration with Siemens branch colleagues will allow additional solutions and services to be added to the overall smart building solution. This IoT Solutions Sales Executive will be based out of our North America Branch locations and report to the Branch Manager and IoT National Sales Manager</div>
