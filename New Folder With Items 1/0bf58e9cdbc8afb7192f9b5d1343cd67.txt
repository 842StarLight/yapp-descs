<b>Job Title</b>: Senior Commercial Finance Analyst - Contigo, Bubba &amp; Coleman</div>
<div>
<b>Reports To</b>: Director, Commercial Finance
</div>
<div>
<b>Location: </b>Chicago, Illinois

<div></div>
<div><b>Job Summary:</b></div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>The Senior Commercial Finance Analyst - Contigo, Bubba &amp; Coleman will support Walmart/WM.com, Costco, Other Club, and Value/Dollar Markets.</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div><b>Key Responsibilities:</b></div>
<div></div>
<ul>
<li>Support the Sales Team to create, own and manage planning, reporting, and forecasting across all customers</li>
<li>
Operate as an integral member of the Commercial organization involved in Day-to-Day Impactful Business Decisions and Processes</li>
<li>
Lead the financial forecasting and evaluation of sales initiatives including customer proposals, terms negotiations, events and post event evaluations</li>
<li>
Effectively makes decisions on more complex issues, considering relevant costs, benefits and risks.</li>
<li>
Use accounting principles and knowledge of internal controls to provide objective, timely, and accurate financial information</li>
<li>
Work with Sales leadership to establish promotional spend strategy and develop customer and program level spending details to support the initiation of that strategy</li>
<li>
Analyze monthly promotional spend actuals by product line to assure Sales Finance forecast is aligned with plan, as well as ensure forecasting reflects changes to phasing</li>
<li>
Work collaboratively with the demand planning team to ensure APO projection align with Sale LE/Forecast</li>
<li>
Provide coaching to sales team to enhance their financial skill sets to ensure both functions are working efficiently and collaboratively
</li>
<li>Participate actively in monthly consensus meeting on items such as volume, trade, and advertising and promotional spending.</li>
<li>
Perform varied ad hoc analyses on the business across stated strategic priorities and metrics versus our Plan Assumptions</li>
<li>
Assist on Customer specific product development (GKM express) as needed</li>
<li>
Ad hoc analytics as needed</li>
</ul>
