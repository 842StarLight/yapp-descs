<h2>Team Lead Retail-Gainesville North (FT)</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Job Description:
</b></p>
<p><b>Goodwill Industries of North Florida</b> is currently seeking <b>Team Leads </b>for our retail stores<b>.</b> The Retail Team Lead is responsible for assisting the Store Manager in the store’s daily operation and achievement of sales results. Manages the processing of donated goods and work with assistant managers to ensure staff is trained and that good customer/donor relations are maintained by setting high standards of customer service.</p>
<p><b>
Our Philosophy: Work is the Cornerstone of Life</b><br>
At Goodwill, we believe that the opportunity to work can - and does - change a person’s life. A job offers the independence that comes with a paycheck, increases self-esteem, and breaks the cycle of poverty. It’s a philosophy that we have been following in North Florida since 1940, when our local organization was founded as one of a growing number of independent non-profit affiliates of what is now Goodwill Industries International. Today, Goodwill Industries is the nation’s largest private provider of training and employment services for people with disabilities and special needs. And our local organization has grown into one of Goodwill’s top performers in placing people into employment.</p>
<p><br>

<b>Job Duties include but not limited to:</b></p>
<ul>
<li><p>
Supervises and is the primary authority concerning processing of donated materials in a timely manner following the production system.
</p></li>
<li><p>Responsible for the store revenue goals and budgeted expenses.
</p></li>
<li><p>Ensures compliance with all store policy and procedures as outlined in the Store Manual, the Employee Handbook, and the Worker’s Compensation manual.
</p></li>
<li><p>Ensures proper accounting for all store revenue.
</p></li>
<li><p>Ensures good customer/donor relations by maintaining high standards of customer service as outlined by the customer service manual.
</p></li>
<li><p>Trains and develops staff in all aspects of their responsibilities, including adherence to all safety and company protocols and procedures promote a safe, productive work environment.
</p></li>
<li><p>Processes customer transactions by ringing the sale on a cash register, collecting payment, making change, bagging the merchandise and thanking the customer.
</p></li>
<li><p>Assists customers with shopping needs.
</p></li>
<li><p>Pulls racks for transporting clothing from the back room to the sales floor and places clothes on sales floor racks. Ensures that clothes are placed on sales racks with similar types and colors of clothing.
</p></li>
<li><p>Rearranges clothing on racks and items on shelves, removes items from the floor, dusts and pulls clothes from racks that are too full to ensure that the sales floor is kept clean, neat and properly displayed.
</p></li>
<li><p>Greets donors and receives merchandise, providing donor with an itemized receipt, if desired.
</p></li>
<li><p>Reports known or suspected security and/or theft problems to the Store Manager or other members of management.
</p></li>
<li><p>May remove donations from donor’s car.
</p></li>
<li><p>Breaks down donation in to appropriate bins of like merchandise.
</p></li>
<li><p>Examines shoes, electrical, and mechanical merchandise and other non-clothing items to determine, appropriateness for sales floor, in accordance with established standards.
</p></li>
<li><p>Place salable clothing on hangers to meet established quotas. Hangs clothing on racks for transporting to the sales floor, keeping similar types of clothing grouped together.
</p></li>
<li><p>Counts and records pieces of clothing hung.
</p></li>
<li>
<p>Places unsellable clothing in appropriate bins.
</p>
