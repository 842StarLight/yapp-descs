<div><b>1. Position Summary:<br>
</b></div>
<div></div>
<p>Technology Partners (TP) is the organization that establishes and manages the Amadeus relationships with third party developers. TP has developed and maintains a program designed to meet the unique commercial, technical and operational needs of the developer community. The key components of the “Amadeus for Developers"" program are interface packaging and delivery, developer support and communications.<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<div><b>2. Job Duties:<br>
</b></div>
<div></div>
<p><i>Business Development &amp; Account Management:
</i></p>
<ul>
<li>Identify, pursue and maintain business relationships
</li>
<li>Promote and sell, through direct contact and events , products, services and solutions that will drive incremental and alternative revenue to Amadeus
</li>
<li>Secure departments revenue goal
</li>
<li>Identify and capture new business opportunities that advance revenue diversification
</li>
<li>Participate in the preparation and definition of the long term plan (sales forecast and solutions based on customer feedback)
</li>
<li>Identify and qualify business opportunities by establishing and developing relationships with top-level executives
</li>
<li>Identify customers’ specific needs and submit business proposals
</li>
<li>Coordinate and participate in RFP/RFI responses and follow-up
</li>
<li>Support Custiomer Solutions teams in Technology Partner engagements
</li>
<li>To evolve, promote and support the Amadeus For Developers program. This program facilitates the pursuit and maintenance of business partnerships and alliance opportunities. These partnerships are strategic and contribute to the team’s achievement of its objectives.
</li>
<li>Collect intelleigence on the travel technology marketplace (market intelligence)
</li>
<li>Gather prospect information
</li>
<li>Conduct Technology Partners analysis and size business opportunities
</li>
<li>Coordinate the alliance process with all the areas involved in the company in order to close the deals
</li>
<li>Negotiate with third parties
</li>
<li>Act as an intermediary between Amadeus and third parties
</li>
<li>Bring to a successful completion the Technology Partner responsibilties within the the Solution Partner process
</li>
<li>Assist Technology Partner customers with the optimization of their access to Amadeus and Amadeus customers implementation and use of their platforms.<br>
</li>
</ul>
