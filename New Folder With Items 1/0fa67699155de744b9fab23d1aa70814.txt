<h2>Lead Product Designer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Lead Product Designer</b></p>
<p>Koneksa Health is a technology company bringing the possibilities of digital sensors and devices into real world clinical trials. Our software is being used by clinical researchers globally to collect data from patients with respiratory disease, cancer, Parkinson's disease and more.</p>
<p>Our software suite consists of native patient applications as well as web-based research portals. We are looking for a product designer who can help us continue to build user-friendly research tools. This will include actively engaging with the technology, product, and client teams to interpret requirements and design the next version of our software. You will be a core part of the team, helping with product design/development as well as customer delivery through the creation of support materials.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Responsibilities</b></p>
<br>
<p>
<br>
Product design:</p>
<ul>
<li>Work with business stakeholders to distill product ideas into designs and features</li>
<li>Designing product features using low-fidelity &amp; high-fidelity prototyping for native mobile and web platforms</li>
<li>Collaborating in creating and maintaining product requirement specifications</li>
<li>Creating and maintaining the Koneksa style guide</li>
</ul>
<p>Web design:</p>
<ul>
<li>Managing the corporate website hosting on the SquareSpace platform</li>
</ul>
<p>Marketing &amp; Graphic Design:</p>
<ul>
<li>Creating sales and materials, including presentations, user guides, company swag, icons, flow-charts, etc.</li>
</ul>
<p>Customer-facing support materials:</p>
<ul>
<li>Creating copy for customer-facing materials (e.g., product videos, user guides, FAQ/ support pages)</li>
<li>Creating customer-facing product videos</li>
<li>Creating data flows and and imagery for user guides</li>
</ul>
<p><br>
<b>Required Qualifications</b></p>
<ul>
<li>At least 5 years experience as a UX/UI Designer</li>
<li>Strong understanding of user-centered design principles</li>
<li>Low-fidelity &amp; high-fidelity prototyping skills</li>
<li>Cross-platform design experience (Web, iOS, etc.)</li>
<li>Experience with end-to-end product design</li>
<li>Experience with agile product development methodologies</li>
<li>Experience collaborating with engineering teams and product managers</li>
<li>Experience with responsive web design</li>
<li>Excellent communication skills - you should be able to clearly articulate your design decisions</li>
<li>Strong understanding of front-end development capabilities</li>
<li>Proficiency in Adobe Creative Suite, Sketch</li>
<li>Familiarity with HTML, CSS &amp; JavaScript (for managing Koneksa website)</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Nice to have:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Familiarity with SquareSpace platform</li>
<li>Experience in creating marketing copy</li>
<li>Video editing experience</li>
<li>Healthcare / pharmaceutical experience</li>
<li>Experience conducting user testing and user interviews</li>
</ul>
