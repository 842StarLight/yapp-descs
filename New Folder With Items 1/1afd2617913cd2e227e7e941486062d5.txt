Currently, we are seeking a qualified <b>PLC Programmer</b> at our division based in Aberdeen, WA.<br>
<br>
<b>About The Position</b><br>
<li>Programming and tuning of multiple axis of motion control.</li>
<br>
<li>Programming and debugging of PLC’s and HMI’s.</li>
<br>
<li>Design control systems used in high performance machines.</li>
<br>
<li>Plan and coordinate new equipment installations.</li>
<br>
<li>Supervise installation and optimization of machinery.</li>
<br>
<li>Identify and implement process improvements.</li>
<br>
<li>Administer modifications of existing PLC programs in conjunction with mill personnel.</li>
<br>
<li>Efficiently manage multiple projects and processes across a large scope of work, simultaneously gauging task priority.</li>
<br>
<li>Train personnel in maintenance and operation of equipment.</li>
<br>
<li>Provide phone support for division technicians for troubleshooting.</li>
<br>
<li>Some overnight travel to divisions within California and Washington.</li>
<br>
<br>
<b>Qualifications</b><br>
<li>Strong working knowledge of motion controllers, PLCs, servo systems, and HMIs.</li>
<br>
<li>Experience with servo and hydraulic motion control and tuning preferred.</li>
<br>
<li>4 years of experience with Allen Bradley Control Logix and Kinetix systems preferred or educational equivalent.</li>
<br>
<li>Machine vision experience and AutoCAD proficiency is a plus.</li>
<br>
<li>Ability to manage time and priorities under demanding circumstances.</li>
<br>
<li>Excellent communication, listening, and reasoning skills.</li>
<br>
<li>Strong math skills.</li>
