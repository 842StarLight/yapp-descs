<h2>Junior Technical Account Manager - West Coast Representative</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>We are ViriCiti; an international clean-tech scale-up working hard on the electric transition of commercial vehicles. We started our adventure in 2012 in Amsterdam and are now adding our 2nd office in the States. We are looking for a hands-on Technical Account Manager with an engineering degree to join our new team in SoCal. In this challenging role, you will be working as an Account Manager for existing and future customers in the Pacific Northwest and Southeastern US. Are you looking for the next step in your sales career and do you have what it takes to bring ViriCiti LLC to the next level? Check if we fit your job expectations and apply!</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
WHAT YOU WILL BE DOING FROM DAY TO DAY</b></p>
<p>
As a Technical Account Manager (aka Sales Engineer) you will be part of the exciting growth phase of ViriCiti LLC in the States as a real pioneer. The West Coast will be your playground where you can use your consultative selling skills to develop and secure new customers. Visiting potential customers, managing sales calls and video conferences are a big part of your job, but there is more. As a SaaS company, upselling and Account Management are an important part of our business model. Not only to generate revenue, but to make sure we are always engaged with our customers and providing the perfect solution to meet their specific requirements. Being part of ViriCiti means you get a lot of freedom to take initiative, but you also need to be able to adjust quickly to new situations and to learn fast. Your position will be key in enabling ViriCiti LLC to continue exponential growth in the States.</p>
