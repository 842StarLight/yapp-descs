<p><b>Job Description</b></p>
<p><b>TruGreen</b> is committed to providing personalized attention for our associates. We focus on developing our people by building proud, dynamic teams while helping associates reach their personal and professional goals. We continuously strive to make TruGreen an employer of choice and "<b>a great place to work!</b>"</p>
<p><b>Position Overview</b></p>
<p>TruGreen Information Technology team is responsible for providing solution supporting next generation IT Solutions that rapidly enable digital transformation. The Cloud Solutions Architect will be responsible for working with our 3rd Party Technology Partners to define, develop and validate Multi-Cloud Solutions that enable achievement of financial and business objectives.</p>
<p><b>Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Ability to break down customer requirements into Solution and Engineering requirements</li>
<li>Document Requirements for Multi-Cloud IT infrastructure Solutions</li>
<li>Analysis of all aspects of the IT market, competitors and feature gaps of existing solutions</li>
<li>Work with our Business Units and 3rd Party Technology Partners to develop joint solutions</li>
<li>Implement Solutions in Lab and execute POC’s to validate designs</li>
<li>Develop business cases that validate technical and commercial feasibility of a joint solution</li>
<li>Work with 3rd Party Technology Partners to package products and services that complement our technology portfolio</li>
<li>Manage cross-department technical team relationships and facilitate collaboration</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Competencies</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Customer Focus/Sales Growth</li>
<li>Results Orientation/Sense of Urgency</li>
<li>Change Mastery</li>
<li>Integrity/Values</li>
<li>Problem Solving and Decision Making</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Education and Experience Requirements</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Engineering or Computer Science degree and 8+ years in IT Software or IT Infrastructure design required</li>
<li>2+ years’ experience implementing Cloud Solutions required.</li>
<li>Experience as an Enterprise Architect preferred</li>
<li>Experience working with customers and BU product team preferred</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Knowledge, Skills, and Abilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Industry expertise with Enterprise infrastructure solution development (compute, networking, storage or Software Defined Data Center concepts).</li>
<li>VMware and IBM XIV and Pure Storage product knowledge is critical.</li>
<li>Ability to document solution reference architectures and deployment guides</li>
<li>Able to develop and articulate business cases</li>
<li>Superior communication skills</li>
<li>Ability to manage cross-department technical relationships</li>
<li>Self-motivated, team player with demonstrated collaboration skills</li>
<li>Attention to detail and accuracy</li>
<li>Written and verbal communication skills</li>
</ul>
