<h2>Social Media Marketing Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>Job Summary:</p>
<p><b>
Unity in Diversity
</b></p>
<p>Our team members are as diverse as our music venues. With its owned/operated/managed venues, Live Nation Clubs and Theaters, a division of Live Nation Entertainment, is wide-ranging, including the legendary Fillmore venues and the intimate House of Blues clubs throughout the United States. Live Nation Clubs and Theaters is the country’s preeminent group of intimate music venues, featuring superior sound and lighting technology in one-of-a-kind custom-designed environments aimed to bring fans and artists together in unparalleled musical environments.</p>
<p></p>
<p>
Our mission is to create a profitable and principled global entertainment company. To celebrate the diversity and brotherhood of world culture. To promote racial and spiritual harmony through love, peace, truth, righteousness and non-violence. Our venues bring fans as close to artists as possible and our aim is to create teams that are just as close.</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Who are you?</b></p>
<p>
Passionate and motivated. Driven, with an entrepreneurial spirit. Resourceful, innovative, forward thinking and committed. At Live Nation Clubs &amp; Theaters, our people embrace these qualities, so if this sounds like you, then please read on!</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>
What You’ll Do</b></p>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Develop and execute a best-in-class social media strategy in collaboration with the Director, Digital Marketing.</li>
<li>Work cross-functionally with other teams in the company such as, Communications, Partnerships, Product, etc., helping to define and execute social initiatives in line with brand strategy</li>
<li>Create monthly content calendars for each social platform.</li>
<li>Conceptualize and produce content tailored to each platform to drive engagement and build brand equity.</li>
<li>Write captions for social media posts consistent with the brand voice and tone.</li>
<li>Develop social media campaigns, from concept to planning to execution to analysis, to support brand initiatives and product launches.</li>
<li>Plan, storyboard, and create Instagram Stories.</li>
<li>Photograph and film content for social as needed.</li>
<li>Determine goals and track performance of brand’s social media platforms and campaigns and provide regular reporting.</li>
<li>Community management, including posting to and responding to customer questions and concerns in an on-brand and well-informed manner.</li>
<li>Regularly review the social and digital work of competitors to stay at the forefront of creative trends.</li>
<li>Strategize, manage, and execute collaborations, partnerships and contests with like-minded brands.</li>
<li>Attend PR, retail, and other events to help capture social media content as needed.</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>
Oversee social media channels throughout life cycle</li>
<li>
Conceive, implement &amp; measure marketing campaigns</li>
<li>
Create and source visual content (photo, video, gifs)</li>
<li>
Use channel insights to develop content strategy</li>
<li>
Manage weekly / monthly content calendars</li>
<li>
Track key performance metrics</li>
<li>
Ability to demonstrate ability for copywriting, campaigns to drive business goals, audience growth, engagement reach and more!</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Who You Are</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Creative with an eye for prestige photo and video content.</li>
<li>Strong writing skills either with prior experience as a journalist or as a writing major in college.</li>
<li>High attention to detail.</li>
<li>Thrive in a fast-paced environment.</li>
<li>Self-starter with the ability to initiate and drive projects to completion with minimal guidance.</li>
<li>Comfortable with ambiguity and wearing many hats.</li>
<li>Scrappy startup mentality. Take action and propose new ideas and solutions before asked.</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>
Proven B2B social media marketing experience</li>
<li>
Strong written and verbal communication</li>
<li>
Impeccable grammar and spelling</li>
<li>
Attention to detail and high standards</li>
<li>
Problem-solving skills</li>
</ul>
<ul><li>Excited to work in a collaborative, fast-paced, high-energy environment.</li></ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Requirements</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s Degree required.</li>
<li>3+ years of social media experience.</li>
<li>Extensive experience with Instagram and Instagram Stories.</li>
<li>Extensive writing experience, including writing for social media.</li>
<li>Experience shooting photos and video for social.</li>
<li>Proficiency in publishing and planning tools with experience creating content calendars.</li>
<li>Experience tracking and reporting on social media performance a must.</li>
<li>Knowledge of Adobe Creative Suite (Photoshop and Illustrator) a plus.</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>
4+ years of experience</li>
<li>
Expert-level knowledge in utilizing Facebook, Instagram, and LinkedIn advertising to grow a B2B business</li>
<li>
Expert-level knowledge in utilizing Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn, YouTube, and Twitter to organically to grow a B2B business</li>
<li>
Ability to use social media scheduling platforms like Sprout Social</li>
<li>
Confidence in using Facebook Business Manager and its tools, such as Facebook Ads Manager and Creator Studio</li>
<li>
Experience managing social media budgets of $100k+ per quarter</li>
<li>
Experience optimizing social media ad campaigns on a daily basis</li>
<li>
Experience working with and communicating regularly with an inbound sales team</li>
<li>
Ability to collaborate with the ad team and other stakeholders to maintain brand alignment</li>
<li>
Understanding and implementing best practices for social media copy and overall creative</li>
<li>
Ability to manage a Social Media Coordinator and lead them through the ever-changing world of social media</li>
<li>
Experience creating and executing a comprehensive social media strategy</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>7+ years of experience in digital or social media role</li>
<li>Highly preferred big brand experience (i.e. Hulu / Netflix)</li>
<li>Expert in developing social strategies native to individual social channels (Twitter, Instagram, Snap, YouTube)</li>
<li>Excellent project management skills</li>
<li>MUST have passion for digital, mobile, social &amp; pop culture</li>
</ul>
