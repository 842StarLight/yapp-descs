<h2>Senior Buyer, Food Colors US</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div></div>
<div>
<div>
<div>The Sr. Buyer role is a member of the Global Category Management Team. This team is responsible for partnering with key stakeholders across all Business Units (BU’s) to develop global sourcing strategies for key categories, establish a best in class global sourcing footprint, act as a center of excellence for their category, actively support new product development, and help the BU’s achieve their goals. This role has responsibility for securing raw materials, packaging, equipment, supplies and services to maintain the operations of the St. Louis manufacturing facility in support of all BU’s supported. As a team member, this position requires micro-execution of tactical spend budget and savings and category strategies to maintain business continuity, growth, cost viability, and maintaining world class quality associated with Sensient’s brand.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
<b>
Responsibilities</b><br>
</div>
<ul>
<li>Develop detailed global category strategies. Categories typically are high spend that impact multiple BU’s.</li>
<li>This position will act as the category buyer for bulk and distribution, packaging, capital equipment, MRO items, and packaging components.</li>
<li>Secure the necessary raw material, equipment, supplies and services in an effort to meet/exceed service level objectives for both internal and external customers</li>
<li>Work collaboratively within Sales and Operation Planning process to enable automated procurement activities and integrate Materials Requirement Planning</li>
<li>Maintain alternate suppliers for Top 50 raw materials; To be identified as an alternate, supplier must be approved and material specifications documented</li>
<li>Meet budgeted spend and leverage economy of scale and strategic negotiations to achieve savings objectives</li>
<li>Meet Cash Conversion Cycle objectives for raw material inventory levels by coordinating with planning on inventory targets for all raw materials</li>
<li>Works cooperatively with internal Quality Control to achieve / maintain approval supplier status for both suppliers and individual materials</li>
<li>Analyze current state of systems, processes, and organization to recommend continuous improvement and enact change</li>
<li>Timely communication and master data updates of raw material costs to finance, supply chain and marketing functions</li>
<li>Coordinate purchasing support to Engineering Department on capital projects</li>
<li>Provide purchasing service and information for other departments and affiliates as required and/or requested</li>
<li>Act as the lead liaison with external service providers for duty/tariff drawback program.</li>
<li>Provide purchasing updates once per month to leadership teams</li>
<li>Coordinate transfer pricing with Sensient affiliates</li>
<li>Maintains department records and validates pricing of invoices</li>
<li>Coordinates with the planning coordinator for return of obsolete and surplus materials, return of rejected materials, and contacts vendors concerning billing errors.</li>
<li>Coordinates delivery of materials through procurement coordinators</li>
<li>Supports procurement needs of the Technical Service / Lab functions as requested</li>
<li>Adheres to spend authority guidelines as published in the company policy<br>
</li>
</ul>
<div>
<b>Education &amp; Experience</b><br>
</div>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree is required;</li>
<li>Required five (5) year minimum experience in Procurement demonstrating knowledge of the principles, concepts and methodology of the profession, along with supplemental complementary training and qualifications.</li>
<li>Required knowledge of ERP systems processes. JD Edwards Enterprise One is highly desirable.</li>
<li>Beneficial is the acquired knowledge of the principles, concepts and methodology of the planning and logistics professions to enable deeper collaboration.<br>
</li>
</ul>
<div><b>About Sensient Colors</b></div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
Sensient Color Group is a leading developer, producer and supplier of natural and synthetic color systems for customers around the globe. The Company's high-performance products play a central role in the manufacture of foods and beverages, cosmetic and pharmaceutical colors and coatings, inks for commercial and consumer printers, and colors for agricultural uses, household cleaners and paper products. The Color Group is located in St. Louis, Missouri.</div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div><b>
About Sensient Technologies Corporation</b></div>
<div></div>
<br>
<div>
Sensient Technologies Corporation is a leading global manufacturer and marketer of colors, flavors, and fragrances. Sensient employs advanced technologies at facilities around theworld to develop specialty food and beverage systems, cosmetic and pharmaceutical systems, inkjet and specialty inks and colors, and other specialty and fine chemicals. The Company's customers include major international manufacturers representing most of the world's best-known brands. Sensient is headquartered in Milwaukee, Wisconsin.</div>
