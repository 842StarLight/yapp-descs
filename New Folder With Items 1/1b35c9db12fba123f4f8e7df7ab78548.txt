<h2>Local Business Executive, Youngstown - The Compass Experiment</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>The Compass Experiment is seeking a creative, dynamic local business executive to forge partnerships with local business owners and drive new revenue streams for our local sites.</p>
<p>
This role is responsible for selling solutions to local partners aimed at helping them with their advertising, marketing and branding needs. This person must be well-versed in digital display advertising but also able to work beyond it, including, but not limited to event sponsorships, branded content and reader revenue.</p>
<p>
The ideal candidate brings a strong understanding of the local marketplace and where our offering fits in. They must be able to tell our story to local partners and encourage them to be a part of what we are building.</p>
<p>
This person will work closely with the central Compass team on strategies to grow our audience and our value to local business partners.</p>
<p>
This role reports to Compass Business Operations Manager and will be based solely in the local community. Local applicants strongly preferred. Previous publishing sales experience is preferred, but not required.</p>
<p>
Duties and responsibilities:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Develop partnerships with local businesses and community organizations to assist them in reaching their goals through comprehensive media solutions.</li>
<li>
Own and execute a solutions-oriented sales plan tailored for the local market.</li>
<li>
Support local partners in self-service advertising as needed.</li>
<li>
Pursue and close new business and revenue streams in a timely fashion.</li>
<li>
Perform cold-contacts with sources and networks.</li>
<li>
Retain, manage, and grow local partnerships over time.</li>
<li>
Identify, monitor and accurately forecast sales activity on a monthly basis.</li>
<li>
Prepare presentations and sales proposals in cooperation with central staff.</li>
<li>
Negotiate terms and conditions with advertisers and sponsors.</li>
<li>
Work collaboratively across the Compass team as well as the larger McClatchy company on refining local revenue strategy.</li>
</ul>
<p>
Skills and Experience:</p>
<ul>
<li>
2 or more years of experience in a sales role with a history of meeting and exceeding sales goals. Digital media sales experience a plus.</li>
<li>
Be a motivated self-starter who can approach the job with an entrepreneurial spirit.</li>
<li>
Ability to identify and understand local market potential.</li>
<li>
Be articulate when presenting our story and products to business decision makers.</li>
<li>
Have good listening and problem-solving skills.</li>
<li>
Ability to communicate with customers on a proactive basis via phone, email and in person.</li>
<li>
Have a solid understanding of the online marketing/advertising industry.</li>
<li>
Comfort conducting face-to-face customer meetings, presentations, proposals and demonstrations.</li>
<li>
Willingness to continuously learn, try new things and adapt to change.</li>
<li>
Proficiency in PowerPoint, Excel and Word (or similar programs).</li>
<li>
Requires dependable transportation and a valid driver’s license.</li>
</ul>
<p>
Benefits and Application:</p>
<p>
All Compass team members will be full-time employees of McClatchy. Employee benefits include 401K and competitive medical, dental and life insurance offerings.</p>
<p>
In your cover letter, please tell us how your professional experience reflects the attributes we’re looking for, and why you’re uniquely suited for this opportunity.<br>
