<h2>Customer Service Representative</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Peerless Umbrella is a leader in umbrella technology and the largest umbrella company in the promotional products industry.</p>
<p>
Our products aren’t plucked from a catalog. We design and manufacture our products to be the highest quality available. From budget to luxury, we aren’t just a company that happens to sell umbrellas. We’re a true umbrella company – and that makes a difference!</p>
<p>
We are seeking a proven, self-motivated, customer service representative with experience in preparing jobs for production as well as being a liaison between sales, customers and the production team.</p>
<p>
Ideal candidate will have a 1-2 years customer service experience; be proficient in computer usage utilizing Microsoft Office and be familiar with ERP and CRM software; have exceptional organizational, multi-tasking and communication skills; be comfortable working in a deadline-oriented environment. Ability to speak Spanish a plus.</p>
<p>
Responsibilities Include:</p>
<ul>
<li>
Entering customer requests into production</li>
<li>
Ensuring finished products are produced correctly and on-time</li>
<li>
Providing exceptional customer service throughout the process</li>
<li>
Assisting customers with production questions, job tracking updates and delivery options</li>
<li>
Supporting sales team</li>
</ul>
<p>Our office is located in Newark, NJ and we offer 401K, 401K company match, vacation/paid PTO.</p>
