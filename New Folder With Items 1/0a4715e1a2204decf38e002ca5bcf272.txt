<h2>Head of Project Management, G Suite</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">Join SADA Systems as the Head of Project Management!
<br><br>
The Head of Project Management will lead the Google Professional Services Project Management Team through successful delivery of enterprise-level engagements, as it pertains to G Suite deployment. You will work on operational challenges by collecting critical input from the experienced Project Managers and shaping tools/procedures to further the collective operational effectiveness of the team. The ideal candidate will be energetic, creative, enthusiastic, intelligent, analytical, collaborative and eager to take on responsibilities of leading a team. She/he will enjoy working in a fast-paced and agile organization and be a part of a creative, exciting, fun, teamwork-oriented.
<br><br>
