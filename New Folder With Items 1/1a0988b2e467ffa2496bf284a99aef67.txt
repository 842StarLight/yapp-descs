 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Title: Senior Accountant (CAS)</p>
<p>Overview:</p>
<p>Premier Napa Valley CPA firm seeks responsible, professional, enthusiastic individual to join our Client<br>Accounting Services Group (CAS). The CAS Team delivers a range of high quality bookkeeping to CFO level services to multiple clients across various industries.</p>
<p>Responsibilities:</p>
<p> Lead all accounting operations for a portfolio of clients including but not limited to A/P, A/R, GL, payroll, budgeting, forecasting, custom reporting, and cashflow management</p>
<p> Month and year-end close processing, including monthly account reconciliations and timely financial reporting</p>
<p> Manage and comply with local, state, and federal government requirements regarding tax filings and payments</p>
<p> Prepare and file annual client 1099s, W-2s, and business property statements<br> Calculate sales tax and file CA sales tax returns<br> Provide consistent, high-quality service across all assignments required<br> Advise clients regarding proper accounting methods, policies and principles<br> Recommend and successfully implement business software and applications when appropriate<br> Onboard new clients<br> Provide training for new clients and client staff in the use of shared software applications<br> Anticipate issues and proactively communicates with team and clients as appropriate<br> Identify client needs and opportunities to improve service delivery<br> Develop and document business processes and accounting policies to maintain and strengthen internal control<br> Collaborate with clients and internal leaders to complete challenging projects<br> Develop functional specialization and/or industry expertise<br> Other projects as assigned</p>
<p>Required Qualifications</p>
<p> Bachelor’s degree in Accounting or Finance<br> 3-4 years of accounting experience<br> Strong communication skills, personable and outgoing, ability to build strong relationships<br> Knowledge of accounting principles and practices<br> Experience with QuickBooks Online<br> Payroll preparation experience, and working knowledge of time &amp; labor laws (Federal &amp; CA)<br> Awareness of CA sales tax regulations<br> Attention to detail and accuracy<br> Willing and able to work overtime as needed<br> Located within 30 minute of Napa CA or willing to relocate. Location assistance may be provided.</p>
<p>Desired</p>
<p> Some business tax knowledge helpful<br> Experience with QuickBooks desktop, Intuit Online Payroll, Bill.com<br> Experience with accounting system implementation preferred</p>

<p>Salary: Competitive salary</p>
<p>Benefits:</p>
<p> Health insurance<br> Dental insurance<br> Vision insurance<br> Retirement plan<br> Paid time off</p>
