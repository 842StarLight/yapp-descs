<p><b>MSP - Senior Systems &amp; Project Lead Engineer</b></p>
<p>
We are a growing IT services firm (MSP) located in Queens that has been in business for over 25 years providing IT service, support, management &amp; consulting services to small/medium size business clients throughout the NYC Metro area.</p>
<p>
We are looking to hire a highly motivated, experienced &amp; talented Senior Systems Engineer for a full-time position that is available immediately.
</p>
<p><b>JOB DESCRIPTION</b><br>
This is a front-line customer facing role that requires solid communications skills, expert troubleshooting &amp; technical abilities. The position has many responsibilities that include but are not limited to the following:</p>
<ul>
<li>Client Support – Provide daily support to our existing clients, including their servers, workstations, storage, backup, DR, firewalls, switches, applications &amp; overall connectivity</li>
<li>Systems Architecture – Work directly with our VP of Technical Services &amp; Sales Team to help architect major infrastructure &amp; system upgrade projects for our clients</li>
<li>Project Lead Engineer – Perform project installations &amp; implementations as a hands-on engineer and be directly responsible for the complete life cycle of the project from inception to client hand off</li>
<li>R&amp;D – Work directly with VP of Technical Services to research, test and evaluate new products &amp; technologies</li>
<li>80% of the work will be performed from our office in Queens, NY</li>
<li>20% of the work will be performed onsite within our clients' offices located throughout the NYC Metro area</li>
</ul>
<p><b>TECHNICAL REQUIREMENTS</b><br>
Must be able to support the following software, hardware &amp; technologies:
</p>
<ul>
<li>Windows Server (all versions)
</li>
<li>Exchange Server (all versions)
</li>
<li>Azure &amp; Office 365</li>
<li>Remote Desktop Server</li>
<li>SANs</li>
<li>VMware &amp; Hyper-V Virtualized Environments</li>
<li>Firewalls, Managed Switches, VPNs &amp; WiFi</li>
<li>Windows Desktops
</li>
</ul>
<p><b>OTHER REQUIREMENTS</b></p>
<ul>
<li>MUST HAVE previous experience working for an MSP</li>
<li>Ability to multitask &amp; work on multiple issues &amp; projects simultaneously</li>
<li>Ability to work independently &amp; with a team</li>
<li>Valid driver's license, car &amp; willingness to travel to local clients’ sites</li>
</ul>
<p><b>SALARY &amp; BENEFITS</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Salary commensurate with experience</li>
<li>Health Insurance, 2 Weeks’ Vacation, 5 PTO Sick/Personal Days, Simple IRA, Reimbursed Expenses</li>
</ul>
