<h2>Sr. Product Manager, Commercial Digital Channels</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<div>Sr. Product Manager, Commercial Digital Channels-1904685</div>
<p></p>
<div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
Description</b></h2>
Description Santander’s Commercial Banking Division is an active provider of capital, treasury management and risk management solutions to thousands of small and mid-sized companies in the northeast and across the country. We also have expertise and provide service to specific industry sectors including real estate, asset based lending, energy, education, health care and government.<div>
<br>
Our clients rely on the strength of Santander’s balance sheet and the degree to which our relationship managers understand their businesses in order to develop the most relevant and compelling solutions. In addition, Santander’s significant international presence in regions around the world uniquely positions us to help companies grow their business, both near and far.</div>
<div>
<br>
Santander’s Corporate Digital Channels product set allows clients to effectively access treasury management and self-service support features. Reporting to the Head of Electronic Channels &amp; Product Development, the product manager will assume product management responsibility for:<br>
<ul>
<li><div>Development of related major enhancements to Santander Link Access portal.</div></li>
<li><div>
Prioritize the expenditure of resources to ensure cost effective development enhancements.</div></li>
<li><div>
Ensure that the appropriate sales and marketing functions are informed of new products and enhancements.</div></li>
</ul>
</div>
<div>
We are looking for a Product Manager with online and mobile banking experience in the financial services/ technology sector. Experience will include product development associated with API-enabled portals serving corporate practitioners.</div>
<div>
<br>
This role is client-facing, it will also lead product UI/UX design, focused on optimizing User Experience and expanding digital capabilities while maintaining security and compliance standards.</div>
<div>
<br>
The ideal candidate will have a strong technical aptitude, experience in corporate banking platforms and software to deliver solutions that simplify experiences for customers and internal clients.</div>
<div>
<br>
Position Duties and Responsibilities</div>
<div>
<ul>
<li><div>
Providing Product strategy and execution leadership for Santander Portals initiatives.</div></li>
<li><div>
Leading cross-functional teams through discovery and design projects for the UX&amp;UI roadmap; negotiating agreements and budgeting decisions with supporting Cash Management Product Managers, Technology, Service, and Marketing.</div></li>
<li><div>
Engage customers to ensure there is a clear and differentiated value proposition including testing of concepts and ideas with our customers working with both our internal team and an external partners.</div></li>
<li><div>
Conduct research on portal and mobile UX to ensure products work well for users; channel/ product gap analysis, competitive research and industry evaluations to extract actionable insights and inform roadmaps.</div></li>
<li><div>
Execute a clear, actionable product strategy roadmap with priorities to deliver optimal results for customers and the business.</div></li>
<li><div>
Collaborate with internal and external partners including Technology, Fraud, Risk, Operations, Customer Servicing, Onboarding, Sales, Marketing.</div></li>
<li><div>
Work within a team of product managers accountable for defining digital product strategy, engagement, and best in class customer experiences.</div></li>
<li><div>
Design and test user interfaces for client applications, interactive prototypes, and visual design elements using methodologies/ criteria (i.e. Agile).</div></li>
<li><div>
Manage risk: create and reinforce the risk culture within the group, and across support functions.</div></li>
</ul>
<i>
At Santander, we value and respect differences in our workforce and strive to increase the diversity of our teams. We actively encourage everyone to apply.</i><br>
</div>

<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Qualifications</b></h2>
<ul>
<li>
Bachelor's Degree, Business or Finance is preferred, or Computer Science. MBA is strongly desired.</li>
<li>
9 to 12 years' Product Management-based experience leading Web channel development (and/or rationalization) efforts for mid-to large Bank or FinTech.</li>
<li>
Intaking requirements and establishing development priorities and providing ongoing Product guidance to the Technology and Business stakeholders.</li>
<li>
Developing and maintaining a Commercial Portals roadmap and ensuring that roadmap-related decisions are informed with a deep understanding of the competition, industry and market trends, key technology trends and internal/external customer requirements.</li>
<li>
Building and defending business cases for key features using strong financial acumen.</li>
<li>
Strong knowledge of competitive, industry, market and technology trends in the Web Portals space.</li>
<li>
Experience mapping customer journeys and translating requirements into Epics and User Stories.</li>
<li>
Able to produce Executive-quality presentations and to interact confidently with Executives at the SVP, EVP levels.</li>
<li>
Experience in managing end-to-end commercialization plans, including defining business objectives and strategies through implementation and analytics.</li>
<li>
Proven experience in using product and marketing to build an organization's revenue, brand and identity.</li>
<li>
Demonstrated ability to manage and drive digital transformations, including data/analytics, technology, user experience, UX/UI, content, etc.</li>
<li>
Highly proficient in Microsoft Office (Word, Powerpoint, Excel, Project, Access, Visio).</li>
<li>
Excellent communication, interpersonal and leadership skills.</li>
<li>
Highly effective facilitation, presentation, planning, organizational and problem-solving skills.</li>
<li>
Experience with SaaS, Cloud-based tools, Content Management Systems (LifeRay), UI (HTML, Java, Ember, etc.) collaboration platforms (i.e Confluence, Jira).</li>
<li>
Specifically looking for candidates with Corporate Cash Management, and/or related Financial/ Technology sector, preferably on business/ product side.</li>
<li>
Direct experience working as a Product Manager within Waterfall, Agile and DevOps development environments.</li>
</ul>
