<b>Help Desk Technician- Part Time</b>
<br>
<br>
Marketstar IT team currently has an opportunity available for a Part-Time Help Desk Technician in Ogden, UT.
<br>
<br>
The Help Desk Technician will be responsible for managing support phone calls and emails. He/she will work effectively with end-users and clients to provide timely solutions to service issues and requests.
<br>
<br>
<b>Key Responsibilities &amp; Attributes for Success:</b>
<br>
<ul>
<li>Respond to support phone calls and emails from employees and clients
</li>
<li>Track, log, and trend issue resolution using Helpdesk ticketing system
</li>
<li>Troubleshoot customers’ technical issues in person and on the phone
</li>
<li>Repair and troubleshoot hardware and network connectivity issues
</li>
<li>Remove old equipment and perform data migration to new machines
</li>
<li>Manage inventory and track assets
</li>
<li>All other duties as assigned by IT management or the Support manager
</li>
</ul>
<br>
<b>What’s required to be considered?</b>
<br>
<ul>
<li>One or more years of hands-on experience with PC hardware and software
</li>
<li>Working knowledge of current Windows OSs and Microsoft Office Suites preferred
</li>
<li>Knowledge of TCP/IP, wireless networks, LAN, WAN, virus protection, email functionality preferred
</li>
<li>Experience with active directory, file servers, and tape backups preferred
</li>
<li>Self-motivated with the ability to follow through on projects in a timely manner
</li>
<li>Professional demeanor; good customer service skills in person and on the phone
</li>
<li>Ability to work a flexible schedule
</li>
<li>Excellent verbal, writing, and technical writing skills
</li>
<li>Excellent problem-solving and organizational skills
</li>
<li>College Degree preferred
</li>
</ul>
<br>
