<h2>Service Desk Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p><b>Position Summary:
</b></p>
<p>The Service Desk Manager maintains a high standard of customer service for the service desk staff, delivering beyond our member’s expectations. This person contributes to member retention, as well as new membership sales. The Service Desk Manager has the responsibility of checking members in and being responsive to member inquiries regarding goods and services while in the club facilities. The Service Desk Manager also responds to members’ comments and suggestions, acting as the clubs the first line of communication with members and creates a friendly and welcoming environment. The Service Desk Manager manages all front desk staff and ensures an exceptional member experience and collaboration between the front desk and other club departments.</p>
<p><b>
Essential Functions:</b></p>
<p>
The responsibilities of the Service Desk Manager include, but are not limited to:</p>
<ul>
<li>Say, “Hi,” and greet members/guests enthusiastically and efficiently handle the check-in process</li>
<li>Schedule member service appointments either in person or by phone and assist with purchase at the point of sale</li>
<li>Be knowledgeable and educate members about all programs and activities offered by Active Wellness</li>
<li>Assist with the maintenance of retail shops and managing of vendor partners</li>
<li>Assist in managing club Operational Standards of Excellence - OSE and completing OSE audits</li>
<li>Recruit, train, and develop the Service Desk Team</li>
<li>Set a schedule for service desk team and be responsible for maintaining staff coverage</li>
<li>Ensures that the club is open on time</li>
<li>Works a minimum of 10 hours per week at the desk</li>
<li>Inventory of food items as well as locker and laundry service</li>
<li>Maintain safety book/ emergency log, message log, and lost and found the ledger</li>
<li>Keep club well stocked and clean</li>
</ul>
</div>
