<h2>Restaurant Aloha / NCR Support Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>Job Description</b></p>
<p>The Aloha/NCR Support Technician will be responsible for supporting and troubleshooting all aspects of the Point of Sale systems, including both new and existing applications. This includes but is not limited to Aloha Table Service, Aloha Configuration Center, and Quick-Service Takeout.</p>
<p>Requirements - The ideal candidate for the Aloha/NCR Technician Specialist position will come from a directly similar hospitality environment and be experienced in Point of Sale (“POS”) configuration and/or troubleshooting, back office application and support.</p>
<p>Respond to and resolve calls regarding questions, problems, or errors about hardware or software by phone or ticketing system</p>
<ul>
<li>Identify, research and resolve technical and operational problems</li>
<li>Effectively handle POS support calls from initiation to resolution, and escalating issues appropriately</li>
<li>Support on-call after hours emergency troubleshooting calls in an on-call rotation</li>
<li>Maintain passwords, data integrity, and file system security</li>
<li>Perform various project related tasks as needed</li>
<li>Configure and support network parameters on user systems</li>
<li>Use and track information using ticketing system</li>
<li>Monitor and respond quickly and effectively to end-user requests</li>
<li>Document recurring issues and resolutions for efficient handling of future incidents across the team</li>
<li>Troubleshoot and diagnose hardware, software, network problems, performance of systems affecting end-users and provide solutions.</li>
<li>Desktop, Server, and network troubleshooting and support</li>
<li>Answers phones and performs other administrative duties</li>
<li>Works in front line positions as required</li>
<li>Ability to work well under pressure and in a fast-paced environment</li>
<li>Other duties as needed or assigned</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Experiences and Competencies</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Must be able to work different shifts, including Saturday and Sundays</li>
<li>Excellent communication skills (written and verbal)</li>
<li>Strong computer skills (i.e. excel, word, windows-based products, client/server environment).</li>
<li>Self-starter, self-motivated and capable of working independently or within a team</li>
<li>Strong organizational and time management skills and able to handle multiple projects and tasks</li>
<li>Positive attitude and ability to handle stress well in a highly changing environment</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Preferred Qualifications</b></p>
<ul>
<li>1+ year of experience in a full-service hospitality environment</li>
<li>1+ year working with a Cloud POS</li>
<li>1+ year Aloha/NCR POS experience</li>
<li>1+ year of back office experience</li>
</ul>
<p>Job Type: Full-time</p>
<p>Experience:</p>
<ul><li>relevant: 1 year (Preferred)</li></ul>

<ul>
<li>Health insurance</li>

</ul>
