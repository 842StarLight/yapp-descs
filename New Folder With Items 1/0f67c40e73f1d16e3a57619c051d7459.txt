<h2>Call Center Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>***THIS POSITION WILL BE RELOCATING TO OUR FLOWERY BRANCH BUILDING AT THE END OF THE YEAR***</b></p>
<p><b>Position Summary</b></p>
<p>We are looking for a skilled Call center manager to supervise daily operations and personnel aiming for maximum efficiency and cost-effectiveness. You will ensure that technology is utilized to a maximum and that staff are well-organized and productive. An excellent call center manager must be an organized, reliable and results-driven professional. They must have a practical mind to solve problems on the spot partnered with an ability to see the “big picture” and make improvements. As a call center manager, you must have excellent customer service and communication skills. The goal is to do everything possible to attain goals and achieve great results for our company. Reports to the Operations Manager and must be able to motivate, develop and drive a Call Center team. This involves ensuring individual agent and team success through continuous evaluation, constructive coaching and results-oriented motivation.</p>
<p><b>Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Develop objectives for the call center’s day-to-day activities</li>
<li>Conduct effective resource planning to maximize the productivity of resources (people, technology etc.)</li>
<li>Collect and analyze call-center statistics (sales rates, costs, customer service metrics etc.)</li>
<li>Assume responsibility of budgeting and tracking expenses</li>
<li>Hire, coach and provide training to personnel to maintain high customer service standards</li>
<li>Monitor and improve ordering, telephone handling and other procedures</li>
<li>Evaluate performance with key metrics (accuracy, call-waiting time etc.)</li>
<li>Prepare reports for different departments or upper management</li>
<li>Manage Call Center operations within company policies and procedures.</li>
<li>Assist with developing strategies and ongoing training programs.</li>
<li>Responsible for achieving and maintaining client’s contractual service levels.</li>
<li>Responsible for the successful day-to-day management, administration and reporting of all call center business activities including, but not limited to, adhering to budget and ensuring proper staffing at all times.</li>
<li>Conduct employee performance reviews.</li>
<li>Fosters a well-trained and motivated staff.</li>
<li>Identifies and resolves escalated customer issues.</li>
<li>Position requires great flexibility and availability due to varied work schedule.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Skills</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Proven experience as call center manager or similar position</li>
<li>Experience in customer service is required</li>
<li>Knowledge of performance evaluation and customer service metrics</li>
<li>Proficient in MS Office and call center equipment/software programs</li>
<li>Outstanding communication and interpersonal skills</li>
<li>Excellent organizational and leadership skills with a problem-solving ability</li>
<li>Positive and patient</li>
<li>Certified Call Center Manager (e.g. CCCM) or equivalent qualification is a plus</li>
<li>Strong management experience and leadership skills.</li>
<li>Strong verbal and written communication skills</li>
<li>5 or more years of supervisory experience</li>
<li>Proven ability to demonstrate the following skills: problem-solving, priority-setting, planning, delegation, directing others, analysis, managing/measuring work, reviewing direct reports, motivating others, building effective teams, managing diversity.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Physical Demands: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Required to sit and talk on the phone for extended lengths of time.</li>
<li>Occasional assembly of products may require kneeling and or squatting.</li>
<li>Some callers can be extremely demanding and create very stressful phone conversations.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Work Conditions: </b></p>
<ul><li>Normal office conditions.</li></ul>
<p>Job Type: Full-time</p>
<p>Experience:</p>
<ul>
<li>relevant: 5 years (Required)</li>
<li>Call Center: 5 years (Required)</li>
<li>Call Center Management: 5 years (Required)</li>
</ul>
