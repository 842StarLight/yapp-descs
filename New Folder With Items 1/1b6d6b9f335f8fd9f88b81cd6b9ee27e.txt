<h2>Residential Remodeling Project Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>Arquity is a locally owned company that is changing the face of real estate in it’s own backyard. With Arizona being one of the fastest growing states in the US, our company is positioned for exponential growth.</p>
<p><b>About Us: </b></p>
<p>Arquity is the pre-sale home renovation company that helps homeowners make signiﬁcantly more money on the sale of their home, without the hassle or upfront costs of working with a traditional home improvement company.</p>
<p>We transform properties into the move-in-ready listing buyers are looking for, and homeowners don’t pay anything until they sell their home.</p>
<p>We are seeking Project Managers to run residential remodeling construction projects. We strive to create a streamlined and supportive team environment so you feel set for success and can focus on what you love the most, building.</p>
<p><b>About You: </b></p>
<p>You ensure that projects exceed quality expectations because you won’t accept anything less. You have superior attention to detail and are a master of communication. You thrive on planning and strategy while finding a solution for problems before they arise.</p>
<p><b>About the Job: </b></p>
<p>This is a full-time, salaried position. The Project Manager is the anchor and backbone of every successful project managing the daily on-site work, monitoring subcontractors’ performance, and working closely with the Site Superintendent to assure that QC standards, as well as financial and scheduling goals, are met. This means the ideal candidate takes “pride of ownership” and is not just an accomplished builder, but also a dedicated problem solver.</p>
<p><b>Responsibilities (including, but not limited to): </b></p>
<p>Review &amp; learn all project construction documents. Accurately prepare takeoffs and layout, as required.</p>
<p>Prepare and maintain course of construction production schedule; effectively communicate with homeowners and subcontractors. Schedule all Inspections, as required.</p>
<p>Provide suggested solutions and options to production and design team, as required. Attend all on-site meetings with team.</p>
<p>Effectively liaison with outside interested parties (i.e., neighbors, HOAs, etc.).</p>
<p>Perform administrative duties relevant to each project (Daily Logs, RFIs, Submittals, etc.); manage all relevant project information. Document “as-built” &amp; “unforeseen” conditions.</p>
<p>Communicate and implement company policies and procedures, including</p>
<p>IIPP/Safety directives. Be familiar with OSHA requirements; maintain jobsite cleanliness; attend tailgate safety meetings.</p>
<p><b>Position Requirements: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>The ideal candidate will have a <b>minimum of 5 years progressive experience</b> (1 -2 years in a supervisory role) in residential construction.</li>
<li>Must be a licensed and bonded remodeling contractor.</li>
<li>Excellent &amp; demonstrable communication skills (verbal &amp; written); fluent in English,</li>
</ul>
<p>Spanish speaker a plus.</p>
<ul>
<li>Self-starter and directed, but also a team player/builder. Strong interpersonal skills, with emphasis on tact, discretion, and confidentiality.</li>
<li>Knowledge of relevant building trades, and current building codes/permit requirements.</li>
<li>Computer proficiency: Excel, Project or Fastrack, Word, email; familiarity with BuilderTrend software a plus.</li>
<li>Ability to manage high-pressure situations, prioritizing competing issues, &amp; concurrent deadlines</li>
</ul>
