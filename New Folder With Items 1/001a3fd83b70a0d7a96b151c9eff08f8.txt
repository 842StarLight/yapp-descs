<h2 class="jobSectionHeader">
<br>
<b>Job Description
</b>
</h2>
<p>The successful candidate will be directly responsible for entry of sales orders and all related aspects as well as shipment scheduling and support of related accounting processes for Avtec, LLC with the goal of supporting the revenue generation processes of the business. The Order Entry / Accounting / Scheduling Analyst will assist in the audit, administration and on-going development of processes, systems, and documentation required to meet the objectives of the position. The Order Entry / Accounting / Scheduling Analyst at Avtec, LLC will have the opportunity to work with many departments allowing for quick impact to the business and the opportunity to learn general business practices and strategies.<br>
</p>
<p><br>
<b>Duties
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Primary Order Entry specialist requiring attention to detail, understanding of product offering, and general contractual elements
</li>
<li>Direct and Assist Sales with follow up to ensure timely Order Fulfillment
</li>
<li>Work with Shipping and Receiving to schedule shipment and staging activity
</li>
<li>Utilizes all current company accounting and financial tools and recommends or develops financial tools or methods for adoption/use
</li>
<li>Assist with Monthly Close Cycle
</li>
<li>Support on other Accounting functions as required
</li>
<li>Other Duties as Assigned
</li>
</ul>
