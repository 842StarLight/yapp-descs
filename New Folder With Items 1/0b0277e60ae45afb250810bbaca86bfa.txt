<h2>CNC Programmer</h2>

<p>Schupan's 50-year formula for success has been simple: to treat their employees and customers as they would wish to be treated. Honored with various community-based and national awards, Schupan leads the list of top companies to work for and to do business with.</p>
<p>
Please read the position description below and send us your completed application and resume if you're interested in a fast-paced, family oriented environment.</p>
<p><b>
SUMMARY</b></p>
<p>
The CNC Programmer is responsible for programming equipment to perform production tasks, developing programs to control machining or processing of parts, determining production settings, selecting production equipment, and conducting test runs.</p>
<p><b>
ESSENTIAL FUNCTIONS</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Assist and support A&amp;B in building and implementing new processes and new part program launches.</li>
<li>
Accurately quotes machined parts to customers in a timely fashion.</li>
<li>
Assists in the analysis and justification for new and or additional equipment.</li>
<li>
Ensures continued improvements and justifications to current machine practices and/or procedures.</li>
<li>
Assists in the supervision of Set Up personnel, Manufacturing Technician and tool room operators to ensure timely coordination and effective and accurate execution of assigned customer jobs.Complies with mandated ISO quality procedures.</li>
<li>
Program and machine new fixturing using CAD and CAM software and CNC machines.</li>
<li>
Design and build custom vise jaws and simple fixtures for part production.</li>
<li>
Set up new CNC machines and pallet changers to be ready for production.</li>
<li>
Process improvements on existing parts that include new fixturing, part programs, and</li>
<li>
custom tooling.</li>
<li>
Maintain existing workholding that includes making new fixturing and vise jaws to repair any that are worn or damaged.</li>
<li>
Work with vendors/suppliers to purchase consumable and custom tooling.</li>
<li>
Assist quality personnel</li>
<li>
Assist sales personnel.</li>
<li>
Assist maintenance personnel.</li>
<li>
Assist scheduling personnel.</li>
<li>
Train and assist CNC operators.</li>
<li>
Available to provide set up personnel with evening and weekend support.</li>
<li>
Occasionally lift, move, or position materials weighing up to 50 lbs.</li>
<li>
Maintains regular and predictable attendance.</li>
<li>
Exhibits behaviors that reflect the company core values: Teamwork, Integrity, Tenacity,</li>
<li>
Compassion &amp; Diversity</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
REQUIRED EDUCATION AND EXPERIENCE</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
3-5 years of experience with MAZAK, HAAS, &amp; FANUK cntrols</li>
<li>
Solid understanding of CADCAM/Mastercam</li>
<li>
Previous experience with SolidWorks.</li>
<li>
Knowledge of tooling and tooling capabilities.</li>
</ul>
