<h2>Ohio Valley Regional Sales Director</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div><div>
<p>The Regional Sales Director will report to the Regional VP of Sales to be the primary customer relationship owner, responsible for developing and executing on both strategic and tactical sales plans. As a vital member of the US Sales Team, based in the Ohio Valley, the Regional Sales Director is responsible for prospecting and penetrating new accounts to identify new revenue opportunities. In addition, responsibilities include aligning the appropriate company resources to enable success at the account.</p>
 <p>Roles and Responsibilities</p>
<ul>
 <li>Develop and maintain sales plan for assigned territory of large enterprise customers and prospects.</li>
 <li>Meet or exceed assigned sales objectives.</li>
 <li>Build new licensed revenue within the territory.</li>
 <li>Use Customer Centric Selling methodology to sell enterprise data security solutions.</li>
 <li>Acquire and integrate industry and vertical market knowledge related to general trends, emerging technologies, and competitors.</li>
 <li>Develop and maintain opportunity pipeline that drives sales activities, ensuring that revenue goals are exceeded.</li>
 <li>Disciplined and accurate reporting of opportunity pipeline and sales activities into Salesforce.com.</li>
 <li>Maintain and drive high level of sales activities.</li>
 <li>Develop strategy and sales plan for each opportunity, align and coordinate all resources to execute the sales plan and progress the opportunity to closure.</li>
 <li>Execute each sales opportunity in accordance with PKWARE’s Customer Centric Selling sales process, including: opportunity plans; customer needs assessment; customer correspondence; meeting and conference reports; weekly opportunity reviews; tailored presentations and demonstrations; solution proposals; term sheets and contract negotiation.</li>
</ul>
 <p>Experience Requirements</p>
<ul>
 <li>Minimum of 10 years of sales experience with a successful track record in security software sales involving complex selling solutions to Fortune 500 firms</li>
 <li>Successful track record selling into PKWARE’s key verticals, such as Banking and Financial Services, Insurance, Healthcare, Retail, Telecomm and IT</li>
</ul>
 <p>Key Skills</p>
<ul>
 <li>Results orientation with a high level of energy, persistence, discipline, and desire to do whatever it takes to win</li>
 <li>Proven ability to gain access to, build, and maintain strategic relationships with C-level, senior business IT executives and business unit heads</li>
 <li>Ability to identify customer needs and business outcomes, to determine and uniquely position recommended solution</li>
 <li>Knowledge of enterprise security</li>
 <li>Organized, with well developed verbal, written and telephone communication skills</li>
 <li>Proficient at identifying, engaging and developing a coach and coaching network to gain access to power</li>
</ul>
 <p>Up to 50% travel and based in Ohio Valley</p>

 <p>PKWARE, Inc., is…</p>
 <p>PKWARE has been a trusted leader in global business data protection and encryption for 30 years. PKWARE's solutions keep data safe from internal and external cybersecurity threats for more than 30,000 enterprise customers, including 200 government entities. Its software-defined solutions provide cost-effective and easy-to-implement protection that is transparent to end-users and simple for IT to administer and control. PKWARE is a privately held company based in Milwaukee, WI with additional offices in New York, Dayton Ohio and the United Kingdom.</p>
