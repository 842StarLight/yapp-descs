<h2>LEAD QUALIFICATION SPEACIALIST</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>OVERVIEW</b></p>
<br>
<p><br>
Primary personnel responsible for the qualification of customers – prospects who utilize Sonny’s Consulting services. Supports the New Business Development team in the completion of all departmental tasks and responsibilities. Communicates with internal and external partners to ensure that customer needs have been met and exceeded. Positively promotes and represents Sonny’s through various mediums including social media, special events, trade shows, group presentations, and industry associations.<br>
<br>
Job Category: Hourly<br>
Department: New Business Development<br>
Reports To: Director of Sales<br>
Hours: 8:00am – 5:00pm or 9:00am – 6:00pm. Staggered shifts, additional hours as needed.<br>
<br>
<b>ESSENTIAL DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES</b></p>
<br>
<div>
<br>
<ul>
<li>Follow-up and qualify all inbound lead contacts requesting information or asking for consultation or site evaluation services within established service level guidelines</li>
<li>Proactively manage inbound leads to drive new business opportunities and build a solid lead pipeline for the sales team</li>
<li>Communicate with customers regarding their site evaluation needs and ensure that all required information needed for the consulting process is collected and relayed to the appropriate team members</li>
<li>Promote Sonny’s The Car Wash Factory and Sonny’s One Wash™, the industry’s only complete suite of car wash business solutions, to customers and prospects as the partner of choice in the car wash industry.</li>
<li>Update and maintain car wash site database and share information as appropriate with the sales and new business development teams</li>
<li>Generate demographic reports, determine traffic counts, review site images, and analyze other information in order to properly prepare a car wash site analysis</li>
<li>Conduct site evaluations as part of the consultation process by taking customer provided locations and analyzing them through Sonny’s proprietary business model to determine their financial viability as a car wash site. This involves a qualitative and quantitative analysis of each site that includes the collection of local city information, evaluation of nearby businesses, demographic reviews, forecasting, and financial projections.</li>
<li>Ensure that all demographic report and information requests are completed in a timely manner per established service guidelines</li>
<li>Schedule appointments and arrange factory tours for customers with the consulting team and other members of the Sonny’s team</li>
<li>Create and present detailed reports, spreadsheets, and presentations regarding the lead pipeline, work volume, and other related new business development activities.</li>
<li>Timely enter lead card information and contacts into the CRM database and assign to the appropriate personnel in sales and new business development</li>
<li>Ensure only qualified leads, as per Sonny’s guidelines, are referred to internal and external partners</li>
<li>Utilize the CRM system to manage all leads, contacts, activities, notes, documents, and other related information to organize all new business development activities</li>
<li>Update and maintain the lead database housed within CRM to ensure collected customer information is complete, accurate, categorized, and appropriately stored</li>
<li>Develop a thorough understanding and familiarity with all Sonny’s collateral material, website information, and advertisements</li>
<li>Stay in tune with the marketplace by understanding customer’s evolving needs, continuously evaluating Sonny’s competitors, learning about new products, and understanding automotive trends that may be relevant to car washing</li>
<li>Build, encourage, and maintain long-term relationships with all internal partners, external partners, and key stakeholders</li>
<li>Other duties as assigned</li>
</ul>
</div>
