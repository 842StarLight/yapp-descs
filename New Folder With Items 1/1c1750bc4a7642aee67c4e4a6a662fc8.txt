Securian Financial has consistently measured strength in human as well as financial terms. The continued success of our Company relies on the strength of our energetic, goal-oriented and focused team of Internal Wholesalers who contribute to the bottom line by driving sales results while balancing the ongoing multitasking and challenges that go along with sales.</p>

<div><div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
What you would do in this job:</b></h2>
<div>
<div></div>
<div>
<ul>
<li><p>Create and identify sales opportunities through outbound and inbound calls.</p></li>
<li><p>Work closely with external wholesaler to grow, schedule meetings and diversify the distribution for assigned territory.</p></li>
<li><p>Profile new advisor relationships to target the appropriate prospects.</p></li>
<li><p>Provide intermediaries advisors with sales/marketing concepts and ideas.</p></li>
<li><p>Communicate effectively to external and internal customers by answering incoming calls, asking appropriate questions and accurately disseminating information about individual annuity products.</p></li>
<li><p>Analyze competition data and position our products in a favorable manner.</p></li>
<li><p>Utilize systems, technology and tools to track activities, fulfill requests, and profile advisor data.</p></li>
<li><p>Prepare proposal materials and run illustrations.</p></li>
</ul>
</div>
</div>
</div></div>
<div><div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>
What we'd expect from you:</b></h2>
<div>
<div></div>
<div>
<ul>
<li><p>Personable, energetic, persistent and passionate about sales, with the ability to stay on the phone for lengthy periods of time.</p></li>
<li><p>Excellent interpersonal, communication and persuasion skills to deliver effective sales presentations.</p></li>
<li><p>Technical skills using spreadsheets and sales related software.</p></li>
<li><p>Sales or product marketing experience in a business- to- business or corporate business setting.</p></li>
<li><p>FINRA 6 and 63 and life/health/variable annuity licenses preferred. If not, ability to obtain within first three months of employment. In addition to FINRA exams, fingerprinting and background checks are conducted.</p></li>
</ul>
