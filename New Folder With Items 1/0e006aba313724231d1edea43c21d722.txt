<h2>Director, Retail Marketing</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p>The Director of Retail Marketing is responsible for growing enVista’s Retail Practice and Unified Commerce SaaS Platform. You will plan, execute and measure enVista’s marketing messages and strategies for our Retail Practice and our Unified Commerce Platform working in collaboration with Campaigns, Communications and Digital teams. You will grow and optimize the awareness of enVista’s Retail Practice and Unified Commerce Platform and measurably increase sales leads through thought leadership, collateral, events/speaking engagements, campaigns and more. You will deliver innovative, intelligent, and inviting marketing strategies, research and collateral that drive awareness, lead growth, and revenue to support the sales and marketing goals at enVista. This role will report directly to the Vice President VP, Retail Marketing.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
What you will do:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Craft and develop innovative, intelligent, and inviting marketing strategies that drive awareness, lead growth, and revenue to support the sales and marketing goals at enVista</li>
<li>
Collaborate with Campaigns, Communications and Digital to flesh out strategies in to measurable and timely campaigns and tactics</li>
<li>
Develop a consumer and retail executive research agenda and execute survey fielding, analyze results and write reports on the findings</li>
<li>
Concept and develop (write) thought leadership in formats such as White Papers, Articles, Surveys, Presentations, Infographics and more</li>
<li>
Develop dynamic digital content for website, blog, etc.</li>
<li>
Work well under pressure as you juggle requests from multiple stakeholders, while managing and prioritizing competing projects</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
What you will bring:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Strong marketing acumen and understanding of integrated marketing practices/principles, and understands their application to systems</li>
<li>
Excellent communication skills verbal, written, presentation</li>
<li>
 Strong project (and people) management skills to ensure flawless campaign delivery support</li>
<li>
 Great at building relationships with internal and external business partners with the ability to advise and influence decisions at all levels of the organization</li>
<li>
 Ability to think strategically to recommend improvements for individual campaigns that will best leverage our marketing and sales activities</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Prerequisites:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Education (preferred level): Master’s in Marketing, Communications or related</li>
<li>
Deep level of proficiency in Retail Operations</li>
<li>
10-15 years of progressive marketing experience, preferably with software/SaaS experience in the retail technology industry</li>
</ul>
