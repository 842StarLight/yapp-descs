<h2>Implementation Project Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Utilant is dedicated to providing innovative, software applications to the insurance industry for both residential and commercial use. One of our applications captures inspection data and distributes it to the parties that make underwriting decisions; the other is a sketching software for real estate appraisers and insurance inspectors. We continue to expand the software capabilities, customize the application(s) for our global customer base and provide support services on an on-going basis.
</p>
<p>The Implementation Project Manager is responsible for leading the implementation of our LC360 software for new and existing clients. During the implementation, the Project Manager will direct and lead a cross-functional team of subject matter experts and will be responsible for the successful and on-time completion of the project and the contractual deliverables.</p>
<p>
The ideal candidate has experience with custom software implementations for large and complex organizations, applying leadership, rigor and discipline around establishing, documenting and communicating project plans; effectively coordinating resources, monitoring and reporting progress and intervening as necessary to ensure that quality, schedule, budget and other requirements are met.</p>
<p><b>
Position responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li> Lead and monitor the full implementation process from initiation to deployment to ensure all deliverables are met according to contract</li>
<li>Build and manage client relationships</li>
<li>Manage multiple client implementations and projects/tasks simultaneously</li>
<li>Assess &amp; evaluate new client requirements against the scope of the project</li>
<li>Manage project schedules, budgets, resources and tasks to ensure that quality solutions are delivered within time and cost constraints</li>
<li>Use ticket tracking system to document client/project needs and manage these through the workflow to completion</li>
<li>Identify, document, and manage project risks during implementations; escalate issues as appropriate</li>
<li>Work with Business Analysts/Solution Engineers to translate business needs into technical requirements</li>
<li>Follow the Utilant project implementation methodology using our existing project management tools and other tools as necessary to guide and ensure customer decision making on required implementation issues</li>
<li>Contribute to the continuous improvement of the Utilant project implementation methodology by ensuring that best practices and decisions are documented throughout the implementation</li>
<li>Prepare project status reports and presentations to keep management, customers and others informed of the project status and issues</li>
<li>Leading and monitoring quality assurance across the full implementation process to ensure all deliverables, User Acceptance Testing, client training and final deployment to production are met according to contract</li>
<li>Work closely in partnership with the Utilant Sales team &amp; Account Managers to ensure internal handoffs are clean and complete</li>
<li>Serve as a subject matter expert in our major product Loss Control 360 User Interface</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Preferred skills:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Demonstrated skillset in planning, executing and delivering projects on time and on budget</li>
<li>Excellent organizational &amp; time management skills</li>
<li>Self-motivated and able to provide results with minimal supervision</li>
<li>Impeccable attention to detail and follow through</li>
<li>Outstanding interpersonal skills, including strong communication skills, both verbal and written</li>
<li>Ability to work effectively in a fast paced, high energy, team-oriented environment</li>
<li>Ability to multi-task and perform effectively under pressure</li>
<li>Critical thinking, ability to challenge others and drive productivity, while maintaining strong business relationships</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Experience &amp; Education:</b></p>
<ul>
<li> Bachelors’ degree in a technology or business major
 </li>
<li>2-3 years’ experience with large-scale project management
 </li>
<li>Experience with Waterfall and Agile approaches to software development and implementation preferred</li>
<li> Experience working directly with large/complex clients preferred</li>
<li> Strong Experience with Microsoft Suite, including MS Project or equivalent</li>
</ul>

 </li>
