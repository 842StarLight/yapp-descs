<h2>Director of Aircraft Scheduling and Vendor Relations</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>The Director of Aircraft Scheduling and Vendor Relation serves as the direct point of contact for both domestic and international aviation companies. The position is responsible for fostering strong partnerships for outsourced lift providing resources to the sourcing team to find appropriate aircraft for the needs of the operation. Working directly with vendors, negotiating preferred terms and pricing as well ensuring operating standards are maintained. This position will also require direct sourcing when needed and will be responsible for database management relating to PJS preferred operators.
</b></p>
<p><b>Essential Functions:
</b></p>
<p><b>Draft and negotiate strategic sourcing agreements.
</b></p>
<p><b>Create and maintain strategic partnerships.
</b></p>
<p><b>Evaluating sourcing needs relative to new and existing sales activity.
</b></p>
<p><b>Ensure operating standards are documented and maintained.
</b></p>
<p><b>Design, implement and manage reporting metrics on team-wide KPIs.
</b></p>
<p><b>Evaluate current and future demand to ensure adequate support.
</b></p>
<p><b>Create an opportunity to improve efficiencies and maximize margins.
</b></p>
<p><b>Efficiently source aircraft based on charter requirements or Jet Card demand.
</b></p>
<p><b>Create and maintain a database of preferred vendors.
</b></p>
<p><b>Performance reviews and training.
</b></p>
<p><b>Competency:
</b></p>
<p><b>Excellent written and verbal communication.
</b></p>
<p><b>Ability to draft and negotiate contracts.
</b></p>
<p><b>Proficient in Excel, Word, CRM software.
</b></p>
<p><b>Must have strong leadership skills.
</b></p>
<p><b>Attention to detail, highly organized.
</b></p>
<p><b>High degree of confidentiality and discretion
</b></p>
<p><b>Ability to act as an ambassador of the PJS brand to help grow company culture.
</b></p>
<p><b>Supervisory Responsibilities: Directly lead a team of 3 or more aircraft sourcing specialists and schedulers.
</b></p>
<p><b>Work Environment: This position is based out of the Seabrook, NH office headquarters.
</b></p>
<p><b>Position Type: This is an office based job, assigned to one of the established work shifts within Flight Operations which may include weekend and evening hours as needed. Frequent work hours beyond 40 hours per week.
</b></p>
<p><b>Travel: 15-20% Vendor visits, Trade Shows
</b></p>
<p><b>Required Education / Experience: Bachelor’s Degree Minimum 5 years experience negotiating, scheduling, sourcing FAR - 135 executive jet flights. Requires prior knowledge of FAR - 135 industry “Jet Card” market and industry wholesale pricing models. 10+ years experience preferable.</b></p>
</div></div>
