<h2>Manager, Paid Social Strategy & Media Buying</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p><b>About the Team</b></p>
<p>
You'll be part of our in-house Paid Social team that is responsible for the strategy, execution, and optimization of paid social campaigns for our 7 Brands. Our job is to drive strong sales on Paid Social platforms in a brand enhancing way. We partner closely with Facebook and other partners to support our brand teams in staying at the forefront of the ever-evolving paid social space.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
About the Role</b></p>
<p>
In the Paid Social Manager role, you will oversee the execution and optimization of paid social campaigns for all 7 Brands and international websites by furthering our in-house capability. Candidates must be willing to work directly in social buying tools(s) and will develop the near and long term strategies in order to increase revenue, site traffic, and conversion and maximize return on ad spend. Responsibilities will focus on Facebook campaigns as well as emerging platforms such as Snapchat. Candidates must have expertise with social media planning and buying processes as well as using first party data for targeting. Additionally, the role will oversee the performance and career development of a team of 3 analysts.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
You're excited about this opportunity because you will...</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Lead in-house management of Facebook (&amp; other social platform such as Snapchat) campaigns across various tactics (site retargeting, first-party CRM data retargeting, prospecting) whilst hitting or exceeding program KPIs</li>
<li>Proactively lead team to track campaign pacing and performance, ensuring accurate and timely delivery of budgets</li>
<li>Manage budgets closely, ensuring spend is aligned with seasonal performance and weekly and monthly targets met</li>
<li>Identify opportunities for new tactics/improved ROI and forecast investment and return, working directly with Brand Customer Relationship Management teams to fund</li>
<li>Be a platform expert, familiar with all optimization levers and features of both the 3rd party social advertising tool and Facebook Ads Manager</li>
<li>Track, analyze, and provide regular reporting/updates on KPIs</li>
<li>Work with IT and feed manager to drive dynamic creative</li>
<li>Collaborate with IT, Ad Operations, on site tagging, creative tag delivery, campaign strategy/execution, and reporting</li>
<li>Collaborate with Analytics and Database Teams to leverage customer segments for specialized targeting and on advanced attribution reporting</li>
<li>Ensure WSI stays at the forefront of Facebook advertising and keep up-to-date with industry trends and be a subject matter expert on social for WSI</li>
<li>Educate brand partners on best practices, platform and process changes, and new strategies</li>
<li>Create presentations on performance, strategy and industry updates and share with various levels of WSI organization</li>
<li>Manage the career objectives and work of 3 marketing analysts</li>
<br>
</ul>
