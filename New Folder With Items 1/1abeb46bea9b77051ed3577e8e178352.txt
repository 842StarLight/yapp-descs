We are seeking resumes for a <b>Corrugated Business Associate</b> (Production) in our Los Angeles, CA facility.<br>

<div>
<b>Description of role</b>: The incumbent is expected to exercise solid judgment in prioritizing projects and determining how the department’s limited resources can be utilized for maximum return. The individual is expected to have strong communication skills and the ability to influence colleagues to ensure desired outcomes; individual also needs to interact with the Plant General Managers, Sales Managers and Sales Reps on an ongoing basis. The incumbent is expected to be able to prioritize requests and ensure the department does not miss any customer driven deadlines. This position is a part of the GIFT program (Generational Investment for Tomorrow).<br>
<br>
</div>
<div>
<b>What is PCA’s GIFT Program?</b> PCA GIFT is a comprehensive training program designed to give the incumbent experience in all departments of a containerboard plant and the packaging industry as a whole. Departments include Production, Design, Sales, Accounting, Customer Service and Quality. The training lasts anywhere from 10-14 months. At the end of the training, the incumbent transitions into a full-time position either at the sponsoring plant, or another plant that has an opening in the field they desire to pursue. The program is designed to help build bench strength across all departments for future management and growth opportunities.<br>
