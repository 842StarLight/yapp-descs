Verified First is looking for an ambitious Partner Relationship Manager and Recruiter to significantly expand our client base. The successful individual in this position must be able to identify, cultivate, and negotiate closer business relationships with our mid and enterprise level market partners. This position will work within the Channel Partner Team, ensuring effective recruitment and management of our partners, managing the lead process so that leads are sent to the correct account executives and driving towards deeper, more exclusive partner relationships.
<br><br>
<b>Job Responsibilities:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Analyze, review and target a specific list of mid-market and enterprise level partners to recruit and onboard to the Verified First Partner program.</li>
<li>Manage lead generation, grow the revenue stream, and increase pipeline and offer advice/support to partners on how to increase volumes;</li>
<li>Assist partners with larger business opportunities and deals, including involvement in presentations and proposals to key stakeholders;</li>
<li>Support the overall growth target of partner revenue;</li>
<li>Build strong rapport and relationships with our partners' organizations at multiple levels from front line support &amp; sales to C suite individuals;</li>
<li>Serve as a liaison with other Verified First departments to advocate the needs of partners;</li>
<li>Attain sales targets for nationwide channel partner sales;</li>
<li>Deliver resources to the partners via the partner portal including marketing packages, content, and webinars;</li>
<li>Work within Allbound, our partner portal, to facilitate greater partner sales activity and encourage partners sales teams to use the tool.</li>
<li>Facilitate partner sales and services training sessions online, over the phone, and in-person;</li>
<li>Deliver partner marketing plans with assistance from the channel partner marketing team;</li>
<li>Assist partners in selling to their customers while ensuring they comply with contractual terms;</li>
<li>Work with marketing to develop materials needed by partners to sell products;</li>
<li>Track total leads generated and estimate resulting revenue;</li>
<li>Travel to visit partners on site and at trade shows as necessary;</li>
<li>Performs other duties as needed.</li>
</ul>

<ul>
<li>Predictable and reliable attendance/punctuality required;</li>
<li>Comfortable with meeting sales objectives and managing the pipeline.</li>
<li>Creation of strategic and tactical plans for each partner.</li>
<li>Ability to apply and execute marketing activities that grow partner activity and output.</li>
<li>Able to self manage and critique own work.</li>
<li>Strong independent judgment as it pertains to client operations;</li>
<li>Creative in nature with the ability to think outside of the box while remaining one step ahead of the problem;</li>
<li>Business acumen with the ability to develop marketing and sales plans that achieve the goals of the business;</li>
<li>Ability to create annual plans for partners that define how those business goals will be accomplished;</li>
<li>Ability to be held accountable to department and organizational numbers, data, and deadlines;</li>
<li>Strong interpersonal skills with an outgoing, friendly disposition;</li>
<li>Must be able to stay professional at all times while working in a fast paced environment;</li>
<li>Ability to multitask, quickly pivot, be process-oriented, with high attention to detail;</li>
<li>Have a team-based approach, lead with a servant-based mentality, entrepreneurial spirit;</li>
<li>Highly organized with the ability to prioritize exceptionally well;</li>
<li>Excellent problem-solving skills, including active listening, identifying the issue, and recommending solutions, often of a customized approach.</li>
