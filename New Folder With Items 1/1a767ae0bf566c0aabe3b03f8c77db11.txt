<p><b>Role and Responsibilities
</b></p>
<p></p>
<p>This position will be responsible for the 4G/5G System Test with emphasis on the RAN CP and OAM features. He or She will perform complex system verification, and customer support of Samsung 4G/5G RAN Equipment including virtual EMS, vRAN. The candidate will provide technical guidance to the junior team members to ensure deliverables are met.
</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Specific responsibilities include:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Perform complex system verification, and customer support of Samsung 4G and 5G RAN Equipment including virtual EMS, vRAN
</li>
<li>Perform feature validation, interoperability testing, field evaluation to get product acceptance from customer
</li>
<li>Aid in product &amp; feature performance analysis, evaluation of new product &amp; SW releases
</li>
<li>Ensures that customer commitments are achieved according to project plan
</li>
<li>Work with team members to prepare test plans/cases and execute to validate RAN CP/OAM functionality
</li>
<li>Interface proactively with Product/Technology Engineers to ensure comprehensive test coverage
</li>
<li>Responsible for analyzing the customer requirements, developing test strategy for features and functionalities, converting customer requirements into test plans and detailed test procedures
</li>
<li>Educate our customers on Samsung’s new products and services both individually and in a team environment. Ensures that customer commitments are achieved according to plan.
</li>
<li>Works closely with various support organizations, logistics, and cross- functional teams from SEA and Samsung HQ to achieve successful new product roll-outs and developments
</li>
<li>Ability to travel sporadically during the project; up to 20%
</li>
</ul>

<p><b>Skills and Qualifications
</b></p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Background &amp; Competencies Required:
</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Must: 10+ years of relevant industry experiences with BSEE or BSCS or 8+ years with MSEE or MSCS
</li>
<li>Bachelor’s Degree is EE/CS is required. Masters in EE/CS is beneficial
</li>
<li>Must: Excellent verbal and written communication skills
</li>
<li>Must: Some travel required for customer presentations, design reviews, and onsite debugging.
</li>
<li>Must: Hands on ability to establish and demonstrate new technology.
</li>
<li>Must have PC and UNIX Workstation experience in a LAN/WAN systems environment
</li>
<li>Strong knowledge in 5G/4G air interface and call processing technologies and network architecture specifically focused on Radio Access Network
</li>
<li>Good understanding of overall Wireless systems with emphasis on PHY, MAC, RLC, RRC and PDCP layers
</li>
<li>Must have understanding on wireless concepts such as Hybrid Beamforming, Massive MIMO, New Waveforms, LTE-NR interworking &amp; co-existence, 5G Numerologies etc. .
</li>
<li>Experience with system engineering activities such as feature requirements identification, feature description documentation, parameter optimization, performance analysis, test strategy creation, problem troubleshooting is desirable
</li>
<li>Have in-depth technical engineering experience including wireless telecommunications engineering activities.
</li>
<li>Must be able to write, read, analyze, and interpret technical journals and procedures utilizing mathematics language, non-linear communications, vectors, complex numbers and logarithms as reference.
</li>
<li>Must: Have a good understanding of the overall 5G/LTE RAN Network Architecture.
</li>
<li>Must: Experience with product testing, development of Test Plan and Test Cases
</li>
<li>Must: Technical lead working with team members and providing technical guidance
</li>
<li>Must: Working knowledge and experience using RF test equipment
</li>
<li>Must: Thorough understanding of SW development cycle
</li>
<li>Must: Ability to support market issues to meet commercial SLAs.
</li>
<li>Must: Hands on experience with mobility management &amp; mobile broadband Radios.
</li>
<li>Must: Working knowledge of 5G/LTE protocol stacks
</li>
</ul>
