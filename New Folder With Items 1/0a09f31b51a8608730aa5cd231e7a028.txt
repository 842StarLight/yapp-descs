<h2>Senior Revenue Analyst</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>Job Description<br>
<br>
<p><b>Role</b><br>
Tradeshift is looking for a seasoned Revenue Analyst to support the adoption of ASC 606 and implementation of an advanced revenue recognition tool, RevPro</p>
<p><b>
What a day is like:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Review sales arrangements to validate bookings and ensure proper recognition of revenue</li>
<li>
Apply appropriate treatment of revenue recognition by identifying, documenting, and verifying areas of risk and recommending improvements under ASC 606 to improve effectiveness</li>
<li>
Understand revenue recognition differences between US GAAP and IFRS</li>
<li>
Support the development and documentation of accounting conclusions and associated accounting policy; reevaluate key assumptions on an as needed basis</li>
<li>
Work to continuously improve accounting and reporting processes, improve system efficiencies and help streamline/automate procedures</li>
<li>
Build relationships and liaise across business groups including finance, sales, sales ops and legal</li>
<li>
Perform month/quarter/annual close activities which include flux analysis, calculating metrics, preparing journal entries, performing account reconciliation, preparing reports and resolving issues as needed</li>
<li>
Prepare journal entries, reconciliations and supporting schedules related to revenue; support month end close</li>
<li>
Work with FP&amp;A and M&amp;A to provide support on special requests, developing new financial schedules and management reporting as needed</li>
<li>
Help with and implement processes for special projects as needed</li>
<li>
Support external audit readiness and requests as required</li>
</ul>
</div>
<br>
<div>Qualifications<br>
<br>
<p><b>You’re perfect for this role if you:</b></p>
<p>
You have technical and practical knowledge of revenue recognition accounting standards ASC 605 and ASC 606 a plus. You have experience with RevPro revenue automation software or Netsuite ERP also a plus</p>
<p><b>
You also have:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Bachelor's degree in Accounting, Finance or related</li>
<li>
Experience at an enterprise software company with both product and service revenue; SaaS/subscription highly desirable.</li>
<li>
4+ years of accounting experience</li>
<li>
An ability to efficiently provide high-quality work in a fast-paced environment</li>
<li>
Strong analytical skills, problem solver, detailed oriented and appreciation for accuracy</li>
<li>
Very strong attention to detail and a passion for digging into processes to better understand them and communicate the big picture</li>
<li>
Ability to communicate complex scenarios and topics in a clear and ingestible manner</li>
<li>
Ability to research and apply applicable accounting guidance</li>
<li>
Proven ability to identify inefficiencies, re-engineer processes, prepare an action plan, and execute</li>

</ul>
