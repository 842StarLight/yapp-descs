<h2>Graphic Designer/Customer Service Representative</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">Do you have a high drive for success? Are you looking for an opportunity to work and learn new skills in a fast paced environment? If so, Alphagraphics Wilmington could be the fit for you as our new Graphic Designer/Customer Service Representative.
<br><br>
AlphaGraphics locations in Wilmington and Newark are locally owned marketing and visual communications companies that specializes in print, mailing services, signs and banners amongst other products.
<br><br>
As a graphic designer at AlphaGraphics, you will be using the latest printing technology to help our clients effectively create and produce business cards, brochures, booklets, high-quality banners, wall decals, promotional items and much more! Every day brings a new opportunity to expand your skills in this exciting fast past industry.
<br><br>
<b>Responsibilities:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Creative, ability to interpret customer, Sales and Marketing Consultants, Center Owner and marketing requests; turns ideas into tangible products.</li>
<li>Communicate effectively with Customers and Internal team in writing, orally and over the phone.</li>
<li>Produces artwork/proofs based upon customer/management needs and specifications in conjunction with materials they provide or otherwise.</li>
<li>Answer customer calls/emails and learn how to provide estimates and convert estimates to jobs</li>
<li>Notifies customers when proofs are ready or when order is complete.</li>
<li>Experience in print production preferred.</li>
<li>Plan, analyze, and create visual solutions</li>
<li>Create print or electronic media using color, type, illustration, photography, animation, and other various techniques</li>
</ul>

<ul>

<li>Ability to Multi-Task Efficiently</li>
<li>Professional appearance &amp; conduct</li>
<li>Attention to Detail</li>
