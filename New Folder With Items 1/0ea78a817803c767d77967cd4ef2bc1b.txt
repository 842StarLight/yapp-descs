<p>Title: Copywriter</p>
<p>
Location: Dallas, Texas (near Uptown area)</p>
<p>
Reports into: Marketing Team</p>
<p>
The role of Copywriter will be responsible for creating digital and print content for rewardStyle customers including but not limited to training &amp; education, thought leadership, industry strategy papers, blog posts, email subject lines and body copy, conference titles &amp; descriptions, social media posts, consumer messaging, taglines, brand positioning and propositions, etc. This role is key for expanding rewardStyle’s creative team to further increase customer interaction and performance.</p>
<p>
The candidate will be highly investigative and full of new ideas, ready to own the development and maintenance of the master content calendar. S/He will remain current on today’s trends, best editorial practices, industry insight and communication vehicles for consumption. This role requires strong written, verbal, and listening skills as well as an extreme attention to detail and thoughtfulness. Proven experience creating strategic online content and a true understanding of how to encourage customers to ENGAGE with our brand is a must.</p>
<p><b>
Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Deliver all copy needs across audience on time and on brand</li>
<li>Create messaging that can transcends from a website or digital advertising campaign to other facets of the digital landscape</li>
<li>Create work that drives consumer engagement and wins industry acknowledgment</li>
<li>Write and edit short form, marketing-type (ads or taglines) as well as long form (writing articles, or more blog-type writing).</li>
<li>Write, edit, and proofread all customer-facing print and digital materials</li>
<li>Collaborate with a creative team to maintain cohesion in campaigns</li>
<li>Effectively absorb the tone and quickly and efficiently weave through communications</li>
<li>Collaborate on and ideate around compelling, inspiring, emotive messages that are on brand tone, voice and experience</li>
<li>Develop brand narrative and storytelling across our audiences</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Qualification</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree in Communications, English, Journalism or related field</li>
<li>Versed in digital and audio capabilities</li>
<li>Proven experience creating engaging online content</li>
<li>Creative, investigative, full of new ideas</li>
<li>5+ years experience writing creative, editorial and technical content</li>
<li>Transition seamlessly between different writing styles, depending on the audience</li>
<li>Exceptional communication skills and highly organized</li>
<li>Ability to manage multiple projects and handle stress in a fast-paced, deadline-driven environment</li>
<li>Proven ability to work effectively within cross-departmental teams</li>
<li>Versed in customer behavior and understand how to engage them with copy</li>
<li>Be scrappy and self-directed - you'll have to be the roll-up-your sleeves type and work autonomously</li>
<li>Must be very comfortable with continuously changing opportunities and priorities</li>
<li>Proactive with curiosity and desire to learn.</li>
<li>A “can-do” attitude and superb interpersonal skills, both verbal and written, are an absolute must</li>
