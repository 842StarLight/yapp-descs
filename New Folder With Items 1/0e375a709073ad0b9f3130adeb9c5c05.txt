<h2>Patient Services Coordinator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>· Accept phone calls from potential patients who are inquiring about FST, our Physicians, and our reproductive medicine services and convert those calls to new patient appointments.</p>
<p>· Be a “Patient Advocate” and remain available to those patients who call you with questions, concerns, complaints, etc. regarding their experience at FST; help the patient in anyway you can at the time of the call, and pass on all the information about the call to the Director of Marketing.</p>
<p>· Promote patient satisfaction by giving feedback to Practice management regarding ways FST can improve operations-based services to better address patients’ expectations, schedule appointments, develop referral sources, etc.</p>
<p>· Stay informed regarding all FST market/advertising campaigns and the role of the New Patient Coordinator in accepting related inquiries by telephone and email, especially since those inquiries should transition to new patient appointments.</p>
<p>· Contribute to team effort by maintaining an excellent rapport and working relationship with Patient Services and supporting front desk activity as needed; cooperate with other FST departments as needed.</p>
<p>· Sitting for long periods of time; minimum standing and stooping. Grasping and feeling are necessary body movements.</p>
<p>· Sensory requirements of speech, touch, vision, and hearing to normal range, with or without correction.</p>
<p>· Requires manual and finger dexterity and eye-hand coordination for easy and skillful use of hands when filing, using the telephone, computer, and other office equipment.</p>
<p>· Associate Degree in Business, Marketing, Healthcare, or related field preferred. High School Diploma and applicable work experience will be considered in lieu of degree.</p>
<p>· Administrative or clinical leadership experience in a multi-physician specialty practice preferred. Women’s Healthcare experience a big plus.</p>
<p>· Experience in medical/intangible service sales with demonstrated results or telephone sales another big plus.</p>
<p>· Excellent interpersonal skills. Empathic.</p>
<p>· Organized with excellent time management skills.</p>
<p>· Ability to access, input, and retrieve information from a computer. Medical software program experience required. Excel a plus. Keyboard skills 25 wpm required</p>
<p>· Must have the ability to work successfully under stressful conditions and must be capable of adapting to varying workloads and work assignments on a frequent basis.</p>
<p>· Must have the ability to make sound, independent judgments based on medical reimbursement protocols, and be able to collaborate with other multidisciplinary team members in an appropriate fashion.</p>
<p>· Must have the ability to comprehend and perform oral and written instructions and procedures.</p>
<p>· Must have effective reading and comprehension skills.</p>
<p>· Must have strong communication skills, written and verbal to interact with people of all professional and social levels.</p>
<p>· Ability to remain calm in emotional or stressful situations.</p>
<p>· High level listening skills. Ability to communicate accurately and concisely.</p>
<p>· Aptitude to work independently and demonstrate good judgment</p>
<p>· Must be able to work overtime as needed and work some holidays.</p>
