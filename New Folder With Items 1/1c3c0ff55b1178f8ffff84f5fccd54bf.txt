 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Overview
</b></h2>
<div><div><div>
<div>The primary purpose of this position is to provide administrative and event coordination support to the Community Relations Director.</div>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Responsibilities
</b></h2>
<div><div><div><ul>
<li>Assists with maintaining current database of existing and potential referral sources, professional contacts, and sales inquiries.
</li>
<li>Act as a backup to the Community Relations Director, assisting with organizing events for prospects, their families, and professional referral sources.</li>
<li>
Facilitates a team player partnership with co-workers. Shows a passion for serving seniors with tact and compassion while helping families in difficult situations. Displays proper etiquette and mannerisms that reflect the mission and values of The Episcopal Church Home.</li>
<li>
Records and maintains medical and administrative information in accordance with established charting and documentation policies and procedures.</li>
<li>
Prepares inquiry and admission packets, assists with organizing and copying the required documentation for resident admissions and transfers.</li>
<li>
Assists with creation and maintenance of community model apartment(s).
</li>
<li>Partners with Maintenance and Housekeeping teams to ensure apartment readiness prior to resident admissions.</li>
<li>
Contribute to maintaining ECHâ€™s occupancy goals.</li>
<li>
Assists with on-site and off-site marketing events and efforts as needed.</li>
<li>
Exhibits strong interpersonal communication and customer service skills with residents and staff.
</li>
<li>Attends in-services, education sessions, and other meetings as expected, maintaining compliance with all regulatory requirements.</li>
<li>
Perform any other related work as directed by immediate supervisor.
</li>
</ul></div></div></div>
