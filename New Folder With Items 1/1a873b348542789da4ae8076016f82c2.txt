<h2>Sales Development Representative</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div></div>
<div><div>
<p>The Sales Development Representative (SDR) is responsible for prospecting to generate leads and opportunities for the Account Executives. This is a great opportunity to join SQLstream sales organization and begin the path towards becoming an Account Executive.</p>
<p>
The Sales Development Representative (SDR) is responsible for prospecting to generate leads and opportunities for Sales. This is a great opportunity to join SQLstream sales organization and be a critical part of the sales organization as we grow our team.</p>
<p>
We want you to be successful so it will require a clear vision, passion, and a desire to be part of a dynamic business. SQLstream is a high-performance environment so you must have a self-starter attitude and continually strive to achieve over quota. Sales performance will be rewarded with bonuses.</p>
<p><b>
Job details</b></p>
<ul><li>Uncover, qualify, nurture, and build the sales opportunity pipeline for the Enterprise.</li></ul>
<ul><li>50% calling marketing-generated (inbound) leads in primarily $100M+ accounts to qualify and schedule meetings for the Enterprise Sale’s Lead.</li></ul>
<ul><li>50% calling companies (outbound) to schedule meetings for the Enterprise Software sales teams.</li></ul>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Desired skills and experience</b></p>
<ul><li>A hunter mentality to find and cultivate new business.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Bachelor degree or equivalent years of experience preferred.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Experience and previous success with prospecting for the Enterprise Software market and selling via phone.</li></ul>
<ul><li>At least 2-3 years of demand generation or inside sales experience with Enterprise Software sales or related organization.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Proven success in a role with weekly, monthly, and quarterly targets.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Strong capacity to develop relationships quickly over the phone.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Skilled at prospecting for new business by discovering need for SQLstream.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Comfortable with detailed conversations both at a business and a technical level.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Strong organizational and time management skills.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Excellent communication and interpersonal skills with the ability and skills to develop relationships quickly.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Strong written and verbal communication, time management skills, and team orientation.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Experience with tools such as SalesForce.com and “standard” inside sales tools.</li></ul>
