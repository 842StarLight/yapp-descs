<h2>Pre-Sales Engineer - Naples, FL</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p><b>Job Summary</b></p>
<p>
Adaptive Computing is seeking to fill the position of PRE-SALES ENGINEER in the Naples, FL office. The Pre-sales Engineer will collaborate with the sales account team by acting as a technical expert in customer presentations. In addition, this individual will determine the technical requirements to meet client goals and act as liaison between the organization’s sales/business development and engineering groups. They will also respond to requests for information or requests for proposals from customers, supplying the technical details of proposed solutions.</p>
<p><b>
Responsibilities and Duties</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Present HPC solutions in a professional manner to customers in-person, via email,<br>
and by phone</li>
<li>Create and present real-time demonstrations both in person and via webinars</li>
<li>Must be able to run both command-line centric and GUI-centric interfaces. Must be able to proficiently answer, at a deep level, all common High-Performance Computing (HPC) technical questions that arise in the demonstrations and evaluation process</li>
<li>Work with customers to determine customer needs and current situation</li>
<li>Develop proposed solutions supported by Adaptive Computing products</li>
<li>Estimate level of effort and timelines for proposed solutions</li>
<li>Effectively present solutions to customers and the market</li>
<li>Coordinate the transition between pre-sales specifications and implementation engineering once contracts have been awarded</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Requirements of Education and Experience</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree in computer science, information technology, information systems, or a related field</li>
<li>Experience in software technical sales at the C-level</li>
<li>Experience in Linux scripting, systems design, software design theory and networking</li>
<li>Good understanding of infrastructure, operations and cloud computing</li>
<li>Ability to communicate effectively both verbally and in writing</li>
<li>Ability and willingness to travel (Both domestically and internationally)</li>
<li>Experience with the standard HPC software stack and how HPC works</li>
<li>Linux fluency and scripting</li>
<li>Proven success working in a fast-paced, changing environment</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Desirable Knowledge, Skills, and Abilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Experience with HPC schedulers<br>
(Moab, Torque, SLURM, LSF, PBSPro, SGE, etc)</li>
<li>HPC system administration</li>
<li>Cloud computing knowledge</li>
<li>Strong familiarity with the HPC industry and customers</li>
<li>Familiarity with python programming language</li>
<li>Familiarity with network monitoring tools and how to debug network traffic (wireshark)</li>
<li>Familiarity with gdb, valgrind, autoconf, make, and other Linux development tools</li>
<li>Familiarity with RESTful Web Services, Mongodb, Postgres, Tomcat, Spark, Hadoop, Kafka, SQL</li>
<li>Familiarity with setting up environments in public cloud offerings (AWS, Google Cloud, Azure, Oracle)</li>
</ul>
