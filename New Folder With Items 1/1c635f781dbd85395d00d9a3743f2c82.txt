<h2>Leasing Consultant</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p>TriBridge Residential is actively seeking to hire a Leasing Consultant for the McBee Station property, located in Greenville, SC.</p>
<p>As a <b>Leasing Consultant</b>you will be in charge of using your dynamic sales and marketing methods to provide superior customer service to potential and current residents of our multifamily community. You will maintain high occupancy levels by using your skills to sell and demonstrate the value in our community to prospective residents, thereby finalizing the sales process.</p>
<p><b>Essential Duties: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Leasing apartments. Included in the overall responsibility of leasing apartments is taking telephone inquiries, greeting prospects, showing apartments, taking applications and deposits, verifying information, following up with all prospects, and ensuring that leases are renewed on a timely basis.</li>
<li>Upholds a professional, yet friendly, atmosphere in the leasing office and other areas where residents or prospective residents meet.</li>
<li>Answers incoming phone calls and handles each call accordingly, whether it is a prospect call or a resident.</li>
<li>Greets prospective residents, qualifies, determines needs and preferences, and professionally presents community and specific apartments while communicating features and benefits.</li>
<li>Inspects models and market ready vacants daily to ensure cleanliness.</li>
<li>Maintains awareness of market/industry conditions and trends. Contributes ideas to the Property Manager for marketing the community and for improving resident satisfaction. Conducts monthly market surveys.</li>
<li>Physically inspects marketing path of the community on a daily basis.</li>
<li>Inventories office supplies on a periodic basis and reports needs to the Property Manager.</li>
<li>Maintain and restock caf√© and/or coffee bar.</li>
<li>Update social media sites to market the community. Assist in creating advertising campaigns and newsletters.</li>
<li>Develop the online presence for the community by targeting new followers and creating engaging content.</li>
<li>Post information and photos from special events on social media sites.</li>
</ul>
