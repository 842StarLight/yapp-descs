<h2>AV Support Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p>Job Summary</p>
<p>
As a member of the Jabra Support organization in North America, the Jabra AV Support Specialist will act as a Level 1 technical resource for both internal colleagues and business partners on post-sales enquiries and technical issues on all audiovisual products.</p>
<p>
Jabra is very successful in their growing B2B business unit and has many large enterprise customers across the region. The Jabra AV Support Specialist will play an important role in maintaining good relationships with these customers by providing first-class technical support via telephone, email, click to chat as well as video calls, thereby empowering them to maximize the use of their Jabra video collaboration products through education and awareness.</p>
<p>
The company works with a clear service escalation process and utilizes a ticket management system to collect and enrich all support cases from customers. Furthermore, a comprehensive Knowledge Base is available containing key product information on open/closed issues across the product portfolio.</p>
<p>
In parallel with above responsibilities the position will assist the Jabra Audio Support Specialists in North America with handling Level 1 technical issues on Jabra’s audio products when relevant. Being a part of a global company, the role will engage with colleagues in the technical, operational, sales, and marketing departments from all over the world.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
SPECIFIC WORK TASKS AND RESPONSIBILITIES</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Solve technical customer enquiries by performing troubleshooting activities</li>
<li>Provide general product information, installation, configuration, and upgrade support</li>
<li>Collect relevant technical problem identification information including known issues</li>
<li>Fill in case tickets and escalate to Level 2 Technical Support according to company standards</li>
<li>Define the relevant action plan to solve the customers’ problems, and execute accordingly</li>
<li>Educate customers on online tools and resources</li>
<li>Test and evaluate new products prior to release</li>
<li>Process in-warranty return merchandise authorizations (RMAs) when applicable</li>
<li>Work closely together with the technical, operational, and sales departments</li>
<li>Determine product warranty and provide customers with upsell opportunities on new products on out-of-warranty products</li>
<li>Ensure that high customer focus and satisfaction is the basis of all activities</li>
<br>
</ul>
