<h2>Mortgage Loan Originator</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p><b>PROCESSING &amp; COMPLIANCE</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Collect and analyze customer financial information to assess customers qualifications for different loan alternatives that are most advantageous to borrower(s).</li>
<li>Provide information to customer on risks and benefits of selected loan alternatives based on customers individual financial circumstances.</li>
<li>Pre-qualify and pre-approve prospective loan applications using Scenic Oaks Funding approved forms, subject to all Federal and State laws and regulations.</li>
<li>Obtain complete loan application information into laptop and supply borrowers with all disclosures required by Federal or State Laws.</li>
<li>Obtain borrower authorization to perform credit check and collect credit application fee.</li>
<li>Provide all forms and disclosures, including: GFE, TIL, HUD booklet and/or ARM booklet, ARM disclosure and any other Federal or State specific required disclosure.</li>
<li>Utilize Automated Underwriting, independent judgment, SRP calc, and discretion on all eligible programs to help determine a customers eligibility for different loan programs.</li>
<li>Review purchase contracts and addendums for conflicts or concerns.</li>
<li>Collect applicable appraisal fee and/or credit report fee.</li>
<li>Register and/or lock loan applications with Investor or Secondary Marketing Department.</li>
<li>Comply with all Lending Regulations to include; Reg B ECOA, Reg X RESPA, Reg Z TIL, FCRA, Reg C HMDA, HOPEA, Privacy Act, Security Act, US Patriot Act, OFAC and the Flood Disaster Act, and State Laws of California.</li>
<li>Comply with all Privacy and Security laws to include borrower records kept in locked file cabinet, software passwords, retentions, storage and shredding requirement.</li>
<li>Deliver Loan Application within 24 hours of client signature and/or application date.</li>
<li>Return customer calls promptly.</li>
<li>Positively respond to files that need attention.</li>
<li>Meet with Production Manager as requested to discuss production standards, goals and expectations.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>REALTOR INTERFACE</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Obtain referrals from agents identified and targeted in Annual Review.</li>
<li>Return agent calls promptly.</li>
<li>Communicate loan status and circumstances that may hinder loan approval.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>TOOLS AND PROGRAMS</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Study lender websites and program offerings.</li>
<li>Review marketing efforts and business opportunities with Supervisor semi-annually.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>BRANCH OFFICE AND SALES PRODUCTION ACTIVITIES</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Attend weekly branch sales meetings and provide market/product updates.</li>
<li>Maintain and deliver a weekly update on loan status to both agents and borrowers.</li>
<li>Meet monthly with Branch Managers to discuss production and strategy for partnering with specific agents.</li>
<li>Aggressively seek new applications volume.</li>
<li>Review Success Rate Reports and Sales Volume Reports monthly.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>SYSTEM RELATED</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Maintain all equipment and software provided by Scenic Oaks Funding.</li>
<li>Comply with all e-mail requirements to include the clean-up to keep system running effectively.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>MISCELLANEOUS</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Perform other duties as needed and requested.</li>
<li>Successfully originate and facilitate home loans through existing referral partners, current data base, and creating a larger sphere of influence.</li>
</ul>
<p>Job Types: Full-time, Commission</p>
<p>Education:</p>
<ul><li>High school or equivalent (Required)</li></ul>
<p>License:</p>
<ul><li>NMLS (Required)</li></ul>
