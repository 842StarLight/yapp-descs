The Gap Inc. Product Management Team is looking for a Product Manager to joinour Payment Product team to drive product vision, strategy development andexecution of the Payment experience and capabilities on our InternationalPoint-of-Sale out of our San Francisco headquarters. Werelooking for someone who is curious, innovative and driven to join the team thatis on the leading edge of the transformation of retail in an omni-channelworld.</p>

The Product Manager will work with the business stakeholders as well as storeassociates to identify problems to be solved, quantify the value in solvingthem, and determine how those problems can be solved via technology. They willconsult with domain architects, User Experience, store operations teams, andother Product Managers and then partner with software engineering teams todeliver technology solutions which continuously improve the shoppingexperiences for our external customers while also providing enhanced capabilitiesfor internal business partners.<br>
<br>
Were seeking a technical problem solver with a track record of productinnovation, leading complex projects independently and prioritizing features,bug fixes and technical debt to ensure the health and stability of our ProductDatabase Platform.<br>
