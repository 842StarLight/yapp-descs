<h2>Senior Director, Associate Client Partner</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div></div>
<div>
<div>
<div>Qualifications &amp; Responsibilities</div>
<div>
Epsilon is seeking high performing candidates focused on developing strategic client relationships and driving growth. Candidates should have strong relationship management skills, a proven track record in selling professional services and software, an entrepreneurial spirit, and relevant industry experience. In this role, candidates will have responsibility for strategic account planning and execution, client retention and growth, and development of executive-level client relationships for one or two large Fortune 500 clients. The candidate should have demonstrated the ability to create solutions and services that deliver breakthrough campaigns and customer experiences informed by strategic insights, data and technology.<br>
<br>
The Work You’ll Do<br>
You will be responsible for contributing to the development and execution of a three year strategic plan for assigned clients; account strategies that deliver business value to clients and generate growth for Epsilon. You will build and maintain trusted advisor status between Epsilon and the client; this includes identification and cultivation of all individuals who influence, buy, or have a stake in solutions / services that Epsilon can provide. You will work closely with an enterprise sales rep and cross functional team to execute on account growth and development strategies. The candidate will play a critical role in the customer experience that we aspire to create at different stages of the customer life cycle. You will be able to quantify and articulate the business value that Epsilon provides to your clients; the client will understand the business impact of Epsilon solutions and services. Finally, you will be accountable for insuring that revenue forecasts and sales pipelines are accurate; you will also be accountable for all contracts and legal agreements between Epsilon and the client.<br>
<br>
Successful associate client partners will hit profit and client satisfaction targets, retain their clients, and generate growth year over year.<br>
<br>
Responsibilities:<br>
<ul>
<li>Developing an in-depth knowledge of the client’s: business, strategic objectives and annual goals; the industry within which the they operate, key trends, disruptors, economics; competitors, positioning, key trends, marketing programs; organization structure, decision making process, key influencers and stakeholders, budget ownership; key initiatives, objectives, decision makers, budget; marketing technology landscape, relationships with Epsilon competitors</li>
<li>Communicating the client vision to the overall account team (delivery, client services, sales, strategic consulting and partner organizations)</li>
<li>Leading a cross functional team to create and execute on a three year strategic account plan</li>
<li>Building strong consultative relationships with key client executives and navigating various levels within the client organization and across marketing, IT, and procurement functions</li>
<li>Meeting with and maintaining an onsite presence with your clients (up to 50% travel)</li>
<li>Playing a leadership role in pursuits, proposals and overall account management</li>
<li>Acting as the voice of the client for solution design</li>
<li>Forecasting of revenue and growth opportunities, negotiation of contracts and legal documents, and oversight of billing</li>
<li>Staffing and developing the client services team</li>
<li>Serving as an escalation point for key challenges with accountability for the resolution of client issues</li>
<li>Proactively assessing the market landscape, what competitors are doing, as well as continuously keeping up to date with Epsilon’s offering and solution/product development pipeline.</li>
</ul>
<br>
Qualifications:<br>
<ul>
<li>Experience working comfortably in and with large, highly-matrixed corporate environments</li>
<li>Experience in and with organizations with large scale and complexity</li>
<li>Experience achieving against growth/sales targets</li>
<li>Experience working in a consultative manner with clients and serving as a key advisor and partner</li>
<li>Experience in driving high-stakes work (e.g. key accounts, strategic initiatives)</li>
<li>Experience in fast-changing environments</li>
<li>Experience managing challenging (external) stakeholders</li>
<li>8 - 12 years’ experience as account relationship and/or business development manager serving Fortune 500 clients</li>
<li>Track record of delivering high value solutions and experiences and collaborating with cross functional teams</li>
<li>Relevant industry experience and knowledge, preferably in leadership or account management roles.</li>
<li>Extensive and varied expertise in in digital marketing.</li>
<li>Passion for client service and delivering positive business outcomes for clients</li>
<li>Success in playing a leading role within a matrixed account team</li>
<li>Strong background in crafting and delivering proposals</li>
<li>Ability to influence and lead cross-functional teams in client pursuits and initiatives concurrently</li>
<li>Strong business acumen and strategic mindset</li>
<li>Poise, executive presence, outstanding communications skills</li>
<li>Bachelor's degree preferred</li>
</ul>
