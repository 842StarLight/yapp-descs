<h2>Contract Management Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p><b>Major Goals &amp; Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Effectively communicate with a high volume of internal and external customers about billing and contracts via phone and email.</li>
<li>Utilize internal resources to pull original sales and leasing paperwork for internal and external review.</li>
<li>Provide thorough explanation of and practice consistent adherence to the terms and conditions of various contracts.</li>
<li>Set up new customer account records and correct existing accounts as needed.</li>
<li>Use financial software to review and correct contract billing records with a high level of attention to detail.</li>
<li>Perform thorough research of an accountâ€™s billing history and determine the appropriate actions needed to address/resolve billing disputes.</li>
<li>Determine when credit memos are needed and calculate/issue credit memos using the proper financial codes.</li>
<li>Process customer data changes and make filing entry of the actions taken.</li>
<li>Make outbound calls to obtain meter readings necessary for billing; calculate estimated meter readings when actual data is unavailable.</li>
<li>Complete other duties within the accounting department as assigned.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Ideal Candidates</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Excellent communication skills, both verbal and written</li>
<li>Strong numerical, analytical, and problem-solving skills</li>
<li>Customer-focused and dependable self-starter</li>
<li>Ability to work closely with teammates to meet deadlines</li>
<li>Ability to handle a fast-paced, high-volume call queue environment</li>
<li>Ability to multi-task through different applications/screens</li>
<li>Ability to handle sitting for long periods of time</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Prerequisites</b></p>
<ul>
<li>High school diploma</li>
<li>Computer experience/ability to type 35+ wpm</li>
<li>Office work or customer service experience</li>
</ul>
