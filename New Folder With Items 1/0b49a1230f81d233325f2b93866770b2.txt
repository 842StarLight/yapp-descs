<p><b>Job Summary</b></p>
<p>
The <b>Customer Success Manager</b> is a key part of customer health and value directorate, stewarding customer-centricity. This role is charged with playing a pivotal responsibility to managing one-to-many large, enterprise-size customer’s experience, with the goal of utilizing high health and satisfaction benchmarks as guides for success. In extension to this, they will be the customer’s value advocate and champion, constantly establishing and monitoring current value received, whilst collaborating with the customer on value expansion. This role is expected to be intimately familiar with Alert Logic’s organizational approach to product and service delivery, and will be looked upon by the customer as the subject matter expert in bringing connections between both organizations effectively and proactively. A deep and sustained understanding of our customer’s needs on product and service delivery expectations is required.</p>
<p>
This individual will be strong in Alert Logic’s proprietary metrics utilized to evaluate health and value realization. Ultimately, your role is evaluated upon concentrated metrics of NCR, NPS, and CHI; this informs and becomes catalyst to Alert Logic’s operational and strategic priorities associated to our customer enterprise segmentation delivery success.</p>
<p>
The Customer Success Manager will champion the customer throughout the engagement lifecycle, enabling them to get best value from Alert Logic solutions. Further, this role is expected to establish themselves internally with the Alert Logic organization as a revered, trusted advisor for enterprise customer segment improvements, as well as ensuring Alert Logic is considered as a preferred vendor to the customer. This individual will ensure that our delivery infrastructure is modern, innovative and effective, forging a strong and positive overall Alert Logic identity and brand with the customer.</p>
<p><b>
Primary Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Build strong, trusted, and influential relationships with assigned customers and within the Alert Logic organization:</li>
<li>Obtain a thorough understanding of the value that drives assigned customer decisions for using Alert Logic products and services. Proactively offer appropriately tailored advice and bring unique industry insights to the customer, affording opportunity to realize optimal current value, as well as drive and expand value propositions.</li>
<li>Provide feedback to customers to streamline service and product delivery.</li>
<li>Engage in on-going, two-way dialogue with customers regarding ways to improve engagement and use of our product and services.</li>
<li>Develop trust-based relationships with multiple levels within the customer’s organization. Understand what feedback strategy strengthens the customer relationship and indoctrinate an effective feedback mechanism between the customer and Alert Logic.</li>
<li>Develop strong and trusted relationships within Alert Logic leadership as to</li>
<li>Encourage reception to organizational improvements and added-value propositions.</li>
<li>Ensure customer is consistently, and appropriately, aware of the added value of all the services and products Alert Logic is providing, and/or can provide in the future.</li>
<li>Responsible for day-to-day management and execution of Customer Success programs: the objectives and goals required to achieving metric improvements.</li>
<li>Establish synergistic relationship with the Alert Logic sales representative(s) as to promote partnership in evaluating and presenting product / service portfolio expansion with the customer(s).</li>
<li>Contribute in defining and establishing internal product and service delivery processes to help scale the company: to Identify, promote, and drive efficiencies in the enterprise customer segmentation service delivery model, both with the customer and within Alert Logic.</li>
<li>Consistently evaluate inputs from customers to identify delivery satisfaction impact trends. Develop and propose solutions for negative trends.</li>
<li>Proven results on delivering/exceeding goals on time, with an attention to detail. Experience managing and prioritizing multiple customer requests in a fast paced environment, including timeline scoping and effective re-prioritization.</li>
