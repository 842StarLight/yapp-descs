<h2>Regional Sales Director</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>
<div>We are Telular, a business unit of AMETEK and a technology leader in IoT solutions for the commercial telematics, security and home automation markets. For over thirty years we have made smarter connections for our customers. By combining wireless technologies, purpose-built devices, and SaaS commercial offerings, we’ve been a disruptive force in the IoT market.<br>
<br>
We’re proud of our culture of innovation and dedication to customer success that has put us in a favorable growth position based on a large and loyal customer base and an ever-increasing number of applications for its solutions.<br>
<br>
We are seeking a Regional Sales Director who is a proven sales leader with a relentless drive for success to join our SkyBitz Sales team. The ideal candidate will have a passion for sales and be able to promote our vision, as well as collaborate with their teammates to generate extraordinary growth. This individual will be a fierce advocate for our clients and our brand. In addition, the person will provide customized solutions to clients and ensure a positive customer experience during the sales cycle in order to drive repeat business and continued customer loyalty.<br>
<br>
The Regional Sales Director will report to the VP of Sales and be responsible for the development and growth of a client base through strategic outside sales activity. This includes prospecting for new accounts in a defined territory (US – West Region), developing consultative relationships with clients, and managing ongoing account and territory activity. If you have high energy, passion and vision and you want to help provide our customers with superior products, services and assistance, then we are looking for you.<br>
<br>
Responsibilities include:<br>
<br>
<ul>
<li>Meet and exceed all revenue goals and sales targets</li>
<li>Develop leads and prospects within a defined territory, securing new business through a combination of outbound telephone calls, online communications and in-person visits</li>
<li>Effectively present and demonstrate our full suite of products and services to new prospects and existing clients</li>
<li>Maintain a consistent pipeline of existing and prospective business</li>
<li>Cultivate relationships with existing and prospective customers through professional demeanor and strong interpersonal skills</li>
<li>Manage a travel expense budget effectively</li>
<li>Communicate effectively with management regarding business opportunity progress</li>
<li>Travel to existing and prospective client locations within the assigned territory to foster the relationship and identify future opportunities</li>
<li>Partner with operations, billing, and other internal teams to ensure customer orders are fulfilled</li>
<li>Collaborate with sales team to identify, develop, and execute sales strategies and processes</li>
<li>Contribute to the development of leads and prospects and securing new business by participating in conference calls, webinars and in-person visits</li>
<li>Travel to and attend trade shows as needed
</li>
</ul>
</div>
