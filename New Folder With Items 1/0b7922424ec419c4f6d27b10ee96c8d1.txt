<p><b>
About the role</b></p>
<p>
As the Senior IT Lead you'll be responsible for all day to day IT operations. We have no in house development so you'll wear a lot of hats including:</p>
<ul>
<li>Managing external IT vendor partners</li>
<li>Partnering with other teams and vendors to lead issue resolution and post mortems</li>
<li>Planning the IT release roadmap and calendar</li>
<li>Managing the IT Backlog</li>
<li>Defining and communicating technical requirements</li>
<li>Validating the work of external vendors and approving delivery of code</li>
<li>Defining the process required to manage multiple IT platforms, internal and external stakeholders, and providing outstanding delivery to a user base of over 500,000 users.</li>
</ul>
<p>in this role you'll report directly to the Director of Operations. If you've ever wanted a chance to make your mark and run your own show, this is a great opportunity for you.</p>

Requirements</b></p>
<ul>
<li>3+ years building highly dynamic and scalable web applications using PHP, WordPress and WordPress plug-ins in a fast cycle development environment.</li>
<li>2+ years experience with AWS</li>
<li>Extensive experience with HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and MySQL</li>
<li>Extensive experience with integrating multiple systems (API, etc)</li>
<li>Hands on experience in Release Management and DevOps</li>
<li>Exceptional written and verbal communications</li>
<li>Strong architectural comprehension</li>
<li>Deep commitment to quality, scalable code and agile delivery</li>
<li>Ability to work unsupervised and take ownership</li>
<li>Hyper organized</li>
<li>Great sense of humor</li>
<li>Able to work remotely with your own phone, computer and high speed internet access in place.</li>
