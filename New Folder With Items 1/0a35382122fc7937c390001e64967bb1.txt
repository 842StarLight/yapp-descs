The world's leading private security organization, G4S, has an immediate job opportunity for a Dispatcher. As a Dispatcher, your primary responsibility will be to acknowledge and dispatch for alarms by using state of the art situation management software. You will also take incident reports over the telephone, conduct preliminary investigations on reports, handle access control issues for an entire corporation, maintain compliance of regulated locations, and immediately report violations to management. This high energy position must be able to multi-task and perform computer data entry while callers are on the phone.
