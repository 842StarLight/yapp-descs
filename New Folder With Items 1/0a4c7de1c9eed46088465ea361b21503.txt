The Team
<br><br>
Qualtrics is in the business of helping organizations manage experiences. Of all experiences an organization delivers, employee experiences have a lasting impact on talent retention, productivity, and the overall bottom-line of the organization. However, Many of the worlds organizations have followed a very outdated approach to understanding their employees.
<br><br>
As the employee landscape shifts, these outdated approaches are not delivering the insights required to truly engage their employee base. As such, these organizations are also looking for a new way to not just monitor, but understand and improve the experiences they deliver to their employees. Qualtrics has provided a new approach built on a foundation of world-class technology and solid expertise that is driving results for many of the world's top organizations.
<br><br>
Our Mission
<br><br>
The mission of the EX Solution Consultant team is to drive EX revenue with a focus on winning within key accounts and supporting strategic initiatives that will increase EX revenue.
<br><br>
A Day in the Life
<br><br>
The EX Solution Consultant is a very senior role that is hyper-focused on growing global revenue. To accomplish this, they will work side-by-side with the sales organization on strategic account planning, deal specific strategy, and sales campaign execution.
<br><br>
This team will go deep on strategic accounts where there are domino effects, be proactive in creating opportunities, develop a center of excellence around competitive intelligence, and support high value cross functional initiatives. While most of their efforts will be focused in a pre-sales environment, some of the additional cross-functional initiatives could include input in product direction, content generation, contributing to analyst reports/relationships, attending events, delivering customer workshops, and directing account based marketing activity.
<br><br>
Responsibilities
<br><br>
<ul>
<li>Drives new revenue from key strategic accounts</li>
<li>Proactively engages the sales organization to penetrate key strategic accounts</li>
<li>Works closely with sales leadership to forecast and direct internal resources to key accounts</li>
<li>Conducts deal reviews with the Enterprise sales team and ensures accurate forecasting</li>
<li>Conducts 1on1 account planning and strategy sessions with the Enterprise sales team</li>
<li>Creates and leads training for the sales organization</li>
<li>Contributes to product and business development activities</li>
<li>Conducts original research and presents and published research in applied and academic forums</li>
<li>Coordinates with other HR subject matter experts within Qualtrics to build innovative solutions that meet our clients' needs and push the field forward</li>
</ul>
Successful candidates will possess most of the following qualifications:

<ul>
