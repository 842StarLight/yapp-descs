 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>SUMMARY</b></h2>
<p>Candidate must have military experience !</p>
<div><div>
<p>Responsible for serving as the primary link between the facility, community, and region’s military bases/installations. Position identifies and maximizes military referral opportunities by assessing, managing, and developing referral sources. Position communicates with military referral sources on a regular basis to ensure needs are met. Position will also make marketing presentations at military installations throughout region to develop referral sources. Approximately 80% travel, including overnight, will be required. Coverage included but not limited to: Texas, Arkansas, Oklahoma, Missouri, Louisiana</p>
<br>
</div></div>
<br>
<p></p>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>PRIMARY RESPONSIBILITIES</b></h2>
<div><div><ul>
<li>Direct the development and continuous refinement of the facility’s comprehensive business development plan within the scope of the changing health care environment.</li>
<li>Develop new military / TRICARE accounts and market in the business and industry, employer EAP, Primary Care Physicians/Licensed Practitioners and managed care segments. Respond to all business development opportunities as appropriate.</li>
<li>Develop a designated number of qualified business development contacts per week, which will achieve the overall business development goals and objectives.</li>
<li>Develop account management list to maintain current account information, and update as required.</li>
<li>Contact all primary accounts through personal visits, telephone calls and letters/mailers on an ongoing basis, developing and encouraging a relationship of trust with all active accounts.</li>
<li>Maintain accurate records and statistics, prepare business development reports, assist in preparation of departmental budget.</li>
<li>Consult with internal staff and external referral sources concerning the establishment of goals and development of new programs.</li>
<li>Monitor referrals and admissions, and plan an appropriate strategy to maximize referral potential.</li>
<li>Maintain current awareness of changes in the health care industry through self-education, peer education, and professional and community involvement for the betterment of this position and the facility.</li>
<li>Develop and implement community workshops and seminars.</li>
<li>Conduct presentations to educated prospective referral sources on services provided at the facility.</li>
<li>Maintain a competition informational file and contribute to the ongoing SWOT analysis.</li>
<li>Promote the team concept within the facility.</li>
<li>Participate in the evaluation and analysis of facility statistics and provide recommendations to overall business planning strategies as appropriate.</li>
<li>Participate in the continuous analysis of the changing health care environment and health care market.</li>
<li>Attend scheduled department meetings to provide the Director with timely reports on month-end reviews, client servicing schedules, customer sales forms, and other reporting requirements.</li>
<li>Attend in-house planning and development committee meetings, as requested.</li>
<li>Adhere to facility, department, corporate, personnel and standard policies and procedures.</li>
<li>Attend all mandatory facility in-services and staff development activities as scheduled.</li>
<li>Adhere to facility standards concerning conduct, dress, attendance and punctuality.</li>
<li>Support facility-wide quality/performance improvement goals and objectives.</li>
<li>Maintain confidentiality of facility employees and patient information.</li>
</ul></div></div>
