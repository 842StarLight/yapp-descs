<h2>Marketing Coordinator 2129.080619</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<p></p>
<div>
<p><b>Position Summary</b><b>:</b></p>
<p>The Marketing Coordinator works with the Marketing team to contribute to the overall success of ConServe’s marketing strategy.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>Essential Duties &amp; Responsibilities:</b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Facilitates the preparation and content management of Request for Proposals (RFP’s) and Sales Proposals.</li>
<li>Responsible for designing, creating, and delivering marketing programs to support the growth and expansion of company products and services.</li>
<li>Coordinates and assists with the marketing activities of a service or business line which may include advertising, direct mailing, printing, customer events, and trade shows.</li>
<li>Creates, implements, and oversees communications programs effectively describing and promoting the Company and its products through graphics, brochures, company or servicer product fact sheets, logos, or other promotional products.</li>
<li>Manages the research and development of content for publication of internal and external communications. (i.e. newsletters, training materials, reference guides, etc. etc.)</li>
<li>Prepare status reports on sales performance and marketing initiatives.</li>
<li>Adherence to ConServe’s Professional Practices Management System (PPMS), Code of Conduct, and Compliance Program</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Education:</b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>High School Diploma or GED</li>
<li>Bachelor’s Degree or equivalent combination of education and experience</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><b>Skills/ Requirements:</b></p>
<br>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>Two (2) years of proven success in a marketing capacity</li>
<li>Familiar with a variety of marketing concepts, practices, and procedures.</li>
<li>Adobe CS experience preferred.</li>
<li>Ability to rely on experience and judgment to plan and accomplish goals.</li>
<li>Ability to perform a variety of tasks.</li>
<li>Ability to travel up to 15%</li>
<li>Ability to travel between ConServe’s offices as needed</li>
<li>Demonstrated wide degree of creativity.</li>
<li>Possess excellent verbal and written communication skills.</li>
<li>Be computer literate including competent data entry, word processing and graphic software skills</li>
<li>Have the ability to act independently to resolve problems.</li>
<li>Be motivated to learn and continuously improve.</li>
<li>Demonstrate a positive and team-oriented attitude</li>
<li>Ability to obtain and maintain Government Security Clearance</li>
<li>No conflicts of interest with ConServe or our Clients</li>
</ul>
