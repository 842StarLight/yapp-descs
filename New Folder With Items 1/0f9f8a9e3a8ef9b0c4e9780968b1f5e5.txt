<p><b>
Cannabis Cultivation Facility – Senior Plant Manager/General Manager</b></p>
<p>
This job is to be the general manager/senior plant manager of a cannabis cultivation in a 43,000 square foot facility in Akron, OH leading a team of horticulturalists, growers, sales &amp; logistics, human resources, maintenance and operations crew. This position would be working during the build out of the facility and report directly to the owners/executives of the company.</p>
<p><b>
Required Experience and Background:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree in Engineering, Chemistry, Business, or related fields.</li>
<li>Minimum 5 years managing a plant, office, or sterile manufacturing facility in a supervisory role.</li>
<li>Proven project management skills.</li>
<li>Computer efficient and well versed in MS Office (excel too), CRM (software use preferable), and familiar with accounting reports.</li>
<li>Must have spent minimum 3 years in office environment</li>
<li>Outstanding time management and organizational skills.</li>
<li>High degree of accountability and awareness.</li>
<li>Effective communication and demonstrated leadership skills.</li>
<li>Must have a clean felony record in past 10 years and be able to pass State background checks.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Duties and Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Manage the Cultivation, Operations, Facilities, HR, and Sales teams</li>
<li>Provide daily direction to teams and operational personnel</li>
<li>Engage with all team members to find inefficiencies and execute improvements</li>
<li>Assess and improve facilities morale</li>
<li>Create budget estimates and reports</li>
<li>Report to owners on continual basis</li>
<li>Oversee sales &amp; logistics and report to owners/executives weekly inventory and sales reports</li>
<li>Accountable for all safety and sterile plant environment</li>
<li>Review, train and implement all SOPs while continually looking for ways to improve and revise them</li>
</ul>
