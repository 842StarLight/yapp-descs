<h2>Graphic Designer</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<div>
<p>Seeking a full-time, in-house Graphic Designer. Candidate needs to possess exceptional taste for beauty layout, design and typography. Ability to provide marketing aspect to design for both print and web. Experience working for a skin care or cosmetics company a big +</p>
<ul>
<li>Design and prepare files for <b>PRINT</b>: packaging, mock-ups, point-of-sale signage, brochures, postcards, sell sheets, public relations materials, advertising and other marketing materials.</li>
<li>Design and prepare files for <b>DIGITAL</b>: online and eCommerce website marketing materials including, landing pages, banners, mass email marketing templates, product pages, press mentions, website design, etc.</li>
<li>Photoshop retouching skills required.</li>
</ul>
<b>Responsibilities:</b>
<ul>
<li>Expert knowledge of Adobe InDesign, Photoshop and Illustrator.</li>
<li>Experience and understanding of HTML production, web graphic formats and design software.</li>
<li>Experience with CMS programs (Bigcommerce) a big +</li>
<li>Working knowledge of email client programs (Constant Contact)</li>
<li>Working knowledge of social media platforms: Facebook, Instagram, Pinterest, YouTube.</li>
<li>Photography and Social Media skills a +</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Background:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Ability to multi-task and to effectively deliver under deadlines.</li>
<li>Ability to follow direction and communicate clearly with vendors and colleagues.</li>
<li>Actively contribute to discussions and brainstorming sessions.</li>
<li>Must be able to work independently, be responsible and punctual.</li>
<li>Ensure all projects are delivered on time and to the highest possible standard.</li>
</ul>
<p>Requirements: Bachelor's degree in related field, 2-4 years relevant experience.</p>
