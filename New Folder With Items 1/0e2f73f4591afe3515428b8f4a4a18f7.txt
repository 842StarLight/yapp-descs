<h2>Account Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div></div>
<div><div><div><div><div>
<p>ABS Technology is hiring an <b>Account Manager</b> for our team in the <b>Knoxville, Tennessee</b> area.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
ABS Technology is a leader in technological innovation, implementation, client services support and staffing, headquartered in Virginia Beach, VA. With deep expertise across collaboration, enterprise networking, data center technologies, staffing and client support services, ABS Technology designs, architects, implements, and supports the most advanced technology solutions for our clients, while providing unmatched customer service, ensuring our clients and their organizations are successful in achieving their operational and financial goals. Our Sales organization has been built on passion, high levels of integrity, being an advocate for the customer and forging meaningful relationships within our company, with our vendor partners, and with our customers. Our sales team is supported by a sophisticated operation that includes direct access to purchasing, quoting, Pre-sales, Project Management Office, Implementation and Support Services. In addition to a great culture and stellar support, our sales team enjoys extremely competitive compensation packages, recognition for top performance and customer satisfaction, and the opportunity to be part of building a world class organization.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
The Account Manager is responsible for the sales and account management efforts for ABS Technology in the Public Sector Vertical, within the state of Tennessee. Demonstrate exceptional product and industry knowledge to effectively position ABS Technology products and services as must-have technology solutions to contacts at varying professional levels with a focus on C-Level sales. Work with Sales Leadership and Executive Leadership Team to achieve annual sales objectives while ensuring profitable growth aligned with ABS Technology business objectives.</p>
<p><b>
THE DETAILS (WHAT YOU’LL BE RESPONSIBLE FOR)</b></p>
<p>
Account Management/Sales Goals</p>
<ul><li>Achieve assigned personal and territory sales objectives leveraging best-practices in sales and customer relationship management.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Accurately maintain, share and track customer information on an on-going basis leveraging CRM tools (e.g. SalesForce), shared drives and web 2.0 applications</li></ul>
<ul><li>Leverage Data from the CRM to expand territory and client-based sales</li></ul>
<ul><li>Maintain accurate forecasts and account information at all times within the CRM with (e.g. no delinquent opportunities)</li></ul>
<ul><li>Use best-practices, top-down, consultative sales approach to build, maintain &amp; strengthen relationships with all key decision makers and key influences within accounts</li></ul>
<ul><li>Understand goals and objectives for all assigned accounts and have a thorough understanding of where sales opportunities exist (i.e. demographics, industry trends, funding cycles, other key external influences)</li></ul>
<ul><li>Develop proactive approach to territory management, anticipating impact on ABS Technology products and services resulting from competition, Public Sector and Education industry trends, etc.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Negotiate proposals and contracts that ensure profitable growth aligned with ABS Technology business objectives</li></ul>
<ul><li>Manage and maintain oversight on the accuracy of all quotes (i.e. Discounts, Project Scope Accuracy, Contract Vehicles when appropriate, and High-Quality formatting and presentation.</li></ul>
<ul><li>Work closely with team members, vendors, and client contacts to ensure highest quality customer experience</li></ul>
<ul><li>Demonstrate exceptional interpersonal, written/verbal communication, organizational, follow-up, consultative sales and presentation skills</li></ul>
<ul><li>Take the lead on all RFP/RFI/RFQ Processes</li></ul>
<ul><li>Deep knowledge of E-Rate Processes and Best Practices</li></ul>
<ul><li>Deep knowledge of Tennessee public sector customers</li></ul>
<ul><li>Establish strategic planning initiative to provide clients with consistent and accurate upgrade paths across network technologies</li></ul>
<ul><li>Demonstrate ability when necessary to have difficult conversations with clients and Vendor/Manufacturers and know when to escalate to leadership for support</li></ul>
<ul><li>Understand and effectively manage various deal registration systems on behalf of clients and ABS Technology</li></ul>
<ul><li>Work with Operations / Finance to ensure smooth order, invoice and on occasion, collection processes</li></ul>
<ul><li>Develop and manage a referenceable client base for ABS Technology</li></ul>
<ul><li>Serve as a key Point of Contact for Customer issues during open cases between client support, project management and engineering staff</li></ul>
<ul><li>Travel approximately 60-70% of time (with seasonal variations)</li></ul>
<ul><li>Manage high outbound call volume whether traveling or in the office</li></ul>
<p>
Product /Industry Knowledge</p>
<ul><li>Demonstrate exceptional product knowledge to be successful in increasingly competitive environment</li></ul>
<ul><li>Knowledge of business trends and issues in the Public Sector, including State, Local, K-12 and Higher Education markets, specifically in the state of Tennessee</li></ul>
<ul><li>Thoroughly understand deals/rates and processes for all ABS Technology products and services as well as the overall goals and strategic vision of ABS Technology</li></ul>
<ul><li>Maintain an understanding of industry trends, current events, product and technology developments by reading professional publications, researching all available resources prospect/product resources and participating in professional organizations</li></ul>
<ul><li>Understand new technologies and industry trends and be able to speak to how they impact customers and ABS Technology</li></ul>
<ul><li>Understanding of best practices in effective management of vendors/manufacturer relationships</li></ul>
<p>
Team Oriented Environment</p>
<ul><li>Partner with company personnel (i.e. Executive Leadership and Sales Leadership, Pre-Sales, Project Management and Engineering teams) to develop strategies to ensure that personal and territory sales goals are met</li></ul>
<ul><li>Work with team to:</li></ul>
<ul><li>Ensure excellence in design and scope of projects</li></ul>
<ul><li>Ensure the highest quality in Bill of Materials quotes</li></ul>
<ul><li>Ensure proper implementation of new accounts</li></ul>
<ul><li>Build value with customer by implementing strategies to ensure usage of ABS</li></ul>
<ul><li>Technology products and services • Properly merchandise key activities in territory to decision makers • Maximize sales and revenue opportunities • Communicate workload focus to team on a regular basis (daily, weekly, etc.) • Proactively share information that you’ve learned in the field to support other</li></ul>
<p>
reps/verticals/industries meeting their objectives</p>
<ul><li>Attend and actively participate in all required team and company-wide meetings</li></ul>
<ul><li>Attend, Manage, Lead and actively support corporate events (National, State, Regional, Local and Client Specific)</li></ul>
<ul><li>Adhere to budget by managing travel, sales and account management expenses</li></ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
THE ESSENTIALS (OBLIGATORY REQUIREMENTS)</b></p>
<ul><li>Bachelor’s degree or an equivalent combination of skills, training and experience</li></ul>
<ul><li>Approximately 5-7 years in highly successful sales and account management experience with a proven track record of territory management, consultative sales and exceptional client relations (within the State, Local and Education markets in Tennessee - preferred)</li></ul>
<ul><li>Strong interpersonal, presentation, organizational and verbal/written communication skills</li></ul>
<ul><li>Attention to detail and the ability to manage a diverse workload</li></ul>
<ul><li>Ability to work in a fast-paced team-oriented environment</li></ul>
<ul><li>Proficiency with Microsoft Office products and Customer Relationship/Dashboard Enterprise Systems</li></ul>
<ul><li>Candidate must be willing to travel (60-70%)</li></ul>
<ul><li>Must have the legal right to work in the United States</li></ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
THE NON-ESSENTIALS (BUT NICE TO HAVE)</b></p>
<ul><li>Masters’ degree preferred in Business, Engineering, Technology, Education Leadership/Administration or related field</li></ul>
<ul><li>Technology or Education industry sales experience in Tennessee preferred</li></ul>
<ul><li>Solution Selling/Consultative Selling experience preferred</li></ul>
