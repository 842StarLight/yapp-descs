<h2>Account Executive</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>FirstLight is seeking an experienced and success oriented Account Executive interested in taking their career to new heights. FirstLight, headquartered in Albany, NY, provides fiber optic data, voice, and high-speed Internet services to enterprise, carrier and wholesale customers in Upstate New York and Northern New England utilizing its own fiber optic network. FirstLight offers a robust suite of advanced telecommunications services, including dedicated Internet access, Metro Ethernet, traditional TDM solutions, SIP trunks, virtual PBX and Data Center Colocation.</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Position Summary</b></p>
<p>Account Executives are expected to cultivate existing and new relationships in order to develop and close new revenue opportunities on behalf of FirstLight.</p>
<p></p>
<p><b>Job requirements include (but are not limited to):</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Identify and close sales with new customers in the assigned territory.</li>
<li>Consistent cold calling and prospect engagement with meaningful volume of in-person appointments per week which leads to successfully achieving the assigned revenue targets.</li>
<li>Successfully conduct customer telecommunications needs assessments; identify suitable solutions based on those needs; develop, present and follow up on written proposals.</li>
<li>Act as a liaison between the customer and FirstLight personnel and contractors during the design, construction and installation process.</li>
<li>Process contracts and sales orders for new or upgraded services.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Job Qualifications &amp; Preferences</b></p>
<ul>
<li>2-5 years of successful sales experience in a telecom or ISP environment including cold calling and prospecting.</li>
<li>Working knowledge of telecommunication industry and terminology.</li>
<li>Proven track record of success in exceeding assigned sales quotas.</li>
<li>Experienced in creating and presenting compelling written sales proposals to decision makers.</li>
<li>Willingness to represent FirstLight at networking events, tradeshows and activities.</li>
<li>Proficiency with Microsoft Word, Excel, PowerPoint and Outlook.</li>
<li>Experience with Sugar CRM software a plus.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Personal Attributes</b></p>
<ul>
<li>A self starter driven to achieve revenue targets primarily through cold calling.</li>
<li>Excellent written, verbal and interpersonal communication skills.</li>
<li>Strong customer orientation.</li>
<li>Good problem-solving skills, positive and results oriented attitude.</li>
<li>Familiarity with new and emerging telecommunications technology, including but not limited to fiber optics, Ethernet, virtual PBX, software as a service (SAS) with a willingness to learn new skills and technologies quickly.</li>
<li>Highly proactive and responsible.</li>
<li>Team player.</li>
</ul>
<p>We offer a competitive base salary and a generous commission plan as well as an outstanding benefits package including health, dental, vision, and life insurance, short-term and long-term disability, 401(k) with company match, flexible spending accounts, tuition reimbursement, paid training, and paid holidays and vacation.</p>
