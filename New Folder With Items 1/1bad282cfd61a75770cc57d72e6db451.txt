<p>
NTT DATA Services currently seeks a I.T. Services Sales Executive - Life Sciences to join our team in Boston, Massachusetts (US-MA), United States (US).</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
Life science organizations are facing two significant challenges. The first is the need to speed time to market for new treatments while reducing the cost of research and development. The second is the need to adapt to an increasingly complex market that demands measurable outcomes and greater patient engagement. How does Life Sciences organizations will thrive? NTT Data partners with customers across Pharmaceutical, Medical Devices, Biotech, Contract Research Organization (CRO), and Medical Distributors segments to deliver the most value from their investments that enables them to survive and thrive in this complex industry. International Data Corporation (IDC) named NTT Data a Leader in Worldwide Life Science R&amp;D ITO Services and Everest Group named us a Major Contender in its Life Sciences Digital IT &amp; Application Services PEAK Matrix™.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Role Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Focuses complete P&amp;L responsibilities across targeted Life Science customers in the North East Region</li>
<li>
Executive goals and strategy focused on positioning NTT Data as a “Business Partner” who can operate and add value across their enterprise</li>
<li>
Drive conversations at the C Suite to demonstrate NTT Data capabilities across their Commercial Operations, Research &amp; Development, and Supply Chain Integration &amp; Analytics, and Value-Based Contracting functions that impact to their business</li>
<li>
Develop opportunities within targeted customers and design/execute sales strategy and associated plans to expand NTT Data’s footprint within customers business portfolio</li>
<li>
Sell to large and national size accounts that may include global operations</li>
<li>
Create demand thru prospecting and networking as well as working with client management on existing accounts</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Basic Qualifications:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Comprehensive background to include 8 plus years of I.T. Services sales/business development/large account management experience across a combination of Application, BPO, Consulting Solutions (Digital, Analytics, IOT, etc.), and Infrastructure, Cloud, and Security offerings</li>
<li>
Demonstrated history of success expanding business footprint and add-on business with targeted customers</li>
<li>
Experience to include selling services within one or more Life Sciences (Pharmaceutical, Medical Devices, Biotech, Contract Research Organization (CRO), and Medical Distributors) Sectors</li>
<li>
History of developing C-suite relationship through consultative selling approach</li>
<li>
Able to anticipate how market and competitive factors will influence the selling of NTT DATA services and partner with internal subject matter experts to develop and demonstrate value add solutions to customers</li>
<li>
Experience working in a matrixed organization that consist of complex multi-tower transactions a plus</li>
</ul>
