<div><b>
Description</b></div>
<div>
The Test/Project Engineer will coordinate and execute new product development testing of Variable Speed Drives - Hardware and Software as a member of a development team tasked with design and qualification of reliable and cost effective motor control drives for global markets.</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div><b>
Schedule</b></div>
<div>
We offer the flexibility of a core hour work schedule. Employees can flex their start and stop times given they are working during the core hours of 9:00 am – 3:00 pm. Our teams work together to ensure their chosen work schedules are supporting our customers.</div>
<br>
<div></div>
<div><b>
Responsibilities</b></div>
<ul>
<li>Develop and execute test plans for hardware and software designs to meet new product development and quality objectives.</li>
<li>Project Management: Execute technical projects and test campaigns.</li>
<li>Perform software quality assurance testing and evaluation.</li>
<li>Provide troubleshooting and problem solving of variable speed motor drives and controls integrated in HVAC systems and compressors.</li>
<li>Compile testing data analysis from multiple sources and make recommendations on product performance.</li>
<li>Devise new approaches and solutions to accomplish testing objectives.</li>
<li>Coordinate and support field testing of drives, compressors and electronics.</li>
<li>Provide assistance to third party laboratories conducting performance tests.</li>
<li>Provide appropriate documentation and record keeping of testing programs and results.</li>
</ul>
<br>
</div>
<div></div>
<div>
<div><b>
Requirements</b></div>
<div><b>
Basic</b></div>
<ul>
<li><div>Bachelors Degree in Electrical Engineering plus three (3) to five (5) years of relevant industry experience with power electronics products
</div></li>
<li><div>Strong communications skills and ability to collaborate with a variety of stakeholders
</div></li>
<li><div>Hands on, high comfort level working with power electronics and software integration</div></li>
<li>
<div>Hands on, high comfort level working with oscilloscopes, power meters and data acquisition equipment</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Project management experience</div>
</li>
</ul>
<div><b>
Preferred</b></div>
<ul>
<li><div>Prefer five (5) to seven (7) plus years relevant experience
</div></li>
<li><div>Experience configuring and applying variable speed drives</div></li>
<li>
<div>Strong competency in the area of HVAC systems and controls</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Experience with automotive or off road vehicle electronics testing</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Experience with Hardware in the Loop (HIL) simulators</div>
</li>
<li>
<div>Experience with software quality assurance testing</div>
</li>
