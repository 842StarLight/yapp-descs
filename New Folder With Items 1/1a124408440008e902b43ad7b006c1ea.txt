<h2>Supervisor, Copy & Print Marketing</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<b>Description</b><div>
Staples focuses on our customer and our community. As a <b>Copy &amp; Print Marketing Supervisor</b> you’ll assess customer needs and provide solutions. You will be positive, inclusive and collaborative in helping our customers in their Print and Marketing needs.</div>
