<h2 class="jobSectionHeader"><b>Job Description
</b></h2>
<p><b>SUMMARY </b>:
</p>
<p>A Parts Advisor / Counterperson will assist all customers, retail, technicians and wholesale departments in selecting required parts in a friendly, professional, and efficient manner. You will be responsible for achieving monthly sales and gross forecasts as well as seeking out and soliciting business.<br>
<br>
</p>
<div>
<b>ESSENTIAL DUTIES FOR PARTS ADVISOR / COUNTERPERSON:
</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Answering phone calls professionally and providing price quotes and other information
</li>
<li>Reviewing body shop estimates and service orders for accuracy to ensure orders are correct and all pricing is in line with the estimate
</li>
<li>Pulling and filling orders from stock
</li>
<li>Ensuring that all charge sales are signed by customers and that they receive their copy of the invoice
</li>
<li>Setting up orders for daily shipments, delivery, or pick up.<br>
</li>
</ul>
</div>
<div>
<b>QUALIFICATIONS FOR PARTS ADVISOR / COUNTERPERSON:
</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Ability to keep current on new products and product updates
</li>
<li>High level of initiative and ability to work in a team
</li>
<li>Must be able to lift up to 50 lbs.
</li>
<li>Basic MS Office knowledge; computer software, internet proficiency, and general mathematic skills
</li>
</ul>
</div>
