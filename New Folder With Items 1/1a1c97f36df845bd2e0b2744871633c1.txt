<h2>Purchasing and Contracting Specialist</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">Southern Oregon University
<br><br>
<b>Position Number:</b> C00259
<br><br>
<b>Rate:</b> $2,718/mo (@ 1.0 FTE)
<br><br>
<b>Position Summary:</b><br>
While reporting to Service Center management, this position works independently with general supervision to provide support for the purchasing of quality, and cost-efficient, services and supplies for campus departments and provides campus departments support for business travel. The position provides some vendor and product research, and development of detailed bid specifications up to certain limits as specified in established SOU policies. Involved in the procurement of a wide variety of commodities, and need broad technical knowledge when acquiring services and supplies. Works with Service Center management, Business Services, and Finance and Administration when looking to purchase more highly technical and sophisticated equipment and supplies, which require complicated vendor and product research and more complex bid and specifications writing, in situations where dollar amounts involve high levels of approval authority as specified in the SOU Contracting Signature Authority policies. This position will also provide assistance with negotiating and writing of bid specifications and contracting.
<br><br>
<b>Minimum Qualifications:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Experience with spreadsheets and word processing.•Strong customer service skills working with diverse populations. One year of full-time purchasing experience which must include purchasing practices, procedures, and techniques such as determining specifications, placing purchase orders, and processing requisitions.</li>
</ul>
<b>Preferred Qualification :</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree in Business Administration or a closely-related field.•Experience with Ellucian Banner-Finance Database systems.•Experience with negotiating and writing bid specifications and contracts.•Experience using querying and reporting tools.•Experience with Microsoft Office applications with emphasis on Excel and Word.•Experience with Google applications with emphasis on Forms and Sheets.</li>
</ul>
<b>Knowledge, Skills, and Abilities:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Excellent communication skills; ability to effectively communicate information in a clear and understandable manner, both verbally and in writing. Demonstrated customer service experience requiring a very high level of diplomacy and professionalism to effectively handle a broad range of sensitive interpersonal situations.</li>
<li>Demonstrated ability to interpret and consistently apply a wide variety of complex policies and procedures where specific guidelines may not always exist. Ability to work with frequent changes in policies and procedures, under pressure of deadlines in a fast-paced environment.</li>
<li>Strong analytical and research skills; ability to gather, evaluate, and to develop well-reasoned conclusions and recommendations. Ability to proactively assess work operations and anticipate potential problems; ability to develop and implement strategies for preventing/resolving problems.</li>
<li>Ability to effectively perform work of a highly sensitive and confidential nature that requires access to information. Ability to exercise sound judgment and discretion, tact, and diplomacy.</li>
<li>Initiative in independently planning, organizing, and performing work assignments within broadly defined parameters.</li>
<li>Ability to coordinate several functions and determine the relative importance of each; ability to work with a high level of productivity and accuracy/attention to detail. Excellent organizational and time management skills with the ability to set own priorities to coordinate multiple assignments with fluctuating and time-sensitive deadlines.</li>
<li>Excellent computer skills and proficiency with a variety of computer applications including word-processing, spreadsheets, databases, online systems, social media platforms, Internet as well as online calendaring and email.</li>
<li>Ability to initiate, establish, and foster communication and teamwork by maintaining a positive, cooperative, productive work atmosphere in and outside the University with the ability to establish and maintain effective working relationships within a diverse population and with those from various cultural backgrounds.</li>
<li>Ability to adapt to and work effectively in a heavily bureaucratic environment which requires regular interaction with a number of levels within the organization and multiple outside agencies.</li>
<li>Working knowledge, or ability to quickly learn, university infrastructure, policies and procedures.</li>
<li>Ability to provide training and direction to others, as well as student assistants.</li>
<li>Extensive knowledge of University travel policies and procedures.</li>
<li>Extensive knowledge of University procurement policies and procedures.</li>
</ul>
