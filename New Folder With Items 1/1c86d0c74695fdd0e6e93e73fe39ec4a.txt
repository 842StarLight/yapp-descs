<p><b>Director of Sales - SLED</b></p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
The Regional Director of Sales will report to the Sr. Sales Director, US East. The RDS is responsible for working across the region to define key sales opportunities and managing a high-performance sales team.</p>
<p><b>
GENERAL DESCRIPTION AND OVERALL OBJECTIVES</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Overall objective is to drive sales of Extreme's products, software, solutions and services to Enterprise customers in the region.</li>
<li>
Assess, build and develop individual and team capability, ready to scale in support of Extreme's accelerated growth.</li>
<li>
Lead a team of Sr. Account Managers and extended team of Partner Account Managers (dotted line) and Systems Engineering counterparts (dotted line) to drive and support Extreme's business within the assigned geography.</li>
<li>
Apply both tactical and strategic go to market models aimed at growing the customer base both in the short and long term to drive higher revenues, customer growth and customer satisfaction &amp; retention</li>
<li>
Develop territory plans and quota assignments for assigned Mid-Atlantic U.S. geographic area</li>
<li>
Model the "Extreme Way" and Extreme Ownership as a leader of the company</li>
<li>
Work with Account teams and Partners to develop and build a pipeline of opportunities</li>
<li>
Accurately forecast monthly, quarterly, weekly and annual sales achievement</li>
<li>
Ensure that the Sales team is focused on selling based upon value to customer, features, and solutions (sales plays)</li>
<li>
Represent Extreme at appropriate customer forums, Partner functions, Executive Briefing visits, etc.</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
KEY ORGANIZATIONAL RELATIONSHIPS AND DEPENDENCIES</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Sr. Sales Director for the US East &amp; Sr. Sales / Extreme’s Leadership team (CEO, CTO, CRO, COO, SVP Sales)</li>
<li>
Finance, Product Mgmt., Sales Ops, Supply Chain, Service,</li>
<li>
Peer to RSDs to share best practices and resolve issues</li>
<li>
Extreme Business (Product) Group executives to stay plugged into product roadmaps and be able to leverage them or their teams in support of key sales opportunities</li>
<li>
Support Functions for strategic and operational support</li>
<li>
Regional channel partners as source for both selling and deploying Extreme's products and solutions</li>
<li>
Global and Named account senior leaders/decision makers to build the relationships that allow the team to articulate Extreme's value proposition and brand</li>
</ul>

<p><b>
LEADERSHIP SKILLS</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Puts the Customer First: Has a relentless focus on the customer. Understands what the customer wants and how to best deliver the experience.</li>
<li>
Works Well with Others: Listens and communicates well with others within and outside of company. Creates a team environment that is positive and productive.</li>
<li>
Leads Courageously: Takes personal responsibility to do the right thing and persists in times of challenge or uncertainty. Adapts quickly to change and makes timely, thoughtful decisions</li>
<li>
Develops Continuously: Continuously seek opportunities to improve self and others. Leads with trust, honesty and commitment to hire, coach and develop partners to achieve their potential.</li>
<li>
Achieves Results: Understands what drives overall business success and is accountable to prioritize and deliver quality results. Demonstrates knowledge of core products and processes to get results. Anticipates obstacles and takes action to prevent or minimize their impact.</li>

Embraces Extreme Ownership</li>

<p><b>
BACKGROUND REQUIREMENTS</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Applicants must have BA/BS degree from a four-year College or university, MBA Preferred (or similar like experience).</li>
<li>
Minimum 10 years’ experience managing regional sales teams focusing on major accounts (or similar like experience).</li>
<li>
Strong candidates will possess a background in a networking sales environment (preferred); other areas of IT will be considered.</li>
<li>
Must be able to relate to all levels of management and employees with ease and enthusiasm.</li>
<li>
Excellent oral/written &amp; presentation communications skills are necessary.</li>
<li>
Understand the business acumen necessary to sell in the enterprise IT/network space</li>
<li>
Ability to interact with and deliver solutions to an executive audience</li>
<li>
Customer identification, business analysis and research to uncover product purchase motivators</li>
<li>
Ability to position Extreme products correctly and provide customer with a complete technological advanced story that solves specific business problems (use cases) with a quantifiable results</li>
</ul>
