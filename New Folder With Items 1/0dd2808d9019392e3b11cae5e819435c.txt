<h2>Marketing Manager - Americas</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">
<div>
<p><b>Purpose of the Role</b></p>
<p>
The Marketing Manager – Americas is responsible for leading and executing marketing efforts for the US and Canada. You will be tasked to ensure that our various marketing programs in the region are exceptionally well planned, measured, and executed. You will deliver on our marketing team goals for providing sales leads, meetings, and opportunities, and assist with global branding initiatives.</p>
<br>
<p></p>
<p><b>
Key Responsibilities</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
<p>In partnership with the Directors of Marketing, Americas and EMEA, formulate, execute, and measure all marketing strategies in region for revenue goals as well as build our B2B brand.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Help develop and execute and measure tactics to deliver on our goal of driving qualified prospect leads and meetings that lead to sales opportunities and ultimately new clients.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Build genuine cross-departmental relationships with key sales and customer success leaders so that we can deliver on company revenue goals.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Launch and execute our extensive “own” events programs with excellence, including our annual global marquis event, Innovation Day, Executive Dinners, Client and Partner Happy Hours, on an ongoing basis. Where outside resources are necessary, you will manage those resources to ensure execution with excellence.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Execute our ongoing sponsored industry events including assisting sales team with lead generation and coordinating brand awareness tactics such as booth presence, and sponsored content.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Assist and produce region-specific, valuable and engaging content for our website and blog that attracts and converts marketing qualified leads in collaboration with the global content team.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Collaborate with other regional marketing leaders to share ideas, globalize programs and assets and continue to build a powerful and consistent brand impression.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Be a genuine collaborator with the global marketing AND a voice for the unique needs of North America.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Stay abreast of industry news and the requirements of GDPR regulations and, working with our legal team, ensuring compliance.</p>
<br>
</li>
</ul>

<p><b>Company Overview</b></p>
<p>
These days, more and more brand sales are being generated through partnerships. It stands to reason. When one company can collaborate with another to drive more sales and revenue, the combination can drive better results and outstanding ROI.</p>
<p>
But partnerships take management and attention to flourish. It can be incredibly difficult to form partnerships, manage them, analyze and optimize campaigns, and make payments to your partners when you try to do it manually. Brands need something better. A way to make partnership easier, more efficient, and more profitable. That’s where we come in!</p>
<p>
Partnerize makes it easier for the world's leading brands to build powerful business partnerships that drive extraordinary business growth. The Partnerize Partner Management Platform is an end-to-end, SaaS-based solution for forming, managing, analyzing, and predicting future results for partner marketing programs using artificial intelligence.</p>
<p>
Many of the world’s largest and most innovative businesses rely our real-time technology to Partnerize. We work with more than 300 of the world's great brands and are proud to have business relationships with more than 25 of Fortune's top 100 global brands. Every year, we drive and manage more than $6B in partner programs and $500M in financial exchanges across 214 countries and territories worldwide. And our growth line is pointed up up up.</p>
<p>
Partnership is a big deal, and we play an important role in helping the world’s great brands get the most out of this powerful business channel. Working for Partnerize gives you the opportunity to be part of something big. To learn more about Partnerize and partner marketing, visit Partnerize.com.</p>
