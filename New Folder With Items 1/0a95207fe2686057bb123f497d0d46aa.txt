<p>Founded in 1979, McGrath RentCorp is a diversified business to business rental company that rents and sells relocatable modular buildings, storage containers, electronic test equipment, and liquid and solid containment tanks and boxes. We operate through five divisions: Mobile Modular, Mobile Modular Portable Storage, TRS-RenTelco, Adler Tanks, and Enviroplex.</p>
<p>McGrath RentCorp is headquartered in Livermore, CA with branch offices located throughout North America. We are traded on the NASDAQ Global Select Market under the symbol "MGRC."</p>
<p><b>Position Summary:</b></p>
<p>The Accounts Payable Manager will lead the Accounts Payable department and related processes. Responsibilities include the direct management of the Accounts Payable team, invoice payment processing, vendor management, certain general ledger close activities, resolution of external and internal customer inquiries and process improvement initiatives. This position will govern accounts payable policies and procedures, and ensure effectiveness and efficiency of the overall process. We are looking for an experienced accounts payable manager with 10+ years’ experience in a similar role. Excellent communication, process improvement, systems and leadership skills are a must.</p>
<p><b>Duties/Responsibilities of Position:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Manage the company’s accounts payable operations in a multi-company, multi-division, multi-branch environment</li>
<li>Supervise, coach, and train the accounts payable team on procedures, controls, and issue resolution</li>
<li>Oversee the invoice payment process, including the planning of staff utilization, verification, posting, monitoring for timely payment</li>
<li>Perform monthly accounting close activities related to the Accounts Payable function, including Markview application and Oracle sub-ledger month end close procedures</li>
<li>Review and update policies and procedures, and ensure understanding and adherence for consistent results</li>
<li>Analyze accounts payable activities for performance, including timeliness, accuracy, and proper general ledger coding</li>
<li>Work closely with operational department managers and teams to hone transactional processes, create efficiencies and enhance performance</li>
<li>Explain discrepancies to internal personnel, and develop plan for process improvements as needed</li>
<li>Possesses excellent writing, communication and presentation skills</li>
<li>Participates and leads Oracle, or other software, enhancements as needed</li>
<br>
</ul>
<p></p>

<li>Is good at establishing clear directions, setting stretch objectives and brings out the best in people</li>
