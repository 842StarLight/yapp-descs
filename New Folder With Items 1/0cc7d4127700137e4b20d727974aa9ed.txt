<h2>Regional Practice Manager</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p>Central Ohio Primary Care is seeking a Regional Practice Manager for our central business office in Westerville, OH. This position serves as the liaison between the Operations Department and COPC practice locations to provide operations support, drive corporate strategy/initiatives, mentor/train office managers/site reps and participate in annual performance evaluations. This is a full time position working M-F 1st shift.</p>
<p><b>
Duties/Responsibilities:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Drive practice alignment with corporate strategy and initiatives intended to improve operational success, including but not limited to quality and financial success.</li>
<li>
Provide a direct resource to practice physicians and practice managers/site representatives to promote maximum efficiency.</li>
<li>
Serve as resource for sites to address any and all operational matters.</li>
<li>
Promote compliance with COPC policies and procedures.</li>
<li>
Train and mentor site managers and site representatives.</li>
<li>
Participate in screening process of new managers/site representatives.</li>
<li>
Participate in annual performance review process of site managers/site representatives with site physicians.</li>
<li>
Assist with planning and transition of new practices joining COPC.</li>
<li>
Ability to work outside normal Monday to Friday, 8 am - 5 pm work day as needed to address work duties.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br>
<p><b>
Requirements:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>
Ten or more years of healthcare management experience: BA/BS Degree preferred, or equivalent combination of education and experience.</li>
<li>
Extensive knowledge in all aspects of practice management including:<ul>
<li>
Medical office policies/procedures</li>
<li>
Administrative and clinical operations and workflow</li>
<li>
Revenue Cycle management</li>
<li>
Regulatory compliance</li>
<li>
HR management and oversight of front office, administrative and clinical staff</li>
<li>
EHR optimization</li>
<li>
Knowledge and understanding of KPI's, reporting and analysis</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>
Exceptional customer service skills and ability to work in a fast-paced environment</li>
<li>
Ability to learn and proficiently use microsoft office software applications</li>
<li>
Travel between multiple locations required</li>
</ul>
</div></div>
