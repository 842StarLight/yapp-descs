<h2>Director, Product Management</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader">
<b>Healthcare’s helping hand.</b><b>
</b>
</h2>
<div><div><div>
<p>CHG shook things up in 1979 by inventing the locum tenens staffing model. We connect doctors with patients who need their care. As the largest physician staffing firm in America, our providers treat millions of patients each year.
</p>
<p>Our industry is growing and demand is high. This means you’ll have plenty of opportunities to grow and develop in your career. Keeping healthcare healthy can be as fun as it is rewarding.</p>
<p></p>
<br>
<p>
We are looking for a passionate, dedicated Director of Product who wants to work for one of “Utah’s Most Admired Companies.” The Product Director will lead a new Digital Experience initiative aimed at improving how our customers engage and work with CHG. The candidate will own the Product vision and objectives, lead creation of new features, oversee UX improvements, own Go-To-Market activities, and will collaborate closely with engineering, CHG customers, and internal CHG sales teams to deliver an industry-leading digital experience. The Product Leader must be able to develop a team of product managers, support the creation of roadmaps, and have excellent leadership, communication, presentation, and interpersonal skills.</p>
</div></div></div>
<h2 class="jobSectionHeader">
<b>Your role:</b><b>
</b>
</h2>
<div><div><div>
<ul>
<li>Lead the vision, strategic direction, and user experience for a new digital platform that will make it easier for customers to work with CHG companies. Develop product strategies and feature roadmaps.</li>
<li>
Collaborate with product, design, engineering and data partners to research user needs, frame the problems, choose the approaches, and produce an engaging product experience that will delight our customers.</li>
<li>
Build strong relationships throughout the company and be able to leverage those relationships to drive business value through product</li>
<li>
Balance the needs of internal employees and external customer needs</li>
<li>
Be passionate about outcomes and able to evangelize and keep focus on desired outcomes</li>
<li>
Operate within an Agile environment with other Product teams, and internal stakeholders, to launch features that will help transform how our customers engage with CHG.</li>
<li>
Must be user research driven, and share market and competitive analysis to define Product roadmap</li>
<li>
Use data analytics to get insights on how customers are engaging with your products</li>
<li>
Contribute to the overall direction and continuous improvement of the product organization</li>
<li>
Guide 3rd party partner and contractors in support of strategic initiative</li>
<li>
Demonstrate the CHG way of Leadership; effectively coach, mentor, and develop a committed, engaged and high performing team of Product Managers</li>
<li>
Champion your team and foster an environment for their success.</li>
<li>
Understand, embrace and live the CHG Core Values: Putting People First, Growth, Integrity, Continuous Improvements and Quality</li>
</ul>
</div></div></div>

Excellent relationship building skills and demonstrated ability to collaborate with multiple internal and external stakeholder groups</li>
