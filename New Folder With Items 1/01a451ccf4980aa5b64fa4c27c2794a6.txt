<h2>Director, Demand Generation</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText">Inkling is a mobile learning enablement platform built for employees who interact directly with their customers. It helps these frontline workers access the training and resources they need to quickly get up to speed in their roles at any time, from anywhere—and deliver excellent customer experiences.
<br><br>
At Inkling, we’re committed to helping the modern frontline workforce do their best work by making learning accessible—and are looking for top talent that shares our passion.
<br><br>
<b>The Role:</b><br>
Inkling is seeking a passionate growth marketing lead with expertise and a proven track record of developing and executing effective marketing campaigns to generate pipeline for a B2B SaaS company (bonus points for experience in Human Capital Management space). You will possess a strong understanding of marketing automation, digital marketing, account based marketing and effective analysis and reporting of campaign effectiveness and pipeline velocity.
<br><br>
<b>Responsibilities:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>Develop and execute the overall demand generation plan.</li>
<li>Design and deliver highly integrated marketing campaigns leveraging all appropriate marketing channels.</li>
<li>Work closely with product marketing to develop and deliver the demand generation portions of Inkling’s go-to-market strategies and plans.</li>
<li>Work closely with sales/business development to ensure alignment and develop a strong pipeline of opportunities.</li>
<li>You have the ability to apply your marketing expertise across a broad spectrum of account-based marketing, content marketing, email marketing, social media, events, and digital marketing.</li>
</ul>
<b>Requirements:</b><br>
<ul>
<li>B2B SaaS experience for a global, high growth enterprise software company</li>
<li>Experience in managing growth marketing</li>
<li>Hands-on experience with Marketo (or other marketing automation platforms, SFDC, and Google Analytics.</li>
<li>Experience with managing external agencies and consultants.</li>
<li>You can easily move from strategic marketing planning to tactical execution of the plan.</li>
<li>You have a passion for startups, growth, an agile work environment, collaboration and SaaS marketing.</li>
<li>You are a voracious learner and desire to be continually challenged and learning new skills as the marketing discipline constantly evolves.</li>
<li>You regularly roll up your sleeves to execute on a plan, doing whatever it takes to get the job done.</li>
<li>You’re flexible and comfortable with change and ambiguity involved in early-stage startups.</li>
<li>Metrics orientation and strong marketing budget development and management skills</li>
<li>Strong storytelling/messaging/positioning/differentiation skills</li>
<li>Demonstrated track record of growing top-line revenue</li>
</ul>
