<b>Summary of the Position:</b> Accountable for developing the brand innovation and communication strategies for a product (within a brand) to build consumer demand and market share. Brand Manager will be involved in development of brand strategy, pricing strategy, innovation, product portfolio management, new launches and integrated brand activation plans. Plans and coordinates the retail marketing and promotional campaigns for assigned product lines. The Brand Manager will provide leadership to Associate Product Managers at the direction of the Marketing Manager. Assists Marketing Manager with overall Marketing Strategy, to ensure all company directives are met.
<br>
