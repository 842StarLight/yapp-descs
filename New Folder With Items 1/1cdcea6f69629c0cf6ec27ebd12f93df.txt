<div><div>
The CPQ Architect will be responsible for defining the Oracle Configure, Price, Quote (CPQ) solution including alignment with business processes, enterprise technical architecture, user experience and change enablement. Primary responsibilities will be focused on defining the solution, driving client workshops, and managing the technical work stream. The candidate will need to be client-facing and can serve as a subject matter expert for key capabilities such as: Product Catalog, Pricing, Quoting, Guided Selling, Discounting, Pricing Analytics, Opportunity Management, eCommerce, Quote to Cash, Order Management.</div></div>
