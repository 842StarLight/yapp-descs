<p><b>Bi-Lingual Recruiter</b></p>
<p><b>

<p>
We are looking for a recruiter to be responsible for full-cycle recruitment for Carlo's Bakery! You will be responsible for collaborating with department managers to proactively post and fill jobs by designing and implementing recruiting strategy.</p>
<p><b>
Performance Responsibilities:</b></p>
<p>
*Full life cycle recruiting process including sourcing, coordinating and participating in industry-specific and college job fairs, including communicating with store managers and follow up with the outcome to determine best use of resources by developing and fostering recruitment *Support Retail Managers at new store openings by coordinating all aspects of career events, advertising, and posting *Sourcing quality candidates through various media, events, and job boards. *Utilize and follow up with the applicant tracking system *Provide a professional and welcoming atmosphere when sourcing and interviewing candidates *Recruit at events, colleges, and local businesses *Attend career fairs *Meet and exceed personal and company staffing goals *Research additional resources including, but not limited to local universities, networking groups, job fairs etc *Manage candidates, job postings, and the interview through the ATS *Stay current on recruitment trends *Assist in the day to day operations of the HR Department *Provide operations support on issues such as talent and performance management and employment law *Assist/lead the development of HR processes, policies and systems *Liaise with employees, supervisors, managers, co-workers in problem resolution *Maintain compliance with federal, state, and local employment and benefits laws and regulations
</p>
