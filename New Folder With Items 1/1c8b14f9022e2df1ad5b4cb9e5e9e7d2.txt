<p><b>Lee &amp; Cates Glass Customer Service Associate - Auto Glass</b></p>
<p><b>
Job Description</b></p>
<p>
Lee and Cates Glass, Inc. is one of the largest independent glass companies in the southeast corner of the United States. We believe that well-trained, professional employees are our most valuable business assets. We are committed to exceeding our customers’ expectations and dedicated to building long-term relationships with those we serve.</p>
<p>
We are building a team of Customer Service Associates who will interact with our customers at a fixed location. They will greet customers in a cheerful and professional manner and conduct a walk-around inspection of the vehicle with the customer. During this walk around is an ideal time to educate the customer on our products and services that may be applicable. This is a sales and service position but not cold-calling or knocking on doors. Rather, we are looking for professional and courteous individuals that want to be part of our team which is providing quality products and services to our automotive customers.</p>
<p>
The Lee &amp; Cates Glass Customer Service Associates represents Lee &amp; Cates Glass to targeted customers in order to build awareness, create a consistent image, generate sales opportunities, and build customer preference.</p>
<p><b><i>
This is an entry level position that is a competitive base salary ($11.50-$14.50 per hour for 40 hours per week) with an incentive per sale.</i></b></p>
<p><b>
 </b><b>Job Tasks</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Network and build trusting relationships with potential customers</li>
<li>Thoroughly understand our products and services to inform potential customers</li>
<li>Use word of mouth marketing techniques</li>
<li>Assist in organizing marketing events</li>
<li>Track customer preferences and suggest advertising and positioning ideas</li>
<li>Plan and implement awareness creating programs to reach out to potential customers</li>
<li>Engage in outreach programs to involve existing customers into the company’s offerings</li>
<li>Understand the company’s goals and missions, and apply them within a sales and marketing platform</li>
<li>Relay customer feedback to the company in anticipation of improving products and services</li>
<li>Generate ideas to maximize sales to achieve or exceed sales goals</li>
<li>Develop and maintain strong and clear communication with existing and future customers</li>
<li>Assist in creating and installing marketing displays and insuring brand advertising material is placed strategically</li>
<li>Build and sustain business relationships with key customers and accounts in an operating area.</li>
<li>Leave any literature, if any, regarding our products and services</li>
</ul>
