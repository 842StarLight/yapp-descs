<h2>Digital Director</h2> 
 <div id="jobDescriptionText" class="jobsearch-jobDescriptionText"><div>
<p><b>Digital Director | New York, New York
</b></p>
<p>Informa is looking for an innovative, passionate Digital Director to conceive and execute integrated “go-to-market” client facing services for all things digital including online, social, editorial content development and measurement, and related products. A deep knowledge of digital media best practices, including social, advertising tactics and monetization strategies are required. The person hired for this role will be a key player in leading these efforts on a strategic and tactical level. Market focus is B2B clients in financial services, and B2C readers such as financial advisors, lawyers, accountants, and financial planning professionals.</p>
<p><b>
In this role you will:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Work with business and editorial teams to conceive and execute integrated “go-to-market” client facing strategies for all things digital</li>
<li>Conduct ongoing competitive market and industry analysis. Identify marketplace trends and potential product development avenues for digital revenue growth.</li>
<li>Work closely with sales team and business owners to deliver digital product/program solutions to clients (marketing services) and users (readers and subscribers) within the industry</li>
<li>Assume a role of digital expert overseeing the day to day digital operations of the business including growth of digital display campaigns, research, traffic growth and implementing digital best practices across the group</li>
<li>Work directly with cross-functional teams (marketing, content, editorial, sales) and business owners to prioritize product work</li>
<li>Be passionate about delighting customers via strong value propositions and top-notch marketing collateral</li>
<li>Become the resident Subject Matter Expert on designated products and markets served and support sales efforts through client presentations, product decks, RFP responses etc.</li>
<li>Build and implement marketing strategies that promote product set across multiple channels including e-mail, social, SEO/SEM, using data analytics and behavioral tactics</li>
<li>Track product metrics and manage to goals</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
What you'll need to succeed:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>A Bachelor's Degree in business or a related field, MBA a plus</li>
<li>Minimum 5+ years of relevant product management and business analysis experience</li>
<li>Deep knowledge of digital media best practices</li>
<li>Experience working in a digital marketing services business centered on content</li>
<li>Understanding of product piloting, product packaging and launches</li>
<li>Proven ability to influence cross-functional team and business owners to prioritize work</li>
<li>Strong time management and project management skills</li>
<li>Ability to thrive in a fast-paced, dynamic environment</li>
<li>Ability to develop KPI's, roadmaps and resource allocation plans</li>
<li>Strong oral, written and presentation skills; ability to convey highly strategic concepts into deliverable results to both internal and external teams/clients</li>
<li>Agency/editorial content experiance in finance or wealth management industry a plus</li>
</ul>
<p><b>
Who we are:
</b></p>
<p>Informa's Wealth Management Media Group delivers independent editorial expertise and proven financial services marketing solutions for measurable ROI. Over 540,000 financial advisors and wealth professionals rely on WealthManagement.com's information, editorial insight, and analysis to assist them in their client activities to improve practice management and gathering of assets. Our marketing services and multi-media channels are used by several hundred wealth management firms daily to promote and communicate product, brand, and career opportunities to financial advisors and wealth professionals.</p>
<p><b>
About Informa
</b></p>
<p>Informa is a leading business intelligence, academic publishing, knowledge and events business, operating in the Knowledge and Information Economy. We are listed on the London Stock Exchange and a member of the FTSE 100.
</p>
